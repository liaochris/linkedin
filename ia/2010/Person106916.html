John Reade’s Summary

Started working for Quanterion Solutions, Inc. in the summer of 2005 as an Intern in the Reliability Information Analysis Center's (RIAC) Library. Continued by working part-time on RIAC and Quanterion projects while attending college at St. John Fisher. While at St. John Fisher, I also worked as a research assistant for Dr. Scott Harrison, gathering information on the Committee on National Security Systems 4011 standard in order to effectively map courseware to the CNSS 4011 standard. I was also a Math/Computer Science tutor in the Math Center of St. John Fisher. In May 2008, I was hired as a full-time employee and began working on several web development projects such as the development and maintenance of the QSI website and Intranet. My goal is to help get Quanterion Solutions to be not just a reliability engineering company but an IT solutions company as well. Also, merge the two fields to become a unique company that provides the best, most-efficient solutions to our customers.
