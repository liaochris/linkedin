Kathy Telban CPT, SPHR’s Summary

Kathy Telban is a master facilitator, a systems thinker and a boundary crosser who provokes her clients to think in new ways.

Using an inspired-centered and collaborative approach, she can facilitate the following types of sessions:
• Visioning sessions
• Strategic/Operational planning sessions
• Change management planning and evaluation sessions
• Project management planning and evaluation sessions
• Process development and improvement sessions
• System development and improvement sessions
• Technology selection and evaluation sessions
• Curriculum program and course outcome sessions
• Curriculum assessment planning sessions
• Business mediation sessions

With 20 years of industry experience and a decade in higher education, Kathy uses her multi-dimensional background to help organizations improve their outcomes and achieve their mission.

Kathy Telban CPT, SPHR’s Specialties:
