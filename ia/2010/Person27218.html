Brandon Uttley’s Summary

Brandon Uttley, APR, is a web development, marketing and public relations professional with more than 20 years of experience. Currently, he is founder and president of Web Business Freedom, Inc.

Uttley was previously co-founder and president of carbonhouse, inc. from April 1999-August 2008. Prior to that, he was director of public relations at Luquire George Andrews.

Uttley earned the Accreditation in Public Relations (APR) designation from Public Relations Society of America. He is past president (2009) of the PRSA Charlotte chapter (www.prsacharlotte.com).

A 1987 graduate of the School of Journalism and Mass Communication at UNC-Chapel Hill, Uttley began his career in New York with the public relations firm Blacker/Hunter, which specialized in media relations for publishing companies and other businesses.
