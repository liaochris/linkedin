Marisa Smith’s Summary

Since my early days of playing office in my parents' basement, I've been obsessed with creating processes and automating repetitive tasks. I first developed an interest in system design and workflow automation after I got my master’s degree in Higher Education Administration at University of Michigan. While working as an advisor of undergraduate engineering students, I was frustrated by the lack of good electronic tools for managing student records, and decided to solve the problem myself.

I worked at the University of Michigan for several years, helping administrative offices solve their paper problems with electronic solutions, and decided to strike out on my own in 2002 so I could help even more people.

I've realized that my real passion is for improving the quality of life for individuals and organizations and I'm devoted to helping people implement electronic solutions to improve efficiency and create more time and abundance in their lives.
