    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    September 2006 — Present (4 years 2 months)

    At Jobs2Web I have the distinct pleasure of working with some of the most forward thinking talent acquisition teams in our industry.

    Each client is different. My role is to understand and propose solutions for the idiosyncratic needs of clientele taking into consideration factors like current goals, culture, recruitment channels, budget, employment brand, the candidate experience and gaps in strategy.

    The solutions I deploy via Jobs2Web's Recruitment Marketing Platform target candidates within Web 2.0 channels like Search Engines, Aggregations Sites and Social Networks. In addition, recognizing that targeting the right people isn't enough, my clients engage and pipeline talent for hard to fill roles.
