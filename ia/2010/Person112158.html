Kathy Barthel’s Summary

Kathy Barthel is an online content specialist with over 10 years experience in new media and journalism. Her focus is writing and editing for the web but she is also an experienced video editor, photo editor and print journalist who brings a well-rounded skill set to online publishing.

Kathy has created or edited a variety of content for media outlets such as:

• MSN.ca [ Canada’s #1 Portal ]
• CTV.ca
• CBC.ca
• WNetwork.com
• YTV.ca
• TV GUIDE Magazine
• The Globe and Mail

Her articles include profiles of many well-known figures in media, architecture and design including:

• Viscount David Linley, furniture designer and nephew to HRH, Queen Elizabeth.
• Ross Lovegrove, award-winning industrial designer.
• Colin Mochrie, actor and comedian.
• Jamie Kennedy, executive chef.
• June Callwood, broadcaster, author and social activist.

Kathy has written about television, film, architecture, design, business, lifestyle, cooking, gardening and spirituality. She has also written or edited press and marketing materials for clients such as YTV`s media relations department and Strategy Magazine. While working in TV production she created online content for YTV and W Network shows and has even written TV game show questions!

As video editor at Canada`s number one portal, MSN.ca, Kathy was an integral part of the team that launched the site. Within six months, video stream targets had surpassed historical numbers. Her video editing skills were also put to good use in her role as video and stills editor at CBC.ca news.

As a former photo editor Kathy knows how to pair compelling images with text and is skilled at using Photoshop. At TV GUIDE she directed photo shoots in Canada and the U.S., negotiated international rights and drafted contracts. She has given photo workshops to MSN.ca staff and to publicists at CBC and CTV.
