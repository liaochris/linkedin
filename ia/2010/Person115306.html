Kevin Buchser CPA’s Summary

Kevin currently works as a staff auditor with Cahaba Safeguard Administrators (CSA). In his current position Kevin preforms Medicare Cost Report compliance audits and reviews on various types of medical providers. Kevin is a member of several workgroups at CSA and is currently working to help develop a software system that helps track the results of the audit work preformed. In addition Kevin is work with a workgroup that is using Data Analytics in order to target Cost Reports for review.

Kevin is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) in the State of North Carolina.

Kevin earned his Master of Accounting degree from North Carolina State University (NCSU) in May of 2008. He also obtained a Bachelors of Science in Accounting from NCSU in 2006. During his studies at NCSU, Kevin took elective course work in Information Technology, Supply Chain Management, and Enterprise Risk Management.

After graduation from the undergraduate program Kevin obtained an internship with KPMG. Upon completion of the internship he received a full time position offer with KPMG. While at KPMG he worked across multiple areas of accounting to including AR, AP, Fixed Assets and Inventory and gained experience in both the accounting and auditing of the those areas.

Prior to and during Kevin’s course work at NCSU, he worked as an operations supervisor at UPS. He was responsible for a work group of 15 people at the UPS next day air operation at Raleigh-Durham International Airport.

Also during his time at NCSU, Kevin served as a soldier in the US Army National Guard. As a Signal Systems Support Specialist, Kevin was responsible for maintaining communication systems, both computer and radio, in addition to supervising his team and training his unit and team on the communications equipment.
