Ann Sachs’s Summary

I have been a theatre professional since 1969, working as an actor, writer, director, mentor, business owner and thought leader. I think of myself as a creative collaborator.

Since 1992 I have collaborated with my husband, Tony Award winning designer Roger Morgan, in our company Sachs Morgan Studio: Theatre Design Specialists. We design theatre buildings that work for their owners, their patrons, and the theatre professionals that use them. I run the business and Roger provides the service with our talented staff.

My newest venture is called Theatrical Intelligence™, which I launched in May of 2009. It is a process I developed that uses theatre concepts to impact business performance. It's the fun part of being smart!

Who is it for? Those who are looking to re-ignite that creative spark in their work, in their workplace, and in their lives.

Why is it needed? Because according to a recent Gallup poll, 73% of all employees in the US are not happy in their jobs. They crave creativity in their work and are looking for "something more" in their personal and professional lives.

Check out my (beta) blog: http://www.theatricalintellligence.com and let me know what you think.
