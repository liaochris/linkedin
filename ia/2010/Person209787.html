Upkar J. S. Dhaliwal’s Summary

A Wireless Technology Entrepreneur Business Developement Expert with extensively wide contacts and specialist knowledge on Future Wireless Direction – Standards, Engineering & Markets.

An Entrepreneur & System Architect with over 20 years of hands-on experience in System, RF, Wireless, Space & Defence. As a Professional Chartered Engineer, an industry domain expert serving on numerous industrial/technical bodies, with specialized knowledge & technical leadership in IP, design & development novel wireless circuits, devices & systems.

My focus for past 15 yrs has been busienss, architecture, system requirements/specifications & market requirements in Wireless Communications. Worked for STMicro/NOKIA, QUALCOMM/QCT, SAMSUNG Telecom, Marconi and EMI.

Present focus is Convergence Technologies of IEEE 802 stds like 4G: 802.16 WiMAX & 5G: 802.22 Cognitive WRAN, plus new study group on 802.15.6 BAN and .15.3 UWB, whilst keeping up with 3GPP LTE (& UMB) for new applications like FemtoCell, Home Gateway and Wireless Medical.

Recent focus includes Front End solutions for UMTS, CDMA NxOFDMA, MIMO & 802.11,15,16, 20 & 22 WLAN/WiMax multimode.

Major accomplishments are variousw world-class first including early calls in either 3G CDMA, UMTS & UMTS+GSM with chipset platform development – complete design cycle of HW/SW integration from product requirement definition, High Level definition thru to chipset verification & customer sampling/support into production of the First single chip transceiver, single SW stack & integrated radio modem;
world-class first space qualified RFIC/MMIC based
