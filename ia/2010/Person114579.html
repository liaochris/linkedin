Kenneth Clemmer’s Summary

Creative problem solver with barely contained contempt for orthodoxy and convention. Excels at designing objective, streamlined, fact-based approaches for resolving notoriously squishy and intractable business issues. Values truth, honesty and fairness. Promotes disruptive innovation, work-life balance and just reciprocity. Seeks to solve tough and important problems through collaboration with brilliant people who have complementary skills and tell good jokes.

Kenneth Clemmer’s Experience

  • Head of Analytics

    Zoopla, Ltd.

    (Consumer Services industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    Zoopla is an online property information service and community website integrating elements such as house pricing, free valuation estimates, and ‘For Sale’ listings, as well as local community information – all on one award-winning site. Launched in January 2008, the company has become one of the most visited property websites in the UK. It has achieved record website traffic, with over five million visits and 40 million page views, boasting over a million user contributions within the first 12 months of launch.

    Zoopla is now the UK’s leading website for house prices and value data, providing the most comprehensive source of residential property market information. Zoopla expects to become the website of choice for anyone interested in the property market.

    Responsible for design, testing and refining our valuation products and services, as well as management and professional development of the analytics team.

    (Market Research industry)

    September 2002 — October 2003 (1 year 2 months)
