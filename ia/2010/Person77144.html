Graham Hurdle’s Summary

Professional Background

I am highly experienced in Road Safety and Driver Training.

I sits on a number of influential committees within the UK concerned with Road Safety and Driver Training.

I am always prepared to push the boundaries and has a proven track record of innovative ideas within the Road Safety and Driver Training Industry.

Typical Projects

Road Risk Management Audit – a comprehensive audit covering all aspects of the safety and operational procedures and the management systems that companies /organizations have in place.

Risk Assessments – giving a comprehensive review of the risks faced by employees making work related road journeys. Based on the three fundamental areas of work related road safety – the driver, the journey and the vehicle plus the skills of individual drivers including their attitude, behaviour and knowledge and hazard perception awareness.

Developing interactive on and off line driver risk assessment and training solutions.

We are about to open up new markets by developing online training systems on topics such as Manual Handling, Fire Safety, Customer Care etc.

Running one day seminars and presentations on Road Safety
