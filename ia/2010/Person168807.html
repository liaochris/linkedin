Randi Martin’s Summary

My main goal for getting into radio was the magic one gets to create in a listeners mind....using the tools of music, voice and smarts. My radio career has allowed me to do this with great success and I have worked in many markets and worked with many wonderful people. From small markets to a National one...I have entertained and informed many.
Along the way ..the voice market began calling..and my home studio was cranked up..
It would be my pleasure to work with you on any project , be it a radio show or a commercial.
Everyone Needs a Voice..Now You can Have Mine!

  • On Air Personality Plus

    XM Satellite Radio

    (Public Company; Broadcast Media industry)

    August 2003 — June 2009 (5 years 11 months)

    A wonderful aspect of XM was the fact that you could start on one station and find yourself attached to others, simply for the joy in doing that. I was the evening host of the alternative country channel "X-Country" and helped with progamming and music. I was the morning host for the AC channel "The Blend" and maintained the logs, did station interviews and represented the channel on major airline channels. I had the opportunity of being one of the first short form show producers at "Take 5" XMs womens channel , these vignettes were also written and voiced by me. And to my delight XM created a Hanukkah channel, and I was content manager for 3 years..picking music, finding show content and discovering new jewish bands.
    Toward the end of my XM ride, I was able to be a part of POTUS, the political channel, with daily repaorting on political blogs.

  • On Air Personality/Music Director

    Clear Channel Communications

    (Public Company; CCU; Broadcast Media industry)

    August 1993 — December 2002 (9 years 5 months)

    I was host of the Afternoon Drive show for most of the time (did mornings for the last 2 years) Show always maintained high ratings and high visability. Being nominated for a Marconi Award for 1998 and 2000 as "Major Market Personality of the Year" certainly said I was doing something right. I ,also, received nominations from Billboard and R & R for my role as Music Director for this "Soft Rock" station.We prided ourselves on being community minded and created and carried out many outreach projects. The Afternoon Show had short form programs within itself..all geared toward our women driven audience. These shows were created and produced by me. All in all..a terrific ride for many years!

  • Air Personality

From there other heritage stations called..ZETA 4, Love 94, WA1A, The Coast, WINZ, WQAM and WIOD. (where I did a talk show)
