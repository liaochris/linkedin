Cheryl Harrison | Social Media Strategist’s Summary

I am a strategic and creative thinker who can take broad marketing strategy and insights and translate them into successful initiatives in social networks. I have proven experience engaging online audiences and empowering others to share brands. I have worked to establish metrics and best practices in a field that has few.

I've been active in social media for 10 years, and working in social media professionally for 2 years. I have spoken at several conferences and workshops on best practices and strategies in the field of new media marketing.

In addition to new media tactics, I have experience in traditional public relations (writing, media relations, internal communications) and event planning, from intimate corporate events to huge social networking parties to large scale conferences.

I blog at http://beingcheryl.com about social media marketing and at http://greatestcityofall.com about Columbus, Ohio.
