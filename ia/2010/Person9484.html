Angela Hey’s Summary

Business planning consultant, tech columnist and market analyst with both a technical and marketing background. A team leader who can bridge the cultures between established Fortune 100 companies and agile technology startups.

PhD in engineering, specializing in mathematical programming. MSc in management science, specializing in corporate finance. MMath, specializing in computing and optimization, MA, specializing in mathematics.

Experience:

- developed business and marketing plans
- started marketing departments
- started ventures
- launched products
- developed market forecasts
- managed promotional campaigns
- written articles on emerging companies
- wrote analyst reports
- presented at trade shows, universities and conferences
- developed webs with Wiki, CMS, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Global awareness - consulted to Israeli, Japanese, German, French and UK companies - elementary French, Italian and German.
