Joe Cockrell’s Summary

I’m journalist-turned-PR pro with more than a decade of experience, which includes working with major universities, corporations, non-profits and start-ups.

My personal mission statement is “I live to share my passion as a communicator, artist, leader and friend. I strive for balance in all of the roles I play on life’s stage, directed by the principles of integrity, excellence, empowerment, enrichment, compassion and humility.”

My Expertise:
• media relations
• public affairs and public relations
• organizational spokesperson
• strategic communications
• management of external marketing and PR firms
• writing, editing, and website content generation
• online branding
• strategic use of social media for branding
• using creativity to formulate unique solutions
• complex project management 

I am also an experienced actor who loves improv, an avid photographer, and dog lover. I donate my time and talents to support charitable causes, including the United Way, Human Rights Campaign, United Performing Arts Fund (UPAF), Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, G.L.A.A.D., and the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA), Pursue the Passion, and Taste of the Nation.

Most recently, I founded I Should Be In The News, a national community service project that leverages a network of volunteer PR professionals (see http://ISBITN.com).
