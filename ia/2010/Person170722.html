Reggie Aggarwal’s Summary

I have been an entrepreneur for the past 12 years. The ride has been a tough one, but i would not trade it in for anything. I was fortunate enough to be named the 2009-2010 Ernst & Young's Entreprenuer of the Year for the Great Washington area. In addition, Cvent was also recently named one of the "50 Best Places to Work" in Washington by the Washington Business journal in 2010 and 2009. We were also just named the 2010 "Management Team of the Year" for companies under 2500 employees and we were finalist in 3 other categories - "Best Company", "Best Customer Support" and "Best CEO" - by the American Business Awards ('Stevie Awards").
