Madhukar Puniani’s Summary

As a leader my goal is to unlock the potential of every individual who works with me and develop a highly productive team. As a manager I like to manage resources and risks to enable the company to charter an organic growth path

My "PAQT" stands for productivity , accountability , quality and team work! These are my guiding principles for my team.

I have substantial experience in international business development, B2B marketing and sales, logistics and processes streamlining, product innovation and customer relationship management.

I am passionate about accelerating pay back, energy efficiency, water conservation, productivity enhancement, waste management and rationalized reduction in total cost of ownership - in short business sustainability. I have worked on multiple projects with international customers on these topics. I have always worked to find creative solutions to their problems.

I am adept with managing cash flow, profits, innovation and achieving market share growth and customer loyalty through strategic services and market positioning. I try to analyze the risks and manage them in the best possible way.

I am a multilingual professional, fluent in English, German, Hindi & Punjabi & basic Spanish skills with strong focus on building and co-ordinating international project teams and fostering inter-cultural communication.
