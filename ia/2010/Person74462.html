George R. Halsey’s Summary

George R. Halsey is the Owner of George R. Halsey Law. He counsels technology start-ups and established companies on a global basis in all aspects of corporate formation, equity and debt financing, executive compensation, human resources, technology licensing, marketing and distribution. George provides a legal framework to the product development life cycle which aids in the acceleration of new technology. He draws on his extensive experience in high technology packaging cost effective services as part of the George R. Halsey offerings.

Prior to George R. Halsey Law, George was a General Counsel at Sun Express, a subsidiary of Sun Microsystems, and an Associate General Counsel at Sun Microsystems and Honeywell, Inc. He was the lead legal negotiator in technology acquisition, licensing, distribution, trans-national transactions and multi-party tax-leveraged transactions including transactions in excess of $550 million.

His accomplishments include being a founding member of the American Committee for Interoperable Systems (ACIS) which influenced changes in US, European and Japanese copyright law. This culminated in international treaties promoted by the World Intellectual Property Organization portions of which are included in the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998. George also was a member of the legal steering committee that launched the Infiniband Trade Association, (IBM, Sun, Dell, HP and Intel) that defined new standards for high bandwidth interoperable systems. He was elected chairperson of the Boston Bar Association, Computer Law and Internet Committee and has presented on various intellectual property law and electronic media topics.

He is a graduate of Harvard College, and he earned his JD from the Boston University School of Law.

George has an affiliation with Hare Associates to provide expanded services in technology litigation.

George R. Halsey’s Specialties:

Technology development life cycle, intellectual property law, technology licensing, distribution, contract manufacturing, marketing, corporate formation, equity and debt finance
