Matthew Arieta’s Summary

I've been doing internet web development for over ten years. I'm passionate about the industry and have been aboard through the birth of the graphical internet, the .COM bubble bursting, the rise of Web 2.0 and am seeing glimmers of Web 3.0 - the semantic web. It thrills me that front-end web development has finally come into its own, with those in the know recognizing the many benefits of standards-compliant markup and CSS. In the last 18 months I've expanded into UX, IA, and SEO, making me feel more well rounded than at any other point in my career.
