Peter Polit’s Summary

Managed businesses and P&L centers ranging from $1.2B in annual revenue to startups with annual revenues of under $10M. Currently focused on large digital network infrastructure delivery and global business and technology valuation services.

Peter Polit’s Specialties:

Proven business and technology development in entrepreneurial and corporate environments. Large and complex team management for growth and results. Video processing, production and post production for digital cinema, MPEG1, MPEG2, MPEG4 and JPEG compression. Statistical multiplexing. Security and watermarkig.

  • General Manager, Americas Satellite

    Thomson, Inc.

    (Consumer Electronics industry)

    May 1992 — October 2006 (14 years 6 months)

    Managed Thomson's satellite buiness in the Americas, securing Thomson as the top provider of satellite receiver technology in this market. With a dedicated team of over 150 individuals and unmatched core video expertise, Thomson is the top supplier of set-tops in this market segment, serving all major satellite service providers (MPEG2, MPEG4, SD, HD, PVR, Home Gateway and MDU platforms for both the head-end and customer premises).

    Immediately prior, Peter managed Thomson's world-wide DOCSIS cable modem and VoIP eMTA business, tranforming Thomson from the world's 5th largest CM supplier to 2nd largest by expanding product range (advanced DOCSIS, eMTA, home gateway), while growing revenues and profitability.

    Prior to 2000, Peter participated in and managed a number of engineering efforts at Thomson, including (chronologically) TAK TV in Paris, France, DOCSIS cable modem development, TeleTV network deployment, and DIRECTV video encoder and multiplexer design for the uplink.

  • Various - Digital Video and Image Compression and Processing
