Steve Stewart’s Summary

Through increasing levels of responsibility, I have worked as a construction manager on the owner's side for over 10 years. Given my interest in air quality, both outdoor and indoor, I have also worked as a consultant providing testing services and recommendations for almost 20 years, concurrent with my construction management work. On a wide variety of construction and consulting projects, I have worked in a team environment as well as independently, to achieve success in resolving construction conflicts and landlord/tenant challenges, so that projects are well executed and relationships are strengthened. My professional goal is to work into an executive capacity, managing staff and associated responsibilities for a Fortune 500 company.

Steve Stewart’s Specialties:

Communication, diplomacy, owner liaison, change order negotiation, financial management
