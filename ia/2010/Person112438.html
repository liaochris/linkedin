Katie Juhl’s Summary

From newsroom to boardroom, Katie boasts wide-ranging experience in business development and news production for broadcast, online and mobile. As head of mobile in North America for one of the world's largest news agencies, she builds products, revenues and client base for Agence France-Presse. Katie's journalism roots and newsroom experience, combined with her expertise of emerging platforms, gives her a unique perspective of the media business.

She broke into the news business with ABC and PBS affiliate stations in her native Nebraska and moved on to Jim Lehrer’s NewsHour. She later worked as a coordinating producer for ABC News then as producer and talent for Reuters Television in Washington, DC.

In addition to AFP, Katie is the Executive Director for international non-profit Global Media Forum Training Group, which focuses on training of journalists in developing markets, communications training for NGO's and journalism training as a 'life skill' to underprivileged youth.

Juhl has taught graduate courses at Syracuse University, Radio Free Asia and VOA TV to Africa and is active in several professional groups, including the Radio Television News Directors Association. She is also a classically trained pianist and is a piano teacher to, an ongoing member of The Washington Chorus (Alto II) as well as a freelance videographer for weddings in the DC area.

She lives with her husband and their two rescued greyhounds on Capitol Hill.
