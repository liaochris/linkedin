Doug Erickson’s Summary

I significantly improve ratings and revenue for broadcast media in the most competitive markets on earth by helping talent forge lasting emotional bonds with their audience. We create content so compelling it can't be ignored, turning listeners into evangelists for our brands.

I've been involved in radio programming for 30 years. I've worked with some of the greatest stations and companies in the world over that span, including: KIMN, KHOW and KPKE in Denver, KILT in Houston, KDWB in Minneapolis, WBIG in Washington, DC, WABC in New York, WJR in Detroit, KINK and KUPL in Portland; GROUP NRJ in France, VIACOM, CBS, Clear Channel, Cox, Jefferson-Pilot, South Central Media Group, and others.

Great stations are different, and I can help your station go from good to great.

I am primarily concerned with content, everything that comes between the songs on music radio stations. Not that music isn't a key part of that success. It certainly is. But what comes between the songs is what makes a good station great and a great station exceptional. And now we have the web, which makes sharing content that can become viral so much easier.

When you need a Strategic partner, a programming veteran, an experienced talent coach and mentor, with a proven record of ratings success in major cities around the world, call me.

Doug Erickson’s Specialties:

I'm a former Billboard Magazine Program Director of the Year. I also have the unique perspective of having been raised overseas. My parents were missionaries and I bring a cultural sensitivity that most American consultants do not possess, which may explain why I have had long term, continuing success in International consulting.
