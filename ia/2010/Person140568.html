Melinda Moses’s Summary

I am a change agent, idea person, and roll-up-my-sleeves and do what it takes to get it done strategic marketing & alliance development resource who utilizes innovative techniques, proven methodologies, and a heavy dose of personal interaction to 'spin up the results'.

I enjoy working with organizations to create online communities that extend their reach, build their value, and enable the development of a sustainable ecosystem that helps them appreciate ... and maximize ... their potential.

I help companies "visualize their value", developing pragmatic, action-oriented rich media marketing campaigns that enable viewers to instantly understand and respond.

I work with organizations to identify goals for implementing E2.0 solutions, and then roll up my sleeves to facilitate execution of those solutions.

I have been involved with creating programs that accelerate adoption of ECM content management, workflow, collaboration and conferencing solutions for ~ 20 years including a long history with AIIM, where I am currently an Advisor and recipient of the 2007 President's Award.
