Dana Lookadoo’s Summary

Dana Lookadoo's approach to Search Marketing is with a focus on Word-of-Mouth SEO (social media & search engine optimization).

Dana's passions include:

* Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Copywriting
* Social Media Marketing & Engagement
* Information Architecture & Planning
* Website Usability & Conversion
* Technical Training & Curriculum Development

******
Dana began a career in computing in 1984 and in the Internet in 1996. Internet marketing provided the medium to merge interests in people, writing & technology. She taught as Adjunct Faculty at Cal State University, Monterey Bay for 3 semesters from 1999-2000. Dana has operated two Web development firms and a search marketing agency and is now working with a team of website optimization and analytics specialists.

Education:
B.S. (magna cum laude) in Telecommunications, Multimedia and Applied Computing, minor in Instructional Technology

A.A. in Business Administration

Studied Virtual Reality Modeling Language at the Naval Postgraduate School - focus on visual application for instructional design.

Corporate Trainer:
Provided technology curriculum training for Sun Microsystems, Monterey Institute of International Studies, U.C. Santa Cruz Extension, General Colin Powell's Youth Peace Corp, PowerUp - America's Promise, Steve Young’s NFL YET Center. Guest Lecturer at University of Reno, Information Technology.

Author:
Wrote “Traveling the Internet” for Sun Microsystems, a 100-page guide for how to travel safely and securely on the WWW, 2005.

Goal:
"Be a Professional Cyclist when I grow up." :-)
