Heather St.Clair’s Summary

Heather has over 12 years of corporate experience, including such positions as executive assistant, marketing research specialist, and research director for various media outlets. Her skills are not limited to the aforementioned; she also excels in Internet research, website design, copywriting, editing and proofreading. Heather is extremely proficient in numerous software and hardware applications, as well.

Her passion and strengths are within the broadcast media industry and include a strong background in analyzing Nielsen ratings, thinking outside the proverbial box to create compelling research and sales pieces in addition to creative sales promotions to advance both the client and the company. Having worked in DMA ranked stations from market 55 to market 137, Heather has positively positioned stations ranked number one or number five. She can creatively position stations across hyphenated markets and play the ratings game with the best of them.

Her expertise in creating realistic estimates coupled with her strong negotiating skills make her an asset to any sales team in a variety of positions.
