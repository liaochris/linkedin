Overview
I make Microsoft CRM work for you and your business.

I am a CRM specialist. I empower companies to optimise sales by getting to know their customers and delivering the services & products they require.

I own and operate Opsis-a consultancy totally focussed on implementing and improving the Customer Relationship Management strategies and requirements of businesses. I lead each project and lead the business analysis or the project.

I am a CRM Guru specialising in ensuring that customer interactions from sales to implementation and the ongoing nurturing of the customer work smoothly. We work with you to find out where you are losing time or missing opportunities and help you to implement solutions to overcome these issues. I act as a conduit between the business & technology to ensure that solutions delivered meet your business requirements.

I have been specialising in CRM for over 10 years, and for the last 5 years have been focussed on Microsoft CRM as the market leader in CRM when considered from a value for money perspective.

Contact me to see how we can help you.

As a CRM Consultant with significant experience in Microsoft CRM as well as Clarify & Seibel, my speciality is ensuring that a CRM implementation delivers quantifiable results for all users across the company. I’ve worked across all phases of implementation including requirements workshops and solutions design as well as training and project management.
I am passionate about ensuring that all users can get the maximum benefit from the software. My troubleshooting & problem solving skills are frequently used to overcome troublesome issues.
