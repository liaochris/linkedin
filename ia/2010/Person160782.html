Paul Ficalora’s Summary

As a commercial real estate agent in NY and CT my efforts are on office and retail leasing as well as establishing the value, marketing and selling all types of commercial properties.

Having provided financial and marketing services to restaurants for over 15 years my understanding of the restaurant business and its challenges, as well as the psychographic nature of restaurateurs primed me to be a very insightful restaurant broker. Restaurateurs are typically very proud and hard working individuals who I have a great deal of respect for. This understanding and sensitivity had led me to be very adept at creating a meeting of the minds between a restaurant buyer and seller.

My associates and I are recognized experts in business brokerage, business valuation, commercial real estate valuation, leasing and sales.

Paul Ficalora’s Specialties:

Specialties include: Office and retail leasing, hotels, motels, restaurants and restaurateurs. I act as a business broker and commercial real estate agent representing investors, sellers, buyers, owners, tenants, lessors and lessees. My NY office has a New York State Certified General Real Estate Appraiser who is licensed to appraise all types of property. In CT Colwell Banker Commercial has an international footprint, relationships with national accounts and a team of real estate experts.
