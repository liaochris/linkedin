I wrote producer notes, email wine offers, and tasting notes for wines in inventory. I produced video tasting notes, used social media tools such as Facebook and Twitter to promote sales, and contributed to the Staff Picks blog using Movable Type publishing platform. In 2009 I facilitated the database integration with partner, Cellartracker, for the launch of Vinfolio's online fine wine Marketplace.
