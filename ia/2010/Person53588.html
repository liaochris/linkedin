Deborah Dubree’s Summary

Since childhood, Deborah Dubree looked for new challenges and faced them head-on. As a young woman, she helped run her family gas-station. In her corporate debut, Deborah jumped into – and excelled in – the male-dominated construction industry.

Tenacious and gutsy, she quickly moved from inexperienced receptionist to respected executive management. And later, bought a $20 million commercial construction company.

Deborah’s leadership skills earned her several respected positions including: President of Construction Financial Management Association and International President of the Timberline User Group. Her contribution to the construction industry was so valued, industry peers nominated her National Woman of the Year in Construction.

Throughout her successful 26 years in the corporate world, Deborah always sought ways to personally expand, grow and push herself further. From the beginning of her career, she also studied leadership, quantum physics and human behavior.

Now Deborah focuses on her passion for coaching and teaching others. Launching ClearEdge – a resource for high-achievers who choose to get more from life.

Deborah’s wit, compassion and straight-forward style makes her a popular coach and speaker. She spoke and conducted workshops all across the United States and internationally from Canada to Australia.
