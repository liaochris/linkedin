Lauren Cucinotta’s Summary

In both my work and life, I enjoy connecting people with other people, companies, shows, stores and restaurants

I'm plugged in, but not overly obsessed. I believe in good timing. I love the words analyze and strategize. I work (and sometimes read) back to front. I think being immersed in something is the best way to learn, and I'm constantly reading and exploring, and taking the occasional class, to supplement my knowledge.

Right now, I'm most interested in the semantic web, upgrading search capacities within websites, the future of and enhancing the usability of e-commerce applications and any other nerdy (or totally cool) innovations that improve and simplify our life experiences.
