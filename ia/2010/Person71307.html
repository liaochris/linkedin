Frank Siebenlist’s Summary

Frank Siebenlist works on applications for the next generation of mobile and tablet platforms...

Frank has a Ph.D. in Physics from the University of Amsterdam. He has over 20 years of experience with distributed computing and security in financial services and high tech. Frank held senior roles as a Lead Technologist and VP at Citibank and J.P. Morgan. As Chief Architect at Silicon Valley security startup, DASCOM, he was instrumental in the company's success with Fortune 500 companies, which lead to the company's eventual acquisition by IBM. He was also the Chief Security Architect at startup, Eazel, and Senior Security Architect at IBM/Tivoli.

Experience and expertise include:
Security Software Architecture, Grid Computing, Enterprise Architecture, Cloud Computing, Web Services, Virtual Machine Technology
Healthcare and Bio-Medical Research: Security Architect on caGrid/caBIG, CHI, BIRN projects
Grid & Cloud Computing and Virtual Machines: Security Architect of Globus Toolkit, security consulting on many Grid & Cloud Computing related projects (ESG, TeraGrid, EGEE, OSG).
Research & Development: academic research (author of papers, invited speaker, PI), contributed to security standards (OSF, IETF, OASIS, OGF), and development of industry strength software used in real-world projects/industry.
