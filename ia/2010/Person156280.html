Tyler Pryor’s Summary

Each project at North Texas Customs gets the focus it deserves. Our specialty is metal work, including extensive restoration, custom fabrication, panel replacement, and media blasting. We also offer suspensions, custom paint and engine work. With over 14 years in the industry, we have in-depth experience you can trust to see your project through from concept to completion. Visit our website for photo galleries and more.

Tyler Pryor’s Specialties:

Frame-off resto's, resto-mods, chopping, shaving, fabrication, media blasting, custom paint and more. Let us help you tackle your dream. Pickup/del avail. Open M-F, 8a-6p and Sat. & Sun. by appt.
