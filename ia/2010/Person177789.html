Rose Shuman’s Summary

Rose Shuman is an experienced international management and social enterprise consultant, a recognized social entrepreneur, and a TED Fellow.

Currently, Rose is Partner at BrightFront Group, a global consulting firm. Rose takes on select clients in the international business & philanthropic space, specializing in:

- New market entry, particularly international
- Solutions for underperforming overseas operations
- International philanthropy, social enterprise & CSR

Rose is also Founder and CEO of Open Mind - Question Box, a nonprofit company. Question Box provides Internet information to persons in India & Uganda via local language telephone hotlines.

Question Box has been recognized in the New York Times; UK Sunday Telegraph; Times of India; Indian Express; BBC Digital Planet; Forbes.com; New Scientist.com; BoingBoing; PC World; and ABC.com amongst others.

Prior, Rose directed market entry research & strategy throughout Latin America, as well as Ghana, India & the United States for Adlens, an Oxford venture-backed developing world technology start-up. Adlens is developing unique lens and eyeglasses technologies, allowing laypeople the ability to correct their own vision.

Rose also managed corporate relations with other 300 generic pharmaceutical companies for Direct Relief International, one of the largest international medical aid organizations in the United States.
