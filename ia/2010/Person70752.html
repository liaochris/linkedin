Francesco Guerrieri’s Summary

I am a skilled online marketer with over six years work experience in the digital marketing arena. I started my career in the broader online marketing field, developing a long-term Search Engine Marketing (SEO and PPC), web advertising and email marketing strategy for Exodus Travels (part of TUI’s Adventure Travel division). By giving valuable insights gained through our web analytics tool, I also helped Exodus to make its website, www.exodus.co.uk, more community-centred.

I discovered a particular interest in email marketing and CRM management and thus decided to take the opportunity I was offered to work as Account Manager for Experian CheetahMail, one of the world’s leading Email Service Providers and part of Experian, the leading global information services company (www.experian.com).

Now based in Dubai, I have the great pleasure to consult the Emirates Group and develop a consistent and sound permission-based email marketing strategy worldwide for its business units, helping it to maximise its return on investment through targeted and relevant email messages.

On a more personal note: I am a very flexible person who easily adapts to different situations. I have a strong interest in foreign cultures - particularly Asian ones - and speak seven languages (Italian, German, English, Spanish, French, Japanese and Indonesian). I love travelling and cycling as well as playing piano and football, skiing and a number of other sports.
I am a self-motivator and keen to succeed in whatever I do.
