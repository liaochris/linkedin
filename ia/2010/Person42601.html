    (Construction industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Michuda Construction, Inc. is a full service General Contractor/Construction Manager based on the south-side of Chicago since 1919. Michuda specializes in constructing and renovating facilities for health care, educational and commercial clients. Michuda typically performs $60 Million construction volume each year and provides General Contracting, Construction Management, and Design/Build Services throughout the Midwest.

    Michuda has more than 40 experienced managers and staff in our Chicago office and between 20 and 75 skilled craftspeople depending on current workload. Michuda utilizes state-of-the-art project management and estimating software that assists our employees in building quality facilities that are completed on time and within budget for our clients. Michuda also has the flexibility to self-perform demolition, millwork, doors, frames, hardware, acoustical and drywall services.
