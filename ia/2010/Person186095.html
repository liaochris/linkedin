Semra Erden’s Summary

Eloquent U Marketing Solution is a boutique agency providing a cost effective virtual "in-house" services including creative, marketing solutions coupled with customer service, print buying and preferred vendor selection. Eloquent U Marketing Solutions agency also specializes in asset management for direct sales. Some of their services include consulting, photography art directing, brand identity development, package and print design, point of sale, environmental display structuring and outdoor advertising to name a few. Eloquent U Marketing Solutions connects the customer to a wide variety of vendors delivering exceptional products.
