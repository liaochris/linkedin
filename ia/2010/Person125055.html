Linda Alpert’s Summary

Senior broker-dealer/financial services attorney with in-depth expertise in sales practice, customer/product suitability, employment law and general securities compliance. Solutions-oriented self-starter with unique ability to communicate complex legal issues in simple terms and to enhance bottom-line results. Interested in advising firms and brokers on their legal and compliance obligations and on ways to reduce litigation risks and expenses.
