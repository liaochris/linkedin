ILSA, Inc.’s Summary

Our company's strength lies in the depth of our knowledge and dedication to providing personalized quality service.

Corporate Mission

Insurance Licensing Services of America, Inc.'s mission is to exceed client expectations. We seek to be a partner and resource to your licensing administrator whether it be for the world's largest insurance brokerage firm or a one person agency in Plantersville, Texas. ILSA looks forward to the opportunity to provide a high level of practical licensing experience, know-how and confidentiality to all clients.

Success will be measured by our clients choosing us because of their belief in our ability to meet and exceed their expectations of price, service, and expertise.

Keys to Success

Knowledgeable, friendly staff that can efficiently deal with clients' needs and circumstances, especially in handling emergency licensing situations.
Excellence in fulfilling our commitments. ILSA achieves this by providing reliable, trustworthy expertise and information.
Personal attention and quality service -- the cornerstone of our success.
