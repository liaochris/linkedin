Andrew Brooks’s Summary

Andrew Brooks is a Specialist Sales Manager for Advertiser Tools and Technologies, within Microsoft Advertising. Andrew directly manages new business growth and retention for this enterprise level technology, which is focused on integrating the complete management of digital marketing tools for direct marketers and advertising agencies.

Prior to his position as a Regional Sales Lead at Microsoft, Andrew lead National Sales for Mediaplex, a leading ad serving and digital marketing platform.

Andrew has over fifteen years experience in Internet marketing, sales and media delivering high-level consultancy and big-ticket sales success in over twenty-five countries worldwide.
