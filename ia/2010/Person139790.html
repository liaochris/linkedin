MediHelp International’s Summary

MediHelp International was established in 1998 inspired by the vision of being a model for answering the health-care needs of expatriates and local executives in Eastern Europe.


MediHelp International is the representative of reputable international and local Health and Medical Insurance companies.


MediHelp International activity in Insurance enables to supply a wide range of private Health Insurance solutions. One of them will answer the needs of your staff, family and your own.

Excellence in customer service is our strength and represents the key to our achievements and philosophy for future growth.
Perhaps this is the reason why MediHelp can be proud of around 90% loyalty portfolio.
