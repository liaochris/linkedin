Tom Lawrence’s Summary

I've run our business, TLC Business Supplies, since 1990 and this has given me experience in all sorts of areas which gives me a great understanding of small business.

TLC, based in Ripon, services not only the whole of Yorkshire, but also businesses across the UK. We have built our business on the belief that people come first: ‘people’ being you, our customers, who we work hard to get to know so that we can fully understand your business and its requirements.
TLC has a substantial 3000 sq ft premises, with a warehouse full of stationery essentials. We can make available next-day over 20,000 office products, literally everything you and your business needs.

As Regional Leader of 4Networking I meet and have got to know lots of fantastic business people across a wide spectrum of business. Therefore whatever your issues or whatever help you may need I probably can help you or recomend someone who can. Use me as a conduit to get the support you need.

TLC is the Official Stationery Supplier to 4Networking and its network of thousands of members.
