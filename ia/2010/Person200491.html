Dan Rosenfield, E.T., Ed.D.’s Summary

Dedicated to developing & delivering cost-effective, easy to understand & use working materials & programs that can improve your ROI & prevent or mitigate the effects of litigation pursued against your company & its stakeholders by an employee or a customer.

Your company’s prosperity is dependent on employees who represent you to your customers & safely operate & maintain its equipment & facility; and on your customers' safe & effective use of its products or services.

Employees will be more loyal & productive when they are prepared & equipped to achieve & maintain customer satisfaction; improved communications, accurate decisions and workflow; compliance with government & corporate policies & practices; quality practices leading to quality products & services; effective safety & health practices; consistent operations in & between shifts; increased capital equipment & facility uptime by preventing problems & rapidly restoring operations after a stoppage.

Your customers will be more loyal when they understand & are prepared to safely & effectively use your product(s) or services.

I specialize in developing & delivering, for your company & for your customers: understandable, integrated job & safety instructions, guides, & technical manuals; quality-program tools & materials; custom safety indicia (LOTO/PPE, etc.); mitigation materials to prevent or reduce litigation effects; documentation & retro-documentation of new and existing processes, procedures, facilities, with illustrations, photos, & text; visualization of P&ID; OSHA & industry compliance materials; flow-charts-maps of process, work-flow, critical-path, decision-making, organization; ISO audits & preparation for certification; document control; root-cause investigation & analysis; capture of corporate knowledge & wisdom; change-transition management; train the trainer; instructional design; job descriptions; technology & soft-skills training.
