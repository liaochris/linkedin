Philip Hunt’s Summary

After 10 years radio announcing in Queensland, I joined World Vision Australia in their communications dept. I was Communications and marketing manager for a couple of years, then CEO of World Vision Hong Kong for 4 years. Back in Oz I was CEO of World Vision Australia 89-96. Then Vice President of WV International for the Middle East and Eastern Europe until end of 2000. Two years consulting, mainly with senior management development. The five years General Manager of Deakin University Student Association (two organisation reinventions!). I took up the CEO role at VicRelief Foodbank in January 2008. I retired from full-time work in August 2010 to concentrate on research and writing.
