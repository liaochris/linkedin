Gregory Harris, m.NLP, CAMF’s Summary

Gregory Harris, m.NLP is an energetic Speaker with a powerful message for helping Individuals, Executives, Sales and Operations Teams reach their full potential. He is a Certified Anger Management Facilitator trained by the internationally recognized Anderson & Anderson Psychological Services. He is also trained in Neuro Linguistic Programming, Time Line Therapy & Hypnotherapy, by the largest NLP training organization on the planet, The Tad James Company.

Gregory started facilitating training seminars and presentations over 10 years ago. Traveling nationally, Gregory presented to over 10,000 high school students regarding options in choosing schools and funding their higher education through the “Making High School Count” and “Making College Count” programs. Gregory trained over 5000 adults across the country during this time in the disciplines of soft skills, such as Communication Skills, Time Management Skills, Customer Service Skills, Managing Multiple Projects, Goal Setting, Meeting Deadlines, Achieving Objectives, Personality Styles, Outstanding Receptionist, Conflict Resolution and First Time Supervisor Seminars.

There are, without a doubt, different schools of thought in reference to the field of Personal Development. Neuro Linguistic Programming, in essence, teaches us more than just behavioral management. It trains us in how to reprogram our thinking, restructure our communication and anchor empowering behaviors. The spectrum is broader than its name. These strategies can be tailored to any situation involving human behavior and interactions.
