Dan Roder’s Summary

Highly motivated, quality minded ETL, Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence developer. Mr. Roder has thirteen years of ETL experience with a wide variety of other programming skills and languages on an assortment of hardware platforms including eight years of expert Informatica PowerCenter skills. Background includes successful implementation of marketing and financial Data Warehouses, payroll, Human Resource, OLTP and Operational Data Store systems in wide variety of business including telecommunications, pharmaceutical, agribusiness, healthcare, publishing, banking and defense. Mr. Roder combines a superior work ethic with excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Dan Roder’s Specialties:

Marketing, operational and financial Data Warehousing, payroll and human resource, OLTP and Operational Data Store systems in industries such as telecommunications, pharmaceutical, agribusiness, healthcare, publishing, banking and defense.
