Molly Nichelson’s Summary

My family have asked me many times, "OK, so what do you really do for a living?" "I help people advocate for themselves on Capitol Hill," is what I tell them.

And yes, grassroots advocacy has been my career and my passion for over 10 years. Call me a social media geek. I love social media. Figuring out better and more creative ways to reach members of Congress and their staff is what moves me.

To see a few of my presentations, go to my SlideShare page: http://www.slideshare.net/mollynichelson
