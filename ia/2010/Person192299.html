Mike Spencer’s Summary

I have worked in the Printing Industry for 12 years, as a Sales Executive for various printers. In January 2008 I decided to put my acquired knowledge to better use and formed my own Printing Agency. I am now able to place a wide range of print, with the most suited printer, at the best available price. I have clients all over the UK, whilst the Giclee Print service is International, including clients in Jamaica & Canada.

I also actively promote ethical issues like Global Warming, Animal Welfare, & spiritual matters. Please feel free to view my profile on an ethical web site I belong to at
http://www.pledgingforchange.com/profile/MikeSpencer

I am now developing a new & exciting service called Talking Product Videos.

I can use a photograph of any character, product, or animal & make them talk on videos.

As well as promoting products on a home page, or video hosting sites, you can also send these in emailed newsletters, using a powerful email video marketing tool called "Attain Response".

The videos can also be viewed on mobile phones. I am forming a close alliance with a wireless mobile phone content provider which will see further developments of the service. This will for instance, allow a retail store to send out my video adverts to any potential shoppers mobile phones, within a 100 metre radius of the store. The company have their services in many shopping malls & are also looking to place both our services in major soccer grounds.

For further information, see the PDF guide in the "Talking Product Video" directory in my Boxnet files.
