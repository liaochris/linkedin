John Ibrahim’s Summary

John Ibrahim just finished his Masters of Business Administration (MBA) at California State University, Los Angeles with an option is Finance. He graduated with a Bachelor’s of Arts in Economics and minor in Business Administration from the American University in Cairo. He studied International Economics and Sustainable Development at the University of East London.

Ibrahim received the 2007 – 2008 California State University Alumni Association Superior and Distinguished Scholarship, Leadership, and Academic Achievement award.

Ibrahim has pioneered the initiation of many clubs and social organizations. He launched Glory of Egypt, GO Egypt, a university club activity aiming at helping people to know his country to value and help it to excel. He presided over the club for one academic year. Also, he volunteered at China Town Service Center to prepare income taxes for low income people. He is Internal Revenue Service (IRS) and California Franchise Tax Board certified. In addition, he volunteered at California State University Tutorial Center to present time management and test taking skills presentations.

Currently, Ibrahim is working as a research coordinator with a group of rheumatologists from University of Southern California (USC) Keck School of Medicine and University of Southern California/ LAC + USC Medical Center. As of today, the research group has four outstanding abstracts and poster presentations.

Ibrahim has been awarded many certificates of honors and merit for his outstanding achievement and contribution in several occasions. He received a certificate of achievement from the Desert Development Center where he interned in Summer 2004 and prepared an intensive marketing plan to market the center green produces. He was awarded a certificate of achievement from National Société Générale Bank and a certificate of merit for his dedication and contribution to the success of the 17th Cairo International Model United Nations.
