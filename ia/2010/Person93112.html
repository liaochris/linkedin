Jeff Dorsey’s Summary

After 5.5 years of working as a marketing representative for Ticor Title Company, I redirected my ambitions in 2007 to pursue my dream of working for an advertising firm. Through my time with Ticor Title I learned how to maneuver within a corporate atmosphere and how to deliver the utmost quality of service for the clients I served.

I now serve as account executive/project manager for Guerrilla Design + Advertising, a local advertising firm located in Bakersfield, CA and serve an extensive list of clients in Kern County as well as the surrounding areas including Santa Barbara, Santa Clarita and Los Angeles. With a company called Guerrilla, I was a perfect fit for bringing a corporate touch to alternative marketing and advertising. Plus, I no longer have to wear a tie!
