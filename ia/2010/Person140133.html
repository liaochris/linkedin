    The wedding industry continues to be a growth industry. Currently in the United States, there are approximately 2.5 million weddings annually, with an average budget of $28,000- thus totaling $70 billion being spent annually. While the wedding industry is, to a degree, recession proof, many vendors are seeing a more conscious and creative effort on the part of the brides, grooms and families to keep costs down. With this in mind, it’s vital that wedding vendors are doing everything they can to get the business.
