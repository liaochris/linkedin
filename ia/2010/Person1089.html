Aaron J. Stanley’s Summary

After graduating in from the University at Buffalo with a Masters of Business Administration, Aaron had a yearning for an entrepreneurial opportunity. In 2005, Aaron sought out a career in the real estate industry. Through his very own home purchase process, he noticed some key items that could use improvements to make the whole transaction a much more comfortable experience.

After being licensed as a salesperson for two years at a large broker in our area, Aaron came upon an opportunity at a moderately sized real estate brokerage. Here, he could help make improvements to their business model. During his tenure as Business Systems Analyst there, Aaron had helped the brokerage make several strides toward improving their marketing efforts as well as the increasing the utilization of the existing technology that was available to them at the time.

In 2008, another opportunity came knocking. This time it was at another, yet smaller real estate brokerage. Here, Aaron was given the opportunity to implement a new agent mentoring program. In addition, Aaron helped develop a marketing program that fit with the corporate philosophy of the organization.

All the while, as Aaron made process improvement suggestions at the various real estate brokerages, he had a desire to develop the idea of introducing energy efficiency concepts to the real estate industry. Feel free to contact Aaron at any time, by e-mail at aaron@greenhomerevolution.com or by phone at (716) 430-8036.
