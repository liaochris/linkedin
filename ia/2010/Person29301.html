  • President

    Turfbuilder Marketing, LLC

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2002 — Present (8 years 7 months)

    Founded in 2002 by Brigitte Casemyr, Turfbuilder Marketing builds on her rich marketing experience from both individual and executive positions at Fortune 500 companies and start-ups to develop lead generation programs in close cooperation with clients’ management and sales teams.

    Creativity and resourcefulness combined with in-depth social media and inbound marketing knowledge with strong strategic planning skills help bring prospects into clients’ sales pipeline. The firm is a strong advocate for lead nurturing and client retention programs, and knows how to adapt proven techniques to each client’s unique value proposition. We work with companies in many diverse industries, from technology companies, to accounting firms, service providers, non-profit organizations, retail customers and management consulting companies. No two businesses are alike, and each program is tailored to the strength of each client business, to build on successes and avoid misdirected efforts.

She is a past member of the Board of Directors of the Corridor Nine Chamber, past member of the Ad-Hoc Master Plan Committee and was awarded Small Business of the Year in 2006 for her numerous contributions to the Chamber’s initiatives.
