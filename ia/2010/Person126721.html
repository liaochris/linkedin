Elizabeth Burns’s Summary

I am currently an Assistant Manager at American Eagle Outfitters in the College Trek Program. I have been so impressed with what AE has been doing in fashion and also in the community since I started with the company. I look forward to discovering where I will land after my one year training program is completed in May.

My work experience has been in two primary fields: Retail and Education. I have worked at American Eagle, Banana Republic, Gap and Dillard's over the past five years. I discovered new strengths while working for each of these four retailers. Though I went to college and grad school to pursue business and retail, I have also enjoyed my teaching and tutoring experience along the way. I have taught the lab portion of a junior-level retail course, graded Spanish assignments and tests for elementary and intermediate level Spanish college courses, and also tutored in mathematics for several years during high school.

Currently, my main focus in work and in life is to have the opportunity to maximize my strengths daily. I love working one-on-one with people. I enjoy achieving my list of tasks every day. I coordinate everything from researching to analyzing to organizing and finally to planning. Teaching and training others makes my day. Finally, I have to stretch myself mentally to feel like I've truly accomplished something.
