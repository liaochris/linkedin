Hillary Bressler’s Summary

.Com Marketing, Inc. Founder and President Hillary S. Bressler, is a recognized authority on interactive marketing and one of the nations’s online marketing pioneers. Her leading full service interactive advertising agency specializes in driving motivated and sales traffic to Web sites.

Bressler established .Com Marketing (www.commarketing.com) in April 1997 after identifying the need for Web sites to be marketed. Her interactive agency has serves such notable clients as MP3.com, Nestle Foods, Marriott & Renaissance Offshore Resorts, Marriott Vacation Club, Tribune Interactive, Universal Orlando and SeaWorld. .Com Marketing has received numerous awards, most notably, a Web Marketing Association Silver Award for Excellence, HSMAI Golden Click Award, ADDY award, eTravel World Award and awards for excellence from the Web Awards

Under Bressler’s leadership, .Com Marketing has enjoyed robust growth and is today ranked among the nation’s top 100 interactive agencies, according to Ad Age Agency Interactive Agency Report (and one of two Florida companies on the list, and one of four in the southeast). Bressler attributes her success to “applying stable, proven old economy tactics to the new economy paradigm.” These tactics include a diverse client base, methodical investment in staff and leading-edge technology, and a focused service approach.
