Kurt Wondra’s Summary

My young career has taken me through many different realms of Information and Computer Technology. I began my career with a stronger focus on the hardware side of the industry (troubleshooting, installing, upgrading, etc), Soon thereafter, I would start doing many of those same tasks as my own side business (KW Technologies). As my career progressed I was able to acquire knowledge in personal and business networks, system builds/configurations, and the general corporate America environment. Moving forward, my career began a push towards software design and development. Working with data from a repository (typically a database), manipulating it in code, and then presenting it in a user front-end (Windows Apps, Silverlight Apps, ASP.NET websites, etc) have been the areas of focus in my present and recent past.

I am incredibly passionate about the field of Information and Computer Technology and I know that the years ahead of me are only loaded with potential. In my future, I hope to earn some form of a leadership position on my way to becoming a manager. Moreover, later in my career, I hope to earn the opportunity to shine as a Chief Information Officer. Nevertheless, for now I will focus on increasing my knowledge base, gaining as much experience as I can, and taking any steps I can to advance in my career.
