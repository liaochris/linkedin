Ingrid van der Locht’s Summary

Most workers waste on average 1-2 hours per day looking for misplaced or lost information. With the onset of the technology and information age, it is vital to be efficient and organized with your data and time. I will help employees develop systems so they feel in control of their workday and increase their personal productivity. Through a hands-on, customized approach, I work with individuals to transform their organizational habits can so they can be maintained for a lifetime.
