Ray Knight’s Summary

There are many rainmakers in the field of business development and marketing, but there is only one Ray Knight—the Raynmaker.

Ray Knight is the founder and managing principal of The RaynMakers, a Florida Corporation—a unique organization of trusted advisors that deliver not just service but long-lasting marketing and sales capabilities the client can use long after The RaynMakers are gone.

The RaynMakers exists to help clients set and achieve their top-line revenue goals by walking in their shoes, developing practical plans, then making them happen.

The RaynMakers help our clients find the answers to these 3 questions about their business:

1) How can we sell more-increase volume?

2) How can we sell smarter with a more effective sales process?

3) How can we sell differently: sell something different, sell in new/different places?

Ray is a marketer’s marketer and a sales executive’s salesperson. He leads a unique team of client-focused business developers, marketing experts and creative communicators who work both strategically and tactically to get results when others can’t—or simply won’t. For small-, medium- enterprises, Ray and The RaynMakers team help businesses grow and prosper.

The RaynMakers is the FIRST and only for-profit, WomenCertified business development agency in the United States.

Mr. Knight relies on 25 years successful, diverse and progressive experience in market positioning, marketing strategies and product planning as he guides client’s businesses to new levels of market share and profit growth.

Ray is educated and trained as a bi-lingual (English/Spanish) businessman. He earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from Georgetown University in Washington, D.C. and an associate of arts degree from the College of William and Mary in Petersburg, Virginia.
