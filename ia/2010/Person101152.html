Joe Rolfe’s Summary

I develop software for high performance systems ranging from device drivers to middleware to proof of concept/customer acceptance applications. Previously I spent a significant amount of time developing board to system level stress tests and diagnostics. I have a solid understanding of the hardware/software interface level and excellent system level debug skills.

Over the past several years I have also been involved in the real estate industry for which I hold a active Massachusetts Sales License. Other real estate training includes residential/commercial appraisals, foreclosures, preforeclosure consulting and mortgage origination.

Real estate investor seeking 5+ unit multifamily properties and partnerships anywhere in the continental US.
