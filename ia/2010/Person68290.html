Evonne Ryan’s Summary

In March 2010 with the release of STEPPPING STONES TO SUCCESS (a book with Deepak Chopra, Jack Canfield and Denis Waitley), Evonne is able to help people understand the scope of her work over the last five years. Building on her background as a teacher, lobbyist, speaker, Emmy award winning television producer, and financial radio commentator, Evonne Ryan has dedicated the last five years of her life to creating tools (URSLA(tm) and the WHEN-Score (tm)) and a certification (the Certified Financial Coach(tm) to empower individuals in their financial life. In the financial field she developed a system to guide coaches, advisors, and financial planners to work with clients to redesign their relationship to money and wealth. From 1990-2008 she served as a highly respected Financial Securities Advisor, Evonne always felt that something was missing from the typical financial conversations. In 2008 she dedicated her full energies to building WEALTH STRONG. She knew there should be more to the relationship between advisor and client than just helping a person decide how to invest his money or how to create her tax shelter. She knew that people first needed to understand their relationship to money and wealth and, second, needed tools to powerfully create the future they want to live into before they decide how to invest, how to save, and even how to earn their living. She developed new models with powerful results - she knew that she had to bring an extraordinary set of tools to the world of financial advice. Evonne recently reinstated her Registered Securities Principal series 24, 7, 63, 65, and insurance licenses. She is trained as an Accredited Investment Fiduciary®, Certified Financial Coach(tm), and Mediation/Conflict Management Specialist. She has lived her life as a contribution to others throughout her career as a published author of “Changing More Than the Channel”, co-founded financial planning firms dedicated to helping people in their life transitions.
