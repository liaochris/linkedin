  • Co-Founder & CEO

    SkinnyScoop

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    August 2009 — Present (11 months)

    SkinnyScoop (www.skinnyscoop.com) is an easy and fun online surveying tool that provides a sanity check to busy moms. Its where moms get the skinny and share the scoop on products, parenting or more personal topics. “Mom Mavens” who are already answering questions from the center of vast mom networks are the cornerstones of theSkinnyScoop’s growth strategy. theSkinnyScoop makes it easier for these moms to share their experiences and perspectives with their existing networks and helps them to track, understand and expand their circle of influence. With the Mom Mavens serving as the initial foundation, theSkinnyScoop will leverage existing networks that women have already built via Facebook, Twitter, blogs or other websites to drive user adoption cost-effectively.
