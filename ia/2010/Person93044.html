Jeff Justice’s Summary

Jeff’s career began as Producer/Director for Arisen Corporation's Alive Today television program which aired globally on the Inspirational Network & continued as On-air Announcer & Production Technician at the Arisen's 7-Station FM Satellite Network. After 4 years with Arisen, Jeff returned to his beloved hometown of Orlando, Florida.

There Jeff launched Godspeed Communications International, a Freelance Media & Technology group. GCI grew quickly to provide IT, Production & Multimedia integration services to small businesses, large corporations, non-profit organizations & government agencies. Jeff managed the development of a national live production company & with the Godspeed team directed productions for the Department of Defense, provided multimedia design for several Fortune 500 companies & designed & managed the construction of a multi-use production/demonstration facility for Altec Lansing Technologies.

While at the helm of GCI, Jeff accepted the position of Director of Technical Services at St. Luke's United Methodist Church. There he managed all IT & Production personnel, providing IT & Audiovisual support along with other Technical services to St. Luke's Church & School of the Arts campuses.

In 2004, Jeff joined Dynetech Corporation, the world's #1 Business Process Outsource provider, as Multimedia Specialist where he received the Circle of Excellence award for his exemplary work in the Sales Division's IT & Audio-Visual Departments.

In 2006 Jeff served as Compass Knowledge Group's Outreach Support Specialist where he managed IT, audiovisual and peripheral technical support, repair and maintenance for all Outreach teams.

Today Jeff melds his years of diverse experience with distinct & innovative approaches to bring relevant & powerful Information Technology concepts to clients worldwide as a Freelance IT Consultant & Microsoft PowerPoint Expert.
