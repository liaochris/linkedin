Joseph Morin’s Summary

Newest project: launching Fly.com (backed by Travelzoo, Inc. Nasdaq:TZOO), also advising startups and organizing internet marketing conferences.

Previously Co-founded Storybids, Inc., a venture capital funded Web 2.0 start-up focusing on online video product placement and advertising. A seasoned Internet advertising and marketing executive and heavily involved in online marketing. Storybids was acquired by IDT Media Group of Irvine, CA in October of 2009. Currently on the Board of Directors of IDT Media Group.

Serves on the Board of Directors of Boost Search Marketing, a Southern California based international marketing consulting firm,specializing in enterprise level search optimization for large scale and global web properties. Formerly Founder and Managing Partner.

Has worked with many companies on their online marketing strategies including Austin Ventures, Autobytel, Inc., Azure Capital, Business.com, Clearstone Venture Capital, CreditCards.com, Dentist.net, Education.com, Enterprise Rent-A-Car, Microsoft, MGM Mirage, MRM Worldwide (div. of Interpublic Group of Cos), Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino, Travelzoo, Tribune Interactive, UsedCars.com and World Publications.

Served as Vice President of Strategic Marketing for UsedCars.com where as a principal, was instrumental in the sale of the company to Cobalt Automotive Group in a multi-million dollar cash transaction. Previously was Strategic Marketing Director for Autobytel, Inc., the nation's largest online new car buying service for a two year period and pioneered their search marketing program.

A featured speaker at Internet Marketing conferences around the world having spoken at conferences in New York, Chicago, Los Angeles, Miami, Seattle, Las Vegas, San Jose, Toronto, Stockholm, London, Tokyo and Sao Paulo.

Organizer and Evangelist for the PubCon series of Internet Marketing Conferences by WebmasterWorld
