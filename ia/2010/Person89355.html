Janet Washburn’s Experience

  • Long Term Care Specialist

    LTC Financial Partners

    (Partnership; Insurance industry)

    November 2009 — Present (8 months)

    Since my insurance career began in 1997, I've always assisted my clients or trained my agents in Long Term Care.

    The reality is that 7 out of 10 people will need care during their lifetime. Since no other programs cover the costs of Long Term Care, we risk being a burden on our families, physically and emotionally, plus risk having to compromise the wealth we've built. Long Term Care costs are so substantial that many times the healthy, surviving spouse is left without sufficient income and assets.

    Becoming a dependent on others, financially or emotionally and physically, is not what anyone intends for their future, myself included. I know from personal experience, as a daughter, the devastating physical, psychological, and financial realities of the promise I made to care for my mother. This is why my singular focus is on Long Term Care Planning, assisting others in making intelligent choices about protecting assets and families.
