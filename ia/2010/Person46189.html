Damien Patton’s Summary

Damien Patton is a successful serial entrepreneur who's latest venture is the Boston based search engine and social media company, Sixth Sense Strategies.

In addition to Sixth Sense, Damien Patton is the Founder and active CEO of FindAnyFloor.com, an online lead generation and information website for the flooring industry. Previously, Damien created and managed Pacific Imports International (PII). PII started as a distributor of hardwood and bamboo flooring, and expanded their footprint into a large contract manufacturer, with offices in Las Vegas, Hawaii and China.

In addition, Damien has owned and operated several other successful businesses in the construction, technology and consulting industries. Damien graduated magna cum laude with a B.S. in Finance, and, at only age thirty-seven, he has achieved excellence in several industries. After serving in the military during Desert Storm, Damien had a successful career in NASCAR as the Chief Mechanic for the Lowe’s Racing Team, as well as serving on the Pit Crew (jackman) for several other top NASCAR teams. As if his endeavors have not been exciting enough, he also served as a Crime Scene Investigator in Davidson, North Carolina, while working full time in the role of COO for a technology company.

One of Damien’s passions is to be certain that websites including FindAnyFloor.com are accessible to those with disabilities. Many websites do not easily accommodate individuals with disabilities. Damien’s goal is to ensure that everyone, regardless of their ability, can make full use of the site, with the hope that other web sites will follow suit.

FindAnyFloor.com and Sixth Sense Strategies are proud to be carbon neutral companies.
