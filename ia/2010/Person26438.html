Brad Johnson’s Summary

Upon graduating in '89 with B.A. in Computer Information Sciences, joined Wal-Mart's Information Systems Division. Held programming, systems analysis, and project management positions, supporting various HR, .com, intranet/portal, and real estate systems throughout 18 year career.

Sunrider International has been in my life for the last 8 years. My overall well-being and lifestyle have been tremendously increased; first by their exclusive product lines helping to improve/maintain my health and active lifestyle. The Sunrider business and compensation plan has provided increased income, and allowed me to leave corporate America to further pursue the unlimited income potential full-time for the last year.

I now spend my workdays looking for and mentoring other like-minded individuals who have their own entrepreneurial dreams. Whether at home or on the road getting to see new places, we always enjoy helping people help themselves.
