Chia-Lin Simmons’s Summary

A proven Marketing and Business Development Executive with 14+ years of experience in digital media and consumer mobile / web / media sector(s) with expertise in multi-platform distribution / licensing, business development and marketing for companies such as Time Warner, Amazon, Wired, etc.

Highlights

Multi – platform Distribution / Licensing: Drove third-party distribution / licensing revenue for media companies. Proposed, negotiated, developed and managed wireless, retail and emerging technology strategies for distributing content for Random House, Wall Street Journal, New York Times, etc. Negotiated and closed distribution of content through mobile handset, wireless carriers, satellite / cable, retail and emerging technology companies.

Mobile / Wireless: Launched and led all strategy, licensing, marketing, business development and product development for the purchase, distribution and playback of media via mobile handsets, wireless carriers and telematics.

Strategic and Brand Marketing: Developed marketing propositions, brand creation, brand awareness, and PR. Purchased advertising and managed large-scale promotional and advertising initiatives.

Physical / Online Retail: Negotiated and closed partnerships with retailers such as Target, Best Buy, etc. Managed marketing acquisition programs including in-store product placement, and advertising. Experience in creating synergetic marketing programs tying physical marketing assets with digital media assets to maximize sales and distribution of content.

International: Negotiated and closed global distribution partnerships. Created and implemented international marketing programs in the UK, Germany and France. Experience in global expansion strategies and other international strategies affecting media companies.
