Tammi Franke’s Summary

I'm a business attorney with business experience. My style of legal counseling reflects this experience. I know how hard it is to build a business and I won't waste your time or your money. As an entrepreneur and former in-house attorney, I understand the mechanics (product development, sales, marketing, operations and budgeting) of running a business and the need to develop long-term strategies for maintaining and growing a competitive business. As a client, you want options and solutions. You don’t want a laundry list of potential legal problems and barriers.

At my firm, Fitzgerald + Hewes, our services typically include:

• incorporation
• angel & venture financing transactions
• copyright and trademark counseling
• licensing
• employment & human resource matters
• stock option plans
• standard contract preparation
• vendor contract negotiations

Tammi Franke’s Specialties:

Business law, digital media law, communication law, copyright law, licensing, software licensing, equipment leasing, business continuity/disaster recovery, trademarks, technology, start-ups.
