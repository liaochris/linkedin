Jennifer Polk’s Summary

I am the Director of BU Social Strategy at Sears Holdings. In this role, I focus on developing and leading social strategies for Sears Holdings operating business units ensuring alignment between specific social programs and business unit objectives and promoting ongoing partnership and coordinated efforts between Social Media, Online and Marketing.

Prior to this Jennifer served as Chief of Staff to IT. Since joining Sears Holdings Jennifer has held the position of Brand Manager of Kenmore Cooking, participated in strategic planning and development of the Kenmore Brand and led a key business initiative for the $400 million DieHard brand and assisted with formation of the $9 billion Brand Business Unit.

Strategic planning; social media and communication strategy; idea generation; research and analysis; sales and marketing; structuring and execution; relationship management; verbal and written communications; financial modeling and forecasting; project management; organization skills; and attention to detail.
