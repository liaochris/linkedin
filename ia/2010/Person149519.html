Jill Waldron’s Summary

We provide Intelligent Staffing Solutions for the Global Wireless Industry. Our clients include companies designing, manufacturing, building, and selling innovative wireless communications and infrastructure integration technology. These companies focus on components and systems that provide - as either manufactured products or services - the delivery of voice, data, audio, or video signals.


Our focus is on the RF communications industry, providing both career opportunities and talent placement services

If you are involved in RF infrastructure - whether in product engineering or executive management and your background includes skill and experience with wireless hardware, cellular devices, and/or RF communication systems - and seek to advance your career then reach out to us today.
