    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    September 2007 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    Rudyard Partners LLC is the 2.0 version of private equity. In short, we are a specialized venture capital firm focused on investing in consumer technology opportunities for the global marketplace. Our passion is the creation, rediscovery, and deployment of many forms of technology with ubiquitous network access. We advise our companies to think beyond the device and service provider so that consumer experience becomes the apex of consideration. Our goal is to provide our companies with access to capital, ‘venture-design’ development, and unique commercial opportunities for their new discoveries. Though we seldom invest in ‘bleeding-edge’ technologies, we are aggressively pursuing ‘cutting-edge’ entrepreneurs that have consumer-targeted businesses and compelling market solutions. As a result of our focus, we reduce the time to launch strong businesses and their corresponding funding needs.
