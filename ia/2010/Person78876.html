Sumit Gupta’s Summary

I am a technologist and strategist and have over 12 years of experience in web technology startups. My technology experience ranges from deep understanding of social media, social gaming, web 2.0, consumer behavior patterns, web analytics, internet fraud and security and retail applications. Find below my notable experiences.

Presently I am the CEO and Founder of BitRhymes, a social media startup focused on developing engaging social dating and casual gaming apps on popular social networks and smart phones. BitRhymes already boasts of apps with more than 12 million users and growing fast, signing up more than 100k users a day. Previous to BitRhymes, I was VP Strategy and Business Development at Monetize Media, the first internet TV platform with video intelligence.

Previous to that, I was founding team member at Business Signatures and played a key role in every aspect of the business from product strategy and solutions, sales strategy and hiring the team, eventually to a successful acquisition by Entrust in 2006. Business Signatures provided real time fraud detection and prevention capabilities to internet banking using patented activity corelation, behavior analysis and access pattern analysis on HTTP data stream.

Prior to Business Signatures, I was an early employee at Escalate and Oracle CRM division.
