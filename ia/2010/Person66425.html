Eric Simone’s Summary

Currently Founder/CEO of ClearBlade LLC. ClearBlade is a consulting company focused on Enterprise Modernization utilizing IBM technologies. Specifically, ClearBlade focuses on IBM's Enterprise Generation Language (EGL) and Rational suite of software products.

Serial entrepreneur specializing in technology and retail industries. Active venture capital and angel investor. Key investments include:

- Bazaarvoice - www.bazaarvoice.com
- Bigfoot Networks - www.bigfootnetworks.com
- Boundless Networks - www.boundlessnetworks.com
- Gamecock Media - www.gamecockmedia.com
- Montecito - www.montecitocorporation.com
- Perception Software - www.perceptionsoftware.com
- Robot Genius - www.robotgenius.net
- uControl - www.ucontrol.com

Founded Girl Next Door, Inc. (www.girlnd.com) with his wife Toni. A women's clothing boutique located in central Austin, Texas.

Founded Compete, Inc. A IBM software based consulting company. Sold to Perficient, Inc. in May 2000 for $60MM.

Eric Simone has over twenty years of experience in the computer industry. He founded Compete, Inc. in 1994 along with two other IBM colleagues. Compete focused on providing consulting services for IBM’s WebSphere brand of software products. While Eric served as President and CTO, Compete grew to over $12MM in revenues, and was sold to Perficient, Inc. (PRFT NASDAQ) for $60MM in May 2000. The acquisition of Compete introduced the IBM relationship to Perficient, a core component of their business today. Mr. Simone served as a Director of Perficient for three years before founding Girl Next Door (www.girlnd.com) in Austin, TX with his wife Toni. He founded ClearBlade in 2007. Mr. Simone graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science from Purdue University in 1988.

    ClearBlade

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 2007 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    ClearBlade provides Enterprise Modernization services to companies looking to build solutions that leverage their existing legacy applications. By partnering with IBM, and utilizing IBM’s best of breed Rational suite of software products, ClearBlade provides focused expertise to solve these complex problems.

    Specifically, ClearBlade concentrates it’s talents on IBM’s Enterprise Generation Language (EGL). EGL is a procedural language that allows businesses to integrate with their legacy applications, achieve platform independence, and develop new enterprise applications with SOA and Web 2.0 functionality. This allows developers to focus on creating business logic instead of spending valuable time coding platform specific, low-level implementation logic.
