  • Penciller

    Revolutionary Comics

    (Publishing industry)

    January 1994 — January 1995 (1 year 1 month)

    These were one of my earliest gigs. I drew the Dave Winfield issue of Sports All-Stars, Issue #63 of Rock N Roll Comics: The Janis Joplin Story and my art was also used for issue #64: 60s San Francisco mostly because I drew a couple of VW Beetles in the background which other artists neglected to include in their period pieces. As soon as Jay Allen saw the Beetles he wrote me to say how it struck him that it was such an integral part of the visual scene of the late 60s but it never occured to him that it was missing until he saw my Joplin pages. That's the kind of attention to details that I bring to all my work and that's what made this such a great experience.
