John Southee’s Summary

T-shirt Designer:

I am the owner and t-shirt designer for J. Benjamin Clothing Co. The goal for the company is to inspire and motivate others who wear or are seen wearing the t-shirts to follow their own dreams and passions. Retail Sales and Management:

I have worked in my favorite industry for over 10+ years, clothing sales. I have worked in regular hourly sales as well client based sales. I know how to drive traffic and how to develop a loyal client base. I love building wardrobes for people who need help with styling.
