JuanPablo Martinez-Blat’s Summary

Juan Pablo Martinez-Blat is a business and financial expert and CEO/founder of Onne Consulting Partners a consultancy firm based in Miami, Florida that specializes in supporting European, North and Latin American families and their Family Offices by helping them keep integrity, coherence and focus on their financial, investment and operational processes.

He is a bilingual executive with experience in challenging environments in the US and overseas. Mr. Martinez-Blat has deep understanding of global and corporate finance, business valuation, financial forecasting, investments, Real Estate and Family Office operations. He has been involved in multiple international transactions and operations in countries such as Turkey, Italy, Brazil, Venezuela and Colombia.

Mr. Juan Pablo Martinez-Blat has over 20 years of experience in finance and general management matters; he managed a Family Office for a large Latin American family and has been a key player on several start-ups. He has held top management positions at Latin American and US Multinational companies including a $340m+ IT outsourcing Joint Venture and a Fortune 500 company. He has also acted as a Trusted Advisor and a consultant to Latin and North American families having an oversight or advisory role on their investments, real estate, financial matters and philanthropic activities.

Mr. Martinez-Blat is a Civil Engineer and holds a MBA degree from a joint program between the University of North Carolina and the IESA. He has also taught as a professor at an MBA program.

JuanPablo Martinez-Blat’s Specialties:

General Management. Financial Forecasting and Investment Analysis. Extensive Investment Banking Relations. Due Diligence, Deal Structuring and Negotiations. Real Estate Investment & Construction Projects.
