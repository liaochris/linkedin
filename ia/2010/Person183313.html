Satish K Amara’s Summary

Extensive experience in software research, architecture, design and development in Networking, protocols, OS, Embedded Systems and router areas.

Earlier worked as Software R & D engineer for Cisco Systems developing networking protocols and security software for multi-core routers.

Earlier worked as Staff R&D Software Engineer in Juniper Networks on Security products for SSL-VPN and IPSEC-VPN areas.

Also worked as Software Design Engineer for Microsoft on MSMQ and IPV6 support for MSMQ.

I was Staff R&D Software Engineer at 3COM. Worked on MPLS, QOS, IPSEC, VPN, NAT/PAT and Firewalls. Filed multiple patents on various topics in networking. Involved in RFC drafts and IETF activities.

Previously worked for SUN Microsystems as consultant for Java OS and JVM and JavaStation

Worked in Wipro Infotech Global R & D as Senior Software engineer on communication products (LTS) developing networking protocols and maintained TCP/IP stack.

Additionally, holds M.Tech Computer Science & Engineering from IIT Kanpur India and B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering from Nagarjuna University
