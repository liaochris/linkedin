Rachel Assuncao’s Summary

My passion for healthy cooking coupled with my training from the Institute for Integrative Nutrition allow me to assist my clients to create an achievable plan for uncovering total health and balance through simple and easy to implement dietary and lifestyle changes.

I work with my clients in ways that are beyond simply watching what you eat, it's about balancing the way you treat, feed, and exercise your mind, spirit, emotions and body. As well as working to resolve the problems that you may see, sense, or feel in your life – our approach of holistic health focuses on creating a whole-body, whole-mind approach to optimizing your well being.

Rachel Assuncao’s Specialties:

health coaching, meal planning, healthy cooking lessons, weight loss, adopting a healthy diet
