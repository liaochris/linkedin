    Queen of the Quick Meal, LLC

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    June 2009 — Present (10 months)

    QQM is a recipe development and meal idea company that offers answers to dinnertime dilemmas. Utilizing everyday ingredients and an ingredient searchable database of original recipes, a free e-book, personal replies, plus a store with simple seasoning solutions, QQM provides assistance in creating fast meals from the food on hand. QQM believes in putting healthy, family-friendly, home-style dinners on the table in 20 minutes (or less).

  • Teacher

    Brave Writer

    (E-Learning industry)

    2007 — Present (3 years )

    As a teacher with Brave Writer, I have the privilege to share in kids’ passions, interests and enthusiasm. In teaching the Kidswrite Basic Class, we have six weeks to tap into the words hidden inside each student, to encourage them to realize that they do have important things to share and say, while retaining authorship and ownership of their work.

    Working for Brave Writer, I’ve also had the opportunity to create and teach the Nature Journaling course, part of the One Thing series. Capturing the beauty of the natural world in simple sketch and word is something that has helped provide balance in my own life and being able to share it with others has been deeply fulfilling. I also created the 4-week Hand Holders class that offers homeschooling parents the opportunity to gain support in helping their kids take one nonfiction piece of work from initial idea through to its completion.
