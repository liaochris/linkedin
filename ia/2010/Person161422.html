Paul Simino’s Summary

oPaul J. Simino, founder & President of OneSimpleLoan®. Since the establishment of OneSimpleLoan, a national student loan finance & consulting firm, in 2003, Mr. Simino has been an active member of various community & industry organizations, Upper Tampa Bay Chamber of Commerce as a Trustee, & the National Council of Higher Education Loan Programs (NCHELP). A staunch advocate of financial literacy, Mr. Simino has been appointed to NCHELP's Debt Management, Private Loan & Financial Literacy committees, as well as serves on the Board of Governors for the Florida Chamber of Commerce & the Florida Education Foundation, chairing its Scholarship subcommittee.At OneSimpleLoan, Mr. Simino initiated a rigorous training program that provides student borrowers with accurate information about their rights & privileges, contributing to the company’s rapid growth & emergence as a dynamic player in the student loan industry.In recognition of his business acumen & commitment to serving as an active, responsible corporate citizen, Mr. Simino has been bestowed 2007 Business of the Year honors by the Upper Tampa Bay Chamber of Commerce & nominated as an Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year finalist within the state of Florida. Prior to establishing OSL, Mr. Simino founded ALU Promotions, a very successful travel promotions & marketing company within the timeshare industry. At ALU, he built the company to a 150-person staff. When the travel industry experienced a dramatic downturn due to the events of 9/11, Mr. Simino overcame this adversity by redirecting his marketing abilities to his present company. Before going out on his own, Mr. Simino rose through the ranks of GTE Directories to become one of the company’s top producers as Advertising Sales Executive. An alumnus of Jesuit High School of Tampa, Florida, Mr. Simino graduated with top honors from Nova Southeastern University with an Associate’s Degree in Business Management and holds a life, health and variable annuities license.

Paul Simino’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    Financial Education Services, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    November 2009 — Present (1 year )

    MISSION
    Financial Education Services, Inc. (FES) was formed to provide a high standard of meaningful personal financial education programs and services to consumers nationwide, including underserved populations, to enhance their financial quality of life.

    PROGRAMS
    FES offers financial education programs to individual consumers and groups of consumers. These programs are delivered in a variety of methods including the internet, telephone, and in traditional classroom settings. FES works with partners to develop programs specific to partner needs. FES has programs focused on debt, credit, savings, credit reports, budgeting, savings, and retirement planning. Many FES programs are also available in Spanish. Additional programs are being considered and FES welcomes feedback from partners and potential partners on what subjects should be explored.

    EXPERIENCE
    Our staff at FES has over 40 years of combined experience in providing education to students. Our staff uses this experience to design and deliver meaningful personal financial education programs to consumers. Each member of our staff holds either a state teacher's certificate or a certification as a credit counselor or a financial counselor from a recognized independent organization.

    WHO WE HELP
    FES is interested in helping several specific groups of consumers. These consumer groups include recent college graduates, bankruptcy filers, current or potential homeowners, and those experiencing financial difficulties. By targeting these specific consumer groups, we believe that our educational efforts will lead to the maximum amount of social good possible.

  • Board Member

    National Financial Literacy Foundation

    (Non-Profit; Financial Services industry)

    November 2009 — Present (1 year )

    The National Financial Literacy Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the personal financial knowledge and financial quality of life of consumers through the development and nationwide state-of-the-art delivery of high-quality yet cost-effective comprehensive financial education and counseling programs.

    FINANCIAL EDUCATION
    NFLF offers financial education programs to consumers. These programs are designed to cover a wide variety of topics including budgeting, savings, credit, debt, personal financial management, insurance, retirement, and financial solutions. These programs can be tailored to meet the needs of the individual consumer or a consumer group. Please contact our office today to learn how NFLF can design a program to meet the needs of your organization.


    FINANCIAL COUNSELING
    NFLF offers personal credit counseling services to consumers needing such a service. A counseling session consists of an analysis of what has caused financial distress if any exists, creation of a budget and then spending plan, and asset analysis, followed by a recommendation of financial solutions.

    Music is what dreams and memories are made of! It's been around for longer than language itself.

    Our vision for the Florida Music Hall of Fame is to create an organization that will educate youth, bring generations, nationalities and all people from Florida and around the world to honor, celebrate and learn about Florida's rich music heritage!
