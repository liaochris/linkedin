Jim Begley’s Summary

I work with young businesses in the Tampa Bay area helping them to use technology to drive profitability and efficiency. Whether it is finding a particular software solution, designing and implementing corporate networks or even managing a custom software development project, Glide has the expertise and industry contacts to guide you down the path to success.

I have worked in the software industry for over 20 years and have deep experience in both product management and product strategy. My combined background in IT and marketing allow me to work effectively with both business and technical teams and act as a bridge between the two,

I do some writing and speaking on small business technology and hope to make that a greater part of my practice over the next few years.
