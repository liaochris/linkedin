Eddy Badrina’s Summary

Based in the Dallas / Ft Worth Metroplex, Eddy is a proven small business marketing consultant and business development executive with vast experience in capturing the voice of a small business or startup, persuasively communicating it to a target audience, and developing new business as a result of it. Highly experienced in developing marketing strategies and processes, he also crafts the appropriate tactics, and executes them in a timely manner.

He is a critical thinker with an extensive history of public speaking, marketing initiatives, copy-writing, meeting facilitation, strategic planning and diagnostic selling. He has a reputation for strong work ethic, team attitude, servant leadership, and commitment to maximizing business value with existing resources.
