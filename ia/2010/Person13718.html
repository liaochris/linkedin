Ankur Jain’s Summary

* My goal is to build products which makes the users of my products happy and satisfied.

* My expertise is in building web products, web search quality, user interface, interaction design, Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and software development.

* I have over nine years of experience in diverse areas: product management, business development, marketing, software development, hacking Linux kernel and porting it to embedded devices, designing web interfaces etc.

* I am a metrics driven product person whose goal is to find an optimum balance between user requirements and business needs of the product/company. I feel very comfortable in wearing multiple 'hats' and particularly work well in small dynamic teams.

* I have worked in five countries, three continents and with team members from 25 different countries. I can say 'hello' in 10 different languages!

I am always up for an interesting conversation - please ping me at ankjain@stanfordalumni.org if you want to meet up for coffee.
