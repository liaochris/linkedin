Jared Wright’s Summary

Hello! Thanks for visiting my Linked-In profile!

I own Wright Direct, we're a full service database marketing company in the heart of Omaha, NE (Dundee). I played football at UNO and started my own business three years ago. I provide list brokerage and list services to ad agencies, other list brokers, mortgage companies, hospitals, roofing companies, mail companies nationwide - anyone who needs to send a targeted message to their best prospect at the best possible price.

Now, I understand you have many options to purchase your mailing list - and believe me, there are a TON of places online you can go to get the cheapest price. However - and I warn you - there are no guarantees on the quality of what you're investing in.

You'll never have to worry about that with me. Here's why:

1. Quality - I offer the most deliverable databases in the industry and we're compliant / up to date with all the changing mail standards. This means more of your mail gets in the hands of people who are going to buy from you.

2. Service - I pride myself on being very accessible for my clients - phone, email, text - and even social media! I'll help identify your best prospects and build a list marketing strategy that works for the long term. Whenever you need me, I'm available.

3. Experience - I've been in the database marketing industry for over ten years. Not only do I have access to your very best potential customers, I know how to get a list of them for you at the right price.

Questions? Visit my direct response marketing blog http://www.thewrightblog.com and let's start a conversation about how we can grow your business. I look forward to helping you out!
