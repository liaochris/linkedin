    Infosys Technologies

    October 2002 — Present (7 years 8 months)

    3. Managing the UI design part of production for big-scale projects

    4. Participating in pre-sales activities on-site to present Infosys’s UI Design capabilities and drive/generate business.     5. Intra-organization brand building exercises and initiatives, branding for different client projects. Using creative tools like different 2-D Animation techniques, illustration and Graphic Design.

    6. Guiding junior designers within the team and working with them to define and work towards career development.
