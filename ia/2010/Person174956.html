Robert Mandelberg’s Summary

Since establishing The Creative Edge Resume & Writing Service in 1987, I have had the pleasure of helping thousands upon thousands of clients reach their career goals. I specialize in creating high-impact resumes, cover letters, and other job-search materials for people at all levels in all industries - and in all areas of the country!

As the job market becomes increasingly more competitive, it is essential to focus on value to capture the attention of recruiters and hiring managers. Through personal interviews with my clients, I undercover the unique value of each individual, and then convey it in persuasive, achievement-rich resume presentations. In addition, I coach clients in communicating their value and "staying on message" during job interviews.

I am proud to have helped countless job-seekers understand their worth, achieve their career goals, and improve their lives!

---------------- Jumpstart Your Job-Search Tele-Summit -----------------

After 20+ years of experience in the career industry, I designed a comprehensive job-search program covering every area of job hunting. Featuring a panel of industry leaders, this groundbreaking tele-summit taught job seekers how to develop and execute a methodical job-search plan. From identifying a target position to resume creation, networking, and interview preparation, this vital program provides all the resources and information needed to launch a successful job search.

----------------------------- Published Author ---------------------------

In addition to writing resumes, I have also authored six books (published by Sterling Publishing and available at Barnes & Noble and Amazon.com) on Brainteasers and Magic geared towards middle-school readers. I have also written several full-lenth plays (one of which toured nationally with more than 200 performances), and written and directed a movie short (which won the Audience Award at the Big Fish Film Festival).

Robert Mandelberg’s Experience

  • Resume Writer, Published Author, Owner

    Creative Edge Resume & Writing Service, LLC

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    March 1987 — Present (23 years 11 months)

    Yes, it's true: I create powerful, interview-generating resumes, cover letters, LinkedIn profiles, and other documents for a diverse client base; but that's not actually *what I do* ...

    The value of my services is in the process of working one-on-one with my clients to understand their backgrounds, situations, challenges, and goals. It is the personal communication that enables me to transition clients into new positions.

    Specifically, I ...

    * Develop a job-search strategy that works for each individual client. I take the time to understand my clients' career goals and create sensible solutions to get them to where they want to be.

    * Uncover clients' true value points through probing questions and creative interviewing techniques. I am an expert in getting to the core of a job seeker's worth by determining the impact he or she has made to an organization.

    * Help clients understand their unique worth and their promise of value. Many people do not realize *why* they should be hired, or *how* they can help an employer solve their challenges and achieve their goals. Do you?

    * Position clients as viable candidates by strengthening their online presence, sharpening their interviewing skills, and teaching effective ways to uncover the hidden job market.

    I am thankful that I have a job that enables me to make such a meaningful impact to the lives of others.

  • Chief Writer

    Professional Resume & Writing Service

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    June 1986 — February 1987 (9 months)

    Here is where it all began! Isn't it funny how you can look back through the years and discover a moment that shaped your life forever?

    At 23 years of age, I had many options ahead of me. I was contemplating several career paths, when I just happened upon an advertisement for a resume-writing company. Who knew that the nine months I spent with this firm would lead to a career that has, thus far, spanned more than two decades?

    As the Chief Writer (okay, I was the sole writer in the office), I quickly learned the ropes, creating at least a dozen resumes per week. With a natural entrepreneurial spirit, it didn't take me long to realize that I could be doing the exact same job on my own.

    This short-term position set the stage for what has turned out to be an extremely satisfying, rewarding, and challenging career as a resume writer and career coach.

  • Editorial Department

    Meetings & Conventions Magazine

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    1985 — 1986 (1 year )

    Fresh out of college and eager to make my mark in the workforce, I landed a job with a publication within the Murdoch Magazine Publishing Group. My primary role was researching, verifying, and editing company listings for an annual directory.

    I had the opportunity to experience an hour-and-a-half commute to work in a crowded, windowless office with a half-dozen know-it-alls (like myself) and enjoy MSG-ridden fast food during a 15-minute lunch break.

    I have to say that it was among the most memorable and enjoyable experiences of my life!
