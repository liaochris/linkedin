Jacob Correia’s Summary

I have over 10 years of Web Dev experience, primarily Flash ActionScript Development. The majority of my work has been done as the lead Flash Developer for Say Design, with projects coming from a range of small to large companies such as
• Disney
• MTV
• Yahoo
• HP
• Nick Jr
• SuperDudes.net
• AddictingGames
• Kongregate
• Shockwave

Beginning with design and web development, I found my skills to be strongest as a programmer. As a result I am familiar with most professional web media programs.

I am accustomed to pushing the envelope and overcoming obstacles. I not only have much experience with OOP and dynamic coding, but thoroughly enjoy it. I enjoy learning and am driven by the desire to expand my skills and become better and more efficient at what I do; possessing irreplaceable real-world experience.

• Extensive time spent at events with industry professionals. (IGDA, Minna Mingle, GDC, Flash Forward, FlashKit, etc)

• Experience in seminar teaching for Flash Development, haven spoken on several occasions at Flash festivals and universities.

• Have built or been consulted with for Flash Game API's and related functionality for a variety of game sites; including:
• Shockwave.com
• AddictingGames.com
• Kongregate.com
• Others

• Founding team member of Kongregate.com, as Flash technology developer. Duties included planning Flash integration, chat dev and working on the currently used badge APIs.

• Extensive experience in Flash 3D work, going back all the way to before A-blast 3D (a first for 2.5d Flash games) and up to custom written full 3D projection and rendering engines in ActionScript.

Other accomplishments include breaking ground in Flash gaming in general, paving the way for current games and showing what Flash was capable of. I built the first fully functioning pinball game in Flash for Superdudes.net years ago when it was said it couldn't be done..
