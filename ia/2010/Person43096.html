David Lowe, PMP, CISSP, CISA, CISM’s Summary

David Lowe is the Senior Director of Enterprise Technology Services at Avery Dennison Corporation, a Fortune 500 manufacturer of pressure-sensitive technology, self-adhesive base materials, and self-adhesive consumer and office products. He has over 20 years of comprehensive IT background and expertise in the areas of enterprise architecture design, systems implementation/administration, and technical project management. Prior to Avery Dennison, David has held various managerial or technical lead positions in the motion picture, engineering, legal, consulting, academic, and music industries.

David is an active member of the Los Angeles Chapter of Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA) and has helped facilitate the chapter’s annual spring conference since 2003. He is also a member of the organizing committee for itSMF USA Fusion conference, working with other industry professionals to leverage IT service management practices.

David Lowe, PMP, CISSP, CISA, CISM’s Specialties:

David earned his BS degree in Applied Mathematics from University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) and his MBA from University of Southern California (USC). In addition, David holds current certifications in Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Systems Manager (CISM), and Project Management Professional (PMP).
