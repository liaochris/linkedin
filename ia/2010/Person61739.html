Eddie Freeman’s Summary

Edward Freeman is currently a student earning his Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering, but plans to switch to Computer Science at the earliest convenience. He plans on getting minors in both Psychology and Philosophy, while specializing in Artificial Intelligence.

At Noblis Inc. Mr. Freeman gained experience in Agile Software development, project prototyping, Objective-C, documentation standards, presentation design, and more. His first project was working on an iPhone App prototype for a medical recall web-service called RASMAS.

Edward continued his work in the summer of '09 with Noblis Inc. on a project for the National Guard learning a new browser-based data analytics system called Centrifuge from Centrifuge Systems Inc. where he was the "Quick Learner" and tester of new technologies. He also helped develop methods of collecting the data to be used for this data analytics project.

He further continues his self-growth by learning new technologies and gaining understanding of both business and development at a production level. Currently, he pursues this through his work at CDM Technologies and on the side developing mobile apps for various problems he faces.
