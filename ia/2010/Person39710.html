Christopher Hunt’s Summary

Chris has been working in the legal technology industry for nearly 15 years. As Director of Information Technology at Boston-based Tarlow Breed Hart & Rodgers, a 50-user, 22-attorney office, he is responsible for all facets of I.T. from strategic planning to implementation and day-to-day support. Chris also oversees Records and Facilities Management, and works closely with the firm’s Marketing Director and Firm Administrator.

Chris is an active member of the International Legal Technology Association (ILTA) since joining the organization in 2000.

Chris led a panel discussion on Managing Mobile Device Fleets at the 2009 ILTA Annual Conference.
