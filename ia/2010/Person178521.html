Robert Harris’s Summary

Entrepreneurial, user-focused, managing engineer with sixteen years of leadership in the planning, development and incorporation of user experience focus into B2C and B2B software applications.

Highly experienced, seasoned leader in the areas of technology, social media, user advocacy, user research methods/methodologies, product/project management, business development, vision and strategy, quality process, team building and servant leadership.

I am a usability evangelist who not only possesses extensive experience with numerous software development lifecycle (SDLC) processes (including Agile, SCRUM, RUP, Spiral and Waterfall) but also incorporating UX into these processes as a core function.

I have vast experience with off-shoring, outsourcing, working with, on and managing remote personnel and geographically diverse teams as well as operating within highly process oriented development houses. I've excelled in organizations possessing Capability Maturity Model (CMM) Level 5 and International Organization for Standardization (ISO) 9001:2000 Quality Management System (QMS) certifications for both Systems and Software Engineering as well as less mature models with equal success.

My UX team has grown exponentially (from 2 to 12 staff within 14 months) and become a recognized strategic business partner on all initiatives.
