Erik Gunn’s Summary

* Freelance reporter and writer for magazines and other media.

As a regular writer for Milwaukee magazine since 1995, I've won more than a half-dozen awards from the Milwaukee Press Club and the City and Regional Magazine Association in the areas of investigative reporting, service journalism, business writing and general feature writing.
Click on "My Portfolio" to see an ongoing and frequently updated collection of my work that's available on the web.

* Editor and consultant for businesses, nonprofits, academic institutions and individuals.

I've worked with a major, privately held company to coach employees on more effective internal and external presentations.

I have edited three books on education policy as well as several collections of papers on the topic. I have also edited a number of policy briefs and shorter papers on all aspects of education reform.
