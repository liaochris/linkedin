Jim Shelton’s Summary

Professional Experience:

Commercial Banker: 25+ years. Started as commercial credit analyst upon graduation from college. Has performed in the role of loan administrator, commercial lender, division manager, chief lending officer.

Entrepreneur: 10+ years. Founded and managed consulting firm/turnaround management firm for small businesses (annual revenues up to $50 million). Functioned as interim CFO. Later founded and managed executive search and recruiting firm. Gained national recognition. Clients from the East coast to the West coast. Specialized in search in the bank lending space, management and finance personnel.

Graduate of University of Md. with degree in Bus. Admin. Double focus: accounting and finance. Graduate of the Maryland Bankers Association School. Lifetime member of the University of Maryland Alumni Association.

Now serve or has served as a board member of the following organizations:

* United Cerebral Palsy of D.C. and Northern Virginia
* Virginia Asset Financing Corp. (SBA 504 Program Company)
* The Chesapeake Center (Advisory Board)
* YMCA of the Metropolitan Washington, DC area
* United Negro College Fund (Advisory Board)
* Center For Multicultural Human Services (Dir. & Treasurer)
* The Leo Weiss Venture Clinic (Charter Board Member)
* Risk Mgmt Assn - Chspk Chapt. (f/k/a Rbt Morris Assoc.)

Jim has been a resident of Northern Virginia for more than 20 years.
