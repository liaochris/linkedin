Doug Holmberg, PE/CPE/MBA’s Summary

Professional Project Management, Inc. (PPM) specializes in providing Project Management, Construction Management, Project Controls and Cost Estimating Services.

Founded in 1990, PPM is solely owned and managed by Doug Holmberg. PPM offers unmatched professional service backed by proven performance in the areas of fast-track, design-build, build-to-suit new construction and renovation / expansion projects.

PPM serves as the Owners “connecting link” providing overall team management to assemble and lead Development, Design and ConstructionTeams.

We are a select group of construction professionals providing in-depth expertise in all areas of Project Management:
PPM offers unmatched professional service backed by proven performance in the areas of fast-track, design-build, build-to-suit, new construction and renovation/expansion projects. PPM provides Project Management services for Private Clients, Architects, and Contractors.

Our Team provides a compliment of education, expertise and experience that few can match. Our services range from preliminary budgeting and planning to serving as an Owner's authorized Project Manager to oversee and coordinate an entire project from Master Planning, Entitlements, Design through Construction and Occupancy. We provide invaluable pre-construction phase services consisting of Project Scheduling, Value Engineering Analysis, Systems Design Recommendations and Project Cost Estimating including Development Costs and Construction Costs.


PPM’s Six Construction Commandments

1. Never pretend you are smarter than you actually are!
2. Always know what you are buying (and selling)!
3. You have control of nothing unless you have simultaneous control of your schedule and budget at all times!
4. An imperfect schedule and imperfect budget are better than no schedule and no budget!
5. Anyone can take a project to 95%!
6. He who has the best documentation almost always wins!
