Brenda Whitfield’s Summary

I am a marketing and branding manager, and graphic and web design with 18+ years experience in all aspects of branding, marketing and graphic design working across the US and UK. I work across the board with corporations, public sector, tourist, real estate, and services industries in a freelance marketing, branding and consulting capacity to full-service graphic and web design, and multi-media campaigns that integrate marketing and design.

I hold a Bachelor's in Communication from the University of West Florida with an emphasis in Public Relations and Advertising, and a Master's in Marketing Management (Managing Creative Enterprises) from the Norwich University College of the Arts, Norwich, UK. I will be starting an MA in Internet Marketing at Full Sail University in September 2010.
