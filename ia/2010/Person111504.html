Kate DiChristopher’s Summary

Marina Maher Communications has been named a TOP PLACE TO WORK in the AGENCY category by PR News AND one of the BEST AGENCIES TO WORK FOR by The Holmes Report

Our Values at MMC: Make your clients your partners. Respect everyone. Foster teamwork. Treat the client's money like our own. Live the "can do" spirit. Invest in people. Think big. Work hard. Play hard.

Marina Maher Communications is an independent communications agency with special expertise in marketing to women. Our agency delivers enduring brand relationships through the passionate execution of big ideas and extraordinary added value. Named Creative Agency of the Year in 2007 by the Holmes Report, we develop strategic platforms and build brand authority for a range of clients in beauty, consumer products, food and beverage, healthcare and technology arenas. We possess a unique understanding of women’s relationships with brands, developed over 25 years of success for clients such as Procter & Gamble and Pfizer.

MMC is a member of the Worldcom Public Relations Group, the world's leading network of independently owned public relations counseling firms.

GRADS & INTERNS: Join the Marina Maher Communications Graduate & Intern Opportunities group on Facebook! Our page is designed to provide you with program details and application criteria for opportunities at both the intern and graduate level. We have also posted information that highlights the agency’s culture and staff; our development opportunities for junior staff; and the outstanding work we do for our clients.
