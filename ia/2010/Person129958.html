Maggie Kalas’s Summary

Maggie Kalas’ career spans over 25 years with a diversity of professional experience ranging from executive assistant to office manager to leading direct sales manager to marketing consultant to independent well-being practitioner.

A natural people-person, Maggie has lead successful sales teams as well as worked closely with captains of industry. As a top performance seller with a leading direct sales company, she consistently earned local and national recognition. Maggie has coordinated and directed several successful trade shows, fundraising events and community forums.

More recently, Maggie was instrumental in the publishing of two self-help books: Simple Happiness: 52 Easy ways to Lighten Up by Jim Ryan, an easy-to-digest approach to achieving personal and professional success; and Games2Careers by Susan H. Gubing and Karen McKenna, which uses a young person’s natural abilities to stimulate career choices.

Currently, Maggie works independently as a success coach, using Reiki therapy, mediation and stress management workshops to help individuals, groups and professionals achieve success in all areas of life.
