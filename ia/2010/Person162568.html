Peter Bakke’s Summary

More than a decade spent growing online audiences and revenues on news and information sites.

Goals: Get online newspapers to own their local news cycle. After all, it is the first time in 500 years newspapers can compete and win with breaking news and updates. Think about that.

Also, we need to own our local marketplaces. Studies show online newspapers beat other local online media outlets in terms of frequency and reach, including TV and radio competitors. Let's take advantage of that fact.

Adage: You can't grow revenue by sitting behind your desk and hoping it will happen.
