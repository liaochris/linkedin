Gabriel Topete’s Summary

I started design prompted by an equal passion for both concept and general design. I currently live in coffee loving, beach going San Diego.

I am a multi-talented individual with a diverse knowledge of both advertising and graphic design, over 3 years of experience in the design field and 2 years experience in the advertising field, with high aspirations to succeed and a deep rooted passion for Graphic Design.

I spend more time studying design then doing design this has helped me fully understand the true meaning of what design is and I have developed a set discipline.

big thanks to my grandma for teaching and exposing me to art at a young age.

Gabriel Topete’s Specialties:

Talents in both Static and Dynamic design. Both schooled and independently taught in design practices ranging from but not limited to, typography, color theory, The psychology of form and function, fitts law / hicks law, composition, and current design / culture trends. Yeah I like design that much.

I am also expanding my skills into Motions Graphics, Sound engineering and Video editing.
