Antone Johnson’s Summary

Lawyer, advisor and executive with more than 14 years experience advising entrepreneurs, investors, lean startups and growth companies in the full range of legal matters. Training at top-tier Wall Street and Silicon Valley law firms, followed by in-house roles at market leaders in social media, online dating, digital media and entertainment content, ranging from $100 million to $1 billion in annual revenue. Drawing on 5+ years experience as an in-house lawyer and executive at pioneers MySpace and eHarmony, I offer clients unparalleled expertise in legal and business issues that are unique to social media/Web 2.0. With offices in San Francisco and Santa Monica, we are well positioned to bridge the gap between Silicon Valley and Hollywood business and legal cultures.

Antone Johnson’s Specialties:

Business formation and structuring, lean startups, social media, user-generated content, corporate and business development transactions, Internet advertising and marketing, angel and venture capital financing, M&A, joint ventures, IPOs, Sarbanes-Oxley, corporate governance, stock option plans, employment and executive compensation matters, litigation management, legal department management, government affairs
