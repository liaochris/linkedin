Barry Waxman’s Summary

Accomplishments & Core Skills:

20+ years of increased responsibilities and success in Executive Management (VP, SVP, GM, COO) & Leadership, in the areas of Sales, Marketing, Operations, Manufacturing and General Management.

Scalable experience from private early stage companies to public, over $1 billion companies

Broad technology experience in Semiconductors, DSP’s, MEMS devices, Imaging (Hardcopy, Still & Video), Wireless, Touch Screens (components, monitors) Storage/Networking, Mobile devices, Set Top Boxes. Consumer related market segments.

Business experience that includes Building/Motivating High Performance Teams, Early Revenue Design Wins, Product Marketing Launches, Strategic Planning, Operating Plans, Channel Management, Pay-for-Performance Compensation Plans, Fund Raising, M&A’s

Worldwide geographical strength and knowledge that includes the Americas, Europe, Latin America and significant experience throughout Asia, in particular Japan, Korea and Taiwan/China.

Served on Executive Management Teams of Western Digital, Adaptec (successful IPO), Mylex (acquired by IBM), PixelMagic (acquired by Oak Technology/Zoran), Microtouch (acquired by 3M) and startups, Standard MEMS, Connex Technology, Tak Imaging, as well as founding mVision Technology www.mvisiontechnology.com) and TechASIA Management. (www.techasiamanagement.com)

Other experience and involvement includes Director of Semiconductors at Trudeau & Trudeau, an Investment Bank, Member of the Advisory Board of JAAG, a Storage Aggregator, Founding Member of GlobalScot’s Advisory Board, promoting and advising emerging Scottish company’s. Barry is also a member of Launchpad Venture Group, an Angel investor in the Boston area.

His Degree was earned in Electronics and Electrical Engineering from Paisley College of Technology, King’s College, London.
