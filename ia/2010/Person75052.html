Gregory Gull, Ph.D.’s Summary

I bring more than twenty-five years of experience, working in business and industry in positions ranging from research and development to advertising. This diverse experience affords me a unique integrative perspective on organizational design and leadership. Additionally I have a decade of experience as a consultant in the areas of leadership, strategic planning, organizational learning, organizational change, quality, statistical thinking, systems thinking, and decision-making & problem solving across a broad range of industries that include high precision tooling, manufacturing, communications, pharmaceuticals, bulk color, packaging, human services and trucking. My vision is that all institutions will be enabling to the individual; enriching people’s lives for the betterment of all.
