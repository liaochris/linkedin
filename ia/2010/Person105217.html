Jose Palomino’s Summary

Jose Palomino is founder and President of g2m Group, Inc., delivering methodology, training, coaching and strategic assistance for accelerating “go-to-market” for business product and services companies.

With a focus on plug-in processes, people and support, g2m Group is an accelerator for go-to-market initiatives in even the most sophisticated marketing and sales organizations.

Jose Palomino’s Specialties:

 Entrepreneurial leader with over 20 years of experience in technology and service sectors.
 Author of “Value Prop: Creating Powerful I3 Value Propositions to Enter and Win New Markets."
 Strengths include tactical creativity, strategic orientation, marketing savvy, strong interpersonal skills and technical acumen combine to deliver leadership and results.
 Experienced dealmaker & proven closer.
 Skilled presenter, facilitator and team leader with MBA.
