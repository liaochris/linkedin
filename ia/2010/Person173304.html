Rick Silva’s Summary

I've been playing, writing, teaching, and engineering music for over 25 years.

I am comfortable and qualified when working on any professional large format analog or digital console, and I am a proficient DAW operator.

I own and operate a successful production facility in Studio City, CA that is also an Accredited Waves Certification Training Facility and have a team of qualified sound technicians that can handle all stages of audio production.

I am a published author for Alfred Publishing, and the Hal Leonard Corporation. I write books for Music Technology, Music Education, and Sound Engineering. I was given the opportunity to create and revise books for the highly acclaimed Power Tools series of books. My first two titles are available now.

Power Tools for Pro Tools 8 / Power Tools for Logic Pro 9

Titles for Waves Plug-ins, Bias Peak Pro 6, Mixing Techniques, and several Complete Idiots Guide to various titles are all on the way. Including a DVD on how to buy your first DAW.

My goal is to continue being successful in the music industry by using music and music technology to inspire, educate, capture, and create the beautiful music that surrounds us every day.
