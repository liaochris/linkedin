Lucie Belanger’s Honors:

In 2006, received a Gold Beacon Award at BearingPoint for a benchmarking project developed for China MNCs. In 2007, received a second Gold Beacon Award for my focus and dedication to the Supply Chain global team, communications with the trade analysts and trade groups, and constant efforts on finding resources and IP for client efforts.
