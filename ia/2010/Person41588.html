Cliff Bailey’s Summary

It takes the right candidates and the right opportunities for successful long term relationships. Over 25 years of successful experience has secured a solid presence in the business of executive search. Clients have come to know and trust me and I understand their expectations.

Great relationships and involvement in networking organizations have been excellent venues to meet a lot of people. Sales, marketing, accounting, finance, human resoures, engineering, construction, real estate, IT, etc. encompass my connections and memberships in the following organizations:
National Sales Network, Construction Recuiters Network, Metropolitan Officials Association, Ace's Business Network A'Fair, CAMR, Coalition of Presidents, just to name a few. And now LinkedIn!

The experience of three (in a row) NAIA collegiate baseball championships and a successful career as a basketball official working at the high school, college and professional levels, I know many athletes who are now successful corporate executives. We understand what it takes to WIN and the value of teamwork. These people are a huge part of my network as clients, candidates and/or referral sources.

It's my goal to continue to develop those life and career lasting relationships with those who come to know, like and trust me and I them!
