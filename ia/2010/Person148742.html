Kate Moore’s Summary

My name is Kate Moore and I am a Career Coach. Through my years of working in both an educational and business setting, I have seen men and women of all ages seeking passion and fulfillment in their professional lives. Too many people are unhappy with their jobs and I truly believe that through coaching and self- examination, you can find a profession that you enjoy going to everyday while providing the stability and financial needs of your family.

Currently I am also a Career Counselor at a university. I have a Masters of Education degree in Higher Education Administration and Counseling and have been working in higher education for many years. I also have experience in the corporate setting, in particular, marketing.

Through coaching, I believe anyone can be successful in both their personal and professional life. I would love the opportunity to speak with you regarding your goals. Please contact me to set up a free Strategic Planning Session.
