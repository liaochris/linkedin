John D. Butler’s Summary

John Butler is passionate about the projects he accepts and specializes in Ghostwriting, SEO/Keyword copywriting, and content writing for Social Media Marketing (SMM) campaigns.

JB, "Writes that which is hard to write; copy that gets results!"

Telecommuting and online experience dates back to 1998 and includes 10 years as a Senior Field Trainer with Nielsen Media Research.

Going freelance in 2007, JB has authored multiple magazine articles, posts regularly on half a dozen blogs, is an invited guest expert giving salacious relationship advice, and is the recipient of back-to-back Special Commendations from the City of San Diego for his volunteer work as the Communications Coordinator of a San Diego based breast cancer charity.

John D. Butler’s Specialties:

Ghostwriting non-fiction books for professional, public and political figures and content writing for Social Media Marketing (SMM) campaigns.
