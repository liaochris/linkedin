Guy Taylor’s Summary

I am a behavioural theorist, analyst and change agent. The crux of it is I'm a people person, I like to understand the patterns around how and why people interact the way they do, and establish how to optimize people for their own lives.

I'm a great believer in human systems as fractal networks, so as we shift our perspectives and frames to view interactions at different levels, we see the same behaviours playing out in culture, and as we shift meta to that sub-cultural behaviour playing out in larger frameworks.

    The name Cognasium is a fictional hybrid of the words “cognitive” and “gymnasium” - A place where your idea’s work out.

    The seed of Cognasium was established in late 2006 when one of the founding members became frustrated with the legacy of how business is done. A legacy where people were trapped by the system that they had created to free themselves. Slaves to the wage. Time has become money, and hierarchy was more important than outcome. People where trying to build on their weaknesses, in order to become better in their roles, but by doing so were losing their strengths, and although the world of the internet has changed the way we interact at so many levels, we remain cuffed to 9 – 5 jobs which have required us to be attached to our desks, regardless of outcome.

    Cognasium was borne out of the thought of freedom. Cognasium was created so that individuals and businesses alike could grow towards their own outcomes, using a model of collaboration, and playing to strengths.
