Russ Bowers, MPA, SPHR’s Summary

Twenty years' experience as HR generalist, manager and director at small to mid-sized companies. Start-up experience at two of them. My goal is to help my employer become the best it can be -- an employer of choice -- and it's employees to be happy with their jobs and their working conditions. Also, on a personal level, to continue to learn, develop and advance in HR field.
