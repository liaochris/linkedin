Olga Obolonskaya’s Summary

As the Founder of OLDI Ethnic Marketing I understand the value of niche marketing in US. That is why I put together a team of multi ethnic professionals who strive for success and business growth through fulfilling our clients marketing strategies based on their unique knowledge of different cultures and traditions in US.

OLDI works with a variety of Ethnic Medias throughout America and beyond. We help our clients to better understand their target markets by analyzing products that they offer, competition and our capabilities in a particular ethnic segment.

The main goal of OLDI is to educate our clients, show the value of promoting to ethnic groups through comparison with other forms of marketing, and help our clients go through the process of penetration of niche markets in the most beneficial manner.
