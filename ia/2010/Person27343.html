William Bray’s Summary

I am an attorney in Charlotte, North Carolina with a business law practice emphasizing work in the areas of sports and entertainment. I also teach sports law to MBA students at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte's Belk College of Business. In 2006-2007, I served as chair of the Sports & Entertainment Law Section of the North Carolina Bar Association. My firm is consists of three attorneys. Our practice is evenly split between corporate representation and commercial litigation.

Motorsports (primarily NASCAR) is the single largest area of our firm's practice. We currently represent or have represented teams, drivers, crew members, sponsors, agencies and marketing groups. Our clients have won events in all levels of racing, including Nextel Cup, Nationwide, Craftsman Truck, Indy Car, ARCA, Busch East and the APBA. The work we provide includes their general representation (contracts, corporate affairs, licensing) and litigation. Other than motorsports, we currently represent or have represented clients in other sports, such as golf and basketball.

Our entertainment practice includes work in a wide variety of entertainment-related industries, including music, film production, publishing and media. My clients include record labels, film and record producers, musicians, artists and many of the assorted, peripheral entities that complement the industry as a whole.

The remainder of our practice consists of the representation of small to mid-size companies in the context of general corporate transactions and business litigation. We work with clients in dozens of industries such as construction, marketing, media and retail commerce. From a litigation perspective, our attorneys handle millions of dollars of disputes on an annual basis, ranging in diversity from business break-ups to noncompete disputes and trade secret infringement.

William Bray’s Specialties:

Sports, entertainment, motorsports, corporate representation, contracts, incorporation, business litigation, music, film, television, media, construction, trademark, copyright.
