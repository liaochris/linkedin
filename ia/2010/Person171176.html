Renee Shannon’s Summary

Self motivated individual with outstanding leadership abilities. Professional sales person that is dedicated to building and maintaining relationships with my customers. Began my career in the automotive industry in my early twenties then founded the Fort Worth Tie Company in 1990. A niche market selling ties to busy male professionals in the city and surrounding areas. As corporate casual hit the business sector, the Tie Company evolved into Bird Dog Ideas, a full service promotional products company.

In an effort to connect to my passion for automobiles, in 2002 I founded A Car Buying Service that provided assistance to busy professionals in the car buying service. In 2005, Sewell Lexus of Fort Worth approached me about an Internet sales position that peaked my interest since the company has a legacy of putting the customer first. It was a great fit with my values of customer service.

Entrepreneur at heart, I continue to seek companies that put the customer first and provide excellent servie!
