Linda Hertz’s Summary

As a graduate of Michigan State University, Linda has Recruiting experience in her tenure at RecruitBUZZ® along with 25 years of medical sales experience with leading fortune 500 companies from medical disposable sales to surgical sales (Parke Davis, American Home Products; Sherwood Davis & Geck, and Smith & Nephew). This includes 10 years as an award winning Senior Surgical Sales Representative and 15 years in a hiring capacity as Division Sales Manager, Region Director, Area and Region Vice President levels. She understands the customers’ needs from their perspective and the value an excellent recruiter can bring to busy middle managers and executives alike; she walked in their shoes for most of her career. Linda resides in Southern California with her husband and three children.

Linda Hertz’s Specialties:

Medical Sales Management experience at general and VP levels, Start-up Medical Manufacturing Consulting experience, Co-founded RecruitBUZZ from conception to high volume and huge growth privately held company, comprehensive branding and marketing experience, executive recruitment and career counseling expertise within the medical sales arena.
