Michelle Ackers’s Summary

I am a qualified and experienced Marketing Manager with expertise in the Tourism industry, including 5 years with Air New Zealand, and more recently 2 years with Vianet International a provider of online booking software for tourism operators and retailers.
My goal is to help businesses achieve their objectives and give them guidance on how to improve, manage and implement effective marketing strategies within their budget requirements.

Michelle Ackers’s Specialties:

My specialty is in online marketing and website strategy, particularly for the tourism sector with understanding on how all parts of the tourism sector work together to promote and sell to travellers. I have an excellent knowledge of the online travel market in New Zealand and Australia.
I currently provide one on one coaching services for BizNorth in the area of website and online marketing.
