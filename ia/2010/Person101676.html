John Buckley Gordon’s Summary

I Produce. This means I do everything and anything that is needed to bring a story to life, be it a :30 second commercial or a two hour feature film. I look for stories and story tellers, I look for money to help get these stories made, I look for the other people who can help tell these stories (as they are always a collaborative experience, which I love).

I love my job. I love telling stories. I love the people I am blessed enough to work with. I love hearing someone reflect back to me what they got from one of the stories I helped produce.

My goals are to build Tantrum LA into a viable brand here in the US, and to continue to make feature films that add a ripple in the wave of evolving human consciousness.
