Simone Brunozzi’s Summary

My career can be divided into several phases:

Phase A, 2000-2008: consultant and entrepreneur for WEDOIT, my own company. Technical skills, entrepreneurship, networking.

Phase B, 2004-2006: University Professor at Perugia University, Italy, teaching programming languages and compilers to more than 300 students in two years; supervising professor as well.

Phase C, 2006-2008: System administrator and Network Administrator at University for Foreigners, Perugia, Italy.

Phase D, 2008-present: Amazon Web Services Technology Evangelist for Europe, Middle East and Africa. Cloud Computing, distributed systems, speaking in public, marketing, PR.

People say I'm really passionate about what I do, which I believe is the main secret to be successful and happy at work.
