Scott Wasserman’s Summary

In 16 years with the Hair Club, (Ten as a franchise owner) I understood the benefits of high quality customer service both for the consumer and the business. I always felt grateful to have a customer put their faith and trust in my company and myself. I will always be Thankful for the opportunities that Hair Club gave me and my ability to help people feel better about themselves.

Toward the end of my tenure, I began to research the level of low end service that some companies give to their customers; in almost all cases unintentionally.


"One year on the internet is like a dog year!! In other words it's growing faster and faster. Those words were uttered by Vint Cerf, one of the TRUE founders of the internet, and currently Google's Chief Internet Evangelist. a few years ago we all wanted "hits" on our websites. Last Year it was "traffic"... We've finally grown up. What is traffic without converting that traffic in to a lead or a sale?

The Internet is a constantly moving organism. If you don't have an strategy for it, watch it. Chances are your competition already does. If you don't have a team of consultants directing you on how to see dramatic ROI, well... let's just remember that Bill gates said it best... "In the future there will be two types of companies...those on the Internet and those out of business."

The advent of Internet marketing, currently at its infancy is giving me a chance on a b2b level to help small to medium size businesses grow their own business by helping them service their customers properly, help potential new customers find their business, in turn growing a company's ROI. WSI has afforded me the ability and the expertise to do just that.
