Frank L. Buquicchio’s Summary

FRANK L. BUQUICCHIO, is a Partner with the law firm of Vincent J. Russo & Associates, P.C. of Westbury, Islandia, Lido Beach and Woodbury, New York. He earned his law degree at Hofstra University School of Law and was admitted to practice in 1994. Frank is a member of the New York State and Suffolk County Bar Associations. Frank is also a member of the National Academy of Elder Law Attorneys (NAELA), a member of the New York State and Suffolk County Bar Associations’ Elder Law Committees and the Suffolk County Bar Association Surrogate's Court Committee.

Frank is on the Legal Advisory Committee for the Alzheimer's Association-Long Island Chapter and on the Board of the Arthritis Foundation-Long Island Chapter.

Frank is the immediate past Co-Chair of the Elder Law Committee of the Suffolk County Bar Association. He is admitted to the New York State and New Jersey State Bars and is also an accredited attorney with the Department of Veteran’s Affairs.

Frank has published articles in The Suffolk Lawyer, 50+ Lifestyles newspaper and Eldercare, Ltd. He has appeared in Newsday’s Act II as “Ask the Expert” and in "Gray Matters" and also as a frequent guest speaker on WALK (1370AM) Radio’s “Memories in Melodies”.

Frank has presented numerous seminars on Elder Law and Estate Planning and Veteran's benefits throughout Nassau and Suffolk Counties to both consumers and professionals.

Frank L. Buquicchio’s Specialties:

Frank's areas of practice include: Elder Law, Estate and Trust Planning, Veterans Benefits, Trust and Estate Administration, Real Estate and Corporate Law.
