Dennis Ahlgrim’s Summary

Dennis has been designing and building brands for non-profit and corporate entities for 25-years. He’s directed and led creative teams at top-tier branding agencies and firms, and now as owner and driving force behind Ahlgrim Design Group, brings to bear decades of relationship-building and expertise to solve comprehensive communication challenges. Dennis was trained at FIT in New York City and has since won numerous awards for design and creative excellence. He teaches design at NYU-SCPS, is a member of the AIGA, lives in Brooklyn, and is a lifelong fan of the Mets.

ADG does not specialize in any particular industry, however, we have deep, relevant experience in Financial Services, Pharma, Not-for-profit, and Publishing, having partnered with many global entities to develop and present their stories and services. Additional industries include:

• Business Consulting
• Entertainment
• High Tech
• Telecommunications
• Healthcare
• Real Estate

Likewise, we have had many wonderful and rewarding experiences providing world-class solutions for national and international not-for-profits. These clients work to make a difference in the areas of:

• Environment
• Social Services
• Human Rights
• Disabilities
• Education
• Poverty
• Business Development
• Computing

As a multidisciplinary firm, we offer creative solutions in many different forms and media (print and electronic) and have a significant track record building visual brand identities for corporate and not-for-profit organizations alike.
