Kush Jain’s Summary

Kush is co-founder and CEO of GlocalEdge. He oversees the company strategy, business development, and operations. Kush brings over 15 years of diverse leadership experiences in the United States and India, and has a strong track record of successful execution in Sales, Marketing, Consulting, and Software development at Microsoft and Oracle.
During his 8+ years career at Microsoft, Kush held key leadership roles in product marketing and sales. Before starting GlocalEdge, Kush was Director of Sales, Marketing, and Operations for Microsoft India's Developer Evangelism organization. Under Kush’s leadership, the organization delivered the highest worldwide revenue growth and made a strategic shift to internet marketing. Kush won Microsoft’s Worldwide "Sales and Marketing Best Practices Award" for two years in a row in recognition of his innovative sales and marketing initiatives.
Prior to Microsoft, Kush worked at Oracle in California as a software development manager and shipped several flagship Oracle products. Kush also worked with Deloitte Consulting as a consultant in San Francisco.

Kush holds an MBA from University of Chicago, an MS in Computer Science from New York University and a BE in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IITR). Kush is a world traveler, and enjoys playing squash and watching movies.
