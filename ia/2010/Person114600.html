Kenneth Gary Edelstein’s Summary

I'm an award-winning journalist who's excelled as a reporter, editor, manager and communications consultant. My new company, AEC Inc., specializes in reaching Atlanta audiences in the environmental arena.

AEC operates MyGreenATL.com, a news website that aims to give metro Atlantans a dependable place for credible information on the environment and local eco-friendly businesses an effective way to reach to Atlanta's green audience.

AEC also has the capacity to assist media organizations, nonprofits and others in developing and amplifying their messages. And we are capable of performing a full suite of editorial services, including research, writing, editing, design, print production and web production.

Here are a few highlights from my experience as a journalist and manager:

• I've built large local audiences online and in print. I've even conceived and hosted successful radio and live talk shows.

• I've won awards as a business, political and investigative reporter, and have edited the award-winning work of critics, feature writers and news reporters, among others.

• I've managed as many as 24 full-time journalist and have led teams of three or four. But I also am an excellent team player.

• I've built a strong network of associates, sources and connections, including politicians, business leaders, small businesspeople, community groups, activists, non-profit leaders and journalists.

• I possess exceptional knowledge of Atlanta, Georgia and the Southeast, particularly in the areas of technology, transportation, politics, business and the environment.

• I've overseen the development of successful blogs and other websites, and have served as a consultant to journalists making the transition to new media.
