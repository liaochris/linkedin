  • Sr. Software Engineer

    Claria Corp.

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    November 2002 — March 2007 (4 years 5 months)

    * Senior software engineer, PersonalWeb server software development team (www.personalweb.com)

    PersonalWeb was "Behavioral Targeting meets My Yahoo!" - a browser home page product that adjusted automatically and brought fresh content to you based on your recent browsing history. Worked on the core team of engineers responsible for developing: (a) the custom JS widget framework that powered the individual widgets on the page, and (b) the mod_perl web server backend that received chunks of behavioral data from the client-side tracking app and rendered the proper HTML skeleton for the page.

    * Lead engineer on SearchScout, a high-volume, international mod_perl search application

    SearchScout called for paid/sponsored search results in realtime from third party XML APIs (e.g. Overture, NexTag, and Shopping.com), parsed the results, and displayed them in a search results interface. In addition to US & UK, SearchScout was also launched in Korea and Japan, so the application included full Unicode support. At its peak, SearchScout was responsible for nearly half of the company's revenue.

    The primary technologies used in my work at Claria were Perl, mod_perl, and JavaScript.
