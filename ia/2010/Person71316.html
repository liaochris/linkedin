Frank Stegmaier’s Summary

Since the turn of the century, I have been producing creative work of all sorts. My passion, drive, commitment and talent has quickly elevated my career. I am an asset to any project, campaign or client.

I LIVE to create cool work that stands out, brings results and wins awards...that makes me the same as everyone else. What doesn't? My involvement outside of the agency. I critique student projects, speak at my Alma Mater, serve as a professional advisor for a local AdClub and serve as a the President of the American Advertising Federation of North Dakota. My job is about me, about our company and about helping our community...you can see from my involvement that this is more than a statement, it is part of my passion for communications.

Frank Stegmaier’s Specialties:

Marketing, Design, Art Direction and Creativity in any medium – print, online, television. Every medium is different, but the end goal is always the same.
