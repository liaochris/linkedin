Dave Halpern’s Summary

Chief Innovation Officer @ The Alternative Board (TAB), bringing together owners of privately held companies to grow and optimize their businesses. Advisory boards we create, manage & facilitate are comprised of local business owners, presidents, & CEOs who run non-competing businesses.

TAB members meet monthly—led by an executive coach—to share their business expertise and address each others’ challenges confidentially. Members learn from one another's successes and mistakes enabling them to overcome challenges and seize new opportunities.

► Passionate to grow and nurture strategic business positioning; I have gained experience in all functional areas - marketing, sales, operations, strategic thinking, strategic positioning and refining processes and procedures to gain efficiencies and effectiveness. My expertise provides a depth of understanding for full range business enterprises.

► Business coaching and guidance to help maximize the potential of ventures. I work as both a business coach and facilitator assisting with strategic planning in all areas of operations. I am highly creative and able to think “out of the box” to create maximum impact for the organization.

► I train TAB facilitators to bring value to clients; coaching business owners to get the most from their endeavors and to harness the power of contacting and selling to prospects.

► Possess a wealth of sales knowledge and provide sales training in all areas from acquisition to processes. Authoring easy-to-understand sales system for small to medium size companies.

► I love motivational speaking and presenting motivational programs. I take great pleasure in engaging audiences and sharing best practices I have learned through my experiences and life lessons.

► One of my great joys is spending time with my grandchildren. Balancing business and family priorities can be difficult but richly rewarding. “Grandchildren are God’s way of thanking you for not killing your children”.
