Jeff Schwartz’s Summary

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Award-winning business talent with 20-plus years of global success developing and executing strategic programs in media and public relations, issues & crisis management, marketing communications and stakeholder relations to earn unprecedented publicity and funding for the management, programs and services of Fortune-500 energy, environment and defense firms, U.S. healthcare, American science and technology initiatives, our government, higher education and the news media.

SUCCESS OVERVIEW
With a master's degree (Phi Kappa Phi), Jeff Schwartz has:

• Authored thousands of press releases and web articles
• Represented some of the highest-visibility issues we've known via more than 5,000 quotes in the media worldwide
• Pioneered Internet strategies and online apps for global use
• Trained CEOs, astronauts, scientists, elected officials and civic leaders
• Earned national honors for quality, and
• Developed strategic plans that earned $100M+ in new, capital investments.

His experience includes another ten years anchoring-reporting accurate news and information under deadline pressure for network-affiliated TV stations in four U.S. markets.

Schwartz offers a network of press contacts and proven spokesperson skills with appearances on the ABC, NBC, CBS, CNN, CNBC, PBS, and BBC networks, and in print, electronic and new media worldwide, including The Wall Street Journal, BusinessWeek, TIME, USA Today, The Washington Post, The New York Times and more.

LEADERSHIP
Schwartz has managed nationally-recognized external and employee communications, and developed speeches, executive briefings, issue papers, Internet sites, multi-media marketing campaigns and investor presentations. Active in local affairs, he serves on a City of Lafayette Committee. He shares his expertise in guest lectures at universities and professional societies. He is a trained NOAA-NWS weather spotter and into sports. An active blogger, he thrives across all media.
