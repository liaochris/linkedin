  • Editor, MacFormat presents The Essential iLife Handbook

    Future Publishing

    (Public Company; Publishing industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    I created and delivered this magazine from a standing start in four weeks flat, five days ahead of schedule and under budget.

    "Every new Mac comes with a copy of Apple's phenomenal suite of media apps, iLife, but you don't get a manual that tells you how to get the very best from iPhoto, iMovie, GarageBand, iWeb and iDVD. It doesn't even tell you how to get started!
    That's where our Essential iLife Handbook comes in. Written by the same expert writers that bring you MacFormat - the UK's biggest Mac magazine - every month, it's packed with unmissable advice on every one of the iLife applications, and guides you through the whole process of getting to know each tool, importing and polishing your photos, movies, music and more, and printing or publishing it all online to share with your friends and family.
    As well as easy-to-read, jargon-free tutorials, there's also some in-depth technical information for when you want to dig deeper, and 'best practice' advice from the pros on how to avoid all the mistakes beginners often make so you can give your projects a real polish.
    It includes all the new features of iLife '09, but even if you're using an earlier version, there's plenty here to help get you going.
    Whether you want to take stunning photos, edit movies worthy of Hollywood, compose a hit single or build a slick website to show it all off, you just need iLife and this guide!"
