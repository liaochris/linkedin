Ashley Brown’s Summary

I currently work at Jones-Dilworth, a PR and Marketing firm that specializes in bringing early stage tech start-ups to market.

Each day, I have the privilege of working not only with an incredibly talented, yet diverse, team of professionals, but also with some of the most innovative thinkers in this industry. Spanning multiple sectors from B2B, AI, and big data, to consumer electronics, lifestyle apps, and more, I am given the opportunity to witness the evolution of teams and products from conception through exit.

After working at an edtech startup during my undergraduate career, I feel so fortunate now to be exposed to not one, but multiple startup teams on a daily basis.
