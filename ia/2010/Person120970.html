Lisa Lane’s Summary

Lisa Lane is the pharmaceutical sales industry's most visible author and consultant. Since 1999, she has helped tens of thousands start careers in pharmaceutical sales.

With over 20 years in the pharmaceutical industry, she is currently President of Drug Careers, Inc, a leading pharmaceutical sales career development company. She is recognized as an authority in her field and has served as a career expert for many pharmaceutical websites and newspapers including Career Builder, Sales and Marketing Magazine, The LA Times, The Baltimore Sun, The Miami Herald, The Chicago Tribune and over 20 others. She maintains daily contact with professionals in all areas of pharmaceutical sales, consults with customers, and devotes much of her time to staying current on pharmaceutical news and information.
