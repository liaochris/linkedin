Jason Jay Stevens’s Summary

The common thread in all my work is an effort to share and celebrate a sense of wonder for the phenomena of the cosmos. My favorite routes are those most poetic, because they are the most successful at building bridges between people, deepening understanding, and inspiring awe.
I am at the service of experiment, rhyme, novelty and metaphor. This is the "medium" of all of my work, in an effort to make the invisible seen, the inaudible heard, the unfeeled felt, and the unthinkable thought.
From galleries and museums to theaters and other stages, as well as unexpected places, my hope is to spread inspiration around to people of all ages, of all persuasions.

    1998 — 2005 (7 years )

Excellent record in keeping projects on-time and in-budget.
