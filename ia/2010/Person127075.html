Larry Miller’s Summary

Experienced Management Consultant with over 15 years of Consulting Experience and more than 30 years of business success!

My entire business life has been spent in Convenience Retailing and business related to Convenience Retailing - Where the customer is only satisfied if you have what he wants, when he wants it!

This experience has come in the traditional Convenience Industry, with 17 years at 7-Eleven, in Quick Service Restaurants while working as a consultant with McDonald's Corporation in the late 90's, and in the development and implementation of new products within the Foodservice Equipment Industry as a consultant/ VP-GM with Duke Manufacturing Company.

Additionally, to round out the expertise, I have also been involved in four separate Bankruptcy work outs and turn-around projects all in the C-store business.

Early on in my career I set a goal to be trained and to spend actual time in position in Operations, Marketing, Merchandising, Human Resources, Store Design, Real Estate Analysis & Acquisition, Security, & Training to become a true Industry Generalist who has a complete holistic view of our Industry. I pride myself on being a person who still tries to learn something useful every day!

In my most recent endeavor, I gained in-depth knowledge of publishing, written communication, website development, social networking and overall management of a true start-up while planning for long-term diversification. While timing was not in our favor, we truly put forth a new and unique way of looking at our customers. During this time I continued to hone my sales and marketing skills, as "Nothing happens of consequence, until a sale is made and goods or services are exchanged!"
