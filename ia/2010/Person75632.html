Gino Federici’s Summary

Professionally involved in the performing arts since 1971 on an international scale. Spent 23 years performing in Las Vegas. At the young age of 67 I decided it was time for a change and left Las Vegas for Michigan for a two year sabbatical. I was looking for a new direction in life that would excite me and energize me for the next 50 years.

After losing two wives to cancer in the span of 5 years - the second wife in 2002 - my soul seemed to be yearning for renewal. Believing that renewal was possible and I would be shown my new "meaning," I left Las Vegas in July of 2007 and moved to the Michigan countryside with a new lady who became my wife and guru.

A strong proponent of the philosophy that following one's heart is the true way to life's fulfillment, I began producing a cooking show called The Italian Singing Gourmet in the fall of 2009. Now, with renewed vigor, vision and interest expressed by fans worldwide following my weekly kitchen's escapades, I'm in the process of gathering sponsors to produce a weekly half an hour program to be broadcast on the upcoming new Cooking Channel in July.

Once The Italian Singing Gourmet takes off I plan to resume my international concert career. This will be the time when "my story" - expressed through my program "The Power of Purpose" - will have the ability to impact new generations. Presently I present webinars at the Leaders Café Foundation, whose goal is to embolden 20 million leaders worldwide by the year 2020.

The Leaders Café Foundation is also a supporter of Wisdom In Your Pocket Press, the company Boni and I created to publish "The ABC's of Life™" booklet series; pocket size booklets where each letter of the Alphabet presents a value or a principle for a life of leadership and fulfillment. They are truly: "Wisdom In Your Pocket™."

Please visit our websites for further information. Thank you. I have enjoyed sharing these few moments with you.

Life is an exciting journey!

  • President/Owner

    ISG Productions

    (Entertainment industry)

    August 2008 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    ISG Productions is the Television arm of Gino Federici. The Italian Singing Gourmet is the first of a series of television programs to be filmed on location in Italy. Currently being produced weekly in Gino's kitchen, the show takes Gino's love for cooking and his talent as an international recording artist and combines them into a weekly half an hour program, garnering fans since its launch in August of 2009. The show can be seen on www.YouTube.com/GinoFederici and www.TheItalianSingingGourmet.com.

    It's ISG Production's vision to create a weekly series to be filmed in Italy, bringing viewers spectacular cuisine, framed by the local traditions of each region in a genuine, entertaining home-style approach.

    At The Italian Singing Gourmet we celebrate all that is Italy and the Italian culture. We cook, we laugh and sing and show that "La Dolce Vita" is not a privilege of the rich but a way of life where family, friends and neighbors come together to celebrate one another around good food, a glass of wine and lots of "allegria" (cheerfulness)..

    Every week we shoot at a different location. From Agri-Turismo farms (Agricultural tourism) to the local "trattoria" in the heart of Trastevere in Rome, or the "special restaurant" locals swear by, Gino will present a regional dish while interacting with the chef in Italian at the market and by the stove, at the pastry shop (pasticceria) or at the gelato store (gelateria); traveling by car or by scooter, creating the opportunity to shoot scenes in black & white with subtitles when appropriate, giving the show a "retro" as well as a contemporary look.

    Occasional guests from time to time will share a song or two or showcase fashion or discuss the arts and il "bel vivere" (the good living) over an espresso or a glass of wine…but, primarily…it will be a cooking show!
