Stu Benner’s Summary

EMConsulting was founded to provide Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC) / Electromagnetic Environmental Effects (EEE or E3) consulting engineering services to clients in aerospace, military, telecom, broadcast, and consumer industries. I help clients design electronic equipment and systems to prevent or eliminate electrical interference to their equipment while ensuring their equipment is compatible with other nearby electronic equipment, systems, and natural interference.

By providing guidance and review during the definition, design, and development phases of projects, I help clients reduce overall cost, schedule, and technical risk. Realizing that up-front EMC design support is sometimes unavailable or impractical, I help clients diagnose and fix problems found during EMC qualification or certification testing.

The breadth and depth of nearly 30 years in corporate engineering environments provides a strong skill set for preventing and solving problems in complex systems and has provided a stepping-stone in the formation of EMConsulting. I have worked in the aerospace and military business in engineering and engineering leadership positions being responsible for technical, cost, and schedule performance.

My experience has focused on analog design, RF communications, EMC, EMI, Signal Integrity (SI), Power Integrity (PI), and TEMPEST. Since 2000, I have concentrated on the EMC design of electronic systems and circuits used on air, land, and marine military platforms and commercial aircraft. I have developed and implemented guidelines and processes that have improved ‘first pass’ EMC test success on military avionics from 5% to better than 95%.

My experience extends from system to circuit level design and test of electronic equipment on defense, scientific, and commercial spacecraft, and associated test equipment and ground stations. I have extensive experience in the successful diagnosis and remediation of ground test and on-orbit spacecraft anomalies.
