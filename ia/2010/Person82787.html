Jim Hooker’s Summary

I have over twenty years in hospitality logistics, warehousing and installation. In the last few years I have been hands on with project management involving interior and exterior renovation with customized millwork and FF&E installation.

Through ELS and my global network of Specialists, I want to be your "Hospitality Go To Guy".

People Ask Me: What does your company do and what makes you different from your competitors?

Answer: We provide global regional or local management in the areas of FF&E installation, warehousing, logistics, construction and renovation that can be aligned with client headquarters or wherever most of your activity is managed. We have over 800 worldwide operations across more than 50 countries. Being headquartered in the USA, we have crews and over 250 domestic operations here in the states as well.

The main difference between my competitors and what we do is explained very simply. Each of my competitors offer great services, but we are the only group that can facilitate warehousing, FF&E installation, complete logistics, full construction and or renovation services for hotels, resorts, casinos, cruise ships and theme parks. We service most of the major flags and do a lot of work for Disney including their cruise ships.

By "Uniting the Elites in Hospitality" I network with a consortium of businessmen who supply funding, operational management and/or construction management for Luxury Hotels, Resorts and the Gaming Industry.

If you are seeking additional funding, operational management, construction management or even a JV partner and have a build out and finance plan, we can help. Whether it's an equity participation, project fee, or monthly retainer we offer these services for hotels, resorts and casinos world wide.

From the streets of Las Vegas USA to many points around the globe they are currently involved with financing, procurement, operational, construction and project management.
