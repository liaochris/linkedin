Phil Salvador’s Summary

34 years experience as Petroleum geologist with ConocoPhillips in a variety of clastic reservoir settings. Now a geologic consultant, with emphasis on production geology and field development. Waiting for final confirmation of employment as deveopment geologist with Qatar Petroleum, Doha, Qatar.

Phil Salvador’s Specialties:

Production geologist with extensive experience working for a major oil company. Can address the entire geologic spectrum of oil and gas reservoir development, from reservoir characterization and hydrocarbon volume estimation to development drilling. Effective and dynamic member or leader of multi-disciplinary teams. Encouraging mentor of junior staff. Effective user of reservoir mapping, log analysis and well planning software. Understanding of the geology of numerous reservoir settings.
