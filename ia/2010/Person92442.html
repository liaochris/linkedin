BIO:

J. David Morris (JD) focuses on buyouts starting at $5 million EBITA. He also serves on the board of advisors and board of directors of several private and publicly traded companies.

JD's merchant banking and investment banking activities have resulted in many successful deals. JD advises clients ranging on business strategy, mergers and acquisitions, revenue models and future growth opportunities. His focus today is to help high net worth individuals (HNWI), pension funds, and other funds acquire assets.

He has been a speaker at various industry forums, has been quoted in numerous leading publications, such as the Wall Street Journal, and has made several appearances on CNET and CNBC.

He received a B.A. in economics with mathematics from Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia and Omega Rho student with focus in operations research (#4 world) from The George Washington University in Washington, D.C.
