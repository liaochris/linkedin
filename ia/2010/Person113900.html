Kelly Hunt’s Summary

I have experience as a contract manager, writer, website editor, newspaper editor, senior reporter, copy editor and photographer. My career has always had its base in print journalism, but I am interested in public policy and international public affairs, as well. I have gained substantial knowledge in the Web sector while being a communications specialist at EPA, focusing on social media and Web developer. And I gained experience during graduate school, operating on both Drupal and Django content management systems; I plan on developing those skills with on-the-job training and during official continuing education classes.
I believe now is one of the most exciting times to be in communications, with new technology and social media opening doors we've never seen before. I embrace the new technology that has helped me become a better, well-rounded, more efficient writer, photographer, Web master, communications specialist, contract manager and copy editor.
