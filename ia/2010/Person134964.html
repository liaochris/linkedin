Marlene Edmonds’s Summary

Marlene Edmonds, Principal of Mind2Market, LLC began her career as research chemist for the FDA and then spent seven-plus years in sales and business development positions at SmithKline Beecham and Bio-Pharm Pharmaceutics where she helped launch several brands and services across several therapeutic categories including: CNS, CV, and anti-infectives.

In 1998, Marlene joined Astra Merck as a Forecasting Analyst and then directed marketing research initiatives for two compounds in Phase II/III development. Her success in these roles led to a New Products Marketing role where she developed a global lifecycle plan for a Phase III CV product. She went on to accept a Senior Product Strategist position on Toprol-XL, a blockbuster cardiovascular product for AstraZeneca.

In 2004, Marlene joined Shire as a Product Director where she developed commercialization plans and launched a product for bipolar disorder. Shortly after, she was asked to lead preparation for Shire’s next product launch, Vyvanse for ADHD. Not yet three years on the market, in April 2010, Vyvanse surpassed a 14% share of the ADHD market and over $700M in sales. In 2008 and 2010, Marlene was named one of the top 25 DTC marketers by DTC Perspectives and Parade Magazine and accepted a number of team awards including: best integrated campaign, best digital media, and best branded print campaign. In January 2010 the Vyvanse Team was named as Small Pharma Marketing Team of the Year by MM&M.

Marlene earned a bachelor’s degree in chemistry from the College of William and Mary and an MBA from St. Joseph’s University in Philadelphia. She began her consulting practice in April 2010 and currently resides in Wayne, PA.
