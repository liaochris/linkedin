Jonathan Schreiber’s Summary

Jonathan Schreiber, SVP, Business Development of Pipl.com, the leading people search engine has been a leader of the intersection between technology (inclusive of wireless) and entertainment for the last 13 years. In his last position, Mr. Schreiber was the, SVP of Strategy & Business Development for PeopleJam, previous to PeopleJam, Mr. Schreiber was President & CEO of Xingtone the leading mobile software and service company for the independent content market. Previous to Xingtone, Mr. Schreiber was the SVP of Strategy & Business Development, reporting to the Chairman and CEO of Island Def Jam Recordings, responsible for all business and revenue outside of the companies core business of music. Before IDJ, Mr. Schreiber was the founder of Infinite Mobility (a Mobile Service Provider to the Sports and Entertainment industries), mobileStorm (a Database management and marketing ASP) and part of the start-up team for Farmclub.com (Universal Music’s answer to MP3.com). Mr. Schreiber is an industry insider in the entertainment and technology industries, a regular on the speaking circuit and an advisor to some of the industries' most dynamic companies.
