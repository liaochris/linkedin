Anjum Hussain, CFA, CAIA’s Summary

• Investments:
As the Director of Risk Management in the Office of Investments at Case Western Reserve University (supporting their $1.6 billion endowment fund), I play a key role in the investment, asset allocation/risk budgeting decision-making process (building and deploying various quantitative models such as Mean Variance Optimization & Conditional Value at Risk Optimization) and derivative trading strategies (futures, swaps, options and structured notes). I am also involved in manager review and selection, systems development, as well as risk management. Prior to this, I was an Investment Analyst in the Investment Office at Oberlin College supporting their $700 million endowment fund. I have an institutional investment background, having spent several years as an equity research analyst/portfolio manager supporting the Victory Growth Mutual Fund (Victory Capital Management, Key Bank), as well as several other pension plans.

• Technology & Operations:
I have spent several years designing and implementing systems to support the Trust and Investment Management systems for Key Bank (and their predecessor banks Society and Ameritrust). Part of my experience includes managing the Technology and Operations for Victory Capital Management. I have advanced knowledge of MS Excel, MS Access, Visual Basic, SAS and SQL.

• Managerial/Supervisory Experience:
I have experience managing personnel and multiple projects simultaneously in my role as project manager as well as department manager. I have about 7 years of managerial/supervisory experience having managed systems analysts, programmers and clerical staff.
