Suzanne Shinnie’s Summary

I founded Techs in the City in 2006 to provide a friendly IT support service to Small Businesses and Voluntary Sector organisations that need to outsource their computer management. The company provides a helpdesk service and also the necessary hardware, software and networking structure that many small organisations don't have the skills to implement.
