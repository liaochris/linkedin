Vera Marie Badertscher’s Summary

For 14 years I have written articles, essays, and web articles for national (eg. National Geographic Traveler, American Style, Islands), local and regional (eg. Arizona Highways), various AAA magazines (Midwest, Indiana, Southern region,and others) specialty luxury magazines (Rolls Royce Owners and Steinway & Sons), and several web sites. While I specialize in travel, I also enjoy writing about anything that makes me curious, which includes animals, people and inanimate objects. With the decline of print media, I have turned my attention to the Internet and developed my blog, A Traveler's Library as my main writing outlet.

Because of past career experience in political consulting, my direct mail and other marketing materials get attention and results. Currently creating web content for several web sites.

Recently completed a joint project biography of Quincy Tahoma, Navajo artist with writing partner Charnell Havens.

Hope to continue expanding markets and exploring new topics. See personal web site for more details.
