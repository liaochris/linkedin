Troy L. Redstone, AIF ®’s Summary

As the retirement plan practice leader for Bearence Management Group, I serve as the Director of Retirement Plans for the entire firm, overseeing production and operations for offices from Minneapolis to Des Moines to Kansas City. As a Retirement Plan Consultant I help plan participants close the retirement gap and I help plan sponsors sleep better at night.

Bearence Management Group is one of the fastest growing independent brokers in the upper midwest, and is already one of the largest consulting firms in the Twin Cities and Des Moines. With a focus on Redefining Risk, Bearence specializes in bringing a holistic approach to the client with expertise in Risk, Investments, Communications, Consulting, HR Outsourcing, Employee Benefits, and Retirement Planning, both for the corporate client as well as the individual client.

My experience includes serving as a plan sponsor and plan manager; in retirement education, client services and sales; in the 401k market and the 403b market; with startup plans in the micro-market and jumbo plans in the large market. My specialty is working with the mid-large market client, bringing solutions generally provided only to Fortune 500 companies.

We are committed to full fee disclosure, at the trustee level and the participant level, and we only work with vendors who share these values. We built Bearence around the values of consistency, transparency and accountability.

We're working to make a difference in how Americans retire.
