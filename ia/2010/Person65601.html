Eric Axelrod’s Summary

Data management is a concept which, even in our high-tech modern world, continues to elude organizations large and small, private and public. While many organizations do an adequate job generating data, they struggle to turn it into information which can be used to make decisions.

Business Intelligence market leaders such as Microsoft, SAP Business Objects, IBM, and Oracle sell products which allow organizations of all sizes to manage data and distribute information. The uniqueness of Business Intelligence concepts creates the need for specialists.

Business Intelligence is my specialty.

I have worked on BI projects for companies large and small. My experience spans many different industries, the majority of which has been in the healthcare sector.

* Designing SAP Business Objects Enterprise architecture
* Performing an SAP to Business Objects 3.x integration
* Implementing several Business Objects Enterprise deployments.
* Implementing Enterprise-wide BI platforms
* Administering multi-terabyte databases
