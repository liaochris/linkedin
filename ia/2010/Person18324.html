Atul Mehta’s Summary

Atul Mehta has been building a professional career in information technology for over 18 years building rock solid experience in software engineering, architecture, development, debugging, training, and technical support. A well respected thought leader in the industry, his vision and attention to detail has found appeal with many clients. He has expertise in developing applications using Java, J2EE, Oracle, and Open Source technologies. His consulting experience spans creation of several business portals along with integration of custom applications with SAP, PeopleSoft, Oracle Applications, and Siebel using various middleware technologies. He co-authored a once popular "Oracle8 How-To" book published by Waite Group Press in 1998.

Looking to network with strong motivated partners with backgrounds in recruiting, technology, and finance. Freelance telecommuting requests are welcome. Feel free to send inquiries to atul.v.mehta@gmail.com.
