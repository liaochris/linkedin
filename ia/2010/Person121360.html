Larry Russell’s Summary

I provide comprehensive financial planning services to clients in the greater Pasadena, California area. In addition, I designed, developed, and sell VeriPlan to end users and financial advisors. VeriPlan is a comprehensive and automated financial planning software application that is used as a decision support tool in lifetime personal financial decision making.

I am also a successful corporate development and marketing executive with a versatile record of accomplishment. During my corporate career, I have managed numerous multi-million dollar product lines, strategic partnerships, technology licensing agreements, and M&A transactions. I have lead hundreds of personnel either directly or through cross-functional teams.
