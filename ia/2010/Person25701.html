Bob Stewart’s Summary

Bob Stewart is the Founder and Chairman of VitalLock - The Privacy Company. Over the past year Bob led the boutique development services of enterprise scale rich Internet applications (ESRIA) for high-profile, media and consumer sites including; Adobe, Boeing, BT, CBS, CNET, Disney, Facebook, Fidelity, Gomez, Hyundai, Intuit, Kodak, NFL, Ribbit, SearchMe, Times Mirror and other public and stealthy Web 2.0 startups.

Stewart served as Internet Chief Technology Officer of Motorola's $1B Internet and Networking Group where he earned Motorola's highest information technology technical ladder rank of Strategic Adviser Technical Analyst and Webmaster of eTAC the Gartner acclaimed electronic Technical Assistance Center.

Bob has performed technology due diligence for a number of venture capital firms in North America and Europe for whom he has at times served as an interim Chief Technology Officer to select portfolio companies. Bob has also been involved, both as an investment adviser and a transition manager, in substantial M&A activity including a $220M merger with BellSouth and a $290M acquisition from Compaq.

Bob also earned global recognition as Chief Technology Officer for CMGI portfolio companies Planet Direct, CMGI Solutions, Zip2 and MyWay.com.
