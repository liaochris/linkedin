Carmen Cooley-Graham’s Summary

A Realtor and San Diego Native who assist both buyers and sellers in all aspects of real estate, especially short sales where my negotiation experience is invaluable. From first time home buyer to savvy commercial investors- REO to REIT.As a certified in California Short Sale Foreclosure Resource, I am the one stop aid to helping people to connect to the right advisor to aid them in their distressed situation. A Home Retention Consultant that goes the extra mile to assist home owners preserve their home if possible.
