Jodie Niles’s Summary

To begin, I have a vast array of experience with newsletters, direct mailings and other marketing materials, as well as copywriting for a national retail website. I worked for a health and wellness magazine writing monthly articles, editing, proofing and even working the sales end of things a bit. I also freelanced for other publications in the Milwaukee area for many years. Plus, I was an Independent Representative for Silpada Designs, a direct sales company that features sterling silver jewelry. That experience helped me to further hone my people, managerial and marketing skills.

I was a member of the Women in Communications Southeastern WI Chapter for two years, one of which I served on the Communications Committee. I've been involved with various organizations and events in my community, done some local theatre activities and am great with PR-related activities on a volunteer basis. I enjoy being involved in things I am passionate about.

I spent six months as a Marketing Assistant and Event Coordinator to a well-established growth and leadership development trainer, who is also an author/speaker/TV personality. I also worked with a Spiritual Life Coach and Angelic Message Artist.

These experiences inspired me to enroll at the School of Spiritual Psychology, where I have dug deep into the truth of who I am and how I can not only lead an authentic life, but help others do so as well. I am in the process of becoming a certified Empowered Spiritual Life Coach and I am currently writing my first book. I hope to incorporate motivational speaking into my career plans. I'll then seek publishers and agents.

In March of 2009, I launched my website, www.journeyswithjodie.com, which features a blogspot as well as travel planning services. It also has a calendar that lists the monthly experiential support groups on self-help/life betterment topics, which I facilitate.

Jodie Niles’s Specialties:

Writing, copywriting, editing, proofing, researching, interviewing, organizing, speaking, planning and....on my way to life coaching, motivational speaking, and currently leading experiential group workshops on self-discovery.
