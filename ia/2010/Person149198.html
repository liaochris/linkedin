Michael Pierantozzi’s Summary

Michael Pierantozzi has over 20 years of entrepreneurial leadership experience in the information technology industry, combining knowledge of strategy, innovation, intellectual property (IP) and operations. He has a proven track record of creating profitable growth opportunities in emerging and established markets through strong partnerships, and delivering innovations that produce economic value for customers and investors. Michael is known as an energizing, collaborative leader skilled at building teams and driving innovation and operational initiatives, while providing a clear, strategic view - globally.

Michael holds a M.S. from the Management, Science and Engineering program at Stanford University, where he focused on Technology Entrepreneurship. He also holds a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Drexel University. He is married to Kim, an M.D. in private practice, and together they are raising their son Paul in Mountain View, California.

On weekends, you can find Michael sailing SF Bay from OCSC in Berkeley!
