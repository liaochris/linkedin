Jeff Zelaya’s Summary

My name is Jeffrey Zelaya and I am a Senior majoring in Marketing Management. I received my Associate degree from Broward College and transferred over to FIU last year in order to continue my studies. Recently, I interned at ADP in the Major Accounts Division, where I assisted Workforce Business Consultants in preparing presentations and researching potential clients. I am an alumnus of the Hispanic National Internship Program (http://www.hnip.net) & also a member of Toastmasters International.

When I am not busy with work & school most of my time is occupied with volunteer projects and self development. I lead a volunteer group of about 30 people that regularly visits the Broward Outreach Center to serve meals to the homeless. I am also an ambassador for the Hispanic Association of Colleges & Universities. As an ambassador I visit colleges to present information to students through seminars & career fairs about internship & scholarship opportunities. To unwind & for exercise I ride my mountain bike on the beautiful bike trails at Oleta, Markham & Amelia Earhart Park.
