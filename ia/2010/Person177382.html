Ron Miriello’s Summary

At a grade school Professionals Day I remember explaining to an eager group of six year olds what I did for a living, "You guys are all naturally curious and very creative right now. But as you get older, people tend to become less and less curious and creative. So they hire people like us to be curious and creative for them. That's my job." They seemed to grasp that simple explanation of essentially what we do at Miriello Grafico.

Miriello Grafico was founded 25 years ago. Today Miriello Grafico is a graphic communications firm that provides the strategies and coordinated creative implementations that help under-performing brands realize their full potential.

Our focus is on brand expression. Inspired creative strategies and creative results that can establish closer customer connections for businesses. In a world increasingly numbed by globalization and statistic-driven sameness, we believe brands that stay relevant and creative have much better odds of success.

Ron Miriello’s Specialties:

Brand strategic planning and consultation, corporate identity, brand standards, collateral, packaging, merchandising, catalog, web communications.
