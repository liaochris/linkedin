I have worked on a number of different projects each including different responsibilities. The following is a summary of my experience:

Field Engineer - Prepared Shop drawings and sketches as needed to coordinate concrete pours and facilitate answers to RFIs. Contributed, Recorded and Distributed the 3-Week Look-Ahead-Schedule on a weekly basis.

Project Engineer - Was responsible for coordinating concrete pours, coordinating and facilitating the PG&E application including final inspections of major equipment.

Superintendent/ Project Engineer - Created Project Schedule that was used throughout the project to track the major activities of the project including the final completion date.
