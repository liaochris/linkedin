Mike Ward’s Summary

Entrepreneur, Web Architect, domain investor. I studied computer science and math in college. I've designed and built web applications for companies small and large since then. I also manage a few domain portfolios, and some networks of sites.

Web Architects is a company of mine focused on building web applications for small businesses to realize productivity gains through automation of core business processes. We design applications, websites, APIs and offer web hosting and consulting services.

Boca Names is a company I founded which is a domain name reseller, offering varied services including domain appraisals, domain acquisitions and portfolio management.

Currently interested in consulting opportunities involving bringing ruby on rails development into enterprise environments. Also looking for partnerships to develop networks of revenue-generating websites for developing markets or up-and-coming TLDs. And in general I'm interested in high-risk, high reward opportunities on the web.

Mike Ward’s Specialties:

Experience designing and building web applications and web service APIs on many platforms with various tools. Broad and deep understanding of Internet technologies. Proficient in several web dev technologies but my current preference is developing rails apps. Have run a domain registrar and a hosting reseller company for several years. Have developed company websites for hundreds of small and medium-sized businesses. Experience acquiring, selling and managing domain portfolios.
