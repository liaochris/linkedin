Mike Robinson, CCIM’s Summary

Mike has been in various aspects of real estate for over 30 years. Mike's experience includes the following: Vice president of the nation's third largest retail real estate investment trust; vice president of a west coast developer, owner and operator of office, retail, industrial and r&d properties and as a real estate negotiator at the Century City office of one of the nation's leading law firms.

We provide the following services in the Beach Cities of Manhattan Beach, Hermosa Beach, Redondo Beach, the coast up through Malibu and other select communities in the Los Angeles Region:

-Acquisition and disposition of investment properties
-Leasing of commercial properties
-Purchase and sale of residential properties
-Short-term and long-term leases of residential properties
-Specialists in serving entertainment clients' real estate requirements, from short-term residential leases for visiting talent, to residential purchases, and consultation and representation in the acquisition and disposition of investment properties.

Mike holds California Broker and Officer/Broker licenses. Mike is a Certified Commercial Investment Member (CCIM), a member of ICSC (International Council of Shopping Centers ) and is TRC certified (International Consortium of Real Estate Associations, Transnational Referral Certification). He is also a Realtor® and a member of the Beverly Hills/Greater Los Angeles Association of Realtors®, the South Bay Association of Realtors®, The MLS and The South Bay MLS.
