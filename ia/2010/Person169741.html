Brenda Ray, Esq., CPA’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE -
Brenda has over 19 years working in national and international accounting firms and law firms and brings a wealth of experience in areas such as business, tax, estate planning, retirement, and asset protection techniques and strategies for companies, business owners, and individuals.

TAX AND ESTATE PLANNER -
Advises individuals in income and estate tax savings strategies, estate planning, trusts, probate avoidance, asset protection strategies, compensation and stock options, retirement and education planning, life insurance for retirement, family legacy, charitable and estate tax savings, other tax saving techniques such as stretch IRAs, Irrevocable Trusts, and Private Foundations, Lifetime gifting programs to children or children's trusts utilizing family limited partnerships and other discounted gifting strategies to remove value of assets from estate

BUSINESS ADVISOR -
Advises national and international corporations, mid and small corporations, family owned businesses, charitable organizations, physicians and other professional practices in areas such as general business and income tax saving strategies, choice of entity, asset protection planning, retirement planning, business succession planning, estate freezing techniques, tax advantanged techniques to transfer business interests to children/family, mergers and acquisitions, like-kind and other tax-free exchanges, corporate tax, partnership tax, S corporations, LLCs, individual income tax

TAX DISPUTES AND TAX PLANNING -
Federal and State Tax Planning
IRS representation
Represenation before the Ohio Deparment of Taxation

NETWORKING -
