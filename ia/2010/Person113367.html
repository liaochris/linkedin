Keith Feighery’s Summary

Keith is a professional technologist with over 12 years experience programme and project managing, designing, building and delivering enterprise software applications and developing online retail businesses. Keith has worked with large international software providers such as Siebel Systems, Eontec and Allfinanz. He provides strategic consultancy services to senior management teams on how to implement internal and external organisational social and collaborative strategies along with technical auditing and third party product evaluation services.

Keith has extensive consultancy and management experience working on large scale business transformation projects. He is an expert provider of corporate workshop and facilitation services. He is a leading professional in helping organisations understand new and emerging technologies with a particular focus on social technologies and facilitates the integration of these technologies into all aspects of corporate channels such as internal communications, collaboration networks, marketing and sales, and customer service.

Digital Insights provides strategic consultancy, management and production services to organisations who are implementing digital and online solutions applications for b2b and b2c customers.

Keith lectures in Digital Media, Web and Digital Strategies for both B2B and B2C and Online Sales Optimisation for DIT Aungier St, Dublin Business School, Griffith College, and the International Business and Technical College as well as providing training services to the Dublin enterprise centres.
