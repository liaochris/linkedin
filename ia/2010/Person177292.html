Ron Holst’s Summary

Ron Holst has qualified himself as a responsible marketing manager through his experience with TradeBeam, a global market company and Curon Medical, a medical device company by instituting marketing logistics for profit. Known for his “can-do” attitude, Ron’s tactics for designing databases for lead generation and implementing promotional campaigns has established effective marketing platforms at both companies.

As a Marketing Manager for Tradebeam, Ron was accountable for managing all trade shows and promotional campaigns. Ron’s strong interpersonal skills with outsourcing design firms and self-motivation to work independently contributed to positive results directly relating to B2B marketing, lead generation, and email marketing. As a Marketing Coordinator for Curon Medical Ron was responsible for shaping the leadership of the direct marketing database that resulted in able-bodied leads and worked with sales to generate and distribute leads. Ron’s coordination of trade shows and implementation of processes supported his ability to market the product and influence lead generation through to close.

Ron’s start-up experience has allowed him the opportunity to wear several different hats and his enthusiastic participation with his previous performance teams exemplifies a strong desire to excel in an opportunist environment. Ron starts and completes his work successfully because he pays attention to detail and has excellent time management and organization skills. Ron has experience with working with outsourced design firms and web developers. Ron has a strong rapport with people and his high energy and creativity assures success in any project.
