Ronald J Kroeger CSP’s Summary

Highly motivated staffing & business development professional who has enjoyed working in the industry of connecting talented people with great companies for 17 years. Since 1995 I have been involved with the staffing and recruiting industry and continue to provide access to people with skills and companies with a mission. Throughout my career I have learned and continue to learn about people and what it is that drives them to do what they do best, as well, I have learned and continue to learn about companies and organizations and what drives them to pursue their mission and vision. This learning process has brought me to a place of appreciation for those trying to carve out a name for themselves’ and make a difference in this wonderful world we live. I look to further partner with the great ones and utilize our mutual strengths to achieve goals and pursue accomplishments.
