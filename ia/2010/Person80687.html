Heather Bachman’s Summary

Former White House Intern and campaign manager, Heather Bachman is a increasingly recognized political strategist and correspondent. While earning her Bachelor Degree in Political Science, Bachman began two political programs through Monmouth University's radio station: The Bachman Group and The Political Hour. As a writer for Calder.net and TheCollegePress.com, Bachman interviewed many members of the political community.
Currently, Bachman is in talks with many media stations for both a radio and television program. She hopes not only to bring politics to the youth but also allow the other side of the story that is politics to be finally heard.
