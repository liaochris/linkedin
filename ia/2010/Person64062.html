Elliott Fey’s Summary

Elliott Fey is owner of Fey Ventures LLC which owns, in part, Metaphor LLC and IACO Media LLC. Elliott oversees all operations and finances of Metaphor which is a business concept developer and marketing consulting firm. He is also Co-founder of IACO Media which develops web applications and database back-end technology for content driven web sites. IACO also owns majority shares of Division-Six, a hosting company located in Bloomfield, Iowa. Metaphor and IACO have been developing marketing strategies and web presences for their clients since 1999 and 2001 respectively.

Prior to joining partners in Metaphor and IACO, Elliott served as vice president of client services and interactive services for Toma & Associates in Denver, CO, an award winning advertising and marketing firm. Elliott acquired new clients and developed new interactive media strategies for national brands like HH Brown Shoes, Gerry Baby Products, Autralian Outback, Hewlett-Packard, Telluride and Aspen Ski Areas, Colorado Real Estate Developers, and Head Sportswear to name a few. Elliott also directed german business activity and developed new business relationships in Germany for Quark Inc. during his time with the company in the US and Germany.

Elliott Fey is a highly technical executive with a broad skill set drawn from diverse experience. He speaks German fluently and is conversational in Spanish, French, Dutch, and Italian. Languages are a serious hobby for Elliott as he is currently studying Chinese, Japanese and Arabic. Elliott spends his free time in the outdoors and developing new internet business ventures with his family and partners. He resides in Carbondale, Colorado.
