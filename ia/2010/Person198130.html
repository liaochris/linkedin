Susie Holland’s Summary

Partners in Marketing provides freelance and project based marketing consultancy; helping businesses maximise their marketing success. I support clients based in Cheshire, Manchester and the wider North West.

I advise businesses across a range of sectors on how to establish, review or refresh their business development and marketing.

Although I work across a range of business sectors, I have a particular specialism in services marketing including:

* Professional services
(accountants, law firms, surveyors, IFAs, consultants, coaches etc)

* Healthcare services
(private/NHS hospitals, practices, consultancies and insurers)


I was a professional services marketing specialist for 15 years, spending seven years with Grant Thornton (RSM Robson Rhodes) and seven years in healthcare marketing in the independent/private sector and within the NHS.

I'm a Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM) member with a CIM postgraduate diploma in marketing and have been a Chartered Marketer since 2001.
