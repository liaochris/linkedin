Rebecca Olkowski’s Summary

Graduate of CEO SPACE Forum 709.
Club President for the San Fernando Valley of Los Angeles

CEO SPACE is a private club for Entrepreneurs. CEO SPACE hosts five business trade forums each year. Our mission is to reduce cost and time to reach growth goals for our members. CEO SPACE employs advanced networking for CEO-to-CEO deal making. Clients become life time members to an exclusive club to upgrade MBA level business skill sets, rapidly develop client base, and to accelerate resource acquisition in a wide application of areas. Investors and Venture firms welcome. CEO SPACE produces ongoing support through a growing global system of local city business clubs.

Teen Entrepreneur Programs are offered in July and December Forums. Retreat Weeks attract CEOs at the top, those in the middle as well as development space CEOs. CEO SPACE does not advertise or promote membership. Member referrals invite business owners to join. Advanced University level skills training rapidly improves management skills

CEOSpace is endorsed by Bob Proctor, Mark Victor Hanson, Jack Canfield, Harv Eker, John Gray, Les Brown and many other top speakers, business owners, and investment firms.

If you are looking for more clients, business plan, sequencing, branding, marketing, social media, licensing, distribution, board of directors, vision team, capital, you will want to find out more about us. Please visit our website and watch or extensive list of DVD's customized for individual projects and businesses
