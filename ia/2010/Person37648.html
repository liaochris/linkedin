Chris Middleton’s Summary

A degree qualified and highly experienced IT specialist with a strong service ethos, specialising in the support of business critical systems. Payment systems subject matter expert. Personable team player with a sound technical background. Highly analytical, calm under pressure and excellent at prioritising.

• ITIL Version 3 qualified, with extensive experience of working within the ITIL service management framework.
• Specialist in payment processing with experience of BACS, UK Faster Payments, and real-time payment authorisation systems.
• Exceptional ability to communicate complex technical issues to non-technical audiences at all levels.
• Excellent problem solving skills backed up by both technical and business knowledge ensure appropriate prioritisation of incidents.
• Strong technical background includes UNIX, Oracle and SQL plus a wide variety of other platforms and technologies.
• Skilled at root-cause analysis to identify problems and design solutions.
• Proactive approach to risk spotting and problem management.
• Equally comfortable working on own initiative or liaising with local or offshore teams and suppliers, ensuring that the required resources are applied to incident resolution.
