Ram Mohan’s Summary

Manage the strategy and overall business direction of Afilias -- help shape the company's strategic vision and tactical direction. Lead company through successful acquisitions and expansion through M&As - including successful integration of new teams without a loss of cultural and ethical values.

Ram has been instrumental in identifying and developing policies that affect Internet users today and in the future through his work with various Internet standards groups.

Selected by the Philadelphia Business Journal as one of the region's top up-and-coming business leaders, Ram was the recepient of the '40 Under 40' award in 2003.

Experienced in leveraging technology for business advantage and market leadership. Built technology, product management and delivery teams from the ground up, running on first-generation or n-generation technology, each with special challenges.

Ram serves as an advisor to startup firms, and entrepreneurs who have an idea but are figuring out how to make it reality.

Ram serves on the Board of ICANN, the global non-profit organization that provides technical coordination of the Internet's names and number resources, as well as on the Board of several startup companies in the Internet technology sector.

Ram Mohan’s Specialties:

Operational management, technology architecture, team building, leadership through change, M&A. Experienced Executive, Innovator, Startup Adviser, Technology Architect
