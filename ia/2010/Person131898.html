Marc Meyer’s Summary

I have held positions ranging from web developer, internet marketing specialist, software sales rep., & sales manager, to positions of VP of sales & marketing, Dir. of Internet marketing, Dir. of Social Media and SEO, to Dir. of e-commerce & CTO.

Those Co.'s include The Internet Consulting Group, Executrain, Computer Enterprises Inc., Marblejar, Mind Over Media, Emerson Direct and Digital Response Marketing Group.

My experience over the last 3+ years, has been focused on using social media strategies and content marketing in an effort to enhance the direct marketing and e-commerce space; Primarily to drive traffic,create brand buzz & enhance reputation management, these efforts have included educating, evangelizing and writing extensively about social media and marketing on my nationally recognized blog, to me taking a more of a consultive and educational type of role with numerous SMB's & Fortune 500 Co.'s on how best to implement social media into their overall digital marketing plan.

In parallel I continue to focus as well on SEO, SEM, email marketing & brand management for companies of all sizes.

My sales, marketing, and management background consisted of being one of the top sales people at CEI (Computer Enterprises Inc.) for almost 3 years. CEI is an app. development, integration and management services co. in which I generated over $9 million in revenue and was consistently a leader in everything from call volume, to opportunities uncovered, to training new hires, to closing sales. This resulted in quick advancement to EVP of sales.

Prior to that I cut my teeth in various tech sales & marketing positions, learning, growing and understanding all things related to web development, internet marketing $ software sales as I went along.

As we enter into the challenging & exciting uncharted waters of social media & the enterprise Web 2.0 environment, I think I am uniquely qualified to guide & lead companies into the deep end.
