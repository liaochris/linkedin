Chelse Benham’s Summary

Chelse Benham is a multi-EMMY© and Telly© award winning producer & creative director with 29 international, national and regional awards, including the Webby People's Voice Award. She received her Bachelor of Science in Radio/Television/Film Production from The University of Texas at Austin and her Masters degree from The University of Texas-Pan American (UTPA). She is an American Film Institute Fellow in Digital Content Design and twice a U.S. delegate at INPUT.

Integrating 18 years of diverse marketing and technical experience into the development a of social media strategy.

Ms. Benham produced for HGTV, A&E, The History Channel and CBS affiliate WVLT. She has extensive experience in television program development, promotions and marketing with emphasis on social media, integrated marketing, edu-gaming, and app building. She authored articles on business protocol and best practices, immersive experiences & interactive video games, and its future relationship with marketing and social media. She designed an iPhone game as a marketing tool for UTPA and garnered more than $600 thousand in resources for her department.

She was the Project Coordinator and co-designer of the planned Educational Programming Interactive Consortium (EPIC); multi-player interactive computer gaming and television collaboration for education with the Corporation of Public Broadcasting in partnership with Johns Hopkins University, USC, and UTPA.

She was a founding board member of the Texas Motion Picture Alliance and served on the Board of Directors for PBS KMBH TV 60 and the Rio Grande Valley Film Commission.

Ms. Benham seeks ventures that incorporate traditional broadcast marketing with edgy digital media, with an emphasis on social media strategy, to create strong Web presence, mobile applications, and immersive virtual expereinces.
