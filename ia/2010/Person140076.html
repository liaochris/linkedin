Megan Wheatley’s Summary

My main area of strength is communication, written or otherwise. I'm a natural at customer service. I enjoy helping people, and if the customer isn't actually crazy, I can make every interaction a great one.

I also have a deep understanding of human behavior and patterns in general. I have great ideas and I act on them. While holding a full-time job that I excel in, I also play with my band, write new songs, am working on a book, and am recording my own album. When I am not working on one of my many projects, I enjoy spending time with like-minded friends who keep me grounded and encourage me as much as I encourage them.

In addition, I am also a graphic designer and am constantly coming up with new and creative ideas for people (and myself) every day. Also, and I'm not going to be modest about this... I'm pretty funny and very fun to be around. I can make any job seem effortless and light.

My one weakness is that it is hard to keep up with myself. Everything comes in so quickly, I don't really know which direction to take. I could go into so many different fields. This is why I am involved in so many different things. I want to do it all.

I think, before it is all over, I will own my own store... a store that would be a meeting place for people, sell art and music, drink coffee, internet access, have shows at night.... I could work through it- help other musicians, which is what I really want to do..... something like that. Jury is still out. : )
