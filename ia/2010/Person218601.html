Zeke Camusio’s Summary

His Career as an Entrepreneur

Zeke Camusio is a serial entrepreneur. At the age of 18 he started his first company, Electrolife, selling consumer electronics such as digital cameras, iPods, and the like. It was a great first experience for Zeke but the market was very competitive and he had a hard time remaining profitable.

Six months later, Zeke Camusio founded Data Systems, an educational software venture. Things went really well and the company became extremely successful. Zeke realized that Data Systems became something bigger than he could handle at that very young age and decided to sell it.

When Zeke turned 20 he decided to start his third company, iSales. He was selling infomercial products using the Internet. This was the first time that Zeke heard about online marketing and he never knew that he would later become one of the most respected experts in this field.

Proxies Online was Zeke’s fourth company. It was an identity-protection system for those who wanted to surf the Internet anonymously. Then came Lux Leathers, a designer leather goods business.

The Outsourcing Company is Zeke’s sixth company. It’s an online marketing agency with offices in Aspen, Colorado and New York.

Some Facts About Zeke:

• He loves traveling, reading and doing sports.

• Zeke’s ultimate goal is to start an entrepreneurship school to give people the tools they need to make the world a better place to live.

• Zeke lives in Aspen, Colorado, one of the most beautiful places in the world, with his wife Tiana. Zeke works 12+ hours a day and he couldn’t love it more.

• Zeke is a columnist and Internet Marketing expert at major publications such as StartupNation, Entrepreneur.com, PartnerUp and Toilet Paper Entrepreneur.

• Zeke writes an entrepreneurship and marketing blog called Let’s Do It! You can read it at www.TheOutsourcingCompany.com/blog.
