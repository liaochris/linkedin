Vernon Adams’s Experience

  • Head of Technology Architecture & Strategy

    GeoPost UK

    (Public Company; Package/Freight Delivery industry)

    February 1999 — Present (11 years 1 month)

    GeoPost are one of the foremost UK Express Parcel Carriers incorporating DPD (formally Parceline), Interlink Express and Interlink Direct brands. A core division of the La Poste (France) group of companies.

    Responsible for shaping the Information Technology strategy to deliver the organisations ambitious vision and maintain it's competitive advantage.

    Headed the IT Development teams to consolidate and streamline GeoPost UK’s IT solutions by exploiting web technologies. Architected a single business process framework supporting the UK brands (Parceline & Interlink Express) to deliver significant cost savings across the operation. Implemented intuitive Customer web solutions for shipping and parcel tracking that delivered revenue generation with rapid year on year growth.

    The core operational solution provided the foundation to enable aggressive business growth and achieved seamless integration within the GeoPost Group across Europe (including Germany, France and Ireland). Delivered an integration platform for the large customer base to enable data exchange and application synchronisation.

  • IT Development Consultant

    Slimstock - Ridge Technology

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1997 — February 1999 (2 years 2 months)

    Slimstock are a leading Inventory Management Consultancy in Europe. Co-founded Ridge Technology as a solutions provider to Slimstock.

    Designed and developed leading edge inventory optimisation solutions requiring flexibility for world-wide distribution including multi-lingual capabilities, integration with ERP solutions and multi-database compatibility. Specialised solutions incorporated complex statistical formulas and techniques to improve stock management disciplines. Initial customers in Holland included Phillips, Lucent Technologies and Dutch Rail.

  • Head of IT Development

    Parceline

    (Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    February 1992 — January 1997 (5 years )

    Parceline were one of the leading Express Parcel Carriers in the UK. A core brand within the Mayne Nickless Group (Australia).

    Headed the IT Development teams to ignite and transform Parceline's technology solutions, delivering streamlined process excellence and cost reduction. The initiatives resulted in Parceline leading the UK express parcel carrier sector with innovations to support the operational workflow.

    Delivered Industry award winning solutions included package bar-coded routing, parcel tracking, warehouse management and document imaging. Parceline became the first UK parcel carrier to provide web parcel tracking services and pioneered hand-held technologies to enable a paperless collection & delivery process. The leading edge solutions and innovative ideas were consequently shared across the Mayne Nickless Group in Australia and Canada.
