Marilyn Kleinberg’s Summary

Since January '08 I have been serving as the Managing Director of eWomenNetwork(www.ewomennetwork.com) for Southern New Jersey chapter. Bringing professional women together to network and transact business throughout the southern New Jersey region. Look for events in Atlantic, Cape May Camden, Gloucester and surrounding counties.

Past president of Northeast High Alumni Association. Member and past president (Philadelphia chapter) of Women in Communications. Current and/or past member of PPRA, PRSA, IABC, AMA, PDMA, ISES and others.

In addition I own and operate a CruiseOne franchise in Egg Harbor Township. Prior to purchasing the franchise I was the VP of Special Events and Marketing for the Chamber of Commerce Southern New Jersey.
