Cynthia A Adams’s Summary

I love the work I do and the people, organizations and institutions I have the opportunity to work with!

I am a veteran in leadership and organizational change; I know how to achieve results and I know what it takes to navigate through the varied waters along the way.

A master coach, coaching C-level and senior leaders to maximize their contribution, capacity and personal fulfillment in leadership. Core in the work is also developing/enhancing the effectiveness of the collective executive team.

A partner with executives to create strategic focus in their organization through comprehensive strategic plan creation, alignment and strategic process as well as the design, facilitation and implementation of large-scale organizational transformation and engagement that is sustainable.

My clients hire me to partner with them in:

- Executive Coaching and Development that is integrated, holistic, and transformative
- Strategic Focus, Alignment, Buy in and Deployment
- Executive Leader Team Development and Alignment
- Whole Systems Change and Transformation
- Large Scale Employee Engagement and Commitment

I bring an integrated systems perspective combined with a practical "in the trenches" approach that only 24+ years of experience can offer.

For further reference, along with numerous articles, I co-authored the book, The Whole Systems Approach: Involving Everyone in the Company to Transform and Run Your Business. (Provo: Executive Excellence Publishing) and also contributed to two editions of The Change Handbook: Group Methods for Shaping the Future (1999 - San Francisco: Berrett Koehler Publications).
