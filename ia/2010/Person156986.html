Dennis O'Hara’s Summary

Having started out in sales at age 18 and now many years later having started 3 of my own business I am not sure what other goals to have other then to provide the best service and products that we can and to be the best boss and person I can be. The future of business is very bright and we are all on the cusp of some dramatic changes in this global economy.

My company Associated Call Centers provides complete call center services nationwide, we specialize in complex inbound programs for small and large companies.

My motto is "Try and leave things better then when you got there"

Dennis O'Hara’s Specialties:

Blending technology and people to create new opportunities. I also have experience in starting and developing new business and by extension sales and marketing. We have always made it a point to help our customers sell and market their services.
