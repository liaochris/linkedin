Personnel Source, Inc. / Staffing Specialist

I am proud to work within a professional, ethical & loyal organization; to be a part of the success in assisting others seeking to obtain their employment or career goals such as we have here at Personnel Source, Inc. Salem Branch Personnel Source, Inc. constantly strives to improve our service based business and meet those needs of our customers; from the companies we staff for to the potential employee that walks through our door.

Keeping up with the rapid shift in the field of employment is a difficult task. Personnel Source can help by recruiting and placing employees in a wide range of positions, from the Plant Floor to your CEO.

Our recruiting and hiring services provide many employers with the opportunity to expand their staffing levels, while cutting down on paperwork and administrative costs. With more than 30 years of experience in the field, Personnel Source attracts the top job candidates, and our staff works hard to place them in the perfect positions. You maintain supervisory control of your employees; we take care of the rest.
