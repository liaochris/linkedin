Jenny Dudley’s Summary

Development executive with 18 years experience in academic medicine and fundraising. Currently Vice President for Advancement at Baylor College of Medicine in Houston, Texas, where we are in the seventh year of a ten-year, $1 billion campaign.

As of January 2010, the Best Minds Best Medicine Campaign has raised $751M. Campaign highlights include two $100M gifts, a new Annual Fund program, and 45 new endowed chairs.

My experience crosses the spectrum of advancement activities, including major gift fundraising, foundation and corporate relations, marketing, campaign planning and management, communications, and development resource management.

Jenny Dudley’s Specialties:

Major gift fundraising; campaign planning; campaign communications; marketing; development strategy; development resource management; academic medicine; advancement.
