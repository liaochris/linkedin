Jed Wexler’s Summary

Eight-Eighteen Strategies provides integrated brand marketing, strategy, interactive and publicity solutions for fashion & lifestyle brands. We help fashion brands navigate today's rapidly changing new media and marketing environment.

Levi's. Universal Studios. MAGIC. Howe Denim (A div. of Union Bay). Original Penguin (Perry Ellis). Bluefly.com, Ocean Pacific. Smashbox, AT&T Wireless. U.S. Dept. of Commerce. Earthlink. SK Telecom. Fosters Beverage Group. Vestal Watch. Seril (luxury). Streets Ahead Belts. LA Fashion Week. Microsoft. Fornarina. Shiseido. Intel. Caparros Shoes. Various High-End Streetwear & Luxury.

Founding President Jed Wexler (former fashion magazine editor) writes and speaks regularly at conferences on topics related to fashion, marketing, branding, mobile, and new media. His most recent marketing column in the California Apparel News is titled, "Marketing 2.1 - How fashion brands can position themselves in a rapidly changing new media world."
