Yvonne Lowe’s Summary

My name Is Yvonne Lowe and my company is Y Lowe Signature Events, LLC. I have been working passionately in the event industry for the past 14 years and recently started my own company after completing my certification as an Event Manager at George Washington University.

My company’s mission is to create your events, your style, your way. This means that we work with our clients from conception all the way through production to create a pleasurable and memorable experience. We help you to design, manage & execute your event within your budget to achieve the outcome that you envisioned. We pay special attention to every detail and create perfect moments that are tailored to you.

Yvonne Lowe’s Specialties:

We plan Corporate Events like golf tournaments, incentives, awards ceremonies, small meetings & seminars, product launches and Special Events like wedding, birthday, anniversary, family reunions, baby & wedding showers.
