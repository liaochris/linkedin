Theresa A. Matacia, CFA’s Summary

Senior executive with over twenty year experience in corporate development, mergers and acquisitions, finance, capital markets, and strategic planning.

Multi-industry experience in Business and Technology drives high level of success in sourcing, negotiating, and executing domestic/international strategic transactions and partnerships.

Managed the development and implementation of corporate and business unit strategic initiatives that best leverage an organization's resources, capabilities, and capital.
