Mark Nardone’s Summary

With over 25 years of Martial Arts and fitness training experience, Kru Mark is recognized and certified by the Association Institute of Thai Martial Arts and the Kru Muay Thai Association of Thailand as an Assistant Master in Muay Thai and Muay Boran. He has taught and promoted authentic Muay Thai for over 15 years, and was one of the first instructors in the Boston area to offer training in the Thai Arts. Kru Mark has taught self defense and fitness classes for women, run programs for the Police Athletic League, consulted on local movie projects on fight coordination, and trained successful fighters who have competed nationally in the US and in Thailand.
Kru Mark has been tasked by Grand Master Chinawut Sirisompan to assist in the promotion and recognition of teachers of authentic Muay Thai throughout the US.
