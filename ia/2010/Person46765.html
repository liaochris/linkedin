Dan Franc’s Summary

Leading a consultancy focusing on technology-enabled communication and collaboration in organizations. Helping school teachers with the Net Generation. Running & developing several niche communities, incl. the Google User Group Czech.

9+ years of experience as a trainer and consultant in the area of management soft-skill development with a specialization in the IT industry and virtual teams and electronic/network collaboration. 12+ years of experience in managing software teams and designing architecture of Web applications.


Published author in English and Czech in the area of innovative and experiential methods in education.
