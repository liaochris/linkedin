Ann Newland’s Summary

As the voice of the consumer, Ann is responsible for ensuring that campaigns are relevant to the target group, have the desired persuasive impact and are presented in the right channels. She continually pursues ideas that grow from an understanding of the connection between brands and consumers.

Throughout her career, Ann has utilized a variety of research methods to identify ideal audiences and optimal methods of communication. She combines insights with a core value proposition to bring the consumer to the forefront of the overall communications strategy.

A planner, a strategist, a digital sociologist, Ann’s relentless search for consumer insights has helped brands find relevancy. And for the consumers they serve, she provides something often lost in modern advertising: an advocate.

Ann Newland’s Specialties:

Consumer insights, brand engagement, digital strategy, social media strategy and measurement, communication and content strategy, brand architecture, CRM, and direct marketing.
