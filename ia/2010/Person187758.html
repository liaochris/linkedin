Sharon Chamberlin’s Summary

A professional who successfully balances two passions – fitness and facilities management.

Sharon enjoys helping others improve their lifestyles to become healthier and happier with themselves and their lives. Based on her clients' goals, history, and equipment they have access to, she creates fitness programs customized for them, combining traditional and bodyweight training styles.

Many clients have embraced the online fitness and nutrition coaching service she offers. It allows them to work out whenever and wherever they want while still being held accountable, as well as establishing nutrition goals and tracking daily meals.

Sharon creates and maintains the content on her website, and also writes articles for other popular sites.
