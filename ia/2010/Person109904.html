Kairi Brown’s Summary

Kairi Brown started his music industry experience in 1994 when he worked under Quincy Jones. Studying under Quincy for 5 years taught him all the principles of the music business.
He has been able to work with many talented artists such as: Ray Charles, Quincy Jones, Tamia, Brandy, Neyo, Jaheim, Missy Elliot, Kiley Dean, to name a few.

Kairi has created formidable relationships with super producers: Timbaland, Neptunes, Rodney Jerkins, & The Underdogs.

After working for major labels for 10 years (Qwest Records, WB Records, & Beatclub Records) Kairi decided to start an independent production company, "StormWatch Entertainment". StormWatch inked first deal with vocally gifted, "Kiley Dean" (www.myspace.com/kileydean).

Kairi's newest adventure has taken him back into the Food & Beverage industry where he started his career at the young age of 12. He started a new Food company, "Bubba Q's" where he's been traveling with NASCAR for the last 3 years. Bubba Q's started in 2005 with the idea of providing a new casual dining experience for the race fans. Bubba Q’s is a restaurant on wheels that can set up anywhere and feed thousands of people.

Bubba Q's has traveled to many states to build their brand (Arizona, Talladega, Daytona, Michigan, California, Richmond, & Kansas). They have served over 100,000 customers to date. We plan to enter the retail market in 2009 by introducing BBQ sauces & seasonings.
