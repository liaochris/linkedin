Mark Grindeland’s Summary

Mark is co-founder and Chairman of ShesConnected Multimedia Corp., and has been exclusively focused in Social Media since 2007. His unique skill set encompasses Advertising, Mobile, and Social Media at executive levels. With over 25 years of experience working with some of the world’s leading consumer brands he is well equipped to provide strategic leadership in the ever changing media landscape. Mark was a co-founder of m-Qube Inc. the premier provider of mobile messaging and data solutions in North America. He was also the Chief Executive Officer, Wunderman Cato Johnson - Europe, Middle East, and Africa region. Prior to that Mark was a Senior Vice President at Digitas.


Mark has worked with leading companies on growth strategies including; American Express, Citibank, FedEx, IBM, Ford, General Motors, Ericsson, Rogers Wireless, State Street Bank, AT&T, T-Mobile, BSNL, Vodafone, O2, Bell Mobility, Cingular, MTN, Sony, Chubb Insurance, Prudential, Sears, Loblaws, President's Choice, P&G, Harcourt, Houghton Mifflin, US Airways, Alliance Air, Mitsui, and others.

Mark Grindeland’s Specialties:

digital media, mobile, wireless web, social media, community management, database marketing, market strategy, have worked across multiple consumer categories including: telecommunications, financial services (banking, mutual funds, insurance, credit cards, commercial lending, and leasing), CPG, airlines, consumer electronics, professional services (management consulting).
