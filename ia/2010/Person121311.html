Larry Davis, MBA, CPA’s Summary

Metrics-focused leader with success in maximizing shareholder value for early stage and midsized companies by developing strategic plans that promote growth through acquisitions and improved internal operations, enhancing systems to provide timely and accurate financial and operating information to all levels of management, and implementing innovative cost saving measures that are aligned with corporate culture and values.

-Sold a $400M family owned business to a private equity firm for 13x earnings

-Reverse merged a $500M retailer into a public shell while raising $250M in debt financing for expansion. Grew business to $1B in one year through seven acquisitions while increasing the operating margin from 2% to 3% versus an industry average of 1.5%

-Collaborated with CEOs of venture funded and private equity backed companies to raise $70M in convertible debt and equity financing

-Spearheading more than 20 acquisitions ranging in size from $2M to $100M that were accretive to earnings. Handled deal negotiations, the due diligence process, and post closing personnel and systems integrations
