Rami Saad’s Summary

Rami Saad has an unmatched passion for initiating digital marketing initiatives to help organizations establish competitive edge & achieve business objectives in a sustained growing manner. His leadership skills, energetic curious character coupled with his digital brain & strategic business understanding meant that he could lead project teams of marketers, technology experts & developers to their success.

He has led a great deal of digital marketing strategies over the past 7 years in sectors varying from iconic real estate projects, hospitality, entertainment, FMCG, e-CSR initiatives, tourism & public transport. His specific areas of expertise include digital strategy, digital metrics & optimization, portal marketing solutions, e-Commerce, e-CRM, integrated marketing, and leveraging enterprise information & technologies to create value & resolve business challenges.

Rami holds a B. Sc. In Information Technology earned with honors from University of Dubai. During his initial career stages, he wrote code developing content management software for fortune 500 companies. Shortly after, he decided to utilize his thorough knowledge of technology in building digital brands through helping Starcom Dubai start their digital arm; Starcom IP. The next step was a client-side role where Rami took the opportunity to widen his marketing perspective when by joining Nakheel, the world’s largest real estate master developer, where he managed a team of digital professionals achieving the organization’s hyper-rapid digital growth. Currently, Rami is back with the PGM network assessing & strategically introducing emerging technologies to the region.
