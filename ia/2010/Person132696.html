Marie Paule Richard, M.D.’s Summary

I have an academic degree in medicine and a business degree in general management.

Strategy and Team Management for 20+ yrs in global Clinical Development and Medical Affairs in big pharmas and biotechnology companies in the international regulatory and business environnement of anti-infectives, preventative & therapeutic vaccines, both in France, the UK, the US and Switzerland.

My core competences:
- Lead a department: organisation, processes, resources management, budget.
- Lead strategy and operations of global clinical projects (phase I-IV), submissions to US & EU to regulatory bodies.
- Manage cross-functional and multicultural teams.
- Develop internal collaborations and external partnerships.

I was involved in 4 major company mergers, I have gained comprehensive experience of change management, performance management and development of work processes and management systems.
