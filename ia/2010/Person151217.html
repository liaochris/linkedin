Nancy Fellinger’s Summary

CFP® - CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ practitioner
CRPC® - Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor(SM)
CAP® - Chartered Advisor in Philanthropy®
CLU® - Chartered Life Underwriter®
ChFC® - Chartered Financial Consultant®

With over eighteen years of experience in both corporate and private client arenas, I design and manage highly personalized financial planning, retirement income and investment management solutions for women who are single, widowed or divorced, and for couples who are enjoying retirement or who want to plan for it. A high premium is placed on excellence in client service, clarity in communications and transparency in fees.

Currently the president-elect of the Financial Planning Association of Connecticut/CT Valley Chapter, I also serve on the board of directors of the Planned Giving Group of Connecticut and am a past member of the board of the Connecticut Estate & Tax Planning Council, Inc.; I'm a member of the Professional Advisory Committee of the Hartford Foundation for Public Giving and serve on the Investment Committee of both Charter Oak State College Foundation and Asylum Hill Congregational Church. Recently appointed to the board of Aurora Foundation for Women & Girls, Inc., I'm committed to advancing economically disadvantaged women in their efforts to achieve independence and support several local non-profit organizations in their efforts to do the same.

Coaching hockey and curling occupy much of my free time during the colder months, while, in addition to entertaining local wildlife with Great Highland bagpipe classics, I perform with Police Pipes & Drums of Waterbury during the warmer ones. My husband and I live in Simsbury with our son, now a freshman at Gettysburg.

Nancy Fellinger’s Specialties:
