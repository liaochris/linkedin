Jon Jaroker’s Summary

Jon Jaroker has engineering and business experience in commercializing technology.

He is the founder and CTO of SOFIns, a software development operation that is creating a new way to deliver IT services and install, configure and integrate software to remote computers. He leads a software development team using agile development practices.

He led the identification, analysis and commercialization of new services and technologies for Columbia Venture's operating companies. At Lucent Technologies New Ventures Group, he was responsible for identifying raw Bell Labs technologies, developing commercialization strategies and launching startup companies.

He founded and operated BullDog TeleWorks, an innovative provider of software applications that are integrated with hosted telephone systems and S.C. Research Corporation, a sales and services company providing satellite communication and navigation products to businesses in Ukraine and West Africa.

He developed consumer hardware and software products, including "triple play" type devices for a Korean-based Samsung spinout company and a Unified Communications service for Net2Phone, a VoIP service provider. As product manager at Lucent Technologies Advanced Technologies Group, he analyzed and forecasted demand for Lucent's MPEG-2 encoder.

He holds a B.S.E. degree and certificate in Engineering Physics from Princeton University and an MBA in Finance and Information Systems from NYU Stern School of Business.

Jon Jaroker’s Experience

  • Founder

    SOFIns

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    SOFIns is a software development operation that is commercializing the SOFIns application.

    SOFIns is an online, trusted intermediary service that provides a way for technical experts to productize technical knowledge and access remote computers belonging to non-technical users.

    The development operation consists of a distributed team following the agile development methodology of continuous build, automated deployment and continuous testing. We use Struts2 in MVC framework, Spring and JPA technology stack and message-bus architecture to connect to Java and C++ based subsystems.

    For the software development operation, I am the Technical Project Manager. My work includes:

    • system architecture design,
    • release and iteration planning,
    • development team recruitment and
    • management and infrastructure planning and provisioning.

    I work directly with the development team on the creation of the SOFIns application.

    For the SOFIns venture itself, I am the product manager with such responsibilities as

    • identifying customer requirements,
    • converting features and functionalities into user “stories”,
    • defining automated functional tests and
    • managing the graphic design, IT admin and manual testing support staff.

    I have overall responsibility for commercialization strategy so that SOFIns meets specific customer requirements, competes effectively against available alternatives and generates sales revenue.

    SOFIns is seeking senior management and technical developers who have an ability to work in a startup environment:

    • Engineering: experience operating a software development operation using agile methodology and a distributed development team;
    • Marketing: knowledge of IT Consultancy and Systems Administration market and experience promoting new, high-tech services to technologists as well as non-technical consumer and business users;
    • Development: java and, c++ developers
