Nancy D. Brown’s Summary

With over 26 years of agency experience, Nancy knows how to work both sides of the media fence. As a public relations consultant she knows what it takes to garner media coverage and as a working journalist, she is a solid writer who gets the job done and delivered on time.

Nancy's true passion is travel. Her goal is to incorporate more international travel into her life.
