Bronwen Murray’s Summary

Bronwen Murray currently serves as Development Director with the AIDS Action Coalition, a nonprofit health organization. Prior to joining AAC she presided over a marketing agency founded in Chicago. Before working in the windy city, Bronwen was the Communication Director with a technology development company that specializes in launching new ventures from university-based inventions. She began her career at Northrop Grumman/XonTech, Inc. where she learned the ropes of corporate communication.

Throughout the course of her career, Bronwen has participated in numerous marketing, communication and development roles in Fortune 500, start-up and nonprofit environments. Her experience spans both traditional and digital marketing for consumer packaged goods advertisers such as Snapfish, Yum Brands!, QVC and Pizza Hut.

She holds a master of science degree in integrated marketing communication from Northwestern University and a bachelor of art in communication from Auburn University. Many people find it interesting that, while a teenager, Bronwen worked as a professional actress. Her most noted appearance is as Cousin Mary in Walt Disney's Tom & Huck.
