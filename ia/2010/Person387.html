Alyson LaBarge’s Summary

I began blogging while awaiting the adoption of my 3rd child and found my perfect niche, combining my retail expertise with my love of family and parenting.

3 P's in a Pod now serves as my outlet to connect with other Stay at Home and Work at Home Moms while providing them with all kinds of information on new and exciting products to benefit their lives.

I provide great shopping deals online, excellent product reviews and product giveaways. I hope to continue to build a solid base of contacts and learn more that, in return, allows me to benefit other moms.

Companies I've worked with include but aren't limited to: Dove, Tiny Prints, Hoohobbers, KEEN, and Team Mom.

Niche market: Adoption, homemaking, crafts, animals, Internet marketing, humanitarian projects, and parenting.
