Marcia Feola’s Summary

Marcia is an executive coach and organization development consultant based in Washington DC. Marcia specializes in developing leaders with clear, compelling visions of the future, and supporting them to realize significant and sustainable organizational growth. Marcia believes in building organizations based on personal responsibility, shared leadership models and social consciousness.

Marcia is the author of Reboot! Your Working Relationships and is currently writing her next book, Reboot! The Workbook.

Marcia is the former the Director of Georgetown University’s Organization Development Program and the Facilitation Mastery Program. She has served as faculty to the Organization Development Program at Georgetown University and George Mason University.

She is a guest panelist on Clear Channel’s Sunday radio program, “Women Talk” broadcast in the Washington, D.C. area on WMZQ, WASH-FM, WBIG and DC101.

Marcia is a regular presenter and speaker at national and international conferences.

Marcia earned a Bachelor’s Degree in English and a Master’s Degree in Health Care Administration from George Washington. She is a graduate of Georgetown University’s Organizational Development Program. She is certified as a Professional Co-active Coach from Coaches Training Institute and holds the distinction of a Master Certified Coach from the international Coach Federation
