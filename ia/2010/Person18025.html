Astrid Gaiser’s Summary

Astrid Gaiser Design specializes in the design of serene gardens that make their owners relax, smile, and enjoy.

Exclusively working on residential home projects, we develop designs that reflect our clients’ personality and lifestyle as well as California's wonderful opportunities for extensive outdoor living.

Our plans articulate sustainable, unique designs with drought-tolerant plants and beautiful hardscape materials.

We specialize in Mediterranean, Modern, and our very own “Northern California Tropical” style, in which native Californian and Mediterranean plants are artfully interwoven with some of the more adaptable tropicals. This results in a lush look that is drought-tolerant, low-maintenance, and soul-nourishing. Northern California Tropical style often shows strong influences from Tuscany and Asia.

Plan a new look for your garden today and contact us for a design or consultation at:
