Bob Leckridge’s Summary

General Practitioner for 13 years; built a two doctor, 2000 patient practice up to a six doctor 9000 patient one. Left it behind when I felt like a cog in my own machine!
Worked on local radio in Edinburgh for a year (ScotFM), and wrote a textbook on Homeopathy for Primary Care, published by Churchill Livingstone.
Current post at Glasgow Homeopathic Hospital since 1995, providing a mix of clinical and teaching work.
President of UK Faculty of Homeopathy 1999 - 2005. Still member of Council and Academic Board
Developed and delivered a patient-centred teaching programme in homeopathy for health care professionals. Currently delivering this course in Glasgow and Tokyo.
Have taught extensively around the world - Holland, Italy, Poland. Portugal, Russia, South Africa, India, USA, Japan, Costa Rica.
Blogging for just over a year - Heroesnotzombies.wordpress.com - about my belief that we can wake up from our autopilot, zombie existence and write the story of our own lives with ourselves as the heroes of our own story
