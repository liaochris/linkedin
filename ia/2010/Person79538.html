Hal Sims’s Summary

An Account Executive and part of MR Spokane’s Contract Staffing division. He recently moved back to Spokane after retiring from 20 years of Naval service. He has worked in healthcare for 20 years. His diverse background includes all functions of human resources, clinical facilities management and personnel management. He was also an Naval Officer Recruiter for the US Navy and was awarded “Recruiter of the Year” for Seattle’s recruiting district. So far at MR Spokane he has received local and worldwide recognition for recruiting productivity.

Hal Sims’s Specialties:

He stays connected to the national market’s client and candidate pools. He understands square pegs do not fit into round holes and looks for “the best fit” when addressing a client’s needs. His detailed approach to screening candidates makes him a great choice when looking to make a career move.
