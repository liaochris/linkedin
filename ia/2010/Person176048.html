Rob van Steenbergen’s Summary

14 years of experience in testing. Started as a tester at the company “Quality House”. Here I got the change to experiment a lot within the world of testing, such as requirements-based testing, data-driven testing and automated testing. After Perfectview I started working as a consultant for LYNC and worked in banking, consumer products, embedded software and government organizations.

I am a specialist in TMap Next and also experienced with other testing methods. I'm flexible, creative, independent, a teamleader. I can set up and organize the test and test-related processes (eg issue management, configuration management, assignment processes) or do hands-on testing.
