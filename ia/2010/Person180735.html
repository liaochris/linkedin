Sally Maki’s Summary

I have an interdisciplinary MS in Energy and Resources and a background in Chemical Engineering. My first job with renewables was at a wood stove company in Brazil in 2005 and since then I have worked on a wide range of renewable technologies and project types.

At Black & Veatch's renewables consulting group, I support the strategic planning and solar PV teams. Our clients include: utilities, government/regulatory agencies, developers, banks, manufacturers, research laboratories, non-profits, and the military as well as other consulting agencies. Example types of projects include: resource assessment and planning, supply curves, capacity valuation, RFP development/bid evaluation, scheduling/timelines, transmission planning, due diligence, project siting, grid integration. The work environment is highly collaborative, fast-paced, ambiguous, and always interesting.

I am usually doing one of the following: Excel modeling, data acquisition/processing/analysis, making presentations, data processing, GIS, programming, research, or report writing/editing/formatting.

I work with renewables but am interested in a wide variety of topics (see "Interests") - in particular international development, complex systems, information technology and education.
