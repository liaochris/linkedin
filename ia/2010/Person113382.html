Keith Johnston’s Summary

I have a passion for the meetings and events industry. Passion is good, but what does it mean? Passion means l love helping people produce meetings and events that not only meet their stated goals but surpass them. Putting the fun back in meetings and events doesn’t hurt either.

I have learned that by connecting your audience to an event you make them a part of the event, not just an attendee.

Whether the event is a meeting, a conference, an incentive trip, trade show or product launch, if you open a conversation and start a dialogue with your audience before, during and after the event you extend the reach of that event and make it a living thing, not just a “one and done” affair. Corporation, association or non-profit, the result is always the same.

To accomplish these amazing feats of event daring, I am the Group Vice President of OnSite Events. OnSite Events is the premier Chicago based full service meeting and event planning agency that produces meetings and events globally for companies across the U.S.

More than just meeting and event planning, OnSite Events lives up to its agency moniker and works with clients to integrate social media, marketing and message to create an event environment.

I am also a twitter junkie, a blogger, a writer, a Chapter Board Member for SITE International and the Publisher of PlannerWire, a news, information and community website for meeting and event professionals.
