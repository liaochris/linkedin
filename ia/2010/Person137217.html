Matthew E. May’s Summary

I write, speak, conduct workshops, advise and coach teams and individuals on topics related to design thinking, creativity, innovation, and lean thinking.

Expert in design strategy, process innovation, lean thinking for knowledge work, creativity in organizations, process design, process improvement, kaizen, complex problem solving.

Author, IN PURSUIT OF ELEGANCE: Why The Best Ideas Have Something Missing (Broadway Business, 2009).

Author, THE ELEGANT SOLUTION: Toyota's Formula for Mastering Innovation (Simon & Schuster/Free Press, 2006).
