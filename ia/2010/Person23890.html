Bill Lampton, Ph.D.’s Summary

GOALS: My goal is to help individuals and organizations maximize their potential by strengthening their communication, motivation, sales, and customer service. I coach dedicated professionals who want to become dynamic speakers, and I bring practical guidelines, zest, humor, and vital interaction to convention audiences.

EXPERIENCE: Former university professor, then vice president of three colleges, then vice president of a medical center, prior to forming my company, Championship Communication, in 1996.

Client list includes British Columbia Legal Management Association, Ritz-Carlton Cancun, Krystal Company, Missouri Bar, Celebrity Cruises, University of Georgia Athletic Association, Club Managers Association of America, and Crawford & Company.

As a communication expert, interviewed by Los Angeles Times, Washington Post, Delta's SKY, Cosmopolitan, Sales and Marketing Management, Investor's Business Daily, and United Press International.

Bill Lampton, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Communication Coaching, that helps people strengthen their presentation skills, so they can move into leadership positions effectively--or maximize opportunities in their current leadership posts.

On a more public scale, my speeches and seminars ignite conferences and conventions, after intensive preparation with clients covering several weeks. When the meeting starts, participants practically know me well already.
