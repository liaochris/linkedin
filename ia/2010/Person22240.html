  • Prep for Prep

    Since 1978, Prep has identified New York City’s most promising students of color and prepared them for placement at independent schools in the city and boarding schools throughout the Northeast.

    Each year, over 6,000 students of color with strong academic ability, nominated by over 500 New York City middle schools, apply to Prep for Prep’s annual Talent Search. The selection process results in the admission of about 150 fifth- and sixth-graders into Prep for Prep for independent (private) day school placement.

    Once accepted into the program, Prep for Prep students must complete a rigorous 14-month course of study to prepare them for independent school success. Once placed, Prep offers support services and leadership development opportunities that foster our students’ success at whatever they choose to pursue.
