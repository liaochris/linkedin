Megan Bell’s Summary

Megan Bell directs data analysis projects and leads business development initiatives for an investigation and computer forensics company. She has 15 years' experience designing and implementing reporting and analysis solutions for software, insurance, and consumer product companies.

Megan has 10 years' insurance-industry experience and assisting claims, sales and customer support organizations. She worked with Top 100 insurance carriers to evaluate and deploy decision-support software that measured and improved consistency in adjusters' decisions on auto bodily-injury claims settlements.

Megan has 10 years' experience consulting on new business/product development initiatives and structuring underlying business processes.

Megan is a California-licensed Producer in Fire & Casualty, Life and Accident & Health insurance lines.
