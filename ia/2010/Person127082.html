Larkland Morley’s Summary

My professional background is a Network Infrastructure or Solutions Architect. To summarize what this means

1. Done several design projects involving Enterprise Network design and usability.. This involved routing architecture and protocols such as BGPv4, MPLS, PBT, OSPF, RIPv2, EIGRP and some others. Using most L2 Protocols for connectivity as well as Management protocols such as SNMP flavors.

2. Lead Systems Test teams on several Switching Products Development. These included L2-L3 Aggregation switches, Metro Ethernet Switches and L3 Routing Switches. This included QOS, COPS-PR and many other protocols.

3. Work extensively on Wireless Product Development as System Designer mainly in WiMAX (802.16e), LTE (3GPP) and Wireless Mesh (802.11). This involved work in Systems Architecture, performance evaluation and analysis, KPI definition and measurements and post-deployment troubleshooting.

4. Project Management - Most of my work has involved some degree of project management and I have educated myself especially on PMI BOK, Prince2, Business Analysis and methods.

Lately researching Social Media, Collaboration Tools, Multi-Media Architecture and Business Analysis Techniques.

Information Technology Strategy (TOGAF v9), Technology Market Trends, Network Architecture Design, Routing Protocols, Network Deployment, Quality of Service, Serviceability, Network Performance Metrics, 4G Wireless (802.16e and 3GPP-LTE) and associated Project Management
