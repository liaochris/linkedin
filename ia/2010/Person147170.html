    Held various postions on the Board of Directors for over 10 years including Vice President of Programming, President and Chairman of the Board.

    "Direct marketing" (DM) is the art and science of sending one-to-one promotional messages directly to consumers, rather than via a mass medium such as print advertising. Direct marketing campaigns include direct mail, email, catalogs, telemarketing and similar methods. Database management is a major component of DM campaigns. The 'call to action" and measurable nature of DM campaigns make this a popular approach among many business owners.

    The Florida Direct Marketing Association educates its members and the industry in an effort to promote excellence and maintain the highest ethical standards. FDMA also provides a monthly forum to gather and discuss relevant industry topics, network and promote the benefits of a direct marketing approach to advertising and marketing efforts.
