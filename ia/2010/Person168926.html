Randy Goruk’s Summary

A 26-year veteran in management and leadership positions in Canada and the United States for Trus Joist – A Weyerhaeuser Business, most notably as the President of Trus Joist Canada, and Sr. Vice President of North American Residential Operations, a $750 million business unit.

In these roles, I was accountable for managing business growth and profitability while I fine-tuned my personal leadership style and techniques. During this time I learned the importance of;

1. Ensuring that a sound plan was in place to achieve the desired goals

2. Identifying and recruiting the right individuals

3. Properly training those individuals in the details of their technical specialties

4. Transforming those individuals into a well orchestrated, results-oriented team by directing them in a highly motivated, team environment

5. Leading by example in the areas of integrity, respect for the associate and the customer and personal life/work balance

During this period I personally coached, mentored and developed many of the company’s past, existing and future leaders.

Additionally, I've experienced entrepreneural business success through 2000

Randy Goruk’s Specialties:
