Randy Yerzyk, AIA, LEED AP’s Summary

Randy is an experienced architect in all phases of project delivery from design conception through the construction phases of residential, commercial, hospitality, and other project types. He has led design and production teams, managed consulting architects and engineers, and worked with public agencies during the project approval process.
