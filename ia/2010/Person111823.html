Katherine Kennedy’s Summary

Katherine Kennedy is an experienced licensed real estate agent offering a wealth of real estate knowledge to each of her clients. She enjoys the climate of the local real estate market and the lifetime relationships that are built through involvement in all aspects of the real estate transaction. Her experience includes Buyers and Sellers of residential property in the Bay Area; new development, 2-4 unit buildings, condominiums, and single family homes.


Kathy is National Association of Realtors certified e-Pro, which emphasizes utilization of web-based marketing and client communication, and Senior Real Estate Specialist, SRES, which works with a growing population of older adults, their estate planning and financial needs. She has also completed continuing education courses to become a broker.
Kathy has been selling property in San Francisco, California for fourteen years. She has been involved with real estate for most of her life. Her family owned and operated a small estate brokerage in Southern California. Prior to moving to San Francisco, she worked in the Marketing and Training Development department of a Los Angeles-based estate property real estate firm.


Kathy is a graduate of the University of California, San Diego, where she studied Language and Political Science. After graduation, she moved to Berlin, Germany, where she worked as a chef. Today, Kathy, her husband, Kipp, and daughter, Alexandra live in San Francisco with their dogs Mucke and Buddie. She enjoys gardening, cooking, working on their home and entertaining with friends and clients.
