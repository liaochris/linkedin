Erin Garcia’s Summary

As a veteran in the online marketing field I have seen the industry change from the virtually unregulated “Wild West” days to a maturing industry that has been accepted by top companies around the globe as a necessary part of their marketing strategies! My involvement has been primarily focused on sales, business development, team building & management and strategic planning & execution in traditional and performance based channels.

As a results-driven leader in the online marketing industry I have vast experience in developing strategic plans and executing campaigns for Advertisers and Direct Marketers with a focus on ROI, Brand Protection and Compliance. I have in-depth knowledge of CPM, CPA, CPL, CPC, Revenue Sharing and Traffic Arbitrage models, which allows me to execute innovative strategies that bridge the gap between what advertisers and publishers need to be successful.
