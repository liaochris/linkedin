Douglas May’s Summary

Trusted Strategic Advisor & CFO with Big 4, Public & Private Company Background
Specializing in Helping Companies Find Cash – from external sources and internally by streamlining and controlling processes, effectively managing cash and working capital, improving the accuracy and effectiveness of financial statements, taking the hunch out of decision making, and determining the key measurements that drive success and profitability.

- Certified Public Accountant & Certified Financial Planner.
- Served since 2000 as both a full-time CFO and as a contract, part-time CFO and COO for numerous private and public companies within Wireless Infrastructure (RE/A/E/C), Telecommunications, Entertainment, Distribution, Registered Investment Advisory (RIA), Private Equity, Real Estate and Law industries.
- Fifteen years of public accounting and consulting providing both personal and business financial advice, including implementing “Best Practice” business processes designed to achieve organizational strategy for a variety of entities ranging from venture-backed start-ups to Fortune 500 companies.
- Experience with depth in finance, taxes, accounting, process improvement, strategic and financial planning, and strategy-focused performance management.
- Track record of streamlining processes and turning value streams profitable through continuous improvement methods.
- Secured $10.6 million of angel and venture capital funding ($5MM pre-public and $5.6MM post-public) for a publicly traded company.
- Successfully executed and integrated seven acquisitions for a publicly traded company.
- Experience negotiating settlements for outstanding liabilities asserted by vendors, avoiding costly restructuring alternatives.
- Acted as “Family CFO” for family limited partnerships, family limited liability companies, and family foundations.
- Co-author of extensive CPA CPE program, published and distributed nationally.
- Served 15 years as an Army Reserve officer.
