Georgia Paterson Dargham’s Summary

Press consultant and independent writer. I write, translate and edit, specialising in society, press, current affairs and literature in the countries where I have lived, Britain, France and Lebanon. My articles on the Near East are regularly featured online by Lonely Planet. My blog GingerBeirut was selected as one of its top travel blogs.

Browse my work at www.gingerbeirut.com and contact me at georgia[at]dargham[dot]net.
