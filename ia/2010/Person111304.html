Karyn Jensen, MS, SPHR’s Summary

As the President and founder of The Human Resource Connection, Ltd., I've worked in Human Resources nearly 20 years. I also hold a Masters of Science degree in Human Resources, in addition to being SPHR certified since 1999.

In addition, I have been teaching Human Resource and Supervisory courses for the College of Extended Studies since 1991 and partnered with UNR to plan, develop and execute a structured review program for the Professional in Human Resources certification (PHR). We have run this program for 3 years, with a total of 7 sessions and have a 73% pass rate (this is well above the national average pass rate of 60%). Our pass rate for the Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) for last session is 79% compared to the national average of 54%.

We help companies like yours by:

- Clarifying legal issues and recognizing existing company practices that may cause potential lawsuits.

- Providing management and employee training on operational and communication approaches to improve workplace environments.

- Creating HR procedures and Policy Guidebooks that enable management to have an easy-to-understand resource on all HR questions that arise.

Karyn Jensen, MS, SPHR’s Specialties:

We audit I9 forms for completeness and accuracy; conduct HR assessments reviewing every aspect of your HR function; recruit for open HR management positions; provide customized management training on legal issues, interviewing, documenting performance and writing effective performance reviews. All of our services are customized to EXACTLY what outcomes are desired by the client.
