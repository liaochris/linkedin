Nancy Bleeke’s Summary

Snapshot of what we do: We help companies increase sales (5-25% in 60 days or less), enhance service levels and develop stronger leadership skills thorugh training, assessments and consulting.

The mission of Sales Pro Insider, Inc. (formerly Corporate Resource Development, Inc.) founded in 1998 is to "build performance, profits and people". All three of these are necessary for long-term business success.

Nancy Bleeke's goal is to provide tools and skills that assist organizations and indivduals in achieving higher sales, service and leadership results.

Nancy and Sales Pro Insider differ from other consulting firms because we focus on long-term habits and results that ensure a healthy ROI on your training investement.

After working with thousands of business professionals, Nancy started capturing the best practices observed in successful professionals and writes about them in a e-zine called Timely Tips. The first 52 Timely Tips booklet for Sales Managers was published in 2006. The 52 Timely Tips booklet for Sales Professionals, co-authored with Lynn Zimmer, was published in June 2008.

Nancy's background in sales, human resources and training allows her to relate well to business professionals and their "real world". The methodology and processes of the Integrity Selling, Service, and Coaching courses are some of the proven methodologies that quickly help companies and people see results.

Integrity Selling was recently acknowledged as one of the Top 10 Sales Training Methodology companies (2008)! Great to be acknowledged for something that Nancy Bleeke has seen work for over a dozen years.
