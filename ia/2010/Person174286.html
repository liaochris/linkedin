Rob Bremault’s Summary

I am a self-driven energy professional with a majority of my career spent in California utility industry. I have broad and deep knowledge of the regulated utility and de-regulated energy markets including commercial customer needs, utility process re-engineering and enabling clean technologies. I have direct sales experience in Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems with utilities and translating customer needs into business solutions. I have a record of success in creating high performance teams and achieving No. 1 results in customer service.
