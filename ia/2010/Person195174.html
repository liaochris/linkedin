I am interested in speaking with experienced Pharmaceutical and Green professionals looking either to recruit for their department or looking for new opportunities. Find out what people want and give it to them.

The latter being the obvious competitive advantage offered by Stelfox & GreenSearch afforded through the knowledge and experience of each individual consultant.
