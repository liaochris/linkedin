Mike Ellsworth’s Summary

IT Program Manager, emerging technology strategist and writer, and social media trainer with experience in big business, government, and startups. Decision support and data warehousing knowledge from 15 years with market research company ACNielsen. Formerly VP, Strategic Planning for an Internet incubator. Six years as an independent consultant helping senior leaders understand the business impact of rapidly changing new technologies. Founder of The WiMAX Guys, a wireless networking company. Currently Program Manager in charge of a huge federally-funded Website for the State of Minnesota. Expert at training and consulting on LinkedIn and other social media, writing technology white papers and translating complex technical concepts into plain business language.
