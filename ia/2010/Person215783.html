Willi Paul’s Summary

Planetshifter.com is an online blog network devoted to bringing clients and the community together to share ideas and news on sustainability, green politics and the impact we have on our planet.

Planetshifter.com Networks (PSN) is a client relations network where socially responsible business owners can contract our services to further their mission to educate and market their products and services to the public. See:
http://www.planetshifter.com/PS/networks.htm

PSN is an effective and direct channel to the individuals and businesses on the web. This brings increased sales opportunities for clients. Willi Paul, Founder of PSN broadcasts client info in his extended network of seven additional green community websites, LinkedIn.com, and one-on-one consulting. Nowhere else can business owners connect with each other on such a direct, diverse and cost-effective mechanism.
