Donna Marie Thompson, PhD’s Summary

Sharpen and refine marketing strategy for entrepreneurs, coaches, and other small business owners to increase sales and profits. Focus on demonstrated strengths to leverage customer relationships. Create a meaningful dialogue with customers. Increase entrepreneur productivity and results.

By sharpening focus on core competencies and areas of distinct competitive advantage, business owners can significantly increase their sales and profits. Key steps are to refine the business niche and target market, and analyze and create suitable product and service offerings to meet the pressing needs of the market.

Get back in touch with your customers to better meet their needs. Your business is changing - and so is theirs.

If you are a business owners who is stuck at your current level, I also conduct Energy Leadership Index Assessments and provide guidance to identify blocks and limiting beliefs that are holding you back from getting what you want - both in your business and in your personal life. After the debriefing and training session, entrepreneurs have more focused, positive energy to get more done in less time. They become aware of their default behaviors that keep them stuck.

Products include e-books, teleseminars, mastermind groups, training, e-zine, and membership options for premium content.

Co-author of book "Bouncing Back: Thriving in Changing Times" featuring Steve E, Brian Tracy, David Riklan, Wayne Dyer, and John Assaraf coming out in January 2010.

Prior experience in Management Consulting at PricewaterhouseCoopers, Booz-Allen, and small business.

Certifications: Master Practitioner of Energy Leadership Index, Certified Professional Coach, Certified Public Accountant, MBA in Marketing, PhD in International Business
