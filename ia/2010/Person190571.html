Skip Andrew’s Summary

A serial entrepreneur, I have been self-employed for 40 years, mostly consulting, coaching, and business development under the rubric of AH&A – sometimes as a solo practitioner and other times in collaboration with trusted colleagues, experts, and ad hoc teams.

My business passions are uniquely effective marketing strategies, learning, freedom, relationships, high level wellness, and the opportunity-rich challenge of environmental sustainability and the new economy.

With a core competency in problem-solving, we create innovative approaches to 1:1 marketing campaigns that fly under the radar of the competition.

We plan, design, and implement marketing projects and campaigns to include three critical elements in today’s environment:

#1 TRANSPARENCY. Deception kills business. Every aspect of the marketing campaign must communicate transparency, honesty, rightness, functionality, and sustainability.

#2 UNIQUENESS. Only the unique stand out. Marketing messages must uniformly reflect the unique core values and mission of our clients, our client’s unique place and value in the marketplace, and the unique needs and desires of our clients’ employees, prospects, customers, vendors, and environment. – always with the intent of creating positive buzz and validation in social networks on and off line.

3. PROFITABLE. Every marketing element must prove its profitability in well-designed tests comparing it with a control and/or competing test element. Thus, each marketing element must enhance the perceived value of our client’s products and services.

GUARANTEE. All of our work is always done with a simple money back guarantee: “AH&A does not keep your money until you are 100% satisfied that we have delivered as promised.”
