Peter Anderson’s Summary

There is no easier, faster or more professional way for you to find the right Building Services position than to contact B-A-R. We understand that finding the right M&E job can be a big step for many so we ensure we adopt a consultative approach, we listen carefully to your needs, and adopt a personalised tailor made service everytime.

We provide you with an honest and open level of service from the moment you register with us to the moment you secure your next career move.

We will develop a thorough understanding of your experience, skills, abilities and career aspirations so you can be sure were only ever talking to you about the most suitable vacancies. We'll use our industry expertise to offer you meaningful advice and a variety of exciting work and career choices. As a permanent applicant registering with B-A-R you can be sure that we take confidentiality seriously.

We are not in the business of knocking square pegs into round holes, and we have excellent relationships with building services consultants both in the UK and worldwide.

For our contractors we are in a position to offer the most competitive hourly rates, with regular reviews, dependable payroll services, and the support and attention you need to ensure a positive experience working through B-A-R. .
