Janet Walker’s Summary

Janet Walker is the CEO/Founder of Pulse Point Productions, Inc a multi-platform entertainment company.

Her duties at Pulse Point Productions include all corporate, creative and communication decisions. Additionally, she is the New York Associate Editor and Journalist for Splash Magazines Worldwide and maintains a feature beat focusing on Film, Culinary, Luxury Brands and Social benefits.

She is an expert content development and creative specialist with over ten years experience. She began her investigative news reporting career covering fiscal news and any story with a hint of controversy. She has maintained a freelance news beat, freelance editorial services agency and now a feature beat for more than ten years.

She has collaborated with numerous PR agencies promoting their clients. She is recognized in market as an independent and dedicated Journalist; member of invited press for Film Junkets, Culinary events and premiere business and industry events. She is quoted in multiple media and entertainment articles.

Ms. Walker is inviting investors to be a part of an exciting journey as Pulse Point Productions, Inc., seeks a partner, gap financing, institutional financing, private equity or acquisition.

Pulse Point Productions first feature, The Six Sides of Truth, is a high tech contemporay thrill ride through the inner sanctums of the Pentagon and is producer ready.
