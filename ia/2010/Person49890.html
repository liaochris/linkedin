David Smith, PhD (K6DLS)’s Summary

I have just started at Asuragen, Inc.where my formal training and experience classify me as a biochemist. I have a broad range of skills and experience relating to experimental design and implementation on numerous platforms. I have more than 20 years of experience in molecular biology, protein expression, purification and characterization, and a strong background in bacterial and phage genetics. My current position will involve the transition of proprietary assays from development into the CLIA lab. Very much looking forward to this new challenge in my home state of Texas!

David Smith, PhD (K6DLS)’s Specialties:

CGH & microRNA arrays, RT-qPCR, assay development and high-throughput screening (HTS) via robotic implementation. Executed complete primary, secondary screens and deconvolution assays against oncology targets with compounds now entering the clinic. That body of work included lead optimization and Structure-Activity Relationships (SAR) of small molecule kinase inhibitors in drug discovery. I find that genuine satisfaction often comes from projects that benefit others or fill an unmet need.
