P.J. Padilla’s Summary

The fact that I've been service-oriented for nearly 35 years is not by accident. Simply stated, I enjoy helping people, perhaps something I inherited from my father, Oscar Padilla. Applying this to a livelihood, I am able and skilled at providing my client's peace of mind knowing they are adequately insured, whether they be in Mexico or in the U.S..

My effort begins with establishing a relationship — a partnership, if you will — between my client, the insurance company and myself built on trust and reliability.

My objective is to provide my clients with the adequate coverage(s), from the right providers, at the right price. I am committed to personalized customer service; I will always demonstrate full understanding and compassion for my clients and their individual circumstances, regardless of specific need. The most important difference I try to make comes from simply caring.

My goal is to continue growing as an insurance professional, ensuring my clients are given the most up-to-date information to make good decisions regarding their coverages and, most importantly, providing them peace of mind.
