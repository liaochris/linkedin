Debbie Nixon’s Summary

Debbie Nixon, PCC, NCC is Principal and founder of Masterful Advisor ®, a full-service coaching company for Financial Advisors.

Her Blind Spot Removal ® programs for Financial Advisors deliver efficient, effective and lasting improvements to her clients through one-one, group and teleclass options help financial advisors get - and - keep …

- Dramatic increases in assets under management
- Record-breaking FYCs and trailing commissions
- A steady flow of ideal client referrals
- A smoothly-running practice - even in their absence
- A work schedule that fits their life
- Honor and recognition (MDRT, Diamond Club, etc)
- More time for fun, health and community activities

Debbie knows what it takes to create results in chaotic settings. Her ability to to help quickly uncover blind spots and profitable solutions is the common denominator that made her sought after by Fortune 500 companies and solo-preneur businesses alike.

Debbie holds PCC-level and NCC certifications through the International Coach Federation and Newfield Networks. She holds a MS in Human Resource Economics.

Debbie brings her love of learning, NY humor, compassion and laser-like focus to help her clients get the most from coaching - and have fun doing it!

She currently lives with her husband Mark in Tampa where they enjoy the weather, golf and the "Florida Lifestyle" in general! She has a wonderful step-son, Josh, who is living his dream in L.A. as an improvisational actor extraordinaire (we hope and pray daily for his big break!)

Debbie Nixon’s Specialties:

Her ideal clients are experienced Registered Investment Advisors, Certified Financial Planners and Independent Advisors in broker-dealer arrangements, who are already successful but 1) their practice owns them or 2) need help getting to that top echelon.
