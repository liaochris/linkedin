Nancy Aiello twitter.com/ItalyTravelista’s Summary

My experience of introducing travelers to the cultural treasures of Italy, and particularly those of Rome, goes back many years. While working as a travel director, I led numerous travellers on tours of Florence, Venice and other cities of historical importance in central and northern Italy.
