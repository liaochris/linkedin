Rob Shore’s Summary

Over the course of a 30 year career in corporate America, Rob Shore's been recognized as a leader and producer known for sales success, innovation, creativity ….and memorability.

His career has been dedicated to Financial Services in positions ranging from entry level bank teller, to wholesaler, to president of a broker dealer. He has built successful sales organizations from the ground up and led firms to number one market share position.

Whether in personal production, or leading teams to great success, the same underlying principal is in effect: the ability to be memorable.

That is why in 2007 Rob began to ask audiences across the country What’s Your MQ - Memorability Quotient®?

Today as CEO and founder of shorespeak®, Rob consults with sales organizations on strategic direction, coaches clients on career development and growth, and speaks to audiences large and small.

In 2009 Rob added the peer coaching group Wholesaler Masterminds™ to the mix.

In 2010 the LinkedIn Wholesaler Mastermind Group was born:
http://www.linkedin.com/groups?gid=2729875

Also in 2010 the first and only magazine dedicated to the Art, Science, and Lifestyle of wholesaling was launched: I Carry The Bag http://goo.gl/VG8h

The common thread of these endeavors is the same. How can your firm, your team, or you stand out from the sea of sameness? What will you do in your business that achieves lasting memorability with prospects, clients, employees and colleagues?

As a leader who has been tested, both through experience and through time, Rob Shore looks forward to bringing you his style of new thought leadership.
