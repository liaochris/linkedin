The BRAIN LOUNGE will be open in a trial version in November - December 2009.


    In the BRAIN LOUNGE users will be able to play computer games that have been designed specifically to train and enhance brain functions such as concentration, memory, visual-spatial orientation, motor skills and analytic functions.
