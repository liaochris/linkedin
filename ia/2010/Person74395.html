George Luntz’s Experience

  • President & Co-Founder

    Native Remedies LLC

    (Privately Held; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    August 2002 — Present (7 years 9 months)

    Horrified at the devastating effects of pharmaceutical drugs on our nephew and other kids with ADHD, and the incredible acceptance of putting young kids on all kinds of harsh meds for depression and other emotional disorders, my wife, our son and I decided to embark on a mission to provide a natural alternative and a safe approach to helping parents, their kids and others.

    As a result, we formed Native Remedies, an online provider of herbal and homeopathic remedies, along with online health resources covering lifestyle and holistic approaches to long term wellness.

    We are proud to say that since then we have helped over 500,000 customers around the world find safe, natural products for their families' wellness, even their pets! And we have created what is now a leading online brand of herbal and homeopathic products addressing over 200 health conditions, disorders and long term wellness areas. Our web site is now ranked in the top 20,000 on the entire internet by Alexa.com, and we are working hard to fulfill our mission of inspiring people to embrace a natural approach to complete family wellness.

    It has been a wonderfully fulfilling ride so far, and we have so much further to journey!

  • CEO and Co_founder

    Allegiant Legacy Solutions, Inc

    (Computer Software industry)

    January 1996 — December 1999 (4 years )

    Co-founded ALS, co-designed and developed ADAPT/2000, which became a leading code re-engineering tool for anlayzing and remediating COBOL code on almost any computer platform to make applications Year 2000 compliant.

    ADAPT/2000 was quickly selected as the tool of choice by Hewlett Packard for its midrange systems, Data General, Digital VAX computers and many more.

    The underlying technology also included a comprehensive code re-engineering tool set to migrate proprietary COBOL code to newer systems with GUI interfaces.

    ALS was launched in January 2006 and was profitable from month one, growing at an exponential rate each quarter. In October 2007 the company was acquired and I stayed on in an advisory role for another year.
