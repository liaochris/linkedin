Timothy Yager’s Summary

Timothy Yager, Regional Vice President-Healthcare Practice, AT&T Business Solutions – GEM Markets.

Currently, responsible for $300M revenue stream. Leading a team of sales and service professionals that provide communication solutions to healthcare customers. These solutions include products ranging from basic telephone service to the most complex Data and Transport services, Wireless, Consulting, Hosting and Managed services. Manages the overall strategy and vision of his critically important relationships with all AT&T healthcare customers.

During 25 year AT&T career, has had extensive background in telecommunications outside plant, installation & repair, sales and management. Prior to being named his current role, served as Regional Sales Manager for Healthcare Sales for KCMO, Kansas, Oklahoma.

A native of New York, holds a B.S. degree in Business Administration from Sterling College, and served honorably in the United States Air Force from 1980-1984 as a member of the distinguished 1836 EIG while stationed in Wiesbaden, Germany.

Currently resides in Lawrence, Kansas with his wonderful wife Deb. In his spare time, enjoys everything KU, family, travel, fly fishing, bicycling and the outdoors.
