Danielle Westfall’s Summary

Is it possible to be left and right-brained? I think so....it’s who I am and what WMG is all about.

As an innovative Consumer Direct Marketing leader I launched WMG to furnish clients with a depth of expertise and innovation not known ‘til now in the wine, premium foods and luxury service sectors. With my distinct combination of creative savvy and business sophistication, my clients enjoy progressive, relevant and executable concepts and programs that capture today’s consumers and translate into impressive Direct revenues.
After a seasoned +18 years of traditional and new media marketing experience, I’ve built westfall marketing group into a full-service partner for $300MM brands like Ghirardelli Chocolate Company, iconic figures like Mario Andretti and emerging brands like NASCAR’s favorite winery, Childress Vineyards. Across the board, my clients recognize the distinct difference between partnering with me and “outsourcing” to others.
