Neil Perlman’s Summary

Information Technology and business executive with experience at global market leaders. For the past 20 years, Neil has been the VP of IT / Chief Information Officer (CIO) for 3 companies ranging in size from $300 million to $5 billion. In these companies he was a member of the Senior Operating team, and worked hand-in-hand with the business executives to significantly improve the bottom line. He has introduced new technologies that have transformed the company’s way of doing business.

In addition to working closely with the business executives, Neil has been on the business side as both a Chief Financial Officer (CFO) and a Chief Administration Officer (CAO).
