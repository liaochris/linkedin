Maryanne Ross, CFPIM, CIRM, CSCP, P-LOG’s Summary

Maryanne Ross is an international trainer, speaker and instructor specializing in Operations and Supply Chain Management. She has engaged and trained several thousand participants, working for clients such as Exxon Mobil, AOL, Merck, DuPont, Volvo, Northrop Grumman, Hollister, Fairchild Controls, and Grove. She has a passion for teaching and speaking on Personal and Professional Development topics, including leadership development, motivation, goal setting, personal empowerment, and team building.

For over 20 years, Maryanne has been an active member and volunteer for APICS, The Association for Operations Management She is now District Director for the APICS Mid Atlantic District for 2008-2010, serving on the APICS Board of Directors. Maryanne has developed and presented interactive workshops and keynote addresses at Congress for Progress, Seminar 1, APICS International Conferences, APICS Leadership Institutes, and at other local and international events.

Maryanne is a graduate of the Dale Carnegie Leadership Development Course. She is a trained facilitator of the Coveylink Speed of Trust Workshops. Maryanne has completed the Jack Canfield Breakthrough to Success Workshop, the Advanced Breakthrough to Success, and is one of a hundred individuals from around the world to be accepted into Jack's Train the Trainer program.
