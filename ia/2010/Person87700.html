Jane A. Matthews’s Summary

"There are no small design jobs just small designers."

My first art experience (that I remember) was in first grade when my teacher awarded my drawing of "birds in their nest" as the "best in class" and it hung on the wall for one whole week. That is when I knew that I wanted to be an artist.

I took art classes in grammar school all the way through high school. I majored in art in college and planned on becoming the next hot shot, or at the very least sell a few paintings.

Well, as it turns out the "starving artist" route is not for me, so I turned to graphic design and I LOVE IT. From the common to the esoteric, I love doing it all. I take the same pride in creating a lowly form to a major direct mail campaign from a logo to a Photoshop dream composite. Divas need not apply.
