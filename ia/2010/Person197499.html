Matthew Langford, CDPE®, SRS’s Summary

Matthew has over 10 years experience in the Real Estate and Investment industry. As a former Real Estate Economist and Consultant, Matthew has traveled extensively across the United States analyzing and assessing the nation's housing industry. He has particular expertise in evaluating local and national economic conditions and pricing trends.

Committed to providing exemplary service, Matthew is thorough and very detail oriented. Matthew's understanding and knowledge of the real estate market combined with his integrity and enthusiasm makes him uniquely qualified to guide you through the real estate process.

As a member of the San Diego Chapter of Urban Land Institute, Matthew is also actively involved in the community. He earned his BA in Economics from the University of Southern California and then went on to earn his MBA with an emphasis in Real Estate from the University of San Diego's School of Business Management. Prior to obtaining his undergraduate degree from USC, Matthew served in the United States Army as a Field Medic.

In his spare time, Matthew is involved in a myriad of activities including Swing Dancing, Polo, Surfing, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, USC Football and spending time with his family.
