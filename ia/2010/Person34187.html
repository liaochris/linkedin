Cathy Larkin’s Summary

At Web Savvy PR I bring social media tools to the traditional PR toolkit to help expand the reach of your brand and build your business; I also coach individuals on how to use social networking sites and blogging more effectively.

I put my 20 plus years of Public Relations and communications experience to work helping non-profits, small-business owners, solopreneurs, and individuals bring their message to their customers and clients through both the traditional art of PR, and with a Web-Savvy PR toolkit.

E+C=R or Experience+Creativity=Results

I am also a Social Media Guide (coach or consultant), helping organizations and individuals learn to navigate, and be effective in, the new social networking landscape. While I speak “Techtalk,” also speak plain English; I can explain geeky things in a non-tech, easy-to-understand way.

I am a writer with a knack for finding creative slants & crafting media pitches that match the needs of the organization with the needs of the editor, freelancer or blogger.

Whether in my PR work or in my social media consulting, I get to know my client's needs, craft an integrated strategy and match it with the right on-line networks and specific tools, and show them how to use them effectively.

On Twitter, you can find me at @CathyWebSavvyPR. One brand I helped to build is @SmallBizLady and her weekly Twitter "talk show" chat called #SmallBizChat (@SmallBizChat); I am also the co-host.

As a PR consultant, I can create & execute a full PR campaign, or simply write a dynamic press release. I can edit website copy, or set up a blog. I can create a new media strategy, sifting through the many social networking sites and on-line tools, to target the ones that will help you spread the word about what you do. This avoids the "If I build, it they will come" trap. And I can support his effort with PR outreach to traditional media channels.

Let me put my 20 plus years of communications experience to work for you.
