Kristin Kaufman’s Summary

An accomplished transformational leader and passionate leadership coach/consultant, with experience in strategic planning, leadership development, building/growing high performing teams, and maximizing business results.Key capabilities: helping individuals and organizations align their values, strengths, and goals to the overall strategic vision of their organization while maximizing individual and collective potential. High energy transformational leader, with experience in executing multi-dimensional change throughout organizations - non-profit and publicly traded entities.

Consistently delivered tangible, quantifiable results in each executive responsibility. Proven track record of coaching other executives/leaders to achieve their goals and build sustainable transformation in their organizations.

Awarded PCC (Professional Certified Coach) level distinction through International Coach Federation (ICF) and CLC (Certified Leadership Coach) from the ICF accredited distinguished program at Georgetown University.
