Ralph Gaston’s Summary

For nine years, I've worked tirelessly to hone the skills necessary to work most effectively in the world of broadcast journalism.

Need a story covered? I can report it, write it, edit the video and present it on-air.

In my career I've covered the world of sports on radio, television, and in print. I've worked as a lead anchor, a color analyst, a play-by-play announcer, and even a live truck operator.

I believe that my varied skill set is a benefit, if not an outright requirement, for the 21st century journalist.

My goal is to advance my career in sports journalism, and to do so while maintaining--and adding to--the skill set that has become my calling card.
