Victor Gutenmacher’s Summary

In addition to the famous book Lines and Curves, Dr. Gutenmacher, N.B. Vasilyev, and their colleagues J.M. Rabbot and A.L. Toom wrote in Russian the book "Mathematical Olympiads by Correspondence." (In fact, many of Dr. Gutenmacher's papers with N.B. Vasilyev were written under their collective pen name "Vaguten," which blends both their last names.) Dr. Gutenmacher also coauthored Homotopic Topology with A.T. Fomenko and D.B. Fuchs.
Last 5 years he is also the author of a few brochures in math published in National Russian Correspondence School for Russian High School students and teachers.
