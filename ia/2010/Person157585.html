Ajax Greene’s Summary

My experience is as a serial entrepreneur with over 25 years experience mostly with triple bottom line companies. My work with On Belay Business Advisor's primarily involves advising, coaching and mentoring the owner/founder/CEO's of companies who are feeling the pain of transition. Transition can translate into growth issues, founder succession, family issues, turnarounds for troubled companies or significant internal change like new products, new operational changes, new branding efforts, significant staff changes, integrating social responsibility etc. I am actively looking for new small and mid size company clients. I also enjoy working in interim senior management roles (GM, CEO, COO,ED, VP etc) filling in for an owner or other senior team member until a permanent solution can be accomplished.

Ajax Greene’s Specialties:

By using integrative general management I work with entrepreneurs to combine synergies from all aspects of your small and mid size company including marketing/branding, people management, finance, operations and your strategic commitment to social responsibility.
A few of the industries I have experience with include health care, fitness, wellness, recreation, adventure travel, hospitality, publishing, retail, manufacturing, seminars, social marketing, social enterprises and non-profits
