Abhilash Nallattil CCDM’s Summary

Over 5 years of experience in Clinical Data Management domain in both drug and device studies. I am a Certified Clinical Data Manager by SCDM currently working with Cognizant Technologies in its Life Science vertical in a lead role. My career started supporting customer service division of a US MNC BPO and later joined Lambda, an Indian CRO as a Data coordinator. Trained and certified in Clintrial and I-Review, CRF designing, database build, SAS programming, Data Review, Statistical report preparation and more CDM activities. With CDM expertise I had prepared SOPs and implemented. Besides experience in preparing DMP, ECS & UAT Scripts. With the experience of study startup, actively participated in study Conduct activities such data review, discrepancy management, reconciliation, data loading, auto coding and the close out activities.

In the present role as a group lead, I manage data managers for various projects with responsibilities including team management, performance management, process improvement, standardization, implementing quality control mechanism, training, driving Idea generation campaign, besides playing the role of SME for Clintrial and Inform.

Worked in device studies in Cardiology and drug studies in Respiratory, Arthritis, Oncology, Psychiatry, Neurology, Device studies besides many Phase I / BA/BE studies. Attended workshops and training programs on ICH GCP, CDISC, PMP, GCDMP, 21 CFR Part 11 and Six Sigma methodology.
