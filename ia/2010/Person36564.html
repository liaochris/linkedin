Chelsea Kempchinsky’s Summary

I am a graduate of Elizabethtown College. I have earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration. I have gained a concentration in both marketing and management. Please contact me at kempchinskyc@yahoo.com if you would like to review my resume for possible employment.

SWEET Sounds Chair has allowed me to gain valuable insight into the music industry. I have learned how to plan an event from beginning to end. My time here has taught me how to research potential performers for the Elizabethtown Campus. After the research is done I must present the program to our activities board. After the program is passed the next step is to get all necessary documents from the agents. This includes tech riders, hospitality riders, and any other relevant documents. I have learned how to fully understand a contract and make sure all guidelines are met.

I will be graduating this May 2010 and I have aspirations to work within the music industry as a manager/agent. I have plans to move down to Nashville by the end of August. Music is my true passion in life. Nothing would provide me with greater satisfaction than developing music artists tomorrow.
