Michael Pitzer’s Summary

Metro Consulting, LLC was founded to assist new entreprenuers with setting up a new company the right way. In 1997, we opened our first "real" (this one had a SBA loan) business which ultimately ended up failing because we were grossly under capitalized from the beginning. A failed business venture is embarrassing and not much fun to clean up after. Realizing the need for more education our founders went back to school and earned degrees which gave them a foundation of knowledge from which to grow a successful business model. They also examined the factors that led up to the business failure and applied lessons learned to start several successful ventures. When we give business advice it is based on coursework from the school of hard knocks not some fantasy world we read a book on in college. We have not been in some ivory tower reading about business startup, we have been living it!
