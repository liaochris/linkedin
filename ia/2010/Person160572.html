Paul Brin’s Summary

Results-driven management professional with over 25 years' experience in Data Center environments. Expertise in the analysis of cost-effective and green energy solutions.
Detail oriented and focused on customer needs.

• Paul Brin established Creative Facility Design, to provide the finest green energy solutions for new and existing Data Centers. Through careful analysis, attentive customer support and cost-effective solutions Creative Facility Design will become an industry leader serving the Data Center community.

• Paul brings 25 years' experience across a variety of positions and understands all aspects of building and maintaining Data Centers.

• Paul began his career at CVS Pharmacy immediately following graduation from New England Institute of Technology. He held the position of Computer Operator, Documentation Specialist, Operations Manager and Data Center Manager during his tenure at CVS.

• Paul became a major contributor to the design of the CVS Data Center. Paul collaborated with outside engineering groups, CVS Facilities, IT Department and the General Contractor to build a 25,000-square-foot, Tier 3 Data Center.

• In September, 2004 CVS Pharmacy outsourced their Operations and Operations Support Departments to Perot Systems. Paul transitioned to Perot as the Northeast Technology Center Facility Manager. In addition he became a member of the Leadership Team, responsible for ensuring a successful transition to Perot Systems.

• During his four years with Perot Systems Paul was a major contributor in the design and build stages of three major Data Center projects.

• In July 2008, Paul became the Chief Operating Officer of C.E. Communication Service Inc. Paul held this position until he established Creative Facility Design in December 2009.
