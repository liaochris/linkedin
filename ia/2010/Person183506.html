Sayamindu Dasgupta’s Summary

My focus areas include Localisation/Internationalisation, GNOME/GTK+ based software development (including PyGTK and PyGNOME), and Python related technologies.

I have significant experience in working remotely over the Internet using communication tools such as mailing lists, email, Internet Relay Chat (IRC), etc, and I have successfully coordinated volunteer teams whose members were scattered all around the globe.

I was one of the founder members of the Ankur Bangla project - an umbrella organisation responsible for localisation of Free/Open Source Software into Bangla (Bengali). I developed one of the very first Unicode compliant OpenType fonts for Bangla, and coordinated the font development subproject of Ankur Bangla for two years. I also coordinated the GNOME translation project of Ankur Bangla for just over a year and am currently the coordinator for bn_IN (Bengali/India) translations of GNOME and also one of the leads of the Bengali native lang projects of OpenOffice.org

I have also contributed patches and code to the Sabayon project, as well as the Exaile media player, and to Pango.
