Cynthia Kincaid’s Summary

Cynthia Kincaid is an award-winning marketing, media relations, and management consultant with more than 15 years of marketing and management experience.

Her marketing and management expertise has helped a range of Fortune 500 corporations, small and large organizations, ad agencies, marketing firms, and non-profit associations.

Cynthia has crafted many potent marketing and media relations strategies that have garnered her clients millions of dollars in revenue and hundreds of thousands of dollars in free media.

She has written numerous high-impact annual reports, manuals, proposals, and advertising and marketing collateral to provide solutions for a wide variety of communication and organizational challenges.

Additionally, Cynthia has written hundred of articles for numerous commercial and trade publications across the country.

She is the recipient of a 2007 Excellence in Journalism award from the Society of Professional Journalists.

She was a contributing writer for a business publication that was the winner of the 2008 Houston Business and Marketing Lantern Award.

Cynthia was also a contributing writer for KidsHealth.org, winner of a 2008 Webby Award and named One of the 30 Best Websites by U.S. News & World Report.

Toastmasters International awarded Cynthia a Divisional Motivational Speaking Award, and her dynamic speaking ability has earned her interviews in newspapers and on various radio stations. She has also presented seminars on marketing and writing for a wide variety of organizations.
