Heidi Karlsson’s Summary

Heidi Karlsson, M.A. has gained global expertise in the areas of tour leading and travel management, luxury hospitality, ecotourism development, MICE meetings-incentives-conferences-events, cruise, conservation and multi-cultural diplomacy through her studies, her professional experiences and travels to more than 20 countries.

A truly global citizen, Heidi has over 10 years of extensive travel experience throughout the world and has lived in Japan, England, Sweden, France, Mexico and the Caribbean. Her assignments have included travel to Asia, North and Central America, Europe, and North Africa among other places. She holds a Master in Arts in International Dispute Resolution and Negotiation specializing in Cross Cultural Relations and Environmental Issues. She has completed training in tour management and group procedures for airports, hotels, motor coaches, narration, briefings, handling challenges and emergencies, documentation and more.


Her experience includes work at UNESCO, Interpublic Group, WPP/MJM Creative, Publicis Groupe, Visionpilots, Caribiner, Evian and Apple, Inc. She has had a major influence on clients in the Hospitality, Pharmaceutical, Financial, Publishing, Consumer Products and Technology sectors. Clients have included all the major Airlines/Cruise Lines, Pfizer, IBM, Merrill Lynch, Maritz, San Pellegrino, Gallo, Sugar Foods, Phillip Morris, Pharmacia, Kraft, Citi, PepsiCo, YUM! Brands, PGI, Schering-Plough and sanofi-aventis.
