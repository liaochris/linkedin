Andy Green’s Summary

Andy began his real estate career in 1999 as a New Home and Community Specialist for Costa Pacific Homes in Bend, Oregon and has since been marketing and selling new homes for the past 10 years. He then made the decision to move back to his home town in Boise, Idaho to raise his family and had the opportunity to work for one of the largest builders in the state of Idaho. Over the next three years he was one of the company's top New Home Specialists and helped hundreds of families achieve the American dream of home ownership. Finding that he needed a new challenge he entered the re-sale real estate business and formed his own company, Green Group Real Estate.

While developing his sales skills and seeing a different side of the real estate business he gained invaluable experience that will serve him well in his current position. Because of his commitment to excellence and a strong emphasis on education he has been featured nationally as a top selling real estate expert.

Andy is a proud husband and father to three children. In his free time he enjoys spending time with his family going to the park, playing ball with his kids, hiking or playing miniature golf. His hobbies include running (ran in his first half marathon last year), reading and watching college football in the fall. Andy and his family place a strong emphasis on their faith and are active in their local church.
