Necia Dallas’s Summary

As an independent contractor in the field of strategic online design and marketing I've been specializing in interactive design ("design" encompassing tactical marketing, functionality, and usability as well as the surface of things) since 1995. I've designed and built innumerable web sites and campaigns, including Nike's nike.com site, which I designed many times over during my years as their Interactive Art Director, before moving on to Creative Director positions over the last five years.

Now I prefer to work for a wider variety of clients as a consultant, assisting with pitches, campaigns, and online strategy with a more recent emphasis on the potential of social networking.

Having started early in a young field, I’ve made every stupid mistake imaginable, which is why I now may seem smart. Now, of course, I strive to do only brilliant things...but remembering those mistakes–made with earnest passion–keeps humility omnipresent.

I help clients to create interactive experiences which actually generate sufficient interest to buy things, or to investigate things, or to talk about things. Campaigns which do not merely show a product–cue the drop-shadow and gleaming reflection!–but reveal its merits beautifully, adapting to the visitor and delivering just the right information to be useful, and worthwhile, to all parties. This means deep involvement in design and interactivity, well-turned copy, social networking incentives, and a genuine immersion in–and enthusiasm for– whatever is being featured. Good design can engender gratitude, and gratitude in turn generates great fondness. In the marketing world, this can translate to coveted Brand Loyalty.

I excel at creating resonances in a B2C environment (talking to people) because talking with people, and storytelling is something I like in general–in work and beyond.

Necia Dallas’s Specialties:

Boundless ideas and the savvy to execute on them, caffeinated speed, and undistilled interactive impulses. Translating content into adaptive, highly dynamic and user-responsive sites, engendering trust and brand loyalty through positive, intuitive, and rewarding UI, persona-based marketing, defining paths of action (in short: tactical interactive).
