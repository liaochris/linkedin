Qiuyi Duan’s Summary

I have recently relocated to Seattle area, and I'm currently looking for a software developer position.

My most recent working experience was with GE Healthcare - Surgery as a software engineer. My primary responsibility includes IPC protocol and software platform research with the goal to improve system reliability and efficiency; software design and development for medical devices, primarily back-end serviceability components for web based on-site and remote services; medical device verification and validation; third-party software and tools evaluation; leading field non-conformance investigations on software issues.

I achieved my Ph.D. in Computer Science with an emphasis on Computer Networking from Brigham Young University in 2008. I developed an intelligent radio switching system applying artificial intelligence and statistical techniques, so that the communication between devices can seamlessly switch among different radios and network stacks on the fly and better serve the users. Five papers have been published at IEEE major conferences.

In addition to the self-motivated academic research, I have been active in software development since 1998, ranging from individual driven projects to large system build up in team collaboration.
