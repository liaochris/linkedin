Jesse Albert’s Summary

A leading interactive marketer, strategist and consultant with 15 years experience on the front lines of digital media. Early adopter and creative thought leader in uses of digital and traditional media to enhance and extend consumer engagement. Comprehensive understanding of digital, social and branded entertainment. Experience creating and implementing innovative digital strategy on behalf of world leading artists, brands, corporations and properties. Often collaborate with and/or represent thought leaders and best of breed to create activations that distill cutting edge theory into mass consumer adoption. Equally comfortable with creative, business and technology.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS:

Produced first ever “American Idol: Songwriter” as part of Season 06 of television series. “Songwriter” was a fully integrated online component of the television series, featuring in-show calls to action resulting in online selection by television viewers of the “Finale” song “This Is My Now”. Winning song was revealed and sung live on show’s TV Finale and recorded as winner’s first single.

Initiated and managed iTunes relationship and all digital music sales, marketing and promotion strategy for Season 06 American Idol, achieving multiple Billboard chart positions and generating $1,000,000 + sales of recordings via iTunes and other digital distribution channels.

Recent partnerships executed with Ford, Showtime, Ikea, Sony, Airtran & others.

Produced first ever internet presence for consumer technology company Epson America.

Speak in press and on panels regarding digital strategy and branded entertainment
