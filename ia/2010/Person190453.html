Stephanie Steyer’s Summary

With over 25 years of experience in the design field, I am searching for a job with a company where I can contribute to their creative work and further enhance their business. My background is in all aspects of print design, as well as web design. I am competent in meeting with clients, managing people and large projects. I have experience in editing, proofreading, working with photography and illustration, layout, design, and production. Typography is one of my passions. With a relentless eye for detail and aesthetics, and the ability to quickly grasp and distill complex matters, I take pride in producing excellent design results.

Stephanie Steyer’s Specialties:

I feel that I can design anything once I understand the specifics of the job. I love a challenge!
