Michael Thacker’s Summary

With over 14 years of professional experience, Michael Thacker holds a wealth of experience in creating, developing and implementing public relations campaigns, including product launch and reviews programs, brand-building campaigns, and programs designed to reach technology audiences ranging from grass roots developers to C-level executives. Michael’s dual background in engineering and communications brings a unique prospective to the field and the companies he represents.
