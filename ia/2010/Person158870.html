Pamela Turay, M.S., SPHR’s Summary

Pam is a Senior Executive with extensive experience in partnering and working with senior leaders in the design, development, and execution of human resources strategies and initiatives, including talent management, learning and development, total rewards, performance, succession planning and change management. Known for creating excitement around organizational goals and objectives by engaging all levels of the organization in the process to drive results. Pam is high energy and has a passion for people, performance and results.
