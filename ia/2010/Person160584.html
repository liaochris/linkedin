Paul Buijs’s Summary

I develop cost effective, easy to manage web sites for the small to medium sized business. Sites are search engine friendly and powered by the award winning content management system, WordPress. With minimal instruction, they can be self maintained. No need to pay a webmaster for simple content addition or editing. Of course, I am happy to be there for all of my customers and am glued to my Blackberry.
All of my sites come with built in blog functionality that you may choose to utilize however you wish (or not at all). Call this feature company news or whatever suits your need. Multiple authors (say for different departments)? Piece of cake. Having an aesthetically integrated corporate blog keeps your site full of fresh content, your organization relevant in the search engines, and your customers informed.
I am a veteran, one man, shop. Each client gets my full personal attention. My customers are my advertising. Your success is my success.
Please feel free to contact me with any questions. You may be surprised at how easy it is to have me implement a highly functional solution for you. I'm in Arlington and more than willing to meet with you locally.

As the LinkedIn Guy, I also offer search engine optimized LinkedIn profile creation services for individuals and companies looking for assistance getting started. I freely share my enthusiasm and the tools and resources I come across on my LinkedIn related blog and resources page.
