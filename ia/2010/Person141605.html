Michael Mahan’s Summary

Senior executive with 15 years of legal experience, most recently serving from 2004 to 2010 as General Counsel, Senior Vice President, Director, and Corporate Secretary for Colemont Insurance Brokers, the 5th largest U.S. specialty lines wholesale insurance brokerage firm.

Broad in-house experience in litigation management, corporate governance, employment issues, capital-raising, stockholder and private company securities issues, and transactional matters. Developed a highly effective legal department and IT, communications, and compliance teams from the ground up. Designed services and deliverables for insurance products. Aligned legal, technology, and HR objectives with business goals.

Personal traits: Resourceful, creative thinker and achiever who thrives in fast-paced environments.

Graduated with a B.A. from Johns Hopkins University and earned J.D. from Southern Methodist University in Dallas, serving as Associate Editor of the SMU Law Review.

Michael Mahan’s Specialties:

Corporate Legal Matters, Risk Analysis & Mitigation, Regulatory Compliance, Stockholder & Private Company Securities Issues, Employment Law, Capital-Raising, Acquisition Evaluation, Board of Directors & Executive Collaborations, Strategic Planning, Contracts & Leases, Budget Planning & Administration, Credit & Finance Operations.
