Alan Bauman, M.D.’s Summary

Dr. Alan J. Bauman is the Founder and Medical Director of Bauman Medical Group, P.A. located in Wharfside at Boca Pointe in Boca Raton, Florida. Established in 1997, his practice is dedicated primarily to hair restoration and the treatment of hair loss in men and women, but also includes treatments such as non-invasive body-contouring, facial-rejuvenation and more. Dr. Bauman is one of the few board-certified Hair Restoration Physicians in the world. For over ten years, Dr. Bauman had advocated a scientific, results-oriented, multi-therapy approach to hair loss, using the latest medical, surgical and alternative techniques to help his patients maintain and/or restore their scalp hair. He attributes his success to his passion for delivering the highest quality results and patient care which surpass the currently-accepted standards-of-care within the field of hair restoration.

Dr. Bauman was one of the first practices worldwide to use the ZERONA laser device for non-invasive, non-surgical body-contouring and fat reduction--an alternative to liposuction surgery.

Dr. Bauman uses the Omnilux LED phototherapy device which is FDA-approved to reduce the appearance of fine-lines and wrinkles with no pain and no downtime.
