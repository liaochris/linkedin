Kimberly Gomez, MBA’s Summary

Entrepreneur of image consulting and fashion styling business. Sells current and vintage fashion accessories. Works with clients who believe the image they present to the world is critical to their success in all areas of life. Results-oriented and completely customer-focused. Strategic thinker and outstanding communicator. Unparalleled time manager due to being highly organized. Proficient in a variety of different software solutions.

Many years of corporate experience in different areas including compensation. This includes extensive experience with corporate expatriate, international program.

Color analysis and draping, figure analysis, style profiling, wardrobe consultation, personal shopping, personal branding, fashion styling, image consulting, makeup consultation, hair consultation.

Corporate and business experience and education combined with fashion savvy equals consulting and instruction that is value added for corporations and other organizations interested in their domestic and international image.

Project manager, CCP (Certified Compensation Professional)
