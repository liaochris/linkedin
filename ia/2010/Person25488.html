Robert Jacobson’s Summary

I'm an innovation-management consultant with a lifelong interest in human experience and its systematic design.

I provide value to policy and commercial organizations concerned with change, opportunities, risky situations, and novel approaches to the future.

My LinkedIn contacts are on the cutting-edge of their respective disciplines and practices. Please ask to join if you are, too.

• Holistic and technology-driven experience design
