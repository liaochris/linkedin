    Faces of Fortune (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Entertainment industry)

    May 2009 — Present (1 year )

    I paint the human body for unique photographic projects, fitness photography, and parties.

    My background and award winning designs in Wearable Art and also experience with advanced international Cake designs gives me an edge to incorporate some techniques of my other art avenues.

    I am in the planning stages to make a move back to CA where this will be in more demand and I can eventually develop a very unique business for high end parties.

    (Self-Employed; Textiles industry)

    January 1992 — Present (18 years 4 months)

    I design elaborate coats and jackets for both exhibiting in homes or commissions for wearing. I also design and create textile wall hangings framed to flow nicely with the design of each piece.

    I have won numerous awards for my work in textiles, some have traveled the country and abroad in runway shows, highlighted in many magazines about textile work, and I have written articles about my own innovations in fabric manipulation and use of ethnic textiles in design.
