Thomas Farrell’s Summary

PEO Spectrum’s mission is to help companies realize the benefits of the PEO industry by streamlining the process of learning, shopping, switching, and contracting with the ideal PEO for their business.

Our position as an unbiased third-party intimately involved in the selling/buying process allows us to expedite communication between the potential customer and all suitable PEOs available.

PEO Spectrum will allow a company to drastically the reduce the time it takes to shop for a PEO, increase the efficiency of finding a PEO that fits their business needs, and through spurring a competitive selling situation with multiple vendors that are familiar with the business function of PEO Spectrum, lower the average cost of doing business with a PEO.

PEO Spectrum also lowers the cost of goods sold for the PEO as we are actively prospecting, qualifying, and finding great clients for them. Essentially we’re an extension of their sales team without the risk and cost of having an employee.
