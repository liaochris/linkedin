Phil C. Solomon’s Summary

An innovative executive with a 20 year track record of sustained success in creating and building profitable organizations. Experienced in all facets of business development, profit & loss, strategic planning, market analysis, operational analytics, marketing, sales and customer relations. Currently, holding the position as Chief Executive at iSolutions I.Q., a healthcare data analytics firm and simultaneously holding the position as Chief Client Officer and Senior Vice President of Sales & Marketing at UCB, Inc., a revenue cycle solutions and optimization firm. Experiences in the past 15 years have been focused on the adoption of leading edge technologies and business process outsourcing solutions for the healthcare industry. The identification and development of solutions addressing emerging patient access and revenue cycle issues facing health care providers in this difficult economy is a key focus.

Through leadership as a Managing Partner, at Collectech Systems, gained national acclaim from INC Magazine as one of the 500 fastest growing private companies in America two years in a row. Other positions held include serving as CEO for Knowlagent, an Atlanta based Fast Tech 50 software development firm and Senior Vice President for IntelliRisk, a Global Technology, and Outsourcing Company headquartered in Columbus, Ohio.

An accomplished speaker, entrepreneur, futurist thinker and active member of HFMA, NAHAM, AAHAM, MGMA while holding a B.A. degree from San Diego State University.

Phil C. Solomon’s Specialties:

Revenue cycle optimization consulting focusing on registration quality, insurance verification, point of service collections and self-pay work flow, presumptive charity processing and consumer behavioral payment scoring.
