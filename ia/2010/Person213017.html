Matthew Allen’s Summary

Thanks for visiting my LinkedIn profile. I'm assuming that you're here because you're passively searching for interesting new job opportunities and want be as transparent and discrete as possible in the process? I'd like to invite you to visit http://www.verticalmove.com/jobs for more than 100+ positions within some of the worlds more prestigious names in high-tech, including well funded start-ups backed by Kleiner, Sequoia, Benchmark or Goldman.

It's important to understand that our business has always been defined by a simple, but unique approach that defies the industry-standard... We're NOT out spamming resumes or acting dishonestly. We take the time to do things right and this includes being ethical and having a deep passion for building lasting personal relationships with those we come in contact with each day.

We also invest time into understanding the technology and trends that make our industry go around. We've been around long enough to know that Java is an object-oriented, interpreted programming language, originally code-named Oak (trivia: other names that were suggested prior to Java included DNA, Silk and even Ruby). We know that AJAX was coined by Google and prior asynchronous Web GUIs were often developed using the XMLHttpRequest API (a technique originally developed by Microsoft).

We've always been regarded as trusted advisors for professionals searching for their next big thing, or a client looking to hire the best talent for their team.

For perspective clients.. We've always considered ourselves as being Headhunters rather than Recruiters. We do not rummage through resume boards searching for that proverbial needle in the bottomless haystack. Find one good resume and its safe to assume that twenty other recruiting firms have found the same individual. No thanks; we'd rather avoid overly competitive situations by identifying professionals that are passive and not announcing their availability to the masses via the Internet.
