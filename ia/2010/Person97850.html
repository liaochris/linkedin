Jake Henry Wise’s Summary

I am currently putting my efforts behind advancing strategies for:

(1) Smarter Electric Rates for a Smart Grid:
Once the advanced metering infrastructure is fully rolled out, home-area-network compatible energy management systems will provide customers with access to real-time usage information. Accurate price signals, a.k.a. dynamic rates, therefore stand at the nexus of transformations in transportation, electricity generation, and building design. At the core is an understanding of which behavioral levers, from prices and efficiency standards to feedback and social norms, are most effective at inducing demand responses and achieving energy conservation. I believe that energy efficiency is our greatest resource and that we find ourselves at a stage whereupon technological innovation has achieved scalability, public awareness has heightened, and incentive structures and instruments promoting renewables combine to form a powerful set of opportunities for growth and change.

(2) Social Venture Finance:
I am interested in projects that endeavor to:
- Foster innovative enterprises which combine a strong social purpose with sound business principles, rather than being solely driven by the need to maximize profit;
- Create financially self-sufficient organizations that are able to deliver strong and measurable social impact for the communities they serve;
- Embrace the parallels and opportunities for knowledge transfer between the technology entrepreneurs and social entrepreneurs;
- Create sustainable, long-term value as neither solely economic nor solely social;
- Achieve real social returns together with financial returns that are not concessionary to the risk-adjusted rate investors could otherwise attain;

Both paths converge at the quantification of societal benefit
