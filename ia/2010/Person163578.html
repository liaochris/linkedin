Phil Belleville, Social Media Marketing’s Summary

Help businesses create a custom social media marketing strategy by providing a custom approach for businesses to leverage social media marketing, local search marketing, mobile marketing platforms and video as well as internet marketing methods utilizing organic search to engage with their prospects and customers. In this way I can help businesses increase their client base as well as build a network of loyal followers and establish social and local authority.

Designer of a unique and highly effective marketing approach which I call the Social Media Marketing Blueprint. The process is completely unique and based on my 15 years of Internet Marketing experience. The Social Media Marketing Blueprint consists in using in combination the power of Social Media Platforms along with Youtube Marketing, Local Search Marketing, Mobile Marketing, LinkedIn Marketing, Blogs and Google organic search marketing to create a large lead generation and sales funnel. And then to integrate this approach with their offline (face-to-face) networking. This results in increased Social Authority, visibility, sales, leads and profits.

I’m an Experienced Internet Marketing, Social Media Marketing and Business Development executive and I have a broad experience in all aspects of marketing specializing in online, mobile and social media. I have direct experience social media, facebook marketing, twitter marketing, local search marketing, video marketing, linkedIn marketing, search engine optimization, search engine marketing and lead acquisition. Proven ability to help others grow a strong online presence and grow their business through digital media.
