Damon Flowers’s Summary

Part Entrepreneur...part Maven...part Thinker...part Contrarian

..Hands down, this is one of the most exciting seasons of my life. Currently working on joint venture collaborations w/ product & service companies, opportunities in investment real estate, and personal information marketing projects..

Gifted with creative ability to see and articulate a shared vision, bridge the gap between the vision and priorities, draft a tactical plan and see it through to successful completion. Success comes from the synergy created when I'm working as an "editor" alongside other great "authors". I think about life in terms of systems, process, optimization and efficiency. Enjoy figuring out how to take offline behaviors and replicate them in more efficient online experiences. Naturally gifted forming and leading teams to bring a product or service to market...

My personal mission is to create and guide spiritual optimism through Jesus Christ...to reflect God's mercy and grace to the young who are seeking to live a more fulfilling and transparent life through abundant sharing of time and money...
