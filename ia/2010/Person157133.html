Oleh Myroshnichenko’s Summary

Analyst with experience in the areas of European integration, public policy in Ukraine, European politics. Participated in a number of research activities both on my own and in a team. Fast learner and professional user of Internet tools for research.

Have a network of contacts in Ukrainian think-tanks, ministries, technical assistance projects.

Guest speaker at European Union Delegation in Ukraine, National Academy of Public Administration, Kyiv Politechnical Institute.

After graduation from National University “Kyiv-Mohyla Academy” with MA degree in economics (2004) studied European economy, politics and law at the European College of Parma Foundation (Italy, 2005-2006). Consequently worked for International Centre for Policy Studies (Kyiv) as analyst in state policy and European integration. Received training in public policy research from the Institute of Public Affairs (Warsaw, 2008), attended a course on European Economic Area (Kyiv-Brussels, 2007) and participated in several summer schools in the EU. Have a number of national and international publications and research focusing on the issues of technical regulation, public procurement, EU-Ukraine Free Trade Area, European integration of Ukraine and others.
