Cristian Martinez, PMP’s Summary

I can feel myself living a great time, both personally and professionally. I have plenty experience on my back and extreme self confidence to carry out whatever challenge comes upfront. My wife and my two daughters give me the best reason to love life every day and the horizon of my self development is just showing up.

Throughout my life I've taken several risky decisions, and I do not regret of anyone. Each step is a new adventure for me, and a new conflict is a new opportunity for growing up.

I started in a technical position at the BigBlue and quickly discovered that was not for me. I needed more contact with people and real life, so I changed to pre-sales and sales afterwards. I learned in the field to deal with people, and a while later I felt the need to open up my mind and decided to move to Europe. In those times I couldn't imagine all the transformations my life was going to undergo.

I found a great place to work and to develop my future, starting as a Knowledge Management consultant, and then specializing myself in Project Management focusing mainly in people, team building, group trust and counselling.

Now I'm opening a new page of my life, I am more in touch with feelings and emotions, and developing my abilities in group facilitation, conflict resolution and communication.

Thank You !
