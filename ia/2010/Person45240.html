Curt Adams’s Summary

Equipped with a 20-year track record of increasing product sales across diverse industries with in the retail, Government, Military, Medical channels, I am confident in my ability to contribute value to your business. I have been involved in procuring Gov contracts for rifle scopes, laser and night vision as well as products for AFEES, NEXCOM, and the USMC Exchange System. I also sold these products heavily in the marine industry and to state and local government. As a U.S Marine, I had a secret security clearance to work with microwave and encryption radio equipment.

During my career, I excelled while performing as national sales manager at Comfort-Trac, where I directed product sales spanning numerous channels to include orthopedic centers, Dr’s offices, and medical catalogs. Notably in this role, I built a national sales force of 45 representatives and drove a 54% increase in sales while growing the account base from 8 to 63 national accounts. As the VP & Director of Sales/National Sales Manger at Dyno Merchandise and Comfort Trac my major accounts were 7 Eleven, Walgreens, CVS, Longs, Wal-Mart, K-Mart, Office Depot, Staples & All Federal, State and Military Accounts. No matter the role in which I performed, I developed and implemented sales and marketing plans that facilitated growth and profitability for my employers.

At Simplex I learned the ins and outs of selling and implementing LANS & WANS and the Software and hardware from everything from industrial to retail accounts. Some of the hardware consisted of bar-coding equipment, retina scanners, hand and finger print recognition equipment etc. Also at Simplex I completed a 10 day long Solutions Sales “Boot camp”
My desire in a new position is to work and grow with a company that is financially stable and will afford me the opportunity to contribute to the company’s continued success! Currently I am a VP of Sales for A Water and Coffee Company that is extremely green oriented.
