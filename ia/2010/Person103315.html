    Tripology

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Tripology is an online travel referral service that connects travelers to travel specialists. Since Tripology.com opened its virtual doors to consumers in June 2007 the site has received over 100,000 requests from travelers seeking the help of the over 13,000 travel specialists in the Tripology network.

    Tripology provides travel specialists, regardless of size or location, a cost effective way to increase their sales and profits with qualified travel leads. Travel specialists maintain control of the leads they choose to purchase and the ability to alter their profile and lead filters at anytime.
