Jeremy Mitchell’s Summary

Hello. I am Jeremy. I am an Interactive Art Director / Web Designer / UI Developer. I live in Nashville, Tennessee with my wife. I love meeting new clients and hearing about their wants, needs, and dreams. My role is to make sure my clients' websites are beautiful and function well.

My marketing degree and my constant study habits of the market help me succeed as a Web Designer. It is a blessing to be on both sides of the spectrum: marketing and design. I also speak geek and lots of it. No pixel pusher or code monkey found here. I have an excellent understanding of html and css. I love learning and teaching new things and I am always learning about the newest trends in my industry.
