Pran S. Dasan’s Summary

In 2005, together with a team of aviation professionals we conceived the idea of an aviation consultancy firm drawing from my experience in the airline industry especially in sales/marketing and general management. Our brainchild is Pathfinders Aviation Pvt. Ltd., founded in February 2006 offering the following services:

> Market research
> Airline business solutions
> Aviation & Flight Training solutions
> Facilitation and Government liaison with the Ministry of Civil Aviation and the DGCA.
> Airport development projects

My 18 years of aviation industry experience includes a nine-year stint with Lufthansa German Airlines including management positions. Presently I head Sales & Marketing for Kuwait Airways Corporation in the Indian Subcontinent with 10 online stations and over 50 weekly flights.

I received a MSc degree in Air Transport Management from City University, London including a distinction in my project on the problems facing low cost airlines in India. Besides the above, I have completed over 15 training courses/seminars with Lufthansa German Airlines and IATA. Also received a certificate from Cessna for participation in their Seminar on Cessna Pilot Centers held in Tampa, Florida in March 2006. I also recently qualified as an IATA Certified Instructor securing a distinction.

I am a Member of the Aeronautical Society of India - a premier body for the advancement and diffusion of knowledge of the Aeronautical and Aerospace Sciences and Engineering and the elevation of the Aeronautical and Aerospace Profession.

I am also a regular columnist for several aviation industry journals and publications and am currently working upon the compilation of a reference book on the development of jet aircraft through the years.

Pran S. Dasan’s Specialties:

Air accident investigation, Fleet Planning & Managment, Route & Network Planning, Pricing & Revenue Management, Scheduling, Loyalty programmes, Distribution Systems, Airline Economics
