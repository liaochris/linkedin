Kenneth Schieffer’s Summary

As an SAP Education Principal and SAP Financials/HCM Business Development Manager, Ken is responsible for (1) the rollout of the North American certification program; (2) the content provided on the US SAP Education public website; and (3) SAP Financials and Human Capital Management North American education portfolio.

In addition, I own the Education@SAP LinkedIn Group (http://www.linkedin.com/groups?gid=1856570), write blogs for SAP Education (http://www.sdn.sap.com/irj/scn/weblogs?blog=/weblogs/topic/131 - registration req'd) and manage the SAP Education SDN Discussion Forum (http://forums.sdn.sap.com/forum.jspa?forumID=480&start=0 - registration req'd).
