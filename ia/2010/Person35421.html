Chad Meyers’s Summary

Chad Meyers is an aspiring product manager who believes he has found his forte working new product development projects that develop innovative solutions to customer problems. Through rotational programs at GE Healthcare and Honeywell, Chad has held diverse roles in engineering, customer support, information technology, marketing and Six Sigma. While acquiring his 10 years of experience in the aerospace and medical technology industries, he almost interned for the CIA, went underground river tubing in Australia, and worked ten feet from the 1996 Olympic Village in Atlanta, GA. Since obtaining his MBA and Masters in Information Technology, Chad has been using his past experience in remote monitoring and services at GE to support Honeywell Aerospace’s remote monitoring services initiative for commercial aircraft equipment.

Experience in innovation, new product development, gathering customer needs, six sigma, process design and improvements and project management
