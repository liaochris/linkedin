Jeff Rothman’s Summary

The executive search firm I founded more than 11 years ago works nationally to provide retained and contingency recruiting services for companies in direct marketing, database marketing, e-marketing and fundraising. Our clients include for-profit direct marketers, non-profit organizations and direct marketing service providers such as direct response agencies, direct mail printers, direct mail lettershops and database marketing companies.

With 20+ years of combined experience in direct marketing and recruiting and a collaborative, customer-focused approach, we've established an outstanding track record as one of the premier national search firms in our industries.

Jeff Rothman’s Specialties:

Recruiters for direct marketing service providers, direct mail direct response, database marketing, e-marketing, direct marketing for non-profit organizations and corporations, working primarily on positions in sales, sales management, senior account management, direct response management, direct marketing management, fundraising, digital marketing, emarketing.
