Steve Trover’s Summary

Founder and CEO of All Star Vacation Homes, a vacation rental management firm focusing on luxury vacation home rentals. All Star is involved in the management, marketing and hospitality services of vacation rental properties.

Founder and VP of Troventure Inc. d.b.a.Beyond Furnishings. Beyond Furnishings is the exclusive vacation home furnishings and interior design partner for All Star Vacation Homes.

Founding member of Discover Vacation Homes. Discover is an initiative of the Vacation Rental Managers Association with the charge of raising the awareness of vacation rentals as a top of mind lodging option.

Steve Trover’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurship, vacation rentals, vacation rentals, vacation rentals - vacation home management and marketing.
