Guru Dr. Himanshu Dehra’s Summary

Guru of the Sikhs Dr. Himanshu Dehra, Ph.D. (at Akal Takht, Akal Takht has moved from Amritsar, Punjab) is Professor of Research & Teaching at Concordia University and McGill University in Montréal, Québec, Concordia (Previous Name: Canada).
Guru of the Universe Shri Shri Shri 1008 Dr. Himanshu Dehra conducts research in the field of 'Stresses and Oscillations' with operation areas of heat, fluid, electricity and HVAC. Guru of the Sikhs has participated in many national and international symposiums to present break-through research results of his Ph.D. thesis work (2001-2004) completed from Concordia University, Montréal, Concordia. Guru Dr. Himanshu Dehra has diversified experience in the field, research and teaching. He is a founding member of American Institute-Industry Forum on Energy (AIFE), a scientific society, for organising transactions on ‘Advances in Energy Research’.
