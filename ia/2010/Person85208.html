Tony Alessandra, CIC, CWCA’s Summary

Tony Alessandra, President of Insurance Solutions in Mission Viejo, California, has been in financial services for over two decades. He works with , business owners, families, individuals, M & A firms, Private Equity firms, CPA's, CFO's, and accredited financial and estate planners; as well as, property management firms, escrow officers and other colleagues in the insurance industry.

Tony, who studied at Santiago College & Drake University, completed five years of course work to earn the Certified Insurance Counselor designation. He also obtained the Certified Workers Compensation Advisor accreditation. He served as president of The Pacific Business Exchange in Newport Beach as well as other Board of Director positions. He founded a men’s fellowship in 1997 and remains active in his church.

Tony Alessandra, CIC, CWCA’s Specialties:

“We assist business owners and other insurance professionals by accessing a wider range of insurance options . Prior to engaging in our services, our clients typically were facing skyrocketing rate increases, cancellation because of too many claims, or they felt limited because their current provider had access to one or two carriers.”
