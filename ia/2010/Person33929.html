Cate Poe’s Summary

Cate Poe is an experienced community organizer, communicator and connector.

While her early organizing experience was in urban areas, in recent years she has focused on issue organizing and political campaign work in the toughest turfs in the country: the suburban and blue-state swing districts critical to the balance of power at both State and National levels.

Her goal is to lead the charge to effectively combine on-the-ground and on-line community building and organizing. Towards this end, she has embarked on a crash course in social media and is actively seeking opportunities to work with forward thinking organizations to build virtual and real-world communities.

Cate Poe’s Specialties:

Long-term labor and community organizing experience.

Cutting-edge combination of on-the-ground AND on-line organizing strategies

Ability to hit the ground running, assess the situation, and provide back-up to less-seasoned staff and leadership

Extensive nation-wide network of relationships

A passion for building alliances, and a deep appreciation of what it takes to sustain them.

A focus on winning short-term victories while building to last.
