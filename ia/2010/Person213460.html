Waikit Chung’s Summary

Dutch born Hong Kong Chinese Industrial Designer with passion to design meaningful products and to serve the industrial design community.

3 years design experience in consumer electronics/products, professional equipment and interactive applications on an international level for Western and Asian clients, including SABIC Innovative Plastics, Philips, Samsung, Cisco, Nokia, ChangHong, Lenovo, TCL, TEFAL, Freecom and more.

Currently living in Shanghai and working as an industrial designer and manager for Speck Design. Also a managing director and editor of Product Design Hub / Forums.
