Marlene Chism’s Summary

Who is in charge? Is it the trouble maker in your office, the voice in your head, or the worries that keep you up at night?

Drama is any obstace to your peace and prosperity. Drama manifests in the workplace as office gossip, turnover, absenteeism, negativity, workplace bullying and power struggles.

In your personal life drama manifests as financial problems, a fight with your spouse, or the obsessive thoughts that you are not quite measuring up.

There are always three core components always present in Drama. Lack of clarity is one of those components. The Stop Your Drama Methodology will help you get clear and step on the top deck. I always say,"The one with clarity navigates the ship and everyone else shovels coal." If you feel like you are shoveling coal, I guarantee, clarity can change your situation.

About me: I have a Masters degree in HR Development and I'm getting ready to publish a book with Wiley, entitled Stop Workplace Drama: No complaints, no excuses, no regrets leadership.

My passion is helping indivuals develop leadership skills through self-mastery, which is achieved by learning how to make conscious responsible choices.

The methods I introduce into the workplace also work in your personal life, so if you are having problems with your spouse, your child, or your in-laws, going through the SYD training will also significantly improve your personal relationships.

I provide individual coaching and consulting as well as training and speaking.

Last year, I developed Attitude Builders, a resource for HR Managers, Sales Directors, Practice Administrators and Business Owners who want to stop workplace drama, and eliminate negativity in the workplace.

If you lead other, you can offer these content rich and quick programs as a staff development program with no prep, no plan and no problem. Not only does this promote camaraderie, this program accelarates awareness and increases teamwork and ultimately productivity.
