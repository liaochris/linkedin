Tonia Mosteller’s Summary

The design industry has been a passion for me since I can remember. Over the past 15 years I've been working in Interior Design - Interior Redesign, Real Estate Staging business. My previous career in corporate management, sales and recruiting has added value and experience to my design career.

I am certified and licensed in Home Staging, Interior Home Redesign, One Day Decorating and Interior Design. As a owner and partner of Redesign + More I take great pleasure in creating beautiful and functional spaces for my clients. Creating rooms that are warm and inviting, by reflecting your lifestyle and personality.

Creating your personal retreat, is my goal. I look forward to building my business in the communities we service. As a professional Home Stager and Interior Redesigner at Redesign + More I am eager to work with Home Sellers & Realtors to maximize the selling potential of their properties.
