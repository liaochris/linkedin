Nina Platt’s Summary

Strategic consulting services for law firms, law libraries, and legal information and software vendors including strategy, product solutions, research and training.

Examples of work done for law firms include:

* Cost recovery strategy & planning
* Law firm library of the future planning
* Knowledge management strategy & planning
* Knowledge management initiative implementation
* Library staffing studies
* Research resources cost containment
* Law firm intranet expert review and planning
* Market, competitive intelligence and general research
* Project management
* Library software selection

Examples of work done for legal information and software vendors:

* Competitive intelligence research
* Market research
* Product review & design
* Product usability review
* Pricing model review & development
* Outsourcing of marketing, sales, account management and requirements analysis for product development
* Project management
* Business of law training for marketing and sales staff

In addition to the work listed above I have also been a speaker and trainer in topics including knowledge management, library of the future, intranet strategy, marketing, etc. for large conferences to in-house training. See http://npcitraining.wordpress.com/ for more information.

Nina Platt’s Specialties:

Cost recovery and containment strategy; Knowledge management strategy; Intranet strategy and planning; Integration of third party information sources with intranet and other firm systems; Project management; Market research; Competitive Intelligence; Marketing strategy; Product review and design; Product user group development; Vendor advisory board development
