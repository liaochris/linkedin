John Stacy’s Summary

J.P. Stacy & Company, Inc. is a solutions based commercial real estate firm.
As owner, I provide my clients with solutions-- or sometimes patches-- to their real estate issues without breaking the bank.

We clarify the issue, develop Plan A-Z alternatives, allow the client to make an informed decision and then carry out that decision.

I have 32-years experience and expertise as a commercial real estate practitioner. I have handled commercial real estate issues/challenges/transactions from Nevada, Missouri to Las Vegas, Nevada.

In this challenging market, I will be a trusted confident making sure you get the information and advise necessary for you to make the best decisions for your company.
