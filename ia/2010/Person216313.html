LaKesha Womack’s Summary

LaKesha Womack has set out to prove that you can have it all!

She is the mother of a busy four year old; serves on a regional committee within her church; is the owner and event planner for Premier Event Planning; is a business consultant for small and start up businesses; serves on the Board of Directors of three organizations; is an advisor to a local agency and national organization; serves as the vice-president of a professional women's group and writes a daily blog chronicling her journey toward achieving her goals. In her spare time, she enjoys playing golf and baking sweet treats.

Just reading all of that may make you say, “Wow, how does she do it all?”

LaKesha says, “We are all given the same 24 hours. Living your priorities through efficient time management will help you to create balance and success in your life.”

Contact us to have LaKesha speak at your next event. She will impress you with her common sense approach to living a life of integrity as well as inspire you to reach new heights in your personal and professional life.

You too can have it all!
