Matthew Moore’s Summary

Matthew graduated from Georgetown's McDonough School of Business in 2008 as a marketing and finance major. After a semester at Hofstra University School of Law, Matthew has decided his skills, passions and goals align more firmly with a marketing career. Currently, Matthew is seeking opportunities in sales, brand management and marketing research to supplement his marketing education and prior marketing experiences and begin a long, creative and fun career in the business world.

Through his experience with Georgetown's Office of Advancement, Matthew had face-to-face discussions with over 500 Georgetown alumni in varying industries, including law, finance, and entertainment, to build a pipeline of alumni interested in getting involved in Georgetown’s community. This unique opportunity allowed Matthew to develop flexible communication and networking skills, and strong logistical and organizational skills. Furthermore, Matthew was able to utilize his amicable, outgoing and passionate personality to represent Georgetown in a professional and credible manner.

Matthew also has significant experience in event coordination. Through his work as business manager of the Georgetown Chimes, Matthew organized over eighty performances within one academic year in Washington D.C., New York, New Orleans and Europe. Additionally, through his work with the Senior Class Committee, Senior Class Gift Committee, and Georgetown’s Office of Leadership Development, Matthew organized social events for over 1,000 Georgetown students, leadership programs for incoming freshman, team-building seminars for campus organizations, community service events and fundraisers, each requiring effective team management, strong attention to detail, and a positive leadership style.

Matthew is excited about his current job search and welcomes opportunities to speak with other passionate and focused business-minded individuals about career advice, further contacts and job leads.
