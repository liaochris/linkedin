Sue Rogers’s Summary

Sue Rogers, the ease your workload expert and certified Internet marketing and social media consultant, owns Rogers Executive Administrative Services in Erie, Pennsylvania. With 30 years experience, Sue helps ease the workload of solo-professionals and small companies in need of marketing strategies and professional executive administrative assistance. Sue is able to provide professional, high-quality workmanship, done quickly and at a cost-savings that is passed on to her clients. She is also the first virtual assistant in Pennsylvania to assist the agriculture communities with their marketing strategies and database needs to improve their farm profits and sustainability. She works with all her clients to showcase their expertise and to help them keep their exposure and recognition as an expert in their field. Sue can be reached at smrogers@easmyworkload.com and blogs regularly at http://agriculture.easemyworkload.com.
