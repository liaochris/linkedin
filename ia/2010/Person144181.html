Michelle Conlon’s Summary

Michelle Conlon has over 15 years experience in Web Design, Development, and New Media Marketing Strategies. For the last two years at Pragmatic Marketing she has helped grow the online community by over 20% with well over 45,000 unique visitors per month. And she plays an instrumental role in Pragmatic Marketing's online webinar series, site/blog designs, and online video production.

Michelle also works as an independent consultant for various companies as she utilizes her diverse background in moving her clients forward into the New Media Marketing arena.

With New Media Marketing becoming the focal point for many small business and organizations, her goal is to give Pragmatic Marketing, her clients, and their customers the best online experience possible.
