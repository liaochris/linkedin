Sergio Mugnaini’s Summary

Sergio Mugnaini has a degree in Communication and completed his studies at the School of Visual Arts in New York. Recently graduated from Berlin School of Creative Leadership, through the Steinbeis University in Berlin. He worked at Ogilvy Interactive, J. Walter Thompson, DM9DDB and now he is a Creative Director of AlmapBBDO.

Sergio has 8 lions at the Cannes Festivals, among other awards like One Show Interactive, Art Directors Club of New York, D & AD, New York Festivals, London International Advertising Awards, El Sol, El Ojo Iberoamerica, FIAP and some national awards.

He also participate at some internationals juries like Cannes, One Show Interactive, D & AD, El Ojo de Iberoamerica, London Festival Awards, FIAP, CCSP and MMonline / MSN. In 2005, he was elected the creative of the year in MSN / Mmonline and the same year led to AlmapBBDO to be elected the agency of the year.

Client list: IBM, Volkswagen, Pepsi Havaianas, Telefonica, Renault, Unilever, Honda, TAM, Philips, Greenpeace and others.

In 2007 and 2008 the AlmapBBDO is among the 10 most awarded agencies in the Gunn Report Interactive.

More information: www.mug9.com
