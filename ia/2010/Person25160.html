Brian Rubin’s Summary

Brian, the father of a son with autism, limits his law practice to serving the legal & “future planning” needs of his fellow families of children & adults with special needs.

* Past Chair, State of Illinois Dept of Human Services Statewide Advisory Council on Developmental Disabilities
* Member, State of Illinois Autism Task Force (Co-Chair)
* Member, State of Illinois Disabilities Services Advisory Committee (Olmstead Committee)
* Member, State of Illinois Dept of Human Services’ Office of Inspector General Quality Care Board (Over-Sight Board)
* Member, Board of Directors of The Arc of Illinois
* Member, Board of Directors of PACT, Inc.
* Member, Board of Directors of Clearbrook
* Member, Board of Directors of SLSF (Foundation for Northwest Suburban Special Recreation Association)
* Former Member, Board of Directors of Keshet (Jewish Parents of Children & Adults with Special Needs)
* Former Member , Board of Directors of Northpointe Achievement Centre
* Former Member, & Founding Chair, Board of Trustees of The Foundation for Special Education District of Lake County (SEDOL Foundation)
* Faculty Member of & Author, Illinois Institute for Continuing Legal Education (IICLE)
* Member, Board of Directors of The Special Needs Alliance, the national, membership by invitation only, non-profit association of experienced Special Needs Planning Attorneys (www.specialneedsalliance.org)
* Was a Charter Member of the Academy of Special Needs Planners
* Member, National Academy of Elder Law Attorneys Special Needs Law Steering Committee
* Former member , Illinois State Bar Association Section Council on Estate Planning. Probate & Trusts.
* Former member, Illinois State Bar Association Standing Committee on the Mentally Disabled.
* Past President, Rotary Club of Buffalo Grove, Illinois
* Past Assistant District Governor, Rotary District 6440
