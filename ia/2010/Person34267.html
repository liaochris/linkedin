Catrina Catalano’s Summary

Catrina was born in Australia. Raised and educated in both Singapore and Papua New Guinea. On coming to Orange County, California in the late 80’s, she attended college in Business and Culinary Arts whilst working for a large property management company. In the late 90’s, Catrina was diagnosed with Thyroid Cancer and after a successful treatment over the next few years; she completed her college classes obtaining her Real Estate Agent license.

As part of the Prudential California Realty team, she is dedicated to helping you with all of your real estate needs. Catrina was honored for her superior performance in real estate last year. She received the prestigious Leading Edge Society award by Prudential Real Estate Affiliates. This award recognizes the extraordinary achievement of placing her in the top 7% of realtors in the nation. Catrina’s professionalism has not gone unnoticed by her real estate clients, who are constantly impressed by her energy, intelligence and focus.

Catrina is a member of the Thyroid Cancer Survivors Association (Thyca), and has been a participant and Team Captain for the American Cancer Society's Relay For Life in Fullerton, Irvine and Newport Beach for the last 8 years.

Catrina enjoys traveling and has been to five of the seven continents of the world and will discover the two remaining continents in the next few years.

Catrina Catalano’s Specialties:

Catrina specializes in the finest homes and neighborhoods in Orange County and accomodates her clients in Los Angeles, Riverside and San Diego Counties.
