Kyle Vineyard’s Summary

I specialize in providing comprehensive tax consulting and compliance services to closely held businesses, high net worth individuals and multi-generational family groups. In working with clients, I make it a priority to understand their needs, concerns and goals. With this information, I work to provide effective, personalized planning and solutions to help them achieve their goals.

I have significant experience in dealing with complex partnership, limited liability company, corporation, fiduciary, foundation and individual tax returns. I have planned, implemented and monitored complex estate planning strategies including family partnerships, installment sales, grantor retained annuity trusts, charitable vehicles, and low interest rate loan strategies. I work closely with clients and their advisors to fulfill complex income and estate tax needs with an emphasis on minimizing income, gift and estate taxes.
