M.Usman Mirza’s Summary

Entrepreneur, Investor, Consultant since 1987 in diverse business activities: started the first franchise for Snapple, operated a turnaround public company with technology in ultrasonic diagnostic device, assisted in the sale-acquisition of large and small companies, representing a variety of funding sources for ventures and projects worldwide.
Currently President and CEO of Life Products, Inc. (medical diagnostic device in spirometry) and business advisor to various clients.

A broad experience of 15 years with various parts of The Coca-Cola Company in marketing, advertising, bottling operations, new products, pricing and promotions and business development. Held senior positions with turn-around teams at The Coca-Cola Bottling operations in San Antonio and Chicago and with Coca-Cola USA as Home Market Manager.

Part of a start up at Time, Inc in 1969 which grew successfully as a major information provider, competitor to AC Nielsen company.

Marketing and Business Development/Media executive at J.Walter Thompson and Leo Burnett ad agencies in Chicago, NY and overseas for three years.

My personal objective is to join or represent a successful financial group that is serving the pre-VC needs of growing companies, has access to debt-equity funds for pre-mezzanine transactions, and is able to acquire, manage and sell businesses opportunistically. I may also consider interim executive positions or serve as a consultant in marketing, business development and turnaround projects.
