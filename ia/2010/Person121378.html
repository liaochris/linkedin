Larry Stapleton’s Experience

  • Vice President, Worldwide Sales & Marketing

    BSQUARE

    (Public Company; BSQR; Computer Software industry)

    February 2005 — March 2010 (5 years 2 months)

    Responsible for the worldwide revenue figure and top-line growth of this NASDAQ company (BSQR). The role involved Sales and Marketing Leadership and both strategic and tactical management of sales in all geographic regions. Responsible for organizing the sales force and Field engineering organization, setting account and territory coverage responsibilities, determining quotas, sales forecasting, key account management, communications of customer product/service needs and staying involved in day to day sales and marketing activities.

    BSQUARE is an industry leader with a proven track record in providing engineering services and production-ready software products for the smart device market. BSQUARE provides device manufacturers with software solutions for personal navigation devices, point-of-sale terminals, handheld data terminals, smart phones and many other device categories allowing them to get to market more quickly and cost effectively. Customers include China Mobile, Qualcomm, Intel, Hyundai, Ford, Microsoft and Verizon.

  • Vice President, Global Business Development

    Terabeam

    (Privately Held; Telecommunications industry)

    July 1999 — August 2004 (5 years 2 months)

    Mr. Stapleton was Vice President of Global Business Development at Terabeam, where he was responsible for developing telecomm carrier business for broadband wireless access equipment in Asia, managing employees and VAR partnerships in Singapore, Malaysia, Japan, China, Philippines, and South Korea. Originally at Terabeam Larry was VP, Product Development, responsible for developing all of Terabeam's Optical Telecommunications equipment.

  • Vice President, Sales and Marketing

    SelfCHARGE

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    1997 — 1999 (2 years )

    Prior to Terabeam, Mr. Stapleton was Vice President of Sales and Marketing for SelfCHARGE, a contract product design and manufacturing (ODM) company. SelfCHARGE designs and manufactures products for leading companies in the industrial, medical, and consumer products industries.

  • Senior Director, Client Services

    Teague

    (Privately Held; Design industry)

    1992 — 1997 (5 years )

    Founded in 1926 by industrial design icon, Walter Dorwin Teague, Teague is one of the most established and respected product design firms in the world. Teague provides design expertise in various design disciplines including aviation, user interface, consumer electronics, consumer lifestyles as well as research and strategy. Their broad client base includes globally recognized companies such as Boeing, Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, Panasonic, AT&T, Siemens, Samsung, Kodak, and Nike.

    Built on the philosophy that design is a founding principle for change and growth, Teague's aim is to create design that ultimately enriches people's lives and benefits our core culture. For over 80 years, Teague has made a lasting impression on consumers and the design industry alike.

  • Director of Sales and Marketing

    Technology Design, Inc.

    (Computer Software industry)

    1990 — 1992 (2 years )

    Technology Design was an award-winning product development firm apecializing in office equipment, medical devices, and in-flight entertainment. Technology Design was absorbed by Walter Dorwin Teague in 1992.
