Wei Wu’s Summary

Wei has 20 years in the IT industry, and has successfully led many complex Oracle BI implementation projects through the full life cycle. During his 7.5 years services within Siebel and Oracle consulting organization, Wei was the top consultant leading both CRM and BI/Analytics implementations. Wei held the Consulting Technical Manager (equivalent of Siebel Regional Architect) position with the Oracle's Business Intelligence Consulting Practice.

Wei has received many individual recognition. Such as Oracle North Americas Consultant of the Year Award (FY 2006), Oracle Consultant MVP Award for Siebel Analytics/CRM Practice (FY 2006), Siebel Global Services Excellence Award, Siebel Chairman's Circle Award, Siebel Process Improvement Leadership Award, and Siebel High Flyer Award (multiple times), etc.

As Co-Founder at Dyna-BI, Wei and his team led the Ingersoll Rand BI program to win the 2007 Oracle Excellence Award, 2008 CIO 100 Award, 2009 TDWI Best Practices Award in "Radical BI" Category, and 2009 Oracle BI/EPM Innovation Award.

Wei holds the world record for the most (20) Siebel/Oracle certification achieved, as well as 4 other certifications. Wei also served as part of the core team that compiles the 3 Siebel analytics professional certification programs.
