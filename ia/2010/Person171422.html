Chad Smith’s Summary

Chad serves businesses and organizations in using the web effectively.


Chad's clients want effective web tools and a well managed web program. We know that success on the web is defined by strategy, management and measurement. Our clients want to see results from the web and understand clearly how the organization is better off by making the investment.


Chad provides strategy, planning, and well-managed teams of skilled web knowledge workers to organizations of all types.


We serve clients predominantly in the Carolinas, but often remotely. We serve knowledge workers across the US, as well. But, again, we have local tendencies and value face-to-face interaction.


The web is increasingly an interactive tool BY WHICH businesses and organizations operate. The reality is that your solution is an operational one. You need a good team and a direction. You want innovation with metrics, so to speak. Activity should be well managed.

Your team works to define goals and create an effective strategy to use the web wisely to see real return. Then, the team works with you or your internal team to implement the activity to follow.
