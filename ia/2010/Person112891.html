Kay Limb’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic, committed and quick learning individual and crave early responsibility and excitement in my work.

I have over 10 years experience in both agency and client environments and also took the opportunity to further my knowledge of marketing by successfully completing the CIM Postgraduate and ISP diplomas.

My past work has provided me with an in-depth knowledge of most below the line aspects, but I specialise in POS & packaging design, branding, sales promotion and instore communications for FMCG and retail clients.

I am now looking to further my career on the agency side and would really like the opportunity to be part of a medium-sized, fun and dynamic agency, working on a lively and interesting account.
