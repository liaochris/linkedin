Jennifer Fong’s Summary

Jennifer is a passionate thought leader in the fields of social media marketing and cause related marketing, as they specifically relate to direct selling. A former direct sales company CEO, Jennifer knows what it takes to increase sales and recruiting, while building sales force loyalty, through a systematic, thoughtful marketing approach.

Jennifer is gifted at designing social media and cause-related marketing plans based on measurable objectives, as well as authoring and delivering the associated training to support organizational goals. She is a strong communicator, and her on-stage style in engaging as she delivers complex topics in ways that are simple to understand. Her enthusiasm and passion are infectious, and make planning and implementation a pleasure!

She has led key initiatives, including founding and growing several companies, to consulting for Fortune 500 clients. Jennifer is a great choice for your company!
