Steve Scott - Tampa SEO Training Academy’s Summary

Hi! My name is Steve Scott and I am the owner, head trainer and lead consultant of the The Tampa SEO Training Academy, a Licensed Training Associate of the Search Engine Academy.

I offer students access to the finest SEO Training Certification Courses in the internet marketing industry. Courses include a 2-Day SEO Basics, a 3-Day SEO Advanced or my 5-Day SEO Mastery Workshop.

Our workshops are offered every 6 - 8 weeks and enrollment is limited to 10 students so everyone gets plenty of personalized attention. No questions go unanswered.

As a 10 year SEO industry veteran with a history in computer training dating back to 1990, I've trained and consulted with Fortune 1000 companies and have logged 2,000+ hours in a hands-on training environment. My experience with Internet Marketing and SEO dates back to 1997 when I first opened Starship Computer Services.

Tampa SEO is actually owned and operated by Starship Computer Services, Inc of Tampa, Florida. Under the Starship name, I've been crafting customized search engine optimization programs for clients, both large and small, since 1997. Proudly, all of my SEO clients have top rankings in Google, Yahoo, and MSN. I'll personally show you exactly how you can do it yourself.

I began working with computers in my high school days back in the early 1980's. I've seen it all from the days of the first PC's to the rise of Microsoft to the breakneck speed of today's online economy. Over the years I've worked with companies such as General Re, IBM, American Express, Pfizer, The College Board, Revlon, and the United Nations providing training, consulting, design, development, and more. I know from years of experience that only due diligence, patience, and careful planning can assure you consistent top search engine rankings.

Call me toll free at 877-736-7361 to speak to me directly about my SEO Training Courses. You may also email me for more information or register online at http://www.tampa-seo.com.
