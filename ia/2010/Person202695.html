Mark Kuta’s Summary

DO YOU SELL TO C-LEVEL EXECUTIVES? Be sure to check out my book, (thinklikeaceo.com) Think Like a CEO – Sell to Any Company in Any Industry…Better and Faster than a Harvard MBA.

*2008 Axiom Business Book Award, Sales Book - GOLD MEDAL
*2008 Independent Publisher, Business Book- GOLD MEDAL

As one reviewer stated, “Think Like a CEO gives you a roadmap to become that “trusted advisor.”

About Mark Kuta - I've built teams to sell to C Level executives for publicly traded multinational companies, closing over $88 million in technology sales. My teams can speak the executive's language, and have differentiated themselves from all other sales teams. My expertise lies in manufacturing, and technology. For five years I worked closely with John Costanza, Nobel Prize nominee, Fortune Magazine's Hero of American Manufacturing in 2000 and considered the "Father of Flow Manufacturing". I sold and led many Lean deployments at companies and maquiladora plants throughout the world. Some of my clients include GM, Nortel, TRW, GE, Honeywell, StorageTek, Ingersoll Rand, Harris Industries, Trinity Industries, American Standard, Grupo Industrial de Saltillo, Desarollo Termoacusticos, Vitro, Toyota de Mexico, and many others.

I've been a top line driver in development of sales strategy, and new market penetration. I've been successful in both a P&L role and for international market development, and I am interested in moving back into this type of responsibility.

I served on the board of one of the largest financial institutions in Colorado, and speak fluent Spanish and Portuguese, which has helped me in the international responsibilities I've had.
