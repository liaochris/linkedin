Jonathan Sobel’s Summary

I am an experienced trial attorney and litigator, and focus my practice on intellectual property litigation and commercial litigation. After almost 15 years at large firms, including 5 years as a partner at Hogan & Hartson, I recently started my own firm with a partner. We offer the same service as before, but at a more affordable cost.

My intellectual property litigation experience includes the areas of patent, trademark, copyright, trade secret, false advertising, and unfair competition law. I am also experienced in intellectual property licensing.

I have had key roles in numerous patent jury trials and arbitrations. I have litigated patent cases in various jurisdictions, including Delaware, Texas, New Jersey, Virginia, and the International Trade Commission (ITC). Those cases have involved the areas of wireless networking technology, bar code scanning technology, light-emitting diodes, computer software and hardware, on-line business methods, and numerous types of medical devices.

I was selected for inclusion in "New York Super Lawyers" in 2008 and 2009, for Intellectual Property Litigation.

I also litigate and have trial experience with commercial disputes, and often draw upon my finance background in these matters.

I have had formal training in mediation, and am able to mediate both intellectual property and commercial litigation matters.

Jonathan Sobel’s Experience

    (Partnership; Law Practice industry)

    April 2009 — Present (10 months)

    Sobel & Feller is a full-service IP counseling and litigation firm. We have nearly 30 years of combined legal experience resolving IP issues at premier law firms. We also litigate and help resolve commercial disputes. We offer our clients the best of both worlds -- large firm experience, quality, and service, without the corporate overhead. The result is exceptional value work at a lower cost.

    An efficient firm structure allows us to tailor our legal services to meet clients’ needs, expectations, and budgets. While we litigate aggressively and are prepared to take cases through trial, we do not litigate for the sake of litigating.

    Sobel & Feller works with our clients to develop creative business solutions to IP issues and problems. We are mindful of clients’ business needs and goals from the outset and formulate our strategies and counsel based on those needs and goals, whether in litigation, as part of a transaction, or in response to other client needs.
