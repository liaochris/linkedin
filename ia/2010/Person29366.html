Kirstie Wilson’s Summary

British Business Connection is the premier place for British business owners and executives doing business, working and living in the United States to connect, as well as those aiming to immigrate here.

We work proactively to enable our members to do business with each other and to partner on joint ventures that foster business growth; enabling their connections through our online membership directory.

We aim to provide an online business networking environment where our members can ask for information and support, and access beneficial resources through our online forum.

In addition, our members will have access to an expanding knowledge base of information pertinent to British expatriates in the United States, as well as a vendor database of US businesses experienced in working with British expatriates.

We also offer promotion and marketing capability in the form of advertising and sponsorship opportunities.

Lastly, we will be building a network of local Chapters that will meet monthly to enable further connecting and networking opportunities to our members.

If you are interested in membership please visit our website at www.BritishBusinessConnection.com.

For more information please contact us at Rosie@BritishBusinessConnection.com
