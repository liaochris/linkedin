Javier Poveda García-Noblejas’s Summary

During my career as a marketing specialist I have worked as an Account Manager for a communication company (now called Adverbo.biz), a Marketing Manager for an IT company (Buongiorno MyAlert), an IT&Internet Project Manager for many companies integrated in Grupo Planeta, and lately in the insurance market as the Deputy Marketing Director for Asisa, and the Product Development Manager for Caser, in the bankassurance department.
Additionally, my educational background in Marketing and Advertising and the Executive MBA in Instituto de Empresa give me a wide vision of the company needs and makes me capable to take part in any of the links included in the marketing chain.
