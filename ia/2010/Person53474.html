Deb Dobson’s Summary

I work with individual professionals and organizations to help them understand how to use social media tools effectively and efficiently, and how to integrate them into the overall marketing strategic plan to achieve their business goals.

I work one-on-one or with key members of an organization to develop a social media plan, identify the key members of the social media team, train and then measure the ROI of the social media campaign.

With a legal IT/marketing technology background, including social media, and a business consulting background to nonprofit, government and corporate industries, I offer a unique blend of technology and business marketing understanding.
