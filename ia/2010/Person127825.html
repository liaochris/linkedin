Liliana Ospina’s Summary

Previous to my position with Booz Allen, I briefly worked as a contractor for The U.S. Department of Defense at the The National Defense University in the Africa Center for Strategic Studies where I created searchable databases to be used by National Defense University, in particular, the Office of Academic Affairs. I was also afforded the opportunity to attend various seminars on defense strategies in Africa, UN involvement, and U.S. policies towards Africa. It was an extremely insightful learning experience that only furthered my desire to work with an organization that makes a global impact.

I believe that with my international work, study, and travel experiences, combined with my attention detail and ability to multitask, I would be an excellent asset to any international organization.
