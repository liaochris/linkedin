Khürt Williams’s Summary

I am an information security analyst and project coordinator with over seventeen years of computer, software, and information technology experience, with seven years in senior-level technical positions.

I’m a systems thinker, which means I tend to think about how all the pieces and parts of things fit, and work, together. I’m a big fan of patterns — recognising them, observing them, creating them.

I help develop security strategy, architecture, process and policy to manage the security risks associated with employee access to the Internet and serve as an advisor on the Network Security and Secure Awareness Teams.

I manage and/or assist with assigned projects that will range from less than 10 users up to enterprise-wide solutions and serve as an security advisor to other departments and teams as well as lead in the investigation, and evaluation of emerging security-related technologies.

I am a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and ITIL v3 Foundation certified.
