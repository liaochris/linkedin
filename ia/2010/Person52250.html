David O. Volpi, M.D., P.C., F.A.C.S’s Summary

David Volpi, M.D. is one of New York City's foremost snoring specialists and head of the Manhattan Snoring and Sleep Center. As an Ear, Nose and Throat surgeon in his private practive for more than twenty years, he has provided expert care for all ENT disorders and diseases, with a specialty in snoring and sleep apnea treatment.

David O. Volpi, M.D., P.C., F.A.C.S’s Experience

  • Founder, Otolaryngology Surgeon

    Manhattan Snoring and Sleep Center

    (Medical Practice industry)

    2000 — Present (10 years )

    The Manhattan Snoring and Sleep Center is New York City’s leading sleep clinic for the diagnosis and treatment of snoring and sleep apnea. Conveniently located on the Upper West Side of NYC, it is a full-service sleep clinic certified by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine (AASM).

    The sleep study center was founded by snoring specialist David O. Volpi, M.D., P.C., F.A.C.S. who has had a private Ear, Nose and Throat practice in New York City for more than twenty years. As an otolaryngology surgeon, he realized that there is a lack of information on the part of the public about the potential severity of snoring.
    He founded the Manhattan Snoring and Sleep Center to be a total resource for diagnosis of the many physical causes of snoring, and to provide patients with the full range of treatment available today, whether it’s non-surgical or surgical.
