About Marie-Chantale Turgeon:
Artist and founder of meïdia, she’s also well known as an active blogger and podcaster. An extremely creative person, ideas seem to get out of her head at a speed anyone would find hard to follow. She is known to be a trend observer in the development of the ever expanding medium that is the internet, not without her artistic point of view, which makes her a part of this new indie subculture.

Art director, artist, illustrator and explorer of the world, Marie-Chantale Turgeon's innovative, thought provoking work, has been featured in Elle, Spin Magazine (US), ISh Magazine, Taxi Magazine, Goodie Bag Media (Asia), The Gazette, La Presse, Urbania (Canada), Le Monde (France), Skirt Magazine (US), and the Huffington Post (US).

About Meïdia Studio:
We strongly believe that helping people communicate with each other online is more important than trying to sell them something. Our goal as individuals is to contribute to the development and evolution of the internet, as this medium is in our opinion, one of the most exciting communication tool of our era.

Clients: La courte échelle (Epizzod.com), National Film Board of Canada (NFB) for CitizenShift and Parole Citoyenne's communities, Le Cirque du Soleil, Ubisoft, Radio Canada, Musique Plus's VoxPop, Culture Québec, Tellabs, Article Studio, P45, Maison d’édition de La courte échelle, Astral Média Interactif, Passeport Santé.

Marie-Chantale Turgeon’s Specialties:

Illustration, calligraphie et art pour les marchés de la mode, de la musique, de l'édition et de la publicité.

Illustration and art for fashion, music, editorial, advertising, and publishing markets.

Tel que vu dans / As seen in: Skirt Magazine, The Huffingtonpost, Ish Magazine, 1000 Artist Journal Pages, Cr@ate Online, Taxi Magazine, Goodie Bag Media, and the Fox River paper Agenda.
