Lea McLeod, M.A.’s Summary

Thrive in the transition to professional life!

Degrees of Transition LLC prepares college graduates to successfully move from academic to professional life.

According to a study published by the Conference Board in August 2009, employers are finding new entrants to the workforce inadequately prepared for the demands of the professional workplace.

One important factor is that the skills, competencies, and behaviors most highly regarded - and rewarded - in academic life are not the same as those employers value most.

A survey of recent graduates also revealed that although they were emphatic about getting a job, and clear about the need for a resume, they did not understand that they needed to change their behavior, demeanor or attitudes, or cultivate new skills, in order to succeed in the workplace.

Degrees of Transition LLC teaches young adults to thrive in the workplace, and adapt to the transition from student to professional. This means developing new skills, acquiring new confidence, practicing new poise. We focus on what to take forward and what to leave behind, in order to create a new, adult persona.

Our mission is to prepare young adults to engage in professional work with confidence and competence, thereby providing a competitive advantage to them, and their employers. We are passionate about creating the opportunity for young adults to be prepared, self-assured and intentional as they embark on the professional journey!
