Vikki Chowney’s Summary

Vikki Chowney is currently heading up Reputation Online (the latest addition to the new media age family at Centaur Media), contributing to The Really Mobile Project to indulge her inner mobile geek, as well as trying to make of things by writing about them on her own blog.

Recent projects include working with Talk Talk, Big Yellow Self Storage and WCAFI (World Clean Air Forest Initiative), as well as Contagious Magazine.

Vikki Chowney’s Specialties:

Unique insight into how the blogosphere/media space works from both points of view; as a PR and as a writer. Communicatons experience across the board. Specialisation in the digital and social media space, with projects relating to Mobile and 'Green' issues, as well as how technology is used for Social Change.
