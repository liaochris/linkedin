Logistics); Handled different retail engineering and optimization projects like allocation systems; auto replenishment; transfer optimization algorithms; price, mark-up and mark-down optimization tools and store space management instruments; Expert in BI tools & Business Analytics.

Now, directing department of retail (merchandise) planning & giving consultancy on retail systems.
