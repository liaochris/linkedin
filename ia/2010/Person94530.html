About Jenna Gruhala Oltersdorf
Jenna has managed and executed programs for sole proprietorships and multi-billion dollar companies. Her agency experience ranges from a small firm in St. Louis to the largest independently owned PR firm in the world. She has expertise, contacts and strategy to help your business grow.

Jenna's client experience includes Chinet, Feline Pine, NASCAR, Hardee’s, North Carolina Division of Tourism, Unico System, Georgia-Pacific, St. Louis Effort for AIDS, GMAC Real Estate, Agility Recovery Solutions, Heatcraft Refrigeration Products and Levi’s Signature Series, to name a few. She's a member of the Public Relations Society of America and sits on the board for HeartGift.

Recognized for excellence in public relations, Jenna has received a number of industry awards including Communicators, Summits, Bronze Anvils, Silver Anvils, Platinum PR Awards, Tellys, U.S. International Film Festival Award and a nomination for an Emmy … but it’s not all about a fancy piece of stone and glass, is it?

When she’s not making PR magic for her clients, Jenna enjoys drag racing Corvettes, baking cupcakes and painting. You can find her playing Halo 3 under the Xbox Live gamer tag xxxJENNACIDExxx and @JennaSnacks on Twitter.
