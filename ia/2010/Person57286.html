Dmitry Kozko’s Summary

Dmitry Kozko believes in the American Dream. Since coming to America from Russia at the age of 11 he has proven that combining vision and hard work can lead to success.  After spending several years running marketing and sales programs for consumer companies, this enthusiastic and energetic entrepreneur founded Openfilm, a company that is helping independent filmmakers embrace new distribution channels on the Internet.

A "hands-on" executive, Dmitry is involved in all aspects of Openfilm's strategy.  His first priority is building a robust online community by offering filmmakers and other users features and programs that enhance their Openfilm experience. He closely manages marketing and sales programs, working with partners to ensure their goals are exceeded. He directs the company's business development strategy and is involved with capital raising initiatives.

Dmitry starts his workday before the sun comes up so he can communicate with Openfilm colleagues and filmmakers located around the world.  He maintains a watchful eye over every detail of Openfilm’s website from its massive hosting server to the implementation of video streams. He also oversees the development of technology products that will improve operational efficiencies for filmmakers and  film festivals. He likes to end his day watching the new videos that filmmakers have uploaded to Openfilm. 

Dmitry Kozko’s Specialties:

His skill-set combines creative marketing aptitude, sales know-how and technical expertise. He has launched campaigns for companies and clients in the online entertainment, real estate and consumer goods space. A certified business instructor, he leads seminars that teach executives how to increase sales and acquire new customers. Dmitry’s drive, determination and relentless work ethic are assets to his company, as well as their advertisers and partners. He is fluent in Russian and English.
