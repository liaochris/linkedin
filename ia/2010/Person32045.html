Kevin K.’s Summary

I am the editor of Career College Central, a bi-monthly publication for executives who own or work within career colleges as well as vendors serving career training institutions. I am always looking for story ideas, contributors and bloggers for our magazine and web site, www.CareerCollegeCentral.com.

Kevin K.’s Specialties:

Educating about the career college sector, connecting execs in the higher education field, writing and public relations, telling stories that illustrate the value and mission of career-training oriented colleges.

    Career College Central is the definitive voice of the career college sector of higher education. Both the magazine and its affiliated web site, CareerCollegeCentral.com, provide in-depth coverage of the latest news and events impacting career colleges. Each edition of the magazine features analysis of current trends, interviews with prominent executives, research on topics relevant to college directors, and wisdom from leading experts in all areas of for-profit education.
