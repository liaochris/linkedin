Derek Groniger’s Summary

Enterprise Technology Sales Specialist – career professional with 19 years experience of B2B sales; demonstrated ability to over-achieve revenue objectives possessing exceptional technical abilities.

The keys to my successful sales performance have been to:
1. Listen more, talk less (not being pushy, rude or inconsiderate);
2. Ask the right questions (finding the missed opportunities);
3. Ask for a commitment (early and often);
4. Set the right sales call objectives (plan the call or plan to fail);
5. Differentiate myself (do not make the customer do all the work).
6. Focus on the prospect (not my products);
7. Build rapport, stay in rapport (people need trust to buy);
8. Consistent and respectful (on time, prepared and ready to serve).

Sales is my career not simply a job. I try to live by the credo of Power, Presence and Passion.
Knowledge of my Product, Market, Prospect and Competition gives me Power. Focus on my customer and going the extra mile to find ways I can serve them gives me Presence. Reflecting, taking note and developing a clear vision of where my customer and I should be going is my Passion.

Henry Ford said “You can't build a reputation on what you are going to do”. I love to get results – whether it’s exceeding quotas, providing team leadership or staying ahead of the technology curve. The strong desire to serve my customers with integrity and the pigheaded discipline to be an expert by learning my products has been at the foundation of my repeated success.

I possess highly specialized knowledge and experience of Managed Hosting (Dedicated and Cloud) for complex environments that include network security (load balancing, firewalls & intrusion detection), mass storage (SAN, DAS & NAS) with Linux & Windows operating systems. I have successfully sold ERP systems (Sage MAS 90/200, MAS 500, Microsoft Dynamics GP, Dynamics NAV, SAP B1, Infor Syteline) and CRM systems (SalesLogix, Oracle (Janna through Siebel) and Sage CRM hosted).
