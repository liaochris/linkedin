Bridget Nelson Monroe’s Summary

I am a passionate, diligent marketing and communications professional specializing in all things digital, social media, and content strategy. I started my career as a reporter and fact-checker at the award-winning Reader's Digest magazine. This experience sparked my ability to be both a creative, big-picture thinker and an analytical stickler for details.

I honed my digital marketing skills in the New York office of Digitas Health, a global leader in the field of health care marketing. There, I managed the content on several websites for clients in the pharmaceutical industry.

In 2010, I co-founded a consulting agency called Mediamazing, which helps small businesses use social media and video to engage with customers and enhance brand awareness. I truly enjoy helping individuals discover the power of social media.

I recently relocated to my home state of Minnesota, where I am a Digital Strategist for Beehive PR, a strategic PR boutique.

I believe it's important to donate one's talents and skills outside of work, which is why I've spent a significant amount of time volunteering for a variety of nonprofit organizations. In New York, I was deeply involved in groups such as She's the First, the MacDella Cooper Foundation, and Step Up Women's Network. Now that I'm back in the Twin Cities, I'm excited to find new ways to donate my time and talents in this amazing community.
