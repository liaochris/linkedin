William Parris’s Summary

I’m a sales and marketing professional with over twenty years of marketing and sales experience in the hospitality industry.

Over the years, my work has generated over $14 million in revenue, strengthened revenue flow, improved staff performance, and led to the successful openings of hotels such as: Le Marquis Hotel (now the Hotel Chandler), the Vincci Avalon, and Embassy Suites.

My professional experience across a multitude of director-level positions at major hotels in the Big Apple has allowed me to hone a distinct service offering: ParrisConnection.

ParrisConnection, headquartered in New York City, serves as a “Lifestyle Designer” for savvy travelers. We specialize in providing custom-made travel options created to fit your way of life. We foster relationships with hotels possessing inimitable style, restaurants, personal shoppers and health clubs to offer travelers an upscale, extraordinary experience far beyond the norm.

ParrisConnection is also a sales and marketing collaborator that provides independent hotels with representation, and wide-ranging support for in-house sales and marketing efforts all for less than the cost of an on-staff DOSM. We are business developers with years of experience in strengthening revenue flow during strong and soft periods. We also provide pre-launch sales and marketing strategies and services to ensure stellar hotel openings.

To learn more about ParrisConnection's unique, lifestyle services, email us: info@parrisconnection.com or call (917) 656-2142.
