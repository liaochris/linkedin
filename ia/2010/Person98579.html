Jim McLennan’s Summary

Seasoned real estate finance professional with a passion for the real estate industry. Having started my career in public accounting and rising through the ranks at Kenneth Levental & Company to partner and then continuing as a partner post merger with Ernst & Young, I eventually left public accounting to become more hands on in the industry as a CFO. I have experience in all facets of real estate from land development and home building to all types of commercial real estate and have also worked in the securitization of real estate debt portfolios. I have developed and maintained relationships with both investors and lenders to the industry.
