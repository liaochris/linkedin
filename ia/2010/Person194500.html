Stephen Meade’s Summary

What I have determined in my personal and professional life is my mind never stops working.

As a result, there are few things I have become very good at:

1- Making connections and introductions.

2- Looking at a current product/solution/offering and coming up with a simple, straightforward and unique way to accomplish something more efficiently.

I tend to view myself more as a business architect. The ability to create the vision to know what something should look like at the end. It’s almost like creating the building on paper, but in this case it’s a business, idea or solution.

From there, the connecting the dots and building the teams becomes an art as much as it is a science. Getting the right people in the right places can help ensure success on any project.

This has led me to create businesses and relationships in which pushing up people and making the world a better place are paramount.

Background-

Mr. Meade is the Principal in BigBamboo, LLC., a holding company formed to help bring companies from conceptualization to creation. Serving as Chairman and “Resident Rainmaker”, the company has created other entities such as-AdSnips- unique mobile advertising for cell phone industry, MonetaPro- closed loop transaction processing systems, ComCom Networks- private labeled transaction system for affinity groups and communities, RONAStar- an enterprise solution which empowers large corporation to save money, SpoonMantics- a romantic gift line of Jewelry, Chocolates, and Clothing, EAMS- Homeland Security, disaster response and business continuity, TouchPoints Media, a convergent media and marketing company, AlleyWayProductions- an online calendar contest utilizing social networking, 6Bacon- Social Networking Platform, and several more.

Mr. Meade is a life long entrepreneur, having created 9 successful companies.
