Janet Kapa’s Summary

Prior to opening her bridal boutique in January 2008, Janet spent a decade working as a wedding planner and coordinator catering to elite clientele in the southern Michigan region. Her experience and knowledge of the wedding industry coupled with her desire to create a unique and personalized shopping experience for brides led to the opening of Brooklyn Bridal & Prom, a full service bridal and prom boutique nestled in the Heart of the Irish Hills of Brooklyn, Michigan. Janet has quickly been recognized as an expert in the wedding industry for her professionalism, knowledge of products, resources, and simple desire to exceed the bride’s expectations.

Janet Kapa’s Specialties:

Retailer of: designer wedding gowns, designer prom and pageant gowns, custom veils and headpieces, heirloom bridal jewelry. Additional services include wedding planning and day-of coordination, wedding gown cleaning and preservation, invitations and ordering assistance.
* catering to professionals during their wedding planning
* intimate and charming bridal boutique offering VIP treatment.
