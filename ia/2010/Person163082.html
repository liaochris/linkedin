Peter Rozema’s Summary

Rozema de Vries - HRM Services & Projects is established in the city of Groningen in the north of The Netherlands. We exist since 1994 and have the disposal of a large and wide-spread network. Our relations are small and middle companies, municipal and provincial authorities. We find providing good services as very important to our clients and we like to work closely together with them. Only then we can connect exactly to the needs and whishes of our clients.

Our services are among other things:

Training
We have provide training for many years, like training in:
• Human resource problems.
• Communication.
• Management and leadership.
• Conversation techniques.
• Cooperation skills for teams
• Advising.
• Competences.

Recruitment, selection & detachment
We provide personal for al different sorts of sector (with the exception of ICT), for almost all functions, on every level, in an fast and effective way.

Coaching
We provide active support and accompaniment of personal who like to develop themselves. We coach in a concrete, effective and resulting way. We are graduated Co Active Coaching coaches and authorized and certified for using the CoreQuality method by Daniel Ofman. We offer coaching in English, Dutch and German.

Outplacement
Sometimes a co-worker does not fit into an organization anymore. Outplacement can be a good alternative. When it comes to outplacement we approach our network in an active way.

StartPool
StartPool is an project in which we mediate, detach and accompany students who have just finished their study at college or university and who would like to work for an authority.
