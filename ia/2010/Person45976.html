Dale Emel’s Summary

A successful technology leader defined by my vision, innovation, and passion for technology, as well as being able to deliver cost effective solutions that drive businesses forward.

I engineered 24x7x365 availability and quadrupled performance at several Entrepreneurial Startups by pioneering the use of Cloud based services for Media Streaming, Application Caching, and Content Distribution.

I lowered technology costs at both Fortune 500 and Startup organizations by leveraging un-used capacity, eliminating waste, implementing Software as a Service (SaaS) applications, and streamlining operational processes.

I secured Internets, Intranets, LAMP, .Net and VoIP architectures by hardening Operating Systems, installing Firewalls deploying VPN’s and encrypting network connections.

I orchestrated and established business partnerships and agreements with companies and partners in the United States, South Korea, China, the Middle East, Europe, and India by fortifying relationships and unifying goals.

I spearheaded the delivery of business applications and operational infrastructures by cultivating cultures of collaboration, agile development, and business focused accountability.

I earned a reputation for calm and focused leadership by managing crisis’s and turn around situations with compassion, conviction, clear communication and the empowerment of others.

I developed expertise in building organizations as well as reengineering, multi-sourcing and downsizing in response to changing business conditions.
