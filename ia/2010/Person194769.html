Steve Averill’s Summary

Principal at Averill Media a small business marketing consultancy. Online marker with 10 years experience. Joined startup eGrad in 1999 which grew into College Media Network, the largest online network of student newspapers in the country. Experience working with brands large and small including Merrill Lynch, Radio Disney and Dreamworks.

Work cities include LA, Boston, NYC and London.

Sideline hobby writing custom poems for executives and individuals.

Blogging at OCbizblog, a small business marketing blog that highlights Orange County businesses. We offer insight and information on marketing and small business trends.
