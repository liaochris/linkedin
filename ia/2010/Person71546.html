Frederick Dudek’s Summary

"I attract business for others by elevating their valued relationships into enthusiastic brand advocates that create new customers."

L. Frederick Dudek is an accomplished relationship marketing executive with over 25 years experience achieving breakthrough sales performance results in domestic and global business markets. Prior to founding 2 Maximize, Inc. – a leading sales performance and relationship management consulting firm, Dudek led corporate sales and marketing efforts for major companies in the software industry including Caelix and TekSoft.

As a relationship management expert, Dudek lays out a concrete plan of action for his clients to make things happen to build real business and rewarding referrals. By combining his sales, marketing and consulting expertise, Dudek has helped clients rapidly accelerate their growth curve. Clients of Dudek have realized up to 20% growth in new business and referrals within the first 3-6 months after engaging his services.

Clients benefit from Dudek’s extensive certification trainings in sales performance effectiveness and marketing management including certification training from Wilson Learning, Holden Corporation, American Management Association, Dale Carnegie, Sun Microsystems and Applicon-Schlumberger.

When he’s not helping clients navigate their business relationships, Dudek navigates the globe as a world traveler. He speaks fluent French and understands German, Spanish and Polish enough to ask the locals where the best jogging paths can be found in their respective countries.
