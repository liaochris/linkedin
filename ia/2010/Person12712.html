ANDY MURRAY’s Summary

Andy Murray has 10+ years of experience building strong relationships and presenting winning business solutions to a variety of clients. Andy has a track record of success developing new business opportunities in three very unique industries, including commercial landscaping, professional recruiting, and consumer lending.

Andy's core focus at Moore Landscapes is developing new business opportunities for the company's landscape maintenance and landscape construction divisions. In addition to developing new business for Moore Landscapes, Andy is currently studying and training to earn the ISA Arborist Certification and his Commercial Pesticide Applicators License.

Andy earned his B.S.B.A. in Management from the University of West Florida. His personal interests include spending time with family and friends, playing golf, watching college football, and volunteering with his local Lutheran church youth group in Palatine, IL.
