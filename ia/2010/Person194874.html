Steve Chitwood’s Summary

My expertise lies in building and growing online ventures, managing interactive products, and leading digital strategy and online marketing efforts.

Executive operations – 15 years senior management responsibilities. Set and execute on short and long-term business objectives, manage and build staff and organizational structures, build customer management processes, negotiate contracts, define product and service offering and market focus, evaluate and respond to success metrics across multiple businesses, build shareholder growth, set and manage budgets and financial tracking mechanisms, manage and maintain strategic and agency relationships, evaluate and plan for international expansion.

Multi-sector experience – account experience in almost every sector and size. Directed more than 450 projects over 15 years and managed all phases of the relationship. Sample clients include Developers Diversified Realty, DaimlerChrysler, Occidental Petroleum, LG Mobile Phones, Colorado Department of Natural Resources, TimeWarner Cable, GreatWest Life and Annuity, Chimney Rock Capital Partners, Qwest, Children’s Hospital, Warner Bros., Snapple, Con Agra, ASA Entertainment, The Irvine Company, Nabisco. I am the recipient of several industry awards.

Subject matter expert – direct experience in all phases of digital marketing, ecommerce and advertising strategy development, execution and analysis. 15+ years directing complex web, mobile, email, SEO/SEM and CRM marketing strategies. 3+ years expertise and focus on developing social media strategies for public and private, B2B and B2C client companies. Proven track-record of success in exploiting online and mobile marketing trends to achieve success in audience development, customer relationship management, transaction optimization, marketing analytics, content and message distribution and branding, social media marketing, mobile marketing, and identifying and leveraging emerging trends to develop global audiences.
