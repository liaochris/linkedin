Andy Kromidas’s Summary

Extensive experience as a compliance manager/analyst dealing primarily with the federal regulation of retirement plans. This includes legal, form, operational, and investment requirements under the Internal Revenue Code (IRC) and ERISA affecting qualified (including IRC § 401(k) arrangements) and nonqualified plans, IRC § 403(b) plans, IRC § 457 arrangements, etc. In addition, have strong exposure to other regulatory issues affecting insurance company institutional investment products such as guaranteed investment contracts, separate accounts, etc. Strengths include ability to analyze and effectively communicate complex issues, a self-starter who can operate equally well independently or as part of a team, and a willingness to accept responsibility.

Andy Kromidas’s Specialties:

Retirement plan compliance, e.g., Internal Revenue Code, ERISA and other related laws, etc, including related investment products
