mauro bellocchio’s Summary

As IT Manager of Banca Mediolanum, my role is to provide well-engineered, up-to-date and cost effective networking and security infrastructure. Such an infrastructure must be able to support business services for 1.000.000 remote banking clients and 4.000 desktops/laptops/smartphones.
We focus on deeply understand and describe corporate business processes, keep up with technology innovation in networking/security/pc’s fields, provide budget forecasts, restrain costs trend.
Our IT Department provides demand, planning, engineering, implementation and operations management for medium to large IT infrastructures.

I also act proactively in TI Milan Club– Finance Committee, a series of events involving outstanding CIO’s and IT Managers from Milan area. Main goal of this club consists in establishing and keep growing a collaborative environment where members exchange ideas, experiences, problems and their solutions.
We run these events on a monthly basis, in friendly and unconventional locations, with a final meeting to sum up yearly activities.

Outside of work, I practice running. I love to run along charming South Milan Park paths and roads. These activities help me keeping fit and healthy, both mentally and physically. Sound Mind, Sound Body (Mens sana in corpore sano).
