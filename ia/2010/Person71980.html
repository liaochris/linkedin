MaryJane Mudd’s Summary

Full Tilt Communications is a strategic marketing and public relations business committed to providing its clients with results-driven integrated business communication and development solutions. MaryJane Mudd, Principal, has more than 25 years of experience creating and implementing award-winning communications plans impacting the bottom line of Fortune 500 companies, international law firms and non-profits. A tenacious problem-solver, outstanding writer, speaker and facilitator, MaryJane engages and motivates work teams to set and achieve measurable goals.

MaryJane can be reached at mjm@fulltiltcomm.com.

MaryJane Mudd’s Specialties:

Specialties include: strategic marketing and public affairs consultation, planning and execution; media relations and crisis communications training; copywriting and executive speech writing; and presentation skills training and executive speech coaching.
