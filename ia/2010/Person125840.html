Lisa Batcheller’s Summary

LisaB@StayHomeweb.com

At Stay at Home we “Equip and Serve the Compassionate Entrepreneur” for a rewarding career helping seniors. The cornerstone of our company is “ Compassionate and Dependable Care" . We choose only those candidates who can demonstrate compassion, dependability, and a proven track record of success.

YOUR COACHES…YOUR FAMILY
We know that running your own business is made easier when you can rely on others for help. We have assembled a talented team to equip our franchise owners in all aspects of running your own Stay at Home franchise.
Central Staff – Our folks become your trainers and coaches; professionals who are positioned to help you meet the everyday needs of running your franchise.
Specialists– We have a cadre of specialists who offer a wide variety of expertise in key areas including: accounting; marketing & branding; internet marketing; information technology; CRM software; licensure compliance; and even ergonomics.

OUR DIFFERENCE…WE INVEST IN YOU
We invest in our franchise owners by providing the support we would want and expect ourselves. We understand that the start-up of a new business can be challenging so we will be by your side every step of the way. Whether it is helping you to understand finance options, develop a business plan, apply for a license, or developing your marketing plan, we provide step-by-step help and guidance. Our guidance doesn’t end here, we are committed to supporting our franchise as they grow their businesses.
