Nick Beaumont’s Summary

I am the senior Facilities and Waste consultant with Penman Executive. I am an expert in the search and selection field of mid to senior level professionals within the facilities and Waste industry. I specialise in working with experts from M & E, Maintenance, Waste Management, Cleaning and Security backbackgrounds to source candidates that not only have the necessary background achievements but also the right personality fit for the company.

My main focus at Penman sourcing candidates and vacancies at Director level in Operations, Sales, Account management, Business Development and Genral Management as well as other senior positions within the industry.

I work closely with both candidate and clients to make sure that the recruitment process is run as efficiently as possible so that it can fit in with the day to day workload of both the clients and candidates. I have worked with both major blue chip companies and SME’s alike adapting to the individual needs of the client, whilst helping the candidate maximise their career potential.

I look forward to working with you in the future.
