Andreas Krohn’s Summary

Andreas Krohn is an independent internet professional focusing on Mashups. He has previously worked as the Senior Product Manager at Kapow Technologies where he worked at the cutting edge of Mashups and Web Data Harvesting for 3 years. He has a solid experience in using APIs, web scraping and similar technologies to create web applications that have a higher value for the end users than the sum of the parts. He has a degree in Computer Science that includes studies in Sweden, UK and Canada. Previously to joining Kapow Technologies Andreas has worked as, among other things, a Java developer for several years. Currently he runs his own company based in Sweden.
