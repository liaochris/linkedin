Barry Adams’s Summary

I'm an experienced online marketing specialist based in Northern Ireland. At Visual Script I engage in search engine marketing (SEO & PPC), social media marketing, conversion optimisation, web development projects, email marketing, and consulting.

I write about all aspects of a successful online presence on my blog, the Great Websites Blog. Additionally I'm a contributing writer on State of Search and Searchcowboys, two of Europe's leading search marketing blogs.

Barry Adams’s Specialties:

Web strategy, search engine optimisation, search engine advertising, social media marketing, web analytics, email marketing, conversion optimisation, web usability, intranet, localisation, content management.
