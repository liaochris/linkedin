Emilie Barta’s Summary

It only makes sense that a little girl nicknamed "mighty mouth" would get paid to talk for a living as an adult!

Emilie Barta has been a Host / Spokesperson / Emcee / Moderator / Presenter / Product Demonstrator / Narrator since 1996. She represents exhibitors, associations, and event management companies at trade shows, corporate meetings, and any type of event that requires an energetic professional to create a clear, concise, and consistent message to attract, engage, and interact with their audience.

Emilie is one of the forerunners of the virtual and hybrid event industry and is passionate about developing ways to actively engage the virtual audience.

She can also be seen on TV, on video, and on the internet telling people's stories, promoting a product, or training a company's staff. Whether she is talking to the camera, interviewing a subject, or being interviewed herself, Emilie knows how to connect with her audience through the lens.

And when it comes to voiceover, Emilie has voiced everything from commercial to corporate to cartoons.

Emilie has a BFA from Missouri State University, graduating Summa Cum Laude in the Honor College, and a Certificate in TV and Radio Broadcasting from Broadcast Center.
