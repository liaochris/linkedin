Inflatables And More’s Summary

U.S.A.-MADE INFLATABLE PRODUCTS FOR CHILDREN AND ADULTS

A leading manufacturer and seller of inflatable products for children and adults, including inflatable bouncers, bounce houses, games, jumpers, moonwalks, spacewalks and water slides, Inflatables and More promises you quality, safe and long-lasting products, made right here in the U.S.A.! With excellent customer service and guaranteed customer satisfaction, there is no need to look anywhere else for your inflatable products.

Since 2002 Inflatables and More has offered quality inflatable products at factory direct prices, helping to create lifelong memories with creative and fun designs which bring joy to any special occasion, small or large. We proudly stand behind our guarantee of 100% Customer Satisfaction, with engineered products built with attention to safety and detail.

As a leading manufacturer of commercial grade jumpers of all types, designed for both kids and adults, Inflatables and More is able to pass its savings onto industry distributors. Inflatables and More is one of the few inflatable product companies offering such great custom made value at wholesale prices.

Whether you are a new or experienced distributor in the profitable inflatable product industry, our experienced customer service representatives will support your business and market your ideas! Inflatables and More provides business and product support, full warranty options and individualized customer packages, designed to suit your particular needs and budget. We will support you when you are setting up essential aspects of your rental business, such as insurance, marketing and rental contract negotiation.
