Joe Heller, High Performance Sales Training’s Summary

I am known globally as a sales evangelist who helps sales professionals aggressively win new business with the effective use of sales tools such as lead generation to qualify prospects, market penetration, copywriting, follow-up, referral systems and joint ventures that are designed to rapidly build TRUST in a market and capture new business.

As a Sales Evangelist, I've dedicated my life to helping people sell in hypercompetitive markets. As an entrepreneur, I have co-founded several companies with sales totaling $287 million. I've built a $1.1 billion dollar pipeline of acquisition candidates for an industry consolidation and I have raised over $13 million dollars in venture capital.

I've authored a powerful selling system for professionals that teaches how to rapidly build trust in a market to grow sales. I have spoken to audiences of 4,000 people globally on how to rapidly build trust. My sales articles have appeared on leading publications such as Reuters.
