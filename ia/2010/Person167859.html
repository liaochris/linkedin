Rahul Neel Mani’s Summary

Having completed academics in 1995-96, it's been a relatively short professional journey for me so far. I have spent nearly 15 years in journalism, communications and research - all of these years have been in telecommunications and IT industry.

It’s weird that a post grad and research scholar of International Relations pursued journalism and that too in the areas of telecommunications and information technology. But that’s how life treats you at times.

In profession, after spending nearly five to six years in making a solid foundation, I took up semi-leadership roles in various media houses before stepping in as a full-fledged leader as an Editor. I continue to be in a leadership role today.

Out of the total exposure so far, I've devoted a substantial chunk in writing on enterprise technologies and have built a strong relationship in the CIO (Chief Information Officer) community. Today this exposure has crossed the geographical boundaries. I am connected with a sizable number of IT leaders outside India as well.

Currently I am part of a relatively new venture-funded company known as 9.9 Media which has 360 degree media offerings in IT, Education, Finance and Entrepreneurial communities. As part of the senior management team, I lead the group which caters to the CIO community. My job includes editing a fortnightly magazine, leading a large team of events and also overseeing the online offerings.

I started as a journalist and grew in stature to also do a lot of other things, my passion still remains with writing, editing, conducting events, seminars, session moderation (also for non-company events) and creating niche programmes for the community that I work for.

I also run a couple of groups and communities on Linkedin (with participation from various walks of life). I am on twitter and use the social networks to my advantage.
