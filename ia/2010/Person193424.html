Serrano Star-Twinkle’s Summary

Extensive experience in providing customer service as a priority in all activities within different industries. Verifying that all is organized & running seamlessly striving each time to make a customer’s current experience better than the last.

At The New Yorker I assist in managing all groups amidst a $70 million renovation, configuring & implementing a new PMS system(Opera) as well as Delphi, Delphi Diagrams & MeetingBroker, co-create & implement eBrochure, eProposal & eMenu Sales Tools.

As Group Services Manager I am responsible for the management of group room inventory and all of the many details large and small, around arriving Groups. This includes keeping track of payment deadlines, acquiring & input of room lists in PMS, processing payments, providing bills, preparing Group folders, preparing Group check-in procedures, coordinating with Front Staff on all Group information & closing all group folios. I also maintain the accuracy of the whole Delphi Database including but not limited to training new employees, merge documents, Profit Pack & Delphi Sales Home; verify that the interfaces between Delphi, PMS, eProposal & Meeting Broker are running & troubleshooting when a problem presents itself. Work very closely with Sales Managers in trying to acquire new business by meeting client’s budget needs while maximizing revenue.

Prior to that I was the Assistant to Director of Sales & Marketing where I managed the administrative duties of the DOS&M, helped create reports to be distributed to Management & assisted in gathering information for the marketing plan & budget.

Before joining the hotel I worked at PNC Bank as a Teller. In doing basic teller functions I established & maintained customer satisfaction while ensuring a high level of safety & soundness. Performed managerial functions such as maintaining cash vault, proofing branch totals & closing the branch.
