Patrick Van Renterghem’s Summary

I founded I.T. Works (Belgium) in 1992 to offer vendor-independent information about technical and strategic issues in information and communication technology (ICT) through seminars, newsletters and on-line resource centers.

Today, I.T. Works is the leading organizer of technical and business IT seminars. It is a registered trademark in the Benelux and the strongest brand name in IT seminars in Belgium, known for its information-packed and vendor-independent seminars. We were voted "IT Management Seminar Organizer of the Year" by the readers of Data News.

Our most important deliverables are seminars, workshops and IT updates. Besides that, we produce various newsletters, blogs and press articles. Most of all, we filter information from hundreds of on-line and off-line IT resources, and we listen to end-users, consultants and vendors to know what is happening in ICT today (and what is going to happen in ICT tomorrow). Over the years, I.T. Works has grown a network of excellent speakers and happy customers. This makes it possible for us to deliver seminars that answer the needs and requirements of IT professionals more accurately, more quickly and more effectively than our competitors.

Some of the seminars we organize regularly are:

* Service-Oriented Architectures (SOA), Web Services, Integration, EAI, ...
* Portals, Content Management, Search, ...
* The Future of ICT (our annual ICT forum)
* Designing Data Warehouses
* Business Integration Solutions
* Voice over IP (VoIP)
* Business Process Management & BPMS Solutions
* Identity and Access Management & Security in general
* RFID: Opportunities and Challenges
* Logical and Physical Database Design
* Wireless LANs, Mobile security, ...
* Legal Aspects of ICT (contracts, outsourcing, ...)

We are always looking for new topics, new speakers and new opportunities
