Ryan Wynia’s Summary

Author of www.BeYOB.com, Be Your Own Brand, where my guests and I discuss the art of creating brand alignment and the science of delivering its promise.

Social brand architect. Culture connoisseur. Anthropologist.

I lead social brand innovation at a new social brand consultancy called Firebone in Chicago. I’m a Certified Human Behavior Consultant, a designation that affords me insight into the way an organization’s personalities, strengths and character qualities shape a brand’s ethos and culture. A degree in communication arts with a unique emphasis in communication rhetoric serves as a foundation for my passion to create meaningful brands—helping organizations on-board social technologies and strategic thinking to achieve branding objectives and implement immediate marketing initiatives.
