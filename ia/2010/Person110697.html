Karen Maze’s Summary

You, captured.

Showing your true self to the camera is difficult for most people. Everyone wants to project the "right" image for a given situation. Working together, we will portray your individuality with authenticity. Whether you are looking to project your professional persona or reveal your true nature, we will capture it.

I grew up in Connecticut and relocated to Los Angeles after graduating from high school in 1992. I love to travel and have been lucky enough to do quite a bit of it - spending a semester in Paris as well as visiting Italy, Ireland, Barcelona, Switzerland, Prague, Guatemala, and Germany. Travel is refreshing and inspiring, renewing my eye for photography and expanding my perspective.

Karen Maze’s Specialties:

In addition to my private clients, in the last few years I have worked for publications like Muscle & Fitness, Dog's Life Magazine, and Malibu Beach. As photographer for the Santa Monica Pier's Twilight Dance Series, I have had the rare opportunity to photograph some of today's most intriguing musicians, including Los Lobos, Michelle Shocked, and Spanish Harlem Orchestra.
