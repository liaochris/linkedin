David Fox’s Summary

http://www.ElectricEggplant.com

I've been at the forefront of using computers in entertainment and education, starting with a public access microcomputer center in 1977, then Lucasfilm/LucasArts, and online community at Talk City/LiveWorld. The overarching theme in all I've done is finding ways to use technology to enrich people's personal lives, and to use humor and entertainment to empower change.

Currently, freelance multimedia and Web designer/producer.
