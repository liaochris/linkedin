Yoendry Torres, Psy.D.’s Summary

I am a bilingual and bicultural Cuban American with a strong desire to promote mental and physical health in the Latino community. I am highly interested in couples and family therapy with culturally and socioeconomically diverse people who are having relational difficulties. I take a culturally sensitive, relational, and existential/systemic approach that is tailored to help meet each individual’s needs.

Yoendry Torres, Psy.D.’s Specialties:

Bilingual (English & Spanish) counseling; couples issues; parenting challenges; loss & grief; depression, anxiety, and panic disorders; LGBT; substance abuse; psychological assessment.
