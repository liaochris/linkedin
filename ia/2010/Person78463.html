Grzegorz Albinowski’s Summary

Certified project manager and IT service level lifecycle manager. Specializes in IT services and projects portfolio & organisational change: transition management, change management. Project mgmt experience with companies like GlaxoSmithKline (GSK), ACS, Google, British Petroleum (BP), Ernst&Young, Capgemini, RWE, EDS, PZU, Warta, BGŻ & BZWBK. Member of professional associations: PMI, ISACA & itSMF.

Objectives:
■ Challenging and professionally rewarding managerial position in a business unit focused on IT projects management and service delivery. Contribute accrued expertise to control and improve corporate portfolio of IT projects and services to deliver promised business benefits. Assess strengths, weaknesses, threats and opportunities (SWOT analysis) of proposed solutions. Manage risks and impact on the organisation.
■ IT Project Manager. Execute, monitor and control full lifecycle of concurrent projects from planning to benefits delivery. Implement best practice and standards PRINCE2 and PMI's PMBOK Guide. Use clear communication, reporting and recommendations for all involved stakeholders, directors and board members. Increase effectiveness of IT investments in alignment with corporate strategy to improve company’s competitive position.
■ IT Service Level Manager. Implement & monitor Service Level Agreements (SLAs) based on ITIL & COBIT, using adopt&adapt methodology. Improve quality and efficiency of the IT services by introducing Service Desk and Incident Management for the benefit of business users. Improve internal IT processes by introducing Asset, Configuration, Change and Release Management.
■ IT Governance Auditor. Audit strengths and weaknesses of the existing IT organisation using international frameworks like COBIT, ITIL, ITAF, ISO20000, ISO27001 security and ISO 9001 quality standards. Recommend improvements to achieve quick wins and long-term efficiency benefits. Introduce IT processes, procedures, roles and responsibilities (RACI charts).
