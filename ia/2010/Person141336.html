Meryem Ersoz’s Summary

Owner, Black Wing Digital
A Mind-Bending Media Company
We will be producing 3 feature films over the next 5-7 years. Our first feature, Mind's Eye, is a psychological thriller and is slated for production in May-June 2011.

Owner, Red Pine Studios
A few of our commercial studio highlights include: shooting a documentary in Bhutan, our first national TV broadcast of the Boulder Peak Triathlon, shooting a feature film project in France. Our work has appeared on Lifetime, Versus, CET, dish network, Comcast on Demand, and CNN.

Contest Coordinator, UWOL Challenge
Forum Moderator, DVinfo.net
Now in its fourth year, my labor of love has attracted players from all over the globe--Norway, Sweden, South Africa, Taiwan, Australia, UK, Canada, and many other countries who have shot, edited, and delivered over 400 nature and outdoors short films and built a supportive global community of players at DVinfo.net who are dedicated to improving their craft. Most recently, one of our regular players was named a finalist for the prestigious BBC Newcomer of the Year Award.

Instructor, Boulder Digital Arts
I have taught courses in RED Digital Cinema production and post-production. I also taught a series of Outdoor Filmmaking courses through a collaborative agreement between REI and BDA.
