Todd Tullis’s Summary

For the first ten years of my career, I gained experience in diagnosing and solving problems in a diverse set of clinical development businesses: biotechs, large pharmaceuticals, CROs, medical devices, and even an academic research institution. I continue to be fascinated with the operational, technological, and strategic aspects of the industry; I feel my skills and experience can have a significantly positive impact. And that ultimately is what drives me - I am an "impact junkie" - I want to make a difference everywhere I go, because without that feeling, work feels a lot more like "work."

One of my other big passions is budget travel, and after many trips to places far from home, I've set out to address part of the trip planning problem faced by the planet's independent travelers. I've been through one iteration of development & feedback with positive results, so I continue to push forward with Passport Junkie v2.0.
