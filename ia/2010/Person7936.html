Allen Bourne’s Summary

Senior executive with corporate marketing and advertising agency experience in a broad range of industries, most notably public and private real estate investment securities (REITs and real estate limited partnerships) and wireless services. Practiced in developing and implementing brand-building research, positioning, advertising, marketing communications, distribution, customer support, media relations and other programs. Veteran of entrepreneurial and start-up ventures, both large and small.

Industry experience also includes retail banking, consumer electronics and retailing, transportation, hospitality, publishing, HVAC manufacturing, and not-for-profit health and fitness.

Hands-on experience with product development and management, pricing, marketing communications, distribution and channel development, and customer support services/telemarketing.
