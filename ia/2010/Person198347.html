Suzette Sommer’s Summary

We work with US manufacturers who need great packaging at reasonable cost, and manufacture a wide variety of consumer products in China.

We specialize in working with inventors and designers, often assisting start up businesses.

Global Resource US is 100% minority owned, an award-winning member of the Minority Business Council and a preferred Macy's and REI vendor. Excellent client references are available.

Our core focus is food and confectionary packaging, but we also work with inventors and designers to manufacture their products, package them & import them at very competitive pricing.

Clients are often surprised by how much we can save them on manufacturing, packaging and litho lam print projects. We are happy to offer comparative bids on your current projects. Contact me!
