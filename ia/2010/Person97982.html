Jill Hamer’s Summary

IN GENERAL:
Taylor Solutions, LLC is a short sale negotiation and investment company that negotiates short sales with mortgage lenders on behalf of property owners. By using the Short Sale System for Stopping Foreclosure, we manage the entire short sale process from beginning to end including finding a buyer.

Taylor Solutions works with homeowners, investors and real estate Brokers.

Short Sale System for Stopping Foreclosure includes:
1.Collecting all documentation from the homeowner.
2.Submitting all documentation to the lender.
3.Negotiating with the lender.
4. Obtaining a written short sale price approval price.
5.Finding a qualified buyer to purchase the property using our extensive list of cash buyers.

We can negotiate for a short sale anytime before the foreclosure sale date.

MORE SPECIFICALLY:

Short Sales for Brokers
For Real Estate Brokers who do not want to negotiate their clients' short sales, and want to have a professional company handle the negotiation.

Real Estate Brokers choose us because they receive the same commission as they would if they did the negotiation, they have more time to market for clients, and they never have to speak with lenders.

Short Sales for Investors
For Investors who have identified a short sale property and want a professional company to handle the negotiation. Taylor Solutions also provides investors with properties to purchase.

Short Sales for Homeowners
For homeowners who want to avoid seeing their home foreclosed and wish to do a short sale instead. Homeowners choose short sales because it is less damaging to their credit then a foreclosure, it enables them to be eligible for an FHA insured home loan in just two years, and the homeowner does not have to have any contact with their lender.
