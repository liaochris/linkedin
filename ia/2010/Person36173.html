Charlie Davenport’s Summary

Over twenty five (25) years professional recruiting experience, with a "too brief" 3 year interruption by a dream job in my hobby.

Currently serve as Senior Recruiter for Dampier Recruiting Associates (www.dampierHR.com), a boutique recruiting firm established in 1999 in Brentwood (Nashville area) TN. Led by Al Dampier, PharmD, our focus is on medical affairs, mid-senior level marketing and managed care executives in the Pharmaceutical/Biotech industry. Our retained search model offers a recruitment process outsourcing (RPO) model that is highly effective in identifying candidates nationwide. Notable is the fact that, since 1999 over 38,000 contacts in the Pharmaceutical/Biotech industry have been established, recorded and serve as an ever growing database of "who we know" in the industry. In recruiting, where contacts are king, we have the contacts.

Just a note about my dream position: I served from 2006 - 2009 as the Director of Membership for the 28,000 member, 820 chapter international Barbershop Harmony Society. Joining in 1979, I had not only loved the singing, but enjoyed the leadership opportunities afforded by this International organization. Unfortunately for me, the economy of 2009 forced the restructuring of Society Headquarters causing the elimination of three directorship positions, including mine.
