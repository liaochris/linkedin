Bhavesh Bhagat, CGEIT, CISM (LION)’s Summary

Bhavesh is Co-Founder of EnCrisp, an INC 5000 privately held global leader in enterprise solution management for Governance Risk and Compliance (GRC) solutions. He blends international experiences as an entrepreneur, software, audit risk management and financial executive. He brings strategic execution and insight as a seasoned technology executive, trusted management consultant, professional international speaker and published author. A globally recognized visionary on GRC subjects and their impact on complex IT landscapes such as SAP.

He is a frequent speaker internationally at industry leading forums such as Sapphire, ISACA, IIA, SAP GRC and Financials. He served appointments as a member of the Board of Directors for the ISACA National Capital Area Chapter and the Board of Advisors to The Commonwealth of Virginia JCOTS. Bhavesh has been recognized as a TiE-Smith Fellow in Entrepreneurship for his GRC ventures. He is a member of the Northern Virginia Technology Council, The Indian CEO Council (TiE-DC), and a visiting adjunct faculty at American University. Through his keen understanding of both corporate and IT goals, he is instrumental in delivering world class GRC solutions to companies such as Constellation Energy, Deutsche Telecom, Waters, T-Mobile, Coke, ISACA, Commonwealth of Virginia, etc.

He also serves as the Chairman and Founder of the angel investment firm Arneva guiding early stage ventures interested in tapping into the global GRC space and also invests in other diversified investment strategies - encompassing corporate, environmental, green technology and social governance. He is involved as a founding board member of the GRCXchange, the world’s only peer-to-peer network and research platform for connecting GRC professionals globally in all industry verticals through Arneva. As leaders in the GRC field, He and his firm EnCrisp have been featured in INC, IndUS Business Journal, and in ISACA publications.
