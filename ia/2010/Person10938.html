Andrea (Wright) Crisp’s Summary

My husband died from the complications of Lupus at the age of 36. His myriad of specialized doctors didn't know what to do. Each specialist treated his particular organ as if it was a closed system; completely unrelated to the other symptoms or issues. They never treated him as a whole human being. There was no focus on healing. The medications used to try and mask the symptoms became part of the problem. If the Lupus didn’t kill him the pills surely would. His experience with western allopathic medicine and my own personal struggles with anxiety, fatigue and reoccurring urinary track infections lead me down the road to health counseling and healing.
Through health and life-style coaching and incorporating Laughter Yoga, my intention is to guide my clients on their life long journey to balance and wellness so they can experience a joy-filled life without limitations. I serve as a personal advocate for living an energized, passionate and joyful life.
I believe a true holistic approach to life incorporates things that nourish other than food, including honest and open relationships, a meaningful spiritual practice, a career that inspires, and enjoyable physical activity. I work to help clients find balance in both these areas and the foods eaten on a daily basis. Whatever the concern, my passion is to help find which food and lifestyle choices work best for the life that is desired.
I often work with people suffering from stress related conditions. I also specialize in working with people in the midst of crisis following a great loss (parent, spouse, child, friend, job, chronic illness diagnosis). I help my clients find the strength to live their best life by focusing on a healthy diet, exercise, extreme self-care and finding joy in everyday living. It is my belief that by taking into account the body-mind as a whole and addressing the role of diet, stress and emotions the body can often times heal itself.

Andrea (Wright) Crisp’s Specialties:

Individuals & groups - anxiety, auto-immune diseases, balance, self-care, health benefits of Laughter, abundance, avoiding hereditary conditions, and developing healthy life long habits.
Workshops - consult to develop material to address issues confronting the organization. one time or a series, in person or via tele-conference.
I look forward to working with companies looking for unique holistic ways to improve employee health and wellness and decrease health insurance costs long term.
