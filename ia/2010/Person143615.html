Michael Swendrowski’s Summary

Michael is a "specialty vehicle" industry consultant with vast experience in all aspects of specialized vehicle design, construction and operations. With over 20 years experience on the manufacturing side, he now presides over Specialty Vehicle Services LLC. ("SVS") - an independent consultation and design firm committed to assisting clients make their mobile concepts a successful reality.

After many years of observing clients struggle for solid, independent information and resources pertaining to this very unique industry, SVS was founded with a single mission – to help clients Decide Wisely! The expertise that Michael and his associates bring, along with the unilateral information and contacts gained along the way, provide clients the information and services needed to make wise decisions regarding the course of their initiatives.

To date, Michael has tremendous experience with government, corporate and private clients worldwide, including high profile brands including: Starbucks Coffee, Nestle CoffeeMate, Nestle Wonka, Kraft Foods, PepsiCo, Motorola, Bertolli Foods, Digiorno Pizza, and many more.

In 2007, Michael forged a "Preferred Mobile Partner" agreement with GE Healthcare Lunar to design a new generation mobile DEXA health unit, and agreed to oversee production and distribution of the units in partnership with GE sales forces.

With a love for the services and people that provide them, Michael has special interest in the bookmobile community and works to assist those within this tight network, as well as increase overall public awareness of the valuable services they provide.

Michael Swendrowski’s Specialties:

In addition to general knowledge, Michael is considered an expert in mobile hybrid and alternative power systems, green construction technologies/techniques, and vehicle maintenance.
