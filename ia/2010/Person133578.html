Mark D. Redman’s Summary

An accomplished, design and standards oriented Electronic Systems Engineer with broad and in-depth expertise in the research, development and engineering of communications, control and security systems and components. Combining technological and communication skills with a passion for design excellence and for the creative potential of candid, collaborative team efforts, brings strong analytical and management skills coupled with a practical systems approach to the design process. The result is characterized by focused, complete, innovative and cost-effective engineering solutions to real world, root cause problems which address requirements and performance verification and validation through the entire system development and lifecycle. A diverse background encompassing design methodologies, network systems, lightwaves, signal processing, image processing, digital security, circuit development and satellite applications offers a unique perspective which is open to new and varied application.

I am ultimately seeking a either an individual contributor or a management position in a challenging environment which encourages collaboration and a shared long term vision. Alternatively, I am open to contract engineering opportunities for immediate and highly focused development efforts and startup activities.
