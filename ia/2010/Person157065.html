Ola Ayeni, DVM’s Summary

Ola Ayeni, DVM
Chief Idea Officer, Dining Dialog

What I do: I help companies develop and implement marketing strategies that increase their revenue.

Dining Dialog is a full service restaurant marketing company that uses new media tools to drive foot traffic. Tools that we can track and measure results.

We provide social media, mobile , email marketing , Wifi hotspot solutions to increase foot traffic.
A leader in integrated mobile marketing and social media marketing solutions for restaurants.We help restaurants, franchising organizations and food companies identify how to add mobile and social media marketing to their existing marketing channels without any extra work but increase response rate and results.We are a Sysco icare partner.

What we provide: We provide full services that help drive repeat business and foot traffic. Our services include

1. Restaurant Mobile Marketing;

A) Text messaging coupons,specials & reminders

B) Mobile websites for restaurants - Your present website is rendered for cell phones.

C) Iphone apps, android and blackberry websites and mobile food ordering services.

2. Social Media Marketing:

A) Crafting, design and development of a social media presence on popular websites like Facebook, Twitter and Foursquare

B) Coaching your employees or managing your social media presence online while you focus on your business

3. Integrated Marketing and New Customer Acquisition Strategy Development, Training and Coaching

4. Online Review Monitoring and Reputation Management: We monitor, manage and resolve conflict of your brand.

5. Hotspot and WiFi : We provide free Wifi and customer data collection tools that help you reach out to your customers and diners. A reason for people to keep coming back.

Clients and partners include Great Clips,Harrolds Chicken, Navister,Walter Payton Roundhouse,Nabos,Fireplace Inn, Heroes West & Grill,Justins,Village Tavern and Grill,Top Gun Dogs,Salinas Pizza.
