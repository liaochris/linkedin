Mary Maloney’s Summary

Ms. Maloney is an established brand equity and marketing executive with a track record of successfully re-positioning companies for rapid growth, and developing innovative go-to-market strategies and lead production models for emerging organizations. She has over 19 years of experience across multiple industries with expertise in building global brands, demand generation, marketing metrics and website optimization. Most recently, Ms. Maloney rebranded Persystent Software, positioning the company to expand down market. She also spearheaded the launch of the Numara Software brand after Intuit divested the business unit to a private equity firm in a $200 million buyout. She has consistently served as part of the leadership teams that record record sales growth year over year. Her background also includes serving as Managing Partner for a brand identity and marketing firm in the Northeast USA which acquired the marketing communications healthcare specialty boutique she had founded.

Building brand equity/valuation; developing strategic marketing plans & innovative demand generation programs; creating marketing metrics systems/ROI; implementing effective SEO/SEM/SMM programs; overhauling web presence/instituting conversion engines; managing media & analyst relations; viral marketing, organizational development; executive leadership.
