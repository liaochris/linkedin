Marcia Katz’s Summary

Innovator: developed Health Care PR incorporating consumer tools/insights into Rx, biotech & medical device industries. Works at highest levels, counsels on corp.reputation, branding,positioning, CSR, employee relations, marcom, issues (IP, recalls, environment, reimbursement, etc).Multi-disciplinary, integrated approach influential in shifting gov't policies. Judgment honed by experience across full spectrum of medicine & diverse stakeholders. Instrumental in bringing profit & nonprofit together to collaborate on isssues/diseases.

Industry firsts by developing ingenious solutions to complex situations:First VNR, global satellite press conference, major disease awareness program (Breast Cancer Awareness Month), leading crisis model & relaunch (J&J Tylenol), pharma-sponsored PBS TV series, CME physician/patient comm, medical journalist education, corp.-sponsored NIH programs. Innovations stem from consumer roots: first CSR event at Statue of Liberty, special events/celebrity programs for wines & spirits, spokesperson/educational liaison for 5-times Emmy winning TV series, first campaign targeting women investors for major financial company.

Experience in agency and corporate worlds. Founded/led world’s first/largest healthcare PR at B-M. Established Rx PR at C&W. Rebuilt healthcare PR at H&K. Co-founded/President InterScience Comm. world’s largest independent healthcare PR agency. Director Corp.Comm. #1 global fluid management/filtration company (industrial & life sciences).Successfully integrated comm. across C-suite, marketing & web building reputation externally & internally. Key spokesperson, led global media relations, issues management, IR (annual reports, presentations, speeches).Today counsels broad range of companies.Developed blueprint for leading cancer biotech acquisition on reputation preservation & employee engagement. Provides counsel to several PR agencies heads. Serves as adjunct professor, NYU M.S. program in PR & Corp.Comm.
