Levan Iordanishvili’s Summary

www.vel9studios.com

Welcome to my LinkedIn profile. I am a Los Angeles-based composer specializing in providing high quality music for video games. I started Vel9 Studios in May 2007 with the purpose of providing a reliable, professional and accessible original music service for game developers.

In the three years of Vel9 Studios’ existence, I’ve had the great opportunity of working with many creative and independent game developers on over fifty projects, with sizes of projects ranging from short menu themes to full soundtracks.

My strong musical background and a well-equipped in-house studio allows me to successfully provide a professional and competitive service that I'm proud of.
