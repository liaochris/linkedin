Anne Madison’s Summary

Results-driven marketing and corporate communications executive with 25 years of experience at national and international organizations. Successes include launching major re-branding efforts, garnering prominent media placements resulting in millions of dollars of advertising equivalency, creating compelling public relations campaigns to support the launch of new products, and leveraging emerging media and social networking strategies to build strong brand awareness.
