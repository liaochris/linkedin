Kevin Hedge’s Summary

Current focus is Green Business Solutions, including earth friendly printer solutions, green computing, energy savings and more.

Also able to provide on the ground solutions for land use and development related to Wetland Science, Natural Resource Analysis, Forest Conservation Act reports, Clean Water Act Section 404 permitting and mitigation plans.

Water quality solutions include Biohaven Floating Island wetland treatement systems and construction site inspection monitoring tools (web based).

I am very interested in green building techniques and technologies along with development and utilization of alternative energy sources such as wind, solar and geothermal.
