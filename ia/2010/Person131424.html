Marcia Golden’s Summary

At DJD/Golden, what you see is what you get. A right-sized marketing, public relations, branding, telemarketing, and advertising machine committed to ensuring each client generates the right tools to communicate its messages to the right targets, efficiently and effectively – on time and on budget.

DJD/Golden is seven hard-charging, experienced marketing professionals who are at the top of their game and who love “moving the needle forward” for their clients in their markets.

DJD/Golden’s flat agency structure and bottom line orientation means that every DJD/Golden professional is totally responsible and responsive to the client and networked to each other as a cohesive team. There are no interns, no junior creatives, no middle management. DJD/Golden is all muscle, no fat.

Although every practice is available to clients as a standalone resource, the opportunity DJD/Golden offers to clients to cost-effectively integrate…

- public relations - marketing - advertising - social media marketing - collateral - design - telemarketing - websites - business plans - marketing plans - Marketing Department In A Box - direct mail outreach - branding, etc.

…to ensure you work with a fully-informed team to identify the right strategy to fit each initiative.

The result? Your organization receives a carefully researched and designed program that generates the right tools for your marketing arsenals and also meshes seamlessly with whatever you or your marketing department already has in place.

The firm works both with clients' in-house marketing departments and - where no in-house marketing function exists, as a client's outsourced "marketing-department-in-a-box." Clients include a roster of local, national and international clients drawn from a host of industries including professional services (accounting, law firms, real estate, architects, etc), financial services, banks, technology, manufacturing, non-profit and academia among others.

Marcia Golden’s Specialties:

DJD/Golden is in the business of helping clients grow rich and famous through those inimitable business and practice development tools of: Public Relations, Marketing, Advertising, Collateral, Design, Telemarketing, Websites, Business Plans, Marketing Plans, The Marketing-Department-In-A-Box, Direct Mail & Dimensional Direct Mail, Branding, etc.
