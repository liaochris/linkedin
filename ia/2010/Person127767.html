Lorraine DeSimone’s Experience

  • President

    DeSimone & Associates / Premier Properties

    (Construction industry)

    1998 — Present (12 years )

    Strategically guided the launch of this firm, which specializes in providing expert consultancy and marketing, sales, and operations leadership to residential construction firms. Sold a total of $265 million worth of homes for a diverse client base.

    KEY ACHIEVEMENTS:

    * Drove post-9/11 sales for TriMark, a high-end semi-custom builder in Scottsdale, AZ, that previously recorded zero sales for 7 months prior to takeover, which consisted of both aging inventory and more than 82 unsold lots.
    * Captured $19 million in sales in year 1, generating $78 million in total revenue, by redesigning 4 model parks, crafting a community-specific marketing campaign, and ensuring buyer satisfaction.
    * Based on this success, was presented with an offer from the owner of TriMark Homes to become a partner and acting president for a new home building company, Silverstone Homes.
    * Engaged by the Matthews Group in Surprise, AZ to develop a sales and marketing strategy for this struggling community. The community consisted of 57 lots, including 3 model homes and 8 inventory homes, and had not procured a single sale in the prior 6 months.
    * Closed 9 contracts in under 4 months, and completely sold out in 2 years, to generate $11.2 million in sales revenue.
    * Dynamically merchandised the sales office and model complex, improved visibility of the subdivision, revised the marketing plan, and recruited and trained effective sales specialists.
    * Delivered $130 million in revenue for Highland Builders, managing sales, marketing, and construction for 310 single family homes and condominiums in subdivisions including Desert Springs, Highland Five and Coronado Ranch.
    * Directed sales for Great Western Builders, and generated $25 million in 3 years selling single family homes in the San Michelle, Acacia, and Mountain Vista subdivisions.
    * Conducted custom home sales for builders including Arizona Classic, Classic Design, and JBI, generating $20 million in total revenue.

  • President / Principal

    Silverstone Homes

    (Construction industry)

    2002 — 2008 (6 years )

    Visualized and brought to fruition a home building company that was recognized and respected by both its peers and national competitors alike. Defined the business plan, secured financing, led land acquisition, planned the new communities, and recruited and trained the sales teams.

    KEY ACHIEVEMENTS:

    * Sold out the 66 duplexes in record time. Value-engineered the city-approved product to reach the sales pricing goal of $104.38 per sq. ft., and beat out the competitor’s single family home offering of $102.12 per sq. ft.
    * Doubled projected sales from 4 per month to 8, by conceptualizing a vision for the community. Created tree-lined streets, and changed interior non-structural elements to carve out a unique niche position.
    * Created a level of success such that the competitor hired a flatbed truck with a driver on a megaphone and a 10’x5’ sign, driving through the property each weekend advertising the competitive single family community.
    * Assumed the land and plans for a bankrupt builder in Goodyear, AZ with 8 inventory homes and 67 vacant lots, sold 35 units in 6 months, and sold out in 14 months, by redesigning models and crafting new marketing.

  • Vice President, Sales & Marketing

    Maracay Homes

    (Construction industry)

    1995 — 1998 (3 years )

    Directed a full-scale turnaround for 2-year-old community, to avoid selling it to a national builder at a $2 million loss.

    KEY ACHIEVEMENTS:

    * Generated $49.5 million in 1996, and $44.2 million in 1997. Sales increased from 1 per month to more than 7 in the 1st month alone, and garnered recognition from RL Brown & Associates as the top-selling Westside community.
    * Conceived and executed a 3-dimensional billboard to promote the community and drive traffic. Won the M.A.M.E Award (Major Achievement in Merchandise Excellence) for Best Billboard by the Home Builders Association of Central Arizona.
    * Became a M.A.M.E. Finalist for Sales Manager of the Year, Best Newspaper Ad, and Best Interior Merchandising.
    * Integrally contributed to product development for homes ranging from the $140s to the $400s. Selected and supervised interior designers and merchandising specialists. Conceptualized and executed model home grand openings.
    * Conducted comprehensive market analysis, analyzing buyer demographics, crafting corresponding buyer incentive programs, selecting and supervising approved lenders, and structuring creative financing.
