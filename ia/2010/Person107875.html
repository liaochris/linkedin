Judy Murray’s Summary

I specialize in helping Sellers and Buyers of residential Real Estate. For my Sellers I make the sale very user-friendly. That is one of the reasons my customers continue to seek my representation. I help my Sellers to get the most money for their home in the shortest time possible. I accomplish this by incorporating Web 2.0 strategies in my marketing plan.

I assist Buyers by helping them to locate the best home to fit their needs. My specialty is creative solutions!

Through my company, Access Property Investors, LLC I am able to assist Sellers who need to sell "yesterday". My network of investors are always buying regardless of location or condition. I match Buyers to the best deals and help provide direction on alternative financing.
