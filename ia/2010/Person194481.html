Stephen Hart’s Summary

I see my job as a vocation and my philosophy for that vocation is very simple - I want to help people and companies achieve their potential ~ and to do that I have to create results.

This means that whether as a corporate trainer or coach when I am tasked with presenting material it is my job to see that it gets to the delegates or audience in a format that they can understand, relate to and benefit from.

I delight in getting my delegates or audiences to challenge their views on the world and themselves and to encourage them to think differently.
