  • Aquires Super Rewards

    Adknowledge

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    July 2009 — Present (9 months)

    KANSAS CITY, MO – July 22, 2009 – Adknowledge has acquired KITN Media, owner of Super Rewards, the largest virtual currency monetization platform for online games and social network applications. The acquisition combines the Super Rewards platform with the technology expertise, financial strength, and international presence of Adknowledge to create a full suite of social advertising solutions for developers and advertisers.

    The Super Rewards platform enables developers of social applications and online games to offer virtual currency to consumers who sign up for advertising offers. Consumers redeem the currency for virtual goods or other services in more than 1,000 social games, including five out of the six largest virtual currency applications on Facebook. Super Rewards has experienced rapid growth month-over-month and recently announced its expansion into iPhone applications and Twitter games.

  • Adknowledge - Adds Domain Channel

    Adknowledge

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Kansas City, MO - April 30, 2009 - Adknowledge announced today the launch of Adknowledge Domain, the newest addition to the Adknowledge line-up of publisher and advertiser offerings. Adknowledge Domain, which includes Miva and FindWhat.com, was officially introduced at the T.R.A.F.F.I.C. Domain Conference in Santa Clara, CA.

    Adknowledge's Domain Channel provides a new revenue-generating opportunity for domain owners by matching high-quality domain traffic with advertisers from the Adknowledge Network, which now includes Miva and FindWhat.com.

    The introduction of domains strengthens the company's position in providing online advertising and publisher solutions across multiple channels. In addition to domain, Adknowledge also offers publisher solutions within email, affiliate, search, and social networks.

  • Acquires Miva Media

    Adknowledge

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Kansas City, MO – March 12, 2009 – Adknowledge announced today that it has acquired the Media division of MIVA, Inc.

    MIVA Media, one of the largest independent online performance advertising networks, was founded in 1999 and includes the Espotting and Findwhat.com brands. Another of MIVA, Inc.’s divisions, MIVA Direct, was not affected by the transaction and continues to be owned and operated by MIVA, Inc. Going forward, Adknowledge will retain the MIVA, Findwhat.com, and ESpotting brands.

    The all-cash acquisition reflects Adknowledge’s aggressive strategy to expand its position as the leading multi-channel advertising solution for advertisers.
