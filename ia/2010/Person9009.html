Amanda Quimby’s Summary

Armed with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Design and a keen eye for simplicity, Amanda Quimby strips her work down to the basics. Using anything from pencil to her trusty Mac, she creates bold, streamlined work that appeals to a wide audience. Her experience as a gallery intern, as well as a freelance artist, has made her capable of working under demanding conditions, both on her own and as part of a team. Quimby's sleek, compelling designs pack a lively punch.
