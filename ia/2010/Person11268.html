Andrea Zanetti, MBA CHRP’s Summary

An action-oriented senior Human Resources professional known for applying a holistic perspective to leading the HR function of Canadian subsidiaries of global organizations, I have provided strategic guidance, direction and coaching to the management team regarding the effective management of the company's human resources. With a strong continuous improvement orientation, I excel at linking HR strategies to business objectives, vision and values. My background includes over ten years of experience in all aspects of human resources, including:
• organizational development
• talent management
• coaching of executives and managers

• learning and development
• performance management
• total rewards (compensation, benefits, pension)
• succession planning
• restructuring
• legislative compliance
• HRIS
• payroll
