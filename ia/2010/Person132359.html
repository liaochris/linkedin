  • 360-degree assessment

    Certificate, Refflect , 360-degree leadership assessment , 2008 — 2008

    The 360-degree assessment is a leadership development tool that facilitates feedback processes from all around the leader in question. "360" refers to the 360 degrees in a circle. The feedback comes from subordinates, peers, and managers in the organizational hierarchy, as well as self-assessment, and in some cases external sources such as customers and suppliers or other stakeholders.
    The results from 360-degree feedback are used by the individual receiving the feedback to plan their training and development.

  • Neo Pi-R psychological personality inventory

    Certificate, Dansk Psykologisk Forlag , NEO PI-R personality testing tool , 2008 — 2008

    The NEO PI-R is a psychological personality inventory; a 240-question measure of the Five Factor Model: Extraversion, Agreeableness, Conscientiousness, Neuroticism, and Openness to Experience. The NEO PI-R provides a systematic assessment of emotional, interpersonal, experiential, attitudinal, and motivational styles - a detailed personality description that can be a valuable resource for a variety of professionals. It is a concise measure of the five major domains of personality, and facilitates a comprehensive and detailed assessment of normal adult personality. It has useful applications in counseling, clinical psychology, psychiatry, behavioral medicine and health psychology, coaching, career counseling and organizational psychology.
