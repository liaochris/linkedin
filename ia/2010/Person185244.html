Sean Casey’s Summary

I am a full time Realtor® specializing in residential home sales in New Castle County DE. I work out of the Newark, Delaware office. Whether you are buying, selling or both, I provide guidance through every step of the process to ensure complete satisfaction. As a listing agent, I will provide pricing and marketing plans targeted at getting the home visibility both online and in print media. I will focus on locating prospective buyers for a home like yours. When presented with an offer to purchase, I will assist you in contract negotiations to ensure a successful outcome. As a buyers agent, my services go far beyond house tours. With access to extensive resources, I am able to quickly locate the type of property desired. If you aren't sure what that is, my knowledge of the area can help you define and refine your ideas until we find the perfect home for you. My goal is to exceed the expectations of my clients every time. You have the chance to voice your opinion once the transaction is completed by returning my customer satisfaction survey!

Sean Casey’s Specialties:

I specialize in exclusive seller and buyer representation, investment properties, second homes, and first time buyers.
