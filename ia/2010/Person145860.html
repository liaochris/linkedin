Mike Philips’s Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIn Account. I am running for Congress this fall. I am ready to serve in the House of Representatives where I will to bring leadership, strong management skills, and common sense solutions to the problems that we face in America.

These are tough times for our community. The families and businesses in Montgomery County and Prince George’s County face daily financial challenges. As I travel around our part of the state, I hear the concerns from so many in our community about the Government’s continuing fiscal irresponsibility, runaway spending and mounting job losses. While families and businesses tighten their belts, the federal government continues to push our nation deeper into debt.

The Citizens living in the 8th Congressional District of Maryland are concerned that their voices are not being heard. The current administration’s policies being advocated in Washington regarding fiscal responsibility, the economy, jobs, national defense, health care, taxes, and other issues that make it harder for our economy to grow, create jobs, pay our mortgages, pay our credit card bills, and basic living expenses.

The communities within Maryland’s 8th District, need representation in Congress that will focus on creating new jobs, put an end to home foreclosures and lead us through these harsh economic times. These are just some of the reasons I am committed to bringing my business experience, my Marine Corps leadership and my common sense approaches to solving real problems to Washington. We must change direction. We can’t wait until it is too late to say we have had enough. We have the ability turn things around.

Please visit my campaign website: www.mikephilipsforcongress.com. I have an open mind, practical ideas, and I want to find solutions. Please get involved and join my team; there is an opportunity for everyone to be a part. Every job is important and with just an hour or two of support, we can get our country back on track.

Mike Philips’s Specialties:

Practical Approaches to Complex Problems, Real world Views on Government Spending, Consensus Building
