Bethany Spilde’s Summary

Social Buzz Media works with businesses & service providers who understand the importance of having a web presence & the value of relationships. We help you develop relationships with your potential clients through social media, which will expand your web presence, increase your client base & help you grow your business.

Our mission is to help you understand social media and to build your web presence through a strategy tailored to your business.

"Because You're Buzzworthy."

Social Media Services:
♦ Social Media / Marketing Strategic Planning. Are you dazed and confused by all of the social media buzz and wondering how to utilize such a strategy? We put together a specific social media plan utilizing the right mix of social media tools that are specific to your business, audience & goals.

♦ Social Media / Marketing Set-Up/ Implementation: Based on your social media strategic report, we set-up your profiles on all the recommended sites & implement the necessary social media items/content.

♦ Social Media Maintenance and/or Training: The social media strategy will need to be maintained (updates, fresh content, etc) going forward. We will outline what you will need to do periodically. Or you can hire our team to help with your back-end office on the day-to-day social media activities (posts, updates, tweets).

Why I'm on LinkedIn:
