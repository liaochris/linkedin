Daniel Welch’s Summary

Mr. Welch is a Financial Analyst in Houlihan Lokey’s Dallas office, where he is a member of the Financial Opinions &
Advisory Services practice.

Before joining Houlihan Lokey, Mr. Welch completed an internship in the investment banking division at Sanders Morris Harris Group.

Mr. Welch graduated with a B.B.A. in finance from the Hankamer School of Business at Baylor University. He is registered with FINRA (formerly the NASD) as a General Securities Representative and Registered Investment Banker (Series 7, 63, and 79).
