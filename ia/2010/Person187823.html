Sharon Fisher’s Summary

I'm running as a Democrat for the Idaho House of Representatives, District 21, which covers southern Ada County.

I interned with the Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee for the 2006 legislative session, and I've covered JFAC and the legislature for the website www.newwest.net/StateOfTechnology for the 2007 and 2008 legislative sessions.

I have a Masters of Public Administration with a focus on state and local government, as well as a graduate certificate in Community and Regional Development, from Boise State University. I am also a member of Pi Alpha Alpha, the national MPA honor society. While at Boise State, I was a graduate assistant for the Social Science Research Center, working on projects for the State Commission for Libraries and the Idaho Commission for the Arts.

Before earning my MPA, I spent my professional career as a journalist and industry analyst in the computer industry in Silicon Valley.

Sharon Fisher’s Specialties:

Technology-based economic development, state budgeting, city and urban planning, and rural development.
