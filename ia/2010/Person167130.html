Rachael Pollard,MBA’s Summary

Human Resources Experience:

Currently, my role as the Director of Corporate HR has been to evaluate and enhance existing employment practices across the organization relating to Employer Branding, Recruitment, Onboarding, Leadership Development, Internship Program Development, Performance Management and Succession Planning in a global manufacturing environment.

Previous H/R Director roles were in Retail environments. My responsibility in those roles were to oversee the total HR function(day-to-day operatons) with respect to designated territories. Those roles were comprised of HR Generalist responsibilities; Staffing, Training, Corporate HR Initiatives, Associate Relations, Retention.

At one point, I was a Divisional Learning Manager and charged with LMS implementaion across the Western part of the US store locations.

In summary, I've realized that my professional experience in Human Resources has been progressive and uniquely diverse. I've been fortunate to have met and worked with many of the top leaders in the field of Human Resources and Operations in both Manufacturing and Retail environments. The needs are very different based on the product, service and culture.

The moral of the story is....people are your most valuable assets!
