Shemika L. Moore, MBA’s Summary

Shemika is highly organized, communicates effectively and can multi-task with easy. She currently teaches students ages 16 to 24 how to use the following software, Windows 98, 2000 and XP, Microsoft Office, Word, Excel, Access and PowerPoint. Students also have the opportunity to use a typing skills building program. Shemika also instructs students on Business English, office procedures, filing systems, keyboarding, windows operating system, word processing software, spreadsheet software, internet, 10-Key calculator skills, and record keeping.

She also is a Human Resources/Training Specialist with more than 5 years experience in a variety of roles including Recruiting, Continuing Education and Compliance – both regulatory and ethics. Skilled at working within corporate New Hire processes as well as providing corporate liaison with field personnel. Shemika believes enthusiastically in the mission of giving back to her community and encouraging individuals to engage in life long learning by applying to college or advanced training.
