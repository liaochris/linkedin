Skip Graham’s Summary

TopLinked.com ** [LION] LinkedIn Open Networker ** MyLink500.com 2000+ Direct

I thrive on discovering talent and helping great companies and people get what they want. This is what makes my role as a recruiting expert fun, challenging, and rewarding.

My pre-recruiting background is in technology. I started my career as an entrepreneur and later worked many years in corporate technology, mainly in the software/application development world. That led me to a career in technology consulting with some of the top companies in the world, and then back to entrepreneurship as a company owner. It was there that I shifted gears into recruitment and talent acquisition.

Through the entire journey, what I've learned is that creating opportunity for companies and people, and observing them take hold of it, is what energizes me. So, I found myself recreated as a recruiter, not only because it seems effortless for me, but it goes straight to the core of my passion. I also love to lead and mentor those coming behind me into this rewarding career.

I believe that LinkedIn is an incredibly valuable resource. When used properly, it can serve as a mega-tool for growing a business through networking, sales, and certainly top talent acquisition. My LinkedIn network gives me direct or indirect visibility to over 15 million people around the world. That is amazing!

I am a LION and member of TopLinked as well as many other groups.
