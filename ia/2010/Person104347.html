Jonathan Hung’s Summary

Designer of web and desktop applications, currently specializing in wireframing and design strategy, with end-to-end experience in all aspects of the iterative design cycle. I have designed for non-profit & small business clients and am looking for opportunities with design teams working for established brands.

Enriching brands and products with an empathetic & unique user experience since 2008. Known for quickly absorbing domain knowledge, liaising between management, users and developers, and relentlessly advocating a "get-in-the-users'-shoes" perspective. Through working with teams of various sizes, I crystallize design problems, explore concepts and solutions, manage and educate clients, and amplify the ideas of those around me. Using social media and a gargantuan RSS reader, I do my best to stay ahead of trends in technology and UX design.

Responsibilities include incorporating research to inform information architecture and design strategy. I enjoy making beautiful, intuitive, people-driven technology.

Deliverables:

- personas & user research
- mock-ups and wireframes
- site maps
- paper and digital prototypes
- content strategy
- usability studies

Jonathan Hung’s Specialties:

cross functional teamwork, cognitive psychology, information design, small business, design consultation, user research, wireframing, usability
