Margo Rose’s Summary

Social Media, Talent acquisition, & Human Resources Professional with more than 10 years of successful experience in talent management, organization development, and coaching. Provides brand development, internet intelligence and recruiting solutions. Offers detailed, and time activated project plans. Develops multi-media marketing plans.

Laser targeted focus providing exemplary outcomes to develop a strong social media presence:

* Social media communication strategist creating stellar visibility.

* Uses twitter, facebook, and other social media outlets to establish employer brand and attract top talent.

* Strengths include project management, masterful networking, workforce development,and strategy execution.

* Offers seamless group facilitation to build stronger teams, and to improve effective the organizational culture.

* Develops employee recognition programs that enhance retention & recruitment efforts.

* Provides employee relations solutions by opening communication channels, preventing conflicts and providing collaborative bargaining alternatives.

Margo's writing and communication skills are evident in the job descriptions, interview guides and performance-based evaluation tools she creates. She is also a prolific writer, having published numerous articles, blog posts. Rose is passionate about social media, and seeks to improve project management strategies. She is a skilled event planner, and coordinator. Margo is committed to being a steward in her community, as evidenced in the volunteer projects she undertakes. Beginning in January 2010 She launched a new blog talk radio program, "Compassionate HR," featuring Human Resource professionals going the extra mile to be of service in their community. She is also proud of being a newly appointed board member of Heartprints. She takes pride in receiving the #Ammy bestowed by The recruiting @Animal on twitter.
