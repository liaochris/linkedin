Philip Newman MedDeviceHeadhunter.com’s Summary

Philip Newman is Managing Director of 3Sixty Consulting, a leading boutique search firm headquartered in Atlanta, GA that provides executive search services exclusively to the medical technology industry.

Editor of the Blog, "The War For Talent" and Publisher of the eNewsetter, "The Inner Circle", Philip is regarded as one of the top medical device recruiters in the country. His specialization is placing specialty technical, mid to senior management, and executive level roles across project and program management, and functional areas of R&D, Product Development, Engineering, Manufacturing, Operations, Quality and Supply Chain.

Philip's career in talent acquisition spans full-desk executive search, business development, and branch management for both public and privately held search firms ranging from $55M to $5B in size. He received his undergraduate education from Georgia Tech and the University of Tennessee and was a two year varsity Letterman and two year SEC Scholar-Athlete award winner as a member of the University of Tennessee football team. After a brief flirtation with the NFL, Philip stepped into the recruiting trenches. He now brings the same type of intensity to his pursuit of talent acquisition excellence as he once did making overtime kicks in front of 100,000 fans.
