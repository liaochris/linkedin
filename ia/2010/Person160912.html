Paul Simon’s Summary

You have the greatest training, service or product to be found. Are you sharing your message clearly, concisely and professionally?

Why risk your reputation by poor communication that fails to accurately convey your expertise to your prospects and clients? Avoid messay, confusing language. Present webinars absent embarrassing technical glitches or missing pieces. Stand out from the crowd -- consistently serve up compelling messages that clearly reflect what you offer?

I'll massage and manage your webinars, your blogs and your written content.

I work closely with solo entrepreneurs and small businesses – fostering relationships that we all know drive sales. Let me make sure your content sparkles and you can focus on what you do best. I polish your content for blogs, articles, newsletters, books and other written materials, assist with press releases, and manage and host your webinars.

I've been a newspaper and wire service reporter, editor and manager and also held editing or managerial roles at four sales-related Web-based companies. Check out my recommendations.
