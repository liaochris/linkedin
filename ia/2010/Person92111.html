Jaswinder Chadha’s Summary

Jassi co-founded Axtria with the vision of building cutting-edge analytics and technology solutions that will help global companies to effectively manage and get insights from customer data.

Jassi is a pioneer in building advanced analytical software tools for designing sales forces. He has provided marketing strategies to a number of major global clients. Clients value his intelligence, perspective and commitment to the highest quality of service. In recognition of his leadership and entrepreneurial achievements, Jassi is the winner of 2004 E&Y "Entrepreneur of the Year" award and was awarded the year 2003 "Top Forty under Forty" by NJBiz Magazine. He is currently on the board of New Jersey Technology Council (NJTC) and is a Chartered Member of The Indus Entrepreneur (TiE) organization.

Previously, Jassi was co-founder and CEO of marketRx, which was acquired by Cognizant Technology Solutions in November 2007. marketRx was named to prestigious “Inc. 500”, a list of the nation's fastest-growing private companies and to “2005 Hot 100”, a ranking of the fastest-growing new entrepreneurial businesses in America by Entrepreneur magazine and PricewaterhouseCoopers. marketRx was also recognized and honored by various business organizations including “Private Company of the Year” in 2004 by NJBIZ and as one of the "Finest" Companies in New Jersey for the year 2003, 2004 and 2005. In addition, New Jersey Technology Council (NJTC) awarded marketRx the Prestigious "Private Company of the Year" in January 2003. marketRx was named second fastest growing Rising Star in the Deloitte Technology Fast 500 Program in 2003 and in Deloitte’s Technology Fast 500 Program for US in 2004, 2005 and 2006.
