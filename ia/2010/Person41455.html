Craig Duckett’s Summary

I caught the adult education back in 1999 when I taught my first Visual Basic and HTML classes for a technical school in Bellevue, WA. Since then I've enjoyed many aspects of adult learning, including designing instructor-lead and web based training, managing training centers, and teaching off-site at the community college level. Wanting to re-enter the adult education field, I returned to college and earned my Master of Arts degree in Humanities from California State University (DH) in August 2007. I am currently an Instructional & Technical Support Specialist at Cascadia Community College in Bothell, WA, where I maintain the college's computer labs and equipment, and provide training in the use of the hardware, operating systems, software applications, databases, storage, and client/server desktop imaging.
