Mohamad Khawaja’s Summary

I prefer to include a short bio here:

Mohammad Khawaja is currently the Executive Director of the Queen Rania Center for Entrepreneurship, and the founding project manager for the Queen Rania National Entrepreneurship Competition – The first and Technology specialized business plan competition in Jordan.

Khawaja has received his B. Sc. in communication engineering before he co-founded his business "overscore Inc." that offers compelling content management services to hundreds of clients from all over the world during the past five years.

As part of his work at the management of QRCE, Khawaja is now in charge of managing the “Google Award for The Best Online Business” and the “Cisco Entrepreneur Institute” in addition to his involvement in the establishing and mentoring other startups run by family and friends.

Khawaja is married with two cute little kinds !
