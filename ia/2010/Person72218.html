Gabriel del Rio’s Summary

Senior publicist and GdR Productions principle, Gabriel del Rio is an alternative programming executive with extensive experience in linear television as well as non-traditional web series production. Having launched more than a dozen television series, del Rio has compiled a comprehensive skill set including unit publicity, series premiere coordination, red carpet events, press screening orchestration and complete EPK production.

Carrying over nine years of public relations experience, del Rio has fostered intimate relationships with some of the world’s most notable programming brands. Partnering with clients such as Fox Television Studios, Current Television, Comcast Entertainment Group and MWG Entertainment, Gabriel has consulted C-level executives in directing a brand’s visibility and perception.

Additionally, del Rio has provided complete branding consultation for tech-centric clients and has earned executive producer credits by sourcing funding, licensing or network acquisitions for viable content partners.

del Rio earned a Bachelors of Science degree from Florida State University, is a competitive roller hockey player and currently resides in Los Angeles, CA.
