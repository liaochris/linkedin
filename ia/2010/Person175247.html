Robert Grossman’s Summary

Robert Grossman founded Focus Creative Group in 1993 and is currently the President and CEO. Focus Creative Group specializes in using new media to grow businesses and increase profits. Our mission is to forward our client’s businesses – at a strategic level – as they face opportunities or at critical junctions in their business.

Our services include: strategic messaging, content development and execution of live events, video production, social media, web broadcasting, presentations & digital signage deployments.

Robert has served great companies such as Fox, Lucas Films, Lucas Entertainment, Sony, Universal, Walt Disney and Warner Bros. Corporate clients include, American Heart Association, Blue Cross, Deloitte, Direct TV, Dole Foods, Ernst and Young, ExxonMobil, K2 Inc., Levis, Marsh, PacifCare, TRW, United Way, Volvo USA, and WellPoint.

Mr. Grossman is also the Co-Founder and COO of a innovative education and advertising venture called Waiting Room Theater (WRT). WRT is delivering award-winning content to over 3000 dental offices (expected to grow to 25,000 in the next three years) throughout the United States via Digital Signage and DVDs.
