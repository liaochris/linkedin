Iris Pittl’s Summary

Iris started her professional media career already during her time at University while working for the Austrian Broadcasting Corporation (ORF). Being responsible for several multimedia and marketing projects she gained sound media experience with an insight in all the different areas such as radio, TV and Internet.

After finishing her studies with a thesis focussing on the upcoming IPTV and Video on Demand market, Iris started working at Telekom Austria as Product Manager for their PC based broadband TV service in late 2003. After being promoted to Head of Content Acquisitions & Management in early 2005, Iris was leading the content negotiations for Austria's first IPTV service known as AON.TV - including contracts with more than 40 linear TV channels, VoD deals with Major Hollywood studios and other licensors as well as the national collecting societies. After the launch of the product, Iris also became responsible for the service's product management part.

In January 2007 Iris joined Internet startup Kyte as Business Development Manager Europe managing relationships with European telcos and media companies including contract negotiations, project management and marketing.

Since December 2008 Iris has been heading the business development of 123people, a leading people search site. With local domains for the US, UK, France, Spain, Germany, Austria, and Switzerland, 123people is the world’s largest people search offering a global service to a local audience and allowig users to look-up information on friends, relatives, or people of public interest by searching across more than 160 international as well as regional sources.

She holds a degree in MultiMediaArt from the University of Applied Sciences Salzburg with major studies in film and video production. She furthermore holds a diploma in economics & languages and is currently studying law at the University of Linz.

Iris is a German native speaker and fluent in English, Italian and French.
