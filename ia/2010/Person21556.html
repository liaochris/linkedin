Ben Fung’s Summary

Ben Fung has been in the IT and telecom field more than 15 years. Since 2004, his jobs had been focused more on the VoIP. In particular, he has been leaped to become a consultant on IP Multimedia Subsystem (IMS), Push to Talk (PoC), Fix Mobile Convergence (FMC) and various enterprise VoIP solution.

Ben Fung also has strong experience in interfacing with different levels of people. These included direct customers, presales, post-sales support staffs, sales, developers, partners, product management, etc. In 2006 to 2007, he was assigned as the trainer to teach the details behind the SIP and IMS protocols plus hands-on labs in Singapore and Hangzhou. His audience included post-sales and presales engineers and developers.

In 2006 to 2008, Ben Fung was assigned to participate in the development project of the demo version IMS end to end solution. The solution composed of various Linux servers including open source DNS servers, SIP products such as OpenSer and Asterisk. The ultimate product had saved the sales teams and testing teams millions of dollars from investing on the original full-scale chassis based IMS gears for customer trials, training and interworking testing.

Besides the extensive experience in VoIP, Ben Fung had involved in many carrier grade network implementation projects in his early telecom career. These included country wide Asynchronous Transfer Mode (ATM) networks, Frame Relay networks, Voice Networking, IP VPN networks, etc.
