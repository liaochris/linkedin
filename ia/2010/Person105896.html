Magician Josh Norris’s Summary

I've been a professional magician for the last 12 years of my life. I've performed for some of the largest corporations in the country and I've been lucky enough to travel the world doing what I love. My current work focuses on magic for corporate events, including serving as a trade show pitchman and brand ambassador for companies across a variety of industries. My company Interactive Magic, is also expanding its roster to include some of the areas top magicians.

If you are looking for entertainment for your next event or just some advice on a venue your considering, feel free to drop me a line. I look forward to meeting you! -josh

Magician Josh Norris’s Experience

  • Founder

    Metro Event Specialists (MES)

    (Events Services industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    Metro Event Specialists (MES) is a professional trade organization for event service providers working in and around Washington, DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

    MES organizes networking events on a regular basis to allow area professionals an inexpensive (and often free) way to meet others in their industry. There are no monthly dues, no mandatory meetings, and no politics.

    Find out more at: WWW.METROEVENTS.ORG

  • Magician / Corporate Entertainer

    Interactive Magic

    (Entertainment industry)

    January 1998 — Present (12 years 5 months)

    Interactive Magic provides Business Minded Variety Entertainment for corporate functions, private parties, and special events. Interactive Magic was founded by Magician Josh Norris in 1998 and since has organized performances at over 500 live events throughout the United States.

    For more information visit: www.joshnorris.com
