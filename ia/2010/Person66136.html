Eric Le Gendre’s Summary

Spécialiste Solaire à Toulouse ( www.garonne-ecosystemes.fr ) Photovoltaique à Toulouse, Récupération eau à Toulouse, eolien à Toulouse.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I founded Garonne EcoSystèmes in 2008, after a 27 years carreer in the Car industry, with high level responsibilities either in France or abroad.

Garonne EcoSystèmes is a provider of Environmental products & Services, for individual housing in SW-France.
We aim towards providing a high level of customer satisfaction in the environmental branch in the Toulouse Area.

Our dedication is toward EnR's (Renewables Energies) as well as on water savings and treatment. We've built up a fair and eficient network of strong-professional business & industrial partners.

We sell and set up : solar panels, solar installations & devices, water savers/collectors, water treatment & cleaning devices, air cleaning hardware, afordable small & medium windmills ( very innovative products from Nheolis ) ... for anyone in SW France, particularly around Toulouse.
