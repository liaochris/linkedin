David Long, PMP, Six Sigma Black Belt’s Summary

I am a proven senior project manager wth multiple degrees (B.S. and M.B.A) who is PMP and Six Sigma Black Belt certified. I have managed groups ranging from two senior project managers to 30-40 professional and clerical personnel. I have extensive experience both operating and working in project management offices (PMO) and have used my expertise to analyze project performance for reporting to executive management. Through my various assignments I have developed extensive experience in IT, telecommunications and the oil and natural gas industries. I can work easily with software developers and teams in that I have "hands-on" experience in programming, database and graphics design.

I am a project manager who looks beyond the current task and the next few that need to be performed. I am constantly trying to anticipate risks and propose solutions before they become show stoppers. Through my experience in introducing and applying earned value analysis I have learned who to help the shareholders in a project understand exactly where a project stands and provide the measurements that take the “guess work” out of project progress.

I attained the Six Sigma Black Belt certificate to give me an extra tool in evaluating program performance not just an individual project. By understanding the practical application of DMAIC methodology I have been able to improve processes and, at the same time, provide focus for the individuals that are part of that process.
