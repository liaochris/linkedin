Belinda Acosta’s Summary

I have been the TV Eye Columnist at the Austin Chronicle for ten years. I write the weekly column in addition to news and features for the other sections of the paper as well.

My freelance work has appeared in Poets & Writers, Alternet, the Austin American-Statesman, Latino Magazine, Latino USA the radio journal of news and culture, the San Antonio Current and the San Antonio Express-News.

I am a member of Macondo, the writing collective launched by acclaimed Latina writer, Sandra Cisneros.

I have written the first of two, thematically related books, DAMAS, DRAMAS, AND ANA RUIZ. The pub date is August 11. Book signings, readings, and other appearances are being planned right now. I am in the process of re-writing the second book in the series, SISTERS, STRANGERS, AND STARTING OVER.

My goal is to survive the flurry.

Belinda Acosta’s Specialties:

Featuring writing, research (film, TV, books, events). Public speaking, writing workshops and "how I do what I do" workshops.
