Walden Wong’s Summary

Lighthouse Advisory Services (LAS) is a commercial real estate valuation, advisory and due diligence services firm dedicated to bringing expertise, efficiency and reliability to its clients and the investment community. The principals of LAS combine more than 45 years of real estate finance and capital markets experience with the values of loyalty, honesty and professionalism. Our team adopts a practical approach to our business platform, creating a first class level of service for financial institutions and individuals across the globe.

Lighthouse Advisory Services real estate finance platform:
- Portfolio Valuation and Consulting
- FAS 114 and 5 ALLL Analysis
- Loan Portfolio Sales and Advisory
