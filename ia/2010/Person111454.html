Kate Bayless’s Summary

Hi - Kate Bayless here. I work as a writer, product reviewer and editor with experience in a variety of topics and fields including parenting, religion, spirituality, humor, retail, education, the arts, data analysis, technology and design. My work has been featured in national print magazines, on award-winning books and for a number of online sites and communities. I am a fan of witty banter, the hidden story, intriguing insight and the subjunctive clause. I don't like unnecessary commas unless they are, indeed, necessary. I enjoy searching out page-turning stories, crafting new narrative and turning other people's ho-hum writing into life-altering prose.

Have a writing or editing need? Let's talk.

Kate Bayless’s Specialties:

substantive edits, line edits and proofreading; product testing and review; brand development; manuscript consulting and rewrites; innovative story pitches; ghostwriting; meeting deadlines; turning first-time clients into satisfied, repeat customers
