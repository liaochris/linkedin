Mike Geiger’s Summary

For more than 12 years I've been leading multidisciplinary teams in strategy, branding, production and development of cutting-edge new media and advertising content for clients including HP, Adobe, Comcast, Sprint, Got Milk?, Gucci and the NBA.

In 2003 I started the Interactive Production Department at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners and in 2008 I became the agency’s first Chief Digital Officer. Next to still overseeing the production of all interactive and emerging technology projects, I'm also in charge of improving and expanding and fully integrating the agency’s digital offerings.

Work that I produced has won numerous awards at the most prestigious award shows including: One Show Interactive, Cannes Cyber Lions, Clios, The Art Directors Club, D&AD and FlashForward.

I'm also involved in many speaking engagements at venues like the CAT Conference, Boards Creative Work Shops and Summits, the Clios and Flash Conferences like FITC and OFFF. I also guest lecture in the MBA program at Stanford University and am a member of the International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences and was honored as one of Madison Avenue’s Top 20 ‘IT’ advertising professionals in 2009. Since 2009 I've been a charter Board of Director's member at ‘Boulder Digital Works‘, a Board Member at 'CAT Creativity and Technology' and an Advisory Board Member at The Creative Circus.

In addition, I'm a frequent juror in award shows such as the New York Festivals, the Webbys, the One Show Interactive, the Clios, the London International Advertising Awards and the Seattle Show.

Before joining the world of advertising, I worked at design & production studios like Method, Spyplane and Thunk Design. Originally from Munich, Germany I now live in San Francisco.

I graduated from the University of San Diego with a BA in Business Administration and received an MBA in Marketing Management from the University of San Francisco in 1995.
