Fernanda Domingues’s Summary

Fernanda Domingues is the founder and CEO of FD Comunicação.
She has more than 15 years of strategic communications and business development expertise in the Digital Entertainment Industry, Games, Education, Information Technology, Tourism and other issues, and her ability to bring companies together and provide strategic guidance has led to business growth for industry giants as well as start-ups.
During this time FD Comunicação team has launched more than 500 games in Brazil. She has held communications for Capcom, Activision, Atari, Midway, Take Two, Konami, Ubi Soft, Hoplon (achieve 100 articles about the launch of these Brazilian MMO Game, in 2008), Hive Entertainment and more.

These companies and their games are frequently in the greatest Brazilian TVs, magazines, newspapers and blogs. Fernanda is specializing in social media. FDcomunicação uses twitter and other tools to spread the news for opinion leaders and journalists.

FD can help companies as a Business Development, Corporate Strategy, Communications Strategy, Self-Publishing Services, Pre-Publisher Awareness, Branding, Corporate Image, Public Relations, Interactive Entertainment, Digital Media, Content Culturalization.
