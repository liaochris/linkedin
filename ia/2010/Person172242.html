Richard Goeglein’s Summary

PROVIDING EXCELLENCE THROUGH LEADERSHIP EXPERTISE AND EXECUTIVE MANAGEMENT EXPERIENCE
___________________________________________________

Evening Star Holdings, LLC (ESH) was formed in late 2001 creating a privately owned boutique resort, limited service hotel and travel services company. After eight active years, a decision was made to exit the hotel ownership and management sector. This decision was fully executed and completed at the end of April 2009. The company remains active in the travel services business through its association with the travel industry leader, Rovia.

The current global economic distress has set the stage for company founder, Richard Goeglein, to continue to lend his unique and diverse expertise to a select group of Boards of Directors, company founders and their management.

Richard is available for presentations on Creating A Wining Culture, as a Keynote Speaker as well as presentations to selected group meetings and conferences.

Richard Goeglein’s Specialties:

Corporate Strategy Assessment; Organization Assesment, Stucture/Design; Team Building Initiatives; Remedial Management, Turnarounds and Workouts.
