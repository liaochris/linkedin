Barb Dickey’s Summary

Several years of large agency experience with a focus that is multi-disciplinary, so I can take a brand into whatever brave new world that makes the most strategic sense from traditional advertising to bridging the digital divide. It’s what I call “Rapid Idea Prototyping.”

Living in the heart of DC, I also specialize in issues advocacy and “inside the beltway” campaigns for such clients as Citizens Helping Heroes, Inc; a non-profit that supports the military and their families, Pharmaceutical Care Managers Association, Nike Paralympics Health Benefits Coalition, Asbestos Alliance and Water Advocates, a non-profit dedicated to increasing funding for access to clean water and sanitation worldwide.

Barb Dickey’s Specialties:

Integrated approaches utilizing both traditional and new media advertising and marketing strategies. Crossing the bridge between advertising and public relations, I've been helping clients see the strength of ideas and capitalize on the power of images and words.

Barb Dickey’s Experience

  • President & Creative Director

    Cre8tivision LLC

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Cre8tivision LLC, a creative hotbed and freelance consultancy, is committed to our clients' success. We’re great at tackling the thorniest business problems–and solving them. Our focus is multi-disciplinary, so we can take a brand into whatever brave new world that makes the most strategic sense. We’ll find the sweet spot between the new and tried and true from traditional advertising and marketing to bridging the digital divide. It’s what we call “Rapid Idea Prototyping.”

    Over the years, we've produced award-winning work for Microsoft, Nevada Power, McDonnell Douglas, PNC Financial Services, American Chemistry Council, AMOCO, IBM, Mercedes Benz, Propane Education & Research Council, Clorox, US Chamber of Commerce, Michelin and Nike to name a few.

  • Director of Marketing

    Citizens Helping Heroes Inc.

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    While the need for emergency services to help military service members and their families has risen with recent and continued deployments in the global war on terror, donations to the organizations that provide such services have not kept pace.

    This is the driving force behind Citizens Helping Heroes Inc., a nonprofit organization formed to help raise citizen awareness about the unique struggles facing the military and their families.

    Events in the past have included the Heroes Red, White & Blue Inaugural Balls, Heroes Centennial at Walter Reed Medical Center. CHH Inc. often partners with the USO, TAPS (The Tragedy Assistance Program for Survivors) as well as various veteran's organizations.
