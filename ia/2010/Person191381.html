Daniel Burleigh’s Summary

I began my career in a small business/startup environment within the nutritional industry, where I quickly moved into the area of international market development (marketing/operations/business development). My passion and interests in international business and organizational change led me to the BYU MBA program, where I graduated with a focus in organizational behavior, a minor in strategy, and a Global Management Certificate. I am now working in the Talent & Org Capability group within Microsoft in the HR Trax rotational program.

My career mission statement is to enable individuals, teams, and organizations to reach their full potential. My goal is to continue gaining knowledge and experience through driving positive business results for a global company, and partnering with individuals and organizations to bring about positive change in society through business, nonprofit organizations, and other mediums.

Daniel Burleigh’s Specialties:

Recruiting/Staffing, organizational development, change management, social entrepreneurship, nonprofit development, conflict resolution/mediation, project management, program management, international market development.
