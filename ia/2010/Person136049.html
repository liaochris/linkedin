Mary Elise Chavez’s Summary

Tangible results sprinkled with magic, this is what Mary Elise provides each one of her clients. She supplies clients with an integrated and progressive approach to defining and realizing the needs and dreams of their ventures via her informed perspective to energize new and existing online properties. She carries rich experience in the web, social media, graphic, information, photo, fashion, beauty and film industries.

As a Senior Account Manager, Mary Elise builds strong client relationships by becoming an advocate for the client and intimately understanding their business. She identifies their goals and objectives, then leads members of Blue Fountain Media's expert design, development and online marketing team, to create a strategic approach to best position their business via models that increase user engagement, brand loyalty, repeat visits and conversions.

Her strong creative direction background, assures every project is aesthetically innovative and has optimal user-interface design, while her robust business and strategic management background ensures a profitable and results-driven project.

Mary Elise established the Women's Practice at Blue Fountain Media, which brings together our best talent for female-centric projects and industries. Her projects consistently win industry awards and gain recognition for their innovative execution. To ensure clients are on the cutting-edge of their industry, Mary Elise stays on top of current trends that are synonymous with successful online properties and is actively involved in Women in Tech, Fashion 2.0 and the NY Tech Meetup.

Mary Elise’s list of achievements and accomplishments can fill a book. She is a seasoned publisher, fashion photographer, jewelry designer and entrepreneur. In what little spare time she has, Mary has been known to sky dive, kickbox and has reached upwards of 165 MPH behind the wheel of a 1966 Shelby Mustang.
