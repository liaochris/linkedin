Kevin Goodman’s Summary

Sales, marketing and business development role at BlueBridge Networks, a world class SAS 70 Type II & PCI compliant data centers, offering secure data center services. I contribute to BlueBridge Networks success, winning awards such as The COSE 10 Under 10, SBN Rising Star in Innovation Awards, NEOSA 2007 Tech Awards-Best Emerging Company, 2008 Team NEO Asset Creation Finalist and the 2009 NEOSA Best IT Services Company.
