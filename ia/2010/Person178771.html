Peter Rukavina’s Summary

I am a committed generalist, with a technical bent, inveterately curious and prone to speculative experimenting and bouts of iconoclasm.

Peter Rukavina’s Specialties:

Open source software (use of, contributions to), long-distance collaboration, skunkworks projects, simplicity, feats of apparent wizardry in situations with few resources and little time, communication of complex technical ideas.
