Timothy Knight’s Summary

I am a User Experience Engineer that focuses on developing applications and interface systems using Ruby and Ruby on Rails. I completed my first web project in 1995 and graduated with a degree in Network Engineering from Keiser University in 2001. I have worked as a Project Leader, Information Architect, Senior Web Developer, Computer Security Advisor, and Interface Designer over a period of the last 10 years.

I am currently employed by GravityFree as a specialist in concept development, business objective analysis, and user experience as well as the founder of the think tank, Innovative Thought, Inc. I hold a strong history of competency with developing standards-based web applications (XHTML/CSS) using ColdFusion and Ruby on Rails, and running successful teams as a Project Manager, and a Team Leader. I am a member of the Interaction Design Association, the Usability Professionals Association, an honorary member of the International Council of E-Commerce Consultants, and have served on the Advisory Board of Keiser University for Engineering and Cyber-security.
