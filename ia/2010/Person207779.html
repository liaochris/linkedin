Travis Close, PMP®’s Summary

Travis Close is the Chief Technology Officer of Defense Software Corporation, a Northern Virginia based I.T. company working with the federal government and prime contractors.

Involved with several CMMI related projects, Mr. Close is assisting commercial and government organizations in building high quality business/technical process assets and improving existing processes to become compliant with multiple models and frameworks (CMMI, ISO 9001:2000, ISO 12207, Six Sigma, etc).

Mr. Close is a highly qualified technologist experienced in all aspects of the Systems and Software Development Life Cycle processes. With a thorough understanding of the Software Engineering Body of Knowledge, Mr. Close provides expertise to clients and stakeholders.

He maintains a top secret clearance for supporting sensitive projects, along with the PMP credential from the Project Management Institute.

Travis Close, PMP®’s Specialties:

Software Engineering, Database Engineering, CMMI, Process Improvement, Systems and Software Development Life Cycles (ISO 12207), Process and Product Quality Assurance (PPQA), Software Design and Modeling, UML, Model Driven Architecture.

Enterprise Web Applications and Software. Full Stack of Software Engineering Technologies. J2EE, .NET
