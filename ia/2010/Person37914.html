    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    Beyond the Applause is committed to educating families throughout college admissions and career search. We empower students to develop their passion while creating a solid plan for the future. Our educational planning system emphasizes finding the student’s appropriate matches for living, learning and success beyond high school and college. We work with students in grades 8 through college.

    Beyond the Applause has designed a flexible college admissions and career exploration curriculum containing a wide range of services designed to educate students and their families throughout the process. Including but not limited to the following services:

    * Family and student assessment and intake sessions
    * One-on-one student education and career planning sessions
    * Student database and organizational tools
    * Phone and live meeting services
    * College research and application facilitation
    * Parent informational sessions
    * Newsletters and Webinars
    * Financial aid and scholarship planning
    * College to Career Guidance
