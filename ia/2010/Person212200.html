Vincent Battaglia’s Summary

My name is Vincent Battaglia but everybody calls me « Vinch ».
I'm 26 (born on Oct, 18 1983) and I live in Brussels (Belgium).

I have got a master in computer science at the University of Mons-Hainaut.

I like learning new things anytime. I'm very interested in XTHML, CSS, Web standards, accessibility, semantical Web, JavaScript, AJAX, Web 2.0, SEO, PHP, Web frameworks, Open source technologies and softwares, geographic information systems, and many more.

I have worked since the middle of 2005. My first job was in T4HR, the software service of the job agency Trace! where I was a "Front-end architect". My job there was slicing PSD layouts and integrating them into MyFaces (Java Web framework from Apache based on JSF). In early 2006, I decided to start as a freelance. I was free to use new generation tools to create websites and it was cool to work at home! Despite that, I was very interested in working in a growing company like Emakina Belgium where I was officially Web integrator and Flash developer.

I'm now Chief Geek Officer and Communication Manager at my own company, the brand new Web agency 1MD.be

What we do : design, Web integration and development, mobile Web, Flash, motion design, sound design, 3D, video, print, etc.
