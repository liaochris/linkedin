Jacquelyn Ekern’s Summary

Jacquelyn Ekern is the founder of Eating Disorder Hope. She completed her Masters degree in Counseling/Psychology, at Capella University. She received her Bachelor of Science in Human Services degree from The University of Phoenix. She manages the Eating Disorder Hope website(www.eatingdisorderhope.com) website. She also is a licensed professional counselor with a closed private practice, exclusively treating eating disorders. Jacquelyn's past battle with and recovery from an eating disorder fuel her passion to help others.

Jacquelyn offers extensive experience in the eating disorder field including: advanced education in psychology, participation and contributions to additional eating disorder groups, symposiums, and professional associations. Jacquelyn is also an active member of the National Eating Disorder Association(NEDA), Academy of Eating Disorders(AED), and the Eating Disorders Coalition.
