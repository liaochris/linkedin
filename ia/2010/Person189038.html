Bill Shipley’s Summary

I am a professional photographer that specializes and enjoys working in the commercial and editorial fields. I have been a contributor to Sports Illustrated, ESPN the Magazine, professional sports teams, Nascar and NCAA organizations. I look forward to expanding my skills in the visual mediums to encompass enhanced video / non-linear editing capabilities.

Additionally, I am currently responsible for supervising the Prepress department with a top 400 print group that specializes in large format lithographic printing for POS and out of home advertising. We work with agencies and advertisers on a national level. I am extremely proficient with the Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, color management and Kodak Prinergy software, among others.

Bill Shipley’s Specialties:

Digital Pre-press, photography, color management, design, digital enhancement
