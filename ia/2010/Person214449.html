Nadir Weibel’s Summary

I am originally from the southern and Italian part of Switzerland (Ticino) where I completed all the basic schools. I hold a master in Computer Science and Engineering from ETH Zurich, where I also completed my Ph.D. in Summer 2009.
I recently joined the Distributed Cognition and Human Computer Interaction Lab at the University of California San Diego as Postdoc in October 2009.

In my research I explored new ways of enhancing a seemingly mundane, but ubiquitous, resource such as paper to support everyday work, interaction and collaboration. During my Ph.D. I investigated how to support and extend the authoring and publishing infrastructure for Interactive Paper documents, providing distinctive ways of interweaving paper documents with digital materials.

I am currently looking at how to extend Interactive Paper technology to other aspects of cognition and HCI.

My main interests ranges from software engineering to human computer interactions, including computer supported collaborative work, information visualization, mobile and ubiquitous computing.
