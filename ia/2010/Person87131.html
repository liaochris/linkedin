Jacques Warren’s Summary

I have been involved in Internet Marketing for fourteen years, focusing solely on Web Analytics for the last eight. I truly believe it is essential to get Web Analytics right in order to efficiently manage a Web site. I intend to spend the next professional years deepening the implementation of Web Analytics, and integrating Database Marketing in Web marketing.

I am also the co-author of the book: "Web Analytics : Mesurer le succès et maximiser la profitabilité de votre site Web", published by Eyrolles, Paris, in October 2009.
