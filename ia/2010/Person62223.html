Edvin Lee’s Summary

Edvin Lee has been a practicing artist for over 15 years. His paintings have been exhibited in Vancouver, Toronto, and New York City and his passion for the arts extend to graphic and fashion design. He attended Ryerson Polytechnic University in Toronto Canada and received a degree in Fashion Design. Upon graduation, he pursued a career in the field of fashion design working for the Toronto Fashion Incubator and many local upscale fashion designers. During those years his designs have been published in local newspapers and appeared on national television. As well, he actively participated in two Fashion Cares (Aids Fundrasier) fashion shows held in Toronto.

In 2004, The Banff Centre employed him as an interactive designer collaborating with internationally recognized new media and visual artists creating interactive projects for thematic and self-directed residencies. He also provided his design expertise to the Banff Centre Visual Arts department as well as the Banff New media Institute creating online and print promotions.

At the end of 2005, he relocated back to Toronto where he has been working as a senior designer at a leading edge award winning interactive agency, Blast Radius. He has art-directed websites for a gaming company Electronic Arts and provided creative concepts for clients such as Sharpie and Disney.

His works have been published in magazines and books in Japan, UK, Canada and the Netherlands. He was recently profiled in the UK design magazine, Computer Arts and his site www.piperboy.com will be featured in an upcoming book Web Design Index in December 2006. Different international online design communities such as K10k.net, Designiskinky.com, Newstoday.com, surfstation.lu, pixelsurgeon.com, and metafilter.com have praised many of his works.
