Michael Kempner’s Summary

MICHAEL W. KEMPNER is the founder, President and Chief Executive Officer of MWW Group. In addition to running the agency as a whole, Mr. Kempner plays an active role in the management of many individual accounts. Prior to its sale to the Interpublic Group of Companies in 2000, Kempner was responsible for building MWW Group into the 4th largest independent public relations firm in the United States. Headquartered in East Rutherford, New Jersey, MWW Group has 11 offices in the United States and Europe and is currently one of the 10 largest public relations firms in the United States.

MWW Group was named public relations “Agency of the Year” by the Holmes Report, one of the public relations industry’s premier trade publications and “Mid-Size Agency of the Year,” by PR News. MWW Group was also voted top public relations firm for client satisfaction for an unprecedented four years in a row, 2003, 2004, 2005 and 2006.

In addition to MWW Group, Mr. Kempner is CEO of Financial Relations Board where he directs the operations of the nation’s largest investor relations and financial communications firms.

Additionally, Kempner is Founder and CEO of StoneAmerica Licensing, one of the nation’s premier licensing and sponsorship companies.

Kempner was recently honored with a Lifetime Achievement Award by PR News for his two decades of contributions to the public relations industry and his leadership of MWW Group. In 2005, he was named PR Professional of the Year by the Public Relations Society of America, NJ Chapter.

Mr. Kempner was named one of the Top 50 NJ Business People by The Business Journal of New Jersey and one of the Top 100 Business People in New Jersey by Business News New Jersey. Additionally, Kempner was named one of the 50 Most Interesting People in New Jersey by New Jersey Monthly magazine. Kempner is a two time winner of the "New Jersey's Finest" award and a four-time finalist of Inc.magazines' Entrepreneur of the Year award.
