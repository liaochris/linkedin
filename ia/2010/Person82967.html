Howie Appel’s Summary

I am a multi-tasking individual:
Over 20 years in the recruiting or talent acquisition of engineers and IT professionals in both corporate and agency environs, both as a contractor and full-time. My primary focus has been the full-cycle process of Civil Engineers in the construction industries. I am a results oriented professional. My goal also is to become a major contributor to the success of my employer. I have the enthusiasm, dedication, and passion for success which will be vital to my next position.

In my efforts to help people with their job searches, through ProNet Career Resources aka ProNet Central Florida, I also enjoy volunteering at the Central Florida Employment Council and Christian HELP, a very worthwhile organization.

Having served in the USAF (MSgt-E-7) as an Administrative/Personnel Specialist, my "hat is off " and I salute those who serve our country. I served 17 years...and proud of it!

Originally from Needham, MA, I am a caregiver to my wife who is building a home based business as an Independent Avon Sales Representative. We have reached "Honor Society" level and Unit Leader. Remember, Avon is the "Company for Women...and a few good men!"

Howie Appel’s Specialties:
