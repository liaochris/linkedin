John Eyles’s Summary

Recently returned from UK attending Games Based Learning conference, giving a seminar at Cambridge Uni Faculty of Ed and spending time with relatives.

Currently working at Telecom New Zealand in Group Strategy. Recent focus has been on Mobile Learning, Social Media, eHealth and Virtual Worlds. Most recent is the development of a ONE Telecom strategy for Education.

I'm also director of a media and consulting company, and chair of the EON Foundation.

• Expertise in education, creativity, communication technologies and business development.

• Experience with local and international business partners, venture capital, overseas development, academia and start-ups.

• Proven capability to harness the power of distributed groups and social media to increase performance and reduce costs.

• Capacity to effectively build and leverage generative relationships across platforms, nationalities, disciplines and sectors to deliver outstanding results.

• Ability to creatively make associations between seemingly disparate ideas and turn these into high value business opportunities or powerful insights that manage risk.

I enjoy speaking at conferences and other events as well as meeting and sharing with others. I have a fun filled attitude and lifelong love of learning. I seek to work with visionary thinkers and creators engaged in building a sustainable world through cooperation and mutual respect supported by new communication technologies.
