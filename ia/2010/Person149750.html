Michael G. "Mickey" Shackelford’s Honors:

Mr. Shackelford served on the Board of Directors for NSPS and ACSM during the development and passing of several versions of the ALTA/ACSM Land Title Survey Standards that are in use today by title company officials and land surveyors nationwide. He has been actively involved in the civil engineering and land surveying business for over 30 years after receiving his degrees in civil engineering technology and land surveying from the University of Oklahoma and Oklahoma State University, respectively. Mr. Shackelford has taught related courses at these and other schools as a full-time instructor and as a guest lecturer at other colleges and universities. He has presented numerous seminars and workshops across the country for various professional organizations and has written many technical articles on the subject for national trade magazines including Professional Surveyor, The American Surveyor, Backsights, The Bulletin and Point of Beginning (POB) magazine.
