  • Chairwoman

    Dutch Bed & Breakfast Foundation

    (Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    August 2007 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    The Dutch Bed & Breakfast Foundation strives to improve quality and thus promote the image of Bed & Breakfast accommodations in the Netherlands and aims to be beneficial or useful to the hospitality industry in the broadest sense of the word. The Foundation endeavours to achieve its goal by legal means by doing the following:

    * Developing and keeping a classification system of Bed & Breakfast accommodations in the Netherlands;
    * en effectuant des contrôles de qualité auprès des accommodations de Bed & Breakfast par un visiteur-mystère et des enquêtes de satisfaction qui vise le contentement total de la clientèle de Bed & Breakfast (questionnaires);
    * Stimulating Bed & Breakfast accommodations to improve their quality in the interests of the consumer and the hospitality industry itself;
    * Providing both national and international consumers with a clear overview of small-scale types of lodging accommodations available, so that these consumers can quickly and easily find a suitable Bed & Breakfast accommodation;
    * Providing all other means to promote and achieve the goal of the Foundation.

  • Owner

    Bedandbreakfast.nl

    (Online Media industry)

    July 1999 — Present (11 years 5 months)

    Bed & Breakfast Nederland (BBN) is an organisation for bed & breakfast owners in the Netherlands. There are currently 4.000 bed & breakfast owners, who have joined this organisation.Thanks to BBN, it is now so much easier to find bed & breakfast owners on the Internet. Online marketing is an essential link between the bed & breakfast owner and the bed & breakfast visitor and BBN uses this to fulfil an important promotional role for bed & breakfast owners in the Netherlands. Besides the www.bedandbreakfast.nl portal, BBN offers other marketing tools to promote bed & breakfast in the Netherlands, including the book entitled 'Bed & Breakfast in the Netherlands' ('Bed & Breakfast in Nederland'). Thanks to its promotional activities, BBN is easily accessible to everyone all over the world and BBN maintains contact with various parties both locally and abroad, such as the government, bed & breakfast organisations from other countries, as well as the (international) media.
