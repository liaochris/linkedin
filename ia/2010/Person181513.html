Sam Wettling’s Summary

Present Sam:

My most recent venture is a partnership with Launch Interactive Media. I couldn't be more thrilled to be working with such a fun and innovative company. If your business needs a CMS-based, incredible looking and interactive website, then Launch Interactive is for you. Give me a buzz and we will chat.

Past Sam:

I have been a passionate technologist my whole life, getting my first computer when I was 3 (an Apple II+). I've been working with them ever since.

After college I began working at Dell Computer on their Premier team. I was responsible for the development of the customer creation processes for corporate customers as well as writing an early form of content management system which was able to update content anywhere on the Dell Premier site in six different languages.

Upon leaving Dell, I wanted to work for a business where he would not only be able to continue improving my technical skills, but where he could truly make a difference. This landed me a job at the Texas Computer Education Association (TCEA). For the next seven years, I was responsible for all facets of TCEA’s technology including their websites, software development, internal networks, convention technology as well as their iMIS association management system.

After leaving TCEA in early 2009, I started Wettling Enterprises Inc., an IT consulting business with a specific focus on nonprofits and small businesses. Wettling Enterprises currently assists a number of associations and small businesses in Austin and is always on the lookout for innovative ways to save clients money and make them more efficient and effective in their missions through the proper application of technology.

Personal Sam:
If I'm not sitting at a computer, I'm probably spending time with my wonderful wife, Diana, my cat Mina and my dog Stella. Or, I could be camping, fishing, or at Peace Park playing Frisbee golf.
