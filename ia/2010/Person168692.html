Rana Killough’s Summary

Wipe Out is an Eco-friendly Water Damage and Mold Cleanup service in the Duval/Nassau County area. Wipe Out was designed to offer an Eco-friendly choice for the ever growing market of water damage restoration, while reducing the environmental impact on our planet, by implementing lean production standards, and green business solutions that reduce environmental waste, savings which are passed on to their customers.

Water Damage can be most homeowner’s worst nightmare. When dealing with this situation, consumers today should not have to worry about toxicity of products companies use in their home. Whether they are harmful to use around your children, pets or the elderly. Wipe Out uses Eco-friendly products which leave behind no harmful residue and contain non-toxic, non-synthetic materials. Their products are less harmful than vinegar. With Eco-friendly or green removal products, you can keep your home free of molds without harming the environment.

Another of Wipe Out’s green initiatives include being a paperless company. It provides all billing and documentation services electronically, which allows its customers to receive and pay their bills online.

A private, employee-owned company designed around the idea of “jack-of-one trade”. We formed this company after being affiliated with other environmental restoration companies who found themselves branching in many directions and stretching their expertise too thin. Our vision was to create a company that was great at one thing—environmental restoration (water damage/mold).

By eliminating the archaic corporate structure, ensuring all employees own a part of the company, and being highly innovative, expertly trained and IICRC Certified in our industry, we have created a customer oriented environment that specializes in ensuring your home is dry the first time.
