Debbie Price’s Summary

In 1987 Deborah A. Price was the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer for International Research Bureau, Inc. (IRBsearch as it is known today) located in Tallahassee, Florida.

Professional affiliations have included the Tallahassee Chamber of Commerce, National Public Records Research Association, and Society for Human Resource Management, Private Investigator Association of Florida, Florida Association of Licensed Investigators, and the South Florida Investigators Association.

In 1994 developed an on-line database division within IRB for nationwide public record retrieval.

In July 1995 IRB merged online division with Database Technologies, Inc., (DBT) of Pompano Beach, Florida, (NYSE DBT) and served on the board of directors. In 1997 DBT merged with Choicepoint (NYSE CPS) of Alpharetta, Georgia.

  • Co-Founder/CEO

    International Research Bureau, Inc. (now known as IRBsearch LLC)

    (Partnership; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 1986 — June 2005 (18 years 10 months)

    IRBsearch—as the company is known today—traces its origin to the founding of the International Research Bureau, Inc. (IRB) in 1986. The company became one of the largest public record retrieval companies in the State of Florida by 1994 with over 14,000 customers from law firms, insurance companies, private investigation services, law enforcement agencies, and general businesses.

    In November of 1994, IRB launched one of the first online public record databases in the country. It allowed users to instantly search Florida public records such as drivers licenses, vehicles, and real property. IRB was sold to Database Technologies, Inc., (DBT), also known as Autotrak, in July 1995 and the databases merged. DBT was later bought by ChoicePoint (CPS; NYSE) in 1998. In December of 1995, Deborah Price and Darrell Goodwin- the original IRB partners bought back the rights to the name International Research Bureau, Inc., and began providing pre-employment screening services.
