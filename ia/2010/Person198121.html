Susanna Kunkel’s Summary

I've dreamed of being a Realtor for 20 years. However, as a single mother I found I needed to have a "regular" job to provide the security needed for my daughter. During that time, I gained some very valuable experience doing administrative, contract and paralegal work for various industries such as Technology, Investment Banking, BioTech and Estate Planning. In 2004, I finally stepped into my dream of being a Realtor, and have been working full-time on Kauai as a residential specialist since then.

I've watched markets change, studied the success of others and am extremely motivated to help my clients achieve their dreams and goals. Real Estate is one of the best ways to develop and secure financial independence. My goal for my family's future generations, and for my clients and community is to pass on the information and support needed to create financial independence, retirement security and wealth.
