Jim Cashel’s Summary

Jim Cashel is Chairman of Forum One Communications, a digital communications firm which works at the nexus of technology, public policy, and online community. At Forum One he oversees strategic engagements involving health, education, global development, and other topics, for a long list of influential organizations. Previously he served as Senior Program Office and Chief Financial Officer of the Eurasia Foundation, a grant-making organization based in Washington, DC.
