Deon Binneman’s Summary

Deon Binneman is a reputation management specialist, thought leader, keynote speaker and seminar leader on building, sustaining, protecting and restoring business reputation.

He presents to business audiences all over the world on the ways to build, sustain and protect business and personal reputation.

Whether it is about the value of reputation, or what damages it to how to respond to a crisis that can destroy the reputation of an institution, Deon vows audiences with his hard-hitting and entertaining messages.

Deon is considered among the most influential thought leaders on the management of corporate reputation with stakeholders and the mitigation of reputation risk. He has an ability to communicate reputation and crisis issues into language that non-experts can easily understand.

Deon’s presentations are based on 30 years of consulting, 13 of it in private practice, and working with companies in ten countries.

As a seminar leader, he is well known for both his reputation expertise and his experience in dealing with organization developmental and communication issues.

As a result, he is in constant demand as a leadership speaker and his business focuses exclusively on speaking and running seminars.

Since his calendar fills up quickly, it is a good idea to book him early to avoid disappointment.

Deon lives in Johannesburg, South Africa and has presented at over 65 conferences and events in over ten countries. He is a prolific writer and has been widely published in many articles in traditional and electronic media.

Deon is a graduate of Henley Management College (UK) and the Graduate Institute of Management and Technology and the Port Elizabeth Technikon and holds qualifications in Public Relations, Strategic Management, Strategic Human Resources Management, Occupational Health and Safety and Governance, Risk and Ethics.

Deon formerly worked as an internal Organizational Behavior consultant and as a Small Business Adviser.
