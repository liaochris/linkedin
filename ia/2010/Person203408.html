Robert Cooney’s Summary

Having led transformational IT initiatives for more than 15 years for clients and employers ranging from Fortune 500 companies and governmental agencies to small businesses, I am now seeking my next opportunity. I am a previous business owner and leader who thrives on meeting challenging goals, and a technology specialist who excels at strategy and implementation.

My latest position was the owner of Corporate Technology, Inc., a service business that specialized in providing technology consulting and troubleshooting to clients nationwide. Highlights from my current and previous positions include:
• Increased annual revenues to $3/4 million, resulting in favorable positioning for corporate buyout.
• Planned and managed full-scale IS departmental migration of leading healthcare provider to ensure minimal service interruption.
• Delivered significant operational cost savings to major client by recommending and managing full-scale relocation initiative.
• Transformed technology infrastructure of multi-branch financial institution and contributed to expanded geographic presence through seamless project management and implementation of new branch.

I am a motivated and driven leader with a commitment to success. Possessing a combination of strong technical competency and exceptional business acumen, I provide guidance and vision that produces valuable results.
