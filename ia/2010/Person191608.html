Keith German’s Summary

Solution Explorers exists to transform the software technology buying and selling experience. I'm a broker for buyers - helping them clearly identify and document their needs and related costs, explore options that exactly meet their needs, and filter irrelevant information and solution providers from consuming my client's valuable time and other resources. For sellers, I offer a highly qualified prospect, detailed system requirements documentation, and if they qualify then introductions to the right buyer audience.

Solution Explorers, as an independent consultant, services its clients in the following ways:

- Assess needs and opportunities
- Explore technology system options
- Filter out irrelevant information and solutions
- Broker “short list” introductions
- Advise during presentations and agreement negotiations

These services will produce the following:
- Comprehensive understanding and agreement on the core business issues that create operational inefficiencies, hinder growth, and increase costs.
- Documentation from which a sound decision can be made on whether to proceed with a software investment.
- Time savings, by using industry research tools and experience designed to narrow solution options BEFORE sellers get access to buyer resources.
- Financial savings, both for buyers and sellers, by streamlining the process through turn-key programs so that buyers can minimize interruptions and data clutter and sellers can talk directly to buyers whose needs are pre-aligned to their solutions and services.

Buying and selling doesn't have to be a "rat race", instead it can be quite rewarding for everyone that is involved. Contact us today to learn more about how your business or organization can benefit from these proven best practices.
