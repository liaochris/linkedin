Deborah Reynolds’s Summary

Passage Yachts, Inc. is Northern California’s exclusive dealer for Beneteau sailing yachts and trawlers, Island Packet sailing yachts, and Alerion Express sailing yachts. We are also a full service brokerage for both sail and power boats. We are the largest boat dealer in Northern California, and the only boat dealer that has a fully staffed Service Department handling all phases of new boat commissioning, new and used boat repairs, annual services, and all phases of maintenance. We also provide concierge services for our clients who want a turn-key boat ownership experience where we handle all the maintenance and provisioning so their boat is ready to go anytime. We also handle boat financing and insurance placement for our clients buying boats.

We’re committed to growing boaters through our seminar series that include a variety of topics from diesel engine maintenance to sail trim to cruising destinations. Our staff are not only knowledgeable boaters, but professionals that know how to match the boat to a client’s sailing goals. Buying a boat is buying a relationship, and we want to make sure all our clients are happy and totally satisfied with their purchase. We want them to feel confident that they can count on us as resource for all their boating needs. We also know that there is no such thing as a "last boat" and have a guaranteed trade-in program on all the boats we sell, new or used. We're confident that the boats people buy from us will give them years of joyful boating and a good dollar value when it's time to sell or trade.

Our core business approach is that we sell only boats that represent true value. We believe that the boat lines we represent should be aesthetically beautiful, seaworthy, safe and well designed for their purpose — they're fun to sail and motor and handle well in all kinds of conditions.
