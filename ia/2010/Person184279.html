Scott Ganyo’s Summary

Entertainment:
Scott Ganyo is a veteran actor and award-winning producer in Los Angeles. A star of many films and improvisor at ComedySportz, Scott has recently been seen on ComedyCentral, Spike TV, and in the blockbuster film The Dark Knight. Scott is the owner of IMP Films.

Technological:
Scott has an exceptionally broad range of business and technology experience including distributed software, web technology, operations and marketing. He has successfully driven innovation and delivered outstanding results in every size of organization from startup to Fortune 500. A founder of eTapestry.com, Scott now provides his extensive enterprise computing expertise through Ganyo Enterprises LLC.
