VICE PRESIDENT OF CORPORATE REAL ESTATE & FACILITIES
Facilities Management / Corporate Real Estate / Construction Projects

President of Essential Corporate Services & Strategies, an evaluation and consulting specialist delivering essential Real Estate, Facilities, Construction and Project Management systems to all size businesses. Helping businesses operate just the level of Corporate Services they need with existing staff at the lowest possible cost rather then building complex, costly in-house structures.

Extensive global experience managing activities in Leasing, Facilities Management, Project Management, Construction and Asset Management Strategic Planning in multi-industry corporate environments. Adept at working with Corporations seeking meaningful cost control of facility operations, balanced allocation of scarce capital towards real facility needs, and to strategically outsource Corporate Services.


I have substantially contributed to each company that I have been associated. Highlights of my recent career include:
• Leased & renovated 141,000 square ft Headquarters project at Giralda Farms, Madison, NJ for Fortune 500 Healthcare Services provider. Established HQ quality Facilities staff and procedures utilizing Six Sigma tools to manage metrics measuring costs to industry standards while monitoring employee satisfaction & productivity.

• Leased & renovated 61,000 square ft Laboratory Project in New Delhi, India for Fortune 500 Healthcare Services provider.

• Accomplished 180+ surplus store disposition project for Leading Big-Box Retailer within 12 months with a $30+ million gain.

• Managed Real Estate portfolio for Fortune 500 Publisher representing 100+ locations & 5+ million square ft, including Project Management for 3 new warehouse/distribution center projects representing 1 million+ square feet.
