Gilbert Corrales’s Summary

Gilbert Corrales has over 8 years of experience working in high profile projects with industry heavy weights like Unisys, Intel and Schematic.

Most recently with the Research Division of Artinsoft, Gilbert collaborated with the launch of a developer oriented product and helped drive awareness of the company's offering to web developers around the world.

Gilbert started his profesional career as an intern at Unisys Center's of Excellence back in 2002 working with the automation of biometric identification documents.

In 2004 he joined Intel, where he collaborated on the automation of robotic-driven manufacturing processes and co-designed Shrek Fx, a research effort that enabled the rapid development and distribution of web-applications company wide.

Since then, Gilbert has collaborated in projects for companies such as HP, AvVenta Worldwide and Schematic, where Gilbert served as Senior Solutions Architect leading interdisciplinary teams for clients such as Time Warner, Target and Microsoft.

Parallel to his professional career Gilbert has taught undergrad courses on Human Computer Interaction and Object Oriented Programming at Cenfotec and Universidad Interamericana in Costa Rica, while also spoken at several conferences worldwide including Adobe MAX.

Currently, Gilbert resides in Seoul, South Korea where he is pursuing his Master Degree with an emphasis in Human Computer Interaction while researches at Korea Institute of Science and Technology. His main research area is Social Augmented Experiences with a focus in Mobile applications.
