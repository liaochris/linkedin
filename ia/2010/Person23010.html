Betty Luedke’s Summary

A requirements consultant, requirements analyst and team leader with over 26 years experience in the analysis/design/development of object-oriented, relational and hierarchical data systems for insurance, transportation, consumer finance, government, food/beverage, research, engineering, medical/healthcare and energy management organizations. A thorough and well-organized professional with demonstrated strengths in:
• Establishing sound requirement and change management practices for large organizations.
• Detailed/high level requirement analysis that produces testable requirements, definitive models and effective design.
• Data analysis and modeling for logical/business understanding and physical implementation.
• Team leadership in which communication is clear, roles/responsibilities are defined and the team is encouraged to solve challenges together.
• Facilitation of information gathering/problem resolution sessions that encourage a common understanding and identify actions/responsible parties required for the desired outcome.
• Development of comprehensive technical/procedural standards and innovative technical/concept instruction.
• Asset development and management to facilitate a viable and consistent approach.
