Jonathan Shapira’s Summary

I am an associate in the Boston office of Goodwin Procter LLP. I practice in the firm’s Business Law Department, and I focus on representing emerging high-tech, cleantech, and life sciences companies.

While attending law school, I was an intern at Israel Cleantech Ventures and served as a law clerk to Justice Ayala Procaccia of the Supreme Court of Israel.
