Brenda Neckvatal’s Summary

A progressive leader with 24 years of retail experience building a diverse business background ranging in Human Resources, management, web development, leadership development, training facilitation and development, and sales leadership.

My passion is to help people in reaching their accomplishments, analyzing and addressing the roots of their concerns, and assisting in formulating a resolution to their problems.

My leadership focus is on acknowledging employees for their contributions, help them develop their short term and long term goals, assist in identifying their career path, and I rely on their feedback to strive for continued organizational excellence.
