John Shapiro’s Summary

As one of the top young innovators driving new product development at Adobe and with a broad background spanning pre-revenue startups to Fortune 500 corporations, I am a software enthusiast with a stong engineering background and business mindset. Combining a Stanford CS degree with a Harvard Business School MBA provides a unique perspective and guides my product management leadership in the high-growth technology space.

At Adobe, I created the concept for what would later be branded Adobe Wave, a social, consumer web product targeted at website publishers to help them engage their users by displaying desktop notifications to generate traffic. Pitched concept, won executive approval, built up engineering efforts, led 12+ person team, and delivered the product to market as Adobe Wave beta on Adobe Labs.
