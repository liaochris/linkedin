Jennifer Stark’s Summary

Practicing law since 1985, initially from the defense perspective and recently primarily on the plaintiff's side. Currently focusing on representing clients and families in personal injury situations, including those involving medical and nursing home issues. We strive to assist small businesses and employees in employment matters, such as contract issues, termination, and discrimination.

A goal for the future is to assist more start up businesses, particularly those owned by women, to get them directed to resources in the community who can assist them in thriving, profiting and hopefully hiring from the large local pool of potential employees suffering in the current economic climate.
