Chris O'Hara’s Summary

Mr. O'Hara is currently SVP of Sales and Marketing at TRAFFIQ, where he leads its agency and publisher development efforts, and is responsible for marketing. TRAFFIQ is the online media industry's only free digital media management platform integrated seamlessly into a premium buying marketplace. Our comprehensive system is engineered to incorporate all components of the media planning & buying process, including automated RFP distribution, order execution, campaign optimization, and publisher reconciliation.

Recently, Chris was Senior Vice President of Reviewed.com, where he oversaw the sales operation for individual publications that included CamcorderInfo, DigitalCameraInfo, PrinterInfo, WirelessInfo, HeadphoneInfo, TelevisionInfo, CamcorderInfo.jp, MediaPlayerInfo, and more.

Prior to Reviewed.com, he was the Vice President of Sales for Mediabistro.com, the leading employment services provider in the Media space, where he was in charge of selling online advertising and sponsorships, business development, and developing custom interactive solutions. He successfully positioned the company's online advertising sales to assist in its eventual acquisition by Jupiter Media.

Prior to his role at Mediabistro, Chris was Associate Publisher of Nielsen's photo group, where he managed print, online media sales, and face-to-face media sales and ran a stock photography business.

He is an expert on transitioning traditional media companies into profitable new media enterprises, and frequently speaks at industry functions. He is a member of American Business Media's speakers bureau, and a contributor to Ad Club symposiums. He is also the author of 6 best-selling books including Great American Beer, and Hot Toddies, the classic holiday cocktail book, which is now being featured as a recipe card deck this fall in Williams Sonoma stores and online everywhere.
