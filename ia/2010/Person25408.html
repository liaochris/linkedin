Bob Fitzgerald’s Summary

After years and years of working in both large and small advertising agencies I came to a critical conclusion: the traditional way of doing things does not work. It is not just that everything moves faster nowadays. More than simply consumers being saturated with messaging. Beyond Tivo, blogs, and the boogie man de jour.

It is that somewhere along the line the point got lost. Marketing, advertising and design resources serve their clients for one purpose. To generate and deliver ideas.

REPUBLIC exists solely to create and deliver great ideas to our clients. We eliminate, as much as humanly possible, everything save the insight, the execution, the idea.

Bob Fitzgerald’s Specialties:

Collectively our partners, collaborators and employees have managed huge successful brands, won serious and silly awards and been "Big People" in agencies etc. Everyone also has seen enough to believe in a new way of doing things.

  • Creative Director/Founder

    REPUBLIC

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    REPUBLIC is a collaborative of professionals from every area of marketing and advertising expertise.

    REPUBLIC is not an advertising company. Not a branding company. Nor is REPUBLIC an interactive, production, planning, design or direct company. We are in the business of ideas. Our ideas impact on all areas of our clients’ communications.

    The basis for the idea: Years ago a revolution of sorts took place in the creative departments of several progressive advertising agencies. Art directors and copywriters began working together as one team. Up until that point the writer wrote the copy, delivered it to the art director and she put it in the layout. It was when they formed a team, working as one to form the idea and build the concept that creativity took a giant step forward. At this moment forces are driving another revolutionary step. More barriers are being taken down, the team is growing. And it is happening at REPUBLIC.
