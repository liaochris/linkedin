Dennis McCafferty’s Summary

As founder/president of DM Enterprises, I "put my clients on the fast track" when it comes to B2B/B2G/custom content in multiple platforms/formats. My clients have included IBM, Kroger, Lowe's, USAA, GM, Nationwide, MasterCard, Ritz-Carlton, Amtrak, American Legion and Ziff Davis. From corporate clients like these to trade associations to the top custom/trade/mainstream publications both in print and digital, DM Enterprises provides the very best in targeted and brand-building/customer-loyalty content. I'm open for new clients, and up for discussing returning to full-time employment as well.

Before launching my business, I’m served as a longtime senior editorial staffer for USA WEEKEND, the nation’s second-largest magazine with more than 22 million readers. You name the newsmakers/celebs -- Presidents Bush and Clinton, Clint Eastwood, Russell Crowe, Peyton Manning -- and I've likely had a nice chat with them. I also took a lead role in publicity/digital/social-media promotion and value-added partnerships:

* I initiated, negotiated and executed an ongoing partnership with Country Music Television that has resulted in immense national broadcast and online exposure with the launch of the USA WEEKEND Magazine Breakthrough Video of the Year Award at the CMT Music Awards for the past two years. The first broadcast was a ratings record-setter.

* I launched our Facebook fan page. As a result of continued efforts to leverage assets, I increased the number of ‘fans’ on our page to literally six times that of our top competitor, Parade magazine, without spending a dime on Facebook’s advertising program.

* I landed partnerships with wide-ranging, top brands such as NASCAR, Eons.com, Spin magazine and the World Sports Humanitarian Hall of Fame that has resulted in high-profile, beneficial exposure for both parties. Most recently, I launched one with bestselling author Bruce Feiler for his new, remarkable book, Council of Dads.
