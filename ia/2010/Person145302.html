Mike Ellow’s Summary

Over 17 years of executive sales/technical management and direct sales experience building high performance teams and delivering results. Passionate, driven sales leader who always wants to win and pushes a team to meet/exceed ever increasing corporate goals in revenue, bookings, margin and cash while providing a balanced customer perspective on the relevancy, applicability and quality of the company’s product/services delivered to the customer.

High integrity, hands-on manager who leads by example and excels at setting clear expectations for the team while developing a culture of accountability and lives by the motto “what gets measured gets improved”. A strong communicator and active listener who sets a conceptual vision and builds a pragmatic plan of execution.

Creative thinker, data and process-driven, detailed-oriented, operationally proficient with the ability to synthesize large amounts of data for use in the achievement of corporate strategies and sales campaigns and delivering on commitments.
