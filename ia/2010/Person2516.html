Adam Marchick’s Summary

Part of the Bain Capital Ventures team, making technology investments across the Unites States.

Prior: Part of early technical team for multiple start-ups and a few years building software for Siebel prior to Menlo Ventures. During my three years at Menlo, I focused on investing broadly in IT at all stages. At Menlo Ventures, I helped make investments in a number of companies at A, B and C-round stages. Always interested in meeting entrepreneurs and discussing new ideas.
