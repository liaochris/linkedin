Lalith L. Silva’s Summary

I’m a network design engineer, business development professional and a management accountant currently working as the Snr Technical Consultant at Cisco Systems Sri Lanka Office. I spend my days helping Sri Lankan Enterprises from large banks, manufacturing organisations to Government and Defense institutions on leveraging the networking for their businesses on and around Cisco network equipment and solutions.I have been with Cisco for almost 4 years now after joining as a Tech Consultant in 2005.

I joined Cisco after a stint of 4 years at Millennium IT, Sri Lanka , working as the Pre-sales Team Lead during the latter half and before that as the Systems Engineer. Millennium IT is a leading Systems Integrator in Sri Lanka having partnerships with global leading IT companies viz. Cisco, Sun , Oracle etc. As the Pre-sales Team Lead I was responsible for the pre-sales techincal activities of the networking unit and managing and expanding the the pre-sales team.

During my 8+ years in Networking Career,I was involved with small, medium to large scale networking projects in Sri Lanka and Maldives,including the MPLS,VOIP,ISP network building in Telcos, Network and infrastrcuture building and revamping for Banks, Manufaturing organisations and Governmet Organistions, leveraging the network infrastructure for Voice Video Data and Mobility, Consolidating the Data
Centres to support Virtualisation, Bringing in Unified Communication for better collaboration and tightening the these Network Infrastructures with Security.

I completed my MBA in IT in 2007. I passed out as a B SC (Eng) in Electronics and Telecom from University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka in 2001 and I went on to complete my Management Accountancy Examinations as well with Chartered Institue of Mangement Accountants,CIMA UK.
