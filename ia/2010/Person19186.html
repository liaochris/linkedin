Ayman van Bregt’s Summary

After my marketing studies I started working in the ICT-business always combining marketing and ICT-knowledge. I worked on projects for Microsoft, IBM, was over 2 years marketing manager at Sonera. After Sonera I was employed by Syntens for over 3 years as a consultant in the SME-market, focussing on strategy with issues like ICT and marketing. I was an entrepeneur in cooperation with partners for three years of Fresh Interactive (mobile marketing agency), U-Lab (internet agency) and The Buccaneers (advertising agency).


My goals are developing myself continously and keeping ahead of the crowd ;-)


I've developed myself as a driven entrepeneur with passion focussing on internet & new media, marketing & communications and entrepreneurship & innovation. Within Ayman || digital marketing | business concepts we focus on concept development and consultancy for the three areas above (http://www.ayman.nl).

I'm also the Author of Lex-i; The first Dutch Internet & Web 2.0 Lexicon: http://www.lex-i.nu.
