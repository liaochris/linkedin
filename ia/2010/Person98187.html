Jim Berrong’s Summary

Residential Real Estate Broker and Investor, Marketing-Sales Professional, and Design-Build Architect.

Jim has successfully helped people sell and buy their homes in northeast Texas since 2002. His clients include:
* First time buyers as well as
* Home owners who are moving up, down or new to the area.

He's expertise includes:
* Single family homes, town-homes to high-rise condominiums.
* Urban to suburban locations.

Jim is also a real estate investor and architect. His specialization is investors:
* Helping them find the best deals and
* Coaching new investors to help them create a profitable deal on their first investment.

Jim's strength is his passion to listen, ask the right questions, and help his clients achieve their needs and wants.

Jim's business model is built on helping his clients' make sound decisions and that they are happy and satisfied with the results.

Jim Berrong’s Specialties:

Helping Real Estate Investors:
* Find heavily discounted properties.
* Reposition properties for the most appropriate exit strategy.
* Be successful on each deal through coaching.
