Michael Yublosky, MBA, DIY Web Coach and Mentor’s Summary

I retired from ADT Security Services after 28+ years in sales/marketing. Its peaks and valleys consumed the majority of my adult life. I leave the every day touch of friends, associates and customer friends with reluctance. All of you mean a great deal to me. I will truly miss the times we shared together and the memories will linger.

However, it is time to pursue a new passion, my love of E-Marketing on the Web. It started several years ago when I put my wife Judy's Web business up in my spare time. I learned the intricacies of do it yourself on site Search Engine Optimization and off site marketing using free Social Media (Web 2.0) techniques.

My education and business experience enable me to view the Web's potential from a unique perspective. My curiosity and intuitiveness allow me to extend past the normal inquiry as I continue to ask the follow up question. I seek the best and most honest opinions from leading authority figures. It only took 2-1/2 years until the light bulb went on in my head as the clouds parted and sunshine flooded the arena.

Now I share what I learned with others through information, coaching and mentoring. I help self employed and small business owners face and defeat the current economic challenges. The Web is a level playing field where only the wise can profit; a small player's advantage is being nimble. But you must also overcome the same fear of the unknown I once had as well as the over abundance of information.

I personally lead you through the decision process one on one so it will be shortened considerably. I am also there to discuss things when you have a question. I head a DIY small business networking Meetup group in the NW Buffalo Grove area.
