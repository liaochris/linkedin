David Mullins’s Summary

Described many times as an inspiring serial entrepreneur and innovator. Goals which inspire creative thinking, innovation and a pragmatic approach. Skilled tradesman and professional. Safely saying, he has designed houses, bus, boat, aircraft and built them, now currently developing new technology for the next century.

A career doted rubbing shoulders with like minded entrepreneurs, innovators and hard workers. Leading into areas of advanced networks, communications, manufacturing and financial sectors, with companies like IBM, Odenberg, American Express, Intertanko, Maxtor, BR, Railtrack, Speis Hecker, and many others. Keenly aware of (networked) Internet commerce before its birth, led one of the first funded courses in Ireland and UK, instrumental in Internet Specialist Groups in Europe and early day teleworking Industries. Initiated topics of Internet Law and secure transaction. Pioneered Notes training, before IBM takeover and later partnered with IBM.

Sailing as much as possible and a solo Transat, transatlantic boat race at some stage.
