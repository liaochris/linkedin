Scott Godown’s Summary

I grew up in the construction industry. While growing up in Southern California, my Dad was a general contractor for swimming pools. He taught me, at a very young age, the importance of hard work. I was plumbing pools at the age of 14. I also learned every phase of the construction process through experience in the field. When I was 21, I started to learn about sales and design. I soon became the top producer in sales and earned several design awards in the industry. My Dad's work ethic rubbed off on me and I have been off to the races since.

I started my own pool company when I moved to Arizona from Southern California. We earned our excellent reputation by doing the right thing regardless of the situation. Honesty, integrity, trust, hard work, and passion for building are a few of the things that I prided our company on. Never cutting corners and always keeping the lines of communication open.

I'm grateful to be with Rising Revenue. David Antley (founder) holds the same strong morals and values that I have grown up with. We simply do the right thing no matter what. Being involved in the largest marketplace in the world holds countless opportunities. Assisting others in their marketing efforts is where we shine.
