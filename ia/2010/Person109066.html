Justin Dula’s Summary

I have a Master's degree in City and Regional planning from the Ohio State University. My Bachelor's degree is from Kalamazoo College in International Area Studies with an East Asian Concentration. I focused on transportation, as I feel it is fundamental in the shaping of communities. I think often there is an unnecessarily large a disconnect between the transportation field and the public. This led me to shift to a position working on general County policy which may allow for more direct interaction with the public. I feel planning is really about facilitating the public to meet their goals. I have some experience with Chinese language and culture that I would love to incorporate into an urban planning related job.
