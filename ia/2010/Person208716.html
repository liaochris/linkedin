Tiago Veloso’s Summary

After more than 15 years working in almost every kind of Tourism business, from off-shore catering in Angola to snow resorts in Austria and ecotourism in Brazil, the greatest conquest I’ve had was the full perception of the role of sustainable tourism in the regional development, no matter where in the world you are.

Managing delicate (and sometimes extreme) situations, working and leading people from such heterogeneous environments, learning to understand the local cultures and overcome difficulties unimaginable during our studying days at ESHTE, is something that not only enriches you professionally, but most important, makes you a better human being.

And that’s all Tourism is about. Knowing expectations, and exceed them with creativity and professionalism, trough a process of continuous business analysis and a profound understanding of the human condition in all its different dimensions.

As an independent Consultant, I also rely on a network of excellent companies that fully understand the need to help and stimulate (even teach) the small-medium entrepreneur in African and South America countries, especially in projects associated to the Tourism Industry. These partners attend to almost every step of any project or business, public or private, in the Industry, and are qualified to work with non-speaking Portuguese companies, making easy the collaboration with English, French, Spanish and German speaking partners.

We provide from automation & IT solutions, to architects and building companies, from full-size international ad campaigns to the small business e-commerce solution, from real-estate to LEED certification, and much more.

In my role as facilitator and solutions provider, I’m looking to establish long-term business partnerships with other companies/ investors around the world, that have businesses or at least some level of interest in the regions I’ve mentioned. Any additional information, please, feel free to contact me.

Tiago Veloso’s Specialties:

Long-Term Business Strategy, Risk & Crisis Management, Sustainable Tourism projects, DMOs Public Policies and Promotion, Intelligence Marketing (including Market Research), Branding Solutions, Concept Management, CRM, BSC, BI, BPM and Workflow solutions applied to the Travel & Tourism industry (including Hotels and Restaurants), LEEDS green building certification, Offshore Catering Logistics, Golf & Hotel investment analysis and general business opportunities in Brazil, Angola and Portugal.
