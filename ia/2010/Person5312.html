Alan Wolk’s Summary

One of the only new voices to come out of the creative side of the ad business, Alan Wolk has staked out a distinctive space for himself. In his new role as Managing Director of Social Media Strategy for KickApps Corporation, Wolk combines his common-sense approach to strategy with his hands-on experience as an advertising creative director and strategist. It's a combination that has made him the go-to guy for digital thought leadership, speaking and consulting.

His blog, The Toad Stool, is a popular thought leadership site that’s been described as a “frank but fair” look at the confluence of advertising, marketing and the social web.

Adweek Editor Brian Morrissey has called it “one of the ‘must read’ blogs for our industry” and it’s been selected for Guy Kawasaki’s exclusive Alltop blog aggregation site.

The most popular series, “Your Brand Is Not My Friend,” which deals with the false assumptions marketers make in the social space (and how to remedy them), has gotten much play in the blogosphere and that has led to columns in Ad Age and Adweek as well as numerous speaking engagements.

Wolk is also the founder and guiding force behind the Hive Awards for the Unsung Heroes of the Internet, a new award show that debuted at SXSW Interactive in March 2010. Aimed at all the coders, programmers, user experience designers, information architects, content strategists and the like, the show rewards the people who do most of the heavy lifting but rarely get any of the credit. The show attracted a sellout crowd over over 400 people live, with close to 3,000 watching live online.

Wolk’s stature in the digital community has also lead to his being named an Advisory Board member for Social Media Week.
