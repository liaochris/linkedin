Susan C. Mathews’s Experience

  • Owner

    Stillwater Studio

    (Graphic Design industry)

    February 1995 — Present (15 years 8 months)

    Graphic design, art direction, project management for print and new media. From highly-illustrated tome to identity package, local to international clients.

  • Treasurer

    The Graphic Artists Guild

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    September 1989 — July 2008 (18 years 11 months)

    Serving on the Guild national board as Treasurer since 2004, I have served on the national board in various capacities for over a decade. As Treasurer, responsible for organizational budget, financial planning, and reporting.

    Previous to that, served as Albany Chapter membership chair, tech group chair, newsletter designer, national representative, and finally, Chapter President from 2000-2004.

  • President

    Graphic Artists Guild Albany Chapter

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    July 2000 — July 2004 (4 years 1 month)

    As president of the local chapter, established agenda for chapter based on the Guild mission and goals. Chaired monthly board meetings, presided at events, developed board and volunteers, represented the chapter at national board meetings.

  • Sr. Art & Design Supervisor

    Thomson Delmar Learning

    (Public Company; Graphic Design industry)

    1986 — 1995 (9 years )

    Thomson Delmar Learning (previously Delmar Publishers, currently Cengage) hired me as design coordinator in 1986. Responsibilities increased gradually, culminating in promotion to senior art/design supervisor. Responsibilities included creating or hiring freelancers to create covers for 200 textbooks annually (at peak), text design, photo shoots, management of budgets, input on project funding, equipment purchase, hiring part- and full-time employees, selection and oversight of interns, teaching new hires, and educating other departments on the art/ design/ production process.

  • Art Director

    National Trade Publications

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry)

    April 1984 — April 1986 (2 years 1 month)

    Started as paste-up artist and was promoted over time to Art Director. Responsible for overall design of publications, cover concepts and photo shoots for three monthly trade magazines.
