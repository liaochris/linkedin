Andrew Rose’s Summary

Andrew oversees the marketing, business development, strategic alliances / channel distribution, and direct sales for Naden/Lean, LLC, a CPA firm based in Timonium, Maryland. An innovative marketing authority, Andrew is frequently asked to speak to groups about Cold-Calling Techniques, Business Development, Vertical Marketing Strategies, Marketing without a Budget, Tactical Joint Marketing, Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Social Media and Networking. Andrew is also deeply involved in the non-profit community, sitting on or advising several non-profit boards. Andrew has had excellent results in founding and chairing various peer support and networking/referral based groups. He founded the Marketing Director’s Support Group in 2003, a well respected, educational forum for marketing executives in the Baltimore region. Andrew is a dedicated outdoor enthusiast who currently relishes in summiting 14ers in Colorado.
