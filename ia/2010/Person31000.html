Abbas Zaidi’s Summary

My goal is to streamline any situation I encounter. I have primarily worked as a consultant with a wide-ranging background in numerous corporate environments and management structures. I have served as a Project Manager at client sites, as a Program Developer as well as Program Manager (for efforts as significant as Y2K Remediation at Fannie Mae,) a Corporate Trainer and instructor, and have always been a problem solver, resulting in numerous accolades from clearly improved client situations.

A recent background in Technical Writing & Training Development enables me to enrich today's discerning clientele with well-rounded skills.

8+ years as a solutions architect (meaning great communication ability,) saw me always placed at the front of market-development efforts. I am great at mentoring resources & Relationship Management, and have always been put in challenging situations, where I identify breakdowns and start with relationships in order to overcome issues.

My diverse background is my greatest strength: 5 years of experience in FrameMaker-based single-sourced documentation, & Oracle-based app servers for which I've had to maintain my own environments & play the DBA role. 2+ years of experience in NovaSoft (NovaManage) Workflow & EDMS, & Vignette StoryServer (V/5), & 1+ year with ColdFusion (mostly for RAD), and XDB (predecessor to IBM's UDB,) for a massige transaction processing application for which I created the robust front-end & UI design. Add to that a broad sprinkling of SaaS & other applications suites, & one can see how I can handle any set of roles.

I have taught and effectively managed every conceivable audience. Applying lessons from strategic vision is a strength which I use to examine an industry from a variety of perspectives. I identify issues by asking questions, focusing on developing relationships, and clearly declare my goals/plans up front, always engaging clients by welcoming feedback.
