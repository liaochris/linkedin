Steven Moore’s Summary

As evangelist for EcoDiscoveries, my role is to build and execute on strategies for creating, partnering, educating, sustaining, and growing our communities of "Green inspired" vendors,partners,educators,retailers, distributors, customers and prospects through the most effective channels and means available. We add value to our communities through listening, learning,and collaborating with them to meet and exceed their mission and goals by producing the products or services they need.

We focus on development and implementation of break-through products, system development, and innovation through the continuous search of needs and desires of the green community. We also bring the new technology breakthroughs to bear and ask our communities- "What can we do with this?"

If you have a need or desire to create change in your business through the adoption of green technologies let's see if we can help you? We have created great green cleaning products but we can do a lot more just ask.
