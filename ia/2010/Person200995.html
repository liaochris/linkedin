Teresa Flowers’s Summary

A dynamic senior level professional with 20 years of experience managing multi-million dollar initiatives within a broad range of industries working across multiple organizational entities and functional areas in enterprise environments. Teresa’s daily focus is on improving operations and functions, increasing line of business growth, providing processes for strategic transformation of the business, and satisfying customer and stakeholder requirements.

As a seasoned LEAN Six Sigma certified SME and strategic leader of Business Process Improvement, Enterprise Project Management, she is organized and strives to exceed customer satisfaction EVERY time. Teresa encourages fact-base exchange of information where colleagues work in a spirit of team leadership, positive collaboration, and open communications. For internal/external customers, she determines how to best achieve effectiveness and increasing company value, while assessing strengths and weaknesses, building consensus, managing change, and identifying ways to boost revenues and reduce waste functionally and operationally.

Experienced working with entrepreneurial start ups to larger Fortune 100 corporations, Teresa posses professional maturity and genuine analytical abilities when dealing with high performance teams and managing the oversight and direction of small to large scale initiatives. Her ability to build relationships by providing a Win-Win environment for functional organizations is an asset to C-level executives. Taking on this win-win approach, Teresa incorporates value-added knowledge and understanding of due diligence practices bridging the gaps in complex initiatives or difficult situations. As a result, her leadership style integrates strategies that are cost-effective, team-centric, delivering high performance business and technical solutions to meet and exceed ‘voice-of-the-customer’ expectations.
