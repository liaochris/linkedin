Roger Salvati’s Summary

Cypress Business Partners, LLC is the exclusive distributor for Altapure Health in California and Nevada. Our mission and purpose is to help healthcare facilities see and decide that dedicated implementation of the Altapure technology will reduce their HAI rates and make their facilities safer for everyone.

Altapure has developed a high level disinfection technology that provides safe and reliable treatment of large spaces, equipment and electronics independent of humidity and temperature. Healthcare facilities battling devastating infectious pathogens like MRSA and C. Diff now have a safe and efficient weapon for eliminating these threats.

The Altapure System is non-corrosive, leaves no residue, is EPA approved and its' end products are green. Treated rooms can be re-occupied in 30 minutes. The systems will eradicate bacteria, spores and fungi in less than 10 minutes. When "Complete Kill" is required, Altapure is the clear solution.
