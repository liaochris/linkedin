Justin Horvath’s Summary

I began my customer service experience by working as a supervisor at the Kirkland Costco warehouse. After three years with Costco I left to join HouseValues Inc. (now known as Market Leader Inc.).
I started as an account executive and executed corporate sales plans by cold calling into targeted and potential subscribers and meeting monthly sales quotas. This is where I expanded my knowledge of the sales process and learned what it took to maintain high closing percentages on both cold and warm marketing leads.
Just five months after beginning my employment with HouseValues I changed roles to what was called a Customer Success Coach. Here I was responsible for on-boarding new agents and assisting in their account set-up. I provided long-term support and coaching on not only how to use HouseValues CRM tool but also how to build a stronger internet presence and better portray themselves to their clientele. Another large aspect of this role was dealing with requests from agents to cancel HouseValues's services, this would include negotiations as well as contract revisions.

My goals now are to further expand my knowledge within the sales process. Currently I am working as an account executive at DataSphere inc. helping business owners connect with local consumers through online advertising.
