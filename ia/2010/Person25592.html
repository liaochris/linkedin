Bob Nestor’s Summary

Product and Business Development Professional

Entrepreneur, niche marketer, product manager and successful salesman for healthcare and IT companies both locally and nationwide.

Have 20 years of direct selling experience to Fortune 100 and Fortune 500 companies, 14 years of Marketing experience, and 6 years of healthcare experience. Over my career have sold Information Technology products, consulting services and large enterprise based decision support software systems on a local and national basis. Entrepreneur for over 8 years building a startup Information Technology Company into a profitable entity in year one in a vertical market (Creative Arts and Graphic Design). Reinvented myself and took on the challenging industry of healthcare and was ultimately successful with the sales of large-scale enterprise decision support tools to healthcare providers that included large IDN’s, physician offices and ambulatory surgery centers on a nationwide basis. After 4 years of successful sales, was promoted to Director of Product Development for this healthcare company where I managed Product Managers, Application Support Specialists, Data Processing Analysts and worked in conjunction with IT developers in the design, development and support of (5) software products that are available to a national audience.
