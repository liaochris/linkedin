Adam Perle’s Summary

The full scope of my responsibilities as Vice President of the Princeton Regional Chamber of Commerce include: oversight of operations for the Princeton Regional Chamber of Commerce Foundation; direction of the Princeton Regional Convention and Visitors Bureau; enhancing Membership Recruitment with the utilization of the Chamber's Amabassador Committee; coordination of the Princeton Leap program; website and information technology management; and other tasks. Recently, the Chamber's work with the Princeton Regional Convention and Visitors Bureau has gained statewide and regional notice for its impressive efforts and production.
