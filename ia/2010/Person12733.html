Andy Person’s Summary

I work for a private institution that is a member of a select group of schools (Service Opportunity College Consortium) asked to educate the leaders of our U.S. armed forces.

These leaders range from seasoned veterans to junior enlisted, but they all have one thing in common. They are all driven to advance their education and military resume by earning their undergraduate and graduate degree.

The name of my institution is Mercy College. We are an accredited, 4-year, private college that enables students to choose from over 90 undergraduate and graduate degree choices. Unlike most colleges, our degree programs are designed to meet the needs of even the most demanding military schedule in an affordable, flexible, and individual approach with special emphasis on distance learning and service relevant programs in homeland security and health services.

To those who serve our country, I want to thank you in the best way I know how, through the gift of education.
