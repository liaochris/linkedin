Martin Miglioretti’s Summary

Martin Miglioretti is a senior-level creative professional with substantial experience in creative strategy development, graphic design, art direction and copywriting for advertising and branding programs in print and digital media.

"I’m a business artist. My aim is to develop simple, dramatic visual and verbal imagery that exerts a powerful and irresistible effect on the viewer. I believe the intuitive aspects of a design strategy are as important as the logical elements. When we speak to someone at a comfortable emotional and intellectual level, the message is more apt to be retained."

Martin Miglioretti’s Specialties:

Martin has produced compelling, award-winning visual concepts in print, outdoor and direct mail advertising, brochures, logos and corporate identity, websites, invitations and e-vites, PowerPoint epics, exhibits and displays, and other marketing and event promotions.

Additional Information

Martin Miglioretti’s Interests:

In addition to Martin’s remarkable design and writing talent, he is an accomplished photorealist painter, specializing in automotive subjects in acrylic on canvas. He is also a talented blues singer and historian. Among his other creative pursuits, he worked in film production as a special makeup effects artist in the 1980s. Martin hails from Pittsburgh, PA. He and his wife Lori live in the River Oaks area of Houston.

Martin Miglioretti’s Groups:

Martin served four years on the board of the Art Directors Club of Houston, including two years on the executive committee as Public Relations Coordinator. He also helped helped charter the Houston Blues Society and served on their board for 2 years.
