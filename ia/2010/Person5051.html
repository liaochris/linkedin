Alan Chain, MBA’s Summary

My motivation is guiding individuals that are reaching for the stars, and providing each the tools to achieve and succeed. Being a professional with a passion for forward thinking, exceptional customer service, and understanding what the customer wants, and then deliver! . With a passion for workplace learning and performance, classroom facilitation, and a successful management career, I have a proven track record providing quality and quantity within Fortune 500 companies.

Logistics, Operations Management,Manufacturing, and most recently in the Gaming and Casino Management industry, are the industries that have benefited from my experience. Areas of Expertise (AOE's) include, Course Development, Lean Manufacturing, Lean Sigma, and 5S.

I have successfully steered a Safety Committee of 15 employees, facilitated New Hire Orientation, Train-the-Trainer programs, driving decreases in organizational health and wellness claims; improving human performance; measuring and evaluating training programs, while ensuring alignment with company goals and performance metrics; e-learning development with the use of MS Office, and computer-based training programs. Human Resource experience including recruiting,soft-skills training, and selecting and retaining top talent.
