Award winning Art Director with fourteen years experience designing for the web. In that time I have produced everything from online advertising, websites, microsites, trade show video installations, eLearning applications, iPhone applications, widgets, print marketing, CMS backends, online gaming, Facebook applications, kiosk interfaces, Flash and After Effects motion design to short films and audio design.

I’ve had the pleasure of working with over a hundred brands including Nike, Sony, Nokia, Hewlett-Packard, Subaru, Chase, ESPN, Hanes, Purex, Microsoft, Champion, Verizon, Verizon Wireless, Wells Fargo, Charles Schwab, Citi Bank, Loreal, SC Johnson and Avaya.

I have also freelance Art Directed for companies such as Sapient, Draft FCB, Atmosphere BBDO, McGarryBowen, Razorfish and Kadium. I understand and enjoy the process from brainstorming to launch.

Aside from creating interactive design experiences, I founded and am the Creative Director for a publishing company and the street photography magazine I Heart. My camera’s always loaded.

I love what I do and love new challenges, challenge me.
