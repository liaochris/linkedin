Mark Alan Robinson, MBA, LEED AP’s Experience

  • Green energy broker

    GREEN POWER 4 TEXAS

    (Partnership; Oil & Energy industry)

    November 2006 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    We are Texas' first & most economical electricity aggregator, broker, consultant & distributor focusing solely on green energy.

    We added this business unit because electricity represents building owners' largest operating expense, & many of our clients were not receiving fair, transparent pricing from other brokers, whose commissions were 2-5 times higher than ours.

    Additionally, we became a leading green power broker because purchased electricity typically accounts for roughly 90% of a building's potentially regulated carbon footprint, i.e., Scope 1 & Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions. Our green business & green building clients hadn't realized that approaching carbon neutrality could be so cost effective, simple & strategically significant.

    In 2009, we also became a registered REC broker, & added onsite solar & wind generation to our service offering.

    With our clients' green power purchases having surpassed $10 million, we are excited to be securing a healthy, sustainable Texas.
