Alan Furley’s Summary

ISL specialises in recruiting professionals with a range of expertise, including areas such as project / programme management, business transformation, finance and IT on a permanent, contract, and interim basis.

ISL has recently been awarded a place on the Buying Solutions Framework Agreement to supply temporary (interim and contract) and fixed term staff, including board level roles, to NHS Trusts and organisations throughout the public sector. Buying Solutions is the national procurement partner for UK public services and is an Executive Agency of the Office of Government Commerce in the Treasury.


Driven by over 30 years of experience in the industry, ISL adopts a 360° consultative approach to recruitment. We work closely with both our clients and candidates to understand their needs and expectations, ensuring the best results are achieved for both parties.

It is not simply about sending CVs at ISL; it is about identifying and implementing the best recruitment solution available, no matter the size or market sector of the business, or whether it is a single vacancy or an entire staffing campaign.

Through industry knowledge, expertise and constant research and development, ISL’s key to success is in appointing the right individual first time, every time, through clear, transparent and robust recruitment solutions.

I have worked within the UK recruitment market for 8 years, and in that time I have worked on an incredible variety of roles. With the experience I have and the environment we operate in at ISL, I am confident that I would provide a valuable resource whether you are looking to fill gaps in your organisation, make the next move in your career, find a new interim assignment, or simply speak to someone who can offer some impartial advice.

I am always interested in developing my business by building good quality relationships – please contact me to discuss in more detail how we may be able to help each other in the future.
