Michelle L. Long, CPA’s Summary

As the owner of Long for Success, LLC, I specialize in QuickBooks consulting and training and helping others start and grow their own business!

As one of the few national speakers for Intuit (the company that makes QuickBooks), I have presented numerous seminars at conferences for accounting professionals. I have written and recorded numerous courses for Intuit Academy and I'm a consultant for Intuit on a product development team. I served as the technical editor for Laura Madeira's book--QuickBooks 2009 Solutions Guide.
