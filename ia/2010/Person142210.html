Michael Chinnici’s Summary

"I enjoy exciting challenges... turning dreams into reality, and bringing the next great idea to the world."

Michael has been a Professional Photographer, Creative Director and Designer for over 30 years. He founded ad agencies, Chinnici Direct (est. 1982) and Scuderia O2 (est. 2005), and NYC’s first digital photography studio, Icon Digital (est. 1996), where he was CEO and Creative Director. He is the owner of Red Octopus, a virtual digital design agency, and Photo Workshop Adventures, both based out of New York.

Michael Chinnici’s Specialties:

Visionary, Designer, Strategist, Inventor & Photographer. Specializing in Design, Creative, Brand Building, Photography, Film, Direct Marketing, Finanical Services Marketing, Motorsports Marketing, Business Development, and Technology.
