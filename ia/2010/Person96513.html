Jerry Monkman’s Summary

For the last fifteen years I have worked as a freelance nature, travel, and adventure photographer, creating stock imagery as well as working on assignment for editorial, commercial, and non-profit conservation clients. With my wife Marcy, I have written and photographed six books about New England. I also lead a few multi-day photography tours and workshops each year to locations in New England. I was recently elected to the board of directors of the North American Nature Photography Association for a two year term, ending in 2012.

Jerry Monkman’s Specialties:

Outdoor photography, documenting conservation issues, photographing adventure activities in remote (and not so remote) locations, photographing kids in the outdoors, writing, teaching digital imaging techniques.
