Erik Kabik’s Summary

Photography, Graphic Design, Advertising, Marketing, Public Relations and Sales are skills that Erik Kabik has combined throughout his career and his multiple entrepreneurial ventures dating back to 1990.

Working as a professional photographer since 1990, Erik is one of the longest running event, celebrity , architectural and concert shooters in Las Vegas today. His eye for live music and capturing iconic public figures has landed his photos on the pages of magazines such as Rolling Stone, Maxim, Esquire and People. Erik's Photo clients include Universal Records, Live Nation, AT&T, Access West, 20th Century Fox, Hard Rock Hotel and House of Blues. Erik is on staff at RETNA, one of the worlds largest independent photo agencies that just celebrated it's 30th anniversary. His work can be seen at http://www.erikkabik.com and can be found internationally on a daily basis in entertainment, news, sports, music and architecture magazines, web sites and newspapers.

Erik has also been providing world class design and advertising services to Casino properties all over the United States since 2005. Some of his clients include MGM/Mirage, Wynn Las Vegas, Atlantis Bahamas, Pala Casino Resort and Spa, Venetian Resort, Horseshoe Casino & Harrah's properties. In 2009, Erik founded and also operates Blackstone Creative Services llc. and now offers a full menu of advertising design services to Gaming and NON-Gaming clients. See the work at http://www.bcslasvegas.com

Erik founded and had been operating Las Vegas Stock llc., the worlds largest Gaming image stock photo collection, since 1998. For over 10 years Erik worked side by side with the top Casino Properties and Advertising Agencies in the US helping them plan, select, create and execute images into their advertising materials.

Erik Kabik combines his experiences to give his clients a broad range of services and promises the highest quality customer satisfaction time and time again in all areas of his expertise.
