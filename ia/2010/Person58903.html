Doug Heath’s Summary

DJH Consulting brings more than 28 years of corporate experience in Business/Community Advocacy, Finance, Human Resources, Labor Relations, Engineering, Executive Coaching, Quality Control and Compliance capacities. During those 28 years many valuable relationships have been established that will be accessed to assist with any project.

Our processes and materials provide rapid results and positive, measurable outcomes. They are tailored to help your company achieve its objectives of improving performance and driving the company’s organizational values and principles.

Our proven processes and world class facilitation enable us to provide our clients with powerful solutions for successful growth. What makes DJH & Associates Consulting unique is our inside-out, development process which focuses on individual and organizational development. This proven process, along with our extensive experience gained, while working with clients and their challenges, enables us to provide our clients with outstanding solutions that are focused on achieving maximum results.

When we accept a client engagement, we accept the responsibility of getting you measurable results, not just delivering training sessions. We will tailor a process and develop supporting material that will act as a catalyst for the development of a focused effort to ensure the entire organization realizes results.

Further, we will provide the materials and insight that will focus on the individual responsibility for organizational results and sustained growth and improvement.

Doug Heath’s Specialties:

DJH Consulting’s background in executive coaching, leadership, human resources, benefit planning/administration, and financial management uniquely positions us to provide a full complement of financial services to individuals and businesses.
