Samuel Banks’s Summary

Mr. Banks currently heads Catalyst Communications as President. As Catalyst's President Mr. Banks has developed innovative advertising solutions combining cutting edge technology and traditional Out-Of-Home media.

Mr. Banks began his career in Information Technology more than 18 years ago working for firms such as Intel, Kaiser Permanente, Dell, Restaurant Technologies Incorporated, and InstallShield in the areas of logistics planning, sales development, project development, joint venture structuring, strategic planning, quality assurance/quality Improvement, and enterprise deployment planning and execution.
Mr. Banks founded Peace Communications to provide strategic information technology solutions including but not limited to: eBusiness website development, search engine optimization, web application development, remote helpdesk services, IP television in airports and general IT infrastructure design and systems integration. Peace has provided solutions to multi-billion dollar organizations like SERCO and many government agencies such as Cobb County, City of Chicago, Gwinnett County and more.
While Mr. Banks was working for Restaurant Technologies as a Director of Quality Assurance he directed the development of the largest .NET restaurant accounting software in the market. Clients gave high praise for the reliability of the software and ease of deployment over the web.
