  • President /General Manager

    NewsTalk 890 WLS-AM Chicago

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    1995 — 2004 (9 years )

    Restored an ailing 50,000 watt WLS to profitability by increasing revenue by almost 100% within his first year after the station experienced negative cash flow for 10 years straight prior to his leadership. The station went on to ratings dominance within a few short years and rose from a 17th ranked position to a top four ranking in one of the most competitive talk radio markets in the country. Jones reengineered the programming line up including the most successful talk duos in the market ,The Roe and Garry Show. This show took a 23rd ranked PM drive ranking to #1 among persons 12+, Adults 25-54, and 35-64 within 5 years. The long standing morning show "Don Wade & Roma" enjoyed their best ratings performance ever and became one of Chicago’s most successful morning shows.
