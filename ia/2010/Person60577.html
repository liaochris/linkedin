Rick Erickson’s Summary

I just joined a friend, Paul Wember and his firm, Wember Inc. as the division leader for the assessments required for the acquisition, rehabilitation, renovation or facility management requirements of any type of commercial real estate. Using my value engineering capabilities and years of assessment experiences to provide quality consulting and assessment services, Paul and I will combine services to offer new and existing clients the best in Owner's Representation Services available.

Services continue to include lender required Physical Needs Assessments, Capital Needs Assessments, ADA Barrier surveys and other inspection services including acquisition assessments, code compliance reviews, renovation budgeting, value engineering , subcontractor selection and project management … to name a few.
