Grant Folkard’s Summary

Grant is an accomplished sales and marketing professional with over two decades of progressive experience and a successful record of developing both direct and indirect business opportunities in technology sectors across North America and around the world.

Most recently, Grant’s career has focused on channels development - including program creation, partner recruitment and revenue generation. He has worked with Distributors, LARs/DMRs and VARs/SIs. His keys to success include an ability to understand the partner’s business model and helping them maximize their profitability.

Previously, Grant held end-customer facing sales roles with Large Account Resellers (LARs) and Value-Added Resellers (VARs), achieving and exceeding sales targets while selling both hardware and software solutions. His success resulted from the creativity and effort required to uncover new business plus the ability to retain and grow existing accounts.

A results oriented team leader with expertise in consultative sales, Grant is a strategic thinker with an intrapreneurial spirit and a proven ability to drive company (and partner) revenues.
