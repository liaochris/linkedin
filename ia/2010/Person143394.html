Michael R. H. Stewart’s Summary

"Give me faith, freedom, resources, and a little time ... and I will make things happen that matter. Michael Stewart, 2010."

Michael Stewart is a respected Internet executive with broad experience in all aspects of online business, with an emphasis on social media development, and company management. He enjoys 19 years of direct experience with corporate, entrepreneurial, governmental and non-profit clients, having advised them on all aspects of their online initiatives.

Prior to his Internet career, he served as a Senior Vice President of AIG Marketing, (during AIG's period of world prominence), doing business in 135 foreign countries as well as the United States. Mr. Stewart is an experienced public speaker and communicator, with worldwide experience; an expert on corporate branding; an accomplished writer, a creative thinker and problem solver.

Michael R. H. Stewart’s Specialties:

social media (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, FriendsFeed), social media campaign development and implementation, social media analytics, social media web design,
web design (WordPress, All Adobe software), digital strategy, ROI analytics, consumer and B2B marketing,
online marketing, strategic planning, business plan development, non-profit advising, branding, public speaking, professional writing, convention speaking, motivational speaking.
