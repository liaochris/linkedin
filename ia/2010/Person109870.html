Kai D. Wright’s Summary

As an award-winning strategist, Kai uses his 5+ years of experience to help build bridges between consumers & Fortune 100 companies. He is passionate about the digital space and joined Organic in 2008 to play an active role in creating exceptional consumer experiences for Bank of America and P&G.

His current role in strategy spans account planning and business consulting allowing him to advise clients about channel strategy, multi-channel integration, and social media. In 2010, he won a Gold Ogilvy award as well as a Grand Ogilvy award for research innovation - both from the Advertising Research Foundation (ARF).

Prior to joining Organic, Kai was an associate at Ogilvy & Mather. At Ogilvy, he was responsible for consumer research and insights for IBM, SAP and Johnson & Johnson. He was a member of Ogilvy’s Employee Advisory Council working with senior management to introduce more diversity into the advertising industry; helping earn the agency accolades from the American Advertising Federation for its efforts.

Before Ogilvy, he spent three years in nonprofit at the Collegiate Scholars Program. In his role as Assistant Program Manager & Program Advisor, he was responsible for managing an entrepreneurship program sponsored by the Goldman Sachs Foundation and the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. Duties included day-to-day program management, grant reporting, and program evaluations.

As a former military brat, Kai considers himself a global citizen. To date, he has lived in the US, Germany & France. Italy is his favorite country. In his spare time, he maintains a marketing blog with a Gen Y perspective and teaches marketing at a charter high school.

He holds a master's degree in Strategic Communications from Columbia University and a bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Chicago.
