Francoise Luca’s Summary

A seasoned marketing and business professional who gets projects done.

Francoise has held high-level marketing positions with municipal programs, television affiliates, advertising agencies, and national companies like AT&T. Through her years of experience, she has implemented new marketing strategies to solve business problems and generate and improve results. From launching new products, to lead generation programs, to customer segmentation models that drove offer strategy and customer experience, to award winning advertising campaigns and loyalty programs, Francoise creates innovative solutions through practical uses of the latest technologies, on budget and on time.

As a marketing consultant, Francoise focuses on developing client’s core competencies with effective marketing strategies, staying within client’s budgets and achieving business objectives. An excellent communicator and loyal team player, Francoise approaches each project with infectiously high energy and a winning attitude. Francoise works exclusively by referral and only with a select client base, thus providing individualized service, unique solutions and results.
