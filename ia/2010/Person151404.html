Nando Costa’s Summary

I’ve worked in various fields of design, ranging from creating traditional print material, to building bold interactive animated experiences and directing a multitude of TV spots and other types of complex animations for broadcast and other medias.

My experience also ranges from working as a freelance director, to creative directing at an advertising agency and owning and managing my own design firm.

Some of my side projects have involved curating and publishing two books in the field of design and illustration, speaking at over a dozen design related events and conferences in the U.S., Europe and South America as well as exhibiting artwork at over 20 different events.
