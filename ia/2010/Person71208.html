Frank Mack’s Summary

Frank R. Mack has 20 years of experience as a private equity special situations investor, and interim manager & management consultant for distressed and under-performing companies and in operational, financial and investment & loan monetization capacities. He has acted as a Chief Restructuring Officer, Interim Chief Executive Officer, Court Appointed Receiver and Plan Monitor, as an Assignee on Assignments for the Benefit of Creditors, and testified as an expert witness on quantifications of damages and other matters in US Federal and International Courts.

Frank is a frequent speaker at professional conferences and universities, is a published author, and has been quoted in international publications on special situations investing, change management, bankruptcy/insolvency and international topics.

Frank Mack’s Specialties:

Equity Sponsor Rep, Hedge Fund Rep, Creditor Rep, Debtor Rep, Bankruptcy, Special Situations / Distressed Investing, Manufacturing & Distribution, Corporate Renewal, Financial Advisor, Restructuring, Strategic Planning, Trustee-Examiner, Workouts, Mid - Late Decline

Frank Mack’s Experience

  • Senior Managing Director

    AS Capital Partners, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    June 2010 — Present (5 months)

    Frank R. Mack is the lead investment professional for Accretive Solutions Capital Partners, Inc. (“AS Capital Partners”), a wholly owned subsidiary of Accretive Solutions. AS Capital Partners provides private committed investment capital in certain special situations and turnaround scenarios, targeting American-based middle-market companies with $50M - $250M+ in revenues.

    AS Capital Partners targets investments in the $10 to $30 million range per transaction, with an average holding period of three to five years. Investments are typically structured through DIP loans, first or second lien term loans, and convertible and mezzanine loans that provide for warrants or other equity options. AS Capital Partners also invests via preferred equity securities and/or purchases of direct equity. AS Capital Partners capital may be offered to existing clients of Accretive Solutions’ in scenarios where the client requests and qualifies for such an investment, and existing parties-in-interest consent to it.

    AS Capital Partners focuses on industries with manageable technology risks such as: aerospace/defense components; automotive, truck, motorcycle, recreational and performance vehicle parts; building materials; business/consumer services; consumer products; commercial capital equipment products; food products, healthcare services, power infrastructure, packaging products and specialty chemicals. Industries not described above may be considered depending on specific investment considerations.

    AS Capital Partners possesses committed capital, its team has been investing private equity for over 15 years with over 100 transactions closed, is designed to respond quickly to difficult situations, and its professionals receive economics upon closing and on the back-end of the investment.

    Refer to www.accretivesolutions.com for more information.

  • Senior Managing Director

    Accretive Solutions

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    June 2010 — Present (5 months)

    Frank R. Mack leads Accretive Solutions' Corporate Renewal & Restructuring practice and AS Capital Partners, Inc. out of Accretive Solutions' New York, NY office. Both entities leverage Mack’s extensive expertise in turnaround consulting and distressed investing to provide middle-market clients with financial advisory, interim management, operations & logistics consulting, fiduciary services, senior and subordinated lender advisory services, litigation support/expert witness, select M&A services and potential special situations capital.

    AS’ Corporate Renewal & Restructuring practice possesses substantial expertise in a wide range of sectors including, but not limited to: automotive, commercial truck/recreation vehicles, aerospace/defense, consumer & food products, commercial capital equipment, healthcare services, information technologies, transportation, hospitality, steel & commodity manufacturing, energy (oil and gas) and commercial finance. AS’ affiliation with AS Capital Partners provides AS with the ability to both consult on, and invest in, significant restructurings.

    Clients include private and publicly held strategic owners, private equity sponsors, special situations investors, hedge funds, senior and subordinated senior secured lenders, venture and other special purpose funds, and fiduciaries.

    Accretive Solutions is a national consulting and executive search firm that delivers business solutions to help companies manage and improve their financial, operational and IT performance, specializing in Business Transformation, Financial Accounting and Reporting, Governance and Compliance, and Information Technology Strategy. Headquartered in the New York City area, with 13 offices located around the country, AS is a portfolio company of Saybrook Capital LLC, Goldentree Asset Management LP, Babson Capital Management LLC and Commercial Industrial Finance Corp. (CIFC).

  • Operating Partner

    Saybrook Capital, LLC

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    January 2010 — Present (10 months)

    Saybrook Capital, LLC is an alternative investment management firm that believes that superior performance is achieved by applying specialized expertise to inefficient markets. For two decades, Saybrook’s professionals have produced demonstrable results by knowing more about an asset class, for longer, with fewer distractions. Founded in 1990, the firm is headquartered in Los Angeles. Saybrook’s Capital Management professionals direct approximately $1 billion in private equity and fixed income investments, more than half of which are dedicated to distressed investing. Saybrook clients include a cadre of domestic and off-shore investors, including public funds, insurance companies, private clients, fund-of-funds and private partnerships.

    Saybrook Capital, LLC's Corporate Opportunity Fund ("COF") invests in special situations in lower middle-market companies in North America. COF makes investments through the purchase of debt in the security market, usually from banks and other distressed sellers, or injection of new capital into a company that is unable to secure conventional financing. COF has the intent of ultimately creating an influential or controlling ownership in each target company.

    Historically, COF has been able to invest at attractive valuations because of the firm's ability to identify, analyze and execute complex value oriented investments. Consistent with Saybrook's sister funds, COF seeks out situations that are complicated by factors such as financial distress, an intricate business plan, an accelerated transaction timetable, or changing industry conditions. This dynamic creates opportunities for COF, which has the expertise, experience and resources that are required to properly evaluate and invest in these types of transactions.
