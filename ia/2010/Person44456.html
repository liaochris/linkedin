Rochelle Rubin (rrubin@solomonpage.com)’s Summary

I am an experienced recruiter helping employers hire exceptional creative + tech + marketing talent while guiding talent in their job search.

I launched and manage the Creative Division of Solomon Page Group (a national personnel agency ranked #2 by Crains New York). I market and promote the skills and talents of creative, new media and technical professionals of all levels in New York City. My clients are everyone from small design firms and in-house creative departments to international investment banks and companies of all sizes in between. I place freelance, temp to perm and permanent staff in creative and tech positions.

On the creative front, I place everything from print/web designers and copywriters of all levels to production pros, project manager/producers, content people and web development folks. I can help with staff for animation (flash + motion graphics), direct mail campaigns, marketing materials, email blasts, as well as presentation designers - anything in the “creative” realm.

On the technical front, I place technology professionals of all levels from technical project managers, front end and software developers as well as UX folks, IA folks, data analysts, database developers, etc.

On the personal front, I graduated from art school (painting and design) and have been placing creative people in both interactive and print positions since 1994. I have been actively involved in the growth of the interactive space and have a firm understanding of design, technology and strategy. I have launched 3 creative departments and have managed people on the client side as well as the agency side. These experiences have helped me gain a deep understanding of my clients’ needs.
