Nick Panayi’s Summary

Summary:
Dynamic professional with extensive strategic planning and cross-discipline marketing experience in high tech. Expertise in simplifying complex concepts into digestible plans using clear thinking and laser-focused communications. Skilled in launching new technology solutions and building innovative demand-generation programs that fuel profitable growth. Seasoned company spokesperson. Bio:

Nick is a Marketing and Strategic Planning executive with 19 years experience and a diverse background in technology-related industries. Nick’s last corporate role was Vice President, North America Marketing for Avaya, a $5B global market leader in business communications solutions. His business travels brought Nick from his beginnings as a product manager for a pioneering online services startup in the early 90s, to Compaq Computers for 8 years in a variety of Marketing, Strategic Planning and Corporate Development roles, and finally to Avaya where he spent 8 years in executive-level Strategic Planning and Marketing positions. Nick led multiple strategic planning initiatives in marketing, business planning and services functions, managed a $300M P&L, launched multiple new products and managed successful product life cycles for PC-based professional workstations, and eCommerce solutions. Nick also has experience building and supporting winning partnerships, from a high visibility eCommerce alliance between Microsoft and Compaq to innovative alliances between early-generation Internet pioneers looking for ways to monetize nascent online services. He is also an experienced and effective public speaker with multiple mentions in technology media outlets.

Nick has a Bachelors degree in Marketing and an MBA in Management from Fairleigh Dickinson University. He is married with two children and lives in Princeton, NJ
