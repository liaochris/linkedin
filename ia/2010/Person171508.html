Reynaldo Morales’s Summary

BIOGRAPHY:

Reynaldo Morales is a Telephony Professional with experience in Cisco IP Telephony (CIPT), telecommunications, and networks. Rey executes CIPT, telecommunication, and network solutions for New York City Fortune 1000 corporations.

With Catalyst Internetworks, Rey delivers solutions to support organizations in CIPT and telecommunications. He plans and designs projects in CIPT and telecommunications.

Formerly at Verizon Business, he performed system engineering, planning, design, configuration, qualification and quotations of CIPT and Wide-Area-Network (WAN) enterprise solutions. Rey responded to RFP, RFI, and RFQ of CIPT and WAN opportunities.

Previously at NET (Network Equipment Technologies), he performed system engineering, planning, configurations, and implementations of enterprise WANs. At NEC Unified Solutions, Rey provided technical support and coordinated the execution of moves, adds, changes, implementation projects and service on enterprise telephony switches. Also as a Consultant with Eagle Electric Manufacturing, he performed a Technical Project Management role on the voice and data network implementation project.

While in the Marine Corps he performed system control, technical project management, system engineering, planning, design, configurations, and implementations of voice and data internetworks.
