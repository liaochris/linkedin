Jimmy Harrell’s Summary

Have excelled in product development from concept to market in the natural stone industry for over 10 years. Initially in Information Technology, then Sales & Marketing and currently as co-owner of Quiescence Iron & Stone.

I am personally involved in the designing, manufacturing, sourcing, operational, financing, selling and marketing of design driven natural stone products targeting the National wholesale Kitchen and Bath, decorative Landscape, and Architectural markets and secondarily the Gift and Home Decor industries.

Short term goal is to become the #1 supplier of decorative stone products in the industries we target.

Long term goal is to grow domestic manufacturing and enter new stone related industries such as Specification and Memorial.

Jimmy Harrell’s Specialties:

Business strategy development and execution.
International sourcing of iron and stone products.
Develop, launch and measure marketing initiatives.
Recruitment, training and management of sales force.
Travels and trade show management for product promotion.
