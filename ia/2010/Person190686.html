Tom Hanberg’s Summary

Owner / Project Manager for Internet website design and custom application programming company -- Skyhound Internet. We are celebrating our 12 year anniversary in 2010!

Skyhound provides website design and programming for all types of companies, but is currently specializing in custom web application programming for Independent wireless retailers with the new product, BaZoing! located at http://www.bazoing.com.

Skyhound is also a Verizon FiOS dealer offering our customers unlimited phone service, 50MB Internet download, and FiOS TV services. We also sign up other retailers to sell FiOS services, and the company has been contracted to manage a Verizon FiOS retail store in Long Beach, CA.

Previously have 3.5 years of experience as the owner of Skyhound Wireless, a Verizon Wireless and T-Mobile authorized dealer.

9 years experience in television and video production.
