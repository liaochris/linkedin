Amod Deshpande’s Summary

Amod is well-rounded, energetic, highly motivated and results oriented with 15 years of networking, telecommunication and application software architecture, development, and critical customer support experience.

Amod is a strong leader, great team player in multi-platform teams with excellent analytical, debugging, problem solving skills and great track record of diversity participation.

Amod holds a MS in Computer Engineering from Illinois Institute of Technology (2003) and a B.E. in Computer Technologies from the Nagpur University, India (1994). Amod was born in Nagpur, India in 1972 and now lives in Baner, Pune with wife Archana and daughter– Arushi.
