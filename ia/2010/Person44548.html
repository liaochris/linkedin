Cris Beswick’s Summary

As an adviser to some of the most forward thinking and progressive companies and organisations in the world I regularly speak on subjects like strategy, culture, leadership and innovation and having built successful and award winning companies for more than a decade my perspective is based on genuine experience and commercial success not text books or academic study.

Key things I usually get asked to do:

• Consulting on subjects like strategy, culture, leadership and innovation.
• Corporate thought provoker and official enemy of the status quo.
• Board level strategic advise (no management text books).
• Work with leaders & teams to develop strategic approaches to innovation.
• Help build creative, forward thinking business environments.
• Increase design thinking & entrepreneurship within organisations.
• Work with companies & organisations to increase capacity for innovation.
• Create strategic and cultural change to embed innovation capability.
• Mentoring, consulting, keynote speaking and facilitating.
• Help leaders build exceptional companies and organisations.

I'm also the author of 'The Road to Innovation'. A practical look at how to create an innovative company, what it takes, and where to start!

Take a look at www.letsthinkbeyond.com/products.html

In short if you want to understand innovation, how to engage your people on the journey and how to lead them in order to create an effective and sustainable strategy to push your business further and faster forward then your competitors. And you want to decrease the turnover of your people by creating an inspirational place to work then get in touch.

I also mentor senior executives & MD's and hold several Non-Executive positions with various companies and am a business mentor for the Princes Trust.

Cris Beswick’s Specialties:

Helping organisations understand what it takes to become exceptional, making people think differently and waging war on the status quo!
