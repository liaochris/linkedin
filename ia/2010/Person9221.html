Amber Gunn’s Summary

But after my accounting career, I started the path towards making my career dreams come true which is in real estate. As a single mom raising two wonderful children, I have a deep soul passion for inspiring and helping other single mothers with all their real estate endeavors.

Here is a note from one of my clients: “There is no one else I would rather partner with. You are very personable and very smart. And you really have your customer’s best interest in mind - you're not just in it for yourself.” My business is earned one customer at a time. My mission is to create Customers for Life and to do that, I have to make sure I am surpassing every customer expectation. Over 75% of my business stems from referrals of past clients and her personal network base.

I often get this question: Why should I work with Austin Domain Properties?

1) I offer real solutions…no fluff, just results. As a mother and through life experience, I have become an expert in problem solving, negotiation, mediation, coaching, and empathy.
2) When you work with me, you get a team of professionals! I have taken the time and initiative to build a strong real estate team with proven systems. That means you will have your own personal transaction manager in addition to me to provide exceptional customer service.
3) I am on the cutting edge of technology and professionalism and utilizes RELAY, an online platform where you are able to access all documents pertinent to your transaction as well progress made and what is still outstanding.
4) Your dreams and passions become my dreams and passions. You become part of my family.
