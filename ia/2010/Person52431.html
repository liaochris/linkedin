Davis Barber’s Summary

Running on the creative edge of realism and presentation, Davis Barber Productions utilizes new technologies and state-of-the-art equipment to create compelling, exciting, and effective videos and multimedia projects for clients worldwide.

With experiences that range from backstage at the Academy Awards to the Rain Forests of Brazil, Davis Barber uses photography and filmmaking to share an expertise in storytelling: REAL people and REAL life. Barber's credits span Sierra Club to Life Magazine and the New York Times to Forbes Magazine.

*Corporate and Editorial Video in Orange County, CA
*Video and Photography for Corporate Events
*Video and Photography for Collateral Marketing Materials for Academia, Small to Large Businesses, Community Events

Davis Barber’s Specialties:

High Definition video, original scoring, event and public relations, training videos, political campaign productions and life story biographies.
