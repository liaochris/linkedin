Andrea Herran’s Summary

I am an HR Consultant with a passion for helping entrepreneurs be great people leaders. People is what truly makes a difference in an organization.

Working one-on-one with business owners to understand their people. The majority of people work in small businesses (less than 100 employees) - would love each company to be a great place to work!

Setting up traditional and non-traditional HR programs to lay the foundation for a great business and great leaders.
