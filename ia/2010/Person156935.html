Józefa Fawcett’s Summary

I work as a Learning Specialist focusing on the areas of Training, Learning, Knowledge Development and Quality Management.

Essentially, I have a portfolio career, balancing my role as Director of my own two small companies, LearningVoice Ltd and OFQT Ltd with a part-time 0.3wte Senior Lecturer role at Westminster University. Which means that I retain a good practitioner/academic balance working when working with clients.

With expertise gained over 34 years my work has been in the private retail and employment agency sector, public healthcare sector, voluntary charity sector and a range of other industries and organisations across UK and Europe.
