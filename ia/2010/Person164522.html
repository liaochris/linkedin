Robert Johnson’s Summary

Who woulda thunk it? Way back in the 8th grade I knew I wanted to be in Printing. I just never counted on the changes. Hot Lead type gave way to computers, then the Mac (Thank You Steve Jobs) and now Computer to plate (CTP), DocuTechs, Digital Presses and color everywhere. Frankly I've seen and done most of it and the thrist for learning never stops. This zest for learning and life in general is what fuels me. Life is meant to be lived not observed and with every change comes opportunity. Helping customers find new and better ways to do their work. Motivating others to high achievement, being the "Go To Guy" on High Volume Color & Black and White Production equipment. The joy of teaching others and seeing them go on to success.

What is the value of opening that e-mail that says "Bob, thank you so much for the training you provided. I got my first deal and just like you said it feels GREAT!" I intend to wring out every once of joy and passion from life both personally and professionally. I love a challenge whether it be helping customers or going out and closing business to helping a 9 year old lay down the perfect bunt. Even something as simple as drivng the Handicap transport at the music festival I work for. Life for me is not measured in the number of breaths taken but in the number of moments that take my breath away.
