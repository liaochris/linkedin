Deirdre Goebel Edgar’s Summary

In nearly 20 years as a newspaper copy editor, I have extensive experience meeting daily deadlines, editing for grammar and style, writing compelling headlines, managing a staff and acting as an advocate for readers -- our customers.

I copy edited the Pulitzer Prize-winning series "Altered Oceans," which won for explanatory journalism in 2007, working closely with the reporter and other members of the project team.

I have been fluent in html for more than 10 years, since starting a Geocities webpage that featured my dogs. Now, I edit stories and blogs for latimes.com and Twitter as @LATimes and @LATimesnational to help promote our work.

ACES:

As vice president/conferences for the American Copy Editors Society, I coordinate all aspects of the national conference, which draws hundreds of copy editors annually for three days of journalism education. I have served on the board of ACES for seven years, the last four as VP/conferences.

Deirdre Goebel Edgar’s Specialties:

Organizing projects. Managing tight deadlines. Grammar and Style. Social Media. Journalism advocacy and education.
