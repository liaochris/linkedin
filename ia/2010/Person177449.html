Ron Opher’s Summary

Entrepreneurial business leader and attorney who has successfully grown both for-profit and non-profit businesses from the ground up. Founded and led law firm providing credit and collection solutions to both small and large global financial institutions; grew to 12 person firm collecting $5+million per year. Nationally recognized expert in web-based and traditional marketing, cash management, and practice development within professional services firms; called upon to speak and publish nationally. Has created and led philanthropic organizations, from vision and strategy through successful operation.
