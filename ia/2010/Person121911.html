Laura Lozano’s Summary

Laura Lozano, CH is a Holistic Counselor, Intuitive, Hypnotherapist and Herbalist. She is the Founder of Inner Solutions, LLC, a wellness company dedicated to assist you in discovering your greatest potential. She has studied with traditional and non-traditional healers in the United States, Belize and Italy. She has taught classes at the Community College of Aurora, Denver Botanic Gardens as well as local venues and conferences. For over eighteen years, Laura has honored the traditional ways of healing by integrating the mind, body, spirit and heart to empower a sense of wholeness and inspiring full self expression.

Laura Lozano’s Specialties:

Removing blocks and sabotaging beliefs that inhibit you from living the life you desire.
