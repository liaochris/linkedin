Michel Lent Schwartzman’s Summary

Michel Lent Schwartzman was born in Caliornia, USA, and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. A Graphic Designer holding a MPS degree from the Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP) at New York University, started his career in 1995 in New York at EURO/RSCG. In 2000 joined DDB Brazil as Creative Director before starting his own agency, 10'Minutos, in 2002. His company was shortly acknowledged by the industry as one of the leading interactive agencies in the country. In 2009, 10'Minutos joinned operations with OgilvyInteractive in Brazil.

Michel has rendered digital marketing services for world class clients such as IBM, Shell, 20th Century Fox, BMG, Warner Music, International Paper, ABM AMRO Bank, Philips, Siemens, Honda, Santander and Coca-Cola.

In the course of almost 15 years in the industry Michel has lectured at universities and other institutions all over Brazil and abroad, developed innumerous digital marketing campaigns, structured and managed departments, teams and processes being awarded in key international festivals such as Cannes, Clio, Art Directors, One Show, The New York Festivals, and London Festivals.

Son of a Brazilian father, an Argentinian mother, and having lived and attended grad school in the US, Michel is fluent in Portuguese, Spanish and English.
