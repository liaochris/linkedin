Mitch Fournier’s Summary

A passionate, detail-oriented product development leader combining 15 years of business experience and deep analytical thinking with a broad set of product development skills. The unique combination of marketing, technology and design provide for effective leadership of diverse product teams and efficient hands-on development of new Internet products.

Most recently, I was the Vice President of Product Development and Technology for BuyerZone.com, an online Business to Business lead generation service. At BuyerZone, I was responsible for the creation and management of the core online products which were the foundation of our online revenue. As well, I was a member of the senior management team that led the company through the post-Web 1.0 bubble and on to profitability and a successful sale to Reed Elsevier in 2007.

Prior to BuyerZone, I served as the Director of the Lycos Advanced Product Development group, an intrapreneurial group within Lycos. Previously, I founded 2d Interactive, Inc. a new media company, and served as the Vice President of Product Development where I led the creation and management of a college-oriented web site and a nationwide network of 350+ retail kiosks. I have also held positions at Harvard University and the Federal Reserve Bank of Boston.

I graduated Phi Beta Kappa and with honors in Economic from Boston College and also hold a Masters degree in Architecture from Harvard University.

Mitch Fournier’s Specialties:

BUSINESS EXPERIENCE: technical team building and management (Web Development, Engineering and Operations teams), deep analytical ability, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, conversion rate measurement and optimization.

PRODUCT EXPERIENCE: product specification, usability testing, page layout and design, graphic design, Java application coding, Javascript and CSS development, database development, and IT resource management.
