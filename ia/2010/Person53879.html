Daniel Edgell’s Summary

Daniel is employed at Stantec Consulting as a Senior Architect. He has earned a BArch from Kent State University and an AAS degree in Civil Engineering (Arch. Opt.) from Stark State College of Technology. Before moving to Rochester, he worked in the construction field, designing high end residential remodels and additions as well as building those designs.

During his educational and professional career, he has had experience working with various memberships and committees on different levels. During the past years, he has been involved in activities such as: facilitating charettes with the Rochester Regional Design Center, and working as the AIA New York State Associate Member Director, and organizing programs and events for New York State Associates.

Daniel recently participated in the creation of the Emerging Rochester Architects (ERA), which addresses the needs and concerns of Assoc. AIA and recently licensed AIA members. He also served as a guest panelist at the AIANYS Convention for Got License?, which was designed to answer questions about earning your license to practice architecture in New York.

During his term as the AIA New York State Associate Member Director he completed the Architectural Registration Exam to earn licensure in the State of New York and has earned his NCARB certificate. His next professional goal is to earn his LEED AP certification in the near future.

Currently, Daniel serves on the AIA National Associates Committee as the Regional Associate Director for AIA New York State where he is a member of the advocacy committee. He will also be serving on the AIANYS convention committee for the 2009 convention and is the current President Elect for the AIA Rochseter Chapter.

As a member of the AIA, Daniel is committed to help maintain the reputation of an AIA Architect as a respected name, and is committed to helping emerging professionals to achieve their goals.
