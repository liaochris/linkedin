Katja Myyryläinen’s Summary

I am innovative, versatile organizer and designer in the field of visual communications and marketing.

I provide visually attractive, informative and functional designs and marketing communication plans for businesses and organizations.

With each case, I start by finding out the real needs of the customer and then proceed to make those needs visible and visually pleasing. In order to express the true identity, I believe that one needs to see the big picture from many viewpoints first.

The result is a multi-layer cake made with a special recipe, consisting of words and ideas and maximum taste, all glazed with appropriate images.

Viestintä on ja vaatii toimintaa.

I work with Adobe Creative Suite 4.
