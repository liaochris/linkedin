Andy Sachs’s Summary

Before joining Harris-Sachs, LLC, Andy was a National Sales & Marketing Manager at Scholastic, INC. Andy developed and implemented targeted and specific marketing plans on behalf of his varied clientele which included corporate, government and non-profit clients. He had also developed his own real estate business in Newtown, CT which involved successful portfolio of residential sales and commercial real estate investment. Andy's responsibilities at Harris-Sachs involve the development of Pizza Fest™, The Ultimate Pizza Topping™ brand of edible images for pizza, manufactured by Lucks Food Decorating Company, a client of Harris-Sachs. Working closely with the President of Lucks Andy is responsible for sales, marketing and licensing development of this innovative product. Andy received his B.A. in Politics and Speech Communication from Ithaca College and earned his Masters in Public Administration from the Rockefeller School at SUNY Albany. Andy's addition to Harris-Sachs, LLC brings new energy, skills and a fresh perspective that better serves our clients in an evolving licensing and economic environment.

Harris-Sachs, LLC serves a wide array of manufacturing clients across industries and in several countries. We work to secure strategic licenses and brands on behalf of our clients, assist in marketing strategy, and distribution to market of the newly branded product.
