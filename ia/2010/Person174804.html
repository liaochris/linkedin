    ecoConnect CIC

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    June 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    ecoConnect is the UK's low carbon industrial and cleantech coalition, support group and lobby, we help companies Collaborate, Innovate, Invest and Achieve.

    A not-for-profit, and politically independent, ecoConnect unites the low carbon industrial and cleantech community, bringing together business leadership, engineers and entrepreneurs, investment firms, scientific agencies, government agencies, academia and media.

    We are open to collaborations and partnerships with other environmentally focussed companies and agencies.
