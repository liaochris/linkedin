  • Graduate Student Researcher

    UCLA

    (Educational Institution; Computer Software industry)

    September 2003 — April 2010 (6 years 8 months)

    * Designed and implemented a social network based recommender system (SNRS) which predicts user interest in items based on user preference, item acceptance, and friend influences in social networks. Experiments on a dataset of 9067 user reviews on 4152 restaurants at Yelp.com showed that SNRS improved the prediction accuracy by 18% compared with the state-of-the-art methods while maintaining good coverage even when the data is highly sparse.

    * Investigated the issues of privacy disclosure via friend relationships in social networks, and proposed a Bayesian network framework to model the casual relationship between friends. Experiments on a Livejournal dataset reveal the possiblity of such privacy disclosure. Proposed a privacy protection rule with four corresponding techniques, and showed that selectively falsifying user information achieves the best protection.

    * Participated in a cross-disciplinary research team in research on neuropsychiatric disorders by integrating basic, clinical and information sciences. Identified the latent semantic relationships between phenomic concepts by using statistical literature mining techniques such as TFIDF, LSA, pLSA, and OpenHTMM. Implemented a knowledge-based query expansion and document retrieval system which facilitates searches on PubMed, a service of the US National Library of Medicine.

  • Internship

    Teradata

    (Public Company; TDC; Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 2007 — September 2007 (4 months)

    * Designed and implemented an algorithm to estimate the sizes of all possible views in an OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) cube operation. The computational cost and memory usage are reduced by an order of magnitude, compared with the existing method used at Teradata.

    * Developed a cost model for a new join geography PRPD (Partial Redistribution Partial Duplication), on Teradata System. Discussed the trade-offs of PRPD compared with redistribution join and duplication join. PRPD is being implemented for the future release of Teradata System.
