Ariana Gordon Stecker’s Summary

As the previous owner and founder of JoonBug.com for almost a decade, I have produced some of the most popular events in NYC, Miami and Los Angeles on the biggest nights of the year. Launching Joonbug in 2000, I have produced events at many of NYC's hottest venues, including Capitale, Marquee, Guastavino's, The Park and Cipriani and have worked with clients such as The Trump Organization, PepsiCo, and Esquire Magazine.

I have taught numerous classes and seminars on "How to Become an Event Planner" and "How to Break into the Business". I've also been featured on MTV and Fox 5 New York as an event planning expert, giving tips and commenting on the hot spots to see - and be seen at.

Combining my past experience with my Marketing & Communications degree from NYC's Fashion Institute of Technology, I have started a new company: Iris Fields - a wedding planning service that produces flawless events, every single time. Please visit my new endeavor at www.irisfields.com.
