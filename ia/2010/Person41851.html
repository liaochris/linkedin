Chris Macomber’s Summary

I am currently a Senior Product Manager at ReputationDefender, a leader in the growing online reputation market, where I am responsible for the MyEdge and MyReputation offerings. Previously, I was the Product Manager for QuickBooks Online at Intuit which is our flagship online financial management service for small businesses. I have over 10 years of experience in the Internet and software working for companies such as Intuit, Cisco, and Sapient. I have also worked on other SaaS offerings at Intuit including QuickBase, an online solution that enables business users to build their own Internet apps, as well as Intuit Community, our online community for small business owners to help them start and grow their business. I am well-versed in new concept creation, product strategy, customer development, online business models, conversion funnel optimization, A/B testing, and web analytics for SaaS and Internet offerings.
