Sharon L Erdman’s Summary

Bio for Sharon Erdman:

Sharon Erdman is currently the Creative Director and Unit Head for Strategic Design at Mayo Clinic. She leads a diverse team of innovative designers who develop creative solutions to support Mayo Clinic's mission.

Every creative solution from this team helps advance Mayo’s goal of excellence in patient care, research and education. Strategic Design has expertise in environmental design, web, social media, publications, campaigns and brand identity. Sharon’s team has won multiple awards including HOW magazine’s In-house Design Award for the national art exhibition Scalpel to Sketch: The Science and Beauty of Medical Illustration at Mayo Clinic.

Prior to joining Mayo Clinic in 2004, Sharon served as art and creative director for over 20 years in the fields of branding, publishing, product development, direct mail marketing, packaging, capital campaigns and national promotions. Properties and clients include: Disney®, Toys ‘R Us®, QVC®, Scholastic Books®, PBS®, and Discovery Channel®.

Sharon earned a bachelors in visual art at Florida State University School of Visual Art. She continued her education in graphic design at the University of Florida’s School of Art.

Sharon views her career in strategic design as a way to create new dialogues between business partners and creative professionals. These dialogues open new avenues of innovation and collaboration that allow the business to explore new and impactful ways of communicating to their audiences.
