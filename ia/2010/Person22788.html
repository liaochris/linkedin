Beth Kaplan’s Summary

I am a experienced Retail Executive with demonstrated expertise in diverse range of Management, Operating, Strategic, and Merchandising roles. Successful track record in managing Specialty Retail businesses, as well as; new business development, financial and operational analysis, and retail store real estate expansion.

Over the last twenty-five years I have worked for several retailers with responsibility for store operations and communication, strategic business development, business analysis and expense control, problem determination and resolution, customer service and client relations, day-to-day management and new store design and expansion. In addition, at the beginning of my career I was both a retail buyer and wholesale sales representative.

My objective is to find an appropriate position with a growth company that will benefit from my vast experience and provide me with new challenges.
