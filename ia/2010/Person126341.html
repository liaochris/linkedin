Lisa Young’s Summary

I'm a direct, no B.S. kind of person. Clarity and focus are critical factors in ensuring the success of any project. My expertise lies in helping people identify obstacles, and brainstorm solutions that can benefit them. In my coaching and mentoring, I take that process a step further and outline action plans to achieve those goals or eliminate those obstacles.

Despite my directness, I have a personable, approachable demeanor that makes it easy for clients to be open with me. Through powerful interviewing questions, I am able to quickly cut to the heart of the matter in most situations, enabling rapid problem solving and branstorming possibilities.

Lisa Young’s Specialties:

Helping people gain clarity and focus. I use different methods (interviewing, brainstorming, etc) to help clients get crystal clarity on a problem and then find solutions they can quickly implement to improve their situation.

  • Founder

    The Renaissance Mom

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2009 — Present (1 year )

    The Renaissance Mom is a company built on the notion that you can build a successful business AND be a mom.

    The razor's edge of "balance" that most mothers walk can be tough enough to navigate with children underfoot. Add a business to that equation it's no wonder women struggle with bringing harmony to their relationships.

    Women are born multi-taskers, and the entrepreneurial mom has an even tougher time of it. Learning to focus - on what really matters most - can be a daunting task. The Renaissance Mom offers tools, strategies and resources for working mothers - particularly entrepreneurial moms - to find their own definition of balance between business and family - without making excuses or apologies for either.

    Lisa Robbin Young founded The Renaissance Mom out of a need to help other moms in similar circumstances. She began her career as a single mom, struggling to balance work and the demands of her special needs child. Resources were few and far between, and Lisa found herself bouncing from job to job - including a career in direct sales, before realizing that there were other factors for her Entrepreneurial ADD - factors she has since learned to navigate.

    Fast forward twelve years. Lisa is now a successful business owner, wife and mother to two energetic boys (three if you count her husband). She now works with women around the world to define and achieve their own sense of balance at home and in the workplace, realizing that balance is defined differently for every woman.
