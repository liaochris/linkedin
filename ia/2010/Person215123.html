Whitney Quesenbery’s Summary

Whitney Quesenbery is a user interface designer and usability specialist with a passion for clear communication.

She helps companies develop new concepts for product designs and has worked on award winning multimedia products, web sites, and web & software applications. Her earliest work explored the new world of hypertext with a pre-web tool called Hyperties.

Her projects include with work with The Open University, National Cancer Institute, Sage Software, IEEE, Mosaic, Amtrak, CodeCorrect, Novartis, Siemens Health Services, Hewlett-Packard, and Eli Lilly. As part of UsabilityThatWorks, Whitney works with Effortmark in the UK and Apogee in Asia.

She publishes, presents and offers training on usability issues.
