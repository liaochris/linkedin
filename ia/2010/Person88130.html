James Hilliard’s Summary

After starting in the broadcast radio field in San Francisco, I turned my attention to the world of technology in 1998. Currently I host video interviews/webcasts/webinars/podcasts for companies like AT&T, Oracle, Intel, Sophos, HP and others on a variety of media outlets such as CNET Networks, CMP, TechTarget as well as individual client sites. I work with clients from conception to delivery, helping to communicate marketing messages that are second to none.

I am also a trained non-union Voice-Over artist having done work recently for the San Jose Merc. News, Bio-Rad, Oracle, Macromedia, Lenovo, McAfee and PC Connection.

I continue to look for opportunities in Voice-Over, Video Production and in webcast/podcast hosting. Most of my time is spent with technology companies and clients, however I do have a strong interest in the bio-tech/pharma/medical industries and am pursuing ways to apply my communication skills to those areas.
