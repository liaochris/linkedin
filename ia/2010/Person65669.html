Eric Brooke’s Summary

A leader and innovator, who excels at complex problem resolution, and at bringing people together to create change. He is frequently engaged in a leadership capacity or as a strategic adviser to executives, as well as to offer his wealth of expertise in communications and marketing. Eric always becomes known in the organizations he works with for pushing the envelope in new technology and product innovation.

Leadership & Strategic Advisor

Eric has 11 years of leadership experience, 7 years at a senior exec level. He has led several divisions in communications, marketing and service delivery (both corporate and consumer-facing). As an executive he has been responsible for, in one instance, departments of £71 million in revenues and 400 staff. His board level experience includes service as Governor for a British university.

Marketing & Communications

Eric has worked in communications for 17 years, in both Europe and North America. This has included the B2B and B2C marketing for businesses, brands, products, services, policies and individuals in the private, non-profit and government sectors.

In addition to leading divisions responsible for delivering core marcoms strategy and execution, nationally and internationally, Eric has also held roles responsible for advertising (print & digital), social media, employee comms, stakeholder comms, market research, information systems, information technology, product development, sales, fundraising, branding, public relations, CRM, public affairs and campaigns (political & charitable). This rare diversity of experience has facilitated Eric’s success in breaking down silos and building strong inter-linkages between practices.

Eric Brooke’s Specialties:

Strategy creation/planning/implementation, innovation, communications/campaigns/marketing management, service management, product management, process and systems development, organisation development, change management, project management, alliance/coalition building, training, facilitation
