Alexandra Kirsch’s Summary

I am a writer, editor, publicist, and social media consultant. I use words to communicate across a variety of genres and media platforms.

My writing, design, and editing experience includes but is not limited to: press releases, memos, ad and website copy, style sheets, proposals, books, articles (academic, feature, and news), essays, reports, and brochures.

Additionally, I have experience with such technical tools as:

* Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Outlook);
* FileMaker Pro;
* Acumen;
* Adobe Photoshop;
* Adobe InDesign; and
* WordPress.

I currently work for Planned Television Arts (PTA), a division of Ruder Finn, as the social media coordinator. My job includes working closely with authors and publishers to decide which social media tools are most appropriate for a particular campaign, coordinating blog tours to promote a client's titles, as well as developing meaningful relationships with bloggers and other pillars in social media so as to create a community of people who share and distribute information.

I have been published three times in the Detroit Jewish News for feature articles on campus activity and young entrepreneurship. I have also had one piece published in The Adelante Forward, and regularly contributed to the recap blog for The Lisa Wexler Show in the spring of 2010.

For more information, please visit my online portfolio at www.alexandrakirsch.com.
