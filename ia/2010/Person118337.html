Alex Korol’s Summary

I’ve been in the Real Estate industry for over thirty years, both in Europe and in the United States.As project manager, chief engineer and principal of various companies I have been involved in the construction and revitalization of residential, commercial, and industrial real estate. After arrival in the USA I joined MJE, International REAL ESTATE concern with offices in Los Angeles and London, as a project manager and worked my way up to senior vice-president and partner .
