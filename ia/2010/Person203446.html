Tiffany Blackden’s Summary

Currently a Fort Collins massage therapist specializing in massage for individuals with chronic pain, athletes and pregnancy, in the field since 2000. My extensive background before that in sales, marketing, software training and non-profit work helped to evolve our entrepreneurial adventure into a thriving massage clinic and networking business. We mentor other healthcare professionals and train small business owners in how to build their businesses. We've learned some very specific skills in running online businesses and online marketing for business. Our non-profit volunteer efforts have been focused primarily with helping the charitable sector to network and market themselves more effectively.

Tiffany Blackden’s Specialties:

Helping people in chronic pain find ways to feel better through bodywork and evaluation of chronic postural strain. We also mentor service business professionals with a local market in effective marketing techniques, primarily networking and online strategies.
