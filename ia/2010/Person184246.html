Scott Gibson’s Summary

I help growing businesses protect their intellectual property and other intangible assets from employee theft and disloyalty and from unfair competition. We assist clients in developing systems that allow them to identify and preserve their trade secrets, and to promote their business through enforceable non-compete agreements and other restrictive covenants.

We help businesses grow and succeed. When necessary, we help clients resolve their business disputes through litigation, arbitration, or mediation. I am an effective advocate and creative negotiator for my clients.

Over the past 23 years, I have learned how to quickly discern the most important issues in a case, and to focus on ways to resolve rather than to expand litigation. By focusing on the most important legal questions, we have helped many clients bring cases to an early conclusion through carefully crafted motions for summary judgment and motions in limine. When a case cannot be resolved through legal motions or favorable negotiations, I am a well prepared and capable trial attorney.

Scott Gibson’s Specialties:

Ethical rules preclude me from claiming areas of specialization. My practice focuses on trade secrets, non-compete agreements, employment law, and business litigation.
