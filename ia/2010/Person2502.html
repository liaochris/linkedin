Adam Litt’s Summary

Originally a Philadelphia native, Adam headed west to Los Angeles in 2002 to bring his East Coast sensibilities to the silver screen. Graduating top of his class from The University of Southern California’s Writing For Film and Television program, Adam has since won numerous accolades for his short and feature length screenplays.

Adam has also served as a script reader for several companies including FX Networks and ScriptReaders.Com, and has produced commercials and music videos for a variety of clients including Warner Brothers, Sony, and Red Bull. Additionally, Adam has production managed multiple live broadcast sporting events including the Vans Triple Crown of Surfing on Fuel TV. Currently, he is working as a Development Executive at Skybox Films, where is busy developing a project for MTV.
