Courtney George’s Summary

Design isn’t just a shiny candy coating, but is often treated that way. Effective design makes information (even the most complex information) accessible to the viewer and easy to understand. Good design works, not just because it’s inviting to look at, but because the information is engaging, easy to navigate, understandable and relevant.

I’ve been a designer for 12 years in which time I’ve acquired a well-rounded working education in design studios, advertising agencies, in-house departments and alas, as an independent.

Skills
Expert knowledge of Adobe CS applications – InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop – as well as Quark, on a MAC platform. Proficient in a wide range of additional applications including MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), iWork (Pages, Numbers, Keynote), FTP client software and font management applications. Experienced in designing for the web and working with web design applications, including Flash and Dreamweaver. Advanced color management, prepress/print production and project management abilities. Highly skilled in design and development of complex print materials and digital illustrations. Excellent organizational, collaborative, and time management capabilities with an aptitude for learning new processes and software applications quickly.

Some things clients have said:

"With her organizational ability, people stay inspired to generate ideas for the creative process. – D. Robinson, Easylite ILS"

"Courtney's design mind fluidly puts ideas and concepts together and she performs on tight time lines that few other designers can duplicate. She is able to build marketing materials from the ground up, and I have seen her create diamonds out of the roughest and most disorganized set of materials. – J. Laman, Blueprint Admissions"

Courtney George’s Specialties:

Brand development, design and marketing strategies, and project management. Print and print-to-web collateral: brochures, marketing kits, manuals, catalogs, magazines, posters, signage, direct mail, trade show graphics and ads.
