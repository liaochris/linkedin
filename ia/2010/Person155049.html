Nikhil Nilakantan’s Summary

Co-Founder & CEO of Memory Reel Inc. (formerly 46 Blocks Inc.) Memory Reel is a simple, fun way to create a personal journal of your life. We pull together the photos and updates you already share on your favorite web sites and seamlessly place them into an online journal that you can share with friends and family. Sign up at www.memoryreel.com

Co-Founder & CEO at Social Span Media LLC. Social Span Media is an interactive media company that focuses exclusively on marketing campaigns for social networking platforms. We help our clients create and extend their product and brand strategies by building virally engaging marketing solutions for social networks such as Facebook, MySpace, Hi5, Orkut, LinkedIn and many others.

Founder and principal consultant at Inventive Path LLC. More than 7 years of global experience working for Fortune 500 companies such as Texas Instruments, Hewlett-Packard and Microsoft during which time I held various roles in Product Strategy, Product Management & Quality Assurance.

Before launching Inventive Path, I was one of the lead Product Managers for the TI-Nspire (TM) handheld product family at Texas Instruments Inc. which I helped design and launch world wide. Aside from my extensive corporate experience, I hold an MBA in Marketing & Strategy from SMU’s Cox School of Business and a BS in Computer Science from the Florida Institute of Technology.

Nikhil Nilakantan’s Specialties:

Expertise in web 2.0, product and brand marketing, product strategy, product management, product innovation, interactive media strategy, new media, interactive marketing, social network marketing, internet marketing, requirements gathering, digital content strategy, product globalization, localization, process definition, process improvement, quality assurance, management; full life cycle expertise in software, consumer electronics, embedded handheld devices, wireless networking products.
