PHILIP T. MAHONEY’s Summary

Phil Mahoney is Parsons' Mission Critical Systems Sector Manager. With 29+ years of management experience in the commercial engineering/construction industry, he has developed a unique and extensive track record of delivering results: developed 9.0 million GSF of new construction and renovation (approx. $5.5 billion aggregate CapEx), profit and loss responsibility for projects and capital programs in excess of $250 million, managed portfolios of 500+ facilities worldwide, and successfully negotiated $900 million lease transactions. Phil has extensive data center experience due to his working relationships, i.e. to name a few; Social Security Administration, US Dept of State, Microsoft, Bank of America, Bell Canada and AT&T.

Mr. Mahoney brings to his clients a tremendous working knowledge of engineering/construction of mission critical facilities. His hands-on knowledge of the complete lifecycle management of global data center portfolios: strategic planning, site selection, due diligence, lease negotiations, planning, engineering, construction, project management, facility management, and outsourcing will provide the required leadership and management.

Managing from a strategic position, Mr. Mahoney will leverage the Parsons' extensive resources as warranted. Having managed the development of 100+ data centers and other global mission critical facilities, his leadership and insight will insure a fully integrated project delivery solution for an on-time/on-budget project.

PHILIP T. MAHONEY’s Specialties:

Complete lifecycle management of commercial real estate: strategic planning, site selection, due diligence, lease negotiations, planning, engineering, construction, project management, facility management, outsourcing, dispute resolution, expert witness evaluations and testimony, disposal and bankruptcy activities.
