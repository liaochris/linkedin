Cory Edelman’s Summary

RF Systems architect and high-frequency, microwave engineering specialist. Created applications for supporting and marketing the industry’s most widely used RF system and circuit EDA tool sets to customers in aerospace, defense and commercial environments.

System-level modeling and simulation of radio-frequency hardware provides valuable insight into performance, reliability and manufacturability often unobtainable using most popular or typical design methods. My goal is to expand applications of RF system simulation to help organizations reduce their product development cost and time to market by 30-50% while delighting customers with reliable and well-documented products that work as they should.

Cory Edelman’s Specialties:

RF cascade "spreadsheet" analysis, RF transmitter and receiver architecture, power amplifier verification and linearization, non-linear modeling and simulation, spread-spectrum communications, 2G-4G wireless RF PHY layer design.
Skilled using EDA tools including ADS, Momentum, EMDS, Cadence Virtuoso, SPICE, Ptolemy, MATLAB(tm), SystemVue, HDLs, Statistical Design, DFM, Six Sigma.
