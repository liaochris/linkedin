Jeremy Ellis’s Summary

Founding member and a Principal of Genesis Capital. Jeremy has over 10 years of investment banking experience, specializing in mergers & acquisitions, and has been involved in over 60 transactions for an aggregate transaction value in excess of $6 billion. During his career, Jeremy has been involved in all types of mergers and acquisitions, including sell-side and buy-side advisory assignments, corporate divestitures, recapitalizations, capital raises and fairness opinions. He has represented numerous family-owned and privately-held businesses as well as public companies such as Infocure Corporation, Northrop Grumman Corporation, Theragenics Corporation and TTM Electronics. Buyers of his clients include numerous private equity firms as well as strategic buyers such as Cobham Plc, ITT Industries and Moog Inc.
