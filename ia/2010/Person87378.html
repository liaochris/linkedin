Jaime Steele’s Summary

I have quite a number of hats and I wear them all regularly.

Sometimes I wear more than one hat on the same day and at times you’ll find me wearing two or three of them at the same time.

My Financial & Tax Planning Hat

When I’m wearing this one I concentrate on tax reduction and goal based financial planning. I believe that an individual can take charge of his or her own life to stabilise risk factors and maximise the benefits of income and investment.

I’m a technology nut (Hat number 2) and have discovered amazing software tools to take the doubt and speculation out of financial planning so that instead of piecemeal advice, my clients can move forward with a viable life plan. Isn’t it better to reach for your dreams, occupy your mind, test your strengths and to feel really alive instead of just making it through by blowing in the wind?  

My Digital Hat

I’m evangelical about the power of connection and collaboration made possible by Social Media. If you’re reading this you’ll already know I’m on LinkedIn, but you’ll also find me hanging out on Twitter, Facebook and YouTube among others. I’ve taken my love for social media and human connection one step further and gathered together a team of marketing and technology friends under the banner SocialGloo. We exist as a blogging platform - to share our expertise and discoveries with you but we’re also available to collaborate with, creating social media strategies that will weld eyeballs to your online presence. Check us out at www.socialgloo.co.uk

My Spare Time Hats

I love my work and I treasure time with my family. But I still find time for a range of personal interests from fitness and sports through to good food, music, business intelligence and what I call my flipside: serious continental films and heavyweight classics of literature. (This Hat is particularly weighty and it sometimes makes my head hurt.) Plus I also make time for a bit of website design on the side.
