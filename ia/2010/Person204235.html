Timothy McNamara’s Summary

As a practitioner in the field of divorce for over seven years I am interested in developing new solutions to the often damaging and costly process of divorce. I am currently working with a team of highly respected professionals to offer couples seeking divorce a new way forward. One that treats couples with respect and places at the forefront the most important considerations in a divorce: children and an objective evaluation of the marital assets.

I am also the co-founder of New England Divorce Solutions, New England’s leading provider of consulting, litigation support and forensic accounting services for matrimonial disputes. Our practice has extensive experience in areas of discovery, drafting interrogatory questions, preparing financial statements, tracing of marital assets, investigative accounting, trial preparation, providing expert opinions and assisting in settlement negotiations. We are qualified expert witnesses in the Massachusetts courts. We also provide business valuation services to value a business in a divorce. I am a Certified Forensic Financial Analyst and Certified Divorce Financial Analyst and a candidate for the Accredited Valuation Analyst designation. I am also trained in alternative dispute resolution methods including mediation and collaborative law.

I am the Founder and currently on the advisory board of McNamara Capital LLC, a boutique wealth management firm. Our practice specializes in advising divorcing individuals on how to properly manage their settlements along with assisting with asset allocation, tax-efficient investment strategies, estate planning issues, risk management and comprehensive financial planning to meet the unique needs of divorcing individuals. I also hold the Certifed Investment Management Analyst (CIMA) designation.
