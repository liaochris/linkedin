Jim Duncan’s Summary

I am a Real Estate Broker in Charlottesville, Virginia, Partner at Nest Real Estate Group, a full-service real estate firm that is redefining real estate representation in the Charlottesville region.

I am a real estate blogger, real estate analyst,public speaker, early adopter of emerging technologies and skilled at effective implementation of same.

I write about real estate on several blogs - locally in Charlottesville, for the State Realtor Association's blog and nationally for AgentGenius, covering everything from local growth, politics and real estate trends and market analysis to trends affecting Realtors and consumers from a state and national perspective.

Always looking for new technologies and career opportunities and am always willing to listen to new ideas.

I am an advocate for the elimination of Dual Agency, a real estate analyst (so-called by a local publication), consultant, and staunch defender of my clients' positions.

Jim Duncan’s Specialties:

Technology implementation, negotiation, listening, dedication to my clients, intimate expert knowledge of the local real estate market and the influencing factors.

Jim Duncan’s Experience

  • Associate Broker/Partner

    Nest Realty Group

    (Real Estate industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Being part of a small new real estate company in this economy is exciting. We have shared values, shared goals and each are fiercely independent.

    The real estate brokerage model as it was known two and three and thirty years ago is broken. Nest is our attempt to represent the new breed of Realtors - those who listen to and respond to consumers' demands.

    - We don't do Dual Agency.
    - We do either Exclusive Buyer Representation or Seller Representation.
    - We use technology in ways that no others do.
    - We want to empower the consumer.
    - We do not fear the consumers having the same information we have.
    - We don't have significant overhead.
