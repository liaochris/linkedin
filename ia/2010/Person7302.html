Alicia Boone’s Summary

Alicia Boone completed her M.P.S. for Art & Cultural Management at Pratt Institute in 2009. Ms. Boone is an entrepreneur, educator and arts advocate. She is currently the Adult Programs Coordinator at the Brooklyn Museum, whose mission is to to act as a bridge between the rich artistic heritage of world cultures, as embodied in its collections, and the unique experience of each visitor.

Her previous work includes serving as the Education Director at World Up, a non-profit organization dedicated to educating the public about international politics and issues that affect the global community through Hip-Hop and socially progressive culture.

In addition to her work in education, she founded So Live! Arts in 2006, an arts exhibition and professional development company that has gained sponsorships from Vitamin Water, Pratt Institute and Love Brigade.
