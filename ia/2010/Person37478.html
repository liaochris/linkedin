Christopher A. Hutcherson’s Summary

I am a seasoned corporate executive and/or executive consultant looking for a high growth nonprofit that wants to fulfill part of their funding and corporate partnership needs by utilizing my unique and wide ranging breath of skill sets. I am a business development, fundraising and gift card pioneer who can bring vision to reality. I am the grandson of an Elementary school principal. I am proud that I started his career in education and continues to be a teacher at heart holding a Masters in Education and Texas Teacher and Administrator certification.
