Joi Surya Dharma’s Summary

Having about 8+ years of professional experience in upstream oil and gas industry in areas of business planning and strategy, commercial, operation, project planning and construction, required and developed me to be an adaptable person, a fast learner, a team player and be able to liaise with internal departments in the organization, as well as the external organizations such as the government, other oil and gas companies, consultant, data provider and vendor.

I had education in Engineering (BSc and MEng in Project Management) and Business (MBA in Energy Management). I am also involved in some professional organizations i.e. Indonesian Petroleum Association (IPA), Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE), Indonesia Renewable Energy Society (METI) and World Energy Council (KNI-WEC). Therefore, I believe that the combination of my academics and working experiences allow me to have and enhance broad perspective and analysis in energy with focus in oil and gas industry.

I currently active at House of Representatives of Republic Indonesia (DPR - RI) as Expert Staff in a Commission that overseeing Energy Sector and as Marketing Director in Energy Center - University of Petroleum.
