Jeff Briskin’s Summary

Veteran marketing and creative professional Jeff Briskin delivers client-centric insights, ideas, and innovative online/offline marketing and communication programs and sales optimization strategies to help companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 leaders gain, sustain, and retain client relationships more effectively.

His firm, Briskin Consulting, provides a wide array of services to help asset managers, energy firms, high tech companies and not for profit organizations gain greater visibility and purchasing consideration by positioning their capabilities in greater alignment with the needs and challenges of their target audiences.

For smaller companies and startups, Jeff can employ his proprietary DAPPER Analysis marketing tool to help them articulate their competitive strengths and differentiators in alignment with their clients' purchasing considerations.

In this role Jeff leverages his fifteen years of experience helping Fidelity Investments, Pioneer Investments, Bank of America, U.S. Trust and other companies developed effective client segmentation strategies, targeted sales campaigns, cutting-edge ecommerce applications, online advertising campaigns, web sites, branding strategies, thought leadership publications and comprehensive suites of collateral and sales materials.

Jeff is also well known in client research circles, having served as project manager for the 2008 Bank of America Study of High Net Worth Philanthropy, which generated media coverage in The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Barrons, Newsweek, and other national and international media outlets.

These efforts resulted in measurable increases in sales win rates, greater client satisfaction, and billions of dollars in new assets from high net worth clients, retirement plans and endowments and foundations.

Visit www.jeffbriskin.net for more information, portfolios, and free thought leadership articles.
