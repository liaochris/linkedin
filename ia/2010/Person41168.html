Alan Nash’s Summary

Licensed shiatsu therapist, International teacher and public speaker with cum lauda diploma for classical shiatsu from Tokyo and from the Japanese Cultural Center in Amsterdam, Holland. Has 38 years of experience.
Accredited lecturer and therapist/ teacher by the European Shiatsu Society where he functioned as the invited representative of Shiatsu in Holland 2007 and -

At the 4th International Congress for Shiatsu in October - 2009, for the FFST( French Federation for Traditional Shiatsu) in Le Canet, France. (see: www.shiatsu.nl)
International public speaker/teacher on this subject and its roots since 1972. English and Dutch languages. Runs and teaches at the Dutch School for Classical Shiatsu in the Hague. More than 100 students.
Medically qualified by the Dutch Institute for Psychological and Holistic Treatment (NVGN) in Holland.
Intent on the expansion and appreciation of this subject through exposing audiences to seminars at which live and public audiences can experience self-empowering techniques to be more impervious to stress and to prevent psychological and emotional disturbances and their consequences.


Wants the public to be aware throughout Europe, U.K. and U.S. of the depth of possibilities and not the surface.
