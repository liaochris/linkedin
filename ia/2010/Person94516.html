Jenna Broughton’s Summary

Jenna brings a combination of deep tech savvy, digital and social media prowess to her work. Her experience in high-tech public relations spans telecommunications, online marketing, mobility/wireless and enterprise security.

Recently, she worked at Waggener Edstrom Worldwide with high-tech companies that are market leaders and early entrants. As part of her ongoing role, she developed and executed communication plans that meld traditional communication strategies with dynamic media rich content and social media tools like blogging, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and YouTube. In addition to that, she regularly engaged with technology and trade press – securing case studies, Q&As and exclusives in key publications.

Prior to joining Waggener Edstrom Worldwide, Jenna was employed by Engage PR where she worked primarily with start-ups and high growth companies. During that time she performed M&A communications activities; launched companies coming out of stealth mode; and developed messaging and positioning that would resonate and get these early market companies on the radar of influential media and analysts.

Jenna earned her bachelor’s degree in Public Relations from the University of South Florida, with a minor in Economics.
