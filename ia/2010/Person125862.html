Lisa Bowen’s Summary

I am an expert in buying and building lists for business-to-business prospecting and lead generation. I help companies get the data they need fast so they can spend their time on what's really important for them -- reaching decisionmakers.

I can find the best lead-generation sources for your campaigns, or teach you to do it yourself.

As a list broker, media buyer and Google Adwords Professional, I help companies and agencies plan and place media orders for all their sales and marketing lead-generation campaigns. My company, Prospects To Go LLC, is your one-stop source for:

-direct-mail, telemarketing and e-mail lists
-e-mail list blasts and e-newsletter sponsorships
-web banners and all other online advertising
-paid search, vertical search, local search
-social-media strategy
-print ads
-outdoor billboards

I also offer online training on List Buying, Database Building and Prospecting, with topics such as:

-How to find more names at key accounts from business social-networking sites
-How to build a sales and marketing database that will feed you for years
-Become a list expert in an hour.
-Building an in-house prospecting database from scratch fast
-Choosing a sales-lead database: How to evaluate the expanding choices. Is there a difference between InfoUSA and Dun & Bradstreet?
-Your first/next 100 leads

Lisa Bowen’s Specialties:

Everything prospecting. Lead-generation media, from postal direct-marketing lists to Search advertising. Over almost 14 years, I've worked on business and consumer marketing campaigns.

I also help sales department select the best online lead databases and learn how to use them.
