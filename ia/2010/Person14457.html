Annie Presley’s Summary

Annie Presley is an accomplished fundraising consultant in the political and not-for profit arenas, and has enjoyed the privilege of spearheading many successful and fascinating campaigns. She offers clients advanced strategies designed and perfected by her unique business experiences working throughout the United States particularly in Missouri, Kansas, Washington DC and on Wall Street. As a fundraising devotee and developer of The Art and Science of Fundraising, Annie is pleased to have helped McKellar clients raise more than $700 million for charities and political figures over the last 30 years.

Currently, she is providing strategic counsel to UMKC and recently finished repositioning the Kansas City Ballet’s $38 million Capital and Endowment Campaigns toward successful completion. In the past she has managed high profile and successful efforts for the NLBM, such notables as Baseball Hall of Famer George Brett’s $1 million campaign for ALS and management of Kansas City’s Science City at Union Station Capital Campaign which generated $263 million.

Annie’s vast entrepreneurial and political experience is well known. In 1983, she started Legal Assistance, Kansas City's first temporary paralegal service company which still thrives today under a different name. Annie worked in politics as Finance Director for Senator Kit Bond’s re-election to the United States Senate in 1992. In 1993, she opened The McKellar Group and introduced the political-model of fundraising to non-profits. An early client, Swope Parkway Health Center was a believer, invited Margaret Thatcher to launched their inaugural Great Leaders event, raised more than $500,000, and drew more than 1600 people to Hyatt’s ballroom dominating the media. The success of this event positioned McKellar as the leading event fundraising firm for area non-profit organizations.

To read more, check out Annie's full bio on www.mckellarconsulting.com.

    McKellar Consulting is a fundraising consultancy serving non-profit organizations and political figures. As a fundraising devotee and developer of The Art and Science of Fundraising, Annie is pleased to have helped McKellar clients raise more than $700 million for charities and political figures over the last 30 years.

    The Art and Science of Fundraising is a highly customized system created to provide clients a plan to reach their fundraising goals. The Science behind our fundraising method was discovered by the knowledge attained through 30 years of success. It is in the charts, formulas, and calculations needed to reach goals. When we apply the Science to our clients’ plans, their specific goals become clearer, and the process a solvable equation. The Art entails stylizing what clients naturally bring to the table. We help them recognize their innate skills, build courage and confidence, and assist in personalizing the ways in which they can achieve their goals. When we help them discover and apply the Art, clients will have developed their own fundraising style, at their own comfort level. We specialize in fundraising efforts including annual fund campaigns, capital campaigns, cause-related marketing, feasibility studies, in-house development, partnerships with for-profits, public finance, sponsorship, strategery, and sustainable revenue.

    As a result of Annie’s extensive political contacts, McKellar Consulting also offers advice and counsel at the Federal, state, and municipal levels of government. This is the ultimate in leveraging privately raised dollars.
