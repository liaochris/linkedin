Brian Ko’s Summary

As a senior Digital/Marketing/Media executive, Mr. Ko brings a deep leadership focus to lead a diverse list of clients, vendors, and agency operations. Mr. Ko is currently involved in several consulting and advisory positions with agencies, digital startups, and media companies. In the recent past, as Senior Vice President, Marketing/Global Client Management Lead at Publicis Modem & Dialog (formerly also known as DIGITAS, a Publicis Groupe Co.), he had the added oversight of the worldwide account management, media and business services for the agency clients. With extensive multi-disciplinary marketing experience, Mr. Ko combines his diverse portfolio of traditional and digital creative and media knowledge to shape the organization's digital marketing and business development practice.

Most Recently, as Executive Vice President of Dedicated Media, Mr. Ko led the company's integrated digital strategy, media, and business development efforts across multiple client brands and categories. Previously, he was the Senior Group Account Director, at Initiative (an Interpublic Group Co.), where he led digital and integrated marketing strategy, client management, and new business pitches across multiple client categories, including Automotive: Hyundai/Kia, Games/Entertainment: LucasArts, Lions Gate Film, Consumer Electronics: Hitachi, Software: Intuit - QuickBooks/Quicken, Online/Education: University of Phoenix, Energy: ConocoPhilips. Preceding Initiative, Mr. Ko served in traditional and digital advertising/marketing leadership positions at several companies, including OMD/TBWA/Chiat Day, Grey Global Group, DONER Advertising, Team One/Saatchi, EER, Ford Motor Co., and The Miller Group Advertising across Auto, Quick Service Restaurants (QSR), and Retail industries.
