Rahul Desai’s Summary

Me:
Computer Science Engineer by qualification, I started my career in Software industry as programmer & then moved on to presales.
After enjoying presales & international exposure, entrepreneurship seemed obvious progression, which was well supported by my friend Ashish & we started Epsilon.

My Work:
Epsilon Consulting, is a HR Consulting firm focussed in Executive Search & Recruitment, Training & Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO). It is managed by a Team of like minded professionals, having experience in the industry, Executive Search, Training & successful track record in serving, hiring needs of companies. We are based in Mumbai & Pune.

In Executive Search, Epsilon specializes in various sectors & we have successfully done assignments in...
Retainer Executive Search
Project Based Recruitment
Contingency Recruitment
RPO

Our clients include reputed National & Multinational companies in various sectors.

Objective in Business:
Constantly trying to move up the value chain.
Constantly trying to Diversify.
We always look forward to greater client satisfaction & long term mutually beneficial asoociations.
We search, manage & develop client's human resource so that they can focus on business and grow.

Objective in life:
Create Value for the client.
Create Wealth & distribute the same.

Our Goal & Approach to business:
Our goal is to further build a HR Consulting firm that is a true partnership, with an enjoyable professional atmosphere, broadly shared ownership & control dedicated to the highest levels of client services, also providing a very attractive financial return to its members.

We long to create an organization that grows beyond the limits of caliber of its founders and a win-win platform for its clients and members.

Our Approach to Our People:
We believe that ordinary people can do extra ordinary things.

Our Dream:
Every Epsilon family member has enough to pay back to the society.
