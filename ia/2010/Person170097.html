Chris Reaburn’s Summary

Service manager and marketing strategist with over 10 years experience developing, implementing, managing and marketing world-class service experiences that realize companies’ strategic service vision and drive profitability.

Known for leading cross-functional teams through strategic market planning and new service development initiatives, incorporating voice-of-the-consumer into service design, service operations management and service marketing.

My mission: To improve the way companies design, manage and market service experiences for customer value and profit.
