Jonathan Graber’s Summary

http://threepillarsrecruiting.net/search.htm

Jonathan Graber started his career as a merchandise planning intern in Lillian Vernon Corporation's marketing department. There he got to see firsthand the transition from traditional print media to online media sales. After graduating with honors (Magna Cum Laude) from Yeshiva University with a B.S. in Finance, Jonathan joined First Investors Corporation as a financial services representative. Within two years he was promoted twice, managed a $1.5MM portfolio and was ranked as a top 50 insurance producer for the company. After two years Jonathan decided to leave his position to pursue an MBA at University of Georgia and join a new venture, Three Pillars Recruiting. Jonathan is a native of Memphis, TN and is a proud husband and father.
