Carl Schumacher 3800+’s Summary

Mr. Schumacher has been working for Electronic Search Inc. as an Executive and Technical Recruiter since 2000. Electronic Search has been a recruiting agency for 30 years and in that time has gained a reputation as being the top recruiting firm that specializes in wireless in the country. They have literally recruited, at one time or another over their tenure, for almost every major wireless company in the country.

His Motto is "jobs are temporary, but relationships can last a lifetime". Many of the candidates that Carl has placed in positions early in his career are now in executive roles and are his clients. By treating people the way you want to be treated it definitely comes back to you when candidates you placed are looking for a recruiter to fill positions in their company.
