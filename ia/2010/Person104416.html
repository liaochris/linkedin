Jonathan Luttwak’s Summary

Working in Cushman & Wakefield’s global headquarters, I specialize in commercial tenant representation work in Manhattan. I began my real estate career in 2005 following six years of service as an Army officer including deployments to Kuwait and Iraq. I have been involved with hundreds of real estate transactions throughout my career and have worked with clients that include Shearman & Sterling, Moelis & Company, Hearst Publications, AT&T, Goldman Sachs, Essar Global, Alcatel-Lucent, Flintlock Capital, Sound Harbor Partners, Octavian Advisors, Kohn Pederson Fox, and many others.

My focus is on long term business with relationships built on trust, character, delivery, and service.

My partners are a seasoned team of professionals who collectively have nearly 100 years of experience. Our team’s clients include Mass Mutual, Rabobank, the National Football League, Kaplan, and many others. The team includes some of the most respected professionals in the industry and was even chosen to represent Cushman & Wakefield in its own headquarters search.

Utilizing a global platform, I can help facilitate virtually every kind of commercial real estate service around the globe. From lease negotiations, project management, capital markets, real estate investment banking services, valuations, and consulting, we have the capabilities to creatively facilitate even the most complex real estate solutions.

If you have any real estate requirement, questions about your lease, your building, expansion, or are simply curious about the commercial market, please feel free to reach out to me.
