Cameron McLeod’s Summary

Cameron has combined a life-long passion for travel and a proven record in the corporate sales and management world.

Cameron has been with FCm Travel Solutions for over 7 years, the first 6 being at Global Headquarters (Flight Centre Ltd) in Australia. In May 2008, he relocated to Chicago in the role of International Business Manager to spear-head the growth of major business development.

In June 2009, Cameron was promoted to Regional Sales Director and is responsible for the corporate sales teams for major clients in central and eastern United States including New York, New Jersey, Chicago and Dallas.

He maintains strong friendships with clients, suppliers, colleagues, peers (and competitors!) around the world and this plays an instrumental part in his enthusiasm, motivation and accomplishments. Cameron prides himself on having a thorough attention-to-detail and seeking to understand people and situations.

FCm Travel Solutions is one of the world's leading corporate travel management companies. With a global presence in over 67 countries, FCm has an approach that is local, flexible and distinctly personal.

Prior to Cameron’s career with FCm Travel Solutions, he spent over 6 years with the Seven Television Network Ltd with similar success.
