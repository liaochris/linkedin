Ann Pearson’s Summary

Consultant to small and mid-sized firms to transform their paralegal groups from cost centers to profit centers.

In addition to PRM’s consulting services, I also provide in-house paralegal education programs to law firms of all sizes and develop the paralegal education conference series “Paralegal Resource Conferences by PRM.”

Ann Pearson’s Specialties:

Coaching attorneys and legal administrators on effective paralegal utilization, paralegal profitability and profession development for paralegals and other non-attorney staff.
