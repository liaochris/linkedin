Lisa Jones’s Summary

Lisa S. Jones is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of EyeMail Inc.com. Responsible for integrating audio and video, to bring traditional email communications to life. Under her leadership, she directs and oversees the strategic roadmap and development of the corporation, to include management of the international expansion and distributorship of the EyeMail brand worldwide.

Lisa has received several awards and honors recognizing her many accomplishments. She is the winner of Season 1 of the CBS Show, The Next Tycoon. She is rapidly emerging as a trailblazer for women owned technology companies, specializing in email and online innovation. Jones was selected into Microsoft Corporation Mentor Protégé Program and recognized as an Emerging Business Honoree for Women in Technology.

Prior to founding EyeMail Inc., Lisa advanced her career in the Telecommunications Business Sector. Prior to her career in Telecommunications, Lisa served as Logistics Implementation Director in Huntsville, Alabama, responsible for NASA mission critical projects for Marshall Space Flight Center and McDonnell Douglas Aerospace Corporation.

A native of Montgomery, Alabama, Jones received both her Bachelor of Science Degree and M.B.A from Alabama A&M University.
