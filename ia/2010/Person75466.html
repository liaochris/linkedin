Gina DeFrancesco (gina@nslpn.com)’s Summary

The National Senior Living Providers Network (NSLPN) is a premier online community for industry news and career opportunities for all professionals involved in the Senior Living Marketplace. We provide quality recruitment opportunities for employers and superior job seeking opportunities for industry professionals across all of the Senior Living disciplines.

Through a targeted job board and career center, relevant Senior Living news, industry information and articles, and a community forum, The National Senior Living Providers Network presents a multi-dimensional venue for professionals, employers, and recruiters who want to stay informed and in-touch to the Senior Living Provider community.
