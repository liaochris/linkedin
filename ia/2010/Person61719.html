Eddie Bell’s Summary

President & Founder of Horizon Search & Recruiting Inc. a Professional Recruiting & Personnel Consulting Firm & Texas Rural Healthcare Recruiters

The services we provide to “people” are important to me. We have an extensive network of World Wide contacts we network to source top level talent for most any position.

Although I have a background in law, years ago I chose to become an expert in the recruiting profession and view what I do is one of the most challenging, exciting and rewarding professions there is. There is a person looking for a Company to make a positive difference in their life; There is a Company looking for a person who can make a substantial, meaningful contribution to grow its business. The challenge and reward is to bring the two together and it is a good fit.

For me, and the foundation on which I built this firm, it’s not merely about filling a position and earning a fee. The perplexing dynamics aside, I never loose sight of the fact we don't deal with Companies and Candidates, we deal with people.

For job seekers, career, family and other considerations intertwine when people evaluate opportunities and my job is to present the facts, understanding people’s lives are involved. For hiring authorities my job is to uncover hidden talent, provide detailed background information, accept ownership of the entire process, then become an extension of you as your Ambassador to Top Level Talent..

I WILL NOT tolerate misrepresenting opportunity to a person for purposes of persuasion. I WILL NOT tolerate misrepresenting a person's competencies to fill a position. A “Partnership” of people working together to produce results beyond individual capabilities or efforts, isn't forged by “Companies” rather by people and built on a foundation of mutual understanding, confidence, respect and integrity.

If there is a good match that will grow a company’s business and effect positive change in a candidate’s life, let it. If not, let it go.
