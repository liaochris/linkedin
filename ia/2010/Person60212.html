Dr Tim Jaeger’s Summary

Dr Tim Jaeger has over 10 years experience as a lifestyle and wellness coach. He is an avid researcher and has one of the largest book collections - both offline and online - in the world. Dr Tim is very passionate about helping others create their best life and about being a major player in a global shift towards peace, synergy, harmony, and love.

Dr Tim has over 17 pages of goals that are updated consistently. Action plans and mind maps are huge to helping you create what you want in life.

It is rather ironic that Dr Tim is not a big believer in traditional schooling because he spent over $150,000 to become a Doctor of Chiropractic. What are your values and what is your purpose? That isn't taught in school.

Much of what creates happiness in life is self-taught and is a journey towards the excellence that resides within you.
