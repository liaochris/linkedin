Cathy Jones’s Summary

I am an experienced user experience & information architect. I have designed and produced around 100 projects over the past 11 years including:

- websites
- web apps
- interactive tv
- intranet tools
- e-learning
- flash games

I am flexible and hard working - you can drop me into a project at any point and I am quickly able to make a valuable contribution to structure, usability, taxonomies and information organisation and presentation at the tactical or strategic level.

I thoroughly enjoy user experience and information architecture and always bring enthusiasm as well as the latest knowledge to a project.

I can write valid xhtml tableless css code (abeit a little clumsily!) and know how to customise and implement cms driven sites. I have recently been able to apply these coding skills to creating rapid html, css & jquery prototypes.

I also teach web design, IA & UCD on a part time basis at Brighton University - a rewarding and challenging job.

I am always on the lookout for interesting paid or collaborative projects.
