Scott Henderson’s Summary

Scott Henderson is the senior Enterprise Architect for Allegis Group, Inc., in Hanover, Maryland. Scott’s experiences are rather eclectic, including service in the Air Force’s Electronic Security Command, lighting and scenic design for theater, opera, and dance companies in the U.S. and in Germany, systems infrastructure positions with a regional food brokerage firm and with an international portrait photography firm, technical work in major motion pictures, and as a Senior Upgrade and Infrastructure Consultant for PeopleSoft, Inc. Scott has served in the past as the chairperson of the HR-XML Consortium's Technical Steering Committee and as the co-chair of PeopleSoft's Staffing Industry User Group.
