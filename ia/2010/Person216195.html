W.L. Pulsipher’s Summary

W.L. Pulsipher has participated in the marketing and origination of Reverse Mortgages since 1993. He is President of American Reverse Mortgage® (ARM) which exclusively provides Reverse Mortgage and is one of the oldest and largest (Sixth in 2007) Reverse Mortgage Bankers in the United States. W.L. is a nationally known authority and a frequent guest speaker on the Reverse Mortgage subject to various service clubs, senior organizations, mortgage associations and mortgage bankers interested in the Reverse Mortgage. He became a Certified Senior Advisor (CSA) in 2000 and has many other CSA’s on the American Reverse Mortgage® staff. The ARM website is www.AmericanReverse.com.

Prior to entering the Reverse Mortgage field, W.L. was Operations Manager of Wayne Owens For United States Senate; President of HCS Corporation (AutoCAD Value Added Reseller-VAR); Northwest Region Trainer for Globe Life and Accident Insurance; Internal Control Manager for Xerox Corporation and Customer Service Manager for American Express Credit Card Division.

Past State Vice President of the Florida Motion Picture & Television Association (FMPTA); Past Vice President of the Film Commission of Real Florida; FAA Licensed Commercial, Instrument & Multi-Engine Pilot; Lifetime Member Delta Sigma Pi; Charter Member Florida Atlantic University Parliament of Owls.

W.L. holds degrees from Florida Atlantic University (BS Business Administration), Miami Dade College (AA Business Administration) and he graduated from Southwest Miami High School majoring in college preparation. FAA Commercial, Instrument & Multi-Engine Pilot. Mortgage Broker. He is a Florida Native, born in Miami, currently residing in Ocala, Florida, the home office of ARM.
