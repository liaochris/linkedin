Pawel Grochowicz’s Summary

I have 10+ years' experience in driving transformational change and growth at Executive and Board level in sectors ranging from Telecommunications, Retail and Aviation to Infrastructure.

I bring tenacity and a contagious positive outlook. I create opportunities where others see issues.

Through my untypically broad perspective and lateral thinking capabilities, I successfully connect people, operational/strategy elements and partners from multiple sectors and geographies towards powerful, synergistic outcomes.

  • General Manager Next Generation and Transformation

    Telecom New Zealand

    (Public Company; NZT; Telecommunications industry)

    November 2006 — January 2009 (2 years 3 months)

    I had executive accountability to establish, business case, secure the funding for and execute the transformation program to deliver an entirely new $2bn+ Service Provider business for Telecom's Retail division in New Zealand.

    I brought together the disparate teams and parts of the organization with select global partners, established the requisite culture of customer centricity, changed the work ethic and the metabolic rate. At the time of unprecedented capital scarcity, I secured the funding to build the requisite capability (the largest integration in the country this decade) and maintained the integrity of the program for two years through multiple changes at the Board and CEO level.

    With the Proof Of Work (50 live connections) delivered ahead of schedule and significantly under budget, I operationalized the program into the business.

  • Head of Strategy

    Telecom New Zealand

    (Public Company; NZT; Telecommunications industry)

    October 2003 — October 2006 (3 years 1 month)

    I helped establish and grow, and then led a 50-strong strategy group with deep hands-on operational mandate spanning wireline, mobile and internet provider divisions in Telecom's New Zealand Business.

    Focus was on bringing the three disparate divisions together for customers. The accountability spanned marketing strategy, product portfolio strategy, channel strategy, pricing, regulatory strategy and customer insight.

    With broadband penetration in New Zealand significantly below average, I helped establish, business cased and then co-directed the Telecom-wide broadband penetration program which saw broadband growth exceed 100% year-on-year, one of the highest rates in the OECD. Broadband growth has not reached similar levels since.

    Through my seat on the Plenary Forum of the Fixed Mobile Convergence Alliance, I shaped the strategic agenda and offer development across operators with 300m+ customers. I led the development of the first fixed-mobile converged offers in New Zealand, now penetrated to significant and sizable segments.

    Under my custody, Telecom grew the share of broadband and mobile, despite growing competition and regulation.

    - I was one of three leading a supply chain restructuring and national market roll-up through a $1bn+ 'clicks and bricks' play

    I was also involved pro bono in initiatives which changed the tone of business and growth in New Zealand, notably the Great New Zealand Business Venture, where I was on the business case review panel and The 'Knowledge Wave' conference where I organized a think tank.
