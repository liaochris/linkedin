Katy Habina’s Summary

Katy Habina brings over twenty years of information technology leadership to her role as a Program Manager in Wipro’s Global Delivery Organization. Her experience includes development and delivery of solutions in data architecture and business intelligence, IT management, and enterprise system selection, implementation, customization, and integration.

Katy’s experience is in the context of e-commerce, wholesale distribution, and corporate sales management, corporate learning, marketing management, customer satisfaction, and IT service.

She has advised clients in industries such as securities and investment banking, auto insurance, pharmaceuticals, food service, chemical, telecommunications, and energy & fuels.
