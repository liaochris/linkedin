Don Nadeau’s Summary

Don Nadeau is the former Co-Founder & Managing Broker for Timeshare Broker Services, and Timeshare HotDeal, LLC. Founded in November, 2005, both companies provide traditional real estate services of listing and selling timeshares and fractional ownership on the resale market. In 2009, Timeshare Broker Services closed over 1000 resale transactions, with over $16 million worth of timeshare intervals changing hands. Don is currently a licensed real estate broker in Florida, and also New Hampshire.

Timeshare Broker Services was the number one resale agent for Hilton Grand Vacation Club, an accredited Member of the BBB (Orlando and West Florida) and proud members of ARDA. The company’s industry contacts with developers, outside brokers, and closing companies set them apart from the competition. Sellers listing with Timeshare Broker Services see results because their properties are also advertised on the Timeshare HotDeal and Sell My Timeshare NOW websites, adding tremendous value and exposure for customers.

Prior to launching a real estate career, Don founded a Detroit-based sales engineering & marketing company in 1983 and held numerous senior level management positions in the automotive and electronics industries, with product inventions and manufacturing processes, spanning 35 years. Married for nearly 39 years to Diane Nadeau, the co-managing broker for TBS, they enjoy their 2 children and 4 grandchildren and currently reside in the Tampa area. They are avid boaters and love to fish for relaxation from their busy schedules.
