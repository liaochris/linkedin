Captain (Chris) Allen’s Summary

Nanticoke Maritime LLC is seeking to develop domestic and international markets for our new marine aftermarket product, SternMate™.

We are very optimistic about the continuing success of the recreational boating and fishing industry, but we do expect some type of change. That is not necessarily a bad thing. The passion for boating and fishing is an inherent part of most all cultures and we believe that people worldwide will continue the experience in some manner regardless of the economy. While "fast and fancy" may no longer fit the lifestyles of many, we believe that the same public will find some way to get out on the water. In fact, we firmly believe that the industry will grow (possibly exponentially) as more people will find and appreciate the simplicity and solitude of boating and fishing as history has already proved.
