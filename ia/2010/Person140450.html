Melanie Renzulli’s Summary

Old media/New media? Right brain/left brain? I've got you covered.

I have more than 10 years experience as a professional food, culture, and travel writer, and have written for a number of esteemed publications including Michelin Travel Guides, The Washington Post, and Saveur. In 2006, I created Italofile.com, which has become one of the highest rated blogs about Italy travel on the web.

Now, I am on a mission to conquer the social media circuit. In addition to maintaining a companion social networking site, Facebook page, and Twitter stream for Italofile, I am helping companies leverage the power of social media to interact with their fans and followers and legitimize their online brands.

Let me know if I can help you. Find me on Twitter at @melanierenzulli or @italofileblog.
