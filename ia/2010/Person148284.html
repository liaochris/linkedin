Molly Sandquist’s Summary

Sales and Marketing professional with a track record for growth.

- and more…

Growth and sales are the number one priority and every project must have a positive ROI. To me, a successful incentive starts with first understanding the objectives, then developing the right strategy, employing the right tactics and then carefully measuring the results.

As a Partner with cookingwithcaitlin.com, I get to spend part of my days helping to grow our start-up company. My focus is on PR and Marketing. - A Partner on Gannett's Cincinnati.com Blog Network

- Hosts of Dinner and a Movie on Star 64 (coming Winter 2010)
- Founders of Internationally Recognized Foodies' Night In (#fni) on Twitter
- Hosts of the Signature Event: Third Thursday

This, not to mention over the oversight of production of 70+ videos and hundreds of pages of website content.

In both positions, consider myself lucky to get to connect with people and work together to grow our businesses.
