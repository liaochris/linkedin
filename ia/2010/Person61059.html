Dave Wirsching’s Summary

An experienced consultant and successful entrepreneur, Dave Wirsching has provided advice and managed projects for a diverse set of local and international businesses and organizations ranging from Fortune 500 companies to not-for-profit entities.

With a significant experience in project management, customer care, marketing, and technology, Mr. Wirsching brings a wealth of knowledge and practical experience to his current activities in digital marketing. An avid participant in social media, he has been active in online communities since 2005 and a blogger since early 2007. He is regularly called on to provide presentations and participate on panels on a variety of digital marketing topics.

Mr. Wirsching is currently the Principal at Why About Marketing, LLC, a digital marketing consultancy located in Blue Bell, PA. He also maintains involvement in the title insurance industry as co-owner of Community First Abstract and as a contributor to the Clearing Title and PA Title Insurance blogs. He is active in the Pennsylvania Land Title Association and currently serves has the chair of its Technology Committee.

Mr. Wirsching is a member in a number of local business organizations including the Montgomery County Chamber of Commerce and the Eastern Montgomery County Chamber of Commerce. A Navy veteran, he is also a member of the Philadelphia Navy League.

Mr. Wirsching holds Bachelor of Science in Microbiology from Cornell University and a Masters in Information Technology from the George Washington University.
