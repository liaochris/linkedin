Nicholas Creswell’s Summary

I help organisations keep the promises they make to candidates and employees - which means these companies get better hiring, engaging and retaining the right people into the right roles.

Functionally, I've worked in graduate recruiting, executive search, employer brand, employee engagement, diversity, talent management and social media. From an industry perspective, I've covered technology, media, consumer products and professional services. And as for geography, I've usually worked in the UK, but have lived in France and worked in pan-EMEA and global roles, including extensive travel to locations across the U.S. and Asia.
