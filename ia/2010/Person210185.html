Valerie Orsoni is the CEO and Founder of the acclaimed coaching company in North America and Europe: MyPrivateCoach and the creator of the fastest growing online healthy living platform: LeBootCamp.
She is a sought-after weight loss expert and international speaker on motivation, women and entrepreneurship, nutrition, and healthy & permanent weight loss.
She survived a brain tumor, a cancer and a plane accident, which made her very strong and inspirational.
Originally from Corsica, Valerie now lives with her family in sunny Silicon Valley.
