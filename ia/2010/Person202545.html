Chris Cardozo’s Summary

Lead Generation is the core area of focus for The Ultimate Sales Connection™. With over fifty years of experience, we help businesses and sales professionals connect to greater opportunities. This is accomplished by an in depth discovery, assessment, recommendation and possibly implementation of strategies and concepts that will aid our partners in achieving greater success in this radically changing business environment.

We have developed several tools to benefit our partners that range from our Making Connections® Round Tables, Making Corporate Connections®, Virtual Connections Institute®, and Ultimate Connections®.

Making Connections® Round Tables benefit local professionals by providing a round table format to systematically connect in a structured environment to the networks of other business professionals. This format allows attendees to gain incredible networking connections and knowledge for immediate implementation!

Making Corporate Connections® provides businesses with sales teams of twenty five to one hundred a method of discovery, assessment, recommendation and implementation of a customized plan that will produce measurable results to increase revenue or decrease wasteful expenditures.

Ultimate Connections® is an annual commitment to an elite level of Leadership, Sales and Lead development. Teams of successful business and sales professionals of no more than ten will form to expand their network, receive education and design plans whereby participants will be challenged in the areas of life and business management.

Virtual Connections Institute® provides a web based curriculum of over three hundred modules for self and team assessments, development and management. The areas of study include: Leadership, Sales, Management, Customer Service, Administration and Marketing. This tool provides all professionals the opportunity to continually sharpen there edge to obtain maximum results.
