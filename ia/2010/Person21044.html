Chris Becker’s Summary

Prevailing interest in the area of enabling people to use the web to extend their presence and interaction with others anywhere. This includes identifying opportunities to create new ways for people to interact and experience their surroundings. Currently working on Filemobile: a company that provides web platform services for sharing and extending social media and networking - www.filemobile.com.

Previous experience in management consulting. Working with clients on large scale change initiatives including strategy articulation, organization design, merger integration, sales force effectiveness, customer segmentation, systems implementation (i.e. CRM, ERP, custom).

Experience working with organizations in North America, Western Europe, Russia and the Middle East in various sectors including energy, investment banking, retail banking, insurance, health care, professional services, retail, technology, government and non-profit.

Chris Becker’s Specialties:

Interactive: Web based application development, social media and networking, broadband video.

Management Consulting: Strategy development and execution, business case development, organization design, mergers and acquisitions, change management, client relationship management, sales force effectiveness, customer relationship management and customer segmentation.
