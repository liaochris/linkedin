Gerd Kueppers’s Summary

Developing and designing state-of-the-art electronics for consumer, industrial and military applications as a consulting electrical engineer and employee.

Having 20+ years of experience in analog, digital and mixed signal hardware design in the frequency range from DC to 4 GHz the applications range from low to high power electronics (1 kW). Many designs incorporate FPGAs and embedded mico-controllers. Simulating the topologies and circuits using typical engineering CAD tools I have been also deriving and implementing algorithms for micro-controllers.

Major design examples are available in consumer electronics (LNAs, Tuner, IF, Audio, Video etc.), bio-medical instrumentation (magnetic resonance imaging coils, temperature and pressure signal conditioning, cell culture rotators, syringe cyclers, magnetic stirrers, etc.), military and commercial rf applications (high power lasers, array antennas for electronic beam steering), industrial (dc-dc converters, stepper motor control for rotational beam switching of max. 6 mirrors; each less than 20ms switching time, etc.) as well as electro-optical and photonics applications (OTDR for eye exams, power meters, high power CW and pulsed CO2 and fiber lasers with high speed, low jitter electronics for scanning laser systems).

Most designs utilize newest surface mount technology with high component count on stacked multi-layer boards. In special cases the designs have to be robust regarding mechanical vibration for use in flight.

Currently I am enjoying DSP and embedded micro designs at a global manufacturer of high end audio electronics systems.

Also, I am planning on refreshing my knowledge on rf pcb design for 4G applications.

My goals are learning and succeeding, when taking on new challenges. My motivation is increasing the return of investment by successfully leading small design teams, providing efficient project management and achieving customer satisfaction.
