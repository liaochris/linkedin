Michael Margolis’s Summary

I am the author of Believe Me - a Storytelling Manifesto for Change-makers and Innovators.

As the President of Get Storied, I advise businesses, nonprofits, and entrepreneurs on how to get others to believe in their story. I also deliver keynotes and learning programs for groups in the business of "change".

I teach Brand Storytelling at the business school level and offer a range of training programs, from one-day workshops, month-long telecourses, and six month executive education.

With a background in cultural anthropology, I was previously a social entrepreneur -- co-founding and leading two innovative nonprofits. My work and ideas have been featured in Fast Company, Brandweek, and Storytelling Magazine.

Visit www.getstoried.com to learn more, or www.believemethebook.com to download a free pdf version of my storytelling manifesto.
