James Manning’s Summary

I am the founder of Mass Appeal Media, an Internet company with a primary focus of providing small businesses and non-profit organizations with their online media needs. Beyond designing websites, we educate small businesses on how to utilize the power of social network and interactive application to maximize their Internet presence. Our services include, online marketing campaigns, click thru campaigns, blog development, and video production. We also provide graphic design solutions for companies in need of brochures, flyers, postcards, business cards and organizational apparel.

Along with Mass Appeal Media, I am the host of the Mass Appeal Radio show which airs every Sunday at noon. I also write for a sports blog, www.allworldcrew.com and manage a political blog for Alderwoman Toni Preckwinkle.
