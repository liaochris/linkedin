Ron Holmes’s Summary

Over 16 years of professional work experience with Geographic Information Systems (GIS), with increasingly responsible project management duties. Major areas of expertise include the management of GIS programs and staff. Experienced with the implementation of complete enterprise systems including workflow and database design. Extensive experience working with all levels of government organizations, evaluating their GIS needs and providing cost effective solutions. Project management experience includes overseeing internal staff and managing contractors to perform a myriad of tasks. Additional experience using GIS to support business needs, including demographic and marketing analysis. Most recently held a DoD secret clearance.

Management and Leadership

• An extremely detail-oriented and well-rounded professional with over 16 years of experience building and directing teams in challenging environments, leading and training staff in the use of Geographic Information Systems and similar technologies. Project manager with an ambitious attitude and a willingness to provide mentorship and a learning environment for all subordinates. Able to grow positive relationships with customers and colleagues at all organizational levels.

Organization and Communication

• Experience and leadership capabilities routinely influenced executive level decision-makers. Facilitated and successfully achieved consensus often among disparate groups of company and/or municipal leaders with differing amount of buy-in towards GIS technology, which would often times challenge current methods of doing business. Very adept at selling the concept of GeoSpatial technology to a non-technical audience.
