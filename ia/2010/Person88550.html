James Staton Jr.’s Summary

www.debriants.com
See Bio on web site.
We are a single source equipment repair company. We repair office equipment regardless of the manufacturer.

We have found the practice of routinely throwing equipment away when fully depreciated costs 3 to 4 times what is required to repair and maintain the equipment.

After your manufacturers warranty has expired we provide repair and preventive service to optimize the operation and extend the life of the equipment beyond it's depreciated life.

Every year beyond that point gives you hard dollar savings equal to the replacement cost for that piece of equipment.

Our program has provided cost reductions of 35% in direct expense to our customers. Additiionally, we have increased productivity more than 50%, while providing service on 100% of equipment that is under contract.

This is a 80% improvement over the standard industry practice of only working on equipment when it fails.

Our (19) years of experience has established us as a leader in this arena.
