Jeff Zalla’s Summary

My goal as Managing Director of Diatom Ventures is to become the most-referred source of growth capital for great entrepreneurs who care deeply about corporate social responsibility.

I am a values-driven leader with proven expertise across all aspects of global finance, operations, corporate responsibility and communications. Experience in complex global environments, including four years as CFO of Chiquita, a Fortune 500 company with $4 billion in revenue. I serve on the board of Business for Social Responsibility, and within a decade, helped transform Chiquita’s 100-year-old reputation.

Jeff Zalla’s Specialties:

Private equity, global finance, corporate responsibility, communications, risk management, acquisitions and divestitures, change management.
