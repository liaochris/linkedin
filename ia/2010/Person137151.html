Matt Clark (mclark@sparkplug.net)’s Summary

I spent the last 14 years of my professional life building business grade wireless networks here in the Phoenix Metro area. In April 2007 my partners and I sold our interest in RNi (Renaissance Networking) to Sparkplug Inc (www.sparkplug.net) which then merged with Airband Communications (www.airband.com) in July of 2010. I currently reside in Scottsdale Arizona as the General Manager for Airband’s Phoenix operation.

Airband is the largest company in the country servicing business to business customers through a fixed wireless network. We currently offer service in 17 markets nationwide and we are always expanding. Our Phoenix operation is a significant market for Airband. We cover more square miles and have more tower locations than any of our other markets. Our subscriber count and revenue continue to grow and our customer churn rate is one of the lowest in the industry. In a sluggish economy, we have found that our customers need the speed and price point that Airband can deliver. Listed here are some of our core product lines (Internet access, Metro Ethernet, Voice service (PRI, Analog Line, SIP), Hosted VoIP services, Disaster Recovery Solutions).

My goal is to help grow this company and this market to new levels. With the close network of technology professionals in the Phoenix market as well as the southwest region, I believe the next 10 years will be an explosive time for Airband and the fixed wireless industry.
