Why LinkedIn:

Social Networking is growing. The benefits of professional relationships from services like LinkedIn, are vital, even necessary for the pace of business development today. Whether it’s finding good referrals, new business partners or a friend, marrying real world communication skills with online tools is, in my opinion, intensely valuable and rewarding.

To that end I offer the following:

► PROBLEM - Individuals and families in the 35-60 age range, are racing toward retirement. Too many Americans had a “spend now, worry later” attitude which is crippled them with debt. They have no cash flow or strategy for accumulating and sustaining income needed to support the lifestyle they crave. Instead, their lack of planning dooms them to financial insecurity, stress and misery in years to come.

► OPPORTUNITY - Our approach is different from others. Because we understand the fears and limitations that keep many people from taking advantage of present opportunities and planning. Because so many have so little time, we offer a ‘guided’ approach that rapidly increases real estate holdings, reduces monthly payments so their level of comfort increases accelerating the accumulation of wealth and passive income for life.
