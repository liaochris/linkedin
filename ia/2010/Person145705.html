Mike Maslyn Open Networker’s Summary

At the Financial Staffing Network, we differentiate ourselves from the competition by bringing a higher level of financial competency to bear in assisting our clients and candidates. We leverage our experience gained "in the trenches" to find the best solution to each unique situation. We deliver candidates to clients that have the requisite skill sets and character that will enable them to succeed.

For clients seeking talent (Temp, Perm, Project, etc), we strive to understand the "who, what, where, why, when and how much" questions that will enable us to provide only "Top" candidates. We take pride in our efforts to sort out the less qualifed candidates and will only submit the best to you. That's a promise!

For candidates seeking employment, we ask many of the same questions of you to assure a "good fit". We want to get to know you so we can effectively advocate for you and help you further your career.
