Greg Johnson, Esq.’s Summary

Giving Athletes & Artists is a company which develops and implements educational, physical and cultural programs and provides marketing and fundraising support to professional athletes and recording artists whose non-profit organizations serve at-risk and underserved youth in urban areas and on Native American reservations.

GJohnson Law, PLLC is a law firm practicing in (1) Complex Insurance Coverage (Garage, CGL, D&O, EPLI); (2) Consumer Finance and Dealership Litigation; (3) Sports Law; and (4) Native American rights.

Have comprehensive knowledge of commercial policy forms and represent both insureds and insurers in coverage and extra-contractual liability claims. Have written several insurance publications (listing of publications and seminars in folder below). Significant experience in construction defect and garage liability coverage litigation and contractual risk transfer issues. Have been involved in cases across the country and appeared before the Minnesota Court of Appeals, Minnesota Supreme Court and Eighth Circuit Court of Appeals on numerous occasions. Have prevailed in over 80% of appeals (listing of appeals and reported case law decisions in folder, below). Have drafted EPLI policies and manuscript endorsements for commercial auto and garage policies.

Considerable portion of practice related to representation of auto dealers and finance companies in individual and class action consumer finance claims under the Truth-In-Lending Act (TILA), Motor Vehicle Retail Installment Sales Act (MVRISA), Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA), Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), Consumer Fraud Act (CFA), Deceptive Trade Practices Act (DTPA) and privacy laws as well as identity theft and wrongful repossession claims. Have provided compliance training and drafted proposed legislation, consumer credit disclosure forms, commission plans, rental contracts, demo agreements and buy-sell agreements.

Alternative billing arrangements available.
