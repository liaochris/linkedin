Daniel Brown’s Experience

  • Product Manager

    Creaform

    (Privately Held; Mechanical or Industrial Engineering industry)

    February 2010 — Present (7 months)

    As the Handyscan 3D Product Manager, I am in charge of supporting the growth of the Handyscan 3D line of scanners. I have to guide our Sales force in offering the right solution for our customers’ 3D scanning needs.

    The Handyscan 3D scanners have become the most versatile and user-friendly solutions for reverse engineering, inspection, design and prototyping. Even with this success, we have not stopped innovating. Creaform still believes in innovation and continues to move forward in offering better solutions to the 3D technology community.

    My main objective is to make sure that the Handyscan 3D line of products continues to grow in the right direction, and continues to bring 3D digital solutions to more people.

  • Application Engineer

    Creaform

    (Privately Held; Mechanical or Industrial Engineering industry)

    March 2006 — February 2010 (4 years )

    I started at Creaform a short while after the launch of the Handyscan 3D on the 3D scanning market. I experienced the incredible growth that the company experienced in 2007 and 2008 firsthand. We were 60 employees at the time and we are now more than 225.

    During my time as Application Engineer at Creaform, I performed hundreds of scans for our customers. I worked on projects in medical, manufacturing, molding, tooling, forging, heavy industry, automotive, aerospace, design, prototyping, and so much more.

    I was also in charge of Handyscan 3D systems integration as well as training on our customers’ premises. I also worked on several product improvement and testing projects.

    With such hands-on experience of various hardware systems, I have very deep knowledge of Handyscan 3D products, Kreon scanners, Leica HDS systems and Romer CMM arms.

    As for software, I am an advanced user of VxScan, Rapidform XOS, XOR and XOV products, Geomagic Studio & Qualify. On the CAD side, I have a certain knowledge of CATIA V5, Solidworks, Pro Engineer and Dassault 3DVia Composer. I was also able to acquire basic working knowledge of Autodesk Maya, 3D Studio Max, ZBrush and Topogun for multimedia applications.
