Cecil Taylor’s Summary

20+ years in telecommunications and data communications, the last 16 in wireless data. After working for years in various R&D roles, I have transitioned in the last four years into the business side in product management, business development and sales/marketing.

My background includes 11 years at Nortel, including five as a software design senior project manager, leading telecom R&D organizations in developing large, complex, carrier-grade releases.

Cecil Taylor’s Specialties:
