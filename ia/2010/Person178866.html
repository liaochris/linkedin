Russ Blackburn’s Summary

I am a Raleigh, NC based freelance designer with a full range of graphic design experience - from highly complex e-commerce websites to simple, elegant print promotions. With over eleven years experience in advertising, I specialize in award winning web design that is clean, clear and focused on usability.     Graphic Design and Marketing Solutions for Your Business

    Blackburn Advertising & Design is a creative freelance agency that produces content for marketing and branding projects. We are a small, full-service advertising agency located in the heart of the Raleigh-Durham area. Our staff is composed of some of the area's best freelance designers, writers, and programmers. Our clients are located from Charleston to New York and many points in between.

    The idea behind our agency is to give clients a more personal, cost effective approach to their communication and advertising needs. Getting back to being more hands on and more involved with the clients we work with is something we feel is lost in dealing with bigger agencies. With us you deal directly with the people responsible for your project. We find that a one-on-one approach helps save time and money by opening up the communication between clients and design team.
