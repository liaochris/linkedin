Scott Moore’s Summary

I perform the roles of CEO and CTO for Indigo Interactive. Indigo operates a real time online lead scoring, validation and trading platform. Our system processes millions of leads per day finding the best lead at the best price for the buyer. Our company is born from over a decade of online advertising and marketing experience which pioneered online sweepstakes, utilization of rich media content for advertising and permission based email marketing.

We officially launched IndigoExchange, our trading platform, in October 2009. Since then we have seen double digit percentage growth in revenue month over month. It is exciting to lead a group of highly skilled professionals to build a new company where one did not exist before.

Prior to working in online advertising I led the global technology architecture, integration standardization strategies as a Senior Director for a Fortune 50 Telecommunications company in North America, Europe and Asia. My work there included participation in the leadership team and oversight of the technology infrastructure which developed into Sprint’s Internet Solutions group. Among other achievements I was appointed by Sprint’s COO to begin the work bringing together Sprint’s three disparate IT divisions. By coordinating with Sprint’s three divisional CIO’s this work started the process which lead to tens of millions of dollars in savings for capital and SG&A expense.
