Ian Silver’s Summary

I've been with the company now known as Ameriprise Financial for over 25 years, in a wide-ranging set of jobs and assignments, including Project Architect, Project Manager, Team Lead, Software Developer, System Administrator, Database Administrator, and Programmer/Analyst.

Prior to this job, and as an avocation for many years during, I had a career in youth work and summer camps. I was a camp counselor, drama specialist, songleader, and camp director, for both day camp and overnight programs.

I'm fascinated by technology and how we can use it to do great things, but what always drives me is the passion to make a difference, to bring something uplifting into the lives of those with whom I'm privileged to work.
