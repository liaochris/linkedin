  • Risk Counselor

    Smart Decision Services, Inc

    (Financial Services industry)

    August 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Advise C-Level decision makers on resources to increase revenues, lower costs and improve effectiveness of their organizations. For example: A 350 Employee technology manufacturer, was experiencing an employee confidence crisis from poor customer service. We developed a two pronged strategy that would restore BCBS as the insurance carrier at competitive costs or introduce a world class customer service administrator at the current cost levels. Plan A focused on restoring BCBS program at a competitive cost. Plan B required finding a service provider that would deliver world class customer service. The team prepared a comprehensive presentation of risk, vetted potential customer service organizations and implemented the replacement on time, retaining cost savings and regaining employee confidence. Year two, after a single digit rate increase, the client installed a comprehensive wellness program with the Plan B customer service element. .
