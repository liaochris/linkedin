Gary Kiss’s Summary

I spent most of my 25 year career on Wall Street as a Financial Advisor with several of the largest investment banking firms. I have earned several top salesman awards going back to 1984, as new associate of the year. I belong to various networking groups and have served on the board of several not for profit organizations.

I am currently the Chief Marketing Officer for an IT Recruiting firm where I utilize my prior sales expertise to assist my clients in achieving their current and future goals.

My goals are to guide professionals toward achieving their career objectives. My prior career helped individuals plan for their financial future, and now I am helping them realize their professional potential. Additionally I provide staffing solutions for organizations faced with technology staffing challenges.

Gary Kiss’s Specialties:

My innate ability to network has lead to a natural progression into executive search and staffing. Having worked for many years in the financial services industry; I have spent my entire professional career helping others. I would like to network with people who enjoy seeing others prosper and succeed through their efforts.

Innovation in technology keeps IT professionals in demand. I specialize in solving these issues, from startups to fortune 100 companies.
