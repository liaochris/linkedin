Oriana Crotti’s Summary

Given her background (7 years experience in the international online gaming field) and current location (settled in Malta since 2008), Oriana specializes in recruiting professionals for the i-gaming industry in Malta.

Italian native, Oriana graduated with full marks as a Bachelor of Linguistics with a Major in Social Communications from the Universita’ Cattolica in Milan.
She then moved to the UK where she spent 5 years working for the Sports Betting industry and gained considerable exposure to international business practice.
Oriana obtained her Master’s Degree in Marketing and HR in the UK.

On relocating to Malta in 2008, she joined the Antal International Recruitment group, where she has been successfully spearheading the group's I-Gaming recruitment sector throughout Europe.
She managed to establish herself as Antal International’s leading gaming recruitment consultant.

In March 2010, Oriana was hired by CSB Group (Vacancy Centre) as Account Executive, in charge of managing clients' recruitment needs within the gaming and IT fields.
