Bob White’s Summary

I'm a business lawyer. I work on business, technology, entrepreneurial and corporate finance issues and matters. I provide legal advice and corporate strategic counseling on technology law matters, venture capital, private equity and other corporate finance transactions, mergers and acquisitions, corporate governance, entrepreneurial company matters and situations, corporate growth legal strategies and other business, corporate and transactional legal matters. I utilize my law degree and my MBA along with my real-world experience to provide strategic counseling and legal advice to my clients. Recent representative matters include the sale of the assets of an internet retailer, providing advice regarding a company's website privacy policy, representing a telemedicine company in a private equity investment, representing an industrial chemical company in the sale of its assets, advising a health care services company in connection with an equity repurchase and an associated corporate restructuring and providing advice to a software development company regarding its software development documentation . I'm currently a member of the Firm's Technology and Entrepreneurial Companies Practice Group and its Corporate Law Practice Group.
