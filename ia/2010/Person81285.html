Helene Desruisseaux ("Desrusso")’s Summary

Both my companies are about preventing great ideas from dying without profitable implementation and preventing great people from being under-recognized and under-rewarded.

After years of being known as someone who makes problems go away and gets things done, I joined forces with the R4H Group who specializes in implementing strategies for entrepreneurs who need a more profitable online presence.

An interesting specialty of the R4H Group is its ability to turn a passive membership into an engaged community.

I am also known for someone who can reverse engineer your knowledge into profitable educational programs and products.

Or you might simply need a revamp and/or delivery of your training programs in an interactive engaging way.

Finally, I have an important side interest in helping executives and entrepreneurs who have a quieter style be more influential.
