Dmitry Green’s Summary

Dmitry Green is a professional in the financial services sector. He is the Head of Risk Management at BlueMountain Capital, a credit-focused hedge fund in NYC.

Before BlueMountain, he was the Product Manager for credit derivatives at BlackRock, where he built the credit derivative infrastructure. Prior to this, he was an Engagement Manager within the Financial Institutions Group at McKinsey & Co., advising major financial institutions on strategic and operational issues.

Dmitry received his B.A. in Physics from the University of Chicago, a Masters from Cambridge University and his PhD in Theoretical Physics from Yale in 2001, where he was an NSF Graduate Fellow.
