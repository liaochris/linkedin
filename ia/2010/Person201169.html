Terri Gaines’s Summary

Terri’s has a passion for mission-driven entities and her expertise in this area includes: the complete re-branding of all marketing collateral and event materials and collaterals for The Market Foundation at the Pike Market; the development of event branding and collaterals for the 2008 and 2009 American Heart Association Pacific Mountain Affiliate’s Heart Ball gala; the launch of Metro Parks Tacoma Northwest Trek’s a successful “So Close” multimedia campaign and the complete re-branding of admissions and recruitment materials for the University of Texas at San Antonio.

As Director of Marketing Strategy, Terri created and implemented a comprehensive three-phase process to brand marketing and creative services: educate : create : evaluate—a process that identifies the most persuasive messaging and visuals to engage the target audience. Her brand marketing skills include: marketing plans, brand strategy and creative execution for the mission driven.

Terri served two terms as the president of the Communication Arts Society of San Antonio from 1998 – 2000. She is a member of the American Advertising Federation and the American Marketing Association. In 2000 Terri was awarded the Business Journal’s prestigious 40 under 40 award. In 2007, she was recognized in the 2007 Who’s Who in Advertising and Public Relations. Terri has recently joined the Northwest Development Officers Association.

Terri Gaines’s Specialties:

Terri has over 15 years of experience in marketing, brand consulting, advertising, project management, and creative services. Terri’s track record includes developing marketing and creative strategy for government, non-profit organizations, and institutions of higher learning. With the majority of Clockwork Studios’ clients in these categories—Terri understands the unique messaging and fiscal needs of such organizations and institutions.
