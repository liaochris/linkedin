Bill Williams’s Summary

Bill Williams has been helping to propel the electric car movement in the United States since 1995 as a Marketing and Sales Executive. He has focused his efforts in the Alternative Fuel Transportation industry. For the past twenty years he has held executive positions with Planet Electric, EVI International and ZENN Motor Company as Vice President of Sales in North America and Director of Sales in the Western US. He has also worked with Lee Iacocca, Bombardier, and US Government agencies. He has been a regular spokesperson for the EV movement.
His volunteer efforts include many years of non-profit experience, such as, serving as the Kansas state chairman with the March of Dimes, Board Member of the Arthritis Foundation and as a fundraiser for the Make-A-Wish and Leukemia Foundation. While continuing his involvement with the EV industry, Bill is bringing his organizational development expertise can get results for your organization's future. Bill believes in creating multi-stakeholder initiatives that benefit all parties involved while also serving the greatest good. His positive energy and hard work ethic will be a great addition to your team.

He has his home running on 33 Mitsubishi Solar Panels for over three years now.

Bill Williams’s Specialties:
