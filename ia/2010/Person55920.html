Dave Hurst’s Summary

Twenty six years in the Telecommunications Industry, (15 years as a engineering consultant and business owner) has allowed me expertise in the fields of RF testing and analysis, antenna design, testing, network operations, problem solving, customer service, US and Foreign government licensing application preparation, and the ability to work with a variety of professionals. I have over tens years experience in program and project management. I have in depth knowledge of computer software, hardware, control systems and most operating systems. I have extensive hands on experience with fiber networks, data, digital and analog video and voice systems.

Dave Hurst’s Specialties:

I have designed video conferencing networks, internet carrier circuits, performed countrywide site surveys for telephone and data networks. I have prepared RFQ’s and proposals for large banking institutions and maintained a large database of vendor contacts. I have built (turnkey) over 300 communications facilities around the world. I specialize in all aspect of satellite and microwave systems and connectivity for these types of facilities
