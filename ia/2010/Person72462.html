Gail Parminter’s Summary

I'm currently working at Dialogue141 as a Senior Copywriter working on all the agency's accounts. I present all work both internally and to clients, and work closely with the other agencies involved in the account as part of inter-agency loop teams.

During my varied career I have worked ATL, BTL and TTL. My experience covers many areas including FMCG, automotive, retail, financial, healthcare and beauty. I have also created concepts and written copy for all media including TV, press, poster, digital (websites, online ads, virals, email) and radio.

I have a degree in English from UCL and trained as a copywriter on the late John Gillard's pioneering creative advertising course at the Berkshire College of Art and Design.

I've also been lucky to have worked under some of the best copywriters in the business including Tim Mellors, Andrew Cracknell and Patrick Collister. The main thing all my mentors have taught me is that ideas aren't just important, they are everything.
