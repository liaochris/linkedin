William M. Joyce’s Summary

Goal:
To give select private investors access to and a full equity platform of participation in the acquisition opportunities available in non-public markets for non-syndicated, distressed, senior, commercial mortgages, traded at steep discounts to par, in one-off transactions, with the least risk to and greatest returns for their capital.

Expertise:
Banking, bankruptcy, real estate and corporate law, particularly secured and secondary mortgage market transactions, regulatory compliance, real estate development financing, commercial, foreclosure and bankruptcy litigation, and business organization and governance.
