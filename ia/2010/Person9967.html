Amy Auer’s Summary

Creative, supportive and energetic Career Counselor with over nine years in career and employment services and significant experience in higher education. Enjoys connecting people to their passions through one-on-one meetings and group programming that focuses on individual professional development including self assessment, resume and cover letter writing, interview preparation, and graduate school applications. Serves as the liaison to the Kalamazoo College Business Guild and also specializes in students interested in careers in the arts.

Amy Auer’s Specialties:

Career Development, Public Speaking, Museum Studies, Moroccan architectural history
