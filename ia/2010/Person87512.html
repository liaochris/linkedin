Jake Moore’s Summary

My passion is building, restoring and rehabbing buildings with a special affinity for historic structures, especially if there can be exposed brick.

My legal background enables me to bring many intangibles to a project. I am a visionary designed to see the big picture and the long range viability of a project. I like short term profit, but want projects I can be in for the long haul.

Who I work with is an important as any project I do. I no longer do handshake deals, but want to work with people that I could do them with.

In addition to real estate I participate in an eclectic mix of small business ventures. My strengths run to the business end of the transaction and I actively seek to partner with people who are passionate about what they do. I've partnered with everyone from tattoo artists to gun shop owners.

My greatest goal in life is to change the world. I do that by doing business as I wish it were done to me. I give time and resources to non-profits I believe in with a special passion for third world social development projects including drilling wells, building schools and building hospitals. Though I may not change the world for everyone, I seek daily to make it better for someone.
