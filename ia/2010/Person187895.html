Sharon Williams’s Summary

Originally from the UK, I've worked in Marketing and PR for over 20 years in London, Europe, Hong Kong, throughout Asia and now in Sydney. My experience before founding Taurus, included Management Training with Marks and Spencer UK, in-house Marketing Management in the IT, Shipping and Travel industries. I set Taurus up in 1995 working with first client Peter Kazacos of the KAZ Group and helped build the KAZ brand into the largest Information Communications Technology (ICT) company in Australasia, bought by Telstra in 2004.
I'm passionate about empowering, educating and inspiring people. My credentials include public speaker, trainer, CEO, media commentator, international marketer, entrepreneur and editorial contributor. As a highly experienced mentor I have frequent speaking engagements here in Australia and overseas.
I contribute regularly to Dynamic Business magazine and am also a resident SME blogger for ninemsn and have featured on Channel 7’s Kochie’s Business Builders and Sunrise as their guest marketing expert.
