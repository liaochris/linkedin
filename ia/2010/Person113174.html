Kevin Dugan’s Summary

Talented senior manager with progressive record of success and nationally recognized achievement within highly competitive consulting environments. Blends exceptional marketing communications acumen with strong strategic focus, creativity and the ability to guide teams in polished tactical execution. Natural leader and “big-picture” thinker; with more than 17 years experience driving results in integrated marketing communications. Dugan has been blogging since 2002 and speaks at industry events across the nation.

Kevin Dugan’s Specialties:

Proven expertise in wide variety of internal/external communications, Internet marketing, social media, all facets of media relations, blogging, public speaking, custom publishing, special events and process development.
