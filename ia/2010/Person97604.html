James Bailey’s Summary

I have more than 25 years of experience in corporate communications acquired in Canada, the UK and the US--doing everything from managing media relations for a telecommunications product launch in Ankara, Turkey to acting as a one-man IR team for the IPO of a new financial institution in Kingston, Jamaica.

I've written executive speeches for events ranging from the World Economic Forum at Davos to birthday greetings for Princess Anne. My clients have included C-suite executives, cabinet ministers and many other business and political leaders.

The fourth edition of my book, "The Law Enforcement Handbook" was published in January 2009.

Strategic planning, media relations, investor relations, speechwriting, event coordination, direct marketing, advertising copywriting, book writing--at one time or another I've done them all. Right now I'm particularly interested in working in social media, which are clearly going to be as ubiquitous in corporate life as email and the fax machine.

Before long, every aspect of traditional corporate communications activity is going to be transformed by the addition of executive blogs, internal wikis devoted to training and policy development, consumer feedback microblogs, Youtube-style videos, and hundreds of other features to the mix. I want to help clients facilitate that transformation.

If you think we might do some good work together, give me a call at 416.505.5617 or email me at jgbailey@rogers.com.
