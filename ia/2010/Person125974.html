Lisa Ellis ACMA’s Summary

The Financial Management Centre is a leading financial management consultancy that is committed to helping organisations achieve performance excellence.

In today’s challenging environment, there are many untapped opportunities. Capitalising on them may require new management methods, efficient utilisation of assets and exceptional financial management talent and knowledge. We enable you to succeed through our innovation, performance and passion.

The Financial Management Centre provides you with the necessary skills and insight through its highly qualified and experienced professionals. Whether you need a part-time bookkeeper for a new start-up, a part-time and highly experienced financial controller, a complete finance and accounts department, or an entire enterprise resource planning system, we can examine, develop and answer your needs.

We have three categories of service for organisations of all sizes and in all stages of growth. These services are The Online Bookkeeper, The Local Bookkeeper and The Local Accounts Department.

• The Online Bookkeeper is an innovative and collaborative service that is highly flexible in its delivery to the client. It combines secure and stable online accounting software with the professional services of The Local Bookkeeper.

• The Local Bookkeeper delivers bookkeeping, management accounts, credit control, payroll and self assessment tax return services. It becomes a ‘financial cornerstone’ within your business, providing regular and up-to-date information in language that is easy to understand, giving you the tools to drive your business to success.

• The Local Accounts Department provides professional accounting and financial management services to ambitious and fast-growing businesses, helping them to raise response and performance levels. It enables businesses to outsource entire accounts departments, cutting costs while providing a finance department with the security of qualified and experienced experts.
