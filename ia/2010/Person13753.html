Anllelic Lozada, M.S.’s Summary

Marketing and Communications entrepreneur with education in Advertising, Psychology, Marketing and Business; Professional experience in B2B marketing, online publishing, internal and external communications, product development, media relations and online advertising for Multicultural Markets.
