William Ziegler, PMP’s Summary

PMI certified Project Management Professional (PMP) with over twenty years of management experience and thirty years of engineering experience. Excellent strategy, communications, computer and organizational skills. Member of Montgomery County Nanotechnology Business Development Forum Planning Committee.

Management experience includes hardware, software and webpage development for government and commercial entities in the United States and abroad.

Product and technology development experience includes: optical data storage technology R&D, secure online storage networks, remote sensing, robotic actuation systems, fiber optic sensing and control, fiber and free space optical communications and imaging technologies, fly-by-light and GPS denied systems architectures.

William Ziegler, PMP’s Specialties:

Program/Project Management, Risk Management, Cost Analysis, Opto-mechanical Engineering, Team Building, Mentoring, Full Lifecycle Product Development, Intellectual Property Control, Product Quality Control, Contamination Control and Bid & Proposal Writing
