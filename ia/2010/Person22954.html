Betsy Zikakis’s Summary

Seasoned executive and marketing professional. Specific strengths in branding, lead generation, product marketing and product strategy.

As head of marketing at RightScale, Engage, and Workscape, I have been responsible for all marketing functions, even IR at one point. At RightScale, I am responsible for building the marketing organization, creating corporate awareness as well as generating leads through both inbound and outbound marketing programs.

During my tenure at Engage, I grew the marketing department from just a handful of people. Together, we quickly drove brand awareness from almost nothing to over 50% in an incredibly noisy market, generated leads that resulted in a run rate of $250 million in revenue annually across five product lines and three continents, and through an IPO and seven acquisitions.

At Workscape, I was hired to re-start marketing. That task included redesigning the marketing function, hiring top talent, creating a clearly positioned brand, and building a lead generation engine and a new product strategy.

I’ve run marketing organizations as small as 3 people to as large as 117 people and budgets from $300,000 to $22 million.
