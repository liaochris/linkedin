Todd Dean’s Summary

A proven visionary and leader in the entrepreneurial investment community. Created a powerful and collaboritive environment for bringing together like-minded community leaders, business executives, angel investors, serial entrepreneurs and early-stage companies that translated into investments in over 102 companies, exceeding $61 Million dollars placed in start-up companies and other investment opportunities from the Northwest. Thus, creating history in the accomplishments of the amount of angel capital and number of companies funded under his leadership. An expert in connecting individuals for mutual benefit; bringing angel investors to investment opportunities and providing resources and capital to companies. A dynamic public speaker and facilitator of monthly forums, educational academies and social events for the private angel investor membership. Very knowledgeable in helping entrepreneurs receive capital and valuable resources, creating the future economics of our region and country. Dedicated to maintaining a reputation built on quality, structure, consistency, and uncompromising ethics.
Todd donates considerable time and energy to several regional charities and non-profits including being a board member of Junior Achievement and the Montana Ambassadors.

My other passions are spending time with Dana and our 5 boys; Scott (14), James (13), Trent(8), Gage(7), and Michael (6).

Dana and I also enjoy doing hot yoga 4-6 times a week.

Todd Dean’s Specialties:

The reason for bringing Keiretsu Forum to the Northwest was to create a foundation of expertise, resources and capital to help our entrepreneurs be successful.
