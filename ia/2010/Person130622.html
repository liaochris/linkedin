Mandie Holgate’s Summary

With years of Coaching experience, I successfully formalised my studies with the U.K. Coaching Partnership.

I have clients come to me from all walks of life to tackle issues, realise ambitions or to achieve personal and professional goals. Professionally I have worked with people’s productivity, proposals, time management and marketing to name but a few areas, and personally the list is endless.

I currently write for the newsletter “Life Loves” as it gives me another opportunity to bring Life coaching to people from as far afield as Australia.

I am also the resident Business Coach for "Newsround" a Magazine in North Essex.

I realise that my unique skill is that I naturaly connect with people. I enjoy helping people get the most out of their business through Coaching and Networking. My goal is to always be able to have the opportunities to continue to do this and to to extend the opportunities to do this.
