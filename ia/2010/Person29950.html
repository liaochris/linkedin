Bruce Snyder’s Summary

I am an engineer and consultant for SpringSource where I spend most of my time working on SpringSource tc Server and SpringSource Hyperic HQ.

I am a member of the Apache Software Foundation and I work on a few Apache projects. I've been involved in open source for over 15 years and do everything from designing and developing software to building communities and everything in-between.

I also write articles and books on open source, I speak at conferences around the world about open source and I build communities around open source. I reside near beautiful Boulder, Colorado with my family where i enjoy cycling, running and anything outdoors.
