Nate Erickson’s Summary

I'm a graduate of Michigan State University, Class of 2009, with a B.A. in Advertising. My main focus was management, but I made the most of my education by developing skills in several different areas, from public speaking to digital media.

I took on a highly-active role with Cedar Banks, MSU's student ad agency. We developed an anti-binge drinking campaign for the 2009 AAF NSAC. Incorporating non-traditional and digital media, we created a campaign that spoke to students on their level. I served as the lead copy editor for our plansbook and was later chosen to a five-person presentation team to present our campaign at regionals, where we placed 2nd.

I also spent the semester on the e-board of Hubbell Connections, MSU's student PR firm. As Creative Director, I designed the agency logo, as well as creative materials on behalf of our clients.

As an intern at Ingenex Digital Marketing in Ann Arbor, MI, I have become savvy in the use of Web 2.0 to connect businesses with people. I believe the time I've spent at Ingenex has more than prepared me to make the new media revolution my own.

All three experiences gave me the opportunity to work with incredible people, and in a short time, I've had the benefit of gaining knowledge and insight that will serve me long into my future.

I am captivated with the growth of social media and the infinite possibilities that it holds for the future of business. The ever-evolving nature of the web is fascinating to me, and will continue to change the way we communicate, in both our personal and our professional lives. In my career, I hope to be part of that innovation.

I would love to work in a major city where I could showcase my talents and desire to take on any challenge. With my passion, creativity, and a love of solving problems, I hope to make a living using all three while treating every obstacle as an opportunity to improve.

Contact me here or email me at natemsu@gmail.com. Hope to talk to you soon.
