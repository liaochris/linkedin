John Hogan, CHA CHE MHS PhD’s Summary

As a successful senior executive with accomplishments in leading hospitality industry organizations, my passion is blending pragmatic traditional hospitality with “ high-touch” and “high tech” to significantly improve financial, staffing and operational results.

• Frequent speaker at Franchise, Management Company & hospitality association events

• Author for global online services with almost 400 articles published & co-authored of hotel sales book

• University adjunct professor at 3 institutions over 20 year period, while managing with Sheraton, Hilton, Dunfey, Park Suite & independents

• Professional experience includes over 35 years in hotel operations, sales & marketing, training, food & beverage, management development & asset management on both single & multi-property basis

• Service as Senior Vice President of Operations for a client in specialty hotel brand for 8 years

• Service as principal in independent training & consulting group for more than 12 years serving associations, management groups, convention & visitors’ bureaus, colleges & as expert witness

• Created & developed a blended learning system as the Director of Education & Cultural Diversity for world’s largest hotel chain

• Earned 5 industry certifications

• Past recipient of operational & marketing awards from international brands in General Management, Marketing, Housekeeping and Renovations

• Serve on several industry boards dealing with education and/or cultural diversity

• Have led an estimated 3,200 workshops & seminars
