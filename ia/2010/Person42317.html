Cohen, Matthew’s Summary

I am an Associate Group Director at Signature Bank, a multi-billion dollar private commercial bank serving the needs of privately held business in the small and middle markets. Signature’s unique model of banking offers our clients a single point of contact in accessing all of our services and a dedicated, experienced private banker to manage their relationship.

My portfolio of clients includes borrowing and non-borrowing companies, as well as high net worth individuals. I have experience in a multitude of specialties including accounting firms, law firms, staffing companies and the entertainment industry, including several high profile musicians and entertainers.

I bring a strong business background to my clients, as well as a spirit of creativity that I developed as a classical musician prior to entering the banking world. I attended the Juilliard School and Mannes College of Music as a french horn player. The ability to work with privately held businesses to help them grow and provide first class consultation and service continues to be my motivation as a private banker. I truly believe in Signature Bank’s ability to offer a better banking relationship for companies and their owners.
