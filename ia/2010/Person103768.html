Are You Looking For A Job?

Are you frustrated with finding the perfect job, and then applying?

And, waiting…

Stop the Frustration!

I’m sure you’ve heard, “It’s easier to get your foot in the door, if you KNOW someone at the company!”

It’s TRUE!

Employers hire more Referrals than any other type of new employee!

But you say, “I don’t know anyone at the company?”

Really??

What if you DO know someone, but you didn’t KNOW it?

What if an old friend from college works there… but you didn’t know it?

What if an old co-worker now works there… but you haven’t talked to them in months, or years?

What if your neighbor, or someone from your church, or local industry networking group, or your children’s sports team, or even that person you see at Starbucks all the time, works there?
Or, What if your friends knows someone who works there?

Another missed opportunity!

Getting Hired is about building connections with people at the companies.

What if you and your friends don’t know anyone?
Want to learn how to find employees to connect with?

Wouldn’t it be great to have a long list of online friends who could help you get your foot in the door at almost any company you want to work for?

You Can!

In fact, you can start building your own Referral Network, Today!

As the founder of one of the first Internet job boards, I have helped millions of job seekers find jobs! Additionally, I have been on the front lines of the many frustrations facing so many job seekers.

There are jobs out there! You’ve probably found several, and even applied for them. But you never hear back!

It’s a frustrating mess!
