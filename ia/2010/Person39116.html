Christina Tobin’s Experience

  • Founder and Chairman of the Board

    The Free & Equal Elections Foundation

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Free & Equal Elections Group (www.freeandequal.org) is a coalition of political parties, independent citizens and civic organizations formed to promote free and equal elections in the United States. Our services include legal assistance to defeat the difficult ballot access laws created by the two-party duopoly and coordinating nationwide ballot initiatives, referendums and recall efforts.

    Free and Equal Election organized a Presidential debate broadcast live nationwide on C-SPAN from Washington, DC. Free and Equal also organized a Vice-Presidential from Las Vegas, Nevada. You may view these debates at www.freeandequal.org.

  • President - CEO

    Free and Equal Inc.

    (Management Consulting industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Free & Equal, Inc. is a full-service ballot access consulting & petitioning firm specializing in ballot access for independent & third party candidates.

    Free & Equal, Inc. is a non-partisan firm that works with candidates from across the political spectrum. Free & Equal, Inc. does not endorse any candidates for office.

    Client Candidates: www.freeandequalinc.com/past-candidates/
    Client Endorsements: www.freeandequalinc.com/client-endorsements/

  • National Ballot Access Coordinator

    Ralph Nader For President

    (Political Organization industry)

    February 2008 — November 2008 (10 months)

    National Ballot Access Coordinator

    As Ralph Nader’s National Ballot Access Coordinator in 2008, I helped collect more than 500,000 signatures to put Nader on 45 state Presidential ballots and the District of Columbia, more than any other third party or independent candidate. While coordinating the National ballot drive in 50 states, I organized a successful petition drive in Illinois, Pennsylvania, Connecticut & New York. I'm non-partisan and helped gather signatures for Independents, Green, Constitution, Republican, Democract & Libertarian Party. I'm an expert in both defending signatures & coordinating petition drives.
