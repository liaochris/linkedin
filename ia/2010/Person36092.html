    (Privately Held; Investment Management industry)

    July 2009 — Present (10 months)

    Cambridge Advisors, founded in 1987, is an independent employee-owned investment advisory firm located in Richmond Virginia, and registered with the Securities Exchange Commission. A partial list of services provided for our clients are financial, business, investment, retirement, and education planning. Cambridge utilizes world-class recognized financial service providers such as Fidelity, SEI, Morningstar, and Genworth to give our clients peace of mind in the turbulent world we live in. In other words, Cambridge provides third-party certainty for their assets as well as regular performance reporting. Our independence allows us to be totally objective and client centered, and our clients always know where their assets are and how they are performing. To insure our objectivity we generally maintain a fee-only relationship with our clients, and fully disclose all fees prior to a client engagement; therefore we are stakeholders in our client’s future success.
