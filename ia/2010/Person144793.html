Miguel de Reina’s Summary

My professional career after more than 20 years can be summarized in one word: sales.A vocational activity that I love.

Twelve years ago the Internet began to revolutionize the world and also my professional life. Since then, Internet & Sales are a perfect match in which I will continue to develop. Brands like Antevenio, Terra and Google have enabled me to learn a lot in Spain and around the world: Latin America, U.S. and EMEA.
People & Partners and Advisors & Partners, my last two corporate initiatives are projects of a very long journey. The keys: The good and true professionals with solid values who know and want to develop in the vast world of possibilities, which is presented to us in the digital age.
