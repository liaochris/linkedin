Rod Boothby’s Summary

At Joyent, I lead a team that focuses on our largest enterprise clients, helping them get the most out of Joyent's secure public and private cloud offerings. We work with our most security conscious clients to implement comprehensive protection within the cloud at the network, storage, OS, application, physical hardware and interface layers. I also work with Joyent's partners to make enterprise grade commercial software available in cloud.

My background includes 12 years in financial risk management and information systems. Highlights include 2 years as a Manager in Ernst & Young LLP's Financial Services Advisory practice and 4 1/2 years at Wells Fargo where I was the Derivatives Systems Manager and worked with the Bank's Chief Risk Officer to help redefine the Bank's lending practices to be compliant with Basel II regulations. I have also worked for Internet start-ups Integral and Teqlo in business development and product management.

I havea BA Hons. in Economics from Canada’s Queen’s University and Masters in Economics, specializing in Financial Engineering and Econometrics from Canada’s Simon Fraser University. I live in San Francisco with his wife Cindy and our two daughters.
