Dr. Marc Kossmann’s Summary

Feeling lost and overwhelmed by the Online Jungle?

Thought you were at the summit of your career, only to discover you’re trapped on a plateau?

Can you see the Summit, but not the shortest path to get there?

If you are a mid-career, mid-life professional or business owner who can say yes to these questions, we’d like to chat with you. Our goal is to help you regain your vision on the path to the pinnacle of your success.

At Personal Success Marketing, we eat, breathe and sleep one mantra: Trading time for dollars will never get you where you want to go. Your income will never grow greater. You will surely run out of time in your day.

We’d like to show you a better way. We call it the 40/20/40 System, and it represents a 100% transformation of your business model.

We focus on mid-career professionals like you specifically because you are highly trained, highly skilled and have the expertise to take what we’ll teach you and hit the ground running. After all, the second half of your career and your life is the perfect time to convert your skills and experience, and knowledge into gold, don’t you agree?

When we show you how to combine your talents with your unique personality, you are then ready to Create Your Own Legend, shedding the collective identity of your professional peer group. We will show you how to break free and stand alone as a unique professional personality who commands a unique price point. We will help you to create a powerful social media marketing platform using our The Universe Model of Social Media Marketing.

And we will help you convert years of knowledge, experience and expertise into today’s hottest information products…products that thrive in the Online Jungle. 

Forget trying to "stand out." We say Get Out. You are not a commodity. There is untapped power in your story. Learn how to use it!
