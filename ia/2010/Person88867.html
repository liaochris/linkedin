Jamie Prince’s Summary

For more than ten years, I've been working alongside passionate team members to showcase dynamic organizations to their stakeholders, members, community, and the greater public through strategic marketing and communications tactics, leading to heightened brand awareness and company growth.

I founded FLOURISH after having served in management positions at Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia and subsequently for The Cliffs Communities. I have a knack for targeted idea generation and “seeing what‟s missing” in an organization‟s current marketing and communications plan.
