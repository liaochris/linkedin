Christopher Haig’s Summary

Training as an actor allowed me to develop an understanding of the human machine. How it thinks, moves and speaks both vocally and physically. Completing that training at the University of the Arts in Philadelphia, PA allowed me to connect with artists and performers from every discipline of the arts and entertainment fields. One of the motivations behind the establishment of the Society of Collaborative Imaginationists was the desire to see artists join together in new and exciting ways to produce high quality, innovative and inspiring creations.
After completing my masters in Arts Administration next year, I plan to work in the arts and entertainment industries and establish SOCI as a real-world presence.

  • Founding Director

    Society of Collaborative Imaginationists

    (Think Tanks industry)

    March 2002 — Present (8 years 5 months)

    Our goals at the Society of Collaborative Imaginationists (S.O.C.I.) are to reinvent, recapture and rekindle the lost art of imagination and facilitate collaboration between diverse artistic disciplines. The world of entertainment today seems lacking in this most vital of ingredients to its very existence: imaginative collaboration. This, in our opinion, is due to the abandoning of imagination and creativity for the more lucrative use of stereotypes and “proven” storylines.

    At S.O.C.I., we hope to inspire the artistic community to push their work beyond the limits and expectations of today’s audiences. To challenge them to think of worlds and ideas they may not have even dreamed were possible. To connect and collaborate. To entertain and enlighten. To provoke and persuade.
    By gathering intelligent, educated and experienced artists together, we develop a powerful community of artistic imaginationists and produce long-lasting, world-changing works of art together. We will neglect no medium in this pursuit and will explore every channel of creativity, even creating new ones.
    We see the world as a place where artists work together to create imaginative, inspiring and innovative new artwork.

    Allow your daydreams to become your plans!
