  • Director of Programming and Leadership

    The TRIAD Trust Inc.

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    In 2007, TRIAD was founded on the principle that local leaders are the agents to sustaining social change and fostering healthier communities.

    The initial vision was to create opportunities for local leaders, specifically linking the local leaders at the epicenter of the HIV/AIDS epidemic with high profile personalities around the world. TRIAD would create an opportunity for long term partnerships; utilizing the expertise of these high-profile personalities to identify and train local community leaders to create and run culturally relevant programs aimed at reducing the transmission of HIV/AIDS.

    Three years later, TRIAD experts partner with local leaders providing them with the necessary training, mentoring, and support to establish and implement sustainable not for profit and/or for profit organizations whose main objective is to reduce the transmission of HIV/AIDS and provide health and life skills education in areas seriously affected by the HIV/AIDS epidemic.

    As of December 2009, TRIAD offers its expert services at the epicenter of the HIV/AIDS epidemic, the Nkomazi region of South Africa.

    In January 2010, TRIAD Trust's first sustainable partner organization was registered in South Africa as a Section 21A not-for-profit organization, called TRIAD Nkomazi Rush. It offers health and life skills education, employment opportunities, organized youth sports leagues, professional development, and sport facility development.

    TRIAD believes that if the services and programs TRIAD offers to communities at the epicenter of the epidemic are able to assist these communities in conquering HIV/AIDS, then the epidemic can be conquered globally.
