Scott Martin’s Summary

Scott Martin has more than 15 years of experience in creating effective operational infrastructures delivering tangible results for clients. He has held a range of executive positions in senior management, operations, sales, marketing and customer management roles in addition to operating a successful consulting practice. He has worked with diverse clients including: AT&T, AT&T Wireless, GEICO, Aloha Airlines, America West Vacations, Verizon, Verizon Wireless, United Online, Earthlink/ PeoplePC, National Geographic, Time Warner Cable, Toyota, Ford Motor Corporation, USAA, U.S. Army, U.S. Air Force, U.S. Navy, U.S. Marines, Department of Defense, Bank of America, Microsoft, NBC, and CitiGroup. Scott has also worked with due diligence teams on projects relating to mergers and acquisitions.
Utilizing his experience, Scott is focused on day to day operations with significant involvement in customer and vendor enterprise solutions that benefit clients. He is a member of Rotary International and the President-Elect of Solvang Rotary in California. His philosophy is to uphold the ideals and ethics of the Rotary organization in all of his personal and professional actions. He has served on several boards and spoken on the topics of partner development, customer acquisition and operational planning.
