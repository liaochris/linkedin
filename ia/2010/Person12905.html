Angee Linsey’s Summary

Angee Linsey is a rock solid recruiter and recruiting process consultant with a diverse background and a knack for understanding the needs and goals of both clients and candidates.

From Fortune 500s like the caffeinated Starbucks to the privately-held Kendall-Jackson wines to fast-growth entrepreneurial companies in technology, Angee has worked intimately with company leaders to implement her recruitment strategies to secure talented team members for her clients.

Her clients keep coming back and they tell us she is a trusted advisor who makes sure the only candidates she presents demonstrate A-level competency and compatibility. They emphasize the thing they value most is that she understands that one without the other results in a miss-fit for the company and the candidate. The people recruited by Angee share with us that they trust and respect her as thorough, honest, open and exceptional on follow up, regardless of hire.

Prior to her career in search, Angee was a public relations and corporate communications professional in both corporate and agency environments. She also served as a Navy Reserve Public Affairs Officer. She earned her bachelor’s degree in journalism from the prestigious School of Journalism at the University of Missouri, and a Master of Arts degree in Career Development from John F. Kennedy University in Northern California.

Angee Linsey’s Specialties:

For more than 13 years, she has worked with clients in industries including wine, food, hospitality, public relations/corporate communications, technology and more.
