Steven A. Rodriguez’s Summary

As a student leader at CUNY Kingsborough, my role is to bring together students, faculty and staff to collaborate in various capacities to enhance students' experience at the college. I focus on three core areas: listening, community management and outreach programs. As a College Assistant I am also exposed to the administrative culture and act as a liaison to strengthen Student2Faculty relations.

I also lead the Responsibility To Care (R2C) Stewardship project, a program dedicated to educating the student community on the potential of sustainable practices/communications and helping direct the green initiative at CUNY Kingsborough. We aim to implement a sustainable practice every year and are featured at the Annual Kingsborough Eco-Festival. (Contact me to discuss joining the student program).

Outside of work and school, I blog about community and leadership at http://www.StevenARodriguez.com .
