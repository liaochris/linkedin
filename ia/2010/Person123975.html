Leonard J. McGill’s Summary

Strategically focused corporate business lawyer and executive officer with transformational leadership qualifications. Has more than 20 years' experience as general counsel to large public companies and in private practice with international law firms as a securities, M&A and finance lawyer, with clients ranging from large multinational corporations to startups and growth companies in such diverse industries as global manufacturing, tech and biotech, and defense and aerospace.

Leonard J. McGill’s Specialties:

Corporate and finance transactions, SEC filings, mergers and acquisitions, litigation management (including class actions and multi-district litigation), internal audit, ethics and compliance (including regulatory compliance), legislative affairs, corporate governance, and all other aspects of law department management.
