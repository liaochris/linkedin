Chris Bunney’s Summary

Creative entrepreneur with over seventeen years of production experience working in film, video, interactive and event media. Marketing, communication, entertainment, advertising and design experience on projects for Apple, BNP Paribas, Dreamworks, Ducati Motor, General Motors, HP, Intel, NBC/Universal, SanDisk, Sprint, Walt Disney, Wildlife Conservation Network and Yahoo!. Productions for these brand names include Websites, product launch videos, Flash demos, live events and broadcast commercial spots.
