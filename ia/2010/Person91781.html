Jazmin Alvarez’s Summary

I began my career by working in Advertising as a Marketing executive at an Advertising/Marketing Firm in San francisco, Ca. I then started working as a model scout for a local modeling agency and scouted freelance for a local fashion magazine as well. I then went on at Michele Pommier in Miami as the Assistant booker while completing my undergraduate degree in Fashion Marketing. I worked directly with all the bookers as well as the agency director. Immediately after graduating college, I relocated to New York City and worked as an assistant booker at Ford on the men's board for several months until I landed at Fusion model management also located in New York City as a mens agent for 2 years. I left the agency and for the past 2 years I have been working freelance in Casting and Photo Production. I'm also a Senior editor for an online magazine.
