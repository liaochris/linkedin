Karen DeMatteo’s Summary

Karen DeMatteo is the CEO and Team Leader of Strategic Kingdom Investments, LLC & KDL Solutions, LLC. Karen is a highly experienced real estate investor and industry leader specializing in the Virginia, Maryland and Washington DC market. She works with a team of top agents in the Washington Metropolitan area to serve the needs of distressed property owners. Karen focuses on Pre-Foreclosures, and Short Sales, for both residential and commercial properties.

Over the last several years Karen has positioned herself as an industry leader, helping distressed property owners make tough decisions while preserving their financial futures Karen has helped hundreds of troubled homeowners, as well as those who just need to sell their home fast. She works directly with the banks and uses that knowledge to help homeowners who are upside down and need relief. She consults on loan modifications and works with a team of Realtor partners to successfully close their short sale listings, helping many clients walk away owing nothing to their lenders. Karen DeMatteo with her negotiation company, KDL Solutions, LLC has a 96% success rate closing her agent’s short sale listings. She has full time professional negotiators to manage fast disposition and provide ultimate care and assistance to the homeowner. Karen is committed to help stabilize families, communities and cities around the Washington Metropolitan area.

Recently she has teamed with some investors who’s mission is to help families retain their homes. This is a very unique strategy where by the family’s mortgage payment is reduced by 40-60% immediately and then after a 2-3 year lease period; the family can purchase their home back at 90% of what the current market value us. Her mission is to help as many families qualify for this program so that they retain their homes. This program available for investment and commercial properties on a case by case basis.
