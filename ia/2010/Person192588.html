Sreejith Kumar Gopalakrishna Panickar’s Summary

Someone who aspired to become a space physicist in his childhood. Someone who got appointment as bank officer in his final year in college. Someone who started blogging to kill time till employment. Someone who got mails and calls from the CEO of an American e-learning company, after the latter went through his blog posts. Someone who attended tests and interviews regarding an unknown working sphere. Finally, someone who preferred software to banking after a deep thought.

That marked the beginning of my career, in 2005. It was a great experience learning new things everyday, at Vitalect, that e-learning company. After a year of working with Vitalect, I moved to one of the best transaction billing companies in the world, SunTec Business Solutions at its development centre in Technopark, Trivandrum. I started writing different chunks of various product manuals, then moved to writing modules, then to individual products, and then to application manuals.

After a stint of about 3.5 years with SunTec, I moved to UST Global in Technopark, Trivandrum, where I work for one of our most prestigious global accounts. UST has given me a great opportunity to migrate from documentation engineering to program management.

Writing is my passion, no matter whether it is technical or creative. Ah, yes! I have also published some of my articles in print and online media.

Apart from management activities, I devote time for reading Indian history, especially the life and times of revolutionary Indian freedom fighters. The one I admire the most is Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose, the most organised of all leaders. I am also the founder member of Mission Netaji (www.MissionNetaji.org), a Delhi based national non-profit trust conducting research on Bose. In fact, I am so happy to tell you that I coined the name 'Mission Netaji' for the organisation. :)
