Tim Bursch’s Summary

Tim Bursch is a guy that cares about making connections and helping people. He likes relationships vs. transactions. Tim values community and giving back. He spent 12 years trying to fit into a normal job. When the opportunity came, he decided to take take action on an idea using both his experience and his passion for making connections. He started a business that invested over 10% of it’s revenues to impact an overlooked community. Recently, Tim has jumped into helping businesses with social media marketing and online community development. He is really good at drinking coffee and reading lots of books.
