Ash Jain’s Summary

I have been providing Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) consulting and Hyperion software implementation services for almost 10 years. I am a certified Hyperion Planning and Essbase consultant. In addition to Hyperion System 9 Planning and Essbase, I have extensive experience with HAL, DIM (Data Integration Management),eports/Financial Reporting, MaxL/Esscmd, Business Rules and Analyzer/WebAnalysis. I've played the role of “hands-on” technical/functional architect during the implementation of EPM Solutions at numerous global Fortune 500 companies. My passion is to bake world class best practices both from the functional and technical standpoint in the design of EPM solutions. My consulting experience covers the complete project cycle from project management, requirements gathering, design, installation, implementation, deployment and training. Prior to becoming independent consultant, I worked as a consultant for The Hackett Group (Formerly known as Answerthink) and Big 4 Systems Integrators such as BearingPoint and Andersen Business Consulting.
