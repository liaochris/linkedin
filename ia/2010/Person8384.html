  • Food Specialist

    Culinary Compulsion

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    January 2007 — October 2007 (10 months)

    A South Florida-based freelance food writer and food blogger and a self-taught cook, I have been writing about and photographing food and its intrinsic connection with life for over two years on my food blog, “Culinary Compulsion."

    This blog is written in both English and Spanish offering a new culinary adventure every Thursday as well as recipes and photographs.

    Born in Venezuela to an American mother and an Israeli father, my multi-cultural background offers a plethora of culinary tastes, customs and images seen weekly on my blog, http://www.culinarycompulsion where each Thursday I add a new story, recipe and photographs.

    In addition, I serve as a food consultant, recipe developer and chef for high-end boutique events.

    I recently was awarded Honorary Mention for the prestigious James Peterson Food Writing Award offered by the Greenbrier Symposium for Professional Food Writers. I have published in Every Day with Rachael Ray magazine, The Sun Sentinel, The Miami Herald, The Dallas Morning News and The Oregonian My images have appeared on some of the above as well as on James Beard Award-Winning websites such as Nat Decants and Leite’s Culinaria.

    I am available for hire as an independent and/or consultant in any of the above capacities and am fluent in English and Spanish.
