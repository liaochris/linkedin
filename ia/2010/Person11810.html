Andrew Hepp’s Summary

Hello, my name is Andrew Hepp and I am recent graduate of Purdue University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Graphics Technology and a minor in Film. Over the past few years I have developed a strong affinity for modeling, lighting, rendering, and the motion capture production pipeline. I can honestly say that I love learning about CG and I look forward to developing my skills in the years to come.

After my sophomore year at Purdue, I was lucky enough to have an internship at Academy Award-winning Giant Studios in Los Angeles, California. This fantastic experience lead to a total of 11 months working as an intern and employee. During this time I worked on a variety of projects such as Avatar, I Am Legend, and the EA/Gillette holiday commercials, to name a few. During the fall semester of 2008, I was fortunate enough to manage the second unit motion capture stage of James Cameron’s Avatar.

While interning at Giant this past summer, I directed my own motion capture fight sequence on the stage of Steven Spielberg’s TinTin. I worked closely with the staff at Giant to take my motion capture sequence through the production pipeline. Over the spring semester, I incorporated this motion capture data into my Senior Project at Purdue University. Within the next few months, I intend on continuing my career in the Visual FX Industry.

Andrew Hepp’s Specialties:

Motion Capture Operating, Motion Capture Tracking, Lighting, Rendering, Environment Modeling, and Compositing.
