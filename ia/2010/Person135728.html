Marti Schiff’s Summary

Groundbreaker in Partnership Marketing. Conceive and develop innovative significant marketing partnerships. Early programs included Amazon Magazine Store, Ticketmaster Upsell Program, Best Buy, use of frequent flyer miles to purchase magazine subscriptions, and others. Extensive work in developing innovative programs online and in print. Prior experience as EVP Time Warner (Synapse, Entertainment Weekly), as COO for venture-backed site (Earthnoise - a too early YouTube) as well as with Bain & Co. (leading strategy consulting firm) and Bank of America.

Marti Schiff’s Specialties:

Partnership Marketing: Work with content purveyors, eCommerce companies and Catalogers to find alternate sources of customer acquisition via targeted partnerships.

Content monetization programs for publishers - both digital and print.

Upsell programs: We have implemented many of these programs.

New Business Development: Developed website for new subscription-driven diet program for Reader's Digest.

Sponsorships.
