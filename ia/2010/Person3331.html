Adrian Fung’s Summary

Senior product marketing leader and entrepreneur with a proven track record of successfully growing new businesses both at startups and within larger organizations. Innovative approaches to web and social marketing have garnered both repeated market successes and industry awards. Experienced team leader that has built and led high performing organizations of over 50 people.
