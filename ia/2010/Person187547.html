Shannon Seery Gude’s Summary

I specialize in helping companies leverage web technologies and social media to communicate and monitor brands, to engage and interact with consumers, candidates and employees. I am a frequent speaker, writer, and producer of social media and digital engagement strategies. I am a self professed "geek" and have spent 13 years in Interactive marketing and web development.

Along with a team of digital solutions consultants, web designers, programmers, and project managers, I implement award-winning career websites, web applications and strategies, including online reputation management strategies, social recruiting, SEO, Onboarding, and next generation Intranets and enterprise communities. I have developed recruitment web strategies and talent management initiatives for Fortune 500 clients such as Bank of America, Citi, FedEx, MetLife, and WellPoint.

An Internet original - my education and career developed in parallel with this industry. The combination of technology and how it affects people is the root of my fascination. Talk to me about the web and social media - and my hope for this business quickly rises to the surface with the passion of a true industry evangelist.

To connect directly with me, find me on Twitter at http://twitter.com/seerysm; on facebook http://www.facebook.com/shannonseery or on my blog at EXCELER8ion.com.
