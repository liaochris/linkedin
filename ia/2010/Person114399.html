Ken Gibson’s Summary

A 30 year business veteran and entrepreneur, Ken has founded 5 businesses, in management consulting, market research, tourism/hospitality, computer rehab and sales, e-commerce and auction resales.

He applies decades of varied experience to organizations seeking business improvement. Currently, he has developed a consultancy that helps auto dealerships grow their business through Internet Marketing.

He has developed education and training programs for the auto industry, and a proprietary Internet Ranking Report for various market areas.

Ken uses proven tools and techniques to help his clients succeed in this tough environment while others fail trying to do business as they have in the past.

One key area currently is helping businesses understand Social Media Marketing,and apply it for business growth. Ken has found it is about applying the appropriate strategy to each business model in conjunction with the selected social media application(s).
