Andy Middleton’s Summary

I am a social entrepreneur, facilitator, speaker and consultant to business and government for a wide range of sustainability and climate related challenges and solutions.

My work ranges from the TYF-sponsored Hay on Earth seminars, run at the internationally acclaimed Hay Literary Festival through to bespoke culture change programmes for private and public sector clients.

I've worked internationally, across Europe, India, Asia and Africa and own a fantastic Eco Lodge in St Davids, that is a great venue for big conversations and wide thinking
