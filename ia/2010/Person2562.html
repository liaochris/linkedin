Adam Nadrowski’s Summary

I started my hobby fixing my neighbors' household electronics and personal computers 12 years ago, but I didn't make the move into full-time troubleshooting until I enlisted in the Marine Corps. I learned that the same problem solving I’d done in the past let me fix complicated avionics equipment for the military. I became an avionics technician until I got promoted to an advanced technical position just three years later, where my ESD program received a flawless score during an important biennial inspection. Now, I am looking for a technology-based vendor who needs a qualitative/quantitative mix to offer support in the electronics or information technology industry. There's not much I can't quickly get up and working, and my passion (in life) is the feeling of accomplishment after solving a difficult problem.
