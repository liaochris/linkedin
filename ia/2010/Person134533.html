Mark Salyzyn’s Summary

Architect, OS Generalist, Platform, Security, Server, OpenSource & Tools Engineer with more than 2 score of experience. No job too small, no light at the end of the tunnel too far.

Advanced RAID, Storage, Network and Operating system solutions. On-time delivery of a wide variety of new product offerings resulted in one of the strongest engineering teams within ADEC, DPT, Adaptec & now Xyratex.

Champion and Tiger Team member. Support of Linux, Solaris, FreeBSD, MAC OS/X & many others, BIOS & new Hardware, Firmware & Software architectures. Active in Linux scsi list & attended/presented at OLS. Patent holder. Mentor new & old engineers into architectural & software techniques. Involved in Standards development (SATA, SAS, I2O, ADI, CHIM, Clustering, Libraries, Internal Proprietary Protocols & other Interface frameworks), SCSI command set. Architected multiplexed protocol (HTTPS, HTTP, SSL & Proprietary) daemons in support of distributed RAID management.

Languages

Fluent in C, C++ and Objective C (+FORTRAN, Basic, Pascal, APL ...). Object oriented design methodologies. Assembler level programming spanning the millennia: 4004, 8008, 8080, SCMP, 8085, 8088, 80x86, i960, ARM, MIPS, AVR, uP1802, 6800, 6805, 6809, 6811, 68000, TM320C25, Z80, SBC11-21, PDP11/73 as well as several macro language dialects.Well versed in UNIX shell scripting. PCC and GCC internals, (providing Firmware toolset company wide).

Systems

Intensive kernel development and operating system management on the following derivatives of UNIX, V7, including AT&T UNIX System 3, SysVR2. Driver fundamentals for Berkeley 4.3/4.4 BSD, BSDI 1.0-4.0, NeXTSTEP/Openstep 3.2-4.2 (Mach for Intel), Linux. MAC OS/X, OS/2, NetWare, AIX, FreeBSD, UnixWare, SCO, Windows NT/95 & Int13 BIOS.

7 children, 3 grandchildren. Avid mechanic, Motorcyclist, Extra-class Amateur Radio operator.

Mark Salyzyn’s Specialties:

Driver, Operating System, Storage, Network and Application architecture. Hardware Design, Firmware & Software in a variety of programming languages & Compiler tools.
