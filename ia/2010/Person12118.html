Andrew Phelps’s Summary

Andrew Phelps is a Boston-based journalist with experience as a reporter, host, editor, and Web developer in print and public broadcasting.

Andrew Phelps’s Specialties:

Reporter. Editor. Host. Photographer. Video cutter. Versatile, dependable, adventurous. Expert proficiency in the following: Multitrack audio editing (Audition, ProTools); Associated Press style (broadcast and print); NewsBoss, ENPS; content management systems (WordPress, Movable Type); Web design (XHTML, CSS, PHP); SLR photography, Photoshop; slideshow production (SlideShowPro, Soundslides Plus); video production (Final Cut, Premiere). Intermediate Spanish speaker.
