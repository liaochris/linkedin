Raymond Joyce’s Summary

Regional Coordinator for New York's Eastern Division for National Grid, the second largest electric and gas utility in the US. Responsible for the management of all company, interconnecting utility, and customer station activities on National Grid’s electric transmission and distribution system for Eastern New York. Responsible for the scheduling and managing of all prearranged work to ensure the reliability of electric service for all transmission and distribution facilities (2,400 to 345,000 volts) to over 200 National Grid electric substations, 171 customer-owned substations and nearly 600,000 of National Grid’s 1.5 million customers in New York State. Additionally assists with emergency management operations and storm restoration activities during major events.

Raymond Joyce’s Specialties:

Electric Reliability, Asset Management, Project Mangement, Capital Planning, GIS, OMS, EMS, Regulatory, energy management technologies, innovation in power system analysis tools.
