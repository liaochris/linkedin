Mike Everleth’s Summary

For over 10 years, I have created and managed online content for entertainment companies such as ABC, MTV Networks, Viacom and Walt Disney. My work has also appeared on the sites IFILM.com and Movies.com, both of which received several million unique visitors per month during my tenure with them. I also founded and continue to run the #1 online journal dedicated to the world of underground film, which is well-respected and praised by filmmakers and film festival programmers from around the world.
