Esther Slater McDonald’s Summary

I am a senior associate in the Commercial Litigation Practice Group of Seyfarth Shaw LLP. My general practice includes litigation on business torts, contractual disputes, and insurance matters. My insurance practice includes advising clients on coverage matters and extra-contractual liability. Insurance issues include, among others, bad faith, mass tort, products liability, and general liability. In addition, I have an active pro bono practice.

I have extensive experience in litigating dispositive motions and appeals, with special emphasis on complex procedural matters. This experience enables me to provide strategic advice on achieving a favorable dispositive judgment in advance of trial, thereby limiting or negating a client's litigation expenses for trial and discovery.

My appellate and procedural experience complements my trial practice by providing a comprehensive perspective others may lack. Often, cases are won or lost on procedure. Failure to preserve an issue for appeal or to recognize the future adverse impact of a procedural move can be a costly mistake. I advise clients on how to avoid these mistakes. I also advise clients on how to rectify or mitigate the adverse impact of such mistakes when former counsel failed to anticipate the consequences of procedural decisions.

I have represented clients before the United States Supreme Court, the federal appeals and district courts, and various state courts.
