Jerrilynn B. Thomas’s Summary

Jerrilynn B. Thomas has spent the past 15 years helping business and professional women tap into the online women in business market by assisting them with identifying and forging win-win business relationships to build their client base.

Jerrilynn started with Mothers INC to help working mothers launch their own businesses using their job skills then morphed into the Women's News Bureau to assist women with publicizing their companies followed by metamorphoses into The Business Diva Network, and the International Virtual Women's Chamber of Commerce which both help women tap into the Internet to grow their companies.

To address the changing economy she closed the virtual doors on IVWCC and launched WomenPartner International now known as Marketing 2 Women International to reinforce her commitment to helping women think collaboratively instead of individually when it comes to growing a multimillion dollar business.

Marketing 2 Women International is a cross marketing consortium for seasoned business and professional women who want to fast track their first million in sales by servicing each others’ existing client base while sharing marketing costs to stretch their precious marketing dollars. It is not a networking organization. It’s the organization women and men join to enhance their membership in other business organizations.

Companies pool their resources to purchase advertising (ads, tables, sponsorships, booths, etc.) from women's business organizations so they can strategically expand their client base throughout every US and global metropolitan area.

Marketing 2 Women International’s focus is educating businesswomen and men about the metro women in business markets in the US and other countries from the point of view of a businessperson putting together a marketing plan and assisting them with developing cross marketing partnerships with the business, executive, and professional women who reside in the metro areas they want to tap into.
