Nomi Farooq’s Summary

Currently running operations at Rapid Learning Life (RLL) creating rapid e-learning courses (from scratch) with media rich features such as video and interactivity. Also taking content directly from Subject Matter Experts (including academics) and presenting it in a useful e-learning environment encompassing features such as high quality video and interactions. Distributing these e-learning courses and learning "bites" online professionally for end users.

Over 5 years experience in online learning development. Passionate about creating, developing exciting and engaging e-learning.

Have worked with a range of people from subject matter experts, small to large companies. E-Learning projects to deliver high quality courses to professionals and non professionals with accreditation.

We are living in a time where software learning technology is moving at fast pace and is becoming easier to use and develop. In parrallel to this people are using the internet and social media to learn in vast numbers. The challenge is to connect these two in a simple, authentic, cost effective and engaging way.
