Carla Stanmyre McDonald’s Summary

Carla Stanmyre McDonald is one of the leading consumer PR strategists in the country. Once named one of the country's "40 Under 40 Stars in PR" by the industry's leading trade publication, Carla is currently the founder and CEO of Dynabrand, a national public relations firm that specializes in building awareness and sales for consumer lifestyle brands.

Dynabrand's clients include Aquasana, a leading maker of water filters for the home; Deep Eddy Sweet Tea Vodka, the only sweet tea vodka made with real, brewed tea; The Worth Collection, a luxury fashion brand; Zeno, a beauty device manufacturer; PureSport, a patent-pending new class of sports performance drinks; Sweet Leaf Tea, a leading ready-to-drink beverage company; and Whirlpool Corporation. Dynabrand has senior executives in Austin, New York, San Francisco, Los Angeles and Atlanta.

Previously, Carla held executive leadership positions at two of the world's largest public relations agencies: she was the Executive Vice President and Managing Director of the North American Marketing Practice at GCI Group and, before that, served as Senior Managing Director and head of the consumer marketing practice for Hill and Knowlton in New York.

Carla has also worked on the client side, serving as chief marketing officer for a venture-backed Internet company that she helped to rename andrebrand and for which she helped to raise fventure funds. The company was sold to Omnnicom.

The architect of Dynabranding, a brand positioning methodology, Carla is a go-to expert on issues relating to public relations, brand strategy, reputation management and consumer marketing.

Carla also serves as the host of News 8 Austin's "Arts Minute," and writes a monthly column for Tribeza, a monthly lifestyle magazine serving Austin.
