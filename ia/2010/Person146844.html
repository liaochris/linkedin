Mirna Bard’s Summary

Mirna Bard is more than an Internet marketing expert. She is a consultant, a coach, an educator, an entrepreneur, a public speaker, and a tenacious learner.

In all Internet marketing/social media work she takes on, Mirna works to incorporate social media and online marketing into the entire marketing mix. It is her philosophy that all marketing efforts should complement one another and without a fine-tuned strategy and clearly set goal, she believes that no Internet marketing tactic will be truly successful.

Throughout Mirna’s career, her clients have consistently praised her genuine nature, energy, reliability, resourcefulness, thoroughness, and attention to detail. She prides herself on these traits too, saying: “I may be a bit passionate, but if it means I get to live the life and enjoy the career I’ve always wanted, then I’m proud to be so passionate.”

With her bachelor’s degree in advertising, a master’s in business administration, and 12 years of experience in advertising, she draws on a combination of scholastic knowledge and real-world Internet experience in every client project she engages in. Many companies now look to Mirna to educate them on the ever-changing world of social media and search engine optimization (SEO).

Today, most of Mirna’s consulting and coaching efforts go toward aiding small- to medium-sized businesses reach their business goals. In addition to her consultant work, she is also an instructor at the University of California, Irvine where she lectures on social media and SEO.

Mirna currently sits as Social Media Chair on the Public Relations Committee for NAWBO-OC (National Association of Women Business Owners, Orange County).

To inquire about Mirna’s consulting business, lecture services, or to interview her for an article or news story, please contact her directly at mirnabard@gmail.com.
