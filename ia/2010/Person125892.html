Lisa Chaffey’s Summary

I started 'In a Pickle Virtual PA Ltd' in January 2009 after quickly realising the need for an efficient, reliable and pro-active virtual assistant service in today’s more flexible working environment.

My proactive approach to the Virtual Assistant concept has given many small enterprises the step-up they so desperately needed. Now, after celebrating our first year of trading, In a Pickle has over 40 clients from all over the Reading, Newbury and Thames Valley area.

We have recently been voted the Most Loved Virtual PA in the UK and also voted the 5th best loved business in Henley-on-Thames - this is a great honour and our thanks go to our clients for providing us with such great testimonials.

In a Pickle continues to go from strength to strength and we are now working with Associates to offer the full 'one stop' package to our clients to extend our products and services.

In a Pickle has helped put our clients back in the driving seat of their business and helps them find the best route to success too. Instead of getting snowed under with paperwork and other people’s demands, they can finally concentrate on making their business work for them.

Working with In a Pickle has so many benefits:

• No employment costs
• No office overheads
• You only pay for the work undertaken
• Seamless support
• Your own team behind you – without the expense
