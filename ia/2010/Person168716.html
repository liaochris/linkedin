David Randall’s Summary

Creative, Production and Video Marketing Services for Entertainment, Corporate, Higher Education, Renewable Energy, Sports Events, Non-Profit and Fundraising Clients.

Our Mission: To take a company's content strategy, develop it, deploy it, and deliver unprecedented results at twice the speed and half the cost of a traditional advertising firm.

We bring emerging web technologies and trends to clients and create the exclusive vide content to give them a competitive edge with their online marketing programs.

Our Client list includes:
Full Speed Promotions, BP Solar, The Cydcor Corporation, Earthworks Films, Lakeshore Entertainment, The City of Beverly Hills, Warner Bros. Advertising and Marketing Services,The Walt Disney Company, NBC, ABC, The Art Directors Guild and the Emmy Awards.

The first year sales of over $20 million exceeded the $2 million target and continues to outperform in '09.
