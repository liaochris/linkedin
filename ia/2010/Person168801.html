Randi Bussin, CCMC, CPBS, MBA, MA’s Summary

I am a career reinvention and Personal Branding strategist. I thrive on partnering with bright and successful individuals seeking more meaningful work aligned with their values, and reignite the internal spark and passion that has been dimmed by their current professional role. I guide them to gain clarity, a renewed sense of direction, and an actionable career reinvention and Personal Branding plan. A career reinvention could be an entirely new career, a new job more closely aligned with one’s values, an entrepreneurial pursuit based upon a passion, or a retirement game plan.

My personal story is what drives my passion for reinvention. After studying languages and obtaining an MBA, I had a long and successful career in high tech marketing in Europe and the U.S. I, then, launched a high tech consulting firm (my first career reinvention), and ran that firm for 10 years. After that experience, I became an MBA Career Counselor in numerous Boston-based business schools (second career reinvention), only to be followed once again by another reinvention to a private practice career coach. I have FINALLY found my ideal role.

For me, self-reflection and introspective analysis are the foundation of a successful reinvention, without which a reinvention strategy cannot be mapped out and enthusiastically pursued. I leverage cutting-edge career and personal brand assessments, collaborating with clients to help them step back from their busy lives and analyze their goals, hopes, dreams, and passions. These assessments, coupled with almost magical visioning, move clients to envision possibilities, test out hypotheses, and begin to reshape their lives.

I am a highly trained career coach, Personal Branding and online ID strategist, and résumé writer with numerous credentials, and a particular expertise in career assessments.

I speak and write nationally on career reinvention and personal branding, and also have appeared on national television and radio programs.
