Diane Hibbs’s Summary

Small businesses in the U.S. account for half our economy and employ 80% of U.S. workers; they are the backbone of our economy. I have a passion for small businesses and have set myself on a mission to help them succeed. I have positioned myself with several referral partners who, along with me and my services, can provide all aspects of marketing to attract, convert, and retain new customers.

The best clients for my services are businesses who want to get the most bang for their buck. They want to direct every marketing dollar they spend to their exact target market.

The best-performing industries for us are restaurants, service businesses (plumbers, electricians, construction, remodeling, carpet cleaning, pest control, etc.), classes (gymnastics, dance, art, martial arts, etc.), kids’ camps (afterschool, summer, holiday, etc.), schools (private, preschool, daycare, etc.), limousine services, spa and beauty services (hair stylists, massage, nail salons, etc.), photographers, event planners, realtors, resorts, retail shops, automobile services (repair, sales, auto glass, etc.)
