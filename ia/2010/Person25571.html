Bob McIntosh, CPRW, MBTI’s Summary

My success as a Career Trainer is a result of a tenacious appetite for learning and staying abreast of the latest job search trends, and disseminating that knowledge to jobseekers of all levels. Knowledge is the key to a successful job search.

I demonstrate a creative approach to leading nearly 20 interactive workshops (see Work Experience). Customers consistently comment on my extensive knowledge of job search topics; be it Résumé Writing, Behavioral Interviewing, Networking, or LinkedIn—as well as keeping them engaged.

I’m never satisfied with yesterday’s information; I research extensively to develop new workshops and enhance existing ones. I'm constantly reading the most current books and Internet articles and asking questions of professionals from various industries, including the LinkedIn community. My innovativeness has led to the first LinkedIn group at the career center. This group is one of the the largest, if not the largest, among One-Stop career centers in the state of Massachusetts.

As a Certified Professional Résumé Writer (CPRW), I address the jobseeker's needs on an individual level when I critique his/her résumé. Honesty is my policy, and I demand résumés that will land in the employer’s "must-read" pile.

My ultimate goal is to help jobseekers secure rewarding employment. To this end, I have been extremely successful. To me, there is nothing more rewarding than helping jobseekers realize their ultimate goal, finding employment.
