Sonia Gallardo’s Summary

Knitting Peace is a for profit organization that employs women incarcerated in Bolivia to co-create luxurious high quality hand knit accessories, with the intention of providing the women opportunities to sustain themselves and there family, as well as heal through the art of knitting.
