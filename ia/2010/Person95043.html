Jennifer Kendall’s Summary

I am currently the new CEO and President for Mindstorm Creative, Inc.

Mindstorm, a creative multimedia and film production company, has been producing multimedia campaigns for over five years while offering marketing solutions for over 20 years. During that time, we have evolved from an in-house creative team for northern California's largest production facility to an independent company located in midtown Sacramento. Today, we service an expanded client base.

Mindstorm occupies a unique niche in the marketplace. We believe that the marriage of traditional and new media formats provides clients with the greatest reach and the richest informational experience. We use traditional methods of communication such as print, radio, outdoor, television and direct mail. However, we maximize their impact by using CDs, DVDs, or websites to position your social marketing message. And we do it all from under one roof.

Mindstorm offers full-service consulting to companies in the areas of: project development, public relations, as well as multimedia communications. We are the creative experts, each member of our award winning team has valuable experience working effectively in both the private and public sector.

Whether you are launching a multimedia marketing campaign, looking to increase your client base, hoping to carry important messages to a wider audience, or simply looking to expand your online presence, Mindstorm can help you.

For more information about us, our work portfolio and processes, please visit our websites at:

www.mindstormcreative.com
