Ruth Holland’s Summary

As a certified health coach I teach people how to overcome the effects of obesity in their lives, for free. My clients lose weight, lower blood pressure and cholesterol and also to eliminate type II diabetes. The average weight loss is 2-5 pounds per week. Health coaching is a fun way to keep edcuating your self while helping others! If you would like to join a team of health focused people, have fun and make a bit of extra money, please contact me about joining the Learn to Stay Fit team of Take Shape for Life Coaches.
