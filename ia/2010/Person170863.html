Steven Reinemund’s Summary

I am a bilingual general manager with a proven track record of strong execution & results. I know how to drive lasting change by innovating within the standard operating procedures of large Fortune 1 & Fortune 1000 companies alike. I have been able to integrate my passion & entrepreneurship into the corporate environment by quickly defining the issues and path to success while leveraging the power of great cross-functional teams. Whether a struggling or mature business, I have demonstrated the ability to revive a business in the short term while establishing a plan for sustainable long term growth.

I love retail & consumer packaged goods (CPG). I thrive on the opportunity to take a Steve Jobs/Apple approach to ESTABLISHING what consumers want & need by reading trends & anticipating needs, not just following & optimizing the competition's products.
