Chad Elliott’s Summary

Graduated from Kent State University with a Bachelors Degree in Finance and Business Management. SGT/ E-5/ Team Leader in the US Army from 2000-2006 and an Iraqi War Veteran from February 2003- May 2004. Started career at FirstMerit Bank in the Bankcard Department with the Merchant Sales and Service team. Went on to be a Personal Banker at FirstMerit Bank and moved up to an Assistant Manager and then Licensed Branch Manager. Now with a defense contracting company, Mission Essential Personnel, starting as a Finance Analyst and moving up to a Financial Systems Analyst, Lead. Currently working towards an MBA from Franklin University.
