Jessica Griffin, MA’s Summary

I am an eternal optimist with the passion to learn and expand my depth of knowledge in interactive marketing communications. Such as, social media marketing, online advertising, search engine marketing and various avenues of interactive communications.

My best work has been integrating online and traditional media for a broader and more successful integrated marketing communications campaign.

I've garnered five solid years of experience in the field of communications and marketing in industries such as higher education, healthcare, non-profit and small business ownership. That experience coincides with a master's degree in Integrated Marketing Communications.

I'm a natural organizer and planner. I pride myself on keeping my work organized and meeting deadlines. If a situation occurs outside of that plan I thrive on making it work.

I am constantly finding new ways that blogging, social networking, web technologies., podcasting, twittering, emailing, texting and other forms of communication that was unheard of 5 minutes ago, will change the face of marketing communications.
