Richard Paul’s Summary

For over twenty years Richard Paul has presented his hilarious educational/motivational programs to conferences and educational institutions throughout the world.
He is an award-winning speaker, ventriloquist and author. His topics include stress management, self-esteem, anti-bullying, eliminating cyber bullying, diversity and character building.

He is a keynote speaker, school speaker and certified trainer offering programs and training materials that inspire attendees to want be proactive when it comes to respect, understanding and having a positive attitude.

His goal is to motivate his audiences to feel and understand that every thought and word spoken will create a positive or negative experience depending on which course of action they choose. With a mix of humor, educational tools and audience interaction he gives his participants direction on how to laugh more, live better and embrace our world with love and understanding.
