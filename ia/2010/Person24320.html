Bob Ward’s Summary

Extensive experience in expanding the commercial footprint of biologic and small molecule therapeutics to increase sales and product lifecycle. Therapeutic Focus in Immunology, Oncology, Neuroscience and Infectious Disease. Specialized in Follow-on proteins.

Track record of success in leading teams in sales, marketing and R&D to achieve sales targets; launch new products; register new indications; develop new line extensions including drug delivery and optimizing intellectual property.

Alliance Management experience working with EU-based Biopharm launching Global (Ex-US) Product; Japan-based Biopharm launch Japanese Product; US Biotech commercializing US/EU Brand. Top 10 Global Pharma R&D Partnerships; Biotechnology discovery and development programs.

Business Development agreements concluded for product in-licensing; co-promotion; co-development; material transfer, drug distribution. Commercial analysis in support of Licensing/Mergers and Acquisitions.

Technologies for new product offerings have incorporated antibody phage display; pharmacogenomics; crystallization/polymorphs; enantiomers; recombinants (glycosylated, pegylated); nasal and subcutaneous drug delivery; mammalian cell culture.

Experienced in working with outside counsel on FDA matters including CRADA, sNDA, 505(b)(2), Pediatric Submissions, Orphan Indications, White Papers and Citizens Petitions.
