Kori Clemmer, SPHR, CCP’s Summary

My professional experience has covered 20 years in the HR area with particular emphasis in the HRIS and Compensation worlds. My SPHR and CCP knowledge and application has only enhanced my professional effectiveness. Current passion is to optimize HR processes and provide actionable HR information by the efficient use of technology. Or in other words to get HR people stop using paper and start using technology to make their lives easier and have greater influence within the organization.

Kori Clemmer, SPHR, CCP’s Specialties:

HRIS, Lawson HR, Benefits and time accrual, implementation and support, applicant tracking, project management, compensation administration, time and attendance implementation. Worked with Lawson, Taleo, Authoria, ICIMS, Kronos, ProSource, CHRIS
