Aaron Spence’s Summary

Aaron Spence currently serves as CEO and Sr. Consultant for Roberts & Spence, LLC located in Philadelphia, PA, where he also resides.

Aaron began his career in consulting while still an undergraduate at La Salle University. There, he served on various committees, organization boards, and outreach networks. Namely, he was involved with the Resident Student Association, TEAM Lasallians, College Democrats, and the Community Service Scholarship Program.

He graduated from La Salle University in 2007 with a dual degree in Philosophy and History.

Aaron’s professional areas of expertise include: program development and management, non-profit infrastructure and management, customer service administration, government interaction, and leadership development. He has consulted on a number of projects of varying types. He has assisted with the creation and development of several non-profit organizations, a dynamic social networking website, a sustainable (green) information technology group, and other projects.

In his spare time, Aaron dedicates himself to various professional and community organizations. Most notably, he works with the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and National Democratic Parties; Philadelphia Young Democrats; the La Salle University Alumni Association; Greater Philadelphia Cares; the Clinton Foundation Millennium Network; Young Involved Philadelphia, the Young Adult Network; YAN Mentoring; and The HER Project, where he serves as a board member.

Aaron also enjoys spending time with family, reading & collecting books, politics, philosophy, music, golf, and immersing himself in different cultures.

You can follow Aaron and his work via Facebook, Twitter, his personal and corporate websites, and his blog.
