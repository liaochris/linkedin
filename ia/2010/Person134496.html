Mark Rockefeller’s Summary

I founded Rockefeller Consulting to identify key opportunities and challenges to sustainable competitiveness facing businesses in today's rapidly evolving world. In turn, Rockefeller Consulting endeavors to build strategic partnerships to acquire the knowledge, skills, insights, and competencies to help identify and address those opportunities and challenges that correlate to optimum growth potential.

After starting and running several small businesses, serving on a variety of boards, and working closely with venture capital investors it is clear that companies are facing more sophisticated, competitive, rapidly changing, and complex environments and constituencies than ever before. These considerations directly affect internal and external communication strategies, the importance of constituency and enterprise alignment, and the need to understand and leverage social technology integration as a business tool to achieve core business goals. These and other competencies are currently the focus of Rockefeller Consulting through Rockefeller Consulting Insight Capitalists (RCIC) and Rockefeller Consulting Technology Integration (RockTech).

I am also passionate about the value and importance of protecting and preserving our natural world. My efforts with the National Fish and Wildlife Foundation (NFWF), Legacy Connect, South Fork Lodge and Outfitters, and Henry's Fork Anglers all contribute in different ways toward this goal.
