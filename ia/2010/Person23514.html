Started Big Noise Entertainment in 96 and dropped Back to Basics with B-Flatt and Tyranny. Dropped Keepin U Open in 98, a re-mix project featuring classic hiphop MCs (EPMD, Kurupt, Big Pun, Craig Mack and Mob Deep to name a few).
