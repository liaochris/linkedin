Craig DiLouie’s Summary

Highlights of Craig's contribution to the lighting field include:

* author of four books about lighting management, lighting controls and electrical systems design

* regular contributor to Electrical Contractor (NECA), The Electrical Distributor (TED) (NAED), LM&M (NALMCO), Illuminate, Architectural SSL, Lighting India, Enlighten (Wiedenbach-Brown), Power Outlet (Rexel USA) and other construction magazines

* producer of the LightNOW blog and newsletter

* has served prestigious education clients such as the U.S. Department of Energy, IALD, IES, Howard Brandston, NEMA, NALMCO, New Buildings Institute, Lighting Controls Association, Home Lighting Control Alliance, Lighting Design Lab

* notable current projects include education course on commissioning and generic commissioning specification for the Lighting Controls Association, Department of Energy's Commercial Lighting Solutions for Offices, revision of the IESNA's Recommended Practice for School Lighting, IES seminar on nonresidential lighting controls, 2010 What's New in Lamps and Ballasts seminar for Lightfair, update to NEMA's LightingTaxDeduction.org website, and contributions to the 2009 Advanced Lighting Guidelines

* has worked with leading manufacturers such as Acuity Brands Lighting, Acuity Brands Controls, LC&D, WattStopper, Peerless Lighting, Ledalite Architectural Products, Cooper Lighting, Honeywell
