David Bausola’s Summary

Specialist in designing collaborative media communications using disruptive technologies and free culture business models.

Deeply passionate service designer exploring Social Intelligence Systems for product development and entertainment products. http://bit.ly/dgrbot

Founded Ag8: "An independent studio developing currency for content makers, media platforms and brands". Sold my stake in Dec09.

Devised Purefold, an open media franchise designed for RSA Films, the commercial arm of Film Directors Ridley and Tony Scott.

Pitch strategist for 'Riot' (With PHD/EVB/Critical Mass/TBWA/180 Amsterdam). Won global digital account for Adidas from Avenue A|Razorfish, Droga 5 and Isobar.

Creator of "Where are the Joneses?" : 'social media' sitcom using Creative Commons based business practices. www.wherearethejoneses.com

Managed and delivered the multi-award winning, first online user-generated, video documentary site [www.channel4.com/fourdocs], delivering both its first major online video service and Creative Commons licensed UGC platform.

Re-engineered and delivered Channel 4’s education video distribution business [www.channel4.com/clipbank], as a peer to peer network and desktop client.

Devised and delivered the software for the largest public collaboration TV show [www.channel4.com/bigdig], bringing together over 10,000 participators who produced the UK’s largest online public archaeological database, which delivered a week of prime time live television to a daily audience of 3.5million.

Launched a non-commercial open-software collective to develop innovative music interfaces. ixi-software.net

Co-designed and delivered the UK’s first Broadband CPD system, which pioneered full screen interactive video-on-demand with integrated personal development records.

Planned, managed and delivered the first Playstation2 mobile to web convergence platform for SCEE in 2001, giving gamers the opportunity to build online leagues and communities.
