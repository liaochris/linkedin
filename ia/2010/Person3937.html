Agnieszka Gang’s Summary

Recruit 121 is an SAP staffing specialist, serving organisations
via its EMEA and US offices. Our sole focus is the identification
and delivery of pre-screened, high caliber SAP consultants for
contract and salaried positions. Quality of service, speed of
response and professional representation through the provision
of a compliant local solution are the main components of our
delivery model.

Recruit 121’s fundamentals:

Quality
Waste no time reviewing inappropriate CV’s. We know SAP and
evaluate/interview hundreds of SAP candidates to provide only
the two most appropriate.

Screening
Information on the CV’s you review will be accurate and
pre-verified including nationality / working status, qualifications
and previous work experience. We don’t just take their word for
it, we seek independent references.

Speed
Need to fill a resource gap quickly? We can present two
candidates within 24 hours for contract positions and within 72
hours for permanent positions.

Honesty
Avoiding exposure to employment and local tax related liabilities
is critical. We are candid and transparent in every interaction
and guarantee compliancy with all local and international
legislation.

Simplicity
We embrace time saving concepts such as electronic paperwork,
integrated billing, consultant care charter and a ‘minimal contact’
approach. Our job is to simplify yours.
