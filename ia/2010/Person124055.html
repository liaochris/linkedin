Leon Mills’s Summary

I have been involved in ALL phases of new building and subdivision development from land acquisition, planning, design, entitlements, and construction. My experience encompasses both the engineering side, and the private developer side giving me a strong understanding of the complete process for bringing new projects to completion.

I have recently passed all of the national test to become a RESNet/HERS Rater, a BPI Building Analyst, a NAHB Certifed Green Professional, and a NAHB Certified Green Building Verfier. I have set up my Corp. called "Energy Insight" to provide energy audits and green building verifications.
