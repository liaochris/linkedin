Alfredo Medina, Jr.’s Summary

Possess over 15 years of administrative experience in the grant development profession with ten of those years spent in higher education. Professional experience includes local, state and federal government relations with a demonstrated understanding of principal federal funding agencies (i.e., NSF, NIH, DoE) and the legislative process. Also serve as the College’s state lobbyist. Dual reporting to the VPAA on grant-related initiatives and to the President of Siena College in advancing the government agenda of the college.
