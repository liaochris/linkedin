James Famularo’s Summary

James Famularo is often described as “One of the top commercial brokers in the city". Large and small developers, restaurateurs, as well as news outlets including New York Magazine, The New York Observer, Daily News and The Real Deal have recognized Mr. Famularo's ability to make deals happen. James' strong work ethic and hands-on approach has established a record of Outstanding Achievement and fostered tremendous success, utilizing a combination of tenaciousness, approachability and prominent standing in the hyper-competitive ever-changing Manhattan real estate environment. With over fifteen years in the business, James is an expert at what most brokers would consider “impossible deals”; he specializes in mixed-use buildings, development sites, bars, nightclubs, lounges and restaurants.

James has developed a keen sense of the up and coming neighborhoods, which provide profitable opportunities for his clients. His numerous contacts and extensive experience in all aspects of the commercial real estate industry has allowed him repeated success. "He creates something out of nothing,” noted prominent real estate attorney Adam Leitman Bailey. Besides large development deals, Famularo has used his magic to assist many business entrepreneurs, celebrities and world class chefs.

With his in-depth knowledge of the latest market trends as related to the food and beverage industry, James is in an ideal position to provide local demographic profiles of specific neighborhoods based on accurate statistical data and reliable research. He follows through on all phases of a project to ensure client satisfaction. “Before I used James as my exclusive agent, I would reject properties because I did not think of such an optimal use. “ real estate developer Mr. Michael Russo who recently finalized a $80 million dollar real estate development acquisition.

James is is consistently a Top Producer in his firm and winner of the Highest Grossing Award (five years consecutively).
