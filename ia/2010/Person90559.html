Jason Jacobsohn’s Summary

Jason Jacobsohn is a master connector who is truly an advocate for the entrepreneur. Ultimately proactive and congenial, Jason possesses vast expertise in relationship building, both in face-to-face settings and through social media. He has provided trusted counsel, guidance and mentoring for many in the entrepreneurial and business communities.

Currently, Jason is Director of Venture Development for AEGIS Professional Services – Capital Sourcing Group, where he has been assigned the pivotal role of raising capital for its clients, lead generation, and relationship management.

Jason has spearheaded the development of several prominent networking events in the Chicagoland area. He is the Co-Producer of the Greater Chicago Networking Extravaganza, the premier annual business networking event in downtown Chicago. The 2010 event will be held on April 6.

Jason’s passion for business networking finds its outlet in his blog “Networking Insight,” and column in Magazine Soho in which he shares his experience and insights about successful relationship building.
