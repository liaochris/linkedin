Briac Guibert’s Summary

Briac Guibert is a passion-driven architect of interactive solutions. By combining several years of experience in digital marketing communications with his current business education, Briac is known for using creative and sound solutions to drive results in all manner of online operations.

While still in high school, Briac began his career in a French web agency as an Interactive Media Developer. Over the last 10 years his professional skills have been honed ad tried through a variety of freelance and permanent positions. Briac is fluent in English and French; having the opportunity to work and obtain degrees in both France and Canada.

Most recently, Briac served as a web analyst for Modasuite, a startup in the online fashion retail industry with a disruptive business model. Briac instigated a web analytics culture and helped drive online strategy.

Prior to multiple freelance experiences, Briac was appointed as an Interactive Designer & Developer for Bluesponge, one of Montreal’s leading interactive agencies. While in this position, he developed his skills as an Interactive Designer and Front-end Developer working collaboratively to deliver award-winning projects for major Canadian and international accounts such as the Montreal Canadiens hockey club, the Canadian Centre for Architecture (CCA), Ministère du Tourisme du Québec, and trueroots from Tata Communications.

Briac has an exceptional entrepreneurial spirit, a passion for both business and technology and a creative edge, all make him a great asset to both technical and creative teams. Briac holds an Associate Degree in Digital Communications & Technology from Université de Franche-Comté and is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce with a major in Marketing at Concordia’s John Molson School of Business in Montreal. He is a Certified Web Analyst as well as a Google Analytics and Google Adwords Qualified Individual.
