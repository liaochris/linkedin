Lasse Jensen’s Summary

Goal: World Domination

Lasse Jensen founded Denmarks first online DVD-rental company, selling it to LOVEFiLM in 2006 and was part of the founding team of LOVEFiLM Germany, as operations director. He then started what became the Denmark’s largest online incentive marketing company. Since 2008, Lasse has been building Interweb Europe, an online vertical ad network.
