Boyan Josic’s Summary

Boyan Josic is a career entrepreneur who invests and incubates internet and technology startups. Some of his current projects include being the Founder and CEO of ProfileSnaps.com. Boyan is also Founder and Managing Partner of Troy Ventures LLC, formally known as IncubationGroup which has incubated and grown the following companies: ProfileBuilder, ProfilePulse, and ModelDash, among others. Prior to that was the co-founder and President of BOSSdev, an IT consulting firm based in Troy, MI.

Mr. Josic enjoys discovering new business opportunities, incubating startups, traveling, snowboarding, and spending time on his mountain side estate outside of Denver, Colorado.
