Christine Ford’s Summary

I am a senior level product manager who knows how to actively engage users while maintaining a strong ROI. Experienced in full product lifecycle, user experience, metrics, consumer testing, SEO, developing BRDs, specs and wireframes, working with large relational databases, social networking and familiarity with mobile.

In addition, I have a strong background in print and online content strategy and community development. I have often worked closely with business development and advertising sales to deliver engaging content, tools and sites that increase traffic, add revenue, and interest sponsors.

I have strong project and employee management skills, plus a deep understanding of how sites work under the hood. I am often given the highest priority and most difficult projects.

I currently oversee the My Calorie Counter tool suite on Everydayhealth.com and am actively involved in the social networking and community area of the site. Some other recent projects include the complete redesign and launch of the well-known Mommy&Me brand, where I added new functionality and a powerful set of loyalty-building playgroup organizer tools. I also implemented a social marketing plan to promote at the grass-roots level.

Well-versed in the creation of all types of content such as print, online and user-generated, social networking including FB Open Graph, affiliate marketing, custom campaigns and ecommerce.

Managed large online communities and experienced in social networking sites such as Facebook, Twitter, MySpace, YouTube, PhotoBucket, wikis, blogging, podcasting, Ebay.

Edited and wrote for the following categories: babies, parenting, real estate, pets, food, health, fitness, technology, work/life balance, travel, finance, fashion and beauty

Major sites worked on include: EverydayHealth, ModernBride, MommyandMe, Kaboose, Babyzone, Parentzone, AmazingMoms, BubbleShare and PetPlace, plus Managing Editor of Working Mother magazine for three years.
