♜♞♝♛ Doug Devitre ♚♝♞♜’s Summary

Doug Devitre delivers dynamic educational programs to real estate professionals, brokers, REALTOR® Associations, and other instructors.  His approach of “knowing the why and delegating the how” empowers the techchallenged with simple steps to build an effective online brand using social media, maximizing free media channels, and increasing the value that you provide your clients. 

 What will I get if I hire Doug for my group’s next event?  
☑ You will receive consistent follow up to ensure all the details have been arranged.  
☑ You will get customizable marketing materials including video, flyers, and an email-marketing campaign. 
 ☑ You will get a professional speaker who is bound by the NSA (National Speakers Association) Code of Ethics.  
☑ You will not need to rely on the projector.  Doug doesn’t need technology to present.  
☑ You will receive an electronic recap which contains pictures, video, and notes from the event.    

What will the audience walk away with?  
✍ They will receive a simple-to-follow action plan to implement new ideas.  
✍ They will get a list of recommended websites for new technologies, communication tools, and online resources.  
✍ They will feel empowered to overcome their fear of using technology and take action.  
✍ Lastly, every attendee will receive an email with the PowerPoint, templates, and action scripts for FREE.
