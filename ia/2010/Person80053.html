Harold Johnson’s Summary

Harold is currently Senior Pastor of Faith Deliverance Family Worship Center. He is a progressive church leader of an urban ministry with an empahsis on equipping the Body of Christ, cultivating witnesses, leadership development, and community development.

He was formerly Vice President at Commerce Bank, where he developed business for the bank in the nonprofit sector.

He remains very active in the community, having worked with such organizations as the Council on Philanthropy, the United Way of Wyandotte County and many others. He has been recognized as a Black Achiever in Business and Industry and is a graduate of the Urban League Multicultural Leadership Institute. Most recently he served on the Board of Directors of the Kansas Black Chamber of Commerce.

Harold Johnson’s Specialties:

Consulting nonprofits and religious organizations with regard to commercial banking services to include business services an emphasis in commercial lending (both conventional as well as bond financing).
