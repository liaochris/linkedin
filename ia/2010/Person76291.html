Lisa Creech Bledsoe’s Summary

I am a senior-level media professional with more than 20 years of experience in business leadership, eight years specifically in life-science related media and marketing.

Since March of 2005 I have been working with Calvert Holdings in Cary, NC as a Brand Manager and Creative Director, which allows me to draw on my design and leadership skills while diving full force into online marketing and social media. At the Holdings I am responsible for brand development and oversight, research and reporting, and developing our online profile.

I am also the Director of Calvert Creative, which lets me offer seminars and give keynotes on internet technology and online communications.

At home I am mother to three boys, and I make it to most of my husband's rock and roll gigs. I am an amateur boxer, and I also blog about boxing, parenting, music, and media.
