Numerous, Good Times "Best Doctor in Santa Cruz" awards.

As a start-up entrepreneur who has risen to the top of every profession or industry he has chosen to enter, Dr. Piazza's hands-on expertise in start-up business, platform sales and public speaking has resulted in over 75 million dollars in business revenue to date.
