George De Marco’s Summary

Founded The Greater Alarm Company, Inc. in 1981. Initially offering access control, CCTV, fire life/safety and intrusion systems including monitoring for residential, business and governmental clients, Greater Alarm later expanded services to include audio/video systems, home theaters and structured cabling solutions.

As CEO and President, entrepreneurial spirit and industry involvement was instrumental in growing Greater Alarm into one of the largest and most respected electronic security companies in California. Greater Alarm received nationwide recognition as one of the top 100 largest electronic security firms in the United States by Security Distributing & Marketing (SDM) magazine and received SDM’s most prestigious award, Dealer of the Year. In addition, Greater Alarm was named one of the INC. 500 Fastest Growing Private Companies in America for three consecutive years.

Developed the strategic vision, acquisition strategy and customer service standards for Greater Alarm. The company acquired several companies and opened five additional branches.

Held numerous positions within the security industry, including President of the Orange County Alarm Association, Vice President - Southern Region for the California Alarm Association, Board of Director and Founding Member of the WBFAA - a California Unilateral Apprenticeship and Training Organization for fire/life safety technicians, Chair of the ESX Conference - an industry trade show, Chair of the Executive Strategic Partnership for the National Burglar and Fire Alarm Association and was appointed by the Governor of California to serve as a member of the Disciplinary Review Board for the Bureau of Security and Investigative Services, the California state agency that governs the electronic security industry.
