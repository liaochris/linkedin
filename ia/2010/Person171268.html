Ren Reynolds’s Summary

My professional experience spans: marketing strategies & product road maps, product development (internet, hosting and hosted solutions), writing, product management and hard-core IT.

My particular expertise is in developing products and services around emerging technologies, I do all aspects of development from Imagineering the service, creating the business case, developing specifications, negotiating partnerships, creating processes, launching and on-going management; though I favour the initial stages. I have worked on very high budget global telco projects and with smaller companies.

Increasingly I am working with people in order to make the most out of emerging virtual spaces such as Second Life, There, Habbo etc.

I also write academically about the ethics of technology and commercially about the application of emerging technologies.
