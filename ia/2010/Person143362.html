Michael Raspatello’s Summary

After spending four years founding and developing Rivalfish, Inc., a leading rivalry-themed online comedy brand regularly featured in national print, broadcast, and radio outlets such as WGN, ESPN, Sports Illustrated, USA Today, and Entrepreneur Magazine, Mike and his partners sold Rivalfish to National Lampoon in January 2008. A serial entrepreneur in entertainment management and production, Mike served as the Executive Producer of the nationally-syndicated Mancow Radio Experience, founded the Chicago Bluegrass & Blues Festival, and co-produced a series of live music events at leading Chicago venues.

Michael Raspatello’s Specialties:

Conceptualizing and creating successful marketing campaigns, managing websites, developing new business, writing copy, leading brands, and producing top-tier events, producing HD films, brokering sponsorship relationships, etc, experiential marketing.
