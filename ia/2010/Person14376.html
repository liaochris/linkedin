Anne Wilks’s Summary

Anne Wilks has over 12 years in the Learning & Development field. She is currently working as a Business Development Manager at Clarity Consultants, a national L & D outsourcing firm. Anne owned and operated her own training, coaching and consulting firm,The Success Institute, for the past 7 years. She graduated from The University of Louisiana in Lafayette, LA with a BA in Sociology & Psychology. She completed her coach training at Corporate Coach University International. Anne is actively involved with several organizations such as the American Society of Training and Development where she served as the VP of Marketing, National Society of Human Resource Managers, and The Greater Baton Rouge Chamber of Commerce. She maintains ongoing certifications and advanced training and stays current on training trends.
