Michael Marinoff’s Summary

WHAT IS FM RESOURCES?

WE ARE NOT A STAFFING COMPANY.
WE ARE A STAFFING BROKER.
WE ARE YOUR RESOURCE TO SUCCESS!

WE CAN HELP BRING STAFFING "IN-HOUSE"

Our extensive industry experience, vendor neutrality, and broad array of resources allow us to determine the best solutions and the best rates for you.

Type in a simple internet search for “staffing agency.” You get nearly 2 million hits. Open up your local phone book. You narrowed that search down to 500 staffing providers. Or even pick up your phone- you have 5 different sales people from 5 different agencies calling you all for the same thing. Staffing problems are often difficult enough in today’s market. Deciding which vendor to use just adds more complexity. WE STOP THE PHONES FROM RINGING.

We screen and interview staffing providers and recruiters in the same way you interview your candidates. We make sure they meet the qualifications required to do the job you hire them for. We eliminate the guesswork, risk, and uncertainty from vendor selection. We carefully match employers to qualified vendors. We negotiate to get you the best rates and terms- with your existing vendors and with new providers. Vendor neutrality allows FM Resources to recommend the most effective combination of vendors and resources for any business need.

We're flexible in our approach and committed to your success. Our innovative process incorporates current market realities as well as your goals, timeframe, and budget. We also offer consulting solutions specifically dedicated to the needs of small business.

How Can Vendor Recommendations be FREE? It’s designed to be simple. An independent staffing broker is paid by the staffing providers, and therefore our vendor recommendation service can be offered free to the employers. We eliminate the guesswork from vendor selection for you, and we attract new customers for vendors. Both parties benefit by having an objective expert manage the entire process.

Michael Marinoff’s Specialties:

1. Vendor Neutrality. FM Resources takes a Vendor Neutral approach to finding the staffing solution that is best for you.

2. Quality and Cost Efficiency. As industry professionals, we help you determine the best solution and also negotiate pricing rates and terms on your behalf - to ensure that you get the best service with the best rates.

3. Experience!
