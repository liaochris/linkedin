Jarrod Holt’s Summary

Jarrod Holt is an experienced IT Systems Administrator, Project Administrator and Help Desk Manager, with responsibilities, including configuring, implementing and maintaining enterprise infrastructure systems, local and wide area networks, also in managing and deploying software specific to the financial services industry, including EpicorEnterprise, SalesLogix, Encompass, Leads360, and others. Holt's experience includes management of Cisco Unified Communications VoIP, Call Center applications, and PBX systems, he has experience implementing virtualization migration and consolidation strategies using leading products from VMware, HP, Veeam, and others.

Holt has provided technical expertise to International Corporations such as Wendy’s, in engagement with Point of Sales systems development projects, and has provided technical consulting to a variety of industry leaders in Health Care, Financial Services, Human Resources, and others. In 2004 Holt graduated from DeVry University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Information Systems, with a field of study comprised of Systems Analysis and Design, Programming, Systems Management, Directory architecture, and Project Management. After graduating, Holt joined Aurum partners as a Project Administrator and coordinated software development efforts with developers overseas. Since then, Holt has continued to create value in every role he has filled, with the knowledge and expertise necessary to analyze needs and opportunities for improvement, resulting in an increase to the bottom line. Holt is currently a member of the Association of Information Technology Professionals (AITP), CIO Forum, Columbus, Ohio Technology Professionals, and Practitioners of IT Economic Value Creation.
