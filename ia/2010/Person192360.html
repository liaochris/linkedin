Lindsay Spiller’s Summary

Why I practice Law...

I love what I do. I get great personal satisfaction helping people overcome challenges, resolve problems, and manifest their dreams. As an estate planning lawyer, I help individuals and families create estate plans to provide for future generations. As a business lawyer, I help startups and medium sized business grow and flourish. As an entertainment lawyer, I help artists successfully pursue their dreams and thereby enrich our community and our culture. I am one part dealmaker, one part mediator, and one part counselor --- skills that I've acquired as a result of my own varied life experiences. It is a privilege to be able to make such significant contributions to people's lives and I feel very lucky to be a member of this marvelous profession.
