    Socialmark Media ~ Start Your Movement

    Opening our doors as Muirhead Marketing & Sales, Socialmark Media has dramatically changed over the course of our 3 year history. When I founded Muirhead Marketing, I wanted a simple marketing firm, with a focus on New Media, including Social Media, Blogging, Podcasting, email Marketing etc.

    However, over the course of the three years, I discovered that there are two overwhelming factors in business. One is the persevered value of your product or service.     Hence our shift in focus, from a campaign driving marking company, to a business with a drive to build movements! It's an amazing process, and we're thrilled to be a part of it.

    Connect with me here, and find out a little bit more about our philosophy of creating movements to increase to increase the value of influence of businesses.
