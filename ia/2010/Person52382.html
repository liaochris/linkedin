Dave Gilbert’s Summary

Business owner, manager and consultant with a proven track record of maximizing resources, streamlining production and costs while increasing ROI. Cross industry functional expertise includes: aerospace & defense, pulp & paper, electronics, consumer goods and medical supplies. Extensive experience in discrete, repetitive and process manufacturing environments. Strong technical and interpersonal skills. Certified Lean Professional (CLP) and Certified in Production and Inventory Management (CPIM).

SELECTED ACCOMPLISHMENTS

• Purchase of Mille Fabricators - assembled a team of professionals, conducted the search, prepared a business plan and secured the financing for the purchase.
• Through better utilization of internet technologies, added 43 new customers and increased sales of the digital imaging product line by 130% and the wood fabrication by 65%.
• Led a $105,000 capital project to procure a CNC panel saw.
• Using lean methodologies, reduced inventory by 23%
• Directed a successful $1,500,000 software development project to modify the MANMAN system for use in multiple mill locations.
• Starting with a staff of 13 grew an IT department to 31 employees. Implemented department administrative and project development systems.
• Led a successful “Proof of Concept” project to demonstrate the use of pervasive technology methods using hand held devices to enter maintenance work orders and perform remote stock status inquirers of repair parts
• Successful conversion of an outdated version of MAX and Great Plains to QuickBooks 10.0 Enterprise.
