Lauren Perdue’s Summary

I've enjoyed parlaying the fascination I've held with the internet since I was twelve into all facets of my professional life, which has included fashion, journalism, marketing and public/media relations. Getting to grow up in tandem with the advent of Social Media has made me fluent in Facebook, Twitter, Myspace and all aspects of Social Networking culture. Pursuing multiple disciplines academically and professionally makes me well-rounded and relatable, which in turns makes me a better writer, designer, and search marketer. Understanding multiple points of view helps me market to any audience. I'm especially media and web-savvy for someone my age and am eager to learn more every day in our swiftly evolving field.
