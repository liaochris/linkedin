Timo S. Helenius’s Summary

Publications:

* Uskonnonfilosofia (The Philosophy of Religion). Coeditor with Timo Koistinen & Sami Pihlström. Helsinki: WSOY. 2003. The first Finnish comprehensive university level introduction to the philosophy of religion.

* Tukihenkilöopas (A Study Book for Supporting Persons). Editor. Helsinki: Sylva ry. 2004. A study book related to a nationwide project, under my coordination, of building up a training program and network of supporting persons for cancer suffering young persons.

* Meille annettu maa: pohjoisen kirkon ympäristökirja (The Land Given to Us: The Northen Church and the Environment). Editor. Helsinki: Edita Publishing. 2007. The book relates to a multi-institutional environmental project, of which I was in charge, in the geographic area of the Evangelic Lutheran Diocese of Oulu.
