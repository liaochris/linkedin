Steven Gould’s Summary

Senior technical advisor, published writer, application architect and software developer with experience designing and developing n-tier applications utilizing Object Oriented languages including C++ and Java. With over 19 years experience designing and developing commercial software applications on a variety of hardware and software platforms, experience spans the video entertainment, software development, telecom, travel/airline, retail and food processing industries.

A postgraduate degree in Management Science (MBA) with a specialty in Operations Research (Industrial Engineering) provides a strong business perspective complementing a strong technical background from an undergraduate degree in Computer Science.
