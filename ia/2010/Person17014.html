Arun Kumar’s Summary

A veterinarian and in vivo pharmacologist with over ten years of experience in cardiovascular and vascular complications associated with diabetes and hypertension, with good publication record (Total Impact Factor: 122.35 and Scholar Index: 5). Expertise in preclinical pharmacology, transgenic mice phenotyping, vascular and cardiovascular phenotyping using in vivo and in vitro models, imaging, regenerative medicine and cell biology.
