Tara Wagner’s Summary

I am a communications professional with 10+ years of experience in the entertainment and consumer technology industries. Expertise includes: media relations, communication/PR strategy, program management and execution, messaging, writing, editing, offline event production.

I have a passion for entertainment and pop culture and I’m on a mission to help translate “tech” to the average consumer.   

I’m currently a consultant working to launch and enhance exposure for consumer tech start-ups. I have worked with a variety of clients on company strategy, product and company launches, messaging, media relations, program management.

Some past clients include: VUDU (acquired by Walmart), Trulia, Slide, RealNetworks, Lala (acquired by Apple), MedHelp, TrunkClub, AtomFilms (acquired by Viacom), Shockwave (acquired by Viacom) Doppelganger, Babycenter, Airplay.

Prior to moving to moving to San Francisco, I was engaged by Dreamworks SKG to work on the national publicity campaign for animated movie "Over the Hedge". Before that, I worked for Metro Goldwyn Mayer managing national publicity campaigns for many studio blockbusters. I also served as a publicist at New Line Cinema working on national publicity and promotional campaigns for major films such as "Lord of the Rings" and regional film campaigns for their independent studio Fine Line Features.

In addition, I have significant event production and brand management experience. One of my most notable projects was a product launch for Chrysler's PT Cruiser, where I oversaw the production of national and regional film, fashion, art and music events surrounding the Chrysler brand.
