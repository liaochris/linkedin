Marc Jackson’s Summary

Film and Television composer.

Specializes in intelligent, high quality custom music with quick delivery time. AIM: moonlabm.

Marc's music company: MoonLab Music, specializes in song and score placement for television, film, movie marketing and advertising.

Clients include, ABC, NBC/Universal, CBS, HBO, Paramount, Warner Bros., Lions Gate, DreamWorks, and Disney.

As a composer Marc created score for series on ABC, NBC/Universal, HBO and other major networks.

Film composition: Comfortable and capable in all musical styles.

Custom songwriting with lyrics: Provided custom original songs for television shows on NBC and CBS. Prolific singer/songwriter in all styles.

Music Supervision: "Tourgasm" (HBO) and shows for other major premium and cable networks.

Executive Producer: "Tourgasm" (HBO) soundtrack on Rhino/Playtone Records.

Musician and artist. Formerly signed to MCA records. Later toured with Roger Daltrey as guitarist and vocalist as well as other popular acts. Has made numerous television appearances as a musician. Has performed live in venues from The Universal Amphitheater to the famous Radio City Music Hall and all points inbetween.

Currently scoring for numerous theatrical trailers, a main title for HGTV and 2 feature films for release in 2010.

Marc Jackson’s Specialties:

Speedy delivery of inspired music in virtually any genre for any medium. Well-versed in long and short form composition. Able to sing in any style.

Particularly adept at main titles and themes for TV shows and commercials.

Unique understanding of the challenges of production from all sides. Having produced shows, music supervised and worked in music clearance, I have a unique 360 degree view and appreciation of the process.
