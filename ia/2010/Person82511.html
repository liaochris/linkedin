Holly Fisher’s Summary

I spend my days as a freelance writer/editor and an account executive with Step Ahead Inc. I previously was a long-time editor and writer for SC Biz News LLC, publisher of the Charleston Regional Business Journal and other business publications around the state. I handled a number of projects there, ranging from overseeing special publications to launching the company's e-mail newsletters.

I am a member of the Society of Professional Journalists and former regional director and board member with the Society. I also served on the Sigma Delta Chi Foundation board.

After completing my master's degree in mass communication at the University of South Carolina, I tried my hand at teaching. I spent three semesters as an adjunct professor at the College of Charleston.

I spent more than a decade in the newspaper business, working in West Virginia, Indiana, Texas and South Carolina.

A native of Ohio, I have a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Ohio University.

I lives in Mount Pleasant, S.C., with my husband, Clint; daughter, Katherine; and two Labrador retrievers.

Holly Fisher’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, creating new publications/products, experience with online media, project management, teaching at the college level.
