Bill Perlman’s Experience

  • Staff Photojournalist/Video Journalist

    The Star-Ledger

    (Privately Held; Newspapers industry)

    March 1991 — Present (19 years 4 months)

    William Perlman is an award-winning photojournalist living and working in the New York Metropolitan area. A full time staff photographer and videographer for The Star-Ledger. He covered the resignation of NJ Gov. James McGreevey and was part of the 2005 Pulitzer Prize for Breaking News Reporting for The Star-Ledger. He’s been a newspaper photographer for a quarter century, including stints as a staff photographer at three NYC / NJ area newspapers & previously he was the chief photographer at The Daily Register in Shrewsbury. His photographs have been awarded both local and national recognition. His work has also appeared in Newsday, USA Today and on ESPN.COM. He has covered all major sporting events including the Stanley Cup Finals, The NBA Finals, NCAA Final Four Tournaments, Major League Baseball All-Star Game, World Cup Soccer, Super Bowl XLII and numerous World Series. He has cover News events including Presidential Conventions, Airline Crashes and the Attacks on the World Trade Center in New York City. Perlman graduated from The American University in Washington, DC in 1984 with a BA in Visual Media. He is currently in his 3rd term as the Treasurer of the New York Press Photographers Association as well as a Sports Committee member. He is also a member of the National Press Photographers Association and the New Jersey Press Photographers Association. US/NY mobile #: 1.516.857.0625
