Derek Hildreth’s Summary

In a nutshell, I'm a student finishing up a B.S. degree in Computer and Electrical Engineering at BYU-Idaho. I'm married to Heather and we have a beautiful two year old baby girl named Qeirstyn. When we can find the time, we love to get outdoors and take in the sights and activities like swimming, rappelling, biking, snowboarding, camping, hiking, and much more.

I have what some would call an unhealthy obsession (I would call it a passion) with computers that started back when my Dad picked up our first computer in 1993. So, 16 years later I'm anxious to being a career that will put me in the front seat of emerging technology. I've done pretty much anything that has to do with computers. I've had direct experience in web design, on-site technical service, software and hardware consulting services, and website hosting. I have taken it upon myself to become an entrepreneur of sorts and explore each of these areas with my own freelancing business which supported the community in which I lived. I've also been a Computer Systems Administrator several different organizations in the Rexburg, ID area. I'm looking forward to what the next opportunity will bring me.

I support and use Linux and FOSS whenever I possibly can. In the spirit of the FOSS mainstream community, I give back as much as I can by contributing artwork, ideas, tutorials, and I help promote the use of the software by example. My passion for Linux and FOSS has driven me to many different and exciting areas including embedded systems, servers, virtualization, professional typesetting, engineering, and more.

In the end, this geeky father is fun loving, optimistic, curious, and enjoys living life to its fullest.
