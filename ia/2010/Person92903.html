Jeff Adkins’s Summary

A seasoned, talent acquisition professional with a solid track record of providing high-caliber talent to his clients, Jeff has successfully provided nationwide executive search and recruitment-related services to a number of organizations, large and small, from start-ups to Fortune 100’s. He has successfully recruited hundreds of professionals for positions ranging from individual contributors to senior executives. He has provided talent acquisition support to his clients in the areas of recruitment and sourcing strategy.

Companies that he has worked with: Dell, Dell Financial Services, Microsoft, Apple, eBay, Varian Medical Systems, Spansion, Mentor Graphics, Sprint, Intel, Sun, Cisco, AMD, Freescale, Analog Devices, Alcatel, Tellabs, Amphenol, SolarWinds, BMC Software, USAA, and Motorola, to name a few.

Throughout his professional career, with an expertise in recruitment strategy planning, Jeff has developed a strong track record of quickly assessing client needs, and is adept in providing solutions specifically tailored to these needs. His extensive background in business and management has provided him with excellent insight into the organizational needs of The Adkins Group clients.

Before founding The Adkins Group, Jeff was a Senior Partner of an Austin, Texas-based, executive search firm, and, before that, was a top-performing Executive Search Consultant at a technology-focused search firm, also based in Austin.

Jeff earned his B.S. degree in Business Administration from Southern Illinois University, and a Masters Certificate in Human Resource Management from St. Edward’s University. He is a Certified Professional in Human Resources (PHR), and a member of the Austin Chamber of Commerce, Austin Technology Council, and the Society of Human Resources Management (SHRM). He has been a regular speaker on the subject of talent acquisition/management, and when time allows, provides career management/job hunt strategy advice to professionals in need.

Jeff Adkins’s Specialties:

Executive Search; Targeted Research; Recruiting Process Model Development/Developing a Recruiting Plan; Identifying Sources of Qualified Candidates/Target List Development; Candidate Selection Model Development/Screening Candidates; Attracting Potential Candidates, Selling the Organization; Implementing Strategies to Keep Your Top Employees; Align Staffing and Retention to the Core Culture of the Organization; Training and Management (train and manage in-house recruiters).
