Caitlin Berens’s Summary

Freelance journalist with experience in Colorado, Des Moines, Los Angeles and New York. Magazine journalism is my passion. I am an optimist who thrives off of accomplishment, who expects a lot out of the people she works with and ten times more from herself. In my career I have covered a myriad of topics in the print and online spheres. I want to work for a lifestyle, popular culture, fitness, music or women’s magazine as a writer, working my way up the ranks to become an editor of a successful publication.
