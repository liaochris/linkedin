Paul G. Hauf’s Summary

For the past three decades, Paul G. Hauf has focused his customized financing services to a wide spectrum of business and commercial clients. From 1980 until the present, Paul G. Hauf has built a large network of 350 lenders that include both the banking and non-banking industries. The stated goal of Paul G. Hauf and his team has been to service clients with financing solutions that are personalized specifically to meet the needs of local and regional private businesses and professional practices.

The mission of Paul G. Hauf is to provide lending platforms for those borrowers who might be unable to obtain financing through more traditionally structured lenders. These are borrowers who seek solid financing options, and Paul G. Hauf has successfully provided those financing solutions directly to businesses or individual professionals as well as working through a network of independent, business intermediaries. The objective of Paul G. Hauf is to provide as many business entities with the funds necessary in order to meet their growth objectives.

For many years, Paul G. Hauf has also served many business clients with comprehensive brokerage services. At various times, Paul G. Hauf has facilitated one or the other side of the transaction equation, acting for either the seller or the buyer of businesses or professional practices. His role as a professional intermediary between parties has assisted in simplifying many business transfer transactions.

Acting on behalf of the seller of a business, Paul G. Hauf offers services in business valuation and can market the business without interruption to the owner, identify potential buyers to pre-qualify them, assess and facilitate necessary financing and even complete due diligence evaluation necessary to close a sale.

For clients who are on the buying side of the equation, Paul G. Hauf offers the ability to locate a business opportunity that best meets their specific needs and budget.
