Dave Olson’s Summary

I have a wide variety of experience, from banking and Wall Street to being an individual consultant. My MBA is in finance. I have assisted large IT firms acquire federal contracts in excess of $100 million, created a program for a wholesale student loan lender that brought them over $100 million of retail loans, assisted Dartmouth College when they went co-ed, sold commercial real estate, conducted major direct mail marketing programs, been on my local school board and planning commission and have been on the Board of George Mason University. I have also been a part of startup firms, including a firm that designed and sold content managment software and a digital publishing company that captured older texts, created digital images and indices for searching the material.

Dave Olson’s Specialties:

Education, direct marketing, sales, consulting and project management.
