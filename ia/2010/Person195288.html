Steve McEwen’s Summary

As an Illinois Licensed Real Estate Broker – with 75 hours of extensive real estate training, I embrace all technology, tools and market statistics available to aggressively market your listing or find you the perfect home.

As a former licensed Appraiser – I use my professional training and appraisal skills to accurately evaluate residential and commercial property by cost, income approach or sales comparison to help safeguard your biggest investment…your home.

As a Certified Negotiation Expert - I have the training and experience to negotiate everything on your behalf from price, upgrades, extras and closing terms.

As an Accredited Buyer Representative – I have the training and experience to help first time buyers and seasoned investors in both conventional and non-conventional terms. From FHA properties to "short sales" and foreclosures, I will protect you from making a bad investment and guide you to make the best investment.

As a Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist – I have the tools and proven track record that targets the luxury home buyer & seller. I specialize in luxury single family homes and residential developments, serving Chicago's north side and the surrounding suburbs.

As a Chicago native and real estate investor since 1994- I grew up in Lincoln Park and Lake View to name but a few of Chicago's great neighborhoods. I will be exceptionally resourceful in helping you find ‘hidden treasures’ in the perfect neighborhood that suits your lifestyle.

I am honored to be awarded "Top Producer" by The Chicago Association of Realtors (2007, 2008 & 2009) and @properties (2007, 2008 & 2009). I owe it all to my clients and friends who provide 90% of our business from your referrals.

I’m grateful for your business, and I appreciate your referrals.

Thank you for your trust.
