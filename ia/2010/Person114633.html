Revenue recognition projects:
For this client specific requirements are in place concerning software revenue recognition. The SSC is required to report to the ultimate parent under US GAAP. I have been responsible for leading 5 experienced team members. The team tested whether revenues have been recognized in compliance with SAB 104 and SOP-97-2. Both SAB 104 as well as SOP 97-2 gives guidance in how to account for revenue recognition relating to activities that represent licensing, selling, leasing or other marketing of computer software. As the subject is considered to be highly complex special accreditation was required to perform the tasks. The main objective of the project was to review contracts, obtain substantive evidence, review deferred revenue movements, review revenue transfers. As the contents of underlying contracts were highly complex a profound understanding of revenue recognition under US GAAP was required. Within this project my role was to coach team members and consult on technical complex matters and challenge management’s accounting treatment.

EMEA Coordination:
The main objective of this project was to provide local PwC teams with sufficient comfort on audit areas related to commissions, for their local statutory audit, based on audit work performed at the SSC. The following tasks were my responsibility:
· Plan audit
· Review risk assessment
· Review evaluation of control design effectiveness
· Review evaluation of control operational effectiveness
· Review substantive audit evidence
