Sheri Archidiacono’s Summary

Presently engaged in a well rounded corporate and business law practice. Areas included complex corporate business transactions, domestic and international contract review, drafting and negotiation, international and domestic intellectual property law, UDRP process, unfair competition, litigation, mergers and acquisitions and e-commerce.

Litigation subject matter includes issues of breach of contract, breach of warranty, trademark infringement, domain name disputes, unfair competition, Chapter 93A, and interference with business transactions.
