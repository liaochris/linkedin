Leslie Beck’s Summary

A Kansas City native and KU grad, I have spent years learning to think like prospects in dozens of industries and market segments. This helps me understand your vocabulary, and quickly grasp your projects needs and interests. Some say  I am a jill-of-all-trades when it comes to designing for print  or interactive.

Qualified and comfortable in a variety of business venues, my years of experience and assignments have helped conceive, create and execute award-winning designs for the Lyric Opera, Argus Health Systems, Bollé and Serengeti Sunglasses, Hallmark, the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation of Kansas City, Swirk Jewelers, Houlihan’s Restaurants and the Residence of Kirkwood. I have also used my problem solving ability and attention to detail working with many of the areas noted advertising and marketing companies.

My extensive experience in logo, corporate identity and branding development has helped many clients create, establish, refresh and manage their brands products  and services.

Leslie Beck’s Specialties:

Logo design, identity, web design, book design, typography, print media
