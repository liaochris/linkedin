Robert Melok, Jr.’s Summary

I'm a student at the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism. I graduated from Montclair State University in 2009 with a degree in Broadcasting.

For almost two years, I served as the Sports Editor for Montclair State's student-produced newspaper, The Montclarion.

In June 2008, I became the newspaper's Editor in Chief. I worked with Montclair State University's administration, the American Civil Liberties Union of New Jersey and the Nissenbaum Law Group to establish the newspaper as an independent news organization, which came to fruition on July 28, 2008, when we formed Montelican Publishing, Inc., and filed for incorporation with the State of New Jersey. Montelican Publishing, Inc. is the company that prints The Montclarion, and is filing for 501(c)(3) status.

Outside of Montclair State, I had the opportunity to cover the 2008 Hambletonian for the Asbury Park Press this summer, as part of the Clyde Hirt Journalism Workshop. Two stories were published in the July 31 and August 3, 2008 papers.
