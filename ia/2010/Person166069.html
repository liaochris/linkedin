Steve Selengut’s Summary

I've been a private investment manager since 1979, working primarilly with individuals and a few small businesses. I sell no investment products, receive no commissions, and focus on individual securities.

I invented, use, and teach my unique Working Capital Model investment methodology. Other proprietary concepts include: the QDI, Smart Cash, and Base Income. I commissioned the development of the IGVSI, and the Value Stock Watch list.

Steve Selengut’s Specialties:

Hands on investment management, portfolio restructuring, retirment income portfolios, investment education.

In a Working Capital Model methodology world, there may well have been no 2008 to 2009 financial crisis.
