Brian K. Miller’s Summary

I am a contract and freelance business journalist specializing in commercial and multifamily real estate finance, investment and development since 1997. My exclusive outlet for the past eight years has been Real Estate Media [now a subsidiary of American Lawyer Media], which owns the GlobeSt.com web site and Real Estate Forum, a national monthly print magazine. With the New Year came my freedom to write for other publications, which I have already begun to do. Prior to 2002, I was a bureau chief for GlobeSt.com, managing multiple writers and web pages, as well as a business writer, a legislative correspondent and a city hall reporter for several daily and weekly publications in the Puget Sound region.

I also currently co-own and operate a media relations and strategic writing services business whose clients have included real estate services firms, law firms, software development firms and public relations firms. My wife and I formed the company in 2002 order to work from home while raising our daughter.
