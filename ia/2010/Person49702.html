David McLauchlan’s Summary

Presently the Senior Business Development Manager in Microsoft's Zune team, with responsibility for mobile applications & games, on-the-go scenarios (automotive, hospitality, mobility, inflight), connectivity, new marketing strategies and 1st/3rd party accessories.

Additionally, I chair the USB-IF Media Transfer Protocol working group, and the CEA R6 WG15 Standard Connector working group.

Formerly responsible for delivering Microsoft's digital media success in the automobile. As the PM for Windows Media for Automotive, I was tasked with the adoption of Windows Media technologies (codecs, DRM, protocols, standard connector) in the automobile.

Formerly managed Microsoft's PlaysForSure program for portable and networked devices. The PlaysForSure program certifies that portable media devices and networked media devices will work with PlaysForSure certified online download services (MSN Music, Napster, Movielink, etc...).

Formerly the Windows Media Devices Group Program Manager for Standards and Strategy, before that the Program Manager for Digital Still Cameras in the same group at Microsoft, and prior to that - the Program Manager for Print & Imaging Connectivity at Microsoft.

Before joining Microsoft, I was a professional musician with an international CD release in 1997 - "Memories of Madison" - Australia's biggest selling contemporary pianist.

David McLauchlan’s Specialties:

Vendor relationship management, requirements setting, legal & partnership agreements, standards representation, device evaluation and certification, technology evangelism.
