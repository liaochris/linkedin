Jon Pope’s Summary

I have over sixteen years of consulting experience in learning & development, people management and communications, covering pharmaceuticals, IT, professional services, retail/fmcg, financial services and the public sector.

My clients include Asda, Fujitsu, the Home Office, Ofcom, Learning & Skills Council, Philip Morris, East Sussex County Council, Highbury College, British Gas, The Wellcome Trust, The Royal College of Physicians and Pfizer. I also work as an associate consultant for A&DC Consultants, SHL Group, the leading provider of assessment tools and assessment consulting services, and as an associate member of the Chartered Management Institute, specialising in accredited and bespoke management development programmes.

I formed Barker Dewson Ltd. to allow myself and a small network of experienced associates to deliver high quality consulting services at affordable cost to the small and medium-sized business sector. I am a member of the Institute of Business Consultants, and Barker Dewson is audited to ISO9001 Quality Standards.

If you are interested in talking, please contact me through LinkedIn.

Jon Pope’s Specialties:

Learning & development, coaching, assessment consulting, management and leadership development, change management, employee engagement, strategy development, team building, customer service development, and compensation and reward programmes.
