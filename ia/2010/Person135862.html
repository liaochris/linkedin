  • Co-Founder & President

    .TV Technologies

    (Internet industry)

    2004 — 2007 (3 years )

    Managed the design and development of an open internet television software platform that enabled video content owners to create their own live and/or on-demand internet TV channels and distribute them to any internet-connected television in the world. The platform's three components – .TV ControlRoom, MainMenu.TV, and EasyPay.TV – gave content owners full control over their channels and content while making it possible for viewers to use a remote control to navigate a virtually unlimited selection of ad-supported, subscription, pay-per-view, and pay-per-minute video from around the globe. Successfully demonstrated platform and gained significant interest from film studios, television broadcast networks, and major content owners such as National Geographic Channel and E! Entertainment Television. In 2007, .TV Technologies was liquidated due to investor concerns that sufficient barriers to competition had not been created against potential competitors such as Google, Yahoo, Microsoft, Apple, and cable and satellite companies.
