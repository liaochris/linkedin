Yvonne English’s Summary

Yvonne English has a unique professional background that brings the best of both worlds to the table: deep experience within Corporate America and Corporate Canada as well as with her own entrepreneurial ventures. She started her first company at the age of 24. That company, which was eventually sold, was a boutique consulting firm specializing in investments and retirement plans in the Washington, DC metropolitan area. Two years after founding the firm, she had the honor of being asked to work in Toronto, Ontario, Canada as an International Management Consultant for a large international insurance organization.

Upon her return to the States, Ms. English joined the 5th largest accounting and consulting firm in the United States. She became the director of a large pension consulting office within the firm and was eventually promoted to the position of National Operations Director of the retirement services division.

While working her way up the corporate ladder, Yvonne continues to follow her passion for entrepreneurship in her spare time. Through the years, she has played a key role in an alternative energy/biodiesel project in Texas, founded and sold Salvagist.com – an online retailer of high-end accessories made from recycled materials and has started Social Animal Media - a new media company that allows her to mentor entrepreneurial Gen Y college students as they deliver social media and online marketing services to clients around the world.

She continues to give back to her alma mater, Grove City College, by mentoring students, judging the annual business plan competition and acting as a member of the Entrepreneurial Advisory Council.

Ms. English has been featured in Arizona Woman Magazine, Phoenix Business Journal, video and an online for Kiva.org/Kiva B4B and in several blogs for her innovative products and focus on corporate social responsibility. She has also been featured in the Gedunk, Grove City College's Alumni magazine.
