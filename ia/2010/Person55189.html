Deseri Garcia’s Summary

Deseri is a dynamic coach, facilitator, speaker and a multi-sport adventure racing athlete. A coach at heart, her passion for genuinely connecting with people and identifying what is important to them are the cornerstones of the company. It is this dedication that complements her ability to develop team and personal effectiveness. Prior to launching Vida Aventura, Deseri spent nearly 20 years in business development, where she gained invaluable insight into the value of relationship building and communication within organizations. In 2009 Deseri served as President of Rainmakers Marketing Group. Deseri is ACCT certified (Association for Challenge Course Technology). She combines her coaching and athletic excellence to help runners and walkers prepare for charity race events.

With Vida Aventura our relationships begin with a single goal: to empower people to become their best. Whether you seek positive change for yourself or your organization, we're ready to help you reach that next level.
