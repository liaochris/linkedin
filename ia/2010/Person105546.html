My extensive experience in real estate investing and my background in computer software have converged in OnlyTheBestPractices.

Transitions are nothing like a real estate transaction... but they should be! Maybe if they were, the success rate of associateships and transitions would be higher... Think about this: Would you ever enter a real estate transaction where the industry expects the transaction to fail 85% of the time? Me neither.

Would you enter a real estate transaction where the selling agent does the appraisal? Would you trust it? Me neither.

I think I have uncovered the reasons why the failure rate of associateships is so high, and I know what can be done about it.

I have built a company that helps new dentists significantly increase the odds of finding a successful practice to join. We help senior dentists find the right buyers for their practices as well.

When the choice is between using a 10% commissioned practice broker, and going it alone... check out our services. We standardized the transition process. Your practice probably qualifies for our services.

Check out OnlyTheBestPractices.com to see what we offer... I think you will be amazed.
