Zennie Abraham [LION] - toplinked.com’s Summary

Zenophon "Zennie" Abraham is both Chairman and CEO of Sports Business Simulations (SBS) and Executive Producer of Zennie62.com. He's multi-talented with expertise in blogging, video-blogging, online marketing, search engine optimization, online communities, online publicity, and online game making, specializing in Forio Macro Language and System Dynamics.

His 2010 goals are as of this writing, to gain partnerships with organizations in entertainment, gain sponsors for Zennie62.com and investors in both Zennie62.com and SBS, and conduct video-blog interviews of celebrities and movie industry executives, and achieve 20 million views by year's end.

SBS makes online simulations of the business of sports organizations for use in the classroom. It consists of three online games created by Mr. Abraham: the Oakland Baseball Simworld, the XFL Simworld, and Buffy The Gym Girl. All games are designed for use as part of an online classroom or for traditional courses in business, marketing, and sport management. SBS is used in several colleges and high schools in America.

SBS itself was created in 2003.

Zennie62.com consists of a 100-blog network and several video channels, featured by his YouTube channel at http://www.youtube.com/zennie62. His channel has gained over 10 million viewers in just over two years. Zennie62.com also consists of a television show "The Blog Report with Zennie62" on The CoLoursTV Network at DISH 9407, Comcast, and Fox cable.

Zennie62.com covers pop-culture, politics, news, sports, and tech. It's featured on SFGate.com and Examiner.com
