Renee Rubin’s Summary

Extensive experience as technical writer for start-up and Fortune 100 companies.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

CISCO SYSTEMS, San Jose, CA 1996 - 2009
A global enterprise company that develops internetworking products and end-to-end product networking solutions.

As a Senior Technical Writer, Renee developed single-source documentation for network security management, hardware and software products, and Cisco IOS software used in enterprise and service provider environments. Documentation was developed using SCRUM agile and traditional waterfall development models. The documentation suite included user and installation guides, online help, command references, and release notes. Renee introduced product quick start guides to aid users in getting complex products up-and-running as quickly and effectively as possible.

Earlier roles at Cisco saw Renee leading project teams, while often serving as key contributor or lead writer. In addition, Renee took on special assignments involving usability, WIKI development, and multimedia. She mentored documentation staff at junior and intermediate levels, delivered in-depth peer edits, and developed new methods for documentation reviews.

AMO-Abbott (formerly VISX, Inc.) Santa Clara, CA 1993 – 1996
A start-up company that developed an excimer laser to perform corrective vision.

As Technical Publications Specialist, Renee was the sole person comprising the Technical Publications Dept. As such, she designed a corporate style guide and produced all writing, editing, and graphics used in preparation for FDA approval in the U.S., and regulatory approval in Europe. She was responsible for outsourcing print reproduction, hiring and mentoring contract writers, hiring translators, and document control.

Renee relied on her working relationships with affiliated surgeons and licensed medical trainer, engineering specs, and clinical trial results to produce user guides, field reference guides, and surgical procedure guides.
