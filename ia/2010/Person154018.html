Nick Hopkins’s Experience

  • Director of Product Management

    NAVTEQ

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; NVT; Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 2007 — Present (3 years )

    I manage a Product Management group creating a new Location-Aware Mobile Advertising product suite and business for NAVTEQ.

    I am involved in all aspects of this new line of business, including driving the business and product strategy, creating the ad platform products, and doing the deals with carriers, device manufacturers and publishers to get them into market.

  • Vice President of Product Management

    Traffic.com

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; TRFC; Broadcast Media industry)

    November 2004 — March 2007 (2 years 5 months)

    I managed a Product Management team which defined, led the development of, and launched and marketed a broad suite of Direct-to-Consumer services and B2B products.

    The D2C services I was responsible for included the Traffic.com website, which was built from nothing to over 2M monthly uniques and 700,000 registered users, and a suite of mobile products across WAP, IVR and J2ME platforms which were launched on Verizon, Sprint, T-Mobile, AT&T and other carriers.

    The B2B products included traffic information feeds and APIs which provide real-time roadway traffic information (flow/speed and incident) to a wide range of clients including Microsoft, Garmin, Comcast, AOL, OnStar and many other. In addition to running these products, I was deeply involved in business development and deal-making relating to them.
