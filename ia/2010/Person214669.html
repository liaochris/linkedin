Wendy Lewis’s Summary

Wendy Lewis & Co Ltd, Global Aesthetics Consultancy was founded in 1997 after having managed aesthetic surgery practices for some of New York's elite board certified plastic surgeons for 12 years. WLCO offers a wide range of specialized services dedicated to multi-specialty healthcare professionals, pharmaceutical brands, skin care marketers, biotech and medical device companies including Strategic Marketing, Brand Positioning, Successful Product Launch Support, Key Opinion Leader Management, Aesthetic Market Penetration, and Consumer Insights.

Author of 10 books including America's Cosmetic Doctors & Dentists (Castle Connolly Medical) and Plastic Makes Perfect (Orion), Lewis has also edited over 15 books for healthcare professionals including a New York Times bestselling diet book. She is currently a Columnist for Plastic Surgery Practice, Aesthetic Trends & Technologies, Healthnewsdigest.com, Editorial Director for Glow Beauty Magazine, and a Contributor to TATLER, Beautynewsnyc.com, Beautystat.com, Makemeheal.com, Merge Magazine, and many other trade and consumer publications and websites.

Founder and Editor in Chief of BEAUTYINTHEBAG.COM since 2008. Like us on Facebook and join our growing community of over 7,000 worldwide.
