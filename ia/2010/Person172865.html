Ken Balog’s Summary

As President of ActiveGroup, Ken is able to combine his unique background of more than 20 years of webcasting, executive management and his product development experience with his more than 15 years of operations management. ActiveGroup is the leading provider of online qualitative market research solutions for businesses that understand the importance and strategic advantages provided through Market Research. ActiveGroup's live and on-demand solutions act as enablers that assist businesses to more effectively conduct qualitative research.
