Professional Summary:

Ms. Boothe has 25 yrs experience as a journalist, communications expert in the areas of, Strategic Communications, Media, Civil Society Engagement and Participatory Trainings. She has lived, travelled, worked and/or reported from more than 50 countries.

She has worked in both private and public sectors. A short list of employers-and funders of projects she has served for: NDI, DFID, Freedom House International, MCC/MGTCP-Moldova, USAID, DAI, ACDI VOCA, MCC-Mongolia, European Commission and more.

Her communications, elections, civic training, public outreach and media consulting has taken her to: Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Mongolia, Moldova, Cambodia, Macedonia, Serbia and Sierra Leone. Today, she serves as a key adviser on a European Union support project in Kosovo.

Journalist, Various Countries Jan. 1987-Aug. 1999 Below are some highlights of int'l reporting:

Reporting had included reports on NATO actions in Serbia over the Kosovo Crisis and IDPs in Serbia.

Reporting on Palestinian women's voices from the West Bank in early 1987 foretold the eventual Intifada that same year.

Reported on women’s involvement in anti-trafficking efforts in Cambodia/Thailand and on women and HIV/AIDS education in Central Asia.

Reporting in Italy focused on modern cultural influences of ancient fresco art traditions on a project in the Church of St. Francis in Assisi.

Filed reports on the rising civic and political participation of young women in Egypt and Morocco.

Was Sr. Poltical Reporter for MPR. Also filed/reported for: BBC, NPR, Christian Science Monitor Radio, CBC, Common Ground Public Radio in U.S. and various publications.
