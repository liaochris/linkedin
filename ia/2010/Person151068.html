Larry Najera goRoundTown.com’s Summary

Larry Najera, Jr. is a multimedia communications designer, technology consultant, and web marketing specialist based in Middle Georgia. Winning over 100 awards since 1988 for his design work in the field of broadcast video, print, signage, interactive and web design, Larry and his team of designers can help you and/or your company to maximize technology to help you achieve your goals.

Larry is the founder of the revolutionary goRoundTown® Network, a group of websites, blogs and social media applications that promote everything local and features 360 degree high definition virtual tours.
