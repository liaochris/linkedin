Tracy Moon’s Summary

StudioMoon is privately owned by Founder and Creative Director, Tracy Moon. With nearly 25 years' experience in design and identity related firelds, Tracy has worked for some of the most prestigious design agencies in the world. Currently an Identity instructor at The Academy of Art University in San Francisco, Tracy is a published author and the recipient of numerous awards for her firm's work in the design industry.

StudioMoon specializes in Visual Identity - the art of helping companies communicate who they are, and why they matter, to the people who matter to them most.

The firm handles all aspects of the development or revitalization of a visual identity for companies of all sizes and from all industries – helping them from the earliest stages to determine their "reason for being," their edge against the competition, and their most efficient and powerful means of getting credit in a crowded competitive field. Ideally our work is the first step in a company's decision to seek recognition for their efforts. A commitment to developing a strong identity before proliferating a myriad of materials will ensure that the company is not spending good money after bad, and that their "corporate house" is built on a truly solid foundation.

A strong, cohesive identity program might include naming, taglines, symbols, colors, typography, systems for online and printed materials - anything that helps establish a definite "look and feel" for the company (as well as it's products and services) over the life of the company.  This usually includes stationery systems, business materials, web sites, sales materials and all of the printed and online vehicles which represent the company in the marketplace.

Unlike more temporary forms of communication - short-term efforts to meet short-term needs – Identity focuses on the more permanent media that makes a company's image strong, appropriate and appealing over the long-term.
