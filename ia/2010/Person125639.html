    Dreams for Kids mission is to empower young people of all abilities through dynamic leadership programs and life-changing activities that inspire them to fearlessly pursue their dreams and compassionately change the world.

    Dreams for Kids empowers youth who live in poverty or with a disability by reminding them they have something to give. It is in that giving that they each find the power that exists in them to transform their lives and the lives of others. The youth in our programs lead others to their dreams and in the process realize their own.

    Dreams Leaders Program: Bringing together young people of all abilities, from every race, religion, gender, and socio- economic background, to serve their less fortunate peers, create positive social change, celebrate our common humanity, and be the leaders we are seeking to transform the world.

    Projects and activities our leaders help create include: Holiday for Hope, the world's largest holiday celebration for homeless youth and Extreme Recess, the first of its kind adaptive sports and activities program for kids with disabilities.
