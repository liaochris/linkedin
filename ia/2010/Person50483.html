David Demangos’s Summary

I reside in San Diego, California. I am married to an unequivocally exceptional wife of 17 years, Gina. I am an Entrepreneur and have been self-employed since 1995. I am in the Top 3 Coaches/Trainers in my company. My desire is to connect with business leaders and referral sources to assist them in building their own Enterprises through Relationship and Referral Marketing.

My target markets are Entrepreneurs, Business Owner's, Independent Salespeople and Network Marketers. I focus on those that are growing a business and looking for ways to retain clients, give and receive referrals and build name recognition and credibility while standing out way above their competition.
