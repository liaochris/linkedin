Hanan Gelbendorf’s Summary

For over 15 years I've been busy generating great ideas and executing them. I've done that within R&D groups of global corporations, marketing and distribution units of corporate America, as a co-founder and executive in start-ups and in the past 3 years as the Managing Partner of 2creativ, a Digital Marketing Network, which I founded.

We work with Non Profit organizations, Small and Medium Businesses and even Global Corporations, to define and execute their Digital Marketing strategy.
Our focus is on providing tools, technologies and training for sustainable success.
We also invest in early stage start-ups, mainly through deployment in executive positions.

2creativ has a wide network of partnerships, which allows us to provide a wide range of services including social and digital media campaigns, web design, web development, creative services and art directing, branding, marketing communication, business development, strategy and business planning and deployment engagements in various capacities of marketing and business development.
