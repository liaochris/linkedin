Larry Fulton’s Summary

Larry E. Fulton is President and CEO of LEFCO Worthington, a joint venture company with Worthington Steelpac, a subsidiary of Worthington Industries (NYSE: WOR). LEFCO Worthington provides engineered and welded steel returnable racks, steel pallets, and other shipping platforms, including custom wooden crates and pallets. LEFCO has a diversified customer base that includes the U.S. government and several Fortune 500 companies in the defense, aerospace, transportation, utilities, and general industrial and manufacturing markets. Learn more at www.LEFCOWorthington.com.

Prior to starting LEFCO Worthington Mr. Fulton was general manager for a business unit within Alcoa, Inc. The business spanned North America and principally supported a Fortune 500 company in the Class 8 truck market. Mr. Fulton’s background also includes mergers & acquisitions, corporate finance and strategy experience with Key Bank, Marconi Communications and the U.S. Small Business Administration.

Mr. Fulton is a graduate of the School of Business & Industry at Florida A&M University, and earned an MBA in Management Policy and Entrepreneurship from the Weatherhead School of Management at Case Western Reserve University.

He is married to wife Arlishea, a corporate attorney, and has three children.
