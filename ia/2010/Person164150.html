Phil Yanov’s Summary

Phil Yanov is a Blogger, Podcaster, Technologist, and Speaker. He is founder and President of the GSA Technology Council, technology columnist for Greenville Business Magazine, and co-host of the Your Day technology shows heard on NPR stations across South Carolina. He is a frequent contributor to technology stories appearing on WYFF-TV and recently received an honorable mention among Greenville's fifty most influential people for his support of technology professionals and entrepreneurs with his Tech After Five networking events. He serves as President of the South Carolina IT Leadership Council and has, since its inception, co-chaired the South Carolina CIO and Executive IT Forum. He was emcee for Innoventure 2009, will be for Innoventure 2010, and was, most recently, the master of ceremonies for the first ever TedxGreenville event.
