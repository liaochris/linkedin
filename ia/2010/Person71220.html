Frank McDade’s Summary

Before joining Zeta Interactive’s SEO and Social Media team, Frank was the Marketing Coordinator for IMPAQT, a leading Search Marketing agency. He was instrumental in developing the company’s strategy in social media marketing, managed the corporate Web site and search campaigns, oversaw content development, coordinated e-mail and public relations initiatives, and co-coordinated Search Marketing Day. While in Pittsburgh, he was part of the founding committee for SEMPO Pittsburgh. Frank is a social media advocate outside of the workplace and has been a featured blogger for sites such as the PA Tourism Web site and BroadwayWorld.com, as well as photographer for HeyPittsburgh.com. Frank thrives on helping arts and entertainment organizations ease in to the online space and utilizes his background in event management and theater production in doing so. He holds a BS in Sport, Arts, and Entertainment Management from Point Park University.
