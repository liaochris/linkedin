    (Educational Institution; Research industry)

    March 2010 — Present (9 months)

    Designed and implemented MediaFlow Core Systems (MFCS)

    MediaFlow is an Automated End to End Lecture Capture and Publishing setup originally designed by Technology Architect Andy Wasklewicz for the Li Ka Shing Center for Learning and Knowledge inaugurated in September 2010.

    MFCS is an High Availability and High Performance Computing setup composed of several layers of interconnected technologies including Xsan,Xgrid, Podcast Producer,Tomcat, MySQL, Final Cut Server, Xserve, Fiber Channel, TCP/IP, Ethernet, Shell Scripts,etc.

    MFCS is also the hub and repository for several custom applications written as part of MediaFlow by Senior Software Engineer Carter Youngblood.

    MediaFlow is in the process of being "Open Sourced" which will include detailed MFCS Architecture documentation.
