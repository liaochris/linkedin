Mark Sheffer’s Summary

Early in my life, I knew I wanted a good education. I loved learning and helping others with what I learned. I received a Chick Evans Scholarship to Marquette University. One summer while at Marquette, I met the love of my life and somehow tricked her into a marriage that has lasted 27 years so far.

I was into PCs when people still asked what they were and warned me they would be just a fad like CB radios - remember those? I went from programming to selling computers and was one of the most successful sales account managers at one of the most successful PC companies CDW for 14 years. Thanks to my fellow CDW coworkers and I, we were so successful that I was able to take a few years off while I looked for my next big career.

Then while I was looking for a way to help my kids through a health issue I stumbled upon the answer - Crossinology Brain Integration Technique. The results for my family were so dramatic that I learned how to do it myself and now I have a business helping others get great results. I continue to be amazed at how we can solve more and more of our problems, pains, traumas and illnesses by using our new understanding of the brain and our personal energy fields. So here I am again at the brink of a revolution trying to convince people to jump in with me, just like my start in the PC world 25 years ago.

This time instead of getting people to use machines to help them in their lives, I now help them use their own brain and energy system to transform their lives in ways that amaze them. I get to help people be happier, healthier, smarter, more in tune with their own being, and more prepared for their futures.

I do this in my comfortable, peaceful home office where they lay comfortably on a massage table while I check and optimize their brain pathways. I give free consultations because it is so easy and amazing you have to see it to believe it.

My goal now is to help as many people as I can with this new work.
