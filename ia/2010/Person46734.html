Dane Storrusten’s Summary

Versatile, senior-level Designer and Creative Director with more than ten years experience and vibrant background in interactive/user experience design, branding, print collateral and packaging design, and illustration.

Currently a Creative Director and founder of MediaKatalyst, Inc., a boutique design studio and consultancy focused on user experience design, brand strategy, and killer print promotion and packaging.

Recently an accomplished, Microsoft user experience designer with several foundational user interface design and utility patents targeted at 'natural user interfaces'.

Stellar reputation for pushing creative boundaries, remarkable concept visualization skills, and world-class craftsmanship along with influence and leadership amongst both design and non-design disciplines.

Design approach includes a balanced mix of creative vision, business-centric principles, great communication, and unique processes and techniques.

Mastery of many design tools including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, After Effects, and excellent sketching and storyboarding skills. Vast technical background in web and software applications including Flash Actionscript, XHTML, CSS, WPF, XAML, Microsoft Surface SDK.
