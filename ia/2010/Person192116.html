Cameron Sowder’s Summary

I have been in the telecom expense management industry my entire career, since before it was officially called TEM. For many years at AT&T I saved the world's largest business customers hundreds of millions of dollars in telecom expenses.

My wife and I then co-founded Sowder Communications, and under this umbrella I am doing two things. Working with companies on helping them choose and/or implement a new TEM provider, as well as helping TEM vendors grow their businesses and project manage customers.

Currently I am sourcing and managing the largest TEM project ever undertaken to date, with a Fortune 20 financial services company. I was recently called the top independent TEM manager/consultant in the country by a respected source.

I am in the process of developing the world's largest TEM network at TelecomExpenseManagement.com, along with several industry experts. I am also writing a book on global TEM that will be available in 2010 online and in bookstores.

You may read my blogs, as well as excerpts/updates from our upcoming TEM book at TelecomExpenseManagement.com. The matching group here on LinkedIn is called simply TEM. Feel free to join, post, and learn more about TEM on either site.

I have had equal years on both the financial/reporting/auditing side, as well as the telecom/technical/IT half of TEM. This very unique experience combined with my education in IT Project Management, make me an authority in all areas of telecommunications expense management.
