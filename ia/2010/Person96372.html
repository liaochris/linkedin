    3.1 Music (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Entertainment industry)

    January 1989 — Present (21 years 7 months)

    Visit http://www.3pt1.com and listen to FULL length 3.1 songs... . .

    3.1 is HIGH-ENERGY INSPIRED electronic music. Checkout 3.1's NEW song 'Sound Of The OverGround'. All music, vocals and production by Jerome Mark Mikulich.

    Mikulich, a graduate of Columbia College in Chicago, IL where he received a B.A. in the Arts, Entertainment and Media Management Program (AEMMP) with a focus on Sound Engineering in the recording industry.

    While in college Mikulich was educated and trained by some of the best in the music industry including; the late Marty Fledman, who was the president of NARAS (Chicago) and the owner of the Paragon Recording Studio. Feldman was best known for working with artists like Styx, Tina Turner and Earth, Wind and Fire. Mikulich's final exam in Feldman's Digital Audio Production class included; remixing the original master of the Eagles famous hit 'Hotel California'. Mikulich said, "...that's an experience I'll never forget.'

    From 1989-99, Mikulich was the Chief Recording Engineer & Producer at his famous 'Playroom Recording Studio' located on Chicago's 'Magnificent Mile' (520 N. Michigan).

    During his career Mikulich worked with Grammy and Platinum award winning artists on various major & independent labels. Mikulich received a platinum record for engineering "La La La Hey Hey' by the Outhere Brothers. Mikulich also recorded the Outhere Brothers 'Boom Boom Boom' track featured on the multi-platinum 'ESPN Presents: Jock Jams, Volume 2'.

    Mikulich has written and recorded songs several artists and received a record review by Larry Flick in Billboard Magazine for song one entitled 'It Feels Right' featuring R&B female vocalist 'Nature Love'. He wrote the song in honor of his mother 'June'.

    3.1's debut album is entitled '31 Degrees (and two miles of fog)'. Featuring (10) original songs. 3.1's sophomore release is entitled 'Spiritual Forecast' and contains (14) songs written in a NEW music style called 'Message Progressive'.
