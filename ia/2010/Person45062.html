Cristian Sturek’s Summary

CTO for Condo.com. Founder and Principal SOA Architect of XWebServices, Inc., a boutique Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) Services firm. Founder, Managing Director & Chief Architect at Third Screen Labs, a mobile strategy and solutions provider. Have over 12 years Enterprise Architecture (EA) experience primarily in the Financial Services, Health Care and eCommerce fields.

Developed SOA principles and best practices that guide planning, development, integration and management of application infrastructures. Successfully architected AND implemented services-based architectures for companies such as First Data Corporation, SCAN Health Plan, First American Corporation and Wright Express.

Also held technical leadership positions at AFX, a medical billing solutions provider; Artful Style, an online art gallery; Realtor.com, the largest provider of real estate listings in the United States; IXL Inc. and Genesis 2000. Honorably discharged in 1998 after 4 years of Active Duty in the United States Marine Corps.
