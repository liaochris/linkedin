Patrick Kraska, CPA, CMA’s Summary

I am an accomplished Finance and Accounting leader with a diversified background and an exceptional ability to set strategy, launch initiative, mitigate risk, minimize costs and achieve a positive impact on operations.

As a leader, I am ethical, convey a strong vision of the future, and win trust and loyalty, while ensuring the organizations continued vitality. I pride myself in my ability to develop talent, build teams, improve cooperation inside a cross-functional team and improve cross-functional teamwork within an organization.

I have successfully managed the full cycle accounting and Financial Planning & Analysis functions. In addition, I have lead technology initiatives including systems analysis, designs, development and implementations while guiding IT to continuous alignment with business goals.

I have partnered with senior management in stabilizing the transition resulting from the acquisition and restructuring of numerous businesses. In addition, I have successfully selected and implemented numerous software packages such as General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Fixed Assets and Cash management.

My background in the public and private sectors enriches my leadership capability by broadening my perspective that benefits problem solving, technology initiatives, cost reduction, internal controls, and cash flow management.
