Daniel Michael’s Summary

I have been photographing weddings in Cincinnati for over 10 years now. Outside of my little guy, seeing the growth of our studio and the people in it has been the most rewarding experience of my life. I have met the most amazing people and had a great time doing it. My background is in the fine arts and has been adapted to the speed and requirements of wedding photography. We pride ourselves on the emotional nature of our photography. If we do only one thing with our photography, it should be to remind our couples in 20 years how they felt on their wedding day.

Daniel Michael’s Specialties:

Our specialty is creating an environment where our couples can be themselves. We don't want to create a look for them, we want to find the looks they already have.
