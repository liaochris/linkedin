Christopher Watters’s Summary

Since beginning my career as a real estate practitioner serving Austin Texas, I've been on track to achieve extraordinary goals in record-breaking time. I've been consistently a multi-million dollar producing agent and was ranked the #1 agent at Texas Ranch & Home Realty from June 07' to June 2010.

In June of 2010 - "Watters International Realty" was born. A team of agents driven to give elite service to its clientele. We provide our clients cutting edge technology, personal attention, and believe in ALWAYS going the extra mile.

I'm a 2006 graduate of Texas State University, in San Marcos, TX, where I studied Business Administration in Finance. Shortly after graduating I became a Realtor(R) and completed mentor-ships with two of the top producing agents in Austin Texas. In January of 2010 I took my education and professional status to the next level, acquiring a Texas Real Estate Brokers License.

I've been working hard to claim my mark in the real estate world, A personal goal of mine is to make the 2011 issue for the Top 30 Agents Under 30, a national publication by REALTOR Magazine. I was most recently quoted in REALTOR Magazine in the April 2010 issue, where I explained ways to help fellow Realtors sell their clients condominiums.

I'm an active member of the National Association of Realtors; Texas Association of Realtors; Austin Board of Realtors; ActiveRain Real Estate Network, and support a variety of other community causes.

Christopher Watters’s Specialties:

Representing clients in the purchase and sale of their home or condo in Austin Texas and the surrounding suburbs.

I service the greater Austin Texas area. I enjoy helping clients with luxury estates and condos in Austin Texas.

Ask me about "New Age Marketing" of your home in Austin Texas!
