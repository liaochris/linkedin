Ed Elliott (eelliott.dm@verizon.net)’s Summary

For nearly three decades Ed Elliott has been providing strategic creative leadership and training for teams of direct marketing designers, copywriters, marketing directors, and creative directors.

He is co-inventor of the “magalog” direct mail format, an innovation that tripled the customer universe for an entire product category and boosted acquisition response rates and revenues throughout the direct marketing community.

GARY BENCIVENGA'S ENDORSEMENT

"If I were looking to hire perhaps the best creative director in direct marketing ... someone who has supervised hundreds if not thousands of split-run tests and knows, as few others do, what works and what doesn't ... someone who knows how to bring in the cash by the mail-bag-full because he's specialized in cash-with-order offers ... someone whom people love to work with and for, because he always brings out their best (as he invariably did with me) ... someone who thinks constantly about his work, is never satisfied, and probes everyone from copywriters to clients to production staff on how to improve results ... someone as honest as the day is long ... someone who worked his way up from freelancer to become a universally respected creative director at two of America's largest and most successful direct marketers ... well, if I were looking for someone like that, there is actually only one person I would call first. His name is Ed Elliott, and I'd hope to high heaven that he'd be available."
— Gary Bencivenga, direct marketing copywriter (now retired)
