Deborah Baxley’s Summary

Deborah Baxley is an international retail payments consultant, recognized expert in the industry, creator of growth strategies for new and existing markets and 30-year IBM veteran with more than 15 years experience consulting to cards and payment companies. In-depth experience in mobile payments, credit cards and retail banking
• Former leader of IBM’s 400-person global credit card practice of services, sales, development and research professionals from 15 countries who collaborated on issues and insights for the credit card industry
• Performed strategy work in 14 countries for 8 top global issuers and payment brands, 2 top payment processors, and with 3 top bankcard acquirers (Australia, Canada, China, Greece, Hong Kong, Israel, Mexico, United Kingdom, Brazil, Denmark, India, Taiwan, South Korea & United States
• Advised companies on product direction and competitive positioning, delivering in up to $20M in new revenue or $50M in operating cost savings, including MasterCard, Bank of America, Visa, and American Express
Widely recognized for insights on the future of retail payments
• Current Officer and former Chair, Smart Card Alliance Payments Council, co-chaired 2009 Alliance Payments Council Summit
• Defined IBM's solutions for payments and credit cards and credit card strategy for China
• Member, Smart Card Alliance Leadership, Education and Advancement Program, and Certified Smart Card Industry Professional
• Frequent keynote speaker and prolific author on topics of mobile and advanced payments innovation
• Author of 15 published articles and white papers, most recently “Proximity Mobile Payments Business Scenarios: Research Report on Stakeholder Perspectives,” Smart Card Alliance Contactless Payments Council white paper
• Spoke at 13 US and international industry events in the past 5 years, most recently CardTech SecureTech 2009, on topics of payment security and product innovations for payment processors
