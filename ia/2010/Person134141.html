Mark Lobo’s Summary

I have a Masters Degree in Physics and have worked a total of seventeen years in RF and Microwave Semiconductor technology; working with various types of Si and GaAs devices. The list of product types that I worked on includes SoC Bluetooth devices, CDMA, GSM, PCN and other cellular products. I have three years “hands-on” experience on the Teradyne Catalyst and Credence ASL 3000RF ATE platforms.

Mark Lobo’s Specialties:

I have developed Automatic Test Equipment sets and written ATE code for the RF and DC testing of both packaged transistors and die at the wafer level. I have extensive experience in the RF and DC testing of products at the wafer level, both at the product development stage and for production test. I have developed and deployed a number of high volume RF package test systems.
