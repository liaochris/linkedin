Jay O'Connor’s Summary

An expert communicator, passionate about excellence and delivery of thoughtful communication campaigns that have a positive, tangible impact on reputation and organisational objectives.

Excellent understanding of business strategy and corporate governance issues: UK's youngest Chartered Board Director (2004); former member of the Institute of Directors Professional Standards Board.

Advocate of PR profession best practice and governance, with a particular focus on measurement. Recently led the development of Chartered Practitioner, the CIPR's 'gold standard' designation for senior practitioners; former Consultant Professor of PR at Syracuse University.

Founder and Managing Partner of Fuse PR. Sold to Racepoint Group in 2006. Following acquisition became Managing Director of Racepoint Group Europe. Both consultancies are multi-award winning, including Communicators in Business Consultancy of the Year two years running.

Currently President of the Chartered Institute of Public Relations (CIPR) and a postgraduate student at the London School of Economics & Political Science. I am a CIPR Fellow, Chartered Practitioner and Chair of the Executive Board and Council. Former Chair of the CIPR's Education and Professional Standards Committee; Freeman of the Guild of Public Relations Practitioners and Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts.

Have won and been shortlisted for over 20 individual and consultancy awards in 8 years, including CIPR Young Communicator of the Year Winner; numerous Best Consultancy and Best Campaign awards and Media Employer of the Year Finalist (three years running).

Jay O'Connor’s Specialties:

Expertise in corporate communications, crisis management, corporate governance issues, message development / positioning and professional development structures. Sector expertise in technology, education and health.
