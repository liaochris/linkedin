Lisa DiVirgilio’s Summary

I am currently the in-house social media expert at syracuse.com. I am in charge of developing and implementing social media strategies to increase user engagement.

I also create, coordinate and maintain syracuse.com's "News" and "CNY Life" pages with the Movable Type content management system and Adobe DreamWeaver CS3. I edit still images for different web packages with Adobe Photoshop, assist newspaper staff in online design and content, track statistics on site content using Omniture and Google trends, and blog entries that feed into the site about trending topics to increase user interest and engagement throughout site.

Syracuse.com is owned by Advance Internet, a media leader in advanced community engagement within local news websites. The site, syracuse.com, is home to The Post-Standard on the web.

On the side, I do Web design and social media consultation for various clients including small businesses, television news reporters and on-air personalities.

Previously, I was an online content producer and Web journalist for WTNH - TV ABC News Channel 8, a top 30 market located in New Haven, Conn., owned by Lin-TV Broadcasting.

I have a master's degree in journalism and a bachelor's degree in English, general rhetoric and literature.
