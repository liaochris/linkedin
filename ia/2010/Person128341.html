Paul Wilson’s Summary

Every business is unique. So it’s not difficult to understand why many businesses aren’t satisfied with standardized, off-the-shelf solutions. At INTRUST, our business banking professionals are dedicated to the needs of our clients, putting the resources and financial strength of a 130 year-old institution with $3.5 billion in assets behind each one. We get to know our customers, and by discovering what makes their business different, we can help them achieve their maximum potential.
INTRUST’s experienced commercial bankers understand how businesses work and use that knowledge to structure financial solutions tailored to each customer’s unique situation. We’ve worked with many successful businesses, entrepreneurs, franchisees and medical professionals, from the smallest entrepreneurial business to the largest, most established corporate client, sharing their vision and providing the financial guidance and resources they needed to thrive.

Paul Wilson’s Specialties:

INTRUST offers virtually every financial resource a business needs to succeed and makes every effort to bring the most advanced technology available to our customers. INTRUST has also been recognized by the Small Business Administration (SBA) as one of the organization’s most active and expert lenders, and has been granted Preferred Lending Partner (PLP) status, enabling us to approve 7(a) loans without consulting the federal agency.
