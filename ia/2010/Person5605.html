Aldo Novak’s Summary

Aldo Novak is a well kown Brazilian speaker and author. His first book, O Segredo Para Realizar Seus Sonhos” (Secrets For Making Your Dreams Come True) is a best seller in Brazil.He has just published his second book, A Vida Não Tem Segredos" (Life Has No Secret). He written and presented a documentary called "Você Atrai o que Transmite" (You Attract What You Transmit), sold on DVD for the Portuguese countries.

His main focus is in the planning, organization and management of business networking conferences, personal development courses, business conventions and Human Resources activities. His passion: to help people to overcome their highest dreams, working with what they have, or don`t have, here. And now.

He helps people to design and drive their lifes.

Novak has been speaking professionally since 1992 and has written over 300 articles. He is now in the process of writing his third book.

Novak's weekly newsletter, Sucesso Gera Sucesso (Success Makes Success), has subscribers in 75 countries and a readership of over one million people (numbers from July 2008), making it the world's most widely read Portuguese language newsletter on success, personal and professional mission and leadership.

He is well known for his conferences and training programs in several different areas of expertise, and has been traveling to many cities in Brazil and abroad.

Aldo Novak is an expert on Mind Maps and Mind Cartography, Coaching for Life, Law of Attraction (from a solid behavior standpoint), Newspaper, Newsletter and Magazine Creation, Business & Personal Mission Statements, Communication Skills, Personal Identity Branding, Inner Peace, Close Relationship Program, How to Find Your Life's Purpose, Media Training, Motivation, Public Speaking & Successful Presentations and Relationship Success & Communication for Couples .

He is also a consulting expert for business implementation.

Elected life member of Academia Guarulhense de Letras, in 2008.

Aldo Novak’s Experience

  • Director, Speaker and Coach

    Academia Novak do Brasil

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    November 1992 — Present (17 years 6 months)

    The company has started as "Novak Communication Center", in 1992, with its main focus on ESL for Brazilians, plus courses for professionals and business owners.

    Some years later, the English Division (by that time, part of Skill Aliança Inglesa franchise) was sold to Cultura Americana English Courses. Nowadays it has three areas of expertise: Training & Coaching, Online Media Industry and PR Consulting.

    Visit Academia Novak do Brasil at www.academianovak.com.br (Brazilian Portuguese)

  • Owner / Editor-in-chief

    Relatório Alfa Newspaper

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    February 1988 — November 2006 (18 years 10 months)

    This online newspaper has subscribers in 70 nations and was founded in 1988 with the name "Aeroespaço Urgente" -- covering only space activities. At that time, it was printed and sent by the mail. It had a very small circulation, during those years.

    Closed in 1993, it came to life again in 1998, but it changed format and began covering many different subjects, including environmental dangers, biotech/nuclear risks, national and world politics, human rights, Nafta, Consumer rights... and similar themes with a "left-liberal" taste. It has become a standard for Brazilian independent online media to follow.

    Currently, it has more than 130.000 double opt-in subscribers with a circulation number as high as 400.000 readers per edition. The website is located at http://www.relatorioalfa.com.br (Brazilian Portuguese language).

  • Guest Professor on Media History and Press Strategies

    UATI - Open University for Senior Citizens Programme - Universidade Federal de S. Paulo

    (Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Higher Education industry)

    2000 — 2004 (4 years )

    This is a pro bono job, where I teach senior citizens about media creation and its history. We cover the basics of press production and about the real history of the radio.

    Topics discussed include manipulation (and how to detect it), who do what in a press room and how to understand the dynamics of television, newspapers, radio and internet.

  • Owner, Producer & Voiceover

    Soho AudioLines

    (Non-Profit; Myself Only; Broadcast Media industry)

    April 1996 — December 1999 (3 years 9 months)

    Using Viatel technology (1995-1997) and Vimeltary/Telesp Technology (1998-1999), Novak created a new product in Brazil: free telephone news and motivational messages.

    He used his knowledge in both fields, Science Fiction and Journalism, besides his work in the motivational area, to produce daily 3 minutes messages that anyone could listen, from any telephone.

    It began in 1995 and, since that time, Aldo wrote and recorded over 6.800 messages (in 3 different lines, called Sucesso, Trekline and Excerline, later renamed Alfaline) that were listened more than... 5,6 million times in almost 4 years.

    What started as a hobby, grew too much and became too expensive. Because it was 100% free, Novak had to cancel when more than 50.000 people were calling daily, to hear his messages. No company showed interest in buying 15" spots, to keep the service free, at that time.

  • TV and Cinema Convention Producer & Director

    Novakorp Eventos

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry)

    1982 — 1999 (17 years )

    For many years I have planned, organized and worked in the production of hundreds of conventions for fans of cinema and television, including many sci-fi shows, in Brazil.

    Those conventions were mostly dedicated to Star Trek and X-Files fans, as well as the "Jedi Knights" of Star Wars. The events included international guests from US and Europe, performing actors and directors from the series, like George Takei.

    Many conventions were done for the brazilian fandom representatives, like Frota Estelar and Clube Arquivo X Brasil, with partnership with Grupo Teatral Zona Neutra, Ticketeatro and Sinope. Part of the conventions had trade fairs organized by Holodeck Feiras Comerciais e de Negócios.

    Up to now, no other Sci-Fi convention promoter has done more conventions, or bigger ones, in Brazil.
