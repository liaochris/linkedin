    Digital Strategy Consulting

    (Internet industry)

    October 2009 — March 2010 (6 months)

    With 15 years of digital media sales and business development experience, Juliana Jaoudi founded her own consulting firm. As she was dividing her time between both continents, her main projects included consulting for Daily Mail Group Trust, plc in London and iLarious, Inc. in Los Angeles, advising both companies on digital sales strategy. Her deep knowledge of the US market combined with meaningful professional sojourns in Europe give Jaoudi a unique perspective for companies looking to launch or grow their interactive businesses in either/both regions.

Jaoudi built the digital sales team by hiring the right talent; identifying growth areas; and building out operational and support teams. She helped create and execute the company’s content and revenue strategies, focusing on the Entertainment category; played a critical role in the Moviefone, Spinner and Winamp acquisitions; was a founding member of AOL Europe’s Business Development team in London and Paris; and drove significant revenue as a Media Sales Executive. Prior to joining AOL, Jaoudi worked for the U.S. Foreign Commercial Service and KPMG in Paris, France.
