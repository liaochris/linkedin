Alejandro Rivera (arivera@quali-find.com)’s Summary

At Qualifind I focus my services to connect professionals with job opportunities in the Medical devices, Automotive, Aerospace and Electronics industries.

Prior to joining Qualifind, I was the Director of the Tijuana Economic Development Corporation (DEITAC), where I traveled throughout Asia, Europe, U.S., Canada and Mexico to solicit and successfully secure foreign direct investment in Tijuana’s business and industrial sectors. I consulted with and counseled companies in the medical device, automotive, aerospace and electronics industries on the benefits of establishing start-up operations in Mexico.

Prior to DEITAC, I was involved in sales and business development with suppliers to the manufacturing industry.

I am bilingual in both English and Spanish and hold a Bachelors degree in International Business from CETYS University in Tijuana.
