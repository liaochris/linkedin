Neal Nodvin’s Summary

Results driven hands-on manager with 30 years experience in corporate, start-up and growth-oriented companies. Proven track record of creating, managing and operating high-growth mutli-industry business platforms.

Since 1994 to present, as CEO, President, COO, EVP and/or CFO, founded and/or built companies the following companies:
• With cumulative annual revenue in excess of $1.0 billion &
• Corporate equity value in excess of $1.4 billion

These companies have included:
1. Portfolio Technolgy Group (www.portfolioUS.com)
2. ProfitLab (sold to Cass Information Systems - www.cassinfo.com)
3. NewSouth Communications (now Nuvox Communications - www.nuvox.com)
4. Atlas Communications LTD
5. RFC Capital (now Textron Financial Corp - www.textronfinancial.com)

Mr. Nodvin has achieved his success by:
• Leveraging strong finance, marketing, operations and sales skills and capitalizing on talents for:
• Taking complex businesses, divisions and/or processes → distilling into efficient manageable tasks, then → automating and embedding these streamlined business practices
• Cultivating unproven ideas into successful businesses
• Creating work environments where team members are empowered to enjoy the pursuit of excellence while taking ownership for the success of the organization
• Leveraging technology to enhance efficiency and growth in all aspects of the organization and
• Using his hands-on successful track record in virtually all major business functional areas (i.e. Business & Corporate Development/Strategy, Branding & Packaging, Finance & Accounting, Information Technology, Sales & Marketing, Operations & Product Development/Management) to provide value to his teammates, investors and customers.
