Clifford Harris’s Summary

In his first year in Commercial Real Estate, Cliff Harris was named “Achiever of the Year” by management at Prudential Verani Realty. His energetic approach and tireless work ethic continue to earn accolades from clients and his employer. In 2008, Cliff’s award winning work style earned him a place in the very prestigious “Verani Top 12” of all agents, commercial and residential combined.
Cliff’s personal, hands on approach continues to attract clients from large corporations and small business. Cliff attributes his early success to his mentor, Ron Boufford, owner of Global Commercial who served the Bedford, NH market for over 25 years. Lessons from Ron mixed with Cliff’s energy and sales experience make for a very powerful combination for his clients.
Prior to Cliff’s success in real estate, he owned and operated ALL CELL in Windham, NH., building that business to over 5000 business accounts and before that, Cliff enjoyed a 20 year career in corporate America working in the field of Management and Finance for IBM, Nextel, PageNet and Wang Laboratories
