Isaac Kight’s Summary

I have worked in multiple sectors focusing on management and team leadership on many levels, with emphasis in marketing, public relations, and social networking. I have supervised a small sales team and managed a sales office branch. During my first year as a branch manager sales increased 15% over the previous year. The international marketing campaign I launched brought a significant increase in revenue for the firm.

I founded an all-volunteer non-profit and served as its executive, during which time I also offered my services as an education consultant to school districts and parents. During that time, I also created a new department for a school that serves children with autism and learning disabilities, and educated the staff on how to increase the use of technology in the classroom. Employee turnover decreased as a result of my efforts and the number of students more than tripled over the course of my contract.

While earning my MBA this past year at one of Israel’s most prestigious universities, I have been presented with the unique opportunity to apply my skills to the public sector in an international setting, assisting a member of the Israeli parliament with his social networking and fundraising in the United States. Connections increased by 22% and funds raised increased by 13% despite the fact that elections are three years away.

I am proficient in multiple versions of Microsoft Office Professional Suite and have excellent verbal and written communication skills. I am well versed in theories of team building and ideation and have applied them successfully throughout my career. I am a self-starter, eager to learn new skills, and take direction well. My experience includes a wide range of cultural settings and international negotiations. I am comfortable speaking before an audience and have conducted seminars and training sessions.
