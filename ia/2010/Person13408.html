Anh Thu Vo’s Summary

Twenty years of experience in the software development and consulting business.

Distributed systems (SOA/Web Services, Orchestration, EJB, CORBA), business processes (BPM), business rules (Blaze, Corticon), design patterns, and software development methodology.

Professional services experience in pre-/post-sales implementation, training, support, account and practice management.

Co-founded Locera, a global, wireless, lightweight messaging system for the machine-to-machine (M2M) telematics market.

Co-founded avolab, a wireless medical monitoring device company, in 2003-2004. Products are in pilot stages at Southern California HMOs and outpatient clinics.

Co-founded Talinda, an e-commerce startup in 1999. Raised funding, marketed to customers, built and deployed e-commerce systems for Netscape, Synopsys, Oracle, and PeopleSoft software and documentation stores. Earned first year revenue of $1 million with a team of 4.
