Shari Fitness’s Summary

ShariFitness is an ISSA Fitness Professional, Fitness Nutritionist, Natural Health Advocate, blogger about Natural Health and Fitness, Host of FitTalk on Blog Talk Radio and Social Media Consultant specializing in the health, fitness and supplement industries.

ShariFitness is a real life transformation success story that has been featured in several fitness magazines. She was frustrated, frumpy and a perpetual “before” picture. No longer a perpetual “before” picture, Shari discovered her passion for health and fitness and set out on a mission to help others become more fit and healthy. Shari walks the walk and talks the talk. Her positive attitude has motivated and inspired countless others who were frustrated with their efforts to lose weight and help coach them towards better health and fitness.

Hire Shari to work with you in person or on line to get you on your way to a better body, improved health and a new YOU!

Shari is currently writing her first book about her transformation success secrets and how she not only transformed her body, but her life.

Shari Fitness is your complete source for optimal health and fitness by providing you the three major components: exercise, nutritional and supplement coaching to help you achieve your overall health and fitness goals along with success coaching to help overcome food cravings and improve self-confidence.

Shari is highly respected by many in the industry on a national level, and regularly consults with fitness professionals across the United States.
