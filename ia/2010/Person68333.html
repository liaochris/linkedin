  • Co-founder

    Cool Gel 'n Cap

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Have you ever tried holding an ice pack to a child's head? It doesn't work so well... does it. Well, my husband and I invented the CoolGel'nCap to help alleviate that problem.

    Our CoolGel'nCap is a unique and cute hat that allows a cold/hot gel pack to be placed on a child's head and secures it so that parents have free hands to comfort their toddlers when they get a head bump instead of wrestling with them.

    Our first character is "Toby" a puppy dog and he comes with two gel packs that can be cooled for icing a bump or heated to soothe earaches. Toby also comes with an instant ice pack for on-the-go pain relief.

    We have a few more characters in the works that will be available for purchase as well.

    To learn more please visit our website: www.coolgelncap.net
