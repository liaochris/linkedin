Cyn Reneau Oss’s Summary

Thanks for your interest in my profile!

I specialize in voice/data telecommunications – an industry in which I have two decades of experience.
I work with most carriers throughout the USA and Canada for voice/internet/data services, and I can provide data/internet services globally. I also can provide proposals for NEC hardware.

I am able to assess your needs and truly find the best deals available. Even if you’re satisfied with your existing dialtone and data/internet configuration and/or you’re under a contract, I can review your invoices to ensure you’re receiving the best possible rates from your carrier(s). Best of all, there is no charge for this audit!

In the future, if my clients have a concern or a change, they have the peace of mind of knowing they can contact a live person (me)…not an automated call center somewhere. Yes, I am a salesperson...No, your rates aren't higher if you utilize my services (they're tariffed).

Have questions about SIP trunking, MEIP or VoIP? Ready to update your telephone hardware? I can address those needs too! From fax lines to connectivity for telecommuters, I can support your telecom and I.T. needs. My expertise allows me to meet the requirements of large corporations and 'mom and pop shops' with the same level of service and dedication!

Thanks in advance for your consideration. I hope to earn your trust and save you money! I look forward to hearing from you!
