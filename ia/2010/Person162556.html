Peter Arceo’s Summary

I have over 18 years of experience in the hospitality and gaming industry. I am skilled in casino operations and marketing, database marketing and analysis, e-commerce & new media, and information technology. In April 2010 I launched a new company, Strategic Gaming Advisors, with a colleague. Together we have over 40 years in the Hospitality and Gaming Industries.

I worked with the Las Vegas Hilton in Las Vegas, Nevada from 2004 - 2010. My first role with the company was implementing their casino systems and technology. In 2005, I played a large role in the installation and implementation of gaming systems in a newly acquired property in the Indiana gaming market. I was primarily responsible for the development of strategies and tactics for Casino Marketing related events and programs, Database Marketing, E-Commerce, and New Media which contribute over $160 million annually.

Prior to joining the Las Vegas Hilton in 2004, I spent 9 years working for Boyd Gaming Corporation (BYD) in various capacities from Casino Operations to Corporate Marketing Manager. During his stint at Boyd Gaming, I assisted with the opening of new and acquired casinos in the Louisiana and Atlantic City markets. I was also responsible for providing support to the company’s 17+ casinos in their marketing and analytical efforts.

I received a BS from the William F. Harrah College of Hotel Administration at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas in 1996. I am currently enrolled in the MBA program at the University of Southern California with a graduation date of 2011.
