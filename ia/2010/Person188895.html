Shervin Bain’s Summary

I am a recent college graduate who has already begun to establish an impressive resume with internships at AJR & Partners, TrialGraphix/Kroll Ontrack, and Johnson & Wales University.

In these roles, my managers have noticed my strong writing skills, work ethic, editing ability, professionalism, creativity and understanding of public relations strategy.

As a student, I have also worked hard to make my presence felt outside of the classroom by participating in student organizations such as Public Relations Student Society of America; The Beacon (campus newspaper); and the Student Organizations Council.

As part of my public relations curriculum, I recently worked with a team and launched a mock campaign for the well-known brand Victoria Secret's Pink. The full-service campaign included extensive research, ad copy writing and development, media buying, promotions and PR Stratagies.

I've gotten some good experience in Seach Engine Optimization (SEO) from Optimum 7, but decided to return to my passion for public relations at my current employer LEFT Communication where I work with one of their PR departments to handle the PR for Verizon Wireless in Florida.
