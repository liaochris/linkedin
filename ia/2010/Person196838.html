Lisa Goodwin’s Summary

We utilize a blended learning approach that takes one-on-one evaluations and integrates that learning process with team development and overall goals. By identifying both individual and team goals and objectives, we leverage human capitol while developing success strategies that improve performance, increase business goals and further leverage the company vision.
