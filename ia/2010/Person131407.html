Marc Hustvedt’s Summary

Our mission at Tubefilter is simple – to grow the audience of web television. Our news division, Tubefilter News, is the leading authority in episodic web television and web series.

The web is full of great storytelling. As you’ve probably noticed, there are a lot of stories out there, but too often they get lost in the web video shuffle. Tubefilter News helps you discover and keep track of the stories you care about.

We care about quality shows, whether they are studio or independently produced – if they’re good, we’ll help you find them. We follow the shows, their creators, the cast and the people that make these shows come to life. Make sure to follow Tubefilter on Twitter, and get into the community conversation.

We are based in Los Angeles, CA, and created both the Streamy Awards, and the International Academy of Web Television. We host a monthly gathering of web television creators and industry folks called the Hollywood Web Television Meetup, which also takes place in cities around the world. For more information on the Meetup, and to learn about our other events, follow us on Twitter or join our Facebook Group. Want the inside scoop? Read our company blog, or join the team.
