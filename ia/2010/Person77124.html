Graham Anderson’s Summary

I am a venture capitalist who concentrates on business to business software, convergence of information technology and life sciences, and specialty pharmaceutical opportunities. I have served on the boards of directors of several private companies. I am currently Chairman of the Venture Investors Association of New York (VIANY), and have authored the chapter entitled “On Making Investments and Working with Venture Capitalists” in The Ways of the Venture Capitalist (Aspatore Books 2003). I am an active participant in software and information industry organizations with an emphasis on the convergence of information technologies and life sciences. I am presently seeking PIPE opportunities in several late-stage, life sciences companies.

Graham Anderson’s Specialties:

Structuring venture capital deals; Corporate board committee experience -- Audit, Compensation, Corporate Governance and Nominating; Business and corporate development; Investment banking liaison; CFA Charterholder
