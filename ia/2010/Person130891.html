Manolo Almagro’s Summary

Manolo Almagro has over 18+ years of experience creating place based, digital experiences for international brands and their agencies. He specializes in building unified customer experiences across the multiple digital media channels.

Manolo holds advisory position in a variety of companies in the US and Asia Pacific region, providing strategic direction on digital + interactive media. His cross-disciplinary expertise has helped him to cultivate innovative solutions for digital media ecosystems, mobile commerce platforms and helping technology companies establish go to market strategies for international expansion into both emerging and mature markets.

In addition to his advisory roles, Manolo is a speaker at trade conferences, and a Senior International Correspondent for the digital out of home industry’s most reputable blogs- The DailyDooh.com-where he provides his personal analysis and insights to an international membership.

Previous technical accomplishments include Manolo being a named inventor on a digital signage software patent (US Patent #6038545), his work engineering McDonald’s largest digital menuboard for the London’s Millennium Dome and his pioneering efforts in creation of The Paris Metro (Subway) System’s first digital signage network.

Manolo has worked with various brands and market segments in the US, EU and Pacific rim, highlights of his work include projects with companies such as; ABC Television Networks, Disney, McDonald’s, M&M’s AT+T, United Airlines, ASDA/Walmart, France Telecom, Orange Telecom, Samsung, Jollibee Foods, SingTel, Globe Telecom, KFC New Zealand, Hungry Jacks/Burger King Australia.

Manolo Almagro’s Specialties:

Asia Pacific Region, Business Development Emerging Markets, Digital Interactive Media, Mobile Marketing, Mobile Payment systems, Digital Brand Development, Digital Out Of Home.
