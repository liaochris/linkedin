REAL ESTATE CHANNEL’s Summary

REAL ESTATE CHANNEL™ (www.RealEstateChannel.com) is in the real estate news and property information business - worldwide.

REAL ESTATE CHANNEL™ is an Internet News Network that distributes timely and relevant real estate news stories, market reports, industry expert opinions and property information to local, national and global real estate audiences.

Our audience consists of real estate consumers ("B2C" audiences); along with real estate industry leaders ("B2B" audiences) that include real estate investors, brokers, builders, developers, banks, industry associations, Wall Street, Government officials and other news media - worldwide.

Our news coverage includes all major categories of real estate (Residential, Commercial & Vacation-Leisure) from all across the U.S. and globally. We currently have reporters, columnists and guest news contributors based in New York, Los Angeles, Orlando, Miami, Austin, Westport, London, Abu Dhabi and Dubai, with more coming soon.

As company Founder Michael Gerrity continually states: "Our goal is to be the dominant provider of relevant and actionable real estate news and market data in order to help our audience make more informed real estate purchase, investment or rental decisions."

REAL ESTATE CHANNEL™ is headquartered in NBC Universal Studios (Orlando, Fl).
