Rodney Payne’s Summary

I was born in England and grew up in Australia. My business interest was fostered young after receiving an award by the Governor of South Australia for outstanding achievement in business studies in my final year of High School.

I was accepted into Adelaide University Business School and graduated with a degree in Commerce (Management) in 2002. In 2005 I graduated from Adelaide University School of Law having been awarded automatic Honors and spent my final year of study on a South Australian Government scholarship to the University of Oregon in Eugene. At the University of Oregon I undertook postgraduate studies in Environmental and International Law and completed a thesis transporting the US doctrine of Public Trust into Australian law for use in environmental protection.

While studying I co-founded and successfully exited a home-services business in Adelaide, Australia.

After graduation, I coordinated a partnership to collaborate with various Australian Companies and Tourism Organizations to promote Australian interests on the West Coast of the USA following the 2005 free trade agreement. This project, named the Platypus Express, involved fundraising, sponsor negotiations and planning and executing all elements of a large-scale road show from pitching an original concept to reporting on success.

Since inception in August 2007, I was involved in a young financial management company; ClearRock Capital. In this role I was responsible for all aspects of investor relations, trading through Charles Schwab and asset allocation.

I am now immersed in my latest and most exciting projects; myCal.com - a free online scheduling solution for small businesses - and getlifepoints.com - a Facebook application designed to inspire people to get off the couch!

Rodney Payne’s Specialties:

Business development and product managment in a fast paced, internet start-up environment.
