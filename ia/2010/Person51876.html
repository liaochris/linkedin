David Wright’s Summary

IDU Creative Services is a full-service agency providing graphic design, brand identity, web design, copy writing, photography and printing services.

We use the power of your brand to create exceptional design.
Our mission is written in three letters: IDU — we “identify you” in the marketplace through effective design.

Our mission is simple, and so is our promise to you. When we work for you, we promise that you will always receive:

• Quality — with the highest standards of creativity and graphic design.
• Personal service — sensitive to your needs and management style.
• Agency know-how at in-house prices — so your budget buys more.

--

Our creative services will help you stand out from your competition
because we:

• Conceptualize your ‘big picture’ themes
• Develop the creative elements to support them
• Determine your brand promise, benefits and personality
• Create materials to perfectly convey the essence of your business
• Pinpoint your core marketing messages
• Present them in compelling graphic design

You want success, and we can help you achieve success with creative design — the secret that marketing expert Philip Kotler calls “a powerful but neglected strategy tool”.

--
