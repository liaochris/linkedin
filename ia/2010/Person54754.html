Dennis O'Hagan’s Summary

After 25 successful years in various sales, marketing & management positions with IBM Corporation developing, teaching & implementing sales strategies & marketing plans,

I am now applying those skills to help people realize their Real Estate dreams. I am a licensed RE/MAX Real Estate Agent (REALTOR) in Texas, and I am also an International Real Estate Specialist (I.R.E.S.). Whether selling an existing home, buying a home (pre-owned or new), fractional ownership, locally, nationally or internationally, my goal is to help make the real estate transaction process as painless & stress free as possible while completing the transaction in the shortest amount of time for the best possible price. I have built my career & reputation on my commitment to customer satisfaction & attention to details which is exactly what is required to be successful as a REALTOR. Having moved with my family nine times through corporate relocations, I am an expert in dealing with the frustrations & stress that go along with the real estate selling & buying process.

Through comprehensive training and experience, Certified Distressed Property Experts are able to provide solutions for homeowners facing hardships in today’s market.

    The prospect of foreclosure can be financially and emotionally devastating, & often homeowners proceed without guidance of any kind. The best course of action for a homeowner in distress is to speak with a well-informed, licensed Real Estate professional. We have the tools needed to help homeowners find the best solution for their situation.

    While enduring financial difficulties is challenging for any family, selecting an agent with the CDPE Designation ensures you are dealing with a professional trained to address your specific needs.

    CDPEs don’t merely assist in selling properties, they serve & help save their clients in need.

  • Graduate REALTOR Institute - GRI (Real Estate Education/Training Program)

    National Professional Real Estate Education/Training Program for Real Estate industry specialists. 2007 — 2007

    The GRI designation indicates to buyers, sellers and other Real Estate industry professionals that you have made the commitment to provide a high level of professional service to your clients by securing a strong educational foundation.

    The GRI designation is obtained by attending a specific, intensive series of courses that total a minimum of 90 hours of classroom instruction. Subjects covered include the Sales Process (business development, sales & marketing, customer & client services, & cultural diversity) Legal & Regulatory (risk management & contract law) Technology (communications & Real Estate business technology), & Professional Standards (NAR code of ethics, standards of practice & arbitration/mediation).

    The subject matter has been chosen to educate practitioners about local, state and national Real Estate practices that affect them and their clients. GRI courses are taught by leading Real Estate professionals from around the country.
