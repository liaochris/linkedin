Jason Holland’s Summary

I'm passionate about all things global and digital. Having worked on the client, agency, and ad sales-side of digital media, I understand the complex communication and organizational aspects of developing a brand strategy and delivering it's message to consumers. Over the last 4 years, I have worked for Gorilla Nation, leading teams on both the business development and sales side of the firm. My core competencies at Gorilla include developing tactical ad sales plans, strategic partnership negotiation, new business acquisition and personnel management experience.

I possess a qualified track record of achievement in revenue growth by way of new business generation and account management in the entertainment, gaming, CPG, automotive, sports, lifestyle, and telecom vertical markets. My agency and client-direct relationships are strong, as well as diversified.

I also thrive on connecting people, generating new business, and facilitating the growth of existing business. With vested professional interests at GN, I pride myself on making direct contributions toward the growth of the company, the business model, and core objectives.

In August 2009, after having spent more than 8 fulfilling years on the west coast (where I enjoyed living everywhere from Hermosa Beach, to West Hollywood, to Culver City), I accepted an opportunity with Gorilla Nation to relocate to Chicago (I now live in River North). In my current professional capacity, I oversee branded ad sales for various Midwestern digital accounts, while transitioning oversight of our International expansion to the LA-based team.

My personal passions include travel, culinary exploits, and understanding global affairs / current events. I also love playing and watching sports.
