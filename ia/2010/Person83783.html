Ian Gertler’s Summary

Ian Gertler is currently the Chief Marketing Officer of Cursor Marketing, a Lexos Media company.

As a respected & successful professional, he focuses on the marketing, branding, public/investor relations, fundraising, corporate communications, social media & Internet strategy disciplines.

Gertler has served in full-time, consulting & volunteer capacities with established Fortune 500 leaders, early-stage entities, community venues & advertising firms. As President & CEO of Symplegades, he serviced various industries including Internet, technology, financial services, media, publishing, education, CPG, sports & government for Computer Associates (CA), TRAVELSAVERS (international travel consortium), American Home Mortgage, 1-800-FLOWERS.com, FindWhat.com/MIVA, CMP Media, The Nexus Group & Special Olympics - among others.

He has been actively involved in the emerging technologies arena, including software-as-a-service, business intelligence & wireless/mobile.

Among the accomplishments & honors:

* Inclusion in The New York Times technology columnist David Pogue's upcoming book, "The World According to Twitter"

* International Who's Who List of Professionals

* Web Marketing Association competition judge (numerous years)

* Leading creative marketer - the 1st edition of Seth Godin's "Bull Market"

* Delta Mu Delta International Honor Society

Gertler received a Bachelor of Science in Business from the State University of New York @ Oswego, w/ a concentration in marketing & a minor in Economics. He currently serves in advisory & Board capacities, including Smart Stamp (RFID). He's also been an active entrepreneur serving as founding member or advisor to many start-up initiatives, both domestic & international (proposed venture addressing small-to-medium size businesses with Inc. in China, online travel in India, Web 2.0 viral marketing company in U.S. & business efforts w/ various sports and entertainment celebrities.
