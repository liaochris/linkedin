Varun Singh’s Summary

Varun is determined to disrupt business-as-usual in the building industry. Having delivered software and consulting services for a range of industries including construction and capital projects, HVAC design and manufacturing, aerospace and biomedicine, he understands the significance of customer focused solutions.

As head of Sefaira’s building physics team, he is responsible for managing the development and delivery of Sefaira’s analysis framework and engineering solutions. His solution philosophy emphasizes the often overlooked aspects of engineering software - ease of use, speed and robustness, while ensuring accuracy and scalability.

He obtained an M.S. from U.C.L.A. and a Ph.D. from University of Maryland - College Park, in Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mathematics. He holds an EIT license and is a LEED Accredited Professional in building design and construction.

Varun Singh’s Specialties:

Modeling and simulation, Optimization, Green buildings, Energy Consulting and Management, Whole-facility Energy Audits, HVAC Systems
