Elena Acoba’s Summary

Call me a content provider, copywriter, freelancer or simply a writer. I provide business communications support for corporations and organizations as an independent contractor.

I help clients present clear, concise and engaging messages to their audiences by crafting content for websites, newsletters, corporate reports, marketing collateral, employee communications, public relations and media relations. I help them reach both internal and external audiences.

What else I do:

Write features and other articles for newspapers and magazines on assignment.

Teach writing skills, including "Introduction to Copywriting" at The Art Center Design College and "Writing for the Web" for staff and faculty of Pima Community College.

Currently serve as communications and networking director as a long-time board member and past president of the International Association of Business Communicators, Tucson Chapter.

Volunteer as a judge in programs awarding best practices in communications and to help students further their career goals in communications.

You can contact me at acoba@dakotacom.net.

Elena Acoba’s Specialties:

Writing topics: Employee and business activities, destination marketing, family activities, health, fitness, home and garden, food, cooking, personality and company profiles, history, education, community issues, attractions, events, arts and culture.

Other specialties: Writing coach, public speaker, creative writing teacher.
