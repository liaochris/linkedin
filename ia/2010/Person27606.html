Brenna Yaekle’s Summary

I'm excited to be joining the new BA Center for Excellence at the Phoenix office of Catholic Healthcare West. I will be working on a team focused on building the best practices for BAs to support the business.

My background and experience focus on corporate training and Business Analyst functions with extensive knowledge of the health care industry and customer service. After working in the field of Recreation Management for eight years, I returned to school and completed my M.Ed in 1987 at Bowling Green State University in OH.

In 1991 I started working for CIGNA HealthCare and now have over ten years working with training development, delivery and management. In addition, I have worked with all phases of the Project Management cycle on projects ranging from single production enhancements to full implementation of a multi million dollar call and task management application.

Prior to moving to CHW, I had been working with CIGNA IT as a Sr. Business Analyst writing Use Case requirements, coordinating training development for a new application and developing creative solutions for managing application access and security. In this role I became known for my strong organizational skills and consistently meeting aggressive deliverable deadlines.
