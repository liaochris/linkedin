About Premiere Global Services (PGI: NYSE):

PGi is a global business communications company servicing over 95% of the fortune 500 as well as over 65,000 other clients worldwide. The reason we have earned and have maintained their business is that we are able to help them in four areas critical to all businesses:

• Improving Communications
• Growing Revenues
• Reducing Costs
• Improving Awareness

This is the great part. All of our solutions are housed on our platform that can be accessed with a simple user name and password. There is nothing for our customers to install, maintain or upgrade. Because of this, customers can achieve instant ROI while eliminating the headache and inefficiency of doing it themselves.

Solutions include web and audio conferencing, electonic (email based) faxing, emarketing, and blasting of email, voice, sms or fax.
