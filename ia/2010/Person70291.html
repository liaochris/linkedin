Euphrosene Labon’s Summary

I am a specialist journalist and artist (mind body spirit ), as well as a generalist freelance copywriter. My articles have appeared in trade and consumer publications, in print and on the web. My clients range from SMEs to large organisations.

I am also an author, and I have written several educational books designed to make life and learning easy. Topics include time management, self-coaching, self-publishing, creative thinking, abundance and sales. They are pocket guides, designed and styled for corporate training and employee development and can be bought in volume from established suppliers as well as Floreo 33.

My USP is Words Ideas And Images To Make You Think.

My art is another element of that and is now for sale. I am a contemporary spiritual artist and cartoonist. The former also fits into the abstract art category. To view examples please visit www.euphrosenelabon.com

I am currently working on producing a fine art collection for exhibition, as well as motivational merchandise with a range of cartoons using the main character of Wise El. My fine art is also suitable for corporate buyers.

Please note: When I joined LinkedIn, it was not really geared for art sales. As this has now changed, so too has my profile description.
