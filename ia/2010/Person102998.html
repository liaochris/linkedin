John "JR" Rickinger’s Summary

Welcome to my world.

A driving passion for developing creative solutions. Solutions that unify the voice of companies. Solutions that work with sales teams promoting these effective results to customers on both a rational and emotional level. This passion and understanding of the power of creative has led me to a successful career in marketing and advertising.

I believe so strongly that result-oriented creative is based on industry knowledge applied throughout all phases of a successful integrated marketing program. With over 35 years of experience having directed creative teams for both small and large marketing agencies as well as having operated my own strategic marketing company, LRG, for ten years–Faced with an unexpected decision, it energized me to create what I feel is the next evolution of where marketing support is going and launched The JR Rickinger Company.

Tapping all my years of experience having led teams developing integrated strategic marketing principles, advertising, public relations, direct marketing, sales, web, interactive and trade show efforts for many Fortune 500 companies. I'm very blessed that I have established a strong reputation for knowing and understanding firsthand clients markets, the challenges and the products that the sales team, service departments and customers use, I'm offering my services directly to companies and corporations.

From heavy construction, mining and power tools to human health care. Animal health to mental health. From building brands for major brewery's to building materials for homes and commercial. Financial institutions to food packaging technologies. Pest management to pesticides. From car manufacturers to motorcycles and golf cars. State fairs to city wide inner city initiatives. Large business organizations or small, no matter what the challenge, I believe measurable results for clients are the best rewards for the effort. "Awards, when they come, are just the after dinner mint."

    May 2010 — Present (3 months)

    Creative Advantages That Make a Positive Difference

    Every successful business knows its products, its markets and its core competencies. However, many times good business strategies get lost in the details. You know the results you want but there is a gap between the strategic objectives and the tactical action plans. And that gap can be daunting – The difference between positive results and growth and mediocre attempts at success.

    The JR Rickinger Company is not an ad agency. It's internal, creative gap filler. We offer a new way to look at successfully marketing your company – A resource that works from the inside to offer results-oriented creative based on years of industry knowledge that will be used for input and implementation of all phases of your integrated marketing program. We will work with you to bridge those gaps between corporate vision and strategy and a comprehensive marketing plan.

    How does JRC work with your company? That depends on your marketing needs and challenges.

    If you are currently working with an ad agency, we can liaise with them for you. We will work in harmony with them. JR will offer them his knowledge and support to effectively improve their marketing programs for your company. JR brings the credentials and expertise to be a valued member of your team. He offers over 35 years of experience having directed creative teams for both small and large marketing agencies as well as having operated his own strategic marketing company for ten years. As a creative director who has worked firsthand with many Fortune 500 companies, he will continue to demonstrate that result-oriented creative with positive results and growth is based on industry knowledge applied in all phases of your comprehensive marketing plan.

    Welcome to the new way to successfully market your company – Working from the inside.
