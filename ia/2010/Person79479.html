As an Entrepreneur i'm always looking to create new and innovative software solutions.
My latest projects are: MobileLayers.com & Subscriptionstream.com.

MobileLayers specializes in mobile software solutions and has it's own unique mobile tracking platform (TraQ Platform). It allows you to track mobile phone's or vehicles using a very

http://www.aStateOfTrance.com/player.
