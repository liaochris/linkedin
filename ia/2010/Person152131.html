Nathan Lands’s Summary

Nathan Lands is a Self-Made Gaming & Internet Entrepreneur that since the age of 15 has started multiple successful internet businesses with sizes of the startups ranging from 1 person to 60.

In between startups Nathan moved to Taiwan and traveled Asia, studied Mandarin Chinese, acted in TV commercials and learned Asian business culture.

Nathan is the Co-Founder of GameStreamer, a currently 60 person and growing B2B digital distribution platform company that is on a direct path to overtake Direct2Drive and eventually aiming to surpass Steam as the leading digital distribution platform for games.

GameStreamer, unlike Steam, will work on multiple devices and utilizes a hybrid distribution model that gives the user the option to download or stream a game and will eventually work for movies and music as well.

Nathan lives in San Francisco and is extremely well connected in gaming and technology and is often called upon by executive friends from major Fortune 500 companies to help open doors around the world.

Nathan aspires to eventually be considered a linguist and currently speaks Mandarin Chinese and is learning Japanese.

Nathan also is an avid gamer, having originally been one of the first members of the guild "Soul Assassins" on the original EverQuest which was one of the leading guilds in the world.

Nathan's favorite games to date are: Warcraft 3, Starcraft, Quake 2, EverQuest and Zelda.
