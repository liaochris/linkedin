John D. McCarthy’s Summary

John provides strategic CFO/CEO services on an interim or consulting basis to investors and their portfolio companies. Focus on startups, turn-arounds and growth situations in technology, manufacturing and services.

John is currently Acting CFO and a consultant for 3 NYC VC-backed technology companies: Outside.in (Union Square Ventures), Jen Bekman Projects, Inc (True Ventures) and Better Advertising (Warburg Pincus) . Prior to this he was Acting CFO for Etsy.com (Union Square Ventures, Accel Partners) and Stylesight.com (Fidelity Ventures).

John has over 15 years experience in financial management for startups and publicly traded companies. Focus on financial planning & analysis, restructurings/turnarounds, M&A, capital raising and corporate development in the US and China for private (VC/Distressed Debt/PE) and public technology, manufacturing and service companies. Experienced in leading change and transformation in difficult situations. Broad experience in M&A deal sourcing, due diligence and post-merger integration.

John held senior positions, including acting CFO and SVP Sales & Corporate Development with Globix Corporation (hosting and managed services provider) during its successful turn-around and restructuring through bankruptcy.

Prior to joining Globix, John held financial and general management positions with Young & Rubicam, United Technologies and the NYC internet incubator LC39 Venture Group. He received an MBA degree from The Wharton School of Business of the University of Pennsylvania and a Masters degree in International Studies from Wharton's Lauder Institute. He received his undergraduate degree in Asian Studies from Connecticut College and is proficient in Mandarin Chinese.

John D. McCarthy’s Specialties:

Startups, Turnarounds, Restructurings, M&A, China Investments, Capital Raising, Operations, Sales, Financial Management of publicly traded and private (distressed debt/venture/private equity backed) and public entities, SEC Reporting, S-1, Sarbanes-Oxley, Turnaround Management Association (TMA) and Association for Corporate Growth (ACG)
