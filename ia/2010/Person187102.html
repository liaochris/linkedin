Shailendra Vyakarnam’s Summary

Passionate about enterprise education and development for over 20 years. This can best be described as being a pracademic - publishing in academic journals, books etc., and advising owners of businesses on strategy and boardroom team building. More recently I have become much more active in start-ups as co-founder and as investor. The two most recent companies are www.tristart.co.uk which is based on early research to help entrepreneurs succeed, by identifying what new team members they need and new knowledge and skills are required.

another manifestation of this passion is seeing the innovations in India and recognising that they have global potential. We have a great team of people starting out with this agenda through www.acceleratorindia.com


This work has been the result of research based development - both academic and policy research. Some of this work is visible as downloads on www.transitions.co.uk

There is so much to do - and growing the Centre for Entrepreneurial Learning is part of that agenda. Over the past couple of years I have been working with a team on what education should look like for the next wave of entrepreneurs through the World Economic Forum.

Passionate about enterpreneurship and taking the lessons to policy makers; corporates, Universities and to schools. Unlocking human potential is really what wakes me up in the mornings.

Shailendra Vyakarnam’s Specialties:

Board room team development across industries. Business growth strategies. Mentoring early stage businesses. I am familiar with most industry sectors due to the long experience of working with businesses.
