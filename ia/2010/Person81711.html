Robin Heppell’s Summary

Robin Heppell, CFSP, possesses a deep and diverse skill set. By combining his expertise in technology and pre-need, his formal business knowledge and his deep-rooted legacy in the funeral profession, makes Robin an asset to funeral homes and to cemeteries desiring to be more competitive, more profitable, and provide the best possible service for the families they serve. Through his consulting firm, Heppell Funeral Solutions, of Victoria, British Columbia, he assists death care professionals throughout North America embrace and incorporate innovative strategies and technologies. Robin is a fourth generation funeral director and a Certified Celebrant.

Robin Heppell’s Specialties:

Robin specializes in business analysis, pricing strategies, operational efficiencies, cremation strategies, website consultation, email marketing, continuing education, Photoshop & Excel tutorials, and professional development. He is also an internationally renowned speaker for funeral and cemetery professionals.
