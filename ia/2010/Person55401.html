Deven Shah’s Summary

A potent mix of financial services, marketing (specialization in digital mktg), communication and technology.

Deven started his career at 17 by setting up his own stock investing & advisory business. After graduation he worked for about 4 years as a software developer and has built successful web applications for clients based in the US and UK. In his next avatar, for 8 years he managed marketing and communication for financial services companies/institutions in India and also developed some information + technology tools for them. He has recently joined National Institute of Securities Markets (an institution promoted by SEBI) as an Advisor.
