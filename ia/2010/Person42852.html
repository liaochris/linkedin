Lawrence Axil Comras’s Summary

Focused on leveraging the Internet, guerilla marketing, innovative PR and making use of new technologies.     (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

Worked with Chuck Williams to create and produce award-winning CD-ROM Williams Sonoma Guide to Good Cooking. Featured speaker at M.I.T.'s 10th International Conference on Technology & Education.
