Mark Henderson’s Summary

As a Senior Consultant at Abel Solutions, I work with Fortune 2000 clients and have responsibilities that include gathering requirements, analysis and design, project management and successfully supporting the implementation of applications that are utilized both internally, as well as publicly accessible on the Internet. I have over 20 years of experience in all areas of the software development life cycle, primarily focused on financial services and manufacturing, along with the processes that support these areas. I am responsive to my customers needs and responsible for their expectations.
