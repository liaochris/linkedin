  • Senior Auditor

    Ernst & Young LLP

    (Partnership; Accounting industry)

    August 1995 — March 1999 (3 years 8 months)

    Clients included Power Computing (first authorized cloner of Apple computers), EZCorp (owner / operator of 350+ pawn shops), Austin Ventures & Texas Growth Fund (VCs), Human Code (educational software), MyTech (occupancy-sensing lighting technology), and a bunch of others.

    Recommended for early promotion to manager. Opted for career change instead, moving to L.A. to go to music school while working my way into a start-up in Austin.
