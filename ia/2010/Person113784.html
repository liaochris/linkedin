Kelly Brisbois’s Experience

  • Creative Director and Consultant

    My Storycatcher

    (Museums and Institutions industry)

    March 2010 — Present (3 months)

    As the Principal and Creative Director at My Storycatcher consulting, I develop and implement oral history projects and multi-media programs for museums, schools and libraries. In addition, I provide consulting and training for museums seeking to incorporate oral history and digital storytelling in their programming. As a personal historian, my services include recording oral histories, preserving photographs, and creating digital stories for families seeking to preserve their family history, and for corporations seeking to document organizational history and pass on corporate values.

  • Oral History Curator

    Vietnam Veterans of California

    (Museums and Institutions industry)

    December 2009 — Present (6 months)

    The VVC oral history project will document the first-hand accounts of Vietnam War veterans, volunteers, and family members who participated in the creation of programs needed to help veterans as they faced the many personal and societal challenges of that era. This project seeks to facilitate a meaningful dialogue with this early, core group of volunteers who developed thoughtful approaches to helping veterans reconnect to their communities and move forward to rebuild their lives. The oral history accounts of this movement will be detailed, organized and made available to the public in order to provide an important link to this part of California history. The oral history interviews, supporting documents and artifacts will be archived and interpreted to provide educational materials for a website and traveling exhibition that are instructive for society as we seek to understand and help veterans today.
