Leesa Shearman-Mitchell’s Summary

Events in Bloom provides personalized floral services to clients in Toronto and the surrounding areas. From simple wedding bouquets to complete corporate events, we ensure quality floral designs that are imaginative, elegant and unforgettable.   Experience the Events in Bloom difference - enhanced individual attention:

● Discuss your needs in the relaxed atmosphere of our design studio         
● Develop options for creating unique themes and concepts        
● Entertain with designs that exceed expectations, not your budget   
 
Principal designer Leesa Mitchell has over ten years floral design experience, specializing in weddings and intimate functions. As a graduate of Sheridan Colleges Crafts and Design Program, Leesa draws creativity from a solid foundation of design principles and colour theory.
   
Events in Bloom is a full-service florist, offering: consultation, theme conception, creation, delivery, full set up and other decor services.

Corporate, Weddings, Private events, dinner parties, Trade shows, commercial & residential standing orders.
