Roberto Alcazar’s Summary

Roberto is the Founder and Chief Creative Officer of EOintegration. His career in advertising spans over 18 years, working in Spain, Latin America, and the U.S. for top advertising agencies such as FCB, Saatchi & Saatchi, Euro RSCG, and Grey Global's winglatino, as Chief Creative Officer. Over this period of time, Roberto has gained considerable experience in directing advertising for a wide range of companies, such as Bank One, US Bank, DIAGEO, Panasonic, Kmart, Coors Brewing Co., General Motors (GMC), The Home Depot, General Mills, The Partnership for a Drug Free America, Budweiser and Bud Light among others. He has also directed several music videos and advertising spots, and more recently, a campaign for the Ad Council/AdoptUSKids.

Roberto's work has earned numerous awards including the Clio, Addy, New York Festival, Advertising Age Creative Award and Radio Mercury Award among others. He is frequently invited and has served in the judge panels for many prestigious awards and festivals.

In 2007 Roberto produced and directed his first documentary, CASI 10, based on the life of a bartender from Barcelona living illegally in New York. Capturing the essence of the Latino immigration experience in the US, CASI 10 has been feature in more than 10 Film Festivals worldwide.

In 2009 Roberto completed production of subHysteria, his first feature film as Co-writer, Producer and Executive Producer, due to be worldwide release in April 2010.

Roberto Alcazar’s Specialties:

Branded Content, Branded Entertainment, Multicultural Advertising, Feature Films and Documentaries. Writer, Producer, Creative Director.
