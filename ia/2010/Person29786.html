Bruce Edwards’s Summary

Twenty-one years progressive audit and information security experience. Experience includes: leading teams and task forces on enterprise level as well as dedicated information security endeavors, issues or concerns; co-ordination and handling of information security response and press relations; working with senior and executive management as well as stakeholders at all levels of the organization to receive feedback and gain consensus to move the organization forward on information security policy and strategy; participation in metropolitan area, state wide and national eHealth privacy and security research and health information exchange initiatives; co-ordination and leadership of enterprise information security governance program. Audit experience includes information systems, operational, financial, and compliance audits as well as specification and testing of audit software; writing software and training documentation; training co-workers on field procedures and field audits. Preceded by three years experience training field office administrators on field procedures, technology and system conversions.

Demonstrated abilities to manage projects within time constraints; establish productive working relationships; use analytical skills, attention to detail and a systematic approach to solve problems and write software specifications; take initiative; self-manage; develop realistic and appropriate action plans; work well within a team or individually; and build morale and group commitment to goals and objectives. Effective written and verbal communications skills. Proficient with numerous computing programs as well as hardware configuration, maintenance and construction.

Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) and Fellow, Life Management Institute (FLMI), Master of Public Health (MPH) Management Concentration (UofL), Bachelor of Science (BS) Industrial Management (Ga Tech).
