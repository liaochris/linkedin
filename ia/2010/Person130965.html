Manuel Canales’s Summary

Manuel Canales has been working in the Energy Renewable business development in Canada, USA and the Caribbean and Latin America regions since early 2007.

Manuel is currently working towards the development of a Caribbean and Latin America Energy Renewable Investment Fund.

Manuel Canales is the co-founder of The Media Group Inc., a Canadian private corporation that holds several subsidiary marketing and development companies and that is dedicated to bridging corporate culture and opportunity sourced among well-funded mature companies in the communications industries.

Manuel has been involved in the broadcast and communications industry for over 30 years, first as a producer in his native Chile, and later in various facets of the radio, TV business in Canada and the USA.

Manuel is adept at understanding the business climate in the community and has a strong knowledge of the interests of companies, their clients and customers. He has completed in Canada University studies in Television Production, Economics and Marketing. Manuel also graduated from the Sales Representative and Marketing Course. He is originally from Chile where he completed Accounting studies and National and International Television Production job assignments.

Manuel has traveled and worked extensively in media and communications in North America (USA, Canada, and Mexico), Europe, the Caribbean and The Americas over the last 30 years.

Manuel is a seasoned broadcaster and has prepared several broadcasting briefs on behalf of clients and regulary participate in the broadcasting industry regulated environment (Local Sales Agreements, etc) Manuel has appeared and consulted with and before the CRTC on numerous occasions, either for specific clients or for policy matters, such as the 2006 Radio Review.
