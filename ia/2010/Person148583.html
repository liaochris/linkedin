Monique H. Altheim, Esq., CIPP’s Summary

Member of the New York Bar, as well as the Belgian Bar. Six years experience as a EU attorney in tort, contract and maritime law litigation.
Leave of absence due to full-time concentration on non-career elements of life: marriage to a senior partner in a law firm, child rearing, home building and community involvement, late husband's long illness and subsequent passing.
Current expertise includes ediscovery and privacy law, especially collision issues between US ediscovery and EU privacy and data protection.
