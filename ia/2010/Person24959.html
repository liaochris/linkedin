Srijib Mukherjee’s Summary

Dr. Mukherjee is an expert in interconnected grid operations, bulk power operations, energy portfolio management and energy trading & risk management. He brings over 16 years of experience from the electric utility and energy consulting industries in energy risk management strategy, NERC compliance, bulk power dispatch operations, generator siting and interconnection studies for wind and other generator interconnections, EHV system plans, distribution system planning, and EMS/SCADA advanced technology applications. In the past 17 years, Srijib has managed and led teams in the areas of system operations, regulatory policy, energy risk management, resource & transmission planning and energy trading/portfolio management. He is an expert in bulk power operations, generation and transmission asset management and planning studies to develop utility capital budgets and rate case support. Additionally, he has developed, streamlined and standardized various control center applications in relation to building Control Center Organizations, Regional Transmission Organizations (RTO) and Independent System Operator (ISO) organizations and helped structure and build metrics to bring profitability

Srijib has an MBA from Duke University, MSEE and Ph.D. from the University of Miami and is a Registered Professional Engineer both in the States of California and North Carolina. He is an IEEE Senior Member, a recipient of the IEEE PES Outstanding Engineer Award in 2001 and past Chair of the IEEE, Eastern NC Power Engineering and Industrial Applications Societies.

Srijib Mukherjee’s Specialties:

Energy/Electric Uitlity - Transmission Asset and Risk Management, Wind Farm Interconnection siting and modeling, Energy portfolio optimization with the use of GENTRADER, Transmission economics and congestion management using market based tools such as PROMOD, GE-MAPS and Strategist, Generator Siting/System Impact and Feasibility Studies using PTI PSS/E (steady state and dynamics), PTI MUST, Long range transmission planning using POWERWORLD.
