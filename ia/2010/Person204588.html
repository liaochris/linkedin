Tina Gleisner’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur who loves finding opportunities, building teams and creating unique solutions. For 30 years, I developed expertise across a broad array of "technologies" at IBM, developing operating systems, middleware and industry solutions like video-on-demand with Hong Kong Telecom in 1994.

In 2003 it was time to try something new so I used my 25+ years of real estate investment experience and jumped into NH's construction industry, opening My Handyman of Southern NH. What's been fun is sharing my new found expertise with content on my web site, newsletters and blogging.

More surprising is discovering that selling is natural, that when I help people solve problems with their home ... that's selling! I love networking and enjoy mentoring other business owners who ask for help building their businesses. I started a cooperative marketing group, 4 Walls 1 Roof, to help small business owners get online quickly and inexpensively. I am continuing to look for ways to leverage the marketing skills I'm developing ... to create new solutions, so send me your ideas!

You can contact me through LinkedIn or better still, tinagleisner@pobox.com

If you want to learn a little more about me, check out my blog at http://blog.myhandymannh.com or there are several articles out there.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 2006 — Present (4 years )

    Women are assuming more responsibility in buying, maintaining and enhancing their homes. Home related service businesses to be successful, need to find more effective ways to communicate with today's home owners (women, younger people more comfortable with a computer than a hammer, seniors living independently for many years).

    4 Walls 1 Roof was started to enable connections between home owners and women business owners/professionals (expanding soon). These business owners benefit from cooperative marketing activities like a shared web site, home show exhibits, etc. and women really like working with women who understand their unique needs.

    Work is underway to revamp the business to create a more robust solution offering home owners a more comprehensive solution, and more marketing tools for business owners.
