Ed McLaughlin’s Summary

I'm the sales and marketing professional who people call when they’re ready to grow by thinking big and acting small. Which means that I take an entrepreneurial approach to firms - and help them grow in a way that is organic, sustainable, and predictable.

____________________________________________________________

Here are the answers to my most frequently asked questions:

Q: Why did you name your blog “The Baby Gorillas”?
A: This is a reference to my intended audience. “Baby gorillas” are companies that act small - they’re fast, flexible, and most importantly, they think big.

Q: Your Twitter profile says that you’re “anti-karaoke.” What does that mean?
A: If you want to imitate musical hits in bars, that’s OK. But imitation in business isn’t smart. In my opinion, the leaders of tomorrow are those that defy conventional wisdom and do things differently.
