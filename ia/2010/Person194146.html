Stephanie Rudat’s Summary

Stephanie is a principle-centered business woman with an innate ability to recognize opportunity, build successful business strategies & lead organizations which has been demonstrated through multiple business ventures. Her diverse experience extends from having worked in entertainment to currently being active in real estate sales, a growing non-profit organization & as a purveyor of fine art.

In real estate, she has represented 1st time home buyers to multi-property investors thru out Orange County. She consults on property development, handles REO's & leases.

Serving as a Founder of Alliance of Youth Movements is her most proud philanthropical achievement. Stephanie, along with 3 friends created A.Y.M. to serve as a catalyst to pioneering leaders using new media to create change by providing various resources allowing these international leaders to thrive. The inaugural summit was a huge success partnering the State Dept with sponsors including Google, MTV, YouTube, Howcast, AT&T and more leading to the launch of A.Y.M. as a 501c3.

Another rewarding role includes serving as Treasurer & Secretary of Nourish America, which supplies nutritional supplements to Americans in 37 states while maintaining a 4 Star rating by Charity Navigator. Nourish America provides the daily essential nutrition required to live productive, successful lives for impoverished American children, families, pregnant moms, seniors & the National Guard.

As a purveyor of fine art, Stephanie manages art fund investments & consultants for fine art collectors. The gallery's collection is world-class including original works of Picasso, Chagall, Warhol, Matisse, Close & more.

The eldest of 3 children, she was raised by terrific parents who encourage selfless involvement. She's a member of Gen Next which appeals to her desire to educate herself on political affairs and to expand her need to ‘make a difference’ by actively participating in life & special causes.
