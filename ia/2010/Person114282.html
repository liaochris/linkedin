Kendall Allen’s Summary

As adviser to agencies and media companies, Kendall runs her pursuits through a company she established, Influence Collective, LLC, in NYC.

A primary focus is Carolyn & Co. Media, where she oversees digital ventures, namely the establishment of Work Her Way (www.workherway.com), general managing the business and supporting Carolyn Kepcher, former Trump Organization EVP and the company's founder.

Allen’s 16-year path has led from print to digital media, from publisher to agency-side. She first spent a few years covering emergent media, tech in the SF Bay Area as a moonlighting, independent journalist obsessed with the broadening media landscape.

Then moving from print to the digital realm during its most formative years, Allen helped found Thrive, an AOL/Time Inc. JV, ultimately leading consumer marketing, distribution for the company. She has held top posts at USWeb/CKS and Euro RSCG Circle. Allen’s most recent post before advising the team at ‘mktg’ was Managing Director for Incognito Digital, LLC where she spent two years building the agency from its nascent state, expanding the client base, establishing its voice in the market.

A highly regarded thought leader in media and marketing circles, she was on the founding floor of Search, as original executive at one of the industry's very first SEM agencies, Fathom Online, for whom she moved to New York to open the East – and then regional Director of Client Services for iCrossing. She contributes heavily to thought leadership in the field, as Adjunct Instructor for Laredo Group; occasional speaker at SES; editorial or programming contributor to Media Post and iMedia; and periodic NYU lecturer. She sits on the Producers Guild of America, New Media Executive Council; the Association of Downloadable Media, Ad Council; Ad Club of New York Digital Marketing and Media Council; Board of Directors for 212NYC, serving as Vice President, recently having revamped Professional Development programming.

Kendall Allen’s Specialties:

Blended management profile, with broad and deep business background on agency- and publisher-sides of the media business. Avid connector, with stellar reputation with reports, clients, partners and within industry. A business developer at heart. Converged media background, with integrated marketing services bent, due to progression from print to digital, in various capacities, during industry evolution. Strong reputation for complex programming, instructional and editorial work in the sphere.
