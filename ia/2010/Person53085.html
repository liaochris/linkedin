Dean Douglas, Esq.’s Summary

Dean Douglas is currently an associate at Gervais & Davenport, is a boutique law firm that specializes in the defense of doctors, nurses, hospitals, nursing homes and other healthcare providers. Represent clients at both the Federal and State court levels, as well as before professional licensing boards.

Dean is also a case manager at Life Links, Inc. a provider agency for the Massachusetts Department of Mental Retardation. Dean has over six years experience working with Non-profit organizations.
