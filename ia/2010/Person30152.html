Bryan Daniel’s Summary

My skills and experience are in driving strategy, guiding creative, content and user experience, then technically managing the overall project across a cross-functional team. I have extensive client-facing experience as a PM, Producer and Account Supervisor.

11 years of experience in Consumer and B2B traditional and online advertising, creative development/management, user experience development, CMS, SEO, database/technical deliverables marketing and software development.

Roles
As a Producer/PM, I work with clients for discovery of their needs are from a strategic, business and functional perspective, consult with them to develop a plan for their site, online ads or landing pages (that includes the optimization of the web pages so the search engines will find them when a user searches for a local product or service), user experience, user interface, marketing issues, technical and hosting issues, then assemble a production team of various disciplines, oversee the development of user interface, content and back-end for the site and the technical production to bring it all together, testing, training of client and finally launching the site live.

As a Business Analyst, I work with clients to understand their needs are from a strategic, business and functional perspective, consult and interview stakeholders to conduct research and analysis, make strategic and tactical recommendations for web sites, software applications, landing pages as well as write and edit white papers.
