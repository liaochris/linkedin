Betsy Kent’s Summary

Betsy Kent is the president of Be Visible Associates, a specialty firm that uniquely provides Internet and Social Media Strategy and Design services to businesses. Because business people often need a wide range of help with their web presence, Be Visible Associates advises companies on search engine optimization, Google ads, Twitter, Facebook and social networking to help them achieve the growth, visibility, and sustainability that they need. That includes analysis of the usability and functionality of a website, getting the right people to the website, measuring results, interpreting them, and taking action.
What sets Betsy apart from many Internet marketing specialists is that she seeks to make the rapidly evolving Internet understandable to the average business owner. Betsy's blog, "Just Ask Betsy" is a great place to get information about web marketing in language that is simple to comprehend.
With Be Visible Associates at their side, any business can compete on a level playing field with with even much larger competitors.
