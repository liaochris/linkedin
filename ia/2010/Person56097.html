Diana V Faustmann’s Summary

Diana V. Faustmann offers creative and results-driven communications and marketing services combined with an exceptional work ethic and commitment to organizational objectives. She is a professional with 15 years of distinguished performance in the architecture/ engineering/ construction (A/E/C) industry and 10 years in the publishing field. Her career has focused on communications and presentations that (1) engagingly inform, (2) compel consideration, (3) effectively attract new business relationships, and (4) strengthen existing ones.

Diana has competently supported and facilitated new business development in the millions of dollars. To that end she has successfully developed trade and consumer magazines; numerous newsletters, articles, brochures, direct mail pieces, news releases and website content; and countless statements of qualifications (SOQs), RFP/RFQ responses, award submittals and presentations. Her efforts always skillfully consider complex client decision-making processes and augment (in print, online and in person) all corporate efforts made in building relationships with key decision makers and influencers, project/account managers and user groups.

At the core of it all is a persuasive storyteller, a proactive manager, a personable team/ relationship builder and a tactical planner. Contact Diana today to discuss your situation and explore whether working together might be a good fit in the long or short term.

Inquiries from outside the A/E/C and publishing industries are welcomed.
