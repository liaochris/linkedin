Michelle Cobb’s Summary

"Of course it’s personal … every design has to be.”

Imagine a very personal area, a place you want to be, a place where you are at peace and a place of joyful abundance. If you close your eyes now, let yourself feel this serene space, a most welcome escape from the everyday stresses of life. It’s your freedom.

It’s my passion and mission to transform your outdoor space into something that’s infused with your personality, style and energy. Everything will be harmonious and colorful using superior materials that create a positive, life-affirming energy flow. Perfect balance is achieved through color, style and function – the essential ingredients of good design.

Your new landscape will make you feel exactly as you want to feel … like your sublimely personal oasis is within reach, just a few steps away.

“The end result of your design is that you love it.
The process is not complete until you do.”

As an independent designer, Michelle's focus is to listen to her clients needs, wants and desires, because every design is personal. An expert landscape designer for 15 years and an empath, she brings a wealth of artistic intuition to transform a space from just a backyard into an oasis. She is also a certified Feng Shui practitioner which helps her find the perfect balance of energy and abundance. It’s her unique blend of expertise, communication, creativity and outside-the-box thinking that creates the most distinctively personal landscape for her clients.

After the design is completed, she assists her clients in choosing the right contractor by acquiring bids from contractors she knows and trusts, or helping them find materials and plants so they may install the design themselves.
