Jennifer Welsh’s Summary

I'm currently living in Philadelphia, as an editorial intern for The Scientist until mid-September.

I would like to write science news and features, quirky science/tech/nerd culture blogging or possibly work as a public information officer for a med- or bio-tech company or university.
