Mark Van Vessem’s Summary

mindIT supports companies and physicians all over Texas, Louisiana, Arkansas, Florida and Oklahoma. We are a full service, fully managed IT and Medical Billing firm that focuses on the requirements and needs of physicians offices. Our solution is unique because we offer a Proactive approach to technology and billing rather than the more common Reactive solutions. We ensure that your office runs as efficiently as possible and that each physician collects every dollar that they earn. mindIT currently manages over $20 million in annual physician charges and brings over 10 years of medical billing and revenue cycle management experience to our clients.

We offer our physicians the same type of service that massive practices enjoy at a fraction of the cost. We want to be the ONE call and ONE solution for all of your IT and billing needs. We are not a large firm that view our clients as numbers. Our mission is to remain small enough that we support people and the technology that they use—all with a singular drive for amazing customer service. We see ourselves as part of your team and we take that relationship very seriously!
