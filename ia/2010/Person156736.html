Omar AL Zabir’s Summary

I am a Technology Entrepreneur.

I founded Pageflakes (www.pageflakes.com), a social personalized web 2.0 Start Page, with my friend Christoph Janz. Pageflakes has been acquired by LiveUniverse which is founded by MySpace founder Brad Greenspan.

Currently heading the development team spread in USA and Bangladesh and working as Cheif Technology Officer of Pageflakes.

I have 14 years of experience in Software Development mostly on Smart Clients and Web based applications. My previous works include projects for Bank of America, US Federal Reserve Bank, Hewlett-Packerd US, CitiBank, Wellsfargo, PMTC etc.

Besides professional work, I write articles in MSDN and Code Project, run open source projects and write Web 2.0 technology books.

I am a Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) from Bangladesh.
