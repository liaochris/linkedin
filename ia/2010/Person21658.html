Ben Allen’s Summary

Ben is a New York City based corporate professional who is passionate about the rapid adoption of mobile location awareness technologies, and the related products and services which will be created as a result... particularly in the areas of advertising and marketing.

He has spent the past few years focused on location based services, including serving as Director of Business Development at Zoombak, a consumer products spin out-of of True Position, a leading provider of wireless location intelligence solutions.

Prior to joining Zoombak, Ben held a leadership position at MTV Networks for over half of a decade, building out the $150+ million digital advertising sales operations behind one of the biggest names in branded entertainment.

Before joining MTV, Ben earned his MBA from Vanderbilt University, and spent five years rising through the ranks of some of New York City’s leading advertising agencies.

Ben currently writes a blogs about developments in Location Based Services and Location Based Advertising at http://www.locationawhere.com
