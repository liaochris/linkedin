Barry Boone’s Summary

I've been working with computers the past 28 years doing everything from assembler language programming, computer networking, and have been working on the Internet (TCP/IP routing, Cisco admin, and UNIX admin) since the days when the Internet was just text and a 56K connection was a dedicated Internet line that a local ISP might even have :)

I once wrote a freeware application back in the 80's that was used by many tens of thousands of people as the standard data/file compression software for the TI-99/4A. I served as a SysOp and contributor to the old networks like Compuserve, GEnie, and Delphi. See http://www.ti99hof.org (specifically http://www.ti99hof.org/Bios/BarryBoone/BarryBoone.htm ) if you're interested in my TI-99/4A exploits.

Other fun experiences in this industry include running a 24 line BBS (for Creative Labs), doing my own full duplex sound driver for the Sound Blaster Pro (horrible CPU utilization but it WORKED :) , and helping the founder of the first ISP in Tulsa OK get his network and services set up.

These days I find myself in the role of running a marketing company that has seen 10+ years of solid growth. I never really saw myself managing people or doing marketing, but I've found that many of the analytical problem solving skills I've picked up over the years lend themselves nicely to the tasks at hand. Plus I still run the company servers and write much of the code in use on the sites, so I do get to have some fun :)
