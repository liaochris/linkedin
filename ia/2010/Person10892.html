Andrea Bolder’s Summary

Andrea Bolder is a professional Internet Success & Network Marketing Lifestyle Coach. She loves helping people with an entrepreneurial spirit find success beyond their wildest dreams! Encouraging, Teaching, & Inspiring Is What She DOES!

After achieving her goal of becoming a Gold Medal Olympian & a successful career as a professional track and field athlete she entered the corporate world.

As a top sales consultant she found that she was unfullfilled, working to make someone else rich. So she decided to be the captain of her own ship, write her own destiny.

Full-steam ahead she entered the realm of home-based business & started her Network Marketing career.

Though she hit the ground running, listening to her upline, attended trainings and business opportunity meetings, making phone calls, doing presentations, passing out flyers, business cards, did trade shows, she struggled for MONTHS trying to figure out how to succeed in the MLM/Network marketing industry.

Looking for solutions to be able to work smarter, not harder Andrea found what she calls the Power of 3: Internet Marketing combined with Affiliate Marketing combined with Direct Sales. There was actually a proven system - My Lead System Pro - that worked, and provided RESULTS. The skills and training obtained from MLSP put Andrea in the position to automate her business, produce RESULTS and get on the fast-track to success in her company.

With that she created StepByStepMLMSuccessOnline.com, a site designed to be a step by step playbook to network marketing success online. Her goal is to help people WIN in the game of network marketing, generate multiple streams of income & develop their greatest asset - Themselves!!

If you know the rules, you can play the game!
