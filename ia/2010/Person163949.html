Phillip LeBlanc’s Summary

Retired at 27, Debt Free, Working 2hrs A Day

Chasing American dream, we went to school, got great jobs, married after being high-school sweethearts, had four kids and earned a healthy six-figure income. What we did not have was freedom – freedom to spend time with our kids, cultivate our marriage, vacation when we wanted to, and the freedom to earn as much income as we desired.

Stressed out, near divorce, gaining weight and discovering two of our precious kids had autism, which would require $50,000 per child to support, we needed a change – we needed a miracle

After applying some key elements to our business we have been featured in publications with world renown authors such as Steven Covey, Robert Kiyosaki, Donald Trump and Dr. John Gray. We were not only featured magazines for our personal success but for helping other individuals and families be successful in business. Our Desire is for you to have More Time Freedom, More Money and Fast Results.
