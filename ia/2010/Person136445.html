jason manley’s Summary

After years spent in the industry trenches, I built Massive Black from the ground up. We have gone on to create rich content for games, film, toys, comics, advertising, and new media. We have 175 major entertainment clients including 19 of the top 20 game publishers and have completed 235 major AAA projects to date. All three of those statistics lead the field.

MB clients include Microsoft, Nintendo, Sony, id, Bethesda, EA, THQ, Blizzard, NCsoft, Warner Bros. and others. We have established teams in San Francisco and Asia.

Key upcoming and released titles: God of War 3, Starcraft 2, Diablo 3, Bioshock 2, Killzone 2, Transformers 2, Fallout 3, Bioshock 2, Sonic, Red Faction 3, and Dragon Age...

Massive Black is the only Art outsourcing studio to have successfully completed major Art projects on first party titles for all three console developers simultaneously. MB's impressive portfolio is available online and further information about pipelines, and available bandwidth may be shared upon request. MB offers a comprehensive package of concept to 3d to animation services, encompassing all artwork necessary to complete AAA video game titles

Massive Black Entertainment, the intellectual property development wing of the company creates beautiful and fun worlds for the global market.
