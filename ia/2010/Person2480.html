Adam Lapsevich’s Summary

Adam Lapsevich is President, Producer and Chief Creative Officer at Digital Design, a video production company.

Digital Design offers a full range of video production services with a specialty in web video production and a web video hosting platform. DDV creates video content for a variety of industries and has carved a unique niche within the travel industry as the "preferred video production company" and is regarded as a "preferred supplier" to six of the industry's leading consortia: Signature Travel Network, Ensemble Travel Group, Vacation.com, MAST, Cruise Planners and Westa.

Adam Lapsevich is also an incredible public speaker on the subject of Online Marketing and using Social Media to develop business for travel and non-travel industries.

He was a featured speaker at the 2009 COSE (Council of Smaller Enterprises) Small Business Conference in Cleveland, OH in October and speaks at various COSE functions throughout the region. He was the featured speaker at the MAST and Cruise Planners annual conventions in Las Vegas and Fort Lauderdale and will be speaking to the Suncoast Travel Industry Association in February 2010 and will be a featured speaker for the Public Relations Society of America on the Kent State University Campus in April 2010, delivering his message on the "Power of Video Marketing and Social Media".

He is a dynamic and engaging presenter and is available with advanced scheduling to present his expertise in person.

His web seminar "The Travel Agent Crash Course in Online Marketing" has been received with accolades as well as the 5 part series of presentations, "The Travel Agent Seminar Series in Online Marketing".

Visit www.digitaldesignvideo.com/ for more info.

Adam Lapsevich’s Specialties:

Web video production for travel and non-travel industries. Corporate Video Production, Broadcast Commercials, Training Video, Promotional Video, Duplication and Packaging as well as an online video delivery platform with measurable analytical reporting.
