James J. Gillespie, Ph.D., J.D.’s Summary

Dr. Gillespie focuses his research, teaching, and consulting on the strategic, financial, and operational aspects of the global healthcare and life science industries, with particular focus on the biotechnology, medical device, and pharmaceutical sectors. He actively participates in leading conferences, research reports, white papers, and other intellectual endeavors exploring biopharma and healthcare issues.

He has held tenure-track research and teaching positions at the Illinois Institute of Technology’s Chicago-Kent College of Law (teaching corporate law and corporate taxation) and the University of Illinois at Chicago’s College of Business Administration (teaching competitive strategy and Honors College seminars on healthcare entrepreneurship and healthcare innovation).

He is a successful entrepreneur with significant hands-on operational experience. He founded a commercial, industrial, and residential contracting company and grew annual revenues from $0 to $1 million, increased employees from 1 to 15, and created a base of 3,000+ customers.
