Pamela Starr’s Summary

StarrParalegals, LLC's mission is to provide results that surpass expectations through our dedication to our clients; adding maximum value to their businesses by handling complex bankruptcy matters locally and nationally; being professional, accessible, efficient, responsive, and technologically sophisticated; helping to provide a green alternative to the traditional office paradigm; and providing the opportunity for our employees to pursue their religious, family, and community obligations and interests.

Applying 25+ years as a Paralegal to STARRParalegals, LLC -
Virtual Paralegals Specializing in Bankruptcy & Creditors' Rights
Providing Expert Services to Attorneys, Law Firms, & Corporate Legal Departments

By providing 'virtual' services (online, via email & internet) we increase the value of your billable dollar & save you hundreds of dollars a year in overhead costs (office equipment, supplies, taxes, benefits). As independent contractors, we are more cost effective and can complete your job in less time than an in-house employee.

STARRParalegals, LLC can draft, prepare, record &/or e-file (ECF proficient) various documents including: Bankruptcy Proofs of Claim, Notices of Appearance, Motions for Relief & other Motions & Responses; Adversary Complaints & Answers, Preference Actions; Loan Documents, Modifications, Forbearance Agreements, Demand Letters, Notices, Memoranda & Deeds Under Power of Sale, PT-61 forms, etc.; Perform UCC & Real Property Collateral Searches; Notary Public for the State of Georgia - Mobile Notary
