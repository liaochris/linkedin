Nicole Magdovitz, IIDA,LEED AP’s Summary

I aspire to help people enjoy where they work and play. While my experience allows me to work independently, I prefer to collaborate with co-workers, consultants and end-users to create and implement unique and high caliber design solutions to problems.

Motivated and persistent professional with experience in design, sales and client relations. Excellent visual skills, a passion for my work and responsiveness to changing business demands are but a few notable characteristics.
• Adept in conveying the benefits of products/services and generating customer interest.
