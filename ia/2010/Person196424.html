STORIS Inc’s Summary

STORIS Management Systems is the pace-setter in business solutions for the retail industry. Over 350 leading big ticket retailers rely on our management software systems to integrate operations, streamline processes and outperform the competition.

Vision R8, STORIS' powerful enterprise system, brings business rules for more profitable operations within reach of all retailers. Vision R8 is a fully integrated, real-time solution that connects all the essential aspects of your retail operations - from POS and Supply Chain Management to Financial Reporting and Analytics, providing retailers with complete control over their business.

For more information please call 1-888-4-STORIS or register for a complimentary online demo by logging on to www.storis.com/OnlineDemo
