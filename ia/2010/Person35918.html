    Charles Dolan, born in 1961, is the Chief Investment Officer of Glenrock a registered Commodity Trading Advisor. Mr. Dolan received a B.S. in Chemical Engineering from the University of Rochester in 1985, an MBA in Finance from New York University in 1990 and has 18+ years of experience in the futures markets. Mr. Dolan has solely designed and is responsible for implementing Glenrock’s proprietary fundamentally driven trading and risk management strategies. The portfolio is globally diversified across foreign exchange, interest rates, stock index values, energy and commodities. Glenrock's Fundamental Diversified Portfolio has a five year track record.
