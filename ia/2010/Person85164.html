Kevin Smith, CPP’s Summary

I have over 25 years of security and safety experience with numerous Fortune 500 companies including Ross Stores, Inc., ConocoPhillips, Circle K and Target. I am a Certified Protection Professional and am 'Board Certified in Security Management' with the American Society of Industrial Security of which I am a member.

Kevin Smith, CPP’s Specialties:
