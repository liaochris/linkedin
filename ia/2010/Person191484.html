Michael Kelly’s Summary

For the past 20 years (both employed and as company owner) I have specialised in helping technology companies increase their sales results and sales effectiveness, helping them identify and win major sales opportunities worldwide. The majority of companies I have worked with are in the enterprise software space, ranging from ERP, CRM and Supply Chain to middleware tools and financial software.

I have been lucky enough to work with both major blue chip technology companies as well as many smaller, fast growing technology companies that are leaders in their niche. I have been through the dot com boom, as well as the bust with them, and understand the varying challenges that both good times and bad bring to technology marketing and sales professionals.

I am continuously seeking new and better ways for technology companies to identify and win enterprise sales opportunities. In recent years I have become increasingly involved in online marketing solutions to achieve this. If you have innovative, cutting edge ideas or solutions in this area please do not hesitate to contact me.

An example of an innovation I have recently spearheaded in this area is the world's first "browse, then buy" solution for enterprise software sales opportunities located at www.newitprojects.com . This has received an enormously positive response from both existing and new clients.
