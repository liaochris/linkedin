Deborah Bell’s Summary

Deborah Bell has over 12 years working with clients developing an understanding for their needs to help improve bottom line dollars and productivity for their company. Her experience with a billion dollar international staffing company has given her the ability to work with many types of clients. Deborah managed one of the top producing branches at Kelly Services and dealt with large volume accounts. In addition she is very involved with the community and serving others, for example she is the fundraising chair for the American Lung 2011 "Fight for Air Stair Climb in Dallas.

Financial Additions was founded 11 years ago and has become a leading provider of searching and staffing services for accounting and finance professionals. Voted #1 Best Place to Work by Dallas Business Journal, our philosophy combines integrity, objectivity, and adherence to professional standards. We take the time to understand the demands, personalities, and challenges of our clients. This insight allows us to match the right client with the right candidate.

Our clients' range in sizes from top Fortune 500 companies down to medium and small companies within Dallas, Fort Worth, Los Angeles, Orange County, and San Diego.

Our stable base of experienced recruiters and staffing professionals include several CPAs, MBAs and finance professionals that have previously been hiring managers. Our long term, established networks and relationships have enabled us to quickly fill search and staff augmentation projects.

Whether you would like to search one of our hundreds of open positions with top companies or a hiring manager trying to identify a top accountant please visit www.financialadditions.com
