Jamie Thomas’s Summary

Jamie Thomas is a Financial Specialist with The Bulfinch Group and a Financial Advisor of Park Avenue Securities. Jamie is a dedicated advisor who works with individuals, families and business owners to help them purposefully build and protect their wealth. Jamie utilizes a process which integrates a wide range of financial strategies that organizes his clients’ financial world, integrating individual strategies into a consolidated, comprehensive plan. This process ensures that planning is completed with the goal of maximizing wealth and with the ultimate purpose of helping clients realize their goals, hopes and dreams. Potential outcomes after completing the process include:

Wealth maximization
Education funding
Asset and income protection
Retirement income maximization
Minimize income and estate taxes
Estate planning
Creation of cash recovery strategies
Investment efficiency
Risk management
Charitable planning

Jamie Thomas’s Specialties:

Financial strategies for business owners, sales professionals, executives and high net worth families.

When working with clients, Jamie utilizes an electronic wealth organizational platform called The Living Balance Sheet® (www.thelivingbalancesheet.com).
