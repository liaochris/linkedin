Monika Weiglova’s Summary

My background and my work as a professional psychologist include 5 years experience in Marriage and Family Counseling Service (conducting individual, couple and family consultations), leadership of group therapy with children and adolescents and psychodiagnostic assessment in the Centre for Neurological and Psychological Care (Neurops).

Because of my interest in expressive therapy (nonverbal and metaphoric expression), art is the tool of my choice in my work, especially in group sessions.
Besides various courses in nonverbal and art therapy area in the past, I'm currently attending Psychoanalytic Couple and Family Psychotherapy Course and have an interest in cross cultural psychology.
My goal is to apply aspects of all my courses and professional interests in my future career.
