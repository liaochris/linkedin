Jan Sauer’s Summary

Raised and educated in Europe, and fluent in Danish, German and English with a working knowledge of French, Jan Sauer is an international business expert with a passion for helping international companies navigate the waters of establishing and ramping up their U.S. operations. Sauer’s international expertise combined with proven results and extensive education has positioned him perfectly to take on leadership roles in international business.

As the Danish Trade Commissioner, Sauer has helped over 45 Danish companies build U.S. outposts, serving as a day-to-day coach and team leader. He has lead the execution of a total of over 500 assignments.

Prior to becoming the Danish Trade Commissioner, Sauer was Vice President at the Conrad Group where he led projects that ranged from launching new product lines to helping companies start new businesses in emerging markets. In every case, his ability to balance analysis with creativity yielded great financial results.

Sauer also brings an extensive knowledge of international marketing from his Account Manager position at Kunde and Co. He worked with top pharmaceutical companies such as Sandoz, Coloplast and Ferring to create marketing strategies to build their brand and corporate identities.

Sauer has a Master of Science in Design & Communication Management from Copenhagen Business School, has completed MBA studies at the Georgia Institute of Technology, and is a graduate from the Harvard Business School. He has a Bachelor’s degree in International Business from Flensburg University in Germany and also studied under Socrates at Clermont-Ferrand Business School in France. During his tenure at the Trade Commission he has focused on expanding and refining his skills in Innovation and Business Building, and has undergone yearly training in the Chasm methodology, Blue Ocean, sales, and leadership.

Sauer is a frequent speaker and involved in a number of community and leadership groups in the Atlanta area.

Jan Sauer’s Specialties:

Starting up high tech, med tech and clean tech firms in North America (from strategic planning to the details around legal, immigrations and accounting related issues), devising and implementing growth strategies using blue ocean and chasm institute, developing sales tactics and operations, coaching sales individuals, international branding, developing and implementing best practices operations processes within human capital driven businesses.
