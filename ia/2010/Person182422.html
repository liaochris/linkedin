Sara Hand’s Summary

Everthing Sara does involves building community. Whether working with an organization or a community initiative, she focuses on identifying strengths, developing leadership and the synnergies that develope through great connections. Sara’s background and experience across a variety of industries in the public and private sector; interesting combination of skill sets as a professional trainer, speaker, ballroom dance instructor and studio manager, as well as a counselor and coach make her a valuable team player and asset to clients she works with. Passionate about education and economic development, Sara believes strongly that even one person can make a difference through the “Power of Permission.” She encourages others to get involved and brings this unique perspective to the BarCamp Sarasota community building activities she organizes.
