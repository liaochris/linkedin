Rebecca Gill’s Summary

Rebecca has a well-rounded business background that began back in 1994 when she graduated from Central Michigan University’s School of Accounting. Upon receiving her degree, she began her career as an Operations Manager at Lowry Computer Products and focused on developing her skill-set in core business activities such as inventory management, purchasing and vendor negotiations, customer service, and accounting information systems.

Rebecca’s expertise quickly began to develop a much broader scope as she joined TGl as a Senior Consultant. At TGI she further cultivated her love of technology and strengthened her abilities in the project management, training, and consulting of enterprise software solutions. After leaving TGI she joined Delphi as a Sales Manager, where she received the division’s top sales award for achieving excellence in sales and customer satisfaction.

In more recent years Rebecca rejoined Technology Group International as Vice President of Marketing. Rebecca greatly expanded TGI’s branding and industry presence through the creation and execution of a strategic and highly focused Internet marketing campaign. These efforts brought TGI’s lead generation to new levels and helped provide the foundation for sales growth that exceeded 400% in a two-year period. Unable to quench this desire for technology-based marketing, Rebecca left TGI to start her own Internet marketing firm.

With the launch of Web Savvy Marketing, Rebecca is able to focus entirely on leveraging the Internet to help small and mid-market businesses (SMB) achieve the same exceptional sales growth she helped obtained at TGI. Web Savvy Marketing is a boutique marketing and public relations firm that specializes in search engine optimization, social media campaigns, pay-per-click management, affiliate marketing, and website design, analysis, and reporting.
