Kyle J. Britt’s Summary

Kyle J. Britt has been active in media and social marketing for over ten years. He is an experienced communicator, trainer, and analyst with a proven record of success in online brand management.

Kyle is an internationally published photographer, has been a producer for a nationally syndicated television show, has directed a music video airing on FUSE and the MTV networks, has directed live events for over 30,000 people, and has delivered over 10,000,000 page views for clients.

His wide range of experience and "jack-of-all-trades-ness" has given him great insight and led him to be known on every team he has worked as the “solutions guy.” Whenever there is a problem, Kyle will creatively and effectively solve it.
