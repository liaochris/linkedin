Traci Allen’s Summary

Veteran of the culinary and hospitality industry, DC native. Unique marketing perspective gained from extensive experience in restaurant operations, catering, event planning, marketing, sales and public relations accompanied with a fresh modern approach.

AOS Degree in Culinary Arts and BS in Food Service Management from Johnson and Wales University in Providence, RI.

In 1992 completed culinary internship with Walt Disney World at the Grand Floridian Beach Resort and Spa, rotating through 7 restaurants and convention center.

I had my daughter in 1993. In 1995, moved to Dallas, Texas, where I worked as Restaurant Manager and Banquet Manager at Bristol Hotels and Resorts for over two years.

In 1997 moved back to D.C. and worked six years with Capital Restaurant Concepts, as the Restaurant Manager of Paolo’s Georgetown and Georgia Browns and General Manager of Georgetown Seafood Grill.

2003 to 2007 worked as Director of Sales and Catering at Sea Catch Restaurant.

In 2007 formed Traci Allen Inc. a boutique, marketing • events • PR firm. We target and cater to the unique needs of each of our clients by incorporating a deep understanding of marketing, event planning and public relations from every perspective and an insider’s knowledge and commitment to the Washington D.C. community.

Active resident with a strong understanding of how the city operates, including the point people and organizations that can be key in executing successful events. Served on the Executive Board as an Affiliate Representative for the Washington Area Concierge Association (WACA) and currently serves on the Executive Board for the National Association of Catering Executives (NACE). Vice President of NACE, Greater Washington Potomac Chapter, a position held for two years. Longstanding member of the Restaurant Association of Metropolitan Washington and Private Dining Group of Washington. Serves on the Destination DC Partnership Advisory Panel.

Traci Allen’s Specialties:

Marketing, event planning, public relations, magement, consulting, sales, prospecting, tradeshows, conference management, writing and managing sales budgets, forecasting, press releases, media relations, press parties, client open houses, concept development, branding, product launch, advertising, commujnity outreach, restaurant training programs
