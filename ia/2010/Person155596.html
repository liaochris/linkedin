Nita Dusek’s Summary

While I have been passionate about photography all my life, I got my first paid assignment in January 2007 photographing architecture. That, in turn, prompted me to make it a business and in June of 2008, Nita Dusek Photography was born. I have attended many workshops and classes during the last three years as I have moved from architectural photography, to commercial photography, to my current love of portraiture, and specifically womens portraiture.

Currently, I am working towards earning my Masters degree through Professional Photographers of America. It takes a minimum of 3 years to attain, with the average being 5-6 years. If all continues to go well, I am on track to earn it in 4 years. Only about 4% of PPA members have their Masters Degree. International Print Competitions, classes, workshops, and service work are all components in earning your Masters degree. I also have a goal to become a Certified Photographer before the end of 2010. Less than 8% of all professional photographers are Certified.

Why women's photography? Women are especially critical of their appearance. I want to make each and every woman feel beautiful. The surprise and joy they have (not to mention their husbands joy) when they see their images is what motivates me. Every woman is beautiful no matter how curvy and deserves to feel that way.

Nita Dusek’s Specialties:

Tasteful, classic, glamorous, sexy and soulful portraits of women. I aim to highlight her favorite part of her body and camouflage those not so much appreciated.
