Sally StJohn’s Summary

Author and talk show host Sally StJohn has over ten years of experience as the on-air relationship/sex expert for many TV and radio networks. She earned her BA in psychology (1981-1985) and MS in counseling from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas (1986-1990), and a PhD in Psychological Counseling from LaSalle University, LA (1992-1995). She also earned her mediation credential through the Eighth Judicial District Court in Clark County, and has been the clinical director of the StJohn Success Center (formerly the Center for Holistic Rehabilitation) since 1996. A sought-after speaker, St. John lectures nationally on her publications: True Love, Vantage Point: How to Understand Yourself and Others Instantly, The Truth Reflex, The Instant Answer, and How to Find Your Soul Mate. Her purpose is to help others discover and transition into theirs.

Decision making, kinesiology, life coaching, resistant behaviors, relationship chemistry
