Joe Territo’s Summary

Managed development, implementation and maintenance of editorial and advertising products on 10 highly interactive local news and information web sites, in affiliation with 30 newspapers, including several major metro dailies.

Provided editorial and production direction, guidance and oversight to editors, general managers and production staffs of web sites, with offices at 10 locations across the U.S.

Set priorities, goals and editorial requirements, for corporate
design, production and technology staffs, including more than
two-dozen employees. Directly managed production staff of five full-time producers, two full-time HTML coders and freelancers.

Fulfilled requests for new features and products from corporate and local web site managers, working with them as clients.

Coordinate implementation and maintenance of vendor-hosted products. This sometimes included evaluating vendors and negotiating contracts for editorial products.

Edited and produced web site updates for Money.com, Pathfinder.com and Time.com. Wrote and produced news reports and interactive features for Money.com and Time.com.

Managed relationships between Pathfinder.com and multiple Time Inc. magazine web sites, including Fortune.com, Money.com, People.com, SportsIllustrated.com, Time.com, others.

Managed team of designers, producers and programmers to create, develop and launch Advance Internet's first local web site, NJ.com, in affiliation with three leading New Jersey newspapers, including TheStar-Ledger of Newark. Coordinated day-to-day web site operations and
development of new editorial features.

Reported on local, statewide and sometimes national news stories, primarily focusing on the most populated towns in suburban Morris County, NJ.
