B. Quentin Foster’s Summary

CRC is a real estate firm focused exclusively on investments for the high yield passive investor. We review 1,000's of deals and offer a wide yet selective pre-screened group of the most favorable offerings.

Our investment options include:

*TIC Tenant in Common Investments
Fractional co-ownership of large institutional quality properties with steady, secure income and appreciation

*NNN Net Leased Investments
Ownership of single tenant sale-leaseback properties with strong corporate/franchisee income guarantees

*LLC Partnership Interests
Partnership interests in higher equity return risk/reward balanced projects directed by experienced managers

Finding the best high yield passive real estate investment requires information, knowledge and experience. CRC acts as a clearing house to sift through the myriad of choices to select the best investments available; saving investors’ considerable time, energy and money.

Your choices are simplified. The process is streamlined. We help you zero in on deals that we believe deliver the lowest risk, highest possible cash on cash yield, and most reliable increase in value
