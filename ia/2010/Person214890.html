Wes Phillips’s Summary

With over a decade of experience in public relations and journalism, Wes Phillips has video games, consumer electronics and web technology covered from all sides. In addition to covering the video games space for years as a journalist, he has a broad scope of PR experience. This includes launching titles for best-selling video game franchises like Street Fighter and Command & Conquer to counseling startups in the interactive entertainment space to helping guide a Fortune 50 company into the social media generation.
