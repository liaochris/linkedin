Shelly DeMotte Kramer’s Summary

Shelly Kramer has spent her 20-year career in marketing working with small to medium sized companies, accelerating growth through highly effective marketing strategies and brand building initiatives. In addition to traditional agency/client relationships and a myriad of project management work, she has often worked with companies and functioned as their outsourced marketing department. This has been very effective and serves to not only stretch clients’ marketing dollars but, also allows them to integrate senior level marketing thinking into all aspects of their businesses and to take advantage of executive level management and leadership from a marketing standpoint.

Shelly has become well known for her ability to create buzz and awareness about her clients and their products or services in both traditional and non-traditional ways. She understands business, she understands target audiences and their behavior, and she is adept at putting all the puzzle pieces together to generate measurable results for her clients.

A huge area of focus is the Social Media realm and the integration of this tool into other marketing initiatives. Shelly is known for her no-nonsense approach to figuring out solutions and it’s clear that she loves every minute of what she does. She loathes the word ‘expert’ and tries to avoid using it at all costs, but when you ask her clients they will be the first to say that with Shelly’s years of experience, strategic thinking abilities in the world of integrated communications, having her on your team is a great asset to your business.
