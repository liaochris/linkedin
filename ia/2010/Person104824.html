Jordan Mitchell’s Summary

Since 1995, I've been growing Internet technology companies from their founding to profitability and market leadership positions. I'm highly motivated by large market opportunities, and attribute a pattern of success to strategic thinking, rigorous execution, clever innovation, hard work, passion, integrity, and intellect -- across all the people/teams I've been fortunate to work with. Since 2003 I've focused on online advertising, with a particular specialty in audience data collection, analysis, targeting, and optimization. I'm currently helping build the largest publisher-side yield optimization and audience data platform available on the Web.

Jordan Mitchell’s Specialties:
