Dr. Sourav Kundu’s Summary

Sourav Kundu has a Ph.D. in Non-Linear Control Engineering using Genetic Algorithms. Sourav started Knowledge Solutions Group Inc. in 2001. With office in Phoenix, (Arizona, USA), Kolkata, (India), Sydney (Australia), Dubai (UAE), Singapore and Tokyo (Japan), Knowledge Solutions Groups' expertise covers extensive business consulting, process improvement, business assurance and technology consulting areas. With vast experience both in Academia and Professional Services, solving Real-World problems, KSG offers Solutions to high profile customers worldwide.

Sourav's research interest cover Genetic Algorithms, Engineering Design Process, AI and Theoretical Biology, specifically The Price Equation applications in a Generalized Selection Theory. Sourav's personal Homepage is at http://www.sourav.org and Sourav's Blog is a http://blog.sourav.org
