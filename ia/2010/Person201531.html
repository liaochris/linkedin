    May 2004 — June 2006 (2 years 2 months)

  • Chief Software Architect

    Accelent Systems Inc

    (Computer Software industry)

    March 1998 — May 2004 (6 years 3 months)

    I provided essential innovation and technical guidance to help this technology start-up become a leading Systems Integrator specializing in the Microsoft Windows CE operating systems and ARM-based systems. I developed a framework of highly configurable embedded system software that provided rapid porting capabilities to widely diverse hardware designs and a large library of Windows CE OAL and device driver source code written using that framework. Managed the design and development of a series of code-sharing and configuration tools to organize, extract, and configure an arbitrary set of components from a library of sharable source code that executed a customizable build process of essentially any complexity to automatically generate a desired build target. I oversaw projects involving the creation and upgrading of board support packages to new versions of Windows CE. I managed the company's participation in Microsoft beta- and alpha- testing programs as well as the technical relationships with the Microsoft Mobility Partner programs and Microsoft Premier Support Services, and participated in Microsoft design reviews and product development team conferences to help shape future Microsoft product releases. I was instrumental in securing Accelent an invitation to the Microsoft Portability Advisory Council on which I served as a technical representative. I provided assistance to the legal staff on the wording and interpretation of the technical language in business contracts and other documents. I provided pre- and post-sales support, including demos, customer design reviews, project estimates, proposals, and developer technical support. I attended two, four-week "MBA Ultra-Lite" classes that taught various aspects of business operations and entrepreneurship.
