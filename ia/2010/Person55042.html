Derek Page’s Summary

I serve as a Wealth Advisor for The Private Bank at Wells Fargo. My team operates as a personal Chief Financial Officer (CFO) for high net worth individuals advising multi-millionaires regarding their comprehensive wealth management, investment, financial, trust & estate, credit, and private banking needs.

We consult and assist ultra-wealthy individuals to structure their assets in such a way as to optimize their investment portfolio, minimize estate and gift taxes, and effectively provide for their family and future generations.

We work with Fortune 500 corporate executives, professional athletes, entertainers, successful entrepreneurs and business owners, lottery winners, large settlement recipients, heirs to large inheritances, and multi-millionaires with complex and sophisticated financial and wealth management needs.
