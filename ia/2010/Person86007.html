Duane Addy’s Summary

An honors graduate of the Architectural Design program at Fanshawe in London, Duane originally started his career as a building designer in 1996 focusing on all aspects of architectural design – with work being completed in many sectors throughout North America, for projects that ranged in size from the small to the large and complex, and built a significant background in high-end residential.

For the past decade Duane has helped to build Imaginethis Media Studios Inc., into an award winning dynamic creative studio focusing on interactive marketing methods that cater to a variety of industries and has been the core leader in the development of all of its interactive technology.

Duane Addy’s Specialties:

Architectural Rendering - Interior - Exterior, Animated Walk-Throughs, I-Select Material Selection System, I-Tour Integrated Virtual Tour, I-MotionVR Interactive Media Center, 360 Walk Around, Web Site Development, CRM Database Development, Branding & Identity, Sales & Marketing Materials, Presentation & Packaging, Advertising Campaigns, CG Animation, Character Development, Green Screen Productions, DVD Production, Web Site Videos, Post Product Effects, Digital Matte Paintings.
