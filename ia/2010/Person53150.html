Dean Landeche’s Summary

Senior-level strategic marketing professional with leadership experience in business-to-business marketing of durable capital goods.

Named "Top 10 Business-to-Business Marketers" by B-to-B Magazine. Received William Marsteller Marketing Leadership Award, Business Marketing Association and Business Week magazine.

Marketing Function Leadership. Develops strategic marketing for complex durable capital goods through distribution. Developed industry-leading global "green" marketing initiatives. Led brand marketing “reinvention”, including development of premium and multibrand strategies resulting in increase in sales, share and margins.

Multi-Brand Marketing. Initiated brand and multi-brand collaboration to leverage brand leadership positions with national accounts and distribution, focusing on leadership, innovation, and other high-value attributes. Refocused marketing and advertising efforts from product orientation to brand and portfolio management, expanding reach to key audiences through effective use of integrated marketing programs targeted at industry thought leaders, key decision makers and end users.

New Product Development. Leads marketing research, insights and specification development for new products. Collaborated in pioneering and codification of a proprietary new product development process to accelerate successful product introductions through market insights, customer input, technology development and supply chain management, resulting in introduction of record number of new products.

E-Business and E-Marketing. Established and implemented e-business and internet-based marketing programs, including creation of key partner extranets, dealer-based sales channel for internet purchasers, and interactive programs designed to generate qualified sales leads and other inquiries.

Managing Training and Sales Support Functions.
Strategic Outsourcing and Change Management.
Employee Communication.
