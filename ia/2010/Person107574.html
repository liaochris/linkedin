Juan Diego Velasco’s Summary

I've been a web designer for more than ten years, learning almost everything from the experience of facing a problem and trying to solve it the better-looking way possible. I've decided to focus in this area of the Graphic Design because i consider it's the area that evolves the most and it's a great way to learn new things every day.

My first web projects where a mere excuse for learning new things. That's why i created expresorojo.com and circulodebaba.com (now offline, try the waybackmachine at archive.org). Along with the design process i learned that to take things one step further i had to learn coding. So i started with Perl/CGI, and later Coldfusion and Javascript.

Since then i've been writting in my blogs (eltatuajefalso.com and modernois.blogspot.com) and getting involved in any kind of project i can. Actually i'm part of two active web projects: gacetilla.org (design/layout/css/admin) and usarte.org (design/layout/admin). I also love photography, so you can check my flickr portfolio if you like (http://flickr.com/photos/tatuajefalso)
