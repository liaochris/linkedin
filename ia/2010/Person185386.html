Sean Kennedy Santos’s Summary

Creative Director and principle fotografia .
New York based with representation in Paris , London and Germany

With 10 years experience in big brand advertising combining skills in creative direction, principle photography and film direction, Santos has created award winning campaigns for the following clients –Holland Casinos, Mercedes Benz, British Airways, Harley Davidson, Sony, Puma, Nike, Morgan Hotels and Christies Great Estates in luxury property. Fashion branding is increasingly his new medium .

A comprehensive body of work speaks for him at www.sksantos.com
Santos is an ideas individual with energy. He is hired for his photographic eye, unique art direction and his cinematic vision, but principally because of the ideas and ability to put them in motion is key to each success .

Santos has a self motivated ability to take a blank canvas and produce new mood-driven
concepts which has seen the client list grow from strength to strength, award to award and now progressive thought to big production .
