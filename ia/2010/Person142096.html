Michael Bloom’s Summary

Versatile, flexible, highly innovative embedded-systems software engineer and team leader comfortable and successful in verification and development roles at both startup and established companies. Leverages comprehensive expertise in low level software/firmware that directly controls hardware and FPGAs in a variety of domains, including semiconductor verification, airborne reconnaissance/defense, telecommunications and networking platforms used in classical POTS and IP telephony, and storage arrays.

Energetically approaches new technologies and enthusiastically supports mature products serving general purpose and niche markets.

Michael Bloom’s Specialties:

Individual contributor, team lead, and liaison experience on bring-up, verification, and enhancement development projects for embedded chips and boards, as well as large system sustaining efforts, in commercial and defense arenas.

ASIC/FPGA post silicon verification: Serial RapidIO, HyperTransport, PCI Express, Infiniband, Fibre Channel; message appliances; SIP/telephony; airborne reconaissance; routers and media gateways; embedded Linux; vxWorks; low level software and firmware.
