Andrew Thompson’s Summary

Andrew has spent over twenty years in the IT industry, starting off on IBM mainframes, before moving into the contracting market as a Java programmer.

After the 'Year 2000' challenge was debunked he went in a new direction in speech recognition systems, and was a main contributor to the success of the Nationwide Building Society 'Say Anything' enquiry line. You can try it by phoning 0800 30 20 10. Andrew also had a major part to play in the Avon Cosmetics representative ordering line as a Technical Consultant.

Having spent many years debugging his own & other peoples code, Andrew's goal is to now help development teams to reduce this arduous chore by spreading the word about Automated Defect Prevention.

Andrew Thompson’s Specialties:

Pre-sales consultancy; Post sales support, Account Managment; Communication at both the Managerial & Technical levels.
