Nic Faitos’s Summary

Hello Everyone!

Thank you for visiting our page! I would like to welcome you to this gateway to Starbright Floral Design. Our purpose here is to network, meet people, sell lots of flowers and hopefully be able to reciprocate with the services that we buy as individuals and as a company from those who we meet online through Plaxo.

We all need great lawyers, real estate agents, accountants, travel agents, and yes FLORISTS TOO! We hope you take the time to see our photo albums, visit our website (www.starflor.com), email me any questions that you have (nic@starflor.com) - we will always be happy to hear from you.

Read on....

I am a florist! In September of 1994 I started Starbright Floral Design as a concept florist that would be a little different than what our industry is used to.

Starbright is a designed as an organization that directs its efforts to the corporate and institutional client.

You would want to connect to me (for business purposes and networking) if you are looking for contacts in floral industry and decor. We can assist with:

Event decor for special events (we have worked at pretty much every venue in New York City).


I would like to invite you to visit our website at www.starflor.com. If you go to our section entitled EVENT PORTFOLIO, you will see a large variety of florals and design styles that we have created over a little more than the past decade.

In the SEND A STARBRIGHT GIFT section, you will be able to send flowers (or other gifts), to your friends or clients anywhere in the US or around the world.

Human Resources Departments of many of our corporate clients use us to place orders for florals and gifts that are sent to the employees of their organizations for their special occasions. If your company has a flower policy of corporate and employee gifts, we will be more than happy to assist.

We will always be happy to hear from you and to help in every way possible! Stay in touch!!!

Nic Faitos’s Specialties:

Our work is mainly at the corporate and institutional level. Roughly half our work is driven by Corporate Events. Another large portion is florals and gifts for the Hospitality Industry (Concierge Desks), Weekly florals for offices, restaurants and lobbies, etc.
