Rohn Jay Miller’s Summary

I lead teams that solve the strategic communication challenges businesses face in the new social media landscape. I'm an experienced executive who manages internal organizational change and communications and collaborates on the messaging and creative branding companies go to market with.

We do this by integrating customer research, strategic planning and creative design into a single process. This brings strategists and designers together to deliver brilliant creative work + clear, measurable results connecting marketing, sales, customer service and product development.

I'm a former Partner in USWeb/Ikonic, San Francisco, SVP - Product + Technology, Knight Ridder Newspapers. Currently I'm Strategic Director, IconNicholson LBi, New York City, part of LBi Group AB.
