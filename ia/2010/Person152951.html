Neil Peterson’s Summary

As Vice President of Sales and Marketing at Hybricon, Mr. Peterson was responsible for Sales, Business Development and Marketing of Hybricon’s embedded high performance computing hardware and services. Hybricon is a leading supplier of innovative Systems Packaging for Rugged Embedded Computing solutions serving the Military, Aerospace, Medical and high-end Industrial markets.

Mr. Peterson is also Chairman of the VITA; VPX/OpenVPX Marketing Alliance (VMA), tasked with establishing an ecosystem of interested parties that promote and create name recognition, as well as to grow adoption of the VPX /OpenVPX specifications and technology. The alliance is responsible for educating, training, and promoting the potential capabilities of the VPX family of specifications and informing the broader community and press.

Mr. Peterson demonstrates 27 years of experience in tech companies with the last 20 years focused on WW sales, marketing, business development, sales management/process, P&L, organization development, intellectual property/product licensing, and partnership development. He also possesses strong leadership capabilities and people management skills, plus excellent communication and interpersonal skills at all levels. Prior to Hybricon he was the VP of WW Sales and Business Development at QNX Software Systems. QNX is a Embedded Systems Development Company; Real-time operating systems, development tools, middleware and services for embedded applications targeted at large OEMs in the Auto, Netcom, Med, IA and Mil markets. He also served as Sr VP of Sales/Marketing and GM of NetSilicon's Intelligent Devices Div, a provider of integrated system-on-silicon and software solutions for OEMs of networked enabled devices.
