Richard Verdoni’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur with many skills and experience in creativity and technology development. I've worked in the entertainment, video game industry as well as film, TV production, travel, healthcare and business service industries.

I started company that develops iPad applications and we aim to be a big part of the iPad/mobile app industry. I'm excited about the innovation potential within the areas of technology, augmented reality, and communication using a new platform.

I was born into one of Mexico's most famous family circuses, oddly enough my birth was in Muskegon Michigan - the circus "El Circo Atayde Hnos." is celebrating 120 years in 2008.

I grew up in Mexico City and the United States and learned how to use all of my creative skills to help people give life to their ideas.

Someone once told me that creativity, with the best intentions, can yield some great work.

With imagination and skill, from mind to hand - a translation outward with full desire can express the message and idea with passion.
