    1. Encourage and support research to reveal causes and discover effective treatments of neuroblastoma.
    2. Provide services to children with neuroblastoma and their families.
    3. Educate the public and healthcare providers about the disease.
    4. To provide a forum for patients and family members to share information concerning medical treatment, palliative care, hospice services, bereavement groups and emotional support.
