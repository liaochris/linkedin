Chris Klimecky’s Summary

15 year veteran of the videogame development industry with experience in developer and publisher side production, consoles and PC, licensed and original IP, children's and mature content, action and strategy, and more!

Chris Klimecky’s Experience

  • Executive Producer

    Smith & Tinker, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    April 2010 — Present (3 months)

    Responsible for development of future versions of the Nanovor franchise. www.nanovor.com

  • Producer

    Warner Bros. Games

    (Public Company; TWX; Entertainment industry)

    October 2009 — January 2010 (4 months)

    After the WB buyout of Midway/Surreal, I was moved to Snowblind Studios (part of WB Games-Seattle) to work on Lord of the Rings: War in the North managing cinematic development, coordinating with WB shared services' cinematics team.

  • Senior Producer

    Surreal Software/Midway Games

    (Public Company; MWY; Computer Games industry)

    June 2003 — September 2009 (6 years 4 months)

    Led a 100+ member team developing This is Vegas, an open world action game for Xbox 360/PS3/PC using Unreal 3. Led establishment of Midway-wide tech and art sharing production responsibilities. Shipped The Suffering and The Suffering: Ties That Bind for Xbox/PS2/PC (All US & FIGS). Established studio-wide planning, scheduling, and production strategy. Built internal production, localization, and QA teams from scratch. Responsible for publisher and executive communication, marketing coordination, dev. team leadership, 3rd party contract negotiation, and dept. leads problem-solving. Oversaw making builds on all platforms while driving localization and QA for simultaneous release (20 versions, 15 skus).

  • Associate Program Manager (contract)

    FASA Studio/Microsoft

    (Public Company; MSFT; Computer Software industry)

    February 2002 — February 2003 (1 year 1 month)

    Shipped MechAssault for Xbox/Xbox Live– drove internal and external teams along with project PM under intense time and company pressure. Managed Xbox Live areas and certification of the game for Live launch. Led intricate matrix of additional content, demos, and Xbox Live feature updates. Also owned team schedules on an untitled internal AAA open world action game Xbox project and led the re-design/launch of the FASA Studio website.

  • Producer

    Humongous Entertainment

    (Public Company; Computer Games industry)

    January 2000 — July 2002 (2 years 7 months)

    Led internal and external teams for Backyard Sports projects: PC - Backyard Golf, PS1 - Backyard Football, and Gameboy Color - Backyard Football and Backyard Baseball. Created and presented multiple concepts for franchise expansion and started relationships with professional leagues, including the NHL for Backyard Hockey. Drove improvement of company efficiency during the Atari (then Infogrames) buyout by leading restructuring, mentoring, and redefining roles of the production department. Also shipped Blue’s Clues Reading Time Activities.

  • Associate Producer

    Media Station

    (Computer Games industry)

    May 1994 — September 1999 (5 years 5 months)

    Producer and Lead Designer from concept to ship on PC games Stuart Little: Big City Adventures and Rocky and Bullwinkle’s Know-It-All Quiz Game. Associate Producer and game design roles on hardcore strategy PC games Crags and Extreme Tactics as well as multiple online projects. Sound Director and localization work on best selling and award winning titles such as The Lion King Animated Storybook, 101 Dalmatians Animated Storybook, Ariel’s Story Studio, Fractured Fairytales: The Frog Prince, Barbie as Rapunzel, Tonka Search & Rescue, and many more!
