Chris Munro’s Summary

Overview - A proven, transformational, entrepreneurial international CEO, board member, and private equity partner. Mr.Munro has designed, developed, led and repositioned public, private and private equity owned $200m to $6bn product, service, logistics and manufacturing businesses in Western Europe, Asia Pacific and the Americas. He possesses an exemplary track record of increasing sustainable stakeholder value based upon strong execution and governance skills coupled with strategic repositioning, acquisitions/disposals, deep customer relationships and developing high performing process orientated leadership and board teams.

Core Skills/Background - Mr. Munro demonstrates proven transformational leadership of businesses at all points in their business maturity cycle. As a proven international CEO/Chairman he possesses a deep understanding of driving company leadership at national, international and global levels which involve government/lobby groups and new country entry, governance and development plans.

Currently as Private Equity Managing Director/Operating Partner of a midsized fund Fenway Partners he has helped design, acquire and operate four North American logistics platform concepts. Most recently, as CEO of a newly acquired portfolio, Mr. Munro has led and sponsored the build out of a $500m group to become the North American intermodal logistics market leader.

Munros last three private equity experiences highlight success in designing and executing high impact strategies. He has built teams around investment theses, and has sourced and closed 8 self-generated transactions. This has been done in public, private as well as inside distressed and chapter 11settings. He has delivered sustainable growth engines and lasting high performing leadership teams in 4 continents. The end results have been measured in hundreds of millions of dollars of shareholder value.

He is an active member and network chair of YPO, and CEON.
