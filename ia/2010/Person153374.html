    (Philanthropy industry)

    July 2008 — January 2010 (1 year 7 months)

    The Newson Foundation was formed to address many issues faced by individuals and families struggling with addiction. One of the Foundation’s initial goals was to develop the infrastructure needed to create a fund that would scholarship individuals who are ready for treatment, but could not afford the large costs of the better treatment facilities. The Foundation’s mission has evolved and now explicitly states that we combat drug addiction through Prevention, Harm Reduction, and Recovery.

    The Newson Foundation also supports continued research in the field of addiction treatment and more specifically, those programs focused on crystal meth addiction. The Foundation is a privately funded philanthropic institution for good.

    The Foundation’s Board of Directors is composed of leaders in the medical community, business community, addiction treatment community, and lead by Mr. Newson’s vision to see real people into real recovery.
