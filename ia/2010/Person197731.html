Accredited Public Relations professional with Canadian Public Relations Society (CPRS).

Collaborative, strategic-doer with capacity to handle complex agendas and topics.

Ability to effectively counsel clients using strategy, research and tactics with focus on results.

Experience integrating higher level of Communications and PR function with Marketing, Operations and HR while at Future Shop over 1-2 year period.

Managed global PR team for technology company, PMC-Sierra over 7 years.

Identified and leveraged new cost effective online and social media methods to build and protect reputations as well as build customer relationships and generate new leads.
