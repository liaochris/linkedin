Alex Kaufman’s Summary

Seasoned marketing, communications and ski industry professional. 30 years old and well traveled with diverse marketing and operational awareness. Riding the social media wave with style. 33 different logins and tries to listen and participate on them all. Speaks terrain park to athletes, media and staff. Relations leader between resort and local chambers, state agencies, non-profit groups, etc.

Armed with large rolodex of contacts and marketing best practices thanks to key role at major drive market resorts in VT, NH, ME and OR. Former resident of Boulder and CO I-70 mountain corridor. Managed crisis communications from skier death to flash flooding. Entertains crowds of thousands on the microphone. Initiated resort coverage in NYT, GMA, CNN, ESPN and more.
