Rich Connors’s Summary

VISTA TECHNOLOGY was established in 1991 for the sole purpose of recruiting Life Science Professionals for the Biotechnology / BioPharmaceutical / BioEnergy / Medical Device industry. As a pioneer in the Biotechnology search field, VISTA TECHNOLOGY continues to lead the way in finding qualified talent for the "hard-to-fill positions". Our Company is based in Massachusetts, home to over 350 Biotechnology / BioPharmaceutical Companies.

The types of Companies we do search and recruitment for include start-up companies (< 50 employees) to mid size to well established Biotechnology and Pharmaceutical Companies.

The types of positions VISTA TECHNOLOGY recruits for include Research (Biologists, Chemists, Informatics, Immunologists and Pharm/Tox); PreClinical Development; Clinical Research (CRA's, CDM, Biostatisticians, Regulatory Affairs, Medical Directors and Drug Safety / Pharmacovigilance); QA and QC; Engineers, Manufacturing and Process Development; Business Development and some Marketing and Administration.

Level of positions range from individual contributor to Vice President.

MYnextjobchange.com was established in October of 2009 to respond to the challenge of assisting "Job Seekers" who have been referred to us that do not work in the industry we recruit for.

MYnextjobchange.com will assist ALL job seekers, regardless of which industry you work in (computers, retail, manufacturing, healthcare, etc.); what your position is (accountant, buyer, engineer, IT, etc.); how many years of experience you have (entry level to Director to C-level); or where you are located (United States or Overseas).

MYnextjobchange.com is a revolutionary website that centralizes 100% of the tools, resources, and information the Active or the Passive "Job Seeker" requires to conduct a thorough professional successful job search.
