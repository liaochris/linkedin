Mike Smith’s Summary

I am a freelance writer with 35 years of engineering work and leadership, most recently with R&D for telecommunications groups as full time employee, equity participant and/or consultant. I have consulted for early stage companies. I have assisted new start ups or early stage companies with innovation, development of resources, coaching for executive levels and networking. I have done freelance writing for newspapers, magazines and various businesses. One of several of my books is nearing completion.

I can help you with lots of experience behind my freelance writing. I'm also a top notch research and editor. I'm always looking for new areas in which to write. See samples of my work at http://www.gulfshorewriter.com.
