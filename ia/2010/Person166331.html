Maureen McCann, Career Strategist’s Summary

An inspiring, resourceful, and well-organized master certified resume, interview, and career strategist, Maureen McCann is passionate about communicating the value of business leaders. Maureen works with clients to develop the documents and interview skills required for success. A strategic thinker and writer, Maureen is an expert in forging open and honest communication between clients and colleagues and engendering strong partnerships.

Maureen holds the role of senior advisor and member development chair for Career Professionals of Canada. A highly proactive professional, she founded and moderates a tele-networking program for practitioners across Canada that provides professional development, strategic advice, and support for Canadian resume writers and career consultants.

As a testament to her leadership Maureen was honoured by the Career Professionals of Canada with six Award of Excellence for the past four years. Her resume writing talents were honoured with awards for:

* Best Canadian Online Profile
* Best Canadian Technical Resume
* Best Canadian Creative Resume
* Best Canadian New Graduate Resume

In 2010 Maureen was honoured with an International award from Career Director's Internationals. Toast of the Resume Industry (TORI).

* Best Career Change Resume

As a respected leader in the Canadian career industry, Maureen has been a central speaker at Cannexus 2010, the national career development conference, Opportunities 2008 “Workforce 2010,” the Ontario-wide conference for the employment community; Ottawa Citizen Job Fair 2008; and National Career Event for IT Professionals 2007. Participants and organizers alike enthusiastically receive her presentations, with their messages of empowerment and action.

As your dedicated career strategist, Maureen will draw upon her in-depth knowledge, skill, and experience, to position you for success. Her passion will inspire, her ethic will convince, and her results will impress.
