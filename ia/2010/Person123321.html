Lee-Ann Knowles’s Experience

  • Manager: Printing Services

    Rhodes University

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    May 2007 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    Management of the entire unit, including the Stationery section, where stock and assets are valued over R2.5 million. I supervise and manage 9 staff members: 1 clerk, 2 financial staff, 3 factory technicians, 2 graphic designers and 1 specialised computer technician.
    The Unit is responsible for the production of the printing requirements for the whole University and the facilitation of the production of any print matter which may need to be outsourced.
    We offer services in a copy-shop type function and also in large format production and laminating. A comprehensive design service is also on offer.
    In addition to the printing & production aspect, the Unit fulfills a resale function in terms of stationery and office supplies needed on campus. The Unit buys bulk stationery and resells to the departments & institutes within the University at a small profit. All aspects of stock management and process are ultimately my responsibility. As the Unit is entirely self-funded, the full financial management is also neccesary, budgets, asset management and accounting, cost centre management, costing exercises and regular financial reporting is done from within the Unit.
