Eric Snelz’s Summary

My primary experience was with print related companies. Most of my background was with Moore Corp where I went from Sales Rep to ultimately as a Regional VP for their BCS division (Think of who sends you your bank statements, 1099's and all of those credit card offers). I also worked at Intraform which was a regional print distributorship based in Phoenix, Arizona.

Currently I am a partner at Defero which is a digital agency that straddles the world of traditonal marketing and tech to deliver "MarTech" solutions to clients. Essentially we use web based solutions to deliver the right message, across the right medium, to the right audience at the right time.

My primary goal is to keep growing this company. But my avocation is golf where I struggle with my putting!
