Rick Bauer’s Summary

From data to information, from technology to learning, from technology to the core business, my skills and talents have taken me to many interesting roles, but they have a common denominator in this--being able to distill the key insights of technology, and allow strategic thinkers to make good decisions in another world with the benefit of that insight.

I marvel at the phenomenal growth of digital storage, and how storage is transforming the world around us. From managing fast-paced production environments and data centers, to introducing technology in education, to serving as CIO for one of the first schools where every student owned a notebook computer--it has been a rare privilege to watch human endeavor aided and transformed by various technologies.

Long term goals include technology and business management wherever there is a challenge.

Rick Bauer’s Specialties:

Business alignment of technology, data center design, green IT, web project management, consumer/SOHO storage, bilingual in Spanish/English, educational technology and rich media development.
