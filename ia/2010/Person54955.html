Derek Duke Noble’s Summary

Derek Duke has over 20 years of experience in the fitness and wellness industry as a health & fitness wellness coach, creative fitness consultant, fitness lifestyle tv producer & on air talent, marketing and brand strategist, spokesperson, fitness author & corporate wellness speaker.

Coach Noble personally offered his expertise and services to many A-List celebrities such as John McEnroe, Tatum O'Neal, Maria Shriver, Michael J. Fox, Rutger Hauer, John Cusack, as well as Royal clients from around the world.

Derek Duke Noble has also created, produced and hosted an internationally distributed hip, stylized fitness lifestyle tv series called Urban Fitness TV. It has aired on Discovery Channel's Fit TV, Canada's One TV, Australia's Foxtel and in Israel. Urban Fitness TV series (26X30 min) is currently available for tv and online media distribution.

Noble's list of TV credits include CNN’s Headline News, ABC, NBC, CBS, FOX, KTLA’s Morning Show, TLC, CBC and CTV. He has motivated millions of people with his fitness and lifestyle expertise on various radio programs worldwide.

Coach Derek Duke Noble is also the best selling author of The Body Noble - a fitness lifestyle book that provides you with all the fitness lifestyle tools to stay in shape anywhere and anytime.
Noble also had the opportunity to speak at Microsoft Campus, Amazon Campus and First Canadian Place to rave reviews during his corporate wellness speaking tour.

Derek Duke recently consulted with the leader in Swim Spa's - Dimension One to create their new consumer aquafit workout dvd and their new Professional Aquatic Fitness Systems scheduled to release summer 2009.

Noble is excited to announce his new Body Noble Transformational Health, Fitness & Lifestyle Programs located in New York City. Coach Noble's programs are designed to heal and transform your entire life.
