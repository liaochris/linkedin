Wilson Kerr’s Summary

Wilson brings 10+ years experience in the Location Based Services industry to bear and specializes in the placement of branded locations and the rapidly growing area of social media marketing.

Formerly Business Development Director for Tele Atlas North America, Wilson has expertise in mobile marketing, product development, content aggregation, and licensing across a broad range of navigation technologies (In-Car Nav, Personal Nav, and Wireless). He has worked with top tier brands, retailers, content providers, device manufacturers, and application/platform developers.
