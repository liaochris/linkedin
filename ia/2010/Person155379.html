Nina Hale’s Summary

I own an agency for Search Engine Marketing. With twelve employees and a number of contractors dedicated to search marketing, that's a lot of high-level search power. We integrate website search engine optimization and paid search marketing with website analytics, or provide these services as single tactics to our clients. We utilize social media to build reputation and links. We're crazy about localization and getting you in the map listings! We work directly with companies in both B2B and B2C arenas, and also partner with local website developers and agencies to provide advanced expertise in Search to their clients. Common acronyms in our business are SEO, PPC, and SEM.
