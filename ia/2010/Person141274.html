Merlin Mann’s Summary

Merlin is the publisher and creator of [43Folders.com][] as well as the man behind [Inbox Zero][], the [Hipster PDA][], the [Procrastination Dash][], and the popular essay, "[Better][]."

Merlin also co-hosts the cult comedy podcast, [You Look Nice Today][], and is a frequent guest on the much-loved [MacBreak Weekly][] podcast.

Merlin's other projects include [5ives][], [That Phone Guy][], [The Merlin Show][], and, of course, his popular personal blog, [Kung Fu Grippe][].

Merlin's been called a "productivity guru," "a king of nerds," and "a student of the creative mind." While none of those is inaccurate, Merlin is primarily a guy who loves to write and is always looking for new, entertaining, and thoughtful ways to help people get better at whatever they care most about.

Merlin's also available [for-hire][] as a speaker, writer, or advising consultant, to help you or your organization get better at whatever it is YOU care most about.
