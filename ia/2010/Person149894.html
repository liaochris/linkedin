Mitchell Stern’s Summary

Mr. Stern is a Managing Director in the firm’s Healthcare Group. With close to 20 years’ experience, Mr. Stern has worked with numerous clients ranging from start-up companies to multi-billion dollar conglomerates in various areas of the healthcare industry, including biopharmaceuticals, medical devices/supplies, nutraceuticals, healthcare services and healthcare IT. Prior to joining Morgan Joseph, Mr. Stern held positions at SMH Capital Inc., Glocap Advisors LLC, Needham & Company, Inc., Schroder & Co. Inc. and Salomon Brothers. Mr. Stern also worked as a C.P.A. with Deloitte & Touche and Ernst & Whinney. Mr. Stern holds a Master of Business Administration from New York University's Stern School of Business and a Bachelor of Arts degree from Franklin & Marshall College.
