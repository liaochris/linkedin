Kristy Naddell’s Summary

8 yrs of professional sales experience with proven performance in the financial services sector, hospitality, mobile marketing industry and internet advertising sector. Following my college graduation, I moved to New York City & obtained a Series 7 and 66 and started my career on Wall Street. My first job was at a smaller, "Boutique Firm" as a broker. After nearly a year of pure cold calling (mind you this is AFTER the tech bubble burst in 2002) I finally got a job at the well respected Merrill Lynch, in the Private Wealth Mgmt Division. While I was doing well at Merrill, I felt my dream of working on Wall Street might not be what I really wanted. I took a huge risk & moved to Florida without another position lined up, without any friend or family in Miami, I just "went for it!" After a short stint selling bonds for Israel Bonds, and starting my own consulting business, I met John Ferber who inspired me to change directions in my career path. I started learning about online advertising & immediately became obsessed with it. Slowly, I moved into a position with one of Ferber’s internet start-ups, a Mobile Marketing company called Cellware (aka MyMojo). After a few months, I was promoted to Business Development Mgr, and was responsible for negotiating all new content partners for the site as well as fostering new business relationships.
In April, 2007, Vandelay Industries launched USO Networks. I became Sales Director and was responsible for generating new client accounts. As we grew, and started offering more solutions, I was promoted to VP of Sales.

We have truly developed an Enterprise Solution for Video Production and VSEO.

The results that we generate using our propriety technology platform combined with the 4 years of research and development in understanding video search cannot be matched by our competitors to date. Due to client demand, we have expanded our service offering to include: SEO, SEM/PPC, Social, Blogs, News, Etc.
