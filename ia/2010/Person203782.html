Tim Dooley’s Summary

Coaching you to financial freedom:
Any successful venture involves making informed decisions. With over 3,000 franchising concepts to choose from, you want to be absolutely certain you’ve found the right match. With a proven record of matching individuals with business concepts, Tim Dooley gives you the insight you need to succeed.

Proven Track Record:
With experience across a range of industries, markets and economic climates, Tim has decades of success in the corporate and start-up world.

Some highlights include:
* Sylvan Learning Center, a start-up tutoring franchise with 14 units, exceeded revenue objectives by 28%. Within 9 months, Tim had increased Sylvan’s franchise base by 400% and contributed 69% of the total corporate revenues.

Why waste your time or investment dollars on a venture that may not be suited for your personality, experience, or skill set.
