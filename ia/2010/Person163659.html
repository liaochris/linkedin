Philip Stanley’s Summary

Business Coaching, Communications and Accelerated Change.

20 years advising and building communicative, multi-discipline teams to work smarter, crush problems and find creative business answers when there are none. There's always one.

Retail design and merchandising for Conrans, design consultancy, life and pensions for HBOS plc, PFI pathfinder projects in education outsourcing, corporate comms for Old Mutual plc, change initiatives in public engineering for multi-million pound local government contracts, and helping small businesses grow.

In the middle I was cofounder of a company called Newspath, grew a two-page brief into a Broadcast news video trading exchange as CTO and got everyone excited in 2000 (well, who knew?). Boy did I work. Where was my coach? I needed one of me now, then.

This was just after designing and delivering Bulgari suitcases for Cadillac's Imaj concept car launch at the Geneva Motor Show, to a crippling deadline.

Art and science is now tuned to rallying the disenchanted, helping small businesses and big executives get it together faster and keep it together longer.

A constant in this? Really, really fast, creative clarification for others.
Yes. No more the white charger.

Now I'm just the egg white in your consommé.

Philip Stanley’s Specialties:

Coaching and support, problem clarification, communication and change, concept translation and realisation, thought leadership and challenge, bigsmall tech, morale and performance.
