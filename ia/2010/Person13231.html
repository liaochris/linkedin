Angelo Cirocco’s Summary

Software engineer with MBA education and ten years work experience in system integration, application management, human resources, outsourcing services and sales.

As job rotation experience, he spend one year in HR dept. Thereafter, due to passion for the business and sales, he joined the IBM Global Technology Services organisation as business developer and services sales manager for Strategic Outsourcing.
Recently, he was appointed as Executive Assistant of IBM Italy Country General Manager.
Visionary, enthusiastic, fast learner with a continuing passion for technology, business, finance and market. Reliable, customer orientated and innovation promoter.
