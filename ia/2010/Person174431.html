Robert A Pabich’s Summary

Since the inception of my first small business, I tasted the freedom and flexibility associated with entrepreneurship. Seeking new challenges and avenues, and strategically analyzing the direction I wish to navigate present and future ventures, lead me to pursuing opportunities in the real estate industry.

Currently, I am a real estate professional based in the Greater Hartford area of Connecticut (CT) in the United States, with a motivation of expanding my reach into other reciprocal license states. I'm particularly focused on cultivating new relationships and opportunities in the New York City, Miami, FL and Boston, MA markets. In addition, opportunities in Europe, Asia, Central America, South America and United Arab Emirates are always welcome.

I'm always eager to develop new business relationships and add to my network of professional real estate contacts, including brokers, investors, developers, realtors, agents, mortgage professionals and realty companies.
