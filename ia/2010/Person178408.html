sean jackson’s Summary

As president of hotflops, inc I am part of a very exciting consumer product that actually brings innovation instead of imitation to the marketplace. Our product literally makes people smile and we are enjoying the early stages of a startup company that is seeing dramatic growth now entering its third year.

Past experience and expertise includes 14 years with Wal*Mart Stores, Inc as a General Manager running Sam's Club operations as well as a corporate HR Manager for 13,000 employees. Upon leaving WMT, I was involved with an exciting dotcom startup out of New York, Kozmo, and went on to run operations in DC and then launched the European expansion in London... well almost, that was 2000/2001 when the bubble burst. I have served as East Coast Manager for Distribution with Hollywood Entertainment in their Tennessee facility where I led the launch of their Game Crazy distribution format. I worked for a while as an independent sales rep placing products for manufacturers at Wal*Mart Stores, Inc. In 2005 I began my entrepreneurial venture with my investor/partner in a collegiate licensed apparel business. In March of 2008, we acquired hotflops.

sean jackson’s Specialties:

Specialty areas are anything project related... startups, relocations, turnarounds, shut downs, etc. Most of my background has been in executive leadership, building and guiding teams encompassing all areas of business.
