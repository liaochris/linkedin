Eric Stiens’s Summary

A little of this, a little of that. Always looking for a project or two that makes the world a better place. Have experience in all parts of non-profits -- from program design/eval to grantwriting, from designing a contact management database to upgrading a CMS, from door-to-door organizing to heart-to-heart talks to twitter and facebook advocacy, from paying the bills to cleaning the toilets. Especially interested in racial justice work, work around urban sustainability, and work with youth living in poverty.

Research/Writing:

Freelance social science research focusing on social problems. Proficient with a variety of quantitative and qualitative methodologies and system dynamics modeling. Multiple publications (from op-eds to book chapters), experience ghostwriting.

Design/Web:

Freelance web work for 10+ years. Can hand code, use Dreamweaver, mostly front end work, but can oversee integration with databases. Specialize in small projects and rapid turnaround.

Group work/Social work:
Experienced with group facilitation, conflict resolution and consensus-based decision making. Have worked with at-risk youth in various capacities for 3+ years.
