Dena Rosko’s Summary

Currently I attend Gonzaga University as a Graduate Student to study Communication and Leadership. I graduated with a BA in English and Communication with an emphasis on mass media studies and creative writing from the University of Washington.

I aim to devote myself (1 Tim. 4:14-16) to learning and living God's calling for me (i.e. a vocation), and have high interest in working as a professor, among other roles involving these action research areas: Ethics, Restorative Leadership, Power Dynamics, Biblical Justice, Autoethnography, Creative Nonfiction, Community Development, and Editorial Event Photography.

I desire to love Jesus with my life and to live by a vocation in the classic sense. I am married to James.
