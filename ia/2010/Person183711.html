Steven Feldman’s Summary

As team lead for Bing, Steven oversees planning and buying across all media types, and heads up a media team of 12 members.

The Bing account is quite unique in that all media is measured extensively in terms of its ability to drive movement amongst key performance indicators. As team lead, Steven oversees this entire process. This includes using advanced econometric modeling to help guide investment and predict campaign performance, real-time optimization of plans across all media types, as well as developing strategies for modeling new media types.

Steven is a seasoned agency professional, with extensive media experience spanning 12 years. This includes a unique combination of both strategic planning and buying across all media types including Online, TV, Radio, Print, Cinema, OOH and Emerging. He has worked on many blue chip brands such as Microsoft, Coca Cola, and Converse.

Steven Feldman’s Specialties:

In depth knowledge of TV planning and buying process, including local and national, traditional and emerging, online and offline.

Online planning and buying across mutliple channels and tactics including High Impact, Video, DR, Demand Gen, and Display.
