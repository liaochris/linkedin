Merold Mueller’s Summary

Dr. Mueller has received his biology diploma (1989) and doctorate in molecular Biology (equal Ph.D.) (2000) at the Free University Berlin. He has worked as a student and scientist in several prestigious laboratories [including Max-Planck Institute for molecular Biology (Berlin) (Wittmann Lab, Hardesty Lab, Luehrmann Lab), The Free University Berlin developmental biology department (Berlin) (Pfannenstiel Lab), Max Planck Institute for Biochemistry (Martinsried, Munich) (Sänger Lab, Huber Lab, Ullrich Lab), HelmholtzZentrum München (Munch Lab), at the Technical University München in Freising (Wenzel Lab)]. During his academic career he taught and supervised students, scientists at BSc, MSc, and PhD levels. He has been a senior scientist, lecturer and head of research groups. During his long research career he established international collaborations and was awarded german grants.

Founded a biotech company (IKINOWO GmbH) (2007).

Additional work includes being an independent scientific advisor for example for patent research and scientific movies.
