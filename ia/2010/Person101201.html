Joe Stampone’s Summary

Joe Stampone received his undergraduate degree in Economics and Entrepreneurial Leadership from Tufts University. While at Tufts, he was involved with the young entrepreneurs at Tufts club and was a four-year varsity soccer player. After graduating, he went on to intern for FJ3 Development, a small real estate investment firm. With FJ3 he worked as an analyst focusing on drafting pro formas and conducting market feasibility analyses.

More recently, Joe’s passion for real estate and writing has become evident in his blogging. Joe is the founder of A Student of the Real Estate Game, a premier blog for real estate education. There, he has worked diligently to provide readers with a comprehensive educational base for the real estate industry.

Joe Stampone is also a masters student at the NYU Schack Institute of Real Estate, taking classes full-time with a focus in real estate development. His curriculum covers disciplines including real estate finance, real estate development, and real estate legal principals and practices.

After the program he hopes to get involved with sustainable real estate development, investment, and management.

Besides being passionate about real estate, Joe enjoys sports, boating, spending time with his family and friends, and traveling.
