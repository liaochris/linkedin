Joshua Steimle’s Summary

Since 1999 I've owned and managed MWI, a firm providing search engine optimization, web development (mostly content management systems), and high-end web design from offices in Salt Lake City, Utah to clients in California, New York, Virginia, Boston, Reno, Utah, and Idaho. Clients include 3Com, Altiris, Bank of American Fork, Brighton Ski Resort, Connect Magazine, Extra Space Storage, FranklinCovey, Garage Technology Ventures, Hubble Homes, Sony, Sorenson Capital, the law firm of Strong & Hanni, Wasatch Venture Fund, Utah's Hogle Zoo, and Zions Bank.

I am currently working on a side project called The SEO Consultants that will provide high-end search engine optimization services to customers with budgets in the sub-$500/month range.

I'm originally from Arcadia, California, near Los Angeles.
