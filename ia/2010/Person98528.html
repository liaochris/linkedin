Jim Lefevere’s Summary

Jim Lefevere is an innovative global marketing and technology professional in consumer goods, healthcare, and medical devices. He has the traditional expertise expected of a senior marketing executive with the pioneering enthusiasm of a new marketing entrepreneur. He has worked for Fortune 100 organizations and fast-growth start-ups. Named one of the top Forty business professionals under 40 by the Indianapolis Business Journal in 2009, Jim currently heads global Internet marketing for Roche’s Diabetes Care Accu-Chek brand.

Jim also contributed his leadership skills to the Continua Health Alliance as Chair of the Marketing Work from 2008-2009 where he was responsible for leading all marketing activities. Continua is a consortium of companies dedicated to enabling interoperable health care products and solutions worldwide that empowers people and organizations to better health and wellness.

Jim’s focus at Roche is on the interactive strategy, marketing and operations for the global brand. Jim is the strategic architect for global brand web sites and all interactive marketing. He has lead numerous global marketing campaigns and product launch efforts across multiple geographies. He also manages the development of strategic alliances with companies like Google and Forrester Research. Jim’s work for Roche has attracted recognition by industry and technical organizations, earning multiple Web Marketing Awards, honors from the Diagnostics Marketing Association, Hermes Creative Awards, and eHealthcare Awards.

He also blogs on digital strategy, interactive marketing and connected health care at www.jlefevere.com.
