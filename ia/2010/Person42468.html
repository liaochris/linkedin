Colin Mahoney’s Summary

Colin's experience includes development of public relations and crisis campaigns for a diverse range of national and international corporate clients including software developers , health care providers and technology companies, native-American tribes / casinos, health insurance corporations, defense contractors, consumer clients and nonprofits among others. Employing tactics including product launches, trade show support, analyst campaigns, M&A/IPO strategies and retail community relations, Colin has helped clients maximize corporate brands and market value.

As a crisis, public affairs and government engagement strategist, Colin has helped clients win several campaigns including ballot questions, zoning regulations and approval of construction projects. He has secured millions in federal and state funding for start-up clients by casting companies as industry thought leaders and has counseled senior executives during crisis campaigns. Colin has also helped nonprofit and advocacy organizations gain visibility with key stakeholders through strategic grass roots and government engagement campaigns resulting in increased ability to raise capital and promote policy initiatives.

As a political operative, Colin has worked with elected and appointed officials including Senator Edward M. Kennedy, Congressman John F. Tierney, former Massachusetts Senate President Thomas F. Birmingham and 2004 Presidential Candidate, US General Wesley K. Clark as well as foreign leaders throughout Europe and the Middle East. Specializing in various field, policy and media strategies, Colin developed aggressive campaigns aimed at specific constituencies and targeted voter bases. Colin has also served as a media consultant to several local races in Massachusetts including successful races for State Representative, Mayor and District Attorney.

Colin is a graduate of the University of Massachusetts.
