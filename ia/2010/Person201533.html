Tim Giuliani’s Summary

I am currently the VP of Corporate Outreach and Engagement at the Florida Chamber of Commerce. I manage all outreach efforts to small businesses, local chambers of commerce, and the 16 Fortune 500 companies that are headquartered in Florida. I also manage our grassroots program that has a network of 139,000 employers in Florida that are organized to help us accomplish legislative and political initiatives that will transition Florida to a new and sustainable economy.

Since 2005, I have been responsible for sales and membership retention in chambers of commerce, as well as marketing and business development. In my time with the Florida Chamber, I have been involved with the redesign of the Chamber’s website, wrote a market research report that the organization's 5-year strategic planned was based on, and led the 5 year strategic planning process for the membership department. I have also managed a regional business development and advocacy team, given numerous legislative presentations to local chambers of commerce and launched a Small Business Council with the help of an awesome team.

Tim Giuliani’s Specialties:

Non-profit management, sales, market research and planning, strategic planning, grassroots advocacy, social media integration, public speaking
