Paul Lancaster’s Summary

As Web Development Executive for Shell LiveWIRE, Paul is responsible for the maintenance and development of the www.shell-livewire.org website which harnesses the latest Web 2.0 technologies to provide up-to-date information and advice to young entrepreneurs throughout the UK.

Having started his career in IT Support and Web Development for large private sector companies including Sage, British Airways and BT Broadband, Paul then pursued a career in the music industry by travelling the world as a DJ, record producer, club promoter and independent record label owner whilst working for popular music development agency Generator in Newcastle upon Tyne.

In 2005 he joined the PNE Group, a social enterprise based in NewcastleGateshead that has been managing the Shell LiveWIRE programme on behalf of Shell since 1985, along with a wide range of regional, national and international development projects.
