Jessica Holbrook, Expert Resume Writer’s Summary

I'm an expert resume writer, career and personal branding strategist, author, and presenter. I have written more than 100 articles that are featured on some of the best career advice Web sites today. In addition, my writing has been included in Launch pad, a career search strategy guide featuring exclusive information by the top career experts in the industry. Published quarterly, Launch pad is the respected guide used by career development centers and MBA programs throughout the country.

As CEO of Great Resumes Fast, I enjoy collaborating with forward-thinking professionals and executives, identifying their personal brand and value proposition and leveraging their unique talent, passion, and vision to position them as a leader in their industry. My passion is helping professionals and executives uncover what makes them stand out in the crowd and strategically positioning them to move forward in their career.

I am a member of the Association of Online Resume and Career Professionals and Career Directors International.

For a free resume analysis, e-mail your resume to info@greatresumesfast.com. For a free telephone consultation, call toll free at 1.877.875.7706.
