Andy Halliday’s Summary

Andy had strategic roles as Entertainment Group President for Edison Brothers Stores and as Co-Founder and President of Simon Brand Ventures, a division of Simon Property Group (the nation’s largest retail mall developer). In 1998 he crossed the divide from brick-and-mortar to the internet, joining Excite as the first VP GM of Excite’s Electronic Commerce Group. Andy was recruited to become the CEO of Contact.com (Accel and Polaris), and then to become the first COO of In-Q-Tel, the CIA-funded venture entity providing technology to the IC. After leaving IQT in 2003, he co-founded Open Field Software and then was tapped to lead business development for Spoke Software. In early 2005 Andy felt that technology and timing had aligned to the point where he could realize his vision to create the social media experiences at OurStory.com and Tribbit.com.

OurStory users distill, preserve and share personal and family media in a lifelong timeline. At OurStory.com you showcase your photos and videos in multimedia stories about important events and perspectives on your life. These are organized in a legacy collection of events, people, places, and memories.

Tribbit.com is an implementation of the tribbit group media collection and presentation platform built by the team at OurStory. Tribbit.com showcases the inviting ways that anyone can contribute to a group media collection, adding photos with captions, text messages, voice recordings, videos and widgets, all organized and presented in automatically-generated slideshows and timelines.

Recently Andy has been working on several startup initiatives, including the founding of Tagvault.org, a repurposing of the tribbit group media platform, and a novel biometric identification technology for healthcare identity management.
