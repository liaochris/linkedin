Nico Faaij’s Summary

My biggest goal in life is to help finding solutions for cancer and especially for the braincancer called primary brain tumors. So every cancer professor, neurologist, neurosurgeon, oncologist, doctor, researcher, foundation, company or other people from all over the world, who wants to work in synergy together on this global problem, please contact me. Really every (also little) help you can give will be more than welcome for the thousands families that get involved with this terrible disease every year. My goal here is to help, to help those that follow me with a brain tumor, help to speed up the timeline for finding a cure for malignant primary brain tumors and to help identify improved treatment methods and improved quality of life for individuals living with brain tumors. I don’t put energy in living with cancer as there are already, even in Holland, other foundations doing that. I put all my energy in supporting medical brain tumour research en knowledge sharing.

Mijn grootste levensdoel is om te helpen bij het vinden van oplossingen voor kanker en dan speciaal hersenkanker in de vorm van primaire hersentumor. Dus elke professor, neuroloog, neurochirurg, oncoloog, dokter, onderzoeker, stichting, bedrijf of andere mensen die in synergie samen willen werken aan dit probleem van wereldformaat, neem alstublieft contact met mij op. Echt elke hulp, hoe klein ook, is meer dan welkom voor de duizenden gezinnen die hier jaarlijks mee te maken krijgen. Mijn doel is om te helpen genezing voor kwaadaardige primaire hersentumoren te vinden. Ik stop geen energie in het leren omgaan en leven met kanker want daar zijn al genoeg andere mensen en stichtingen mee bezig. Ik stop al mijn energie in het ondersteunen van hersentumor onderzoek en kennisdeling daarvan.

Nico Faaij’s Interests:

Dear connection, Imagine a cure, or a survival rate, for primary brain tumor patients. I really believe that every problem must have a solution and I will be devoted till death to help find the solution for this terrible disease that can become everyone fatal at any moment in life, no matter how healthy, careful and good you have lived your life. In the last 20 years there is almost no progress booked in this area. Only in (little) Holland it already hits about 4 persons a day (1500/year). So it's really time that more and other people get involved in this battle for welfare against one of the most frightened disease. Can it really be that difficult or are we fore more than 20 years on the wrong track or just with to less people and money? If we solved cancer in and with the brains, this can make a revolutionary step forward for cancer in general. Please help these almost forgotten cancer group also! With kind regards, Nico - More info: www.STOPhersentumoren.nl -
