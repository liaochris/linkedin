Casey Clear’s Summary

I am leading a national team of experienced mortgage recruiters for PNC Mortgage which is part of PNC Financial Services. I have a team of Recruiters across the country that are experts in cold calling, Internet Boolean searches and work hand in hand with the hiring manager to hire exceptional talent as well as provide a WOW experience for the candidates. I have recruiting experience from the agency world (Robert Half International and Ajilon) and corporate recruiting with Equity Consultants (Mortgage Broker) and also National City/PNC. I’ve successfully handled placements ranging from non-exempt to Director level in Finance, Administrative Professionals and Mortgage and Retail Banking professionals.
