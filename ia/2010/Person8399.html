Alonso Mayo’s Summary

I'm a Director & Writer who's interested in compelling characters and unique stories, whatever medium they may be in. I grew up back and forth between the US and Peru and have kept traveling ever since. A short I made after college landed me a spot on the directing program at the American Film Institute and I've made Los Angeles my home base ever since. Since arriving to LA I've won a Student Academy Award, written a handful of features, a couple of which are in the process of being financed, and directed 2 documentaries, the latest of which was shortlisted for an Academy Award. I like a good challenge and appreciate projects that break from the norm.
