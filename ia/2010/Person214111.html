David Jacobs LEED AP’s Summary

David is an accomplished construction project professional with over 20 years of successful leadership focused in energy and heavy industrial sectors in the US. In his career, he has progressed from site roles such as Senior Resident Engineer and Site Manager to corporate roles of Senior Construction Manager and Manager of Projects. In these corporate roles, he was responsible for multiple construction projects in coal-fired power, gas-fired power, chemical processing, sour gas processing, and other miscellaneous projects in several states.

David thrives in an atmosphere of challenge and complexity. His best contributions have been on projects that required something more than the "everyday" tasks. Other key traits include:

• Maintaining priority of project safety and quality in all on-site construction activity with a record of improving safety records.
• Skill at synthesizing and refining information to produce overall project objectives by being able to easily evaluate data and events in light of the overall needs of a project.
• Adept at accomplishing multiple tasks simultaneously and working effectively under pressure.
• Collaborative team player who is able to grow positive relationships with clients and colleagues at all organizational levels for the good of the project.
