Lars Kirstein Andersen’s Summary

I believe leadership is best exercised by having a vision, mission and supporting strategies, that - through a clear and continuous communication - defines the framework for competent and intelligent individuals to find together in teams and execute according to plans.

I conduct “constant involvement” through an open spacious leadership. I try to understand others motives, emotions and perspectives relating to a project or problem. I ask questions that will appear helpful and will normally try to avoid direct confrontational dialogue.
I always seek to bring two conflicting partners together to solve the issue. Conflicts relating to specific persons are translated into concrete tasks that can be solved without anybody loosing face. I will admit to own mistakes and draw full responsibility.

I take responsibility of complex and demanding projects. My focus is on minimizing risk, by checking details and the quality of processes. I show personal commitment and loyalty towards projects and the organisation. I will keep focus on set goals, and maintain high ambitions and stick to the agreed decisions. I will invest all my personal prestige in achieving the set goals.

I come prepared to meetings and focus on the most important aspects. I trust in my preparations and use them actively in my decision process. I have the ability to analyze a problem fast and identify important facts and data. I will always define success criteria and calculate consequences before important decisions are made. I have the ability to challenge all – no matter their position – with facts and a thorough analysis of the issue being discussed.

When crises appear, I will consider if we need to work around established processes and delegated responsibility to solve the problem. I will seek to create momentum in critical projects that have been abandoned by others or that have ended in a dead lock. If needed I will get directly involved and seek direct hands-on control and lead the process.
