  • Principal & Chief Creative Officer

    Hyde-n-Seek Designs

    (Design industry)

    July 2007 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Hyde-n-Seek Designs is a couture design studio based in Pickerington, Ohio. It specializes in unique, one-of-a-kind paper communication and event products ranging from the traditional to the elegant, the luxurious to the fun and whimsical. Hyde-n-Seek Designs target market is businesses, corporations and non-profits that expect to get people to functions to present their products and services. ie. Financial advisors, insurance sales people, etc. And for people who have events for their clients and or associates--thank you events, service events, etc. Most of these items are 3 Dimensional in nature--they provide a WOW element.

    The products Hyde-n-Seek Designs designs are products which will WOW people into participating in the event.
