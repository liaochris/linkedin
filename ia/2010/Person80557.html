Hugo Cuevas-Mohr’s Summary

Hugo Cuevas-Mohr has over 20 years experience in the money transfer and remittance business, both in Latin America and in the US.
For the last 10 years Hugo Cuevas-Mohr has been a consultant for Money Transfer Companies and Banks in Latin America, USA, Spain and the United Kingdom. He routinely gives lectures and seminars on subjects related to money transfer and remittances.
