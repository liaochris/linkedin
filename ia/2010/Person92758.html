Jeannine Fisher’s Summary

Jeannine Fisher is a driven, self-motivated professional with a proven track record of success in marketing management and sales. She excels at public speaking, customer relations, business training, new media marketing, and project management. Jeannine is applying this specialized skill-set towards her current position as Marketing Coordinator for PMCentersUSA.

PMCentersUSA helps project managers, business analysts, and executives improve their management and analysis skills. We have worked with and delivered training programs to Fortune Global 500 companies as well as individuals throughout the United States. Better project skills lead to higher profile projects and improved results.

If you want to make yourself more valuable to your company, you need training that will provide real, hands-on experience. PMCentersUSA teaches these valuable project management/analysis skills using a customized, interactive approach.

We go beyond just holding a generic training class. Our program is unique because it assesses your exact needs and developing a training program that will ensure your company's projects are effective. We connect you with professionals who have a wealth of experience completing projects in your specific industry.

To improve your project skills and overall business results through project management and/or business analysis training, please visit PMCentersUSA.com or call us at 1-888-762-3683.
