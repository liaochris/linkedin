  • Freelance Violinist and Violin Teacher

    (self employeed)

    (Performing Arts industry)

    2001 — Present (9 years )

    I have played violin since the age of three and have both a bachelor's and master's degree in music performance from the Setnor School of Music at Syracuse University, where I studied with Jim Krehbiel.

    I currently perform with the Magnolia String Quartet, a professional quartet available for weddings, graduations, and other special events in Central New York. I also work as a freelance violinist, with engagements including the Utica Symphony Orchestra, Oswego Performing Arts, choral accompaniment, musical theater and opera productions, solo performances, weddings and special events, and recording projects.

    Additionally, I teach private violin lessons and coach chamber music groups. I have more than eight years of experience teaching students of all ages and abilities, and individualize my teaching methods to meet the needs of each student.
