George Fox’s Summary

I've been working professionally in print and electronic media including interaction design, web development, video production, authoring and 2D/3D animation since 1989. I'm a creative and comprehensive problem solver who works well alone and on teams. I understand the implementation process as well as the importance of style and user experience, with emphasis on accessibility and information design. I enjoy the opportunity to work on innovative high-profile projects that engage and impress.

I am comfortable with many multimedia authoring and design software packages. My expertise is in Adobe CS and Apple Pro tools for print and screen, but I have experience in many aspects of software development and administration. I know the key concepts involved with developing material for press and web including workflow, device-dependancy issues, bandwidth, content management/structuring and styling. I have a thorough knowledge of Windows XP and Apple OS X operating systems with skill in ActionScript, XML, CSS, PHP, ASP, JavaScript, Apache, MySQL and other related tools.

I also have excellent communication and creative problem-solving skills plus the ability to lead creative concepts and presentations. I'm a good team leader and enjoy the challenge of interacting with others to develop business opportunities, gather requirements, develop standards and procedures and deliver great creative products and services.
