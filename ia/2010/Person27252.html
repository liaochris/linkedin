Dale Berkebile’s Summary

BRANDWISE Located in Fort Worth, Texas, is a marketing, design & brand strategy firm offering a wide range of design solutions to both local and national clients. BRANDWISE is focused on using design as a tool to help companies grow their businesses. We can help you establish long term goals that will help build awareness, increase sales and shorten the sales cycle. By developing a Unique Selling Proposition (USP), we are able to differentiate you from your competition. We then build your USP into your web site, advertising, brochures, direct mail and trade show collateral.

It is our passion for excellence that differentiates us from other firms. BRANDWISE not only sells strategic design and marketing, but actually uses it in our own business. That is why we understand you just can't afford to do something that doesn't work!

Check out our Facebook page at http://tiny.cc/MIohw. Don't forget to become a fan on this page. Also, follow us on Twitter to get more up to date info about market. Follow us at http://twitter.com/Brandwise.
