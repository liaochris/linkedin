Marco Marlia’s Summary

WORK

I partecipate (through the holding Kerios Group S.r.l.) different companies in Internet and E-business.

Nextre Engineering is a software/system integrator, we develop and integrate web software mainly on the Java Enterprise stack (J2EE). We have a vertical expertise in Rich Internet Applications and Rich User Interfaces.

Digital ReveNew is one of the top Web Marketing and Search Engine Marketing company in Italy.

Nomesia is the first lead generation company in Italy oriented towards SMEs.

I co-founded Treeweb a web design agency.

I have started my first company (Juxt S.n.c, a small web design and web software company) during college.

I have also worked at Università Commerciale 'Luigi Bocconi' (ELAUT and MOET courses) as IT Tutor, and as Administrator at OLE.

I've worked during the college at Merrill Lynch IPCG in Milan.

PUBLICATIONS

Co-Author of two books:
"Wikis: Tools for Information Work and Colalboration" - Chandos Publishing
"Oltre Wikipedia" - Sperling & Kupfer / Egea
"E-learning e Piccole e Media Imprese" - Franco Angeli

EDUCATION

I graduated with 109/110 in "Economia delle Istituzioni e Mercati Finanziari" (Economics - Institutions and Financial Markets) at Università Commerciale 'Luigi Bocconi'.

After two years of research and following as assistant two edition of the Master in Online Education and Trainging my thesis was: "ICT, Learning Communities and Communites of Practice: a proposal for a decision-making model".

I have studied management, leadership, communication, NLP (I have completed a Master in NLP with Andrea Favaretto and attende courses with Richard Bandler and John La Valle).


SPORT

I have been the youngest instructor in Jeet Kune Do, Kali & Escrima with Combat Dept (Vinicio Del Beccaro). I teached for 6 years. I have also trained in Brasilian Ju Jitsu, MMA and Free fight, Acrobatics and Capoeira.
