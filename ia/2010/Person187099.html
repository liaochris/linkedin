Shai Stern’s Summary

Shai Z. Stern serves as the CEO of Vintage Filings, a PR Newswire company which serves over 2500 public companies that are listed on Nasdaq, NYSE, Amex and OTC. Vintage Filings was acquired in April 2007 by PR Newswire. Mr. Stern was formerly a Vice President at American Stock Transfer Company where he initiated public company relationships and was responsible for outstanding share balances of over 2,000 publicly held companies. Mr. Stern was instrumental in the growth of AST Stock Plan which administered stock options and stock benefit plans to 500 public company clients before being bought by Citibank.

    (Financial Services industry)

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    SRSRun, or Securities Reports Streamlined, allows brokers, companies and their attorneys to manage their restricted and registered securities on a web based platform.

    Through this platform, communication with transfer agents, be it conversion/exercise or new issuance requests as well as instruction for removal of restrictive legends when a shareholder so requests is seamless and efficient. With its security and updating features, SRS accurately reflects outstanding private placement balances, allows the company to get away from spreadsheets that are used to keep record of private placements, allows a company and/or its attorneys to quickly determine whether federal and company rules and regulations are met when it comes to a shareholder requests which the company in turn instructs the Transfer Agents to remove stock legends and will generate reports with a click of a button.
