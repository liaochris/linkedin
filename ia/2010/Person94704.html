Jennifer Walsh’s Summary

Jen has worked in several communications, marketing and digital media roles in her career. She started out as a foreign correspondent in Hong Kong and continued working as a journalist for about six years. Since then, Jen's worked for a technology start-up, a management consultancy and two of the biggest brands in the world, IBM and GE.

She is currently the Digital Media Director of GE managing the company’s brand and reputation online, while also creating original multimedia content for digital distribution. Jen and her team are responsible for GE.com, insideGE (the company intranet) and all of GE’s online advertising programs and digital events.

Jen and team have also been lucky enough to win some awards for their work, including a CyberLion, a Clio, Effies and Tellys, as well as some nice recognition in the New York Times, the Wall Street Journal and Communication Arts.

Jen can be reached at jennifer.a.walsh@gmail.com.

Jennifer Walsh’s Specialties:

Jen is a results-oriented leader with a proven track record directing highly successful global digital media programs, including major corporate Web sites, social and search campaigns.
