I recently joined Rosetta Stone, the fast-growing, world-renowned leader in language learning software. I am responsible for setting up plans to execute on the company's strategic goals, as well as supporting the company's senior executives' decisions. Select focus areas include new product launches, market and industry analysis, customer segmentation.

I have 7+ years of experience in advisory, benchmarking and consulting services from the Corporate Executive Board (NASDAQ: EXBD). I specialized in working with Fortune 500 chief financial officers, treasurers, controllers and heads of shared services organizations. While 50% of my responsibilities focused on client-facing activities, the other 50% related to project management; staff hiring, training and management; and general management of a department that at one time consisted of 5 research managers and 10 research associates and business analysts, located both in the United States and India.
