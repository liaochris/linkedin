Russell Yardley’s Summary

Left IBM in 1985 to start what is now one of Australia's oldest multimedia businesses. Strong skills in attracting and managing highly creative design teams with a real drive for innovation. In 1995 I participated in the Williamson Community Leadership Programme and have enjoyed many challenging opportunities as a Williamson Fellow.

Having spent 23 years running Acumentum and other small business interests now I am looking to taking on non-executive director roles as well as helping the new entrepreneurial companies grow and prosper!
