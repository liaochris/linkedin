Bill Smith’s Summary

*** THE SEI's 2010 "OUTSTANDING MEMBER REPRESENTATIVE" ***

CEO of Leading Edge Process Consultants, a Partner of the Software Engineering Institute (SEI). Nationally recognized software engineering trainer, speaker, and humorist.

SEI-Certified CMMI Instructor. Has taught Introduction to CMMI 48 times to 838 students (through June 2010). Amazingly, still not bored. Teaches publicly every six weeks to sold-out classes in Reston VA (Washington DC area). Has taught Intro to CMMI onsite to two dozen organizations, including AT&T, DHS, IRS, and Lockheed Martin. (Full list: www.cmmitraining.com/onsite-cmmi-training.html.)

Also teaches the one-day SEI Services Supplement for CMMI; provides unique insight, based on incremental adoption of CMMI-SVC within his company.

Known for keeping classes lively and engaging. Uses frequent examples, props, and energizers -- whatever it takes to keep people awake and actively learning. (Discontinued use of cattle prods in 2008 due to legal concerns.) Often attempts humor; occasionally succeeds. Average instructor rating over 4.9 (adjusted to 5-point scale). (Public class schedule: www.cmmitraining.com/cmmi-classes.html.)

"I loved the creativity, humor, instructor stories, inventive and unique activities… I will actually retain the information long-term" (Sept. 2009 student quote). Has received on-the-spot customer awards for excellence in training delivery, more than once.

Recently launched CMMI Rocks!, a social network for alumni of his CMMI classes (www.cmmirocks.ning.com). Discusses his own use of CMMI-SVC on his blog, the CMMI for Services Diary (www.cmmiforservicesdiary.com). Tweets every now and then (CmmiRox on twitter).

Outstanding Presentation award winner at the NDIA CMMI Conference in 2009 (CMMI for Services, Lean, and Small Settings) and 2008 (Practical Guidance).

Contact Bill directly at 571.490.3229 or bill@cmmitraining.com. Or visit our site at www.cmmitraining.com.
