Rainer Praesoll’s Summary

Globalisation and emerging markets, rapid changes of technologies and short-term planning horizons define high pace. To be able to (re)act quickly and professionally companies need flexibility, know-how and adequate ressources.

At the same time especially in small and medium-sized companies lean structures and cost pressure cause low staff levels: mostly there are no reserves for special projects, crises or unpredicted leaving staff available.

More and more there is a short-term-demand for temporary management-capacity, that means for

>>> additional management-expertise in the sense of "know-how-on-time",

>>> new concepts and impulses including sustainable realisation as well as

>>> quick wins and results in initiation and implementation of change processes.

If you are interested in one of these services or if you have identified another point of contact please do not hesitate to contact me by email to rainer.praesoll@gmail.com.
