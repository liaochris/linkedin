Egon Heidendal’s Summary

Currently I'm getting my Masters at Northwest Missouri State University and I will be actively seeking a job in the Spring of 2009.

In the Netherlands I did an internship as a web developer for a web design company that makes websites for different clients ranging from a publisher to a real estate agency. I made contact with a lot of clients and the owner of the company was really pleased with me. After my internship they hired me for an extra 3 months and during that period I even ran the company for two weeks when the boss was on vacation.

While working for Bearcat Productions I made a DVD for the Missouri Academy, which is a high school program from Northwest Missouri State University. They wanted their five year history in news articles digitized and put on a DVD.

Also, while in the States, I worked on a John Deere ad campaign. This was done through a class I took at Northwest Missouri State University. At first the entire class did primary and secondary research, and then divided into two groups, both groups made an advertisement campaign. The campaign my group came up with has actually been used by John Deere. Within my group I was responsible for the art work and part of the media buying and planning, I also did some storytelling.

I also did an internship for the Dutch government. They wanted me to analyze an online web application and come up with a marketing strategy to sell it better. Next to that I also needed to produce a document that had usability improvements for the program itself.
