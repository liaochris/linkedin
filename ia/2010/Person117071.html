Kim Owens’s Summary

I have been a freelance digital marketer for over 13 years, complementing the marketing projects of small and large business in a variety of vertical markets, from technology and higher education, to healthcare, insurance and automotive. I work with internal marketing departments, consulting clients on best practices and strategies to ensure the success of their marketing projects.

Two years ago I could see micro-blogging and social networks were poised to change the grid within the marketing transit system. So I dove in feet first, absorbing best practices and strategies to utilize social networks for:

- Business branding
- Customer service
- Sales
- Relationship management

As most in the social networking space know, there is no such thing as an "expert" in social media. It changes on a daily basis and doesn't show any signs of slowing down.

That's what I write about on my blog, Buzz Boulevard: the latest strategies that are working, what isn’t working, and what new applications and tools can help make what we do—better. I also point readers to resources and other blogs that can help us all navigate the fast moving marketing transit system.
