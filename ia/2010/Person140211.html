Meg Montford’s Summary

Executive Career Coach helping executives and professionals convert their career development, career management and career transition challenges into opportunities for success. President and Founder of Abilities Enhanced, 1999. Experienced since 1986 as a careers professional in coaching, consulting, recruiting, and business development.

President & Founder of the Career Coaching Club, a membership website for self-employed career services professionals who want to become truly successful by bulding and growing their practices into sustainable businesses.

Meg Montford’s Specialties:

career transition coaching, radical career change, life/work balance, culture fit, workplace communications, job interview coaching, networking strategies, salary negotiation, resume writing, coach training, coach mentoring, small business marketing
