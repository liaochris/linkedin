Doug Monahan’s Interests:

I work 80+ hours a week, so it doesn't leave much extra time for other extra-curricular activities. Fortunately for me, my work is my hobby, and my hobby is my work. I love direct marketing. I love working with people to find new and exciting ways to help them market their high-technology products. I'm especially interested in how video is going to replace outbound telephone calls, or face to face visits for a sales force. I believe Google/YouTube provide a platform that can drop the cost of sales while concurrently increasing revenues. I see tremendous applications for both the B2B and B2C markets. I view the internet as the cake and the video as the icing. I'm especially excited about what's going on in the business social networking sites such as FaceBook. Everybody keeps talking about Web 2.0 - but what I see coming quite quickly is Web 3.0.
