Steve Saint Fleur’s Summary

Steve Saint Fleur is an Interactive Producer based in Brooklyn, New York, with professional experiences in digital marketing and in the online industry. As a Digital Producer, Steve Saint Fleur is responsible for the overall project management and productions which includes preparing prototypes, overseeing the functional specifications, project timelines, scheduling, finding resources, budget management, coordinating the account service, executing the strategy, as well as managing the creative and technical teams. Steve love's and thrives on the energy and excitement of working in a creative and dynamic environment. Steve's passion is to execute great ideas into living breathing interactive works of art and expression that not only engages the mind but also captures the heart and emotion of the audience.

Steve has worked on diverse digital projects that include Interactive Websites, Viral Marketing, Social Media Strategies, and E-commerce development. Steve has produced various types of digital channels such as websites with customized CMS, UI Designs, landing pages, email newsletters, viral videos, and branded social media networks.
