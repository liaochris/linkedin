Sandeep Manudhane’s Summary

I am a proud first-generation entrepreneur based in India.

My schooling was from St.Paul's Indore, and during that, I won the National Talent scholarship. Having done my Engg course from the IIT Delhi, and securing final admission calls from all IIMs, I became an education enterpreneur. Professional Tutorials, the proprietary firm I started in a garage with zero capital on 10th July 1993, is today a pan-India training institution PT education(www.PTeducation.com). PT was adjudged India's no.1 MBA Test Prep company recently by the first survey of its kind. The group successfully entered mainstream education with the launch of the PROTON business school (www.Proton.in) at Ahmedabad & Indore. PROTON - India's most positive b-school - works on the mission of 'creating responsible professionals for the 21st century'. The School of Business Marshals (SBM) was launched in Jan 2009 to cater the needs of the vast Indian SME sector - the real India(www.BusinessMarshals.in).

PT universe (www.PTuniverse.com) - the mother brand of our entire group - includes
(1) PT education - India's leading chain of training centres for exams
(2) PROTON business school - India's most positive b-school, and
(3) SBM - the value business school for the real India
(4) Kommerce King - range of commerce training programmes (XI, XII, CA-CPT, CS etc.)

Rigorous 17 years of grassroot level work has finetuned my business and personal skills, and I find it exciting to help others. The 1000+ professionals working in PT universe have trained more than 3 lac students since 1993.

I love to teach, motivate, read, travel, play the Guitar, watch movies and play cricket.

My personal life-goal is to give back to society much more that what I will have taken from it. My professional goal is to create outstanding world-class education experience for each of my students, always. Kar ke dikhayenge!

My blog: http://smblog.proton.in
Connect with me on: Orkut, Facebook, Twitter
