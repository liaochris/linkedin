Marty Thomas’s Summary

I am a solutions provider for a company in the direct marketing industry. Teagle & Little takes a client’s concept, transforms it into a tangible marketing offer and seamlessly delivers it directly to their end user.

We do this through a highly efficient production facility utilizing services in many core competencies including offset sheet-fed printing, data collection and management, laser and inkjet imaging, finishing, lettershop fulfillment and other integrated direct marketing solutions such as personalized URL’s.
