Jeff Smitley, LEED AP, GAC’s Summary

Mr. Smitley has over 20 years experience in consulting, facility management, construction, maintenance and engineering. His responsibilities have ranged from strategic planning, construction management, engineering management, capital equipment planning, real estate development and property management. Mr. Smitley is an advocate for the advancement of sustainability, particularly in the construction and product manufacturing industry. As a visionary leader, Mr. Smitley was responsible for leading the development of the first U.S. Green Building Council’s LEED certified healthcare facility in the State of Michigan and the second LEED certified hospital in the U.S. For the past 3-1/2 years Mr. Smitley has served as Chief Sustainability Officer for Tishman Construction Corporation where he has been responsible for LEED construction compliance at CityCenter, the largest known private development and LEED registered project in the world located in Las Vegas, Nevada. Mr. Smitley was also responsible for leading Tishman’s internal corporate sustainability initiatives and advancing Tishman’s sustainability consulting practice, which most recently served as the executive LEED consultant for Echelon, the second largest LEED registered project in the world, also located in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Mr. Smitley is a LEED Accredited Professional of the U. S. Green Building Council, Green Advantage Certified, a founding board member of the U.S. Green Building Council West Michigan Chapter, former board member of the U.S. Green Building Council Nevada Chapter and current member of the City of Las Vegas Green Council. Mr. Smitley’s focus is to provide innovation and support to sustainable building projects world-wide while he continues to develop his private green building, renewable energy and green product consulting practice.

Jeff Smitley, LEED AP, GAC’s Specialties:

Green building consulting, LEED certification, Green Globes certification, UAE Pearl Breen Building Assessment certification, Energy Star ratings, green product development, green manufacturing processes, renewable energy technologies, energy efficiency projects, project management, preconstruction services, value engineering, feasibility studies, cost benefit analysis.
