David Olson’s Summary

Thirty years of strategic communications, advocacy and nonprofit management experience that produced measurable results on five continents and in four languages. Managed effective development programs in Paraguay, Bangladesh, Zambia and Mali. Founded NGOs in Zambia and Paraguay that have become highly successful and leading health programs and a grass-roots network in Mali that is still thriving. Managed external relations, brand management and communications via traditional and new media. Pioneered and managed evidence-based global health advocacy with Congress and executive branch. Advised and developed external relations capacity of African, Asian and Russian non-profits. Managed relations with major U.S. and European media. Lectured widely on social marketing and media relations at prominent universities. Proficient in French and Spanish, with working knowledge of Portuguese.
