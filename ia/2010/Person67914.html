Eva Dogan’s Summary

I am a Structural Consultant. I do personal and organizational consultation, coaching, analysis and decision making.

The approach Dynamic Urge uses is based on Structural Dynamics, an area developed and founded by Robert Fritz.

Everything has a structure, be it a person, a group of persons, a relationship or an organization. This structure determines our patterns and how we behave. A structure is either oscillating – you create success but the that success is neutralized; or advancing – you use your success to build momentum over time to build future success yet again.

We start from the basis of creating what matters to you! By consulting us you explore what matters most to you, what patterns you have and how you can build a structure that supports your goals and dynamic urges.

Read more on our web side: www.dynamicurge.se (English is yet to come - for now use Google translator) and on www.robertfritz.com.
