Katie Nhu Nguyen’s Summary

At the age of 18, Katie Nguyen has been actively helping her community at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF) as a Volunteer & Service Center’s Project Coordinator for Project Step Ahead. Fundraising over $3,000 worth of school supplies to donate to children of low-income families, Katie not only amplified the voices of under-served communities, but also served as a role model to the children that she has helped.

After four years as a University Honors Student and a Business Marketing major, Katie has proven to be a rising star at the CSUF Mihaylo College of Business. She has earned various recognitions and 12 scholarships, such as the Dean’s List Award and the Northwestern Mutual/ Waltos Group Scholarship. Professionally, Katie has been awarded a 1st place prize of $4,000 from the Target Corporation’s Sustainability Case Study Competition, as highlighted in The CSUF Indicator Magazine. In May 2010, she was featured in the Orange County Register for winning the first place team honors at the 6th annual Western States Collegiate Sales Competition. Katie was mentioned in an article by the Westminster Journal for winning the 2010 VACOC Scholarship Award in August 2010. In her undergraduate years, she has completed four successful internships with the Target Corporation, mUrgent Corporation, Nestle Coffee-Mate/AMP Agency, and Southern California Edison.

Today, she continues her success and involvements by maintaining her role as Editor/Writer of the CSUF Vietnamese American MBA Association, VP of Public Relations for the CSUF BGS Honor Society, and Student Liaison of the Vietnamese American Chamber of Commerce of Orange County.
