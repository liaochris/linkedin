Joe Mescher’s Summary

Social media has gone from being a buzzword to a suite of tools that empower businesses and individuals to start conversations and develop relationships like never before.

The next evolution of social media is human business. My focus as the Chief Branding Officer of Social Brand Solutions and as a search engine marketing professional is to teach people how to use social networks to build armies of raving fans.

Once you learn how to create a powerful syndication and distribution network with buzz-worthy strategy, your fans will find you.

My team and our combined resources deliver results based on your specific goals. We're certainly not the cheapest, but the results speak for themselves.
