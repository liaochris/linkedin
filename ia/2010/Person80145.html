Harry Chen’s Summary

Harry Chen is a software engineer and technology innovator at Apple. He is a Java software engineering expert in building high-performance and scalable server application. Before joining Apple, he was a member of the start-up team that developed Siri, a virtual personal assistant that provides services to mobile computing users through seamless natural language understanding and web service aggregation. Siri was acquired by Apple Inc. in 2010.

Chen holds a Computer Science PhD from UMBC. He has broad research interests in artificial intelligence, mobile computing and social web technologies. He is the author of 30 referred publications. He has extensive experience working on US government funded R&D projects.
