Cathy Kuzel *www.theconnectedwoman.com’s Experience

  • Founder/President

    The Connected Woman

    (Privately Held; Think Tanks industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    "We know the quality of connections and the relationships developed determine the results achieved." ~ CK

    When Cathy Kuzel chose to make SWAN the network to belong to on the North Shore, she used her 20+yrs of expertise to make it happen. She recognized the need for women to have a supportive environment to develop both business and networking skills.

    While doing research for her upcoming book, it became evident that women were looking for something in addition to the business networking that was available.

    Cathy has expanded her vision to create The Connected Woman© Network, a community of professional women whose focus is business not sales, and who understand the difference.

    It provides women in business the opportunity and means to create and establish connections where sharing resources, information, and expertise is a reciprocal process. Being a member of The Connected Woman© Network is an investment in yourself and your business, not just another date on the calendar.

    Cathy is a respected authority on networking and an asset to all who know her. She is the quintessential 'Connector' as described in The Tipping Point - she reaches out to women, invites them in, introduces them to others and on it goes.

    At The Connected Woman© Network we look "Beyond the Business Card©".

    Vision: to make The Connected Woman© Network the standard by which all organizations and networks will measure their success by.
