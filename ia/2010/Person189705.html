Chris Mullett’s Summary

Silencio Music is a London based independent record company with a highly regarded catalogue of beautiful therapeutic music composed specifically to aid and enhance relaxation, meditation and stress-relief.

Our flowing ambient music is based on extensive research into the beneficial effects of music, and the wide ranging influence of healing music from many different genres and cultures. The compositions combine acoustic instruments, the voice, digital samples and atmospheric recordings to create music which emanates peace, tranquility and harmony.

We work closely with a core group of experienced musicians who are passionate about the music they create, and who are willing to share the secrets of heart and soul.
