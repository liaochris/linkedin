Mario Zorz’s Summary

More than 8 years of experience in mobile technologies, design, development and implementation of products and mobile solutions for different industries throughout Latin America.

In year 2003 I cofounded ZA Systems Development (www.zasysdev.com.ar), in the role of core R&D consultant.

WURFL contributor since September 2008.

I'm the creator of The Prosciutto Project (www.prosciuttoproject.org) which is an open source ZA spin-off for its
core J2ME framework called ZAMAE (ZA Mobile Application Engine) designed and architectured envisioning a mobile browser
that will fit low and mid end handsets.
