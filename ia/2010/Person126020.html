Lisa Goldsmith’s Summary

I've been working in the Entertainment Industry for the past seventeen years. For the first two years I was working in Film Development at Monarch Pictures/MGM Studios,the following four years were spent working in Production on a variety of Film and Television projects. The next twelve years, and still today, are being spent working as a Set Decorator and Buyer for Film, Television, and Commercials. For a complete film list of projects I've worked on, please go to www.IMDB.com, simply look for the search engine and enter my name. I have two strong portfolios of my Set Decorating/Buying and it will also be availble to be viewed online in the near future. I'm currently in the process of transitioning to New York City, where I shall continue my Set Decoratng/Buying career. I'm very interested in expanding my Decorating experience; to 'Prop Styling'. Prop styling is essentially Decorating sets that will be used for Editorial/Print work which gets published in magazines, and I am hoping that my experience will make for an easy transition into this new arena. I am trying to make all the contacts I can now. Another part of my career is spent doing Voiceover which I absolutely love, my goal is to one of these days have it be my main source of income! I'm currently with D.P.N Talent in Los Angeles. To hear my demos, please go to www.lisagoldsmithvo.com, I'll also be doing voiceover in New York.
