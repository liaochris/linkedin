Sudhanshu Jain’s Summary

Sudhanshu Jain has over 15 years of experience in telecom and networking industry. Sudhanshu is a result driven seasoned executive with experience in product development and marketing along with team management leveraging cost arbitrage. His rich experience varies from technology focused pre-funded/Tier-1 VC funded to midsize and fortune 500 companies.

Sudhanshu has run engineering organization with complete product development responsibilities, including strategic planning and full P&L ownership. Sudhanshu also has very rich experience in marketing and business development managing customers, account team, OEM/ODM/eco-system partnership and execution in cross-functional areas.

Sudhanshu has authored more than 8 patents in networking and traffic engineering space.
