Rick Diamond’s Summary

Most recent business adventures: Founder of Breathe Laser smoking cessation clinics and Doceo Solutions (provides business consulting services and is also a vehicle through which Rick identifies and evaluates potential business opportunities for investment or participation).

CEO of ACI: Rick and his partner Gary Cohen, founded ACI with just $4,000, and they led it to its international scope. ACI had an annual growth rate that averaged nearly 50% per year for the first 15 years. ACI completed its I.P.O. in 1996, operated 13 offices and employed more than 2,000 people in the US and Canada.

Rick has spoken at many conferences: DMA, TBT, ATA, Natl Conf.on Database Marketing, and Direct Mktg to Business and Industry. Lecturer at U of M-Carlson School of Business, Morrison Ctr for Entrepreneurship, U.-St. Thomas MBA program.

Rick has served on the Bd of Directors of the Animal Humane Society, the Bd of Governors of Alzheimer Assoc, was President of the Harvard Business School Alumni Club of MN, and the Boards of Directors for ThreeWire, C-Tech, IntelAccount, Webstore, Non-Profit Partners and MN-SNAP (Spay-Neuter Assistance Program).

Rick has been featured in many publications, including; Twin Cities Business, CitiBusiness, MN Ventures, TelePro, Wall St. Reporter, St. Paul Pioneer Press, KARE 11 News, Intl Voice Technology Review, JATA, Wall St. Transcript, CEOcast, Ventures Mag., Profits Journal, Upsize Magazine, Bureau of Business Practices, and others.

Awards include; CitiBusiness 40 Under 40 Award, Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year finalist, many recognitions given to Rick’s companies; including the ACCE Excellence Award, GTE World Champion Vendor, Ventures Magazine Top 10 Best Performing Businesses, Quebec Coup de Coeur, many Top 50 and Rising Star Awards.
