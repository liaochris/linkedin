Christian Stock’s Summary

Website design, development & marketing: focus on profitable websites, maximizing conversion rates, building customer relations, solutions tailored to existing business values & branding. Community sites, e-commerce, small business, personal profiles & blogs.

Virtual worlds: automatic building of real-time 3D landscapes in a variety of platforms (incl. Garagegames' Torque Game Engine, Microsoft Flight Simulator), building of decision support platforms, real-time linkage to geographical information systems, 3D environments in the context of security training, climate change, sustainability.

Spatial science: geographical information systems & digital mapping, spatial analysis, web mapping, ESRI ArcMap & ArcServer extension programming (ArcObjects).

Other: technical & academic writing, writing of tutorial and technical documentation, creating engaging presentations for various international audiences, project management, supervision of post-graduate students, teaching to under- and post-graduate students.

Clients include: Westpak Rescue helicopters, Mount Cook Airline, Flight Experience, Dept of Environment and Sustainability (Vic), Dept of Primary Industry (Vic), Defence Science and Technology Organisation, Defence Imagery and Geospatial Organisation.

Christian Stock’s Specialties:

web design & development (html, php, mysql), online & permission based marketing, developing customer relationships, programming (C/C++, Java, VB), 3D modelling, video game programming (TGE), geographic information systems, teaching & supervision, project management, statistical analysis, writing & communication.
