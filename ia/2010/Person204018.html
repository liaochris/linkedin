Tim Matthews’s Summary

I work as a specialist quality assurance / testing recruitment consultant for TechNET IT recruitment. I have placed candidates in many different companies including testing consultancies, IT consultancies, on-line gaming, financial & banking, healthcare companies, software houses amongst others. I recruit for companies throughout Europe, most notably the UK and the Netherlands.

I have also recently taken more of a focus on the development market. This has extended my knowledge to recruitment of candidates through .NET, Java, C#, C++, PHP amongst others.

Due to the variety in the testing world I have placed testers with the following skills: ISEB / ISTQB qualified, automated, manual, functional, non-functional, black box, white box, Unit testing, LoadRunner, WinRunner, Test Director, Quality Centre, Quick Test Professional, Borland tools, Empirix tools, Compuware Tools, Selenium, Fitnesse, Cucumber, penetration testers, real-time embedded, C / C++, C#, Java, Perl, Ruby, Python, Groovy, .net, unix / linux, windows, SC clearance and many more.



Please contact me if you are looking for testers or test roles at tmatthews@technet-it.co.uk
