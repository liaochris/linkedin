Simon Small’s Summary

I’m lucky enough to…

…lead the strategy team at one of the best creative agencies in Australia, Visual Jazz (Visual Jazz (VJ’s Blog). I work on key clients and oversee all strategy work nationally. We’re a nimble & growing team of 8 (8 more than when I started in 2009) planners, consumer insights researchers and data analysts.

We do…

…broad digital brand strategy & campaign planning through to comprehensive email, search & social media research, strategy and management. We’re developing the process and methodology from the ground up which means we’re able to craft quality thinking that’s 100% made for the current digital world. It’s not all roses though, as most of what we’re doing is new to the industry, finding good people is tough and there’s lots of trial and error.

Since I’ve been at Visual Jazz…

…we’ve helped a range of clients from Holden & DefenceJobs to the likes of National Foods, Porter Davis and Spirit of Tasmania. You’ll see some of what we do in campaign work, MobilesApps, social media, and lots of things you wont, like research, reporting and analysis.

Outside of work…

…I’ve been involved in a couple of business ventures, like Melbourne Bar Guide, Know Ballyhoo, Rotary, Love Digital and more recently a founder of Social Media Club Melbourne (#smcmelb @smcmelb).

Simon Small’s Specialties:

Digital strategy, research & data analysis. Search (SEM & SEO), social media, campaigns, web architecture, user interface, sales & client relationship management, project management
