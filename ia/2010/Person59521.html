Dr. Cheryl Lentz’s Summary

Add me to your network: drcheryllentz@gmail.com I will add you back without IDK. Join me!

Video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7gL5JzGiGmU

My teaching career started when we were stationed in Tokyo, Japan at Yokota Air Force Base (AFB). There was a great demand for English teachers and thus my entrance into the world of education began. While an English Teacher, I became more of a linguist and tutor, for private companies as well as within the Japanese K-12 Education system. What an experience!

With my new found love of teaching, I pursued my master's degree through Troy State University and so the adventure continued. My experience includes on-ground, Flexnet, blended and online modalities within Web CT, Blackboard, and OLS for both undergrad and graduate students. The disciplines taught include: Leadership, Management, Critical Thinking, History, Marketing, Ethics, Organizational Behavior, and Cultural Diversity.

I found myself back in school years later to complete my doctorate, a Doctorate of Management (DM) in Organizational Leadership. During this program, my first book began to crystallize--The Golden Palace Theory of Management, published June '08.

As a result, I found the model the publishing world used to be quite frankly ineffective and inefficient. Having spent a career of asking why and especially why not, I created a new publishing model that shares both the expense and promotion and a new company was born--The Lentz Leadership Institute, and The Refractive Thinker®.

I found many others were looking for ways to gain entrance into the publishing world to share their dissertation and other scholarly works. Since form follows function, the idea of a doctoral anthology has taken root, giving birth to The Refractive Thinker®, the series (Vol I. published March of 2009).

Now as both publisher and author, I have the honor to serve my colleagues in helping them to share their ideas with the world

Dr. Cheryl Lentz’s Specialties:

Professional Scholarship in Research: Quantitative Dissertation in Strategic Decision Making; The Golden Palace Theory of Management, The Refractive Thinker® (Vol I): Fail Faster, Succeed Sooner, Vol II (Research Methodology) and III (Change Management), IV (Ethics, Leadership, & Globalization) and coming soon, Leadership Comes Softly.
