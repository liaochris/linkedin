Roger Shindell’s Summary

A multi-disciplined and seasoned professional with over 20 years of broad based experience in the areas of Operations, Finance, Marketing, and Information Technology. A both an advisor to and principal in a number of technology, service and manufacturing companies, has extensive experience working with and in start-up and rapid growth as well as turn-around environments.

With both operational and executive experience, has a demonstrated ability to design and implement marketing and sales campaigns, design and implement financial controls, manage inventory, and restructure and manage capital structures, to maximize both cash flow and profits. Extensive involvement in raising capital, negotiating with stake holders and creditors, re-engineering of business process and the implementation of enterprise wide information technology systems..

President of the Board of Directors of A Silver lining Foundation. A former presenter at the Second Annual Global Environmental Investment Conference: Making Technology Transfer Financially Viable (1995); a Lecturer at the Pacific Coast University program in Executive Management Studies, and a presenter on the implementation of Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) systems at the 1997 Business & Technology Solutions Show sponsored by the Illinois CPA Society.

Inducted into the 1998 Edition of International Who’s Who of Information Technology, the 1998 Edition of Who’s Who among American Executives, and the Millennium Edition of Who’s Who in Executives and Business. Roger is a member of the Technology Executives Club, The Colorado Software and Internet Association, CTEK, and The Colorado Internet Keiretsu. Roger attended Washington State University for his BS in Biology and the University of Idaho for an M.S. in Economics.
