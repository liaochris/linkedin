Michael S. Black’s Summary

I am a business focused information technology professional with 15 years experience in information security, data privacy, and technology consulting. As a team leader and business manager that feels just as comfortable in the boardroom as in the datacenter , I bring a focus on industry-specific regulatory compliance with an overall goal of building an secure, sustainable environment. My diverse information sources and contacts allow me to keep up with the latest circumstances in the ever changing threat landscape that encompasses the modern InfoSec world, all of which allows me to provide my company with the best information in which to make difficult Risk Assessment decisions.

I'm looking forward to getting in to a company that is in need of a seasoned Information Security manager and assisting them in the process of implementing a well-rounded program.
