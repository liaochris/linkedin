John Bowen’s Summary

Management labor attorney focused on providing effective and proactive solutions to labor and employment challenges. By implementing preventive strategies -- identifying and solving workplace concerns before they become major problems -- employers can develop and promote positive employee relations and avoid employment-related disputes, costly employment litigation, as well as potential union-management issues.

John was recently recognized by the Labor Relations Institute (LRI) as one of the Top 100 Labor Lawyers in America for 2009.

John Bowen’s Specialties:

Representation and unfair labor practice proceedings before the NLRB, developing proactive union avoidance strategies (based on preventive labor relations and communication), collective bargaining, strike planning and management, grievance arbitration, employment law compliance, human resource consulting, wage and hour compliance and litigation, and management training and development.

John Bowen’s Experience

  • Labor and Employment Attorney

    John F. Bowen, LTD

    (Law Practice industry)

    February 2010 — Present (7 months)

    For nearly 20 years, John Bowen has successfully represented both union and non-union employers with regard to labor and employment law matters and human resource consulting. Mr. Bowen advises non-union clients concerning effective union avoidance strategies and proactive labor-management relations. He also represents unionized employers in collective bargaining, contract administration, arbitration proceedings, and strike management. Moreover, he has extensive experience representing employers in unfair labor practice and representation proceedings before the National Labor Relations Board.

    He regularly counsels management concerning labor issues that result from corporate mergers, acquisitions, and sales as well as workforce reductions and plant closings.

    Mr. Bowen frequently conducts seminars, workshops, and in-house training regarding effective union avoidance techniques, collective bargaining, preventive employee relations, and managing employees in a unionized workforce.
