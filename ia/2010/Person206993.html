Tony Muia’s Summary

Manhattan?

FUHGETTABOUDIT!

A Slice of Brooklyn Bus Tours features guided bus tours of Brooklyn's favorite foods, neighborhoods, historical landmarks, famous movie locations and more.

Brooklyn Pizza Tour - featuring stops at Grimaldi's under the Brooklyn Bridge and L& B Spumoni Gardens in Bensonhurst! Neighborhoods on this tour include DUMBO, Red Hook, Bay Ridge, Bensonhurst, Coney Island and more. Movie locations inlcude Saturday Night Fever, Goodfellas, Scent of a Woman, The French Connection and more.

Brooklyn Neighborhood Tour - featuring a stop at Junior's for cheesecake and an egg cream! Neighborhoods on this tour include Brooklyn Heights, Park Slope, Windsor Terrace, Green-Wood Cemetery and more. Movie locations include Moonstruck, The Godfather, As Good As It Gets, Dog Day Afternoon and more.

Christmas Lights Tour - highlighting the neighborhoods of Bay Ridge and Dyker Heights, which is known around the world for its over-the-top Christmas displays. This tour features a stop at Mona Lisa Pastry Shop.

ALL TOURS LEAVE FROM MANHATTAN!
