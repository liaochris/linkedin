Hazel Dooney’s Summary

Since 2006, Dooney has emerged as one of the Asia-Pacific region's most controversial young female artists. According to the Australian Financial Review, she "walks the razor's edge between respect and celebrity in today's artworld" (September, 2006). She has exhibited in solo and group shows in major cities all over Australia, as well as in the USA, Japan, and England. Her early work has achieved high prices at auction through Christie's in London and elsewhere.
