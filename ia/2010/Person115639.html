Kevin Kennemer’s Summary

Work with CEO's to create long-term, profitable businesses through positive people practices. A company's work environment and culture should be a primary concern to executives who want the highest level of performance and commitment from their employees. Without a great working environment, the best of breed compensation and benefits programs are marginal at best.

Kevin is a people practices professional with 20 years experience in compensation, benefits, rewards, organizational culture and development, employee relations, and mergers and acquisitions.

Subscribe to my company's e-newsletter, the People Power Newsletter, at http://www.thepeoplegroupllc.com.
