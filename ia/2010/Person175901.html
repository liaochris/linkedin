Rob Peters’s Summary

Rob is a Master Connector and Relationship Capitalist with 25 years of large account new business development experience with Computer Sciences Corporation (CSC), Capgemini Financial Services, Headstrong, Keane, Romac International. & IBM.

Rob's core business development strengths include:

• Customer Relationship Management
• Business Development Strategy & Tactics
• Client Business Strategies
• Strategic Partnerships & Alliances
• Competitive Market Positioning
• Advanced Contract Negotiations

CSC is a global leader in providing technology-enabled business solutions and services.

With approximately 92,000 employees, CSC reported revenue of $16.2 billion for the 12 months ended July 3, 2009. CSC makes change practical for leading financial services firms around the world.

To complement its capabilities in consulting, systems integration, and business process and IT outsourcing, CSC brings deep financial services industry knowledge and experience, a comprehensive portfolio of financial services application software, and an extensive network of industry and technology partners.

More than 10,000 CSC employees are dedicated to serving financial services clients, which include more than 1,200 major banks, insurers, and investment management and securities firms. Financial Services is CSC’s largest commercial vertical market, representing nearly 17% of the company’s revenue. Learn more about CSC in Financial Services at http://www.csc.com/financialservices.
