• Communication Plans enable you to increase your company’s market valuation by 30%, increase shareholder value by over 26%, attain a greater connection and commitment from your employees, create a more robust and inclusive corporate culture, and create a proactive involvement that drive change and growth.

• Professional development in the core communication skills—writing, presenting, and listening—through our Solari Classroom. Excellent communication skills are the most highly valued attributes in professionals today. Through our Writing for Results class, you can cut your writing time by a third while increasing your writing’s effectiveness by over 50%. Increase your results (include sales) by up to 75% through our Presenting with Poise class. Professionals spend up to 3 out of every 4 minutes listening; double your listening effectiveness through our Are You Listening? class. Allow us to customize these classes for you to attain a solid return on your investment.

• Effective Technical Communication has the power to reduce returns and technical support calls by over 50% while doubling retention and understanding.

At Solari Communication, we are expert in making the complicated simple. We help create new profit centers within your business by capitalizing on the often overlooked yet vital role that simple, clear communication plays in the success of your company.
