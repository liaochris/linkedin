Changzhi Lee’s Summary

I am a non-typical Ad Man currently based in NYC and I specialize in communication through performances, spacial activation, experiential marketing, and event-based advertising campaigns.

I work towards creating brand loyalty and equity among customers and those within an organization.

I am motivated about telling compelling stories, re-instilling core values and building experiences that stir up passion in people so that they resonate with the identity of a brand.

I have a strong interest in pursuing a career in environmental, exhibition, scenographic and large scale event design. I am an avid researcher and fanatic of the Olympic Games, Sporting Spectacles, Theme Parks, Music Festivals and World Expositions as expressions of mega brand experiences.

I have had my work featured in numerous well known design publications sold around the world including books by Rockport Publications, Harper Collins in the U.S. , Page One and The Sunday Times in Singapore, Sandu Cultural Media/ Design 360 in Guangzhou, LST Publishing House in Beijing, Maomao Publications in Spain, the Creative Review Magazine Blog in the United Kingdom and many more.
