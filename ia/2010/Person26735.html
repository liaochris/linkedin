Brad O'Brien’s Summary

For 15+ years Brad O’Brien has created and implemented multi-million dollar user experiences extending many top tier brands. His extensive experience includes working as both an innovative executive producer director of digital and social media strategy and emerging platforms for companies including Ogilvy, TBWA/Chiat Day, R/GA, Nickelodeon and Sirius XM Radio.

Brad has matched brands, including Nike, Nokia, HP, SC Johnson, Wal-Mart, Absolut, Chanel and IBM with creative advertising and marketing solutions that have enhanced not only the brands’ recognition but also the actual products themselves. His past projects include launching Nickelodeon's TV Land as well as the Howard Stern, NFL, Tony Hawke and Martha Stewart brands on Sirius Satellite Radio and the creation of the ‘In an ‘Absolut World’ and ‘Find Your Flavor’ international campaigns for Absolut vodka.

His multi-channel background includes interactive, mobile, broadcast and all forms of emerging platforms.. These have included working on event and experiential marketing as well as matching brands and game titles for in-game advertising working with EA, Ubisoft, Konami, Rockstar & 2K Sports.

Brad currently resides in New York City and can be contacted at hibrad@me.com or 917-279-1584. He also has a strategy and social media blog at twitter.com/adhabit and www.adhabit.com launching in the coming weeks.
