Christian Kalled’s Summary

Christian was most recently Chief Negotiator for Sedo.com, the world's largest domain aftermarket and monetization platform. He was responsible for high-value brokerage transactions in the United States and Canada, and specialized in the acquisition and divestment of super-premium assets.

Christian joined the company in 2004, immediately following its incorporation in Massachusetts. In collaboration with then-CEO Matthew Bentley and President Ulrich Essmann, Christian developed the company's North American brokerage group while completing a Bachelor of Arts at Boston University.

Upon graduating summa cum laude in 2005, Christian became Director of Brokerage for Sedo North America. Since that time, he tripled the division's transaction volume and increased gross annual sales by more than 900%.

Christian has also been the principal architect of some of the industry’s most prominent transactions, including Annuity.com, Blue.com, Bike.com, Wifi.com, Nasty.com, Pizza.com, Fly.com, and most notably, Vodka.com, which sold to luxury vodka-producer Russian Standard for $3,000,000 in 2006.
