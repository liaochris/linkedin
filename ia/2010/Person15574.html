Andrew Peel(andrewpeel09@gmail.com)’s Summary

I have worked in local government in the UK for 28 years in a wide variety of roles. My profession is Change Project Manager/General Manager. I have managed in excess of 600 staff during my time in local government. I have worked at the highest strategic level deputizing for my Director at Chief Officer group meetings. Former Chief Executives and City Treasurer's have been my direct Sponsors for corporate level change projects. I am also a member of the Manchester Manager Mentoring Scheme and was a pilot Mentor. I also have Programme Office Management experience on behalf of The Manchester Children's Trust.

I was a Trustee and later Chairman of Trustees for Greater Manchester South RELATE the largest UK mainland RELATE Branch. I was also Chair of their Clinical Governance Group and drove through changes in practice with the CEO who reported to me.

My current goal is to become a full-time entrepreneur using my professional experience and qualifications plus my passion for transformational change and the internet. I aim to have a team of 10 talented entrepreneurs working with me to grow a network marketing organization that will give me and all involved financial freedom.

I am 51 years old and one of the oldest male spina bifida patients in the world. I am a passionate advocate for reasonable adjustments in the workplace for disabled workers. For those not able to work I will create wealth creation opportunities to get them out of the benefit trap. 30% unemployment of disabled people in the UK, some with the brightest minds on the planet, is unacceptable and I will start to change that statistic. We are the fourth wave and we will take our position in society at all levels.
