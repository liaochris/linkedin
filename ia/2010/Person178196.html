Roy Rodenstein’s Summary

Experienced technology executive, innovator and entrepreneur as founder, board member and leader at Going, Inc., AOL, IBM, iPhrase Technologies, and Simon and Schuster/Viacom. Covered in Techcrunch, Read/Write Web, Boston Globe, Mass High Tech, CRM Daily and other publications. Published human-computer interaction researcher across work at MIT Media Laboratory, Xerox Palo Alto Research Center, AT&T Labs, and Georgia Institute of Technology. Advisor to numerous startups. Patent author.
