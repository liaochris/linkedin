Ryan Peal’s Summary

I love winning, have been competitive all my life (thanks to some serious swimming years) and this healthy attitude has served me well over the years, helping brands kick some serious you know what (don't think I can cuss in my profile, my mom would kill me). I use innovations, inspirations, imagination and insights to help clients create ideas that truly have impact.

Each day I help to inspire and challenge people to think, dream, act and do things differently through a variety of workshops, brainstorm sessions, all-office excursions and more. It's a never-ending task but somebody has to do it :). And based on previous success I know it works.
