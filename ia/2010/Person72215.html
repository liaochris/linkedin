Gabriel Cheong’s Summary

Attorney with an interest and focus on family law and estate planning issues. The goal in each family law case is to help the client acheive their goals and when their goal is unclear, to counsel and inform them of the legal and personal effects of their situation.

To educate the public about the importance of an estate plan for each and every person over the age of 18. Planning for your family's future begins today!
