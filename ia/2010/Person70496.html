Scott Horton’s Summary

Stereotypes exist for a reason. In my career as an engineer, I have felt like the proverbial square peg trying to fit in a round hole. Engineering should be very creative, imaginative, inventive ... It should be about exploration, discovery and transformation. In practice, it is often exactly the opposite. Joseph Campbell says we have become that hieratic state wherein every man enacts his role, ruled by the self-annihilate king, the tyrant Holdfast.

At PowerPlus Engineering, we are creating something completely different. Every day we slay the tyrant Holdfast and release from its ban the vital energies that feed our creativity, growth and transformation. It is the most meaningful and fulfilling work I have done in my career as an engineer.
