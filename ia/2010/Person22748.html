    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Confessions of a Mom is a site featuring the talents of mothers; entertaining to read, fun to follow, and helpful to moms with ideas on ways to share their talents. Confessions of a Mom encourages all mothers to express their interests. Designed to be fun and light-hearted, Confessions of a Mom will celebrate the food on your shirt and mourn the hair you pull out each day. All moms from all walks of life are invited to sneak away to the bathroom with their laptop for a few minutes of lively discussion, mom humor, and camaraderie with the only other people on Earth who fully understand why it is that you have a laptop in the bathroom.
