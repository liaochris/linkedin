Hallie Hawkins, JD & CCRR® & CITRMS®’s Summary

My broad depth of business experience allows me to see the big picture and to bring complex issues into an easy to understandable vision finding common ground in training diverse people with diverse views while being a strong leader.

Get It Together provides neutral financial, legal and credit score education and mentoring for individuals and for employees as an employee benefit.

For individuals, we have a radio show to learn about legal and financial issues that people face on an every day basis. We have mentoring programs and booklets about critical issues that affect individuals and families every day. You will work with a team of financial and legal professionals to understand legal and financial issues that affect you. This includes debt management, cash flow, credit scoring information and tools for improvement.

For companies, either mentoring or workshops can be added in a employee benefit program easily and give the employer a great Return on Investment. It can also be a critical piece of the ERISA 404 C requirements of providing unbiased financial education. It has been created by the legal and financial experts. We have written all the materials with our professional easy to understand style.

One of the missions of Get It Together is to encourage individuals and families to take control of their legal and financial world. The Get It Together Kit is created by a professional with experience in the Legal, Finance and Insurance Industry. The kit assists you in the process of organizing documents and other critical information for you, trusted family members & professional advisers and is easy to update.
