Richard Seireeni’s Summary

Founder of The Brand Architect Group, a brand consulting and design firm based in Los Angeles with affiliated offices in Tokyo and Shanghai.

Extensive experience in American, European and Japanese markets. Focused on support for environmentally sustainable businesses.

Author of The Gort Cloud: The Invisible Force Driving Today's Most Visible Brands.

Co-author of Marks: New Directions in Logo Design. Author of numerous articles on branding, design, consumer trends, sustainable business.

Coined the term Brand Architect in 1991 to describe a new hybrid professional discipline bridging brand strategy and creative implementation.

Seireeni lives in Los Angeles with his fashion designer wife Elaine Kim and their two children.

A licensed pilot at age 17.

Richard Seireeni’s Specialties:

Experienced in all aspects of brand development, communications, design and project management, including print, packaging, branded environments, live media, film & video, digital and social media.
