Jerome Poiraud’s Summary

Broadband in our lifes has already fully changed the way we listen to music, watch TV, give a phone call, get in touch with our friends, be informed about the world or how to re-think health or home automation services at home.

Let’s create together tomorrow’s usages and services

To make this business opportunity come true, Orange has opened its digital services world (at home or on the move) and especially its enablers (livebox, set-top-box, mobiles, APIs,…) to accelerate the creation of new products and services.

So we welcome ideas from partners and offer them privileged work place, help, tools and access to Orange experts (technical & marketing) in order to transform new concepts into commercial success.

You are entrepreneurs, you have great ideas, so don’t hesitate, contact us or go directly to www.orangepartner.com & www.broadbandthursday.com
