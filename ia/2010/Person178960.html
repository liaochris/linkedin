Russell Sparkman’s Summary

I have 25 years of experience in visual communications (photography, video), the last 10 of which have been as the head of Fusionspark Media, a new media content production and integrated marketing communications agency.

Why did we launch Fusionspark Media? We created Fusionspark Media in 1999 because we identified the opportunity for organizations to use quality content and storytelling to reach audiences directly via the Internet to advance their missions. We were particularly enthralled with the creation of cause related content and storytelling (environmental and health related) in support of Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives.

I am a seasoned content strategist, a content marketing evangelist and a veteran new media storyteller.

I am uniquely qualified:

♦ To develop content strategies that achieve communications goals and objectives
♦ Train B2B, B2C, Non-profits & Government Agencies in New Media tools, techniques
♦ To manage teams of content creators
♦ To help clients navigate the complex Social Media and Social Networking ecosystem
