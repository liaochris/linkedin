Mikolaj Norek’s Summary

The emphasis of my work at the Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship lies on handling the schools marketing towards its five member institutions and co-ordinating entrepreneurship events.

Privately I am developing a workshop and mentoring concept promoting, facilitating and implementing corporate responsibility (CR) ideas and tools in small and medium sized businesses.

Parallel I am always working on a number of small, partly very early stage projects.
A future wish includes the foundation of a business ethics network in Sweden as a national chapter of EBEN (european business ethics network).

*********************************************

Thoughts on my background:

Qualified & Educated: I am continuously working towards a well balanced tool-set of experience and knowledge. Of particular importance to me is interdisciplinarity that I am trying to pursue in enriching my Business Administration education with a Philosophy degree.

Entrepreneurial & Creative: Having founded a number of ventures and worked in a number of different places, settings and industries, being entrepreneurial is a way of being for me.

Multicultural & Flexible: Born in Poland, raised and educated mainly in Austria, travelled the Middle East and Africa extensively, I am now living in Sweden. Multinational perspective, flexibility and adaptability are vital parts of my life.
