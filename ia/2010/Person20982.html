Beatriz Mallory’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur with expertise in crafting marketing strategy, generating new revenue and identifying new opportunity markets. A pioneer of Hispanic direct marketing, I launched and lead HispanAmérica CRM, a 17-year old marketing services company.

HispanAmérica CRM has acquired and retained Hispanic customers since 1993. We design and execute relationship-marketing strategies to optimize Hispanic investments. HispanAmérica CRM consistently delivers:

1) Increased quantity, quality and lifetime value of Hispanic customers,
2) Excellent value and customer experiences for them, and
3) Growth in revenue and category share while maximizing return on Hispanic marketing budgets.

My heritage and global business experience strengthen my ability to lead the development and execution of marketing strategies for multicultural America. A passion for data-driven marketing led me into the direct response industry. My prior career includes consulting, technical sales, retail management and systems programming.

I strongly support direct and interactive marketing education and programs which broaden career opportunities for minority youth. I am also a yoga instructor, working to bring the benefits of better health to communities in need.
