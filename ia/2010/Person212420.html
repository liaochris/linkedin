Virgina Harlow’s Summary

Seasoned Marketing Manager, whose tenacious style has earned her the reputation of "someone who can get things done.” After starting out as an illustrator at General Motors' in-house graphic department, I moved to New Orleans creating still animation, video editing and production. My proficient use of the "up-and-coming" MAC provided me the opportunity to return to Detroit and work at the top advertising and marketing agencies in the area. It was through this experience that I was also afforded the opportunity to teach web design at the famed Center for Creative Studies. Today my focus is on strategic planning in the field of Customer Relationship Marketing (with a specialty in digital) at Ford Motor Credit Company.
It is through this adventure that I have met many of the extremely talented individuals who I am now Linked-In.
