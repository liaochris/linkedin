Stephen O'Sullivan’s Summary

Stephen O’Sullivan, CISSP, is the Network & Security Manager of Continuum Commerce Solutions. Stephen’s primary focus is Information Security & Compliance as well Infrastructure & IT Service delivery.

During Stephen’s time spent working in the Payment Services industry he has gained experience providing multifarious technical solutions to many large and blue chip companies including the set up, design, and configuration of many green-field sites. From an Information Security perspective Stephen has designed and implemented several security solutions and processes that ensure the companies PCI DSS compliance obligations are continually met.

Stephen is an (ISC)² Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), which attests to his experience, general skills and ethics in Information Security. The CISSP is an independent information security certification governed by the International Information Systems Security Certification Consortium (commonly known as (ISC)²).

Stephen is also a certified Cisco and Microsoft Security professional and is currently pursuing the Cisco Certified Security Professional (CCSP).
