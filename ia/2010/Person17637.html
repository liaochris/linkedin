Ashley Usiskin’s Summary

Over 15 years international brand, marketing and communications strategy experience across UK, Europe, the Indian Sub-continent and Australia.

I have held senior management/marketing roles at digital entertainment companies, world-class advertising networks and strategic communications agencies - Bartle Bogle Hegarty, Publicis, Grey Worldwide - working in a host of categories with a variety of clients eg Travel and Tourism boards, Online Travel Agencies, Digital/Mobile Entertainment, Financial Services, Telecommunications, Public Awareness/Climate Policy, Online Classifieds, Fashion, Alcohol & Skincare.

I’ve lived and worked in London, Sydney, Karachi and am now based in Madrid. Here in Spain I've had the opportunity to apply my extensive business development, management and marketing experience to pursue my passion for travel and tourism.

I’m currently working with the Boutique & Lifestyle Lodging Association to help establish it globally and specifically in Europe. The core goal is to unite a currently fragmented and unofficially represented industry. It provides innovative ways for boutique hotel brands & owners to grow their businesses, as well as help travellers find their ideal place to stay and enjoy unique boutique experiences.

In the long term I'm keen to pursue innovative, exciting and sustainable opportunities in the boutique hotel sector, and help under-achieving but great properties and initiatives fulfil their undeniable potential.
