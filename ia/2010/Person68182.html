Evelyn Tribole, MS, RD’s Summary

Evelyn Tribole, MS, RD is registered dietitian with a nutrition counseling practice in Newport Beach, California. She helps people create a healthy relationship with food, mind and body; specializing in Intuitive Eating, eating disorders and celiac disease.

Evelyn has written seven books: including the bestsellers, Intuitive Eating and Healthy Homestyle Cooking. Her most recent book is the Ultimate Omega-3 Diet (2007). Tribole has written for magazines, newspapers and LifetimeTv.com, where her monthly feature and a column appeared for 3 years. Her Recipe Makeover column ran for 12 years in Shape magazine.

ABC's "Good Morning America" hired Evelyn to be their nutrition expert for their health and nutrition segments from 1994-95. She was a national spokesperson for the American Dietetic Association for six years. Recently she worked with Grey’s Anatomy celebrity, Chandra Wilson and the news media, demonstrating how to pack a trade-proof school lunch for kids. Evelyn’s work has been profiled in People magazine, USA Today, Prevention and other national publications.

Evelyn qualified for the 1984 Summer Olympic trials in the women's marathon; though she no longer competes, she still runs for fun and fitness. She enjoys surfing, skiing hiking, yoga and kayaking. Evelyn’s favorite food is chocolate, when it can be savored slowly.

Evelyn Tribole, MS, RD’s Specialties:

Intuitive Eating, eating disorders, celiac disease, omega-3/6 fatty acids, inflammation, speaking and writing.
