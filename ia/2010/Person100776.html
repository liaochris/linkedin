Joseph Lemieux’s Summary

Engineering and Business Leader with a strong ability to develop all areas of advanced mechatronic systems and demonstrated ability to apply the right solution (technical and commercial) to complex techincal problems. Solutions applied to multiple industries - Automotive, Military Vehicles, and Medical Devices. Always focused on creating the best solution, balancing the mechanical, electronic, and software state of the art that meet all requirements at the lowest cost in the shortest time. Developed many cutting-edge systems including Hybrid Propulsion Systems, Wireless Medical Devices, Telematics, Vehicle Powertrain Systems, and many other technologies.

Joseph Lemieux’s Specialties:

Systems Engineering of Mechatronic Systems including Powertrain, Automatic and Dual-Clutch Transmissions, Hybrid Propulsion, Anti-Lock Braking, and Medical Devices.

Embedded Systems for Engines, Transmissions, Brakes, Audio, and Vehicle Electronics in Automotive and Military applications. Embedded Systems for Medical Monitoring Devices.

Program Management of multi-million dollar engineering and tooling programs for automotive electronics and interiors. Contract negotiations.
