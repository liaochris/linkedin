    Ivy Augusta

    (Entertainment industry)

    April 2009 — Present (11 months)

    Julia’s passion for music and love of film and television ultimately led her to seek out a career as a music supervisor.

    Landing in the Fox Sports Music Department in 2004, Julia has since been working closely with every aspect of the music and television industry, eventually assuming the lead creative role in the Department alongside department director Jerry Davis.

    Spearheading creative concepts, she worked closely with Fox Sports producers to enhance production value within broadcasts in addition to creating alternative marketing strategies for artists, labels, brands and the network.

    Julia now continues to reach beyond the realm of sports programming to include TV, film, advertising and new media.

  • Show Producer

    Fries on the Side

    (Entertainment industry)

    2006 — Present (4 years )

    Is it possible to create a completely new sketch show every week while giving actors only 2 hours to prepare and teleprompters as a backup?

    Fries on the Side does just that with no compromise to quality. This
    incredible mix of great writing, a cast of Groundlings, IO, and UCB trained performers, a live band and even celebrity hosts guarantees excellence and an absolutely unique and phenomenal show week after week.

    Like any other sketch comedy show, we have a Main Cast and a Writing Staff, however we also accept sketch submissions every week from any freelance writer who wants to contribute, and let actors show up to be cast in sketches any time.

    But here's the catch - the actors aren’t cast until just 2 hours before the show. That means lights, sound, costumes, sets, and rehearsal all has to happen in a couple hours. The actors have teleprompters to fall back on during the show, and the audience can feel the
    energy of risk in the air.
