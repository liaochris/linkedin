Zachary Lezberg’s Summary

I am a very driven and passionate Entrepreneur.

In 2004 I started Quantum NY LLC, a Broadway, Film and Live Events production company. I am currently developing some Broadway shows and produce live events (ShowBiz Expo).

In 2007 I started the "revived" SHOWBIZ EXPO, the world's largest networking event and tradeshow for the Entertainment Industry (ShowBizExpo.com). ShowBiz Expo has been around since the 1980s.

Right out of college I was hired by the leading Broadway Producing, Marketing & Merchandising company - THE ARACA GROUP. Some of Araca's Broadway shows include: WICKED, HAIRSPRAY, JERSEY BOYS, VIEW FROM THE BRIDGE, and many more.

I have worked in Casting and Production for Season Four of NBC's Reality hit television show: "FEAR FACTOR."

Worked for The Production Group at UNIVERSAL STUDIOS FLORIDA - helped with coordinating various film and commercial shoots at the studios.

As a Freshman, I started Quantum Entertainment at the University of Miami. Quantum is a film and theatre production organization completely run by students. The organization produces large scale musicals costing approximately $35k each show. Quantum has successfully produced a number of shows and films including: "ROCKY HORROR SHOW," "A CHORUS LINE," "WEST SIDE STORY," "GREASE," and "HAIR."

If you'd like to contact me directly, please email me at Zach@ShowBizExpo.com.
