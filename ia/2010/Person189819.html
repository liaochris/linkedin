Sima Dahl’s Summary

I wear many hats but at the end of the day I'm a marketer. My passion is helping you tell your story in such a way that it resonates with your target market, raises your visibility and generates leads.

My clients are businesses that need to build a corporate brand and generate sales as well as business professionals who need to build a personal brand and encourage referrals. Through a combination of traditional marketing, branding and social media I help my clients move from Message to Mindshare™.

If you're a busy professional who wants to tap into the power of social networks for business development or career success, I offer Sway Factor™ sales training, workshops and 1:1 coaching. Sway Factor™ is a proven framework for achieving maximum results on sites like Linkedin and Facebook in just minutes a week. My book on the same subject will be published later this year.

I offer keynote speeches on a variety of business marketing themes:
- The Digital State Marketing (business owners / entrepreneurs / retailers)
- Social Media for Business (business owners / entrepreneurs / retailers)
- The Art of Networking in a Digital Age (all rainmakers, ladder climbers and job seekers)
- Speed Networking (works well alone or with any of the topics above)

In addition to maintaining two popular blogs of my own I write about social networking, personal branding and careers for Marketing News Magazine, Social Media Magazine and MarketingPower.com. I am the founder of MarketingJobWire.com, a peer-powered community where Chicagoland marketing, communications and PR professionals share job leads.

Previously I spent 20 years in corporate marketing building marketing campaigns and brand strategies for manufacturing, services and high tech firms. I volunteer for several industry associations and social service organizations and enjoy skiing, cycling, family and friends.

You're still here? Really? Cool. I look forward to meeting you!
