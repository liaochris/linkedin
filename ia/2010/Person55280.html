Desirae Hill’s Summary

A change agent manifested as artist, graphic & web designer, and copywriter, I am driven by communications design. I truly believe the sky is the limit.

Design is both aesthetic and functional and form and experience go hand in hand. I specialize in holistic communications design that encompasses the five senses. I keep my mind on meeting goals while keeping my eye on the details, creating tangible communications systems that work.

My work experience includes retail management, bookkeeping, banking, customer service, coaching and social services as well as freelance consulting and design.

I'm currently looking for the perfect position in Bellingham, Washington while steadily working on a startup that will both provide artistic products and create momentum to support progressive women's movements.
