Thomas Lukasik’s Summary

I'm a hands-on Software Architect and Developer with 25 years of continuous experience meeting information needs and solving IT problems in the areas of Healthcare, Financial Accounting, Manufacturing, Sales and Service, and most recently, Social Networking and Communications.

I'm flexible, resilient and keenly interested in my work. I move along and learn something new every day. If I don't know how, I know how to find out.

I work regularly with both Java and .NET technology and have solid working experience in many languages, although I find that I get almost everything done these days using Java and C#, JSP and ASP.NET, PHP, JavaScript, (X)HTML, XML, JSON and CSS.

Most of the last 10 years have been spent building Portal solutions for the Healthcare Industry, including Clinical and Patient Portals, developing custom HL7 interfaces, and doing Java and .NET integration between both legacy and new applications, systems and services.

Over the past 2 years I've also developed applications using the Facebook and Google App Engine platforms and APIs, and created numerous Web 2.0 applications that included social networking features and functionality, and Twitter integration.

Let's talk about what I can do to help you or your organization meet objectives, solve integration problems, and satisfy information requirements.
