Kurt Scholle’s Summary

Entrepreneur and founder of Web Asylum, Inc and www.Website-ROI-Coach.com. My company, Web Asylum, Inc, has been building and marketing successful websites for more than a decade.

I blog about successful website strategies at www. Website-ROI-Guy.com. I am also a partner in www.CertifiedSocialMedia.com. Website success and social media are passions.

I have been an investor in Internet-related companies in Chicago, including Novacon and SISNA, both of which supplied high speed Internet connections and website hosting.

Prior to the Internet, I spent nearly 25 years in radio as an announcer, program director, newsman and music director, working for some legendary stations: WLW-Cincinnati, WKQX-Chicago, Oldies 104.3-Chicago, WISN-Milwaukee, WIRL-Peoria and the Satellite Music Network and the Interstate Radio Network.

Kurt Scholle’s Specialties:

Online business development. Website architecture, planning and marketing. Competitive website analysis. Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, Guerrilla Marketing. Working to become certified in AdWords.
