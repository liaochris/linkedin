Denys Shortt’s Summary

Denys Shortt, aged 45, has been ranked in Britain's Top 10 Entrepreneurs (Management Today). Winner of Entrepreneur of the Year, Exporter of the Year, CBI Growing Business Awards, Fasttrack 100 and Techtrack 100.

Founder - DCS Europe - a British company doing well - Sales have grown from £100m to £120m in 2009. www.dcscentral.com

Denys uses Linked In extensively.Denys is a power-networker and has built his business through recommendation. He is busy finding exciting new sales channels for brands...the internet, TV stations and more.

DCS is also exporting brands to 70 countries and winning in tough markets including Iraq, Pakistan, and Africa.

Denys grew up in India where his father managed tea estates in Assam. Also lived in Uganda and Kenya. Aged 16 Denys was selected to play hockey for England and played in both indoor and outdoor teams.

DCS comprises of -

DCS Central - UK distributor for Procter & Gamble, Gillette, Unilever, Colgate, SC Johnson, L'Oreal, Sara Lee, www.dcscentral.com

Enliven Health and Beauty - a range of 70 health and beauty products Made in England www.enliven.co.uk

DCS Export - exports to 70 countries world wide www.dcsexport.com - annual sales £9 million

DCS Manufacturing - a new factory producing liquid fill health and beauty products - all Made in England www.dcsmanufacturing.com - Annual sales £6 million

Founder & CEO - Enable Software - www.enable.com we build .net software to replace critical excel spreadsheets and access databases. Clients - Sainsbury, Asda. HSBC, RBS, NCP.

Founder Enable Informatrix www.myeix.com

In his spare time Denys runs a farm in the Cotswolds breeding belted galloway cattle. He is also a keen Robinson R44 helicopter pilot.

Governor at Cheltenham College and helps at the Royal Agricultural College Cirencester.
