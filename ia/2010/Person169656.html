Raymond McCauley’s Summary

Working on practical nanotechnology, genomics, and bioinformatics. Good communicator with an entrepreneurial bent, who enjoys working on small, fast teams in start-ups. Can speak both science and engineering, and translate between the two. 15 years of experience in the biotechnology and high tech arenas, working with Illumina, Ingenuity Systems, QIAGEN Genomics/Rapigene, consultancies with various pharma and governmental agencies, and start-up experience in data mining and media ventures, including Insurgent Technologies and TANSTAAFL Media.
