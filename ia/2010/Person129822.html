    Affluent Magazine

    (Publishing industry)

    March 2004 — Present (6 years 6 months)

    Affluent Magazine is the premier bi-monthly national lifestyle publication for over 400,000 affluent individuals. Our readers, turn to the pages of Affluent for valuable information that will enrich both their personal and professional lives.

    Additionally, onboard outbound flights at 100 FBO’s throughout the US, more than 10,000 copies of Affluent reach the most affluent audience of sophisticated, world travelers every month. The onboard distribution on outbound private jet flights gives Affluent Magazine a national and international readership.

    Affluent Magazine's online version, AffluentMagazine.com is an expanded version of our magazine with content on a wide variety of topics that include but are not limited to: Wealth Management, Gourmet Food, Consumer Electronics, Eco-Friendly Living, Beauty, Entertaining, Luxury Real Estate, Home Furnishings, Fashion, Travel and Leisure, Luxury Products and Services, Wine, Health & Wellness, etc.

    While there may be a lot of great web sites in cyberspace to ‘visit’, I believe in my heart AffluentMagazine.com is the one you will want to call "home." - Madison Freeman, President & Editor-in-Chief
