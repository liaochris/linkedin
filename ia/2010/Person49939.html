Dave Van Horn’s Summary

Create, Conquer, Adapt or Die....

Dear Reader,

If you aren't doing the first three above, you can count on the fourth one to be on your doorstep soon. This is the age of the internet. If you aren't changing, you'll be changed. If you aren't constantly reinventing, you'll be marginalized. If you can't prove your value, you'll be disengaged.

I specialize in helping companies, organizations, individuals, and agencies put their future in sharp relief using today's social media networks, and smart tools to either find or create 'intelligent life' looking for what you have to offer and bring them to your organization.

Does your site sell, or capture prospects, and once it does, do you make them happy? That's what I care about!

While 'usability' matters, ---sales revenue matters more. If people are not getting to your site, what's the point? If they aren't moving through your funnel, I help you understand why, and make adjustments.

I take pride in getting my clients into a position to have the prospect all to themselves. I practice what I preach. You're here aren't you?

If you want to end up on top, please contact me at your earliest convenience, I would love to hear from you.

Thanks for listening.
