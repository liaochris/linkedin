Elizabeth Raymond’s Summary

My immense passion for fitness, nutrition and living a well balanced lifestyle propelled my career change into the fitness industry. A former dancer of over 10 years, yoga enthusiast for over 7 years and personal experience strength and endurance training for over 10 years; becoming a Personal Trainer & Fitness Coach had been the ultimate personal goal. Recent Alumni of American Academy of Personal Training, where I gained a wealth of knowledge both academic and hands-on in kinesiology, nutrition, anatomy and sports specific training. Through this experience I launched a Bootcamp in the South Boston area which quickly grew in popularity and began training privately.
