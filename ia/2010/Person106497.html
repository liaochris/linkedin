Joyce Solano’s Summary

Bilingual entrepreneur and MBA holder, with over a decade of impacful business, marketing and development roles in the consumer goods industry and high-tech. I have great passion for early stage technology startups and cutting edge new media agencies. Great eye for design and smart branding. Strengths in business development, social media marketing, and strategic partnerships.

Wide breadth of experience in both startups and Fortune 500 firms. Guerilla marketing sensibility, self-starter and BIG thinking. Ready to roll up my sleeves and problem solve at anytime. Not a fan of the word's "no", "can't" or "impossible".

Leads a team that provides the roadmap and the execution of social media and integrated marketing campaigns. Joyce has advised clients from the mom and pop level to emerging startups, all the way to world class brands.
