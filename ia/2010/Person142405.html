Michael Doyle’s Summary

An entrepreneur and chief executive whose focus on strategy, innovation, people and process has enabled him to create value for investors in healthcare and technology.

He became president and CEO of Medsphere in late 2007 bringing executive experience working with a variety of software solutions sold on a subscription basis like Medsphere’s OpenVista EHR solution. Most recently, Mike served as president and CEO of Advantedge Healthcare Solutions. Advantedge is a New Jersey-based provider of medical billing software-as-a-service (SaaS) systems to physicians. Earlier, he served as chairman and CEO of Salesnet, one of three SaaS pioneers in the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) marketplace.

Earlier in his career, Mike founded The Standish Care Company, a pioneering assisted-living provider. As chairman and CEO, Mike guided the company through the first successful initial public offering (IPO) for an assisted-living service provider and co-founded the Assisted Living Federation of America (ALFA) to give this new industry a voice. Following a series of mergers and acquisitions, and Standish's eventual merger with Carematrix, he served as CEO of the combined companies under the Carematrix name. Mike's efforts ultimately yielded a highly successful organization with 2000 employees and a market capitalization in excess of $500 million. He has also held senior management positions at Voluntary Hospitals of America (VHA) and National Medical Enterprises, the predecessor to Tenet Healthcare Corporation.

A frequent speaker at industry conferences and university forums on healthcare technology and quality improvement, Mike is a registered Business Wire “ExpertSource” and available to help educate industry, business and consumer audiences. He earned a BS in biology (with a minor in community health) from Tufts University (Medford, Mass.), and an MBA (concentration in healthcare and finance) from the University of Chicago, where he was a Kaiser Fellow.
