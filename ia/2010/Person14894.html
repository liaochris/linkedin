Anthony Knopp’s Summary

Currently serves as President at Spotlight Ticket Management - providing firms with the tools necessary to get the most from their current sports & entertainment spend as well as full service consulting and advisement. I realize that's a mouth full so we've put together an online demo that shows how our tool works: http://www.spotlighttms.com/demo/demo.html

Spotlight centralizes everything to do with sports & entertainment tickets, suites, and events. This spend offers what PWC calls the most potential for immediate savings.

Spotlight currently partners with over 30 teams and is backed by Point Judith Capital along with industry experts including Brian Bedol of Classic Sports Network (now ESPN Classic).
