Robbie Young’s Summary

Mr. Young has been managing a group of talented individuals in the very demanding Electronics/Communications department at Snapping Shoals EMC since late 2000. In addition to direct supervision, planning, and project management, he has extensive experience with various manufacturers substation, distribution control, and communications equipment including Motorola (Harmony/iDen), Cooper (Form4C, Form5, Form6, CL5e, CL6, Peercom), ABB (PCD2000), MDS (Licensed and Unlicensed MAS Radio), S&C (Intelliteam), Schweitzer - SEL (351R), Fisher Pierce (Capacitor Controls, Current Sensors), Lindsey (Current Sensors), QEI (RTUs - 6CPP4, 6ACP4 & 5, Capacitor Controls), Siemens (IJ, MJ3 & 3A, MJX, MJXL), Beckwith, GE Fanuc (F35, Intellusion Master), Schneider Modicon (PLCs), TWACS (Substation Equipement and TNS Operation), Ruggedcom (RS2100, 2200, 9000, 8000, 900), Moxa (Ethernet Switches, Media Converters), and Dymec (Serial and Ethernet Devices). He is also fluent in software and programming of all of the above apparatus and controls including creating detailed custom logic and control schemes.

Robbie Young’s Specialties:

SCADA, TWACS, network and communications system design, installation, and troubleshooting down to the bit level, managing competing demands in a multi-faceted environment.
