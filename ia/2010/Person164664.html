For 12 years, she has tapped into the fascinating power of the internet to help others grow their companies online, create buzz, generate leads and successfully increase profits. Her specialty is designing beautifully crafted, professional websites, memorable to the audience and intimidating to

Marzena's ability to bridge innovative, captivating concepts with clients’ aspirations, offers businesses unique opportunity to continually create and maintain successful relationships with their customers.
