Catie Brand’s Summary

Catie works within Daversa Partners’ Consumer Practice recruiting executives for top tier, venture backed companies in the areas of Consumer Internet, Digital Media and Enterprise Software. She has worked on executive search projects for companies such as Ice.com, Big Fish Games, Playfirst and Lockerz. Catie reports directly to Partner Jamie Sanger who serves as the firm’s resident expert on consumer technology and has performed extensive work for top-tier venture firms such as: Benchmark Capital, Sequoia Capital, Polaris Capital, Kleiner Perkins, Accel Partners, General Catalyst, Mayfield, and Matrix. Catie has a B.A. from Boston University in English and Business Administration and has prior sales and marketing experience in the Fashion and Retail Industry.

Established in 1992, Daversa Partners is North America’s leading retained executive search firm in technology. Our practices focus on Software, Consumer and Services representing leading early-to-mid stage companies, predominantly backed by Tier one and Tier two private equity and venture capital firms. With Daversa Partners you can expect results, speed and proactive recruiting to close the desired candidate. We focus exclusively on recruiting material impact players across the entire technology value chain.
