Julien Condamines’s Summary

In love with foreign languages and cultures, fond of travels, businessman and entrepreneur by nature, I can only imagine myself with a wide autonomy, real responsibilities and risks to handle in an international environment. My taste for innovation and my creativity take me likely towards new and original markets.

In October 2008, I enthusiastically joined the so famous Google, a company reputed for its taste of innovation and its leading position in online advertising. There, I continuously develop my expertise in online advertising to serve as a consultant for the top spender online communication agencies.

Beside that, I launched the Dublin Chapter of EYP, a global networking organization that promote links between business professionals through fun and innovative off line events.

I also created and co-write www.network-rules.com, a French blog that promoted networking as a fun and useful activity.

In background, born hyperactive and insatiable of curiosity, I keep monitoring the market, ready to jump on exciting opportunities, projects to take up on the other side of the globe, new concepts to launch and new partnerships to create.
