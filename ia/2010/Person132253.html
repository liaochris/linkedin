Maria Anderson’s Summary

As the founding director of Sustainable Marketing Services (Australia), Maria Anderson is passionate about creating sustainable profits for growing businesses.

Maria identifies and delivers top performing marketing activities for business in the services, professional services, technical product and green product sectors in Brisbane and throughout Australia.

She is a Certified Practising Marketer with the Australian Marketing Institute with extensive experience in helping growing businesses develop.

Maria has over 18 years experience with small and large brands in developing and implementing marketing plans, marketing campaigns, branding, sales processes, product launches, advertising, online marketing, direct mail, brochures, client surveys, sponsorships and client relationship programs.

Her qualifications include a Bachelor of Business (Marketing), a Diploma in Financial Services, and a Certificate IV in Small Business Management.
