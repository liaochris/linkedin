A. David Schwarz III, SIOR’s Summary

I have been a commercial and industrial Realtor since 1970 in Houston, Harris County, Texas. I have been an SIOR since 1984 and served as President of the Houston Gulf Coast Chapter of the Society of Industrial and Office Realtors. In addition, I served as a Director for the Houston Assocaition of Realtors and theTexas Assocaition of Realtors. I currently and have served on the Houston Association of Realtors PAAG since 1998. Most recently I was reelected to serve at the Commercial Member of the Houston Association of Realtors Board of Directors for 2010-2011 and the Texas Association of Realtors Board of Directors for 2010-2012.

A. David Schwarz III, SIOR’s Specialties:

Although, I have brokered transactions in nearly all areas of commerial, investment and industrial real estate I mainly specialize in land for users and developers, tenant representation on a select basis and investment sales.
