Michael Tipper’s Summary

Recently voted Best New Speaker in 2007 by the Academy of Chief Executives, Michael Tipper is an experienced speaker, facilitator, presenter and writer specialising in the practical business applications of Accelerated Learning principles and techniques, Mind Mapping, Creativity, Speed Reading and Memory Improvement. Over 80,000 people have directly experienced his live presentations and trainings, and more than half a million have benefited from programmes he has developed.

His clients include IBM, Shell, MOD, Grant Thornton, Eircom, The Prudential, Royal Sun Alliance, John Sisk, The CIPD and Smith Williamson. The author of three books his 15 minutes of fame includes appearances on The Big Breakfast, The Guinness Show, Sky News and Amazing People and has featured in articles in The Daily Mail, The Guardian and The Times. He regularly appears on Local BBC radio shows around the UK.

A Chartered Engineer by profession and 16 years service in the Royal Naval Submarine Service, Michael developed his practical use and understanding of effective thinking tools in a dynamic and ever changing operational and then business environment.

He came second in the World Memory Championships in order to demonstrate that anyone can take simple concepts to use the mind more effectively and get much more from it. Michael achieved the coveted Grand Master of Memory award, ran and managed the World Championships for two years and is now considered to be one of the leading exponents of memory improvement techniques in the UK.

Michael’s strength comes not only from his deep knowledge of his subject but from his enthusiasm and ability to convey relevant ideas and concepts to people at all levels in an entertaining, enjoyable and practical way. Tony Buzan, the inventor of the Mind Map says of Michael:

"Michael Tipper is an entertaining, informative and energetic speaker who is emerging as an inspirational teacher of Mentally Literate principles"

Michael Tipper’s Specialties:

Practical business application of Mind Maps and Mind Mapping, speed reading, memory improvement, accelerated learning, creativity, training and development, powerfully liberating group facilitation, interactive and engaging workshops, entertaining and tailored keynote speeches
