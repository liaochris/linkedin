Jared Levine’s Summary

I specialize in the acquisition / disposition of Multifamily assets (apartment buildings) located in Los Angeles, Hollywood/West Hollywood, Korea Town, and The San Fernando Valley. Up-to-the-minute research, intense dedication, and a commitment to client satisfaction has allowed me to become a expert in Multifamily Investments.

I started my professional life by founding and managing an international apparel consultant agency. This unique opportunity afforded me the opportunity of gaining experience in business development, risk evaluation, negotiations, and client relations.

Having gained valuable business experience and a bachelor of science from California State University of Northridge, I accepted the opportunity to advance my career and become an Investment specialist with RE/MAX COMMERCIAL.

After a year and a half at RE/MAX COMMERCIAL where I closed over $19 Million Dollars worth of multifamily reale state, I left to advance my brokerage career and become Vice President at KW COMMERCIAL new office in Calabasas California
