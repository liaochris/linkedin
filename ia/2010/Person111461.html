Kate Bernhardt’s Summary

I am a media professional with more than 25 years of experience in the production and business sides of film, TV, multimedia, and the Internet. After one career, as an award-winning producer/director/writer of many nationally and internationally recognized projects, including PBS science and arts programs, I left the world of film and television in the early 1990s, intrigued by the power and challenge of new media. I was a key team member in creating the groundbreaking multimedia biography "A Jack Kerouac ROMnibus" and have been part of several pioneering Web content ventures. I have had extensive experience as a senior manager for Web and multimedia content initiatives, acting as project leader, editorial director, and creative contributor to those products. Recently, I have added large community events management to my portfolio of activities.

Kate Bernhardt’s Specialties:

Creativity and creative management, collaborative leadership, complex problem-solving, marketing and communicaitons expertise, legacy building.
