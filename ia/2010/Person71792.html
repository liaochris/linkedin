David French’s Summary

I am a seasoned marketing and communications executive with 11 years of high-profile media, brand and crisis experience across the media, non-profit and communication agency sectors. I am currently excited to be working to protect and preserve America's iconic treasures, from the Grand Canyon to the Statue of Liberty, by overseeing all marketing and communications for the National Park Foundation, www.nationalparks.org.

Prior to this role, I was VP, Communications at MTV: Music Television where I managed the network's day-to-day communications efforts across the business including programming, public affairs, ad sales, social media and more.

Outstanding track record of developing and implementing long and short-term multi-platform communications and marketing strategies, creating engaging Web 2.0 and social media campaigns, managing crisis communications, leading and mentoring staff, media training executives and orchestrating high-profile events.
