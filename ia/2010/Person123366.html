Lee Cottier’s Summary

I'm a Trainer, Teacher, Coach and Facilitator.

I combine a strong commercial training background with the mindset of an educator, being a fully qualified teacher as well. My key skills include training design and delivery, coaching, action learning and event design and facilitation.

In my work I help individuals, teams and organisations achieve more through two complementary service lines:

Learning and Development
- acquiring new knowledge, skills and behaviours.

> Creative, engaging, supportive and transferable learning experiences
> Training needs analysis, training design, delivery, facilitation, evaluation
> Leadership, management and team development
> Personal development and "soft" skills
> Communication, presentation skills and "train the trainer"

Productivity and Effectiveness
- putting these abilities into practice to best effect.

> Personal coaching and team training
> Time management (and *so* much more!), email management etc.
> Personal organisation, workflow and motivation

Lee Cottier’s Specialties:

Learning and Development, Productivity and Effectiveness.
