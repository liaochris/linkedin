Olivia Yeung’s Summary

Olivia Yeung is the Marylhurst University Mentor Program Coordinator and connects students, alumni and community members to support personal and professional growth.

She has an extensive background in volunteerism and student support programs. Olivia is also a board member of the Ford Scholar Alumni Association Board of Directors. Her love for advocating "green" issues and solutions has also lead her to be an active member of the Marylhurst University Sustainability Advisory Council (MUSAC).

Olivia believes the quote from the late Gandhi, "Be the change you wish to see in the world" are not just words, but a way of life. Her talents in project management, communication, organization and passion to help empower others makes her exceptional in creating partnerships, public presentations and event planning.

She currently resides in Clackamas, OR with her family.

Olivia Yeung’s Specialties:

Volunteer recruitment and retention. Workshop and event planning. Public speaking and professional presentations. Online marketing and social media (Constant Contact, Informz, Facebook, LinkedIn, Wordpress).
