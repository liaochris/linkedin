Michael Long’s Summary

Briefly after college, I started in the recruiting field. Since then, it's been an amazing journey!

Having worked my way up from the most entry-level position to a Branch Manager, I feel grateful for having received exposure to many facets of the recruiting industry. I worked hard, but, more importantly, I had some fantastic people around me... mentors, recruiters and overall superstars!

In 2007, I moved to San Antonio, TX and accepted a role as a Director over a Professional Services division at a San Antonio-based search firm.

In 2008, I started my own business and called it Red Recruiting. Shortly thereafter, I started a blog called The Red Recruiter. TRR opened a number of doors and exposed me to what ended up being a huge passion... employment branding.

In 2010, I accepted a role with Rackspace Hosting to oversee our Global Talent Branding initiatives. It's an amazing organization and I'm very grateful to be a part of the team!
