Shana Douglas’s Summary

High performance marketing strategist with 13 years of sales and business development experience. Expertise includes problem analysis, project management, branding, operation and systems management, client relationship development and management, SEO/SEM, Social Media, Communications.

I have spent the last 6 years in the Mergers & Acquisitions industry assisting both brokers and business owners in maximizing their Exit Strategy preparations and goals. I have personally assisted with over 40 transactions in a wide range of industries and markets with business valuations, research, target fielding and optimizing marketability.

In addition to my full-time role at Sunbelt, I am an active member and volunteer to several Dayton Organizations. I currently offer Social Media insights and coordination for the Dayton Tool and Manufacturing Association (DTMA), The Greater Dayton Advertising Assoc., March of Dimes Miami Valley Division and the Dayton Chapter of the American Marketing Assoc. Consequently, this has also given me the opportunity to assist many great business owners across the region to engage in social media.
