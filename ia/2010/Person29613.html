Brooke Kenney’s Summary

I work strictly with ad agencies and design firms to develop concepts and headlines. My experience makes me a quick study.

Quarter after quarter, Miami Ad School has hired me as a typography instructor, so I bring type knowledge to every project I touch. I also design advertising in a multitude of styles; direct photographers and illustrators, and work with production crafts people to develop tasteful and well-executed end products.

The collaborative give-and-take of the creative process has always been a strength, but I no longer have a desire to manage creative people or direct freelance clients. I’d love the opportunity to work with your people, inhouse — or you can give me a solo project to work on in my studio. If needed, I can draw upon my well-established creative network to fill-in any specialty gaps. Travel outside of the Minneapolis market is welcomed.

You can reach me at 612-659-9970, on my cell at 612-655-8954, or by e-mail at brooke@brotheroke.com.
