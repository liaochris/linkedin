Kathy Jones LION kathy@kathyjonesrealty.com’s Summary

Please join my Short Sale Specialist group here on LinkedIN as well..OR Houston Association of Realtors Networking Group w/ Subgroups....Please join my Real Estate for sale in Greece Group OR Hot On Property International Listing Service

I am a real estate broker associate/investor who enjoys helping my clients buy or sell real estate.

I began my real estate career in 2004 primarily as an investor, where I was doing short sales before they became the "Transaction Du Jour". Most of the properties I own today were purchased through my work on a short sale. As I began doing more business as a "realtor", I discovered how truly passionate I am about this industry, and I loved what I did for a living. How many of us can say that?

A lifelong learner I am; I began networking, reading, taking classes, earning designations... basically doing whatever I could to learn as much as possible about this industry. I earned my Broker's license in 2009; I am also a Certifed Relocation Specialist and an Accredited Buyer's Representative.

My business has branched off the short sale track and into the luxury home market. I find myself doing a little bit of both. I am interested in selling homes all over the world as well as Houston. I plan on launching a new website in the early 2010 www.HotOnProperty.com where I will showcase properties that are for sale or lease from around the world.

I absolutely love the real estate industry. It is exciting, rewarding, and best of all .... challenging. I love a good challenge.

Immediate goals: 1. Sell lots of real estate 2. Acquire as many investment properties as I can and rent them out (isn't being a landlord great?) 3. Launch a phenomenal International Listing Service Website called HotOnProperty.com.

Gonna bust my tail to meet these goals doing what I love to do, sell real estate. Financially free is the only way to be!
