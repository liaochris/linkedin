Paul Setti, Jr, Assoc. AIA, CSI’s Summary

An accomplished Residential Designer who has been practicing architecture for more than twenty years in South Carolina and Connecticut. Specializing in the design of Custom Homes, Additions and Renovations. I enjoy designing homes unprejudiced of style or size and am able to guide the client from design through construction in all phases of the project. Service, attention to detail and communication are my primary tools for building a strong relationship with clients and builders resulting in a great project experience for the client and project team through out the lifetime of the project.

Paul Setti, Jr, Assoc. AIA, CSI’s Specialties:

Site Planning, Code Research, Schematic Design and Planning, Construction Documentation, Specification Writing, Construction Contract Administration, Client Relations, Construction Products and Methods.
