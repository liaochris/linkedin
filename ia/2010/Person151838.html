Natasha Perez’s Summary

I have a diverse career background ranging from 8 years in the biotech industry to my current position as Director of Sales at the Homewood Suites San Diego Del Mar. I have recently switched gears to focus on management contracts and acquiring new properties within our hotel's management company.
Many people ask, "Why did you go from science to hospitality?" The answer is simple; I can blend my analytical skills with my sales skills to execute a smarter, better plan faster than the competition can.
I fell in love with science in college when I took my first college level chemistry class. I thoroughly enjoyed working on projects that we hoped would eventually eradicate certain diseases, and make animals healthier resulting in a better food source for humans. What I was lacking in my science career was the chance to touch people on a day to day basis and make their lives more enjoyable.

Hospitality gives me that opportunity. I am able to combine my analytical and problem solving skills from the biotech industry with my passion for hospitality, business and travel to create a win-win situation for the company that I represent while negotiating solutions and creating value for my clients. The hospitality industry thrives and is sustained by personal relationships. These relationships are what make me love my job every day.

I have several goals which include broadening my hospitality experience, earning my MBA, completing the Ironman Triathlon, traveling the world, learning more languages, and working on site selection and development of new / re-branded hotels.

At the end of the day what really matters to me is having a happy, healthy family, good friends, and people that care about you.

Natasha Perez’s Specialties:

Full-service resort, extended stay, focused service, SMERF, IBT, contract negotiations, marketing plans, social media, trade agreements and group business. On the biotech side my experience lies in DNA extraction and purification, protein purification and quantitative analysis, cell culture, primary preparations, DNA sequencing, RNA extraction and purification, virus construction and purification.
