Andrew Heller’s Summary

Heller Capital has provided financing for start-up companies and acquisitions in manufacturing, financial services, health care, real estate development and finance, and natural gas exploration and development.

Heller Capital provided the founding capital for American Finance & Investment -- the first company to originate a home mortgage over the Internet.

Mr. Heller is Chairman and CEO of Putnam Precision Products, a contract manufacturer of high precision machined components for surgical devices and other industries which he purchased in 1989.

Mr. Heller is also a principal of Ironshore Capital, LLC, a purchaser of distressed residential and commercial real estate properties and debt.

He is also an Advisory Board Member of Veritat Advisors, a full service, objective, fee only, low cost personal financial planning service that combines online efficiency with a human interface.

In addition, he is a director of Emco Energy, a developer of natural gas, and of United Plant Products, a distributor of organic fertilizer.

After serving as a member of the Wharton Graduate Advisory Board for 11 years, Mr. Heller is now a member of the Board of Overseers of the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. He is also a former chair of the Wharton Fund.

In 2004 Mr. Heller received the annual Joseph Wharton Award for public service and leadership from the Wharton School Club of Washington, DC.

He has also served as a trustee and board officer of the Langley School in McLean, VA and as chair of the Campaign for Langley’s Future.

Mr. Heller is the trustee of the Andrew M. Heller Family Foundation which focuses primarily on educational philanthropy.
