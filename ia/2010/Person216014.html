Winnie Anderson, SMS, SPHR, CPBS’s Summary

Winnie is an Online Business Manager / Virtual Chief Marketing Officer who helps small and mid-sized companies position themselves as leaders and attract more clients. These organizations are ready to make the next big leap in their growth and want to attract better qualified leads, build strong relationships with current and past clients, and close more sales.

Winnie creates and executes an integrated marketing strategy that's particularly focused on leveraging the web. She and her team communicate your message through your website / blog , through social media / networking tools like Facebook, Twitter, and You Tube. She ensures you're using all the appropriate channels to connect with your audience and grow your reputation.

Her marketing strategies and copywriting have helped businesses achieve multiple first page rankings in search engines, set revenue records, and won a silver 2007 International Davey Award. She also coaches, consults, and trains business owners on how to craft and spread their message, how to generate a steady flow of leads, and how to use online social networking tools to build a strong brand and engage audience members.
