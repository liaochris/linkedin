Yol Swan’s Summary

I have been involved with art and the creative process since childhood, in one way or another. I like to bring my sense of balance, depth, and color to every aspect of my life and work, whether it is creating jewelry, making a collage, or designing a web site or a t-shirt.

I have been creating 2D and 3D collages since I was a little girl. For me, life truly is a collage of learning experiences, glued together by destiny and karma. Now I design fine jewelry collages. My designs are not just about beauty or fashion. I have to resonate with the energy of a finished jewelry piece or graphic design. It has to have some uplifting value or depth of meaning, not just be pretty or fashionable.

I draw my inspiration mainly from the Hindu and Buddhist spiritual traditions and ethnic cultures of the world, as well as from my interest in the symbolic and psychological aspects of the mind and my exploration of the human subconscious. I am a self-taught designer and business manager, and utilize those talents to grow and promote my business, as well as to share my marketing experience with others.

I am also a poet and creative writer, and was involved with the publishing world (writing, editing, translating, proofreading, transcribing, and doing layout) for over 20 years. I am in the process of writing a couple books, one is a (mystic) poetry collection and the other one is on self-healing utilizing my practical understanding of yoga philosophy and psychology, my life and spiritual experiences, and my never-ending exploration of the mind and its unconscious processes.

I still work as a free lance editor, proofreader, and transcriptionist, as well as a voice talent, in case you need any of these services! I speak English, French, and Spanish fluently.
