Bo Buckley’s Summary

I founded Fearmakers in 1999 to fabricate props and effects for film, television, attractions and exhibits. In 2002, the company reshaped its objectives when we began the development of our first feature film, DEATH4TOLD. The film featured actors like Margot Kidder and Tom Savini as well as rising talent including Rich Sommer who has gone on to appear in The Devil Wears Prada and the Golden Globe winning Mad Men on AMC. Our second feature, DISMAL, will be out in 2009 followed by our creature feature, SAVAGE, starring Tony Becker (TVs Tour of Duty), Martin Kove (The Karate Kid, Rambo) and Lisa Wilcox (Nightmare on Elm Street 4&5).

Our goals are to produce quality horror, sci-fi and suspense thriller entertainment with mass market appeal. By using the collective efforts of highly creative and resourceful artists and filmmakers, Fearmakers is able to produce quality entertainment on a low budget.

Fearmakers is currently developing several projects we hope to produce later this year.

Outside of Fearmakers, I have worked on many independent features, commercials and TV shows as AD or UPM. This past year alone I worked on 3 feature and 2 TV shows as AD or UPM including GHOST TOWN: THE MOVIE which should be released later this year and THE LENA BAKER STORY shot in Georgia.
