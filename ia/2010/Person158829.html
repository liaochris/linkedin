Pamela Ruiz’s Summary

Pamela Ruiz has over ten years of comprehensive experience in the areas of international education, staff development and project and volunteer management. Her career has also included work in translation and international relocation. Ms. Ruiz has lived, studied and worked in Italy, and has traveled through Europe, the Caribbean, the Middle East and North Africa. An experienced director in the field of international education and exchange, Ms. Ruiz is also a knowledgeable facilitator and speaker for workshops and intercultural meetings.

Pamela Ruiz’s Specialties:

Bilingual in Italian and English. Basic knowledge of Spanish. International education, exchange; study abroad and international internships; staff development and volunteer management. Nonprofit management, public speaking, international affairs, event planning, networking.
