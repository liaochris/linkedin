Sally English’s Summary

If you searched for homes in the Emory University, Centers for Disease Control, VA Medical Center neighborhoods in Atlanta, GA; as a Realtor, prospective buyer or potential seller; you encountered active listings by The English Team. The English Team averages 60 to 70 transactions per year and our website at www.englishteam.com is a primary stop for sellers and buyers in our neighborhoods.

Since 1982 we have assisted families in their quest for well located homes with high financial and emotional value in the Metro Atlanta area. The English Team looks forward to providing you with the same excellent and professional service hundreds of Atlanta home owners have already experienced.

The English Team ranked # 3 in real estate commisssions earned in DeKalb County in 2008. Why? We are honest and forthright in our dealings with buyers, sellers, fellow Realtors, lenders and closing attorneys. We earn our fees by providing excellent service and strive to improve every situation we encounter.

English Team listings are featured on over 50 websites and we maintain our own website at www.englishteam.com so we know our content is reaching interested buyers and sellers.

English Team sellers are updated immediately upon receipt of Realtor feedback from showings. Sellers are given real time market valuations and we tour each home before providing advice about staging, repairs and pricing. Feedback from sellers gives evidence our advice is valuable for decision making by motivated sellers.

English Team buyers are prepped for mortgage loans, coached on market conditions and have a game plan prepared for a successful home search.

Relocation services know The English Team provides fair and honest service and we achieve outstanding results.

Because we do the little things right, our clients refer us to their associates, friends and family when real estate advices is needed. That is a compliment we always appreciate.
