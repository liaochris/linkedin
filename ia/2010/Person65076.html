Ennie Lim’s Summary

In the past year, I was an Associate for a private equity firm, Diamond Pacific Capital, based in Los Angeles, I created value for the firm's current portfolio, conducted research, analysis and assisted in the execution of new opportunities, furthermore I launched the firm's website and engaged with the firm's marketing and communications.

Prior to Diamond Pacific Capital, I spent the last 3 years in Hong Kong as a certified broker and business development manager at Aon Corporation, a leading provider of risk management services, where I focused on sales and closing high-profile deals with high net worth individuals across South East Asia. Aside from advising high profile clients on risk management, I also secured new partnerships with leading financial institutions, such as Merrill Lynch, UBS, Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs and Credit Suisse.

Earlier in my career, I also have experience working in the entertainment and fashion retail industry, first by being Research & Leasing Analyst and Marketing Coordinator for Aldo Group and Point-Zero Canada in Montreal, Quebec, and later a Marketing and Public Relations Manager for M1NT Hong Kong Limited, a private shareholder’s club in Hong Kong where I organized celebrity events, fashion shows for the media, and also helped the company’s expansion into Shanghai.

Furthermore, I have fulfilled my desire to travel and to further understand diverse cultures when I visited Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, Japan, Hong Kong, China, Indonesia, Dubai & London. Also, my passion in practicing Bikram yoga has provided me balance with my body and mind.

I am confident that my unique marketing background spanning a number of major industry sectors is a valuable asset that will help me succeed at your company and make me adaptable to the diverse marketing landscape which your firm thrives in.
