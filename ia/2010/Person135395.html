Martin Cerda’s Summary

Founded in 1989, Encuesta, Inc. is one of the largest independent U.S. Hispanic survey research and opinion polling companies in the industry.

We live and work by a simple idea: Explore. Interpret. Empower.® For our clients. For our employees. And for our community.

Martin Cerda’s Specialties:

Martin Cerda’s Experience

  • Founder and Research Director

    Encuesta, Inc.

    (Market Research industry)

    June 1989 — Present (20 years 11 months)

    Founded in 1989, Encuesta, Inc. is a leading U.S. Hispanic market research and public opinion polling company that guides decision makers using proven quantitative and online qualitative research designs and objective analysis. Backed by exceptional technical ability and highly experienced in-house resources, we are known for our creative and sophisticated solutions. Encuesta, Inc. is also responsible for realizing the Americanos Poll®, a pro bono public opinion poll created in 2005 to help bridge the knowledge gap that exists regarding the U.S. Hispanic community.

    So how can we help? Simply put, Encuesta, Inc. has the experience, knowledge and expertise to help you design and conduct research that:

    Is valid and reliable (using traditional, online and hybrid methodologies);

    Provides culturally relevant insights into consumer motivation, attitudes and behavior;

    Is defendable to the point that it generates consensus and confidence in the recommended action; and

    Stimulates your organization so that it will support attempts to grow volume profitably.
