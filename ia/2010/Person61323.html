Ethan Smith’s Summary

Digital media is my passion. I know every aspect of it to an almost unhealthy degree. I am the person that is always thinking ten steps ahead. I have a background rooted in visual design and multimedia, but my real strength lies in user experience and digital architecture. Usable, useful and desirable digital products are the result of engaging people in a way that is meaningful to them, not the result of a ten step list of usability rules.

I can talk to designers and programmers with ease. Better yet, I can make them understand each other.
