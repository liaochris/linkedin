Peter Vogt’s Summary

GESD Capital Partners is a private equity firm based in San Francisco which makes investments in middle-market companies in the consumer, restaurant, entertainment and business services sectors.  The firm manages over $250 million in equity capital for its limited partners and is currently investing out of GESD Investors II, the firm’s second institutional investment fund.

GESD focuses on making investments in the following industries:

▪ Consumer, with a particular interest in food and beverage
▪ Restaurant, company-owned or franchised
▪ Entertainment
▪ Business Services, including professional, health and other businesses

GESD targets companies that possess the following characteristics:

▪ Revenues between $25 million and $200 million
▪ Cash flow or EBITDA up to $20 million
▪ Private or publicly held businesses
▪ Stable history of profitability or cash flow
▪ Proven and experienced management teams
▪ Significant opportunity for growth internally and/or through acquisitions
▪ Diversified customer base and strong customer relationships
▪ Add-on acquisition opportunities that may or may not have the above characteristics
