Stephanie DeGraw’s Summary

We have now have four companies:

1. Power Media Group www.powermedia.us
A full service advertising and public relations agency with video and radio production services.

2. Power Media Entertainment www.powermediaentertainment.com
A music artist management group, including branding, distribution, booking, publicity, music video and more.
Need some Public Relations for your company or next album, festival or project? We specialize in getting you known! We write, distribute press releases and follow up to see that they are printed. We put together media tours. We can get your CD into retail outlets if Artist or Band is ready for that step.

3. Power Media Records - currently have a national distribution channel into major retail stores, contact us for details.

4. Power Publishing


We also offer TV show creation, production and placement. have an in-house TV and radio studio.

Power Media filmed the Weber State Coach's Corner and football games for three years. The programs were picked up by FOX College Sports Network FCSN and broadcast nationwide.

DeGraw has 25 years experience in public relations, advertising, journalism and broadcasting. She has a Bachelor of Science from Weber State University in Broadcasting & Journalism.

During the Olympics, she was the TV Station Manager of ch.16 the ION Network (formerly PAX) in Salt Lake City. She recently served as Vice President of the Historic 25th Street Association, is on the Arts Festival board and in Women in Business. She was just inducted into the LOGSTAR group of local leaders that work with Hill Airforce Base in Utah.
