  • Vice President and Managing Director

    NExT - Network of Excellence In Training

    (Education Management industry)

    February 2010 — Present (11 months)

    One of the most serious challenges in today’s oil and gas industry is a profound weakness in the supply chain of human talent. For many years in the 1980’s and 90’s the low price of oil curtailed recruiting in most oil and gas companies and today we are paying the price. Although recruiting has now picked up, lack of recruiting in the past causes a permanent shortage of skilled professionals in the mid-career age range. This has prompted the entire sector to rethink how professionals, and particularly young professionals, can develop in an accelerated timeframe to fill the gap.

    NExT presents a new learning paradigm that addresses this issue, and that has application also for other industries besides the oil and gas sector. It builds on benchmark studies that identify the elements of an efficient, fit-for-purpose professional development program. These elements include using competency management as an essential baseline, a mix of learning styles to provide variety and relevance, including on-the-job training, and mentoring to ensure a hands-on passing-on of human know-how and experience.
    NExT is a unique partnership with Texas A&M University, Oklahoma University, Heriot Watt University and Schlumberger to develop world-class competency for tomorrow E&P Challenges.

    Selected Achievements
    • Directed a team of 250+ of consultants and Subject Matter Experts to deliver on the various programs and courses and generated over 30MM$ in revenue for 2010.
    • Successful implementation of technical programs for key Oil and Gas private and governmental companies to accelerate by 25% the time to autonomy of their young workforce.
    • Became a trusted advisor to several government directorates, assisting them in the nationalization plans of their Oil and Gas workforce.
    • Launched the digital workbook and working on setting up the social learning consulting.
    • Established key programs around Customer Loyalty, ROI in Learning and Blended Learning Methods.
