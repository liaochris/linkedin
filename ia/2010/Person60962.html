Damion Wasylow’s Summary

18 years in the ad game and I'm only 33 - not bad! I started out making ads look pretty, now I make them make sense. I can write you a headline, write you a jingle or write you a sonnet. (Admittedly, the sonnet business is kind of slow right now.) I've directed dozens of award-winning TV and radio spots and written fascinating copy on everything from AIDS awareness to compost heaps. Heck, even this little blurb sounds pretty good! Words have value and crafting the perfect message is often the difference between failure and success.

Health education and behavioral modification campaigns, telecommunications marketing and retirement living promotion.
