Richard Parker’s Summary

I'm a co-owner and director of a strategic marketing and design agency based in Clerkenwell, London. I'm responsible for developing marketing strategy and campaigns for our clients, and for bringing in new business.

I have spent the past 8 years developing and running integrated content marketing campaigns with branded content agencies. I have experience working across web design and build, eCRM, e-commerce, email marketing, SEO and SEM, social media and customer magazines. Before this I spent 2 years working in sales promotions following a degree in economics.

Sector experience includes retail (ASOS.com, Duchy Originals, Capital Shopping Centres, Fortnum & Mason, Vision Express, Waterstone’s), finance (Churchill, Coutts, Direct Line, Norwich Union, RAC), public sector (The Open University) and healthcare (Janssen-Cilag, Johnson & Johnson).

I have an uncompromising attitude toward creative execution, I love content (when used in the right way), and have a passion for measurement and evaluation - if we can't tell whether it's effective, there's no point doing it.

Richard Parker’s Specialties:

Specialities include e-commerce marketing, social media strategies, CRM/eCRM and email campaigns, analytics, measurement and evaluation (including KPI setting), distribution strategy.
