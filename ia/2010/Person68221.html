Everett Bracken ebracken@CareerPartnersUSA.com’s Summary

Career Partners USA, Inc. is a Global Human Resource Solutions Corporation that specializes in EXECUTIVE RECRUITING, CONTRACT LABOR & OUTPLACEMENT. The company works on assignments worldwide: North America (35%), Asia-Pacific (30%), Latin America (15%), & Europe - Middle East - Africa (20%). Headquartered in Lexington, Kentucky, in the United States, we have international offices and subsidiaries located worldwide. The company operates 24 hours per day / 7 days per week and is often hired for "Diversity Recruiting" (i.e., women, African American, Hispanic, disability, & global experience). We are:

* Known for our "aspirational hiring strategy" with our global customers in every industry (i.e., aerospace/defense, banking, biotechnology, chemical, computer peripherals, consumer goods, federal government, healthcare, hospitals, medical instruments, pharmaceutical, retail & sports leagues).

* Focused on recruiting qualified, passive candidates on our client's behalf through networking, referrals, and cold calls for corporate roles (i.e., accounting / finance, communications, engineering, HR, IT, logistics / supply chain, loss prevention, marketing, sales, strategy, and visual presentation).

* Hired primarily because of our SPEED (in 2010, we are averaging 10 days per recruiting assignment globally) and LARGE POOL OF DIVERSITY CANDIDATES. Plus, we are former corporate executives that have done these jobs before.
