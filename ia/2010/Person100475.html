Joe Caston’s Summary

Joe Caston is a successful entrepreneur and leader with 20 years of experience in sales management, finance and general management in the information technology and software industries. Joe helps companies grow rapidly by developing effective sales and marketing strategies, recruiting top-notch talent, building results-oriented teams, and implementing solid financial management processes.

Caston joined Cadre Technologies in 2006 as CEO in a difficult turnaround situation. He integrated an acquired company into a single team, achieving growth and market development plans while managing the P&L. Joe restructured key agreements with senior and sub lenders. Joe built the company quickly and profitably. He increased EBITDA by 1,800 percent and raised millions of dollars in equity and debt financing.

Joe previously worked as vice president and COO for ToolWatch Corporation, where he was responsible for achieving growth and market development plans while managing profit and loss. At ToolWatch, he increased company sales by 39 percent and net income by more than 1,700 percent. Previously, he held senior-level sales, marketing, and general management positions with various companies. Among these are Sprint, Infolink Audit Technologies, Sales Prodigy, and Covad. He ran the Western region of the national accounts division for Sprint. He also has been recognized as a seven-time member of Sprint’s President’s Club.

He started and built InfoLink Audit Technologies, a software asset management company into a multi-million dollar business, and sold that business to Software Artistry.

Joe is a member of ExecQuity, a forum for corporate CEO's and ExO, an executive organization that serves more than 5000 C-level executives. Joe holds a bachelor of science degree in marketing and business administration from the University of Nebraska.

Joe Caston’s Specialties:

Start-ups, turnarounds, marketing, SEO, lead generation, business development, sales management, expert negotiation, mergers and acquisition, general management, and equity & debt financing.
