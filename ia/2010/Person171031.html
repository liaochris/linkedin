Renae Romans’s Summary

Print Production Manager and Project Manager with over 17 years of experience.

My three-pronged approach to print production involves supporting the creative team to bring their ideas to life with the latest techniques and innovations available, listening to the account team to meet the client's budget and timeline parameters, and creating long-lasting relationships with vendors who help educate and keep me up-to-date in our ever-changing marketplace. I have a keen eye for problem-solving, I strive to be proactive versus reactive, and I am very flexible, and I adapt well to change.
