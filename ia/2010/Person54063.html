Dee Schiavelli’s Summary

Dee Schiavelli, founder of Results Marketing, is a strategic business partner to lawyers and other professionals. She advises and coaches clients on effective ways to develop new business and create client loyalty. Dee is a professional speaker and trainer and trains lawyers and other professionals to: improve business development skills, design client satisfaction programs, implement strategic marketing plans and initiatives, and significantly improve ROI of current marketing activities.

Dee has more than 25 years of experience working with lawyers and other providers of professional services. She served in senior marketing roles at several top-tier law and accounting firms in New York for many years before founding Results Marketing. Dee has worked extensively with firm leadership, practice area and industry management and individuals — helping them to develop exisitng client relationships and respond to new business opportunities.
