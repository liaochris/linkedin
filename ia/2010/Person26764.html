  • Partner

    XCD Performance Consulting

    (Privately Held; Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    September 2005 — Present (5 years 3 months)

    • Led ten different labor management programs across a variety of industries. Responsible for program adoption and financial results. Projects ranged in size, product and process complexity and team structure yet every project exceeded its financial projections. Managed and trained project teams of engineers, operation specialists, and XCD consultants. Trained entire management teams, and implemented various labor management systems (LMS).

    • Project lead for supply chain execution strategy/implementation for a high-end consumer products brand. Evaluated and prioritized strategic alternatives; mapped all supply chain execution processes; facilitated three different teams to a successful consensus for shifting from 3PL to an in-source solution. Led the on-site implementation including facility renovation, material handling layout, WMS start-up, process and methods standardization, transition management and organizational development. Results showed cost savings, lead-time reduction and ship-complete improvements.

    • Project Manager for a big box retailer. Led the redesign, testing, training and implementation of the network wide warehouse management solution (WMS), integrating industry best practices and long-term workforce motivation strategies in order to improve process and employee tracking capabilities while minimizing or reducing labor requirements. Project positively impacted well over a thousand associates at five distribution centers.

    • Helped multiple clients recover operational productivity and throughput after WMS implementations by focusing on root cause analyses, process and system optimization. These quick recoveries also required proper execution by management and associates so there was additional focus placed on conducting brainstorming sessions to generate and garner commitment to new ideas in conjunction with conducting detailed and extensive process and system training.
