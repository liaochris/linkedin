Arline Wall’s Summary

Executive with global supervisory roles in the development, marketing and merchandising of billion dollar consumer brands at international Fortune 500 companies such as Toys ‘R’ Us.

Expertise in turn-key product management of consumer packaged goods with award-winning results. Proficient in all phases of product development from concept through product design, sourcing, testing, production, packaging, pricing and vendor management. Accomplished at creating in-house concepts, revitalizing mature brands, enhancing inventor submissions, and launching licensed products.

Skilled in marketing and retail merchandising strategies such as defining and adjusting brand positioning, determining target customer and occasion, brand promise/value proposition, competitive advantage and gaps in brand performance/style against competition (competitive mapping), and customer expectations.

Category management experience in accessories, apparel, electronics, gift, home décor, jewelry, plush, seasonal, sporting goods, stationery, tabletop and toys. Distribution experience in book, convenience, drug, grocery, mass and specialty channels.
