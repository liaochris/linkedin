Jenaro Sanchez’s Summary

Founder & Networking Officer with Redes Ejecutivas to serve INCAE Business School Alumni and their professional communities.
Business Developer, Promoter & Project Manager.
Particularly interested in new ventures arising from CAFTA to manufacture industrial goods in Central America for export to the US.
CAFTA is the US-Central America free trade agreement.

Problem solver executive/entrepreneur with 30+ years experience in multiple industries with emphasis in chemical industry, cement production and consulting.

Skilled in setting strategic and tactical direction, leading high-performance teams that cross both organizational and geographical boundaries.
Collaborative, ethical leader who can orchestrate many situations simultaneously at multiple levels

• Managing complex organizations composed of internal teams and domestic/ offshore vendors/partners.
• Managing functional organization
• Managing budgets up to $24MM
• Workplace Technology Delivery
• Incident/crisis Management
• Outsourcing business processing.
• Program Management for component-based development.

My local coordination was with plant's General Manager in Managua, Nicaragua, while global decisions and reporting was to Latin American headquarters in Brazil.
