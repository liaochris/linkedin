Peter Guzzardo’s Summary

Anyone over thirty-five who says they do one thing is either living a very unfulfilling life or is a liar.

I’ve been a successful ceo, president, salesman, marketeer, engineer, biologist, solar energy consultant, movie producer, writer, father, decent cook, and an incredibly terrible musician. For every success I've had ten failures, and I learned a lot from those too.

Right now, with the help of a lot of great people, and on a shoe string budget, I’m running a company with five incredible divisions; all taking a shot at doing new and exciting things in the sustainable energy, entertainment, telecommunications and venture capital industries.

Real Life Television is developing reality television projects designed to entertain, raise social awareness, and make money. Real Life Moving Pictures is developing and producing feature films for commercial theatrical release, Real Life Energy is working with Polygen Energy to build power plants which convert animal waste into bio-fuels, Real Life Telecom is marketing the services of tier one carriers, enabling companies to function more effectively, be more green, reduce their carbon footprint and increase their profits, and Real Life Capital is working hard to build an investment group so we can explore other opportunities where we can do valuable things and make great money too.

Add to this two thirteen year old boys, life in Greenwich Village, and an extremely spirited British X- wife , and you might say I lead a very busy life. Well I do. And I love it this way.

Right now,The financing for the Polygen Energy project which will build 1800 Bio-Digester refineries across the US beginning with two in New Mexico is nearing completion. For more info contact us at 646-470-0341 or ideas@reallife.com Still some room to get involved.
