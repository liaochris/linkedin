David Monk’s Summary

Overview:
Taking advantage of my prior experience and strong analytical skills, I identify GE’s highest-impact chemical, hazardous and non-hazardous waste pollution prevention (P2) opportunities, define their scope, and develop elegant and effective solutions that result in leaner processes, reduced waste, increased safety, sustainable manufacturing, and financial savings. I address complex technical/logistical challenges in my P2 projects, because manufacturing processes are intricate and interdependent. Once I’ve demonstrated technical feasibility, I work with GE’s engineering and manufacturing leaders to perform pilot tests, implement final changes, and ensure the new processes meet expectations. My projects frequently require presentations to all management levels (Business Level SVP & CEO).

Goal:
My personal goal is to make a major environmental impact while saving money in the process. By proving that waste reduction translates to financial savings, I believe that others will be encouraged to follow suite.

David Monk’s Specialties:

Waste Reduction, Sustainable Manufacturing, Process Improvement, Product Development, Communication, Troubleshooting
