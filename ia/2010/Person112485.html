Katie Moreno’s Summary

Traditional Marketing vs. Social Media: It's the difference between buying your way into a new culture as a tourist and earning your way in as a participating citizen. Like traveling in a foreign country, you get high marks for trying to speak the language, no matter how badly.

At it's core, social media is still people, talking to people. It has transformed the traditional monologue (one to many) into a dialogue (many to many). Here are three simple rules I like to follow when it comes to social media:
1. Don't do anything with social media unless you know what, and why you're doing it.
2. If you're going to say something, have something real to say.
3. Using social media is not magic, it's still real work.
Source: John C. Welch, Bynkii.com
