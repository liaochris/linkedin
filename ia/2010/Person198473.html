Svitlana Kurochkina’s Summary

Svitlana is currently an Assistant to the President of a Real Estate brokerage firm with 50 to 100 brokers. Gerard Longo, President of Madison Estates, is also a Developer focusing on rebuilding, restoring and re-creating historical landmarks in New York City. As Gerard's Assistant, Svitlana is the Director of Marketing, Advertising and HR. She is currently working on the development and improvement of online-marketing for the company and its official website. She oversees the day-to-day operations of the staff and advises the sales directors, agents about on-line and inprint marketing and advertising.

Amongst other projects, her usual responsibilities include Real Estate Weekly Market Analysis, focusing on residential sales and rentals in Brooklyn and Tribeca. A major focus of Svitlana's research falls into social networking and its impact on the future of Real Estate business. Recruitment is another aspect Svitlana takes very seriously as Madison Estates has team of unique, creative and innovative individuals carefully selected to ensure flawless and rewarding 'real estate experience.'

Previously, she has worked, interned and volunteered in various European countries. She interned as an Event Manager at the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development's headquarters in London. She also worked in Paris as Technical English Teacher for an Engineering School. Some summers, she volunteered with an NGO focusing on cultural/ethnic issues in Odessa, Ukraine.
