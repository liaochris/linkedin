Gillean Smith’s Summary

My father, Merriman Smith, known to millions as ‘Smitty,’ spent many years as Dean of the White House Press Corps. under five presidential administrations.He was known to end every presidential news conference with, 'Thank You, Mr. President.'

In many ways I am thankful for the 20 years experience that has included broadcasting (TV & radio), Public Relations and now training and consulting. However, I have never lost my passion for objective news coverage as writer, reporter, field producer or segment producer.

With my experience comes solid results such as a 40 percent increase in sales for Smithfield Foods, a 25 percent increase in sales and brand awareness for a global pharmaceutical company (Banner) and numerous media and analyst connections that have resulted in increased business from current and new customers.

Results like this only come when you are working on something you love. And I love it all

From TV news, broadcast reporting & production, public relations, media training, and employee training and crisis consulting, there is much to keep a person energized.
