Ashley Schweitzer’s Summary

At Nonprofits Assistance Fund, our mission is to build financially healthy nonprofits that foster community vitality. Right now, there are many factors undermining our sector’s financial health and viability. Let’s take advantage of what we do have — tools that facilitate sharing information, as well as savvy and strategic thinkers working to sustain our sector.

As Marketing Coordinator, my job is to:
• Develop and implement our marketing strategy
• Craft all our our print and online communications
• Maintain our website, blogs, and social networks
• Engage with our clients and partners
• Manage media relations
• Support our training and outreach activities, including coordinating the Social Enterprise Network

I also organize the Twin Cities Net Tuesday Meetup, a group that encourages social media adoption among nonprofits. It's a space to share ideas and resources, bounce ideas off of peers, and discuss how social media can be a tool for social change.
