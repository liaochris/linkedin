Ed Wu’s Summary

Ed is currently the Head of Testing & Optimization at Dell's Global Consumer and SMB Online Business Unit. He is responsible for driving strategic analytics and testing initiatives, evangelizing, championing web analytics and sharing best practice across Dell’s global Consumer and SMB online business. His prior experience includes marketing strategy, business intelligence, competitive pricing, strategic consulting and sales management.

Ed is passionate about and commited to the fast growing field of online analytics. In his blog www.managinganalytics.net, he likes to share his experience and insights on how to turn data into action and action into value. He is currently serving as the Board of Director for the Web Analytics Association. He is also WAA 2009 Championship winner and speaker and panelist of eMetrics Marketing Optimization Summit.

Ed graduated from Michigan Business School in 2004 with focus on marketing and general management.
