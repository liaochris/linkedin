Teri Gorrell’s Summary

After being honorably discharged from the Marine Corps in 2005, I have been working in the motorcycle industry. Sales, marketing and training have been the main focus of my recent positions. Both ICON and ScorpionEXO were relatively small, start-up companies when I started; so every staff member has been fortunate to experience every aspect of the business. Scheduling, budgeting, copy writing and counseling are also daily tasks that I perform from a remote office.
It has been and honor to work with industry professionals like Mat Mladin (AMA Superbike Racer), Eric Anderson (former VP of Sales and Marketing-Scorpion Sports) and many more!
