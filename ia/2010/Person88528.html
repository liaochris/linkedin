James Simon, PMP’s Summary

I am an accomplished project manager with 10 years of information technology experience in the software development and financial services industries working in both large and small company environments. I have earned my PMI - Project Management Professional (PMP) and Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP) certificates.

My project management experience extends in both traditional and agile (SCRUM) methodologies to deliver high quality software and web solutions on time, on budget and meeting client and user expectations. I have significant experience using project management tools such as Microsoft Project (5 yrs) and SharePoint (2 yrs) to create, maintain and update project materials. I possess a passion for learning and achieving and an innate internal motivation to succeed in every endeavor. My responsibilities as a project manager include: team leadership, business system analysis, quality assurance, client satisfaction, project scoping/budgeting, resource allocation and the management of many projects simultaneously. I have experience in requirements gathering, writing procedural documentation and leading quality assurance efforts. Prior to my PM work, I was a software developer in object-oriented design using SQL, HTML, and ASP.Net and a quality assurance tester.
