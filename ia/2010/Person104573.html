Jonathan Stein’s Summary

I'm an engineer. My "polestars" are efficiency, transparency, reliability, process, and fairness. What excites me most about my work is making everyday activities and products more efficient, accessible, and easy to use.

In my years of experience with banks and brokers, I learned their inner workings, how to build great new products, and how to get the word out about those products. I found the financial and banking world to be overly opaque, slow to change, and lacking sufficient fairness, transparency, and usability in product design.

With Betterment.com, I hope to make smarter saving and investing more accessible to everyone. Because I love making complex problems easier, making life a little better, making the world ever-so-slightly more fair and efficient.

Jonathan Stein’s Specialties:

Managing teams, simplifying complicated problems, making processes efficient, retail banking and brokerage, financial markets, technology, and marketing.
