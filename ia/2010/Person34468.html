Cynthia Burkhardt’s Summary

Experimenting with combinations of various imaging and processing techniques allows me to fuse the realism of the moment with my own personal vision. Communicating photographically is a rewarding process.

I thank God, the ultimate Creator and Artist, for the opportunity to do this. "The earth is the Lord's, and the fulness thereof..." Psalm 24:1

It is my desire to draw the viewer into the picture in a way that makes them see the scene with perspective and emotion that they would not see otherwise. I love the way color can affect your emotions and your senses, and I love the drama of black and white.

It's about the joy of creating.

My work has been featured on the cover of the American Frame Corporation Catalog, the front page of Imagekind.com and I've had work featured on various travel websites.

Exhibition and show schedules include fine art exhibitions, juried competitions, and fine art festivals.
