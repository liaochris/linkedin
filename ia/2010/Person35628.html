Chandlee Bryan’s Summary

You may have the skills and experience required for your next job, but can you pass the scan test?

You’ve heard the stats: When you apply for a job, employers spend an average of 15 seconds reviewing your credentials. But that is after you pass the first test: Apply for a job online and your resume will likely be scanned for keywords and given a relevance rank before it’s ever seen by human eyes. Do you know how your resume will stand up?

Clear the resume hurdle, and expect to be Googled next. According to ExecuNet, 90% of recruiters check out candidates online; and 45% have ceased to consider a candidate based on what they’ve found.

I can help you optimize your resume and online presence. Rise to the top of the applicant pool and increase your visibility—as a candidate, and as an expert in your field. I do this by partnering with you to understand your preferred work style and environment, and by working side-by-side with you to develop career collateral that meets employer needs and presents your strengths. Should you have an interest, I also offer coaching and job search strategy services that go beyond resumes and LinkedIn profiles.

I bring to my work over 12 years of experience, contacts across industry sectors, and an insatiable curiosity. I have a Master’s degree in counseling, and have worked in the careers industry from the Ivy League to the recruiter’s desk. I’m a writer at heart, and rarely tire of writing cover letters, refining resumes, and creating the perfect LinkedIn summary statement.

Outside of my client work, I study and blog about job search trends and strategy, run one of the U.S.’s largest MeetUp groups for job seekers, and work part-time as a Career Advisor for Catapult Advising, a provider of career management services to college students founded by Treacy & Company, a management consulting and research firm serving Fortune 100 companies.

I’m currently taking on a limited number of new clients. How can I help you?
