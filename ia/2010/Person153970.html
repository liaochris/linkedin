Nick Genevish’s Summary

Nick Genevish is currently the Online Marketing Manager for Smart Destinations, Inc., a leading attraction tourism company featuring the Go Card and Explorer Pass products. Nick acts as the lead contact both for the company's online affiliate program as well as other online advertising opportunities and partnerships.

Prior to Smart Destinations, Nick served as an Affiliate Marketing Program Manager at Carat, Inc., a multi-service, interactive advertising agency. His client list included adidas, TiVo, Hyatt, TracFone and Kodak.

While at Carat, Nick delivered proven, results-driven contributions to his affiliate programs by devising strategic recommendations for promotion of client products and services. Nick also offered promotional copywriting and HTML development services to his programs. On the TracFone account, Nick also contributed in account management role.

Prior to joining Carat, Nick worked for two years in public relations with Schwartz Communications specializing in the healthcare and medical device industry. His client list included Philips Medical, Cyberonics, SynCardia and SleepApneaInfo.com. Between 2003 and 2005, Nick and his respective account teams won six New England-based public relations awards, including one for his placement in the Massachusetts-based MetroWest Daily News.
