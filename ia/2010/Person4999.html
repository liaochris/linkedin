Alan Altman’s Summary

Branding truly entails so much more than just a logo design. It expands into every piece of communication that your company puts into the public. From internal corporate collateral to a national advertising campaign, your brand and message need to be creative and clear.

While we work with predominantly print media, our capabilities are endless. Our creative partnerships within the industry allows you to work with experts that share our standards of high-quality and supreme expectations. And with A3 as your “Brand Patrol,” your company can be assured that it will establish a cohesive, recognizable brand across all media outlets.

Being a smaller studio allows us to focus on building trust and confidence in our client relationships. The communications between us and our clients throughout the life of a project help to realistically manage expectations and ensure that ideas are delivered on time and within budget. Ideas will not get lost in translation and will always get attention from top talent.
