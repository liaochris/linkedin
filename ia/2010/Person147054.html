Mitchell Ashley’s Summary

My passions are creating innovative products and businesses, while teaming up with talented people who have a passion for making a difference. My technology and market focus includes social media, collaboration, mobility, networking, security, virtualization, cloud services, SaaS, Microsoft and open source.

Many of who I've worked along side say I have a knack for effectively understanding customer and market needs, translating those into successfully delivered systems and products, and effectively simplifying complex topics and communicating them in a way a broad audience can understand.

I love to evangelize the work I am a part of with customers, analysts, and media. I am fortunate to have appeared in industry publications such as USAToday, NetworkWorld, ComputerWorld, InfoWorld, InformationWeek, CRN and others.

I regularly blog about my activities and topics of interest at The Converging Network, http://theconvergingnetwork.com, the Network World Microsoft Subnet blog, http://www.networkworld.com/community/ashley, and the podcast I co-host, "StillSecure After All These Years", http://ashimmy.podomatic.com.
