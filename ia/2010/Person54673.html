Dennis Henry’s Experience

  • Accountant/Consultant | Interim CFO | QuickBooks Trainer & Coach

    AccuTrainers, Inc. - QuickBooks Training Seminars Seattle

    (Privately Held; Accounting industry)

    July 2009 — Present (10 months)

    This employer profile addresses QuickBooks Training and consulting. I also actively perform Accounting and Consulting services of which can be viewed below under Dennis Henry, PLLC.

    Years ago, after attending a two day QuickBooks Training Seminar in Seattle with Intuit's recommended training company, I assessed that there was a clear gap between the training content and process...and the complete needs of most of the students. The unfullfilled student needs included having their current and industry specific issues addressed during the training and in follow-up conversations/consultations days after the course. It was clear to me that the opportunity to apply the newly gained knowledge was lacking, since there was no opportunity to follow-up with the instructor as an included feature of the course.

    This lack of satisifaction witnessed by other students motivated me to develop improved QuickBooks Training Seminars that provide the Standard QuickBooks Training material most commonly found in books, online and in live classes, with the addition of adding processes that address the individual needs of each attendee and that provide a forum for individual follow-up support, coaching and training.

    Hence, AccuTrainers was born. AccuTrainers provides enhanced QuickBooks Training Seminars that address all of the QuickBooks software functional areas with the addition of new content that includes individual followup coaching sessions.

    We've broken concepts down into four major categories as follows:

    EDUCATION | DEVELOPMENT | ASSESSMENT | PLANNING

    AccuTrainers' courses offer EDUCATION. Dennis Henry PLCC assists with Accounting process DEVELOPMENT, reporting analysis and ASSESSMENT, and Business PLANNING. The goal of providing services under these four categories is to prepare and assist the Accountant and/or Business Owner with the education and tools needed to successfully manage their business.
