Bill Cooke’s Summary

Senior media, marketing and communications executive. Equally facile with traditional and digital media. Experience in both publicly and privately held firms. General management responsibility in varied environments, including stable, start-ups and turnarounds. Effective at taking organizations to the next level by leading with creative vision, sound business planning, high energy and disciplined execution. Skilled communicator, motivator, team builder.
