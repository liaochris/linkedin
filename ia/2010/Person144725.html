Todd Miechiels’s Summary

Since 1997, Todd Miechiels has been helping B2B companies and agencies alike get more out of their search engine and internet marketing.

In 2000, Todd co-founded Medium Blue Search Marketing with Scott Buresh. After five years of successfully growing Medium Blue into a nationally recognized search engine optimization agency, Todd sold his stake in the company to open up a solo internet marketing consulting practice.

Today Todd enjoys working with companies who need customized and special attention in one or more areas of internet marketing. Many times Todd will serve as the liaison or bridge between busy companies and agencies, helping to coordinate successful relationships and outcomes.

Todd is happily married to his wife Kim and is the proud father of three young sons. He is an active member of First United Methodist Church of Tucker and is a guitarist and one of the worship leaders for The Forgiven, the contemporary praise band.
