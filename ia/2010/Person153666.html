Nicholas Davies’s Summary

Seasoned technology leader with many years combined experience in professional services and software delivery in Tier 1 financial service organisations driving value creation in business lines and products based on collateral management, credit risk management, clearance and settlement, global trust, credit and equity derivatives, and international regulatory compliance and AML fraud solutions.

An expert in innovating, architecting, designing and managing integration and providing robust systems solutions. Has strong business-facing skills and extensive experience with Global 500 clients seeking enterprise-wide technology solutions.

Excellent communications skills, expressing ideas clearly and concisely, bridging the gap among decision makers, business analysts and technologists to come up with the right solution for the situation and business need.

Has consistently shown that complex ideas can be brought to reality through a clear understanding of business needs and value.

Change leader around CMM and SixSigma initiatives, driving cost reduction through strategic development of alternative delivery models using partners in offshoring.
