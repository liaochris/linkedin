Elijah May’s Summary

I've been in PR & marketing for over a decade and, as I see it, my job is pretty simple:

1. Reach people
2. Engage them
3. Convince them to do business with you.

I refer to myself as a troublemaker because communications is a rapidly evolving field and I'm always pushing people to look at things a little bit differently and try things they haven't tried before. I do this because the status quo just isn't good enough any more.

On one hand, our world is saturated with advertising and it is harder than ever to get people's attention. On the other hand, it is easier than ever to reach out as a company, organization, or individual professional and engage would-be consumers.

At the end of the day, my partners and I agree that our simple goal is helping you to achieve your goals. It is an idea we're so committed to that we guarantee results.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to email me emay@zuphix.com.
