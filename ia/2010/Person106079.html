Joshua Fletcher’s Summary

I have worked in the Business Intelligence industry for almost ten years. My main focus has been on the SAP BusinessObjects toolset.

I have provided consultancy services and provide SAP BusinessObjects training for clients with Crystal Reports, Desktop Intelligence, Web Intelligence, Xcelsius, Explorer, Universe Design, QaaWS, BI Widgets, Live Office, Crystal Reports Server, SAP BusinessObjects Edge, SAP BusinessObjects Enterprise, Data Integrator, Data Quality, Data Services, BO 6.5 to XI 3.1 Migrations, all up to and including XI 3.2.

I am an SAP BusinessObjects Certified Professional in BusinessObjects Enterprise XI 3.x, Data Integrator XI 3.x, Web Intelligence XI 3.x and Crystal Reports 2008. I am also an accredited SAP BusinessObjects Migration Specialist and SAP BusinessObjects Certified Trainer.

I have extensive experience in report design, query, analysis, dashboards, platform security, data governance and business intelligence strategy. I've also developed strong skills in data warehouse architecture, ETL design and implementation using dimensional modelling. I have also had experience in providing and driving workshops to engage with business users to understand their business intelligence requirements.

I have worked in both small and large teams, as the sole consultant providing an end-to-end solution with SAP BusinessObjects Edge Professional, or in a technical project management role within a long-term project. I also have experience providing training for the complete suite of available SAP BusinessObjects training courses for a SAP BusinessObjects partner.

I have participated in the SAP Data Quality Enablement Program, and also participate regularly in SAUG and SAP community events. I have presented at Perth, Darwin and Sydney SAUG events.
