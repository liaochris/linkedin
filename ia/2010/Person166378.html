Jim Koewler’s Summary

To help as many seniors as I can keep money in their family rather than pay it to nursing homes. The nursing homes can get paid through the Medicaid program.

Today's seniors got the country through the Great Depression and fought World War II. They deserve the ability to leave part of their life savings to their family.

Jim Koewler’s Specialties:

I help senior citizens who need nursing home care keep their money in the family. I qualify the seniors for the Medicaid program, I take them through the Medicaid application process (so they don't have to do it,) and I help them set aside money for the family that otherwise would have been spent for their long term care, using Medicaid to pay the nursing home.
