Patrick Oborn’s Summary

Vice President of Marketing and Co-Founder of Telarus, Inc., the nations fastest growing commercial telecom "master agent". My job is to let the world know that there is an easier, better, way to sell voice and data carrier services. By automating much of the pre-sales process (price quoting, proposal generation, etc.), Telarus has been able to successfully capture the attention and imagination of independent sales agents across the country. We currently have over 200 full-time agents who use our patented tools and who write business through our carrier contracts, which include: AboveNet, AT&T, ACC Business, AireSpring, NuVox, TelePacific, One Communications, XO, Covad, Cavalier, Level 3, MegaPath, New Edge Networks, Telnes, Qwest, PAETEC, Smoothstone, tw telecom, and USA Digital.

Since our humble beginning back in 2002, we've bootstrapped our way to over $8M in annual commission revenue and have been growing at over 20+% per year since our creation. We've put together a talented team of sales managers, programmers, and executives that are all focused on one thing: making commercial telecom sales efficient and profitable to all agents, large and small.

How I got started in this business: soon after completing my masters thesis in high-speed analog circuit design I realized that the web could be engineered to deliver traffic to any web site using mathematical and statistical principles I learned in the EE program at BYU. I have since applied these principles to web design and SEO to help take Telarus from concept in 2002 to the number one online business telecom sales agency in 2005. As web search evolves I remain focused on harnessing the power of real-world affiliates to deliver unprecedented marketing value. Through the creation of Google-friendly web modules I am able to assist many work-at-home independent agents in their marketing efforts, which in aggregate, amount to a huge force in the telecom advertising space.

Patrick Oborn’s Specialties:

GeoQuote(TM) real-time T1 pricing software (US Patent No 7,496,184), VARSearch(TM) real-time VAR search engine, custom telecom agent back office software which includes CRM, email delay-send, accounting, SEO training, education, and traffic analysis modules.
