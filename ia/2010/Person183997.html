Scott Ariotti’s Summary

Currently working for DiSTI, a global leader in Human Machine Interface (HMI) development tools. The Flagship product, GL Studio, leads the industry in the development of Real-Time, 2-D and 3-D Human Machine Interfaces for:
• Safety Critical Applications
• Embedded Systems
• Full Mission Simulators
• Maintenance & Part Task Trainers
• Computer Based Training

In the space of a few years, GL Studio has come to dominate the HMI tools market for aerospace and defense applications. We are unquestionably the product of choice for most companies designing complex HMI solutions given the numerous benefits that GL Studio offers.
