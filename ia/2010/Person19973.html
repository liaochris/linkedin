Barbara Zaccone’s Summary

I consider myself a web strategy and online marketing expert. I love speaking to groups about online marketing, web strategies, social media and most recently mobile marketing.

My company, BZA helps organizations to maximize and sustain their return on investment in their Internet presence. We are firm that innovates, creates and executes. We simplify the decision making process for our client by giving them an honest assessment of their online marketing options.

Our most notable clients include Vira Manufacturing, The Big Green Egg, NJ Council for the Humanities, Paterson Public Schools, American Conference on Diversity, Yale University, Accurate Box Company as well as many small and medium-sized businesses and nonprofits.
