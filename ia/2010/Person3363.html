Adrian Mendoza’s Summary

Adrian's career is highlighted by over 10 years of design and user experience in the handheld, pharmaceutical, financial, and educational sectors. His first studio, Synthesis3, worked with several Palm OS software companies in creating their brand for both a web and retail presence. In the financial and education sector, customers included Sovereign Bank, Houghton Mifflin, MIT and Harvard. Adrian has consulted in UX and Information Architect lead roles for Fidelity's E-business design group, Thomson Financial, and T.Rowe Price. His current role is as a principal/owner at Mendoza Design, a Brookline MA. based design studio with customers including Boston University and Razorfish. Adrian earned his Bachelors from the University of Southern California and his Masters from the Harvard Graduate School of Design.

Adrian Mendoza’s Specialties:

User Experience, Performance, and Optimization. Mobile Web Performance, Mobile User Experience and Design, Mobile Web Analytics, Mobile Device Platforms, Mobile Site Optimization.
