Rachel Baker’s Summary

I team my 12 years of IT Administration experience with 15 years of freelancing web development for a very broad range of knowledge. Currently I focus on help desk IT services for both Windows and Apple OSX, data backup solutions, Google Apps/Hosted Exchange setups & migrations, and Wordpress migrations & security.


I was recently employed as an IT Consultant for Small Businesses with PC Discovery, a boutique IT consulting company in Chicago. As of May, 2010 I am back to being an entrepreneur and I would like to introduce you to Plugged In Consulting. http://www.pluggedinconsulting.com


I have given presentations on keeping your Wordpress site secure as well as Google Apps changing business communications. I also am a Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist and Microsoft Certified Professional. I am very active in the technology community in Chicago and am also the Co-Founder of the Chicago Nerd Social Club, where we hosts events for nerds.
