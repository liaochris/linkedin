Jawahar Muniyandi’s Summary

With a BS in Civil Engg and MS in Structural Engg, Jawa has been engaged in Design and Build Activities since 1986.

At Larsen & Toubro, one of India's largest construction companies (www.lntecc.com), Jawa held the positions of Site Supervisor, Planning Engineer, Construction Manager and Structural Design Engineer. Jawa founded Shriram Construction Products in 1992 which was engaged in the manufacturing of Cost-effective building materials for use in various Cost-Effective Building Technologies. Jawa was instrumental in helping LAFTI, an NGO, in adopting cost-effective building technologies in their quest to build safe and sound low-cost homes for the economically weaker section in India. LAFTI's founder Krishnammal Jaganathan, Jawa's aunt, was awarded 2008 Alternative Nobel Prize (www.rightlivelihood.org) by Swedish Parliament and 2008 Opus Prize.

In the Triangle Area of NC, as the CEO of Isha Developments, Jawa has been engaged with Developing Residential and Commercial Properties. All projects are either partially or fully owned and operated by Isha Developments.

Recent projects include:

(a) Residential Subdivision called PARK TOWNWES at RTP -Research Triangle Park (www.ParkTownes.com)

(b) Commercial Retail in Raleigh called FARMINGTON SQUARE SHOPPING CENTER (www.FarmingtonSquare.US)

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yHwyOblT4to

(c) Convention and Event Center in Greenboro called MERIDIAN (www.MeridianConvention.Com).

Jawa has been instrumental in the creation of about 5 millions in equity for Investors over the past 4 years. Currents development efforts should translate to the creation of an equity of $8 millions by EOY 2010 for Isha Homes and its Equity Investors/Partners.
