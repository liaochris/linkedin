Tim Reid’s Summary

My current professional goals include:

1. To build a strong Marketing Consultancy (www.TheIdeasGuy.com.au) that specialises in providing simple, effective and easy-to-implement marketing ideas to the owners of small to medium-sized businesses.

2. To develop an on-going series of workshops that allow me to share my experience with as many owners of SMEs and Not-For-Profits as possible.

3. To create an online webinar series (www.SmallBusinessBigMarketing.com) that provides quality marketing information to SMEs globally.

4. To look back on 2009 having created the above as well as written a book and produced two seasons of a the podcast Small Business Big Marketing.

  • Project Manager - Reach Trek

    Reach Youth

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    November 1998 — March 2000 (1 year 5 months)

    I had the amazing opportunity to bring to life a project known as the Reach Trek. In recognition of the fact that there existed a great divide between corporate Australia and our country's youth, the Reach Trek was designed to address this problem. It involved finding 60 seniors managers from some of Australia's largest corporation to pay $10,000 each as well as get 40 young people sponsored for $5,000 each. We then all spent three full days trekking in and around Johanna Beach. It ended in a full day's summit at the Cumberland in Lorne, Victoria where with the help of leading politicians and community leaders we generated a hole host of ideas on how this great divide could be removed. As well as raising a significant amount of money for Reach to continue its great work, the ideas generated continue helping our young people to this day. Feel free to ask me questions about this as it was an amzing experience for all concerned.
