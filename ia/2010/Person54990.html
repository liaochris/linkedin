Derek Potts’s Summary

Mr. Potts has spent his entire career as a trial lawyer. He started his career as a corporate defense lawyer where he defended insurance companies, corporations, and doctors. However, after several years as a defense lawyer, he changed the course of his career by becoming a trial attorney dedicated to exclusively representing individuals injured or victimized by others.

Mr. Potts is invited to speak regularly to both state and national groups concerning litigation, trial strategy, and various legal topics including the annual American Association for Justice Convention and yearly training seminars for lawyers related to trial advocacy, medical malpractice, and litigation. He has also testified before committees of both the Missouri State Senate and Missouri House of Representatives concerning nursing home neglect and abuse.
