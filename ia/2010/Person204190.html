Tim Hogan, CIC, CRM’s Summary

TIMOTHY J. HOGAN CIC, CRM, is a High Net Worth Risk Advocate who is an experienced property and casualty insurance broker with vast expertise in all lines of private risk coverage.
Tim grew up in Hoffman Estates, Illinois and attended Hoffman High School. At Northern Illinois University, he pursued a history degree with an emphasis in computer science. He followed the path of his father and step-mother into insurance. He cut his teeth on the insurance company side through working at Standard Insurance, Safeco, State Auto, and Fireman’s Fund. As a 24 year veteran of insurance, Tim concluding that no one company can meet every need. “As an independent broker,” Tim explains, “I can shop the market for my clients rather than try to pigeonhole their situation into only one viable option.” To that end and the welfare of those he serves, he has forged cohesive relationships with the top property and casualty insurance carriers in the industry.
He is a well-schooled and highly experienced property and casualty insurance broker with fluency in all lines of personal coverage. Tim has an impressive history of assessing risk, managing large accounts, and resolving underwriting issues. He structures asset preservation programs for Affluent / High Net Worth clients. He is an exceptional team builder and problem solver for both personal and business needs.
Tim helps support the National Fallen Firefighters Foundation through fundraising events and is an active sponsor of their Chicagoland Golf event each July.
Tim is a graduate of Northern Illinois University with a B.S. in History. He holds the designation of Certified Risk Management CRM and Certified Insurance Counselor CIC. He served in the US Naval Reserves and has held an insurance producer's license in the State of Illinois since 2004.
