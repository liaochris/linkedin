Patrice Panis’s Summary

1983 - Graduated of the internationally-known "Hotel Business School of Lausanne", Switzerland, which I can be proud of... but, because of a rather poor health/nutrition as a child (was born in the ex-Belgian Congo), developed a life-time passion for sharing and teaching all the ways to "a healthy mind in a healthy body" (or "menta sana in corpore sano") but with the approach of pleasure rather of abstention.

1993 - After teaching 3 years Hotel Techniques, Nutrition and the French language in a tourism school in Barcelona, Spain (Like my mom, who was a home economic teacher many years in Switzerland, I always loved teaching !), I made my dream come true to move to the U.S., Miami at first (as I could then speak Spanish)

2003 - After working already 10 years as an international model, I started to give more time again to one of my main passions in my life: gourmet-healthy cooking and eating... (www.eatwellforever.com) offering guilt-free advice to my friends and colleagues...

2008 - Going on with projects to "change the world" into a better place, working back at my cook-book manuscript, I want to be at the head of a world-wide network of people who, like me, are looking to feel the youngest and healthiest possible, still enjoying good food and a busy life; after the fight against hunger in the world, there will be the fight for access to a healthy lifestyle for everyone on the planet ! Since I was 4 years old, I have always felt to be a visionary and an idealist :-)
