U.B. Ciminieri’s Summary

Business Controls, Inc. is the country’s leading incident reporting and risk mitigation firm. Our services include incident reporting, corporate investigations, litigation support, and training. Clients include Robert Bosch Corp., Westaff USA Inc. and the University of Northern Colorado. Business Controls, Inc. serves over one million client employees in over 22,000 client locations in 128 countries via our award-winning and patent-pending anonymous reporting systems.

By combining our state of the art technologies and professional services we put our clients in a better position to manage employee related issues, create safer work environments, limit their exposure to litigation, and stay compliant with numerous regulations.
