Lois Freeke’s Summary

Lois Freeke is widely recognized as China's top specialist recruitment, outplacement consultant and careers advisor for the fire, security, safety, heating, ventilation, air-conditioning and refrigeration (HVAC/R) and building automation systems (BAS) and automation industries. She is also a professional, and experienced, career/marketing coach, working with clients and candidates from all industries and countries.

Founding Partner in Niche, China's only specialist recruitment firm for the above industries, and provider of the Asia Pac's largest database of candidates for fire/security, security consulting and investigations, HVAC/R and BAS industries.

Unmatched, extensive specialist recruitment, consulting and careers advisory experience in China and the Asia Pacific region for the above industries. More than five years' industrial recruitment experience, specializing in the fire, security, security/risk management, investigations and security consulting, safety, BAS, HVACR and automation industries in China and the Asia Pacific. An additional 14 years' experience in sales, business development, publicity/press, marcoms, marketing and corporate training positions, primarily gained in China and the Asia Pacific, in leading service and B2B organizations. Native English speaker with fluent conversational Mandarin Chinese.

Professional career, life purpose, personal brand and marketing coaching for clients from all industries and countries. Authority on China job searching techniques and strategies.
