Tony Kwon’s Summary

I have a broad experience working for Aerospace, Federal / State / City Government, Telecommunication, Union, Manufacturing, Retailer (Traditional/Internet), Restaurant, Insurance, Entertainment, Airline, Health Care, Financial Industry customers in providing solutions to their Telecommunication/Network/Security requirements.
Having worked for employers in many of these sectors, I understand where they are coming from.

15 years of IT experience has prepared me to handle vest array of requirements.
