Ani *Chaudhuri’s Summary

At work:
My professional journey started 16 years ago. Since then, I have worked at Fortune 500 companies (steel, food & technology). I have also founded a couple of technology startups: eCircle was acquired by Reliance and Opelin by Hewlett-Packard.
