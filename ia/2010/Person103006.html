John Lang’s Summary

For 21 years, I worked mostly in senior executive management for an international, 501 (c) (3) non-profit organization headquartered in my home state of Michigan. I also successfully managed a mid-sized book store in Florida for a 300+ store chain and was the Executive VP of an independent movie studio in Ontario, Canada.

Many years of education, interest and experience in library and information science led in 2006 to a full-time professional position as a librarian/historian/researcher in Grapevine, Texas for an international, non-profit that lasted until budget cuts eliminated the position in the last quarter of 2009. In February 2010, I began working full-time as digital/electronic librarian for a sporting specialist company leading a large-scale digitization project to create digital content libraries.

The right management and/or consulting position as well as information professional possibilities in the DFW metroplex are of interest, however, relocation would be considered under the right circumstances.

I have lived at various times and in various locations in SE Michigan, SE Ontario, Central Florida, S. California, and N. Texas.

Member Cross Timbers Community Church - Argyle, TX
