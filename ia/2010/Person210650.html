Varun Munjal’s Summary

At Pantheon Advisory’s Delhi office Varun was a member of the firm's Technology, Communications & Media practice. Varun specializes in recruiting senior executives to leadership positions in these sectors.
Varun joined Pantheon with 4 years of experience in the technology, industrial (automotive & chemicals) and CIO industry and functional sectors respectively. Prior to joining Pantheon, Varun worked with two of the world’s leading executive search firms.

As a part of the global research support centre for one of the firms, Varun led the CIO and Technology research teams providing high end research products and services to the internal consultants at the global level. Varun has also executed CXO level assignments in the real estate, industrial and technology domains with his last
firm. Varun’s prior roles were in the area of software development, initially as a part of the project team that led and executed various government and private assignments and later as an entrepreneur.

Varun has a bachelor's degree in computer applications from IGNOU, Delhi, and an E-commerce specialization diploma from Aptech, one of India’s premier providers of computer training. His other interests include numismatics and music.
