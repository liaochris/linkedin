Carleton Bedell’s Summary

Bedell International is a information technology company focused on back-end business support and providing IT and OEM Services on a global scale. Our core objective is to streamline the business process of small to enterprise scale corporations and organizations on a global scale. We fulfill this objective daily by focusing on three core areas of operations namely: call center services, document management services, commercial software applications and OEM/ODM packages.

Our approach to IT Services and Software Development is very transparent, enabling us to see beyond the basic needs of our customers and getting 100% involved in their projects. We are not satisfied until our customers are satisfied, making sure they get 110% of the products we deliver in terms of design, architecture, and user-friendliness.

Our work ethic is a strong dedication to cutting edge information systems to enhance the productivity of business development. Our corporate structure is also dynamic, utilizing the most self-motivated individuals to support our business models and promote a viable service-based enterprise. Through a solid corporate governance and network infrastructure, our products and services are built to facilitate international customers.
