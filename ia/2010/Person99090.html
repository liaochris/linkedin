Jay Norris’s Summary

I'm the author of "Mastering the Currency Market", McGraw-Hill, 2009 and Mastering Trade Selection and Management, McGraw-Hill, 2011. I host The Daily Forex Report on ForexTV.com. I also conduct Live Market Exercise during the London/US overlap for 3 hours a day 3 days a week where I point out set-ups and trade triggers for the our clients in the major currency markets.

I'm also a principal in www.Trading-U.com, a trading education company

Advice I would give traders is always be aware of the trend on the higher time frame, and know how to measure trend shifts. Also know that the best defense you have as a trend trader is knowing how to counter-trend trade.

I can be reached at 800-496-2070 and jnorris@clovernest.com
