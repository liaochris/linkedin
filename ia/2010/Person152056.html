Nathan Fitzgerald’s Summary

I am passionate about authenticity and changing the landscape of the way marketing has been handled. We have chosen to focus on reaching the Boomer & Senior population because they are under served.

It's funny where life takes you...I have a Psychology background, but somehow I ended up in Sales. What I quickly discovered was that it was an opportunity to connect with PEOPLE in a manner that was helpful and relational.

From Stockbroker to Executive Recruiter, and now to Marketer, I have learned that what makes a career exciting to wake up to everyday, are the relationships that are built along the way.

We laugh a lot at Immersion Active, because we share in the stories of our lives. And stories is how we teach our clients to make an emotional connection with their audience.

We are the Interactive Marketing Agency for the mature markets. We help companies leverage digital media to effectively connect with Baby Boomers and Seniors online.
