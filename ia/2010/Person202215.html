Theofilos Vasileiadis’s Summary

In 1997, mr. Theo St. Vasileiadis (born in Greece in 1976), with co-pilot his friend and then fellow student in the Athens University of Economics and Business mr. Fanis Rigas, started a journey, full of intense and passionate moments, by founding the company Kariera SA.
In the years to come, having the blessing to lead a group of extraordinary talented and creative individuals, he was honored (jointly with mr. Rigas) with the Award for Extraordinary Entrepreneurial Excellence, from the President of the Greek Republic and the Greek Entrepreneurs' Club. The award praised the two for their groundbreaking business activities, which were crowned by the success of the leading on-line recruitment web-site in the Balkans (www.kariera.gr), the innovative executive training company Business Seminars, the largest meeting between young people and corporations in Greece (Career Days) and many more line of services and products.
In parallel with the above, Theo is committed to promoting innovation and entrepreneurial spirit, co-founding with other young leaders 7 more companies since 1997, in Greece, Dubai, Italia and Romania. These organizations vary from Exhibitions to Creative Productions and Students Orientation. Finally, from 2009 he serves as a member of the Board of Advisors in AIESEC and YSN.com.
From November of 2008, Kariera SA was acquired by CareerBuilder.com, the leading on-line recruitment company in the USA. Mr. Vasileiadis currently serves CB as the VP of Strategy for Europe, Middle East and Asia.
Furthermore, mr. Vasileiadis is often participating in task forces for the promotion of Entrepreneurship as an alternative to the job market, while he is repeatedly invited in several educational organizations and the Media in order to speak about Innovation, Leadership and Strategy. Enriching his knowledge on these issues, he is participating at the OPM program (Owners Presidents Management) at Harvard Business School in Boston, from 2008 to 2010.
