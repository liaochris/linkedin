Fernando Labastida’s Summary

Helping Latin American Software Companies enter the U.S. Market –

My goal is to help 100 Latin American software and technology companies enter the U.S. market by the end of 2010. I believe that there is a lot of technical talent coming out of Latin America, and they can compete on an equal footing with companies out of Asia, Europe and North America.

The best approach for Latin American companies to gain visibility and credibility is to have a good story to tell about how they resolve real business problems for their customers.

I help Latin American software companies craft their story via eBooks, blogging, their website and what they say to their customers in person and on the phone. This way they can:

- Provide a clear benefit
- A significant and relevant differentiator
- A reason to believe and
- A fruitful target market

The point is: don't sell your product or service. Sell what you do for your customers. Customers buy benefits, they don't buy products or services.
