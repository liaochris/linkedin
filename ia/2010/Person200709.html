Ted Barron, CPP’s Summary

Ted Barron is the Managing Director of Technology Services for Kingdom Worldwide Inc., a global Risk Management company. In this role he will provide services similar to his prior position as Vice President, Senior Security Manager for Wells Fargo & Company, a major financial corporation located in San Francisco. Mr. Barron is a physical security technology specialist with over 20 years of experience in evaluating security systems and product applications resulting in secure facilities. He specializes in providing recommendations for improvements, system upgrades, threat level assessments, enterprise access controls, intrusion detection, and surveillance systems. All system functions integrated over corporate networks. His responsibilities over the past 10 years total $200,000,000 in security construction contracts. Mr. Barron is experienced in risk research, vulnerability assessment, building controls, cash areas, cash rooms, operation centers, campus environments, data centers, command centers, retail delivery, X-Ray screening, pedestrian controls and biometric applications. He may be contacted at: 415-760-1604 or 415-233-6940. Or by email at ted.barron@kingdomworldwide.com
