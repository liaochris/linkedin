Kelly Schuh’s Summary

With over 10 years experience in employee relations, benefits administration, compensation and general human resources, I have a passion for all aspects of human resources management, employee development, and leadership. After earning my BA in Business Administration & Human Resources, I worked in benefits for a major paper/timber company with locations in Washington, Oregon, Idaho, California, Arkansas, and Minnesota. I also spent 5 years with a large utility, furthering my interest in human resources and employee relations. This is one of the many reasons I pursued and earned my MA Organizational Leadership from Gonzaga University.

After relocating to Rochester, NY in June 2007, my passion for staffing and recruiting in began in September 2007. Currently a Sr Recruiter with The Employment Store, I serve as a lead recruiter for one of our largest clients, a major university. I have also served heavily in the annual United Way Campaign, serving on the committee with Avista Corporation for 2 years consecutively, and serving as the campaign chair for the 2009 campaign at The Employment Store.

The Employment Store specializes in "Matching Great People with Great Companies!". I live by this motto everyday, both in working with my candidates as well as my work with our clients. The Employment Store takes pride in delivering the utmost service to our clients, while holding a great deal of care and compassion in improving our candidates quality of life and positively impacting our surrounding community.

If you have any staffing needs of information on our services, please feel free to contact me at kschuh@employmentstore.com or 232-4880, Ext. 119.
