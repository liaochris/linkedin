Steven David Bloomberg’s Summary

Steve is an expert English language teacher and English language consultant for the international community, specializing in communication coaching for professional purposes and business. Steve's progressive approach to English language training and communication coaching distinguishes him from other providers. He provides an unparalleled learning experience for the unique English language training needs and goals of international professionals and their companies: Steve's students and customers receive an incomparable value-added experience.
