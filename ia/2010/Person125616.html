Michael Walsh’s Summary

Former senior copywriter for Acer worldwide I have recently self-declared myself Creative Director for Strategic Projects as no-one else is actually doing the job and that's exactly what my role requires of me.

I am responsible for nearly all of the recent Acer and Packard Bell product names from the Aspire Gemstone onwards but my role also involves:

- Brand Guidelines
- Brand Development
- Product Guidelines
- Product Naming
- Visual Identities
- Institutional Material
- Global Event Support
- Web support (web strategies, architecture and content)
- Marketing collateral
- Video storyboarding and scripts
- Radio advertising scripts

I have written speeches for most of the senior management and have worked on press kits, websites and crisis communications documents all within extremely tight deadlines.

I have a particular interest in the complexities of social media within the realm of brand communications, so it should come as no surprise that I am also the founding partner and CEO of Social Starter Srl, a company established with the sole goal of helping medium to large-sized companies bring their unfocused social media strategies into check or get them off to a great start.

We believe that a social media strategy isn't voodoo, or rocket science, but that it can produce tangible returns for corporations if it is based on best practices, solid foundations and of course, a considerable amount of creativity.

Currently in Milan, Italy, Social Starter will soon open an office in London, UK.
