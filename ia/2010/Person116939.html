Kimi Ceridon’s Summary

My objective is to develop integrated technologies that make a positive social impact on the world in three core competencies:

• Ecologically Sustainable and Product Design - By applying the principles of Life Cycle Analysis (LCA) to the early stages of product development and engineering, Kalepa Tech designs to minimize ecological impacts by deploying more sustainable products, equipment and engineering tools.

• Clean Energy and Technology Development - Through experience in industries ranging from appropriate technology, green and sustainable design, engineering management, consumer product design, integrated electromechanical design, capital equipment design, technical research and development, Kalepa Tech offers a depth of engineering skills applicable to developing the world's next groundbreaking technologies.

• Technology development for the developing world - By applying a systematic product development process and our large range of engineering skills to the development of appropriate technology, Kalepa Tech develops products specifically for developing world applications to address the UN''s Millennium Goals.

As Mechanical Engineer with Master's Degree and 8 years of diverse experience, I worked technical roles ranging from contributor to lead engineer to project manager of multidisciplinary teams including working with organizations such as Sustainable Health Enterprises (SHE), MIT's D-Lab and the International Development Design Summit to develop appropriate technology for third world applications.
