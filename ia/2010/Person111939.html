Kathleen Smith’s Summary

Throughout my career I have been viewed as a strategic and creative leader in the areas of marketing, strategy development, business planning and management who is adept at working small and large organizations, public, private and non-profit.

My great passion is finding niche markets to research, explore and implement new strategies to create community. This is why I love my current position at ClearedJobs.Net and exploring the many ways to support interaction between cleared facilities' employers and professionals with security clearances.

As part of a veteran owned company, I have enjoyed the connection we have with the military community and relish the opportunity to create opportunities to support our men and women in uniform as they transition into the workforce.

Strategic business development, marketing, advertising, social media, event planning and implementation, non-profit marketing & fundraising
