Felix Nyilu’s Summary

Excellence, integrity and expediency towards our clients' needs are an integral part of what Smart Accountancy Corp is all about. We leverage modern day technology and security to provide services miles ahead of competition.

Our web-based accounting software is feature rich, making it suitable for a wide spectrum of business types and sizes. We also customization our solution to best fit any specific business needs.

We set ourselves apart by delivering the extensive industry specialization, resource depth and service opportunities of a national firm, but with the accessibility, service continuity and level of personal relationship expected from a local business.

It comforts our clients to know that Smart Accountancy is there to sweat the details, to keep an eye out for their financial dealings.
