Adam Caplan’s Summary

Internet/digital media executive with experience building and advising companies across the broader online media space. Operational experience in the performance advertising and social media/gaming sectors. Currently Vice President - Virtual Currency of Adknowledge Inc. (the largest global independent advertising network with offices in New York, LA, Kansas City, Fort Myers, Vancouver, London and Sydney) leading business development and strategy for the Virtual Currency Channel which was acquired through the acquisition of Super Rewards in July 2009. Shareholder and President of Super Rewards from May 2008 with primary responsibility for business development, advertising and publisher sales functions, having successfully grown the business to $100 million in annual sales prior to its acquisition by Adknowledge.

Previously Founder & CEO of startup in the mobile/social gaming space, as well as an investment banker with Morgan Stanley for eleven years, heading the emerging internet/new media corporate finance team in New York. Significant experience advising companies in the sector with public and private financing as well as strategic m&a advice. Strong relationships throughout the online media and VC community.

Adam Caplan’s Experience

  • Vice President - Virtual Currency (Super Rewards)

    Adknowledge Inc.

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    August 2009 — Present (8 months)

    Super Rewards (www.srpoints.com) is the leading virtual currency monetization solution for social network applications, online games, MMOs and Virtual Worlds. The Super Rewards platform powers the monetization needs of more than a thousand apps, websites and online games reaching over 100 million monthly unique users. The simple to integrate technology provides a full spectrum of transaction options for internet users to earn or purchase virtual goods and services either directly via PayPal, credit card, direct bank debit, stored value gift card or mobile phone, or indirectly through completing direct response/CPA advertising offers such as surveys, lead generation programs or product purchases.

    Super Rewards was acquired by Adknowledge Inc. in July 2009, solidifying Adknowledge as World's Largest Independent Social Advertising Network. Adknowledge utilizes powerful predictive technology to connect advertisers with consumers across multiple channels, including email, search, domain, and social networks. Adknowledge partners with thousands of app developers to deliver close to a billion banner impressions daily to consumers using Facebook, MySpace, and other social networking sites providing a powerful distribution channel for game application developers partnering with Super Rewards.
