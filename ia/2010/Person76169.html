Glen Sturtevant’s Summary

Glen's practice focuses on environmental litigation, products liability and toxic torts, multidistrict litigation, bankruptcy and general commercial litigation.

Relevant Experience:

Represents national and multinational Fortune 100 and Fortune 500 companies in all aspects of litigation and mediation in state and federal courts nationwide.

Extensive experience handling pre-trial dispositive motions, depositions, expert witness preparation, mediation, trial preparation and discovery for major corporate clients.

Mediated product liability lawsuits at state and federal levels.

Assisted in successful defense of Fortune Global 500 corporation during two week jury trial of multi-million dollar product liability lawsuit resulting in a defense verdict.

Prosecuted more than a dozen criminal bench trials and argued numerous motions.
