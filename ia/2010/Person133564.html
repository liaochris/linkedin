Mark Lurie’s Summary

Seasoned labor and employment attorney, representing both plaintiffs and defendants in all aspects of employment disputes, including allegations concerning whistleblowing, harassment, discrimination, slander, breach of contract, theft of trade secrets, breach of non-competiton obligations, ERISA violations, and wage and hour issues, as well as conducting internal investigations. Experience as in-house counsel, Chief Compliance Officer and member of senior management provides unique advantage in investigations and other proactive preventative measures, and in conducting litigation and other means for dispute resolution.

Mark Lurie’s Specialties:

Negotiate severance agreements, and litigate full range of employment disputes (discrimination, harassment, retalation, breach of contract, defamation etc.), with special focus and experience in whistleblowers, retaliation, pharmaceuticals and medical devices and representation of in-house counsel, compliance personnel and executives. Impartial investigations. Assist with development, implementation, monitoring and review of pharmaceutical and medical device compliance programs.
