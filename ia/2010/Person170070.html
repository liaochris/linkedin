Robert Leahy, CCO/CMO’s Summary

rdleahy@msn.com

CHIEF CORPORATE COMMUNICATIONS & MARKETING OFFICER
As a chief corporate communications and marketing officer, Bob Leahy has distinguished himself in the capital markets, technology, manufacturing and consumer sectors. He has earned wide recognition as a leader who can combine strategic thinking with hands-on capabilities, and a global outlook with bottom-line business focus.

Presently, Bob serves on a consulting basis as chief marketing communications and investor relations officer for an international portfolio of Prime Asset Finance Ltd., a Zurich, Switzerland based private equity firm.

Previously, he held the position of Senior Vice President, Devonshire Communications (FMR Corp. - Fidelity Investments) where he was the top communications officer for FMR's private equity operations overseeing a portfolio of 19 operating companies, 47 minority investments and 13 investor funds. With nearly 25,000 employees, Devonshire operated in 19 countries.

Earlier Bob served as EVP & NY General Manager of the Financial Relations Board, an international investor relations agency and part of the Interpublic Group of Companies (NYSE: IPG) where he oversaw approximately 60 clientele.

Previously he was Senior Vice President and Director of Marketing & Communications for Friedman Billings Ramsey Group, Inc. (NYSE: FBCM), then the seventh largest independent U.S. public investment banking firm.

Earlier, Bob held leadership roles with Teleglobe Communications Corporation (NYSE:BCE), PSINet Inc. (NASDAQ:PSIX), the National Association of Securities Dealers, Inc. (now FINRA). Bowater Incorporated (NYSE:BOW), International Paper Company (NYSE:IP), Andal Corporation (AMEX: ADL) and the International Telecommunications Satellite Organization (INTELSAT). He has served at New York and Washington-based public affairs agencies and began his career at Congressional Quarterly.
