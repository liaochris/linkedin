Jim Cheng’s Summary

I have the honor of being appointed as Secretary of Commerce and Trade for the Commonwealth of Virginia. Meanwhile, I run a Secretariat that is responsible for Economic Development, Job Creation, Tourism, Film, Industry Regulation and Safety, Housing and other Commerce related fields. I have begun to travel all over the commonwealth seeing the great people of VA and the beauty of the country side!!! Relocate your business to VA - the "Best State for Business".

I was President of Totus Lighting Solutions, a "green energy" LED streetlight and indoor lighting startup with international presence. Also, I was actively involved in working/mentoring with several other technology startup companies and small businesses.

I previously owned CHM, an IT services firm specializing in Government (DoD and Civilian) contracting. We grew that company from 5 people to 550 in about 9 years.
