Sean Frank’s Summary

Sean Frank is a Director within the Business Transformation Workforce & Payroll Management Practice of the Hackett Group. He is responsible for and involved in a wide range of consulting assignments covering business and IT strategies, business case developments, software selections and implementations, system/architecture performance tuning, SDLC /project management, new product/service development initiatives and HR Technology integration assessments.

Sean Frank’s Specialties:

Significant experience with large enterprise Time & Attendance and Recruiting implementations, performance testing, business development, and upgrade/consolidation projects. Other skills include project management, team-based development, disaster recovery/Sarbanes Oxley methodologies, Kronos Implementation, strategy planning and development, business and IT liaison.
