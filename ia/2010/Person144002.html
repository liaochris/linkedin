Michele Ross Luminato’s Summary

After owning a brick and mortar business that relied on traditional marketing methods, large overhead, inventory, and employees, we knew there had to be a better way to do business. My husband and I have found a new business model that allows us freedom to spend more time with our family, and travel the world. We now help others who would like to learn how to market anything to anyone, from anywhere in the world. This business model has up to 80% profits with lucrative income streams that only require a laptop and phone. It is not an MLM.   We are looking for people who are interested in making CEO level incomes by tapping into 1.5 billion people on the internet. They must be self motivated, ready to quickly learn new skills, ambitious, and willing to help others.  We also assist small business owners, and in-house marketers learn a better way to market their traditional business by teaching them new skills that will set them up as the industry expert, drive more customers to their website with an effective sales funnel which convert into buying customers.

Michele Ross Luminato’s Specialties:
