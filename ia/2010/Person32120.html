Carey White’s Summary

Experienced senior commercial real estate investor who conducts objective evaluation of problem loans, reviews options, recommends appropriate course of action for workouts or restructuring and implements approved recommendations and to capitalize on attractive new investment opportunities created by the disruptions in the real estate and capital markets today.

Successfully applied financial skills and investment experience to originate new real estate investments totaling over $2.5 billion. Investment experience is primarily through debt, in all major property types in all markets. Have also invested in commercial real estate equity and debt funds.
