Rich Fennessy’s Summary

I enjoyed 17 Years at IBM, most recent position was running IBM.com out of White Plains, N. IBM.com is a division of IBM responsible for IBM's on-line presence and revenue generation through IBM's websites and call centers across the world. Prior to that, I was responsible for worldwide marketing for IBM's PC Business, headquartered in Raleigh, NC. I Left IBM in 2004 to be the President & CEO of Insight Enterprises, Inc, a global Fortune 500 IT solutions company. During my tenure, Insight grew net sales from $2.4B to $4.8B and expanded from a presence in 3 countries to a having a presence in 23 countries through the world. I left Insight in 2009 and became the CEO of PeopleJar, Inc. PeopleJar is a new internet platform designed for connecting with new people and promoting yourself and your interests across the world to make connections that matter.
