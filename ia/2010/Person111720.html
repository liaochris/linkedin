Kate Whatley’s Summary

In the past eight years, Kate's creative and leadership abilities have garnered her numerous awards, constant recognition and a swift journey to the top of her field. In roles including founder of a major in-house creative team, Art Director for one of Tampa's most established agencies, and national President of the Year with Ad 2 (a chapter of the American Advertising Federation), she has proven her aptitude for creativity and leadership time and time again.

Kate recently struck out on her own with partner Carl Vervisch to form Social Forces, a cutting-edge creative advertising team in Tampa, Florida—tackling the field of social media and exploring the new advertising frontier.

Her keen insight into consumer behavior in the online and social realm has assisted in the marketing efforts of several local, regional and national brands. Her work has ranged across industries from celebrity brand building to education and more, and includes brands such as Aflac, TBS and Aveda Institutes.
