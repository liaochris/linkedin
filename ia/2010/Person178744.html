Rufus Deuchler’s Summary

Communication designer who has always been fascinated by technology that makes creation easier. Adobe Certified Expert - Print Specialist and Adobe Cross-Media Guru since 2002. Consulting and training on Adobe software and workflows to the publishing industry. Author of interactive courses, writer for specialized publishing magazines, webmaster of the first InDesign User Group in Italy. Teacher of graphic design to US students for over ten years. Software evangelist at Adobe Systems until November 2009.
Fluent in English, French, German, and Italian.

Rufus Deuchler’s Specialties:

Graphic design, publishing worflows, graphic workflow optimization. Adobe Creative Suite training. Software Evangelism.
