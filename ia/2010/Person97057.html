Jessica Lee’s Summary

Devott Co., Ltd. (former Chinasourcing Co., Ltd.) is the primary consulting company focused on the offshore service outsourcing industry of China. With the support of its extensive industry resources and experiences, in-depth industry understanding and internationalized expert team, Devott provides one-stop outsourcing solutions, offshore outsourcing advisory, China entry and market services.

Founded on Feb. 15th 2007, Devott is formerly known as Chinasourcing Co., Ltd. Headquartered at TEDA of Tianjin, China, Devott now owns 8 representative offices in Shanghai, Australia, US, Canada, Latin America, Italy, UK and India, and over 30 business agents as well as hundreds of business partners and customers all around the world. With two years of rapid development, Devott has constructed its leading position in China’s service outsourcing industry. It has successfully helped several oversea outsourcing companies to enter the China market, and delivered solutions on outsourcing project management, company management & development, stuff training etc. to many domestic and foreign outsourcing companies. Devott has also offered advisory services to some China service outsourcing base cities and parks such as TEDA, Xi’an, Nanchang, REDA etc. and made up development plan for the growth of the local service outsourcing industry. Meanwhile, united the strength of all Chinese service providers, Devott accepts service outsourcing projects as the most powerful general contractor in China, and its services cover ITO, BPO, and KPO etc. As long as China can do it, we can do it!
