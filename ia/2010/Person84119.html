Mark Harlien’s Summary

Mark Harlien founded Ideawire in 1998 in response to the growing need to integrate communication design and online technology with measurable results. Since then, Ideawire has provided award-winning, results-oriented print, online and visual communication for a variety of clients across multiple industries.

Ideawire is an award-winning firm optimized for organizations in need of marketing discipline and online implementation. We specialize in creative solutions for complex marketing challenges and are a single source for strategy, design and production services. Ideawire’s branding and online methodology brings alignment and clarity to regional and national organizations across a variety of industries - with an emphasis on results.

Mark Harlien’s Specialties:

Online/moble strategy, custom web application programming, marketing strategy and fulfillment, custom print and online publishing (including social media publishing) and all areas of fulfillment, including copywriting, photography and illustration.

Mark Harlien’s Experience

  • President/CEO, Creative Director

    Ideawire

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    April 1998 — Present (12 years 7 months)

    I started Ideawire as a company equally versed in web, print, technology and marketing, to fill a void in the late '90s. I learned from my experience that there were two groups of people developing websites: designers who couldn't program and programmers who couldn't design. I had learned both at the very beginning of the Internet explosion. I wanted to offer superior design and sophisticated programming, which was hard to find in the marketplace.
    Soon, companies such as Advantage Rent-A-Car (the largest independently-owned car rental company in the U.S.) and Cargill (the largest privately-owned corporation in the country) were added to Ideawire's roster of clients.
    Another key advantage in using Ideawire is the marketing background. My degree is in Advertising and applies marketing strategy to many projects handled by the company.
    Our measure for success focuses on the IABC Quill awards - a contest that focuses primarily on the success/ROI of the work we provide our clients. Since 2007, we have won over a dozen quill awards, including three prestigious Gold Quills. These are handed out only if our work meets or exceeds our clients' goals.
