Barb Leon’s Summary

LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT PROFESSIONAL: blended learning / eLearning / instructional design / performance improvement / knowledge management / informal learning such as online communities and social media (often times 'internal' company communication)

I analyze work practices to determine the need for blended learning, employee performance improvement and/or knowledge management. I then set goals and strategies, followed by implementation and measurement of results. I work to reduce WOMBATs (waste of money, brains, and time). My skills are also in training development - utilizing several software applications such as Articulate, Lectora, and Adobe and Microsoft products. I have been a high-level administrator of two LMSs.
