Francisco Acosta, Sociologist’s Summary

• Sociologist with extended experience in putting people, ideas and resources together, with over 20 years of leadership in community organizing. In my professional life, I have organized, seven non-profit organizations in the United States as well as in Latin America.

• Extended experience on advocacy at the federal and state and community level; broad experience on grassroots networking and fundraising. I have advocated on education, immigration, fair trade, Social Security and Medicare, conflict transformation and on human rights issues.

• Skilled in strategic planning, institutional strengthening and coalition-building; strong interpersonal skills.

• Self starter with extensive experience in non-profit organizations; media outreach; public speaker, mediator, bridge builder, diplomatic skills.

• Experience in training and facilitation on social issues. In fact, I help professionals to find their dream job.

• Strong leadership with ability to work effectively with people from diverse cultural, social and political background.

• Demonstrated experience in personnel management up to 80 employees. As Executive Director of a non-profit organization in El Salvador, I managed about 80 full time and part-time employess.

* US citizen; bicultural and bilingual in Spanish and English.
