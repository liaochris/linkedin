Sean Zinsmeister’s Summary

After graduating from Tufts University in 2006 with a BA in Music Theory and Composition, I pursued my career in music Production. From 2006 to 2007 I worked as a studio manager, and continued to build a network as a freelance audio engineer.

My last job (2007-2009) was as a Project Manager / Audio Engineer at AudioLink, a recording studio that specializes in sound design for interactive media. At the end of 2009, I completed my graduate degree at Northeastern in Project Management, and currently work as a professional voice talent

My career path thus far has given me a unique blend of creative, managerial, and technical skills. I am currently working on my MBA - Marketing concentration at Suffolk University, and expect to graduate in May, 2011.
