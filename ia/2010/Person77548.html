Greg Chapman’s Summary

At Sears, I guided their credit card in-statement marketing business to consistent profitability. I lead a web merchant team through a phenomenal growth phase. I managed the direct mail business and provided leadership on integrating brand affinity and loyalty strategies in a multichannel environment. I ultimately lead the catalog business for Sears, positioning the business for growth as a component of an integrated multichannel strategy.
I learned about the television home shopping business and short form infomercial businesses as Director of Merchandising at America’s Health Network and VP of Hardlines Merchandising at Value Vision (Now ShopNBC).
At Quorum International I created and lead a brand new catalog division with offices in the U.S. and Europe.
I helped launch the SkyMall catalog business as their first Vice President of Merchandising, and lead subsequent web expansion and in-flight shopping strategy efforts.
At The Sharper Image, I headed up a merchandising division and was known as an intelligent and creative merchant.
I spent 11 years with Best Products learning the nuts and buts of both retail merchandising and operations. As a merchant, I ran highly volatile seasonal businesses. As a field merchant/operator, I ran a store, a district, and a $500M region.

Greg Chapman’s Specialties:

Known for my ability to think creatively about new solutions to conventional problems. Intensely collaborative in my approach to business dealings both internally and externally. Thought of as both an idea person and a team player. I’m not afraid to ask “why”. I’m equally unafraid to ask “why not?”
I’m able to look at a business and quickly separate tactical issues from those strategic. I’m a fast learner – effectively drilling down to core business strategies and developing effective plans.
