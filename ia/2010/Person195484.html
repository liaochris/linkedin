Steven Fielding’s Summary

I am an award-winning Senior Public Relations Professional with 15 years of experience driving positive perceptions, media relations, and global brand awareness for consumer technology (e.g. gaming, mobile device, personal computing, wireless) and B2B enterprise software organizations. I have directed and managed strategy for product launches, messaging/positioning, special events and ongoing media (including social media) and analyst programs for technology clients. My background shows a successful track record for leveraging traditional and new media to achieve communications goals.

Key problems I love to solve for employers:
• Increasing public awareness to drive sales
• Analyzing media perception to help create even better messaging
• Handling ad hoc issues/crises to preserve reputation
• Providing communication counsel to senior executives
• Creating compelling communications strategies to maximize positive exposure

Key clients served include: Microsoft Windows Vista, Zune, Xbox LIVE, Microsoft Pro Photography, Seattle Symphony, VoiceBox, Jobster, Baan, TIBCO, Candela, Genesys Telecommunications, Logitech, Verifone, Wright Express, VenturCom.

Industry experience includes: Consumer technology, software, enterprise software, software security technologies, financial services, medical devices, non-profit arts organizations, telecommunications, call center technology, B2B services, SaaS.
