Ronna Detrick’s Summary

I am a renegade. I am drawn to living boldly and out-loud. I am drawn to others who (want to) do the same. Sometimes this can be seen as unconventional or risky – whether personally, professionally, relationally, or spiritually – but that’s what being a renegade is all about. When we live in renegade ways, we get to be courageous, daring, stunningly beautiful, and powerful. And who wouldn’t want that?!?
