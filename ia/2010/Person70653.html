Stephen Fox’s Summary

Mission Statement
The Fox Insurance Group is committed to providing professional insurance services and expertise to our clients new and old. We will seek out the best needs based solution while delivering outstanding personal service, promptness, and accuracy with comprehensive and competitive insurance products. We know insurance so you don’t have to!

Our History
The Fox Insurance Group is conveniently located in Warminster, PA. Our staff has over 30 years insurance industry experience. We have 2 personal lines specialists, 2 commercial lines specialists and a life insurance specialist.

Why Choose The Fox Insurance Group?
We are committed to offering individuals and business owners Professional Advice, Personal Care and Responsible Service that goes beyond expectations.

What to expect at The Fox Insurance Group:
• An agency you can trust
• An agency who spends time reviewing each of your coverage parts and makes sure you have exactly what you need, no more - no less
• An agency who will seek out the most cost effective insurance plan available
• An agency whose only priority is doing what is best for you
• An agency with a service department that is receptive, efficient, friendly and expert in advising you
• An agency who will process your claim for you and advise you through the process
• An agency where you feel at home, like a member of the family.

We tailor a comprehensive insurance program based on your specific needs. As a full-service insurance agency, it is our responsibility to be able to find you the protection you need no matter how obscure. We are to provide you with the best coverage at the best possible price. Customer service is of paramount importance to us. And why not, referrals are our most significant source of new clients.
