Jeanne Fu’s Summary

I aspire to inspire, and want to be a part of an organization where passion paired with performance, and commitment coupled with creativity drive the future of digital marketing.

My ambition, diligence, self-motivation, and enthusiasm for new challenges play a pivotal role in the way I confront challenges as Paid Search Manager at EducationDynamics.

As International Director of Marketing at Distance Education Co., I revamped our paid search strategy by segmenting campaigns and setting explicit cost per lead (CPL) targets for products based on their profitability. Additionally, I developed and directed non-traditional, viral, and grass-roots digital marketing campaigns with the objective of reaching younger, untapped markets through blogging, online photo contests, social networking sites, and YouTube. One of our most significant achievements in viral marketing was the production of the first episode of "Project Redeye," (a parody of “Project Runway”) which was featured on YouTube's homepage and has received nearly 2.5 million views to date.

During my time as Marketing Consultant at Research Solutions, I authored technically challenging reports based on substantial market research interviews I conducted in both English and German. This experience helped deepen my commercial acumen and knowledge of industry market share and penetration rates, and develop my communicative skills as a result of having interacted with clients and end users from an early stage.

My endeavors in digital marketing are motivated by my lifelong passion for learning. As a philosophy undergraduate, my passion for learning motivated me to study and attain fluency in German, enhancing my engagement with philosophy as an intellectual discipline.

Moreover, my international experience as a graduate student and marketer, fluency in Shanghainese and German, and proficiency in Spanish provide me with vast resources from which to draw.
