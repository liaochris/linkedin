Kathie Nitz’s Summary

I am a professionally trained Life & Relationship Coach who received my training through The Coaches Training Institute in San Rafael, CA. I completed both their Core and Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching (ORSC) curriculums.

The true spirit of my work is to help my clients establish an unwavering sense of self, so they can thrive in any relationship or environment. I am passionate about helping others break patterns that no longer serve them well and empowering them to live a life of choice.

I specialize in helping my clients communicate more effectively within their relationship(s). This typically enables them to live a more authentic life and helps minimize the drama and chaos that often times surrounds them.

Along with providing individual, couples and family coaching, I deliver group coaching to corporations, small businesses and non-profit organizations.

As a caregiver to a mother with dementia, I also work with caregivers of loved ones and guide them on how to give care from a stronger, healthier and more capable place.
