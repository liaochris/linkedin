W. Jarrett Campbell’s Summary

I have a wide variety of experience in both technical and business roles throughout my 13 year career in the Industrial Automation & Control business. My career started as a control engineer, where I was awarded 25+ patents as a Member of the Technical Staff for one of the leading computer microprocessor companies in the world. I gradually shifted my career in the direction of business management by first becoming an sales applications engineer in the semiconductor industry and then moving into software product management and finally marketing management roles. As I have developed my practical understanding of strategic marketing and business development, I have been given progressively larger leadership roles within the marketing organization for a $500M division of a $25B global manufacturer of automation, control, and power products & services.

My goal is to continue apply my unique combination of technical knowledge and business management experiences in marketing leadership roles within the Industrial Automation and Control business.

  • Segment Marketing Manager
