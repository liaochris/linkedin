Paul Girardeau, AIA’s Summary

I am currently Abri Design Studio Inc. In my 28 years of experience I have been responsible for Architectural services for a long list of clients, and for a wide range of building types. My experience includes custom residential, subdivision entrance/clubhouse, retail establishments, retail roll outs, office buildings, manufacturing facilities, sports facilities, churches, medical buildings, and prototypical buildings for restaurant chains, internet POP sites, institutional, and television broadcasting projects. My responsibilities include coordinating the total A/E effort for specific projects, ensuring design intent and owner criteria have been met, interfacing with various planning and zoning boards and other regulatory agencies involved in the design and construction process negotiating contracts with clients, subcontractors and special consultants.
(contact: paul.girardeau@abri-ds.com)

Paul Girardeau, AIA’s Specialties:

My primary experience includes programming, code analysis, concept design and design development of projects with an overview role during construction administration phases of the team's projects. I currently supervise members of the architectural and engineering project teams, coordinate the architectural work with all engineering disciplines and consultants, oversee administration of the design budget, and maintain overall project schedule.
