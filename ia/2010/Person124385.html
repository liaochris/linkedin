Leticia Pérez Miller’s Summary

As founder of Tilde Translations and as a native Spanish speaker raised in Guadalajara, Jalisco, México, I am passionate about maintaining the authenticity and correctness of the Spanish language and bridging bilingual communication accurately and effectively.

I have been providing English/Spanish translations services for businesses, government agencies and individuals for the past 5 years here on California's central coast. On October 1, 2007 I launched my new business, Tilde Translations, a team of passionate and professional linguists. In other words we are "word geeks," we love what we do! Bridging communication and melting bilingual language barriers.

As a Spanish instructor I served the San Luis Coastal Unified School District for 6 years and taught levels I, II, III and Spanish for Health Professionals for the Continuing Education Department at Cal Poly State University.

Leticia Pérez Miller’s Specialties:

English to Spanish translation, interpretation, proofreading and revision, Spanish tutoring, workplace Instruction, coordinating events, networking, promotion, and most importantly choosing to see the best in people and in every circumstance.
