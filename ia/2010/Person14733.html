Anthony Birks’s Summary

Fidelis is an industry specific recruitment company focusing on key sectors of the European packaging and processing industry.

I concentrate our efforts on developing close relationships with leading companies in 4 specific market sectors – packaging machinery, processing machinery, materials and containers and marking and coding.

My aim is to offer consistently high levels of service to key clients within these sectors and become a trusted and reliable recruitment partner of choice.

I have over 10 years experience working on high profile confidential campaigns for leading companies focused within the European packaging and processing industries. I have spent time understanding the dynamics and performance of this industry and have built up a strong reputation for the recruitment of management to Board level positions.

I am driven with a purpose of using my knowledge, expertise and savvy to get the right resource for organisations and build long term client relationships. It's fairly simple, only through delivering the right results for my clients do I build a sustainable and successful business.

Anthony Birks’s Specialties:

My areas of expertise include recruiting sales, service, engineering and supply chain positions from management through to board level.
