Christina Wagner’s Summary

I started my experience in radio by co-hosting two radio shows called ‘Darn Good Deals’ and ‘Wisdom from Experience’. The show ‘Wisdom from Experience’ focused on bringing people together to solve everyday life problems. ‘Darn Good Deals’ focused on bringing local businesses together and show the public where to find deals. DGD used the airwaves, networking, and social media, specifically Twitter, Facebook & Linkedin as well as RSS feeds to drive traffic to their businesses.

As the mother of two I also serve as Vice President on the Parent Teacher Student Organization for Harris Elementary. We are now social media for the school allowing us to connect with parents posting news/events on the Facebook Fan Page. In addition for the first time the school will be offsetting the cost of their carnival by inviting businesses to sponsor rides and games.

Now as Marketing Director for KFNX News Talk Radio 1100 I have been able to take what I know about social media to promote the station, programming, and it’s advertisers.
