Justin Beidleman’s Summary

"Success in business requires training and discipline and hardwork, but if you're not frightened by these things, the opportunities are just as great today as they ever were."
–David Rockefeller

I joined Arbor Insurance Group in August of 2010. I am a graduate of Kutztown University with a Bachelors of Science degree in Industrial/Organizational Psychology. It was at KU where I realized my passion: helping and working with businesses and business owners to better enable them to succeed in their future endeavors, as well as, being able to offer my risk management skills and insights to allow both businesses and individuals grow respectively.

I chose Arbor because of their long successful track record that includes serving over 6,000 happy customers. I have joined a team of experienced insurance professionals that will allow me to be confident in all recommendations I make to clients.

I've learned there are a lot of insurance agents that are just looking for accounts. To those particular agents, any account is just another notch on their belt. After the sale the client doesn’t hear from that agent until renewal. I desire to be the agent who does the complete opposite by forming a relationship with my client and helping their business flourish in a safe and insured environment.

I am a lifetime resident of the Lehigh Valley. My intention is to develop long lasting business relationships within the Valley. My education coupled with my familiarity with our community will enable my clients to be confident that I am their “partner for peace of mind”.

Justin Beidleman’s Specialties:

I am a licensed Property & Casualty Insurance producer, as well as, a licensed Life & Annuities and Accident & Health Insurance producer. My particular interests within the Valley are small businesses, general and sub contractors, large and small restaurants, as well as, franchise and private auto dealerships.
