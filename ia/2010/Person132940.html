Mario Marquez’s Summary

Mechanical Engineer with 25 years of international experience in the pipeline sector of the Oil & Gas industry, including 13 years in management of pipeline inspection operations.

Has worked in pipeline design, corrosion control, and in-line inspection.

Has worked for EGEP Consultores (1985-88), PDVSA (1988-90)
Gulf Interstate Engineering (1991-95), PII Pipeline Solutions (1995 to 2009), and NDT Systems & Services (2009 to present).

Has been based in Caracas DF Venezuela (1985-90), Houston TX United States (1991-2004 / 2007 to present), Rio de Janeiro RJ Brasil (2004-05), Munro BA Argentina (2005-06), and Stutensee B-W Germany (2006-07).

Has been on business to Argentina, Brasil, Canada, England, Germany, Italy, Mexico, Nicaragua, the United States, and Venezuela.

Is proficient in Spanish (native language, fluent), English (second language, fluent), and Portuguese (third language, intermediate).

Is trained as a Black Belt in Six Sigma.

Is a member of ASME (www.asme.org), ASNT (www.asnt.org), NACE International (www.nace.org), and PIPE (www.pipeinst.org).

Serves on the Board of Directors of the INGAA Foundation (www.ingaa.org).

Mario Marquez’s Specialties:

Pipeline Inspection, Operations Management, Six Sigma.
