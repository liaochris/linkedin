Tammy Burnell’s Summary

Tammy Burnell is a dynamic professional who has become the most sought after Real Estate Coach and Professional Speaker for newer Real Estate Agents.

Tammy brings over 25 years experience to the industry and has the ability to combine real world know-how with new marketing technologies to achieve results many would not dream possible. She has been featured on radio and television including the Dr. Phil Show. Her methodology of harnessing the power of social media like Twitter, Facebook and blogs have become some of the biggest secret tools for the most successful new Real Estate Professionals of our time.

Tammy is passionate about helping Real Estate Agents who have been in the business 5 years or less, grab the opportunity of the formation stage of the career. Through virtual coaching and training, and speaking engagements she is able to take an agent step by step on creating their niche, their brand and their business.
