Paddy Srinivasan’s Summary

Experienced leader with diverse background in Research and Development,product strategy, software delivery, business development and strategic investments.

Currently building a startup in the area of Cloud Computing focussed on product development and managed services in the Windows Azure platform. Cumulux is a recognized leader in this domain and has been identified as a Top Cloud Innovator to watch out for.
