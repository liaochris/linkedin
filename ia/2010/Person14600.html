Ann Wicks’s Summary

My experience includes all aspects of employment law from employee hiring and discipline through the termination of the employment relationship. I have worked with technology start-ups, venture capital firms, large and small corporations, union and non-union employers, and a variety of public and private employers, as well as individuals. I have worked with various industries, including airline, trucking, insurance, food, retail, manufacturing, automotive, agricultural, high technology, biotechnology, venture capital, and hospitality.

My experience includes:

The Employer/Employee Relationship:

o Preparing employment agreements, consulting agreements, and other hiring documents
o Employee handbooks, leave policies, arbitration agreements, etc.
o Managing workplace relationships
o Improving employee performance and accountability
o Administering pregnancy, medical, disability and other leave policies
o Managing in a unionized work environment
o Reduction in force audits and counseling
o Responding to EDD claims or inquiries
o Terminating the employment relationship
o Responding to post-employment civil and administrative claims

Wage and Hour:

o Responding to wage and hour claims for overtime, meal period violations, commission claims or other wage issues
o Conducting exempt/non-exempt audits
o Preparing commission plans, bonus agreements, retention plans and other compensation agreements

Discrimination and Harassment Laws:

o Responding to internal and external complaints of harassment or discrimination
o Investigating complaints of harassment and discrimination
o Provision of sexual harassment and discrimination training in accordance with federal and state requirements
o Responding to administrative charges of harassment or discrimination filed with the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission or the California Department of Fair Employment & Housing
o ADA Compliance
o Alternative Dispute Resolution

Ann Wicks’s Specialties:

Full service employment law advice, litigation and counseling, including employment discrimination, harassment, wage and hour, family medical leave, ADA, employee training, and all other aspects of hiring, firing and retaining employees.
