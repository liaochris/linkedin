Rose Miller, SPHR’s Summary

Rose is the Owner of Pinnacle Human Resources, LLC. Rose’s primary area of expertise is in consulting related to human resources support that helps clients reduce costs, focus on their core business, reduce administration and provide expert HR consulting to clients. Rose's team consults in matters of employee policy and procedures manuals, HR law compliance, wage and hour compliance, recruiting, benefits and HR risk analysis, performance management, and HRMS systems.

Rose has over 25 years experience in both large and small companies. She graduated from Lesley University in Cambridge, Mass with a B.S. in Organizational Behavior and is certified as a Senior Professional in Human Resources.

Rose is an active member of the Society of Human Resources Management (SHRM), and the Capital Region HR Association. Board member of Parsons Child and Family Center, Girls, Inc., Girls, Inc. and Sand Lake Center for the Arts, as well as a member of a variety of professional chambers and associations.
