Robert Grey’s Summary

Rob Grey’s experience in the financial services industry spans over 30 years, and includes the areas of banking, insurance, and investments. In 1980, he registered as an Investment Advisor with the Securities and Exchange Commission when, disenchanted with the typical commission-driven advice of stock brokers, Rob took on the task of becoming a “student of the stock market.”

Today, Denver Money Manager manages millions of dollars for personal clients and institutional clients. As fee-only advisers, their financial advice is free of conflicts of interest.

Your financial future is not just about the money. Being "Rich" implies more than just dollars... it means a life filled with purpose and serenity for you and your family.

At Denver Money Manager we act as fiduciary for our clients. A fiduciary is obligated in all situations to put the interests of the client before their own and to avoid all potential conflicts of interests between themselves and the advice that they give to their clients.

At Denver Money Manger we take pride in our procoess. Our process empowers you to direct and control your financial life regardless of your current stage of accumulation. Denver Money Manager provides the education, discipline and strategies to help you achieve financial independence.

Robert Grey’s Specialties:

Understanding you and the relationship that you have with money. Empowering people with the knowledge to Get Rich, Stay Rich, and Live Well without being plagued with money misery.
