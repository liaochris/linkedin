Claire Griffin’s Summary

Experienced Executive Search and Recruitment Consultant with over 15 years recruitment expertise in the Advertising and Marketing Industry in the UK and Europe. Having started my career working with the leading direct marketing and promotional agencies of the day as well as leading advertising agencies seeking to integrate, in the last few years I have been very much focussed on the digital and mobile marketing and media industries.

I work primarily on senior strategic, planning and client service roles for both pure play digital and integrated agencies providing them with the best in digital and mobile talent.

I also work on Board, Managing Director and CEO roles where increasingly experience in a broad range of digital and non-digital disciplines is in demand.
