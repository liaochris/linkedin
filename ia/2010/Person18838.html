Avik Roy’s Summary

I am currently a founding partner at Res Partners. We strive to be our clients' trusted adviser of choice, helping them to make informed business decisions and implement well conceived plans. We believe that the best people are needed to solve the most difficult problems, which is why every Consulting Principal on the Res team must be a seasoned executive with at least 15+ years of experience.

Moreover, we believe, lasting innovation arises from knowledgeable cross-functional collaboration. The talent we bring together enables our clients to benefit from the depth and breadth of our collective experience, and from our ability to act as a change agent within our clients' organization.

My personal capabilities are best suited for leading smaller growth businesses focused on technology-enabled information, knowledge and learning services and solutions. My strengths include planning and driving growth, driving performance improvement, developing new avenues to expand revenue sources, building new service offerings from concept to commercialization, and managing modest-sized P&Ls.
