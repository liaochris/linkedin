Elizah Leigh’s Summary

There once was a formerly bright-eyed, bushy-tailed individual who finally decided to shake off the self-induced career coma that she had succumbed to for over 10 years. Recognizing that it was high time to pursue a field that gave her passion rather than merely just mortgage payment security, she searched her soul long and hard, realizing that she had long been an eager greenie and knowledge hound who could inspire people through her words to make a positive impact on the world. Conveniently, this skill offered her immense fulfillment and actually made her spirit smile, so with reckless abandon, she launched herself headfirst into the wild, wacky world of online freelance writing. She hasn't looked back, despite selling her blood for supplemental income more frequently than should be allowed by law.

**************************************************************************

I am extremely passionate about greenifying the world, reaching out to like-minded individuals and offering a digestible perspective on how to shift over into a more eco-enlightened lifestyle. I am a voracious reader of all environmentally-related news & resources and feel that I have become somewhat of a self-taught expert, but at the same time I am also always eager to expand my green knowledge database.

I'd like to continue to utilize my solid research & writing skills as an eco-writer-blogger-educator-environmental steward but I am always open to additional opportunities that will enable me to exercise my grey matter while making a positive impact on the environment and the world at large.

Elizah Leigh’s Specialties:

Stringing a collection of words together so they sing a happy little eco-tune.
