Brad Doyle’s Summary

Business Web Solutions, LLC (BWS) evolved naturally from the Technology Development Group (TDG) formed in March of 1994 to develop a new business model and platform for Amway Corp. The result of that four year project was the launch of Quixtar, a $1.1 Billion online retailer, in September of 1999. Brad was the TDG co-chairman along with Amway COO Tom Eggleston and with teams from both Business Web Solutions and Amway Corporation they conceptualized and implemented the Quixtar business model.

Business Web Solutions, LLC was formed by a handful of the founding members of the TDG. The first major project was to build the online version of Robert Kiyosaki's popular board game, Cash Flow 101. Since that time BWS has patented the most successful online game for business and sales organizations, PowerPlay Game, the patent pending iPresenter presentation software, and a web-based membership website with 3000 subscribers.

212° Training, LLC serves as a consultant to the Direct Selling Industry. Clients include Amway/Quixtar, Univera Life Sciences, AIM International, L'Bri, Arbonne, Shaklee, Global Hot Buys!, Dale Carnegie Training, and Next Level Sales.

Brad Doyle’s Specialties:

Business Web Solutions, LLC is an emerging leader in the business and sales motivation industry. PowerPlay Game and the iPresenter are lead products.
