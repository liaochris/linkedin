Dave Osmer’s Summary

Dave is Vice President for Executive Search Services at Hagel & Company. He leads the firm's Executive Search practice which is focused exclusively on conducting searches for nonprofit and public sector leadership positions.

Before joining Hagel & Company in 2005, he served as Executive Director of the Greater Everett Community Foundation and Big Brothers Big Sisters of King and Pierce Counties. For the eleven years immediately prior to his nonprofit leadership experiences, he held several senior management positions with the Community Relations Department of The Boeing Company. He also served as a member of search committees for United Way Chief Executive Officers in Pierce, King (twice) and Snohomish Counties. He is a member of the Executive Alliance, the Northwest Development Officers Association and is President of the Board of Directors for the Eastside Domestic Violence Program.

He is also currently a member of the Visiting Committee for the Executive Masters in Not-For-Profit Leadership Program at Seattle University. He is a past member of numerous nonprofit Boards of Directors including: Communities in Schools of Washington, Music Works Northwest; Leadership Tomorrow (Board Chair); Family Services; Washington State Commission on National and Community Service; Philanthropy Northwest; Page Ahead (Founding Board); and the Board of Governors, University of Michigan Alumni Club of Seattle (Board President).

Dave holds a Certificate in Corporate Community Relations from the Carroll School of Management at Boston College, a Master of Science in Engineering from the University of Washington, and a Bachelor of Science in Engineering Mathematics (cum laude), from the University of Michigan.
