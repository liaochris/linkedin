Long Version
A Inventory Manager- Activator (thank you StrengthFinders 2.0.) with the ability to create motion and momentum in people and energize the plans and ideas of others. I also have something else. Something I call…

Variable. So what’s this variable thing? It’s a trait-talent-training that sets you apart from your peers. Can you be specific? Yeah, sure. Take Madonna, the rock singer. Her variable is a talent for self promotion. How about Gary Vaynerchuk, the wine guy? His variable is creative marketing ideas and tremendous passion.

Well, a lot of people can do the organizing shtick of an activator, start up guy or turn around expert. What’s your variable? I’m a un-manager. Huh? UN-manager. Okay, I’ll bite. What is a un-manager.

A un-manager is a person who has the ability to quickly read the strengths in others and play to those strengths. They operate without formal job titles and pecking order to place people in positions of passion and responsibility that fit who they are. This makes them happier and more productive, more willing to own the job and contribute with their brain as much as their body. It’s truly a win-win for everyone.

• Operations Activator with ability to create motion and momentum in people and energize the plans and ideas of others.

• Un-manager with ability to quickly read the strengths of others and play toward those strengths regardless of job titles and pecking order.

• Adaptable ~ flourish in situations where success depends on responding to constant changing circumstances. This flexibility also helps to quickly recover from setbacks.

• Responsibility ~ very determined to get things done to fulfill the metrics / goals that gauge my work.

• Job Functions: Activator, Manager, Organizer, Analyst, People Developer
