Michael Wolf’s Summary

In my 12-year career I have worked in a wide range of testing and software development life cycles. These include system verification testing on industry leading C-Arm X-Ray machines, 9 multi-award winning video games, audio design for video games, and software testing at a large video game company. While working in software and hardware environments I’ve created and implemented several test cases for manual and automated testing processes. I have extensive experience as a lead Validation Test Engineer in post-assembly validation testing of central processing units, chipsets and motherboards for a world-renowned semiconductor developer and a white box tester in the device driver development arena for a hi-definition audio company. While working for these companies I have provided all levels of testing and software design as a manager, team leader and team player.

I have been involved in a number of highly technical projects requiring product design, development, integration, project management and quality assurance. I supervised and managed a team of 16 software testers in the development of several PC based software titles and Playstation titles.
