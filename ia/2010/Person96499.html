Jerry Kennedy’s Summary

Jerry Kennedy is the head of Sales and Marketing for KPEnterprises Business Consulting, Inc., a Microsoft Certified Partner and Small Business Specialist located in Sacramento, CA. KPE is the industry leader in zero-downtime network migration, cloud computing and green computing.

Jerry is also the owner of Inside Out Business Solutions, a sales and customer service training and consulting firm based in Northern California. He is a STLE Certified Lubrication Specialist® with an extensive background in the petroleum industry.

Jerry has combined his experience selling in the field to his personal development philosophy to produce sales and customer service training programs that get back to the basics and focus on building lasting relationships with customers. If you have comments, questions or would like Jerry to speak at your next sales meeting, feel free to contact him today!
