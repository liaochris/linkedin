Todd Rideman’s Summary

1996-2002:Store Manager, CVS/Pharmacy-During Todd's tenure at CVS, Todd specialized in returning underperforming stores to profitibility.
2002-Present:Co-owner of Crystal Clean Boston Inc. Crystal Clean is a professional residential cleaning company. Crystal Clean has been recognized in the Boston Magazine’s “Best of Boston” list two years in a row.
2007-Present:Founder, Rideman Services. Rideman Services is a merchant services company offering credit card processing and alternate business funding.
2009-Present-Founder and Ceo, WowWhatSavings.com. Please check the website for updated information.
While working full time and raising two small children, Todd finished his college education at Harvard University’s School of Extension Studies with an associate’s degree in 1999 and a bachelor’s degree, cum laude, in 2002. Todd is also an occasional guest lecturer at Harvard University’s School of Extension Studies and is the Alumni Chair of the Harvard Extension School Fund. Todd is active within the Harvard community and volunteers for many Alumni activities. Through Todd's businesses, he actively works with the annual Coats for Kids drive and collects food for the American Red Cross.
