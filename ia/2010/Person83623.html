Iain Brown’s Summary

Iain Brown has over twenty years industry experience in geological interpretation, and business development in the upstream oil and gas business.

Currently based in Singapore, Iain is the Interpretation Director for PGS in Europe, Africa and the Middle East. In this role he is responsible for Reservoir’s G&G teams and business in these regions.

The principle goal of PGS Reservoir is to add value to the PGS Seismic Data Library and to provide interpretation services as a critical component within integrated seismic acquisition projects. New Venture business support is a substantial component of Reservoir activities.
