Tiffany Winbush’s Summary

Tiffany began her public relations career in the nonprofit sector, working with a NYC based organization that provided educational programs to needy communities and served children and adults with developmental disabilities. In this role, she increased participation in 60 community service-based programs through media outreach and community relations. Tiffany also worked closely with the development department.

Tiffany transitioned into the media industry, working for a subsidiary of Clear Channel Communications, a global media company specializing in mobile and on-demand entertainment for local communities and providing premiere opportunities for advertisers. Tiffany was responsible for providing public relations strategies for the company's 12 individual units. She also secured speaking engagements at high-level conferences and increased the company's on-line presence through social media. Tiffany also worked closely with the Director of Diversity to operate the Minorities Achieving Corporate Success affinity group.

Dedicated to forming strategic partnerships, Tiffany prides herself on the solid relationships she's established with various media outlets and her colleagues in the PR field. Her expertise also includes integrating social media strategies with traditional media campaigns through the use of blogs, Twitter and Facebook Fan Pages.

Tiffany attended Louisiana State University and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communications with a concentration in Public Relations. In addition to Tiffany's professional interests, she is active in a number of community organizations including Girls Write Now, Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc., a community service-based organization founded in 1913, and serves as a board member for Manhattan's Community Board #1. Tiffany also mentors high-school girls throughout Manhattan and is chair of the Public Relations Society of America New York Chapter New Professionals committee.
