Dixie Gillaspie’s Summary

Helping clients take their natural path to success - "because no one ever reached their ideal destination by following someone else's roadmap!"

Working with clients who are passionate about transforming themselves in order to supercharge their career success.

I act as coach, mentor, change agent and general trouble-shooter to many business leaders and teams. I've been helping clients identify and eliminate roadblocks to success for more years than I care to admit and I'm committed to creating my signature “energy, synergy, harmony” combination in every engagement whether I'm facilitating a brainstorming session or sitting down for a one-on-one coaching session.

I am a Certified Kolbe Specialist which means I add the power of understanding your individual striving instincts and Kolbe Action Modes™ into our work together.

I have developed, with the support of author Bob Burg, coaching programs based on his best selling book, The Go-Giver and am the first coach certified and licensed to use the book as a basis for coaching.

A love of synergy and a passionate belief in the power of business to change the world and the power of the group to change business drives my enthusiasm for being a leader in a networking organization such as Yellow-Tie where I volunteer as President of the Yellow-Tie International Board of Directors.

Dixie Gillaspie’s Specialties:

Business and networking consulting/coaching, Kolbe consulting, Go-Giver workshops and coaching, individual and group coaching, custom workshops/presentations
