Dan Beam’s Summary

I've been a web developer for 6 years now, starting at 16 designing pages for my high school / free-lance clients.

This later transformed into working as the advertising webmaster for my college newspaper, The Daily Titan, and as a "web design specialist" for Mt. San Antonio College.

I also interned as a software engineer at Central Desktop, and ever more popular small business management website.

I had a great time at all of these places, and thoroughly enjoyed working with everybody there and [almost] everything I did.

However, I have recently reached the culmination of my professional career working for my current employer - Anschutz Entertainment Group (AEG). I am doing the hardest and most rewarding work I've ever done, at a much higher profile and scale than I ever imagined I would be a part of at 22 years old.

I work with some awesome people and consider myself extemely lucky to not be making missiles for a living (the original plan, had a start date and everything).
