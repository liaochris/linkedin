Darren Cannell’s Summary

Staff president for 1998-1999 school year (E.D. Feehan High School).
Member of the professional development committee (E.D. Feehan High School).
Chair of the E.D. Feehan Staff meetings.
Directions Committee at E.D. Feehan High School.
Participant and chair of numerous long range planning sessions for the Saskatchewan Volleyball Association.
Developed the Cyber School Initiative for the Saskatoon Catholic School System.
Presented at numerous provincial, national and international conferences on distance education.





Active Member of St. Thomas More.
Developed and ran the largest volleyball club in Saskatchewan for 10 years. My role in the club was administrator and head coach. (T-Shirts Volleyball Club)
Organized and chaired the first beach volleyball committee and started the program in Saskatchewan.
Venue manager for Canada Games Volleyball.
President of the Saskatchewan Volleyball Association for four years.
Director for Volleyball Canada for four years.
Proposed and hosted one of seven Pro Beach volleyball events in Canada.
Member of the committee that received the first Junior World Volleyball event in North America.





One unique contribution to my teaching of art is the development of the Art Showdown which is a yearly province wide art contest.
The second major contribution is the massive E.D.Feehan website and the Virtual Physical Art Gallery which displays artwork in the front entrance of the school, this has greatly increased the awareness of art in the school.
Wrote the first online course in the division. (Information Processing 10) Proposed the Cyber school concept to the division and then became the project leader of the Saskatoon Catholic Cyber School.
Developed and maintain the SCCS website.
Developed and maintain a Developer's Toolkit (WebCT) course for SCCS teachers.
Writer and Developer of the award winning Teaching and Developing Online Blog
