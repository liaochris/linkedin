Bill Grout’s Summary

Why I'm on LinkedIn:
•As a life coach and business coach, I am looking to help people get past whatever is stopping them from living the life they truly want. In most cases, what's stopping them is fear--fear of failure, fear of rejection, fear of embarrassment, fear of not being good enough, fear of responsibility...the list goes on. I'm trained to help clients understand exactly how fear shows up in their lives--and how it keeps them stuck. I help them get past fear to find meaning and passion and purpose. I support them to lower stress, increase wealth, find self-acceptance and create better relationships at work and at home.

• One of my specialties is coaching people who are looking for a change and don't yet know what it is--what used to be called a "mid-life crisis." Making life changes usually means finding the courage to face fears and take risks.

•As a workshop facilitator, I'm looking for businesses, organizations and government agencies who need powerful and effective leadership training for their managers and supervisors. I am also looking to work with publishing companies (editors, publishers and advertising sales people). Before becoming a coach and workshop facilitator, I enjoyed a 25-year career in publishing with companies like Ziff-Davis, Times Mirror Magazines and Time Warner. I was editor-in-chief of SKIING Magazine and editor-in-chief of Mountain Sports Press, a book publishing unit of Time Warner that I founded. I have over two decades of experience in corporate management, years of personal-development training, and a commitment to a life that includes loving relationships, career success and vigorous health.

Two Key Things I Teach:

• Powerful coaching skills for managers.
• Time management from choice rather than obligation

I've been a professional coach and workshop facilitator for the past five years and a successful corporate manager for over 20.
