Jeff Davis’s Summary

Jeff Davis recently retired as VP/General Manager of Global Operations for P&G’s Salon Professional (Wella) business headquartered in Frankfurt, Germany after 23 years of service. Prior to this assignment, he was the Director of Global New Business Development & Alliance Management for P&G Pharmaceuticals in Cincinnati, Ohio. Jeff has worked for Procter & Gamble in 6 different product sectors including consumer packaged goods, pharmaceutical and beauty businesses and worked in 5 different countries in North America and Europe (USA, Slovakia, Czech Republic, Germany & Canada).

His experiences span from opening developing markets in Eastern Europe in the early nineties to building P&G’s global business in the Pharmaceutical industry in Germany, Canada and in the U.S. before accepting the job to lead the acquisition and integration of Wella into P&G, creating a new global Professional Hair company in the Beauty sector. Jeff has been involved in many areas to influence positive changes in the industry and communities in which he has lived and worked. He has served on a number of boards in the Pharmaceutical, consumer package goods, drug retailing and beauty sectors.

Jeff is now bringing his experience from P&G to key partners. He is collaborating with University of Utah David Eccles School of Business where he will assist in teaching, strategy development and stakeholder relations to bring real-world Fortune 20 Executive expertise to the classroom and in the further development of the business school with key stakeholders. Secondly, Jeff is advising Ernst & Young on the development of their business advisory practice working with their top 40 global customers. Finally, Jeff is working as an Executive in Residence with DW Healthcare Partners (www.dwhp.com), a private equity company, assisting to scan for investment opportunities.
