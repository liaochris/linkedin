Michael Young’s Summary

MBN Recruitment Solutions are a young but vastly experienced Recruitment Consultancy. Established in 2007 by a group of senior Recruitment professionals, MBN has grown to become a recognised quality supplier of recruitment services to a variety of prestigious organisations. We value most highly our reputation and passion for our profession.

Since our establishment, MBN has grown to include separate but complimentary business divisions – each headed by experienced recruitment professionals. We focus upon Technology recruitment – with divisions supplying candidates to the Information Technology, Insight and Analytics and Engineering sectors.

We understand our markets – and the professionals employed within those markets. We value our reputation as trusted advisors in a number of key areas – from contingency recruitment through to retained assignments, salary surveys, intelligence and understanding of market conditions.
