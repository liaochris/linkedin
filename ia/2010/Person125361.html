Owen Linderholm’s Summary

Content and editorial director and strategist. I have experience with producing all forms of content from traditional journalistic writing to interactive online 'experiences', from technical writing to corporate presentations, from multi-author blog platforms to publishing printed books. I have been successful at organizations from tiny startups to large enterprises.

My core skills include writing for all kinds of audiences, editing, publishing, presentation, marketing communications, online and rich media and efficient and economic budgeting and P&L management. I have a track record of consistently innovating and of developing new techniques and processes to improve audience numbers and the quality of service to the audience. I am a firm believer in understanding the audience, testing what you deliver and then optimizing what you deliver, using solid data to drive decision making. I am an effective project manager with the ability to work across all levels, bring together internal and external team members and deliver on budget. I am a confident and concise communicator with experience in TV, radio, conferences and exhibitions and in dealing with broadcast and print journalists.

Owen Linderholm’s Specialties:

Online and interactive media, content management and publishing systems, editing at all levels including highly technical and B2B, producing and publishing content that meets audience and business needs, content strategy, content hierarchy and taxonomy creation, social media and blogs, business strategy, product development, site optimization, SEO.
