Saman (Sammy) Mansourpour’s Summary

As co-founder of TheAgency, Saman is behind one of the UK's fastest growing integrated creative agencies.

Saman is one of the UK's leading brand response specialists, having pioneered the practices of online and offline marketing campaign integration in early 2000. His involvement in one of London's most successful Interactive agencies enabled him to test early brand response campaigns for leading blue chip organisations such as BT and Trainline.com.

TheAgency has this year delivered some of the highest performing advertising campaigns in the UK, including 20% growth quarter on quarter for British School of Motoring, alongside developing two ground breaking online targeting campaigns with Times Online and Wall Street Journal.

Saman (Sammy) Mansourpour’s Specialties:

Planning and managing integrated advertising campaigns designed to build brands and drive sales.

Specific experience in delivering Viral marketing and tracking, TV, Advertising networks, Social media.
