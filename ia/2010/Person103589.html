Clarity has been established since 2006 and has built a first class reputation for supplying IT Recruitment professionals to the Recruitment Industry across the UK.

For Clients: The team has over 15 years experience working in the IT Recruitment market all of which have achieved excellent results in their time as technical recruiters/ managers. This enables Clarity to offer its Clients an unparalleled knowledge of the qualities that make up exceptional Recruitment Consultants as well as being strongly networked in the industry.

For Candidates: We have a fantastic range of Clients from which we can talk about and arrange for consultants to visit. We ensure all opportunities offer top quality career prospects ranging from Resourcer /Trainee positions to Senior Management & Directorship and we provide our candidates with information on all areas of prospective employees which cover; earning potential, existing billing figures, growth, training, retention, environment and unique selling points about each business/ opportunity.
