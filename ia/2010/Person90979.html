Jason Smale’s Summary

Jason has a broad range of technical skills from defining the user experience right through to developing backend code for enterprise applications.

Throughout high school and university Jason ran his own business specialising in standards based website design and development utilising open source platforms.

Jason also worked in a rapid development and integration role situated in Deloitte’s Innovation Acceleration Team. This position revolved around taking an initial idea to market. He specifically focussed on the internal development and implementation of the Vizzl data visualisation platform.

Since joining Deloitte’s Online Practice in March 2009 he has continued to push the boundaries of online technologies to provide sustainable & engaging user experiences.
