Scott Sala’s Summary

Relentlessly-focused Paid Search (PPC) Marketing Strategist & Account Manager. Intense knowledge of all aspects of paid search, with a surprising simple philosophy of hard work on the basics - something often lost in a world of flashy strategies, complex testing proposals and over-analysis of reports.

I seek opportunity to lead passionate individuals in the dynamic search space, working towards extremely-profitable efficiency and explosive growth for a diverse selection of clients. My 15+ years of online/digital experience lend itself well to leading, motivating and training teams to excel at work they love.
