Jean Lukens’s Summary

WHO WE ARE:
The North Phoenix Chamber is celebrating 47 years of service to businesses throughout the North Phoenix Valley. We unite the people of commerce, business, education, and the professions to work together for the development of the Greater North Phoenix Area. The Chamber believes that by fostering business growth and prosperity, the quality of life for the whole area is improved.

WHO DO WE SERVE?
Our service area reaches from Northern Avenue north to Anthem; and 51st Avenue east to Scottsdale Road. There are over 18,000 businesses in our service area and the numbers keep growing. Our Members range from individual business men and women who want to become involved within their business community; small business owners and entrepreneurs with fewer than 10 employees; all the way to companies with over a hundred employees.

NPCC MEMBERSHIP – WHAT WE PROVIDE:
The past several years NPCC has transformed itself into an Organization that truly recognizes what its Members want and need from a Chamber of Commerce.

We understand the importance of keeping up with advances in technology and business strategies and the kinds of training and support businesses need to keep a sharp competitive edge. We do this by:

 Providing Members with a technical edge – their own website within a website hosted by NPCC
 Weekly Networking, Marketing, and Lead Generating Events
 Monthly Seminar Luncheon Series
 Monthly Mixers hosted by our Members
 Promoting your Open Houses and Grand Openings
 Health Benefits
 Free Inclusion on our online Directory
 Discounted Advertising on our website and in our Newsletter
 Discounts on other Member Services including – Identity Theft; Advertising; Marketing; Insurance; Storage

Attending just one function can pay for your membership several times over – all it takes is meeting your business neighbors and building strong relationships within your community. You never know whom you are going to meet…
