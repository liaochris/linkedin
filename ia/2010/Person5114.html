Alan Gecht’s Summary

Commercial Real Estate Broker for over 30 years.
Prior to that was a practicing CPA for 7 years with various firms including Arthur Andersen.
Originally associated with “traditional” national commercial real estate firms that represented both Landlords/Sellers and Tenants/Buyers. Was a continued recipient of Salesperson of the Month and Year awards.
Spent an additional 5 years as a Vice President of a commercial real estate development/leasing company; gaining tremendous insight and better understanding of the leasing process from the Landlords’ perspective.
Through this experience, it became clear of the lack of and need for Tenants/Buyers to have their own professional/competent representation.
Since 1990, committed to and have practiced with this philosophy in mind: successfully representing only Tenants/Buyers (never Landlords) to prevent conflicts of interest. The diverse yet real estate related background brings distinct capabilities to the table; ultimately proving beneficial during the entire negotiation process. This, coupled with a commitment to stay personally involved in every deal, ensures accountability and consistency from transaction to transaction regardless of geography.

Alan Gecht’s Specialties:

Tenant Rep: Real Estate Broker for Local & Nat’l Office Tenants. We reduce stress & frustration: get Tenants protection & time back to focus on their daily business activities!

Skills utilized when renewing a lease or relocating via creative planning, market surveys /site evaluations & aggressive lease negotiations. Assist with expansions, contractions, economic challenges, review of operating expense & rent increases, lease interpretations & support if disputes w/Landlord.
