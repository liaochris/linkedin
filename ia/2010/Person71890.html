B. Kathy Wright’s Summary

I spent 25 plus years in the alternative wellness industry as a Certified Nutritional Coach, Raw Foods and Macrobiotic Consultant teaching lifestyle habits that create health and wellness. You know how you all would like to be healthier, well I have made that a reality for hundreds of individuals.

My life took a turn when in 2000 Renee Russo gave an international endorsement for a product created by my family business. In Good Housekeeping Magazine Renee Russo said FROWNIES wrinkle reducer skin care patches were her secret beauty weapon.

The interest in this anit-aging product was so great that I put everything else on hold and together with my husband took over his family's business.

Since the FROWNIES business was off and running I added my personal passion health and wellness to the mix by creating a wellness newsletter and presenting beauty from the inside out at spa shows.

As a Natural Health Professional, Nutritional Coach,and Quantum Bio-feedback Specialist, I spend two days a week coaching and teaching wellness lifestyle and food preparation. Coaching only four individuals at any one time is most comfortable for me. I like to make myself available to my clients and stay focused.

I finished my ND (Naturopathic Doctor) and first published book in 2010. It is avaliable on amazon and http://www.frownies.com web site. I am available as a seminar speaker or individual personal wellness consultant.

I am a seminar speaker for the Spa Industry, Human Resource Wellness and Health events, Trade Shows, Woman's' events.

I am looking for opportunities to inspire and motivate others to make changes in their lives that create life long personal wellness. FROWNIES help remove wrinkles giving you a beautiful appearance and I as a lifestyle consultant and ND assist with the health and beauty from the inside out.
