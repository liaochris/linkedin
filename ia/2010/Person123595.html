James Cameron’s Summary

I develop online tools for law firms and attorneys to help them effectively market themselves and provide critical information for their clients. In addition, I prepare and distribute marketing material, press releases and anything else that can be used to brand clients in a variety of online forums, including Web sites, blogs, enews, social media sites such as Facebook, and lawyer-specific sites such as Avvo and Super Lawyer.

Many of my clients, who include some of the top names in the state of Washington in various practice areas, work in fascinating areas of law and have great stories to tell about their work and accomplishments. Many of these stories have a compelling human element. I want to help my clients tell these stories.

I have been working in this area since 1995, just as the World Wide Web starting rolling. I worked with the WSBA during this period to roll out the first large scale CLEs on how attorneys and law firms could use the Internet in their practices. These first programs were immensely popular and were part of the great changes just starting to shape the industry. My first clients were Jon Scott Fox, one our state's great DUI attorneys (and still a client), and Joan Feldman, one of the pioneers in computer forensics (now retired).

I have great respect for my clients and the work they do.
