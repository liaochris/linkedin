Stephen Waybright’s Summary

EXECUTIVE-LEVEL MANAGEMENT
Program Management, Account Management, Product Engineering and Marketing

Tech Product Business Leader with over 25 years experience bringing digital imaging, computing and consumer electronics products from concept to market while fostering an environment where making a difference is valued as much as making the numbers.

I get energized by devising creative approaches to drive business to the next level and I've been lucky to have had such opportunities through each turn in my career. I've applied emerging technologies to create new product categories, overhauled processes to better deliver programs in the race to market, and shifted business models to get revenues and profits into high gear.

Through all this, I would characterize the key to my success in general business management from three things: a laser focus on the critical few issues, making those priorities clear to an empowered leadership team, and driving for processes that allow the organization to succeed in its mission.

• Possess broad functional experience in building successful P&L businesses built on market insight, product innovation, solid engineering, operational efficiency and customer service.
• Lead successful teams by driving alignment of vision and objectives through consistent messaging of purpose and priorities.
• Direct global, cross-functional teams through all phases of product life-cycles and ongoing business operations for digital cameras, personal computers and other consumer electronics products for Kodak, Compaq and HP.

I am ultimately looking for the opportunity to apply my broad experience to a position as General Manager for a technology product business unit or auto enthusiast business.
