Andreas Hamberger’s Summary

Also knows as Andreas Hamberger and Andreas Girardet. Recognized internationally as an innovative and dynamic person, Enterprise Architect and Senior System Administrator, who has developed strong technical, sales and executive knowledge, I enjoy working in a team environment and thrive on daily challenges. I have experience as a motivational speaker and technically oriented sales person and manager. I design, engineer, implement, advocate and sell Enterprise Software and Computer Services into the Australia and New Zealand market place. In my varied ISP- and IT-centric career I have managed staff of up to 15 direct reports, 1000+ indirect reports and helped drive some medium to large scale projects.
