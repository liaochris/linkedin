Karen Clark’s Summary

With 12 years in internet-based direct sales, developing her skills and business both offline and online, Karen’s expertise ranges from website development, social media integration and online branding, to community networking, publicity and training in personal and professional development. These, combined with her teaching background and professional communication skills, result in inspirational, educational, and useful content through MyBusinessPresence.com

Nothing replaces connecting with people in person or by phone, and as an experienced and active sales leader, Karen walks the walk as she sells, books, recruits and supports a team offline, while expanding her reach and service online and through the media. Karen enjoys teaching others exactly where, when, and how to spend their precious time to establish a presence, while remaining true to their principles and personal business.

Karen is an Executive National Director with Story Time Felts. She is has been certified as an ELITE Leader through the Direct Selling Women’s Alliance (DSWA), and was also honored with their exclusive “Spirit Award” in 2008. Karen has been the President of the Sonoma Marin Chapter of the DSWA for two years and has been featured as an Online Presence expert in the DSWA’s Mentored by the Masters CD series. Karen has 12years of direct selling experience with her company and has appeared in “Step Into Success” and “The Home Business Connection” magazines, as well as Top Sellers Tell, a book highlighting successful home-based entrepreneurs. She is a Co-Author of the sales guidebook, Direct Selling Power
.
In her 12 years experience with Story Time Felts she has built a team of 100s of consultants who report to her and who distribute their products through the direct sales/party plan model. As the Director of Consultant Development with Story Time Felts, Karen works directly with the President as a remote trainer developing training, incentives, and recognition for the field.
