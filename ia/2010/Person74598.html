Georgina Brown’s Summary

Georgi Brown has been working in the IT recruitment industry for 7 years. She commenced her professional career with Accenture in 2001 as a Communications Consultant, later being promoted to Recruitment Consultant and then again to Account Manager.

During Georgi’s tenure with Accenture, her focus was mainly within the Banking and Finance sectors, working with clients such as MLC, IAG, Westpac, and Accenture. Georgi was responsible for the search and selection of permanent and contract employees across multiple skill sets.

Georgi advanced her career by commencing with Finite in 2004 as an Account Manager on the Optus account. In this role she works closely with Colin Westwood in developing relationships across the Optus Resourcing Centre as well as across the various business divisions. Georgi is heavily involved in the sourcing of quality candidates for both permanent and contract roles, and has an exemplary track record of service delivery. She was recently promoted to Senior Account Manager status.
