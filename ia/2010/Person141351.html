Cindy Nevarez LION’s Summary

Creature Feature Pet Sitting, LLC is actually the fourth animal-related business that I've had in my life. Previously I've had a grooming shop, a hobby breeding/showing business, and another pet sitting service...all in California in the years prior to moving to Arizona where Creature Feature Pet Sitting, LLC is located.

Over the years I have developed expertise in care of multiple animal species, and intuitively know what's needed in most situations. In many cases it's not just about our clients' pets, but it's about the security of their homes, which is why it's important to have a good, specific insurance policy. Even though it's not overtly advertised, a pet sitter is also a house sitter.

A pet sitter must be on his/her toes in regards to the environment in and around the home where the animals live. Not only that but he/she must be sure the pet is completely secure inside that home. Also, even before a pet sitter steps one foot into a client's home assurances must be made that protect the client's privacy, keys, and personal information.

My own goal as a professional pet sitter has always been to set an example of knowledgable, caring, reliabilty, with assurances to our clients that we have thought of every possible thing to insure that their pet will be happy, healthy and stress-free while they go away...GUILT-free and enjoy their trip!

Cindy Nevarez LION’s Experience

  • Owner

    Creature Feature Pet Sitting, LLC

    (Consumer Services industry)

    February 2004 — Present (6 years 8 months)

    I am the managerial co-owner of this company with my daughter Mae. Since she is a now full-time university senior. I run, manage, and do much of what is required to keep this business going during her semesters. In the meanwhile we serve the city of Mesa, AZ, and its closely surrounding border areas.

    We care for our clients' animal family members in their own homes while they are away. We do this with 30 minute visits, overnight stays, or actually living in their homes. We may also do extras like administering medications...including insulin injections, keeping up the animals' normal activity level, assuring emergency care if needed, taking in mail/newspapers, and otherwise making the home looked occupied, thus keeping it secure.

    The most important part of our business is keeping the pets happy and stress-free while their "mommies and daddies" are away from home. Many times a kennel environment causes more stress than an animal can normally handle. When their people return from their trips and see the evidence of this, it often causes them to not want to take any more trips...mostly from guilt at having gone away in the first place! However, we assure our clients that pets are happiest in their own homes...as they are creatures of habits...wanting their own bowl, their own bed, and their own toys around them. They just know everything is fine if their world is undisturbed. Keeping their lives normal and their routine the same is what we do best.
