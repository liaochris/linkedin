Calla Gold’s Summary

Jewelry Designer in Santa Barbara since 1983. As a Personal Jeweler, I visit my clients by appointment. I specialize in designing wedding rings, restoring inherited jewelry, repairing fine jewelry, including difficult ot work with gems, like Opal, Emerald and Tanzanite.
I also do single event (Jewelry Show) fundraising for local non-profits.
Topics I speak on include, "Revitalize Your Jewelry Wardrobe" and "Discover your Best Jewelry Look."
I write industry articles on my specialty, choosing the right shape and style of Jewelry for the face shape, body type and lifestyle of my clients.
My goals include to mentor newer jewelers who choose the Personal Jeweler business model.
I'd like to design for a wider audience.

Calla Gold’s Specialties:

Custom Jewelry design, Wedding RIngs, Inherited Jewelry Restoration and Re-design,article writing on Jewelry and Sales topics, Replication of lost or existing Jewelry, Bridal ring design.
