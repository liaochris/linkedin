Lou DiPalma’s Summary

Garnet Capital Advisors, LLC is a New York-based financial-services firm specializing in managing loan-portfolio sales for credit-granting institutions Garnet also completes valuation and sale assignments for stakeholders and federal agencies.

Garnet Capital has 20 professionals with expertise in all areas of bank assets.

Garnet Capital was founded in 2004 by industry veterans myself, Sean McVity and Robin Ishmael. The Principals have 50+ years combined experience in the loan-sales industry, and have managed the sale of over $30 billion of financial assets in 500+ client transactions. Garnet Capital provides career-length expertise and intensive, cradle-to-grave advisory services across the full range of asset types and performance categories:

Garnet Capital has offices in New York, Tucson, Boca Raton and San Juan. Irving Place Merchant Banking and Fortress Investment Group are investors in Garnet.

Lou DiPalma’s Specialties:

Garnet Capital is one of the largest provider of assets to the distressed debt marketplace, as seller's agent. We also sell numerous performing portfolios every year, including commercial, residential mortgage and unsecured consumer.
