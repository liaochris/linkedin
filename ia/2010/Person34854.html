Celeste Blackwell’s Summary

My strengths lie in the Training/Teaching, consulting, presentation, project management, infrastructure and creating solutions. I've recently certified as PRINCE2-Practitioner.

I am quite satisfied when I can serve people in putting technology to use as a “problem-solver”. I am very comfortable with both Macintosh and Windows technology, but I am not afraid to use other systems when the need develops.(Successfully trained as SUN administrator for example.)

English is my mother-tongue, my German is fluent in spoken and written word, and I have a basic command of spoken french. I’ve been primarily employed in the Advertising, New Media and Software and publishing branches, and my most recent position was with DoubleClick, a division of Google. prior to that I worked for a System integrator in Germany, where Web Master, email based marketing, Sales support, Training, End User software documentation and troubleshooting belong to my list of responsibilities.

My idea of the ideal job- having responsibility for implementing useful technology that has a positive impact on the “bottom line” and training people to use that technology to increase efficiency as well as fun and motivation in the workplace. Given my international / digital experience, and contacts, I can also imagine a virtual home office position. This opens up possibilities for a satellite office or a solid yet inexpensive way for a company to get started in the US market.
