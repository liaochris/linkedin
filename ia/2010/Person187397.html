Shannon Barnes’s Summary

I've had senior leadership opportunities in a variety of industries and environments -- as an executive in a multi-national manufacturer, a manager in a successful financial services company, and as a senior strategist, communications director, and pastoral leader for two growing churches. This diverse portfolio has given me a unique understanding of the needs of people and organizations, and the knowledge and experience to help both excel.

Crafting preferred futures and helping organizations achieve them is my core skill and my personal passion. I am a visionary leader with experience in strategy, leadership and board development, staff and project management, conflict resolution, communications, and community building. Throughout my life, I have consistently demonstrated the ability to organize complex relationships and work environments into highly productive boards and teams.
