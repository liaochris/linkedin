Craig Ury’s Summary

A senior professional with in-depth accounting, financial and management experience with start-up and high growth, public and private companies. Exceptional interpersonal and quantitative skills and track record to manage complex projects within large cross-functional teams. Extensive experience in:

Accounting and Financial Planning: Developed annual budgets and long-range financial forecasts in accordance with GAAP, developed DCF models and utilized ROI analysis to determined capital allocation among R&D projects, and developed cost management strategies relating to contracts with purchasing groups.

Financial Reporting: Generated quarterly internal financial management reports and conducted variance analysis, drafted SEC disclosure documents (10-K, 10-Q, Proxy).

Mergers & Acquisitions: Executed numerous M&A and JV transactions with an aggregate transaction value in excess of $2.0 billion. Coordinated due diligence, conducted valuation analysis, drafted and negotiated term sheets.

Capital Raising: Raised over $2.0 billion in capital through IPOs, follow-on offerings and private placements. Coordinated preparation of offering materials, prepared investor presentations, contacted potential investors, coordinated investor road shows, and coordinated funding/closing process.

Venture Capital / Private Equity Investing: Assessed and recommended over $100 million in investments. Responsibilities included reviewing business plans, evaluating the competitive landscape, and monitoring performance of investments.

Personnel Management and Development: Managed a 20-person Corporate Finance Analyst Program. Responsibilities included recruiting, training, supervising, conducting performance reviews and determining annual compensation. Developed financial training programs and process protocols for a Pacific Island government’s finance department.
