Purandar A Amin in Nutshell
Purandar A.Amin, at the age of seven, began reading the books in a hermit’s ashram near the village where his father taught in a grammar school. Within four years, walking every day along the flowing river to the Ashram, Purandar had read every book in the hermit’s library. Purandar graduated with honors in Economics, Accounting and Laws and was a Chartered Accountant in India. He moved to California in 1972, passing the CPA exam the same year. In 1978 he opened his own business, Puru Properties, and started his current business, Central Capital Corporation, in 1999.
He shaped himself as a proactive multifaceted leader. His creative, analytical problem solving, negotiating, perseverance and Value and people orientation gave him a remarkable success and exposure to multiple fields. The
An author
Purandar has authored a few prominent books including ‘Foreclosures: How to Prevent, Stop, Beat and survive' in 1994 published by Om Sai Publications. The book is republished as: Everything you ever wanted to know in Foreclosure but were afraid to ask.” He recently authored “How to live stress free in foreclosure”. He also authored ‘River Flows” containing the letters of spiritual truths as he addressed to his daughter ‘Rhutu'.

On a Social and Charitable mission to create Change
‘Serve All – Help All' Inc., a non profit corporation, for educational and health issues was formed by Purandar in the year 2005. This is an initiative to make human life contributory to any one who is in need and enable one to get out of ones troubles or miseries.
He now leads the efforts to establish the Sai Raiba Ambalal Memorial Trust (Sai RAM Trust) in Ahmedabad, India. The Trust Endeavors to create an Institution of Excellence – a fully accredited University that will provide a free education to deserving young people.
