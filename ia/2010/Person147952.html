I'm Founder and CEO of ZGroup Mobile. I personally supervised all the work done ZGroup Mobile from both a manager perspective and technical perspective.

I have also started NextStep (a business consultation company) back in Jun/2007.

My main goal (as the owner of ZGroup Mobile) is to maintain my company's ever-evolving nature, both in terms of financial growth and launching new, innovative services.

We started in 2002 and we were among the first to deisgn and deploy black/white mobile games to many operators like AT&T, Vodafone, and O2. Our games were on Top 10 games for more than one year.
I also designed and implemented zMoviePlayer which is the first true movie player for mobile phones. The solution worked even on black/white devices by using 3d dithering techniques.

We started as publisher from 2003. Our distribution list includes more than 400 distribution channels worldwide. Our catalog of mobile content is one of the best and biggest. We have more than 1.5 million items in our catalog plus more than 5000 mobile games (midp) plus 5000 mobile applications (pocketpc, brew, blackberry, iphone and java). Our catalog has branded content such as ringtones (top billboard), wallpapers (celebrities), Videos (hidden cameras, sport, movie trailers, ...etc) and branded games/applications.


We have also developed Content2mobile solution which will convert video, ringtones, wallpapers, and animations to suitable formats on the mobile.

We are a pioneer in the Arab world (MENA market) for Mobile Content business. We designed several mobile Arabic games that were published on many operators like Dama (MTC), PrayerTimes (STC), Chess (Omantel). . . etc.

We have also designed mLocalization which allow any publisher to localize mobile java games between any two languages without the need for sourcecode (on the binaries level). read more <a href="http://www.zgroup-mobile.com/mLocalization.html"> Mobile Localization </a>.
