Eric Zoromski’s Summary

Eric is President/Partner at Fullhouse, an Interactive Agency headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin with offices in Chicago and Wisconsin's Fox River Valley. As President, Eric provides overall strategic direction to the agency, leads Fullhouse's business development & marketing programs and oversees its business-to-business and consumer marketing practices. Eric joined Fullhouse in 1995 (known then as Channel One) to add interactive capabilities to what at the time was a small, successful video production company. Over the past thirteen years Eric has been instrumental in the growth and evolution of Fullhouse, helping lead Fullhouse to its position as one of the largest independent interactive agencies in the Midwest.

Throughout his career with Fullhouse, Eric has guided the development of business-to-business and consumer marketing and communications programs for a wide range of companies spanning many industries, including Kimberly-Clark, MillerCoors, Johnson Controls, GE Healthcare, and Manpower.
