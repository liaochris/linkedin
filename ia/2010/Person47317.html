Daniel Lieberman’s Summary

It's never been easier to start a business, reinvent a career, or invigorate a municipality. People just need to be shown how to start. I help clients get to know Internet technology and how to use it effectively.

I'm a generalist. I've been called a polymath (complimentary) and a dilettante (not) because I'm interested in lots of different things. Clients find my perspective different and invigorating.

Many-to-many communication and sharing are simply how the Internet is. Nothing else can really work in our future. Sharing is the 'secret sauce' of civilization, the warp to competition's woof. I'm delighted with how easy the web makes it to be mutual (and competitive!).

The Internet and Social Media revolutionize everything in civil society, business and government. I'm excited about the future because the networked world we live in can be a richer, fairer and smarter world.
