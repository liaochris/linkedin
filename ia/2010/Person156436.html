Nathan Schock’s Experience

  • Director of Public Relations

    POET

    (Privately Held; Renewables & Environment industry)

    February 2007 — Present (4 years )

    As the Director of Public Relations for the largest ethanol producer in the world, I have a wide variety of responsibilities. I lead media relations and regularly work with national media who cover cleantech, energy and the environment. I initiated the company's new media efforts, which now encompass Twitter, FriendFeed, Facebook, blogs and YouTube. I also play a role in community relations, special events and working with relevant NGOs and industry groups. I am a regular speaker at a variety of events and work with other groups to enhance the image of ethanol and cleantech.

During my tenure as director, the public relations billings of the agency increased twenty-fold and we grew to specialize in renewable energies like wind and biofuels.
