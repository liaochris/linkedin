Mark A. Silver’s Summary

Working as the CIO for Health Services ($1B), the IT business of Siemens HealthCare USA, focusing on increasing profit, improving productivity & reducing costs through effective knowledge systems, people and their roles, and processes. Am also an active volunteer helping set strategic direction, manage finances and develop marketing and communication programs through the board of directors for the Geographical Society of Philadelphia, and as part of the leadership team of GPSEG through the Not-for-Profit sub group.

I have held several senior leadership positions during past 25 years across multiple business disciplines in large complex global organizations, government agencies, not-for profits and newspapers. I have cross-cultural experience having worked in Australia, the United States of America, and Germany, with some brief stints in Canada, England, Mexico, Brazil, and Chile.
