Ken Neitzke’s Summary

My passion is helping others to succeed and excel beyond their own expectations.

 Being a CAD Coach who listens and observes first; then suggests solutions and strategies.

 Working as a CAD Manager to trouble shoot and solve AutoCAD or Revit problems.

 Conduct CAD training to teach others the tips and tricks on how to work more efficiently and faster with AutoCAD.

 Proactive hands-on CAD Manager who shares in the production of design solutions which gives a greater understanding of the needs of the CAD end users.

 Engaged in and directing Revit training and Revit pilot projects.

 Participate, learn and practice the BIM process; to be on the cutting edge of new technology and practices.

 Work as an architectural designer to solve space planning problems to make facilities more efficient in workflow and energy use. Upgrade existing building interiors and exteriors to create enjoyable working and retail shopping environments.

 Work as a LEED AP to encourage the use of sustainable building materials, Green Building practices / methods and energy efficiency to preserve our precious resources for future generations. To create the base for Energy Star and LEED certification.

 Embrace change and continuously looking for new ways to solve old problems.

Successful designer of small to medium size projects ranging from build-outs / renovations for retail tenants, to retail bank branches, industrial facilities, dental offices, commercial offices, religious facilities, and exterior building upgrades. I designed and worked on over (550) projects in the past nine years.

Strong believer in:
 Prompt customer service is First Priority!
 Giving clients the best solution and value for their building and facility projects.
 Doing the Right Thing because it is the Right Thing to Do!
These beliefs have allowed me to work on over (250) projects for one client.
