Glendal Jones’s Summary

My background is as a broadcast journalist where I worked in several television markets including Columbus, Ohio and Indianapolis, Indiana as an Emmy-nominated Reporter, Anchor and Public Affairs Host.

Currently, I work as the Director of Communications/Intergovernmental Relations for Congressman Andre Carson.(7th District-IN) I handle public/media relations, spokesperson, press secretary, crisis communications point person, speech writer and community liaison duties. I am also Congressman Carson's liaison to the City of Indianapolis and State of Indiana.

I most recently served as a public relations representative for American Income Life Insurance, a national and only 100%union supplemental insurance company.

I also greatly enjoy writing and working with team members for a common goal. Also important is giving back to my community where I am involved on several boards, past and present as well as youth oriented committees.

Additionally, I worked as a company spokeperson through my own company, Glendal Jones Communications Group where I handled pr/media relations, marketing, producing and served as a television journalist. I also instructed corporate clients with service excellence training for internal and external clientele. Through my company, I also served as Producer/Writer/Reporter for a segment of the CBS Television show, "The Doctors." My company also focuses on executive media coaching, service excellence training and crisis communications.

Glendal Jones’s Specialties:

Public/Media Relations, crisis communications, Spokesperson, speech writer, writer, service excellence trainer/facilitator, television journalist, executive media coaching and community liaison.
