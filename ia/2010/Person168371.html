Ralph Mortensen’s Summary

My clients "put psychology to work." I have more than 30 years of broad experience in consulting, human resources and teaching.

I am board-certified in Organizational and Business Consulting Psychology by the American Board of Professional Psychology.

Ralph Mortensen’s Specialties:

Individual assessment for selection, promotion and development, executive coaching, leadership training, talent and organizational development program design.

80+ clients: financial services, retail, energy, health care, high-tech, automotive, chemical, utility, natural resources, food and spirits, heating and air conditioning, office furniture, construction and non-profit sectors. U.S., Canadian, European, Australian and Caribbean projects.
