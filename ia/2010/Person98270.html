Jim Conning’s Summary

Technology executive and entrepreneur specializing in innovative new products and scalable high volume applications. Jim has managed cross-continent development teams, and has particular experience with Eastern Europe. Jim brings particular wisdom and experience focused on early deliverables, quick iterations, and balancing good scalable software development with speed to market and efficiencies. Jim has founded Piczo, a VC funded startup which received strong support from Sierra Ventures, US Venture Partners, and Catamount Ventures.

Jim is an accomplished software innovator who honed his skills within the context of large databases and high volume applications. He conceived of and developed tools that manipulate the large digital map databases of NavTeq and TeleAtlas. He participated in world class software development integration at Oracle, and contributed to the launch of the Oracle 8 RDBMS. His work at Charles Schwab culminated in the development of a division-wide integration toolkit that resulted in significantly improved developer productivity and QA capability.

Jim's most sigificant career milestone was the founding of Piczo, which started with a sketch on a napkin. Jim conceived of the product, wrote the original code, built a sweat equity startup team, and led the company to a hockey stick growth pattern and VC funding. Jim then served a variety of roles, including VP Engineering, VP of Products, CTO and board member. Jim's leadership continued through to the merger with Stardoll in 2009.

Web applications: high scalability, consumer applications, in-house tools, AJAX, Amazon Simple Storage

Technical operations: management, data center cost reductions
