Amanda Trost’s Summary

Well, a little about myself. My name is Amanda Trost and I have been an artist for some time now. But as I've gotten older I have found different ways to express myself and my art. I think it all really started right out of high school where I got a job as a studio photographer and within the first week I was promoted to a studio manager and continued to rock the house from that moment on! I ran the highest numbers not only for my studio, but for my entire district! I found something that truely made me happy and I was very good at it. Its all about what you put into it and I made sure that everyone that came into my studio were making a memory last a lifetime. I stayed there for about 4 years and was given various promotions from studio manager to district trainer, and then towards the end I had 2 different districts fighting over me. At that point I realized that I was the youngest and most successful photographer to enter that company.

From that moment I quit the studio and went to college at Fullsail University. I wanted to take my design and all the fun I've had and see what I could do with it. While at Fullsail I was taught so many different tools and got to pick which ones I was good at! I graduated in May 2008 at the top of my class, with a Bachelor of Science in Digital Arts and Design Degree.

From Fullsail, I have stayed busy working various freelance jobs as well as my fulltime jobs working with "As Seen on TV", "T-Mobile", "Dollar General", "Dell", "WalMart" and "Target" as well as still getting out there and being apart of art shows, photography competitions and exploring the wide world of art.
