Darren G. Austin’s Summary

Darren G. Austin is a wireless internet industry pioneer and passionate technology entrepreneur with more than 12 years leading product teams for major technology brands including AOL, Amazon.com and Motorola.

In 1999 he was a part of the successful exit of a venture-backed wireless business when the start-up he was with was acquired by Amazon.com. He subsequently went on to help start mobile initiatives at Amazon.com and RealNetworks before co-founding SeattleRentals.com an online real estate business serving the Pacific Northwest.

Darren is currently serving as the Director of Product Management for AOL's Mobile Messaging & Social Networks team. He is passionate about innovating the consumer experience and is active as an adviser in the Seattle-area start-up community.


Highlights
• 12+ years in product management, marketing and business development leading cross-functional teams focused on technology products.

• Participant in successful exit of wireless start-up business in 1999. Sold 8-person business to Amazon.com for $23 million and established Amazon.com’s mobile e-commerce initiative (Amazon.com Anywhere).

• Director-level experience marketing and partnering in leading-edge, wireless technologies for global internet and technology brands including AOL, Amazon.com, RealNetworks, Motorola.

• Experience managing mobile internet-based products and services with revenue streams in excess of $55 million per year and ~80% OIBDA.

• Member of AOL’s core mobile team responsible for strategy and negotiations with key industry partners: Apple (iPhone & iChat), Google (Android), Nokia (N-series devices), Palm.

• Considerable international business experience in Europe and Asia focused on mobile industry partnerships.

• Active in the Seattle-area start-up community as an adviser for a number of early-stage internet ventures.

Darren G. Austin’s Specialties:

Technology Executive, Mobile Industry Veteran, Product Management, Product Marketing, International Business, Business Development in Computer Hardware, Software, Internet and Wireless Services industries. Strategic business planning and public speaking experience.
