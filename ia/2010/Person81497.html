Amy Henderson (amy@hendersontraining.com)’s Summary

Every business has people challenges.

These challenges can get in the way of production, quality, service, profit and even job satisfaction. No matter your industry, from time to time you need someone who can help address these challenges (without breaking the bank) and keep them from derailing your goals.

That’s what we do. We’ve spent over 17 years helping our clients solve people problems: teach their managers to manage, hire the right people, motivate their staff, address performance problems, develop leadership and keep employees focused on business results.

Whether you’re in financial services, construction, manufacturing, service, entertainment, high tech – we’ll get to know your issues and create a cost-effective solution that’s not “canned” or one-size-fits-all. But - you also won't get saddled with "consulting fees" for reinventing the wheel. We draw from our existing resources for customization and we keep adding to those resources.

Maybe most importantly: your managers and employees will buy-in. They may initially be wary (let's face it, we've all been through excruciatingly boring programs that were a waste of time and money), but over and over again, people want to come back and keep learning. They enjoy it and experience great value. They actually use what they learn. So will you.

Specialties: Custom training & coaching tailored to client needs. Creating a comfortable, engaging atmosphere. Collaborating with clients.
