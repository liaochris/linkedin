Noel Gordon’s Summary

The Trusted Beauty Guide & The Trusted Fitness Guide is founded by experts who have been in the Health, Fitness and Beauty Industry back in the early 90's.

We offer a trendy, quick and easy way to find information. Finding the most innovative, new & revolutionary people, services and products available on the global market.

We pride ourselves on having many years of experience in dealing with real people. People who have come to us, who believe in our advice, information & guidance for their beauty, health and fitness needs.

We aim to revolutionize the industry by bringing you trendy internet viewing.

The internet seems to have lost sight of the people that matter. The Trusted Beauty Guide & The Trusted Fitness Guide is trying to bring back what matters, to the people that matter, in a way that matters. We're making the internet personal again.

This is why you see TRUSTED in our name, because we believe with all the information on the internet, you want to be able to find somewhere you can trust the information your given.

We can guarantee the advice, information and articles you come across on The Trusted Beauty Guide & The Trusted Fitness Guide are only from experts within the industry and based on facts and not made-up surveys. As a company we aim to test and review beauty, hair, health & fitness to the fullest and make a point of taking part or trying any exercise, service or product we recommend.

We know the industry inside out. Health, beauty and fitness is our passion.
We live it, we breath it and most of all we love it. It's what we know best.
