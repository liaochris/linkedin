Roy Vandoorn’s Summary

I am a very lucky person, I have a wonderful wife, I live on Maui and I am in a profession that I am passionate about, residential real estate. People tell me all the time that they dream of owning real estate on Maui. I am in the business of making dreams come true. I help people own their own Happy Maui Home. It does not matter if you are a full time resident, a part-time resident or an investor, I would love the challenge of making your Maui Real Estate dream of having your own Happy Maui Home come true.
If you are even just thinking about getting a property on Maui, give me a call or send me an e-mail so we can put a plan together to make this a reality. I have a great team of professionals that I work with who can help you throughout the entire process. You may have your own Happy Maui Home before you know it.
If you need to sell a Maui Property, I am ready to put my 15 years of marketing experience to work on getting your property sold. There is nothing as frustrating as having your property just sit on the market with no interest from buyers. I provide all my sellers with a complete marketing plan, following the classic “5 P approach for product marketing” as taught at the top business schools in the country. Working together, we will determine the best alternative for selling your property, even if that alternative is not to sell today.
My goal is to make all your real estate transactions smooth sailing. I hope you give me the opportunity to win your business and make your Happy Maui Home dream come true.
