Mark Aldiss’s Summary

Thanks to a disturbed and disfunctional childhood I have spent the majority of my life talking and being with people with problems of one kind or another - drink, drugs, emotional instabilities, interpersonal problems, problems handed down from one generation to another, etc., Some of these problems have been extremely violent others self harming. I have been an ill fitting member of society in the UK and this has helped with understanding differing viewpoints and possible answers. However it has been detrimental to my own health at times. I have grand mal epilepsy and had a heart attack two years ago. I am 'better' at the moment and have started to push forward projectbrainsaver goals as I can. projectbrainsaver is the culmination of over 20 years of personal research into problems.

The next goal is to trigger Worldwide use of an 'intelligent' voice controlled interface that allows anyone to achieve far more than is possible at the present.
