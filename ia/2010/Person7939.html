Allen Cox’s Summary

I am an independent commercial copywriter and editor, magazine writer, published author, writing coach and communications consultant whose clients span multiple industries. I have a career background in business management, marketing and CRM.

In commercial copywriting, I am a generalist with a portfolio ranging from creative promotional and web copy to marketing plans.

As a magazine writer, my articles about travel, food, wine, the arts, the outdoors and profiles appear in several publications, including AAA Journey, WellBella, Northwest Travel, South Sound and many others.

I serve on the Board of Directors for International Food, Wine and Travel Writers Assoc and chair their Excellence Awards Committee for travel/food/wine journalism, destination marketing, tourism, hospitality, and beverage and culinary achievement. I also co-chair the Pacific Northwest Travel Writers Conference and am a member of Society of Professional Journalists.

My two books, Best Easy Day Hikes - Seattle and Best Easy Day Hikes - Tacoma, published by Falcon Guides, are currently available.

Please visit my website for published clips and more information.
