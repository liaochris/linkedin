Lyria Howland’s Summary

Over twenty years of experience in diversity communications and public relations. Howland PR develops, implements and measures national strategies to help clients gain access, awareness and credibility with diversity audiences.

We specialize in strategic sponsorships with national organizations, negotiating benefits to allign with the business case, designing creative solutions, managing activation of benefits and measuring the results.

Howland PR's diversity media relations programs have raised awareness for our clients with all diversity audiences and in all diversity categories (talent, suppliers, thought leaders, consumers and media).

Lyria Howland’s Specialties:

Diversity communications strategist in all divesity categories: talent/recruitment/retention, suppliers, consumers, media and thought leaders. Howland PR's proprietary data base system, VALUE MATRIXtm measures diversity communications processes and outcomes.
