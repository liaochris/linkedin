Dana Todd’s Summary

Newsforce helps news publishers triple the value of their banner ad inventory. Our turnkey platform helps them convert unsold banner space into high quality sponsored content or advertorial units. We call it "storytelling media." 500+ top news sites are Newsforce-enabled. The platform is white-labeled for publishers to sell directly to their local or national advertisers who want to promote sponsored news alongside regular editorial.

About Me:
Dana Todd is considered one of the pioneers of the search marketing industry, but her expertise includes all forms of online marketing and interactive visibility.

Prior to Newsforce, Dana Todd led interactive agency SiteLab for 12 years, developing a deep expertise in search engine marketing and highly effective online media. Her passion for helping clients understand and dominate the search space while still upholding core branding principles evolved SiteLab’s best practices in information architecture and online marketing.

In 2004, Ms. Todd co-founded SEMPO, now the largest independent trade organization serving the global search marketing community in 40 countries. She served on the Board for 6 years in various positions, including Chair. Separately, she serves as an adviser to industry organizations ad:tech and the Yellow Pages Association Local Search Panel, and companies such as Clickable.

Press (Wall Street Journal, Business Week, The Street.com), industry analysts and technology leaders frequently tap Ms. Todd for her knowledge of the search engine marketplace, particularly search engine advertising programs. Ms. Todd regularly appears as a top-ranked speaker at national conferences such as Search Engine Strategies, Ad:Tech, BIA/Kelsey Group and DMA.

Ms. Todd was a newspaper journalist and editor before switching to advertising in 1991. She became physically addicted to the Internet in 1995. Her hair color is not even close to natural.
