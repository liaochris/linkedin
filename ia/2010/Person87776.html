James Barisic’s Summary

I practice Corporate Commercial and Charity Law and my specialisms are IT and Intellectual Property. My IP work involves Trade Mark, Copyright and Registered Design matters while on the technology side I advise on a wide range of matters including e-commerce issues, terms and conditions for websites, privacy and data protection.

I privately advise charities, businesses and political campaigns on public relations and social media strategies, providing training and other support. My experience of PR (both traditional and new media) and work within Government has given me a unique approach to using group dynamics to promote causes and disseminate information.

In addition, I give talks and present seminars on social media and other technology subjects.

I am a professional Member of the British Computer Society, Plymouth Media Partnership and the Charity Law Association.
