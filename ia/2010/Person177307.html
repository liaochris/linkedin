Doug Stryker’s Summary

Currently available for contract or permanent employment. Senior-level advertising professional with 20+ years' experience in large and small agencies, building and leading creative teams while driving all phases of print, broadcast, and online projects for regional, national and global brands. Excellent time, budget and resource management abilities.

I'm a new media aficionado; highly responsive to culture and always seeking new venues for creative exploration, casting a wider net to deliver big ideas. I enjoy inspiring and creating authentic content which is media-agnostic and evolves brands regardless of channel. My fundamental aim is to convert consumers into brand evangelists.
