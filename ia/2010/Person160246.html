Pattie Simone’s Summary

Pattie Simone is a Speaker, Writer & Business Success Activist with oodles of energy & expertise. As a motivational speaker & guerrilla marketer, Pattie brings fresh perspectives, humor and smart solutions to every project and venue. Speaking credentials include motivational women's programs to general audience skill-based tracks on leadership, client-focused marketing, web marketing and social media. She is an Ask Entrepreneur Expert and has a regular Sales & Marketing Column on WomenEntrepreneur.com.
