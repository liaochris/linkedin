Jim Tate’s Summary

Jim Tate brings a unique combination of skills to the eHealth field. His direct experience encompasses both clinical and technological environments. As a clinician he was the Director of the Cardio-Pulmonary lab at a major acute care hospital and the Clinical Director of one of the largest private specialty practices in North Carolina. He is an experienced project manager in the implementation of both EHR and Practice Management systems and has led numerous implementations in the United States, as well as China.

Jim founded and serves as President of EMR Advocate, LLC. which provides extensive consulting services to EMR vendors, developers and other stakeholders in the Health Information Technology industry. EMR Advocate has actively supported more than 25 Ambulatory and Inpatient EHR vendors in their CCHIT Certification projects since 2006. Their documentation specialists have extensive experience in creating, editing, copy writing and proofreading CCHIT Self Attestation documents, User and System Manuals.
