Paul J. Beets’s Summary

10 years of experience in the Automotive Infotainment Electronics industry.

Experience developing PC-based applications using Visual Studio including Visual Basic, C++, C#, and .NET.

The author of the original PC-based SAINT Bus Engine and Monitor tools developed internally by Delphi Automotive Systems in Kokomo, Indiana but sold externally to well over 100 other automotive and aerospace companies.

Experience with software validation and PC-based vehicle communication bus analyzers such as SAINT, CANoe, CarDAQ, VBIT, and many other J2534 protocol supporting tools.

Experience using Agile Development methodologies to manage large and complex embedded software engineering projects with many different targets and operating systems.
