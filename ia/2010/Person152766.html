Neetika Maheshwari Kasat’s Summary

Accelero proudly speializes into start ups accounting, finance, management strategic decision making and taxation areas and we will be extremely delighted to be a partner in your exciting venture. We work hand in hand with start ups and stay firmly during each stage of company life cycle.

Our services range from basic bookeeping to complex decision making such as international transfer pricing, revenue recognition, IFRS compliances etc.

We offer three different levels of packaged services for start ups - Early stage, Expansion stage and Exit stage. For details, visit
www.accelero-corp.com.

We have valued customers from various industries such as software, semiconductor, bio tech, finance and other areas with whom we have been partnering successfuly. We have helped our customers through angel, VC financing and also helped them getting acquired by Tier 1 Silicon Valley giants.

In nutshell, Accelero is not just the accountant but a partner for start ups. We are founded and run by CPAs and MBAs who are thriving to provide world class services. We go a step ahead to take care of your offshore or nearshore units' accounting and tax compliances.

We also provide traditional CPA services such as periodic tax filings, compilations, reviews, audits etc
