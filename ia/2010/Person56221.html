Diane Propsner’s Summary

Since beginning her recruiting career in 1986 Ms. Propsner has functioned as a headhunter, corporate recruiter, recruiting consultant and overseer of the research and information service function of a retained search firm. Known for her recruiting passion and strategic vision, she has excelled as an invited speaker and author. Ms. Propsner’s clients rely on her expertise for acquiring top-talent.

Track record: 100% fill ratio on retained searches within the pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical industries!

Trained as a biologist at Wilson Collage (B.A.), Diane began her career in sales and represented various scientific and medical-based product lines before entering into the world of recruiting. Diane’s interest in the sciences played a major factor in her recruiting career since most of her experience is within the life-science and high-tech arenas.


Pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, CRO's, and life sciences.
