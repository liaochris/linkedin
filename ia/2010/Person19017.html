Angela Williams, Esq., MBA’s Summary

Angela Williams, Esq. offers a diverse background in both education and work experience. Her career aspirations are to provide strategic purchasing and business development decisions to domestic and international businesses. Her previous professional experience involves work across various disciplines; primarily in the private sector both domestically and abroad. A graduate of Spelman College and the University of Tennessee College of Law, Angela realized early her desire to marry her two greatest passions: business strategy and international relations. "As a Sociology and Anthropology major, I found an appreciation for diversity and social normes. I grew passionate about international travels as I learned the importance of understanding people and cultures, and how various cultures affected Western civilization. Living abroad in Tokyo and Milan afforded me the great opportunity to intern with international lawyers. It was abroad that I gained my first experience in international contract negotiation and drafting. The great ability to balance individual social normes, with business needs, and legal requirements fascinated me and drove me to pursue a formal legal education." Angela worked as an in-house attorney where she drafted international contracts and supported business managers with international suppliers and vendors. Of particular interest to her were the regulations of international trade. Angela's decision to obtain her MBA stemmed from a desire to learn more about the what drove business decisions with respect to global expansion. Angela's international experience, coupled with her corporate legal experience and the solid foundation she will obtain as an MBA will prepare her for a successful career managing international trade contracts, contract negotiation, and purchasing needs for multinational corporations. Angela is a recipient of the HUCESCM Scholarship Award and is currently interning at Dell, Inc. as a World Wide Procurement analyst.
