Bill James’s Summary

Bill James is the CEO of 'We Engage' LLC. 'We Engage' is an outsourced provider of effective social media engagement. The company conducts the work of online engagment subject to our client's stated strategy and tactics. Bill is dedicated to creating a culture of brand advocacy and trust in online communities that results in increased sales of product and service for his clients.

As an international management consultant with over 20 years experience as Analyst, Program Director, Business Development Director and CEO, Bill is dedicated to driving engagement plans that result in worthwhile financial ROI.

He has worked internationally for consulting firms like Alexander Proudfoot and the Hudson Highland Group in the Asia Pacific area and founded and built consulting businesses in Australia, Thailand and the United States. His clients include organizations like Deutsche Bank, General Motors, Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, Alcoa and Hitachi America.

Bill James’s Specialties:

Specialties:
cost effective programs for outsourcing client social media engagement, managing the work of social media engagment for marketing and social media directors, aligning brand strategy with social media tactics and engagement plans, planning and reporting ROI for engagement, defining procedure, protocol and standards for real time engagement, supervising work in progress for engagement and installing management, training and reporting systems.
