Richard Palarea’s Summary

PA & Associates, ranked in the Top 50 Fastest Growing Private Companies and in The Largest Turnaround & Management Consulting Companies lists by the Baltimore Business Journal, provides best practice logistics spend management strategies and tools for mid and large market organizations. The Company is a member of the Spend Management Center of Excellence and it's methods are certified by the same. The organization has also been featured by Microsoft in "Success Stories", Startup Nation as a "Top Financial Performer" and appeared on USAir's SkyRadio on the in-flight program "Industry Innovators -- Best of Breed".

Through stringent analysis of present shipping patterns, a proven request for proposal (R.F.P.) framework, traditional consulting practices, third-party negotiating and ongoing data analysis, clients realize an average reduction in shipping expenses of 42% through the initial project. Additional savings and optimization are realized through use of analytic tools delivered over the Web.

Compensation of professional fees is based on a performance-contingent model; fees are only due if savings are realized and fees are paid out of the savings obtained. The gain-share model provides a means for project payment without new budgeting or expense allocation.

Due to the detailed and professional nature of each project, the organization accepts a limited number of new clients each year. New business is generated only through personal introductions to Presidents, CFOs or senior executives with a role in finance.

Richard Palarea’s Experience

  • Founder and Founding Member

    The Center for Spend Management Excellence

    (Think Tanks industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    The lifeblood of business enterprise is a series of daily financial and administrative processes that enable organizations to monitor and control expenses, track payments and invoices, purchase goods and services, and organize and manage workflow.

    The Center for Spend Management Excellence (CSME) promotes best practice development for expense reduction and spend management among its members and provides completely outsourced projects for its clients on a global basis.

    CSME possesses strategic expertise and proven spend management experience in a number of direct and indirect spend areas including logistics (freight and small package), telecommunications, waste management, energy procurement, sales tax audit, property tax audit, engineering-based cost segregation analysis, printing and print management, media buy, 401(k) cost management, and a number of other disciplines.

    Through its screening and certification process, CSME has defined a minimum level spend management approach and delivery of services that allows a CFO or CPO easy access to pre-qualified experts in a variety of spend disciplines. The organization also furthers the practice of spend management through its seminars, educational opportunities, networking functions and publications.

    CSME is the CFO's best friend.
