J. Michael Peace’s Summary

J. Michael Peace has a strong SAP for Utilities (CCS / IS-U) background and has been working with SAP IS-U and the Utilities Industry for over 8 years in many aspects, including taking on lead consultant roles in IS-U Billing, Invoicing & Rate Functional Areas during entire project lifecycles, with experience and extensive knowledge in the Customer Services, Device Management and FICA areas as well. Michael has been an SAP CCS/IS-U Lead for the Billing, Invoicing, Rates, and Bill Print areas during project prep, project team training, blueprint, realization/configuration, testing, training, go-live, and post go-live support phases of CCS/IS-U Implementation projects, and has also participated in numerous pre-sales meetings with multiple utility clients, SAP demo sessions to potential SAP customers, and Assessment Projects with SAP customers who will soon be kicking off full scale CCS/IS-U implementations.

Michael is currently focusing on lead functional consulting roles in SAP IS-U/CCS Implementations, managing products & new requirements in post go-live IS-U environments (including Deregulated markets), SAP IS-U post go-live Support, SAP Business & IT Process Improvement Initiatives, and SAP Quality Assurance.

Michael's SAP Utilities project experience includes Entergy Corporation, Reliant Energy / NRG Energy, Pinellas County Utilities, Aquarion Water Company, Enbridge Gas Distribution, and New York Power Authority, among other clients as well. His track record proves that he can provide creative solutions to complex business requirements and he ensures that solid, reliable processes & systems are in place for clients that have chosen SAP as their Customer Service & Billing solution.
