Robert W. Kendrick’s Summary

Robert W. Kendrick has been an officer in various public and private companies, and the founder and principal of a private investment banking firm specializing in reorganization, turnarounds, and recapitalization. Mr. Kendrick’s strategy for success is simple: convey the firm’s vision to demonstrate benefits to all parties, lead the team through effective and efficient business processes, and bring value to the community.

Robert W. Kendrick’s Experience

  • Partner / Co-Founder

    NorthWest Stewards LLC.

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    January 2007 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    NorthWest Stewards specializes in the design, implementation and completion of challenging real estate projects that require a high sensitivity to environmental, social and cultural values associated with the property.

    Northwest Stewards takes a value driven approach in the design of properties, identifying all value components to the property - natural, environmental, social, cultural and economic - to determine the best approach for development that results in the protection of the values of the property which must be preserved.

    Northwest Stewards is committed to the principle that all projects must be self-sustaining, that no debt - environmental, social, cultural or economic - be left behind for our children and future generations to bear...
