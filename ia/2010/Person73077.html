Gary Ledford’s Summary

Gary L. Ledford is General Manager of Total Insight, an organization that was founded in 1989 as Petra International and merged with Transportation Insight in 2009. Total Insight offers business solutions in three areas . . . . BQ = Business Quotient improvement, CQ = Community Quotient improvement and EQ = Execution Quotient Improvement using their trademark approach called the Wholistic Path™. He has also served as a Senior Consultant with Franklin Covey, were he facilitated and delivered the 4 Disciplines of Execution, 7 Habits of Highly Effective People/Managers and the 4 Roles of Leadership. His other areas of practice include Lean Enterprise, Six Sigma, and Gainsharing compensation systems focused at creating a multi-functional high performance work place.


Gary has held various Senior Management positions such as Chief Financial Officer and Vice President of Operations. He has helped many organizations implement “World Class” concepts that he learned while studying the Toyota Production System at Toyota City in Japan.

He holds a Black Belt in Six Sigma and is considered a Lean Enterprise expert by the Lean Institute. Additionally, he is certified and is licensed to administer the Myers Briggs Type Indicator. He is a member and past officer of the Institute of Industrial Engineers and has served on the Executive Board on the Y.M.C.A. and the Lean Institute.

He has run 27 Marathons with a personal best of 2:21:05 at Boston in 1983 and is currently making progress toward a personal goal of 50 marathons in 50 states by age 60. Gary resides just outside of Charlotte near Hickory, North Carolina with his wife, Kate and their 3 children.

Johann Goethe

    HICKORY, NC, February 23, 2009 – Total Insight, LLC, an affiliate of Transportation Insight, LLC expands its business services by acquiring Petra International, a professional solutions provider.

    In a response to the changing market, Paul Thompson, CEO of Total Insight, partnered with Gary Ledford, President of Petra International, to acquire the associated proprietary consulting services and expertise of Petra International. The acquisition resulted in the formation of Total Insight’s Professional Solutions Division headed by Gary Ledford as Vice President.

    Petra International, founded in 1989, has helped over 300 Executive Leadership Teams across numerous industries accomplish game-changing goals for excellence, productivity, and financial performance. The acquisition by Total Insight pulls together Petra International’s resources with top management employees and experienced professionals from Total Insight and its affiliate Transportation Insight.

    “In today’s evolving business environment, only the companies with exceptional human capital which produce superior products and services, with high client satisfaction levels will thrive,” stated Paul Thompson, CEO. “We are excited to add a Professional Services Division to meet client needs for organizational development, executive coaching, lean consulting, and whole company strategy development and execution. With the addition of these services to our existing supply chain management, technology, business intelligence, and business process optimization offerings, we are able to orchestrate many of the most ambitious and game changing initiatives our clients need in our increasingly challenging marketplace.”

    With almost 20 years in organizational consulting and management solutions, Petra (now Total Insight) complements and expands Total Insight’s services with comprehensive business solutions.
