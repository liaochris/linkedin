Laurian Gridinoc’s Summary

I've been developing for web since 1995 for fun, and since 1998 for more fun (i.e. profit). In 1999, tired to be told to do things wrongly, I co-founded Grapefruit, which rapidly became one of Romania's leading branding and interaction design agencies . There, I was involved in exciting projects for big names, like Vodafone, HP, Pfizer, Eli Lilly, etc.

In 2006, I left Grapefruit due to the growing gap between the market and the emerging technologies (namely semantic web ones).

Now, I'm waiting for the market to catch up while investigating how semantic web technologies can change everything (products, experiences, services, and ultimately us).

What next? I would tell you in person.

Laurian Gridinoc’s Specialties:

Semantic Web, "Web 3.0," linked data, open data, and any related issues (identification and reference on the web, etc.)
