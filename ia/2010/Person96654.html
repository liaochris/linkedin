Jesse America’s Summary

Executive Producer and Game Director with 12 years of experience in the games industry and broad understanding of all aspects of game development, direction and project management. Hands-on experience leading a development studio; experience producing console (PS2, Wii, iPhone and DS) and pc titles on the publisher’s side and the developer’s side; good knowledge of QA procedures; a talent for game design and creative direction, and the planning and budgeting of projects. Specialized in producing high-quality mainstream entertainment for a broad audience. Exceptionally strong sense of conceptualization aesthetics, game branding, maintenance of a game’s design vision and the creation of intellectual property; great attention to detail. Good writing skills.
