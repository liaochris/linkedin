Björn Wellenius’s Summary

Consultant on telecommunications policy, regulation, and economics in emerging markets. Strong analytical and communication skills as well as proven ability to conduct a high-level dialog with governments and international financial institutions. Worked in over 40 countries in Latin America, Africa, Asia, and Europe. Published extensively, including five books on telecommunications and economic development. Fluent in English and Spanish, working knowledge of French.

Björn Wellenius’s Specialties:

Design and implementation of telecommunications sector reform, competition and privatization strategies, regulatory policy and capacity building, and universal service and rural infrastructure financing. Current interests include strategic issues of information infrastructure development, the political economy of sectoral change, institutional capacity building, and spectrum management policy.
