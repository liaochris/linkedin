wesley chun’s Summary

Technical professional with over two decades of programming, writing, and teaching experience available for short-term and part-time consulting projects to help you design the right solution! We can tackle your Python project regardless of where it stands in the timeline, whether you need actual software engineering, design and architecture, or just to take a look at your existing Python code for meeting style guidelines, correctness, optimization, refactoring, etc. Need help defining structure and process (documentation [PRD/MRD, SRS/FS, DS/SDS], software lifecycle, and recommended tools [source control, issue tracking]) between your Engineering team and other organizations? We can help out with that too.

You will also benefit from our excellent communication and corporate training skills. Unlike other technical trainers and consultants, we don't just "teach" clients the Python syntax (which you don't really need a book nor an instructor, right?). Instead, we can relate real-world work experiences in the past decade using Python as a full-time software engineer -- this is something no other Python trainer can offer. In other words, not only can we "talk the talk," but we can also "walk the walk."

Always willing to learn new things, thus ready to use our technical expertise and experience to aid your sales or systems engineering, professional services, business development, or product marketing and research teams.
