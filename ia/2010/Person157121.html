Oleg Ivanov’s Summary

I have intensive experience in building web applications based on the open source technologies, mainly (but not exclusive) with MySQL, Ruby/Rails and PHP, working with agile small teams to achieve results quickly and efficiently. Started the carreer working for educational institutions and building the IT infrastructure to support educational processes, later moved on to work on the intranet and B2B services. As a first full-scale project succesfully developed and launched private online market and prices tracking tool. Then moved onto mobile-oriented technologies and spent three years developing (mostly) SMS-based informational and educational services, including currently available aerostatus.com service.
Successfully worked as a freelancer, building different web applications and services from ebooks library backend to customized blogs and torrent trackers.
As a hobby took part in building and shaping the biggest Russian anime fandub community at AnimeReactor.ru, acting as technology and community leader.
