Hemant Kumar’s Summary

My research and work interest encompasses areas of Wireless Communications, Networking and Embedded Software Development.

Currently I am working as Graduate Research Assistant in Network Centric Cognition and Information Fusion Center at Penn State.

This summer'09, I worked as an Engineering Intern in Bay Area R&D group, Qualcomm, Santa Clara on enhancing the Transport layer features including Radio link layer in CDMA EVDO stack to improve browser performance in wireless environment.

Last summer'08 I worked in Qualcomm as an Engineering Intern in Corporate R&D software division at San Diego facility. I was assigned three major objectives viz. develop a Wireshark based Qualcomm proprietary dissector tool, support the Base Transceiver System Software release for CDMA 1x EVDO system and prepare a User Guide for the tool.Working in Wireless Company has exposed me to lot of Industry 3G and upcoming 4G wireless technologies like LTE and given me a practical insight into the working of Wireless Systems.

As a Graduate Research Assistant in School of Information Science and Technology at Penn State, I have worked on couple of project in areas of Wireless Sensor Networks, Mobile Networking. One of my projects deals with aspects of fair scheduling in Non Cooperative Wireless Networks. I have developed a mathematical proof supporting my game theoretic Incentive based mechanism to encourage non cooperative users to participate in the Network so as to increase overall throughput of the Network.

Before starting with my MS, I worked for two years as Embedded Software Engineer in HCL Technologies Noida in Wireless and Embedded Systems Group. There I worked majorly in Multimedia based devices and was exposed to audio and video codec standards like AAC, H264, MPEG2 etc.

In course of pursuing bachelors I did summer internships at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, Bangalore in Aircraft Engine Test Bed and Reseach Center.
