Dave Weaver’s Summary

An Executive with over 35 years of International Manufacturing and Import Experience in the design and manufacturing of high volume consumer electronics including digital and web cameras, thermal printers, kid friendly cameras products, and internet radios. Possess extensive business knowledge in Hong Kong, China, Taiwan, Japan, Korea, Malaysia and Singapore having managed manufacturing organizations from 10 to 3000 people. Adept at restructuring operations, including mergers and acquisitions to create efficient supply chains to create significant increases in throughput, improved inventory turns, decreased product cost, and reduced inventories in factories. Led multiple ERP systems implementations, including SAP, re-engineering business processes, streamlining operations, and reducing inventories by $200M while working as a Supply Chain Manager at Kodak. Directed hardware and software product development efforts leveraging process management and advanced engineering tools to improve development lead times by 4 to 6 months. Throughout career recognized as a hands-on manager that empowers teams; aligns strategy, actions, and measures; flawlessly executes fundamentals; and communicates with the workforce to consistently hit quality, cost, delivery, and service targets.
