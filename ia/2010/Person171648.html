Richard Roberts’s Summary

Founder and owner of three companies and assisted several others since 1969. Currently own two companies that service residential and commercial real estate industry proving property condition assessements for due diligence applying ASTM E-2018 standards, inspections and consulting providing property condition assessments and inspections of commercial, industrial and multi-unit office/apartment buildings, retail, lodging and long-term care facilites. Service area includes Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Michigan, Ohio & Wisconsin. We are in the process of expanding commercial real estate services to include environmental assessments and other services. Expansion of service area underway to include mort of the continental United States by the end 2011.
