Sharon Ann Holm’s Summary

5sensespr is a creative consultancy that provides realistic and sound public relations and marketing (English and Danish) advice to clients looking to strongly market their businesses/products/services to an international market. We offer consultancy advice without the exorbitant hourly rates that come with working with a big agency.

5sensespr works with their clients to develop, position and market their company, product/services that will increase sales and enhance brand equity.

We handle every communications need, from social media to mainstream press, developing local, national and overseas public relations campaigns to audience-specific outreach efforts, connecting you with key influencers and launching or repositioning your products and services.
