Nikki Groom’s Summary

After a five-year stint as Marketing Manager for igus' UK and Eire subsidiary, Nikki was transferred to the North American subsidiary in January 2008 to help handle the company's marketing communications there. She is currently based in Rhode Island, USA.

Nikki has a proven track record of success in implementing integrated marketing strategies, including website development and content management, SEO (Search Engine Optimization), social media communications, Pay-Per-Click advertising, e-mail marketing, online/offline advertising, PR, branding, and tradeshows.

Nikki serves on the planning committee for the ISA Marketing & Sales Summit (social media committee) and volunteers for GeekGirlCamp, a non-profit organization based in Cape Cod, which aims to educate and empower females of all ages, skill levels and income levels on computer technology.

Nikki Groom’s Specialties:

Nikki enjoys new challenges and has developed a passion for inbound marketing techniques and social communications – specifically, how they relate to the industrial community.
