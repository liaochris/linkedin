Terri Swallow’s Summary

I'm a LAMP developer specializing in PHP and MySQL web applications. I'm also particularly interested in relational database design, and applying intelligent use of AJAX to dynamic websites. I am a fan of the design concepts behind designing for mobile first especially since it is one of the fastest growing segments on the web. I enjoy poking through data and have many a time built personal systems to help myself analyze data when websites lack good reporting or a good UI. All-in-all I'm a nerd, and I'm okay with that.
