Diane Moura (nee Cloutier)’s Summary

Managing Director responsible for Unisys Global Outsourcing Southern US region, including direct leadership of a 200+ person top tier financial services account. She joined Unisys in 2002 to manage the Latin America practice for CRM and went on to lead development of the Strategy and Process layers of the 3D Blueprinting methodology before joining the Global Outsourcing division.

During her 22 year career in IT management, she has performed a wide variety of roles, from practice leader to strategy consultant specialized in bridging business and IT to deliver competitive advantage. She has worked with finance, telecom and government clients in Canada, Latin America, Australia and Europe, speaks three languages and excels at creating large, high performing teams.

Immediately prior to joining Unisys, she was a Senior Principal with AMS (now CGI) where she led CRM strategy development as well as credit risk management and collections programs leveraging business intelligence and applications. She facilitated over 100 workshops and has driven customer service transformation through workflow, decision engines and call center optimization as well as organizational change management. Moura also held the positions of Collections Practice Manager for the Americas and Latin America CRM Practice Manager.

Prior to AMS she spent 8 years with Digital Equipment where she held a range of staff and line positions including Network Services, Outsourcing, Marketing, Credit and Collections. She led Digital’s entrance into the Canadian Disaster Recovery business and spun off a start-up focused on consulting to the Government of Canada. The venture achieved profitability in 8 months, one year ahead of plan.

Moura earned a Bachelor of Commerce from Carleton University in Ottawa and an M.B.A. from Concordia University in Montreal. She lives in Miami with her husband and young daughter where she enjoys fitness, natural health practices and green living.
