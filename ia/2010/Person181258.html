Sam Lawrence’s Summary

Sam Lawrence is known for going big. He is an unconventional and experienced leader who knows how to execute big ideas to reality. A frequent industry speaker and popular blogger, Sam is well known as a firestarter and thought-leader. He draws from 20 years of technology experience having lead Fortune-level companies and start ups to success. Sam is CEO of GoBigAlways. Previously, he was CEO/Founder of Blackbox Republic. Prior he was CMO, Jive Software where he reshaped the company, got them on the map, and on track to go big. He has had experience at McCann Erickson where he was a senior vice president in charge of marketing efforts for Microsoft's global Microsoft Office business unit, including Microsoft SharePoint, Live Meeting and Live Communication Server. Prior, Sam has led Marketing for a roster of marquee companies including Dell, CNET and 3Com. He was named a INVESP top 100 Influential Marketer in 2008.
