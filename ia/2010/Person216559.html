Wright Thurston’s Summary

Over the course of his career, Utah-based businessman Wright Thurston has made a major mark in both the cell phone industry and home security industry through his dynamic leadership and keen understanding of sales. Currently, Wright Thurston leverages his significant talents toward the management of Elevate Communications, LLC, a telecommunications firm he founded in 2008 with several business partners. Originally appointed CEO of Elevate Communications, Wright Thurston now serves as the company’s Chairman of the Board. Prior to his current work with Elevate Communications, Mr. Wright Thurston served as the CEO of Firstline Security, a national private home security firm. Founded in 2002, Firstline Security quickly became one of the country’s most successful home security companies, garnering praise from the National Alarm Computer Center for being the “Fastest Growing Alarm Company in the Nation” in its first two years. Spanning 47 locations across the United States and Canada, Firstline Security joined the prestigious Inc. 500 list in 2006, landing on the 280th spot for 2007. Wright Thurston began his career in entrepreneurship as the manager of numerous successful cell phone retailers. Demonstrating impressive business savvy, Wright Thurston sold his retail outlets to Omniserve Wireless in 1999. In 2000, Wright Thurston founded Future Phones Inc. Utilizing the power of the Internet in tandem with a unique direct fulfillment shipping model, Wright Thurston revolutionized cell phone sales, quickly growing Future Phones into the largest non-retail direct seller of VoiceStream Wireless (now T-Mobile) phones in the nation. A dedicated member of his community, Mr. Wright Thurston frequently volunteers with his church and sponsors the Greater Good Foundation. In his limited spare time, Wright Thurston enjoys playing tennis, surfing, and spending time with his kids.
