Dan Perez’s Summary

After 10 years as a Sales Manager and Director of Sales in the Special Event/Catering industry in New York & South Florida, Dan Perez pursued his passion in video production and editing and started www.DansVideoCreations.com in the Spring of 2006. Since then, Dan has produced videos for Miami Children's Hospital, Coral Gables Chamber of Commerce, National Kidney Foundation of Florida, Bruce Rossmeyer's Harley Davidson and Goldstein Schechter Koch (to name a few).

In 2007, Dan teamed up with Taurus Production Group to help produce the dvd 'Live in Concert "by me" Ethan Bortnick' featuring then 7-year-old piano prodigy Ethan Bortnick. Dan served as Associate Director, producing and editing all non-concert footage.

In early 2008, Dan completed his first documentary film, "The Motivator: The Business of Selling Hope" which featured International Motivational Speaker/Sales Trainer, Victor Antonio Gonzalez. The film went on to win the 'Director's Choice' Award at the 2008 Rincon International Film festival. . .'Dan Perez Films' was born!

Dan completed his second film, "P.A.T.H." (which stands for Preserving, Archiving and Teaching Hip-Hop History) in 2009. The film documents the P.A.T.H. Hip-Hop Academy, the first ever summer camp of it's kind in Miami. The film premiered at the 2009 Chicago International Hip Hop Film Festival and won the "Best Florida Film" award at the 2010 Sunscreen Film Festival. The award, presented to Dan by John Travolta himself, was the top prize of the festival.

Dan Perez is also active in the wedding/social event industry where he has produced dozens of distinctive videos for his clients and has created a unique wedding video concept that he calls the "Wedumentary". Visit www.DansVideoCreations.com to learn more!
