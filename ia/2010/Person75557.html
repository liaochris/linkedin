Gina Snyder’s Summary

I work with private companies, small businesses, agencies and small-cap public companies in the Central United States. I counsel prospects on domestic, international and multimedia communication options, help plan strategic public/investor relations and marketing efforts, share industry knowledge and win new business. I love my company and the culture -- and it's great to see my prospects' projects come to life with the help of our services.

I come from the land of news and entertainment -- writing, shooting, editing, producing and hosting. I'm also a seasoned performer with a strong background in musical theater, comedy, vocal performance and various forms of dance.

When I'm not at the wire, you can catch me hosting an Italian entertainment show on Dolce Channel or singing in the pop duo, Cool it with the Boom Booms.
