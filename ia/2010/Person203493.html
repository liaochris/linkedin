Tiffany A. Kahnen’s Summary

Tiffany Kahnen is a corporate & strategic transactional attorney. Most recently as Associate In-House Counsel for Bloosky Interactive, Ms. Kahnen was involved with mergers & acquisitions, deal financing, IP protection, risk management, corporate governance, contract management and litigation. Ms. Kahnen helped oversee and implement a company-wide contract audit and a new contract management process.

At Newport Beach based business law firm LaFlam Sullivan, Ms. Kahnen was involved in M&A Services, IP Protection & Maximization, Private Placements, Strategic Transactions, Co-branding efforts & Litigation, as well as sports and entertainment law. Formerly an associate with Pierce & Weiss in Los Angeles, she focused her practice on business & IP matters, as well as, media, commercial and general civil litigation. Ms. Kahnen has experience litigating disputes involving the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), Lanham Act, The Copyright Act of 1976, Infringement of intellectual property within the internet medium & misappropriation. She also has experience with ADR and international service of process under the Hague Convention.

Ms. Kahnen received her Juris Doctorate from Whittier Law School, where she completed a specialized curriculum earning a certificate in Intellectual Property. Within that curriculum, she earned CALI(AmJur) honors in the study of Internet Law and was a Sr. member of the Whittier Law Journal, an honors board.

Ms. Kahnen completed her undergraduate degree in Broadcast Journalism at the University of Southern California (USC). While at USC, she completed a year-long investigative journalism piece on counterfeit trademarks in the garment industry of LA. Her piece led to an extensive investigation where she was in close contact with the FBI, Federal Trade Commission, private investigators and corporations such as Nike and Gucci.

At USC, she was a member of the 2004 NCAA Women’s Waterpolo Division I National Championship team.
