Ginger Grant, PhD’s Summary

My focus is digital storytelling - the intersection point between the use of technology and the strategic use of story for branding or strategic initiatives. My passion is business by design - the creation, design, development and implementation of innovative people programs that transform corporate culture. Working with high-performance teams, I use the Heroic Journey model as a foundational tool for digital storytelling, including internal and external branding initiatives.

Areas of expertise and applied research include:

• strategic intelligence and visual analytics
• creativity and innovation
• business by design
• cultural due diligence
• psychographics and demographics (predictive modelling)
• the use of archetypes and memes in developing corporate culture
• building high-performance cultures through strategic design
• organizational culture assessments and change management
• employment branding initiatives

Ginger Grant, PhD’s Specialties:

Author: 2009-Finding Your Creative Core. 2005-Re-Visioning The Way We Work. Only Canadian in teacher/trainer group established by founder Michael Ray for the Stanford GSB "Creativity in Business" program. Further information can be found at http://www.mythsamongus.org
