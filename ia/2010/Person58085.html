Don Clemens, CWE/CSW/CSS’s Summary

Most of my career has been involved in sales, marketing and wine education.

I recently left Terlato Wines International (TWI), where I had been employed since July of 1996, during which period I held positions in many different areas, including marketing, sales and educational support.

My career in the wine business has included retail and restaurant management, wholesale distribution, regional sales and even winery sales management. My love of wine has led me through many challenging and rewarding opportunities, including a stint as sommelier at one of Chicago's leading restaurants (where my wine list won a Wine Spectator "Grand Award"). I have held the position of Regional Sales Manager for several companies - among them, Maisons Marques & Domaines, Charles Krug Winery of St. Helena and Vintage Wine Merchants of San Francisco. Prior to returning to Chicago 1990, I held the position of Vice-President of Sales & Marketing at Napa's William Hill Winery for four years.

A Certified Wine Educator and Certified Specialist in Spirits with the Society of Wine Educators, I am frequently called upon to talk about my favorite subject, WINE.

Known as a lover of fine wines, fine spirits and great food, I am always happy to have an opportunity to "get in front of the stove" and cook. I really enjoy Italian, Indian, “Country French” and Cajun/Creole food, and I'm learning more about Asian cuisines all the time.

My favorite wines are generally richly flavored reds, particularly from northern California, Burgundy, Bordeaux, the Rhône Valley, Australia, Italy and Spain - but I find that I have an appreciation of all things vinous, especially when Riesling is involved.

I live near Chicago in northwest suburban Elgin with my wife, Susan, along with our two dogs and two cats. My wife thinks that I have too many outside cooking toys; I think that there are never enough surfaces for grilling or smoking!
