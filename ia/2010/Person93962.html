JEFF ROSSET’s Summary

I am an individual who is very passionate about developing leaders and organizations. People always ask me why I decided to leave my corporate leadership role in order to start Compass C&D. I tell them the decision was due to 2 main reasons:

1) Through my experiences, I came to the conclusion that an unbelievable amount of organizations fell victim to 3 common challenges. They had:
● Far too few skilled and proactive leaders
● Disengaged, or often even worse - apathetic employees
● Teams and individuals focused on differing goals/objectives (lack of synergy)


2) Most Leadership and Talent Development programs are SET UP TO FAIL from the word "Go." The typical reasons are:
● Focus and emphasis on the wrong competencies
● No accountability to make changes and grow
● Goals and objectives for the initiative are not clear
● Lack of support and resources from top leadership
● No follow-up or support from program facilitator
● Attempt to pack years of development into 2-3 days
● Little transfer of knowledge to others back at work
● Lack of buy-in and commitment from the participants

Based on these 2 glaring issues, I formed Compass C&D so that our firm could help companies achieve High Performance when it comes to the areas of Employee Engagement, Effective/Proactive Leaders, and Organizational Synergy. We achieve these results by delivering leadership development initiatives that are strategically designed to help ensure long-term behavior changes, accountability and knowledge transfer.
