Chad Lefevre "THE Pathological Optimist"’s Summary

Chad Lefevre, THE Pathological Optimist

The Founder of Against the Grain and THE Pathological Optimist (WARNING: Don’t tell him it CAN’T be done…), Chad has 15 years of business experience in both the United States and Canada as a visionary, strategist, and viral connector / relationship builder.

As a business philosopher, Thought Leader and writer, he has been referred to by many as “the lightning storm in human form” and is known for his ability to “see” what needs to be done.

Chad is the owner/co-owner of several companies in various industries, including health care (World Dental Club), author/speaker/publishing (BE); fashion (A.L. Gems Global); real estate investment (Cross Ocean International Finance Corp.). Chad sits on the boards of V.I.P. Talent; Arts for Humanity Global; iDuplicate; SANG IV; Revolution Yes / Revolution 9; and a new independent film festival to be formally disclosed later in 2010.

His serial entrepreneurial exploits have made him an expert generalist with a mastery of the specifics and nuances comprising the areas of business development and strategic and operational planning. He has designed, implemented and project managed multiple client organizational and departmental strategies, operational models, financial models, and marketing operations contributing to hundreds of millions in revenues for his clients in the energy sector, the real estate sector, the health and wellness sector, the technology sector, and at various levels of government.

His clients have included Shell, Conoco Phillips, Bell, Investor’s Group, Politicians, several small-to-mid sized start-ups, and recreational real estate development firms. Today he has extended his domain expertise into the industries of Thought Leadership / Publishing, Film and Music.
