Deirdre ní Dhubhghaill’s Summary

2D Graphic Design is a graphic design service based in Wexford, Ireland. The purpose of graphic design is to create unique, visual solutions, suited to our customers requirements.

• My main areas of expertise are in general graphic design, Visual marketing development, Brochure, Annual report, Leaflets, Point of sale, Branding/ identities, Logos and images for the web. My design work can be seen on my website at 2dgraphicd.com.

•I have worked with SME to large business, organisations, individuals on a one to one bases or workinmg in a team.

• I also have a selection of graphic art suitable for the wall. My typographic expression of proverbs, wise sayings and seanfochail, phrases I have collected since childhood. Here @ designandart.net, there are two collections 'Seasons' and 'GoFerIt'
