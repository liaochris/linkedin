Tyrone Gorham’s Summary

Supplier Diversity executive with over 6 years of coaching minorities and women business enterprises. Supported team that established the first Supplier Diversity Initiative within Wachovia Corporate Real Estate Department in 2001.

Increased the minority and women spend by more than 300% over the last 7 years. Proven track record for advocating and support minority and women business development in Alabama, Georgia, Florida and Texas.

Built a strong strategic Second-Tier Program with successful M/WBE business partners that provided value-add to our global sourcing and procurement groups.

Developed company's brand recognition, outreach, awareness, education and training in both local, regional and national business communities and government agencies.
