Joseph Palumbo, Executive.Coach@sympatico.ca’s Summary

Joseph Palumbo has the rare knowledge of what organizations desire from their future leaders and what tomorrow’s leaders want from their organizations. With a keen eye for talent, he has successfully sourced successful leadership candidates for many of the world’s top organizations on a global basis.

As Executive Director of the Career Development Centre for a premier global business school, Joseph is a senior relationship executive.Joseph develops on a global basis, new relationships and cultivates existing relationships with diverse firms at the most senior strategic, operational and human resource areas.

A life-long learner,Joseph obtained a marketing degree from York University and his MBA from The Schulich School of Business. Joseph was just been selected as a Fellow of the Society of Management Accountants of Canada (FCMA). Recently completed certificates include: Forging The HR/CEO Alliance, Executive Sales Management, Succession Planning, Recruiting & Retention, Coaching & Career Counseling, Strategic Decision Making & HR. Joseph is certified in delivering career and skill assessments and Emotional Intelligence (EQi) tests and workshops. Joseph has lead leadership, recruitment, retention and career workshops for various clients on four continents to diverse audiences. Joseph is also an Executive Coach in his own practice.

Joseph is an active member of a number of organizations including: The International Coaching Federation (ICF), Human Resources Professional Association of Ontario (HRPAO), The Society for Human Resources Management (SHRM), Canadian Association of Professional Speakers (Professional Member), Canadian Professional Sales Association (CPSA), Certified Management Accountants (CMA) of Ontario, Canadian Association of Career Educators (CACEE) and National Association of Colleges and Employers (NACE). He is Past Chair and Campaign Cabinet Member with The United Way of Peel Region; Advisory Board Member to Universum.
