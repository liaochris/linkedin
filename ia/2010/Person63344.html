Elina Ivanova’s Summary

Owner and CEO of Princeps Advertising Agency – one of the first and oldest advertising agencies in Bulgaria.

Born in Varna, but has grown up and at present lives in Sofia. A certified specialist in advertising management, graduated in New Bulgarian University, subject Visual Arts / Advertising. Business consultant with broad experience in all aspects of communications, advertising, marketing and pr.

Passed several advertising and marketing trainings in the classes and seminars of some of the legendary names in the sphere. Coordinator of national and international business projects and trainings in the sphere of communications, local self-government, economics, culture and etc.

Previously worked as a pr manager for Business Forum Agency and M3 Communications Group, Inc. ( a Hill & Knowlton Associate).
