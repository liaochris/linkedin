Craig Settles’s Summary

Consultant. Broadband specialist. Media go-to person. Internationally recognized speaker. Craig Settles helps clients develop business strategies for using broadband technology to accelerate economic development, reduce costs and operate more effectively.

Mr. Settles is in heavy demand for his expertise navigating the broadband stimulus grant process. His work in broadband started in 1999. Mr. Settles was Dir. of Electronic Commerce for Metricom, which marketed the Ricochet wireless Internet access service, the pre-cursor to today's municipal wireless networks.

He authored Fighting the Good Fight for Municipal Wireless in 2005. This, together with his numerous in-depth analysis reports (www.successful.com/msp/reports.html), established Mr. Settles as a prominent thought leader on appropriate business strategies for municipal broadband network deployments.

In recent years he has consulted with several cities on municipal broadband strategy. Technology vendors and service providers in this market tap Mr. Settles' industry knowledge and marketing expertise to implement programs that generate sales leads.

Mr. Settles is a frequent expert resource for a wide range of journalists covering municipal broadband issues, including reporters with the Wall Street Journal, NY Times, Time Magazine and a host of business, technology and daily media outlets(www.successful.com/news/articles.html).

Mr. Settles has written several additional books on wireless and Internet business strategies which have been translated into three languages. Prior to his work in muni broadband, Mr. Settles provided strategic market development services to Nextel, AT&T Wireless, AppForge and others, and wireless strategy development services to Kindred Healthcare.

Craig Settles’s Specialties:

The ability to cut through the hype and tech jargon to help clients 1) benefit from broadband and other technology applications, 2) build a business case for them and 3) effectively put the technologies to work. I deliver on-site and conference presentations that deliver valuable practical advice attendees begin using immediately. Finally, I'm highly proficient at analysis and developing business strategies for broadband networks.
