Heather James, Visual Design & Online Specialist has over 12 years expertise in visual communications, specialising in financial and communication sectors. Heather has worked within large multi-national and global corporations over this time.

Drawing from her experience she has a high level of creative direction and executes creative design material aligned to her clients specific requirements provided by a brief. With IT and design confidence on new styles and techniques, Heather efficiently transforms concepts into appropriate designs for clients.

Heather works closely with clients to understand their needs, is responsible for managing and monitoring projects and communications. Overall project management ensuring complete transparency throughout the process.

Whether it's travelling the world with underwater photographers, designing brochures or illustrating children's books, Heather has a life long passion for design and creativity and this shows through her determination and focus to deliver the highest quality products and services to her clients.
