Mike Paul’s Summary

Top reputation management, corporate communications and strategic public relations expert known in the business as the Reputation Doctor. With over 20 years experience, Mike helps build, maintain and repair reputations in both good times and bad. He is listed among the world's top crisis PR experts by PRWeek Magazine, called "the Master of Disaster" by Business Week and "Mr. Fixit" by Sports Illustrated.

Provides senior counseling and strategic public relations consulting to leading corporate, government, nonprofit, entertainment and sports clients (both orgs. and individuals). Some of the clients Mike has counseled over the years include Citicorp, United Airlines, Verizon, Timberland, Merrill Lynch, Goldman Sachs, Nuveen, Pfizer, Samsung, Hitachi, Toyota, Baxter Healthcare, Manor Care, Dunkin Donuts, Kraft Foods, Muhammad Ali, Joe Frazier, Rodney Hampton, Phil McConkey, Mark Gastineau, Freeman McNeil, Kerry Rhodes, Dr. Laura Schlessinger, Aretha Franklin, American Red Cross, FEMA, former Detroit Mayor Kwame Kilpatrick, Jesse Jackson, GM, Mercedes Benz, Cancer Vixen Fund, Trinidad & Tobago Tourist Board, Petroleum Co. of Trinidad & Tobago, The People's Republic of China and others.

Mike is also a leading TV news commentator regarding all types of reputations in crisis. You've seen him weekly on CNN, MSNBC, Fox News, ABC, CBS, CNBC, BBC and ESPN.

He is also founder and editor of the Reputation Doctor blog (http://www.ReputationDoctor.com), a blog read worldwide by those interested in managing personal and organizational reputations. The Reputation Doctor is a nickname given to Mr. Paul by a longtime client.

Mike is also a keynote speaker for top conferences and events regarding the importance of reputation management, corporate reputation and how to best manage the reputations of politicians, celebrities, pro athletes, nonprofits, leaders, and every day citizens. For more info, please call 212-595-8500 or email mpaul@mgppr.com. http://www.mgppr.com
