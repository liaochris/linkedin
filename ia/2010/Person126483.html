Lisa Woodford’s Summary

As the Director of Marketing for Cooley Manion Jones I am the primary person responsible for developing and implementing the overall branding and communication strategies for this mid-sized, high stakes trial firm. CMJ is based in Boston with branch offices in Rhode Island and Delaware.

From inception through deployment, I oversee all of the firm's various marketing and communication channels including web, print, events, conferences, e-mail campaigns, industry sponsorship and charitable contributions.

I also have similar responsibilities regarding our firm's affiliate, Cooley Manion Jones Hake Kurowski LLP. CMJHK is based in San Francisco with a branch office in Los Angeles.

My prior experience includes event management, global sales and creative design. I also possess a decade of litigation support experience.

I am an active member of the Northeast Chapter of the Legal Marketing Association. I support the professional development of myself and others in my industry through continuing education, ethical compliance and a personal commitment to employing only the highest standards in our advertising efforts.
