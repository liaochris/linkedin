Paul Rios’s Summary

Paul was born and raised in Queens, NY and is the son of an Argentinean mother and Puerto Rican father. He holds degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Columbia University (B.S.) and the Georgia Institute of Technology (M.S.), and is currently a student at the MIT Sloan School of Management.

This past summer Paul Rios was a Senior Leadership Program Intern at Sears Holdings Corporation in Hoffman Estates, IL. Paul was part of the Tools Business Unit, a $1.9B operating business unit within the holding company. Paul's responsibility was to develop the social media marketing strategy for the business unit. He established a social media measurement strategy and created a “Social Media Listening & Moderating Playbook” outlining KPIs, metric measurement procedures, and community management guidelines. He also created a social media monetization strategy outlining direct/indirect methods of measuring social media ROI.

Prior to Sloan Paul was an Operations Research Analyst for ZS Associates, a marketing consulting firm specializing in the Pharmaceutical industry. His primary focus was designing, implementing, and overseeing the incentive compensation programs for client sales forces generating as much as $2.8 billion in yearly sales.

Prior to joining ZS Associates Paul was a Systems Engineer for the Ford Motor Company, where he was the program and design lead for several super duty diesel engine subsystems.
