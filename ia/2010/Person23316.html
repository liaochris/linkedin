Bhavin Turakhia’s Summary

I am the Founder, CEO and Chairman of Directi (http://www.directi.com) a pioneer in the Web Services Industry, since 1998.

Directi is a $300+ million group of Businesses that develop innovative, mass-market Web Products for a global audience. Some of these businesses are - ResellerClub (http://www.resellerclub.com), LogicBoxes (http://www.LogicBoxes.com), Skenzo (http://www.skenzo.com), WebHosting.Info (http://www.webhosting.info) etc

Directi is a 450+ employees strong, technology-centric company serving a growing audience of Customers in 230+ countries. Every business unit within Directi is amongst the top 5 or 10 businesses in their respective space worldwide. Several of our business units have been consistently ranked amongst the Deloitte and Touche Technology Fast 50 and the Red Herring 100 lists.

I am chiefly responsible for the entire Vision, Strategy, and Product/Service Offerings of Directi. Apart from Directi, I also serve as a technical advisor to the local CyberCrime Investigation Cell and have won several awards, including the Entrepreneur of the year award in 2005. I have also served as the youngest elected chairman of the Global ICANN Accredited Registrars Constituency for 2 consecutive years. I am also the inventor of WebHosting.Info, an innovative statistics portal about the Web Hosting industry. I am a frequent speaker at various industry conferences and have written several papers on various Internet related subjects
