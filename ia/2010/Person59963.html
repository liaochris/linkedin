Dr. Pedro Kuri’s Summary

Dr. Pedro Kuri, a pioneer of the Lap-Band System in Latin America, Dr. Kuri has performed over 4,800 procedures in the last 12 years as a respected specialist in his field. Having one of the warmest bedside manners of any surgeon, Dr. Kuri is widely known for the loving care he provides his patients. He believes in the Lap-Band procedure, in his patients, and is dedicated to their success.

Dr. Kuri has chosen the best of the best for his team to provide his patients with the most skilled and experienced surgeons. Four surgeons of Dr. Kuri's team have practiced medicine for over 30 years, and two others have over 20 years of experience.

The best thing about Dr. Kuri and his team, is that together, they do what they love...provide people the tool to achieve a happy and healthy life.
