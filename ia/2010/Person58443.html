Donovan Adams’s Summary

IF YOU ARE A RECRUITER, PLEASE DON'T CONTACT ME AT WORK.


Originally coming from an Audio Engineering background, Donovan Adams officially switched gears in 1999 and re-entered the world of web development. Currently he is the sole Flash Developer at the Syfy cable network. Based at 30 Rockefeller in New York City, Donovan is responsible for the interactive and Actionscript development for the Syfy.com website as well as managing freelancers on special initiatives.

In addition to working at Syfy, Donovan runs the flash blog hydrotik. Here you can find topics about Flash and Actionscript 3.0, source code, examples and tutorials, in addition to his photographic and creative work.

Donovan has worked for clients such as NBC/Universal, ABC, Anguilla Tourist Board, Madame Tussauds, and more.

Donovan was also a co-presenter for FlashBelt 2008. He covered his self contained tweening system HydroTween that runs on the Go framework along with Moses Gunesch (Fuse) and Jud Holliday (zaaz).
