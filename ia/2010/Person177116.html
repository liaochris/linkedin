Ronan Mooney’s Summary

Entrust your recruitment requirements or job search to a proven market leader – Cpl’s Sales and Marketing division was named Best in Practice at the NRF Recruitment Awards for 2009.

The Cpl Sales and Marketing team is Ireland’s leading provider of sales and marketing professionals and is committed to providing scalable recruitment solutions to clients ranging from small start-ups to huge multinationals. We recruit across multiple industry sectors such as telecoms, gaming and finance. The team is highly trained and recruits specifically in the area of sales and marketing, allowing them to be the experts in their field.

The positions that we specialise in include; Marketing / Sales Directors and Managers, Product / Brand Managers, Account Directors and Managers, PR and Communications, Business Development Executives, Field Sales Executives, Telesales Executives and Inside Sales Managers.

The Sales and Marketing team fully embraces Cpl’s culture of “placing people first” and applies this to all interactions with candidates and clients
