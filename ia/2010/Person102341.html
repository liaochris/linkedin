John Maturo’s Summary

As the CEO of nu companies LLC, you will see my background is pretty diverse, unique (not to mention enjoyable to experience).

While I have accomplished significant business success including pioneering one of the largest fitness centers on the East Coast for ten years (1990-2000) as managing partner. During this time I was recognized for award-winning customer service, retention of both staff and membership. In fact we were the record holders most dollars generated per square foot by I.H.R.S.A. (International Health and Raquet Sports Assoc.) in both 1994 and 1995!

I have trained with leading professionals of Jay Abraham in more than 400 industries for marketing, innovative advertising and customer incentives. I personally spent 3 years with Jay when completing mastermind, protege and personal coaching programs.

In addition to my time with Jay, I have also spent years in New York City with a leading instructional seminar company (Landmark Education) with a combined 7000 hours of personal education and coaching training while developing thousands of people in their individual pursuits and business training.

On the design end of the spectrum, I worked with famous designer Thomas O'Brien at AERO Design on several design projects, some of which were for celebrity clients.

In 2004 I founded nu companies which specializes in coaching executives, individuals and consulting businesses. We create life coaching services, programs and business models that support professionals in creating careers in the field of professional life consulting.

During the last few years we have coaching and consulted numerous businesses while also bringing commercial products to market and designing luxury living residences.

My passion is to help you get the most out of your life, both professionally and personally. If there is ever anything that I can do to help, never hesitate to reach out to me: johnjmaturo33@yahoo.com.
