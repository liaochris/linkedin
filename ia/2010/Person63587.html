Elizabeth Cooper McFadden’s Summary

With over 13 years experience, Elizabeth Cooper McFadden, founder of elle marketing communications, brings the expertise, creativity and award-winning strategy of a big-time ad agency to small business. Over an eight year period, Elizabeth worked solving marketing + PR needs at two well-known advertising agencies, Valentine Radford and Cramer ~ Krasselt. During her tenure at CK, she and the SRP team created a campaign that not only significantly increased consumer awareness but also won Best of Show at the Phoenix Addy Awards and a Silver Addy at the National Addy Awards. Elizabeth developed and honed her skills working on campaigns for such clients as Sprint, Salt River Project & the Kansas City Zoo, just to name a few.

Since leaving CK in 2003, Elizabeth has consulted with both b2b and business-to-consumer clients in industries ranging from mortgage lending services and technology to non-profits and retail. She’s successfully positioned clients in the marketplace by developing brand strategies and executing marketing and media plans. Her clients have seen their brand awareness ascend to new heights through PR campaigns and online/offline marketing activities. Elizabeth holds a Bachelor of Science in Communications and Journalism from Missouri State University.

Portfolio: American Red Cross, Aziz Anis (published author), Bayer Garden and Professional Care, Better Homes & Gardens Real Estate, Captain D’s Restaurants, Ground Source Systems, Hallmark Cards, HomeAssume.com, Kansas City Zoo, Knights of Columbus, Lake Winnebago Sailing Association, The Law Office of Brian J. Darling, Liberty Wildlife, Meeks Building Center, Newmerix, Payless Cashways, Sacred Heart Catholic Church, Salt River Project (SRP), Sprint Imax Theater, Sprint Publishing & Advertising, St. Joseph Light & Power, Sweet Pea Baby Planners, Sutton Place Gourmet, Tallgrass Flooring & Design Center, Town of Vail, Tracy Spurck (mortgage consultant), Verdict Systems Software and VNSA
