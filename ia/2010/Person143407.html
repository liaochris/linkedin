Michael Kisha’s Summary

Senior Level Business Leader with diverse P&L and Financial experience in highly competitive manufacturing environments. Focus in the automotive, industrial, durable and consumer goods markets. Industries include forging, milling & machining; printing & converting, steel, injection molding and protective packaging. Results Oriented Visionary, who has grown businesses, driven product innovation, identified new markets, optimized profitability & working capital and built motivated teams. Strong financial acumen. Experience as General Manager, CFO / Director of Finance.

As CFO at Colfor Mfg, I was successful in building a strong financial team and delivering a dramatic $20mm operational turnaround of the business earning 'Turnaround of the Year'.

As General Manager at Avery, I led and maintained Automotive Products (automotive OEM market) to the position of leader, serving as sole or key supplier to all the OEM’s and Tier One’s in the industry. This was accomplished through introduction of new products and innovation ($2mm+ annually), expanding and developing new markets and creating a strong customer centric focus.

I have been very effective in improving profitability through Continuous Improvement Teams ($1.5mm annually), Six Sigma, Lean Mfg. concepts and implementation of major quality programs (TS, QS, APQP). I have built and developed management teams that provided the leadership and depth to grow the business and drive increasing levels of income (32% income growth, record profitability and ROTC).

As Director of Finance (Industrial & Automotive Products Group), I led major business expansion projects which included a new facility, equipment and abatement packages ($3mm savings over the period); key player in a successful $4.5mm divisional 'turnaround' in profitability and led numerous major system migrations and implementations, resulting in large operational savings.
