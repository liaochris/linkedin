Ron Means’s Summary

Ron Means is a Founding Partner at GroundWork Equity, LLC. He is also a Vistage (formerly TEC) Chair in the Westlake Village area and is looking for qualified business leaders for his group. Ron has an in-depth knowledge of business operations, growth strategies, domestic and international distribution and has a strong sense for understanding complex market opportunities. A well-respected business veteran, he is known for his leadership and management expertise in the electronics and technology markets, including a role as CEO for the portfolio company E5 Clean energy.

Previously, Ron served as Chief Executive Officer for Market Scan Information Systems. Market Scan is a private, family founded business with over 2000 retail automobile dealer customers and provides finance, customer service and sales management software and hardware systems.

Ron served as president and chief operating officer for Quantum Digital Solutions, an applied sciences firm that developed a multi-patented secure digital communications and content retention technology. The company raised initial and secondary funding and was successful in completing a significant strategic partnership within the health care industry.

Ron served as President of JBL Professional, a Harman International and Fortune 500 Company. During his tenure at JBL, He obtained record-breaking revenues and profit levels and created a dominant market share in each of the company's business segments. He transformed the JBL Pro branded segment into the dominate market leader. In addition, Ron was successful in completing three additional brand acquisitions for the company.

Throughout his career, he has served on boards for a number of corporations and is an active member of World Presidents Organization.
