Gina Benedetti-Petnic’s Summary

Gina has managed design and structural engineering projects in Sonoma and Marin County for over 20 years. With more than 25 years of combined construction site and structural engineering experience, Gina has a proven ability to lead and coordinate successful projects from design development, through the local permit process and construction phase completion.

Her goal is to provide engineering consulting services that ratchet the integrity of the project upward in every way. Gina is a listener who values her client's input and uses every resource at hand to deliver a successful project.

Gina Benedetti-Petnic’s Specialties:

Engineering/Design: steel, concrete, timber and masonry structures - new and seismic retrofits.
Construction: cost estimating for structural and civil projects; field construction supervision; field engineering, and project scheduling.


Residential, Commercial, Municipal, Institutional/educational facilities.
