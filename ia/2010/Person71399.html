Frans Twisk’s Summary

When I first came in contact with computers as a boy I instantly knew I wanted to play and work with them from that day on. Today, after finishing a study in Interactive Media and a Master in Interaction Design, I simply love to design beautiful and compelling interfaces and software for any kind of device.

I am interested in the whole process of software creation and how people use it. Simply said: "from concept to code". I am always eager to work on new software and services that solve problems and support the needs and goals of people.

When designing software myself, I prefer to work on web-applications, more specifically visual design and front-end development. I like my applications to be clean and accessible, taking (web)-standards and usability into account. I always design with the users’ needs and goals in mind, applying several (Interaction Design) methods to get the job done right.

I am also a sucker for good visual design and music.
