Eric Levy’s Summary

Marketing research professional with over 20 years of experience. Conceptual, intuitive thinker, with classic background in brand and advertising development, marketing research design, methods and analysis, organizational development and strategic planning. Supplier- and Client-side roles with significant P&L, budget, management and client service responsibilities. Helped build new Financial Services practice to more than $20M. Helped engineer one of the largest brand mergers in US financial services history.

Experience with blue chip clients in a wide variety of industries including financial services, retail, beverages, entertainment, utilities, IT and health care. Direct clients include: Bank of America, Washington Mutual, Allstate, WW Grainger, Godiva, Jack Daniel's, Johnson & Johnson, Merck, AT&T, Seagate and Agilent.

Experienced with qualitative and quantitative methods, from design through execution and analysis, including:

Customer Experience, Customer Loyalty, Brand Positioning, Advertising Tracking, Segmentation (clustering, latent class and cluster ensemble), Concept Development (Ideation through Market Tests, including Q-Sorts, Forced Ranking, and Choice Experiments), Advertising Concept Development, Price Sensitivity, and Awareness and Usage studies. Trained focus group moderator, skilled in-depth interviewer and observational researcher.

Solution designs include:

Integrative Voice of the Customer Architecture (Closed Loop Process Improvement, Touchpoint Integration and Linkage Analysis); Loyalty Solutions (Competitive Loyalty Studies predicting Brand Choice); Customer Experience and Loyalty Program Assessments (including Senior Leader facilitation, program design assessment, questionnaire refresh process and recommendations).
