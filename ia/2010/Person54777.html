Dennis Sadowski’s Summary

Award-winning journalist and professional communicator with more than 30 years of writing and editing experience. Now venturing into multimedia reporting. As a top editor, led a publication to 34 major national awards while managing a staff and directing newsroom operations. Experience covers local, national and international news. Also experienced in public relations and branding in higher education and nonprofit sectors.

Dennis Sadowski’s Specialties:

Deadline reporting, column writing, editing, management, graphic design, public speaking and external relations with media and other audiences.
