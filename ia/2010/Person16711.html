Kristin Taghon’s Summary

I am an IT Manager gone wild for Art! My love for art is long-lived, from the time I won blue ribbons for posters my sister and I created for the school bazaar to drawing from photos as a kid, to taking drawing classes, photography, painting and finally to getting a degree in Interior Design! I worked at a gallery to be close to the art. learn about the business of a gallery and art consulting. I also am starting my own business as an Art Consultant. I am great at networking and art searches to find that perfect art for your space! I have a great eye for design and can find the right piece that will work for you! I will work with your current art pieces or can help you create your own personalized pieces. I use my past experience as a manager and my technical skills on the computer to do art searches and to create a package that is creative and matches your criteria. I love to pass my enthusiasm for art onto my clients!
Additionally, I am working as a Project Manager for a Web Design company and really enjoy the creative aspect!

Information Technologies Manager with a background as a Lead Software Engineer and Architect with advanced expertise in Object-oriented Technologies. Creative and resourceful in problem resolution. Excellent interpersonal and communication skills with the capability to work both independently, in a team environment and as a lead. Organizational skills are effectively used to implement new strategies.

Interior Design, Art Searches, Art Background, Painting, Photography, Drawing, Gallery Front End and Sales, IT Management, Data Warehousing in a Grid Environment. Extensive GUI and Server Side Design and OO Development for Telecommunications Networks Maintenance Software.
