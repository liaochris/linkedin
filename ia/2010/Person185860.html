Arnel C. Reyes’s Summary

ACR is currently the CTO of Global Content & Research Ltd.

He has served various management positions in IT. To date, he has held positions as IT Director (Network & Systems Security) of Global Content & Research Ltd., IT Security Admin & DBA of U.S. Auto Parts Network Inc. April 2009, ACR earned the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) Foundation Certification in Singapore.

September 2009, ACR acquired the MS Certified Information Technology Professional Database Administrator (MCITP∫DBA).

Determined to give out everything for the world, ACR firmly holds on the quote, “Human Knowledge Belongs to the World.” He developed www.security-science.com to provide explicably extensive information to the world in all defense-in-depth on network & information security aspects.
