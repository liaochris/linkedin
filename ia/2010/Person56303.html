Diann Valentine’s Summary

D. R. Valentine & Associates, Inc. is a Los Angeles based full-service events and weddings production and interior design firm. In our world, creative templates do not exist and I believe that it’s bad business to do the same thing twice. We take pride in knowing that our firm avoids the cookie-cutter approach to weddings, special events and interiors as we assist our clients with interpreting their vision for their life celebrations. We boast over twelve years of experience and bring a wealth of knowledge and invaluable experience to producing life-changing memories that last a lifetime.

For over a decade, DRV has sustained an impressive celebrity clientele due to the development and production of awe-inspiring lifestyle activations. While we are most known for jaw-dropping and tear jerking weddings, our private and corporate events and interior design projects offer creative, logistical and functional strategies which incorporate breath-taking furnishings and architectural influences from all over the world.

Diann Valentine’s Specialties:

Full-service production company, CAD drawings and 3-D renderings, Space Planning, Vendor Negotiations, Location Scouting, Design, Management and Execution
