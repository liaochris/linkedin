Elaine W. Krause’s Summary

This writer's professional career spans over twenty years as a business and technical writer, and is moving more and more into social media consulting. Most recent engagements: helping corporate clients expand and optimize their social media presence; Web content for small business start-ups; charting and information mapping projects; large construction project deliverables inventories.

Elaine has done extensive work in the energy industry: project execution plan for a $$-multi-billion refinery expansion; project services handbooks and feasibility studies; course materials on corrosion control in refineries; an award-winning comic book for school children explaining corrosion.

Work also includes Web page content for: an international safety products firm, a chain of retirement living centers; instructional content for a computer company’s line of “thin client” servers; Quick Start and Help Manual for an Internet-based home security product.

Expertise in business and competitive research: edited a private subscription newsletter for US defense contractors identifying business opportunities, competitor capabilities, and upcoming new federal contracts.

Captured best practices and developed other knowledge management pieces for a variety of energy, business and industry clients. Topics: change management, constructibility, cultural behaviors, employment policies, quality and safety policies, project execution planning, and risk management.

Prime or subcontract clients include: 2Develop, AG Edwards, BHP-Billiton, BP, BG Group, Continental Airlines, Council on Foundations, the Corrosion Society (NACE), Encana/Alberta Energy, ExxonMobil, FEResources, Flexible Packaging Association, Hewlett-Packard (HP), Institute for Makers of Explosives, Merck Pharmaceuticals, the Marion Group, NACE Foundation, Rosen North America, Shell Energy Services, Total Safety, Turner & Townsend, UT Petroleum Extension Service (PETEX), Westney Consulting Group.
