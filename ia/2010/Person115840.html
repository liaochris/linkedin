    CADharbor is a Microsoft SharePoint add-on that benefits any organization needing to collaborate with partners, suppliers and customers on architectural, engineering and construction projects, or any design, manufacturing, compliance or marketing and support activities.

    CADharbor forms a centralized platform to capture, control and communicate CAD related content. Its application-specific content types and rich Silverlight web parts extend SharePoint’s collaborative foundation, accelerating the organizational and workflow processes required for effective Product Data Management (PDM).

    CADharbor sweeps CAD documentation into SharePoint from any directory source, automatically producing project, product and catalog views with fast highly detailed visualizations. It synchronizes CAD-based meta data with SharePoint, enabling action, delivering accurate and timely CAD-sourced information across the enterprise.
