Bilal Jaffery’s Summary

Strong Strategy and Marketing background at IBM, Tech Data, Microsoft, Epson, Rogers Wireless and DesiVibe Media in Marketing and Business Development. Special focus on Online Marketing, SEO, SEM and Web 2.0 Social Media marketing.

Currently leading the web/social media efforts for Lotus Software Group. Main focal point for SMB, IBM Smart Market Midsize marketing initiative. Part of the core team behind IBM's latest Lotus Knows campaign. Launched IBM's first C2B online jam, IdeaJam.

B2B, B2C & C2B Sales, Brand Positioning and Project management in SMB markets. (Wireless/Mobile, Hardware and Software).

Quoted in ComputerWorld, LinuxWorld, CIO, InfoWorld, LinuxToday, Windowsrelated.com, and various other IT magazines. ie, http://bit.ly/3dvP1S

Knowledge of upcoming technological trends and consultation experience. Regular speaker at University of Toronto's Technology mentorship program.

I've been involved with Web since the early 1990s, when I launched my first web portal that offered web services for a niche segment. I developed that portal into a 43,000 member strong community (back in 1996) and provided email services, online streaming media, file storage and 'community discussion'. The portal's mail services were offered by my partner firm at that time, Outblaze, now IBM (2009). The portal was featured in various magazines and TV shows.

My first experience with online media was back in 1987 with BBS systems (PCBoard, WildCat, FIDONET).

Editor at Lotus Journal.com

I also sit on the boards of Jobtec, MarketingMinds and my previous employer, DesiVibe.ca as well. Along with web, my other passion is my co-founded non-for-profit charity project, Our Foundation, with the deeply held and shared belief that we all are capable of giving back to our communities, not only financially, but also through our time and talents. We support registered non-profit groups through donations and volunteering.
