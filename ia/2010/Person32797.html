Carmen L Perez’s Summary

Carmen Perez has more than 15 years of experience in Project & Program Management and 8 years of International Business and Policy. Her professional profile includes responsibilities at Schlumberger, an international services corporation which supplies Oil, Energy, Network and Telecommunications services Worldwide. At the start of her career, she focused on Telecommunications companies in Latin America from, Argentina to Mexico. Ms. Perez initiated and managed the complete project life cycle for more than 25 projects, as well as oversight and guidance of the quality and expertise of resources while maintaining budget requirements for long term projects where goods and services would total more than $100 million annually. Ms. Perez has additionally served as Project manager for the largest private corporation network migration incorporating employees in 140 countries. Her team was responsible for drafting all Business and Technical requirements and operational processes for the Schlumberger MPLS network implementation. Her expertise showcases a successful track record implementing many high profile projects for business partners around the world.

Highly experienced in issue resolution, change management, supplier diversity and organizational effectiveness involving various functional areas with the ability to link teams, leadership and technology to be strategically utilized to improve bottom line results. A well-versed facilitator, proficiently skilled in developing enhanced client management and vendor negotiations.

A skilled negotiator, having forged corporate-level strategic partnerships successfully driving long-term business growth, profitability, and stable market analysis, for public and private concerns, surpassing project goals for time and budget
