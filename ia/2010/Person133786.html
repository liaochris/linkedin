Mark Gake’s Summary

Engineering Manager/Project Manager with 18 years of experience in fossil and nuclear energy projects. Skilled in working with clients to capture new work and build lasting relationships. Excellent background in engineering design, procurement, construction, scheduling, and budgeting.

Mark Gake’s Specialties:

Experienced in ASME Section III and B31.1 piping design, nuclear and fossil equipment specification and procurement, nuclear and fossil plant arrangement and equipment layout design, NQA-1, and ISO 9001. Professional Engineer (licensed in Kansas). Registered Professional Engineer (RPE) for ASME Section III work.
