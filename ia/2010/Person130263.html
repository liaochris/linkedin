SVP & CFO at Nirvanix, Inc. Manage all finance, treasury, human resources, legal, and procurement functions for Nirvanix worldwide. Also serve as the Corporate Secretary for the Board of Directors.

Major Horton’s Experience

    Nirvanix

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2007 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    Nirvanix is the premier provider of cloud storage for the enterprise, offering its customers a fully managed storage service designed to meet their needs for secure, reliable, long-term data archiving. Nirvanix provides customers with storage as a service via its Storage Delivery Network™ (SDN) an unrivaled globally distributed intelligent storage cloud, or by installing managed storage nodes in our customers’ data centers, all powered by Nirvanix's proprietary and patented technology. The Nirvanix SDN has won numerous awards including being named as one of the top storage products since its inception in 2007. Nirvanix has also been continuously recognized as one of the top startups to watch by Byte and Switch, InformationWeek, Red Herring, and other industry experts. Nirvanix is backed by Intel Capital, Mission Ventures, Valhalla Partners, Windward Ventures, and the European Founders Fund.
