Christine Crandell’s Summary

There are three mottos that I live by: ‘Take your business personally’, ‘deliver high impactful results’ every day, and ‘stay outside the box’. These are reflected throughout my work, play and life.

For more than 20 years I have advised and worked with enterprise technology CEOs around the world on the opportunities and challenges that stand between them and success. By crafting successful business strategies that achieve measurable business goals, I’m proud to be recognized in Silicon Valley and European technology centers. My expertise is in shaping successful business strategies, driving revenue through ecosystems, shaping emerging markets, and building world class organizations.

In my spare time I serve as a business advisor to early stage companies and non-profits, and am a frequent speaker, writer, and guest lecturer on issues of technology, strategy and marketing. I hold a Masters degree in Management from Florida Atlantic University as well as completed post-doctoral studies at Golden Gate University. The Santa Cruz Mountains in where I call home.

Business Strategy, marketing strategy and planning, product strategy and direction, new market entry, revenue growth planning, channel and sales management and international marketing and management.
