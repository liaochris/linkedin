Tina Bennett’s Summary

I have been with Touchstone Homes as the Administrative Assistant to the Land Development/Acquisition Manager since July 2004. Since that time, I have been involved in permitting, plan review routing through various government agencies, DRI review with the Atlanta Regional Commission, submittal of a Development of Excellence nomination with the Atlanta Regional Commission, and various other land development related activities.

In March 2008, I was given the responsibilities of Marketing Coordinator for Touchstone Homes as well. Not coming from a marketing background, this position was a struggle in the beginning but I have since fallen into line with the duties and enjoy them quite well. I handle agent events, company event planning, promotional items and flyers, design various print material using Adove Illustrator Creative Suites, marketing signage and public relations.

I work closely with a PR firm called Tribute Media Consulting based in Smyrna and we have successfully pitched several stories to local magazines and newspapers that have run and created excellent press for Touchstone Homes. Most recently we had a cover story in the AJC Northside Real Estate Section on New Haven Walk townhome community located in downtown Roswell. The story covered our new model home and floorplan there.

One of my passions is the Susan G Komen Race for the Cure. I have done approxmately ten 5k walks in Atlanta and did the Atlanta 3-Day Walk (60 miles in 3 days) in 2009 for the first time. I am walking again in 2010 and proud to be part of a team of 11 beautiful women with one goal in mind - to end breast cancer for good! We had 3 survivors on our team and we walked in honor of them. Our team raised over $31,000 in 2009 and we hope to exceed that goal in 2010!!!
