George Gurney’s Summary

Over thirty years experience in executive search,placement,training and coaching. I applied my years of experience to author two weekly syndicated newspaper columns dedicated to career advice. Both columns"Career Insider"and "Dear Headhunter" have been published in print and on-line since 2002 and are trademarked property.In 2006 created "The Career Insider TV Show".Wrote,produced and directed twenty-two half-hour talk show episodes shown on local TV in Southern California.My career advice book "Career Insider Secrets" will be published and released through Amazon.com in 2010
