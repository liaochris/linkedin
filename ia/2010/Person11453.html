Andres Fabris’s Experience

  • Founder and CEO

    Traxo

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    April 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Traxo is a centralized, automated, and dynamic travel dashboard that members can use to organize, manage, and share their travel plans (“Mint.com of travel”). Once a user links Traxo to their favorite travel websites (a one-time, set-and-forget process), our system automatically detects and aggregates all their travel reservation information into logical trips. We also make our itinerary tools, travel data and technology available to 3rd-party app developers.
