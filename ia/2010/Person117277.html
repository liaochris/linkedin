Kip Piper’s Summary

Kevin B. "Kip" Piper, MA, FACHE – a top authority on Medicare, Medicaid, and the pharmaceutical and medical device industries – is a consultant, author, and speaker. A skilled business and policy strategist, Kip has advised Fortune 100 companies, top federal officials, governors, members of Congress, foundations, and foreign leaders.

Kip Piper is:

• President of the Health Results Group LLC.

• Senior counselor with TogoRun LLC.

• Senior counselor at Fleishman-Hillard Inc.

• Senior consultant at Sellers Dorsey.

• Health policy editor, American Health and Drug Benefits.

• Editor of the Piper Report, a health care blog.

Kip Piper's 27 years’ experience includes senior advisor to the administrator of the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS), Wisconsin state health commissioner, head of the multi-billion dollar Wisconsin Medicaid program, advisor to Fortune 500 firms, general manager at WellPoint, vice president of AcademyHealth, a senior Medicare budget officer at the White House Office of Management and Budget (OMB), director of national foundation-sponsored programs on quality improvement and payment reform, presidential management fellow, and local elected official.

A frequent speaker, Kip Piper has testified before Congress and negotiated federal legislation. He’s been widely quoted in the press, including BusinessWeek, USA Today, Modern Healthcare, The Hill, HealthLeaders, NPR programs, Investor’s Business Daily, and Associated Press.

Kip Piper is editor of the Piper Report, a blog on Medicare, Medicaid, and health policy. He is author of forthcoming books on Medicare and Medicaid, health policy editor for the journal American Health and Drug Benefits, and editor of GetInsideHealth.com.

Kip is a Fellow by the American College of Healthcare Executives and holds a master's degree in public administration from the University of Wisconsin, Madison.
