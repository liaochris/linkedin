Bruce Levitus’s Summary

My experiences through out my life have led to where I am today. In both good times and bad I have managed to extract something from the experience that moved me forward and led me to the place I am today.
From my first postion in retail management, I learned the discipline one needs in their first management position. I learned to effectively motivate and develop a staff that shared little in common with myself.
When I decided to switch careers, and become a financial advisor it was back to square one; when I finally caught on, the basis of the transactional stockbrokerage did not sit well with me; so again, reinvent, step back, regroup and advance. Each time not only did I grow proffesionally, but also personally as well.
In my latest role, a promotion at AIG Advisor Group I have once again been challenged not only with regards to financial goals, and managing a P &L; but also in managing and developping a staff not only for our business today; but with an eye on the long term.
