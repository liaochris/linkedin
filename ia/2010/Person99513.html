Matt Burns’s Summary

Mr. Burns is a Senior Vice President responsible for the daily operations of Stephens Insurance, LLC’s Austin, Texas, office. Prior to joining Stephens Insurance, LLC, he was Managing Director at Higginbotham & Associates' Austin office, and Senior Vice President and Partner with Nieman, Hanks, Puryear, a division of Frost Insurance Agency, Inc. He has been a commercial insurance broker for more than 15 years, focusing his practice in the technology, life sciences, social services and medical industries. Mr. Burns works closely with many non-profit agencies in the Austin area and currently serves on some of their boards.

One of the largest independent agencies in the United States, Stephens Insurance is unique in the industry. Through strategic industry relationships, Stephens Insurance is able to offer exclusive, customized personal insurance and business insurance solutions unavailable through many other insurance providers. Backed by the Stephens commitment to excellence, Stephens Insurance provides trusted, experienced insurance services to a nationwide client base.

Since its founding in 1933, Stephens has enjoyed long-term relationships with our clients and an international reputation for bringing vision, integrity and innovation to all our transactions.
