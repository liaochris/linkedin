Michael Silva’s Summary

I have 10 years experience in consumer goods sales and management.

I recently have started working in the credit card processing industry. I provide merchants solutions to help "ease the pain" that often is associated with processing cards.

My passion is sports and over the last two years I have produced and hosted my own weekly radio show on New York baseball.

I have been able to create a show with a loyal listening audience. It features interviews with former New York athletes, journalist, authors, and independent bloggers. No other show is as diverse and give the "hardcore" baseball fan this type of content.

Although sports is a part time gig, I have gained valuable networking and communication skills from the experience of creating my own brand.

I believe my career working for two major fortune 500 companies has helped me excel in the radio industry. Rather then take the traditional route, I am focused on embracing new media and introducing a New York baseball show that complements the mainstream outlets of ESPN and WFAN. This experience has allowed me to be the host as well as producer and business manager.
