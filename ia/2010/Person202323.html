Chris Rees’s Summary

Managing Director of the Wessex Swim School for 5 years, grown from 20 swimmers to over 650 in the space of 5 years.
I have created a strong brand along the south coast as a high quality affordable swim school. The swim school is one of the most highly regarded swimming teacher training outlets in the UK, having trained over 400 swimming teachers with a 98% pass rate.

2009 saw the beginnings of Tri Team Wessex my latest venture which involves encouraging youngsters and adults into multi sport activities such as Triathlons, Duathlons and my favourite Aquathlons!
2010 will see 9 events taking place along the South coast as part of 'The Wessex 2010 Triathlon Series' which will encourage the whole family to participate. These events will be supported by training sessions for all age categories so those wishing to continue their training and improve in the sport can do so. Interested in getting fit and doing something different this year then visit www.trytri.co.uk

Something for the business minded, business owners and those brave enought to call themselves entrepreneurs Jan 2010 sees the launch of 'Fitness and Business Networking Meetings' for more information visit our website: www.fitnessandbusiness.co.uk

I am looking for opportunities in the leisure industry inparticular swimming or triathlons.
