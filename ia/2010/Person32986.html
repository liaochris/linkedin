Caroline Andoscia’s Summary

Caroline is a lifelong communications professional with 20 years experience in media and entertainment. She is an experience manager and strategist. She has developed media campaigns for cosmetics lines, fashion brands, magazines, consumer goods and hotels. She has managed budgets budgets of all sizes( for clients and in house pr/marketing departments.) She is analytical, creative and focuses upon deliverables.

She started her career in public relations in New York. At 24 she opened her own agency specializing in fashion and luxury goods. By 30 she fulfilled her dream of working in the film business and worked as a film development executive for Scott Rudin, Lawrence Gordon and Barry Sonnenfeld. She returned to New York and resumed her public relations career. She was able to put her entertainment experience to use while at Banana Republic, Conde Nast and Getty Images. She reopened her own consulting agency in 1999 and has continued to run it. AndosciaMossCommunications/AndosciaCommunications-since 2004 has worked with clients including Salvatore Ferragamo, Movado, Gant, Star Magazine and Scientific American Magazine, Stephen Webster Jewelry and Kiss My Face.
