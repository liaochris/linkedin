  • Photographer

    2009 — Present (1 year )

    I have been photographing for most my life: initially capturing the moments of my own life and eventually, invited to capture moments in other people’s lives: weddings, events, families. Interesting projects soon followed: wine labels, food photography, scanning vintage photographs.

  • Fine Art

    Charlene Collins Freeman

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    1986 — Present (24 years )

    I am drawn to objects and scenes which, through their ordinary and familiar qualities, take on a sense of beauty. I am attracted to and comforted by the richness of every day objects and moments. Something catches my eye, something common, and in that moment it strikes me as exceptional.

    I have been painting and photographing for most of my life. I have merged painting and photography by using my camera to collect images which reappear in my paintings and by making my photographs more painterly through various processes including Polaroid Transfers, computer software, and traditional darkroom techniques.
