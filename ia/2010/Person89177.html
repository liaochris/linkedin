Jane Schneider’s Summary

I am committed to helping people successfully launch or relaunch their businesses online. I want people to succeed in their overall online effort, from the business idea, to marketing, networking, launching, converting, selling and building residual income.

I bring 26 years of marketing experience to my clients: small business consulting, branding, online marketing, marketing research and big agency advertising. I have submerged myself full time in learning the online business for about a year.

I have attended five major internet conferences: Mike Koenigs Paid for Life, Brendon Berchard’s Experts Academy, Armand Morin’s Big Seminar 13, Yanik Silver’s Underground Seminar and the Glazer Kennedy SuperConference. I’ve also completed countless courses in online marketing, social media and am in the process of completing Jeff Walker’s Product Launch Formula.

I’m also in the process of developing Social Media from Scratch, which will be a social media-training program for small business owners. Psychologist Rachna Jane, who founded http://www.mindsharecorp.com is developing the social media components and Denise Wakeman who founded http://www.buildabetterblog.com is handling blogging.

We have a training program with 10 videos that cover goal and strategy setting, metrics, social media basic and advanced techniques, blog set up, business blogging and video marketing. We will be featuring a continuity program that will focus on how to monetize your social media. We’ll have two live webinars a month, one on a training topic and the second one a Q&A on anything anyone wants to cover.

We will also be offering VA training and certification as well as a franchising opportunity for anyone who wants to work in the social media field.

I am looking to partner with JVs who target anyone who would be interested in optimizing their online marketing efforts.
