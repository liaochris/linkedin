Tarun Malik’s Summary

I have worked extensively in setup, planning, budgeting, and managing IT and IT Enabled Services (ITeS) projects across domains.

In my current role as CIO and Chief Architect, I am responsible for creating product design & engineering along with managing Infrastructure. Responsibility includes create Application Platforms which are media agnostic and can be integrated with any delivery medium such as Internet, Voice, Mobile and going forward IPTV and DTH.

Prior to my current role, I lead transitions and managed Service Delivery at NIIT Smartserve (subsidiary of NIIT Technologies Ltd.) in the area of Remote Infrastructure Management projects & Firmware Technical Testing. As Head of Software at NSS, my key responsibility was to roll out BPO oriented products for Workflow Management, Intelligent Data Entry, CRM, and Quality Monitoring along with integrated MIS and Analytics system across these products named TermsMonitor© which got nominated for “IQPC UK 2005 for most advanced automation in shared services” award.

During my tenure as Senior Project Manager with Adecco NIIT Technologies I managed on-shore and off-shore projects along with roll out of Web 2.0 Framework within the organization. I created an In-house system around Folksonomy, and Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) which one the won 3rd prize in NIIT technologies Tech Conference 2009. I have also been IT digitization leader at GENPACT (formerly known as GECIS) in the Analytics division.

I am ITIL V3 certified and am Six Sigma Green Belt certified.
