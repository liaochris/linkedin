Steffi Waltinger Kalil’s Summary

Recently Steffi founded Creative.Interiors and specializes in residential and commercial interior design. Through collaborations with architects, developers, and real estate agents on existing branding projects, Steffi began to get involved with interior planning for sales offices and interior renderings. Her creative thought on interior design can be seen in recent projects such as Silk City Lofts, isabella, and Sunset Cove Condominiums.

The whimsical. The colorful. The theatrical. These are among the many elements that Steffi Waltinger-Kalil used to direct and inspire the Atelier team. A lifelong student of fine design, she co-founded Atelier Design Group with friend and partner Jef Kalil in 2003 to help clients tap into their creative visions and inspire the conscious consumer. Steffi had overall responsibility for the agency's creative direction in its New York office.

Steffi has helped many start-ups develop brands, spearhead campaigns for international audiences, and advise established non-profit institutions on how to best package their international visions. During her earlier design years she interacted with companies such as Lomo PLC and Puky GmbH & Co. at a time when their products were first entering the global market. Her experience spans many industries including fashion, financial services, arts/entertainment, and real estate.

"I strongly believe that we no longer give consumers credit to what they are capable of understanding. I believe that design must inspire and tell a wonderful story. It must take you away from the place you are in for a moment. To do this it must surprise you, make you smile, and make you spend a moment enjoying it."
