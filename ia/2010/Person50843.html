David Grunfeld’s Summary

David brings his strong background in knowledge services to the UAE, having founded two unique yet complementary companies - Prose Solutions and MindSight - to address critical needs in the regional market.

Prose Solutions – Communications consulting and English & Arabic content development for websites, profiles, newsletters, reports, speeches, press releases, etc – www.prose-solutions.com

MindSight – customized executive education in leadership, strategy, finance, etc led by professors from Harvard, MIT, Stanford, Yale, Wharton, etc – www.mindsight-me.com

Since establishing Prose Solutions in Abu Dhabi over six years ago, he has guided the company's growth while overseeing its communications consulting, copywriting, editing and translation services for hundreds of clients across the UAE and around the world.

Since co-founding MindSight over 4 years ago, David has helped bring some of the world's leading minds to a some of the region's foremost public and private sector organizations. Prior to that, he had gained experience in the education and training industry as the general manager and head teacher of the Abu Dhabi branch of the international education company, The Princeton Review, and before that as a teacher in South Korea.

David is a graduate of McGill University, where he earned his B.Sc. in Medicine. He has lived between Dubai and Abu Dhabi for over eight years.
