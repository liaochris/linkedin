Steven Schussler’s Summary

Entrepreneur Steven Schussler is Chairman of Schussler Creative Inc., Inventions, Ideas, Contraptions and Dreams™, creators of theatrical environments for restaurant and retail attractions that entertain as well as educate. Steven founded and created the award-winning Rainforest Cafe™, as well as T-REX Cafe™, Yak & Yeti™, Betty & Joe's™, and Hot Dog Hall of Fame™, Backfire Barbeque™ featuring Orange County Choppers and Galaxy Drive In™. There are several other attractions under development at Schussler Creative including Aerobleu™, Zi Imperial Kitchen™, Winter Wonderland™, Sorcerer's Kitchen™, Musical Garden™, Water, Fire & Ice™ - Polar Ultra Lounge™, Pizza Market™, and Mahogany Bay™ Classic Wood Boats & Great Food™. Prior to entering the restaurant and entertainment arenas, Steven held executive positions in the television and radio broadcast industry, selling television time for ABC, CBS and NBC. For more information, visit www.schusslercreative.com.
