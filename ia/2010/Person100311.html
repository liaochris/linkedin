Jodi Kaplan’s Summary

Jodi Kaplan fixes "broken" marketing. She founded KaplanCopy to help creative small businesses such as graphic designers, writers, and web developers turn their b-to-b campaigns from a cost center into a revenue generator.

My mission is to help small creative businesses improve marketing and earn more, without spamming or being obnoxious.

If your marketing is costing more money than it's making and people leave your Web site without buying, your marketing is broken. For a free ebook, Get More Business Now, visit http://www.kaplancopy.com/free_guide.html.
