Wendy DeLuca (LION)’s Summary

Between my work experience and education sales and marketing have always been a great part of my expertise. While litigation support is a new field for me it really isn't as my husband has been involved in litigation support since the copy only days. I have always worked at home for him, either while I was employed at a steady position or not.
Elite Group has given me the opportunity to help them grow and I plan to do that. With over 20 years of sales and marketing experience be it on the phone or face to face I have always excelled in my field. My goal is to do the same with Elite Group. I find it a bit easier as I am familiar with the business, try and keep up on new things in litigation support, and I know we have a great team. Our president Darrin Campbell not only is the best of the best in both litigation support sales, but in customer service. My husband is also the best IT person for a facility to have; he has grown with the times. He is a certified trainer in IPRO, has impeccable skills in EDD, and many other software and hardware talents. I am hoping that in the very near future we will be one of the top firms in the country with offices throughout, providing the best, fastest, and most competivatly priced services in our market. I am proud to be a part of such a rare team
