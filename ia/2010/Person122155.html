LAUREL KRASNE’s Summary

LAUREL KRASNE is an award-winning corporate communication manager who demonstrates exceptional capability in isolating the core of an issue, presenting realistic solutions and aligning communication strategies and tactics with company objectives. Process-oriented, insightful and versatile, she is a strategic communicator of the highest order, proficient at tracking and measuring the effectiveness of messages and initiatives across all organizational levels. Laurel has established LK Communications to dovetail her pursuit of the ideal corporate scenario, one that leverages her strategic communication abilities, business acumen and project management skills.

With skill sets transferable to any industry, she willingly assumes the communication lead, consistently reaching high thresholds of approval and delivering on established goals. Drawing on a solid background in research and competitive analysis, Laurel measures all aspects of communication campaigns and demonstrates high levels of responsibility, accountability and accessibility. Aware that organizations are bottom-line-focused, she prioritizes well, circumvents conflict and builds stakeholder engagement by crafting solutions with great clarity. Laurel is passionate about raising the level of trust among and between management and employees and delivering meaningful returns on communication investments.

Always in lock-step with top management, Laurel is process-oriented, adaptable and methodical in assessing and prioritizing communication needs. She has a deep understanding of the nuances of communication and exceeds expectations in the design and delivery of projects. Lauded for her ability to transition people into higher plateaus of performance, Laurel is uncompromising in her commitment to excellence in her craft. “Everyone communicates, but how effective they do it is another story,” declares Laurel, “Ultimately, the goal is to help people become better at engaging others.”
