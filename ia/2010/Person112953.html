Kahlil Kenneth Barnard’s Summary

Kahlil Kenneth Barnard has over 16 years experience in the real estate business and is in his 11th year at Marcus & Millichap Investment Real Estate Services Company. Kahlil has consistently been involved in providing his clients with comprehensive research, advisory, finance, and transactional services that are typically behind high profile investment properties ranging from Regional Malls, Community Shopping Centers, Retail Strips, to less visible peripheral transactions like Single Tenant Net Leased Investment Sales occupied by Banks, Drugstores, Auto parts, and restaurant transactions.

Kahlil Kenneth Barnard joined Marcus & Millichap in November 1998, while opening up the Indianapolis office for its retail division and is easily now one the industries leaders in market share for the business. He has excelled in his career while being named Sales Associate in December 2000, amended to Senior Associate in May 2002, promoted to Senior Investment associate in July 2005, and most recently named First Vice President of Investments in 2007. He also has consistently served as a Director of the firm’s National Retail Group since its inception, and routinely serves as a Mentor for a select number of Junior Agents in the Midwestern markets for the firm.

Kahlil actively participates in leadership and mentorship roles both within the company and externally while he sits on the board for the Indiana University Center for Real Estate Studies and holds Midwest Real Estate Equities Club (M.R.E.C.) meetings for real estate investors alike as the founding member. In addition he runs an investment company called Midwest-Properties.com for wealth building of its principle members through acquisition and management of leased investment based properties and is its largest share holder.

Kahlil Kenneth Barnard’s Specialties:

Retail Investment Properties, NNN Properties, Strip Centers, Power Centers, Malls, Sale Lease-Back arrangements and funding, purchase syndications, valuaiton, and developement.
