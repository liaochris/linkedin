Robert (Bob) Stevenson’s Summary

I have over 30 years of experience in retail, 23 of those years with two Fortune 100 companies, emphasis on the Club Store Industry. I have a strong background in operations, buying, merchandising, new store set-up and execution.

I have a progressive and proven track record of consistent sales achievements, specializing in building supplier relationships to achieve and surpass category sales.

I possess proactive leadership and effective communication skills, strong hiring and team building abilities, and exemplary people skills. I facilitate a team approach to achieve organizational objectives.

With my years of experience and knowledge in the club industry, I understand the unique needs of the supplier and what will work at the club level. I have an uncanny vision to discern a viable item from one that needs work or will not work at all in the club environment. I have extensive knowledge in all aspects of the food industry including: Non-Foods, Tobacco, Candy, Beer, Wine, Spirits, Dry Grocery, Beverage, Sundries, Janitorial, Cooler, Freezer and Deli foods.

In addition to my foods experience, I represent a handful of non-foods suppliers and actively call on the Clubs in HBA, Small Appliances and Hardware.

Robert (Bob) Stevenson’s Specialties:

I use my businesses knowledge and experience to take good items and make them better, increase quality, drive down product costs and find creative ways to reduce distribution costs. I look for areas of opportunity to bring new and exciting items to market in a fast paced industry. I am able to work with companies to develop and bring quality products and categories to market. I know what the buyer is looking for and how to present an item/program in a way that makes the buyer want to buy.
