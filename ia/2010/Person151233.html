Nancy Grossbart’s Summary

My personal mantra is “bringing order to chaos.” Whether it's been product development or marketing, my whole career is about solving people's problems. I established my professional reputation as problem solver for clients in advertising. I helped clients find ways to build their business. And I do it now at my current job at MDI, a worldwide manufacturer of injection molded foam products. The process gives foam products unique properties and I help clients find ways to use those properties to give them an advantage in their product design and their marketplace.
