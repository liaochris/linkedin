Eugenia Shcherbets’s Summary

Have 7 years experience in the field of telecommunication. Started my career at one of the leading telecommunication companies in Eastern Europe named MTS in 2001 as a customer service expert. Later joined marketing team at the same company and managed new cross-functional unit responsible for youth brand launch and development. In 2005, I graduated from Kiev Shevchenko's University and received master degree of journalism. In 2007, I have joined GP Company, which operates in IT (Internet) industry. Generalist with broad experience in marketing, sales and cross-functional project management. Direct experience with telecommunications, Internet and investment activity. Interested in early-stage on-line start ups.
