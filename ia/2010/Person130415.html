Malika de Silva’s Summary

I graduated this past June with an MBA from the Fisher College of Business at The Ohio State University (OSU). For the past year of the program, I worked with a couple classmates on a business plan for a scalable, socially responsible apparel retail concept. We participated in a number of business plan competitions, sought advise from CEOs at companies ranging from Ron Jon Surf Shop to Brooklyn Industries, hosted a successful proof-of-concept trunk show sale with a target designer, collaborated with OSU’s Department of Design and local retail design firms on our brand identity, and met with numerous regional retail entrepreneurs about best practices.

I would love to launch this business someday and continue to network and keep current in this space. I am currently doing project-based retail work in Columbus, OH, and seeking full-time opportunities with corporate retailers in the San Francisco Bay Area.
