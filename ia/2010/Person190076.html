Simon Sikorski’s Summary

After 8 years at E-Medical Marketing, on September 6, 2010 Simon celebrated successful completion of Market Development Projects for: 50 medical companies (of which 31 are medical device), 3345 medical practitioners, 75 Thought Leaders, and 17 hospitals all with the goal of bringing highly qualified patients for advanced treatments, procedures, and diagnostics. During the time of initiating market development projects he was directly responsible for bringing over 23,000 patients to highly trained and respected physicians renowned for their clinical excellence.

For the doctors, more than $50 million has been realized in income from the procedures. For medical companies, over $30 million solely in KOL accounts that acted as defining blocks on segmentation and market development strategies. For the 23,000 patients, life-changing events finally made their lives of higher quality.

Since 2002, more than 650,000 patients were able to find experts to treat life-debilitating conditions thanks to Simon's campaigns, and the number keeps growing as the strategies depend on long-term relationships.
