Pietschmann Patti’s Summary

My journalism career began at Florida Southern College where I won a scholarship to edit the Southern (weekly newspaper), won awards and went on to become "Teen Editor" at Newsay in NY and do a radio show on WBAB. Married straight out of college. Husband won the toss and we moved to LA where I worked in radio and began prolific freelance career specializing in luxury travel, cruises, spas and fitness. Pretty much covered the globe by land, air and sea (more than 250 cruises), wrote a dozen ACCESS Los Angeles guides (latest #13 at bookstores now), ACCESS Cruise guides, ACCESS Las Vegas, Hawaii guides and newspapers and magazines pieces for Inflight publications (name it I probably wrote for it), LA Magazine, Harpers Bazaar, Cosmo, Shape, Fitness, Departures, San Diego magazine, Brides, For the Bride, Modern Bride, Diversion, Miami Herald, St. Pete Times, Newsday, LA Times, Atlanta Journal, and many more, been a busy girl. Also was the on-air Dining Diva on Rick Dees in the Morning (93.9 FM) until the station went Spanish and Dees went digital.
