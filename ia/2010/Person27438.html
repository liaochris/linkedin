Brenda Pinnick’s Summary

I've worked in house for Hallmark Cards as a Surface Designer/ Graphic Designer (hybrid) in the Gift and Party Studio creating gift wrap, bags, invitations, party ware and boxes.

At Plaid Enterprises, I headed up the newly formed Paper Crafts design team managing licensed art and artists, in house art, scheduling, concept development, trend presentations and created art and design for product placement in key accounts such as Michaels, AC Moore, Walmart.

I have been an independent Illustrative Designer / Artist for 4 years in which time my Scrapbooking products have sold in the multi-millions via QVC US and UK. I've had my own Brenda Pinnick Hour on both QVCs presenting my own collections and kits with much success. I license to a variety of companies and am now represented by Two Town Studios. I am continuing to grow my brand by adding new art, offering beautiful colorways, creating exciting surface designs and word art.

I belong to the Color Marketing Group as a member in good standing, I serve on the CHA Designer Council and am a member of the Professional Advisory Committee for the Art Institute of Atlanta.

I teach specialty classes/workshops including Color Theory, Painting,
Power Brainstorming and Paper Sculpture.

Brenda Pinnick’s Honors:

I have won many awards in the area of painting, drawing & design, including Best of Shows, Best Body of Work and People's Choice awards. As a lifelong artist, I feel it's important not to rest on old accolades, but rather to move forward in meaningful ways for a fulfilling and rewarding career. While my many awards meant a lot to me at the time, they are just a reflection of what and where I was then, not now. With the goal of creating art and products that speak to the consumer, I now work with several manufacturers who have a commitment to producing quality but affordable products. My papercrafting kits have sold in the millions of wholesale dollars on direct response television where I have had the privilege of presenting them myself on the "Brenda Pinnick's Art of Paper Crafting show" in the U.S. and in the U.K.
