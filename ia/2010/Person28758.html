    (Financial Services industry)

    October 2007 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    Echelon Consulting was formed in 2009 to help enhance the efforts of investment management firms as well as wealth advisors and institutional investors. The etymology of Echelon (e-shə-län) is French and translates to 'rung of a ladder'. Today, it is more commonly known as a formation that helps advance an individual or entity in an efficient manner. We consider ourselves as a rung in your ladder; a partner who efficiently helps take your firm to the next level.

    Advisors & Investors - Our interests lie in educating investors about the long-term benefits of investing in alternatives. Whether you are new to investing your clients' money in this asset class or are looking to expand your alpha seeking product offerings, we can help. We want to partner with you to help take your firm to the next level.

    Investment Managers - We seek to add to your firm's already strong infrastructure by providing marketing and/or operational expertise; the goal: to take your firm to the next level. Our focus is working with all types of alternative investment management firms including hedge funds, private equity and real estate funds as well as funds-of-funds. We also have significant experience working with wealth management firms. If your needs entail asset raising, development of marketing materials and/or other firm related documentation or enhancing your internal operations, we can help.
