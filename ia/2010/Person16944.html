Arturo Duran’s Summary

A very dynamic professional, trilingual with full command of English, Spanish & French. He has lived in Boston, New York, SFO in the USA; Paris, France; Buenos Aires, Argentina; Hong Kong and Shanghai, PRC; Jakarta, Indonesia, Toronto, Canada and Mexico. Over 10 years of professional experience, leading today all the interactive activities of the largest Hispanic News and Information Media company in the US. Arturo has work for leading national and multinational corporations in the internet, imaging, computing, printing, and textile industries.
Created and launched new products, new sites, full networks, head efforts to enter new markets and "fix" Divisions, Subsidiaries, etc. turning them around in record time.
US Citizen, allowed to live and work in Europe (EEC) and Mexico.
Member of the Board of MNet (Ottawa) and of Platial.com (Portland, Oregon) .
