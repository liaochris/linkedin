Dan Heller’s Summary

Thirty years of real estate investment experience, most recently focused on the development and rehabilitation of older commercial buildings in east Nashville. Extensive experience in small business creation, management and leadership with emphasis on marketing and business plan development, integrated communications, strategic planning, brand development, lead generation and sales-team management.
