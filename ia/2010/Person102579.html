John Matthews’s Summary

Principal at Comscient Group, a consultancy assisting clients to research, plan, and execute market-based strategies through business intelligence, social media intelligence, social influence marketing, thought leadership, new market entry and go-to-market studies, executive information solutions, market insight, project analysis, commercial and technology due diligence, screening, alliances and joint ventures, and business-technology forecasting.

Recent projects:

Thought Leadership/Business Intelligence: Writing a white paper for a media and digital technology investment bank mapping out the online display advertising exchange ecosystem looking forwards: ad exchanges, demand side platforms, yield managers, real time bidding, data exchanges...

Social Media: A Twitter social media mining/social influence marketing project for the CMO and Corporate Marketing of a Fortune 500 IT software and services company. Including discovering new key influencers, building an influencer social graph, Twitter monitoring, and obtaining retweet insights. Used social network analysis/visualization and social media monitoring software.

Market and Supplier Screening/Technology Pre-Procurement Due Diligence: A number of financial technology and trading infrastructure sourcing projects assisting a European management consultancy advising various of their financial services clients.

For 20+ years I have been a technology market/industry and emerging technologies specialist, and consultant to the consultants (McKinsey, Boston Consulting Group, Accenture, EDS...). Advised venture capital, private equity, media, marketing, events, technology, Fortune 500, FTSE 100, startups, and publishing businesses. And been involved in implementing and advising on CRM and Financial Sector technology.

Since 1994 I have provided technology market insight, market screening, due diligence, and advisory services to client engagement teams at a top-tier global strategy consulting firm.
