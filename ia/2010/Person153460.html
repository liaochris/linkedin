Nick Hahn’s Summary

Career Summary
Energetic, seasoned financial executive with integrity, passion, and proven leadership ability to orchestrate organizational change and direct performance enhancement initiatives. Visionary leader with ability to conceptualize and organize enterprise-wide initiatives, devise and implement strategic plans, and monitor and deliver results in a entrepreneurial manner. Twenty-six years of broad financial management experience in commercial, retail and mortgage banking, financial services and consumer products industries with experience in organization change management, bank and investor relations, strategic planning, budgeting, administration, mergers and acquisitions, SEC and regulatory reporting, inventory management and point-of-sale, and federal and state taxes. Big Four experience bringing business solutions to medium and large companies. M.B.A., University of Chicago, Registered C.P.A., State of Illinois

Goals & Objective
* Align my background as a senior financial executive and ability to lead people to achieve enterprise goals with an organization that strives each day to be uniquely qualified to serve its employees, customers, and owners, is tirelessly dedicated to continuous improvement and its values, and is committed to being the most successful organization of its kind in the eyes of those it serves and its employees for a long period of time.

* To become a senior executive where I am responsible for leading a group of people to develop innovative ways to build and implement the business model, maximize profitability, and groom leaders, preferably as a Chief Financial Officer, Chief Operating Officer, or Chief Administrative Officer.

* Secure executive position with a company in the following target industries: financial services, including retail and commercial banking, leasing, mortgage banking, brokerage, funds and investment management, insurance, and trust, consumer products with retail distribution channels.
