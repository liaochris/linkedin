Norman Harris’s Summary

HR professional with extensive experience in recruiting operations. Source using various mechanisms that include, but no limited to, internet research, name generation, data mining, and cold calling. Experience with qualifying candidates. In my current role it is my responsibility to source and identify top talent for potential mid-career to executive positions for the Bank.

I like the challenge of sourcing top talent and assisting that talent find their niche within a company. It's especially gratifying for me when I am able to place a candidate with unique skills. In my role with the bank I source talent to fill roles within our Customer Service and Solutions group - co-located in more than 40 locations in the US.

I enjoy being creative and sharing best practices. I like partnering with others to build sourcing strategies, connecting with and building and deepening relationships with colleges,community organizations, managers, and other recruiters. I created a blog on our Intranet which has become a repository of up-to-date information addressing recruiting trends and sourcing techniques and best practices.

I also involve myself in special projects positioning me as a better business partner and enables me to add value to the team and company.
