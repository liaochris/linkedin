Chris Mathan’s Summary

Chris Mathan is a seasoned designer and photographer with 28 years of experience. An award-winning graphic/website designer and marketing specialist, she has worked on Madison Avenue and in London for prestigious design firms including Chermayeff & Geismar, Knoll International and Peter Saville Associates. Based in Maine for the past 16 years, she operates The Sportsman’s Cabinet. Geared to the shooting sports and outdoor marketplace, The Sportsman’s Cabinet collaborates with a nationwide network of copywriters, photographers, illustrators and new media specialists to produce compelling, results-driven marketing.

Location: Gorham, ME

* Marketing, consultation for outdoor businesses
* Corporate identity, print collateral
• Print advertising, media buying
* Website development
* Outdoor photography, art direction of photography


Chris created and along with Mazie Davis of Davis Kennels in Minter, Alabama, is editor to the online publication about pointer & setter field trials called Strideaway.

Chris Mathan’s Specialties:

28 years of design (graphic, website), marketing, outdoor photography, art direction of photography experience. First-hand knowledge of hunting, bird dogs, field trials, conservation, horses, wing shooting, fishing and equestrian sports and related outdoor activities.

Chris Mathan’s Experience

  • Co-creator, Editor

    Strideaway

    (Sports industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Strideaway is a non-profit online publication dedicated to and promoting the great sport of American Field Trials and the dogs that compete in them. The objective is to present the voices and ideas of experienced trainers, handlers, breeders and other knowledgeable participants whether they are involved with All Age or Shooting Dogs, horseback or walking trials with particular emphasis on wild bird trials and the dogs that compete in them. We will be adding interviews, articles, stories and quotes on a regular basis and hope to provide an expanding source of information relevant to field trials.

    Strideaway was founded in 2008 by Mazie Davis and Chris Mathan
