Jacob Minkoff’s Summary

Jacob Minkoff is a professional video game designer at Naughty Dog Inc in Santa Monica, California, USA.

Jacob graduated with a BFA from the School of Visual Arts, New York where he trained in film and visual effects. He was one of the three founding members of Blue Omega Entertainment and worked there as Lead Designer on the multiplatform Unreal Engine game Damnation for 5 years until the company's dissolution in 2009. Following his departure from Blue Omega, Jacob joined Naughty Dog where he designed and developed the acclaimed Train and Convoy levels (among others) for the hit game Uncharted 2: Among Thieves.

He has been interviewed by dozens of gaming publications and has been featured in videos on such media outlets as G4, Coin-Op TV, Machinema.com, and Gamespot.

As Game Designer, he uses his technical and artistic knowledge of the cinematic medium to efficiently communicate his ideas to other members of his team; regularly prototyping system designs using film visual effects tools. In addition, he designs and scripts levels, from prototype to completion.

Jacob has lived all around the world, and spent time immersing himself in different cultures and countries -- from his home country of the United States, to Israel, to Japan – and is passionate about exploring the boundaries of his knowledge. His goal is simple: to work with great people and make great games.

3D modeling (MAX / Maya), level layout and scripting, Unreal Engine, 2D compositing and animation (motion-graphics), GDD development, web-design
