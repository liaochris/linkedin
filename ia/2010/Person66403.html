Eric Fong’s Summary

Marketing professional with over 15 years of experience in Event Marketing, Exhibits, Marketing Communications and Commercial Operations for multiple industries including biopharmaceutical, technology and professional sports. Proven track record of high attention to detail and optimizing return on investment. Ability to excel as an individual contributor or hire/develop talent and create high-performance teams that deliver exceptional results and exceed expectations.

Eric Fong’s Specialties:

Event marketing, tradeshows and exhibits, direct marketing, advertising, media planning, video production, product launches, sales incentive programs, attendee acquisition, project management, PhRMA Code, corporate compliance, teambuilding, budget management, people management, strategic planning, vendor management.

Eric Fong’s Experience

  • Associate Director, Commercial Services

    Gilead Sciences, Inc.

    (Public Company; GILD; Biotechnology industry)

    2004 — 2009 (5 years )

    Created, built and led a best in class Commercial Operations department that supported the HIV, Hepatitis and Cardiovascular franchises with professional Event Marketing, in-house Graphic Design solutions and Vendor Management. Started with 22 events in 2004 and grew the business capabilities to successfully produce over 120 events (scientific conferences, exhibits, product launches, advisory boards, executive programs, sales incentive programs and national sales meetings) and over 350 graphic design and print-buying projects annually. Hired, managed and motivated eight employees. Created the department vision, implemented standard operating procedures, crafted strategic plans, developed efficiencies and built partnerships that gave Gilead a competitive advantage.

  • Director of Marketing

    MediaLive International

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1995 — 2004 (9 years )

    Managed all brand marketing, attendee and exhibitor marketing campaigns (audience acquisition) for 40,000 attendees and 350 exhibitors at Seybold Seminars bi-annual technology conferences and trade shows. Responsibilities included event P&L, direct mail, local advertising (magazine, newspaper, radio & outdoor), online marketing, web & email campaigns, VIP programs and strategic partnerships. Directly supervised five employees. Built strategic partnerships that resulted in over $1.5M dollars in annual savings, and developed sponsorship agreements with major technology companies that netted over $26M in sales. Started as a Marketing Coordinator in 1995 and eventually earned the position of Marketing Director in 2000.

    MediaLive Int'l is formerly Key3Media, Softbank Forums and ZD Events - producers of COMDEX, Interop and JavaOne.

Provided season-ticket holder support and customer relations assistance to attendees during Warrior home games.

  • Account Manager
