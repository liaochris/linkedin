Paul Woods’s Summary

My name is Paul and I am the Lead Developer of Paul Woods Consulting Ltd located near Vancouver Canada. I provide project development support to the local governmental organizations including system analysis, architectural design and long term sustainability support. My recent clients include the Real Estate Foundation of BC, Richmond Hospital and St Paul's Hospital in downtown Vancouver. My work supplements these organizations internally funded projects with web applications, database design, and enhanced reporting tools. I have been designing and building web applications and back end support systems since my first project with Telus in 1999.
