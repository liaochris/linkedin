Kirill Makharinsky’s Summary

Dynamic and analytical entrepreneur focused on use of data and technology to create value in high growth industries. Currently cofounder of YouNoodle, developing the next generation of data products for the emerging business industry

I also advise early stage companies on product/market fit and mid stage companies on data and analytics to optimize growth.

kirill@makharinsky.com

--

Kirill is the co-founder of YouNoodle Inc, the startup innovation company. YouNoodle has the richest database of emerging businesses worldwide and its products have been used by over 25,000 startups with a community of over 400,000 entrepreneurs. YouNoodle is funded by Peter Thiel, The Founders Fund and Max Levchin.

He previously managed analytics and metrics at Slide Inc, the largest personal media network in the world. Prior to Slide, Kirill had been established as the #1 Graduate in the UK based on his work while at Oxford University.

Kirill was also the founding member and President of Oxford Entrepreneurs (the largest student enterprise club in Europe) and graduated from Oxford University with a double first in Applied Mathematics.
