Goliath Marketing & Advertising…
Is a division of David & Goliath, Inc. it was created after the companies Founder & CEO, Mia Moore spent years working in online marketing and offline marketing and developing advanced skills in Affiliate Marketing, Web design, Social Media, Email Marketing, PR and Search Marketing for several start - ups and Inc. 500 corporations such as Zappos.com, Shoes.com, Lamps Plus and PartnerWeekly, Affiliate Partners LLC, and University of Nevada Cooperative Extension – (Chef for Kids non-profit Organization).

After much success marketing e-commerce retail programs and Lead Generation programs and seeing the growth of company sales to 35% -40% of overall sales under her management, it was time to create her own Marketing & Advertising Agency, which currently offers (OPM – Outsourced Program Management) for Affiliate Program or Search Marketing Campaigns. We work with small start-ups to Inc. 500 companies. Goliath specializes in managing Ecommerce retail Affiliate Marketing Programs. However, we offer many other Marketing services and will continue to expand the services offered.
