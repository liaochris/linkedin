Donna Williams’s Summary

Experienced sales professional working with clients to buy, sell, or rent residential properties. Specialize in selling homes in my local community (Prince William County and surrounding areas) by utilizing a proven, successful marketing system - incorporating online, direct mail, and social events.

Provide sellers with online virtual tours, multiple website exposure, sales brochures, open houses, advertisements, and participation in multiple listing services. Provide buyers with lists of properties that are compatible with their needs and financial resources, coordinate appointments to show homes, and comprehensive neighborhood data.

Ensure an ongoing flow of communication with other agents, buyers, sellers, renters, mortgage officers, title personnel and attorneys involved in the home buying and selling process. Negotiate the best possible contract for clients. Work with home inspectors and appraisers to determine any and all actions that need to be taken prior to closing. Ensure that all terms of the contract are met prior to closing.

My education goals are to achieve the NAR's Green and ABR Designations by the end of 2010.

My health goal is to be able to jog a 5K by the end of 2010.
