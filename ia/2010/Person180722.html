Sally Huss’s Summary

Sally Huss has been an artist all of her life. She is a native Californian who graduated from USC with a degree in Fine Arts. At the same time Sally was developing her art, she was honing her tennis skills. She became National Junior Champion, a member of the US Wightman Cup Team and a semi-finalist at Wimbledon. As a skillful tennis player, Huss later taught her special “Zen” tennis to celebrity clients such as Barbra Streisand, Joel Gray and Jill St. John. Sally also worked in the entertainment industry first as an assistant to Samuel Goldwyn, Jr. then as manager of Paul Simon’s music publishing company.

In the 1980’s with the help of her husband Marv, former head of advertising and promotion for Hallmark Cards, Sally returned to art and writing in a big way. Today Sally Huss is considered one of the leading artist/designers in the country. All of her art is happy in nature and free-spirited in style. Her theme, “If you wait to be happy, you will wait forever. If you are happy now, you will be happy forever,” plays out in everything she creates – from books to hats. Her art dances across thousands of products including signature lines of clothing, purses, children’s books and even baby bibs. Her paintings and gift art brighten walls and uplift hearts in homes, office buildings and hotels, including the lobby of the famous La Valencia Hotel in La Jolla. Some of her art can be found in the homes of Larry Hagman, Joel Grey, Kirk Douglas and Jack Jones. Hillary Clinton also has an original Huss, entitled “A Happy Child”, created for her 50th birthday celebration. Her most recent creation, Happy Musings, is a King Features syndicated newspaper feature combining her charming art with a whimsical life thought. Sally’s art and products are available on her Web site.
