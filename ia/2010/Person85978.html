John Mukadi’s Summary

- Created a friendly climate between Dr Congolese and Zambian to visit each others without fear by organizing in Zambia, Congolese night shows and in DR Congo , Zambian night shows, where musicians, models and food from both countries were displayed and travellers getting discounts at the majors hotels in Zambia and DR Congo, to promote the increase of tourism between the 2 countries.

- Recipient of the Most Entreprising Member of Management for Swift Group Dubai in 1999 , for huge achievement during the same year.

- From nothing, managed to set up a highly performant mining consultancy company, International Transit Business, for all information about business in DR Congo , Zambia and Tanzania. Mining and geological explorations with qualified and trained geologists.
Now using hand-diggers , with the strong partnership of Emak and CMKK , the two cooperatives of artisans miners , ITB is producing monthly an average of hundred metric tons of tin ore ( Cassiterite) at min 60% Tin content , twenty metric tons of tentalum ore ( Coltan ) at min 20% Ta content, two hundred metric tons of copper ore ( Malachite ) at a min 20% Cu content and sixty metric tons of cobalt ore ( Heterogenit ) at a min 4% Co content. ITB can , as per request supply gemstones ( Emeralds, rubis, aquamarine, topaze , green tourmaline etc..) , and raw gold , diamond also available per special request.
Our goal is to find committed partners to build metal processing plants in DR Congo, Zambia and Tanzania, to add value at the raw material , generally exported by artisan miners. Our solution is easy and cheat to set up modular electrowinning metals metal processing plants transportable from site to site, to be operated by corperatives of artisans miners, a way to create permanent jobs for them, by empowering them and create a middle class businessmen and industrial.
