Miki Davis’s Summary

My goal is not to just help people buy and sell homes in Raleigh, but to provide a service that incorporates my knowledge, professionalism, enthusiasm and high level of customer service so you will have a smooth and well coordinated move. I have personally bought and sold homes with and without relocation company involvement and I understand the process. I pride myself in not just listening, but in hearing the needs of those I represent in buying or selling a home in Raleigh and the surrounding areas. Having walked in the shoes of those relocating with work and family, I understand the details, emotions and logistics that a family goes through. I am here to assist you in finding and/or selling a home and in helping you obtain the resources and connections needed for you and your family to have a smooth transition.

I have 10+ years experience in representing others in buying and selling homes and have specialized in relocation. I am certified by multiple corporate relocation companies and can work with buyers and sellers that are relocating through their employment. Anyone moving with their company can simply request my representation in the Raleigh area through their relocation counselor and that will start the process for my participation in representing them when buying or selling.

For those that are facing an uncertain market and have to consider options other than selling, I can assist with full service property management. Not just manage from a Broker capacity. Please contact me for more information if you have property management needs for your primary or investment property.

Any referrals sent are greatly desired and appreciated. I can help you find a quality agent in other areas throughout the world. This is income producing, but does not cost the consumer a dime more than they would typically spend in buying or selling. It also helps assure a quality agent will be assisting you.

Call anytime you have any real estate needs!
