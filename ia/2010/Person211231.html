Victor Hurdle’s Summary

I have worked in the Financial Services Industry for 10 years with 5 years experience in the Currency Management field as a Cash Operations Supervisor for SEI Private Trust Company playing an integral role in the daily operation of Cash Services Team interacting with internal and external clients. Additionally, I created the Cash Technology team where I was responsible for production readiness, change management and incident management of our domestic currency platform. I also was responsible for the Private Trust Company's Anti Money Laundering processing. In February 2006, I became a Business Analyst for supporting production deployment, development, support, process enhancement, and operational design of upcoming releases of our Anti-Money Laundering and Compliance programs. In March 2008, I assumed my newest role with in the organization as a Human Resources and Personal Development Partner within the SEI Global Operations Business Services involved in all aspects of recruiting and employee development.


Prior to SEI, I was a HR Employment Specialist for GPU Energy focusing on employee focused initiatives such as dispute resolution, non-harassment policies, and relocation benefits. In 1998, assisted in the launch of the SAP/HR module.
