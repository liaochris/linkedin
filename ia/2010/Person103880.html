Jonathan Levy’s Summary

I am a California attorney, who is highly motivated and ready to bring an unparalleled level of enthusiasm and care to every aspect of legal practice.

As an attorney I have had the opportunity to take part in a wide array of legal matters including, but not limited to: general municipal law, premises liability/tort claims, land acquisition/regulation, election law, employment law, labor law, an assortment of First Amendment issues, and administrative licensing disputes.

While clerking for the U.S. Attorney’s Office in Sacramento, California, I acquired a familiarity with federal practice and custom. I prepared indictments, suppression motions, trial briefs, settlement agreements and habeas responses.

I have experience in all aspects of pre-trial litigation including motion drafting, depositions (offense and defense) and mediation sessions. My court room experience extends to arguing motions in open court and in chambers. Maturity, professionalism, organization, approachability, and dedication are the standards I hold myself to at all times.

Connecting all of my experience, the unifying goal has been to practice with the highest level of ethics and professional responsibility.
