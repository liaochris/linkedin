Translation Back Office’s Summary

Translation Back Office currently works with leading translation agencies from around the world, in locations as distant as Boston and Brussels and Osaka and Oslo.

We have an in house team of over 35 experienced university-educated linguists, 12 senior translators and editors and 12 project managers and support staff to ensure that the needs of our clients are always met in a timely fashion.

Translation Back Office complements its in house operations with a highly flexible pool of over 50 freelancers that work on projects for the company on a daily basis. spanishbackoffice SA also works with a network of linguists in other countries, such as Brazil,Mexico, Spain,
and Puerto Rico.

Our office places considerable emphasis on ongoing training and development and all linguists are skilled with the full MS Office suite, as well as CAT tools such as:
SDL TRADOS 2007, Alchemy Catalyst 8, Transit XV, Dejavú, Wordfast, SDL Passolo 2009, SDL Synergy...

Translation Back Office provide DTP services on both Windows and Mac platforms, including Quark, InDesign, PhotoShop, Illustrator, PageMaker, and Publisher.

Translation Back Office’s Specialties:

Translation, Editing, Proofreading and Quality control by our Quality Assurance Department. Expertise Areas: Business & Finance, Marketing & Advertising, Medical & Health Care, Technical, Legal, Employment & Insurance, IT & Web Sites.
