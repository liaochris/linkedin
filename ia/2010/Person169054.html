  • Chief Executive Officer

    Dr Miracle's

    (Privately Held; Consumer Goods industry)

    2010 — Present (1 year )

  • Interim President

    Reynolds Consumer Products, a Rank Group portfolio company

    (Consumer Goods industry)

    2008 — 2009 (1 year )

    Led the restructuring of this $900MM global business after its sale to a Private Equity firm.

    • Improved EBITDA run rate 40% by:
    - Successfully led a highly tenured workforce through the transition from Alcoa to Rank group while simultaneously restructuring headquarter expenses and reducing headcount 10%. This initiative reduced costs by $3MM.
    - Reduced costs over $15MM by restructuring the manufacturing and distribution footprint. Reduced two union manufacturing facilities into one.
    - Established and executed a two part sustainability initiative that reduced the core product package by a third resulting in easier handling by consumer, supply chain savings of $6MM per year while launching a 100% Recycled Aluminum product projected to contribute $30MM in incremental revenue.
    • Rebalanced portfolio to accelerate expansion and growth of higher margin product platforms while optimizing returns on core products through elimination and redeployment of non-productive trade spend.
    • Created vision for the Reynolds Incubation Lab and developed a 3 year plan for strategic transformation of the division to further ignite growth thru innovation. Focused on rapid development of strategic new products, quick-to-market execution, with low risk and modest capital investment.

  • Chief Marketing Officer
