Dave Rooney’s Summary

I have worked on online projects in one capacity or another since graduating and during college for the past 4 years working on the design, content, functionality and promotion of justroutes.com, aib.ie, dcusnowboarding.com and skoool.ie among others.

I have much experience in dealing with the rigours of managing a brand online while being mindful of the constraints imposed by both legal and regulatory compliance. I believe my experience in promoting both financial and educational websites at different age groups and sections of the population gives me a good insight into how wide and varied online marketing campaigns can be.

I firmly believe the future of online advertising lies in local campaigns targeting user interests and their location, marrying users with the products and services that they need and want.
