Rob Stansfield’s Summary

Twenty years of Information Technology leadership in Pharmaceutical R&D, after ten years research in crystallography. In the course of these two careers I have lived and worked in the UK, in France, and in the USA. My objective has been consistent - to work collaboratively among the disciplines of scientific research that have passionately interested and inspired me, to continually learn, and to further my knowledge, experience, and ability.

Rob Stansfield’s Specialties:

IT strategy and program management, business analysis, international team building, facilitation, people development, native English, fluent French, dual citizenship UK/USA.
