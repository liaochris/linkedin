Cory Van Arsdale’s Summary

24 years experience as a CEO, business development and revenue generation professional, general manager, acquisitions integration specialist and intellectual property/technology transactions lawyer.

I close deals. I have experience with all aspects of transactions, from writing the contract, knowing the underlying law for the transaction, developing the revenue strategy, creating the partner framework, negotiating the deal, closing it, managing the post-deal partner relationship and smoothing out problems big and small in a revenue and partner ecosystem.

I recruit, develop and manage extremely healthy and productive organizations. I have managed from 2 to 200 people, hiring and developing some amazing individuals comprising some extremely high performing teams. I have worked in the smallest startups and some of the world's largest corporations.

I work across industries, borders and cultures. In my career I consistently shown an ability to learn new technologies, businesses and cultures. My experience ranges from advanced technology development transactions to media advertising to online business to gaming; from Silicon Valley to L.A./New York to Europe and Japan.

I deal with ambiguity and get results. I have been in the middle of several technology, media, gaming and other initiatives and shown consistently that I can develop business models, partner frameworks, transaction models and revenue.

Cory Van Arsdale’s Specialties:

Online business, especially in the areas of advertising sales strategy, business development, revenue generation, partnerships and business model creation.

Legal focus on technology transactions, intellectual property, antitrust and litigation settlement.

Acquisition integration skills include pre-deal advice and post-mortem analysis of company acquisition integration and transition.
