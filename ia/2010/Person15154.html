I have been licensed by New York State since 2002. I have successfully worked with both sellers and buyers of residential real estate. Besides my love & knowledge of "the Gold Coast" North Shore of Long Island, New York, I am fueled by my passion to consummate the "deal."

As a "Seller's Agent" I represent the seller-client's best interest. I assist each client when selling a property by providing straightforward advice, objective counseling and I use my deep understanding of the market. I am strategic and creative with my marketing and advertise the property in the appropriate written publication. I utilize the appropriate internet tools, such as Web sites and Social Media platforms that will gain the best exposure. When I am negotiating, I utilize my legal skills to attain the best price and terms for the client.

As "Buyer's Agent" (Certified Buyer Representative (CBR)) I represent the buyer-client’s best interest. I assist each client in purchasing a property by providing straightforward advice, objective counseling, full disclosure and verification of all pertinent details of any property of interest. I utilize my legal skills to attain the best price and terms for the client.

Specialty: Luxury residences, new construction & land parcels throughout the North Shore Communities of Bayville, Brookville, Centre Island, Cold Spring Harbor, Cove Neck, East Norwich, Glen Cove, Glen Head, Glenwood Landing, Jericho, Kings Point, Lattingtown, Laurel Hollow, Llyod Neck, Locust Valley, Matinecock,Mill Neck,Muttontown, Old Brookville,Old Westbury,Oyster Bay, Oyster Bay Cove, Sands Point,Sea Cliff, Syosset & Upper Brookville.
