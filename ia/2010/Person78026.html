Gregory M. Smith’s Summary

I just finished up a four-month stint as a jack-of-all-trades with the U.S. Census Bureau, serving as fingerprint clerk, administrative assistant, human resources/payroll clerk and assistant supply clerk. I handled daily payroll and processed HR changes for personnel files for more than 1300 office and field staff.

I assisted in re-fingerprinting, via LiveScan Technology, more than 150 employees to meet FBI and federal background check procedures. I also received, inventoried, maintained and distributed supplies to field staff, handled incoming and outgoing distribution of Fedex and UPS, and handled proper storage and disposal of sensitive personal information.

I am also a freelance writer, covering black college sports, for Examiner.com.

Training Coordinator/Instructor with professional education in Hazard Awareness and Dangerous Goods Train-the-Trainer with American Airlines.

Human Resources assistant with experience in maintaining and updating physical and computer employee databases.

Copy editor and staff writer with more than 20 years of experience in writing, layout, editing and proofreading for magazines and newspapers, including the Fort Worth Star-Telegram.
