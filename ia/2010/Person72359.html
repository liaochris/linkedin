Gad J Meir’s Summary

My extensive experience in the computer industry since 1976 covers many fields which include: writing code in most of the known computer languages, managing software and deployment projects, training and consulting in many areas of computer industry and technology, locating and identifying faults in development or deployment processes/procedures, analyzing production systems for possible points of failure, analyzing and debugging faulty systems in all stages of the project ,envisioning, planning, development, deployment and production.
My main goal is to be called in to accompany or guide projects and, by using my extensive hands-on experience, to pre-empt problems as early as possible in the process, and to find creative practical solutions to problems which can’t be pre-empted.

Gad J Meir’s Specialties:

My specialties include: detecting errors and problems in processes, deploying new Microsoft technologies, solving Interoperability problems involving Microsoft and non Microsoft technologies, adding instrumentation to software, preparing for and performing production debugging.
