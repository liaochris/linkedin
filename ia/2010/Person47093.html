Daniel Dessinger’s Summary

Daniel got his start in social media by creating and moderating online communities via MSN Groups in 2000. Since 2003, Daniel has provided professional online marketing, copywriting, PR, and reputation management services to more than two dozen clients.

Starting as a copywriter, Daniel served more than a dozen additional satisfied clients. He took the advice of a friend and diversified his skill set into SEO. From SEO he springboarded into Social Media Marketing and Online Reputation Management.

He helps clients understand the value of online reputation management and Customer Relationship Management via blogs, forums, CGC websites, press releases, and email marketing. He brings passionate expertise to B2B and B2C interactive marketing.
