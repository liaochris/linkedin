Yamilet Ramirez-Morgan’s Summary

Born in Bogotá, Colombia and raised in Westchester, NY I now live with my husband in Fort Lauderdale, FL, where MPS is headquartered. I went to school in New York and graduated from Fordham University with a Bachelor of Science. I moved to South Florida in 2001 and it was then where my career in the electronic payments industry began. Prior to my current position, I created and led a very successful national sales team that focused on turn-key electronic payment solutions for the quick service restaurant segment.

In July 2005 I graduated with an MBA in Technology Management from the University of Phoenix. After graduation my entrepreneurial spirit took off and by early 2006 I created and established MPS with the goal of surrounding myself with what I consider today to be the “WINNING” team. I am conscious every day that without my management team, nothing would be possible. I am very lucky and blessed to have the opportunity to share my life with what I consider to be the brightest minds and giving souls around. I strongly believe in the power of multiple positive minds visualizing a mutual goal and making it happen by mutual determination.

"What this power is, I cannot say. All I know is that it exists...and it becomes available only when you are in that state of mind in which you know exactly what you want...and are fully determined not to quit until you get it." -Alexander Graham Bell
