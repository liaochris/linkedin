Wesley F. Lewis’s Summary

Wesley possesses seven solid years of experience in devising attractive, user-friendly, and compliant web solutions for clients ranging from the entertainment business to federal agencies. During these seven years, he has directly interacted with clients from project inception to closeout, involving himself with each and every phase of the software development lifecycle to ensure the design of a solid product. Wesley has extensive work experience with CSS, HTML, and JavaScript, as well as intermediate skills with PHP, ASP, and front-end JSF components. He is knowledgeable in all facets of the latest versions of the Adobe Master Suite (Adobe Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Lightroom, Flash, AfterEffects). Wesley has gained substantial experience utilizing BOBBY as well as conducting his own manual 508 compliance testing. During his tenure with SAIC, he has also familiarized himself with various development tools, such as JDeveloper and Eclipse.

While not at work, Wesley can be found working on his own web development portfolio/tech blog, http://www.wflewis.net. Yes, it just so happens that his career and his primary hobby are (and have always been) one in the same! Because of this, he is constantly honing his craft and picking up new skills to implement at the office.

Wesley also possesses a passion for IT project management. He has gotten his feet wet with several small development/maintenance releases at his current position, and is ultimately looking to obtain the CAPM/PMP certification.
