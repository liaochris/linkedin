David Szafranski’s Summary

A result oriented marketing executive with extensive experience in business and consumer marketing. Extremely proficient in managing the marketing process of strategic planning, budgeting and forecasting, marketing intelligence, advertising, sales promotion, e-commerce, product development, pricing, public relations and community development.

David Szafranski managed the marketing function for the Western Region of Comerica Bank including the national marketing of the bank’s Small Business Administration (SBA) division. He managed the marketing and communications efforts of four bank acquisitions. Arizona regional retail sales manager of thirteen banking centers.

Managed the negotiations, naming rights and sponsorship relationships with numerous sports and business properties to include, San Jose Sharks, Phoenix Coyotes, Oakland Athletics, San Jose Giants, Comerica Bank Challenger (USTA), Comerica Bank Arizona Companies to Watch, Comerica Bank Economic Forecast Conference and other numerous local events.

The work environment must be challenging, exciting and fun. Ultimately, accomplishments are the collective results of bright and driven individuals who believe in the vision set forth by management.
