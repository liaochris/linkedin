Michiel de Kloet’s Summary

The 1st of September 2008 I started my first full-time job, which I got offered by the company where I was doing my Management Training: Sheraton La Caleta Resort & Spa, located on Tenerife (Canary Islands). On the 26th of September of 2009 I officially graduated from the Hotelschool The Hague, where I started in January 2003.

During my studying period I have been working in luxury restaurants in The Hague, which gave me the opportunity to work with the highest standards of hospitality. For my practical release in 2004 I have been working at the Front Office of Le Meridien Barcelona.

As a student I have put effort in the Student Association La Confrérie, which is connected to the Hotelschool The Hague. As elected Event Coordinator of Board year 2006 I was responsible for all events that took place, such as inauguration diners, the annual gala, the beach volleyball tournament and a stage play that is performed every year. The most memorable moment for me was the moment I won the Hospitality Innovations Award at the European Fine Food Fair in January 2006, together with Michiel Nelissen, Dennis Maaswinkel, Alex Luiten & Felix Lepoutre.
