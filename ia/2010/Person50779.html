David Gilbert’s Summary

Research Fellow
Rainforest Action Network - 2009
Developed three case studies of the ecological and social impacts of palm oil plantation development in Indonesia and Papua New Guinea. Currently developing an analysis of Sinar Mas' APP pulp and paper operations in Sumatra. Tasks include writing, identifying and connecting with local NGO’s in Indonesia, interviewing affected local communities in Indonesia, and compiling and summarizing governmental information on industry.

Consultant
Amazon Watch - 2009
Created communications materials and issue briefs on a range of indigenous rights and environmental campaigns, including 'Chevron Toxico' and support for the victims of the 'Bagua Disaster' in Peru.

Researcher
National Geographic Young Explorer
Documenting the environmental and social impacts of large-scale oil palm development and illegal logging in Aceh, Indonesia
