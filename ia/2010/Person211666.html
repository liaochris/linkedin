Carol Lane’s Summary

Video Production Services is a full service production company, boutique-style. My partner and I started the company in 1996. Our motto ...award-winning creativity with a personal touch. We have a following of satisfied repeat clients and a lot of our business is by referral.

I have been involved in TV and video production almost since the day I stepped out of college. I've done everything from pulling cables, to writing and recording voice-overs,script writing, shooting,directing and editing projects. I love the work I do; pretty much all aspects of production. Creativity keeps me going and I hope to keep producing good work.
