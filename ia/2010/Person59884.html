Dr. Jen Faber’s Summary

About Chiro On the Go:

A team of like-minded practitioners, reinventing health and wellness throughout the Washington, DC metropolitan region. Chiro On the Go is the only company of its kind servicing this area through accessible, affordable and comprehensive chiropractic care.

Chiro On the Go is the logical partner for achieving results. Our services offer direct access to physicians specializing in the treatment of repetitive stress injuries amongst other work and lifestyle enabled concerns. Our partnerships with members help to realize dramatic long-term savings.

We know that time is valuable as is the investment made in improved health and lifestyle. This is why we bring our wellness services (care, consulting and education) to members' doorsteps. Our approach is a blend of comprehensive evaluations and manual therapies to address health concerns.

Dr. Jen Faber’s Specialties:

Work- and Sport-Injury Rehabilitation, Preventative Health Care and Wellness Solutions, Workplace Ergonomics
