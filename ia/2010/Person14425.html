Annie Evett’s Summary

I co-authored “Reclaim” a survival guide for couples, am currently co-authoring “The Astonishing Adventures of Captain Juan”, an historical fictional ongoing online adventure series and write weekly columns at Type A Mom and Write Anything.

I draw on years as a teacher and time in the corporate field to bring life experience to my eclectic style of creativity in both writing and artwork. Having scribbled and sketched in the margins throughout school, university and in business meetings, I felt it time to bring my images to light. I have written speculative science fiction, feminist literature, romance, adventure and magazine articles and illustrated for magazines with my line drawings.

I explore themes in mothering, feminism, spirituality and sharing my journey as a woman and mother in all my outlets of creativity.
