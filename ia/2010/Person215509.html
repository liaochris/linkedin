William Gunnar’s Summary

I am a connector, maven, and salesman in the industrial coatings marketplace, a platform for industrial coating companies to build lasting relationships with engineers, product and machine designers, equipment manufacturers across the globe.

Industrial Coatings World.com is a free, easy access route to anyone seeking industrial coatings to improve surface performance.

Our staff, contributing writers, and sponsors are out there everyday, "in-the-trenches," with effective, practical solutions to your toughest surface challenges.

Stop searching. Start finding.

We expect Industrial Coatings World.com to quickly become a well-known, trusted, shared, and indispensable destination on the web.
