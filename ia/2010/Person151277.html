Nancy Bailey’s Summary

President and Rainmaker at Hire Profile, Inc., Roswell, GA. I started my career in LA in agency account management. In 1995 moved to Atlanta to start a new division of a company to recruit high-end creative talent. After seven years of recruiting under my belt, I founded the creative staffing and recruiting firm, Hire Profile in '02. Hire Profile has a team of talent and experienced industry professionals that understand the advertising, marketing and design industry and provide top quality resources to meet marketing objectives through the use of freelancers, new hires and creative agencies. By staying focused on our creative niche we are very skilled at matching our clients' jobs, projects and opportunities to the best resources.

Hire Profile is a certified Women Owned Business (WEBNC)
