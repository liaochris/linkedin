Darren Lee’s Summary

Darren Lee spearheads Intrivent Global - an Internet R&D company that won many awards in 2007 that brought his team to half way across the world from Geneva to Philippines. Some of his prominent involvement in ICT includes representing Malaysia at the 2007 UNGAID Youth and ICT Forum in Switzerland and other projects such as collaboration with UNICEF Malaysia to launch a web portal for urban youth or even leading the content development team for Xfresh – the online teen community brand when he was in ASTRO. He is also currently pursuing other promising Web 2.0 projects such as AdExcel.com.

Darren Lee holds a BSc (Hons) Degree in Computing majoring in Internet Technology from University of Staffordshire. Also, his work experiences span from the marketing line to being a professional trainer; and finally spent about four years in Malaysia’s biggest satellite media broadcasting company at ASTRO.

More about Darren >>

In Astro, Darren Lee led the content development team for Xfresh, a teen community brand initiated by AMP Radio Networks. Back then, Xfresh was a dynamic brand that had multiple media platforms such as Xfresh Online (the official teen community website), Xfresh FM (the official radio station that supports 100% local music), Xfresh TV (a teen review show on Astro), Xfresh Street Surfers and Xfresh Mobile. During his service at Xfresh, Darren Lee was there to assist and advocate contacts and branding initiatives to deliver high impact results for the Xfresh Group.

Darren Lee had also contributed his expertise with a digital media company involving new media projects that handles Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) business and mobile content applications.
