Sheree Lasher, PHR’s Summary

Human Resources Recruiting:
Providing accomplished HR professionals

Your human resources team plays an integral role in your day-to-day business operations—from the caliber of talent you hire to the processes you employ to manage, retain and develop these valuable resources.

When it’s time to hire new HR talent, The Mergis Group will deliver the accomplished human resources professionals you need to be successful.

Built on more than 30 years of experience, our recruiters have extensive knowledge and career experience in the HR field. They are well-networked both locally and nationally to better assist the HR communities they serve, and to keep up with the latest industry trends.

The Mergis Group has proven experience and success assisting businesses nationwide find the top talent they need in the field of Human Resources. We place HR professionals in a diverse portfolio of clients ranging from Fortune 1000 companies to small and mid-sized businesses spanning a multitude of industries.

The Mergis Group specializes on HR positions that range from the junior level to the executive level in both direct-hire and project-based needs.

Our expertise includes:

• Compensation
• Benefits
• Employee & Labor Relations
• HR Management
• Talent Acquisition & Management
• HRIS
• Organizational Development
• Recruiting
• Training & Development
• HR Administration

To obtain more information or to secure additional direct-hire staff and/or project-based employees, please feel free to contact me at 301-634-2540 or shereelasher@mergisgroup.com.
