Michelle Lanter Smith’s Summary

Thanks for visiting my Summary Page. My passion is marketing. It's what I love to teach, what I love to do, and how I love to make a difference in the lives of people I connect with in my day-to-day interactions. During my 20+ year career, my coupling of marketing strategy with creativity, has enabled me to help many people and companies figure out how to best market people, products, and ideas. Sometimes I'm blessed to work with the big brands--like IBM, McDonald's, American Family Insurance; other times I'm humbled by the hard work and perseverance of small business owners and start-ups. And, sometimes I'm able to help in my own community, working with non-profit groups, like local schools and women support groups.

One of my most favorite things is to engage and fall in love with an audience. Lecturing about marketing allows me to do this; but I'm also able to reach many through strategic marketing consulting. One of my current projects at Brillante Multicultural Marketing Group, the Business Accelerator Program, in which I developed and now manage, allows me to do both. As an agency focusing on growing, ethnic and specialty markets, we develop value-based campaigns that deliver really value to the end-user. Our award winning campaign, the Business Accelerator Program, offers free small business education and coaching on a national basis.

At Brillante, we are also working on addressing the critical issue of obesity and diabetes in the Hispanic community. We've created Vive en Forma (Get Fit Chicago). This is a wellness campaign informing Latinos of the obesity crisis and how to live a healthier life through better Latino nutrition and physical activity. In addition to education and motivation to eat right and be fit, Vive en Forma connects community members with resources in their neighborhoods to help them learn to eat, shop, and cook better and access health centers where they can be screened for diabetes and other obesity-related diseases.
