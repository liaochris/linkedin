Terra Andersen’s Summary

Terra Andersen is an energetic Internet Business Consultant and Entrepreneur. Passionate about gaining traffic, knowing what to do with it once it arrives.

Currently doing work within the Food Industry that includes Design, Marketing, Branding, PR, Reputation Management, and more.

Terra is an experienced entrepreneur and Internet Business Consultant who has held her own in the internet business world for the last 12 years. Having spent her entire career in internet-based business, she has become increasingly enthusiastic about the internet's potential, and believes that we've just hit the surface of something much bigger. She currently resides in Southern California, and owns a network of internet-related businesses, in addition to consulting for many mid-sized and large corporations.

Terra specializes in turning an aimless (or nonexistent) campaign into a highly targeted and polished, sales machine.

Her passion is creating passionate users and shoppers.

Terra's expertise lie in internet business, web strategy, e-commerce, analytics, branding, and internet marketing; in addition to being an enthusiastic advocate of social media usage for business.

Terra’s professional speaking includes conventions, seminars, and corporate events. She is also authoring her first book on the subject, as well as heading up local efforts to educate small businesses on how to utilize the internet in a cost-efficient manner, during these tough economical times.

She is outgoing, friendly, and always looking to connect with professionals who happen to be in her area. Feel free to contact her via her site or LinkedIn, and she'd be glad to speak with you, or chat over coffee!
