Adrian Kingwell’s Summary

After 10 years of selling various software solutions, from Oracle databases to supply chain e-commerce platforms, I found my niche in sales & marketing management.

I learned grass-roots online and offline marketing at the highly successful medical recruitment group, Healthcare Locums plc, taking it from start-up to a £60m AIM-listed business in 2 years. There I built a tight team of marketing professionals who concentrated on the multi-channel acquisition of medical locums for the agency's staff banks and by working the online channel harder. In less than a year, we were getting four times the acquisitions of previous best efforts without any increase in budget.

I then focussed my efforts exclusively on online marketing and founded Mezzo Marketing in May of 2006. Mezzo's aim was to improve the performance of existing websites by making sensible changes. After being responsible for the performance of Barclays' insurance websites, Mezzo also moved onto several other projects within Barclays Digital Banking, from managing a LEAN project to improve online banking conversion to implementing a new Contact Us journey on barclays.co.uk.

Mezzo has since gone on to win over a dozen customers in financial service and retail, from Post Office and Investec to Holiday Extras and Kaspersky. Mezzo now focuses on delivering discrete fixed price projects in what it calls "Cubes". Cubes cover all the tasks you'd typically perform in a fully-employed digital marketing department. Mezzo's Cubes help when your manpower or internal skill level is low.

I am currently dedicated to growing Mezzo with the help of my business partner, Chris Carter, and our employees and associates, to be the UK's top digital consultancy.
