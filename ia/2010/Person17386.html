Ashleigh Laabs’s Summary

My current communications position at the Community Foundation for Southeast Michigan triggered my thoughts of attending grad school. The Community Foundation is just now diving into the world of social media and using PR in new and innovative ways to create projects and share those projects and messages with the surrounding communities to help run our business. This transformation has provided me with a great opportunity and learning experience.

Networking is very important to my personal professional development. I enjoy learning from other people and their experiences. I have met many great people so early in my career and am grateful for the knowledge and wisdom they have shared with me.

I have many years in my career ahead of me and was fortunate enough to know walking into college as an undergrad what I wanted to do with my life. I have kept to that path, but have taken many risks turned opportunities along the way. One day I hope to be an entrepreneur running my own PR firm and helping others understand the value and importance of public relations for their business or organization.

I am also passionate about nonprofit and foundation organizations and eventually want to break into the auto racing industry as a PR professional. This is a side stop I want to make as my journey of a great PR career continues.

Stay tuned for the latest and greatest in my PR world...
