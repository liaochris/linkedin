Jerry Schwartz’s Summary

High energy senior executive with extensive experience building businesses and driving global opportunities. Track record of successfully funding growth, building revenue streams and liquidity events (IPO, M&A, Sale). Unique cross-functional skill-set including global sales and marketing, strategic development, operations and corporate governance/legal affairs with broad applicability to a wide variety of industries and geographies. Extensive international experience and successes spanning Europe, Asia and the emerging BRIC economies.

Broad-based strengths and key accomplishments include:
• Strengths: culturally aware, technically savvy, team player, 3rd party relationships

• Over 12 years of experience with hosted B2C and B2B online services (SaaS) and cloud computing platforms
• 10+ years as Chief Revenue Officer with 50+ channel distribution and 200+ OEM licensing agreements
• Successful completion of multiple financing transactions including 7 M&As and 5 public offerings (2 IPOs)
• Extensive international legal work including contracts and formation of 30+ international branches /subsidiaries
• Respected industry leader with frequent sector-expert speaking engagements and multiple governance roles
