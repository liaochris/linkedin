Jean-Pierre Lacroix’s Summary

As President of Shikatani Lacroix Brandesign, Jean-Pierre Lacroix is strongly committed to design innovation. In addition to pioneering the successful firm, Jean-Pierre is also Past President of The Association of Registered Graphic Designers of Ontario, Past President of DIAC (Design Industry Advisory Committee), board member of SEGD (Society of Environmental Graphic Designers), as well as former Director of the Packaging Association of Canada.

An accomplished dynamic speaker, he has lectured at numerous conferences across Canada, the US, and South America on the subjects of branding and design trends. His presentations have always received high appraisals for content, energy, and take-away actionable information. Mr. Lacroix also co-authored a book, which was launched in January 2002, titled The Business of Graphic Design: A Professional’s Handbook
