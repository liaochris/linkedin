Dror Pockard’s Summary

Dror Pockard is a senior consultant and executive with extensive experience in telecommunications, CRM, and other enterprise software companies. Over 23 years of international experience in building and leading companies, in consulting major enterprises and in major turnaround and M&A efforts, specializing in sales, marketing and go to market strategy.

Until October 2009, Dror was the CEO of e-Glue Business Technologies Inc., the leader in Real Time Customer Interaction Management area. eglue manifest Dror's philosophy of the transition in the Enterprise space to Real Time Enterprise, an erea that Dror supports and pushes across the industry.

Prior, Dror was the President & CEO of Telrad Networks (www.telrad.com), a leading Israeli telecom manufacturer. Dror was board member at Olista Inc., a Venture Partner at Israel Seed Partners, an Active Chairman of Xpert Integrated Systems Ltd., and a Director at MS Tech Ltd. and at Scent Detection Technologies (SDT) Ltd. Along that Dror serves as a consultant to many technology companies and VCs in Israel including 888.com and others.

Until 2004, Dror was a Group President in Amdocs Inc., responsible for all Amdocs products business units. He was also a division president of Clarify, the CRM division of Amdocs, and was in charge of Amdocs System Integration organization.

Prior to that, he was with Ernst & Young Management Consultants both in England and Israel, including 3 years as the Managing Director of the Israeli Ernst & Young Consulting Office.

Mr. Pockard holds an MBA in Information Systems and a BA in Management and Economics from Tel Aviv University, in Tel Aviv, Israel. He's a member of YPO (Young Presidents Organization), Adams Institute and is on the board of several charities include the Branco Weiss Institute and Yuvalim. He is a high energy, results oriented, motivational leader with excellent problem solving and team building skills and with extensive strategical capabilities.

Dror Pockard’s Specialties:

Telecommunications, Enterprise Software, CRM, Real Time Enterprise, Internet, Services Organizations, Management and Turnarounds, Mergers & Acquisitions, Strategy, Venture Capital (and also Wine, Good Food, Travel...)
