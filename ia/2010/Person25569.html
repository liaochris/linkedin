Bob McDonnell’s Summary

More than 35 years with experience ranging from high-tech (Hewlett-Packard) to nonprofit (United Way) to higher education (CollegeAmerica).

I grew up in Cedar Rapids Iowa (Washington High 1963), but was born in Oelwein, Iowa.
After high school I spent four years in the U.S. Air Force (USAF). This included one year in Thailand. I was also on an Atlas E missile launch crew.

Attended Kirkwood Community College (then Area 10) in Cedar Rapids before graduating from C.S.U. in Fort Colins, CO.

I live to network.
A collegue (Michael Clingan) said,
"Bob knows more people than a third cousin at an Arkansas reunion."
