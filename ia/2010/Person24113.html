Bill Tilley’s Summary

Bill Tilley is the President and CEO of Amicus Capital Services LLC and is responsible for managing day to day operations.

For the past twelve years, Bill Tilley has been devoted to creating and then fine-tuning the legal finance industry. Bill Tilley joined Themis Capital in 1999, launching the legal finance industry in California. Within two years California became the largest market for legal loans. Mr. Tilley was instrumental in growing Themis from 12mm in loans upon his arrival to more than 135mm in less than four years. After successfully winding down the Themis portfolio in 2004 Bill Tilley became the largest broker of legal financial services as a consultant through 2006. In 2006 due to increasing demand Bill began offering his financial expertise by guiding plaintiffs through the settlement process. Expanded offerings included comprehensive settlement planning, including structured settlement / fee products as well as financial advisory services. He continued his devotion to creating a full service financial services company that caters exclusively to the legal community leading to the launch of Amicus Capital Services, LLC in early 2007.

Bill has personally originated or placed more than 150 million in attorney loans/structures and has participated in the origination of more than 250 million. Mr. Tilley counts several of the most successful plaintiff attorneys in the country as personal friends and has developed a network in the thousands of invaluable industry contacts. As an expert on legal finance Mr. Tilley has spoke numerous times at many attorney conferences throughout the country. In addition, Mr. Tilley was recognized on Public Justices wall of honor for his efforts in promoting the non-profit public interest association.
