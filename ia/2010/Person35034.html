Michael Cervantes’s Summary

Mike Cervantes is a 2008 graduate of California State University, Fullerton with a B.A. in Communications, Broadcast Journalism. He specializes in convergence media with experience in everything including broadcast field production, videography/photography, nonlinear editing, full studio news show production and writing, technical directing and directing, and has experience with basic HTML and web design. His work experience includes positions as an editor, studio camera operator, assignment desk assistant and prompter operator at KMIR6 in Palm Springs; tape operator, teleprompter and field photographer for KCET’s “OC Insight;” production assistant for KOCE’s “Real Orange,” as well as producer and reporter for CSUF’s “OC News.”

Michael Cervantes’s Specialties:

ENG photography, editing, writing and producing.
