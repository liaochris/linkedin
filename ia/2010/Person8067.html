Allie Mac Jordan’s Summary

From January to June of 2009 I worked in New York City at the television network Bravo TV. During my time there I was in charge of three weekly duties as well as a number of other projects. Every week I was to update all of the Bravo shows Facebook pages, organize/submit a weekly inventory of the Bravo Premium closet and update the Consumer Marketing On-Air Status as well as sit in on a weekly call with Bravo’s On-Air Team. Other major projects I assisted with were to create spreadsheets and help organize talent reach out for Bravo’s iPhone Application “Guides by Bravo”, brainstorm premium ideas, catchphrases, copy and questions for contestants for various shows, and write and submit marketing briefs for snipes that would advertise Bravo merchandise on-air. My experience at Bravo TV has helped me hone my skills in organization, multitasking, and articulation of ideas.

The summer after my freshmen year I worked at a marketing firm in Sparks, Maryland called Axiom Marketing. During my time there I was assigned three main projects. I was to assist in the re-branding process of Axiom Marketing itself, develop slogans and interactive promotional campaigns and start the re-branding process of a local business by designing a new website layout. At the end of the summer I succeeded in having two of my slogans chosen for Axiom’s clients. I hope to build on this experience during a co-op at your company. In addition, I possess Mac and PC computer skills, as well as an excellent work ethic, and an ability to follow through on my projects. I’ve demonstrated these qualities both in my academic studies and work experiences.
