Erin Nunes Cooper’s Summary

Erin Nunes Cooper AIA, LEED AP
Erin Cooper is the owner of Cooper Green Design, an architecture and green building consulting firm in Northampton, MA. Her work includes design, planning, green house gas emissions studies, LEED documentation, and green building training workshops.

Erin Nunes Cooper’s Specialties:

Architecture and design, energy efficiency, LEED, climate neutrality strategies. Management of large complex projects.

Follow on Twitter: cgdgreen

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    December 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Erin Nunes Cooper, AIA, LEED AP has over ten years of green building design experience for laboratory, healthcare, corporate, and higher education clients. Services include architectural design, LEED documentation, feasibility studies, campus building inventories, and green building training and education. Erin is a frequent speaker at green building events, and her writing has appeared in Environmental Design + Construction, AIArchitect, and Building CT Green.

  • Lecturer - Sustainable Building Advisor Program

    Gateway Community College

    (Architecture & Planning industry)

    November 2008 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Erin Nunes Cooper, AIA, LEED AP teaches the Daylighting component of the Sustainable Building Advisor Program, a 9-month training program for design and construction professionals. Topics include daylight modeling, history of daylight in buildings, glare reduction techniques, and integration of daylight and electric lighting.
