David O’Connor’s Summary

I'm a managing director in the Highland Consulting Group. I bring 19 years experience in managing construction projects of various sizes and scope.

Prior to founding the Highland Consulting Group, My responsibilities included contract negotiating, cost estimating, scheduling, managing the construction / installation of projects and marketing. My project management work provides me with a thorough understanding of my construction clients’ need to complete projects on time and within budget.

I specializes in recruiting professionals in the commercial construction industry in Pennsylvania, Maryland, West Virginia, Virginia and Ohio. My in-depth experience in overseeing construction projects and daily contact with top construction firms, I understand the current construction trends and the real players in the industry.

My clients range from small to large commercial construction firms. My focus in on recruiting opportunities such as:

• Cost estimators
• Project Managers
• Job superintendents
• Senior level management
