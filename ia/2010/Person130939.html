Christine Mantych’s Summary

Christine Mantych, owner of Mantych Group, is a 20 year veteran of the advertising and marketing industry working with companies around the globe to better understand and utilize information to further the conversation between product/service and the customer.

I focus primarily on new media and the conversation taking place. I can help you get involved with the conversation by starting, managing and maintaining your social media, search marketing and mobile campaigns.

I look forward to discussing your social, search and mobile marketing needs.
