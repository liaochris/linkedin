Bob Stumpel’s Summary

I started my career in the late seventies as an advertising copywriter and concept maker, then filled my days as a marketing strategist, consultant and advertising business man. I've been involved in two advertising agency groups in The Netherlands, helping both to grow to a level of ‘biggest independent agency in the Benelux’, and then merging them into international agency networks.
Since the eighties I've been pioneering in helping entrepreneurs and marketers to use or integrate new technologies, new media and new channels to innovate, enhance or revive their businesses.
As an advertising man, I've worked for clients in virtually all industries and sectors - like Nivea, Kimberly Clark, Courvoisier, Balantine’s, Mazda, Toyota, Lexus, Lotus, Robeco, Rodamco, Cisco, LogicaCMG, Microsoft, KPN, Sonera, Irdeto, Compuserve, Sony, Philips, Cannon Films, Cannon Movie Theaters, UIP, Universal, Sony Music & Entertainment, VNU, Sanoma, RTL, several governemental bodies, and many NGO's.
In 1999, I was the first investor in Result, accepting a position as a board member. But it soon became clear that I couldn't and didn't want to escape a more active role - as managing partner in the Benelux.
During the past 9 years I've been actively participating in and contributing to many of Result's international assignments, taking the lead in a few export cases of Dutch clients (presently e.g. Ines, Web Power and Myngle), in Xing's expansion (between 2004 and 2006), and in Belysio's.
Call me a generalist. I know enough (of business in general, and of every aspect of and niche within the TIME sector) to advise and execute.
Don't hesitate to visit my EVERYTHING 2.0 blog to get a full scope of my fascinations.
