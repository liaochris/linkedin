Kelly Olexa’s Summary

Kelly’s professional experience is a mixed blend of Sales, Marketing, & Business Development. She has worked for tech startups, small to medium-sized businesses, as well as Fortune 500 organizations. Her skilled tenacity has produced strategic partnerships with companies such as: ConAgra, Orbitz, Travelocity, Marriott Hotels, Doubletree Hotels, Choice Hotels, Best Western, Microsoft, N.A.R., 7-ELEVEN, Valero Energy, SBC, Exxon/Mobil, Safety-Kleen, Sally Beauty, Halliburton, H&R Block, Sprint/Nextel, Brinker International, Carlson Restaurants, A.C.S, Lennox International, and Dean Foods among others. Kelly now works independently with businesses on sales and marketing strategies utilizing Social Media.

Kelly has enhanced her business experience by immersing herself in Social Media. Her blog, focused on her passion for fitness, has garnered a captive audience, producing myriad opportunities to provide vlogging services for other brands. Kelly’s natural on-camera presence and charisma landed her a spot as 1 of 100 Agents for the “Ford Fiesta Movement”. Her engagement on Twitter has resulted in over 18,000 followers, regular placement among the “Twitter Elite” Top 100 nationally, Top 25 for Chicago.

She is regularly requested as a speaker, panel expert, guest vlogger, social media strategist, and trainer for conferences and “Tweetups”. Kelly was also featured as one of the “Top Young Female Entrepreneurs in Technology by Business Week”, 2009.

Kelly just finished a book about Social Media for business that will address how big brands and individuals can effectively use social media platforms to generate buzz and produce measurable ROI. Contributors to the book include: Gary Vaynerchuk, David Armano, Olivier Blanchard, Mack Collier, Aronado Placencia, Scott Monty (Ford), Kris Colvin, Liz Strauss, Doubletree Hotels, Microsoft Bing Maps, Dominoes Pizza, The Roger Smith Hotel, Michael Chiarello (Food Network and Napa Style), Katrina Campins and others.

Kelly Olexa’s Specialties:

Social Media Strategy, Blogging, SEO, PPC, Copywriting, Visual Media Marketing, Web 2.0, Interactive Branding & Evangelism.
