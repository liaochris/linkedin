Vivek Bhatnagar’s Summary

A leader with a passion to help make the world a better place to live in. Success to me means many things transcending material definitions. Success to me is not just the destination, it is the journey as well. It is preferring to fail attempting grand things than to succeed at doing nothing. It is our ability to rise above the immediacy of gratification, to work towards a larger purpose, even if that involves enduring pain. It is about vision with action. It is about moving into un-charted territories, creating our own paths, and blazing our own trails. It is about knowing when to persevere, and when to let go. It is about leaving a path a little better than before, just because you happened to tread on it. It is about volunteering for a mission from where the odds of returning are pretty low. In short it is about creating extraordinary stories with ordinary characters.

I truly believe that the best way to get whatever we seek in life is to share the same with others. If we seek knowledge–-which I do--then we must be prepared to share ours' with others. "That which you give another will become your own sustenance; if you light a lantern for another, it will also brighten your own path". - Nichiren Daishonin.

I am a dreamer. And with all my past accomplishments, my journey to explore myself and my potential has just begun...!

In my past life, I served as as a career officer in the Indian Army for over 21 years. During that period I held many appointments such as:

* Commanding Officer of an Electronic Warfare Regiment deployed in counter-terrorism role
* Commander All Arms Wing at Military College of Telecommunication Engineering
* General Staff Officer 1 (Training) at HQ College MCTE
* Officer-in-Charge at Army Internet Cell, Military Intelligence HQ
* Company Commander with Indian Peace Keeping Forces in Sri Lanka
