Jon Ostler’s Summary

Jon is CEO of QED (QEDigital.com) a subsidiary of Q Ltd and is responsible for devising, developing and growing profitable web assets owned by Q. These include email lists, ad networks and other internet based businesses.

Jon has founded, operated and sold a number of successful web related businesses including a web design business, an online restaurant guide, performance ad network and most notably First Rate, a leading specialist in search and performance marketing with offices in Auckland and Sydney. Jon has been a regular speaker at internet related conferences for more than 10 years and is widely respected as an expert in online marketing.

First Rate grew rapidly and consistently over 9 years and was listed in the Deloitte Fast 50 three years in a row (2006/07/08). Jon sold First Rate to Q Ltd (ASX:QXQ) in 2007.

Jon is originally from the UK where he graduated top of his class with a 1st class degree in Communication Engineering (BEng) before taking up a technical management role with JARIC a military intelligence department of the UK Ministry of Defence.

Jon has been based in New Zealand for the last 11 years but also spends a large amount of his time in Australia.

Jon Ostler’s Specialties:

Start-ups, Public Speaking, Technical Management, Business Consulting, Website Strategy, SEO, Search Engine Marketing, Email Marketing, Ad Networks, Website Conversion.
