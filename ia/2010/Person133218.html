Mark Connelly’s Summary

Results driven senior level executive in Information Technology(IT), Security/Risk, and sales support with expert skills in leadership, communications, and management of global businesses. Proven track record in formidable corporate dynamics and turbulent markets. Effective organization transformation architect and solutions-based team builder. Successful corporate career in Information Technology (IT) in one of the world's financial services firms, a leading network computing suppliers and the premier telecommunications research firms.

Over 20 years of management experience with an industry reputation of accomplishments and leadership. Successful in managing global IT Risk and Security Group responsible for IT Infrastructure that provided effective Risk and Security governance in support of a $50B+ revenue stream and an employee base of over 40,000.

Established the first Chief Information Security Office at Sun Microsystems, as CISO, and developed comprehensive security frameworks, methodologies and programs to protect information assets and ensure secure corporate and partner resources.

Consistently, delivered successfully business results thru IT services and business leadership. Created significant value add thru effective organization transformations and global technical sales support. Extensive experience in managing global organizations and developing growth strategies with proven measures of success.
