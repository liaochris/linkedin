Robert Cooper’s Summary

I'm a consultant/strategic director who has worked with great companies like DivX, ACDSee, PhotoWorks, SimpleStar, Ziff Davis, Mixpo, JER Envirotech and TeamPages.

I've been asked to participate in case studies by such respected organizations as DoubleClick, Exact Target and the International Shareware Industry Conference. I've been lucky enough to have my work win a national marketing award, my writing's been featured on one of the world's largest web sites, I had a landing page strategy has be featured at SES and I've built a multi-million dollar email marketing channel from the ground up.

I've written online marketing plans and fiscal models that got funding for startups ($6.5mm,$11mm), had a lead-generation white paper I wrote be used as a feature article in a major trade publication, had a product written about in the New York Times, and doubled the effective ROI on several email databases by applying creative list cleansing practices.

My professional goals right now are to continue to build our client base and continue to make money for my clients.

Robert Cooper’s Specialties:

We offer consulting & marketing services including strategic site planning, conversion optimization, copywriting, a/b testing, online merchandising, lead generation campaigns, email marketing, community building, organic SEO and market planning.

With medium size organizations we often operate as a "one stop shop," while with larger organizations we integrate with internal creative and marketing teams.

We have also manage JumpStartWeb.com, offering marketing services for smaller businesses.
