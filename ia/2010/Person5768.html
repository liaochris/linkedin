Aleks Buterman’s Summary

Note: This is my official bio, I don't normally write about myself in 3rd person.

More than 15 years of expertise in technology management, including strategic sourcing, strategy and operations leadership, enterprise, business, and technical architecture, information security, full life–cycle development, portfolio & project management, and platform management. He is a nationally recognized thought leader with a tremendous track record of driving successful organizational change and measurable value through technology.

Through proven technical and management competencies, Aleks has driven cost reduction and cost avoidance in SOA Delivery, Enterprise Architecture, and Information Security. He is a co-author of the Technology Investment Management Method™ - an advanced technique that allows organizations to achieve their business goals through optimized technology capability management. He has employed TIMM™ to achieve efficiency gains and synergy saves with little or no impact to strategic capabilities and operational steady state, contributing to over $2B in shareholder benefits in Financial and Healthcare verticals with SMB, Fortune 500 and Fortune 50 organizations.

Aleks has held technical, management, and senior leadership positions with Lincoln Financial Group, Allstate Insurance, Discover Financial Services, Caremark Prescription Services, and Rush University Hospitals. He holds bachelor’s degrees in Economics and Mathematics from Lake Forest College and a master’s degree in Project Management from the Keller Graduate School of Management. He is a member of Center for Advancement of Enterprise Architecture Profession, ACORD for Group Working Group, SOA-Consortium, and OMG Business Motivation Model Definition Working Group.

Aleks serves on Board of Directors of Girls on the Run Chicago. He resides in Chicago, Illinois with his wife and children.
