Debra Bennetts’s Summary

Award-winning public relations and marketing communications professional (and former journalist) with more than 25 years experience as a writer, trainer and public relations executive working for a companies in a wide range of consumer product and service industries.

Have developed and worked on programs/projects for nationally-known companies and brands, including The Procter & Gamble Company, Unilever, eBay, Skype, Johnson & Johnson, Leapfrog, Robert Mondavi, Weight Watchers, Kanebo, Best Friends Pet Care and others.

Debra Bennetts’s Specialties:

Writing – Press materials, Website copy, collaterals, speeches, by-lined articles.

Training -- Group writing classes and one-to-one coaching at corporate & agency level. Train executives, scientists and spokespeople for media, speeches & testimony.

Influencer & Community Relations – Skilled at cultivating influencers who can help build brand/business reputation.

Issues/Crises -- Expert in developing communications response and programs for rebuilding reputation.
