Michael Burke’s Summary

As co-founder and president of appssavvy, Michael Burke is responsible for developing the organization, hiring and managing the team, and ensuring that appssavvy will continue to deliver the highest level of integrated, contextually-relevant marketing solutions to brand and agency clients and developer and publisher partners.

He started his career as a technology recruiter, learning the fundamental of sales, hiring and customer management. Michael joined HotJobs.com in 1999, opened the Chicago office and grew with the company until it was purchased by Yahoo! in 2001. After 6 years of leading sales teams at Yahoo!, in 2007, Michael joined Freewebs and quickly recognized the opportunities surrounding widgets and social media applications. Working with traditional brands such as Ford, P&G and Kimberly Clark, Michael proved that the social media revolution was not limited to just teenagers and can be effective for all advertisers.

Michael is also a frequent speaker on social media marketing. Since founding appssavvy in early 2008, he has participated in industry events, including SNAP Summit, Digital Hollywood, OMMA and DigiDay.

Michael graduated from Boston College with a bachelor's of arts degree in Marketing.
