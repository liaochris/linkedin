Fiona Posell’s Summary

* Produce Communications, the business I founded in 2008, provides senior level communications and marketing counsel to established and growth companies across varied industries and time zones. Our sweet spot is working with clients who have ambitious growth plans and a solid understanding of marketing. Such businesses are either in launch phase or have reached a point when they are looking for an extraordinary partner to help innovate, invigorate and augment their communication and marketing efforts.
* Please visit www.producecommunications.com for more information.

Fiona Posell’s Specialties:

Expert in communicating complex topics to stakeholder groups across Technology/Internet/Consumer businesses. Creatively applies and demonstrates the importance of a robust communications function to growth businesses (public and private). Builds and manages successful teams; leading corporate and brand strategy and reputation management; crisis management; executive visibility and use of "below-the line" brand activation tactics. Specialized interest in health, nutrition and sustainability.
