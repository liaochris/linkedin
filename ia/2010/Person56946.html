Peter Brown’s Summary

I am a horticulturist and ecologist by trainng with specialist experience in soil science and agronomy. However, I have spent most of my time in marketing and business development for small business - as well as a stint in journalism. Presently, much of my work involves marketing communications, ghost writing, translating technical information into plain English and working on business development projects in various whole food and functional food areas.

Peter Brown’s Specialties:

Agriculture, Horticulture, Soil science and plant nutrition. Also publishing and media relations, technical marketing, ghost writing and freelance journalism.
