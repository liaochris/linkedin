Dmitry Sotnikov’s Summary

Young energetic leader who can make things happen in a software corporation.

Microsoft MVP, member of the START-2009 and START-2010 venture funding committees for Russian tech start-ups, head of Quest's product incubation team, who successfully lead the efforts developing multiple new products and markets. These include Quest's migration products (DMW, EMW, QMM), auditing products (InTrust for AD, InTrust for Exchange), first SharePoint products, Quest's most successful freeware/community tools: PowerGUI and AD cmdlets, and recently Quest's SaaS product set - Quest OnDemand.

Work experience includes multiple management positions at:
* Software development,
* Technical communications,
* Technical support (set up the team for Aelita, St. Petersburg from scratch and established 24x7 customer support),
* Product management (set up the program managers' team in St. Petersburg R&D lab),
* New product research,
* SaaS development and operations.

Besides the PowerShell and Cloud Computing efforts, I am involved in strategic planning and directions for Quest's Windows Management Business Unit and our Microsoft collaboration. I am doing a lot of traveling meeting with Quest staff globally, our customers and partners, Microsoft, attending conferencing, talking to press and analysts.

I have been presenting at various conferences including Microsoft Management Summit, WinHEC, TechEd US and Europe, TEC, and Platforma, and was one of the organizers of Quest's internal Innovate events.
