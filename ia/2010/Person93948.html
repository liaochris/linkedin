Jeffrey A. Riegel’s Summary

Senior Video Production Specialist ("PREDITOR") with the Dept. of Defense with more than ten years of consistent production, editing, video & audio recording experience including:

1. Overall development, direction & creation of routine and specialized video programs including production, editing, video and audio recording. Samples of work include documentaries, training videos, in-house commercials, briefings & vignettes.

2. Implementing new integrated technologies within a team environment

3. Systems management includes Final Cut Pro editing suite, Final Cut Server, DV and HD cameras, studio equipment, lighting, and engineering of audio-visual signals. 

4. Highly experienced training supervisor, video production manager, technical director, staff team leader, SAN and Mac administrator.


Expert proficiency at working in all media & web formats utilizing emerging digital conversion & compression software such as Sorenson Squeeze & Media Cleaner.

SFX software to include Boris Continuum 6, Fx Factory, Coremelt, & Motion (After Effects, in progress).

Solves complex technical problems by utilizing a myriad of advanced audio-video hardware & software applications along with ingenuity; often develops unique alternatives to meet production requirements.

Applies initiative and forward thinking skills to streamline production requirements, work flow management & visual media developments.

Demonstrates exemplary decision-making skills in all facets of video production including logistics, layout and timing of the edit process, & final duplication.

A confident, dedicated, enthusiastic professional with a passion for the industry & its emerging technologies.

Strong camera work skills enables video shoots to be captured in both professional & creative fashions.

Through dedicated training and education, I continue to sharpen my abilities in new emerging video technologies, streaming & compression.
