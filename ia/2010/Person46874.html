Dan Horn’s Summary

Dan Horn is an innovative, practical, and responsible executive technology professional. Utilizing a strong capacity to lead proficiently under pressure Dan has guided numerous national and international organizations to high levels of success over the years. Mr. Horn possesses an extensive background in technology team building and management, operational reorganization, and system architectures. Dan Horn is a proven leader with successful profit and loss management experience and with particular strengths in technology, international business, and media.
