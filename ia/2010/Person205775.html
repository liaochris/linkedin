Tomaso Veneroso’s Summary

I am native of Udine, Italy. It is my philosophy and dream to work towards using my professional expertise to benefit my community, both locally and internationally. I started my career in a steel mill in Italy and long term one of my goals is to participate in the creation of Bilateral Trade Agreements and the expansion of international trade & commerce.

I began my career at the Fonderie Acciaierie Rojale “FAR Spa” in Reana del Rojale (Italy), a leading company in the production of spare parts for the mining industry, and in 1999 I was made CEO of Amcast Inc.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS
• Senior-level executive experienced in restructuring operations of manufacturing and international non-profit.
• Strategic thinker and seasoned change agent with reputation for being a “go-getter” with a ‘roll-up-sleeves’ attitude and ability for making numbers work. Proven ability to respond to operational and financial challenges with confidence and results.
• Rare mix of innovative thinking, sales savvy, leadership and financial expertise with high-level commitment to growth and development.
• Global network of contacts developed through Italian-American business partnerships. EU & US Citizen.
• Cross-functional communicator, skilled leader and team-builder. Easily interface with high-profile clients, manufacturers, distributors, staff, vendors and government agencies. Work well in a fast paced environment with high demands.
• Intimately understand demands of new and challenging marketplaces; a keen eye for maximizing corporate potential through innovative business process changes, cost containment, quality control and smart risk taking. Successful in streamlining operations and manufacturing to reduce cost and improve rate of return.
