Sara Gonzalez’s Summary

I am a Human Resources professional with experience shaping and growing a College Internship Program, with more than 100 participants each summer. My knowledge of HR spans beyond college recruiting to candidate experience, benefits, and compensation. I am a “big picture” thinker with a pension for taking on difficult challenges and finding solutions.

I am a continuous learner seeking to develop and increase my knowledge of various HR functions such as employee communications, employee relations, and retention strategies. Recently I have become immersed in Social Recruiting, learning how to create brand awareness and meaningful relationships among passive and active candidates. I am always looking to the horizon for new social recruiting tools and their uses, and HR best practices.

If you are interested in learning more about internship opportunities at Amway please visit www.Amway.com, join our LinkedIn Group Amway Talent Acquisition, or send me a message.
