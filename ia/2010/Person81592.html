Henrik Walther’s Summary

Technology Architect at Timengo Consulting with special focus on Microsoft Exchange, Office Communications server, and BPOS (Exchange Online) solutions within the unified communications area.

Been in the IT industry for more than 15 years (12 years with Microsoft Exchange) primarily working with Microsoft BackOffice solutions.

Usually involved in all phases of the project. More specifically the envisioning, planning, and design phases and often also the deployment and migration phases. I usually work with medium and large corporate enterprises.

Exchange MVP since 2004 and Microsoft Certified Master: Exchange 2007 since 2007.

In addition to being a Technology Architect, I work as a Technical Writer. Among other things, I’ve been on the team that did most of the Exchange 2007 related white papers for Microsoft IT Showcase as well as on the team that created questions for the Exchange 2010 MCP exams. Currently hired as an Exchange SME/writer for Microsoft TechNet magazine via vendor relationship with Microsoft.

I'm also the guy behind the leading Danish site - Exchange-faq.dk as well as the lead author for the MSExchange.org site.

Moreover, I have authored and reviewed several Exchange related books and papers over the years.

Henrik Walther’s Specialties:

- Architect/design/implement/migrate large & complex Exchange-based Messaging solutions
- High availability and site resiliency solutions and disaster recovery scenarios
- Co-existence and ILM/FIM synchronization/provisioning
- Technical writing on Exchange and other messaging technologies
