  • Web Content Creator

    CMI Interactive, LLC

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    CMI Interactive is the brainchild of clinical research, accounting, contract staffing, and nursing entrepreneurs. We are committed to cultivating superior professional relationships with contractors within our healthcare ventures. Through this intermediary role, we recognized the growing interest and need for an interactive knowledge tool.

    The site is designed specifically for the purpose of guiding independent contractors, while assisting industry leaders. Our interest in developing this website stemmed from the concerns and questions frequently voiced by our independent contractors. We have identified several key areas of interest, such as entity formation, income tax payments, and retirement planning. To this end, we have devised helpful, easy to use tools to organize and motivate new and experienced contractors alike. The project’s evolution inspired enhanced features: industry news and continuing education. Scientific contractors voiced the need to speak intelligently about their industry with colleagues. Many look for a one stop shop for healthcare continuing education. CMI Interactive grew to encompass those needs.

    Our newfound mission with CMI Interactive is to provide a comprehensive website with the resources necessary to organize and guide independent contractors in their business opportunities.
