Randy Martin, CPA’s Summary

Randy W. Martin graduated from the University of Texas with a B.B.A. and an Accounting degree. He began his career as a financial analyst for the Texas Instrument Defense Systems and Electronics Group (now Raytheon), working with engineers to budget and forecast military projects. Within a year, he was promoted to financial analyst II and also obtained his CPA license. In 2000, Randy was accepted into the Thunderbird graduate school of business where he finished the Master of Business Administration program and then became an advanced financial analyst with EDS. Prior to joining the ACS team in 2007, Randy worked as a recruiting manager for Robert Half and won the “Rookie of the Year” award.

At ACS, A Xerox Company, Randy has served as a Senior Recruiter, Recruiting Manager, and now as a Financial Manager.
