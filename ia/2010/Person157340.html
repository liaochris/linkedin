Olivia Speranza’s Summary

I am currently an on-air host for a music and lifestyle channel on cable television, MYX. I'm hosting my third show for the channel, 360VM. I also host for a web television company, MEVIO. I use a teleprompter and also memorize scripted content. I also have experience with live and improvisational work as well as national commercial and short film experience. I've also produced, written and did some of my own camera work for another international television network, VJIAM TV. My strong writing skills have won a state wide JACC award and a scholarship to study/teach abroad in South Korea at Soonchunhyang University. My goal is to work on-air as a host/journalist, writing my own scripts and producing my content. I also hope to exclusively produce on-air content.

Olivia Speranza’s Specialties:

Writing, comprehensive research, social networking, producing and of course, hosting!
