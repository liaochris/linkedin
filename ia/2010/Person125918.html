Lisa Coppolo McManus’s Summary

In managing the LexisNexis Web 2.0 Communities, my focus is on leveraging social media and networking to raise awareness of our free open web legal sites featuring blogs, podcasts, free case law, and more, as well as the networking benefits of Martindale-Hubbell Connected's legal community. I enjoy social media networking for the sites and for myself. I have practice experience in multiple areas of the law and currently maintain a part-time practice focusing primarily on oil and gas law.
