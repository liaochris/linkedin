Jeff Hodgkinson is a 30 year employee at Intel Corp. with over 29 years of progressive Program/Project Management experience in Facilities, Construction, Product Development, & Information Technology. Over his career, Jeff has managed numerous and diverse programs/projects in varying scope, budget, and schedule. Jeff has mentored dozens of Project/Program Managers in obtaining their PMP® and PgMP® credentials. He obtained his PMP® credential (#713) in 1991, is the 20th person to obtain the PgMP®, and the 247th person to obtain the PMI-RMP® . Jeff has an MBA along with numerous other certifications in Program & Project Management including the IPMA-B, PMOC, and CPPM-Level 10 (currently 1 of only 4). He has the distinction of facilitating 150 MAPP (Make A Program/Project Plan) events. He is a volunteer with the Phoenix PMI Chapter in several areas. Jeff approved as an SME for Program and Project Management with IT@Intel and PMI (Project Management Institute). He administrates two groups and 9 sub-groups on LinkedIn for Program and Project Managers. He has authored and co-authored many articles in various PM publications and websites.

Jeff tries to be known in the PM global community for his expertize, for helping others to be successful, and to always come highly recommended.
