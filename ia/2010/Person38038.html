Chris Kidd’s Summary

Chris Kidd is an entrepreneur, investor, author, speaker and financial freedom coach. He became a millionaire at the age of 24 and is now coaching others so they can achieve their own financial freedom.

The mission of Chris Kidd Enterprises is to build strong families by reducing the financial stress that exists in most modern homes. Financial stress causes us to lose sight of what is really important to us. Outbursts directed at our children, spouses and loved ones are often the result of financial stress. My goals are to help you become debt free and to show you how to build wealth, so that money is no longer an issue in your home.

Chris Kidd’s Specialties:

Creating Your Own Financial Freedom through multiple streams of income, stocks, options, real estate, entrepreneurship,etc. Chris also teaches smart money management and defeating debt.
