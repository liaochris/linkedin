Annie Liou,’s Summary

I am an ICF (International Coach Federation) accredited Certified Professional Coach.

I was an English teacher in Taipei, Taiwan when at 23; I decided to open my own English Language School for adults and children. I was not only responsible for its daily operation; I also personally developed and supervised our extensive teacher training program.

After few years I took on a new career in the health and wellness industry becoming an international distributor for Herbalife. This position enabled me to drawn on my inter-cultural communication abilities to work with clients from around the world. In that capacity I continue to host meetings, train new distributors on the retail of our products and recruit new members as an International President Team member in Herbalife.

I believe in providing clients with a safe and joyous environment that will extend their individual strengths and release their greatest potential. My experiences in education, business management, international marketing, and most recently with ICA (International Coach Academy), have equipped me with incredible coaching skills. These have endowed me with the ability to positively affect the lives of those with whom I have been blessed to cross paths.

As a professional coach, it is my aim to offer my clients the benefit of these experiences to help them move forward in achieving their goals and discovering their own state of peace and happiness.

Though my coaching draws on learned and natural abilities, which have carried me greatly through life’s extraordinary turns, I am first and foremost someone who coaches from the heart.

I am bilingual in Mandarin Chinese and English.
