Gregory Mancuso - Photographer + Writer’s Summary

Gregory Mancuso is an award-winning photographer and writer with over 20 years experience. As a photographer, he creates captivating pictures for advertising and editorial clients and specializes in lifestyle imagery and environmental portraits that reveal the natural warmth and personality of his subjects within impeccably designed and lit compositions. He has worked for many of our finest magazines, agencies, companies and assorted ne'er-do-wells.

As a writer, Gregory has created numerous screenplays and magazine articles. He has a produced feature film credit for the thriller “One Good Turn” ( http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0114041 ) starring James Remar--aka the scary father in Showtime’s “Dexter”.

Gregory has sold a couple of scripts, directed a couple of commercials and was an assistant director for a few movies. His current project, “Inside The Game”, ( http://www.scribd.com/doc/19458705/Inside-the-Game ) a comedic adventure fantasy infused with magic, heart and damn scary thrills, is currently in development at a film studio.

Gregory growed up in da Bronx and is now based in Los Angeles where he engages in various escapades which can’t be mentioned here or in polite company. Nuff said.
