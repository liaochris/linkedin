Kirk Michie’s Summary

I drive the capital markets activities (fund raising and investor relations) for a lower middle market private equity/buyout firm with emphasis on control investments in healthcare services, software & IT, and basic industries (light manufacturing, unique consumer products, specialty finance, etc.).

I've have been in the investment and wealth management business for more than two decades. Having worked with clients at Sanford C. Bernstein & Co., Inc., Bessemer Trust, and Merrill Lynch, I've been involved on a daily basis for 25 years with all facets of wealth management, including investments, financial and estate planning, asset allocation, retirement income planning, banking, insurance, and family office services. Having been a member of senior management at Wells Fargo and Kayne Anderson Rudnick, I've also been integrally involved with strategy, platform development, acquisitions, investment policy, pricing, vendor relations, and compliance. I'm a member of the Financial Advisory Board of Tiburon Strategic Advisors, frequently speak at investment and wealth management industry conferences, and founded an investment and wealth management think tank named Candor Advisors, LLC.

Kirk Michie’s Specialties:

Investment Consulting, Investment Management, Wealth Advisory, Sales Management, and Executive Management. Facile with proprietary offerrings, open architecture, virtually all brokerage & RIA platform models and derivations, including alternative investments and family office services.
