Everett Hoag’s Summary

Everett Hoag’s extensive career as a freelance designer, stylist and consultant includes a variety of jobs such as national marketing and ad campaigns, special events, trade shows, grand openings, product launches, showroom displays, catalogs, and fashion shows. Everett has expertise in general management, marketing, development, strategic planning and a special expertise in facilitative approach (often referred to as process consulting) turning challenges into opportunities.

Everett’s New York experience in event design, fashion, and costume has led to an inclusive understanding of the business of advertising, from designing and styling a variety of marketing experiences to merchandising events that generate sales, making “the shot” look natural and believable, and making guests feel a wave of emotions as they experience a Fortune 500 brand.

Original works by Everett have been commissioned by the National Endowment for the Arts, the Massachusetts Cultural Council, New Jersey State Council on the Arts, and the New York State Council on the Arts funding community-based programming. Relocating to Rhode Island after 9/11, Everett worked extensively with the RI Tourism & Hospitality industries, regional charities and universities with performance, film and workshops celebrating diversity with humor; providing personal development learning tools for Schools
