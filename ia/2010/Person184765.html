Scott Rodgers’s Summary

Web:
You could think of me as an "enabler" (the good kind) that enables organizations to meet their objectives via a cutting edge web presence and strategy. In a nutshell, it is about releasing your potential online: expanding your online footprint, engaging your audience, integrating the web with your operation, and driving results.

Media:
As media, advertising and the web experienced significant change from 2001-2009, I helped local media navigate the evolving space and capitalize on the online opportunities. It involved bridging traditional media with digital media, creating and growing online properties, enhancing audience engagement, solutions for advertisers, and watching trends for relevance to an evolving media and advertising landscape. My online media career spans local radio, television and newspaper, the local and corporate levels, and technology, operations, product, marketing and sales roles. Many of the principles used by online media, along with my in-depth and varied perspective, can also be applied to any organization's web presence.
