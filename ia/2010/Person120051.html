    About Startup Houston:
    Startup Houston exists to promote the business of technology startups in the greater Houston area and to provide access to resources that enable them to be more successful. Startup Houston focuses on those technologies which have the greatest levels of support within the Houston business and investment communities, such as:

    * Information and Web 2.0 technologies
    * Nanotechnology
    * Biotechnology
    * Energy technology
    * Aerospace technology

    It is also hoped that Startup Houston will enhance the communication and coordination between startup and small companies, business leaders, as well as investment firms, and become the rallying point for discussion and promotion of the great work that is occurring within Houston’s technology communities.

Kurt is currently the Director of Marketing and Sales for VisionMonitor Software, where he helps energy companies to better manage their environmental risk through the use of industry leading technology.
