Erryn Cobb’s Summary

Erryn attended Northwestern University where he earned a degree in Communication Studies with a concentration in English. A four year letter winner on the football team, he was voted team captain his senior year. During his time as a student-athlete at Northwestern Erryn learned how to work and succeed in a highly competitive and fast paced environment. When it came time to make the transition to the working world, his experiences both on and off the field paid dividends.

Throughout his career in Public Relations, Erryn has had the opportunity to hone his skills working with many prestigious brands. Select clients include W Hotels of Chicago, Joe’s Seafood, Prime Steak & Stone Crab, Swiss watchmaker Frederique Constant, Bull & Bear, Usagi Ya Sushi, and Reverend Jesse Jackson. Developing countless relationships with the media during his years in PR, Erryn brings not only his entrepreneurial spirit to the launch of Fetch Public Relations - He also provides direct links to key media members.
