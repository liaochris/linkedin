Alessandro Piana Bianco’s Summary

I'm an Information Architect with 10 years' experience in Managing New Media Information, and Knowledge Management. Developing Governance Models, Procedures, and Policies.

As an Information Architect with a strong background in Back End/Content Management Systems, and organizations' Information Workflows. Proven skills in User Centered Design, and Findability.

As a New Media Strategist I help organizations understand New Media, and provide analysis on Content Policies across Private, and Public Networks.

A self-taught Marketer, I'm using my knowledge of New Media to help organizations face challenges in New Media scenarios, tracing trends and rethinking brand positioning. Proven expertise in web content optimization for SEO and SEM.

A background as a Professional Journalist, with experience in Communication, I have exceptional presentation and communication skills. A strong cultural background, I'm fluent in English, Italian, French, and Spanish. I have a basic knowledge of Catalan.

Worldwide working experience, I'm used to working in and operating with multi-cultural environments/scenarios.

Experience working in busy corporate environments, and to strict deadlines, maintaining the ability to think logically and analyze complex processes and issues.

I make complex issues easy to understand, facilitating access and use of tools.
