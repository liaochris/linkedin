Carolanne Grant’s Summary

Specializing in Commercial Book Cover Design, Print & Internet Advertising, Copy Writing, and Brand/Business/Product/Concept Promotions.


C.D.Grant is a Graphic Artist, as well as a Marketing & Promotions Specialist, who is influenced by visual dynamics and uses them for a diverse clientele. Her use of professional RF Photography, color and lighting, fonts and placement, have made her something of an artistic outlaw.

Book Covers & Interior Design:

“I don’t make Book Covers…I am commissioned to create works of art for public display” ~C.D.Grant

C.D.Grant can visualize, design the artwork, complete layout, interior & manuscript formatting and if necessary, create the title and cover copy for your book. She will also create supporting promotional advertising; as well as create this from your own title, or description…all from a partial or full reading of your manuscript, or a well-worded description of your book. Whether subtle or dynamic, her artistic talents in this area are astounding.

I am currently in the development Stage of creating Creative Chaos Studio Productions, an Audio/Video Production company & Goddess7Media. A production company to be focused on Video-Film-TV & Broadcast Media in Europe & the US.

I am also an Internet Talk Show Producer/Host, and extend all promotion to my shows as well as product reviews for privately owned businesses, authors, musicians and artists. I am also about to begin accepting endorsement contracts for worthy and notable products.


My goal is to be able to operate Grant Design Concepts as an independent business with the financial ability to add features, programs and specials for those in need of my services; and to be able to add the necessary staff sometime in the near future.

I am currently in search of private tutorship of Programming knowledge for Computer Programming Languages that coincide with my Graphic Design skills.
