Gail Coleman’s Summary

The core of my work experience is Sales & Marketing no matter what the position or job title. My style of selling is through education and my idea of marketing is me armed with product knowledge. I'm of the firm belief that when someone is looking to fill a need, they'll purchase from someone they trust and can relate to, everything else is secondary and a natural outcome of that relationship.

I am an entrepreneur through and through and seek opportunities all around me from learning what others are doing and asking how can I help that person, how can they help me and how can I make this happen. Mutual exchange is key for me.

Enjoying what I do is not work but play, despite the filing and record keeping. I love all things beautiful and shiny and have a passion for jewelry, high-line import cars and fabulous interiors. Did that sound like I'm one of the "Real Housewives"?
