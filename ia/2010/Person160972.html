Paul Walsh’s Summary

I founded Jellyfish Online Marketing in 1999 and have spent the last ten years building it into the UK's leading agency dedicated to Paid Search.

In 2008 I focused back on the technology side of the business and Rob Pierre became MD. Which was definitely the smart move as our technology is now working even harder and we have doubled our turnover. With a forecast of £20 Million for 2010.

My next exciting venture is Infinity Call Tracking, a next generation call tracking service.
Founded in this Jan and it currently being sold exclusively through Jellyfish. Visite the site for more info: jellyfish.co.uk/infinity

In June we are launching Infinity to sell direct and to agencies thourgh our reseller program. We are having discussions with a number of agencies in the UK and the US to start using this next generation Call Tracking with there clients; so they are able to facilitate granular level keyword management on keywrods that generate calls.

If you want to know more please get in touch.

Paul Walsh’s Experience

  • Founder

    Infinity Call Tracking

    (Privately Held; Telecommunications industry)

    January 2010 — Present (5 months)

    Infinity Call Tracking is the next generation in Call Tracking services, and was designed to overcome the challenge of tracking millions of search keywords without using millions of phone numbers.

    So unlike traditional call tracking services, Infinity allows you to track ALL the keywords in your search marketing campaign, not just a select few. This can significantly benefit your campaign by rewarding all the keywords that actually generate calls and allows you to create efficiencies through granular level keyword management; which any sophisticated PPC Agency will tell you, can make or break your PPC campaign, especially in competitive verticals.

    Oh and of course we can do all the usual call tracking stuff that you would expect from a leading call tracking provider. Like tracking any online or offline channel and with 7 Million UK numbers to choose from, whether its a Free Phone, Low Call or Geographic number we can meet all your business call tracking needs.

  • Founder & Technical Director

    Jellyfish Online Marketing

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 1999 — Present (10 years 9 months)

    I founded Jellyfish off the back of a part-time project building an e-commerce site selling vinyl records online whilst at university.

    From this initial stepping stone, my appetite and desire for IT encouraged me to leave university behind and develop my own IT consultancy in 1999. In 2002 I landed Which? and started to offer search as part of our consultancy. As Paid Search began to evolve, I recognised this as an opportunity for business growth and this become the strategic focus. In 2005 I recruited Rob Pierre to join me and the Jellyfish brand was born, with the sole objective of becoming the UK’s leading agency dedicated to Paid Search. Which we have!!
