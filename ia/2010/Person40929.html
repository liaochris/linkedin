Carey Kleiman’s Summary

CAREY KLEIMAN received his Bachelors & Masters Degree in Music from the University of Miami. Over his career he has performed and produced with Ray Charles, Frank Sinatra, Boyz to Men, Kathy Lee Gifford, Tony Bennet, Bee Gees and served as a musical supervisor/contractor for syndicated television shows. In the 1980’s he formed his own music production company and served as the musical & entertainment director for the Diplomat Hotel working with many of the celebrities and all of the hotel’s convention clients. Later he formed CK Entertainment which specializes in corporate & meeting entertainment. In addition he is an adjunct professor of music business at Broward College.
