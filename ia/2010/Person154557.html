Nicole Chen’s Summary

Creative, human-centered design strategist specializing in application of design principles and practice to the world of business. Avid advocate of human-centered design and business - building strategies from bottom-up to create true value.

Experience includes innovation consulting for various industries such as CPG, hard goods (consumer electronics), children's furniture, and telecomm; and account management and product marketing for online advertising/Internet.
