Brian Breslin’s Summary

Currently CEO of Infinimedia, Inc a web based software development firm with a focus on social media development. Brian has been involved in web development for over 12 years, and heavily involved in social media and social networks for the last 4.

Brian is an active community leader in the South Florida tech scene, having founded RefreshMiami, the largest tech meetup group in South Florida, organized several Facebook Developer Garage events, and assisted with all three BarcampMiami events.

Brian has spoken at numerous conferences around the nation including PubCon, Barcamp, RefreshMiami, FIU Business and Technology Conference, and more.
