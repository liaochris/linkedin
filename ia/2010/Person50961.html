David Hoo’s Summary

David Hoo and Michael von Gonten have joined to form the Marketing Optimization Associates.com consulting firm to enable mass marketers to gain full command of their marketing programs and spending (website under construction).

At the core of their consulting practice is the application of the X-Ray Sales Response Model that is both radically different and better than Marketing Mix Optimization models. The X-Ray model goes to a fundamentally deeper level to measure the specific effectiveness of all of the Brand’s individual ads, media vehicles and promotions. This assessment includes a comprehensive range of highly granular facets of marketing tactics: daytime vs primetime TV, 15-second vs 30-second copy, physician detailing targeting vs messaging, coupons vs rebates, price features vs in-store displays, etc. Over 5,200 X-Ray analyses have been successfully performed for many household brand names.

For the first time ever, X-Ray allows marketers to see how well each specific marketing tactic exactly performs in generating sales, the ultimate end behavior that marketers want to drive, much like direct response and online marketers measure their performance. Now marketing executives can finally provide the accountability that Management today demands. X-Ray provides the learning to intelligently improve both revenue growth and profit margins by doing more of what actually works and less of what doesn’t, plus the key insights on how to develop better performing ads and promotions.

Michael von Gonten is the developer behind the X-Ray sales response model and has more than 40 years of consumer research experience and has worked for major research companies, including ARS, Burke, BASES and ASI as well as Pillsbury and S.C. Johnson.

David has over 30 years of consumer marketing and marketing research experience and has had senior positions for major companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Schering Plough, MassMutual, Medical Economics and AC Nielsen.

David Hoo’s Specialties:

Measurement of the effectiveness of advertising and promotion for frequently purchased consumer products and services for CPG, Rx and OTC pharmaceuticals, financial services and other consumer services.

Strategic and creative marketing consulting on how to develop better performing ads and promotions.
