Adrian Nazari’s Summary

Adrian Nazari is an acknowledged industry expert and founder of Credit Sesame Inc. Credit Sesame is a new, easy and transparent way for people to manage all of their personal credit/debt online, in one place, and for free! Adrian was founder and CEO of Financial Crossing where he brought innovative point of sales systems for consumer credit/debt and mortgage management to top banking institutions. Prior to Financial Crossing Adrian was founder and CEO of Financial Circuit where he brought 1st of a kind solutions to manage the liability side of the consumer’s balance sheet to the lending and financial advisory market. Prior to forming Financial Circuit in 2000, Mr. Nazari served as Vice President of Marketing and Business Development at Savvion, where he positioned the start-up in the emerging business process management (BPM) software and helped the company become the market leader with global enterprise customers such as Bank of America, Intuit, Transunion, First American Title, Checkfree, and First Data. Prior to Savvion, Mr. Nazari held various products, marketing, and business management positions at National Semi where he turned new technology and concepts to leading market products and real businesses for both programmable and embedded systems divisions.

Adrian is an Author and Speaker, holds US patents, BSEE, and Masters in Business Management from Stanford Graduate School of Business where he was a Sloan fellow.
