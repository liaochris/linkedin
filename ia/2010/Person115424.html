Kevin Donahue’s Summary

With nearly fifteen years experience in the hospitality industry and nearly 20 years in direct sales, I have a proven track record of sales leadership, business development in the MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences & Events) markets, revenue generation and associate development. My background includes both day-to-day and pre-opening management of resort, boutique, urban, and suburban hotels, as well as multi-property management, in the luxury and upper-upscale markets.

I have served on the Sales & Marketing Committee for Visit Florida, the State of Florida's official tourism arm.

Additionally, I have served as an Executive Liaison to the MBA program at Rollins College and to the undergraduate program at the University of Central Florida's Rosen College of Hospitality. I am proud to have served as a marketing liaison to the undergraduate hospitality program at the University of Findlay.

Direct sales, revenue generation, group sales, transient sales, sales leadership, revenue management, managing market segmentation, marketing & public relations, meetings, incentives, conferences, events, MICE
