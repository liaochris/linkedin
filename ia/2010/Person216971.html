Patricia Jackson’s Summary

Patricia Jackson is founder and president of Xpect Results, LLC. She is a nationally recognized practitioner and provider of management solutions with an expertise in corporate performance improvement. Ms. Jackson honed her skills in coaching/training, human resources, customer service operations and organizational development positions over the past 18 years in escalating leadership roles, including an adjunct professorship in business communications.

Patricia’s approach to leadership coaching is her best-practice mantra, "doing the opposite," which directs effective leaders on "doing the opposite" of poor management.
Now, in addition to her consulting and training work at Houston-based Xpect Results, Patricia regularly speaks at national industry conferences and is a go-to media source for strategic business enhancement insight and analysis.
She holds a B.A. in communications from Baldwin Wallace College, an M.B.A. from Capella University, a certificate in Organizational Psychology and Development from Walden University and has attended the Ritz Carlton Leadership Center.

Nimble organizations, she advises, must train for results to experience success by understanding change management and upending the behaviors that place roadblocks on the path to corporate goals. If organizations are not investing in the overall performance of their employees, leaders are missing opportunities to increase profits and productivity. They have an obligation to arm their workforces with the tools they need to experience success. Her upcoming leadership publication (2010), Doing the Opposite: Insights for Success, a Guide for the New Leader, is a watershed guide for business influencers.

Patricia Jackson’s Specialties:

Organizational effectiveness, change management, coaching, public/private sector management solutions, leadership development and training.
