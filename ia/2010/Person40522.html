Cindy K. Coffin’s Summary

I am a Knowledge Management and Performance Improvement Consultant who leads companies to leverage their intellectual capital and learning development assets for maximum growth and profitability.

I transform knowledge and learning into value!

My skills include business analysis, assessing needs and identifying gaps; goal analysis, alignment, and mapping; strategic thinking and planning, identifying the best solutions, calculating ROI, developing implementation plans, instructional design and delivery, managing execution, and measuring effectiveness to ensure delivery of targeted business results.

I am a systems thinker with sharp business acumen. My expertise includes communication, process management, information technology, and quality systems.

My work history demonstrates a commitment to root cause problem solving and continuous improvement.
