Dirkjan Brummelman’s Summary

HoverKraft is a multi-disciplinairy design agency with a focus on identity.
We produce campaigns, graphic design, web-design, animation, exhibition-design - we can do this ourselves or have done in co-operation with our network of photographers, copywriters, programmers and fellow designers.

We set out to create and strengthen the communicative value of your company, brand or product through a distinctive straightforward design-approach.

Our strength is to tell a story that connects with your audience in a surprising and visual way. Since we have a background in as well internet- and graphic design, ‘cross-over thinking’ comes by nature to us. — hence the name HoverKraft.

Dirkjan Brummelman’s Specialties:

Strategy, corporate identity, campaigns, graphic design, motion graphics, websites (flash & html).
