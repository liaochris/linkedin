Georges van Hoegaerden’s Summary

Georges is an experienced entrepreneur and venture catalyst with the proven ability to generate double digit early-stage valuations and triple digit returns. Georges acted as a Venture Catalyst to startups, a part-time venture partner to Venture Capitalists and now an advisor to Limited Partners in the venture ecosystem.

His blog (at venturecompany.com), that sheds new light on the performance of the venture sector has found critical acclaim with more than 26,000 venture professionals around the globe, and through mainstream publication in The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, NPR, USA Today, BusinessWeek and more than 50 other distributions.
