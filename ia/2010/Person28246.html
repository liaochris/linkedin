Brian Thomas’s Summary

Brian combines strong management experience with an excellent technical background and has 20 years of experience in sales, management, marketing, and product and business development in process equipment and controls.

He has held leadership roles in almost all functional areas, including operations, maintenance, sales, marketing, product development, environmental, quality control, shipping, scheduling, technical, and engineering departments as well as global project teams.

Brian specializes in partnering with customers on difficult or custom applications, developing and implementing custom solutions and equipment.

He also has developed and implemented many internal programs and procedures for both front office (CRM, Proposal Generation) and back office (ERP) solutions to improve productivity and reduce costs.

Brian holds a Masters Degree in Business Administration and a Bachelors Degree in Engineering.
