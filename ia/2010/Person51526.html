David M. Jones’s Summary

Solutions-oriented business systems manager with over 20 years of experience in the consumer packaged goods industry with an emphasis on supply chain systems and processes. Experience leading all stages of system development efforts, including requirements gathering, design, construction, testing, and support. Thorough understanding of business processes in the areas of planning, procurement, manufacturing, quality and HSE compliance, finance, accounting, warehousing, and distribution. Consistently demonstrated capacity to develop and implement innovative solutions to the most complex business requirements.
