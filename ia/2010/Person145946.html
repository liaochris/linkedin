Michael Rubin’s Summary

I am a 19 year veteran of the consumer electronics, digital imaging, storage, internet and printing revolutions. I've lead the explosive growth of digital photography at two of the largest and most-well known brands. I am blessed to have so many friends in the photography industry, in the press and in related industries.

I try to be an honest broker in all of my dealings and to treat people as I expect to be treated in all aspects of business and life. I believe in straight-talk and I always try to give direct and honest answers. I'd rather tell you the truth than try to protect your feelings, always remembering business is business and time is precious. Again, I expect the same. Just the same, I am glad to give advice, counsel and support if you ask and I am always looking to help young and upcoming photographers and marketers as best I can as long as it does not compromise me or my company.

Please note that the views, opinions and postings expressed on LinkedIn, unless otherwise noted, are solely my own and do not necessarily reflect those of my employer, Nikon Inc. or any Nikon worldwide subsidiaries or related companies.

All of the recordings were made direct to analog stereo Studer reel-to-reel gear modified with clean tube stages. The microphones were also made by the company and usually one large diaphragm stereo microphone was used in the recordings. I assisted the engineer and producer at many sessions and worked with the talent.

    I learned so much about business during that year.
