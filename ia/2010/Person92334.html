JD Beebe’s Summary

I have been working in advertising my whole life, beginning at 6 years old with my design work for Beebe Brothers Inc business cards. More recently I have worked for domestic and international agencies on worldwide brands and start ups alike. Starting at Modernista! in Boston, MA I worked at creating experiential and guerrilla tactics to relaunch Napster. A semester spent in Londons' CDP Travissully put me in every position from Account Manager to Research Analyst to Creative Director. This experience, compounded with years spent in Boston University's Ad Lab program landed me at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners as an Assistant Account Manager on Sprint. With a natural tendency towards creative media, I became a Jr. Media Strategist for Doritos. During my tenure on the account, my campaigns have won top media honors at the Creative Media Awards and my department has been named "Media Department of the Year" three years running. Further, Goodby, Silverstein & Partners has been named "Agency of the Decade", a title I am proud to have contributed to.

I have an affinity for social media and how it intersects with creative problem solving. I work towards helping expand the digital innovation and social media knowledge of my clients and agency. I am fueled by ideas and take every opportunity to explore a new approach to an age old problem.

I have started numerous companies and occasionally one is successful. I consult on subjects of social media and new media production. I chronicle the new and culturally-transformative on my blog newantisocial.com. I write television pilots and publish humor pieces. I live to create.

JD Beebe’s Specialties:

Social Media Strategy, Emerging Media, Bleeding Edge Technology, Technology & Cultural Writing, Media Research & Planning (TV, Radio, OOH, Digital, New Media), Copywriting, Online Trending, Social Media Analytics, Creative Concepting, Publishing, Staying Fresh and Clean.
