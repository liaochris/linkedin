Sean Leach’s Summary

As the Senior Director of Technology for NeuStar's Internet and Infrastructure Services Group (IISG), Sean's responsibilities include defining and guiding the group's technical direction, as well as providing new product design, research and technical evaluation for strategic product and M&A initiatives. The IIS Group includes the UltraDNS, Webmetrics, Registry and all Internet and infrastructure related product lines.

Previously, Sean served as Director of Product Engineering for UltraDNS (acquired by NeuStar in April, 2006) where he led a team responsible for supporting and maintaining the company's directory infrastructure that powers over 20% of the world's registered domains.

Prior to UltraDNS, Sean was the Director of Engineering for NetLojix/Impulse Internet Services, an ISP and managed VoIP provider in Southern California, here he was responsible for engineering and operations. Previous to that, he was Director of Engineering for one of the pioneering e-commerce application service providers.

Sean holds a BS in Computer Science from the University of Delaware. His current research focus is on DNS, Internet infrastructure and combating the massive online crime epidemic.

Sean Leach’s Specialties:

System and network architecture. Team building and management. Sales and new product design and development. M+A.
