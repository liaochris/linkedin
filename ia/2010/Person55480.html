Devin Zimmerman’s Summary

I have been inspired by my experiences and am eager to use them and my education to benefit the world and its people.

I graduated cum laude from the University of Georgia's Grady College of Journalism and Mass Communication with my Bachelor of Arts in Journalism in Public Relations in May 2010. I also minored in French and women's studies.

I've completed several PR internships ranging from traditional media relations to event planning in the nonprofit sector and fundraising. I am also a certified instructor with the American Red Cross where I learn hands on how we, as individuals, can lay an impression on others. Now I'm diving into digital influence with Ogilvy Public Relations' 360° Digital Influence Team and learning how we can harness the social media space to inspire consumers and create branded content. This world is endless and is quickly becoming a passion.

My background is broad, enabling me with endless talents. I invite you to learn more about my professional experiences below and to connect with me on this great social media channel.
