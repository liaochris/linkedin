Abhishek Tiwari’s Summary

Well-rounded, results oriented technology executive with in-depth experience across the complete product lifecycle including; strategy, design, development and delivery of innovative products. Possesses broad expertise in Wireless and Web technologies and delivering large-scale carrier grade systems to fortune 500 customers.

Proven leader with ability to drive cross-functional teams, foster innovation, drive product vision, establish organizational processes and manage key customer relationships. Thrives in highly dynamic environments to consistently deliver against business and operational objectives.

Key Achievements

- Launched North America’s first Mobile Multimedia Messaging service (Q2 2002) on the Sprint PCS network as the Engineering Lead.
- Received award for the Best Multimedia Messaging Product in 2007 from Frost and Sullivan, for PictureMail MMS product as the Senior Product Manager.
- Holds patents around dynamic content editing, media editing and media organization on the web.
- Planned and executed pipeline management, plan of record and change control board process for delivery of messaging products within VeriSign, which processes over 100 releases per year.
- Manage and deliver businesses over $50M for Syniverse
