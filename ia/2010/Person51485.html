David Metcalfe’s Summary

As a writer, illustrator and theorist I focus on communication models inspiring connectivity and cultural relevance; fostering environments where true communities develop naturally.

As Connector for TEDxNaperville I am helping to coordinate and organize the first TEDx event series in the Chicago Suburbs. I also write for Planetshifter Magazine on issues of sustainability and new mythology, and for Alarm Magazine on music and culture.

In partnership with Planetshifter Magazine, I am developing the [open myth source] project. We're gathering conversations, stories, songs, images and creative techniques to foster understanding into the meaning, motives and processes of Mythology in the 21st century.

Various electronic publications have featured my illustrations, including Planetshifter Magazine, The Absurdist Monthly Review, Down and Dirty Word, Richard Stiennon's IT security blog ThreatChaos and my own electronic effort, The Eyeless Owl. Recently illustrations from a year long digital art project hosted by The Eyeless Owl were transmuted into a collaborative animation that was featured at select venues in downtown Chicago.

I've managed successful business intelligence projects analyzing the adoption of advanced technology within education, healthcare service and small to mid-sized businesses; including analysis of the cultural, psychological and social changes that occur during these adoption cycles.
