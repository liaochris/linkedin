Jim Warr’s Summary

Jim Warr was born with a voracious curiosity about the way things work. Jim’s parents took to gluing the screws on small household appliances, knowing that if they didn’t, their son was sure to disassemble anything he could so that he could uncover the inner workings and find a more efficient way to reassemble the item in question. This desire to take things apart and put them back together in an improved fashion has made Jim the financial planner he is today. He knows how to find money for his clients and does it with great care and empathy.
. Yearning for growth and for an opportunity to feed his undying inquisitiveness, he moved into the world of finance, beginning his new journey as a Regional Vice President of Primerica Financial Services.
True to his nature, Jim eventually left Primerica and started his own Financial Services Company, aptly named J.W. and Associates. .
Jim’s financial acumen and undeniable charisma made him a favorite fixture on talk radio in El Paso, Texas, Los Angeles, California and Albuquerque, New Mexico. His radio talk show, Warr on Debt became a local favorite, and inflamed those who subscribed to what was considered the “conventional wisdom” of finance and financial planning. Jim continues to be featured as guest speaker at business meetings and conventions throughout the country.
After working with wealthy clientele, Jim decided that he could use his combination of financial mastery and empathy to open up the world of finance and investing to the masses; the hard working people who needed the opportunity to expand their financial portfolios and secure their retirement. A desire to help this group of people inspired the birth of Warr Investment Group, where the customer gets the lion’s share.
Jim Warr is thrilled to bring Warr Investment Group headquarters to Austin.
