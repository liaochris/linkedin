Richard Suder, CFA, CFP®, ChFC, CLU’s Summary

As the founder of Auri Elan Financial Group, I am an experienced independent financial advisor and insurance agent specializing in providing comprehensive and objective investment consultation, financial and insurance planning to individual investors, companies, select group of affluent families and entrepreneurs.

Auri Elan consists of four divisions: 1. Auri Elan Financial - Investment, Retirement, Education, Insurance, and Estate Planning and Implementation; 2. Auri Elan Insurance - An independent insurance agency for your Auto, Home, Life, Health, and Business insurance needs; 3. Auri Elan Retirement Plans - Design, install, and maintain retirement plans for companies and small-business owners; 4. Yillionaire Family Office - A multi-family office (MFO) established for families with exceptional wealth.

I believe it is important to take a holistic approach to wealth management and aims to manage all aspects of my client’s financial situation. I provide clients customized advice on investment planning (Including Green Investing and Socially Responsible Investing), retirement planning, education planning, insurance planning, estate planning and etc.

Please contact me for a complimentary Portfolio (Morningstar Instant X-Ray) and Insurance Review.

If you do not take medical advice from anyone other than an MD, why should you settle your finance with anyone less than a CFA Charterholder and CFP Practitioner?

Please join my LinkedIn Groups: Innovestive Club of America, Greater Boston Konnection, Greater Boston Chinese Association (大波士顿华人协会) and Overseas Chinese Talent Pool (中国海外招聘人才库).
