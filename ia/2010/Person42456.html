Colin Hayhurst’s Summary

My interest is in helping to create and grow early stage technology and knowledge based businesses. I work with entrepreneurs and small high growth potential businesses and help them realise their potential.

I help to raise investment finance typically in the range £20,000 to £500,000 and organise a local angel investment club. I mentor and coach clients and help them in various ways including connecting them others that can help their business.

I have worked in and with a larger number of universities in commercial and research roles. The former in entrepreneurship programmes and the latter in physical sciences.

Previously I was a co-founder of Century Dynamics Limited, a scientific and engineering software company. Having successfully grown the company it was sold to NASDAQ listed ANSYS in 2005. Shortly after I decided to leave to pursue new challenges.

I am a chartered engineer with experience in a several industry sectors. My passion is software and I have been programming since 1976.
