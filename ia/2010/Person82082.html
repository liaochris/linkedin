Hila Strong’s Summary

A valuable asset to any organization, my diverse background and experience allows me to take intellectual risks, forge new paths, become a subject-matter expert, and produce results.

As a senior analyst, I believe that there is more to life than creating reports and trying to explain KPI's. When a VP asks me to "measure something", I try to separate myself from the task, understand the company's business strategy and use a more forensic approach to analytics, so that I can deliver a compelling story about our site visitors, and transform how we understand and measure our campaigns.

As an optimization specialist, I think it’s not just about the funnel, but about the identity of the users going through the funnel and their potential lifetime on the site. I work to build and test relevant user experiences for 28 social networking sites, running on three separate platforms, with teams located in California, Utah, New York, and Israel.

As a thought leader in the analytics industry, I am routinely called upon to share my feedback and help define Omniture’s product roadmap, as a member of Omniture's Customer Advisory Board. I have also been a repeat speaker at Omniture's annual Summit.
