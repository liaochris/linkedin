Regina Rowland, Ph.D.’s Experience

  • Owner

    Regina Rowland

    (Management Consulting industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    I partner with organizations to help them transform toward Sustainable Stewardship — an effort to co-create compelling sustainable futures where people co-evolve in harmony with their context and therefore thrive on multiple levels.

    This change from business as usual and from a profit orientation toward value creation may include the innovation of social structures and systems, sustainable products and services, or result in new behavior. For organizations this means going beyond leveraging opportunities for financial savings and market advantages by aligning core purpose, mission, and values with their vision of thrivability.

    Informed by extensive experience, I design and facilitate processes, programs, and support systems that start with the client wherever they are and launch them on their path toward systemic sustainability.

    My clients begin by constructing a shared vision and take positive action to make it their reality. They organically grow into a culture of collective wisdom and intelligence, generating possibilities naturally and solving complex problems wholeheartedly. As a result they participate in building social and ecological well-being and a new economy through synergizing environmental, social, personal, and financial needs and desires.

    In addition, I hold teaching and research assignments at multiple institutions in Europe and the United States, in the areas of organizational learning & leadership, sustainability-based strategy, and innovation of sustainable products and services.
