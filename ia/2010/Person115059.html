Kerry Sullivan, CPA’s Summary

Financial Executive distinguished by high drive, thought leadership, creative problem solving, and operations management skills which complement notable financial acumen and collaboration abilities. Experienced in Technology Outsourcing and Financial Services Industries.

Track record of partnering with Executives to improve bottom line results through more clearly articulating long-term goals and strategies to achieve, selecting the best investments, identifying and actively managing revenue and profit levers, assuring alignment with workforce incentives, and effectively administering pricing/costing/profitability management systems to allow monitoring, self-correction and delivery of targeted profits.
