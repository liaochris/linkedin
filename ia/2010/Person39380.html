Christine Shahin’s Summary

We are intrinsically connected to our environment. It is also our "common ground" in that what effects one will effect all. Ecological health, vibrant or poor, contributes to personal health respectively. If the air we breathe, water we drink, soil we grow food in is optimum, assimilated into ourselves, our health in turn is optimum.

What we create, manufacture, and purchase, comes from the earth, the environment. How we use these resources, depleting or replenishing - obviously determines if there will be more to use but also if what we use employed any toxins, also contributes to our personal well being.

Natural resources are our money in the bank - except there is no overdraft protection.

My Goals are to BE THE CHANGE and:
Provide Healthy Safe Beauty Options
Promote Awareness of Same
Propagate an Eco-Economy
