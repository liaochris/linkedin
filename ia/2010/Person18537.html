Marie Kamburoff’s Summary

TOPLINKED.COM
Senior Managing Partner of Aurelius Investment Group, LLC. AIG is a commercial real estate investment group dedicated to providing strong returns by means of cash flow and equity to our investor partners.

We are a private real estate syndicate, versus a public REIT (Real Estate Investment Trust). These latter investment vehicles are priced and tied to the public market on a daily basis. By contrast a private real estate investment syndicate is not as volatile, and it offers the possibility of higher returns than publicly managed REITs. Aurelius Investment Group, LLC pools your capital with that of other qualified investors for the sole purpose of acquiring and investing in commercial income producing properties. This affords the individual investor an opportunity to participate with an organized group of like-minded investors in the ownership of an income producing asset.

Each investor participates by contributing to the equity that is required for the acquisition of the commercial asset through the formation of an LLC (Limited Liability Company). This provides the investor the ability to achieve strong yields with low risk and no liability for debt or litigation. Furthermore, all acquired investments are professionally managed by property management firms holding the CPM designation.
