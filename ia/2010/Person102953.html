John Randolph’s Summary

John is a results-oriented, relationship-focused business leader with solid expertise in areas such as customer acquisition, client development, operations leadership and management. Over the past 21 years, John has successfully built, run, led and managed operations for two billion-dollar service organizations in the staffing, recruiting and executive search industry. In his career, John has successfully recruited and placed over 1000 business professionals primarily in the accounting/finance and healthcare service sectors.

Through his 21 years in the executive search world, John has an insider's view of the challenges facing today's professionials seeking to advance in their careers. His ability to meld that knowledge with a passion for helping people achieve their professional and personal goals was the impetus for Benaiah Consulting Group.

Additional expertise in areas of operational review, analysis, process improvement consulting, sales plan design, implementation and training. Our target client base is growth-oriented organizations that value relationships and results over volume discounts and commodity based service offerings.
