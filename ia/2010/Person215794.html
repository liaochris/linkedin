Will Kintish’s Summary

I help people in the professional, financial and academic communities to gain more self-confidence and get more business. How? By showing you simple step-by-step processes on how to work a room and follow up potential opportunities.

It's not selling, it's relationship building and yes, it can be great fun when you approach it with a positive mind.

I also help you get the best from LinkedIn. YOU CAN'T BUILD RELATIONSHIPS ONLINE...but you can start, reinvigorate and maintain a relationship.

There’s no substitute for meeting and even calling people but when used in conjunction with offline networking, LinkedIn is one powerful and time saving piece of brilliant software.

In today's challenging world there has never been more important time to go networking. We show you just how simple it is.
