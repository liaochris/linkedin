Wendy Wood’s Summary

Wendy is a detail-oriented, award-winning graphic designer with a proven record of success for creating and managing design solutions. Throughout her 15 year career, she has executed and managed large branding campaigns for Sprint, an international ad campaign for Warner Brothers, direct mail for Charles Schwab and facilitated all aspects of production for the Pottery Barn Teen catalog. Wendy is focused on quality work, building strong customer relationships and has a reputation for exceeding expectations.

Wendy is an avid reader who is passionate about cooking, traveling and spending time outside hiking, camping and gardening. She lives in San Carlos, CA with her husband, two sons and two dogs.
