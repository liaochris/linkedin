Nathanaël Lamellière’s Summary

Worketer is an innovative social network designed for recruitment for IT and Internet positions. Posting jobs and profiles consultation is free for recruiters.

Worketer is the only recruitment website that asks its users to complete their IT skills and level. We have a very rich and growing database concerning user’ skills in France.

As we just started the internationalization of the plateform, we're looking for beta-testers and professionals' feedback across Europe.

[2010, febr] After 14 monthes of beta, we started to design the new release of worketer.com that will include new services, for talents, universities and recruiters.

Feel free to contact me if you want some more information on the projects.
