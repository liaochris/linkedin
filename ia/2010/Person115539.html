Kevin Brady’s Summary

20+ years of experience in IT, 10+ years of experience as a security systems engineer and architect including leadership roles at AT&T (SVR4.1) and Novell (UnixWare). Contributor to security standards; POSIX, OpenGroup and Trusix. Author/Co-Author of multiple security standards.

Other roles included product and program management, relationship management with customers including AT&T, Intel, Cisco, Sun. ICL. NCR, Unisys, NEC and Hitachi. Stints as an application developer, Q&A engineer and team leader, software licensing manager, systems administrator and security marketing engineer.
