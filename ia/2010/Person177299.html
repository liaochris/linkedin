Ron Huber’s Summary

The internet is dominated by talented individuals most are designers or programmers. There are still very few business orientated individuals involved the industry today. I help bridge the gap between the drive to produce commerce and the desire to create something unique.

I started my career in the beverage industry with companies such as Coors Brewing and EJ Gallo. I learned a great deal about merchandising and the nuances of directing a potential buyer to take action. I apply those lessons today in the websites my organization constructs for our clients. The environmental psychology of directing a visitor through a website is something missed on many developers.
