Cathy Bormann’s Summary

HomeStyles Staging & ReDesign offers help to homeowners:

ReDesign:
Some of the most dramatic transformations have been on a budget of zero. One day decorating, or redesign, is the use-what-you-have philosophy of decorating that transforms rooms by artfully rearranging what is already in the home. Seeing your existing furnishings come together in the “right” way allows the homeowner to find the joy or recapture the memories of their items by seeing them in a fresh, unexpected place.
Real Estate Home Staging:
You’ll never get a second chance to make a first property impression!
Statistics show that homes that are staged have greater visual impact, sell more quickly with a higher profit than homes that are not staged. Prospective buyers make a decision within the first 10 seconds of being in the home. Staging will help create the model home look so they can “see” themselves living in your home.
Create the ultimate first impression for that perspective buyer by staging your home.. This is accomplished by depersonalizing rooms, removing any clutter and rearranging the furniture and accessories to showcase architectural features. Should the need arise; we can also help by providing furniture and accessories on a rental basis.
Senior Transitions:
Moving from a larger home, with an accumulation of lifetime treasurers, to a smaller more efficient space, can be a daunting exercise for any elderly family member. Whether on your behalf, or to help a beloved relative, we can assist with choosing what to take, how best to transport it, and then arrange everything in the new space. We can also assist with staging the existing home, for a quick sale.
Color:
Don’t feel bad if you have been obsessing over those same paint chips for months! One of HomeStyles most popular services is the Color Consultation. Making decisions about color and paint are often the most important (and the most difficult!) for homeowners or business owners to make.
