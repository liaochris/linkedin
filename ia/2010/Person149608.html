  • Director, Product Management

    Netflix, Inc.

    (Public Company; NFLX; Internet industry)

    January 2006 — May 2008 (2 years 5 months)

    One of the five directors responsible for the Netflix website ("Architects" is probably the closest term). Netflix is focused on connecting people to movies they'll love. My particular exploration had surrounded how connecting people to each other helps them all find movies they'll deeply enjoy. This was almost a mini-start-up within Netflix - smaller focused team, highly iterative and experimental. Goal was to either build it big or kill it within 3 years. Work covered online communities, social networks, RSS, blogs, APIs, and the implications of "Web2.0" on Netflix business metrics. In the end we killed site-centered community and moved to API-based solutions.
