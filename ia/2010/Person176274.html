Rodney Dunican’s Summary

510-260-6833
rod@rodventures.com

Rod Dunican is a Senior Learning Consultant with more than 25 years of experience in corporate training, speaking, consulting, coaching, management and organizational development. He has served in senior executive positions and has worked with a variety of Fortune 1000 clients including: Chevron Corporation, Cisco Systems, Citibank, GAP, Jansen Pharmaceuticals, JD Power & Associates, Let’s Go Learn Inc, LifeScan Inc, Mercer Delta Executive Learning Center, Mercury Interactive, Morgan Stanley, Nike, Novartis, and University of Michigan Center for Public Health.

Rod has developed or project managed over 119 web-based programs and has more than 2900+ hours of in-classroom facilitation experience.

Founder of The Dads’ Club Network—an organization actively engaged in creating and networking Elementary School Dads’ Clubs throughout the country.

Throughout his life, Rod has engaged his passion for learning, business, personal development, and people to help individuals and organizations thrive. He possesses a low-key, fun approach to the insanity of business, life and communication.
