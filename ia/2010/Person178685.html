Rafał Rudnicki’s Summary

Varied management and executive experience
- my whole professional career has always been connected to multinationals and involved intensive international operations, so communication skills, fine tuning and understanding complex business relationship and varied styles of doing business is what I have practised
- for 9 years I have been managing a small company/team as the top executive (the company was a part of a recognised international specialised consultancy network), so have a hands-on operational management experience and am fully aware of the related executive responsibilities
- for 5 years I have been a non-executive manager, introducing management system and responsible for corporate risk control in an international group of logistics companies; so I am at home making my way through complex management structures and understanding their governance as well as supply chain at work.
- I am comfortable with long term change projects and start-ups.

Rafał Rudnicki’s Specialties:

a) Enterprise Risk Management - one of the pioneers of that management discipline in Poland, with very vast knowledge and international experience; also crisis management and corporate governance.
b) corporate insurance - considerable practical experience from the buyer's end, in building relatively vast and complex insurance programmes
