Greg Fossen’s Summary

I started as a Lead Tech in the Oil and Gas Industry in the Gulf states, California and South America. I gained experience in Offshore Data Logging (Seismic, Hydrocarbon, Magnetic Field and Gravity).

In my early days in Colorado, I have enjoyed work in the Solar industry, selling Parabolic Systems, and in an Army support role, troubleshooting Laser Tactical training equipment.

More recently In the Denver area, I was employed with HID Global in the RFID Security Industry as a Design Engineer, Engineering Manager and Senior Manager (1984 thru 2008). I worked in Product Development , headed the DVT department and led a Sustaining Engineering team.

One of my passioons is to facilitate the installation of as many Solar Installations as possible in Colorado. This is one of my biggest motivations of being in the Energy Efficiency field.

50% of our nation’s Energy is used by our homes and businesses. I realized I can make a big difference by helping homeowners take part in reducing their energy use. As a result I have retrained myself to be an Energy Efficiency Analyst. Now I can specialize in saving money on utility bills; reducing the use of electricity, fuel and water, and increasing the comfort of their homes. All this will be a contribution to the larger goal of reducing our Green House Gas (GHG) footprint.

Implementing Renewable Energy, such as Solar and Wind generation, can be used in conjunction with energy efficiency measures to help make the system easier to afford and therefore be adopted by more homeowners.

I am RESNET certified for new and existing Home ratings and I am BPI certified as well. I am a certified Home Efficiency Consultant through Green and Save.com.

I have experience doing the Audits for Low Income Weatherization Assistance Programs sponsored by the Colorado Governors Energy Office, Energy Audits in Boulder City and County, and the Audits required for the GEO Rebates through ReCharge Colorado.
