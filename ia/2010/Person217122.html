Yamil Little’s Summary

DURING HIS ENROLLMENT AT THE ART INSTITUTE OF ATLANTA, YAMIL BECAME INVOLVED WITH STUDENT GOVERNMENT AND THROUGH TIRELESS DEDICATION WAS ELECTED AS STUDENT LEADERSHIP COUNCIL PRESIDENT. WITH YAMIL'S LEADERSHIP, THE SLC PLANNED, COORDINATED AND EXECUTED MANY SUCCESSFUL PROJECTS & EVENTS; WHICH INCLUDE PARTNERING WITH MAYOR SHIRLEY FRANKLIN FOR THE 2007 EARTH DAY PRODUCTION, 3 ON 3 BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT AND MANY MORE SCHOOL EVENTS.

THE NEXT STEP YAMIL'S DEVELOPMENT WAS JOINING GRAMMY U (STUDENT MEMBERSHIP TO N.A.R.A.S.) AND THROUGH PROMOTING THE PROFESSIONAL ORGANIZATION AND BUILDING MEMBERSHIP HE BECAME THE GRAMMY U CAMPUS REPRESENTATIVE AT THE ART INSTITUTE OF ATLANTA. THROUGH GRAMMY U, YAMIL HAS BEEN ABLE TO CONNECT STUDENTS WITH DREAMS OF BEING IN THE MUSIC INDUSTRY TO TANGIBLE OPPORTUNITIES WITHIN THAT FIELD.

CURRENTLY YAMIL IS WORKING WITH SUPER PRODUCER KEVIN "KHAO" CATES AS DIRECTOR OF NEW MEDIA & COMMUNICATIONS OF KHAO MUSIC GROUP. YAMIL STATES, "MY GOAL IS TO BE A PART OR BE ABOUT THE MOVEMENT THAT BRINGS ABOUT CHANGE IN THE MUSIC/ENTERTAINMENT INDUSTRY COMING TOGETHER TO CREATE JOBS, OPPORTUNITIES AND NEW WAYS OF THINKING. MOVING PASSED THE STRUGGLES OF TODAY AND BEING THE EXAMPLE FOR THE NEXT GENERATION GROWING UP RIGHT NOW."
