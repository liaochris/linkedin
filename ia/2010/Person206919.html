Tony Illia’s Summary

Tony Illia is a veteran journalist, writer and editor. His work has been cited by the Federal Trade Commission, US House of Representatives, Harvard University's Joint Center for Housing Studies, the Industrial College of the Armed Forces-National Defense University, University of Utah's School of Architecture, Investor's Daily News, Commercial Property News, Journal of Labor Studies, American College of Construction Lawyers Journal, and the Journal of Legal Education Studies. Illia has appeared on television and radio as a guest commentator, including 88.9 KNPR’s “State of Nevada,” KVVU Fox 5, and KTNV News 13.

Tony Illia’s Specialties:

Construction, development, real estate, engineering, architecture, design, building, labor, transportation, energy, housing, sustainability, water works, environmental issues, manufacturing/industrial process, and business related topics.

Tony Illia’s Experience

  • Board Member

    UNLV CEM Advisory Board

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    2005 — Present (5 years )

    The Construction Engineering and Management Industry Advisory Board to the University of Nevada-Las Vegas consists of roughly 30 individuals representating Southern Nevada's most prominent contractors, engineers, architects and trade associations. The Board supports and informs the Construction Management Program in the Howard Hughes College of Engineering. It meets quarterly. Illia currently serves as chairman of the Recruitment and Retention Subcommittee, which raises public awareness of construction as a profession as well as promote the program.

  • Special Correspondent

    McGraw-Hill Construction

    (Public Company; MHP; Publishing industry)

    2000 — Present (10 years )

    Frequent contributor to Engineering-News-Record, Architectural Record, Southwest Contractor, Constructor, Design-Build. His work received the Robert F. Boger Journalism Award from the Construction Writers Association (2000, 2009); Silver Award for Best Feature Article and Best News Article, American Society of Business Publication Editors, Eastern and Northwest Regions (2002, 2006); Platinum Hermes Creative Award for Best Feature Article (2009) from the Association of Marketing and Communication Professionals; and the Bronze Quill Award for Best News Article (2000) from the Las Vegas chapter of International Association of Business Communicators. He additionally serves as a juror on Southwest Contractor’s annual “Best Of Awards” program (1999-Present).

  • Real Estate and Development Reporter

    Stephens Media Group

    (Publishing industry)

    1999 — Present (11 years )

    Frequent contributor to Las Vegas Business Press, as well as Las Vegas CityLife and Las Vegas Review-Journal. Journalist specializing in real estate, development, and construction-related issues. His work received the Nevada Press Association’s 1st Place Award, Best Business News Story (Class III, 2005); 3rd Place, Best Explanatory Journalism (Class III, 2006); Gold Hermes Creative Award for Best Feature Article (2009) from the Association of Marketing and Communication Professionals; and the Bronze Quill Award of Excellence for News Writing (2000) from the Las Vegas chapter of the International Association of Business Communicators. Illia was named Real Estate Reporter of the Year (2006) by the Nevada chapter of the Society of Industrial and Office Realtors. His article, “Inside CityCenter,” was voted Top Story of 2008 by the Las Vegas Business Press. Illia was master of ceremonies for the Las Vegas Business Press’ inaugural Green Awards (2008); he additionally serves on the Industry Advisory Committee for the University of Nevada-Las Vegas’ Construction Management Program (2005-Present). He also was a guest presenter at the 3rd Annual International Real Estate Expo (2007).

  • Managing Editor and Co-Publisher

    Southern Nevada Real Estate Monthly

    (Publishing industry)

    January 2005 — December 2008 (4 years )

    Founding editor and co-publisher of a monthly industry newsletter dedicated to local commercial real estate news. The publication received the Bronze Quill Award for Best Newsletter (2007) from the Las Vegas chapter of the International Association of Business Communicators, and 3X Tri-State Pinnacle Awards for Best Newsletter (2007-09) from the Las Vegas chapter of the Public Relations Society of America, including "2009 Best of Show." Launched Emerging Leaders Awards in 2007, recognizing the next generation of real estate professionals; Illia served as master of ceremonies (2007-08). The publication is currently on hiatus until market conditions rebound.

  • Public Relations Director

    Associated General Contractors

    (Non-Profit; Construction industry)

    February 1997 — May 2000 (3 years 4 months)

    Developed comprehensive public relations program; organized, coordinated, and publicized chapter events, including "Operation Desert Clean-Up;" served as managing editor of Construction Connection monthly trade newspaper; served as association liason to UNLV student chapter; acted as industry spokesperson at-large; and promoted careers in construction at local area high schools. Work received two Pinnacle Awards from the Public Relations Relations Society of America, Las Vegas chapter, and a Bronze Quill Award of Merit from the International Association of Business Communicators. Illia was additionally featured in the national AGC publication, Constructor, as well as the Las Vegas Review-Journal, Las Vegas Weekly, Las Vegas Sun, and Las Vegas Business Press.
