Andrew Garrettson’s Summary

For me, it has always been about working with and for people. I believe there is not greater calling than being in service to others.

I'm at my strongest and brightest when I'm part of a team. It is imperative that I feel a strong sense of ownership on a project and accountability to my teammates. When this is the case, I can motivate the team to new levels and peak performance through my contagious enthusiasm and commitment to excellence.

I've been a career sales professional for most of my life, while simultaneously pursuing my passion for acting and theater. Ultimately, I wish to leverage my business experience AND theater background to become an entertainment industry professional. Whether this means actually acting professionally, or working in the casting, production, or education sides of the business, I'm very open to all opportunities. I love to teach and tell stories and am very comfortable in front of a group.
