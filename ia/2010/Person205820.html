Tom Bagamane’s Experience

  • Chairman and Founder

    The Giving Spirit

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    December 1999 — Present (10 years 9 months)

    In 1999 founded The Giving Spirit (TGS) as a vehicle to provide acute aid to a few dozen of Los Angeles' homeless - who make up the nation's largest population by far. TGS celebrates its 10th year Anniversary this year. TGS helps Los Angeles’ men, women and children who are homeless to “survive where they stand” by providing kits filled with necessities such as water, non-perishable food, blankets, pillows, sunscreen, toiletries and many other critical items to withstand the elements of their current environment. Volunteers assemble survival kits two times a year, (summer and winter) and have the life-changing experience and privilege of personally giving these items to homeless people right on the streets of Los Angeles.
