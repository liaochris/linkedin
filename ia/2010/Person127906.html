Louis and Jan Hartley’s Summary

We have owned and operated our own businesses for almost 35 years now. We actually started our first business together at ages 19 and 21. At that time we had a gymnastics center in Arkansas.

In 1987 Jan started their family sign business out of the trunk of her car. Soon she rented a building and began a "state of the art" sign business. The business was built on good old fashioned customer service. Louis joined Jan in 1988 and soon afterwards changed his lifelong career as a building contractor into a fulltime sign builder. His talents, combined with Jans customer pleasing skills began what is now the largest signshop in the area. They continue to stay ahead of the curve.
Our sons both have always worked with us. Bobby, our oldest son, is now operating the signshop with very little assistance from Mom and Dad. His degree he earned at UNF in art, coupled with his love of design made him a perfect fit for the business. Jason, his younger brother, has acquired his Dads contruction skills and a nice mixture of his Moms people pleasing skills.

We intend to always be here to help in any capacity we can. Today Jan has some limitations physically, but still tries to provide support. Louis is at the family sign business every day of the week unless he is traveling with our other business. Today our signshop serves thousands of clients all over the nation.

Louis & Jan are Executive Distributors in Monavie. We love teaching people the importance of diet and nutrition. We feel our diets & environmental factors affect all of our lives & play a major role in our well-being. Their network is 22,000 members strong.

Jan also has served in volunteer capacity for 6 years with the State Attorneys Office. There she councils teens who call her "Judge Jan". As a hearing officer she imposes their sentence, thus repaying society for their crime. She also volunteers as a mediator for another division of the SAO.

Louis and Jan Hartley’s Specialties:

Marketing and branding specialists, Networking of nutritional beverage Monavie, Distribution of Enzyme based cleaners for commercial cleaning, mediation and juvenile diversion for the SAO
