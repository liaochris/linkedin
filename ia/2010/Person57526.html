Dmitry Semenov’s Summary

With over 12 years of experience in real estate finance and project management, I specialize in commercial real estate financing, land use policy and planning, land acquisition, project design, project finance, sustainability, energy efficiency, and entitlements. I have worked on a number of in-fill and master-planned projects in California, Nevada, and Moscow, Russia.
