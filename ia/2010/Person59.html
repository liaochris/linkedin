Sherry Brickman’s Experience

  • Partner

    Martin Partners L.L.C.

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    August 2004 — Present (6 years 5 months)

    I am a generalist recruiter with Martin Partners and have over 20 years of retained executive search experience in all industries and functional areas. At Martin Partners, I have concentrated on senior level search engagements in general management, operations, sales, marketing and finance. Industry experience is broad and includes manufacturing, consumer products, technology, healthcare, business services, hospitality, ecommerce, real estate and retail. Very comfortable performing C level searches for portfolio companies of private equity firms.
