Ken Zwerdling’s Summary

With over 25 years of business experience, Ken is responsible for the company’s growth. He oversees the firms Business Development, Marketing, Strategic Partnerships and all Internet/Website activities. Prior to joining Foreign Translations, Inc. as CEO, Ken was the President of New VentureZ Business Solutions, Inc., a Management Consulting firm specializing in the preparation of Business, Strategic, Capital and Marketing Plans for emerging and expanding companies.

Ken has helped launch over a dozen companies in such industries as Technology, Internet, E-Commerce, Telecommunications, Health Care, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Financial Services, Entertainment and other service related companies. He has extensive experience in financial modeling and in raising venture capital. Prior to forming New VentureZ Business Solutions, Inc., Ken was the Manager of Finance at Ryder System, Inc., Controller for a national architecture, engineering and construction firm, Analyst for a publishing firm and an Accountant for Goldman Sachs, a Wall Street Investment Bank.

Ken has an undergraduate degree from the State University of New York in Finance and Economics and an MBA from Nova’s Graduate School of Business.

Ken Zwerdling’s Experience

  • CEO

    Foreign Translations, Inc.

    (Translation and Localization industry)

    January 1998 — Present (12 years 1 month)

    Foreign Translations, Inc. (www.foreigntranslations.com) is a 10 year old foreign language translation, interpreting and website localization firm headquartered in Greenville, SC. We translate documents ranging from technical manuals, legal contracts and marketing collateral to financial statements, training manuals, e-learning courses, web sites, medical journals, software, policy and procedure handbooks, newsletters and much more. With over 1,000 native translators located in over 30 countries, we frequently translate documents that range from 1,000 words to over several million in all the major languages of the world. In addition, we can provide interpreters for depositions, trials, sales meetings and conferences in every major city in the United States as well as every major country in the world.
