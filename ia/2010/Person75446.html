Gina Barrera’s Summary

My professional experience lies mostly in the arts. I am a professional Tap Dancer and Tap Teacher/Choreographer who started dancing at the age of four and teaching at the age of 18. I am a member of the professional tap dance trio, Diva La Tap in St. Louis, MO. I started producing live dance concerts in 2003 in Chicago, IL.. My first was Chicago Dance Movement, a 2-day competition, workshop, and show for Chicago's pre-professional dance community. In 2004 I took over production of the show "Duets for My Valentine" which had been a Chicago tradition since 1998. 2008 will be Duets' return to Chicago and debut in St. Louis.

I have also worked as a professional photographer in Chicago, IL and St. Louis, MO after I graduated from Columbia College Chicago with BA w/ a photography emphasis. I have also worked in the film industry in Los Angeles as a producer's assistant after I graduated from New York Film Academy's 1 Year Producer's Program at Universal Stduios.

Currently I am the founder of the nonprofit arts organization, Luminous Arts Production Co. Luminous' current programming includes the dance concert "Duets for My Valentine" to be presented in Feb 2008 in Chicago and St. Louis.
