Selena Delesie’s Summary

Consulting Software Tester, Agile Coach, and Experiential Trainer

I am available for consulting, coaching and training opportunities. Please contact me to learn about how I may be able to help you.

Software testing consultant and Agile coach with more than 10 years of experience testing, managing, and coaching software testing and agile practices for leading-edge technologies. I facilitate the evolution of good teams and organizations into great ones using individualized and team-based coaching and interactive training experiences. I believe there are no 'best practices' but an 'appropriate solution' for each situation.

I am co-founder and host for the Waterloo Workshops on Software Testing, Board Member for the Association for Software Testing, past chair for the Kitchener-Waterloo Software Quality Association (KWSQA), and an active speaker and participant in numerous industry-related conferences. See my blog and website for more details: (http://www.selenadelesie.com).

Selena Delesie’s Specialties:

Software management, project management, agile coaching, leadership/mentoring/coaching, building and enabling successful teams, context-driven test methodologies, software and organizational processes.

    Waterloo Workshops on Software Testing

    (Non-Profit; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    October 2009 — Present (1 year )

    The Waterloo Workshops on Software Testing, WWST, are an ongoing series of free workshops for experienced software testers and related professionals, co-founded and co-hosted by Selena Delesie and Kristin Goetz. WWST strives to build skills in software testing and allows people who are interested in topics in software testing to network with their peers. The emphasis is on mutual learning, sharing hands-on experiences and solving practical problems.

    Workshops are held over a weekend and are focused on a single topic central to the practice of Software Testing. They are limited to 20 attendees and are invitation only to ensure participant commitment and effective facilitation.
