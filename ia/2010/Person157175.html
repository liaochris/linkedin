Olga Norstrom’s Summary

Ability to research and understand complex medical, science and health studies and concepts, and accurately translate the information into everyday language. Experience in writing and editing service-oriented health and nutrition articles and Q & A's that are packed with information that is fresh, relevant and actionable for the reader. Willing to take direction and criticism from editors to craft the highest quality articles.

Olga Norstrom’s Specialties:

Content specialties include women's health, alternative health, sexual health (including men's sexual health), weight loss and nutrition, fitness, body confidence, and health-related beauty. Articles have been published in First for Women magazine, Child magazine and Pilates Style magazine.
