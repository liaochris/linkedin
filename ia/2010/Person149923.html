M. Susan Wilson’s Summary

I'm a Seattle-based writer, editor, and project manager with more than 13 years in the biz. I've served as Managing Editor of four magazines (two national, two local--including Seattle Magazine) and have worked as an online Content Writer and Project Editor at Microsoft (both as an FTE and on contract).

Over the years, I've written and edited on a range of topics for a variety of audiences. I've learned to facilitate and track multiple projects simultaneously, balance priorities, and review and refine workflows as necessary--all while bringing a sense of humor to the job. (I hold a law degree from the University of Wisconsin--Madison; there's no better training for grace under pressure than law school.)
