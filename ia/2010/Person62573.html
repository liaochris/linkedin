John Egan’s Summary

If you're having communications pain, perhaps I could help. I am an award-winning communicator with MBA and 21 years of energy-industry experience. I offer consulting services to utilities that want to increase the effectiveness and lower the cost of their stakeholder communications. More effective communications can reduce an organization's costs, increase customer satisfaction, reduce friction with regulators and communities, enhance employee morale and organizational effectiveness, and restore public trust. I have conducted research and consulted with utilities on these and other communications challenges. I focus all of my passions and expertise on fixing my clients' communications pain.

John Egan’s Specialties:

Customer communications; media relations; employee communications; customer satisfaction; community relations; marketing.
