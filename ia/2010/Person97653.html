Jordan Goldrich’s Summary

Jordan draws on his background as a chief operations officer, trained executive coach, licensed therapist and certified Employee Assistance Professional to help both experienced and emerging executives and business owners get performance and results.

As COO of a Behavioral Healthcare Company, he was instrumental in creating an 800% increase in revenue over seven years, attaining an HMO license and executing an acquisition by Wellpoint Health Networks. He did this by creating a coaching culture and by utilizing cross-functional team problem solving techniques rooted in the work of W. Edwards Deming and Kepner and Tregoe.

A specialist in resolving workplace conflict, Jordan helps senior teams stop the drain of money, people and productivity caused by valuable executives who do not collaborate effectively with customers, peers and reports.

Jordan is Adjunct faculty for the Center for Creative Leadership. Designer, Faculty Advisor and instructor for the San Diego State University Professional Certificate Program in "Coaching For Organizational Excellence." He is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker and Certified Employee Assistance Professional.
