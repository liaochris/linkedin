Linda Luetjen’s Summary

Integrity, professionalism and knowledge are the basic principles on which I have built a successful twenty plus years career in real estate. These principles have served me well. Working with a small team whom I trust, I place a great deal of emphasis on providing superior service to each and every one of my clients regardless of the size of the property. The purchase of any home represents not only one of the largest and most significant investments a person can make, but also a decision that affects the entire life of a family. It should be handled with all the care and attention such a significant transaction deserves. Ultimately, you may rest assured that I never forget my reputation depends on your satisfaction.

Finding the right home doesn’t start with Looking, it starts with Listening. As a realtor and longtime Gainesville resident, I know the market. But getting to know my clients and their needs is my passion. I help people realize their dreams, attain their goals and achieve extreme satisfaction when it comes to buying or selling their homes. I love what I do. I have the honor and fun of helping people like you find a home that's just right--one in which they can laugh, love and live to the fullest. I also consider it an exhilarating challenge to help people like you sell a home-- to get the property ready, highlight its best features and bring the right prospective buyers to see it.

In the future, I hope we may meet and have the opportunity to see what kind of doors we can open for you.
