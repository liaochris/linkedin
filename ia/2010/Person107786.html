Judith Schwartz’s Summary

MY FOCUS IS ON BOOKS AND BRANDING
In addition to working with publishers and packagers, I guide
individuals who want to self-publish their books through the design and production process, creating a professional product that looks as good as if it was produced at a publishing house.

I can also develop their branding, collateral, and marketing materials.

ART DIRECTOR / MANAGER / GRAPHIC DESIGNER
Over 15 years of in-house experience at design studios and publishing houses:
• Designing book jackets, book interiors, book prototypes
• Managing in-house design teams and outside vendors
• Working closely with editorial directors, editors and writers
• Delegating assignments to illustrators and photographers
• Art directing photo shoots
• Photo research and editing
• Tracking project bids, budget and production schedules
• Nationwide travel, attending focus groups and press runs

Judith Schwartz’s Specialties:

Print projects:
• book covers and interiors
• branding and identity for a wide range of industries including publishing, financial, music, non-profit, food
