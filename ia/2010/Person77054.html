Grace Thompson, SPHR, CTC’s Summary

At Lendor Consulting I coach small business owners and corporate professionals to be effective leaders, maximize productivity through improved relationships, and increase profitability for their organization.

I have over fifteen (15) years of Human Resources experience, which includes coaching executive leaders for the past nine years. I have worked in the financial, engineering, music, television, chemical, and association industries. Key roles have been in the areas of Benefits, Compensation, Learning & Development, Recruitment & Selection, and Employee Relations. In each of these roles, I have coached and developed executives and non-management staff, focusing on achieving peak performance through relationship management. My years of experience in both the for-profit and not-for-profit sectors give me a unique perspective of what is needed for success.

Grace Thompson, SPHR, CTC’s Specialties:

Coaching for professional growth and personal development, career transition, conflict resolution, performance management, relationship management, and goal attainment.
