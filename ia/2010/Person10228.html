Amy Lillard’s Summary

If you need clear, concise and compelling copy for your upcoming marketing projects, shouldn't you rely on a a writer that gets it?

Whether you need medical writing, feature non-fiction articles, or something more creative, I turn the complex into the readable, the product into sales. With more than 9 years of experience and expertise in writing articles, newsletters, web content, brochures, profiles, case studies, reports, and more, depend on me for eloquent and functional results.

Contact me for projects, questions or networking! Email amy@wayfarerwriting.com; visit www.wayfarerwriting.com.
