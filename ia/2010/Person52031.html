David Garland’s Summary

The quick story: I'm an Entrepreneur,marketer and the Founder/Host of The Rise To The Top on ABC and therisetothetop.com, The #1 Non-Boring Resource For Marketing Your Business And Yourself Like An Entrepreneur.

The more exciting story (or something like that):

After starting three companies and marketing some crazy things (including taking on the NHL with inline hockey), I became frustrated that I could not find an entertaining and educational TV show and online hub that resonated with forward-thinking entrepreneurs looking to grow their businesses and also a resource centered around smarter, faster and cheaper marketing...marketing WITHOUT zillion dollar budgets.

Bottom line, like you, I have a passion for doing things differently.

Like many ventures, The Rise To The Top was born brainstorming in a coffee shop and has quickly taken a life of its own motivating hundreds of thousands of entrepreneurs, marketers, experts and even big brands with inspired thinking, expert interviews, practical tips, unique events, creative marketing ideas and useful resources to increase profitability, lower stress and have more fun.

In addition, as Principal at The DSG Agency and Rangency Labs, I advise brands and businesses on innovative marketing, maximizing social media and digital schmoozing. I speak, consult, and hold workshops for Fortune 500 Companies, brands, marketers and entrepreneurs looking to become interactive, social and more profitable.

Lets chat it up! Feel free to email me at David@DSGagency.com
