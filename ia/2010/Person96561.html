Jerry Won’s Summary

Since graduating from the Marshall School of Business at USC, I have been successfully selling homes for various developers in challenging markets, consistently exceeding sales goals and scoring exceptionally high on customer surveys.

I started out my career with Pulte Homes selling the Solera at Kern Canyon by Del Webb community in Bakersfield, CA; I was then in charge of launching a new single-family community in Palmdale, CA called Sonoma Ranch.

I jumped at the chance to come back to Los Angeles and joined the Ryness Company selling The South Group's Elleven, Luma, and EVO bulidings. These buildings are located in the South Park District a couple blocks from Staples Center and LA Live and are Downtown LA's first ground up high rise residential community. I then took on the position of Lead Sales Associate with Doma Properties at Santee Village, a collection of three historic readaptive use buildings in LA's Fashion District. Most Recently, I was a Sales Associate at Vertical Properties for Downtown LA's most talked-about project at Park Fifth. After experiencing a tremendous start to the sales efforts, Park Fifth has decided to reorganize their sales and marketing teams due to its financial and internal restructuring. I then took the position of Sales Manager at Barker Block for The Mark Company. Barker Block is a loft community of 297 homes located in the Arts District of Downtown Los Angeles and is developed by the KOR Group.

I began a new career in the mortgage industry as a Mortgage Loan Associate at Bank of America in July 2009.

In March 2010, our team join the Builder Division at Wells Fargo Home Mortgage to better serve our builder clients in Los Angeles and Orange Counties. Our offices are located in Newport Beach and Century City.

Please visit my Wells Fargo website at http://www.wfhm.com/jerry-won
