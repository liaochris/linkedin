Richard J. Wallace’s Summary

WingIt Innovations offers me the opportunity to expand my duties and areas of responsibility and to more fully employ the diverse areas of expertise I have developed in prior positions and industries. I am certain that explosive growth is part of the immediate future for this privately held company.
I am a generalist, not having been trained in any specific business discipline. I am a quick learner who has held highly responsible positions (operations, procurement, sales and administration) in fire protection (distribution and manufacturing), high efficiency air filtration manufacturing, medical electronics and textile manufacturing. I am now employed by a manufacturer and distributor of grab bars and other uniquely high-end bath accessories.
My most recent but past experience, over seventeen years, has been spent in procurement. I have no need of replicating this and, indeed, have shown my willingness to move on to other more inclusive endeavors. However, I recognize my strengths and the value of my recent past accomplishments and I am enthusiastic about the extensive nature of the procurement responsibilities in my current position. I am exceedingly well organized and can handle a great amount of detail effectively.
