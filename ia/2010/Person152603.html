Neal Cohen’s Summary

I founded CoBe Capital over 15 years ago to acquire non-core, challenged or distress business from leading global corporations. Building upon my earlier endeavors where I acquired and operated small businesses and ran a portfolio of commercial real estate, I developed my M&A acumen and operational knowledge by becoming actively involved in the companies I acquired and running the day-to-day operations of my businesses. Along the way I have embraced lean and continuous improvement business philosophies, based on the Toyota Production System, and have implemented it at all of my companies. Another key component to our M&A strategy is the leadership of our industry specialists, which allows us to evaluate a company, develop our investment thesis and build a business plan based on their insight.

Today, CoBe Capital owns and manages a diverse portfolio of companies that design, manufacture and market innovative products and services. Our North American headquarters are located in New York City and we have established our European headquarters in Düsseldorf, Germany in 2010. My team of seasoned investment executives is responsible for evaluating and closing transactions from the proprietary deal flow that our M&A business development team generate.

Building upon earlier successes, our permanent capital base and the bedrock of a portfolio of companies delivering solid financial results, we seek to expand our existing portfolio of operating companies with the intent of growing our businesses for the long term.
