Cory Ferber’s Summary

Cory is an Interaction Designer, Service Designer, Software Designer & Information Designer with 15 years of experience working with Fortune 100 companies and popular brands. He specializes in human-computer interaction, user interface design, user experience, information technology, and Internet-based solutions. He has a strong mix of creative, technical, and business-related expertise. He has a passion for improving people's interactions with technology.

Cory holds a B.S. with Honors in Information Technology from the Rochester Institute of Technology, with curriculum concentrations in Human-Computer Interaction and User Interface Design. He graduated with a 3.8 GPA in 1997. He received an education in the study, design, development, implementation, support and management of computer-based information systems. He has a background in Industrial Design, Graphic Design, and Marketing. He is a visual thinker and intellectually gifted.

Cory has been working as a freelance Senior Consultant for 4 years. He has completed projects for Top 10 Fortune 100 companies and popular brands including Hewlett Packard, Walmart, Chevron, AAA, Cisco, and Diageo.

Previously, Cory worked as a Software Design Expert in Silicon Valley for over 3 years designing mission-critical web-based cloud applications for Amdocs Customer Experience Systems used in call centers, support centers, and on site by the US Top 5 Telecom, Fortune 500, the world's largest companies, and governments. This includes AT&T, BT Group, Comcast, DirecTV, MetroPCS, Sprint Nextel, T-Mobile, Verizon, and China Mobile.

Cory's designs help Amdocs win CRM Magazine's Best Agent-Facing Universal Desktop Software in 2007, Customer Interaction Solutions Magazine's Product of the Year Award in 2003, and awards from Gartner.

In the past, Cory completed projects for Nortel Networks, Xerox, EDS, Visa, Mitsubishi, Kenneth Cole, BMG, STA Travel, The Museum Company, and Hanover Direct.
