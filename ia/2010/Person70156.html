Mike Jones’s Summary

I have been lucky enough to be a leading light within Rich Application development for over 14 years. Working on some of the most influential projects within rich media, including a BAFTA nomination,

My experience covers a gamut of clients including Adidas, Sony, Playstation, Hoffman La Roche, BBC, IBM, Shell-Ferrari, Macromedia and Adobe to name a few. I have also been responsible for developing techniques within this area that are now cornerstones of development best practices.

I have spoken at and for many clients and companies on rich media development specifically within the Flash Platform arena.
