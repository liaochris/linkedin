Víctor Reyna Vargas, PMP’s Summary

Systems Engineer who is expert in Information Technology with more than 9 years of professional experience in formulation, analysis, design, deployment, and management of informatics solutions, with specialization studies in Project Management at Pontifical Catholic University of Peru and Mexico as well as Public Investment Projects at Engineering National University. He is trainer in Project Management and Microsoft Project Professional based in PMBOK of PMI as well as Process Improvement based in CMMI Model in public and private organizations.

He got the title of Systems Engineer with the thesis work which was approved with Distinction. He is expert in Project Management, Business Management and Process Improvement. Furthermore, he is certified by PMI in PMP certification, IBM in Rational Unified Process (RUP) and Requirement Management with Use Cases, and Microsoft in Microsoft Office Project 2007.

He has an active and leadership personality, self-taught person with easy mood to work in groups and under pressure. He has knowledge of Software Development Methodology, Quality Software Assurance, Development Tools, and English in a high level.

He is Peruvian and 30 years old. Son of Víctor and Flor, brother of Oscar, Jose and Fiorella, husband of Zulima and father of Víctor Leonardo, Sebástian Ándre, and Áaron Octavio.
