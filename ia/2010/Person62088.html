I am an Open Networker interested in increasing my sphere of influence, exposure and visibility.

I am an outstanding Professional Networker continually meeting new people and connecting them with resources they can use to further meet their goals..

Rainmaker Solutions: A Consulting and Coaching firm specializing in placing companies having specific needs with companies possessing the skills to meet those needs. From vendor services to hiring to in-house coaching and training.

Our staff posseses the skill sets and expertise to assist a company to become proficient and successful with improvement in their Customer Services, Sales, Marketing and Management.
