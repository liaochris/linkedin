Nate Roberts’s Summary

I have a lot of experience working with new and very entrepreneurial firms. Through these experiences I have developed a passion for the challenges, and excitement, which only comes from working within an entrepreneurial company. Additionally I am an advocate of Social Media, personally believing that it will change the way business is conducted in the years to come.

Before relocating to Florida for my M.B.A. I worked for LEXLI International. LEXLI is a very small, rapidly growing firm where I was not only responsible for the development of web based applications used by customers, and management alike, but management of operations as well. My high energy and dedication allowed me to thrive in this environment, as I was determined to make an impact in every project I was assigned.

More recently, as part of my M.B.A. requirements I worked for RedTeam Software this past year here in Orlando. RedTeam is developing a set of web based applications that take many concepts from recreational Social Media and is restructuring them in a way that complex service companies can operate more efficiently.

I also have experience working and consulting for more "traditional" companies but have always spent my time working to understand the unique challenges that face entrepreneurs and the companies they start.
