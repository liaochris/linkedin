Robert C. Hill, Jr.’s Summary

I have extensive business experience in the areas of commercial real estate sales & marketing, financing, general management and leasing. Over the years I have participated in positions that were very transactional, finish one transaction and move on to a new transaction, individual project and portfolio analysis, real estate workouts, and corporate survival situations. My work experience included companies in fields as diverse as international oil, life insurance, residential, and commercial real estate lending, development, and mortgage loan services. For the last few years I have been heavily involved in Tenant-Buyer Representation involving primarily office leasing, with limited Landlord representative experience.

The goal of Hill Corporate Partners is to become the Tenant-Buyer Representative of choice by those needing real estate services for their business.

Robert C. Hill, Jr.’s Specialties:

We provide strategic real estate planning for users of office, retail, industrial space, leased or owned. We team with trusted architects, attorneys, commercial furniture dealers, facilities and construction management advisors. We use our own processes to search, report, control time, and measure client identified goals. We become your real estate department.
