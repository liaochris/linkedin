Mark A.M. Kramer’s Summary

Experienced university lecturer, academic researcher and participatory observer actively reflecting upon and covering the intersection between mobile technology usage and its impact on our societies. Noteworthy: Active blogger, seasoned Surgical Technician, Gulf War I Veteran, Emergency Medical Technician and father of 2 very active boys.
