Ron Burkhardt’s Summary

Goal: Helping companies and organizations align their real estate, facilities and operating strategies with their overall business objectives.

Having represented hundreds of tenants in the last 15 years, and also being a prominent member of Newmark Knight Frank's national non-profit division, he works with such clients as Autism Speaks, Inc., Elizabeth Glaser Pediatric AIDS Foundation, The Asia Society, Urban Education Partnership, Alliance for Health Reform and the Institute for Health Policy Solutions. He has also represented corporate tenants such as Amazon.com, Woodside Energy, Neutrogena, On Assignment, Inc., Capstone Turbine Corporation, Acsys, Inc. and Lennar Urban in their leasing requirements.

Ron Burkhardt’s Specialties:

include servicing clients in every phase of a lease or purchase transaction including: facility acquisition, financial analyses, market research, lease negotiations, sublease representation, project management, logistics, construction issues and technological infrastructure.
