Rick Wallace’s Summary

Richard (Rick) Wallace (50) has a broad background in general business and financial management, manufacturing, distribution. engineering, quality systems, and customer relations. Rick is currently the Chief Operating Officer of Irex Holdings, LLC, a design and manufacturing firm that is introducing a Digital Reader (DR800SG) to the US consumer electronics market. Previously, Rick was engaged in professional business consulting for a wide range of operating companies. Prior to consulting Rick was an active partner in the Amandari Group, a private equity fund which focused on acquisition and growth opportunities in small and mid market businesses. Prior to Amandari, he led and successfully completed the sale of Fitness Products International (FPI), and transitioned the business to Precor USA in 2004. Mr. Wallace led a successful leveraged buyout of Icarian Fitness Equipment in January of 1998.

He joined Icarian Fitness Equipment as the President in 1991 leading a successful bankruptcy turnaround for venture capital investor The Voit Companies. Earlier, Mr. Wallace was a Manufacturing Manager for Dexter Electronic Materials, a major supplier of proprietary electronic epoxy resin compounds serving the computer, automotive, and biotechnology industries. Prior to Dexter he served in a variety of Manufacturing and Distribution positions with Procter and Gamble.

He received a B.S. in Civil Engineering from California State University, Fresno. Rick resides in Ventura County, California with his wife and family which includes three children (ages 23, 20, and 13).
