Print Steals’s Summary

GET ALL YOUR PRINTING AT COST!
In today’s economy, all businesses are looking to reduce overhead and operational costs. Printed marketing materials such as business cards, flyers, brochures, postcards, etc, are essential marketing materials to any business. However, printing is expensive. That is where we come in!
Save up to 80%, and still get superior print quality and fast turn around times.

HOW DOES IT WORK?
You will get access to a members-only website where you will be able to browse all the printing products we offer. Printed processes include: Offset Printing, Digital Printing, 1-2 Color Printing, Large Format & Graphic Design Services. We offer 98% of all the printed products that any business needs. Everything is priced to the lowest possible cost, and the savings are up to 80% off of retail. Membership is Only $29.99/month, saving you money from the very first order you place!

Savings Example:
The most commonly printed product is business cards. Buying 1,000 High Quality Full Color business cards from a retail printer will cost you around $120. Online sites offer the exact same product for about half the cost.
With PrintSteals.com, the cost is only $24!!.

Even if you just order business cards at $24 for 1,000, and add the $29.99 membership cost, you still pay less than online wholesale pricing! Which means... you save money with any order!
