Stephania Armani, Ph.D.’s Summary

My priority is to ensure each one of my projects reflects my client’s culture and market, adapts to their current and future needs and inspires their employees' and customers' creativity. By adopting clients' goals and applying strong project management skills and tools, I maximize my clients' opportunities appropriate to each project. My experience includes business development, contract negotiation, documentation quality assurance, managing Cities and consultants.
