Michael Simpson’s Summary

Michael enjoyed a very successful career leading US HR divisions for large, multinational corporations in healthcare, medical devices, professional services, retail, and manufacturing sectors. As Executive Director of Development Institute, Michael works across all industries including healthcare, government, professional services, renewable energy, education and manufacturing. He is a a recognized expert in organizational development, strategic planning, teambuilding, executive coaching, leadership mediation sessions, interdepartmental conflict mediation, change management, continuous process improvement, performance management, stress management, leadership, customer service, competency development, professional image consulting, influencing/negotiation skills, and leadership reinvention programs. In fact, Michael has developed and copyrighted innovative programs in organizational culture assessment, personality assessment, and leadership decision-making.

Michael’s clients love working with him as he quickly develops customized solutions that bring sustained benefit to the organizations he serves. Michael has worked across the US, Canada, Latin America, Europe, and Asia; taught MBA students at top business schools, and is a sought-after speaker for conferences, and executive retreats.

Michael Simpson’s Specialties:

Customized HR/OD solutions in recruitment/talent management, performance management, employee retention program design, career development services, employee satisfaction/engagement surveys, sales and presentation skills training.
