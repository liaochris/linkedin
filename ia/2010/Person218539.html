Chris Mercer’s Summary

Chris Mercer is founder (I got here first!) and CEO of Mercer Capital, a leading national business appraisal firm

● I work with business owners and attorneys, accountants, business appraisers, financial planners and other professionals serving private and public business clients nationally and beyond

● Contact information is found in Personal Information below
● My full biographical information can be downloaded at
http://tiny.cc/Mercer806

● I and others at Mercer Capital work for private and public companies in many industries providing services, including:

─ Gift and estate tax appraisals
─ Business damages, economic damages and lost profits
─ Appraisal review (informal and USPAP Standard 3)
─ Buy-sell agreements (see my book below)
─ Financial reporting
─ Litigation support and experienced expert witness
─ Fairness opinions

● My books (available at www.mercercapital.com) include:

─ Buy-Sell Agreements: Ticking Time Bombs or Reasonable
Resolutions?
─ Business Valuation: An Integrated Theory (with Travis
Harms)
─ Quantifying Marketability Discounts (about the QMDM)
─ Valuing Financial Institutions
─ The One Percent Solution for Managing Pre-Liquid Wealth

● Popular speaking topics include (educating my peers for years)

─ Reconciling Marketability Discounts from Different Methods
─ Subsequent Events: Does What Happens After the

─ Valuation Premiums and Discounts Demystified

─ Is Your Business Ready for Sale?
─ What is Value all About?
─ Building Value in Business and Life
