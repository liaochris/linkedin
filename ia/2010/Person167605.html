Rafae Bhatti’s Summary

. Advanced training in information security management (PhD) from the nationally designated Center of Excellence in Information Security at Purdue University, IN

. Experience in Healthcare Information Technology standards (HL7) and regulatory compliance (HIPPA), designed Privacy Enhancing Technologies for Healthcare at IBM Almaden Research Center. See http://bit.ly/9CLWX2

. Experience in Web-based information management systems, architecture, design, and implementation of a J2EE-based security framework for access control in distributed Web-services. See http://bit.ly/9EJEHi

- Experience in evaluating and analyzing large scale data warehousing workloads, worked on actual client engagement on one of the largest BI deployments in the financial sector in China using the IBM DB2 line of products at IBM Almaden Research Center. See http://bit.ly/a6wb9f

- Experience in design and development of secure database systems, applying cutting edge security research and technologies to Oracle database line of security products. One patent filed.

- Worked with National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) on development of applications based on the Role Based Access Control (RBAC) standard, winner of NIST student scholarship

- 20+ refereed journal and conference papers in the area of information management, with a focus on security management and access control

- Book title “A Policy Engineering Framework for Federated Access Management” published by VDM Verlag (2009), ISBN: 978-3639111019

- Several book chapters on Information Security in leading computer science and health information publications

- 1 patent filed at Oracle (2 internal filings at IBM)

- Post-graduation 5 year academic and 2 year industrial experience in the field

- 3rd place winner of the Burton. D. Morgan annual entrepreneurial competition held at Purdue University, IN

- Gold medal recipient for Best Overall Performance in entire undergraduate class
