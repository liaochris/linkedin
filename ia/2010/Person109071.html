Justine Bellavita’s Summary

Web Content and Community Manager, Editor in Chief, Writer. Start-up of editorial projects online. Launch and debug of websites, online communities and social networks.

Freelance Journalist with a broad experience in International Journalism, specialized in Fashion, Entertainment, Women, Food and Travels.

Nine years experience in web content and community management, four years working for a major Italian media group (Telecom Italia Media S.p.A.). Community manager for the female web sites of one of the biggest publisher in Italy (Arnoldo Mondadori Editore S.p.A.), now working as Editor in Chief and Web specialist for Rcs Mediagroup S.p.A. at the female web portal www.leiweb.it.

Three years in professional journalism corresponding from US and France for both magazines and the Press Agency ApCom.

Expert in web sites projects and launch, web content management, online community generation and in the integration of the Internet with others media.

Assisted in the production of short format news features from pre-production stage through post.

Trilingual: English, French and Italian. Highly motivated and focused, always interested in exploring new challenges in the means of communication.
