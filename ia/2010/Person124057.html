Leonora Giovanazzi’s Summary

Leonora Giovanazzi is an information architect and works on user experience design and interactive environments for web and mobile.

Graduate in 2006 in Communication Technology at the University of Lugano (USI) she designed and implemented the website www.poliform.it, using this experience on information architecture design to wrote her final work of master’s degree.

Since 2004 she has been working as freelance webdesigner and since 2007 as information architect in Sketchin. She works on design and redesign projects for online communication platforms, social networks and portals.

Recently, she is focusing on identifying patterns for mobile information architecture, that she defines “digital devices ecosystems”.

Leonora is interested in photography and she publishes her photos in her photoblog, Frammenti di realtà, where she share her passion with friends and people interested in photography.

She is active on italian geek girls group and barcamps of the italian techology community. She was part of the organization of eTourCamp and ExperienceCamp in Lugano.
