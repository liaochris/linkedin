Alyce Barry’s Summary

I've been certified as a Shadow Work coach and group facilitator since 2001. I've led workshops, facilitator trainings, and other group events, and I have a private practice seeing clients in person and over the phone. For more about my work, please visit www.AlyceBarry.com/ShadowWork.html, where you'll find a link to my online brochure.

My book, "Practically Shameless," came out in paperback October 2007, and sales are approaching the 1000 mark. The book was on Amazon.com's Bestseller list of books about Jungian psychology for seven weeks. The audio book was released March 31, 2008, and is selling well. Both editions are getting wonderful reviews from readers.

I'm working on two more books of my own, plus a DVD and two collaborations with co-authors. I write essays for several email newsletters, including the Shadow Work email newsletter, of which I am the editor (www.ShadowWork.com/swenewsletter.html). I have three blogs; you'll find links to all three at my home page, www.AlyceBarry.com.

Alyce Barry’s Specialties:

As a Shadow Work coach, I help people get their foot off the brake so they can move forward again. Shadow Work goes beneath the surface of a life to find out what's causing an issue and resolve it there. To see the differences between Shadow Work and life coaching, read my essay at http://www.shadowwork.com/pick-goals.html. As an author, I help people understand and feel less shame about themselves. Our shadows say good things about us.

Alyce Barry’s Education

  • Shadow Work Facilitation Training

    Certification , Shadow Work , 1997 — 2001

    Shadow Work facilitation training is known as the best training available in creating emotional safety and honoring personal choice. In addition to teaching facilitation techniques, it is the ultimate in physician-heal-thyself methodology, requiring that facilitators review and process every kind of emotional issue in themselves. I'm not suggesting that S.W. facilitators are "shadow-free" -- working with shadow is a lifelong and sometimes demanding spiritual path that compensates by giving a great deal of meaning to every aspect of life and offering peace of mind. Taking the trainings all the way through to certification takes most people 3 to 5 years because of the emotional processing, but the trainings themselves each last from 5 to 9 days and occupy a total of about a month. There are 2 training tracks, for individual sessions and leading groups, and I'm trained and certified in both. For more information, see www.ShadowWork.com/Trainings.
