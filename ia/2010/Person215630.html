William Owens (wmowens@alum.rpi.edu)’s Summary

Since the technical experts/design engineers of a company and their target market of customers need to know about each other, I provide the necessary bridge of communication so that both parties may flourish with the knowlwedge of each others' capabilities and needs. Its a two-way street of information and understanding that I facilitate. I begin by understanding the technology behind the copany's products and what makes them tick. I do the same for the competing products from other suppliers. Then, I analyze the needs of the target market and determine where and why the company's products fit best. Armed with this information I develop the ongoing efforts to communicate in print, in person, or on the web to the customers the suitability of the company's products to meet their applications as well as informing the company's development leaders the customer inputs and feedback.
