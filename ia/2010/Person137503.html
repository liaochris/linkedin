Matt Kelly’s Summary

Six years in the Mortgage Industry (2002 - 2008). Most recent position was as Marketing Director with one of the top on-line Mortgage Banks in the Country. I also Originated Mortgages.

Five years in the technology business (1996 to 2001) as VP of Sales and Marketing for two companies primarily focused on developing Internet Based technologies for the Real Estate vertical.

Six years working for Investment Banks (1990 to 1996)

In the past two and a half years I have worked on several on-line sales projects. If there is one thing I have learned from all of my experience it is that unless your Corporate Culture is HYPER reactive to on-line customers, you'll fail. For some business, attempting to sell on line is an accident waiting to happen. For those with the right culture, patience and mind set, it is a wonderful channel for developing new relationships, customers, and sales.
