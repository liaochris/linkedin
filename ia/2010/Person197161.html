Sukyi McMahon, MA, CPRW’s Summary

As a CERTIFIED PROFESSIONAL RÉSUMÉ WRITER (CPRW), I am a leading employment-industry expert in creating strategic presentation documents.

In 2006, I founded Forté Résumé Service to equip job-seeking executives, managers, and professionals with powerful personal marketing materials.

With 10 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE in the writing and editing industry and a Master’s degree and Bachelor’s degree in literature, I am committed to excellence in writing.

I am particularly effective in transforming outdated and dry résumés, or "chronological obituaries,” into ACTION-FOCUSED résumés.

My goal is to design résumés that address the needs of potential employers as well as those of my clients, and, in turn, to gain an audience with a hiring authority.
