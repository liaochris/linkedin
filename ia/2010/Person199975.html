Taryn Lomas’s Summary

Underground Elephant is a Performance Based Boutique Ad Agency. Our primary focus revolves around commoditizing information via web-form collection. Our main interests are in the Education, Cash Advance, and Financial areas of traffic and lead generation. Our mission is to bring an every increasing market population down to a more manageable size for our clients.

Our ability to deliver large amounts of self-driven traffic without the use of non-controlled sources, ensures a quality experience for the customer and the user. The difference between us and the competition is that we create a value exchange not just for the advertiser and consumer, but everyone involved in the process.

This spider web affect ensures success by making the designer of the landing page just as important in the process as the user of the data. That intrinsic value from the designer creating a successful campaign or the distribution team weeding out less attractive traffic produces a daisy chain of paying it forward.
