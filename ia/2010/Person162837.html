Peter Fitzpatrick’s Summary

Currently in my second year at the Sauder School of Business, UBC. After completing an internship at Union Securities from April - November 2008 I traveled to South East Asia for 4 months this summer and acquired my PADI Dive Master certification.

Currently I have taken the role of Vice President Marketing with the UBC Venture Capital and Private Equity Club and as the co-chair of our Clean Venture Competition (CVC). I am responsible for designing and executing the marketing campaign for our events and for the success of the new CVC.

I have also taken the role of President with the Top Guns Charity Organization, a breast cancer fundraiser at the University of Calgary, University of British Columbia and the University of Victoria. In three years of existence we have raised over 29 000 dollars. I am currently working on restructuring our operations model after an expansion from Calgary to UBC and am now overseeing the expansion to the University of Victoria.
