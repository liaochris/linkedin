Mike Dodge’s Summary

Mike Dodge serves as the chief executive of a multi-media and technology company with revenue over $35 million and a portfolio of over 50 owned & operated websites. Atomic's websites roll-up under 4 major hub properties: CraveOnline.com, SheKnows.com, GameRevolution.com, and theFashionSpot.com. Dodge oversees a team of 80 employees and 100+ contractors and freelancers responsible for editorial, operations, business development, marketing, ad sales, product development, and strategic planning.

He was formerly General Manager for the Home & Real Estate Division for Internet Brands, Inc. where he oversaw the division’s expansion from three to twelve major web properties. Prior to this, he served as Sr. VP/Strategy at GE Money - WMC Mortgage, where he developed a number of new product and business ventures for GE before running their cross-border mortgage lending business. He also served as CMO for Zensys A/S, a Cisco & Intel-backed wireless technology company, and held management positions with McKinsey & Co. and the Proctor & Gamble Company. He holds a BA in Political Science from the University of Chicago and an MBA from the Harvard Business School.
