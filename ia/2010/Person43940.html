Cedric Pilard’s Summary

I'm a video and multimedia producer with over 8 years' experience in Europe and the US. I have worked on many different projects including corporate films, documentaries, and commercials. I've worked with a wide range of cameras SD/HD and software including After effects, Final Cut Studio, Photoshop, Flash, Dreamweaver and I am currently expanding my skills to 3D with Cinema 4D.

  I've worked full-time as a video manager in a fast-paced ad agency, where I was responsible for all phases of video production, from writing to shooting to editing to posting on the web.
I am used to working under pressure, keeping to tight deadlines, and juggling several projects at the same time.
I do a lot of motion graphics in after effects. I shoot and edit and I really like working from beginning to end on a video project.

For more information, please visit: http://cpilard.com
