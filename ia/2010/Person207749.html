Russell Johnson’s Summary

President of Travelmedia Communications, producing messages and media ranging from issues-oriented video documentaries on the future of travel, sustainable tourism and web-based and multimedia educational programs to promotional videos for cruise lines and destinations.

Also works with high-tech firms in Asia and the US producing viral videos for YouTube and other social networking sites and doing media training for executives.

Produces scalable media for delivery of messages in platforms ranging from high definition television to mobile phone video.

Published writer and photographer.

Experienced public speaker in the fields of travel technology and sustainable tourism.

Master of Ceremonies at numerous international conferences, some featuring heads of state.

More than 30 years in broadcasting as a TV and radio newscaster, documentary producer and radio and internet radio host.

Russell Johnson’s Specialties:

Deep knowledge of the travel industry, destination and travel marketing, sustainable tourism and media technology.

Experience in working with sensitive business and cultural issues.
