Henk van Soest’s Summary

Passionate entrepreneur and manager:

“I thrive on working in continuously changing environments, realizing targets in ever changing situations is what I love most. My true passion is motivating teams, diverse in gender, culture and languages especially in an international environment. Setting a project in its correct perspective on basis of a sharp analysis, followed by its “execution” does bring out the best in me. Continuously learning, from partners and associates, project successes and failures, the projects context, all in combination with maximizing the use of new concepts, methodologies and technologies is always highly satisfying. Creating added value for people and companies is what I’m about.”

Introduction:

Starting in 1993 as interim manager, project and program director, I have been privileged to be involved in the management of a great variety of programs and projects in the Netherlands and abroad.
A range of different projects which took me from strategic re-orientation to, “cleaning” and re-structuring organizations, from designing administrations to implementing ICT policies, processes, departments and systems, from designing and implementing tailor made project management methodologies to the startup of new initiatives and international subsidiaries
In recent years I successfully managed projects for a large number of companies; Sena, InterXion, BP Solar, CitiBank Spain, ING Real Estate Spain, Union Fenosa.
In 2003 I founded Activity PMS a Spanish consultancy group with 25 very diverse and internationally oriented consultants.

My remuneration has always been strongly related to the achieved results. Committing oneself in such away to company and project adds a dimension to ones involvement.
