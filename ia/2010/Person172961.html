Rick Alcantara’s Summary

Rick Alcantara is the award-winning founder and principal of Tara Communications LLC. He is an expert in employee communications, strategic planning, research and online communications. He is also versed in change communications, crisis planning, and merger and acquisition communications.

He is adept at helping organizations identify communication challenges and opportunities. His work has helped clients reduce expenses, increase revenue, generate awareness, engage employees, and align offline communications with web-based outreach efforts. His approach to communication stresses measuring outcomes over output.

Rick has worked with organizations of all sizes across various industries, including: AstraZeneca, Exelon Energy, CIGNA, Subaru of America, Washington Mutual, American Airlines, Public Relations Society of America, Volunteers of America, Chartered Property Casualty Underwriters Society, John Templeton Foundation, City of Woodbury and others.

He is a two-time national committee co-chair for PRSA, past president of the Philadelphia Chapter of PRSA, former vice president of the Professional Communicators of Southern New Jersey, and former board member of the Philadelphia Chapter of the International Association of Business Communicators and the Gloucester County Business Association. He is a member of the prestigious Union League of Philadelphia and Philadelphia's Center City Proprietors Association.
