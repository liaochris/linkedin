James Taylor’s Summary

Educated in Electrical Engineering, with an emphasis on signal processing, microprocessor and logic design, and statistical pattern recognition. I have 26 years experience in competitive product development environment delivering embedded microprocessor, signal processing and biomedical signal acquisition systems. I am skilled at developing real time signal processing and pattern recognition systems for new medical devices. I have had proven success creating and supporting tools for data collection and and clinical studies for FDA approval of new devices.

Experienced and capable embedded programmer, with development experience using C, C++, assembly, shell scripting, Perl and Matlab.
