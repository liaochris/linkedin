Shannon Wallace’s Summary

In my role at Chemic, I do method validations and various SOP-driven assays. I have experience developing potency, purity (with an emphasis on forced degradation, impurities and degradation compounds), and extractables and leachables. Most of my projects center around HPLC methods.

I also do the statistical analysis and technical writing for my validations, from writing the protocols, reports, and resulting SOP's for methods.

I am open to new types of experiences and am also interested in process chemistry and organic synthesis (which I also have experience in).

Also, being a people-oriented individual, I hope to find a career that allows me to work with people, whether just collaborating with colleagues on challenges, or working directly with customers.
