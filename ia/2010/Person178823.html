Mike Rupert’s Summary

More than 15 years of public relations and journalism experience for mid- to large-market daily newspapers in New York City, Southwest Michigan and the District of Columbia.

Currently Communications Director for major agency with the Government of District of Columbia where our campaigns have been featured in the New York Times, Washington Post, USA Today, Washington Examiner, numerous national blogs and other media.

Most recently, I have started a online Twitter chat using @localgovchat called #localgovchat which has brought together leading government social media leaders and novices to share ideas, best practices and develop strategies. The chat literally includes people from around the country and around world - including Australia, Denmark, UK, Canada, Brazil, etc.

I also have a masters degree in digitial communication strategy from Georgetown University.
