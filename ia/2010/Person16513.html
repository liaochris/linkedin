Arne McDaniel’s Summary

Assist owners of successful mature businesses in sale of their business, growth by acquisition, and strategic redirection.

Guide individuals and companies through a systematic process of review, selection, valuation, and finance of businesses available for acquisition.

Certified Business Intermediary and Fellow of the International Business Brokers Association.

Arne McDaniel’s Specialties:

Business Buyer Advisory Service, providing one-on-one counsel to buyer candidates nationwide. Full service mentoring program from deciding to consider the purchase of a business as a career choice, through selection, valuation, and closing.

Business ownership transfer, assisting business owners and specializing in business-to-business enterprises with revenues $750,000 to $50,000,000.
