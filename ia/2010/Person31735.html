Michael S. Paul’s Summary

Michael S. Paul is the President of Local Area Marketing,Inc., a consulting firm specializing in creating sales and marketing strategies for companies marketing to local communities. In addition, Michael is the President of Campus Area Marketing, LLC, a newly formed company, which will provide National and Regional Advertising and Business Development outsourcing for companies that have out of home media boards, college publications (Campus Telephone Directories, Student Planners, College Newspapers), mobile telephone and websites that are marketed to students on college and university campuses nationwide.

For 20 years, Michael was the Chief Executive Officer, President & Director of Business Development and National Sales of AroundCampus, Inc, founded fully owned subsidiary College Directory Publishing, Inc. in his dorm room while a college student in 1986. College Directory Publishing was the largest publisher of campus telephone directories in the country and also published student planners and schedule of classes. Michael offers over 20 years of management experience in National and local sales, business development, print production, and contract negotiation. Mr. Paul began his tenure with College Directory Publishing as Publisher of all publications and has been integral in the tremendous growth of our company. He became Chief Executive Officer of the company in 1988. During the 20 year operating history of the Company, Mr. Paul has negotiated every university contract and developed all third party relationships as well as integrated several mergers and acquisitions in both the private and public arenas.

Contract negotiation, business development, mergers and acquisitions, sales management, publications & production management
