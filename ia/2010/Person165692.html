Prathibha Sahu’s Summary

I hold a Master’s Degree in Human Resource and have experience working in a medium to large, publicly traded company for the past eight years in the field of Human Resources in the Information Technology/ Software Services Industry.

I have worked as both a HR Generalist as well as a Recruiter. As a HR generalist I have framed policies for the organization, created Appraisal Forms and Feedback mechanisms for the employees, have executed employee engagement programs, etc. As a recruiter I have hired 1000+ Campus Recruits in a span of 6 months and have hired senior/middle/junior IT Professionals for the organization.
