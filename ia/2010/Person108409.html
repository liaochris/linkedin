Julie Heidelberg’s Summary

As the owner of Heidelberg PR, Julie has 15 years of public relations and marketing experience in the non-profit, corporate and educational sectors. She was also a public relations instructor at the University of South Florida School of Mass Communications for five years.

In 2001, Julie started Heidelberg PR, a full-service public relations firm serving clients in a variety of industries including healthcare, real estate, higher education, tourism and retail.

Heidelberg PR’s services encompass strategic planning, campaign design, media relations, event planning, advertising, internal communication, web site development and video production.

Notable clients include AAA Auto Club South, BayCare Health System, the University of South Florida, Wal-Mart and Westfield, Ltd.
