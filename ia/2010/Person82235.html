Mel Kleiman, CSP’s Summary

"If you hire the wrong person, all of the fancy management techniques in the world won’t bail you out." – Red Auerbach winningest coach in the NBA

"The day we screw up the people thing it’s all over." – Herb Kelleher, founder of Southwest Airlines

"If you beat your competition to the best employees, those are the employees who will help you beat your competition." –Mel Kleiman

As the onetime owner of three different businesses (including the largest group of Hertz Rent-a-Car franchise locations in the U.S.), my expertise is based on my personal experience as well as over 30 years of work helping companies design systems to implement successful employee hiring programs.

I have worked with hundreds of clients including many leading restaurant, retail, and franchise concerns and have delivered over 500 custom-designed presentations on these subjects.

I was awarded the National Speakers’ Association Certified Speaking Professional designation in 1999 and have written five books including the bestselling Hire Tough, Manage Easy. I am a regular contributor to Restaurant Hospitality, Multiunit Franchise, and Convenience Store Decisions magazines and serve as a board member for both the National Restaurant Association Educational Foundation and the Workforce Institute.

  • Certified Speaking Professional

    National Speakers Association

    (Events Services industry)

    August 1999 — Present (11 years 1 month)

    The Certified Speaking Professional (CSP) designation, conferred by the National Speakers Association and the International Federation for Professional Speakers, is the speaking profession's international measure of professional platform skill.

    The CSP designation is earned through demonstrating competence in a combination of standards:

    Professional platform skills;
    Professional business management;
    Professional education; and
    Professional association.
    Speakers must be members of NSA or another IFFPS organization for at least three years and complete a rigorous application process to earn the CSP designation.

During that time we worked with companies such as Exxon, Shell, JIffy Lube, Tesoro, Hess, Continental Airlines, Sheetz, Caterpillar Tractor, Sysco Foods Coca Cola and many other to help them improve their hiring processes.
