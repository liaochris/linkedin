Paul Sonnier’s Summary

As founder of the Wireless Health group on LinkedIn, I am a passionate wireless and mobile health (mHealth) industry evangelist working to catalyze the efforts of all stakeholders in the ecosystem.

The Wireless Health group is explicitly dedicated to advancing knowledge and building relationships between professionals interested in the convergence of wireless technology with the continuum of clinical healthcare (preventive, chronic, and acute) and consumer health (physical fitness, wellness, and elderly monitoring). Of particular focus is the mobility of people and resources afforded by wireless features within health-related products and services.

Since its inception in August of last year, the group has grown to include approximately 3,000 global members and become the de facto online community for professionals interested in accelerating the development and market adoption of wireless and mobile health solutions.
