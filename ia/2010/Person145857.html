Michael Petras’s Summary

I am a veteran Executive Recruiter who has built a solid network of long term relationships with Executives, Managers, and Professionals within the discrete manufacturing sector. I am in my 15th year of recruiting.

I have a reputation of honesty and integrity. I go about my work in a pragmatic, systematic way through people I know and trust. I always put the needs of my candidates and clients first.

I specialize in recruiting and placing people in the following disciplines: Engineering, Purchasing, Lean Manufacturing, Sales, Plant Management, Directors and Executives.

I serve the following industries: Automotive, Agricultural Equipment and Machinery, Construction Equipment and Machinery, Electromechanical Products, Electronics, Factory Automation Equipment, Hand Tools, Heavy Truck, HVAC, Industrial Equipment and Machinery, Material Handling Equipment, Medical Device, Metal Forming and Fabrication, Plastics, and Recreational Vehicles.

My key client companies and candidates are primarily located in these 10 states: GA, IN, IL, KY, MI, OH, WI, NC, SC, TN.

I have a network of over 10,000 manufacturing professionals who have expertise with complex engineered products manufactured using the following processes: assembly, bending, blanking, blow molding, brazing, broaching, casting, cold forming, die-casting, extruding, forging, glass fabrication, grinding, heat treating, machining, metal forming, milling, plastic injection molding, painting, plating, powder coating, re-manufacturing, roll forming, sheet metal, stamping, surface mount technology, and welding.
