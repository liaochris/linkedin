Kevin Platt’s Summary

I have been working in the advertising world since July of 2008, having had experience in restaurant marketing for the two years previous to that. I am well versed in copywriting for both digital and analog advertising (both long and short copy), and have a very thorough understanding of digital marketing for a variety of different clients.

As you will note from my recommendations, I am driven, knowledgeable, and dedicated to delivering the best quality of work, no matter what it takes. I pride myself on my holistic view of projects; namely, I feel that in order to create the best deliverable — whether it be strictly copy, a full marketing plan, or anything in between — it is necessary to not only immerse myself in the client and subject matter, but also consider the wider range of possibilities for a given project.

This method has forced me to gain a thorough understanding of the technologies, media, and tactics of the advertising industry, as I do not tailor my content to fit my technology, I find the best technology to achieve my desired outcome.
