Vispy Mistry’s Summary

Vispy is a results oriented senior technology manager with over 13 years of proven expertise in analyzing, designing and implementing innovative Business Intelligence / Data Warehouse (BI/DW) and Master Data Management (MDM) solutions for large clients across various industries. He strives to create a collaborative environment that maximizes performance and return on investment for clients. Vispy has also created Business Case, Strategic Vision and Point of View (POV) documents for clients on topics that range from IT Cost Reduction, Application Portfolios, Clinical Information Systems (CIS), BI/DW Architectures and Pre-ERP EIM strategies. Vispy's broad experience, understanding of data warehouse delivery and industry experience position him for strong project success.
