Janet Pomeroy, MBA’s Summary

I am owner and founder of The goodMix, a San Francisco-based strategic eco-marketing firm. My colleagues and partners help socially-responsible companies formulate the best strategies for long term success. My Green MBA + advertising, public relations, and e-marketing skills have garnered amazing results.

Successful Projects:
+ Lead marketing directives for West Coast Green, the first and largest residential green building trade show in the world for inaugural and second year .

+ Marketing plans exceeded registration goals by 30% for year 1. Coverage by national and international media (including HGTV, five local television stations; live radio coverage, and more than 200 print, radio, internet and television media outlets; setting up key sponsorships and partners including PG&E, Google and Whirlpool, in 2007.

Lead marketing, educational and business strategies for new education programs at New College of California, resulting in program launch in five months. Goals to have complete cohort, with diverse student body and business partnerships in place will be met in its new San Francisco location. Worked with multiple stakeholders, leading projects from non-profit, business and student perspectives. Ensured the effective promotion, communication and forum for prospective students.

+ Managed marketing strategy for The Roxie Film Center which lead renovation, innovative marketing research A cultural icon, The Roxie, is making a profit, and has cultural and educational programs for the New College and Mission communities.

+ Founded Digital Hive EcoLogical Design, leading first design firm led by two Green MBAs.

+ Created strategic process for user experience designers and developed research techniques and prototypes for Fortune 100 companies.

+ Ran television production department for leading cable corporation, increasing revenues by 50%

+ Produced, wrote and directed educational video; in national circulation for over 15 years.
