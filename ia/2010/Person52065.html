David Spark’s Summary

More than fourteen years in tech journalism in radio, TV, print, and online. Plus ten years experience in advertising.

Spark Media Solutions helps companies create a company-branded media network so they can build an industry voice. That "voice" and information provides the answers people need as they're going through the decision making process to buy your product.

You can also think of us as offering that middle layer between agencies and new media vendors as we build custom published and new media campaigns.

David Spark’s Specialties:

Content management, custom publishing, social media, new media, consulting, editing, writing, TV production, tech journalism, comedy writing.
