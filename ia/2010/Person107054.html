Jeff Rudluff’s Summary

Current: Online Performance Marketing, LP (OPM) specializes in developing and optimizing the Internet as a channel for small and medium size businesses. We deploy a myriad of tools continually optimized to obtain the best result.

Tools include paid search engine placement, optimization and management, organic search engine optimization, creative development, display advertising, CAN-SPAM compliant email campaign marketing, social media marketing and monitoring, affiliate program development, target marketing and analysis with a goal of increasing sales, positioning the brand consistently and achieving positive return on investment.

Match.com: Responsible for the product development and customer care operations of Match.com

Grew annual revenue from $29m to $148m; grew subscriber base from 25,000 to over 500,000 customers with over 1 billion page views. Consistently #1 ranked website in category according to Comscore/Media Metrix.

Managed outsourcing transition of internal customer care to external organization; grew customer care organization to over 300 care reps.

Responsible for all aspects of the site, including: design, functionality, usability, product specifications, customer support, content management, pricing, and management reporting.

Established New Product Development organization

Online advertising sponsorships implemented with Logitech, and Nokia, among others.

Acted as media contact for new product initiatives. New products featured on 60 Minutes, Nightline, Newsweek, and Wall Street Journal Online.
