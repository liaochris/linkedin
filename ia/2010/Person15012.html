Anthony Spotora’s Summary

Attorney of a different color. Extensive background in business/corporate and entertainment/IP matters. The wherewithal to effectively manage a law practice while simultaneously serving as an Executive Officer to a number of outside Companies. Nominated "Rising Star" in California's "SuperLawyers 2008". Awarded background in writing. Requested panel speaker for ABA's Business Law Section and UCLA's Entertainment Program. For 8+ years, have been building and branding a unique legal boutique whereby our services are "a la carte" to our clients' needs; all attorneys are senior level counsel; clients enjoy personalized attention due to our streamlined size and structure; there are no minimum billable hour requirements which fosters client trust and attorney enthusiasm and; our "virtual lawyer" program allows our attorneys to attend to any client matter...as needed.... worldwide!

Anthony Spotora’s Experience

  • Managing Attorney

    Spotora & Associates, P.C.

    (Law Practice industry)

    2001 — Present (9 years )

    Now in our 9th year, Spotora & Associates has grown into a general practice law firm with emphasis remaining in business & corporate law as well as that of entertainment & IP. Serving both litigation & transactional needs, our clients range from multi-million dollar, publicly traded companies to "Mom and Pop" start-ups, to talent. No client is too big nor too small. We have a number of highly qualified lawyers, all of which are sr level counsel, with experience in business/corporate, entertainment, IP, family, real estate/construction, criminal, tort law, et al. I primarily represent clientele with matters in business/corporate and entertainment/IP, both online & offline. I regularly form and structure companies; negotiate and draft extensive agreements; work with and against various entertainment giants & studios; research and secure and/or defend client related copyrights and/or trademarks; secure financing and; assist our clients in achieving their long-term goals.

  • VP Legal Affairs

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    2006 — 2007 (1 year )

    While maintaining my position as Managing Attorney for Spotora & Associates, PC, I served as VP of Legal Affairs for this start-up company consisting of 5 partners who were venturing into new health supplementation opportunities once realizing the multi-million dollar success of their homeopathic medicinal company, Vaxa (www.vaxa.com). My duties consisted of everything from forming and structuring a Delaware Limited Liability Company to drafting all Company related contracts (online and offline) and researching and securing all Company related intellectual property rights.

  • President & General Counsel

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    2005 — 2006 (1 year )

    While maintaining my position as Managing Attorney at Spotora & Associates, PC, I served as VP of Legal Affairs for this health minded "sister" Company to Nutribrands Ltd out of South Africa. My responsibilites included corporate formation and stock issuance, negotiating and drafting a National Manufacturing & Distribution deal, securing shelf space in 11,000+ retail outlets, negotiating our market position as GNC's new, premiere line of health supplements, corporate formation and stock issuance, business plan and private placement memorandum preparation and, securing all corporate investments.
