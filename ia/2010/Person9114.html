Amara Poolswasdi’s Summary

With five years of experience in branding, marketing and design development, Amara has a proven record as a project manager and design specialist. She is an Addy Award winner who has taken her skills to businesses and organizations around the world. Amara currently operates Unicorn Press, a boutique communications studio, and manages The Rainmaker Network's brand. When she's away from her office, Amara can be found helping non-profit organizations reach their marketing and outreach goals.
