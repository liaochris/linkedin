Jonathan Edelheit’s Summary

Jonathan Edelheit is Chief Executive Officer of the Medical Tourism Association and is considered an expert in medical tourism, medical travel and global healthcare. Mr. Edelheit is also editor of the Worksite Magazine and Corporate Wellness Magazine and has been involved as a healthcare leader in the US for almost 10 years. Mr. Edelheit's experience started in running a national healthcare administrator after graduating from law school. Mr. Edelheit was in charge of marketing and turned his company from a local/regional administrator into a national healthcare administrator dealing with thousands of insurance agents, consultants and employers across the country. Mr. Edelheit has been featured or mentioned in over 100 magazines and newspapers and in February of 2008 was featured as a visionary in US healthcare by Executive Managed Healthcare Magazine. Mr. Edelheit is also editor of the Voluntary Benefits Magazine, The Corporate Wellness Magazine, and the Self Funding Magazine. Mr. Edelheit also organizes and runs one of the largest US healthcare conferences in the country the, "Employer Healthcare Congress", which is made up of 4 conferences, The Coporate Wellness Conference, Self Funding Conference, Voluntary Benefits and Limited Medical Conference, and the Expatriate and Travel Insurance Conference.

Mr. Edelheit is also author of the book, Medical Tourism: an International Guide for Employers, Insurers and Governments. The Book is available at http://medicaltourismassociation.com/en/mta-books.html
