Ronald Liono’s Summary

Ron is a high-performing member of Capgemini, with strong background in large-scale supply chain systems implementation, high aptitude for accuracy, proactive initiative, flexibility to take on new roles, and tolerance for ambiguity. He is a quality-focused and effective team leader, who is also an independent self-starter, combining passion for excellence with a commitment to long-term client success.

Most recently, Ron has been instrumentally involved in the implementation of JDA Demand Planning & Fulfillment Planning version 7.4.2 at an internationally-based grocery and general merchandise retailer. Ron’s responsibilities at this client were very broad, ranging from Functional Requirements Management, Quality Assurance (QA) and Defect Resolutions, Cutover Activities Management & Execution, JDA configuration design and implementation across multiple environments (Sandbox, CRP, System, and Production environments), to post-implementation support activities.

Prior to this engagement, Ron was involved in a supply chain strategic IT assessment at a US$2.2 billion telecommunications provider. In this project, Ron led the IT assessment efforts specifically on the client’s existing supply chain operations, including demand planning and fulfillment planning operations, as well as overall supply chain IT systems.

Additionally, Ron was involved in a supply chain process reengineering work at a $19.2 billion global wireless provider, implementing business process changes and improvements to the client’s Demand Planning organization.

For several years, Ron was instrumentally involved in a retail transformation consulting project at a $10.7 billion fashion retailer, recognized by the industry as one of the largest retail transformations ever undertaken, implementing Manugistics Demand & Fulfillment Planning version 7.2.2, to be integrated with SAP Retail, JDA Enterprise Planning, PkMS, and Vitech Prepack Optimization Tool.
