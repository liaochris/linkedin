Dr. Laureen Wishom’s Summary

Founder and Chief Solutionary Officer of Masterpiece Solutions LLC and Founder of the Global Association of High Achieving Women. Coachsultant in the areas of career success and business growth. Author of the white paper Women on Board.

Background includes twenty years of corporate America experience where she held such positions as Project Manager; Sr. Systems Analyst (several major oil/gas corporations); acting Training Coordinator (major hospital in the Medical Center); Human Services Director, Executive Director and Program Administrator (non-profit organizations); and Outplacement Consultant for one of the largest outplacement service companies in the United States and abroad. Written grants totaling eight million in the past 10 years.

Freelance writer. Articles have been published in numerous Houston-based and online publications. Received “Expert Author” status from EzineArticles.com. Published a series of “how to” books on writing grant proposals, developing an award winning business plan, starting a first time business, networking effectively and obtaining government contracts.

Appeared on DayStar TV Channel 20 (KLTJ) addressing issues relating to anger management and has been featured on the KTSU (90.9) Business Segment. She was featured on Dialogue Houston TV Channel 19 (HCCTV) - introducing the publication: The Greater Houston Resource Guide.

She was recently featured in the Houston Chronicle, the Houston Business Journal and on Fox News for her independent study of “Women on Corporate Boards”.
