Laurie Lonsdorf’s Summary

What makes me bounce out of bed in the morning? I'm passionate about helping small business leaders connect and communicate with people who will grow their organizations. Yes marketing strategy and copywriting form the core of my business services, but what I do is really about connection, engagement, education and growth.

While I've served clients of all sizes and all industry sectors, as a solopreneur, I really understand the pain and pleasure of running a very small business. Small business owners end up wearing too many hats, and typically marketing is something they are not comfortable doing, or they don't have time to do it effectively. That’s where my expertise comes in.

Nearly a decade ago, I was inspired to adopt The Princess of Persuasion™ as my alter ego. I wanted to create a distinctive marketing impression that was fun and memorable for the people I connect with—in person, in print and online. You could say my style is professional with a twist of wacky. And while donning a rhinestone tiara is not everyone's cup of tea, people do appreciate that The Princess always stands for extending the "royal treatment" to others.
