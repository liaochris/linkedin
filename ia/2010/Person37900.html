Chris Hill’s Summary

Twitter address: http://twitter.com/cheaptoday_CEO

Over the last 20 years, Chris has started, established and monetized successful digital media companies within fast growth industries.

In the mid-1990’s, Chris co-founded the consumer division of IDD Information Services that launched a number of innovative services including Barron's Online in partnership with Dow Jones and Tradeline Electronic Stock Guide. IDD was acquired by Dow Jones & Co. in 1997.

In 1998, Chris was a founding team member and became President and CEO of OneCore, a nationwide provider of integrated online financial services, which grew from zero to over $100 million in assets under management. OneCore was sold to Crown Ventures in 2003.

Chris joined Lycos in 2003 as General Manager of Quote.com, a profitable division that achieved significant growth under his leadership and was acquired by IDC.

Chris joined DirectoryM in 2005, a leading online advertising network, as VP Marketing & Product Management, and shortly after being acquired by to BET/Interaction Media Group became its Chief Operating Officer. Chris retains an equity stake in the acquiring firm.

In 2007, Chris was recruited to become SVP of Marketing and Product Management at My Perfect Gig. Chris was responsible for launching the company's first products and for the marketing launch of the firm. Chris retains an equity stake in the firm.

In 2009, Chris was recruited to become the President & CEO of CheapToday and is a Member of the Board of Directors, where he currently resides. CheapToday is an online media company and independent source of quality, and picked deals and expert shopping advice for clothing, accessories and credit and loyalty programs from leading brands such as Calvin Klein, Macy's, Target, and Victoria Secret.

Chris is also a Senior Lecturer at Suffolk University on Marketing, Advertising and Entrepreneurial Studies.
