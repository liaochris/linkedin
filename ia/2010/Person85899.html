Israel Carunungan’s Summary

Award-winning marketing professional with close to 20 years of experience in strategic marketing, creative, branding, account management, research, internet marketing and new business development.

Currently overseeing the marketing of over 13,000 apartment homes and 47 properties for TGM Associates, an investment advisory firm with a single focus: multifamily properties. On behalf of its investor clients, TGM acquires, manages and sells apartment properties throughout the U.S.

Responsibilities include brand positioning, image development, budget management, media/advertising and development, internet marketing, social media, SEO and SEM, production and implementation of all marketing collateral, website, promotions, signage and consumer direct response.

Previously worked as Vice President of Marketing for The Bozzuto Group, a real estate firm with portoflio in the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast regions. Led Bozzuto's marketing transition from primarily print-based advertising to an online-driven marketing strategy, with heavy focus on metrics and ad performance. Implemented a lead management system that significantly changed Bozzuto's business perspective -- from customer interaction to vendor relations.

A leading proponent of targeted internet marketing, I have conducted several speaking, training and webinar sessions on this subject. Work includes initiating new technology partnerships with Washington Post, Google and leading multifamily industry companies.

Currently a member of the Board of Directors of the Property Management Association (PMA). Speaking engagements include moderating a social media panel at the Apartment Internet Marketing (AIM) Conference, Multi-Housing World, CallSource Webinar and NAHB Multifamily Pillars Conference.

Professional recognition include marketing awards for the National Association of Home Builders (NAHB) Pillars, PMA Advertising and Marketing Excellence and Metropolitan Area Marketing Excellence (MAME).

Marketing, branding, advertising, internet marketing, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, social networking, social media, promotions, primary and secondary research, budget management, creative development, new business development, B-to-B and consumer marketing.
