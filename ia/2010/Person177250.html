Ron Friedmann’s Summary

Ron Friedmann is Senior Vice President, Marketing for Integreon, a global leader in knowledge support services for professionals. Ron has extensive experience in law practice management, outsourcing, legal marketing, knowledge management, e-discovery, and technology.

Prior to Integreon, Ron founded Prism Legal Consulting, which advised law firms and law departments on strategic uses of technology and legal market suppliers on marketing and strategy. He also served as CIO at 500-lawyer Mintz Levin, managed practice support at Wilmer Cutler (now WilmerHale), and held marketing positions at two legal market software start-ups.

Professional Affiliations: Trustee of the College of Law Practice Management; member, Board of Governors, Organization of Legal Professionals; founder/organizer of the DC Large Law Firm KM Group; participant in the NYC Large Law Firm Knowledge Management Group; and co-founded and organized the Law Practice Technology Roundtable.

Before working in the legal market, Ron was a strategy consultant for Bain & Company and an economic/financial analyst for Data Resources, Inc.

Ron Friedmann’s Specialties:

Legal outsourcing, offshore document review, knowledge management, e-discovery (EDD), online legal services, marketing
