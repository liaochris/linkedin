Ravi Narasimhan’s Experience

  • Vice President, Optimization and Targeting

    Fox Audience Network (a division of News Corporation)

    (Internet industry)

    June 2010 — Present (6 months)

    Responsible for targeting and yield optimization products at FAN. Oversee a product team that developed a variety of audience targeting and insights products, leveraging some of the largest data sets of social, demographic and behavioral data. Products were deployed across a wide spectrum branded and performance advertisers. Scaled the products to service over 50B impressions per month across 900+ publishers, yielding 3-4x lift in CPM.

    Assumed a leadership role in executing the 3 of the 4 major initiatives identified by FAN for 2010. The first initiative (dubbed "PreSearch") has the ambitious goal of offering SEM/search advertisers a viable alternative to buying search engine traffic. Responsible for the soup-to-nuts development of this product, including, market research, financial modeling, developing requirements, research and development, customer identification and facilitating the sales process. Successfully onboarded several new customers, who are seeing early results of order of magnitude improvements in ROI.

    The second initiative was to diversify FAN's data footprint by partnering with a bevy of 3rd party data providers. Responsible for partner selection, build-vs-buy assessments, negotiating T&Cs, defining the integration plan, and assimilating new assets into an existing data framework. Executed the plan that took FAN from reliance on a few data sources, to a fully-diversified data platform in less than 1 year.

    The third initiative was to integrate dynamic creative optimization (DCO) into FAN's targeting and yield management platform. Oversaw the product efforts for partner selection, build-vs-buy assessments, negotiating T&Cs, and conducting a suite of bakeoff tests between the vendors.

  • Exec. Director, Optimization and Targeting

    Fox Audience Network (a division of News Corporation)

    (Online Media industry)

    June 2008 — May 2010 (2 years )

    Responsible for all ad optimization and yield maximization functions at FAN. Lead a team that implemented a variety of optimization algorithms to maximize yield from both brand and remnant inventory. Created a special data-mining task force that developed deep insights and solutions to complex yield problems. These efforts contributed to year-over-year increases in remnant eCPM of more than 50%

    Authored the yield algorithms that consistently served over 4 billion ad impressions per day using 80,000+ campaigns across 2,000+ publishers

    Played a key role in the launch of MyAds (FAN's self service CPC system) for small businesses. Involved in all aspects of this product, including, integration with ad serving and yield technology, developing a CPC price recommender, techniques to efficiently learn/yield optimize very large numbers of offers, strategies for effectively managing budgets/pacing, strategies for increasing advertiser spend, auction and game strategies, etc. MyAds generated over $20M annually in incremental revenue to FAN – replacing other demand channels that generated less than $10M on comparable traffic

    Also played a key role in the launch of an independent ad network under the FAN brand. In addition to MySpace, the FAN network includes over 2,000+ publishers spanning a wide spectrum of content. Successfully solved some difficult issues around page quality, view frequency and pricing, which played an important role in diversifying revenue and data risk away from MySpace

    Provided tech evangelism and leadership to move several ad network partners from a wholesale purchasing model onto FAN’s real-time bidding platform (RTB). RTB has generated over $15M in incremental annual revenue to FAN

  • Sr. Director, Optimization and Analytics

    Fox Interactive Media

    (Public Company; NWS; Internet industry)

    March 2007 — June 2008 (1 year 4 months)

    Following the purchase of SDC, was offered a senior leadership role in integrating the SDC technology with NewsCorp's digital assets.

    Provided product vision, scientific leadership and technical project management in launching Fox Audience Network’s (FAN’s) ad serving/yield optimization system. The FAN system was highest volume ad serving platform in 2009, peaking at over 6 billion impressions per day. The system serves all ads on MySpace and several other Fox properties, and has increased system-wide eCPMs by over 300% since launch.

    A key contribution was implementing the serving/optimization system that scaled to over 80,000 campaigns and 2,000 page locations. The key drivers were a number of enhancements that allowed the use of a single yield management system for all types of ad deals (CPM, CPC, CPA, variable price, etc.)

    Played a key role in a massive shift in business strategy related to ad network partners - moved most partners to a real-time bidding auctions environment. These strategies significantly improved the partner's buying efficiencies, and allowed FAN to double revenues from ad network partners within 6 months of launch.

  • Director of Technology

    Strategic Data Corp.

    (Online Media industry)

    January 2000 — March 2007 (7 years 3 months)

    Early employee at Strategic Data Corp (SDC), a startup that built the first performance-based, optimized ad-serving system. SDC was acquired in 2007 by News Corp for more than $140 million.

    Joined the company initially in a consulting role. Rapidly promoted to lead the development of the most critical component of the ad server (the optimization engine). My first contribution was to spearhead a brand new philosophy towards ad serving, that laid the framework for architecting one of the most sophisticated yield optimization systems built for ad serving. My second contribution was to adapt classical machine learning/statistical methods to the unique demands for a real-time ad serving system. This lead to the development of a massively-scalable yield optimization system that did not sacrifice predictive power to achieve scale.

    Intimately involved in designing and developing all facets of the full ad serving system from the ground up automated yield management product. Onboarded and successfully yield optimized a variety of online businesses - ad networks, large publishers or portals, large direct advertisers (mortgage lenders), adware vendors, etc.
