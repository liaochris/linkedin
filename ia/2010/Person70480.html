Fonz van der Linden’s Summary

Hello There,

My name is Fonz van der Linden
and I'm a Network Marketer and online Mentor.
Thank you for your precious time to take a look at my Linkedin profile and get to know me a bit better.
Because English in not my native language, it mite sound a little different sometimes..
I'm Part Dutch, part Indonesian and I live in The Netherlands.
I grew up in Friesland, but not with Friesian parents. After 2 technical schools, my dad who
was an Airforce sergeant, decided to put me as well in the Dutch Airforce.
So I joined the Forces at the age of 20: 3 year militairy training and 21 years working as a Sergeant,
stationed on Airbase Leeuwarden in the north of Holland.
Because of cut backs and a superfluous position I had to leave the Airforce in December 2004. Boohoo...
After several non rewarding jobs I found myself in a situation to make some serious choices.
Do I want to continue making any boss a little richer?
Considering my age, do I want a (fysical) heavy techical job?
Do I want to work till the age of 65 or even worst, 67...?

Alright, you guessed it, the answer is NO of course..
So I looked around the internet, started an online business, lost some money, fell flat on my face,
and learned a lot what works for me and what not...
But the most important lesson was: never, NEVER EVER give up..!
When you do something long enough, you get better in it and when the passion kicks in of what
you're doing... everything changes. Situations and people no longer work against you..
But will work WITH you.
Another lesson I learned was: don't work alone and try to do it all by yourself.
So I found mentors on the Internet, a group of people dedicated to help others in any way they can.
Now I'm one of them, doing the same and helping others..
There is nothing more rewarding for me to see other people becoming successful in any kind of business!!

Other passion of me are my drawing and 3D animations skills.
What I create in my mind, I can make with drawings and animations.
