Mike Curran’s Summary

I have been an avid investment student since high school. Although I was a Biology major at KU on my way to medical school I have always been intrigued with the world of finance. After deciding not to pursue medicine, I took a year of business classes in graduate school. I started out my career with a job in pharmaceutical sales that lasted 7 plus years. I then moved on to medical sales before my bug to own my own small business got the best of me. I have subscribed to the Wall Street Journal, Barron's, Fortune and Money for over 20 years. I am now able to help individuals who need help from a company who understands the complex world of wills, trusts and of course, investments in my current position as a Wealth Management Consultant. I work with individuals in any stage of their life cycle whether they are creating and growing, protecting and preserving or distributing their assets. I have 19 years of successful sales experience, including 5 years as a small business owner which gave me great "life" experiences.
Keeping your assets at a trust company allows you to keep your assets safer than if they were at a traditional bank or brokerage house. Your assets are kept in nominee name versus street name. With that, your assets do not become a liability and subject to the creditors of that institution. With the near failure of Bear Stearns and the multiple bank failures as of late, this has become a big selling feature to what I do at the Commerce Trust Company.

I am a candidate to be a Certified Financial Planner. I have taken the course work and the National Board Exam awaits.
