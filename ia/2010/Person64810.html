Emmanuel Marques’s Summary

Currently Director of Media Strategy & Planning for the Global Marketing department of Disney Destinations (Parks & Resorts, Cruise Lines, Vacation Club) my role is to create and implement paid media solutions (Traditional and new media) for influencing identified target demographics.

In addition to my work in media strategy and planning, I have more than eleven years of experience working in Digital Marketing, from the development of concepts and ideas to their implementation within start-ups and large organizations.

Most recently, as Director of New Media and Technology, my focus was to leverage new media (Internet, mobile and advanced TV platforms) to better market Disney Destinations in Europe and Asia. I have developed and implemented new media initiatives, from the launch of multi-players on-line games in China to the launch of VOD channel in the UK and development of cutting edge Mobile applications in Europe.

Previously, as European Head of Internet for Disneyland Resort Paris, I re-launched Disneyland Resort Paris on-line Marketing and Sales activities making the traffic and the revenues grow by more than 100% in two years.

Prior to Disneyland Resort Paris, I served as General Manager and Head of Marketing in internet start-ups and have held positions in strategic consulting firms.

I am a graduate of ESSEC Business School (France) and I reside in Malibu (CA), with my wife and my three children.
