Bill Brenner’s Summary

I'm an experienced journalist, family man and history buff looking to use my writing and editing skills for the betterment of humanity. I'm driven by a passion to write stories that will help security professionals do their jobs, and consider myself blessed to be doing this work.

Along the way, I've gotten to work with outstanding, gifted writers, editors and bloggers: Derek Slater, Anne Saita, Jim Malone, Rob Westervelt, Dennis Fisher, Mike Mimoso, Gretchen Putnam and Jack Daniel, to name a few.

When I'm not obsessing about the next story, I immerse myself in family: my wife, Erin, our boys, Sean and Duncan, our church community (born Jewish, became Catholic), and history. I love traveling to Washington DC and anywhere else with historical significance. I'm also interested in health and wellness matters, especially the fight against mental illness.

If you want to share ideas for security stories or get in touch for no particular reason, please do.
