Sonya Sullins’s Summary

Sonya Sullins, MBA, is the Founder, CEO and Principal Consultant of Human Capital Management Institute. ...Where Employee Engagement is the "Holy Grail" and "Devoted Customers" are the goal.

Working primarily B2B, and specializing in the healthcare and senior living industries, we specialize in helping organization select,motivate and retain top talent and create fiercely loyal, devoted customers.

Sonya enjoys serving her clients from the C-level to the front line. She is adept at identifying key factors which help people overcome challenges and become productive and engaged both personally and professionally.

This has spurred on a B2C leg of her business offerings. We focus on motivating and inspiring professionals to develop their Leadership, Communication and Interpersonal Skills. Sonya offers coursework in the form of teleseminars, webinars and e-courses. Stay tuned for course offerings held locally as well.

Sonya brings to the table over 20 years experience as a keynote, trainer and workshop facilitator, and a dozen years as a consultant, with a specialty in Senior Living and Health Care.

Most recently, Sonya has embarked upon a new venture. She is self-described as a “Wellness and Wealth Advisor”, and Recruiter for Young Living Essential Oils.

Her interest in this comes with personal as well as professional motivators. Her “grand vision” is to seek out, and provide products and services that enable people grow into and achieve goals with respect to physical, emotional, spiritual, social, and financial needs.

After struggling through health challenges of loved ones and then encountering her own health challenges, she decided to get "back to the basics" to achieve optimum health and she is seeing and feeling the results of a healthy lifestyle supported by essential elements from nature.

Passionate and full of energy - expect to hear and see more from Sonya!

  • Author, Speaker, Consultant

    Human Capital Management Institute, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Human Capital Management Institute works with organizations and professionals to help them select, motivate and retain top talent, and create fiercely loyal and devoted customers.

    Our programs enable organizations to make the most of the top performers they have and gain the attention of the those they don't employ (but would like to). As important as keeping your top employees, is keeping your customers. Did you know it costs 10 times more to get a obtain a new customer than it does to retain an existing customer? Our belief is that mere "satisfaction" is not enough... Are you customers "in love" with you? Are they fiercely loyal and devoted, to YOU? Only a highly engaged workforce and do what it takes to go the extra mile and truly WOW! the customer.

    This is exactly what we help you to do. Without a highly engaged workforce, you will struggle to sustain maximum profitability, especially in this challenging economic environment.

    What does it mean to manage human capital? It means leveraging and maximizing the talent, education, strengths, skills, etc. of your workforce. Engaged employees result in increased Retention (of the best), improved Productivity and, of course, Customer Devotion.

    Companies with highly engaged employees increase their profits, on average, by 27% (based on Gallup University research and as evidenced by outcomes).

    After seeing for herself how hard it is to get and keep top talent, how challenging it is when a customer is frustrated with their experience, Sonya quickly realized that most organizations are full of people who are frustrated and overwhelmed. They get stuck thinking that there’s no solution and have come to accept this sad state of affairs as commonplace…low standards and poor results are making life at work hard.

    Sonya can help your organization reach it's goals - aim high and achieve great things!
