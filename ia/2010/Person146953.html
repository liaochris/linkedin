Michelle Vaughn’s Summary

My main drive as a teacher is helping the children who are in need of a little extra understanding and direction. I have the reputation of being able to build self-esteem and help them grow into confident little people. I enjoy the challenge of working with children who have emotional or behavioral issues.
During my 19 years in the early childhood profession, I have maintained a performance that exceeds the expectations of my specific job title. As an employee of Action Day Inc. for six years, I found myself achieving in many areas of the company and was seen a valued team member and mentor. I was invited by a parent to speak privately at a group meeting for the MOPS, Mothers of Preschoolers. In addition to the role of Jr. K teacher, I took on other roles of leadership through the volunteering of Dance Rep for the schools extra-curricular dance program, as well as, Costume Coordinator for the yearly dance shows held at the San Jose Center for Performing Arts. I embarked on a better understanding of the administrative process by running the office for the director when she was out. I also received support in accomplishing promotional experience for the company.
Currently my goals are to receive my Master Teaching Permit and to continue looking for new opportunities to broaden my experience in the field of education, arts, and the development of young minds.
