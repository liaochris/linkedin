Vidya Drego’s Summary

My research and consulting focuses on the tactics and tools companies can employ to improve their customer experiences. This includes user research, design, and usability processes that help companies identify existing gaps in their current experiences or identify opportunities for experience differentiation, and the skills, processes, partners, and design techniques they use to create great customer experiences that improve business results. I've helped Fortune 500 companies find and partner with the right interactive agency, improve their internal design and usability processes with tools like personas and techniques like heuristic evaluations, and helped customer experience professionals show measurable improvements in their customer experience across digital channels.

Vidya Drego’s Specialties:

Interactive/design agency selection, design processes, usability, customer research, customer experience strategy, Web and mobile site design and usability, out-of-box experience, and persona development and use
