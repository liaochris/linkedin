DJ Nicke’s Summary

I started Animation Salvation to help democratize the knowledge and skills of animation.

This art used to be passed down from Animator to Inbetween artist. With CG Animation, there is no Inbetweener, so the entire apprenticeship system is lost!

I hope to recreate that with Animation Salvation.

Previous experience:

Walt Disney Animation: Visual Effects/CGI Character Animator/Modeler; Director (in-house project)

Animal Logic Film: Character Animator/Modeler; Set/props Modeler

Ambience Entertainment: Character Animator/Modeler
