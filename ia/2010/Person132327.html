Maria Hanley’s Summary

Growing up in small town PA, I started dancing at age 3. After high school I attended Slippery Rock University as a dance major, performing in concerts with a diverse collection of choreographers. I moved to New York City the summer of 2005 and began the dance education program at New York University. In this program I gained valuable experience in child development, educational theory, and curriculum writing to name a few. The past four years have been dedicated to building a network and creating a business In NYC.

A long term goal of mine is to have a dance school someday!
