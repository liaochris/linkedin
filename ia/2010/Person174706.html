Robert Funk’s Summary

Developed market from scratch building business one client at a time by building trust, providing top notch service and delivering on our promises. Our clients rely on us for their technical support. We are their trusted advisor when it comes to their servers, computers, network, software, web sites, etc.

CMIT Solutions provides computer and technology support for small and medium sized businesses that do not have computer support on staff. We specialize in network installation and support. Our approach is a consulting approach to technology, meaning we work with our clients to determine how they can use technology to increase their revenue and reduce their expenses.

I am heavily involved in community activities and organizations. I serve on the board of the United Way of Forsyth and Dawson counties. In the past, I served on the boards of Leadership Forsyth and the Johns Creek Business Association. I volunteer with the Chamber of Commerce and have served on several chamber committees.

Robert Funk’s Specialties:

Microsoft Small Business server, Microsoft Server Standard software and related technologies. Technology advisor as well as market development and service product placement specialist.
