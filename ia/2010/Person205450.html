Todd Katz’s Summary

Community bank organizer (two), one public and one private; employee motivator, business developer, delivered 17% annualized return to our investors on first bank (sold 2005) and growing current bank in a tough market ($120 million assets in less than 3 years). Our goal is to create a true, old fashioned community bank that knows our clients personally and is engaged in the local community.
