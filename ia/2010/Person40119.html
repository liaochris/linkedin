Christopher Wallace’s Summary

I am a Client Services Director at iProspect where I'm responsible for strategic development and proactive account management of multiple client-facing teams. I lead overall campaign strategy including the integration of search efforts and other client marketing initiatives. In addition, I oversee training and development for client services team members focusing on organic search, pay per click advertising, and online display.

Since joining iProspect in 2006, I have worked as a Paid Search Specialist managing multiple accounts, then as a Search Marketing Specialist managing several paid and organic search accounts with a focus on the tactical elements of a campaign. Prior to my current role as a Client Services Director, I joined the Chicago office as a Client Services Manager where I was responsible for overseeing development, day to day execution on client accounts, and strategic direction for Search Marketing Specialists and Search Marketing Analysts.

I have worked with a variety of clients in different industries including healthcare, pharmaceutical, retail, health insurance, software, telecommunications, and education across various channels including BtoB and BtoC. Each client engagement has brought a unique set of issues and circumstances including site redesign and re-launch as well as content and structure development.

Prior to iProspect I worked as a Manager for Via Matta Restaurant, as well as a Sales Representative for Johnson and Johnson in Boston. As a Manager, I was in charge of running day to day operations for a staff of 25, event planning, and developing marketing initiatives. As a Sales Representative, I was in charge of managing my territory, carrying out strategic marketing efforts, and delivering effective presentations to doctors and staff on a daily basis.
