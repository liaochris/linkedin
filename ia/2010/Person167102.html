Robert Carpenter’s Summary

Bob Carpenter helps executives and supervisors make a difference at work. Honing people skills in the private, not-for-profit, and public sectors for more than 20 years, his work throughout North America, Europe and Asia has brought him much acclaim.

A recognized authority on leadership and management practices, Bob's background includes sales and management positions at IBM and Digital Equipment Corporation, senior positions at several management development firms, and the launching of three start-up companies. He also holds an MBA degree from Columbia University.

Robert Carpenter’s Specialties:

Workforce training and development, leadership skills consulting, executive coaching.

Barely two weeks later, the group reported noticeably better personal performance and more engaged, collaborative teams.
