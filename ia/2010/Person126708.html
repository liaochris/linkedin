Liz Barron’s Summary

Liz is a qualified coach and experienced facilitator, and with a proven track record in collaborating and communicating with clients, she has successfully engaged with many individuals and teams to help them to realize their goals.

With over twenty years experience in project & change management roles, including consulting, facilitation and training, Liz has worked to achieve positive outcomes for client organisations in both the public and private sector, in Ireland and the UK.

Liz specialises in Career Coaching, to help people achieve their career goals, and also coaches Students from senior cycle upwards to realize their potential.

Liz Barron’s Specialties:

Career & Workplace Coaching, Student Coaching, Life Coaching, Workshop Facilitation, Business Analysis, Process Improvement, Project Management, Project Management Office, Training
