Apryl Parcher’s Summary

I'm a marketing consultant, business copywriter and certified social media strategist. I have experience with a broad array of content creation, and was personally trained by Michael Stelzner as a white paper writer. I love helping businesses plan and execute content strategies that truly connect with their audiences--especially integrating their current marketing strategies with social media.

Interested in white paper writing, advising businesses on social media strategy, helping improve content offerings, and public speaking.

Apryl Parcher’s Specialties:

Sales and marketing integrated strategies, social media workshops, public speaking, web and print content creation, white papers, direct response copywriting
