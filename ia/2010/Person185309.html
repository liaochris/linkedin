Sean Endsley’s Summary

Sean Endsley, Broker Associate - Evergreen Office
Proactive, hard-working, dedicated…These words not only describe Sean Endsley’s real estate career and service to his clients but also how he lives his life. After serving in the Marine Corps and then majoring in Organizational Communication with a Minor in Business from the College at South West Texas State, Sean went on to run his own successful business. Sean and his partner and father are Brokers at Fuller Sothebys International Realty, providing premium services to their clients with unmatched resources and benefits. Using the most advanced marketing tools and his affiliation with KCH custom homes, Sean creates additional and diverse options for his buyers, sellers and investors. His commitment to excellence, strong work ethic and integrity allow for his client’s to relax knowing that all of their needs will be met, exceeding their expectations, and is the part of the real estate business that he enjoys most to this day. Outside of work, Sean enjoys spending time with his wife of fourteen years and their two children.
