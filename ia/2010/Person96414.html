Jerry Allocca’s Summary

Jerry Allocca is President of CORE, an award-winning team of Internet specialists [ www.CORE.bz ]. A recognized Internet and marketing guru, Jerry makes the Internet more useful, valuable, and profitable for his clients.

Jerry is a frequent industry speaker and author of the much anticipated new book "Connected Culture: the art of communicating with the digital generation" [ www.ConnectedCultureBook.com ].

Jerry teaches Internet workshops and is active in numerous business groups, trade associations and non-profit organizations. Visit [ www.JerryAllocca.com ] or do a google search for Jerry Allocca.
