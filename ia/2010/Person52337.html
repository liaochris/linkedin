David Lippman’s Summary

A 28 year collection industry background and a desire to become an attorney took this collection veteran back to school in 1998.

Since starting in the industry in 1982 I have a proven track record of success. During my career I have worked all stages of debt collections, rising from a collector, to supervisor, and department manager at several top credit card issuers.

I have trained hundreds of collectors the ins and outs of ethical collections.

Our firm currently employs over 65 professionals and serves the needs of our clients locally throughout the states of both Arizona and California as well as nationwide through our nationwide network of attorneys. We currently represent some of the nations top credit card issuers, insurance carriers, and commercial claim forwarders.


In 2011, we anticipate the opening of our Nevada and Utah offices.

David Lippman’s Specialties:

Our practice focuses solely on debt collection. We handle all types of commercial and retail claims as well as property and casualty subrogation claims.
