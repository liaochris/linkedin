Jim Kimzey’s Summary

President and Owner of Tender Rose Home Care, a home care agency specializing in Alzheimer's and dementia care at home in San Francisco, San Mateo County, and Marin County.

Jim Kimzey’s Specialties:

Passionate about improving the lives of people suffering from dementia and their families by helping patients age safely and with dignity in their own homes. I am just as passionate about creating a great place to work for professional caregivers.

Jim Kimzey’s Experience

  • President and Owner

    Tender Rose Home Care

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Tender Rose Home Care helps family caregivers of dementia patients take care of their loved ones at home.

    Our caregivers help with the activities of daily living (ADLs), including bathing, dressing, dental care, and eating. We also help with the Instrumental Activities of Daily Living (IADLs), including housework, shopping, preparing meals, driving clients to appointments, and reminding them to take their medications.

    Tender Rose is unique in that we focus on patients with dementia. Most of our patients have Alzheimer's Disease, many have vascular dementia, and some have Alzheimer's along with other dementia-inducing illnesses. We also care for seniors who have mild cognitive impairment.

    Because our focus is on dementia care at home, we provide the most extensive dementia care training for our caregivers in the home care industry. We also train family caregivers in our Caregiver Training Center in Daly City, CA.
