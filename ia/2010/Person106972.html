Justin Kaiser’s Summary

Don’t Let Great Ideas Go To Waste

Broadcast Mastery is about redefining the way you think about radio. Quite simply, our goal is to produce one of the most creative and prolific sites on the Internet focused on helping you create and sell great radio. Simply put, Broadcast Mastery is a “marketplace for ideas”.

Daily Podcasts Focused on Your Passion

Broadcast Mastery is here to provide ideas in daily podcasts focused on each niche of the radio industry. The New Media Monday Podcast will help your radio station web administrator harness the new and changing technologies available to get your radio stations website visible. Tuesday’s Creative Camp Podcast will focus on the Big Talent and Big Ideas needed to get to the next level creatively. The Wednesday Radio Obits Podcast is focused on Killer Radio Programming Strategies… or how NOT to kill a radio station. On Thursday, we’ll focus on the talent that make it work… Superjock or Not… it’s all about the listener on the Life is Show Prep Podcast. And we’ll wrap up the week with timely sales advice, tips, and sales starters so your radio sales reps can surpass the competition on the Excel in Selling Podcast.

Success Stories and Real World Examples

With Broadcast Mastery there is now a place where you can make money off of new, and old, ideas and concepts. We’re going to jog your brain and get your creativity flow directly to the decision makers… whether they are the listener or the buyer.

Lead… Don’t Follow

In addition to discovering exciting new ideas, Broadcast Mastery also offers you access to our pool of world-class talent to answer virtually any of your questions. Our network of Broadcast Masters come from the great radio stations, agencies, and comedy clubs (ok, most are Broadcast Masters!?!?) and have a wealth of great ideas will make you a leader… NOT a follower.
