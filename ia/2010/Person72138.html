Gabe Hendry’s Summary

I have been running web technology companies since late 2004 starting with Notify Quick Corp, and now with my own Focus 44, LLC and Gabe Hendry Designs. My goals have always been to connect technologies to the people who need them; previously in the telephony industry, currently in all things media.

Focus 44 was a solutions firm. My business partner and I worked in almost all media fields including web technologies/application development, graphic design and print solutions, corporate identity and branding, commercial and consumer photography, and pro audio/video.

At Gabe Hendry Designs, I specialize in learning and identifying individual client needs as I attempt to talk through goals and ideas. I help design, develop and deploy custom media solutions for web, print, and everything in between to get clients where they want to go. The goal is to integrate and unify a brand throughout a company while providing custom solutions to meet immediate and future needs.

Currently I am also contracting at Best Buy Mobile as a Senior Web Designer working on several Best Buy Web Applications.
