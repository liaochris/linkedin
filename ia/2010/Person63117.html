Ernesto Lembert Sr., MBA/TM’s Summary

IT BUSINESS ANALYST

I am a Bilingual IT Business Analyst with strong knowledge of process management, technical writing, and reporting and program management disciplines. Extensive experience interpreting business requirements translating them into functional technical specifications for the creation, modification, or enhancement of critical applications; assisting in the establishment and development of procedures, practices, and methods; consulting with customers, and meeting specific technical and business needs.

I also enjoy opportunities to serve as Business Analyst for a major, new initiative...then transforming to the PM or BA/PM to help it become reality. So I see BA and PM skills as very complementary. I enjoy learning new technologies and love getting the job done right.

And while I retain a firm commitment to project stakeholders and IT executives during the entire life of each project, I also believe my role is to serve my project team: ensuring that at all times they know exactly what is required of them (and when), and are adequately empowered to get their work done. I also believe we need to have fun as a team, and that encouragement is the "oil of the engine" for a project team.
