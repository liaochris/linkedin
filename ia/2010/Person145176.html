Michael Citron’s Summary

Michael is an internationally known public speaker and author. He lectures for professional associations worldwide. Michael has authored numerous articles on credit repair, loan modifications, short sales and marketing. His recently published book “The Art of the Short Sale” has instantly become a favorite for many of the nation’s premier real estate experts. Michael is a recognized authority in the financial services industry, he consults and trains individuals and groups on a variety of topics including, credit scoring, credit law/repair, short sales, loan modifications, direct response marketing and debt negotiation.

Michael has shown continued enthusiasm to share his marketing and credit expertise to DisputeSuite members. Members embrace his strategic and creative approach to marketing and business management. He is known for quickly identifying problems with businesses along with permanent solutions. Problems that plague the credit repair industry have become a passion for Michael.

Stigmas associated with credit repair along with organizational and educational issues have not only become a passion for Michael, they’ve become a mission. Michael’s role at DipsuteSuite.com does not lie solely in the software. Michael offers DisputeSuite members education and consulting services. Educating business owners, creating a higher standard and establishing an authority to regulate the industry have developed into newfound goals of Michael and DisputeSuite. Michael feels with the right tools and the proper education the stigmas associated with the credit repair industry can be permanently discarded.
