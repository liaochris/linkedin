Eric Schwertzel’s Summary

As today's broadcast and broadband consumers embrace digital media, the opportunity exists to distribute entertainment content with the speed and simplicity of digital distribution. Alphabit is the catalyst in helping companies market their products and services via a blended digital and traditional distribution channel.

With an emphasis on creating value-add and co-branded partnerships that use entertainment content to compliment consumer product sales, Alphabit has rolled out innovative programs with games, music and movies showcasing the efficiencies of a direct to consumer business model while also encouraging consumers to become engaged fans.

Always open for new synergistic partnerships, technologies and discussions.
