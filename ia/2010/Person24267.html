Bindy Johnson’s Summary

Bindy began her spiritual journey at a young age when her mother taught her hypnosis to release pain in her body, and shared ancient Persian medicine with her. For the past 17 years she has been helping people live healthier lives through full body health and wellness. She is a Usui Reiki Master and Teacher, Magnified Healer, and Wholistic Fitness Coach. She studies Shamanism monthly with Steve Wilson of Aquarius Sanctuary and is learning about the benefits and practice of Classical Homeopathy which she and her family use. Bindy is also a mother which in itself has brought endless gifts in lessons and happiness.

In 1991 Bindy became a certified personal trainer and began helping clients achieve full body awareness through cardiovascular, flexibility, and strength training programs. She spent the last 17 years managing fitness facilities, designing health programs for clubs and owning her own fitness coaching business where she staffed a nutritionist and several personal trainers.

In 2001 her love for nature and backpacking led her to hike the United States for 10 weeks, covering 35 states. Three months later, she spent 21 days in Iran, traveling from Shiraz to the Caspian Sea.

Her journeys have left her with a deep appreciation for life, culture and the beauty of just being.

Bindy offers healing session in Townsend, MA where you may choose or combine Reiki, Shamanic Energy Healing, and Sound Healing. Her workshops are held at Karendyth Holistic Center in Westford MA and Aquarius Sanctuary in Littleton, MA. To learn more visit her at www.bindy.org
