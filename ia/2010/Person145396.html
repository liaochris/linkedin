Mike Glodo’s Summary

Focus upon Knowledge Integration problems: putting together people, processes, and technologies.

Emphasis upon information visualization and clarity.

Agile in developing and working with interdisciplinary teams.

Comfortable in the theoretical and "what if" aspects of "new things", and very focused upon the reality of how to realize the potential of "the new".

Examples include business plans, presentations, and white papers in support of new technologies and new technology driven operations and market breakthroughs.
