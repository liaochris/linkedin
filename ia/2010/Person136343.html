Masa Narita’s Summary

The modeler of new Freddy Krueger's face for A Nightmare on Elm Street 2010.

The modeler of 2009 VES awarded AUDI Rubic Cube commercial.

A GREEN CARD holder.

A professional 3D CG modeler. See demo reel at www.masacgi.com

A semi professional scale model builder. My ship diorama was awarded "Master Modeler" at Tamiya/Con( a US national scale model contest) in 2004. Now it sits in the Tamiya Museum at the headquarter of Tamiya(a world largest scale model manufacture) in Japan. My work can be seen in many magazines and web sites around the globe. Visit www.naritafamily.com

An amateur photography enthusiast. I have experience film photography over 4 years. I used mainly Fujichrome Velvia(reversal film) with EOS-1N back in 90's. My work are on the web site above.

The third largest Japanese brokerage firm transfered me to US from Japan in 1993 as a head of Silicon Valley office. I have worked for them over 20 years mainly as an IT professional and ended up with a head of the New York Times Square office. I am group oriented, responsible and have a good work habit.

In 2008, I quit my 20 year long IT job and jumped into Gnomon School of Visual Effects to re-cap CGI (I have intensive experience with Lightwave 3D 5.5 as a CG job seaker during 1997-1999). Then I have started CG work in April 2009. My 1999 version of demo-reel can also be seen at www.masacgi.com
