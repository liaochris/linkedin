Michael Adams’s Summary

I was the Director of Information Technology at Bush Ross, P.A. in Tampa, Florida. With over 17 years of Management Information Systems experience in law firms and a background in Business Process Management. Projects I have managed include Office Acquisitions & Relocations, Practice Management Systems, Document Management Systems, Remote Access Systems, Case Management Systems, Telecommunication Installations, conversions from WordPerfect to Word, Time and Billing Systems, Electronic Data Interchange, Workflow and Document Assembly.

I have focused my Information Technology career on process improvement utilizing tools of technology to make organizations more efficient. I am also a speaker at American Lawyer Media’s LegalTech events around the United States and was a member of the International Legal Technology Association (ILTA). I am graduate of Tampa College and hold certifications from Microsoft and NEC.
