Lauren Evans’s Summary

I have worked with many builders and designers at Hermitage Lighting and gained many professional connections during my time there. I learned so much about the lighting industry, as well as specific applications, that always helps me in the design and construction field. I enjoy seeing what the right lighting in the correct position does for the space and/or the subject. Lighting can make or break the most well designed space.
I also have the opportunity of working on spec and custom homes from the plan stages to finish. Real Esate experience and knowledge only adds to the functionality of plans and understanding what people are looking for and why they choose certain features. Not only homes, but the construction and their design are my passion.
I grew up in the business, learning many aspects from my Dad, who has been a custom builder for more than 30 years. Both of my Granddad's were carpenters and builders, as well as both also owning hardware stores in our small town.
I would love to follow in their footsteps and progress to obtaining my construction license in the future. I enjoy working with all types of people and personalities and have always had a natural ability to identify and understand with their needs, conerns and desires.
Also, I would like to gain a few more designations in the Real Estate industry and grow from experience and education, as well as from the influence of the great's that I am surrounded by at Zeitlin and Company, Realtors.
