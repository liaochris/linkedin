Steve Jewell’s Summary

Twenty-three years of progressive Human Resource experience across business sectors and industries. Seven years of commissioned executive and technical recruitment. Full-time and contract recruitment and generalist services for corporations such as Inetium, US Bank, Wells Fargo, Medtronic, TCF Bank, GE Capital and American Express Financial Services. Fortune 100 managerial experience with Unisys, PepsiCo (Frito Lay), The Disney Stores and Target ( Dayton Hudson Corp). Proven supervisory and leadership skills. Key contact for corporate and field HR initiatives. A high energy individual with a results orientation. I am a seasoned relationship builder.
