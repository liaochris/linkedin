David Adler’s Summary

I helped found Adler & Franczyk, LLC, a boutique intellectual property law firm based in Chicago, Illinois, as an evolution of my twelve years of providing strategic legal counsel on intellectual property and commercial matters. I have focused on a specific mission: provide clients with a competitive advantage by enabling them to leverage their intangible assets and creative content in a way that drives innovation and increases the overall value of the business.

I have an extensive background, in both private practice and as in-house counsel, with experience in corporate law, including contract interpretation, drafting, negotiation and enforcement and intellectual property law.

The Firm's services are organized to help executives, entrepreneurs and creative professionals align legal needs with business objectives. Please take a moment to explore or areas of expertise and discover how we differ in the way we serve our clients.

Our firm is designed to provide a cohesive law practice able to advise and counsel businesses across interdisciplinary matters. Successful businesses often experience a lack of continuity and consequently a lack of strategic vision in their legal representation. While executives may turn to one lawyer for the general business and contract needs, they must often turn to a second and even a third for intellectual property and technology-related matters.

The five key areas of our practice are:

• Intellectual Property Law
• Finance Law
• Business & Corporation Law
• Electronic Commerce & Information Technology Law
• Arts & Entertainment Law

Organized to provide cost-effective expertise and timely professional service, the Firm brings a strong core competency in corporate & intellectual property law and transactions. We leverage this against a select group of strategically aligned outside counsel to create a multi-faceted strategic corporate legal practice.
