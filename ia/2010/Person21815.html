Benjamin Shoemaker’s Summary

I have more than 8 years of experience in the Connecticut residential real estate market. I have several real estate related licenses and designations, and many contacts locally in the industry. I would welcome contact from any one wishing to work with me, or seeking information about the Connecticut market.

My goal is to grow Alpha Real Estate & Valuation LLC (AREV) into the highest rated Appraisal and Wholesale Brokerage firm in Connecticut. To do that I have a very simple philosophy, and that is give the best service possible to each client, every time. Exceed peoples expectations whenever possible, and always be professional. When you chose Alpha Real Estate & Valuation you get, "Service, second to none."

Additionally, I am very excited to present a New Project, ALPHA INVESTMENTS-CT. This project conducting business under the AREV LLC name focuses on becoming the source for REOs in southern and central CT. I am combining all of my property valuation knowledge, with my vast network of connections (not all on LinkedIn I'm afraid) and my recent exposure to Internet Marketing to create unique investment opportunities available to local & national investors.
