Stefan Nguyen’s Summary

My current focus is on information economics and, specifically, information valuation. My research and writings for the past several years has been on the key determinants of information value, as well as strategies for leveraging data and information to "infocharge" business processes. The information merchants, producers, distributors, and consumers and how they are affected by new paradigms such as social networks, transparency, and the multitude of signals are further fueling my interest to venture into the realm of valuing enterprises based on the information proxy that they possess.

Stefan Nguyen’s Specialties:

Information valuation, information economics, information strategies, market signals, social networks, online communities, entrepreneurial strategies, innovative and disruptive business models.
