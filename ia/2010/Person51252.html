David Lau’s Summary

Over 10 years of capital market experience with a focus on Risk Management from leading buy and sell-side firms. Produce and analyze various risk measures such as sensitivities, greeks, skews, VaR, stress test, scenario analysis from trading position to portfolio level and top of the house. Analyze risks associated with different types of trading strategies, arbitrage and directional, under different market conditions. Funding risk, liquidity risk, hedge fund due diligence, risk system design, model vetting are all within the scope of my past work experience. Have had first hand experience in market ups and downs and dooms.
