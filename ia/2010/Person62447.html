Edwin Pavey’s Summary

My financial planning practice focuses on providing sound financial planning strategies to those who live and work in the creative arts. I strongly believe that those who work in this field should be afforded the same opportunities to lead enriched lives as those who work in other professions. I view financial planning as an integral part of achieving personal financial goals.

In the course of pursuing a degree from the Nova Scotia College of Art and Design and then working for several years in the Film Industry, I gained extensive experience working with Creative Artists in various capacities. I am pleased to offer my services to those who work at the forefront of marrying technology and art.

With the team at Investors Group, we share our combined knowledge and experiences in the hopes of empowering others with the tools and advice needed to achieve their financial security and well-being.

I always enjoys meeting new people on an individual basis as well as meeting representatives from companies and associations, to discuss opportunities for us all to improve our financial well-being.

Edwin Pavey’s Specialties:

Providing financial planning to individuals, businesses, and associations involved in the creative arts. Designers, Web Developers, Animators, filmmakers, motion graphic artists, actors, musicians and other creative artists. Creating Group Retirement and Benefit Plans for Businesses and Associations with 5 members or more.
