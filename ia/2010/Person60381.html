Dave Howell’s Summary

Entrepreneur, software architect, and engineering manager with twenty years of leadership experience. Focused on producing software for the iPhone and iPod touch platform, with a background in creating tools for visual effects, motion graphics, video and film editing, music composition, and imaging.
