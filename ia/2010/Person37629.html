Chris Detzel’s Summary

Chris is an Advisor in the Forrester Leadership Boards Enterprise Architecture (EA) Group. In this role he serves as an extension to the EA’s staff and provides EA members with dedicated focus that helps solve business and technology issues around planning, organization, and technology decisions. Chris acts as a concierge to EA members, assisting with access to business and technology inquiries and access to research, organizing and facilitating meetings with EA members to discuss member-driven research topics, and supporting EA members attending special events geared toward information and knowledge sharing with their peers. He has several years experience building successful client relationships and earning the status of "trusted advisor". Chris' strengths are in understanding different Enterprise Architects perspectives, understanding the business value that Forrester can bring to clients and building consensus between both organizations.
