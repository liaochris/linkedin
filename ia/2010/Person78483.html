Gregory Schwartz’s Summary

John Gambs, former CFO for The Charles Schwab Corporation, says that Greg Schwartz’s work was “distinguished” and “characterized by originality, initiative, and thoroughness.” Patricia Small, Chief Investment Officer and Treasurer of the Regents for the University of California, says “Greg brought a great deal of skill to our senior management team, and I especially recognize his high level of organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills.”

Mr. Schwartz is a finance and operations executive with 18 years of strong management experience in a variety of industries. Substantial experience in financial transactions, corporate operations, systems design, process engineering, best practices, legal, and relationship management. Effective agent of change and re-engineering. Creative and results-oriented, with an empowering management philosophy, and broad vision. Hands-on problem solver with strong turn-around experience. Proven track record of enhancing profitability while producing results on schedule and within budget.

Pinnacle award winner (one of the nation’s highest honors in finance) for innovative problem solving. Published and the subject of several articles in trade journals such as American Banker and Pulse. Loyal work history and excellent references.
