    (Education Management industry)

    February 2010 — Present (4 months)

Launching in the 4th quarter of 2010, it will be the best place for kids & young adults to get career savvy, with professionals guiding them - sharing their how-tos of success.

CAREERSTERS does this by providing engaging and compelling video content of successful professionals "showing the ropes" of their professional journey, as a guide to users creating their own custom career path.
