Siddharth Chaudhary’s Summary

As Senior Product Manager for Omniture, Siddharth Chaudhary holds responsibility for the Display Advertising strategy within the Omniture Online Marketing Suite. Additionally, he is responsible for the Test&Target product.

Prior to joining Omniture, Siddharth has held senior level Product Management and Software Engineering positions at Vignette Corporation and Epicentric Inc culminating in overall business responsibility for software product lines providing annual revenues in excess of $40 million. Siddharth has focused his career on Web Content Management and Personalization products that have been successfully used by Global 2000 companies to power their mission critical web presences. Siddharth holds a Bachelors in Computer Science from the University of California at Berkeley and has 10+ years of enterprise software experience.
