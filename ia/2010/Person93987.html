Jeff Sampson’s Summary

UPSIDE Commerce, LLC is a provider of integrated trade management solutions based on familiar Microsoft technologies. Using UPSIDE’s integrated price, promotion, settlement and predictive analytics, consumer goods companies stop margin leakage, gain insight and realign their trade spending to drive revenue growth and experience typical investment returns exceeding 150%. UPSIDE is based in Redmond, WA with operations in New York and Denmark. Explore your revenue and margin upside - visit us online at http://upsidecommerce.com.

Previously, Jeff was the CEO and founder of Kineticsware, Inc. a provider of trade promotion management, solutions for the consumer goods value chain. Prior to its closure in 2008 Kineticsware had 50 employees and 142 customers in 5 countries. Based in Kirkland, WA with locations in Denver, Dallas, Chicago, Atlanta, NYC, Auckland and Copenhagen. Kineticsware was voted #4 on Top Ten Enterprise Software Companies 2007 by NetworkWorld and #2 Best Startup 2007 by Seattle Times.

Prior to founding Kineticsware, Jeff was the global director of business management for the Microsoft Dynamics AX product line of Microsoft Corporation. His responsibilities included worldwide P&L performance, business and channel development, partnerships, investments and acquisitions. He also acted as the executive sponsor for Dynamics AX partners and customers in the United States. Before Microsoft, Jeff was a senior executive and board member with several startup software companies focused on vertical ERP and digital media. Before moving into operating roles he spent five years as a partner at a venture capital firm funding and building growth companies. Jeff started his career as an investment banker in New York City executing leveraged buyouts and growth financings in consumer products, retail and other industries.

Jeff Sampson’s Specialties:

Startup software executive with sales and marketing, operations, business development, corporate finance, mergers and acquisitions, venture capital background.
