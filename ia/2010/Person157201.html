Christina Olivas’s Summary

I am a graphic designer first and foremost, but I also have a passion for marketing. Studying marketing has complemented my design education and I have had the opportunity to learn how graphic design is applied to the promotion of products and where it fits within a marketing mix.

Recently I entered the world of web design and have taken a great interest in front-end development. I plan to continue growing as a web designer by expanding my knowledge of planning and developing web pages.

My design process always involves finding the right balance within a design composition and eliminating unnecessary elements. Over the years I have come to trust my design process and have learned that research and development of a project is just as important as the technical aspects of the design.
