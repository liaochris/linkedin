Barbara Haimowitz’s Summary

I have been a transitional kindergarten teacher for over a decade. I have always had an interest in nutrition and over the years have become acutely aware of the need to help children and their parents understand how the food industry has been influencing their food choices. I decided to attend the Institute of Integrative Nutrition so that I would be able to expand my skills and ability to assist families with their diet. My goal is to help empower children and parents to make healthy food choices.
After completing the professional training program (with honors) at the Institute of Integrative Nutrition (IIN), I received my certification as a Health Counselor. I am now available to present the following workshops:
* Packing healthy lunches
* Nutrition 101 which includes; reading labels,
understanding whole foods vs processed
foods, how the food industry influences our
choices.
* The eating habits we pass onto our children.
* "Sugar blues" which looks at how our
body responds to sugar and the alternative
sweeteners available .

Barbara Haimowitz’s Specialties:

I am currently available to present:
* The"Nutrition Detective" workshop, developed by Dr. David Katz, for children ages 5-12 years old. This workshop will educate and empower children to make healthy food choices.
* Packing Health Lunches" for children, a workshop for parents or caregivers.
*Workshops for children ages 5-15 on making healthy food choices including how to read food labels and exploring the processed food dilemma.
