Noel DiBona’s Summary

Noel DiBona has over twenty-five years of experience helping companies improve their operations.

He has held positions with major corporations such as Kraft General Foods, The Clorox Company, Fluor Corporation, and Tetra Tech encompassing operations management, engineering, sales & marketing and executive management.

DiBona proudly served the U.S. Army as an Airborne Ranger and then held research positions at the Chemical Research Development & Engineering Lab in Edgewood, MD where he contributed several patents for the decontamination of chemical warfare agents.

DiBona has successfully helped to implement operational improvements for companies such as Electrolux, DuPont, Tetra Tech, BASF, Marathon Oil, Solvay, and Maxwell House Coffee. He has also worked with a large number of service companies involving engineering, research & development, large law practices, global sourcing and e-commerce businesses.

DiBona graduated from Northeastern University in Boston with a bachelor’s degree in chemical engineering and received his master’s degree in business administration from Central Michigan University. He also completed an executive management program at the Thunderbird American Graduate School of International Management.

Noel is active in a variety of civic and professional organizations and he has held various leadership roles in the Boy Scouts of America.

He resides in the Greater Washington, D.C. area with his wife and two children. In his spare time he enjoys many outdoor activities with his family and is an active member of Broadlands Community Church in Ashburn, Virginia.
