Jim Glaser’s Summary

A senior executive level recruiter also involved with socially conscious creative ventures. An arts world community leader pursuing the development of large events, interactive art forms and dynamic people networks.

Jim Glaser’s Specialties:

Connecting people for business, creative and social opportunities. Retained executive level recruiting in Media/Entertainment, Telecommunications, Technology, Management Consulting, Consumer Products and Private Equity/Venture Capital. Community leader in the "participatory culture" arts movement and social activist.

Jim Glaser’s Experience

  • Founder

    Open Kulture, Inc.

    (Entertainment industry)

    January 2010 — Present (4 months)

    A revolutionary business network and events production company that is bringing together thought leaders in the participatory culture arts, open source computing, free culture media and environmental advocacy communities around several potentially world changing projects. Creating an unstoppable coalition of forces capable of realizing tremendous visions and important issues advocacy while also being a profitable company. Get in touch if you'd like to know more.

  • Founder and Director

    Action Arts League

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    February 2005 — Present (5 years 3 months)

    A league of 'action oriented artists' and a 501c3 arts association that promotes interactive artforms bringing people closer through the development of public festivals, theme events, costuming, installation art and educational programs. The organization behind FIGMENT*, a multi-day arts event on NYC's Governors Island as well as Green Halloween NYC, part of a national movement towards 'creativity over candy' and sustainable practices for all holidays. (www.figmentnyc.org and www.greenhalloweennyc.org)

  • Founder, Chieftain

    Kostume Kult Arts Collective

    (Entertainment industry)

    August 2001 — Present (8 years 9 months)

    An NYC-based underground arts collective supporting costuming, street theater, interactive art and some of the most colorful events anywhere. We have more fun than anyone you know while bringing delightful people together. Annual events include the hottest float in the NYC Halloween Parade (since 2000), a fashion camp at the Burning Man Arts Festival (since 2002), the Horned Ball and Black & Light Ball, the Zombiecon and Leprecon street theater events, the "Alt.Oscar" Awards Event (an annual Oscar Party spoof event) and numerous other creative community activities.

    February 2008 — January 2009 (1 year )

Managed the executive level recruiting function for Lime Wire and several other Lime Group businesses while taking part in corporate development and event related activities.
