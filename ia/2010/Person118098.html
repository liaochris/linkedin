Knut Meyer’s Summary

Knut is an accomplished sourcing, transformation, shared services, and outsourcing leader with over 20 years of experience in global and multi-industry large-scale strategy and operations transformation, enterprise cost and performance optimization, and technology enablement. Knut has positively impacted financial and operational performance of numerous global companies in financial services, consumer products, automotive, IT and process industries. Examples include global sourcing and expense containment initiative generating $300 million cost reduction for global bank; enterprise cost and performance improvement for P&C company and life insurer, generating $ 100 million and $90 million respectively; and $80 million cost reducdtion and operations efficiencies at regional U.S. money center bank.

Knut's more recent focus included global delivery models including global shared services organizations, SG&A BPO/offshoring, enterprise performance, and technology enablement. As a leader in Deloitte's strategy and operations practice, and prior to that during a decade with ATKearney leading global initiatives for Fortune 100 financial services and industrial companies in sourcing, cost and performance management, organizational and operational performance improvement at corporate center and international operations in the Americas, Europe and Asia. Knut started his consulting career with Coopers & Lybrand Consultants (now PwC) as Manager, Business and Marketing Strategy.

Knut has extensive global and multicultural business experience working with large multinational and leading local companies; in Brazil, Venezuela, Mexico, the U.S., Western and Central Europe, the Middle East, and most Far East markets. He is fluent in English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, and French; has an M.B.A. degree from Kellogg, Northwestern University; and an economist degree from Universidad Catolica Andres Bello, Caracas.
