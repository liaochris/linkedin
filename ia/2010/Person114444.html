Ken Hudson’s Summary

As Managing Director of the Virtual World Design Centre I oversee a team of designers and developers building 3D virtual world environments for business, government, and education. I speak frequently on the successful application of virtual worlds for applied learning and training. I am also the author of several publications defining the best practices for using virtual worlds.

Ken Hudson’s Specialties:

Environmental and experiential design of 3D virtual environments. Public speaker and author on the applications of virtual worlds for education and business.
