Josh Jansen’s Summary

The Jansen Multifamily Team at Coast/Sperry Van Ness specializes in acquisition and disposition of apartment properties in the Puget Sound Region. Our market knowledge, in-depth financial analysis and proprietary sales processes have enabled our Team to differentiate ourselves from our competitors and successfully close over $21 million in commercial real estate Year-to-Date 2009.

The goal of the Jansen Multifamily Team is simple: maximize our clients' returns. We succeed in achieving our goal of "maximizing returns" by creating "maximum competition" for our clients' assets through disciplined underwriting, aggressive marketing and superior customer service.
