Devin Voorsanger’s Summary

Devin Voorsanger, VP, Business Development & Agency Services
Devin has been working in the Interactive world for 14 years, longer than even Al Gore. Before most of us were using email, in the mid-nineties Devin was charged with creating the online presence for well-known publisher, and brought the magazine's loyal following online, while ensuring that the organization's online presence adequately represented it brand and image.

In 1999, Devin founded the interactive marketing firm Parabolica, where he created web presences and charted marketing strategies for clients as diverse as Luminant Worldwide, JWT, De Beers and JVC. Four years later, Parabolica merged with Viking, Hospitality Marketing and Media to offer cross-channel marketing and performance marketing strategies for the travel, cultural, and hospitality industries.

Today, Devin is a leading authority on leveraging personalization, marketing automation and cross-channel marketing technologies to enable high-performance marketing campaigns for direct marketers. He specializes in the incorporation of Personalized URLs (PURLs) into social, broadcast and direct response marketing initiatives. He frequently speaks at conferences, trade shows and other industry events, including most notably marketing workshops held by the Direct Marketing Association (DMA).

When he's not helping Easypurl.com's clients sell, produce and manage industry-leading cross-channel marketing strategies, Devin can usually be found hiking in the mountains throughout the tri-state area or training for the marathon he will probably never run.
