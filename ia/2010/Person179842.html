Ryan Paul Peterson’s Summary

I've been in the digital media space for the past 5 years with a primary focus on innovations in the electronic entertainment industry.

My internship with OnlineWorlds gave me first hand knowledge of gaming research for the creation of browser-based RPGs. Additionally, I wrote script for existing games and developed several popular TELNET-based MUDs as a hobby.

Following school, I started in the digital media space working on IBM, Texas Instruments, and Lufthansa Airlines at mOne. After joining the Ogilvy Emerging Tech department, I took on the added responsibility of working with clients such as Cisco and DHL on all advertising solutions related to the videogame industry.

Currently employed at OMD New York, I am responsible for finding the best videogame advertising solutions for all of our agency clients. I work with AAA developers and platform innovators to seamlessly integrate my client's brands in videogames and on gaming platforms such as Xbox Live and Playstation Network. My campaigns include in-game integration, product placement, DLC sponsorship, custom game development, co-media promotions, and a variety of original videogame initiatives for ourclients. My group has created and executed industry-first, award winning programs for clients such as Frito Lay, Nissan, State Farm, Gatorade, Reese's and Visa.
