Jenna Bennett’s Summary

I am an assistant account executive at LaBreche with a strong passion for media relations. I have a knack for developing strong relationships with the press, and I really enjoy targeting the best strategic opportunities for my clients.

Prior to joining LaBreche, I spent four years as a broadcast news reporter, working at KTVH-TV in Helena, Montana, and WAOW-TV in Wausau, Wisconsin. I worked a general assignment beat, covering everything from the environment to healthcare to politics. After learning a lot about media relations, my drive to focus on strategic communication led me to transition from being in front of the camera to being behind the scenes.

Jenna Bennett’s Specialties:

Brand development, key messaging, media relations, social and new media, writing & editing, media training.
