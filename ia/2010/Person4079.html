Ahkim Staman’s Summary

I have built up significant experience within the Online Industry at Google. My background as both a Sociology Master and MBA with Honours has given me a pretty neat approach to things. I can see where improvement is needed, but can also keep an eye on the human, organizational and political aspects as well.

My career started with a short stint at Capgemini as a Consultant, sadly the crisis hit us there pretty hard and promised us a good 6 months of bench sitting. Preferring a path of action and (calculated risk) I decided to venture into the labour market again and found a job in the AdSense team at Google.

Shortly after starting at Google I was able to turn a 6% negative QoQ market growth into a 25-30% growth. Through solid advice and innovative strategies I was able to sustain this growth for my portfolio during the entirety of the crisis. This resulted in an interesting achievement, whereby the value of the Dutch AdSense portfolio more than doubled within the space of a year.

After Google I felt it was time to put my experience into practice independently. To my taste Google reduced an immensely dynamic industry to relatively simple and repetitive actions. A danger of hyper scalability perhaps.

This resulted in the choice to found Ad-Sensei. A company focused on providing publishers with the finest information on monetization of their traffic through AdSense, exchanges, direct sales or other network players.

I'm currently working with one of the largest Online Communities on the globe to improve their Online Marketing revenues through various channels.
