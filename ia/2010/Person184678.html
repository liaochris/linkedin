Scott O'Loughlin (Scott@LEDSigns4Less.com)’s Summary

Relationships are the foundation of any business. Scott makes it his business to forge new relationships and foster his existing connections.

He has an impressive breadth and depth of experience in marketing, sales, electronic media, direct mail and promotions.

His experience includes working for one of Jacksonville, Florida's largest advertising agencies and account management for America's two largest outdoor-media companies.

Scott earned a bachelor’s degree in mass communications with a minor in consumer science at Western Kentucky University where he was a four-year letter-earner as a Hilltopper cheerleader.

Scott resides in Prospect, Kentucky, where he lives alongside the Ohio River.
