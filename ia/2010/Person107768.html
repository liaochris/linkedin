Judith Poole’s Summary

Judy is a seasoned recruiter In NYC and Silicon Valley in information technology and services, the internet and other technologies. Her research intensive nationwide approach, high integrity, and heavy use of references early in the search process have surfaced candidates never seen through other recruiters.

Judy has performed over 125 CEO searches for small companies and senior management at Fortune 100 companies.
