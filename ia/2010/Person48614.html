Dan Moon’s Summary

Knowledgeable and skilled technology and business manager with a breadth of experience in web, mobile and social technology.

Highlighted deliveries include websites, ecommerce, mobile applications and telesales for public companies Vodafone UK, Quintain Estates & Developments PLC and Walt Disney Internet Group.

My experience can fall into the general categories of thinking, rationalising, and engineering.

Quick to see complex interrelationships between people, things, and ideas. These interrelationships are analyzed in profound detail, resulting in an in-depth understanding of the way things and relationships work, and how they can be improved.

Frequently described as clever, cerebrally and verbally quick, enthusiastic, outgoing, innovative, flexible, loyal and resourceful. I am motivated by a desire to understand and improve the world we live in. Usually accurate in sizing up a situation, one is ingenious and adept at directing relationships between means and ends.

I "think outside the box," devising fresh, unexpected solutions to difficult problems. Though best placed strategically to generate ideas and possibilities, am also proficient in generating and following through with detailed plans.

Within a team environment, I offer deep understanding and a high degree of flexibility and problem solving ability. I regard a comment like "it can't be done" as a personal challenge, and will spare no expense to discover a solution.
