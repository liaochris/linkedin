Our goal is to have Camp Better America's offered throughout the country. We host military families that have a soldier that has been on multiple deployments(single or married), soldier coming home as a wounded warrior, or the widow(er) and children dealing with the loss. There is a breakdown of the family unit and they are not equipped to deal with this by themselves . Some of the issues are PTSD which can lead to depression, alcoholism, suicidal thoughts, anger, frustration, anxiety, and unfortunately the staggering divorce rate among our military families.These families will come to our resort-like vacation spots where the atmosphere is family oriented and filled with fun, an opportunity where the soldier can relax, let their walls down and reconnect with their families but at the same time be exposed to some of the leading experts in the fields of mental, financial, emotional and physical well being.
    It is our goal to maintain the relationship and continue to offer support tools and opportunities to these families as they progress with being home.
