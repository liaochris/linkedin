Dean Long’s Summary

As a business and career coach, I partner with individuals and organizations in helping them attain greater success and fulfillment in their lives, both personally and professionally. Through my one-on-one coaching sessions, I guide them in realizing and implementing actions needed in order to realize the vision that they have for themselves and their organizations.

My 30+ years of business experience includes consulting engineering, predominately in project management, and 3 years as a branch manager, along with 14 years as a financial advisor with Morgan Stanley.

The common thread through my three careers is my passion for helping my clients achieve their goals and objectives. I love and enjoy the collaborative process in developing and maintaining the client relationship!
