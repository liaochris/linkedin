Brian Tomkins’s Summary

Brian Tomkins is the Chief Architect & CIO of Boom Media in San Diego. He is responsible for helping clients build community around their brand by supplementing traditional marketing techniques with online social media and other online tools. Tomkins is a regular TV contributor on “Good Morning San Diego” (KUSI) and is a weekly contributor on KCBQ radio discussing the latest trends in social media as they apply to business, politics and current events. Boom Media is a modern media firm that helps politicians, businesses, organizations, and entertainers increase revenue, profit, volume or identity through online media campaigns.

He is a former educator and coach. He received his B.A. from Lindenwood University and M.A. from Governor's State University.
