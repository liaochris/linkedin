Andy Klee’s Summary

From starting out over 30 years ago as an elementary school teacher, to his 2008-2010 enrollment in Harvard’s Owner/President executive education program—Andy knows firsthand the impact that great teachers and learning experiences have on one’s career.

Mix in his 20 years of ERP consulting experience, and the result is someone who is passionate about serving the ERP community with services that make a difference in the lives of those who must make use of (and fully understand) SAP and JD Edwards software.
