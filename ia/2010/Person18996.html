Ashley Wendel’s Summary

Ashley is a former “Big 4” management consultant with a unique background in both individual / organizational change and clinical psychology. She has over 15 years combined experience.

Ashley works with physicians and healthcare teams that are in a state of “transition”. Individually, she acts as a Physician Executive Coach, helping doctors to identify/assess, plan and implement strategic changes in their professional lives. She acts as both a career transition coach, working with physicians who are exploring non-clinical options or actively leaving clinical medicine, as well as an executive coach with physician leaders who are looking to build their core skills in communication, conflict resolution, EQ development, influence, and expert management of change. Organizationally, Ashley works with executive leadership and healthcare teams to identify and address systemic / team dysfunctions that are hindering critical factors such as patient safety, clinical outcomes, credibility/referrals, and the overall financial viability of the program or healthcare organization.

Utilizing both her backgrounds in clinical psychology (specifically Behavioral Medicine) and management consulting with Fortune 500 companies, Ashley has trained in and developed specialized skill in working with the complexities of healthcare organizations and current mandates for physician leaders. One of her greatest strengths is developing and executing strategies to promote the true behavior change that is required for sustainable initiatives.

Ashley pursued and became a Certified Physician Development Coach as well as a Certified Master Coach, holding a Graduate Certificate of Applied Coaching from the Behavioral Coaching Institute. In 2007 Ashley founded High Agility Healthcare, a coaching and consulting practice which specializes in working with physicians and/or healthcare organizations “in transition", helping them to implement positive and sustainable change.
