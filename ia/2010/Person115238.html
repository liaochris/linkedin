Kevin Meyer’s Summary

Though the titles “CPA” and “web developer” are rarely used in the same sentence, the relationship between high-powered corporate finance and the development of sophisticated websites and web applications is, in fact, quite logical. Both require in-depth understanding of technology, strict attention to the streamlining of process improvements, and a strong knowledge of business principles and objectives.

With six years of experience working as a CPA in Illinois, I have held positions ranging from financial consultant to Vice-President/Controller at a multi-national corporation.
My web experience includes three years of developing and implementing both custom-built static and database-driven solutions for small to mid-sized businesses. My experience in e-commerce solutions, PHP and MySQL gives me confidence to understand complicated open source applications. My code is clean and intuitive, and my keen ability to understand whole projects quickly and accurately saves my clients both time and money.

A graduate of the University of Illinois with a double major in Accountancy and Finance, Kevin is interested in riding motorcycles and skiing, among other things.
