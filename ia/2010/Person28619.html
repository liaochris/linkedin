Brian Konopa’s Summary

Chauffeur since 1995. I have always had an appreciation for domestic and foreign luxury vehicles. The seed for the desire to own and operate my own limousine company was planted years ago while working as a valet at the local country club and also influenced by limousine sightings along the highway when traveling between home and Chicago. I took the initiative to persue employment as a chauffeur in order to gain firsthand knowledge of the intricacies of the business. My long term goal is to own and operate one of the top three limousine companies in the area in terms of size and reputation, either through growth, acquisition, or a combination of the two.

Brian Konopa’s Specialties:

Luxury ground transportation service provider for corporate and private sector. Area served is the Greater Rockford Metropolitan area including airport transfers to O'Hare, Midway, Mitchell, and others and round-trip or one-way transportation to areas within an approximately 100 mile radius from Rockford.
