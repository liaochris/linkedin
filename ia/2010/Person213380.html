Werner Adam’s Summary

TSI is the genesis of ITCi. My responsibilities at TSI include the development of new business opportunities, planning and direction of TSI’s BD objectives and initiatives. I share the responsibility with our VP of Operations to see that TSI is successfully aligned with SBA SDVOSB, 8(a) & HubZone Programs as well as other resources that can assist TSI with contract needs. I am tasked with the development of strategic relationships with potential business and technology partners promoting CloudeFX as the next generation framework for Platform as a Service (PaaS) in the Cloud Computing ecosystem.

TSI provides a next generation IT solution for business transformation assisting companies to modernize and maximize their IT assets. Our approach from business concept to automated executable process is unique to CloudeFX and permits individuals or teams to create, assemble, and implement their processes quickly and easily without programming while at the same time ensuring that core business processes remain accessible without being held hostage to proprietary systems and program code.

CloudeFX causes business solutions to be rapidly deployed through the web to constituents and communities of shared interest. The capability to access disparate and authoritative data sources – without writing code to extract structured and unstructured data, from disparate legacy silos into a single, coherent system is unique to CloudeFX. This results in a common operational picture for executing business process, decision support and mission plans.

CloudeFX augments ongoing Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) design principles used during systems development and integration. The CloudeFX SOA architecture can be deployed on any secure, scalable, interoperable and dynamic platform within multiple domains where the user assembles the "process workflow" through a browser allowing enterprises and developers to automate business processes, collaborate across IT and business domains.
