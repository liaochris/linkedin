Byron Rode’s Summary

I started working freelance as a web designer during my final years at school and naturally progressed to web development which I feel is more challenging.
I opened my own company about 2 years after starting, but still focused on freelance work until my business was able to sustain me.

I have worked with international companies and have collaborated on many projects regularly with a company based in JHB that is currently relocating to the UK.

I want to try and pioneer standards and cross-browser development and design in South Africa. I think that we have a wealth of knowledge in the web development, design and related fields, but our education system does not implement the newer practices into their courses and people are learning outdated information. I want to change this wherever possible by offering talks, consultancy and events based on the likes of the ones held overseas.
