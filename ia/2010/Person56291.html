Dianne Lobes’s Summary

I've worked in my chosen field of counseling for most of my life - a span of almost 30 years now. And I love it.

My counseling focus - consciousness and mindfulness - is different from most other therapists.

Because of my training with Jean Houston, Jon Kabat-Zinn, Joan Borysenko, and my current association with physicist Amit Goswami (from the movies, "What the Bleep Do We Know?"), I counsel and work on the deep level of conscious choice - where our consciousness actually works, where we can learn to make better choices for ourselves.

Because of these teachers and many others, I have the understanding and tools, such as The Map of Emotional Recovery, to help folks make immediate and lasting transformations in their lives.

Too often we don't realize that we have choice, much less that we can use our choice, in so many difficult situations. We get mired down in old conditioning that creates problems and delays in our life's development.

My choice is to help people understand how the powerful process of "getting to choice" works, and to increase the number of options they can give to themselves. It's immensely satisfying to be able to help people with depression, anxiety, relationship problems, career and educational problems, and those who are in transition and overwhelmed with our current world.

My workshop, "Making Conscious Choices", teaches these concepts in an interactive and entertaining format, and is available to groups, businesses, organizations and individuals at their own locations or at my group training room in Eugene. It's a cutting edge blend of psychology, science and spirituality that's great for leaping out of the box and into your own life.
