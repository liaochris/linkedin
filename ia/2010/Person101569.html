John Baldasaro’s Summary

I am a senior executive with 25+ years of experience in Healthcare Information Technology. I have set the vision and direction for a product line for one of the largest vendors in hospital information systems,which I helped build from its inception.

When I first joined SDK Healthcare, a regional company, they were beginning the transition from a regional service bureau to an in house solution. As VP, Product Development, I established the product strategy and developed a new product that became the leading product in the sector, establishing them as a national player and significantly increasing their customer base.

I developed the first product to view the hospital billing as the revenue cycle, which moved the back end billing process to the registration and clinical front end. This product was the basis for marketing partnerships with many of the major clinical vendors including Cerner Corporation. In 1997, SDK and TDS were combined to form Eclipsys Corporation.

As part of Eclipsys, I assumed the role of VP Systems Integration. I lead a team to integrate acquired products with disparate technologies into a single user experience. I developed a message broker which allowed systems to exchange information including single sign on and desktop management

Assuming the role of VP, Revenue Cycle solutions in 2002, my role expanded to manage the overall revenue cycle product portfolio direction. I ensured that the products met customer and market needs, and led existing and new customer contact. As a result, market share and sales revenue accelerated at a rapid pace. I introduced new access management products tightly integrated with the full set of clinical offerings. I established a 360 management team, coordinating the activities of the functional areas of sales, marketing, development, implementation and support. I was a key member of senior management, establishing an effective product management function within the corporation.

John Baldasaro’s Specialties:

I bring a unique blend of business acumen, industry experience, product management skills and technical savvy. I have strong communication skills and am able to relay the value proposition and competitive edge of solutions to internal teams as well as the external market.
