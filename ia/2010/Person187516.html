Shannon Mullen’s Summary

I'm interested in building long-term relationships with creative business leaders in marketing/communications, brand management and advertising.

I have always been interested in how brands help us express our own identities. I left an academic track years ago as a would-be cultural anthropologist to work as an applied anthropologist in an ad agency and have pursued this same interest in brands by developing brand strategies, new product development, interactive communication and brand experience stores.

In recent years, I have been combining this interest in brands and their connection to people by connecting my clients' brands to individuals who will best be able to help them accomplish their goals.

I have deep relationships in the NY market, Western Europe and Asia. Mullen Marketing Search is now a network of recruiters and researchers around the world. We are and will remain a boutique specializing in marketing and advertising. We work for marketing departments of Fortune 500 brands and global integrated ad agency networks and brand consultancies.

If you are searching for CMO or global brand management team members or their advertising agency partners -- account management, strategic planners/brand planners -- please email me directly to discuss: shannon@mullenmarketingsearch.com

I'm always interested in meeting creative business leaders even if you're not currently in the market to hire or be hired.
