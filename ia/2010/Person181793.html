Sandra Mallut’s Summary

I was a mortgage broker for many years and had always dreamed of being a Pastry Chef and Cake Decorator. One day I just did it and changed my career and path in life to live in the culinary world. I enrolled in the California School of Culinary Arts and received my Le Cordon Bleu Diploma in Pastry and Baking and have been going strong since. I have worked in restaurants, bakeries, cake shops, caterers, and gained experience in all of them. I continue to educate myself with classes, reading, research and experiences in kitchens. I am a freelance Culinary Consultant in baking, cooking, flavors, recipe creation, trade shows, social media, networking, marketing and more.

I am doing a lot to focus on Social Media and what is has to offer to the local Bakery, Restaurant, Small Business. I am working on monthly contracts with clients.

I teach private and public cooking/baking classes, I have done demonstrations at local gourmet food stores, local, trade shows, competitions in baking/culinary, events, weddings and more. I recently just participated in the Disney Food & Wine Festival 2010 with demonstrations. I owned my own business called Henrietta Poodlestones Bake Shop where I created specialty cakes, desserts, cupcakes for Film & TV sets/locations. I was involved in doing custom "Wrap Party" cakes for shows and movies which was a lot of fun.

My goals are to continue my education, get involved with more competitions, teaching, demonstrations, hopefully teaching at my school CSCA is in my future I would love that. I look forward to doing more as a social media consultant to get more restaurants, bakeries and small culinary businesses on the map.

I have also been working with creating an online blog that will offer information on how to market yourself online and how to use the online sources to get business and create a brand. I have many ideas that I am working on and a new business in the process too! Got to keep moving and creating.
