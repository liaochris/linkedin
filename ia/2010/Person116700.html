Kimberly Kitchen’s Summary

As a professional in the social media arena it has been my expertise to increase brand awareness through the use of many tools. These tools are constantly changing which makes it imperative to stay in front of the newest applications - from Facebook, Twitter, blogs, LinkedIn and how RSS feeds can impact the constant messaging.

Many companies know they want an identity through these mediums but aren't sure how to proceed. It's my specialty to identify their objectives, explain the limitless possibilities and implement a strategy that will increase their business.

My experiences as a past business owner, sales professional, sales manager, personnel development specialist, recruiter and facilitator have been instrumental in developing the skill set to identify and implement the social media marketing strategy that is necessary for each client to succeed.
