Roger Smith’s Summary

ACQUISITIONS: Provides leadership and project management for all aspects of M&A – pipeline cultivation, strategic fit, valuation, financial modeling, bid preparation, negotiation, Board presentations, contract drafting, due diligence, risk analysis and post-acquisition integration.

STRATEGIC PLANNING: Drives an effective, practical approach to building business strategies, in which simple, straightforward approaches work best. Recognizes that strategies only become useful when implementation steps are clearly defined, and are reflected in operating plans and annual budgets.

FINANCIAL PLANNING: Financial visionary with strong analytical skills, good judgment and strong operational focus. Constructively challenges the status quo. Demonstrated success in revenue generation, cost management, operational improvement, capital expenditure & investment analysis. Able to communicate and interpret complex financial issues at all levels, from Board to work groups.

GENERAL MANAGEMENT: Experienced leader of turnarounds and start-ups in technology-based companies – electrical and electronic equipment, lighting, software – where quality & service levels are paramount. Motivates teams and achieves goals across organizational lines, with or without direct authority. Straightforward communication style, leading by example to build team commitment for superior results.
