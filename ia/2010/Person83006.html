helio p. felix is at this moment in phase of graduation in cinema (Film Director)

helio p felix started its initial formation through the Portuguese Photograph Institute, of where he removed the formation form base to exert photograph of professional form, The Professional Course of Photograph of the Portuguese Photograph Institute aims the cultural, visual formation and technique of who search the Photograph as profession or as a way of personal expression.
