Craig Duswalt’s Summary

UPCOMING ROCKSTAR BUSINESS SEMINAR

Craig Duswalt's RockStar System For Success
How to Achieve RockStar Status in Your Industry

February 5, February 6, and February 7, 2009

Hyatt Valencia Grand Ballroom
24500 Town Center Drive
Valencia, CA 91355
(just 20 minutes north of Los Angeles)

Did you ever dream of becoming a RockStar while growing up. Of course you did. You probably sang your favorite song along with the CD in front of a mirror, or played air guitar in your bedroom when no one was watching. But that's where it ended. Aside from a few Karaoke gigs, you never became a RockStar.

Well there's good news. Even though you never became a RockStar in the music industry, it's still possible to become a RockStar in your business.

If you're an entrepreneur and own a business, or if you're self-employed and you want to make a ton of money and still have a life, or if you work for a corporation and want to climb the corporate ladder, or even if you work in the non-profit sector and need to raise capital, it's not too late to become a "RockStar" in your industry.

On February 5 through February 7, 2009 an exciting E-vent is coming to the Hyatt Valencia Grand Ballroom in Santa Clarita, California.

Craig Duswalt combines his experiences working with Axl Rose, Guns N’ Roses and Air Supply, with his experiences as a million dollar entrepreneur, an ad agency owner, and a creative director, to teach you clever and unique ways to generate more business by thinking outside the box – by marketing your business, and yourself, like a RockStar!

So secure your spot now!

Craig Duswalt’s Experience

  • Creator, RockStar System For Success

    RockStar System For Success

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Craig Duswalt is the creator of the RockStar System For Success -- How to Achieve RockStar Status in Your Industry. Craig holds 3 and 4-Day RockStar Marketing BootCamps every March and September in Los Angeles.

    Craig's background includes touring with Guns N' Roses, as Axl Rose's personal manager, and Air Supply, as the band's personal assistant.

    Craig was also an award-winning copywriter, working as a senior copywriter for a Los Angeles-based ad agency until opening up his own shop, Green Room Design & Advertising, which was named the 2002 Santa Clarita Valley Chamber of Commerce Small Business of the Year. Some of his clients included Baskin Robbins and the Los Angeles Dodgers.

    Craig combined his backgrounds of music and marketing into a system that he calls, The RockStar System For Success, where he teaches people across the United States how to achieve RockStar status in their industry.
