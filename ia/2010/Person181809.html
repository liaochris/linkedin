Sandra Nordmeyer’s Summary

I am thrilled that I have joined Indianapolis Signworks as Vice President / Business Development Manager and I look forward to partnering with people who need a creative, passionate, solutions provider for all of their signage needs.

Locally owned and operated since 1992, Indianapolis Signworks Inc. has built its distinctive reputation on high quality, creativity and excellent customer service. You might say “service” is what built our reputation and has grown our business. In addition to outstanding quality and superior service, our advanced techniques set us apart from the competition.

Indianapolis Signworks is located in Indianapolis, Indiana and services clients through-out the Midwest and the United States. Whether you're an Architect, Designer, Property Manager or just need a sign for your business or personal needs, we provide it all. Our “state-of-the-art” equipment enables us to produce custom work for you.

Contact me at Sandra@IndianapolisSignworks.com or (office) 317-872-8722 or (mobile) 317-264-9229 and we will begin collaborating on creating awesome signs that get you noticed and grow your business!
