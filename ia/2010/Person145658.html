Mike L. Murphy’s Summary

I'm an award-winning film director working in Los Angeles. I've directed numerous short films and commercials and am currently in development on a few feature films. I'm repped by Peter McHugh at Gotham Group and have sold and optioned script properties to major studios. I have studied acting at The Lee Strasberg Institute, Carter-Thor Studios and 2nd City Improv.

I gained my knowledge of filmmaking working as an CG artist on such franchises as Lord of the Rings, Fast and the Furious, Shrek, Stuart Little, Microsoft's Halo and Harry Potter. The total combined grosses of all the properties I've worked on exceeds 3 billion dollars worldwide.

I have also taught University courses and lived abroad.
