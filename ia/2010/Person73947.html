Gene Northup’s Summary

Gene Northup, a specialist in upper tier communities and luxury homes, has earned renown as a leader in marketing Southern Nevada's most exceptional properties. Gene has maintained his position among the top producing real estate agents in the world, believing in taking his profession to the highest level of quality service. His commitment to providing the finest real estate experience on behalf of his clients is his number one philosophy, earning him recognition as a member of the Who's Who of Luxury Real Estate. Currently serving as a board member for The Institute for Luxury Home Marketing, Gene has gained a wealth of knowledge and experience to better serve his client's interests. A dedicated family man, Gene is committed to exceeding his client's needs, continually striving to bring together remarkable homes with remarkable lives.
