Jason Strachan’s Summary

Hi my name is Jason Strachan, creative currently based in Melbourne, Australia.

I love to design interfaces and information, we consume more information than ever before and I aim to make the experience of digesting it as compelling as possible. I work in both online and print mediums and have worked on everything from enterprise content managed sites to rich 3D experiences. I have been awarded for both web design & photography projects. Have a look around and get in touch to chat about startups ideas, business opportunities and creative consulting.

  • Senior Designer

Working on a project of such a large scale made me think about design slightly differently, the goals were still usability related, but coordinating a common approach between many designers became the most important task.

  • Designer

I worked on numerous blue chip accounts, which included both content driven and e-commerce sites.
