Oshan de Silva’s Summary

Oshan is a qualified engineer having completed his degree at the University of New South Wales. Gaining his experience within the engineering team of a large global recruitment company and making a name for himself as a specialist consultant, Oshan decided that a specialist boutique agency would enable him to provide world class recruitment expertise and a personal service to his clients and candidates. Leading the Engineering business of Ensure Recruitment allows Oshan to work with clients on a national scale and source the right skill set for the role with only one point of contact.

Being a qualified engineer, Oshan understand the engineering industry and has worked across private industry and government clients sourcing candidates from both the domestic and international candidate pools. As a client he will understand your business needs and as a candidate he will be able to work with you to identify the right career opportunities for a successful future.

Some of the products and services we will provide include;
• Executive/retained assignments
• Exclusive recruitment
• Project recruitment
• Research based recruitment
• Contingent recruitment
• Unbundled services
