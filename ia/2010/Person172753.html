Richard Geasey’s Summary

Experienced professional with over twenty years of technology business development and marketing experience in domestic and international markets serving a variety of sectors including commercial business, government agencies and a variety of vertical markets.

Published author and speaker on small business marketing using the Internet. Co-founder of Practical Local Search; a developer of books, seminars and services for small business owners, independent professionals and store owners who desire to market themselves more effectively on the Internet.

Social media marketing consultant and business development manager focusing on providing practical social media solutions and campaigns for all size businesses seeking to better promote their products and services through on line social networks.
