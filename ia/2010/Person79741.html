Hannah Coffey’s Summary

Dedicated dental professional for over 30 years. Expansive knowledge in dentistry as a Certified Dental Assistant for 15 years, through expanded-duty dental assisting in all dental specialties. As a Clinical Teaching Associate in Dental Hygiene, engaging students in a commitment to excellence and focusing on the reformation of professional roles facing dental hygienists' scope of practice and advancement in dentistry. Working as a Registered Dental Hygienist for the past 13 years, striving to overhaul the level and quality of periodontal care. Expertise in diagnostics and specializing in nonsurgical periodontal therapeutics. To shed the traditional paradigms of dental hygiene and develop its constantly evolving role in preventive and proactive dental treatment in conjunction with its systemic role in the overall health of society.
