Felicia Miller-Stehr’s Summary

Felicia Miller-Stehr has worked as a Producer and Talent Executive on many syndicated, cable and network shows for nearly 20 years.

She served as Executive Producer of "This Evening with Judith Regan" on Fox News Channel, Senior Producer on The Montel William's Show, Coordinating Producer on Lifetime's "Live From Queens" and Producer on "The Jane Pauley Show", "The Ricki Lake Show", "The Dr. Joy Brown Show", CNBC, MTV, Tupelo Honey Productions, CBS News Productions and "The Edge w/ Jake Sasseville". From live shows to long form documentary , Felicia has worked in almost every type of television genre.

In addition to producing hour long shows, she has honed her skills as a Talent Executive and has served as The East Coast Talent Executive for Oxygen Media , securing A list celebrity talent for two daily shows, plus many weekly shows and web content. In addition to Oxygen she has booked celebrities, athletes, comedians and musical talent for "The Brick Awards", Nickelodeon, MTV, Lifetime, CNBC, Fox News Channel, CBS News Productions, Fox and various award shows and specials.

She also managed all aspects of talent coordination at various award shows such as The Billboard Music Awards, The Essence Awards and The Espys.

Lately , she has been working at Digitas focusing on branded integrated content producing webcasts such as Mind, Body , Balance on MSN w/Faith Ford and The Digital Content Newfront's with Al Gore, Tom Green, Paula Deen and Milo Ventigmilia.

She also wrote and produced promo vignettes for the Dr. Oz Show and published her first Children's book "Are You Chanukah or Christmas?".

Felicia is a seasoned professional who is well versed in all areas of production. Her ecclectic background makes her a a great candidate for any type of production.
