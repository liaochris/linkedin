Chris Fredericks’s Summary

More than 17 years in media, advertising, and promotional marketing experience. Prior to joining Wingman Media, as VP Business Development, Chris was founding partner and Managing Director of Media Makers LLC in 2002 after his 11 year tenure with Initiative Media an IPG’s unit (NYSE: IPG) where he rose thru the agency ranks to become EVP & GM of the promotional marketing services division.

Chris is a seasoned sales executive responsible for securing new advertisers for multi-channel campaigns including digital, radio, television and print.

Media Makers LLC., a division of Williams Worldwide Television (DRTV) connected major corporate sponsors with various entertainment properties including Cirque du Soleil, Vans Warped Tour, Sony Pictures and Clear Channel Entertainment.

Chris works with clients including Toyota, Hot Topic, Dodge, PerfectMatch, 1-800 Flowers, Samsung and others. Media Makers LLC is head quartered in Los Angeles with offices in New York, Beijing and UK and is rapidly emerging as a leader in the growing branded entertainment field. Chris sold tens of millions in gross sponsorship sales.

Previously as Initiative, Under his tutelage, Chris managed a number of specialized in business-to-business and business-to-consumer marketing and loyalty programs programs. During his ten-years at Initiative, he grew the division’s billings three-fold and managed over 30 staffers.

Some of the many of clients Chris has worked with at Initiative include Motorola, Disney, Warner Bros, Verizon, Foot Locker, Home Depot, Federal Express, Toyota, Dodge, Darden Restaurants, Carl's Jr., CBS, University of Phoenix and Boeing.

Chris, an avid yachtsman and a California native, with the exception of spending the first four years of his life in New York, received his Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a major emphasis in Marketing and a minor in International Business from California State University system.
