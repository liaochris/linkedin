Joe Anzalone’s Summary

As an advertising and marketing consultant in the NY/NJ area since 1986, I've been providing solutions for promoting business development to small and medium size businesses, and independent professionals. Prior to establishing Online Market Group, LLC in 2003, and relocating the business to Jacksonville, Florida in 2005, I created a dot com company targeting boat sales on the Internet with two partners. I was a Regional Vice President & General Manager for IntelliSpace; a broadband company based in New York City, and entrepreneur. I'm an accomplished award winning professional photographer who has also produces television commercials, and corporate videos.

As Owner and Managing Director of Online Market Group, LLC, I bring extensive experience in penetrating new and existing markets through innovative advertising and marketing content development including production for new media.

My company specializes in: Advertising (electronic & print), Graphic Design, Websites, Commercial Photography, Video Production, Website Marketing including SEO, PR and Business Networking.

Online Market Group, LLC is based in Jacksonville Florida area. I'm also an active member of Rotary, and The Jacksonville Chamber of Commerce.
