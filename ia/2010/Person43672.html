Dick Costeris RI’s Summary

I am a senior consultant and trusted advisor with a passion for managing the relationship between business and IT. My field of expertise lies in the management of IT environments, with a strong focus on quality management and IT service management.
In 2009 I was the first in the world to achieve the manager/consultant level in ISO/IEC 20000.

In the 25 years of working experience, I built and designed information systems, ran (large) projects and programs and finally specialized in setting up and optimizing IT departments that occupy themselves with the management of information systems.
My approach is always pragmatic, combining an extensive experience with a thorough theoretical background. As an advisor, my assignments vary from strategic to operational levels.

IT Service Management, consulting, trusted advisor, regievoering, governance, it control, it management
