Kevin Flatt’s Summary

An accomplished and experienced creative leader, Kevin's expertise span many forms of visual communication, advertising, digital interaction and design. His foundation is based in the design industry knowing how to connect with consumers both at a brand and retail level. This has guided him as the consumer environment shifted and opportunities grew in the advertising and digital worlds. He has worked in, and helped to create, highly collaborative creative environments resulting in broad creative platform thinking that successfully connect with consumers in many ways.

Highly awarded and accomplished, he has been most recognized for his efforts in the industry game-changing BMW Films campaign. Creating ground breaking solutions is the kind of challenge he enjoys. His track record in new business wins is impressive.

Kevin is actively involved in the the industry, speaking at events and judging award competitions.
