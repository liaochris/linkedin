Brent Swisher’s Summary

Well i ran my own small contract lock box courier service for over 30 yrs , Swisher Mail Service . Became a great mechanic keeping the vehicles operating 150 to 200 miles a day . Then got my doctorate in classic car restoration working for over 9 yrs at Thoepes Corvette Shop in Ofallon , Il. building Barrett Jackson Saturday night quality cars . We ran them through on saturday night in Scottsdale Az. on a regular basis . They don't get much nicer than that ! You can check some of the builds out on a little blog i was doing on them @ mopargrovis.blogspot.com . Still love them fast cars and there is nothing more satisfying than the completion of one of the long term projects with the guys . Most relaxing is doing a little gardening and thinking . It saved my life a couple of times ! And that is me !
