Ryan Berry’s Summary

I am a litigator primarily serving DLA Piper's defense and intelligence community clients. I provide litigation services and risk mitigation counseling to companies who send their employees to dangerous places -- battle zones, as well as post-conflict and post-disaster relief and reconstruction sites.

My broader practice at DLA Piper includes commercial litigation, national security law, white collar and regulatory defense, and government contracts litigation.

Ryan Berry’s Specialties:

National Security Law, Information Technology, Alien Tort Claims, Foreign Sovereign Immunity, Iraq, Afghanistan, Africa, Contingency Operations, Commercial Litigation, Government Contracts, Contract Disputes, Federal Acquisitions, Eastern District of Virginia, Appellate Litigation, Private Security Industry, Virginia law
