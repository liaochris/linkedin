Ann Rowe-McMullen’s Summary

With a background in crisis communications and disaster preparedness, Ann brings determination, focus and skill to her H1N1 public information work at the Florida Department of Health.

In addition to disaster preparedness preparedness, response, education and outreach, Ann has expertise in growth management, affordable housing and land preservation issues.

Ann has led public and media relations projects for clients including Innovation Park, the Florida Coalition to Prevent Underage Drinking, the United Way of Florida Children's Week, U.S. Cat Adjusters, the Florida Governor's Hurricane Conference, Destin Water Users, the Florida Department of Health, Storm Proof Roof, Florida Retail Federation, HealthCHECK, Emergency Response Educators and Consultants, Babcock Ranch Inc., and Front Porch Florida.

Ann's work with the Able Trust earned the Florida Public Relations Association Capital Chapter's 2008 Golden Image Award, and a Florida Public Relations Association Statewide 2008 Golden Image Award for the Children’s Week campaign.
