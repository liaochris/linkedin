Dr. David & Lisa Frisbie’s Summary

Nationally recognized experts on post-divorce family dynamics and structures. Named and quoted in print and broadcast media including USA Today, NY Times, ABC, CBS. Authors of ten books and dozens of articles about marriage and family life, especially post-divorce adults, single parents, remarried couples, and blended (step)families. Based in Southern California, frequent travel within North America and beyond.
