Rahul Dewan’s Summary

Rahul has been heading Srijan and giving direction to it over the last 9 years. The tryst with open source was a chance event, which has transformed the company from a "we-do-anything" business to one of India's leading "open source software services" firm.

Srijan specialises in implementing content management solutions and intranet/internet business applications using open source software. It has deep expertise in Drupal, TYPO3, and Django/Python.

Goals for Srijan
---------
- be recognised as a dependable partner for open source consulting and technology services, globally
- design, build and launch SaaS applications to morph into a products company in the next few years
- work a lot more with the development sector and governments around the world, to build citizen interfacing applications for enhancing transparency, accountability and thus bing about a change in quality of governnance for global citizens
- diversify our core services to include data visualisation, open source research, GIS
- make it an employee owned company

Personal Goals
--------
- diversify Srijan into business areas such as renewable energy, clean food, health, micro-finance, organic agriculture, CDM and similar areas of socio-economic importance
- become a social entrepreneur creating businesses addressing the BoP; thus uplifting the social and economic status of rural India
- be able to dedicate 75% of my work life in starting up social enterprises and social work (areas such as afforestation and water conservation)
