Alex Lambert’s Summary

Bio // Art Director + Project Manager
Alex Lambert started designing in 1997 providing local businesses with graphic design services and web sites. In 2003 he founded nü, a design house offering trend-setting creative services with print and internet application. Alex studied Graphic Communication with an emphasis in Print and Image Management at California Polytechnic State University. After moving to San Francisco, he started providing freelance Senior Design and Art Direction for advertising agencies and design houses such as IDEO, PunchCut, Organic, Ogilvy One, Modem Media, Agency.com, Swirl, McCann, and many others. He acted as Product Design Manager at 3jam, a small startup, helping create innovative communication applications for web, desktop and mobile. Recently he has been working on a range of consulting projects from iPhone application UI to company branding.


Bio // DJ + Promoter:
With roots in early electronica, a rave promoter in the 90’s, pirate radio owner and manager, KidHack brings a fresh edge to the Bay Area dance scene. Based in San Francisco, Kidhack has toured around the world and killed local dance floors. The crowd goes crazy with his swiss army knife of mixing styles and genres. Spinning everything from electro bangers, minimal techno, glitchy dustup, and pop remixes. With friends in the industry, KidHack spins on the bleeding edge of music and culture.

Keeping busy, KidHack is in the process of building a premiere talent and promotion collective, Future Universal, focused on bringing some of the best talent to the masses. Past events include Efflorescence, Electropolis, Robotronika, Future, and Super Ego. Building on a long history of throwing some of the Bay Area’s best parties, KidHack is constantly collaborating with promoters, DJs, and venue owners working on parties like Pop Roxx, LoveSick3, Bootie SF, and the Cal Academy of Science: Nightlife.
