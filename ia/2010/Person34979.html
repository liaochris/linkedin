Laurentiu Cenusa’s Summary

My career started in 2002 when I was still student and I was hired at SBC Romania as sales executive.

Most probably because my entrepreneurial spirit I quited to be a simple employee and in 2003 I decided with my associate to found Mundis Advertise.

In 2004 I chose the Internet as main communication channel and we focused all our attention to developing and improving the image of our online identity, Way2Web.

From 2004 until August 2009 I have been the General Manager at Way2Web.

Since August 2009 I started a new company focused on 2 directions:
1. web hosting services
2. online marketing strategy for Aoro.ro and Astratex.ro.
