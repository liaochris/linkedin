Steve Cantu’s Summary

My professional graphic design experience was molded from the outdoor advertising realm. I learned how to incorporate my creative and graphic design skills to benefit the clients I worked with to the best of my ability. Learning the outdoor medium opened my eyes to the world of advertising and is a core element of my art skills today.

The design skills attained from advertising have helped me excel into the world of UI art. One of the hardest parts was to stop thinking in inches/feet and work in pixels! Once my mind was set in pixels, UI has become my current passion and is going strong.

My goals currently are to further my art skills and possibly branch into other game art fields such as texturing/3D art. Once I feel ready, I would like to blossom into an Art/Creative Director role.

Steve Cantu’s Specialties:

Graphic Design (all aspects), Outdoor advertising knowledge, Art direction, Color correction, Photo manipulation, Quality Assurance of video games in black and white box capacity, User Interface work in video games from concept to implementation. Flash Animations.
