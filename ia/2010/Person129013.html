Lou Unkeless’s Summary

I am an experienced Digital Marketing Consultant with over 25 years experience in e-Business consulting, marketing, and operations. I specialize in building tailored Internet business solutions that help small to medium-sized companies generate more leads, increase revenue, and reduce operating costs through integrated on-line marketing strategies.

At WSI, we create visible, persuasive positions for our clients on the Internet, attract qualified prospects to their websites, convert those prospects into customers, and maintain profitable lifetime relationships with them.

With over 25 years experience as a marketing and operational executive at several enterprise business solution providers and consulting firms, I have led these companies through the process of designing, developing, and delivering business technology solutions to small, medium, and large companies in virtually every industry. My success is directly due to my focus on clearly understanding the client’s needs, and rapidly delivering affordable solutions that enable a company to achieve it’s objectives while creating a sustainable competitive advantage.

Lou Unkeless’s Specialties:

We provide lead generation and revenue growth solutions that combine the right go-to-market strategy with websites designed to drive and convert targeted traffic, paid search marketing (Pay Per Click), search engine optimization (SEO), social media optimization (SMO), e-mail marketing, web analytics, e-commerce solutions, and e-learning solutions. We also ensure the strategy includes traditional marketing techniques where appropriate to maximize ROI.
