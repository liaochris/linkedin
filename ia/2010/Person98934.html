Jing Feng’s Summary

LiveRail is a leading video monetization technology company providing products and services for Publishers, Ad Networks and Advertisers.

LiveRail technologies were designed from the ground-up to address the unique challenges and complexities of IP-distributed video content. LiveRail for Publishers is designed to streamline the trafficking, delivery, optimization and tracking of in-video advertising for publishers, distributors and content owners, whether from direct-sold campaigns or third-party sources.

Campaign Management and Delivery
From streamlined ad operations and trafficking workflow, support for linear and non-linear video ad units,VAST and VPAID compliance, native-transcoding, and CDN integration to rich video-specific metrics and reporting and custom ad packages like sequencing and exclusivity, LiveRail for Publishers is the complete solution for publisher video ad management.

Distributed Monetization Solutions
LiveRail for Publishers enables content owners to syndicate and track their videos across any IP medium. LiveRail enables custom rule sets for building complex and detailed revenue share reports for scheduled or real-time delivery.

3rd Party Ad Sources
LiveRail makes it simple to connect to any ad platform - with zero integration. Whether you need to plug into a 3rd party ad server used by your partners like DART or Atlas, or a 3rd Party Ad Network like Brightroll, ScanScout or Google.

Yield Management and Optimization
LiveRail for Publishers provides rich inventory allocation rules allowing content owners to easily accommodate and mirror digital sales strategies. LFP's optimization layer searches across multiple ad sources with real-time campaign pricing information providing unparalleled fill rates and yield.
