Tom Lunney’s Summary

Who am I?

I am a people person first who wants to bring value to any place I am involved. If I am not being valuable to the situation I am in I should not be there.

I am a connector. I network all the time so that I have resources I can connect together. I am constantly trying to help others build their businesses by working with like minded people.

I am here to help. As a business counselor with SCORE I am again constantly helping people get into business, stay in business, or close their business with some type of dignity. I take the time to talk to people and to give them ideas on where they can network, where they can find clients, where they can find like minded business associates, and where they can get good no strings attached advice.

I am a mentor’s mentor. I seek opportunities to make people, situations, and businesses better than how I find them.

This is not done with a balance sheet. There is no expected return on my investment. I make long range investments in people so they will make long range investments in themselves.

How do I make money? How do I get paid? The Law of Attraction. What you put out always comes back to you 10 fold. If you do not believe me try it yourself. If you do not understand what I am talking about talk to me. I will explain like I always do.

It is not my money anyway.

    US Veterans Resources

    (Financial Services industry)

    June 2010 — Present (4 months)

    US Veteran Resources is uniquely focused on the Veterans Benefit - Aid & Attendance.

    The Aid & Attendance benefit is available to individuals over the age of 65 that have served in the United States Military during an armed conflict. Veterans as well as their surviving spouse may be eligible. Benefits are as much as $1949.00 per month tax free.

    U S Veteran Resources, Inc. provides information about the aid and attendance benefit including what it is, who can qualify and what information and documentation are necessary in order to file a claim. U S Veteran Resources , Inc. does not participate in any way in the application process. All prospects are referred to a certified veterans benefit counselor or an Attorney who is a certified veterans benefits counselor representing his or her client in proceedings before VA. US Veteran Resources, Inc. is a private practitioner and is not connected with the Department of Veterans Affairs. A veterans benefits consultant must comply with federal statutes governing the preparation, presentation and prosecution of any claim for veterans benefits with the US Department of Veterans Affairs.

  • Lt Col (Ret)

    USAF

    (Government Agency; USAF; Military industry)

    July 1974 — November 1994 (20 years 5 months)

    One lucky fellow!! Accumulated over 3000+ flight hours in the A-7D, A-10A, and O-2A. Flew in every corner of the then free world and every square inch of Alaska. Scared hell out of our friends the Russians – cost you a beer to hear that story. Never fired a shot in anger. My guys flew A-10s in the first Gulf War. My part was I trained them to fight and they all came home. Did not lose a one of them. I was in Alaska, on a secure base, in a secure building, in a vault, in another vault. My girls and Mom were happy I was far from the action. I called everyone I knew and could not even get a job sweeping sand off the runway. Flight Commander several times, Assistant Operations officer, Instructor Pilot for several operational squadrons, Plans Officer, USAF guy in charge of all logistics movement analysis for USTRANSCOM. It was quite the ride, and I worked with some of the finest people in the world. One man should not be so blessed.
