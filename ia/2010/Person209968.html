Heather Shoup’s Summary

With over 12 years of technical and marketing experience, Heather has a wide aptitude of technical ability to quantify & understand all aspects of a project.

In 2007, Heather was responsible for the overall planning and successful launch of VOX Wine Lounge in Henderson Nevada. Heather also assisted in planning, technical planning, branding, and development of EDGE Hospitality Group, First Friday Downtown Art District, and EDGE Design Group in Las Vegas, Nevada.

In 2008 Heather took her technical skills and applied it in the web and digital media industry. In 2008 Heather moved to New York to assist the NYC division of Decision Counsel as Web/Technical Project Manager.

In 2009 Heather successfully assisted Decision Counsel with the successful launch of their new Chicago division.

Heather has a wide array of technical knowledge and has hands on experience with backend development, IA, database administration, programming, social media development, and understand all steps involved from start to completion of a technical project.
