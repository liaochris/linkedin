Martin Soorjoo’s Summary

Martin is an Investor Pitch Coach and Founder of the Investor Pitch Clinic. He works with entrepreneurs and business owners across the United States, Canada, Europe and India, helping them secure business funding from Angel Investors and Venture Capitalists.

Martin provides advice by telephone,Skype and in peson on how to create winning

• Elevator Pitches
• Executive Summaries
• Investor Presentations
• Investor Focused Business Plans
• Structure, content and delivery of Investor Pitches

Martin also conducts Skype Mock Pitches and Video Pitch reviews and advises on how to identify and approach investors.

A former high flying attorney for over 15 years, Martin worked with numerous business investors including Senior Investment Bankers, Venture Capitalists and Angel Investors. During this period he raised several million dollars, including negotiating one deal worth $75 Million.

This experience equipped with him unique insights into how business investors make their decisions and the skills to help clients create and deliver an investor presentation that addresses the key questions investors ask, in a concise and compelling format.

Martin works with entrepreneurs and businesses seeking the full range of business funding, from seed to mezzanine financing. The majority of his clients are from the tech web and cleantech sectors.

Martin was nominated for national Human Rights Lawyer of the Year in 2004 and consistently rated by the world’s leading legal directories as a ‘leader in his field’. He is a certified Master Practitioner of Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP),expert in body language and has written extensively on a range of communication topics.

If you are trying to securing business funding from Angel Investors or Venture Capitalists and think you would benefit from professional advice then contact us for a free initial consultation on 650 373 2036 (US), email coaching@investorpitchclinic.com or mail him directly here.
