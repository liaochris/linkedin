Vincenza Restiano’s Summary

As the Director of Sale and Marketing for SmartPay Plus I am in charge of all sales activities and personnel involved in Sales and Marketing, while providing leadership to the sales department. Development of the Strategic Marketing Plan, establishing performance goals and maintaining and imporving business relations with all customers.

I am a sales associate with Joseph Anthony Real Estate, Inc. and I am a Division Manager with Lighthouse Catholic Media.
I started my career as an elementary school teacher in the Bronx. I was the owner of Park Place Studio in Bronxville which is now owned by my son Ricky Restiano.

I am an experienced former elected official and member of staffs of the City of Yonkers and State of New York. Highly motivated with many years of experience in government and in operations that can benefit private corporations.
