Ross Kodner’s Summary

Ready to help law firms, legal departments and Bar and professional associations learn about, leverage and best apply technology. Internationally known authority on legal technology and law practice management.

Founded MicroLaw, Inc. in 1985 as one of the first independent legal technology consultancies in the world. Helps law firms and law departments with strategic technology planning and reviews, legal-focused tech and business education. Frequent speaker for legal and business groups on technology with an "edutainment" approach.

* Founded and Chaired ABA GP|Solo's National Solo & Small Firm Conference
* Founded and Chaired Wisconsin Solo & Small Firm Conference
* Helped several states develop their initial Solo & Small Firm Conferences

Developed the widely-used Paper LESS Office(tm) process for turning physical paper into digital paper. "Inside-Out" marketing consultant to top companies in the legal marketspace. Co-founder of the Legal Technology Alliance. Co-founder of the Annual Legal Consultants and Technologists Dinner (www.thedinner.net). Website (www.microlaw.com) has a massive collection of educational resources on law practice technology.

Blogging at Ross Ipsa Loquitur (www.rossipsa.com) since early '06 about all things related to legal technology and law practice management, as well as the inevitable gadgets, fascinating web resources and more.

Always looking for a challenge with new clients who "get it," organizing a legal tech conference or a chance to spread the good word about the practical use of technology in modern law practice.

Favorite quotes: "Friends don't let friends word process without Reveal Codes (even in Word)" and "If you think hiring an expert is expensive, try hiring an amateur."
