Tim Dillard’s Summary

Tim Dillard has a degree from LSU and a Masters from Regent University in Virginia. He has worked with some of America’s largest corporations and ministries for many years, having helped to build the largest home service corporation in America, the largest dog racing company in America, the largest church in America and one of the largest homebuilders in the country – a division of Fortune 200 company, Weyerhauser.

Founder of North Pole Network Television, a mall-based TV network with 8 channels targeting mothers with young children. It has sponsors such as Warner Brothers, SONY, Chik Fil A and can be seen in more than 200 malls in the U.S. He has won more than 150 industry awards for his work and his non-traditional marketing methods have been the subject of study at major American universities.

He is the owner of more than 1000 websites. His brands have been featured in magazines such as Time, Bon Appetit, Newsweek and many others and on TV networks such as CNN, FOX News Channel, CW and CBS. Tim is a noted speaker having spoken to many organizations and clubs around the country including the NCAA and many major Universities such as Rice, Houston, Florida International, University of New Orleans and more. His current topic of interest that has him in demand for speeches is: How the Internet Works for Small Businesses the way it works for Large Businesses.
