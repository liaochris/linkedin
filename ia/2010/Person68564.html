Carlos Ezquerro’s Summary

Carlos was born in San Francisco, raised in Mexico City and returned to California and the Bay Area in 1982. Fluent in Spanish and English, he brings a depth experience to his real estate business and is uniquely qualified to collaborate with buyers and sellers in the multicultural Silicon Valley marketplace.

Prior to joining Green4Wealth, he worked with the Los Gatos office of Alain Pinel Realtors, the Citrus Heights office of Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage and Century 21 Golden Hills in San Jose. Carlos brings to the real estate business a wealth of skill and professionalism servicing the community as a realtor since March of 1998, at Casa Propia, Real Estate.

Carlos has an extensive real estate experience in Mexico City since he worked for Mac Division inmobiliaria, and Grupo Hir, Bienes Raices, and Remax in San Miguel de Allende, Guanajuato. His dream was to learn real estate in the United States.

Carlos graduated from San Jose State University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication Studies where he focused on Cross-Cultural Business and Organizational Behavior. Carlos, shows extensive multicultural experience, and demonstrates a deep understanding of diversity.
