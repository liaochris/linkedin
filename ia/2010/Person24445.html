Dan Meyer’s Summary

BizTimes Media LLC
Founded in 1995, based and locally owned in Milwaukee, BizTimes Media provides news and operational insights for CEOs, CFOs, presidents, owners and other top executives throughout southeastern Wisconsin. BizTimes Media provides these insights across a diverse multi-media platform.
• In Print BizTimes Milwaukee is a glossy bi-weekly magazine that provides exclusive business news, industry trends and analysis to help company leaders make better decisions.
• Online BizTimes.com was named one of the top three local business news web sites in the nation by the Alliance of Area Business Publications (AABP) in 2009. The web site is the home of the BizTimes Daily e-news bulletin, featuring exclusive Wisconsin business news of the day. The site features must-read content, including several exclusive weekly e-newsletters: BizTimes Manufacturing, BizTimes Money Weekly, BizTimes Real Estate, BizTimes Bubbler, BizTimes Nonprofit and BizTimes MKE Politics.
• In Person BizTimes Events provide access to key leaders, experts and decision-makers while honoring the most dynamic companies and executives in the region. The annual BizTech Conference & Expo is the largest business-to-business conference in Wisconsin, where more than 4,500 professionals come to learn, network and grow their companies.

As a locally owned and nationally recognized organization, BizTimes Media is an industry leader with a focus on convergence media, providing exclusive business news and insights for the leaders of companies throughout southeastern Wisconsin, including the counties of Milwaukee, Waukesha, Walworth, Kenosha, Racine, Washington, Ozaukee and Sheboygan.
