Brian Winters’s Summary

Brian Winters was initially published in 2005, releasing his second book a year later. The Other Realm and Blood Vengeance generated a buzz that inspired the author to expand into new territories of writing.

Describing his life as "a tragically hilarious sitcom" caught the attention of an up and coming euro magazine. Months of crafting what would have been a column following the author's misadventures in life, fell flat when the magazine lost funding. Not wanting to waste the time and energy that had gone into his work, Brian put together the blog Rent(minus)Control. The blog has captured a fan base that continues to inspire the author.

The Anomaly was released February 6th, 2010 and is the author's third published novel. This story is more than just words on a page. Taking not only several years, but three laptops, and several life lessons to complete. Brian is excited to continue The Other Realm story with the newest installment and looks forward to the completion of his current work.

Brian has been working for ADCIEO as a social media expert and copy editor. This type of position comes across as new to many, the idea of social media being a part of an organization's marketing strategy still in the infancy stages. The goal is to not only create new ways for organizations to communicate and connect with their donors and clients, but to integrate social media in such a way that the organization has all the tools for success.
