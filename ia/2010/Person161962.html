Mike Pedone’s Experience

  • Attorney

    Venable LLP

    (Partnership; Law Practice industry)

    September 2002 — Present (8 years 3 months)

    Corporate law practice focusing on the design, negotiation and execution of complex transactions, including:

    -- technology and commercial transactions, including licenses, service agreements, supply and distribution arrangements, joint ventures and strategic alliances, and

    -- Representation of the United States Department of the Treasury in various matters relating to the Troubled Asset Relief Program (TARP), including the Auto Supplier Support Program, a $5 billion program established to provide suppliers of General Motors and Chrysler with short-term liquidity and credit assurance for trade receivables.

    -- A variety of matters for Ferris, Baker Watts, including its acquisition of Arthurs LeStrange & Co. (2006), and its sale to Royal Bank of Canada (2008)
    -- MBNA’s 2005 acquisition of Nexstar Financial Corporation, a portfolio company of Kohlberg Kravis Roberts, for $212 million
    -- Several acquisitions by Mercantile Bankshares Corporation, including F&M Bancorp (2003), Community Bank of Northern Virginia (2005), and James Monroe Bancorp (2006)
    -- The $34 million sale of The Pfaltzgraff Co. to Lifetime Brands
