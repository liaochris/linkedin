Thomas Hogan’s Summary

My professional goal is to sustain myself based on my film scoring and audio production projects. I have a BA in Music & Sound Recording and have since continued my education in music, web design, and business related areas.

I am a percussionist with over 20 years of experience in a multitude of settings, including orchestral, jazz, rock, punk, and funk, to name a few. I have been in musical groups that have toured the United States and Europe and have been featured in many nationally distributed music publications.

I've been producing audio for radio since college, and upon moving to the west coast in 2006, I was able to produce audio in the commercial realm for a local station.

I also have experience doing sound in a live environment for musical groups as well as at high-profile conferences and award ceremonies.

Film scoring is a fairly new endeavor, but I have two short films, a television commercial, and a promo video under my belt, and am currently working on a score for a feature length movie as well as another short film.
