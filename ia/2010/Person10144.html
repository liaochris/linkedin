Amy Henthorn-Powell’s Summary

Certified Financial Planner, Certified Divorce Financial Analyst

I am a experienced financial advisor, who spends a significant amount of time ensuring that my client' s personal goals and objectives are at the center of what I coordinate for them. As the majority of my career has encompassed working for large corporations, I have still been afforded the ability to work within corporate guidleines and meet their objectives.

In the management of client's needs, it is important to be cognizant of their current relationships and incorporate those individuals into the planning process. Their opinions are valued by my clients. It is important for me to understand the relationship, earn the respect of their trusted advisors, and as a result, earn the respect of their advisors. If we create a communication network for the client, the end result is a more fluid relationship for that client and a team of adviosrs working on their behalf.

In the years I have been in financial planning, it has been very rare that I have found two situations the same. Each one is unique, with different people who have different concerns about what is important. Part of financial planning is realizing I don't always have the answer, being comfortable saying that to a client, but also helping the client understand that I will do whatever I can to assist them in finding the answer.

Amy Henthorn-Powell’s Specialties:

Divorce Financial Analysis is working in conjunction with a client's attorney. The goal is to protect the client's confidentiality as I am hired directly by the attorney to protect the "Attorney-Client" privilege through the work product exception.

When a client is divorcing there is a significant amount of emotion driving the settlement process. My assistance in the process is coordinating the numbers, the tax implications and the overall effect on the client.
