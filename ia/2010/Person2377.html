Adam Hoge’s Summary

I am an aspiring sports journalist with TV, radio and print experience. I currently freelance at the Big Ten Network as a production assistant and work as a part-time producer for WSCR 670 The Score in Chicago.

After a three-month internship with WGN, I was hired as a part-time producer to produce Chicago Blackhawks games. I also filled-in producing "Sports Central with David Kaplan" when needed.

I was recently hired by WSCR, an all sports station, to produce a variety of shows, including Chicago White Sox games.

My goal is to be on the air covering Chicago sports and/or college athletics in some capacity. I also take great pride in my writing skills, which is arguably my greatest strength.

Adam Hoge’s Specialties:

Play-by-play and analyst experience (radio): UW football, basketball and hockey.
