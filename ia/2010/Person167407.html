Rachel Selber’s Summary

Rachel Selber is a marketing and branding professional with proven ability to build and reposition brands for marketplace success. With a high level of competency as well professional experience, Ms. Selber approaches her work with high standards, a commitment to the consumer’s wants and needs, and an eye for growing the bottom line. Adept in grasping the big picture, she is innovative and laser focused on getting marketplace results. As well, Ms. Selber brings a passion for connection and approaches her work with the aim of building sustainable customer relationships by delivering brands they love. She is a natural leader who brings a contagious high energy and positive style that inspires teams to excel.

Ms. Selber has a rich and diverse professional background gained over 10 years of experience in branding and consumer marketing, including building new and developing brands in children’s products as well as on established global brands including Coke, Miller Lite, Cinnabon, and Finlandia Vodka. Currently she is a Marketing Consultant with the Gracia Group where she helps her clients employ marketing strategies to obtain sustainable top and bottom-line growth. Prior to that she was a Brand Business Manager at Brown-Forman where she led the brand-building efforts for Finlandia Vodka along the east coast. Ms. Selber oversaw strategy for the brand’s positioning, resource allocation, media, programming and financial management. As a result, she not only garnered excitement from colleagues and customers, grew gross profit per case by 16% (amid brand health concerns and intense competition); but also her division outperformed the aggregated US growth. Ms. Selber’s results, business acumen, and high energy, positive leadership style earned recognition that led to an expanded role and territory.
