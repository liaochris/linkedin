Nancy Hartman’s Summary

Nancy Hartman, FACHE, is a results-driven, senior-level healthcare executive with proven expertise in: strategic relationship management, new business development, consultative/solutions sales, planning, and finance. Her career experience spans healthcare services, disease management, consulting, banking and healthcare design. Throughout Nancy Hartman’s entire career, she has sold sophisticated, high revenue-generating services to strategic clients and prospects where the sales cycles have ranged from 4-6 months to over two years and the resulting fee income has ranged from the high five figures to mid-to-high seven figures. An innovative problem solver, she has demonstrated success in developing relationships and persuasively influencing senior decision makers to create and maintain long-term sales value, often by navigating resources from across and organization and leading multifunctional, product specialty teams. Nancy Hartman expertly manages a sales pipeline from lead acquisition to contract signing by:

• understanding the client’s key issues,
• tailoring the offerings provided to a client/prospect to align with the client’s unique needs and preferences while achieving her employer’s profit and price targets,
• advancing the client through the sales process by adding value throughout the process, and
• driving a complex selling process to closure.

(And now that I have appeased the search engines by writing about myself in the third person), I am highly motivated by connecting people with solutions and bringing value to the client. The kind of role at which I excel is courting and developing long-term relationships at senior levels in organizations, using consultative/solutions sales skills to solve strategic problems in ways that are a “win” for the client and a “win” for my employer. I firmly believe that a company is most successful when it helps its clients succeed.
