Dave Mayo’s Summary

President and co-founder of Everware-CBDI, a small IT services company focused on architected approaches to solving business and IT problems. Specialization in service oriented architecture (SOA), enterprise architecture (EA) and model driven architecture (MDA). We assist commercial and government organizations align IT with business objectives and help organizations achieve AGILITY.

Vice Chair of the Industry Advisory Council's Enterprise Architecture Shared Interest Group (IAC/EA-SIG).

Chief Architect of World Wide Consortium for the Grid (W2COG).

Co-Chair of Federal SOA Community of Practice (SOA-COP).

dmayo (at) everware-cbdi.com
