Peter Vo’s Summary

Peter Vo - Online Marketing Professional

I am currently a Junior Web Analyst at Fairfax Digital in Sydney, Australia. My role at Fairfax Digital involves the reporting and analysis of website data across online classifieds in the automotive, jobs, and real estate verticals.

Prior to joining Fairfax Digital, I worked in the travel industry and have a solid background in search engine optimisation (SEO) and pay per click management (PPC). I am also a Qualified Google Advertising Professional.

I have a keen interest in analysing website performance and online user behaviour, as well as digital marketing in general and can be found online at Twitter.com/petervo.
