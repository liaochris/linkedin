Simone Nelson’s Summary

A passionate & innovative marketing, public relations, development, & production executive with significant record of achievement developing & managing cross-functional & inter-organizational relationships with board-level & C-Suite senior leaders in the public & private entertainment, digital, social media, & technology sectors.

Expertise in digital media & event marketing strategy & public relations, branding & collateral strategy & development, & client/partner relationship-building & management. Record of success in creating, writing & editing marketing collateral & creating compelling sponsorship packages & pitch decks. Developed unique integrated online, live-event & traditional marketing, publicity & public relations programs & solutions to promote all forms of traditional & new media & support sales response to client RFPs.

18 years experience and record of success in developing, producing, marketing and managing entertainment endeavors including: film, video, theater, opera, music, large-scale, multi-media, and site specific performance events in national, international, commercial, academic and local community settings. Experience includes business development, strategy and new media product and project management in the digital media industry. Non-profit background offers creativity and resourcefulness in financial, development and organizational areas and systems. Successful record of working on high profile socio-politically charged projects and adapting to diverse communities and cultures both in the United States and abroad. Established entertainment industry contacts and relationships and successful record of working closely with acclaimed film and stage directors producers and actors, musicians, writers, designers, and business leaders.
