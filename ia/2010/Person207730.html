Karen Cooper’s Summary

I have an MBA in Finance and many years experience in a consumer lending environment. I have been with Transworld Systems for over 8 years and currently serve over 600 clients personally (Transworld Systems Inc. serves 70,000+ clients nationally). My goal is to continue to introduce more businesses to a more effective, less expensive way to resolve past-due receivables rather than chasing after delinquent accounts for months and years on end and then giving up high percentages to agencies and attorneys.

Karen Cooper’s Specialties:

We improve our clients' cash flow by shortening the time it takes them to get paid for their products and services by acting as an extension of their current A/R management procedures. We bridge the gap between internal A/R follow-up and external collections services by resolving past-due accounts amicably, effectively and at a cost that is lower than what our clients would spend to continue to pursue those accounts themselves. www.web.transworldsystems.com/kcooper
