Tyra Sartor Brown’s Summary

Tyra Sartor Brown has over 25 years of senior management experience in the financial and telecommunications industries with Fortune 500 companies that included Xerox, AT & T and Bank of America.

Prior to the inception of Coaching Associates, LLC, Tyra provided business coaching to entrepreneurs for a national non-profit organization in addition to managing Business Resource Group, LLC, a small business consulting firm.

Tyra is a graduate of Rutgers University and attended Seton Hall University School of Law. She is also a graduate of CoachU, a member of the International Coach Federation and a Board Member of the New Jersey Professional Coaches Association.
