Mark Savoia’s Summary

Still River Editions is the fine art printmaking department of Connecticut Photographics under the direction of master printmaker, Mark Savoia. Clients include photographers, painters, printmakers, museums, national parks, preservation organizations, and dedicated enthusiasts. Specializing in fine art digital printmaking ( giclées ), with a focus on individual service and attention to detail, we reproduce artwork and original photography.
