Andy Cawston’s Summary

Andy is a natural and popular leader who has established and is leading the New Zealand operations of a major worldwide not-for-profit, community-based organization, from the ground-up. Politically-astute and comfortable operating in political environments, Andy develops and maintains extensive networks and is constantly seeking to “add to the collection”. He is comfortable consulting at Chief Executive / Board level, has good written and verbal skills, and enjoys public speaking.

Andy is available for Management Consulting, Troubleshooting, Directorships, Trusteeships, Team Development, Motivational Speaking, Media Training, Course Development and Instruction, Project Management, Procurement (EOI / RFI / RFP), Risk Assurance, Bid Management, Strategic Planning, Sales Training, Coaching, Facilitation, Op/Ed Writing and Negotiations.

He is particularly interested in Public Safety issues, Youth-at-Risk, Leadership, Not-for-Profit and Charities, Local Government, Law & Order, Anti-Bullying, Public Policy, Volunteerism, Social Issues, History, Current Events, Politics, and Mental Health Issues.

He is fond of saying "anything I can do, I can teach you to do."

Andy is a Citizen of New Zealand, Canada and the United Kingdom, and is therefore able to live and travel freely and/or easily nearly anywhere in the free world as a matter of right.
