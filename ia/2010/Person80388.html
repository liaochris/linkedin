Cheri Tracy’s Summary

A self proclaimed ‘product junkie’, Cheri started out collecting and testing cosmetics at an early age. From her humble beginnings as the president of the Lip Smacker Club in the first grade to her present position, she has an insatiable appetite all things beauty related.

Cheri has been involved in everything from a stint at reflect.com to operating a successful salon and spa and serving as editor for one of the internets' first successful indie beauty e-commerce sites right into her present dual life as working woman, editor, serial entrepreneur and mom of two.

Working as an industry expert with savvy brands featured in international publications such as InStyle, New Woman UK and Lucky, over the past twenty years, she has gained an unprecedented amount of knowledge and insight into the world of beauty.

Haute Beauty was born out of the her creative spirit and desire to offer high quality, reasonably-priced indulgences using only premium ingredients, nourishing oils, and proven extracts.

The secret to Cheri's creativity is curiosity. Cheri is a strong advocate for the consumer and is always in search of the latest and greatest beauty products, innovative ingredients, eco-chic packaging and color trend. She’s dedicated to demystifying makeup and empowering women to explore their individual beauty.

Cheri Tracy’s Specialties:

Beauty ace with phenomenal experience in beauty manufacturing, social media, marketing and divine strategies for new product and brand introductions.
