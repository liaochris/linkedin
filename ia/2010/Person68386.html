Diana Matthews’s Summary

Executive Career Connections was founded by two highly talented degreed women HR professionals with proven track records of Recruitment and HR Consulting. Each of us brings value added services to the table such as Human Resources, Recruitment Management, Medical Device Manufacturing, Telecommunications, R&D, Energy, Engineering, Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, Power and Sales from various industries and from Fortune 100 company's. As a result of our combined professional talents, we can offer employers passive, exclusive and referred top performers in all types of industries.

Currently, we have a 100% Client retention rate and 99% Candidate retention for 2009!

Executive Career Connections goal is to create a partnership with your team and create long lasting relationships that will result in an extension arm of your recruiting staff and HR department. In addition, we have partnered with state-of-the-art industry leaders, which allow us to provide you with customized and additional superior services.

Executive Career Connections will learn your business, your culture, your competitors, and your job requirements. As a result, we can best represent you and ensure qualified "Top Performers." In return, reducing the loss of valuable time.

Executive Career Connections provides flexibility and the ability to offer customized solutions based on your unique recruitment goals and budget. We allow you to set your own fees because we understand that every organization is of a different size and budget!

Executive Career Connections team listens, provides honest and open communication, keeps commitments and follows through on our responsibilities to you, the client. Our team honors the personal attention that you deserve.

Executive Career Connections believes in education and keeping current with tomorrow's trends. As a result, we are members of various professional groups, attend numerous seminars and can be found in various directories.
