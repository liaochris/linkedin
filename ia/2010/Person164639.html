Scott Hummel’s Summary

I am a student at the Art Institute of Phoenix studying web design. I am nearing graduation. In order to supplement my resume, I am seeking part-time opportunities for web design work. I am a full-time student, so I cannot take on full-time assignments; I seek opportunities that require 10-15 hours of work per week. The pay rate I am seeking is $30 per hour, although that is negotiable.

I am knowledgeable and quite proficient in XHTML-compliant HTML/CSS. In addition to Dreamweaver, I am knowledgeable in Illustrator and Photoshop. I am somewhat new to Flash, but proficient in intermediate-level projects. I have a bachelor's degree in journalism, so I work well in editorial work for the web or graphic design. I also have experience in print-based work, including a background with newsletters, which I produced in Quark (although I now favor InDesign). I have also completed several video projects, some of which were converted to Flash video. I am used to working under tight deadlines, although my availability would have to take into account my schooling.

Currently, I have three websites active on the web, and am starting to build a fourth website this month. All of my websites include Paypal e-commerce. Two sites include PHP/MySQL databases, as will the new project. Some of the PHP projects include basic contact forms, email sign-ups, a database backend (still under development) and a silent auction page.

Scott Hummel’s Specialties:

HTML, CSS, PHP/MySQL, Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, editorial (writing and editing)
