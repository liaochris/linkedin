Susan Bagyura’s Summary

This has been an amazing year for me! I moved from Vienna, Austria to the Sultanate of Oman in the middle of August, 2008. It was my first trip to the Middle East and I came here to live for at least 5 years.

In December I received the first hard cover copy of my book The Visionary Leader: How To Inspire Success From The Top Down and then started planning my book launch in Muscat. I found 2 sponsors, Turtles Bookshop and the shopping complex called Jawharat Al Shati, and we held the book launch on the 24th of February.

Since April I have been teamed up with a Canadian leadership coaching group and working with major clients here in Oman. I am currently heading a succession planning project for one of the governmental ministries and heading another sales assessment project for the largest telecommunications company.

I held the PR/Marketing board position for the American Women's Group last year; successfully enlarged their discount partners' list and got several sponsors for several of our charity events.

Now I am the local Ambassador for an online networking group called InterNations. We are meeting for social get togethers on a monthly basis and have decided to increase it to twice a month. There are more and more people coming to each event.

My company, Blue Danube, is a coaching, consulting and training company that takes the best of products, skills and techniques to help clients achieve better results. Working closely with clients, essentially serving as an extension of in-house training staff, Blue Danube brings its focus and expertise to help clients set achievable goals, increase revenues with a better work/life balance.

My book is a 'how to guide' for leadership in today's economy. It is filled with exercises that really help a person define their leadership style(s). The first chapter can be downloaded from my website. I am currently turning the book into a self-study program that will have a workbook and up to 12 CDs.
