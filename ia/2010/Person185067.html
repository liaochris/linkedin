  • President

    Main Event Fitness and Nutrition

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    November 1993 — February 2009 (15 years 4 months)

    From the late 80's to today, MEF is widely considered one of the premier gyms in the country. Established in 1989 by professional wrestlers Lex Luger and Sting, Main Event Fitness quickly acquired a reputation among the serious workout people as the destination for Atlanta's club-savvy member. As the bodybuilding phenomenon waned in the late 90's, MEF evolved and became a niche fitness and performance mecca. The 30,000 square foot facility boasted three floors of multiple fitness disciplines from powerlifting to boxing to speed and agility. NFL superstars, rap artists, professional wrestlers, MMA fighters, professional boxers, and regular folks all made this place was one-of-a-kind!
