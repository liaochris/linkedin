Faye Regina Viray’s Summary

I completed my PhD in Resource Development-Environmental Toxicology at the Michigan State University under the guidance of Dr. Dan Bronstein as my academic advisor and Dr. Bob Hollingworth as my research advisor. My dissertation was focused on dietary risk assessment of pesticides in fruits and vegetables. I completed both my M.S. in Environmental Science-Environmental Chemistry and Toxicology and my B.S. in Agricultural Chemistry from the University of the Philippines Los Baños.
