Steven DiMartino’s Summary

After graduating from SUNY Oswego in 2002, I worked for 6 years at Weatherbank, INC where I was responsible for detailed and accurate forecasting for major energy, transportation, and media clients throughout the Northeastern United States. I was promoted to overnight manager from 2004 to 2006 where productivity increased along with an enjoyable work environment. In 2007 I left Weatherbank , INC to pursue other opportunities.

For a brief time I consulted the National Weather Station on new divisions for energy consultation in the Spring of 2007.

Since July 2007, I have started my own website business for meteorology called NY NJ PA Weather. Since the the first day, NY NJ PA Weather has expanded in traffic and scope on the internet. I currently also write for Examiner.com and for the Asbury Park Press online addition for inFreehold.

NY NJ PA Weather has expanded into a Premium Members section where in depth forecast discussions and coverage is provided to members. This paid section of the website has grown rapidly and is being used by individuals, businesses, and various organizations!

I am also available for other opportunities as well.

When I'm not following the latest storm, I love to go to the gym, enjoy a new restaurant, go to the movies, and enjoy a nice comfortable drive. I have a love for old sci fi monster movies like Godzilla. Of course, die hard sports fan that follows the New York Mets and the Kansas City Chiefs!
