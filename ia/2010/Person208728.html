Ted Struhl’s Summary

A proven business-builder in many vertical markets, Ted has emerged as one of the top-performing real estate professionals in the region. His broad skill set facilitates all types of real-estate transactions including investment property, country club & waterfront vacation homes, residences for retirees, corporate relocation, commercial real-estate, and developer sales & marketing.

With more than 25 Years experience as a high-end sales and business development rainmaker and team-builder, Ted has been instrumental in business-building and in structuring watershed deals to capitalize on market opportunities. He is a visionary professional whose customer-centric approach towards conducting business allows him to identify innovative approaches to the problem-solving of complex issues; transforming them into win-win propositions for all involved.

In the past, Ted worked in a strategic capacity to spearhead the launch of innovative product development and sales initiatives for a diverse set of vertical markets. His contributions led to substantial recurring revenue streams for organizations in industries as varied as law, finance, real-estate, small-business, construction, technology, content vendors, publishers, trade groups and not-for-profits.

Ted's extensive personal network coupled with his experience working with major financial institutions, corporations and trade groups suits him well to serve as an agent for commercial real-estate transactions.

Ted has an MBA in Finance and International Business from the NYU Stern School of Business and a BS in Computer Science from the State University of New York at Albany.
