Patricia Beasock’s Summary

First and foremost: What I do for a living feeds my soul, and I love it.

At 52 LTD., we connect members of the creative class and pay it forward for the greater good.
_________________________________________________________________________

I have been trained in and have mastered the fundamentals of recruiting and I am capable of executing modern sourcing techniques via traditional (job board) and non traditional (personal networking, employee referral bonuses, employee incentive programs) methods.

I am able to set priorities and parameters to implement search plans for recruiters to target active and passive candidates. I have partnered with group directors, and well as recruiters in other offices to expand job searches. I am very capable to act as a Project Manager to ensure that recruiting team members stay on plan and to task to achieve and exceed expectations. I am very comfortable with and believe it is crucial for success to have daily morning meetings to check in, to set daily goals, create accountability and manage priorities. I have assisted junior recruiters as well as teammates to help them plan their days, track their activity, market their jobs to candidates, market candidates to hiring managers, taking well qualified job orders, prepping candidates for interviews, following up with the candidate debrief, assisting with counter offers, resignations, candidate on-boarding and closing the job.

My upbeat personality and the ability to connect with each person as an individual is a huge asset in my success as a recruiter and mentor.

I am the candidate that has: an unusual blend of leadership, strong work ethic, team playing, as well as a definite sense of ownership, control of situations and a passion for recruiting.
