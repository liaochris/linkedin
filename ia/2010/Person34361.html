Christopher Dempsey’s Summary

I am the Chief Executive Officer of American Environmental and Agricultural, Inc. AEA is an import/export and trade consulting ﬁrm focused on the environmental and agricultural technology sectors in the United States and China. AEA was formed through a network of strategic alliances and partnerships between companies and individuals with China trade expertise and experience in environmentally sustainable products, services and technologies. In addition to product sourcing, AEA provides turn-key trade solutions for small-to-medium enterprises that includes: Representative Offices, Agents & Brokers Services, and Trade Consulting.

I am a graduate of the Dual Masters Degree in International Trade Policy and Business (MA/MBA) from the Monterey Institute of International Studies. In addition, I am involved in other entrepreneurial ventures, and am continuing my education in project management. I have previous experience in government, policy/advocacy, law and consulting. I am a functional speaker of Mandarin Chinese and continue to study the language, culture, history,development and economy of China.

I aspire to develop environmental projects and businesses both in the U.S. and abroad. I am interested in exploring all qualified opportunities, and am happy to provide expertise when I can.

I am lucky enough to live in Pacific Grove, California and enjoy the outdoors every day. I have the best dog in the world, love to cook and travel. Please feel free to contact me should you have interest in learning more about my endeavors, qualifications or interests.
