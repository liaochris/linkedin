Abby Locke, MBA’s Summary

Abby Locke is a career marketing architect, master resume writer and brand leadership coach who partners with professional/executive MBA women to help them discover brand distinction, overcome job search obstacles and achieve career mastery in highly competitive, crowded marketplaces.

THOUGHT LEADERSHIP & MEDIA HIGHLIGHTS

She has been featured and quoted in Essence Magazine, Black Enterprise, Washington Post, Washington Post Express, Wall Street Journal, KISS 105.9, ABCnews.com and CEO Update.

Today, she is profiled contributor on the Career Thought Leaders blog, Excelle.com, Careerealism.com, The Ladders.com, MORE.com and Localcareernetwork.com and recently served as the Seattle Resumes Examiner at Examiner.com and the Regional Career Expert for National Career Fairs.
