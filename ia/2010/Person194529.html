Steve O'Donnell’s Summary

Steve O’Donnell leverages his experience as a senior IT Operations Executive as an IT Industry Analyst. He has over 30 years IT experience working up to and including board level in large multinational organisations. He has international experience in Europe, North America and ROW

Steve is recognized as a specialist in IT Operations.

Steve has a clear understanding of application and infrastructure architectures in the Banking and Telecommunications industries as well as experience in Oil & Gas and Insurance.

Steve has a rare mix of business and IT experience, having taken a consulting business from start-up to winning a major industry award for rapid growth, followed by a trade sale as well as significant IT Operational experience in large corporations.
