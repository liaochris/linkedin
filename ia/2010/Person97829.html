Jeff Roberts’s Summary

The Roberts Group is a full-service, boutique style real estate and small business consulting and brokerage firm serving the Fairfield County, CT marketplace. The Roberts Group leverages high tech communications capabilities to deliver consistent, high touch service to our clientèle. The Roberts Group is a humanistic business treating the consumer as a human being and not as a commodity.

My experience in the real estate brokerage field, combined with experience in corporate real estate (employee relocation) and Internet based marketing and service delivery (Monster Moving.com) allow me a unique perspective on blending high tech service capabilities with high touch customer service values.

The goal of The Roberts Group is to serve the consumer by managing the information flow from the market to the consumer in the shortest time possible.

CONSULTATION. PROFESSIONALISM. RESULTS.

Jeff Roberts’s Specialties:

Residential brokerage services including first time home buyers, real estate investors, home marketing, rentals and consulting services.

Also specialize in the marketing of small, privately held businesses (under $5 million gross revenue) specifically in the retail, trades and professional services markets.
