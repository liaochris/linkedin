Scott Kimmel’s Summary

Experienced financial business partner with over fourteen years of corporate finance and management experience. Highly adept in partnering with executive management, financial and strategic planning, financial reporting and analysis and P&L management. International finance experience through expat assignment in an international subsidiary in the UK.
