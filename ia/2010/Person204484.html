Tim Tyrell-Smith’s Summary

I am a classically trained consumer products marketing executive. With 20 years of successful experience in food, beverage, computer accessories and automotive, I have a proven ability to drive revenue, improve EBITDA and build strong brands across a diverse set of industries.

I started my career with one of the great marketing boot camps in brand/product management - Carnation Company owned by Nestle USA. Since leaving Nestle, I have applied this experience and skill at a number of smaller, more nimble companies with a focus on brand revitalization and strong top line growth. I believe in a fix, build and drive strategy. This includes early attention on removing impediments to growth, the identification of new products, new categories, new channels and a focus on high impact growth opportunities. Focused on branded companies looking for accelerated business growth and classic brand turnaround opportunities with a leaning toward, fast and pragmatic decision-making.

In 2008, I created a career-focused website to give back to the community that helped me in a 2007 job search. "Tim’s Strategy" is about ideas. Ideas that can create change, encourage people and drive positive results in life. Written from the perspective of a job seeker and a hiring manager, the site delivers free advice, tools and templates to support anyone looking for a new job, to create a new career path, network into a better situation or find the right life/work balance.
