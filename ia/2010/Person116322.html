Asim Khan’s Summary

Asim Khan - CEO

Asim has a multi-cultural / multi-industry / multi-functional background, enabling balance between day to day and strategic management in many international environments, with proven ability to develop opportunities, establish, and maintain long term alliances at the highest level.

An entrepreneur and strategic thinker Asim sees the ‘big picture’, and is objective in approach, cutting through smokescreens and enjoys the challenge of a changing environment and technologies.

Before recognition as an international trade expert, educator, and author, (MBA/CSP) Asim successfully and progressively performed in Financial Management, General Management, Corporate Development and CEO positions.

For over 15 years Asim has advised a range of manufacturers and franchisors on their foreign expansion requirements via joint ventures, distribution and licensing partnerships by providing ongoing marketing and sales support and direction to company executives and government officials in the attainment of Increased Business Value and Sustainable Above-Average Returns.

He has developed a global support network of high level personal contacts in industry and government, enabling manufacturer and franchisors to achieve their business objectives in a cost-effective, efficient and proven methodology.

Asim Khan’s Specialties:

Asim deals with all companies desiring to establish market share in the United States, South America, the Middle East and North Africa whether establishing a physical presence or market presence, whether for strategic partnerships or for sales.
