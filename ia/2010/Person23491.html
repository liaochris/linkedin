Ace Luciano’s Summary

Ace Luciano is a consultant, author, seminar speaker, and consummate outdoorsman for over 30 years. He has travelled the globe in pursuit of both game and fish throughout the United States, Canada, Mexico, Africa, Europe, and Australia.
He is involved in numerous conservation organizations and youth projects. In 1995 Ace was the youngest director ever elected to the board of directors of any Safari Club International chapter. The Chapters that he has helped to found have earned both Rookie of the Year and Chapter of the Year honors.
In 2007, he was a finalist in the Field and Stream Total Outdoorsman Challenge, leading to his discovery by Bass Pro and subsequent invitation to join their pro staff. Most recently, Ace has been spending time pursuing his passion of introducing youths to the outdoors through the Edward Cawley Youth Mentoring Program that he helped to develop in memory of one of his outdoor mentors, the SCI Sensory Safari program, and performing marketing and consulting for numerous outdoor companies.

Ace's highly successful booking agency, World Game Hunts, Ltd., specializes in affordable, unbelievable hunting and fishing trips for all budgets. If you think you can’t afford to go on that trip of a lifetime, be sure to visit them at www.worldgamehunts.net.

A 20 year veteran of sales and marketing, Ace has won the pinnacle sales award at every company for which he was employed. As a mentor to new sales representatives, Ace shared the techniques that he had developed in order that others could also become successful.

Now, Ace is busy with the launch of his websites: www.BigGameAce.com, www.WIoutdoors.com and www.ILadventures.com, linking up sportsmen, women, and outdoor companies for mutual benefit.

Whether you are looking for a new source of customers, keeping the customers you have from the competition, or simply want to streamline your business and make more money, you NEED this “Ace” in your hand!
