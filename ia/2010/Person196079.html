Steve Stanganelli, CFP ®, CRPC ®’s Summary

o Five-Star Rated, board-certified financial professional with 18+ years of consultative project management experience in entrepreneurial banking and financial planning firms assisting clients with an instruction manual for building & preserving wealth. Special focus on counseling people to avoid the problems of "Sudden Wealth Syndrome" caused by business or life transitions (i.e. business sale, retirement, divorce, inheritance).

I work with families, retirees and busy professionals. I specialize in working with entrepreneurs in technology and medical industries.

I help clients manage their personal and business wealth for their life now and legacy later.

My investment management strategies provide a range of choices and are designed to be tax-wise and globally diverse.I offer tactical and strategic asset allocation using Exchange Traded Fund indexing at the portfolio management core.

Grounded in the golden rule I do not compromise my ethics or advice for a quick buck. I am fee-only in advice and services and take a consultative, educational approach to advising.

I think I'm a little different and my approach is not for everyone. I am not striving to do it all or deliver homeruns in the market every single day. Most of my clients are successful in their endeavors and want consistent relative total returns with less risk.

The measure of my success is my client's success in reaching their goals rather than beating the benchmark of the day every day. This is what I strive to deliver.

I often work with existing CPAs and attorneys to complete the team approach to managing your affairs.

I am selective in who I take on as a client to ensure a high level of service is maintained for all so that mutual expectations can be met.

That being said, with the intellectual capital of my team, firm, and network of experts there is little I can't do to help my clients envision and accomplish the goals for their organization, life, family and legacy.
