Roger Pavane’s Summary

I have been in the digital media and mobile technology space for quite some time. I started early on as an electronics engineer and realized I was a much better marketer. With a passion for success; my approach has always been to aggressively drive revenue and build teams that garner major growth resulting in the biggest impact possible.

Offering over 18 years management experience devoted to business development and senior-level sales and marketing for start-up, early-stage, lean and rapid growth online and mobile advanced technology, media and service companies.

Throughout my career I have developed a strong combination of sales, P&L, management and leadership skills with an acumen for successfully identifying opportunities; building organizations and delivering strong revenues and profits despite intense market conditions. As a thought-leader with a demonstrated ability to build, train, motivate and lead high performance teams along with the capacity to produce results; think creatively, sell strategically, and communicate efficiently internally and externally, I have a number of pursuits but my # 1 passion is digital media and the new technology space.

Experienced in a multitude of technology disciplines and in working with top companies including Paypal, AT&T Wireless, T-Mobile, Liberty Media, Viacom Nickelodeon, Blip.TV, Digital Chocolate, Sony Music, Nintendo, Google, and many others with a special focus on cross-platform services including mobile, online, social media, gaming, virtual & digital goods, location based services (LBS), mobile media and advertising, e-commerce, m-commerce, Software as a Service (SaaS), Web 2.0, consumer/enterprise software platforms and applications.
