Pete King’s Summary

Competing for waning attention spans in a crowded and noisy marketplace is a losing battle. Yet, 9 out of 10 marketers still are vying for their customers’ attention with their email blasts, radio or television commercials and telemarketing, to name a few.

If you’re doing this, then your marketing is out-of-whack. And it’s not your fault. Practically overnight, a radical shift in the marketing landscape has taken place. Consumers gained control of the conversation and shut you out.

Marketers need to be where the attention is, right? And so, because consuming behavior has adapted, so must we. Instead of blasting people with one-way interruption media (aka advertising), we need to woo prospects and build meaningful (and profitable) relationships when our customers and prospects are actively pursuing us.

If you’re not taking major initiative to enhance your online presence, then by the time you wake up to it, you’ll be inconsequential. The time for action is NOW.

Today’s marketer needs to adapt from “getting in their face” to “getting found.” Woovertising is the proven, results-oriented process that allows you to get noticed by your prospects and build rapport for massive growth (all the while lowering current advertisement expenses!). We call this process Relationship-First Marketing™ (RFM).

The RFM methodology focuses on 5 general marketing channels to get massive attention, build relationships and grow business.

1) Social marketing (web 2.0, viral, strategic alliances, joint ventures, affiliates)
2) Paid advertising (PPC, banner ads, ezine ads)
3) Search engine optimization (SEO)
4) Syndication
5) Offline and other marketing channels
