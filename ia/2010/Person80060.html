Harold Olaf Cecil’s Summary

After years of helping clients grow and develop their businesses, I'm putting my money where my mouth is with my own venture, Giant Loop LLC. By applying to Giant Loop the brand-building, high bang-for-the-buck marketing strategies and tactics I recommend to my clients, we've been able to quickly ramp up our startup and create a worldwide presence in less than a year. My life-long fascination with motorcycles is finally paying off, as is experinece in the outdoor recreation industry and in-the-trenches marketing for a staggering array of business (most of them fewer than 50 employees).
