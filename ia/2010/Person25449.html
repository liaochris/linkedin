Bob Hartman’s Summary

At this point I have over 30 years of experience developing software. I am a big believer in using a logic-based approach to software development and quality that can be traced back to my college years when I obtained both Bachelors and Masters degrees in Computer Science from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Since that time I have broadened my industry knowledge by serving in almost every role in the software industry including developer, tester, documentation writer, trainer, product manager, project manager, business analyst, senior software engineer, development manager and executive. For 8 years I ran my own consulting firm and I have over 15 years of corporate experience at the Vice President level.

Over the past 10 years I have grown from being an early adopter of agile to my current status as a Certified Scrum Coach (CSC) who has done significant training, coaching and mentoring in all areas of agile development. I recently founded Agile For All to continue to promote the use of agile development methods. I teach a variety of courses including Lean-Agile Testing, Agile for Teams, Agile Business Analysis, Agile Project Management, Agile for Enterprises and Lean Software Development. In the past few years I have been a speaker at numerous conferences, seminars and workshops where my engaging style, holistic view of development, and personal anecdotes are always well received by attendees. Anyone that is using agile, considering making a switch to agile, or just wants to learn more about agile, is encouraged to contact me via my blog at www.agilebob.com so we can discuss your needs in more detail.

Bob Hartman’s Specialties:

Team and enterprise transitions to agile software development processes.
Role specific agile training (tester, developer, product manager, project manager, business analyst, etc.) as well as team-based agile training.
Agile readiness as well as level of agility assessments.
Coaching individuals and teams to become self-sufficient with agile as quickly as possible.
