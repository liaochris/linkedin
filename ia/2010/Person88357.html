James Morosky’s Summary

I am currently a Doctor of Chiropractic student at National University of Health Sciences. I have a strong science background, and I received my B.S. in Biology at Pennsylvania State University.

I would like to focus on delivering functional medicine to my patients to improve their daily living, in addition to eliminating their pain and symptoms. This will be accomplished by using multiple therapies, such as chiropractic manipulation, neuromuscular re-education and rehabilitation, and nutrition. I intend to use all the therapies and resources at my disposal to benefit the patient beyond their own expectations.

Also, I would like to focus on patient advocacy through education. It is my belief that if the patient can understand what’s happening in relation to their health, then the patient and doctor can work together to improve the health of the patient. I intend to help to introduce chiropractic and alternative and complementary care to more people, and assist in bridging the gap between chiropractic, alternative, and allopathic communities in order to continue to strive for greater public health.
