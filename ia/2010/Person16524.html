Arnie Gullov-Singh’s Summary

Since joining Fox in 2006, Arnie has led the strategy, development and rollout of the company's advertising technology initiatives, including HyperTargeting - now the leading vehicle for connecting marketers to audiences in social media, MyAds - Fox's self service advertising solution for small businesses and Audience Insights - the new marketing analytics platform to help marketers and publishers understand who their users and customers are.

Building on the success of these programs he currently oversees product, technology and operations for Fox Audience Network, a top 5 advertising network in the US, reaching 80% of the population every month.

Before joining Fox, Arnie ran product management for Yahoo's contextual ad program and prior to Yahoo, he spent eight years in product management at Ticketmaster and startups in Los Angeles and Europe. He began his career managing national account sales at Universal Music in the UK.

He has a Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from the University of Bristol in the UK and an MBA from the University of Southern California.
