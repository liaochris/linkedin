Ellie Johnson’s Summary

Seasoned interactive media professional. Passionate about digital media; helping businesses identify their target audience on the web and quantify a return on their marketing investment.

An enthusiastic project manager, I enjoy collaborating with clients and design teams to engineer creative assets that become tools to solve business challenges and help brands expand, listen, engage, promote and measure positive change online.

Ellie Johnson’s Specialties:

Interactive advertising. Online marketing. Online community development. Performance advertising. Client satisfaction.

Business development; strategic introductions, contract negotiation, account management and identifying creative, new means to drive revenue with interactive opportunities.
