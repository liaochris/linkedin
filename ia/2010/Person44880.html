Crystal Thies’s Summary

Are you a small business owner, solopreneur or sales professional? Do you keep hearing about all of the great things with social media, but aren’t sure exactly where to start? Or, have you dabbled a bit, gotten no results and dropped it?

Let me tell you a secret that few in the social media industry are sharing. The web 2.0 conversation may be fun and it may be great at building awareness, but it isn’t necessarily effective at directly growing business. Not unless you know how to use it correctly.

As a social media strategist, I help my clients focus their social media efforts on those strategies that truly help them grow their business. The goal is to generate and identify viable prospects, take them out of the virtual world, bring them into your traditional sales system and grow your revenues.

I help you develop the strategies, and then either help train you or help put your team together to properly execute so that you get the results you want.

Whether you’re a small business wanting a complete social media plan or a professional wanting to get the most from LinkedIn, I’m the “go to” person.


If you’re a marketing or communications small business serving other small businesses and you’re interested in being a team member, contact me to join our referral network!


With Crystal Clear Buzz, your message comes shining through!
