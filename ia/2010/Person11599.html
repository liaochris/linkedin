Andrew Burridge’s Summary

I am a chartered architect with over thirty years involvement with architecture. With my own practices since 1995, my work has been described as having a "...a refined simplicity of design coupled with a restrained palette of materials..."
Experienced in a wide range of building types particularly domestic projects & interior design for individual clients, new office building & fit-out projects.
Member of Royal Institute of British Architects RIBA
Member of Royal Incorporation of Architects in Scotland RIAS
Registered Architect - ARB
Professional Practice Part III examiner for APEAS
Architects' Professional Examination Authority in Scotland
Professional Practice Part II examiner University of Edinburgh
Deputy Chairman of the Friends of the Royal Scottish Academy

Andrew Burridge’s Specialties:

Careful detailing, materials & innovation, sustainable design, timber buildings.
