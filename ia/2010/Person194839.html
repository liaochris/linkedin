Steve Brooks’s Summary

As Mission Control Director for Tumbleweed Southwest Grill I am the Marketing Director for the company. I still head up Information Technology. These two areas work hand and hand to build guest counts, support our guest, the employees and our Brand. I am on the Menu Team, Creative Menu Team, and head up the Marketing Team and Beverage Team. I was in Restaurant Management for 14 years. Was a Manager, General Manager for 2 years and Area Director for 12 years. Opened 17 stores as an Area Director.
Currently I am on the Hospitality Technology Editorial Advisory Board.

Steve Brooks’s Specialties:

Marketing, I.T. innovation, Wireless POS, Hospitality Operations, exception based Business Intelligence, Business Analysis and Consulting, Public Speaking, Negotiation, Menu Creation, Menu Engineering, Menu Funding, and Beverage Creation.
