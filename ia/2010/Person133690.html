Melodie Tao’s Summary

Melodie Tao is a Social Media Consultant and Online Marketing Educator. She educates and inspires businesses to optimize their online presence through new technology and social media. Melodie empowers you to strengthen relationships and build engaged communities of loyal customers and brand evangelists. She works with you to establish a strong foundation through a strategic marketing plan. Melodie provides ongoing campaign execution and consulting to ensure long term success.

Melodie teaches courses at Platt College and WeekendU that educate students on new technology and social media skills for their professional and personal development.

Melodie has successfully launched interactive campaigns, and promotions for national brands, including Ralphs/Kroger, Westfield Malls, Colgate Palmolive, Liberty Tax Service, AMC Theaters, The San Diego Padres and Red Bull.
