Peter Albers’s Summary

Startup and Management of Consulting Businesses in Europe, Latin America and the US.
Currently CEO of Politec USA Corp. the North American arm of Brazil's leading Information Technology Service Provider and a Mitsubishi Corporation affiliate company. Prior to that Vice President responsible for the SAP Service Line in the East BU of Capgemini. Before that President of Emeritis North America and Managing Director for German Plaut Consulting Group in Brazil. Also worked as a consultant for Plaut in Germany. Projects delivered in companies such as DaimlerChrysler, Bayer, ZF, Karmann, Paramount Pictures, Thyssen Krupp. Strong Startup and Turnaround background as well SAP Implementation, Outsourcing and Operations Management experience.

Languages: English, German, Portuguese, Spanish

Peter Albers’s Specialties:
