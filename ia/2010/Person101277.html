Joe Wolf, CPA’s Summary

Joe Wolf is a partner and owner of Inemer & Wolf LLP, a leading CPA firm serving clients in New York City. Joe has worked with hundreds of businesses across a variety of industries including medical, dental, healthcare, media, entertainment, real estate, manufacturing, legal, hi-tech and many other professional service firms. He has the ability to understand the unique challenges facing small business owners and the experience to provide the right solutions.

Businesses and individuals have hired Inemer & Wolf when they need help with:


• Tax planning and preparation
• Business accounting
• Tax compliance
• Outsourced accounting
• Outsource controllership and CFO services
• International tax
• Financial management and consulting
• QuickBooks set up and maintenance
• Starting a business
• Incorporations and entity selection
• IRS problem resolution
• Succession planning
• Business valuations
• Estate and trust planning
• Personal financial statements

Joe Wolf graduated from Baruch College with a BBA in degree in accounting in 1983. He spent 6 years as controller and principal of 2 local wholesale-manufacturing companies and 5 years working in small and mid-sized public accounting firms.

He was a partner in the firm of Gilbert, Cohen & Wolf, LLP until 1996 when he became a sole practitioner, and in 2004, after a 4 year association, he joined with Ira to form Inemer & Wolf, LLP. Joe's 17 years of broad public accounting experience combined with his background in business as controller and principal are extremely valuable in addressing clients' needs and providing solutions to the challenges they face.
