Tim Heath’s Summary

Open Source – ya, that the direction things are moving. Be Open, Be Free.

I have worked on the commercial side for more than a decade with domestic and international companies through a combination of brick and mortar & online. All of that experience was around technology and primarily focused on the telecom space in one fashion or another.

Today, the real movement in the industry is towards an open source model, where customers take greater advantage of:
• Easy access to new markets
• Support through open source communities
• A low pressure environment to learn about & test the technology
• Reduced costs.

Open Kernel Labs has a dual license model which makes it easy for people and companies to become familiar with, evaluate and use our embedded system virtualization applications and confirm that it is something they in fact want to use in a commercial application.

I leverage my experience of working within the telecom industry to foster trusted relationships with the goal of building a thriving Open Source Community. Here like minded researchers, developers, academics, entrepreneurs and others contribute and share alike.
