Jay R. Kaplan’s Summary

Jay Kaplan is a highly successful Search Engine Marketer and Interactive Marketing Professional with over eleven years of experience in paid search management, online and offline lead generation, email marketing, direct marketing, customer relationship marketing, market analysis, as well as marketing research and development. Jay has managed paid search campaigns across every major engine with multi-million dollar budgets. Jay’s focus is on companies that depend heavily on Internet and e-Commerce for their marketing, lead generation, and sales channels. His industry expertise includes Health & Medical Devices, Education, Government, Industrial Supplies and Equipment, Publishing, Retail, Stock Photography, B2B, and B2C.

Jay’s mission is to help his clients achieve results by continually learning and applying new search marketing techniques, finding unique solutions to search engine related problems, keeping on track with key performance goals, and by staying on top of the latest industry trends to continuously build upon his professional business and marketing skills. Through his creative and marketing-oriented thinking, Jay has excelled at building high-performance paid search programs and orchestrating implementations that have enabled marketing campaigns to exceed their growth objectives in the face of rapidly changing search engines.

Jay received his MBA in Marketing from the Zarb School of Business at Hofstra University. He also holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and Finance from the University at Albany, State University of New York. Jay’s passion for search engine marketing is an asset to organizations who want to push the internet’s marketing technology boundaries or breathe new life into existing products in a way that re-energizes the overall brand. Jay thrives in an environment that encourages others to get excited about change and inspires them to generate new ideas.
