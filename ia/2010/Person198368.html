Suzanne Jarvis’s Summary

MY STORY: West Meets East
___________________________________________________

In 1980 I started losing the function of the right side of my body. I couldn't lift my foot, hold onto a pencil and had no feeling in my face. Hospitalized for a week of tests revealed no physical cause. Medicine couldn't help me. I had to find a solution.

So, I sought Eastern approaches to wellness. I changed my diet, meditated, exercised and worked with personal transformation techniques. I got better.

This lead to continued study with 4 Asian Mentors/Global Experts in their disciplines. I gained so much from my trainings I changed my profession to energy medicine and transformational sciences. It's my passion to give back to others...

The Journey? Absolutely fascinating! This pioneering and universal approach to life offers practical techniques that allow you to directly access your physical, emotional, mental and spiritual power and faculties so you can take your health, personal development and performance to the new levels.
