Jim Texter’s Summary

Leader of the engineering, program management and sales divisions of an automotive manufacturer of highly-engineered products for the past eleven years.

Built the R&D division at American Boa Inc. (ABI). Led ABI to best-in-class engineering know-how, and product development time-to-market.

From 1998 through 2005 developed new and innovative products that helped increase sales from $22 million to $34 million

Since 2005, led effort to increase sales from $34 million to $48 million becoming the number-one flex supplier in the automotive industry.

Reduced lead-time from concept to serial production from two years to ten months

Led global sales and engineering synthesis/coordination meeting with German sister company that reduced duplicate effort and eliminated repeated mistakes
