Cassy Kramer’s Summary

Cassy joined Pac/West in 2007 after several years working in educational marketing as a writer and project coordinator. At Pac/West, Cassy manages the company brand, develops proposals for new business and works on marketing campaigns for a variety of clients including health care, government and public/private partnerships.

Prior to her current position, Cassy worked on award-winning admissions campaigns for major colleges, universities, and independent schools. Additionally, she offers more than seven years administrative, fundraising, and events planning experience in the arts and hospitality fields. She has successfully collaborated with a wide range of individuals and organizations to advance project initiatives-from creative and research partners to university presidents, politicians, and CEOs.

Cassy received a bachelor of arts in mass communications with a minor in art history from Montana State University-Billings, and is a community mediator.
