Celina Reising’s Summary

I was born in Lorain Ohio, approx 40 miles West of Cleveland. I moved around Lorain County then landed in Strongsville when I was 22. I worked retail for 8 years and was a retail manager for 6 1/2 of those 8 years.

I went to film school for editing and sound and then discovered Line Producing, I love it and am good at it. Since graduating I have worked freelance Line Producing, Production Managing, Art Dept Coordinating, and Production Sound Mixing. As well as working my way up at The Los Angeles Film School. I started off as a part-time temporary assistant to the Head of Production. He liked my work and decided to keep me on and give me more duties. I began Production Managing and Line Producing short films and TV shows we would put together for the students to learn film production. Now I am an Industry Specialist teaching Production Managing and guiding the students on making their thesis films.

The Complete Film Production Handbook 4th edition by Eve Light Honthaner was released on March 29th, she interviewed me for the book. You can see what I have to say about low budget indy Line Producing on page 418 & 420.

Now I am looking for more freelance Line Producing jobs including features and music videos and am also interested in a full-time position with a production company as a coordinator eventually working my way up to Line Producer. I'm also looking for a full-time job.
