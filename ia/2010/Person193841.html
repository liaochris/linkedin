Stephane Blanc’s Summary

I-play / Oberon Media is one of the leading casual game publishers (mobile/online/TV) and I wanted to be part of its promising future. I started as Business Development & OEM Director Europe in March 2007, then, thanks to my experience of Asian markets, I kicked off I-play distribution in China in October 2008. In 2009, I set up some important partnership in South East Asia in order to extend I-play footprint in growing territories. 2010 is the year of a strong focus on smartphones and I lead the Android initiative within the company.

Attracted by the Mobile Entertainment Industry, I joined In-Fusio in 2004 to develop the marketing proposition related to mobile gaming services. I then took the Global responsibility of the Business Development, meaning the relationship with handset manufacturers (Nokia, Samsung, Sony Ericsson, etc.) and strategic partners (Orange Group, Vodafone Group, etc.).

Before joining In-Fusio, I worked for Vodafone Group, based in the UK for four years, going from Senior Product Marketing Manager within the Vizzavi / Vodafone live! Division, to Partner Network Manager in the Corporate Division.

Prior to this, I worked at SFR (French subsidiary of Vodafone) for two years. My positions ranged from Mobile Internet Product Manager to Group Product Manager within the Strategy and Marketing division. I was responsible in particular for launching BtoC mobile data services (Unified messaging, WAP solutions, etc.).

I first became product manager at Cofinoga Group (Galeries Lafayette Group), a leading credit and loan company in France.

I began my career setting up an internet service provider with some friends from university in 1995.
