Sue Armstrong Ryan’s Summary

What an exciting time this is for me! After working in sales in the technology industry for more than 25 years, I met the man of my dreams, married, and took several wonderful years sabbatical from work. What a great time of discovery of other interests and learning about balance in my life.

As much as I have enjoyed our sabbatical, which has included travel, spending time with our grandchildren (isn't it wonderful that every grand child is the most beautiful and the smartest!) and volunteerism, I have really missed some of the passions I had in work. The process of discovery of what to do next has been an awesome journey. How do you leverage your passions for work with a wonderful married life? Well, I did a lot of searching, got out of my own way, and here's what happened - I met Mikki Williams.

Working with Mikki, as her Director of Opportunities, I can leverage so many of my professional passions while helping people have their lives transformed by her dynamic presentations and amazingly perceptive coaching abilities.

Throughout my career, I have always felt my greatest satisfaction and achievements were when I was able to effectively learn what would make people successful and align solutions to best achieve success. I have always truly cared about success for everyone at an individual level, and while my role was titled sales, I never sold anyone anything; I prepared people to buy. I'm really excited to have that opportunity again through working with Mikki. Having watched her, it's easy to educate people on "Why Mikki?".

As Mikki's Director of Opportunities, my main goal is to exponentially introduce Mikki to people so they can have the opportunity to be touched by her. I am going to do this by leveraging the ability of social media to communicate, generate awareness, and help educate, so i'm looking to all of my connections to check Mikki out at: www.mikkiwilliams.com.
