Ryan Trotman’s Summary

New media pioneer seeking to develop the first successful generation of interactive television in Canada through industry-leading brands and broadcasters.

Over twelve years in online development experience at MuchMusic, MTV, AOL Time Warner, Microsoft Network and the Toronto Star.

Successfully promoted through the production chain from editor, to editorial manager, business development, strategy manager and director.

Business experience in management of staff, managing P&L budget, project management, business development, data analysis, marketing planning and PR communications.

Trained in journalism with a background in copyediting, writing for the Internet, Canadian Press Style and a strong understanding of best practices employed by major online portals.

Strong technical understanding of Web publishing languages and tools including HTML, XML/RSS, JavaScript and advanced digital photo editing.

Ryan Trotman’s Specialties:

Forward thinking strategic leader with a focus on optimizing digital media consumption for the user to make it informative, entertaining and fast.

Experience in managing editorial and business development teams. Able to balanace sales goals online against presenting leading content.
