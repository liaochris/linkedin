Jeff LaFlamme’s Summary

With over 20 years of experience in the animation industry, Jeff LaFlamme is an experienced professional. He created his first animation at age 10 (paint on film) & won his first animation award in 1977 (stop-motion clay).

After working in Los Angeles for years on several features (under Chuck Jones, Joe Barbara & Richard Williams), numerous tv series (Simpsons, Garfield, Bobby's World,) with major studios (Warner Bros., Film Roman, Film Fair,), Jeff relocated to Santa Fe, NM with his wife Suzanne. They have a daughter, Annelise.

He regularly works in Autodesk Maya & Adobe Flash, Photoshop & Illustrator.

Jeff LaFlamme’s Specialties:

Direction, Animation, Design, Illustration & Storyboards.
