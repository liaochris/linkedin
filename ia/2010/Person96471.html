Jerry Hilburn

CEO -Catfish Software Inc. (CSI)

CSI is a Social Media E-commerce Marketing company that focuses on the use of social marketing to drive sales and conversions for our customers products and services.

Located in San Diego California, CSI has operated for 15 years, currently employing 10 full time staff members servicing clients throughout the United States and abroad.

Our focus is to provide the best mix of low cost social media publishing services, analytic and tracking software, e-commerce transactional technology, and 15 years of online sales experience helping our clients meet their sales objectives.

CSI has helped its clients reach their goals of integrating the best social media marketing techniques with the latest tools in e-commerce merchandising, affiliate programs, and card service / cart service support.
