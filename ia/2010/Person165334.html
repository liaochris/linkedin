EJ Posselius’s Summary

Feel free to email me with any questions at posselius [a] gmail.com

I am an Ethnomusicologist focusing on the impact of Music Technology on our contemporary society. I use my skills to help develop media centered around music studies as well as the arts. By utilizing a history of experience with high end audio equipment, digital / analog signal paths, and experiences from a number of other venues, I intend to facilitate a community-enterprise highlighting the importance of audio/media education and methodology for the modern musician/artist.
