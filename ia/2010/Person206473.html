Tom Sheives’s Summary

- Trained 175 executives in Project Management of the Panama Canal Canal Authority in 2007 as a part time faculty member of the University of Texas at Arlington
- Has Led the effort in establishing a project office and has developed and installed project management processes and methodologies including guidelines, templates, checklists, and project management enterprise tools.
- Current goals are to apply the many years of systems and software integration experience to help students, individuals, and companies manage and execute their projects by coaching and training individuals and teams to become more effective project teams and to help them learn new processes and support tools.
- Has consistently developed processes and developed and trained teams that have generated high quality, useful, custom software and hardware-based systems

Tom Sheives’s Specialties:

-Keynote speaker, educator, coach, consultant, business manager, project manager, and management consultant.
-Broad range of product development and systems engineering disciplines including software, electronics, and mechanical systems.
- Proven success in building highly productive teams in both manufacturing and systems integration of automation systems, information technology, wireless telecommunications, oil exploration, and defense guidance systems.
