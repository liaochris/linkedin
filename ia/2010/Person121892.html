Laura Lee Carter, M.A., MLS’s Summary

After 25 years as an academic librarian and personal coach, Laura Lee transitioned to freelance writing in 2006. Since then she has been published in regional and national magazines, worked as a professional copy editor, and joined Northern Colorado Writers and the Colorado Authors' League.

But she still felt frustrated with the lack of useful information and support for those stuck in midlife hell, so she crowned herself the "Midlife Crisis Queen" in 2008, launching her popular blog: www.MidlifeCrisisQueen.com. Through her blog she has now reached thousands of midlife sojourners worldwide, with 100,000 views so far!

She published her first book: Midlife Magic: Becoming the Person You Are Inside, at the end of 2008. And now also has a companion workbook available through Scribd.com

But writing was not enough! Laura Lee now advocates for the needs of those in midlife through customized life change coaching. She specializes in offering extra support to those struggling to become their best self, and also provides professional presentations on healthy aging and change.

She believes everyone has the power to transform their life and become the person they are inside! Are you ready for that challenge?
