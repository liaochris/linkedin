Tanya Benzaquen’s Summary

Extremely creative and resourceful. Fashion writer/founder of established and exciting fashion blog RedPoppy Fashion. Award-winning Hat Designer and owner of Hats by Tanya, designing and producing seasonal hand-made line of felt and straw hats and couture headpieces. Working wardrobe stylist backstage at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in New York City.
