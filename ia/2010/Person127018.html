Lloyd Melnick’s Summary

I am currently the General Manager for International Operations at Playdom, spearheading our expansion into Europe. I have been involved with development, publishing and licensing of social media (Facebook, MySpace, etc) and casual games for over 16 years.

Previously, I initiated and brokered Merscom's sale to Playdom. As a co-founder of Merscom, I helped orchestrated our pivot to social gaming. I also initiated, negotiated and oversaw Merscom's Pet Resort project, the first social game that truly integrated a major advertiser.

As Chief Customer Officer of Merscom, I built Merscom's licensing and co-production relationships with numerous media companies, including AETN Lifetime, Starz Entertainment/Overture Films, Lionsgate, CBS, CAA, Paramount, Showtime, National Geographic and Granada Ventures.
