Patrick Leahy’s Summary

I am an award winning Senior Planner/Designer, Project Manager and Architect with a specialty in Healthcare facilities. As a NCI Certified Charrette Planner, I have led Charrettes with a large diverse team of clients, user groups, and community stakeholders. As an experienced leader of projects of all sizes, complexities and number of phases, I have led Master Planning, Programming, Schematic Design, Design Development, Bidding, Construction Administration and Client Program Management. As a LEED Accredited Professional, I have a commitment to Sustainable Design. My career in healthcare design has produced planning and design that successfully supports and enhances the optimal delivery model developed with the client, as well as utilizing the latest research in Evidence Based Design. My focus on Healthcare affords me more research and development in this critical and ever changing field.
