Marcie Maxwell’s Summary

I'm proud of my commitment to the quality of life in our Puget Sound community that has been my home for a lifetime. That quality of life can only be maintained and improved with efforts invested in public schools, early education, higher education, economic development, transportation, good jobs, and housing choices. A healthy, safe, and beautiful environment to live, work, learn, and play in is important to me and to you. I have always strived to share opportunities with citizens of diverse ethnic and economic backgrounds, and to respect and care for people of all ages and abilities. You should also know that you have my commitment to accountability and ensuring that taxpayer dollars are spent wisely.
