Diane Kamer’s Summary

I have 30 years' experience in advertising copywriting, with special emphasis on direct response, e-commerce, and SEO copywriting. I've written everything from print ad campaigns to direct-mail campaigns to collateral to catalogs to e-commerce websites. Some broadcast, too, but I prefer print and pixels!

Diane Kamer’s Specialties:

Strategic thinking; creative concepting; in-depth research (re product features and benefits, marketing strategy, and brand/product positioning); copy "wordsmithing"; SEO copywriting; proofreading and copyediting; Quark, InDesign, Microsoft Office; some basic HTML.
