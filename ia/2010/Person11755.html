Andrew Gardner’s Summary

For the past 11 years I have worked as a visual design and UI specialist and in recent years as a consultant spearheading UI solutions across the main portal technologies. My focus has been on taking the UI from the initial concept phase, through branding and user analysis, to UI coding and development.

A key aspect of this has been around SAP specific technologies. I have introduced a Usability lead approach to many SAP portal projects and have developed strategies for overcoming the natural limitations of the UI and for improving overall user acceptance.

I have merged a strong background in visual design with the knowledge needed to facilitate technical delivery of the solution. This ensures an innovative approach which is possible within the confines of the chosen technology and a solution that meets accessibility requirements.
