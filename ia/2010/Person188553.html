Sheldon Nix’s Summary

Dr. Sheldon Nix is a native of Wilmington, Delaware. He earned a B.A. from Princeton University, a Masters of Social Work from Hunter College School of Social Work in New York City, and earned the Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology from Michigan State University. He has also received extensive training in personal and executive coaching from Coach University and the Graduate School of Coaching (now CoachVille).

Dr. Nix is the President of Successful Practice Institute (www.successfulpractice.com) which offers training and life & business coaching to individuals and organizations.

He and his SPI team of experts have created several innovative web-based training/coaching sites to provide just-in-time eLearning and coaching in several areas:
- Training for super small businesses: http://www.small-business-gameplan.com
- Training for Women about Men: http://www.whatmakesmentick.com
- Career Training: http://www.change-your-life-forever.com
- Training for Relationships: http://www.change-your-life-forever.com

He has coached many individuals, especially professionals trying to build a practice, couples trying to build a marriage, and leaders trying to build a team.

Dr. Nix has also assisted many organizations to improve their long-term capabilities to meet the needs of the communities/markets they serve. This has led to a particular focus on: 1) identifying the needs of their target communities; 2) identifying the best strategies for meeting those needs; and 3) maximizing their human assets.

Dr. Nix for 14 years was a (tenured) professor of counseling at Eastern University in St. Davids, PA, and during that time chaired the Department of Counseling and served as a college therapist.

Dr. Nix is the author of two published books and the Senior Pastor of Life Worth Living Fellowship (www.lwlf.org) in Wilmington, Delaware, a highly innovative contemporary ministry.

Small business coaching and training, life coaching, career coaching, fundraising, psychology-related training and counseling.
