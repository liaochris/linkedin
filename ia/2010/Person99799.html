John Nemo’s Summary

I have 15 years experience as a print, broadcast and online journalist for numerous national media outlets, along with extensive experience on the other side of the fence working in Public Relations. I've also authored four books.

During its first 90 days, my most recent PR campaign (for the consumer financial education Web site http://www.askdoctordebt.com) led to more than 125 interviews across the United States, reaching an estimated 25 million consumers and netting an estimated $1 million in free advertising/publicity value. I also was able to secure a weekly, ongoing segment for "Ask Doctor Debt" on top-rated Fox News Channel that ran for more than four months straight.

My professional goal is simple - to use my creativity, passion and talent to help the people I'm working for tell their story in a unique way that gets them noticed on a large, national stage.

John Nemo’s Specialties:

* Public Relations planning, strategy and execution for both traditional and new media campaigns.

* Video production/editing using Final Cut Express, iMovie, iDVD, etc.

* Social media campaigns, including Twitter, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Blogs, Podcasts, etc.

* Audio production/editing using Flash players, Dalet, Cool Edit Pro, GarageBand and other software.

* More than a decade of writing, reporting and editing experience for regional and national publications and Web sites.
