Louis Bowers’s Summary

Lead Meteorologist for World Leading Renewable Energy provider Iberdrola Renewables East Coast pipeline. Direct experience producing wind resource and energy analysis, producing wind farm layouts, and coordinating with project managers. Expert experience using mesoscale meteorological numerical modeling for wind energy: greenfield identification and analysis, resource analysis, preliminary sound modeling, forecasting, and operational statistics.

Previous experience producing operational meteorological forecasts for a large NJ public electric and gas utility's (PSE&G) severe storm operations (4 years), operating a real-time numerical weather modeling system (7 years), meteorological consulting, marine forecasting, and applied meteorological and oceanographic research.
