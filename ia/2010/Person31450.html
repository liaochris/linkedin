Scott Henkel’s Summary

SUMMARY OF QUALIFICATIONS

Highly motivated and results oriented leader with 14 years of contact center operations experience. Experienced at leading large numbers of employees in multiple locations to achieve or exceed quality of service metrics while employing workforce management techniques to reduce labor expenses while maximizing productivity.

HIGH LEVEL CAREER ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Within my first 10 months at Express Scripts, I increased contact center occupancy 7.6% with zero up-front costs saving Express Scripts 15.5 million over 5 years via less labor requirements.

Successfully directed contact center operations and technology handling approx. 50,000 to 400,000 contacts per day in virtual and single site environments with budgets ranging from $40 million to $220 million

Reduced variable costs from process improvements including efficient full-time, part-time, flex, and compressed schedules, accurate contact and labor forecasts, increased agent productivity, improved contact quality, reduced attrition, associate engagement, etc.

Provided leadership and strategic direction to diverse geographically dispersed teams ranging in size from 6 to over 30 with indirects ranging from 700 to over 4,000

Established cohesion between corporate departments and the contact center resulting in increased first call resolution, customer satisfaction, and decreased costs from proactive planning

Managed six sigma process improvements to contact center productivity, process redesign, and service level consistency

International call center site direction and implementation experience in the Caribbean, Asian, and Indian markets
