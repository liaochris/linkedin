Matthew Tenney’s Summary

I am an Associate practicing in areas of securities, mergers and acquisitions, aircraft financing, secured transactions, real estate, and general corporate representation.

I have worked on registration statements and public filings under both the 33 and the 34 Acts (8-K, 10-Q, 10-K, etc.), private placements under state and federal registration exemptions (Reg D), public tender offers, proxy materials, SEC confidentiality requests, and responses to state securities inquiries.

I also work in aircraft financing, both in debt acquisitions and leasing transactions.

My practice has also included real estate matters as well as representing a sewer improvement district with annexation, improvements, and general special district laws under Utah state law.

I have also had the chance to represent local community theaters in matters before the Utah Department of Workforce Services.
