Matt Moderski’s Summary

I believe that we all share a common vision; progress. In my experience as a progressive marketing professional, I have seen companies grow, people adapt, and ideas come to life. My experience from both agency and client backgrounds is a balance, I feel, that when combined with my passion, steadfastness, and genuineness, has allowed me to demonstrate measurable success with every company I have been a part of.

With more than five years of combined experience in managing fast-paced marketing operations, my enthusiasm has been driven by e-commerce, search engine marketing, and social media marketing. This experience, which includes managing and operating my own retail company, creating a fully integrated advertising campaign for Coca-Cola, and implementing numerous successful interactive tactics has only heightened this enthusiasm.

My professional experience, when combined with my education from Emerson College, has taught me to keep an open-mind when faced with new challenges and to approach each one as an opportunity to contribute, learn and grow. I know, however, that the greatest lessons are learned outside of the classroom and workplace. The depth of my life experience has taught me how to present something I love with audacity, persistence, and conviction; with the understanding that feelings of that magnitude are seldom felt. I know how to believe in myself and express pride in my abilities. And I believe the greatest risks we take are the ones we don't.
