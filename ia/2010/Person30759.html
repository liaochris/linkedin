Paul W. Anderson, Ph.D.’s Summary

Getting successful is not the hard part. Sustaining success is. Sustained success is possible if the success and goal(s) is achieved in the right way in the first place. Together we will get you where you want to be and make sure you stay there. That's BulletProof Coaching!

Coaching is not only "cheer leading". It is a process of honest assessment of your current reality coupled with individualized and/or family or group strategies that sustain what has been "cheered" and encouraged to grow.
