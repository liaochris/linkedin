Jorge Martinez’s Summary

1999: I was the lead creative at a startup during the dot-com boom. The years that followed were spent broadening my creative skills at top advertising agencies including the award-winning digital agency, R/GA in New York. My clients included household names like Johnson & Johnson, Verizon, Toshiba, Nokia and SC Johnson.

2008: My team and I launched one of the largest and most successful launches in Johnson and Johnson’s history. A website and mobile app were developed to provide allergy sufferers with information, tools and the support to do the things they enjoy the most despite their allergies.

2010: On a mission to empower others to follow their passions, I launched Access Media, a social media company. Our owned-and-operated niche websites provide relevant content written by passionate individuals looking to connect and engage in conversations with others sharing the same interest. Each vertical niche is an opportunity for our brand advertising partners to connect with qualified potential customers.

Qualifications:
Seasoned interactive designer / art director with an eye for design.

Strong group leader able to mange concurrent tasks and deliver high-end design executions through team collaboration.

Versed in social media for marketing and increasing online visibility.
