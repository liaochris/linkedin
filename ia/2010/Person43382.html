Corian Design’s Summary

DuPont™ Corian® Design Studio Shanghai is adept at bringing East and West cultures together. Drawn from the rich history of Chinese art and building techniques, plus interpreted them with state of the art computer-aided technology, have pushed the boundaries of modern surfaces and its applications to unlimited possibilities.
The Studio Shanghai presents the designer material Corian® in the context of an Art & Design gallery experience. Each piece is designed to appeal to the differing market segments that the material is used for in both traditional and experimental contexts, displayed within what is created architecturally as a complete user experience.

杜邦®可丽耐®上海设计中心，灵感源自博大精深的中国建筑艺术，在东西文化的交融中获得新翼，以现代数字技术诠释材料的不同特性，并将可丽耐®在设计领域内的无限可能淋漓尽致地展现其中。每一件作品皆顺应不同场合的需求，在传统特性应用中尝试新的设计概念，拓展新的可能，使参观者在直观地欣赏之余，感受全方位不同凡响的体验。
