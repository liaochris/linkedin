Brian Handley’s Summary

My business background includes 25 years of growing companies large and small. I've led sales teams, business operations, worldwide distribution channels and global alliances. I've been fortunate to have extensive international experience giving me a perspective on the development of business in different cultures and business environments.

Specifics:

- Business Development: I have a track record for building an effective alliance/channel/OEM business, articulating a strategy and identifying key strategic partners in various global geographies resulting in increased revenue.

- Power Products & Green Business: I see the "green" revolution expanding the need for alternative thinking. My current position with Emerson provides me deep insight into power protection, distribution and energy storage. This and my direct work with large clients have given me a unique insight on energy use and appropriate techniques on the application of efficiencies.

- Enterprise Software: I have extensive experience in global enterprise software sales, direct and indirect. Developed partnerships with large players (IBM, Accenture, Fujitsu, Siemens) and regional players in specific markets and verticals. Led national and regional direct sales teams.

I volunteer time as a board member of the National Kidney Foundation-Pacific Northwest and am a member of the executive board of the Assoication of Strategic Alliance Professionals.
