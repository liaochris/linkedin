Jim Schultz’s Summary

Highly accomplished Sales and Marketing Executive with over 19 years of sales management and product marketing experience in the pharmaceutical industry. Recognized as an innovative and strategic thinker who consistently exceeds sales objectives. Proven ability to build and inspire highly engaged sales teams.
