Olivier Gérardin’s Summary

Author of the book "Créer des applications web interactives avec Google Web Toolkit (1.7 et 2.0) " (Dunod).

Experienced Java/web architect and project manager: more than 12 years of experience in designing and building software projects in distributed Java-mostly environments, 15 years of experience in object-oriented modeling and programming. I also have a very good practice of designing and giving trainings, coaching and mentoring. I have excellent writing skills in French and English.
