Rasheed Muhammad’s Summary

Rasheed Muhammad is a First Vice President for SunTrust Private Wealth Management Sports and Entertainment Specialty Group based in Atlanta covering New York City, Los Angeles, and Miami. As Client Advisor, his role in the group includes credit and investment management with a
thorough understanding of cash flows associated with the sports & entertainment industries. He has been with SunTrust for seven years and spent this time working with high net worth individuals in the music and sports industries nationally. Prior to joining SunTrust, Rasheed was a dual associate of Bank of America and Banc of America Investment Services for over 6 years where he began carving out his entertainment specialty niche. Rasheed holds the Series 7, 63, 65, life accident & sickness, and variable products licenses and is a graduate of The University of South Carolina Columbia where he also served post graduate on the advisory board for the College of Hospitality, Retail, & Sports Management. Rasheed is currently a member of the Atlanta Chapter of The Recording Academy, after serving as Vice President for one year and on the Board of Governor’s for four years. He also serves on the board for Atlanta Children’s Shelter. He has been featured or quoted in Jezebel Magazine, Rolling Out Magazine, XXL Magazine, Atlanta Business Magazine, Music Connection Magazine and the Atlanta Business Chronicle. Rasheed was recently acccepted into Leadership Music's Class of 2010.
