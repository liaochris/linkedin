Jose Siandre’s Summary

Jose proudly served in the U.S. Navy and most recently, as a commissioned officer, Lieutenant, in the U.S. Coast Guard. As an officer he served in various assignments throughout the East Coast and was awarded the Humanitarian Service Medal during Hurricane Katrina. For the past 20 years, he has held positions in various industries, telecom, retail, military, janitorial franchise, higher education, IT consulting and financial services. He graduated from Saint Francis College in 1999 with a B.S. degree in Special Studies (Liberal Arts) and from Polytechnic University in 2003 with a M.S. degree in Telecommunications & Information Management. He also served as a member of the National Society of Hispanic MBAs while in graduate school. He has held senior level positions with Toys ”R” Us in Wayne, New Jersey, and Polytechnic University in Brooklyn, New York. He is currently a business development manager helping businesses and non-profits save money on their electric utility bill. He also donates his time as a member of the West Milford Rotary which helps raise funds for community projects and other needs across the world. He has a wife and two boys and lives in the outskirts of Northern New Jersey.
