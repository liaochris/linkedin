I’m a generalist in all things technology & business. If you need someone that can understand a wide array of business & technology issues, I’m your man.

Here’s what I bring to the table:

Real World Experience
I have over fifteen years of experience in corporate and consulting environments…with most of my experience coming from the corporate world. I’ve been where you are today and can bring that real world experience to your organization.

Technical and Business Acumen
Whether you need assistance with developing a strategic plan, help with selecting the proper technology, implementing a new technology, developing software or outsourcing your operations, I can help. I’ve assisted organizations that range in size from 100 to 50,000 people and have experience in many types of technologies from many vendors.

Passion
You might think that this is a strange one to include here…but I feel that it’s a vital piece of what I bring (call it a piece of my competitive advantage). I’m passionate about what I do…and I’m passionate about helping other people.

In my career, I have always tried to work toward these five goals:

1. Build long-term relationships
2. Deliver Value
3. Deliver Results
4. Help people
5. Continue to learn
