Scott Doré’s Summary

In May 2008 I helped start Luminous Capital, an independent wealth advisory firm. Our goal was to create an objective and fully transparent platform for advising our high net worth clients while minimizing the conflicts that are present at many large financial institutions.

Our team of 26 professionals is based in Los Angeles and Menlo Park, CA and manages $3.2 billion in client assets.
