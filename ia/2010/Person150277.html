Murray PJ Simser’s Summary

Murray Simser brings 15 years of experience in developing corporate strategy and running organizations.

In summer 2009 he began offering consulting services to restructure businesses to optimize revenue streams and raise capital.

Before that Murray was the Director of Worldwide Services Sales for Microsoft Corporation, where he led an incubation sales team that developed then globally deployed sales practices and assets. He was responsible for creating then implementing global sales readiness systems and practices, brought into the global mainstream of MCS an internally funded two year business architecture project that became the backbone of customer sales, engagement models and data gathering exercises globally.

Prior to Microsoft, Murray co-founded the award winning 80 million dollar investor-backed SaaS CRM software company eAssist Global Solutions.

He co-founded the business process offshore (BPO) company OneSource Partners which was responsible for unlocking shareholder value by least cost shoring over 250 million dollars in operating expenditure to places around the globe.

Murray was the Vice President of business development at Wireless Services Corporation where he focused on raising capital and developing channel and partner strategies.

Before that he was employed in various management capacities at Clearnet Communications a fast growing Wireless Carrier, and IDRC.

Murray originally hails from Toronto, lived in London, New York, San Diego, and Ottawa and since 2001 makes his home in the Pacific Northwest's Puget Sound Region.

Murray studied Economics at the University of Ottawa where he now commits time as Director-at-large for the Board of Directors of the University of Ottawa Alumni Association.
