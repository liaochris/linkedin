Caitlin Jewell’s Summary

Silverscape is a leading independent digital agency that creates engaging online experiences, custom web applications and interactive marketing solutions across browser, desktop and mobile environments. We help companies become market leaders through innovative brand development and strategic marketing programs. Silverscape is the firm you hire for innovative strategic options, an accessible creative process and the experienced resources to move forward with agility.

As Principal and Creative Director at Silverscape, Caitlin works with organizations as a catalyst for change, champion of design and technology and partner in synthesizing complex paths to transition. She’s done work for Disney, Boston Beer Company, RIM, Genzyme, DRIFIRE, Lernout & Hauspie, and a long list of national architecture/engineering/construction, high tech, consumer, and financial firms. She holds memberships in the American Institute of Graphic Artists (AIGA), Society of Marketing Professional Services (SMPS). Caitlin is an active presenter at in leading industry events and conferences.
