R. Trent Thompson’s Summary

As a Social Media & Engagement Strategist with over 18 years of brand development, marketing and communications experience, I have worked with clients ranging from start-ups and nonprofits to members of the Fortune 500. I help organizations leverage the power of social media by developing robust digital media strategies, and deploying and managing integrated social media marketing programs — to increase visibility, extend reach, drive purchase intent and build brand loyalty.

As Publisher/Director of Digital Strategy & Content for Boomerous.com, my responsibilities included developing digital content, publishing, editing, social media marketing, strategic planning, expansion of the company’s multimedia publishing platform, venture investments, establishing relationships with strategic partners and rich-media advertisers, and new business initiatives.

Prior to Boomerous.com, I served as Creative Director for a brand consultancy, where I developed comprehensive brand identity systems; social media marketing strategies, tools and integrated programs; interactive media and web-based solutions; and traditional marketing and communication programs.

Earlier in my career, I held key corporate positions in research and development and emerging technologies at Lincoln Financial Group and held various positions in information technology at Electronic Data Systems.

R. Trent Thompson’s Specialties:
