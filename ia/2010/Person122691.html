laurie seligman’s Summary

The goal at Transformation Consulting is to inspire people to make worthy decisions from clear intentions.

We meet people where they truly are , utilizing the benefit of a group environment to expedite attitude shifts and breakthroughs.

In working with physicians, patients, and general population, and an education in psychology, vibrational medicine and from participatory research with dolphins and whales, I draw on a diverse and variegated range of ideation in my seminars.

People have answers and need the tools and methods to relocate the ability to help with healing, self monitor, and be more accountable in all areas of wellness. It's the center connecting all the spokes in the wheel- the ability to discover, decide and move from a centered place within- that's the way to face and live life. That's a green life. that's a transformed life. that's the goal.
