Eric Majchrzak’s Summary

Eric Majchrzak has been named one of Buffalo's "10 People to Watch in 2010" by the reporters and editors of Buffalo Business First. He is a professional services marketer with 18 years of experience in developing and implementing results-oriented marketing strategies and programs that generate organic growth, deepen client relationships and increase revenues. Recently elected a Board member of the national Association for Accounting Marketing.

Accounting Industry Leadership

Eric was honored nationally as the “2006 Rookie Accounting Marketer of the Year” by the national Association for Accounting Marketing (AAM), where he currently serves as Board Member. He is also a Past-Chairman of AAM’s website committee from 2007-2009. Eric is one of the accounting industries' "in-house" leaders in the area of search engine optimization (SEO) and social media. He has been practicing SEO for nearly a decade. His efforts utilizing SEO in combination with social media as a means to build relationships and generate leads have resulted in hundreds of thousands of dollars of new revenue for his firm. Eric was published on the topic of SEO in CCH's CPA Practice Management Forum and has been profiled in several other media outlets and industry trades on the topic of social media, including Buffalo Business First, Accounting Today and Marketingsherpa.com. Eric was also a featured speaker at the SM2Day Conference 2009, a premier social media event in New York State featuring Chris Brogan. Freedmaxick.com was featured as a case study at the national CCH User Conference in 2009, where samples of successful web metrics for accounting firm websites were featured. Eric was a featured speaker on the Marketing Masters panel at one of the accounting industry's leading practice management conferences, "Winning-is-Everything Conference" in Las Vegas, Jan 2010. Eric's firm is the 1st public accounting firm in the U.S. to launch an outdoor interactive digital Twitter Billboard.

Eric Majchrzak’s Specialties:

Leading professional services marketer. Creation of value-based integrated marketing systems, public/media relations, media strategy, lead generation programs, media planning, website development, social media, search engine optimization (SEO), conversion strategies.
