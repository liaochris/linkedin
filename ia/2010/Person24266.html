Bindy Bonnette’s Summary

Bindy is the Creative Director for Shift’s Wellington office. She leads the Wellington design team in developing compelling experiences and consistent online branding for all of our clients. Bindy is a passionate advocate of the end user and is obsessed with creating content and experiences that are both relevant and enjoyable.

She is the complete package: a tenacious creative with exemplary visual skills, and an accomplished illustrator with a vibrant style all of her own. She has a wealth of creative experience and knowledge gathered from working with creative agencies in the UK and has designed for some of the world’s biggest brands, including Dulux UK, British Telecom, Sony PlayStation Europe, T-Mobile, Television New Zealand, Deutsche Bank, Sainsbury’s Bank, and American Express.

Bindy has a Bachelor’s degree in Design from the Wellington School of Design. She has lectured in design at tertiary level and is a 50/50 citizen of New Zealand and the UK.
