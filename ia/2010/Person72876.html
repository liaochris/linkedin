Gary Babbitt’s Summary

Professional news and sports videographer with substantial experience in breaking news coverage, fast/accurate/creative shooting & editing, and quick/safe live truck operation. Very experienced sports videographer/editor as well.

Gary Babbitt’s Specialties:

News and sports videography, non-linear editing, helicopter videography, lighting, live microwave truck (2ghz & 7ghz) operation, portable microwave use and production shoots.

Gary Babbitt’s Experience

  • Photographer/Editor/Live Truck Operator

    WGN-TV

    (Public Company; Broadcast Media industry)

    August 2002 — March 2008 (5 years 8 months)

    Shot and edited news and sports stories. Operated a live microwave truck on a daily basis. Tapeless newsroom non-linear editing. All digital, fiber optic live microwave trucks (2ghz & 7ghz). Trained on Final Cut Pro HD non-linear editors and Sony 3rd generation XdCam HD cameras. Used ENPS and Outlook extensively. Helped out on the Assignement Desk when needed.
