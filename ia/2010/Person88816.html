Jamie Lynn Morgan’s Summary

I currently live in Coeur d'Alene Idaho. I moved here 18 years ago where I met my wonderful husband Tom. We now share our lives with our three beautiful children, and are enjoying the great life we have in the Inland Northwest.


With Social Media constantly changing and evolving I stay at the forefront of all the technology. I am constantly learning about how to use not only current media, but any new emerging media. That way I can provide you with the most up to date and relevant information on how you can promote your business, yourself, and your passion.

Social Media Consulting and Professional Networking are my main passion, but not the only business venture that I am a part of.

The second is Nutritional Cleansing & Weight Loss Coaching, for years I have struggled with weight and health issues, but since starting a wonderful program in August of 2009 it has been a life changing experience. Not only is it allowing me to lose and maintain a healthy weight, the health issues that I had experienced for years are virtually non-existent. I now want to share that with others who may be needing to lose a few pounds or just be healthier at you ideal weight.

Third, is My HVAC Guy. My husband Tom and I run a Heating and Air Conditioning Business. With his 20+ years of experience he can repair, replace, or maintain just about any piece of HVAC equipment & water heaters. We happen to make a great team, if I do say so myself:

What ever you put into life, you get back!
