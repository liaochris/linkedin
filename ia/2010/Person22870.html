Beth Steinberg’s Summary

Beth Steinberg has over 18 years of human resource experience in both emerging and Fortune 500 companies. Her focus is helping leaders and companies with complex organizational and growth issues. Beth currently focuses on leadership coaching, executive development and organizational development for companies in both technology and consumer products and services. Her work includes leadership and organizational development for The Motley Fool, lynda.com, SeaChange Capital, Mozilla, The New Teacher Center, and other emerging companies. Beth has held numerous Human Resource Executive positions including the VP of HR at Facebook and a Vice President of Human Resources at Electronic Arts. Beth has also held HR positions at Hewlett Packard and Nordstrom. Beth has her BA in Psychology from San Jose State University and has completed graduate work in clinical psychology at San Diego State University. Beth is also a graduate of the Executive Program at Stanford University’s Graduate School of Business. Beth is skilled in the diagnosis and resolution of complex organizational development issues that result in improved business performance. Highly proactive, well-developed organization and planning abilities, and adept at managing multi projects simultaneously. Extensive international experience, including China and India

Beth is very involved with the Silicon Valley non-profit community, focusing on environmental and nutrition education. Beth has been an active member and board member of The Junior League for over 15 years. In addition, for six years, Beth was on the Board of Directors of Collective Roots, is currently on the Board of Trustees at Hidden Villa and is on the Board of SV2, the Silicon Valley Venture Philanthropy Group. Beth sits on the Advisory Boards of Rypple, Talent Spring and Bojam, emerging technology companies.

Beth Steinberg’s Specialties:

executive and leadership coaching, people development strategy, organizational development and structure strategy, innovative performance management strategies, talent acquisition and management. Exceptional ability to build relationships and business partnerships with senior executives and employees of all levels.
