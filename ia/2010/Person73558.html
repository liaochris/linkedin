Graham Barnes’s Summary

Born in England near Liverpool; went to school in London (Imperial College) then came to USA in 1980. Traveled to over 30 countries and completed major projects in six countries. MBA (beta gamma sigma) from Santa Clara University (1989). Became US citizen in 2005. CEO of NextWeb, Inc. 2001-2006; currently CEO of BidShift, Inc. and on several Boards, including Santa Clara University's Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

Wide range of leadership experiences with both large and small companies, in the high-tech, service provider, software, healthcare, staffing, wireless, telecommunications manufacturing, VOIP, ISP and TV / radio broadcast industries both in the USA and internationally.

Recently as CEO of NextWeb, Inc. a VC-backed wireless Internet Service Provider acquired by Covad Communications in February. In 2005, I was named finalist for the Entrepreneur of the Year Award by Ernst & Young’s San Jose office. Also in 2005, NextWeb was named to the prestigious Inc 500 list at position #104, being the #8 company in telecommunications on the Inc 500 list. Additionally, for the second year in a row, NextWeb placed in the Top 5 of the 50 fastest growing Bay Area companies by the East Bay Business Times.

Graham Barnes’s Specialties:

Hands-on senior management experience, with strong execution, financial, engineering, sales and customer service skills. Capable of hiring and developing motivated teams to achieve results, introduce new products and exceed customer expectations. Multiple M&A deals negotiated and completed for strategic goals and business development.
