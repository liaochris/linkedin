Ted Bird’s Summary

Goal oriented, entrepreneurial leader with a diverse background, including 27 years of international and domestic experience in sales and marketing of medical devices. Nineteen years of experience and contacts in U.S spinal device market.

Ted Bird’s Specialties:

Marketing, (product management, strategic planning, market research, medical education, professional society relations), Sales, (recruiting, building, developing and managing direct and/or independant distributor sales networks, new business development/strategic accounts and sales training), Clinical and Regulatory Affairs, (510K submissions and PMA/IDE studies - site selection and study enrollment strategies & tactics), International sales and marketing experience.
