  • Community Planner

    Cranberry Township

    (Government Administration industry)

    July 2007 — January 2009 (1 year 7 months)

    I served as the Community Planner that dealt with subdivision and land development within the Township. I reviewed all subdivision and land development plans in accordance with Township regulations and prepared development reports and presentations for both the Planning Commission and the Board of Supervisors. In addition to reviewing the plans, I attended all Planning Commission and Zoning Hearing Board meetings and workshops. I also provided land development project management for the department and assured that all land development site work was being done in accordance with the approved site plans, the developer’s agreement and Township regulations.

  • Associate Planner

    Mifflin County Department of Planning & Development

    (Privately Held; Government Administration industry)

    January 2007 — July 2007 (7 months)

    I served as the Associate Planner and Subdivision Officer for Mifflin and Juniata Counties and reviewed all subdivision and land development plans in accordance with the various municipal subdivision and land development ordinances as well as for compliance with zoning regulations. I was involved with the development of the Juniata County Comprehensive Plan and the Mifflin, Perry, and Juniata Counties’ Multi-Jurisdictional Hazardous Mitigation Plan. In addition, I created GIS maps as needed for the department in support of planning activities.
