Sarah Fox’s Summary

Vixen Hospitality Solutions is a niche business turnaround and 'new concepts' consultancy servicing the UK Restaurant and Hospitality sector.

With almost 30 years experience in the international restaurant and bar industry, including several directorships within both Blue Chip and SMEs including my own successful venture, I recently founded Vixen Hospitality Solutions.

With a risk sharing business model, Vixen partners with clients to achieve business goals ranging from revenue growth, cost reduction, operational improvement and trade sale. This includes working alongside Venture Capital firms with stakes in restaurant and hospitality businesses.

* Broad knowledge of wide range of issues and challenges facing operators *

* Tried and tested turnaround strategies and financial models which can be applied to all clients *

* Latest brand concepts and differentiation strategies required for successful new initiatives *

  • Managing Director

    2005 — 2008 (3 years )

    Interim MD and Consultant for leading industry Brands I led the following initiatives:

    Client / Brand (1) - to facilitate business sale:
    The restructuring of head office to reduce overheads and improve efficiency and profitability. Hiring a new Operations team to improve cost control and standards. Property leases negotiation to reduce rents and improve profitability. Introduction of a new menu to reducing wastage, improving sales and profitability.

    Client / Brand (3): My client was in final negotiations for a sale to the market leader. My role was to halt the sales and profitability decline within my estate of 200 pubs . The brand achieved the strongest margin and profit performances during Q3 and best improvement in standards.
