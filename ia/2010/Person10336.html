Amy (Ekross) Quigley’s Summary

Amy E. Quigley, Vice President of Marketing at Continuum, is responsible for providing strategic and visionary leadership to the marketing team by delivering targeted and integrated marketing solutions. Amy also assures that all marketing strategies are consistent with overall company objectives.

Throughout her career, Amy has developed experience in a variety of industries ranging from technology and financial services, to fashion and not-for-profit organizations, specializing in professional services. Prior to Continuum, she was responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies at Molecular, ATG, Fidelity Investments and Bank of Boston, and she contributed to the marketing strategy for two fashion start-ups. Amy also spent several years at Leo Burnett/Boston where she managed several major accounts, including CMGI, Keane and exchange.com (acquired by Amazon.com), developing strategies to articulate their business objectives and values into succinct and influential brand messages.

Amy currently serves on the Board of the American Marketing Association's Boston Chapter and the Board of the New England Chapter of PM Forum.
