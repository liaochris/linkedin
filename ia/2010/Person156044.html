    ♦ NATIONAL LEVEL:

    Our mission at Empire Real Estate, LLC is to protect the financial interests of our equity partners, to effectively manage risk, and to maximize their investment returns. By holding true to this mission, we will earn a reputation as the preferred partner when it comes to investing in commercial real estate.

    We focus on multi-tenant existing properties with little development requirement. Our strength will be in our ability to turn a property challenged by occupancy, tenant mix or operating issues, into a stabilized high performing asset.

    ERE has identified and will continue to evaluate our target portfolio by consistently reading the trends affecting our marketplace. Specifically, we are concerned with long-term demographic changing with aging baby-boomers, migration, and urban vs. suburban lifestyles. We're monitoring world issues that will affect us locally, along with changes in regulations for both taxation and land use.

    We consistently evaluate the financial markets to stay on top of access to capital, inflationary pressure, and returns from comparable investments. Our view extends to changes in technology that will affect how and where we live & how our properties must operate to remain competitive.

    ♦ LOCAL LEVEL:

The majority of our properties are sold to Investors for significantly below market value, who will rehab the properties and resell them at full market value.

    • Our Investors receive "first look" at these properties for purchase prior to us putting them on the open market, or rehabbing ourselves.

    • We have a network of bird dogs, servicing firms, compilers, and reo companies who present us with GREAT deals on a regular basis.
