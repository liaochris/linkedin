Allen Mireles’s Summary

Allen Mireles is principal of Allen Mireles Social Media Consulting, a social media/PR consultancy based outside of Toledo, Ohio. She specializes in developing social media strategies that will help you:
• Develop expert status in your industry
• Increase traffic to your website
• Build trust
• Respond quickly to customer complaints
• Convert customers into evangelists for your brand
• Monitor your reputation and respond effectively

An experienced public speaker and trainer, she is passionate about social media and its importance in today’s marketplace. She offers coaching programs and seminars for businesses and professionals interested in understanding social media quickly. Her firm also provides social media management services for busy executives and business owners.

In today’s changing world economy it is all about conversation. Listening is essential. Allen’s strength is in her ability to ask endless questions, to listen intently and then develop powerfully creative solutions. Allen integrates social media marketing with traditional marketing/PR tactics to create powerful results-based campaigns.

Educated at Earlham College in Richmond, Indiana, she is originally from the East Coast and is proficient in Spanish. Her background includes advertising, public relations, television and radio. Allen has also directed several major NW Ohio fundraising efforts for educational institutions and non-profits.

Endlessly curious and a voracious reader, Allen is constantly learning—about everything. Her experience, high energy, compassion and creative nature make her a natural partner for companies who want understand and utilize the power of social media marketing to build business in a slow economy.
