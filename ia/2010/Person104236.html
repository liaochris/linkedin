Jon Wretlind’s Summary

My interests in the past 7 years have taken me back to graduate school at Denver Seminary to study theology and chaplaincy. Additionally, I am pursuing ordained ministriy within the Anglican Mission in America (AMiA) denomination and have completed 1 unit of Clinical Pastoral Education (CPE). I am also a Third-Order Franciscan friar with the Company of Jesus, an Anglican religious order (http://www.companyofjesus.net).

I also work as a freelance graphic/web designer and I volunteer as a musician in our Arts Ministries at my local church. I enjoy writing poetry, songwriting and composing original music, painting, and just about anything artistic and creative. Some of my songs are available for free download at http://www.soundclick.com/jonathanchristopher.

As for technical skills, I am a specialist in the Adobe Creative Suite tools, including Flash and Photoshop. In addition, I am very adept in coding HTML, XHTML, Javascript, C# ASP.Net, XML, CSS2, PHP, jQuery, and can adapt to just about any other script language that comes around since I have been working with code since the early 1980's. In short, I am a very creative thinker with the ability to "see" in computer code.

I would love to hear from you! I am amazed at how these social networking tools like Linked In work to keep people connected in new life-changing ways!

Blessings to you!
