Steve Nimmo’s Summary

Business Process Management (BPM) Leader: I have had a highly successful leadership career that has included significant contributions to market leaders. I have a strong balance with both business and technical application of BPM. I have earned multiple Innovation of the Year recognitions for enhancing operational efficiency.

Over the past 15+ years, I have been deploying highly effective BPM programs. The results have reduced costs, increased sales, improved customer loyalty, and streamlined operations in a variety of situations.

Recognized leader in BPM Community: I have had the honor of presenting the BPM programs I have developed at multiple peer conferences, including the 2010 Gartner BPM Conference and the 2009 / 2010 BPMInstitute.org Brainstorm.

ACHIEVEMENTS AND SKILLS

Implemented comprehensive Business Process Management Suite (BPMS). I successfully implemented the BPM Tool, Lombardi Teamworks, allowing the organization to deploy essential business processes through technology resulting in significant productivity gains.

Orchestrated reduction of non-value added activities throughout organizations. Standardized operations of 30+ offices, allowing for increase in efficiency and sharing of best practices across the organization.

Skills: Problem solving and developing innovative solutions under tight deadlines, Facilitating diverse groups, Exceptionally strong written and verbal communication skills, Highly personable and capable of working with all levels of an organization.
