Eric Chadwick’s Summary

• Veteran game development artist with 17 years game development experience, involved in the development of over 30 game titles and simulations, on PC, console, and mobile.

• Concepting, storyboarding, modeling, texturing, animating, particles & effects, emphasis on real-time 3D.

• Directing teams up to 15 artists and programmers, hiring art talent, managing outside contractors, developing and maintaining schedules and budgets.

• Organizing and directing motion capture sessions.

• Researching and developing art tools and pipelines for PC and consoles, documenting art technical constraints for each client's rendering engine. Training and mentoring art staff.

• Video production for NTSC broadcast, DVD, and streaming video (mov, mpeg, flv, wmv).

• Writing and maintaining a Normal Map wiki page written specifically for artists:
http://wiki.polycount.net/Normal_Map

Eric Chadwick’s Specialties:

• 3ds Max, Photoshop, Sony Vegas, video compression, advanced DVD authoring, custom art tools, ImageMagick, Shader FX, Filter Forge, Crazy Bump, constantly testing new software.
