Pat Jackson’s Summary

I am a seasoned global marketer with extensive experience in brand building and brand positioning across a diverse group of clients in the CPG, beauty and pharma categories. I’ve had the opportunity to learn my trade and hone my skills while working for top advertising agencies including Saatchi & Saatchi, McCann Erickson, and Jordan, McGrath, Case and Taylor (now Arnold).

As a skilled brand architect and strategist, I’ve led the development of integrated campaigns across multiple media platforms by utilizing both traditional media (TV, print, and out of home) as well as new digital options (digital, social, viral, and mobile.) My efforts have resulted not only in award-winning campaigns but also in record profits for marketers through supporting creative and selling across ideas to clients. My roster of blue chip/Fortune 500 clients includes General Mills, P&G, Novartis, L’Oreal, Johnson & Johnson and Welch Foods.

I’m a strong presenter, a great strategic thinker with solid creative instincts. It’s this combination of skills that has enabled me to lead and motivate teams throughout my career, to grow market share, and drive increased media spending. My innate curiosity and enthusiasm for understanding the consumer has led to my success in building strong campaigns that resonate with the target audience.

Notable career achievements include:

• Boosted Old El Paso brand awareness to attain title of one of the 50 “hottest brands” in Ad Age, 2009.
• Won global Novartis assignment, resulting in $1M revenue increase.
• Oversaw $2.0 billion global Acuvue Contact Lens and Band Aid businesses.
• Authored L’Oreal Hair Color ad submissions resulting in four Effies and three AME Awards.
• Recipient of McCann-Erickson “Truth Well Told” Award in honor of business contributions and leadership.
