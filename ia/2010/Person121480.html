Lars Rengersen’s Summary

My main (team) role is creator/innovator, which means I am imaginative, future-oriented, enjoying complexity and very creative. Summarised this means I really enjoy new things. I get inspired by talking to and working with other people. In my work I am able to quickly absorb (business) needs by listening carefully and develop a vision how to move forward significantly.

Therefore within Monito, the focus on prototyping is strongly driven by me and off course also to large extend supported by my colleagues. The activities within SusaGroup in the field of 'creating meaningful experiences' are completely new and thus giving me a lot of energy.
