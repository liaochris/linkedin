Michael Malloy, Ph.D.’s Summary

A lifetime of diverse experience, in international settings and in the use of Internet technology, places me at the crossroads of the global, Internet, economy in the 21st Century. This allows me to pursue my passion, and "mission", to help business leaders and college students to achieve their business and personal goals in today's globalized, Internet-driven era.

Currently leading a team of motivated, experienced executives who are taking IntellaCore around the world.

At IntellaCore, we provide expertise and resources to expand businesses beyond their borders by increasing revenue and distribution, decreasing cost, and by positioning companies to compete successfully in today’s global marketplace.

IntellaCore has a global team of experts that will evaluate your company, remove uncertainty as we take you in to new markets, introduce you to new global vendors, and ensure that your technology is allowing you to compete in today’s 21st Century marketplace.

IntellaCore has a unique point of view because we are positioned at the critical crossroad where very few are -- that place where the global economy meets the Internet.

Over 20 years of experience, and formal training, in teaching, training, and advising business leaders and today's youth about the global, internet world in which we live today, and what it takes to be competitive to reach your goals.

International: Extensive experience doing business,teaching, traveling, and working around the world my entire life. Taught in several countries, ran the operations of 2 international studies centers.

Internet: Started participating in internet services companies in 1997. Owned and grew a telecommunication and internet solution company.

Participate in US government affiliated Non-Profit, the District Export Councils, to help businesses export and to be globally active. Active in multiple non-profit organizations dedicated to assisting businesses.
