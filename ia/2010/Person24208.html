Billy Melnyk’s Summary

Bacardi is the world's largest privately held, family-owned spirits company; a producer of Premium Spirits: rums, including Bacardi Superior, Bacardi Gold, Bacardi Black, Bacardi 8, and Bacardi 151. Bacardi also owns Bombay Sapphire Gin, Martini & Rossi Vermouths, Dewar’s Scotch, and act as the agents for Disaronno Amaretto and Drambuie. Bacardi acquired Grey Goose vodka – the World’s Best Tasting vodka. Most recently, the company acquired 42 Below Vodka - and launched Seven Tiki Spiced Rum from FIJI

Baby Guerrilla is a marketing and business consultancy.
