Mike Ferrari’s Summary

I am an experienced and versatile graphic professional who has worked both in-house for a global corporation on a variety of projects, as well as run my own company.

Currently I run my own multimedia company that produces a print publication (Aural Fix Communique), webcasts & podcasts (Aural Fix Transmission) that has a focus on the Long Island music scene.

I recently became a partner in 3 Creative Inc., a full service design studio which has been doing a lot of packaging, mockups and planograms.

I'm always on the lookout for challenging and fun projects! Bring it on!

Mike Ferrari’s Specialties:

Hands on project management from concept to print, involving all phases from bids & quotes, photography, design, print ready production, and printing. Also have a special talent for problem solving!
