Jason Verdelli’s Summary

As President of Phase 2 Solutions, LLC, an internet marketing company based out of Harrisburg, PA, I focus a majority of my time on researching, educating, speaking and implementing internet marketing strategies for companies that need to start using the internet as a key part of their growth.

I believe in an educational system where I can educate everyone, especially business professionals, out there who want to start making the most out of internet marketing and social media. Check out http://www.jasonverdelli.com to learn more about what I am constantly researching to stay on top of the industry. My goal is to be the best internet marketing and social media expert that can relate and educate those interested in bridging the gap with what they know what they need to know to be successful on the internet.

My company, Phase 2 Solutions, LLC (http://www.phase2solutions.net), focuses primarily on internet marketing implementation services such as research and strategy, email marketing, social media planning, search engine optimization, online advertising and business development services.
