Laurie Kirk’s Summary

THE BOARD FORUM CEO Roundtable has numerous CEO peer groups and eight world class facilitators throughout New England.

Laurie Kirk brings more than 30 years of strategic experience with leading Fortune 500 and small to mid-size companies, including Manager of Strategic Forecasting & Competitive Analysis for The Gillette Company.

Laurie has published articles in The Journal of Business Forecasting on leadership and organizational change, led seminars receiving the “Best Bet” from the Boston Business Journal, and was selected for the “Successful Strategic Planning Practices Benchmarking Project” by the University of St. Gallen, Switzerland.

Laurie Kirk’s Specialties:

CEO Peer-to-Peer groups with high level facilitators, several who are CEO's, bring concrete expertise and innovation for growing companies, including strategic planning, finance, human resources, growth and exit strategies, branding, mergers and acquisitions, board development.
