Brian Geyser, APRN, MSN-BC’s Summary

I am a clinician, consultant, educator, blogger, and serial entrepreneur with over 20 years experience in health care, pediatrics, geriatrics, and psychiatry. Currently, I run CareNetworks.com, a social technology company that offers online community and social media solutions to the senior living, long-term care, and eldercare industries. Essentially I help companies in the senior care space build community using various social technologies, online community platforms and emerging web technologies. My blog, http://carenetworks.com/blog, is where I write about everything related to the convergence of senior living, eldercare, long-term care, and social media.

In addition, I practice emergency psychiatry at Yale-New Haven Hospital, lecture on various topics, and provide clinical consultation to senior living communities, home care agencies, and long-term care companies around the country.

My company, CareNetworks, LLC, helps senior living, eldercare service providers, and other care-related organizations build and nurture online communities for their various constituencies (patients, residents, care givers, family members, staff, referral sources, prospects, business partners, etc.)

CareNetworks provides companies with a simple and cost-effective solution to the complexities of online community, interactive marketing, real-time Websites, and social media; and offers a comprehensive way to organize and manage a company's online presence across multiple channels like Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin and others. It's the easiest way for organizations to leverage the power of the social Web to engage customers and prospects and provide them with 24-hour access to helpful information and support.
