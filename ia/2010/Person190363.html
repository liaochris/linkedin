Marty Sixkiller’s Summary

With over 18 years experience in the computer graphics industry, I've worked on the production side as an animator, the post-production side, as well as the management side as a technical manager. Specializing in people, communication and the feature-length animation pipeline, I have spent 9 years as the Layout Technical Director in our feature animation division. I have a concrete understanding of the layout process for a feature film and how it feeds the animation pipeline, developed integral software processes for interfacing with the editorial process, as well as the computer graphics workflow. Having worked as an Avid editor, I have in-depth knowledge of how Media Composer integrates into a CG workflow. I enjoy a team-oriented production environment that thrives on collaboration. I have high expectations of the people on my team, as well as for myself and enjoy challenges both in life and in the workplace.
