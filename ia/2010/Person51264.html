David Christopher, M.S., MFT’s Summary

Since graduating from U.C.S.D., I have provided over 25,000 hours of face to face therapy in a career that spans thirty years. My work is characterized by empathy, genuineness, and a collaborative spirit. I believe that Life is About Relationships™ and this belief is reflected in my work as a psychotherapist.

My mission is to strengthen relationship skills for my clients and to assist in furthering our ability to more effectively communicate to one another across all of our relationships.

David Christopher, M.S., MFT’s Specialties:

Brief Solution-Focused Adult Psychotherapy; Relationship Therapy w/ Married or Non-Married Couples (Straight or Gay); Brief Support Oriented Counseling w/ Teens

Stepfamily Issues-Substance Abuse Disorders-Depression-Anxiety-Communication & Intimacy-Men's Issues
