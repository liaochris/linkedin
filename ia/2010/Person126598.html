Peng Liu’s Summary

Mr. Peng is an M.S. candidate of biomedical engineering at CMU (expected to graduate in May 2010). He has project experiences in medical device design, FEM/CFD simulation, image processing and software development. He is familiar with FDA guidelines and regulations and has experiences in medical device development. He is also an experienced coder with Java/C/C++/Python/Matlab.
He would like to work in the healthcare related industry (e.g, Medical Device, Healthcare Software) in United States.
