Russell Roberts’s Summary

Mr. Roberts provides counsel and representation to industry, professionals, health care providers, academic and research institutions, and individuals in all areas of U. S. immigration law. Mr. Roberts advises clients in the areas of temporary visas, permanent residence visas (“green cards”), naturalization, I-9 and related human resources issues, investment and job creation visas, establishment of regional centers to attract direct foreign investment, and consults on the impact of immigration law on business transactions. As U.S. immigration law grows more complex, organizations require highly-skilled immigration counsel to guide them in establishing uniform policies, to prepare petitions for foreign national employees, and to maintain compliance with the burgeoning array of regulations governing this field.

Prior to joining Harris Beach, Mr. Roberts was a name partner with Brent & Roberts, a Rochester-based immigration boutique firm. Mr. Roberts is a regular speaker at business, community, and continuing legal education seminars on immigration matters. He is the co-author of Inside the Minds: Employing International Workers, for Aspatore Books, a Thomson West business publication. Mr. Roberts has also practiced commercial and creditor’s rights law, and was the successful lender’s attorney in In re Scarpino (2d Cir. 1997), a decision that prevents bankrupt debtors from discharging judgment liens on after-acquired real property.

Russell Roberts’s Specialties:

U.S. immigration based upon employment, investment, family relationship; Employer compliance including I-9 and electronic employment verification (E-Verify); Immigration issues in business succession; Health care provider immigration matters.
