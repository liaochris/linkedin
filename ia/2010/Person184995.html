Scott Yankton’s Summary

15 year C Level (CEO, COO, CAO, CCO) executive in software as a service (SAAS), eCommerce, retail, wholesale, operations, financial transaction processing, software development, supply chain, Internet marketing, social media, and financial institutions.

Currently the President and COO of Clarinova. Clarinova develops collaborative marketing software that allows manufacturers, distributors, and brand owners to promote their products to consumers by using their networks of independent retailers. Front Window, Clarinova’s flagship product, creates web-sites—called “microsites”—for their stores. These sites facilitate local searches for the brand by guiding consumers to local retailers carrying the brand's products. The sites also display actual inventory at the retailer and allow manufacturers to syndicate product information and promotions to the retailers’ websites. This system delivers the most cost-effective local Web traffic to manufacturers and will produce an extraordinarily valuable database of business and inventory data for those same customers.
