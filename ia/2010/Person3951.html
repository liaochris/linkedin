Amit Golan’s Summary

A versatile and seasoned high-tech professional, Amit Golan has extensive leadership experience in start-ups, SMBs and corporations, in both Silicon Valley and in Israel. A team player with excellent communication, analytical and soft skills, he’s held business management positions and R&D leadership roles. His experience encompasses all phases of the product lifecycle, from new product introduction, through tripling a product’s revenue stream, to managing a mature product with 5,000 customers.

Amit is Vice President of Products at BDNA.
Prior to BDNA, Amit became a part of the HP team following the $4.5B Mercury acquisition. As Product Manager, Amit was responsible for $30M in annual license revenues, managing the creation and execution of the vision, roadmap and strategy of multiple software products.
Prior to his promotion and relocation to the Silicon Valley, Amit held a position of Technical Product Manager. Acting as a link between US-based Product Management and Israel-based R&D, he directed 30 developers working on the release of 5 commercial products simultaneously.
Prior to this, Amit served as Functional Lead, directing the functional design of the product suite's integrations with major partners like SAP and Oracle.
Prior to HP/Mercury, Amit co-led the development of the flagship product for Sabratec. As the start-up’s 10th employee, he was involved in the design and implementation from initial conception to market rollout.
Amit started his career in the Israeli army. Graduating with honors from the officers’ course, he supervised a team of 9 during his commission as IT Manager. His complete overhaul of the IT service delivery processes won him the Excellent Officer Award.

Amit holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Tel-Aviv University and an MBA from INSEAD.
Amit has lived, worked and studied in the US, Israel, France and Singapore. He is fluent in English and Hebrew, and conversational in Spanish.
