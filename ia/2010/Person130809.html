Christopher Manning, CFP®’s Summary

Chris Manning, CFP® is the principal and founder of Manning & Associates Financial Services, a fee-only firm, a firm dedicated to providing effective solutions to select groups of people to help them manage their future life and financial planning issues. Based in Houston, TX, Chris is passionate about the unique issues families, women and retirees often face.

Before founding Manning & Associates, Chris served as the Finance and Accounting manager for a local specialty Property & Casualty insurance carrier where he was responsible for GAAP reporting and maintaining the Treasury relationships.

Chris, in addition to his role at Manning & Associates, is the author The Big Picture. His blog that features his insight and advice on a variety of different topics relating to financial and life planning. The posts are often times full of actionable items that you can take today.

Christopher Manning, CFP®’s Specialties:

Debt Management, Budgeting, College Funding, Guardianship issues, parenting issues (stay at home or work, etc.), retirement income and the unique issues women face (longer life expectancy, lower earning, to name a few)
