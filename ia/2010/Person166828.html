Paolo Werbrouck’s Summary

Highly accomplished Director of Web / Mobile Software Development with 20 years experience creating and optimizing website and application performance. Visionary strategist with ability to collaborate with senior management and technical teams to create total web and mobile software solutions. Known for delivering fast platform environments and leading teams to tune applications and middleware to achieve the fastest throughput possible. Excellent communicator and facilitator with background leading multi-cultural teams in multiple countries.

CAREER ACHIEVEMENTS

Managed the implementation of digital music product for Dada Entertainment in the US market. Transformed a flat social networking site into a vibrant digital music ecommerce destination. Integrated credit card and mobile payments, music intelligence and social media.

Co-founded and acted as chief architect of a start-up company, tipic.com, which created one of the largest and most active blogging communities in Europe. Created platform and directed development teams. The community grew virally over the course of 5 years with no marketing budget and limited IT resources, from zero to 5 million unique visitors per month with over 500,000 hosted blogs. Acquired by Dada SPA in 2006.

Managed IT operations and security for Buongiorno SPA, a leading mobile and email marketing company in Europe. Oversaw operations with 100s of servers distributed in 3 server farms connected to 10's of mobile carriers worldwide. At the peak of the email marketing cycle supervised delivery of over 5 billion emails per year.

Managed multiple projects in matrixed and multi-cultural environments. Directed initiatives with stakeholders in the US and development, QA and release teams in Europe. Developed processes and techniques to ensure clarity of mission and alignment of efforts.

Single-handedly led the professional service organization in Italy for Netscape Communications for multiple years
