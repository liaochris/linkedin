Ronald Rothman’s Summary

“Scientific Insight ... Competitive Intelligence Perspective”

Scientific Advisor and Strategist spotlights the commercial value of biomedical innovation, identifying market opportunities and threats. Communicates the commercial value in language useful to investors, marketers, and other nontechnical business clients.

Insightful perspective of the science and strategy of innovation from extensive and diverse experiences in biomedicine as biopharmaceutical consultant advising on commercialization of pipeline therapeutics and emerging technology; and co-principal investigator providing vision and direction at a leading health-sciences research center.

Scientifically multidisciplined, and trained at top tier institutes. As a problem-solving, accomplished biomedical researcher with a portfolio of innovative discoveries having therapeutic potential, I offer a unique perspective on Life Science issues.

Transformative biotechnology has revolutionized biomedical research. Understanding this revolution, though, requires guidance and insight. Mine has been honed over many years by a combination of unique experiences in biomedical research and competitive intelligence.

Interested in providing insight and guidance on emerging biotechnology, including opportunity assessment, licensing, and scientific communications.
