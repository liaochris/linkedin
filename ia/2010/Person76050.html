Glenn Earls’s Summary

I began a career in the Yellow Page industry in 1993 with GTE Directories. GTE Directories was at that time the largest yellow page publisher in the country.

In the last 25 years I have seen the print yellow page product dramatically decline in usage by consumers and significantly increase in cost to the business owners who advertise. Many Businesses who have been advertising for years in print yellow pages often find themselves with a yellow page program that has increased in cost to the point that it is no longer cost effective to advertise.

Over the last 10 years, utility yellow page rates have more than doubled! At the same time retail charges for goods and services have barely kept pace with inflation.

Businesses know they are spending too much on yellow pages but don't know where to turn or what to do about it and are afraid to make a change that might impact their business. It is because of this, that the end result in most cases is improperly purchased ads and misspent advertising dollars.

Yellow Page directories are just not as effective as they once were. Part of the reason is the internet. The Internet is the advertising and marketing meeting place of the future. The internet works just like the Yellow Pages.

When a Buyer has a need they look for a business to fill that need. Over 67% of buyers research the product or service they are interested in on the internet before they make a purchase! Most businesses when they consider the internet as an advertising medium, can’t see the forest for the trees…
