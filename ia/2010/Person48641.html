Dan Zelikman’s Summary

"We are living in a digital world, and I am a digital girl." I play in all things digital but my focus lies with strategic digital marketing and advertising that provides measurable ROI. What does this mean? I’m not entirely sure, but it has a lot to do with media planning, buying, optimization, and reporting.

Search engine marketing (SEM). From R&D to campaign design and implementation. Various industries, initiatives, and conversion objectives. International and domestic targeting with experience on multiple portals and 3rd party platforms. I can speak too and dabble in elements of search engine optimization but an SEO guy I am not.

Digital marketing and advertising (CPM, CPC, CPL, CPA). On a monthly basis I have overseen millions of dollars in spending under the digital marketing umbrella, optimizing thousands of placements on hundreds of sites. Domestic and multi-national campaign targeting in dozens of countries and languages. Conversions include investment bank account openings, consumer bank accounts, insurance quotes, hotel room revenue, telecommunications sales, luxury real estate leads, e-commerce, and event sales.

Strategic direct response creative design. Promotional copy, banner creation, destination page testing and optimization. Everything can perform better, there is no status quo.

I can communicate in 140 characters or more. I have designed successful social media marketing campaigns that have produced accountable revenue and ROI. I carry social marketing experience in education, campaign design, implementation, and social analytics. I even blog sometimes.

I play well with others. I have worked on numerous campaigns that have aligned digital strategies with traditional marketing avenues that include television, radio, and print advertising. Whatever works.

I scrutinize over analytics. Site, media, search, creative, social media, and mobile. If it can’t be measured, then it better be something really freaking cool.
