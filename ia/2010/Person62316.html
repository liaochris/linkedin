Dr. Edward Lin elin[at]ingenious[dot]com’s Summary

I’m a proactive positive thinker who applies creativity+perseverance to attain uncommon results. Personal strengths include integrity, social conscience and a sincere desire for win-win.

Innovative in pioneering advanced pain-relief after major surgery, and devising technique to obviate transfusions or blood banking, I left clinical medicine on a mission to better the world on a global scale. In 2006, I became an Honoree in the Modern Marvels Invent now Challenge, cosponsored by National Inventors Hall of Fame, Time Magazine & History Channel.

Founder and CEO, Ingenious Technologies Corporation (www.ingenious.com), USA, a think tank research and development company of diverse inventions;

Founder, Medica360, Pharma360 and BeneGreen, think tanks and developers of innovative medical devices, pharmacotherapeutics and green environmental solutions;

Holder of multiple US and international patents and other patent pending intellectual property, providing innovative solutions across diverse industries in medical, telecommunications, automotive and consumer product sectors.

Previous professional qualifications include:
Three years as Henry Woodburn Research Fellow, Roswell Park Cancer Institute, the highest honor bestowed by the State University of New York at Buffalo Graduate School of Biomedical Sciences;

Residency trained and board certified in Anesthesiology; Yale-New Haven Medical Center, Yale University School of Medicine, with additional training in internal medicine and emergency medicine;

Chairman of Anesthesiology at Ohio hospital; Clinical Associate Professor of Anesthesiology, Ohio University College of Osteopathic Medicine
