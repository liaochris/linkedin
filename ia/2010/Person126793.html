Liz Keating’s Summary

A veteran researcher, Liz has been gathering competitive information for many years. As early as 1990, she was a guest of the United States government on several occasions as they began to evaluate the potential of the Internet for academia. Now, as millions of people worldwide access the Internet everyday, Liz remains a leading practitioner both in her research knowledge and as a speaker/seminar leader to businesses throughout the United States.

Liz holds a bachelor's degree in education from University of Colorado at Boulder. She also holds a master's degree in Library Science from University of Denver and a master's degree in Administration from Western Michigan University. She currently lives in Denver where she owns her own company, NetSearch Solutions. She is a founding member of “Competitive Insight,” an alliance of intelligence professionals in Colorado. She is also on the chapter coordinator for the Rocky Mountain chapter of SCIP (Society of Competitive Intelligence Professionals) and a committee member of the Turnaround Management Association.
