Steve Horton’s Summary

Professional writer since 1999 with years of experience writing for high-circulation national magazines, newspapers and websites. I've had articles published in PCWORLD.COM, POKER PRO, WPT MAGAZINE, ALL IN, BLUFF, POKER PLAYER NEWSPAPER, POKERBIZ, CAESAR'S PLAYER, POKERNEWS.COM, PC TODAY, LAPTOP, MOBILE, TECHLIVING, PCWORLD.COM, SCRYE, INQUEST GAMER and ANIME INSIDER.

I've done live blogging and photography for several major poker tournaments. My clients have included TropicalPoker.com, Borgata Hotel Casino & Spa, and PokerNews.com. For the second time, I was part of the official Live Update Team for the World Series of Poker in the summer of 2008. For the fifth time, I covered a major Borgata poker event in January 2010.

I've managed teams of writers and artists through my work in comics e-publishing, initially in the PDF format and then through iPhone applications.

Writer of two books: WEBCOMICS 2.0 from Course PTR, and PROFESSIONAL MANGA from Focal Press.

I'm the co-creator and writer of Image Comics limited series STRONGARM. I wrote the Captain Battle story in Image Comics' NEXT ISSUE PROJECT: SILVER STREAK COMICS #24, the Clickwheel.net comic GAMBLING SOULS and a Captain Marvel short story in the 2009 DC Universe Holiday Special.

I wrote ONE GIANT STEP, a short story in the non-profit benefit anthology READING WITH PICTURES.
