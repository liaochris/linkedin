Ted Vinzani’s Summary

Business Development Consultant for several different types of companies for 7-1/2+ years. 32+ years in sales and marketing.

Business Owners - Are you satisfied with your current sales and profitability?

Relevance Marketing and Sales Prototyping may sound like disconnected disciplines, but they go hand in hand to help small to medium sized businesses increase sales and profits.

Relevance Marketing is marketing that prospects identify with - marketing that causes your prospects to say, "Wow, these guys are talking about me!" If your marketing is all about your products or services, only prospects "hot" for your offerings at the exact moment (a tiny percentage) will relate to it. Relevant messages will be remembered over time.

Sales Prototyping designs and proves the sales cycle, sales methodology, account entry techniques, collateral materials, web tactics, and customer relationship building/maintenance practices for new or revised products and/or services.

If you think your advertising, marketing, and sales efforts have lost traction, we need to talk.
