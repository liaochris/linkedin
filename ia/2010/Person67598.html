Esther J. Cepeda’s Summary

Esther J. Cepeda is an opinion journalist and expert on the issues of U.S. Hispanics/Latinos. She writes about that and much, much more on www.600words.com and on Twitter: @ejc600words


My calling is to represent the country's fastest growing demographic in the mainstream English-speaking media.

Esther J. Cepeda’s Specialties:

Agitating, going against type while being true to myself, writing for regular "Joes."
