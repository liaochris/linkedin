Catalin Tenita’s Summary

Romanian online antrepreneur, age 32, located in Bucharest with 10 years experience in online business, in the development and marketing of large scale online publishing projects expecialy for large media groups, both for the Romanian and foreign markets (US, Japan).

Our client portfolio includes major companies, more than 80% of them being large Romanian media groups like: PRO TV - part of the Central European Media Enterprises, Prima TV – part of ProSiebenSat1 Group, Realitatea TV – no. 1 Romanian News TV station, Mediafax – no.1 Romanian News Agency, Adevarul – no. 1 Romanian Quality Newspaper.

You can read more about our online publishing platform at http://www.treexpress.net

TreeWorks also owns NoPrimeTime.com - a video ad server focused on serving the need of the TV stations that are migrating their content online (on their websites) and need a enterprise-grade solution to monetize their assets via ads inserted in the video content.

Catalin Tenita’s Specialties:

online media and online publishing, online video advertising, publishing content management and digital assets management, project management for online projects, strategic marketing, online development know-how
