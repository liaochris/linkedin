    Eco-Me®, was founded in 2006 by Robin Kay Levine out of a desire to develop environmentally friendly, chemically free cleaning products. Eco-Me products can be found in over 420 outlets throughout the US and Canada. The Company’s core products are DIY (do-it-yourself) cleaning kits designed for Home, Body, Baby, Dog and Cat. The eye-catching Kits appeal to consumers looking for alternative cleaning products that limit the amount of harmful chemicals entering the home.
