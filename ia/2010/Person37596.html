Chris Curtin’s Summary

First President of Florida Turnaround Management Association (TMA).

Previously chaired the International Awards Committee and served on the Executive Committee of the International TMA.

Previously owned a Commercial Finance Company.

Previously owned a manufacturing plant.

Currently on the Boards of the Angel Investment Forum and ACG.

Chris Curtin’s Specialties:

Bankers Advocate specializes in designing successful Exit Strategies for Entrepreneurs. The Exit could be a financial/strategic buyer we find or a controlled liquidity event/transfer to key employees or next generation. We also have a very active targeted Search program were our individual, corporate and Private Equity Clients entrust us to find them businesses to purchase in the "Hidden Market".
