Eric Farst - Sales & Marketing Consultant’s Summary

Eric Farst has spent more than 16 years in direct selling and sales management assignments. His career includes sales and management experience with regional sales companies, national telecommunications firms and international manufacturing organizations. As a career sales professional, Eric combines his disciplined work ethic with his strengths in networking and relationship selling to help companies grow sales territories, build direct sales team and develop effective sales channel programs.

SALES & MARKETING CONSULTING:
Eric provides a unique, personalized approach to working with each of his clients. Whether it is building corporate sales strategies, channel partner or distribution programs, marketing programs, communication plans or business networking, Eric works with organizations to customize a program that will fit the company's goals and objectives.

DESIGN SERVICES FOR BUSINESSES:
Eric Farst operates a marketing design company, helping businesses create an attractive and distinctive brand for their organization. His design team creates custom graphics, logo designs, custom websites, E-zine newsletters and custom print business cards, letterhead and presentation material.

SOCIAL MEDIA TRAINING:
Eric works with companies to help them effectively use the nation's top social media tools to help them grow their businesses. He provide training on applications like LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and many more.

SPEAKER:
Eric Farst regularly speaks at a local college conducting seminars on Goal Planning. His seminars also include Sales 101, Time Management, Matching and Pasting, Developing Relationships, The One Time Sale and Closing Techniques.
