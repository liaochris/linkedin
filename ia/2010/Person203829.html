Tim Frick’s Summary

I am the owner of Mightybytes, a Chicago-based creative communications firm and author of the books 'Return on Engagement: Content, Strategy and Design Techniques for Digital Marketing' from Focal Press and 'Managing Interactive Media Projects' from Delmar Cengage Learning.

Mightybytes creates rich user experiences to tell our clients' stories, brand their products, and meet technology and marketing needs.

I also regularly present at conferences and give seminars and workshops on various aspects of design, media, technology, and marketing.

Tim Frick’s Specialties:

user experience, social media optimization, digital marketing, media, design, technology, brand strategy, information architecture, web video, etc.
