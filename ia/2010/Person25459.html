Bob Herbert’s Summary

My sense of purpose is to bring meaning, order, and completeness in the midst of complex or confusing situations. Whether it is managing client accounts, projects, events or ongoing operations my challenge and success is fueled by my gift to conceptually and innovatively organize large amounts of information pertinent to the issue at hand. As a hands-on manager, I am then able to collaborate and direct all the resources necessary to generate extraordinary results. My gift of organization is complemented by a number of other core competencies including my analytical and problem solving capabilities. Also having a natural propensity to teach allows me to communicate and connect to individuals in a way that brings effectiveness and efficiency to the growth and success of the organization.
