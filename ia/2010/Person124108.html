Leo Watts’s Summary

Results-oriented, high energy, collaborative manager with a demonstrated track record of driving change and operational excellence in various cross functional assignments including Supply Chain Management, Operations (sheet metal fabrication and electrical equipment assembly), Quality, Engineering and Human Resources. Fifteen years of progressive managerial experience, seventeen years experience in Operations (traditional manufacturing, distribution/value-add center and service) roles including ten years of experience in Supply Chain Management (Materials Management, Purchasing, Distribution and Logistics) roles.

Proven ability to successfully analyze an organization’s critical business requirements, identify deficiencies and potential opportunities and deploy innovative and cost-effective solutions that improve customer satisfaction, drive operational excellence and create competitive advantages., Major strengths identified by previous managers include: high integrity/ethics, excellent communication skills, customer focus, strategic planning skills, leading by example, strong work ethic, analytical skills, team oriented, fact based decision maker, adaptability and drive for change/continuous improvement.
