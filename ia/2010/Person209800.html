Michael Fishman’s Summary

16 years of sustainable infrastructure, transportation, waterfront and emergency management experience.

Mr. Fishman serves clients engaged in development and infrastructure projects locally, nationally and internationally. Clients have ranged from , Whole Foods Market, The Brooklyn Navy Yard, Consolidated Edison of New York, Legion Software and The Battery Park City Authority to Madison Square Garden and The Brooklyn Museum of Art.

AREAS OF EXPERTISE:
• Owner / Operator Representation: Maintaining and enhancing client vision simultaneously relieving cost and time restraints.
• Transit Oriented Development: developing public-private partnerships by recognizing landuse and tranportation synergies + adjacencies, within and beyond the project site.
• Pedestrian Engineering: Transportation Planning and design that puts pedestrians first from concept through construction; ensuring safety, clarity and improved levels of throughput and service.
• Design for Disaster: Design for climate change and bring emergency preparedness to communities prone to crisis.
• Community Based Facilitation: Engage community members and project stakeholders to help develop a project's sense of place and sustainability.
• Waterfront Revitalization: Promoting a mixed use, working waterfront for residential, recreational, commercial and tourism.

Traffic Graphics /Pedestrian and Vehicular Access / Site Planning / Water and Landside Transportation, Sustainable Development Strategies / Emergency Management / Construction Staging / Design Mediation / Facilitation
