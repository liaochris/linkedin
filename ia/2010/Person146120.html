Mike Vardy’s Summary

I am an accomplished comedy writer, having founded and written for Victoria-based comedy troupe The 30 Cent Players. The troupe has performed at festivals throughout North America, and performed on CBC Radio nationally in Canada. I have also written humorous commentaries for CBC Radio and am currently writing for my website Eventualism, where I satirize personal productivity systems and websites based on the topic.

I have been performing comedy (improv, standup, sketch) for ten years.

satire, sketch comedy writing, improv, pop culture, promotion
