Michael M. Collarin, P.E., LEED AP BD+C’s Summary

Passed the Professional Engineer's Exam in October 2010 and am a licensed P.E. in the state of North Carolina.

I've worked in the MEP design industry for nearly 5 years now, mostly as a Plumbing Designer. I am proficient in AutoCAD MEP (currently using 2010) and have been experimenting with Revit MEP (2010) as well. I've posted some of my plumbing layouts in the SlideShare Presentation.

My previous experience was working for an HVAC manufacturer and representative who specializes in air distribution equipment for "clean spaces". I handled most of their in-house technical support, created sizing and performance software and oversaw the coordination between manufacturing and shipping. While with another firm, I was briefly contracted to work with the Sterile Operations Mechanical Services group of a major pharmaceutical company.
