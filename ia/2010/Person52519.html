Dawn Bugni’s Summary

Talented writer, with a strong business background, skilled in creating compelling, interview-landing career documents. Adept at uncovering hidden talents and identifying marketable skills. Able to help clients sharpen and refine basic interview skills with accomplishment-focused questioning during initial consultation. Certified Professional Resume Writer – CPRW – and member of the Professional Association of Resume Writers (PARW), Career Management Association (CMA), The National Resume Writers Association (NRWA) and Career Directors International (CDI). Current in existing and emerging career industry trends.

Dawn Bugni’s Specialties:

Generalist -- I work with individuals at all career stages -- recent graduates, retirees starting a second career, people returning to the work force after brief or prolonged absences, up-and-coming professionals, managers -- across myriad industries.

I also work with clients editing biographies, personal statements, doctoral thesis, Web site copy and other types of professional and business correspondence.
