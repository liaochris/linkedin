Andrew Bloo’s Summary

Andrew Bloo currently holds both the title of Chief Operating Officer and Vice President of Online Marketing (Interim) at Jian.com – Tools for Business while also maintaining active interests in several start-ups. Previously, Chief Executive Officer of SciTech Software, a company whose innovative-systems approach to device driver development were instrumental in providing the tools and cross-platform device drivers required by the world’s top hardware manufacturers (IBM, ATI, HP, Intel, ..) and early 3D game developers with products like SciTech SNAP and SciTech UniVBE.

Andrew holds a degree in Information and Communication Studies with an option in Visual Communications from California State University Chico and has been recognized by the SIIA for his software package design work (WinWay Resume). Andrew currently lives in Chico California.

Andrew Bloo’s Specialties:

Strong technical experience managing and implementing e-commerce platforms (Magento, OsCommerce, custom solutions)Hands-on business building expertise, systems based product development strategies, partnership development, sales management, team leadership, product life-cycle management (from concept to business profit center and ultimately building end-of-life revenue opportunities), multi-faceted cost effective marketing strategies.
