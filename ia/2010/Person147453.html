Mike Bergman’s Summary

CEO of Structured Dynamics, which assists enterprises and non-profit organizations and projects to adopt Web-accessible and interoperable data;

Editor of the lightweight UMBEL ontology, a reference structure of subject concepts for the Web;

Previous experience as an independent consultant, a Web scientist, and an entrepreneur;

Founder and/or CEO of four information management software companies; sustained profitability; total of ~ $10 M in venture capital raised

Innovator in alternative energy commercialization in the 1970s to early 1990s.

Mike Bergman’s Specialties:

Software development and management experience: Linked Data and semantic Web technologies, Internet search tools, semi-structured data, data warehousing, bioinformatics, platform-independent user interfaces, accounting, finance, management, planning, electronic mapping and technical areas; accomplished writer; noted speaker.
