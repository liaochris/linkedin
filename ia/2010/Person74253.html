George Ciardi’s Summary

George Ciardi, Head of Volumetric Forecasting and Normative Database at TNS, has over 20 years of hands-on experience in marketing research, R&D, and management. He works closely with clients to develop creative research designs and innovative approaches to new product opportunities. During the course of his career, he has conducted and consulted on 2+ thousand studies across Financial Services, Consumer Goods, Pharmaceutical, Quick Serve Restaurants and Durable Goods within the Americas, Europe (East and West) and Asia.

Since receiving his degree in Economics at Miami University, he has held senior positions with several research and consulting firms, including AC Nielsen, MMA, Delta Information Group, FYI, NFO and TNS. He was founder of Delta Information Group that specialized in Pharmaceutical analytical software solutions, which later became part of FYI.

He is an acknowledged expert in new product forecasting, marketing mix analysis and new product research in general. Of note, he has contributed to the successful launch of more than 100 new products that have generated in excess of $100 million dollars each in sales during their first year of launch. George’s research has been published in market research industry journals. He has presented at industry events and is a member of the Business Week Market Advisory Board.
