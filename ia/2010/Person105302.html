Joe Montgomery’s Summary

Trained by P&G in the art of sales and sales management, I have enjoyed a 25 year carreer selling CPG products to all retail channels on a national level.
I have worked with the best food and confection/snack brokers nationally and am familiar with most of the owners and key personel.
