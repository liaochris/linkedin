Lera Montminy’s Summary

Integrated and non-traditional account manager/marketer. I have a breadth of experience through working at a large, national advertising agency to working and interning at small-medium agencies. I have worked on a diverse set of clients and industries with experience ranging from local accounts to publicly traded Fortune 500 companies and multi-million dollar accounts in retail, quick-service, corporate communication, health care, non-profit and finance.
