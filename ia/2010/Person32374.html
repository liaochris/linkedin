Executive Summary - Carl Bowen

Carl is an exceptional visionary capable of cultivating Search Engine Strategies that raise global brand awareness and market position. Over the last few years, Carl has expanded his search strategies to reach additional consumers via mobile devices, smart phones and PDAs. As a seasoned search engine professional, his mobile media strategies have led consumer product sites to elevated levels of success through broadened global reach via mobile commerce programs. His latest vision is to lead the charge for the best user and mobile shopping experience that includes geo-targeting of consumer relevant, desired ads.

Carl is an MBA with a specialization in eCommerce and has B2B, B2C and Affiliate Partner site experience. With 11 years of search engine experience, he requires minimal supervision and is always prepared to hit the ground running.

From defining campaign strategy to relentless performance optimization and growth, Carl prides himself on being a data-driven decision maker. Working within business parameters to gain management and peer “buy in”, Carl's analytical aptitude combined with his finely tuned relationship management skills have made him a trusted advisor to clients and a valuable mentor to colleagues.

As a Senior Manager for SEM at Ask.com, Carl managed a multi-million dollar online budget and was responsible for every aspect of a campaign's lifecycle: keyword and creative development with A/B testing, bid management, account structure and agency performance management. His special projects included designing in-house software that grew campaigns across each search engine. His role on the Search Engine Team provided effective communications that resolved complex issues with professionalism and expertise while promoting cross-functional synergy.
