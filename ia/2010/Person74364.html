George Kellerman’s Summary

I am a strategist and growth driver for technology companies in the US and internationally. In general management, business development, and strategic advisory roles, I have helped companies such as Dell, Yahoo!, and Experian Interactive generate millions of dollars of profitable new revenue in competitive marketplaces. My leadership has been tested during periods of crisis, significant change, rapid growth, and downsizing, and I have managed a P&L as large as $500M.

With a J.D. degree and early career experience with Venture Law Group, I excel at identifying opportunities, structuring deals, and overseeing fundraising and M&A activity specifically for high-tech companies in B2B, B2C, and D2C markets. As an operating executive, I have led belt-tightening initiatives and put in place processes and operating procedures to ensure efficient, consistent, and compliant business practices.

My international experience includes more than 10 years living and working in Asia, leading market-entry and market-expansion strategies for Dell Japan, Experian Interactive, and Yahoo Japan. I speak fluent Japanese and understand the nuances of doing business within multiple Asian cultures and economies.

In the US, as a senior executive and business advisor to diverse corporations, I have orchestrated profitable alliances, structured favorable licensing deals, created winning go-to-market strategies, and managed strategic relationships with high-profile partners.

Throughout my career I have repeatedly excelled in new roles, operating without a blueprint and setting high standards for innovation, performance, and results. Most recently, I led Dell Japan’s transition into the retail arena and in less than 2 years grew the new channel to more than 20% of revenue while building one of the most profitable countries within Dell Consumer.
