Chad Razmus’s Summary

As a CCIM candidate with over 160 hours of instruction for commercial investment real estate, I have applied the knowledge base learned in the classroom and have helped clients achieve desired investment goals for their real estate assets.

Experience includes helping potential purchasers through all phases of due diligence, including, but not limited to, site plan approvals, environmental issues, surveys, legal description mis-matches, general sketching of proposed new building on vacant land with parking and 3-D drafting for preliminary approval with planner, as well as difficult title issues.

Attention to detail, full knowledge of the closing process, working with third party vendors for due diligence, and keeping all parties on track throughout the complete process of a commercial real estate transaction.

My experience as a professional photographer has aided in graphic design work with InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator. Designer of postcards and contracting professional printers to produce end products.

Professional Aerial Photography since 1999 in the Greater Grand Rapids area.
