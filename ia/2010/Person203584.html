Jim Stone’s Summary

I've always had a passion about my job as a sportscaster, a dream job for many. For years I've communicated that passion to large audiences on both television and radio.

I'm also passionate about development. My ability to cultivate and sustain relationships has served me well over the years. I am a people person, who enjoys being with and working with others on projects...especially fund raising.

My experience, and skills also transfer into a career in Public/Media Relations.

Jim Stone’s Specialties:

Communication skills both verbal, and written. I have had to sell a product for almost 37 years on air. The ability to write, produce, and edit video stories for air. Writing and doing stories for "new media," or the web. Developing contacts and sources mainly through relationships.
