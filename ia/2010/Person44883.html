Crystal Vilkaitis’s Summary

My social media experience (and addiction) started in 2006 when social media was in its infancy. I marketed a local website using social media sites like Twitter, Facebook and Stumble Upon to increase national exposure and sales. Through that experience I realized the power social media had to help launch companies online, and so I started 3 Elements, LLC. I've been exposing companies to their target audiences online using social media ever since, and have been loving it.

Professional experience includes: social media, internet marketing, marketing strategies and consulting for start-ups, small and mid-size businesses, event planning and execution, viral marketing, management of up to 30 people in house and online, website content management systems, creating and helping in implementation of business and marketing plans, strategic business planning, new business development, sales management, public relations including writing press releases, booking speaking engagements, and outreach to media outlets for story coverage.

Crystal Vilkaitis’s Specialties:

Expertise in social media website like Twitter, Facebook, StumbleUpon, Digg, YouTube and Linked In. Creating marketing plans including online marketing and pr strategies. Excellent researcher for finding industry trends, competitive advantages, and products and services for mainstreaming processes.
