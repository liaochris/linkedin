DeAnna Lynn Englezos’s Summary

Ever since I was a little girl, I was a "glass half full" kind of person. I have always been an optimist, a dreamer and able to highlight the "upside" of any situation. I am also fascinated with business and the synchronization of the global world, as well as, any tools that help us connect with our friends around the world.

My company, "Create Your Great Life" was developed to motivate, inspire, and support individuals in creating a strategy to catapult their success to levels they've only dreamed of. I am a true believer that "life is not about finding yourself, life is about creating yourself."

I am interested in partnering with people to achieve outstanding success in their lives, relationships and careers. I believe that every person can be living the life of their dreams...and it can start now!

Everyday through my business and social media outlets, I partner with small businesses, entrepreneurs and passionate individuals to take their lives and business to the next level through one-on-one personal coaching, public relations and social media outreach, editorial placement in print and digital media, events marketing, brand management, personal and professional development. All while accessing and developing systems and procedures to achieve the maximum efficiency, productivity, and profit, the least amount of stress and an environment that is creatively fulfilling, peaceful and forward-thinking.
