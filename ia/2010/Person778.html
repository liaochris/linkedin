Aaron Hall’s Summary

I am an Investment Advisor with broad financial planning experience. The majority of my clients are retired and are relying on me to make sure their money lasts as long as they do.

I began by doing fee-based financial planning with American Express Financial Advisors (now Ameriprise). Then I moved to Merrill Lynch as an asset manager, and now work as an independent investment advisor and as an MBA student.

My academic experience focuses on topics in the fields of Finance, Management, and Statistics. My academic interests include Financial Mathematics, CEO compensation, security selection, and portfolio management.
