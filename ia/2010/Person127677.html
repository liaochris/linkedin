Lori Martinek’s Summary

Lori's experience (agency/client/media) has made her a sought-after branding strategist. As the owner of Presentation Plus (www.pplusonline.com), a national marketing and PR firm, Martinek gets her clients noticed, online and off.

Martinek is an accomplished speaker, author and consultant to CEOs and officials. She is a former president, board member and consultant to chambers of commerce and other non-profits.

Her economic development resume includes projects at the state, regional, county and municipal levels.

Martinek began her career at Foote, Cone & Belding Worldwide in Chicago, where she worked on accounts including S. C. Johnson and Coors. As a national marketing manager for Wickes Lumber, Martinek developed innovative programs for Owens Corning, Black and Decker and Kohler.

Her credits include articles for national publications and bylines in the Chicago Tribune, the Wisconsin State Journal and other papers. Martinek’s first book, Be The Bulb!, was published in 2009 and is available on Amazon.com.

Martinek presented Creating A Winning Online Brand with GoDaddy CEO Bob Parsons as part of the NFL Super Bowl VXII Playbook for Small Business. She travels the country to speak to audiences on branding, social networking, entrepreneurship and economic development marketing. She is a champion of women, minority and Veteran-owned businesses and a mentor to aspiring entrepreneurs. Her appearances include the Veterans Small Business Conference, ASBDC National Conference in Chicago, Governor's Regional Development Conference (2008 - 2010), WomenVenture, the IEDC Leadership Summit, Women Entrepreneurs Bootcamp in Scottsdale, the Greater Phoenix Chamber's Business Expo in 2010 and keynote at the Lake Land College Social Media Conference in Illinois. Lori is also the founder of MindingHerBiz, a mastermind group that works with female entrepreneurs to help them confront and move past the challenges of startup and growth.

Lori Martinek’s Specialties:

Brand development and strategy; strategic planning, exceptional design and marketing collateral development; community marketing and economic development branding and outreach; new business development; digital consulting and reputation management; personal and professional branding strategies that build visibility and credibility.

Lori is the author of Be the Bulb!, which introduces the concept of Attraction Marketing for entrepreneurs, growing businesses and career-minded professionals.
