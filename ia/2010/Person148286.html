Molly Sly’s Summary

The demand for talented individuals experienced in the digital interactive field has grown exponentially. I am on the hunt for digital everyones on the client side and on the candidate side. I have over ten years of experience as a Technical Recruiter and am eager to continue working with and placing people who are passionate about digital/interactive, technology and social media. I am a big advocate of social media and will continue to be a student of it, as things there are changing daily.

The last few months have been a whirlwind so here's what I've been up to. Hire Logic, LLC is a new venture that I’m thrilled to be starting. In these tough economic times, my timing probably isn’t ideal but I had a plan and a dream and I didn’t want to wait. I continue to work with Campbell-Ewald Advertising on a contract basis so don’t be surprised if you get a call from me on their behalf. Finally, I am an Executive Consultant for Rodan + Fields Dermatologists. Again, my timing is questionable but I could not let this ground-floor opportunity to work with the doctors that created Proactiv pass me by. You shouldn’t either. If you are looking for additional sources of income (who isn’t?) and can spare ten hours a week then email me!!

All of these things going on concurrently should solidify one thing. I am a recruiter through and through. I recruit with purpose, passion and integrity. I believe strongly in relationships. In meeting people and developing those relationships. Most importantly I am here to network so please send me a note and tell me about yourself. I accept all invites!
