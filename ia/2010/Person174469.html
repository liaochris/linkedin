Rob Benjamin, CGEIT’s Summary

A hands-on business and technology consulting practice leader, writer, advocate and coach. Experienced in multiple industries and business functions.

Now focusing on sustainable community development, development and improvement of green and sustainable business processes, and improvement of corporate and IT governance in sustainable and socially-responsible businesses.

* Founder of Sustainable Hamilton, a partner in support of sustainable community capital projects in Hamilton Township, in Mercer County, and across New Jersey.

* Developer of the Hamilton Township Municipal Climate Action Plan, a no-regrets municipal strategy for building sustainable community capital.

* Certified in Governance of Enterprise Information Technology through ISACA.

* A published writer and speaker on business and technology alignment, operational and project risks, organizational excellence, and business process management.

* Skilled in identifying, assessing, prioritizing, and managing risks and improvement opportunities in financial controls, business operations, and technology projects.

My target clients are:

* Financial and operational executives in socially-responsible and sustainable businesses, (or businesses that want to achieve these states).

* Municipal governments and civic groups working to build sustainable community capital

Core services:

* Development, support, and public awareness training for sustainable community capital projects

* Analysis, assessment, and Plain Language Documentation of current business and IT processes.

* Redesign and implementation of business and IT processes for maximum efficiency, effectiveness, adaptability, and sustainability

* Assessment and improvement of corporate and IT governance and compliance practices

* Independent due diligence for business cases, vendors, technologies, mergers and acquisitions, and partnerships.
