Ann Douglas’s Summary

OVERVIEW: author; journalist; copywriter; speaker: pregnancy, parenting, health, lifestyle, women's issues

Author of numerous other books on pregnancy and parenting. Total of 28 published books.

Author/creator, The Mother of All® books series (Titles include The Mother of All Pregnancy Books, The Mother of All Baby Books, The Mother of All Toddler Books, The Mother of All Parenting Books, The Mother of All Pregnancy Organizers; all Wiley) and The Mother of All Solutions® books series (Sleep Solutions for Your Baby, Toddler, and Preschooler and Mealtime Solutions for Your Baby, Toddler, and Preschooler; both Wiley).

Columnist for numerous magazines, including Conceive Magazine.

Parenting blogger, Yahoo Canada! and ParentCentral.ca (Toronto Star).

Freelance copywriter specializing in mom-to-mom communications.

On-air parenting columnist, Channel 12 TV, Durham, Oshawa, Ontario.

Mentor, Creative Writing Program, Trent University Continuing Education Department.

Frequently interviewed by/featured in major media in Canada, the US, and the UK. Appearances/interviews have included The Wall Street Journal, USA Today, The Globe and Mail, The Toronto Star, The Globe and Mail, Parenting, Parents, Working Mother, American Baby, Baby Talk, Fit Pregnancy, Pregnancy, ePregnancy, Junior, Today's Parent, Canadian Family, Parents Canada, Good Housekeeping, Better Homes and Gardens, Woman's World, BabyCenter.com, Parents.com, CNN.com, AOL.com, Parenting.com, SheKnows.com, WebMD.com, iVillage.com, CanadianParents.com, CanadianLiving.ca, The Parent Report, Parent Journal.

You can find out more about me by visiting the bio page of my website at http://www.having-a-baby.com/author.htm

Ann Douglas’s Specialties:

research and writing; content creation; author; magazine and newspaper journalist; copywriter; keynote speaker; workshop leader; spokesperson; in-depth knowledge of pregnancy and parenting, mothers and motherhood, and how to develop parent-friendly communications.
