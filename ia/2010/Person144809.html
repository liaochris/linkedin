Miguel Goodbar’s Summary

After graduating in Communications at the University of Buenos Aires, Argentina, Miguel Goodbar attended the General Studies Program at the International Center of Photography in NYC. After its completion, he assisted several commercial photographers on a freelance basis for the course of 2 years.

In 1995 he started working full time at the Equipment Rental Department of FotoCare until 1998, when he was hired by 5th & Sunset Enterprises -a full photo production company with branches in LA and Miami- to create and develop their Rental operation in New York City. The outcome was a definite success, making 5th & Sunset Equipment one of the most
prestigious Equipment Rental houses in NY for the following 6 years.

In 2001 Miguel Goodbar founded “aftersunset,” a photo assistants organization that supported a community of over 200 members, providing access to studios, equipment, training, networking,exhibiting, and many other benefits aimed to help develop their careers as assistants and photographers.

From 2004 to 2008 he worked again for FotoCare Rentals.

As of September of 2008, he has joined Adorama Rentals, now known as ARC - Adorama Rental Co - as the Director of Business Development.

Co-founder of stopassisting.com, an organization led by ARC, Profoto, Resource Magazine and Tribeca Skyline Studios.

Communication and Business Development. Identity and Branding. Photographic & Motion equipment rental expert.
