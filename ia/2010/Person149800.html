Mark Johnson’s Summary

I have over 20 years of commercial real estate property management and leasing experience, including 15 years in health care real estate. In my leadership role at DASCO and the healthcare real estate industry overall, I have managed medical real estate assets in numerous markets across the United States. In addition, I have spoken to industry groups, authored a number of articles, and co-authored a book on healthcare real estate topics.

My present goals include the expansion of DASCO's nationwide property management portfolio in conjunction with supporting the mission of our healthcare clients. I am always interested in networking with fellow healthcare real estate professionals and speaking to groups that are interested in the healthcare real estate industry.

Mark Johnson’s Specialties:

Health Care Real Estate Leasing & Management. Nationwide portfolio management experience working with healthcare institutions, REIT's and institutional investors.
