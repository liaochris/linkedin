Nathan Egan’s Summary

I love new technologies and I love helping people.

When I left LinkedIn to form Freesource, it was just me and the mission was simple: help the businesses unlock the power of all the FREE resources available on the internet.

Today, that mission holds true and we are proud to be recognized as the world’s premier B2B social media sales training company, servicing some of the largest and most recognized brands across the globe!
