Aileen Martinia’s Summary

Interior design is my passion.

Residential spaces:
To me, residential interior design is an extention of the home owner’s personality. I love helping people creating functional and beautiful environments that are unique to their individuality. I also enjoy the challenge in creating them with whatever budget available to me, thus pulling off magical transformation for everyone.

Commercial spaces:
To me, commercial interior design is about creating new experiences. Originality, flair, and drama are things that make a space unique; something to remember, something that stays in memories. When time, knowledge or budget are an issue, I offer a hand in a one-on-one way of working.

Still looking for the right space?
Just ask/tweet me, I know different people who can help you.

Interested in contacting me?
Send me an invitation message or email me at info@levenssfeer.nl. I always respond positively to new linking possibilities.
