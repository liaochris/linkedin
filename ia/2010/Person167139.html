Rachael White’s Summary

Rachael White has over 20 years’ experience in global ports, maritime logistics and transport as a researcher, journalist, editor, conference producer and communications consultant.

She founded Next Level Information Ltd (NLI) in 1999 to provide conference production, event management, marketing & PR services in the maritime sector. In the past 5 years, NLI has also built a growing business in event management for the UK healthcare services market.

In the past 15 years, she has created agendas and recruited executive speakers for leading port and maritime logistics conferences, chaired a number of events and travelled extensively in Europe, Asia, Americas and Africa to meet senior members of industry and government.

Rachael also acts as Secretary General to the Port Equipment Manufacturers Association, a growing association for the global port equipment and technology industries.

Most recently, NLI has partnered with Freshwater Logistics to organise the Cool Logistics conference, bringing together experts from all sides of the business to review trends in global reefer trades and perishable distribution chains from farm to fork and factory to shelf.

In addition to well-honed verbal and written communication skills, Rachael has significant knowledge of the global ports and container logistics industries and contacts with senior management worldwide.

Rachael White’s Specialties:

Business conference research, agenda design & speaker recruitment; full event management services (planning, project management, logistical support, marketing finances); PR and marketing services (copy writing in particular); journalism
