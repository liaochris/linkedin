Scott Wiley’s Summary

Experienced healthcare information technology executive with great experiences/successes both in start-up and Fortune 500 environments having: generated 3rd-party sponsorship of application software and transaction services for 70,000 plus physician practices; developed partnerships with major ISVs to accelerate development and deployment of desktop and web-based applications; motivated and trained 500+ sales reps of a Fortune 100 medical manufacturing firm to sell online application software into their sales, and; managed profitable joint venture LLC's between competitive billion dollar healthcare organizations.

The acquirers and/or strategic investors of these companies include Siemens, EDS, Eli Lilly, Cardinal, CSC, McKinsey & Co, AdvancePCS, Pacificare, BC/BS plans and McKesson Corporation. ($250MM+ in investments)

Scott Wiley’s Specialties:

Launching new products and partnerships; Developing and integrating teams and operational processes post merger/acquisition; Developing strategic teaming, customer & partner relationships within complex business environments; incorporating "Solution Selling" strategies across organizations - from Marketing, Business & Product Development, to Implementation & Support Services. HIEs, Healthcare IT integration strategies, tools, and standards.
