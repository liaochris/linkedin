Heather Flaherty’s Summary

While other BDs are shrinking or closing their doors, American Beacon Partners is growing its network of Advisors across the country.

I am currently recruiting independent and entrepreneurial minded reps from various financial disciplines to join my team and provide innovative financial solutions to their clients.

ABP offers its reps excellent services and back office support, superior technology, competitive commission rates, and profit sharing opportunities. Our organizational structure is built around a PARTNERSHIP with our Advisors, which fosters independent minds working in collaboration to form an alliance of succcess. ABP's parent company, Beacon Acquisition Partners, is a publicly traded company on the Toronto Stock Exchange (Sym BAP).

"Virtual Open House" presentations on who we are and the many benefits of joining ABP are held every Tuesday morning. Contact me for details.

In addition to building and managing my sales team, I am leading the alternative investments arena as liason to sponsors to find the types of investments my reps need; performing marketing material creation and compliance; and assisting in the development, management and growth of ABP as a member of the management team.

I specialize personally in alternative investments designed for high net-worth investors, including Real Estate Syndications, Single-tenant Net-leased properties, Tenant-in-Common and Delaware Statutory Trusts for 1031 exchange strategies, REITS, and Oil & Gas Drilling and Royalties Programs.

I use these types of hard assets with low correlation to stocks and bonds to diversify and mitigate portfolio volatility, take advantage of substantial tax benefits, and potentially generate streams of income for my clients.

Member of REISA and ARC-Insight.
