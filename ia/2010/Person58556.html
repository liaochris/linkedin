Don Zinn’s Summary

My experience set has been focused around two parameters: revenue growth and organizational creation/advancement. As a revenue minded general manager with a laser focus on go-to-market strategies and achieving/growing revenue as quickly as possible, I have founded, transitioned, turned around, and aided many companies in raising the best kind of capital - internally generated working capital (REVENUE). I have also successfully raised over $25 million in VC/seed/acquisition investment.

I apply those skills to executive search work - as EVP of Starpoint Solutions (Starpoint Exective Search as Managing Director ). I learned the art and dilligent process model of retained exective search during the 1990's at Bishop Partners as Susan Bishop taught me the right way to do retained search - the value added process model she taught is now being applied at Starpoint to bring creativity and excellence in execution of senior management retained searches. Combined with my revenue and organizational building expertise, with real hands on experience as a CEO/COO of venture funded early and mid stage companies, this creates a powerful background for doing excellent executive search work.

If you are looking for business leaders to help your company scale to the next level, I can think of nobody in the retained search field who brings more direct hands on experience than I - combined with a strong organization at Starpoint and the resources needed to do great search we deliver just that - great searches that result in finding and placing great leaders.

I have frequently partnered with "product creators" to enable the transformation of a product into a company.

I have served on many boards and have developed a strong strategic sense of business development with an eye on practical tactics to make those strategies realistic and achievable.

Don Zinn’s Specialties:

My focus is organizational growth. As a retained search consultant I help my clients by applying hands on expertise in finding the right people. Most searches fail because there is too much focus on skills/experiences & not enough focus on behaviors /cultural fit. We fall in love with resumes and forget we need to live with that new hire every day - their card will bear the name of our company. My expertise is in finding the right match - one you can live with long term.
