Clark Gilder’s Summary

CEO and founder of GSF, we have built an innovative electronic payment product around our patented paperless check technologies or Electronic Payment Orders (EPO) as defined in a recent Fed whitepaper. GSF's "Digitally Originated Checks" or DOCs will provide a next generation payment product for individuals and small business AP/AR which utilizes the Check 21 "electronic check" standards (X9.37). GSF products will serve a wide range of markets including OLBP, EBPP, e-commerce and P2P electronic payments. Our broad patented technologies are the foundation for the business. GSF utilizes the executive level software skills developed during my 12 year tenure at Microsoft in Redmond WA, working across the entire software eco-system. Finally, GSF has leveraged my angel investing activities and is supported by world-class banking advisers which have been helpful in giving GSF a head start in a complex, slow moving and heavily regulated banking and payments marketplace.

Clark Gilder’s Specialties:

My 25 years of software management experience provides leadership skills in product management, design, development management, sales and marketing, working with industry analysts and PR firms, evangelism, managing 3rd party ISVs and channels, product training, and customer support. I've also leveraged my angel / VC investment experience with ATA. GSF's business plan is focused to deliver ground breaking technology which will provide a sea-change of innovation into the payments industry.
