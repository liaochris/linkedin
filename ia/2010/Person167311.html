Rachelle Love Jones, MBA’s Summary

Rachelle Jones, MBA is a lifestyle event designer. So FabuLuxe!® is a focus driven innovator of luxurious event designs. We specialize in the art of creating beautiful couture invitations and designing fabulous lifestyle events for every celebration.

With over 15 years of experience in marketing, public relations, and producing special events, Rachelle has pursued her passions and creativity by offering an online-luxury boutique featuring custom designed invitations, fine papers and highly-personalized services and that's what differientiates So FabuLuxe!®

Our passion is delivering environments for entertaining that exceeds our client’s expectations. We add value to those we serve, by offering the luxurious gift of time. As a premier event design firm, we limit the amount of engagements we accept allowing our designers to provide highly-personalized services. Our unique niche is our ability to utilize fabulous embellishments creating a memorable couture experience.

So FabuLuxe! Is all about offering a “couture experience” from designing a classy invitation to creating a savvy atmosphere, it’s what sets So FabuLuxe! apart from its competition. Every invitation we create is a work of art! So Fabuluxe! offers top-quality, hard to find papers, luxury designs, letterpress and embossed printing as well.

Rachelle is passionate about people, serving others and helping them to achieve their goals. As a recent graduate student, Rachelle received her Master's of Business Administration Degree (MBA) in Marketing from University of Phoenix, 2008 and a B.S. in Business Management from University of Phoenix, 2005.

ISES, PRSA, NBMBA, a Board member of Women Encouraging Women and supports Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation and other charitable foundations.
