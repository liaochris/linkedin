John B. Savage, EMBA - OneAccord NFP’s Summary

John B. Savage, EMBA is a co-founder of the Not-For-Profit Practice of OneAccord LLC. He serves as a OneAccord-NFP Principal, and has been working and partnering with NFP executives and organizations for nearly 30 years.

The NFP Principals of OneAccord help not-for-profit organizations, ministries, and churches make successful transitions in leadership and increase revenue to fulfill their mission and realize their vision.

Over the years, John's clients have included entrepreneurial start-ups to organizations celebrating their 100th anniversary. This includes private schools, foster care agencies, rescue missions, large and small church boards, family and church foundations, camps, overseas ministries, hospitals, inner-city agencies serving the poor, as well as faith-filled family businesses - and their leaders - among others.

John currently serves on the teaching faculty of City Vision College, is an Independent Study Advisor in Leadership for Bakke Graduate University, serves as a Planning Commissioner for the City of Des Moines, Washington and Chairs the Board of Des Moines Creek Classical Christian Academy.

He is a columnist for InSite Magazine and the Publisher of Boardwise, a bi-monthly newsletter on Stewardship and Leadership for board members of Christian ministries in North America.

He can be found teaching fundraising, leadership, and board development at various national conferences including the Christian Leadership Alliance, Engstrom Institute's Stewardship Leadership Summit, Christian Camping & Conference Assoc, Strategic Leadership Institute, Council of Christian Colleges and Universities, Assoc of Christian Schools Intl, Mission America, and the Assoc of Gospel Rescue Missions, among others.

John is most often retained to help clients grow their revenue and transition through changes in leadership.

John B. Savage, EMBA - OneAccord NFP’s Specialties:
