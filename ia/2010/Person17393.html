Ashleigh L. Womack, CLTC’s Summary

Commitment to Excellence ~
As a New York Life agent, Ashleigh is committed to providing individuals, families, and businesses with life insurance and financial products and services that meet their needs.

By keeping up with current insurance and financial product trends and offering outstanding service, she has earned a reputation for integrity and for showing true concern for her clients.

Ashleigh has been a resident of Savannah, GA for six years, originally from Statesboro, GA. She is a member of the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors, an active volunteer for the United Service Organization, Founder of the social networking group PRO Women of Savannah, and a 2008 international finalist for eWomen Network's Emerging Leader Award. Ashleigh enjoys history, travel, and exercise, and can be found participating in local community events.

Personal Mission Statement ~
In our fast-paced and ever changing financial world, my mission is to help build, protect and preserve capital. I assist my clients in the three cornerstones of financial security - Retirement, Protection and Accumulation.

At New York Life Insurance Company I have a passion for helping people and a conviction for doing what is right for my clients. I work with my clients to achieve the financial success and goals they desire through the building of relationships, trust and peace of mind, as well as maintaining the highest level of service and integrity.
