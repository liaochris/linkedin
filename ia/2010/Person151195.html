Nancy Daley’s Summary

My goal is to continue to work with top-level professional companies, agencies and organizations to develop plans that work, projects that flow, and clients that rave about the results we achieve together.

I love the creative process and respect the crafts of copywriting, design and creative direction. I also understand that creatives are most empowered when they have the time and budget they need to do good work.

I have a solid understanding of project management principles and approaches. More importantly, I am an expert in the art of problem solving. I find ways to "work the system" so that vendors, clients, creatives and CFO's give us what we need, get what they need and stay civil during the process.

I am ready, willing and able to help you manage multi-phased projects of any level of complexity.

I plan, scope, schedule, resource, drive and actively manage projects across departments, from start to finish, to achieve our collective success.

I have a passion for great work and an understanding of how to most efficiently utilize individual and team strengths to maximize our collective abilities to get the results we all want.
