Travis Norman’s Summary

ReactionNow offers a comprehensive approach to social media campaigns tailored for any organization, brand, or business.

Assessment:
Thorough analysis of client goals within social media and associated web 2.o opportunities to determine appropriate channels and tactics. Detailed base measurements taken of any and all existing efforts within these channels as a means of providing eventual metrics and reports detailing progress made and quantifiable ROI.

Strategy:
Determine appropriate channels for deployment. Create a timeline of action items necessary to reach the goals determined during the Assessment.

Optimization:
Utilize keyword focused copywriting within blogs, status updates, and social profiles to generate organic SERP rankings and make the most of available text spaces. Aggregate and share recurrent optimized content with members of a client’s social network. Introduce efficiency methods including Pixelpipe, Ping, Siesmic, Hootsuite, TweetDeck and mobile applications.

Maintenance:
For those businesses interested in selecting individuals from within the organization to receive training, seminars are available to teach best practices and policies to insure that employees are participating in a manner consistent with established brand guidelines. As an alternative, we also offer options for ongoing social networking account maintenance featuring laser targeted demographic identification and authentic community interaction – tactics proven to expand the reach of a client’s existing public relations, customer service, and marketing endeavors.

Travis Norman’s Specialties:

Due to a growing awareness amongst business owners of the need to participate in social media channels as a way to connect with existing and potential demographics, coupled with the fast pace of progress and emerging capabilities within fields related to internet marketing, Reaction Now is well positioned to provide in demand consultation services to a wide array of businesses.
