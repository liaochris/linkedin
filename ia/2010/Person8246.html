Randall Agee’s Summary

Allshore Global Resources, LLC is a United States owned and operated outsourcing company based in Norman, Oklahoma. We help small- to medium-sized businesses realize substantial savings from employee and overhead expenses by providing them with a highly talented and professional workforce outsourced from leading BPO hubs around the globe.

Once you, our most valued US client, provide us with your list of IT-related human resource needs, we scour our manpower database of web and software development professionals to find the perfect solution to your personnel requirements. We even go to extent of involving you in the screening process, allowing you to select from a prescreened list of highly qualified candidates, to ensure you get exactly the competencies you require to get the job done. Once your perfect full-time Allshore outsourced employee is onboard, you get to sound off your ideas and requirements directly to him/her and track progress in real time. This way, you get what you need in the manner you want it.

When you hire an Allshore professional developer, you also get a support team dedicated to fulfilling your needs - an overseas Project Manager and a US Account Executive - at no extra cost! That’s three people watching out for you, striving to fulfill your needs, for a fraction of your current manpower costs!

We stand by the exemplary service we provide. To make it worth more your while, after you have tried us and are delighted with our services, should you refer us to your own network of entrepreneurs seeking to address the same need you had before you met us, Allshore Global Resources will pay you if your referred client continues past the 1st month trial period! When the client you referred reaches the end of the 2nd month - as we are most certain he/she will - we will send you a check just for helping us grow!
