Mik Quinlan’s Summary

From a ‘Big 4’ consulting background and with over 10 years in industry, Mik has extensive experience of managing, architecting and implementing major software development projects through all stages of traditional, RAD and Agile software development lifecycles. Mik has had pivotal roles in leading the architecture and design of large scale implementations using Agile methodologies. Mik’s hands on approach and delivery focus is combined with attention to detail and is supported by a deep understanding of designing and implementing highly transactional systems. His extensive experience of Agile software development methods (Scrum, XP, DSDM) enables him to perform the crucial role of Agile mentor to both technicians and the business, turning Agile theory into effective practices with visible ROIs.

Mik Quinlan’s Specialties:

Roles: CTO, Lead Architect, Agile coach (SCRUM, DSDM, XP).

Experienced in architecture of scalable, high performance, enterprise solutions in the financial, gambling, media, telco and utilities sectors.

Full lifecycle experience specialising in development and performance testing.

Skilled in all aspects of Java technology (J2ME, JEE, Java 5+), application servers (WebLogic 5 to 9, Tomcat 5+ etc.) and industry standard frameworks (e.g. Hibernate, Spring etc.).
