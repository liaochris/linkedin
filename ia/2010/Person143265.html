Michael Patino’s Summary

Marketing Manager/Web Developer/Community Manager specializing in the development of Internet Communities & Online Social Networks, and their promotion.

What separates me from the crowd is my expertise in using a proactive form of community management through the implementation of targeted data mining tools and CRM technology. With this system, it is possible to identify influencers and evangelists who live "off the grid" from normal community efforts, and even find potential PR/CS disasters within minutes of their posting (be it on twitter, facebook, or even some random blog/forum).

It is possible to expand your brand's current outreach efforts outside of your existing community/marketing structure, feeding Customer Support, Technical Support, Community, and Marketing/PR agendas with relevant information in real time. With the right tools, we can increase a brand's competitive edge within the social market while simultaneously encouraging customer retention and loyalty, all while providing a constant stream of metric data to prioritize incoming issues and the attention they are receiving through company assets.
