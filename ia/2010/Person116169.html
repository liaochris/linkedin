Kevin Flores’s Summary

With nearly 20 years of professional experience in the interactive media industry, I have worked with start-up companies to large corporations on brand, marketing and training initiatives. Throughout my career, I have acted as a cross-functional team leader directing diverse teams of account reps, project managers, content producers, courseware developers, engineers and programmers, creative designers, audio/video production personnel, and QA testers.

Since 2007, I've been working with a global team of designers, engineers and content producers on a large-scale social network/media website, Dipdive. As Product Manager, I oversee design and usability, determine and prioritize requirements for feature development and launch schedule. I also act as liaison for: in-house and partner content that launches on the website and via mobile delivery, corporate brand sponsor relationships and viral marketing efforts including pushes to external social networks.

In 1994 I co-founded an interactive multimedia company, Lightspeed Interactive, Inc. The company grew successfully for 6 years with a small, but effective production team. From the business end, I managed day-to-day operations including facility, personnel and financial management. From the production end, I led development on a majority of projects.

In 2000, we redirected the company focus from multimedia services to software development. Based on our company history and new business plan, we secured $11M in venture financing and acquired intellectual property in the form of a content management system product, engineering team and client base from Crystal Software, a Xerox Corporation subsidiary. Thereafter, two additional acquisitions were made: an Internet firm in Atlanta and an XML software product and engineering team in Silicon Valley.

With a desire to return to interactive media, I chose to begin consulting for new clients with a team of designers and developers as an Independent in 2002.

Kevin Flores’s Specialties:

Online community and social media website design and development; user interface and experience design; interactive marketing presentations; online training and rich media applications using Flash, video, animation, and narration
