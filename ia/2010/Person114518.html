Ken Maynard’s Summary

Successful at driving organizational change, having designed and implemented Lean Six Sigma with executives and senior leaders at GE, The Coca Cola Company, Caterpillar, Nortel and others. Coached these organizations to continually improve the business. Recognized for the ability to accelerate the rate of improvement and establish industry best practices for driving results.

Expert in producing results system-wide as measured by system level indicators that cover financial performance and drivers of customer satisfaction. Train and coach executive leadership to establish the top priority measures, and then deploy the strategies and tactics to drive the desired decreases in costs, increases in revenue and improvements in customer satisfaction.

Expertise in various industries, successfully implemented LSS in Service, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Telecommunications, Supply Chain and R&D organizations. Worked with over 30 Fortune 500 companies and have proven ability to adapt the approach to the nature and needs of the business.

Hands on experience leading strategic projects, having led over 20 client projects. These projects resulted in significant financial impact as well as served as the model for future projects in the business. Project benefits range from $1MM to $10MM per project, conservatively.

Successful at mentoring and coaching with over 20 years of experience in training, coaching and mentoring senior leaders, Master Black Belts (MBB’s, internal consultants and change leaders trained in advanced improvement methods, change management and leadership skills) and Black Belts (BB’s, project leaders trained in analytical improvement methods and team leadership) in the industry recognized methods of Lean Six Sigma. Trained over 100 MBB’s, 1,000 BB’s and directly coached over 200 projects. Project benefits average $200K per project.
