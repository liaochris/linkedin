Steve Cassidy’s Summary

Steve’s roots can be found in the Volunteer State. He was an offensive lineman for Tennessee Tech and was later training with the Dallas Cowboys. After a brief stint in the NFL, Steve took on a bigger challenge - U.S. Navy Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD).

As founder of SGC Group, a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business specializing in IED and WMD manufacturing and training, Steve continues to look for the best way to serve SGC Group clients.

SGC Group's clients include Advanced IED Defeat, NAVSCOLEOD, NSWG2, DHS and many EOD commands. Steve and the staff of SGC Group is available globally for Readiness and Training and Operations.

His fourteen-plus years included a worldwide range of experience: Documenting WWII unexploded ordnance in Papua New Guinea, Explosive Incident Countermeasures in Pakistan, IED countermeasures in Africa, Conducting humanitarian demining operations in Bosnia, capturing war criminals in the former Yugoslavia as a member of NATO’s QRF and was a part of the United States Secret Service advance team for both President Clinton in Warsaw, Poland and Madame Secretary Albright in Jerusalem and Palestine.

A relentless networker and member of numerous business and civic organizations. An avid reader and personal development student, his interests are as diverse as his career background, including his roll as Managing Partner of SGC Group, LLC.

Steve Cassidy’s Specialties:

IED manufacturing and IED training. EOD and UXO operations, Radiation Dosimeters, Security and Access Control, Blast Mitigation, Private Investigator.
