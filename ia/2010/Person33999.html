Catherine Glynn’s Summary

With two MFA's, over two hundred theatrical and commercial credits to her name, and ten years of teaching experience, five of which have been dedicated to executive coaching Catherine has a multi-faceted career. She is a professional actress and an executive speech coach. Although the majority of her theatrical work has been in the Chicago area over the past few years, she is continually seeking to branch out into more regional work.

She also excels in on-camera work (commercials and independent films), voice overs and print modeling. In the past five years alone she has been in national spots for Kitchen Aide, Sears, Coors Beer, Walmart & Disney, and she has been the national spokesmodel for Stella brand cheese since 2006.

When it comes to her executive coaching career, it is her passion for clarifying and honoring the spoken word that she brings to the table.

Catherine takes great care to develop curriculum that is specific to each client. She is considered to be an exceptional listener, with a compassionate demeanor, but she's also a coach who tells it like it is and gets her clients to step up their game with each session.

Her ultimate goal when coaching her clients is to help them discover and strengthen their true voice on both a literal and figurative level.

Her greatest desire in life is to create compelling characters on stage and screen; generate confident mind sets, robust physical demeanors, resonant voices with her clients; and design beautiful objects and environments that reflect people's true power and purpose in life.

  • Co-Director

    Voce Veritas, LLC

    (Partnership; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    November 2008 — Present (2 years )

    Catherine Glynn and her husband Jeremy van Meter are the Co-Directors of Voce Veritas, LLC. (Voce Veritas means "true voice" in Latin. ) They specialize in one on one, group presentation coaching, accent reduction, leadership/gravitas training, interviewing skills and image consulting.

    Catherine believes everyone has an innately powerful voice, both literally and figuratively speaking. By listening carefully to each client's story and applying the techniques that she learned as an actor, cultivated as a teacher at UT Austin, and honed as an executive coach over the past five years, she helps her clients explore and expand their voices in a safe and confidential setting.

    Catherine's creative communications consulting combines the best executive coaching practices and blends them with the creative aspects brought forth through her extensive training as a performing artist.

    Some of the areas she is currently branching out into are: life & career coaching, speech writing, and helping emerging companies host innovative networking events.

    All the work she does can be done in person, on the phone or virtually via on-line sessions.

    So whether you are in the Chicago-land area or abroad, Catherine is delighted to help take you to the next level with your communication and networking skills.
