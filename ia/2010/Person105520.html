Joseph Rubino’s Summary

Joe has been shooting films, commercials and corporate work since 2004 and also has had several award winning films in top festivals. He is dedicated to making groundbreaking productions and raising visual standards. At the age of 18 he started his own videography company in Chicago while also attending Columbia College. His cinematography training and experience has helped him bring a new dynamic vision to everything he shoots. Joe is trained in nearly all HD/SD pro cameras and many film cameras. He also has a keen eye for lighting and creative composition. He can be seen meticulously checking light with his sekonic light meter on location making sure it is perfect. Joe stops at nothing to get the shots needed. He is not afraid to get his hands dirty and to go above and beyond to capture breathtaking shots. Joe is a passionate cinematographer but most importantly he loves being one.
