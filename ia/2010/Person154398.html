Nick Caretta’s Summary

I'm currently studying my undergraduate at the University of Minnesota- Carlson School of Management. My intended majors are Supply Chain/Operations Management and Entrepreneurial Management. I recently accepted another summer internship with Target Corporation as a Business Analyst Intern. Upon graduation, I hope to continue my interests in distribution or possibly pursue green operations as a LEED accredited professional.

I am an Alpha Epsilon brother of professional business fraternity, Delta Sigma Pi and also a member of the Undergraduate Supply Chain Club. Other interests include being a Welcome Week Leader for incoming Freshmen students and taking part in both intramural indoor soccer and ultimate frisbee.

Nick Caretta’s Experience

    (Privately Held; Sporting Goods industry)
