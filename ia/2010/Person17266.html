Ashby Green’s Summary

Ashby Green has more than 14 years of experience in management, accounting & finance. His experience includes auditing, financial management, mergers and acquisitions, and litigation advisory consulting (extensive modeling). Ashby joined TSYS Loyalty in 2005, serving as the vice president of finance. He is actively involved in the development and maintenance of client relations as well as vendors and has built a strong reputation with both working with client services and the purchasing department.

Ashby holds a bachelor’s degree in Accounting and a master’s degree in business administration from Mississippi State University.

Ashby is currently Treasurer of the Indian Hills Civic Association, a community of 1,600 homes with over 5,000 residents. He is happily married to MacKenzie Green.
