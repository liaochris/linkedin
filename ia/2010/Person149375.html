Brian Elmore’s Summary

Brian Elmore has served as a world builder and texture artist for TWU's Second Life project since its inception in mid-2007. He earned a BFA in Graphic Design from the University of North Texas, and is currently pursuing a Masters degree in International Game Production Studies from Gotland University, Sweden.
