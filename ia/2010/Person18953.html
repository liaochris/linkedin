Steve Scott’s Summary

I have always enjoyed creating, imagining, building and improving things. I enjoy looking at things from a different perspective and no matter how good something might be I enjoy contemplating ways to make improvements.

This is why the Audio Visual Business Entertainment industry is the perfect outlet for those with an entrepreneurial mind and spirit. If the thought, “imagine it - create it” appeals to you, then you might consider this booming industry.

Consider the following:

If you feel rewarded from helping others while your own life improves as a result, this can be a great field of work.

If you enjoy business travel, but don’t like the red-eye or enjoy the airport-hotel-airport routine this industry allows you the freedom to choose when, where and how you travel.

AV Business Entertainment is project driven versus time-clock driven. With today’s communication tools, planning events and creating entertaining ways to deliver a message can easily be done from a virtual office – whether at home, a hotel, a coffee shop, yes even a golf course, or anyplace else you can imagine, you can be productive in this industry with the right tools.

The Audio Visual Business Entertainment industry is ripe with talented professionals who wake up each day excited about being challenged with an entirely new and creative project, something that stimulates their mind and allows them to build, create and improve things – all the while, helping others and themselves enjoy better more successful lives.

For my part, I’m just glad I took the leap and I am grateful that I can help so many others do the same.
