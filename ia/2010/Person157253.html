Oliver F. Lehmann, PMP, PPM.APMP’s Summary

After more than 10 years experience running and supporting projects in the engineering/processing business with a focus on automotive, I started my private business in 1995 as a trainer.

In 1998 I became a member of PMI, an organization whom I served since in various positions, currently as the Vice President Members of the PMI Munich Chapter.

I obtained my PMP certification in February 2001.

I passed the sequence of APMP certifications in 2007 and 2008 and finally obtained the PPM.APMP and trainer certification.

Oliver F. Lehmann, PMP, PPM.APMP’s Specialties:

I am here to help professionals in the project management field to become PMP certified.

I am also prepared to help future PMP prep trainers to start their carreer and gain benefit from the years of experience I have collected in this field.

An example for my support are the 250 (75 + 175) free PMP style sample questions to be found at http://www.oliverlehmann.com.
