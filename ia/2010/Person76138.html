Glenn Stebbings’s Summary

More than thirty-five years of experience dedicated to international and domestic transportation industries providng insurance products and services, including underwriting, claim adjustment, recoveries, risk management, and marketing.

A noted speaker at trade association conferences.

An extensive network of contacts within every area of the insurance industry and trade community.

Developed Nationwide Insurance and Risk Management Program "Broker Shield" for domestic logistic operations which provides all lines of coverage required, contract review process, risk management service, claims control and claim management and adjustment support.

Designed and instituted nationwide insurance training program for fortune 500 companies involved with international freight forwarding in providing cargo insurance to their customer base.

"Freight Insurance ASAP" was launched in 2005. This program is an online cargo insurance application that allows truckers, shippers, and domestic freight brokers to purchase instant spot cargo coverage online, on a shipment by shipment basis. The purpose behind the web based application is to make the process of obtaining quotes, and binding instant excess cargo insurance coverage simple and efficient. This is filling a void where excess spot cargo insurance can be instantly purchased online when the limits of a high trucker's Motor Truck Cargo policy are not high enough to respond to the total value of the shipment.

Global Solutions Insurance Services has been featured in the August 1998 article of "American Shipper."

A published author in logistics trade journals.
