Bert van Dijk’s Summary

More than ten years experience in business and on line financial reporting. Specialised in the economics of technology-businesses.

As of September 13th 2007 I became a China-correspondent based in Shanghai covering business, social, media, technology and financial topics.

Currently I'm working on a book about the watercrisis in China, which will be published next year. I will be traveling along the Yellow River to document the consequences of droughts, melting glaciers, pollution, urbanisation and the solutions the Chinese governement is implementing.

Any (Chinese) experts, organisations or companies that are involved in these issues and think they can contribute to my project, feel free to contact me.
