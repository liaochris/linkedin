Omeed Salashoor, CMPS®’s Summary

It finally happened! A mortgage company has emerged who has the capital, commitment, untarnished brand and the best sales and operations leadership in the business. Having been in the mortgage industry since 1990 and having had the opportunity to work with multi-nationals as well as local organizations, I can say that I have never been more excited about the future as I am today!

Our Management team truly cares about our clients and team members and is committed to robust communication, strong field support and culture. Our branch leadership is a three-pronged approach and is comprised of a non-producing branch manager, non-producing branch operations manager and a producing branch sales manager.

My position as a non-producing branch manager offers me the rare opportunity to focus and hone in on my Loan Officer’s needs and to help our originators grow their business. I partner with my Loan Officers and offer my support through coaching programs, business development, industry expertise as well as advanced sales training and ideas.

Our team structure enables our producers and operations staff to concentrate on what they do best…taking amazing care of our client and ensuring that the American dream of homeownership is delivered on-time and as promised.

With a 2 to 1 loan officers to processor ratio, our in-house application-to-funding operation model enables us to give each file the utmost attention. This process ensures that we retain maximum control over the transaction from start to finish. Almost all of our production never leaves the Lake Union branch.

If you are an experienced mortgage professional who is looking to be a part of a dynamic and proactive culture, I encourage you to contact me directly to find out how we can add value to your bottom line while improving your work / life balance.
