Peter Collins’s Summary

Peter is a PR agency executive/veteran who has worked with hundreds of clients over the course of his nearly 25-year career. Peter specializes in corporate, consumer marketing, health care and financial clients demanding branded results.

Over the years, there have been a variety of great projects. One of the more formidable media-genic events that he helped create and organize was the St. Joseph's Heart Health Hop, which was held at the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

The St. Joseph's Heart Health Hop generated tens of millions of branded impressions and sales for the Baby Boomer brand. It was also named by the Guinness World Records as the "World's Longest Dance Party."
