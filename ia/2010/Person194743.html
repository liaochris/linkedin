Steven Tremko Bellante’s Summary

Graduated from Marist College in 2007 with a Bachelors of Science in Information Systems and in 2008 with a Masters of Management in Information Systems (MIS). While at attending college as an undergraduate, I worked at NXP Semiconductors where I developed and implemented an entirely new web-based ordering system. I switched jobs in graduate school and began work at Macquarie where I managed and implemented IT disaster recovery planning for all 18 North and South America sites.

I now work at Aetna in the Leadership Development Program where I rotate throughout the IT organization of the company through a different department every 8 months. During these rotations, I act as a project manager for various IT projects specific to that current department. My past two rotations have given me plenty of engineering work as well. The purpose of this program is to build strong leadership skills while giving you exposure to many different parts of the company so that one can graduate into a final position that meets the goals of both Aetna as well as myself.

Looking into the future I plan to continue my career within the field of IT focusing more on project management as I continue onward. It is important to me that I continue to keep my technical IT knowledge strong, which is why I will continue to pursue Cisco certifications and additional education avenues. I also have a strong interest in entrepreneurship and business startups, which is something I hope to pursue further at some point down the road in my career.
