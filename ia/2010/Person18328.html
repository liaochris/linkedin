Atul K Rathore’s Summary

Atul is a TOGAF certified Enterprise Architect with over 20 years of real world IT experience with exceptional track record of turning vision into achievement. He has extensive experience in stakeholder management, cross functional client engagement, leading subsystem architects for multi-million pound projects with the role of mentoring and governance, bringing delivery under control, defining the strategic direction and making the tough decisions. He is accountable for the overall architecture, technical risks, IT strategy, technology change and sub contractor engagements.

Atul is with British Telecom for over 5 years as an Enterprise Architect for Spine one of the world’s largest public sector project NPfIT from NHS CFH, Department of Health, UK. He is a specialised SOA architect for integration and design of Messaging, Business Intelligence and Data Warehouse applications on Java enterprise platform for one of the highly complex and secured messaging system with one of the largest transactional database with very high availability/volume/performance and reliability.

He is a Security Cleared professional and has BT architectural accreditations in “Architecture Conformance Framework” and “Cloud Computing Bronze Level”.

Atul is a SOA Certified Professional and Consultant. He is Oracle (Sun Java) Certified Enterprise Architect with 6Sigma and Prince2. He is a chartered fellow of the British Computer Society, the highest and most senior grade of professional membership of the society. His high level of professional experience is evident from subsequently awarded titles of European Engineer and Chartered Engineer.

He is M.Sc in IT from University of Liverpool UK, engineering M.Tech and B.Tech (honours) from IITK the best technology institute of India.

Atul K Rathore’s Specialties:

An Architect with the real world experience along with high qualifications, certifications based knowledge and titles based competence ensures downstream interoperability, expedites return on investment, mitigates risk on projects and allows investing in Enterprise Architecture with confidence.
