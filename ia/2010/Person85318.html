Mark Nodzon’s Summary

=== Overall Experience Summary ===
Since 1998: extensive experience in traditional and online marketing and bridging synergies between traditional marketing practices (direct marketing, promotions, creative and branding) with online marketing (SEO, SEM, pay per click, social networking, link building, copywriting).

=== Creative ===
Desktop publishing & advertising (online advertising, e-mail blasts, newspaper/magazine/journal ads and tradeshows), and Linux server management and administration using cPanel and WHMx.

=== Social Media & Networking ===
Further skills include blogging and social media, utilizing tools that include Wordpress, FeedBurner, BlogSpot,Technorati, StumbleUpon, Twitter, Facebook/Facebook Pages/Facebook Groups, Myspace, Squidoo, Hubpages, Mixx, FriendFeed, Digg.com and a multitude of other services.

=== Affiliate Marketing ===
Extensive experience in affiliate marketing and publisher advertising programs since 2007 utilizing Google AdSense, eBay Publisher Network, Amazon Affiliates, eJunkie.com, Commission Junction and ClickBank.

=== SEM ===
Extensive experience managing corporate Pay-Per-Click accounts utilizing Google AdWords, Yahoo Search Marketing and MSN adCenter (Bing.com), keyword research & implementation and PPC optimization. Knowledge of the implementation and maintenance of local business marketing tools including Google Local Business, Yahoo Local Marketing, YellowPages.com, CitySearch.com, Yelp.com and database integration service Localeze.

=== Internet Business Law ===
Full knowledge & understanding of business law in respect to the internet: experience in filing for registered trademarks for internet businesses, reporting trademark violations, reporting cases of online plagiarism, drafting cease and desist letters and other related tasks.

=== Personal Business Ventures ===
January 2009: founded ClickOn.Us LLC, an internet marketing & search engine optimization venture.
