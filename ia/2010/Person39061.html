I am a Corporate Recruiter for Humanscale Corporation - which is a high-end, ergonomic manufacturer. We manufacture and design ergonomic products that are geared towards the health and safety of computer users. Our product line includes seating, lighting, glare filters, keyboard trays, monitor arms, and footrests; our products are designed to help people with Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, back pain, and eye strain.
