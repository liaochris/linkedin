Clemens Wan’s Summary

After applying Artificial Intelligence algorithms to different research projects in foreign laboratories and universities in Japan and Germany, I decided to settle down and believe more in happiness, health, and community. Most of my practical work experience involved web design, project management, backend infrastructure design, and software development, but I've learned that programming is not my passion. Even though I can understand this language and relate to it, I find the peak of my interests in a project within the design phase. Although inventing new tools that can change the world is a possible path, I'm more excited about the usage and application of these tools that will lead the coming advancements.

My current position at Credit Suisse as a Technical Business Analyst gives me healthy balance of financial exposure on the trading floor and conveying these ideas to developers. Understanding both sides of the story and being able to effectively convey these ideas keeps the content of the work very interesting.
