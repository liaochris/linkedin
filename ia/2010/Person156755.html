Kevin Stein’s Summary

Profile Summary
Kevin has many years experience in user experience design and business analysis, with a focused vision on designing user experiences through the understanding of client needs and brand awareness.

Kevin has experience with all phases of the development life cycle, particularly gathering requirements, use case development, process flow design, user experience practice, interface design, rapid prototyping, conducting usability testing, as well as quick hit studies. Kevin is adaptable, able to trouble-shoot, think analytically, design interfaces tailored to users needs, and has strong communication skills.

Industry experience includes: automotive, entertainment, technology, energy, and telecommunications.
