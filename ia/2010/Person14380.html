Ann Farrell’s Summary

"Executive Coaching by Successful Executives" - a key differentiator in the quality of my corporate and business coaching and my value to my clients. After a very successful 30-year corporate career as the only woman to have risen from entry level to the top of the house in my Fortune 100's company's 150-year history, I created trademarked programs and tools leveraging my experience and coaching best practices to support you to enjoy the success that you want in your career and life. Quantum Endeavors is an Executive, Leader and Business Success coaching and consulting company specializing in engaging leaders at their best! More than 35 corporate coaching firms have selected, are licensed and are using my programs and products to deliver executive, leader and team coaching to organizations and professionals!

Passionate that executive coaching, the most effective development tool be available to all, I launched a powerful, affordable, convenient way to do so! "Your Corporate Success" (www.yourcorporatesuccess.com), is a member-based a group executive coaching engagement delivering - for the cost of one dinner out a month - the same tools and programs that I use to support my executive coaching clients!

Special niches include professional women, leadership development, and emotional intelligence. I am a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach and have earned Professional Certified Coach credentials with the International Coaching Federation, one of less than 1,500 coaches worldwide to hold this distinction.

I also have special product and programs to support certified coaches to build their own thriving corporate coaching businesses while delivering transformation growth and success to their clients.
