Saar Gur’s Summary

I love helping entrepreneurs build great companies. I have been helping entrepreneurs for as long as I can remember...
The more professional bio:

Saar Gur is a partner at Charles River Ventures (CRV), with 12+ years of experience working with entrepreneurs as a founder, investor and advisor. Saar is passionate about discovering and growing early-stage companies and focuses on CRV’s consumer and internet investments. Saar currently sits on the boards of Progress Financial, Wonderhill, and Blippy (incubated at CRV). Saar was also involved with CRV’s investments in Twitter, Scribd and LilGreenPatch (acquired by Playdom).

Prior to joining CRV, Saar was a co-founder of Brightroll, the world's leading Internet video advertising network. He also acted as an angel investor and advisor to numerous companies including RockYou, Admob (acquired by Google, NASDAQ: GOOG), Flixster, Xobni, and Voicestar (acquired by Marchex, NASDAQ: MCHX). Saar was also the co-founder of Carebadges.com (acquired by Facebook Causes) and remains an active advisor to Causes. Previously, Saar was VP of Customer Acquisition, and responsible for the P&L of Adteractive, a leading performance-based marketing company with over $120m in revenues achieved with no outside capital.

Most recently, Saar launched FounderDating, an endeavor aimed at helping talented entrepreneurs find co-founders and compelling business ideas. Saar has an M.B.A. from Stanford University and a biochemistry degree from University of Wisconsin-Madison. When Saar is not working with tech entrepreneurs, he loves kitesurfing, helping his wife with her offline food business, and spending time with his newborn son.

Saar Gur’s Specialties:

Building companies from great ideas. "Willing" things to success. Working myself out of a job, sales, managing people, attacking complicated problems, not taking myself too seriously.
