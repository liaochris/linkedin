Greg Sawin, PMP’s Summary

Greg Sawin is a senior project manager with 15 years of experience and his PMP: 7 of those years as an IT PM, 7 years as a non-technical PM and 1 year as an IT contractor. Known for delivering on high visibility multi-million dollar projects that span multiple business units.

A graduate of Bucknell University and the William Penn Charter School and former nationally ranked public speaker, he currently provides project management services at LS Tech Solutions.

• Managed a multi-million dollar ecommerce web development project that launched on-time and under budget.
• Managed an ecommerce software application through 2 major versions and multiple customizations.
• Oversaw construction of hundreds of client web sites.
• Served as liaison between IT and all internal and external stakeholders.
• Expanded project management skills by choosing to temporarily leave IT to manage building construction, maintenance and environmental remediation projects.
