David Palella’s Summary

***

David A. Palella is the Founder of: (1) BioScience Ventures Inc.; and (2) Carbon Manna Unlimited, both in San Diego, California.

BioScience Ventures, Inc. is a San Diego-based biopharmaceutical consulting firm founded in 1991 specializing in:

(1) Global Strategic Alliances in Japan (yes, I lived/worked there & speak Japanese), North America & Europe

(2) Worldwide Business Development / Corporate Development

(3) Deal structures & terms; and

(4) VC investment and M&A advisory.

-----

Carbon Manna Unlimited is non-profit, international standards-setting organization advocating and propagating several novel forms of MARKETS-BASED, SUSTAINABLE micro-finance and new micro-economic paradigms -- all of which are fairer to the Poor in the Developing World and more scalable than micro-debt or micro-loans, which won the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006.

It is very rare in Human History that someone can clearly say they invented something better than what won a Nobel Prize. This is one such case.
