Tom Wagenhauser’s Summary

As a Partnter at The Ryntal Group, Tom serves as President of the multi-unit division. The Ryntal Group focuses on superior service to the owners and tenants of investment properties with an emphasis on wealth creation through superior property management. Tom brings over 25 years experience in real estate construction, acquisition, development and management. As an experienced real estate financial executive, Tom has a thorough understanding of the needs of property owners and tenants and has been highly successful in wealth creation through real estate.

Tom understands the various financial and tax information and system needs of the industry having been responsible for over 25,000 units at more than 100 facilities and involving over 100 partnerships. Tom is an expert in business process optimization bringing these skills to The Ryntal Group to serve its clients.

As an active member of his community, Tom has served in as a board member, officer and committee chair for various companies, charitable and community service organizations, and associations. Tom also actively supports his profession as a member of the AICPA, OSCPA and IMA. Tom has received various awards recognizing him for his contributions to the profession.

Tom holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration and Accounting from The Ohio State University and resides in Florida with his wife Kathy.
