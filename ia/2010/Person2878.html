Ada Wells’s Summary

Ada Wells, MPT, owner of ProBalance, Inc., graduated with honors from UC Davis with a B.S. in physiology and went on to graduate at the top of her class from Chapman University with a Masters of Physical Therapy. She has been an active member of the American Physical Therapy Association, Sport and Orthopedic Section since 1993. She is also a Polestar Certified Pilates Practitioner and a member of the Pilates Method Alliance. Ada specializes in combining manual therapy with Pilates based exercise for sport specific rehabilitation and performance programs with a special interest in golf.

Ada has contributed to national and local publications and provides seminars to Bay Area corporations, small businesses, golf clubs, and health clubs on topics such as injury prevention, Pilates exercise, and golf. She has provided specialized athletic programs for local organizations, including running analysis clinics for Nike and golf fitness programs for Bay Area health and golf clubs. Through her various experiences, she has had the opportunity to share her therapeutic and Pilates talents with many professional athletes and television personalities.

Ada lives in Alameda with her husband, Stefan, and daughter, Anika. When not in the clinic, Ada enjoys sea kayaking, adventure travel, running, backpacking, and sailing.
