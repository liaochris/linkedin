Mary Lindewirth’s Summary

Analysis, Planning and Experience Bring Results --No Matter What the Business

For more than 20 years, I have been an innovator in strategic and creative direct response techniques. With a focus on analysis and creating "right-time, right-message" campaigns, I have developed marketing plans to successfully market everything from publications and audio books to luxury resorts. I have produced winning results for notable clients such as American Greetings, Intrawest Resorts and Sony. I am interested in working with companies that have a great product and want to optimize their sales via direct channels. I work with both experienced direct marketing teams and those new to this method of marketing.
