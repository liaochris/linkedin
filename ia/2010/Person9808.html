Amanda Millin’s Summary

After receiving my B.A. in English, I moved to New York to attend the M.S. in Publishing program at Pace University and pursue a career in book publishing. Working in the industry taught me, however, that I prefer the fast-paced atmosphere of imposing deadlines. So I'm currently living in Los Angeles looking to meet others in the magazine industry and find work as an editorial assistant.

I know my passion for food and travel along with my well-rounded publishing experience will make me a valuable asset to various publications.
