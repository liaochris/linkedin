Colton Michael Carothers’s Summary

Currently at the University of Washington School of Law, I am active and engaged in law school organizations and the Seattle legal, artistic, and French communities. Before coming to UW Law, I worked as a paralegal at Krasnow Saunders Cornblath LLP in Chicago (2007-2008) and served as Seattle's first Young Ambassador to her sister-city, Nantes, France (2006-2007). A graduate of Seattle University in Political Science with Departmental Honors and a minor in French Studies, summa cum laude, I also have experience as an administrative and executive assistant for the French Consular Agency, the French American Chamber of Commerce and a Seattle-based law firm, Larson Hart & Shepherd, PLLC.

Colton Michael Carothers’s Specialties:

Able to effectively lead detail-oriented group-based and individual projects, organize complex community events and adapt to multicultural and international environments as is seen through transnational educational experiences as well as concrete work experience with domestic and international businesses and governmental agencies. Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office products and computer use.
