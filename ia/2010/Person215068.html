Melissa J White’s Summary

As a writer: Branding identities, screenplays, advertising stories, marketing materials, short films, ebooks, narratives, business plans, script subervision, continuity, and travel writing.

As a designer: Web and print design, concept, creative development, art direction, photography, and production.

As a brander: mini-branding for small businesses™, creative exploration, vision statement rewrite, team development, budget, and taglines.

My past and present clients include Recycle Runway (marketing materials and business development), Brainstorms (art direction and domain management), MI Productions (screenplay rewrite), and Milagro Compost (PR and development).

I work on a project basis in advertising, entertainment, film, the environment and the arts.

My personal work includes a narrative nonfiction travel book, "Dizzy Sushi" coming out this fall from Tres Chicas Books, "Wastewater" a short love story at sewage treatment plant, and a 4-minute art-documentary, "Mixing: A Dialogue on Wastewater."

Melissa J White’s Specialties:

Branding and identity management, website content development, non-profit arts organization marketing, high-end web and print design, photographic art direction, and screenwriting and adaptations.
