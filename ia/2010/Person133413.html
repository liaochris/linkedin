Mark Burnett’s Summary

My goal is to create game changing innovation. I work with people to develop better understanding of their needs, aspirations and potential and how this relates to the bigger picture of the inevitable pressures from outside that must be overcome, and the necessary tensions from within that must be balanced. My interest is in enabling others to create new value and sustainable progress.

In my experience, opportunity is created by recognising possibilities and matching them with our desires and capabilities. Understanding what motivates us, what we each have to offer, and the talents and resources we can each bring to bear is key to success in any endeavour. Growing our social network encourages sharing of ideas and stimulates creativity and collaboration; more possibilities, more opportunities, more success.
