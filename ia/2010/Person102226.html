John Heaney’s Summary

John's professional life has centered around marketing, branding and designing memorable customer experiences that fuel business development. His current professional pursuits are focused on developing strategic digital marketing programs that capture attention and engage participants with compelling social media components.

As an adherent of design thinking, John challenges every business he works with to rethink the business they're in, exploit their innovative capacities to deliver exceptional user experiences and to inspire creative thought throughout their organization.

He has worked in a variety of industries, including manufacturing, professional services, online retail, software and technology development and distribution, targeting B2B, B2C and B2Government. He specializes in working with small and medium sized businesses who have the resolve and capacity to commit, act and react quickly and decisively.

John received an MBA from Case Western Reserve University, focusing on strategy, marketing, organizational behavior and entrepreneurship. He's applied these sharply honed skills working with an assortment of technology entrepreneurs in Northeast Ohio whose exceptional ideas simply need a distinct position, a clear message and a receptive audience.

When away from his perpetually running Mac, John can be found on the tennis court, ignoring the steadily advancing impairments of middle age and relying increasingly on the palliative effects of Advil and ice packs.
