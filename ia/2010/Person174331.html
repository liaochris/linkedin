Rob Cornell’s Summary

Business today faces many challenges, and therefore potential opportunities. With a metrics driven goal oriented attitude, and detail oriented - my skills, experience and passion have allowed me to overcome past challenges, create opportunities for increased operational effectiveness, and reduced costs of operational processes, while still meeting increasing customer expectations.

My career has been focused on processes (development, assessment, improvement and implementation), from my beginning in process engineering to quality management. My major strengths and abilities are in business process/system improvements; continuous improvement with Six Sigma and Lean methodologies; root cause analysis and problem solving; development and improvement of corrective and preventive action programs; developing, implementing and improving Quality Management Systems; and dealing with customer and supplier quality issues.

Specifically, I have developed, deployed and implemented a Quality Management System for two companies - each in under six months.

For one company, I implemented new processes resulting in saving approximately $1 million (annual sales of $18-21 million) over three years.

And during a period of five years as a consulting engineer - developed & deployed, improved, and/or verified Corrective actions, systems, and analysis techniques.

Having worked in the defense, aerospace, automotive, and nuclear industries, dealing with both mechanical and electrical products, I possess a synergy of successful practices and processes.
