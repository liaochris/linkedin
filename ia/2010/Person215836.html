WHO IS WILL ROBBEN?

Will Robben is at the helm of Venrooy Cable Equipment as CEO and owner. Since the takeover in 2006, Will radically professionalised and rejuvenated it. This was and is regarded by Will as a basic condition to achieve the EU top-3 position targeted at: maintaining yourselves in a local market is OK with an amateur performance. But heading for the top in Europe requires topquality in strategy, people, tools. This is why 70% of personell changed, why innovation took off, why production output grew 50% with lower headcount.

Will Robben is surrounded by a professional team of specialists. In his MT, Will is joined by an experienced Operations Manager and a strong Sales Manager. Will's focus is on where his own strengths are: on business strategy, marketing and finance.

BUSINESS OF VENROOY:

Venrooy Cable Equipment offers a complete array of cablehandling solutions for the 4 clientprocesses
1. Storing
2. Processing (lengthmeasuring, winding/unwinding, cutting)
3. Transporting and
4. Laying of cables

in the 3 endmarkets
1. Building and Infrastructure
2. Electro and Energy, and
3. Maritime and Rigging.

STRATEGY OF VENROOY:

Venrooy Cable Equipment is growing towards a top-3 position in the EU with its modern engineering- and assembly company. Three internal “engines” are core with which Venrooy achieves and enlarges its lead:

1. Engineering (best technical knowhow, smartest solutions)

2. Supply Chain (best mix of selfproduction, outsourcing and assembly)

3. Sales (best marketknowlege and customer relations, as well as the ability to translate client demands into strong tangible solutions)

These three engines are facilitated by Venrooy’s Board with best people, education, technology, tools and support. Their key common denominator: delivering top quality.
