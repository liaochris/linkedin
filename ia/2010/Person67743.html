Ethel Drayton-Craig, Ph.D.’s Summary

Ethel Drayton-Craig,Ph.D., is an inspirational mentor and consultant focusing on Personal Breakthrough Coaching. She offers a new paradigm for success and gives a biblical foundation to assist clients in going beyond what hindered them in the past. Dr. Drayton-Craig teaches how to sustain, encourage, protect, and empower oneself in meeting obstacles, dealing with adversity, toxic people and work environments, and in finding balance in creating the things that one desires.

She is an Author, Blogger, Publisher of a weekly Newsletter of inspiration. Dr. Drayton-Craig is an accomplished community leader who has a long history of serving on non-profit community boards. She has received national exposure
through television and radio.

Dr. Drayton-Craig is the co-author of "Stepping Stones To Success" with Deepak Chopra, Jack Canfield, Dr. Denis Waitley, and others. It is available on her website: http://www.etheldrayton-craig.com

Her blogsite is: http://dretheldrayton-craig.blogspot.com

Dr. Drayton-Craig offers telephone consultations and a unique customized retreat experience called "Opening The Way". These are two products she offers on her website.

Former Vice President of an international Management Consultant Firm; Former School Psychologist; Former College Administrator.

Dr. Drayton-Craig has been an independent consultant to corporations and individuals in Success Motivation, Stress Management, Employee Selection, and Effective Presentations.

Media and Press Credits include WOR-TV; WLVT-PBS TV; WNBC-Radio, Cable TV, "Heart and Soul Magazine", "New York Daily News", "New York Magazine", " Black Enterprise Magazine" among others.
