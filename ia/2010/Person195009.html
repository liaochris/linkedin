Steve George | Technical Recruiter’s Summary

RECRUITING & STAFFING
With 13+ years of Technical Recruiting, Steve George has established many business relationships across a wide range of industries. From his first recruiting position where Steve George set up an Engineering and Technical recruiting and placement desk. To the current position with Alliance Technical Solutions where Steve George focuses on Information Technology positions and placements. He has built his success on the principle of open and honest communications and hard work.

IT/IS – SALES & ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT – ELECTRONICS & COMPUTER ENGINEERING
For 15 years prior to his recruiting career Steve George worked with Information Technology / Information Systems companies selling software, hardware and services in startup and fortune 1000 companies. Sales seemed to be his natural course after Steve George started and built a profitable computer sales and service organization for a computer manufacturer.
