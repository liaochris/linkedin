Leslie McKee CPO’s Summary

Leslie's career as a teacher has prepared her well for her career as an organizer. Her experience allows her to quickly assess needs and teach clients how best to use systems to handle the "stuff" in their lives more effectively. With a positive, nonjudgmental attitude and a sense of humor, she tackles projects systematically and efficiently. Because she can look at the problem objectively with less emotional attachment, she helps streamline the process, keeping her clients focused on the task at hand rather than being pulled away to the many "mini-tasks" they find during the process. Once a system is in place, Leslie often extends her services to maintain these systems to aid clients in keeping order in their lives and businesses.

With order, comes peace of mind. Clients tell Leslie that's ultimately what she has brought to their lives. Nothing could make her happier.
