Silver Screen Arts’s Summary

Live Broadcast director, news editor, multi-camera shoot producer, live event coordinator, commercial editor, writer, and director of photography.

Equine photographer and videographer.

Sound room acoustics trainer, sound room wiring, sound design, sound recording techniques, lighting designer, cinematographer in film and video, film workprint editor, non-linear video editor, linear video editor.

Color timing expert in tele-cine environments as well as digital and film projection environments.

Scriptwriter in Final Draft / Movie Magic. Two live action films finished as producer and director, director of a two hour feature film and several shorts in 16mm film and video.

Reconstruction editor on the main-stream commercial feature film “Little Big League”.

AC-3 Sound designer, patch bay and video transfers machine work, location manager.

Storyboard artist, illustrator, book cover designer, college teacher, animator.

DVD/Blu-Ray author.

Assistant director of a 35mm short called, “Escalation” that is still in the works, producer and grant writer on two 16mm films.

Knowledge of all types of camcorders and audio recording devices as well as linear video editing decks from ¾”, digital betacam, DVCPROHD, HDV, D-2, D-5, and HDCAM. Sixteen years experience in non-linear editing, and six in linear ¾” deck editing.

Shot a 28 minute documentary in 24p for PBS that is available for viewing with a more extensive Fine Art portfolio. Second film to play on PBS in October, 2009.

Silver Screen Arts’s Specialties:

Pre-production, Production and Post-production are available in HD digital formats for cinema and television. Feature films, long format, short format, PBS.

B.D.Kuchera is the owner and operator of the company. He has been producing films and videos for over fifteen years. Additional services in New Media, Electronic Meida, Blu-Ray authoring, Directing, Producing and Consulting.
