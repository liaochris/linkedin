Barbara Jacobs’s Summary

Over 20 years helping residential and commercial clients create supportive environments. A special area of interest: applied color psychology. My thesis for accreditation through the IACC (International Association of Color Consultants/Designers) was a case study: The Importance of Color in an Elementary School.
Accredited by IACC in 1996.

Goals: to be a resource for architects, developers, homeowners and business owners, using color to help them achieve their own goals for buildings/exteriors, interiors, and business branding through supportive color design.

Creative Color Solutions for Architecture, Interiors and Business.

Barbara Jacobs’s Specialties:

Architectural Color Consultation, color consult for business and marketing, color consultation interiors and exteriors; fine decorative finishes including Venetian Plaster and Murals; Rug design and implementation from inspiration to delivery
