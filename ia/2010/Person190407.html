Steven J. Edelstein’s Summary

Steve’s background in Direct to Customer Marketing Management, Financial Modeling, Retail Distribution, Licensing, supply chain management and logistics, along with advertising, marketing, public relations and promotions has allowed him the ability to not only understand all the nuances of a particular client need, but provide solutions that will have a dramatic impact on the longevity, as well as bottom line results of a given program. Steve has over 20 years' experience in the marketing communications, consumer promotions and interactive media lines of business with particular emphasis on Internet commerce. Steve's experience in developing online and offline marketing and sales solutions for a wealth of Fortune 500 corporations brings a unique blend of strategic and technical expertise to The Logical Step, LLC and Common Sense Direct, LLC

Steve is a former member of the Electronic Retailing Association’s (ERA) Board of Directors, Chairman of the Board for Electronic Retailer Magazine, former Board Chairperson of the ERA’s Marketing/Communication’s Committee and Branding Task Force, a contributing writer to many industry publications such as Electronic Retailer Magazine, Response Magazine, Target Market Magazine, DM News, and Internet Retailer, a frequent speaker at industry events such as ERA, DMA, PROMO, and NCOF, a Chairperson of the Special Advisory Board for the Direct Marketing Association (DMA), a former board member of the American Marketing Association, and the recipient of the 2005 Volunteer of the Year Award from the Electronic Retailing Association.

Steven J. Edelstein’s Specialties:

Expert in Direct Response Marketing and Product Development, Media Funding, Tactical and Strategic Marketing, Trade Marketing and Promotion, Catalog, Literature, and Retail product distribution.
