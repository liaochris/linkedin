Sharon Tucker ABR,CRB,GRI,ITI,SRES,SFR,BS Acct’s Summary

Sharon Tucker is Broker / Owner of a Rockland County based Real Estate brokerage company, ERA Tucker Associates, Inc., founded in 1998. Tucker Associates, Inc. has become a premier Real Estate company. Mrs. Tucker has built one of the fastest growing real estate companies in Rockland County. The company services Rockland and Orange Counties. The sales staff of agents & Associate Brokers comprises a full time staff of marketing experts and international affiliates.
As an active member at the Rockland County Board of Realtors, Sharon was President in 2003 and she continues to participate on various committees. The Rockland Board of Realtors awarded Sharon President of the Year in 2005 and 2007 and Realtor of the Year in 2006. In addition to being recognized by the Rockland Board of Realtors, she was presented with the NYS Realtor Society Award from 2004-2009. She is currently a NYS Director and the Education Chairperson for the Rockland County Board of Realtors.
Prior to beginning her Real Estate career, Mrs. Tucker graduated from St. Thomas Aquinas College with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and practiced for 15 years in the private sector.

Her knowledge has been invaluable to the growth of her firm. Her agents are always, educated, ethical and forward thinking with their clients best interests in mind.
