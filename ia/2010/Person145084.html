Mike Blanda’s Summary

A Manager of Information Technology solutions with a track record of providing my business partners with a competitive advantage by leveraging my experience and leadership. I have filled the roles of a business analysis, program/project management, and resource management.

Businesses supported include Retail and Mortgage Banking as well as Manufacturing.

As a PMP and a member of PMI, I have successfully architected and managed software systems utilizing both packaged solutions and in-house development.

I came to my management positions with an extensive technical background having designed, coded, tested and implemented solutions using several technology platforms. I have experience with both newer technologies and legacy systems. I have used Project Lifecycle and CMM techniques to deliver strategic plans, feasibility studies, system design, construction, implementation, and support.
