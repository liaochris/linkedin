Keith Golinski’s Summary

I have worked in several different areas of the film and video field. My roles have varied over the years from supplying power, hanging lights, and keeping the crew fed to my current seat behind the camera. My professional background started at the age of 17 working on commercials and short films. Currently I co-own fulvew.com, where we have done several short films and produced national broadcast programming. Our future plans are to produce our feature films here in Michigan.
What I bring to the table is a completely rounded film background. I have worked almost every position on a film set and can step into any role when needed. My rounded background has helped in knowing how to keep the shoot moving, staying focused and on schedule. Staying focused and on time is key to keeping a happy crew and ultimately a great final product.
Formerly, we produced a show for the Outdoor Channel which lasted 6 seasons,(3 years). My business partner Chris Randall and I were our own self-contained crew. Every time we headed out, we needed to get the shot, no second takes. The shooting conditions were extreme and we had to be ready for anything, any climate, any terrain. That experience has helped shape us and helped us realize that we can handle anything.
In addition to the great outdoors, we had the unique experience of shooting for a weekly dance club show for E! entertainment television. It was an interesting experience and was a lot of fun. Shooting in a bar environment inevitably has it's own challenges especially when alcohol and the public are involved.
FULVEW continues following our goals and dreams of creating feature films full time because it's the best job in the world.
