Janet Reiniger Coburn’s Summary

After 30 years in corporate marketing and running my own business, I am focusing my efforts on something I consider fun! Real Estate in Summit County Colorado allows me to help folks who love to ski, golf, sail, hike, and mountain climb to enjoy the mountains near Breckenridge, Keystone, and Copper Mountain ski resorts. I am interested in connecting with anyone who wants to invest or sell in Summit County. Our home prices have not declined like other areas, and we have tremendous opportunities for break even cash flow investments.
