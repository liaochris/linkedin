Christian Rohrer’s Summary

Managed teams ranging from 5 to 20 and budgets from $500,000 to $2 million per year (not including wages). Outputs are both for specific product teams, as well executives (synthesis and dashboard readout).

    * New Opportunities is a blend of Market Research and User Experience Research focusing new market segments and product opportunities. These are often far-reaching, off-the-roadmap type of explorations that combine the best of market analysis, early concept design and testing, ethnographic field insights, and business assessment.
