Matthew Aaron’s Summary

Senior executive, strategist and information architect with over 13 years experience in the interactive space, evolved from a career in advertising and journalism.

Major projects I have recently completed:

Working with Adrenalin as the executive producer, we've just completed 'one of the most successful' sales promotions for McDonalds Australia and New Zealand, the "Drive Thru to Win" promotion, based upon Adrenalin's proprietary eScratch web-based promotional technology.

A strategic communications analysis for property giant Stockland, including stakeholder and customer research covering business objectives and customer requirements. Deliverables included online strategic recommendation, IA and development specifications for a major Australian web development.

Visit my blog: http://interactive-marketing.info

Some earlier career milestones include:

• Online Designer, IA and content editor for Nestlé Oceania regional intranet.
• Designer, GUI Designer, IA, project manager and content developer of the Tallwoods Village community web site supporting the $280 million property development majority owned by the Fairfax family company Bridgestar Pty Ltd.
• Designer, GUI Developer and Editor of the Sydney 2000 Olympic Media Portal from the New Balance Gold Club hospitality suite during the 2000 Olympic Games.
• Strategist, Designer, GUI Developer and Business Director of Winetrader.com.au. In the late 90's it was the largest fully e-commerce enabled wine auction site on-line.
• Co-author of “Step One - A first look at the Internet” (Cybertree 1996)
