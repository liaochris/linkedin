Lara Joy Brynildssen’s Summary

At LJBryn&Co. we represent, market and sell cutting-edge, hand-made and small-batch children's products in addition to the big name brands. Our territory is the greater Midwest including Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Missouri, Minnesota, Ohio and Wisconsin.

We specialize in giving each unique brand the attention you would give it, if you were still out there marketing it yourself. Many of the brands we represent are family-owned companies and we understand the individual challenges those businesses face when finding their niche in the children's marketplace.

We strive to give our buyers, many of whom are also nurturing families, that same level of care and commitment. We believe that a successful children's boutique shopping experience begins with the design, manufacturing and marketing of great products, continues with an open rep/buyer relationship and ultimately ends in happy retail customers at the store level.
