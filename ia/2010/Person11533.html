Andrew Badera’s Summary

I am a versatile and motivated consultant informed by years of experience with startups, small business and international corporations across a wide variety of verticals. I viscerally understand the unique pains of startups and small & medium business (SMB) from the ground up, inside, out. I am ready to hit the ground running and deliver value immediately.
