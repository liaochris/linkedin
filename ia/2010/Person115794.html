Kevin Jones’s Summary

Currently I am an engineer for several multi-million dollar networks. My specialty is satellite communications. But I also support routers, switches, VOIP equipment, microwave, GSM telephony, and other communications equipment. I have experience with Frame Relay, ATM, TCP/IP, and various other communications protocols.

My previous position required me to travel worldwide. I spent time in over 31 countries installing various types of communications systems. My responsibilities included: design and integration of the systems, testing of the systems, installation at the customers facility, customer certification testing, and customer sign-off.

Prior to that I proudly served in the United States Marine Corps. My first job was as an electronics technician, maintaining and repairing various communications systems. For this job I spent over a year going through various electronics schools.

Also while serving as a Marine, I was a satellite communications technician. I maintained and repaired several types of satellite communications systems. This position required that I spend another year in training going through various electronics schools.
