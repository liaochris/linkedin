Rex Whisman’s Summary

The BrandED Consultants Group, helps colleges, universities, school districts, government agencies, non-profits, businesses and individuals develop sustainable brands, based on their mission, core values and stakeholder engagement. We honor the traditions of our clients and position them in a modern context by executing brand strategies using contemporary communications channels like social media and mobile marketing. BrandED takes an inside out approach to brand building that emerges all stakeholder groups, even the skeptics, in the brand development process. As a result, our clients have a better understanding of what they stand for, and why that is important.

Rex Whisman’s Specialties:

Brand development, brand audits, brand research, verbal and visual identity building, brand and social media strategist, keynote speaker. Author of http://www.brandchampionsblog.com.
