Brendan Moon’s Summary

An information technology professional since 1990. Brendan possesses years of technical, business and managerial experience that span a wide variety of organizations and technologies. He has experience in system integration, solution architecture, enterprise architecture, project management, strategic planning, and technical training. He works with senior management in government and commercial organizations to develop and implement solutions which align IT with established business goals.

Areas of technical expertise include Active Directory, Microsoft Exchange, Windows Server, and Security. He also has experience with system virtualization including VMware, Voice Over IP (VOIP), storage area networks (SAN), and IP networking.

In addition to technical expertise, he has well-established communication skills including technical writing and presenting in various formats including briefings, workshops, product demonstrations, and classroom technical training.

Brendan lives in Southern Maryland with his wife and twin daughters.
