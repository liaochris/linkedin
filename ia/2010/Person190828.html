Sloan Seymour’s Summary

Building a market leading sales organization in support of the Marchex performance based advertising solutions. Goal is to build the market leading call based perfomance business.

Transformed an IT publishing business to a digital media company. Built, launched and integrated websites, enewsletters, digital events, digital lead generation directory, f2f events and magazines.
