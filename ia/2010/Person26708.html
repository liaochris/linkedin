Brad McCann, PMP, PPMC’s Summary

A career I/S professional, I started as a developer and moved into mangement in 1982. From there I have had experience in many areas of I/S across many platforms and technologies. I have been responsible for as many as 70 people at any given time managing multi-million dollar strategic projects for my company.

I am a certified PMP with the Project Management Institute and a founding Board of Directors member for the PMI Montgomery AL Chapter.

I enjoy the strategic aspects of business, coming up with creative ideas on ways to improve and/or grow the business, although my primary role is implementation of systems and solutions that support the strategic direction of the company.

I am very interested in personal and professional growth and enjoy sharing and mentoring others with similar interests.

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Insurance industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    I am now working full time on Alfa's enterprise Business Intelligence initiative, through the corporate Business Development Office.

    We are in the process of establishing the framework, technology, infrastructure, and business processes required to transform our enterprise data into actionable information which should significantly impact our decision making ability as a company at multiple levels.

    By effectively joining the data from our disparate systems (insurance, loans, claims, financial, human resource, and others) with external data (demographic, geographic, etc), we will enable all areas of Alfa (underwriting, actuary, marketing, claims, etc) to manage in new ways while being able to get a more complete view of our customer than ever before.

    Using ETL, Profiling, and Quality tools from Informatica to extract, transform, and load our data into our Teradata warehouse, we will deliver incremental solutions to the users, primarily utilizing Cognos BI tools.
