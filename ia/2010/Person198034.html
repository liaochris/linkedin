Susan Stalteri’s Summary

Owner of The Stalteri Group and My Money Life. The Stalteri Group is a company focused on being an Advisor & Resource to small to mid-size employers. We help companies develop and maintain a focus on their greatest asset; their employees. We find solutions so that you don't have to take the time to do it yourself. Our model is based on 8 key areas; career/role, health & fitness, family, financial well-being, community, recognition & appreciation, self-development, and vacation time.

Our niche is to bring ideas, products and solutions to small to mid-size employers who may not have access to the tools that will help them be a sustainable business.

The priority is to build a culture that actively sets practices, policies and programs that will achieve work/life effectiveness. We focus on the employee's health, happiness, motivation and productivity which ultimately improve your bottom line.

My Money Life is a place focused on empowering women to be strong and independent financial decision makers by creating an environment where women can build their confidence, competency and cash flow. Annual Workshop & Expo, monthly workshops and website tools and resources.

Accomplished executive with extensive experience leading organizations within small to large companies. Focus on building strong service and operations teams to deliver high quality interactions with customers while minimizing risk to the organizations. Proactive, results oriented relationship management experience with business customers and consumers in a highly regulated industry. Success building effective centralized and decentralized performing organizations using a comprehensive approach to problem solving, product development, technology implementations and communications strategies.

Susan Stalteri’s Specialties:

Advisor and Resource for small to mid-size employers. Implement work/life enhancing solutions. We go beyond the basic employee benefit fundamentals. We focus on the well-being of your employees. How can we make life easier and less stressful so that they are more creative and productive? And how can we get your employees to use the self-development tools available?
