Fred Thiel’s Summary

Fred Thiel has nearly 30 years experience managing software and technology companies for rapid growth in both revenue and shareholder value.

As a Managing Partner at Triton Pacific Capital Partners, Fred leads the firm's software and technology investment portfolio, targeting investments in entrepreneurial companies with $5 - $50 million in revenue and historical EBITDA >$1.5 million.

Prior to joining Triton Pacific, he was CEO of several companies including GameSpy Industries, a leader in on-line media where he dramatically grew revenues and then sold the company to a strategic acquirer at industry leading multiples in under twelve months, and Lantronix, a leader in device networking and systems management technology where he repositioned the company, tripled revenues in four years, acquired and integrated four companies and successfully raised over $120 million through an IPO and follow-on secondary offering.

Fred's industry expertise spans internet media, Web 2.0, software (enterprise, middleware, embedded, firmware), semiconductors (networking and storage controllers), systems (networking, storage, infrastructure, automation, security, medical device), and systems integration.

Fred attended the Stockholm School of Economics and is fluent in English, Spanish, Swedish and French.

Fred sits on a number of private and not-for-profit boards of directors and is a current member and former chapter chairman of the YPO/WPO (Young Presidents Organization/World Presidents Organization) California Coast chapter.
