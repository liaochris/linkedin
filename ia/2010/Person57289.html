Daniel Kreps’s Summary

Experienced international banking executive and strategic risk manager with successful record managing businesses in challenging environments. Adept at identifying opportunities, creating effective strategies and motivating diverse teams of talented individuals to work together in achieving common goals.

Throughout my career I have ridden waves of change from one part of the world to another. Now the tide has brought me home and I am seeking to use my skills and experience in assisting the unbanked to access critical financial services and raise themselves out of poverty.
