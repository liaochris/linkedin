Jill Hernstat’s Summary

Hernstat & Company is a leading boutique executive search firm based in San Francisco that specializes in building dynamic, results-driven teams at top companies across the United States.

What sets us apart from our competition is our commitment to our clients. At Hernstat & Company, we know that recruiting is about more than just matching talent. It’s about matching people. Our extensive network of qualified professionals across sectors coupled with our ability to develop and maintain personal relationships serve as proof that at Hernstat & Company, we know people.

Jill Hernstat, President
Jill Hernstat, President and founder of Hernstat & Company, is a well-rounded executive search professional known throughout the industry for her knowledge in all facets of the recruiting process. Equipped with a network that is unparalleled in the industry. Jill is dedicated to understanding her clients’ needs in terms of culture, skill and experience.

Having held positions at Charles Schwab, Commerce One and Ziff Davis, Jill has more than 15 years of experience as an executive recruiter. Jill holds a bachelor’s degree in Communications and Latin American Studies from Southern Methodist University.
