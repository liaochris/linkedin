Fernando Pons’s Summary

Engineering and environmental professional focused on delivering integrated engineered construction and environmental consulting/permitting services. His professional interests include sustainable design enhancements and Brownfields.

Experienced with the entire design-construct-maintain life cycle of projects including master plan studies, design-phase engineering and environmental studies, preparation of design drawings and specifications, construction monitoring, construction materials testing, facilities & environmental due diligence, and forensic studies.

Experienced with Design-Build project delivery and NEPA permitting.

Registered Professional Engineer in DC, VA, MD, DE, PA, and OK. LEED AP

Fernando Pons’s Specialties:

Due diligence, geotechnical engineering, NEPA/environmental permitting, construction monitoring/materials testing, geothermal, geo-environmental, Brownfields.
