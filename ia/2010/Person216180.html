Wolf Leonard’s Summary

Accountant with broad experience in small business accounting and auditing. Specialized and hands-on experience in residential property management, real estate investments, and mortgage origination.

I have always embraced communication and information technology, and try to work with similar minded people and companies.

I take great pride in having introduced their first desktop computer to a Federal government office in the 1970's. Rather than waiting on a Data Processing Center for a week or more to deliver requested reports, I demonstrated that a Radio Shack TRS-80 computer could automate and track our Navy Public Works Department 1000+ employee personnel records and their skill/trade demographics.
