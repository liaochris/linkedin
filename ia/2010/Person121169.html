Larry Blumenthal’s Summary

After nearly nine years heading Web and social media strategy for the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation, one of the largest private foundations in the U.S., recently opened my own consulting practice. Open Road Advisors specializes in helping foundations and other organizations use the Web and social media effectively.

While at RWJF, I set overall strategy for Web initiatives, built the Web team, delivered an award-winning Web site that included a robust e-mail service and database of thousands of research publications. Also, led the charge on social media, helping to launch blogs, Twitter feeds, Facebook pages, user comments, communities and more.

Before RWJF, played a leading role developing various health-related Web sites, including DiscoveryHealth.com for the Discovery Channel and InteliHealth.com for Aetna U.S. Healthcare. Also worked as a newspaper reporter, a business editor at Dow Jones & Co., founding associate editor of the NonProfit Times and editor of Publishing Economics magazine.

A member of the Social Media Business Council and co-chair of the Creative Good Customer Experience Councils special interest group on community and social media. Also an experienced speaker, who has presented at numerous conferences and workshops on Web and social media strategy.
