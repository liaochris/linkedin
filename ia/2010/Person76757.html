Gord Gin’s Summary

Gord's main role is to provide pipeline consulting services and to mentor the Project Managers within New Century Software. Gord also works with the pipeline operator community to identify and develop solutions that meet their ever changing needs. Gord has a solid track record of successful GIS implementation and integrity management projects as well as establishing GPS data collection services for pipeline construction and maintenance.

Before New Century he spent more than 12 years at TransCanada in the pipeline design and pipe engineering departments fulfilling drafting coordination, engineering design, survey management and GIS project management roles. Gord also worked for an additional 7 years in the pipeline construction industry as an inspector and surveyor for TransCanada’s Winnipeg office and another 7 years with Stantec engineering in the Regina and Calgary offices. While at TransCanada, he was instrumental in pioneering the design and build of one of the first enterprise pipeline database management and GIS. Under his leadership, TransCanada was selected as the recipient of GITA’s 2001 excellence award for GIS in the pipeline industry.

Gord also has a second career as owner and operator of Yellow Dog Lodge in Northern Canada. Serving up to 16 guests at one time Gord and his wife Kathy operate the sport fishing lodge from June to September. The lodge specializes in corporate, domestic and foreign clients as far away as USA, France, UK, Germany, Switzerland, Australia, and South Africa.
