Jon T.O. Severson’s Summary

Came up with the idea for and run the Colorado Springs Young Professionals, Colorado's largest young professionals organization with over 5,000 members and growing. The organization, started in January of 2004, is responsible for successfully bringing together 20/30 something young professionals in Colorado.

-Advancement of young professionals through "open source" networking. Teaching real interaction and new thinking to make your local community better.

-Anything involving bikes. Could try to pigeon hole this into one area but well why? Focus is, for now, on the bicycle magazine, getting kids on bikes, and making some of my own designs a reality.

-Education. Though I should say by keeping government out of it and through supporting efforts of Wikipedia and Ted.com. Teaching kids how to explore on their own is more powerful than showing dated material/ideas down their throat.

-Advancement of Music. We've taken many steps back from music as a form of interaction between human beings to tap into emotions life with todays RIAA crap. It's wrong. We've taken the soul out of it. It's the equivalent as giving a bunch of people color by number canvases then selling it to the masses as "art".

Any mixture of the above, off shoot of the above, or people who believe in the above I'm interested in working with. Just like the angle of thought? Well then maybe you need to hire me. Even if you don't know what for. In a week you'll see why.
