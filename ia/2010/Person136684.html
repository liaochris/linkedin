Mat Rosa’s Summary

As Director of Research & Creative Services I am able to explore both the technology side of my interest areas - DRM, rich internet-based media applications, informatics and data architecture, and social network architecture - as well as define my creative side - UX and UCD, graphic and media development, heuristics, and so forth. I've been with iStreamPlanet since 2001 and both watched and coddled its growth into the machine that it is now.

I am currently working on a Masters in Informatics at UNLV with a cognate in HCI/UX, and plan to continue on to the Doctorate program afterwards (possible cognates: digital rights management, social informatics or maybe even environmental). I am also teaching undergrad now.

As an Instructor at the Art Institute, I am able to give back all that I've learned in both the technology and hospitality industries, and able to nurture the development of new web designers/architects. I truly enjoy teaching and believe that towards the latter part of my working life I would very much like to pursue it on a more full-time basis.
