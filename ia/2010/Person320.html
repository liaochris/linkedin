Mark Martin’s Summary

Mark W. Martin is the President and Principal Broker of 2M Realty, a Houston-based real estate brokerage specializing in property management, land development, investment partnerships and virtual office solutions for independent agents in Texas.

A true expert in the online real estate space, Mark has leveraged the power of Internet search to raise awareness of 2MRealty.com and become the most visited brokerage site in Houston. He maintains an active blog on the 2M Realty website, and has posted guest columns on numerous real estate websites including ActiveRain.com, RealTown.com, BiggerPockets.com, BrokerAgentSocial.com and RealEstateWebmasters.com, BostonRealEstateObserver.com and WannaNetwork.com. Mark has also published many online articles speaking to real estate topics on sites such as eHow.com, EZArticles.com, ArticleBase.com, ArticleSnatch.com, ArticleMonkeys.com, Squidoo.com, and Aboutus.org.

Mark also serves as the Chief Executive Officer of NetMass Incorporated, a safe data company he co-founded in 1998, which specializes in online data backup and recovery solutions for small and medium sized businesses and individuals. Additionally, he holds the position of Vice President with both Martin Realty & Land, Inc. and Crystal Springs Water Company, Inc., which are both family owned and operated companies.
