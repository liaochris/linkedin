Brendon Ogilvy’s Summary

Effective Measure provides cutting edge digital Audience Measurement, website rankings, internet demographics and market intelligence for website publishers, media agencies and digital marketers. With unique demographic technology, Effective Measure gives advertisers more representative, continually evolving demographic profiles.

Effective Measure enables advertisers to gain unrivaled insight into web, mobile and console usage across all publishers & websites, including full support for IFABC and IAB metrics and their definitions as well as insight into the user journey amongst publishers and websites, discover the trends that can give your clients an advantage.

Our patent pending Digital Helix methodology addresses key industry concerns surrounding unique browser audience calculation, cookie deletion and the cost effectiveness of providing this type of solution to the market.
