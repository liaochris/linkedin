Ann Page’s Summary

I am an NLP master practitioner and professionally trained coach. Since 2003 I have worked with corporate organisations, law firms and individuals in providing leadership, management and interpersonal skills training courses and presentations to nearly 900 delegates. I have written a book called 'Managing External Legal Resources.

I worked for nearly 30 years for a number of PLC's as a senior in-house legal adviser and manager. During that time I was responsible for leading and developing lawyers, as well as providing first class legal services. My last client survey achieved 100% satisfaction rating. I was recognised as a top 100 lawyer in 2003/3.

I am passionate about providing lively and engaging training so that all participants will leave inspired to implement their action plan.
