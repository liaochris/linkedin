Lawrence Fox, CMA’s Summary

I liberate entrepreneurs from paperwork.

Nobody starts a business in order to wade through piles of paper (invoices, bills, bank statements, what have you), but sooner or later they have to deal with it, one way or the other. One way is to waste their time and energy on doing something they don't enjoy, instead of spending time building up their business or spending time with their family and friends.

I give them back their time---because I'll do the paperwork.

But I also provide advice and a sounding board and introductions to other people that I know.

I love working with entrepreneurs and small businesses. They are excited about what they do for a living, and that gets me going as well.

When I'm not wielding my magic Wizard's wand to help entrepreneurs, I try to do magic with a watercolour brush. Once in a while, it happens. Several of my paintngs are now available as note cards, perfect for thank-you notes, birthdays, anniversaries, or just to brighten up the day of someone special.

In January 2009, I became President of a not-for-profit group of independent consultants--the Association of Independent Consultants (AIC). AIC is an educational and networking group for all independent consultants, from accountants to web-designers and everything in between. Monthly meetings are held in Toronto on the last Monday of every month (except December). For more information on AIC, contact me or check out our website at www.aiconsult.ca, or come out to our next meeting as my guest!
