Jeff Smith’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    RIISnet, LLC

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    January 2004 — Present (6 years 10 months)

    RIISnet is an electronic trading platform designed for the Commercial Real Estate Industry that matches real time demand to real time supply. This functionality leads to increased efficiency in the marketplace, allows for the creation of a central repository for any kind of asset, and provides worldwide electronic access to those assets.

    The RIISnet search engine aligns specific descriptive data points to sort the best matches between buyer and seller or seller and buyer, with the click of a mouse.

    Sellers can assess all interested buyers based on the completeness of the buyer’s search to the seller’s asset, and invite those interested buyers into a virtual deal room where documents can be viewed and broker assisted conversations can take place. RIISnet‘s interactive database gives owners the ability to determine the level of demand and potential value of any asset using this real time supply and demand information

    RIISnet creates a pull marketing environment rather than a traditional push marketing model. Buyers and sellers are already registered allowing for real time access to one another without the need for lengthy and expensive push marketing campaigns. The information provided within RIISnet will stimulate activity in the marketplace by allowing wide and efficient access to the market coupled with instant connectivity between buyer and seller.

    RIISnet’s patented system is the only platform in the Commercial Real Estate Industry today that delivers this information and connectivity efficiently, electronically, and in real-time.
