Pamela Enz’s Summary

I am an experienced sales representative of high end products for the building industry. See below to see the products I am currently representing.

I have owned and operated a residential and commercial interior design studio that specialized in large project remodeling including kitchens and bathrooms. Our studio develops design solutions, strategies for implementation, and project management of fabrication. I design everything from kitchens, bathrooms, custom furniture, and soft goods. During the 20 years of owning and operating the studio I have employed as many as 6 individuals and as few as one.

I have served at the Minnesota Chapter President of both the National Association of the Remodeling Industry and the National Kitchen and Bath Association. I have served on the National Board of the National Association of the Remodeling Industry at the Strategic Planning Chair.

I am a trainer in the areas of Strategic Planning and DiSC.
