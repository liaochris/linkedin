Gregory Bergman’s Summary

VP, Financial Planning & Analysis and interim Corporate Controller.

Finance Leader at three premiere, growth-oriented companies -- Scansource, Monsanto and Pfizer -- ranging in size from $2B to $75B in annual revenues.
