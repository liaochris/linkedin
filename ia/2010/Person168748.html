Randall Mauldin’s Summary

Questions about Supply Chain Management? Take a look at this article http://tr.im/LuE9

Comment on my blog about supply chain metrics: http://tr.im/LuFM

I am passionate about supply chain management as a strategic part of business operations with over 20 years of experience in planning and executing operations in supply chain management, logistics, transportation, and information technology.

I help businesses and government organizations increase profits and reduce costs by improving their supply chain and procurement efficiency with military precision and a "failure is not an option" approach to solving problems.

In addition, I hold a Doctorate in Business Administration and professional certifications as a Certified Professional in Supply Management (CPSM®) from the Institute of Supply Management and Project Management Professional (PMP®) from the Project Management Institute.

My career focused on leading cross-functional collaborative teams to meet objectives and strategic goals. We used analytical processes to analyze requirements and develop actionable plans to fill the needs of customers. Success was based on the development of a strong network that fostered collaboration.

As a Supply Chain Manager, I managed and directed all fiscal activities of the activities related to supply chain management for a large training organization. I introduced strategic planning and quantitative analysis that resulted in reduced inventory losses by 96% and reduced spending by 53%.

During my last tour, I managed operations for acquisition program office responsible for the development, acquisition, and sustainment of logistics information systems. We conducted strategic planning sessions that identified the need to increase our work force in our software security branch. The results of this planning effort was the development of action plans to secure the budget and identify the skills necessary to achieve this strategic objective.
