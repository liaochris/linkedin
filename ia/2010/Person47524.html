Danielle Moss Lee, Ed.D.’s Summary

Currently heading a leading-edge non-profit organization dedicated to helping high-potential students of color from New York City to become high-achieving college graduates. My dissertation topic looked at program implementation in public school after-school programs, but I've developed a strong interest in the value of supplemental education programs in exposing the "hidden curriculum" and expanding intellectual curiosity and personal resiliency in African American and Latino students. I'm also very interested in expanding my consulting base in this area in 2009, and have already begun a book project aimed at African American parents looking to improve educational outcomes for their children. Additionally, I'm interested in coaching newbie non-profit executives looking to make an impact in this growing field.

Danielle Moss Lee, Ed.D.’s Specialties:

Leadership and management in the non-profit sector, change management, after-school and supplemental education, Black student achievement, college access and completion.
