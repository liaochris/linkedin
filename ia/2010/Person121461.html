Lars Kongshem’s Summary

Online strategy executive with 15+ years of experience in web marketing and development, content strategy and publishing, and online communities. Most recently, I was Director of Web Marketing & Online Communities at Symantec, where I led global web strategy and publishing operations for the Enterprise & SMB corporate marketing website and technical user community.

My background spans corporate, start-up, and nonprofit organizations. I'm a seasoned manager with a demonstrated ability to build and scale high-performing teams. I combine strong technical skills (HTML coder since 1994) with the ability to communicate complex ideas in layman's terms (award-winning technology journalist and magazine editor).

• Built a team of 16 web professionals to manage and reinvent the Enterprise & SMB corporate marketing website and online community for Symantec, a Fortune 500 company.
• Led the marketing website content integration strategy for Symantec's $10.5 billion merger with Veritas Software.
• Drove the editorial strategy for the launch of a national technology magazine at Scholastic, a Fortune 1000 company.
