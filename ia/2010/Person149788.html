Manish Singh’s Summary

I worked as a Software Engineer at Computer Sciences Corporation (CSC), India for two years (2004-2006) and gained valuable experience working on Mainframe Technologies ( e.g. CICS,TSO,z-OS,JCL etc.).Those two years exposed me to professional world for the first time and taught me great lessons in life.

Furthermore, since my undergrad years, I had always been interested in the field of Wireless/Computer Networking and worked on numerous related projects both at IIT Roorkee (undergrad) and at Texas A&M( grad school).

I interned at Nortel Networks in summer 2008 and was involved in the design of the IP network-based A1 interface (MSC to BSC) for CDMA cellular networks. This experience exposed me to the understanding of real-world challenges in mobile communication networks.

I am passionate about working for a position that requires software development/design with the domain knowledge of computer/communication networks.

I am presently working at Juniper.
