Steve Gill’s Summary

Steve is a Founding Partner & Board Member of TRIARQ. TRIARQ is a network of Physicians, Physical Therapists, Occupational Therapists, and Patients working together to create a new standard in musculoskeletal healthcare based on collaboration, scientific evidence and trust.

Steve is also the President of Chapman Gill International LLC. The firm advises CEO’s, entrepreneurs & practice owners as they guide their companies to achieve demanding business goals.

CGI clients include CEO's of medical, legal & other professional practices, banks & financial companies, hospitals, consulting firms, small & medium sized enterprises, not for profits ... & executives in transition.

Steve brings 20 years experience as an Executive Vice President at HSBC (Assets $315 billion, Staff 108,000) and Standard Chartered Bank (Assets $141 billion, Staff 33,000). He was a Chairman of subsidiary companies at both banks and provided accessible, hands-on leadership of nationally & internationally dispersed businesses spanning North America, Europe, China, India, Africa & across the emerging markets.

Steve has helped build profitable businesses in health care, consumer goods and banking. He also brings direct experience of 30 mergers and acquisitions and of business recovery in NYC post 9/11.

Steve helps CEOs and senior Executives ensure that key success factors are addressed, including:
-- Strategy formulation and execution
-- Decisions and negotiations
-- Organization restructuring
-- Profit improvement, cash flow and banking
-- Market planning & strategic positioning
-- Innovation and co-creation with clients, vendors, and partners
-- Strategic recruitment & retention
-- Building effective Executive Committees
-- Employee engagement
-- Focusing time and energy and staying on track

Steve has also established a separate On Line Survey business.
