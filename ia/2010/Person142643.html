Michael Halligan’s Summary

I founded Engage Marketing in 2008 as a company to enable SMEs from Melbourne, Australia to grow and achieve all that they want to achieve.

I find great pleasure in working with small businesses to create a better future for not only them, but their family and employees. Basically that is why we exist - to improve the future of us and our clients. At the end of working with a client we will sit down with the client with a beer and be proud of the work we have done.

As much as I enjoy marketing and business growth, you won't find me on LinkedIn too much as having the right balance between work and personal time essential in finding happiness. At the end of the day, im a person, not a corporate with no personality. I like to speak to my clients on that same level.

I believe in the following as integral to marketing success:
- Design
- Positioning
- Emotional branding
- Word of Mouth
