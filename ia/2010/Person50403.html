David Cochrane’s Summary

Professional career has been predominantly in the digital sector (Monster, Independent.ie, Loadzajobs.ie, ICAN Advertising) and managing online (and some offline) campaigns.

Founder of leading current affairs website Politics.ie.

Most recently Head of Online at Libertas.eu - managing website in 22 languages in 27 territories, before that Campaign Director of the Libertas No to Lisbon Treaty campaign (successful campaign). Cited signficantly in the press (and by leading yes campaigners) for the professionalism of the Libertas campaign.

Presently exploring new projects, including significant redevelopment of Politics.ie - Ireland's leading current affairs website.

Frequent media appearances over the past five years on TV and Radio and some press.

Qualified in the area of Public Relations and Digital.
