Blue Bliss’s Summary

Animator, graphic designer and illustrator with additional skills in portraiture and murals. Skilled in Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, AfterEffects, Lightwave and Painter. I use all of these plus hand-drawing and painting abilities to concept, design and create graphics and animations for the web, print, video, broadcast and film. I was an assistant lead animator on the movie "A Scanner Darkly". I also have experience creating interactive CD-ROM's for corporate training and NASA, as well as graphics and animations for other educational applications including Johns Hopkins University.
