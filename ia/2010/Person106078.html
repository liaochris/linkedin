Joshua Fishman’s Summary

Joshua is a junior, Entrepreneurship and Marketing Management double major at the prestigious Whitman School of Management at Syracuse University. In addition to holding high academic achievements, Joshua is an active and diligent student engaging in the community and business sector. On campus, Josh is a member of Delta Sigma Pi, the professional business fraternity, serves as the VP of Technology for Students in Free Enterprise (SIFE), is a returning Whitman Ambassadors, and charter member of University 100, a selected group of students to represent the university to dignitaries, prospective students, and visitors. Josh also works on campus as an Apple Specialist in the Whitman School of Management Technology Support offices.

Josh enjoys social entrepreneurship, scholarship in action, and getting involved with the community. Over the past two years at Syracuse U. Josh has consulted various retailers from Wig Boutiques to packaged imported foods and African handmade fair traded goods. During the summer of Josh's sophomore year, he interned at Unilever, the parent company of hundreds of well known nationals brands. Josh interned with the Ice Cream and Frozen Novelty categories, working on promotion and marketing effectiveness of Ben & Jerry's, Klondike, Breyer's, Popsicle and the remainder of the ice cream portfolio. While working on the Kroger Team at Unilever, Josh was able to work with the largest grocery store chain (over 2500 stores) in the U.S. and utilize the dunnhumby platform.
From these experiences, Josh has been exposed to incredible opportunities and separates him from his peers.

During the Spring of Josh's junior year, he plans to travel abroad for the semester to Hong Kong. He is excited to explore the world, intern, study, and emerge himself into a new culture while abroad.
