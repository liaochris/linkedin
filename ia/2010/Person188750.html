Sheri Jacobs, CAE’s Summary

Sheri is a 15-year veteran of the association and nonprofit community. She currently serves as president and chief strategist at the Avenue M Group. Prior to this position, Sheri served as the managing director and chief consultant in Chicago for McKinley Marketing. She worked on assignments including member and market research, strategy and planning, branding and positioning and recruitment and retention campaigns. Sheri is a frequent speaker and a contributor to various association magazines and publications. She is the co-editor for the industry’s official membership handbook titled Membership Essentials (published December 2007).She is currently serving on the ASAE and The Center Marketing Council, the Association Forum's Professional Practices Committee and is a judge for the ASAE Gold Circle Awards.
