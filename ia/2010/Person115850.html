Kevin Panet MS HRM’s Summary

Kevin Panet has over 15 years of experience in the field of Human Resources Management, with deep expertise in employee relations, training and organizational development, team building, and continuous process improvement. Additional strengths include strategic planning, recruiting and on-boarding, employee communications, performance management, management development, team building and HR project management.

With a MS degree in Human Resources Management and a BS degree in Business Administration, Kevin continues his professional development with an active member in PIHRA (Professionals In Human Resources Association).

During his career, Kevin has worked with Fortune 500 companies, including General Electric, The Boeing Company and WellPoint, as well as several smaller and companies.

Kevin has the HR skills, knowledge, experience and technical knowledge that today’s complex work environment demands. Currently in an active job search, he can be reached at (805) 501-0574. Call Kevin today for an interview. He will be the best hire you make this year!
