Thomas Rees’s Summary

Sales/Marketing Exec. with career experience on front lines and in management across several industries. I spent almost 20 years in Financial Services with a discretionary Senior Portfolio Manager's license and a daily radio program until leaving Wachovia in 2005.

Areas of specialty include creating innovative, customized multimedia campaigns in both start-up and corporate environments -- where assuring top quality client service to assure retention and organic growth is of primary importance.

I had a revelation about 4 years ago upon having my second stress-related heart operation when I realized the public markets would probably survive without me -- but I might not survive them -- and it was time for a change.

Returning to an entrepreneurial venue, I founded a company that is pioneering a search technology applicable to the recovery industry to assist people in treatment for drug abuse and alcoholism. Rees Networks LLC is the parent company of Sober Living Search (reesnetworks.com and soberlivingsearch.com)

Thomas Rees’s Experience

  • Founding Manager

    Rees Networks, LLC and SoberLivingSearch.com

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Rees Networks has identified a critical need for search technology in areas related to Drug and Alcohol Abuse, Addiction, and the Recovery industry.

    Rees Networks is the parent company of Sober Living Search.com, a company that was founded on the premise that Prospective sober living guests and owners/managers of sober living/recovery housing each have unique problems that can be solved with one critical service.
    Prospective "guests-in-need" sadly do not strengthen their program with sober living all too often due to the difficulties of calling house managers, setting up appointments , and touring houses they really don't want to be visiting in the first place. Owner/managers, on the other hand, have no effective way to showcase the best qualities of their homes and make themselves widely visible to guests.
    Sober Living Search.com is the largest database of sober livings in the world and offers managers a way to show off their homes on an interactive web page (a virtual website), while offering a free search to users who can locate homes by location, price, and certain amenities relevant to sober living guests.

    Now, in that inspirational moment of clarity that every addict and alcoholic has, the person can grab a computer and find a house by getting a "feel" for one he likes by way of a pictorial and descriptive visit to different homes on the site. Our goal is to eventually cover over 90% of all housing in the recovery arena.
