Rachel Hilgers’s Summary

Rachel Hilgers is an international public speaking expert and life coach who has coached and empowered hundreds of business owners by helping them clarify their message, tap into the deepest desires of their potential customers and followers, motivate, captivate and influence their audience. Having reached peak professional public speaking performance, she now empowers others to speak skillfully, concisely and powerfully. Rachel has a unique intuition about people and is able to immediately put them at ease and connect with them, regardless of their personality, background or field of work. After a brief consultation during the coaching session, she quickly understands the complexities of any business and together with her client is able to create a systematically strategized speech. Rachel has presented in Atlanta, New York, and Paris, France.

Rachel's specialties are the creation and delivery of customized business introduction speeches used in networking, as well as the response to "'What do you do?" In addition to these services, she also provides training in the areas of speech writing, preparation and delivery skills, panel discussions, seminars, workshops, basic stand-up comedy, memory and memorization techniques, one-on-one communication and significant reduction of public speaking nervousness.

Professional Speakers aspiring to be on the speaking circuit receive customized assessment, speech writing, training, and videotaping. Public Relations assistance is available to gain visibility through various media and secure local, regional, national and international speaking venues for recognition and compensation.
