Gregg Masters, MPH’s Summary

Strategist for hospitals, parent health systems, physician networks, and emerging 'member based' medical practices, including concierge, retained or direct practice models. A practitioner of disruptive innovation in both development stage, and mature company cultures.

My inspiration and intention is to meaningfully participate in the reform of the unsustainable, and inefficient health care finance and delivery system. 46 million uninsured, and the growing ranks of under-insured Americans is "per se" unacceptable.

I am committed to exploration and adoption of emerging digital interactive or social media applications to facilitate, and perhaps drive essential health care transformation, while simultaneously building connected communities between hospitals, physicians and the people they serve.

There are both granular and eco-system solutions short of top down, one size fits all remedies. Lets get to it!

Gregg Masters, MPH’s Specialties:

Application of social media to purposeful change; SEO, PPC, web design and online marketing. Contract negotiations, public speaking, copy writer, strategic thinker and "tinkerer", can implement as well as vision. Plays well with others, with nothing left to prove in the 'ego-sphere'. Am committed to transparency, integrity and making the strong group stronger.
