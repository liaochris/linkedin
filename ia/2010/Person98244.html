Jim Castiglia’s Summary

How do you grow sales in a tough economy and sustain your business? This is the biggest concern of today's modern business owner.

Surveying hundreds of owners, the National Federation of Independent Businesses found these additional concerns: the cost of Health Insurance and Workers' Compensation, as well as Federal taxes and tax complexity in their top 10 Measures of Small Business Problem Importance (out of 75 measures).

Imagine learning the powerful, dynamic, and proven business development tools of the Ultimate Business Mastery System (UBMS), which has been forged and tested in the crucible of national and international industry!

This unique program features personal and professional achievement guru Anthony "Tony" Robbins; marketing and management master Chet Holmes; author and consultant Stephen Covey; Zappos.com CEO Tony Hsieh; marketing guru Jay Abraham; entrepreneur Eben Pagan; wine critic, author and entrepreneur Gary Vaynerchuk; master marketer Scott Hallman; top sales management consultant Andy Miller; PR consultant Jill Lublin; internet marketer Mark Thompson; leadership expert Brendon Burchard; and Internet gurus Vishen Lakhiani, Anik Singal, Mike Koenigs, and Doug Williams.

And, take the guesswork out of your own personal performance enhancement initiatives. Get accurate, spot-on input on your strengths and soft spots and learn the secrets of Olympian gold medalists, Navy SEALs, crack S.W.A.T. teams, and top-performing Fortune 500 executives who perform at the highest levels under the most intense pressures.

If you're serious about growing your business, dominating markets, and achieving high-level results (and you're 100% committed to doing what's necessary to achieve your vision), let's talk!
