Laurent Torno III’s Summary

First published nationally while in high school, apprenticed with
fine professional photographers, early background in public
relations and journalism, competent writing and communication
skills, sole proprietor of photo studio by age 23, went on to daily
retail photography for industry benchmarks the May Company
and Federated/Macy's. Award winning team member with tight
portfolio of over 60 full page national advertisements in Home,
Beauty, Jewelry, Housewares, Luxury, Diversity, Holiday, Gifts,
Fashion, Portraiture, Cosmetics, Shoes, and Electronics.

Laurent Torno III’s Specialties:

Advanced experience in digital imaging hardware and software
applications including PhaseOne, Adobe Photoshop CS3,
Adobe Illustrator, Quark, InDesign, & Powerpoint. Experience
with branding, marketing, graphic design, fine and decorative
art, architecture, set design, film, and video production.
