Dr. Kevin Ong, M.D.’s Summary

Dr. Kevin Ong is a medical doctor, a certified health coach and a certified Net Profit Quest business coach. He was also a Singapore Government Medical scholar since 1996.

He has been actively involved in direct marketing of health and wellness products and services since 2001, and has engaged in social media marketing and internet marketing since 2007.

He started Health Coach International, a company that offers training, coaching and therapy to help busy corporate executives create slim, sexy and healthy bodies swiftly and safely.

He is also a charter member of Net Profit Quest, a training, coaching and consulting corporation, that assists business owners to double their net profits without increasing their marketing budget through business re-engineering, social media optimization and business coaching.

His business motto is to “Empower Entrepreneurs with Effective Marketing Strategies”. As a seasoned Social Media Marketer, he had provided input and implementation to many entrepreneurs on marketing strategies and tactics.

He is personally responsible for increasing sales and net profits in his clients’ businesses. He has also been able to rapidly build large networks of potential leads and clients both online and offline.
