Mark Newman’s Summary

In 1980 I began my career in the Music Industry with an emerging band called Ranger (later called “Night Ranger”). By 1982 I was with Ozzy Osbourne's “Diary of a Mad Man” World Tour. Since then I have spent time touring as a technician and/or stage manager with world renowned Recording Artist such as, ZZ Top, The Artist Formerly Known As Prince (TAFKAP), Ted Nugent, Smashing Pumpkins, Styx, Soundgarden, Ozzy Osbourne, Night Ranger, Damn Yankees, Bad English, The Kooks, Dokken and Hole. In addition to touring with these artists I worked in the recording studio on many of their award winning albums.

I took a 5 year hiatus from touring to get involved in the launch of the Experience Music Project (Seattle, WA.), specifically the innovative interactive music room known as Soundlab. During this time I was able to participate in the opening of the Science Fiction Museum and Hall of Fame.

Since Leaving the Experience Music Project, I have returned to touring with top tier recording artists including the rehearsals for Michael Jackson’s “This Is It” tour. I continue to be based in the beautiful Pacific Northwest just outside of Seattle and commute through SeaTac International Airport.
