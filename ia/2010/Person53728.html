Debra Black’s Summary

Debra Black is the owner of Coverups, LLC, a company that manufacturers stretch terrycloth seat covers for golf cars. The seat covers make an excellent way to advertise a sponsor or group of sponsors for a golf event. They will get maximum exposure as a "billboard" advertisement on the seats of various golf carts. The seat covers can also make a wonderful membership gift for exclusive Country Clubs. The seat covers will help to protect the CC investment of the golf cart along with constant name recognition. Your members will use their seat covers when traveling to other country clubs. The seat covers will help to protect the user from sticking and burning themselves on the hot vinyl seats as well as helping them to keep warm in cooler temperatures. Call for more information: 615-221-2222 or visit the website at: www.coverupsllc.com. We offer distributorship pricing, call for more information.

She has also been in real estate since 1988 and specializes in commercial real estate and residential land development. She owned and operated her own development company for over 8 years.
