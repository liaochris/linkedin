Gage Couch’s Summary

Gage received his bachelor's degree from Clemson University and also studied abroad at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia in Barcelona. Since joining EDSA in 2005, he has been actively involved in the planning and design for a broad range of projects varying from master planned communities to detailed design for several destination resorts. He exhibits a respect for nature, a unique attention to detail and a strong dedication to sustainable design. In the spring of 2008, he completed a 3-month exchange program in EDSA's Beijing office, working on a variety of regional projects in China

Some of his most relevant project experience since joining EDSA includes:

Bahia Beach: Rio Grande, Puerto Rico
The Haven: Cat Island, Bahamas
Liberty Harbor: Brunswick, Georgia
Candelero Point: Humacao, Puerto Rico
Copamarina: Guanica, Puerto Rico
Punta Lobos: Todo Santos, Mexico
Los Micos: Bahia de Tela, Honduras
