Kirsty Wilson’s Summary

Interim Business Solutions was established during 2005 when a need was recognised in the market place for businesses to have access to business support services without having to call on a Temporary Employment Agency.

Since its inception, Interim Business Solutions has evolved and now offers a range of services to ensure it is the ‘one stop shop’ for small business owners sourcing business administration and social media support. Interim Business Solutions specialise in:

- Virtual Assistance
- Certified Social Media Marketing Specialist
- Social Media Packages & e-Books
- Facebook Pages, Tabs, Side Banners & Apps installed
- General website / blog updates
- e-Newsletters
- Transcription
- Recruitment Support
- Business Support
- Special packages available (see our website for full service outline)
