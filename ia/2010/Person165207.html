Jack Korpob’s Summary

- Jack has extensive training in different types of tutoring styles, including individual tutoring, group tutoring, classroom teaching/tutoring, and online tutoring.

- Jack have gained many organizational skills working with a non-profit student service organization, Alternative Breaks@UCSD, at UC San Diego. I have facilitated many training workshops for site leaders and members of the organization. As a site leader development coordinator, he helps to create a cohesive group of leaders who are trained to plan logistics pertaining to international and national volunteer trips. Once site leaders are trained, they will be able to train members of the organization. He also serves as a site leader traveling to Belize with group members to work on Environmental Issues in 2009.

- Jack has been on 2 Alternative Breaks (service-learning trips). He has traveled to Yaroslavl, Russia to work with kids and Biloxi, Mississippi to work on Hurricane Relief and Voter Registration. He served as a site leader on the trip to Mississippi through the National Organization, Break Away.

- Jack made a presentation and request for scholarships for Alternative Breaks@UCSD 2008-2009 from the Vice Chancellor of Student Affairs Office at UC San Diego, and was able to acquire $10,000 in scholarship funds to award need-based financial aid students.

- In 2006, Jack helped to create and present a presentation about Blood Donation through the American Red Cross at a leadership conference from the American Red Cross held at El Monte High School.

- Jack has extensive leadership training through high school CADA conferences in 2004 and 2005.
