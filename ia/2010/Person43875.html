Richard L Cox, Sr. CFP®’s Summary

Richard L. Cox, Sr., a native of Chattanooga, Tennessee, Started his financial services career in 1976, He has earned the prestigious CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ (CFP®) certification from the Certified Financial Planner Board of Standards, Denver, CO, the Chartered Financial Consultant (ChFC®) and the Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU®) designations from the American College, Bryn Mawr, PA, the Accredited Tax Advisor (ATA®) designation from the Accreditation Council for Accountancy & Taxation, Alexandria, VA.

Richard has also been accepted as a Fellow of the American Academy of Financial Management (FAAFM™) and has been awarded the Board Certified designations of Master Financial Professional™ (MFP™) by the Academy, and has been accepted as an Accreditied Estate Planner (AEP) by the National Association of Estate Planners & Councils, Cleveland, OH.

Richard works primarily in the areas of (1) Wealth Management & Financial Planning (2) Estate & Charitable Planning (3) Tax Planning (4) Insurance & Risk Management (5) Business Consulting for Corporations, LLCs and Partnerships and (6) Employee Benefits, Retirement Plans.

Richard’s 32-year practice of Financial Services began with Life & Casualty of Tennessee, Nashville, TN in 1976, until 1984 when he founded his own independent agency Cox & Associates (now Cox Financial Centers, Inc.). In 1996 he founded Cox Financial Advisors, LLC (now Cox Wealth Management, LLC) an Independent Registered Investment Adviser, in order to offer comprehensive financial planning and wealth management services to his clients.
