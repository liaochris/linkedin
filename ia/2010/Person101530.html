John Ansbach’s Summary

John Ansbach is the Chief Legal Officer for Mothers Against Drunk Driving ("MADD") located just outside of Dallas in Irving, Texas. MADD is a non-profit organization that seeks to stop drunk driving, support those affected by drunk driving, prevent underage drinking, and overall push for stricter alcohol policy.

In addition to serving as CLO of MADD, John also serves as the President-Elect of the General Counsel Forum - Dallas Chapter, where he is also a member of the board. He also serves on the Board of Directors of the Texas Association of Business; as Treasurer and member of the Board of Directors of the National Foundation of Women Legislators; and, in his community, on the Board of Directors of Susan G. Komen for the Cure in Dallas.

When time permits, John also speaks on request from Fortune 500 companies and national non-profits on topics related to leadership, customer service and diversity. He has been privileged to speak for PepsiCo, Frito Lay North America, Toyota Motor in the U.S. and Canada, Hewlett Packard, and the National Association of REALTORS, among other groups.

John Ansbach’s Specialties:

Specialties include legislative affairs and public policy development and advocacy; commercial contract negotiation, drafting and compliance; labor and employment, regulatory compliance, business development, client management, risk management, corporate communications, public speaking, generational dynamics and leadership.
