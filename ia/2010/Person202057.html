Lauren Thomas’s Summary

Lauren Thomas is a digital marketer and social media consultant. She plans and executes social media campaigns that leverage the power of the social web to promote brands, products, and events.

In addition to Digital Marketing and PR, Lauren provides social media training and teaches clients to effectively use Twitter, Facebook, WordPress, and other social platforms and tools. She also advises clients on how to optimize their websites and social media platforms to engage customers and attract prospects.

Lauren launched her marketing career at GREY Atlanta where she worked closely with the New Business and Account Management Teams.

She is currently a Digital Coordinator at Everywhere, a social media management company based in Atlanta, GA. As part of the Everywhere team, Lauren helps clients tell their stories and engage their customers via the use of online/mobile tools and the social web. Her clients at Everywhere have included Cox Communications, Macy’s, PGi, and Lexus.

Everywhere also holds the distinction of being the first and only agency to hold a Guinness World Record for #BEATCANCER, the social media movement that raised $70k for cancer charities in 24 hours.
