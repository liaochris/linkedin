Adriana Koeck’s Summary

Substantial accomplishments in representing, domestically and internationally, multinationals from the manufacturing, construction, agricultural and telecommunications sectors on a wide range of corporate and banking legal matters.

Considerable experience with both institutional lenders and company borrowers in debt financings, including commercial bank loans, letter of credit facilities, bridge loans, assignments, syndicated loans/participations, debt restructuring and equipment lease transactions. Counseled in all matters involving non-performing loans and loan workouts.

Fluent in Spanish, Portuguese, English, and French and a working knowledge of Italian.

Adriana Koeck’s Specialties:

Broad executive team management experience in reviewing and analyzing deals, planning strategic legal and corporate direction, and combating international white collar crime.
