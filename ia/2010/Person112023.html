Kathleen S. O'Dea’s Summary

My passion is communication and culture, and I work with my clients to polish their communication skills so that they excel in their professional and personal lives.

I do this by designing and implementing business communication and cross-cultural classes and workshops appropriate for native and non-native English speakers. In addition, I coach executives and managers in all areas of communication. When it benefits my clients, I collaborate with experienced and talented colleagues, and thus Global Write-Speak is referred to as "A business and intercultural communication training group."

Most recently, with a team of subject matter experts, I designed a virtual writing course for Microsoft employees, and I am presently delivering this blended learning to engineers and architects worldwide. My clients have largely been in the biotech, pharmaceutical, and financial services industries, including Bayer, American Express, Gilead Sciences, Citi Private Bank, to name a few. Some of these I worked with when employed by World Learning for Business.

My work has always been about empowering others to excel as communicators, and to offer the specific skills they need to do so. I began my career as a teacher and administrator in Turkey, and I have worked for over 25 years with learners of all ages and nationalities: in the workplace, multicultural centers, universities, and public and private schools. I have loved every minute of it!
