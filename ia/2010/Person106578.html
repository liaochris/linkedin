  • Deputy Director General of Shipping

    Government of India, Ministry of Shipping

    (Government Administration industry)

    July 1999 — July 2001 (2 years 1 month)

    As Deputy Director I was tasked with analyzing and validating the financial and legal terms and conditions for a 25-year, $8 billion project to import liquefied natural gas from Qatar to India. I played a key role in improving growth prospects for the Indian shipping industry by analyzing international shipping trends, completing a global study and formulating new fiscal policies.

    I coordinated two national conferences with ILO, Geneva and drafted legislation to protect seafarers’ rights and was responsible for personnel management, human resources development, budgeting, crew labor relations and framing of legislative proposals in addition to monitoring regulatory compliance. I successfully argued cases before two Tribunals involving interpretation and application of law to crew and brought an end to a strike held by a crew on a passenger ship through a carefully conducted eight plus hour-long negotiation. I also managed investments of $25 million in the bond market for the Welfare Fund Society.

  • Joint/Deputy/Assistant Commissioner of Customs & Excise

    Government of India, Ministry of Finance

    (Government Administration industry)

    July 1987 — July 1999 (12 years 1 month)

    In this position I collected $130 million excise and customs duties, adjudicated 500 evasion/classification/valuation/audit cases and decided 100 refund and 25 transfer pricing cases, which exceeded performance targets by 15%. I was integral in the detection of numerous cases as well as nvestigation and issuance of show cause notices in numerous excise duty evasion, customs and narcotics cases which effectively countered tax evasion, smuggling and drug trafficking. I initiated and implemented a proposal for airborne surveillance for coastal security and anti-smuggling operations.

    In addition I spearheaded and implemented Information Technology projects to introduce online payment of service tax as well as identified and analyzed new rapidly growing but untaxed services and sectors for inclusion in the tax net to cut fiscal deficit. I led a successfully marketed tax amnesty scheme, which collected $20 million in six months and cut litigation. I also conducted indirect tax audits and inspections of over 60 different companies checking for substantial tax evasion.
