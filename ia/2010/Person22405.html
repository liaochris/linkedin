Bernard Slede’s Summary

Bernard Slede is a thought leader and change agent who thrives on business challenges. He is able to envision business strategy, create marketing and business development initiatives, quickly develop solid channels and alliances, and drive sales programs to generate new revenue.

Bernard is a true cross-functional collaborator, at ease with marketing, sales, operations, engineering and legal. He has a proven track record of providing a global business perspective and measurable value in Fortune 500 companies as well as many technology start-ups.

Career Highlights:

• Developed tangible innovation and returns via corporate venturing at Elf Aquitaine (energy, pharma, new materials), new business units at RTL and Intel, TVC (a Lockheed Martin foundation) and now HP.

• Negotiated agreements worth over $250M annually with content providers such as CNN, MTV and the BBC. Pushed through deals despite shareholder hesitation.
Saved company over 16% of content costs.

• Advised 2 laureates of California Clean Tech Open

• Established new B2B service as the #1 resource for wellness professionals

• Established new distribution channel for media company, growing annual sales from 0 to $10M. Strategically targeted and negotiated agreements, covering 4 of the top 5 cable operators in a European market.

• Launched new satellite TV service in 5 months from the get-go, including: negotiating licensing of technology, specification and manufacturing of consumer appliance, setting up distribution and call center.

• Defined export strategy for an SMB and won 2 national awards.
