Brian Swisher’s Summary

Innovative, process-oriented web designer/developer and team leader with more than eleven years industry experience. Background includes taking over and completing one of the biggest projects spanning web, mobile, and traditional broadcast technologies at Weathernews Inc., an international full-service weather company, designing UI and production processes from the ground up, allowing the company to integrate with 60 demanding and brand conscious content partners in less than 6 months. Effective time management skills that facilitate delivery on tight deadlines while staying abreast of latest web technologies. A rare combination of creative and technical skills along with a deep understanding of UI design and usability. With the vast knowledge in nearly all areas of web development comes the ability to apply that skill set in various industry verticals from global online marketing & lead generation, to consumer targeted e-commerce, to content driven web portals.
