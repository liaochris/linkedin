Ruth Tan’s Summary

President
RJS Associates Inc.
January 1999 - Present

As a small, HR Consulting Firm, RJS has always focused on working with small and medium sized (SME) software development companies, systems integrators, and software/IT consulting firms for their technical, sales and management positions. We help clients keep their operating costs down, and improve business efficiency, by channeling the HR area to us. This allows the client to focus their time and resources on what they do best, usually product and business development. Every recruiting project follows a customized process based on our client’s specific needs.

Our team of 3 recruiters and 2 account managers form two business units as needed.

1) Staffing and Recruiting Services - specializes in IT, Sales and Marketing, Executive level recruiting. Charges are typically fee or hourly based.
2) One-stop HR Consulting Services - focused on staffing, employee training and retention for SME. Charges are hourly based.

As President, I actively participate in, and am responsible for, all of the above activities.
