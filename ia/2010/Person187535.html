Shannon Register’s Summary

My Goal is to be the Realtor that markets properties better online than any other Realtor in Houston, Texas. I am well on my way now that I have the Best Real Estate Blog in Texas. I am a Social Media Pro and EPro with a Business degree from UGA and a minor in Marketing. I promote my listings online in ways other Realtors haven't even thought about. My listings sell fast, even in a down market. That's why marketing matters! I love helping people sell their homes quickly so they can move on to new things. I help buyers in The Woodlands and Spring, Texas find exactly what they are looking for in a home. I specialize in Spring Trails, Spring Lakes, Benders Landing, Lexington Woods, Enchanted Oaks, Cypress Forest Estates, and many other neighborhoods. I stay current on Texas Real Estate issues because it's important to me, but it's also important to my clients.
