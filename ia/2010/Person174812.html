Rob Howe’s Summary

An experienced sustainability consultant and business builder, I am the Sustainability Consulting Manager for Environmental Compliance Services. I assist organizations in adopting sustainability as a core component of their long-term strategy and this includes developing baseline GHG studies, climate action plans, facility assessments and climate risk mitigation strategies, all of which are short and long-term strategies for triple bottom line realization. I use a systems-based approach that integrates the mis-aligned department "silos" in modern organizations. In bringing these groups together to work collectively, sustainable strategies can be very effective. I am experienced with the Framework for Strategic Sustainability Development.

I co-founded and am the former president of Sustainnovation Consulting, Inc. where we developed clients such as Proctor and Gamble and HJ Heinz to adopt sustainable practices. I also was the CEO of Recyclaholics, a sustainable packaging products and zero waste services company. Clients included Nvidia, Doris Duke Foundation, and Vanderbilt University.

From 2004-2006, a teaching opportunity in Indonesia shifted my focus to the application of business strategies using the principles of sustainability. Before moving to Indonesia, I was a business development IT executive for 13 years managing Fortune 500 clients such as Starwood Hotels, Thomson Companies, Zurich Financial, and Fidelity. Today, as a founder of the Greater Newburyport Eco Collaborative and leading member of various environmental and community efforts, I am actively promoting regional development of sustainable and economic initiatives.

GOALS:
Drive business to adopt sustainable practices to realize the benefits of sustainability as a strategy.
