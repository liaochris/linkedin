Chris Turitzin’s Summary

I have been working in the internet world since 2002, when I created my first online business (ThinkTee, design custom t-shirts online) at the age of 17. I have worked with startups, as a design consultant, and recently I have been building my own products.

Since being a part of the Facebook Fund in 2009, I have developed a profound interest in how masses move, how movements are created, and how social networks function. I have created applications used by millions of people, and I am now applying all the principles and techniques I have learned to creating movements and building masses for political campaigns and non-profits on social networks.
