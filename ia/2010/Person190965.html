Linda M. Lopeke’s Summary

—CAREER HIGHLIGHTS—
Internationally recognized professional management consultant, business development strategist and virtual business coach and mentor with over 25 years of experience supporting corporate, government, small business and entrepreneurial clients.

Over 1,000 projects and success stories delivered (IT systems, corporate services & programs, merger and acquisition integration planning, strategic planning, social media marketing and ecommerce)

Outstanding 10-year management career in banking and financial services prior to becoming an independent consultant

More than 100 books and technical communications written and published on behalf of corporate clients

Holds record as youngest project manager in the world to lead a major technology implementation in the history of banking and financial services

Recognized leading expert in 6 professional management disciplines. Master team builder and strategic planner.

Selected by Financial Post at age 29 as one of the nation's top 5 entrepreneurs.

Also a dynamic speaker, an award-winning author, a challenging mentor and an inspiring professor teaching MBA Master Classes to students attending the world's top 50 business schools.

Served 2 terms as President of Women in Information Processing (1985/1986) and 1 term as Director of Public Relations.

Received the Crowntek Award for Outstanding Achievement in Information Technology (1986); multiple awards for technical writing from the Society for Technical Communication. Also won awards for web site design (Applied Arts Design Award) and the SmartStart Mentoring Program.

Detailed biography published in Who’s Who of Canadian Women (1986).
