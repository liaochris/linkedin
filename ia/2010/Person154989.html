  • Co-Founder

    Manovega Communications

    (Privately Held; Telecommunications industry)

    2008 — February 2010 (2 years )

    Working to develop the new frontier in download speeds over cellular networks, providing high-bandwidth connectivity in a mobile or rural setting. Manovega has developed a unique, patented technology that can increase the bandwidth of a mobile broadband connection for data intensive applications, without making any upgrades to the network infrastructure. This enables us to provide fixed-line DSL/Cable type speeds over cellular networks through a patented system that cleverly utilizes bonding multiple channels on 2G/3G/4G technologies.

    Our technology also allows bonding of mobile and fixed networks for applications like backup and failover. In addition to enabling a wide variety of bandwidth-demanding applications (such as video surveillance, remote patient monitoring, fixed wireless, high-bandwidth telemetry, real-time backup, and redundancy/transparent failover, etc), our solutions can provide high-level encryption and other features. Users can incorporate advanced tools like compression and proprietary codecs for even further communication enhancements.

    The most distinctive features of Manovega technology are as follows:
    - It provides speed-ups of 200% - 1000% times for data transfer (download & upload) on existing cellular networks.
    - It requires absolutely no changes, upgrades or modifications to the cellular network operator’s infrastructure.
    - It provides for significantly improved reliability and availability for mobile/cellular communication.
    - It works in a roaming environment.
    - It is time-proof.
