Nina Burokas’s Summary

Kellogg Marketing & Finance MBA with a demonstrated ability to drive business performance and brand value. Core expertise in brand strategy, business transformation and strategic communications. Proven track record as a change agent on a scale from Inc. 500 to Fortune 50. Excel at building relational capital and influencing across roles, cultures, generations and media.

Accomplishments
-Developed the brand concept and collaborated on the core messaging and social media engagement strategy for an award-winning computer back-up and recovery solution.
-Developed a social media SWOT framework, conducted industry-specific and best practices research and presented findings to CPG, financial, manufacturing and technology industry executives and management teams.
-Developed the brand engagement strategy and managed the web site, social media and PR for the 2009 Linda Elias Memorial Women's One Design regatta.
-As a consultant, recruited interns, developed project scopes and provided leadership for new market research, sales process and Salesforce.com implementation projects.
-As Board member and Brand Champion, drove awareness and adoption of behaviors and programming that delivered on the International Association of Business Communicators' "Be Heard" promise.
-Trained and coached multiple tiers of account teams on brand messaging and solutions selling, reinforcing market value and minimizing requests for pricing discounts.
-Re-positioned and launched professional services as a priced service versus a cost of doing business, resulting in a 270% increase in revenue.
-Developed and implemented the brand and go-to-market strategy and all marketing, communications and PR for a portfolio of award-winning software products.
-Conceived of and implemented training, automation and process initiatives that resulted in closure of chronic audit issues, a 60% reduction in financial and statistical reporting timelines and elimination of monthly budget overruns.
