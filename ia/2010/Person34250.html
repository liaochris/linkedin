Chris Talbott’s Summary

For the last 10 years, I have worked in sports and entertainment marketing and PR. Through my previous experience at Beck, BDA, Wasserman, Razorgator, and Sports Spectacular, my network has become one of my key assets. With this, I'd like to introduce people within my network, build my network and utilize key people within the network to maximize our success. If you are interested in all of this, then let's connect.

Fortunately, I've had amazing experiences and learned from my peers, some great leaders, and of course my accomplishments and mistakes. Some career highlights include: running a very successful PR campaign for StubHub.com in 2003 and 2004, securing several endorsements deals for athletes including Derrick Rose, Brandon Roy, Steve Nash, Tracy McGrady, Carmelo Anthony, Yao Ming, Mia Hamm, Gina Carano, Hanley Ramirez, and more, booked covers or features in USA Today, The New York Times, People, Newsweek, Men's Journal, Rolling Stone, ESPN The Magazine, SI, LA Times, and more, and worked with some of the top sports and entertainment executives across the board.

I hope to keep adding to this page as more experience is gained and more goals are drawn up (and achieved).
