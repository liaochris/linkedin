Kalpesh G Patel MBCS, CITP.’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic customer focused IT professional with over 10 years commercial experience, I am an adaptable professional who is able to grasp new concepts with ease and eager to learn new skills. I enjoy being part of, as well as leading, successful teams. I have developed innovative pragmatic solutions for IT Service Delivery and Support Management processes based around the ITILV2 & V3 framework.

I am able to work well on my own initiative and can demonstrate the high levels of motivation required to meet the tightest of deadlines. I possess a strong ability to perform effectively and have a keen eye for attention to detail regarding IT Service Management Best Practices.

I am an active Associate Member of the IT Service Management forum (ITSMF) and a Member of the British Computer Society (MBCS) since 1992, having been awarded Chartered IT Professional (CITP) status SFIAplus Level 5 in 2007.

I often attend various Master Classes regarding ITIL, ISO27001 Security practices & ITIL Best Practice frameworks, I have a passion for IT Asset Management (ITAM), Software Asset Management (SAM) & (PCI DSS) Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards.


I use effective SAM/ITAM processes to ensure unnecessary costs and mitigate corporate risks. I can provide ITIL Management Reporting from numerous complex IT Service Management Tools such as Remedy, Axios Assyst, Tesseract, HEAT,Marvel, Infra and various other ITSM tools as well as Asset Discovery tools like BMC, Centennial etc via Crystal Reports from V7 up to BOXI R2.
