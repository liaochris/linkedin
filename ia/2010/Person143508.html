Mike Scott’s Summary

I have a very diverse background that I feel sets me apart from other marketing professionals.

My work has included significant experience in strategic marketing, marketing communications, public and media relations, event planning, writing and graphic design, social media campaigns, Web content management and more.

I also have experience as a marketing consultant for small businesses, developing marketing strategies for entrepreneurs that do not have full-time marketing staffers employed in-house. This work has included the creation and implementation of marketing plans, collateral development, sales strategies, direct marketing campaigns, public relations and more.

I have contributed to nearly 100 magazines, newspapers and websites in a freelance capacity. I have written on numerous topics from business to public and consumer safety to sports with an emphasis on profiles and features.

In addition to my published work I have completed writing projects for private clients, including small businesses in such fields as automotive, finance, health care retail and other professional service firms. I have also worked closely with several marketing communications, graphic and web design firms as a freelance copywriter for their client in an outsourced capacity. In these and other capacities, I have written copy for brochures, direct mail pieces, websites and more.

My work also has included ghostwriting for professional service providers and placing these articles in professional trade or consumer journals. In fact my ghostwriting services include not only the article writing, research and editing but the public relations work securing publication.

My goals are both professional and personal. I want to become a critical marketing component at a growing company in an emerging industry, especially one that truly values marketing expertise. And I want to spend as much time with my wonderful family as I can (especially while our daughters are still young!).
