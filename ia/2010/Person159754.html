Patrick Ewers’s Summary

Who am I? A person who believes that relationships is the key factor in driving your professional and personal success. Moreover, I believe that you will be even more successful if have a positive, open and inviting “everyday” attitude, making it easy to build those important relationships. Furthermore I strongly believe that you have full control over your “everyday” attitude as it is controlled by your mind. This makes your mindset (and the way you manage it) the key aspect of all what is important in your life. Finally and most importantly this path (through your mind) is the only way that leads to sustained satisfaction and happiness and becomes the source all opportunities presented.

Have I lost you? If not and you felt that there is some truth here then we should talk, it will be an interesting conversation. No doubt.

What do I do? I help people and companies (that depend on their network for professional success) to develop deeper meaningful relationships with a large set of contacts (given a fixed time budget). My job is it to show how you can overcome the asynchronicity of mindshare and opportunity to generate more (financial) value out of your growing professional network.
