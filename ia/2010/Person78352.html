Griffin Cogorno’s Summary

I graduated from Laguna Beach High School in 1997 and UC San Diego in 2002 where I was the captain of the NCAA division 1 men’s volleyball team. While at UCSD; I founded and operated my first company NineFourNine Ent. distributing a line of casual active apparel throughout the West Coast and Japan with sales reaching $200k within 3 years. I have been involved in both commercial and residential real estate since 2003 with Newport Capital Group managing over 30 employees and facilitating over 100MM in transactions. Being able to complete the due diligence and analyze the financial needs and opportunities as well as the sales side helps my clients be practical and informative in their decisions. I then spent 2007-2008 competing on the AVP, Professional Beach Volleyball Tour. In 2007, I founded BrokerFree LLC. and developed a SMS client notification system that manages large groups of clients and data currently being used by agents as well as national brokerage houses. After the sale of Brokerfree; I went to work for Pico Metal Products restructuring digital media and sales tools thus increasing margins and sales by 25%. I am a hard working, assertive, entrepreneur who sees what needs to be done and takes the initiative. Currently, I am working for The O’Donnell Group a well respected Industrial Development and Management firm in Orange County.

I am very passionate about my life and being successful. I live for the challenge and the opportunity to succeed through social, technological, and personable avenues

Hobbies: surfing, snowboarding, fishing, mountain biking, playing beach volleyball and being with my family and friends.
