  • COO

    Fixstars Solutions

    (Computer Software industry)

    November 2008 — August 2009 (10 months)

    In October of 2008 Fixstars of Tokyo, Japan acquired the majority of the assets of the former Terra Soft Solutions, carrying forward the Yellow Dog Linux product line.

    Staats served as the Chief Operations Officer for a ten month transition period, providing product management, customer relation management, and sales.

  • Founder & CEO

    Terra Soft Solutions

    (Computer Software industry)

    January 1999 — October 2008 (9 years 10 months)

    Staats launched Terra Soft in the fall of 1998 and in the course of ten years was able to grow the Yellow Dog Linux brand name and customer base to one of the most recognized Linux operating systems in the world.

    Terra Soft was noted as one of the top five Apple Value Added Resellers and carried a unique license to install Linux while maintaining the full Apple warranty.

    Customers included IBM, Sony, Lockheed Martin, Boeing, NASA, and major universities around the world. Yellow Dog Linux was the leading desktop and supercomputing Linux OS for the Power architecture.

    In October of 2008, Fixstars of Tokyo, Japan purchased the majority of the assets of Terra Soft Solutions and today carries forward the Yellow Dog Linux branded product.

    Starting with his personal credit card and just a half million investment in the first two years, Staats grew a multi-million dollar company which provided a positive ROI to its shareholders in a time when the global market was failing.

    Additional information about Terra Soft may be found in Staats' personal biography: www.overthesun.com/about/staats/
