Amber Rae Lambke’s Summary

My first profitable business idea came at five, around the time I started playing with computers. I had an online magazine with 5,000 subscribers and 2 corporate sponsors at 12. In high school, I started a babysitting business that changed my neighborhood's expectations of what "babysitting" even entailed. And now it's time to make it happen in New York City.
