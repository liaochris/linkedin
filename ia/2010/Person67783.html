Ettie Shapiro’s Summary

In addition to being a Business Coach and Life Coach I am a graduate of Coach U and I have over 25 years of experience as a Psychotherapist and Executive in the non-profit sector. As a Coach, this all comes together in my work with people who want to get unstuck, work better, achieve work/life balance, make more money and have more fun. My professional goal is to help people achieve their dreams and live a life they love.
