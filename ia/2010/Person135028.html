Jeff Marmins’s Summary

Jeff founded Social Media Path to offer online marketing to emerging brands that is viral, social and interactive. Known as a marketing savvy entrepreneur, Jeff is articulate and persuasive in company positioning; how to add value, differentiate competitively and improve customer retention. Jeff is passionate about helping business grow through strategic viral marketing, online community and engaging events.

New course offerings launched at http://www.socialmediapath.com, both online classes in Social Media and classroom format in the Sacramento area.

Jeff Marmins’s Specialties:

Development and execution of an effective Social Media Strategy. Identifying desired online outcomes. Carrying out actions that are impactful. Measuring effectiveness. Integrating social media and community building with engaging events and marketing outreach.
