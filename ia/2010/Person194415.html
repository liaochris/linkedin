Stephen Hoey’s Summary

Stephen Hoey has extensive experience in solutions based document management, reprographics and color graphics. His focus is in applying his unique talents to your organizations critical business objectives to achieve long term results.

KDF Reprographics Inc., located in a 33K Sq. Ft. Headquarters in Rockleigh NJ began in 1995, and is comprised of three divisions which are:

Color Graphics: Vehicle Wraps, Murals, Banners, Posters, Trade Show Displays, Storefronts, Custom Vinyl Graphics, Legal Graphics

Reprographics: Printing for the construction industry providing innovation bid set printing & distribution and developing cost control for owners, managers and AEC.

Document Management & Scanning Services: Small business, AEC, municipalities, uitlities, schools, hospitals and the legal community offering both monthly backfile maintenance service, in-house and on-site scanning services.


KDF has enjoyed success in both the private and public sectors with notable clients such as United Water, Orange & Rockland Utilities, County of Rockland, Westchester Medical Center and Englewood Hospital.
