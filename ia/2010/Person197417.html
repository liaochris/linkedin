Sunny Conley’s Summary

WRITING
Established magazine, newspaper and business writer for regional and national publications and the Internet. Published nonfiction author. Named Author of the Year in 2002. Honored with numerous New Mexico state awards.

EDITING
Edited four nonfiction books and numerous corporate and business materials including newsletters, reports, proposals and brochures. Experienced in copy editing and proofreading.

BUSINESS & CORPORATE WRITING
Experienced in developing, writing, editing and designing a wide range of business materials, including newsletters, brochures and annual reports.

FOOD COLUMNS (Current)
Chile Knights – Chile Lore, Tales & Tidbits Plus Recipes: The Las Cruces (NM) Bulletin and online at East Valley Living (Phoenix).
Phoenix Mexican Food Examiner appears online at examiner.com.

PUBLISHED BOOKS
Mesilla Valley Roasts! Chile Lore, Tales & Recipes Plus Diners (Hot on Chile! LLC, 2009)
New Mexico Farms & Ranches Folks and Fixin's (Barbed Wire Publishing, 2002)
A Bite of History Recipes & Tales from the Mesilla Valley (Yucca Tree Press, 2000)
Cafe Hopping in the Southwest: 100 Charming Places to Eat, Plus Tips for Tourists (Arroyo Press, 1996, 2nd editions, 1997)

RADIO & TELEVISION
Radio
Food and restaurant commentator for National Public Radio Station KRGW-FM, Las Cruces, NM.

Television
Host of “Hot on the Trail! with Sunny Conley” for Public Broadcasting Station KRWG-TV22, Las Cruces, NM. Produced locally, the show aired nationally for three years. Program featured hot and spicy eateries in New Mexico, Texas and Mexico.
Anchor and scriptwriter for mini documentary: “Southern New Mexico Media Literacy.” Produced locally at Las Cruces (NM) Cable (now Comcast), the program aired nationwide.

GOAL
Recently relocated from Las Cruces, NM to Scottsdale, AZ. Primary goal is to continue to pen articles, offer editing services and to conduct research on various topics including Southwest cuisine, culture and business.

Sunny Conley’s Specialties:

Feature writer, book editor and reviewer, restaurant critic, food commentator, chile pepper specialist, public relations, marketing and promotions.
