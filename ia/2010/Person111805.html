Katherine Godin’s Summary

My successes in 2009 include getting the court to strike down a residency restriction in a Rhode Island Sex Offender Statute as unconstitutional, winning a Motion to Dismiss hearing in a child abuse case, and obtaining a dismissal/acquittal after a multiple-count child molestation bench trial.

Prior to 2009, I have aided in the submission of an amicus brief to the R.I. Supreme Court in State v. Mackie (in which the Court ruled in our favor), an amicus brief to the United States Supreme Court in Exxon v. Baker, as well as three published law review articles. I also helped overturn a murder conviction on the grounds of propensity evidence used at trial.
