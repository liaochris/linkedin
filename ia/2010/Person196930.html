Sue Ann Harkey’s Summary

Have over ten years proven experience in complex business and systems environments on market share enterprise sites in both agency and client-side atmospheres. Work collaboratively with international cross discipline teams, management, and clients through all development phases. From concept to launch of complete redesigns, content migrations, new product design and integration, enhancements, implementation, research and testing.

Specialize in dynamic, multi-asset-type, database driven, web sites and application information architecture, interaction design and content management. Utilizing asset and content metadata for ecommerce, search and account management platforms.

Lead and manage small teams of permanent and contract user experience architects, usability specialists, copywriters, designers and vendors in Head of User Experience, Site Director and Art Director roles.

Veteran champion of user centered design techniques through ‘design thinking’. Often find much of the role of a user experience professional is facilitation, mediation, and representation. Author and moderator of diverse contextual and observed usability studies, workshops and prototypes. Accustomed to working remotely in conjunction with international technology, business and design teams in methods resembling RUP, waterfall and agile.

Have dual citizenship with the United Kingdom and United States. Background includes graphic design and art direction in entertainment, games, and publishing industries. Tenured instructor at Seattle's School of Visual Concepts. Visit my website for complete work history and samples.

Sue Ann Harkey’s Specialties:

EMEA web sites. Focus on dynamic search and data base driven web sites. Ecommerce transactional interaction design, information architecture, content strategy and management, business analysis, usability testing and research, UAT bug tracking, requirements and functional specifications authoring for commercial sites and backend web application design and implementation.
