Phil Baltazar’s Summary

Energy is the most important issue of my generation. Realizing this in 2006 after having the summer seasonal gas price birch five dollars a gallon and having experienced rolling brown outs in Southern California I committed to build my career on the inevitable change underway. Starting in PV solar sales and working in the Southern California Edison territory I have experienced and implemented a wide array of strategic energy systems.

With my firsthand experience in the rapidly evolving energy business I am now applying my knowledge with the Automation services of Sure Grid for commercial customers. For companies that are interested in extracting the full value from their energy dollar we have been able to increase bottom line profit via reduced energy expenses.

With a career goal of enabling people to develop a relationship with energy, similar to how Google has developed our relationship with information, I am more convinced then ever that energy is the most important issue of my generation.
