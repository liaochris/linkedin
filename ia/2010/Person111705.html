Kate Therrien’s Summary

I am an industrial engineer by training and am passionate about healthcare systems improvement. My current role involves facilitating cross-disciplinary teams that focus on making patient-centered improvements at one of the US News & World Report's Top 100 hospitals in the nation.

In addition to my process improvement background, I have 2 years of IT consulting experience at Deloitte Consulting helping clients implement technology-enabled business process solutions. I am a highly-motivated professional with experience in all phases of a project and software development lifecycle in the healthcare provider and consumer and industrial products industries. I am an experienced team player and quick learner with strong analytical, facilitation, and communication skills.

I am interested in about applying my industrial engineering experience in non-traditional applications and have experience optimizing the workflow of a clinical laboratory, improving the referral process for a cardiology specialty clinic, and creating an optimization model for equitable distribution of antiretroviral medications in Sub-Saharan Africa.
