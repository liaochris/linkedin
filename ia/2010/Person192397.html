Carolyn D. Townes’s Summary

Spirit Women Institute is dedicated to equipping, empowering, and encouraging women to journey from a place of adversity and difficult life transitions to a place of inner peace, passion, and purpose.

Spirit Women Institute trains, coaches, and mentors women to be more effective leaders, facilitators, and communicators.

Women in Transition is a monthly support group where women gather to share, network, and find support while transitioning from a life challenging experience.

Becoming a Woman of Purpose is a monthly gathering where women share about what their passions are and what their life mission and purpose are. Visit blog at http://spiritwomen.blogspot.com.

Equip and Empower is a training and coaching program designed to give women the tools and support needed to become more powerful and effective leaders in their communities. Visit blog at http://equipandempower.blogspot.com.

Carolyn D. Townes, S.F.O., a professed Secular Franciscan, is a Spiritual Life and Leadership Coach, Life Transition Coach, Spiritual Grief Counselor and Spirit Woman. She is also a dynamic and highly sought after speaker, writer, facilitator, singer, and storyteller.

She is trained, certified and teaches pastoral ministry, end-of-life issues, Franciscan spirituality, and servant leadership. She teaches programs on leadership and life purpose and facilitates workshops for women on life issues such as anger and forgiveness, change management, and grief and healing.

She is the author and creator of The Joy of Living on Purpose™ – a spiritual program designed to guide clients in finding their soul purpose; and Equip and Empower™ – a spiritual program dedicated to equipping and empowering women as servant leaders, facilitators and communicators.

Carolyn D. Townes’s Specialties:

Group and one-to-one coaching and mentoring, individual and group facilitation, networking, creating support systems, inspirational speaking and writing, creating and delivering dynamic and power-packed workshops, classes and lectures.
