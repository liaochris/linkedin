Rare El'ements’s Summary

Rare El’ements International Hair Collection announces it’s entry into an age of sophisticated style.
The days of camouflaging damaged hair is so antiquated, Rare El’ements offers a fresh perspective on professional hair care, one that maintains a high level of performance without the use of harsh sulfates, synthetic enhancers and toxic parabens.
Rare El’ements began as a simple and distinct hair collection, and has quickly become one of the most respected names in hair care.This exotic and unique brand was not just created for luxury as much as it was a necessity. Former model and salon owners Teri LaVerne and John Amato of Beverly Hills, experienced firsthand the effects caused from the styling demands the beauty industry puts on the condition of the hair. Teri and John’s passion to find an alternative to the temporary fix of synthetic ingredients caused them to boldly push forward and revolutionize the hair care industry. This new take on hair care would ultimately bridge the gap between salon-Professional and natural hair care lines. They created innovated products that would reverse the signs of damage by combining the nourishing wealth of nature with the latest advance technology. Focusing on the treasured luxuries of cultures from around the world
they created an anti-aging hair collection that not only works for the entertainment world but was ideal for the individual needs of everyone.
Formulating each product with the healing properties of nourishing botanical,nutrient-rich nut oils, moisture boosting rain-forest butters and the calming notes of essential oils they took an everyday routine and turned it into a luxurious
pampering experience without sacrificing the performance stylists and Hollywood depend on.
