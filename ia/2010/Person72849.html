Garth Nobis’s Summary

Quality Assurance and Regulatory Affairs Executive experienced in Medical Device quality systems, regulatory submissions, and quality management for development and production life cycle phases of companies having innovative products. Over 15 years Vice President or Director level leadership in early stage, pre-IPO and Fortune 500 companies.

Major accomplishments include:
--Established and revised quality systems
--Achieved (4) 13485/9001 registrations
--Achieved (10) 510(k) clearances
--Hosted >20 FDA and State inspections
--Hosted numerous Notified Body audits
--Achieved CE, Japan, and Canadian marketing approvals; submitted IDE
--Executive team in 500% growth company
--Participated in successful IPO
--Strengthened Design Control, Software QA, and IEC Standards performance
--Established Quality/Regulatory platform in a small company for CE, clinicals
--Established productive teams
--Supported significant aggressive stent business growth

Leadership strengths include:
--Company and product strategy
--Planning and project management
--Business growth management
--Team building, employee development
--Hands on, “can do” implementation
--Internal and external communication
