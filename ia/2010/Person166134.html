Paul LaRiviere’s Summary

Augury Network Advisors LLC, an information technology enabler and managed service provider in the Southern New England region.

Augury's expertise couples technical proficiency with an experienced management perspective.

Our goal is leveraging information resources in non-profit and for-profit health care providers.
