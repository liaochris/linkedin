Stephen Bushman’s Summary

I have been many places and seen many things. For the last 8 years, most of my time has been spent as an independant contractor at Sprint, supporting their internal corporate video production facility, Creative Media Services (formerly the VCC). I acted as a sort of librarian who managed incoming production originals and edited Masters, maintaining the database and providing copies for internal requests. I also provided simple assemble edits on tape (BetacamSP, Digital Betacam, HDCam, DVCam, and MiniDV) digital (.wmv, .mpg) and disc (CD and DVD) media. I've also provided voice and on-camera talent, run robotic cameras, and run live tape playback for various productions.

I also draw; and I own, occupy and manage a small apartment building on the Westside, near downtown Kansas City, MO.
