Brian Smith’s Summary

Primary Objective - To impact business results as Talent Acquisition / Staffing Director where my breadth of talent program development, recruiter management / training, and staffing process improvement expertise will offer an opportunity to contribute, add value, and make a difference.

He is an expert direct sourcer and talent acquisition leader with ability to both recruit in high volume settings as well as improve processes, and mentor and train recruiters. He possesses strong knowledge and competency in establishing organizational practices and policies, communicating and championing values and behaviors in the organization, establishing workforce planning and diversity programs, developing talent acquisition programs, and improving the recruitment function. He demonstrates effective skills in managing P&L, branding, orientation, training, HRIS, and operational program enhancement.

Brian's balance of successes both in the executive search field and in Fortune 500 staffing gives him a multifaceted perspective and deep understanding of what it takes, in the trenches, to be a successful Corporate Talent Acquisition Partner.

Currently, Brian is providing Recruit Process Outsourcing services to Fortune 1000 and mid-sized companies. He has designed and put into practice strategies and programs for companies in need of building best practice talent acquisition solutions resulting in better quality hires, a reduction of “days to fill” for mission critical job openings, fewer agency fees paid and lower average cost-per-hire.

Brian Smith’s Specialties:

Over the years, Brian has recruited professionals in healthcare, technology, manufacturing, sales, engineering, product development, medical device, retail, and management consulting and has worked exclusively with companies such as Siemens, Sun Microsystems, Oracle, Quantum, Belkin and Edwards Lifesciences. He has run a desk, owned a search firm, held senior level corporate positions, and managed and trained recruiting teams. And he absolutely loves recruiting!
