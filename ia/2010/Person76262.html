Gloria Geller’s Summary

We all need fresh eyes to look at what we write--to see if what we're trying to say is coming through. Let me help you communicate your message clearly, concisely, and consistently--with maximum impact.

Home or office papers in a mess? Let me organize your personal and business papers so you can get tasks done on time and find the information you need.

For your business:
* All kinds of print or web-based business communications, such as advertisements, press releases, invitations, letters, newsletters, brochures, product descriptions and instructions, and web page content
* Test drive your web pages to make sure they meet the needs of potential customers
* Create filing and follow-up systems so you can move papers off your desk and retrieve information

For your home office:
* Organize the paper flow so your desk is clear
* Set up follow-up systems to remind you of deadlines before they happen
* Create filing systems so you can find what you need

For your nonprofit:
* Fund-raising appeal letters, letters of inquiry, donor reports, newsletter articles; web content; thank-you letters
* Organize your office paper flow and create filing systems
