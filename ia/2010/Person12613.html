Andy Hunt’s Summary

Heavily armed with a BFA in Graphic Design and 12 backbreaking years of experience manhandling html and pushing pixels around like they owe me money, I firmly plant my boot on the back of the unwitting populace. I have a magical noggin full of great new ideas and imagination to spare (greatly due to the second brain I had surgically duct-taped to the back of my head).

I'm expanding my horizons every day and learning how to do things more newer better faster stronger cooler. My mental reservoir of secret tricks and special mutant powers has been focused mainly on CSS, HTML, AJAX, JQuery, and all manner of illustration lately, and I try to learn new things every day. I like learning. It makes me feel smarts.

Andy Hunt’s Specialties:

Designing and implementing interactive website environments that fit strictly with current web 2.0 standards and are custom fit to suit the personality and style of the client.

Creating custom fine art illustrations using both traditional and digital media (or a heavy mix of the two) for use on websites, posters, fliers, t-shirts, books, magazines, or any where else you can think of.
