Entrepreneurial and non-laboratory roles: IT support, blog authoring, purchasing/negotiating, technical sales/support, manufacturing support.

Broad business experience at each technical position, as well as eleven years as a Watkins man: honing customer service skills, building spectacular personal and professional relationships, and investigating the concept of running a business. Bonus: discounts on the best vanilla in the world.
