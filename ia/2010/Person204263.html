Timothy Christy’s Summary

My professional background covers advertising sales, market research and strategic planning, and quality assurance. I have extensive professional writing experience, including features reporting, commercial real-estate news, and event planning materials conceptualization and production.

I have been teaching at the university level since 2000, predominately within the advertising discipline. Although my core teaching competencies are market research, media planning, and advertising strategy, I have taught the full range of advertising courses as well as courses within the mass communications and marketing fields.

My academic research thus far has centered on consumers' perceptions of promotional messages and products, with particular emphasis on offensive advertising. I have published research in the Journal of Current Issues and Research in Advertising, Journal of Marketing Communications, Journal of East-West Business, and Journal of Advertising Education. I have also presented work at the American Academy of Advertising conference, the Association for Education in Journalism and Mass Communication convention, and the National Conference on Tobacco or Health.

I am currently working on research related to online message dissemination and "brand championing."

I strive to bridge the gaps that often exist between fields and between the academic and professional worlds. My varied background and broad perspective allow me to add value to my interactions with both clients and students.
