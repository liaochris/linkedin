Dr. Kate Wachs’s Groups:

Dr. Kate Wachs is a clinical psychologist, relationship expert, communication specialist, sex/romance therapist, as well as an author, popular speaker and media professional. Dr. Kate is Founder/Director of The Relationship Center™ in Chicago. As the first & only matchmaker-psychologist in the US for years, "creating" couples as well as counseling them, she has gained valuable insight that other "experts" just don't have.

Dr. Kate previously hosted "Dr. Kate Radio Show" on WGN, heard in most of the US. One of the longest-running AOL advice personalities, Dr. Kate’s site (drkate.com) has amassed several million hits. She has written regular columns for 11 magazines/newsletters, including million+ circulation. She has lent her expert opinion to Oprah, Donahue, NY Times, Chgo Tribune & Sun-Times, LA Times, Washington Post, USA Today, Time, Newsweek, most top US publications, TV shows & radio programs. She loves thinking on her feet, recording live and interacting with an audience.
