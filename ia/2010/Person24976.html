Vishnu Dass’s Summary

Discover India's ancient science of health and rejuvenation. We offer health consultations, Panchakarma and rejuvenating therapies, nutritional and lifestyle counseling, Ayurvedic Day Spa packages, weight management, therapeutic massage and bodywork, yoga, and yoga therapy, and Ayurvedic cooking.

I have been in clinical practice for over 12 years, treating people of all ages. My holistic approach has enabled me to create individualized programs, integrating into the Ayurvedic consultation nutritional and lifestyle counseling, herbal nutrition, and marma therapy, as well as other Ayurvedic therapies and weight management programs, depending on your specific situation, goals, and needs. I am clinically trained to offer full Panchakarma detox programs and Panchakarma therapies, and personally guide and supervise your process throughout your treatments.

I also offer lectures and workshops on yoga and Ayurveda throughout the southeast at several yoga studios, colleges, and herb schools, and am always looking for new venues to teach at and spread the ancient knowledge of Ayurveda. Contact me if you are interested.

Vishnu Dass’s Specialties:

Treatment and prevention of disease, including digestive disorders, skin diseases, allergies, arthritis, auto immune diseases, liver disorders, diabetes, hypertension, and many other chronic and severe conditions.
