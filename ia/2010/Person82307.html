Michael Lee’s Summary

Innovative, experienced mechanical design/development engineer with more than sixteen (16) years of hands-on new product design/development and project management experience. Have demonstrated success in multiple new product launches working from concept development to market introduction stages. Adept at close tolerance injection-molded plastic, stamped metal, silicone and rubber component design and complex assembly design. Special expertise and experience working within FDA regulated design and development environments. Special expertise with product specification and qualification for high volume and high speed manufacturing environments.
