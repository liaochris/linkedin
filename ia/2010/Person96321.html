Jeroen van Baardwijk’s Summary

The great Chinese philosopher Confucius said: "Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life". So that's what I did, and now I have my own webdesign agency "Cyber Constructions" (www.cyberconstructions.nl). My target audience consists of business start-ups, sole proprietors (Dutch: ZZP-ers) and other small organisations.

My goals are to become financially independent by 2015 and be a "Conscious Millionaire" by 2020, after which I can devote my time and money to helping people climb out of that very deep "hole in the ground" where so many people are suffering. I have been there myself, nobody deserves to live there.
