Danny Leake’s Summary

“I specialize in “Urban Guerrilla Engineering” which means “By ANY means necessary”. With Tom Waits, I did a different recording setup for each and every song he did. (He wanted them all to sound different, like “pictures you tear out of a magazine”) For The Police, I recorded their entire “Synchronicity” show LIVE tying three studios together in the process. With Stevie Wonder and The Tokyo Philharmonic Orchestra, unassisted, I did Live Sound Mixing while simultaneously sending a different satellite uplink mix for Japanese Television and recording the show on a 48 Track Sony Digital Multi-track for the “Natural Wonder” Live CD. I believe in creating a fluid setup that is “transparent” to the making of the music…. Music should NEVER become a slave to the Technology.

I bring a lot of experience and technical “Firepower” to any situation. However, this technical ability is always tempered by my musicality and willingness to use “unorthodox” methods to get the job done.”

I Paint Pictures with Sound
