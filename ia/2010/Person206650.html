Toni DasGupta’s Summary

Toni is managing partner of Paradigm Select Assets, a fund that invests in debt related assets. The fund seeks to achieve superior returns that are uncorrelated to the equities market and economic cycle. The fund buys distressed debt instruments and processes through a proven legal and financial value add procedure, and liquidates the enhanced portfolio over a short time horizon.

Toni was previously President of BizOrigin, a business formation and strategy consulting company. Toni is active with the Tech Coast Angels and on the board of advisers of a venture fund.

A serial entrepreneur, Toni has started 3 companies. She founded WN Distribution, a distributor of specialized test hardware and software solutions to the mid market R&D and manufacturing industry. WN Distribution was sold in 2004. Toni remains active on the board of the company.

She also co-founded and headed the operations for ZennComm Communications, a satellite company based in Mountain View, California, which was formed in a collaboration with Boeing and Hughes Electronics. She was also part of a venture fund in Northern California, where she invested in and advised 5 start up companies.

Prior to that, Toni was with Schlumberger Oilfield Services, for 9 years. There she managed cross functional product development. Toni has also consulted extensively on technology and business strategy matters, and has managed several web properties.

Toni has a Ph.D. in Physics, an MBA in Finance and owns 3 patents in Oilfield Technology.
