Krista Kramer Hartman’s Summary

Krista is a collaborative, energetic, results oriented coaching, and organizing partner.

Being forward thinking, organized, and personable comes naturally to Krista. Her clients benefit from her diverse life experiences and people skills developed as a result of her dedication to working in people focused environments during her 22-year career. Her business background comes from three key areas:
1. Former golf professional
2. Former commercial real estate marketing professional and residential realtor
3. Small business development and acquisition, management, marketing and operations

Currently building upon existing spheres, sourcing referrals, and making new connections. Networking especially with Entrepreneurs, Golf Industry Professionals, Realtors; Individuals Making Transitions; and Coaching and Professional Organizer colleagues.
