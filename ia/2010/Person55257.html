Tony Weremeichik’s Summary

Tony Weremeichik is a professional residential and building designer who helps lead land developers, urban planners and home builders with innovative design ideas and excellence in architectural design. He has over 20 years of architectural design experience in master planned communities and resorts worldwide.

As the Principal and Director of Design of the architectural studio at Canin Associates, Tony’s expertise includes project visioning, urban design, production housing design and repositioning, the design of international housing types, mixed-use and village center buildings, concierge-level custom home design consulting, writing design codes and participating on architectural review boards.

During his thriving career, Tony has introduced pioneering residential design trends that have led to numerous design awards and he has contributed feature articles in some of the foremost magazines in the housing industry. He was also featured as a residential design expert on several local television episodes of HomeTalk TV and did a guest spot on WDBO talk radio.

In addition to judging the prestigious Aurora Awards for the Southeast Builder’s Conference, Tony serves on the board of directors of the American Institute of Building Design, Florida Society; he’s the past Chairperson of the Design Council of the Home Builders Association of Metro Orlando, a member of the Institute of Classical Architecture and Classical America and the Congress for New Urbanism.
