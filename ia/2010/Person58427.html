Donny Harvey’s Summary

My experience with music began in middle school with private piano lessons, clarinet in a school band class and singing in a church youth choir. I soon moved to alto saxophone from clarinet. In high school I continued with sax, focusing on jazz, but also picked up tuba in concert and marching band. I auditioned on piano, saxophone and voice at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. I was accepted on all three. I chose to study voice and eventually went on to sing professionally for the Greensboro Opera Company chorus while still in school. Also while in school I marched on tuba (actually contra) with Carolina Crown in 1996 and the world champion Blue Devils in 1997. After graduation I moved to South Carolina where I now work for Pecknel Music Company in the Greenville store's print music department. I continue to sing professionally for area churches and was music director at the Greenville Unitarian Universalist Fellowship for a year and a half. I teach private lessons in theory, ear training, music history, and to a select clientele, voice. I am also available to instruct marching band horn lines in playing and marching. If you need anything music related (other than acoustic pianos which Pecknel does not deal in) then I'm your man! Please contact me and I'll be happy to help.
