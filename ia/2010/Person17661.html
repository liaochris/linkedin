Calvin Patrick’s Summary

Patrick Company LLC is the parent company for Ashli Jewelers and Ashanti Interiors. Both companies are High-End E-commerce retailers who goal is to provide products with Extraordinary Creativity in Design with Exceptional Materials and Quality that are rare and extremely unique to the world’s most discerning Clientele. Our product mix includes men and women diamond and gemstone jewelry and products, collectibles, Objects D’ Art and watches. Our niche market is the Luxury Consumer, Collectors, Luxury Home Builders, Luxury Interior Decorators and Architects, Upscale Hotels and Spas.

What makes Ashlijewlers.com stand out from other luxury jewelers? As a commercial entity, Ashli Jewelers is committed to optimizing, not compromising, creative genius with customer-centric goals and objectives consistent with our luxury DNA. Yet, we are completely flexible as to how the objectives are achieved. We believe in welcoming and nurturing our clientele where price premium is earned and our key assets is creating long-term trust and collaborative relationships with, and between, its customers, employees, suppliers, partners, and society-at-large.

Our main objective is to focus on six + one key areas that luxury marketing must pay attention to—people, product, passion, pleasure, purpose, and price + PHILANTHROPY. We believe it makes good business sense to be charitable in new ways that truly make a documented difference to those intended to be helped. Our philanthropy program we will donate up to 5% on jewelry and 2.5% on watches and objects d' art of total sales. Ashli Jewelers will issue a check to one the following charities that our philanthropy program will support:

• American Cancer Society (ACS)
• Kiva.org – Third World loan program
• United Negro College Fund (UNCF)
• United Performing Arts Fund (UPA)
• United Children Relief Fund (UNICEF)
