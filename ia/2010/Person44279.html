Craig Slavin’s Summary

For over 30 years I have been involved in concept creation, franchise growth, recruitment and management.

I have been fortunate to have assisted many worldwide companies either develop new concepts or expand their businesses throughout North and South America, Europe, the Middle East, Australasia, India and many other countries.

ATT, Shell Oil, Walt Disney Company, Bally Total Fitness, Sears, Apple Computers, Reliance Industries, Bata Shoes, and Hewlett-Packard are some of the companies that hired us to create their prototypes.

But, the most important lesson I have learned is that I can create the best business model in the world, or someone could bring us the best business model to grow . . . but if it is not sold to the right person, with the right skills, values and behavior the business will not perform to its fullest.

As a result, in 1987 I started researching how to be predictive in the recruitment and selection of the right franchisees. Between 1987 and 1997, using a team of Industrial, Clinical, Organizational and Behavioral Psychologists we tried every personality profiling tool we could get our hands on in real life situations to see if they would work for us.

We came up with two very important conclusions:

First, all of the tools are very good tools but are specifically designed for the Employer/Employee environment. Franchisees, as we all know, are very different than employees. Their decision making process is different as are their goals, expectations and values.

Second, we learned it is not about "personality." It is about whether an individual has the right stuff to execute a particular business model and that is skills, values and behavior.

Franchise Navigator is the only franchise recruitment and selection process that leverages a values-based, behavioral profiling survey tool. We benchmark existing franchisees and create a high performer profile which all inbound candidates are instantly compared to.
