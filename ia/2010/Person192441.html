Lawrence Tam’s Summary

Mechanical Engineer
Certified Solidworks Professional with machine design experience, downhole measurement tooling with cased hole and open hole wireline, rapid prototyping, and semiconductor product packaging.

Online Network Marketing Specialist
The Network Marketing industry is struggling. Thousands if not millions of business owners are scrambling to find a business to solve their money issues. I "was" in the same boat.

Talking to everyone you know (100 names list) and cold calling people to join an opportunity was NOT the way to build a business so I could leave my mechanical engineering job behind. False promises and poor marketing training runs rampant on the internet. People are searching to connect to a "real" person to help them build their business.

Then the light kicked in. Just like in my engineering profession. People are looking to solve their 2 main problems:

1) Not enough qualified leads to speak with
2) Not making an money quickly enough to cover initial expenses and on going business expenses. Digging a hole deeper

This is the main reason for creating my teaching platform at TeachingMLM.com so normal people can actually "learn" the nuts and bolts of online marketing. Once I understood online marketing my business turned overnight.
