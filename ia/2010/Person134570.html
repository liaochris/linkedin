Sean Moran’s Summary

Seasoned emerging media executive with proven success in high growth organizations; thrives in strategic and executional roles; broad experience in internal functional management and external management consulting. Demonstrated financial planning and investor relations acumen within both startup and mid-sized environments. Strong technology background in digital video and audio systems, media networks, software development and custom hardware development. More than a decade of international business experience in Europe, Latin America and Asia. Recognized as a pioneer in place-based media with supporting expertise in retail, entertainment and logistics industries.

Sean Moran’s Specialties:

Strategic analysis, planning and execution; business development for high growth companies, especially in emerging markets; internal financing experience related to private equity, public offerings and M&A; strategic contract analysis; placed-based media; video network technologies; media management systems; targeted advertising. Industry Experience: Digital Out of Home, Retail Marketing and Merchandising experience, Transportation and Logistics, Management Consulting and Change Management
