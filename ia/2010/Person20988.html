Beau Beach’s Summary

Prowess Real Estate Companies is an innovative, local real estate firm which consists of Milwaukee Apartment Finders, Prowess Residential, and Prowess Commercial.

Milwaukee Apartment Finders

Our 100% free service includes:
* Licensed experts find apartment, condo, and house rentals that meet your requirements.
* We schedule tours and drive you to see each unit saving you time and hassle.
* Professional advice and local knowledge from search to lease signing.
* Protection and safety working with an unbiased, trusted advisor.
* Exclusive membership in Prowess Club.

MilwaukeeApartmentFinders.com will be available to renters April 15, 2010.

Prowess Residential

We are not your traditional residential brokerage. Our value packages are tailored to include the services consumers need at the lowest possible cost. We offer a $4500 flat fee listing package for home sellers and a 1% cash back home buyer package. Visit ProwessRealEstate.com to search homes.

Prowess Commercial

Our commercial services include condo management, residential property management, asset management, and investment sales.
