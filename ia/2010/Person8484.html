    VHMnetwork LLC is dedicated to providing solid, cost-effect online advertising opportunities for clients all over the globe. Through an extensive affiliate network and via its own proprietary web sites, VHMnetwork delivers what many others fail to provide - results.

    Founded in 2007, the New York City-based VHMnetwork is a leader in online advertising Cost-Per-Lead (CPL) and Cost-Per-Sale (CPS) advertising. These cutting-edge online advertising vehicles put clients in the driver's seat, by assuring they do not pay unless their ads pay off. Both vehicles are distinctly different than other forms of online advertising in a number of important ways.

    VHMnetwork also offers a lucrative affiliate program for web site publishers. This program enables VHMnetwork to ensure its clients' advertisements are placed on the best, most relevant sites possible, while making sure site owners can earn a generous income in the process. The delicate balance makes CPL and CPS advertising a win-win for advertisers and the right publishers.
