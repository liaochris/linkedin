Julie Ann Cohn’s Summary

Award winner, #1 best-selling co-author in How Did You Do That?, co-author in best-selling series' Thank God I-Vol. 2, 111 One Pages of Wisdom and author of the book CASH-Creating Achievement & Success Harmoniously, Julie Ann Cohn brings 30+ years as an advertising executive with a stellar reputation. She was founder/Chairman/CEO of Techmorrow Inc. Julie conceived the idea & co-created and founded The Media Research Council. She is respected throughout the advertising industry as an expert and innovator in Database Marketing. She has worked for major ad agencies such as DDB Worldwide, Western International Media, McCann Erickson, Davis, Ball & Collombatto, Chait/Day, Telmar Group Inc., Donnelley Marketing Information Services, D&B & Response Technologies, on biz such as: American Airlines, Toyota, McDonalds, Disney, Pepsi, Ameriquest, & many more. Julie has regularly been a guest speaker for the NCDM, The Advertising Research Federation, UCLA Extension, LMU, UCSD, NEA, CAMB & CEO Space LA.

Julie is world renowned in her healing & teaching work, having taught and provided healing to clients in Israel, London, Brazil, Bulgaria & Australia. Julie is also founder and co- creator of The Blend™ & an active partner in Silver Unicorn Spirit Gifts. A partial list of her spiritual credentials include: Usui Reiki Master, Magan David Life Energy Master, Vibrational Healer, Sufi Reiki Master, Shaman, Crystal Resonance Therapist & Ordained Minister.

In addition to her current work as CEO of J-Team Database Marketing, Inc., Julie is The Empowerment Strategist, coaching individuals and corporations in "forward motion. Success with ease and grace". She is also a certified Coach giving service to incarcerated individuals through the Getting Out By Going In Program. Work she says "feeds her soul!"

Julie graduated from UCLA with BAs in English and Theatre, The Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts, London, Master’s in Theatre and holds a Doctorate (PhD) in Metaphysics.
