Gary LaRoy’s Summary

I recently went out on my own to form Natural Path Management in order to introduce a new value-based paradigm of value-added advertising and marketing services. The approach we take at Natural Path Management is very similar to the 'Buyer Agent' in the real estate industry. Rather than represent all of the vendors to the business, I represent the business to the vendors. My seat is beside the business principals helping them make the right decisions for the media, design, and visual communication strategies and tactics.


From May, 2002 until June, 2005 I worked as a consultant for the Institute for Professional Development marketing higher education for non-traditional, adult students. My principle focus during my tenure with IPD was marketing and and recruitment management for a small private school. During this time I was able to double the number of leads generated through paid media.

From August, 1988 until May, 2002 I served as Director of Internet Technology for Cendant Travel Services. In this capacity I had full-charge responsibility for development and marketing of an Internet booking engine that supported 5 million members of a private-labeled travel club.

Gary LaRoy’s Specialties:

I specialize in developing the management processes and marketing strategies for small and emerging businesses, non-profit foundations and schools with a specific focus in educational marketing. My team of strategic partners is capable of executing branding and direct response across a variety of institutional clients.
