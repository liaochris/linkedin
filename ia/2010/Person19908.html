Barbara Pagano’s Summary

After five months on a small boat, Barbara and her daughter, Elizabeth, sailed alone over 2000 miles. The Paganos’ story motivates individuals and organizations learn how people can “Step Out to Step Up” careers and lives.

Together, they launched yourSABBATICAL.com – the gateway for people and companies interested in sabbaticals - with resources, research, planning tools, and real people who share their career break experiences. Discover companies that know the benefits and offer sabbaticals, and – if your company already has a sabbatical program – find out how it stacks up.

Combining over twenty-five years of leadership development experience, the Paganos' help companies attract, retain, and engage top talent through highly planned and structured leaves of absence.

Barbara’s blog on the sabbatical movement
http://yoursabbatical.com/the-sabbatical-mindset how and why individuals can super-charge success in career and life by taking a break- is a behind-the-scenes look at what bold companies need to know to step up and into the future to develop talent for their future.

Barbara combined her experience in leadership and extensive research in human behavior in the groundbreaking book, THE TRANSPARENCY EDGE: How Credibility Can Make or Break You in Business (McGraw-Hill, ©2004), chosen by Fast Company magazine as their “Book of the Month." Written with Elizabeth, an award-winning business journalist, the book has been among Bookscan’s list of 50 best-selling new business books in America. It hit #7 on Barnes & Noble’s list of bestselling business ethics books.

As a respected leader on credibility, Barbara offers leaders more success and higher performance through proven methods for building trusting, loyal and high-performing relationships. She inspires, teaches and holds leaders accountable for results.

Barbara is certified in Myers-Briggs, Hogan Suite of Assessments and TE9: 360 Assessment for Building Leadership Credibility and Trust.
