Dale Williams’s Summary

I am an Executive Coach and Business MacGyver* retired from corporate life and currently on sabbatical actively exploring new opportunities.

My interests lie in four areas:
1. Support for entrepreneurial opportunities that require investment.
2. Consulting opportunities requiring an accountable person to deliver well-defined business outcomes.
3. Facilitation of Strategic Processes at Executive and Board Level
4. Executive coaching.

My previous roles include heading up Standard Chartered's Retail Bank in South Africa and as an entrepreneur who with my partners sold our company at the height of the 1990's technology boom.

My Executive Coaching Practice (since 2002) focuses on leaders within large corporations and entrepreneurs facing the challenges of their own business.

I completed my (MA) in Executive Coaching through Middlesex University in 2005. See Clem Sunters comment on my thesis below.

I have a strong belief that organisations are only as good as the people that they nurture. Healthy and functional relationships within an organisation are key to its financial success.

My facilitation, coaching and consulting work with companies combine practical successes in this area with my academic learning.

When consulting I believe that the only way my assignment can be successful is to have direct accountability for well defined business critical deliverables. Writing a report or presenting a powerpoint doesn't cut it nor adds value to the business.

I am keen to take on challenges with
1. organisations going through change;
2. new businesses; and
3. with individuals who believe they would benefit from coaching.

* Urban Dictionary Definition of MacGyver: "To use ingenuity to fix or remedy a problem using only the tools available at hand."

Dale Williams’s Specialties:

Solving tough business problems, strategy development, facilitation, leadership, technology, brand building, executive coaching and mentoring.
