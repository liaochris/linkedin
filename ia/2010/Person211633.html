Victor Wachter vwachter@gmail.com’s Summary

I've been working with online communities in some fashion or another since I first discovered the Internet in 1993. I've been fortunate to have been able to build a career as a Community Manager out of what started as a hobby.

Game/Community Projects: EverQuest: Planes of Power (beta community), PlanetSide, EverQuest Online Adventures, Dungeons & Dragons Online: Stormreach, Marvel Universe Online (Cryptic Studios version), Champions Online, Star Trek Online

Other Projects: AOL brands ecommerce sites (Netscape, CompuServe, ICQ), AOL Recipes and CookingLight.com, ETRADE Mutual Fund Finder, CNET Computer Buying Guide

Deep Expertise in:

* Community strategy to maximize acquisition and retention.
* Management of feedback pipelines: I've developed processes for regular communication of community feedback and issues.
* Events Management: I've planned and executed events, online and off, including convention presence, player lunches, pre-order and stress test events and more.
* Enthusiast media relations: If they like our product enough to make a site, mod or other community-centric endeavor, I want to talk to them.
* Forum interaction: A friendly, transparent and consistent presence within the community is like gold. Plus, interacting daily with people who love what you do is my favorite part of the whole job.
* Policy development and online safety, including Terms of Service, Privacy Policy and Code of Conduct.
* Internal communication and coordination across the organization.
* Web content development, management and asset coordination.
* Website production, including technology review and selection, development planning and scheduling, online brand strategy and more.
* Crisis communication and coordination.
