Lindsay J. Furman’s Summary

Lindsay J. Furman joined Cashman & Associates June 2010 as an Account Coordinator and is recently actively seeking a position in Philadelphia.

Prior to joining Cashman & Associates, Lindsay was a Public Relations Associate for The Nouveau Image. She focused on media relations and account management. Accounts Lindsay had managed include, StrongBOX Lounge, Adopt-a-Pig Children's Charity, Union Trust Steak House and Raya Coiffure & Boutique. Lindsay was able to create exposure for her clients with coverage in the following media outlets; Philadelphia Style Magazine (Niche Media), Philadelphia Magazine, Philadelphia Weekly, The Philly Post, etc. With experience in event planning, Lindsay managed and organized a number of events including the Sonia Rykiel Fashion Show, Korto Momolu Fashion Show, Hope for Haiti Fundraiser, StrongBOX Lounge Grand Re-Opening, Union Trust The Mezz Opening, etc. In March 2010, Lindsay's interior design experience led her and Nouveau to renovate StrongBOX Lounge, located in Rittenhouse Philadelphia. (Photographs upon request)

Prior to joining Nouveau, Lindsay was the Marketing Manager for Chorus Communications and the Hair O' The Dog gala in Philadelphia. Lindsay was known for demonstrating terrific interpersonal skills. Throughout her tenure at Chorus, Lindsay created a number of innovative and successful marketing strategies that penetrated the Chorus brand deeper into its vertical market than ever before, and was successful in cross marketing the company's products and services over all other industry markets.

Outside the service and sales side of marketing, Lindsay was instrumental in the 2009 Hair O' The Dog gala. Her responsibilites included chartiy, photography, graphics, media relations, marketing materials, sponsor sales, and PR coordination. In the throes of a recession, Lindsay assisted to sell out the space while setting tickets at their lowest cost in years.
