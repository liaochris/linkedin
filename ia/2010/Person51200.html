David Kovar’s Summary

I've been involved in IT, software engineering, and computer forensics for over 20 years. In my spare time, I was professionally involved with local and international search and rescue, executive protection, and investigations . My consulting firm served as the framework to successfully combine these experiences and skills into a computer forensics and high tech investigation practice.

I am currently working for Guidance Software as a senior consultant in the professional services division. I continue to conduct forensics examinations and work on ediscovery projects, but am focusing more on cyber security and incident response.

I received a BA in Computer Science from Dartmouth College in 1986 and continue to pursue educational opportunities through certification programs, conferences, reading, podcasts, and other mediums.

As a consultant, I have worked for diverse array of organizations ranging from Sun Microsystems, Apple Computer, and Cisco to a wide variety of smaller high tech startups, non-profits, and volunteer organizations. The range of projects provided me with an opportunity to excel in some rather unstable, complex, and high pressure environments when tasked with sorting through the priorities and delivering a solution.
