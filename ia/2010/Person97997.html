Jillian de Beer’s Summary

Jillian de Beer is an international strategist specialising in enterprise development, economic and community development, brand identity and new market development.

She is an expert in business strategy, business transformation, marketing communications, relationship management, and fostering and establishing global business and social-cultural networks.

Internationally, Jillian advises on entrepreneurial business development, country brands and cultural identity, creative industries strategies, cultural tourism and destination marketing. She has developed innovative methodology in ethnography, cultural mapping, identity and sense of place analyses, experience audits, community building, and brand positioning solutions for businesses, cities, regions, national economies, and communities of interest. Jillian has developed and implemented strategies for 55 cities and urban communities and over 80 private companies.

Jillian maintains cross-disciplinary links with international networks of artists, designers and entrepreneurs at the cutting edge of creativity and innovation. She regularly facilitates creative thinking and cross-over initiatives that pioneer new product and service developments, and new market spaces.

Working with communities, she has a strong focus on cultural identity and achieving transformational experiences in urban centers through the fusion of art, design and interactive social-cultural engagement and participation. Jillian weaves this ethos into cultural policies, approaches and practices.

She has won numerous awards for taking businesses and brands to national and global leadership positions. She has chaired the prestigious IABC Gold Quill Awards and the EXCEL (Excellence in Communication Leadership) Award, and has won the IABC Chairman's Award.

Jillian is owner, entrepreneur and strategist for Jillian de Beer, de Beer Marketing & Communications and New Zealand Events, working out of Auckland, New Zealand.
