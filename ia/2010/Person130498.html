Malorie Janasek’s Summary

I am a new media enthusiast with a passion for all things digital. My undergraduate education in telecommunications, media and advertising from Indiana University opened the door and allowed me to explore the ever-evolving digital space. My current role as a new media professional in public affairs has developed me into a skillful thinker and strategic communicator who is passionate about developing campaigns to advance movement, raise awareness and galvanize audiences around important issues and causes.

Every day, I use digital platforms and online channels to successfully package and push out dynamic messaging for our clients as well as for myself. I am captivated by the emergence of technology and new media as a mainstream form of communication and seek to absorb as much knowledge as possible as I continue to grow professionally within this industry.
