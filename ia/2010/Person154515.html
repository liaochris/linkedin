  • Biology Teacher/ Science Dept. Head/ Science Club Advisor

    Valley Catholic School

    (Education Management industry)

    September 1999 — January 2002 (2 years 5 months)

    This position included overseeing the budget, conducting teacher evaluations, and teaching classes in AP Biology, general biology, and health. I also spent a great deal of time redesigning the general biology curriculum to engage my students with collaboratively led field-based experiences.

    The highlight of my time here was running the after-school Science Club program. When I took the program over, there were 4 students in the club. I changed the scope of the organization to involve environmental education, tracking, wilderness skills, overnight camping and team-building skills. Membership increased to 75 students and thus necessitated the creation of a Varsity and Jr. Varsity Science Club. Within two years, our club became the most popular after-school activity.

    I continued advising the Science Club through 2006, while also working as a substitute teacher at a variety of elementary, middle and high schools.
