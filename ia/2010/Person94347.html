Jen Capstraw’s Summary

Well-rounded, award-winning communications professional with 14 year record of success in corporate communications, public relations, marketing, consulting, account management, and broadcast journalism. Proven ability to quickly adapt to new situations and excel in fast-paced environments, leveraging early career experience in broadcast news. In-depth vendor and client negotiation experience, including technology services and print-buying. Strong problem solver. Experience in trade show exhibitions and management. Solid interpersonal and communication/collaboration skills across diverse audiences at all levels, with exceptional copywriting and editing talents. Demonstrated ability to meet conflicting deadlines in demanding environments. Strengths include ability to negotiate and influence others, entrepreneurial spirit, and can-do attitude. Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Acrobat), Macromedia Dreamweaver, Microsoft FrontPage, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook), HTML. Hold Bachelor’s degree in English/Journalism/Communications.
