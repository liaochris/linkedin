Hafiz Greigre’s Summary

In today’s society, it seems as though every third person is a recruiter of some sort. Even worse, most of them think they have the solution to your problem without even taking a moment to not only listen, but understand and evaluate the situation at hand. With such low barriers of entry into this industry it’s quite understandable why this is the case, but unfortunately it saturates the industry with poor abilities, creating this image that all staffing agencies are a commodity rather than a valuable and helpful asset. With this in mind, at TreeTop Technologies we continually set ourselves apart from most others out there; utilizing our tremendous network of professionals, developed through personal networking and word of mouth advertisement, who are able and willing to assist our search for ideal candidates, as desired by hiring mangers.

My intentions, as Human Hub, are to ensure continual development of great relationships with amazing people! Additionally, I am here to help clients and consultants alike, making their jobs, and more importantly, their lives, easier in finding the opportune matches. I encourage everyone to ask for help where help is needed as you just never know who knows who and how they can help you! Whether it’s identifying an amazing consultant, finding a job, or even something simple not related to work in any manner. This is networking! Together we can dilute the stigma that comes along with the term, as networking has always been the key to success. Especially these days where it’s not so much of what you know, but rather who you know.

LinkedIn provides a unique and compelling way to get connected. It's such a small world, so if you know me, feel free to linkup with me (I try to limit my contacts to people I know first-hand), but I’m always willing to help out!
