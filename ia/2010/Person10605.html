Anand Kuchibhotla’s Summary

At Muzigle (www.muzigle.com), it is our mission to provide the best tools artists need to create, nurture, and strengthen relationships with Indian music lovers across the globe. With these relationships the artists could promote and profit from their works. It is our mission to help artists maximize this through several features of Muzigle. Muzigle gives the artists the power to digitally distribute their works.

At Muzigle, music lovers and fans enjoy a great listening experience. You can find Albums and Tracks from your favorite artists and films as well as top notch new talented artists at a much higher quality of up to 256 kbps. Create a profile to connect with your favorite artists, share the music you like with your friends on FaceBook, Orkut, MySpace etc. One could create playlists, upload videos and photos. Moreover you could bring out the singer in you by singing Karaokes. Your Karaoke performance videos could be shared across your social networks.

At Muzigle, record labels have the resources to drive sales of their Albums/Tracks. Today the financial success of record labels comes down to the strength and breadth of the digital distribution they have on the web. Muzigle would be a great place to start with to create an effective digital distribution across the globe. Muzigle provides you a dashboard to track everything that is happening with your content and report it back to you so that you understand what is working and what isn't. We even offer you the ability to create microsites for different albums to promote effectively.

In the digital world and in the age of social networks, Muzigle creates a harmonious music community. Muzigle brings together artists, fans and record labels to build a better music ecosystem that benefits everyone.

Muzigle, Inc. is a California corporation headquartered in Cupertino, California, USA. The company is completely funded by the founders and a small group of Industry leaders.
