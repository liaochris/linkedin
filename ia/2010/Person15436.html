Anurag Harsh’s Summary

Winner of the 2009 Outstanding Asian American in Business Award, Anurag Harsh is currently the Vice President of Business Development at Adify, a division of Cox Enterprises where he is actively involved in helping publishers and leading media companies increase their audience reach, create strong brand communities, experience sustainable growth and realize incremental revenue from new sources. Prior to Adify, Anurag was VP of Marketing, Syndication and Networks at IDG, the world’s largest tech media company, where he helped rebrand IDG’s Tech Network by creating radical opportunities for ad sales and publisher growth via custom integrated deals between IDG’s owned and operated, and ad network properties. Anurag ran IDG’s worldwide Syndication business featuring premium tech content from IDG’s properties with IDG sold and embedded Ad units on celebrated websites such as ABC News, Fox, LA Times, Dow Jones & Business Week. He was also in charge of new publisher acquisition, strategic planning and corporate expansion. Before that, Anurag was VP of National Strategic Solutions at CBS incharge of media strategy for CBS Music Websites, white labeled partnerships with AOL Radio & Yahoo Launchcast, Last.FM, & 700+ streaming stations. Prior to CBS, Anurag was Director of Business Development at CNN responsible for driving growth and revenue for CNN.com. Anurag has also spent many years as a management consultant and venture capitalist with Deloitte and Charles River. He has authored the McGraw-Hill media bestseller book titled “M-Commerce Security”. Anurag maintains an active weekend concert calendar and recently performed a solo vocal concert at New York’s Carnegie Hall. He is the Chair of the Wharton Media Leaders Program of NY where he oversees focused discussions between Wharton educated media leaders such as Donald Trump, Matt Blank and Donny Deutsche. Anurag holds an MBA from MIT Sloan and MS in the Management from Wharton.

Anurag Harsh’s Specialties:

Digital Media Business Development, Media Sales and Marketing, Media Syndication, Ad Network Monetization Strategies, and building sustainable Brand Communities.
