Bill Sitter’s Summary

Bill owns the firm, Jordan-Sitter Associates (JSA), and manages the San Antonio, Texas office which also serves as headquarters for JSA. His broad equipment industry experience and accomplishments have prepared him to serve our clients well. Eight years with major equipment manufacturers in the US, Europe, and the Middle East, including management positions, are blended with seventeen years of dealer senior management experience in Texas and the Midwest. Manufacturer titles include: National Sales Manager, National Account Sales, Product Manager, District Representative Overseas, and Area Manager.

While in dealer management, Bill served as a charter member of two dealer councils, officer of a Midwestern AED Chapter, and President of the San Antonio AED.

Applicable dealer management experience was gained from positions of Branch Manager, Director of Marketing, and President and Chief Operating Officer of a major AED Distributor.

Bill has had management responsibilities for a wide spectrum of new, used and rental equipment including: Caterpillar, Komatsu, Dresser, concrete and asphalt pavers, crushing and screening, compaction, forklifts, cranes, pumps, trailers, concrete plants, excavators, conveyors, engines, compressors, drills, industrial equipment, plus attachments, recycling and maintenance items.

Bill reached the rank of Captain in the U.S. Infantry, has a degree in economics and business, and at the age of 50, finished at the top of his MBA class. The Institute of Certified Professional Managers awarded him their highest status of Certified Manager.
