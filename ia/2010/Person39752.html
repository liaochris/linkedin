Christopher Korody’s Summary

Award winning creative director and copywriter with the proven ability to coordinate concepts and content on large projects. Proven business development strategist. Power researcher.

Founder of three innovative companies. Agency experience developing customer-focused selling strategies and presentation skills.

Now offering writing for social media in addition to proposals, presentations and scripts for all media including video case studies, webinars and podcasts.

Christopher Korody’s Specialties:

Copywriter, creative director, new business proposals and presentations, video scripts, case studies, white papers, social media.
