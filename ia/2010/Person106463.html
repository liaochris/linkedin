Joycee Awojoodu’s Summary

A smart choice saves you money.

As a Sr. Energy Consultant for Smart Choice Energy Services, LLC, I help small- to large-scale commercial and industrial businesses in MD, PA, NY, and NJ conserve energy and save up to 25% on monthly utility bills. I am committed to providing top-notch customer service to all clients through high-quality, on-going management of energy needs.

As a Pricing Analyst for a top retail energy company, I was responsible for pricing all deals, staying up-to-date on the market and tariffs, analyzing new territories for possible market penetration, market analysis, and pricing green energy. I have trained extensively in the MD, DE, DC, NY, NJ and PA markets and have a keen interest in the energy industry. My strong background analyzing market trends based on historical and current market data will help me ensure our clients get the best rates possible. I approach all business relationships with honesty and integrity. Ensuring our customers are 100% satisfied with our energy brokerage services received is our priority.

As your energy broker, I will:

• Develop and maintain strong customer relationships
• Stay current on the energy market
• Keep you informed of current & pertinent energy information
• Maintain a highly proficient and professional presence
• Help your business conserve energy & reduce utility bills

Smart Choice Energy Services, LLC takes the burden of understanding trends and market changes off of your hands so you can focus on your business operations. We are fully-licensed energy consultants dedicated to managing your energy needs on an on-going basis. We will contact you periodically to ensure your 100% satisfaction, answer questions, and help you further reduce your energy usage. We maintain a relationship with only the best suppliers who are as equally committed to providing quality customer service to their clients as we are. We provide our clients with information in a truthful, open, and reliable manner.
