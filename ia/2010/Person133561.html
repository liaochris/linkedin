Mark Divine’s Summary

As entrepreneur, Mark Divine is founder and leader of several highly successful enterprises including the online community and commerce site NavySEALs.com; Government services firm and tactical supplier U.S. Tactical, inc.; US CrossFit a regional CrossFit training center and a local brewery-restaurant, the Coronado Brewing Co.

U.S. Tactical, inc. is the developer of the Navy SEAL NSW-NSO Mentor program, which took the pass rate for Navy SEAL candidates from 33% to over 80% on the Physical Screening Test at Navy Boot Camp. This program is instrumental in helping the Navy grow the SEAL force by 30% in 5 years.

NavySEALs.com is a key online destination for SEAL candidates and others inspired by the legendary SEAL ethos, training regimen and fitness. The site provides gear, training and fitness tools, and up-to-date info about the NSW. NavySEALs.com works directly with Navy Recruiting Command and Naval Special Warfare Center to ensure current information about recruiting initiatives within the Navy SEAL and SWCC programs.

Past experience includes 4 years as a CPA with Arthur Andersen and PriceWaterhouseCoopers, followed by a total of 18 years (9 Active, 9 Reserve) with the Navy SEAL community. As a mobilized reservist he help craft a Final Battle Exercise (CERTEX) which has proven critical in training SEAL Squadrons for combat operations. He also led the Navy's internal study on the inclusion of the USMC SOCOM Det 1 as a unit within the Special Operations Command.

Mark is formerly an Adjunct Professor of Leadership at the University of San Diego and has written extensively on leadership, team building, and character development. Mark's SEAL Fit training camps draw aspiring SEAL candidates from around the country, as well ordinary people from around the world who seek to challenge themselves in a program which has been called "the most physically and mentally demanding training course outside of the actual Navy SEAL training" by former camp trainees.
