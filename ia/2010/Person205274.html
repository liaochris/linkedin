Todd Barrs’s Summary

Founder & CEO of SpyGlass, LLC:

SpyGlass, LLC is a conversion optimization, website usability and online marketing agency in the Denver Metro area. Our “data-driven-decision” approach to online marketing ensures that our services provide positive ROI for our clients’ online marketing budget and supply valuable, revenue-generating contributions to their businesses. We pride ourselves in providing “best in-class” services, and reaching above and beyond our competition by placing a strong value on transparency and client education.

Founder and Head of the Boulder/Denver Conversion Optimization Meetup Group:

The purpose of our group is to provide education to marketers and executives in the fast growing industry of website conversion optimization and usability. While the subject of usability is not relatively new, there is a growing public awareness and demand for the industry. The goal of the group is to educate by offering monthly presentations that provide value to members' businesses and clients.
