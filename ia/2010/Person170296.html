Rebecca Geier’s Summary

For the majority of my career before co-founding TREW Marketing, I led communications for National Instruments. That included responsibilities for all areas of corporate communications, from Investor Relations to PR to customer publications to community relations. I had the unique opportunity to get involved in global communications, training all our leaders from Europe to Asia and across the US for on-camera interviews and large event communications.

Our goals with TREW Marketing are to become a smart, collaborative and measured partner to our clients while growing our company to provide a meaningful, challenging and fun work environment for our staff. We work primarily with B2B technology companies and education organizations.

Rebecca Geier’s Specialties:

I love communications - from internal employee and executive communications to external PR, media relations, and training executives and spokespeople. I thoroughly enjoy the process of getting to the message of a campaign, an interview, the title of an email - whatever the venue may be.

Rebecca Geier’s Experience

  • Principal and Co-Founder

    TREW Marketing

    (Partnership; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    March 2008 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    TREW Marketing is a business and strategic marketing consulting firm based in Austin, Texas working with B2B technology and education organizations. TREW Marketing founders have nearly 30 years combined experience in high-tech marketing for one of the country's recognized top companies, National Instruments. TREW Marketing specializes in architecting a customized marketing plan based on your business objectives, with special focus in the areas of PR and communication, events, and electronic marketing.

  • Director, Corporate Communications and Investor Relations

    National Instruments

    (Public Company; NATI; Electrical/Electronic Manufacturing industry)

    August 1994 — July 2008 (14 years )

    A primary focus of my career has been working with the media and managing corporate reputation to all stakeholders, including the trade and technical press, business press and local media, and employees and the community. I worked closely with the global leadership team, and worked with employees and members of the press in most regions of the world. Rebecca worked closely with NI CEO, CFO and VP of Marketing to set the strategy, expectations and overall financial communications program to Wall Street. I managed customer publications – both print and electronic – and worked closely with in-house creative and media teams to build effective programs to drive engagement to the web. I also managed the global employee communications team responsible for honest and timely communication, was instrumental in establishing the company’s world-class community relations program, and co-led the development and ongoing management of crisis communications, serving as the first point of contact in case of a crisis to all management worldwide. I am very proud to have been selected to lead the company’s team responsible for establishing the NI Citizenship program, encompassing the company’s commitment to its people and culture, its innovative products that empower and inspire customers, and its commitment to reduce the company’s environmental footprint.

    My team rocked at NI! We were honored in 2007 and 2008 by PRWeek as one of the top Corporate Communications departments in America. In addition, we were named the Best PR Team in Texas in 2003 by the Texas PR Association among previous winners including Southwest Airlines, Dell and USAA. In addition, in 2006, PRSA-Austin recognized us as having the best employee communications program in honor of National Instruments’ 30th anniversary celebration.
