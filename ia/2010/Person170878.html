Reinout H.M. te Brake’s Summary

Currently Reinout is working on MMO LIFE (http://mmolife.org). MMO Life owns the world’s largest portfolio of popular, local and successful MMO, RPG and related domains. The strategy is based on relevancy and simplicity. Driving online traffic to an easy-to-navigate site that is relevant to them demographically, linguistically and with appropriate content.

The company then launched MMO Traffic, a division of MMO Life, in March 2010 as a new international MMO marketing company.

Reinout is well connected in the global online entertaiment industry. He started TM Ventures where he focusses on assisting companies with opening his network, strategy and know-how. Reinout is an advisor of several online companies and venture funds.

Prior to that Reinout operated since april 2005 as a Strategist of SPILL GROUP where he played a major role in transforming the company in less then three years from a local game portal owner into a worldwide acting game distributor, operating game portals in 20 countries, reaching almost 70 million unique players a month. Under his supervision Spill opened offices in Poland, France, Germany and China.
