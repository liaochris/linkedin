Kathleen Dorsey’s Summary

I have been a business owner since 1999. I founded Global Results for two important reasons. The first is to aid companies with their Internet exposure while teaching them how to execute our strategies consistantly themselves.

By using advanced Search Engine Optimization (SEO) techniques in conjuction with strategic Social Media campaign- a company can watch their website traffic grow as well as their sales.

The second reason that I work so hard is to help women and minority owned businesses achieve Internet success. I love to watch companies achieve Internet power!

Global Results focuses on getting targeting traffic to your website by implementing advanced SEO and Social Media techniques as well as creating Google "friendly" strategies.

We work with "entry" level companies as well as those whom are Internet savy but may need strategic plans created.

One consistant comment that I receive from our clients is that we "get it". We understand the everyday obstacles that a business owner conquers. You do not have 3 hours a day to focus on your Internet Marketing- and you shouldn't. That is where our Social Media Management services come in handy.

We explain Internet Marketing in "business talk" not "tech talk" while giving you strategies which will fit into your daily time constraints.

"Global Results Brings The World To Your Site."

Some of our clients include: Irvine Nature Center, NAWBO- Baltimore Chapter, ROMG Chamber of Commerce, Gerstell Academy, mycity4her.com, Long and Foster

Kathleen Dorsey’s Specialties:

Our highly trained SEO programmers and experts are amazing. They have taken companies from being lost in space on the Internet to the first page of a Google search quickly and efficiently. In addition, we create and execute strategic and customized Social Media campaigns.

One of my passions is to teach how to harness LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter for your business and career. I do this through webinars, live seminars as well as private training sessions for individuals or groups.
