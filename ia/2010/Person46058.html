Dalila Ali Rajah’s Summary

Dalila Ali Rajah is currently working on "Cherry Bomb!", a talk show she created and Executive Produces for OutTV and MTV owned www.afterellen.com. She is a graduate of California Institute of the Arts with an MFA in acting.

She was most recently seen on stage in "Blubonnet Court", **WINNER**, glaad Media Award for "Outstanding Los Angeles Theatre 2007", "The Vagina Monologues," and "Joleta," whose cast was nominated for an NAACP Theatre award for Best Ensemble Cast. She also appeared in "Darwin Awards" starring Winona Ryder and Joseph Fiennes.

She followed in the footsteps of five generations of women from her family by attending Spelman College, receiving her BA in theatre. She is a published poet, spoken word artist, and painter.

She made her first foray into producing film with the short film "echoes", which just completed it's tour of the film festival circuit. She is in the development process for her second undertaking as a film producer, Secrets & Toys, and enjoying the success of "Cherry Bomb!" which currently airs on www.afterellen.com and www.downelink.com.
