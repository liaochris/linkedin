Dr. Marvin Lee’s Summary

Referring to himself as Korean-Canadian-American, Marvin was born in Los Angeles, raised in Vancouver, Canada and is of Korean decent.

He received a Bachelor of Arts in International Relations from the University of British Columbia and eventually relocated back down to Los Angeles beginning an internship with Warner Bros. Studios.

After getting 6 teeth knocked out in a hockey game and visiting his cousin who is a practicing Oral Surgeon, Marvin decided on a change in career and started the pursuit of a new profession. In 1995 he began his Doctor of Chiropractic program from Cleveland Chiropractic College of Los Angeles, and finished his 4 year studies in only 3 years, graduating Cum Laude.

Marvin C. Lee is currently the Owner/President of Lee Chiropractic & Rehab Wellness Center, Inc., which provides a total body, and holistic approach to healing, specializing in industrial injuries, auto accidents, sports injuries, and pre & post surgical rehabilitation.

He has been involved with Love L.A. Ministries on skid row in downtown, Los Angeles and his interests include the reunification of Korea through the organization, LiNK (Liberty in North Korea), and is a member of Young Nak Celebration Church, through which he has been commissioned to participate in medical mission trips; most recently to Mongolia.

Marvin’s career path and goals have come full circle and he is now actively pursuing a career in the entertainment industry which includes voice-over work, commercial and theatrical work. He is actively training with some of the best in the industry, such as Howard Fine, Margie Haber, Laura Gardner and Anthony Barnao.

Marvin is creating for himself and his life the possibility of Fun, Unstoppability and Love!

Dr. Marvin Lee’s Specialties:

Dr. Marvin C. Lee is a Cum Laude graduate of Cleveland Chiropractic College in Los Angeles, CA and has managed numerous clinics throughout Southern California. Since then, Dr. Lee came to Santa Monica, CA and founded Pacific Chiropractic & Rehab Center. He specializes in, but is not limited to, a number of the following chiropractic techniques: Diversified, SOT, Flexion Distraction, Postural Analysis, and Golf-related Injuries.
