Bryan J. Zaslow’s Summary

JBCStyle is a team of 35 and the leading full-service recruiting agency with a focus on the Fashion, Beauty, Action Sports, Retail and Home industries. With over a decade of experience matching key brands with their ideal candidates, we are uniquely positioned to help you, whether you are looking for top talent or you are looking to take the next step in your career.

JBCStyle was recently honored to receive recognition on the prestigious Inc. 500 and Inc.--List of the Fastest Growing Private Companies, ranking #74 overall, (additionally, ranking No. 3 in the Top 100 Human Resources Companies category, and ranking No. 7 in the Top 100 Businesses in New York-Northern New Jersey-Long Island category).

Why should you get to know JBCStyle? Our recruiting specialists, each with a distinct style and skill-set, have invaluable relationships and that is what our business is built on. We are plugged in to the worlds of fashion and retail beyond staffing and have the ability to assess the market and attract the best talent out there. We have earned a reputation for quality, reliability and personalized service. As our team continues to grow and broaden its reach, we maintain our commitment to providing the best service in the business. We want to get to know you.

JBCStyle has offices and an extensive network of recruiters in New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco and Boston enabling us to recruit nationwide. Finding and retaining top talent is critical to the success of any business. We look forward to opening our network up to you.
