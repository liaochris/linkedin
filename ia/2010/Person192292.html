Spencer Newman’s Summary

I am a seasoned executive with fifteen years of strategic planning, financial modeling, database marketing, sales force development, venture capital, and entrepreneurial experience. In the past, I have held positions as Vice President of Marketing at Home Bistro, Director of the TastyGram division at Vermont Teddy Bear, and CEO/ founder of Adventurous Traveler, which was sold to the Away Network in 2000. I received my MBA from Kellogg in 2009 and assumed the CEO position at Jvillage Network in April, 2010.

Spencer Newman’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, financial modeling, CRM/ database marketing, sales force development, market analysis, product innovation, and private equity fundraising.
