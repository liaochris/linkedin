Rob Dewey’s Summary

Rob Dewey has been a strategist, creative director, writer, editor and musician for more than twenty years. His work connects creativity, strategy, insight and performance to deliver understanding, differentiation and deep engagement. Rob's work and and ideas have been published by Communication Arts, Print, Eye, I.D., How, Step, inForm, AIGA and in the books Looking Closer 2: Critical Writings on Graphic Design and Designing Identity: Graphic Design as Business Strategy. Rob serves on the adjunct faculty at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design, and was named a Fellow of the American Center for Design in 1995.
