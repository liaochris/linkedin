Andrew Fawcett-Wolf’s Summary

Senior General and Commercial Manager with over twenty years experience in print, digital media related (multi-market) commercial product (advertising and software) and services management. Focus on making it happen rather than the theory which is all well and nice.

I have been instrumental in the European commercial launch and management of four separate US advertising led media/software related companies - allowing them to establish themselves into the diverse and sometimes bewildering (especially for them) European marketplace.

My aim is to help companies engaged in the digital advertisning arena to leverage their assets more efficiently and / or bring them to market via various means. Happiest when rolling up my shirtsleeves closing mutually advantageous deals - largest deals closed $52m (back in the day!), then $10m $7m, $1m etc

Clients are digital media owners (including: AOL Europe, CNET Networks, Hachette Filipacchi, Johnston Press, Macrovision, Magicalia, Metro International, LinkedIn, Lunarstorm, News International, WeeWorld, Sulake etc), agencies (OMD, Mindshare, MEC, Zed etc) or digital marketing / publishing technology owners (Emediate, Macrovision, Microsoft, Yahoo!, Message Systems, ). I have advertising sales management experience in print (VNU & CMP), mobile (Vodafone), direct (24/7, Vodafone) and digital media (AltaVista, 24/7, Vodafone, Trafficmac / Operative, Thrive )

Andrew Fawcett-Wolf’s Specialties:
