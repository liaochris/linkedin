Dylan Conroy’s Summary

Dylan Conroy went to UCSB and studied screenwriting earning a BA in film and new media. Dylan has a certificate of Entertainment PR and screenwriting from UCLA. He is a graduate of the Act One Screenwriters program.

While at UCSB Dylan interned at The Santa Barbara Film Fest and Ivan Reitman's Montecito Pictures.

Dylan was as an account exec with Howard Bragman @ BNC. He worked with Stevie Wonder, The Red Hot Chili Peppers, Mark Cronin and Phil Rosenthal.

Dylan then served as Bruce Nash's personal Research Assistant. While at Nash he developed reality television and feature film concepts.

Dylan left Nash to pursue experience as a Segment Producer on the Profiles TV (Amazing Race) Harpo (The Oprah Winfrey Show) joint, The Big Give for ABC.

Dylan then joined Chris Usselman as a producer and development consultant on a reality show called The Hearts and Minds project which is currently under consideration at major productions companies and agencies.

Dylan now runs a successful consulting business where he helps clients develop, package, sell and produce reality content as well as manage other creative producers and writers.

Dylan has optioned shows to National Lampoon and Eco Maniacal Productions.

Dylan is a member of entertainment professional development groups including Connecting Reality and Act One. He is also the founder and president of Sigs in Entertainment, a networking group for Sigma Chi's in entertainment.

Dylan is also a Founder of FlashCity.com a new media company in the arena of celebrity news.

Dylan was a financier @ Strata Productions where he managed a group of equity partners who invested in independent films.

Dylan joined Ferrazzi Greenlight to work for one of his all time busines heros, Keith Ferrazzi, by heading up West Coast Sales for the Relationship Masters Academy.

Keynotes: Hollywood Connect, UCSB Film and New Media Alumni Panel and Film 54, First Allied Securities Next Generation Wealth - Relationships for Revenue Growth

Management, Screenwriting, Networking, Relationship Building, Development, Production, Post Production, Negotiations, Public Relations, Event Planning and Execution, Launching Start Ups, Independent Film Finance and Production
