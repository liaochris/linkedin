Anna Marie G’s Summary

I have owned Devious Wigs &Things here in the valley since 1998. In 2001 i went online with an ecommerce site at www.deviouswigs.com and in 2007 i opened up ShopDevious.com, an Upscale Resale Boutique, Beauty Supplies Shop & Internet café along Grand Avenue in downtown Phoenix. My boutique is the ‘08 winner of “Best of the Valley” by New Times, and '08 “Best Of” by Phoenix Magazine. I pride myself in offering devious deals like the recently acquired Micheal Kors sandal high heels sold for $35, or the Prada mens shoes going for $65. You will find me shopping, creating, styling or directing fashion at any given moment.

Anna Marie G’s Specialties:

I am a custom anime wig designer. My boutique, offering vintage, name brands, antiques and overstock, is the monthly host to a free clothes swap (every 3rd saturday from 12-3pm) as seen on FOX 10 and creator of the "Bag Lady" program, encouraging recycling by accepting recycled store bags for discounts on daily purchases.
