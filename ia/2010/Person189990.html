Simon Higgs’s Summary

With formal training in art, music and telecommunications, Simon Higgs provides expertise in the converging media industries, integrating technology with the arts. He pioneered online consumer promotions and social networking, founding Coupons.com in 1994, and won the 1996 CommerceNet VIP Award for Best Online Community.

Simon Higgs’s Specialties:

Digital music distribution, artist relations, consumer promotions, social networking, convergence, design and planning, strategy.
