Don Stewart’s Summary

After a career in the computer industry I wanted a change and became a Realtor in Oakville, Ontario.

I was reasonably successful selling houses, but found myself spending more and more of my time thinking about how agents marketed and promoted their services.

I parked my license and started a web service - Agent Invitation.

We help consumers find the best agent for their specific needs - we're all different.

We help agents spend less on traditional advertising and promotion and concentrate on giving the consumer the information they need to be able to find and hire the best agent for them.
