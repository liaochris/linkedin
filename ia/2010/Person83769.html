Ian Emerson’s Summary

Ian has over 25 years experience of the application of IT technologies and process improvements within industry. His most recent role, with Autodesk has focused on market research into a number of industries including materials handling, automotive and consumer products to support of an industry solutions initiative.

After completing MSc studies at Napier University into Interactive Technologies for E-commerce Ian undertook a number of assignments: Scottish Power - development of a project office across six business divisions, Agilent Technologies - research into product portfolio managent.

Priot to going to Napier University Ian moved into freelance consulting and secured a 6 month contract with Babcock Engineering Services to support a major process change initiative across their facilities at Rosyth Dockyard.

Ian has also held a senior-consulting role with CIMdata, a consulting firm specializing in product lifecycle and product data management (PLM/PDM). During this period he was involved in defining and delivering strategic PLM/PDM solutions for a number of large multi-national European engineering companies including Renault, Schlumberge, adidas Salomon and GIAT defence.

Prior to working for CIMdata Ian was the lead Product Data Management (PDM) consultant for Bull Information Systems and was responsible for the first Bull UK Metaphase installation.

During his career Ian has worked in the US and a number of European countries including France, Germany, Norway and Spain with companies within the defence, aerospace, utilities, manufacturing and project industries.

Ian is a professional member of the British Computer Society and has qualifications in Mathematics, Statistics, Numerical Analysis and Computing.

Ian Emerson’s Specialties:

Market research into manufacturing domains, industry and computer business solutions consulting, process analysis, training, all aspects or data management, pre-sales and bid-management and project management.
