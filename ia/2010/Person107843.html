Judy Jewell’s Summary

Judy is an exceptional business leader with extensive experience in managing complex projects and negotiating multi-million dollar contracts. Judy is a visionary who quickly recognizes the importance of a technology to meet a business objective.

One of the biggest achievements in her successful career was bringing her company into the world of converged technology utilizing Cisco VoIP. She led two large data center build out and moves with hundreds of servers over a two day period with zero downtime.

Judy is an expert negotiator and was responsible for negotiating multi-million dollar contracts relating to wireless, local, long distance, bandwidth and data housing resulting in a $750,000 annual corporate-wide savings.

Judy serves on the Board of Directors for the Virginia Musical Theatre and work with the Virginia Zoo and American Cancer Society. She is also active in the Norfolk Chapter of InfraGard. Although she is passionate about every non-profit group she works with, she is most proud of leading the Virginia Zoo’s annual fundraiser, ZooToDo. The event was one of the highlights of the fundraising season and drew thousands of people raising much needed operational funds for the zoo. Judy has appeared on local television news shows, has been featured in The Virginian-Pilot and the National Civil War Museum’s newsletter. She was featured on a recent segment on ABC's Good Morning America.
