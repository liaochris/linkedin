Rick Whittington’s Summary

For the past three years, I've been living the dream as the President of my internet consulting firm, Rick Whittington Consulting. While the name's not very original, the work is, and I'm having a blast getting measurable business results for our clients.

My responsibilities include pitching new business, growing the company, managing design/marketing strategy, information architecture and creative on our projects, and creating meaningful relationships with other like-minded firms and talented web workers.

Before starting my company, I spent two years as a web project lead at the United Network for Organ Sharing.

I also have experience living the corporate lifestyle -- I spent nearly 6 years as the Design Manager for the Circuit City web site (long before the company went belly-up) and a short stint at Crutchfield Corporation as their Design Manager.

Work must be fun and it must be a challenge. I enjoy working with my clients because I like to meet people and solve their business problems. Simply put, I love my job.
