Athan Rebelos’s Summary

I have over 23 years of experience in all sectors of the taxicab industry. I began my career on September 17th, 1986 in Seattle, Wa. I have since owned my own cab company, owned a taxicab equipment company and have driven taxis in Los Angeles, Oakland and San Francisco. I am a shareholder in a New York City based taxi medallion brokerage

I have been involved with the San Francisco taxicab industry since September of 1995. I was heavily involved in the restructuring of Seattle's taxi system in the early 90's. Here in San Francisco I was the Driver Representative on San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency's Taxicab Advisory Group. I serve now as a Color Scheme Manager on SFMTA's Taxicab Advisory Council.

I am very active, enthusiastic and involved at SF Green Cab as a Member (by consensus and acclamation), Board Member, General Manager and a Driver.

SF Green Cab LLC, is a start up which is socially & environmentally responsible. Unfortunately this is a first for the taxicab industry. I am very happy to be a part of it.

I am very much enjoying my work in taxicab management. I find it to be very challenging however it is also very rewarding. The challenges are exhilarating and the participants greatly vary. I try to be involved with every aspect of the taxi industry. My typical work day will have me meeting with part time taxi drivers and CEO's and everyone in between. I can be in a suit at 10:AM, in coveralls at 3:PM then casual until 7:PM. I certainly have full work days.

I hope to continue along this path and to retain this variety in my work day but I also intend to develop my career in management and consulting.

Athan Rebelos’s Specialties:

I am excellent at pursuing an objective goal. I can identify issues and the solutions. I have excellent fleet management and staff management skills. I am a great listener and I have excellent coping skills. I am a leader.

I have an incredible ability to understand a taxi market and the best way to maximize profit while maintaining a high level of service and integrity. I am tenacious to the extent of achieving my goal.
