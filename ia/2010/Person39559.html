Christopher Buls’s Summary

Chris Buls is a skilled leader with extensive experience across a broad spectrum of finance and accounting activities within S&P 500 companies as well as small businesses. His organizational skills and sense of urgency, combined with his ease in building relationships and establishing lines of communication with critical stakeholders has facilitated the success of organizational goals based on top line growth, cost reductions, installation of streamlined processes and improvement in the controls environment.

Chris is also a quick study enabling him to rapidly learn new businesses and seamlessly translate his skills across industries as diverse as aerospace and defense, specialty materials, chemicals and utilities. He has routinely entered organizations in transition and collaborated with the leaders to drive necessary and lasting improvements while building a strong, business focused finance function. His understanding of the development and utilization of metrics to drive process improvements and increase profitability has resulted in successful implementation of numerous projects delivering over $3 million of annual cost savings, significant inventory efficiencies and a step change in the controls environment.

Chris led a Companywide revenue process remediation enabling a successful IPO at American Water. He has also conducted acquisition due diligence and integration along with evaluating divestitures and reorganizations in the United States and internationally. Additionally, he has developed the modeling of complex business activities including lease/buy analysis of a railcar fleet, make/buy analysis supporting back integration of the supply chain, decision support of “Greenfield” manufacturing sites in the US and Latin America and detailed cash flow projections supporting business and financing activities.
