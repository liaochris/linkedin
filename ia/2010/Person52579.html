About EngineerOutsourcing - An AutoCAD & Engineering Service Marketplace

EngineerOutsourcing (EO) is an online marketplace that connects small and medium sized businesses with qualified and cost-effective service providers for outsourcing requirements. Engineer Outsourcing focuses on serving your needs as a corporation for Engineer Process Outsourcing (EPO) functions such as CAD drafting and simple engineering solutions.

EO is a Canadian company located in Calgary, Alberta and has built connections with reputable engineering firms in India that provide first-class work. These providers hold international accreditations and certifications and complete projects at a very low cost. The cost savings can be tremendous as companies can save up to 85% on labour cost through using EO compared to domestic in-house staff.

EO has service providers that can work in all major engineering areas. Our experts work in a variety of industries with the major areas including:
1. Architecture and structural Engineering
2. Civil Engineering
3. Mechanical Engineering

What are the Benefits of EPO to Your Business?
Aside from achieving substantial cost savings there are many additional benefits from using EPO in your business. These include:
• To focus on your business’ core functions.
• To acquire new skills.
• To focus on strategy.
• To avoid major investments.
• To handle overflow situations and cyclical work.
• To improve flexibility.
