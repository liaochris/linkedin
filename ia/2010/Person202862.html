Thomas Ellis, PE’s Summary

Have spent most of my professional career on the engineering and design side of construction. However, I have considerable experience with electrical construction estimating, pre-construction activities as well as project management. As an engineer with knowledge of electrical construction costs, I have been able to consistently engineer and design facilities that meets the end users needs at a reasonable cost. As a design/builder I am able to keep a handle on construction costs, plus provide a quicker turn-around time for construction documents, and shorter permitting periods.
