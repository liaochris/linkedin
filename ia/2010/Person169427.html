Ravi Kabra’s Summary

An eMarketer by Profession, Ravi Kabra has been instrumental in setting up and managing both Media Contacts and Center of Excellence (CoE) operation in India. In 2008 the CoE was made an independent company called Ecselis and he took charge of the company as its Managing Director.

He has been involved in Online Marketing since 1999 encompassing areas across e-marketing. He has deep understanding of marketing, web technology and associated tactics. He has rich experience in Online branding, cross channel ROI measurement using analytics, SEO, PPC / SEM / contextual advertising, Affiliate marketing, Content Management Systems and Social Media marketing. (He is also a Google Adwords Professional). In the past he has run a portal, been a web service provider and an e-marketing adviser to some leading advertising agencies, he has also worked for about 2 years as Senior Researcher with a leading Indian Market Research company, where he worked with clients across industries on quantitative research projects covering the entire marketing cycle.

Key client experiance:
Online: Dell, Expedia, Air France, Emirates, Reckitt Benckiser, Geojit
Research: Effem India (Masterfoods), Tata Teleservices, Parle, Diageo

He holds a PG Diploma in Marketing & Advertising and a Bachelors in Business Management. He has participated in several conferences and has been a key note speaker in few. He occasionally gives lectures at educational institutions in the area of Management, Marketing, Market Research and Online Marketing

Ravi Kabra is an evangelist of Toastmasters in India - He is also the Founder President of the first and only Toastmasters community club in Mumbai - called Mumbai Toastmasters (www.mtm.in)

entrepreneurship, people, !deas, places, advertising, technology, innovation, strategy, consulting, and philosophy are of his chief interests
