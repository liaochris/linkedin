Michael P. Wallace’s Summary

Michael Wallace brings over three decades of entertainment, music, and technology experience to his role as a producer at Firepan Media. As a seasoned executive and musician/performance artist, he has a keen ability to balance creative inspiration and business savvy to help clients use music to set the mood, capture attention and support their creative vision.

Prior to founding Firepan Media, Mike was the President & CEO of 28FootCat Media Group, a company that provided media and technology solutions for corporate and individual clients. Mike also spent over 20 years recording and performing as a guitarist and vocalist with well-known artists such as Kenny Loggins, Keith Emerson (Emerson, Lake & Palmer), Marc Bonilla (Toy Matinee, Dragon Choir), Glenn Hughes and David Coverdale (Deep Purple and Whitesnake). He is able to tap into this deep experience with a wide variety of music styles to provide Firepan clients the right musical solution for their specific needs.

Mike has also enjoyed a rewarding career in technology working as an executive for companies such as PacifiCare Health Systems, Prescription Solutions, United Health Group and most recently Corinthian Colleges, Inc (CCi). At CCi, he was the VP of Infrastructure and Operations with overall responsibility for the support and delivery of Information Technology services across 128 geographically dispersed locations. He uses the management and business skills he garnered through these experiences to help keep client projects on track and ensure Firepan’s creative products are always in line with customer business objectives.

Mike holds a B.S. in Business Management and is a graduate of Jamie Faunt’s Creative Music program. He has also earned several Information Technology and business strategy certifications and has completed numerous leadership courses. He enjoys time with family and friends, mountain biking, off-roading, and boating, and is actively engaged in his church and throughout his community.
