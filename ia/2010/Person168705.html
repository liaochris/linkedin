Randal Moss’s Summary

Randal is a digital marketing strategist with over a decade of experience in developing, launching, and measuring digital engagement programs, brand management, driving revenue, and managing relationships.

He is a nationally recognized social media marketing, and social network advertising professional having keynoted national conferences such as SXSW, HeSCA, AAMC, and run training for the American Marketing Association.

Randal's experiences include managing and delivering projects based on technologies such as; Interactive Websites, SEM & SEO Campaigns, Mobile Devices, Social Networks (white label and mass media), Online Communities, CRM systems, User Generated Content, Virtual Environments, Loyalty Programs, and E-mail marketing.

Clients include: Ethicon Endo Surgery (Johnson & Johnson), AIDS Research Alliance, American Association of Medical Colleges, Financial Care Inc, Northlich, Best For Babies, The United States State Department, The United States Congress.
