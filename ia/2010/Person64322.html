Justin Milliun’s Summary

From missiles to microscopes, storage systems to networking gear, radio telescopes to high-performance semiconductor chips: deep experience implementing and managing embedded system development and test. This means I’ve coded and/or managed from assembly through compiled languages, script languages and beyond…

Silicon (chip) test and validation, deep experience with embedded real time operating systems and application software including image processing, auto-focus, closed-loop systems. Experienced in hardware/software tradeoff consideration.

Looking for opportunities to leverage software skills and experiences in the areas including data storage, networking, green technology/energy, medical/industrial or aerospace, wherever there are critical embedded systems.

Development engineering lab management including facilities, equipment procurement, system maintenance and resource allocation.
