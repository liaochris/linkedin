Paul Chaloner’s Summary

*Update*

I am currently working out 30 days notice with Heaven Media as their Director of Esports Content and I am actively looking for new roles or opportunities in the same sector of video gaming or esports.

I am also available for paid work on voice overs, TV, Radio, advertising or event presentation (on stage).
-----

I have also been a videogame commentator since 2002 and in 2005 went fully professional whilst working for GGL and iTG. I covered numerous major tournaments around the world including World Cyber Games finals (05/06/08), Electronic Sports World Cup (05/06) and Quakecon (05/06) and the CPL World Finals 2008 as well as many other international tournaments in China, Singapore, Australia, North America and all across Europe. In 2007 I moved to set up my own company to broadcast Internet TV and Radio for videogame tournaments and matches from around the world. This led to working with blue chip companies such as Intel, Xfire, DirecTV, Sky, Multiplay, Ubisoft, EA and many others helping to provider commentary and presenter services to them.

I have also appeared in many international magazines for gaming in the last 7 years including EDGE, PCZONE, PC Gamer, Chief, Pro Gamer, Custom PC and many others. My TV appearances include work for DirecTV, Sky One, Xleague.TV (Sky), GameplayHD, V-Music Asia, Singapore TV, OnGameNet (Korea), GzTV (Denmark) and many other smaller appearances on national and international news stations. These were all as videogame commentator or gaming personality, including punditry, talk shows and as an expert on the CGS Draft show shot live at the Playboy Mansion.
