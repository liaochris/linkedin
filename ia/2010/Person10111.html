Amy Goodwin’s Summary

Amy has worked with the Recruitment Industry for the past 10 years in both Queensland and Victoria. Having recruited with a variety of industry sectors she has a strong understand of businesses needs for all areas of their business.

Having worked within the Construction Industry both within Recruitment and Management, Amy has a through understanding of the requirements and ever changing trends of the industry.

With a focus on delivery quality results to clients and candidates, Amy has built strong relationships over the past 10 years.
