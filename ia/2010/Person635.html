Akash Agarwal’s Summary

Akash Agarwal is a veteran business executive in high technology with over 18 years experience spanning general management, marketing, product development, strategy, and sales. Akash's experience is in the consumer Internet, wireless and software markets at both small and large companies. Akash has been an entrepreneur, founding and running several venture-backed Internet startups, and in management at HP (via Mercury acquisition) and Oracle Corporation. He holds an M.B.A. from the Harvard Business School and a B.S. in Computer Science (with honors) from the University of Kent at Canterbury, England.
