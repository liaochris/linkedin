Gerald Holubowicz’s Summary

French freelance photojournalist based in New York since 2006, I'm specialized in news, portraits and event imagery.

After studying Law and Journalism in France, I worked with Abaca Press as staff photographer for almost 3 years. In September 2006 I moved to New York and, after a brief stint with ABACA USA and Gamma Press joined POLARIS Images as Contributing Photographer.

Equally comfortable behind a camera or a computer, I've learned to provide high quality at all levels of the production process. As a part of a new generation of professional, I'm definitively a digital photographer, strongly believing in the new way to stream photographs not only by the traditional way, but also, by Internet and its multimedia tools.

Gerald Holubowicz’s Specialties:

Photography specialty: News, Portrait and Feature. Collecting, editing, and presenting of news material for publication. Digital photography, Image processing.

Web Development and Web Design, Website Optimization for Photographers.
