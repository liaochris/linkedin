What are my results? We track the following metrics of performance for our distribution team;

· Retention: 98% of all Egret candidates since 1999 have remained with their new companies for a minimum of one year.

· Time to present: 16 calendar days.

· Time from presentation to start: 21 calendar days.

· Completion: 98% of our assignments are fulfilled.

When you've played out all other options; internal, advertising, networking with reps and vendors and you can't find the right person...put my experience to work for you. Call me when the candidate requires SIGNIFICANT industry experience and a fast ROI.

Ted Konnerth and Prudence Thompson incorporated Egret Consulting Group in 1999 as Egret, Inc. Our core values are reflected in our adoption of the philosophies and core tenets of the book TopGrading, by Bradford Smart, and our true passion for our jobs as summarized by Confucius; “find a job you love and never work a day in your life’.

Egret has grown to become the largest search firm dedicated solely to the electrical products industry serving two major verticals; manufacturing and distribution. In the past 10 years, Egret has placed over 500 new employees at over 75 clients within the electrical industry and has become the first name in recruiting for the electrical distributor facing hiring needs requiring significant market, product and industry experience.

I have a unique insight into the issues and trends facing today's electrical distributor and have established myself a trusted partner to both client and candidate. I have a solid reputation within the membership of the industry's top industry associations and buying groups; AD, NAED, NAUD, IMARK, Equity, Vanguard and EDN. I GET it!!
