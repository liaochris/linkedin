Aimee Whitcroft’s Summary

I've had a varied experience thus far in my career. While I started off in science, after my degree I segued into business. After studying entrepeneurial business, I moved into a management strategy consultancy to gain 'real-world' experience. Next, I moved into a qualitative market research agency, and from there I worked for a retail and market research consultancy. All these companies gave me highly honed skills in writing and analysis, which I leveraged to help me come full circle: I am now part of the burgeoning science communication industry.

Aimee Whitcroft’s Specialties:

The ability to synthesize large amounts of disparate information into a coherent whole, using both creativity and logic. My expertise is in "big-picture" integrative thinking.

Other skills include: excellent communication skills, a strong understanding of science, qual and quant market research prep and analysis, and a great deal of enthusiasm.

Finally, I'm widely read, possessing knowledge across a very wide range of subjects, and a highly adaptive mind.

  • Analyst

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    October 2007 — December 2008 (1 year 3 months)

    As an analyst for a retail consultancy, I was principally involved in gathering and analysing data - desktop, qualitative and/or quantitative research - and the resulting generation of real consumer behaviour insight.
