Yusuf Chowdhury’s Summary

I am Internet Marketing Consultant, Social Media Strategist that focuses on helping to promote and build relationship with your potential new clients, using social media and online marketing strategies. It is a new phenomenon in this time and age so why not embrace it and get started? Most of all, I love to help others grow their business and teach them how to reach their full potential.

My exposure to the world of Social Media started in 2006 when I was helping a friend start a project with short films. We had no experience whatsoever in film, but took the plunge on faith. For one reason or another our focus changed from short films to video blogs, and from there the project took off beyond our expectations.

Through that experience, I realized the huge role that Social Media plays not only in business, but also in creating awareness and connecting with the rest of the world. Best of all, it’s a form of media over which you have complete control.

Over recent years, I’ve consulted with many non-profit organizations, small businesses, and online education organizations to help them use social media and online marketing, developing creative ideas and ways to promote and grow their businesses. I get great satisfaction from empowering others to start their businesses, become independent, grow, and create a lasting legacy for themselves and their families.
