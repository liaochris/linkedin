Raheel Ahmad’s Summary

Raheel is an APICS certified supply chain management professional with over 9+ years experience in purchase, eprocurement, supply chain and operations. Currently, he is working with a US multinational, Cummins India Limited at Pune. Prior to this, he worked with mjunction services limited (a TATA Steel-SAIL joint venture). As a Key Account Manager, Raheel led the company’s esourcing initiatives at Roukela Steel Plant. mjunction is India's largest eCommerce company (having eTransacted goods & services worth over Rs.45,193 Crores, i.e.US$10 billion till date) and also runs the world's largest eMarketplace for steel. Leveraging strategic sourcing and eprocurement methodologies, Raheel helped in identifying esourcing opportunities, implementing them, and realizing cost savings targets. He initiated and formulated esourcing and eProcurement strategies for high value project and contract cases, achieving significant cost savings and procurement efficiencies for the clients.

Previously, after completing his college with degrees in B. Engg (Mech) and MBA, Raheel worked for global MNCs like LG Electronics and TMG in various capacities related to sourcing, eProcurement, supply chain, distribution and business process re-engineering. He was also involved with internet technology startups - ushops.com and eJobs Zimbabwe. He has widely traveled across South-east Asia, Europe, Middle-east and other places including Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, South Korea, Netherlands, Dubai, Nepal, etc.

Successfully completed Executive Program in Supply Chain Management (EPSCM) from IIM-Calcuta, 2009-10.
