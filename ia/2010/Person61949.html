Mark C.’s Summary

Currently a freelance editor, I have 12 years of experience that spans editorial, acquisitions, and production in nonfiction trade and business-to-business publishing. I have worked on books, e-books, journals, and online information products. I wrote the editorial style manual used for the #1 New York Times bestselling Chicken Soup for the Soul series and have copyedited or proofread more than 75 books and journals, including bestsellers (see www.mcinconline.com/markc-projects.html). My experience also includes editorial project management, book proposal evaluation, and copywriting.

Adaptable and ever willing to learn, I have worked in a variety of subject areas—business, social sciences, humanities, education, self-help, and wireless technology, to name a few. My educational background is in languages and law. I have lived, worked, and studied in Europe and am fluent in written and spoken French.

At present, I am open to freelance or in-house opportunities.
