Scott Mitchell’s Summary

Mr. Mitchell serves as the chair of a nonprofit called the Open Compliance & Ethics Group. OCEG provides objective standards and online resources to help organizations Drive Principled Performance™ by integrating governance, risk management, compliance (GRC) and ethics processes.

Mr. Mitchell also serves as the chair of NextSlide; and serves on the boards of DoubleDrum Capital, Walk with a Doc, and the Children's Museum of Phoenix.

Mr. Mitchell served on the COSO Task Force and was recognized two years in a row by Business Finance Magazine as one of the “Top 60 Influencers” in corporate finance. Treasury & Risk Magazine ranks him in the "Top 100" financial professionals. He was also recognized two years in a row by Human Resource Executive as one of the top 20 thought leaders regarding the future of human resource management. Mr. Mitchell has been featured in USA Today, Wall Street Journal, Institutional Investor, Treasury and Risk, Business Finance, Inc. Magazine and other leading publications.

Mr. Mitchell began his career by using his education in both accountancy and information systems at the Small Business Administration, Arthur Andersen, and Andersen Consulting (Accenture). Throughout his career, he has spent equal time in the board room, in the c-suite, and in the trenches advising clients including GE Capital, ADM, Raytheon, Walmart, Staples, American Express, IBM, E*Trade, Deloitte, Ernst & Young and others.

A technologist at heart, Mr. Mitchell was awarded patents in business simulation technology.
