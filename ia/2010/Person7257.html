Alice Letts’s Summary

Law:

Alice is an experienced employment and litigation lawyer. She advises and acts for employees in their claim. She has experience in both contentious and non-contentious employment law, occupational illness and dispute resolution. She handles an extensive contentious employment tribunal litigation caseload. She litigates claims as well as negotiating settlement via mediation.

She is an effective communicator with common sense. She has excellent computer skills and good organisational skills. She brings enthusiasm, integrity and dedication to her work. She remains calm under pressure and is able to work to deadlines.

Alice also has wide experience in advising on public law/human rights related issues from within central and local government. Alice also has broad experience in all aspects of public policy and legal analysis, and business development, bid writing and project management.

Piano Lessons:

Alice is a musician and piano teacher. She currently teaches the piano and music theory in central Derby to both children and adults. Her students range in age and ability from absolute beginners to the more advanced. Alice is also available to play the piano at weddings, birthdays and corporate functions. She also performs on a voluntary basis at church and community fundraisers. She has a wide repertoire including the classics, jazz, pop and musicals. She has extensive experience that includes playing the pipe organ for weddings, funerals and church services.

Hobbies:

Alice is very creative and likes to make things. She enjoys drawing and painting, designing fashion and jewellery. She also indulges in travel writing and travel photography on a regular basis and to see samples visit Alice's blog at www.alicestories.wordpress.com Alice is a great fan of watersports and her favourite locations are the lakes and beaches across New Zealand. Scario in the south of Italy is another popular destination.

Alice Letts’s Specialties:

All aspects of employment law, with particular emphasis on unfair dismissal, breach of contract and flexible working, unlawful deduction from wages, the terms of compromise (severance) agreements, and the full range of discrimination claims. She can also provide advice and assistance to employees and employers on drafting contracts of employment, disciplinary and grievance procedures, and consultancy agreements - for all levels of employee, from junior to the most senior.
