Matthew Page-Hanify’s Summary

A certified Dale Carnegie Trainer. I work with corporations and individuals to help them tap into their potential and maximize their opportunities, through goal-setting and vision creation, and developing communication, leadership and people skills. My background in public speaking and marketing provide a perfect platform for this kind of coaching. As an Australian in Canada and having traveled and lived in many countries I am interested in how cultural differences play a part in how we interact with others. And as an artist I like to look at the creative side of problem-solving.
