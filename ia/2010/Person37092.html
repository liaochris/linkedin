Chip Curle’s Summary

Charles Curle Design was created in 2000 offering simple web design services to businesses in Jacksonville, Florida. In 2004 we relocated to Cumming, GA, located just above the Atlanta metro area. Since then our business has grown and now provides web design, web development solutions, and web marketing strategies such as Search Engine Optimization. We can also create feature-rich, custom web applications to suit your business needs.

We have many years of experience in developing large scale websites (hundreds of dynamic pages) down to small one page brochure websites. Our work has helped lift non-existant sites from obscurity to the top of their field. You can count on our experience and expertise to provide your business with what will and will not work to ensure that you have a beneficial website that will help you grow online.
