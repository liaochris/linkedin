Linda Hardman’s Summary

As president of KKf Marketing, Linda helps people and businesses achieve their online goals by effectively communicating their messages through highly interactive, custom web sites. Her repertoire includes sites for professional associations, small businesses, e-commerce, talent agencies, professional directories, online award entry and judging systems, and simple online auctions for fund raising.

Linda augments her website building with her extensive expertise in market strategy, public relations, web communications and technical writing that are based on a solid background in marketing and marketing communications for high tech storage and networking companies.

Huffs List, which Linda built, offers a convenient, easily searched resource that is used to locate and learn about independent professionals and solopreneurs who can help businesspeople up against time restraints and in need of outside expertise. In it, you can find all types of independent professionals by services they offer.
