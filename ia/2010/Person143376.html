Michael Redman’s Summary

HI THERE WORLD!!!
Lets see... my official bio

Michael Redman is an entrepreneur, award winning composer and the President of MyMusicSource, a highly regarded Music Licensing company. In his career he has composed music for Disney, Universal, FOX, ABC, and NBC and worked with Steely Dan, The Bee Gees, Pointer Sisters,etc as well as scored numerous Feature Films.

Before launching MyMusicSource Michael founded the Hard Rock Academy ( one of the Hard Rock brands), GoActionVideo ( the largest supplier of personal video the Endurance sports industry), and RedHouse ( a film company in Orlando, Florida )

The goal of MyMusicSource is to fundamentally change the way music found, filtered and licensed for all commercial use

Michael Redman’s Specialties:

I feel like I have a pretty good grasp of technology and where it is going, as well as what a producer might be looking for in a track of music having worked on all sides of the music business as a Band member, Composer, Audio and Film Studio Owner, and Producer
