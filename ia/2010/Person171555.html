Ron Finklestein’s Summary

The Business Mastery Advisory Board

The AKRIS INC Business Mastery Advisory Board is a based on Ron Finklestein’s book Celebrating Success! Fourteen Ways to Create a Successful Company. Each company president, CEO and business owner interviewed discussed the importance of having trusted advisors dedicated to providing feedback, advice, suggestions, accountability, and best practices that allow them to grow, flourish and get results. These individuals understood that multiple brains and experiences are far smarter, effective and efficient than any single brain or experience.

The AKRIS Small Business Advisory Board is for the small business owner who is:
Trying to figure out how to create additional revenue streams

Needing intelligent and objective feedback on challenges they are facing and decisions that must be made

Being pulled in a thousand different directions not getting the things done they know they need to do

Interested in someone to hold them accountable

To get their life back.
