Karen Escalante-Dalton’s Summary

Accomplished nonprofit and foundation specialist with extensive experience in public health issues and Latino populations. Specializes in initiative design, grant making, grant writing and organizational development. Strong analytical and communications skills. Fluent in verbal and written Spanish.

Spanish language translations, grant writing, grant proposal development, program design, meeting facilitation, cultural competency, strategic planning, needs assessments, organizational development, board and staff trainings, and grant review and assessment.
