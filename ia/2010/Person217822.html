Mary Kay Hoal’s Summary

I'm a proud wife and mother of five children (both biological and adopted, ranging in ages from 5-18 years) and face the same challenges every parent does: keeping my children safe, raising them to be good, happy and well-balanced people; and providing them with the tools and support to create their own future.

When I was introduced to the social networking phenomenon, I quickly recognized its importance in the lives of my children. After researching the disturbing landscape of social networking sites - including endless inappropriate content and thousands of predators targeting youth - my mission became clear: raise the bar of social networking to protect our youth.

With that I conceived and founded Yoursphere, the first social networking service for youth that parents can embrace. Drawing on my corporate entrepreneurial experience as a media strategist, advertising, marketing and publishing VP formerly with CMP LLC, I built the Yoursphere team.

My mission is best summed up in this quote by Margaret Mead - "Never doubt that a small group of committed individuals could change the world, indeed it's the only things that ever has."

Feel free to connect with me here on LinkedIn with yoursphere@hotmail.com

Mary Kay Hoal’s Interests:

Yoursphere.com is a social media platform that engages kids and teens in rewarding, purposeful and positive youth-selected activities and interests – sports, music, fashion, academics, gaming, art & photo, movies & tv, performing arts, fashion, etc. – all while putting their safety first. We offer a members-only scholarship program, support member-selected philanthropies and sponsor young writer contests in which participants can become paid site contributors. While Yoursphere is advertising free, we offer brands, organizations, schools and groups the opportunity to directly leverage our secure platform to interact, engage and support youth interests. Partners have the opportunity to create their own sphere within our platform. We offer revenue share programs as well as fundraising opportunities for nonprofits and schools. Yoursphere has been featured on CNN, View From The Bay, Daytime TV, Fox, ABC, NBC, CBS and is approved by the Privacy Vaults Online Safe Harbor of the FTC.
