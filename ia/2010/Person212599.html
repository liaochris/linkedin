Patrina Mack’s Summary

Vision & Execution, Inc. helps companies turn innovative ideas into global success. We specialize in identifying the most cost effective route to building a global business. International companies use us to establish or increase their U.S. presence. We bring our breadth of business expertise -- business creation, strategic marketing, innovation sourcing, product development, and sales and operations program development –- to help companies identify the optimal market opportunity

Past clients include: Agilent, BeVocal (acquired by Nuance), Cisco, EasySpeedy, Intuit, ISTC, Macromedia (acquired by Adobe), Oblicore, Visa, Voxify, Wind River.

Sample projects include:

CleanTech:
* Developed market-driven business plan for solar thermal startup
* Identified most profitable R&D projects for international lab and made connections to corporate partners for acquisition

Healthcare
* Developed market penetration and launch strategy for medical services business to effectively compete in rapidly consolidating market.

Information Technology
* Conducted market analysis and developed go-to-market strategy with robust financial analysis to support business case for components manufacturer seeking to enter end-user marketplace.
* Developed and "productized" consulting services to increase channel partner revenues for network management enterprise software solution.
* Guided strategy and implemented affiliate marketing program for market leader in web tools, improving partner relations and growing sales for the previously underutilized channel.

Telecom:
* Assessed current product development process gaps and identified improvements to reduce time to market, improve quality and reduce costs for bringing new products to market.

Consumer Products
* Optimized phone channel, growing annual incremental revenues by $12 million, for major consumer financial software company with extensive product list.
