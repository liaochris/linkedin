MSc. Fabian Berger’s Summary

My short CV:

In 1987 Fabian co-founded “medgate.de”, which he established as a market leader in international chronic health patient treatment, in Germany. He grew this company to service 20,000 chronic disease patients in more than 60 clinics across Europe. Fabian developed and implemented software, methods and processes to operate the business and generated a strategic alliance with Fresenius AG, to implement such software and processes in dialysis clinics around the world.


In 1998, Fabian co-founded Calcucare Inc. (a shared IT services company) and Calcucare AG (an IT development/R&D company) in order to facilitate, secure, and maintain data logistics services to deliver applications in the general healthcare industry. These companies built healthcare compliant data solutions meeting stringent healthcare regulations, using the latest IHE&HL7 compliant models as well as best practices for virtual health records, certified by ISO 9001, ISO 13485 and which are IEC 60601-1-6 compliant. The biggest customer was Fresenius Medical Care, a €10.1 billion market cap DAX 30 company, which uses Calcucare to deliver data services to more than 2,500 users in Europe and Asia.


In 2003, Fabian co-founded CareExcel Solutions LLC (“CareExcel”), a Houston based firm, which built on the founders’ 20 years of health care IT expertise. CareExcel focused on business continuity techniques with unique 24x7 end-to-end business monitoring and disaster recovery services to healthcare businesses around the world. It served clinics in Germany, Spain, Italy, Hungary and Taiwan, where it worked with the clinics to deliver electronic medical records’ systems


Fabian has a Master of Science degree from the Danube University Krems.
