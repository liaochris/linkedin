Bob Alperin’s Summary

Bob is a high intensity, growth-oriented executive with a strong background in sales, marketing, finance and software engineering management. He has served as CEO & President of start-ups to more mature companies, managing P&Ls ranging from $5M to $80M.

As an active board member and advisor to promising young, technology companies, he is currently considering GM / COO / EVP opportunities for companies or divisions of companies with revenues between $20-$250 million.

Bob Alperin’s Specialties:

Domain expert in online direct marketing (PPC SEM & SEO). Exceptional at recruiting, retaining and inspiring top leaders, of sales, marketing, finance, engineering, product management, strategic planning, and mergers & acquisitions. Experienced in all facets of M&A including valuation, negotiation and post-acquisition integration. Known for identifying and developing major business development partnerships that result in meaningful positive impact.
