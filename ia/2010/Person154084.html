Nick Lindauer’s Summary

Nick is the Vice President of Integrated Digital Media for Farther Digital, a wholly-owned division of FKM, one of the largest independent agencies in the Southwest with annualized billings of $250 million. As the VP of Integrated Digital Media, Nick is responsible for managing and growing the list of existing SEM & Online Media clients of Farther Digital, as well as developing the team of Online Media & Search Engine Marketing professionals to manage daily campaigns for a diverse list of clients.
