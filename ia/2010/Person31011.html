Scott Clark’s Summary

I am a consultant for mid to large level companies in the areas of:

a) Organic Search Marketing - the process of growing a website's authority and subsequently its rank in Google, Yahoo!, and Bing search engines. This is done through on-website and off-website approaches.

b) Paid Search Marketing - the setup and optimization of paid search endeavors through systems such as Google Adwords, Facebook, Yahoo! Search Marketing and Microsoft Adcenter.

c) Social Media Marketing - helping companies join in relevant conversations where they can become productive, trusted members of social networks, and subsequently grow authority which benefits their business.

d) Online Reputation Management - an offshoot of SEO and SMM, this is the process of protecting brands' public perception during Internet Searches.

e) Web Business Consulting - Website Usability Audits, Market Viability surveys, Demographic Analysis and General Business Advice at the 15-year experience level.
