Jason Velick’s Summary

Jason has been an executive in the healthcare, biotechnology and equipment finance marketplaces for nearly 10 years. His focus has always been on the health-care and technology sectors and has been a top performing sales representative for firms including : Pfizer and GE Medical Systems. At Pfizer, Jason became involved with clinics and hospitals at the physician level all the way up to the corporate suite. At GE Medical Systems, he managed a region in the upper Midwest and worked with major universities, hospitals, and clinics. Jason was able to further his background in capital equipment sales and financing in this role through a direct relationship with GE Capital (Healthcare Financial Services) and later with an independent equipment leasing company.

vMed Systems, represents the collective experience of 10 years in the healthcare market. We provide solutions for medical practices that enhance patient care and provide a potential source of profit and cost savings.
