Angela Baxley’s Summary

Senior User Experience Designer with twelve years in the Web and Software industries with a focus on Interaction Design.

Often responsible for the entire continuum of User Experience Design from researching and interpreting user needs, clarifying the site or software objectives, informing the functional specifications, defining the information, interaction and interface design, and the visual design.

Continually seeking new challenges where the experience design affects the lives of millions of users, and to solve difficult problems with innovative concepts.
