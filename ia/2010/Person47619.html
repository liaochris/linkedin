Daniel McCrobie’s Summary

I am passionate about process improvement. I am a certified six sigma black belt and have championed process improvement in several different industries. I am a whiz kid at statistics, and present data analyses in a way that allows the data to help make the right decision.

My unique specialty is the soft-side of change management. Once you have decided to make a change, you need all of the employees to embrace that change. Working with process workers and other key stakeholders to drive that change is more important than the new process development.

Lately, I have been looking at metrics to ensure that they are driving the right behaviors. There are many occasions when a metric is in place but it is not supporting behaviors that are in line with company goals. The example here is the first call resolution for the help desk. Operators quickly figure out that they can ship the call over to another group and that resolves that call for them. It does little for the organization and in fact hurts the customer experience, but that help desk team is getting rewarded for their high resolution numbers.

I am interested in helping companies transform their business processes so that they can be more agile in dealing with customer desires, spend less time doing time-intensive tasks, and save money. I can help companies improve in dramatic ways, do more with less, and improve customer and employee satisfaction.

    Our firm works does a variety of interventions to engage your customers and find out what they really want. We can quickly see what is working and what isn’t so that you spend your marketing dollars effectively, with a high return on investment.

    We know data analysis so that you can make solid data-driven decisions about your marketing budget to maximize reach to the customer and minimize your overall expenses. Here is how it works. For each of your current marketing methods, we calculate the business generated from that ad. If this is a positive ROI, then we know this works. In not, then we pilot in different channels – social media, ad-words, magazines, billboards, etc. Each of those channels are tracked and compared back to the outlay costs, constantly looking for positive ROI. We can continually monitor marketing investments to ensure a positive ROI in everything that you do.

Through targeted survey techniques using Kano methodology, conjoint analysis and in-depth interviews, we provide you with a profile of your customer, how to reach them, and what they are looking for in a company. Additionally, we work with your company to improve the front-end processes, looking at each customer touchpoint and improving that experience, so that you are providing the best service in the business, winning over new customers and turning existing customers into zealots.
