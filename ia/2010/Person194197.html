Stephanie Wong’s Summary

Stephanie Wong is passionate about developing rich and dynamic consumer experiences with an emphasis in style related content, consumer trend analysis, branding, and social media.

Founder of StyleMaven.com, an online magazine focusing on womens apparel, accessories, beauty, home and lifestyle products. Winner of Glam Media's "BEST NEWCOMER Award" for 2009. Featured in Vogue and InStyle.

Experienced in style-based trend development used by marketing teams including Nokia for consumer intelligence insights.

Stephanie has a B.S. in Marketing with an emphasis in the Fashion Merchandising from California Polytechnic University SLO, studies at the City University in London and has traveled extensively in Japan. Also a graduate of the Renaissance Entrepreneurship Center with award of "Best Business Plan" for 2004.
