Lekhanh (Lea) Tran’s Summary

Lea Tran is a professionally trained designer and fabricator specializes in custom window treatments for residential and small commercial applications. We partner with our clients, or team with local designers, to provide solutions that will transform your space, showcase your personal style, and align with your budget. We offer virtual window treatment service which saves time and ensures successful results. Our complete service includes consultation for custom blinds, shades, shutters; and the design and installation of top quality in–house fabricated soft window treatment products.

Lekhanh (Lea) Tran’s Specialties:

We are authorized dealer for Hunter Douglas, Comfortex, Norman Shutters, and B&W Window Fashions. Our products include Horizontal Sheer Shades, Honeycomb Shades, Nature Woven Wood Shades, Wood Blinds, Vertical Blinds, Shutters, Draperies, Valances, Cornice Boards, Fabric Shades, Custom Bedding, Cushions, Upholstery and all other soft furnishings.

Lekhanh (Lea) Tran’s Experience

  • Owner/ Designer/Fabricator

    Distinctive Designs

    (Design industry)

    January 2004 — Present (6 years 11 months)

    Distinctive Designs was officially launch in December 2005. Lea Tran decided to take a leap of faith and leave her job of 17 years as a chemist in the pharmaceutical industry and started her own company to become a top quality window fashion designer and fabricator. Her success story was featured in the Drapery & Design Professional Magazine recently in 2009.

    Distinctive Designs has grown largely by word of mouth and Lea enjoys the growing friendships and partnerships she has built with new and repeat clients. To help ensure that her clients always get the results they want, Lea uses leading edge computerized software to produce virtual window treatments before any commitment is made. This service has saved precious time and money, and assures happy results.

    To remain ahead of industry innovations and trends, Lea attends the annual International Window Covering Expo regularly and continues to refine her exacting capabilities by taking advanced design courses and participating in professional conferences and meetings on an ongoing basis. She is a graduate of the Advanced Window Covering Program at CHF (Custom Home Furnishing) Academy, a drapery professional school in North Carolina. She is also certified as a Custom Window Treatment Consultant from WCAA (Window covering association of America).

  • Research Associate Chemist

    Merck & Co.

    (Pharmaceuticals industry)

    1990 — 2005 (15 years )

    Lea synthesized, analyzed, and used many different instruments such as Lc-Ms, NMR and MS to identified compounds which were tested in animals for AIDS virus suppression in Medicinal Chemistry Department. Among thousands of compounds she had synthesized, Sustiva, a benzoxazinones inhibitor for HIV Reverse Transcriptase, has become a drug in the market to treat AIDS since 1998.
