Peter Chrzanowski’s Summary

Consultant for The Rehancement Group, Inc. (TRG) with over five years of professional experience in the areas of financial and economic analysis, cost estimating, earned value management, business process reengineering, contract and project management, and client reporting. Background includes positions supporting several federal agencies including the Transportation Security Administration (TSA), U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP), National Cancer Institute (NCI), General Services Administration (GSA), and Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Installations and Environment (DUSD I&E). Active member of the Society of Cost Estimating and Analysis (SCEA) and holds the designation of Certified Cost Estimator/Analyst (CCEA).
