Michael Vargas MBA, CMIT, LEED GA, CQM, CEPE’s Summary

Atlas Project Support maintains a staff of Construction and Energy Management professionals with over 50 years experience in cost estimating, scheduling and planning, project leadership, earned value analysis, project management, quality systems management, and project support. We specialize in energy efficiency consulting, LEED certification consulting, environmental and sustainability consulting services, photovoltaic systems design and installation, general contracting, environmental remediation, demolition, heavy civil, structural steel and miscellaneous metals bid packages, but are knowledgeable in all the means and methods to be used in the execution of any project in accordance with the contract documents and Owner’s requirements.

Atlas Project Support was founded to provide highly technical and sophisticated management systems in numerous ecosystems to serve our clients’ best interest. Our company has collaborated on many large complex projects in various capacities functioning as problem solvers and preparing defensible financials, implementing effective controls by controlling the time, cost, quality, scope, and team and reacting according to measurable, verifiable results based on actual performance measured against planned performance. The projects we have managed and estimated range in the 100s of dollars to the multi-millions. These projects were carefully monitored for cost and schedule performance and measured in terms of Earned Value. APS have worked with Caltrans, DOE, California DSA, USACE, NAVFAC, and many other Private and Public Owners.
