Michelle Hartley’s Summary

I have over 12 years of management and senior level experience in channel and direct marketing, business development, product management, strategic alliances/OEM and channel/direct sales in the enterprise software and networking markets.
I am a passionate Channel professional who has had hands on experience and great success in managing, developing and implementing partner programs including: recruitment, demand/lead generation, MDF/CDF, training, and incentive/promotions together with game-changing marketing tools to support such programs. I am a revenue and data driven marketing professional with a strong technology background and deep expertise in the channel who continues to explore ways to drive partner profitability, revenue, and marketing efficiencies.
