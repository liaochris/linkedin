Dave Robbins, PMP’s Summary

A highly skilled Project Manager, Business Analyst and Organizational Change Consultant with recent and deep experience in all three roles.

Demonstrated ability to deliver complex systems and business transformation projects with diverse teams of professionals and organizational stakeholders to achieve streamlined procedures, efficient systems, and a better understanding of business performance.

15+ years experience in project and IT organization management and leadership. 15+ years experience across the full business and systems analysis life cycle, including strategic planning, business process re-engineering, requirements analysis, solution design, testing, and deployment.

Focused on partnering with clients and engaging stakeholders to obtain the best results, since, at the end of the day it is the business team that will sponsor, commit to and implement the business and system improvements.

Solid operational and IT experience in many industries, both private and public sector. Deep experience in finance, order fulfillment, production planning, and supply chain logistics

An analytical problem solver with a strong business acumen who demonstrates professionalism, integrity, pragmatism, innovation, motivation and quality on all his projects.
