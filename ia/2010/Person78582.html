Gerry Tywoniuk’s Summary

Seeking Chief Financial Officer (CFO) role with an organization that aspires to build a great team focused on creating shareholder value. Also interested in Corporate Controller / Chief Accounting Officer (CAO) role with larger organization. Permanent or interim position in the Los Angeles / Orange County metro areas.

16 years of experience as Chief Financial Officer of three public companies, and VP/Corporate Controller of a fourth public company. Verifiable track record of success with each company.

A diversity of experience obtained by working with different organizations in a variety of industries and industry segments through good times and bad: accounting, SEC reporting, investor relations, capital markets, IPO, acquisitions and divestitures, joint ventures, private equity ownership, restructuring and chapter 11, planning and analysis, economic evaluations and risk management.

Effective in leading accounting and finance teams through growth and through downsizing.

Successfully worked with boards of directors and audit and other committees since 1985. Member of the board of directors of the general partner of Oxford Resource Partners, LP (2009 - present), MarkWest Hydrocarbon, Inc. (2002) and MarkWest Energy Partners, L.P. (2002).

Dual Canadian and US citizenship.
