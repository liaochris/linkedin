Dénes Székely (aka webGóbé)’s Summary

Fell in love with IT in the fall of 1980 as a student. I left my physics engineering (more precisely Nuclear Fuel Engineering - Masters in 1985) career for computing, obtained my MBA degree in computing in 1989, since working as programmer, sysadmin.

Main hobby is the innovative use of IT, and as a side effect I have almost two decades of experience in computer driven DTP, CAD/CAM, GIS. In 1994 finally reached the Internet. Since then I have mastered tons of Internet related things, from open source software, community building, software localization, and, of course, websites. Various sizes and shapes, from one page straight HTML to sites with more than 1000 pages, different dynamic solutions, database driven sites, DHTML, Java, etc.

I'm always experimenting to find new solutions (currently Joomla is the favorite pet's name) and yes, the "worst" that can happen to such a person happened to me too: I'm teaching all these topics at one of the oldest universities of my country, having 2 academic course books released (Networks and Web Authoring is one, Communication and Security on the Internet is the second). Another side effect is the 10.000 plus Google hits on my real name, and also a considerable amount of hits on my relatively recent nick.

“What's next?”- you might ask. Who knows? The most recent of my Internet related experiments is this: building a freelancing career. You can be part of this experiment too! Consider yourself invited!
