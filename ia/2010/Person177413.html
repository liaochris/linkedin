Ronnie Liew’s Summary

Ronnie is a creative technologist, an evangelist for technology and design in the advertising industry. He is an innovator, he revels in exploring emerging technologies and pushing them through to achieve digital marketing goals.

He has extensive knowledge and experience in various disciplines of web technologies. He crafts websites, interactive media, mobile and rich internet applications. A strong believer in web standards and compliancy. A strong leader and good communicator, he is no stranger to conveying ideas, thoughts and solutions to audiences at various levels.

Till date, he has worked with international brands like Nike, XBox, Gap, Nokia, HP, DHL, Vodafone, and Visa. He has handled numerous international and regional projects. His projects have won various international awards and gained recognition in the industry.

Key projects includes NikeLab, Nokia Phone Selector and Levis Reborn.
