Mike Gallucci’s Summary

In addition to the successful acquisition and redevelopment of SportsMemorabilia.com, Triton Web Properties' portfolio has expanded to acquire Boating.com, Biking.com, Barbecuing.com, Babyformulas.com, and CigarMarket.com. Additionally, Triton Web Properties also leads and directs the development of HollywoodMemorabilia.com.

During 2007 to 2010 most time was spent directing the day to day operations of the SportsMemorabilia.com network of e-commerce web sites. Has seen a 10x increase in order volume year over year in our busiest month, December 2007 to December 2008 with a 3x increase in revenue. SportsMemorabilia.com claims the # 1 ranking for the industry's most popular searches which include both player or team name plus memorabilia, autographed, signed, etc. Strategic positioning through in-house expertise has allowed SportsMemorabilia.com to claim Alexa's #1 traffic ranking for sports memorabilia sites.

During his time with SportsMemorabilia.com, Mike has created internet exclusive products with companies such as Steiner Sports Marketing and Park Ave Sports Marketing, with athletes such as Mariano Rivera and Alex Rodriguez.
