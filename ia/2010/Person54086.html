D. Garcia’s Summary

For over 22 years, Demetrio 'D' Garcia has served the aviation industry from various perspectives as an airport planner, designer, airport construction manager, properties real estate manager and aviation consulting services and business development marketing manager.

D has extensive experience with numerous airport construction and development projects throughout the United States and abroad. He brings with him a solid background and working knowledge of airport and airline administration, operations, finance, systems, equipment, capital programs and Program Management. He is also knowledgeable and experienced with many industry agency (FAA, TSA, ICAO, IATA, etc) standards and regulations for airport planning design and construction.

D.Garcia also has a successful and proven track record in project management and construction with on time and within budget projects completed for both regular and large-type projects at both large hub, regional and international airports; from planning through design, to construction and operational completion.

Having been both the client and service provider, he understands the importance of customer satisfaction, performance and results. This diverse background and broad perspective brings a value added vision and fresh approach to any airport project.
