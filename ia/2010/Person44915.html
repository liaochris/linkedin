Cristina Santiestevan’s Summary

It comes down to two things: a good story and a healthy planet.

Driven by a passion for environmental change and the belief that individual actions matter, I write copy that connects with readers.

Over the years, my work has appeared in consumer publications and nonprofit member magazines, and I've covered issues as varied as global climate change and the local food movement. From my desk in Northern Virginia, I have written about clouded leopard conservation in Thailand, the water wars in Colorado and carbon trading in New England.

Select publications include:
• American Forests
• Aqualog / Blue
• Breathe Magazine
• Flavor Magazine
• SmithsonianMagazine.com
• Virginia Wildlife Magazine
• Zoogoer

I also provide communications support for non-profits and small businesses, writing copy for e-newsletters, websites, annual reports, brochures, rack cards and other marketing materials and educational handouts.

Select clients include:
• Audubon Society of Northern Virginia
• Clean Water Action / Clean Water Fund
• International Fund for Animal Welfare
• New England Aquarium
• Newport Harbor Nautical Museum
• World Land Trust-US
