Edward Poll, JD, MBA, CMC’s Summary

Practice/Organization Description

Would you like to take your law practice to the next level of success? Corporate America creates strategic plans to achieve their goals; would you like to emulate their successes? Would you like to have more clients? More important, would you like to increase your realization rate? Would you like to reduce the stress from your practice?

If you answer "yes" to any of these questions, you should engage Ed Poll, the coach to growing law firms. Ed enables lawyers to be more effective with their clients, more efficient in the delivery of their legal services and dramatically more profitable!

Client successes resulting from working with Ed include:

1. Increased realization rates.

2. Reduced stress and increased enjoyment of the practice

3. Improved cash flow

4. Increased revenue

5. More effective collection practices

6. Greater harmony among staff and legal professionals

7. Increased enthusiasm for being the helping and caring professional that lawyers are

Ed becomes your ally, is available 24/7, and lights the path for you to see opportunities that you would otherwise miss.

Ed is a sounding board for your new ideas ... Learn to avoid pitfalls that he will see but you might miss.

Ed has written extensively in the field of law practice management; this includes more than 10 books, hundreds of articles and many blog postings. His audio works set the standard in both his audio magazine and now his podcasts.

His honors include being a Fellow of the College of Law Practice Management; Charter member of the Million Dollar Consultant™ Hall of Fame;
Leader of the Year (ABA).

For greater success, call Ed now! (800) 837-5880. See more about Ed at www.lawbiz.com and www.lawbizblog.com.
