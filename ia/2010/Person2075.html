Joey Fiero’s Summary

Mr. Fiero holds a diversified background ranging between Information Technology, Marketing through entrepreneurship and production. With over 15 years of experience in business, film, television and music he has been able to combine a producer's creative vision with a corporate executives savvy and business acumen.

Prior to starting Community Dream Studios 501(c)3 non-profit motion picture studio and taking on his position at Datavideo corporation, a manufacturer of production switchers and camera equipment, Mr. Fiero co-founded GBTVonline, a music artists development marketing company that co-branded alongside with Konvict Muzik Records, Geffen Records and featured going behind the scenes with celebrity music artists such as AKON, Colby O' Donis, Rihanna Fenty.

He also worked as a development producer at PlayAStream, a live streaming site, creating a variety of live online streaming shows such as "The Garage" which showcased aspiring Indie rock bands, a promotional producer at Univision Radio in Las Vegas, NV, a executive producer with Creative Entertainment & Media Inc. and is a producer for his own independent projects.

Mr. Fiero holds a bachelor's degree in business marketing from Ashworth College. He has served his country as United States Marine Reservist, a past Fire Direction Control specialist and has been a member of the Screen Actors Guild since the age of 12.

Mr. Fiero is honored and is privilege in helping others pursue knowledge and careers of their choosing within the arts and entertainment community and will has always stated that he will continue his goal to bring a positive change in society through arts.
