Alex Grant’s Summary

In March of 2008, I began my career with Heartland Payment Systems as a Relationship Manager (Payment Solutions Consultant). In May, I was promoted to the position of Territory Manager, and as of December 1st, 2008, I was promoted again to Division Manager of Cincinnati & Dayton. April 15, 2009, I also took on the Division in Indianapolis. I now manage two Divisions, (Cincinnati & Dayton) totaling 14 Sales Reps.

Heartland is growing at leaps and bounds and we need talented, capable sales people.

After college, I started my professional career with Quantum Mobile Fitness, Inc. I built this company from the ground up. Running a successful business was a spectacular experience. I handled everything involved in running the business; creation of a new concept, sales of a new concept to the public, client retention, advertising, PR, finances, etc. I sold this business in 2007 for an opportunity to make an impact in Corporate America.

In 2007, I completed building a rental cabin in Tennessee (last of 3 that I have built). I served as the General Contractor.
