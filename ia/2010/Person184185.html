Scott Drummond’s Summary

With a background in brand strategy and consumer experience, Scott co-founded Forty Forty Agency to redefine experiential marketing. The approach combines a mix of advertising, digital media, identity and brand experience with a weighting of each that is relevant to a client's business challenge. As an independent agency, Scott and his partners lead work that is more efficient, a bit smarter, and always ambitiously creative.

Scott Drummond’s Specialties:

NEW PLACES: Strategy and creative for extending brands into unexpected places with new opportunities for growth, including sports and entertainment, accessed by sponsorships, naming rights, or direct investment. NEW PARTNERSHIPS: Strategies and branding for joint ventures, co-branding, licensing and other business model expansion. NEW COMMUNICATION PLATFORMS: Brand positioning through the layering of emerging media and technology onto the foundation of traditional marketing tools.
