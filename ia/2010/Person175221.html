Robert Samuelson’s Summary

Dynamic professional leader with a holistic approach to the technical and artistic components of project management and astute understanding that projects exist to serve the strategic needs of the business or enterprise. Proven ability to lead project teams and motivate individuals towards success. Able to address the big picture concerning business investment and risk as well as work at a technical and detailed level when necessary. Combined 20 years experience in project management, change management, information technology and project engineering in Oil & Gas, Aerospace and retail products & services industries.
