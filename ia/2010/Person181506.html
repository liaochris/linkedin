Sam Vassa’s Summary

Sam Vassa leads the operations and strategy of PaymentEvolution. Sam founded the company with a team of technology and subject matter specialists and devised an award-winning solution that empowers small and medium businesses to manage their own payroll process. He also heads the North American business for Figlo – one of the fastest growing companies in financial advisor software worldwide. Previously, Sam led business development for the worldwide capital markets industry at Microsoft Corporation and was responsible for alliance relationships with Microsoft’s largest global partners. He was a channel manager at Digital Equipment Corp and served with the Canadian Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade at their foreign missions worldwide. Sam is also a board member of Telecompute – a leading software development firm pioneering the use of portable database technology for vertical markets. He is also active in the emerging business market as an advisor and retained consultant.

Alliance negotiation and performance; technology pioneers and start ups; converting ideas to real revenue
