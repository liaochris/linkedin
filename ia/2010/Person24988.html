  • Owner

    Blue Ridge Wireless

    (Privately Held; Wireless industry)

    July 1998 — Present (12 years 1 month)

    Blue Ridge Wireless is a privately held company founded in 1998 as an independent dealer selling wireless services. In the years since, we have evolved into a specialized cell phone repair and wireless service center. We offer a range of services that include repairs, forensics consulting, data recovery and back-up, asset recovery, refurbished phones & OEM / specialty accessories.

    Our aim is provide a range of high quality cellular phone repair and service solutions to meet our client's needs and requirements. To this end, we are constantly learning, upgrading our facility, and adding new services, including creating custom programs.

    Blue Ridge Wireless works with individual customers, retail dealers, rural wireless service providers as well as third party warranty and protection plan sellers. For our larger clients, we offer discounted pricing and a range of value added services from drop shipping of repaired units to custom invoicing solutions. Our custom online database system allows for accurate inventory control and tracking of handset throughout the repair / remanufacturing process. We can custom tailor the database to meet specific needs and it can be integrated with a client's existing systems.

    When it comes to repairing your phone(s), our knowledge and experience coupled with our extensive quality control and testing procedures allow us to guarantee your phone(s) will be repaired to the highest standards. We back up this committment with a 90 day warranty.
