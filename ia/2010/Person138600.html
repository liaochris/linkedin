Maxwell Finn’s Summary

I am a 20 year old entrepreneur, specializing in social media marketing and web development. I have been involved in 5 start-ups in various industries. I am also an avid blogger and took my last blog, the4eblog to an Alexa ranking of 35,000. The blog also won several awards and I have been featured on the front page of top social media blogs, such as Social Media Today and Junta42. Several of my blog posts were published on a weekly basis in the New York and New England Real Estate Journal.

Currently, I am the co-founder and a managing partner at GST Media. We provide web development and web design services which facilitate interactive information sharing, interoperability, user-centered design and collaboration on the World Wide Web. We are capable of developing web-based communities, hosted services, web applications, social-networking sites, video-sharing sites, wikis, blogs, mashups and folksonomies which allow your users to interact with other users or to change website content. Some of our current clients include Jessica Cohen (Executive VP at Prudential Douglas Elliman), the Women's Council of Realtors Boston Chapter and NAI Global.

I am always looking to expand my virtual and physical network. Let's connect...
