Mike Parker’s Summary

Mike has over 24 years experience in the Real Estate Industry. Mike has been and still is recognize as one of top Real Estate Agents in United States. Mike has help buyers and sellers in all price ranges from the first time homebuyer to the CEO of Companies to the Farmer who needs to sell his/her farm. Mike's unique Real Estate Experience and hungry for knowledge has helped him help over two thousands families buy and sell real estate in Northern Kentucky!

Mike thinks outside the box, he has never been one to just to go to work. Mike has always an extreme love for the Real Estate Business, Mike has been quoted as saying" Every day is different in the Real Estate Business; you got to love this business to be in it. No two transactions are the same; you just have to roll up your sleeves and go to work for your clients."

Evidently that is what Mike has done; Mike is the only agent in North America who is in two different Hall of Fames, RE/MAX and HUFF Realty. Mike was awarded the "Nate Sanders" Educator of Year Award at 2009 Kentucky Association of Realtors Convention!

Mike also is a member of The "Top 5" Network.

Mike Parker’s Specialties:

Pre-Existing Homes, New Homes, Relocation, First Time Buyers, Internet Sales and Marketing, Technology, Townhomes and Condo's, Farms and Lots and Acreage!
