Chris Sitter’s Experience

  • Vice President

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    January 1997 — Present (13 years 1 month)

    Jordan-Sitter Associates (JSA) is a 30 year old family-owned retained executive search firm. We are centrally based in San Antonio, Texas, and serve clients nationwide.

    Our company focuses on filling senior and mid-level management roles in the following sectors:
    - Wind Energy
    - Construction Equipment
    - Mining Equipment
    - Material Handling Equipment
    - Forestry Equipment
    - Agricultural Equipment

    Our firm has made a number of successful mid and senior level placements at multi-billion dollar firms in the industries we serve, and we have helped many smaller to mid-sized companies land key executives.

    We invite you to visit us online at (www.jordansitter.com), and to review our testimonials page (www.jordansitter.com/testimonials.htm), to hear what some of our past clients and candidates have to say. At JSA, we make it our goal to treat all candidates politely and to honor their confidentiality.

    Jordan-Sitter Associates combines decades of executive search experience, with the latest in executive search technologies and a sincere dedication to excellent customer service, to help our clients "Hire the Best".
