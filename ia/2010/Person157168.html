Olga Kirnos’s Summary

Olga Kirnos, International Body Art & Make Up Artist, discovered her love for the arts at a very early age. Practicing for over twelve years she possesses a distinct background in fine arts with key influences from the Classical, Traditional & Surrealism Arts.

Her passion for the creative and exceptional understanding of color, light and shape are fundamental elements which are distinct in her Body Art & Make Up work.

From enhancing with natural styles to expression through creative designs, Olga continuously strives to provide fresh ideas through her craft. Steadily growing as  an internationally established name in Make Up Artistry, her multi-cultural inspired background and sense of aesthetics transform her work into a unique statement.

With a vast range of clients in both Canada & India, Olga provides comprehensive Make Up Artistry Services, ranging from Make Up Instruction, Consultations & design, Beauty, Fashion TV & Film Make Up to Airbrush Make Up & Body Art. Including a unique form of dazzling Glitter Body Art.

Her work has been showcased internationally in numerous Film & TV productions as well as Fashion Projects. Working with global clients such as Front TV, Vision TV, Club Fashion, GQ Magazine, Brazilian Carnival Gala Ball, Chatelaine, Global TV, The Canadian  History Channel and numerous International Photographers.

Olga Kirnos’s Specialties:

Fashion, Editorial & Print Make Up, Make Up & Body Art Design, Music Videos, Motion Pictures, TV & Commercials, Award Ceremonies, Galas & Special Events, Wedding Make Up, Personal Make Up Artist, Make Up Consultation, Lectures & Pro Training, Group Sessions, Private Lessons, Airbrushing Classes
