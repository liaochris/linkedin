Jeff Gentry’s Summary

Managing Partner and practicing consultant at Jeff Gentry & Associates. With 20 years of experience in interactive marketing, customer relationship management and information technology, Mr. Gentry serves consults in digital marketing strategy and technology architecture. For the past 15 years, he has focused exclusively on customer information at firms including EDS, dbINTELLECT Technologies, Digitas, Foote Cone & Belding, Intelligent Solutions, Rapp Collins, Tribal DDB Worldwide, and now Jeff Gentry & Associates. As a leading authority on gathering, managing, and using customer information, he has helped companies large and small to gain competitive advantage and improve profits. His clients have included Pepsi-Cola, Frito-Lay, American Express, American Airlines, AT&T, General Motors, Citibank, NTT Dokomo, Metropolitan Life, Pfizer, Merck, Intel, Hewlett-Packard, Regis Corporation, Blockbuster, Brinker International and BMG Entertainment. His focus on consumer information has resulted in experience in many industries, including consumer packaged goods, education, energy, entertainment, finance, government, information technology, insurance, manufacturing, marketing, pharmaceuticals, publishing, restaurants, retail, telecommunications, transportation and utilities.

Mr. Gentry has consulted, taught, spoken and published on business intelligence, customer relationship management and interactive marketing topics in North America, Asia, Europe, and Australia. He has spoken for corporations, universities, users groups and conferences on a variety of topics.

He can be reached at jeff@jeffgentry.com.
