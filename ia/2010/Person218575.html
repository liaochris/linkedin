Zeenia Mehta’s Summary

A little bit about me and what I believe in:
6 years in the digital industry is like a decade on a non-stop roller-coaster ride. Every day is a fresh start. What’s now becomes then in just the blink of an eye. Unlike conventional advertising where ideas dominate the medium, the converse is true in the digital space. Social communities, blogs, video platforms, mobile phones, applications, etc. are a thing of the past used in the present. So how do brands leverage these overly populated platforms of expression? Ideas. Ideas that involve today's digital natives. Today, the reader is becoming a writer and the viewer a participant. Advertising is truly getting personal and the only medium that allows this freedom of interaction is the internet and the devices it runs on.

Current role:
I'm a Creative Director at Media2win, where I aspire to build new, innovative, and creative consumer-centric digital-media ideas. My goal is to build and direct a team of digital enthusiasts who think out of the box. Our objective is to provide concepts and strategy aimed at Generating Brand Demand, marrying creative excellence with tangible returns-on-investment.

Prior assignments:
I started my journey at Tribal DDB India, where we initially struggled to sell the medium first, and then our ideas. Later, when clients understood the future and strength of the digital space, we rocked, and won a few international and national awards that were a first in India.

I then joined RMG Connect, part of JWT, as Creative Supervisor where we leveraged the digital platform to help our brands to optimize ROI.
