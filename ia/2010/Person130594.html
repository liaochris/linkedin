Andre Mancl’s Summary

Andre currently works with the Internet Investment Banking Team at RBC Capital Markets.

He focuses most of his time working with public and private growth Internet companies in the online marketing, content, and e-commerce sectors. Notable transactions include the IPO of MercadoLibre (MELI), the sale of SureHits, and the private capital raise for a large Online Retailer in the Team Sports Merchandise Sector.

Prior to joining the firm, he served as an SH-60B helicopter pilot in the United States Navy. As an Aircraft Commander in Operation Enduring Freedom, he flew missions in the Arabian Gulf, South America and South East Asia and was awarded by the the Order of the Daedalians for landing a stricken aircraft aboard the USS Paul F. Foster.

After his US Navy flying tour, he was an assistant adjunct professor at UCLA teaching courses in Leadership and Ethics and Advanced Naval Weapons Systems.

HE received his B.S. in industrial & systems engineering from the University of Southern California and an M.B.A. in Finance and Entrepreneurship from the Anderson School of Management at UCLA.

Andre Mancl’s Specialties:

Working with middle market Internet Companies, M&A advisory, Raising Growth Capital.
