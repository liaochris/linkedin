Maya Shoemaker’s Summary

I am a medical illustrator and fine artist with a passion for anatomy. I am most interested in complex and vivid pieces that in their creation require both skill in rendering and a knowledge of science.

My fine art is the place for a more loose approach, where I let emotions become abstract areas of paint. However, there is often a tight sketch or scientific drawing incorporated: a bird, or lung, or kidney, because I am an illustrator and because I have a love for these things.
