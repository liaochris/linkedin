Luke Knight’s Summary

After graduating university, I worked as a unit leader at a multi-sports summer camp in America. I managed 15 other counselors to make sure they were supporting their campers and giving them the best experience possible. This experience has made me extremely confident in a professional environment, and I am confident in being a team leader.

Once back in the UK I spent 6 months in various intern roles ranging from production in a printing company, American political PR, and traditional/digital PR. During my first professional role at Intelligent PR I was in charge of developing the new digital PR department. I gained experience in many areas including project management and press communication. The client I worked with were from the bar/restaurant industry.

I am currently working within the 4Social department of 4Ps Marketing as a social media executive. It is a growing department which means I spend a lot of time on business development, internal marketing and sales. However my key role within the team is creating an integrated social media strategy that brings all forms of the clients marketing activity together.

Ideas are fantastic, but what really matters to e client I'd ROI. As I understand this need, I spend much of my time monitoring social media communities and evaluating current campaigns. It's always important to ditch the ideas that have little return, and create or maintain ideas that are beneficial.

I am a very motivated person; I aim to grow 4Social into the best integrated social media marketing agency in the UK.

Luke Knight’s Specialties:

I am very much a people person, which means I am at my best when meeting or presenting to clients face to face. However when in the office I am focussed on delivering results.
