Ian Rubin’s Summary

I have been in the health and wellness field for over 10 years and in that time I have come to believe that we learn about our bodies by being with our bodies. While you are moving, shopping, cooking or eating, thoughts, feelings and ideas about food, fitness and your body will come up. What a great time to talk about them! This approach allows you to get the benefits of personal training, cooking and shopping lessons, mindful eating practice AND gaining a deeper insight into your relationship with those issues at the same time.

I will help improve your relationship with food by focusing on more than the science of nutrition. In fact, a healthy relationship with food is as much about how we eat as what we eat. Most diet programs fail because they don’t consider the fact that food is one of life’s great pleasures. I help people remember that they’ll have more success if they honor the positive aspects of eating and lose the guilt that, in the long term, often leads to disappointment.

My approach to fitness is similar. Instead of focusing on the best science of fitness, which may include exercises you don’t enjoy, I use multiple approaches to intuitively create your workouts that are fun, challenging, safe, and based on how you're feeling that day. In this way, you will learn how your body works and what types of movement benefit life’s everyday challenges, such as lifting children, gardening without tiring yourself prematurely, or running in a way that maximizes the benefits and minimizes the risk of injury.

Ian Rubin’s Specialties:

Body Mechanics, Kinetic Chain, Improving Movement Patterns, helping those with illness, improving fitness in those with injuries that haven't responded well to other modalities, holistic approach to wellness.
