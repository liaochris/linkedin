Rania Batayneh, MPH’s Summary

As a Nutritionist, focused on lifestyle management for busy professionals, new mom's and brides to be, I change lifestyles for lasting results.

As a West Coast based Nutritionist, Essential Nutrition For You was started in San Francisco, California and now I practice from both San Francisco and Portland, Oregon. I also work with clients nation and world-wide via on-line consulting.

As America’s Eating Strategist®, I am able to identify a client's needs/barriers in order to create an effective strategy for change allowing the client to not only reach their goals, but to maintain them.

I am looking to expand my personal meal catering business to target specific markets in the US and in Dubai.

I am also writing my first book, "Confessions of a Nutritionist" and looking for a publishing deal at this time.

Moving into health and wellness reporting on a National level is also something I am pursuing at this time and look forward to these opportunities to help educate the public at large.

My experiences in Local News in San Francisco, Portland, and nationally on MSNBC have helped pave the way for my entry into mainstream media. (www.youtube.com/essentialnutrition).
