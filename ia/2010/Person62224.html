Emma Taylor’s Summary

I co-founded European Disaster Volunteers (EDV). EDV is a volunteer driven, UK registered charity that aids disaster affected communities world wide by providing initial aid and helping them achieve sustainable recovery.

Our origins are European, but our approach is global - we welcome all participants regardless of nationality.

EDV is unusual in the world of disaster response in that we don't charge any joining or participation fees and we accept all volunteers regardless of how much time they can commit, occupation, or financial status.

We founded this organization on the principle that you don't have to be a doctor, structural engineer, or rich to affect change in the lives of those affected by disaster.

In response to the recent string of natural disasters in Asia and the Pacific we have launched our Asia-Pacific Disaster Recovery Fund and plan to begin working abroad early next year.

To find our more about our deployment plans, please visit EDVolunteers.org
