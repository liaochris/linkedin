Valberta Guthrie’s Summary

I am a driven and hard working Graphic Designer interested in working full-time for a growing and steady company; as well as freelance. I’ve been in the graphics and printing industry for over 5+ years and I have seen the many changes since in regards to print and design.

When it comes to freelancing or working in-house for an individual or business, all my energy and drive is put through into the design process and the final piece. As a creative thinker, depending on the project, my designs will cater to that theme or business.

Through my years of designing, I have worked with numerous clients in different areas of the industry. They include: Real Estate, Health, Hair Care and Beauty. A side note: with my personal interest of nature, animals, automobiles and more, I will gladly assist you in those areas. All in all everything what I see and have experienced I put into my work.

I am currently going to school for web design in hopes of expanding my interactive design skills in this ever changing industry.

I also enjoy meeting and networking with new people. I believe my background in working with people have encouraged me more to transfer skills into other areas of interest. That is my greatest accomplishment!
