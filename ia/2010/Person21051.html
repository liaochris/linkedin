Becki Burrows’s Summary

Creative multimedia producer with a varied background in both production co-ordination (in both Film and Television), research and video production. Having created content for MTV News (broadcast in over 55 countries) to editing football highlights for ITV.com to being personally sponsored by both Pepsi and Vodafone for my own creative content production. I have a proven track record of working for and alongside high profile talent.
