Ariel Ferreira’s Summary

Ariel Ferreira is the president of Expansion Group, a government relations and public affairs consulting firm. Mr. Ferreira specializes in guiding nonprofit organizations of all sizes through various stages of their growth such as program development, staff training, board development, and grant writing. He is also a well-recognized political consultant, who provides assistance to candidates in all aspects relating to their campaigns such as campaign management, financial reports, voter and district analysis, media relations and other areas.

Mr. Ferreira is no stranger to the non-profit and political sector. Prior to launching his own consulting firm, Mr. Ferreira served as a legislative and budget director for an elected official in the New York City Council. While maintaining the legislative agenda and monitoring the progress of all legislative items, Mr. Ferreira directly assisted in developing and composing new legislation. He was also responsible for reviewing and processing the acquisition and distribution of $6.4 million in capital funding and $676,000 in expense funding for community based organizations. Mr. Ferreira worked closely with the Manhattan Delegation and Black, Latino & Asian Caucus on various legislative and budget issues. Mr. Ferreira also sat as a board member in the Manhattan Community Board 12. In addition, for more than 5 years, Mr. Ferreira worked as a youth program supervisor in Alianza Dominicana, Inc, where he empowered youth to become leaders of their community. In 2003, as one of the many acknowledgments for his selfless contributions to the community, he received a Proclamation Award from the New York City Council for his outstanding services and contributions to the City of New York.

Through his consulting firm, Mr. Ferreira is currently enhancing the non-profit sector and changing the face of local and state politics. Mr. Ferreira epitomizes the strength, vitality and conviction of the young leaders in today’s world.
