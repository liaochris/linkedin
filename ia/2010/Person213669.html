★ Deborah Hymes’s Summary

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Social Networks Have Always Existed
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Ever since people began gathering around campfires they’ve been telling each other where to find the best hunting and which toothpaste gives them the sexiest smile. Should your company add social media to its communications mix? That depends.

All media are tools for talking to your audience. Depending on your goals, some tools work better than others. But figuring out which tools to use can be confusing: How do they work? What’s the learning curve? How will they help you?

The WanderNot Social Survey™ untangles the confusion. It simplifies social media for you. It makes your job easier.
_______________________

WanderNot is a creative communications firm specializing in strategic social media for business. I show companies how to extend their brand story by using social media.

What story do you want to tell?
