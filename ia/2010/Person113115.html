Kellie DAndrea’s Summary

I have over 20 years of sales and marketing experience and am a successful speaker and business coach. I specialize in developing core teams and marketing/sales strategies that yield significant results. I am a seasoned executive with a passion for mentoring and have strong leadership skills to work with the team on problem solving and creative thinking. In 2009, I joined the info marketing revolution and created marketing and motivational systems to help small business owners and entrepreneurs achieve the business and life of their dreams.

The clients I work with are strong business owners with a passion for their business but struggle with marketing and sales execution. They want to build a brand and attract more clients with ease while maintaining their service integrity. My ideal clients are committed to making a difference and setting themselves apart from their competition. My marketing approach is based on branding techniques that include expert positioning through using a combination of grass root techniques with new marketing to support the brand concept. The last piece is delivering on the brand each and every day. This differs from traditional marketing which is more about taking about why you think you are great versus living it and proving it every day.

I'm author of the weekly eZine "The Marketing Edge" and the "BLAST System to Profits”, which help local retailers and store owners build a marketing strategy around branding and local area selling techniques. In addition, I am the creator of the Mentor in Me program, an on-line coaching program designed to offer advice, education resources and access to some of the top minds in a variety of industries and geared towards work-life balance. In addition we offer monthly teleseminars, articles and discounts on new products and services.

If you are interested in learning more about any of these programs, please email me at info@kelliedandrea.com
