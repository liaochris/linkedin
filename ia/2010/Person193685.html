Stefan Silke’s Summary

My ethos: Great jobs. Unbeatable service.

Did you know that by changing jobs you have a better chance of getting a promotion or a pay increase? Perhaps you feel like a change of environment or a new challenge?

I search for the best IT people from around the world for jobs in Amsterdam and The Netherlands and discuss with them the vacancies, their new employer and life in Amsterdam so that they can make an informed decision. I’m originally from Australia, but I’ve lived in Amsterdam for almost 7 years so I understand the needs of internationals here.

I monitor and assist the interview process and keep everyone up-to-date throughout. Once a job offer has been accepted I also assist with relocation and organise work visas for the successful candidate and their family.

For you the candidate my service is 100% free, so there are no costs and no obligations.

To take the first step simply visit thewhitedoor.com

Stefan Silke’s Specialties:

I am motivated by the long term needs and satisfaction of both my clients and candidates. I encourage the job seekers that I work with to take the opportunity that best suits them, and I stay in touch even if we don't immediately find them their new job.

Before moving into recruitment I had studied and worked in IT for almost 10 years, including 6 years in Australia and 7+ years in the Netherlands.
