Christopher J. Scharer’s Summary

Christopher is an elected Vivit Iowa Chapter leader and Sr. SQA Lead Automation Test Consultant with experience in software development and automated software testing since 1980. With experience in Data-Driven, Keyword Driven and Hybrid methodologies Christopher has helped companies achieve testing success in a wide variety of business applications (Banking, Finance, Health, Insurance, Mortgage, etc...). Most recently Christopher rallied several companies in Nebraska to form the new Nebraska Vivit Chapter. Christopher is primarily focused on Automation Training using HP (Mercury) products combined with the latest testing methodologies. Christopher is always interested in new technologies, as well as close interaction with the HP (Mercury) manufacturers. Christopher also serves on the Vivit Board of Directors.

Applications: WinRunner (WR), Test Director (TD), Quick Test Pro (QTP), WR/QTP Conversion
Environments: .Net, Client Server, Java, MainFrame, Web-Based
Databases: Access/Excel, Oracle, SQL Server, SQL Plus
Testing Methods: Black Box, White Box, Grey Box, Acceptance/Smoke
Testing Levels: Unit, Integration, System/Integration, Regressoin, Acceptance, Alpha, Beta
Processes: CMMI/Waterfall, Agile
Specialties:
