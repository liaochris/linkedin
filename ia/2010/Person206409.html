Tom Cavanagh’s Summary

Tom joined BCC Advisers in May 2006. He is a Transaction Adviser working primarily with BCC's M&A clients.

Tom's responsibilities include assisting business owners and managers in identifying and accomplishing their business transfer goals, as well as gathering, synthesizing, analyzing and documenting company background, financial, guideline company, economic and industry information pertinent to arriving at the value of businesses, economic losses and supporting the development of merger and acquisition intelligence on various M&A projects.

A Des Moines, Iowa native, Tom received his bachelors degree in Management: Business Administration from the University of Northern Iowa in 2006 and his MBA from Drake University in 2009.

Tom Cavanagh’s Specialties:

Mergers & acquisitions and business valuations.
