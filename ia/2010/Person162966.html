Peter McGratty CFA’s Summary

Precursor Research is a leading Investment Practice Management firm serving emerging, break-away and established independent financial advisors nationwide.

Whether you are an advisor looking to free more time to service your clients and grow your business, or whether you are an advisor looking to improve investment performance or risk management, we can help.

And importantly, in an environment where so much information comes from product vendors, we operate independent and free of conflicts because independent financial advisors deserve the same independent and un-conflicted support as their clients.
