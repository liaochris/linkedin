Omer Golan’s Summary

Omer Golan is a multimedia artist based in Tel-Aviv. Painter and stained glass artist, Omer have exhibited in group and solo exhibitions in the Netherlands, Israel and the United States.
He is presently immersed in generating multiple forms of digital arts, and socially aware interactive art installations with the graphical development environment Max/Msp/Jitter. Actively seeking inspiring collaborations with artists and scientists. Searching for new ways to use new and old technologies for sociological and artistic expressions. A devoted fan of TED.com.

Omer Golan’s Specialties:

Omer has been developing and designing websites and web applications from 2002.
We specialize in both front-end and back-end (CMS) design and development. Some of our services are Web-design, Graphic design, Interaction design, UI, Logo Design, Banner Design, We advise individuals and companies on Internet Marketing, Branding, production, and creative problem solving.
