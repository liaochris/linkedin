Rahul Jindal’s Summary

Rahul is an innovation driven leader with innate ability to build, motivate and run high performing multi-disciplinary teams. He thrives on delivering results with limited resources, driving innovation to adapt to change and taking decisions through consensus building. He brings a rich experience of scientifically driven operations management, complex product ideation and development, business development, and relationship with C-suite executives. He has built and run both value-led and volume-driven businesses. He believes in helping his clients, his company and his people succeed.

He joined CPA Global in Jan 2006 where he built and now has the overall responsibility of a multi-disciplinary operations team comprising engineers and technologists, statisticians, software developers and IP valuation professionals.

He has a proven track record of providing superior consulting related to IP Portfolio Optimization.

In addition to providing Intellectual Property Strategy consulting to clients in US and Europe, Rahul's team is building patent-pending, world-leading methodologies related to assessment and valuation of Intellectual Property.

Rahul is also responsible for supporting other large (multi-million GBP) businesses providing Patent, Trademark, Plant Rights & Design Data Verification service - a one of its kind service in the world.

Rahul also leads internal corporate development projects e.g. assessing location attractiveness of Indian cities from development of an Offshore Research Centre (ORC) perspective; conceptualization-to-implementation of an India based telemarketing channel for Knowledge services (KPO and LPO); Process Mapping and Re-engineering; and Metrics Development for Knowledge Services.
