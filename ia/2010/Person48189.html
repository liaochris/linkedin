Dan Newhall’s Summary

As a recruiting professional I stake my reputation on the quality of my candidates and personal service. When working with client companies I approach the relationship as a partnership, and work closely with the hiring manager to meet their needs. The reputation and success of both my clients and candidates is the priority, and I am an expert in finding and nurturing synergistic relationships. I always say "it takes 2 to Tango.".

At NCompass, our vision is to provide unparalleled service and top tier talent to thriving medical device companies. We are guided by our honest approach and commitment to integrity. We provide sensitive care distinguished by trust, respect, and an appreciation for individual differences.

Please contact me directly with any questions regarding job opportunities or recruiting needs.

Dan Newhall’s Specialties:

We recruit for medical device companies located in California. Our areas of expertise include: Manufacturing, R&D, Marketing, Quality Assurance, Clinical Research, Regulatory Affairs, and Operations.
