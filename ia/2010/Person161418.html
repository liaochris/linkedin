Paul Shrimpling’s Summary

Remarkable business practice requires strong business roots. Like the grass roots of bamboo that take up to 3 years to fully establish, and then help it grow to twice the height of a house in just one growing season.

Remarkable Practice helps accountancy firms develop strong business roots so they enjoy remarkable growth too.

Having bought accountancy services for fifteen years as a business owner I have subsequently spent 6 years helping accountancy firms apply business common sense to their firms. Initially with AVN and more recently with Remarkable Practice.

To discuss what it is we do and how we can help you and your firm please email theteam@remarkablepractice.com.

Paul Shrimpling’s Specialties:

Remarkable Practice help accountancy firms become remarkable at marketing, remarkable at running their practice and remarkable at building great teams. By doing so these firms secure a bright future and remarkable results.
