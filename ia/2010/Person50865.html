David B. Haines’s Summary

David Haines is a senior media and entertainment executive with over 18 years of experience creating cost-effective organizational structures through an understanding of key business and market drivers.

Mr. Haines started his career in the entertainment industry at Deluxe Entertainment Group, as Director Media Business Development, where he spearheaded the company’s inaugural digital media initiatives, and was instrumental in the discovery and acquisition of Vision Entertainment, Sonic Foundry Media Services, and Ritek Global Media.

Following his position at Deluxe, Mr. Haines founded Fundamental Strategies, Inc, which provided strategic business development consulting services to established and early stage companies. Clients included, CalTech, Uniloc, SONY , and MidNet. His next opportunity lead him to THX, where he was hired as Director, Media Business Development to spearhead all business development activities for a new consumer entertainment technology called Media Director. In September of 2008, Mr. Haines founded (BPL) Best Practices Laboratory for Entertainment Technology, a non-profit entertainment technology laboratory located at historic Raleigh Studios-Hollywood. In addition to his duties as Chairman and Executive Director of BPL, Mr. Haines is President and CEO of The nipperrunes Company, a children’s multi-platform content company, and sits on the advisory board of Prima Cinema, Inc.
