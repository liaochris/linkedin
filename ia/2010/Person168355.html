Danielle DeRogatis (Gletow)’s Summary

As a creative and passionate marketer, I thrive in organizations that value an entrepreneurial spirit and encourage the development of new and innovative ideas. My primary expertise lies in online marketing - specifically creating successful CRM programs and utilizing the web to reach, engage and secure loyal customers. By redesigning website navigation paths and layouts, I have been responsible for impressive increases in web traffic, transactions and overall customer satisfaction.

With strong writing skills, an analytical mind and a focused, multi-channel approach, I have created and sustained unique and profitable marketing programs for over eight years.

ABOUT MY PAST WORK:
In my role as Web Marketing Director at Films Media Group, my work resulted in a significant increase in online and email revenue. The enhancements and improvements I contributed to online initiatives proved to both increase customer satisfaction and improve the bottom line.

With the vision of creating an online community targeted to our educators, students and industry experts, I was responsible for creating my.films.com - a portal designed to allow these individuals to come together to share ideas and discuss our educational media products.

Previously, as the Internet Marketing Manager for Chelsea Premium Outlets, I took our online VIP Shopper Club from 125,000 members to over 750,000 in just under two years working with over 200 retailers including Kate Spade, Liz Claiborne, Van Heusen, Reebok and Cole Haan. We used innovative viral marketing strategies and intriguing feature enhancements to attract new members and establish loyalty among our current users. Plus, we created fun, interactive games and promotions to keep our customers interested in coming back day after day.
