Julie Niles Petersen’s Summary

I am known for my dedication and passion for teaching reading and sharing knowledge. I am currently blogging about teaching reading at www.TWRCtank.com and serving as the president of the Orange County Reading Association.

I was a reading specialist in the Irvine Unified School District for four years and taught first grade in Santa Ana for five. If you would like to learn more about me, feel free to visit my blog. My post titled, “How I Lost My Reading Specialist Position & What Makes a Good Literacy Coach” might be of special interest to you. I invite you to read the comments on that post as well.

I stay current with the research in the field of literacy by attending conferences and conventions all over the world and by being a part of my local, state, and international reading associations. I am skilled at assessment, analyzing the results, and matching instruction with student needs.

Above all, I am a TWRCr* who strives to make continuous refinements in order to be the best I can be. I relish the opportunity to work with people who feel the same way. I find discussions about best practices in teaching reading intellectually stimulating.

*“TWRC” rhymes with “work” and stands for think, wonder, reflect, and connect.
