With our 53,000 square foot state-of-the-art production facility located in Lake Zurich, IL, Failsafe Media is one of the largest full service CD/ DVD/ USB Flash Drive/ Memory Card Replicators in the United States. Since 1983, we have provided our many Fortune 500 customers with complete manufacturing, printing, packaging, assembly, warehousing, fulfillment, and drop shipping services. We save you time and money by coordinating all components of your project from A to Z.

With our CD manufacturing capacities at 800,000 per day and DVD manufacturing capacities at 100,000 per day, we are able to offer quick turnarounds. Our 133 LPI silk screen and 175 LPI offset disk printing is the highest quality in the industry.

Failsafe Media is also the largest Memory Card Duplication center in North America. Our service includes, MMC duplication, secure digital duplication, Wi-Fi duplication, mini SD duplication, smart media duplication, CompactFlash duplication, flash card duplication, IDE flash drive duplication, Micro SD duplication, USB duplication, & cruzer duplication.
