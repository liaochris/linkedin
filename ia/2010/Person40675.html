Cindy Yantis’s Summary

Approach life, career, relationships, leadership from a powerful place.

• Deliver a powerful speech to a room of one or 500
• Enter and own a room
• Present a pitch like a Broadway star
• Reinvent yourself
• Define your Personal Brand
• Find your dream job or go for a promotion
• Be a more effective leader

As a Career Architect Cindy Yantis takes the guesswork out of the game plan for success. She guides business teams and executives, and individuals wanting to make improvements or changes in their careers and lives.

PRIVATE COACHING
Whether in-person, via email or by phone, Cindy has the program to fit your schedule and deliver the results you require. The best programs many times are a combination of all three!

* Life & Career Transition Architecting
* Presentations, Speeches & Pitching
* Executive & Leadership Building
* Physical Presence & Image Presentation
* Personal Branding & Marketing

PERSONAL BRANDING

And, your story is your brand. What is your Personal Brand?

It is when:

* You are aligned with your values
* You know what makes you and your market tick
* You understand and own what makes you distinct
* You put that knowledge into a blueprint that helps to guide your life and career and presents you to the world in a way that is only yours...

Cindy and her brand team will take you through her unique steps toward defining you, both to yourself which can be a surprising journey; and to the world as it opens up to you in ways you didn't imagine.

The work begins with dedicated private coaching and ends with your blueprint for success and a Personal Brand Platform you will use to remain consistent and on point in your pursuits.

From that you'll have your unique "Foundation Statement" and be prepared to Own the Room, book the job, get the promotion, network with confidence, give the speech, etc!

Cindy Yantis’s Specialties:
