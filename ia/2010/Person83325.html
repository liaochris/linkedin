Hugo Eccles’s Summary

Hugo has 15 years of professional experience, including 9 years of directorship experience, working with European, Asian and American clients. He graduated from the Royal College of Art in 1995 with the prestigious Kokuyo Award for 'Outstanding Innovative Furniture', joining IDEO as part of the team which won a 1997 IDEA Award for Design Exploration. Hugo worked with British designer Ross Lovegrove from 1997 until 1998, when he founded the Baionik Studio to service an international clientele including Honda, Laurent-Perrier, Nike and Ford Motor Company. In 2003 Hugo joined Fitch Inc., directing a team which won 7 awards in 3 years including an IDEA Gold Award in 2005, the consultancy's first Gold Award win in a decade. In 2006 Hugo accepted the position of Design Director, Innovation Lab. at the Arnell Group, developing and managing strategic partnerships with Fortune 500 clients. Hugo is currently Creative Director & Partner at Teroforma, an new tableware company launched in Feb '08.
