CAREER SUMMARY

Mentored for decades by the best in class - Toyota USA and the Walt Disney Company top marketing executives; I develop strategic, innovative, unique and comprehensive Branding & Marketing Communications Plans.

An outstanding achievement was my collaboration with Lexus, authoring the Creative Brief, detailing the graphic treatment of the logo, in writing. This process created the final Lexus logo design. The logo is the world’s second-most recognizable automotive logo, according to Forbes.com. The Lexus logo also helped to build the foundation for the brand to become the #1 luxury vehicle in the U.S. market.

My numerous, long-term collaborations with Fortune-rated clients, illustrates a dedication to continuous improvement, innovations and accomplishments.
