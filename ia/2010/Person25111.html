Bill Molnar’s Summary

Senior executive with extensive General Management experience managing P&L, Sales & Marketing, R&D, Manufacturing, Strategic Planning and Business Development. Business experience with companies offering reagents, kits and tools for life science research, diagnostics and analytics as well as specialty and fine chemicals.

Major international company experience working at Agilent Technologies, Merck KGaA (EMD Chemicals), Allied Signal, Baxter Healthcare, Fisher Scientific, Erachem Comilog (formerly Chemetals) as well as small entrepreneurial business units.

Proven track record of leading turnarounds, integrating acquisitions and generating growth in diverse businesses, including fine and specialty chemicals, as well as reagents, kits and tools for research and life science applications.

Accomplishments include repositioning mature product lines, introducing new products, advancing product sales both directly and through multiple distribution channels, and delivering profitable growth.

Proven ability to analyze businesses and markets; to envision growth opportunities, to develop leading products and services and to implement strategies which improve operational effectiveness, market leadership and customer satisfaction.

Successful at identifying, securing and leveraging strategic alliances and partner relationships to improve competitive advantage, increase market penetration and drive sales growth.

Big-Picture and Hands-On day-to-day leadership with a passion for interfacing with customers.
