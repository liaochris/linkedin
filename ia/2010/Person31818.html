Candice Frederick’s Summary

Savvy publicist, award-winning journalist and film blogger who has maximized her sharp media skills to spearhead successful book tours, launch a New York Times bestseller, produce a record-breaking digital and print content campaign and helm an interactive movie review website.

Candice Frederick’s Specialties:

top-notch written and verbal communication skills, generating innovative ideas, writing and editing, excellent organizational skills, project management, event planning, film reviews, relationship articles, proven ability to develop key media contacts
