Matthew Gardner’s Summary

In January 2009, we launched Yepser Internet Strategies - www.yepser.com. The nature of the business is to allow small and mid-size business owners the opportunity to grow their existing business by using the Internet as a tool. From Internet Advertising, Search Engine Marketing, Social Media Marketing and Website Optimization, Yepser provides a complete package to a business owner.

In July of 2009 we were fortunate to be given a "behind the scenes" look on how business can use Social Media to grow their business. We entered into a competition to handle Nestle Butterfinger's Social Media account in Atlanta for the summer and even through we placed 2nd out of 450 total applicants, we did learn how Social Media could be used for business.

We now use a Social Media strategy that can both grow a business's brand as well as generate leads for their business.

The Internet is very easy to use for people to find what they are looking for. However, it is very difficult for a business owner to use the Internet to help their business grow. That is where we can help.

We work with business owners to create, develop and maintain an Internet Strategy. An effective Internet Strategy allows a business owner to grow their business with less cost utilizing the resources of Yepser.
