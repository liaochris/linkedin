Wes Brewer’s Summary

Based on nearly 30 years of experience in the Consumer Electronics and Data Storage technology sectors, I bring a strong compliment of innovation, development talent and management experience in both OEM and Retail channels. I have direct experience in engineering, product line marketing, technical support, business development and strategic planning, and have managed a $2B+ P/L. With that perspective, I’m capable of guiding both large and small enterprise organizations into new, dynamic and uncharted markets. My operational experience, presence and communication style inspire confidence and respect from peers and senior leaders. Through mutual trust and respect, I lead from the front in motivating and stretching my employees to innovate and develop breakthrough products, lower costs and improved profitability. My hands-on style of management and balanced approach to problem solving allow me to perform at the highest levels and the results are reflected in a proven track record of achieving positive results in diverse environments and challenging markets.
