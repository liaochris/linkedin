Qiang Lin’s Summary

Extensive experience (over 15 years) in Electronics System/Circuit Design of Wireless Communication / Telecommunication Equipments and Test Equipments especially in Analog and RF.

More than 15 years of experience in software/firmware and digital hardware design (standard TTL components and CPLD).

Strong communication and signal processing background with 9 years of experience as a
Communication Systems Engineer, PhD in Communication Science, multiple years of experience wireless communication systems design, plus DSP involved in many designs.

Experience with multiple Digital Mobile Communication Systems and Wireless LAN Systems.

Experience in both designing and tuning circuits over very wide frequency range from DC to 15GHz.

Designed over 100 industrial products and multiple commercial products with world-class performance.

Coauthor of 3 pending US patents, and author of 4 pending US patents.

Extensive experience with typical RF test instruments. Very strong lab skill including soldering
(can handle parts as small as 0201 size). Always actively participate debugging and characterization in lab.

Qiang Lin’s Specialties:

RF, analog, Wireless and Wired Communications, Signal Processing.
