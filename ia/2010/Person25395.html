Bo Bergstrom’s Summary

“Demonstrates ability to identify, analyze and solve problems. Manages the unexpected with coolness.”
— Colonel D. M. Richtsmeier, USMC

It is not often that one gets the opportunity to hit the "Reset" button on life. However, after 7 years in the Marine Corps I am getting just that chance. The once in a lifetime opportunity to decide what to do with the rest of my life, for the second time!

As a Captain who recently picked up his MBA from a top school, I am not your average jarhead getting out of the military. Since graduating from the United States Naval Academy I gained experiencein team leadership, project management, operations, finance, supply chain management and strategydevelopment. In addition to seven years of Marine Corps service I have consulted for two startups in the biotech and renewable energy industries and am interested in finance, business development, and product manager positions.

The more I learn about the business world the more I keep coming to the conclusion that beyond the business model or technology it is the team that determines success. In the right team each person understands their role, but also that we win or lose together. However, when it comes down to the wire they know there are no roles; just work that needs to be done. With this in mind, I am not looking for just a 9 to 5 or to be another cog in the machine. Instead, I am looking for a team with a purpose where I can make an immediate impact. A place where I can truly work and use both elbow grease and ingenuity to earn success.

If you know of any great teams, opportunities or contacts I should talk with then please send them my way. In the meantime, I will be networking with all I'm worth to try and meet and greet my way into that next career!
