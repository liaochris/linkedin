Keith Munshower’s Summary

Keith is proud to join MCA Business Brokers as a Sales Associate. Keith’s knowledge and background in the financial services industry with a specialty in estate planning brings a unique prospect to business owners. For over fourteen years Keith has been working with small businesses, family owned companies, and farms to help them with transfers, inheritance, and succession planning. This unique and innovative approach has helped him bring a new approach in helping buyers and sellers obtain the right business and price for their projects. Over the years Keith, has developed a strong network of contacts and business professionals that provide every project with a focus on details, individual attention, competitive pricing structures, and marketing that ranges from grass roots to a national presence.

Keith was the founder and Vice President of Premeir Wealth Management. As Vice President Keith help grow what was a single office into four offices located throughout Central Pennsylvania. He also increased it’s sales force and client base while researching and obtaining smaller agencies to increase market share. After selling his company, he was a direct appointment as a Partner and Director with two different Fortune 500 companies with offices here in the Harrisburg area. As a manager he helped both companies in expansion of employees and client base while helping in day to day operations. As a current business owner himself Keith understands the ups and downs that business owners experience. Such as human resource issues to growing and marketing a new business. Thinking outside of the box has always helped Keith both in the past and in todays changing environment.

Keith was born and raised in the Harrisburg area and knows how important that is when it comes to serving his clients. Knowing the area and working with so many professionals over his business career has helped Keith understand what it takes to make the deal complete.
