Scott Gould’s Summary

In 2003 developed early social media applications a bit too far ahead of their time!

In 2005 started a youth organisation, with over 1,000 young people in attendance each month. Made lots of mistakes but learnt what young people want.

In 2006 worked in TV production, podcasting, new media.

In 2008 founded Aaron+Gould, an agency specialising in brand experience and making products and services that are emotionally engaging... It took me a year to learn how to express what I wanted the agency to do in just one sentence.

In 2009 co-founded Like Minds, "a discovery service for people to find new stuff and new ways of doing things with other like minded people" - first conference on the subject on Measuring Social Media in October 2009.

I'm young but had the opportunity to be exposed to wide range of creative disciplines and ideas. I've made plenty of mistakes, and blog regularly about them.
