Kendra Cleary’s Summary

The constant change and evolution of the media business really excites me, specifically the convergence of digital media and television. Representing top sports websites including NASCAR.com, PGATOUR.com and PGA.com has allowed me to work extensively with live event streaming, online video and companion viewing opportunities. One of my favorite projects was integrating a client’s branding into a weekly cartoon series.

I am passionate about the media sales business where I maintain a strong track record of exceeding revenue goals. My emphasis throughout my career has been on developing lasting relationships by providing superior customer service and quick follow-through. I enjoy working with clients to develop creative sponsorships and advertising placements. This has led to a long list of valued contacts at major advertising agencies and clients across the Chicago and Detroit territories including Minnesota, Missouri, Kansas, Ohio, and Wisconsin. My expertise lies in a wide range of categories including QSR, CPG, Retail, Travel, Automotive and Financial/Insurance.

I am a loyal team-builder with exceptional work ethic and experience, ready to take on new challenges and grow revenues for your company.
