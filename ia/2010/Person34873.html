Celia R. Dyer, D.M.D.’s Summary

Founder and Executive Producer at TechDrawl.com in Atlanta, GA, a video blog showcasing technology startups and emerging privately held companies and their executives from "D.C. to Texas." Strategic planning, content aggregation, editorial and video content production, and video post-production in Final Cut Pro for http://techdrawl.com.

Recently appointed member of the Board of Directors at the Atlanta Community Food Bank, a 30 year-old organization providing over 2 million pounds of food to 800 non-profit agencies each month to feed the hungry. Advisory Board member of AppointmentCity,LLC, a lead-generation startup for dental and medical practices founded by former COO of MFG.com, Sherwin Krug and MFG.com marketing strategist, Chris Mitchell. A founding member of StartupChicks.net, an organization founded by Atlanta technology entrepreneur Jennifer Bonnett of eTour.com fame. StartupChicks' mission is networking and mentoring for women founders of high-growth technology companies.

Licensed general dentist by profession, formerly specializing in general and cosmetic restorative procedures including dental implant services. Owned Tuxedo Dental, P.C., in the Buckhead community of Atlanta, GA. Previously owned an apparel manufacturing company, Bello Bianco Sportswear, Inc., HQ in Atlanta, GA with operations in Birmingham, AL and Atlanta.
