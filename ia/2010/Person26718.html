  • Contract Design Data Management Project

    Toro Corporation

    (Mechanical or Industrial Engineering industry)

    2004 — 2005 (1 year )

    The Toro Company over the years has purchased a number (8 to 10) different companies to enhance its product line.

    A project was developed to merge all of the prints and drawings from all of these sources into a single database for each type of major part classification. To do this, prints were evaluated and the characteristics of each part were entered into a data base for that general part. The idea being, that it was not necessary nor economical to have 8 or 10 different part numbers for the same 1/4-20 UNC X 1" machine screw within the company's data base.

    Approximately 12 to 15 different part categories were established during this project with some 15,000 prints being evaluated, classified and the data entered into the company's design database.
