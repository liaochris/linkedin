Denise Cicchella, MBA, CIA, CFE, CCA, FLMI,’s Summary

I have founded Auspicium to provide the highest levels of service and expertise to my clients. I am an audit and risk management leader with experience across multiple industries. My passion is Construction Audit: I enjoy enabling my clients to achieve significant recoveries from overpayments and facilitating the creation of effective processes to prevent recurrence.

I am a Certified Internal Auditor, Certified Fraud Examiner, Certified Construction Auditor, Fellow of Life Management and a NASD Series 7 Representative (currently my registration is parked).

Author of Construction Audit: Overview, Monitoring and Auditing published by the Institute of Internal Auditors and Co-Author of Essentials of Construction Management published by Informa.

Training: I have been a speaker and trainer for the IIA (North Jersey Chapter, Las Vegas Gaming Conference and Dubai); the NJ Healthcare Financial Management Association; North Carolina Association of Certified Fraud Examiners and I also deliver internal training tailored to client needs. I will be speaking at the IIA Atlanta International Conference in June 2010.
