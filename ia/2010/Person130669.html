  • Worldwide Benefits Manager

    Worldwide Benefits Manager, reporting to the VP of HR (2008 – Present)
    Compensation and Benefits Manager (2005 – 2008)

    Grew benefit programs from a small company of 375 employees in 19 countries, to over 1500 in over 30 countries in Asia-Pacific, North and South America, and Europe.

    • Aggressively worked each year to find creative ways to minimize or eliminate any cost increases in the US Benefit Plans. For the 2009 plan year, this resulted in a million dollars in savings.     • Led project team to change 401(k) providers from MFS to Fidelity, which resulted in improved employee satisfaction due to the better investment options, lower fees, better employee education tools, and ease of administration.

    • Created Disability Management Program, which centralized worldwide management of leaves of absence, reduced compliance errors, and provided better case management to affected employees.

    • Responsible for international and US benefit plans integration and termination during 3 company acquisitions.

    • Created and implemented annual compensation planning tool for focal compensation reviews that resulted in allowing the management team to more accurately forecast salary costs for the fiscal year.

    • Managed staff of two full time employees and one part time contractor
