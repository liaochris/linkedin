Josh Berger’s Summary

Josh Berger was born in Los Angeles, CA and grew up in Santa Cruz, CA. He was raised in a spiritually conscious family with the focus of creating peace and awakening in the world. Josh has always been tuned into spiritual awareness and underwent a vision quest at age 13. He also attended traveling school and studied in South Africa where he was immersed in the culture and visited and attended school in Cape Town, Sawato, and Zimbabwe, doing community service in the poorest neighborhoods along the way.

Josh fell in love with acting at 8 years of age. He has studied with some of the top teachers in the Los Angeles including, Ivana Chubuck. He has starred in numerous plays and participated in several films. Josh looked for ways to expand his creativity and found poetry. He has continued to develop his craft with music and spoken word.

Josh started a company called Berger Bros. Inc. which is a comprehensive multi-media production company. Its subsidiary companies comprise of Fortress Film House and Interconnected Worldwide.

Fortress Film House is a production company that focuses on making bar-setting films. They are currently in production with a film titled, Thicker Than Blood.

Interconnected Worldwide is a promotion and marketing company, which uses the social networks to promote businesses and products. This company deals with top programmers and professionals in this ever growing industry of social network marketing.

Josh continues to learn and educate himself on the arts. He is enrolled in classes as we speak and has formed an on-camera collective to stay fresh and ready to work in the industry. Josh Berger is a force to be reckoned with in the young actors of Hollywood.
