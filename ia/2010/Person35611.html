I am currently looking for a full time role that combines my analytical background in engineering and lean manufacturing and my experience and passion for marketing. I used my passion for marketing as a participate of the employee recruitment teams and worked independently to spearhead a scholarship program for a previous employer.

I believe with strong conviction that the organizations that succeed in the long run are the organizations that work to develop and grow transparent relationships with employees, suppliers, customers, and the community in which they exist.
