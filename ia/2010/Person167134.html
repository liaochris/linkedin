Rachael Ann Siciliano’s Summary

I am currently a User Experience Architect / Senior Product Manager at Fusebox where I translate business requirements into usable interfaces for targeted audiences.

Working under tight deadline, I rapidly develop clean XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP prototypes with accompanying back-end data models and technical specifications. I then coordinate clients, writers, designers, and coders through an agile project development cycle, delivering work on time and under budget.

Between 2000 and 2005, I served as Fusebox's Director of Information Architecture. In this role, I trained and managed a team of 7 Information Architects, oversaw over 25 projects for more than 20 clients, gathered business requirements, articulated users' needs, developed technical specifications, prepared test scripts, coordinated testing, and wrote manuals.

Prior to joining Fusebox, I worked as a Senior Information Architect at Wechsler Ross and Partners (1999), an Instructional Technologist at the University of California Santa Barbara, Office of Instructional Consultation (1996—1998), and a Foreign Language Instructor at various universities (1989—1996).

While my extensive work with JPMorgan, New York Life, Goldman Sachs, and Discover Bank has given me particular expertise in the data, regulations, and online marketing of financial products, my first love is designing educational software.

I hold 3 degrees in French Literature: a BA from the University of Wisconsin at Madison, a Masters from Rice University, and a Ph.D. from the University of California at Santa Barbara.

In my spare time I ride bikes and play harps.
