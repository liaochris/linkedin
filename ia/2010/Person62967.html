Elaine Love’s Summary

Few, if any, excel by staying in the crowd and doing what everyone else does. I certainly did not do that. I am an entrepreneur at heart. True entrepreneurs step away from the “tell me what to do” employee mentality and reach for their goals.

It’s fun to step out of my comfort zone and step away from the crowd. I know how to dare to be different and achieve all my goals. Even better, I know how to teach you how to achieve all of your goals.

My entrepreneurial spirit demanded to be expressed. I have managed a chain of Goodyear tire dealerships in Detroit as a very feminine lady, owned and operated a full scale bakery in a ski resort, started a property management company from initial idea and built it to the largest one in the entire resort specializing in single family homes.

Time for a new challenge. Entrepreneurialism runs through my veins; however, the long hours and being on call 24/7/365 leaves a dent in the ideal business model. When the business owns you as it did me, it was time to sell.

Finally, I found my ideal company: essential products people really need and want, generous compensation plan, co-founders and leadership with integrity, and a solid business plan. My entrepreneurial spirit thrives, my bank account smiles, and I genuinely help people achieve their dreams and goals. Wow, I found it all.

Life is more than just work, life requires balance. I firmly believe a lady can be feminine, athletic and successful.

Enjoy your life, achieve balance and prosper. Now I truly have it all. Health Wealth and Freedom to live and Enjoy Life. If you are ready to start living instead of existing, come join the most successful team and let us share our dreams and achievements. Even better, let us inspire and mentor you to achieve all of your dreams.
