Laurie Ayers’s Summary

Laurie Ayers is a speaker, author and personal safety and self defense expert who has given seminars in schools, organizations and corporations. Her message is inspiring, humorous and empowering; delivering the importance of making wise choices to protect ourselves and those we love. She is an active contributor on the subjects of preventing abduction and safety for college students. Laurie is a member of AWSDA (Association of Women's Self Defense Advancement). She holds a Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do and is the former owner of LA Self Defense.

For fun -

Laurie is an internet marketer who offers Scentsy Wickless Candles. Her website, http://www.ThrivingCandleBusiness.com, serves as a support and information network for candle consultants and those interested in working from home.
