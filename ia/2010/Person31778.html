Candace Klein’s Summary

Today’s political and economic climates have combined to fuel a growing intersection between commerce and government. Whether it is increased regulation or proposed legislation affecting your industry or new opportunities for project procurement, the impact of government on our businesses is unmistakable.

As a business person, government advocacy and compliance is not how you want to spend your time and energy. Rather, your products, services and areas of expertise are your principal concerns. Understanding these realities, Graydon Head serves as an advocate for our diverse business clients, working to facilitate those intersections and create a favorable climate for success with governments locally, regionally and nationally.

Our Business Advocacy & Governmental Relations practice area brings together the trusted legal counsel to which Graydon Head business clients have long been accustomed, the governmental relations capabilities traditionally found only in independent consulting firms, and, importantly, the political and governmental experience and familiarity necessary to achieve our clients’ goals. We offer all of the industries we serve a team of full service legal professionals dedicated to providing comprehensive legislative, regulatory, economic development, tax, real estate and land use services. Let us help you navigate the often arduous governmental processes your business faces to secure your needed legislative changes, licenses, approvals, financing, and projects.

Candace Klein’s Specialties:

I have worked with local and state government for 8 years and have extensive experience with economic development organizations through past work and volunteer experience.
