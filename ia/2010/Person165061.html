Ashish Deshpande’s Summary

Ashish Deshpande is a detail oriented thought leader and people manager in the area of delivery and program, change management, quality management and processes. He has pioneered programs in delivery, change and process management and brought out and effectively managed the behavioral aspects in times of transitions and change in a few of the world's historic acquisitions. Ashish is also a behavioral and process trainer and has authored several training materials and processes which are being used by fortune 500 organizations.
Ashish currently is employed as a program manager in one of the IT giants and is responsible for the healthcare applications delivery, people management and customer relationships across the United States. He has an experience of working with varied global customers across Europe, US and Australia with business critical deliveries and cross cultural teams. He has a certified project management qualification and is a six sigma green belt.
