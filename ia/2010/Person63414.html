Elisa Doucette’s Summary

Elisa Doucette is a freelance writer and small business social media consultant based out of Portland, Maine. She is a frequent contributor to many young professional and social media websites, and has been named to multiple listings for Generation Y writers. In addition to her own website, Ophelia's Webb, she has written for numerous publications - including The Single Slice, her popular online relationship and dating column on MaineToday.com.

Elisa works with various small business owners and non-profit organizations to optimize their online presence through website design and copy, strategic use of social networking, brand awareness and “virtual perception.” She believes passionately in the power of relationships and social networking and educates her clients on not only the basics of setting up and utilizing social media tools but also the extreme relevance of thoughtful interaction and strategic engagement.

She is always looking for new and interesting ways to challenge herself and engage others to achieve their greatest potentials, both professionally and personally.
