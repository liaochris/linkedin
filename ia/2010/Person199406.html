Tammy A. Morton’s Summary

I looked for years to be able to work for myself. I like getting paid twice and having money in the bank.

I didn't want a traditional business, like the type that I helped my parents run- due to the amount of time it takes to get it off the ground. So I got started in Network Marketing.


I learned some very valuable skills from my mentors, and they helped me with increasing my people/relationship skills and other critical skills. But I did realize that due to my full-time work schedule, I didn't have the 'time' to do the outbound dials needed to accomplish my goal.


Now don't get me wrong... old school Network Marketing works like a charm and it does take a specific Skill Set (which is very rewarding) that I was fortunate enough to learn from the very best in the industry.... BUT I needed my 'time' to be leveraged. So I turned to the Internet- determined to learn Internet Marketing and implement a system that wasn't old and rusted out- like the one that we are taught to use in traditional Network Marketing.

It is vitally important to become a MASTER MARKETER.

That is why we created AttractWealthAtHome.com and AttractNewReps.com as an online training facility to teach these “secrets” to success to struggling business owners.
