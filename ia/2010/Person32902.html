Carole Elam’s Summary

I founded Career Vision (Global) Ltd in 2002 on my return to the UK from the USA. I am a Leadership Development Consultant, Executive Coach, Facilitator and Learning and Development specialist, who has extensive international and domestic experience in both the public and private sectors.

I have lived and worked in Europe, Pacific Asia and America where I was instrumental in the development of staff for Shared Service Centres. I have gained a proven record of achievement in developing and implementing coaching programmes for individuals and teams, organisation development, leadership development, performance management, psychometric assessments and learning and development to support an organisation’s business strategy. I take an all round approach to the development process of individuals and enabling leadership through change.

Carole Elam’s Specialties:

Leadership and Management Development, Change Management, Performance Management, Executive and team Coaching, Facilitation, Learning & Development programmes, Assessment Tools, Emotional Intelligence, NLP, Shared Service Centre implementations.
