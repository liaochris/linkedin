Alex Shapiro’s Summary

While I have done PR and Corporate Communications for a wide variety of brands - from architects to art logistics - my true passion and expertise lie in B2b Tech PR.

My current role at Wind River, where I've been for over three years, has pushed me to develop expertise in aviation, transport, automotive and mobile software. This unique role has meant learning to own the issues of Linux kernel forking, DO-178B safety critical certification, Automotive In-Vehicle Infotainment and a wide variety of other fascinating glimpses of what makes the machines around us stay safe and secure or just innovative and exciting.

I currently manage Wind River's PR agency network: creating content that can be leveraged globally, pushing agencies to achieve their maximum potential and ensuring that Wind River owns the topics relevant to our customers.

I believe that effective PR must rely on a mixture of traditional and forward-thinking content - from by-line articles and engaging photography to twitter and video - a marketing mix thrives on diversity of content and channels and, at least for the moment, the rumors of B2B print media's death have been grossly exaggerated.
