Chris Moody’s Summary

As Product Marketing Manager, Chris Moody leads the marketing and social media efforts for Phonebooth.com, a leading small business phone systems provider built by smart software guys. Phonebooth helps companies across the country establish reliable phone connections through the use of internet lines instead of traditional telephone lines. Operated by Bandwidth.com, Inc., Phonebooth offers two unique small business phone solutions: Phonebooth Free and Phonebooth OnDemand.

A powerful yet simple business VoIP provider, Phonebooth uses the latest technology available to provide high quality, competitively priced small business phone solutions. Unlike phone plans using traditional telephone lines, Phonebooth offers HD voice quality sound and other unique options such as the ability for different office branches to use the same phone system. Additionally, Phonebooth Mobile offers Phonebooth services integrated with the most current smartphones and mobile broadband. With a focus on savings, simplicity, sound and productivity, Phonebooth has a phone plan to fit the needs of any small business.

Chris blogs for Phonebooth at phonebooth.com/blog and also personally at chris-moody.com. He is frequently seen guest lecturing at local universities and discussing social media and new marketing methods. Chris received his MBA from North Carolina State University, along with a BS in Business Management. In addition to his passion for promoting Phonebooth and participating in social media events and education, Chris also enjoys playing basketball and softball in his spare time.
