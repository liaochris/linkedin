Rithy Vong’s Summary

Rithy is currently Chief Operations Officer, Co-founder and owner at Nemo Agency : the first Net to Mobile Agency on the French Market.
Nemo is “full service” agency dedicated to the Mobile Marketing:
Our goal is to create value for advertisers and media in linking Internet to mobile marketing. Our references: Fnac Music, Easybourse, Warner, MCM, Virgin…

Rithy joined DIGIPLUG in 2004, he is in charge of sales , business development & partnerships. Focused on Music & telcos industries.

Rithy served as Sales & Marketing Manager at SPB (French leading insurance broker) from 2001 to 2003, He was responsible in branding new products based on IT for French banks. He launched "BNP Net Card" for BNP PARIBAS bank to insure the online web purchase Visa credit card holders against non delivery items and internet virus attacks on PC/MAC. He also worked on key customers : CREDIT LYONNAIS & CIC group.

Before working in SPB, Rithy was at Internet Telecom (French white label ISP for brands) as Sales Manager for banking, financial & insurance companies. He provided consulting on white label ISP for banks (CREDIT AGRICOLE, Barclay’s, AXA, etc.),

Rithy began his career in IT company and joined CEGETEL Enterprise company (VIVENDI Group) in 1998, a leading telecom provider for B2B, as an Account Manager in charge of international global accounts.

Rithy holds a Master degree in Business & Marketing Strategy.
