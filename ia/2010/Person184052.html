Scott Blinn’s Summary

I am a 15 year industry veteran of award wining software and video game development for PC and consoles. I am also an expert of the video game production pipeline, commercial software/hardware, and designing custom development tools.


• Building and leading world-class development teams.
• Deep understanding of all aspects of game development pipelines and tools.
• Design and development of custom development tools and technology.
• Agile game development methodologies and practices, inc. SCRUM (I was Scrum Master certified in 2006).
