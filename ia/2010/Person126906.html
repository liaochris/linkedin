Laura Crest’s Summary

After my education in journalism at MSU, I sought out a writing profession that actually pays a living wage, and found Professional Resume and Writing Service in Denver, CO. Within 8 months, I was twice promoted from the newbie "test" location in downtown Denver to the franchise's highest-volume office near Aurora, CO.

I have written resumes and cover letters ever since - 25 years' now - as the profession evolved from typewriters and crude cut-and-paste to online applications and networking.

The online aspect of resume submission brought with it a whole new set of challenges. Resumes seemed to disappear into cyberspace. Professional candidates discovered that they had to get involved in social and business networking platforms. Yet much remained the same, at its core, namely, the need to distinguish the candidate from the "herd": now, most appropriately, it's called "Branding."

That's precisely what I do, and where I get involved: writing resumes rich with keywords, and developing a professional brand unique to the serious candidate, coordinated across the spectrum of social media sites.

My other vocation naturally dovetailed from my resume business: Web content development and SEO strategy coupled with conversions/sales copywriting.

It is a rare qualification I possess, to be a gifted writer, as well as an SEO & Conversions savvy website auditor. I've been fortunate, in that my journalism background provided me with a relatively smooth transition from traditional print media to online copywriting.

It will always be the great fact that content is king, and I excel in content...the rest falls into place.

I am continually honing and refining my professional skills through workshops, webinars, and teleconferences from a variety of industry experts in the fields of SEO/Sales copywriting, website optimization, and resume writing. I bring the most recent and relevant skills to any project, as well as years' of "real world" experience.
