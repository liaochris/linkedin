  • Chief Technology Officer

    City of Bee Cave

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2007 — August 2009 (2 years 4 months)

    As CTO, I was responsible for aligning technology vision with business strategy by integrating government processes with the appropriate technologies. Responsible for all aspects of developing and implementing technology initiatives within the organization, including project management tasks. Designed and developed a new data center, supervised and maintained existing enterprise systems, while providing direction in all technology related issues in support of city operations, which included the Police Department, Public Library, Administration, Community Services, and elected officials.

    I believe that excellence is only limited by opportunity, and that innovation should be the goal of government. As a result, we were the first organization, public or private, in the Austin metro area to successfully integrate an open source Voice-Over-IP solution with Microsoft Exchange 2007 and Unified Messaging. Also, designed and successfully deployed a microwave backhaul solution between municipal buildings that eliminated the need for leased lines entirely. The entire project was done in-house, including tower mounts, fiber termination, low voltage systems, alignment, and radio configurations.
