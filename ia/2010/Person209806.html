Nishika Jones’s Summary

Many people have had bad experiences working with real estate agents and simply don't trust us. Urban Living Realty is a residential real estate brokerage that makes every effort to make the experience of working with a real estate professional an enjoyable one. Believe it or not, it is possible.

We do simple things like call you when we say we will and promptly return emails and messages.

Since 2003 we have had the honor of working with many individuals and families that we proudly continue to serve.

Nishika Jones’s Specialties:

Sellers depend on our flat fee real estate services for an affordable alternative.

HUD homes and specifically the Teacher & Officer Next Door program are the basis of our referral business. Other foreclosures such as Short Sales or even VA homes are additional specialties for buyers.
