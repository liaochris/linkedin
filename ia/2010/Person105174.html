Jose Mallabo’s Summary

Nineteen years of public relations and investor relations experience -- working globally and domestically -- in high-tech industry working with emerging and Fortune 500 companies like Kodak, Xerox, H-P, eBay and LinkedIn. Former clients include: FedEx, PreviewTravel, Sony, Spectrian, LivePerson, SDL, Navigant International, AEHR Test Systems, Socket Communications, ISACA to name a few. Served as lead IR account manager for ~15 publicly traded companies.

My experience includes external/internal/executive communications work in the consumer and enterprise technology markets; and non-technology industries such as food, hospitality, travel and steel.

I am currently establishing LinkedIn's international PR programs, agency partners and organizational design for expansion in Asia Pacific and Europe and have recently launched operations in India, Australia, Netherlands, Ireland and Canada. I also oversee PR strategy for 6 markets in Europe.

Prior to that, I served as corporate communications director at eBay Inc. and led global corporate and financial communications including support for global M&A activities as well as public affairs. I also led PR / brand and employee communications for eBay Classifieds Group globally; managing 15 direct reports.

In addition to my PR skills, I have expertise in developing research and measurement metrics for communications programs. No matter what the industry or the PR program the impact of a communications program can be measured and tied to business objectives and operations. It's not rocket science. It's social science.

My experience also includes founding and running a web services start-up during the go-go dot.com days. Nothing quite gives you a guerilla marketing edge like pitching a VC from a pay phone, selling your car and liquidating your 401(k).

Organizational design only matter if its led by people with talent, vision and the guts to execute.
