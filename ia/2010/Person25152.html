Bill Babcock’s Summary

Billb@bnj.com 503-748-3344

I've backed away from the day-to-day operation of Babcock & Jenkins. The company immediately started doing extremely well--which is testimony to my management and leadership skills (huh?).

I'm still writing the occasional article for the B&J blog, and I'm diving deep into web 2.0 technologies to aid the company in future vision. But the team running B&J is the best that we've ever had, and I'm enormously proud of every one of them.

In the meantime I'm playing very hard. I live three or four months of the year in Maui where I'm perfecting my windsurfing, surfing, stand-up paddle surfing, diving, sailing, and fishing skills. Which will all help greatly in my future career as a beach bum. You can read about it in my blog www.ponohouse.com/ponoblog .

I'm also preparing to undertake an odyssey: a four month, 15,000 mile road trip to all the classic road racing tracks in North America, racing my vintage car--Peyote. I customized an airstream trailer to do the hauling. Built it myself (not a great idea). You can read about that in www.allaluminumtour.com.

All the best. If you're ever in Maui drop by for a Mai Tai, or intercept me on the All Aluminum tour. I'll be the guy hauling the 34 foot Airstream. In the meantime, enjoy. Life is not a rehearsal.
