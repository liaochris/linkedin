Mike Salerno’s Summary

Greetings! I am a video producer and editor with more than five years experience in all phases of video production.

Working in video has given me the opportunity to hone my technical and creative skill set across a myriad of disciplines; advertising, marketing, film, public relations, corporate communications, cable TV and pharmaceuticals.

My work can be seen across a variety of different mediums including broadcast spots, interactive web campaigns and traditional DVD's.

Regardless of what industry I am working in, a commitment to exceeding client expectations and delivering high-end production values is always at the forefront of my work. I've been able to execute creative editorial solutions for the following fortune-500 companies; AT&T, Georgia Pacific, JVC and USSA, Ortho-McNeil

I've also found that creativity continually flourishes by staying motivated and inspired outside of the office. I've found travel to be my most rewarding hobby to date. Other hobbies include; bouldering, skiing, scuba diving, photography, reading and writing music.

    (Media Production industry)

    Funding the film completely out of my own pocket, "Plasterhead" was my first ambitious undertaking which proved I could see a project through from development to distribution.
