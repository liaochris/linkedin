Gail Perry CFRE’s Summary

Author, Fired Up Fundraising: Turn Board Passion into Action. (Wiley and AFP Fund Development Series). Creator, Easy Fundraising for Board Members Retreat. Popular international speaker on how to turn board members from fear to enthusiasm about fundraising, led own consulting firm for over 14 years, helped nonprofits raise over $200 million.

Goals: Write another book or two. Create techniques into a model that can be easily diseminated and licensed. Have fun while helping to change the world!
