Christopher Valenti’s Summary

Mr. Valenti is currently a Registered Principal for Starlight Investments, LLC (www.starlightinvestments.com), an investment bank (FINRA/SiPC), which is focused on private placements of equity securities for private and public companies. Starlight has approximately ten investment professionals with offices in various U.S. cities. Mr. Valenti received his B.A. in English Literature and his M.S. in Civil Engineering with honors from the University of Colorado at Boulder and holds the following securities licenses: 7, 63 and 24. Mr. Valenti resides in Denver, Colorado and formerly worked as an Engineering Consultant for a private engineering firm in Denver.
