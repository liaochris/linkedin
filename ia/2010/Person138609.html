Max Goldberg’s Summary

I've been actively engaged in professional comedy for eleven years, focusing primarily in web video and live stand-up. My original web content has garnered over six million views.

In a freelance capacity, I assist companies and individuals create and market content online, with a special expertise in web video and social media. I've served in Creative, Editorial, and Marketing for a range of comedy content websites, including Break.com, Comedy.com, HolyTaco.com, ScreenJunkies.com, MadeMan.com, and Chickipedia.com.

Additionally, my extensive list of live performances have included national festivals, universities, and major comedy clubs around the world.
