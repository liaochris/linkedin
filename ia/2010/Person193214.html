Stacy Shaw’s Summary

I started Urban Networks in 1991 because I realized that small and medium businesses were being under-served in terms of quality IT solutions. So many companies were coming to me frustrated by unreliable networks and software that kept them from performing at peak efficiency. Since that time, my goal through Urban Networks has remained the same- to deliver reliable network and accounting platforms that are tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

We specialize in solutions for Retail, eCommerce, Distribution and Manufacturing businesses as well as Non-Profit Organizations. We help our clients implement strategies and corporate culture commonly discussed in The Goal.

By devoting our services to a customer-first operating philosophy, Urban Networks has grown throughout the Pacific Northwest. Our clients reach from California and Oregon through Washington and the Idaho, and our non-traditional business model for network management has sparked attention.
