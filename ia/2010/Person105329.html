Joseph Einav’s Summary

As one of the newest members to the Sales and Leasing team at Platinum Properties, Joseph brings energy and fresh excitement to Platinum . Before working at Platinum, Joseph worked at an Upper East Side real estate firm where he gained experience in many different areas of Manhattan, and was able to establish relationships with some of the East Side’s best landlords. Wherever he works, Joseph prides himself on treating clients as if he was moving into the apartment himself and always tries to get them the best price possible. Having recently graduated Magna Cum Laude from Syracuse University, Joseph has plans to pursue his Juris Doctorate at the Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law. Now a resident of New York’s downtown neighborhood , he is familiar with all Manhattan has to offer. For now, Joe is focusing on providing the best service to his clients' real estate needs, whatever they may be.
