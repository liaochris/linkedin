Michael Shmarak’s Summary

I am a senior-level professional whose expertise spans several industries and acumens within the public relations world.

I founded Sidney Maxwell Public Relations in August 2003 looking for ways to do these things "differently" while creating optimal client value. Thankfully, it has been a great ride, as the models we have in place are opening the eyes of people who have been in the industry for some time.

I have always said that the best part of PR is not finding what is on a client's desk, but inside of a client's mind. Thus, I strive to seek out the drivers within a business that can be showcased as often (and in front of as many influential audiences) as possible. I would be glad to share any references or additional insights on my background.

Michael Shmarak’s Specialties:

Clients have knocked on my door to assist them with media relations, executive positioning, strategic writing, issues and adversity management. But at the end of the day, my expertise comes from putting my clients at the center of my professional universe.
