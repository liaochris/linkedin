Chris 'Ruff Diamond' Robinson’s Summary

'THE DIAMOND OF PRODUCERS'

Chris aka 'Ruff Diamond' is a producer who has contributed to millions of record sales worldwide.

Originating from the UK, a talented guitarist from an early age, he became a successful session musician recording and performing live for a number of major & indie artists.

Having great natural ability,feel and the vision to create, arrange and produce music, plus a calm, assured, amiable and witty character with excellent people skills, he decided to pursue his career as a producer and songwriter, the studio being the natural enviroment for where his creative genius could be realised to it's full potential.

Chris is now based between London & Los Angeles, working with major & independent Recording Artists, world class songwriters, and also TV Networks / Production companies making music for Synch/Film.
