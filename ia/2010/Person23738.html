Bill Emerson’s Summary

Over 30 years of executive experience in retailing. His experiences have included P&L responsibility as well as senior leadership roles in merchandising, planning and allocation, IT systems, and store operations. Experience in a broad variety of retail formats including department stores, off-price, category killers, and specialty stores with annual volumes ranging from $2million to $13billion. Advises boards of directors, is a frequent guest speaker, and consults.

Bill Emerson’s Specialties:

Merchandise management, Organizational design, Localization, Operational improvement.
