Modele Oyewole’s Summary

DC to BC, LLC started in 2007 as a twice-weekly radio show with my friend Quinn Coleman and I (we went to high school in DC together and ended up at Boston College, thus the name.). We've made some respectable leaps and bounds as it evolved from a low-budget radio show to a makeshift blog. We were fortunate enough to land KiD CuDi’s first ever radio ever interview in ’07. In ’08, we brought you creative content such as Shy Guy Chronicles, and were crowned the Robin Hoods of the internet for providing those without HBO subscriptions with HD-quality Entourage episodes. At the year’s close, we were blessed with the Black Weblog Award for “Blog To Watch”. In ‘09, Roc Nation signee J. Cole shouted us out in his Complex interview in November, and XXL called us “one of the 100 Best Hip-Hop Web Sites” in their December / January issue.

In 2010, DC to BC coordinated some of the DC metropolitan area’s most talked about branded events, including a community service / field day for adults, or "Child's Play", and a Belvedere sponsored birthday bash for myself known as "The Kobe". Our concepts for events are unique and fun, and have been incredibly successful thus far. We've got the videos to prove it, too:

The Gee Pee Cookout & Kick Back (May 31st, 2010): http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t3pRm0JSShY (Attendance: 400+)

Body Beat at The Donovan Hotel (July 18th, 2010): http://vimeo.com/13445285 (250+)

Greenwood's Gathering (End of August, 2010): http://vimeo.com/14048167 (150+)

Child's Play (An Adult Field Day. Sept. 11th, 2010) http://dctobc.com/2010/09/the-ultimate-childs-play-recap/ (150+)

The Kobe (My 24th B-day; Sept. 18th, 2010) http://dctobc.com/2010/09/the-kobe-recap/ (300+)

DC to BC has recently expanded to include a boutique digital branding / social media marketing agency, as well as The Great Progression, the next step of the DC to BC movement (coming soon!). We appreciate your support greatly.
