  • Business Development Manager

    New Directions

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    April 2007 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    In 2003, a team of industry professionals came together to discuss a new business venture. Over the previous decades they had partnered together in both small to large start-ups in the Information Technology Consulting and Staffing Industries.

    As they met, one thing continued to nag at them in regards to evaluating both professional services and IT staffing firms. They agreed that both industry segments were comprised of IT Staffing players who were “jacks of all trades and masters of none” and professional services firms (the final four and alike) that almost always over promised and under delivered.

    We decided that New Directions goal was to be different. We would leave the jacks behind and be a specialist in our field and would never … over promise and under deliver.

    Since our founding in 2003, New Directions has been recognized as a specialist in our field. We are a woman-owned professional services firm that provides its clients with consulting; project staff augmentation and traditional hire services for: SAP; BI and Analytical Reporting; Data Warehousing & ETL Development; Infrastructure; and Project Management.

    Since our inception, our success and subsequent growth has been based on leveraging our clients’ enterprise software or business intelligence efforts with talented people. Whether our client is seeking a tool, module or business process related consultant for a short or long period of time or are challenged with the efforts of identifying full time direct employee resources, New Directions is continually recognized as a trusted subject matter expert.

    With that said, nearly 80% of our business is with repeat clients. They tell us that our specialization; our attention to detail and the ease in working with us are some of the many factors they evaluate in working with us. Oh, one more thing, we’re told that we’re fun to work with.

    If you would like additional information, please reach out to us at ckerwick@nddatagroup.com.
