Lisa Marie Dias’s Summary

LisaMarie Dias, owner of LisaMarie Dias Designs, has had a wide range of artistic, business and teaching experience.

She works with business owners to create effective online marketing campaigns offering support with strategy, implementation and maintenance in addition to specializing in E-newsletter start-up on Constant Contact

If you have an online marketing campaign, she can help you make it more attractive, efficient and effective. If you want to create an online presence for your business, she can help you get started!

She also offers webinars and one-on-one sessions demystifying Social Media and helping people create effective strategies for using Linked In, Twitter, FaceBook, free directories and other online tools to grow their businesses.

You can learn more about LisaMarie and the services she offers by visiting her website at www.LisaMarieDiasDesigns.com, follow her on Twitter, @LisaMarieDias and reach her via email at LMD@LisaMarieDiasDesigns.com

LisaMarie is a Constant Contact Business Partner and Certified Constant Contact Trainer. She publishes a monthly e-newsletter to support those of you that are maintaining your own online presence. It is filled with helpful tips and suggestions to make your online publishing efforts more effective. Visit the website www.LisaMarieDiasDesigns.com Home Page to join her mailing list and the calendar page to see a list of her upcoming classes and speaking engagements.

Originally trained and licensed as an architect, she worked at SOM NY as a technical architect for 15 years before launching her own jewelry design and fabrication business in 2002. There, she designed, created and sold unique handcrafted jewelry for women that enjoyed celebrating their own unique sense of style. In 2006, LisaMarie Dias Designs was honored by the Hispanic American Chamber of Commerce of Essex County and received the Businesswoman of the Year Award.

Lisa Marie Dias’s Specialties:

Creating effective, affordable online marketing materials with a specialty in e-newsletter start-up using Constant Contact.

Also offering strategizing services to tie together all aspects of your online marketing campaign including your online social media presence.

LisaMarie is a Constant Contact Business Partner and Certified Trainer - please visit our site www.LisaMarieDiasDesigns.com to learn more about how she can help you optimize your e-newsletter and your online presence!
