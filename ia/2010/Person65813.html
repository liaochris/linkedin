Eric Ewald’s Summary

Eric Paul Ewald - MBA, CAE

Eric Ewald serves as an Account Executive with Ewald Consulting, an association management company. Ewald Consulting is based in Saint Paul, MN. Eric has been the firm since 1994. The firm is accredited by the Association Management Company Institute and the American Society of Association Executives. Ewald is now Vice President-Consulting and part owner of the firm.

Ewald earned his BA in Political Science at the University of Minnesota and his MBA from the Carlson School of Management where he was a double major in entrepreneurship and strategic management. He also completed a comprehensive program in non-profit management at the University of Saint Thomas.

Ewald earned his Certified Association Executive (CAE) designation in February of 1999. The CAE is a designation awarded by the American Society of Association Executives recognizing competence and achievement in the field of association management.

Eric Ewald’s Specialties:

Board leadership development, strategic planning, budgeting, government relations, marketing.
