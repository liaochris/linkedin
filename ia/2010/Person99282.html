Jeff Lawler’s Summary

Focus on the restoration of overgrown landscapes in older neighborhoods and residences, either to original design or to thoughtfully integrate new uses on the site.

Experience in landscape design/build. Particularly in areas of deck design, perennial garden beds, rainwater gardens, and outdoor kitchen areas.

In the immediate future, professional goal is to expand on academic experiences into the areas of therapeutic garden design and golf course clubhouse programming in addition to historic property design work.

Jeff Lawler’s Specialties:

Design solutions for historic landscapes and small to mid size private gathering spaces.
