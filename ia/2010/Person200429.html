Tom Miller’s Summary

I have 22 years of healthcare experience and am currently the Director of Quality and Regulatory Affairs for SightLife, which is one of the leading eye banks in the nation. I started there in October of 2000 and am responsible for the day-to-day quality assurance operations. I also hold the position of Director of Regulatory Affairs and Contracts with Vision Share, Vision Share is a not for profit consortium of nineteen eye banks working together to provide quality eye tissue and superior eye banking services. I have a Bachelor of Science is Business Administration from Columbia College, and am a current member and past chair of the EBAA Quality Assurance Task Force, current Vice-Chair of the Technician Education Committee and member of the Legislative and Regulatory Affairs Committee. I have also served on the education committee for the Washington State Association for Healthcare Quality (WSAHQ).
