Steven A. Carpenter’s Experience

  • Founder and CEO

    Cake Financial Corporation

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    March 2006 — January 2010 (3 years 11 months)

    Founder and CEO of venture-backed consumer Internet company that helped mainstream individual investors effortlessly manage their brokerage and retirement accounts. Cake was the first financial services company that allowed investors to see other's real trading activity in real-time, to view all of their accounts across brokerage firms in one place, and to get personalized and objective recommendations. Cake was widely and positively covered in the media by Walt Mossberg at the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Forbes, BusinessWeek, US News, USAToday, TechCrunch, to name a few. I sold the company to E*Trade Financial in January, 2010.
