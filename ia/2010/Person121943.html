Laura Melbourne’s Summary

I am a user experience designer with 14 years of industry experience designing corporate and consumer websites and web applications. In addition to being a creative thinker and a pretty darn good designer, I have extensive expertise in graphic design, design management, user interface design, usability, information architecture, and prototype development. I am a tireless advocate for a better user experience and an ardent protector against all things bad in design today.

An Informal Summary:

Laura is approximately 403.5 pica in height and has an affinity for fruity desserts, Dada and anything indie. She had early aspirations of being a teacher, nurse, psychologist, biologist, scientific illustrator, painter, printmaker, cartographer and Indian chief, then found she could be a little bit of all of these by focusing her efforts toward user experience design.

Even as a small child Laura's favorite word was "why?". While older and much taller these days, her passion for learning remains firmly intact, though the answers she finds are not nearly as absolute.

When Laura is not creatively solving problems at work, she loves to enjoy a nice glass of Vognier or a lovely Meritage blend, all of which tend to make her dream in colorful photoshop layers.
