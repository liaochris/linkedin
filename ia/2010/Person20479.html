Barun Kumar’s Summary

Barun Kumar is the founder and a Director with MANTRAN Consulting Pte Ltd. Before founding MANTRAN, Barun was an Associate Director with IT Advisory practice of KPMG LLP in Singapore. Barun has previously worked as an Assistant Vice President (AVP) with Technology Risk Services (TRS) practice of EXL Service, a NASDAQ listed US Corporation and as Manager with IT Advisory practice of KPMG in India.

Barun is an engineer, MBA and Certified Information Security Auditor (CISA). Barun is also an Approva Certified Professional (ACP).

Barun has written various newspaper articles, which have been published in leading newspapers in India (such as The Economic Times and Business Line).

Barun has approximately 9 years (including more than 8 years at KPMG) of experience in information security and SAP consulting services.

Barun has experience in serving clients globally across diverse industry segments. Barun has worked in various countries across Asia (Dubai, Hong Kong, India, Qatar, Singapore and Thailand), Africa (South Africa), Europe (Belgium, France, Switzerland and UK) and North America (US).

Barun has executed and managed projects in various areas with primary focus on SAP security, and Information Security governance, risk and compliance. Some of his large projects are as follows:
1. Post implementation review of SAP systems across five countries (South Africa, Switzerland, France, Belgium and UK)
2. Post upgrade review of SAP system including configuration controls, user authorisations, and segregation of duties conflicts
3. Develop a BS7799 compliant information security policy and IT and network security controls review
4. SOX IT documentation and management testing assistance
5. Internal and external network penetration tests and review of the IT systems, including operating systems, routers, switches and firewalls across five countries (Dubai, Hong Kong, India, Qatar and Singapore)

Barun Kumar’s Specialties:

Barun has executed and managed projects primarily in the following areas:
• SAP consulting - security, business process, data migration and adminstration controls (with specific focus on user authorization design and audit, pre and post implementation reviews and Segregation of Duties)
• IT and network audits
• IT governance (including SOX, SAS70, J-SOX, MAS 634 compliance)
• Penetration testing
• Information security policies and procedures development
• SAP security trainings
