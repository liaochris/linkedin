Jeffrey Hartmann’s Summary

I am available, able and eager to return to work. There is always something I can do.

For the past ten years I have been developing websites. I know a variety of web-coding languages and I am capable of learning many more. I have experience with a variety of databases. Beyond that I appreciate the needs and wants of website users which often transcend the ever-changing technologies behind websites.

Regardless, my next job does not have to be in website development. My background is in media studies or, if you prefer, communications. I studied film, journalism, television and creative and critical writing in college with a goal toward a career in media. Throughout my changing career, I have maintained an interest in classic and cult movies.

My first priority is a permanent, full-time position. However, I am also open to long-term (weeks/months) contract-to-hire work. Although Chicago is the only home I ever knew, the ideal permanent, full-time situation may compel me to relocate anywhere within the United States.

My personal website, www.jephmann.com, is simply a workshop, of sorts. Here I test ideas, resolve coding issues, improve my skills, and otherwise stay in practice. A modest site, www.jephmann.com leads with a few of my strengths, including my ability to present database queries online. You can see an example of this at http://www.jephmann.com/MyDVD/About.aspx

In 2010 I look forward to networking with positive, engaging people who can show me how networking is truly done.

By the way: I am an "open networker" in the sense that we all begin as strangers; that said, I am not a "LION".

I am grateful for your time and I thank you for finding me. So where may I find you?
