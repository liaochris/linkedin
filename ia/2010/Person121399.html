Larry Walters’s Experience

  • Partner

    Your Wellness Partners LLC

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Your Wellness Partners provides turnkey, value-added Wellness and Health Care benefit programs to affinity groups, professional associations, insurance companies and their policyholders, reverse mortgage markets, employee assistance programs, and for direct purchase by consumers using a variety of direct response techniques.

  • Founder and Owner

    Diversified Growth Solutions, LLC

    (Insurance industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Diversified Growth Solutions, LLC is a marketing company that specializes in insurance-related programs.

    Larry Walters is a consulting actuary with over 30 years of diverse insurance and reinsurance experience in both the life/health and property/casualty industries. In addition to holding technical actuarial positions of increasing responsibility with insurance companies earlier in his career, Larry has more recently been responsible for sales and marketing activities in a variety of company environments, including Investor Relations Officer for three large public insurance companies, head of the domestic life insurance division for a large life insurer, a life reinsurance broker at two leading reinsurance brokers and as head of sales and insurance services for Secured Independence, a health risk management and wellness services provider.
