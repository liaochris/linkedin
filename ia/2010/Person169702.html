Ray Nugent Jr.’s Summary

A senior accredited Personal Property appraiser specializing in books, documents & manuscripts, and antique maps, with the American Society of Appraisers (ASA)., with a concentration in the appraisal of larger personal and professional collections of documents, photographs, manuscripts, correspondence, artistic works, and historical memorabilia, for donation, resale, settlement, distribution, and insurance. Client list includes universities, museums, libraries, government agencies, lenders, insurers, wealth managers, and litigators. A principal of Nugent Appraisal LLC, a general personal property appraisal firm founded in 1976, with multiple appraisers specializing in all areas of residential and business contents.

Ray Nugent Jr.’s Specialties:

Member, American Institute of Conservation, with specialization in all forms of paper, leather, and textiles. Historical analysis of significant documents, people and events with an understanding of their relevance to important perspectives in world history. Expert witness in litigation involving government and private sector.
