Todd Dewett’s Summary

Dr. Dewett has dedicated his career to developing and inspiring people in organizations. He has spoken to, consulted with or trained professionals all over the United States and beyond. Dr. Dewett combines a massive dose of energy and world class knowledge to deliver a message that brings leadership to life. Through his speaking, training, writing, and consulting he inspires leaders to new levels of success. Dr. Dewett’s credentials include a BBA from the University of Memphis, an MBA from the University of Tennessee, and a PhD in Management from Texas A&M University. He has worked with Fortune 500 firms and startups, product and service companies, private companies and government agencies, in maquiladoras and executive suites. Dr. Dewett has been quoted in the New York Times, CNN, BusinessWeek, Chicago Tribune, MSNBC and many other outlets. Before going it alone Dr. Dewett enjoyed stints at Andersen Consulting as well as Ernst & Young. Currently, he is settled in Dayton, Ohio where he continues his quest to improve our capacity to lead.

Contact Dr. Dewett at todd@drdewett.com.

Todd Dewett’s Specialties:

Management and leadership skills, managing for creativity and innovation, leading change, employee motivation and productivity, positive conflict, effective communication.
