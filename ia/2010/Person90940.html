Jason Scales’s Summary

A combination of 17 years experience encompassing U.S. Military, Government Contractor, Department of Defense, and the Commercial industry in managing and performing design, integration, engineering, Configuration Management, technical support and maintenance for Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (C4ISR) Military Systems, RADAR, and Exterior Communications (EXCOMMS). Performed Systems Evaluation, Requirements Analysis, Technical Studies, Field Engineering, and Project Management for programs to include: multiple Local Area Network (LAN) Installations onboard U.S. and USN Ships, Field Change Verification Program, INSURV Program, overhaul of various C4ISR Systems, Commercial Cellular Antenna sightings and installations, and the Government Test Equipment Standardization Program. Managed multiple single installations in excess of $275K and up to 15 Technicians, provided technical assistance and maintenance of Navy Systems including: MF/HF/VHF/UHF and SATCOM (Tactical Data Links) Systems, Secure Voice Systems, Global Positioning Systems, Tactical Air Navigation, Radar and IT Infrastructure Systems.
