Lauren Perkins’s Summary

Lauren Perkins is the founder and President of Perks Consulting and an experienced marketing and strategy expert within health/fitness and spa/beauty. Lauren’s instinctive skills for strategy, branding, marketing, and management were cultivated by her work at Nike, The Boston Globe, and Crunch Fitness. As the former Director of Marketing at Laser Cosmetica, where she drove growth with marketing, branding, and infrastructure development, Lauren leveraged her experience within the beauty and fitness industry to Perks Consulting, while creating synergy of all available marketing channels and social media platforms. As a marketing strategist at Perks, clients are provided with innovative and cost-effective solutions. With a strong focus on emerging trends and media platforms, she uses every tool necessary in a creative fashion to communicate the brand message of her clients.

Perks Consulting is an outsourced marketing department for emerging and growing brands in the lifestyle sector; health, fitness, spa, beauty, green, medical industries. As a marketing innovator, Perks Consulting understands how to boost your bottom line by integrating the most compelling traditional and online marketing strategies, whether you’re starting a small business, creating a new brand extension, or looking to grow your product and/or services. Perks leverages the advantages of emerging media and utilize traditional techniques. Our goal is to ensure synergy between all of your marketing efforts to enhance your brand, engage your audience and impact your sales.
