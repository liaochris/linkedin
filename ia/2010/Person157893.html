Matt Bixby’s Summary

I started my company (then called Willamette Valley DJ) in July of 2001. My intent was to start a DJ business that would focus on helping brides and grooms have the wedding of their dreams. After a few years, and a lot of success I truly realized that my role was substantially more than just a DJ playing music. I was the couple's master-of-ceremonies, I was their creative planner, and in many cases I was also their wedding coordinator. I began to specialize in working with clients who saw a big need for someone to provide "more than just music" and who would help their day be personalized and headache-free. Later I added the game show to my company's repertoire as I sought to expand interest outside of the wedding season, and delve into the corporate market. The game shows I offer are completely customizable, so that content may be developed for any group or specific purpose. My most exciting event took place in October 2008, when I traveled nearly 2000 miles to deliver a game show for a corporate client in Chicago, IL. The trip was a huge success. 2009 promises to be my best year yet.
