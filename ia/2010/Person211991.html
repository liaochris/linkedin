Vinaykumar Mummigatti’s Summary

Business Leader with 18+ years of experience in Global IT consulting and Consumer industries. Career marked by achievements in setting up new business initiatives, Strategic alliances, building world class consulting practices and innovative business models. Deep passion for innovation, highly entrepreneurial, risk taking and relationship oriented. Significant Contributions made to technology strategy in enterprise solutions space and Business process management. Strong exposure to Financial services, Insurance, Healthcare, Telco and manufacturing verticals.

Currently handling the role of VP and global head of BPM practice at Virtusa, a global IT Consulting firm. In his current role he leads a global team of 600+ BPM consultants and is responsible for BPM strategy, alliances, consulting and delivery. He has contributed significantly to BPM Center of Excellence and BPM on Cloud initiatives. He is passionate about the role of BPM in Business Transformation and Customer Experience Management. He has led various IP and non-IP based BPM frameworks to help clients accelerate their BPM implementations and achieve measurable performance improvements.
Vinaykumar has more than 18 years experience in the IT Industry, handling leadership roles in Enterprise solutions, Business consulting, Global delivery, Strategic alliances and business operations. Vinay has worked with the leading BPM, ECM, Portal, EAI/SOA and CRM technologies over the last 10 years. He has worked with multiple Fortune 500 clients in helping them define roadmap for enterprise solutions and successfully implementing strategic programs. He contributes regularly to his blog on www.BPM-experiences.com.
He has presented at multiple conferences and authored white papers which are published in BPM handbooks and online media. He holds a Bachelors degree in Electronics Engineering and MBA in Finance and Marketing. He has worked previously with Mahindra Satyam and IBM.
