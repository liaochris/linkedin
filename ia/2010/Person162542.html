Peter Rodgers’s Summary

Peter A. Rodgers
Owner/Director, Montara Creative, mcreative.com, since 1993

Peter Rodgers is an accomplished artist and brand expert with a keen eye for original creative and business strategy. He’s been active for more than 25 years in art, music, design, education and business development. The identity and integrated marketing agency he founded, Montara Creative, produces campaigns that combine the art of design with the priorities of business.

Rodgers has helped bring to life brands and products for Hanson Bridgett, Dun and Bradstreet, Digital Fountain, E-LOAN, NextCard, Dolby Labs, Zoom, Cattlemens Restaurants, Cox & Kings, San Francisco Art Institute, LiveCapital, Apple Computer, SONOMA Magazine, Primedia, Macromedia, eLocal16, Pantone, E*TRADE Financial, Seybold Seminars, Frank Howard Allen Realtors, Rabin Worldwide, Costello & Sons, Shutterfly, Collins & Company, Houlihan Lawrence and McGuire Real Estate just to name a few. Peter and his Montara Creative team continue to develop and execute award-winning brand, advertising and marketing campaigns for travel, hospitality, business and technology, arts and music industry clients.

In addition to his work, Peter volunteers time and resources to multiple local San Francisco Bay Area organizations including; The Buck Institute, Bread and Roses, The Center for Volunteer and Nonprofit Leadership/The Marin Human Race, Youth in Arts/The Italian Street Painting Festival, Pets Lifeline and is a founding member of the Sonoma County Mariachi Festival Organizing Committee. Peter has also served on industry advisory boards including The Realty Alliance Conference/Marketing Task Force and CVNL Business Volunteer Council. Featured speaker engagements include RealTrends Gathering of Eagles and Leading Real Estate Companies of the World™/Luxury Portfolio™ annual conferences. Peter serves as Youth in Arts Board of Directors President and co-chair of the Marketing Committee.
