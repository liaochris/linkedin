Todd Ebert’s Summary

As Vice President of Marketing at ReachLocal, Todd leads a team responsible for developing and marketing the ReachLocal brand globally. A seasoned B2B veteran, Todd brings over 17 years of experience in brand strategy, market research, new product development, product marketing, and marketing communications at technology startups and Fortune 100 enterprises. Prior to joining ReachLocal, Todd was Vice President of Marketing at Entrust, a publicly-traded security software company. Before Entrust, Todd led marketing efforts for an Internet startup, managed Internet products in the small business division of Sprint, and held global product marketing roles at Pfizer and 3M. Todd earned an MBA from The McCombs School at the University of Texas at Austin and a B.S. in Business & Economics from Lehigh University.
