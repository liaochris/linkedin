Bernd Kestler’s Summary

I am an independent consultant and trainer dedicated to help individuals and businesses in designing and/or developing skills and strategies for document management and knowledge acquisition to produce high-quality and consistent documentation.

► Performing document inventory/ analysis;
► Information architecture/structure design;
► Re-writing or re-structuring of documents;
► Semantic information structuring;
► User training for data models and XML authoring tools;
► Keen interest and knowledgeable in Human Factor (HF) in document design;
► Developing style-guides;
► Experienced in using Moodle (VLE) for e-learning.

I provide hands-on support and training to flight documentation & publication departments of commercial airlines in re-structuring and re-editing flight operation documentations.
Was granted permission by the FAA, to incorporating their Human Factor Design Standards (HFDS) into my trainings.

Basically, I make sense out of a pile of garbage content, so it can be understood, trained and reused.
