Massimiliano Cremonini’s Summary

I've been working in the digital world for more 11 years.

I've taken responsibility of risky and profitable web, mobile and digital television projects in both international and local companies.
Among my clients are: Blockbuster, Esselunga, Fastweb, Poste Italiane, Volkswagen group, Telecom Italia, BNL, Regione Liguria, Comune di Milano, La7, MTV, Nestlé, Esso, Adobe, Alfa Romeo, Ikea, Whirlpool and Hansenet.

I've also worked on DTT/MHP technologies, developing a multiplatform data center for a major Italian Broadcaster and managing digital TV MHP projects for LA7 and MTV(Telecom Italia Media), releasing the very first italian MHP applications.

Before joining RCS publishing company as resposible for digital special initiatives, I have worked in an international advertising company (DDB - OMNICOM GROUP) to develop digital strategies for prospect and clients.

I have now come back to the agency side joining Publicis Groupe to help them build solid and profitable relationships with their clients creating for them the best digital strategies.
Currently operating 2-3 days a week in Germany I am based in Milan, Italy.

I'm always willing to face new challenges and exploit my knowledge to give projects and clients the best solution to influence the online conversation.
