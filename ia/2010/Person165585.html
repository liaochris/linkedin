PRANDI Property Management, Inc., CRMC’s Summary

PRANDI Property Management, Inc., CRMC is centrally located in Marin County and locally owned. Established in 1978, we serve a growing need for a professional property management company that specializes in residential properties only.

We provide unlimited services to investment property owners. Our strengths are in real estate management, leasing, contract administration and accounting. PRANDI Property Management delivers prompt and reliable service to owners and other professionals who serve owners (e.g. accountants, attorneys, auditors, brokers, contractors, vendors, et.al.) to maximize property owner’s investment profits.

- Locally owned and operated by a Marin County native
- 27 years of specialized experience
- Personalized full-service residential property management
- Professional Financial Management and reporting
- Advise/inform competitive rental/lease prices and rates
- Friendly efficient full-time office staff
- Year end summary
