Gary Brown Internet Marketing NH ME MA’s Summary

Thanks for visiting my LinkedIn profile today. My companies have helped hundreds of clients find their way on the internet. I can trace my roots in internet marketing back to 1979 when I began work at Wang Laboratories. First building and testing computers then selling their office information and word processing systems to mid size business. As a regional sales manager for a national sportswear and apparel supplier, I was introduced to what would become the e-commerce industry that we know today. EDI was a fledgling electronic inventory and re-order system back then in 1992. It became the testing ground for online sale practices and theories currently used today in e-commerce applications. These experiences led me to open my first internet marketing company in 1996. At first I only developed for myself, building e-commerce marketing collateral and uniform sites for national franchise operations. Supplying all their branding and promotional products needs in a convenient on-site store. Hands on trial and error over the last 14 years along with intense continuing formal education for the past ten years, has made me and my companies the experts’ that we are today, in all aspects of the internet. Continuous dedication to the future of this communication revolution has generated more than 20 certificates in Web Design, SEO, SEM, PPC, Website Analytics, Content Writing, e-Commerce Development, Internet Marketing and Social Networking Skills. I am now well on my way to a graduate degree in the Internet Marketing field.

For the past ten years, I have been helping clients realize their potential on the World Wide Web, communicate effectively in the social networks, and lately visually communicate on the 2G, 3G and 4G networks.

It is a new and exciting future with bold unlimited possibilities, here for you now! Let me help you achieve your dreams, today.
