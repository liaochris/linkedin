Suzanne McDonald’s Summary

As Designated Editor’s Chief Content Strategist, Suzanne draws on 15 years' newspaper experience, most recently six years at The Boston Globe.

Since 2008, Suzanne's deep dives include Search Engine Optimization and Social Media, currently dipping into Usability, Mobile & Behavioral. Suzanne's philosophy – the user experience drives results – was recently echoed at OMMA Behavioral and at South by Southwest Interactive (SXSWi).

As 2010 progresses, social media practitioners, search marketers, and interactive media-makers can expect greater convergence: Search, Mobile, Local, Social, and innovations we're not yet aware of. For more on Suzanne's outlook: Check out her end-of-2009 post: http://blog.designatededitor.com/2009/12/29/peering-into-my-crystal-ball-what-does-2010-hold-for-search-marketing-seo-and-social-media.aspx.

CURRENT PROJECTS:

- Designing a social media community to support an iPhone app.

- Linking sponsorship offerings to an event's social media strategy utilizing: blogging, Facebook, landing pages, and off-line cross-promotional tools.

- Launching a strategic WordPress blog to complement an existing site but targeting a different segment of decision-makers.

CAREER MILESTONES:

- Tested and implemented numerous platforms to create Boston.com's interactive events.

- In-depth environmental and community reporter for award-winning Cape Cod newspapers.

- Editor-in-Chief of campus weekly newspaper, managed staff of 40 unpaid students.
