Erit McTernan’s Summary

My success as a Blue Cross General Agent came from my application of Leadership and Growth Management as a Blue Cross District Manager.

Over 20 years in both responsiblities proved to be my stepping stone as a leader in personal and business consulting.

In addition to my insurance career, I am in a position of success in Home Based Business. I assist people in making a change to success and financial independence. I'll explain a little about how I combined the two and how it improved my life.

But first, let me tell you a little about myself. I am the creator and CEO of The Millionaires in Process Club.

A few years ago I felt the need to help others who are looking on the Internet to make money, build a home based business and get the training and support they need to find their lifetime dream of financial indepedence.

My first venture into my own home-based business was in residential real estate, in the San Fernando Valley in So. Ca., which lasted a year because of the real estate interest rate bust years ago.

My next opportunity was with group medical insurance with Blue Cross, in Los Angeles. I immediately succeeded and went into management quickly.

As a district manager, with a $15 million dollar book of business and 15 sales and office staff when I took over. It was increased to $20 million three years later!

I immediately became a success by mass marketing life insurance policies using several proposals from many sources, which served as an alliance of insurance professionals - not just one insurance person.

Later I found CarbonCopy PRO and Wealth Masters International. For additional information, please visit my web site: www.theMillionairesInProcessClub.com.

It will explain positive steps to get started in your home-based business. Be sure to include in your library "Rich Dad, Poor Dad," written by Robert Kiyosaki and "The Law of Attraction" by Michael Losier.

See you on my videos at www.youtube.com/EritMcTernan.
