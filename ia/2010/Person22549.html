Bertrand Racine’s Summary

Over 15 years of product design experience including footwear for The North Face, consultant work as well as a decade designing sporting goods for Nike, inc. Forever interested in pursuing excellence in industrial design.

Bertrand Racine’s Specialties:

- Experienced in design project management
- Holds a very solid technical background.
- Skilled in 3D form and aesthetics, effective usability studies and problem solving, design concepts and collection presentations, and collaborating closely with diverse functions of a company as well as with external consultants.
- Sensible to cultural as well as functional issues when designing products.

Bertrand Racine’s Experience

  • Senior Designer

    Nike Golf

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; NKE; Sporting Goods industry)

    2004 — 2006 (2 years )

    - Design lead for golf footwear, and golf bags with improved performance at all price levels while maintaining exciting aesthetics, clear product differentiation and consumer relevance for North American and global markets.
    - Defined and designed over 25 different Athletic and Casual Golf footwear models, including all Nike Golf Shox models and men’s and women’s 2006 SP-5, the largest selling series of Nike Golf footwear.
    - Designed cushioning system for Golf Shox and Caged Zoom to specifically address golf performance needs.
    - Designed Asia specific collection of Golf footwear for Asian Fit. Also, designed entire line of cart bags for North American and regional markets that helped Nike regain its 20% market share of cart bag business.
    - Gained extensive experience in working with marketing, sales and development teams in many regions of the world.

  • Footwear Product Designer

    Nike

    (Public Company; NKE; Sporting Goods industry)

    2001 — 2004 (3 years )

    Nike Football (Soccer) Portland, Oregon.

    - Designed franchise products (Total 90 and Tiempo) for Euro Cup 2004 and for World Cup 2006 as well as complementary lifestyle Football footwear. Designed entire line of Tiempo products including Tiempo Legend, associated with football star Ronaldinho and other world-class athletes.
    - Individual designs helped grow share of Tiempo products from 8% to 38% of Nike Football footwear sales, representing over $300 million in sales annually for the following four years.
    - Incorporated extensive input from professional athletes, consumers, marketing, research and manufacturing.
    - Developed expertise in designing injection molded outsoles, rubber and foam tooling and on-field uppers with emphasis on improving performance while growing business.
    - Experience working with overseas teams in Taiwan, China and Italy simultaneously.

  • Industrial Designer

    Bauer Nike Hockey

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; nke; Sporting Goods industry)

    1996 — 2001 (5 years )

    - Worked within Nike and Bauer branding strategies to design Hockey Helmets and Face Protection, Ice and Roller Hockey Skates, In-line Skates, as well as Helmets and Protective Equipment for In-line Skaters.

  • Industrial Designer

    Consultant Firms

    (Design industry)

    1991 — 1996 (5 years )

    Projects range from pools to camera housings and from tableware to furniture.
