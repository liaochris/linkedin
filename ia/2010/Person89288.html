Janet Klinger’s Summary

Voted Best Photographer- Readers Choice 2008, Seattle Bride Magazine.

At Janet Klinger Photography our passion is to reflect the essence of your special day, whether celebrating a wedding, pregnancy, or fleeting family moments.

You deserve the luxury of excellent service and visionary photography, for your extraordinary life.

Janet Klinger’s Specialties:

From photographing your wedding, pregnancy, newborn through high school senior portraiture, Janet captures the full spectrum of priceless emotions by blending candid, photojournalistic moments with illustrative portraiture, for a spontaneous look and feel.
