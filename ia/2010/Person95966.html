Jeremy Dickamore’s Summary

Jeremy demonstrates the energy, enthusiasm and meticulous attention to detail that is vital to clients in today's ever-changing market. He understands the importance of listening to clients and their needs, takes the time to learn what is important to them, builds trust, and then proceeds in a professional manner, helping his clients reach their objectives.

Working with an experienced team of specialists at Commerce CRG Jeremy is backed by the most comprehensive information and research capabilities available. These resources provide up-to-date information on vacancies, absorption, and comparable sale and lease information - all to help the client make the best informed purchase, sale or lease decision possible. Jeremy is currently working on several large industrial parks in Utah ranging from 800 acres to 14,000 acres in size.

Prior to joining Commerce CRG Jeremy was the manager of a manufacturing company in Northern Utah. While working in this position he gained a tremendous understanding of the industrial arena. He understands the needs of clients in this industry and knows what it takes to put deals together. He has experience and knowledge of the construction process and how this can affect a new or expanding business in their real estate decisions.

Jeremy is currently working with several clients on relocation and expansion. He is also working to expand the use and development of renewable energy in the Utah area.
