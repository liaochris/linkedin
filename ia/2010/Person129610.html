David Macfarlan’s Summary

I am very interested in Mining and large earth moving projects throughout the world.

Over the years I have built up an extensive network of contacts that are end users of this class of machinery.

Goals:
Accurately cataloging sites and the equipment utilized at each location throughout the world.
Connecting with users of machines and getting genuine feedback about the quality, production data and service of machine brands and models.
Share my information and help others find solutions to their equipment needs.
I would like to sponsor and develop a web based equipment maintenance, planning and scheduling application.

    Macfarlan Machinery, LLC.

    (Machinery industry)

    May 1998 — Present (11 years 10 months)

    Macfarlan Machinery is a mining and construction equipment resource with offices in Lexington, KY. We have a worldwide customer base with clients in construction aggregate production, mining, steel mill services and heavy civil construction.

    Our main focus is on larger pieces consisting of rigid frame trucks, wheel loaders, shovels/backhoes and track type tractors. We provide our clients with the best value equipment available from our worldwide resources.
