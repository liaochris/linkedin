Jonathan Weeks, PMP’s Summary

Jonathan is an award winning, energetic senior IT leader with 15 years of extensive health care experience managing highly complex IT environments primarily in large scale health care systems, including an academic medical center.

With a proven record of success in the medical field, Jonathan adds value through dynamic IT leadership, resulting in successful implementations and support of hospital and ambulatory applications including Electronic Health Records.

SIGNIFICANT ACCOMPLISHMENTS AND SKILLS

• Awarded the only Stanford Hospital IT Leadership award by the client CIO in 2007 for “being the bridge” by successfully bringing IT teams and operational departments together collaboratively
• Improved relationships with COO, CIO, SVP and multiple directors and physicians by acting as the single point of contact for IT service delivery
• Skilled at partnering with executive management as a corporate IT representative on the local hospital IT Executive Steering Committee
• Avoided any unplanned interruption in patient care by successfully directing the Inpatient Epic activation cutover and go-live activities
• Significantly improved client relations by effectively rolling-out an InterSystems Ensemble interface engine, and building the associated highly performing Interface team
• Improved client satisfaction by architecting and reorganizing IT teams into efficient and effective units with embedded best practices (example: ISO, ITIL, PMBOK)
• Ensured a tried and tested disaster recovery plan by leading the application business continuity exercise; bringing up critical applications in a remote SunGuard data center
• Successfully brought cross-functional teams together to minimize or avoid downtime in unforeseen crisis situations (e.g. power interruption, generator failure)
• Enabled use of new 70,000 sq. ft. facility as scheduled by overseeing technology deployment including network, 400+ PC’s, 100+ printers, and application customizations
