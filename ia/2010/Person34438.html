Mark Read’s Summary

As a sales professional, Mark Read was a consistent top producer, recognized in the Rancho Bernardo "Top Five" every year and was the Koll Company "Broker of The Year" for San Diego County in 1985. In 1993, Mr. Read received national recognition with the J. Frank Mahoney Leadership Award from CB Commercial for his work in Jacksonville. In 1997, Mr. Read received the Mahoney Award again, this time for his work in San Diego. In 1998, Mr. Read and his San Diego County Management team were recognized by the San Diego Business Journal with a "TEIM" award for Total Excellence In Management. In 1999 and 2000, Mark received the James J. Didion Award from CB Richard Ellis. This award is given to managers who have distinguished themselves through exceptional market leadership. Under Mark Read's leadership, the San Diego Region of CBRE is consistently one of the company leaders in creativity, client service, and performance.
