Andrew Wilcox’s Summary

Currently developing a service called ConferenceREACTION for recording in a variety of media: events, meetings and conferences and publishing them rapidly on the Internet. The unique aspect of ConferenceREACTION is being able to summarise a speaker or meeting in a single screen or sheet of paper.

The established parent business Cabre resells MindManager and associated software and trains its users to get the best out of the product in a wide variety of applications.

Cabre also works with clients to help them structure complex information and publish it in visual maps and traditional documents. This work has included visualising early stage businesses, visual communications in business plans and next year action plans.

Previously Andrew has been working as a Process Improvement Consultant, Project Manager, Chief Engineer, Control and Electrical Engineer for Food and Chemical Industries.
