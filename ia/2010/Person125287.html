Linda Sacha’s Summary

Author of QUEEN FOR A DAY - RECLAIMING YOUR HAPPINESS ONE BIRTHDAY AT A TIME released by Gotham, January 2010.

Sacha has facilitated, motivated and inspired for 35 years as an award winning teacher, trainer, professional speaker, author, improv performer and voice over artist; the common thread - creative communication. She has addressed audiences of one to one thousand.

Co-hosting a radio and television show are next on her agenda.

Sacha's greatest professional joy is being a member of a collaborative team creating multi-media projects that educate and inspire.
