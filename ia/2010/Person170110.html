Read Hayes, PhD’s Summary

Research scientist specializing in studying offender decision-making in varying store conditions in order to focus and improve situational deterrence of crime attempts.

Our goal: sell more while losing less- safely and cost-effectively.

Thirty second year in loss prevention starting out as a store detective in 1977 at Robinson's of Florida.
