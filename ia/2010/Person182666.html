Sarah J. Wilson’s Summary

I use my years of editing and teaching experience to the benefit of my clients. I style-edit documents, articles, and web postings, originally translated by non-native English-speakers, for both U.S. and U.K. audiences. To ensure my editing remains faithful to the intent of the original author, I do background research on the topic. Not only does this strengthen the final project, but I find I gain so much more from the editing experience. My work is clear, accurate, enjoyable, and prompt.
