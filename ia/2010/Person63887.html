Ellen Lloyd’s Summary

Ellen is the author of the FRECKLES® children's book series. Her books and Freckles plush are available on her website, www.funwithfreckles.com, as well as from local bookstores and major online vendors.

Her most recent title, FRECKLES® and The Great Beach Rescue, is the recipient of several awards including: Mom's Choice Award, Moonbeam Children's Book Award, USABookNews.com Best Books Award, Eric Hoffer Awards Notables Book, and the Indie Excellence in Publishing Award.

Ellen has delivered many guest author presentations to schools, libraries and community groups. She has also organized and participated in numerous bookstore promotional events. Her school visits include a reading of her most recent book followed by a presentation about the writing and/or publishing process, and a visit from Freckles himself!

She is an avid reader and supporter of all things related to children, especially promoting literacy and the love of reading in children. For additional information, please visit her website at www.funwithfreckles.com.

Ellen Lloyd’s Experience

  • Founder and President

    Butterfly Creations, Inc.

    (Publishing industry)

    1994 — Present (16 years )

    Butterfly Creations is the publisher of the FRECKLES® picture books for children. The current titles are FRECKLES®, and FRECKLES® and The Great Beach Rescue. The latter is the recipient of multiple publishing awards, and both books are Accelerated Reader (AR) titles. As author and publisher, Ellen manages the daily operations of being an independent publisher including sales, promotions, marketing, billing and distribution. In building the FRECKLES® brand, she partnered with GUND, Inc. to develop and manufacture a plush toy of Freckles, the main character in the FRECKLES® children's book series, and with Costume Specialists to create an adult sized costumed character of Freckles. In addition, she developed the website www.funwithfreckles.com.

    Ellen is a frequent guest author at elementary schools, offering fun-filled, engaging and age-appropriate presentations designed to foster an enhanced interest in reading and an introduction to the world of publishing.

    The benefits of hosting an author visit with Ellen and Freckles are:
    •It can raise money for your school - - 10% of all sales generated the day of the visit will be donated back to your school
    •An autographed copy of FRECKLES® and The Great Beach Rescue will be donated to the school's media center
    •Students have an adult-sized Freckles character available to meet and greet them (provided the school secures a volunteer)
    •Complimentary bookmarks are given to all participating students
    •Complimentary master coloring sheets are provided to coordinator
    •All books will be personalized and autographed through pre-sales

    Ellen is the creator of www.mindfulmom.wordpress.com, a weblog with musings from a mom and author discussing writing for children, the daily joys and challenges of parenting, and great books for children. Please join in on the discussions and share your thoughts and comments, and together we can all be more mindful moms, and dads, too!
