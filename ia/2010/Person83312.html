Hugh Weber’s Summary

Hugh is President and Head Coach of Deep Bench, a strategic planning and innovation firm that has helped underdogs become overachievers with a "work. play. do good." approach since March 2006.

Hugh's mission statement is to educate, engage, and empower those he encounters to maximize their personal potential through their church, community, charity and career, in turn maximizing each of these institutions.

Hugh prefers guitars to drums, baseball to basketball, and good home-cooked pasta to just about anything.

Hugh lives in Sioux Falls with his beautiful, intelligent and extremely patient wife, Amy.

Hugh Weber’s Specialties:

Social Media & Online Community Organizing, Thought Leadership, Trendspotting, Strategic Planning and Messaging, Internal Communications & Training, Personal Branding, Creative Talent Recruiting, Emerging Technologies, Business Development, Grassroots Outreach, Campaign Management & General Consulting, Political Technology, Political Education & Training, Young Voters
