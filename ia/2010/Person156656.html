Dave Hurley’s Summary

My Philosophy:

We only get one chance at this thing we call life, therefore, what we choose to do with that one chance is what makes all the difference to the quality of not only our lives, but also the lives of others. Some people’s greatest desire is to make huge amounts of money, and their efforts are expended upon gaining something which, only once it’s too late, do they realize never made them happy, nor made any lasting impact on the world. I do not want to leave behind a “non-legacy”, an empty space in a world that is no different because of my presence. I would rather give away all I ever had for the sake of a worthy cause, something that can make people’s lives better, easier, and happier long after I die, than hoard riches and have that be all I leave behind. After all, as they say, spend it now because you can’t take it with you!

Experiences:

I have traveled the world extensively both for work and pleasure. I have been lost on the streets of Singapore, Chased by bats in Australia, Stood in Awe at the top of the of a mountain in the Hindu Kush and fell asleep in a hamock on the shores of Mexico. There is one thing that all these beautiful places have in common, it's not everlasting. We must work to make a positive step in sustaining these wonders.

About New World Volunteer:

New World Volunteer is a not-for-profit venture that will provide a place where social and environmental organizations, charity groups and do-gooders can connect with those people around the world who enable them to make a difference, an interactive social forum where individual causes are broadcast to an audience of donors and volunteers. Through transparent micro-funding and community involvement, projects are transformed from idea to reality and can successfully create lasting positive impacts on our planet and people.

Dave Hurley’s Experience

  • Crew Leader, Founder

    New World Volunteer

    (Philanthropy industry)

    January 2010 — Present (8 months)

    NewWorldVolunteer.ORG is a unique volunteering adventure dreamed up and made a reality by myself and brother Devon Hurley. This round-the-world expedition aims to produce positive impacts across the globe, improving quality of life for people wherever the crew sails. The projects undertaken along the way will be the means by which to achieve this end, and the method of travel will hopefully prove to be that which most significantly minimizes the carbon footprint of the voyagers. The idea of course, is not only to create awareness of the project, but on a larger scale, to increase awareness of environmental and humanitarian issues across our planet. The vision of this journey is simple:

    1) Create a continued support structure to help others that will endure long after our adventure is over.


    2) Try our best to create a unified global vision of how environmental stability can be implemented around the world.


    3) Educate others on the importance of our planet’s natural diversity and why we must protect it.
