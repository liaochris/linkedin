Konstantin Guericke’s Summary

I am a social architect with a passion for exploring the intersection of psychology, sociology and computing.

I have 17 years of experience across product management, marketing, sales, international and business development. Most of my experience is in consumer Internet-based communication services and social networking, but my background also includes on-demand business software (SaaS), graphics and rich media.

My technical concentrations at Stanford were in artificial intelligence and reasoning under uncertainty. I grew up in a small town in northern Germany. I have studied, lived and worked in Japan and traveled extensively in South-East Asia.
