Debra Dion Krischke’s Summary

DEFINITIONS:
"Foodie": a person keenly interested in food, especially eating and cooking.

"Foodie Fundraising": Events designed around food to attract Foodies so they will open their wallets for a good cause with one hand, while happily stuffing their faces with the other.

Drawing on over 20 years' experience in producing special events, Foodie Fundraising is dedicated to sharing fundraising best practices and expertise, especially for the support of women's initiatives.

Visit our website (www.FoodieFundraising.com) for national trends and best practices. You'll also find PR and fundraising tips for grassroot efforts.

We devote a percentage of our proceeds to Care International 's I AM POWERFUL Campaign!
