Kenisha Beal-Dickey, CEP’s Summary

Everyone wants to know who is the lady behind it all. Well, that lady is me. I’ve been told that I have a million dollar smile and a priceless heart. I choose to believe it. My purpose in life is to teach and set the example for others by helping them to put determination, dedication, and faith behind their dreams in order to manifest them into reality. You have to sow the good seed of thinking. One of the reasons why people do not achieve their dreams and goals is because they desire to change their results without changing their thinking. To make progress in any field you have to take action, but the success of the action you take depends entirely on how you think before hand. “If people can grow in their thinking, they will constantly outgrow what they’re doing and their potential will always be off the charts.” When this happens, I'll be there to help them. Most of the above are not my words, but they are good words to live by. I like to think of myself as balanced. I’m not all work and no play. However, I like to work smart and hard so that I can play hard and live smart. I am very family-oriented and love to have BBQs, game parties, theme parties, dance-offs, you name it and we will have fun doing it. It is not the event, but the company that makes it fun. If you want to know a little more about me just drop me a note and I’ll be glad to dialogue with you.
