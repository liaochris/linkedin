Jesse Gift’s Summary

Recipient of the 1997 Prime Time Emmy Award for Breaking News - Dateline NBC -“L.A Heat”. In 2008, Mr. Gift was nominated for a second Emmy Award for a feature segment (Los Angeles Area Emmys KCET-The PBS Station).

20 years experience, a consummate storyteller - known in the television business for creating compelling, content daily.

Jesse recently landed national media coverage for Wigix.com on Dr. Phil, http://www.wigix.com/dr-phil/theme_community/index/9 and Rachael Ray http://www.wigix.com/rachael-ray/theme_community/index/10

He landed PR client, Larry D. Woodard major TV media attention discussing Tiger Woods Endorsements. http://abcnews.go.com/GMA/video/tiger-woods-play-masters-10122339 + landed Mr. Woodard his own weekly ABC News column http://tinyurl.com/larryabc

While at Entertainment Tonight, Jesse produced and edited high profile stories featuring Hollywood's biggest stars for over 10 years.

Mr. Gift was a key player for years at NBC Network News, including Nightly News with Tom Brokaw, The Today Show & Dateline NBC. He worked on many of modern history’s biggest news stories including the O.J. Simpson Trial, the L.A. earthquake, and the North Hollywood Bank Robber Shootout (the latter aired on a Dateline Prime Time Special and received the Prime Time Breaking News Emmy Award).

He started his network news career at ABC News during the LA Riots as an editor, and quickly became the primary editor on the Rodney King federal trial story for World News Tonight with Peter Jennings + numerous other major stories for a variety of ABC Network News shows including Good Morning America, 20/20, David Brinkley, Prime Time Live, and Nightline.
