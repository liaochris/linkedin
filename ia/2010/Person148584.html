Monique Anderson’s Summary

Monique Anderson is an experienced Consultant for the pharmaceutical/biotech industry, specializing in oncology. One of her key areas of expertise is in cancer epidemiology, including the creation of models to estimate cancer patient populations, along with a thorough understanding of global epidemiology sources, their caveats, and their use in such models. Additionally, Monique has in-depth knowledge of treatment practices (multiple modality) and drug development issues across 25+ cancer sites in the United States, Europe and Japan. She regularly analyzes the emerging technologies of novel agents and their potential impact on global treatment practices. Monique also has considerable knowledge of global provider, regulatory and reimbursement issues. A current area of focus includes the impact of biomarkers on patient subtyping, drug development, and treatment.

Monique Anderson’s Specialties:

Epidemiology; modelling; forecasting; technology assessment, including critical evaluation of clinical trial data; market evaluation; commercial/situation analysis; competitive intelligence; training/education
