David Anthony (danthony@21ventures.net)’s Summary

David Anthony is an experienced entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and educator. Since founding 21Ventures in 2004, the fund has provided seed, growth, and bridge capital to more than 40 technology ventures across the globe. David Anthony sits on the board of of 21V portfolio companies including: Advanced Telemetry; BioPetroClean, ETV Motors, and Variable Wind Solutions. David also serves on the board of directors of publicly traded companies including: Axion Power International, Clean Power Technologies; Solar EnerTech and ThermoEnergy Corporation.

David Anthony is an Adjunct Professor at the New York Academy of Sciences (NYAS). The Academy brings together scientists of different disciplines to advance the understanding of science, technology, and medicine, and to stimulate new ways to think about how their research is applied in society and the world. The Academy is widely recognized as one of the world's foremost organizers of scientific conferences and symposia. David's students at the Academy hail from such prestigious institutions as Columbia, NYU, Princeton, Yale, Rockefeller University, Sloan Kettering, and the Albert Einstein College of Medicine.

Previously, Mr. Anthony, collaborated with Dartmouth College professor Dr. John K. Shank and consulted for Fortune 500 clients on capital investments, mergers and acquisitions, and entrepreneurship. In addition David is an adjunct professor at the NYU Stern School of business where he began teaching technology entrepreneurship in the Fall of 2009.

David received his MBA from The Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth College in 1989 and a BA in economics from George Washington University in 1982. He is an entrepreneurship mentor at the Land Center for Entrepreneurship at Columbia University Graduate School of Business. In 2002, David was awarded the Distinguished Mentor of the Year Award from Columbia University.

David blogs at www.davidanthonyvc.com

  • Co-investor, Board Member

    Advanced Telemetry, LLC

    (Renewables & Environment industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Corporate Overview
    Founded in September 2007, Advanced Telemetry is enabling energy and resource conservation through its proprietary EcoView line of products. EcoView is available under the Advanced Telemetry brand as well as being white-labeled for certain distribution partners.

    The EcoView product line embodies Advanced Telemetry's corporate belief that change in energy wasting consumption habits can only occur if the results of these habits are visible to energy users in real time. As such, the EcoView product line enables users to observe and take immediate action to address excessive resource consumption. Advanced Telemetry greatly supports efforts by federal, state, and local governments to bring energy efficiency and conservation incentives and devices to the forefront of the dialogue regarding global warming and resource conservation. The EcoView product line is utility and meter agnostic and does not require the participation of utilities to achieve full functionality.

    The Company is headquartered in San Diego, California and venture backed by 21Ventures (www.21ventures.net).

    BioPetroClean is a cleantech company that specializes in the bioremediation of industrial wastewaters. Our breakthrough technology, Automated Chemostat Treatment (ACT), is a solution that offers significant improvements over other existing processes on the market. ACT can be applied to a wide variety of sites (including but not limited to oil refineries, terminals, drilling sites, marine ports, power stations, contaminated resevoirs, storage tanks, etc).
