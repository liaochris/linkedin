Christopher Bull’s Summary

Chris Bull serves as a Co-Founder and Managing Director of McDermott & Bull, based in Irvine California. He is responsible for overseeing the majority of the firm’s Search Consultants, as well as their training and development. Formerly, Chris was an Executive Vice President in the Orange County office of DHR International, the nation’s 6th largest executive search firm.

Earlier in his career, Chris spent six years operating the company he founded, American Liberty Capital Corporation. American Liberty combined cutting edge technology with the standards of commercial and residential mortgage banking retail services to become one of the largest privately held consumer finance companies based in Southern California. The 130-person America Liberty staff operated in 32 states and underwrote more than $300 million in loans in 1997-98. Previously, Chris served as a Vice President for two real estate investment banking firms – Lomas Financial Corporation and Mason McDuffie Financial Corporation. His clients ranged from national real estate developers to local investors, averaging an investment size of $6 million. Chris was a top producer with Mason McDuffie Financial in their Newport Beach office during his tenure.

Chris has served as a Board Member of the Association for Corporate Growth, and is currently on the Board of Big Brothers Big Sisters of Orange County. He currently resides in Laguna Hills, California. Chris received a Bachelors Degree in Economics and Finance from the University of Texas at Austin in 1985.
