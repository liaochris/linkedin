Shelley Holmes (Davidson)’s Summary

My corporate career has been in the Human Resource side of Manufacturing industries. I had expected that this would continue once I began my own business in 2001, yet funnily enough the majority of my clients have come from Service industries - but still do have manufacturing clients as well.

My focus is on creating online resources that leaders can use in the time and place they need 24/7. I've created the Align-Lead-Inspire Club ( http://www.align-lead-inspire.com ) which enables leaders to get the learning they need when they need.
