Christine Kirk’s Summary

Christine advises luxury, technology, and lifestyle brands as well as national food/beverage clients on social media marketing and branding. She has amassed over 5,600 engaged followers on Twitter (find her @luxuryprgal), and growing, and is a guest blogger on various social media, PR and lifestyle subjects.

Christine also has experience in traditional PR campaigns and media relations and has landed influential placements for clients in local and national television, radio and print media; Representative placements include: USA Today, The Wall Street Journal, The Los Angeles Times, The Hollywood Reporter, CNN Headline News, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Good Day LA, etc.

Christine has conducted online communications and traditional PR campaigns for food & beverage, restaurant/nightclub, luxury travel, luxury real estate, consumer lifestyle products, consumer electronics, wireless, consumer technology, and automotive clients.
