Hernan Charry’s Summary

--------------------What am I doing?-----------------------

On a mission to create value for my connections and friends in the Valley of the Sun using the potential of the internet.

--------------------Where have I been?--------------------

Much of my career was spent learning then teaching others how to be smart with money, make wise investment decisions, and find money for their businesses. In finance, I've held every position I wanted to - insurance salesperson, financial planner, small business banker, manager, most recently investment banker. Having worked with over one thousand businesses in my career, would you like to know what I learned?

Two things

1) Having your own business is the best path to success in this country. Investing is simply giving your money to someone else's business. Why not invest in yourself?

2) The internet provides the greatest opportunities for leveraging yourself to lasting success (Don't worry, it's not too late to get involved --- the internet is still in its infancy.)

--------------------Where am I going?--------------------

After years of meeting, studying, and getting to know successful entrepreneurs, my partner and I launched ourselves full force into our own e-business. PawPosse.com is a site dedicated to cool stuff for big dogs. Check it out and tell me what you think.

In the process, I formed a partnership with Split One Technologies, our web developers, so I am now able to provide the premier web development service in Arizona. We do more than just websites, we build business management systems. Contact me for a consult on your system.

I am always looking for like minded people to join my Meet-up, Split One Alliance, where we mastermind to share helpful information for those looking to grow their web presence. See the link above for more information.

So, how can I help you today?
