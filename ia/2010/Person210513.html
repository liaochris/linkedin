Vanessa Van Petten’s Summary

I am a business adventurist and venture capitalist. I started with my first company, Radical Parenting--a parenting blog written by kids and now invest in a number of small start-ups. I am working on my third book and started a training program for youthologists--coaches specializing in youth.
