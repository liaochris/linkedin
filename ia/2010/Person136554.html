Mathew Robitaille’s Summary

My experience has been wide in its range, with 5 years on Commercial Floorplan Financing at Bombardier, 2 years of Defense Contracting Finance at General Dynamics, to 3 years and counting of Commercial Banking at KeyBank. Combine that with my Six Sigma credentials, and I bring what I consider a unique approach to my clients, the ability to look at their business from a multitude of angles. I pride myself in working with my clients to determine not what product sells the best, but what product works best for them, and helps them achieve their business goals. Nothing is more satisfying to me then seeing a successful business and knowing that I helped get them started.

I seek out positions that offer me a challenge, I am not content to sit in the office and pass the hours, I look for something that keeps me on my toes and pushes me to learn and grow in order to complete the job.

Above all, I consider myself as having a good sense of humor, easily approachable, and the type of person you want in your corner when you need help.
