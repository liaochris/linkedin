Jody Davis’s Summary

Longtime TV & radio reporter, anchor and manager. Trivia: I'm the only person you'll meet who was an anchor and correspondent for two networks that no longer exist (CNNfn, All News Channel). I even had a fan club while at ANC. As Yogi Berra sez...you could look it up.

Jody Davis’s Specialties:

Excellent writer, editor, storyteller, voiceover talent, creator of memorable promotional material. I'm a manager who improves product, attracts eyeballs, builds brand, and stays within budget.

Guest lecturer in journalism at University of North Florida, Illinois State University, Mansfield University (PA), Ithaca College, Corning Community College, John Wood Community College (IL). Mentor at Southern Illinois University at Edwardsville.
