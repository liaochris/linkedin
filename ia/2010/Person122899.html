Lazaro Fuentes’s Summary

Internet executive with deep strategy, business development, M&A and business operations experience. Sharp grasp of media industry convergence and demographic shifts currently underway.

Expert in developing and executing strategies to capitalize and build products based on those shifts. Exceptional deal envisioning and closing experience with a knack for bringing together the right mix of people, products and capital to benefit projects, clients, or partners.

Innate resourcefulness coupled with a keen sense and understanding of capital deployment, maximizing the impact of resources to efficiently build and grow business revenues.

Eclectic mix of experience including game development, multiple internet startups, investment banking, private equity, strategic marketing, strategic consulting, non-profit founding and a military foundation, all with a consistent record of success.

Currently building a Latin-themed brand for Tween girls called Hip Chicas™ with a branded social game where users quest to Help Improve the Planet [HIP] and where we then donate a % of each micro transaction to offline organizations based on where users go in game.

Succumbed to a revenue strategy that was dependent on having access to more capital for advertising our services just as the capital markets dried up.
