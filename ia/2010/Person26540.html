Brad Hinton’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience in knowledge management, information management, and research and analysis.

I have worked in government, not-for-profit, and commercial sectors. I have also completed consultancy projects. I therefore have a range of workplace experiences to bring to different organisational contexts.

My key strengths include situation analysis, knowledge management and information management, and practical solutions for enhancing organisational effectiveness. I have initiated and managed a range of information and knowledge management processes and practices that include information audits, situation analysis, business ethnography, intranet design and web portals, communities of practice, learning logs, and strategic planning. I have also managed traditional library and records management functions, including budgeting, staff management, and mentoring.

I am strategic and practical in analysing and determining an organisation's information and document needs, assessing organisational capabilities and constraints, and in delivering appropriate solutions. I am also experienced in web and content management, internal communications, and development education.

My goal is to ensure that organisations maximise their human, intellectual, and social capital to achieve quality and sustainable organisational performance.

Brad Hinton’s Specialties:

Situation analysis, information management, knowledge management, content management, communities of practice, social media, facilitation, people management, internal communications, and records management.
