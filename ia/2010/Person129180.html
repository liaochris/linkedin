Lyle Payne’s Summary

I have over fifteen years of experience as an accounting professional in Denver, Colorado. During my professional career, I have progressed through all areas of the accounting department, most recently serving four years as a controller in the Integrated Security industry before returning to school to augment my experience and enhance my ability to use accounting information and financial management techniques to improve business decision making. I will receive my MBA with emphasis in Finance and Accounting from Regis University this summer. I am currently looking for a leadership role in a company where I can help everyone around me succeed.

I have a wide range of experience from which to draw inspiration for creative solutions. I spent my entire career prior to 2005 in small family owned businesses. I am intimately familiar with the challenges facing those companies in both favorable and unfavorable economic environments. As controller for the Colorado branch of HBE, I became familiar with the additional challenges facing public companies, including the implementation of Sarbanes-Oxley compliance. Closer to home, I have served continuously as a member of the Board of Directors of Brittany Oaks Homeowners Association for over sixteen years including six as President. Additionally, I serve as the photographer’s assistant and accounting guru for my wife’s business, Katherine Payne Photography (www.katherinepaynephotography.com).

Last year, I participated in the Help-Portrait (www.help-portrait.com) movement but my preferred charitable organization is the Now I Lay Me Down to Sleep Foundation (www.nowilaymedowntosleep.org) , which both my wife and I support. For recreational fun, I play goalkeeper weekly on an indoor soccer team I cofounded several years ago. While life has not allowed for much golf or skiing during the past few years, I do acknowledge the blessing of being able to spend additional time bonding with my two young granddaughters.
