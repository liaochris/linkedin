Seth Kaplan’s Summary

Seth was most recently Director of Engineering for the 900 person Health Information Management practice of Unisys Corporation. He led the group's research & development programs, managing a multi-million dollar annual budget. Seth set the vision and technical strategy for the overall enterprise, as well as provided technical oversight to individual customers including the states of Maine, Idaho and West Virginia, representing over $500M in won business.

Seth has held a succession of leadership positions at Unisys and facilitated the sale of Unisys HIM to Molina Healthcare for $135M. At Molina, Seth continues to be responsible for the vision, strategy and development of emerging technology solutions for the Molina Medicaid Solutions practice.

Seth is a veteran IT leader with expertise in technical architecture, program management, P&L, planning, execution, and delivery assurance. Expertise as an Internet strategist, product manager, and even programmer, Seth provides the necessary background to be an extremely effective product strategist, software developer, leader and opportunist. Seth successfully builds and leads teams of variable size, mentors, and is a reliable communicator with excellent social, written and oral skills.

Seth can be found often speaking on panels, in committees, or blogging about it on the Internet.
