Elizabeth (Liz) Handlin’s Summary

Liz Handlin, CEO of Ultimate Resumes LLC, has nearly 20 years of experience in marketing, communications, and recruiting. Ms Handlin earned her BA from the University of Chicago and an MBA from the University of Chicago Graduate School of Business.

In Chicago, Handlin may be best known for creating and managing Money Smart Week during her employment at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago. Money Smart Week is an annual financial literacy campaign that brings together banks, public libraries, not-for-profits, financial experts, schools, the media, and government agencies in an effort to help consumers learn where and how to access resources to improve their personal finances. For more information on Money Smart Week visit: www.chicagofed.org

Ms Handlin was honored by Microsoft Corporation and featured in Smart Money Magazine (June 2004) for creating Money Smart Week (MSW), now in its sixth year. She is the author of numerous articles on access to banking issues including: Chicago Fed Letter, Remittances and the Unbanked, March 2002, Chicago Fed Letter, Tapping the Potential of the Unbanked – Private Sector Interest Increases, January 2003.

Ms. Handlin has been interviewed for and quoted in numerous articles in publications that include: The Economist, The Chicago Tribune, Chicago Sun Times, Community Banker, and American Banker. Ms. Handlin writes articles on resume creation, careers, and related issues and these articles can be found on a variety of websites.

After years of using her recruiting and marketing background to help colleagues to create winning resumes, Ms Handlin launched Ultimate Resumes LLC in June 2004.

Ms. Handlin is a past member of Illinois State Treasurer Judy Baar Topinka’s Advisory Board on Women’s issues. She was a founder of Chicago Street Project. She is a past President of the Austin Chapter of Kappa Alpha Theta Alumnae and is currently the Alumnae District Officer for Kappa Alpha Theta in South Texas..
