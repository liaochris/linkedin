William (Bill) Snee’s Summary

I am an IT Program Manager with experience in enterprise architecture, systems and software development. The focus of my experience has been on developing data strategies and services within a SOA environment. My primary career path has been within the Department of Defense including the Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Modeling and Simulation (M&S) and the Chemical, Biological, Radiological, Nuclear (CBRN) domains.
