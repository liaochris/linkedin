Craig Leith’s Summary

I help organisations of all sizes improve.

There are 4 issues I see in organisations all the time:

1) Current management thinking holds organisations back. If we truly want to improve we need to change the way we lead and manage.

2) Skillsets are underutilised. Usually people in the organisation have the necessary skills, but somehow they don't get applied. What a waste!

3) Learning is a social as well as individual experience. To learn, people need to have a chance to discuss, act and reflect.

4) No-one has time to learn. Everyone is too busy "cutting down the tree" and no-one has time to "sharpen the saw".

I free organisations from past thinking and help them move over to a culture where individuals manage themselves, where leaders truly lead and where teams use principles of lean and high performance.

If you would like to discuss how I can help, please contact me.
