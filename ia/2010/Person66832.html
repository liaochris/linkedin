Erik Pitts’s Summary

Over 20 years delivering results in a broad range of sales, marketing, manufacturing, and commercial leadership roles. Extensive experience as a sales and business leader at e-health medical records start-up, rapidly growing European IT and women’s health company, and U.S. multi-national medical technology corporation. Expertise includes sales, team building, managing sales organizations, legal and contract oversight, and building strategic partnerships.
