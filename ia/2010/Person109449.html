Michel JUVILLIER’s Experience

  • Managing Director France

    Improve Digital

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    July 2009 — Present (1 year )

    Improve Digital is the leading yield optimisation technology provider in Europe. Our core solution today is the ad network optimiser. For publishers it increases revenues from ad networks and exchanges with an average of 82%, but with peeks up to 350%.

    In 2008 44% of all UK display budgets went through ad networks (IAB). Hence what we see is that Publishers nowadays work with more than one network or exchange and this represents a lot of work, inefficiencies and fragmented reporting and billing interfaces and often low CPM’s. Our 'self-learning' optimization technology is able to match the right view with the right ad network in real time and this result in a much higher CPMs and more revenue.

    To do this, we are the exclusive partner of US-based Pubmatic, and we combine its optimization technology with our own solutions. Next to technology, we believe it’s essential to have a local presence in all the markets we serve and together with PubMatic we have offices in the US, Asia, UK, France, Germany and the Netherlands. Each geographic region, each publisher, each website and each placement requires a different mix of networks.

    90% of our clients are premium publishers, hence publishers looking to benefit from ad networks and exchanges in harmony with their direct sales strategies and brand image protection
