Shashin Shah’s Summary

Shashin has over 28 years IT experience, working for clients in the Communications, Finance, Transport, Utilities, Consumer, Broadcasting and Media sectors. Shashin has previously worked for Logica, System House, Deloitte & Touche and Andersen Business Consulting.

Over the past 23 years in his spare time, Shashin has helped charities with their IT strategies, encompassing desktop, server, networking and Internet technologies with specific emphasis on Open Source (Alfresco, Zimbra, Liferay, MindTouch etc) to reduce licensing costs.

Shashin established his own consultancy in 2005 specialising in Enterprise Architecture, Enterprise Content Management, Messaging & Collaboration, Web & Enterprise 2.0.

Over the last 5 years Shashin has been heading up the Architecture teams on major CRM/OM/Billing multimillion pound projects for Carphone Warehouse, AOL Broadband and Tesco Telecom.

Shashin Shah’s Specialties:

Technology Strategy, Enterprise Architecture, Application Portfolio Rationalisation, Technology Transition/Separation, Selection of Technology and Systems Integration Partners, Governance (TDA and Design Council) on large technology Programmes, Business Case, Enterprise Content Management, Open Source Solutions, Architecture Frameworks and Software Development Life Cycles. Open Source (Alfresco, Zimbra, Liferay), Collaboration, Enterprise Content Management, Messsaging, Web 2.0, Enterprise 2.0
