Seth Gordon’s Summary

Seth is the managing partner of GDB Miami, a business advisory firm specializing in the Miami / South Florida market. He knows the history, the people, the customs, the institutions, the economy, and the politics.

Widely known as "Mr. Miami" for his sweeping knowledge of the Miami community, Gordon impresses upon clients that Miami changes so fast you need to move quickly just to stay in the same place. Stand still and the world will pass you by.

The corollary is that today’s “high profile player” can easily become tomorrow’s “who’s that?”

The firm's core function is to help clients understand their surroundings and grow their businesses. Specific activities include:

- Business Development – Taking clients directly to the decision makers with the authority to OK a deal

- News Media / Social Media – Blending broad knowledge of both traditional media and social media to deliver our clients’ messages to the correct audiences.

- Civic Positioning – Helping clients understand Miami’s community dynamics and how to use the civic structure as a business platform

- Public Affairs –Big Government is back. Taking clients in the right door to make their case to the right people

- Constituency Development – When a client needs to demonstrate public support for their business plans, they know how to enlist and deploy such allies

- Survival Communications – Attacks on business can come from many directions. Angry consumers. Antagonistic competitors. Aggressive government. They’ve been there before and know how to respond.

- Raising Capital – Matching clients in search of angel, seed, early stage or venture capital with appropriate prospective investors

Seth Gordon’s Specialties:

Gordon works with clients in many sectors but has particularly strong record of achievement in healthcare, technology, entertainment, media, real estate, financial services and arts & culture.
