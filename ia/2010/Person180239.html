Sabu Francis’s Summary

Main domain expertise: Architecture.
Other domains: Information Technology, Knowledge Management, Artificial Intelligence

Practice of architecture requires both abstract thinking and delivery on the ground. Hence I got involved in both research along with realization of architecture projects. I did original research in the field of architecture representation systems and discovered some mathematical principles. These were incorporated into a software (TAD Designer Lite) that was written in Prolog

My software has proved itself in the design of architecture; especially in the early design stages. I have presented my work in international peer-reviewed conferences and my work has been cited by other researchers. My work was utilized by IIT Bombay for two of their research projects in the area of energy calculations in buildings.

The cross-over of domain expertise happened subsequently: Today I have experience and knowledge in many areas relating to both architecture and computing. I own original intellectual property in the area of architecture representation systems, music, messaging, etc. My tutorials for Visual Prolog are quite popular. See http://www.visual-prolog.com/vip70/Tutorial/
I can deliver focused solutions to complicated problems; especially solutions to those problems that straddle the fields of architecture, computing and knowledge management. I am looking for opportunities where some of my original intellectual properties in the areas of my expertise can be taken ahead.
