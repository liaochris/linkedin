Dinesh Jain’s Summary

Have been in Life Sciences and Information Technology offshore outsourcing business for over 25 year, including being the CEO of 3 companies. Specialize in business deals of North America, Japan, Asia and India.

Current focus is to provide Strategic Consulting Services to companies in Life Scineces or IT field, with their India initiatives whether looking investment from or to India, In-Licensing, Out Licensing, M&A, JV, Finding Right partner etc.

We also partner with Indian companies to provide Clinical and Contract Research Services in Biotech and Pharmaceutical fields from India to the world.

Thirdly we provide Technology Licensing Services (TLO) to Universities for commercialization of their technologies
