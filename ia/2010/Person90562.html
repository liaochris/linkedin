Jason Knapp’s Summary

Internet display advertising monetization expert, including creating the first implementation of "Real-Time-Bidding.” Co-founded and led product, sales and client services at SDC (Strategic Data Corp), which built the first performance, optimized ad-serving system in 2000. Sold SDC to News Corp in 2007 for more than $140 million, where product managed building and led support of the Fox Audience Network’s (FAN’s) ad serving / yield optimization system, 2009’s highest volume ad serving platform. The system monetizes all ads on MySpace, which saw its ad performance nearly triple within months of the system’s launch, as well as additional inventory from 1,000's of other sites. Previously launched various tech ventures and consulted to or held positions with high-tech companies including Microsoft, Intel, and HP. UCLA Anderson School MBA.

Jason Knapp’s Specialties:

Monetization of online display advertising, Display ad serving systems product and project management, Business unit leadership and P&L responsibility, Technology / Internet startup management, Venture funding, financing, mergers and acquisition, Patents, patent applications
