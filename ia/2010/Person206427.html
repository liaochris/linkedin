Tom Rosseland’s Summary

- Over my past 23 years of legal practice, I have worked with and successfully handled a wide array of complex legal matters for individual and corporate clients, with a dedicated focus on achieving client goals timely and efficiently.

- I represent clients engaged in all areas of business, including heavy equipment financing and leasing companies, retail and wholesale distribution businesses with credit facilities, and importers of high quality and high-value products for the consumer and business markets. These clients are based in and operate on the local, regional, national as well as international level.

- I am committed to each client and view each matter as an opportunity to demonstrate a level of dedication and persistence that serves to strengthen the relationship with the client and to justify their confidence in the Firm. Early in my career, I served as in-house counsel for Exxon Corporation (now ExxonMobil). My experience as in-house counsel for a global corporation has provided me with a unique sensitivity to needs of business clients and their in-house counsel.
