Ryan Frankel’s Summary

Ryan is currently an MBA Candidate in the Class of 2012 at The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania where he is double majoring in Finance and Entrepreneurship with a Secondary Focus in Real Estate. Prior to enrolling at Wharton, Ryan was a financial analyst at Goldman Sachs in New York City. Ryan joined Goldman Sachs in 2006 in the firm's Special Situations Group where he focused on middle market private equity deals and the operational management of portfolio companies. Prior to joining the private equity business, Ryan worked on structured transactions and alternative energy private equity deals. In 2006, Ryan also founded QuietEmber.com, a web-based resource designed to support the college athletic recruiting needs of student-athletes and college coaches. Ryan is a triathlete and a recent Ironman triathlon finisher and a volunteer with the Network for Teaching Entrepreneurship. Ryan recently founded VIYASports.com, a community platform to support the endurance sports and lifestyle needs of members of the athletic community.
