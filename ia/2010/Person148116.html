Mohan Dutt’s Summary

A warm welcome to you from the world's leading Oracle E-Business Suite Evangelist: Mohan Dutt.
Mohan is an expert on Governance-Risk-Compliance (GRC), Records to Report, Contracts to Renewal, Procure to Pay, Order to Cash and Projects to Profit business flows.
Mohan has worked in varied roles of Business Systems Manager, Project Manager, Functional Team Lead, Principal Consultant, Lead Analyst, Business Analyst and Production Support Specialist for numerous implementation, upgrade and roll-out projects involving Oracle E-Business Suite.
Throughout his Oracle E-Business Suite career, Mohan has been part of organizations that are Oracle Certified Advantage Partners namely Hyperion Solutions Corporation, Pricewaterhouse Coopers and Solix Technologies. Prior to his current role, Mohan was a Principal Consultant at Oracle Corporation. Mohan is a naturalized U.S. citizen.
In the role of an Oracle E-Business Suite Evangelist, Mohan has preached to the choir, more than fifty times at Oracle and OAUG conferences worldwide at Beijing, Sydney, Melbourne, Toronto, London, Las Vegas, Dallas, Denver, Nashville, Orlando, Chicago, San Diego, San Francisco, San Ramon and Santa Clara.
As a Oracle Certified Expert (OCE) and Oracle Certified Professsional (OCP), Mohan encourages others to get certified and provides certification tips on his blog. Mohan is also spreading a good word about Oracle E-Business Suite Release 12: The Global Release by highlighting new features on the Oracle R12 blog.
For his contribution as an Oracle E-Business Suite evangelist at large, Mohan was awarded the OAUG Member of the Year Award for 2007.
Mohan looks forward to contributing in the coming years building the world's best enterprise application solution yet: Oracle Fusion Applications.
Drop an e-mail to: mohan.dutt@gmail.com and get to know a mind of a maverick - a mind on fire!
