George Harvey’s Summary

Linda and I lead a team from our North Canton, OH office serving the real estate needs of our clients in Canton / Akron and surrounding areas in NE Ohio. We offer a Client Appreciation Program which provides useful information on many aspects of life ... dealing with safety, finances, identity fraud, personal development and even the real estate market. We have an ever expanding website (georgeharvey.net) which gives you access to properties all over the USA. We are also a part of a national organization of the best Realtors in the country which we refer clients to on a regular basis when moving into or out of our local market. Our blog provides regular and up-to-date information on how events of today are impacting real estate.

Our office works almost exclusively by referral. We would welcome the opportunity to work with you and your referrals.

Oh, by the way ... we are never too busy for your referrals.
