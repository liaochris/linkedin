Steve Banfield’s Summary

Steve Banfield is a technology industry veteran who has built and marketed consumer products and services that are widely used by millions of consumers. He has held senior leadership roles at premier companies like Sony, Microsoft, and RealNetworks. Most recently he was CEO of Mixercast, Inc., a flexible widget creation, syndication, tracking and management platform for social media advertising.

Over the last few years Mr. Banfield has served as a consultant and advisor to several startups in the social networking and digital media ecosystem. He's worked with them on projects from business development, product planning to business planning and fundraising.
 
Prior to beginning his consulting practice, Mr. Banfield held a number of executive roles with Sony. He was General Manager and Senior Vice President of Sony Connect Inc., where he was responsible for two strategic businesses: Connect, Sony’s platform for selling digital music, e-books and video, and Sony Media Software, developer of award-winning digital media creation tools for consumers and professionals.
 
Before joining Sony, Mr. Banfield was at RealNetworks, rising from RealPlayer Group Program Manager to Vice President of Strategic Relations. Under his leadership, his consumer products team shipped four key client releases, including the RealPlayer, RealJukebox and RealOne Player, and substantially grew the company’s user base from 30 million to almost 300 million.
 
Mr. Banfield’s experience also includes leading product development and marketing initiatives at such innovative technology companies as IBM, NCR and Microsoft, where he was involved in the development of Windows 95 and was one of Microsoft’s first multimedia evangelists.
 
Mr. Banfield was an executive at two technology startups while a full-time MBA student at Harvard Business School, graduating in 1998. He received his undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Transylvania University in his home state of Kentucky.
