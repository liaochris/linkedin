Quwania Jai’s Summary

TSF is a marketing & promotions company designed to assist independent and emerging artists in the entertainment industry.

We offer an array of services that include:

* Public/Media Relations
* Grassroots Marketing
* Street Teams Nationally
* Brand Ambassadors Nationally
* Promo Models Nationally
* Event/Tradeshow Coordination/Facilitation/Staffing

Currently, TSF has brand ambassadors/promo models and lifestyle ambassadors in over 28 markets throughout the U.S. Serving the entertainment and lifestyle sector, TSF provides professional grassroots services that surpass that of their counterparts. Over the years, TSF has provided grassroots marketing for companies such as: ABC network, BET network, Columbia Records, Bishop T.D. Jakes, Arrow Records, Verity Records, Integrity, Tyscot and many more.

Quwania Jai's PR experience includes serving as the lead publicist for the American Gangster, Frank Lucas, Universal Motown artist, The Pop It Off Boyz, Bishop Thomas Weeks, III and Khalil Islam, to name a few.

The experiences of TSF and Quwania Jai encompass Gospel, Mainstream and everything in between! This fact alone is the premise of our motto, "We Are Where YOUR Business Takes Us!"
