Valto Loikkanen’s Summary

As an entrepreneur, I'm always building something from nothing - now growvc.com & startupcommons.org.

Besides my own businesses, I have great passion to work with start-up's that have innovative/crazy ideas and passionate & entrepreneurial people with potential to change the world, specially in web & mobile stuff.

Have a graphics designer background and about 15 years of experience in entrepreneurship and general business management, with a good track record for identifying new business opportunities.

My first start-up business VTG, was specialized in mobile phone design covers IE. personalization. Established in 1995 and quickly grew up, to 100 employees with offices in US. Australia and Finland.

After that, have started few other startups. Some have been successful while some have not. For the past 10 years, I have focused to mobile & web businesses.

I like to push good plans into action to get results. Plans are like great ideas, there can be many good ones, but without result oriented execution, those rarely generate any real value.

Experienced in creating and executing new business concepts, developing and managing sales, marketing, operations and teams. International work experience in Europe and USA.
