Andrew Wheeler’s Summary

Andrew is a talented internet solutions developer with strong traditional design skills and particular flair for web and search engine optimisation.

Task focused and very deadline aware but with an ability to accommodate a variety of work practices and styles.

Andrew is able to offer the following skills and services to his clients:

• Proficient with XHTML, CSS and PHP, MySQL database's and hosting platform environments.

• Thorough understanding of organic search engine optimisation, ongoing maintenance, split A/B landing page testing, PPC Adwords advertising and stat reporting.

• Proven track record in developing 'niche' and long tailed keyword based websites, and integrating ways to monetize these sites via affiliate advertising and marketing.

• Comfortable with developing and managing email marketing material. Full understanding of legal spam requirements and currently managing multiple campaigns using third party providers.

• Full understanding of Social Media and custom branding to client requirements.

• In tune with ‘Web 2’ technology, including; RSS integration, Mashups and flash video.

• Awareness of W3C standards, the web accessibility initiative, and website cross browser compatibility.

• Fully proficient in a range of Opensource solutions for businesses, including customising CMS, CRM’s, Wiki’s and E-commerce applications.

• Good client facing skills and comfortable offering technical support for website, email and other internet issues.
