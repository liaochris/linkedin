Matthew Cohen’s Summary

What do I do?

I am currently the Manager of Business Development for the Tax Resolution Institute in Woodland Hills, CA. In addition to managing the office, I am responsible for heading up the marketing division of the Tax Resolution Institute.

The Tax Resolution Institute is the nation’s top tax resolution firm with over 100 years of experience helping taxpayers in trouble with all Federal and State taxing agencies. With a diverse and talented mix of Certified Public Accountants and Tax Attorneys, the Tax Resolution Institute offers the best in initial diagnosis and eventual solution. Through proven strategies and learned expertise, The Tax Resolution Institute can assist you through tax audits, help reduce your tax debt, legally abate penalties, release liens, stop wage garnishments and remove bank levies. The Tax Resolution Institute specializes in tax relief through various means including Offers in Compromise, Installment Agreements, and Currently Non-Collectible status. In addition, we file delinquent tax returns and bring our clients back in compliance and up-to-date.

In addition, we assist individuals and corporations in achieving their highest potential by providing superior accounting, tax and financial planning solutions while upholding generally accepted accounting standards and principles. Once we resolve your tax issues, we can help on your path to future prosperity and success.

What did I do before?

I was the General Manager of a mid-size design/construction company, Romate.
Prior to that I was a Project Manager for Romate. I managed a team of over 15 employees and subcontractors in all aspects of company.

Prior to that I was a Project Supervisor for Romate. I supervised multiple teams of employees and subcontractors on a range of projects.

Prior to that I worked at Wells Fargo Bank.
