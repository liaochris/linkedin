Martin Burns’s Summary

Accomplished talent acquisition expert, with experience across third party and corporate recruiting. Brings a mix of technical, marketing and communications, and recruitment expertise to help companies solve their talent needs. Currently working on several interesting (i. e. challenging) direct recruitment assignments; developing ReferralBonus, a web-based recruiting technology start-up; consulting with companies on their employment branding and recruiting strategies.

Martin Burns’s Specialties:

Talent acquisition strategies and technologies. Design and deployment of highly sticky career sites. Talent assessment, recruitment, and onboarding. Product marketing and management. Business development and sales-oriented marketing campaigns.
