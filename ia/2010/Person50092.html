David Reed’s Summary

David Reed has held various senior management roles in the energy services industry. He brings extensive experience in delivering a comprehensive range of energy efficiency and renewable services for his clients. His experience in developing successful and sustainable projects with specialized financing solutions including Shared Savings, Purchase Power, Off-balance sheet, and Third party lease agreements have led to many notable projects in the Federal, Commercial, and Public markets.

David's solid experience in forging strategic growth and creating a competitive advantage, have led to substantial growth. His background in expanding and driving business into new and emerging markets have led to the multi year award as the top 500 fastest growing, privately held companies in America.
