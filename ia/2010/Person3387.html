Genisi Adriano’s Summary

I’ve been working within the pearl sector with over twenty years of experience representing some big international brands as group purchaser for any type of cultured pearl.
Further I was general manager and product manager one of the most successful Italian manufacturer brand among the jewelry industries.
I’ve an in depth knowledge in the way the goods are produced right to through the wholesale and the retail aspects.

Recently I’ve formed my own business under my own name offering my sourcing service to jewelers across the globe.
What’s Adriano Genisi difference?
Whilst most pearl suppliers are product focused – invariably pushing their own inventories out - we are entirely focused on our customers needs selecting the best quality pieces at the best price.

One key factor in getting the most for our customers is that we regularly place big orders from pearl supplier – this in fact give us the buying power of major chains which we can pass onto our customers even if they placing small orders.

Our knowledge of the market and current trends enables us to identify the best sources to keep our customers ahead in their competition.
