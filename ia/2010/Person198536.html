Bill Swartz’s Summary

Bill Swartz is President of Swartz & Associates, Inc. a retained executive search boutique. The firm works with entrepreneurs, entrepreneurial companies, and their investors to recruit CEOs, CFOs, Board members, and other senior executives in all functional categories from across the US and around the world.

Bill has completed over three hundred searches since 1982, and has developed a reputation for integrity, success, and loyalty, and is singularly unique in his particular blend of passion, intensity, and candor.

EMAIL: bill(at)swartz(dot)com

Bill Swartz’s Specialties:

Retained executive search on behalf of entrepreneurial companies worldwide.
