Kevin Francis’s Summary

Personal profile

I spent my early years as an officer in the British Army developing the finer arts of leadership, people management, personnel development, technical strategy and planning in the IT / telecommunications area. After arriving in NZ 15 years ago, I have been involved in a variety of industries, including telecommunications, internet, IT and the Online / Digital Marketing industries.

The corporate experience has been rewarding with a variety of increasingly senior management roles, culminating in Executive Management roles for Asia Online, Ericsson Enterprise and Online Marketing Consultancy, First Rate and most recently COO and CEO roles at Australian listed digital agency Q Group (ASX:QXQ).


I have a quick, intuitive mind that combines well with a pragmatic and open approach to problem solving. Thinking on your feet skills, honed by years as an officer in the Army in situations as intense as the Gulf War, have served me well in overcoming the trials of corporate life. I'm often described as the "make it happen" person. My drive to succeed has always seen me outperform my peers and exceed my performance goals.

By maintaining humour and a sense of perspective, leading teams through periods of change has been a key success, often repeated. I thrive on the challenges and rewards that are the mainstay of the executive manager’s role and enjoy the variety and intensity that those challenges often represent. Integrity, determination, humour, perspective and passion are traits that I apply whatever I am engaged in.
