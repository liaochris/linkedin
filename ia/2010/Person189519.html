Sibyl Graham’s Summary

Over the past five years I have had the opportunity to experience most aspects of the event planning industry. From my experiences during the grand re-opening of the de Young museum in Golden Gate park to the various events that I produced with Wilsowest, I have developed a keen understand of the meticulous and focused attention that event production requires. I delight in seeing the myriad moving parts come together to provide a flawless and memorable event experience for my guests and clients.

I am available for consulting, logistics, execution and full-service event management.
