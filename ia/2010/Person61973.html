Ed Kraay’s Summary

I am an experienced Agile Coach and Trainer, with 6 years experience mentoring and leading software organizations to high performance.

I have a unique pragmatic, facilitative style that aligns your organization's goals with your product development efforts. I can help your organization reduce the time from concept to cash for new product initiatives, while simultaneously improving quality and team satisfaction. I have experience mentoring executives, vice presidents, directors and development managers in leveraging Lean and Agile methods to deliver breakout products.

I have expertise with both agile and plan driven methodologies. I have lead large, complex Agile programs, using Lean methods to scale Scrum to 5+ teams.

I have broad experience coaching and mentoring software teams in a wide variety of best practices including agile estimation and planning, test driven development, refactoring and configuration management.

I am the founder of the Agile Philanthropy: San Francisco Bay Area Chapter, and a member of the Bay Agile Project Leadership Network (APLN).

Ed Kraay’s Specialties:

Agile Coaching, Agile Training, Agile Tuning, Large Scale and distributed Scrum and XP, blending user experience with Agile, collaborative meeting facilitation; web development, ecommerce, mobile, enterprise software, web analytics, software engineering best practices, estimation, technical writing, identity management.
