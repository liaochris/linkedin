John T. Lawlor’s Summary

Successful entrepreneur, veteran consultant, author and national speaker with over 30 years of experience providing research-based marketing expertise, strategic counsel, and successful results to colleges and universities throughout the United States.

Specific focus of consulting services is in the areas of enrollment management/college admissions/student recruitment, institutional marketing and brand management, and the development of integrated marketing communications using print,web,mobile,and social media.

Founding member of the Forum for Higher Education Marketing, a group of thought leaders dedicated to advancing the theory and practice of marketing for colleges and universities.

John T. Lawlor’s Specialties:

Private colleges & universities, market research, positioning, strategic planning, brand identity and management, development of marketing communications, enrollment management counsel including audits, satisfaction studies, brand strength, non-matriculant/prospective student research, retention surveys, pricing/affordability studies, college admissions, student recruitment, fundraising, social media, publications, web sites, mobile,board retreats, advertising, organizational development

John T. Lawlor’s Experience

  • Founder & Principal

    The Lawlor Group, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1987 — Present (23 years 7 months)

    The Lawlor Group (TLG) is one of the nation's preeminent higher education marketing firms.The focus of TLG's business consulting practice is on five core areas of expertise—market research, brand management, institutional marketing, marketing communications (advertising, print collateral, web sites, e-marketing, social media) and enrollment management (college admissions, student recruitment, and retention). The firm works almost exclusively with private higher education clients throughout the United States.

    The Lawlor Group also publishes The Lawlor Review, a critically acclaimed education marketing journal; Lawlor Perspective, a series of white papers for senior management and trustees; Lawlor Focus, a monthly e-newsletter that explores topics relevant to marketing and communications professionals in higher education; and, The Smarketplace, a blog about issues and topics related to the marketplace and higher education marketing.

    The Lawlor Group co-sponsors the annual Summer Seminar, a leading higher education marketing symposium for chief enrollment officers and senior management of private colleges and universities throughout the United States.
