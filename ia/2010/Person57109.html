Dennis Jenders’s Summary

Dennis Jenders, Digital Strategist, has more than 10 years of professional experience in digital media. His depth and breadth of experience has helped to influence and reinforce his strategic and tactical goals for his clients; while his enthusiasm and innovation are clearly reflected in his implementations and client relationships.

Previously Dennis has applied his innate creativity and technical knowledge as an interactive creative director where his expertise has been showcased and awarded in interactive design, advertising and corporate branding for both local and national clients.

He is a founding board member of the Milwaukee Interactive Marketing Association and has shared his experience through speaking engagements, most recently for Alverno College and Milwaukee Public Schools.
