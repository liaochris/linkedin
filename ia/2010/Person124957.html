LILLY LEE’s Summary

As Director of Development, Los Angeles, I have been introducing, engaging, exposing, and inviting, the Los Angeles industry, talent agencies, companies to expose NFFTY and to get involved.

NFFTY helps enable, nurture, promote and encourage the next generation of filmmakers to inspire, lead and entertain the world with their stories.

We are the largest youth film festival in the country, now a 4-day event in Seattle, WA., showcasing the work and talent of filmmakers who are under 22 years of age.

NFFTY Earth Project: Showcases films about the environment and social justice issues from around the world. We are incorporating a "what you can do" element this year so people can learn about world issues and take action.
