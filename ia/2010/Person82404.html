Heather Garcia, LAC, GCDFI, CWDP’s Summary

Heather Garcia serves as the Counseling & Career Services Coordinator at the University of Arkansas Community College at Batesville. Since joining the institution four years ago, Garcia has designed and implemented a comprehensive Counseling & Career Services program including career development workshops, an internet-based job search system, an annual career fair and personalized counseling services to include disability support and crisis intervention. She collaborates with campus staff and faculty to provide a well-rounded service delivery system which allows for the greatest student participation possible.

Garcia also serves as an Adjunct Instructor for the college teaching the Business Internship course. This is a collaborative program between the student, the College, and business and industry to begin to develop the necessary skills needed to be successful in the work environment. This course requires the student to develop a professional career portfolio and participate in a job-training position with a company where the site provides on-the-job training as an extension of the classroom.

As Career Counselor for Del-Jen, Inc., Garcia oversaw the admissions and career transition services for seventeen counties in Northeast Arkansas. She educated a continuous caseload of up to fifty graduates in areas such as resume writing, effective job search strategies, and proper interview etiquette. Garcia also conducted regularly scheduled presentations to over fifty high schools and other youth serving agencies and partnered with area employers to provide job placement to new program graduates.

Garcia is a highly motivated individual with a passion for helping students reach their goals in life. Through continued education and involvement in professional organizations she works diligently to hone her skills and provide the best service possible.
