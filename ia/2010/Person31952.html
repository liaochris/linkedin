Capt. Mohit Sabharwal’s Experience

  • Proprietor Orion Marine / QA Advisor

    Orion Marine Concepts (Third party for Great Eastern Shipping)

    (Maritime industry)

    March 2006 — July 2008 (2 years 5 months)

    •Involved in Development and Implementation of “IMS” (ISO 9001, EMS 14001 & ISO 18001), including Identification of Risks, Environmental Aspects, Evaluation and Controls.
    •Reviewing SMS for shore Office and Vessels (Oil, Chemical, Gas and Bulk carriers).
    •Conducted Internal audits – ISM, EMS, OHSAS, ISPS and Navigational safety – onboard Oil tankers, Chemical, Gas and Bulk carriers.
    •Conducted Pre-Vetting Inspections and represented Owners during Oil major inspections onboard various ships
    •Monitoring vessels positions including instructing masters and agents.
    •Conducted Internal Audits of the Shore Office to ensure compliance with ISO 9001, EMS 14001 & OHSAS 18001.

    •Represented Owners as Port Captain for Charterers requirement.

    •Safety inspector at Terminal during Gas cargo discharge of LPG Vessels.
    •Attending Superintendent for Crude Tanker’s Tank cleaning operations and Dry Dock Preparations.
    •Dry – Dock Safety Officer on behalf of owners for Aframax & Very Large Crude Carriers.
    •Assisted in inspections and steel calculation for renewal during CAS and CAP surveys.
    •Preparation of Manuals for Chemical Tankers for the Amended IBC and Marpol regulations.
