Marshall Thompson’s Summary

My video production and photography company's motto is "We tell your story - contamos su historia." The skill set I offer works well for virtually any human activity you can imagine, because everyone has a story to tell: the story of your success, the story of how you developed your winning product, invention, idea, concept, machine, song, medicine, airplane, rocket ship, electric motorcycle or clothing line. A "day in the life" documentary about a victim of crime, accident or corporate malfeasance. There is a story everywhere and I am the best guy to help you tell it to the world.

I have been joyfully involved in writing, producing, directing and filming stories about products, persons, things, institutions and personalities for more than 20 years. These stories are distributed to professional news outlets, cable companies, websites, displayed at trade shows, distributed on DVD and shared through YouTube.

Thanks to my American military family moving all the time I was lucky enough to live all over the US and in Paris, France and spent my high school years in Rome, Italy. My interest in all cultures resulted in my Anthropology Degree from San Diego State University. No matter what it is, there is something interesting, vital, passionate in your message. I'm here to tell your great story to the world.

I work with PR professionals on the agency side, independent practitioners as well as with in-house pros.

Marshall Thompson’s Specialties:

The first step is to listen to you as you and your staff introduce me to the broad strokes and relevant details of your enterprise and message. I'm a quick study and right away I begin to see the outline of how to tell your specific story: what are the message points; what NOT to reveal; what are the features and benefits; how your idea or product improves lives, outcomes, solves problems, makes things better for the ultimate consumer.
