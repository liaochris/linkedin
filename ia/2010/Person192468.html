Todd Spraggins’s Summary

todd.spraggins@gmail.com
214.616.8750

Todd Spraggins is an innovative and strategic technology executive. He has a track record of understanding where markets and technologies are heading and picking winning plays ahead of the curve. Through his career Todd has created and deployed leading-edge telecommunications solutions to many of the world’s largest service providers, capturing new revenue and cost reduction opportunities. As an evangelist for what’s possible, he pushes organizations to stretch beyond their current technology and commercial boundaries through strategic partnerships and new business models.

Most recently, as Sr. Director of Technology for the CTO at GENBAND, Todd developed corporate strategies to transform the company beyond legacy voice equipment into a broadband wireless player. Prior to that, he spent 15 years at Nortel in progressive R&D Leadership, Product Management, Engineering and Operations roles. Under Todd’s technical leadership, Nortel delivered the first packetized Mobile Switching Center to over ten million users in Verizon Wireless’ LA market with fewer issues than any previous product introduction. This solution enabled Verizon to avoid more costly network upgrades while allowing Nortel to see close to $10M in new revenue.

Todd has also been active in sustainable development and green practices. He is working with a like-minded group of architects, builders and developers to bring a sustainable and modern residential development to the North Texas area.

Todd is an ITILv3 certified professional and a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt practitioner. He holds a Masters of Science from SMU and a Bachelors of Science and Engineering from Tulane.
