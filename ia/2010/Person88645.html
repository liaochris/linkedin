James Miller’s Summary

In May of 2009 I launched a Business and Social Networking Event Company called Chicago After Work. Chicago After Work has been featured on Fox News Chicago, The Chi Guide, and Examiner.com. With attendance of each event above 200 people and high demand from other cities we have decided to launch in 15 other cities in the next year and half under the name Network After Work. The objective of the company is to provide an event in each city with atmosphere conducive to mingle with other professionals, expand your network, create new business opportunities, and enjoy cocktails in the cities best and most stylish venues. The events offer an open bar and appetizers for a small cover while allowing professionals to check out a venue that would be opening exclusively for the event. The events also offer new or local businesses a chance to put their product in front people with a large disposable income. The next stop will be Detroit in February, LA in March, San Francisco in April, and San Diego in May.

My background includes managing clubs for Jay-Z in NYC, Vegas, and Atlantic City. I also have extensive food and beverage management experience for both Harrah’s and MGM Mirage Casino's in Las Vegas.
