Per Kvalvaag’s Summary

More than 10 years experience as an Advertising Art Director has given me strong skills as a strategic thinker, graphic designer, photographer, typographer and visualizer, working across a wide range of media including TV, cinema, radio, posters, press, point of sale, direct mail, books and packaging as well as interactive media.

I enjoy working by interrogating a problem until it confesses to its solution, and I am fascinated by the many different ways of telling a story or expressing an idea.

Ideally I will be more involved with work that takes seriously - and deals with - the challenges facing our environment, both natural and man made, and our health, both physical and mental.

The internet will bring us many revolutions in the years to come. Big or small, I hope to be part of at least one of them.
