Tim Borchers’s Summary

Since 1984 I have been developing and refining legal solutions and processes to assist clients in fulfilling their goals for the care of their loved ones, their assets and their businesses. We have been working for a solution to the problem of unfunded trusts in our clients' estate plans (causing unnecessary probate) and in 2007 had a break through in our program we call "Review and Refinement," which assures that trusts are funded through regular maintenance. In 2008 we recognized our strength in asset protection and began to emphasize this planning niche, especially in serving business owners and professionals, and high net worth individuals generally. We formalized our outreach to the medical community in 2009 by participating in a national community known as Docworthy, a national community of advisors and service providers catering to the unique needs of physicians and dentists.

Tim Borchers’s Specialties:

We are estate planners, protectors and trustees. We advise, draft and implement plans using trusts and other entities for the estate, for asset protection and for charitable, real estate, and business succession planning and maintenance for total probate avoidance. We regularly serve as Trust Advisors and Trustees to guide or manage trust-owned assets.
