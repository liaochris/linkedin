Artie Isaac’s Summary

Speaker, entrepreneur, teacher, and marketer, I co-founded SpeakerSite (an online social network of public speakers and event planners) and Young Isaac (a marketing consultancy).

Who I help:
1. event and meeting planners (and public speakers), and
2. corporate executives.

How I help them:
1. Through SpeakerSite, I connect event and meeting planners to speakers.
2. Through Young Isaac, I offer a creative brainstorming, innovation, and brand marketing strategy.
...and, as a speaker, I speak and train on creativity, ethics, marketing, and public speaking.

My SpeakerSite profile is at http://www.speakersite.com/profile/artieisaac.

Artie Isaac’s Specialties:

Speaker, keynote speaker, corporate trainer, and teacher.

I speak, teach, and entertain people on issues of creativity, marketing, ethics, and public speaking.
