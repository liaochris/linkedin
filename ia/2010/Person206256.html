Tom Mighell’s Summary

I work with corporations and their lawyers to ensure that their people, processes and technologies will support an efficient, repeatable, and defensible e-discovery process. I assist corporations in assessing their readiness to respond to an electronic discovery obligation, and develop response plans that companies can use to strengthen the credibility of their e-discovery programs.
