Susan Silberisen’s Summary

Talented and accomplished Senior Executive and Corporate Officer, with expertise in operations transformation, technology innovation, program and process development, strong business development, and effective leadership through collaboration and consensus building. Previous experience in ERP and large-scale software implementations, multi-national recruiting and off-shoring, enterprise architecture planning, project management office (PMO) design and build-out, strong IT governance, corporate and non-profit compliance, business continuity, and much more. Skilled agent of change.

★ PROFESSIONAL GOALS

My motivation is to be a leader who serves others by working to build a solid foundation of shared goals. I strive to achieve this form of leadership by listening deeply to understand the needs and concerns of others; by working thoughtfully to help build a creative consensus; and by working together to find additional “right answers” that rise above the we/they negotiations.

My approach to leadership involves a wide-open sharing of information, building common vision, and encouraging self-management with high levels of interdependence. I believe in learning from mistakes, encouraging creative input from all team members, and questioning present assumptions and mental models.

Professionally, I seek a position that can provide the satisfaction of establishing a strong vision and strategy that supports a positive Corporate and global mission.
