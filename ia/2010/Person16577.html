Antonio "Tony" Arocho is committed to serving the legal needs of a wide variety of businesses, trade associations and nonprofit organizations at Arocho Law Office (ALO).

He founded ALO on the premise that clients should be able to find proven, top-quality outside counsel in a more cost-effective setting than large firms. The goal of the the Arocho Law Office is to provide high quality legal services with integrity, timeliness and professionalism.

At ALO, Antonio "Tony" Arocho puts to work his expertise and energy that includes many years of experience as a lawyer, working with nonprofits and trade associations, public servant, federal and territorial court litigator, fundraiser, policy maker, and executive. He seeks to understand not just a clients’ immediate legal needs, but also the vision, philosophy and mission driving their organization or business. He listens carefully to clients, asks the right questions and carefully analyzes the issues. Then, he draws upon his talent, experience and knowledge to respond effectively. In doing so, he is able to offer innovative and individualized solutions to each client’s needs.
