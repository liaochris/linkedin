Shannon Steffen MBA/IT’s Summary

Delivering value to end users and consumers for over thirteen (13) years through user-centered design and social media expertise. I am a SEO/SEM Social Media Consultant with five years of experience in search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), landing page development, keyword analysis, link building strategies, content management, Web 2.0 and social networking strategic planning. My experience is online-focused and always maintains a balance between user experience and building customer relations through such social networking communities as Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, MySpace, Meetup, Flickr, YouTube, StumbleUpon, Digg and Blogging.

Following my MBA in technology management, I delivered results as the e-business technologist during my five years at a search engine optimization, online retail, graphic design and web development company. I have worked in several industries including advertising, government, finance, utilities, education, information technology and retail goods.
