Keith Cooksey’s Summary

Keith is a highly experienced and results driven private equity professional with deep modeling, execution, strategic planning and operational integration skills, who has led multiple private equity transactions in three years of experience.

At North Africa Holding Keith was responsible for all facets of portfolio company acquisition and fund manager selection on companies located in the MENA for the $180M investment arm of KIPCO ($25bn, Member: KSE). He worked intimately with senior managers of portfolio companies to create value by creating and executing on Strategic Planning, Benchmarking, Reporting and Monitoring initiatives.

He completed several high profile privatizations in North Africa in multiple sectors, including SACEM Industries, the leading transformer manufacturer in Tunisia. He worked with management to develop strategic planning capabilities, implement a regular corporate reporting system, generate benchmark metrics, and incorporate global best practices.

Keith also spearheaded fund manager acquisition and the public equities programs. Including manager due diligence, benchmarking and negotiating.

Keith worked in the financial sponsor group at RBC’s Daniels & Associates, the leading global Media and Cable M&A group where he conducted buy and sell side execution support of M&A transactions for media, telecom and technology companies ranging from $20 million to $1 billion in enterprise value.

Keith also worked with West Coast investment bank Mosaic Capital performing M&A advisory and valuation projects on middle market companies. During his MBA, Keith worked with Mexican companies in various corporate development roles.

Keith began his career in derivatives trading and asset management prior to attending business school. He earned his MBA from Thunderbird and BS at Purdue.

Keith Cooksey’s Specialties:

Value creation through portfolio company and fund manager analysis, strategic planning, deal structuring, governance, and financial reporting, monitoring and benchmarking. Highly versed in project management, conducting due diligence, M&A execution and post transaction integration. Adept at creating multivariate valuation models, as well as preparation of internal and external investment documents and corporate communications.
