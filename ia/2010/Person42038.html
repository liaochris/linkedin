Chris Campbell’s Summary

Dear Friend/Acquaintance/Future Fan,

So I have this day job. It's at RSM McGladrey's Real Estate Group, focusing on Due Diligence Transaction Support. We do NOI Verification, Agreed-Upon Procedures and Lease Analysis, among other things. Good people. It's a pretty sweet job. I like it.

By night I'm a indie folk-rock singer/songwriter, recording and playing my songs at live venues around the city. I love it and I don't suck. I'm currently recording my first EP, "Celebrate by Sleeping, Side 2" to be released this summer. It will be the single best and most important piece of music of the year. Guaranteed.

All this talk about me, it's so egocentric. What about you, dear visitor? I want to hear what you think. You can check out my MySpace page (link next to "My Music" above) to hear the early versions of these landmark, instant-classic songs and marvel in their furious beauty.

Best wishes,

Chris
