Julie Deane’s Summary

Jobboo - Your Recruiting Hub on the Web

Built by recruiters for recruiters, Jobboo helps you:

* Get connected with recruiting peers and network easily with agencies
* Fill open positions while minimizing your time-consuming searches
* Find qualified candidates for open positions requiring just the right person

Our vision is to help recruiters and staffing agencies share open positions and potential candidates for available jobs. We do so by reflecting the ideology symbolized by the bamboo plant: longevity.

That's what we want for you – longevity in your business. We connect recruiters together, providing ways to exchange business opportunities and help build long-term success in the recruiting and employment industry.

Put simply, we help you find your bamboo.

Julie Deane’s Specialties:

Jobboo specializes in recruiter networking. We offer a number of ways to connect with others & promote your business:
* Customize client & candidate listings in a single tracking system to share & access information
* Find the right candidate for the job & find the right job for your candidates.
* Use time more efficiently by letting Jobboo manage your database while you manage your business
Jobboo is ready to work for you with 3 flexible ways to improve your recruiting endeavors.
