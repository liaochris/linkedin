Susie Sharp’s Summary

I grow small businesses. Currently I manage a Cleveland area commercial architecture firm licensed in 17 states. On the side, I consult with individuals and small businesses and speak to small groups interested in incorporating a social media strategy. Remember, "the medium is no longer the message; YOU are".

Raised in my family's 4th generation CLE company, City Blue Printing Co., I have an immense pride in this city, its people and possibilities.

In the 80's I quadrupled a mid-sized commerical architectural firm in 5 years. Since 2005, I have increased the billings of my current employer by 67%, while bringing the firm current on state, county, and city financial obligations. Run Quickbooks Premier, handle the CEO's finances, all A/P, A/R.

Susie Sharp’s Specialties:

Well-heeled in social media strategy, content management, and using Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and other platforms for business. While I believe in fluid thinking and am an early adopter, traditional marketing, advertising, communications and public relations still form the backbone of any successful strategy. Those who do not remember the past are destined to repeat it. Formerly in IT, I have all the standard MS Office skills as well.
