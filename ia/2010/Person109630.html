Jeff Schmitz’s Summary

Engineering companies often manufacture solutions looking for problems. I deliver new revenue streams for manufacturers by strategically and organically building bridges to prospects and customers with the business problems that are solved with my employers core competencies.

Following this creed I've built partnering relationships with valued customers---leading manufacturing companies and automation providers--- in North America, Europe, China, Japan and SE Asia.

I plan to continue to develop new business for high-integrity solution providers and to partner with innovative engineering companies delivering best-in-class revenue-generating and cost-saving solutions. Technology expertise includes machine vision, traceability, industrial networks and wireless communications.

Jeff Schmitz’s Specialties:
