Jeff Erwin’s Summary

Accomplishments
- 30+ years experience in high-tech computer and network industry in President/CEO role creating world-class products and services in network management.
- Performed consulting roles mentoring startup CEOs and serving on startup boards.
- Served at VP level of two different public companies
- CEO of four different high-tech companies through successful acquisitions.
- Responsible for production and shipping of numerous award-winning management applications over last 25 years
- Selected as one of the top 25 industry drivers in networking and management markets in 1997
- Most recent focus in wireless LAN, mesh networking and last-mile technologies.
- 7 patents filed:
• Network Analysis and correlation of Frame Relay Circuits
• Dynamic Egress Routing Through A Single Default Gateway Machine in a Multi-hop Mesh
• Distributed, database driven DHCP server for peer-to-peer networks
• Automated Internet Egress Selection Using Hop Limited Broadcasts in a Mesh
• A method of establishing a wireless mesh network using multiple frequency bands to allow early mesh formation given sparse participation
• Using broadband connectivity as a method for expanding the range of a mesh network
• Self configuration of network management reporting infrastructure using peer to peer graphs

Jeff Erwin’s Specialties:

Wireless mesh technologies, peer-to-peer networking, wireless networking, network and systems management, startup experience (CEO/Founder of four successful startups), VC experience (6 rounds raised)
