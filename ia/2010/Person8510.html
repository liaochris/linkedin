Robert Altchiler’s Summary

Boutique law firm specializing in white collar criminal and corporate investigations and trials, entertainment, tax, IP, business, corporate, media, commercial and related matters. We handle all federal prosecutions and investigations throughout the country, as well as state prosecutions and investigations in New York. We also provide litigation counselling to other trial lawyers, assisting them in developing trial strategies and tactics, and helping them formulate arguments. We aid clients in business development and regulatory compliance and with issues relating to entertainment, IP. media, tax, corporate and a wide variety of other areas. Mr. Altchiler is also Of Counsel to Helaine Brick-Cabot, Attorney at Law, a preeminent business law firm.

We specialize in helping clients make the right choices no matter how difficult the circumstances. We are an extraordinarily detail oriented practice, and demand of ourselves the necessary ability to see five moves ahead of the situation.We have had remarkable success in preventing clients from being charged in federal and state investigations where avoiding those charges seemed nearly impossible.We believe in moving aggressively and quickly upon the first notice of any problem in our client's businesses, whether a medical practice, law practice, securities firm, or other business.
Our international contacts and relationships allow us to comprehensively protect out clients' interests globally.

Robert Altchiler’s Specialties:

Representing targets, subjects and witnesses in criminal investigations, conducting corporate and asset investigations; business and compliance counselling in entertainment, tax, IP, commercial and other areas; full service criminal defense trials and appeals.
