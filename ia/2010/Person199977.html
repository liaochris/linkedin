Taryn Pisaneschi’s Summary

Ms. Pisaneschi is an accomplished public speaker, a trainer on business networking, referral marketing and social media marketing. She is not new to the social media arena + is a true social media pioneer, having been immersed in social media + internet marketing since 1992.

In 2000, Ms. Pisaneschi received a B.S. degree in Public Relations from the Boston University School of Communications. Upon graduation, she worked with the United Way of Asheville, NC to increase donations at a time when the organization was experiencing a significant drop in funding. During her successful tenure there, donations from her company base tripled as result of her innovative use of public speaking + new media marketing. She later moved to Atlanta + served as a legislative assistant at the Georgia State Assembly in the State Senate for 2 terms.

Ms. Pisaneschi joined Lenox Chem-Dry in 2002 with the goal of increasing market share + brand awareness. Using non-traditional marketing methods, she helped the company quadruple its market share in 18 months. She went on to help build a client database of approx. 4,000 clients prior to the sale of the company in 2008. During her tenure, Lenox Chem-Dry received the Consumer's Choice Award in Atlanta for 5 consecutive years.

In March of 2007, Ms. Pisaneschi established Women Intelligently Networking (WIN), a community of business women that supports the success of professional entrepreneurial women through various events, luncheons, keynote speakers and professional development and business skills workshops. WIN! has gained regional + national attention.

In April of 2009, Ms. Pisaneschi + 3 others formed the RMJ Group LLC and built and launched the prototype for a future franchise system, LeatherSmiths, a premier service-based business. She serves as Exec VP of Marketing.

Taryn Pisaneschi’s Specialties:

Workshop facilitator on business growth, social media and relationship marketing strategies for small businesses and entrepreneurs. She educates on how to leverage both networking and online social media, Social networking educator providing workshops and public speaking, branding/identity and marketing thinktank, extreme "out of the box" thinker, extremely resourceful and detailed researcher...and yes, an expert networker.
