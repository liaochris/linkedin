Geir Bækholt’s Summary

Organiser, inventor, strategist, evangelist and open source software developer.

President of the Plone foundation, a non-profit foundation for the protection and promotion of Plone - web content management system.

Also director of Jarn - a wonderful small consulting company where happy expert developers build awesome high-end intranets, websites and web-applications for our customers — with a focus on social return and free software.

Also an enthousiastic software-designer, trying to squeeze in some time to help shape the next couple of versions of Plone and actually write some free software in between all the management and cultivating of organisations.

Half people-person, half hardcore old-style geek.

12 years experience building advanced web-applications, predominantly with the Zope application server and Python. Key developer in the Plone Open Source Content management system.
