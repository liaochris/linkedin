Jim Green’s Summary

Jim Green (James K. Green) is a senior-level communications strategist and manages internal communications for Brookhaven National Laboratory, one of ten national laboratories overseen and primarily funded by the Office of Science of the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE). Jim draws from a unique blend of government, not-for-profit and corporate public relations allowing a diverse perspective in communications planning to target and engage key stakeholder groups.

Prior to Jim's current leadership role at Brookhaven Lab, he was an internal and external global communications leader for Honeywell International Inc.'s Honeywell Life Safety, Honeywell Scanning & Mobility, and Honeywell Security Group businesses, and director of internal communications for global electronic commerce and payments leader First Data Corp.

Jim is also an experienced television reporter, including assignments with "BusinessNow" on WABC-TV and more than a decade with Cablevision Systems Corp./News 12 Networks in the New York television market.

In the community, Jim serves as a trustee, administrative council member and lay reader for Bayport United Methodist Church.
