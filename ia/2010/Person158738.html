Pam Baker’s Summary

A prolific and versatile writer, my published credits include numerous cover stories for international, national and regional media from women's and general interest to finance, business and technology magazines, online content and newspapers; analytical studies on technology; and, eight books. Member National Press Club, Society of Professional Journalists and the Internet Press Guild. 2004 nominee for the Templeton Cambridge Journalism Fellowship in Science and Religion (UK). Wrote and produced an award-winning documentary on paper-making.

I am an expert level SEO (search engine optimization) writer. Also, I am a highly skilled web curator and news aggregator..

I have extensive experience as a contracted analyst for research firms and as a consultant/strategist for a variety of businesses seeking to develop a sharp competitive edge.

My interests include finding a literary agent to represent a book property. I am seeking freelance writing assignments for mainstream media or corporations. Also interested in building my contacts and expert sources data base for news stories.

Previously owned and operated a marketing, advertising and PR firm in Columbus, Georgia for 11 years. The firm’s client list included Mead, Pratt & Whitney, Disney On Ice, TSYS, Johnston Industries, Diaz-Verson Capital Investments, and other high-profile clients.
