Julie Welch’s Summary

Julie Welch, is currently Managing Associate with New England Financial, a Met Life company, in Houston, Texas.

Three Riverway, Suite 900
Houston, TX 77056
713-963-4152
jwelch1@houston.nef.com

Prior to joining New England Financial, she was Vice President/Career Development Supervisor at Legacy Financial Partners, a Guardian Life Insurance Company affiliated agency, in Houston, Texas.

Julie was a Field Vice President for American Express Financial Advisors, from 2002-2006, in Baltimore and Houston where she became the youngest Field Vice President to be promoted in American Express history. During her 9 year tenure with the company (1999-2007), Julie received multiple honors and accolades, including the Mercury Award, President’s Award and the 1st and 2nd Year Top Achiever Awards.

Julie has also served as an adjunct professor at the University of Houston in 2004-2005, where she taught Introduction to Selling.

In addition to her extensive experience within the financial services field, she also has worked in the private business sector. Julie is currently President of Jane Content, a copywriting and ghostwriting company based in Houston, TX. Before taking her position with Jane Content, Julie was President of McCraw’s Confections, a manufacturing corporation that made fun, retro-chic candy.

Julie is an active leader in the Houston Junior League, Alpha Chi Omega alumnae, Houston Downtown Alliance, the Houston Museum of Art's Art Crowd and is a member of both NAIFA and GAMA. She currently serves as an Advisory Board member for Heroes for Children, a Texas based non-profit agency serving families with children battling cancer.

She has previously served on the Executive Steering Committee for the Houston Downtown Alliance – Emerging Leaders, as well as the Houston Chamber of Commerce, and the Greater Baltimore Committees.
