Raffaele Nicolussi’s Summary

Researcher in the Multimedia Communication Department.

Researcher in the fields of Information Mining, Machine Learning, Mobile Device Interface, Data Mining and Search Engines.

Digital Television analyst and scientific coordinator for the GLAD 2 of “Ambiente Digitale” a working group for the rollout of the Digital Terrestrian Television in Italy with participants from different society like Sun, Bull, TeleSpazio, IBM, Wind, ACI and others.

Project analyst and developer of SPOCk, a web based CMS system (PhP+MySQL) for the administration and maintenance of projects and human resources.

Researcher in the field of accessibility of the e-learning.

Project of an European telecommunication monitoring system for the Italian Ministry of Communication

Application Developer, Database Administrator, and Project Manager.
Actually interested in developing application and definitions of standards for the digital terrestrial television.
Also working on the developing of guide line for the accessibility of the e-learning.
