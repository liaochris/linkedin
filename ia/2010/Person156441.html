Nicholas Senske’s Summary

I am a designer, teacher, and researcher specializing in procedural literacy and software culture in architectural education. Interested in developing best practices and principles for designs that bridge information and the built environment and educating tomorrow's architects in the same.

Nicholas Senske’s Specialties:

3d modeling, architectural design, graphic design, graphics programming, design education, software training, design ethics, web design, branding and identity

Proficient in a wide range of design software, scripting, and programming languages.

Taught courses in: 3ds Max, Rhinoceros, Processing, AutoCAD, AutoLISP, Photoshop, Illustrator

    (Educational Institution; 10,001 or more employees; Higher Education industry)

Lastly, I taught a course of my own design focusing on procedural media using the Processing programming language.
