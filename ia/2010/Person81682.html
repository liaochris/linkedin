Henry Tsai’s Summary

Graduate of The Wharton School with a B.S. in Economics and over 6 years of market research experience. Solid expertise in the design, execution and analysis of consumer research, both quantitative and qualitative, to inform and drive critical business decisions. Extensive experience in managing teams and working collaboratively with diverse departments and functional roles.

Strong command of a wide variety of research techniques including advertising research, concept testing and product development, brand equity tracking, customer satisfaction, creative testing and attitude and usage studies. Solid understanding of advanced analytic methodologies to guide research on pricing, feature optimization, discrete choice modeling and segmentation.

Henry Tsai’s Specialties:

Seasoned in working across a wide variety of industries including automotive, pharmaceutical, consumer packaged goods, healthcare, financial services, home entertainment, video games and telecommunications/mobile and in emerging industries including digital media, social networks, and mobile content.
