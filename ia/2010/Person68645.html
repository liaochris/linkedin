Fabien Gueret-Jullien’s Summary

Fabien Gueret-Jullien is a financial risk professional with 14 years of capital markets exposure. He has experience in market, liquidity, counterparty and credit risk. He has worked and managed teams at preeminent investment banks in London and Tokyo.

He has been through many financial crises from which he has learned valuable lessons. He is dynamic and efficient communicator. He relies on a very strong quantitative background and commanding data management skills.

He recently founded TerraMollis in order to supply his expertise and that of others to small to medium organisations looking for cost-effective solutions to their risk management problems.
