Ann Hanson’s Summary

Ann Hanson is recognized as a global business leader building sales and profits through break-through innovation and streamlined operations. In both corporate and consulting roles, her skills extend beyond her first passion – marketing – to include strategic planning, sales, engineering, sourcing, and operations. Her track record is distinguished by P&L success across consumer packaged and durable goods categories, as well as work in automotive, transportation, oil and gas, and alternative energy sectors. Currently, Ann is driving smart, bold marketing initiatives delivering bottom-line impact through her consulting practice, brannds.

Dynamic, forward-thinking, and goal-oriented, Ann offers sharp consumer insights grounded in classical marketing experience. Attuned to subtle market shifts, Ann leverages her perpetual curiosity to uncover trends and opportunities, creating concepts with distinct benefits for long-term commercial viability. Time after time, Ann has directed new product development and process implementation for sustainable business growth.

Ann’s marketing muscle in brand strategy and communications has been celebrated by numerous awards, including the American Marketing Association’s prestigious Edison Award, the Gold EFFIE for advertising, and Fortune’s Top Ten New Products of the Year.

Ann’s international experience is just as impressive as her work in the US. Following achievements in North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific, Ann solidified her global savvy with expatriate roles in Hong Kong and Singapore, enhanced by travel to more than 50 countries and territories.

Throughout her career, Ann has succeeded in large and small enterprises, leading and empowering diverse teams across business functions, drawing on the best of skills to invigorate and enrich the workplace. Always straight-forward as an agent of change, Ann enjoys the challenge of growing sales and profits in tough categories, and now in tough times.
