Jamie Owen’s Summary

Jamie is an interaction designer adept at eLearning, visual communication, UXD, usability, and information architecture. He has over 14 years experience creating successful customer facing and internally-directed solutions in web use strategies, instructional design, visual design, and branding. In short, he uses functional creativity to address business goals. He is at home on a design team, using PM structure for both understanding the larger initiative as well as defining tactical requirements. Clients have ranged from regional companies to the federal government to global enterprises.

His academic focus is on multiculturalism in design across the usability and eLearning fields--learning experiences in which technology empowers the user.

Jamie Owen’s Specialties:

UXD, IXD, eLearning, instructional design, usability, graphic design, information architecture.
