Gordon Shaw cmc’s Summary

Gordon is an experienced Certified Management Consultant.

Gordon's areas of expertise include: Management Consulting, Sustainability and Green Consulting, Project Management, Change Management, and Transportation Consulting.

Gordon has many years experience working in diverse areas including the central and local government sectors, transport , insurance, sales, banking, law, and debt collection fields.

Gordon has worked with organisations in Australia, Fiji, Singapore, Japan and the USA.

Gordon has operational management expertise, general management skills, effective relationship building skills and the ability to build beneficial working relationships. He has contributed to strategic planning in both public and private sector organisations and has been responsible for the operational delivery of those plans including building organisational capability for change and successful organisational change and restructuring initiatives. Gordon has proven successful staff and line management skills as well as project management skills, with an ability to work strategically and to work as part of an effective team. Gordon’s coaching and mentoring skills have been applied in a number of assignments.

Gordon has proven experience at building successful relationships at all levels of the state sector and business community. He has managed governmental relationships from within the public sector and also as a service provider to the public sector. This allows Gordon to frame his dealings with public sector organisations from both perspectives. This leads to outstanding outcomes. Gordon has established relationship networks throughout Asia-Pacific and has facilitated cross cultural relationship building with Japanese and Fijian stakeholder groups.
