Roman Tsunder’s Summary

Thanks for checking out my profile. My personal and professional passion is the media industry. I have been at the forefront of the digital communications shift that includes industries such as social media, mobile, alternative and out-of-home media, video, gaming, action sports and entertainment.

I have been the Founder and President of Access 360 Media, Inc. (access360media.com), that started with one venue and today is the nation’s largest in-mall network, comprised of leading malls such as Simon Property Group and largest in-stadium digital network, comprised of 46 venues including Yankee Stadium, Madison Square Garden, Dodger Stadium, Staples Center, United Center and more, reaching over 100MM consumers each month at the point of purchase and the point of passion.

In 2009, I created the PTTOW! Media Summit, an invite only event bringing together 45 top CMO level execs from the world’s most innovative companies to discuss the future of the media industry, representing over $12 Billion in media spending across every major industry category, including: wireless (AT&T), clothing (Quiksilver); gaming (Activision), social media (Facebook), technology (HP), online video (YouTube), beverage (Pepsi), athletes (Kelly Slater) and the US Government.

I have extensive operating and private equity experience specializing in mobile, social media, digital technology and consumer products.
