Gary Matthews’s Summary

Gary is a Vancouver-based business development specialist assisting businesses establish a voice with a combination of branding, internet marketing, SEO, social media technologies and traditional print strategies. Gary has led commercial software strategy, design and development activities since 1990. His experience includes conception, production and execution of custom solutions for forest product and legal software ventures. Gary managed his own software development company for a decade before turning his attention to assisting other businesses. He studied Business Administration at UC Santa Barbara and Ryerson University and also earned a Diploma in Instructional Media while teaching Computer Programming in the Business School at Ryerson.
