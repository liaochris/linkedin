Linda Zimmer’s Summary

Do you want a lower stress environment?
Does the prospect of discovering your life’s purpose and passion excite you?
Are you willing to take on new concepts and stretch beyond your comfort zone?

Linda’s purpose is to connect people to concepts, ideas and other people so they can go off and create something that is bigger then was possible before. Their process and the results her client’s create allow pure freedom and total self-expression.

Since 1999 Linda has been coaching small business owners and professionals who want more time to do the things they love.

Working together in partnership, she takes them through her “Discover and Live Your Life Purpose” process. Then helps her clients design a balanced business and personal life by incorporating their purpose, strategies, methods and practices that make them more effective, more productive, and more successful.

Clients who work with her are somewhat lost, out of alignment or unhappy in their job, business or at home. They sense that knowing their purpose will help them distinguish what they could be doing to bring back freedom, self-expression, alignment & happiness in all areas of their life.
