Victor Terpstra’s Summary

Over 10 years of international senior consulting experience analyzing, architecting and delivering advanced, knowledge-based business automation projects with a proven record of accomplishment in agile project management methodologies. Strong analytical and leadership abilities combined with excellent interpersonal and communication skills that allow me to deliver cost-effective technological solutions to complex business issues.

Highlights: Vessel scheduling for the Panama Canal • Semiconductor supply chain optimization for Intel • Simulation and scheduling of a nuclear waste treatment plant • High-speed train scheduling for the Russian rail roads • Monitoring and decision support for the UK national gas distribution network • Water distribution optimization in Florida • Automated pricing tool for a European-wide self-storage operation • Oil field operation analysis for Shell Brunei.

Exceptionally strong analytic skill set. Proven track record of rapidly delivering both practical and well-founded solutions for complex projects. Very strong academic background, in particular in Systems, Control, Optimization and Constraint Propagation. Extensive Object-Oriented design and programming experience.
