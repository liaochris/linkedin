Kelly Fitzgerald’s Summary

For years I have been photographing the California Eastern Sierras and the Hawaiian Islands. I began this series of work largely out of a desire to preserve a beauty still largely unhampered by commercial development. I also recognize that progress is encroaching and it will not last.
Today, more than ever we need to have a true appreciation for the wonder and beauty of the natural world.

I wanted to reach beyond what the color photograph conveys, so I chose to document both works in black and white Kodak HIE infrared film to reveal the dreaminess and magical quality each place holds.

My photographs have appeared in numerous exhibits in various art galleries around the world. My work has also won several awards including The Golden Light Award and the IPA Lucie Award. Limited edition prints are available as archival fiber base silver gelatin and chromogenic – Type C prints. Each print is scrupulously hand printed by a master printer.

Kelly Fitzgerald’s Specialties:

I specialize in black and white landscapes.
