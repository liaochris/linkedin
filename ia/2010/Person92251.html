Jason Cronkhite’s Summary

As a professional marketer and sales leader, Jason Cronkhite has worked with some of the world’s largest brands to some of the most cutting-edge start-up companies. From organizing and promoting world class sporting events to managing international marketing campaigns, Jason’s experience provides a Swiss Army Knife like depth of knowledge. His diverse industry background is comprised of professional sports, beverage marketing, telecommunications, advertising and branding, and streaming media.

Jason specializes in creating metric-driven marketing and optimal sales strategies that drive organizational growth. Fueled by his determination, creativity and passion with a unique ability to find connections between seemingly disparate phenomena you can expect that any project Jason is involved with to succeed.

Jason grew up as a competitive soccer player in South Texas where his collegiate team, UIW, competed in 4 National Championships during his tenure. He enjoys reading about and studying business leadership, technology innovation, is a distance runner and active member of his church Grace Memorial. He holds an MBA in Organizational Leadership and a BS in Sports Management. He resides in the Austin-San Antonio, TX area with his wife Julie and two children Kendall and Connor.
