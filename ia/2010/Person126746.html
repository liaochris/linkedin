Liz duToit’s Summary

At my heart, I'm a coach and trainer. I love learning, and then turning around and sharing with others what I've learned. I've put that passion to use in call center training, process improvement training, and most recently in career services training. Currently I help individuals and businesses leverage the power of LinkedIn to make new connections, research job potentials, and perform competitive intelligence on companies they're pursuing and who the competition might be.

I have recently introduced an interview coaching package which includes:

Review of job description, company website and resume

Post interview written evaluation

I'm currently meeting with leaders in the career services field and would love an introduction to those you know in training or career services.
