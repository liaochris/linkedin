Timothy Newhard’s Summary

I got started in organizational development, specifically in learning and development. I am currently in the business development and project management side of marketing.

My first job out of school was to assist the Learning and Development Dir. of a large regional construction company. We ran the corporate university.

After about a year I was promoted to a managerial role in order to oversee the implementation of a learning management system (LMS). I had ultimate accountability over the project.

Our LMS won the 2009 AGC B.I.T.S. award for the best IT solution of the year for the entire construction industry.

In addition to this project, I oversaw many learning and development projects. In short, my job was to conduct ongoing needs analyses throughout the organization, identify skill gaps, inefficiencies, liabilities, and mitigate them with the right type of learning.

After more than 2 years at Clyde Companies I was laid off.

After a short time I was picked up by the marketing services vendor I used for several projects, Serfwerks. My job is to help our client base grow through the execution of our sales and marketing strategies. My secondary job is to team up with clients in order to get their projects done correctly and quickly.

I have also managed the creation and launch of Serfwerks' consulting and training services.
