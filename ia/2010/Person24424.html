Dejan Bizinger’s Summary

I'm Web professional with more than 10 years experience, mainly in Internet marketing and other areas of e-business. I'm Business Development Manager at Rate Solutions, responsible for emerging markets. Projects that I lead or I'm involved as a coordinator include Karike.com (in Top 10 in Serbia, 60 000 daily visits, 2 million daily page views), other Rate sites in ex-YU region, establishing Rate sites presence on the new markets and taking care of new projects, like a social web site aimed for classmates, ex-colleagues and old friends Znanci.com. In one sentence - everything that increases the community and popularity of Rate Solutions web sites.

I also have great experience in email marketing and web portals because I worked for two companies in email marketing field, published several tech email newsletters and was one of the key people for one of the most visited web portals in Southeast Europe B92.net.

Beside that, I run a non-profit, award-winning project called Humanost.org which collects pleads for help so other people can help people in need. My professional experience starts from 1999. with launching Serbia OnLine web portal, one of the first general portals in Serbia, then publishing group of Internet email newsletters, working for software companies SKYLIST Solutions and Infacta Ltd. Prior to my current job, I was web manager at B92.net, the most visited web site in Serbia with an average of 120 000 daily unique visitors and 1 000 000 daily page views.

Beside my regular 9-5 job, I'm often contacted by companies to consult them related to quality Internet presence and I often give speaches at conferences and webinars. I have thourough knowledge of the best practices for successful Internet presence and about web markets in Central and Eastern Europe.

Beside Serbian, my second language is Hungarian and also I speak fluently English.
