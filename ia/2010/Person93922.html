    January 1986 — January 2002 (16 years 1 month)

    Integrity, Service & Excellence.

    JEFF WEILLER President of New York Commercial Lending NYCL specializing in the acquisition of seller financed & non-performing notes. with our affiliate we have closed some 30,000 deals valued at $500,000,000. NYCL has available funds to close deals quickly; Mr. Weiller has some 30 years of experience in the commercial real estate and capital business which includes secured financing deals ranging in size to $100 million, investment property sales, landlord representation, and tenant representation. Prior to forming New York Commercial Lending, Mr. Weiller specialized in the World Trade Center submarket of New York City and was a business partner with one of the top dealmakers/leasing brokers in the country. Together they completed many major transactions including: the sale of the Colgate Development Site; with 6 million square feet of development rights to Hartz Mountain/Leonard Stern, two 1 million sq. ft. leases with Prudential Securities and Paine Webber Securities for which entire office buildings were built and rebuilt around, the sale of 100,000 sq. ft. of air rights on Broadway to the Related Companies and represented VNU and Thomson Reuters Publishing, both major publishing companies, nationally in their multi-million square foot portfolios of real estate. Mr. Weiller has had daily dealings with major Manhattan and nationwide property owners and completed many tax lien foreclosures in New Jersey for his own account.

    Prior to forming New York Commercial Lending, Mr. Weiller was President of Jeffrey Weiller and Associates (2002-2007), a commercial real estate brokerage and advisory company active in the New York City region. Previously, Mr. Weiller was a Vice President at both CB Richard Ellis (CBRE) and Joseph Hilton Associates which was acquired by CBRE in 1996; . Weiller was involved in structuring capital equipment financings ranging in size to $100 million.
