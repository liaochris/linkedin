Lysle C Wickersham’s Summary

Lysle has enjoyed 25 years of success in advertising including brand development, general advertising, digital marketing, direct marketing, and business development. He’s an award-winning creative director who’s run ad agencies, technology companies and co-founded an investment bank- a truly right and left brained thinker. Lysle is a Partner at Rockefeller Consulting/Insight Capitalists. Insight Capitalists is the cornerstone of Rockefeller Consulting, which combines proven business practices with leading-edge technologies to accelerate growth and create sustainable value for its clients. Its focus is on addressing both short-term revenue goals and long-term value objectives. Insight Capitalists provides tools to identify challenges within an enterprise, align businesses strategically at all levels, optimally connect with customers and identify and leverage untapped opportunities to realize a business’ full potential.

Lysle built Wickersham Hunt Schwantner, a highly regarded full-service agency. Lysle was a pioneer of brand-driven, discipline/channel-neutral marketing. WHS was sold (along with parent agency Arnold Worldwide) in 2000 with $170MM in billings. Lysle created brand strategies and integrated communications for Bose, Volkswagen, Media One, Fidelity Investments, IBM, Arbella Insurance, Blue Cross/Blue Shield, New England Financial, Steinway & Sons, Ocean Spray, Friendly Ice Cream, NEC, Putnam Securities and Boston Acoustics. Lysle has been recognized in awards shows for both creativity and for work that produces quantifiable results. Lysle has been honored at The Effies, The International New York Festivals, The Mobius Awards, CA, the Hatch Awards, and the International Echo Awards, to name a few.

Lysle is a co-founder of Briggs Capital, LLC, an investment banking and mergers & acquisitions firm where he remains on the Board of Directors. Lysle serves on the Advisory Board of DesignBlox, LLC. and PerkStrret Financial, LLC.
