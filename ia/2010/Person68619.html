Fabian Low’s Summary

Fabian has been involved in a wide array of landscape architectural projects throughout Auckland. He has been involved in many of these projects that have progressed from design inception onto completion and also winning institute awards.

Fabian is currently enrolled in the University of Auckland studying towards a Masters of Urban Design. He intends to use this academic experience to generalise into the other fields that are affiliated with Landscape Architecture such as Architecture and Planning.
