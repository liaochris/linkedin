Riggs Eckelberry’s Summary

A co-founder of OriginOil and one of the inventors of the Company’s breakthrough technology, Riggs Eckelberry brings his veteran technology management skills to the alternative energy sector. He is involved full-time in driving OriginOil to market success.

Riggs Eckelberry founded TechTransform in February 2001. A technology operator with a strong focus on sales and marketing, he advises in the firm's engagements.

Riggs Eckelberry’s Specialties:

Transformative technology leadership - the ability to intelligently iterate and maintain discipline to get a market success.
