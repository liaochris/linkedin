Alessandro Perilli, CISSP’s Summary

Alessandro Perilli is an independent analyst with a major focus on cloud computing, virtualization and IT security markets.
Alessandro has been well known in the virtualization industry since 2003 thanks to his website virtualization.info, which attracts more than 10,000 visitors per day.

Alessandro works as a trusted advisor for multinational companies, large system integrators, venture capital firms and IT vendors.
His customers are in in North America, Europe and Asia, and include Fortune 1000 and Global Fortune 2000 companies, civil and military government agencies, and some of the most relevant market players in the IT industry.

He provides a broad range of strategic and technical research analysis, including company profiling, market trend forecasting, competitive strategy planning, product evaluations and comparisons.

Alessandro has served as a keynote speaker at international conferences arranged by some of the most influential analyst firms worldwide, such as IDC and Burton Group:
http://www.virtualization.info/about#alessandroperilli

Since 2003, his website, virtualization.info, has been recognized as an authoritative and unbiased source for news and insights about virtualization vendors and technologies in the hardware, OS and application virtualization markets.

Available in English and Japanese, it has over 250,000 pageviews / month and over 14,000 subscribers / day.

Linked-to by more than 100,000 websites, virtualization.info is mentioned on virtualization vendors websites (including VMware, Microsoft, Citrix, Parallels, etc.), and by popular news publications published by leading media companies.

virtualization.info also counts more than 25% of Fortune 100 companies among its readers.
