Shubho Ghosal, Ph.D.’s Summary

More than 10 years of experience in business intelligence (BI) and data warehousing (DW) architecture, leadership and management. Focused on assisting organizations in formulating their DWBI strategy, gathering requirements effectively, planning enterprise-wide deployments, developing processes and projects, and translating requirements to robust designs and architecture.

As Principal at Collaborative Consulting, responsible for providing strategy, planning and implementation guidance to clients on DWBI.

As Technical Architect at Harte Hanks Inc., a marketing solutions provider, provided leadership in the design and development of CRM and BI applications for large ($1B+) clients.

Led the team that delivered Cognos Performance Applications for the Oracle eBusiness Suite. Also participated in an R&D group at Cognos innovating new products.

Provided architectural guidance to Kronos Inc., a leader in the Workforce Management software space, in bringing their data warehouse design to a best practice standard.
