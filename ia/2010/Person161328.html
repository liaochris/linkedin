Paul Richardson’s Summary

I focus on holistic financial planning and specialise in pension, investment and tax planning where I help clients achieve their objectives. This may range from purchasing commercial property within a pension scheme or simply consolidating old pension schemes and building a well diversified portfolio. I am also qualified to advise on final salary schemes and explain options to clients in a clear way that is easy to understand. I use this experience and expertise to work for The Pension Advisory service on a voluntary basis. I am a firm advocate of mordern portfolio theory and as such adopt strategic asset allocation into portfolios for my clients.

All my clients work hard to earn their wealth and want to preserve it without paying tax – legally. There are many ways to do this and I work on various opportunities to realise this.

I have a true belief that everyone needs to plan financially and it should not be left to chance – it is my role to give you the information to make informed decisions in a way that you will understand and work with you in a true partnership to help you achieve your financial goals.

I work with many different types of clients but they all have one common theme – they take their financial planning seriously and are all working towards their individual goals. I also work for other IFAs in assisting with solutions for their clients, for example, managing sizeable retirement funds and finding the most tax efficient ways to take their benefits
