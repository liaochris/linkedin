Stephen Clarke’s Summary

British EdUKation Centre philosophy is to provide the best professional service to enable Ukrainian / International students to enter the leading UK universities and top Boarding Schools.

We provide a personal service and care about the educational aspirations of students through to successful graduation.

Higher Education in the UK is recognised as a global leader and Ukrainian students deserve the best education. Employers throughout the world, particularly in Ukraine, are eager to recruit graduates who obtain high-quality degrees from leading UK universities.

I have extensive management experience in the private and public sectors where I have acquired valuable knowledge and skills in UK education.

UKRAINE 2000-2003: Active member of the Supervisory Board for the Ukraine Social Investment Fund (World Bank - $70M loan). Active member of the Donetsk Steering Group and Lugansk Steering Committee with Governors Anatoliy Blizniuk and Aleksandr Yefremov.

Leader of a UK study tour to British Parliament and UK regional governments to demonstrate best practice, and best models for Ukraine - tour members included Ukrainian Cabinet Ministers, Regional Governors, Mayors and World Bank team. I was co-team leader with United Nations Chief Adviser (New York) on UN / UK design mission for joint British Government / UNDP programme.

As a chief executive officer, I was responsible for the overall activities of a development and construction company, with over eighty employees, including professional staff of architects and engineers. Responsible for financial administration and reported to a board of directors on financial budgets and expenditure and prepared monthly management reports.

Responsible for training and development plans both in this organisation and in my role at the British Embassy in Ukraine and in other UK Government positions.

Employed in football management & FA coaching at semi-professional level.
