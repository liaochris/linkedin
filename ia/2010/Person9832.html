Amol Wayangankar’s Summary

Mr. Wayangankar is an Director in the Ventyx Advisors Fuel Markets Practice. For over 7 years, Mr. Wayangankar has focused on natural gas & oil markets, asset valuations, commercial negotiations and financing of energy infrastructure projects. His expertise is in natural gas markets (including LNG) and other fuels.

Mr. Wayangankar has performed numerous fuel-related due diligence reviews of independent power generation facilities, LNG projects, natural gas storage and pipeline infrastructure projects for major financial institutions in support of financing Greenfield development or acquisition.

Mr. Wayangankar’s recent natural gas activities have been focused on the development, financing, and M&A support of midstream gas storage assets in both domestic and international markets. Within the U.S, Mr. Wayangankar has supported over a dozen storage projects in Gulf Coast, Northeast, Florida and West Coast. He has also led numerous natural gas market consulting projects for utilities, large industrials, natural gas asset developers and independent power developers. Internationally, Mr. Wayangankar has supported storage developers, lenders and private equity funds with project due-diligence in Northwest Europe and the U.K. Additionally, Mr. Wayangankar has significant LNG experience performing market assessments and due diligence for LNG terminal developers and prospective capacity holders in the Gulf Coast, Florida, Northeast U.S., Canadian Maritimes, Central Mexico Pacific Coast, Baja/Southern California and the Pacific Northwest. He has also assisted clients in negotiating and reviewing LNG commercial agreements (LNG TUAs, SPAs, OCAs, FSA & transportation).

Amol Wayangankar’s Specialties:

Energy Director with strong background in domestic and international natural gas, LNG and oil markets including business strategy advisory, commercial contract negotiations, fuel price forecasting, market assessments, regulatory affairs, portfolio optimization, fuel procurement planning, and midstream asset valuations. Strong and proven track record in business development, strategy development, project management, product development, and team building.
