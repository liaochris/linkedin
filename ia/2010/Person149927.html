Valencia Wade’s Summary

Upon graduating with my MBA in Entrepreneurship, I want to start working right away in the Radio market whether it's production, writing or brushing up on my skills. I am willing to work my way up and do the BEST work I know I am able to do.

I have worked in the Radio field doing volunteer service and being an on-air personality for almost three years, with me doing production work as well during that time. The radio industry is my main focus and is what I have a passion for doing.

A goal of mine is to own and operate my own radio station. I also want to start a non-profit organization that stresses the means and importance of education to young children and teenagers. Lastly, however I get to this stage of my future career, I would like to have my own talk show in Houston. I strongly believe that I can attain these goals and more as long as I have a strong mind and the persistence to make it.
