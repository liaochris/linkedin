Steven Martel’s Summary

Waitley Enterprises, Inc., a real estate investment business established in 1995, focusing on the acquisition of under-managed, under-valued, income-producing properties in carefully targeted neighborhoods of merging markets across the United States. The company’s areas of focus are industries that provide immediate cash flow and large potential appreciation. More specifically, large apartment complexes, mobile home parks, and self-storage facilities.

Waitley Enterprises is taking advantage of this exciting market by actively buying properties with an eye on adding value. Waitley’s success is based on the company’s ability to acquire properties at the optimum price, maximize returns through aggressive professional property management, and implement successful exit strategies, which are carefully planned from the time of acquisition. Most importantly, Waitley has stood the test of time.

Steven Martel’s Specialties:

Steve has extensive real estate sales and acquisitions experience. He's been the broker of Martels Real Estate inc. for six years. Martels Real Estate inc. has been under his management for five years and it has developed into one of Ottawa, Ontario’s largest private firms. In 2008, he also outsold 2,425 real estate agents in Ottawa and placed 8th in the number of homes sold out of all real estate agents and #1 Sales agent in North America under the age of 30.
