Mike Howell’s Summary

My career includes over 20 years of driving revenue growth in sales and senior sales management positions and top line success across a variety of high tech B2B market segments, around the globe, working with companies like IBM, HP, Motorola, Abbott Labs, and Amazon.com among others. My extensive enterprise sales background gives me the ability to fully grasp a multitude of business issues from my customer’s point of view, and I am relentless about communicating those issues to my company, and ensuring that our solutions and services match to expectations. I’ve been fortunate throughout my career to be with a few really strong organizations, and I’m very excited to be part of Seattle’s premier website design and development firm, efelle media!

Efelle media offers an unparalleled value, specializing in website redesign and development, and our internally built content management system that allows our clients to keep their websites fresh and updated and while delivering strong SEO results. Our websites are designed from the ground up to facilitate unpaid SEO and yet are so easy to manage that anyone with basic busness computer skills can add and update content as easily as sending an email or creating a word document.

All of our online marketing solutions are designed 100% in house, and completely integrated into our CMS platform. Included are over 30 separate modules that support blogging, e-commerce services, social media tools, and many other e-marketing specialties. This comprehensive web services offering and years of search engine optimization experience gives us a very strong SEO capability, as evidenced by our proven track record of delivering amazing search engine results for our clients, time and time again.
