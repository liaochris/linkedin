Randall Goya’s Summary

I develop Web 2.0 (I prefer "Web 2.x") community, social publishing, ecommerce and rich media web sites.

In 2004, I identified Drupal as a useful tool, and today I am a "Drupalist" building about 80% of my projects with it. I am a member of the Drupal Association http://association.drupal.org/user/2602

I have many talented experts in my network. We can handle any kind of project, including PHP MySQL AJAX SOAP DHTML XML CSS .NET C# MSSQL SharePoint Flash mobile and iPhone. My "team" includes graphic, Flash/Flex and 3d designers, writers, marketers, User Experience experts, programmers, media producers and more.

As a developer and project manager, I maintain the highest standards of best-practices and usability, with consideration for extensibility and SEO/SEM, SMO using tools such as ShareThis and Gigya Socialize, and the Semantic Web with apps like Tattler.

I worked in the film postproduction industry for 10 years on major feature films http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0333025/

I have been developing web sites since 1996, beginning with a free GeoCities site, where I explored the possibilites of DHTML/JavaScript. The site won awards, and my code was featured on the front page of JavaScript.com. My code has also been featured on drupal.org and dzone.

FWIW I have earned the most "best answers" in Web Development on LinkedIn - 38+
