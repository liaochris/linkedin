Steven Robinson’s Summary

Intellectual Property and InformationTechnology Attorney, efficient and experienced in all aspects of technology and media licensing, technology services, ASP and SaaS agreements, subscription agreements, online terms and conditions, content development agreements and licenses, with high level, in-house experience in the healthcare technology and financial services industries.

Adept in counseling clients in complex, fast-paced transactions involving evolving technology and business requirements. Provides consistently clear legal analysis, as well as practical recommendations and business oriented advice, to allow clients to achieve their business objectives with minimized legal risk. Skilled negotiator, innovative problem solver, and an insightful, collegial communicator at all corporate levels who establishes lasting client relationships.

Steven Robinson’s Specialties:
