Don C. Peterson’s Summary

Entrepreneur and executive leader with a track record of business model innovation, rain-making, M&A success, and profitable growth in competitive and rapidly changing environments.

Committed to the principles of Servant Leadership as the key to achieving extraordinary results from teams of skilled professionals.
