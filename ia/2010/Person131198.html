Marc David Miller’s Summary

Marc David Miller has extensive experience in market development and related issues, concentrating on Russia, Europe, Central Asia and the former Soviet Union. He is also the founder of Discovering Russia, dedicated to enriching cultural and business travel to Russia and the former Soviet Union.

In addition, Marc has advised numerous non-profits and arts groups on fund raising, branding and programming, including the Kolodzei Art Foundation, the American Friends of the State Hermitage Museum, the White Nights Foundation, the Junior Associates of The Museum of Modern Art, the American Friends of the State Tretyakov Museum, the American Russian Young Artists Orchestra, and the Bergen Museum of Art and Science. Because of his experience in the business, political and cultural spheres Marc brings a unique, realistic and imaginative perspective to Discovering Eurasia.

Marc's article, “Russia!: A Guggenheim Exhibit Gives New Perspective" (co-authored with his wife, Natalia Kolodzei), appeared in the November-December 2005 issue of Russia Profile. In addition, he has been quoted on trade and other issues in the Russian, Uzbek and other countries' press.

Marc speaks fluent English (native speaker), as well as basic Russian and French. He obtained a Certificate in Real Estate Finance from New York University's Real Estate Institute. He can be reached at 1-212-758-7699 or MDM@DiscoveringEurasia.com.

Marc David Miller’s Specialties:

Strategic Advice and Development to promote bi-lateral trade and investment between the US and the former Soviet Union (Russia, Kazakhstan, Ukraine, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Armenia, Georgia, and Azerbaijan).
