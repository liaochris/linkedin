Lucile CORNET’s Summary

As a graduate student from the EDHEC Business School in France, having just completed an exchange term with the Sauder Business School MBA in Vancouver, I'm looking for a position in M&A or Corporate Finance in UK, USA, Asia or Middle East. I am available to work from now. I speak english fluently.

I have over a year of work experience in finance. During my role as an LBO analyst with the Crédit Agricole Private Equity, I gained experience in evaluating new opportunities of investment, as well as an understanding of strategic and financial statement analysis. I prepared presentations for the Investment committee, forecasted performance and return with financial models, and worked on portfolio companies’ issues (debt restructuring or acquisitions). This knowledge has been supplemented by a 6-month internship as an analyst in Mergers and Acquisitions for Aforge Finance, one of the main players for French middle market transactions. This experience contributed to develop my financial structures knowledge, as well as my company valuation, database research and IT skills.

I really enjoy working in an international environment. I am excited by the opportunity of being part of the development of a company abroad because I like challenges and taking initiative. I bring a strong work ethic and significant interpersonal skills. My former employers have commented on my initiative and efficiency.
