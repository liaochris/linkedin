Bradley Petitt’s Summary

Licensed Insurance Professional serving those in need of Life Insurance, Health Insurance, Disability & Critical Illness Insurance, Long-Term Care Insurance, and Annuities by assisting them in assessing their need and making appropriate recommendations as part of their overall financial strategy for a secure future.
Ordained Pastor having served in communities in Goodland, Kansas; Taylor, Michigan; Adrian, Michigan; Richmond Heights, Ohio; Orwell, Ohio; Eastlake, Ohio; and Rockford, Illinois. Served on numerous district committees and community organization boards.
Executive Director of a non-profit housing corporation serving individuals with mental retardation and developmental disabilities. Responsible for fundraising and grantwriting resulting in the purchase of 4 new homes in four years.
Business Director of a non-profit Children's Residential Treatment Center and Private School.
Supervisor and Field Service Technician in the manufacturing industry. Hydraulics, High Temperature Ceramic Refractory, Lubrication, printing, aviation and automotive are among the industries worked in. For the past three years I have worked as a Lubrication Field Technician for the Steel, Automotive, Mining, and Agricultural industries designing, installing, and servicing Trabon, Farval, Perma, Graco, Dropsa, and OPCO centralized lubrication systems. Expertise in tube bending, troubleshooting, and hydraulic hose manufacturing. I have also installed centralized lubrication systems on Heavy Duty CAT Equipment.
My Goal is to add value to each company & relationship I work with and leave behind a legacy of professionalism and integrity.
