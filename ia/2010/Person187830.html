Sharon Markovsky’s Summary

Sharon Markovsky is a marketing and marketing research strategy expert with over 16 years of professional experience who excels at designing and applying practical and effective marketing and marketing research programs as well as customer acquisition, cross-sell, and retention strategies Throughout her career, Sharon has provided thought-leadership support to both internal and external clients in many different industries including: insurance, healthcare, energy, manufacturing, plumbing, distribution, and safety. Her adeptness and ability to cull out consumer insights from data has helped companies improve their operations by better understanding their potential and integrating various strategies across direct mail, online, competitive intelligence, brand, media, advertising, and distribution to reach their potential. In addition to her client side experience, Sharon is one of the founders of Fast Forward Strategies, a marketing strategy company.

Sharon serves on the board of directors for the Society of Insurance Research and finds enormous inspiration while brainstorming with other insurance research professionals. Sharon is a member of the Direct Marketing Association, the Marketing Research Association and the American Marketing Association and holds a BS, in management with emphasis in marketing from Rutgers University. She has achieved her PRC certification at the expert level. She lives in Reno, Nevada with her husband son and is currently enjoying pursuing her MBA at University of Nevada, Reno (UNR).

Purpose: To change attitudes and behavior through effective marketing and market research.
