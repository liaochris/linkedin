Jorge Lázaro Díaz’s Summary

Jorge is a program/project management, business development and marketing professional at Terremark with a solid software development, information technology, and security/compliance background. He develops strategic solutions maximizing the value of Terremark offerings by combining them with vendor offerings.

His responsibilities involve analyzing and understanding complex business issues in the area of lead generation, purchasing logistics and vendor management and developing comprehensive solutions addressing them. He has the ability to:
• quickly evaluate organizational needs,
• understand the technical implications,
• communicate the issues to project stakeholders and sponsors,
• motivate/organize team members and resources and
• deliver on the requirements within the prescribed schedule and budget.

Prior to working at Terremark, Jorge served as the Director of Information Services at the Subway Independent Purchasing Cooperative overseeing the internal IT team and strategic vendors delivering solutions supporting the purchasing logistics for the North American Subway Restaurant chain.

He has served in technical architect, security/compliance project manager and technical lead roles while at Bank of America and KPMG Consulting/BearingPoint. He served as Director of Internet Technologies at Stratasys Group, a South Florida integration and consulting company and HP(Compaq) and IBM.

On the personal side, Jorge has a passion for helping adults looking for work and college students as they launch their careers. He serves as Back on Track Network board member and runs the CareerJockey.org blog providing job hunt, career discernment, leadership & proactive career management resources.
