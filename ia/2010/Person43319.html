Corey J. Lewis’s Summary

After a short stint in the United Sates Military, I decided to enter the workforce, and became one of the youngest store managers with Lowe's Home Improvement. I then had an opportunity to enter the construction industry in sales and project management. Spending the next 12 years in the commercial door and access control industry I learned the business from the ground up, completeting coursework with the Door & Hardware Institue as Architectural Hardware Consultant, one of their their higest honors. During those 12 years I worked for and with some wonderful people and had some of the best mentors. In November of 2003, I started my own company until I closed the doors in April of 2008 and went into the field of Recruiting. I highly enjoy work as a professional recruiter and continually strive to be the best and keep a personal touch with all of my candidates and clients.

Corey J. Lewis’s Specialties:

As a Senior Recruiter, my company specializes in the palcement of Engineering and Senior Level Management candidates. My specialty fields are with aerospace, defense and avionics companies. The more difficult of the position, the more challenging of the search, the better for me.
