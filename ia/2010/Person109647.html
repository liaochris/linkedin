Jill Yaracs’s Summary

Creative, team-oriented marketing manager with product development experience in both commercial and retail market segments. Bachelor’s degrees in both mechanical engineering and interior design provide a strong foundation for technical capacity and ingenuity. Instrumental in selling and promoting products to major retail organizations such as Lowe’s, Home Depot, Ace Hardware, and True Value Hardware. Core competencies include:
* Product development / management. Able to manage the product development process from conception through implementation. Understand engineering processes and timelines in product development. At Cooper Lighting, for instance, introduced two product lines into Lowe’s consisting of 12 SKUs, six of which are slated for national roll out. At Siemens Energy and Automation, awarded three patents for new products.
* Marketing. Worked with consultants to Cooper Lighting in compiling product concept Voice of Customer to determine market demands in retail lighting. Worked closely with cross functional teams in the organization to develop and take those concepts to market. Managed 20+ existing decorative and contractor product SKUs.
* Collaboration / partnerships. Comfortable communicating regularly with vendors, customers, and team members as well as executive team members. At Cooper Lighting, collaborated with domestic and Asian suppliers to reduce product costs, resolve quality issues, and develop new products. Interfaced directly with retail buyers on new products and existing product brand management.
