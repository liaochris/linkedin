Jason Mills, MBA’s Summary

My professional career includes almost 4 years of public accounting experience in the St. Louis metro area. My career in Public Accounting has focused mainly in the areas of Audit and Review engagements for non-public corporations in the construction, manufacturing, distribution and mining industries. My responsibilities have also included the preparation of Federal and State tax returns for C-Corps, S-Corps, Partnerships and Individuals.

Aside from tax and assurance services I performed other various tasks for our clients such as monthly reviews of financial statements, fixed asset management and assistance with month-end and year-end close.
