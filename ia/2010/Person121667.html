Laura Hess’s Summary

I have over 12 years of experience in human resources and training and development. Many of those years were spent coaching managers through employee issues. This experience led to my passion for putting the right people in the right jobs the first time and making sure employees are set-up for success. As a result Solutions InSight & Associates, in partnership with Profiles International, Inc, the world leader in selecting and developing a high performing workforce, was created. My goal is to provide assessment tools and training that will lead to better hires and more successful coaching and development of employees.

I believe in partnering with my clients to understand specific needs. We work together to create and implement the solution that will lead to improved performance, efficiency, and promote positive communication and employee morale. My clients see results through increased productivity, reduced stress, tension, and conflict all leading to a positive impact on the bottom line.

I feel personal reward when employees are satisfied in the right job and companies have happier and more productive employees.
