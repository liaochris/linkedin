Kathleen Lowe’s Summary

I have more than 10 years experience in communications and marketing management, comprised of brand development, online marketing, product market research, competitive analysis, public relations planning, as well as overall strategic planning and execution. Most recently responsible for implementing and managing social media tools into the marketing mix.

A leader in communications, new business development and the electronic media industries-- from broad based branding and direct marketing campaigns for consumer goods--- to starting marketing & communication departments, to producing public service announcements as well as deploying new interactive technologies, I offer unique skills and experiences in the media.

My experience includes a wide range of corporations, industries, non-profits, and governmental organizations, where I have successfully deployed numerous brand initiatives, both in traditional and online environments.
