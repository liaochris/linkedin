My goals are to contribute my experience by leading an automation and instrumentation team or division and at the same time contributing to the empowerment of staff members. My positions held within industry include the following:

2. Automation & Information (C&I) Lead Project Engineer: De Beers Marine. This role included the project engineering of automation (DCS and PLC systems) for the De Beers fleet of maring mining vessels.

3. A&I (C&I) Project Technician: De Beers Marine (April 2003 to October 2005). This role included the project implementation and commissioning of marine based automation and information systens.

4. Control and Instrumentation Technician: Tongaat Hulett Sugar (Sept 1999 to May 2003). This was a systems engineering and project role primarly focused on the sugar plants PLC and SCADA systems.

5. Control and Instrumentation Technician: Illovo Sugar (Dec 1997 to Aug 1999)

6. Control and Instrumentation Technician: Illovo Sugar (Jan 1997 to Dec 1997)
