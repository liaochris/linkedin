The Monolith Extension Project presented an opportunity to explore techniques for capturing the language of variants found in the network security domain. Extending a programming language in the form of a Domain Specific Language provided a means to use the language of the network security domain. A technique is introduced to avoid duplication of business rules through the use of Value objects.
