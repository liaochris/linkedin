Robyn Sidersky’s Summary

I am currently a municipal reporting fellow at The Patriot-News in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.

I just graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and a minor in political science from the University of Central Florida.


I completed an internship at the CNN Wire at CNN, in Atlanta.

I wrote for my college newspaper all through college. I've interned at The Seminole Chronicle and News/Talk AM 580 WDBO and the Orlando Business Journal. I filled in part-time at WDBO Radio as a Web producer and reporter. I also worked at the UCF Undergraduate Research Journal as the student editor.

I try not to let my age hold me back from accomplishing my goals.
