Dan Lonergan’s Summary

Dan Lonergan is the Founder and Managing Partner of L6 Holdings Corporation, a private equity firm based in Atlanta, GA that acquires controlling interests in lower middle market companies in the business, financial and marketing services industries as well as franchise companies. Mr. Lonergan is the Chairman of Global Connection Holding Corporation, an L6 portfolio company that directly markets prepaid telecommunication and financial services to the unbanked / underbanked consumer. Previously, as a Managing Director of Roark Capital Group, Lonergan made eight platform investments totaling over $200 million of invested equity capital.

Prior to joining Roark, Lonergan completed $25 billion in corporate finance and mergers and acquisitions transactions as an investment banker. From 1999 through 2001, Lonergan was a Director at Credit Suisse First Boston Corporation in the firm's Mergers and Acquisitions Group where he participated in a broad range of industries, including consumer products, basic industry, media, and healthcare. Prior to joining Credit Suisse First Boston Corporation in June 1999, Mr. Lonergan worked for over three years in the New York office of J.P. Morgan & Co., Inc. as a generalist in the Mergers & Acquisitions department.

Prior to joining J.P. Morgan, Mr. Lonergan worked for six years developing a business he co-founded. The business, CompuTower Technologies Corp., provided a much more efficient means of parking and retrieving automobiles and other bulk storage items.

Mr. Lonergan received a Master’s degree in Business Administration from The Pennsylvania State University Smeal College of Business in 1986. His concentration was Finance. Mr. Lonergan received a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Accounting from The Pennsylvania State University in 1984 where he also was a Varsity Football Letterman and Graduate Assistant Football Coach. Mr. Lonergan signed with the Washington Redskins in 1985 as a free-agent quarterback.
