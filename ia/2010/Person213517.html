Jerry Walker, SFR CC’s Summary

The life thread through Jerry's existence feeds his passion for professional speaking, and for helping others through being a public speaking coach.

Widely varied experience incorporates carpentry, aircraft factory work, Record Room Work Leader for the U.S. 11th Circuit Court of Appeals in Atlanta, Research Librarian and Office Services Mgr for Ogletree Deakins Law Firm, SE Regional Sales Mgr for MCS Management Services in Philadelphia, Account Mgr for STG Marketing and Promotions, General Mgr for The Faulkner Group of Solid Source Realty, Inc., and in each of these endeavors his passion for public speaking has found outlets!

Now a professional speaker in addition to his General Manager duties, this outgoing, and fearless speaker has set his sites on multiple streams of professional speaking income:
Look out for The Fearless Speakers' Conference, catch his talk show on Mondays at 7:00pm EST, "The No Limit Life," where Jerry interviews successful men and women living a life with no limits on their ability to dream and reach for the stars. In each of these the goal is to leverage his skills and talents as never before, and to continually encourage and challenge men and women to make living their passion a life priority so that they too can live a life with no limits!
