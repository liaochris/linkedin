Randy Krum’s Summary

Leader of product development, product marketing and marketing communications groups within large consumer products companies. Focus on innovation, consumer research, product launches, brand development, awareness marketing, category management and intellectual property.

Solid experience developing and launching consumer package goods. I have worked with Wal-Mart, Target, PetSmart, Petco, Bed, Bath & Beyond, Macy's, Bloomingdale's, Williams-Sonoma, Dillards, Crate & Barrel, Kohl's and more.

Randy Krum’s Specialties:
