Alexis Roig’s Summary

Currently taking a Master of Business Management and Entrepreneurship in France, Alexis Roig worked as Marketing Manager at A.E. SOL, biggest High Education Consultancy Institution in Spain specialized in advising regarding university education, offer private tutoring and coaching for students, as well as support lessons. Alexis was in charge of branding, product, promotion and new markets research and he led a 5 people team.

Alexis is a born entrepreneur and he founded his first company in 2003, GC Events. He was responsible of organization and promotion of events and conventions, mostly for TV /Cinema companies (such as Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer Iberica, AXN Europe - Sony Pictures Entertainment Inc, Canal+ and Televisió de Catalunya).

Nowadays, Alexis also offers his marketing experience (especially in media events, high education and internet fields) as Freelance Consultant.

As a master of Computer Science, Alexis has a large experience in the field of system design, computer programming, data processing, networks, operating systems, software and hardware development. Some technologies he works with:
- C/C++, Java, Prolog, HTML, XML/XSLT, CSS, PHP, SQL
- J2EE, J2ME, AWT/Swing
- MySQL, Informix, eXist
- Eclipse, Netbeans, Rational Rose, IBM Rational Software, RUP

Alexis is also fluent in four languages; English, Spanish, French and Catalan.
