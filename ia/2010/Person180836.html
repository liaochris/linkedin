Salvino A. Salvaggio’s Summary

Salvino brings over 15 years of international management and consulting experience as well as of entrepreneurial mindset.
His main focus is on strategy definition and implementation and business building, mostly in sectors such as IT/High Tech, Telecom/Internet, Biotech, Media (incl. digital media), and Bank & Finance.
In 2007, Salvino was recognized with one of the highest Italian civil decoration by being designated a “Commander of the Republic” by the Italian President’s office, in a tradition that is analogous to the British Queen/King’s New Year’s Honours List.
• Since November 2005, Salvino A. Salvaggio works at the Qatar Science & Technology Park (QSTP)
• From June 2003 to November 2005, Salvino was Head of Internet Portal in Qatar Telecom. He led the project of creating an Internet portal supporting both corporate communication and a set of value added services for broadband and mobile users
• Before joining that position, Salvino was a High-Tech and Telecom/Internet expert consultant in McKinsey & Co. He was mainly involved in strategy definition, implementation and business building projects
• Before, Salvino worked as an Experienced Manager for Accenture. He led the Innovation Center supporting new business projects
• During the 1990’s, he also launched and managed three businesses (two of which were media-related)
• From 1999 to 2003, Salvino also was external anchorman at the Belgian radio and TV public network (RTBF), expert in digital media & communication
• From the mid 1980’s till 2003, he was an academic : holding a Ph.D. (doctorate), Salvino taught and ran research in several European and North American universities
• Salvino published 9 scientific books, a novel and more than 95 articles in academic, scientific and professional journals and reviews. His novel won a first European literary award

Business strategy, business planning, business building, general management, management of innovation
