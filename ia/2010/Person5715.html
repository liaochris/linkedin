Alejandro Paschalides’s Summary

Mr. Paschalides is the Founding Principal of Carina Capital, LLC. Mr. Paschalides formerly served as an analyst for Pharos Capital Group, a private equity firm based in Nashville, Tennessee, where he analyzed and performed due diligence on potential investments in the healthcare and business services sectors. Prior to Pharos, Mr. Paschalides was a summer analyst for GPX Enterprises, a private equity firm in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Mr. Paschalides graduated magna cum laude with a Bachelor of Science in Economics with concentrations in Finance and Real Estate from the University of Pennsylvania’s Wharton School of Business.
