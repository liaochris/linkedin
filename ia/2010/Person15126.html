Anton Ansalmar’s Summary

At Rapid Consulting, we believe that the environment is an important issue that affects us all. That is why we are committed to building a relationship based IT consulting services firm that will create a foundation of support for environmentally friendly businesses.

Rapid Consulting is a Services Partner for SAP providing consulting and development expertise to the SAP Netweaver and Business Objects Platforms.

We provide full end to end solutions for Sustainability from Project Management to GHG Accounting and Reporting to building Carbon and Energy Management Platforms.

Providing companies the ability to control their energy costs, report carbon footprint, maximize operational efficiency and better manage their impact to the environment.
