Tim Shipman’s Summary

Consummate loss prevention executive that can take any loss prevention, crisis management department to the next level. I know how to develop a vision and strategy and the ability to communicate such vision and strategy to those around me. I have the ability to train, teach and coach as well as motivating and building a first class organization. I have a proven track record in the field of loss prevention. I am respected for integrity, honesty, passion, authenticity and know when and how to have those fierce conversations that is needed that is in the best interest of the company that I provide service for.

I have a very keen understanding of shrink and how to reduce and control shrink in a manner that is sustainable. I know how to develop relationships and partnerships across cross-functional areas in order to achieve the desired outcome for everyone concerned.

I have been a speaker at regional, national and international conferences such as RILA, FMI, RLPA, and various other retail and food associations.

I currently serve on the board of the Loss Prevention Foundation, the Asset Protection Leaders Council at the Retail Industry Leaders Association, and the Editorial Board for Loss Prevention Magazine, past President and current Vice President of the Retail Loss Prevention Association of the Carolina’s and serve as Chair of the Board of Directors of Crime Stoppers of Salisbury-Rowan County.
