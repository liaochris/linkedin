Laura E Bland’s Summary

Graduate of Virginia Commonwealth University, covered the Virginia Legislature, local government, business, health and environmental issues during 13 years as a newspaper reporter and freelance writer.

Worked as an online news content coordinator, multimedia producer and manager for DailyProgress.com in Charlottesville, TimesDispatch.com and other Media General news and special interest Websites.

Served in the press office of Gov. Mark R. Warner and as director of communications for the Democratic Party of Virginia during the 2003 and 2004 election cycles.

Laura E Bland’s Specialties:

News writing, investigative reporting, editing, Website content management and development, multimedia production, social networking.
