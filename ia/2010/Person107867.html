Judy Mick’s Summary

JJM Marketing is my online retail business that I started late 2007. I sell unique and quality College Jewelry and Gift items, and RADA Cutlery (quality kitchen knives and knife sets).

I grew up in the retail business - my parents owned a retail clothing store for over 40 years. Summers and week-ends were spent working there and learning the retail business. After college, I was still there until my husband was transferred here to Roanoke.

Most of my experience here has been in the placement industry - I have worked both permanent placements and 10 years as a manager with a temporary/permanent staffing service.

However, I never lost my love of retail. My husband and I also own and restaurant that I worked every day for 8 years. When time came to back out - I decided to open an online retail business! I love it!

In the future, in addition to my retail business, I would like to work as an consultant helping other (individuals and businesses) learn how to sell on eBay. With this, I would like to show them how Social Media Marketing is essential to their business.

Judy Mick’s Specialties:

Customer Service is a passion of mine. I want my customers to have the best possible online purchasing experience they can have.
