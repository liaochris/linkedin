Pam Pujals’s Summary

Consumers choose not only “what” and “when” but “where” and “how” they use media. Critical elements include understanding what drives consumers and delivering content in a manner, time and platform most acceptable and receptive. Effective strategy goes beyond measuring the number of consumers reached. You must engage consumer and become part of their conversations and experiences.

Our working philosophy is simple. Know your consumer.

You need a media expert with passion – passion for creating programs that address your business goals and a passion for applying current industry and consumer trends, the media and the competitive environment. Pam Pujals, a media expert with more than 30 years of agency experience planning, buying and targeting U.S. and international audiences, Real Media Solutions delivers programs that generate desired results — cost-efficient, integrated strategies with effective placements that always provide value far beyond the dollars invested.

Real Media Solutions, based in Tampa specializes in paid media strategies with a “beyond the numbers” approach to media planning and buying. We deliver customized marketing solutions through a brand centric planning process. The process starts with gaining a full understanding of your brand. From these insights we explore and identify opportunities to fully engage and motivate consumers. We believe that media planning, buying critically evolves, rather than just phases of the execution process. By collaborating with our clients in each of these disciplines, we reach the right consumer at the right time in the right way.
Current Accounts: Amelia Island Plantation, Panama City Beach CVB, project work for AAA Auto Club South
