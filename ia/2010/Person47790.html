Daniel Seidler’s Summary

Daniel is an entrepreneurial physical therapist with a strong desire to dramatically improve the quality of health in the Bronx, NY.

In 1999, along with Perry Bonomo, Daniel wrote and edited "Why Does Working @ My Computer Hurt So Much?" - a comprehensive guide to preventing and treating Computer Induced Repetitive Stress Injuries. The book sold several thousand copies and paved the way for a successful ergonomic consulting business.

Two years later, he opened Westchester Square Physical Therapy to provide the highest quality of PT services in the Bronx. WSPT has evolved into a full service rehab practice offering physical therapy treatment, wellness programs, rehab/fitness products, and community education workshops. WSPT recently moved to an 11,000 square foot facility with a therapy pool, cardio gym, yoga studio, and retail store. From prevention to treatment to ongoing wellness - WSPT is designed to manage programs for the full spectrum of patients of all ages.
