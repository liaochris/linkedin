Cheri Riley’s Summary

CHERI'S CREDENTIALS INCLUDE MORE THAN 20 YEARS IN RESIDENTIAL REAL ESTATE SALES AND BROKERAGE, with almost $2 billion in sales generated personally or directly under her supervision.

She served as the launching Broker and President for one of Atlanta’s most unique boutique real estate brokerage firms, prior to that she consulted with builders and developers through her firm, which focused on existing communities with new product offerings into the marketplace.

She served eight years as a Senior Vice President and Managing Broker and five years as a top producing agent for one of the most respected real estate firms in Atlanta. Before relocating to Atlanta she was consistently one of the top three agents in her firm in Winter Park, Florida. Her vast experience in nearly every facet of the residential brokerage business makes her a formidable negotiator and advocate for all of her clients.

According to Cheri “The opportunity to reconnect with great friends and long-time business associates as a Founding Partner in Atlanta Fine Homes Sotheby’s International Realty stands out as the highlight of my career. I take very seriously the responsibility of creating the best experience possible for my clients, I do not believe there is a better combination of expertise and image to support me whether my clients are buying and selling a home or evaluating a development opportunity.

Board Positions-
She has served on the Board of Rhodes Hall, which is the Georgia Trust for Historic Preservation’s headquarters in Atlanta. She has also served as Vice-Chairperson of the Board of the Morse Museum of American Art in Winter Park, Florida, and the Board of the Women’s Council of REALTORS for the Atlanta Board of REALTORS. She has also served as a corporate Board member for the Horseshoe Bend Country Club, the Advisory Board of Atlanta Woman Magazine, Today's Custom Home Magazine.

Cheri Riley’s Specialties:

Cheri holds the prestigious CRB designation from the Realtors National Sales & Marketing Council and is completing the MIRM designation from NAHB. Her upbeat, fast-paced training and coaching has produced some of Atlanta’s top agents over the last ten years.
Cheri currently serves on the Advisory Board of The First Multiple Listing Service in Atlanta, is a member of the mother-daughter National Charity League organization and is the Real Estate Trends Columnist for Southern Seasons Magazine.
