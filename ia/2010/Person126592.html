Liudvikas Paskevicius’s Summary

After 5 yrs of studies at Kaunas University of Medicine I gained both a broad view to Pharmacy as a science and a field of business & interest. Having decided to gain more experience at different side, I switched to Government, the Healthcare sector, and later on followed the chance to a partly-governmental institution, the CPMA.

Today I proceed as a team member at the Purchasing Solutions' Division within current organization, being responsible for further implementation of CPO.lt™, a newly elaborated e-catalog, under www.cpo.lt, allowing governmental and non-profit organizations to purchase the required goods in a convenient way, using e-tools.

My responsibilities, including knowledge & supervision of public purchasing & procurement, have deepened my understanding of the collaboration of government & public-private sector.

I'm proud to proceed gaining the valuable experience and extending the purchasing abilities for our clients further-on, by launching new products at our modern catalog CPO.lt™.
