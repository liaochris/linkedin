Kathy Roberts’s Summary

Administration of Justice, Cum Laude, Academic and Presidential Scholar, Dean's List and other awards

World, National, Regional and State Powerlifting Champion.

Competed in sport of powerlifting for over 12 years as a lifetime drugfree competitor. Currently ranked in the Top 20 Strongest Females in the World. Followed my own method of training for well over 12 years w/ focus, dedication and discipline to become a champion.

Chosen as an unsung hero for community outreach and youth related work in the Portraits of the City Campaign that was published in numerous newspapers to incude the "Washington Post".

Contributed to several books to inspire young women. Written several articles for publication to include "Personal Excellence" magazine.

Featured in "Powerlifting". "Kathy is very adamant about being 'lifetime drug free'." Dr. Barney Groves PhD, author.

Guest on "Championship Thinking": Success Or Less: It’s All Attitude, Dude! Raise your APB-Attitude Performance Bar.

Guest on Sports Talk, the Glenn Harris show on News Channel 8

Participated in several radio show interviews WPGC, 95.5, WKYS, 93.9, WTEM SportsTalk 980 with Rick ‘Doc’ Walker and also with Kevin Kiley who hosted Kiley and the Coach, WOL, 1450 AM News Talk Radio with Gary “Digital” Williams and Henry “Discombobulating” Jones, (Where Information is Power), WTEM 570 AM with Jerry Phillips on Metro Talk, On the Road Show and various other sports radio programs in Maryland, Virginia, and the District of Columbia.
