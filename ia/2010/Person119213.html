Kristin Bockius’s Summary

Biography:

Kristin Bockius is the government relationship and social media marketing manager for Microsoft’s U.S. State and Local $900M government business. Bockius ensures that Microsoft communicates with its key audiences through an integrated marketing approach including advertising and image campaigns as well as events for customers and partners including the US Public Sector CIO Summit. Social media has quickly become a key element to these programs. Under Bockius’ leadership, Microsoft launched the award winning Bright Side of Government social community which gives state and local government employees and decision makers the opportunity to share best practices through videos, blog posts and discussions within Twitter, Facebook and Linkedin.

Bockius, who demonstrates a great love of technology, also oversees DigiGirlz Day, which is a program sponsored by Microsoft to encourage girls to get involved in technology. Bockius has also been recognized for several awards within the Microsoft U.S. Public Sector marketing team for her programs and efforts.
