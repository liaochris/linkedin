David Trombino’s Summary

I'm the vision, strategy and business development guy for TecDoc Digital Solutions. My passion is assisting clients with 1:1 direct marketing strategy, and campaign implementation and measurement to produce over-the-top results.

Our team has great expertise integrating digital technology (digital print, e-mail, personalized micro-sites (pURLS), cross-media), data, and creative to deliver highly targeted marketing offers that are relevant to each recipient's needs.
