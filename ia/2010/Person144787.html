Miguel Conchas’s Summary

Over 30 years in field of management.
Head of the Laredo Chamber of Commerce since 1993.
Started career in public school education. Served as Director for a Teacher Corps Project at Texas A&I at Laredo in the late 70's.

Miguel Conchas’s Specialties:

Management -- particularly in area of Non profits. Accreditation process for chamber of commerce. Design and production of published materials. Fluent in English & Spanish. Have done extensive work collaborating with Mexican organizations/governmental entities.
