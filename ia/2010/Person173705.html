Rituparna Chatterjee’s Summary

Rituparna is a consummate communications and research professional with over five years of international experience across industries and continents. Rituparna is an independent writer, journalist and photographer who has written over 200 analytical articles (and shot photos) for several international publications. While business, technology and world cinema remain her niche areas, she has also researched and written extensively on subjects as diverse as space, travel, robots, food&wine, futuristic cars, lifestyle, zeppelins, arts&culture, aviation breakthroughs, and so on. Rituparna writes on business&technology in the Silicon Valley and the US for Outlook Business magazine. She is on the Panel of Authors at Dear Cinema, India's first world cinema portal, in association with EU's CineEuropa and was the cinema columnist for Canadian magazine, South Asian Living. Rituparna is also a creative marketing strategist. She led and created innovative marketing, corporate branding, communication and PR strategies for eminent Silicon Valley-based IT consulting firm Akraya. Her strategies led to Akraya winning multiple awards and recognition as a socially-responsible company. She also created (design and content) and launched The Green Vibe (Akraya's first corporate green newsletter) and transformed Akraya into a government-certified green business. Back in India, Rituparna wrote in depth feature articles -- analysing the marketing and management strategies of India's top 100 companies -- for Business Standard (India's second-largest national business newspaper, also, the Indian subsidiary of the Financial Times, London).

Rituparna is always looking to learn and grow from challenging new avenues. Among other things, she helps design and install solar power systems for underprivileged households in Northern California and champions the cause of ethical, slow and "real" food. She tries to make a difference to society with every role that she takes up.
