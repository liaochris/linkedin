Jennifer O'Neill Ross’s Summary

Creative Interior Design professional with 13 years of experience in commercial design and space planning. What differentiates me from others is my unique combination of both design and space planning skills. I am capable of designing from the perspective of a planner, and space planning through the eyes of a designer. This along with my ability to manage multiple projects concurrently, while still maintaining strong attention to detail, allows me to consistently exceed my client’s expectations.

Expertise includes the following: retail design and planning, prototype development, corporate design and planning, tenant design/finish-out and architectural illustration.

Full resume and portfolio are available upon request.

Jennifer O'Neill Ross’s Specialties:

Project management, space planning (hand sketching & CAD) conceptual design, architectural drawings and CD's, fixture design and details, perspective drawing and renderings both hand drawn and computer aided, finish/furniture/artwork selections, ADA codes, proficient in AutoCAD 2008, trained in AutoCAD Revit Architecture, Experienced with all MS Office programs, Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat and Impression, Preparing to take the N.C.I.D.Q. Exam for State licensure.
