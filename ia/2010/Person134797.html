Mark van Diggelen’s Summary

Mark has been actively involved with the Internet since 1995 and more so with online gaming since 2000. He started and initially self funded SkillPod Media in March 2004 and is currently the CEO. To date SkillPod have built a proprietary game management engine which currently powers a number of key portals games offering across the globe, including clients such as Media 24, RTL Group, KTV and Independent Newspapers to name but a few. The business started evolving into the mobile space in December 2007 and was part of the Adobe Distributable Flash Player launch. SkillPod is part of the Nokia Launchpad and has a number of game titles on offer in the Nokia Ovi store, with at least 10 new titles being added every month. More recently the business launched a mobile games portal, which is the brain child of Mark. To date the response has been overwhelming with a number of key international media businesses expressing interest in the offering. The business is a finalist in the current TT100 (Top Technology) awards, the winners will be announced on 28 April 2010.

The position he held prior to SkillPod Media was as VP International Business Development at Osiris Trading (one of the largest online casino operators globally), his brief was to develop new innovative brands in order to complement and diversify the current offerings by the group, the first brand was launched within 2 months and went on to contribute significantly to the groups bottom line, after just 2 months of operation. He was involved in the conceptualisation, scoping, development, deployment and management of all business development initiatives. Typical projects include: scoping, developing and marketing of gaming portals, gaming solutions, email / online marketing initiatives, interactive multimedia, solution roll-out etc. Some key partners included: Microgaming systems (London), NIS Group (Toronto), MIT (Montreal), SCA Promotions Inc. (Dallas), ProWagerSystems (Toronto) and GameHouse Inc. (Seattle).
