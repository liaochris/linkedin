Alex delaCruz’s Summary

A financial management executive with over 20 years of experience in complex program finance and a track record of producing extraordinary results.

Proven senior-level experience in executive decision-making, policy direction, internal and external audit support, strategic business planning, government relations, and financial management, including the management of grants and donations.
