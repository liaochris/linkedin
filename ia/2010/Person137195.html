I am super passionate about marketing and entrepreneurship. My current company, Clixo, helps businesses double or triple their sales from the internet by combining search engine marketing with conversion rate optimization (CRO).

If you have tried search engine marketing and been disappointed with the results, are looking to start a new Internet marketing program, or you have a decent program you suspect could be improved, you should contact me at matt@clixosearch.com.

There is a lot of conflicting and confusing information about search marketing floating around. I am happy to talk to anyone and give free advice and point you in the right direction. If our services are a fit for your organization, great, if not, I am still happy to help you and answer your questions --my partners yell at me because I give away to much free information, contact me before they shut me down.

WHY CLIXO IS BETTER

Making a website “look cool” or “getting ranked” on Google are only a means to an end. Clixo is great at helping people achieve search rankings and driving lots of qualified traffic to their websites. However, what really separates Clixo from just about any other agency is our ability to Convert Clicks into Customers using Conversion Rate Optimization.

Most agencies know little or nothing about website usability and how to persuade visitors to take action. If they don’t take action, you don’t sell anything. Let me show you some examples. It will blow you away.
