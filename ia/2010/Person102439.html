John Pearce’s Summary

Information entrepreneur. Now building new web 2.0 venture. Brief track record:

* Founder/CEO of MediaMap, for 15 years the leader in media information and software for the public relations industry.

* Founder/Director of "Ralph Don't Run," a campaign in 2003-2004 which helped drive Ralph Nader's vote total from nearly 3% in 2000 down to 0.38% in 2004 by mobilizing battleground-state progressives to support Kerry.

* Has appeared on CNN, CBS, ABC, NBC, and Fox networks; internet video featured on Meet the Press; wrote OpEds for a host of top-20 papers & LA Times Syndicate; spoken at dozens of national conferences on public relations and marketing topics.

New venture in stealth mode.

    The campaign morphed into a remarkable progressive mobilization in support of the Kerry campaign.
