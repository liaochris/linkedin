Gary Guseinov’s Summary

Gary Guseinov spent his college years learning about technology, business and psychology. Immediately after graduating with a BA in Psychology - Gary began his entrepreneurial career.

Since graduating from college in 1994 Gary has been actively involved in developing new internet technology. In 1994 Gary started a web development firm, Digital Media, which quickly gained Fortune 500 clients including AT&T, British Petroleum, Disney, Sony and Bank of America. In 1998 Digital Media merged with Synergy, a multi-channel performance based internet marketing company. While serving as President of Synergy, Gary developed the first online media buying platform, a consumer lead generation and distribution platform and an email delivery platform capable of managing over 1.5 Billion emails per month. At Synergy Gary was responsible for generating over $2 Billion in revenue for Synergy's clients which included Citibank, Conseco, Wells Fargo, AIG, New Century Financial, Citrix/GoToMyPC, and Oracle.

In 2003 Gary co-founded CyberDefender Corporation, a publicly traded company, (NASDAQ: CYDE) and raised over $25M from multiple institutional and retail investors. CyberDefender develops and markets internet security products and services which protect consumers and businesses against spyware, viruses and identity theft.

In 2005 CyberDefender, developed the first (patent pending) Collaborative Internet Security Network based on a secure Peer-to-Peer network infrastructure. The CyberDefender Collaborative Internet Security Network (or Argus) is the first security network which updates all users within hours of new Virus, Spyware, or Identity Theft attack. CyberDefender employs over 300 full time staff at its downtown Los Angeles headquarters.
