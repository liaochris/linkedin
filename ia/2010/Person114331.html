Kendra Crispin, RYT’s Summary

I embrace the belief that learning happens anywhere, anytime and cannot be limited to a classroom. Reading is for research and pleasure, which is critical to any writer. Not only do I write about what I know, I use my imagination and research to explore what I need to write about (a skill which I feel that anyone who went to college can master).

As a writer, I create evocative pictures using words. This will drive the creation of my future books, both fiction and non-fiction. I have two novels in progress, and the basis for two non-fiction works on the back-burner. I will attend whatever writing events and classes which I feel will benefit my craft - and my business success.

As an Ambassador for Your Local City, I co-led the Lakeway networking group, where we all learned from each other as we sought to build each others' businesses. I use creative energy to help local businesses succeed.
