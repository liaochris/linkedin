Malcolm Goodwin’s Summary

Currently, I am the President and CEO of Promise IT Solutions, Inc. Promise IT Solutions is a company that specializes in working with large companies to assist them with their information technology needs. The company was founded in March, 2007.

I have also spent over 10 years in Insurance business working in various disciplines from sales & marketing to management . Past positions include Director of Sales, Allied Insurance and Assistant Vice President, Marsh, Inc. I also co-founded a successful benefits communications consulting firm, Benefits Communications Group, which worked with several large companies including: Wal-Mart, DaimlerChrysler, and ArvinMeritor.
