Michael Leventhal’s Summary

Until my recent retirement, I had been a Special Educator for 26 years, 24 at the Robin Ward School For Exceptional Children (PS177Q, NYC). First New York City teacher to submit video evidence for New York State Alternate Assessment. Developed the Beta autism website for the Department of Education. Produced three documentaries on pilot programs for autism for the State Department of Education. Program proposals: Video Portfolios for Autism; WorkCenter Education, an academic/vocational program based on a current business model.

In my last three years, I employed videos and computers to successfully engage hundreds of language-impaired students in activities promoting metacognition, language and communication and volition.

I administer an online project to encourage the use of video-based instruction with autism. Towards that goal, I am preparing a webinar series preparing parents and schools to become smarter, more effective consumers of this technology.
