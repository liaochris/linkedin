L.A. Pomeroy’s Summary

Professional equestrian sports photojournalist and publicist with additional experience in development/fund raising in the zoological fields. Clients have included 1996 Atlanta Committee for the Olympic Games, EquiSearch.com, U.S. Equestrian Team, Intercollegiate Horse Shows Assoc., Northeast Reining Horse Association, numerous regional and national publications.

L.A. Pomeroy’s Specialties:

Olympic equestrian sports history and trivia; equus zoological behavior and ancestry; equestrian collectibles and carousel horse history; photography; deep connections with the elite in the equestrian field.
