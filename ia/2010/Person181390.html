Sam Thom’s Summary

I have a broad range of experience creating text copy, audio and video content for a range of offline and online platforms.

* I am developing a podcast strategy and content for the Scottish Agricultural College
* I coordinate an EU-funded social networking research project called Incluso (at Station House Media Unit) in Aberdeen
* I deliver media skills training for various groups around the city
* I work with a number of other regular clients around Scotland delivering mainly video podcasts.

My goals are to develop contacts in business, social enterprise and urban regeneration and use the social web to help businesses as well as disadvantaged communities.
