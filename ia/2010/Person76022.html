Glenn Andert’s Summary

I spent 2005 thru 2008 sailing about 16,000 nautical miles between California and New Zealand. That activity has a whole new set of learning curves! I am just getting my feet back on the ground in Wellington, New Zealand, where I am on the board of two software companies.
