Neil Ganey’s Summary

I have the unique combination of an extensive human factors education, a track record of innovation, and the ability to guide and champion the user experience of award-winning products. My experience in both the areas of hardware and software gives me a unique view into the area of hardware/software integration. In addition to working on Lenovo's notebook, peripherals, and software products, I have also worked with clients including Motorola, Cisco, and Intel.

I am an inventor on 13 patents pending with the USPTO and on an additional 10 submitted patent disclosures.

I am a member of HFES, as well as an Associate Human Factors Professional (certified by the BCPE).

Neil Ganey’s Specialties:

user experience, hardware usability, software usability, user-centered design, heuristic evaluation, data analysis, hardware/software integration, human factors & ergonomics, consumer electronics, warnings, alarms, forensics
