Tracy Viselli’s Summary

Interactive and new media strategist who successfully combines traditional public relations, communications, and interactive media strategies to increase brand awareness and online reach. I have a background in online community building and strategic planning, assessment, and data management.

As the Sr. Manager of Social Media at QuinStreet, I manage a division that combines traditional marketing and public relations strategies with integrated media strategies to increase website traffic, online reach, and brand awareness. I have forged valuable connections among national level media and strategists that I use and strengthen every day.

As a part-time consultant, I work with non-profits and political organizations to develop and implement new media strategies that leverage integrated content strategies and social media.

My political opinions are featured weekly on the National Journal's Blogger Poll. I have also won numerous blogging awards and have been an active participant in some of the biggest online progressive projects including:

- TwitterVoteReport, a grassroots election reporting project
- TweetProgress, a directory of progressives on Twitter.
