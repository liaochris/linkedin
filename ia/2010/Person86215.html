Ivan R. Puckett, P.E.’s Summary

29 years of oil and gas exploration and production experience with Shell; both onshore US and offshore GOM. Registered Professional Engineer (Mechanical-Louisiana). Specializing in Managerial and Technical aspects of topsides (offshore) and surface (onshore) engineering, construction, and operations. Exploration Venture Manager of subsurface Geoscientists during the discovery and development of 4 significant offshore projects: Alex (HPHT), Einset, North Marlin and Crosby. Most recently involved in evaluating and entering 3 world-class onshore gas plays; Pinedale Anticline, Barnett Shale, Haynesville Shale, Eagleford Shale, Marcellus Shale. Currently assisting exploration with new entries and assisting A&D in evaluating new business ventures. Interested in pursuing an upper level management position.
