Steve Ontiveros’s Summary

Businesses today are faced with unprecedented challenges. When it seems like meeting payroll, paying operational expenses, and staying above water seem like the only thing you focus on, little energy is left over to do what it is you do best: Sell & produce your product or service!

At The Factoring Place, we take the stress out of customer payment trends. Here's how it works:
1. You mail your invoice to your customer as usual
2. Send us a copy of the invoice when the work is completed
3. After verification, we immediately advance you 80% of the invoice amount.
4. Use that "early money" to pay your suppliers & vendors, take advantage of their "early pay discounts."

Think about it next time you're in a bind: "If you got cash today for the invoice you billed yesterday how would you grow your business tomorrow?"
