Elizabeth Ross’s Summary

EXPERIENCE
Ms. Ross is a Project Environmental Scientist for the Charlotte Branch of ECS Carolinas, LLP (ECS). She has over 9 years of experience in the engineering and environmental consulting field, including technical applications in a variety of project types for environmental assessments, water quality monitoring, and indoor air quality assessments. She is also a former OSHA Health Compliance Officer. Her present responsibilities include client interaction to identify project requirements; preparation of proposals; establishing project scopes of work; managing and conducting Phase I Environmental Site Assessments for potential real estate transactions; managing and conducting indoor air quality assessments and various other industrial hygiene assessments. Ms. Ross is also the Radiation Safety Officer for the Charlotte branch of ECS. Her responsibilities include monitoring dosimetry and training records for employees utilizing moisture density gauges and the x-ray fluorescence device (XRF).

INDUSTRIAL HYGENE SERVICES
Ms. Ross has experience consulting with clients and performing air quality services consisting of asbestos, mold and lead-based paint surveys. Asbestos, lead and mold services include identify the presence and general location, plans for removal, pre-bid meetings and contractor selection. She has performed VOC monitoring for indoor air quality studies as well as personnel monitoring for exposure analysis and OSHA compliance. Additionally, she has experience in performing indoor air quality and industrial hygiene monitoring and testing for determining engineering solutions to indoor contaminant levels as well as health and safety audits.
