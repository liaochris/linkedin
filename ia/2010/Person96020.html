  • Senior Geophysicist

    Shell International E&P

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    November 2005 — July 2009 (3 years 9 months)

    Member of "Integrated Quantitative Interpretation" team concentrating on reservoir surveillance.

Work includes probabilistic inversion techniques (“K From Seismic” project), structural analysis of fractured reservoirs.

  • Senior Geophysicist

    TOTAL

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    August 2003 — November 2005 (2 years 4 months)

    Validation des Gisements (Reservoir Evaluation Group), Paris

    Member of multidisciplinary team working on major operated development deep water West Africa. Responsible for structural and sedimentological interpretation, QC, analysis and facies and lithoseismic interpretation of elastic inversion, co-ordination of feasibility and operational studies for 4D monitoring. Used “traditional” (GeoFrame) and “volume” (VoxelGeo, GeoProbe) software.

    TotalFinaElf Exploration UK PLC

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    September 1998 — August 2003 (5 years )

    Aberdeen

    Undertook geophysical interpretation to optimise well locations in development projects, as well as appraisal and exploration projects. Performed quantitative fault seal analysis to optimise development programme. Also built 3D geo-models and contributed to static/dynamic calculations. Undertook geostatistical simulation of volumetrics in Bruce field. Participated in OBC seismic acquisition. Participated in successful Nelson field equity process and 4D/AVO seismic analysis. Used various software including Landmark, Stratimagic, gOcad, IRAP, Petrel.
