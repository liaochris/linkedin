Brett Relander, Marketing & Social Media’s Summary

Tactical Marketing Labs
As Director of Sales & Marketing at Tactical Marketing Labs, my primary focus is to develop strategies, actionable tactics, and execute targeting marketing/promotions campaigns for companies seeking to engage their community via social media, expand branding, and drive revenue.
Tactical Marketing Labs is truly a hybrid organization that provides combined solutions in the online creative, social media, PR/Communications marketplace.

Professional & Major Collegiate Sports - Social Media & Interactive Marketing for You and Your Sponsors:

Our goal is to build your online/social media assets to enhance your sponsorship value and revenue. Utilizing our core competencies organizations will be able to offer current and potential sponsors the ability to co-brand creative promotions via social media and other interactive means. Here are some of the key propositions:
•Utilizing our team to develop specialized campaigns that are designed specifically for each sponsor you eliminate the need for additional staff and overhead.
•We can work in conjunction with key account reps to familiarize us with each sponsors unique success matrix and develop targeted strategies.
•No upfront cost or risk: Upon meeting with each sponsor and developing a proposal and costs we will work with your staff to determine the appropriate value and price. That means you don’t pay us $1 until you know they’re going to make $2, or more.
•Each promotion, regardless of specific sponsor, will grow your social media assets and therefore inherently increase its value for future promotions.
•Off-season promotions: In addition to in-season options we will work with sponsors to develop additional off-season promotions to continue their branding efforts with your organization all year. This greatly increases the value of your sponsorship options, generates added revenue, and keeps fans engaged year around. And we all know that engaged fans buy more jerseys, hats, and koozies.

    Green Star Builders, LLC

    (Construction industry)

    February 2007 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    As the leader of this environmentally conscious construction company my responsibilities have included sales, public relations, contract negotiations, bidding, estimating, material procurement, social media marketing, traditional marketing, business development, and architect, owner, subcontractor, and supplier consultation and education. One of my most notable projects is the design and construction of a green luxury suite at the Dallas Cowboys’ Valley Ranch practice facility. I personally designed & estimated this project, negotiated this contract with the Jones family, evaluated & secured materials and labor, managed the construction process and verified performance of the building. The three-story outdoor suite features the latest in green building techniques, including a high-efficiency ductless HVAC system, low-e insulated windows, tankless water heaters, low VOC paint & sealants, and a custom terrazzo floor made from recycled glass.

    At Green Star Builders Relander has been pivotal in introducing innovative green building techniques to the Dallas market. Under his leadership, Green Star has established a partnership with the EPA’s Energy Star program, the Sustainable Skylines initiative, and is a member of the U.S. Green Building Council. Relander is a LEED AP (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design Accredited Professional) through the U.S. Green Building Council and also holds a real estate license.
