Wendy Steele’s Summary

Alongside a 20+year career in banking that culminated with a position of Senior Vice President & Regional Manager of the Private Financial Group for Huntington National Bank, Wendy has been a passionate entrepreneur. Finding success in both the for profit and nonprofit world, Wendy has a demonstrated ability to execute concepts into successful reality. Wendy serves as CEO and Owner of Stexley-Brake, LLC, a certified WBE that designs, manufactures and distributes a family of consumer & industrial products under the Tape Wrangler brand. In a complementary enterprise, Wendy serves as CEO of Advanced Production; providing Direct Digital Manufacturing, prototyping and short run production serving Tape Wrangler and other, unrelated businesses.
