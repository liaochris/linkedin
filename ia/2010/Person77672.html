Gregg Harrison’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic, intelligent and highly skilled interactive project manager. I infuse my work environment with positivity- I bring a smile and boundless energy to work with me everyday.

My philosophy on planning, developing, and implementing top quality interactive work stems from an intimate understanding of each phase of project development. At the core of my talent is a love for the process- indeed, the journey is the destination. The detailed time-lines, estimates and creative briefs that I write aren't just pieces of paper- they are deliberately crafted with strategically sound reasoning, they set realistic expectations, and provide a framework around which the project can flourish.

My projects move along smoothly because the details are attended to- they exceed expectations because I understand that these details are steps on a journey. I am a shepherd, lighting the way toward the successful execution and implementation of project objectives. This is not to say that I am content with the status quo, rather my proactive style affords the luxury of risk taking and my insights and passion help inspire it.
