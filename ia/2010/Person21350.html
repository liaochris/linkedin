Ben Armstrong’s Summary

I am a fifteen year old Graphic Designer and Teen Internet Entrepreneur living in Raleigh, North Carolina.

I have been experimenting with both emerging and current web and graphic technologies for just shy of four years. I created my first graphics-oriented company at the age of eleven, and have had an amazing journey to where I am today. I am blessed to be able to work with an extremely talented group of teenage graphic and web designers. In my spare time - I am also an amateur photographer, audio recording/live engineer, videographer, podcaster, and student pilot.

When I'm at my desk, I help keep the money coming in over at 9miles Media. I get passionate about bringing a diverse range of creative talent to the table, and absolutely adore stimulating creative people to perform at the best possible level, in all their professional and personal endeavors.
