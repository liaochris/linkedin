Valentina Vitols’s Summary

I am writer, a photographer and a daydreamer from Caracas, Venezuela. I am currently living in Seattle, surrounded by warm coffee, delicious chocolate and the beautiful Pacific Northwest landscape.

I am passionate about my work. I own Valentina Vitols Studio, where I develop my photography practice. I am also working on utilizing this space as a gallery for emerging artists' work. I recently participated as a freelance curator for the Mission Arts and Performance Project (MAPP) in San Francisco.

I am also a contributor for Art Voices Magazine, a national Arts publication based in New Orleans, LA. I have been writing for 20 years, and tuned my talent at several writing centers in Caracas and at the Summer Writing Festival in the University of Iowa.

I have traveled thoroughly as a photographer. I have documentary work from Thailand, Cambodia, Myanmar, Mexico, Argentina, Uruguay, Peru, Costa Rica, Venezuela and the US, and have shown my work in several cities in the United States and in Caracas. I also published a book in 2008, Buenos Aires, Diario de Viaje.

Currently, I'm conducting photographic tours in Seattle for a great start-up company, Shutter Tours (http://www.shuttertours.com/). I'm very excited to be able to talk to our visitors about Seattle's history and teach them photography at the same time.

Among my academic achievements, I have a Master in Fine Arts (M.F.A.) in Photography from Savannah College of Art and Design, a Specialization in Integrated Marketing Communications from Universidad Metropolitana and a Law Degree from Universidad Católica Andrés Bello.
