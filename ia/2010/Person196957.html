Sue Canfield’s Summary

Co-Authored The Commonsense Virtual Assistant - Becoming an Entrepreneur, Not an Employee, available at Amazon.com.

Working as a virtual assistant since 2005, Sue now coaches new and aspiring virtual assistants in what it takes to succeed in business. She and her husband, Joel D Canfield, have developed a coaching program to help these new business owners succeed.

For over 25 years Sue Canfield, Virtual Office Administrator and Owner of Awesome Assistant, has helped small business owners with administrative tasks. Since 2005 she has worked with over 30 clients to help them grow their businesses, specifically by using online marketing strategies such as email newsletters, blogs, articles, and social networking. Her mission is to partner with her clients to work together to create and implement strategies to promote their businesses. For more information about how she can help your business, visit http://ChiefVirtualOfficer.com

Sue Canfield’s Specialties:

Coaching new and aspiring virtual assistants to become Chief Virtual Officers now that they are entrepreneurs and business owners and no longer employees.

Co-authored the book, The Commonsense Virtual Assistant - Becoming an Entrepreneur, Not an Employee, available at Amazon.
