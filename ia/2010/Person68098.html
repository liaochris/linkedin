Evan Williams’s Summary

Evan Williams, CEO of AEGIS Consulting, Inc. assists clients with complex SAP solution architecture design, project planning, implementation projects, change management and systems integration projects. This experience encompasses both US and International clientele.

Prior to AEGIS Consulting, Inc., Evan held C-Level Management positions with Southern Pacific Company, Santa Fe Southern Pacific Company, Southern Pacific Land Company, Southern Pacific Development Company, Rio Bravo Oil Company, and Santa Fe Pacific Timber Company.

For the past ten years Evan has worked with the Big Four Management Consulting firms and other mid-market implementation firms in the SAP space of the ERP market.

Evan specializes in SAP Project Management, ASAP Project Methodology, Solution Manager for Process and Configuration management, SAP Integrated process design, configuration testing and knowledge transfer to client personnel. Evan's experience in SAP's New G/L enhances client Management Accounting and Legal Reporting provides compliance with GAAP and Tax rules and provides real time Management Reporting to enhance business effectiveness and profitability.

Several projects have also involved participation in the design and maintenance of Master Data Management (MDM) and Data Migration and Conversion from legacy systems into SAP. Evan helps guide clients in defining Master Data Strategy and Data Governance related to Vendor Master, Customer and Material Master,
