Bartek Witek’s Summary

People are my interest. In my business I want to build strong relationship between people I am dealing with. I am privileged to photograph most important moments for my clients.

I am a member of WPJA - Wedding Photojournalist Association (www.wpja.com) and [AG]WPJA - Artistic Guild of the Wedding Photojournalist Association (www.agwpja.com).

Main goals:
Capturing beautiful weddings of fantastic couples all over the world in photojournalistic style, quietly without interrupting.

Bartek Witek’s Specialties:

Documentary Wedding Photography, photojournalism, reportage wedding photography, story telling, lifestyle portrait photography.
