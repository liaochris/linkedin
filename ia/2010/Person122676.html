Laurie Oswald’s Summary

Laurie Oswald has over twenty years of successful sales, strategic planning, and marketing management experience with a broad business background in both manufacturing and service sectors of the communications industry. She has held roles with increasing levels of responsibility helping top companies in the premedia and print sector increase their revenues, improve margins and strengthen customer focus. Her efforts have influenced a wide variety of direct marketers, publishers and retailers to improve advertising results and enhance their brands.

She is now utilizing her passion for true productivity and work/life balance as the Director of Sales & Marketing for McGhee Productivity Solutions, an international consulting company that works with Fortune 500 executives and their teams to increase performance and accountability, execute strategy, and improve work/life balance.
