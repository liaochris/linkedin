Ed Rooney’s Summary

I have a long history in website and web application design and development. I enjoy the challenge of architecting solutions for my clients and I focus on a data-first approach to design. I get at the root of my clients' business model, understand the data model according to work flow and then bridge them together with elegant design achieved in a number of programming languages and technologies,

Combine this with a keen understanding of web marketing and search engine optimization and it becomes clear why VT Web Properties consistently delivers creative, engaging websites that meet the business needs of clients, but also the environmental imperative of being carbon-neutral.
