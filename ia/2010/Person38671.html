    CHRISTIANA ENTERPRISES

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    October 1994 — July 2009 (14 years 10 months)

    AS SOLE PROPRIETOR, AND FOUNDER, I CREATED, PROMOTED, AND PRODUCED CHRISTIANA HANDBAGS, IN 1994. I SPECIALIZED IN HAND BEAD WORK, AND VINTAGE STYLE FRAME BAGS . READY TO WEAR, HOME FURNISHINGS, FOOTWEAR, AND MORE CATEGORIES WERE ADDED ALONG THE WAY. BASED OUT OF MY GARAGE, AND IMPORTED FROM INDIA, , I PIONEERED THE CONCEPT OF BEADED BAGS FOR EVERYDAY, NOT JUST FOR EVENING. AT THIS TIME, BEADED BAGS TYPICALLY SOLD AS EVENING BAGS, AND WERE PROMOTED AS SPECIAL OCCASION ACCESSORIES,HOLDING A FAIRLY SMALL SEGMENT IN THE MARKETPLACE. I WAS DETERMINED TO CHANGE THESE STATISTICS, AND WITH A SLOW STEADY GROWTH, CHRISTIANA HANDBAGS EVENTUALLY HIT RECORD SALES WORD WIDE. IN 2000, MY LITTLE HANDBAG BUSINESS BOASTED ABOUT 1 MILLION IN ANNUAL SALES. IN A 6 MONTH PERIOD, 1 MILLION IN SALES EXPLODED TO 10 MILLION, YES 10 MILLION IN SALES. I HELD ON TO THIS FIRE BREATHING DRAGON, AND MANAGED TO SHIP 8.5 MILLION IN REVENUE THAT YEAR. I'M STILL TRYING TO GET MY FEET ON THE GROUND.
