Mark Yolton’s Summary

Currently: Senior Vice President of the SAP Community Network at the largest and most successful business application software company in the world. Together, SAP's virtual communities exceed two million members, are extremely active, fast growing, influential examples of Web 2.0 and social networking best practices for the enterprise (Enterprise 2.0).

Specific role-based communities include:
* The SAP Developer Network (SDN) which includes developers, enterprise architects, sys admins, and partners that surround and extend the core SAP environment.
* The Business Process Expert (BPX) community which is the world's largest community of business analysts, solution consultants, and similar roles.
* The BusinessObjects Community (BOC) for customers and partners engaged with Crystal Reports, Excelsius, Explorer, and other business intelligence technologies.
* The University Alliances Community (UAC) at SAP, which connects students and professors from 1000 global universities to each other and to current practitioners.

Also responsible for community events including SAP TechEd (four major events on three continents) and SAP Tech Tour (50 events on six continents). Together, these events attract ~30,000 community members to connect, collaborate, and co-innovate with SAP.

Also: SAP's engagement in major business and technology standards organizations (more than 100 standards bodies), and SAP's open source strategy teams and activities.

Previously: Senior Director of Oracle Web Marketing, with responsibility for Oracle.com, Oracle Technology Network (OTN), Oracle Partner Network (OPN), and MyOracle (the employee intranet).

Previously: Led PeopleSoft.com and core aspects of its customer and partner portals. Vice President of Marketing and Strategy for Sun Microsystems. Senior Director of eBusiness at Sun. Regional Director of Marketing Services at Unisys Corporation.
