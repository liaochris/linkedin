Naomi Christine Leapheart’s Summary

Naomi Christine Leapheart is a teacher, facilitator, and consultant with over 6 years of experience in fundraising, communications, program planning, and leadership development.

She specializes in helping start-ups build capacity, training youth to be progressive, college-bound leaders, and supporting young professionals as they shape and manage their careers.

In 2006, Naomi founded The MatchStick Group, a nonprofit that empowers young African-Americans to be benevolent leaders.

A published writer, Naomi is working on the forthcoming book, Sojourner’s Daughters: young black women on sex, femininity, and leadership and teaches a course called Write Change! teens publishing to provoke revolution.

Naomi is president of Philadelphia’s Young Nonprofit Leaders, an organization that strengthens the sector by responding to the needs of next-generation nonprofit leaders. Proud to serve on several local boards, she is guided by the motto ‘with gifting comes responsibility’ (adapted from Luke 12:48).
