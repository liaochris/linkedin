Wendy Marx’s Summary

Wendy Marx is an award-winning B2B public relations and marketing communications executive specializing in B2B branding for companies and executives.

Wendy planned and executed the public relations strategy that helped fuel the spectacular growth of Peppers and Rogers Group, the world’s preeminent customer relationship firm. A recent launch of the Communications Industry Forecast for Veronis Suhler Stevenson, a private equity firm, resulted in more than 200 media placements from the New York Times…to USA Today…to The Financial Times. Her launch of Investars.com resulted in partnership deals with JP Morgan Chase, Dow Jones, the Bank of New York, and The Nasdaq.

As President of Marx Communications, a B2B PR Agency, Wendy is expert at creating thought leadership and content development programs for clients that create visibility, credibility, and ultimately, sales leads.

Outside of her success in B2B PR, Wendy’s technology and business articles have appeared in The New York Times, Computerworld, InformationWeek, Brandweek, and Advertising Age, among others. She has written advertorials for Fortune and Forbes on management topics. Her writing skills earned her a position teaching business writing at the University of Michigan Business School. She also writes a blog on personal branding for Fast Company.
