Carter Cathey, PRC’s Summary

I have spent the last dozen years serving clients and selling research products. These products have included both custom and syndicated research as well as both quantitative and qualitative research.

I have been specifically recognized for both increasing the profitability of existing clients through excellent service and for generating new clients. I specialize in high-touch relationship management and business development built upon my strong background in client service and my written and verbal communication skills.
