Petru Pacuraru’s Summary

I graduated Business Management University with the Academy of Economic Studies in Bucharest.
In 2005, I started a 1 year program of project management with ASEBUSS , which was finished in 2006.
I was a member of AIESEC Bucharest, responsible for fund raising and assistant and project manger on various national and international projects.
In 2003 I joined Leaders Foundation, one of the top Romanian organizations profiled on Leadership in all 4 communities; Business, Media, Public Administration and Civil Society. In 2004 I became the president of the organization, managing national programs such as Leaders Awards, BrandActivity, Leaders Forum, Authentic Leadership, as well as international projects with the foundation of Prince Charles- IBLF or with NESST, developing a national program for transformation from sponsorship- based NGO’S to self sustained NGO’s. Managing one of the biggest virtual community of entrepreneurs in Romania, Entrepreneur’s Club, with almost 800 members, I plan to take my work to another level by building a professional network that can help the SME sector in Romania , with shared know how and performance based recommendations.

In 2003 I joined Human Performance Development International, a Romanian HR company specialized in trainings, marketing studies and B2B consultancy. In 2004 I became Managing Partner of HPDI, representing Jim Bagnola in South Eastern Europe, dealing M&A’s in Romania as sole representant of the international B2B network Enablers International.
In 2008 I started a new advertising project in Romania, multimedia, teaming with Elevate, one of the most known indoor advertising agency in Romania.We built one of the first indoor multimedia network, in office buildings, and public spaces.
2009 was the year when the first HasHas Coffee&Lounge was born, starting a unique and unconventional coffee shop concept.Strong colors, freehand drawings on the walls, beds and couches.In 2010 we opened the second one.
