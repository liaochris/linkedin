Dan Hughes’s Summary

Dan Hughes lives is in Morrisville, North Carolina. The obvious place for a guy born and raised in Bristol, England. Dan is the Principal and Founder of Triangle Response Marketing, an affiliate marketing company. The TRM team are a group of creative, dedicated individuals with an entrepreneurial heart. Serious about business but committed to making a positive difference in the world. On a daily basis they prove that successful business is not just about getting people to spend money. The company is devoted to helping local small business compete and win against big business competition. They offer Pro Bono consulting to qualifying companies. He's the Father of two fantastic kids who have him well and truly wrapped around their little fingers. Dan drinks way too much coffee, which is helpful as he never did like to sleep much anyway.
