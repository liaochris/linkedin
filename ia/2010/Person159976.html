Patrick Levac’s Summary

Holstead & Redmond Limited has been in business since 1952. We were unable to find a time and billing system that fit our industry so we developed our own. This product became a web based project managment system with time and billing, CRM, document management and record index capabilities. We created a business called PRL Info Systems Inc. to market this new application, called PIMARC. This was started in 2004 and we have been successful in selling the application to other surveyors and direct competitors to our surveying business. Both businesses complement each other very well.

Patrick Levac’s Specialties:

We specialize in GPS surveying, digital mapping and detailed project management with the use of PIMARC.
