Stephanie Michele’s Summary

I can honestly say I have been promoting, teaching and talking about social networking for 10 years now. (If you don't believe me, ask me to show you my first brochure printed in 2000.) I am thrilled to be able to share best practices through my new company, SocialBling. We connect people based on their most inspired goal. SocialBling is more than a company to me; it allows me to serve other people in the best way I know how. It is a "good life" to be able to do what you are passionate about, with SocialBling I hope to help people live out their passions too.
