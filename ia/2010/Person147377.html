    The Physicians Wellness Products is a team of physicians, scientists, and business leaders that have come together to help physicians provide educational information to their patients. Our product line follows the OTC pharmaceutical manufacturing standards set forth by the FDA. Due to the quality of our products we have support and have teamed up with credible institutions like NASA, The International Olympic Committee and the World Anti-Doping Association. According to a study done by Harvard, 87% of American's take some sort of daily vitamin or supplement regiment and 90% of those people would prefer to get discretionary information from one of their trusted health care providers as opposed to someone at the local nutrition store (most of whom have no education about nutrition) Annuls of Internal Medicine, August 2006.

    Our program is non obtrusive and fits seamlessly into any health care providers practice regardless of specialty. We implement a professional, seamless, turn-key educational awareness program within practices providing patients with a value added service that generates an additional revenue stream for the practice. The health care providers involved with The Physician Wellness Group feel an ethical obligation to make sure their patients are educated on the lack of standards and quality in the supplement industry. Our health care providers believe it is their responsibility to educate patients and should they choose to take nutritional supplementation products, provide them with an avenue to get products that are safe, effective, and pharmaceutical quality.
