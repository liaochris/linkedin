Florent Peyre’s Summary

I run all aspects of Business and Corporate Development for the Digital Group of Hachette Filipacchi Media in the U.S., including partnerships with portals, mid and long tail publishers and social networks.

I also oversee corporate development and M&A for the Digital group, including acquisition sourcing, acquisition strategy, due diligences, valuation and financial evaluations of contemplated targets.

That enables me to work closely with the financial community (venture capitalists, investment bankers), the portal key players and the entrepreneur community.

Hachette in the US has around 7 websites (representing around 10m UVs internally - 6m in ComScore) including the brands Elle, CarandDriver, Road and Track, Woman's Day, Elle Decor and Metropolitan Home) as well as Jumpstart Automotive, an online ad network specialized on the Automotive space, based in SF. Jumpstart represents major automotive publishers and rank as #4 in terms of UVs size in ComScore.

Hachette is the leading worlwide magazine publisher, part of Lagardere Active, with annual revenues around +$3bn.

I am actively seeking new partnership and acquisition opportunities for our properties.
