J.L. Lewis’s Summary

IF YOU PLAN COMPANY EVENTS, READ ON….

Welcome! I’m JL Lewis, professional golfer and two-times winner of the PGA TOUR. I’m also the author of the POCKET PRO: Golf Tips from the Tour.

If there’s one thing I love as much as playing golf, it’s helping others enjoy more success — on the course and in their lives. That’s why I provide a service of outings and events for corporations.

Spending the day with golfers at all levels, I teach the mental and practical skills of a winner. More than that, I share the stories and wisdom I’ve gleaned from 20 years on the PGA TOUR.


ENJOY THE SPIRIT OF THE PGA TOUR, LIVE AT YOUR EVENT

Naturally, I’m a lifelong student of the game of golf. But I’m equally passionate about mastering the game of life. The PGA TOUR has been my teacher, and what I’ve learned has helped thousands achieve greater happiness and personal success.

During my events, I make a point to give attention to every single guest. Over the years, I’ve developed a rare ability to spot golfing problems and fix them in minutes. My secret is that I can instantly assess the individual’s capabilities and help each guest realize his or her natural potential.

My personal style is informal and humorous. People of all kinds, and golfers at every level, find me approachable and fun to be with. What I most enjoy is interaction — from sharing a joke to advising on a major challenge.

As a matter of course, every attendee receives a copy of my book of golf tips, and after the event your guests are invited to follow up with me personally through my website.

Whether I’m giving a keynote address, providing a golf clinic for a large crowd, or a more personal outing for a small group, my commitment is the same — to provide an unforgettable and uplifting experience to each and every attendee.


WHAT TO DO NEXT

If you’re planning an event, why not call my wonderful team and discuss your needs? You can reach us at 512-263-4016.
