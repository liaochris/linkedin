Bharath Ramakrishna’s Summary

My experience revolves around designing and developing 2D/3D image processing algorithms for Medical Image (MR/CT/US modalities) analysis.

I have worked on several projects in Medical Imaging specifically, the design of Computer Aided Diagnosis (CAD) systems for (i) the Knee (cartilage segmentation, meniscal segmentation, bone abnormalities) on MR Imagery, (ii) Liver (Tumor Segmentation, Tumor Burden estimation for subjects undergoing Chemotherapy and RF ablation) on MR Imagery and (iii) Carotid Arteries (plaque estimation) on CT Imagery.

Some of the earlier projects I have been involved include Embedded DSP solutions for hand-held assays for US Army Proving Grounds and protocols for Chesapeake Bay water quality monitoring for NASA. I have also conducted extensive research on issues involved in non-literal hyperspectral image compression.
