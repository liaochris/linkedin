Celeste-Linguere Johnston’s Summary

Celeste-Linguere has nearly four decades of experience as a motivational speaker and as a consultant for business transactions, jury selection and trial preparation, and news commentary.

With her interest in the dynamics of non-verbal communication, she has used her diverse background and skills to develop the discipline of Physiognomics for practical application in business, law, and politics – wherever one party wants to go beyond what is being said by gaining the extra dimension of being able to interpret the other party’s non-verbal communication.
