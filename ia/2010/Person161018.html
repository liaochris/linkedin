Paul Keetch’s Summary

A lifelong entrepreneur, I started my first business at the age of 13, selling personalized gifts door-to-door. I have owned several successful businesses including Brokenfeet Design & Consulting and Atlas Avenue Artist Management.

My professional career has focused primarily on fast-moving, entrepreneurial technology and information based companies, including leading roles at Interhop Network Services (then Canada’s largest independent Internet provider) and Peak Potentials Training (the fastest-growing personal development company in North America).

In my leadership role at Learning Annex (Canada), I had the fortune and honor to work with some of the world’s thought leaders, including Dr. Deepak Chopra, Dr. Brian Greene and Dr. David Suzuki, among countless others.

A seasoned Marketing professional and experienced coach I specialize in assisting solopreneurs streamline their operations, develop innovative "guerrilla" marketing strategies and leverage their existing product, service and expertise to generate additional dollars on the bottom line.

In 2008 I co-wrote and digitally published a digital marketing book for new and small business owners called Make My Marketing Work: How to win customers and make more money. (http://www.MakeMyMarketingWork.com)

Please contact me at info [at] paul keetch [dot] com for more information.
