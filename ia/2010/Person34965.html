Marjorie Price’s Summary

Psychiatric Nurse Clinician, Certified Hypnotherapist, and author of self-healing, guided meditation, and hypnosis products provides self-help tools for individuals struggling with disempowerment via psychiatric, holistic, and spiritual therapies. Organizer and leader of women's spirituality groups since the late 1980s; lectures and provides seminars on holistic practice and recovery therapies.

Other areas of specialty include grief counseling, empowerment training for nurses and other health care providers, and hands-on healing for individuals with chronic pain and insomnia

Marjorie Price’s Specialties:

Small group dynamics, assertiveness training, creative writing, guided meditation, codependency and inner child recovery, behavioral therapies, motivational coaching
