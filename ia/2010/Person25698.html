Bob Stearns’s Summary

Managing Partner- Longship
Alternative Asset Management LLC.

Longship works with private banks, family offices, fund of funds,
endowments and foundations in the area of alternative investments.
The Longship organization focuses on global macro, currency,
commodities and long / short healthcare hedge fund managers.

Bob Stearns has over 20 years experience in the hedge fund business. He spent
8 years with Bruce Kovner at Caxton Corporation, one of the largest hedge funds
in the business. Bruce taught me how to trade and Peter D’Angelo taught me
how to run a business.

Bob began his career on Wall Street with Nelson Chang in Shearson’s
commodity research division, where he spent four years as a
technical analyst and chartist. Bob then spent two years as a currency trader
at the Chase Manhattan Bank before joining Caxton.

Bob has been the CIO, senior analyst and market strategist at several fund of funds
including Kenmar in Greenwich, CT, which has been in business since 1983.
Bob joined Kenmar as a senior hedge fund analyst,in 2000
after leaving his position as CIO of the fund of funds group at EGM in San Francisco.

Contact Bob at Bob@LongshipLLC.com

Bob Stearns’s Specialties:

We have been through several bear markets. I sat in
Bruce Kovner’s office at Caxton during the 1987 crash and I was
in San Francisco in 1999 at the top of the Dot.com crash. We believe
in the wisdom of grey hair, we earned every one of them.
