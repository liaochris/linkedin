Milton H. Werner, Ph.D.’s Summary

Internationally known scientist from the Rockefeller University who transitioned into corporate management for life science start-up through early clinical stage companies. Seasoned management professional with scientific and business development credentials in oncology, immunology, autoimmune disorders and infectious disease. Highly innovative professional and business leader that injects enthusiasm into every project. Seeks to bring novel therapies all the way to market. Developed cash flow driven business strategy for biotechnology companies.
