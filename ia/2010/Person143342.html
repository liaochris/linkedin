Michael Promes’s Summary

With over 10 years of IT experience comprised of systems, administration, management and client management skills, I most enjoy utilizing my talent to develop great, long-lasting relationships with the colleagues and clients I currently and previously have had the pleasure of working with.

Over the last 10 years, no matter what the position, I especially take great care to approach IT systems management with a detail oriented view and become a trusted advisor to my customers and clients. Once this is established, superb customer service naturally follows. I've always enjoyed being a social, friendly person and providing solutions for people that makes sense and their work lives easier.

My current goal is to further align my company with the IT needs of small and medium sized Biotechnology, Legal, and Non-Profit organizations. Even in the current economic climate, we continue to grow our IT Solutions business into one of the top IT companies is the San Francisco Bay Area (www.servlogix.com) without compromising quality. We pride ourselves on years of experience providing proven systematized IT solutions and outsourced management for clients of many sizes. We're well on our way to soon becoming a Microsoft GOLD certified partner, and certified by the MSP alliance having been recognized by our clients and peers.

Above all, my personal biggest goal is to keep my employees happy and customer satisfaction at its current 100% while, as a team, we all perform the above.
