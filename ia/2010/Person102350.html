  • Creator & Executive Director

    Rickter Duck Earthquake Preparedness Program

    (Public Safety industry)

    1984 — 1989 (5 years )

    In 1984, Mr. Kennedy created Richter Duck, a fun and educational cartoon character and earthquake preparedness program entitled "Richter Duck’s Operation Quake-Quake". The objective of the project is to promote earthquake awareness and safety especially for children in a fun and non-threatening way. He served as Executive Director and public service spokesman, based in California, for many years. He is an expert on earthquake preparedness. He was responsible for all creative aspects of the project including writing and designing the Richter Duck coloring books, instructional brochures, T-Shirts, stickers, safety kits and life-sized costume. Sponsors and patrons of the Richter Duck program
    included the San Francisco Unified School District, the Honorable Cyril Magnin; Chief of Protocol for San Francisco, Mrs. Frank Sinatra, the NFL, the Kennedy's of Charleston, and other private and public foundations.

  • President & Designer

    Facade of New York, LTD.

    (Design industry)

    1980 — 1983 (3 years )

    As President of Façade of New York, Ltd., his fashion parody on Ralph Lauren’s popular Polo logo, Mr. Kennedy's timely and clever concept, combined with his innovative advertising campaigns: "Put on a Façade!"”and "For some of us polo is only a game!" created an overnight marketing and fashion sensation and demand for his product. Mr. Kennedy was directly responsible for the manufacturing and distribution of the Façade shirt label to more than 250 upscale boutiques throughout the United States. Celebrities, models and politicians were among the many consumers who wore Façade shirts.

John Joseph Kennedy’s Honors:

In October 2005 Mr. Kennedy launched his humanitarian and environmental campaign, The Campaign for the New Humanity. When time allows, he has been touring the United States sharing his profound vision and speech: My Dream For The New Humanity.

He has been invited to many countries, including Jaipur, India to be the keynote speaker at the International Conference on Peace.

In 2005 he received the International Speakers Award for Outstanding Achievement.

Mr. Kennedy is also the 2005 recipient of the International Humanitarian Award, Knights of Conscious International from the All India Letter Writers Association (AILWA), Bombay, India, for his letters and speeches promoting global peace.

Throughout his career, his work and inspiring life-giving messages and speeches have reached millions of people around the world.
