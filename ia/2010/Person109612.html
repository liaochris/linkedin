John C. Williams’s Summary

My passion for online communities, virtual worlds and games dates back to my 1980’s membership in QLink, the predecessor to AOL. I was part of the first virtual world community: "Habitat's Club Caribe". Since then, I have worked extensively providing leadership to various virtual communities for over 10 years. Some of those communities include: AOL, Forterrainc.com, There.com, Habbo.com, Big Fish Games and Meez.com.

I have a proven record of success utilizing strong customer service, leadership, and project development skills. I've learned a lot over the years about what works, what hasn't and what's still possible. My background demonstrates strong management talents some of which include:

*Managed employees and large teams of remote volunteer staff in online chat and virtual spaces with responsibility for recruitment, training, scheduling, and performance management.
*Coordinated and implemented multiple live events to build the online community membership and enhance revenues. Including celebrity events, talk-show style events, contests, promotions, and interactive games.
*Acted as a business consultant to leading companies in online communication, creating promotional events and marketing programs to drive revenues and build customer service relationships.
*Monitored and moderated online communication for compliance with operating standards and resolved issues with members and staff to maintain morale.
*Conducted quality assurance and product review for various online community and moderation tools.
