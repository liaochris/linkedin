Denise Spacinsky’s Summary

Denise Spacinsky manages recruitment in both staffing for Diversity and Localization professionals.

Denise is CEO of Equator Sourcing Services (www.equatorsourcing.com) which supports what she thinks is a key ingredient for success for many businesses - staffing for Diversity. Based on her experience in working with complex organizations that celebrate different points of view and her international staffing experience, she looks works with clients who list Diversity and Inclusion on their corporate strategic plans.

Denise also is Partner & Director, Americas at Larsen Globalization (www.larseng11n.com). Larsen Globalization offers recruitment services with a speciality focus in localization and globalization. Denise draws on her globalization experience in staffing for clients.
