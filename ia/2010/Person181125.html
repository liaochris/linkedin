Sam Garula’s Summary

Sam is a successful business operations professional comfortable with the responsibilities of strategic planning, business management and performance assessment. He successfully achieved double-digit sales and profit growth—in a down economy—by accurately assessing market trends in his industry. Adept at identifying customer needs, his expertise includes directing daily business activities, recruiting talent, employee training, performance evaluation, quality control and strategic program development, just to name a few.

Through his principle centered leadership style, Sam successfully develops people around him to exceed performance expectations. His success in business operations is widely known and reflected in higher sales and profits, lower warranty costs, improved asset management, reduced turnover and long-term profitable customer loyalty in companies he's worked with.

Sam's many expert methods include individual and work-group goal-setting, proforma evaluation of company performance, operations auditing, employee education and training, talent recruitment, TQM and customer advocacy.
