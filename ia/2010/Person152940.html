Neil Moran’s Summary

You've got better things to do than to hound a writer for the article you need on
deadline. Or to tie up in-house staff who could be better utilized in the area of
their own expertise. Why not hire an experienced writer? One who you can
develop a close business and personal relationship with so that you'll get the
work you need done without worry or hassle.

I'm a dependable, talented writer who delivers well-researched, accurate
articles and content in a timely manner, time and time again. And quite frankly,
editors like my work, as is evident by OutLookByTheBay editor Tecla
Emerson's recent comment on one of my submissions.

“Thanks so much for yet another great article—hope to hear from you
for the early spring.”
