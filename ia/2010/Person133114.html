Marissa Rousso’s Summary

My goal is to build relationships and hone my interpersonal skills with individuals from different backgrounds. I want to share personal experiences with people in an attempt to gain more knowledge about the environment and how it is changing. In building these relationships and making connections with people all over the world, my goal is to raise public awareness about important global issues and improve international relations. We live in a globally interdependent economy and I believe that we can all be a part of the solution to ending extreme, or absolute, poverty by 2015. I want to embrace the changes that need to take place in order for our world to continue to thrive in a sustainable and positive direction. By educating children and people we encounter about the long term benefits of working together and understanding our system, the future of our world will be promising and the quality of life will improve dramatically. Only with gratitude for our lives will our global economy become rich. We can all be a part of the solution.
