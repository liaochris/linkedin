Gustavo Gil’s Summary

Currently working as a Senior UX consultant and Information Architect at CASANOVA Fullthinking, and breathing internet projects since 1997.
My passions are all matters involving UX, Usability and Internet/Users studies. Graduation on Interface Design and Computing.
I can offer your company:
- Passion and commitment with my profession. Always putting my heart to achieve success.
- Over ten years of experience in planning and development of internet/intranet projects.
- Ability to develop graphic interfaces with the highest standards in usability providing rich experiences for the users.
- Capacity to realize the user needs and to understand critical situations providing creative and innovative solutions.
- Experience in identifying opportunities.
- Ability in develop a product from its conception until its detailed specifications
- Capacity to create workshops and trainings.
- Capacity to planning and application of usability tests as well as analyze the results

In addition to my extensive web experience, I have excellent communication skills. I always maintain a gracious and professional manner when communicating with people. I believe my broad experience and range of skills make me a great help for web projects and teams.
