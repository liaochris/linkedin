Bonnie McEwan’s Summary

Bonnie has over 20 years experience leading marketing and communications efforts for nonprofit organizations. For the past 13 years she has also held various faculty positions with the Milano Graduate School of Management and Urban Policy at The New School in New York City, where she teaches courses in media advocacy, social marketing and nonprofit management.

Bonnie is profiled in the leadership book, Thriving in 24/7: Six Strategies for Taming the New World of Work, by Sally Helgesen, published by The Free Press.

Bonnie McEwan’s Specialties:

My firm, Make Waves, specializes in marketing and communications for nonprofit organizations. We are especially interested in working with advocacy groups seeking social change in the areas of health & medicine, racial, gender & LGBTQ equity, human rights and economic justice.
