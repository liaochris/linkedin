Amit Mukherjee’s Summary

Experience: 25 years of post MBA involvement in business as an educator/researcher, consultant and line executive.

Goal: To help businesses thrive in an economy in which companies no longer compete one-on-one, but as parts of networks against other networks.

Amit Mukherjee’s Specialties:

strategy, organizational effectiveness and design, innovation, technology management, executive education

Amit Mukherjee’s Experience

  • President

    Ishan Advisors, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    May 2004 — Present (5 years 9 months)

    Ishan Advisors (www.ishanadvisors.com) provides strategy consulting and executive education services. Amit has worked on market entry and technology/operations strategy efforts, as well as organizational effectiveness initiatives in blue chip firms. He regularly teaches mid-tier to senior executives at companies like J&J, ConAgra and MDS.

  • Vice President and Strategy Advisor

    Forrester Research

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; FORR; Research industry)

    February 2001 — April 2004 (3 years 3 months)

    Started Forrester's strategy consulting business. Team executed projects for senior executives, including those at the C-level, of blue chip companies like SAP, J&J, United Healthcare.

  • Chief Technology & Strategy Officer

    TurboChef

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Consumer Goods industry)

    October 1998 — April 2000 (1 year 7 months)

    As a key member of the alliance leadership team led by Maytag’s CEO, he oversaw technology transfer to Maytag and shaped the strategy for a $100 million residential product launch. He also negotiated intellectual property partnerships with LG Electronics/LGSI and MIT and led the development of next-generation technology.

  • Director

    Arthur D. Little

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; ADL; Management Consulting industry)

    September 1994 — October 1998 (4 years 2 months)

    At Arthur D. Little (1994-98), where he became Director (i.e., partner) in record time, Amit served on the leadership teams of the Manufacturing Technology and Consumer Industries practices; he had co-founded the former. These practices often applied his doctoral research to improve client operations and develop new products. A major project he personally led created a next generation product that industry experts said could not be done. Amit was an advisor to the CEO of Dunkin' Brands and the Board of Gallo Wineries.

  • Assistant Professor

    INSEAD

    (Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Higher Education industry)

    January 1992 — June 1994 (2 years 6 months)

    As an Assistant Professor at INSEAD (France) (1992-1994), he taught R&D and technology strategy, advanced manufacturing and quality in the Executive, MBA, and Ph.D. programs.

Amit Mukherjee’s Interests:
