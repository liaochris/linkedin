Kevin McGourty’s Summary

My expertise is Product Planning, Marketing, Development and Management. My background includes a variety of senior management roles, and represents a vast set of skills, including strategic and business planning, marketing, operations, research & development and engineering. My multi-discipline management expertise and hands-on experience with new business development and technologies provides me the ability to see the big business picture and anticipate the needs of the market to help clients achieve top line growth and bottom line efficiencies.

Currently I am President & Chief Executive Officer of iNPD Center, Inc., whose mission is to improve clients’ success rate of launching successful new products while eliminating the waste and expense of ill-conceived market entries.

I served as Director of Product Development and Management at SeaSpace Corporation and Vice President of Product Management and Planning at Varitronic Systems. At Varitronics, I led the effort in creating a strategic business and product planning process that resulted in a successful rejuvenation of the business enabling it to increase sales from $36 million to $56 million in less than four years. I started my career in the Silicon Valley at GTE Sylvania and Eaton Corporation as an electronic design engineer in the defense and telecommunication industries.

In addition to my professional activities at iNPD Center, I chair TechAmerica's New Product Development Roundtable. I am a certified New Product Development Professional (NPDP) and served as President of the Southern California Chapter of the Product Development and Management Association. I teach new product development management courses at UCSD Extension and I am an Entrepreneur in Residence at Connect.
