Pat Egan’s Summary

I try to make all sales transactions smooth for my real estate clients. I want them to understand the process as much as possible, so that they feel comfortable with their new home investment.

Taken Courses and completed the required number of Sales to become an Associated Broker in Real Estate.

Always trying to increase my knowledge in the real estate field, so that I may better serve my clients.
