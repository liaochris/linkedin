L. LaShawn Brown’s Summary

SUMMARY

Professional Trainer/Workshop Facilitator

I have provided on site professional development sessions for educational institutions, for profit and not-for-profit organizations, medical/mental health personnel and those in the hospitality industry.

Using a model called “Everybody Matters,” which is an interactive workshop that illuminates and models the conscious act of appreciating others. This is done by discovering, discussing, understanding and thus appreciating the unique value each person brings to every relationship.

Created as a basic course, “Everybody Matters” is tailored to meet the specialized needs and/challenges specific to that entity or organization.

For example, training for board members might focus on peer relations, while sessions geared to those studying or already practicing in helping professions (such as medical, dental or teachers) the content would deal with examples unique to each industry and the population served.

As a result of this training, all participants should able to offer their clientele better customer service by using effective group activities to improve the internal communication of their team. Better communication increases both productivity and revenue.

Finally, not only do I offer a very cost effective method to deal with communication roadblocks and inclusion issues in a safe, fun, extremely interactive and non threatening atmosphere, but I also offer follow up training sessions to reiterate and reinforce the material when requested to maintain effectiveness.
