Joshua Treviño’s Summary

Joshua S. Treviño is the founder and president of Treviño Strategies and Media, Inc. He also presently serves as Communications Director for DeVore for California.

He is an experienced communicator, writer and entrepreneur, with accomplishments to his credit from the head-of-state level to mass-appeal media. He is deeply engaged in American politics and policy, and has been directly involved in the same in Europe, Africa, the Middle East and East Asia. His experience encompasses government, military, nonprofit, campaign, and private-sector affairs.

Joshua Treviño’s Specialties:

A truly versatile communicator and analyst on issues of public policy, both domestic and foreign. Available for consultation and work on media, communications, public relations, campaigns, and public policy.
