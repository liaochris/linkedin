Barry Silver’s Summary

With over 14 years experience in the software industry and 11 years in film and television production, I bring a unique blend of skills and a positive attitude to every project.

As a Certified Scrummaster (CSM) and Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), I manage software development projects to streamline the process and make software better, faster and cheaper. This makes my clients more competitive and more profitable. As a Project Manager, I delight in bringing projects in under budget, on time and as agreed.

Unlike others who focus just on the business needs, or concentrate solely on cool technology, I offer an integrated approach that combines business acumen, creative vision and the effective use of people and technology. I take a multi-disciplinary approach to every project I deliver and I'd like to do this for you.
