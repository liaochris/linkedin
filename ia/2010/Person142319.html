  • Project Manager

    Merck

    (Public Company; MRK; Pharmaceuticals industry)

    July 1990 — June 1995 (5 years )

    Responsible for the testing, installation, and validation of the world’s first paperless pharmaceutical manufacturing facility. Coordinated site installation activities including strategic planning, test philosophy, database population, system interactions, and validation procedures. Responsible for the design and installation of Flexible Laboratory Automation systems throughout the various Merck divisions. Developed a general purpose laboratory controller capable of performing 'lights out' analysis of pharmaceuticals by integrating and coordinating laboratory equipment and instruments from different vendors.

  • Project Manager

    Odetics, Inc.

    (Computer Hardware industry)

    1987 — 1988 (1 year )

    Principal investigator for a Department of Energy sponsored project researching the benefits associated with active force reflection to control robot systems in nuclear power plants. Analyzed the dynamics of a mobile robot used for beer production with three 5 DOF manipulator arms, and developed algorithms for its real-time simulation and control. Specified and detailed the electromechanical design of a laser-based location identification system used for autonomous guidance in combat.
