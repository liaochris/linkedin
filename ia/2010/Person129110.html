Larry Moseley’s Summary

I am a South Bay based Corporate Financial Consultant with over 30 years experience in Finance and Accounting combined with my 25 years experience with spreadsheets I help companies improve their existing accounting and finance processes.
Small businesses always suffer under the need for expert CFO services and the financial constraints of their business. Part of what I do as a consultant or part-time CFO is to review the current business practices to find ways large companies have been using for years to unlock some of the cash in hiding in the finances of the company.
I do this be reviewing the various accounting and finance processes of the company to highlight areas where better processes will unlock hidden cash.
As a part-time CFO I will help keep small companies on track and performing at their best.
