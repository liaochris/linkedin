Sandra Simmons’s Summary

I have been doing accounting and tax preparation for over 18 years. In 2001, I shifted my primary focus to Money Management to help business owners achieve their financial goals. I developed a simple software program that embodies an "old-school" system of correctly managing money, something a business owner is never taught in school.

My software program is in use in 24 countries around the world, and is available in both English and Russian. It is being translated into Spanish, German and French due to the demand of business owners.


THE MONEY MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE PROGRAM

Money Management Solutions Business Edition 2.0 is a cash flow management planning tool for business owners. Cash flow planning is done BEFORE the money comes in and BEFORE it gets spent, so the income of a company can be effectively used to reduce debt, pay current operating expenses on time and set funds aside for company expansion and long-term wealth building.

Money management planning is the missing step of looking to the FUTURE and is done BEFORE recording the executed plan in your accounting software. It is not meant to replace accounting software, however accounting software can only tell you what happened in the past, and making financial decisions based only on the past can be dangerous indeed.

A perfect tool for businesses with 50 employees or less, the software program holds up to 8 companies. It requires no costly upgrades, and can be used "as is" for life.

To explore the features of the software program and watch a selection of training videos from inside the software program itself, visit our website at www.MoneyMgmtSolutions.com

In addition to selling the software worldwide, I am giving seminars, writing articles and working with business owners one-on-one to improve sales and profits, expand their companies and put in place long-term wealth building plans for the business owner and senior executives.
