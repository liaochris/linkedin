Richard Sutz’s Summary

I bring more than 40 yrs experience teaching enhanced reading skills to The Literacy Co. My research shows that our nation's reading curriculum has produced poor readers as current reading instruction stops after oral reading skills are learned. Yet 99.9% of reading requires silent reading skills. My breakthrough developments teach fluent (silent) reading skills, enabling people to read better while comprehending & recalling more.

In the '60s, I was a consultant to Evelyn Wood, founder of Evelyn Wood Reading Dynamics®*.

I began my business career in the '60s as Director for Europe & Middle East at Grumman Aircraft where I was responsible for marketing military & civilian aircraft. I later moved to AZ & began my entrepreneurial career in the field of energy-related technologies & educational software designed to teach enhanced reading skills.

In the '70s I was appointed the Deputy Director of the AZ Energy Office w/ joint responsibility for AZ Energy Conservation Programs. I later joined the U.S. Dept of Energy as Director of Energy Related Inventions. My entrepreneurial spirit moved me again as I then began the creation of a high performance water-pumping windmill, which operates at low wind speeds, desalinates & purifies water & is currently in production in China w/ plans for production in Canada for worldwide distribution.

At one point in my career, I served as a Project Officer w/ the U.S. Navy Bureau of Aeronautics. I invented the key component of fighter pilot's breathing systems that prevent fatal accidents caused by hypoxia. My other major development was to install the 1st rocket-powered ejection seat for Naval aircraft enabling pilots to eject from the runway on landing/takeoff. For this I was awarded a Commendation from the Chief of Naval Ops.

I have a BS in Metallurgical Engineering from the Illinois Institute of Technology & an MBA in International Business & Finance at IMD, Lausanne, Switzerland.
