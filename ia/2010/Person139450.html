Suresh Mallarabanavaadi (MC)’s Summary

Suresh is a creative, results-driven business technologist with a successful track record of conceptualizing, planning, managing solutions portfolios and delivering large scale Business IT programs for global BFSI institutions. He has a deep understanding of the BFSI industry, functional process, technology, market and business challenges. He brings strategic leadership and 17+ years of proven global experience in leveraging IT to drive efficiency, competitive differentiation and solve business issues while managing costs and risks.

He currently works for Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), a Global Top-6 IT Services Company, part of the TATA Group, heading the Banking Technology & Solutions group. He is also a member of TCS’ Corporate Technology Board, guiding the overall strategies and capability investments for the BFS industry vertical.

He currently resides in Chicago with his wife and toddler son.
