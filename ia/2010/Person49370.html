Dave Bergman’s Summary

A management and technology consulting professional with well over a decade of experience designing complex technical solutions and leading performance improvement initiatives for large organizations.

Highly creative problem solver with excellent interpersonal and communication skills who is passionate about delivering solutions that help clients more effectively measure, plan, predict, and manage their business' performance.

Equally passionate about opportunities to contribute to others' professional and personal development, whether in a formal class setting or as a mentor or coach.

Broad functional business knowledge, particularly in corporate finance, budgeting and strategic planning, and deep technical expertise in the Oracle EPM and BI suites, especially Hyperion Planning and Essbase, with Essbase implementation experience dating to 1995.
