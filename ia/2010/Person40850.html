C.J. Reuter’s Summary

My experience is in sales, sales management, and enterprise (Fortune 500) account management for both on-line technology services, and recruitment/staffing services. Having sold early CRM software, technology services and Internet recruitment solutions for over 15 years (the last 13 with Monster Worldwide), I bring a strategic sales process and service oriented approach to my clients and internal organization. Comfortable communicating at the executive level. Experience presenting to VP/SVP and higher level contacts, procurement managers, and Senior Directors. A few past and current clients include HP, Microsoft, Google, Chevron, AT&T, Wal-Mart, Kaiser Permanente, Adecco, Halliburton, Dell and Boeing. Currently I manage a team of Global Account Managers based in the US with only Global Fortune 200 clients to put Monster Worlwide (www.monster.com) recruitment solutions in place in over 50 countries.
