Ann Meyer’s Summary

Kinetic communications professional with more than 15 years dedicated to conveying messages to internal and external audiences solidifying company image in the marketplace and driving sales. Resourceful, hands-on director who asks the critical questions, prioritizes activities, and seamlessly executes programs in support of organization’s goals. An efficient collaborator with cross-functional teams and clients that brings about creative and financial growth. Accomplished entrepreneur experienced in launching new ventures from the ground up orchestrating the significant industry participants, sourcing unique products, and shaping target markets with direct advertising.
