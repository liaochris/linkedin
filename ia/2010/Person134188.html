Mark Marosits’s Summary

For the past thirty years, I have served as a social marketing strategist to organizations working in the public interest, including those in the health, environment, education and microcredit arenas. I have helped to envision their futures, create connections with their most important constituents, and use social marketing to achieve their goals on behalf of improving health, education, human rights and community development. I am passionate about using social marketing to promote changes that improve the well-being of people and their communities. The agency that I co-founded has grown to be among the top social marketing agencies in the United States, with over 100 clients on three continents who educate and inspire and contribute to the relationship as much as they benefit from it. In the past several years, our agency has developed innovative approaches to social marketing and to communicating and connecting through everything from street media to emerging social media and interactive technology. I have also authored a variety of publications on social entrepreneurship, social marketing and-together with my colleagues Ed Maibach and Lorien Abroms- the "People and Places Framework for Public Health". Currently, I serve on the boards of Freedom from Hunger and Share International, both oriented to providing solutions to social challenges through self-help. Prior to co-founding Worldways Social Marketing, I served in Principal and Senior Executive roles with nationally-recognized consulting firms and health systems, and as a graduate program director and educator.
