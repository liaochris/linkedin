John Stahl’s Summary

My passion as a certified business coach is helping other small business owners to gain more balance in their lives by helping them to think more strategically about their businesses and personal lives.

As a business coach John has helped owners develop and implement strategies for growth. These entrepreneurs learn to track and measure their performance as they make the changes necessary to see substantial improvement. Some of John's recent clients have seen up to 300% growth in their businesses in one year. It is common for his clients to see 25%-100% growth in the first year of coaching.

As an executive coach John has helped numerous leaders increase their personal leadership capacity. By improving as leaders they have been able to influence their teams to achieve higher performance and greater impact.

Glenn is an entrepreneur with experience in business development and organizational development. He has been a successful business owner, and a non-profit executive.

John helps companies as a marketing & sales consultant to improve their lead generation and increase sales.

A sought-after trainer and motivational speaker, John has spoken to groups both large and small, including national conventions for building contractors and other organizations. He has inspired and encouraged groups of more than 1,000, providing life-changing insights and fresh information for leaders.

In addition to small business coaching, John also works with non-profit corporations helping them to gain focus and serve their market
