Benjamin Freedland’s Experience

  • President

    Coco Nutz LLC

    (Food & Beverages industry)

    August 2009 — Present (7 months)

    While traveling the world on a self-discovery trip, I found myself on the streets of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia parched and exhausted. Searching to quench his thirst, he spotted dozens of people anxiously waiting to purchase coconuts. Intrigued, I joined the party. I reached the front of the line to find a man wielding a machete into the top of a coconut and plunging a straw inside. After a brief conversation in broken English, I experienced the sweet coconut water for the first time. Never in my life was there something more refreshing. Immediately, I hurried back in line and purchased two more. The Malaysian man just simply smiled a toothy grin and said, “You’ll be back tomorrow.”

    I went back for the next two days.

    Home in the United States, I had an epiphany. I returned to my unforgotten coconuts. Starting at music festivals across the Western United States, Coco Nutz LLC became my new business and new adventure.

    The business plan is simple; sell coconuts and have fun while doing it.

    Coco Nutz LLC’s debut product Baby Thai Coconuts, can be found at festivals and fairs around the Western United States

    Coming soon to a festival near you!

    “Live long and eat Coconuts”
