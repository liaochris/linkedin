Jeffrey Sweeney’s Summary

I am dedicated to helping people and companies succeed, and bring this commitment to my work in investment banking, venture capital, and strategic advising. I have over 25 years’ experience leading several companies in the manufacturing, finance, machine tool, aerospace manufacturing, and equipment leasing spaces prior to my work in private banking and venture capital and funding.

Why is experience so important? Entrepreneurship is a great teacher. I gained not just know-how but wisdom from 25 years in business. I have a great global network of associates. It's incredibly rewarding to build sound companies and help good companies get even better.

I serve on the Boards of Directors of several educational and service organizations and enjoy speaking to groups and educating entrepreneurs in the US and internationally.
