Paul Custer’s Summary

Mr. Custer is a trial attorney at the law firm Eisenberg & Torisky focusing his practice on commercial liability and casualty claims. He also handles professional liability, commercial transportation, and mining claims.

Prior to joining Eisenberg & Torisky, Mr. Custer worked for Chartis in its Litigation Services Division in New York, New York. The Litigation Services Division manages all litigation for Chartis insureds in North America. Mr. Custer was part of a team that visited both staff counsel and panel counsel firms throughout the United States to ensure both legal expertise and compliance with Litigation Management Guidelines.

Prior to joining Chartis, Mr. Custer practiced at Picadio Sneath Miller & Norton - a boutique litigation firm in Pittsburgh, PA, focusing his practice on product liability and insurance coverage claims.

He also practiced at the Pittsburgh office of Margolis Edelstein - a mid-sized Philadelphia based insurance defense firm. There his practice focussed on general property and casualty claims, commercial claims, and subrogation claims.

Mr. Custer has experience litigating cases in Western Pennsylvania with exposures up to several million dollars.

Mr. Custer regularly appears before judges in Allegheny, Beaver, Butler, Cambria, Clearfield, Erie, Indiana, Lawrence, Mercer, Somerset, Washington, and Westmoreland counties, arguing dispositive and discovery motions. He has also participated in mediation of high exposure cases.

Mr. Custer currently resides in Pittsburgh with his wife and four children.
