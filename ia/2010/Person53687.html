Deborah Spector’s Summary

I empower nonprofits to make a difference!

Deborah is president of Creative Solutions & Innovations, a strategic marketing consultancy. CS&I specializes in marketing communications, signature events, and sponsorship development and retention for the nonprofit community.

Deborah has a strong commitment to professional excellence and community. She has served on numerous boards, including appointed chair DeKalb County Community Relations Commission; the Atlanta Regional Commission 2020 Media Task Force Advisory Committee; and Women in Communications, including two years as president Atlanta Chapter.

Deborah's history is rich with meaningful experiences and clients. She fed her passion for horses and community while working on the planning committee for the Equestrian events at the 1996 Olympics.

She is currently working on an event management e-book with tools and templates for the user to download and her first teleseminar entitled: Special Events During a Recession? - Absolutely! for the National Nonprofit Resource Directory.

Deborah Spector’s Specialties:

My specialities include strategic communications plans, sponsorship development and special events planning and implementation.
