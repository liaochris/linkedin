Wesley Whittaker’s Summary

Wes Whittaker spent more than 25 years in engineering design, construction project management and business/technical writing. He has a unique gift for seeing the overall project and coming up with often unorthodox but effective solutions to challenges that arise. He is a visionary who brings that expertise to the business environment helping entrepreneurs and startup business owners DEFINE their dreams, DEVELOP their goals and DELIVER on their potential to live their passionate, authentic professional lives.

Wes holds a BA in Management and Organizational Development from Spring Arbor University. He has written proposals, business plans and created marketing materials for several firms around the country.

He is a former officer in the Grand Blanc Area Toastmasters and former Vice-President of ConnecTech Greater Flint.

Wes is a Vietnam-era Veteran, one of the last of the draftees, who served in the US Air Force from 1972 to 1976. He went through Avionics training at Keesler Technical Training Center in Biloxi, MS. His first duty station was with the 57th AMS at Nellis AFB where he did TDY with the 64th Aggressor Squadron (RED STAR) all over the CONUS. He was then stationed with the 401st AMS at Torrejon AB just north of Madrid, Spain, where he did TDY assignments at Aviano, Italy and Incirlik, Turkey.

Wes can also be contacted through Facebook or Twitter.
