Michael Rendon’s Summary

I was nationally certified as a personal trainer through many different organizations and a sports fitness specialist. I enjoy working with homeowners and seeing there satisfaction when there remodeling project is complete. I have developed a passion for marketing and developing marketing strategies for Rendon Remodeling. My goal is to continue and grow as a person, and better myself in the remodeling industry. I strive to learn more, grow the business, work hard, and continue satisfying our clients.
