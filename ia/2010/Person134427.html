Mark Price’s Summary

Leads consulting firm that specializes in building customer relationships by understanding customer behavior (combining and analyzing customer data) and developing approaches to improve best customer experience, retention and profitability

Responsible for client delivery, thought leadership and firm marketing as well as executive duties. Particular interest in developing customer-centric marketing organizations and the blend of "click and mortar" marketing leveraging customer data.

In addition, I speak on data-driven marketing and technology and write a blog at www.cultivatingyourcustomers.com, as well as post on Retailwire.com and the Minnesota AMA (mnama.org).

Mark Price’s Specialties:

Multichannel customer relationship analysis, social media and creation of "down-and-dirty" practical approaches.
