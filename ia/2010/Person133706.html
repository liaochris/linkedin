Joan Barnes, MIRM’s Summary

Marketing Specifics, Inc., differs from other real estate marketing companies by offering a total, turnkey marketing package for builders and developers. While most real estate marketing companies focus on just one type of discipline, the team of experts at Marketing Specifics, Inc., provides extensive real estate experience in traditional marketing, advertising and internet marketing strategies, as well as, full service interior design of selling environments and amenity buildings, while building all architectural millwork required for both in our own in-house custom millwork shop.

The company has a 20+ year award-winning track record in the successful marketing of master planned communities, TND communities, active-adult communities, urban towers and high-rises, luxury resorts and 2nd home communities. Marketing Specifics, Inc., works nation-wide with developers and builders both as a full-service agency and interior design firm as well as on a specific, project basis.
