Vanja Stace’s Summary

I am a Marketing, Communication and Brand Specialist with experience in the online environment.

I have an excellent track record of increasing brand awareness, market presence and customer loyalty through strategic interaction in social channels (including social networks and blog platforms), direct digital marketing, SEO, SEM as well as the production of relevant content for company websites, microsites and blogs.

Being most passionate about engagement and user experiences, I am experienced in developing concepts, translating marketing objectives into creative strategies, monitoring campaigns, organizing production, analysing campaign efforts, coaching and managing staff.
