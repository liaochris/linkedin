Tiffany Willis (tiffany_willis@yahoo.com)’s Summary

I have always worked towards one primary goal: Empower people by introducing them to tools that will help them in their path of working around barriers towards personal and professional success. Most of my career focus has been on at-risk youth and youth offenders. I work now with adult job-seekers in the DFW area.

I'm of the mindset that if I serve my customers well and keep their best interest my goal at all times, my personal and professional success will follow. I will not compromise on this and this philosophy has not failed me.

Making a difference is important to me. I've been so fortunate to always have work that I find rewarding, and I hope that my work in some way helps others to do the same.
