Tony Rushin’s Summary

25 years of experience in Sales & Marketing, Business Development, and GM roles; the last 10 years as an owner and executive; the first 10 years with IBM selling mid-range IT & business solutions. My blend of experience enables me to quickly diagnose the issues holding back sales growth and implement solutions that work via a hands-on, get-results approach. My experience is with high-technology: hardware & software early in my career; managed services and outsourced services since 1998.

At Network 1 I lead the sales and marketing efforts, helping the company accelerate their growth. Network 1 is the monthly IT support company for over 50 small businesses in metro Atlanta. We have clients in downtown Atlanta and north; from 3 to 150 computer users; and in all industries. We are especially adept at helping professional firms: law firms, medical practices and financial advisory/wealth management firms comprise over 70% of our clients.

Tony Rushin’s Specialties:
