John Cavers’s Summary

I am a results proven Supply Chain Executive at Sysco with a diverse set of experience from running large distribution centers to targeted projects focused on strategy and business transformation. I established a proven track record of lowering costs and improving service through my tenure in running a 400,000 square foot distribution center with $500,000,000 in annual sales, an annual output of 16,700,000 pieces and an employee base of 450 that included a labor union. More recently I spent three years working on Corporate Strategy focusing on inventory shrink expense reduction, addressing the fuel crisis of $125 per barrel of oil and challenging capital markets related to fixed asset expenditures. My latest assignment is as a business lead in Sysco’s 212 SAP Business Transformation Project for key Supply Chain processes in warehouse and transportation. The goal was to integrate SAP into Sysco’s business model by moving to more standardized practices that deliver the most return on investment towards our business case, which can be applied globally across the enterprise. Another area of focus was leading development of operational metrics for Supply Chain benchmarking, financial reporting and creation of a Vice President of Operations executive dashboard.
