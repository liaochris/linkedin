Solomon Poretsky’s Summary

Solomon Poretsky is the regional manager of the Minneapolis/St. Paul office of Marcus & Millichap Real Estate Investment Services, the largest commercial real estate brokerage in the nation focusing exclusively on real estate investments. Since opening the office in February of 2006, Solomon has built the largest investment real estate team in the Twin Cities and has received the distinction of being designated a Marcus & Millichap faculty member. Prior to joining the firm’s management, Solomon was an agent with Marcus & Millichap where he brokered a range of transactions spanning all major product types in multiple states.
