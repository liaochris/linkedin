Aaron Puttroff’s Summary

Living Legacy Financial and Insurance Services goes beyond the spreadsheet when it comes to planning for our clients’ financial futures. Whether we are guiding business owners to turn business success into personal wealth or helping individuals plan for today and live for tomorrow, our focus is on building a financial plan that can withstand life’s unexpected events.

Based in San Diego County, our company helps individuals and business owners answer their most pressing financial questions:
* Will your savings be there when you need them?
* How can you protect the investment you’ve made in your business?
* How can you plan now to maintain your standard of living when you retire?
* How can you finance your children’s college education? Expand your business? Buy a house?
