Christine Cuccio Radlmann’s Summary

Perhaps renowned author Laban Hill said it best in the acknowledgments to his National Book Award Nominee Harlem Stomp: "I would like to thank Christine Cuccio for her eagle eye and unerring copyediting."
It is my eagle eye that helped Little, Brown Books for Young Readers become among the most respected books in the industry. I am known for my energy and enthusiasm, my ability to multi-task under the tightest of deadlines, and my success in hiring and managing top-notch employees and freelancers.
