Todd Jones’s Summary

I have been building and maintaining websites for eight years, and I have decided that Wordpress is best solution for most small companies and non-profits. Therefore, I have dedicated myself to learning more about and improving my Wordpress skills. Now, every website I design is built on Wordpress.

In addition, I have become engaged in improving my social media skills and use them to improve search engine rankings organically, giving smaller companies an opportunity to compete with the larger businesses.

I also like to write. I have been writing on the internet for about four years now dating back to my first blog. I have had the opportunity to cover TCU football games and the Armed Forces Bowl. I am also involved in covering semi-pro and women's football.
