Shai Goldman’s Summary

Provide consulting services for early and growth stage technology companies that are clients of SVB. I specifically focus on the following sectors: SaaS, mobile, consumer internet, digital media. Geographic focus includes companies located in the Bay Area, Southern California, Seattle, Boulder/Denver, Boston, New York, and DC. I have helped numerous companies raise VC financing, including Bookrenter.com, RiseSmart, Uservoice, LendingClub, InfusionSoft, LensVector, Jobvite, Veodia, Solexant, Fastscale (acquired), Gamestrata (acquired)

Assist companies in raising initial and/or follow-on rounds of VC funding. Review and provide crucial feedback on marketing and investment materials such as executive summaries and presentations. Provide targeted introductions to venture capital firms/partners who are relevant based on sector, stage, and geography.
