Boris von Bormann’s Summary

I have been involved in the international trade industry for over 14 years. I am the founder and CEO of accessio (www.accessio.com), an independent consulting company that focuses on the cross- cultural business aspects. accessio specializes in working with foreign companies to facilitate the market entry to the North American markets and US companies that are looking to enter the German/ European market place. Within accessio we have developed strong core competencies in several industries. For the past three years we have been focusing on renewable energies, energy efficiency, and sustainable design. We are and have been working successfully also for companies in the IT, Medical Technology, Biotech, and Film& Entertainment sectors. Our office is located in in Orange County, California and we have representation in Berlin, Germany.

accessio also helps foreign product companies to gain access to the European market and sometimes we take on distribution of products as well. accessio has the exclusive rights for the ecobutton in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland - www.eco-button.com.

I am currently the acting Chairman of the German American Business Association in Southern California, a non- profit organization in Southern California.
