Ratan KK’s Summary

RatanKK has been entrepreneurial spirited and is an entrepreneur.

He is Founder Associate at GutsGo (www.gutsgo.com). GutsGo is into eMarketing Training and Consulting. GutsGo trains and consults in all the tactics of eMarketing viz., SEO, PPC, Social Media, Email Marketing, Mobile Marketing. For Corporates, we do customized workshops to make them understand and strategize for thier eMarketing activities. He is Trainer and also heads Training, Marketing and Operations Functions at GutsGo. In the long run, RatanKK has a vision to build GutsGo as a platform for multiple businesses.

Before GutsGo, he has about one and half decades of business experience across 4 industries: Manufacturing, IT Services, ITES Services and Digital Marketing, in that order. His forte is Operations & Marketing. He has handled responsibilities in Business Development, Consulting, Pre-Sales and SBU / Profit Center functions. Most often, he worked in start-up environment either in a pure start up or in an established company. At one time, he had handled about 600 people and managed about 50 Crores of annualized revenue.

RatanKK is a mentor, a speaker, and a columnist.
He derives satisfaction by mentoring people to better their situation.
He speaks at conferences related to digital marketing and entrepreneurship. Also, had a column of his own (’thinking online’) in a daily newspaper in Mumbai.

He believes that knowledge multiplies by sharing ones experiences. Sharing helps him to develop mutual bonding & inspiration. For him, to be successful is to succeed and inspire others to succeed.

He is an Engineer (Osmania'93) and an MBA (IIMA'96).
