Marissa Dulaney’s Summary

Marissa specializes in designing for ecosystems of products, envisioning new concept designs, identifying strategies for success, and leading teams of interdisciplinary designers.

At her current company, LinkedIn, Marissa is designing web experiences for the LinkedIn professional social network. Her focus is on conceptualizing and refining site features that meet the user's desire for engaging, useful, and pleasurable experiences, and the business's revenue and monetization goals. At her previous company, Adobe Systems, Marissa was a design manager who shipped over a dozen products in 80% of the business units, including business, creative professional, and consumer products.

Marissa embraces the full spectrum necessary for successful design – from the overall idea that is going to grab the user’s attention and meet their needs, to the details that if not met will break the experience, to the final realization of the design in a finished product that drives the business. She has worked in both visionary and schedule-driven capacities, has been a manager as well as an individual contributor, and has designed for a variety of users and technologies.
