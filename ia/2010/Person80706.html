Heather Burns-DeMelo’s Summary

As a sustainability strategist, I help to build valued reputations for companies, organizations and academic institutions while working with them to identify and shape their sustainability strategies for greatest social and financial return. I excel at developing compelling marketing communications out of that strategy, and initiating beneficial partnerships and business development opportunities within the green space.

I have worked with industry associations, a US Naval base, non-profits, and city governments.

As founder of CTGreenScene.com and BostonGreenScene.net, blogs about green news and events in CT, NYC and Boston, I write about the latest national and global trends in sustainability and how those translate to my local area.

I am founder of Fairfield County and co-organizer for Hartford GreenDrinks, monthly networking events for individuals, organizations and companies interested in discussing environmental issues, where I work to connect people from all walks of life to sustainable resources and each other.

My former publishing experience includes launching AllGreen Magazine and acting as Editorial Director of a quarterly print publication and sister website whose mission was to bring green living to the mainstream.

International living and working experience includes Cambodia, Greece, Hong Kong, Laos, Malaysia, Taiwan, and Thailand.
