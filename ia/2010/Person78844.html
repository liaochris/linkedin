Gunther Sonnenfeld’s Summary

THE QUICK & SIMPLE:

I am a brand consultant and social technologist with 15 years of Fortune 500 and global non-profit experience. Currently, I serve as the strategic and platform solutions lead on the Skype, Toyota and Adobe accounts for Omnicom agency, RAPP, and through my own company, ThinkState, I am co-developing several proprietary platforms. I am also a published author and public speaker; appearances this year will include those at Gulltaggen and the Innotech/eMarketing Summit.

DETAILS:

- Multi-channel initiatives for the likes of Kraft, Roll International, Motorola, Safeway, Sony, Toyota, the NBA, The PGA TOUR and WB Music.

- AR (augmented reality) and ARG (alternate reality gaming) launch work for Rockstar Games, as well as 3D rendering, pre-visualization software and military simulation projects.

- Cause-related work for groups such as the NRDC, The Annenberg Foundation and the American Cancer Society.

- Co-Founder and developer of 7 proprietary platforms and a host of community websites, mobile applications & storefronts, software applications, search platforms, ad targeting tools and social technologies.

- Advisor to investment banks, private equity firms and venture capital groups on new media and technology trends.

- Co-author of "Connect! Marketing in the Social Media Era", a collaborative book featuring insights from 100 thought leaders in social communication.

- Regular contributor to leading industry publications such as iMedia Connection.

- Taught the first communications planning bootcamp @ CP+B/Miami Ad School's School of Pop Culture Engineering last year.

- Judge on the inaugural Hive Awards panel @SXSW

- Co-Founder of Canada's Institute for the Future, an unprecedented virtual education and transmedia storytelling platform, an affiliate of Stanford University's IFTF.org.

- Also an award-winning documentarian; took top honors at X-Dance in 2006 for co-writing & co-producing FLOW.

Gunther Sonnenfeld’s Specialties:

Brand development, positioning and identity; transmedia development; collaborative storytelling; digital production; media negotiation; strategic alliances; web & software development; technology integration; social marketing; media optimization.
