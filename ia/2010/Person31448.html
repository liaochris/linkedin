Scott Walker’s Summary

ethosIQ is a full service Call Center Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) solution provider based in Houston, TX that uses our extensive Contact Center and Enterprise Social Media experience and expertise to reduce costs, increase revenue streams and enhance the customer experience.

ethosIQ provides Social Media Analytics (SMA), Online Reputation Management (ORM) and Social Media Optimization (SMO) Consulting, Training and Managed Services. We use revolutionary real time Enterprise SMA solutions as a game changer in Brand Management.

We leverage Enterprise SMA tools that analyze the myriad of conversations taking place on the social web in real time, and integrate the analysis, while providing the additional functionality to route these interactions and information across the enterprise. Our Engagement Platform (EP) integrates to existing workflows embedded within CRM and ticketing systems, allowing you to route and/or escalate to the appropriate department and or your customer service agent in your contact or call center, using rules based decision models.

Our Contact Center consulting services include Assessment, RFP, Design, and Implementation plans in the following areas: Infrastructure Technology including PBX, IVR, ACD, CTI, VoIP, Disaster Recovery, Workforce Management, Quality Management, Agent and Call Routing Optimization to dramatically improve the performance of your Marketing, Sales and Customer Service operations. At ethosIQ we focus upon the critical integration of people, process and technology by offering a full spectrum of strategy, design and implementation services in Customer Service and Contact Center Solutions.
