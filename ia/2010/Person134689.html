Mark Donohue’s Summary

In June 2008, Mr. Donohue was appointed Babson College’s first Clean Technology Entrepreneur-in-Residence. Babson College has the number-one rated entrepreneurship program. In March of 2010, he was honored by the Energy & Environment Club with their annual leadership award, which is inscribed: “For his exemplary efforts in promoting, supporting and advancing sustainability at Babson College."

In 2001, he was the sole Founder and Chairman of Expansion Capital Partners, LLC, a pioneer in cleantech venture capital investing, which manages over $100,000,000. He sold most of his interests in Expansion Capital in June 2008.

His career has focused on socially responsible business and investment, Cleantech investing, venture capital, and personal wealth management "best practices". During the last twenty-five years, Mr. Donohue has built three financial service companies. He has also served in senior operational roles for venture-backed enterprises, beyond serving as Chairman for six entrepreneurial businesses. He currently serves as a Partner at the VC fund Conscious Living Ventures, which is part of the Doerken family office. He also advises other venture funds and family offices on their sustainability investment strategies, venture capital and broader wealth management strategies. His advisory work is done through Sustainable Impact Investing, LLC, where he serves as president.

He has served in a variety of nonprofit roles, including several past committees of the Threshold Foundation. He was Chairman of the Membership Committee for the Social Venture Network, which is the leading organization of socially progressive CEO's.

Mr. Donohue earned his BS in Investment Management, with honors, from Babson in 1988. He has served on the college’s Board of Overseers since 2002. Mr. Donohue has mentored over one hundred students and alumni.
