Cassandra Moore, PhD’s Summary

As a research scientist in cognitive psychology, I have a deep understanding of how people naturally find things, solve problems and form mental models of information in the world around us. As an information architect, I leverage this knowledge to create solutions that resonate with the natural strengths of the human mind. My focus is on new ways of sharing and documenting the tacit knowledge held by experienced employees in large organizations, integration of bottom-up and top-down classification systems, taxonomies and ontologies.

Cassandra Moore, PhD’s Specialties:

Information Architecture, User Research & Testing, UI Design, Statistics, Business Intelligence, Requirements Analysis, Process Engineering, Business Development, Cognitive Psychology (learning, memory, perception, attention)
