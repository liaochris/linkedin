Jason Sarracini’s Summary

Jason Sarracini was born with the travel bug.

His grandfather, Ontario Sarracini, started one of the first Italian-focused travel agencies in Toronto.

A regular globetrotter, Jason has been traveling all his life. At age 9 he was on a bus to Boston with his school choir. At age 21 he was living on the south side of the Arno in Florence where he met his wife. Together they have traveled the world from Bangkok to Bermuda.

As co-founder and President of TargetVacations.ca, Jason has won awards, most recently from Travelzoo as leading provider of travel deals for Canada in 2008. He sees big opportunities in online travel and hopes to be a common thread for many in the Canadian travel industry.
