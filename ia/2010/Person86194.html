Ivan Nikkhoo’s Summary

With over 26 years of industry experience in various senior capacities nationally and internationally, Mr. Ivan Nikkhoo is a Managing Director at Siemer & Associates. In that capacity, he is responsible for all facets of the practice including business development, deal sourcing, origination, conversion, and executing client mandates.

Prior to Siemer, Ivan was the Head of the Technology Practice at Barrington Associates, a division of Well Fargo Securities, focusing on advising emerging technology companies. Prior to WFS/Barrington, he served as the MD and Portfolio Manager for N3 Technology Fund, a long/short hedge fund co-managed with Dalton Investments. Prior to N3, he was the GM at SOA Software, responsible for mergers, acquisitions, investments, strategic alliances, and joint ventures. Prior to SOA Software, Ivan was the President of Vertex Systems focused on technology strategy, implementation, and IP monetization. Prior to Vertex, he was the Director of European Operations for TLD Systems in Paris, France, providing software for European space and military projects.

Ivan is also a partner at a venture fund focused on early stage investing in emerging technology companies, and serves on the boards of several technology companies.

Ivan is a member of YPO, was a finalist at the E&Y Entrepreneur of the Year Award and serves on the boards of a number of charity and business organizations including LACC, USC Marshall Partners, JA of SoCal. and Cedars-Sinai Medical Center. He is a frequent guest lecturer at the USC Marshall School of Business and has been featured in numerous publications including Fortune Magazine, Los Angeles Times, and the Los Angeles Business Journal.

Mr. Nikkhoo received his MBA from USC Marshall School of Business and his Bachelor’s of Science in Engineering from McGill University in Montreal. Mr. Nikkhoo is a General Securities Principal licensed with FINRA/SIPC (formerly NASD).
