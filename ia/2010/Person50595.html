David Hein’s Summary

Past principal of E-Verifier (www.everifier.com), An Automated Employment Verification Service.
Established Heinsite Services® 1995 and merged with The Phillips Agency in 2006. 19 years experience in the credit and collections industry. Served as first Co-Chair of the National Association of Professional Background Screeners (www.NAPBS.com). Supported 50 plus windows based 2000 and 2003 servers running Rockwell XML platforms for Bell South transport division covering the Southeast.

David Hein’s Specialties:

Background Screening for Employers and Landlords; Domestic and Internationally: Criminal Reports, Credit Reports, Motor Vehicle Reports, Application and Education Verifications. Automation of Employment Verifications
