Ron Gibori’s Summary

Ron Gibori is a Managing Partner at UCG Marketing the nations best execution driven experiential marketing and promotions agency.

Ron has been at the forefront of the marketing revolution for over a decade, trailblazing the Internet frontier with his award wining creative strategy. A nationally recognized innovator, he has architected groundbreaking interactive marketing campaign and solutions that address complex social issues and affect true change. Ron brings over a decade of innovative experience managing both experiential and interactive marketing agencies.

Ron’s previous success has been heralded throughout national media outlets including Dateline, The New York Times, Today Show, CNN Headline News, Dr. Phil, MSNBC, Fox News, USA Today, Wired, and the Associated Press. Ron’s previous creative strategy has received two Emey nominations and several nationally recognized awards including a Peabody Award-for the winning Half of Us campaign with mtvU.

Ron is a highly accomplished professional with the proven ability to start, manage and lead several projects from conception to completion. A finance graduate from the University of Arizona, Eller School of Business, who is not afraid of ambitious challenges. Prior to his work with to ULifeline, Ron founded and led the strategic development for two Internet-based companies, while still in college. UniversityAve.com was one of the first B2B internet marketing solutions for campus based businesses and Net Pods, focused on developing intelligent search technology in 1997.

Ron Gibori’s diverse background in business development, applied technologies and corporate strategy gives him strong footing to enhance any strategic opportunity. Ron is currently enrolled in the University of Chicago Executive MBA program.
