Jon Marks’s Summary

15 years experience in IT since finishing Postgrad. About 6 years contracting, and 9 years at the company now known as LBi. Recently moved to News Corp as Lead Architect on an exciting project.

Have implement projects using most large commercial Content Management Systems, Portals, Search Engine and Analytics tools. Recent clients include Etihad Airways, British Telecom, Sony, Corus, National Grid, Mazda, British Gas/Centrica, Virgin Atlantic and Marks and Spencers.

I'm a CMS geek. I write for CMS Wire, speak often at conferences, and co-organise the Last Thursday CMS group in London. Join the LinkedIn group and come for a drink.
