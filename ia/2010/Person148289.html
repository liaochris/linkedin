Molly Steinwald’s Summary

Combining teaching, photography, research and public speaking with commitment to the environment, science education reform, cross-disciplinary education and communication, and humanity, I work to foster connection to nature and sustainable conservation ethics in scientifically and environmentally unaware or disinterested populations.

Molly Steinwald’s Specialties:

-university-level biology and interdisciplinary teaching, in both traditional and inquiry-based settings
-conservation, nature, people, architectural photography
-field and laboratory biological research skills in a variety of subjects and settings
-public speaking
-managing student employees, research assistants, and teaching and research budgets
