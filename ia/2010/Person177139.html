Ron Bott’s Summary

Ron has nearly 30 years of sales and marketing, finance, and management experience. His corporate experience includes management consulting positions with the international professional services firms, PriceWaterhouseCoopers and Ernst & Young. Ron also held senior management positions with an information technology company and environmental engineering firm, both located in Massachusetts. He began his professional career in finance with the two largest commercial banking organizations in Florida. Since 1996, Ron has been an entrepreneur, including starting a sign fabrication company in Waltham, MA. In 2006, he formed TAB Metro Boston, which focuses on increasing company value and improving the quality of life for business owners. As a Certified Sales Development Expert, Ron also helps companies build and develop superior sales forces that achieve superior revenue and profit.

Ron Bott’s Specialties:

Sales force development, executive coaching, group facilitation, finance, marketing, strategic planning.
