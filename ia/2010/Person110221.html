Kane Smaller’s Summary

I have a successful track record of helping organizations develop and execute on forward thinking IT strategies. For the past three years my focus has been on selling Virtualization, Storage Optimization, and Business Continuance solutions into multiple industry verticals. I strive to enable organizations to deliver value to their business by leveraging current and future datacenter investment to create flexible and highly available computing environments.

I have successfully sold into Health Care, Gaming, State and Local Government, Oil and Gas, Print and Digital Media, and Higher Education Institutions. I have extensive experience selling into "C" level contacts within the industries above.

In early 2008, I opened a new sales office for Solutions II in Nevada. Since opening the office, I have since been responsible for driving revenue within existing clientele and developing relationships with net new customers. I have successfully engaged prospects and turned them into repeat customers by developing lasting relationships and showing value above and beyond my competition. Creating value for customers is priority number 1.

As I move forward in my career I have goals to continually learn new and improve my sales skills, focus on developing stronger technical knowledge, and never get complacent. You can always do better!
