Helen Dolce-Lund’s Summary

Marketing Talent Scouting - search and selection of marketing talent on behalf of corporate and agency clients across all industry sectors (fmcg, retail, travel & leisure, online, professional services, financial services, property, technical and engineering, public and not-for- profit). Advising on best resource to meet marketing requirements and recommendation and management of the most suitable search process to suit the requirements, budget and timeframes. Strong experience and successful track record in the recruitment of individuals and agency/suppliers on a permanent/retained, interim/contract, freelance and consultancy basis.

Agency & Marketing Procurement - search, short listing and management of external pitch processes for advertising/design/creative, media, PR, DM, brand, market research and PR agencies. Briefing objectives, negotiating rates, and budget negotiation and control and day to day management where necessary to ensure delivery.

Marketing Consultancy & Interim Management - providing hands-on support and direction to companies of all sizes - from large PLCs to SMEs, on how to cope through times of change (i.e. organisation restructure, redundancy, acquisitions and mergers, industry changes and external influences) helping to develop long and short terms solutions and marketing strategy and ensuring the necessary resource is in place to meet the business objectives.

Marketing Management - planning, managing and delivery of marketing strategy and through-the-line marketing communications activity for some of the UK’s most dynamic retail and leisure brands.

International marketing - launching of new and existing brand through marketing communications activity in international markets, tailoring to local customer needs and conducting in-depth research to find the best communication channels and third party suppliers to suit the local market.
