Jeff Rohling’s Summary

Fluent in global multi-channel direct/online marketing, customer acquisition and retention, online and offline marketing, continuity/loyalty programs, strategic brand positioning, telesales, product marketing, analytics, new media communications, including social networks and end user engagement strategies that drive brand loyalty and shareholder value.

Repeated successes in diverse product and industry categories including; sports footwear, athletic fashion apparel, luxury home décor and furnishings, gifts, Coca-Cola and Olympic licensed merchandise, rare collectibles, printing products and services, office products, orthopedic products, agricultural and food products.

Entered new global and domestic markets and forged successful marketing partnerships with industry leaders including: Hewlett-Packard, 3M, Nike, Addidas, Asics, New Balance, Ironman Triathlon, Readers Digest, The Coca-Cola Company, The US Olympic Committee, The International Olympic Committee, The Campbell’s Soup Company, Georgia Pacific, and Weyerhaeuser.
