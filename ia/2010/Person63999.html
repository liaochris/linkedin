Ellerton Whitney, MBA’s Summary

Ellerton is a heavy-hitting paid search manager with a background in numerous verticals, including automotive, health care, debt management, B2C/B2B tech, education, and many others. He has experience managing large (six figure monthly) budgets, achieving and exceeding client/company goals. He has managed projects with clients ranging from the Fortune 50 all the way down to small business, and has years of agency experience.

An expert in data analysis, Ellerton fully understands paid search's value chain. He uses his search expertise combined with Excel abilities to drive strategy that converts. Ellerton understands the proper implementation of complex paid search campaigns involving great landing pages, ad creative testing, proper keyword mixes, and so forth. He understands what kind of search strategy it requires to break through the clutter, and all of the elements that go into well executed paid search management (Quality Score, campaign structure, etc etc.)

In addition to SEM, Ellerton has experience building links and search engine optimizing web content for a number of properties, including Motor Trend & Automobile Mag. He orchestrated social media marketing strategies to populate links and build traffic.

Ellerton earned his M.B.A. in Marketing from SDSU, specializing in Branding, Market Research, and Consumer Behavior, amongst other subjects. He also earned his B.A. from UCSD.
