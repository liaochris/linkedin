Emmalyne Mathon’s Summary

I am an independent project manager, communications advisor and translator. I work for international companies that are serious about corporate social responsibility and for international not-for-profit organizations. Hence my company name 'Simply Social'.

I offer project management and marketing & communication services. My project expertise ranges from international campaigns and conferences, to staff-days and charity events. I am also available for short-term communication & fundraising positions and for company advise on CSR-related issues.

See www.simplysocial.nl for information and references.

Emmalyne Mathon’s Specialties:

I am multi-lingual (fluent in Dutch, English and French) and I have extensive international experience in the following fields: Project management, marketing & communication, fundraising, translating & editing. I am mostly experienced in working for international organizations and the development sector with a focus on relations between corporate and not-for-profit.
