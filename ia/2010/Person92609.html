Jeanine DeOre’s Summary

CPA Jeanine Kristy (DeOre) Baechtold's design experience includes:
- co-founder, principal and designer for Kristy Valshan, Inc. - art licensing and design studio with art showcased on various product categories in fine retailers such as Costco, Sam's Club, Archiver's, etc.
- scrapbook designer / artist for 6 years (created traditional scrapbooking supplies as well as downloadable graphics to be used with photo editing software and/or printed for crafts)
- founder and administrator of a large scrapbooking community (growing the site to 35,000+ members) for 3 1/2 years
- licensed digital art to scrapblog.com
- licensed designer cardstock / paper line for sale at Costco Wholesale Clubs, Archiver's, etc.
- internet scrapbooking retailer for over 3 years
- scrapbook layouts and designs included in various publications including Simple Scrapbooks & Memory Makers magazines and books by Design Originals
- featured digital artist/designer at Scrapbook.com for 2 1/2 years
- designer at Scrapbook-bytes.com for 2 1/2 years
- featured designer of the month for Adobe's Photoshop Elements ScrapperGuide.com
- featured designer of the month for ScrapWOW.com
- card templates included with Sony Snaplab Printer
- designs included with FxFoto software

Jeanine's CPA experience includes: working at CPA/public accounting firms for over 9 years while specializing in secondary technical review, employee benefit plans, tax exempt clients and small business audits/consulting.

Jeanine is noted for her strong interpersonal, managerial, administrative, communication, and problem solving skills with proven leadership ability.

Jeanine DeOre’s Specialties:

Art Licensing: Create stationery, gift, textile, surface, home decor and craft art and design for licensing, product concepts and design, packaging design, graphic design, color and trend research, proficient in adobe photoshop cs.

CPA: Specializes in employee benefit plans, secondary technical review, auditing, small business consulting, human resource assistance (interviewing, hiring, mentoring, etc), training and leading workshops.
