Valerie Plis’s Summary

I am a Master Certified Career Coach and a Strengths Coach. I have 20 years experience coaching, mentoring, and training Human Resource professionals, recruiters, counselors, career coaches in training, and clients from around the world. I help develop strong leaders, maximize team engagement and performance, transform at-risk employees to high achievers, and help professionals navigate a career transition with purpose and excellence. As a “Champion” I am skilled at getting things done in spite of seemingly insurmountable obstacles. My competitive energy, drive, and passion for a strengths-based approach to performance management propels the development, engagement, and motivation for middle and emerging managers, as well as professionals in career transition.

Strengths-Based Performance Coaching, Leadership Development & Coaching, Engagement, Work/Life Balance, Team Building, Training & Facilitation, Authentic Vocation/Career Discovery, Career Transition for Individuals with Disabilities, Youth Transition Services, Personal & Career Assessments, Strategic Job Search Planning, Salary Negotiation, Goal Setting, Strong Careers
