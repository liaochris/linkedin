Dione Bledsoe’s Summary

Dione Bledsoe stands for the highest in excellence and achievement in the fashion industry. Founded in 2009 to pursue a vision of intelligent recruiting, business development and management practices.

We carry out a direct approach in all practices. Selective and committed to our clients, we are chosen by leading companies and brands for our expertise, confidentiality and availability.

We maintain specific ideals which includes three life practices. Honesty, humility and diligence. We often follow candidates through the entire life of their career, offering development and direction when desired.

Our selectivity makes us unique amongst agencies in both the American and global market. Candidates are hunted, scouted for their unique excellence and passionate personalities. They are one of a kind and are treated as valued individuals with respect not dispensable candidates. Placements at the Bledsoe Agency are made with the good of helping candidates achieve their full potential as candidates and as people.
