Danyelle Palmer’s Summary

My education is in studio arts, writing, theatrical design, performance, music, and business. I hold an AA, BS, and currently working on a MFA degree. I plan on pursuing a Doctorate program in Visual and Cultural Arts from the University of Rochester in the future.

I have experience and adore all types of photography: Fine Art, Fashion, Journalistic, Wedding, Nature, Food, Film and Music Promotion, Live Music, Portraiture; Black & White and Color Photography; 35mm, Medium format, Large format and Digital.

As for costume design, I have designed costumes for theatre, film, and dance. Although, costumes were modern, I am confident in my fashion history and research that I could execute a period production successfully.

I am an avid writer that writes all sorts of genres for screenplays, short stories, and novels. I also write poetry and magazine/newspaper articles. I am also an avid blogger, keeping a few blogs on the most popular blogging sites.

Film experience includes screenwriting, cameras, cinematography, editing, directing, art direction and production design, costumes, producing, and sound recording, mixing, and design.

I have experience with Photoshop, InDesign, Quark xPress, Premier, Final Cut Pro, Dreamweaver, Office, Business Plan Pro, and Final Draft; both on PC and Mac.

My other interests include printmaking, painting, drawing, fashion design, sculpture, art history, publishing, acting, directing theatre, and gallery and museum management.
I am currently working on a number of projects that span many areas including rewriting a screenplay I wrote, brainstorming ideas for producing a short film, designing music inspired maternity clothing line and a baby wrap carrier, and a personal woodcut printmaking project. I am also in the planning stages of publishing an art and literary magazine and a poetry anthology.

I desire to teach in any area that I have experience in and manage a small art gallery or work in an art museum.
