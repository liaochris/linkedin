Jessica Rivera’s Summary

Retail Sales Consultant/Sales Solution Advisor with 7 years experience in the credit card processing industry. My day to day activities include demoing Chase Paymentech products, conferencing with new and existing merchant prospects to discuss ways to implement new methods of payments, making overall recommendations on the best way to process credit card transactions on the Chase Paymentech network, and also assisting sales representatives in closing their deals. Minimal travel is required.

Before Sales Consulting, I worked in the Chase Paymentech IRM group. The IRM group works with VAR software companies and merchants to certify their software solutions to the Chase Paymentech network in order to accept credit card transactions. This group specializes in understanding the Chase Paymentech proprietary message formats and communication methods for all industry types supported by our network.

Prior to the IRM group, I worked in both the PC Support & Terminal help desks. This consisted of answering customer support calls to assist with transaction and batch questions and issues, terminal and POS questions and issues and overall customer support for Chase Paymentech merchants.
