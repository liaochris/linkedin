Scott Seab’s Summary

I am a telecommunications and cable law attorney working with traditional (LEC/MSO) and converged (IaaS/VoIP/ISP) service providers. Daily activities include cascading topical regulatory & policy issues with local, state, and FCC constituents, ensuring new client services fit within applicable regulatory silos, developing consistent policies for SPs, negotiating video programming contracts, pole/tower attachment & conduit agreements, as well as interconnection and IP (fiber optic) contracts. All of these services involve constant productive collaboration with team members across multiple corporate departments for success.

Scott Seab’s Specialties:

Telecommunications (ILECs & CLECs); Cable/MSOs (incumbent and FTTH/VoIP startups); FCC/state/LFA compliance; Real estate - land use, zoning and rights of way management; DMCA/OCILLA compliance policies; Municipal/state/federal administrative law; Video programming contracts; Third-party/soft campaigns.
