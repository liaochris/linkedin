Rob Stephenson’s Summary

I am a Managing Partner and head of the Tax Recruitment Practice at Maven Partners.

Maven Partners is a specialist finance and taxation recruitment services business. The partners have a wealth of experience in executive search, interim management and temporary recruitment.

At Maven Partners, we are holders and seekers of knowledge. In our case this refers to knowledge gained through experience, dedication, and perseverance. Knowledge is Maven's central value.

According to the Oxford English Dictionary, knowledge is defined as information and awareness gained through experience.
