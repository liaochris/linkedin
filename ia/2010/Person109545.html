Jason Crawford’s Summary

I started my career in advertising as a direct response media buyer for Generation Marketing, a small agency in Salt Lake City, Utah. In my role I quickly learned how to create strong relationships with clients and media partners and to sell through ideas and strategies. I quickly advanced and was promoted to media buying supervisor where I managed a team of three buyers and two assistants.

After three years at Generation Marketing, my wife and I moved to Michigan and I took a position with Doner advertising. My desire was to work more on strategy development and I transitioned from a buying to a planning role. As a senior media planner I worked on both national and local plans for ADT, Hotels.com, Six Flags,and AutoTrader.

Also while at working at Doner, I helped create and lead a committee that was formed to educate the agency and clients on emerging media. This opportunity sparked my passion for online and emerging media. To pursue this passion I left Doner to join GM Planworks as a Senior Digital Planner. Working on GM was amazing as I had the opportunity to truly drive very progressive thinking and create multiple 360º programs that engaged the consumer in the brand experience.

I was later recruited to NYC by one of my managers with whom I had worked with at Doner as a Media Supervisor at Universal Mccann. In my role as a media strategy supervisor on J&J (K-Y, Monistat, Stayfree, O.B., Rogaine,) I was responsible for creating and selling through media-neutral strategies and plans designed to achieve the clients' business and marketing objectives.

In my current role as Digital Media Supervisor at Digitas, I've worked on the American Express branding account and I am presently working to grow our social media practice across all agency clients.
