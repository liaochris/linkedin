Mark Sawyier’s Summary

Mark is President and founder of Off Campus Media, an integrated marketing company that targets college students.

Mark has overseen the four year evolution of Off Campus Media from its roots as MovingOffCampus.com, an Internet company to help college students find apartments, into Off Campus Media has evolved into a a full-service integrated marketing company that is 100% focused on the college student demographic.

Mark has successfully led his team in developing strong relationships with local and national companies seeking to drive awareness and results from the ever-elusive college student demographic.

During his time as CEO, Mark has gained significant experience across developing trackable and results oriented marketing programs that leverage an integrated mix of digital and social media, mobile, peer-to-peer and event marketing tactics. Mark also has significant SEO and SEM experience and does consulting work in these areas as well.

Mark has expertise in marketing, sales, Internet strategy, business development and strategic partnerships.

Mark graduated Magna Cum Laude from Washington University in St. Louis in May 2007 where he majored in International and Area Studies and Spanish. Mark is fluent in Spanish and proficient in French.
