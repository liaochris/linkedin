Mary Simms’s Summary

An experienced media relations professional, Mary Simms has a background in journalism, television production and international affairs.

Simms began her career as a military journalist, working in nearly every aspect of television news. She has embedded with U.S. and foreign military troops in dangerous locations around the world and filed reports in the midst of tumultuous conditions in Afghanistan, Pakistan, Kyrgyzstan, Qatar, Kuwait, and the Philippines.

Deciding to join the military shortly after the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan began, Simms was compelled to cover the evolving situation in the Middle East; poverty in developing nations; and many other social issues. During her time as a military journalist she was passionate about highlighting under-served communities and regions, distinguishing herself in humanitarian reporting. In October 2005, Simms traveled to Pakistan just days following an 8.1 Magnitude earthquake to report on the devastation and the humanitarian relief efforts.

In 2003, she spent a year in Afghanistan covering the war, with a focus on rebuilding and reconstruction efforts. Simms was a key member of the public affairs office for the U.S. Army during Operation Enduring Freedom. For her wartime reporting efforts, she was honored by the Foundation of American Women in Radio & Television (AWRT) with a Gracie Allen Award.

Following her time in the military Simms went to work on-camera as a television news reporter for the NBC News affiliate in Honolulu, focusing largely on environmental, military and social issues.

Since joining the EPA, Simms has led the creative development and implementation of hundreds of communication and outreach strategies and media campaigns. A firm believer in the importance of strategic communications, she has developed and taught several successful media training workshops focused on effective environmental communications in order to enhance understanding, education, and outcome.

Public affairs, media training, crisis communications, media relations, marketing, branding, writing, nonlinear video editing using Avid.
