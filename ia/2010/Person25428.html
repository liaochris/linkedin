Bob Gibbs’s Summary

After 12 years of managing and operating a small successful family owned trucking company, American Warehouse was established in September 1999 with partner Dan Havey with idea of providing a professional business partnership to businesses throughout. After 7 years and the addition of partner Jason Darnstaedt we now provide services to many companies in multiple countries. It is our goal to become the top service provider in the warehousing and distribution industry. We continue to research our industry for the most current technologies and beyond. A great example for this is: One of our customer located in the UK needed us to implement a system to account for their total account receivables in the US. We designed a system we call BEAM (Backend Account Management), which handles their complete customer service, collection, order processing, RMA's, sales relations and QC department in conjunction with our excellent distribution services.
