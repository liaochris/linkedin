Peter Stratos’s Summary

International Tax Consultant & Advisor
Peter Stratos is an expert in international tax for both individual and corporate taxes. Peter’s primary expertise in international tax are in the areas of foreign tax credit planning, residency issues, state and local issues for international and multinational businesses, expats and inpats, pre-immigration planning, transfer pricing, FAS109 & FIN48, compensation planning, treaty positions, entity selection, organizational structuring and withholding issues, artists, entertainers, athletes and various other matters.

Author and Speaker on International Tax Issues
Peter is a speaker for the AICPA and other organizations on various international tax topics and has also written several articles and issue papers covering these topics. He was recognized by the Virginia Society of Certified Public Accountant’s and Virginia Business Magazine as a “Super CPA” in corporate taxation for 2008 and 2009.

Peter Stratos’s Specialties:

International Tax, International Tax Treaties, International Income Tax, International Property Tax, International Capital Gains Tax, Foreign Tax Credit Planning, Residency Issues, State and Local Issues for Multinational Businesses, Expat and Inpat Issue, Compensation Planning, Entity Selection, Organizational Structuring, Pre-immigration Planning, International Estate, Trust & Gift Planning, Cross-border Estate, Trust & Gift Planning, Stock Option Planning, Transfer Pricing, FAS109, FIN48
