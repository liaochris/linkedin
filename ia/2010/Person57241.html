David Kelley’s Summary

With more than 22 years of successful experience in marketing, business development, business ownership and management, Dave has focused primarily on the Internet, web development, franchising, commercial printing and graphic design industries.

David established Digital Autonomy in order to help growing businesses make the transition from traditional forms of advertising to more progressive ways of marketing themselves through the implementation of a Digital Marketing Strategy.

Prior to Digital Autonomy, David helped build TruePresence, a national online marketing firm based in Baltimore that specialized in paid search advertising, web development and permission based e-mail marketing. TruePresence was built on top of a franchise network model that David helped build and maintain; going from 5 franchisees to 25 during his tenure.

Before joining TruePresence, David had experienced franchising from the other side of the desk by buying and building out an AlphaGraphics print location in Columbia, MD from the ground up.

In 2000 David was an owner and managing partner for Rand Interactive, a leading web and multimedia development firm for clients such as NIKE, Arthur Andersen, Lockheed Martin, and the USDA. Before that, Dave served the dual role of both VP of Marketing and VP of Interactive Services for ClarkNet, one of the first commercial Internet service providers in the country. His leadership positioned both Rand Interactive and ClarkNet for purchase by Change Technology Partners and Verio respectively, both prominent leaders in the technology world.

When he’s not working, David can be found working with his daughter Meghan (13) on her volleyball skills and helping his wife Krissy chase after their two younger children, Jackson (6) and Lindsay (4).
