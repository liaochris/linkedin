Joshua Gimer’s Summary

Joshua Gimer is a Sr. IT Security Analyst for large university in which his basic duties include performing Penetration Tests against mission critical systems housing large amounts of sensitive/protected/private data and performing security reviews. Training developers on secure programming practices, and has extensive AppSec knowledge. Being first responder to incidents and performing incident analysis, including working with Law Enforcement in digital forensic matters. Establishing security procedures and policies including secure configuration baselines. Performing Network Security Monitoring (NSM) to detect intrusions and intrusion attempts. Reverse engineering malware to determine internals, and generating rules that can be used for detection. He is also interested in Vulnerability Research, and participates in the Zero Day Initiative (ZDI) when possible.

Joshua is a Red Hat Certified Engineer, Holds Global Information Assurance Certifications in Penetration Testing, and Incident Handling. He is also a member of the GIAC Advisory Board with the SANS Institute and is a SANS Mentor.
