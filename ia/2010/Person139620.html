Michael Tanenbaum, MBA’s Summary

My focus is on marketing and brand strategy in digital media. As a consultant, my clients include major corporations, startup businesses and non-profit organizations. I am passionate about branded digital storytelling as reflected in my presentation work. My writing and communication skills have earned the praise of my employers, professors, colleagues and peers.

After receiving a BA from The University of Chicago, I worked for several years before completing an MBA from Pepperdine University. I am always learning. My education is life-long and reflects wisdom gained from the branding books, social media blogs, clients and random mediums through which I constantly find inspiration.

    The Walt Disney Company / ABC Television

    (Public Company; DIS; Entertainment industry)

    January 2007 — April 2007 (4 months)

    I collaborated on a marketing and promotional plan for “Traveler”, a primetime TV serial drama that debuted in the summer of 2007. In addition to conducting market research, we devised an advertising framework that included a mix of traditional and new media such as Facebook, blogs and torrents, for the company to implement in marketing the show. My Keynote presentation to Disney and ABC executives wove together the familiar branding elements of the Disney website, including logos, colors and personality, with the look, feel and sound of the edgy, Generation Y-inspired series.
