Katherine (Fye) Sears’s Summary

Simplicity - I believe that Marketing should be simple - find a need or desire held by a market segment, and create the product or service that satisfies it. Then, communicate effectively to these customers, and follow up by measuring your success to ensure profitability. Of course, today's products have become exponentially more complicated in order to offer customers the illusion of continued simplicity and enhanced convenience.

Balance - I believe you are a success in marketing when you know your customer, your product, and your business - and can balance all the resulting, occasionally conflicting, needs and desires effectively.

To that end, I offer the following skills:
- Experienced marketing leader recognized for strategic vision and execution of resulting projects in a timely and profitable manner.
- Proven ability prioritizing and directing multiple projects simultaneously.
- Effective and charismatic presenter.
- Extensive experience creating quantifiable value propositions, resulting in successful positioning, pricing, and messaging.
- High level of international business acumen including: extensive travel and living experience in both Europe and Asia, conversational French, Japanese culture, and experience behind the scenes in the travel industry.
