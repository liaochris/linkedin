Ryan Maloney’s Summary

Play-by-play voice of Oswego State men’s ice hockey (2007-2009). Called more than 60 games, drawing between 100-500 online listeners per broadcast. Voted “Best College Broadcaster” in USCHO.com Fan Forum poll (2009). Attracted largest audience in college radio station’s history for last hockey broadcast of 2008-09 season (more than 500 online listeners). Established campus radio station’s first remote broadcast pre-game show at local sports bar. Provided hour-long post-game show after each game complete with player and coach interviews

Syracuse University football beat reporter for Sports Radio 570 WSYR (2008). Conducted and edited interviews for immediate playback on WSYR radio post-game show. Covered orange carpet film premiere of “The Express” for WSYR radio

Contributing writer for The Post-Standard (2010). Covered the 2010 NCAA Division III men’s ice hockey Frozen Four.

Produced Syracuse Crunch hockey games for WHEN radio (2008-09). Hosted out-of-town scoreboard and “Bradley’s Jewel of the Game” segments on-air.

Interned at WIXT/WSYR-TV (May 2004-August 2006) gaining experience in writing, shooting, and editing.

Awarded “Most Creative Sportscast” (2008) by campus TV station WTOP at annual awards dinner.

Provided administrative support for SID of SUNY Oswego, supporting in all activities related to game management, statistics, writing, editing, and publishing of news releases. Developed accounts with social networking websites (i.e. Facebook, Twitter) to promote Oswego State athletic events and fund-raisers for athletic teams. Created Oswego State Athletics YouTube channel for video highlights produced by SUNY Oswego students.

Published author of feature articles on Oswego State student-athletes for college newspaper The Oswegonian.
