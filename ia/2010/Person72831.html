Garry Meyer’s Summary

Garry Meyer is a senior executive recognized for delivering significant value within major financial and technology organizations.

He has an extensive track record in leading large-scale initiatives (valued at hundreds of millions of dollars and involving more than eight hundred professionals.) These initiatives delivered extraordinary business benefits and enabled high rates of sustainable growth for Fortune 50 companies.
Garry has extensive international experience in Commercial Finance and the strategic use of technology for Consumer Finance that he gained in assignments on five continents and in more than fifty countries. He enabled profitable high growth by creating and leveraging Global Change Control Boards that ensured development and reuse across markets.

In addition to managing technology, he was a key member of the due diligence teams and Investment Committee that invested over $4 billion in communications, technology, and broadcasting for GE Capital with superior returns on investment.
Garry has been acknowledged as a consistently high-energy outstanding performer with honors including the GE Pinnacle Award and Circle of Excellence. His colleagues, peers, and numerous lines of business have benefited from his ability to build and motivate global teams that deliver outstanding results.

In his most recent position he directly managed more than 800 technology professionals and delivered platforms strategically aligned with a top tier Property and Casualty Insurer’s growth and profitability mandates. Prior to that position, he built and led the IT compliance and controllership initiatives to achieve SOX certification and other regulatory certification as the first IT Controller at GE Money.

Garry is a certified Quality Leader who has successfully applied Lean Six-sigma in services organizations.
