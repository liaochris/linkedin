Robert David Torres’s Summary

Robert David Torres is a recognized and award-winning creative leader with over 17 years of experience in graphic design, e-commerce, marketing, web development and experience design. He offers a rare combination of aesthetic pedigree, technical expertise and strategic business thinking, with an emphasis on ROI driven outcomes.

As Senior Interaction Designer for Intuit's Accounting Professionals Division (APD) team, Robert helps shape the design of their products, but more importantly, the overall experiences they have with them. In addition, Robert will leverage his experience in marketing, management, development and process to help deliver the Experience Design team to a world of rapid iteration.

Formerly the Web Development Manager for APD, Mr. Torres ensured the technical and shop/buy experience success for Intuit's 4 accounting and tax professional web sites. He led a team of developers and delivered on various large-scale initiatives that help made the web a preferred channel for their customers.

As owner of Directly Creative LLC, Mr. Torres helps his clients in all aspects of product, creative and e-commerce strategies to achieve bottom line success. Past success included building cohesive corporate identity for Balboa Travel and achieved higher conversions and qualified lead generation for companies like RealAge and Find Your Customers.

Previously, Mr. Torres held the position of Creative Director for Anonymizer Inc. He was responsible for web site marketing, overall creative direction, web site conversion metrics, corporate identity/branding, and product marketing/positioning. He increased web site conversion by nearly 50% in key areas after joining the company and increased web site average order value 30%.

Prior to joining Anonymizer Mr. Torres was at Provide Commerce (Proflowers). He came on board while the company was a start-up in 1999 and immediately became the creative force behind the Proflowers web site for nearly four years.
