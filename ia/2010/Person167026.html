Quills Language Services’s Summary

* We are a translation company providing translation, localization and interpreting services in a broad range of fields and languages.
* Our translators and interpreters are fluent in their native tongues, competent in their specific fields and have many years of professional experience.
* Quills allows your company to reach international markets by means of specialized translations in several languages and specialization areas such as Marketing, Financial, Medical, Legal and Technical.
* Our professionals specialized in localization services have the necessary tools to adequately adapt your product to a particular market, taking into account its original context.
* We provide interpreters that have many years of professional experience and a well-rounded cultural and linguistic knowledge in an array of languages.
* Quality is our priority; for this reason, our linguists take initial tests and are constantly evaluated.
* Technical translations can be provided in various formats including Microsoft Office, QuarkXpress, PageMaker and FrameMaker.
