Gary Cohen’s Summary

As an accomplished General Manager with extensive global P&L leadership experience in consumer products, I have led businesses ranging from $700 million to $2 billion. By driving integrated marketing, R&D innovation, organic growth, international expansion and acquisition, I deliver exceptional bottom line results in small, mid and large cap enterprises. My strengths include building best-in-class marketing, sales, manufacturing operations, and global research and development departments that consistently exceed profit targets and realize significant cost saving opportunities.

With special interest in organizational effectiveness and talent management, I am known for building high performing, cross-functional teams, creating strong flagship brands, and achieving operational excellence.
