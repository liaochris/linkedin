Ryan Stanley’s Summary

CEO and Lead Coach for Ryan Stanley - Professional Life Coaching Services. A Professional Life & Business Coaching Practice with a focus on Musicians, Music Producers, Engineers, Management Teams, Booking Agents and Others throughout the Music Industry who are choosing to make a significant change in their professional and personal lives that will enable them to have more focus and clarity so they can in-turn make more money, get more gigs, become become better musicians, better businessmen/women, or even just better humans beings.

Dynamic Engaged Caring Professional Coach offering business consulting and advisory services to Musicians and others throughout the Music Industry in the areas of strategic planning, business growth and development, complex business challenges, career/life transition.

Years of experience in artist management, and strategic business development. Expertise in creating forward momentum for artistically creative individuals. Uniquely able to inspire you to focus on, pursue and achieve your specific goals in a most efficient, confidential, and trustworthy manner.

Experienced CFO and Director of Finance for companies in a variety of industries including Day Care, Real Estate Information Services, and International Outsourcing.
