Courtney Wilson’s Summary

In my 9 years in the Homebuilding/Real Estate Development industry, I have effectively promoted and marketed residential homes, developments, mixed-use developments, and brands inside very large organizations as well as independently as a marketing consultant in residential and commercial real estate. I’ve worked with all disciplines within homebuilding and real estate and understand the processes from land selection, engineering and zoning, architecture and design, site plan concepts, land plan design, through the opening phases of sales and marketing of the development as well as closing out a project successfully. I am a team player who works well with all levels of management and has always received reviews to that effect from both staff and managers.

I've recently started my own company, and now I'm enjoying growing my consulting business in both residential and commercial real estate. Managing relationships with contracted vendors and partners such as advertising and public relations firms is something I have done for many years in the Homebuilding industry, as well as created my own designs and graphics when needed. My experience and education in marketing research and strategic planning, budget planning and advertising for builders is what prompted me to start the company.
