Charmaine Bugeja’s Summary

As a highly-competent events planner and tourist guide by nature, I have always been intrigued by the arts, media and tourism industries.

Well-travelled, with ample professional experience in various administrative and marketing segments, I have recently taken to doing what I really love... that is taking you to magnificent places around Malta and organise your special day (because I know you're too busy to do that) :)

I just want my clients to feel at home, comfortable, and remove the stress from the practical part so they can focus on the fun stuff! As long as there's knowledge of the subject, a dose of creativity and some added zest, it's 3 clicks of the heels, and voila'! .... okay it's easier said than done, and might take a bit more than Dorothy's magic slippers, but as long as I'm enjoying it, bring it on!!
