Jon Conradt’s Summary

As an Engineering Program Manager for Google Chrome I have been responsible for driving releases. My emphasis has been on Mac and Linux versions as well as WebKit, Plugins, and site compatibility. Every week we push a new release to our developer channel. Almost every month we push a new beta release to our beta testers and every quarter a new stable release to the general public. I enjoy working on a fast paced, complex, and highly visible product with a fantastic team.

At Microsoft I was part of a talented team of professionals working to revolutionize how television and movies get to your home. Building on Microsoft technologies, the Microsoft Mediaroom product will enable new ways of advertising, entertaining, and communicating. I was responsible for the Application Infrastructure group. My projects included a state of the art XHTML/CSS browser, oversight of Home Media Sharing, WinCE, .NET Compact Framework and the other cutting edge features.

I have always enjoyed using technology and working with technical people to solve big problems. At the Gallup Organization I founded the team and designed the system that collects all of Gallup's web survey data. This system, built in Java and on Oracle, handled hundreds of projects a month in dozens of languages with high availability.
