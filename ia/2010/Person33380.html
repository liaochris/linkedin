Carrie Collier-Brown’s Summary

I am an energy regulatory attorney located in Washington, DC. I represent utilities, independent transmission and generation companies in federal regulatory matters; advise clients on market rules in the restructured ISO and RTO markets; compliance with FERC Order Nos. 888 and 890, including preparation and filing of compliance filings and revisions to Open Access Transmission Tariffs (“OATT”).

I also have extensive experience and knowledge of the electricity market in the Texas ERCOT region.

Carrie Collier-Brown’s Specialties:

Federal energy regulations, ERCOT market, renewable energy development, RTO/ISO market rules
