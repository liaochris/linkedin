Anab Jain’s Summary

Anab is a designer, entrepreneur, speculator with over seven years of experience in both corporate and educational organisations, as well as in research labs and think-tanks.

Key Clients in UK and India: Microsoft Research Cambridge, Nokia Design, Design Council, CBS Products, Mattel, Helen Hamlyn Centre, Unwired Ventures, EPSRC, Surabhi Foundation and Live|Work.

Key Speaker at Conferences: WCIT2010, LIFT Geneva, SIGGRAPH, EPIC, Worktech, DesignEngaged, Interesting09, Interactivos.

Guest lecturer and tutor at several design schools such as the RCA London, CIID, Copenhagen, ECA Edinburgh and Design School, Dundee.

Exhibited at MoMA, New York, London Design Festival and Tate Modern amongst others.

Anab Jain’s Specialties:

Interaction Design and Strategy, Design Futures +Foresight, Storytelling and Communication, Scenario Building, Public Engagement.
