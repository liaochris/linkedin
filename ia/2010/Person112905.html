Kay Paumier’s Summary

I am on LinkedIn to connect with companies that want to become better known and more successful.

My communications and public relations firm, Communications Plus, offers the following services to help businesses get to the next level.

Public relations: We work with reporters and analysts in both mainstream and "new" media to generate favorable publicity and increase credibility. http://www.communicationsplus.net/pr_results.html

Writing: We write everything from news releases to web content. The award-winning copy is clear, concise and compelling. http://www.communicationsplus.net/writ_examples.html

Communications: We help clients improve their online and offline "presence" through enhancing their websites, improving their presentations, preparing webinars and the like.

Traiining: We do in-house writing training.

What I am looking for in LinkedIn.

•Technology companies, preferably small to medium-sized (10-100) or divisions of larger companies that want to become better known and increase sales leads.

•Potential partners, including virtually anyone in marketing or communications such as other PR professionals, graphic artists, web designers, advertising consultants and the like.
