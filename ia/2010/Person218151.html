Yvonne Perez Emerson’s Summary

As an award winning design director with over 15+ years experience, I have a trained eye for excellence in print and online design. I enjoy collaborating with awesome people, providing solutions and inspiring creative.

I've had the opportunity to contribute to many creative teams and projects. Including working with high profile brands to create content that lives on-line and in print such as: blogger outreach and events, social community applications, digital personalization experiences, editorial calendar development, and content creation. I've also been involved in leading city art programs as well as mentoring/teaching graduate design students.

It's my philosophy to lead by example and thoughtful design is always the foundation towards creative solutions.
