Becky Ringler’s Summary

Having worked in various fields throughout my professional life, becoming an entrepreneur, and assisting others to reach their own personal and professional goals in the process, was the most natural thing in the world for me.

My first venture into being a true online entrepreneur gave me the know-how and mindset to put all of my skills into action, and has allowed me to step into mentoring, training, and leadership roles in my newest venture with the Trump Network.

It is a true pleasure to be showing top-shelf executives, entrepreneurs, and other leaders that they can indeed own and run a business of this sort without having to chase friends, family and neighbors via hotel meetings and coffee presentations, that are both time consuming and irritating, and instead introduce them to and teach them how to use the internet marketing system our executive leadership team has in place to get their businesses off the ground and running quickly.

There is a reason our team is the fastest growing in the network, and I am proud to be a leader within that team.

Having led the charge in my previous business into other countries world-wide, I am excited for the upcoming future expansion into 17 countries within the next year and a half (and over 80 in the next five), and am actively looking for leaders in those countries (Mexico, China, Brazil, Canada, Japan, all of South America, Europe...) to be ready, on the ground floor when it opens.

Please contact me if you are interested in being one of those leaders, or if you are here in the States and are interested in finding out how you can be on the ground floor here.

It is my goal to have one of the most successful, and largest international organizations within the Trump Network. Any referrals are much appreciated.
