Joe Lavelle’s Summary

An accomplished consulting executive and business coach with a record of successfully meeting the business and technology challenges of diverse organizations including health plans, health delivery networks, health care companies, and several Fortune 500 companies. A balance of technical experience supported by extensive business management and leadership development skills. Proven ability to assume responsibility for a business unit, to diagnose issues quickly and to execute whatever plans are necessary to ensure excellent service, growth and profitability.

I always appreciate the opportunity to leverage my experience (re)establishing profitable operations, building high-powered teams and delivering outstanding results. I insist on working in an organization that is fanatical about customer service and satisfaction as well as employee growth and satisfaction.
