Michael Weickert’s Summary

Primary Goal: Creating solutions to unmet medical needs that provide substantial benefits to patients and rewards for investors and stakeholders.

Dr. Weickert has 30 years experience in scientific research and for the last 18 years has led drug development and marketing at a variety of Biotech and Pharma companies. He is currently President and CEO of SEA Medical Systems, a Medical Device startup focused on safety of IV drug delivery. As President of Wit Creek Consulting, he advises US and ex-US VCs and companies on product selection, prioritization and development of anti-infectives, vaccines, therapeutics, and drug delivery products. He also served as COO, VP Development and Head Program Management to clients and has written business plans to secure funding. As CBO at Corium International, Inc., and StrataGent Life Sciences, Inc. in 2007, he managed marketing, business development, finance and the merger of the two companies. From 1998-2007, Dr. Weickert held a variety of positions at Nektar Therapeutics including Senior Director Product Marketing, also leading Portfolio Management and New Product Planning. He and his team evaluated hundreds of product ideas and in-licensing opportunities and wrote over 30 business plans. He collaborated with Business Development on partnering efforts in the US, EU, and Japan. In 2000, he created an anti-infectives business unit that led to four products currently in development. At Ligand Pharmaceuticals (1997-1998), Somatogen, Inc. (1992-1997), and Nektar, he led the development of small molecule (Targretin®), biologic (rHb) and drug delivery products (inhaled Avonex®, inhaled Amphotericin B) respectively.

Prior to joining industry, Dr. Weickert researched gene regulation at the National Cancer Institute of NIH. He received his Ph.D. in Genetics from the University of Wisconsin. He is lead inventor on several patents and published many scientific papers in leading journals.
