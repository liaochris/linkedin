Andy Halls’s Summary

I aim to wriggle my way into industry, and defy the critics that claim Journalism is a dying trade.

I'm always willing to change and adapt to improve, and like to learn new things.

Alongside my degree in Journalism, I am News Editor of Pluto: UCLan's Student Newspaper.

I am the joint-editor of www.blogpreston.co.uk, a hub for news, views and information for Preston, Lancs.

I spent several weeks at The Sun newspaper, which started off as work experience, resulting in paid shifts; and I will return to work further shifts in the holiday months.

I have also completed work experience at BBC Radio Berkshire, assisting the news and sports team, over a couple of varied periods.

I am also involved in the BBC School Report Mentoring scheme, where they send journalists into schools to teach core skills to 14-17 year olds.
