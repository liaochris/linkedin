Lisbeth Tanz’s Summary

Freelance writer and editor who enjoys connecting with people and creating work to showcase their expertise. This includes ghostwriting articles for print and online publication (including ezines and newsletters), capturing personal histories and stories, editing your written works to make sure they shine grammatically and story-wise and synthesizing your written works into usable articles.

Lisbeth Tanz’s Specialties:

I've spent years listening to people - from a sales perspective, life/business coach perspective, caregiver perspective - my true talent lies in listening, synthesizing and creating written material that sounds like the person I'm writing for.

My passions - the things I love writing and learning about - include: animals, nature, real estate, humor-based topics and business.
