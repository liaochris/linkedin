Ted Brown’s Summary

An accomplished sales leader with 20+ years of global experience, Mr. Brown holds the position of Oracle Regional Manager, leading a team of field sales reps focused on business intelligence and analytics opportunities in the central US.

Leveraging his general management, sales, business development, channel strategy, relationship management and consulting background, Mr. Brown has held senior leadership positions with a number of successful high tech companies. Prior to Oracle, Mr. Brown spent almost five years at BEA Systems, most recently as Global Account Manager, generating $10M+ in annual revenue. Prior to BEA Systems, Mr. Brown was Director of Sales at Colabranet, an on-demand CRM venture, and he was a top-ranked sales executive at high-growth integration software start-up Extricity (acquired by Peregrine Systems and now part of Hewlett-Packard), creating key business operations from the ground-up as a Regional Sales Manager. Prior to Extricity, Mr. Brown spent four years as a Managing Consultant with Deloitte Consulting, selling and leading high-profile professional services engagements with Fortune 500 clients.

A decorated former Naval Aviator and U.S. Navy Lieutenant Commander, Mr. Brown graduated from The United States Naval Academy with a Bachelor of Science with Merit, and holds a Master of Business Administration from The University of Texas at Austin, where he received the Kozmetzky Award, its highest academic honor.

Mr. Brown lives in Austin, Texas with his wife and two children where he provides leadership to several civic organizations.
