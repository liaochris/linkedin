Jennifer Maggiore’s Summary

Oct 2009 Named One of AZ Republic's 35 Women Entrepreneurs Under 35

"Jennifer Maggiore will invariably bring perspecitive, insight and value to your company. She gets marketing, social media and networking. Now more than ever is the time to market your brand, product and service. You can rely on Jennifer to help you leverage your business marketing strategy. She is refreshing, authentic, professional and energetic." Sherry Azzarella, former ASBA Exec, Founder of non-profit Upwardly Noble

My background includes experience with small and medium business entrepreneurship, business start ups, and both the corporate and non-profit sectors for national organizations including Nordstrom, M & I Bank, the Valley of the Sun YMCA, various real estate organizations, the automotive aftermarket, restaurants and more. My expertise, and passion, includes Social Media marketing, ROI/tracking success of campaigns, branding, integrating web, Television, radio and print campaigns, experiential event planning and management. I am also a writer for the University of Phoenix Alumni Association Phoenix Focus, am a speaker and educator, and am an avid blogger at http://mcmaz.wordpress.com/, and have been featured in FuelNet Monthly and The Business Journal of Phoenix.

I grew up in South Florida and moved to the Phoenix area in 2000. After attending the University of Florida and Arizona State University, I graduated from the University of Phoenix in 2005 and hold degrees in Journalism and Marketing. We are proud members of the Arizona Small Business Association and Human Services Professionals Assoc of Scottsdale-Phoenix.
