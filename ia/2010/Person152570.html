Nichole Devolites’s Summary

For 12 years Nichole has developed strong marketing leadership skills, serving in a wide range of roles from Principal Consultant, to Director of Marketing, to Partner Marketing Manager and Writer/Editor. She has delivered marketing programs and solutions, managing up to $1M budgets, for a variety of companies, including but not limited to Tysons Galleria, Microsoft, Booz Allen Hamilton, Avepoint and BuzzBee.

Most recently, Nichole has assumed the role of Director of Marketing for Tysons Galleria - Washington, D.C.'s premier luxury mall. In this role, she is responsible for elevating The Galleria's profile, not just within the Metro DC area, but nationally and internationally as well. In addition, she is still Owner and Operator of Conversa Group, a marketing consulting firm for Microsoft partners, seeking to build relationships within Microsoft; train existing employees and sell to customers.
