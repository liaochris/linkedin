Lora Cantele’s Summary

Registered aromatherapist focused on empowering individuals to enhance their health and well being through individual aromatherapy services/consultations, providing superior products and aromatherapy eduation. Specializing in stress, womens’ issues and aromatherapy for children. Interested in integrating aromatherapy into hospice, nursing care facilities and heathcare provider settings. Also interested in academic work as well, and perhaps advising corporations on how they can incorporate aromatherapy into the workplace to enhance their employees productivity and health issues.

Lora Cantele’s Specialties:

Safe use of essential oils in skin care, as well as for babies/children, the elderly and throughout pregnancy.
