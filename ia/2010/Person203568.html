Tiffany Yick’s Summary

MarketTools is the leader in software and services for Enterprise Feedback Management (EFM) and Market Research. Our leading EFM solution, MarketTools CustomerSat, empowers you to transform feedback from customers, employees, and partners into a strategic asset for driving competitive advantage. Increase revenue and customer loyalty by listening, analyzing, and acting on feedback across the enterprise in real time.

MarketTools CustomerSat offers integrated listening across the broadest range of feedback channels, including email surveys, social media, online communities, and call recordings. MarketTools CustomerSat users benefit from the strongest Action Management capabilities for taking action to close the feedback loop, powerful analytics, the most comprehensive out-of-the-box reports, and flexible service options – ranging from initial training to full-service engagements – delivered by highly experienced EFM consultants.
