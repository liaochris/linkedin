    2010 Interpunk American Skate Fest

    (Public Company; NWS; Music industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    PR Log (Press Release) – Mar 18, 2010 – The 2010 Interpunk American Skate Fest which will feature some of worlds biggest names in punk rock history is set to take place on June 25th and 26th at legendary Skatopia in Rutland, Ohio.

    The Artist roster which reads like the who's who of punk includes appearences by Gwar, Fishbone, Meat Puppets, Agent Orange, D.I., CJ Ramone, Machine 22, Agression, Dirty Filthy Mugs, Mustard Plug, Murphys Law, Teenage Bottlerocket, Downtown Brown, TRUSTY, Fast Piece of Furniture, Greg Ginn & the Taylor Texas Corrugators, Minus-One, Frontside Five, 7 Seconds, Youth Brigade, DOA, Sleeper Agent, McRad, Nassau Chainsaw, El Destructo & Pinstripes and just added Dirge Within, Green Jello, Mobile Deathcamp.

    "We're very proud of this lineup" states Skatopia owner Brewce Martin. "it's time skateboarding and music come back together"

    Advance tickets for the event go on sale March 20th at Ticketmaster.com. There will also be 1000 special campsite packages available by calling Skatopia directly at 740-416-6459 or by visiting their website at www.skatopia.org.
