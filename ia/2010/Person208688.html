Dimitris Tsigos’s Summary

I received my diploma in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science, University of Crete, in January 2001 and my MBA from the Athens University of Economics and Business, Department of Management Science and Technology, in December 2005. In 1998 I was guest researcher at the Computational Vision and Active Perception Laboratory of the Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, while in 1999 and 2000, I was a member of the Networks and Telecommunications Group of the Institute of Computer Science at the Foundation for Research and Technology, Hellas.

In September 2000 I was one of the founders of VIRTUAL TRIP Ltd, the first successful student start-up in Greece, which has evolved to a leading entrepreneurial group of thirteen dynamic companies, eleven of them based in Greece, one in Akron, Ohio - US and one in Madrid, Spain.

In May 2006, I was nominated the Special Prize of the University of Crete for Continues Entrepreneurial Development while later I served as President of the Hellenic Informatics Union from May 2007 to April 2010. Moreover, in September 2008 I was elected President of the Hellenic Federation of Young Entrepreneurs Associations and in November 2009 I was elected Vice President & Treasurer of YES - European Confederation of Young Entrepreneurs.

My main goal is to create a sustainable working environment, bringing the notion of the "Entrepreneurial Ecosystem", where individuals can work together and materialize their innovation and creativity to the benefit both of their own and their socioeconomic environment.

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    YES – European Confederation of Young Entrepreneurs is the main association of young entrepreneurs in Europe (representing around 40.000 young entrepreneurs under 40 years old).

    It aims at improving the economic and social performance of young entrepreneurship in Europe. Its members include the major national associations of young entrepreneurs in the field of industry, trade and services from 16 countries: Albania, Austria, Belgium, Cyprus, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Kazakhstan, The Netherlands, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Turkey, Ukraine.

    YES actively contributes to the promotion of the entrepreneural spirit in Europe and outside. It stimulates exchanges and network activity between young entrepreneurs, fosters cooperation and sharing of best practices among their national associations, particularly in the fields of education and training.

  • Founder & CEO

    Virtual Trip Group

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2000 — Present (9 years 10 months)

    Virtual Trip is a leading entrepreneurial ecosystem consisted by 13 dynamic companies, eleven based in Greece, Heraklion & Athens, one based in Madrid, Spain and one based in Akron, Ohio - US.

    The entrepreneurial ecosystem has grown organically, boosted by the strong entrepreneurial drive of the founding team, since the establishment of its first member, Virtual Trip Ltd, in year 2000 at the Science & Technology Park of Crete, Greece.

    Virtual Trip is widely recognized as the first successful high-tech start up effort in Greece as well as one of the fastest-growing companies in the domestic high-tech market, having grown by a factor of 75 in terms of revenue and by a factor of 12 in terms of staff in less than nine ears. Its 2008 revenues surpassed €7.5M, with net profits of more than €1M.

    The Group companies offer their broad spectrum of high-tech ICT products and services to the international market.

    Virtual Trip recently introduced the concept of the "Entrepreneurial Ecosystem" and became member of EBAN - the European Trade Association for Business Angels, Seed Funds, & Early Stage Market Players, as an early stage / seed capital fund.

    The Virtual Trip Entrepreneurial Ecosystem currently includes the following companies: Virtual Trip Ltd, Epignosis Ltd, MVNS SA, NGLS SA, IT Center Ltd, Solo Gateway SA, Insurance Manager SA, S4Trips Ltd, Diagramma Ltd, VERITA Ltd, Actech LLC and Virtual Trip España SL. All of these twelve companies are subsidiaries and receive incubation services by Virtual Trip Holdings SA.

    The Virtual Trip vision is to create a sustainable, growing entrepreneurial ecosystem including a large number of non-competing, high-technology companies offering products and services for the international ICT market.
