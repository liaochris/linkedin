Jeffrey Castaldo’s Summary

Jeffrey Castaldo is a Principal and managing broker at Castaldo Properties. A New England native, Jeffrey has lived, worked, and invested in San Francisco for over a decade. Prior to founding Castaldo Properties, Jeffrey was a top producing broker at Sotheby's International Realty in San Francisco. Jeffrey maintains an extensive real estate portfolio and has an invaluable perspective on market conditions and investment opportunities. He has bought, sold, and renovated his own properties in San Francisco for ten years. Jeffrey is a California Licensed Real Estate Broker and a member of the National Association of Realtors, California Association of Realtors, San Francisco Association of Realtors, The Olympic Club, San Francisco Symphony, San Francisco Opera, and San Francisco Ballet.

Jeffrey Castaldo’s Specialties:

Residential and Investment Real Estate in San Francisco.
