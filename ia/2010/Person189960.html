Simonetta Carr’s Summary

I have worked as a translator from English to Italian and Italian to English for over 20 years. I am mostly a literary translator, but I am also on the official translator list for the Italian Consulate in Los Angeles. Among other publishers, I have translated for Mondadori, Italy, Alpha&Omega publications, Italy, Hilkia Publisher, Canada.

As a writer, I have been working for several newspapers and magazines around the world. Among them, Cosmopolitan, Jordan Times, Bangkok Post, Arab-American Business Magazine, Modern Reformation, The Outlook, Leben.

I am the author of the Italian section of "How to Pronounce French, German, and Italian Wine Names: , Luminosa Publishing, San Diego.

Recently, I have been writing a series of Christian biographies for young readers, published by Reformation Heritage Books.

I have also written a historical novel on Olympia Morata, an interesting character from the Italian Reformation, scheduled to be published in 2011 by P&R.

As an Italian instructor, I teach at the Italian Cultural Center in San Diego and hold regular private classes.
