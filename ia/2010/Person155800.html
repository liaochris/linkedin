Nicole Russo’s Summary

Nicole L. Russo graduated from Monmouth University in May 2009, receiving a Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies with a concentration in Criminal Justice and Music Industry. While attending college, Nicole held many positions within her local chapter of Alpha Sigma Tau and was an active member of the Phi Alpha Delta Pre-Law Honor Society. Nicole has also completed internships with Monmouth University’s General Counsel Office, Monmouth County Public Defenders Office and Warner Music Group’s Business Development department in New York City.

Nicole is currently a Legislative Aide at NYS Senator Kemp Hannon's District Office in Garden City. She is one of the the Founding Partners of Millennial New Media Consulting (www.millennialnewmedia.com). She is also attending Vaughn College of Aeronautics & Technology for their Fast Track Air Traffic Control program.

Nicole is the President of the Garden City Young Republicans, on the Board of Directors of the Garden City Kiwanis Club, Corresponding Secretary of the Garden City Republican Club, Garden City Republican Committeewoman, Sergeant at Arms of Next Generation Politicians and a member of the Nassau County Young Republicans.
