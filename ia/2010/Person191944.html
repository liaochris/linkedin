Sophie Neary’s Summary

Lover of pink, donkeys and sailing boats...Macmillan Campaigner...likes doing things...easily bored...doesn't suffer fools...not bossy, just feisty...has thirst, will travel...do get in touch!

Proven track record in both internet start ups and global blue chip organisations - over sixteen years of internet software management, development and deployment of highly successful commercial solutions.

I'm particularly good in taking on complex projects, (especially those that may be going slightly awry), sorting them out and getting them done. Simply put, I like making things happen. I hate procrastination. I believe in the power of positivity and 'can do' attitudes!

A good plan today is better than a perfect plan tomorrow is one of my mottos.

Sophie Neary’s Specialties:

Getting stuff done, sorting stuff out. Internet strategy, marketing, product development and maximising the monetisation of web products and services.

Helping to drive through organisational and commercial change in a positive and empowering manner.
