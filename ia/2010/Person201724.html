Thea Woods’s Summary

Who is Thea? “If I were to describe myself in a few semi-big words, it would be: a believer, an encourager, a dreamer, and an achiever.” After emigrating to the U.S. from Trinidad [and Tobago], her parents taught her the value of hard work, and learned early on that with a little faith, a lot of work, and a ton of kindness, one could achieve anything. Early on, Thea adopted these principles, and developed a desire to help. “I loved knowing that I could do something that seems so simple to make such a big impact on individuals in a positive way. Through one of my older siblings (who has a “disability”), I witnessed firsthand the strength of human desire to never give up. Through her DISability, I’ve been ENabled to believe that with persistence, anyone can become WHOEVER and WHATEVER they wanted to be, as long as they didn’t give up. It may not come easily, and at times life will throw you some curveballs. In fact, at times life may seem extremely unfair. But as long as you put your faith in God, and ask Him to give you the strength to push on, you will succeed!”

So throughout the years, Thea has been making a conscious effort to “spread sunshine” wherever she goes. Whether it is by talking to a stranger on the street, by encouraging the children she has tutored, whether it’s by simply playing with and serving as a listening ear to the MANY children she has cared for throughout the years, Thea wants to make sure that every person she comes in contact knows that they are God’s unique creation, and that they matter. And if she can do anything to let them know that they can do WHATEVER they desire to do—in their heart of hearts—she will. Therefore, Thea continues (on a daily basis) to discover the path that God has set before her, and put into practice the principles she continues to learn. She has a vision of creating a company whose products and services uphold these values and opens up infinite windows of positive opportunities to individuals throughout the world.
