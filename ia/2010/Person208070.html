Trevor Turnbull’s Summary

trevor@trevorturnbull.com

As social media has started to dominate the traditional and online news headlines, I have been focused on how I can monitor these trends and figure out ways to implement social media strategies for the clients I work with. Because of this, I have found myself constantly researching the latest trends in social media as they relate to individuals, athletes, businesses and brands.

As we all know, we are in some tough economic times right now. By engaging in social media, many people can benefit the viral power of social media.....and at a much cheaper cost than traditional one-way marketing. Since most business expenses need to be measurable and provide a return on investment, I spend a lot of my time figuring out ways to monitor, measure and implement strategies to maximize return on investment (ROI) and return on engagement (ROE) from social media marketing.
