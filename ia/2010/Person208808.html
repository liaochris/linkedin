David Tucker’s Summary

Electrical engineer with 16 years of experience designing electrical systems for infrastructure projects for municipal, county, state, and federal clients.

Design experience includes power systems, power generation systems, control systems, lighting and lighting control systems, voice and data communication systems, access control systems, building and perimeter security systems, closed circuit television surveillance systems, fire alarm systems, toxic and hazardous gas detection systems, and energy and facility management systems.

Additionally, expertise includes computer based power system analysis, protective device coordination studies, arc flash analysis, cost estimating, energy audits, project administration, project planning, project management, and construction management.

As a group leader, responsibilities include the management and quality control of CDM's Chicago electrical engineering group.
