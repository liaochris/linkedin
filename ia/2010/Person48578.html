Dan Verdun’s Summary

Insurance and risk management specialist with broad range of experience including health insurance, employee benefits, property and casualty, professional liability, income protection and life insurance planning. As a property & casualty insurance and risk management specialist, I assist professional athletes and their families in meeting their personal insurance needs, while minimizing their exposure to a financial loss and property claim. Professional athletes live a life-style that provides them with the unique ability to achieve and acquire financial wealth and independence, and the more they do, the more risk they assume and the more liability they are exposed to. Professional athletes and their family insurance needs are unique and complex in nature requiring a proactive risk advisor incorporating innovation, insight and world-class service, while constantly monitoring and evaluating their life-style and family trends.
