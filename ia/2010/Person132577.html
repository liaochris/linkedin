Maria Woehr’s Summary

I am the editor of video and podcasts and social outreach coordinator for The Deal LLC.

Prior to joining The Deal, I was the assistant editor for Wealthmonitor, a Financial Times Group publication. Before that I worked at Insurance & Technology Magazine. I started developing my video editing and production skills out in Boston, MA at WHDH-TV, CNN International in London and KSVI-TV in Billings, Montana.

I also write a lot of freelance articles. My articles have been featured in online publications such as BNET; magazines such as The Deal Magazine, Go Magazine and GuidePost's Positive Thinking Magazine; and my videos for ABC/FOX in Billings, MT and The Deal LLC have appeared all over the web.
