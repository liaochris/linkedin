MY CRITICAL COMPETENCIES
A results-oriented professional with over eighteen years of visible success in working with people at all levels of the organization in the public and private sectors, I am committed to helping organizations provide a higher return on investments by consistently achieving stakeholders’ expectations for business solutions and overall effectiveness across the organization’s primary activities. To this end, I possess:
-Demonstrated ability to disaggregate and analyze problems; to probe facts; identify and question assumptions; and analyze issues from different perspectives to inform decision-making process
-Strong understanding of business analysis and process improvement (business process reengineering)
-Strong conceptual, analytical, and problem solving skills with the ability to strategize and interpret business objectives and negotiate favourable results
-Strong analytical, problem identification and solving skills
-Excellent written correspondence and presentation skills: independently and routinely solicits and considers input from others; can accurately restate opinions of others; provide appropriate level of information so accurate decisions can be made
-Strong analytical & product definition skills including an understanding of how to interpret customer business needs to allow for easy translation into operational requirements
-Abilities to provide clear and concise recommendations allowing him to easily establish a reputation of trust and to positively influence stakeholder decisions
-Excellent skills in preparing & delivering oral, written reports and presentations for all levels of the organization - executives, management, SMEs, peers, partner
-Solid Experience with business process improvement methodologies including Baldrige, EQA, ITIL and PMBOK
-Experience in planning, implementing and applying Project Portfolio Management methods. Practices and techniques to achieve timely ROI &benefits realization
