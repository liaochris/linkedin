    (Public Company; KFT; Consumer Goods industry)

    August 1997 — June 2009 (11 years 11 months)

    After a wonderful and deeply fulfilling decade, I have decided to step down from my role leading the corporate philanthropy and community engagement programs for Kraft Foods. It has been a distinct privilege to represent this great company in our efforts to help alleviate food insecurity, working with hunger organizations in the US and around the world. Through the years, our initiatives have also included arts education and domestic violence prevention; this work has also been taught me a great deal and been immensely rewarding.

    Beginning in June, I will use this "sabbatical" to pursue activities I've long desired. I'll take a few classes, write, travel and explore ideas I have for some new directions for my work. I'm very excited about the prospects, so stay in touch!
