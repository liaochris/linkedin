Sharon Simmons Long’s Summary

Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering from West Virginia University

Worked for DuPont and DuPont Dow Elastomers for 15 years in various roles within the Polymers Division – plant engineering support, information technology liaison, technical services laboratory manager, customer service manager, supply chain manager, plant superintendent, SAP project lead for order to cash. Independent IT consultant for four years specializing in order to cash business processes. AMI Doduco as a plant supervisor for three years responsible for production areas that performed melt, cast, mill, extrusion of silver alloys; mix, press, machine of silver powder alloys; machining and assembly of electrical contacts.

Consultant for Sycor Americas, Inc since 2006 with expertise in business process re-engineering / improvement, SAP implementation project management, SD-LE process design with interfaces to Foreign Trade and Warehouse Management systems.

Sharon Simmons Long’s Specialties:

SAP experience with global implementations in DuPont Dow Elastomers, DuPont, Timken & Converteam. Experience in SAP since 1997 includes project management, change management, training, sales & distribution (SD), inventory management (IM), production planning (PP), materials management (MM). Additional experience in optimization of business processes, lean six sigma applied to manufacturing and supply chain optimization.
