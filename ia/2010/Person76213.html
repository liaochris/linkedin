Vinanti Castellarin’s Summary

Passionate director, at times fundraiser producer on themes "celebrating global cultural diversity," "leadership role models," "fantasy dramas... with instructional technology knowhow for illiteracy TV programs. Latest project assignment focusing on CSR films traveling in the poorest of poor rural villages in Haryana, India. Always collaborating with young/matured filmmakers to create "global media communications ..."

Since 2005 directed and produced "JAIN ENLIGHTENMENT - A Cosmic Way of Life" with two extensive location trips to India. Focus on Ahimsa (Non-Violence), Aparigarh (Non-Possessiveness and Anekantwad (There is No Absolute Truth, but a variety of true knowledge ...). With over 100 hours of digital video, scripting/editing 5-part TV series on "Legend of Lord Bahubali;" "King Adhinathan's Golden City and the Dying Dancer;" "Lord Mahavira - last 24th Tirthankar," and "Palitana: Temples on the Hill"; "The Magnificance of Ranakpur"; Art, Scupltures and Architecture of Perfection." Completed 10 min PROMO for budget fundraising.

On Nov 2001- two months after 9/11's Attack on America, created first digital made-for-TV program: "MISTAKEN IDENTITY: Sikhs in America." Six months after distribution release, won 3 first prize awards at American Film Festivals, (thanks to inspirational team) for Best Film, Best Director and Best Producer.

Produced and directed 35mm film on Prime Minister of India Indira Gandhi (1983-84) titled "FACING THE CHALLENGE" for North America and world, focusing on biography on Nehru's daughter and India in the 80s, premiered at United Naitons and NBC network tv after tragic assassionation on 31Oct 84.

Conceived idea, extensive travel throught India for R & D as director for National Board of Canada (NFB) on "SHAKTI: She is Vital Energy" (French/English) "voicing concerns of rural women of India," screened at International Womens Years 80/85/90, worldwide and translated into over 55 languages.
