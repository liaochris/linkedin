Mikael Nerde’s Summary

A strategic leader in program growth and development with 10 years of experience launching highly successful digital content development and acquisition programs for global cellular giant Sony Ericsson. Throughout my career I have built multiple programs from the ground up by defining business plans, implementing growth strategies, and setting major milestones for organisational goals. My eye for business development ensures I never miss an opportunity to expand market penetration and build the company brand.

With a talent for communications and relationship building, I excel at working with partners and content providers to establish profitable agreements and obtain products that best serve the target market. Highlights of my achievements include:

• Nurturing the service offerings to become a major competitor for Microsoft, Sun, and IBM products.
• Launching a smartphone AppStore that outsold competitor platforms within 1 year of launch.
• Improving marketing strategies by cooperating with teams in the local market to evaluate target group needs and coordinate joint efforts.
