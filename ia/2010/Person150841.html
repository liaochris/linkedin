Norman Brooks’s Summary

Highly accomplished Chief Financial Officer with experience in transportation, service, manufacturing and distribution industries. Proven record of significant contributions in Finance, Domestic and International Taxes, Human Resources and Information Technologies. Business partner with executive management team and operations worldwide. Extensive background with SAP software, both in terms of implementation and roll out of tools to help drive productivity and sales growth, such as CRM, Sales Force Automation and APO. Savings from programs and processes I initiated totals over $100 million from last two employers. Improved month end closing process at Yaskawa from 5 days to 7 hours with 36% less staff.

In depth experience in capital management, economic value added (EVA) analysis, balanced scorecard implementation, financial planning and analysis, risk management and acquisition integration. Proven success in medium sized organizations. Experience with private equity firms (Blackstone Group and Gibbons, Green) at both Ekco Housewares and Chicago and North Western. Developed world class IT and Finance Departments at Yaskawa. CPA, with MBA in Finance and MST in Taxation.

Norman Brooks’s Specialties:

Cost savings, compensation management, business restructurings, process improvements. Excels at execution and emerging leader development. Also, experience in transfer pricing, including Advanced Pricing Agreements, multinational taxation and health care cost containment strategies.
