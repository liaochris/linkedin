Stephanie Frasco’s Summary

Stephanie Frasco represents the new age Entrepreneur. At 27 years old she has already started two successful businesses and proceeds to jump on opportunities as they come knocking. Social by nature, Stephanie has an organic approach to Social Media Marketing which continues to thrive. After launching WhatCelebsWear.com, a searchable database for fashion, shopping and celebrity style, she found the internet to be her oyster.

Through trial and error with her own websites, Stephanie knows what works in online and influencer marketing. In her career, Stephanie has been very successful in utilizing web2.0 technologies to create and implement viral social media strategies for clients in the startup, community, entertainment, technology, and fashion spaces.

Stephanie worked for Atlantic Records in the New Media Department, where she produced live webcasts for Diddy, James Blunt, and Simple Plan. In addition she created viral videos, email and SMS campaigns and created overall social media strategies for various artists on the label. Stephanie, AKA AskFrasco, is also a video advice columnist under the wing of E.Jean Carroll, of Elle Magazine.

Stephanie Frasco is currently the founder of Triple Threat Media Group, a Social Media Optimization firm that uses social networking, online word of mouth and integrated search marketing to help help brands engage online, retain customers, and continue growing.
