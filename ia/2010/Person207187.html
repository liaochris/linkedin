Peter Horsfield’s Summary

Peter brings to the table diverse software development, software architecture and software process experience across many vertical and horizontal markets.

Technical capabilities extend from enterprise software development through to real time software development for machine and systems control.

Peter has academic grounding in both computer software and hardware through a computer systems engineering baccalaureate degree, and is expected to graduate in 2010 with a Masters in Computer Science. Courses taken in this degree will solidify Peter's skills in the fields of embedded software and space systems development.

Currently, work with TopCoder (http://archives.subscribermail.com/msg/349a8c0529c941a1b0d7f53c33025f7a.htm) has allowed Peter to lead multiple projects through the complete software development lifecycle; partially overlapped, but each between 8 and 12 months in length. This has allowed Peter to rapidly refine his skills over an ever expanding range of applications and customers.

As author and custodian of the change management process for a Fortune 200 company, Peter has also substantial software change management and audit compliance (SOX) experience to offer. Perspectives on this position allow Peter a healthy respect for process, leveraging the benefits of a higher level CMMI process yet without sacrificing agility.
