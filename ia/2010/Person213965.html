Wayne Hodkinson’s Summary

Since we opened in early 2008, we have always adhered to our main objective - which was to "offer the service of a large agency, at a fraction of the cost in a professional and friendly manner". Overcoming your advertising and marketing headaches - in print and online - easily and effectively.

Services include: web design, web development, graphic design, branding, logo design and development, point-of-sale, exhibition graphic design, brochure design, leaflet design, catalogue design, packaging design, commercial vehicle graphics, magazine design and development, advertising for magazines and newspapers, advertising campaigns.

++++++++++++

About Slightly Curved Cube:
Off the beaten track and around a slight curve you’ll find us – close enough for what you’re looking for, but far, far away from cookie-cutter creative.

We provide a full-range of traditional and new media presentation solutions across all marketing platforms, from a different, fresh perspective. At Slightly Curved Cube, we see things differently on both the creative and business sides of a job.

We represent your business through a regimented process of content development, design and polish. In any medium – in both visuals and voice – we articulate a presence that commands professional respect while punching through and taking hold.

The way we see it, good marketing is about something much more important than looking smart, or looking pretty...

it’s about making the phone ring.
