Marty Barnes’s Summary

Utiliizing my 30 years of independent business ownership , I presently help individuals that want to learn about their self-employment options and determine if business ownership is right for them. As a business ownership coach/consultant with The Entrepreneur's Source, our mission is to assist people in the exploration of business ownership. My goal is to provide a safe place for aspiring entrepreneurs that want to explore business ownership and discover options that best meets their goals and needs without any type of sales pressure. The fact that The Entrepreneur's Source is independent, with no exclusive ties to any company, makes us unique and completely focused on the needs and expectations of our clients.
