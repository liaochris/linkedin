Casandra Miska’s Summary

Casandra's 2010 goal is developing her woman-owned business, EZ Slide Cabinet Hardware. She patented the eco-friendly, Slide-On cabinet handles, and is actively involved in marketing the unique product lines.

Her past experience with client satisfaction began with the real estate industry in Southern California 3 decades ago. Casandra transitioned to the mortgage lending financial services industry in the mid-1990's. Diving into Construction Lending, she made this her forte, while living in Charlottesville Virginia in 2004. Raving fans continued as she advised mortgage clients in Texas.

Clients100% satisfaction experience is Casandra's mission statement. They are pleased to recommend her services to their friends, family and acquaintances. .
