Loretta Love Huff’s Summary

Why I am here on LinkedIn

• I speak on results-oriented business communications: sales, marketing, leadership, conflict resolution, team building and team effectiveness.

• I am seeking opportunities to conduct keynotes, workshops and training sessions to corporations, association conferences and professional groups. I am particularly interested in the financial services industry (financial advisors, insurance, mortgage , and real estate agents, etc.), the technology industry (internet, applications development, telecom, database management, software, CRM) and the public sector (government/education agencies).

• I have extensive experience in business consulting and corporate HR executive management. Specialties include: business development, strategic thinking, performance management & strategic staffing/retention

• My industry experience includes: hi-tech, entertainment, consumer products, financial services.

I have a well-established network in Phoenix, the (SF) Bay area and beyond with clients across the United States and in Canada.

My company, Emerald Harvest Consulting, LLC is a two-time recipient of the prestigious Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) Supplier of the Year award. I am a professional speaker, published author, certified coach and consultant.
