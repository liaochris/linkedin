Eric Hyman’s Summary

Senior level brand and digital marketing strategist with 20+ years in major agency environment. Quick study with strong brand positioning / strategic development skills, especially in complex rapidly evolving markets, combined with tenacious sales and business development capabilities.

Began career in packaged goods account management, moved early to technology with strategic planning focus long before that was considered in vogue, spent nine years in NY prior to moving to Atlanta to join agency that, after several acquisitions, became part of JWT.

Strong aptitude for all things digital combined with the ability to solve complex marketing problems with simple, clear, motivating business building strategies and programs. Active blogger and social media strategist and industry analyst. Change agent. Believer that ROI is driven by creativity. But that creativity must ultimately answer to ROI. A futurist with realistic grounding in past and present. Expert in cycles of change based on hybrid industry models combining new and traditional thinking.
