Mark Klipsch’s Summary

Mr. Klipsch has over twenty-five years of professional experience, with a discipline in risk management and business development. Known throughout the industry for his expertise with regulatory compliance and problem solving skills, Mr. Klipsch is routinely referred by both clients and competitors. Mr. Klipsch is a graduate of North Central College in Naperville, IL with a Bachelors degree in Economics and Marketing and holds insurance producers licenses through the United States since 2004. Mr. Klipsch has direct writing appointments with Travelers, Zurich, Liberty Mutual, MetLife, Allianz, ING, Penn Mutual, MinnLife and many others A rated or better insurers, as well as access to over 100 other A rated or better insurance providers nationwide. He is an active real estate investor with holdings in Illinois, Florida, and Texas.
