Dennis Teschler’s Summary

As a National Account Executive, I have focused on placing mid and senior level executives in many functional positions within the supply chain. My core Fortune 50 and 500 clients are a cross section of durable and non-durable corporations. Third party asset and non-asset clients represent thirty five percent of my client base.

Prior to executive recruiting, I worked in middle and upper management in the hospitality and food service industry for small and multi-chain restaurants. My last twelve years were in direct sales and marketing with a Fortune 500 company developing mid-to large-scale accounts.

I look forward to forging new connections between reputable companies and industry executives. Please contact me at any time.
