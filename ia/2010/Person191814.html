Sonny Buoncervello’s Summary

EXPANDING NEW REAL ESTATE BRAND THROUGHOUT THE STATE OF FLORIDA - CONTACT ME TODAY TO LEARN MORE!

Sonny Buoncervello is an experienced Real Estate professional with over 30 years of experience helping families find the home of their dreams in some of the top communities in Central Florida, in both residential and vacation/resort/golf home communities.

Sonny recently founded Casa Latino International, an International Real Estate Brand and is presently expanding the brand throughout the State of Florida, Santo Domingo, Italy and Spain.

Casa Latino International is giving Sonny the opportunity to serve the fastest-growing segment of the U.S. population, the Hispanic Community, where he loves creating a network of offices throughout Central Florida and in select international markets.

Known as "The Mayor" of Celebration and Reunion, which are two of Central Florida's top planned residential communities, he is respected in the industry for his depth of knowledge about Real Estate development.

Sonny serves on the Kissimmee/Osceola Chamber of Commerce on the ORAC (Osceola Resort Area Council) Board of Directors, as well as Ambassador Chair for the Four Corners Area Council.

In addition, Sonny continues to serve on the Board of Director for the Central Florida Sports Commission, along with being President 2009-2010 of the Rotary Club of Lake Buena Vista, in the heart of Orlando's Disney Area.

Sonny's passion for the Boys & Girls Club of Osceola County, Florida, as well as Rotary International, give him the opportunity to give back and serve the larger community of Central Florida.
