Lopa Brunjes’s Summary

I have a very eclectic background, and use varied past experiences to distill principles from details and use those principles to inform new challenges. My background includes molecular biology, insurance, futures and options, social work, and various management positions. I have been working on midwifing the biochar industry, in various roles, for over 3 years.

I triangulate myself between the business, policy, and science of biochar to bring whole-system solutions & far-reaching vision to the biochar industry.

I work with a wide range of stakeholders, from government agencies, multi-lateral development banks, and large carbon brokers, to small-scale farmers, foresters, and backyard tech developers. I can translate complex technical concepts into lay-speak while preserving the integrity of the topic at hand.

I especially enjoy working with individuals to help steward their passions into viable business models; advising women on how to embrace the challenges and opportunities of being a woman in the business world; bringing compassion and integrity into business; and public speaking.
