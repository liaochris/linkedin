Ranier Keith’s Summary

Over the course of my Professional Experience I have managed to grow my skillsets beyond usual parameters, generating award-winning design in many different industries : Print / Graphic Design ( Newspaper, Magazine, Ads, Posters, CD Covers, etc.) Web Design (Front End Development/ CSS, XHTML, XML, etc.) Motion Graphics & Video Post-Production (Commercials, Promos, Digital Sets, Lower-Thirds, Non-Linear Editing Projects, etc.)

I can truly say that I have done it all, from a commercial artists' point of view. I have made it my priority to expose myself to every form of solution-based design I could get my hands on in an effort to become more than just a competent art director. I want to be one of the best.
