Clinton Cimring’s Summary

Thanks for stopping by. My name is Clinton Cimring. I helped found Search Engine Partner, which is quickly becoming one of the largest SEM firms in the world according to TopSEOS.com. From December 2007-February 2009 I was the senior partner.

My claim to fame is not only bringing SEM to a company that was primarily concerned with optimizing IT, but restructuring and transforming their entire business. Get Updated Internet Media (GetUpdated IM) evolved from a combination of Get Updated AB, a public mobile internet news company in Sweden and Eastpoint AB, a one-stop-shop business solutions firm and public website hosting company under Crystone, both traded on the Nordic stock exchange.

I came up with the idea of uniting the two companies under one roof founded on advertising and business solutions. If you think about why websites and, in turn, SEM, exist, they are here for one reason – advertising. No matter what kind of site one may have, that site is either intended to brand a business, generate leads, or act as a point of sale. Why not brand a business under the umbrella of “Get Updated?” Is your business still operating on 20th century, Web 1.0 principles? Well, Get Updated! The two companies united under the name, Get Updated Internet Media (GetUpdated IM) and I oversaw the acquisition of the largest international SEM firm, Just Search and one the largest SEM firms in the US, eTrafficjams.com. The idea was so successful that the two companies rejected the business solutions model and focused on Internet Media entirely.

My most recent project was restructuring the Phoresite Group. I came on board with the company in January 2008. At the time the company was called Pinnacle Edge. I asked the owner, Patrick Greene, “What is your actual vision for the company?” He responded, “[…]To be able to have the ability to see a city where a forest stands.” Not the most politically correct, but out of it, Phoresite was born.
