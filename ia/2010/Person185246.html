Sean Cavanaugh’s Summary

I got my feet wet developing all levels of retail graphic design. Working for six years at Graphics Atlanta with clients such as Safeway, OSH and Discovery Toys taught me to be deadline oriented and to handle projects effectively. Being able to breakdown all the components in a job, I focus on the process as well as the details. I learned how to balance the creative with the practical production aspects of a job.

While a student, I spent a great deal of time in leadership roles. I was successful because I understand what it takes to lead others to complete a project. At the San Francisco Art Institute I honed my skills in story boarding and project visualization, which has resulted in my ability to develop progressions of sequences, to edit /, and to visualize projects from beginning to end.

In 2007, I benefited from a 6-month internship with Leapfrog in Emeryville. There, I used proprietary software to export game assets. While offering technical assistance on art and programming over the development of two platforms, I archived everything in Excel and Word documents. At Leapfrog I worked on animation, line tooling and post-production.

My most recent job at Right Brain Electronics I handled multiple pipelines as I took 6 titles through the postproduction process. As my responsibilities advanced I found myself performing the work of assistant producer, production artist, pipeline development, concept development and prototype engineering.

Sean Cavanaugh’s Specialties:

I am comfortable with numerous programs, such as Maya, Lightwave, Flash, Photoshop, AfterEffects, Illustrator, InDesign. FinalCut Pro. But my strengths lie in my soft skills. I am very deadline focused with excellent follow through. I believe that there is no problem that can't be solved with communication and hard work.
