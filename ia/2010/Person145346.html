Mike Folie’s Summary

As a business writer I help to create speeches, articles, op-eds and letters-to-the editor for senior executives at companies large and small. For 18 years I served as principal speech writer for well-known magazine executive Steven T. Florio, beginning with his career as President and Publisher of The New Yorker Magazine through his remarkably successful tenure as President and CEO of Conde' Nast Publications, Inc. I have also written extensively for Altria, Philip Morris USA, The McGraw-Hill Companies, PepsiCo, IBM and Milliman, Inc.

As a playwright my plays have been produced Off-Broadway, regionally around the U.S. and internationally, winning several awards.

Mike Folie’s Specialties:

Corporate and Government Affairs issues management. Crisis Communications. Managing controversial issues. Communicating complicated issues in a clear, compelling manner for general audiences. Financial product marketing. Personal care product marketing. Extensive experience in the Magazine, Healthcare, Insurance and Consumer Products industries, among others.
