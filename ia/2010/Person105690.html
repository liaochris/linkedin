Josh Chasin’s Summary

I joined comScore as Chief Research Officer on May 30, 2007 after consulting for the company through my prior gig, Warp Speed Marketing, for 9 months. As a career media research professional, I have been impressed with comScore's innovative spirit and technical know-how since I became aware of the company in 2000. I'm happy to be a part of the comScore team. My colleagues here are an incredibly smart, diverse, can-do, and dedicated group of professionals.

For the prior seven years I'd been doing independent consultation through my corporate entity, Warp Speed Marketing, Inc. Warp Speed clients included Arbitron US Media, Arbitron Outdoor, Arbitron Internet Services, comScore Media Metrix, Project Apollo, Scarborough Research, the Online Publishers Association, the Traffic Audit Bureau, Simmons Market Research Bureau, Symmetrical Research, Northstar Interactive, Next Century Media, Walden Media, and Media Frameworks.

Josh Chasin’s Specialties:

Marketing, strategy, research. Great at translating math to English and finding the narrative in the data (and turning it into a compelling story.) I would say I live at the place where marketing, new ventures, and research overlap and converge.
