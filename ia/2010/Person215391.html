William Avon, LUTCF’s Summary

After completing the collegiate portion of my education at SUNY Buffalo with a degree in Business Administration and concentration in marketing I hit the streets and began developing my sales and management background at ADT Security Services. I started off cold calling and creating marketing territories, moved into developing a team of sales reps as a team leader and retention manager, then into the commercial arena.

Always having a desire to help people succeed and grow both personally and professionally I started a company called Rockin Savings, LLC which accomplished just that. As business sometimes takes time to get rolling, a friend of mine was a manager at Liberty Mutual insurance and brought me on board to utilize my talents and further assist me in accomplishing my goals of helping people with an insurance agents position. This gave me the ability to utilize my marketing company to grow my insurance business at Liberty.

After 4 years of service and earning numerous awards from Life Insurance Rookie of the Year, hitting all of my pacesetter awards and winning many regional and other national contests, I felt I could better serve clients by opening a full service agency of my own. So I left Liberty Mutual, began shutting down Rockin Savings, LLC and opened my full service insurance and benefits brokerage, William Avon Agency, LLC representing over 35 carriers. Combining my education, feet on the street selling, marketing, teaching and insurance background, I am now able to fully assist people by helping them gain a higher quality lifestyle through our income generation and asset protection programs.

William Avon, LUTCF’s Specialties:

Creating and preserving family lifestyles by assisting them with income generation programs and asset protection products. This includes setting people up with income generating activities which they can work from home on a part or full time basis as well as assisting them with their asset protection through our home, auto, life, health, mortgage protection, annuity and group benefits products. We even have a vacation benefits package which saves people thousands on worldwide travel.
