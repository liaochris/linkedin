Lisa D. Jenkins’s Summary

I provide Social Engagement, Education, Integration and Consulting.

I am not the right Voice for every business but, for a select group of clients who choose to outsource their Social Media Engagement efforts, I provide complete Social Community Management. For the balance of clients, I offer training and consulting to achieve specific, measurable goals by integrating social media into current marketing and customer relations efforts.

SOCIAL ENGAGEMENT
Using a custom strategy, I build thriving community across platforms that are most relevant to clients and the customers they wish to build relationships with. I advocate real-time daily interaction to post, reply, search, connect, and monitor the internet for customer service and reputation management. At no time do I use robotic, 3rd-party applications to post content – all interaction is live. A monthly Metrics Report shows the results of Social Engagement efforts.

SOCIAL EDUCATION
I customize presentations for organizations considering the integration of social media into current marketing efforts, as well as focused, platform specific tutorials for those expanding an established presence with additional channels; i.e. Facebook, Twitter, Flickr, Wordpress, etc.

SOCIAL INTEGRATION
I write Social Media Use Policies that address the unique needs of your organization and customer base, construct complete Social Media Action Plans to incorporate Social Media with your existing marketing efforts, and customize social platforms with branding to reflect a consistent identity to the public.

SOCIAL CONSULTING
I provide in-depth training for employees of organizations that have chosen to pursue Social Media Engagement efforts in-house. Employees gain the skills and knowledge to manage, measure and report on Social Media efforts. Consulting includes delivery of branded platforms, a Social Media Use Policy, a 3-month Social Media Action Plan, an Alert Monitoring platform and a reference resource.
