Dante Anderson’s Summary

Dante is a product development veteran of the video games industry with 20 years experience.

At Seriosity, Dante directs all production-related activities for the growing gaming company, creating applications for both the home and the workplace that increase productivity, proift and well-being through behavorial change.

At KUMA, Dante directed all production-related activities for the growing episodic game network, leading a mix of creative and technical talent to deliver high-quality video games and machinima in regular episodes. The games are primarily next-gen PC titles built on Valve's Source engine. Kuma also makes lighter Flash games and award-winning machinima. The front-line titles include original products Kuma\War and The DinoHunters plus licensed titles ShootOut! and Dogfights from the History Channel and The Kill Point from Spike TV. Animal Planet is creating a TV series based on original Kuma game IP. Kuma has built military simulators for various branches of the DoD including TRADOC and the ONR.

As a Senior Producer for GT Interactive, Dante oversaw the development of such best-selling titles as the one million plus seller Duke Nukem: A Time to Kill, 4 titles in the Mercer Meyer children's series and Nam/Napalm and WWII GI.

Dante was a member of the Saturn Launch Team at Sega of America where he produced showcase titles Panzer Dragoon and Clockwork Knight.

Dante speaks on a regular basis on game development and game/new media product integration and strategy, video game economics, and networking/pitching most recently at: Digital Alberta GDC/SXSW Prep Talk; keynote speaker at SBGames in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil; speaker at Gameon Finance in Toronto; and a mentor for the annual Interactive Project Lab at the Banff Centre in Canada.

Dante has a Bachelor of Commerce degree with a Major in Computer Science from McGill University and attended high school at University of Toronto Schools graduating from both with Honors.
