Jennifer Dring’s Summary

I have substantial experience in the public sector in the area of grant writing, economic development and legislative research. Although I briefly left the workforce to stay at home with my three children, I have established a work from home enterprise, SAHM Solutions, LLC, through which I am able to enhance both my personal and professional lives.

I currently am interested in broadening the scope of SAHM Solutions by providing grantsmanship resources, including grant research, writing and administration, for public and private entities in Central Ohio.

Grantwriting; policy research and analysis
