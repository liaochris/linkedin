Greg Fry’s Summary

I set up Careers Coach in March 2009 with the aim of assisting people with their Career Transition. I have over 10 years Recruitment experience in the Irish and US markets. Two years Business and Executive coaching.

I work with individuals, groups and business and always get the best out of people.

I hold a Diploma in Business and Life Coaching and a Marketing degree.I am fully trained in Psychometric training (Thomas International).

I am very marketing savvy and train job seekers and SMEs on how to get noticed, respected and generate business through Social Media.

I have played an active role in two great coaching initiatives this year – think2act and Confidence in Action.

I have given numerous speeches to many groups on Recruitment related issues including the recent Monster Recruitment Fair in the RDS.

I actively write and post video Career Advice online. Some of my work can be viewed at Bloggertone.com

In these challenging times I believe that in order to excel in life and business we need to think and act smarter than before.

The economy and media has knocked our confidence and engulfed many of us in fear. I believe that if we can overcome our FEAR and think and act smart we are all capable of excelling in any situation.
