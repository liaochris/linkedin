Roberto Gomez’s Summary

Roberto Gomez knowledge of multicultural cross-channel marketing, data-modeling, advertising, direct response, and new-media technologies are the skill sets that help and inspire his clients to obtain and retain their business leadership in both general and culturally influence markets, buy connecting at multiple touch points with messages that build relationships and impact decision.

As part of his responsibilities while working at communications conglomerates such as Vertis Communications, RR Donnelly and Qubecor. Roberto is providing integrated cross-channel marketing, interactive-media, and multicultural advertising counsel to leading local and international companies that include Time Warner, AT&T, Coca-Cola Company, Home Depot, Microsoft-Latin America, McCann Erickson, Euro RSCG, Barcardi USA, and European / South American Business Consulates.

At the same time Mr. Gomez is serving in several nonprofit
boards as a marketing and community advisor. For; The Boy Scouts of America, FBI-Citizen Academy, Cobb County Latino Initiative, L.I.S.T.A, The Atlanta Metro-Chamber, and currently the Executive Director of DMA-Atlanta.

Roberto was born in Havana,Cuba. Fluent in both English and Spanish. Attended The Art Institute of Atlanta (Advertising-Major)
