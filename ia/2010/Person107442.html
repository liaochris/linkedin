Dean Steinman’s Summary

JTLBMedia Inc parent of www.TheMobileXperience.com Is a leading mobile & Text message Marketing & Technology firm located right outside of NYC
We are on the cutting edge of Mobile & Text development and are actively involved in App Development, SMS call to action programs, custom SMS deployment, Text Message database development, Reverse billing on mobile projects, text sponsorships & custom mobile & text development projects.
We can work with any media or marketing company & bring a mobile component to the table. We are your "behind the scenes" mobile partner & we will support you in any way we can.
Text Blasts, Mobile Designs,WAP Sites,custom SMS platforms.From I-Phone Apps,to video on phones we can provide a turn key solution.

We also work extensively in the music industry by sponsoring events & tours through our mobile technology platforms. We also help up & coming bands promote themselves & build up a database with our www.TextTheBand.com program.

We also have developed CellularRE.com..one of the leading mobile marketing solutions for the Real Estate Industry
CellularRE has developed a wireless marketing platform for the Real Estate Industry. Thru our wireless technology a Real Estate Broker/Agent can market their properties to the approx 250 Million wireless subscribers in the US, who can view listing info and see photos anytime they wish.
This dual marketing program will allow brokers/agents & developers to market their properties to anyone who sees a CellularRE sign at that property. The public can now get listing info such as price, size, sq ft, number of BR/Bath and other listing info as well as see photos or floorplans of that property. This information will be accessible 24 hours a day.
In addition, a broker/agent can market NEW and hot listings to any of their prospective clients the moment that property comes on the market or into the office. Existing clients will be now be getting New Listing Alerts on their Cell where ever they are.
