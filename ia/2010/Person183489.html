Bill Sawalich’s Summary

I am a commercial photographer for Barlow Productions in St. Louis. I photograph people and products for corporate, advertising and editorial uses.

I am also a writer. I moonlight as the contributing editor for three photography publications--Digital Photo Pro, Outdoor Photographer and Digital Photo magazines.

On occasion I dabble in creative writing and independent filmmaking.

I'd love it if you would look for my work:
at www.barlowpro.com for a commercial photo portfolio.
at www.sawalich.com for a personal photo portfolio.
at www.barlowpro.wordpress.com, the barlow blog.
at www.digitalphotopro.com for writing about professional photography.
at www.outdoorphotographer.com for writing about the outdoors.
at www.dpmag.com for general interest photography writing.
at www.mcsweeneys.net for a short story about a squirrel.
at www.yankeepotroast.org for another short story
at www.mitusa.wordpress.com for my blog about my adventure in 2009--the year in which I didn't buy anything (literally nothing) unless it was Made In The USA.
at www.lacklusterpictures.com for information about movies I've been involved with. Trailers too.
at www.twitter.com/barlowbill for tweets that may be of interest to you if you're a photographer or a photo client.

Bill Sawalich’s Specialties:

My photographic speciality is portraiture, but I also shoot food, products, architecture and other things my clients request.
My writing speciality is photography--also, I like em dashes (and parentheticals).

As a writer I spend most of my time researching, interviewing and writing about the people, products and technical issues that relate to fine art and commercial photography.
