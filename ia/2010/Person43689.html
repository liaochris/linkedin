Erick Laubach’s Summary

I'm a seasoned entrepreneur with over 16 years of creative and new media experience. As VP, Product & Creative at Razorgator, I manage a team of 19 comprised of product managers, designers, writers, marketers, and front-end developers. The team is responsible for the entire brand, design, content, and product offerings at Razorgator.com (as well as TickCo.com, PrimeSport.com, and Noisetap.com).

Prior to Razorgator, I was the Vice President of Creative at Ticketmaster where I quadrupled the size of the team (from 6 to 25) over the course of 4 years and managed the Design, HTML, Content, and Production groups. Working as the in-house agency for Ticketmaster, my team was responsible for all online and offline assets for the brand worldwide. In addition to my role at Ticketmaster corporate, I founded and ran the company’s creative agency, Cottonblend, whose clients included AMEX, TicketWeb, Herbie Hancock, Sony Centre, Shows in Branson, and The Troubadour.

In 2008, I took Cottonblend independent and built an entirely new design and development studio from the ground up. As a creative agency, Cottonblend worked with such clients as The Hollywood Reporter, Business.com, DexKnows, and Crowdrise. The fledgling design studio also founded Cuteness.com Pet Community (http://www.cuteness.com), Driftr.com (http://www.driftr.com), and The Pixel Awards (http://www.pixelawards.com). In addition to design and development, I formulated digital strategies for these bootstrapped start-ups to help define the brands and spread them virally throughout the web using third-party sites like Twitter, Facebook, Flickr, Vimeo, and YouTube.

I currently live in Venice, California with my wife, Lisa, and our twin daughters, Ella and Hadley.
