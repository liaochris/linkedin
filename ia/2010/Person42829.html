Robert Smith’s Summary

Robert Smith is the Managing Director of Complete Hotels Services (CHS), a hotel consultancy and management services company primarily working with independent hotels in the UK.

Details of the hotel consultant and management services offered by CHS, as well as a list of current clients can be seen on the website www.completehotelsservices.co.uk.

CHS are advising a client who wishes to acquire a property with CHS providing the future management services and are interested in acquiring more management service contracts in the future.

Previously an Excutive Director of Paten Hotels, an independent group of 3 and 4 star hotels based in Colchester, Bedford, Stratford upon Avon and Portsmouth. The group was sold to BDL Hotels in a deal in excess of £40 million pounds in April 2008.

During sixteen years at Paten Hotels he held the positions of General Manager, Regional Manager and Sales & Marketing Director ( a main board position). He created the sales & marketing department in 1996 ensuring that Paten Hotels became very recognisable to all UK hotel booking agents and fully embraced the arrival of the internet quickly realising that it was an immense and affordable tool for independents to use when competing against the corporate hotel groups.

He became an active member of the HBAA partner hotels and served on the committee for 5 years and was chairman in 2006. In 2005 he took two of the Paten Hotels into membership of Best Western Hotels and susequently represented the Central West region on the BDF (Business Development Forum) of Best Western Hotels.

Previous to working for Paten Hotels he completed a trainee management scheme with Grand Metropolitan Retailing and worked as an assistant and general manager for a number of small hotel groups.
