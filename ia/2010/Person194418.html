Stephen Howard’s Summary

A resume is one of the most important personal documents you will have and so it should contain the right mix of information to promote you in the most effective and appropriate manner. Professional assets, achievements, extracts from references and a personal profile are all proven marketing tools which can help you achieve your career goals. Remember that employers need to know considerably more about you than just your working history. Employees are selected on more than just their qualifications and experience, so we give employers what they need to make a positive decision, in your favour. A resume is also very helpful in crystallizing your personal and professional qualities.
