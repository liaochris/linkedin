Steven Horovitz’s Summary

Sydney Hill Sales L.L.C., with over 30 years in the industry, represents some of the finest decorative accessory companies for the home and garden to selected markets, specifically internet retail, mail order and key national specialty retailers. Sydney Hill Sales, L.L.C., offers products that include birdfeeders and accessories; indoor and outdoor clocks; licensed clocks and accessories from John Deere to Coke to Marilyn and Elvis and beyond; George Nelson inspired furniture, clocks and lighting; european kitchenware, barware and cutlery; women's jewelry; table top hand painted jewelry boxes; infant's clothing and accessories and much more.

Let me know how Sydney Hill Sales, L.L.C. can add fun, design and profits to your programs. Sydneyhill@bellsouth.net

Steven Horovitz’s Specialties:
