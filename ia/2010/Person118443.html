    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    February 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    First point of contact for a temporary workforce numbering 50-2000. Recruiting, testing, and hiring hundreds per week, and laying off just as many in the following weeks. The Census is an incredible undertaking that at peak will employ 1.5 million temporary workers in 2010.

    I handle everything from new hire packets and researching payroll discrepancies in PeopleSoft, Oracle to providing IT support and helping coordinate the printing of thousands of maps and forms, wherever the need is greatest.     (Education Management industry)

    July 2007 — January 2008 (7 months)

  • Vocational Rehabilitation Counselor

    State of Texas (DARS)

    (Government Agency; Government Administration industry)

    December 2005 — July 2007 (1 year 8 months)

    Consulting with local businesses to enhance their diversity recruitment, EEO and affirmative action strategies. Providing Career Counseling services for blue and white collar workers with all manner of hidden and visual disability. Constructing options for employees who became ill due to disease or injury, enabling them to maintain employment, thus reducing businesses turnover. Determining job search plans for workers having to "retool" into a different industry. Evaluating, testing, screening, and putting people with disabilities into great job matches. Showing individuals how to communicate their accommodation needs, and companies how to use the ADA as a stepping stone to reach America’s last untapped candidate pool.
