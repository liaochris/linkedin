Steve Fulgham’s Summary

Steve is an experienced sales and marketing professional who has turned his passion for the environment into Ambassador Energy, a nationwide sales and training organization. Its mission is to support business owners and experienced entrepreneurs in taking advantage of the “solar revolution.” Ambassador Energy accomplishes this by enabling business owners to build or enhance their current business models through the sale and distribution of renewable energy products and services.

As CEO of Ambassador Energy, Steve and his [team] have developed a unique company roadmap designed to arm Ambassador “agencies” throughout the country with a suite of training and support programs that is unmatched in the industry.

Steve has obtained "B" & "C-46" contractor licenses as well as NABCEP certification so that he could install Solar, Wind, or other renewable energy systems (or build what they were going to be installed on). After building many homes, and installing many systems, Steve combined his corporate savvy, work ethics, and process driven background with his “on-the-job” experience in renewable energy systems and construction to have the ability to think-without-a-box in almost any situation.

Prior to founding Ambassador Energy, Steve spent six years as a senior vice president for Computer Associates (CA), the world’s leading independent IT management software company, where he formulated and executed sales strategy, and drove revenue growth by strengthening CA’s existing customer base and developing new markets for the company. He also was responsible for recruiting, hiring and training new members of the company’s enterprise sales team.

During his career, Steve also has served as Chief Technology Officer (CTO) / Vice President of Sales for Ariston Technology, a developer of USB peripheral products.

Earlier in his career, he served as Vice President of Sales for Orange Micro, a hardware and software manufacturing and Distribution Company.

    Ambassador Energy provides a consolidated platform of renewable energy training, support and closed product-agnostic distribution to our authorized agents, who are contractors and entrepreneurs migrating into the renewable energy marketplace.

    Ambassador Energy enables its nationwide network of Earth Ambassador agents to thrive. We address three critical components in the downstream marketplace.

    First, only those with a strong propensity to succeed are invited to join our agent family. We do not recognize revenue until we have fully trained our agents, providing them a jumpstart and best opportunity to succeed within their marketplaces.

    Second, we recognize that, within the accepted open distribution model, solar contractors are not comprehensively supported. In this model, there exists no consolidated mechanism allowing these contractors to succeed; from quote to close, from rebate/incentive applications to receipt of the checks, from viewing our downstream marketplace as clear and consolidated rather than obscure, confusing and fragmented.

    Third, Ambassador Energy performs the role of "mothership" as new technologies emerge. We push out to our family of agents; best practices, best prices, best products - in general best of breed in all areas which exist now and which will emerge.
