Paula Barton, PharmD JD’s Summary

With nearly nine years of leadership experience in healthcare operations, quality improvement, and patient safety, Paula is an accomplished professional with vision and expertise. Paula’s background offers a rare blend of creative and operational strengths, particularly in the areas of pharmacy operations, compliance, and process re-engineering. As a seasoned clinician with executive management experience, a unique background in healthcare informatics, clinical operations and compliance, and educational achievements that include a Doctorate in Pharmacy and a Juris Doctor with emphasis in Health Law, Paula possesses a perfect combination of skills and talents that qualify her for her career in healthcare operations and compliance. In her current role, she is responsible for implementing clinical and regulatory standards. Her focus is both on proactive standards design and on identification of opportunity for operational improvements of existing pharmacy systems. Paula has up to date clinical knowledge and skills, which are essential when leading healthcare providers at any level. Paula leads and oversees major pharmacy quality audits and risk assessments, and delivers corrective action plans for areas of deficiency or opportunities for improvement. Paula consistently supports compliance with all regulatory and accreditation requirements, has demonstrated knowledge and understanding of complex legal principles, and is well-versed in her ability to apply federal and state healthcare laws and accreditation standards to all settings of care. She has persuasive oral and written skills, strong interpersonal skills, and a proven ability to influence others across organizational and functional lines.

Paula Barton, PharmD JD’s Specialties:

Paula has earned both the Bachelor of Science and Doctor of Pharmacy degrees in pharmacy, as well as a Doctor of Jurisprudence in Health Law. She is currently completing a Green Belt in Six Sigma.
