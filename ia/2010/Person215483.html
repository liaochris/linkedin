Bill Blake’s Summary

From Allister MacRae, Executive Intrapreneur (www.executive-intrapreneur.com)...

"Beginning with The Economist in 1976, business writers have celebrated innovators who work within the organisation to affect positive change, the internal entrepreneurs or ‘intrapreneurs.’ Without a doubt, this is the best description of Bill Blake. As an intrapreneur, he consistently takes total ownership of his projects. He finds solutions to crises and problems, frequently working outside the traditional channels or bureaucracy, accomplishing results inconceivable to the institution. He shares in the risk, sacrificing compensation and time in favor of sharing equitably in the profits of the project. Executive Intrapreneur, the international organisation recognized as ‘the private network of accredited agents of corporate innovation,’ evolved from the white papers of the Cass Business School at the University of London. Membership is granted solely by invitation to executives who have a documented history with 15 years or more of leading organisational change with substantial and measurably successful results for the company(s) affected. Bill Blake is an exemplary member."

Bill Blake’s Experience

  • Business Development Strategist

    PE, VC, private wealth groups, and independent mid-caps.

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    December 1991 — Present (18 years 6 months)

    Bill Blake serves private equity firms with business development support for their portfolio companies and nationally award-winning communications. A veteran advertising & public relations executive, he combines his technical skills, ability to develop and lead innovative sales systems, and his entrepreneurial outlook to help mid-cap companies drive earnings, reorient toward growth, and overcome competitive disadvantages. By contributing this expertise to the financial & accounting expertise naturally found in private equity firms, the strategic potential is profound.

    Since 1991, Bill has directed campaigns surpassing expectations in a wide array of industries including financial services, real estate development, media, manufacturing, hospitality, restaurants, and retail. Among these projects are the successful launch of a $25M core subsidiary for Sarasota-based Levine Equity Ventures; the operational take-over of $50M in acquisitions and the formulation of national sales & marketing plans for the Scottsdale-based REIT, National Home Communities; the engineering of new investor communications and sales systems for Raleigh-based Deaton Investments; and, the completion of a stalled waterfront development for Florida’s ADB, Inc.

    Bill’s successes have earned him 3 national marketing awards, speaking engagements at 2 national industry conferences, leadership of industry associations, the representation of private investors in a highly-charged policy dispute, and a government appointment.

    In addition to his BBA, Bill holds a Masters with Honors from The London School of Economics for the study of economics, finance, political science, and international relations. He serves on The LSE Annual Fund and, with his recent return to Texas, has launched a new LSE alumni chapter for the Metroplex. He actively participates in the professional networks Executive Intrapreneur, the International Assoc. of Business Communicators, and the Business Marketing Association.
