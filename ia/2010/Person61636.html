Eda Kalkay’s Summary

Founded in 2004, Eda Kalkay Public Relations, LLC (EKPR) is a full service public relations firm providing customized publicity programs and the personal attention to build brands in the lifestyle, entertainment, publishing and fashion industries.

EKPR prides itself on delivering the highest level of service through a results-orientated, hands-on approach. The result is stylish campaigns that work in-step with clients’ visions and today’s emerging trends. EKPR promotes both individuals and companies, heightening nationwide awareness of their products, services and projects in an ever-changing media.

Eda Kalkay’s Specialties:

Top-tier media placement, product launches, media tours, special events, fund raisers, personal publicity.
