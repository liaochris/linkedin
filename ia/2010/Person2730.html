Adam Stachelek’s Summary

Adam has over 8 years of experience in both the architecture and delivery of creative technical solutions to business problems related to online and media applications. His focus has been primarily in the areas of online marketing tools, enterprise search, social networking, ecommerce, and audio/video functionality.
