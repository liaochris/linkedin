Joanna M. Pérez Doble’s Summary

Joanna M. Pérez Doble began as an intern student in Grupo Guayacán, Inc. on April 2006. Since then she has play a key role in the coordination and execution of its two largest initiatives, EnterPRize Business Plan Competition, and the Puerto Rico Venture Forum, an annual conference where world class speakers share their experience and knowledge with the local entrepreneurial community, among other administrative duties. Recently, she was appointed Assistant Manager, and as part of her responsibility she has to assist the Senior Manager in the execution of all marketing, communication and operational plans.

She also assists in the execution of the Guayacan Center, Grupo Guayacan’s third initiative that promotes and develops a business environment where world class entrepreneurship in Puerto Rico can robustly grow.

Joanna assists in the development and continuance of university relations, oversee the media plan execution and advertisement deployment, implement new strategies for the web page, and assist in the coordination and execution of EnterPRize workshops and other Grupo Guayacán’s events.

Her next goal is to assist in the development of new content and delivery of presentations to students interested in an entrepreneurship career.

Joanna is about to complete her bachelor degree in Advertising and Public Relations from the University of Sacred Heart. After graduating, she expects to pursue a MBA. She is a Price-Babson SEE fellow.
