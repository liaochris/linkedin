Peter Davison’s Summary

Peter is driven by his passion for helping people work together for the greater good. His tales and tools enrich relationships, deepen employee engagement and promote productive attitudes to maximize the culture of human potential. Peter is a former school teacher and Nova Scotia Government public servant who engages life with gusto, from his proud roots in small town Nova Scotia to trekking mountains on 3 continents. His international audiences are diverse—from street gangs in Los Angeles and community leaders in Northern Ireland.

Peter was diagnosed with “early onset” Parkinson's Disease at age 45 in 2005. Peter has been quoted in Oprah Magazine, has a weakness for chocolate and is proud to be baby Hannah's Dad. .

Peter Davison’s Specialties:

Peter’s uplifting speeches and peak performance seminars celebrate hard-working unsung heroes and revitalize big-hearted human services professionals. His primary audiences are professionals who dedicate their time and talents to help others succeed.
