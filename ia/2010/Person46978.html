Daniel Barran’s Summary

My roles, of increasing authority, have given me the opportunity to lead diverse groups to achieve:

- Sales success in multinational Tier 1 operators including: Vodafone, America Movil, and Telefonica
- Operational excellence for demanding operators such as: Verizon, AT&T and Nextel
- Replacement of competitor networks from Motorola, Lucent and Siemens

I have developed an approach to the market that blends business acumen, technical breadth and strong customer skills. My perception of the unwritten and oft unspoken requirements of a customer combined with the ability to understand what is happening throughout the company and the industry as a whole help to establish strong customer bonds.

My global experience spans 45 countries with extensive knowledge of North America, Europe, Latin America, and the Caribbean
