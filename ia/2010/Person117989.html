Kent Mollenkamp’s Summary

Mr. Mollenkamp's professional career spans more than 27 years in management consulting and alternative asset management and includes the founding of Peacock Capital Advisors, an investment capital firm that offers dynamic global allocation portfolios to high net worth individuals and institutions seeking absolute return solutions.

Mr. Mollenkamp graduated from Taylor University in Indiana with a B.A. in Business/ Economics. He also studied international economics abroad in the Netherlands with Wheaton College . He is a CPA, Series 3, Series 7 & 66 licensed and is also active in hedge fund and social media forums.

Kent Mollenkamp’s Specialties:

In-depth knowledge of global financial markets and especially alternative investments. Proven experience in developing/ executing innovative trading approaches and dynamic option strategies. Active portfolio management & trading experience in equity indexes, currencies, futures, and options. Forwarding thinking in the adoption of applied social media by self-directed investors to connect with global thought leaders and smart money ideas. Entrepreneurial, organized, creative, and resourceful.
