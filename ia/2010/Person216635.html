Bill Snyder’s Summary

A digital veteran, I’m as adept at building and leading teams as I am working independently when there’s no room for error and no team to support me. My work as General Manager of We-Care.com is focused on management and product development, but my communications roots run deep and content chops remain sharp. Having experience in nearly all areas of interactive, I know how to bridge the gaps that often exist between CXOs, business analysts, creatives, developers, and users. I have:

• The vision and skills to build cross-functional teams and lead product development.
• The breadth of experience to meet communications, UX, SEO, and branding needs with a comprehensive strategy.
• In-depth knowledge of interactive trends and emerging technologies, including social media.
• Proficiency in messaging, writing, editing, content strategy, and strategic communications.

We-Care.com provides free technologies to nonprofits allowing them to receive donations whenever theirs supporters shop and (soon) search online. We also offer free corporate-philanthropy solutions that turn a percentage of what a business and its employees spend online — be it business or personal spending, depending on your policy — into donations to the business’s chosen charity. It’s corporate philanthropy without the spending. Interested in learning more? Let me know.
