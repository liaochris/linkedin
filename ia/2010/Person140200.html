Maria Aranda’s Summary

Meghen Group is the leading global expert in contact centre recruitment. With over 10 years experience of recruiting roles for some of the largest global leaders, throughout Europe, we understand the needs of the markets in which we operate in.

We currently work with some of the largest international companies throughout the globe, and understand the importance of customer service, core beliefs, values, and high levels of service provided to both clients and candidates. We understand the importance of recruiting the right candidate, as they will ultimately become a part of your company, and we always focus on this factor during our selection process.

We are more than just a service provider – we endeavor to work in partnership with our clients, and focus our strategy for their company based on their current needs. Our aim is to streamline the recruitment process, making it easier, quicker and a most effective way to recruit for roles.


Finding the right role for the candidate is also a major factor in our recruitment process. We will assess your future ambitions, hopes, skill set and previous experiences, when selecting the right company and roles for you. Changing employment is a big step in everyone’s life, and we can assist with finding you the right job, in a simple, quick and easy process.
