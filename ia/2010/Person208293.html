Trisha Okubo’s Summary

Entrepreneurial product manager skilled at conceptualizing market-driven product features, rigorous business analysis, evangelizing ideas, and guiding products from conception to creation. My strengths include building customer-centric product experiences and the ability to solve problems in a way that marries the analytical with the creative.

• E-commerce experience spans work on both the buyer experience and seller experience, auctions and fixed price, and paid and free listing models.

• Online Advertising experience encompasses work with display and text advertising, using CPC and CPM models.

• Social Media experience includes the creation of a popular fashion website, recently named a Top 50 site by Time Magazine.

I'm an experienced panelist and speaker, with speaking engagements at Web 2.0 Expo and Stanford University. I'm also a sought out shopping and style expert, interviewed by national publications from the Wall Street Journal to Real Simple Magazine.
