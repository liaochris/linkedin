Joseph Schwartz’s Summary

A seasoned software executive with a strong track record of leadership and innovation in enterprise software product development, management and strategic marketing, specializing in complex enterprise solutions, system design and implementation, product positioning and go-to-market strategy.

A senior technology leader and strategist who is versatile enough to engage with business and technology executives, understand and articulate business and technology strategy, develop and deliver strategies, assessments and recommendations at the C-Level.

Proven team builder and leader, communicator, presenter, public speaker and author of numerous technical and executive-level strategy documents, assessments, technology and business roadmaps, white papers and solution collateral.

Helped pioneer several billion dollar software markets with launches of successful, category defining products as a product executive at innovative software companies including Informatica, IONA Technologies and Level 8 Systems.

Executive experience in Tier 1 venture capital backed startups, public and privately funded companies. Large-scale IT operations and development leadership experience.

Has led and delivered complex IT initiatives, programs and projects for Fortune companies as a high-level consultant, program manager and IT executive.

Deep experience and expertise at solving complex, data intensive business and technology problems at the process, technology and organization levels.

Strategic business expertise: Strategy and planning, complex decision making, competitive analysis, pricing analysis and modeling, product packaging and pricing, channels and alliance development and management, complex program management, collaboration and workflow automation, value messaging and selling, new product launch and lifecycle management, innovative demand generation program design, targeted account marketing.
