Toomas Särev’s Summary

Toomas is a hard-working and motivated Consultant Cardiologist with great clinical skills and extensive experience. His main interest is in interventional cardiology. He is excellent in team-work. He has experience of modernizing a Coronary Cath-Lab at Danderyd Hospital, the lab showed excellent results regarding low restenosis and mortality even in complex cases. The Lab is active in utilisation of FFR measurement and IVUS.
Toomas is a good keynote speaker, he likes to give lectures about cardiac physiology, interventions and intensive care.

Toomas Särev’s Specialties:

Advanced Percutaneous Coronary Interventions, Primary PCI, Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS), Rotablation, Invasive Coronary Physiology (FFR), Haemodynamic Assessment, Right Heart Catheterisations, Myocardial Biopsies, Implantation of Pacemakers and ICDs. Transthoracic and Transesophageal Echocardiography, Noninvasive Cardiological Assessment, Cardiac Intensive Care, Interventional Training
