Denise Taylor’s Summary

I'm incredibly passionate about encouraging others to find the gift in everything. As a motivational speaker, I am able to encourage, support, and celebrate the gift of every day with my audiences; sharing with them how I've learned to embrace life, even in the most difficult circumstances.

My daughter, Jonnae's life began with me teaching her and ended with her teaching me. There's a saying, "Learning is a gift, even when pain is the teacher." We received amazing, beautiful gifts in the not so attractive package of leukemia. Jonnae didn't live like she was dying, she died teaching me how to live. As a motivational speaker, it is my desire to teach others, through our story of hope, courage, and tenacious spirit, how to live life fully.
