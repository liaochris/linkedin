Rahul Choudaha, PhD (www.DrEducation.com)’s Summary

Dr. Rahul Choudaha is an international higher education specialist and editor of blog on higher education trends -- www.DrEducation.com. He specializes in institution building and strategic planning, international market development and collaborations, recruitment & marketing, student mobility and education policy. Currently, he is Associate Director of Development & Innovation at the World Education Services, New York. Earlier, he led international recruitment and marketing at the ISB, Hyderabad.

Dr. Choudaha earned his Ph.D. in Higher Education from the University of Denver, US and holds an MBA for NITIE, Mumbai and B.Engg. from Jabalpur. He is a frequent presenter at professional conferences including AIEA, NAFSA, AACRAO, EAIE and NAGAP. He is also a recipient of the NAGAP Research Grant 2008, and the Outstanding Doctoral Student Award, DU.
