Jennifer Lin’s Summary

Jennifer Lin’s experience is in the following two areas:
- Business Valuation
- International work experience

As a financial analyst at Quist Valuation, Jennifer has valued securities for many privately held companies backed by VC firms such as Kleiner Perkins, Sequoia Capital, and Trident Capital. Previously, working for PricewaterhouseCoopers, she audited the financial statements of companies in preparation for an M&A transaction or IPO. Without exception, she completed all her assignments on time.

Jennifer has international work experience in the U.S. and China. She is a CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) level III candidate, and holds a Master’s degree in Financial Analysis from the University of San Francisco with a GPA of 3.84 out of 4.0 and a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Nanjing University, receiving merit-based scholarships all throughout her graduate and undergraduate education. She speaks English and Chinese (Mandarin).

Jennifer Lin’s Specialties:

Private Equity Valuation, Financial Statement Analysis, Investment Banking, Advanced Excel Modeling for Equity Valuation and Corporate Finance, Monte Carlo Simulation, Bloomberg, Morningstar, Capital IQ
