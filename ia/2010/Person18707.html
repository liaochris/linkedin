Jim Bernardi’s Summary

Jim Bernardi is a 3rd Generation Automotive Dealer, His grandfather founded the Bernardi Auto Group in Framingham Mass. The company has grown to be one of the highest integrity based franchises on the East Coast. His family sold the Auto Group in 1986. Jim has been in the Auto Business over 30 years, he carries the family tradition on as being a highly motivated, ethical & professional businessman. Jim has held such positions as; Dealer, General Manager, Director of Operations, District Operations Manager, Parts & Service Director, Service Director, Service Manager & Service Advisor.


Jim is also Publisher/CEO of Automotive Dealers Network, a Global Automotive e-Newsletter that reaches professionals Nationally & Internationally. ADN was formed to serve the Automotive Franchise Dealers, Managers, Suppliers, Employees. ADN has the leading Automotive Experts Guest Writing in every Operational aspect of Variable & Fixed Operations.
