Susan Hartzler’s Summary

Recognized as a leader in the public relations field, Susan Hartzler formed her own company in early 2000 to brand her unique style of relationship marketing. Building on more than 28 years of industry expertise, she has the knowledge, contacts, and enthusiasm that make every public relations campaign a success.

Alpha Dog Public Relations is dedicated to promoting top-of-mind awareness of its clients, based on an extensive network of media and co-promotional relationships. Hartzler is the pet product and service expert and the travel and spa specialist that the media turns to when researching a story on those topics. Her background includes representing such prestigious clients as the State of South Australia; Cousteau Fiji Islands Resort in Fiji; the City of Carmel, California; The Old Course Hotel in St. Andrews, Scotland; and Post Ranch Inn in Big Sur, California. She has also worked in the health care industry providing services for Cedars Sinai Medical Center and for the entertainment industry where she represented everything from the America Ballet Theatre to Marvel Comics television shows. Hartzler has the hands-on experience in the many facets of public relations and inside knowledge of pitching a story that gets results.
