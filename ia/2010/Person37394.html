Chris Beeby’s Summary

I am a recruitment professional with 11 years Technical Recruitment experience and I possess extensive knowledge of the Building Services Engineering market in the UK and Internationally.

After completing my degree, and working for a number of years with two larger recruitment firms I founded Beeby Anderson Recruitment early in 2006, a privately owned, independent recruitment agency and business specialising in temporary and permanent placements within the Technical & Engineering sector, predominantly Building Services.

We are in a position to offer a truly unique level of tailored service and genuinely believe that the key to our success lies in our ability to develop strong long term relationships with our clients and candidates.

We adopt an honest, transparent, and flexible approach to recruitment, and we are professional and committed at all times.

Since we are now well established in Building Services we are currently expanding into other markets including Civil & Structural Engineering, Rail, Architecture, Project Management, Commercial & Quantity Surveying, Process, Power, Aerospace, Defence and Oil & Gas.
