Robert Haywood’s Summary

Completed Chemical Engineering BS degree from University of Delaware 1995. During school years, managed an R&D program for composite materials. In addition to working to fund college expenses, launched a restaurant food-delivery business...a great early sales & marketing experience.

Joined GRACE Dearborn as a technical sales rep, calling on refineries and other heavy industry in the Philadelphia region. Local team out-performed corporate growth objectives threefold. The company was bought and reformed as BetzDearborn where I continued to participate in strong growth results while facilitating the merger of two local sales organizations.

Joined W.L. Gore & Associates in October '97 as regional product specialist for the GORE gasket business. Key projects included product differentiation, flanking brand strategy development, distributor programs, and several operational improvement initiatives. In 2000, my scope extended to global P&L responsibilities for GORE gaskets.

Completed MBA with Marketing concentration from the University of Delaware 2002, receiving class honors for strategic marketing.

Focused in recent years at Gore on team development, enhanced marketing communications, and product commercialization initiatives.

Managed a personal portfolio of real estate investments to include year-round and vacation properties since 1997. And take great interest in website, product, and media development initiatives in my spare time.

Ongoing, I hope to continue leveraging my expertise & passion in marketing in both my role at Gore as well as separate entrepreneurial ventures.
