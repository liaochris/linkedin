Frederick Gray’s Summary

Frederick is a founding partner in (the original) CyberSystems, involved in multimedia communications. As a creative programmer and performer he developed “the visible communicator” series of Internet videos to support training and marketing goals of businesses and government agencies. He is also a commercial pilot and FCC-licensed radiotelephone operator. Frederick and Jane Beresford operate the firm, and its offshoot, The E-Broadcast Center.”
He designed DVDs, and Internet-based training modules for the United States Coast Guard Engineering Logistics Center, Baltimore MD. Past clients include the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory (direct), United States Peace Corps, U.S. Park Service, FedEx, Whiting-Turner Contracting.
