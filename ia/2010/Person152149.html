Nathan McDonald’s Summary

Co-founder of the social media agency We Are Social, developing and implementing strategies for brands to listen, understand and engage in conversations in social media. Advising clients on community strategy, how to identify influencers and build ongoing relationships with them, increasing advocacy and positive word of mouth, and on the wider business impact of social media.

Also interested in exploring different cultures and landscapes, often via their cuisine or through a camera lens, and sometimes on a snowboard or yacht. Travels tend to include long lazy lunches and even longer dinners at restaurants with eccentric owners and local specialities. Equally enthusiastic about creating amazing eating experiences in the kitchen.
