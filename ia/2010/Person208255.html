Olivia McClellan’s Summary

After almost 19 years of experience as a Real Estate professional include acquisitions, sales, foreclosures, development, and management, in 2007 I along with my husband and partner opened Triple8 Associates, Inc.

Our real estate brokerage and property management firm that combines my years of experience and expertise in real estate and my husband's 18 plus years of technology and Internet marketing expertise, to create a different kind of real estate brokerage in direct response to today’s technology and Internet savvy consumer. As a team, we offer personalized customer service while transforming the real estate transaction into a streamlined technology based process, creating efficiencies saving the consumer time and money.

I hold both a Broker's license in Nevada and active salesperson's license in California, as well as a B.A. in Marketing and Communications. I am an active member of the Greater Las Vegas Realtors® Association, currently serving my fourth year on the Association's Education Committee and my first year on the GLVAR Grievance Committee in 2008. Additionally I have been selected as a member of the class of 2008 NVAR Leadership program sponsored by the Nevada State REALTORS® Association and currently serve on the Board for the local Las Vegas Chapter of CRS as the Chair of Membership.

I have effectively worked with bank owned properties, selling, marketing, and purchasing since 1997. Our network of Triple8 websites targets vertical markets, specifically providing exposure for REO properties in the Las Vegas area. Additionally, our marketing includes signage with call capture audio tours, showcased listings on Realtor.com and virtual tours.

My diversified experience in the real estate industry allows me to bring top notch expertise, integrity, and vision to anyone looking to buy or sell real estate.

I truly do treat every transaction as if it were my own, with passion and the knowledge that real estate promotes personal wealth.
