Ted Souder’s Summary

I am a senior, big picture and wildly creative business leader with over 14 years of media experience. I have worked with all types of industries and with companies ranging from local restaurants to start-ups to those in the Fortune 100. For the past 4+ years I have run Google’s Travel vertical in the Central third of the U.S. where my team manages all aspects of the Google experience for some of the largest Travel brands in the world.

Ted Souder’s Specialties:

Digital media, social media, developing, managing and successfully growing large sales and account management organizations, integrated marketing, brand management, creative strategy creation and execution, evangelism, motivational speaking.
