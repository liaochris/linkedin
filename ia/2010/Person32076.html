Gigi Bozzano’s Summary

Founder and Principal of CareerWalk Consulting Group, a national career management strategy and HR consulting company that specializes in career development, job search, transition and executive coaching/counseling, workshops and the development of performance improvement strategies for both individuals and organizations.

A popular speaker and published author on leadership, career management strategy and transition, I have a 20-year plus successful track record in career management/strategy consulting, outplacement, senior corporate management, executive search and magazine publishing having worked for such global companies as Primedia Intertec, Abbott Laboratories, Advanstar, Cygnus Media and Drake Beam Morin. Bachelor of Arts, summa cum laude, Spanish Language and Literature, Master of Arts, Spanish Language and Linguistics. Have started work on Ph.D in Psychology.

Most recently, founder of CW-Network (Chicago Women Network), a 3,500-organization for professional women whose purpose is making connections that support, empower and promote women's interests in every area of their lives. To join please go to www.cw-network.org.
