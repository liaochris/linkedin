Pino Gargiulo’s Summary

The Exclusive in Italy is created only for the privileged few, conceived as a hallmark of excellence. This exquisite collection of “award-winning” properties features the highest-rated villas, chalet, historical castles, luxurious and charming hotels, and welcoming country houses in the most idyllic spots, including six essential elements: Mediterranean sea, lush countryside, delicious wines, cultural and artistic heritage, and Italian history.
Our collection of properties not only offers unique design and ambiance which embraces the local surroundings, but also an unparalleled commitment to the finest experience on every possible level.
Luxurious accommodation can be accompanied upon request by personalised and friendly service, excellent spas, lively entertainment for that unforgettable event or a special holiday and a host of activities, as well as culinary adventures from some of the most accomplished chefs.

Discover a country through the eyes of the people who live there and are passionate about it.
Join us and delight in experiencing the most cherished traditions of our Italian peninsula.
Exclusive in Italy can offer you a personal guide who will lead you in discovering a country rich in magnificent beauty of nature and art, a person whose expertise extends to the history, customs, tastes and fashion, who will take you beyond the beaten path of commercial tourism and make of your stay in Italy truly unforgettable.

Step out of the commonplace and into Exclusive in Italy.
