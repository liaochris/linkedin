Joe Bartling’s Summary

Currently I am leading a team at KPMG in helping our Medicare plan sponsor clients with Part C and Part D data validation and preparing for CMS-required 2011 mandatory independent data validation audits on 2010 data.

I am a computer forensic technology and data analytics consultant in Washington, DC. My team and I help companies identify, preserve, process, and produce electronic evidence (ESI: Electronically Stored Information), including emails, instant messages, personal computers, servers, and transaction-based systems such as large-scale financial accounting systems or online transaction processing systems.

For nearly 20 years, I have been helping litigating attorneys, compliance officers, and in-house counsel in the public and commercial sectors in managing data and electronic documents involved in litigation, dispute, or regulatory response.
