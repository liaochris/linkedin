Felipe Busquets’s Summary

Character animator with broad professional experience in game development.

In addition to game development and 3D short films using 3D characters, I have also worked in feature film as well as directing real image short films using actors.

I regularly work both keyframing and editing motion capture. I am competent and comfortable with both techniques, having produced numerous projects using these methods and often a combination of the two.
