Christophe Van Bael’s Summary

I help contact center managers and customer experience managers with the definition and execution of customer-centric strategies and action plans that:

- focus on customer relations rather than transactions;
- stimulate customer involvement in customer service, marketing and innovation;
- embrace self service yet still allow human interactions;
- help organizations listen to, engage with, and learn from their customers.

Together with my clients, I aim to continuously:

- improve their quality of service;
- improve customer loyalty;
- reduce operational costs ...

… one interaction at a time.
