Carma Spence’s Summary

Carma Spence is a writer, author, speaker, strategy and implementation coach, and marketing consultant. She has more than 20 years marketing communications, public relations, editorial, writing and publications management experience, and has won awards for her editing skills (substantive editing). She loves helping creative professionals develop information products and nurture a thriving online presence that supports their business and career goals.

She is skilled in making complex topics easy-to-understand for lay audiences, and has spent most of her career in science communications. A geek at heart (with an active inner child), she loves biology, astronomy, paleontology, anthropology and archeology. At one time, she wanted to study cetacean (dolphins, whales, etc.) communication, but found that focusing on only one science was too confining.

Carma is very creative, too. She can't leave recipes alone ... tweaking them as she goes, modifying them to either suit her taste or just to see what happens. She writes short stories, poetry and has a few novels in various states of completion.

Collaborative by nature, she loves a good joint venture that creates a win for everyone involved.

Public Speaking
