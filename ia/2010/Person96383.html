Jerome Schweich’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE - Mediation, Law Practice, and Consulting

A broad and unique range of experience and training qualifies Jerome as an able mediator - trials, mediations, and transactions, business, advocacy, and entrepreneurship. He knows how to focus and facilitate. He perseveres, and does not panic. Jerome has the temperament and skill necessary to help get serious disputes promptly resolved.

* Dispute resolutions through mediation
* Early career focus on advocacy in litigation: extensive trial experience
* Drafted and negotiated hundreds of development, licensing, distribution, and joint venture agreements for technology companies
* Counsel to many clients and companies on licensing, protection, and exploitation of intellectual property
* Business formations (domestic and foreign)
* Advice on purchase and merger transactions, private placements
* Represented exceptional start-up and emerging technology companies as general counsel

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT EXPERIENCE - Founder / CEO of WebCorp

* WebCorp developed a hardware-independent, peer-to-peer network browser/operating system (WEB ®) for personal computers using telecommunications protocols for seamless integration across platforms
* Organized corporation, prepared business plans
* Secured intellectual property rights
* Raised capital, recruited talent, managed product development and launch
* Closed major licensing and distribution deals
* Technology sold to Microsoft Corporation
