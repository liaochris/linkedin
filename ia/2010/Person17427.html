Ashley Winters’s Summary

I recently received my Master in Business Administration, with a concentration in finance, from Auburn University in December 2008. While pursuing my MBA, I spent a large portion of my time working as a graduate research assistant in the Finance Department in the Lowder School of Business.

After graduating, I spent the months of December through February as the Temporary Executive Assistant to the Institute Chair for the Healthcare Financial Management Association Region V Dixie Conference which was held in mid-February. Presently, I work as the Marketing Assistant to the Anchor Insurance Agency where I am responsible for the research and analysis of new market opportunities as well as the development of marketing pieces for print, internet and television.

I feel that my educational background, combined with my diversified work experience, has been excellent preparation for a successful career within the Financial Sector.
