Karen Farrington’s Summary

Matura Farrington Staffing Services matches employers with employees to form successful work relationships. We succeed in our mission by providing excellent service to our clients and candidates, consistently exceeding expectations, and setting and maintaining the highest standards and ethics demonstrated in the employment services industry.

We have been working under the “specialist approach” to recruiting since 1994. Our recruiters are the experts in their fields because each one of them focuses on a few specific positions within an industry. As specialist, they are the “Who’s Who” in their niche; therefore, attracting the best talent in the marketplace. Their depth of expertise qualifies them to provide true consultative recruiting for specific candidate pools and trends within an industry. The result is a measurable increase in our clients’ ability to compete for the best talent or the best job.

Our purpose is to create a valuable recruiting experience for our clients and candidates. Our recruiters are trained to listen to both parties specific needs, priorities, and goals, then make an honest evaluation to determine the right match between clients and candidates.

We are dedicated to being the industry leader and thrive on recognition from our clients for successful matches – the primary reason clients and candidates have been with us for over ten years.

Professional certification helps a staffing firm avoid legal missteps that could result in court action, and protects the rights of candidates, employees and customers. CSP is a distinguished credential that demonstrates a staffing firm’s commitment to its customers, high level of professionalism, and dedicated efforts to comply with co-employment obligations and responsibilities.
