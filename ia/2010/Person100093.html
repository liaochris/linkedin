Joann Whetstine’s Summary

With over 15 years of experience in corporate marketing and direct marketing agency experience, Joann Whetstine, Social Media Marketing Consultant and founder of HowDoIMarketMyBiz.com can help you get more customers with online marketing. With a background in Direct Marketing, Joann knows how to create the right message for your company to get the results you need. With a mix of social marketing, online search marketing and standard PR and email retention campaigns, Joann will tailor a marketing plan that suits your business needs and customer acquisition goals.

Online marketing is not the way of the future, it is here now. Traditional direct mail and print ads are expensive and are not reaching your audience the way it used to. To keep current and help your customer find you in today’s world, all businesses must have a presence in the social spaces (blogging, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, etc…) Social marketing is an exciting and dynamic space. Networking online gives you more than just new customers, it also gives your business worldwide visibility, as the internet has no borders. Your business will connect with people from around the world and with the new contacts you will make, the possibilities are endless. Connect with other writers and bloggers to get even more exposure about your product and service. The more places you can get your content online, the better your chance of coming up in natural search results, allowing potential customers to find you.
