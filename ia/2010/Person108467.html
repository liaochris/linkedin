Julie Lemberger’s Summary

As a dance photographer I am working on creating an archive of dance pictures documenting dance history at the turn of the centuries (late 20th to early to mid 21st century). As weddings and events photographer I bring my expertise from dance to this field seeing the story, the movement, the intensity and the spirit between people.

Julie Lemberger’s Specialties:

Photography for press, documentation and historical relevance with artistry and care for print and web medias.
