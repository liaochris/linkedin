Kristi Jackson, MBA, Real Estate Broker’s Summary

While most Realtors will tell you of their credentials, awards, or other client’s transactions, many do not explain how these accolades will benefit you in the current Houston real estate market. I do have these credentials, including a Bachelors in Computer Science, a Masters of Business Administration, over 10 years of sales experience, 5 years of banking and finance experience. I have also experienced and worked through several cycles in Houston’s real estate market as a trusted consultant, liaison, negotiator, property manager and Realtor, servicing buyers, sellers, and businesses.



When it comes to buying and selling real estate in the Houston market, a combination of this experience benefits you. A mastery level education in business, finance, and marketing, helps me to develop a creative marketing plan tailored to your individual home. Finance and business experience enables me to educate my clients of the multiple financing options available to them, and also explain the past, present and future real estate market conditions. My technical background ensures that the latest technology will be used in your transaction, which saves you

time, provides major exposure, while avoiding you inconveniences. Having received training in sales and negotiations benefits the buyers in obtaining a superior deal to fit their needs and a seller in maximizing their home presentation and sales price.



My goal is to not just tell you my credentials, but provide benefits driven service that is a perfect compliment to your real estate transaction. For your real estate needs, contact me.
