Galen Massey’s Summary

I have been involved in real estate investment in the Charlotte NC market for more than 18 years. I have been involved in every facet of investment that I can think of.

My experience in investing includes:
Rehabbing and Building new
Buying, selling, wholesaling, and shorting houses, foreclosures, deed positions, judgements, notes, lots, and apartment complexes.
Trading houses and lots.
Listing and selling houses and lots.
Evictions and Vagrant clearing.
Tax foreclosure initiation and negotiation.
Skip Tracing and Broker Price Opinions for lenders.
Point man and Liaison work for out-of-state investors.

I have owned and sold over 200 houses, 2 apartment complexes, and 20 lots.

My favorite story is locating the owner of an abandoned house in county lockup and getting him to sign a deed
by having a guard deliver it and witness signing behind a glass safety screen.

Galen Massey’s Specialties:

I am a licensed NC General Contractor.
I am a licensed NC Real Estate Broker.
I consider myself an, in the ditches, get it done, grunt, real estate investor.
