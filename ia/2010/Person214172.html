Mihai Anghel’s Summary

I have a long track record of dealing with statistics, data analysis, and project management. As a Certified Web Analyst, I intrinsically know every element and aspect of SiteCatalyst, Discover, Coremetrics, HBX, Report Builder and KPI dashboarding/reporting. Click-path web traffic data, regardless of vendor or collection methodology is descriptive at best - it isn’t accounting software. Thusly, in order to gain insight into campaign performance and site-use/interaction it is imperative to apply cultural analysis and qualitative data in addition to web analytics data. In the course of user profiling, I have created grading tools and prospect models using Bayesian statistics and Markov chain Monte Carlo methods in sampling probability distributions.

My Master’s Thesis focused on the coalescence of quantitative and qualitative data as part of the formation process of self-perception through the individual’s habitation mode. Analysis was performed using ESRI’s ArcGIS in concert with NVivo (qualitative analysis software) on data I collected. My research experience & anthropological education are an excellent compliment to my technical skills. Six years of clinical-trials research experience has made me very attentive to study protocol, fact-finding and object/subject relational organization. This is highlighted by my experience as Project Manager for the Antarctic Space Analog Program - Polar T3 Prevention. Lastly, I would like to note that a major portion of my academic education has been focused on Geographic Information Systems, Statistical Analysis, and Data Interpretation.
