Eric Smartt, PHR, CIR’s Summary

Senior Recruiting Professional with over 10 years of technical and non-technical recruiting experience in both the Defense and Commercial sectors.

Mr. Smartt has 12 years of professional experience, beginning his career in outside sales. He is currently working as a Project Manager with Personified, a leading business intelligence consulting firm focused on talent in Chicago.

He holds a CIR designation (Certified Internet Recruiter) through AIRS. He also has a PHR certification (Professional in Human Resources) through HRCI (Human Resource Certification Institute) and is certified by DDI as a Targeted Selection Administrator. He holds a Bachelors degree in Psychology from The University of Chicago.
