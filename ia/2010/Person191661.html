John Bleh’s Summary

I'm passionate about the outdoors and marketing. I have an MBA with a concentration in marketing and have fished, hunted, and spent time in the outdoors my entire life. This is where I'm coming from:

I work in a number of different areas within the outdoor community. After working for Orvis, a leader in the flyfishing, wingshooting, and outdoor lifestyle world in a variety of marketing roles, I joined Trout Unlimited as their Director of Marketing. There I utilized my direct marketing and corporate experience to create some very successful membership recruitment, fund-raising campaigns and industry partnerships. While developing relationships within the outdoor and conservation community, I was lured away to join an ambitious project to create THE web portal for the hunting and fishing communities. Alas, in 2000, the NASDAQ crashed and venture capital funding dried up.

All this is to explain how my business came together. With contacts in the fishing and hunting world and the conservation arena, it was easy for me to see the need for professional business skills. Like most sports enthusiasts, fishermen, hunters, skiers, hikers, etc., are often drawn to the business as a lifestyle choice. Yet that doesn't lessen the need to run that business successfully. Today's competitive marketplace demands sophisticated marketing strategies and tactics. My professional training as a marketer lets me help business owners and managers reach their goals.

I have over 20 years experience in marketing, direct marketing, public relations and internet marketing. I've worked with Orvis, Trout Unlimited, Elktrout Lodge, the IGFA, The Henry's Fork Foundation, The Angling Report, High Lonesome Ranch and The Adirondack Mountain Club to name a few. My company provides customized marketing strategies and solutions to the outdoor industry, and detailed membership recruitment, retention and lifetime value enhancement to non-profit associations and organizations.
