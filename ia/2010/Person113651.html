Kelley Robertson’s Summary

Author of two books, "Stop, Ask & Listen" and "The Secrets of Power Selling"

A warm and engaging sales trainer/keynote speaker who offers practical ideas and insights to help sales professional improve their results.

Writer & publisher of a weekly newsletter, "59 Seconds to Sales Success". Issued by email every Monday morning, it offers one piece of practical sales and motivation advice that can be applied immediately.

Author of approximately 100 sales related articles.

For info on Kelley's sales training programs, keynote presentations, or sales training products, visit http://www.Fearless-Selling. ca or email him at Kelley@Fearless-Selling.ca

Common misspellings of my name include: Kelly Robertson, Kellie Robertson, Kelley Robinson, Kelly Robertson, and even Kevin or Terry Robertson

Kelley Robertson’s Specialties:

My specialty is helping people close more sales in any type of economy. My programs focus on selling skills, sales strategies, and sales management.
