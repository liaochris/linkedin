John R. Luxford’s Experience

  • Sr. Project Scientist

    Malcolm Pirnie

    (Privately Held; Environmental Services industry)

    June 2005 — Present (5 years 3 months)

    Mr. Luxford is an environmental consultant with 25 years of experience in the environmental health and safety arena specializing in the business development for, and the project management of, a wide range of environmental issues including: environmental audits and assessments, regulatory compliance, health and safety programs, site remediation, and asbestos and lead concerns. He has expertise in the development and implementation of corporate standards of practice for such diverse topics as: air quality sampling programs, industrial hygiene monitoring, site characterization, risk assessment, site safety, emergency response, and auditing operations. As a state-certified (PA and NJ) environmental health and safety trainer, Mr. Luxford has also developed client-specific training programs and has served as a lecturer at Temple University and the University City Science Center in Philadelphia, as well as the New Jersey Department of Health. A published author, he has lectured at national technical conferences and various universities on the subjects of asbestos, lead, environmental health and safety, and regulatory compliance. His qualifications and expertise in providing business development coupled with hands-on project management are evidenced by a client history that ranges from Fortune 500 companies, industrial, chemical, and manufacturing operations to real estate development and management firms, and governmental agencies..
