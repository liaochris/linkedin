Derek Gordon’s Summary

Derek Gordon is a results-oriented marketing leader with more than 20 years experience serving extraordinary organizations. His areas of expertise include brand strategy; thought leadership and content strategy; performance-oriented demand generation; social media and marketing; search marketing; and public relations. He is a proven leader, respected advisor and excellent communicator. Derek is a regular columnist for MediaPost.
