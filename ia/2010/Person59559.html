David Ramsey’s Summary

Dr. Dave Ramsey graduated Cum Laude from Palmer College of Chiropractic in1977. Started practice in San Jose, CA where he specialized in personal injury, workers comp, pre-natal and pediatric care, and sports injuries. He was a key note speaker for several health fairs in the Bay Area, was a guest on many radio shows, and had his own cable TV show on natural health. He was one of 20 doctors of different specialties throughout the country involved as a clinical investigator in the FDA clinical trials of the new technology: Low-Level Therapeutic Laser. During this time he served on the Scientific Advisory Board of LaserMedics, Texas. Dr Ramsey was featured on many Bay Area new shows, in Forbes magazine, and CNN for his work treating Carpal Tunnel Syndrome and sports injuries of the San Francisco 49ers. Dr. Ramsey was a faculty member of Palmer College of Chiropractic for two years before moving to the Central Coast. He now practices on sight at Kennedy Club Fitness in Paso Robles where he specializes in joint pains, tendonitis, sports injuries, and arthritis. Dr. Ramsey is also introducing a new Functional Medicine practice to the community, specializing in nutritional therapies for the treatment of stress and hormone-related conditions.
