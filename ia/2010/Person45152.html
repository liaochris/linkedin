Cam Turnbull’s Summary

Cam is the President of a small website development firm specializing in strategic marketing and e-business consulting.

His 12 years of experience in business development, marketing, sales, client services and consulting is concentrated in fast paced firms in the high-tech, marketing services and healthcare industries.

He is always interested in hearing about new career opportunities, start-ups, partnerships, web-based businesses and innovative marketing campaigns.


Career highlights include:
- Led a sales team of six and boosted company sales from $800k to $2.2m within 12 months.

- Set a sales record that was 136.5% of the previous record and 456% of my sales quota and the President's Club recipient.

- Consulted with senior management of two firms, successfully refocusing their corporate strategy and brand which resulted in nearly doubling sales levels within one year.

- Executed marketing strategies and sales channels that expanded two firms into EMEA & APAC.

- Founded a website design and eBusiness consulting firm that currently employs seven contractors.
