Bill Schneider [LION] [TopLinked]’s Summary

I work with business owners who want to increase their sales numbers and profits. I focus on 5 key areas in Business:
Lead Generation, Conversion Rate, Number of Transactions, Average $ sale, and Profit Margin.

Purchasing Professional: Buying Steel Coil for the Tubing market was the real key to success in the Mechanical steel business. The Relationships that counted most were at the executive level with the owners of the various steel companies. "Buy the raw materials right and your end product is half sold!" - this became a mantra with me, knowing that my margins were entirely dependent on how well I matched the selling market with incoming raw material.
Three critical lessons I learned Quick -
1 - Negotiating never stops. (You will need More Raw Material Next month!)
2 - Focus on being aware of what the other side needs.
3 - Know and ask the key questions at the right time.

ISO 9000 program implemented in the steel industry in 1999 and certified to ISO 9002 standards. Recertified the year after. This program added $2 million to the value of the company which sold a year later to profitably retire the owners. By educating all the workers to the new Quality standards, the new buyer was confident that the business had sticking power.

With 20 years experience in ownership in the steel market, and 5 more years of entrepreneurial leadership in the small business arena (Graphic Arts), my major focus is growing businesses in sales and profits while solving the day to day challenges and having fun.
