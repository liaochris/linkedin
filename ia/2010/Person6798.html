Alex Miller’s Summary

I have 15+ years of software development experience, working across the software and product lifecycle. The majority of that time has been spent working in Java, although I have programmed professionally in several lanuagues (C++, C, Pascal, Python, Perl). I have a strong theoretical computer science background, with focuses in computational complexity and artificial intelligence. However, my real love is in building software for people to use. I am a developer at heart although I have performed in many roles over the years. I am an active and constant learner and always interested in learning new technologies.
