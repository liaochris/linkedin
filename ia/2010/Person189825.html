Simeon Williams’s Summary

Simeon read PPE at Alcuin College, York University.He has worked with a number of UK Senior Cabinet Ministers on a range of international issues, in particularly covering the Gleneagles Summit in 2005 on climate change. As well as assisting various SP500 companies on global matters at DLA Piper Global Government Relations. Simeon has organized several advisory trips throughout Europe and China on business and political matters and has worked on various renewable energy cases with the Competitor Intelligence Team at BP. He developed his understanding of the complex nature of social innovation and enterprise through interning through several influential think tanks most notable, the Globalization Institute and Demos where he worked on the “Atlas of Ideas “project an 18-month strategic study emphasizing the importance of science and innovation in emerging markets such as India, South Korea and China. He has also worked for a number of management consultants such as Maresfield Curnow where he specialized in reviewing strategic policy initiatives for clients looking to conduct business in China. He was a Finalist in the UK vs China Entrepreneurship Competition for the think-tank Suchen which organizes TED-expeditional trips in China on matters of sustainability. He was also ranked 7th in the Global Student Entrepreneur Awards for the UK for Cradles a carbon trading venture in China and is also a Advisor of the private members club OneCircle and The Royal Mile Club . He is also a columnist for the PPE Current Affairs Journal VOX circulated throughout Ivy League and Russell Group Universities. Simeon has an ever-increasing interest in corporate finance and business management and has interned at a number of hedge funds; and other firms such as ING, Deutsche Bank focusing on complex derivatives and financial instruments for clients investing in Asia.
