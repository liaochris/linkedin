Diane Overgard’s Summary

I help people add more joy to their lives!

Through life coaching, speaking, and writing I inspire people to understand themselves, find direction, and develop habits that bring clarity, energy, and satisfaction to both business and personal life.

I am divorced and a single mother of three "almost grown" children. My divorce, while the most painful experience of my life, brought me new skills, expertise, and confidence to guide people during and after divorce. My experiences solidified an overwhelming desire to coach women and men, both single and married, to love themselves and their life right now!

As a Certified Family Life Educator and author of two books, "Let's Talk Parenting" and "Let's Talk Teens," I have 25 years experience teaching about parent-teen relationships, parenting styles, child development, and family dynamics. Busy parents hire me to guide them as they find practical ways to stay connected with their children and spouse, and enjoy family life more!

My clients learn how to live with gratitude, focus, and peace that cannot be shaken by troubling circumstances - divorce, parenting challenges, or other trauma - that life sometimes brings.
