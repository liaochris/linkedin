Cory Rosenfield’s Summary

Founder of Rosenfield Enterprises Inc. a management consulting firm working in the fields of social media, marketing, finance and property management/development.

Rosenfield Enterprises co-owns and operates Silver Platter Inc. which is parent company of InfiniteSM. Specializing in social media integration, InfiniteSM provides customized solutions for companies looking to refine or establish their online presence and scale it to the new digital marketing standard. SPI has clients in industries ranging from entertainment, media, retail, and more.

Eruption Productions / Neesh Marketing:
A SPI property, EP and Neesh Marketing are student hiring, and marketing companies respectively. Fueled by the entertainment industry, the company has built a strong promotional structure throughout the Southwestern Ontario University campuses and represents numerous clients with student targeted branding, marketing and hiring campaigns.

Rosenfield Enterprises Inc. provides property management and development consulting services for CRM Developments Inc., Carlo Holdings, and a number of personal/individual clients.

Other Rosenfield Enterprises Inc. clients include: Fuse'n Productions, Fidelity IT Solutons and www.theworkstationworkout.com
