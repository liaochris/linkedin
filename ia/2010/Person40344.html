Chuck Sacco’s Summary

Chuck is a technology entrepreneur, with a number of company and product launches under his belt.

His most recent transaction was the sale of PhindMe Mobile, which he co-founded and led, to Movitas LLC. PhindMe was Chuck’s second startup, previously launching an enterprise technology consulting business. He grew the consulting and client development operations of that self-funded startup before the business was sold to what is now Infor.

Chuck's career includes a track-record of launching products and growing businesses in the fields of enterprise software, business process management, and mobile applications.

Chuck also led SoftBrands (now Infor) Evolution division in the Americas where he successfully launched new products including an innovative Business Process Management solution for mid-sized businesses.

Chuck’s has specific expertise in selling and implementing technology platforms with all size companies, helping them improve their bottom line through the effective use of technology. He also has specific experience in helping to setup international partner programs in South America and Asia.

With an MBA in technology management from Drexel University and a Beta Gamma Sigma honoree, Chuck has authored articles and speeches on mobile marketing and business process management.

Chuck also sits on the board of Mobile Monday Mid-Atlantic and sits on the Mobile Marketing Assoc.'s Commerce and Academic Outreach committees.
