As a self-starter, he is efficacious and works well either independently or building and leading a Winning Sales Team founded on “coach-like” leadership traits.

Here is Tom's story . . .

Tom has resolved business issues for 1/4 of the Fortune 500, as well as for hundreds of Midsize Organizations across a wide band of market sectors. His concentration is on Customer Relationship Management, Business Intellingence, Analytics, OnDemand-Cloud Computing, and specializing in Client retention and acquisition.

As a Complex, End-to-End Sales Executive, Tom has personally provided over $162 million in Solutions and as a Sales Manager, his direct Sales Teams have responded to over $550 million in Business Solutions to our Clients, many of which continue to work with Tom and his Team presently.

Tom is boldly recognized for his sales and leadership achievements on numerous occasions by Crains Chicago Business, articles in the Chicago Tribune, a multi-page intreview in a National Business Magazine, and awards from the U.S. SBA. He was invited and accepted a speaking engagement by the acclaimed computer industry organization, "Comdex", to present on "Building an Award Winning Sales Team."

Tom's Oracle, Siebel, SalesForce.com, and Peoplesoft BI-CRM-EPM Solutions encompass Sales, Marketing, Online Marketing, Support, Self Service, Help Desk, 360 View, Field Service, Order Capture, Financials, and Business Intelligence/Analytics.
