Tim Vickery’s Summary

Tim Vickery has spent his 30 year career serving retail and commercial businesses. He brings an amazing ability to plan, develop and execute effective, cost-efficient marketing solutions that result in increased sales and improved brand awareness.

Tim oversees the process whereby Ivie & Associates uncovers and fosters valued Business Partnerships.

Tim has served as the Vice President of Ivie & Associates for over 14 years. He is currently overseeing the development and implementation of several products including:
- Call center management software
- On-line proofing print production material
- On-line ordering & fulfillment of printed & related materials
- Procurement of advertising specialty products
- Procurement of ecologically friendly shopping bags
- "Company stores" for branded promotional products
