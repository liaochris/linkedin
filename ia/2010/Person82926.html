Howard Pressman’s Summary

Current specialties include the definition and creation of products, bringing them to market successfully, and providing direct and channel sales the tools to demonstrate value to a wide range of customers. The key is understanding not only the intersection of technology and the marketplace, but understanding what technology can be adapted to fill a need. In many cases, the customer has specifically stated the need. But the real exciting success come from determining the intersection and application of technology that the customer didn't even realize they needed until they see it in the product portfolio. Then the customer want takes over, and the true definition of market-based pricing comes into play.

My goal is to work in a team environment to create a product and solution that the entire company and its customers can be proud of. Success is not only measured in revenue and profits, but also in the hearts of the company's employees.

I have experience in several industries all with the common theme of a technology-focused product, intelligent buying customers, and the need for continuous innovation.
