Marty Agius’s Summary

Having been in the industry for over 25 years, my clients see me as a problem solver first and foremost. My goal is not only to solve a challenge, but to provide measurable results and help my clients meet or exceed their stated objectives

I have worked successfully with clients in Insurance, Education, Financial services, Membership Organizations, and most recently the Energy Industry. I enjoy this sector as it previously was not a competitive marketplace, and there is much to be discovered. In this age of overloaded grids, calls for renewable energy and more need for consumers to practice energy efficiency, there is a strong demand for understanding the utility's customers attitudes. We can fulfill that need and have been delivering outstanding results to our clients marketing campaigns for the last ten years.
