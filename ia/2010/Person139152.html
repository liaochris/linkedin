Tom McCabe’s Summary

A Certified Corporate Travel Executive (CCTE), a designation earned through the National Business Travel Association at the Cornell Universtity School of Hospitality, my specialties include implementation of managed travel programs in North America, Europe and the Asia Pacific regions.

Additionally, I have planned numerous senior level executive meetings and conferences at some of the finest resorts and business conference facilities in the world.

Having retired from PerkinElmer (April 2008), I am now an independent meeting planner operating as a licensee of HelmsBriscoe, the largest procurer of meeting space and related services in the world. That significant buying leverage and my HelmsBriscoe global network of 1,000+ associates located in 40+ countries is the advantage of the clients for whom I source and assist with site selection for their executive meetings and conferences. This includes negotiation and contract preparation providing risk mitigation and saving clients both time and money. Fee is paid by contracting property - no fee is paid by my client and our leveage ensures that rates are always competitve.

Interested? I would be pleased to discuss further.
