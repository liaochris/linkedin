Perry Goldschein’s Summary

Award-winning marketer, consultant, sustainability thought-leader and lawyer, with proven leadership and team building skills, who also rolls up his sleeves to accomplish hands-on what’s necessary to meet goals. Socially responsible business entrepreneur, with background in sustainability issues, corporate social responsibility (CSR), telecommunications, technology, law, publishing, CPG, and nonprofits.

Specialize in helping brands on the path of sustainability and CSR to communicate more effectively to achieve goals. Clients have included Ben & Jerry's, Green Mountain Coffee, Johnson & Johnson, National Geographic, and Yale University, among others.
