Carri Bugbee’s Summary

I’m the proprietor of Big Deal PR (www.bigdealpr.com), a virtual agency that manages campaigns and projects in digital marketing, PR 2.0, community management, advertising, and special events (real or virtual). I have 20+ years in traditional public relations and advertising, but in recent years I’ve incorporated tools and tactics such as Twitter, Facebook, community building, SEM, blogging and YouTube to help businesses build better customer relationships and boost sales.

I've spoken about social media methodologies at numerous conferences, including South by Southwest Interactive, the Cool Twitter Conference (Seattle keynote, Chicago and Los Angeles), The 140 Characters Conference, O’Reilly Twitter Bootcamp. OnHollywood, WebVisions, Innotech, Communicopia, OIABC Annual Awards Meeting, Love at First Website, and events for BusinessWire and the American Marketing Association.

Fall 2009, I started teaching a class in social media marketing at Portland State University, which is part of the Digital Marketing Strategies program. I’ve also provided social media training for many businesses and organizations such as Portland Metro Government.

I received a Shorty Award in the advertising category in February 2009 for being an outstanding content producer on Twitter while ghost-tweeting as @PeggyOlson, a character from the AMC hit drama "Mad Men." I’ve also been named a finalist in the Sammy Awards’ best twitter branding category and the SoMe Awards “wildcard” category.

Currently, I lead Social Media Club Portland. Summer 2008, I helped produce and market CreateCamp (Cre8Camp.org), which was the first ever Bar Camp for creatives, along with the newly resuscitated Portland Creative Conference (Cre8Con.com). If you attended those events or are interested in future events, join our groups for those organizations here on LinkedIn, which I manage.

Carri Bugbee’s Specialties:

Big Deal PR creates strategic marketing campaigns using social media, PR 2.0, advertising and SEM for businesses and organizations in hi-tech, entertainment, health/fitness, retail, education, travel/hospitality, pet products, electrical technologies and construction. Big Deal PR also partners with other creative firms to provide PR for them or their clients. These creative companies have included ad agencies, animation studios, graphic design firms, film/broadcast companies and Web developers.
