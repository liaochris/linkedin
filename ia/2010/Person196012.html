  • Territory Manager

    TaxBreak

    (Privately Held; Executive Office industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    TaxBreak is a business that makes your business better and more profitable. And we do so with no risk to our clients.

    Virtually any business can benefit from TaxBreak's services -- and do so without having to change how you conduct your business. On average, 15-20 percent of the people a business hires qualify for various federal, state, regional and local tax credits. Yet most businesses are unaware of these credits or feel they are too cumbersome to deal with so they fail to take advantage of what they qualify for. Understand, the business gets the credit, not the individual.

    TaxBreak helps a business locate and process these credits. You already hire people, why not get the Credit You deserve? TaxBreak is the runaway leader in the tax credit processing industry -- in large part because that is all we do!

    Our proprietary methods and techniques enable us to find credits our competitors frequently miss. We get you all the tax credits you deserve, not just the easy ones! And we do so on a contingent-fee basis, so you only pay when we deliver for you. As you might imagine, that gives us a strong incentive to work on your behalf!

    Once we find you the appropriate credits, you are then able to deduct the amount of the credit from your income tax return. That is better than a tax deduction, as the credit is a dollar-for-dollar reduction from your company's income tax liability!
