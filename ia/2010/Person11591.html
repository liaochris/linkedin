Andrew Brynjulson’s Summary

Andrew Brynjulson is a blossoming young graphic designer with degrees in Art (graphic design) and Communication/Business (marketing, public relations) from Augustana College. His background and experience have allowed him to approach design from a strategic, conceptual angle as well as artistic, asthetic one.

Andrew is a for-hire freelancer under the guise of Brenni Fresh Creative, an outlet through which he can excercise his talents in design, strategic thinking and social media.

Life goal of a young person: To become an established designer, thought leader and resource in the creative realm. Eventually make obscene amounts of money only to give it all away. Unfortunately philanthropy is less effective when you are a starving artist.

Andrew Brynjulson’s Specialties:

Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Word, Excel, Powerpoint, CorrelDraw, PhotoGrav, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Flickr, Wordpress, Flavors.me. I am in the process of learning Dreamweaver and Flash, so just cool your jets already.
