    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    eblizz is a groundbreaking interactive online platform that enables Web sites to instantly and effectively communicate, market and monetize dynamic multimedia content anywhere on the Web.
    With more speed, power and stickiness than any share widget or platform out there, eblizz enables companies to disperse and monetize their online content through drag & drop technology onto the social networks of their users.
    eblizz utilizes Microsoft’s Silverlight technology, which enables drag & drop import/export and mash-up of multiple forms of content (text, images, animations, video, and music).
    eblizz utilizes Microsoft’s Azure cloud operating system, which enables companies to offer their users dynamic and customizable Web experiences without clogging their servers and PCs with immense download packaging, and eliminating the need for up-front technology purchases.
    eblizz introduces the “Social Space,” which enables a Web site’s users’ social-network friends’ pages to appear on the site. Users can then drag & drop content from the site directly onto their friends’ social network pages.
    eblizz introduces the “ShoeBox,” an online tool that allows a Web site’s users to grab content through drag & drop technology and store or edit it on their own social-network pages for later distribution to their social-network friends.
    eblizz enables Web sites to grow their audience and sell their products by transforming their users into brand ambassadors who recommend and spread the word to their social networks.
    eblizz integrates existing e-commerce tools, creating a powerful system for user-driven product recommendations, promotion and syndication.
