John Bell’s Summary

NeuroTraq (NTQ) is currently on hold while BillTraq is being prepared for launch. NTQ has a unique concept for creating well defined cardiovascular and neurological biomakers from the complex analysis of multiple biosignal parameters. We are literally one year and two full time software developers from commercial prototypes that can significantly impact cardiovascular and neurological diagnosis and treatment. We now need an experienced medical device executive to take over NTQ and launch it.
