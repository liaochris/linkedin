Richard Tidman’s Summary

As a skilled practitioner in graphic design and brand communication, I have a wealth of experience in the provision and management of creative services combined with a solid understanding of the industry.

I enjoy the challenge of working to maximise the opportunities presented by every brief and have a passion and drive that remains undiminished after 17 years in the business.

With time spent in design and integrated marketing agencies in the UK and Australia, I have had the opportunity to work with a wide range of clients in a number of sectors, including Sony, Cadbury, Diageo, Skype, Nivea, De Beers, Adidas, Virgin Trains, Virgin Holidays, Fidelity, EMAP and Levis, delivering brand and campaign communications, both online and offline.

Richard Tidman’s Specialties:

Brand strategy & identity, printed and interactive communications.
