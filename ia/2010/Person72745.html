Garret Acott’s Summary

I am a true Open Source software advocate, believing that licensing models instead of patents are the best way to ensure a proper competitive market in the software industry. Over the years I have specialized in FOSS Business Development, ROI in software deployment and the development and management of both large and small development and engineering teams.

I have a strong understanding of the FOSS licensing models and how they apply to real world open source and commercial software. I strive to help companies understand the Pros and Cons of the open source world, and how they can leverage those to their benefit.

I have a very strong understanding of virtualization technologies and of how they play in the data center, the corporate environment, and storage. I have experience with both commercial and open source virtualization solutions. I also have a strong background in technology and software architecture, and in ensuring that applications and systems are designed and developed with future improvements and needs in mind.

I have a strong and diverse management background, ranging from management of geographically disperse software teams of more than 50 developers, to a centrally located team of hardware testing engineers.

Garret Acott’s Specialties:

Product development, Open source software, FOSS business development, Linux development, ROI in software deployment, team management, on-line community development.
