Phil Megenhardt’s Summary

I got my start in the apparel merchandising industry, and soon found that my talents were easily transferable to community marketing and events – making a difference in Puget Sound neighborhoods that culminate in celebration.

During my stint as marketing coordinator for the Pike Place Market Foundation, I produced Pigs on Parade, along with many marketing strategies to promote Seattle’s most vibrant and tourist driven neighborhood.

I am actively involved in my community by serving on the Communications Board for Seeds of Compassion which welcomed His Holiness, the Dalai Lama, speaking for the annual Washington Events and Festival Conference and sitting on the Fremont Chamber of Commerce Board of Directors.
