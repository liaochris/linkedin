Paul DeMoret’s Summary

As business owners, we face many of the same issues: limited floor space for filing cabinets and misfiled/misplaced documents. Our Scan123 system has proven to be an efficient, easy-to-use, cost-effective solution for your industry, and all its mountains of paper.

By converting your files to digital images (PDF), and by allowing Scan123 to house them on our secure servers, you will allow simultaneous access to that information, 24 hours a day, by whatever personnel you give authorization to. No more copiers, courier, or fax machines needed!

Scan123 assists your company in complying with Federal and State regulations, such as Sarbanes-Oxley, HIPAA, GLB, etc.
