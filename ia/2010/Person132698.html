Marie Pijanowski’s Summary

Marie Pijanowski is the founder of Center for Social Connections, a trainer and a dynamic Connector who helps individuals and companies “bridge the gap” in creating connections and relationships. She is known as the “Queen of Connections” and talented creator of strategic alliances.

Marie advocates healthy relationships with self and others, which creates a quality lifestyle that becomes a catalyst for creating networks where the networks and company environments that work more like communities where employees and guests who come to special events are able to interact in conducive and meaningful ways. One of her mantras borrowed from Anthony Robbins is: "The quality of your life is the quality of your relationships.”

Marie specializes in hosting unique events and workshops where individuals can make meaningful connections in creative environments. She also assists businesses, groups and corporations with training and development and will custom design special events that are memorable and business building.

Marie’s professional history includes twenty-two years as a high level manager in the retail services industries; during that time she was an entrepreneur/co-owner for twelve years and established a successful business through her strategic relationship building strategies. Consequently, she won the designation of one of the Top Ten Businesses in Amarillo, TX award.

Marie attributes the many certifications and awards that she has earned through her ability to form meaningful relationships with others that is based within a framework of reciprocity that allowed her to support others as they in turn supported her in achieving her goals. She has committed herself to providing quality interactive events and training and coaching in the area of relationship building for individuals and companies through her business the Center for Social Connections.
