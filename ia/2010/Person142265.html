Michael Crum’s Summary

Summit Design Remodeling is a full service design/build remodeling firm based in Fairfax, Virginia. Summit Design Remodeling's only goal is to meet our clients' expectations by providing exceptional quality, value and service to each and every client project, no matter the size or scope of the project.

Summit Design Remodeling has built it's reputation on the premise that a staff dedicated to providing ‘knock-your-socks-off’ customer service, on time completions, strict budget controls, excellent craftsmanship, and creative design is the way . . . the only way to our Northern Virginia clients' heart.

When you invite Summit Design Remodeling into your home, we take that invitation very seriously and treat your home as if it were our own. These are lofty words from a remodeler, but our clients' trust is a hallmark of Summit Design Remodeling. As remodelers we're aware of how difficult it can be to trust someone to come into your home and make sometimes-drastic changes. Our team focuses on making your remodeling project a pleasant experience, not a stressful one.
