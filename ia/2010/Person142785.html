Michael J. Carrasco’s Summary

Michael is a diplomatic, problem solving business operations’ professional with over 10 years of progressive experience. Michael is someone who can jump between diverse industries and assignments with ease. He joined Stroz Friedberg in 2003, gaining broad cross-functional experience in the finance, marketing and operations divisions, and currently plays a pivotal role in keeping all aspects of a professional services practice connected, overseeing daily operational management, HR & IT functions, facilities management, logistics and security.

In his civic leadership roles, Michael has been a voice for those with autism and developmental disabilities at the local and State level. Over his 10 years of experience being at advocate, Michael has gained broad cross-functional experience in government relations/legislative advocacy, issue development, fund-raising, strategic planning and problem solving. Michael has first hand experience dealing with issues involving developmental disabilities, autism, special education and mental health services.

Born and raised in El Paso, Texas, Michael earned a bachelor’s degree in political science from the University of Texas at El Paso.
