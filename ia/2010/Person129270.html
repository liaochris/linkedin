Lyn Marsteller’s Summary

My invention has been through the rigors of refining the original paper bag pattern by a soft goods engineer to make it ready for manufacturing and through the scrutiny of the USA Patent and Trademark office, receiving a utility patent in 2004 and a trademark in 2005. Made in the USA, the Purse Perfector® can transform to fit a long lean profile, like a brief case, travel tote or diaper bag, zip apart for a small purse or bag or zip together to place in most structured or unstructured bags.
