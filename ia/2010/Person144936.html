Mikael Thiel’s Experience

  • Executive Account Director

    MRM Starsky

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2003 — Present (6 years 9 months)

    Mikael has been Account Director with MRM Starsky (MRM Worldwide Stockholm) since the intrinsic start in 2003, recruited as the second person in the company. In 2003 Mikael was one of the key figures in winning ‘TeliaSonera’, which during 2004 came to become MRM Starsky’s largest client.

    In the end of 2004 Mikael was an integral part in the second large win for MRM Starsky – the Nordic assignment as CRM and DM partner for ‘Saab Automobile’. This win made MRM Starsky one of the leading relationship and digital marketing agencies in the Nordic region, which became evident when the agency was awarded the Agency of the Year award in 2005.

    Mikael has been a member of MRM Starsky’s management team since 2005, supporting MD Carl Rustner in the evolution and growth of the company from 3 people to today’s 60. During the last couple of years Mikael has developed his role from being an Account Director, through the role of Chief of Operations to the agency’s Executive Account Director, responsible for the agency’s total revenue and a team of 10 Account Directors and 2 Planners. This new role involves Mikael in many of MRM Starsky’s strategic decisions and, during 2008, a key figure in creating an integrated unit of MRM Worldwide and acquired agency Starsky, including the development of a new integrated digital offer.

    In March of 2009 Mikael and his team won the assignment for the insurance company ‘If’, becoming the Nordic lead agency for DM, CRM and Digital Marketing, in competition with Digitas and three other agencies. As a result of this Mikael has spent much of his time establishing working processes and setting up the Nordic McCann operation for the client. And alongside with the role as Nordic Client Service Director for If, Mikael continues to manage the same role for the Nordic client GM.
