Davinder Singh’s Summary

Davinder is passionate about Innovation and uses it to drive profitable business growth. He is an expert on launching new products and leveraging them for commercial growth of the business. Davinder has a consulting practice which works with clients to enable ‘Commercialising Innovation Ideas’.

Prior to starting his own consulting practice, he was leading ‘New Product Commercialisation’ as Director with Coca-Cola India. The company had many successful launches (Minute Maid Pulpy Orange, Fanta Apple, Fridge pack) under his leadership. A long term strategy and a multi-year pipeline of Innovation was put into place with the potential to increase the GP by as much as 40M USD by year 2010.

He has held senior management roles with global companies (The Coca-Cola Co. and Foster’s International) and has experience of working for family run business (Jagatjit Ind. Ltd., DUFIL, Nigeria). His career roles span Front Line Sales, Franchise Sales Leadership, Brand Marketing(global & regional brands), Channel Development, Customer Marketing and Commercialisation of New Products.

Davinder has worked in many emerging markets in Indian sub continent, Central Asia and West Africa.

He likes to spend his free time by going on nature holidays with his family, playing golf, tackling Sudoku. In 2009 he has taken up jazz dancing.

He is regularly invited by leading B schools(MDI, FORE, IILM, Amity and IIPM) in Delhi region for interaction with their students
