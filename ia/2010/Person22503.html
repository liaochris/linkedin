Bert Hagendoorn’s Summary

For Adobe Benelux I work as Marketing Project Manager, Creative Pro and Web, since April 2007. At the marketing department, I do the contacts with the creative and interactive industry (designers, agencies, awardshows, other organisations) building on the network I have. I do projectmanagement and sponsoring of creative events, publications and support the different Adobe communities. When I started at Adobe I joined the new board of the Dutch Adobe User Group as chairman - a community that came from the formal Macromedia User Group. This has now been transformed to an association, organising more then 6 meetings per year with an avarage of 250 visitors attending, more then 3500 creative professionals and 25 sponsors.

As a freelancer, next to the days I work for Adobe, I promote creative-, design- and interactive agencies in the media and help them with their strategy in communication. I work for Addikt, Hanazuki, Red Urban, Tribal DDB Amsterdam and others. I am blogger at Marketingfacts, Dutchcowboys and Ilovenewwork.

Before Adobe, I was Ambassador at Satama FlashFabriek (now part of LBi Lost Boys) and project manager at advertising agency OER. The five years before that, I was Editior-in-Chief at Dzone magazine, the leading Dutch print publication on graphic design and multimedia. This was a result of a background of more than ten years working experience as a graphic designer, multimedia designer, online creative and art director.

My expertise is relation- and networkmarketing within the creative and web industry and communities, I have a real passion for creative and interactive and I have been in many jury's the last years, from SpinAwards to The FWA.

Bert Hagendoorn’s Specialties:

Advising, consulting, large network in creative and interactive industry, PR, jury's, project management, boards.
