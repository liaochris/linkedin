Ralph Weber’s Summary

Employee Benefit Consultant, Certified Financial Planner, and Chartered Life Underwriter with experience international health benefits. I design healthcare and insurance products which remove third party payers from routine care, restore the doctor/patient relationship and restore the principles of insurance. My goals are to design financial products which enrich people's lives, improve their financial and physical health, and reduce their costs.

Ralph Weber’s Specialties:

Free Market Insurance product design and plan design for cost containment. Health policy design and analysis, and health plan development and design.
Only the free market can provide the sustainable reforms we need to fix our healthcare financing system. We do not have a healthcare crisis, we have a health crisis, and a crisis of trying to find ways to pay for the medical treatment we demand. Once we understand this, the solution will become obvious, as it has for me.
