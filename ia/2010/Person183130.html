Sargheve Sukumaran’s Summary

I'am an active, energetic and open minded design professional. In the past 4 years of professional experience, I have been enjoying each and every moment of Designing and Developing Products that makes life easier and better. The most exciting experience for me was the journey across different disciplines to make innovation work.
