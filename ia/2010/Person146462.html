Milorad Papic’s Summary

Milorad Papic (SM) is presently working for Idaho Power Company in the System Planning Department. He obtained his M.Sc. degree from Zagreb University in 1977 and D.Sc. degree from Sarajevo University in 1980. Prior to his arrival at IPC, Dr. Papic held an Associate Professor position at Sarajevo University and he was consultant for Energoinvest and B&H Utility. His principal interests include: reliability modeling and evaluation of power systems, state estimation, transmission planning and economics in power systems. He is currently a member of IEEE, CIGRE, PES Reliability, Risk and Probability Applications Subcommittee, IEEE TF Cascading Events, WECC Reliability Subcommittee and WECC DMWG & WAMS Subcommitties.

Milorad Papic’s Specialties:

Reliability, Security and Transient stability of electric power systems.
