Laurie Mitchell, Cleveland Marketing Headhunter’s Summary

Over the last 25 years, I've personally placed 1200+ Marketing & Communications professionals in full-time permanent positions. As a Certified Personnel Consultant, I provide discreet, ethical and straightforward Executive Search Services for classical and digital/direct/database marketing, advertising, public relations, brand strategy, media, research, corporate and employee communications. My clients range from Fortune 1000s to Closely-Held Companies, Digital/Advertising/Public Relations Agencies, and Professional Services Firms.

I've enjoyed scores of repeat search assignments from executives and HR professionals who seek my input on their hiring needs and workforce challenges.

Relying on an unparalleled network of confidential sources whom I've served, counseled, placed and befriended over the last two and a half decades, I'm able to identify and close stellar talent for employers who want the search done right the first time with the best possible cultural and stylistic fit. My fuss-free searches produce exceptional talent.

If you’re looking to hire a marketing professional, let's discuss various permanent and interim hiring options. My unique creative search approach combined with a direct, no-nonsense, transparent style may be just the breath of fresh air you seek. 216-292-9936.

I founded the Scholarship and Minority Outreach programs of AAF/Cleveland and I'm also active in PRSA, AMA, IABC, WISE, The Web Association, CCWA, The Press Club, COSE and SHRM. Prior to becoming a recruiter, I spent 9 years in Advertising and Public Relations both agency and client side. Check out my monthly columns in The Press Club's "Byliner" and interviews in various publications. Full bio at www.LaurieMitchellCompany.com

I always make time to counsel high potential professionals, mentor select young pros, and speak to industry meetings and associations. I'm readily accessible to all who don't whine, and I love a good laugh.
