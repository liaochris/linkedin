Michael Henry’s Summary

Over 20 years of experience delivering results and building expertise in strategy development, business transformation and operations excellence. My passion is aligning teams so they focus on what drives value. I believe in creating a team-oriented, entrepreneurial culture which has consistently led to increased profitability, agility and a better customer experience. For 18 years, I've driven results from within IT; for the last 5 years, I've worked in business development, operations and general management roles. I like to think of my job as connecting the dots...simplifying complex problems and getting the right things done well.

I'm comfortable working in small and large companies - I can be effective in both. It's more important for me to work in an environment where people give their best - the kind of place you would be proud to have your children work. I am driven by work that lets me create more value for the customer and reduce operational costs. It is what pushes me to higher standards of excellence and performance every day.
