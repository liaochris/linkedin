Robert Green’s Summary

We quickly produce senior, manager, director, vice president, and c-level new hires exclusively for the semiconductor industry. We merged the benefits of contingency and retained recruiting, and the results are remarkable.

● Value Proposition: There are nearly 300,000 semiconductor professionals in the U.S. To tap into this resource, we utilize years of detailed research on over 1700 semiconductor companies and their employees. If you have ever wonder where all of the candidates are when you post a job, they are out there, often working and not looking at job boards.

● Specifications: We have in-depth knowledge of nearly all semiconductor company's products, markets, FAB processes, technical environments, culture, and all levels of management. This is invaluable information for finding the ideal candidate for a company quickly.

● Price: Our fee structure is targeted toward the successful completion of a search. The net result is a savings of $25,000 to $80,000 as compared to traditional search fees, and our headhunting/search division has a 100% success record on all searches.

Divisions: Our two divisions, Headhunting / Search and Contingency / Recruiting, offer the same attentive specialized service regardless of your recruiting needs. We only make one promise. The candidates we locate and submit to our valued customers will not come from any other source.
