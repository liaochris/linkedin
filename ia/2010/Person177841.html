Ross Allred’s Summary

Ross Allred is the Interactive Marketing Director of FamilyLink.com. Ross is responsible for Email, PPC, Affiliate and SEO marketing for one of FamilyLink’s web properties: WorldVitalRecords.com. Within the first three months upon joining the FamilyLink team, Ross helped WorldVitalRecords achieve 25% increase in sales consistently month after month. Ross is currently redesigning the WorldVitalRecords.com web site and implementing a new Email Solution, Lyris.

Prior to FamilyLink, Ross was Coldwater Creek’s Email Marketing Manager responsible for leading the strategy, deployment, deliverability and analysis of all promotional, transactional, and triggered email messages. In over the first year, Ross helped increase the email marketing revenue by more than 30% with an overall Email Deliverability of 98% in the inbox. Ross has further developed behavioral based trigger emails with deeper segmentation resulting in a $30 million lift in annualized sales. Ross tested and implemented an email strategy to support over 300 Retail Stores and it resulted in a 22% sales lift at Retail Store locations.

Prior to Coldwater Creek, Ross was contracted as an Email Marketing Manager for United Online where he managed all email communications and deliverability for MySite.com. Ross also lead the due diligence and acquisition of the email software, Lyris and developed and improved the complete email life cycle, including trigger campaigns, billing, transactional, promotional, and event-driven emails.

Before United Online, Ross managed Ancestry.com and Genealogy.com Internet Commerce Divisions responsible for cross selling products through email, call center and online ad channels. Ross also developed email marketing strategies to promote online subscriptions and commerce-related products (books, CDs, software, etc.) for three different business units showing performance, testing results, and overall strategy while exceeding each sales goal.
