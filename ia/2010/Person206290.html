Tommy Huynh’s Summary

Ambitious, determined, and confident are the best words to describe my personality and who I am. I am here to change the world in a big way!

During my junior year in college I had an urge and thirst for knowledge of the real business world. Knowing this couldn't be taught in a classroom, I took a year off from school (don't tell my parents) to turn my paintball hobby into a full time business. During this time, paintball was an up and coming popular sport allowing Thepbstore to gross $300k in revenue the first year. This was accomplished with one obsessed entrepreneur working 20hrs a day, with friends and family pitching time and money.

Regardless of the risks, entrepreneurs jump in with the thought that every setback is a learning lesson for the next challenge. Even though I was far from my first million, I gained so much experience that no money in the world could have made up for it.

With this new found knowledge, I went back to SJSU, completed my Management degree in less than 2 years as a full time student, while working 35hrs at Ericsson as a Project Manager.
