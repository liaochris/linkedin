Julia Bowlin, M.D.’s Summary

From my deeply rooted medical family and my profound professional and personal connection with the emotionally challenged, a healing giant has emerged. I work to clear the emotional, spiritual and physical clutter in our lives impacting our healing and recovery process through my speaking and medical practice. My dream is to impact the lives of millions of people by teaching them how to connect entirely with their innermost self, impacting how they see themselves and others in a more connected and productive way.
