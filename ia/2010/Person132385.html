Maria Matarelli, PMP, CSP’s Summary

Over the last ten years, Maria's experience has ranged from Project Manager to Agile Coach using traditional Project Management methods, 6 Sigma, Agile and Scrum. She has led multiple Project Management training courses including PMP Prep courses and Agile and Scrum training.

Maria takes pride in her ability to capture her team’s attention and inspiring people to deliver work on the next level. Her focus on the big picture streamlines processes, provides a clear understanding of the product to be delivered, while allowing her teams to focus on their individual contributions.

Maria works with both management teams and individual project teams. She has consulted large enterprise environments and played an instrumental part of role based steering committees in the adoption of iterative process management. She has worked on projects taking Scrum beyond the software development environment, spoken at PMI Chapter Meetings on agile adoption and has been involved in the planning and coordination of worldwide industry conferences. Maria has spent much of her time over the last year speaking with corporations, universities, Not for Profit groups, and has led Open Space sessions with 150+ audiences.
