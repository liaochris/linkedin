Barbara Kremers’s Summary

Summary of professional experience and goals:

I have founded Amylion so I can combine my range of qualities. I'm both doing business analysis, giving internet marketing advice and creating websites that attract free traffic (through organic search results in Google). Nowadays every company has a website. The important question is.. is that website designed and coded to attract targeted traffic? Too often this is not the case and you might conclude 'the internet is not working for me'. When you feel your website is simply not doing its job, contact me and I'll take a look. If you want a new website, better yet.. have it built the right way - right from the start.

A quick overview of my life before Amylion:

I have been ambitiously searching for a cure for cancer. One of the projects I have contributed to has resulted in the vaccine for cervix carcinoma. Then, after a big change, I joined the commercial IT world as a business intelligence consultant, a completely different world but I adapted fast. After an additional study I became financial analyst at Oracle. I have become an experienced Excel user to create custom dashboards that summarize big loads of data (operational and financial), so business managers know what's happening and why.

My interest was then (and still is) drawn to internet marketing, including search engine optimization, email marketing and affiliate marketing. I have created websites based on guidelines learned from experienced internet marketing guru's in the US, a highly competitive market.

For one year I joined the management team of media and publishing company IENS. IENS is one of the prime examples demonstrating that user generated content enables and drives new business in the internet marketing space.

As I'm a continuous learner I also enrolled in a part-time study Professional Communication. This is an education in personal development and coaching. It combines rationality with intuition.
