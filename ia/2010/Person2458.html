Adam Knowles’s Summary

Working in digital marketing over 10 years across disciplines including business development, information architecture, design, development and account/project management. I've worked on hundreds of projects - from Pontin's to Virgin Holidays, Cadbury, O'Neills, Jacob's Creek, P&O Cruises, LA Fitness, Citroen, Peugeot, TheLondonPaper, JPMorgan, Nickelodeon, Slingbox and many others.

Enjoy being a key part of a friendly, small team (that could be part of something larger) with an entrepreneurial spirit and an eye on the big picture. Like to 'do the essential things well'. I care about strong processes and strong relationships, within and beyond the team. PRINCE2 certified (but not limited to thinking that way).

My personal goals are simple: live, learn and have fun along the way!
