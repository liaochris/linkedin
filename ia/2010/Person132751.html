Maria Kontogiorgou _MaRik’s Summary

Hands on art director/illustrator/animator for digital and TV.
Working from 1998 in digital with major and innovative brands, creating web designs, flash animations, storyboards, animatics and illustrations, for brands like Mastercard, Unilever, Starbucks, Tom Dixon, Amstel, Nike, H&M and agencies like McCann, JWT, Ogilvy and likes.
I love creating worlds, stories and original characters by translating visually the creative product development strategy, style guides and product development, participating on the development of an award winning and successful brand.
I am multilingual (english, french, greek) and work in international
environments. Experience to present pitches to clients, excellent communication skills and problem solving behaviour. Work independently as well as part of a team, inspiring, positive, sense of humour, high spirit, proactive, passionate with art and technology.

Maria Kontogiorgou _MaRik’s Specialties:

Digital painting, illustrating, animating, branding and identity. Translate communication briefs to successful and inspiring visuals. Concept & Illustration of original concepts, animation, drawing, painting, sculpting, claymation, storyboards for production and pitching, animation direction for digital.
