Mike Monroe’s Summary

Pleasure to meet you. How can I be of service?

I am looking to connect with small-business owners and workaholics. I have been both for many years.

Starting out as an entry-level sales representative in the year 2000, I rose through the ranks to become a manager. Before the age of 28, I trained well-over 1000 people, expanded the organization to ten offices in three different states, and grew my business from 200K to nearly 2 million in annual sales.

Currently I am consulting small business owners on cutting-edge internet marketing.

But my real expertise? How to be a business OWNER... somebody who spins plates versus somebody who martyrs themselves 8 days a week to pay-the-bills.

Allow me to introduce you to "The Invisible Boss." The Invisible Boss is an online information product that teaches small business owners how to double their free time and grow their business without being there.

The Invisible Boss system is based on psychology and communication. It can be applied in any organization, big or small, across any industry. The interactive lessons and customized assignments will leave you, as the leader, wondering: "what am I supposed to do with all this extra time???"

The Invisible Boss launches in 2010. However, in order to give the proper personal attention to the community, there will only be a limited number of spots available.

Go to TheInvisibleBoss.com to sign-up for the waiting list.
