Chad Raney’s Summary

Chad Raney isn’t one to sit still.

Although confined to a wheelchair since 1983, he has never stopped moving forward on what he calls “the road to resilience.” At an early age, while rehabbing from the all-terrain vehicle accident that left him a quadriplegic, he learned a lesson many never fully grasp: self-pity and an inability to accept change saps us of the energy and drive needed to create new opportunities.

Chad’s story is one of vision, determination, reflection, and inspiration.

Where others see roadblocks, Chad simply sees detours to be navigated around. Life is a detour, Chad believes, so drive on. Although the life he envisioned as a young boy in 8th grade changed in a split second, Chad has continued to drive on. Chad’s fresh perspective on business, life, and the crazy world we live in will help transform your team’s way of thinking.

Would your organization be different if everyone handled change with a resilient attitude?
