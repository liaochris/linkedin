Shane Gray’s Summary

Shane Gray brings over 15 years of diversified experience with leading ad agencies, healthcare, classic retail, sports, and food merchandising companies; providing brands dynamic business development opportunities, innovative marketing strategies and advertising platforms that drive sales and increase top-of-mind brand awareness.

He is highly skilled in product development: naming, logo development, package design, strategic positioning and retail merchandising, utilizing a consumer driven, speed-to-market methodology developed at Omnicom, called www.Thelaunchpoint.com.

His brand positioning strategies have set a multitude of sales records including taking Classic Foods, Inc. Kettle Classics from $100K annually to $6M in 4 years. And his new product innovations and commercials include Pizza Hut’s “4ForAll” pizza, introduced during the 2004 Super Bowl, Pizza Hut’s most successful product launch to date. He pioneered daypart marketing initiatives, positioning Taco Bell as the Late Night Meal, launching the first “Open 24 Hours” electronic advertising campaign. Today, nearly every QSR has a Late Night advertising message.

He has developed brand partnerships with the Dallas Cowboys and Pepsi, Taco Bell and the Lakers, Pizza Hut and the NFL, and most recently AEG (Staples Center) and Classic Foods, Inc. He was the first marketer to utilize courtside ads with the LA Lakers.

Shane’s true passion is working with, and motivating others to accomplish the unthinkable. His mantra is “Whatever it takes”. On a personal note, Shane has been married for 18 years, and is a father of two daughters. He loves music and can be found singing anywhere at any time. He enjoys family, the outdoors and participating in any sport is a welcomed recreational event.

My primary goal in life is to continue to elevate myself as a loving father to my two beautiful daughters.
