Steven Casarez’s Summary

Passion.

I believe in having passion for what I do and it shows in the work that I do.

I have been in the all-terrain-vehicle (ATV) industry for over six years with the creation of TheATVChannel.Com which is a network of six websites geared towards specific makes and models. In a short period of time, I grew the websites from being a small time player in this unique vertical to now being number one with hundreds of thousands of unique visitors a month - all while getting my undergraduate degree from Babson College. 80+ hour weeks are normal for me.

I have also worked as a strategic consultant with PUMA Footwear and Harman International - a maker of high-end audio equipment. In both of these roles, I assessed existing and new market opportunities for the company to expand into. Primary and secondary market research was done through speaking with industry professionals and conducting online and offline surveys. After months of market research and data mining, the findings and guidance were presented to senior executives in the form of a formal presentation and paper.

Now I'm on my next adventure at OpenCandy!
