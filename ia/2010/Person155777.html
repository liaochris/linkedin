Nicholas Kinports’s Summary

Nick has worked in the interactive advertising world for eight years and continues to push the limits of digital communication through social media, interactive, and experiential technologies. He is currently the Digital Integration Manager at Maddock Douglas, the agency for companies driven by innovation, where he develops and executes outstanding multi-channel campaigns for Fortune 50 clients and works to bring ideas from mind to market.

Nick is also the author of ADMAVEN – The Interactive Advertising Blog. Focusing on the Chicago advertising community, Nick discusses developments and areas of interest from his own perspective. His blog was recently named a recognized voice in the advertising industry by DDB, and continues to grow readership and interest nationally.

With help from other advertising professionals, Nick organizes the Chicago Media Marketing & Advertising Group. This gathering of advertising, marketing, public relations, and media professionals meets once per month in the Chicago area to network, hear from exciting panelists, and discuss relevant topics.

Nick holds a Bachelor of Science in International Business from Creighton University and currently lives in downtown Chicago, IL.
