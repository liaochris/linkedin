Ray Garcia’s Summary

Over twenty years of international experience in information technology management and strategy within large global corporations and entrepreneurial ventures having a particular focus on the development and delivery of complex commercial software products to international clients.

Co-founded Smart Systems Technology, a company that delivered contactless proximity payment and loyalty applications to large retail environments (sports stadiums). Secured venture capital funding, organized the team, developed and delivered the products to market, and finally sold the company.

Managed a team of researchers within a major university to generate intellectual property to be licensed by a commercial entity. The research was in the area of self-directed learning using internet resources and how one would mediate such activities when conducted by the general public.

Introduced Internet technology to two major banks and developed software applications. In both cases customer service, account management, and risk management were improved significantly and legacy systems were replaced to reduce the total cost of ownership of the applications. Each project included designing and implementing the technology architecture required for providing an integrated and secure solution for the marketing of banking products.

Successfully delivered numerous commercial Internet software product releases to market across many product lines.

Conducted over 30 technology audit assessments of software product companies as part of investment and acquisition due diligence for various investors and corporations. Six acquisitions were initiated as a result of the assessments. Participated in post assessment activities by producing detailed business plan of action, including the restructuring of the companies and re-architecture of its software products, then executing the plans as part of a transition executive team.
