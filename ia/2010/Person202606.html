Dani Nordin’s Summary

Hi there! I'm a designer and consultant with over 10 years of experience working with specialty food companies, organic products and green businesses to create their brands and promote them through logos, eco-friendly print materials, strategic consulting and websites. My clients range from passionate entrepreneurs to Fortune 500 companies to non-profit and philanthropic organizations. You can view a complete portfolio at http://tzk-design.com.

My writing and design work has been featured in several online and print publications, including "LogoLounge 3," Boston Herald's Women's Business, and Creative Latitude. I also speak regularly on the subjects of women in business, marketing, green design, social media and green business for local business organizations, including the Center for Women and Enterprise and Downtown Women's Club.

In addition to my work with the zen kitchen and promoting sustainable practices within the design industry, I am also an avid writer, gardener, cook and yogi, who works these combined passions into various areas of my life and business.

Dani Nordin’s Specialties:

green design, logo design, standards-based web design (meaning that your site is easier to build and maintain, and looks great in all types of browsers), branding and identity, eco-friendly printed materials, networking, social media, blogging, most things food-related (especially vegetables)
