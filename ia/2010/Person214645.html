Wendy Stevens, SPHR’s Summary

As an exceptionally knowledgeable and experienced HR professional, I identify the strengths and weaknesses of a company and the personnel. I develop and implement a plan to improve the performance of departments and individuals to support and meet or exceed the goals of the organization. I have a history of achievements in leading high-performance HR teams and staff development initiatives. I have revamped HR capabilities, performance management, compliance, benefits and recruiting systems for firms in industries, including legal, banking, insurance, industrial equipment and retail. Furthermore, I have developed core competencies, resulting in low turnover, higher productivity and increased organizational performance, and won cooperation at all levels. Managed budgets to $10M and staff to 42 and saved my previous employers millions of dollars.
My Special Skills Include: Developing training programs to enhance practical skill building and improve competencies. Recruiting and mentoring results-oriented, high-performance teams. Assessing deficiencies and implementing change management solutions. Ensuring regulatory compliance. Improving work life balance.
I am very proud of the knowledge and experience that I have gained and then utilized throughout my career for the betterment of the company and personnel. I also take pride in my experiences analyzing the company goals and employees' strengths and weaknesses, and then developing and implementing a plan to improve performance of the departments and individuals to attain the goals of the company.
Coupling a BS in Human Resources, undergraduate and Master’s level business courses, and my SPHR certification with my skills and experience, I am seeking a position where strong contributions are expected immediately. My broad based business experience provides an excellent foundation on which to build future success.
