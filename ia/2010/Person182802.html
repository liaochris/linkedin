Sarah Oldershaw’s Summary

I recently started my own HR consulting business and got my first client on 3/15/2010. Using my 10+ years of HR management experience, my organizing principal is to focus on organizational development that promotes wellness, using sound HR practices to always work with integrity toward that outcome in all initiatives.

I specialize in companies with 100 or less employees who need to establish an HR function, and/or do not have the need for a full-time HR Director.

I can also cover for key HR employees who are out on extended leave in larger companies, as well as project work (benefit roll-outs, audits, HRIS conversions etc.).

I have the proven ability to lead large initiatives as well as work effectively with employees at all levels to support organizational, departmental, and individual goals.

My background in Human Resources is primarily in the Advertising and Computer/IT industries. Prior to working in Human Resources I worked as a Media Planner in Advertising; and as a Training Manager, and Department Manager for Emporium.

I also went back to school to explore getting my teaching credential and worked part-time while I did that. Additionally, I completed the Professional in Human Resources Certification and the Certificate in HR Management from UC Berkeley Extension.

I am particularly interested in promoting wellness as an HR professional and feel that this is as an integral approach to attracting and retaining a strong, confident, and content workforce.

I am very happy in a hands-on role working directly with business units, as a resource for line employees and managers at all levels. I would like more involvement and projects that relate to organizational development.

I am signed up to take the SPHR exam in October, 2010 and I am looking into MBA programs currently. I would like to begin a weekend/night MBA program in 2011.

Sarah Oldershaw’s Specialties:

My strongest areas of expertise are organizational development, benefits, project management, and employee relations.

More specifically, I have deep skills in: benefit analysis/RFP/administration, recruiting, coaching, counseling, performance management, change management, compensation design/negotiation/administration, policy development, HRIS design/ administration, legal compliance, HR communications (handbook, offer letters, term letters etc.), and training design/implementation.
