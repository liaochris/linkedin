Scott Boren’s Summary

By title, I am a Technical Recruiter. I currently work as a full-time employee of Compuware. For 11 years before that, I worked as an agency recruiter, or headhunter, running a "full desk". I think the term headhunter more aptly describes what I really do: Find the right people with the skills and cultural fit for a specific need. I use every source possible to accomplish that goal. I have placed QA Specialists, Sofware Engineers, Data Architects, Managers, Directors and Sales Professionals.

I believe strongly in helping others where ever I can. If you think I can assist you an in way, feel free to reach out to me directly at scott.boren (at) compuware.com, and of course send an invitation.

I regularly attend networking events including LinkedIn Live!, and Triangle Networking Group.

Scott Boren’s Specialties:

I have been a Technical Recruiter since 1994. I have also spent considerable time working with sales people in the IT industry. This covers software, services and in house IT needs.
