Ben Frank’s Summary

Designed and designing for clients like NASCAR, Reebok, G Unit Apparel, Chevy, Cadillac, Nissan, West Coast Customs, Orange County Choppers, American Chopper, Cartoon Network, Televisa, Earth Communications Office, and many others. I would like to continue serving in this capacity until I meet others who would like to grow a larger design center in a partnership.

Ben Frank’s Specialties:

Graphic Design, Logo design and corporate identity packages, detailed digital illustration for a wide range of categories, muralism and airbrushing. Urban style art and design, graffiti, character design, specialized in creating custom art for apparel companies and marketing firms.
