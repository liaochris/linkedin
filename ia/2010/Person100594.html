II help people better understand Inclusion, Innovation and Leadership in order to build Next Generation Organizations and Communities. I develop and deliver programs (including keynote presentations, interactive workshops, in-depth retreats for small groups). I have done this work for Fortune 500 Corporations, small non-profits, government entities and everything in between.

Some organizations have me come in once a month or once a quarter to conduct an Inclusive Leadership workshop. Some organizations need a one time presentation on Social Media. I have developed a number of programs and the accompanying materials (including e-learning modules) and I have facilitated internally developed programs.
