Randy Lubinsky’s Summary

I’m Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of WellTek Incorporated, an exercise-science based health, fitness and wellness company. In mid 2008, Welltek acquired a 23-yr old brand, MedX, which has earned a global reputation for excellence in the manufacture of medical & fitness exercise equipment. MedX machines are recognized as the industry’s “gold standard” for science-based rehabilitative exercise & for building core muscle strength. To capitalize on MedX’s well-trusted reputation, we are executing a strategy leveraging the brand in hi-growth market sectors within our industry. Of these growth platforms, I am perhaps most excited about our Pure HealthyBack program.

Pure HealthyBack uses MedX machines and rehabilitative exercise to help patients resolve chronic back & neck pain — ailments that afflict nearly 80% of all Americans and cost the healthcare system $100 billion each year. The program provides non-surgical treatment and has documented success in improving spinal function and reducing symptoms in those who have tried and failed a number of other spine treatment solutions. Treatment is directed by physicians and administered by physiologists and athletic trainers.

Pure HealthyBack is developing 6 pilot programs in collaboration with managed care & self insured companies. The pilots will be implemented with patients that have cost the payers more than $10k per year in layered medical costs from other medical interventions or prior to surgery. Each pilot will accept a maximum of 500 patients and continue for 1-yr. Then we will expand the program nationally to thousands of patients who have been on a medical merrygoround and have not found lasting relief.

Our program was developed to reduce managed care & self insured companies’ spine treatment costs. Current methods for treating back & neck pain have major shortcomings, including expensive, unnecessary and unproven procedures and increasing surgical rates.

Randy Lubinsky’s Experience

  • Chairman and CEO

    WellTek Inc.

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Headquartered in Orlando, Florida, WellTek is an exercise science based health, fitness and wellness company. WellTek has acquired and is leveraging a 23 year old brand that has a proven worldwide reputation for excellence. To capitalize on this brand, WellTek has established two complementary service businesses.

    The WellTek vision began with the acquisition of the MedX Corporation - a Company founded in 1986 by Arthur Jones, the legendary U.S. entrepreneur and founder/inventor of Nautilus® fitness equipment. MedX manufactures and distributes a comprehensive line of proprietary, patented, medical and fitness machines that are widely recognized as the industry’s “Gold Standard” equipment for science based rehabilitative exercise and for building core muscle strength. WellTek’s highly accomplished team of business, healthcare and technology professionals recognized that manufacturing the equipment had limited growth, but leveraging the well-trusted brand in the service industry has the capacity of developing WellTek into a $100 million company within five years.

    WellTek is making fast progress in executing two complementary business strategies designed to promote the MedX brand into two high growth niche markets within the global health, fitness and wellness industry. To accomplish this mission, three distinct business units have been established - all of which provide for the integration of the MedX-branded equipment into their operating platforms; apply proven Exercise Science- based principles and life coaching philosophies; and feature strong organic global growth potential through tactical replication and scaling. At present, WellTek’s operating subsidiaries include MedX Systems, PureHealthyBack, and lime Fitness.
