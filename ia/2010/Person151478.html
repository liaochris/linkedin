Mike Napier - Market Researcher & Strategic Planner’s Summary

I am currently pursuing two academic publications (one in international trade / political economy and one in organizational transformation). I was invited to a conference at Princeton University in 2008 where I co-presented the trade paper and it was critiqued by authors from Harvard and Yale.

My involvement with the Junior Chamber International (JCI) Calgary Chapter as a Board member, past chair and founder of the Partnership Strategy Committee (partnering with SIFE Calgary and Junior Achievement of Southern Alberta) and the past chair and founder of the Long-term Alumni Engagement Committee have been experiences that have contributed to my leadership growth. In both chair positions, I facilitated the strategic planning that set the committees off on the right foot with a clarified strategic direction.

As a rookie head coach in Special Olympics floor hockey last year, I led my team to the league championship and tournament gold medal while achieving "Team of the Year" for all of Alberta. While the focus is on learning and having fun, I coach the highest skill level in the league and the element of competition is very high - I possibly learned more from my team than they learned from me!
