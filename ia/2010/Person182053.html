Sanjay Jhawar’s Summary

20-year experienced senior executive with strong leadership skills in technology, strategy and business operations. Track record of leading high performance teams to success in mobile / wireless technology growth markets. Visionary thought leader and strategist for the Mobile Internet – established first markets, industry ecosystems and key early product positions for GSM circuit switched data, GPRS handsets, smart phones, micro-browsing, mobile email services, mobile VoIP/fixed mobile convergence and GPS worker safety applications. 10+ years marketing directly and via channels to mobile carriers.

Led multiple product cycles from concept to revenue in mobile handsets, core network infrastructure, application servers and clients, building go-to-market model and operations. Instrumental in a $128m acquisition exit, the raising of $25m of venture capital and investment/board in series A startups.

Strong international experience, living and working in UK, Sweden, Italy and US - every operating role has addressed pan-regional or global markets.
