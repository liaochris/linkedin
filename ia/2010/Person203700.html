Tim Bosworth’s Summary

Friends and Colleagues,

Referrals constitute a large part of all of our businesses. Paying if Forward (PIF) constitutes the foundation of Sherpa- PIF We all share a commonality of interest. Act always in a spirit of generosity and paying it forward. Desire most of all to serve others we each will be served. What goes around comes around though not necessarily as soon or in the same form as we expect. Just have to be open to receiving it.
Please help me validate these assumptions. Call or email me if you’d like to be referred to someone or would benefit from some information or connection of an organization or business.

SHERPA LITERARY: Issue #1 of By and For Writers is out. People can get it by emailing me or calling me. It’s $9.95 and I’ll give you the mailing address so they can send a check. If anyone you know wants to see stuff from poets and short story writers not published anywhere else send them my way. They would be helping the writers because they get a cut of the proceeds.

The manuscript of my second mystery, No Stop on Red, is about to go to the publisher. I’m going to start revising my third novel as soon as that one is off my desk.
And there’s a biography of the Curtis Quartet I’m in the very first stages of sketching out.

SHERPA MENTORING: A mentoring relationship with a fast growing business based here in Philadelphia is in the works. There will be a lot of people for me to help if this comes to fruition. Know anyone looking to start a business or with questions or doubts about their current one, send them my way.

SHERPA RE: According to the latest NAR research, 96% of home buyers begin their search on the internet? And 80% of them buy the first house they find. And they’re not calling a buyer’s agent first; they’re calling listing agents. So getting listings is a key to success as an agent. Anyone you know looking for an agent or wanting some mentoring through the house buying or house selling process, send them my way.
