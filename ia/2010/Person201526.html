Toufic Gebran’s Summary

I'm a highly motivated broadcaster with insight into devising ideas into TV format, I was the first presenter in the Arab world to produce and present a show specialized in the field of Internet and Information & Communication Technologies (ICT). I have 13 years of TV experience, in different kind of programs: games, quiz, entertainment, musical, talk show, social, cultural and technology shows.
Born in Jbeil / Byblos - Lebanon.
1988-1993 Radio Broadcaster in Lebanon.
1993 Graduated in Interior Architecture - Lebanese American University (LAU). 1993-1996 Interior Architect in Lebanon & Dubai.
1993, Won the golden medal in broadcasting in ''Studio El-Fan 93'' on LBC.
TV Anchor / Producer for 14 years:
1993-1996 LBC Lebanon.
1996-1999 Channel 4 UAE.
2000-2003 Abu Dhabi TV UAE.
2004 Joined Alhurra TV which broadcasts to the Middle East, Europe & Africa from Washington, DC in the USA.
I am now the Anchor/Producer for ''i-Tech'': Alhurra's weekly technology news hour that features the latest in information technology & telecoms with expert guests, gadgets & updates, covering the most important technology conferences & expos in the USA & around the world.
Awards:
