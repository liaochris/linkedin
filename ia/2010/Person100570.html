Joseph Fischer’s Summary

I am a flexible, results-oriented, hands-on, innovative and entrepreneurial senior business leader with a significant history of running highly successful cable telecommunications operations in large MSO environments as well as small, decentralized, independent cable systems.

I am proud to say that I have always been a team player on all levels – with a strong employee track record of excellent working relationships – and a history of building exceptional operating teams that successfully worked in concert towards cross functional goals across multiple disciplines.

While always achieving bottom line budgets and business operating results regardless of outside circumstances, I have been involved and responsible for virtually every aspect of cable operations that the industry has experienced since the mid 1970’s.
