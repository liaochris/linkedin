Paul Hine’s Summary

Possesses a knowledge and understanding of Research and Development, Project/Program Management, Business Development, Manufacturing and Quality, Clinical Development, and Regulatory Affairs. Paul Hine’s Experience

  • Senior Director, Operations

    GenPhar, Inc

    (Pharmaceuticals industry)

    January 2010 — Present (4 months)

    GenPhar is a biopharmaceutical company dedicated to providing vaccines that protect against infectious diseases with great socio-economic impacts worldwide. Our highly flexible CAdVax vaccine platform is ideally designed for multiple diseases, including biodefense applications, because one vaccine can prevent infection from multiple virus subtypes. We are developing single and multivalent vaccines for viral diseases including cell-based seasonal and pandemic flu, hepatitis B, hepatitis C, HIV, Chikungunya, and Yellow Fever. In addition, we are committed to providing vaccine products to combat the world's most dangerous viral diseases such as Ebola, Marburg, and dengue, with several approaching the final stages prior to approval. The company works closely with the DoD in late stage testing prior to conducting clinical studies.
