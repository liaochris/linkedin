Jeff Bloch’s Summary

I believe.

I believe life's short. And choices matter (because some recruiting vendors can sting).

I believe your company has a voice that's screaming to be heard. And that voice is saying "talent will make or break my success".

I believe hiring managers don't have time to screw around. And they need recruitment to be sharper than a Ginsu knife.

I believe revenue is the life blood of a company. And hiring delays are blood clots.

I believe any yahoo can apply on a job board. And the masses of yahoos make it harder to see the true talent.

I believe social media isn't going away...it's warming up its vocal cords.

I believe smaller companies need external staffing experts because they don't have time to keep current on the best tools and skills to source top candidates.

I believe large companies need a core team of their own staffing experts, AND an external staffing partner to handle hiring peaks, valleys, and odd-ball situations.

I believe experience matters. Mine...18 years in staffing: ran contingent search business, a 100 person Cisco Systems recruiting team, 8 years on the vendor side. Recruiting, Sales, Marketing, Management.

I believe in keeping it real, and that I can hear your company's voice screaming. Ring me: 678.262.2319
