Jermyn Shannon El’s Summary

The Blacksonville .COMmunity .NETwork (BCN). specializes in providing local to national marketing strategies and internet solutions. It is our sincere passion and vision to grow a new generation of "Digipreneurs" that will lead institutional change throughout the world, particularly in under-served communities.

Blacksonville is one of the best online networking sites for 21st century trendsetters. Share in our vision by adding me to your professional network by email: marketing (at) blacksonville (dot) com.

Blacksonville's business model was orchestrated to service both the business and social development needs of CBOs, businesses, local urban community settings. We aspire to expand this mission of local social networking globally. Can you envision a Blacksonville in your community?

Our synergy of social networking concepts and our ability to reproduce newly formed, niche markets via the internet separates our firm from traditional marketing and PR firms. Simply put, we are solution providers. We believe that all successful business practices start with understanding the "Double Bottom Line"...that of Profits/Cash Flow, and the Bottom Line that results from proper time and investment in true leadership and the community.

As a member of our Community Network you will have access to a host of professional contacts and "green technology-related" resources to empower your business and revitalize your community. We understand the importance of leadership development and institution-building.

Our subscriber-base is predominantly ethnic women, ages 25-45, seeking inclusion, innovation and strategic partnerships to enhance their professional career or business venture.

Contact Blacksonville to effectively communicate your brand to emerging markets and underserved communities locally or globally.
