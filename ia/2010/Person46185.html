Damien Knowles’s Summary

I am an award-winning Senior Art Director with 8 years of creative advertising experience.

Some would say I’m a traditional creative who went digital. I’d say I was never traditional.
I have made TV and radio spots, and I’ve created online content and mobile apps.
But what I’ve always done is big ideas.

I believe the idea is key.
It can start on Facebook, with a poster campaign, mobile app or cunning PR stunt.
Then it can go a number of ways, if it’s really good it will spread without booking ad space.

That’s why I’m passionate about media-neutral thinking with digital at the core.
I look for new ways to tell stories and solve problems.
Now I’m looking for new challenges.

I feel I have much to offer, and hope you’ll agree that my experience is as relevant and unexpected as the ideas I can create.

Damien Knowles’s Specialties:

Integrated and digital advertising including social media.
Big creative ideas. Selling those ideas.
Art direction of still and film shoots.
Ambient and PR stunts.
