Manik Rane’s Summary

Manik is a highly accomplished marketing professional with 15 years experience in enterprise software and IT services industries.

Manik has deep expertise in creating successful business partnerships. Over the past 8 years he has held several positions at Microsoft and with Microsoft's Gold Certified partner organizations focused on winning new business and growing market share.

Manik has a distinctive blend of technical and business skills across telecom and healthcare industries His approach to sales and marketing excellence is founded on driving a win-win business outcomes with customers and partners. He effectively leverages his strong people skills to build long lasting business relationships.

Manik's passion and interest in healthcare industry stems from his long time desire to make a positive impact on patient's lives by improving care outcomes through innovation in diagnostic and information technology.

Manik holds a Master of Business Administration degree specializing in Marketing from University of Michigan, a Master of Science degree in Biomedical Engineering from University of Akron, and a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Mumbai.

He currently serves on the board of EADS (www.eads-cares.org) a non-profit healthcare service provider in Washington State.
