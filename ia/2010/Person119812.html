Kelly Truelove’s Summary

As President of Integrity Consultants, my focus is meeting the needs of business through Market Research. Thorough assessment, data organization and individualized programs give clients the tools they need to improve profitability and decrease loss. Evaluations are tailored to assess customer/employee satisfaction, regulatory compliance, quality and potential areas of concern related to SOPs, loss prevention and quality assurance. As a Market Research Consultant, Editor, Recruiter and Scheduler, I have developed personal relationships and become an integral part of my clients’ success. I specialize in Special Project needs and meeting the needs of business with Integrity. Additionally, I am the Moderator of Market Research Professionals on Facebook, Squidoo, XING and Brightfuse and Co-Moderator of The Entrepreneurs Club on XING. I am also proud to announce that Integrity Consultants served as sponsor to the 2009 Independent Mystery Shoppers Conference where I was honored to be a guest speaker and provide three presentations for continuing education. For the 2010 IMSC, I will proudly serve as Communications Director.

Kelly Truelove’s Specialties:

Utilizing Market Research tools to assess issues related to Customer/Employee Satisfaction, Loss Prevention, Regulatory Compliance, Loyalty, and Quality Assurance. Recruiting, Editing, Scheduling, and Event Management of Market Research assignments in all industries. I specialize in Special Project needs and evaluations requiring specific demographics and having specific requirements, as well as the development of Training and Reward Programs.
