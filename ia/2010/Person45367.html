Craig Cuyar’s Summary

Dr. Cuyar is currently the Global CIO for Cushman & Wakefield, the world's largest privately-held commercial real estate services firm. With 227 offices in 59 countries and more than 15,000 employees, the firm represents a diverse customer base ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies.

Prior to joining Cushman & Wakefield, Dr. Cuyar served as the CIO for the Realogy Franchise Group. Realogy is a franchisor of six of the most recognized brands in the real estate industry: Better Homes and Gardens Real Estate®, CENTURY 21®, Coldwell Banker®, Coldwell Banker Commercial®, ERA®, and Sotheby’s International Realty®. Realogy franchises its leading brands to residential and commercial real estate brokerages throughout the United States and around the world.

Dr. Cuyar is currently an adjunct faculty member at Columbia University where he teaches Supply Chain Management and serves as a mentor to students enrolled in their Masters of Technology Management program.

Dr. Cuyar holds Bachelors, Masters and Doctoral degrees from The Pennsylvania State University and an MBA in Finance from the Rutgers Business School. He is a regular speaker on the Business Value of IT and serves on a number of advisory boards in both the technology and professional services industries. Dr. Cuyar is also the current President of the New Jersey Chapter of the Society for Information Management.

Business & Technology Strategy, Business Development, Outsourcing, Organizational Change, Finance, Talent Management, IT Governance, and Innovation.
