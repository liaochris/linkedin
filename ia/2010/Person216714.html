Ryan Reaves’s Summary

1 Source Consultants is a recruiting and staffing organization specializing in executive and professional level placement. Our core competencies are in HR, HRIS, IT, energy, oil and gas, engineering and the financial market place. Our mission is recruiting highly skilled candidates within the market place and introducing them to the companies who need these skills and resources for their changing workforce. We strive for perfection in recruiting individuals who possess the right skill level and cultural characteristics for the companies with imperative positions. In permanent positions and in project based work, we make sure that the fit is one that can evolve into a long term relationship between both the client and candidate. Our purpose is make the recruiting process a great experience and deliver results that are measured by long term benefits and success.

Ryan Reaves’s Specialties:

Recruiting for permanent positions, staffing temporary and project based work, assessing the core needs through client discovery processes and relationships with human resources and management.
