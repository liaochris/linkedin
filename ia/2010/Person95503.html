Jenn McGroary’s Summary

Jenn McGroary has been operating her own home-based web design and consulting business since 1999.

She has consulted with many businesses ranging from retail, service, construction, and design. She has also worked in manufacturing and as a customer service representative with the International company Crabtree & Evelyn, Inc. With this range of experience, she has been able to help a wide range of businesses with their marketing and web site needs.

In May of 2007 Jenn McGroary became a Certified Marketing Consultant. She helps local small businesses who want to maximize the profits of their business without spending more money on advertising. This is done by finding marketing assets that already exist in the business but aren't being utilized properly. By using a 'Non traditional' approach to marketing, profits can generally be increased 25% or more within 3-6 months.

If you are interested in finding out how your business could benefit from this systematic marketing method, please contact Jenn McGroary at 860-481-4870 to set up a Free, No Obligation, Opportunity Analysis of your business. Find out how much money could be 'hiding' in your business!
