Michelle Heatherton’s Summary

Utilizing my experience in the fields of higher education, financial services, and public relations, I created my own business in 2007. HIP is truly an experiment in diversification. At the present time, I'm mainly involved in the following activities: investing in stocks, bonds, real estate, and private equity ventures; free-lance writing; assisting nonprofit organizations (mostly charities, fine arts, and educational institutions) with development and fundraising efforts; renewing "blighted" properties; helping local artists/writers make professional connections; and conducting workshops on a variety of topics.
