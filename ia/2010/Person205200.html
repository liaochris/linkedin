Toby Considine’s Summary

Smart energy uses enterprise communications to communicate between smart grids and smart buildings. Member of the teams that developed the national smart grid roadmap and that are expediting its development. Focused on the market oriented interfaces of smart grids and how smart buildings can use them. Chair of OASIS Technical Committee oBIX - the standard for using web services to communicate with building control systems. Editor of EMIX and ENergyInteroperation Technical Committees. Signer of GridWise Architectural Council Constitution. Participant in NBIMS committees. Frequent speaker at conferences on the interfaces between IT and capital assets.

Looking for an energy related venture, or consulting, or other situation in which a firm understanding of SOA architectures, building systems, energy management can be an asset.
