Dan Wardle’s Summary

Timothy James Consulting is a thriving specialist recruitment consultancy, working specifically in the Senior Appointments market placing management-level candidates across a variety of industry sectors including IT Services, Software, Telco/ISP, Retail, NHS, Local Government and more.

Now in our seventh year and working out of brand-new city centre offices, we are looking to expand upon our strong international existing client base and hire the very best Senior Consultants to work as an essential part of our friendly, informal, hard-working team.

Opportunities for advancement/team leadership will be plenty as the team grows over the course of the next year and you will benefit from the very best tools available to a recruitment consultant – state-of-the-art system, unlimited advertising, access to all major job boards and a speedy, non-political attitude to decision-making, with none of the drawbacks of working for a slow, inflexible corporate machine.

So if you feel that you’ve outgrown your current market, feel stifled by the restrictions of working for a large corporate or are just looking to test your skills in a more high-level arena, send your CV to d.wardle@timothyjamesconsulting.com ASAP. We will interview at short notice and are willing to be flexible on interview times - we are definitely serious about complementing our already-strong team with more of the best talent in the industry!
