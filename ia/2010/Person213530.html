Mark Wallace’s Summary

Mark most recently was Executive Vice President & Chief Financial Officer for HCP, Inc. (NYSE:HCP). HCP, an S&P 500 company, is a leading real estate investment trust (REIT) based in Long Beach, CA with a market capitalization near $15 billion and investments in over 800 assets. During his 5 year tenure, HCP's assets under management increased from $5 billion to over $15 billion and HCP was included in the S&P 500 index.

Prior to HCP, Mark concurrently served as EVP & CFO for Tremont Corporation (NYSE: TRE), a Denver holding company, and TIMET (NYSE: TIE), its largest operating company. Tremont’s portfolio of companies had annual revenues of $2 billion with interests in manufacturing, aerospace, metals, distribution, chemicals, insurance, and real estate. Mark led the company's international expansion, developed its six sigma and synchronous process initiatives, and implemented a global ERP system. During his tenure, the company's equity value increased from $100 million to $3.5 billion including the successful completion of an IPO on NASDAQ.

Mark also worked for Valhi, Inc (NYSE: VHI), a $3 billion Dallas diversified conglomerate with interests in restaurants, retail, chemicals, sugar, metals, real estate, manufacturing, natural resources, technology, and energy services.

His career started in the Dallas office of Arthur Andersen LLP where he represented clients such as Halliburton, Dresser Industries, Tracey Locke Omnicom, and The Marmon Group.

Mark is a certified public accountant and is active in industry and professional events. He has served on NAREIT's Financial Best Practices Committee, NAREIT's task force on industry reporting and disclosure issues associated with International Financial Reporting Standards, presented at SAP's Sapphire Conference, and is a frequent speaker and discussion leader at numerous engagements.

Mark A. Wallace is Director of a company involved with electronic medical records technology.
