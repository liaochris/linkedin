Kevin Whinnery’s Summary

Experienced web developer both on the client and server side. As a client side developer, is experienced with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Adobe Flex, Appcelerator Entourage, OpenLaszlo, and to a lesser extent Windows Presentation Foundation. On the server side, has built applications on top of Apache Struts, Hibernate, Ruby on Rails, Adobe BlazeDS, and custom Java EE servlet applications. Kevin is also well versed in creating native mobile and Desktop applications using Appcelerator Titanium.

In addition to listed professional experience, Kevin is an active writer in print, blog, and other electronic media. In his free time, Kevin enjoys spending time with his lovely wife and two beautiful children, and coaches high school football, wrestling, and (occasionally) baseball.

Kevin Whinnery’s Specialties:
