Lee Konecke’s Summary

Lee Konecke (MA, MFTI) is fast becoming one of the new leaders in the fields of creativity enhancement, personal development, and peak performance. Lee has an accessible, systematic and intuitive way to assist individuals and organizations to release the psychological clutter that so often blocks creativity, possibilities, and inspired action.

Lee holds a Masters Degree from The Graduate School of Psychology at Pepperdine University in California.
His passion is integrating the best of these Western and Eastern psychologies to establish creative ways for people to create a dynamic life.

As an addendum to his curricula he immersed himself in aggressive self-study programs to enhance and accelerate his understanding of the mind and human behavior. Some of these areas of interest include the creative mindset, organizational management, Buddhist psychology and Mindfulness meditation
Currently, while building his private practice, Lee has been increasingly sought after organizations, as well as entertainment professionals and creative talent. They recognize his unique talent for transforming destructive energies into possibility and inspired action. These are some areas where he has brought his expertise:

Organizationally: Office meeting…Inter-departmental communications…Change management…Conflict resolution…Injecting human centered qualities into product or services…Stress management

Entertainment & Talent: Keeping creative channels open both in front and behind camera…Stress management…Mediating between left brain and right brain entities…Creativity enhancement…Stage Fright…PR “spin”

His latest interest is in the field of “Deep Practice” and how this process, supplemented with meditation tools, can enhance talent and skill. This can be applied to any artistic endeavor, as well as athletic performance

Ready to immerse yourself in a safe and creative environment that’s all about getting to meet your potential? LET’S TALK.
