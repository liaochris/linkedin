Brian Weiner’s Summary

The Illusion Factory is an endless source of creativity from a company who finds its niche at the nexus of creativity, technology, media and entertainment.

Since 1979, The Illusion Factory has been entrusted with the promotion and marketing of more than $6 billion in filmed, televised and live entertainment. With more than 20 unique verticals in the company’s core business, The Illusion Factory has proven itself both creatively and technically, a master of all media. In addition, seven Illusion Factory satellite companies now compliment the core structure, maximizing efficiency in both core and satellite arenas via vertical promotions and an overlapping labor pool. We pursue excellence by use of cutting-edge creativity and tactical mobility, recognizing that the ability to adapt quickly and insightfully is mandatory, and that the heights of our past triumphs serve only as benchmarks to surpass.

In essence, The Illusion Factory is both a creative think tank where the idea is the preeminent goal, and a home to skilled artisans and technicians who can deliver upon that idea. Here, lucrative ideas have been produced in abundance, with many more being nurtured through development. Our list of clients includes every major film studio, numerous entertainment conglomerates, production companies, nonprofits, entrepreneurs and Fortune 500 companies. And, as a result of our flexibility, The Illusion Factory is currently poised to service an even larger percentage of this business population.

Over our 29 years, The Illusion Factory has won numerous prestigious awards and thrived on rapid evolution and innovation. While the emergence of new technologies is often challenging to a company’s infrastructure, the mercurial staff of The Illusion Factory has moved fluidly between very disparate tasks, demonstrating a tenacious talent to adapt. This core strength facilitates our ability to service an even broader range of emerging technologies and media platforms.

Brian Weiner’s Specialties:

Creative Concept Innovation. The Illusion Factory continues to be at the heart of countless new explorations of What if? Our specialty is to take on the impossible, the improbable and the doubtful and turn that into the expression of imagination that surpasses all else. We have no fear in taking on that which has never been done... it is all that we know how to do.

We negotiate to genuinely achieve a win/win dynamic with all of the people with whom we deal: employees, clients, and vendors.
