John McAughtry’s Summary

Former President, COO and EVP of Sales at early stage and public software companies on the west coast. I am now the President and CEO of a SF Bay area boutique search firm that places top performing individual contributors and senior executives with some of the best pre IPO and public companies in the enterprise technology market.

I have built high performance teams worldwide responsible for generating over $100,000,000 in annual revenues and have personally hired dozens of sales, marketing and customer service professionals in the US and around the globe.

Contact me at john@silveradoventuresinc.com, visit my website www.silveradoventuresinc.com or call 415 845 8152.

John McAughtry’s Specialties:

Having carried aggressive quarterly quotas for many, many years, I know that the only real secret to success in business is to hire the best people. Hiring and motivating winners is your best guarantee that you will meet and exceed your goals.
