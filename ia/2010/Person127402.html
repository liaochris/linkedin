Lora Boffa’s Summary

A "never settle for second best" Corporate Recruiting Manager, Corporate Recruiter and Expert Passive Candidate Sourcer who excels at "thinking outside of the box." Finding needles in haystacks is my mission. Thrive on challenging the status quo recruiting venues and prefer to go where no recruiter has gone before.

Founder of The Hiring Consultants website which brings together Corporate Recruiters who share a passion for effective resumes. The Hiring Consultants offers resume review, critique and revision services. We are not resume writers. Resume reviews involve a written critical assessment of existing resumes accompanied by a revised version to which our suggestions have been applied. The finished product will captivate the attention of Corporate Recruiters and after all, who knows better than another Corporate Recruiter? This is a very unique approach and transforms average resumes into excellent resumes - visit www.thehiringconsultants.com

Concurrently, actively seeking consulting contracts for corporate recruiting, healthcare recruiting or passive candidate sourcing and name generation.
