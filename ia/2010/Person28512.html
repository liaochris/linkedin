Brian Jacobson’s Summary

I help companies improve their go-to-market strategies, whether entering new markets, introducing new products, or reacting more effectively to changing market conditions. My current marketing focus is Social Media Marketing; the tools & technology emerging to support SMM; and key market segments using social and business networking services.

MARKETING – This is the foundation of my professional life. I have created and managed market research, competitive analysis, communications, strategic planning, and product development activities.

SALES – Selling more; more effectively is the purpose of being a marketing and sales professional. I have personally handled sales activities, managed sales teams, and served as an account development coach for business units, sales departments, and the sales teams of customers.

To survive, companies need new ideas … insights from other situations. You may need an agent of change … a strategist that can help you evaluate alternatives. There is no one right answer … but there are multitudes of mistakes that might be made. Let me help you try to avoid as many as possible.

You will come to know me as a Sales and Marketing professional with extensive experience in business development for technical products and services. Customers and associates say they value my ability to ask good questions, listen closely, and develop strategic insights from my observations.

My most recent industry experience includes professional services, information technology, custom manufacturing machines, and factory automation. Not long ago I was part of a not-for-profit group that established and operated a successful community ISP (Internet Service Provider). At the beginning of my career I focused on marketing, sales and information technology with Blue Cross-Blue Shield, other group employee benefit insurance companies, and a major CPA firm.
