Chen Yen, RPh, PharmD’s Summary

My vision is to help as many pharmacists as possible get connected with jobs that fit their personality and strengths. In addition to that, I want to see EVERY pharmacist reach a work/life balance. Link with me if this resonates with you.

I founded Pharmacist Job Connection two years ago (and our temp division RPh Temp Service in 2003). It is unusual to find a company like ours where a practicing pharmacist who understands pharmacists & pharmacy needs runs the company. Most recruiting companies are run by non- pharmacists.

Prior to that, while working as a pharmacist on several Indian Health Service reservations, I noticed that "contract pharmacists" were often referred to differently as regular pharmacists. The quality of contract pharmacists varied and the inconsistency was a headache for pharmacy directors. I decided to start RPh Temp Service (temp division of Pharmacist Job Connection) to provide carefully screened pharmacists (including those who have strong clinical background or are residency-trained) who loved to travel and wanted to serve the Native Americans.

We have inside connections to staff/clinical/management positions in hospital, long-term care, home infusion, ambulatory care, independent pharmacy, specialty pharmacy, pharmaceutical company, etc. settings.

My mission is for every pharmacist to be happy with his/her job; every pharmacy is staffed with the right pharmacist for their positions.

Be a part of my mission & vision! Look forward to connecting with you--Link with me :)
