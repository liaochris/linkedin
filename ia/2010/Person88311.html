James McCurdy, MSA’s Summary

My professional experience began in the non-profit world of government relations. From there I transitioned to Chamber of Commerce management, which was still non-profit but involved a high level of engagement with private businesses. As President and CEO I had maximized what opportunity there was in that career. Calling on my relationship building skills, I was offered a position in banking and served as branch manager at a large multi-national bank. I was successful there, bringing many new and profitable customers to the bank and earning "Business Banker of the Year" in my region. In 2007 I decided to pursue a M.S. in Accounting to add hard skill credentials to my resume. A banking client referred me to a public accounting firm, and they hired me as an accounting associate and had me hit the ground running in the areas of audit and tax. My goal now is to pass the CPA exam in 2010-11 and progress in my accounting career.

James McCurdy, MSA’s Specialties:

Accounting - specifically tax and audit, mid-sized corporate, individual, and non-profit tax preparation. Financial statement preparation. Consulting on internal control, accounting practices, business systems assessment and tax issues.
