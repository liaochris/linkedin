Soul Travelers III’s Summary

We are a digital nomadic family into our 4th year of an open ended world tour, blazing a trail for a new way of being in the 21st century! We live large on 25K a year and help teach others the keys to extended travel, mobile living, education on the road, early retirement and more.

I am writing a book & also write regularly for Huffington Post.

*National Geographic Traveler called Soultravelers3 "One of the best Family Travel Blogs in cyber space".

* We won 2 Lonely Planet Best Travel Blog Awards in 2009 for our travel blog as well as our @soultravelers3 Twitter acct.

*We are featured Case Studies in Tim Ferriss's NYT's #1 bestseller, The 4-Hour Workweek

* Featured Q&A interview in New York Time's Frugaltraveler

* Featured in the UK Guardian's, "The travel gurus' guide to 2010" plus many other national and international publications.

We have traveled to 4 continents, 32 countries, & over 175,000 miles (most overland) so far, traveling green & using every mode of transportation from cargo ships to camels!

We share experiential details, photos and award winning videos through our website plus connect and collaborate via social media web2.0 avenues.

Our Soultravelers3 Youtube Channel has had over 3 million views and Youtube is one of our Partners.

We also have taken thousands of disadvantaged school children with us virtually through the highly praised nonprofit , Reach The World.
