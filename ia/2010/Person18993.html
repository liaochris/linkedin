Anke Weisheit’s Summary

Generalist working in the field of Agroforestry, medicinal plant and tree cultivation, sustainable harvest of medicinal species, herbal medicine formulation & packing improvement, training, poverty reduction impact assessments, corporate identity development. Particularly interested in the integration of indigenous knowledge, practices and technologies in development and research. Planning to set up Institute of Indigenous Knowlegde (IIK) for the advancement of indigenous knowledge in research and development.

Anke Weisheit’s Specialties:

Integrated Natural Resource Management, Indigenous Knowledge & Rights, Rural Development (Agriculture, Natural Resource Conflicts), Development Practitioner, Herbalist Farmer Interface, Training Course Development, Poverty Reduction Impact Assessment, Participant Rural Appraisals, Proposal Development, Branding and Corporate Identity
