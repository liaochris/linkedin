Bipin Preet Singh’s Summary

I currently run a startup MobiKwik which is creating a new web/mobile platform for mobile services users in India. MobiKwik aims to be the Number 1 non-carrier destination for mobile services (voice/data/apps) in India. We do not compete with carriers, rather we use technology to bring mobile services to Indian customers in a faster, cheaper and friendlier manner.

In the past, I have worked as a system architect concentrating on camera, display, multimedia acceleration and Flash technologies for handheld devices. I have architected SATA and RAID6 controllers and tuned the benchmark performance of a high end chipset including an industry leading memory controller. Spent 3.5 years at Intel designing the cache coherence engine of a high speed server microprocessor.

I have deep exposure to the PC as well as Mobile phone system technologies including a taste for what is latest and the hardware software complexity involved.

I am a keen sportsperson with fanatical interest in playing and watching football. I have also been a lead trainer, motivator and content contributor in Janaagraha's flagship training program called CAP - Citizen Awareness Program.
