Brian Skiba’s Summary

A unique blend of investment banking experience including M&A banking, Global Strategy, Hedge Fund Management and Equity Research -- along with operational and functional management skills including former CEO and CTO of several software companies and current operational capacity at MaxID Corp.

A geographic mix of almost 10 years of equity research experience in the US, Europe and globally. Cross border technology M&A banking experience at Arma Partners. Prior to investment banking, a software entrepreneur. Examples of M&A transactions included the merger of Software AG and WebMethods, a $545 million deal, the sale of CapeClear to Workday, and the purchase of NetManage by Microfocus. Successfully completed over 40 capital market transactions -- IPOs, secondaries, and large-block placements totally more than $5 billion for technology companies.

Currently VP product development and Corporate Strategy at MaxID Group as well as a member of the board, and former Chairman for 2 years. MaxID is a leading provider of identity management products, with a particular emphasis on mobile computing/biometric ID company headquartered in Pleasanton, CA. Also board director and CFO/CTO of CatMacArt Corporation and Managing Partner at Treadstone Advisors.

Brian Skiba’s Specialties:

primary domain expertise in software, but extensive knowledge of the broader technology market with a particular focus on disruptive trends and technoplogies. Extensive published strategic work done over the past 10 years with thematic work on the emergence of the Cloud, SaaS, Enterprise mobility, Creative Destruction and communications industry changes and protocols.
