Beth Zimmerman’s Summary

Beth is founder and principal of Cerebellas LLC, a New York-based strategic advisory company that helps clients find hidden revenue potential in their business.

We're hired by CEOs or boards of B2B companies, and serve both nationally- and internationally-operating companies across industry and geography.

Ideal clients are organizations facing a minimum $5M pain or opportunity in their market. We turn pain into opportunity, and opportunity into strategic and sustainable revenue.

********

In 2010 Beth launched Pets for Patriots, a 501(c)(3) charity connecting loyal shelter pets with our country's loyal service men and women. The organization makes adult companion pet ownership achievable and affordable for US veterans, service members and wounded soldiers transitioning to civilian lives. In so doing, Pets for Patriots works to fulfill its mission of providing the gifts of fidelity, joy and companionship to both pet and person.
