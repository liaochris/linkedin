Kimberly Mahr Kwan’s Summary

Crafty from birth and with a professional background in motivational speaking and adult/motivational training, my creative professional resume includes stints as a designer and freelance artist for several product manufacturers and as a regular contributing writer and designer for many of the major industry magazines. I am a noted special events instructor at paper-arts trade shows and retreats across the country and internationally, and my over-the-top personality and slightly “off center” style bring a refreshing twist to paper and photographic arts.

I also maintain a growing portrait photography business in Scottsdale, AZ and Channel Islands, CA and have gained quite a notable following for my personality-filled natural light portraiture. My photography business can manage all of your personal and corporate identity portraiture needs, and with my background in technology and social networking, I am adept at working with marketing, graphic design and web development professionals.

I am bright, talented, creative and results-oriented professional; tenacious and able to thrive in a team environment both “virtual” and face-to-face. My extensive and well-rounded background of entrepreneurial, financial, project management, and leadership skills make me extremely effective in changing and improving the way an organization does business. This, coupled with a strong technology foundation compliments my solid grasp of management and business development. I am able to offer dynamic leadership, technical and business analysis expertise, as well as creative problem-solving skills to any organization. I enjoy interacting with people at all levels in a collaborative environment, motivating people and teams, and adding value to key initiatives of an organization.

I am currently enrolled in Graduate School to obtain my Masters of Science in Counseling/Community Counseling and hope to use this advanced degree to launch a leadership and corporate coaching business.
