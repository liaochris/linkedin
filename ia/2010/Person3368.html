Adrian Mott’s Summary

Consultant and project manager with 5+ years of customer facing experience. Deep technical expertise capable of enterprise product integration, training and reporting.

While at Black Duck, oversaw professional services engagements for M&A transactions and internal audits for large and small companies. Provided product integration training for internal teams at select clients and consulted on licensing obligations having to do with open source and other third party software within target code bases. Also helped to develop a support organization within the company and led the support efforts of an early stage startup company.

Co-founded a small web development company and worked to develop web applications, corporate websites and blogs for companies. Worked with co-founder to develop Unbiasd.com (now down), NYTExplorer.com (a search engine for the New York Times archives) and The Fresh Dish (a successful food and cooking blog). We worked on a number of corporate website redesigns for large and small companies. Also developed a successful personal technology blog focusing on Boston area startups and current technology issues.

Mac and Linux user - web and open source evangelist.
