Ed Jennings’s Summary

After 5 years as Naval Aviator and 33 years as Airline Pilot, I am continuing to serve my community through insurance sales. I am an independent agent, representing many different insurance carriers. My specialty has been in the Senior market, selling Medicare (health) insurance, Final Expense (Life) policies and Fixed Annuities, but I also serve the under 65 market with Individual and Family health plans, as well as Small Business Group health plans.
