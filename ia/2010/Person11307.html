Andree Dolan’s Summary

Andree Dolan is a marketing and sales professional with over 26 years of in-depth experience with creating and implementing innovative marketing and business development plans with an emphasis on growth, diversification, and customer loyalty programs. She has been part of the Microsoft partner channel for over 13 years, and since 2007 has been the marketing manager at The TM Group (www.tmgroupinc.com), a 20-year Microsoft Dynamics President’s Club partner focused on Microsoft Dynamics CRM, Dynamics GP, Dynamics SL, and Dynamics NAV.

Working closely with The TM Group’s management team, Andree is responsible for helping to create and execute comprehensive marketing plans for lead-generation, customer satisfaction, corporate branding, industry sponsorship, and referral partnership programs. She coordinates co-marketing opportunities with Microsoft’s marketing specialists and product managers, with a particular focus on results-driven campaigns blending traditional and new media tactics such as direct mail, telemarketing, tradeshows and events, public relations, e-marketing and social media.

At the World Partner Conference in July 2010, Microsoft named Andree and The TM Group as a prestigious Microsoft Distinction in Marketing Award winner for Vision 2010, The TM Group’s record-breaking 12th Microsoft Dynamics Conference and Expo.

Andree was also a founding member of Automation Alley’s marketing sub-committee and has served for the past two years as a co-chair of that committee in developing and running a series of educational marketing workshops.

In addition to her deep experience in the Microsoft Dynamics channel, Andree has been part of the sales and marketing teams at other Microsoft partners involved with custom web development, advanced infrastructure, systems integration, managed services and training.
