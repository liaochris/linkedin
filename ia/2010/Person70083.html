Jack Fitzpatrick’s Summary

Guidepoint Global is a subscription-based information service that supports institutional money managers. With access to over 100,000 top industry practitioners, Guidepoint Global connects clients to seasoned professionals who refer to as Advisors. Guidepoint Global differentiates itself by its adeptness at finding the “tough-to-find” industry specialist and its dedication to client service.

The Guidepoint Global Advisor network is a carefully selected global network of industry professionals and thought leaders. Advisors are connected to Guidepoint's clients, usually through telephone and in-person consultations, and are compensated for their time. Advisors receive several benefits to membership including insight and knowledge sharing with institutional investors, monetary compensation, and further networking opportunities.

If you would like to the opportunity to join our Advisor network or are interested in our services please feel free to reach out to me and I will be happy to answer any questions that you might have.
