John Hopkins’s Summary

I grew up in upstate New York and moved to Florida in 1986. I have spent the last 33 years of my professional life involved in the fields of insurance and law.

I am a former vice president of a legal professional liability insurance company, and have worked as a claims adjuster, arson investigator, and claims manager. While employed in the insurance industry, I designed and implemented risk management programs and wrote numerous manuals and articles on risk management and claims handling.

I have managed a medical-legal consulting company, a graphics arts company, and a scanning/database company. I have designed and implemented scanning and coding systems for law firms.

I am certified as an electronic discovery paralegal, Mr. Hopkins regularly teaches seminars and lectures on E-Discovery, research and investigation, and complex litigation management. I have worked in the litigation field on both the defense and plaintiffs’ sides of cases.

John Hopkins’s Specialties:

My areas of concentration include professional negligence, commercial litigation, mass torts and digital marketing.
