Damien Rottemberg’s Summary

I left the bank industry last year to try to build my own Internet business. I am passionate about new technology. I built for the last year 2 websites LocalBooking.com and DEALINIUM.com involving some technologies that can be used in the financial industry such as JAVA, C++, Pearl/SQL programming, push and mobile technology, real-time data, database modeling and multi-threading (parallel processing) application. I researched, developed and coded everything myself. My websites are now making some money. They are automated and self-managed. I have freelancers working on it now.

Prior to that, I was a flow trader at Deutsche Bank in Mexican Interest Rates and after that I was hired by Tullett Prebon to start the desk of Mexican Structured Interest Rates (swaptions, caps, floor...) over there. I worked in both positions between New York and Mexico City. During my time there, I developed a lot of models and systems and I automated a lot of their processes to improve the flow between back, middle and front office.

During my entrepreneurship, I learned not only new technologies but also management, marketing, communication and sales skills.

I am “fluent” in C/C++, Java, Pearl/PHP, Python, SQL and VB and I am excellent to automate task and reverse engineer anything. I am as well very familiar to system and network programming useful for optimizing low latency applications.
