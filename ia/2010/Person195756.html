Steven Zoernack’s Summary

As Executive Director of the African Peace Fund, Mr. Zoernack combines his extensive global finance and volunteer backgrounds to help Governments of African Nations manage their assets properly and partner with them to reinvest a portion of the Fund's profits directly into their economies, where needed most

We seek both the worthy projects in Africa to invest profits into as well as those within African Governments and large corporations in Africa who are in need of asset management and can make both project and financial allocation decisions.

Not all that is beautiful in the world is also practical....But here we have both. Africa is Emerging.

Steven Zoernack’s Experience

  • Executive Director

    African Peace Fund

    (Financial Services industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    GlobalHedge, Inc.’s flagship fund, the African Peace Fund, Ltd., is unique in that the Alternative Investment and Goodwill Fund reinvests profits into African Nations’ economies where needed most, whether it be to help maintain peace, improve global image, to build schools, infrastructure, medical facilities, to attract tourism, develop a financial center or assist in the cultivation and mining of natural resources. Rather than relying on the World Bank for additional debt, the African Peace Fund helps give citizens of Emerging Nations the confidence to reinvest in their own economy by opening up their markets to free trade and encouraging Foreign Capital.

    The African Peace Fund is both:
    1. An African Goodwill fund that reinvests its own profits back into the economies of African Nations, where needed most;

    2. An Alternative Investment Fund that helps African governments, sovereign wealth funds, corporations, religious organizations and certain key individuals in Africa manage money by attempting to generate higher than normal returns in both up and down stock markets for accounts of $25 million or more.
