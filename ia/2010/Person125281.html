Linda Chell Rooney, MIRM, CAASH, CMP, CSP’s Experience

  • Partner

    BeaconPoint Associates

    (Management Consulting industry)

    July 2009 — Present (11 months)

    Forming BeaconPoint Associates was the fulfillment of Tim and Linda Rooney's long-time dream of combining forces to provide an array of consulting services to include Project Management and Strategic Sales & Marketing Solutions to entrepreneurial companies and individuals, including homebuilders, consultants, freelance professionals, small and home-based businesses.

    As the economic winds of change began to blow in 2008-09, Tim and Linda realized the time for this strategic collaboration was now, as they witnessed professionals, business specialists, creative types, and others being affected in ways few had ever anticipated, with the New Economy creating a huge number of New Entrepreneurs in a relatively short period of time.

    BeaconPoint Associates combines Linda's award-winning marketing expertise and sales training/coaching/mentoring programs with Tim's impressive track record of business process and IT Project Management achievements into one extraordinary consulting firm focused on the client's success.

    Tim holds the prestigious Project Management Professional (PMP) designation of the Project Management Institute, as well as a Master's Certificate in Information Technology Project Management (MCPM) from The George Washington University School of Business.

    Linda is a Member of the Institute of Residential Marketing (MIRM), Certified Active Adult Specialist in Housing (CAASH), Certified New Home Marketing Professional (CMP), and Certified New Home Sales Professional (CSP) of the National Association of Home Builders (NAHB), as well as a licensed Real Estate Salesperson and REALTOR® for over 20 years. She has a long record of accomplishment in providing the marketing direction for several building and remodeling companies, a downtown merchants association, and many other types of businesses and organizations.
