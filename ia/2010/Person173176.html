Rick Liebling’s Summary

10+ years of experience in media/public relations, currently heading the Digital & Emerging Media group and the Client Services Department.

I provide strategic counsel to clients such as Allstate, Guinness, Jose Cuervo and Evinrude who are looking to enter into Social Media.

My philosophy is that brands need to practice Commitment Marketing within Social Media. Engage the consumer over the long haul by providing value. Social Media is not a short term play.

I also believe brands need to start thinking about Consumer ROI. As consumer's spend more than just money on a brand, smart marketers will start to measure the Consumer's ROI and develop strategies that provide more value, creating true brand evangelists.
