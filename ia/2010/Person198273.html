Suzanne Kovi’s Summary

Suzanne Kovi brings over 20 years of performance experience to every event. Her unique and expert insights in the area of personal performance has won her recognition in local and regional publications as well as film trade magazines. As an author, entertainer and fitness coach she captivates her audience with real life stories, real life challenges and real life solutions that will inspire and move you into action. Fear is not a choice.
Our goal is to ignite and inspire you to embrace you unique qualities and achieve your personal best with courage and purpose.
- Programs involve coaching in the area of personal performance ranging from business strategies, to parenting goals and fitness goals.
-We seek to partner with individuals and organizations to provide alternative tools to overcome stress and anxiety.
-To provide usable tools to release negative limiting actions and beliefs.
-To educate people on alternative health options to support a stressful environment and reduce the chances of adverse physical responses to that stress.
Ignite Your Life Inc is about educating, not judging. We know that each person has a unique situation; our goal is to help you, work on YOU.
