Eve Hernandez’s Summary

The manager of our Bentonville office, Eve Hernandez, is happy to have found a career that helps people feel better about themselves. “It makes a world of difference in their facial appearance,” she says. “A beautiful smile [gives you] confidence in talking to people.”


Eve was born in Mexico but she grew up in California. Except for a sister in Arkansas, most of her family still lives in California and Arizona. Most of her time outside of the office is spent with her two children, 18 year old daughter Lily and 13 year old son Michael. Eve relaxes watching Grey’s Anatomy and Dancing with the Stars. There are two Chihuahuas in the family too and their names are Jalapeno and Blondie.

Having recently undergone knee surgery, Eve is no longer able to play her favorite sport, volleyball…she can only watch. She can still shop, though, which is also fun for her and eating at Shogun is ‘high on her list.”

With a background in management, Eve sought employment through a staffing agency and that is how we found her. She has been the manager of the Bentonville office since it opened in 2004 and has high praise for him and her team: “I work with a great doctor [Dr. Mauro] because he is new to our team and has great energy and a very pleasant personality. And, our team works great together!” she says. If you come to our Bentonville office, you’ll be sure to see Eve. She will be happy to welcome you in English or Spanish and to answer any questions that will make your orthodontic experience better.
