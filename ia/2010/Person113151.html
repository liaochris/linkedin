Kristine Donatelle’s Summary

Experienced attorney/risk manager skilled in products liability, business law and litigation. Proven negotiation, advocacy, motion practice, project management and deadline-drafting skills. Effective at multi-tasking, anticipating risk, proactively managing risk, and achieving cost-effective dispute resolution.
Six years of experience as a civil litigator at Bowman and Brooke LLP representing national automotive, recreational and machinery product manufacturers in all aspects of litigation in many federal and state court jurisdictions nationwide. Negotiated numerous settlements in UCC-warranty, property damage and catastrophic injury cases; managed local counsel and litigation.
As solo attorney, provide variety of legal and contract services for law firms and clients alike including counsel on entity selection, business agreements, trademark registration, contract drafting, legal research, E-discovery, work product analysis, and pro bono estates planning and TILA loan review. Experience with corporate oversight as a board member of a family-owned business.
Admitted to Minnesota State Bar and U.S. District of Minnesota Bar in 2002. Magna Cum Laude graduate of William Mitchell College of Law. Ranked 18th in a class of 232 students, graduating in top 8 percent of my class.

Non-legal experience: Online news blogger and former award-winning journalist for regional daily newspapers. Experience producing promotional materials and event planning for a large-scale national civic event at Mpls Convention Center in 2008. Excellent writing, communications and management skills.
