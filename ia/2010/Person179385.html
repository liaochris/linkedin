Ryan Cady’s Summary

I am an experienced and award winning designer currently looking for opportunities in the realms of branding, interactive, print, and web design. I am open to relocation if the right opportunity were to present itself.

In the past I have worked on everything from touch based application interfaces, web games, informational cd-roms, publishing, website design, posters and more.

Clients I have done work for include (but are not limited to):
Target, Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, Al Franken for Senate, Microsoft, Saab, General Motors, The Detroit Urban Craft Fair, College For Creative Studies, Saturday Looks Good To Me, The City of Pontiac DDA, The Jewish Federation of Metropolitan Detroit, Chicago Artists Coalition, and McDonald's.

If you are interested in seeing some of my work please feel free to contact me.
