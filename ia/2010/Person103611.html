Jonah Light’s Summary

A 2001 graduate of the Pasadena's Art Center College of Design, I'm a professional commercial and special events photographer. While much of what I shoot happens to be in and around Southern California, I'm also happy to travel. I photograph law firms, corporate award banquets, non-for-profit gala events, weddings, children's fashion, lifestyle, senior citizens, high-profile hotels as well as concept-driven advertising campaigns for companies such as ESPN, Alpine Stereo, POM Tea and the LA Dodgers.

Jonah Light’s Specialties:

First and foremost, I listen to what my client needs. Then I make sure that the entire process is an enjoyable experience, from pre-production to the shoot itself, to all the back-end work. I also closely oversee image output, from digital image delivery via FTP to loose prints, from designing coffee table books and wedding albums, to large format printing as well as all levels of digital retouching.
