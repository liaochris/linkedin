Ian MacDonald’s Summary

Ian joined the party supplies, favors and decorations retailer as a part-time cashier. Nearly 10 years later, Ian has held such positions as Marketing Manager and Customer Service Supervisor while transforming the 60-year-old company from a brick and mortar store to a pure-play internet retailer.

In recent years, CenturyNovelty.com has doubled its sales year over year. In 2009 Century Novelty won numerous accolades, including selection as an Internet Retailer Hot 100 for innovation in e-commerce, Multichannel Merchant Magazine’s Silver Web Channel Award for website excellence, and the INC 500 as the 7th fastest growing company in Michigan & the 39th fastest growing retailer in the country. 2010 will be a great year too—we won the Silver Multichannel Merchant Magazine award for ecommerce innovation again and we ranked in the INC 5000 for the second year in a row.

Ian is responsible for managing all the day-to-day operations and strategic initiatives of Century Novelty including advertising, marketing, operations, fulfillment, customer service, and HR.

Additionally, I presented at the Internet Retailer Conference & Exhibition 2010 Chicago on how to select an order management vendor (http://bit.ly/9b0THd). And at eTail Baltimore 2010 I participated in a panel discussion on the unintended benefits of site search.
