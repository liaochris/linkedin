Donna Amos’s Summary

I love being on my own. Always independent and loving to create I have expanded my personal business coaching to provide a array of services to solopreneurs. http://www.donnaamos.com

A new application that allows me to help even more solopreneurs create a successful thriving business is Simple Marketing Pro. SMP is about helping solopreneurs be focused and consistent in their marketing efforts. Weekly webinars are offered to demo the program.
http://www.simplemarketingpro.com

A recent venture is an extension of my business coaching services. On September 29, 2008 I launched the International Association of Solopreneurs. http://www.solopreneurs.org

I first entered the coaching arena in 2001 when I began my training. Business Growth Solutions gave me the opportunity to combine my coaching skills and sales skills. Although only with them for a short time I learned a lot and highly recommend their programs.

I was with Coldwell Banker since I began my sales career in 1994. I moved into management in 1998. I was passionate about what I did and loved that no day is ever the same. Real Estate allowed me to use my skills of coaching and strategizing to help others grow. I will always cherish the time I spent in management with Coldwell Banker.

The years with Coldwell Banker and D & M Marketing gave me the experience and training to polish my sales skills.

While raising my three sons I was the principal at D & M Marketing. We provided direct marketing services to small business owners. Creating direct mail campaigns and providing the telemarketing services behind those campaigns.
