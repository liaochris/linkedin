Scott Becher’s Summary

Scott Becher
Becher Group
Phone: (608) 838-9115
Cell: (608) 513-0213

Scott Becher, has more than a decade of public relations and public policy experience on the local, state and national level. A published author and a former state capital reporter, he has extensive working relationship with national media outlets including The New York Times, creating widely attended state conferences and media events and having been quoted as a source in a variety of national, state and local publications including The Los Angeles Times and The Milwaukee Journal Sentinel. He has been honored for his extensive, and often groundbreaking public policy work by numerous organizations including the Wisconsin Department of Veterans Affairs and UW Health.

He has a track record of successful core interest groups relationship building, developing long range strategic marketing and planning for legislation, act as the senior public policy researcher and writing major policy presentations and media releases. His media approaches have leveraged over 90 national media stories in publications such as The New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Los Angeles Times, Washington Post, Roll Call, Chicago Tribune, USA Today, Christian Science Monitor and on CBS News, ABC News, CNN, MSNBC, Fox News and NPR’s All Things Considered.

Scott Becher’s Specialties:

Government relations, public relations management, fundraising, grassroot development
