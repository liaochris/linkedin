Duane Cutlip’s Summary

I am a licensed independent insurance agent here in North Carolina and I work with L. Harold Poole Funeral Service & Crematory in Knightdale, NC serving members of the surrounding community that wish to pre-plan their funeral arrangements.

I am also running for the NC House in district 39 to help break the one-party rule that has dominated North Carolina and brought double-digit unemployment and one of the highest tax rates in the southeast. It's time to return to a government that is OF the people, BY the people, and FOR the people!
