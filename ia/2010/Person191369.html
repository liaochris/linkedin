Luciano Costa’s Summary

Obtained a B.S. Degree in Hospitality Mgmt. from the U. of Alabama in 1996 with Cum Laude Honors and a Minor in Spanish.

Luciano has enjoyed creating unique events since high school. He created and organized one of the most successful fundraisers in school history and helped raise thousands for the school’s new computer lab. Later in college he was chosen by the International Students Association to coordinate a banquet for 300+ attendees for their Annual International Festival, the highest attended banquet since its inception.

In the Spring of 2006 he was commissioned as an event coordinator by the Lambda Basketball Association, for whom he helped raise over $18,000 during his “Hoop Dreams” fundraiser and made possible to send 4 local GLBT basketball teams to compete in the Gay Games in Chicago. Luciano’s team won the gold medal!

The following year Luciano formed SoCal Social Club, LLC-- now the leading social and business networking organization for LGBT professionals in Los Angeles.

The club's mission is to connect upwardly-mobile individuals and to enrich our lives with a variety of positive experiences in a sophisticated and welcoming environment that sets it apart from the typical gay nightlife. Year-round events presented by SCSC focus on career, arts & culture, community and recreation.

To learn more, visit WWW.SOCALSOCIALCLUB.COM

Membership is currently FREE and open to career-minded individuals, ages 21 and above. Register online to receive exclusive invites to upcoming events.

You may also follow us on Twitter (@socalsocialclub) and join our fan page on Facebook.
