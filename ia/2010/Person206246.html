TOM Merilahti (Tom@Sicusynergy.org)’s Summary

TOM is a Creative and Experienced Marketing Integrator and Communication Coordinator, who has been described as an Inborn Lateral Thinker, Self-willed Way Maker and Rebellious Forerunner. Train of thought: Think the Unthinkable, Move the Immovable, Expect the Unexpected and Connect the Unconnected!

Tom is "hunting" for new ideas, concepts, projects and clients with a mutual set of values, and "fishing" for synergies in order to be implemented in divergent operations and marketing programs.

SICU (“Synergy Society” since 1999), is an Unconventional Marketing Communication, Project Management, and Social Media consultancy, whose professional activities and services are interdisciplinary, innovative and stretch mostly beyond traditional thinking and acting.

SICU is specialized in creating and developing projects with multiple goals. The striving is to see things multidimensionally, and to bring in new perspectives, and an outside angle of vision even for existing projects and programs especially supporting Corporate Communication, Brand Marketing and Social Responsibility.

SICU HUB is a a sub-brand and a extension of SICU. It's a global connection and meeting point. In essence it’s an open and flexible "collaboratory" and a virtual organization operating through a large, interdisciplinary group of high level professionals, "collaborants" located all over the world.

The hub located in Helsinki invites all other capitals and regions around the world to join forces, and to benefit from collective intelligence, power and value creation.

The SICU and SICU HUB names, logo and slogan, Connects the Unconnected, are registered as intellectual property to prove copyright worldwide. The founder and sole owner is Tom Merilahti who has more than 25-years of professional experience.
