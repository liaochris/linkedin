Neal Marshad’s Summary

About Marshad Technology Group and Neal Marshad Productions

Marshad is an interactive agency providing advisory, creative and technological consulting services under the Marshad Technology Group and Neal Marshad Productions brands.

Marshad Technology Group is a technology consulting services firm providing best practices ecommerce strategic consulting solutions, award winning website design, high availability secure hosting, online ad campaign strategy management, website content management, cloud software, technical project management and development services. Marshad's clients seek to extend the reach of their brand and increase revenue through investing in technology with best practices strategy. Marshad Technology Group is a proven information technology solutions provider for the international commercial market. Clients include AT&T, BBC, Beiersdorf, Chanel, Coty, IBM, Mattel, Microsoft, Neiman Marcus Group, Sun Microsystems, and Wella.

Neal Marshad Productions is the Emmy Award winning TV production company credited with producing network television for over 25 years including award winning documentaries for PBS, primetime comedy for Viacom's Comedy Central, and films for NBC's Saturday Night Live, Nickelodeon's Nick at Nite, and CBS Television Studios. IMDB filmography: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1757190/maindetails

Marshad Enterprises, Inc. services clients primarily in United States, Asia and Europe.

Neal Marshad’s Specialties:

Strategy and production for interactive, ecommerce, and TV. Consulting for private equity and investment funds
