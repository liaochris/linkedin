Jonathan Karpfen’s Summary

Everything is DESIGN
Design is about PEOPLE
People are shaped by and identify with MEANINGFUL EXPERIENCES

I'm interested in PEOPLE -- what they do; when, where and how they do it; who and what they do it with; and why.

My goal is to always be playing a role in the DESIGN of the future -- of innovative technologies, objects and spaces; creating opportunities for MEANINGFUL EXPERIENCES that resonate with people's lifestyles while evolving the world in which they live.

I pursue these interests and goals professionally through my work in user experience, public opinion, and market research; product ideation, concept testing and prototyping; marketing strategy; business analysis and analytics; social media; mobile applications and technology; communications and PR; and business development.
