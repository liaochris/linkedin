Jenni Maier’s Summary

I started writing when I was three months old and I never stopped. I published my first novel at six and my first trilogy at nine. In fifth grade I was hired to ghost write a Babysitter's club book titled Kristy and the Myspace Scandal. Unfortunately it was never published. By the time I was 13, I had already turned down Oprah's Book Club endorsement six times. My notoriety, fame, and chronic allergies made high school hard and it didn't help when Universal bought the movie rights to my Live Journal. I graduated college with 19 New York Times bestsellers under my belt as well as an inability to read or write in cursive.

Jenni Maier’s Specialties:

constructing grammatically correct sentences, logging into Wordpress, avoiding vulgarity when unnecessary, creating lists about my talents, taking advantage of free WiFi, incorporating forgotten idioms whenever possible, telling the difference between a Craigslist posting that will get me a job or one that will get me killed, pro-bono blogging, utilizing exclamation marks to express appropriate amounts of enthusiasm, inspiring readers through heartfelt sentiments and necesary lies.
