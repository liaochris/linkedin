Chris Knipper’s Summary

What works today may not work for you tomorrow. We're on top of the technology so you don't have to be. Tell us where you want to go with your business and we'll bring the knowledge to get you there. We know inbound marketing, social media and seo, but we also know how to blend them with strategy and design to build the perfect vehicle for your success.

Kuno Creative offers businesses a team of marketing professionals for a lower costs than hiring additional employees. We are a unique marketing agency that takes a creative approach working on both Outbound and Inbound marketing strategy. Our Inbound strategy will help attract people to your website and convert them to customers. Our Outbound strategy will provide you with your own team of graphic designers, copywriters, web developers, project managers and media specialist. You’ll get better results and a higher return on investment by leveraging the expertise of our team while keeping your overhead low.
