Russ Volckmann’s Summary

The Integral Leadership Review has over 3000 subscribers (numbers growing daily) in more than 30 countries. Our goal is for the Integral Leadership Review to continue to occupy the pre-eminent position as an online publication, free to subscribers that is financed through sponsorships. We publish articles on leadership that are transdisciplinary, developmental, integral and/or multi-level in content. In addition, we publish interviews, book reviews and columns.

We also publish the Insights on Leadership series of books featuring conversations with executives, authors, and writers on the subject of leadership, as well as books by authors on various integrally informed topics.

I am currently affiliated with Union Institute and University, Saybook Institute, California Institute of Integral Studies, Fielding Graduate Institute, and others. I teach courses on integral leadership and sit on dissertation committees.

I have been editing ILR for seven years; prior to that I was the founding editor of Vision/Action, the journal of the Bay Area Organization Development Network, for five years.

I have PhD from U.C. Berkeley, was an organization development consultant for 22 years, an executive coach for the last ten years, a trainer and university professor.
