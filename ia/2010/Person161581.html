Paul Wylie’s Summary

Paul Wylie is a solution-oriented senior executive with 20+ years of experience in Operations and Supply Chain management. With a track record of adapting to and delivering results in diverse and rapid growth businesses -- entertainment (software and DVD), CPG and food -- he has recognized experience building and leading teams in high growth environments such as Genius Products, Herbalife and Activision.

He is skilled at re-engineering and transforming operations and supply chains to provide scalable, cost effective and efficient business solutions that are aligned with evolving business strategies and customer needs. With domestic and international supply chain and operations experience, he has leveraged his logistics, materials management, planning, and customer service expertise to increase profit, margin and cash flow.

Throughout his career, Paul Wylie has demonstrated strong leadership and vision -- combining strategy and execution with collaboration to build high performance teams in faced paced, entrepreneurial environments. During his tenure as SVP Operations at Genius Products he conceived and implemented the operational and supply chain strategy and infrastructure which supported a two year increase in revenues from $30M to $600M. As VP Global Operations at Activsion, he built and led a global team during a 4 year period when the business grew from $50M to over $500M.

A proven leader in organizations ranging from start-up to Fortune 500, Paul Wylie also earned an MBA-Finance from Syracuse University and a BE-EE from Stevens Institute of Technology.
