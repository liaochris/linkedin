Gary Samuels’s Summary

A Sales and Marketing Professional, Gary has over twenty years achievement in the IT industry and most recently in Search Marketing

An energetic individual, single minded and renowned for delivering results

Gary grew up in Southport, England and went to a local grammar school

A talented golfer Gary attended Wake Forest to further his golfing career and came away with a degree in history “I just bit off more than I could chew, Wake Forest were perennial NCAA Champions and I had to shoot -4 to make the squad when +12 that year would’ve got my tour card in Europe!”

Gary then became PGA Assistant at Chesterfield Golf Club then Shoreline GC in Mountain View CA

In 1987 Gary reluctantly let go of his dream of winning the Open and entered the business world. He then moved to Australia where he joined WordPerfect and catapulted WordPerfect Mac to 28% market share accepting the MacWorld award for most improved software 1991 on behalf of WordPerfect

1993 and Gary crossed the Rubicon, moving to Microsoft then in 1996 he returned to the UK to transform the fortunes of Pan-European VAD Azlan's Microsoft business

A 1998 move to Gartner came next and Gary won top New Salesperson in UK

Gary returned to Australia in 2001 to work for Meta Group then fulfilled his desire to help others as a sales trainer

In all of these roles Gary was able to influence positive change and provide innovative solutions to his many clients around the world both large and small. Gary stood out from the crowd due his exceptional ability to influence at all organisational levels. He articulated his vision with a rare combination of superior technical knowledge and a strong awareness of the business value of IT

A move into Affiliate and Search Marketing followed in 2006

Gary has recently established >clickoholics and is committed to building it into a respected business with a reputation for excellence and ethical practice that will become an industry benchmark for the future
