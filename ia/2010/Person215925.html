Daniel A. Wilson, PE’s Summary

Wilson Engineering Services, PC (WES) provides a full range of engineering and consulting services for projects and programs spanning multiple engineering disciplines. WES combines extensive experience in energy, environmental remediation, dredging, agricultural production, waste management and handling, and project and program implementation to provide clients with sustainable answers to energy- and environment-related issues.

WES provides the following suite of services for projects and programs: concept evaluation, feasibility level design and cost estimation, grant writing, permitting, engineering design, management consulting, construction management, and owners representative.

www.wilsonengineeringservices.com

Daniel A. Wilson, PE’s Specialties:

Wilson Engineering Services, PC (WES) specializes in renewable energy projects, particularly anaerobic digestion, biomass combined heat and power, biomass district heating, and waste-to-energy projects. WES is committed to developing energy projects that are economically, environmentally, and socially beneficial.
