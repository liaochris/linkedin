Ron Cseh’s Summary

Principal Broker for St. Andrews Insurance Brokers Ltd.

A Certified Insurance Professional (FCIP) and Canadian Risk Manager (CRM) with over 20 years experience in the areas of underwriting, account management, technology, risk management, and insurance sales.

Focusing primarily on commercial insurance sales for property, general liability, professional liability, and directors & officers liability.

Expertise in using the Internet to generate business and personal insurance leads and sales.

Licenced by the Registered Insurance Brokers of Ontario (RIBO) with an unrestricted broker's licence.

The following inquires are welcome:
-Insurance quotation requests
-Insurance or risk management questions
-Business opportunities (brokerage ownership, partnerships, alliances)
-Information on using the internet to promote your brokerage
