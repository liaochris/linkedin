Allen Draper, MA’s Summary

Effective marketers come in all shapes and sizes, as you can imagine. Yet, most have not had a decade plus to hone their abilities and repeatedly put said skill-set to the ultimate test with success. Primarily, I have successfully conceived and launched several businesses (including a patent-pending business model), and had recently instructed for a graduate program (Emerson College’s Department of Marketing Communication).

From my first foray into marketing to the present, I drive business results by leveraging my “honed” skill-set, relationship building, and a commitment to simultaneously manage and execute on diverse projects. Moreover, I am constantly practicing and learning to "connect the dots" with "New Media," maximizing communications impact , clarity and cost-effectiveness, while monitoring and managing them with "support tools."

My collective experience, as a former business partner at an advertising agency, a marketing manager two-times over, a brand manager, marketing strategist, account planner and director, entrepreneur and educator, has inevitably led to numerous success stories of brand building, and has inexorably made me an experienced project manager. Thus, I see every business challenge, not just as familiar territory, but more so as an opportunity to excel and exceed expectations.

If you seek to continually drive measurable results for your company in these economic and “media-dynamic” times, relying on a marketing professional without a history of successes and a forward-thinking mindset would be less than the ideal. My experience includes working in various industries including wireless communications, medical devices, homegoods, healthcare, software technologies, outdoor sporting equipment manufacturers, system integration, and many professional services.

My goal is to arm those I work for with a cogent, methodical, and fiscally measured integrated marketing communication program incorporating traditional and new media.
