Martin Kleppmann’s Summary

Martin is a highly motivated, efficient and personable technologist and entrepreneur. With 12 years of experience in developing web applications, proven project management and team skills, and a strong academic background, he is constantly striving for shared success in his work with clients and partners. Martin approaches his projects passionately, realistically, with creative and analytic thinking and highest integrity.

Martin's first startup was Ept Computing, founded in 2007, which produced Go Test It (http://go-test.it/li) – the best way of testing your website automatically across different browsers. Go Test It enables you to easily record stories of how a user interacts with your web app, to edit these collaboratively online, and to play them back on a cloud-hosted infrastructure. The company was acquired by Red Gate Software in November 2009.

The second startup Martin is involved in is Rapportive (http://rapportive.com), a simple social relationship management tool which plugs right into your email. When you're replying to an email, you can automatically see that person's recent tweets, LinkedIn info and blog posts. Rapportive also integrates with the services you use to run your business: your existing CRM, helpdesk, email marketing and feedback tools, making all of these available right within your email. With Rapportive, you immediately get a full picture of the people you communicate with, which allows you to provide excellent service with less effort.

Martin also speaks at conferences from time to time: http://www.speakerrate.com/speakers/2924-martin-kleppmann
