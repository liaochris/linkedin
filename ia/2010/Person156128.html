Noor memon’s Summary

Mr. Noor Mohammed Memon has more than 8 years practical experience in Health Insurance Field with Local and Multinational Insurance Companies.

Mr. Memon has cleared Master of Arts, MCSE (Microsoft Certified System Engineer) and A+ Certification.

Presently Mr. Memon is working as a Regional Health Underwriting Manager with AXA Insurance (GULF) BSC (C) – Dubai, since October 2008. He is responsible for New and Renewal Business, Performance Review Reports, MIS Reports and data analysis.

Mr. Memon has also worked as an Assistant Underwriter with National Health Insurance Company – Daman, Abu Dhabi from September 2006 to October 2008. He was responsible for New and Renewal Business terms, Weekly and Monthly KPI and PPI Reports, Monthly HAAD Financial Reports.

Earlier, he worked as Assistant Underwriter, with Alliance Insurance, Dubai, where he was responsible for small and large group quotations, monthly reports to Government authorities, PowerPoint presentations for clients based on their loss ratio and scheme performance.

He has also worked for 3 years with Allianz-EFU Health Insurance Company, Karachi, Pakistan, where he was working as Executive Officer in Underwriting and Claims Department. He was responsible for Inpatient Outpatient claims, MIS Reports, Policy Generation using MCare software.

He has participated and attended various professional trainings.

He has computer competence in - systems, applications & Products like Snag IT -8.2.3, MS-Office (MS-Word, MS-Excel, MS-Power Point, MS-Outlook), windows x/xp/Vista, Internet and E-mail, MS-Visio, MS-Projects. He has worked as data analyst for various projects sponsored by the World Bank, Save the Children Fund, and International Water Management Institute (IWMI), Pakistan National NGOs like Thardeep Rural Development Program, Sindh Agricultural & Forestry Workers Coordinating Organization (SAFWCO).
