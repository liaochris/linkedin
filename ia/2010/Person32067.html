Maggie Mistal’s Summary

CNN recently called me, "one of the nation's best known career coaches." I have appeared several times on CNN Newsroom with Kyra Phillips and Alina Cho as a career expert.

I am a certified life purpose and career coach and host a weekly nationally syndicated radio show. "Making a Living with Maggie" airs every Wednesday at 4pm eastern on SIRIUS 112 and XM 157. I have interviewed some of the most successful names in their fields including Martha Stewart, Sally Field, Deepak Chopra and Cynthia Rowley.

Prior to coaching and radio I was Director of Learning & Development for Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia focusing on management and career development.

But my passion is my career consulting practice, working with individuals to identify their ideal careers and allowing them to make their career change. Unsatisfied employees of all levels and backgrounds seek out my coaching to get their careers on track. Top corporations such as PepsiCo, Credit Suisse, CIGNA, Diageo NA, and Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia also seek my help in developing their employees and managers.

My experience has positioned me to speak at leading events for career-minded professionals. In addition to coaching, radio and speaking, I have been featured in the alumni profiles of Villanova Magazine, my Alma-mater. I have also been quoted in articles for the New York Times, the New York Post, the Washington Post, FoxBusiness.com, WallStreetJournal.com and the Boston Globe.

I am also an affiliate coach of Vocation Vacations -the only company of its kind dedicated to enriching people’s lives by allowing them to test-drive their dream jobs.

You can also find me volunteering for Streetwise Partners using my career coaching services to help the unemployed. With my spare time, I sing cabaret and my show is titled “Follow Your Dreams."

Maggie Mistal’s Specialties:

I help people manage and develop their careers both individually and within companies through my career coaching practice, my weekly SIRIUS/XM radio show and TV appearances on stations like CNN. My areas of expertise include career coaching, career change, helping people find their dream jobs and career development within corporations.
