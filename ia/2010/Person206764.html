Tonya Signa’s Summary

Thank you for visiting my LinkedIn profile! I founded Signature Marketing Services in 2004 after 15 years experience in corporate account management, sales support, and business development for a Fortune 500 company. I have worked with both product and service companies to create pivotal growth opportunities, cultivate relationships with key decision makers, and support their overall sales and marketing efforts.

Our expertise is in positioning our clients to have valuable, insightful sales conversations with executive-level decision makers. We connect sellers of complex solutions to potential buyers, ultimately speeding the process of turning prospects into customers.

By executing the critical “top of the funnel” activity, our clients have stronger sales pipelines, more visibility into their markets, and more predictability in their forecasting.

Our services include:

• Development of engagement strategy: targeting, positioning, prospect contact plans, and metrics
• Outbound engagement to initiate a dialogue, generate awareness and interest, identify opportunities, and gain agreement for a sales meeting.
• Execution of initial sales meeting (via the phone) to introduce solution, uncover challenges, discover insights critical for a successful next step, and ultimately qualify the opportunity.

The end result of our services is a sales team investing their most precious commodity, their time, developing relationships with people most likely to become clients.
