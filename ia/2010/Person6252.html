Alexandros (Alex) Trimis’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur at heart, with the mindset of an engineer and the creativity of a kindergardener. I love ideas that challenge our way of thinking and push us to discover new ways of seeing and interacting with our surroundings.

I have no professional title and I think I will never do. I am not a fan of titles after all. What I do have, is a passion for innovation, business development, business strategies, user experience, captivating presentations, elegance, design, architecture, photography, dance, theatre, cinema, snowboarding, sailing etc. I don’t think that we are born to be something specific, but to combine the attributes of life we enjoy in our everyday and professional life. That is what I do and that is what I will always do.
