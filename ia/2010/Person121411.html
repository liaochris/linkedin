W. Lawrence Wescott II’s Summary

W. Lawrence Wescott II is a licensed attorney and an IT professional with over 10 years of IT managerial experience. He is currently a senior consultant with Kahn Consulting, Inc., a consulting firm specializing in the legal, compliance, and policy issues of information technology and information lifecycle management. He also advises attorneys on electronic discovery issues, focusing on helping them understand their client's information systems. He maintains the Electronic Discovery Blog (www.electronicdiscoveryblog.com), named a 2007 Top 100 Legal Blog by the American Bar Association Journal. He is a member of the Sedona Conference's Working Group 1 on electronic document retention and production. A list of his publications and presentations is available at www.electronicdiscoveryblog.com/?page_id=2.
