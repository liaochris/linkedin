Joe Cormier’s Summary

As a small business owner, I understand how important your network infrastructure is to you. When your server is down, it means loss of productivity and revenue.

Ion Networking’s technicians have extensive experience with numerous aspects of computers and networks and can efficiently resolve any of your technology issues. Our technicians can guide you through the sometime confusing choices - they can help with a new server install, removing viruses, recovering lost data or implementing a disaster recovery strategy.

Ion Networking provides Network Management service contracts that can be customized to your unique needs. We can provide full or partial computer network support at a fraction of the cost of a full-time administrator.
