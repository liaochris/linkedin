Jack W Bruce Jr.’s Summary

BIS Benefits is an employee benefits firm located just north of Atlanta in Alpharetta, GA. Leveraging our extensive experience and benefits knowledge, we are a team of insurance professionals who help our business & non-profit clients find the best benefit plans for their employees. We deliver employee benefits with experience, excellence and execution to organizations with as many as 800 employees down to 2 employees. In addition to group benefits, we also assist individuals and families in finding the right medical coverage.

At BIS you don’t just get a broker, but an experienced team that is uniquely qualified to meet the increasing needs of current and potential clients. This team includes key players with expertise in marketing, claims, HR, & technology solutions. Our skilled staff provides attentive, dependable service and decisive guidance regarding a variety of benefit-related functions. This includes shopping the market for competitive rates and products, enrollment meetings, and new product presentations, along with claims and administrative troubleshooting.

As COO I provide general oversight to our firm in Finance, HR, Operations, & Strategic Management. My role is to continually lead our firm by proactively enhancing our service, communication, human resource assistance, and technology for the benefit of our clients.

Recent examples of our continued enhancements include the addition of Human Resource consulting and the hiring of bilingual staff to assist our clients with Spanish speaking employees.
