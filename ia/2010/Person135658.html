Martin Schobert’s Summary

I am the managing director of the consultancy tourismusdesign. We are engaged in product development (experience design, landscape architecture, service design) and communication consulting (storytelling, online-pr, social media design) of touristic organisations!

Before I experienced "How to communicate a touristic product" by working for the Austrian National Tourist Office for 12 years. After different marketing jobs in Vienna and Paris I have been heading the marketing- and pr-team for the domestic market for 6 years till April 2007. From Mai 2007 to Oktober 2009 I have been Head of Research & Development leading the teams "Internet Strategy", "Tourism Research", "Business Intelligence" and "IT Service & Support", in this capacity I was acting as CIO of the company. My final management position was "Head of Internet Strategy". During the last 3 years acting in these management positions I was responsible for the development of the worldwide online- and social web strategy of the Austrian National Tourist Office.

Martin Schobert’s Specialties:

Storytelling, Online PR, Social Media Design are the recipies for successful (online-)communication in tourism.

Service Design is a methode to improve experiences of guests and travellers in focusing on the customer journey through a touristic service. What I learned the past years is, that you "cannot do no communication", which means that it is easy to bring your message to your target group if your product stands for the values and unique selling proposition of your touristic brand itself.
