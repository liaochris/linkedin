Paul Pittman’s Summary

International Human Resource exec with broad experience in all aspects of HR management.

With Boards of several international companies developed global reward and talent management strategies, led people aspects of several multi-billion dollar M&A transactions and IPOs in multiple countries, Ceo, board director and executive management search, global risk management strategies for employee benefits and pensions liabilities, developed break through mobility and talent pipeline programs.

Works directly with major corporate clients and other HR suppliers to round out talent management deliverables and improve market reach. Direct experience with extraction, natural resources, service, manufacturing, packaging and FMC, professional services and broadcasting industries.

Worked and lived globally.
