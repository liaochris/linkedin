Amy Bomar’s Summary

Strategic Approaches. Innovative Ideas. Proven Results.

As a full-service strategic public relations and marketing firm, Bomar Marketing Solutions, LLC, caters to clients who value individual attention and personalized service. By choosing Bomar Marketing Solutions to meet your communications needs, you’ll receive direct access to the firm’s president and senior strategic counselor, Amy Bomar, an experienced practitioner committed to client service excellence.

We believe in the importance of strategy-driven marketing. From research to project execution, we use a strategic, straightforward approach in accessing your goals, developing key messages, identifying target audiences and developing a thoughtful and cost-effective plan to communicate the right message to your target audiences.

Amy Bomar has worked with both local and national media outlets such as Good Morning America, CNN, ESPN Radio, the Today show and many others. She has written articles that have appeared in national publications including U.S. News & World Report, Good Housekeeping, Redbook, Town & Country and more.

Throughout her career, Bomar has served clients in a wide range of industries including health care, manufacturing, finance, tourism, insurance, retail, hospitality, education, legal, social service, engineering, homebuilding, Internet start-ups, retirement communities, and others. Bomar is accomplished working with professionals at all levels including CEOs of companies, university presidents, physicians and attorneys.

While her career achievements may be impressive, Bomar's real reputation is built upon her ability to achieve real business results for her clients.
