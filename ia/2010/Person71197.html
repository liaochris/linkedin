Franklin von Winckelmann’s Summary

History VonWinckelmann
In the fourth year of the third millennium, the last descendant, Franklin, of the aristocratic Von Winckelmann family decided to immortalize his noble family name by founding an innovative brand. The family name has been written in the original 16th century script and the authentic greyhound of their dignified coat of arms has been stylized into a logo for the 21st century. Together they characterize an unconventional brand stemming from classical traditions.

History worn organic by VonWinckelmann
To emphasize its involvement in the environment VonWinckelmann is taking its message an important step further. The year 2006 marked the date that VonWinckelmann and worn(r-)evolution started to focus on developing and producing fashion apparel in a honest and Fair Trade manner.
In collaboration with Dutch Design in Development Franklin von Winckelmann had the priviledge, in 2007, of acting as advising consultant to a Paraguayen textile company that supplies international customers with organic cotton from seed to the manufacture of consumer products.
After the completion of this project, he chose to specialize in the production of organic, sustainable and fair trade fashion apparel. In cases where the implementation of this is not possible fair trade conditions are accomodated and projects to accomplish environment and labor control are initiated.
The VonWinckelmann collection has been produced according to the GOTS: 100% organic cotton, sustainable and Fair Trade.

worn(r-)evolution
Created and manufactured on one of the most exotic Indonesian islands, Bali. The worn(r-)evolution collection is the refinement of a melting pot of elements forming a synchronized entirety. This progressive jeans & casual brand establishes it’s identity through the use of authentic techniques and details integrated in each style. Worn(r-)evolution is produced according to fair trade conditions.
