Jennifer Derryberry Mann’s Summary

I write magazine articles. My work has appeared in FitPregnancy, Pregnancy, Spirituality & Health, Natural Solutions, Modern Bride Connection, and on MSN.Match.com. I also write for Corporate EVENT, FenderBender, and other trade publications.

When I'm not writing for magazines, I help small- to medium-size businesses write and edit their web sites, business plans, newsletters, and marketing materials.

Before I began exclusively freelancing in 2006, I worked at Vance Publishing (1995-1998), the American Nuclear Society (2003-2004), and Experience Life magazine/Lifetime Fitness (2004-2006).
