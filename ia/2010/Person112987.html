Kate Burdick, SPHR’s Summary

KBHR offers a wide range of human resource contracting and partnering services to small and mid-sized companies to help them get to where they want to go. Whether you need one or all of our services, on a per-project or fulltime basis, KBHRSolutions offers customized solutions for of all your HR needs so that YOU can focus on what you do best – running and managing your business.

Clients call KBHR Solutions when …
• They need to update or improve capabilities for identifying and recruiting new talent
• As a small or emerging business, they only need Senior HR assistance a few hours a week
• Job leveling and pay scales (compensation systems) are not clearly defined
• A small or early-stage company needs HR infastructure, templates and departmental leadership
