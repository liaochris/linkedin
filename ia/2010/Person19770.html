Barbara Assante’s Summary

In 2003, she started a film finance firm, Baja Entertainment, where she assists A list producers with independent films and slate film funding finance up to $500MM. She has relationships with the top media financing institutions, hedgefunds, distribution companies, producers and lawyers in the industry. She has relationships with the top Reality TV producers in the industry as well. Throughout the past she has been invited to speak at a number of film financing seminars, ie. The Jacob Javitz Center Photography Expo (2004 NYC) Sundance Film Festival (2006 Park City, Utah), and was a judge for The 23rd Avignon Film Festival (2006 in Avignon, France). She also manages up and coming music artists, actors and actresses.

Barbara Assante has spent 15 years as a business and finance consultant. Over that time she has worked in real estate, finance, marketing, technology, non profit, entertainment, automotive, landscape construction, technology, restaurant, retail, clothing and investment banking industries.

Some general responsibilities varied from providing sophisticated financial solutions to high-net-worth individuals as well as small- to mid-sized businesses; driving new revenue streams by generating new business opportunities;accounts payable & receivables, payroll, HR, employee training, department management, management of fundraising events and programs, expansion of existing support base, recruitment of event participants and sponsors; design and production of marketing campaigns, organizational development and improvement, communications and public relations initiatives, crisis management, develop good media relations, plan, drive and execute business strategy by developing events and visual merchandising plans; attract, hire, develop,and retain talent; analyze the business and develop the strategies and plans to achieve sales results through business analysis, action planning, effective communication.
