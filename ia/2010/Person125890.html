Lisa C. Clark, MBA’s Summary

I'm an energized, global marketing MBA C-level leader driving strategic and tactical execution in consumer and technical markets, start-ups to Tech 100. I ideate/design products, create/extend/revitalize brands, highlight shopper marketing and customer experience, enter markets, ID core opportunities, open channels and build teams.

- As VP of Marketing at SIMtone Online, Div, of SIMtone Corporation, I designed a cloud computing TaaS and go-to-market targeting K-12 education + consumers
- Founding/launching the Thinker Clothing(tm), "Knowledge as Your Style"(tm) brand highlighting Science, Tech, Engineering and Math ("STEM"), worn on "The Big Bang Theory", presented at the 2009 Oscars, selling online to customers in North America, Europe and Australia, and invited by national retail (full creative portfolio at www.Coroflot.com/LisaCClark)
- Contract CMO strategy/messaging for mobile social network start-up, and for Impulse Mobile patented SaaS development and capital raise
- Themed retail strategy/design as Founder in international home goods and gourmet groceries
- Architect of Sun Microsystems' Campus Dealer Channel, first retail distribution
- Fortune 500 CPG new market entry consulting
- Coaching/advising at management/C-level across verticals.

I'm an effective leader, collaborative team player and loyal lieutenant, and seek management or advisory opportunities in disruptive and game-changing markets making global impact, especially education, SaaS, cloud computing, wireless, genomics/neuroscience and clean energy, with established or funded ventures.

An invited management consulting member of Gerson Lehrman Group, I've also been a repeat guest speaker on Australian Web radio human behavior programs, have published online by invitation at MommyMasterminds and Kalon Women, and am a member of the McKinsey Online Executive Panel.
