Gary Blume’s Summary

As a member of the senior leadership team at Lightwave Security, Gary is focused on the firm’s mission of defining business leadership in IT security, risk, and compliance. By introducing military principles to the corporate experience he brings a unique perspective on staff development and corporate leadership. He is a recognized thought leader in the field of strategy development. His leadership and passion have become hallmarks of a successful career.

Gary's hallmarks are the development and implementation of go-to-market strategies that drive business using an integrated approach that includes building tactical pipelines, cultivating relationships, brand vision,direct and viral marketing, and structuring affiliate (channel) and strategic partnerships.

His approach is to collaborate with and among the business development activities, marketing and PR inititatives that drive the achievement of defined objectives and goals.

He is a passionate proponent of innovative technologies that solve real business problems, always focus on providing a first-rate customer experience, and engineering broad based marketing, sales, and channel campaigns.

His strategic alliances partners will tell you there is no one better or more thorough in the industry!
