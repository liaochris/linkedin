R.K. McDaniels’s Summary

Immediate goal is to work from home as a personnel recruiter, but more so to become a motivational speaker/consultant.
I recently added seventeen months of customer service to my experience with excellent recommendations from manager and team lead/supervisor.
Previously, I have also been certified as a Cable Data Technician through Micor Communications by Cox Communications out of Arizona, qualified in Digital Cable TV and broad High Speed Internet for installation, servicing & quality control.
I have sixteen years' experience as a professional typographer having used several different dedicated typesetting systems as well as QuarkXPress, Pagemaker, Photoshop, Illustrator & Freehand.
I am an internet research specialist who has been certified as an Information Specialist I by state of Florida exam.
I have extensive experience in proofreading, editing, 4-color mechanical paste-up, layout and formatting, as well as being professional in use of QuarkXPress for desktop publishing of any kind. - Also have excellent skill in photo restoration and reproduction.
Ongoing personal goals include continuing to teach deep meditation & helping others in their spiritual goals and interests.
