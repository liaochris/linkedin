Anjelica Hebron’s Summary

With the completion of Master’s Degree in Human Resources from Harvard University, I joined Winter, Wyman & Company as a consultant in the technology division—focusing exclusively on the NY market. Our work spans across agile start-ups to established fortune 500 companies, stretching throughout different industries and diverse corporate cultures. We have a history of uncovering and representing top talent from the following candidates: Software Engineers, Project/Product Managers, Technical Architects, Web Developers, and Director/VP/CTO’s.

I welcome the opportunity to connect with savvy technologists, who are interested in chatting about the market or learning about that next career move. Our motto especially rings true in this current economic crisis, that the best time to evaluate new opportunities is when you are not actively pressured to do so.

I am open to receiving invitations at: ahebron@winterwyman.com. If you are looking to passively or actively discuss career advancements opportunities, I can be reached directly at: 888- 657-5493

Please note, if you send me your resume for consideration it will not leave my office, everything we do is strictly confidential.
