Travis Senenfelder’s Summary

I am a highly knowledgeable, professional and approachable Realtor specializing in buying, selling and leasing properties in the Western suburbs of Minneapolis. With over 10 years of experience, I understand the challenges and concerns people face when purchasing their first home, upsizing or downsizing, or adding properties to your real estate investment portfolio. That’s why I use a unique and highly detailed approach to truly understanding my clients’ needs. With this information in hand, along with extensive knowledge of the industry and Minneapolis’ Western suburban area, I can find exactly the right properties and negotiate the best deals for my clients.

For no-obligation answers to questions about buying, selling or leasing a property, feel free to contact me.
