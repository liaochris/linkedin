Andrea Halley-Wright’s Summary

From 1999 to 2001 I served as SVP, Director of Account Management & Business Development for Young & Rubicam Miami. Under my leadership the company’s diverse services where bundled into client centric, single source solutions for superior integrated marketing management.

As Executive Communications Director at LPG Miami I implementated and activated all marketing communications plans. Driven to create synergies between offline and online advertising, promotion, direct marketing and customer relationship management programs; I propelled the French based ‘leader in connective tissue’ company into a strong niche position in the US health and beauty segment.

In 2003, I was appointed by The Agency to lead the South African Tourism account. For some three+ years I led a ten person global team charged with creating and implementing integrated campaigns to build leisure and business tourism from the Americas, Europe and Asia Pacific. By creating win/win partnerships, like those with FIFA and National Geographic, exponential growth was achieved. Billings tripled by year three as tourist conversion doubled.

Currently I am a consultant at Mindmarks, responsible for leading the strategic business development initiative to expand the brand and activation consultancy; servicing both local advertising agencies as well as Fortune 500 clients across the globe. Responsibilities include both off line and online brand/marketing strategies, marketing programs and activation/implementation of campaigns. Targeting both B2C and B2B, our clients include: Newport Property Ventures, Jarden Consumer Solutions, South African Tourism, JWT, Starmark, Beachgroceries.com and SitOrSquat.com. As a consultant I have led SitOrSquat integrated marketing efforts to build the brand and user base as well as conceiving and implementing cost effective integrated partnership programs with sponsors and advertisers.
