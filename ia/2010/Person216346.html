Won-Taek Na’s Summary

My professional experience can be divided into largely two parts. One is as a network engineer, and the other is as a software engineer. During my positions in network related jobs, I think, I had built strong understanding of the system architecture and its over-time evolution definitely from the network point of view. One of my distinctive approach, I remember, was a quantitative analytics bolstered by my sound background of mathematics, the effectiveness of which actually had resulted me in earlier promotions.
After joining the new company, SECUi.COM, from the starting point, I'd changed my focus more on network devices themselves as opposed to their operating environment. With the understandings of diverse system architecture, I could design my own system of special purpose, firewall, which is usually used to block unwanted or ill-willed traffic through examining policy matchings or abnormal behavior findings.
I had contributed to this company's major product NXG (one of quite early versions of world-wide Gigabit firewalls) by designing overall architecture and providing idea of two very competitive features.
Though showing competency strength holding number 1 market share status in Korea, NXG failed to achieve world-class level status, which is related with one of my life-time professional goals; being an member of a team leading technology globally.
After searching for a second chance of challenge, I found CDN which might enable me to cross the border as a service provider and which has a considerable relationship with my previous job experiences; network, security and performance-oriented system architecture. So I have begun another career here at CDNetworks which is one of big 4 CDN service providers (AKAMAI, Limelight, Level3 and us) in the world.
