Steven Lucas, PMP’s Summary

As a senior management consultant, project manager and solutions architect, I have helped clients across a wide spectrum of industries including manufacturing, pharmaceutical, insurance, financial services and local government.

With over 15 years of experience, I have led projects focused on custom application development, packaged software implementation, infrastructure, e-commerce, process design/improvement, and methodology deployment. While serving in senior leadership positions within several consulting firms, I have managed teams of up to 130 consultants and project portfolios with budgets exceeding $15M providing executive oversight and customer relationship management.
