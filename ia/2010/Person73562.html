Gil Bashe’s Summary

Executive Vice President and Health Practice Director at Makovsky + Co. Associated with many significant product launches and support campaigns including Aricept, Augmentin, Cardiolite, Coumadin, Genotropin, Levaquin, Lovenox, Niaspan, Percodan/Percocet, Sinemet/Sinemet CR and Tagamet.

Before joining Makovsky, served as chief executive of Health!Quest Global Communication Partners, funded by GTCR Golder Rauner, one of the nation’s largest private-equity firms, managing $4.5 billion in active assets.

Formerly Group Company CEO, CommonHealth, a WPP Group company, and Executive Vice President, Worldwide Health Practice Director, Hill and Knowlton (WPP Group).

Held positions with Medicus, a global health marketing company, and Sutton Healthcare Group. Served as pharma industry lobbyist with New Jersey Health Products Council.

Co-editor of the book Branding Health Services: Defining Yourself in the Marketplace.

Gil Bashe’s Specialties:

Health product marketing and public policy, advocacy, employee communications, and issues and crisis management including recalls/withdrawal, staff redeployment, pricing, reimbursement and product-approval campaigns.

Expertise in multiple therapeutic categories and device and diagnostic medicine.
