Scotty More’s Summary

It's the Glue!

Your Social Network is the "Glue" that connects your online relationships together - thus becomes the basic building blocks of your "Viral Sphere." Your "Viral Sphere" is your Twitter, Blog, Landing page, Website, Facebook, YouTube and the world of other Social Networking sites and apps that weave your online relationships together.

Not meant to replace your current and successful advertising and sales programs, Social Network Marketing can drastically improve your brand identity and reach of your products or services. When used properly, Social Network Marketing allows you to have a continual conversation with your customers and prospects - putting you in control of your message 24/7! It is your choice as to how to use your Viral Sphere! Even the smallest of companies can be brand leaders!

The great news is, Social Network users are the same individuals who read the newspaper, watch television, listen to the radio and read magazines. It's a continually growing audience. Unlike newspaper, television, radio and magazine subscribers, Facebook is growing by 600,000+ users per day. Twitter, now well over 6 million members, has a growth rate of 4 times that of Facebook and every single minute, ten hours of video is uploaded to YouTube.

Plain and simple - there is no quick fix nor will someone else's quip make you a fortune. Just good hard work is the answer - and that's what I do!
