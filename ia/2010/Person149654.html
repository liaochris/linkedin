Mohammed Samad, P.E, P.Eng’s Summary

Excellent and versatile knowledge, skills and experience in various fields of electrical engineering, instrument engineering, petroleum engineering, information technology and project management.

Engineering:
• Highly experienced in electrical calculation and design of control and protection systems for generators, switchgears, MCC, Motors, Drives, Starters, UPS, Boiler and Substation for oil and gas industries
• Specialized in modeling (study view and relay view) and studying (Short Circuit Current, Load Flow, Device Coordination, Motor Acceleration, Arc Flash, Harmonic Current and System Stability) of complicated electrical systems using electrical engineering software (ETAP, SKM Tools and Easy Power)
• Good knowledge in PLC programming, DCS and SCADA system, Process control (PID) and communication systems (Devicenet, Modbus and Fieldbus)
• Excellent knowledge of upstream oil production and processing (offshore, onshore and oil-sands) and downstream oil processing (Refining, FCC, Hydro-cracking and Coking) and good understanding of oil production well, injection well and subsea system
• Good knowledge of electrical standards: NEC, ANSI, IEEE, IEC, CEC, API, NFPA and NEMA
• Active contributor to online global electrical engineering forum, CR4 (http://cr4.globalspec.com/forum/electrical-engineering). Click on 'My Blog' above to view the contribution

Information Technology:
• Strong knowledge in programming (C/C++, VB, Java, SQL, JSP and shell scripting), database (Oracle and SQL Server), Communication technology (ATM, TDM, SONET and Frame Relay), Local and Wide area (Ethernet and TCP/IP) network system
• Expertise in MS Office suite and document control software

Project Management:
• Excellent understanding and skills of the project management area of knowledge, PMBOK, tools and techniques of project process groups, project financial analysis (NVP, ROI, IRR and Payback Period) and project management software (MS Project)
