Gene Waxman’s Summary

My professional experience has come full circle from consulting on and building Novell Networks back in 1990 to now helping companies create their ecommerce vision and get the most out of an organically optimized site for both search visibility and more importantly landing page conversion.

After more than 4 years of rigorous study, testing, more study, more testing and experimentation our company, Intermedia Holdings, provides invaluable consulting services to help any online or offline company reach their potential by reducing ad spend and increasing both organic traffic and traffic conversion.

As opposed to other companies that offer consulting services we have irrefutable proof that we are successful because we 'live it' every day and fully understand the intricacies and nuances of SEO, SEM and conversion through our own website portfolio.

From these experience we have created several different viable businesses that all help in the scheme of ecommerce and complement one another. In this way we strive to be a full service ecommerce consulting company with a select group of prestigious customers who we can help with online solutions or overall strategic business solutions.
