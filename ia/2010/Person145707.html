Mike Masten’s Summary

Graduate of Pepperdine University with a degree in International Relations. President/Co-Founder of Christian anti-human trafficking group Project Exodus which seeks to find, rescue and restore victims of human trafficking in the world. Formerly worked for The Ghana Rural Library Project building libraries in West Africa, teaching in schools and organizing US teams for projects.
