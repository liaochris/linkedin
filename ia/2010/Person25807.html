Chern-Yue Boey’s Summary

Boey is the Founder and Managing Director of Three60 Inc, specializing in

• Business performance tuning
• Sales, Marketing & Channel optimization
• Social media marketing
• Competency development in Sales, Marketing & Channels
• Peak Performance Coaching

Prior to setting up Three60 Inc, Boey worked for several large multinational corporations, with the last 4 roles since 2002 being senior executive positions in

• Lenovo International
• Hewlett-Packard Company

His corporate career spans nearly 2 decades, with more than 18 years in the areas of Sales, Marketing and Business Management. As a corporate executive, Boey has a successful track record of developing and executing strategies that result in strong profitable growth in multi-country environments within Asia Pacific & Japan region. He is an innovative strategist with a proven ability to identify & grow business opportunities in diverse markets. As a leader, he is effective in creating positive as well as productive work environment with team and peers.
His experience in managing large scale businesses in the corporate setup gave him the invaluable insights into what makes large internationally focused organizations successful in leveraging sales & partners.

After leaving the corporate environment, Boey started Three60 Inc, a business consulting practice that specializes in providing multi-faceted & comprehensive business performance tuning and growth solution to businesses. His company assists businesses to improve quality of their sales forces and channels partners, as well as optimizing their marketing engines to drive net profit by leveraging on social media marketing medium. He is the developer of the SPIF sales competency model.
He has also been a public speaker on sales, marketing, channels management and social media marketing topics. Boey is also a licensed Sandler Associate as well as Net Profit Quest business coach which assist companies to enable social media marketing.
