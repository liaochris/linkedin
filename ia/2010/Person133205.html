Mark Weinstein’s Summary

Mark Weinstein has worked in higher education since 1989, beginning at Mount Vernon Nazarene University in central Ohio. Since his work as assistant director of communications at MVNU, he has gained significant leadership experience in marketing and communications. He led branding campaigns at Robert Morris University in suburban Pittsburgh and at Maryville University in St. Louis, Mo. As a result of the "It's a Whole New U" campaign for Robert Morris, the University has seen its reputation enhanced throughout the region as enrollment trends continue to increase.

At Maryville University, the visibility of the University has been increased due to the initial phases of the branding campaign and the increasing number of media placements. In 2005, the University reported 25 news stories for the previous academic year. In 2008, Maryville's media exposure increased to slightly more than 400 with expert commentary by faculty members and strategic stories on key university issues and accomplishments.

Because of his extensive work in marketing and strategic communications, Mark freelances with fellow public relations leader Chi-Chung Keung in consulting for selected colleges, universities, seminaries and non-profit organizations. The firm is based in Fullerton, CA.
