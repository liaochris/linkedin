Christopher Peifer’s Summary

CHRISTOPHER PEIFER is foremost a rock musician, having recorded and toured the world with The Kowalskis, sir, Frances Farmer My Hero and many others. He's shared the stage with rock giants Soundgarden, dinosaurs Head East and Foghat, notorious punkers Cheetah Chrome (Dead Boys), Vice Squad, and garage legends The Dirtbombs, The Woggles, and Eddie and the Hotrods. A bass and guitar player, he is available for session work with ORIGINAL acts, playing: Rock, Punk, Pop, Country, Bluegrass, Blues, Singer/songwriter, Americana . . .
Chris currently writes, records & performs with ANOTHER SATURDAY NIGHT and HEAVY CREATURES, and also plays bass with Mishal Zeera and Josh Diamond & the Brooklyn Soul.
Sound designer / composer (ASCAP) with credits from Fringe to Broadway, including Manhattan Theatre Club, Westport Country Playhouse (Paul Newman), Juilliard at Lincoln Center, Williamstown Theatre Fest.
Chris has put sound around, lit and worked alongside such luminaries as Bill Clinton, Joey Ramone, The Roots, Ashley Judd, Spike Lee, Mary Tyler Moore, Sarah Jessica Parker, Amy Sedaris, Cherry Jones, Rudy Giuliani, Michael Bloomberg, Alan Alda, Charlie Rose, Cat Power, Richie Havens, Nick Cave and many others.
