Debbie Gorski’s Summary

Debbie has worked for the Wyoming Small Business Development Center (now WyomingEntrepreneur.biz) for the past nine years as the Associate State Director. June 20008, she moved to western New York with her husband Chuck. She is currently working part time for WyomingEntrepreneur.Biz on special projects and coordinating Web 2.0 activity.

In her spare time she is involved in a local quilt guild and when it is not snowing, she spends time outside gardening or fixing damages from the weather, pigeons or woodchucks on her farmette of 10 acres.
