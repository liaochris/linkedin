Rosa Raskin’s Summary

I turn the traditional to innovative, high-tech.

Researcher in materials science, green technologies, and business with over 25 years of experience as an information specialist/scientist in industry, government, non profits, and academic medical centers. Specialties include manufacturing, polymers, metals, environmental issues, innovative products development, and business intelligence.

Owner of an independent technical and business research services company focusing on providing the highest quality research in record time.

Write state-of-the-art articles for technical publications including APCJ: Asia Pacific Coatings Journal and the PPCJ: Polymers Paint Colour Journal.

Volunteer activities include participation in the digitization of historical records and providing links of interest to the general public and specialists on my web site at http://www.raskinfo.com
