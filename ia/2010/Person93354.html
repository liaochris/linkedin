Jeff Knight’s Summary

I listen, I think, I create ideas, I deliver results.

I am a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Marketing and have been practicing marketing for over 17 years. During that time I have picked up a few awards, including those for marketing campaigns, on-line excellence and achieving the accolade of Marketer of the Year.

I am passionate about marketing and really enjoy helping businesses deliver more effective and efficient marketing, and ultimately profits, as well as coaching and training others to enhance their skills. I use a combination of insight and creativity to deliver results: insight into the business and customers, followed by creative thinking, generating ideas. I then deliver these ideas and maximise performance.

I listen, I think, I create ideas, I deliver results.

I set up Tonic Marketing Solutions in 2009, to give a refreshing approach to marketing consultancy. Having built a successful career within organisations of all shapes and sizes, I can now apply my skills and talents to a wider audience.

My strengths are as follows:

Maximiser: I focus on strengths as a way to stimulate personal and group excellence; I work to transform something strong into something superb.

Ideation: I am fascinated by ideas, of which I have many. I can find connections between seemingly disparate phenomena.

Strategic: I like to create alternative ways to proceed. Faced with any given scenario, I can quickly spot the relevant patterns and issues.

Individualisation: I am totally intrigued with the unique qualities of each person. I seem to have a gift for figuring out how people, who are different, can work together

Adaptability: I like to “go with the flow” which provides the ability to respond to changes and unforeseen requests easily. It is flexibility that means I can stay productive even when being pulled in different directions.
