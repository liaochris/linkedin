Audrey Monson’s Summary

I have a passion for real estate and have been involved in real estate for over twelve years. Prior to becoming a full time real estate agent, I was an investor, buying and selling over 25 of my own properties. I have been a full time real estate agent for over Six (6) years and have represented hundreds of clients in their purchase or sale of their homes, and continue to buy and sell properties for my own portfolio.

Before becoming a full time agent I worked for over 20 years for law firms in Utah and California. My legal background has given me a unique ability to negotiate and understand contracts and the documentation required for complex real estate transactions.
