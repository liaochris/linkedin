Jonena Relth’s Summary

My goal: Strive everyday to be a "Leadership Evangelist."

Recipient of the Joyce Parker Award for outstanding contribution to the field of training and development, Jonena Relth is a training consultant that specializes in performance improvement. As President and Leadership Evangelist of TBD Consulting, Inc., Jonena brings to her clients nearly 30 years of consulting experience. Her expertise with enterprise-wide systems has been instrumental in helping organizations bridge the connection between system implementation and the associated learning and user adoption required for successful transition from paper to paperless environment.

Jonena is the “owner” of the EMR/HER Clinical Transformation Group in LinkedIn addressing HIT issues and user adoption of electronic health records. She is the founder of Learning Innovation Campus, a learning incubator in Second Life whose mission is to foster and support the activities of like-minded individuals and companies who are interested in being part of a campus dedicated to people development and learning.

Jonena is a conference speaker and trainer in Second Life and “real world” on the topics of:
• Transparent Leadership – The Imperative
• Four Keys to Get Your colleagues to Embrace Web 2.0 and Virtual Worlds
• How to Successfully “RESHAPE” an Instructor-Led Leadership Course for Virtual Delivery
• myCOREformance™ Facilitation - Facilitation skills for today’s environments
• myCOREformance™ Design – Instructional design skills for blended learning
• myCOREformance™ Learning Assessment Design – Developing questions and feedback to improve learning retention
