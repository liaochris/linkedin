Paul Breslin, CHA, CHE’s Summary

Panther Hospitality, established in 2005, is on an exciting journey assisting clients develop unique hotels in the southeast. After more than 20 years in the hospitality industry, Paul Breslin, Managing Partner, is leading a team of industry professionals to assist developers and owners turn real estate opportunities into successful hotels of distinction that will produce superior returns for the stakeholders. Mission Statement:
A hotel consulting practice assisting developers and owners in the southeast to ensure each asset becomes a hotel at the industry’s highest standards with exceptional returns for the stakeholders.
