Hector E. Garcia’s Summary

I have over thirty years of experience in public accounting, starting my career with Cohen, Berg and co., P.C. in 1977. I stayed with them until 1992, when I opened my own accounting firm.
Now I work with a wide range of clientele, from entrepreneurs and closely held companies to not-for-profit companies. My firm strives to achieve a prestigious position in the business community through the quality of the services we render.

I am also currently working with the Southwest Minority Supplier Development Council to foster better working relationships among minority business entrepreneurs.

Hector E. Garcia’s Specialties:

Bilingual accounting services, determining tax compliancy, tax return preparation, tax reports for U.S. citizens living abroad and non-resident aliens, management consulting services, general accounting services, attestation services, cash flow and budgeting analysis, tax planning, IRS state/local representation, international taxation, and estate, gift, and trust tax return preparation
