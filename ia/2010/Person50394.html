    (Political Organization industry)

    January 2010 — May 2010 (5 months)

    In 2010, Mr. McFadden ran for elective office as a US Congressman in NYS Congressional District 19 (CD-19). Located in the Lower Hudson Valley, the district includes parts of Orange, Rockland, Dutchess, Westchester and all of Putnam Counties. David came in second of five candidates at the party nominating convention on May 26, 2010. Prior to the convention David had pledged not to force a primary if he did not win the nomination outright. Keeping his pledge for party unity, he stepped aside in support of the winning party nominee, Dr. Nan Hayworth. David ran as a fiscal conservative. (Continued with CFA duties during this period.)
