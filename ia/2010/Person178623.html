Ruben Toral’s Summary

Ruben Toral is a recognized expert in branding, medical tourism and healthcare globalization.

Formerly Group Marketing Director for Bumrungrad International hospital in Bangkok, Thailand, Ruben is widely credited for pioneering medical tourism in Asia and positioning Bumrungrad as one of the world’s leading medical tourism destination.

With over 17 years experience in healthcare marketing, Ruben has built companies, brands, physician networks and has distinguished himself as a thought leader in healthcare globalization. He is an external advisor the Joint Commission International Task Force on Globalization and past president of the International Medical Travel Association.

As CEO of Mednet Asia Ltd., Ruben provides strategic consulting, branding and marketing support services to the healthcare industry at large. His clients include Apollo Hospitals, Bumrungrad International Ltd., Sime Darby Healthcare and United Healthcare lnternational.

Ruben is also the founder and creator of Medeguide, a web portal connecting patients and doctors online, and Executive Health Retreats, a company specializing in corporate health and wellness retreats for Asian based executives.

An accomplished speaker and writer on the topics medical tourism, healthcare globalization and lifestyle medicine, Ruben's insights appear in business, travel and trade publications like Fast Company, Medical Travel Today, BusinessWeek and Spa Asia.

Healthcare marketing, branding, building physician networks, web platforms
