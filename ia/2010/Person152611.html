Neal Freeland’s Summary

Neal Freeland is Vice President of Product Management and Marketing at BuddyTV, the #1 independent social media site for TV fans. Previously, he was Vice President of Online Marketing for Zango, where he deployed a $25 million marketing budget and built a loyal audience of 10 million monthly consumers, leading to a strategic acquisition by blinkx. Prior to Zango, Neal was Director of Marketing at Microsoft, where he managed $125 million marketing budget and helped launch Live Search. During his five years at Microsoft, he also held product management positions in MSN and Windows Live. Prior to Microsoft, Neal served as an officer in the U.S. Navy.

Neal holds a B.A. degree in political science and an M.A. degree in sociology from Stanford University, and an M.B.A with honors from the Kellogg School at Northwestern, where he was an F.C. Austin scholar.

Neal Freeland’s Specialties:

+ Product management: strategy, value proposition, customer research, innovation, design, usability, feature prioritization, roadmapping, business model and partnership development, product evangelization

+ Product marketing: naming, branding, messaging, integrated marketing (online and offline advertising, customer relationship marketing, PR, buzz/viral, social media, SEM, SEO, and promotions), data analytics

+ Team leadership: budgeting, P&L management, talent development, recruiting
