Rich White’s Summary

My primary interest is in how natural interactions and natural user interfaces combined with 3D virtual learning spaces and objects can be leveraged with children and education. I am the Chief Architect of "Immersive Touch" and the Edusim project (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edusim) which seeks to explore the interactions of children, interactive surfaces, and multi-user 3D virtual environments. I serve as a lead 3D virtual environments and learning consultant with several organization throughout the world. I also am the lead LAMP stack web application developer with an education service center in Kansas.

Work Summary:
* Possess technical knowledge of hardware and software solutions as well as possessing a deep understanding of the taxonomies and pedagogies of using 3D virtual worlds (particularly open source solutions) in the K-12 educational setting.

* Chief Architect of "Immersive Touch" 3D Natural User Interface and the Edusim distribution of Cobalt (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/edusim).

* Collaborator on the Duke University and the Kauffman Foundation "Virtual World Assessment Infrastructure" project.

* Designer, Developer, and Work Group Member of the Immersive Education Consortium (http://immersiveeducation.org).

* MIT Scratch Application Educator (http://scratch.mit.edu).

* Full lifecycle LAMP stack application design, development, and deployment experience
