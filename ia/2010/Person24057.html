Bill Shander’s Summary

Tippingpoint Labs is a branded media company focused on driving revenue or reducing costs through the creation of digital content, development of strategic messaging and distribution, and the thoughtful execution of digital experiences.

Longtime clients include organizations such as PricewaterhouseCoopers, The World Economic Forum, the Government of Dubai, Boston Public Library, PBS, RSA Security, McGraw Hill, Booz Allen Hamilton, Booz & Company, Harvard Medical School and others.

Bill Shander’s Specialties:

I make the complex simple. That means making complex ideas digestible, and always finding the simplest technical solution to achieve my clients' goals. A personal specialty is leveraging technology for thought leadership - designing and building web sites and web applications for organizations seeking to communicate big ideas to targeted audiences.
