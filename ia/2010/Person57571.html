  • Chairman & CEO

    Rush Financial Technologies, Inc., formerly, Rushmore Financial Group, Inc.

    (Public Company; Financial Services industry)

    January 1991 — August 2006 (15 years 8 months)

    Mr. Moore served as Chairman and CEO of Rush Financial Technologies, Inc., which he founded in 1991 as a traditional financial services company. Under his leadership, the company, now known as Terra Nova Financial Group, Inc. (OTC: BB “TNFG”), grew rapidly from a startup to a retail brokerage organization with a nationwide account base and a network of more than 2,000 agents and registered representatives. Mr. Moore led Rushmore Financial Group through its successful initial public offering on the NASDAQ SmallCap Market in April 1998. As CEO, he guided the company through a number of public and private-equity transactions, culminating with the merger and acquisition of Terra Nova Trading, LLC in May 2006.

    Mr. Moore also presided over the RushTrade® unit’s multimillion-dollar development of proprietary software and financial technology. Early on, he recognized that real-time financial services technologies and direct-access online trading would revolutionize the online brokerage and institutional trading industry. Accordingly, he and his board of directors divested and redeployed assets, aggressively repositioning the company to abandon the traditional low-margin retail brokerage model and capitalize on this rapidly expanding market. During his tenure as CEO, Rushmore Financial Group ranked in The Dallas Morning News listing of the Top 200 largest public companies in Dallas-Fort Worth. Mr. Moore has been quoted in numerous publications, including The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, The Dallas Morning News, Business Week, Kiplinger’s Finance and Smart Money.
