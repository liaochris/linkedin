Rudynah "Miss D" Capone’s Summary

After working as a lifestyle and business journalist (at Sun Star Cebu) for 4 years, I enjoyed a tourism-oriented job as a coordinator for conventions and international events at Cebu Visitors and Convention Bureau (CVCB) where I was privileged to serve as a Media Chief Bureau of one of CVCB's largest events, the 3rd Global Filipino Networking Convention in 2005, Main Host of the MTV Queen City Run Awarding Ceremonies 2005 and International Culinary Congress 2005, and Accommodations Director for Men's World Volleyball 2005.

Having been quite exposed as a stage and corporate events host (professional emcee) since 1999, I tried venturing into events management and media production wherein few of the events I did were White Summer 2005, Paolo Santos Acoustic Concert, Cebu Youthfest Convention, Nursing Idol 2006, etc. Last May 2006, I established Art Dynamics Cebu speech and talent development company with a vision of bringing out every promise and potential of kids and youth through workshops, stage shows and tri-media exposures. I always believe that individuals can dare to dream, shine and grow as long as you motivate rightly and genuinely. I envision to put up Art Dynamics Foundation that shall grant scholarships to less privileged but talented individuals who want to pursue arts-related or music course. Hopefully, I would like to see myself hosting a lifestyle or talk show on TV or maybe news-anchoring--may it be in America or Europe, as long as "I can empower myself globally". This can come before or after I encounter my idol Oprah Winfrey. (dreaming is for free, isn't it?)
