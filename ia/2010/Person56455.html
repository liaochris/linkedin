Diego Saenz’s Summary

I love to create value. My goal is to add value to people and to organizations. I add value to people by believing in them, teaching them and bringing out the best in them. Over the last 20 years I have had a positive impact on a many lives. I am thankful for that opportunity.

I spent 10+ years in the corporate world working with companies like PepsiCo. I have spent the last 10 years as an Internet entrepreneur.

When I worked with Fortune 500 companies I added value by growing their existing business. Sometimes this growth was very significant measured in hundreds of millions of dollars. Of course never alone, but as part of a team.

As an entrepreneur I add value by solving problems in new and unique ways, by innovating and creating new products and yes, by building wealth for investors, partners and for myself. Most importantly I add value by serving the customers in my chosen market and by sharing my passion for entrepreneurship with others.

Diego Saenz’s Experience

  • President / Owner

    Petplace.com

    (Online Media industry)

    April 2000 — Present (10 years 1 month)

    Left the corporate world and joined early stage internet startup Petplace.com. Oversaw the development of Petplace.com including technology and editorial. Lead the initial site launch in partnership with AOL. Today Petplace.com is ranked by Nielsen Net Rating as one of the Top 10 Pet websites in the world and is a profitable business.

    In addition to Petplace.com, created a B2B product called Vetsuite.com. Lead the process from product idea on a flip chart to designing and building the Website to implementing the sales and marketing program that achieved 10% market share in the US in less than 36 months.

    In Nov 2005, lead the sale of Vetsuite.com to Novartis Animal Health in a multi-million dollar deal.

    Currently providing overall business, marketing and technology leadership for the company.
