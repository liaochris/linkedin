Lon Anderson’s Summary

Lon Anderson is a rare breed of executive-one who understands that current business environments require an effective business / technology partnership which synergistically identifies business objectives in tandem with technology solutions.

Currently, Lon is the Vice President of Corporate IT for ICF International global operations where he has made tremendous strides toward organizational development, introducing improved change management, project governance, regulatory compliance, disaster recovery and contingency planning, operational consolidation, and personnel development practices.

In his earlier consulting tenure, as Principal with ICF from 2006 to 2008, Lon held the role of Senior IT Executive for the Road Home program, the largest housing recovery program undertaken in the US, providing assistance to 200K+ victims of hurricanes Katrina and Rita. In this capacity, Lon consistently achieved program deliverables across infrastructure development and maintenance, program reporting and analytics, application delivery, and information security.

Prior to ICF, Lon held VP-level roles within Capital One Bank and Hibernia Bank (a Capital One subsidiary) from 2004 to 2006, providing direction to e-commerce / e-business initiatives. He was instrumental in Hibernia’s operational recovery following Hurricane Katrina, swiftly implementing contingency and disaster recovery plans to lead the industry in reestablishing operations. Advancing to a corporate-level role within Capital One, Lon developed online and eBusiness strategies leading major, multimillion-dollar Web development, integration, and regionalization initiatives.

Lon’s breadth and depth of expertise as a strategic, business-oriented leader is evident in the range and scope of his professional background, through which he consistently achieves exceptional results across key business performance indicators.

Lon Anderson’s Specialties:

Program & Project Management, ROI & CBA Analysis, Acquisitions, Help / Service Desk Management, Service Level Agreements (SLA), e-Commerce, Internet Marketing, Opt-ins & Privacy, On- & Off-Shore Team Leadership, Business Process Engineering, Business Process Outsourcing, RFI / RFP, Vendor Selection & Negotiation, Due Diligence, Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC), Waterfall / Agile, Enterprise Application Integration (EAI), Information / System Security, and Communications.
