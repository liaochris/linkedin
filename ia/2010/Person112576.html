Katarzyna Janowicz-Jimenez, MSME, MBA, LEED GA’s Summary

Green Engineer (ecotechnology, sustainability, renewables, alternative energy & energy conservation, green buildings, development & construction), MBA, MSME, LEED Green Associate

International experience in multiple, large-scale projects for Fortune1000, Forbes2000, S&P500 and REIT companies; office buildings, interiors, retail, medical, industrial and distribution facilities, multi/single family housing, infrastructure.

Project management from feasibility study, planning, calls for bids, estimating, scheduling, budgeting, design, reviewing plans (MEP, HVAC, plumbing, gas, control, electric), communicating with investors, municipalities, architects, designers, engineers, inspectors through construction, RFIs, RFQs, change orders, testing to completion and close-out, operations, maintenance, capital / tenant improvements, property / portfolio management, due diligence, acquisitions and divestiture.

Commissioning and retrocommissioning project management with focus on energy efficiency and cost control, including identifying energy saving opportunities, fire and safety issues, improving IAQ and noise level, increasing equipment reliability, updating and enhancing O&M documentation, developing preventive maintenance plans, providing trainings.

Multi-disciplinary engineering advisory. Research, analysis and expertise for the design, construction, implementation. Process and performance improvement. Policy and procedure development. Customer service and satisfaction.

Resourceful and decisive with problem solving aptitude, driven by challenge and forcefully dealing with commitments.
