Josh Tabin’s Summary

My passion is building businesses.

- Right out of college I built an office supply company right down the street from Office Max, Office Depot and Staples.
- In the mid-nineties I built "one stop shopping" markets for Southwestern Bell in Houston and Austin.
- After the "dot.com" bubble burst, I helped build Rackspace into the greatest hosting company there is.
- In less than two years, I built RigNet into the fastest growing managed communications provider for upstream oil and gas.
- For the past several years, I have been consulting with great startup entrepreneurs on both coasts helping guide them to the next level.

I build businesses...will it be yours next?

  • CFO and Strategic Finance Practice Leader

    OptimizedNow

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    October 2009 — Present (5 months)

    Optimized Now is the first consulting firm to provide a proven end-to-end solution – from strategy to execution – designed to drive measurable results through clear product positioning.

    Simple solutions. Expert knowledge. Proven results for high-tech companies.

    Unlike other consultants, OptimizedNow does more than talk. We help clients do – through guided execution and venture capital funding.

    To create the most efficient business systems, OptimizedNow provides three core services:

    1. Facilitative consulting to help our clients unlock their business ideas using self-guided assessment, facilitated workshops, and structured planning tools. The result? Clear strategies to reposition an established software product or successfully launch a new one.

    2. Guided Execution that enables our clients to put their ideas into action. This includes honest, involved advice where it’s most needed – from strategic and leadership direction to sales, marketing, and operations tactics.

    3. Funding that provides our best clients with the fuel they need to grow and succeed. Using our own venture capital fund, we put our money where our mouth is.

    By connecting traditional business consulting with guided execution and funding, we not only help our clients build comprehensive, go-to-market strategies, we also equip you with the tools to deliver results.
