Fulvio Romanin’s Summary

It took me a while to focus on the fact that my main skill is not about being a graphic designer, a musician, a teacher or a writer.
Connecting the dots, this is my job.
Whether it's about my communication practices - over ten years in web communication - musical abilities or sheer coordination of work groups, i think that the last ten years of work taught me to have a broader vision of the problems i am facing, and to organize work to avoid at best any unlikely circumstance. I have worked with huge festivals (Rototom Sunsplash, Italia Wave) where communication was the central issue, not only from a technical point of view, as well as growing my own small record label, ReddArmy, which took massive advantage of the latter experience and grew a very good reputation in a small timeframe and with very little money.
My goal as an entrepreneur is to gain exposure for me and the products i'm creating, and to develop profitable partnerships both on a human and economical point of view.
