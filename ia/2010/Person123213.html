LeAnne Dolce (Lay)’s Summary

I have worked in the Information Technology field for 10 years. Within these 10 years I have worked as a technical writer, Web developer, technical communication specialist, System Analyst, and Software Quality Assurance Analyst and Software Quality Assurance Lead, and most recently as an IT Project Manager.

I have my background in process, methodology, and IT and pushed that into my entrepreneurial pursuits. My passion is to assist small businesses and artists in self-publishing their works so I started Akili Publishing LLC. We specialize in web/online, print, and multimedia publications for small to large businesses.

My professional goal is to expand Akili Publishing, LLC into the premier Minority and Woman owned publishing company in the puget sound.
