Jeffrey St Clair’s Summary

My career has been devoted to communicating with clients, graphic and 3D designers, the media, and the public to explain and motivate.

As strategic director of major consulting firms in the U.S. and London, I developed winning branding, communications, and marketing strategies for retailers, financial services firms, and consumer goods manufacturers. My clients included ABN Amro Bank (worldwide), Deutsche Bank, Kodak, Rémy Martin, Philip Morris Duty Free, Marks and Spencer, The Burton Group, Kmart, Sears (Mexico), and Christian Dior. One of the major achievements of my career was heading the project team which created a new retail concept for JUSCO, a format which became a new division of the company.

The retail information service whose communications division I headed received national recognition for a study on the top ten retailing trends of the future. Ensuing speaking engagements nationwide took me to Monterrey, California, New Orleans, Colorado Springs, Nashville, and Memphis. Another white paper on the television home shopping industry resulted in recognition in several retail publications and the Wall Street Journal as well as a guest appearance with Charlie Rose on CBS TV’s Nightline.

More recently at the Ohio Environmental Council, I was in charge of all electronic communications, including advocacy alerts, social media, e-communication with members, e-newsletter, web-site management, brand strategy, and design of all print communications. As a result of my electronic communications management, the OEC’s online subscriber list doubled over a one-year period from 2,500 to over 5,000.
