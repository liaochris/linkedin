I excelled at facilitating communication in every form as an Operations Point person. Working in this arena, I have enjoyed wearing many hats.

People gravitate to my sincere and passionate nature. Expertise from managing schedules, executing policy & procedure, administrative paperwork to social networking mediums and client relations. I seek opportunities that can allow me to utilize my well-rounded experience and perspective in the widest capacity. I can be trusted completely and thrive in an environment I can trust.
