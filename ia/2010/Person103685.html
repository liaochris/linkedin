Jonathan Bandy’s Summary

My interests lie in work which concerns political and economic freedom. When I can be excited and passionate about the things I am doing, I find that work becomes less about a paycheck and more about sharing my enthusiasm and zeal with others who will, in turn, be inspired to do the same. Ideally, I would like to always have a position in a grassroots organization that endorses individual liberty and economic freedom.

In regards to long-term goals, I have pondered the idea of furthering my education by attending law school, thereby enabling me to work as general counsel for a group that shares my ideology. I believe that in order to better enable others through my work I must first enable myself through the pursuit of knowledge. Working before entering school once again would allow me to gain tangible experience as well as giving me ample opportunity to meet and network with others of similar mindset. In doing so, I hope to further my leadership abilities and forge new contacts for future use. If law school does not happen, then I would like to continue working in the political/activism sector.
