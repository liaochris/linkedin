Hugh Todd’s Summary

I provoke new thinking, and generate solutions that actually work in the real world. I turn dysfunctional teams around. I connect people up for great results. I make you think. I smash through limiting beliefs, fears and phobias. I turn 'networking' events into great investments of your time. I help you to survive and thrive!

Take more control of your life, of your business, of your team, of your future, & of the pressures you face - and enjoy the journey while you are at it.

My professional values of integrity, commitment to adding value, my skills and insightfulness should be at the heart of your experience when we work together.
_________________________________

I have 33 years of intense experience in bringing out the best in people, whatever their situation - from young people 'at risk' in deprived areas to NATO managers, Board level and senior managers, building site managers and staff, college students, nursery school staff and children, and a range of others.

I aim to build your community (team, company, organisation) and coach the unique individual people who make up that community for maximum, sustainable value. I create challenging, enjoyable, and impactful ways of focusing on the things you need to pay more attention to if you are going to succeed and sustain.
