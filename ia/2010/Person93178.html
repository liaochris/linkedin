Jeff Flemings’s Summary

For me marketing is all about passion, and I have great passion for marketing ideas that move a business forward. I love helping creative teams develop and implement ideas that drive consumer interaction and business results. I love inspiring ideation with an empathetic, rigorous understanding of the consumer. And I love helping brands harness social media to drive new types of engagement.  

In the past 20 years have honed my craft at some of the world’s greatest marketing agencies. After starting out in management consulting, I spent a decade in account planning at Arnold Worldwide (in 2 stints, bookending an MBA), then created and led account planning at Digitas, the interactive agency in the Publicis network, while helping Digitas become a leading agency for the digital age.  

In 2008 I joined the Publicis Groupe digital and media holding company, VivaKi, which incorporates Starcom Mediavest, Zenith Optimedia, Digitas, Razorfish and Denuo. I’m part of the VivaKi Nerve Center (VNC), a group of analytics, media, and strategy gurus who help our agencies use new technologies to develop innovative marketing platforms for clients.

My current focus is social media (I am the global head of social for VivaKi) - developing a framework to help client teams understand and apply social media to brand campaigns, developing relationships with key industry partners our agencies can harness to deliver results with social, and connecting partners to agency teams to drive client results with social.

I have "expert" level skills in:
- account planning/brand planning/brand strategy
- digital strategy
- social media strategy
- connection planning
- new business pitches
- brand positioning development
- research development, implementation and interpretation
- customer insight development
- brainstorming leadership
- developing big ideas and creative ideas

Jeff Flemings’s Specialties:

Social media strategy, account planning, brand planning, connection planning, digital strategy, customer insight, brand positioning/strategy development, brainstorm leadership, partnering with/leading creative teams, big ideas
