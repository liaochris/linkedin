Andrew Charlton’s Summary

Coming from the finance industry has assisted me greatly with many of the decisions required to run a small business. It seems that my time at NAB was just a training run for what I am doing now. With stints in marketing, business banking and small business auditing, the insights gained have made life as a start-up business owner possible.

My main business goal now is to continue to grow The Condition Report Company and expand it into a truely national business, with offices in each state capital.

My personal goal is to be a regular positive presence at home to assist in the development of my three young boys (James, Will and Sam) as they grow into young men.

Andrew Charlton’s Specialties:

Running a start-up business, you become something of an all-rounder. There are so many issues that you need to deal with; such as HR, sales & marketing, compliance, business and tax planning.
