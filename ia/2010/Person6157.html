Alexandra Lesmes’s Summary

Rapid Refill Ink is a retail store that specializes in offering refilled inkjet and remanufactured toner printer cartridges for a substantial savings to our customers. We offer savings of up to 50% from the manufacturer brand, and all of our cartridges are satisfaction guaranteed for print performance and quality.

Our customers consist of commercial businesses, which we offer a free next day delivery, with minimum purchase. We also service retail consumers at over 20 drop boxes located throughout Hampton Roads and our storefront in Hilltop next to the Nawab Indian Restaurant.

Our goal is to educate people on recycling printer cartridges while saving money.
