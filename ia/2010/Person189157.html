Pete Shively’s Summary

With a degree in Fine Art Photography I bring an artistic eye to my work. I have darkroom experience, color and b&w, but prefer the digital relm of photography where I am limited only by my imagination when editing my work.
I offer 360 Virtual Tours of properties for sale, tourist hot spots, and any other location that deserves better representation on the web. If you don't need a virtual tour then one of my panoramic images is great for brochures or any other form of advertising.
I also offer pet portraits, Furry Fotos, so that people that love their pets like they were their own children can capture their pet's personality in time treasured photographs.

Pete Shively’s Specialties:
