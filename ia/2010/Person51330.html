David Loehr’s Summary

David has built a reputation of design integrity for complex, mixed-use urban projects throughout his twenty-two year career as an architect and urban planner. His projects have won numerous awards for their design statements, their contributions to the community, their innovative use of materials and their collaborative process with all team members. David has designed projects in eleven different countries and helped establish international offices for a multi-national design practice. David is a licensed architect in 29 jurisdictions. He helped to establish the Upper Midwest Chapter of DBIA and served as its President.

Sustainable design, urban mixed-use projects, corporate offices, non-profit organizations, higher education clients, hotels and restaurants
