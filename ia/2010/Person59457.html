David Ray, PMP’s Summary

I am an Associate with Booz Allen Hamilton, with over 9 years of experience including project management, as-is analysis, process mapping, forecast modeling, Six Sigma quality systems, and business case development. I have provided these services to a variety of federal, automotive, and aerospace clients. I have experience with contract lifecycle management projects while working on procurement and supply chain management projects within the oil and gas industry. I also have extensive engineering experience working primarily in the aerospace and automotive industries.
