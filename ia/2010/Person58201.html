Don Low’s Summary

A versatile digital artist with professional work experience since 1999, working fulltime and freelance as an illustrator, designer
and animator, for publishers, ad agencies, web companies and educational institues. He is skilled in both digital and traditional
media, concept designs, storyboarding and experienced working with Maya, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Premiere,
AfterEects, Flash and Dreamweaver. In a nutshell, he is a dedicated, responsible and industrious individual who works well in a fast-paced, team-based environment.

Don Low’s Specialties:

2D Illustration and design with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

3D Modeling, Texturing, Lighting and Rendering in Maya.
Character Design and Visual Development for Game and Animation.
