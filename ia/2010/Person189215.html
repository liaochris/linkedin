Brian Ouellette’s Summary

After graduating from the University of Washington, for over a decade, Brian was a money manager working in Chicago on the Chicago Board of Trade, on Wall Street in New York City & in Seattle, Washington.

In 2000, while managing money for the Private Client Group of Oppenheimer & Co., Brian was invited to be the Market Commentator for Q13 FOX News This Morning. For 3 1/2 years, he reported daily from his office in Seattle. Credited with over +2,000 live TV appearances, he reported during the biggest boom and biggest bust in the history of the US Stock Market to the 12th largest media market in the US.

In April 2004, he left money management completely to launch Shoewallet™ Active Gear LLC. Since then, the "Shoewallet™," from Shoewallet Active Gear LLC has been featured on:

NBC's Today Show, CNN Headline News, NY1 & over 200 radio stations nationwide. In addition, it has been written about & reviewed in the Chicago Tribune, Dallas Morning News, Shape magazine, Runner's World magazine, amongst dozens of others in print & on the web.

As the Shoewallet™ Supplier to the ad specialty, promotional products industry; private label Shoewallets are sold exclusively thru ASI, SAGE & PPAI Member Distributor Partners in the US, Canada & across the globe. Shoewallet™ Active Gear has produced custom branded Shoewallets for such brands as Southwest Airlines, US Air Force, Department of Defense, YMCA, Jamba Juice, Skechers, Pitney Bowes, Dunkin' Donuts, UCLA, etc.

The Mission is to provide a useful, versatile product in the Shoewallet™, while increasing awareness on the need to always carry ID when on the road or trail. This message is driven daily by their Safer Roads & Trails Campaign:

Be Smart. Be Safe. Carry ID.™
(Free ID card included with every Shoewallet™)

By working closely with their Distributor Partners & end retail customers, they are driven to enable all people, young and old, to live Active, Safe & Healthy Lives.

Brian Ouellette’s Specialties:

Over 90% of the business is focused on manufacturing & supplying our ASI|SAGE|PPAI Member Distributor partners custom branded Shoewallets for their corp./non-profit/military/university/event end customers. The other 10% of the business is focused on selling shoewallets thru the retail channels of www.shoewallet.com and www.amazon.com.
