Blair Sorby’s Summary

Manifesto Group is a different kind of venture firm, based on teamwork, serving entrepreneurs and a dedication to building companies of lasting value. We function as a team, so that every partner is invested - financially and personally - in each other's success.

This unique approach gives our companies the benefit of having all of our experienced, well-connected partners working for them, not just one. Whether it's help in recruitment, facilitating corporate partnerships, or advice on strategic moves, each company can access the combined resources of the entire firm.
