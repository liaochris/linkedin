Jim Pierobon’s Summary

Former Chief Energy Writer at the Houston Chronicle and Contributing Writer for The New York Times, Director of Communications & External Relations for the American Council On Renewable Energy and Co-Manager of the Energy and Environmental Practice at Ogilvy Public Relations Worldwide.

Pierobon & Partners LLC is a networked communications and marketing consultancy that brings together industry colleagues to develop and execute plans to scale up sustainable energy initiatives and the policies that support them.

Jim Pierobon’s Specialties:

Strategic communications, social media, product launches, executive visibility, marketing strategies, targeted sales campaigns, media outreach, third-party alliances, event planning, government affairs, public policy modeling
