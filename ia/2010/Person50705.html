David Fowler’s Summary

For business marketers and newspaper publishers alike, I have a lifetime of experience in marketing effective newspaper print and web ads.

My first best-selling book, “Newspaper Ads That Make Sales Jump!” was published in 1998, in three languages and is still considered “The Bible” at most newspapers in the English speaking world.

Today, I do workshops, consulting, and have written another book, Ultimate Money-Making Newspaper Ads. Currently working on third book titled “Ultimate Money-Making Online Ads” which is due out in March, 2010

David Fowler’s Experience

  • Consultant

    Marketing and Advertising

    (Newspapers industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    David Fowler is considered one of the nation’s top experts on newspaper and print advertising. In the 1990’s his Westport, Connecticut ad agency, New Vision for Retail, placed more retail advertising in The New York Times per week than all agencies in the NY, NJ, and CT region combined. His agency was the first to combine store planning, visual display and graphic elements with advertising.

    Today he is the Founder and CEO of Ads Up, a marketing and advertising firm that creates direct response print and online advertising strategies for medium to small companies. His company also provides ad-effectiveness training to newspaper Ad managers, sales reps and production staff.

    David’s clients have included Benjamin Moore Paints, TOBU (Japan’s largest department store), The John Deer Company as well as numerous small to medium size businesses in the U.S., Canada and the UK. His media clients number in the hundreds and include such companies as The New York Times, The San Jose Mercury News, The Seattle Times, The San Francisco Chronicle, The San Diego Union Tribune, and Lee Enterprises. He is also on the faculty of the American Press Institute, and has made numerous presentations to the Newspaper Association of America, as well as many State Press Associations including California, Texas, New York, Iowa, Pennsylvania.
