Martijn Duizendstra’s Summary

Creative to the core. I've been through 5 versions of Photoshop, 3 versions of Illustrator and eat XHTML and CSS for breakfast. I'm someone who loves trying new things to reach my goals - and always keep my goals in mind.

Searching for solutions - both technical and esthetical - is one of my favorite activities as I hate to stand still. I enjoy learning new things, figuring things out and find appropriate solutions for different problems.

I often philosophize about improvement, both personal and productwise. I love creating concepts, sketches and ideas for new products or features for existing products. I'm a person who strives for perfection, which is probably also my greatest weakness.

I dislike people who "think outside the box" because i like to think that there is no box.
