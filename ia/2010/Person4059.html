Allie Herzog’s Summary

I have a passion for helping companies determine how to strategically incorporate digital and social media initiatives into their PR campaigns. I come from a public relations background and offer a combination of creative, public relations and digital/social media worlds. I specialize in helping business differentiate themselves and prevail by embracing change and integrating new and traditional tools.

Whether it is identifying new outlets to reach targeted consumers; analyzing clients' Web presence; guiding them in the process of selecting strategic interactive media initiatives that exceed business goals; or listening to a business problem and helping to come up with a strategic solution—at any time of day or night, I love what I do. A New Yorker by heart, I've brought that Manhattan business savviness back to Texas.

Client experience includes: luxury real estate projects (including brokerages, architects, developers, condominiums, luxury concierge services), national consumer products (including cookbooks, children’s products, DIY inventions, technology products), retail chains, technology products, restaurants, bakeries, law firms, start-ups, and nonprofit organizations.
