David Fisher’s Summary

I have over 10 years of experience in the direct sales field as a representative, manager, and trainer. I started as a sales rep in the direct, in-home sales industry with Cutco Cutlery where I became one of the top ten reps in the country. I then went on to interview, train, and develop over 1,500 sales people as manager for both Cutco Cutlery and Brinks Security.

I have taken that experience and used it to found my own company, RockStar Consulting. Our mission is to help people become RockStars in their personal and professional lives. We offer one-on-one coaching, sales training, and seminars. We specialize in working with salespeople, small business owners, and individuals that combine the two (mortgage brokers, insurance agents, real estate agents, financial planners, etc.). We also work with entrepreneurs who are service providers that are setting up their own company (graphic designers, copywriters, massage therapists, etc.).

I've recently published my second book, and I've also become very involved in training business owners and salespeople on networking and LinkedIn skills.
