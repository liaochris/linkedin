Ashley Motia’s Summary

Communications geek who loves syntax, grammar, and punctuation. I guess this makes me a bit of a nerd and slightly pedantic. I've worn many hats over the years, and, while being a jack of all trades has its perks, I'm gravitating toward being an applied master of a trade.

I practice polite LinkedIn etiquette: if I don't know you and don't see a reason why you would request a connection, I will NOT mark "doesn't know" on your invitiation. I do not agree with LinkedIn's "five declined connections and your account gets restricted" rule. If you don't know someone, just archive the request. No harm; no foul.

I am an even SC type on the DISC model, making me a "coordinator" type with an "analyzer" lean when under pressure. Helping others and a job well done give me some of the greatest joy. Oh, and I've got spunk, too.

I volunteer with the Red Cross of Northeast Indiana and the Allen County SPCA.
