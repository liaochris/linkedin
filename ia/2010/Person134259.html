Mark Moses, CEO Coach and Speaker’s Experience

  • CEO

    Mark Moses.Net

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Mark Moses coaches entrepreneurs and companies on how to grow their businesses, build their strategic plan, raise capital and navigate through the challenges of a difficult economy. He also speaks worldwide on how to overcome adversity, Lead in Turbulent Times, How to Build a $100 million company and How to Build an awesome corporate culture.
    Mark Moses started his first company at age 19. He has successfully built two companies from scratch and sold them. Along the way, he won Ernst & Young’s Entrepreneur of the Year award and the Blue Chip Enterprise award from the U.S. Chamber of Commerce & Mass Mutual for overcoming adversity. His companies garnered rankings of #1 Fastest Growing Company in Los Angeles as well as #10 on the Inc. 500, growing the company by 10,000% in 5 years.

    Mark was the 1992 U.S. National Squash Champion and has successfully completed FIVE Ironman events, including the legendary Hawaii World Championship.

  • Chairman California Southwest Chapter

    Member of YPO's International Events Committee
    Past Chairman of YPO's M2Mx

  • CEO & President

    Platinum Capital Group

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Real Estate industry)

    September 1993 — October 2006 (13 years 2 months)

    As Chairman, CEO and co-founder, Mark was the Chief Energizing Officer. He led Platinum Capital, a national mortgage banking firm headquartered in Irvine, California, through its hyper growth journey from 2 people to 550 people and $1.6 Billion in annual volume. Mark once rode an 8000 pound elephant into his annual meeting to get this employees to "Think Big". He has been on NBC, ABC, CBS and written up in hundred of articles and magazines including the Wall St. Journal, LA Times, Orange County Register, Forbes, INC magazine, Success, etc....
    He survived many times when many of his competitors failed and overcame significant adversity along the way as he navigated through the challenging mortgage market. Mark sold the company in 2006.

  • President

    Student Painters

    (Consumer Services industry)

    January 1988 — April 1992 (4 years 4 months)

    Mark started and led the company to 250 branches and 3000 employees in 4 years. Student Painters taught college students how to run and operate their own business. Mark sold the company in 1992.
