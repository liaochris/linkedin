Gail Ector’s Summary

For almost two decades, I utilized my expertise in communications and marketing to consult with clients on how best to convey their employer brand and the appropriate media in which to do so. I have now transitioned those skills and talents to help aspiring entrepreneurs find, research, and finance the franchise business that is right for their personal and professional goals and financial resources. It is a most rewarding vocation!

So many people dream of becoming business owners, but for a variety of reasons, fail to pursue that dream. For some, it is the perception of risk. Others feel they lack the skills to be successful business owners, or feel they don't have the financial resources to invest in a business of their own. Then, there are those who are simply overwhelmed by the many options that are available for business ownership.

That's where FranNet comes in. As a pioneer in the industry of franchise consulting, we work with our clients to help them define what it is that they are looking for in a business and what strengths they bring to that business. Using this information, along with a number of additional parameters such as their values and interests, we recommend franchise businesses that are a good fit for them. We then guide them through a thorough due diligence process so that they can make an informed decision about their business investment. We also refer them to other professionals whom they will need, such as attorneys, accountants, real estate professionals, and of course, financial institutions that are providing financing for today's small businesses.

My services are at no charge to my clients. I would love to help you or anyone you know who is interested in fulfilling their dream of business ownership.
