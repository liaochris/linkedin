John Head’s Summary

John David Head is a Chicago-based consultant and rainmaker.
He currently works full-time with PSC Group LLC, where he
has served in positions of increasing authority, and currently as
Director of Enterprise Collaboration, since 2000. Throughout his career he has focused on the development and implementation of enterprise collaboration solutions. At PSC he served as the architect and team leader for many of the enTouch Frameworks which are PSC-proprietary, ready-to-use business templates. For this, and other work he has been recognized with IBM Lotus and IBM Beacon awards and is a regularly-featured speaker at Lotusphere

John Head’s Specialties:

Business Development, Business Application Development, Software Application Architecture, Lotus Notes Client Development, Lotus Notes Web Development, Integration of Microsoft Office and other Microsoft Technologies with Lotus Notes/Domino, Microsoft Office Development, OpenOffice.org Development, Lotus Symphony Development, VSTO Development
