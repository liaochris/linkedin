Fred J. Beal’s Summary

Construction / Real Estate Professional focused on the redevelopment of Urban Environments, providing a unique blend of Development Consulting, Owner's Representation, and Construction Services to Building Owners and Real Estate Investors / Developers throughout the Midwest.

Fred has been involved in numerous complex re-development projects, assisting clients through the myriad tasks required to assemble each project and carry it through to completion.

Fred J. Beal’s Specialties:

Development Consulting, Owner's Representation, and Construction Services, specializing in Urban Re-development and other types of tax credit leveraged projects, leading the growth of JC Beal Construction Inc; your restoration, general building renovation, and tenant build-out specialists

Fred J. Beal’s Experience

  • President

    JC Beal Construction, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    April 1980 — Present (30 years 10 months)

    JC Beal Construction Inc. founded in 1962 and with offices in Ann Arbor and Detroit, specializes in "Downtown Construction'; a combination of Historic Restoration and Renovation, Tenant Build-out, and New Infill Construction in Urban areas throughout the Midwest. The firm provides its services as Owners Representative, Program / Construction Manager, General Contractor and Design Builder.

  • President

    MOTOWN CONSTRUCTION PARTNERS LLC

    (Construction industry)

    March 2006 — January 2011 (4 years 11 months)

    MOTOWN CONSTRUCTION PARTNERS LLC was formed in 2006 to lead the re-development of the Broderick Tower, a 34 story former office building in downtown Detroit. Construction is now underway, with completion scheduled for September 2012.

  • Vice President

    Beal Incorporated

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    September 2006 — November 2010 (4 years 3 months)

    Beal Incorporated is a Michigan Corporation specializing in demolition, abatement, waste hauling, and related construction trade services, catering to Building Owners and Construction Firms throughout the Midwest.

  • Vice President

    Beal Properties LLC

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    January 2005 — October 2010 (5 years 10 months)

    Beal Properties LLC is a full service property development / management company specializing in historic / MBT tax credit leveraged developments and the rehabilitation of challanged properties in Southeast Michigan. The firm currently owns / manages commercial and residential properties in Ann Arbor, Ypsilanti, and Detroit Michigan, and Toledo Ohio.

  • Managing Member

    Historic Equities Fund I LLC

    (Real Estate industry)

    June 2005 — August 2010 (5 years 3 months)

    The Historic Equities Fund is a Michigan limited liability company formed in 2005. The company was formed for the express purpose of establishing a fund, specializing in the re-development of historic and traditionally undervalued properties located in Southeastern Michigan. The Company is planning to raise and invest $2.5 Million dollars in the purchase and re-development of a minimum of a $10 Million portfolio of properties, with the intent to provide to its investors a higher than average rate of return.
    The Company plans to achieve its objective of higher than average rates of return through a combination of; increases in property value, rental income, the use of historic and/or other tax credits, including strategies to "sell" credits to third parties, and other tax, property management, and marketing strategies that enhance long term investor income and property value.

  • Vice President

    221 Venture Inc. / Weinmann Block LLC

    (Real Estate industry)

    May 1994 — August 2010 (16 years 4 months)

    221 Venture Inc. / Weinmann Block LLC are single project Real Estate entities that have developed and continue to own commercial real estate in the Ann Arbor Michigan area
