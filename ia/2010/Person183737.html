Stephanie Checchi’s Summary

A magnetic marketing strategist with a proven track record of working with small to medium sized business primarily in the healthcare industry (start-up, software, medical device, & biotech) providing solutions to successfully navigate and produce three core results which are vital for business success.

1.) "Increasing" Traffic =$$ - By implementing several key marketing strategies, I strive to keep focus on traffic and lead generation initiatives to ensure that a solid pipeline for new business is always in queue.

2.) "Optimizing" current products and/or services - Ensuring that a solid brand is communicated in the market and leveraging customers interests in one product or service and cross promoting additional products or services to gain the biggest competitive advantage.

3.) "Retaining" client base - Creating business relationship opportunities and effective CRM solutions to aid in client relations to ensure that when you connect with a new client/customer to your business they remain a loyal and satisfied client/customer

My history and experience in every marketing role has been to produce those three core results whether through presentations, public relations, advertising, lead generation programs, trade shows, brochures , events, and one on one outreach. No mater the marketing campaign, I have always held myself accountable for those three impact focused deliverables.
