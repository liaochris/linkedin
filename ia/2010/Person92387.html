Jessie Deye’s Summary

Graduated with Honors from the University of Cincinnati in March of 2008 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Biological Sciences; focus on Molecular and Cellular Biology. Attained an Honors Research Certificate for research work in breast cancer metastasis through UC’s Genome Research Institute. Spent two years studying Industrial Design at UC’s #2 nationally ranked program prior to focusing on research. Currently working as a Senior Process Analyst in Kroger’s internal consulting and process change department responsible for over $200M in annual savings to the company. Trained in Lean/Six-Sigma, complex data analysis, statistical modeling, and business strategy. President of multiple leadership organizations at the University of Cincinnati, currently the Founder and President of the Cincinnati Chapter of 85 Broads Global Women’s Organization.
