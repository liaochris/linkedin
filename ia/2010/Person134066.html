Mark Kolier’s Summary

More than 25 years in direct marketing advertising and production. Has lead CGSM Inc. into offering full marketing agency services specializing in direct marketing creative, strategy and production by taking a media neutral approach. Website and CMS creation, SEO/SEM/PPC. Branding and Integrated marketing, email and mail list management and media placement services now offered. Founded ecommerce application YourCover.com developed in 2001 which is continually expanding its product line and has been built almost entirely on good search marketing tactics. Making the unusual combination of having an agency have itself as a client!

Mark Kolier’s Specialties:

Experience in working with and helping companies formulate strategy and maximize integrated cross channel direct marketing media efforts as well as handling of production of all facets of direct marketing and website development and marketing campaigns.
