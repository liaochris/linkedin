Artie Romero’s Summary

ARG! Cartoon Animation Studio was founded by Artie Romero in 1994. Artie has been involved in comics and animation since the 1970s, and has screen credits for animation on 4 motion pictures, 2 TV series and one PBS special. Before entering the animation industry as an animator, producer and director, Artie was a comic book artist, publisher, and an illustrator of books and magazines.

After starting out in 1995 as just a few pages on Artie's AOL Web space, the artie.com website was launched in July, 1996, and quickly became one of the most popular animation sites on the Web. The ARG! studio has created over 40,000 original digital animations, and more than 15,000 of them are displayed on artie.com. ARG! animation is available worldwide for download to cell phones through AT&T, Verizon, Sprint, Tmobile and many other carriers, thanks to distribution by Wireless Developer Agency.

Since founding ARG! Cartoon Animation, Artie created the CityStar® brand and expanded his company's services into publishing city directory websites. ARG! is now a division of CityStar® Group, Inc., a Colorado corporation, with Artie serving as CityStar's CEO and President. Artie is focused on further development of the ARG animation studio and the 300+ city portal sites of citystar.com.
