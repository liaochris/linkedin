Matthew Lynch’s Summary

Matthew Lynch is a passionate and engaging Lean Six Sigma (LSS) Black Belt committed to providing business process reengineering consulting services. Engagements have spanned corporations/institutions across Canada and the United States in a number of industries, including, energy companies, EPCM (Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Management), banking, higher education, transportation, and hi-tech. 14 years of both business and technical experience coupled with a strong process improvement background and solid project leadership skills have resulted in a well established track record. Recent highlights include the development of a solid business case at a large global bank in their Accounts Payable area to the streamlining of processes at Calgary’s largest Human Resource team. Matthew excels at not only providing senior business analysis capabilities to a project, but also project management expertise in a way that helps his clients achieve their goals quicker and with the sustainable results they are looking for.
