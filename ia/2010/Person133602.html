Mark Bradley’s Summary

Mark joined HP in February 2007 as a Solutions Architect on the Americas IT Service Management Team. Mark is responsible for leading and support the selling and delivery for ITS&IM market offerings in C&I target and preferred accounts. Before joining HP, Mark supported Peregrine for various companies through a series of mergers and acquisitions in the insurance and banking industries, which culminated into a VP Infrastructure position with JPMorgan Chase (JPMC.)

The apex of Mark's seven years of hands on experience and knowledge of ServiceCenter was his key role of successful in house worldwide SC implementation for JPMC across 11 time zones, delivering high levels of performance & availability. The partnering he helped orchestrate between the businesses to deliver technology services and infrastructure capabilities serves to differentiate JPMC from the competition, and provide superior value for customers.

The rollout earned recognition in the category of Excellence in Business Alignment by improving insight into the business impact of IT for HP OpenView Awards of Excellence 2006 - Global User Forum - Synergy/Openview in South Beach Florida.

Mark is a contributing author of Implementing Service and Support Process: A Practical Guide (Van Haren Publishing, 2005. ISBN: 978-90-77212-43-1) The book is a reference tool compiling IT management best practices and IT Service Management within the ITIL framework from the vantage point of international experts. Mark’s chapter on Service Level Management aligns well with HP's BTO focus on ITSM implementations.

Mark has a BA in Computer Science & Speech Communication from Monmouth College & Masters of Science in Computer Information Systems from University of Phoenix. Mark has obtained certifications in ITIL v2 & v3, MCSE+I, CNE, A+, MCP.
