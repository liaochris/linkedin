Jesse Ellenbogen’s Summary

User experience executive offering 25 years experience in consumer technology development. Specializes in consumer mobile, having contributed to over 80 mobile projects. Significant contributions to iconic, landscape-shaping products, such as Palm's Pre, Pixi, Treo & Centro, and Apple's PowerBook & Newton.

Have held UX design, research & management positions at both startup & established companies, including Apple, Sony, Palm, HP, E*TRADE and MOXI / Digeo.

Have also provided UX consulting for 20 different consumer technology companies, including YouTube, Logitech, and Danger. Portfolio at www.userfit.com.

INTERESTS:
• polishing consumer user experiences until they feel effortless & intuitive
• driving the design process while growing exceptional teams
• using research in a tight design-test-iterate cycle, hand-in-hand with designs and engineers
• working on highly integrated product experiences where it's hard to pinpoint where the software stops & the hardware begins


PRIMARY CONTRIBUTIONS:

MOBILE DEVICES & CONTENT: 80+ projects, including
• Palm's Pre & Pixi smartphones and webOS
• "Classic" Palm OS UX & hardware, including Treo, Centro & PDAs
• Apple’s PowerBook, Newton, and mobile prototypes
• Danger’s Sidekick 2 & 3 smartphones
• Helio’s mobile phones & media download UX
• YouTube for mobile phones
• Yahoo! Mail for mobile phones

A/V PRODUCTS & REMOTES: 30+ projects, including:
• Digeo’s MOXI digital video recorder set-top box
• Sony’s DTR-HD1, Sony's first USA HDTV set-top box
• DVDO’s Edge HDTV processor
• Logitech’s Wireless DJ multi-room music system

Jesse Ellenbogen’s Specialties:

Creating integrated hardware/software consumer experiences that are more than the sum of the parts.
