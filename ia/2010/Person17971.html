Brian Jeffrey’s Summary

In the over 20 years I spent as a sales trainer I lost count of the times I was asked to basically "train pigs to fly," to train people who, at best, should be selling something else or, at worst, shouldn't be in sales at all. That's why I developed the Sales Temperament Assessment (STA).

When I sold my sales training company in 2006, I started a new company that offers the STA as an online sales assessment to help sales managers assess a person's suitability for sales and make even better hiring decisions. The STA is an excellent pre-employment testing tool that helps separate the wheat from the chaff, the winners from the whiners.

By way of background, I've been a sales manager, sales management consultant, and sales trainer for over 40 years. I'm also a professional speaker and author of over 100 articles and 18 eBooks on sales and sales management as well two books titled, "The Sales Wizard's Secrets of Sales Management" and "The Five-Minute Sales Trainer."

Sales managers can sign up for my "$alesTalk" ezine at http://www.salesforceassessments.com/newsletter.php and receive a free copy of my eBook, "The 8 Biggest Hiring Mistakes Sales Managers Make."

You and your sales team might enjoy "The World's Shortest Sales Course" which is available as an eBook from the Resources section at www.SalesforceAssessments.com.
