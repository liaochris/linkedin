Silvia Cambie’s Summary

Cross-cultural communicator and journalist. Raised in Italy, educated in Austria, speaks five languages and spent her entire career working in an international environment.
Founder of Chanda Communications, a consultancy focused on strategic communication, Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and social media.
Specialised in innovative approaches to comms and PR based on practices from emerging economies. Monitors global Web 2.0 trends for clients and advises them on the integration of social media with conventional communication channels.
Blogs about cross-cultural issues at XCulture and is read by 10,000 each month.
Author of " International Communications Strategies - Developments in cross-cultural communications, PR and social media" published by Kogan Page and nominated for the Financial Times Goldman Sachs Business Book of the Year Award 2009.
Recognised public speaker.
Serves as director on the executive board of the International Association of Business Communicators (IABC) and as VP of The International Alliance for Women (TIAW), a global network of 50,000 for the economic empowerment of women.

Silvia Cambie’s Specialties:

Cross-cultural communication, Corporate Social Responsibility, emerging economies, Web 2.0, blogging, the female economy, policy, message development and public speaking.
