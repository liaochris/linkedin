  • Researcher

    (Public Company; MSFT; Computer Software industry)

    I was leading a project under joint Russian NTO office (Microsoft Russia) / PC|3 (Microsoft Corp.) initiative on context awareness and its applications for adaptive user interfaces.     The project was showcased to two Microsoft Corporate VPs, Bill Mitchell of PC|3 and Dan Ling of Microsoft Research.
