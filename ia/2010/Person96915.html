Jessica Cobble’s Summary

Jessica Cobble is a Certified Wholistic Kinesiologist and a Licensed Massage Therapist, in the state of New Mexico. She has a private practice in south valley, Albuquerque on her little farm.
She has had training in a variety of therapies that she integrates, to give the client a treatment specific to their own needs. Jessica has been interested in natural remedies to heal and balance the mind, body and spirit, for most of her life. She is passionate about helping people empower themselves by taking charge of their own healing process.
