Charlie Schiano’s Summary

25 years of progressive experience in the Information Technology (IT) field, held mid-level and senior positions in Operations, Systems Development, Program Management, Proposal Management, Requirements Analysis, SDLC Implementation, Vendor Liaison & Contract Management, and Customer Support, worked with Engineering, Manufacturing, Research & Development, Procurement, Human Resources, and Business Operations departments in accomplishing IT goals.

BS Mathematics Adelphi University and a MS Brooklyn Polytechnic University in Business Management (quivalent to MBA). Various in-house and external training courses relative to the IS industry.

Active with professional organizations within the IT industry and have presented papers at various conferences. Currently active member of the Program Management Institute and maintain contact with many IT professionals who, at various times, have helped me resolve my own IT problems. I was selected in 2005 as one of the top 100 IS professionals in the US by Computerworld for my lifelong work in the IT industry.

Strong written and verbal communications skills, made presentations to senior IT management during my career. Prior to current employment, reported to the President of an IT company and held position of General Manager, Software Development, Project Management, and Implementation at West Coast Division in California, where information technology systems for Police, Fire and EMS departments were developed.

Applications worked on include Missle Defense Systems, Weather Systems, EIS, HRIS, Records Management, Board of Elections modernization, Food Service Distribution, Factory Automation, Shop Floor Automation, Automated Test Equipment (ATE), Workforce Management, and systems for EMS, Police & Fire Departments.
