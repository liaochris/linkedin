Dan Wallace-Brewster’s Summary

Mr. Brewster is currently excited for his next opportunity to contribute to the marketing and promotion team of a great organization. He is available for contract or full-time roles within forward-thinking challengers and market leaders. He was most recently the Senior Director of Marketing & Promotion for RazorGator, Inc., an event experience e-commerce company in the sports and entertainment markets. While overseeing website merchandising, market research, public relations, promotions and traffic/conversion maximization, he strengthened partnerships with strategic partners like NFL Alumni Association, Fox Sports, CBS Sports, Amazon.com, the NCAA and the Tournament of Roses. He formerly served as Executive Director of Member Marketing for International Cruise and Excursions, Inc.(ICE), overseeing the growth of consumer travel clubs. His background includes extensive experience in sports marketing. He has served as the Director of Marketing for the Phoenix Coyotes, supervising all advertising, promotions and in-arena entertainment. Prior to joining the Coyotes, Brewster served as Manager of Advertising & Special Events for the Los Angeles Dodgers and as the Assistant Athletic Director – Marketing for the University of Southern California. Brewster has won national recognition from the Promotion Marketing Association for Hispanic marketing programs at the Dodgers and is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and events. He has also taught Market Research at the University of San Francisco’s School of Sports Management. He is a graduate of the University of Southern California (BA ’93; MBA ’02).
