Mark Sutherland-Fisher’s Summary

Legally qualified former solicitor experienced working with SMEs particularly shareholder owned businesses.

Developing a portfolio of non-executive role as H.R. director in a wide range of companies and industries throughout the UK.

I am a Fellow of the Institute for Independent Business and Member of the Institute of Directors.

As I cannot find anywhere else to include it, as a youth I was a member of the 101st Glasgow company of the Boys' Brigade (1968-1978) growing up with among others David Moyes the Everton F.C. Manager and it would be good to get back in contact with some of the chaps who were also members.

Delighted to see there is a company link for James Fisher and Sons PLC, the company founded by my great great great granduncle James Fisher after whom my grandfather was named.

Mark Sutherland-Fisher’s Specialties:

Sourcing and recruiting personnel for businesses of all types not just in the UK, especially those located in rural communities with limited access to major labour markets.
