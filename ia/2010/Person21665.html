Benjamin Baer’s Summary

Benjamin has more than 20 years of technology marketing experience -- in tactical, strategic and leadership capacities with both start up and Fortune 500 companies.

With a proven track record in creating and managing marketing, product management, and business development teams and demonstrable experience in recruiting, developing and managing marketing talent to achieve a diverse set of goals -- product development, product launches, life cycle management and revenue / sales -- Benjamin has proven his value to a myriad of technology companies.

Benjamin Baer’s Specialties:

Experience in business-to-business marketing and selling information technology to the Fortune 2000, including manufacturing, telecommunications and OEM, finance and healthcare industries. Technology focused on infrastructure from datacenter (storage, hardware), network to the desktop (traditional and alternative clients) and all the software in between including virtualization, SaaS, Cloud Computing and software interoperability.
