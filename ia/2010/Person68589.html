Kurt von Schleicher’s Summary

Kurt has for three decades enjoyed serving pastorally in part-time, bi-vocational (with high-end hotel work) and in full-time local church settings (both in mega and smaller churches in the United States.. as well as in the third world and in Europe).

Missionary and evangelistic outreaches have taken Kurt and his family to 21 nations. See fish4souls.org

Kurt von Schleicher’s Specialties:

Equipping and applying evangelism principles in outreaches with multiple local churches that are fitting for certain geographic areas and cultures with the primary aim to glorify God by seeing the harvest assimilated into those congregations. How we (each individually, or corporately in a local church or a para-church ministry) biblically accomplish our purpose is as important as the purpose itself. And having a right spirit in all we do is imperative as is having right applicable doctrine.
