  • Co-founder

    Spanish Connector L.L.C.

    (Privately Held; E-Learning industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Spanish Connector is an online Spanish tutoring service that uses free video/audio conferencing software such as Skype to connect to students from around the world. We offer both one-on-one and group classes which are taught by our qualified and highly trained native Spanish speaking professors.

    Spanish Connector is based out of Atlanta, GA. USA with an office in La Paz, Bolivia.

    Some of the benefits to the businesses and individuals we work with include:

    •Save on transportation costs
    •Save on materials. We provide you with all course content and supporting materials.
    •Save time. Since you can take our classes from your office, home, on the road, or anywhere else, you will have more time to dedicate to your personal life or business activities.
    •Learn industry-specific-Spanish terms and phrases to advance in your career and to create new business opportunities.
    •No need for extra space in your building to accommodate classes.
    •No need to use work hours to attend our classes.

    We guarantee top notch quality of our Spanish lessons by constantly monitoring and training our professors as well as by asking our students for feedback to always improve our service.
