Fred Feiner’s Summary

Yankee Public Relations is led by Fred Feiner, a seasoned public relations and marketing communications professional with over 20 years of experience in the biotechnology, pharmaceutical, finance and development industries.

Fred represents Octapharma USA a subsidiary of a Swiss-based biopharmaceutical company. He is an experienced pharmaceutical crisis communications consultant, having represented Élan in its historic effort to return Tysabri to the marketplace following tragic patient outcomes.

Fred has also represented many of the largest real estate investment firms and development companies in the NY/NJ region, including Savanna, Palisades Financial, SJP Properties and Normandy Real Estate Partners.

Fred is a past member of the Board of Trustees of the Architecture, Construction, and Engineering Mentor Program of New Jersey and provided the organization with pro bono public relations services. He has represented many non-profit organizations, including the Community Preservation Corporation of New Jersey, Urban Land Institute of Northern New Jersey and Alternatives, Inc. Fred has also led public relations and marketing programs for many non-profit hospitals and health systems. Prior to founding Yankee PR, Fred was a Vice President with one of the largest agencies in the New York metropolitan area.

Fred received a BA in Journalism from Rutgers University and an MPA from New York University’s Robert F. Wagner Graduate School of Public Service. He is a past Trustee of the Alexandria Township Education Foundation and is a member of the Hunterdon County Chamber of Commerce.
