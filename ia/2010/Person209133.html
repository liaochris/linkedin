Tyler Dunham’s Summary

Tyler Dunham received his Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering from The Ohio State University (cum laude) and with Honors in Engineering. Tyler received his Juris Doctorate degree from Capital University (cum laude). Tyler is admitted to practice law in Ohio and before the United States Patent and Trademark Office. He is also a member of the Columbus Intellectual Property Law Association.

Prior to joining Standley Law Group LLP, Tyler designed and tested automotive safety systems and various consumer products. His experience includes tooling design, manufacturing/assembly analysis, technical drafting, and the design and testing of various mechanical/electromechanical systems.

Tyler Dunham’s Specialties:

Tyler's law practice is directed to the preparation and prosecution of domestic and international patent applications involving inventions in the mechanical, electrical, and electromechanical arts. Tyler is also involved in the preparation and prosecution of federal trademark applications as well as general counselling on Intellectual Property matters.
