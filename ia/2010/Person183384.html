Jeff Sauls’s Summary

Online publishing executive responsible for directing strategic partnerships for two of American City Business Journals' new media assets: Portfolio.com & bizjournals.com.

Portfolio.com covers the biggest and most interesting stories about corporations, small businesses, and entrepreneurs.

bizjournals.com provides breaking local business news, industry briefs, and authoritative profiles on local businesses & executives.

More than 700 journalists and nearly 450 advertising professionals work for American City newspapers, magazines and Web sites.

American City Business Journals is the largest publisher of business newspapers in the United States and is a unit of Advance Publications, Inc. which also operates Conde Nast Magazines, Parade magazine, Fairchild Publications, the Golf Digest companies, Newhouse Newspapers and cable television interests.
