Ellen Finkelstein’s Summary

I'm the author of numerous books on PowerPoint and AutoCAD. I've also written on Flash and RSS feeds. (In total, my books have sold over 300,000 copies.) My Web site, www.ellenfinkelstein.com, offers free PowerPoint and AutoCAD tips. I publish the PowerPoint Tips Newsletter and AutoCAD Tips Newsletter, with associated blogs. Sign up at www.ellenfinkelstein.com.

Check out my books on Amazon.com:

- How to Do Everything with PowerPoint 2007 (or 2003)
- PowerPoint for Teachers: Dynamic Presentations and Interactive Classroom Projects
- AutoCAD 2010 and AutoCAD LT 2010 Bible
- Flash CS4 For Dummies

Need help with PowerPoint? 101 Tips Every PowerPoint User Should Know can help. Go to http://www.ellenfinkelstein.com/estore/101tips2003.html.
Need help with PowerPoint 2007? Try my video course, "PowerPoint 2007: Make the Upgrade Easy" at http://www.ellenfinkelstein.com/estore/upgrade_ppt_2007_video.html.

I conduct webinars and seminars on PowerPoint and presentation skills. I've created video courses, e-books, and webinars on these topics. My talks are geared towards creating powerful, high-impact presentations; they explore the full range of content, design, and delivery.

Some topics:
