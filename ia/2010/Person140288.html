Mark Elliott’s Summary

After developing and maintaining pharmacy claims billing and adjudication systems for most of the past 20 years, I have a significant pool of experience upon which to draw to assist other organizations in their efforts toward compliance with NCPDP Standards, real-time pharmacy claim processes, and HIPAA regulations. My more recent work with RxCLAIM systems setup, operations and enhancement requests from both the client and internal perspectives also support my ability to contribute to PBM integration and migration projects that involve this platform. I look forward to sharing these skills and experiences with an organization in need of technical analysis and support of their daily PBM operations, HIPAA compliance efforts, participation in industry standards development and new business development.

Mark Elliott’s Specialties:

NCPDP Standards, real-time healthcare claims processing, HIPAA, pharmacy benefit management, PBM, SQL, RxCLAIM, COBOL, Pascal, Java
