Mike Uretz’s Summary

Nationally recognized expert on EHR and Health IT Selection and Contract Negotiation. Has been involved with all aspects of technology selection, negotiation, and acquisition for over 25 years. Focused on helping providers, CEOs, administrators, and practice managers select EHR vendors, structure and negotiate contracts, and insure projects meet budget and timeline requirements.

Has frequently conducted Vendor Selection and Contract negotiation seminars and workshops on both the state and national level and has authored a number of articles and papers including the popular report “7 Costly Mistakes made When Purchasing EHR and Health IT and the program “How to Survive Your EHR Contract”. In addition has served on various EHR workgroups througout the years and have had the honor of being a judge at a number of EHR awards
.
