Edvarcl Heng’s Summary

Edvarcl is absolutely loony over bites, bytes and the big, bad Web. A food reporter turned senior writer & video host at CNET, and then search engine product manager, he now strategies social media domination while running an array of media projects as a hobby.

These projects include video hosting, event emcee, video production, journalistic reporting, marketing (causes & products), public relations (product & event).

Edvarcl has also recently turned his attention to analyzing social media, mobile applications, game mechanics and CSR. He fully intends to leverage on his existing and new skillsets to fashion an incredible new business concept.
