M Darren Root, CPA.CITP’s Summary

Darren has been successful at creating a highly efficient and effective CPA firm in Bloomington, Indiana. As a result of his successes in building and managing his CPA firm he has become a sought after speaker at many national conferences. Darren created an organization called RootWorks llc to help other accounting firms around the country move their firms into what he calls the "Next Generation Accounting Firm". As a technology enthusiast, he likes all things that relate to technology.

Over the next few years Darren plans on continuing to manage and grow his CPA Firm while working to grow and expand his consulting firm to serve other accounting firms around the country.
