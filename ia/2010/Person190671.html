Susan Kaplan Williams’s Summary

MOTIVATIONAL SPEAKER – Inspiration with a KicK:

Susan has been speaking for many years along with providing storytelling to children, and is the author of Auntie Suzie Raises a Turkey - the first in the Auntie Suzie's Animal Tails series.

Susan is a motivator who speaks with her heart and soul. Her energy, enthusiasm and compassion has audiences begging for more… as she motivates them to live life to the fullest and to reach for their own special dreams.

Life is about opportunities, challenges and obstacles. How we handle those challenges and obstacles often determines the opportunities that come our way. Susan believes an opportunity to speak to your group or organization may just be the opportunity that makes a difference in someone’s life.

COMMUNICATIONS CONSULTANT/PROFESSIONAL WRITER
Susan has more than 15 years in corporate communications, public relations and community relations. She also has more than 20 years in the oil & gas, energy and petrochemical industries. Susan has deep experience in creating positive and effective public perception, building key relationships within communities and their leaders, providing reliable, accurate and timely messaging and positioning or maintaining organizations as industry leaders.

She has served as a communications consultant to executive management by producing strategic direction for communicating corporate objectives and providing executive speech writing skills to local CEO’s and international dignitaries.

Susan has managed the development and negotiation of corporate memberships with civic and economic development organizations and her entrepreneurial experience provides her with a solid ability to build corporate brand image. Susan has demonstrated leadership, organizational and interpersonal skills.
