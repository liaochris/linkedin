Jess deCourcy Hinds’s Experience

  • Librarian/Educator

    Bard High School Early College

    (Libraries industry)

    August 2008 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    I am building Bard Queens' first library and teaching research skills and creative writing, as well as organizing author readings and coordinating the student literary magazine and blog. This is my dream job.

    Our library will open its doors in September 2009 in our new Long Island City location. We have nearly 900 titles now, and we need 5,000 to reach the minimum requirements of a secondary school of our size. In this economic climate, we're having trouble securing funds. So I'm working very hard to fill the shelves with bright, colorful books any way I can -- even driving to Massachusetts to fill 2 vans and a U-Haul with a donation of 660 books! Grant-writing also consumes a lot of my time.

    The Gates Foundation has already funded one project through DonorsChoose.org, and will match any donations from the public on my grant project, "Exciting Science Books for a New Library." To learn about my project and DonorsChoose, please see the link below:
    http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/viewRegistry.html?registryId=21953
