Cesar Abueg’s Summary

Cesar is a ten year web veteran with a passion for business, and people. He is the President & CEO of SDG Agency, a small web design & marketing agency based in Melbourne, Florida. One of the many things he does for fun.

As an aspiring serial-entrepreneur, he recently co-founded FlashDrivePros.com, a USB flash drive data recovery service company, with business partner Ryan Stutzman (whom he has not met in person, yet). He manages the company's online presence, branding, and social media marketing efforts.

He is a firm believer of the "Go-Giver" laws, the "Linchpin Manifesto", and creating your "Tap" moments.

If he is not traveling, building websites & businesses on the side, he spends time with his lovely family traveling all over the US.

He resides in Melbourne & Orlando, Florida, with his wife of 9 years and their two wonderful young girls.
