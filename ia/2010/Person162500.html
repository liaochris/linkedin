Pete Leibman’s Summary

PETE@IDEALIZENOW.COM


CAREER EXPERT AND COLLEGE SPEAKER:

Pete Leibman has spoken to audiences as large as 4,000+ students, and he is the creator of the Washington Wizards’ Sports Careers Day, an event attended by thousands of students since 2004.

Despite being told it would be “impossible,” Pete networked his way into his dream job to work for a pro sports franchise after graduating Phi Beta Kappa from Johns Hopkins University in 2003.

Less than 2 years later, he was promoted into front-office management for the NBA’s Washington Wizards (at the age of 23). He then managed and trained entry-level staff, while being ranked as the franchise’s #1 Salesperson for 3 straight years.

Pete created Idealize Enterprises in 2009 so that he could give students and recent graduates the confidence and strategies needed to get their dream jobs and achieve career success of their own. He has spoken at colleges nationwide, including top schools such as Stanford University and Johns Hopkins University.


FITNESS AND NUTRITION EXPERT:
A former college athlete, Pete is also President of BetterFitness BetterHealth, a private fitness and nutrition company that helps students and young professionals get healthier so that they can live better and longer.

Pete Leibman’s Specialties:

Professional Development, Training, Job Search, Networking, Career Advancement, Speaking to students and recent graduates
