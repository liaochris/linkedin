Gregory Gartland, PMP’s Summary

After consulting for 12 years, I realize there is a need for solid IT support catering to small businesses. Small businesses have their own unique set of challenges not normally found in larger companies. These challenges, combined with limited staff, IT budget or knowledge can make even small computer problems seem huge.

My goal is to take my large company experience and help small businesses grow their computer systems intelligently. Helping a growing company make wise and intelligent IT decisions early can save a lot of money down the road.
