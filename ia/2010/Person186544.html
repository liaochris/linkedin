Jeff Clark’s Summary

During the day, our hero (@ClarkleSparkle) is in Chicago helping crowdspring.com launch new channels, increase new project conversions and polish up their online presence.

At night, he works on creating a mobile scavenger hunt at www.heylookit.org while watching Boston Bruins hockey.

In the past, our hero has worked at Walt Disney World (wore a costume and everything), won a few ADDY Awards and even had his picture on the front page of the Wall Street Journal. One of those cool hand-drawn ones.

Oh, and has worked on web projects in the virtual goods, music and creative spaces.

Our hero does not wear a cape but has been seen sporting a hockey jersey from time to time.

Jeff Clark’s Specialties:

Web design from wireframes to execution (XHTML, CSS, jQuery, PHP, MySQL, Wordpress and a tiny splash of Django), print design (posters, marketing collateral, advertising, etc using Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Acrobat and Illustrator), online marketing, evangelism, word of mouth marketing, social media marketing.
