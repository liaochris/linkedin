Roland Clarke PhD’s Summary

At present, I am in private practice under the name Clarke Energy Associates, Barbados.

My recent experience include:

• Senior Trainer retained by the World Bank to present four (4) workshops in the use of RETScreen for project analysis of renewable energy projects. Workshops presented in Jakarta - Indonesia, Hanoi - Vietnam, Manila - Philippines, and Beijing - China. Workshops sponsored by ESMAP.

• Intervenor in the rate proceeding of the Barbados Light and Power Company to the Fair Trading Commission, Barbados. Proposed an alternative methodology for the computation of the utility’s weighted cost of capital that takes full cognizance of expected returns on equity in Barbados, rather than simply that of the USA.

• International Expert retained by the Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Partnership, REEEP, Vienna, Austria.

• Specified and supplied 8KWp of photovoltaics (PV) panels to the Ministry of Public Works, Barbados.

• Consultant to RETScreen International, Natural Resources Canada. Co-authored a training manual on the legal aspects of clean energy projects. See http://www.retscreen.net/ang/legal_aspects_of_energy_projects.php

• 2002-08, Project Manager of the Caribbean Renewable Energy Development Programme (CREDP), CARICOM Secretariat, Guyana with funding from GEF/UNDP of US$4.7 million.

• 1997-2001, Senior Analyst, electric utility (Northeast Utilities, CT, USA) DSM and energy efficiency program planning and economics, including production cost simulation of NEPOOL using UPLAN. (Assigned by Navigant Consulting)

• 2001-02, Lead Research Analyst, conservation and load management, NSTAR.

o PhD, energy management and policy, University of Pennsylvania
o MSc Alternative Energy, Dept of Mechanical Engineering, Reading University, UK
o Senior Member of the Association of Energy Engineers, USA
o Member of the IEEE

Roland Clarke PhD’s Specialties:

Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency. Trainer - RETScreen. Project analysis and economics, project preparation for financing, green house gas analysis. Electric utility avoided costs analysis, production cost modelling, market price forecasting using UPLAN. Demand side management (DSM) benefit cost analysis, evaluation, market transformation. Smart Grid. Speaker and advocate for clean energy.
