Sarah Allen’s Experience

  • Owner

    Sarah Allen Consulting

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2009 — Present (2 years )

    Sarah Allen Consulting is in the game that I've worked in for the past ten years - Communications, Public Relations, Marketing, Brand Management, Events, Online and Social Media.

    Taking my MarComms background into Social Media, Sarah Allen Consulting shows organisations how to operate in this exciting and friendly space with confidence, all the while keeping an eye on their marketing and organisational objectives.

    We design and deliver Social Media workshops tailored to your requirements, and aim to show that technology is a enabler, a way for you to converse with new markets about your brand and product, and not something to be intimidated by.

    At request, we can undertake analysis of your existing marketing activities and brand - both online and offline - and provide recommendations to freshen your presence.

  • Marketing, Communications, PR and Social Media Lead

    Media140

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    May 2009 — January 2010 (9 months)

    Initiated the first Australian Media140 event, an organisation that investigates the impact of the real-time web on media, marketing, politics and communication. It all started with a tweet to Media140 founder Ande Gregson in May 2009 and went from there!

    For their first event in 2009, Media140 Sydney, held at the ABC Centre in Ultimo, Sarah Allen Consulting was dedicated to the promotion of this groundbreaking two-day event (online and offline), event logistics and looking after our media partners, contributing in no small part to making it a success and sell-out.

    From media interviews, producing marketing collateral, looking after our sponsors and partners and generating buzz about the event on Twitter 24-7, Sarah Allen Consulting took it all on with great passion and drive.

    Media140 now has a strong presence in Australia, with events held in Perth in early 2010 and an exciting line-up for their #OzPolitics showdown in Canberra, July 2010.
