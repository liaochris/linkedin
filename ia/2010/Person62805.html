Erik Jan Koedijk (twitter: @erikjan)’s Summary

More than 18 years experience in IT/Telecom/Mobile industry (technical, sales, marketing and strategic level). I’ve built and managed local and European sales, marketing and channel teams; mainly in the Telecom and ICT sector, for example for NOKIA.

I have had the honor to work with so many great people and have built an international network; mainly in the ICT & telecom arena. I am passionate about people and love to manage (virtual) teams and inspire them, challenge them and implement new ways of customer engagement/interaction.

Designing new (channel) strategies, re-organizing distribution channels and to add the social component is what inspires me too. On a regular basis I speak at conferences, have consultancy assignments, host workshops and assess sales & marketing plans.

Need a sound social media strategy now?
As most (large and midsize) companies are struggling with the new way of customer interaction, social networks, communities etcetera and connect the social part to their CRM, it is nowadays about defining the social media strategy. This is difficult as it touches many departments and agencies and requires someone who is a creative, pragmatic problem solver. I would like to play the director’s role, being the man in the middle who creates and executes by enabling a virtual team of marketing, agencies, comms, customer service and ICT. If you want to implement Social Media into your organization; I am your man!
