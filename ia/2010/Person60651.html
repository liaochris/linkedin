  • CEO

    MUST - the Manchester United Supporters Trust

    (Non-Profit; Sports industry)

    May 1999 — Present (10 years 9 months)

    MUST - the independent Manchester United Supporters' Trust. Formed originally in 1998 (as SUAM) by Richard Hytner (Deputy Chairman of Saatchi & Saatchi Worldwide & now also President of MUST), political journalist Michael Crick (BBC Newsnight), sports journalist Jim White (Daily Telegraph) & a handful of other Manchester United supporter shareholders, the organisation transformed into MUST in 2005. We now have over 36,500 members holding more than £2.6 million of members' funds.

    We're launching a project to grow the e-membership through social media with a target of a million members (one MUFC facebook page alone has > 800k). As part of the membership expansion we're actively seeking partnerships with social media marketing, sponsorship and advertising professionals to grow membership & develop revenues for the trust, incentivising mass member participation through cashback commission sharing.

    OUR OBJECTIVE: MUST believes the interests of Manchester United Football Club & its supporters are best served by a partnership between owners & supporters - ultimately leading to supporter ownership in the long term. The club & business should continue to be managed by the best professionals but also benefit from the maximised affinity between the supporter and the club that only comes with supporter ownership. Barcelona, might be seen as a fine, though imperfect, example of our preferred ownership model for Manchester United.

    Over the short term we aim to build the network of MUFC supporters and businesses in preparation for participation in a new ownership model for the club when the opportunity arises. We are actively exploring options with investment bankers and the established football dealmakers.

    MUST is a not-for-profit organisation, a member trust of the Government backed Supporters Direct (www.supporters-direct.org ) and an Industrial & Provident Society registered with the Financial Services Authority (I-MUST Limited, number 30024R)
