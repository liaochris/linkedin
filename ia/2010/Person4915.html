Alaa Awad’s Summary

Management Researcher since 1995, HR & Organizational Learning Consultant since 1997, Business Lecturer and Corporate Trainer since 2000, Career Counselor & Coach since 2004, HR & OD Director since 2006, and Author of 4 scientific publications in Crisis Management and Organizational Learning (1996; 1998; 2001; 2002).

Business Psychology Scholar since 1992, mastering a battery of personality profiling and aptitudes assessment tools, namely MBTI; Keirsey Intelligence Types; DiSC Classification (Certified International Trainer); Psychodynamic Personality Portraits; and some applications of NLP.

Living and working in Canada for 6 years (1995-96; 1997-2002).

Has visited Italy, Vatican, Austria, Ex-Yugoslavia (Serbia and Slovenia), France, Greece, Morocco, Jordan, Arabia and Emirates, for short trips, training or conferences since 1983.

Sub-Saharan intercultural experience at the International University for African Development in Alexandria "Senghor University" (1993-95).

Trilingual with strong background in American Pragmatism, affiliation to the Francophone Community since childhood, and excellent formal Arabic.
