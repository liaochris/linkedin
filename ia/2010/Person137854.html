Matt Kozlowski’s Education

  • Allegheny College

    Bachelor of Arts , Psychology and Communication Arts , 2000 — 2003

    I transferred to Allegheny College after attending John Carroll University for my freshman year. Allegheny is a liberal arts college with about 2,000 undergraduate students in Meadville, Pennsylvania. My senior thesis studied the effects of AOL Instant Messenger on college campus life. At the time, "AIM" as it is commonly known, was a key communication tool for students (used by more than 97% of respondents). In 2003, we could see the infancy of what has turned into the culture of constant connection (Facebook status, Twitter feeds). Today, with the ubiquity of the text message, we would likely see drastically reduced usage of AIM among college students. If I were graduating this May, my senior thesis would focus on Twitter and its role in the lives of students.
