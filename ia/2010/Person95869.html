Jeremiah Stanghini’s Summary

Jeremiah has a passion for systems and understands that everything in the visible and non-visible parts of the universe are their own system and simultaneously, integrated into a larger system. He uses his most recent work in transpersonal psychology to effect positive change with his clients. Transpersonal psychology is: “an approach to psychology that a) studies phenomena beyond the ego as context for b) an integrative/holistic psychology; which provides a framework for c) understanding and cultivating human transformation,” (Definition adapted from Hartelius, Caplan, & Rardin, 2007). In short, transpersonal psychology takes into account all aspects of a person – mind, body, spirit, and beyond. Jeremiah especially enjoys this approach because he feels that much of what plagues his clients are things that they would not have otherwise been able to determine through cognitive, logical thought.

“I believe that each of us has something unique and creative to enrich the world. However, we have been socialized into habitual ways of thinking that separate us from our deepest wisdom and inner knowing. It is my conviction that one of my roles involves supporting today’s global leaders in the development and responsible use of intuition. Enhanced ability to utilize this powerful human capacity will contribute to a better world by way of effective, more efficient, and successful decisions. I will continue to cultivate my own intuition and the intuition of those around me by publishing works that maximize our capacity to marry intellect and intuition for excellence in decision-making.”
