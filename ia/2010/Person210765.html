Vinny Catalano’s Summary

Healthcare Reform is coming. What will it mean to you and your company? There are many questions that I can help answer and provide a quality employee benefits roadmap for the future so you can make good decisions.

Cost, wellness, recruitment, retention. Let me show you how an integrated plan makes sense for your firm.

Health insurance is a complex business with many products from many companies.

>How do you know your plan offers sufficient value for its cost?
>How do you gauge it's effectiveness with your employees?
>Do your employees really value what you offer?

With over 25 years of sales, marketing and business strategy experience, I can help firms of any size maximize the value of what they spend on healthcare and other benefits.

My connections can help too. I serve on the UC Davis Cal Aggie Alumni Association Board of Directors, the Sacramento Area Commerce and Trade Organization (SACTO) Member Relations Committee and the Kaiser Broker Advisory Board.
