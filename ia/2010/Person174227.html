Rob Bell’s Summary

At Ex Ignibus, I work primarily with small businesses, enabling them to increase revenues, reduce costs and improve customer interaction through internet services, web applications, and digital and new media marketing.

The Internet, and more recently Social Media, have changed the game in marketing, and we help you create and maintain web sites that do useful things that your customers will love, social profiles across all major social networks, podcasting and video site submissions, and run digital marketing campaigns through emails, web-based newsletters, promotions, social media and content strategies to create and extend your online business model.

You no longer need to be a big company with big budgets to make a big impression, what we do works for all sizes and types of business from sole traders to multinationals.

We work with many diverse clients including Financial Services, Language Schools, Transport Companies, top Dance Music Acts and DJs, Event Companies, Success Coaches and Service Providers.

Our Ethos is 5-IT - Integrity, Inspiration, Intelligence, Integration and Information Technology
