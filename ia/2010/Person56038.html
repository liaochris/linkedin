Diana Manos’s Summary

I have 20 years' experience as a journalist in the Washington, DC metro area, including 10 years as an editor. I have covered healthcare on Capitol Hill, at the White House and at federal agencies for 13 years. I have more than three years' experience as a daily reporter, including one year as a Web editor. Currently, I am a senior editor for Healthcare IT News and Healthcare Finance News, providing daily online and monthly print news for healthcare executives. My reporting skills range from hard news and news analysis to magazine-length features. I have also had experience as a media relations specialist and a creative writer for a federal social ad campaign.

Diana Manos’s Specialties:
