Jeff Grundmann, C.E.’s Experience

  • Owner - ACFN of Denver

    ACFN

    (Privately Held; Financial Services industry)

    November 2005 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    ACFN of Denver provides ATM's for a variety of businesses throughout the greater Denver. While we concentrate on the hospitality industry we are always open to new placements.

    Our clients include some of the foremost (and award winning) hotels in the Denver metro area and their chains. Hotels such as The Curtis, The Crowne Plaza Denver, Marriott Renaissance Flatirons, Westin Westminister and The Inverness Hotel and Conference Center, are included in our portfolio. We also have placements in a variety of other hospitality based businesses throughout the area.

    From Castle Rock to Fort Collins and Vail to Fort Morgan and everywhere in the Denver / Boulder metro area, we are the fastest growing ATM network in Colorado. We are dedicated to providing our clients and their guests superior service.

  • Owner

    Pisces Moon Studio

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Arts and Crafts industry)

    July 1999 — Present (11 years 1 month)

    I produce art glass in many forms - stained glass windows and doors; fused dinnerware, clocks and decorative art; and cast sculptural art.

    The stained glass windows and doors I create are one of a kind, each designed for the indiviual location and clients desires. I typically use elements of cast and fused glass in my pieces adding a dimensional element not found in common glass windows.

    My fused glass dinnerware can be found in a variety of locations worldwide. Some of these items include simple daily use dinnerware (plates, bowls and serving dishes) and some are purely decorative. Customization is always available to suite any taste or decor.

    My favorite creations are the sculptural cast pieces. These include the life sized nude torsos which I have gained some notoriety for. Each casting is a unique piece of art within itself. I am always happy to cast individuals as commission work.

    Please visit my website www.piscesmoon.com .
