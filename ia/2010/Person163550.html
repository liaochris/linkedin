Todd Smith’s Summary

Todd has over 11 years of experience in online advertising technology and monetization solutions for Display, Search, Social Media, & Email.

He has held executive and management positions at Intermix Media, responsible for its gaming division (over 100+ games created, realizing over $3mm in annual sales), managing strategy for MySpace Games, and as VP of Search for its paid search division (realizing over $14mm in annual sales).

Todd held the position of VP, Search Alliances at Fox Interactive Media, helping to manage one of the most high profile strategic partnerships; its $900 million dollar Google paid search deal with MySpace. Todd also managed Fox Interactive's new performance marketing initiative, forging performance marketing relationships with direct marketers and large internet brands to advertise on MySpace, American Idol, IGN, and Scout.

Currently, Todd runs all business development functions at Fox Audience Network, focusing primarily on (i) real-time-auction buying/selling; (ii) non-PII data procurement; (iii); evaluation of new ad formats & platforms; and (iv) distribution of company’s insights/analytics products to advertisers & publishers
