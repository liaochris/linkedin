Josh Stoffregen’s Summary

I joined Prudential in August 2009 after spending several years in journalism. Most recently, I held the position of managing editor at Institutional Investor’s Fund Action. There I managed a stable of reporters in New York and Washington, D.C. while also writing and reporting on the retirement beat. Previous to my editorship, I reported on the investment management beat for Compliance Reporter, where I covered hedge funds and other alternative investment vehicles. I have also held various contributing writer and columnist positions over the years.
