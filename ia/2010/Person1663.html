Abi Signorelli’s Summary

Extensive experience in leading internal comms teams at a variety of large organisations mainly within the telecoms industry. All included various elements of large scale business and/or cultural change.

My aim is to turn the workplace into somewhere people love to work with open, timely, involving and engaging two-way communication and to become a leading world-class internal communications team.

Currently researching social media/networking and collaborative communication techniques - let's keep up with the times!
