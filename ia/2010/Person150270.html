Murray Miskin’s Summary

Murray Miskin is the owner of Miskin Law Offices in Whitby and Peterborough, Ontario. It is a consumer law practice representing injured people with claims based on accidents and dangerous products. The firm also provides real estate and estate legal services. We are leaders in Alternative Dispute Resolution. Murray Miskin provides Arbitration and Mediation Services locally and internationally. He is the main trainer of commercial arbitrators in Ontario Canada since 1985. The firm's main office is in Whitby Ontario with a branch offices in Peterborough. Murray Miskin is very actively involved with the Canadian Bar Association on a volunteer basis. He is a member of the Board of Governors of his Alma Mater, Trent University, Peterborough, Ontario.

Murray Miskin’s Specialties:

Mediation, Arbitration, Court representation
