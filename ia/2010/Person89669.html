Johan Janssens’s Summary

Johan is Director and co-founder of Joomlatools. Johan has developed web solutions and tools for some of the largest international development organizations operating around the world. At Joomla! he was Project Manager for 2 years and held a position on the Board of Directors of Open Source Matters, Inc.

Johan is also the principal architect of the Joomla! Framework and held the role of Lead Developer during the development of Joomla! 1.5. Today, Johan has made nearly 3,500 commits helping to create Joomla! v 1.5. In October 2008 Johan was recognised for his contributions as 'Most Valued Person' for Joomla. He is hopeful and confident that with Joomla!'s strong and growing community, Joomla! will continue to help more and more people all over the world connect with others.

Johan earned his master's degree in Computer Science and Interactive Media from GroepT University in Leuven, Belgium, he also has a bachelor's degrees in Electronics and ICT. He co-founded Joomlatools while working on DOCman once of the oldest and most popular extensions for Joomla.

Initially, Johan planned a career in game development. But, he discovered open source and realized working with free software was something he wanted to do. Johan has invested many years contributing to the open source community. He joined Mambo in April 2004, participating as a developer and core team member, and moved with the entire core team to help found Joomla!.

Currently, Johan is working on Nooku Content, a multi-lingual content manager for Joomla!, and Nooku Framework, a rapid extension development framework for Joomla!.
