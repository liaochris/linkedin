Eric Powers’s Summary

I view business, society, and information technology from an ethical and highly opportunistic altitude. Many colleagues and student are curiously engaged with my ability to exploit technology for business needs and identify new opportunities ahead of the curve.

An IT culture should openly encourage innovation failure. Environments that openly embrace the creation of ideas, intellectual property, and rapid prototyping methods are inviting to me. However, so are conservative environments where a catalyst for change brings new results. I tend to thrive in cross functional roles across IT, Business, and/or Marketing that leverage my technical knowledge and experience with databases, web applications, analysis, and solution prototyping.
