Francine LaMarr’s Summary

Francine LaMarr is a Certified Personal Brand/Social Media Strategist. She works with women entrepreneurs to launch or grow their business through client-attracting brands that identify their target audience, refine their messaging to reflect their unique value and benefits and support integrated results-driven promotional strategies.

Francine began her career in the corporate sector, which culminated with 20+ years of marketing experience at Fortune 100 companies such as Warner Bros and HBO, where she oversaw brand development and marketing for diverse, targeted brands and brand extensions.

Looking for a new challenge that would support her own passion for honest branding and creative marketing, Francine founded her Brand Strategy/Marketing consultancy in 2007. Since then, Francine has made corporate marketing strategies accessible, actionable and enjoyable to a clientele of entrepreneurs representing a wide range of businesses including finance, legal, insurance, performing arts, technology, communications, personal services, business services and consumer packaged goods. She works with clients to build traditional marketing strategies and then leverage each tactic to build an On-Line ID Strategy that drives new business.
