Don Goodman’s Summary

In a nutshell, I help people find jobs. I am a nationally recognized Career Expert and President of About Jobs (www.GotTheJob.com) which provides resume writing and job search assistance for professionals and executives worldwide. A certified resume writer (CPRW), career management coach (CCMC) and job search strategist (CJSS), I have helped thousands of people develop resumes and find jobs for over 10 years.

My background is probably a bit different from other resume writers and career coaches. A graduate of the Wharton School of Business and Commerce as well as Stanford University’s Executive Program, I have held a number of challenging executive roles at Fortune 500 and mid-sized firms where I led 4 turnarounds and over 6 startups to record levels of revenues, profits and market share.

I have been a Featured Keynote Speaker at dozens of Job Fairs, Executive Conferences, and State Agency events, have been interviewed numerous times on radio and television regarding employment issues and my work has been featured in over a dozen books for Best Resumes. I have also been quoted in hundreds of articles for resume writing and career coaching.

I have worked with the Career Placement Services of Johns Hopkins University, Clark University and Fairleigh Dickinson University. Additionally, I partnered with Hewitt Associates, a global consulting and outsourcing firm specializing in human resource solutions, to discuss Strategic Staffing with Fortune 500 companies.

So to answer your big question, “Don, how did you end up running a resume writing firm?”, the answer is simple: after leading a company to over 100% compounded annual growth over 5 years, I got tired of corporate politics and dealing with idiots...so I left to do what I love - helping people secure jobs and enjoy their lives.

You can see more about my services at www.GotTheJob.com or call 800-909-0109.
