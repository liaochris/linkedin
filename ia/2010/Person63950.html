Ellen Kozak’s Summary

Ellen M. Kozak is a professional writer as well as a copyright and publishing (and other media) lawyer. She is the author of the prize-winning book, From Pen To Print: The Secrets of Getting Published Successfully (published by Henry Holt & Co. in 1990) and of Every Writer's Guide to Copyright and Publishing Law (also from Henry Holt & Co., 3rd ed. 2004) as well as hundreds of published articles and two psudonymously published novels.

See her article on avoiding publishing scams in the Wisconsin Lawyer Magazine, December 2007: http://www.wisbar.org/AM/Template.cfm?Section=Home&TEMPLATE=/CM/ContentDisplay.cfm&CONTENTID=68934
