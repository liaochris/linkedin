Tony Angelucci, CHCR’s Summary

Over 20 years experience working within the different aspects of the HR Industry. Starting as a Vendor selling the benefits of partnering with a Recruitment Advertising Agency, then moving to the Media as the National Classifieds/Help Wanted Manager at the 2nd largest circulated newspaper in the Country to currently functioning as the Client working as a Recruiter at the largest medical center (960 licensed beds) west of the Mississippi, has provided me unique perspectives and an in-depth knowledge of Human Resources from all sides. In 2008, I became a Certified HealthCare Recruiter (CHCR).

Tony Angelucci, CHCR’s Specialties:

Recruitment Advertising, Media Buying (print, broadcast and online), Employee Referral and Retention Programs, Collateral Development, Extranets, Direct Mail, College Recruitment, Diversity Initiatives, Promotions (giveaways and premiums), Trade Show Booth Hardware, Sales Team Leadership, Ad Agency Selection and Review, Employee Centered Recognition Events, Recruiting, Sourcing, Onboarding, and Employer Brand Management.
