Ronnie Dauber’s Summary

I am a freelance writer, poet and published author with several earned college diplomas in literature for writing children and adult articles and books. I enjoy the challenge of researching and writing editorials for information sites, but my passion is to utilize my creativity and life experiences and write short stories and novels.

As a writer, I have published over 900 articles online and have earned merit badges and a channel management position with Helium.com.

I have written dozens of poems and received my first reward when I was 12 years old for my entry in a contest that involved my entire region. My poem, “A Dedication to President John F. Kennedy”, was framed and sent to the Whitehouse.

My first novel, MUDSLIDE, was published in September of 2009, and is a young adult adventure story that's filled with action and excitement and void of sexuality and profanity. It is currently listed as an ebook with Lebrary.com and as a hardcopy with Amazon.com. MUDSLIDE can be viewed on my website at: http://mudslidethenovel.wordpress.com/

I have just completed my second novel, WEB SECRETS, which is an adult thriller/mystery that is a loaded edge-of-the-chair drama, and again is void of profanity and sexuality. It is currently at the printers and will be available very soon on Lebrary.com and at Amazon.

Goals:
I have already started writing my next novel, have a third one plotted out and am co-writing a cookbook with another amazing author. A sequel to MUDSLIDE is a strong possibility for sometime next year.

I want to write as many novels as I can that will help encourage reading among children and adults. I think it's important to have books on the market that people can read for the enjoyment of the story without suffering the use of profanity, sexuality or substance abuse.
