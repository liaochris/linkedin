Christopher Conzen’s Summary

Since my first year of graduate school, I have worked primarily in the areas of student activities, orientation, and leadership development. I have worked at a four-year private, four-year public, and now a two-year community college. In addition to my current role in campus activities, I also assist with the enrollment management areas, serving as a back-up administrator to both counseling/advisement and admissions.

My long-term goal is to become a Dean of Students, utilizing the diverse experiences I now have the opportunity to be exposed to.
