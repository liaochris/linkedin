Mike Schoof’s Summary

My specialty is in growing revenue and increasing market share for companies, through individual effort and through other sales channels (including direct reports, channel and strategic partners), that have a complex sell (multiple buying influences, medium to long term sales cycle, and fierce competition), that benefit from using a consultative, solution selling-based, sales process and that deliver an engineered solution and a value proposition that have a clear strategic and/or financial ROI.

My primary markets of expertise are in the Energy (Utility/Oil/Gas/Water) and in Manufacturing. I also have worked with Fortune 1000 enterprise accounts.
