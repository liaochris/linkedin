Anthony Anikeeff’s Summary

Anthony Anikeeff’s Specialties:

(1) Government Contracts, (2) False Claims Act, (3) Suspension & Debarment Counselling and Litigation. Advise domestic / international companies on legal strategies to enter, expand, and protect position in Federal and state government contracts field. Represent government contractors in bid protests, claims, government investigations, suspension and debarment proceedings, False Claims Act proceedings, civil litigation and ADR matters. Develop and provide training on compliance plans.
