Betty Didcoct’s Summary

I am fascinated with how organizations form, grow, deal with conflict, and are motivated (or not) to serve the world. The way people interact, process information, make decisions, disagree (well or poorly) and move through various phases is a source of constant inspiration, frustration, and growth for me.
It never ceases to amaze me how our attitudes towards money, how we deal with the lack or abundance of it, how open or closed we are to talk about it affects our lives daily.
My goal is to witness and guide our (and my) growth in these areas and bring my experience and wisdom to facilitate healthy exchange and collaboration in all our endeavors.
