Alexander Zagoumenov’s Summary

Alexander Zagoumenov is a digital marketing expert who helps businesses adapt to a changing digital media landscape, including opportunities presented by social media. Prior to establishing his consulting business Alexander spent 8 years in digital marketing roles on both clients and agency side. Alexander offers a wide range of services – from keyword research and search ranking audits, to coaching in-house marketing teams on how to optimize their digital marketing efforts.

After a successful career managing digital marketing teams and products, Alexander now coaches other companies to achieve clarity in marketing objectives and optimize business processes. Alexander is no stranger to achieving success and managing change. He was ranked within top 15 junior alpine skiers in the world in 1997. Being an active member of the immigrant community in Calgary Alexander helped hundreds of new immigrants through managing a team blog at RussianHerald.com.

To learn more visit Alexander’s website www.zagoumenov.com, where he also blogs about digital marketing trends and practical applications. Alexander is an active member at the Digital Alberta.
