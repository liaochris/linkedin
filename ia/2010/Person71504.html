Adept at leveraging corporate resources into the maximum return on investment. An adroit and tactful negotiator; attaining corporate objectives with prudence and cultural sensitivity. Well-accustomed to the physical and mental demands associated with extensive international and transcontinental travel; international experience encompasses over 65 markets on six continents.
