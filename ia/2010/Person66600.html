Erika Chloe’s Summary

CEO and Founder of My Image Expert, New York's premier Image Consulting and Fashion Consulting firm with offices in Connecticut, New Jersey, Philadelphia, Delaware, Maryland and DC. My Image Expert provides: Image and Fashion Consulting Services, Corporate Image Consulting, Dress for Success Seminars, Individual Image Consultations, Personal Shopping, Male Makeovers, Female Makeovers, Grooming and Beauty tips, Etiquette Seminars, Wardrobe Analysis, Bespoke Clothing and Image Counseling.

Fashion and Style Expert and Innovator, Fashion Designer, Celebrity Stylist, Contributing Editor, Lecturer, On-Air Television Fashion Expert, and Founder of Custom Clothing Collection E.C. STITCH which caters to Athletes, Business Professionals an Formal Wear.

Erika Chloe has designed collections for Bloomingdales, Lord & Taylor, and Macy’s. As a New York stylist she has worked with FHM Magazine, Harper’s Bazaar Magazine, Redbook, Maverick Television, NBC, E- online, and many more.

Erika Chloe’s Specialties:

The Power of Corporate Identity, Dress for Success, Business Etiquette, Interview Etiquette, Creating Identity Though the Power of Image, Development of Personal and Professional Image,Trend Forecasting, Color Theory and Technology, Body Structure Analysis, Fashion Stylist and Editorial Stylist.
