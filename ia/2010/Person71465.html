Fred Atchison’s Summary

Over 38 years of experience as an expert in the field of electrical estimating, including over 25 years as a Sr. Electrical Designer. Designed estimating systems successfully using broad range of experience from field experience and as a Sr. Electrical Designer. Specialty in conceptual estimates, with quick power circuit design for motors and other loads to ensure accuracy in giving a true picture of the facility. Also quick designs are done on lighting, grounding and other systems. Started career as a journeyman electrician, then worked on low voltage to high voltage up to 345KV with hands-on experience. Worked as a Chief Inspector/Project Manager on electrical jobs for major oil companies.

Fred Atchison’s Specialties:

Electrical Estimating, Conceptual Estimating, Material Takeoff
