Christina Schultejans’s Summary

Corporate attorney with broad experience in general corporate transactions as well as specialty expertise with nonprofit entities and in corporate governance matters. Has had many teaching and speaking opportunities to educate students and other professionals on corporate governance and ethics issues. Skillful draftsmanship of wide variety of contracts including sponsorship agreements and vendor agreements as well as business-related shareholder agreements and other related documents. Has prepared website contracts and terms and conditions for multiple clients. Has developed 20+ HUD housing projects throughout the country. Assisted in divestiture of 11 major nursing home/senior care facilities as part of large bankruptcy plan. A team player with leadership, management and recruiting experience and experience building client base for private law firm.
