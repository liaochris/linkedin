Talisa Cherfils’s Summary

As a Human Resource Professional a substantial part of my role involves developing and initiating programs, policies, initiatives, and strategies that are aligned with organizational objectives. I work with leadership teams to promote excellent management practices. I also manage the life cycle of employees and design the workflow from recruitment and selection to separation. My roles have involved due diligence for acquisitions and mergers as well as litigation support. I have had the opportunity to successfully acquire over fifty employees through a major acquisition.
