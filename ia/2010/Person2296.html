Adam Ertel’s Summary

With more than 20 years in consumer product sales, marketing, and development experience, I am a leader in the field of product marketing and management. I have managed top-tier categories for some of the largest retailers. I have developed popular products and programs that continue driving sales today. I have worked with many of the best known brands to license their properties and have sold those programs into major retailers. My record shows a history of meeting or exceeding plans, turning around difficult situations, and driving success and profitability.
