Susan Brandt’s Summary

Results driven, detail oriented, collaborative marketing leader able to understand industries and organizations quickly and manage an array of projects within budget. Over 15 years of marketing and brand management experience focusing on Internet marketing, e-commerce and customer acquisition and retention. Brings a passion to work as well as a creative and strategic style for seeking solutions on key issues.
