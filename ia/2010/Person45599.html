Cynthia Chiarappa’s Summary

Quickly spots relevant patterns and issues, skillfully selects the optimal communication vehicles and creatively executes solutions that help to grow and transform an enterprise. More than 20 years experience developing, promoting and protecting corporate brands. Trusted advisor to top-tier executives, known to be energetic, candid, persuasive, compassionate and positive under pressure.

Cynthia Chiarappa’s Specialties:

Integrated marketing communications; Branding and positioning; Public relations and media affairs; New media; Market research and analysis; Concept, content and graphic design; Growth and change management; Crisis communications; New service launches; Labor communications; Community development; Executive decision support.

Cynthia Chiarappa’s Experience

  • Sr. Director Communications and Marketing

    California Pacific Medical Center

    (Non-Profit; 5001-10,000 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    September 1996 — Present (13 years 7 months)

    Developed and leads marketing, media relations, communications and public relations and plays a key leadership role in brand development strategy for CPMC, a 5-campus, 1,254 bed, $984 million medical center.

    Cynthia developed and manages the Medical Center’s community relations, public relations, and media relations strategies, including crisis communications. She developed a market-focused approach to the hospital’s strategic planning process and provided an organizational framework for the hospital’s leadership to align operational priorities with the hospital’s position and brand identity. She is the creative force behind the Medical Center’s advertising and branding efforts.

    Cynthia also manages the Medical Center's marketing efforts and played a key leadership role in identifying key issues impacting management and employee morale (and therefore the Medical Center’s brand) and recommending solutions to create a new organizational culture.
