Andy Arnold’s Summary

As an operations executive, I design, scale, and optimize business operations of formation and commercialization stage ventures, with a particular focus in online gaming, consumer internet, & consulting services. I specialize in developing high performing teams across the organization, and in a variety of industries.

Recent Success Highlights

• As the 2nd employee at Scient (Benchmark and Sequoia), established the company’s operations. Managed the San Francisco consulting office’s growth through $20M revenue in just 18 months after inception.
• Subsequently, relocated to London and established Scient’s European operations with offices in London, Munich, and Paris. Led European growth through $25M revenue, followed by a very successful IPO in May, 1999.
• At Havok (game development physics engine), established a professional services consulting unit, and quickly built a substantial new revenue channel while further immersing Havok with its customers – without increasing headcount. Acquired by Intel.
• Advisor to a venture capitalist and a renowned Stanford professor, developed business and strategic plans, financial model, and go-to-market plan for a formation stage serious gaming company. Developed investor and customer presentations and positioning.
• As 3rd employee of Beautifull, a venture backed food industry startup, established company operations and infrastructure, evolved company strategy, recruited and hired key team members.
