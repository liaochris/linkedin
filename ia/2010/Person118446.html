Dan Kozlowski’s Summary

Daniel Kozlowski is an architect, developer, and leader. He is continually surprised by how many technology companies asked him during an interview if he is on the business side or the tech side of the industry. Clearly these companies are not getting it. He believes that by looking at the technology industry leaders of today its clear they are not one sided people and have great business and technical minds.

Dan has over 18 years of computer system deployment experience. Included on his resume are large System Integrators such Accenture, Silicone Valley tech startup companies such as SupportSoft and most recently Stellent/Oracle. Dan has served in management and leadership roles as well as development and architecture positions. Dan also has worked with several open source systems to support some personal businesses ventures and discovered the power of applying best practices learned in the open source communities to the enterprise.

In his free time Dan enjoys boating, golfing, and skiing. He also is a tortured cubs fan as well as a fan of all things Illini. He is also hoping for another Bears super Bowl win... there is always hope he says. He reserves comment on the Cubs and a World Series appearance.

Dan Kozlowski’s Specialties:

Oracle UCM, CMS Systems, Really Cool Javascript, and making things work on any scale.
