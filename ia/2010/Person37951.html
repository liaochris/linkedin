Christine Taylor’s Summary

Christine is a resident of Nashville, TN. She is the V/P of Social Media/New Media Marketing for JTMarCom a full service digital marketing communications company.

Christine brings a proven track record in sales, marketing and entrepreneurship. She was one of Nashville's top performing promotional marketing salespersons for several years before starting her own promotional marketing firm. She counted several multi-national corporations as clients before selling her company. Clients included Bridgestone/Firestone USA and Canada, MCA Records etc. Christine designed, created and fulfilled a campaign to introduce Firestone Tires into all Kmart USA stores.

Christine has been a speaker for workshops on new media platforms such as Linkedin, Facebook, and Twitter. She has spoken at the "International Bare Metal Conference", Tennessee Chambers of Commerce State Meeting, National Association of Women Business Owners, and many others.

• Manages and continually optimizes a 50 state search marketing campaigns for Pacifier-B-Gone, Pet-Tao and other clients to promote their brand and customer service capability. These campaign utilizes AdWords, Yahoo!, and MSN with an aggressive ROI goal used to determine the success on a weekly basis.

• Utilizes search marketing and social media to increase traffic

• Develops Facebook Fan pages for current clients in an effort to encourage customer interaction and brand awareness

• Coordinates the social networking and in-game advertising task forces at JTMarCom to keep the entire media department up to date regarding new media advertising.

* Creates campaigns and "listening" platforms utilizing micro-media strategies such as Twitter

A native of Scotland, Christine is married to a John Taylor, President of JTMarCom. They have two daughters - Alexandra ( a screen writer in L.A.) and Tori (a Sophomore at UNC-Chapel Hill).
