Tim Rash’s Summary

As the lead weld engineer at a large automotive facility, my main responsibilities include the development and implementation of new automated and manual welding processes with ability to recommend and specify equipment details for resistance welding, GMAW, GTAW, and laser applications. These duties include developing, qualifying, and optimizing welding processes for applications. Identifying weld defects with solutions by creating repair operations for the defective weldments. Processing non-conformance reports and developing “Failure Mode Matrix” documentation. Coordinate between Manufacturing, Engineers and Customers as required. Develop system wide troubleshooting documentation. Manage the Ultrasound inspection system for quality assurance. Manage new product launches, prototypes, and equipment installation to include personnel training and continual improvement efforts.
