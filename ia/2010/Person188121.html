Shawn Butler, MBA’s Summary

Shawn Butler is an innovator in new media and online brand communication. Shawn’s company, Relevant Social Media, leverages social media technology with traditional marketing strategy to achieve powerful results in sales, new customer acquisition and fundraising. His clients have included legendary professional athletes, professional poker players, top-tier celebrities, as well as internationally recognized authors and speakers.

Previously, Shawn filled leadership roles in developing new media and traditional marketing campaigns for Home Depot, Southern Textile Service, Vytex Natural Rubber Latex, Diversitech, Nimbus Software, Sports Legends and the World Series of Poker. Shawn has a Bachelor's degree in Marketing, a Master’s in International Business, and an MBA from Georgia State University. Shawn is fluent in Spanish and is an international traveler, having lived, worked and studied on four continents. Currently, he sits on the board of directors for Turning Point, a cancer treatment and awareness organization.
