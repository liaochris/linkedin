Mark Read’s Summary

After 20 years with Hambros in IT, I resigned to start my own Internet business in 1999. ‘PropertyShowroom.com’ facilitated the marketing of private property sales on the internet by individual homeowners and provided an alternative to paying hefty real estate agent fees in the UK. It proved to be a successful service and I sold that business in 2004 when I emigrated to Perth, Western Australia. After four years as a Director of a mortgage business, I joined westjobs.com.au in 2009 as the General Manager and I’m responsible for the day to day running of the business and as it is a new venture. I am steering WestJobs through the three initial stages of any new Internet based business, content, traffic and revenue. WestJobs is starting to gain market traction and I am building good strategic alliances with other forward thinking businesses.

Mark Read’s Specialties:

e-marketing, web content optimisation, operational re-engineering, delivering business concepts via the Internet.
