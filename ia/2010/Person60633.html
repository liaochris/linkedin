Dumont Gerken Owen, Ph.D.’s Summary

I’m a proud member of the “Zoomer” generation, a Boomer who is vigorous, has an insatiable love of learning, years of experience, and is NOT ready to retire. Not even close!

Like many of my clients, however, I came to a point where the career that I had worked so hard to build wasn’t working for me anymore. I realized that the reason I wasn’t happy in my career was that I wasn’t being true to my authentic self.

It was one of those “aha” moments! I realized that my true passion was using my expertise to help others accelerate their job searches and find the career to suit their changing lifestyles.

I now coach Boomers and near Boomers who are affected by unwanted job loss or seeking new careers that are more rewarding, both personally and professionally.

When “Laura” found herself suddenly unemployed with no safety net, she came to me. She had enjoyed a successful career in sales but her real passion was public relations. The solution? Together we created a personal brand and marketing strategy to get her through the door to meet people who could champion her in the industry!

“Jack” had a different challenge. He had been unemployed for over a year and was trying to get back into the job market in his chosen field. He was getting responses to his resume but wasn’t getting past the first interview. His solution? He had to persuade interviewers of his value to their organizations. How? By understanding the employers’ needs and concerns and tailoring his answers to address them proactively. The results? Employers recognized his unique abilities, and he’s now working in his field again.

What’s your challenge? I am committed to helping you create powerful, results oriented strategies to land the job you want or transition to a new career. Your success is my success! Together we can create a whole new future... just right for you.
