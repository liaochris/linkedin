Helen Rankin-Moriarty’s Summary

A passionate and versatile project manager/client director with particular experience in corporate communications and annual reports specifically. Experienced in service delivery from both agency and client-side, within the UK and Middle East.

Previous appointments have included channel development for Microsoft Middle East, Account Management for LINKdotNET, project management of marketing collateral for Lehman Brothers’ first UK structured product, and print/online annual report project management for a number of AIM and FTSE250 clients.

Trained at Cranfield University in Senior Management with Leadership Development and Strategy Development with Change Management. A member of the Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM) and currently working towards the IR Society Certificate in Investor Relations and BSc Business Studies with Accounting.

Helen Rankin-Moriarty’s Specialties:

Established skill-set including: corporate communications and annual reporting, PR and investor relations; copywriting of marketing collateral, presentations, press releases, operational procedures and financial product literature; event management; brand development and guardianship; change management; risk management; information audit and business process engineering.
