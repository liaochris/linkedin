Julie Woods’s Summary

BLI$$ness Centers Marketing is the internet marketing company I've founded with my Friend, Dana Neal. Our credo is, "Have Fun. Get Rich. Save the World." We feel that, if THAT makes sense to someone who's a client, then our goals will be in alignment!

I am the founder and chairperson of Fans With Cans, an entertainment-based charitable hunger relief organization. It currently operates in 4 states nationally and is being positioned to go international in the next few years.

Julie Woods In Concert is my music company. I am a singer/songwriter, who plays guitar, and a percussionist.

I am the principal artist for Stuffadoo, my visual arts company. As an "urban painter of found objects", the art world term for me, I paint everything from canvases to journals to stones, pretty much anything that isn't moving... and some things that are. ;-))

I was the principal consultant for The Woods Group. I'm still willing to do INTERESTING individual projects. I specialize in editing and writing, with a powerful background in technical writing/editing for hardware and software products.
