George Charalambides’s Summary

George has covered the basic materials sector for 12 years. He co-founded and was a Vice President at Pontis Capital Management LLP a basic materials long/short hedge fund.

He was a junior analyst at Bear Stearns’ equity research covering metals & mining. His team was ranked #2 on Wall Street by Institutional Investor magazine in 2005.

As a junior analyst at Morgan Stanley’s equity research, he was instrumental in moving the metals & mining team from the #4 to the #1 position in Institutional Investor rankings and to #1 in Global Reuters rankings in 1999. There he also covered the Hard-Line Retail and Building Materials industries.

Prior to joining Morgan Stanley, George was Director of Global Steel Research at Wharton Econometrics, an economic forecasting firm (now known as Global Insight.)

He has also worked as a consultant to private equity firms specializing in retail, and as a finance director at a new media start-up.

Mr. Charalambides graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a B.S. in Finance from the Wharton School and a B.A. in Economics from the College of Arts and Sciences in 1991.
