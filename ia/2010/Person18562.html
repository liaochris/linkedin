Kevin Wojtowicz’s Summary

Kevin is currently the Managing Director and Project Consultant for Australian - Canadian (AusCan) Project Solutions. Previous to this role, Kevin was both a Line Manager and a Senior Management Consultant with significant experience in managing Business and IT&T projects of different sizes and complexities. He brings a well-developed mix of business experience, technical skills, and creativity to the clients he works with.

Kevin is a professional, self-motivated individual with the ability to work at a high level of efficiency in stressful and deadline-oriented situations using effective communication, people and liaising skills. He possesses advanced organisation, co-ordination, interpersonal and documentation skills with meticulous attention to detail and quality.

Kevin has been working in IT&T for over 16 years.
Kevin has successfully managed projects in Australia, New Zealand, Canada and the USA.
