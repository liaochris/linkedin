Mark Lennox’s Summary

So who am I?

An entrepreneurial ‘ideas’ man with passion for building tangible communities within the UK Marketing, PR and Advertising sectors.

Extremely hard-working and target driven with complete focus on achieving personally set goals.

Founder of Only Marketing Jobs (http://onlymarketingjobs.com), the largest marketing job advertising site in the UK.

Founder of the UK Marketing Lounge (http://ukmarketinglounge.co.uk) the UK’s largest marketing group on LinkedIN with over 12,000 active members.

Founder of the UK Marketing Network (http://ukmarketingnetwork.co.uk), a lively hub of marketing professionals who share news, blogs, discussions, videos, photos, events and much more.


What do I do?

Create opportunities for marketing, PR and advertising professionals to find jobs, network and build strong and lasting relationships.

Help direct employers and recruitment companies to reach out and find relevant marketing, PR and advertising candidates in the most innovative ways possible.

Form partnerships with people and organisations to better enhance their proposition and ours.


How did I get here?

I started my career at 18 years old as a resourcer in a recruitment company (MSB International).

At 20, I co-founded Intelligent Solutions (IT recruitment) which developed into a £40 million turnover company. As Sales Director I managed 6 divisional managers and had overall responsibility for more than 100 employees.

At 25, I co-founded the Piranha Group, a niche sales and marketing recruitment company with 12 employees and a turnover of £2 million.

Mark Lennox’s Specialties:

Community building, Proposition development, Leadership, Training, extensive contacts within Marketing, Communications, Financial, IT and Telecoms industries
