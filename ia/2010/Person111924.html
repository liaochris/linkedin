Kathleen Cleary’s Summary

Are you looking to grab the attention of the wealthiest and fastest growing demographic in the nation? Do you want the Baby Boomers to nod their heads and say "Yes!" to your products and services?

I'm a Baby Boomer Direct Response Copywriter and I help businesses attract Boomers in a way that resonates with them and converts Boomers into buyers.

With an adventurous spirit and passion for health, fitness, travel, family life and living well and meaningfully - all traits shared by many Baby Boomers. I've chosen the Baby Boomer market as my copywriting niche.

While my 3 sons were graduating high school and entering college, I was a classic 'sandwiched Boomer' helping Mom care for my non-ambulatory Dad (a multiple stroke victim). I gained a deep understaning of the struggles and exhaustion many Boomers experience, learning first-hand why it is so important for businesses to offer services and products that will make life easier and more balanced for Baby Boomers.

Having raised teens and tots simultaneously and lived in an extended family household, I gained deep insight into the needs and emotions of many of today's Baby Boomers who are blending families with 2nd marriages.

In the spirit of community service, I wrote a successful grant which won $10,000 for the development of a Parenting Center for a local church.

As owner, manager and 'marketing department' of a restuarant in the Berkshires of MA, my marketing materials helped to develop a very successful and profitable small biz for my husband and myself.

As a retail store owner and 'marketing department' of The Lamplighter, a multi-channel marketing campaign I ran increased sales 22% over the same period the year before.

I am the author of the biography, "If This is Heaven, I am Going to be a Good Boy," the Tommy Leonard Story. This inspirational story reveals the untold childhood and celebrated life of one of Boston's legendary bartenders and Founder of the Falmouth Road Race.
