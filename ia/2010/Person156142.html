Nora Femenia, Ph.D.’s Summary

Bilingual(English - Spanish) Executive with innovative and broad experience in Conflict Resolution and Management, with a systemic mindset to provide sustainable system-wide solutions.

Focus both on cross-cultural approaches for global projects and team level dynamics. As a coach, strong ability to inspire and motivate managers to excel in their goals.

Consultant for small and large organizations with high level of internal conflict, designing systems to appraise, process and prevent conflict episodes.

Consultant with wide experience in project design and evaluation for the World Bank, and the IADB, in Latin American areas: governement, education, justice system and mediation, privatization projects.

Experienced on online dispute resolution, processing clients' claims and setting systems to manage claims processing with and without human intervention.
