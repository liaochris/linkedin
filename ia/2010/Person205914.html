Tom Crosthwaite’s Summary

I operate Tom Crosthwaite & Associates (TC&A), a business operations and management firm, specializing in television, the internet and the music industry. We work with clients in content driven industries across media platforms, in both the entrepreneurial and operational aspects of their businesses.

As an expert in verifying the actual, out-of-pocket cost incurred during production, I have saved my clients tens of millions of dollars while helping them reach equitable settlements with networks, studios and production partners on hundreds of series, pilots and specials.

TC&A’s client list includes: The CW Network, Fox Broadcasting, Hasbro Toys, Paramount Network Television, QDE, Showtime and The WB Network; recent projects include America’s Next Top Model, Melrose Place and Reaper. Prior to setting up my own company, I was with the ABC Television Network.

My latest project is Blue Comet TV, a video-based music blog and social networking site that introduces the finest emerging recording artists to the world. I created the concept and head a team of talented and internet-savvy music lovers, overseeing all aspects of the site’s operation, including video production, site design/programming, and marketing.

I started my career in the music business by reinvigorating Intermedia Recording Studios in Boston, an effort that required developing a first rate staff, implementing stringent technical and customer service standards, and the creation of an aggressive marketing and PR campaign. As a result of these efforts, I was able to build a clientele that included Aerosmith, The Cars, The J. Geils Band and Maurice Starr. My success in Boston brought me to Los Angeles and Magnolia Sound/The Enactron Truck, whose clients included Emmylou Harris, Kris Kristofferson, Johnny Cash, Quincy Jones and Diana Ross.
