Michael Stoughton’s Summary

Culture Index is dedicated to asses a company's work culture. Perhaps more simply stated, department and/or companies establish their own culture by the people within them. Whether it's a dysfunctional department or stream-lined company, the sum of the parts are the people that make up the given entity.

What we provide with the Culture Index is an assessment that easily and quickly will give you accurate results from which we can explain why your department/company is successful, or we can determine not what the problem is, but who the concerns are and recommend ways to influence or achieve change.

We can set up your company with a FREE trail where you can have team members take the assessment at no cost or obligation.
