David Landan’s Summary

I consider myself an adept problem-solver. Experience has taught me to go beyond the idea of "think out of the box"--There is no box. I enjoy looking for questions that haven't been asked as well as challenging the questions that have been asked.

Having fulfilled my life-long dream to work in the wine industry, I have now come back to my two favorite disciplines: linguistics and computer science. I spend my time these days looking for hybrid solutions (combining statistical and rule-based approaches) to NLP problems.
