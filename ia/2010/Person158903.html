Pam Hart’s Summary

I am a tech industry search consultant with a proven track record in Executive Search and designing innovative and relevant recruitment and talent strategies. I excel at partnering with executives to build-out management teams from early-stage through market leadership. The results are lasting client relationships built on trust.

Search Services include:
•Branding-exceptional ability to position client’s brand to attract top talent.
•Search Strategy-experience integrating social media with strategy
•Partnering with client’s leadership & hiring teams-training hiring managers and interview teams to assess competency, motivation, and fit.
•“Best Practices” Search Methodology- optimization of technology and tools to source top performing active and passive candidates. Research is extensive, thorough, and current for each search..
•Search Process Management- position profile, search strategy, research, candidate generation & management, interview team training-candidate assessment/interviews, reference checks, offer recommendation/negotiation and candidate on-boarding for long term success.
•Executive Search & Recruiting Initiatives:
*Strategic Executive Searches
*Performance Based Hiring Strategy-Recruiting Roadmaps
*Mentoring Recruiters and Technical Sourcers
*Building a Recruiting Culture-Employee Referral Training
*Candidate Management-Tracking/Pipelining

In addition to recruiting/consulting..........I have a passion for the performing arts and have assisted Non-Profit organizations, Universities, and Performers/Composers (Tony nominated and "up and coming talent") in fund raising and understanding "The Business of the Arts".
