Kimberly Brown’s Summary

I have nearly 30 years of journalism experience from entry-level staff writer at The Blood-Horse magazine to Publisher/Editor of The Horse: Your Guide To Equine Health Care. I have been a leader transitioning editorial and advertising from print to online and from strictly print to multi-media publishing.

I'm known for my work ethic, passion, creativity, problem-solving, and ability to hire talented young writers and mentor them to become better. I have a skill and drive for taking good projects and making them excellent, and creatively having a "Plan B" so projects don't stall or fail.

As the Publisher/Editor of The Horse brand I have expanded my focus from strictly editorial to include advertising, PR, and the total management of the brand.

I'm a published songwriter (albeit small-time) with my professional musician husband, Ben.

Over the next couple of years I'll be looking for other areas to expand my passions of horses, animals, outdoors, and writing. I love to share my passions with others and help them develop their dreams and become better at what they want to do.

Kimberly Brown’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, consulting, public speaking, mentoring, coaching, assisting with project ideas and creative thinking on projects, developing options, transition journalism from print to online.
