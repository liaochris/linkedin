Blake Burris’s Summary

At Dynamo Labs, we build social applications that allow friends to connect around the important activities in their lives for tracking and sharing for fun - and accountability.

Our "Challenge" app was featured at Facebook's developer conference, f8. We are first-round grant recipient of fbFund (operated by Accel Partners, Founders Fund, and Facebook): http://developer.facebook.com/fbFund.php?tab=recipients#

As an entrepreneur, I'm interested in creating a stronger ecosystem where startups thrive. To that end, I operate CoHabitat, startup community with physical workspace in Dallas and Shreveport, La.

CoHabitat Foundation is our non-profit arm which seeks to empower entrepreneurs to create the next break-out companies that will create value and jobs.
