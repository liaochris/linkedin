Dolores Brown’s Summary

After several years at a variety of positions in the Arts, Arts-in-Ed and Administration, I decided to pull from the wealth of skills I have in many areas and use it to create something I really care about.

A few years ago I found myself thinking that if I met one more little girl in the at-risk communities I deal with who's dream was to "become a video model" or to marry a rapper or baller, I did not know what I would do. Well, I decided that being the change I wanted to see would be most proactive option, and set to work brainstorming alone and with my many gifted amigas until LG was born. These girls need to know there is more out there for them than hip hop culture at large may make them think.

I will work with my team to build Lip Gloss until it is a place that every one of my lil sisters knows they can come to feel good about being a girl of color, a girl of extreme poverty or a girl who has made early mistakes. There is always a way out! They will be respected and uplifted and given examples of the heights they can reach with every issue... but we will not neglect the fact that they are teens. Fun, style and entertainment will be a large element as we build our online presence.
