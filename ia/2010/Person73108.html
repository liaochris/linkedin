Gary Giallonardo’s Summary

Professional business developer with extensive experience researching and analyzing, developing customized strategies, accessing vast implementation resources, and creating infrastructures to generate revenues for clients in numerous B2B customers and markets.

Not the run-of-the-mill marketer, as extremely analytical (detailed and process-oriented identifying holes, hot spots and patterns) with an undergraduate engineering degree, and a MBA in marketing and finance.

Thanks to three FORTUNE 500 companies, have fifteen years perfecting technical sales, sales management, product management, marketing management and strategic planning techniques.

Lived on assignment in Europe, and traveled extensively throughout the world with an appreciation for diverse cultures and ability to manage international business development processes.

Gary Giallonardo’s Specialties:

Being the trusted marketing and sales function advisor to small-to-medium sized, closely-held, and family-owned and/or operated firms. Also being “Mr. Fix It” . . . the person who CPAs, lawyers and bankers summon to repair their clients' top lines. With very little low hanging fruit, I specialize in finding the needles of opportunities in the haystacks of prospective customers and competitive alternatives.
