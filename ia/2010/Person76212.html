Jan van der Meer’s Summary

According to Elsevier (Like Time magazine) Jan van der Meer is a real video Guru and award-winning Video Producer, Director of Photography and Web Entrepreneur. Coaching prosumers and professionals in creating better video-productions. Platform for tips and information about latest techniques, smaller camera's, dslr-photo/videocamera's and all kind of gadgets. (Mini 'Obama' tele-prompter for iPhones!)

Jan is Founder Global-DVC First website in 1996 for Videographers (Global Digital Videographers Centre).
Prosumers and Professionals. Based in Zandvoort (Amsterdam Beach) Holland.

Video Director Producer, Camera Consultant, trainer and editor video-bulletins, magazins and newspapers.
Several recommendations on this page proof that DVC members appreciate this information and tips.

Jan trained over 10.000 prosumers and professionals in the practical and critical use of video camera’s and latest photo camera’s with access to HD video.Training centre for TV camera journalists, staff members of big camera compagnies and many professional freelance video producers.

Together with his co-producer wife Lidy, they also organize yearly worldwide trips for videographers to scenic locations.
Favourite destination are wildlife parks in South Africa, Namibia, Tanzania, Bali, Kenia and Borneo.

Productions: Educational videomagazines, Inflight Videomagazine all flights GARUDA INDONESIA. Photographer American Express Travel Brochures Producer Webclips for five star Hotels South Africa, Namibia, Swaziland and Bali. Cameraman for TV series: Fly Away.

Also producing Pond5.com HD stock shots. Growing expertise in Webclip Presentations and new Internet Marketing and Social Media like Twitter and Facebook.

Global-DVC is the ideal centre for the "one (wo)man does it all productions" and Website owners interested in producing their own presentations.

Jan van der Meer’s Specialties:

HD Video Productions, camcorder camera courses, education, schooling, accessories, gadgets, webclip promotion, photo/video safaris, South-Africa Photo- Video-safaries, Wildlife stockshots producer and teacher, Borneo Proboscis Monkey ambassador, Supporting Masai kids at Technical School Father Joseph Nairobi Kenia.
