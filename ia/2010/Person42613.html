Colleen Rice Nelson’s Summary

Currently working with MGM in the music department conducting legal research and clearance. I refer to my skills as Multimedia, because at one point or another everything I have ever learned in production and entertainment have come together. Under this multimedia umbrella, I love to do legal research and clearance on stills, footage and music, web consulting and development, and digital photography. Then there is also my secret plans to take over the world with my internet consulting, web development, social media strategist plans.

In my "spare" time I am a freelance advocate. As such, I am the organizer for the Los Angeles Freelance Meet Up Group (http://www.meetup.com/LA-Freelance), I maintain a resource blog for freelancers and the self employed people (http://www.freelancerforum.net) and I am a Featured host of Freelancer Forum on BlogTalkRadio.com (http://www.blogtalkradio.com/Freelancer-Forum).

I really do enjoy being a Multimedia Muse by helping clients find the right answers for their projects.
