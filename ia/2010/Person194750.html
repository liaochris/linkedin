Steve Adkins’s Summary

I started Paulding Computers in the spring of 1998 to provide service, sales and support for computer related problems to consumers and small businesses in the Northwest Atlanta Metro area.

In 2004 I decided to change careers again, with an interest in real estate. Taking me to Metro Brokers because of their excellent training and Support programs. Covering Paulding, West Cobb, Douglas and North Carroll Counties, I'm working with families to help them find the home of their dreams. As well as working with commercial and residential developers. I have also moved into an office leadership position helping new agents to get started faster in their new Real Estate Career.

Since January 1st, 2009 I have been primarily working with banks, listing and selling their REO properties. This has kept me very busy! Now that we have received some experience, I'm looking to expand this side of our business.
