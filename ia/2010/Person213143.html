Lee Belardo’s Summary

Although unsuccessful in achieving the goal to be elected the next OSBA president, the campaign provided me with new opportunities and associations. I thank all those who supported my effort in this endeavor. As my friend Carmen often said during the campaign, "the OSBA is sailing on the right course" and I agree. Congratulations Carmen and best wishes. I am confident that the OSBA will weather any storm with you at the helm and I wish you and our association smooth sailing during your term as president of the OSBA.
