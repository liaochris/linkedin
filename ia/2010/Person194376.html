Stephen Quinn’s Summary

Experienced in all aspects of major corporate marketing efforts including customer research, marketing and business strategy, brand management and marketing communications. Expereince with Fortune 50 corporations including Walmart. Was Chief Marketing Officer of Frito-Lay North America, a $10 billion division of PepsiCo. During 13 years at PepsiCo, served in a number of marketing roles including innovation, brand management, advertising, market research and customer marketing. Was the General Manager of Convenience Foods and separately the head of Marketing for Frito-Lay Canada. Worked in marketing, sales and finance roles for Quaker Oats, Johnson & Johnson, Procter & Gamble and Nortel. Managed several leading brands including Children’s Tylenol, Tylenol Cold Medication, Johnson’s Baby Products, Quaker Oats, Rice-A-Roni, Quaker Chewy, Aunt Jemima, Lay’s, Tostitos, Doritos, Great Value and Walmart.
