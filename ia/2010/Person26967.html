Brandi Anderson’s Summary

Experienced in the areas of event planning and promotion, booking, publicity, and marketing, I am a senior music industry studies major at Eastern Kentucky University. While I do hope to find employment within the industry, I continuously strive for self growth and lifelong education; I'm currently looking at several different graduate schools which offer master's degrees in performing arts management and/or arts administration.

Last year, I served as President of the Music Industry Organization at Eastern Kentucky University, the promotional arm of the music industry studies and music merchandising programs, which gives students hands on experience in various areas of the music business. My role as spokesperson within the organization gave me the chance to sharpen my verbal and written skills through press relations, working with businesses, sponsors, and partners in the city of Richmond, and speaking to college students about the music marketing program. I have also attended and volunteered at trade shows and conferences such as the 2008 NAMM Convention and Kentucky Performing Arts Exchange, and have experience in the industry through internships with Interscope-Geffen Records, Brumfield and Associates Booking Agency, and Idol Roc Entertainment. I posses excellent people skills and have worked with non-profit agencies such as the United Way, volunteering as a music therapy assistant for Eastern State Hospital. Other experience within the industry includes being showcased as a feature writer for the Groove Magazine in Richmond, Ky, and working the merchandise booth at EKU's Crocs Campus Tour featuring OneRepublic.
