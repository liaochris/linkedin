Luiz SALES’s Summary

I am graduated in Electronic Engineering, post graduated in Finances and specialization in Marketing. I have more than 15-years experience in the telecommunication market, being most of them in multinational companies.

I have proficiency in English, certified by the University of Cambridge, fluency in Spanish and full computing skills. Furthermore, I have participated in the Six Sigma Program training for Black Belt certifications.

I moved from Motorola to SanDisk, where I'm responsible for the sales strategy for Mobile Retail products in Latin America. Prior to that, I was in charge of the sales of Motorola mobile accessories in Brazil. From 02 to 06, I worked at Service Center division, in charge of Motorola Authorized Service Centers and Support Center operations, managing a team of 13 high-qualified employees. Among the main responsibilities were the management of a service network with more than 140 service centers that supports in-warranty repairs as well as the definition of after-sales strategies. The most significant achievement was the best level of customer satisfaction ever in Brazil.

For 3 years prior to Motorola, I held the position of Director of Operations at Seal Eletrônica, responsible for the implementation and development of the telecommunication division in this company as exclusive distributor for Polycom products in Brazil. The most challenging result in this position was a $1M deal in the first year, for the largest videoconferencing project in Brazil.

My professional experience began at Siemens in Brazil as trainee in 1992. During seven years, I had gather experiences in the Service department and afterwards in Product Development department, where I was the responsible for the management of handset terminals, such as Siemens corded phones and Motorola cellular phones. By that time, I was prized twice by Motorola for the remarkable achievements as best Motorola Authorized Distributor in Brazil in 1997 and 1998.
