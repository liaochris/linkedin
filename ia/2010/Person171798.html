Rhyno Stinchfield’s Summary

Rhyno has 30 years experience in the Renewable/Sustainable Energy fields. He has worked with companies in all 50 United States, Canada, the Caribbean, Mexico and abroad.

Besides overseeing GreenWorld Partners, Rhyno is the CEO of Montana Wind Resources, LLC a utility scale wind development company that offers the “Community Wind” model for projects in the Northern Rockies. One of MWR’s projects in development is a 500+ Megawatt windfarm in central Montana that will provide electricity to the West Coast states so they can fulfill their required Renewable Portfolio Standards.

Rhyno is a member of the American Wind Energy Association, American Solar Energy Society and the United States Green Building Council. He has sat on the boards of the Concrete Homebuilding Council of the National Association of Homebuilders and the Insulating Concrete Form Association as well as being a member of the United States Green Building Council and has developed Continuing Education Units programs for the American Institute of Architects.

For over 25 years, Rhyno has orchestrated trade show exhibits on a regional and national basis. He has managed teams that presented first-class displays in conferences with over 100,000 attendees.

Rhyno has been quoted as an expert in magazines/newsletters such as Forbes, North American Windpower, Recharge News, Smart Money and numerous others. Rhyno has also been asked to participate on industry discussion panels and has enjoyed speaking at trade conferences.

Rhyno is an avid skier, hiker and fisherman. He enjoys the company of genuine, inspired people and believes that we are living in some of the greatest times of human history.
