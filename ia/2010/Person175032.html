Robert Murdock’s Summary

For the past decade, my experiences have allowed me to design, direct and lead projects ranging from interface design for products, brand identity and communications design to interactive design for web and evolving mediums. I have experience in building and managing multi-disciplinary creative teams and overseeing all stages of creative development and implementation. I believe in a collaborative approach, creating work that engages and inspires, but also addresses business objectives and moves clients toward their goals. I believe that exceptional design has the ability to meet these objectives, transcend the ephemeral, and make a meaningful connection with its audience. My approach to design blends a passion for developing strong ideas with a rigorous, refined sense of form. I strive to create solutions that are highly creative and simply usable – the products of in depth strategy and meticulous visual engineering. The specifics of a project – its content and its context – always drive the solution.
