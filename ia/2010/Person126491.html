Liz Carter’s Summary

It's about empowerment not entitlement. It's about prosperity for all people not special interest. It's about accountability at all levels.

Prior to entering the 2010 Congressional race and focusing her time to serving those in her district... Liz is a successful business owner and business executive that delivers thought leadership to all she works with. She knows how to get the job done.

Before entering the consulting and search industry, Liz held executive-level marketing and business management positions at high technology and financial companies. She began her career in land development and transitioned into the world of technology at Novell, then continued her leadership to State Street Corporation, Sitara Networks and CheckFree Corporation. Her experience spans across B2B and B2C marketing.

Her many accomplishments include successful management of a $65 million business unit, increasing division revenues by 32 percent and helping define a new 3270 HTML technology patent to differentiate a product in the market. In addition she has earned a Harvey Communication Award and enabled Sitara Networks to earn multiple industry and communications awards, including building 38 percent brand recognition within nine months as the Marketing Executive. At CheckFree she was recognized as a leader who developed comprehensive go to market strategies to drive sales success and as an individual who understands the full sales cycle.

Executive of the Year Representing Consulting & Executive Recruitment by Cambridge Who's Who for the 2009-2010 Edition of Executives, Professionals and Entrepreneurs.

Her mission is to provide Thought Leadership through counsel and individuals who guarantee success and value.
