Robert Hawkins’s Summary

I have more than 25 years experience in all aspects of Project Controls; Cost, Planning and Scheduling, Contract Administration, Risk Management, and Estimating, 18 years in a managerial or supervisory role. Project budgets ranging from $50MM to $2 Billion. I have performed strategic long range manpower planning, budget forecasting, P & L, and change management utilizing GAAP. Considered an expert in P3/P6. Maintained Sarbanes Oxley compliance on projects assigned. Utilizing both PC and Mainframe software, (i.e., Primavera P3 and P3e/c, P2, PERTmaster, @Risk, SAP, Timberline, Microsoft Excel, Word, Project, Access, JD Edwards, Kildrummy Cost Manager, EPCWorks, Prolog, and TRAC cost software)
