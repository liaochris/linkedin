Kendell Lang’s Summary

Exploring my Personal Brand Continuum, which is about my personal brand, my executive brand, my brand statement, and my value proposition. All important things when trying to articulate how I can be an impact player in my circle of influence.

Issue Statement:

There are four potential reasons why your organization may have a need for a Virtual Chief Information Officer (vCIO) to improve the coordination of technical resources for information technology (IT):

· Your organization may not have a consolidated management structure for information resource management which makes coordination of resources between departments, groups, teams, vertical markets or subcontractors difficult;

· The links between strategic IT planning, budgeting, operations, sales, marketing and customer support may need to be improved;

· The distributed nature of your organizational structure contributes to uncoordinated growth of IT infrastructure and unnecessary duplication of resources;

· No single person has responsibility for managing company-wide IT planning and operations

Discussion:

The fast-paced development of new technologies and the rush to implement any kind of dot com strategy has pushed many organizations to bypass tried and true processes and methodologies. At a time when Internet start-ups are both growing and tanking so fast, traditional management methods such as Total Life Cycle Software Development have been virtually abandoned. Improved coordination and increased integration of technology resources on a company-wide level are needed to provide better communication, economies of scale, sharing of resources, cost savings, and services that improve overall corporate quality.

A General Overall Architecture (GOA) planning document is a framework for achieving this.

Conclusion:

Information technology integration is essential in meeting current and future business needs. For many companies, a Virtual Chief Information Officer would clearly facilitate development and implementation of meaningful standards for technology architecture and operations. The engagement of my services as vCIO would help establish an integrated and consolidated approach to the acquisition and development of technology.
