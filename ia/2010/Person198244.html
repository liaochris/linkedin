Suzanne Dalton, PMP’s Summary

Suzanne has more than 15 years of IT experience as a Web Producer and Designer working on major marketing projects and websites. She has the ability to lead, inspire and manage edgy, interactive web teams and align stakeholders developing Internet marketing solutions using state-of-the art digital media. She has a strong background working through the entire SDLC and has developed proposals, managed work streams and budgets, built relationships and created value for her company and clients. A creative problem solver with strong verbal communication skills Suzanne works extensively with stakeholders and clients to develop key marketing strategies and technical documents. Suzanne has a strong reputation for delivering successful web projects that are on time, meet specific customer requirements and showcases her strong visual aesthetic sense.
