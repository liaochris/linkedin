Tracy Valerio’s Summary

Since February 2005, I have owned my own cosmetics business and provide individual and group skincare consultations and color cosmetics sessions to women, men and teens seeking to improve their skincare routines and to look younger and more vibrant for their professional and personal lives.

My clientele ranges from entertainment / broadcast professionals to executives of large companies to small business owners to hard working mothers and grandmothers. Even with this economy, everyone needs to clean their face!

I also provide personalized business training, product marketing, mentoring and motivation to individuals seeking to own their own business in the cosmetics industry.

My goal is to improve the quality of my client’s lives by assisting them in feeling as terrific on the inside as they look on the outside.
