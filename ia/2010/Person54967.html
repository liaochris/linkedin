Derek Garza’s Summary

As an actor, I have worked at Montana Shakespeare Company, Southern Colorado Repertory Theatre for two seasons, and some minor acting at The Santa Fe Opera. I have acted and worked at the Adams State College Theatre Scene Shop for four years and was Shop Foreman for one year. I was the Shop Foreman and Actor for the 2005 Southern Colorado Repertory Theatre season. I have recently graduated from the MFA Acting program at Penn State University. I have worked with Jim Sullivan, Artistic Director of Utah Shakespeare, on "The Rivals", David Rottenburg, TV and Film Director and Teacher, on an staged reading of "Propaganda" (Original play), and John Basil, Artistic Director and Co-Founder of The American Globe Theatre, on "Twelfth Night".

Derek Garza’s Specialties:

Minor Guitar playing, Stage combat (rapier & dagger, quarterstaff, unarmed) and Choreography, Military Training (Honor Gaurd, Drill Team), Tumbling, Juggling, Martial Arts, Dialects (Australian, Standard British, Cockney, Irish, Southern), Vocal range: High Baritone.
