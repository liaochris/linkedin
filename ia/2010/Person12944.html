Angela Coulter’s Summary

I was successful working in Corporate America in marketing/marketing management for 18+ years, but decided I wanted to try to run my OWN business. After much deliberation and planning, I quit my "day job" in April 2006 to give it a shot, and jumped full time into a completely different business, Herban Meadows.

However, things circled back around, and before I knew it, I was helping other business owners with their marketing efforts. That spawned my second business, ABC Marketing, which eventually turned into AboutFace Marketing. In 2009, I had so much business that I decided to devote myself full-time to AboutFace Marketing, and it's been a whirlwind ever since!

Though I've worked with clients across a wide variety of industries, it seems I've been able to infuse my passion for solid, strategic marketing with my other passions in the environment, the planet, organics, and health through gaining a number of clients in these areas. I also help others employ "green marketing" practices, whether or not their focus is on the environment or health.

I love every day, the diversity of clients, and every moment of work that it brings me!
