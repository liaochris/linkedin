Cole Harmonson’s Summary

Far West Capital helps companies that are $15MM in revenue and less with various Working Capital solutions.

We provide: Asset Based Lending, Working Capital Lines of Credit, A/R Financing, Inventory Financing, Purchase Order Financing. Credit Facilities available up to $5MM.

Most of our business comes by way of referral from financial professionals who have direct contact with these type of clients, i.e., bankers, attorneys, CPAs, turnaround professionals, other commercial lending professionals.

The Far West difference is our attention to client success and the ability to completely customize the relationships we construct. We also provide a depth of products that covers the spectrum of need versus a "cookie cutter" box offered by most.

If you know of someone that could directly benefit from our services, your recommendation would be appreciated.

Additionally, we would appreciate your introduction to anyone you know that might come into contact with clients, my goal is to make you look good by providing a solution in a professional way.
