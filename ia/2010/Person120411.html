Kyle Sexton’s Summary

Since 2001, I have been responsible for sales, service and retention, as well as marketing and business development. In my time with the Chamber, I have redesigned the Chamber’s website, successfully transitioned our publications to in-house productions, re-built the dues structure to value-based tiers, and created on online job bank as part of the Chamber's online marketplace.

I am the founder of FastChamber.com, developing franchise-quality systems for chambers of commerce. My research and methods have been featured in industry publications, as well as national and regional conventions. My innovative online membership sales module, Chamber SalesEngine™, has grown to account for as much as 40% of annual new member sales in Salem. Additionally, I am the co-founder of ChamberThink Strategies, a consultancy focused on coaching chambers of commerce through their transition from a traditional dues method to a progressive tiered-investment model.

I am a highly rated speaker for the Western Association of Chamber Executives (WACE) Academy and the American Chamber of Commerce Executives conventions. I serve on the ACCE Board of Directors, and the Membership Development Advisory Board as Chair-Elect. A Lifetime Achievement Award recipient from the ACCE's Circle of Champions with over 1000 memberships sold, my roles have also included Vice Chair of Sales and Recognition Programs and Circle of Champions (Sales Division) chair.

I have been marketing and selling memberships since 1996. Prior to working for the Chamber, I was the Director of Marketing & Corporate Services for a chain of Gold’s Gym locations in Western Oregon. My work was honored with The 2001 Gold's Gym International Award for Outstanding Advertising from among 600 franchises worldwide.
