Sara Jean Goodman’s Summary

At age fifteen I realized one of my greatest passions is marketing and how it works, not just in business, but everywhere, everyday. Quoting Regis McKenna, “Marketing is everything, and everything is marketing.”

In my current position as Assistant Marketing Manager of Advantage Futures, and one of three members to originate the marketing department, my focus is to brand and position the firm, grow awareness, and increase profits through client growth. I handle website development and updates, social media marketing, graphic design, project management, event marketing, search engine optimization, prospect development, lead generation, CRM, sales material creation, and sales assistance. Consistently I prepare market analysis and internal measurement and analytics to review and adapt our marketing plans.

As a marketing professional I continuously educate myself to stay ahead of our ever-changing industry. Participation on committees through the American Marketing Association and others organizations, allows me to engage in areas outside my current position. This also provides the opportunity to learn from those interacting in these networks. I am also personally active in social media to gain knowledge and valuable insight from distinguished marketing professionals, and to share my experience.
