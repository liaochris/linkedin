Pavel Dolezal’s Summary

1997-98 helped to prepare and run a huge cultural event in Brussels under the name Europalia. It took place in the whole Belgium for more than 4 months with several hundred artists, dozens of exhibitions. And the best - all of it was the Czech culture :)

Since 98-2002 I have been working as a Chief Product Manager for one of three leading Czech portals, Atlas.cz
I had led a group of product managers, created business partnerships, architected new products and business ways.

I was responsible for the product groups in Czech, Slovak and Ukrain.

In 2002 I have created a mobile marketing company.

Since 2003 the All Stars venture is in life. Building on our previous experiences and expertise both product wise as well as business wise.

In Czech we operate in Prague, Brno and Ostrava.

In Poland in Katowice and partially in Warsaw.

Slovak market is covered from Žilina and Bratislava.

In addition to that we operate in 18 languages.

We are trying to change the commong views and bring high quality SEM and SEO service to small companies as well as large ones.

September 2006 we rebranded to Ataxo.com and became the leading Search Engine Marketing and Performance Marketing company within the CEE region.
Currently we serve more than 10 000 customers - managing their PPC campaigns at Google, Seznam, Yandex, Yahoo, etc.

Ataxo is growing business with a plan to cover additional markets.

Pavel Dolezal’s Specialties:

Our speciality is SEO and SEM in the European markets. We specialize in East European countries, currently operating in Czech, Poland and Slovakia.
We develop a whole new approach towards the industry which allows us to serve not just big companies, but SME as well. In fact, they create over 60% customers.
