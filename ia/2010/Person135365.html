Martin Bernet’s Summary

Responsible for all system engineering and integration activities for GCSS. Responsibilities include: the overall hardware and software architecture and that the architecture meets the scalability, redundancy, reliability, maintainability, security, and performance requirements. As chief architect work with software, data, and security engineering teams that a consistent system that meet the short and long term objectives of a net-centric architecture based on services. Work closely with requirements and testing team to ensure proper acceptance criteria is developed and adequate testing is used to ensure the system will meet the end-users needs.
