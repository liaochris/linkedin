Greg Iszler’s Summary

An insatiable curiosity about consumer behavior, marketplace trends, and branding innovation helped spotlight, for me, the potential of digital back in 1994. Since then, the evolution in media, marketing, branding, and technology has delivered a never-ending supply of unique challenges and professional development.

I’ve had the good fortune to work with some of the largest companies in the world (including DuPont, GE, Procter & Gamble, and The Coca-Cola Company), and some especially inspiring and ambitious brands (such as Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines, Mazda, Moen, Sherwin-Williams, John Frieda, and Bioré). My agency work has exposed me to a wide variety of categories and a surprisingly rich pool of cross-category learning.

In the last few years, my focus has shifted somewhat to helping agencies integrate strategic planning and digital efforts within their organizations: from corporate positioning and strategic modeling, to departmental collaboration and training, to the development of proprietary processes and tools.
