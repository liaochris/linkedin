Chris Hooper’s Summary

I have Bachelor of Commerce, majoring in Accounting and minoring in Applied Finance from The University of South Australia. Prior to that I was awarded a Diploma of Business from SAIBT.

I am now working at getting a Diploma of Financial Services to cater to my specialty. Following that I intend to complete the Chartered Accountants post graduate diploma. I also plan on taking the Company Directors program with the Australian Institute of Company Directors, I am looking to get Fellowship there in the next three years.

I currently work at Brentnalls SA as an Superannuation Specialist. I moved into the specialty after about two years in business services. I still maintain a couple business service clients to keep my skills sharp.

I am a director of Hooper Govan Capital. The company was established primary as a research project exploring quantitative financial algorithms. The project has been marked as a success with two successful proprietary systems developed.

I am one of the founders of The Start Up Club; it is a support network and think tank for young entrepreneurs, practicing professionals and academics. The club meets once a fortnight in a casual environment to network and learn from our resident experts who run workshops.

I dedicate a significant amount of time to the not for profit sector as well. Most notable is my treasury position with The SAUCNA Group; a community oriented sports group. Since stepping into to the treasury portfolio in 2007 I have been able to implement several policies to boost return on investment and efficiency.

I don't live to work, nor do I work to live. I work to create, to innovate, to inspire.
