Michelle Tripp’s Summary

Michelle Tripp is a Creative Director, Writer, Brand Consultant, and Marketing Strategist based in New York.

Michelle’s experience includes 14 years in brand strategy and creative management, including consulting clients in banking and finance, hospitals and healthcare, higher education, wireless, pharma, energy, technology, luxury brands, sports, and consumer services. During her career, Michelle has developed dozens of advertising campaigns, including hundreds of print projects and more than 30 award-winning TV commercials. Creative and marketing awards include 16 Tellys, an IECF Astrolabium (Japan), The Gold CASE Award, New York Globals, and ADDYs, including several Best of Shows.

As a social media strategist, Michelle is an avid new media researcher, creative technologist, and early adopter. Her digital experience includes developing and designing websites, portals, site strategy, content, and social media programs.

Michelle writes about the intersection of branding, social media, and emerging technologies on The BrandForward Blog.
