Rita Orland, ASID, IIDA’s Summary

Wings, Inc. Design

Rita Orland, President, is an Incentive Program Designer and Consultant to companies in the Incentive Travel Industry. Wings specializes in incentive travel programs for the architecture and design community, which includes manufacturers and vendors in the design / build and decorating industries.

Experienced Travel Executive - plans, organizes and operates "ArchiTours" for the Architecture + Design community. A + D Tours operate around the world. This niche market includes Design Enthusiasts and design-related study abroad programs.

Travel Industry Experience: Pan American World Airways, Inc.; Travel Agent; and, Independent Contractor for Incentive Travel Programs.

Design Experience: Hospitality, Commercial; and, Residential. Professional Interior Designer, NCIDQ # 023364 - National Council for Interior Design Qualification.

Focus - Design Concept, Space Planning and Project Management; Published in: "Living in Style" magazine, www.lismag.com, San Diego Home Garden/Lifestyles magazine , Window Fashion Vision, Decor & Style, and others; Member of several professional associations.
