Shane Hankins’s Summary

Shane provides executive leadership to the organization, and is extremely excited to build the capacity of non-profits around the country to better serve their communities and constituents. He brings a rich experience in non-profit management and fundraising to Grassroots.org. He most recently served as Executive Director of Road of Life: Cancer Prevention for Kids, a Grassroots.org member that provides health education programming for children. He also was a member of the founding management team for the Economic and Community Development Institute, an organization dedicated to building wealth and employment opportunities for refugees, immigrants, and low-income individuals. In his career he has managed dozens of staff and programs, and has raised over $17 million. Shane grew up in Centerburg, Ohio (population 1,000) and graduated Phi Beta Kappa from the Ohio State University, with a degree in History and Political Science. He also went to grad school for City and Regional Planning at Ohio State.

Shane loves living in NYC. He also loves art, literature, music, architecture, white water rafting, canoeing, hiking, family, friends, and coffee. He wants to learn how to sail, but will settle riding the Staten Island Ferry (which is free!) for now.
