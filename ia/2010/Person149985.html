Mark Thurgood’s Summary

Proactive, high energy individual with a proven track record in two careers to date. I have worked in a business analysis role for the last three years and prior to that I worked in adult education and training for over ten years.

While working in adult education, my ability to be organised, motivated and to communicate with people at all levels was vital. As a business analyst, these skills are also a requirement, especially as the audience for my work varies considerably between IT and business oriented clients.
