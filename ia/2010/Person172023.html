Richard Barker’s Summary

My goal is to move towards the electrical energy generation industry, particularly applying my diverse skills and experience towards implementation of the Smart Grid and alternative sources including windpower, solar, and geothermal techniques. I wish to participate in the research and development of these systems looking towards their future while modernizing the present systems.
In pursuit of this goal I have recently attended IEEE Maine Chapter Smart Grid Tutorial at USM, IEEE Boston Section Demystifying The Smart Grid at MIT, IEEE PES Innovative Smart Grid Technologies Conference at NIST, Gaithersburg, MD, and IEEE Maine Chapter Smart Grid Metering Tutorial at CMP.
Throughout my career it has been my passion to continue to learn new concepts and pass them along to others. My proven track record has expanded into various fields that include areas in: computers, internet protocols, packet transmission, microwave transmission, radio / TV broadcast engineering, submarine detection, locomotive traction systems, railroad signaling crossing protection, telecommunications test methods, radio astronomy, satellites, antennas, electronically controlled pyrotechnics, microcontrollers, and veterinary instrumentation. A creative thinker, I have been instrumental in designing, using my analytical skills on projects for product improvement.
