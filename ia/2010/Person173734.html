Jason Rivers’s Summary

Day Job: Jason Rivers is currently managing and selling REO Properties in Central California, national and international financial institutions. He has an excellent understanding of the paperwork and speed needed to expedite the disposition of investor held assets in today’s real estate market.

His team performs thorough property inspections of their assets every week. The inspections are a must in our fast changing region of California. With this he is able to accurately advise asset managers on the assets "mark to market" value and physical condition of their client’s holding.

With over 40 year’s experience, Rivers Realty has been hailed as the preeminent foreclosures experts in our community. And now his multilingual staff is equipped with the latest and greatest in REO software like the new EBrokerHouse. This web based software allows buyers agents to upload offers and terms directly to the website. Upon request, they can even add the Asset Manager to receive a copy of all offers submitted through this system.
