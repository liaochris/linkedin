Susan Adams’s Summary

In three decades as a journalist, I’ve done everything from writing cover stories about billionaires to producing nightly television. My experience includes writing a weekly web column on careers, running the lifestyle section for Forbes magazine, editing the opinion column, covering the art and auction market, valuing the art collections of the world’s billionaires, freelancing for such publications as The Washington Post and The New York Times, book reviewing, and writing investigative pieces and exposés. I have training and extensive experience covering law, business and economics. I’ve produced nightly general interest breaking news and analysis. I’ve also done a good deal of lifestyle writing and editing, which includes wine, food, travel,collecting, automobiles, health, real estate, style. I’ve edited a number of essays for prospective college students and I’m interested in media training.
