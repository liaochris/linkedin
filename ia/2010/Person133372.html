Mark Boumansour’s Summary

Executive Summary:

Mark Boumansour is a Technical Sales and Business Development Executive with specific backgrounds in distributed renewable energy sales, technology commercialization and market development. Mark brings extensive field experience interacting directly with industrial customers in the public and private sectors, state and local government, regional electric utilities and the development and directing of field channel partners.

Mark's combined core competencies in wind and solar energy technical sales, channel partner development, project management, and several engineering disciplines yields a unique qualification set that delivers credibility and enhanced deal flow for technically sensitive OEM's, project developers, owner/operators and service providers in the renewable energy industry.

Mark brings an inspiring attitude, extensive field experience, a network of national relationships and a history of developing, negotiating and closing complex deals in dynamic markets to deliver tangible value to his principals and partners.
