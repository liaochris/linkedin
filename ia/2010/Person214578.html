Wendy King’s Summary

Do you have a vision? Do you have a business idea? Or a product idea? Just about every idea is worth exploring but often you just can’t see that far. We can help you explore your ideas and take a look at the possibilities.

What we like to do is help people develop a plan for what their business could really look like. It involves asking and answering some hard questions about the business and your goals for the business. Then formulating a plan to make it happen. It can be overwhelming but we will coach you every step along the way to make sure you are staying on the right path. You will move from where you are to where you want to be. We will make sure you have the right team behind you as you move forward. We have your back. LET's TALK ABOUT IT.

Our company, bForward, Inc. offers a reliable alternative to in-house strategic marketing resources for business and market development. Acting as an out-sourced Marketing Department for your company, we offer an exceptionally high level of practical experience, know-how, confidentiality, and contacts. Clients of bForward, Inc. know that working with us is a more professional and less risky approach to developing new opportunities and further expand their customer base.

Offering extensive experience in helping people see the possibilities, we will help you identify your areas of expertise and successes and develop programs and strategies to make the most of that experience. We will assist you in utilizing the latest marketing programs and help you incorporate them into an overall marketing plan..
