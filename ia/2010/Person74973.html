Kenneth Clark’s Summary

My goal today is to just improve the world immediately around me.

That can happen in every home that I sell as a Realtor©. I do that through the public policy issues I cover in my government relations practice.

I even do that by composting my food waste and drying my clothes on a clothes line.

Once per month, I host a mixer in Central Phoenix called Get Your PHX. See www.getyourphx.com. We celebrate those people who take a chance on opening a new business downtown.

After 18 years in public affairs, politics and marketing, I've come to believe that we really affect the world best by acting locally.
