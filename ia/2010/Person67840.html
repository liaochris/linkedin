Mr. Eugene Soriano, CPA,CMA’s Summary

Eugene Soriano has more than 4 years of management experience at the BIG 4 firm or total of 9 years total experience which includes stint as revenue accountant in a telecommunications company. Eugene has experience in handling various industries both public and private companies.

He completed his degree Bachelor of Science in Accountancy at Saint Louis University, Philippines and entered the graduate school of business at Ateneo de Manila University while working at KPMG Manila.

He moved to California in 2005 to pursue his career in the US and joined Deloitte & Touche as an Audit Senior with specialization on financial services and consumer business. Eugene has handled clients of D&T for both public and private companies such as Jamba Juice, Toshiba, CalPERS, Basic American, Diamond Foods, Nikon, etc. In 2007, Eugene was offered a job as Audit Manager at Ernst & Young San Francisco. He handled various companies such as Clorox, Marcus & Millichap, Legacy Partners, Accel Partners, Lightspeed Ventures, Toshiba America, Benefit Cosmetics, Autotrade, Evotec, etc. He had extensive experience in SOX404, US GAAP and even IFRS conversion. He had served as Manager for the SEC F4 filing for a foreign issuer that acquired a US public company which includes IFRS conversion.

In 2009, he joined a boutique accounting firm in San Francisco as Audit Manager with specialization in real estate and nonprofit organizations. He performed OMB A-133 audits, cost certifications and compliance audits.

*Expertise in Investment Accounting, Technical Accounting & Financial Reporting, Cost Accounting, Valuation, SEC & SOX Compliance, IFRS and US GAAP
