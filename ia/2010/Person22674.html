Bethany Myers PhD (Candidate), MBA’s Summary

My last corporate position was as a Department Administrator in the Regulatory Affairs Department of a Pharmaceutical Company. I manage the day to day activities of the department which includes the Executive Vice President, Associate and Assistant Director, Safety Manger, an on-site Submission Specialist, as well as two off-site Submission Specialist.

I handle all expense reports for the department as well as all departmental invoices on a regularly and timely basis. Coordinate all domestic and international travel arrangements for the Executive Director. Work with other Administrators and outside agencies as needed.

Staying informed about medical updates from the FDA is a regular part of my job. I also am responsible for submitting the companies clinical trials, annual reports, etc, to the FDA for review and/or approval. I keep the department informed of any changes to our clinical trials through the use of Clinical Trial Updates. I maintain the departments calendar and vacation request. Route all pertinent information from FDA to Executive Director on a daily basis.

I am always looking for ways to improve myself in my current position by seeking out new task to accomplish. I am always willing to learn something new that will build my expertise and help my company achieve their goals.

In previous positions where I have worked as a Loss Prevention Supervisor, and Operations Manager I created goals for myself to become a manager and then to improve on my management skills while developing valuable organization skills and a keen attention to detail. I would like to work my way to being a Human Resource Manager one day and ultimately work as a Human Resource Director.
