Thiam Giam’s Summary

Over 12 years of management consulting, project management, and EPC (engineering, procurement, and construction) experience in the electric power industry.

Management consulting experience includes independent engineering technical due diligence and contract review for over 15,000 MW of power plants across a broad range of technologies (coal, natural gas, wind, geothermal, and solar) as well as power supply economic analyses for various generating asset owners.

Project management and engineering experience includes various power delivery projects operating at voltages up to 345 kV.

Thiam Giam’s Specialties:

Areas of expertise include project management, engineering management, optimal expansion plan and production costing modeling, technical due diligence, litigation support, interconnection review, contract review/analysis, contract negotiation, vendor/subcontractor management, cost estimating, procurement, CPM scheduling, electrical, and control and protection design.
