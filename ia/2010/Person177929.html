Ross Miller’s Summary

As Director of Analytics for Estee Lauder Companies, I focus on enabling the organization to understand and action on consumer behavior. My goals are to increase our ability to make decisions about investment in new initiatives, optimize marketing effectiveness, and create personalized relationships with consumers.

Prior to joining Estee Lauder Companies, I was an Account Executive at Coremetrics and owned relationships with online retailers such as Bass Pro Shops, Macy’s, Williams Sonoma, and QVC. Previously I led client services at email service provider SKYLIST (since acquired by Datran Media) where my team enabled and optimized email marketing programs for leading online businesses such as Disney, JDate, CoolSavings and Nascar.

Ross Miller’s Specialties:

Consumer strategy, analytics, online marketing and consulting.
