    July 1970 — Present (40 years )

    Eitan D. Schwarz MD DLFAPA FAACAP is board certified in both general as well as child and adolescent psychiatry.  During his nearly 40 years of practice and teaching in a variety of public, private, and academic settings, Dr. Schwarz has been steadily examining the needs of children and families throughout their lives. Since Cornell and medical school at Johns Hopkins, he has also been studying the uses of technology in health care and the practice of medicine. Currently on the faculty of Northwestern University's Feinberg School of Medicine, he has recently researched the use of digital media in play therapy with children.

    Dr. Schwarz is the author of KIDS, PARENTS & TECHNOLOGY: AN INSTRUCTION GUIDE FOR YOUNG FAMILIES and founder of developer of the online service www.mydigitalfamily.org .
