Adrian McDonagh’s Summary

You can contact me on adrian@easywebrecruitment.com, I will be happy to hear from you.

At EasyWebRecruitment.com we help clients to manage their online recruitment as well as dramatically reduce their recruitment costs by avoiding agency placement fees. Set out to prove that you can do a lot of work and achieve some spectacular results for a low flat fee, we now work with the likes of Tesco, Nike, Thomas Cook, Canon and over 400 others.

In the summer of 2009, I started running FREE online recruitment training courses for corporate recruiters. Now a large part of my working week is spent reading and researcing all aspects of interent recriutment to keep me sessions and relative and informative as possible.
