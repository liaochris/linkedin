Dr. Jose Guevara’s Summary

We provide advanced spinal correction utilizing state of the art chiropractic techniques. Dr. Guevara and staff provide effective solutions for auto accident injuries, back pain, herniated discs, neck pain, headaches, nutritional counseling, sports injuries, etc.

Our professional satisfaction comes when you are able to do the things you haven’t done in a while because of pain or discomfort.

To complement and enhance the healing effects of chiropractic treatment, we also provide the following therapeutic procedures:

• Ultrasound
• Electrical muscle stimulation
• Cold therapy (Cryotherapy)
• Moist heat therapy (Hydroculation)
• Paraffin wax therapy
• Cold laser therapy

We teach our patients special exercises to help strengthen and correct their own unique problem. These exercises can be performed in the comfort of your own home and can improve the effectiveness of your spinal correction by as much as 30-40%. In addition to skeletal misalignment, muscles and connective tissues can be out of place or strained by improper alignment and use. Specific exercises and stretches can help your body stay in balance and in health.

Get a second opinion from the pain experts and feel better without injections or surgery.
