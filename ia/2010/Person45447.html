Craig Wilensky’s Experience

  • Executive Vice President, Partner

    Knighted Software, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    August 2009 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Knighted is a leading provider of web-enabled inventory and warehouse management software (WMS). Knighted's WMS technology drives over 1 trillion transactions annually at leading companies in retail, consumer goods, 3PL, and pharmaceutical.

    Through our investments in R&D and Java technologies, Knighted provides both on-premise and Saas "Cloud" logistics solutions. It is well positioned to meet the current and future demands of new emerging technologies and the ever-changing demands of the distribution industry.

    Our company’s core value proposition addresses the critical need of complex distribution driven businesses to better manage the movement of materials, products and related information in and amongst multiple, complex facilities.

    Importantly, our company’s solution features real “ROI” and delivers measurable bottom line improvements, cost reductions, and increased supply chain performance.

  • Executive Vice President & Board Member (Nasdaq: IFLG)

    InfoLogix, Inc.

    (Public Company; IFLG; Information Technology and Services industry)

    August 2001 — July 2009 (8 years )

    As co-founder and and Executive Vice President of the company, I had the pleasure of leading InfoLogix from a start-up company to becoming a publicly traded Company on the NASDAQ Stock Exchange. The company had tremendous growth during my tenture to accomplishing over $100M in annual revenue and over 2,000 enterprise class customers.

    The strong growth came from leadership, focus and commitment to providing solutions for retail, commercial and healthcare enterprises using mobile computing solutions. By automating inventory and decision making process at the point of activity, InfoLogix was able to provide companies new levels of efficiency and accuracy.
