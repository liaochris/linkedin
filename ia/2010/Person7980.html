Allen Kampf’s Summary

My entry to the financial planning industry began in 1994 as an owner of Seigal Consulting Group. We designed and administered qualified retirement plans. In addition, we implemented group health insurance, group disability and group long term care for small to medium-sized businesses. In 1997, I joined First Financial Group in pursuit of a career in financial services. My concentration is in providing investment advice, insurance and strategic economic planning for individuals and business owners. In 1998, I became a partner in Wealth Advocacy Partners. My goal is to provide people with economically sound, strategic financial advice and to serve as the advocate of their financial future.
Since my Mother was diagnosed with Alzheimer's diease in April of 2007, I am also on a mission to make sure that everyone I touch does long term care planning. I recently became the State coordinator for Eldercarematters, a national consumer website addressing eldercare issues.Just recently I recieved a CLTC designation, which is Certified in Long Term Care.
