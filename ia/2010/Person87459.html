Jonathan Browning’s Summary

I Co-Founded Screaming Frog Productions in Los Angeles. Through Screaming Frog, I write and direct Award Winning short films that I feel passionate about and that I am proud to create.

I have written and directed six short films; "The Job", "The Sale", "The Hero", "The Party", "Going Green" and "Eco Ninja".

My shorts have screened at over 200 International Film Festivals and have been honored with 33 Awards.

I work with people that I absolutely adore.
