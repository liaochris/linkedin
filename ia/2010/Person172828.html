Rich Libero’s Summary

After 9 1/2 years at the NHL I decided that it was time for a new challenge and moved to Philly to help turn CSNPhilly.com into the top sports site in the city.

Comcast SportsNet is a fantastic Regional Sports Network that broadcasts games for the World Champion Phillies, Flyers and Sixers.

Covered professional sports for a variety of media outlets from 1984-1994. Have written for The Bergen Record, The Journal-News, USA Today, Fox Sports.com, NHL.com and a variety of other publications.

Launched FoxSports.com and served as editorial director from 1995-1999.

Worked briefly at Electronic Arts until my dream job at the NHL became available.

Served as a color commentator on Major League Soccer NY/NJ MetroStars telecasts in 1998.

Published a book about my life as a Miami Dolphins fan: "This could be the Year: My 30 Years as a Miami Dolphins Fan".

Rich Libero’s Specialties:

25 years of online, print and broadcast journalism experience. I have worked in the online industry since 1991. Fantasy gaming, publishing, marketing, public relations, advertising copy including radio and TV spots and technology.
