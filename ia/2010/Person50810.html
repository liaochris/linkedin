David Golding’s Summary

Hello, and welcome to my online professional profile.

I'm currently pursuing an M.A. in History of Christianity at Claremont Graduate University. So far, the experience has been enlightening, challenging, and rewarding. I intend to continue on to a Ph.D. and work as a scholar of mission history, particularly the development of Mormon missiology. I entered graduate school with a degree in European Studies from BYU and a minor in business management. If you are interested in some writing samples (some early undergraduate work, but other contemporary examples), please visit http://www.davidgolding.net/papers .

That being said, the past twelve years have been quite a journey through professional positions in technology, especially web development and design. I have a love for building online communications media and believe that the world will increasingly converge technologically around the internet; no matter the field, therefore, skills in web technology will prove extremely useful. My skills include expert knowledge in HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and CakePHP. I'm able to apply these skills to database management and online networking which create powerful solutions for myriad projects from historical research collection to full-blown e-commerce web sites. I have also studied and worked in graphic design and desktop publishing. My work in this arena is available on my website davidgoldingdesign.com.

I am excited for opportunities to consult with you on web projects and technology, even though my career will focus more academically on social sciences in the coming years. And I certainly enjoy engaging in academic work, especially in the disciplines of religion, history, and missiology.

David Golding’s Specialties:

Writing, historical and marketing research, copywriting, advertising design, graphic design, web development (XHTML, CSS 2.0, PHP 5.0, MySQL, CakePHP, Ruby on Rails), and technology consulting.
