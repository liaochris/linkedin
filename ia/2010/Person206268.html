Tom Moniak’s Summary

I sell homes and I’m very good at it… and that's what my past clients will say: http://tommoniak.crye-leike.com/about.php. Before moving to Tennessee, I have had an extremely diverse career in business. I was a musician for 17 years and during those years always held down the proverbial “day job.” As part of that, I built my own recording studio and did some engineering and producing, as well as a lot of promotion and touring. During the day, I worked my way up the corporate ladder in a company which changed names a few times; Baxter, Allegiance, and by the time I left, Cardinal Healthcare. I started out driving a forklift and worked my way to a regional Environmental and Health and Safety manager responsible for over 300 employees, covering Northern California and Hawaii. I later branched out to explore the online Advertising world with Premium Network as the Operations Manager. I ended up getting into Commercial Real Estate in Los Angeles with Colliers Seeley and then back to the on-line marketing game for a while with go-Zing and Greenfield Online. Which brings me to where I am today. I live and work in Franklin, TN helping people buy and sell real estate with Crye-Leike , REALTORS.
