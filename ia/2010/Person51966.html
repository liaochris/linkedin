David Schump’s Summary

With over 25 years in the creative industry I have the experience and knowledge to achieve any challenge that is asked of me. During this time I have had the privilege to manage small and large teams and am known as a hands-on Creative Director. My experience has given me the opportunity to lead brainstorming sessions, guide, direct and mentor my team members as well as Interact directly with clients. Copy writing and working in conjunction with copywriters gives me an even greater appreciation for the power evoked by the written word when combined with visual imagery.

A strong attention to detail and a willingness to “do what it takes” has helped me to build positive relationships with my colleagues and clients. I believe in strong team building so that we can all work together to provide the client with the best possible solutions to the problems presented. My diverse background is a valuable asset and shows my ability to adapt, grow and learn while always maintaining the highest creative standards.

I have learned and experienced much over my career and continue to learn more everyday. Please take a moment to review my work history that includes a number of locally and nationally known companies.

David Schump’s Specialties:

Branding and re-branding/re-positioning of products, art direction, direct mail, in-store POP, photo art direction, print and large format advertising, web design, retail store design/merchandising, signage, product graphics, museum/tradeshow exhibits, gorilla marketing and annual reports/publishing.
