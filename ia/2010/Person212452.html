Virginie Tison realized during her career that the artists need to work in two different directions: the first one is to work by themselves with their own collectors and patrons; the second one is to work with the professionals of art galleries and agents.

ART | TROPE is the fruit of ten years’ labor for Virginie Tison, an independent painter. She is also a former private school sales and marketing representative, management and legal assistant, corporate coach and decorator. She had many different occupations that have all provided her with real experience in organization and management.

Virginie Tison’s Specialties:

Creates professional relationships for artists by connecting them with high-profile exhibition sites and well-established sponsors in the creative arena.

Helps artists find their place in the professional art market, giving them the opportunity to evolve in every direction they desire.

Ensures effective connections between artists, exhibition sites, and sponsors - everything it takes to break into the art world.

  • ART | TROPE Paris

    ART | TROPE

    (Fine Art industry)

    January 2009 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    ART | TROPE permet aux artistes d’être créatifs, professionnels et autonomes

    Concept
    ART | TROPE délivre un antidote efficace et rentable au dilemme vieux comme le monde de l’intégrité artistique contre la dure réalité économique en offrant à ses membres un ensemble de services gratuits ou à faible coût.
    Notre slogan : Let Artists Create!

    En tant qu’artiste française, Virginie Tison a établi un solide réseau international, mais elle a pris conscience, comme tous les artistes, de la difficulté à trouver le juste équilibre entre business et création.

    En mai 2009, Mlle Tison s’attaque au défi de créer un collectif à New York qui mélangerait business et aspects pratiques du monde de l’art.

    Mission
    ART | TROPE a été créé et pensé afin d’aider l’intégration des artistes, de partager la culture en développant un foyer de mouvements interculturels au niveau international et en défendant les intérêts de tous les membres d’ART | TROPE.

    ART | TROPE construit des relations professionnelles pour les artistes grâce à nos relations avec des lieux d’expositions renommés et des sponsors bien installés dans la scène créative.

    ART | TROPE va au-delà de ce que font les autres collectifs d’artistes : c’est un guide de survie pour les artistes!

    ART | TROPE fournit à ses partenaires une présentation du portfolio des œuvres de tous ses artistes. Ce sont ensuite les partenaires qui choisissent les œuvres qu’ils souhaitent exposer, en fonction de leur emploi du temps et leur besoin esthétique. Les artistes choisissent le service qu’ART | TROPE peut leur apporter.

    Enfin, nos sponsors soutiennent la communauté ART | TROPE toute entière en offrant à tous ses membres des services à prix réduits.

    Contact
    Que vous soyez un artiste ou un futur partenaire ou sponsor, nous vous remercions pour l’intérêt que vous portez à ART | TROPE!
    Pour nous rejoindre ou en savoir plus, visitez notre site Internet sur www.art-trope.com ou envoyez nous un email a info@art-trope.fr.

  • Founder & CEO of ART | TROPE

    ART l TROPE

    (Fine Art industry)

    May 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    ART | TROPE allows artists to be creative, professional and independent

    Concept
    ART | TROPE provides the efficient and cost-effective antidote to the age old dilemma of artistic integrity versus harsh business realities by offering its members discounts and a set of free or low fee services.
    Our slogan: Let Artists Create!

    As a working artists in France, Virginie Tison has established a stable international network, yet she realized like all artists, the difficulty to balance business with creation.

    In May 2009, Ms. Tison tackled the challenge of creating a collective that would merge business and practical aspects of the art world.


    Mission
    ART | TROPE has been thought and created in order to help with the artists' integration, to share culture by developing intercultural movements internationally and to defend the rights of all ART | TROPE members.

    ART | TROPE forges professional relationships for artists through our connections with high profile exhibition sites and well-established sponsors in the creative arena.

    ART | TROPE goes above and beyond the call of many artists collectives: it's an artist survival guide!

    ART | TROPE provides its partners with a portfolio containing the artwork of all its artists. Partners then deceide the artwork they wish to exhibit in their public or private space to best suit their schedules and business aesthetic. The artists choose the service that ART | TROPE can provide.

    Lastsly, our sponsors support the entire ART | TROPE community by offering discounted services for all our members.

    Bear in mind that ART | TROPE is not an art dealer. We want our artists to concentrate on creating art and to leave the logistics to us.

    Contact
    Whether you are an artist, future partner or sponsor, we thank you for your interest in ART | TROPE!
    To join or find out more, please go to our website: www.art-trope.us OR send us an email info@art-trope.us.

  • Painter

    VY

    (Fine Art industry)

    March 2005 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    "Through painting, I express the feelings that inspire me, my perceptions of this world, and the helplessness of living a life on the brink of disaster, both human and ecological. Colors symbolize hope."
    -VY

    VY’s paintings are a splash of color, a subtle but strong wave of the brush.
    Texture, form and atmosphere converge in her compositions to create a tenuous harmony, sometimes teetering towards chaos. Her paintings come alive unexpectedly through lines and textures, like veins, previously unseen, in the body. There is a further sense of wandering urgency to her work; she paints winding turns, patches of spirit and intensity, roaming and unsure paths that slowly circle back to suggest a finer mystery as the lines converge. Hers is a winding and ghostly road that moves, irrevocably, toward a single, unifying composition.
