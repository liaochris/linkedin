Haitham Razagui’s Summary

A versatile Technical Writer with a background in Information Design and strong Graphic Design skills.

Experienced in writing technical documentation using Word, Framemaker, Author-it, Epic Editor, InDesign and Illustrator (amongst various other authoring tools). Highly versatile, the subject matter I have covered includes software, company procedures, military hardware, telecommunications and vehicle diagnostics. I am creative and enjoy learning about new areas of business. I am a fast learner, enabling me to quickly become effective within new teams and projects.

One of my specialties is creating PDF based information libraries that are both easy to navigate on-screen and to print from. I am also experienced in implementing CD/DVD auto-run and software installation front-ends, with quick-start documentation to make them as foolproof as possible.

I have some experience and a strong interest in wayfinding design - this includes analysing the environment as well as architectural plans, working out where and why people get lost. Helping people to find their way through large buildings such as hospitals, museums and libraries is achieved by using a system of strategically placed signs and maps that are conceived from an information design and usability point of view.

Please see my website for more information and examples of projects I've worked on.
