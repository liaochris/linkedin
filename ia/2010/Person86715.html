  • Managing Partner

    Urban Labs

    (Real Estate industry)

    August 2001 — March 2007 (5 years 8 months)

    Jack Illes was previously the Managing Partner of Urban Labs, an urban design and architecture firm specializing in mixed-use retail, hospitality and residential projects. Urban Labs projects included new developments for Harrah's Entertainment in St Louis and Kansas City, a mixed use redevelopment in Phoenix, Arizona, retail strategies for Ballpark Village in San Diego and Kewalo Basin in Honolulu, as well as urban infill residential projects. In Hawaii, Urban Labs was responsible for developing the Strategic Plan for The Queen Emma Foundation Waikiki Properties, and served as Design Architect on the $150 million redevelopment plan for the International Market Place.
