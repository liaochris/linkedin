Jay Westlin’s Summary

Business development executive with over 19 years media, entertainment and advertising experience (more than 14 years, of which, are online). Wide range of skills including expertise in publisher web site affiliation (and M&A), web site monetization, and deep understanding of brand ad sales representation and online marketing.

Jay Westlin’s Specialties:

Online affiliation, acquisiton and licensing agreements; GoogleAnalytics, comScore MediaMetrix traffic and pageview performance monetization analysis; viral widget and online marketing; online sales merchandising; media buying; online business strategy and partnerships; SEO/SEM management; and all areas surrounding the creation and launch of a consumer website that are non-developer related.
