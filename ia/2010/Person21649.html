Benito Castro’s Summary

OTHER ACTIVITIES

- Author of the Comucor blog since January 2006, (http://comucor.blogspot.com ) on topics such as Communication and Management in the current globalization panorama and the push of new social media.

- Teacher at the business school Instituto de Estudios Jurídicos y Empresariales de Cajasol (Cajasol Institute of Legal and Business Studies) (Andalusia´s second savings bank), where I was Corporate Communication Conference director for six courses (1998-2005)

- Guest Teacher at Business and Institutional Communication Conferences at the University of Seville.

- Guest lecturer at Marcus Evans

- Author of the book ‘El auge de la Comunicación Corporativa (The Corporate Communication Boom)’, a free download on the webpage: http://augecomucor.com

- Published article in the collective book “Blog-Book“, available soon, with the sponsorship of Red.es, Fundación Telefónica and BBVA

- Award from the Asociación de Telespectadores de Andalucia (Andalusia’s Viewers Association) (ATEA) in 2002, for the “best behind-the-camera work”.


ACADEMIC RECORD

- Bachelor of Information Sciences, a branch of Journalism at the Universidad Complutense de Madrid (Complutense University of Madrid).

- Master´s in Economics and Business Managment from the Instituto Internacional San Telmo en Sevilla (San Telmo International Institute of Seville), an institute that develops Seville´s IESE program for experienced managers.
