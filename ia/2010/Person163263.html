Pete Trabucco’s Summary

A successful sales and marketing manager, who has created, trained and motivated many award winning teams in the aviation healthcare and broadcast advertising industries. Also has many years as a senior sales professional who has lectured and trained other sales representatives to be just as successful. Has accomplished this through best practice research and training which is created specifically for that particular industry.

Former Chief of Staff for the N.J. State General Assembly Middlesex / Passaic / Essex County.-19th and 34th legislative districts. Oversaw entire legislative operation. Handled all hiring and firing and promotion of bills for several Assemblyman. Television producer and internal legislative lobbyist. Speech and OPED writer. Middlesex County New Jersey Freeholder Candidate in 2004. Received over 93,000 votes (from both democrats and republicans) in that election campaign. The most any GOP candidate has ever received in Middlesex County. In addition to the above articles, he is an avid promoter for GA rights and one of his latest cover stories "AVIATION ACTIVISTS WANTED... APPLY HERE" is at
http://www.americasflyways.com/may09story.html

Licensed private pilot and member of the United States Air Force Aux (CAP) Program. Former CJCS Squadron Commander. Currently the NJ Assistant Director of Cadet Programs - McGuire AFB, Wrightstown, New Jersey. Continues to freelance (feature) write for about a dozen national aviation and healthcare magazines. Covering STS 132 Shutttle launch for Ad Astra Mag this May.

His book "America's Top Roller Coasters and Amusement Parks (tips for those who ride them and especially those who FEAR them) "was released from Tate Publishing in August of 2009. It is now available for order at Amazon, Borders and Barnes and Noble as well as the above book website. Former radio personality (no time for that now!)

Active (and competitive) over 30 baseball player. Former college baseball and football standout.
