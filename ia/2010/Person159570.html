Pat Lozito’s Summary

COMFORCE Corporation is a leading provider of Contingent Staffing, Information Technology Consulting and Human Resource Outsourcing Solutions to Fortune 1000 and emerging growth companies. The Company has an over 50 year history of supplying innovative staffing solutions and providing service excellence domestically and abroad.

Traded on the American Stock Exchange (symbol: "CFS"), COMFORCE provides an array of human resource options through a nationwide network of offices under the COMFORCE and Uniforce brand names. In addition, the Company provides Independent Contractor 1099 Consulting Solutions and Professional Payrolling Services through its PrO Unlimited® Subsidiary. Payroll funding, preparation and back office services are provided to independent temporary service companies in the U.S. and Canada through its THISCO® and Brentwood Services Group® subsidiaries.
