Owen Blevins’s Experience

  • Cretehead

    ConcreteAnswers.TV

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    June 2009 — Present (1 year )

    I'm Owen Blevins, and I advocate for all the concrete plants out there that can't speak for themselves.
    You see, everybody cares about trucks. That's easy. And everyone cares about people.
    But a plant? A plant is a 25 to 40 year investment that is the least-understood yet most important piece of equipment you will ever purchase for your business. Yet you'll see people spending more time picking the color for their trucks than spec'ing a plant!
    Something is wrong here, folks. You can trade in a truck or sell it if you don't like it; not so with a plant. it's like luggage; it's with you for life.
    So, I am going to be a spokesman for the plant.
    Full disclosure: My partner Jay Robinson and I are in the business of selling concrete equipment, and we happen to think that the lines we carry are the best in the industry. If you are not in our 13 state mid-Atlantic territory, or even if you are but prefer to work with someone else, I won’t be offended. But beware the dark side. Find a dealer who shows they care about you and your specific needs. Because once that plant is bought, delivered and erected, there’s no going back.
    For more about plant and equipment sales, go to www.maconcrete.com. Here, my sole purpose is to educate, inform, and entertain. So, watch the videos, form an opinion, and take the time to contact us with your reactions. At the very least, leave a comment at the end of the video.
    Remember, concrete plants don't have to break down, wear out, or collapse into scrap metal. They just need us to listen.
