Eron Hayes’s Summary

I’m a former/future entrepreneur with a passion for building and expanding business through sales, marketing, and development opportunities. I love collaborating with different people, projects, and companies to draw customers and clients to innovative uses of resources and technologies, and I am a strong voice for flawless end-user experience and support. In other words, I like making it easy to do business right.

I have eight years of work experience, beginning as an executive assistant, moving on to running my own personal assisting company (Etc. Services), while attending the University of Washington. I then went on to work as an executive recruiter, and most recently in business development for a comScore Top 25 company. My strengths are in organization, relationship (client) building, and innovation.

I am constantly learning, and am most attracted toward businesses with a dynamic atmosphere that involve change, challenge, and the ability to work both independently and creatively. I am particularly interested in working with a company that affords the opportunities of upward mobility, a variety of intellectually stimulating tasks, as well as a cooperative and fun team environment.
