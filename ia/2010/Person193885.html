Stephane Prud'homme’s Summary

Stephane Prud'homme, M.A. Communication (UQAM), I.E.MBA (Rutgers Business School in Beijing, China), is President and CEO at 180 degrees | public relations and business development. He lived and worked in China from summer 2008 to Nov. 2010.

Appointed "Ambassador of University of Quebec in Montreal" (http://news.180degrees.asia)

More than 15 years of professional experience in Management, Public Relations, Marketing, Business Development, Corporate Communications, Media Relations, Crisis Management, Sustainable Development, Community Relations, and Investor Relations.

Founder of Relations publiques Sans frontières | Public Relations Without Borders. Founded in 2007 with Luc Beauregard and well-known PR professionals.

Fluent in French and English; Mandarin intensive lessons for 21 months; and basic understanding of German and Italian.

More than 19 years of contribution to charities and NGOs in Canada and now in China: member of Boards, committee director, manager, mentor for juniors, and fund raising President.

Five Excellence Awards: Global Public Relations Program, Crisis Management and Business Continuity, Inspira Award (Senior’s Support to Junior PR Professionals), Finalist Inspira Award, and Academic Achievement Award.

Invited Expert in twenty-seven seminars on different topics.

University teacher for five graduate courses (45 hours) on Public Relations and Management.
