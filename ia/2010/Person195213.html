Steve Laufmann’s Summary

Enterprise Architect and Solution Strategist with a deep understanding of business and technology’s roles in creating competitive advantage. Drive visionary thinking, design innovation, technology leadership, and high-value outcomes. Balance near-term benefits with long-term viability.

- Assess business needs, evaluate solutions, identify gaps
- Develop achievable solution roadmaps, define projects, and build persuasive business cases
- Design and integrate solutions using BPM, SOA, and related technologies
- Define SOA granularity, messaging, and governance
- Technology transformation, systems consolidation, and adapting legacy systems for reuse
- Information semantics, logistics, and presentation—business meaning at the right time, in the right place, for the right use
- Accelerate IT agility

Steve Laufmann’s Specialties:

Solution design to create high-value outcomes in difficult settings. Bridging business and IT groups to create achievable solutions to the right issues at the right times.
