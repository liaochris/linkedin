Sefton Oxford’s Summary

Sefton Oxford is the President of SDO & Associates. (Management Consulting Company)
The company is also involved in Recruiting of Key Staff for construction Projects. We have also added Energy Audit to our portfolio of services.
Mr. Oxford's latest project was a mixed-use condominium project in the Pacific North West USA.
In charge of the building and marketing heavy line stringing equipment at Morpac Industries in Pacific, WA.
President of his own water jet cutting company.
All through his long tenure at Balfour Beatty has served in various capacities in the following Balfour Beatty companies, Director and Vice President of Construction Management Services for Heery International, located in Atlanta and has served as Vice President and Area Manager for Balfour Beatty Construction Bahamas Limited and Balfour Beatty Bahamas Limited in the Caribbean. President, Constructiones Balfour Beatty de Venezuela C.A. South America. Mr. Oxford also was Vice President of Bay West Development Corporation in The Bahamas (a company specifically set up to develop building and commercial projects in The Bahamas).
He marketed and managed large complex construction programs and has served as technical director for technically difficult construction projects for Balfour Beatty and other contractors. He has many years of experience in slip-forming, heavy civil construction including rail and tunnel projects and a wealth of construction knowledge and resources to his projects. He has been technically innovative whenever a problem warrants special attention.
He has been instrumental in writing many company procedures and systems from the computerization of records to implementation of special construction techniques and systems.
He invented US Patent # 5,855,181 for a fixed shank plow anchor. The patent was granted 1999.

Sefton Oxford’s Specialties:

Administration, Budgeting, Construction Management, Design Build, Microsoft Office, Prolog, Staff Development, RFI, RFP, Safety, Scheduling (MS Project + P3) Contract negotiations, Hiring Staff, Customer relations, Executive Management, Overseas Projects administration, Safety, General Supervisory skills, Purchasing, Concrete Mix Design, Quality & Quality Control, Inventor.
