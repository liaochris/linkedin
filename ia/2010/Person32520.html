Carlos Gámez Kindelán’s Summary

I mainly make interactive graphics and animations for El Pais.com, AS.com, Kalipedia.com, Parasaber.com, CincoDías.com, Elviajero.com. I am in charge of the investigation, documentation, planning and technical elaboration as much of breaking news as of works in the mid term. I use freehand, Illustrator, Photoshop, Flash, Cinema 4d and Final Cut fundamentally. When the work allows it I dedicate to the investigation to apply the work to new supports.
