Rajiv Mody’s Summary

Rajiv Mody is a 10+ year veteran in social media, interactive and television. Currently, he is serving as managing director for A4R4, a leading edge digital media company. Mody also served as the vice president and executive producer of 3d Worlds at MTV, where he helped develop groundbreaking television/music-based online environments that combined social networking with gaming while exposing new potential revenue streams. The award-winning initiative earned acclaim in the media/entertainment industry for creating first-of-its-kind environments where viewers can live out the story arcs and programming they see on-air.

Previously, Mody has held several positions at MTV Networks, including senior director of franchise development, director of integrated marketing, manager of strategic partnerships and public affairs, researcher and writer in the News & Documentaries department and coordinator of Public Affairs. During this time, Mody managed day-to-day internal operations for 3d Worlds, helped establish MTV’s integrated marketing west coast division, helped coordinate presidential candidate appearances for the voter registration campaign Choose or Lose 2000 and was recognized by the International Association of Official Human Rights Agencies for his work toward MTV receiving their top “Business and Industry Award.”

Before moving to New York City, Mody served as a research analyst in the Clinton White House, working on such policy initiatives as education, crime and the economy.

Mody earned a Certificate in General Management from the University of California Los Angeles’ Anderson School of Management and a bachelor of arts in political science and economics from Brown University. He also completed the National Association for Multi-Ethnicity in Communications Executive Leadership Development Program and is conversational in the Spanish language.
