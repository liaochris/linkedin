Bennett Thuener’s Summary

Finance and Accounting professional with more than 10 years experience dedicated to building and supporting start-ups and emerging companies. Strengths include: Full ownership of GL (account reconciliations, journal entries, monthly close, etc.), cash management and banking relationships, definition and preparation of financial reporting and metrics aggregation, implementation of processes and procedures, budgeting and forecasting, business modeling, fund raising, and strategic plan development based on solid finance and accounting fundamentals. I am team player with a focus on building the optimal infrastructure for every stage of the developing company.

Career Highlights

• Managed multiple financings (both debt and equity) and the eventual sale of Powerset, Inc. to Microsoft, Corp in Aug. 2008 for more than $100M.

• Teaming with Jigsaw’s CEO, I raised more than $10M in venture capital from new institutional investors.

• Managed the acquisition process resulting in the sale of Uptilt, Inc. (dba: EmailLabs) to J.L. Halsey in Oct. 2005. The effective acquisition price was greater than 20X capital raised by Uptilt, Inc.

• While at BenefitStreet, I defined and implemented the processes and procedures for reporting and distributing revenue sharing with customers, which was a core functionality of a new service offering, that grew from 0 to 200 clients and resulted in amassing more than $300M in assets under management.

• Managed the annual budgeting process for Nortel Network’s Large System PBX Manufacturing and Material departments in Santa Clara, CA. These departments were responsible for the final assembly of products that accounted for more than $800M in annual sales and $400M in annual inventory throughput.
