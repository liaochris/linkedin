Arnoldo Madrigal’s Summary

Arnoldo Madrigal is the Managing Director of Link Inversiones ( www.linkinversiones.com ) an IBD (www.iadb.org ) and Mesoamerica ( www.mesoamerica.com ) project in Costa Rica for the development of angel investment , it includes a Business Plan National Event (www.yoemprendedor.net ) and sharing practices with other countries in the region. He is member of The High Technology National Center (CeNAT) (www.conare.ac.cr).
Arnoldo has a BS in Computer Science and MBA in Marketing.
