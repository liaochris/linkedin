David Chapin’s Summary

I am a strategist with two primary foci: 1) marketing for life science companies and 2) invention, development and design of high-tech, high-touch products, primarily in the health care and medical space.
Forma Life Science Marketing is a leading marketing firm for biotech, life science and pharma companies. We distill complex communications into compelling messages for sophisticated audiences.
Forma Life Science Marketing provides marketing strategy, including positioning, naming, and messaging, validated through market research. We then bring this strategy to life through marketing implementation services, such as the development of identities, ads (online, print and broadcast), web sites and interactive, print collateral, trade shows, direct mail, etc.
Forma Life Science Marketing's expertise has been gained through our work in this space since 1987. There are few challenges we haven't seen before, and tackled successfully.
Forma Industrial Design provides product strategy and design, validating this through market research such as focus groups, ethnographic research, user studies, etc. We then bring this strategy to life through design, prototyping, CAD services and engineering.
I am named inventor on over 40 patents in the US and abroad.
