Rob Kramarz’s Summary

Specialist in organization development, team building and corporate culture.

Experience: 30 years in computer and software executive management. Wrote 21 patents. Wrote the original papers on software RAID and co-designed the first server mirroring software with automatic fail-over. CEO of 1776, Inc.

Degree in psychology from UCSC. Greatest interest today is business organization development and venture capital. Currently investing in technology companies.

I combine my skills to enable inventors and scientists to rapidly build companies that are likely to succeed quickly, make their founders and investors wealthy and bring breakthrough products to market quickly.

Investment interest in software, bio-medical, space, robotics and alternative energy.

My higher purpose is to accelerate scientific, social and yes spiritual progress.

I am available to train management teams and investors on how to create and sustain organization cultures that win.

Rob Kramarz’s Specialties:

Organization development, investment strategy and venture capital due diligence. Studied physics @ CalTech and psych @ UC Santa Cruz.
