Kerry Carnahan Ellis’s Summary

Effective communication has always been my passion, and with 15+ years of experience I've proven my ability to deliver well-targeted messaging, campaigns, and content that achieve great results. I believe strongly in the power of effective storytelling, and am fascinated with way digital media is evolving to enable creative, targeted communications that engage audiences like never before.

As a consultant I help corporate clients with positioning/messaging, campaign development, project management, content development, and writing/editing for both business and technical audiences. My key strengths include the ability to communicate complex ideas clearly, manage communications projects effectively, and develop strategic messaging that tells a compelling story.
