Mark Duke’s Summary

Ten-year diversified management background, combination of business development and outside sales, manufacturing operations and technical expertise, and over 17 years’ printing/converting industry experience that includes labels, folding cartons and flexible packaging for multiple markets, as well as medical/specialty converting, in delivering competitive market advantage and maximizing revenue growth and profitability for both converter and supplier.

Technical Overview:
Flexography, gravure, screen & letterpress; flatbed & rotary die cutting; hot-foil stamping; cold foiling; embossing; laminating; heat sealing; printed conductives; diagnostics; PSAs; non-wovens; film, foil, foam, paper & paperboard substrates; water, solvent, & UV ink, coating & adhesive properties, application & printability; FDA/cGMP regulated, AIB & ISO environments
