Melani Ward, MA, M.Ed. CCC’s Summary

I am a performance coach, counselor, retreat leader, blogger, runner, teacher and a writer.

I started my own business five years ago and before that I spent 15 years teaching, counseling and coaching everyone from child victims of sexual abuse to college students to athletes.

My favorite thing to do is help women find their mojo (magic, meaning) through spirit, sport and service. I do this through my writing, counseling, performance coaching and hosting live Mojo Retreats in cool places around the country.
