alladean chidukwani’s Summary

Working for Device Global Technologies (SA) in 2003-2004, i was responsible for rolling out IT Infrastructure donated by Microsoft to remote South African Schools under the DIGITAL PARTNERSHIP project. This involved refurbishing used IT equipment e.g. PCs, laptops, routers, switches and installing network infrastructure in the schools. Extensive computer hardware and networking experience was gained in this project.

Whilst at New Horizons SA, did a lot of Network Infrastructure consulting and training on Cisco CCNA and Microsoft MCSE courses. Also took part in the training of NIA (national intelligence) officials working on 'Hardening the Infrastructure' for the South African government with emphasis on White Hat hacking and Linux administration.

As an Accomplished Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), MCITP-Enterprise Admin, MCSE,MCSA, MCDST,MCP,MCTS, CCNA, CCENT,A+,Linux+,Server+, Security+ and Network+ Professional i have held a numerous number of training and consulting interventions on Microsoft Products and non-Microsot in Southern Africa and Australia with Emphasis on Network Infrastructure - Server 2003 and Server 2008 and also on Office Applications.

I developed/ authored the "I DREAM" training manual whilst working at IT Intellect (SA) which is a training manual for Diabetic Patients in South Africa on how to use the I-Dream website (a tool or portal to educate diabetic patients and the tracking of their health by medical specialists online). As part of the BLUE IQ project, i worked with Diamond Consultants on the development of the manual as the Author.

Whilst at IT Intellect, i groomed the Team of Server operators from T-SYSTEMS who are responsibe for monitoring Linux and Microsoft Windows based servers for the Germany Automobile Company (VW - Volkswagen) world wide.

As a Microsoft Office 2007 Master and Microsoft Office Specialist (2003), i boast of more than 4yrs experience of Office skills and applications training.
