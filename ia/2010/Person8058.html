Alejandra Alvarez’s Summary

Achieving the American Dream is where my voyage begins. I have lived in big and small cities but Kansas City is a place of its own and we have truly found a home here! I’ve completely merged into the American life style but I haven’t and will never forget my roots.

My introduction into the professional world was in Promotions; writing copy, assisting with live remotes, coordinating events and creating an online presence. Eventually I assisted with proposal writing and pitching.

Along the way I exercised very specific duties within the realm of Promotions and Public Relations through various internships and jobs, such as Event Coordinator for the YMCA, Public Relations Assistant and Diversity Coordinator for NWMSU, Promotions Intern for KMBC-TV, etc.

Most recently and before graduating, I was the student-run Public Relations Firm Director at NWMSU. The Student-run firm is an operating Public Relations Firm that offers students a quality experience that will set each apart from the crowd. As a Director, I was in charge of the organizational structure; pitching and maintaining the client list; leading a team of 8 executives; recruitment; promotions; and finally the overall image of the firm.

I am now helping launch and establish a Social Business Training program for Centriq Training that will empower small and medium size businesses to adopt a Social Media strategy throughout the organization, including their leaders, sales and marketing staff. Our program is the only social business training program that is state (Kansas and Missouri) Board of Regents certified in the nation. This is a very exciting time for both Centriq and I as we enter a new phase of opportunities.
