Marnix Wolf [MVP]’s Summary

After years of working as a third line Systems Engineer I have made a careerchange: I am becoming a expert in the Microsoft System Center products range.

At the moment I am certified for OpsMgr 2007 & SCCM 2007 and working on many projects for OpsMgr 2007 implementations for well known companies. In each of these projects I am the technology specialist / consultant. I also do the designs of new OpsMgr environments, advise customers on how to improve/upgrade/update their exisiting OpsMgr environments. Also troubleshooting and making OpsMgr environments run like clockwork are assignments I have been on to. On other occassions I give workshops/trainings to learn the IT staff how top operate their OpsMgr enviroments on a daily basis. OpsMgr has also an Auditing component, Audit Collection Services(ACS). For ACS I have done also designs and installations for Proof of Concepts (POCs). At another company I have worked with multiple OpsMgr environments which were connected with each other. Migrations from multiple OpsMgr enviroments to one production and one testing OpsMgr environments has also done by me.

System Center Products of Microsoft. OpsMgr & SCCM mostly. Since January 2010 I have become a SCOM MVP.
