Danielle Ferraro * LION’s Summary

Danielle Ferraro is a Business Development professional for Internet Connectivity Group (I.C.G.) in Orange County, California.

Danielle is a born and raised Southern California native. She attended one of the nation’s most conservative private colleges, Franciscan University of Steubenville, where philanthropic pursuits rank as high as academics in the formation of well-rounded individuals. While studying Philosophy and Spanish both there and abroad in Valparaiso, Chile, she devoted her time to ministering to the poor and homeless in Pittsburgh, Barbados and central Mexico.

Upon returning to Orange County, Danielle honed her sales skills in the mortgage world for a wholesale broker, eventually specializing in A-paper, jumbo and sub-prime home loans, effectively consulting customers through the entire loan program and lender selection process.

Next Danielle transitioned to the a position as Regional Account Manager for Datamax O’Neil Printer Supplies, an OEM thermal printer and paper company, responsible for all accounts in the Northern US and Canada in her vertical. Finding her niche in channel sales to VARs and direct customers, she excelled in B2B sales bringing in new key accounts from several government municipalities, NFL teams and major universities. Danielle acted as a key representative for Datamax O’Neil at tradeshows across the country, building relationships in a tight-knit industry.

Finally, Danielle found her home in a Business Development role at I.C.G., an innovator of wireless digital signage paired with creative digital marketing. Her role today consists in strategically identifying those Fortune 1000 companies who would be most benefitted by the interactive marketing campaign that I.C.G. provides and seeking out the right VP or higher level contacts. She acts as a liaison for executive officers, partners and direct customers through effective customer education, tradeshow representation and in-person presentations.
