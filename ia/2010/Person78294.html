Gretchen Gary’s Summary

An accomplished marketing specialist, Gretchen Gary recently returned to the United States after several years working and studying in Latin America.

Most recently, Gretchen was the Marketing Manager for two large condo developers in Costa Rica, where she helped to launch the rental program for four different condo hotels, including Bahia Azul, La Paloma Blanca, Bahia Encantada and Jaco Bay Ramada.

Prior to her international experience, Gretchen worked as a direct marketing specialist for a B2B firm in Los Angeles. She is trained in direct response copywriting, email marketing, graphic design, Web development, search optimization, public relations, journalism, print production and social media marketing.

Gretchen has strong interpersonal skills across all management levels, and a demonstrated ability to produce and execute high-return campaigns.
