Randy Higgison - randy.higgison@comcast.net’s Summary

Over twenty years of my career has been in the optical memory/semiconductor technologies; high tech, high volume, thin film mfg. I have developed a track record of proven successes in R&D, process eng, new process/product development, project/new construction management, start-up operations, and general/executive management.

Since the downsizing of mfg in 2005, I have broadened my skills while self employed working as a consultant for other industries. To enhance my experiences I obtained a Six Sigma Black Belt certification from the Institute of Industrial Engineers (IIE). I am often faced with technologies new to me, but in a short time I am still very successful in contributing significant and quantifiable improvements. Some of my recent experiences have been with LCD mfg accompanied with military contracts.

One of my strengths is directing/building lab based concepts and new formats into efficient high volume mfg operations. In this role, I possess the skills to serve as an effective “bridge/interpreter” between the engineers/developers / scientists and the operations teams.

Experiences and Strengths:
• A Six Sigma Black Belt with a track record of process improvements and operational turnarounds with significant cost savings and increased revenue.
• An “on the floor”, people empowerment management style with a firm belief in teamwork and the philosophy of continuous process improvement.
• Project management experience including all functions for eight-figure new format introductions, new construction, and facility/mfg expansions.
• Middle and upper management experience directing R&D / Eng teams in the development and introduction for new product lines, designing new equipment, and creating new processes.

I am now actively pursuing process eng/middle management opportunities in high tech mfg industries like solar, display, semiconductor, and other applicable technologies. I am targeting start-up to mid-sized US companies.
