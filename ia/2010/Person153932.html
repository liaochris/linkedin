Ron Nickelson CPC,CTS,CERS’s Summary

Nickelson & Associates, LLC, based in Houston, is a boutique executive search & strategic advisory firm specializing in the recruitment of experienced energy professionals involved in the upstream and midstream hydrocarbon industries. Our client companies include supermajors, national oil companies, large Independents, publicly traded middle market companies, master limited partnerships, and privately owned companies, along with venture capital and private equity funded organizations.

Functionally, we concentrate in exploration, production, pipeline transportation and storage throughout North America and Internationally. Our upstream and midstream group is divided regionally by Appalachia, Houston, Mid-Continental (Kansas, Oklahoma, North and West Texas), the Rocky Mountains, Calgary, Alaska and California. We have successfully completed assignments in, Bakersfield, Calgary, Corpus Christi, Dallas, Denver, Fort Worth, Houston, Los Angeles, Midland, Oklahoma City, Santa Barbra and Tulsa.

Since inception, Nickelson Associates has been guided by its founding principles. Foremost, the consultants of Nickelson Associates hold the highest of ethical standards, along with an unsurpassable level of personal service, an unmatched dedication to quality, and a unique 'value-add' approach. This philosophy and methodology are consistently demonstrated in Nickelson Associates unparalleled research capabilities, the full utilization of firm resources on every project, and the timeliness with which we complete our assignments. Built on the research first published in “Impending Crisis,” and expanded upon in “Employer of Choice,” “The War for Talent,” “Topgrading” and “The Truth about Managing People,” a CERS credentialed search firm, Nickelson Associates will be able to provide a unique and extended service.


We feel it is our purpose to be a value-add search firm...We measure our own success based on the success of the leaders and talent we bring to your organization

Ron Nickelson CPC,CTS,CERS’s Interests:

Ron L Nickelson CPC/CTS, CERS led the formation of NICKELSON AND ASSOCIATES, LLC in 2009. Prior to this, Ron served as Global Practice Leader Natural Resources and Energy practice of Addison Search, a specialized staffing firm that provides highly qualified personnel, on either a temporary project or permanent placement basis, primarily in the areas of finance and accounting, information technology, geological, engineering, legal, and land administration to a diverse client base of mid-sized businesses as well as Fortune 500 companies. The company was founded in 1999 and has offices in downtown and suburban Chicago, Boston, Dallas, New York and Houston.
