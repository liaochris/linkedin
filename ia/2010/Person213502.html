A. Waleed Rashid’s Summary

With more than five years experience in the online advertising industry, Waleed has been responsible for marketing & advertising development, optimizing campaign performance and media buying within numerous verticals of CPC, CPA & CPM Media. Waleed held the position of Direct Marketing Manager at Adteractive (acquired by Next Internet, 2007) in San Francisco, Campaign/Account Management roles at Quinstreet & Looksmart (NasdaqGM: LOOK), all savvy pioneers in the nuances of online marketing and it's technologies. Waleed was responsible for optimizing strategic marketing campaigns and managing online relationships with partners such as Blockbuster, Time-Warner, Netflix, Columbia House, BMG Music, Strayer University and CEC (NasdaqGS: CECO). Waleed has been involved in numerous start-up ventures from Financial Consulting/Debt Management to Automotive Brokering to Online/Mobile Media.

Waleed holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree from the University of California, Berkeley where he completed his Thesis on Comparative Economic Systems and was a Master of Arts candidate at the California State University, East Bay (formerly Hayward).
