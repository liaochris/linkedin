Rajesh Sharma’s Summary

Private Equity Professional & Specialist at starting & building new, high growth businesses

"Value unlocking" is far more important than "Value creation".......without "unlocking", "creation" has no meaning !

Started career as an entrepreneur at a very young age, setting up ventures in Media & Advertising, Education, Textiles & Tea.

Worked across industries - Private Equity, Consulting, Research & Analytics, Media & Advertising, Information & Content, Internet/IT/ITES, Education & FMCG.

Helped companies like The Times of India Group (BCCL), CRISIL (S&P), KOTAK MAHINDRA, NIIT & BAG Films set up new, innovative and high growth ventures.

An economist by education (Delhi School of Economics), is trained in Private Equity (ISB), Capital Markets (Jamnalal Bajaj & BSE), Brand & Communication (ISB & BVB).

In latest role at Times Private Treaties, is helping companies grow their business & brands through strategic Private Equity investments. With investments in over 250 companies, TPT is the largest PE fund in terms of number of investments. Over the last 4 years we have successfully helped create new brands & businesses........Future Group(Pantaloons, Big Bazaar, Kishore Biyani), MCX, Zicom, Videocon, Provogue, Percept Picture Company, Gitanjali, Wockhardt, Thyrocare, Mahindra Holidays etc

As business consultant helped blue chip cos like Hyundai, Mitsubishi, Maruti, IOC, Emaar MGF etc, across sectors, shape up their growth and expansion strategies.


Is credited with:

- building India’s first Internet enabled Information & Research services business "www.indiainformer.com" much before the dotcom boom

- building overseas Instructional Design/CBT business, much before the world understood it.

- helping CRISIL(Now S&P) grow beyond the ratings business into new businesses - Business Consulting, Research & Information Services, Real Times News Wire, Mutual Fund & Fixed Income Analytics.
