Joshua Ellis’s Summary

Recently appointed as Director of new CRM and Web Services unit within Outreach Marketing / Communications / Recruitment / Adult Learner Enrollment Services at the Pennsylvania State University. Current focus is implementation of CRM and related software systems, and integration of new CRM team with existing Web Services team. In general terms, the management and oversight of CRM, online / e-mail marketing solutions, and custom Web development for higher education.

What I hope to offer in my daily work:
* The ability to explain complex issues to individuals of varying technical skill at all levels of the organization.
* Enough comfort with public speaking to present in the front of a boardroom, classroom, or conference.
* The natural curiosity and strategic thinking ability to stay current with the evolving technical needs of CRM and online marketing.
* The tact, diplomacy, and interpersonal communication skill to work within a campus community (faculty, students, staff, and administration).
* The knowledge and skill to interact competently with programmers, writers, marketing strategists, editors, designers, directors of marketing, and technical staff on a daily basis.
* The project management, change management, and staff management experience to work effectively with senior leadership and direct reports in a team environment.
* The integrity to speak up when something is wrong, and to admit when I don’t know something.

Joshua Ellis’s Specialties:
