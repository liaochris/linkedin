Barbara Gilbert’s Summary

Using the basic principles of design, I transform ordinary spaces into must-see places. With my exceptional organizational and design skills, I am able to take a job from concept to completion, including plans, procurement, fabrication and installation. Past projects include residentail homes, medical offices, club houses & models for apartments, senior living centers, offices and restaurants.

I am a Certified Dewey Color Specialist who uses color to help employees be more productive at work and I enable residents to achieve balance in their homes.
