Web Smith’s Summary

Web development and social media have been integral to my professional development. I have used these acquired skills while completing my undergraduate degree and gaining invaluable experience in the private sector. Just recently, I applied them to my current entrepreneurial venture with Jeremy Thiel, founder and owner of CrossFit Central.

SICFIT is a social media site for fitness enthusiasts of a rare-breed. The CrossFit, functional, sports performance, military training, evolutionary fitness, and elite fitness wedges of the multi-billion dollar fitness market is our collective niche. SICFIT hopes to become a daily stop for those that love the real world application of the fitness they seek. With media content, blogs and professional photography, there is never a dull moment on this site.

Garage Gym Blueprint is a business development and marketing arm to assist small business owners in their pursuit of revenue growth and sustainability. Jeremy Thiel leads these seminars while I focus on the Web 2.0/Social Networking future of business.

My late arrival to LinkedIn is inexcusable.
