John Karnay’s Summary

I am a passionate, creative and focused video game professional that understands how to optimize the results of any project I am on.

I have acquired over seven years of Production, QA and Project Management experience working in the software and video game industries.

Working as both a consultant and a staff Producer/QA Supervisor at Vogster Entertainment, I act as a liaison to multiple production departments and as a communication hub for our international development team in Kiev.

At my former employer, Acclaim Entertainment I was a project lead on over twenty high profile console and PC titles in my 4 years with the company. I oversaw these titles through their full test cycles on a variety of platforms and formats.

At Intelli-Check, I led teams of international testers, programmers and in house programmers on the development of security software and hardware used by the United States Government and Homeland Security. I acted as a direct adviser and liaison to the VP of Sales and corporate officers. I managed the activity and test goals of an international Quality Assurance team.

I have also consulted for several major names in the gaming industry such as SOE, Wizards of the Coast and Hero Games. I have also proven myself as a freelance author, copy editor, and technical editor for several gaming magazines, publishers and websites.
