John Quinn’s Summary

TWO PROMAX INTERNATIONAL Gold Medallion Awards for “Most Innovative Radio Promotion,” “Outstanding Consumer Television Promotion” two consecutive Achievement In Radio (A.I.R.) awards for “Best Audience Promotion,” and two Capital Region PSA Emmy nominations and 3 Telly Awards for video production and an award from the National Assoc. of Black Journalists, St. Louis and TelevisionWeek magazine’s 2008 Marketing Maverick award are but a few of the accolades attributed to John Quinn’s extensive Marketing Programming and Public Relations career. In 1998, he produced a long-form video presentation for a White House Summit on Retirement Savings John also serves on the PSA advisor committee for the National American Red Cross.

John Quinn, a 30-year media veteran, presently manages Public Affairs Marketing for ABC affiliate WJLA-TV, Washington, DC. Before joining the station in July of 1995, he enjoyed a successful first-try as a political campaign manager in St. Louis and served as Director of Advertising and Promotion for WRQX, the Disney/ABC owned and operated FM Radio property serving our Nation’s Capital. John is presently working on public awareness campaigns for the Embassies of Germany, South Korea, Taiwan, The Netherlands Kazakhstan and others He has received numerous citations for his involvement with local and national organizations. John has served as a judge for the Emmy Awards, National Association of Women in Television & Film Imagemakers, The National Association of Broadcasters’ Crystal Awards and is an active member of the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences and has been a 2009 Society for New Communications Research Fellow. The Society for New Communications Research is a global nonprofit 501(c)(3) research and education foundation and think tank focused on the advanced study of the latest developments in new media and communications, and their effect on traditional media and business models, communications, culture and society.

John Quinn’s Specialties:

International and Public Affairs Marketing, Cause–Related Marketing, New Business Development, Public Relations, Promotion & Synergy, Television, Radio and Print Creative and non-linear video editing. Volunteer Maryland for Obama.
