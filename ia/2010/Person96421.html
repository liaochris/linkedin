Jerrold Bartholomew’s Summary

I serve clients throughout the state of Michigan in all areas of elder law and estate planning, including asset protection, Medicaid planning, life care planning, veterans' benefits, estate planning, probate, trust administration and special needs planning.

My firm is client-focused with cutting edge use of technology to allow superior access, collaboration, portability and results.

I am also the author of the popular blog www.MichiganElderLaw.info.

Jerrold Bartholomew’s Specialties:

Asset protection, Medicaid planning, and veterans' benefits qualification are niche areas of the law that require fairly exclusive focus due to the constantly changing laws and complex nature of the planning. I concentrate my practice on these areas so that I am able to remain on the front lines in determining the solutions that will work in Michigan and best solve my clients' problems.
