Victor Fetter’s Summary

Victor Fetter is an internationally experienced Chief Information Officer (CIO) who is known for driving the transformation of IT within Fortune 500 organizations.

As a Vice President at Dell, he currently leads the IT efforts supporting the two largest business units: Large Enterprise and Public. He also manages all of Dell’s eCommerce capability (e.g., Dell.com) and tools enabling sales effectiveness (e.g., SalesForce.com). He recently built an innovative and connected Enterprise Architecture organization that encompasses Business Architecture, Solutions Architecture, Information Architecture and the reusable services, SOA, and Integration stack. This focus, combined with efforts to map and reengineer business processes, has enabled the transformation of Dell and the build out of an agile technical platform that utilizes leading edge IT capabilities.

Over the years he has served as the primary architect of numerous transformation programs that have enabled growth while reducing overall IT investment. With over 20 years of experience, he has had the opportunity to deliver business value for global financial institutions, professional services, eCommerce and manufacturing/supply chain organizations. He understands how to utilize technology as a competitive advantage and has applied this knowledge to organizations in the context of strategic program development, ERP implementations, and data center simplification and migration programs to only name a few. He is an expert in the design, staffing, and leadership of world-class IT organizations that deliver results.

In his various CIO roles, as a Board Member and as a business executive he has continually demonstrated an ability to interpret business and market conditions. He has proven many times over his ability to use his analytical, leadership and strategic insight to develop and implement transformative strategies geared toward new market offers, business change and shareholder value.

Victor Fetter’s Specialties:

Served as Chief Information Officer (CIO), Enterprise Architect, Consultant, Business Leader, and Board Member. Is results oriented, operationally minded and has significant global, cross-industry experience.
