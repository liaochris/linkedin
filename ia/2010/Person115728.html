Kevin Machayya’s Summary

Business development focused on Microsoft channel partners selling and implementing Microsoft Dynamics financial management, supply chain management, and customer relationship management solutions.

Effective business management software (ERP and CRM) is key to timely and insightful business decisions. But customers won't use it if it is complicated or too unfamiliar. Microsoft Dynamics works like and with Microsoft software customers already use. So they adopt quickly and work efficiently.

These solutions include - Microsoft Dynamics AX (Axapta), Microsoft Dynamics GP (Great Plains), Microsoft Dynamics NAV (Navision), Microsoft Dynamics SL (Solomon), and Microsoft Dynamics CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

Survey and economic data has indicated the ability to find, hire and retain sales and technical resources continues to be a very important and cost consuming issue for Microsoft partners as they look to grow their businesses. Microsoft understands these concerns, and has developed a variety of tools, resources, and programs (such as Microsoft Students to Business) to assist partners.
