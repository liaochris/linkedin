Amanda Styron’s Summary

My favorite thing to do is to take a set of ideas and build them into a functioning entity whether it be a program, business or just a great party. It must be in the blood; I come from an family of entrepreneurs and have been starting projects my entire life. In college I earned a degree in business and founded a non-profit social entrepreneurial incubator with a group of friends. Since then I’ve worked with many teams to build projects large and small.

Amanda Styron’s Specialties:

I specialize in program development, marketing, strategic planning, and event production. My passions are the creative process, personal development, and community building, and I come with a knack for strategy that shapes just about everything I do.
