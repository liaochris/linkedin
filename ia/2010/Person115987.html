Kevin Li’s Summary

At ThinkVine, I function in a hybrid role and lead efforts spanning across:
- Corporate strategy: strategic partnerships, new product development, product marketing
- Business development: pre-sales analytical advisory, RFP responses
- Client engagement: project delivery, analytical oversight, client consulting

Previously, I was a quantitative marketing consultant dedicated to various P&G categories focusing on volume forecasting, concept optimization and consumer insights within Nielsen BASES.

I possess strong business acumen coupled with in-depth experience in marketing analytics and technology. I am a trusted adviser to my clients on key issues such as volume attribution/ROI (trade, mass media, social media, digital, etc.), marketing plan optimization, new product innovation, growth strategy, competitive impact, forecasting, etc.

I also have a strong background in designing scalable web-based applications and specialize in the business development and strategic growth of technology start-ups. Prior projects include Symphony Organizer (a web-based personnel management system for fine arts organizations) and IdeaBounce.com (a web-based entrepreneurship portal built for the Olin School of Business).
