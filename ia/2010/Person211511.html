Victoria Kamm’s Summary

Visionary, advisor, educator, speaker and geek.

I have run businesses with employees and inventory and I have been a solopreneur. My companies have been for profit and not for profit. I've been a regular employee and a contract employee for other businesses.

Bringing a working knowledge of the joys and obstacles of running a variety of businesses sets me apart from many social media and search engine consultants. I know every owner needs a return on investment of each marketing activity and I work with them to develop a strategy so that happens.

I love to consider the pros and cons of adapting technology for each client. One may be a perfect candidate for Google Wave, one should devote 70% of resources to a Facebook Fan Page, one should invest in iPads for greater productivity at meetings.

I am also aware of the dark side of social media and technology. The lack of privacy, employee engagement both negative and positive, the new HITECH/HIPAA regulations are examples of the tremendous obstacles that face businesses in our connected world. I gather resources to help clients address those obstacles before they become unmanageable.

My greatest strength is the ability to look at different pieces of a client project and develop a strategy and tactics that result in more customers, more sales, more profit. And at the end of the end of the day that’s all that really matters.
