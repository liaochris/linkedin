Tracey McTiernan’s Summary

R&D Partners, a division of CVPartners, Inc., operates on a set of values focused on long term customer relationships based on trust, consistency in our process and a culture of excellence and teamwork. We are proud to have achieved recognition for a number of years by customers, colleagues and employees as "Best Place to Work" (private companies), "Top 100 Women Owned Businesses" and "Top 100 Fastest Growing Private Companies".

R&D Partners is a specialty Staffing Firm with a focus on recruiting exceptional Scientific, Clinical Research and Regulatory Affairs candidates for our Pharmaceutical, Biotech and Medical Device clients. Our Account Management Team has well over 100 years of combined experience successfully working with our clients on exclusive, retained and contingent searches.

R&D Partners is focused on identifying and placing consultants and permanent professionals in the following areas:

• Scientific
• Clinical Research
• Regulatory Affairs
• Compliance, Validation and QA
