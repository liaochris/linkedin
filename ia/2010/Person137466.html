Matthew Nation’s Summary

A conscientious and detail oriented young designer keen to develop in a fast-paced commercial environment. He is ambitious and focused, with high standards and expectations of himself and those he interacts with. Sets progressive goals, showing strong initiative and the ability to adapt to new situations with ease. Benefits creatively and personally from having lived in London and New York, and is nurturing a design signature influenced by vintage American military and sports wear, classic British tailoring and transatlantic post-war youth culture.

Matthew Nation’s Specialties:

Market and concept research, Adapting to brand DNAs, Designing from vintage references, interpersonal and written communication, Knowledge of garment construction and fabric properties, Mac and Windows literate. Particularly interested in men's tops and outerwear.
