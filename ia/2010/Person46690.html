Dane Sanders’s Summary

I believe the job of marketing is to increase revenue opportunities - period. It can be cool...it can be buzz worthy...it can build a brand. But what truly matters most is whether it's effective in actually making someone somewhere commit to buying what a company is selling, and increasing that company's or its client's revenue opportunity.

Marketing that builds a company's brand and has people "talking" about that company's product is undoubtedly great, but is trumped more so by marketing that actually builds a company's revenue and has people "buying" that company's product.
