Don Zagorski’s Summary

Telecommunications Executive and Industry Leader- During the past 27 years I have had the opportunity to work in an industry through its infancy to now competing with major telecommunication companies for multiple products and services. I have enjoyed Professional growth through such positions as COO, Regional Vice President, Regional General Manager, General Manager, Sales Manager, Chief Technology Manager, and was single recipient of the Mediacom Chairman Award in 2000. I was recently was elected Chairman of the Board of the Cable Television Association of Georgia in recognition of my many accomplished works on the state and local level for the telecommunications industry.
