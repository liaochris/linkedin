David Hulme’s Summary

David Hulme holds a doctorate in International Relations from the University of Southern California (USC) with an emphasis on the Middle East. He has also studied theology, psychology and philosophy. He is the author of "Identity, Ideology and the Jerusalem Question" (Palgrave 2006) and a contributor to "What Makes Us Human?" by Charles Pasternak (ed) (Oneworld 2007). David Hulme is publisher of the quarterly journal, Vision, president of Vision Media Productions and chairman of Vision Foundation International. In Fall 2008 he taught Politics of the Middle East in the Political Science Dept at USC and with the students created the website middleeastpolitics.net. In Summer 2009 he was at work filming Messiahs! Rulers and the Role of Religion in Europe.
