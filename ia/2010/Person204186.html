Tim Frank’s Summary

Vice President

A co-founder of Inergex, Tim currently provides oversight for one of Inergex’s largest enterprise clients. Tim also works closely with the Inergex organization to enhance and develop solutions that we deliver for our clients. As part of this role, Tim works with the Inergex Sales and Marketing teams to help position and market services and products to existing and potential clients.
Tim has spent over 21 years in the information technology industry. Prior to joining Inergex, Tim gained technology experience in leadership and consulting roles, including his previous position as a national Managing Consultant for Inacom.

In Tim's own words:

Helping customers is what really gets me going. I love getting in front of customers, understanding the issues they face, figuring out what we can do and how we can apply our services to help them get to the next level. That’s what it’s all about for me.

I spend a lot of time studying market research and reports to determine current “hot button” issues for today’s marketplace.
I have spent the past year working closely with one of our largest customers to provide oversight and management to several critical areas in their IT organization. It continues to be an excellent experience working with the business and leadership teams to help enable the overall strategy of the organization.

Did you know? I played in a rock band for years—bass and guitar—playing 1970s and 80s music. We played in local bars and even made some recordings. It was a lot of fun but I didn’t want to be a starving artist all my life, so I got into the technology industry.

I am in my 3rd year of Tae Kwon Do and continue to work at it three to four hours a week and really like the discipline, focus, and exercise. It also gives me an additional opportunity to spend some quality time with my son who is also very active in Tae Kwon Do.
