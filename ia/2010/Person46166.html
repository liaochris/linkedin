Damien Basile’s Summary

I empower businesses to be the best they can be in communications. I help individuals and companies implement holistic brand strategies. My blog acts as the voice for many of my brand strategy beliefs that I implement for my clients. I speak at engagements which is just another vehicle to convey my passion for communications and social media.

My past experience in many areas of design, branding and communications enables me to give a fuller assessment of a clients whole strategy and implementation from many angles. The brand strategy model that I use for my clients incorporates tracking, monitoring, reacting, extending brand recognition, customer servicing, marketing, advertising and building relationship equity. I make sure social media is in alignment with their core values.

Social networking has taught me the values of having multi-channel brand extensions that power connections from Facebook, Twitter & Linkedin to good old-fashioned email and the telephone.
