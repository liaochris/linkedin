Larayne Decoeur’s Summary

Content Creator. Communicator. Marketer.

I Create Engagement: I am an award-winning executive producer who leads the team in creating original content with strong story-telling elements and effective messaging for your wide or niche audience. I bring my mad skills in production, marketing know-how and showbiz whiz to every product or project I accept to ensure the best possible outcome. Let's bring the magic now.

I am involved in product roll-out, focus groups, targeted advertising and social media marketing.
