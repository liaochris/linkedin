Cary Bran’s Summary

I am a senior business development manager and technologist for Cisco Systems Voice Technology Group. My current responsibilities include: helping shape the vision and strategy for Unified Communications and Collaboration at Cisco and technical and business diligence for Cisco acquisitions.

I am a passionate technologist and have over a decade of experience architecting and developing unified communications software solutions and leading development teams. Before joining Cisco in 2001, I developed software for ActiveVoice, a Seattle-based pioneer of unified communications, and was co-founder of a small software development consulting business. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Seattle University and an MBA the Foster School of Business at the University of Washington.
