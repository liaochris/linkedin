Sonja Jacob’s Summary

Sonja is a talented copywriter, online video producer, creative strategist and founder of The Cultivated Word LLC. Sonja creates content that inspires people and achieves measurable business results.

Passionate about the creative process, Sonja approaches her work from a multi-disciplinary perspective, drawing on her extensive background in business writing, marketing and sociology. She holds a joint M.A. in Sociology and Women's & Gender Studies from Brandeis University.

Sonja produces fresh and compelling work that transcends the traditional boundaries of advertising and marketing. She recently wrote and produced a video ad campaign for Grasshopper, which has received hundreds of thousands of views on YouTube, and in late 2009, was nominated for a Mashable Open Web Award. The video and the campaign were also named to Marketing Sherpa's Viral Hall of Fame 2009.

As founder of The Cultivated World, Sonja works with agencies, web designers, start-ups, entrepreneurs, small businesses and large companies. Skilled at balancing both the business and creative objectives for every project she takes on, she's proven time and again to be a valuable and dynamic team player committed to producing outstanding work.

Learn more at: www.thecultivatedword.com.
