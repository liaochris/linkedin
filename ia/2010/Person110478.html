Karen Breedlove’s Summary

My experience has been broad based. I have several years of overall experience which includes negotiating and drafting oil and gas land contracts, conducting daily administration of said contracts, populating oil and gas lease and contract databases, paying delay rentals and other required lease maintenance payments, preparing division orders and performing title curative work. I have been involved in performing due diligence and preparing and filing conveyance documents for acquisitions and divestitures. My experience has been concentrated in Texas and Louisiana Gulf Coast and in the Gulf of Mexico federal waters; however, I have experience in the Permian Basin and Mid-Continent areas as well. My previous experience has afforded me the opportunity to have worked closely with geological and engineering staff members which has led to obtaining experience in drafting contracts for the operations and production areas of the business as well.
