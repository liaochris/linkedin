Gregory Coleman’s Summary

As a co-founder and Managing Director, Mr. Coleman brings more than 15 years of management expertise to the C.Y. Strategy team. He has extensive experience leading teams through complex and rapidly changing situations and has advised early-stage, mid-stage, and Fortune 100 companies in a number of critical areas, including strategy, management, operations, business development, and marketing.
