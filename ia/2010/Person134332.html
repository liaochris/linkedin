Mark Olson’s Specialties:

Mark Olson’s Experience

  • President/Operations Manager

    KSS Print

    (Printing industry)

    June 2009 — Present (11 months)

    KSS Print grew out of a desire to connect businesses of all sizes, with all levels of printing needs with high-quality printing at remarkable prices. We do this by enlisting consumers to become the local KSS contacts in their area with the surrounding businesses. We offer very substantial commissions to our local contacts and once we get a new printing client, we rarely lose them, because our prices are so amazing.

  • Owner/President

    Totally Direct Television, LLC, The Quiet DRTV Hit Maker

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2004 — Present (6 years )

    We write, produce, direct and edit television commercials called direct response, or DRTV commercials. The 60,90 and 120 second versions are "spots" and the These are the 2 minute spots and the half-hour is the infomercial. Our most current success is Smooth Away and was the #1 - 2 minute DRTV spot in the country for over a year, see the spot at www.GetSmoothAway.com
    We also, consult, manage and source funding for projects and products that meet the requirements of a successful DRTV product.
