    Program and Project Management duties for control systems, software, and embedded firmware development for the Energy Solutions Group (ESG).

    ESG works with utility and power providers to use the A123 patented Nanophosphate™ technology to deliver high-power solutions for grid stabilization with a high power to energy ratio. The Hybrid Ancillary Power Unit (Hybrid-APU™) can be rapidly deployed worldwide to hybridize a power plant and provide Frequency Regulation and Synchronous Reserve (Spinning Reserve or Standby Reserve Capacity) services. Frequency regulation, a nearly net zero energy service, and Synchronous Reserve, an emergency contingency capacity provision, are traditionally provided by power plants which keep a portion of their capacity on reserve for these purposes.

    The response time of the Hybrid-APU is superior to how the grid operates today. The Hybrid-APU can respond in milliseconds compared to power plants which can take minutes to respond to a call to vary their power output. Using a Hybrid-APU frees up plant capacity reserves that are typically set aside for ancillary services, to enable the utility to focus on the primary function of baseload electricity generation.
