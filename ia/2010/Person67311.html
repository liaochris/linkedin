Erroin Martin’s Summary

The Von Gehr Consulting Group, LLC, was founded to provide business coaching, consulting, and other services to companies both large and small. The goal is to have my clients be passionate about their business and reach the unachievable.


I draw upon a diverse leadership background and international experience to provide value and world-class services to my clients. I have worked with government agencies, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, natural resources, medical device manufacturing, consumer products, and biotech companies as a coach, trainer, and sales team mentor.
