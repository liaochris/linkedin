Paul Kim’s Summary

I’m a passionate entrepreneur with a proven track record of creating a sustainable concept, developing a strong vision, raising VC funding, building talented teams, running a company, signing key deals with partners & customers, and negotiating a successful liquidity event for investors and employees. Currently, I'm building my second venture-funded startup company. Prior to this, as the founding CEO of BilltoMobile (Danal, Inc.), I created the first-ever VC-funded spinout company from a public Korean company by raising $9.5 million in VC and strategic investment. Subsequently, my team and I successfully signed the first-ever eCommerce-focused mobile billing/payments solution with Verizon Wireless in May 2009 to fix the broken mobile billing ecosystem, beating out all established competitors (both public and private) in the process. I also played a pivotal role in selling the controlling interests of BilltoMobile to Danal Co., Ltd. for a generous premium after extensive negotiations that concluded in February 2010.

Throughout 12 years of working in various capacities as an entrepreneur and at technology and consulting firms, I’ve developed a hands-on leadership style that focuses on creativity and innovation, no-nonsense hard work, and delivering unequivocal results. A leader cannot sit back and expect others to be self-motivated and achieve success, especially at startups; rather, he must be the life-giving force of the company who provides clear direction while inspiring employees to be passionate and motivated throughout the inevitable ups and downs of the company. I believe in always moving a team towards a shared and clearly articulated vision, being smarter and more flexible than competitors while outworking them, rewarding people generously for outstanding work, and delivering fantastic results that exceed investors’ and employees’ expectations. I refuse to accept status quo or mediocrity, and I strive to build teams that will not tolerate them either.
