Avery Cohen’s Summary

At Metrist Partners, I work with Marketers (CMO, Director of e-Commerce) and business owners to optimizie ROI from Internet Marketing. We set monthly goals for revenue, lead generation and engagement metrics. Then we help create initiatives that will meet or exceed those goals.

Our staff of analysts creatively turns marketing data into actionable information. We use web analytics as the basis for web site optimization and to guide Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM or pay-per-click campaigns), and Email campaigns.

We now offer a series of four hands-on workshops on Internet Marketing:
1. Setting Goals and tracking results with Google Analytics
2. Search Engine Optimization that Works for You
3. Email Newsletters that Add Value
4. Social Networking that Builds Your Customer Base

More information at the web site http://metristpartners.com/workshops-and-seminars
