Anne Galivan’s Summary

I have been home-educating my children since 1990. As I gained experience as a home-schooler I began writing articles for our state home-school publication and during the 1990's I was published several times in a nationally recognized home-education magazine. I was also involved for several years teaching home-school workshops for a local support group.

As of May 3rd, 2010, I have launched my own home-schooling website (www.homeschooling911.com) devoted to helping prospective and current home-educators achieve their home-schooling goals.
