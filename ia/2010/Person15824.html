Ali Hechmi Raddaoui’s Summary

English language teaching: I have taught English at secondary and higher education levels since 1981, helped with exam specs, administration, and correction.

Educational Management: with select committees, I have analyzed the curricula for freshmen students, undergraduate and graduate students with special emphasis on English language, research, and teaching pedagogy.

Online Learning: for the past three years, I have seen my focus move from English curriculum subjects in general to course delivery via virtual learning environments.

Research: in addition to translation from and into Arabic and English and research supervision at MA and Ph.D. levels, I have published a number of research articles in the field of English language teaching and learning and educational planning.

Management and expertise: I have been involved formally and informally in various management and strategic decisions in higher education.

International representation and partnerships: I have been representing the Virtual University of Tunis in a number of Euro-Med conferences in relation to establishing a Euro-Med Higher Education area.

Business orientation: Given the current shift toward online learning, I now see myself in position of starting an academic and training content production firm. I have the pedagogical, theoretical, practical, and technological knowledge and expertise, and I am looking for associates in this field.

Ali Hechmi Raddaoui’s Specialties:

Oral and written representation; public speaking; consensus building and mediation, strategic planning, localization and translation.
