Arun Manansingh’s Summary

Chief Information Officer with over 13 years of experience leveraging technology to implement business strategic goals and objectives. Experienced in international finance and banking and construction industries. My extensive knowledge includes the entire range of information technology functions, software applications development, infrastructure, operations administration, project management methodologies and business analysis. With my proven ability of working with senior executives I have defined and planned the organizations’ strategic technology strategy and then executed the operational plan to meet the business objectives. I effectively communicate among department managers and executive management by utilizing my written, verbal, and interpersonal skills to translate technical concepts into language that business stakeholders understand and build consensus to ensure that all projects are clearly defined. I focus on managing and leveraging technologies in a cost effective manner to meet and exceed the company’s goals and objectives. I am a change agent and a leader with a proven track record of harnessing technology enabled solutions to enhance business performance and competitiveness.
