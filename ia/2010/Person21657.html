Benjamin Abrams’s Summary

My professional objective is to work as a new media specialist with a strong focus on social media marketing and interactive marketing communications. I aim to help organizations develop and achieve their marketing objectives using creative new media strategies.

Benjamin Abrams’s Specialties:

I specialize in identifying social media trends and new media tools, and understanding how to utilize them to achieve organizational goals. At Boston University, my graduate work focused on examinations of how Internet tools and platforms transform media and communications models. As part of my studies, I researched the future of traditional and social media, including the latest developments in micro-blogging, e-marketing, Wikis, and Web 2.0 applications.
