Chad Roberts’s Summary

The majority of my career has been spent with the same company (Piedmont Plastics) where I have served in the Marketing Department, as the E-Commerce Marketing Manager, Training Manager, Marketing Manager, and as the Technology Development Manager. I excel at process development, search engine optimization, strategic planning, training, and just about anything to do with a computer.

I have been married since 2001 to an exceptionally patient woman, and have a Bassett Hound named “Chance.” My interests vary and include live music, outdoor activities, and volunteering for the local chapter of the Red Cross. I am an avid proponent of the Environmental Business movement and support social responsibility projects for my current employer.

Eventually, I would like to start a consulting business that will provide assistance in Financing, Accounting, Marketing, Information Technology, Operations, and Strategic Planning for small/ medium sized companies in the Charlotte area.

Chad Roberts’s Specialties:

I have a variety of specialties that cover a wide range of disciplines including Marketing, IT, and Strategic Planning. My most recent roles have allowed me to develop skills in: Targeted Marketing Programs (Direct mail and Bulk email), Competitive Analysis, Market Research, Graphic Design, Web Development, Organic SEO (Search Engine Optimization), as well as Strategy Implementation and Development.
