Laurel Bailey’s Summary

As consultants to small to mid-sized customer service-oriented businesses, our executive experience is available for unique consulting opportunities that allow us to share our knowledge and perspective on projects of short to medium duration (1-6 months). My partner, Ron Bailey, has a broad technical and project management background, while mine is more oriented to strategic planning, procedures, and soft-skills.

Our strengths are analysis, planning, IT solutions, customer service strategies and procedures, marketing and communications, social networking, and career coaching.

We have extensive experience in help desk design and implementation, creation of an award-winning technology brand for a worldwide hotel corporation, and design, development and maintenance of mission critical information systems. As a team, we offer a dynamic combination of hi-tech and hi-touch skills that can be applied to finding the best, most cost effective solutions for your business.
