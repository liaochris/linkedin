Jeffrey Ghannam’s Summary

Jeffrey Ghannam is a senior level manager with vast experience in law, media, communications and international development in service to the private sector, nonprofit, government and international donor-funded context, including:

Corporate legal affairs; media law, protecting reputation interests, negotiations, contracts.

Media development: Identifying business opportunities, program design, evaluations, assessments, training, capacity building;

Strategic communications: internal and external media strategies and implementation, using new media to engage stakeholders;

Team leadership, entrepreneurial vision.

Before international development, he was a veteran reporter, writer, and editor with many years of experience handling major breaking news plus national and foreign reporting with expertise in legal affairs. He is a former staff writer and editor at the Detroit Free Press and former legal affairs writer at the American Bar Association Journal, Chicago. He has also served as a contributor to The Boston Globe from Detroit, The New York Times in Washington, D.C., and Time magazine in Cairo, Egypt.

Additional International Development Experience:

In spring 2009, he served as a Senior Media Development Expert in a mid-term evaluation of the five-year $15.5 million Media Development Program for USAID/Egypt. He also conceived and directed innovative media strategies for USAID using new media platforms for projects in Albania and Afghanistan.

Awarded a John S. and James L. Knight Foundation International Press Fellowship to advance media training programs in the Middle East and North Africa.

Served as an editor/trainer for an investigative reporting project in Beirut, Lebanon for the U.S. Agency for International Development's Office of Transition Initiatives.

Conducted media training for the National Democratic Institute in Jordan and Morocco.

Evaluated the needs for a restructuring of Qatar Broadcasting for the Voice of America.

Jeffrey Ghannam’s Specialties:

Lawyer, media development and strategic communications expert, writer, editor, educator and trainer. Media law, corporate legal affairs, international law, intellectual property, marketing, brand identities. Field experience in the Middle East, North Africa and Gulf regions identifying business trends and opportunities. Effective team builder and change agent. Public speaker and TV commentator.
