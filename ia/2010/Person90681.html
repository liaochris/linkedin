Jason (Jinxiang) Lu’s Summary

A seasoned C++ Software Engineer on Multi-thread, High Performance, Low Latency, High Throughput, High Scalability, High Availability and Low Maintenance Stock and Derivatives Trading Platform supporting tens of thousands of clients at the same time from scratch.

Over the years I've stepped into every corner of software development, working directly with hardware for embedded systems, exposed directly in OS core, developing kernel device drivers as well as designing and implementing high level client sever system and GUI applications. Comprehensive experience offers me insights into the design and implementation of mission critical systems.

As an experienced system architect specializing in network based client server systems from ground up, I have designed and developed many network systems. Two most recent ones are stock and option trading platforms. one for retail brokerage and one for prop trading company. I have tamed tremendous feed volume from OPRA with minimal gaps, conquered the issue for one server to support more than 10,000 clients to save expensive rack spaces, invented versatile yet highly optimized protocol and mechanisms to bring the US market data feed to Asia with speed exceeding other trading platforms.

I have led big teams, small teams as well as worked independently as one man troop in huge company and small start-ups. As financial companies typically have smaller but more expensive software developers, independent work from scratch is especially important. I have surprised my managers by designing and implementing the whole system with high quality all by my own from ground zero in such a short period that a team of 3 working closely together can normally achieve.

A long time stock and option trader, I've developed in-depth knowledge of trading and easily understand not just what the trader needs but why the trader needs it. When interest, technology and domain knowledge rhyme together, a powerful system is within the reach.
