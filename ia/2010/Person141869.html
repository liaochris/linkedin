Micah Wright’s Summary

Micah Wright has written twenty five videogames, three feature films, hours upon hours of aired television, three books of political commentary, four graphic novels, and a musical comedy based on the 1980's film "Red Dawn."

Before moving into film and games, Micah worked in the grinding death mills of animation, where his episodes of Nickelodeon’s "The Angry Beavers" were nominated for a Daytime Emmy and an Annie award. Micah also created, wrote and produced Nickelodeon’s first action-adventure show, Constant Payne.

Micah's books of war propaganda posters, "YOU Back the Attack: WE'LL Bomb Who We Want," "If You're Not A Terrorist, Then Stop Asking Questions" and "Surveillance Means Security!" are available now at Amazon.com. His satirical political art has appeared in The New York Times, Sunday Guardian UK, The Christian Science Monitor, The Boston Globe, the New Internationalist, the New Statesman, The Washington Post (twice!), and on the Fox News Channel (much to their chagrin).

Micah's Mature Readers comic "Stormwatch: Team Achilles," ran for 23 issues to high critical praise, and was collected into three trade paperbacks. "Duster," Micah's new graphic novel, is currently in production and will come out in mid 2011.

Micah is currently working on film, videogame and graphic novel projects, but is always available for odd jobs and light yardwork, provided you promise to give him a ride back to the Home Depot at the end of the day.

Micah Wright’s Specialties:

Development of Intellectual Property for videogames, film, & television. Scriptwriting. Member of the Writer's Guild of America (West).
