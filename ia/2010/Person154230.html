Nick Ryan’s Summary

Nick is Co-founder and a Director of Nativ (http://nativ.tv). Nativ is a specialist consultancy providing expert strategy and technology solutions to the convergent broadcast, broadband and mobile sectors.

Previously Nick worked at Tripledash where he led interactive TV and broadband development. Prior to this, he worked at Razorfish, UK. He has a strong technical background, having developed embedded solutions in the mobile, mining and medical industries. He has published papers on digital signal processing
