Guy Borgford’s Summary

As 2011 approaches, we have to ask ourselves what are we doing as marketers to truly capitalize on new technology and communication platforms? Is our communication a one-way monologue being broadcast to the masses, or are we truly embracing the power of conversational branding and marketing? Only by looking within can we honestly answer those questions.

With over 17 years experience in advertising and marketing on both the creative and account side and across client, agency and publisher worlds, I've worked with both Fortune 500 brands and scrappy start-ups. And I believe we've entered a new era of brand conversation that's changing the game.

My channel expertise, combined with extensive experience in B2B, B2C, digital, mobile, print, radio, experiential and social media, delivers a unique, holistic perspective on our world of marketing communication, across multiple product categories.

As an artist and designer, this core strategic sensibility and business acumen is further differentiated with a refined eye to visual communication, while years of experience in the music, entertainment and casual games verticals delivers rich insights into digital branding and sponsorship initiatives.

I'm one to mix humor with often unconventional approaches to marketing and communication challenges. Marketing should be fun. It's why I love what I do. I'm an entrepreneur at heart and thrive in a fast-paced environment that breaks ground at the speed of light.

Please don't hesitate to send me a message now - I'm eager to discuss your marketing and business development challenges and how I can help.
