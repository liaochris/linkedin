Shali Nguyen’s Summary

As Social Network Designer at Ning, Shali is responsible for the design and development of social networks for Ning’s strategic partners, including Enrique Iglesias, Taylor Hicks, the San Francisco Symphony and VA gubernatorial candidate Bob McDonnell. She started her career as a web designer at 3marketeers Advertising where she worked with clients including Cisco, Teneros and UCSF. She also spent time at ad agency Mckinney in N.C. working on the Virgin Atlantic, Qwest, and NBC accounts. Shali is currently the VP of Communications and New Media for AAF Silicon Valley, as well as a member of the Interaction Design Association (IxDA SF), the American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA), and an alumnus of the Multicultural Advertising Intern Program (MAIP). Shali holds a B.S. in Business Administration/Marketing and a graphic design minor from University of the Pacific in Stockton, Calif.
