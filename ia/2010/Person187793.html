Sharon Graham’s Summary

Sharon Graham is Canada's Career Strategist.

A recognized expert in resume, interview, and career strategy, Sharon has elevated industry procedures and practices. She holds simultaneous roles as president and principal consultant at Graham Management Group, executive director of Career Professionals of Canada, and best selling Canadian author.

Sharon assists clients across North America and worldwide through Graham Management Group, the leading Canadian firm for $100k+ professionals.

An industry pioneer, Sharon authored the first fully Canadian content book on resume writing. The Best Canadian Resumes Series is a one-of-a-kind resource supporting practitioners and job seekers nationwide.

A leader in the career industry, Sharon founded the Canadian association for practitioners, Career Professionals of Canada. A firm believer in advancing the field, Sharon has delivered a number of cutting-edge innovations including formal credentialing and awards programs.

Sharon’s own credentials and designations include:
* Master Certified Resume Strategist (MCRS)
* Master Certified Interview Strategist (MCIS)
* Master Certified Career Strategist (MCCS)
* Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW)
* Certified Employment Interview Professional (CEIP)

Sharon is an active member of:
* Career Professionals of Canada
* Career Masters Institute
* Human Resources Professionals Association of Ontario
* Professional Association of Resume Writers and Career Coaches

Sharon is regularly featured as an expert in leading Canadian media including Report on Business Television, the Globe and Mail, the National Post, and the Toronto Star.

Sharon has built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and integrity by exceeding her clients’ needs and expectations. Her business has grown directly through the referrals of satisfied clients.

Sharon knows that her clients' success is her success!
