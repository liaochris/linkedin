Cole Bradburn, D.C.’s Summary

Dr. Cole Bradburn took his undergraduate studies in the Honors College at the University of Missouri. He then went on to graduate Cum Laude, Doctor of Chiropractic program at Logan College of Chiropractic in Saint Louis. While at Logan, Dr. Cole gained national recognition as one of the few doctors to be fully certified and credentialed by the Active Release Technique organization, and also was a recipient of the Clinical Proficiency Award.

Upon graduation, Dr. Cole worked in a family care practice in his hometown of Pittsfield, IL. Here he continued his studies in sports injuries, pediatrics, and wellness care. After helping restore his family to health, Dr. Cole is now fulfilling his dream of living in North Carolina and serving the community by leading patients on the journey to true health and wellness.
