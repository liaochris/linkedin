John W. Hanna’s Experience

  • Health Solutions Executive

    IBM

    (Public Company; IBM; Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 2010 — Present (6 months)

  • Director, International Business Development

    Navigenics

    (Privately Held; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    September 2009 — January 2010 (5 months)

    Mr. Hanna joined Navigenics in September 2009 to lead global business development for its Health Compass genetic testing service. He is responsible for establishing distribution arrangements for the product around the world and is currently focused on expansion efforts in South America, Europe, and Asia. For more information on our service please visit www.navigenics.com

  • Director, Health Services Research

    Humana Inc.

    (Public Company; HUM; Insurance industry)

    May 2006 — September 2009 (3 years 5 months)

    Mr. Hanna led the University of Miami-Humana Health Services Research Center, a public-private research collaboration founded to monetize Humana’s data assets in the creation of new clinical insights for its core business and pharmaceutical clients. The organization of over 30 researchers included Humana associates and University of Miami faculty physicians conducting studies in the areas of health outcomes, health economics, drug safety and health behavior.

    Mr. Hanna oversaw the Center’s staff, its operations and regulatory affairs, and maintained its funding relationships with pharmaceutical manufacturers. His background in health policy and experience in the health insurance industry allowed him to bring a unique perspective to informing both research design and the real world application of research insights. During his tenure, Mr. Hanna led the organization to publish nearly 20 articles in prestigious peer review journals and generate over $6 million in pharmaceutical research funding.

  • Consultant, Business Development

    Humana Inc.

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    August 2004 — May 2006 (1 year 10 months)

    Mr. Hanna joined Humana Inc., a Fortune 100 company, in 2004 and was broadly tasked with monetizing Humana’s industry leading technologies and approaches to health behavior change through adjacent businesses domestically and internationally. He initially focused on predictive modeling and disease management program design services in Germany, Spain and China. He then led the company into DTC product opportunities by helping launch Sensei Inc., a Humana venture company focused on behavioral informatics and health coaching through mobile technology.

  • Operations Manager

    Disease Management Association of America

    (Political Organization industry)

    2001 — 2004 (3 years )

    Mr. Hanna came to Humana from the Disease Management Association of America (DMAA) in Washington D.C., the industry trade group that began in 1999 as the collective voice for over 130 corporations that manage high risk patients with chronic disease. At the DMAA Mr. Hanna managed company operations and public relations. He also played an integral role in lobbying activities and the organization’s milestone achievement of the passage of the Medicare Chronic Care Improvement Program legislation in 2003. Prior to the DMAA, Mr. Hanna supported membership development at America’s Health Insurance Plans (AHIP), the trade group based in Washington D.C. representing all U.S. health insurance companies.
