Michael Silva’s Summary

My objective is to obtain the position of a Creative Director, managing an in-house corporate design department.

Though I can draw on eleven years of experience in the design field, I try to learn something new everyday.

I would like to work with a company where I can continue to grow as a leader and be inspired as a designer.

Thinking of ways to make life just a little more simple is what drives me; it’s my passion.

I’ve taken in depth weekend courses and attended national seminars and conferences, while extensively training hands-on. I excel working directly with clients, designers and developers.

I have had the privilege to work with incredibly gifted individuals on compelling projects for socially relevant brands and products. My niche is branding and corporate consistency.

I vigorously keep up with new trends and industry standards. I am currently pursuing a “Personal MBA” to be completed in February 2010.
