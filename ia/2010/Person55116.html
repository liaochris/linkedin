Dave Derington’s Summary

The biggest question that any employer can ask of you is simple: "Who are you?" My response to this is simple: A self-starter who loves a challenge!

My background has spanned several industries, but above all I have a great passion for Science and Technology and a deep academic background to draw from. I've lived in the Pharmaceutical realm for roughly 15 years of my working career. This time has bridged several disciplines where learned much about both Chemistry and Information Technology. My strengths lie in that interzone between software, science, and people; a difficult combination to find.

Recent years have taken me on a different path where I've focused on Web Development and Entrepreneurship. I relish this experience as I return back to a career in the Pharmaceutical world primarily because it's exposed me to such a rich diversity of projects with extreme deadlines and high risk; exiting times which force you to reach into your core and learn what you're capable of accomplishing.

For those looking for my background in the E-Sports Market, this is another of my passions which I deeply enjoy. As with most people, video games have been a major hobby of mine. Now, my hobby takes the form of several ventures including a part-time teaching position at Webster University where I teach Video Game Theory and Design. Ultimately I see my interest in video games merging with my love of Science and Technology.
