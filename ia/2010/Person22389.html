Bernard McGovern’s Summary

Currently Corporate Vice President, Human Resources (Officer level) for Forest Laboratories, Inc. in New York, N.Y. (since 1995) with full human resources responsibilities for $4 billion late stage pharmaceutical company primarily U.S.-based, with extensive relations with partner firms throughout the world in all stages of development through commercialization.

Direct and lead a robust, comprehensive HR function supporting 5000+ associates in all disciplines.

Served with Sterling Drug (later Sterling Wintrop, then Sanofi Winthrop) in various capacities as Director, Employee Relations for Global Consumer Health; and Director, Employee Relations/Staffing for the Corporate Pharmaceutical Group. Full Generalist responsibilities for multi-divisional business, science, manufacturing & distribution, commercial and corporate.

Executive Search Consultant (retainer) with two boutique firms in NY in areas of technology (computer systems and chip development), life sciences, publishing, industrial and consumer products.
