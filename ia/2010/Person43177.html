Vera Wilkinson CDBC, CPDT-KA’s Summary

Vera E. Wilkinson CDBC, CPDT-KA is a Certified Dog Behavior Consultant, Certified Professional Dog Trainer, and member of the Association of Pet Dog Trainers (#4857), the International Association of Animal Behavior Consultants (#427) and the Association of Animal Behavior Professionals (#103).

I serve as the Dogs Divsion Chair for the International Association of Animal Behavior Consultants (IAABC). I spoke at the 2009 IAABC Conference in Houston, TX and also received the 2009 Dogs Division 'Member of the Year' Award for my work on behalf of the IAABC's Dogs Division.

Vera Wilkinson CDBC, CPDT-KA’s Specialties:

Dog behavior problem resolution through education and the use of non-confrontational training and behavior modification techniques.
