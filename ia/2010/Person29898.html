  • Communications Director

    Marijuana Policy Project

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    December 2001 — December 2009 (8 years 1 month)

    Managed staff of three who handled all media relations work, as well as other publications and information-related projects; wrote the organization’s press releases, op-eds, fact sheets, reports and briefing papers; served as MPP liaison to the scientific and medical community.

    Principal Accomplishments:

    * Wrote and supervised production of MPP’s 2009 California TV commercial urging taxation and regulation of marijuana, turning a $24,000 TV buy into national television stories on ABC, NBC, Fox News and MSNBC, over 300 local TV news broadcasts, and wire and newspaper stories carried throughout the U.S., Britain, Italy, Russia, and New Zealand.

    * Appeared on behalf of MPP on national cable and broadcast television shows, including “ABC World News,” “The Rachel Maddow Show,” “Anderson Cooper 360” and many others. Placed other MPP staffers on hundreds of local and national TV programs, including “The Today Show,” “The O’Reilly Factor,” “The News Hour With Jim Lehrer” and “The Colbert Report.”

    * Got MPP staffers quoted and its activities covered in such prominent publications as the Washington Post, Los Angeles Times, New York Times, USA Today, Time, Politico.com, Roll Call, and HuffingtonPost; in dozens of AP and Reuters wire stories; in columns by Clarence Page, John Tierney, Deroy Murdock, Debra Saunders, Steve Chapman and others.

    * Placed MPP op-ed columns (with my byline or ghost-written for other MPP staff, members or supporters) in dozens of newspapers, magazines, and Web outlets, including the Chicago Tribune, New York Daily News, Providence Journal, Chicago Sun-Times, San Diego Union-Tribune, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, San Francisco Chronicle, Baltimore Sun, Washington Times and others.

    * Wrote and supervised production of MPP’s national radio PSAs in 2005 and 2008, broadcast over 20,000 times on radio stations across the country.

    * Established ongoing contacts with leading marijuana researchers around the world.
