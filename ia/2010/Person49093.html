Darren Whittington’s Summary

Award-winning creative with 15 years experience, having worked in advertising, design, publishing and fashion. He has created work with some of the best creative agencies in the world, such as; Pentagram, Ogilvy, Turner Duckworth, Mother, Wolff Olins and McCann Erickson.

Creating campaigns and designs for Sony Ericsson, Marks & Spencer, Saab, Orange, E & J Gallo Winery, The Eden Project, Waitrose, Beck’s, Microsoft and Michiko Koshino.

Darren Whittington’s Specialties:

Strategy, lateral thinking, vivid imagination, clear communication.
