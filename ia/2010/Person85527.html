Irena Skoda, Architect’s Summary

Irena Škoda is a licensed architect in New York State & New Jersey, a NCIDQ- Certified Interior Designer, and USGBC LEED Accredited Professional. Ms. Škoda has over 20 years of multi-family residential building design, hospitality, commercial design with some of the top firms in New York City. Ms. Škoda established Skoda Design & Architecture, PLLC aimed at providing top quality architectural and interior design services with collaborative teamwork for well designed, built right and cost efficient architecture.

It is the companies goal to provide unequaled architectural and interior design excellence, with maximized client communication / satisfaction, more efficient project coordination and financially maximized benefits to its clients.
