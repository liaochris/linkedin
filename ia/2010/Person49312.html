Dava Guthmiller’s Summary

Noise 13 Design is a strategic branding firm working with food & beverage, hospitality, and leisure companies to create visible, memorable, constant consumer brands.

Design work has received press and awards from Communication Arts, San Francisco Magazine, 7x7 Magazine, Paper City, Metropolis, Interior Design and Architecture Magazines, TheBestDesign.com and the Davey Awards.

Dava has also been a judge for the Adobe Achievement Awards, founded and maintains Pow.wow Network, and is the president of the San Francisco Chapter of Slow Food.
