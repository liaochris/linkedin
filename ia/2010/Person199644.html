Wim Tanudjaja’s Summary

After high school, I started teaching BASIC programming language, flowcharting, database management system, Dbase programming at Budi Mulia Office Training Center from 1986 to 1990. I was also assisting the hospital accounting and financial information system at the Catholic Mission projects in Java and Borneo in Indonesia.

I used to work at Central Asia Insurance in 1990 to 1995 as an Internal Auditor. I audited 10 branch offices at different areas in Indonesia. I ensured all branches have an excellent internal control system in accounting and finance procedures, an excellent claims processing system.

When the Salim group took over the several food companies (mostly Noodle manufacturing companies) in Indonesia and merged them in PT Indofood Sukses Makmur, I moved to Indofood and worked in Central Purchasing division. I managed all raw materials logistic operations for 12 noodle factories and other factories for seasoning, candy and snack from 1995 until mid 1996. Continuing my career in Indofood as a Central Purchasing Manager, I concentrated to manage raw material logistic to the 12 noodle factories which produced more than 9 Billion packs of noodle in year 2000. I also managed 2 flexible packaging factories.

September 2000, I moved to Virginia, USA, I managed and owned Satay Sarinah Indonesian Restaurant in Virginia from 2001 to 2002.

I used to work for Peace Corps as international travel voucher processor for Africa Region from Sept 2003. I am reviewing and processing all international travel claims for Peace Corps staff. In 2005 - Sept 2008, I am processing International Travel claim using Oracle apps. for the whole Africa region and some part of Pacific Region.

I had started iPhone development and decided joining registered Apple Inc. iPhone developer since march 2008 and now have some apps in iTunes App store. I have made some collaboration with other parties to develop useful apps for lifestyle category
