Gen Ford’s Summary

Gen is Founder of Ithaca Business, a company specialising in holistic business process-optimisation for high-growth SMEs, family-owned companies, corporate and government organisations.

Gen's 'fixer' career started while she was an Officer in the Australian Army. After 8 years she moved to the corporate sector, as a troubleshooter with Melbourne Pathology, Mayne and Bonlac taking leadership roles to design and deliver business process optimisation solutions.

Gen founded Ithaca in 2001, motivated to create something more 'in-touch' than many consultancy services she had worked with and observed during her career as a 'fixer'.

Ithaca delivers those differences through:

* rapidly developing deep understanding of the issues

* finding simple - comprehensive (strategy, structure, resources, technology, systems, people and process) - solutions

* guiding clients through the most difficult part, implementation

* ensuring clients - not the consultant - 'own' the solution

* charging milestone-based, not time-based, fees

* not being tied to any one solution - independence

Working this way, Ithaca has successfully delivered solutions for clients including Carter Holt Harvey, CSL Pharmaceuticals, various NHS Trusts, Skanem A/S, RDB Facilities Management, Goulburn Valley Health, DHL and TNT to name a few.

Gen’s interests outside of work include travel, photography and rambling.
