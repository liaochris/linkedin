Erik Falkman’s Summary

As Halcyon Equities is registered with FINRA we are unable to display our current projects, please contact me directly for information.

As a Managing Director of Equity Sales at Halcyon Equities, LLC, Erik oversees the sales, marketing and equity placement on behalf of our network of individual and institutional investors.

Halcyon Equities, LLC, acts a resource for capitalizing and re-capitalizing Blue-Chip commercial real estate projects. We leverage our experience and knowledge in the real estate and investment industries to act as a real estate investment bank, guiding quality commercial projects through all stages, from inception and the structure of the offering, to capitalization.

Erik holds series 63 and 22 licenses, and is also a licensed Real Estate Broker in the state of Minnesota. Erik has been involved in multiple successful ventures playing key roles in various stages of development from start-up through acquisition and disposition, with an emphasis on business planning, strategic analysis and implementation.
