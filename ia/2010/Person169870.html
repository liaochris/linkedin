Brad Thomas’s Summary

Over twenty one (21) years of CRE experience in site acquisition, development, finance, and dispositions. Completed in excess of 150 build to suit transactions for national retailers such as Advance Auto Parts, O'Reilly Auto Parts, Blockbuster Video, Hollywood Video, CVS, Eckerd Drug, Walgreens, Bi-Lo, Wal-Mart, Payless ShoeSource, Cato, The Men's Wearhouse, IHOP, Waffle House, McDonald's, KFC, Pizza Hut, Papa John's Pizza, Dollar General, Family Dollar, Dollar Tree, Dollar General, Sherwin Williams, Radio Shack, Aaron Rents and others.

Most recent experience in arranging equity and debt for single tenant acquisitions. Specific experience in strategic "necessity retail" investments (portfolios) leased to nationally recognized investment grade retailers.

Also, experienced in shopping center development and acquisition with a keen eye for distressed retail opportunities and workouts.

I analyze "real time" pricing indexes relating to CRE cap rates, tenant credit ratings, interest rates, and peer related CRE asset investments. I also keep an active pulse on the non-traded REIT markets as I have ongoing consulting work with several sponsors and investors.

Brad Thomas’s Specialties:

Dealmaker - my experienced single tenant investment background has provided me with the skill set to provide capital to the safest CRE asset class available today. I specialize in providing each investor with a "customized" model for every transaction.
