Jeff Davenport’s Summary

After completing my first deal in commercial real estate nine years ago I knew I was hooked. Since then I’ve had the opportunity to gain nationwide exposure developing, leasing and managing a wide variety of property types ranging from neighborhood strip centers to super regional malls and everything in-between including office and mixed-use properties. My true passions are lease/contract negotiation and relationship cultivation. Furthermore, I’m fully committed to honing skills throughout all aspects of the commercial real estate craft for current and future use.
