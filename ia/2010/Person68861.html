Faisal Jeddy, CPA’s Summary

• Dynamic and results oriented in leading teams for technical accounting consulting, audits and financial reporting.
• Special focus on technology companies and expertise in complex technical accounting areas including revenue recognition, restructuring and integrated audits under Sarbanes-Oxley (Audit Standard No. 5)
• 16+ years in public accounting and audit, and very experienced in applying accounting principles (US GAAP), developing internal controls, accounting methodologies and SEC reporting
• Audit public companies with revenues in excess of $120 million. Help make sure all SEC reporting requirements are met.
• Help private companies establish appropriate internal controls and transition to newer accounting packages. Companies I work on range from pre-IPO start-ups to well-established businesses.
• Where ever internal control weaknesses are identified, provide recommendations to client management to improve internal controls.
• Work on several engagements for integrated audit of financial statements as well as internal controls (Sarbanes-Oxley), focusing on risk compliance, internal controls and software controls.
• More recently, one of my focus areas is the newly effective accounting standard on fair values (SFAS 157), as well as transition to International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS).
• Part of national firm-wide peer review and quality inspection program. Inspect other offices within the firm to ensure adherence to auditing standards (US GAAS), firm policies and correct application of accounting principles (US GAAP)
