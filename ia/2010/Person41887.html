Craig Mathews’s Summary

Innovator, futurist, and serial entrepreneur who is known for breakthrough thinking. With experience in IT, marketing, professional services, and general management, Craig brings perspective across many disciplines, and depth in several.

Craig is passionate about helping people start and grow their businesses. He sees business as a path to achieving life goals, and regularly helps business owners clarify and reach their goals.

He is the author of "Absolute Beginner's Guide to Tablet PCs," and is also a frequent speaker internationally on a wide range of topics, with more recent work on business growth and innovation.

Craig is known for connecting dots - people, organizations, and ideas.
