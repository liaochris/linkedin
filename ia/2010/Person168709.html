Randall Bean’s Summary

Talented and accomplished professional e-Learning developer with more than ten years of experience in the management and development of corporate training solutions for quality-focused organizations. Possess an exceptionally broad skill set and a lengthy history of producing instructionally effective e-Learning and blended learning solutions based on the principles of Instructional Systems Design (ISD).

Since 2004, have delivered dozens of successful projects for high-profile clients including Texas Health Resources, Carlson/T.G.I. Friday's, FedEx Kinko's, RadioShack, and Verizon.

Randall Bean’s Specialties:

Rapid development and ISD methodologies, Project Management, SCORM, AICC, CSS, HTML, Javascript, PHP, XML, Actionscript, Adobe / Macromedia application suites including Authorware, Captivate, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Flash, Freehand, Illustrator, Photoshop. Saba Publisher and Lectora Publisher.
