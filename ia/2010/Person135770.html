Marty Lublin’s Summary

Marty Lublin is the high-octane, dynamic, and lovable traveling host of America’s Favorite Game Show “Wheel of Fortune”. During the fall of 2002, Lublin won the spot of Traveling Road Host for the Wheelmobile. The Wheelmobile, traveling the United States throughout the year, searches for engaging and enthusiastic contestants to be on the broadcast version of “Wheel of Fortune.”

As the Pat Sajak-surrogate, Marty Lublin supports a powerful brand by “cavorting about the stage with rock star leaps, preaching like a Baptist minister,” writes James Sutter of The Daily, University of Washington’s campus paper. With his trademark intensity, Marty’s energy is infectious. He thinks on his feet, brings people to life, and makes contestants the stars of the show. Growing a fan base of his own, Marty performs News interviews and makes local broadcast appearances in the various markets the Wheelmobile visits. He has created and perfected the position of on-air spokesman and host for the traveling version of America’s #1 Game Show.

FedEx selected Marty to host the FedEx NFL Open House Experience in 2007. Marty travels to NFL stadiums to interview and introduce NFL players, FedEx Executive Speakers, and FedEx Product Managers. At each event, he entertains the audience with NFL Trivia, a live Game Show and End Zone Dance Contests.

Prior to the Wheel gig, Marty toured the country as the host of the Nintendo Fusion Tour featuring Evanescence. He also hosted the Dr. Pepper Spring Break in Panama City, Florida. Before becoming the man in the spotlight, Marty earned his stripes in production as talent coordinator and contestant coordinator for such companies as Fox, Universal, VH1, and MTV.
