    Huff Realty

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    March 2008 — June 2009 (1 year 4 months)

    Corporate Listing Coordinator / Outgoing Referral Coordinator, Fort Mitchell, KY
    As the Corporate Listing Coordinator I served as the liaison between the Third Party Brokers and our agents as we work towards the goal of obtaining a listing for a transferee. My role was to receive the requests from the Third Party Brokers, assign an agent, review and approve the Competitive Broker Market Analysis and submit to the Third Party Broker. I also oversaw all Listed Market Updates to ensure that they are submitted timely and reflect the most accurate details of the current market, comparable properties and how that affects the listing for the subject property.

    As the Outgoing Referral Coordinator, I assisted the Agents by finding Brokers outside of the Cincinnati Market to assist our client in the sale or purchase of property or land. This involves speaking with the client directly to compile the information regarding the property or land to have available when calling on outside Brokers, relaying the information to the Broker and ensuring that a top-notch agent is assigned from their company to assist our client.
