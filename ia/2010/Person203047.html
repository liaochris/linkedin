Thomas Marx’s Summary

As a photographic artist and technician, Tom takes pride in his ability to deliver his clients vision on time and within budget by remaining on the leading edge of digital photographic technology and serving his clients with friendly professional service.

Tom is based in rural Wisconsin just minutes from Green Bay, WI. where he is known for his creative execution and technical ability as a photographic artist and pioneer in digital photography.

After receiving his photography degree, Tom has had the opportunity to provide professional photography services in partnership with creative agencies and Fortune 500 corporations throughout the United States on a variety of assignments ranging from straightforward table-top photography to capturing the core of a nuclear power plant, luxury yachts in Florida, and mansions in Maine.

Thomas Marx’s Specialties:

Extensive experience as an accomplished Commercial Advertising Photographer, Digital Pioneer, and Manager with 14 years of proven leadership ability building teams that are motivated to exceed their goals. Tom played a lead roll in creating an In-House Advertising Photography Studio for a Fortune 500 Organization from the ground-up and was responsible for designing, equipping, recruiting and directing a staff of 24 professionals in providing photographic services in excess of $3 million a year.
