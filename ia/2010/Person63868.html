Ella Zwart’s Summary

Senior Recruiter with a background in Search, Interim Management and Contracts.
After a brief spell in Investment Banking Search, I was recruited by ECI Human Capital, to set up and develop a business in Social Care & Housing.
After four years in Public Sector recruitment I decided to move to the more dynamic Digital Creative arena.
Having spent some time recruiting designers, developers and account managers for both agencies and client side organizations I came to the conclusion that I prefer working in a niche area.
I now work solely with Digital Agencies on requirements in Client Services, Strategic Planning and Senior Management Teams.
