Tim Scott’s Summary

Over the past three years Tim Scott has gone from a struggling actor, job hoping, broke punk kid - to becoming a feature film star, and one of the youngest and most sought after authorities in internet marketing. He is best known as the “Entrepertainer”, the nick name sprouted from Tim being a mix between a Entrepreneur, and a Entertainer, or better yet, an actor.

Tim’s natural talent to entertain, led to his passion of acting at the young age of just 15. He has been professionally trained by some of the top acting coaches, and is most regarded for his comedic improv talents. Since 2007, Tim has starred in three feature films, and a soap opera,

Tim Is the owner of two companies, including Top Tier Marketing PRO. T.T.M.P. is an exclusive online community for home business owners to gain valuable business and marketing training, along with crucial mindset development. They also offer professional marketing services such as SEO, PPC, and Social Media Marketing.

Tim is also a huge film buff, avid snowboarder, amateur soccer player, and a professional gamer. He is an active philanthropist, and gives back to the many causes he supports..

Tim Currently resides in Oregon, but will soon be moving to Los.Angeles, CA to pursue acting full time.

Follow Tim on Twitter, Facebook to keep up with his latest business ventures, and acting career.
