Daniel Schutzsmith’s Summary

Daniel Schutzsmith is a rare breed - a hybrid of equal parts business, design, programming, and strategy - ready to change the world. He's a professor, writer, and all around web nerd.

In recent year's Daniel has developed digital strategies for clients such as: CosmoGIRL!, Dave Matthews Band, MTV, Oxygen Networks, PBS, Phish, Simon & Schuster, Sony Picture Classics, TBS, XM Radio, and the like.

Want to do some amazing work together? Bribe him with tasty indian food or single malt scotch.

Daniel Schutzsmith’s Specialties:

Digital strategy, trend watching and forecasting, new business, pitches, web design, web application development, social media, project management/interactive production.
