Ralph Gugar’s Summary

I am a registered professional surveyor, and personally have worked in the land surveying profession for over 37 years, 17 years of which as the President and creator of "CAPITOL SURVEY CO."

I am also a Member of the "INTERNATIONAL HHO INSTITUTE". It is the vision of the IHHOI:
To be the premier authority and open source of information for the discovery, development, and advancement of sustainable, renewable energy sources.
To promote HHO as a viable, sustainable, alternative energy.
To help reduce our dependence on foreign and domestic oil companies and our dependence on polluting, petroleum based-fossil fuels.
To provide standards for Quality, Safety, Installation, and Integrity solutions to meet the needs of its members.
Further, with the goal of becoming recognized as the “UL” for the HHO industry providing product registration and product performance certification.

FYI: "HHO" aka "Brown's Gas" or "hydroxy gas" is the gas that is created by splitting H2O:water back into its two component parts thru electrolysis... we utilize this gas to enhance the combustion process to well over 90% in any gasoline or diesel internal combustion engine, which not only increases power and increases fuel efficiency, but also cleans up exhaust emissions immensely, to the point where a catalytic converter isn't needed. Although currently every newer vehicle is required to have one in place by the EPA. Most people don't realize that all existing internal combustion engines are so inefficient that they only burn at most 75% of your fuel, the rest gets burned in your catalytic converter, that's what it's there for.

With this exciting new HHO technology, now that it's been refined, perfected and thoroughly tested, all that changes... Contact me for more info on how you can simply add an HHO system to any of your internal combustion engines. Other than initial installation cost, it only costs pennies per your normal fuel tankful to operate. It's a win-win situation!
