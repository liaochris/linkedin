Jay Wolberg’s Summary

Jay Wolberg is a telecommunication and wireless industry professional with over 10 years experience spanning competitive analysis, system and software validation, test automation, network engineering, and network operations disciplines. Jay is presently Manager of Software Validation Engineering for wireless communications products at Intel. Previous to this role he managed the Wi-Fi Performance Validation team where he led performance testing, competitive analysis and ecosystem enablement. Prior to joining Intel, Jay worked for Comcast where he was involved in the engineering, testing, and deployment of VoIP and WAN networks.
