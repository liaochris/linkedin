Daniel Graves’s Summary

Born in London, I have a BSC Hons in Product Design & Development.
Having worked first as a designer, then a technical manager in manufacturing I moved into outsourcing from overseas as a necessity to compete in our industry.
I worked for the licensee of celebrity chef Nigella Lawson for some time, managing a portfolio of approx 70 products in various stages of development.
In 2005 I moved to the USA to work at OEM Direct Inc.
The US market has swiftly become an area of success & high growth.
I now turn my hand to some of the sales function & financials in addition to using both my technical & project based skills on a daily basis.
