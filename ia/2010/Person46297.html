Dana C. DeMeo, PE’s Summary

With fifteen years of engineering and management experience, Dana is currently the Director of Electrical Engineering at Intelligent Product Solutions, Inc. (IPS). Dana is also a founder and principal of Electro Motive Designs, LLC (EMD), which is funded by IPS.

IPS provides product design and development services focused on the Auto-ID, data capture, and mobile computing market. Dana and his engineering team bring a solid electrical engineering competency to IPS, providing new design service opportunities in their primary markets.

EMD provides engineering services focused on product development, integration and support for electric/hybrid electric vehicle systems. Dana and his team bring the experience developing and deploying novel power/control systems for conventional and alternatively fueled vehicles.

IPS and EMD share their engineering resources to create a powerful design team spanning multiple markets and vocations. Dana's role is central to the collaboration between IPS and EMD.

Prior to IPS / EMD, Dana led engineering for Odyne Corporation, provider of the first commercialized advanced Plug-In Hybrid Electric Aerial Lift Truck system. Dana helped Odyne raise over $17M and enjoyed the process of the company going public. Odyne sold the hybrid system to Dueco, the country's 2nd largest aerial lift truck distributer, where the designs are saving massive amounts of fuel every day!

Prior to Odyne, Dana gained 12 years of valuable experience working at Symbol Technologies (now Motorola). He was involved in over 6 different product designs, including a lead role in their flagship bar code scanner, which surpassed over 1M units sold, and their premier mobile computing product, which surpassed $1B revenue.

He has a BS and MS in Electrical Engineering from Polytechnic University (NYU-Poly) and is a registered Professional Engineer (PE) in New York State.
