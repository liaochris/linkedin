Michael Williams’s Summary

A seasoned financial executive with over 25 years of experience in senior positions, including a Big 4 CPA firm pedigree in both the UK and US, international experience with public companies, and a strong background in entrepreneurial businesses, both as a consultant and a formal executive level member of the management team.

He has assisted public companies with projects related to corporate governance arising from Sarbanes-Oxley, SEC reporting, and the implementation of new accounting and disclosure requirements such as FAS 123R relating to stock compensation. As a UK Chartered Accountant he is familiar with the underlying concepts inherent in IFRS standards.

He has assisted privately held, VC funded companies in establishing accounting systems and infrastrutures to support growth, and in developing business and financial operating plans designed to minimize cash-burn and facilitate raising additional funding when required.

Most recently as Interim VP Controller at SupportSoft a NASDAQ listed software company, and as CFO of Cloudmark a privately held VC funded marketleader in the messaging security space, he has been primarily engaged in the software industry. Both companies were engaged in the enterprise software sector and the direct to consumer business requiring problem solving and resolution of technical accounting issues relating to both business models.

Prior to Cloudmark, he worked in professional sports as Controller of the Oakland Raiders NFL football team, in the entertainment industry where he was promoted from Controller to Chief Operating Officer of Steady Systems, and in the consumer products industry as Finance Director of Corgi Toys in the UK.

Williams is a UK Chartered Accountant and worked for KPMG and PW in the UK and Deloitte in the US.
