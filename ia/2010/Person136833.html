Matt Cohen’s Summary

Multidisciplined executive with 21 years experience in media online; venture capital; technology strategy. Technical visionary; adept at communicating diverse technical and business topics to non-technical audiences; thorough grounding in all facets of content operations online and in print. Trivia: registered the first newspaper domain name (chron.com) in 1988.
