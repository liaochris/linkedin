Karen Camilovic’s Summary

I am a "total office support" assistant and a problem solver. I am available to help business owners save time and money by offering my services in several ways: Monthly Retainer (set # of hours), Hourly (small fast turn-around projects), Premium Services (complex long-term contract). Barter (exchange of products and services).

I will perform the work virtually from my fully-equipped office or if you prefer, I will travel to your office.

My 25+ years business experience is diverse and includes work in many business arenas such as higher education, retail, public service, publishing, advertising, marketing, quality control, etc.

My skills and abilities are as diverse as the fields in which I have experience working, administrative office, project management, accounting, public relations, graphic print and web design, database management, sales, customer service, teaching, and much more.

I enjoy a challenge and excel when learning new skills. My motto is "I've got it"! I have a positive attitude and enjoy networking and meeting new people.

I know that running a business can sometimes be overwhelming but it really doesn't have to be! Give me a call and let's talk about what you need and what I can do to help.
