Kevin Mercuri’s Summary

Kevin Mercuri is president and founder of Propheta Communications, a Manhattan based PR marketing and social media agency.

Kevin began his career in Washington, DC where he acted as PR counsel and spokesperson for interests ranging from Internet gambling to Microsoft to chemical manufacturers and the national AIDS Lobby. Kevin's sixteen years of experience includes noteworthy work with industry leaders as well as some of the most promising entrepreneurial start-ups.

A former senior supervisor for RLM and PR 21, and senior vice president with 5W Public Relations, Kevin led technology teams through the dot com era and into Web 2.0. During that journey, he acquired a wealth of experience in the business, technology financial and consumer news media.

Kevin is a graduate and former part-time instructor at Boston’s Emerson College, he holds a masters degree in communications.
