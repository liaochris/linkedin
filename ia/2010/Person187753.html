Sharon Burdett’s Summary

The past nine years working as a designer of images, experiences and brands have taught me an important lesson about what it means to make good design...

Keep it simple. Make it beautiful. Make it fun.

Whether designing corporate logos, websites for alternative health care providers, or window graphics for international fashion companies, I always try to keep these things in mind.

Before starting Strand Design, I worked as a freelancer, an in-house designer, and as a member of an interdisciplinary design team. Through this diverse experience, I have gained in-depth experience in branding, marketing campaigns, and web design as well as fine-art printing, illustration and writing.

Sharon Burdett’s Specialties:

Brand Development, Environmental Graphics, Website Design, Direct Mail, Marketing Campaigns, E-mail Campaigns, Copy Writing and Project Management.
