Mario Martin’s Summary

Mario R. Martin’s life is media. His experience, both personally and professionally, has cultivated a background based on successes and networks.

Having worked in radio (WTMJ Milwaukee), written for various publications (The Onion, M Magazine, Lumino Magazine, etc.), did sales/marketing (Coca-Cola) and built visibility for industrial applications (Meltric Corporation), Martin poured the foundation for a successful career in public relations, promotions and marketing.

Martin found himself at Virgin/EMI-owned Narada Records in Milwaukee, WI. While there, he would celebrate various awards with his artists. Through restructuring at EMI Worldwide, the Milwaukee office was absorbed by the New York headquarters.

Martin quickly planned the next move - this time to Los Angeles - to increase the client base for a boutique agency (Gorgeous PR). His countless contacts, passion for music and charisma proved to be a true asset to the business. Recruited by mobile television network GoTV Networks, Martin brought his knowledge and experience to a technological medium as his publicity and entertainment background served the company well and raised awareness and visibility.

The next chapter is Martin's own publicity brainchild, Soulfunk Publicity, still currently in operation. Clientele includes national tours and campaigns with the likes of Happy Mondays, Tack, Ssssnake, etc.

To gain yet more business perspective, Martin took to Sonya Dakar Skin Care. This time still growing exposure, but also delving into product placement and product integration.

Martin left Los Angeles after almost 4 years, returning to his home market with years of experience, and greater networks to lean on. The next step? Stay tuned...
