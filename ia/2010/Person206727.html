Tony Addison’s Summary

My background is customer care and marketing within technology environments – notably for BT and Ladbrokes voice and computer based services and then on to "agency side" managing some major, multi-million pound CRM programmes. I now manage all commercial aspects of Free Rein, established over five years ago, which boasts a host of blue-chip clients, local authorities and smaller local businesses. Free Rein has delivered a wide range of successful projects from simple websites for start-up companies to major event management and enterprise systems.

I am Director of Suffolk Chamber of Commerce, Vice President of the Local Town Chamber of Commerce and chair the Suffolk Local Chambers Forum.
