    American CryoStem is in the business of collecting, processing and long term storage of adipose tissue (fat) derived adult stem cells. We provide individuals with the ability to preserve their stem cells for future use in cellular treatments and therapies.

  • CEO

    Bermuda Segregated Funds Ltd

    (Financial Services industry)

    January 2009 — November 2009 (11 months)

    Bermuda Segregated Funds Limited is an open platform of 5 tradable funds registered with the Bermuda Monetary Authority and the Bermuda Stock Exchange. Each fund is a “Segregated Account” and can be individually named and represented by a separate supplemental prospectus. BSFL, has engaged fund administration, custodian, legal and audit services to create a turnkey offering. Each Segregated Account can have its own unique style and investment mandate with a separate schedule of fees. Fund management is performed by International Fund Advisors, who can engage sub-advisors allowing a great deal of flexibility in choosing portfolio managers. The funds have full trading privileges on the BSX and can trade on the major exchanges worldwide.
