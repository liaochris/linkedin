Jose Sabal’s Summary

Ample experience in seafood processing plants for more than 20 years, involved in different areas like processing, sourcing and export/import. Some areas that I can provide support are: food processing plants setup, food risks assessment, HACCP systems development under FDA/USDA requirements, ISO 22000 or SQF 2000 standards. Development of SSOP's, writing the procedures, manuals, programs based on professional guidelines and training personnel, both inside the US or internationally and pasteurized crabmeat process.

One of the most critical areas for most processors is the validation of the scheduled process on a periodic basis; by gathering and analyzing data acquired from the process with commercial grade / high precission equipment I can review the overall performance and effectiveness of the process and determine the lethality applied to the food into the container. With the previous results, a "Standardization Report" will be written and can be used to support the HACCP plan validation and verification.

Support in developing the import compliance manual for US importers with references to procedures required as per US Customs, FDA, USDA and Fisheries Service.
