Jessica Abraham’s Summary

Jessica is currently responsible for day-to-day recruitment in Chicago, Dallas and Austin. Her searches include, but are not limited to, mid- and senior-level account positions in Consumer, Healthcare, Issues and Crisis, B2B Tech, Sports Marketing, Tourism, and Investor Relations/Financial Communications. Jessica assists with Corporate Human Resources openings as well as openings in other U.S. offices throughout the year. She also provides recruitment support for Edelman’s Editorial and Design/Digital groups.
