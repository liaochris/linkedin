Michael Delaney’s Summary

Managing multiple Plug-in Electric Vehicle (PEV) activities for DTE Energy, including a $5MM grant from the Michigan Public Service Commission. Also, an investment professional focusing on energy storage and plug-in hybrid electric vehicles. Conceived, developed, and launched the Clean Energy Prize presented by DTE Energy and the University of Michigan in 2008.

Developed a model for technology vendor selection and financial analysis of DTE’s proposed multi-billion dollar nuclear power plant. The model analyzes a broad range of financing structures and regulatory treatments and produces a full set of GAAP financials and discounted cash flows.

Performed stakeholder and business/financial impact analysis of political proposals for DTE’s legislative initiative focused on electric regulation, renewable portfolio standards and energy efficiency in Michigan.
