RIC SCOTT’s Summary

Passionately creative Sr. Industrial Designer with extensive experience in toy design, consumer products and graphic design combined with branding/strategy. Proven skills in all aspects of product development, from initial research, concept ideation, computer modeling through manufacturing and management of staff as well as vendors. Core strengths include vast imagination, beyond the box creativity, extreme attention to detail coupled with problem solutions.

RIC SCOTT’s Specialties:

-The ability to solve complex product ‘challenges” both functionally and visually quickly and effectively.

-Create detailed sketches and renderings that clearly communicate ideas, as well as deliver dynamic client presentation.

-Creation of engineering control drawings, 3D computer models, mock-ups, and 3D model making.

-Building positive team relationships while maintaining intense time lines and having fun while working hard and accomplishing “the un-obtainable”.
