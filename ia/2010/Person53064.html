Deana Murphy’s Summary

I have a passion for helping people to see their potentials and not the problems and to recognize strengths and resolve the weaknesses in their lives.

I want people to know that no matter where they are, they can bring order to their lives, live with peace, have strong family and interpersonal relationships, build a successful working environment, and make their homes the safest, most peaceful and fulfilling place they know despite external influences beyond their control.

This is why I've launched the Designing for Success Institute to help people take their lives, home environments and careers from chaos to order, discover peace and power in all aspects of life, and increase their self-assurance and potential. We provide you with the essential tools and then show you how to use them in a "real-world" easy to implement way.

I am also passionate about sharing these wonderful life-changing principles through personal coaching,
key-notes and workshops.

I've just released my new book, Designing for the King: From Chaos to Order by Designing With. I am thrilled about this publication because it has helped me to understand that their is more power within us than we can ever imagine, and it has also illuminated the responsibility of maintaining order in our lives. Order did not end after creation, it actually began.

I have a long journey ahead and so much work to do. People have to know their is a solution to all faces of chaos.
