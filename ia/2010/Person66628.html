Erika Kirsten Beck, Social Media Business Strategist’s Summary

I am the owner and president of Ryder Media Consultants (RMC) which designs, develops and implements custom social media business strategies and campaigns that create awareness for an organizations products or services. I specialize in social media strategy development.

Are you looking for help navigating the overload of information concerning social media?

Ask yourself these questions:
1. Do you have a social media strategy for your company?
2. Are you wondering how to effectively use or incorporate social media the right way?
3. Do you or your staff need social media tool training?

Perhaps I can help? I clear up the confusion on how to build a social media strategy, and what social media tools to use.

Many consider me a Social Media/PR 2.0 specialist because I bring a new arsenal of tools to reach constituents and consumers alike through viral media platforms such as online social networking (profiles, blogs, LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter) and the identification of online media placement opportunities and efficiencies.

Prior to starting my own consultancy, I was the communications director for the Prostate Cancer Foundation--the world’s leading philanthropic organization for funding prostate-cancer research founded by Michael Milken. Before that, I spent several years as a public relations and communications generalist; working both in-house and agency-side with budgets ranging from 50K-10M annually.

How can I help you?

Send me an email to talk about how hiring Ryder Media Consultants for your communications needs could advance your company’s business objective in today’s competitive social market.
