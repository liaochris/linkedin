Roman Markovski, MSc.’s Summary

I have a drive to optimize the way organizations work. This embodies a people-focused change management perspective, smarter and modern ways to work (relying on social innovation, web-tools, open source and flexible organization) and a modern marketing approach (using social networks, modern communication and creativity).

My field of expertise is management of change, social (and results-driven) management, organizational politic sensitivity and broad knowledge of new digital tools and trends.
