Andy Houlihan’s Summary

Currently with Cisco Systems, Inc in San Jose as Global Communication Manager for the Worldwide Customer Briefing Program. I originate and execute communication strategies across 19 centers around the world and handle all Executive Communications for the Director of the Program.

Previously, I was Director of Communications for The Los Angeles Young Actors Company. I handled events and media appearances including Daniel Craig, Ian McShane and Alfred Molina. I also co-coordinated and handled all press for a benefit performance by Sir Ian McKellen at the Freud Theater, with guests including Annie Lennox, Gore Vidal, Armistead Maupin and Bill Condon.

In the UK, I handled all media activity, speech writing and coached CEO’s for major corporations as they floated on the London Stock Exchange for Citigate PR, one of the largest and most respected agencies in the city.

I have acted as Spokesman and Media Relations Manager for the biggest commercial radio group in Britain, GCap. I also successfully managed XFM’s competitive application to Ofcom (British FCC) for an FM License in Manchester for GCap.

I have also served in a communication role in both the United States Congress and the British Parliament.

In addition, I have years of Broadcast Journalism experience – from early days with the BBC, through to reporting and producing on Sky News, to hosting national radio shows and managing teams of journalists responsible for 24/7 News Network Coverage. In my time as a reporter, I have interviewed Nelson Mandela, Tony Blair, John McEnroe and Tiger Woods, among others. I have hosted programs for Fox Sports International in Los Angeles and continue to host for CSR Radio Networks in Santa Monica.

I am also a Consultant to the Millenial Strategy Program for Frank N Magid Associates.

I am British by birth, but enjoy full Permanent Residency and Employment Rights within the US.
