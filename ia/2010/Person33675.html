Casey Mahoney’s Summary

Casey Mahoney is a senior at Middlebury College, Vt., where he is a major in Russian with minors in Political Science and Education Studies.

He spent last year studying abroad at Irkutsk State University in Russia (see travel blog at nRussian.wordpress.com). Culminating his studies in languages, human interactions across borders, education in policy and practice, and the new world of media, Casey is preparing his senior thesis this year.

He currently writes as a lead editor at Midd-Blog.com, and also directs Stuck In the Middle a cappella and Middlebury's Russian and Eastern European society.
