Dennis Monts’s Experience

  • Business Technology Development and Strategic Marketing

    Intermodal Association of North America

    (Non-Profit; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    August 2009 — Present (9 months)

    Traditional modes of moving freight including steamship, railroad and truck have evolved to work together, share equipment and build efficiency through intermodal transportation. The Intermodal Association of North America represents this industry and provides an industry neutral, non profit forum to foster the growth of intermodal transportation.

    IANA administers the Uniform Intermodal Interchange and Facilities Access Agreement (UIIA) which governs 95% of all intermodal business transactions.

    IANA has engaged The Monts Group to help study ways that the industry can further standardize industry business processes and information transfers to bring efficiencies across all modes and grow the intermodal transportation industry.

    This includes leading strategic planning, product development and business development activities for the association's Intermodal Information Services business.

    Current activities include developing and implementing industry-wide compliance programs in order to meet the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) final “Roadability” regulations (Docket No. FMCSA-2005-23315). This involves the application development and launch of the Global Intermodal Equipment Registry (www.GierRegistry.com), as well as electronic Driver Vehicle Inspection Reporting technology and systems.
