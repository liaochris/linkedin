Tom Burzinski’s Summary

A visionary, results oriented Information Technology (IT) executive with an exceptional record of performance. Accomplishments include defining and communicating business and technology strategies, implementing high-value mission critical business and IT solutions, building and aligning resources and teams with business goals, and re-engineering processes and workflows. Expert in business intelligence systems design and implementation, and IT/Business Alignment.

A 25-year career leading teams and departments for national, as well as for leading Wisconsin companies and organizations. Highlights:

* Forming and leading IT teams and departments from organizational startup through maturity
* Managing and delivering high-quality multi-million dollar project portfolios (on-time and on-budget) that meet critical business objectives
* Building a national business intelligence consulting practice
* Building and running Program Management Offices (PMO)
* Leading large business intelligence projects and programs that delivered business information using state-of-the-art technology
* Serving as an IT Strategic Planner where he led successful IT/business alignment efforts, facilitated enterprise technology planning and IT departmental reorganizations
* Developing and implementing software development methodologies, business requirements and quality assurance teams.

Career Goals

Seeking a senior leadership position with an entrepreneurial, customer-centric organization where I can participate as an active member of a strong, innovative, and proactive team. A high growth company with the passion to develop, expand, and transform its business through strategic planning, people development and appropriate investments in technology. I wish to work for a company populated by energetic people whose day-to-day actions lead to tangible and positive results within a company culture characterized by the highest level of integrity, transparency and professionalism.
