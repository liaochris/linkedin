Roger Scherping’s Summary

I am a President/CEO with expertise in growing small companies and making them profitable.

At the core of my experience is 15 years in finance as a CPA and CFO/Controller, so I bring a solid understanding of the numbers to my CEO role. I back that up with a strong background in marketing (former Product Manager) and sales and operations management.

Most of my experience is with companies under $25 million, although I also have Fortune 500 company experience.

My experience is largely in manufacturing. I have experience with turnaround, start-up and fast growth companies.

My strength is building strong teams that communicate well and work together on a shared vision of success.
