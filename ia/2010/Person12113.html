Andrew Finlayson’s Summary

SVP Digital Media Strategies SmithGeiger

Knight Fellow at Stanford University, class of 2010

Advisor to Fizwoz.com

Manager of the "Live Video Streaming" group on Linkedin.com

SmithGeiger is a custom media research and strategy firm, headquartered in Los Angeles, serving all facets of the media industry. We have deep experience in the areas including: Online Video, Broadcast Networks, Local TV Stations, Cable Networks, Major Studios, Online Networks, Broadband Video Companies, Gaming Development and Online Publishing.

Our unique research methodologies uncover consumer attitudes and preferences and leverages this insight to drive strategic and dramatic transformation in media companies.

SmithGeiger works with a variety of online companies to provide a clear sense of how consumption patterns vary in relation to key content elements, daypart differences, purposive and casual consumption, and levels of engagement including video as a social experience. Identifying the key touch points, drivers, needs, and current navigational paths across these different user segments is an integral part of our overall approach.

We have a deep history in helping large and small online clients succeed including past engagements with Yahoo!, AOL, Weather.com, ABC.com, Real Networks, Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, NBC Universal, and Warner Bros., to name a few.

How do you measure success? Higher ratings, improved ROI, increased market share, increased page views and unique users, a stronger brand identity, a successful product launch, or sustainable growth? However you measure success - SmithGeiger has a success story to share about a company we helped to realize its goals.
