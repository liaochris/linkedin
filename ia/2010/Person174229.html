Robbert Huisman’s Summary

Robbert Huisman is currently finishing is 2nd and final year at the Harvard Business School (HBS). Prior to business school Robbert worked as a strategy consultant at Booz&Co. with a focus on Operations in Retail, Telco, Aerospace and Automotive. He also worked as business development manager at Maxeda, a KKR-owned retail company. Reporting directly to CFO and CEO he focused on new business development opportunities in the Do-It-Yourself sector.

At HBS Robbert's primary focus is on entrepreneurship. He started his own business over summer after receiving a Fellowship from the Harvard Rock Center for Entrepreneurship. He is currently working on several new business opportunities.

His current focus is on e-commerce (group buying, auction models), social networks (and how to compete with them), geo- and marker-based augmented reality and digital marketing strategies.

Robbert is a Fulbright scholar
