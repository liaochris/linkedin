Kimberly Cole’s Summary

An extremely hard working person, who always tries to find a solution to the problem. I consider myself the go to person in areas of research, candidate sourcing, client development, and candidate qualifying.

I am always looking for new clients - Referrals are greatly appreciated. I believe in paying it forward - And try to go out of my way giving both contacts and non clients leads to help them achieve success.

It is not how our firm works, we really pride ourselves on selecting the best of the best to send while making sure it is a great fit for both the candidate and the client.
