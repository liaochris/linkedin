Thom Liggett’s Summary

Thom has 25 years experience in business and in consulting to top management and/or boards at public companies, private equity sponsored companies, and other private enterprises. He has been a principal investor, board member, executive officer, and/or top strategic advisor in 21 private M&A transactions with various stakeholders. Additionally, Thom has been engaged as Consultant on dozens of other "performance transformation" initiatives. The diversity of his business experience includes aggressive growth projects, corporate repositioning, supply chain optimizations, M&A transactions, complex post-acquisition integration challenges, organizational turnarounds, and complex restructuring efforts through Chapter 11 and Chapter 7 bankruptcy proceedings as well.

Thom Liggett’s Specialties:

Thom assesses the financial consequences of individual relationship networks on enterprise performance; visit www.StakeholderNetworks.com for more information. Based on his research, he creates simulation models that deliver unique perspectives and visual intelligence regarding the economic alignment or misalignment of stakeholder relationships for clients.
