Venkatesh Ramamoorthy’s Summary

I have completed my Doctoral Degree at Florida Institute of Technology in July 2010. The title of my dissertation is "Automatic Design of Feistel Ciphers Using Constraint Techniques". My advisor is Dr. Marius Silaghi.

Prior to my Doctoral studies, I bring along with me around 12 years of work experience with 7 years in Cryptographic and Security Product Development Suites. At the moment I am continuing at Florida Tech as an adjunct instructor, to teach "Introduction to Software Development with C++" and "Software Testing II".

I am looking for new opportunities at a high level, to harness my skills particularly in the mathematics of Cryptography, Software Development and Testing, and nurturing teams of personnel. The most important skill I now carry is to be able to research and deliver solutions to problems that are hitherto unexplored.

I am open to industry as well as academic opportunities.
