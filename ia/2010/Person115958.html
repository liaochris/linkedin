Kevin Simonton’s Summary

Kevin is a nationally recognized credit and collections manager for private and Fortune 300 companies. His leadership and respect earned him the Chairmanship of two national credit groups. As a general business manager, his businesses grew and never had any legitimate complaints from the Better Business Bureau.

His attutude of taking care of customer service issues before they became credit problems has contributed greatly to his above average success in his business career.

He has been an important team leader in moving various operations of three companies to the Memphis area and setting up common sense, best practice procedures and policies to successfully manage those companies.

He enjoys solving challenges of projects and working with financially distressed customers. Kevin enjoys making the day to day operations more efficient and exceeding the weekly cash flow projections. He is also experienced in mainframe conversions and training. Kevin has up to date Microsoft Office skills.

He is seeking a challenging management position in a positive environment company that will utilize his talents and experience.
