Richard A. (Dick) Thompson’s Summary

I love taking on challenging business situations, sorting through options, getting things stabilized and building the business. Essentially, my calling is being the "Man in the Arena" as Teddy Roosevelt so aptly conveyed nearly 100 years ago!

Small & mid-size enterprises (aka SME), with less than $200 million in sales and 500 employees in "niche" markets are my sweet spot. Fortunately, I have been blessed with direct experience (operating and consulting) in several B2B businesses in multiple industries. I have observed that they always offer value creation opportunity by transforming, repositioning and growing, organically &/or inorganically.

I am passionate about leading change to transform the organization and motivating the team to execute. Connecting the dots in the industry/sector and crafting a plan to become number one or two (a la Jack Welch, my GE mentor) in its niche is exciting! At the end of the day, it’s all about people; from creating the vision/strategy, communicating it, empowering others and making the culture accountable and fun are invigorating.

Richard A. (Dick) Thompson’s Specialties:

Leading change, inspiriting teams, profitably growing businesses, sourcing capital, preparing for and executing liquidity events, acquiring, integrating companies & industry consolidations; penetrating new international, commercial & government market segments.
