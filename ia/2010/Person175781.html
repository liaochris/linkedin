Rob Koster’s Experience

  • Co-founder and co-owner

    SKMG

    (Internet industry)

    January 1999 — Present (11 years 4 months)

    Skwortsow Koster Media Group (SKMG) BV is a Dutch corporation, founded in 1999, which focuses on operating consumer Internet services that are focused around certain niches such as classifieds, photo sharing and social networking.

    In early 2000, SKMG launched the student housing site Kamernet.nl. To this day, Kamernet.nl has absolute dominance in its niche.

    More than a million users registered with the service and hundreds of thousands of rooms were advertised. An impressive feat, considering that The Netherlands only has 16 million inhabitants. Nearly all Dutch students register with the service.

    In early 2002 SKMG launched their dating service Lexa.nl, which achieved a number one position in the Dutch online dating market. Lexa.nl set a new standard for Dutch dating sites, having introduced large-scale singles-parties, video-chatting and new matching technologies.

    Lexa.nl was sold to Meetic (PAR: MEET) in 2006.

    Kamernet.nl was sold to Direct Wonen (AIM: DIWO) in 2008.
