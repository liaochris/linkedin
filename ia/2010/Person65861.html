Eric Genet’s Summary

Passionate about Technology, Talent Management and Social Media, I have spent the past 10 years developing, integrating and selling on line solutions to help customers of all size and in all geographies to attract, develop and retain their best talents.

I have a wide experience in managing Software Engineering operations using technology stacks from J2EE to Force.com, Google App engine or the Amazon EC2/S3 and a variety of development processes such as waterfall or Agile/Scrum.

Part of the pioneers in delivering Software as a Service Enterprise solutions in the early 2000, I have a very deep knowledge of multitenancy architecture, web application security, infrastructure mutualisation and service monitoring.

After 6 years managing from professional service teams to inside sales, I have created and am currently running the SMB operations for Jobpartners Plc encompassing product engineering, marketing, sales to support. The teams in the SMB Operations have been the one creating and launching the company’s latest generation talent platform, already award wining and acquiring customers accross Europe.
