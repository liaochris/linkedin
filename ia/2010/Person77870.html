Greg Mondshein’s Summary

The Max Borges Agency is the #1 public relations agency dedicated to the consumer technology industry. We generate MASSIVE PRESS COVERAGE and specialize in product launch campaigns, social media, media relations and provide complete consumer electronics industry marketing consultation. From North American distribution to complete branding services, we have the capacity to help companies get to market rapidly and efficiently. We currently have 40+ clients actively utilizing our services and project rapid growth and expanded service offerings. www.maxborgesagency.com.

Personally, I'm a well-trained marketing communications, public relations and business development professional with substantial client and agency experience. I have managed a wide variety of domestic and international clients with marketing efforts centered in the US and abroad.

From a Fortune 200 corporation to a rapidly growing boutique PR firm, I have the experience and interpersonal skills to thrive in any culture. I am technology savvy and well-versed on social media marketing tactics. While specializing in B2B and B2C relations, initiating product launch strategies, media relations and business development; I also have experience in community relations, as an on-air spokesperson and as a branding consultant. Currently I'm managing the marketing and business development efforts for the Max Borges Agency.

I have discipline, strategic focus and a unique educational background which allows me to operate successfully in the constantly changing business environment. I have the big picture focus to ensure my organization is always on the cutting edge and delivering industry leading ROI, relevant services and measurable results. My determination and passion for excellence are certainly second to none.

I am always interested in communicating with people in various industries and job functions in efforts to further my development and knowledge of the business landscape.
