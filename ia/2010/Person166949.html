Kathleen O'Connor’s Summary

Your Voice - My Pen

I am an experienced editor, seeking writers who wish to convey their ideas clearly, succinctly, and accurately, and who want to be sure their publications achieve that goal.

I edit theses, dissertations, books, reports, novels, memoirs, poetry, and many other written forms. I am familiar with several style guides, including AMA, AP, APA, Blue Book, CMS, MLA, Turabian, and Zondervan, as well as several university guides.

I track all changes so that my clients will know what has been altered, and I strive to improve the text without changing the client's voice.

Kathleen O'Connor’s Specialties:

Substantive editing, TESOL editing, dissertations, and books.
