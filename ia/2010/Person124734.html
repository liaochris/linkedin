Liberty Harper’s Summary

Liberty is experienced as both a franchisee as well as a franchisor, and understands all aspects of the franchise industry. Previous to beginning and managing Liberty Fitness until 2007, Liberty served as a double franchise co-owner with Curves International beginning in 1998, receiving the prestigious Franchisee of Year Award in 1999. Liberty has fine-tuned her focus on international support and training, having worked with franchisees in Dubai, India, Kuwait, China, Singapore, Korea and Qatar after specializing as a franchise services consultant. Most recently Liberty has joined Aviatech, an interactive and digital marketing agency to assist with business development while still consulting with multiple franchise companies. Ms. Harper is also heavily involved in the 16-Chair Women's Franchise Committee of the International Franchise Association. She lead the formation of the WFN of Austin, Northern California, and now serves as the co-chair of the WFN of San Diego. She serves on the membership committee and as an ambassador for the IFA.
In addition, Liberty was featured in the February, 2006, issue of Marie Claire magazine in an article entitled “I Made it by Age 30, and So Can You!” Liberty has also been included in the new book by Debra Shigley entitled The Go-Getter Girls' Guide, was the cover story for the 2006 June issue of Austinwoman Magazine, and has been quoted in many national and fitness industry publications including The New York Times, CNN Money, Fitness Business Pro, ACE Fitness Matters, Entrepreneur, Club Business International, Club Industry, Franchise Times and Franchising World among other regional and local news sources.
