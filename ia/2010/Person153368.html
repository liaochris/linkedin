David H. Page’s Summary

I started New Road Media in 2003 as a new media investment and consultancy. Previously I was in media sales and management at NBC-TV, Clear Channel, Children's Broadcasting and SONY Pictures/Music Digital, helping create and sell in new media solutions for marketers.

I always had a particular excitement for entrepreneurship and media and thus started investing small amounts of seed capital (and plenty of time) into friends' and colleagues' stewarded new media ventures in return for founder's stock. Some were good, some went bad and some remain dormant. From each and every one of them, however I have had a bird's eye view of what it takes to be an entrepreneur, what works in media messaging and lessons in humility. But what excites me the most is being an active part of those new paths being built. Pretty cool.
