Lucy Allen’s Summary

Four years experience as independent massage therapist. Massage has helped people with carpal tunnel, tendinitis, frozen shoulder, sciatica, headaches, fibromyalgia, multiple sclerosis, low back pain. Clients include musicians, cyclists, runners, health care professionals, clerical and administrative workers, restaurant workers, teachers, construction, visual artists, retirees.

Goals include continuing to assist people in their own healing, and continue my own education. Charter Member of the Society for Oncology Massage and wish to build on helping clients with cancer.

Lucy Allen’s Specialties:

Oncology massage, Active Isolated Stretching, Swedish massage, Deep tissue massage, Chair massage
