Christopher Stone’s Summary

Thirty years of practical, hands-on experience in corporate Human Resources executive leadership, HR/Organization Effectiveness consulting in diverse environments, and university teaching and research. Well-versed in structural design, executive assessment and coaching, strategic workforce planning, and organizational diagnosis and transformation to achieve business results and/or radically change culture and direction. Excels at adapting people strategies and practices to conform to changing business requirements and environmental realities. Dynamic speaker and facilitator with deep experience in process consultation and consensus building. Trusted advisor to executive leadership. Expert in developing and delivering customized organizational intervention and training programs.
