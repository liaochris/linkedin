Jose Camacho’s Summary

I am a Quality Engineer for Baxter Healthcare, my work assures a higher degree of confidence that systems and process that are: modified. brought into service, or have maintenance performed, i assure that this is done according to appropriate change control and that the changes comply with the validation process. Much of this is accomplished through the use of Lean/Six Sigma Tools. The purpose is to comply with current FDA requirements such as GDP/GMP. In the day to day activities I may use some of the tools acquired with the green belt program.

I help define process validation requirements and develop test plans (IQ/OQ/PQ) for drug production support equipment or processes. Provide quality engineering support for the launch of new /existing drug system or processes. Conduct equipment, process and software validations, Failure Mode & Effects Analysis (FMEA), Define Measure Assess Implement Control (DMAIC), statistical analysis, review and approval of documentation, and failure analysis and corrective action. Completed company sponsored Six-Sigma Certification Course and Project Management & Methodology Course.

I am also a US Navy Reservist as a Hospital Corpsman working for the US Navy, keeping our fellow Navy and Marines Healthy and a ready force. As a Navy Corpsman I have extensive experience as a Medical Technologist, working in a clinical laboratory setting.
