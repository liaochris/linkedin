James Shaw’s Summary

I am a seasoned Quality Assurance Methodologist/Architect with over eighteen years of experience in Software Development. I have participated in the development and implementation of many software standards including the Rational Unified Process (RUP, 8 yrs), Six Sigma - Champion & Green Belt (6 yrs), Testing Maturity Model (TMM - 8 yrs), ISO 9001/9002 (13 yrs), Total Quality Management (TQM - 10 yrs), GCP/GLP/GMP/GxP (7 yrs), Sarbanes-Oxley (SOx 404 - 5 yrs), and SEI’s Capability Maturity Model (CMM - 14 yrs).

* Entire career emphasis on methodology, process, and policies for creating hybrid Software Engineering frameworks and instilling best practices.
* Sarbanes-Oxley 404 (SOx) experience at four large companies in differing industries
* Thorough understanding and working knowledge of all phases of the SDLC and STLC
* Implementation of ground-up process improvement and quality programs at many companies
* Industry experience in; FDA, Commercial Software, Web (dot-com), Aerospace, Banking/Financial, Network Security, Anti-Virus, IRS, Retail Energy & Gas, FCC, & FAA
* Working knowledge of various IT standard Processes, Frameworks, and Methodologies
* Strong Technical skills with exposure to many tools, platforms, databases, and applications written for various software architectures
* Comfortable at the Strategic/Management level and also at the Tactical/Practitioner level
* Experience testing and leading testing teams of 100+ QA professionals
* Hands-on expert in Quality Assurance, Software Testing, Automated Testing, and Process Development and/or Improvement

Through the years I have learned that software 'quality' does not begin with testing; it begins with processes and best practices, is nurtured through organizational support, and ends with stringent and effective testing and a controlled maintenance program. True Quality Assurance works where continuous realizations of practical process improvements are being applied.
