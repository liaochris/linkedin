Jack Yan’s Summary

Goal: to create a better world for all humankind through business. Founded one of the earliest virtual companies in 1987, entered web publishing 1993, discussed moral global brands 2000, spirituality in business concept 2003, connected UNEP with Lucire 2003, co-wrote a Carbon Neutral book 2003, took a web magazine brand into print 2004 (and into two countries 2005), published first international sustainable style editorial 2005. Mayoral candidate, Wellington 2010. Designed over 200 typefaces. Director of think-tank Medinge Group (www.medinge.org); co-author of Beyond Branding (2004); author of Typography and Branding (2005); contributor to Nation Branding: Concepts and Cases (2009); publisher of Lucire (www.lucire.com, www.lucire.net). BCA 1994, LL B 1995, BCA (Hons.) 1996, MCA 2000. Visit company site at www.jya.net. Read my blog at www.jackyan.com/blog.

Jack Yan’s Specialties:

Branding (connecting visions to business outcomes), international public speaking, strategy, media developments, future consumer movements, corporate social responsibility, intellectual property management, cross-media issues, business visions, trend forecasting, entrepreneurship, exploration of organic brands, and spirituality in business. See www.jackyan.com for more information about me and jackyanformayor.org for my mayoral campaign.
