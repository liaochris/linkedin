Jonna Stopnik’s Summary

International technology sales, marketing and management professional with over 26 years of successful experience building sales organizations. Expertise in selling enterprise infrastructure, networking, storage, eCommerce outsourcing and web content syndication service solutions. •Creative and innovative leader committed to inspiring excellence and leading by example
