Anne Elster’s Summary

Dr. Anne C. Elster is currently the leader of the IDI/NTNU HPC-Lab and an Associate Professor of Computer Science (IDI) at the Norwegian Univ. of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim, Norway. She helped found NTNU'srComputational Science and Engineering Program and served as Co-Director until Jan. 2007. She is and has published and/or given over 150 technical invited talks, reviewed publications and/or reports and presentations. She recently served on an Expert Panel on research infrastructure for the Danish Research and Innovation Ministry where she is is one of four representing sciences & engineering. Program budget: Total of DKR 600 million (ca. USD 80 million) for 2007-2009. She is also currently part of EU COST Action IC0805: Open European Network for High Performance Computing on Complex Environments.

She will be at Univ. of Texas at Austin during her 2010/2011 sabbatical.

See http://www.idi.ntnu.no/~elster/bio.html for more details.
