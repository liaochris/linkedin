Frances Palaschuk’s Summary

I started my business in October 2007. Since that time, I have used the Internet to market my business.

I have always had a passion for technology, so it was natural for me to adapt so well to the use of social media to market businesses online.

Plus, the progression to become a programmer analyst/web developer complements my previous experience and education in search engine optimization and online marketing.

Today, I am able to setup an entire customized content management system. I have become an avid fan of everything Google. I use Google Apps, Google Webmaster Tools, Google Analytics, Google Maps – and I know how each application plays into one another for maximum results.

I remember the first post I made on the blog - it was as scary for me as public speaking is for most people. I was really nervous. Thankfully, it all worked out for me - I love writing.

My audience is a mixture of people from online business owners, those looking for viable work from home options, those wanting to learn how to use WordPress, to some of my current and potential clients. I like to blog about new and relevant information and/or breakthroughs pertaining to social media, computer programming, and internet marketing.

My passion for technology has enabled me to focus on what I love doing - designing web sites, software applications, hanging out in all the cool social media spots and of course, keeping up with everything Google!
