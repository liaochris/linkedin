Timothy Grammer’s Summary

I manage strategic projects for biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies that lack the requisite internal expertise and resources.

My broad scientific background is in the fields of medicine, biochemistry, protein chemistry, molecular biology, genetics and genomics. This is combined with extensive project leader and management expertise and experience.

Timothy Grammer’s Experience

  • Project Management Consultant

    Integrated Project Management Company

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    November 2007 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Project Manager specializing in the life science industry. IPM is focused on helping companies by bringing industry best practices and discipline to manage their critical projects. For more information, visit the IPM website at http://www.ipmcinc.com/.

    Created document management processes for a large pharmaceutical company for corporate policies surrounding document authoring and electronic submission of New Drug Applications (NDAs) and marketing authorization applications (MAAs) to the FDA and EU regulatory bodies, respectively, using the electronic Common Technical Document (eCTD) format.

    Provided project management expertise for a small pharmaceutical company involved with international partnership to develop new medicines for neglected tropical diseases (NTD). Co-authored National Institute of Health (NIH) grant application to gain additional funding for the company’s NTD program. Created and managed project budgets and plans including alliance, communication, risk, and stakeholder management.

    Managed the creation of an Integrated Project Plan and Schedule for a complaint handling and investigation process for a large pharmaceutical company launching a drug:device combination product. Plan included call center training and SOPs, device return shipment processes, device investigation laboratory set up and process flows, and complaint closure SOPs.
