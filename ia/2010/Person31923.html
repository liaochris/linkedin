Monique Capelta’s Summary

Capelta Couture Boots are glamorous, and unique with a sexy, classy vintage flair. Capelta initially made its mark by creating handmade boots with a variety of exotic elaboration, using a delicate mix of the finest leathers and furs, and accented with an eclectic array of Swarovski gems and hardware to produce each individual boot as its own one-of-a-kind item. Capelta characterizes their authentic signature with a feminine, yet edgy niche using their dynamic artistic drive to fine detail in combination with an innovative concept to fit the function of practicality, the ease of comfort, and the demands of society's trendiest elite figures. The brand's focus is to provide highly specified luxury looks at an affordable price while primarily serving the exclusive boutique market.

Capelta has recently hit the Hollywood mainstream! Capelta boots have been photographed on the likes of Britney Spears, Kim Kardashian, Jessica Simpson, Melanie Griffith, and Kate Moss and many other celebrity icons. The brand has appeared in magazines such as InTouch Weekly, 944, Harper's Bazaar, Italian's IO Donna, Collective Magazine, California Apparel News, & some of the most prestigious fashion magazines across Japan.

In addition, Capelta obtained product placement on the CW's Melrose Place this past season and scheduled to appear on next season's 90210, and currently negotiating with other cable network shows as well. Capelta were also the featured sponsors of the gifting suites for The Oscars, Golden Globes, Grammy's, and Kids Choice Awards.

They have put incredible effort into media exposure and the results are highly beneficial to all who carry the brand. Capelta boots are available in many boutiques across the US, in Spain, Japan, and in Canada. Capelta's reputation to the fashion community at large ideally positions them as a must have frenzy for fall!
