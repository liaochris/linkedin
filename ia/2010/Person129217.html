Lynda Lippin’s Summary

I am currently in my third year as the resident Pilates & Fitness Teacher at the exclusive COMO Shambhala Retreat at Parrot Cay in Turks and Caicos Islands. A Turks & Caicos Islands resident since 2005, I have over 20 years of fitness and Pilates teaching experience and am recognized as one of the top Pilates trainers in the world. I am also a Usui Reiki Master who does Reiki training and treatments in person and via distance.

My academic background as a Philosophy Professor serves me well as I also do freelance work as a copy writer and copy editor for both print and on line media.

My Pilates classes and privates have been mentioned in Hello Magazine, Philadelphia Magazine, Pilates at About.com, Pilates Style Magazine, and the Times UK.

I hold certifications in Fitness Training, Pilates, Gait Training, Pilates for Osteoporosis, & Pilates for Golf. My specialty is rehabilitative Pilates and I have developed some very well reviewed Pilates audio programs for Neck Pain and Back Pain.
