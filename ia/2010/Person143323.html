    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    2008 — Present (2 years )

    Telaminder™ is a start up Tele-Health business that addresses the important market opportunity created by the:
    • increased longevity of Americans
    • desire/goal to remain independent and age in our own homes
    • increased reliance (and historical lack of success) on medication regimens
    • role of caregivers with their own busy agendas and in home health care agencies
    • current technology availability

    Telaminder is a first-of-its-kind service application that combines simple to use robust software and cell phone technology with a specialty reporting platform.

    Telaminder gives seniors and others on important medication regimes (i.e. transplant, diabetes, cardio–pulmonary, chronic illness) a respectful and necessary tool that allows them to remain independent longer and in many cases to avoid institutionalization. It helps not only with medication but with limitless other scheduling and reminder issues. With Telaminder individuals manage their Activities of Daily Living (ADL's) and their daily schedules via voice command or by pressing a single button.

    Telaminder notifications are created on our secure web site by authorized users and are delivered as voice calls to user-centric cell phones - providing users, caregivers and families the peace of mind that comes from our unique problem escalation engine.
