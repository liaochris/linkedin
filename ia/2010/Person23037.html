Bev Drake’s Summary

Known for coining the term "vision-driven resume writing" and integrating the concept into a successful method for helping clients by emphasizing and concentrating on these rules and tactics of engagement: employer needs; industry timeliness; preparedness for the whole process, not just the resume; a “two-way street” attitude about employers and need for value to both sides; appropriate matching of job to candidate and vice versa; and a collaborative effort required by clients, who aren’t allowed to simply drop off info and wait to pick up the results without being involved in the process. Here's a case where the sum of the parts is the real value. If you were to take parts out, which would you say wasn't important: input; drafts and edits; a sounding board; Q & A opportunity; direction and guidance; or (sometimes) -- homework? They're all key components.

Career includes developing professional resumes and cover letters since 1985. Experienced coaching clients on various career issues, including burnout, career change, interview preparation and job search. Other talents and experience: editing and proofreading; medical and legal transcription; writing book reviews and business-related articles for professional newsletters.

Bev Drake’s Specialties:

Certifications: CPRW (Certified Professional Resume Writer); CEIP (Certified Employment Interview Professional); and Internationally-certified as JCTC (Job and Career Transition Coach).
