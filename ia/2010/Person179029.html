Russ Thornton’s Summary

I'm a catalyst for helping people make the most of their one shot at life.

Through a client-focused, proactive process, I deliver personalized financial and investment advice to clients in Atlanta and across the country.

I work as a Vice President - Investment Manager for Wealthcare Capital Management, based in Richmond, VA.

In addition to 15+ years of financial service experience, I hold the following professional industry designations:

Russ Thornton’s Experience

  • Vice President - Investments

    Wealthcare Capital Management

    (Privately Held; Investment Management industry)

    November 2009 — Present (4 months)

    Somewhere along the way, the world of Investments and Financial Planning has lost sight of what’s really important, in an effort to continue to refine the process and provide more and more accurate advice. Somewhere along the way, we’ve made it so difficult to go through the process, that we’ve discouraged those individuals for whom the process was designed from taking the time to do any planning at all.

    We can provide clarity to the situation in the form of our process called…Wealthcare.

    Wealthcare is not an event, it is a process. A process that identifies your priorities (which can and will change throughout your life), providing you with a series of choices you need to make to achieve these priorities and what price, either through more or less savings or more or less risk or other adjustments to goals, is needed to pay for your priorities. It’s an ongoing dialogue that adapts to your life as you adapt to the changes that lie ahead. It’s a service that is needed, is easily understandable, convenient, and more than just reasonably reliable. Why live your life with uncertainty when you can choose…Wealthcare.
