Ariana Vincent’s Summary

Ariana Vincent is a Nationally Certified Massage Therapy Instructor, Continuing Education Provider & Practitioner (National Certification Board for Therapeutic Massage and Bodywork).

She is the founder and owner of Ariana Institute, based in Austin, Texas.
