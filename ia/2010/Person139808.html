Dr. Sudhi Gautam MD.’s Summary

Dr. Sudhi Gautam is a Surgeon-turned-Biomedical Engineering Ph.D. with over 15 years of broad experience in Surgical Procedures, Cardiovascular Devices, ENT Devices, Endoscopy, Diabetes, Blood Processing, Bio-artificial Organs, Medical Imaging, Remote patient monitoring, Medical Information systems and Surgical Practice.

He currently heads the Medical Solutions Group at Patni Americas Inc, a high quality service provider for Class III and Class II Medical Devices OEMs.

•Practiced Ear, Nose & Throat surgery/medicine for 8 years before getting motivated into doing a Ph.D. from IIT, Bombay.

• Helped early stage Biomedical Startups and Large companies to conceptualize new medical products and develop high impact market/business strategies. Evaluate prospective deals, competitive attractiveness of opportunities, due diligence, IP/Market Research.

•Doubled revenues in just 9 months after taking over as Medical Director in a medical education company. Strong Leadership/team skills. Motivated and led a team of 17 direct reports who contributed to the success.

•Currently Life Science Chair for SVASE, an active non-profit in the LifeSciences. Actively networked in the most facets of the medical device/Life Science industries.

•High-energy, motivational speaker, driven, out-of-the-box thinker with a keen sense of humor.
