Amy Skursky’s Summary

Guiding your business where you want to go demands energy and focus. Your business success is highly dependent on your personal effectiveness.

Could you be more effective if you had a trusted right-hand person who is competent, reliable, and committed to your success?

I operate as an extension of your business to support a full range of your administrative functions, much like an executive assistant, except in a virtual environment.

You are the face of your business. My job is to be that resource in the background that keeps everything on track so you can capitalize on higher order business activities.

It's difficult for many executives to trust certain components of their business to someone they barely know. And let's be clear - not every administrative assistant "gets it" when it comes to your business.

A few of the key requirements that quickly build trust are when the support person is a quick-study, capable of executing with minimal direction, communicates effectively, and delivers consistently high performance.

Very few executive assistants comprehend or are measured based on their performance. For that reason, I provide a 100% money-back guarantee...so you can be assured that your performance standards are always met or exceeded.

If you need a reliable and consistent assistant who can quickly adapt to your needs, we would make a great team.
