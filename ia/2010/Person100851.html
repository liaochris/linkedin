Joelle Delbourgo’s Summary

I have been a literary agent for 10 years. Prior to launching Joelle Delbourgo Associates, Inc., Literary Management, I held top editorial and publishing positions at various divisions of Random House and HarperCollins. I bring that "insider" knowledge and track record as an "intrapreneur" to the authors I represent.
My goal is to bring exciting new ideas and excellent writing to as broad an audience as possible.
We are a boutique agency for adult nonfiction as well as selected fiction and young adult books. We specialize in building authors' careers over the long-term, not just selling literary properties. We may be small and personal in our style of doing business, but we are powerful. We have excellent contacts within the book publishing industry, in the US, and also internationally, through a network of subagents who work with us to sell rights in as many languages, and for adaptation to film and television. We also seek to bring our client's work into as many formats as possible, including audio books, e-books, and whatever form content can be delivered now and in the future.
As the publishing industry continues to be challenged by consolidating, contraction and the dizzying speed of developing technologies, it is increasingly important for authors to seek representation that helps them to navigate this complex world, and guides them in terms of seeking and maximizing the opportunities. The team at our agency has the vision, energy and skills, from conception to marketing, to make this happen for each of our clients.

Joelle Delbourgo’s Specialties:

I am a seasoned editor, publisher, marketer and salesperson. I bring those skills to every stage of the publishing process from development and negotiating deals to bringing books to market and giving them a long life. In terms of subject areas, I am particularly knowledgeable about books in these areas: history, science, memoir, narrative nonfiction, psychology, health and medicine, parenting, business and current events.
