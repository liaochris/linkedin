Jamey Leveille, APR’s Summary

Nationally networked results-driven communications executive with proven record of accomplishment in strategically planning and driving PR campaigns that increase sales, brand awareness and heighten credibility online and offline. Identifies creative ways to engage brand ambassadors and entice new prospects into the fold. Provides strategic communications design/planning/execution/measurement, media coaching, media relations/editorial services and strategic counsel on how best to shape and deliver a compelling message that will move target audiences into action.

Infuses communications with multi-tiered creative strategies that grow mind and market share. Bolsters PR effectiveness by employing both traditional and non-traditional approaches that break through in our changing media environment. Experienced in the effective use of broadcast, social media and interactive strategies as part of the marketing mix. Established relationships with top-tier journalists, editors, bloggers and producers across the U.S.

Savvy at interacting with all levels of internal and external personnel, senior management and stakeholders. Maintains strong client partnerships by working closely and synergistically with all business units to achieve company-wide goals.

Leveille’s media relationships and work on behalf of her clients has resulted in high-profile engagements and earned editorial coverage in the nation’s most influential outlets including The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, Washington Post, Chicago Tribune, USA Today, Los Angeles Times, TIME, Consumer Reports, Bloomberg, Reuters, NPR, CNN/Headline News, MSNBC, Newsweek, BusinessWeek, FORTUNE, BBC Worldwide, CNET, U.S. News & World Report, the Associated Press, “The Early Show” and many top consumer lifestyle publications including GQ, Maxim, Runner’s World, SELF, Redbook, Health, Glamour, ESSENCE, JET, Parenting, Travel+Leisure, among others.
