Victor Canada’s Summary

As a Social Media Speaker, Trainer, and Consultant I take a very hands on approach when helping our clients succeed in the Social Media space. During the initial consultation, we bring the right considerations to the forefront in order to develop a social media strategy that works. We also develop a plan for maintaining / promoting their presence including training their staff as desired.

Contact me to speak for your group or organization: victorc@nxtsm.com or 225.270.9022.

Previously as Vice President of Operations for LANtec, my duties included all aspects of Operations Management and Organizational Development including day to day operations, training inventory and fulfillment, human resources development, quality management, and public relations.

As Director of the Virtual Call Center for Affirmative Insurance Holdings, I worked to operate and improve the efficiency and effectiveness of 500+ agents accross the country and the associated technology.

As the Director of Training and Quality Assurance at USAgencies Management Services, I helped to lay the foundation for the training department and created the quality assurance department, an integral part of the company’s strategy at the time. Previously, I spent 5 years as Director of Training and Senior Corporate Technical Trainer specializing in Microsoft, IBM, Sun and open source technologies.

I began my professional career in the Air Force and have spent 20 years in management. I have provided training and consulting for government agencies, Fortune 500 companies and private companies with a special emphasis in IT operations, business intelligence, and process improvement.

Currently hold A+, MCSD, MSF Practitioner, E-Trainer, Quality Improvement certifications and a personal lines insurance license. I am on the board of Mission New Orleans, a non-profit organization assisting Katrina/Rita Evacuees in rebuilding their lives.
