Sharon Reus’s Summary

I help self-employed professionals and small business owners make more money and have a more fulfilling life.

I’m an entrepreneur, coach and consultant with 25+ years of experience in communications and marketing. As a coach, I work primarily with entrepreneurs and self-employed professionals who are struggling with how to start and/or successfully run a small business... you know the ones, who start a business because they are a great (fill in the blank - artist, hair stylist, accountant, etc.) but then find that they need help in business processes, time management, marketing, hiring and other business management challenges. I've been there and I know the pain! I help these passionate entrepreneurs move from overwhelmed to empowered, with tools in place to succeed at their life's work AND in their business.

Strengths include active listening, strategic thinking and creative problem-solving. I help clients get to the "who," the "what" and the "how" in every problem... who they are, what they want to accomplish and how to get there.

As a consultant, I provide marketing and communications programs and facilitation for strategy and brainstorm sessions for businesses of all sizes.

I’ve been blessed to have a bunch of really fun and diverse jobs, including magazine publisher, talk show producer, newspaper editor, teacher and executive producer for a marketing agency. During my career I've worked with clients such as Anheuser-Busch, McCarthy Building Companies, Fleishman-Hillard, Washington University, Ford Motor Company, Volkswagen, John Deere and BJC Healthcare. More than half of my career has been as a small business owner or self-employed professional.

Read my Insight Into Action blog at www.sharonreus.com... if you're a self-employed professional, freelancer or small business owner in St. Louis, join our LinkedIn group Self-Employed St. Louis. Look forward to connecting with you!
