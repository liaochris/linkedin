Cassidy Lackey’s Summary

In the summer of 1997 I launched Mobile Generation Software, a mobile software startup which was acquired by Handmark in 2000. I've continued with Handmark to develop deep expertise in mobile software design, development, business development, ecommerce technologies, online and social marketing.

I am currently leading the business unit responsible for the design, development and marketing for mobile software titles such as Zagat and Oxford on iPhone, Android and Blackberry.

I have a strong entrepreurial spirit and successful history launching a series of internet businesses and real estate ventures.

Cassidy Lackey’s Specialties:

I specialize in defining and implementing mobile content and application strategy including the vision, design, development and business management. I am also experienced in direct to consumer marketing including search engine optimization and search engine marketing. I have proven success managing the P&L of a business unit, building strategic relationships and managing employees.
