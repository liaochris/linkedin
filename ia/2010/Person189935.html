Simon Ekstrand’s Summary

I can’t help it. I just love design challenges. The very thought of taking something, an idea or a difficult design - and to be able to re-design it into something really useful - that makes me tick.

I’m skilled at coming up with innovative, practical solutions. You can say that this is my superpower - to be able to see how things should be put together.

My passion for creating cutting edge solutions, for finding new ways to solve old problems, has broaden my competences. I can today truly say that I have a holistic mindset.

A design solution is just a seed. People and organizations are equally important factors - they must be able to nurture the design in order to make it grow. When I founded Omskiftare (”Shifters”) I had this in mind. Shifters is what some people would call a Think Tank. We like to call ourselves an Idea Studio.

By using common design methods (you know - interviews, iterative design, user needs, personas, target groups, prioritization, prototyping, usability testing and so on) you can reach 80% of the optimal solution. And you must be able to do all the underlying work properly, otherwise you can not reach all the way to the top. There is no shortcut.

I specialize in the remaining percentages, the ability to reach the golden 100%. It’s about talent, skill, ambition and hard work.
In SproutBrain we take ideas that are too good to be ignored and nurture them into thriving businesses. As a co-founder, I’m proud to be able to add my skills, my mojo, into this winning team.

In Usinde (user interface design), where I also am the CEO and co-founder, I work as an user interface architect. Besides developing our own products - like utsokta.se (a concept for visualizing data - for e-commerce sites etc), I do concept design, style guides and UI paradigms. We help your products compete with the very best on the market using our diverse competences and knowledge in new technologies and the possibilities they provide.
