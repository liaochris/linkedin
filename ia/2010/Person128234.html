Larry Schlack’s Summary

Much of my most recent activity has been in the wireless communication site construction industry, Both building communication sites and providing structural inspections and appurtenance audits of communication towers, water tanks and other structures.

I gain a great deal of personal and professional satisfaction from auditing, inspecting and testing of structures and their components in order to provide structural engineers, architects and other concerned professionals the information they need or request to evaluate structures for continued fitness, remediation needs, or simply ensure they are being built in the correct manner.

Something that fits very well into the wireless site construction arena is providing architectural concealment of new and existing wireless sites. My familiarity with site construction and ability to work closely with architectural and engineering professionals allows me to assist Site Concealment, LLC. in providing on time and on budget solutions to architectural concealment needs of wieless carriers, landlords and government agencies

Larry Schlack’s Specialties:

Solving problems - One of my foremost acquired skills is to be able to look at situations and areas of concern and being able to find a solution. Finding a solution is the first part of the story. Third being able to clearly define a course of action (that considers the potential risks and contingencies) and see it through to completion, quite often with hands on involvement ties the whole thing together.
