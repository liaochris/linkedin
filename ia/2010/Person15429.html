Anuradha Udunuwara’s Summary

I have more than 8 years experience in data communication industry. For the past several years, I have been mostly involved in designing, planing, implementing and maintenance of Service Provider Networks. I have designed and implemented SDH and Metro Ethernet based networks with more than 40 Network Elements and 100 Network Elements respectively, to deliver Leased Line, VLL and VPLS services mainly to business customers. Alcatel-Lucent, Cisco, ECI, Huawei, MRV, Tellabs and ZTE are some of the vendors/products that I have worked with. In technology wise, I have been working with PDH, SDH, FR, IP/MPLS, Ethernet and GPON technologies.

Currently I am working on Carrier Ethernet Aggregation, GPON, WiMAX backhauling, NGN/IMS core networks, VSAT networks and network transformation.

Anuradha Udunuwara’s Specialties:

Preparing technical specifications, evaluating products/solutions, conducting testing, designing and planing Service Provider Networks.
