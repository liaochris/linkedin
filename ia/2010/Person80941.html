Heather Schwartz, APR’s Experience

  • Account Supervisor

    Weber Shandwick

    (Public Company; IPG; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    September 2008 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Heather Schwartz specializes in brand-centric public relations and strategic consumer marketing. Heather has more than six years of experience handling the coordination and implementation of product launches, strategic communications programs, events and marketing/brand management initiatives.

    Her current clients include the United States’ Census Bureau, where she serves as a consumer media relations and brand consultant for the 2010 Census project; Amway Global, where her team developed and executed a re-branding campaign designed to raise consumer awareness of the new Amway Global as a company focused on health and wellness and the Egyptian Ministry of Foreign Trade and Alcotexa, where she leads a brand development campaign to educate consumers on authentic Egyptian cotton products. Her passion lies in helping shape brand perceptions of each company’s product and/or service in the minds of consumers.

  • Founder and Chair of Minnesota's Alphabet Bash

    Public Relations Society of America, Minnesota

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2006 — Present (4 years 4 months)

    I founded the Alphabet Bash in 2006 because I saw a need to bring together brilliant marketing communications minds from around the Twin Cities. The Bash, held every August, bring together the most talented people in Minnesota so share insights, make business connections and build new friendships. This networking event is a joint effort with IABC, AMA, AdFed and MWMC.

  • Brand Public Relations Director

    Maccabee Group

    (Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2004 — September 2008 (4 years 9 months)

    Heather served as brand public relations director at Maccabee Group. During her five years at the Minneapolis-based agency, Heather oversaw the marketing strategy, campaign development and execution for the agency’s consumer brand and healthcare accounts. Heather led re-branding efforts and development of cause marketing platforms for OfficeMax, which included campaigns like the viral sensation “ElfYourself,” the brand positioning “World’s Largest Rubberband Ball” campaign and the nationwide teacher support initiative “A Day Made Better.” Heather also led media relations and strategic communication efforts for Tastefully Simple, CIGNA Behavioral Health and national consumer awareness campaigns for American Medical Systems.

    Heather has received a number of awards for her work including the RACie “Innovative Marketing Campaign of the Year” for OfficeMax’s “World’s Largest Rubberband Ball,” the Promo Interactive Marketing Award for Interactive Viral Promotion for OfficeMax’s “ElfYourself” and the Halo Silver Award for Special Event of the Year for OfficeMax’s “A Day Made Better.”
