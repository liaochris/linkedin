Andrew B. Abrams’s Summary

Andrew B. Abrams leads our Wisconsin and Illinois offices for Out Of The Box Technology. He is one of the nation’s leading QuickBooks® and Microsoft consultants, providing Information Technology and Accounting Software Solutions to small and medium-sized businesses across the country.

With more than 15 years experience in accounting, management information systems, retail and sales, Andrew has guided many business owners on how to improve their productivity through the use of their technology. Andrew was one of the first consultants in the country to be certified by Microsoft as a Small Business Specialist and has won numerous awards from Intuit for sales and service as a charter member of the Intuit Solution Provider Program and QuickBooks® Point of Sale Retail Solution Provider Program. Andrew has been a featured speaker for Intuit, Microsoft and the Sleeter Group for webinars and conventions nationwide.

Andrew received a Master of Science degree in Management Information Systems and a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in Accounting, both from the University of Wisconsin—Milwaukee, Sheldon B. Lubar School of Business. Andrew is an Advanced Certified QuickBooks® ProAdvisor, a Certified QuickBooks® Enterprise and Point of Sale ProAdvisor, a Certified QuickBooks® ProAdvisor, a Microsoft Certified Small Business Specialist, a Microsoft Certified Professional and a Sleeter Group Certified Consultant.
