Trevor Thomas’s Summary

1997-2004: Founded LightWerx Graphix. Began doing small Graphic Design & Illustration projects for local clients in Phoenix, AZ. LightWerx Graphix became LightWerx Media, moved to Ashland, OR in 2002 and went Global. Graduated from the Income Builders International Forum, a free-enterprise high-impact seven-day residency business training program. Learned the skills needed to start my own business, and gained the support to make it succeed. IBI teaches complete business skills for success. Worked for The Country Register, a small newspaper, doing ad layout & design. Director of Advertising & Design on the Board of Directors for Keys to Music Recording, Inc. Completed the PSI-World Seminar, a program designed to further a better understanding of human relations. Co-founded the Digital Domain Multimedia Company in Youngtown, AZ. Worked at Copy Club doing a variety of copy & print-related jobs for walk-in clients. Worked at DigitAll One, doing projects for multiple clients, & assisting in the creation of ConsciousOne. Worked at ConsciousOne, designing courses and flash movies, as well as the WisdomFlash site, and portions of the ConsciousOne site.

2004-2005: Completed a number of projects for some more well-known clients, such as James Twyman, Stephen Simon & Neale Donald Walsch. Began working with Global Event Source & Passalong Concepts. Hired subcontractors to take on new projects & administrative functions. Started developing & implementing systems & processes for the different functions of LightWerx Media.

PRESENT: Currently working on multiple projects for new & past clients, & continuing to manage the development of LightWerx Media. Click Here to see my Porftolio
