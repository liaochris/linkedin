Allan Cuttle’s Summary

Allan is currently the Risk Practice Director and Senior Risk Management Consultant with Idea Integration (a subsidiary of Adecco Group North America). Allan is responsible for providing Operational Risk Management, Enterprise Risk Management and Integrated Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) services and solutions to financial services clients. One main service is looking at a company’s current spend on all risk, control and audit activities (including supporting Risk and Compliance technologies) to better align and coordinate control and risk activities across the enterprise with an eye on reducing risk, compliance and audit cost and reducing the burden on revenue generating business units.
Prior to Idea Integration, Allan was a Senior Manager within the Risk Transformation Practice at Ernst and Young, LLP. Within Ernst and Young, he helped to build the Risk Transformation Practice. Prior to Ernst and Young, Allan was a Managing Director of Sales and Business Development within BPS’ (Business Propulsion Systems) Governance, Risk and Compliance Technology Solutions Practice.
Prior to joining BPS, Allan was a Managing Director in PWC's Advisory Practice in NY where he provided Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) consulting services to clients. Prior to joining PWC, Allan was the founding partner of Operational Risk Enterprise, Inc. (ORE), a boutique Technology Solutions and Management Consulting Company specializing in ERM, ORM and Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance.
Prior to founding ORE, Allan was the Deputy Chief Operational Risk Officer (CRO OR) for Deutsche Bank (DB), where he directed the global implementation of the DB Operational Risk (OR) Framework. Prior to that Allan headed up Operational Risk Management for the Private Client Asset Management (PCAM) division of Deutsche Bank (DB).Allan is also a frequent public speaker, trainer and author on the subject of ORM, ERM, and GRC frameworks.
