Amy Jantzer (amy@weedott.com)’s Summary

Since my family was in the military, I’ve had the pleasure of living all over the place…Japan, Hawaii, Washington, Virginia, Arizona and Florida just to name a few. The moving around conditioned me to adjust to change and make the most of my situation. I thrive on new challenges and love to meet new people.

My newest business, weedott, started in late 2008 and I’ve loved it every minute of it! I basically took the knowledge and schooling I have of technology and digital marketing and paired it with the real life experience I gained working with hundreds of businesses over the years. The result was a company that helps businesses and entrepreneurs navigate the digital marketing mayhem and make sense of it for their particular business. I’m proud to say that I have built this business mostly through networking, word of mouth referrals and recommendations. I believe that people buy from people and not companies…so my goal is to be that go-to person. And if I can’t do it…trust me, I have some amazing people that I work closely with who can.

My goal for 2010 is to expand my network through travel and public speaking. I also plan to diversify my network even more so that my clients never have to go any further than me and my network to get products or services they need.
