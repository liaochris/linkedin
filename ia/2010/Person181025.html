Sam Ceccola’s Summary

I have over 19 years experience in the design and delivery of information technology systems for business and government, with a focus on distributed architectures including Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA). I am well known as a technology leader in these fields by Industry, Customers and Partners. During my career I have obtained experience in many different vertical markets, including Public Sector (Intelligence Community, Department of Defense, and Civilian Government), Health Care, Pharmaceutical, Manufacturing, Telecommunications, Financial Services and others.

I have a diverse background in many technology areas as well, from hardware all the way to applications. I have excellent presentation skills, and I am considered an industry leader. I am very active in the Industry with a resume that contains conference keynotes and presentations, leading or participating in several industry forums including Open Group (Board of Directors), Industry Advisory Council, IJIS, OMG and others. I also sit on the Board of Advisors for several other technology companies
