Kevin Fleming’s Summary

My career encompasses experience at nearly all levels of providing supports and services to both adults and children with disabilities.

Eleven years experience with the Supports for Community Living Program, a Medicaid waiver program supporting people with Mental Retardation/Developmental Disabilities.

My previous position was the Program Manager of the state's IMPACT Plus Program, which is a Medicaid program providing therapeutic services to children with a severe, persistent Axis I diagnosis.

I now work for the Department of Public Advocacy as an Advocate for People with Disabilities.

Kevin Fleming’s Specialties:

Regulatory interpretation and compliance, ensuring the rights of people with disabilities, state review preparation, documentation compliance, program expansion, policy and procedure development, mediation with those not satisfied with the agency's supports, and crisis management.
