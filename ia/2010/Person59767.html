Drew Stevens PhD’s Summary

Drew Stevens PhD, (aka) Dr. Drew is an international expert in sales and marketing. Dr. Drew assists organizations to dramatically accelerate revenue.

Drew’s philosophy is based on two core principles. First his 25-year research proves that 92 % of selling individuals do not have a proper methodology, which impacts relationships and more importantly closing ratios. Thus he helps sales leaders invoke strategy and methods that aid sales efficiency. He has then discovered that 96% of organizations lack sound strategic principles that aid in customer retention. His proprietary formula MASS© helps organizations to dramatically retain and acquire clients.

Dr. Drew conducts over 50 presentations per year in over 20 countries. He is also the author of over 100 articles on sales, and sales strategy. In addition, he is quoted frequently in the media, with periodicals such as Personal Selling Power and Sales and Marketing Management, The New York Times, The New York Daily News, and Chicago Tribune.

Dr. Drew’s first book Split Second Selling is acclaimed for it principles in providing a clear and efficient selling methodology. His next book Ultimate Business Bible assists organizations with 12 strategic principles for ultimate success. Dr. Drew is well known for his widely read posts called Sales Fitness on Sales Gravy dot com. To read more on how Dr. Drew can cure the ills of your selling organization read more at www.stevensconsultinggroup.com
