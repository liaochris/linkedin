Robyn Sharp’s Summary

In the past, I've done a little bit of everything when it comes to an insurance agency - CSR, Sales Producer, and Agency Principal. I have spent the last 8 years working in and owning an Allstate Insurance Agency. My husband, John, and I owned two locations, one in Abilene and one in Granbury, TX.

I have now developed my company, Sharp Marketing, to bring marketing services to captive insurance agency owners. Captive agents are often assumed to be supported by their parent companies but many times this is not the case. They may have more rules to follow but are allowed to act as independent business owners. Because their company provides branded materials, posters, and window clings, many captive agencies allow their own personality and marketing to fall to the wayside. I work to help captive insurance agency owners market, automate, and brand their agencies to stand out from the herd and move into internet marketing, marketing automation, lead and telemarketing services, and agency branding.
