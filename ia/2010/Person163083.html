Peter Shepard’s Summary

Mr. Shepard is Managing Director of Athena Business Solutions. Peter has spent his career helping small and medium sized companies “get to the next level” of operational and financial growth – often working through difficult periods of distress or restructuring in the interim. Prior to founding Athena, Peter held senior positions with Venture Capital Funds and a Family Office and was CFO and CEO of several operating companies. He has worked in a variety of industries including business services, software, alternative energy, financial services, and commercial real estate.
