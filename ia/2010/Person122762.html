Lavinia Weissman’s Summary

I am an expert in the application of thought leadership into practice for health and the environment drawing on skills in organizational behavior, knowledge management and social media. I devise educational driven methods of of leading change for global companies, associations of professionals that impact numerous sectors engaged in effecting triple bottom line activity that impacts the environment, health and society.

The thought leaders and partners that I work with have successfully integrated their ideas into practice as it relates to pay for performance and corporate social responsibility.

I have produced web based media that is available for syndication to any company, examining its culture and introducing its social network (market, multi-sector partners and employees) to the opportunity of sustainable change.

My subject matter expertise includes: Work Practices related to Leadership for Change, WorkEcology, Environment, Health and Sustainability.

I am a journalist with Strategy + Business. I have written for HR.com, Boston Women's Business Magazine, SuccessFactors Blog, and numerous other publications.

My recent articles are on the environment, health and sustainability. I have developed curriculum and education programs for leaders who want to lead change in social network and influence change within core groups of economic decision makers.

Lavinia Weissman’s Specialties:

I have an exceptional intuitive skill that I draw from for my executive coaching practice that sparks leaders to draw from the practices of Core Group Theory and it's related practices to successfully introduce to market and society innovations aligned with SRI principles, the Earth Charter's Precautionary Principle and the UN Foundation agenda for the millenial workforce, women and creation of new jobs/economy.
