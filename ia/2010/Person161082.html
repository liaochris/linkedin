Lima Consulting Group, LLC (LCG) serves organizations that seek lasting improvements through the strategic management of technology.

LCG offers a range of professional services that compliment its strategy consulting services, including online marketing, web development, business intelligence solutions, customer relationship management and general ledger solutions, business process consulting, and project management.
