Dave Wedemire’s Summary

Many real estate investors are now saying that the real estate investing market is dead. However, if you change your business model to one of real estate syndication, then you’ll get direct access to all the cash you’ll need.

Syndication is investors with money but not expertise required to close deals. They limit exposure and risk by spreading the money over a number of syndicates.

In a typical syndicate the investor purchases a share of the investment and holds it for a specific period of time. Earn an exponential income when you syndicate your investing business.
