Lisa King’s Summary

I worked in furniture retail management and divisional training, for 18 years. I managed retail furniture stores, such as Rhodes and Rooms To Go, for most of these years, which were multi-million dollar retail stores. I was also a Divisional Sales Trainer, for Rhodes Furniture, in 1996 & 1997. During my years at Rooms To Go, I also trained new managers, as well as new sales people.
My husband and I had a Brick Paving Contracting business, which started in 2003, and in 2006 I achieved a Swimming Pool Contractors License, and we added new swimming pool construction, to the business.
I have since added my Real Estate License, in the state of Florida. I am currently back in the furniture industry with Hudsons Furniture.
