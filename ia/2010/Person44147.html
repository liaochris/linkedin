Craig Husa’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    NeuralIQ, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer & Network Security industry)

    November 2009 — Present (1 year )

    NeuralIQ (www.neuraliq.com) closes the existing hole in network security that is reflected by the numerous reports of successful cyber attacks and intellectual property thefts. The NeuralIQ EventHorizon appliance provides real-time attack capture and forensics. It sits inside the perimeter network defenses and provides visualizations, actionable intelligence and signatures for the malicious attacks that slip in, whether by social engineering, unpatched or unknown flaws in software or in any other way. Instead of the typical weeks or months for discovery, EventHorizon alerts of the attack in minutes and provide the ability to immediately disable and cleanse it from the network. NeuralIQ has offices in Kirkland, Wash., Alexandria, Va., and Santa Monica, Calif.
