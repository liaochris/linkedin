Jill Ory’s Summary

My professional and educational experience is in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) analysis and Water Resources Analysis. I have worked in this field for 10 years. I am particularly interested in data analysis and presentation that helps tell a story of environmental conditions, and elucidates strategic opportunities for improvements. I am interested in working with all types of clients from non-profit to government, to private sector.

Jill Ory’s Specialties:

Geodatabase development and management, mobile GIS, data presentation, culvert analysis, global thinking and problem solving.
