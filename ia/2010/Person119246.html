Kristine (Kris) Carey’s Summary

Got MOJO? Kristine is the MOJO Maven.

Bringing solo, micro entrepreneurs and small business owners the clarity that’s required to build and maintain a successful business/life mix and get their MOJO back. Her approach creates increased financial success, ease, and fun!

COACHING:
Experienced coach, designing custom programs that enable business owners and entrepreneurs to move their business and life forward.

Her private, one on one sessions include:
• Clarification of desired goals and outcomes as well as steps to overcome inevitable obstacles
• Individual homework and exercises and lifestyle design
• Creating an environment of support and accountability
Results produced include an increased sense of ease and flow, better ability to play to one’s strengths, renewed sense of motivation and optimism, and More Financial Success and Fun.

SPEAKING:
Experienced speaker, specializing in helping solo, micro entrepreneurs, and small business owners to get inspired, motivated, clear and ready to act. Her natural style translates quickly, putting audiences at ease so they can hear her message and take inspired action.

As a result of her experiential talks, audiences walk away with:
• A shift in perspective from one of a trade-offs to one of opportunities
• The resources needed to achieve desired business/life results
• An interactive experience designed to inspire, using exercises that propel them forward
• A changed view of business and life from an exercise in conformity to one of freedom and creativity

Available for the following presentation topics (or a title developed around your theme):
• Getting Your Mojo Back: Let Your Business Turn You On!
• Use Your Mojo Daily: Making Difficult Decisions Easier
• Mojo and Your Natural Rhythm: Maximize Your Time and Energy
• Be Outrageous: How To Hook into Your Mojo To Create A Successful Business
• Discover Your Super Powers: How To Use Your Super Powers For Good and Not For Evil
