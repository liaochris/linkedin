Laurie Meyers’s Summary

I'm an award-winning writer with over 12 years of experience in magazine, newsletter, web and copywriting. Currently I specialize in health and science writing, but I also have experience in other subject areas such as travel, shopping and general interest. My writing and reporting has appeared in publications such as People, Diabetes Forecast and Monitor on Psychology.
