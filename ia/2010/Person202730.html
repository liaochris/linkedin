Ritesh Sheth’s Summary

Ritesh has over ten years of combined consulting experience in finance, pricing strategy, financial forecasting, profitability analysis, account management, sales, marketing strategy, business process analysis and systems integration.

Currently, Ritesh is the Interim East Sales Controller for the Global 500 business at Dell. Responsibilities include sales controllership activities for the $1.5B Eastern Global 500 Accounts and a direct staff, pricing guidance for the account team, opportunity reviews requiring finance input/approval, pricing assurance with established contracts, account team quotas, collection status reviews and engagement on past due accounts, potential credit issues, and extended payment terms requests. Ritesh is also responsible for driving the account team to achieve revenue, margin, and strategic targets based upon historical actuals, opportunity identification captured in the pipeline reporting, and forward looking analytics. Additional responsibilities include closing activities such as accruals for future obligations and revenue deferrals for recorded revenue not yet truly recognized.

Over the past 3 years, Ritesh provided special pricing and financially guided $300M in quarterly revenue for Dell’s global customers, structured multimillion dollar global deal P&Ls for executive level review, and negotiated pricing and presented financial overviews to customers during executive briefings.

His most recent research and development is on financial modeling for Dell and his own real estate investments.
