Andrew Fuchs’s Summary

I am a highly experienced technical writer with a software development background and a Master's degree in Information Systems.

My early professional career was as a software engineer, programming microprocessors for video games and computer audio software. I turned to technical writing while I lived in Europe, and this has been my primary trade ever since -- with occasional relapses into analysis, programming, and project management.

I have designed, written, indexed, and managed the development of many types of publications, including:

- administrator, programmer, and user guides
- requirements, design, and test specifications
- online help and quick-reference guides
- brochures and other marketing collateral
- websites and web-based content
- process documentation for quality certification
- internal business process documentation

My software development work has mainly involved tightly constrained microprocessor code, such as sound and protocol drivers, mostly written in C and assembly language. But I have also written higher-level applications and all kinds of tooling and testing scripts, as well as a broad range of requirement, design, and test specifications.

Much of my writing work has been targeted at developers, administrators, and other very technical users, and has mainly involved complex enterprise infrastructure software. But I also enjoy writing for non-technical audiences.

I speak French well, Spanish somewhat less, and my Italian is very much in need of practice. I know a little bit of German and am a beginning student of Russian.

Throughout my life I have also had a passion for sound and music production. From my first radio transmitter that I built at age nine to the small concert sound system that I currently use for bands and music camp, I manage to keep some involvement with the amplified and recorded performing arts.

Andrew Fuchs’s Specialties:

Indexing, information design, structured documentation (DocBook XML) content management systems, leadership and project management.
