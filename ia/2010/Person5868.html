Alessandro Pacetti’s Summary

As a Marketing Consultant I manage various aspects of business developement both offline and online as: market analysis, requirements generation, SEO, SEM, Vidubations, Social Ads, Analytics, Google Adwords, Outreach, etc..

I've worked in Babelgum as a marketing consultant and I follow various internal marketing campaigns, in particular I apply on vidubations, widget ceations, buzz marketing, social networking, blogs: the result of this was 150000 views in two weeks of campaign.

Working with MID, I followed various project of product design for Telecom Italia, Master, Onda, Pirelli, radiators design for Deltacalor, packaging concepts. Also, I helped the company in PR and New Business.

Working for Senzamedia.com I managed different skills like the content selection and marketing campaigns collaborating with Red Cell, Wpp Group.

Working with Lorenzo Palmeri, I followed various project of forniture design for Arthema, design of musical instruments for Noah (Paraffina Slapster played by Lou Reed), light design for Valenti Luce, fair stands, communication design.

Working at Design Faculty at Politecnico di Milano I’ve supported more than sixty students, I helped them in their projects and learning Alias studio.

During the internship in Communicationdesign I was engaged in various projects as fair stands, forniture, brand image, individually and also in team.

Collaboration with the video director Arch. Elsa Milani.
Collaboration with the photographer Enrico Magri.
Collaboation with the video director and Artist Sergio Attardo

From march 2005 to march 2008 I worked with T.E.A.M. a.g. as event organizer for home match days of A.C. Milan and F.C.Internazionale in UEFA Champions League.
