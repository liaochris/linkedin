Sara Rimmerman’s Summary

An analyst and consultant, Sara Rimmerman focuses on developing internal measurement dashboards of key human resources trends for Fleishman-Hillard leadership. In addition to providing management with research information for various aspects of strategic planning, she also serves in a leadership role for the company’s industry-leading internship program and coordinates the semiannual promotion process.

Ms. Rimmerman is an active member of the Society for Human Resources Management and the Soulard Restoration Group, a St. Louis-based community organization devoted to the preservation of one of the country’s historic French neighborhoods. She graduated magna cum laude from Saint Louis University, where she received a dual honors bachelor’s degree in psychology and international studies.
