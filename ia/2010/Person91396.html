Jay Cataldo’s Summary

Jay Cataldo is a sought-after life coach who passionately helps his clients break free of their perceived limitations and effortlessly design the impeccable lifestyle they desire. He has also authored the book, "Get Your Girl Back, A Proven System To Win Her Back And Never Lose Her Again" and advises men and women on how to enhance their social skills and manage their romantic relationships.

Jay is the CEO of Relate Right Inc. and a member of The International Coach Federation (ICF). Over the last ten years, he has studied numerous self-help techniques in an effort to better himself and his lifestyle and uses a combination of these tools (such as self-hypnosis, NLP, EFT, Focusing and more) in his coaching practice.
