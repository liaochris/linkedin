Brad DePew’s Summary

Accomplished sales entrepreneur and business executive in technology industry with track record of driving strategic revenue growth, building productive sales teams, positively impacting profitability and delivering market share growth results.

Career spans over 15 years delivering annual revenues up to $50 million in national, regional and vertical roles as a manager or individual contributor for both large and early stage companies in the US Western Region and across North America.

Highly successful in executing solution selling to achieve desired peformance metrics, closing large deals in major accounts and building multi-million business growth. Also extensive leadership and expertise managing revenue generating sales teams and related field operations and controlling P&L's.

Over the past 3 years DePew has been rebuilding enterprise sales teams to improve sales productivity and achieve multi-million dollar revenue growth in the role of West Region Director at both Adobe and BEA Systems. This includes managing $20 million business from direct, channel and inside sales as well as fostering teamwork with SE's, field marketing, professional services for the region.

During 1999 to 2003 DePew was instrumental in growing Mercator Software from $30 million to over $120 million He was promoted several times from West Region Manager to Managing Director of Healthcare to Managing Director of Manufacturing, Retail and Distribution. In the final 6 months prior to the company's acquisition DePew was the West Area Managing Director overseeing all sales and business activities across the Telco, Financial Services, MRD and Healthcare verticals in western North America.

DePew has also been successful in various sales and management positions at national, vertical and regional levels for IBM, Metastorm and Online Resources & Communications since 1997.
