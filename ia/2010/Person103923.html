Jonathan Lehrer’s Summary

Founded as "Pukelis & Lehrer Communications, Inc." in 2005, we are now doing business as Jonathan Lehrer Communications, also known as "The Message Engineers."

Seasoned public relations consultant who understands the nuances of corporate politics as well as the technology of Web sites and social networking. Strong writer, articulate spokesman, creative strategist, insightful counselor and dynamic brainstorming session facilitator.

Clients include or have included: Illinois Department of Transportation, Illinois Tollway, HNTB Corp., E. Morris Communications, Citgo Petroleum, Will County Center for Economic Development, Foundation for Jewish Camping, Illinois Medical District Guest House, Publicity Club of Chicago, United Synagogue of Conservative Judaism, Fasco Fasteners and others.

Jonathan Lehrer’s Specialties:

Facilitate brainstorming sessions; strategic communications planning; crisis communications; Web site evaluation; printed and electronic newsletters; special events; PowerPoint presentations; media training.

    Jewish Council on Urban Affairs

    (Public Policy industry)

    May 2009 — Present (1 year )

    The mission of the Jewish Council on Urban Affairs is to combat poverty, racism and anti-Semitism in partnership with Chicago’s diverse communities. Guided by prophetic Jewish principles, JCUA pursues social and economic justice for Chicago’s most vulnerable neighborhoods by promoting a vision of empowering communities from within. Since 1964, JCUA has assisted groups in low-income and minority communities, built coalitions with diverse groups, advocated on issues of poverty and racism and mobilized a Jewish constituency to create a more just city.
