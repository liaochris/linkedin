Pat Lang’s Summary

I am a strategic out of the box thinker that has a passion for working with web 2.0 startups and media entrepreneurs to turn early stage ideas into tangible interfaces and launchable web and mobile Products.

I have conducted User Experience Activities on a broad range of new venture Products and proof of technology concepts for many companies including NBC Universal, Intel, Sony Connect, Sony Pictures, Sun Microsystems, Discovery Communications, The History Channel, Anytime Media, Ultimate Fighting Championships, Sirius Radio, Prince, NBA, Houston Rockets, General Electric, and Shelter (Venture) Capital Partners, to name a few.

Pat Lang’s Specialties:

Hands on Product Management and User Experience Design. As a Certificate holder from Human Factors International, I advocate a User Centered Design Process via Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Wireframing, Prototyping, and Visual design. I use industry standard techniques and research methodology to gather and analyze user data such as contextual inquiry, ethnography, user observations, user interviews, persona writing, and formal and informal Usability Testing methodologies.
