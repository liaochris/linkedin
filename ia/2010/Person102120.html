John Germinario (jgermina@aerotek.com)’s Summary

My career with Aerotek began as a Recruiter for the Commercial division specializing in manufacturing, distribution and warehouse personnel. After being promoted to an Account Recruiting Manager in that same division I made the switch over to Aerotek Scientific. I now manage accounts in our Commercial division and also recruit full-time for our Scientific division.

The industries I now specialize in at Aerotek Scientific are with pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, medical device, diagnostics and chemical companies.

Please feel free to give me a call at 973-560-5065 or send me an email at jgermina@aerotek.com if you would like to find out more about my available opportunities.
