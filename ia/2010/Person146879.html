Misael Flores’s Summary

Misael is the managing director and principal shareholder of AME Consulting Group, a leading professional services company in Belize since 1997, and principally engaged in Offshore Corporate & Trust Services ; Realty & Real Estate Development ; and Consulting.

Prior to establishing his own consulting business, Misael was a shareholder of KPMG Corporate Services (Belize) Ltd., the offshore corporate services division of the then KPMG Peat Marwick, in Belize. He was the managing director and responsible for setting up the division. Misael was a member of the financial team of BHI Corporation (now Carlisle Holdings) from 1994-1996. During that time BHI operated and invested in selected growth industries in Central America and the Caribbean Region. Its core business activities were banking and financial services, and construction and property development. Part of his responsibilities at BHI was the management of the Belize IBC Registry operated by an affiliated company of BHI under a concession agreement with the Belize Government.

Misael has also held other positions in Belize at various levels including as Financial Controller at Ramada Royal Reef Resort & Marina and doing audit work with Deloitte & Touche, Price Waterhouse and Pannell Kerr Forster.

Currently Misael is the President-Elect and a founding member of the Belize National Association of REALTORS®, President of the Belize Business Bureau, and Treasurer of the Belize Offshore Practitioners Association. He is a Justice of the Peace for the country of Belize since 2001 and is a former member of the Rotary Club of Belize.
