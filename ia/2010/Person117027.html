  • President / Founder

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Retail industry)

    September 1985 — June 1996 (10 years 10 months)

    Kim's Khocolate began as a candy making hobby when I was eleven years old. As a senior at Bishop Walsh High School, I hand made 18,000 candy bars for a school fund raiser. I transitioned my hobby to a business at the age of 18. I created new products from the recipe stage through packaging to production. I headed sales to wholesale accounts, to the general public through a retail store, and to corporate customers. I contracted custom designed pieces for Apple Computer, AT&T, The Rolling Stones, Range Rover, and many more. I headed up all public relations for Kim's Khocolate and got the company in national publications such as USA Today, Inc. Magazine, People, and Barron's.
