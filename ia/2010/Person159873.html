Patrick Flynn, CFB’s Summary

Patrick has been directly involved in franchising & business development for more than 25 years. His experience offers a diverse background; from assisting in the development of what is now the world’s largest embroidery & graphics franchise to operating two small independent and franchise businesses. Patrick's background allows him to adjust to both large and small corporations, yet focus on the individual needs of clients that he is associated with.

Patrick has been a franchisee and entrepreneur, becoming involved in franchising initially in 1983 with Minuteman Press International (Now the World's largest Printing Franchise). He went on to hold the position of Regional Vice President with the sister company of Minuteman Press (United Franchise Group), a multi million dollar international franchising resource with 1,400 franchise locations in 50 countries and five brands: SIGNARAMA, EmbroidMe, Billboard Connection, FranchiseMart and Plan Ahead Events.Patrick's primary focus was on franchise sales and franchisee support.

From 2007 - 2009 Patrick was the Senior Development Consultant at iFranchise Group / Franchise Dynamics where he managed the development of emerging franchise concepts; Projects include a Used Car Franchise (Bart’s Car Store), Trick Shots (Pool Hall) and a Home Based Business (IntelliTurf).

Patrick is currently a Franchise / Business Development Consultant at Wausau Homes, based in Wausau, Wisconsin. The Wausau Homes Business System enables entrepreneurs to become premier builders in their area. The Wausau systems allows owners to be more productive, serve customers better, and reach more prospects.

Patrick Flynn, CFB’s Specialties:

Lease negotiations, employee management and supervision, purchasing, internet marketing, hosting webinars for Prospective franchisees.
