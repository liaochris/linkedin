Naveen Mahendra’s Summary

Naveen is a seasoned procurement specialist with over 9 years experience as a practitioner and operating project roles utilising Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, IT, Telecom & FMCG sector experience and best practice within the procurement domain.

Naveen is a Procurement Solutions Architect, with Wipro BPO with some of the primary activities such as:-
•Build proposal strategy
•Conceptualize & design off-shoring model to prospective clients
•Propose and defend solution
•Showcase domain capability to clients
•Conduct due-diligence and finalize solution
•Support transition and operations team in seamless knowledge acquisition and transfer
•Provide domain specific support across the organization on an ongoing basis.
•Build/Manage Strategic Relationships with prospective / existing partners.
•Providing consulting for both upstream and downstream processes.

Prior to the existing portfolio at Wipro, Naveen was a Global Sourcing Consultant within Infosys's Procurement Practice - in their unique service offering within ITes/BPO Sector, before he moved out to take up the position of heading CRM/SCM & Globalisation projects with Cypress Solutions Pvt Ltd. He has a wealth of experience in strategic sourcing, outsourcing, Supply Chain, Operations & Delivery Mgmt., Business Development, Programme Management, Pre-sales/ Concept Marketing in diverse domains - IT Services, e-Business Solutions and corporate change management.

His hands-on experience in creating business value through leadership of Procurement transformation efforts and planning, delivery and support of large-scale ERP & Spend Management solutions for Global companies has added exponential value to his end customers.

He also holds a PG Diploma in Materials Management & Diplomas' in the area of Supply Chain, Business Administration & Computer Application. In addition he holds about 25+ other certification in the Procurement / Supply Chain domain.

Naveen Mahendra’s Specialties:

Lead procurement transformation programs for various global companies through procurement best practices - Spend Analysis, Strategic Sourcing (Cost Modelling, TCO Analysis, Commodity Strategies), RFx process, Negotiation, Process re-design, Business intelligence, Bid Management (design, award-scenario), Contract Mgt.
