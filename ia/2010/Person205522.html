Todd Parker, M.A., M.S.’s Summary

Education & Certifications: Masters in Exercise Physiology, Certified USA Cycling Coach, Endurance Sports Trainer, Personal Trainer, Strength & Conditioning Specialist; and Former Pro Triathlete

Specialty Training: Functional Strength Training; Endurance Sports (e.g. cycling, triathlon, running…) training prescription and periodization; Mental Training (e.g. competitive anxiety, negative thought-stopping, mental imagery…) Fueling, Hydration, and Electrolyte Balance for long distance endurance events; as well as facilitating individualized goal specific neuromuscular and metabolic adaptations.

Why did I choose this profession? I train clients because I am passionate about helping others improve their overall fitness, health, functionality, and quality of life. Whether for fitness or athletic performance, I assist in setting and achieving goals that positively change lifestyles, as well as realize optimal performance potentials.

Interests/Hobbies: Cycling, Strength Training, Hiking, Camping, Spectating all sports, and Reading current research and non-fiction

Favorite Type of Client: My favorite type of client is one who is disciplined and willing to commit to what it takes to achieve goals and maintain those positive lifestyle changes.

Motto: “Working on your weaknesses is like castor oil; it may not taste good, but it sure is good for ya!”

Todd Parker, M.A., M.S.’s Specialties:

Trained in Physiological Testing: Lactate Threshold, VO2max, and RMR; analyze results, and prescribe training to improve performance.

Experience with coaching swimmers, cyclists, runners, and triathletes of all ages and ability levels. Perform fitness and biomechanical assessments, prescribe daily training, assess progress, advise athletes and physicians on rehabilitative modalities, and prepare athletes to reach peak optimal physical and psychological performance levels for competitions.
