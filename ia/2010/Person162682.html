Peter J. Di Stefano’s Summary

Pete has over 28 years of technical, sales, and marketing leadership experience. More than half of his career has been in sales and marketing. He has built, managed, and grown, product marketing, product management, and marketing communications teams. He spent 18 years with IBM where he left as their North American Brand Manager for Intel based servers. During Pete's 7 years with BMC Software he ran Product Management, Field Marketing, and R&D. Pete was part of the team that set up BindView to be acquired by Symantec in January 2006 and then owned the responsibility for Symantec's flagship compliance and security management offerings.

Pete's goal is to assume a senior role in a fast growing, aggressive, high tech company and to leverage his diverse sales, marketing and product experience along with a contagious passion and commitment to winning.

Peter J. Di Stefano’s Specialties:

Senior Leadership in product management, product and field marketing, and marketing communications for high tech software and hardware.
