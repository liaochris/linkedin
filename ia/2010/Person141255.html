Meri Kenaz’s Summary

"Meri Ké's voice comes close to capturing the passion and intensity of human nature. The power and passion of her playing does true justice to the incredible quality of her voice" - Isabel Rawlins (CUE)

“blindingly fascinating singer-songwriter… well-constructed songs and strong dramatic capacity…” – Gisele Turner (Tonight.co.za – Independent News & Media)

Soul-Folk” is the closest label I feel comfortable to impose on my music. I aim to let inspiration flow through me, rather than intelectualise my art. My lyrics are poetical and often speak of the dynamics and importance of spiritual and social awareness.

I'm currently recording my second album. One hand packaged EP, "Branches", has already been released, with 2 more to go, each with a slightly different flavour. The final album is due to be released end 2010 or early 2011. I'll be touring my second EP in a national tour starting with White Mountain Folk Festival end of September. Tour dates will be available on www.merikenaz.com and othe online media soon.
