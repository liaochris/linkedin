Jay Gentile’s Summary

Jay Gentile started his first business which was aimed at the computer repair industry. After spending about 4 years in the IT industry it motivated him to push further and discover new opportunities which led him to establish ComputerWerks Web Solutions. His drive and focus is to offer small to large business with a comprehensive online marketing solution.

Jay is a proven expert in the online marketing and IT field; having worked with many different companies from small business to large corporations he has the backbone to support the strength and determination of the competitive SEO industry. His entrepreneurship has made him where he is at today in the online marketing industry. You can find him speaking at local seminars discussing seo strategies, online marketing, and more. His in depth understanding and experience of off-line and on-line marketing has played a big role in the success of the company.

Business is not the only thing on Jay's mind, while Jay likes to spend a lot of time focusing on his clients and staff he is sure to make time for his family which he says is imperative. He enjoys also spending time volunteering as the activities director at a local senior living home in his area.
