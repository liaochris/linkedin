Shawna Mansfield’s Summary

Gamut Design Group provides graphic design services for clients worldwide. Here's a little background on Gamut and Shawna.

In 2003 Shawna joined the widely accredited faculty in Santa Fe College’s Digital Media Technology Department as an adjunct professor. She brought the same high standards of expectation from the industry to the classroom. Her fresh and practical approach in the classroom has made her one of the most desired instructors in the program. In 2009 SFC acknowledged her dedication and forward thinking approach to her students with The Provost's Award - honoring excellence in teaching. This was the first year an Adjunct Professor was considered for this prestigious award.

Established in 2006, Gamut Design Group specializes in logo, print, web and multimedia design and has conducted design software seminars for the University of Florida and Central Florida Community College. Shawna has also presented design tips to local networking organizations as a guest speaker. Gamut’s clients range from local small businesses to established firms worldwide. Above all else, Shawna wants Gamut Design Group to be a design company that not only meets the needs of but also informs the client. Showing a client how to save money – or better spend their budget – is unsurpassed satisfaction.
