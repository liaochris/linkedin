Ross Blankenship’s Summary

Ross attended Northfield Mt. Hermon School, Cornell University and Washington University School of Law. While at Cornell, Ross worked with several nonprofits and helped many students get into top colleges. He assisted with Cornell recruiting and admissions and is an active member of the Cornell Alumni Association, serving as former Vice President of the Cornell Annual Fund. Ross also was elected to Cornell's Educational Policy Committee and Academic Integrity Hearing Board, where he helped develop courses and maintain academic integrity amongst the student body.

Ross was also a Division I, Varsity Rower at Cornell University where his boat placed in the top 10 in the country two years in a row.

He has been published in several magazines and journals, including Next Step Magazine, the St. Louis Business Journal and Education.com.

Ross Blankenship is an admissions expert and President of Top Test Prep.
