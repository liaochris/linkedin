Kelsey Jones’s Summary

I manage the social media and search marketing department at VinSolutions, which focuses on CRM, inventory management, website design, and internet marketing for the automotive dealership industry.

I am a dedicated and growing force in the internet marketing industry-- my social media and internet marketing blog, The Social Robot (http://thesocialrobot.com) is regularly featured on Google News and other websites in my industry.
