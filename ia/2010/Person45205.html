Cüneyt Göksu’s Summary

Cuneyt is a highly experienced Information Management consultant specialized in DB2 with over 20 years experience working with a number of clients within the insurance, telecommunications, banking, and manufacturing sectors. In that time he has gained a significant amount of practical knowledge in many aspects of IBM's Information Management products, including experience in application programming, database administration, technical architecture, performance tuning and systems programming. More recently, he has specialised in supporting large-scale implementations of Corebank Applications on DB2 for z/OS.

He is an IBM Gold Consultant and IBM Data Champion and a regular instructor for IBM Academic Initiative Program in Turkey. He is currently the IDUG 2010 Europe Conference Chairperson and member of PSC for IOD 2010.

Cüneyt Göksu’s Specialties:

DB2 and IBM Information Management specialist.

OLTP & Batch Applications, Content Management, BI and Datawarehouse Applications such as CRM, Campaign Management, E-Business, Multidatabase Connectivity and Integration, DRDA Applications, Data Replication, Parallel Sysplex and DataSharing Implementation, Data Modeling, DB2 Installation, Migration and Integration, System & Application Performance and Tuning including SQL Performance, Application Design, Disaster Recovery, HADR
