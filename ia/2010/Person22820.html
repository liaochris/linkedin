Dr Beth Meriam’s Summary

Are you using your FULL POTENTIAL?

* Are you fulfilling your potential as a LEADER?
* Is your TEAM everything you would like it to be and is your team's impact fully recognized by others?
* Is your ORGANIZATION successfully navigating the stormy waters of today’s complex world?
* And are your leaders, teams and organization working at their best with each other? If not, do you know why?

We help you perform at your best

Most organizations draw on only a fraction of their human potential. At Meriam International we specialise in dramatically increasing results by harnessing the full potential of people. We help you cultivate purpose, passion and productivity, both individually and collectively. With us, you can build leadership and team capacity, foster trust, loyalty and belonging, bring plans to life and ignite purposeful action and create a thriving organisational culture.

Helping you generate your own solutions:

* We ask good questions, and we help you ask good questions so that everyone can understand how your organization works in practice

* We involve as many of your people as possible. The greatest positive potential is released when people collaborate, communicate, and leverage their collective intelligence, inspiration and drive

* We show you how to develop individuals and teams to create a learning, purposeful and dynamic organizational culture

* We are oriented towards action and sustained transformation

Recent work includes interventions with: Unilever, UK Home Office, BNP Paribas Securities Services, Telefónica, Vedior, ING, Girl Scouts USA, Watson Wyatt, Crowe Chizek and the UK Department for International Development. Beth has worked as an advisor on the integration of strategy and leadership programmes (e.g. Columbia and IMD). An effective communicator, she is practised in a number of languages and speaks fluent Spanish. 

Dr Beth Meriam’s Specialties:

Meriam International uses a range of tools for this work, including:

• In-depth corporate assessments (MBTI, Cultural Transformation Tools, SenseMaker social software)
• Coaching and one-on-one leadership development
• Action learning, action-focused research and participatory methodologies
• Self enquiry and future search
• Facilitating business critical conversations using dialogue, World Café and collaborative communication
• Developing change-agents within the organisation
