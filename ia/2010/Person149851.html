Martin Spang Olsen’s Summary

My goal is to introduce to people their own potential and teach them how to use it. I wish to give people an easy access to themselves - and hand them the key to unlimited power.

My experience stretches from consulting in the corporate world, film- and theatre work, pictorial art, music composing and production, martial arts, stunt work and pedagogic concepts.

My background is in primarily in the movie business as well as in arts. My formal education is Musicologist (MA) and Classic History, but since childhood I am privately trained in a great number of creative, artistic and physical techniques.

I have worked in the movies and the theatre business with stunt work, singing, dancing, acting and directing since 1983. Along the way, I have written academic books, released CD's, held pictorial exhibitions and given lectures.

I ran Stuntskolen (The Stuns School) in Copenhagen from 1983 - 97, and have been founding and managing several other school concepts since then.

Martin Spang Olsen’s Specialties:

Lectures and seminars based on: Creativity, innovation, entrepreneurship, communication, Human Resources, stress control, Western and Eastern history, comparative religion, spirituality, classical music, stunt work, film work, stage fighting, singing, dancing, martial arts, acting, Feng Shui, Qi-Gung (Chi Kung)
