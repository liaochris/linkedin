Fabio Ferrari’s Summary

Fabio Ferrari is a muti-lingual international executive with over 20 years experience in management positions where his leadership talent and business acumen have successfully brought companies through situations such as mergers and acquisitions, reorganization and international expansion. His strategic vision, commercial acumen and solid marketing expertise are one side of the coin but what Fabio believes is that “people make it happen”.

This translates into his leadership style which is to empower people by creating opportunities to develop technical and soft skills resulting in organizational and individual growth and development. Fabio’s “modus operandi” is about preparing and enabling people to face challenges, contribute, and make a difference. This has proven to be effective, not only in terms of bottom line results and exceeding targets, but in growing and retaining talent. Fabio believes in the potential of human capital.

Best described as an intuitive communicator with a straightforward, approachable, interactive and hands on style, Fabio has an uncanny ability to seemingly absorb languages.

Fabio’s academic credentials include an MBA, completed while holding a General Manager France position. Fabio has also taken courses on leadership and personal development not only for his own growth but to learn about new methods to incorporate into the sales and training programs he designs for his staff.
