Lorraine Flett’s Summary

I am a multi-faceted project manager and film/video producer with a background in advertising, marketing, public relations, and website development. I freelance as a content and website developer and social media marketer to which I bring a unique set of creative and analytical skills to provide both strategic and tactical solutions.

I am facile with technology and Web savvy, which is why I am called on to research story and pitch ideas with a fast turnaround. I also ghost write articles about technology and communications on the Web.
