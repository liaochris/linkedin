Ridho Putradi’s Summary

I started my journey in IT world as an application developer. That time I use Visual Basic as my development tool. After I graduated from the university, I joined one of the largest research company based in Bogor, CIFOR (Central Information For Forestry Research). That was what makes me interested in Web Developing. I thought that every application should be available online so that everyone can accessed from everywhere.

Web developing became my passion since that time.Developing websites and web application is my primary job until today.

In 2006, me and my friends are joined to establish a web developing community in Cianjur called CWEBBER, stand for Cianjur Web Developing Community. We were gathered each week to talk anything connected to Web Developing. It was fun, we share a lot of information. The community are still there, but since we've a lot of activity we never gather around anymore.

Now, beside working as web developer. I'm also running an SEO consultant called SEO Eleven (SEOEleven.Com). With SEO Eleven I've created a complete SEO solution called "Smart Eleven" (http://seoeleven.com/smart-eleven).

Internet, website and Google will always be part of my life journey.
