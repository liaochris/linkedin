INTERIM RESOURCES
Bridges has a network of senior financial executives, such as controllers and chief financial officers that work on an interim basis to:

Serve as part-time controller or Interim CFO during initial growth period, or pre-revenue.
Strengthen management team during executive turnover or absence.
Complete special projects, such as financings, restructurings, cost reductions, systems implementations.
Added expertise during ownership changes.

The quality of our senior executives ensures the highest level of performance and consistency. All assignments are handled by seasoned professionals with previous experience managing similar situations.
