Janice Masters’s Summary

Highly Intuitive & Practical Spiritual Life Coach and Business Mentor

I have been helping people to live happier, more successful lives for over thirty years, first as a psychotherapist and for the last dozen years as a coach.

My mission is to lead the people I coach to heal the old stories that keep them stuck in life and in business, and teach them how to create a new story that invites true Joy and Success based in authentic living that expresses who they are in very satisfying ways.

I help holistic business owners and world servers help more people and make more money by identifying those who need their help the most and then marketing to them/attracting them in authentic, effective ways.

I support those on a spiritual path who are dealing with life dissatisfaction in the areas of relationship, health, finances, family, etc to find the gifts in their challenges and use them to transform emotionally, spiritually into their best selves in their best life.

Authenticity, spiritual optimism, resiliance and empowerment through choice are the foundations of my work. Using intuitive, visionary gifts and practical solutions I address the whole person and their needs and goals.

Offering group and privcate one to one coaching for your life and your spiritual business.

In my Miami, Florida office and worldwide via the phone and Internet.
