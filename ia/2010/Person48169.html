Dan M. Muntean’s Summary

Mr. Muntean has planned and designed several North African, Middle Eastern and South East Asian Mixed Use Development projects, ranging from new townships in Singapore and Rabat, a residential and hotel development in Abu Dhabi, the American University of Medical Sciences in Kuwait, to Novena Health Care City Concept Master Plan in Singapore. He holds a Masters Degree in Architecture and Urbanism (MIT) and a Bachelor of Architecture Degree (UK), with comprehensive knowledge of all work stages in urban and architectural design practice. Furthermore, he possesses a strong understanding of overall design and construction supervision of high-rise, large-scale residential and small-scale community-commercial projects in Southeast Asia, as well as institutional (museums and libraries), university campuses, and related buildings in the U.S.
