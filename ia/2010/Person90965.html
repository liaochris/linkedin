Jason Shen’s Summary

Hi, my name is Jason Shen and I work with passionate people on innovative projects that matter.

I'm the Chief Operating Officer of The Stanford Daily Publishing Corporation, an independent nonprofit media organization. and I manage the professional and student business staff and financial operations.

Prior to the Daily, I cofounded Gumball Capital, a nationally-recognized social venture that engages students across the country with entrepreneurship in pursuit of ending poverty. I studied Biology and Philosophy at Stanford and earned a BS with honors and an MS.

I have decade of experience as a nationally ranked male gymnast and led the Stanford Men's Gymnastics team as captain to win the NCAA team championships.

Jason Shen’s Specialties:

innovation, business development, entrepreneurship, building teams, new business models, nonprofit management, writing strong marketing copy
