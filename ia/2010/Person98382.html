Okay…here’s a brief summary of who I am and some of my goals:

I am a Husband to my lovely wife Annette, a Dad to my children Julia, Erik, Stephen and Christina thus making me a CED (Chief Executive Dad) which is my most important function in life.

I am a retired Army Aviator who spent over 20 years in the service of our country protecting our nation and our way of life.

I have been involved in the network marketing industry since 1997 during which time I have been the star of a video production that was titled…“They Lied About Your Future” and I am one of the 52 contributing authors of the New Book compiled by friend John Milton Fogg titled…“It’s Time for Network Marketing.”

I live in a very small town called Enterprise Alabama which is located just outside the west gate of Fort Rucker. Fort Rucker holds the distinction of being the Professional Birth Place of over 90% of all of the helicopter pilots trained in the world today. I enjoy the luxury of still training our young Army Aviators how to fly helicopters on a part time basis…that is, when I’m not building my Bazi business.

I also devote some of my time to being a radio personality on shows like the Home Based Business Radio Show hosted by my long time personal friend Mr. Tom Chenault as well as the American Consumer Empowerment Show.

I believe that people are a leader’s greatest asset and I’m dedicated to helping every member of our team to reach for their dreams and change their lives for the better.

One of my personal goals is to help, train and mentor over 1,000 people to become financially free within the next ten years.

If you would like to learn more about me and/or what I do please visit one of my many websites or give me a call and introduce yourself so we can get to know each other a little better.

Remember, Success is a choice…Here’s to yours.
