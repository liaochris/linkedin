Tammy Kelley’s Summary

Good Day and thanks for visiting my profile! My 15 year career has been in the promotion, marketing, and business development of professional services. The most rewarding part of my career has been learning how to identify and meet the challenges that face my clients on a daily basis. The biggest part of this is communication! Listening to the client, asking probing questions, and listening some more helps me learn what their biggest concern is about a project…what wakes them up in a cold sweat at 3AM. Then how do I communicate back to the Client how my company is able to meet those challenges and where we have successfully applied our expertise for other clients. Communication, trust, and quality service…more work…that’s the cycle you must live by to survive in the professional services industry.

Other interests in the business realm include strategic planning and modeling for targeted initiatives. One of the things I helped develop is a graphic model that begins with a client or project in the center and then you add all of the influencers that are known to be associated with the subject…such as contacts, other companies, other projects, etc. As you continue to add to the model you find commonalities that may very well turn a cold call into a welcome introduction. These are the situations that I find fascinating and what continues to draw me to the area of business development.

My company, Nolte Associates, is great! We provide Program/Construction Management, Engineering, and Survey services to clients such as Vestas Wind Systems, the largest manufacturer of wind turbines in the world! We are their representative in the US…with services from site selection, consultant/contractor selection, site design, and construction management, to punch list management when the facility begins operation. We have earned their trust and they keep coming back!
