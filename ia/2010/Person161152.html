Paul Melillo, CHMM’s Summary

As a Project Environmental Scientist at Mundell & Associates I assist a wide variety of clients by offering the following services: soil and groundwater investigations, remediation design and implementation, non-hazardous and hazardous waste management, site closures and a number of industrial and agricultural environmental consulting services. My goal as a Project Manager is to make every effort to respond to my client’s needs in a timely manner with a cost effective and appropriate approach to solve their environmental related problems. I enjoy developing relationships with peers, contractors and clients both within in the environmental consulting industry and in other industries to develop strong mutually beneficial business relationships.

I have completed a wide range of environmental reports including: Investigation and Remediation Work Plan Proposals, Initial and Further Site Characterization Reports, Corrective Action Plans (CAPs), Remediation Work Plans, CAP Progress and Implementation Reports, Request for No Further Action Letters. While working at MUNDELL I have assisted with several litigation support and expert witness projects. Recently I have been specializing in industrial and agricultural environmental consulting services including the preparation of Spill Prevention, Control and Countermeasures Plans, Noise Survey, Personal Dosimetry Monitoring and the management of noise engineering controls design and installation. I have has extensive fieldwork experience with soil lithology classification, soil and groundwater sampling, obtaining field measurements using PID, FID, hydrogeologic data processors and geochemical test kits, drilling oversight, pump test, surveying, air sampling, UST and excavation oversight, remediation system installation and operation and maintenance and in situ bioremediation.

Paul Melillo, CHMM’s Specialties:

I have an in depth knowledge of state, federal and local environmental statutes and regulations including: CERCLA, RCRA, TSCA, SARA, SDWA, CWA, CAA, HSWA, NEPA, OSHA and IDEM’s RISC Guidance to assist my clients work through the often times changeling regulatory frame work. I have over 8 years of professional experience working within many IDEM programs including: the Voluntary Remediation Program and Leaking Underground Storage Tank Program, State Clean up and Industrial Waste.
