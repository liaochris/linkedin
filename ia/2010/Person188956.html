Shannon Hickman’s Summary

I’m looking for projects that will challenge my Flash/Web development background and design skills. After spending eight years in Seattle as an award winning web developer, there are a number of skills I have to offer to fulfill your project requirements. I jockey between Mac and PC with ease. Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Dreamweaver are all old friends of mine. My clients have included Microsoft, InfoSpace, cti, Headstrong and Safeco. I received recognition from Macromedia “site of the day” for the Headstrong International Flash site and AIA Web site Award 2000 for the RMW Architecture + Design Flash site. I will bring a full gambit of skills, experience and flexibility to any project.
