Dr. Gagan Sandhu’s Summary

Dedicated to the best care of its patients, California Shine Dental offers state-of-the-art dental procedures for both general and cosmetic purposes, including fillings, bridges, crowns, dentures, root canals, implants, Invisalign, and veneers. Led by Dr. Gagan Sandhu and his eight years of dental experience, California Shine Dental specializes in aesthetic, restorative, and preventive dentistry. Dr. Gagan Sandhu graduated with honors from the renowned University of India with a degree in Pedodontics. After two years of practice in India, Dr. Gagan Sandhu came to the San Francisco Bay Area, where he earned his DDS and established California Shine Dental. As an active participant in the American dental community, Dr. Gagan Sandhu is a member of the American Dental Association, the California Dental Association, the Alameda County Dental Society, and the Santa Clara County Dental Association. Both California Shine Dental and Dr. Gagan Sandhu are fully committed to giving back to the community. Aside from providing free dental exams to California Shine Dental patients in need, Dr. Gagan Sandhu regularly volunteers to give free dental work in local temples, various neighborhoods, and nearby health fairs. San Jose-based California Shine Dental offers a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere. Presenting a home-like waiting room, California Shine Dental possesses a deeply knowledgeable staff. Thorough sterilization procedures and a commitment to hygiene help California Shine Dental provide both new and ongoing patients with a top-notch dental experience. Open daily from 9:00 a.m. until 6:00 p.m., California Shine Dental accepts appointments made in person, through e-mail (info@californiashinedental.com), or by phone (408-223-9600).

Dr. Gagan Sandhu’s Specialties:

Perform all dental procedures including fillings, bridges, crowns, veneers, invisalign, dentures, root canal and implants.
