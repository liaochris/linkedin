Bob Tormey, CPA, CTP, CIRA’s Summary

I am a partner with Tatum Partners, a professional services firm, offering Chief Financial Officer and Chief Information Officer services. I have specialized in supporting the needs of portfolio companies of leading private equity groups including Summit Partners, FdG Associates, Heritage Partners, Oakhill Partners, Blue Point Capital, Graham Partners and others. I am both a CPA and Certified Turn-Around Professional (See www.actp.org). I also chair the Business and Industry Committee for the California Society of Certified Public Accountants.

I also speak and write on the subject of Exit Planning strategies and advise CEO's and others on the subject.

Bob Tormey, CPA, CTP, CIRA’s Specialties:

I support the needs stakeholders in highly leveraged investments in middle market companies.I have worked as advisor to equity sponsors, lenders, CEO's and other CFO's in companies ranging from $10 million to $200 million in revenues. Often, these companies are burdened with significant constraints associated with their credit agreements. I have supported operational turn-arounds, financial restructurings and other initiatives to ensure the stakeholders expectations are met.
