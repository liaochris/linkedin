Kent Howell’s Summary

Founder and President of KOSA Consulting Group (www.kosa-consulting.com), a sales consulting firm that helps businesses increase and sustain top line revenue growth and improve sales team performance.

We specialize in assisting business in how to align sales and marketing messages and activities for maximum effectiveness. We also work with them to improve efficiencies in their sales process, establish effective sales plans, and train salespeople to be more effective through improved skill sets and increased confidence.

I have extensive experience in both developing and winning new business as well as growing revenue from existing accounts. This includes revenue generation for both new and existing products.

My experience spans working with consumers and small & medium sized businesses, to managing millions of dollars of revenue at large multi-national accounts, and on large state and federal government accounts and contracts.

My entire career has been spent in sales and sales management. I've sold a variety different products/services including insurance, software, pharmaceuticals, and telecommunications equipment and services.
