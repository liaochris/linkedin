Oya Yaşayan’s Summary

I believe, understanding the impact of the internet and the power of online consumers is essential to the success of each and every business.

I have been working in digital marketing sector for 13 years, working in EBI, Ericsson, DDB and OMD, drove digital communication and assets of international brands like Volkswagen, Pepsi, Renault, Vodafone.

I have international awards and shortlist honours in “Best of Web” categories including New York Advertising Festival, Horizon Awards and Cannes Media Lions for innovative web asset strategies and digital marketing campaigns.

In January 2007, I have joined OMD as the Head of Digital, still leading my team of 17 people to operate in digital media planning, buying & reporting; web & mobile projects and SEM / SEO activities.

I am also working on internet measurement research at IAB Turkey. I am a Board member since 3,5 years now and I am the Head of Technical Committee, which is responsible for writing the Technical Requiremens document, controlling the methodology and auditing measurement research internally.

Oya Yaşayan’s Specialties:

My key skills are strategy, leadership and analytical approach.

My expertise areas are; Digital Marketing Strategy, Online and Mobile Project Management, Digital Media Planning & Buying, Search Engine Marketing, Campaign Performance Management, Internet Measurement Research.

I am fluent in English and German.
