Brett Stauffer brett@capitalbandwidth.com’s Summary

Brett Stauffer is the founder & owner of Capital Bandwidth. In 2007, he took over the the telecom division of The Stauffer Company. Prior to Capital Bandwidth, Brett spent over 15 years in both telecom & computer software consulting. Capital Bandwidth partners with technology specialists & systems integrators (CMIT, Teal Data) and telephone system vendors (Precision West, ACD Telecom) in order to develop "top shelf" solutions.

Capital Bandwidth's mission statement: "We provide our clients with the best-fit telecom technology and the most bandwidth, at the most aggressive pricing." They don't stop at just saving money. Stauffer adds, "We strive to BOOST BANDWIDTH while reducing overhead. And after we identify the supplier, Capital Bandwidth coordinates the install and becomes our client's Help Desk for ongoing support of their telecom needs."
