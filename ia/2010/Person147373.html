Michael Perullo’s Summary

Michael has extensive experience providing executive leadership in all areas of complex project management. His calling card is ‘excellence in execution every time’. With more than 24 years of working for a diversified group of entities, his most recent experiences include risk management, catastrophe solution design and financial consulting. Earlier in his career he gained experience in marketing, environmental claims services, operational reviews, and developed a foundation in accounting and auditing as a Certified Public Accountant in Boston.

The hallmark of his experience is his willingness to take early ownership of important objectives, and to fulfill their execution by working expeditiously to bring results.

Michael is an enthusiastic leader with a high tolerance for ambiguity. He has the coalition and team building skills to further agreed upon entity priorities, and the breadth and depth of experience to execute on priorities to be identified. He has a customer orientation. Throughout his professional experience, he has maintained a passion for excellence in execution, every time. This passion is reflected in his incredible diversity of pursuits and achievements, both personal and professional. He has the flexibility, creativity, and service oriented responsiveness that makes him an excellent addition to your team.

    1. What is occupying the attention of senior management?

    2. What should be occupying the attention of senior management?

    3. Are we on a continual search for new advantages to warrant application of our offerings to the marketplace?

    4. Do we have the infrastructure to identify markets that we can’t readily see today?
