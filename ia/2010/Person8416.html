Alpa Agarwal’s Summary

Alpa Agarwal, MBA, is Group Marketing Manager for the Emerging Business Team at Microsoft, working with the startup community. Previously, as Group Product Manager for Strategic Research, Alpa regularly led presentations and product demonstrations to Microsoft’s senior leadership team, including Bill Gates and authored several influential papers for Bill Gates’ Think Week. Alpa has been quoted in international business journals and has taught in several Executive MBA programs. Alpa is based in Mountain View, California, USA.
