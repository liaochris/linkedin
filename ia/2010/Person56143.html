Diane Faulkner’s Summary

Curiosity has driven me to write on topics from business, employment law, education, and health to adventure-historical-enviro travel.

My nature has driven me to pursue editing...love it - and yes, I must be nuts. I've been known to read my fave mysteries with pen in hand...drives my friends crazy, but hey, who cares? Gets me to the end of the book.

I've ghostwritten a biz book, which was also my introduction to editing, which then led to my need to teach an on-line writing course. Now, I'm ghostwriting a memoir... (Oh, the stories I could tell...).

Full-time freelancing is for me. Love to travel, love to learn, love to teach, can't keep the cameras out of my hands - there isn't a better profession to have than this.

Need me? Call me. I'm curious, quick, creative, and accurate. ...Did I mention I'm also a radio host?
