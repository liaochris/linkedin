Michele Payn-Knoper, CSP’s Summary

Michele Payn-Knoper personifies passion. As a professional speaker, consultant and author, she encourages all of her clients to connect with people's hot buttons and speak their language. MPK offers expertise in:

- Agricultural advocacy: connecing farm gate to consumer plate
