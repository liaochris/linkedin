Sarah Frias-Torres’s Summary

Behavioral Ecology and Conservation Biology of threatened/endangered species (large grouper fish, whale sharks, seahorses and pipefishes, marine mammals); Marine Biodiversity; Ecology and Evolution of Life Histories; Habitat conservation and restoration (mangroves, coral reefs, seagrass, tropical estuaries); Ecotourism and marine wildlife; Effects of human-induced global climate change in tropical marine ecosystems; Coral reef crime scene investigation; Development of new technologies for ocean and marine life observing systems. Marine Wildlife Filmmaking and Photography.
