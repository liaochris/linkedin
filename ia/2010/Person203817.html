Tim Day’s Summary

Mr. Day is an independent business consultant and entrepreneur having over 20 years of management consulting experience in “Big 4” and other consulting concerns. For the past decade, he started and ran his own businesses in consulting and building materials supply.

WOOD & Co. (www.theWoodCo.com) is his current venture. Leveraging decades of personal interest and experience, he specializes in assisting clients design and create spectacular wooden room interiors. Their unique contribution provides a complete gallery for designing wooden rooms. Bringing together the essential architectural elements necessary to marry form and function.

As a consultant, Mr. Day is specialized in assisting both national and multinational clients spanning in a wide range of industries with business and information technology strategy and planning issues, and implementing organizational change.

Tim Day’s Experience

  • Owner/President

    WOOD & Co.

    (Building Materials industry)

    1998 — Present (13 years )

    WOOD & Co. provides the consumer, and the design and construction professional a one-stop gallery of fine wooden architectural products. W&C facilitates a federated interaction among the design and construction community, and the consumer – thereby, dramatically increasing the value derived over dedicated, one-on-one interaction.

    Special care is given to the understanding of customer objectives - producing samples, CAD drawings, and specifications to ensure expectations are achieved. W&C’s target market includes luxury residential and high-end commercial projects where design emphasis is placed on unique wooden architectural elements.

    Products include: wide plank flooring, T&G paneling, brackets, trusses, timbers, beams, moldings, custom doors, cabinets, stages, gym floors, ballrooms, and custom mill-work. Clients include: High Museum, Atlanta History Center, Callaway Gardens, Coastal Grand Mall, Atlanta Dream Home, and many other luxury residential and commercial projects.
