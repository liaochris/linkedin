Chip Smith, SPHR’s Summary

An accomplished Human Resources Director leading domestic and global initiatives to ensure people reach their highest potential to understand and exceed corporate goals. Passionate about organizational development, relationship building and clear communication to strengthen employee value proposition and build corporate culture. Proven success in organizational development, leadership influence, and innovative strategic solutions to solve complex problems. Background includes strategic and tactical comprehensive generalist skills in recruitment, employment, employee relations, compliance, compensation & benefits, (including stock and ownership issues). policy development, performance evaluation, organizational development, employment law and HRIS in both global and domestic environments.
