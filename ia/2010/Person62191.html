Eduardo Vier, PCC’s Summary

Hi,
I have an OPEN NETWORK and accept all invitations.
Please send me an invite to eduardo (at) blisslifecoaching.com

What Games are you playing right now in your life? Are you happy with those Games? And what is more important, are you Winning in those Games?

If you look at life as a series of Games. You can have a Relationship Game, a Career Game, a Business Owner Game, a Parent Game, etc, etc. As a Coach I will help you win those Games, by designing Games that are winnable and creating practices that are supporting your goals.

In coaching, my goal is to help you move forward on the agenda you bring to coaching. My sessions are completely customized to meet your current needs and goals. I want to help you bring out the very best of yourself. My method will be to listen and to ask questions, to help you focus, to bring clarity and awareness, to learn and prioritize, and motivate you to achieve the goals you seek and to move them into action. Obviously, the ultimate responsibility of achieving your goals rests with you.

If you want to live fully and play to Win the games that you are playing, contact me for a 30 minutes exploratory interview.
