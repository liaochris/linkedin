Rhys Wilson’s Summary

Rhys is an Atlanta M&A attorney focusing on critical business issues:
(1) buying and selling businesses;
(2) preparing companies for sale; and
(3) aligning key employees through stock options, equity incentives and other executive compensation strategies.

I particularly enjoy representing entrepreneurs in M&A transactions, and add value both in preparing their businesses for sale and in getting their M&A deals closed.

Experience includes: 25+ years as an Atlanta M&A attorney, including 100+ successful M&A transactions; LL.M. in Taxation; Senior VP and General Counsel to private equity group; President of a private company; Director of a public company.
