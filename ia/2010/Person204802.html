Terrell Jackson’s Summary

Almost 4 years of recruiting widely exposed on a broad range, from student recruiting to executive recruiting, focussing on staff augmentation in the IT industry.

Starting out in the IT field after college was something that came natural to him. He was always good in the field and only considered himself good because it kept his attention. Doing contract positions, he found himself isolated, in so many ways. The life of a networker wasn't too engaging. So he decided to step into recruiting. He's been doing it ever since, and loves it to death.

Scouts for top of the line talent in the IT industry. Sourcing to research and find qualified candidates for open positions with his clients, he supports his job openings by pre-screening candidates to ensure skill sets are matched. I am different in that I have a sincere passion to enhance one's skills. Its the foundation of vertical networking, in which, everybody gains something from it. Our clients are happy; you are satisfied, and I have made a healthy relationship that can go beyond my efforts. Consults with a purpose of enhancing your technical skills providing a better opportunity for all.

Also focuses on process improvement by holding company-wide forums with recruiters in the organization (nationwide & New Delhi, India) to discuss day-to-day activities so the focus to maintain constant coverage is motivated to be well kept.
