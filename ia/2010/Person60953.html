Dwana De La Cerna’s Summary

Engaging in the many facets of communication is my passion. 20 years as a civil servant, in the capacity of law enforcement and community justice, has afforded the ability to supervise, assist and serve offenders, the public and judicial administration alike.

People's lives are captivating and intricate. Serving the public's needs broadens the spirit. Enhancing any workplace situation with vitality and attention to detail, caps off the many things I can bring to any workplace environment.

Integrating offenders into mainstream society through community programming, liaison to the court system, and organizing resources to reduce recidivism these last 20 years has been a welcomed challenge.

Successfully hosting events like the famed "Bob Brunch" Tweetup with WildFire Restaurant, Chevrolet Mid-Mission Meetup with WildFire Restaurant and Reinvention Team of General Motors has highlighted the 2009-2010 year.

Looking ahead to the fantastic "Seven 1010 Tweetup" Saturday, July 10, 2010 from 2-4 p.m. with WildFire Restaurant & TargetStars Co.

Interviewing and promoting as many dreams and causes my sites will physically allow, is what I hope for my web work.
