Neil Valentine’s Summary

One of my principal passions in life is online marketing. Through the years I've been involved in virtually every aspect of online marketing from web design to product fulfillment. My collective experience and passion for my work helps keep me driven in my professional career. I got my start in the industry selling CPM banner advertising during the dot-com bubble and I've been feeding my marketing addiction ever since. Through the years I've changed companies and titles but my focus has always been on continually optimizing and improving processes for better conversion, efficiency and profitability.

Our Search Agency clients are experiencing increases in return on ad spend (ROAS), brand awareness, and overall market reach. Even in today's economy we're realizing tremendous growth for our clients in the following sectors: travel (airline, hotels, B&B, home rentals), entertainment (live shows) and retail sales.

As VP of Marketing I strive each day to realize our company vision, achieve specific goals, share my experiences and instill my passions. I encourage you to contact me directly and I look forward to your introduction.

My passion for life also extends beyond the office to an ongoing 12 year career as a Level III certified Snowboard Coach, Trainer and Instructor.

I also speak English, Italian & Spanish.
