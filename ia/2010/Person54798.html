Dennis Tapfuma’s Summary

Dennis has 10 years experience working in the digital media industry. He has worked on the following project types; Corporate web build and redesign, Online media (banners, email marketing), Motion Graphics, Film, Interactive Kiosk, Application builds, Ecommerce, Social Media and Mobile.

He has previously worked on successful contracts for large organisations such as the BBC, 3, AA (Automobile Association), EDF Energy, Camden Council, Big Lottery Fund and award winning international brand communications agencies such as Imagination, Euro RSCG, DNA, Sapient and Salterbaxter. He has delivered work for high profile brands such as Adidas, Shell, Morrissons and many others.

Dennis mainly works on short to long term contracts.
