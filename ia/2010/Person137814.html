Matt Joyner’s Summary

I am a corporate and transactional attorney who helps business owners operate their businesses. I help entrepreneurs and investors start, run, buy and sell businesses. My clients benefit from my prior “hands on” practical experience running a business as General Counsel and Vice President of Administration of an international manufacturing company, as well as my prior experience in the courtroom as a commercial litigator. My goal is the success of my clients.

Matt Joyner’s Specialties:

I focus my practice on privately held companies, LLC formations and operating agreements, corporate asset purchases and sales, mergers and acquisitions, joint ventures, franchises and negotiating and drafting contracts for businesses.
