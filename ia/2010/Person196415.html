Steven Diamond’s Summary

It all started pretty normal actually. Just your average 5 year old in Portsmouth Virginia. Until I saw my first magic show. That day, my life would change forever.

I began as a magician, I was raised around real 500 pound Lions and Tigers. I have traveled all over the world. Appeared on more TV shows then I can count and have sailed the high seas, (all of them) aboard many many cruise ships during my magic years.

All of that came to a complete halt in the blink of an eye in 2001 when I collapsed of a nervous breakdown while performing with the Osmond brothers. It was the best day of my life. I didn't know I had OCD, in fact I had never even heard of it. I didn't realize I was a manic depressive either... I just knew something wasn't right with me. Nor, did I understand that anxiety and stress had brought me to my knees.

I got a second chance at living life the right way. The years that followed would put me before tons of doctors, taking a ton of medication until I finally realized that I had become a walking zombie, just as I use to be only this time without the tigers. That was a good thing I guess. I spent years looking for the right help. Nothing seemed to work.

Then one day I realized that if I wanted to change my life, it was going to be up to me. I had to have the courage to take action. I call it "day of awakening".

After years of research and unable to find the right professional help, I developed my own take on methods I had unearthed. Bits and pieces I had learned from every corner of the world. I was my own guinea pig until I finally figured out what really works and maybe more importantly was does not.

Eventually I decided to take what worked for me and teach it to the world.

Today, that IS my life's goal. Millions have reached out to me, and until I take my final breath, I'll be reaching right back.

You are not alone. If you need someone to help you find your way... I encourage you to join my blog community. It's free.
