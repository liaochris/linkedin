Wendy Britton’s Summary

As an individual investor since 1984, I have gone though the 1987 crash, the 2000 tech bubble and crash, and here we are again in the Great Recession of 2008-2010+. Having worked as a stock broker for the past 10 years, I have learned a great many things from helping clients get through these events. Having owned my own importing business for 10 years previously, I've learned a lot about the American Dream and the tax code that is in place to help us each to achieve that dream on our own terms. After working in Real Estate as a Residential Realtor for 5 years, I discovered that I really enjoyed helping people to buy/sell their largest investment, and to educate them, calm their fears, and lead them through the process in a comfortable setting. In 1997, I took a part time job at a bank and eventually I grew to love the financial business so much, that I moved over to Charles Schwab in 2000 in order to be able to work with individuals on their portfolios to help them build and protect their wealth. I love working with my clients and helping them throughout their lives to plan for retirement and special events. It is a very rewarding career for me because of my sheer enjoyment of working with people face to face!
