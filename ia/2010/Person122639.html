Laurie E. Knight, SPHR’s Summary

A highly skilled, seasoned Human Resources professional with extensive experience in a variety of industries and organizational structures including, but not limited to, Media/Entertainment (newspaper and magazine publishing, interactive, radio, television), Manufacturing, Marketing (including direct mail), and Retail.
