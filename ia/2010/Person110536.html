Karen Emanuelson’s Summary

Are you confused with all the hype over Social Media? Do you think your organization should be active on Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter, and blogging but don’t know where to begin?

You’ve come to the right place.

With knowledge acquired during my decades of marketing experience, I can help you sort through today’s social media marketing trends and information to help you determine how to best market your organization online.

You won’t get a Social Media cookie-cutter approach from me.

First, I’ll help you identify online goals that work with your organization's overall marketing plan. No marketing plan? No problem, I can help with that, too.

Then, I’ll analyze your current online reputation and activities to see what, if any, changes need to be made.

Next, I’ll create and help you implement a practical, personalized social media action plan based on your organization’s goals, with an eye toward time and money constraints.

Along the way, you’ll be educated. You’ll not only know if you should be using The “Big Four” — Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, and Twitter — as well as other social networking options, but also why. With this education, you’ll be able to maximize the benefit from your online time.

My clients include individuals, non-profits organizations, local governments, lawyers and law firms, and small businesses - some just starting up. All have learned to make the most of their time online.

Using today's technology to promote today's organizations and individuals is my passion. I enjoy sharing what I know about social media through presentations to Twin Cities area organizations, through one-on-one personalized training, and through my blog. I also manage the Social Media for the Blogger group here on LinkedIn and would love to have you join: http://bit.ly/dgsT7o

My best days involve creative challenges and changing priorities.

How can I help you share what you know online?
