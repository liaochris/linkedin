    What is The Climate Project?

    The Climate Project is Al Gore's climate change leadership program and is a part of the Alliance for Climate Protection, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. TCP's mission is to educate the public about the harmful effects of climate change and to work toward solutions at a grassroots level worldwide. The Climate Project (TCP) was founded in 2006 as a commitment by former Vice President and Nobel Laureate Al Gore to train 1,000 Americans to educate the public about climate change using the slideshow from his Academy Award‐winning film 'An Inconvenient Truth'.

    Today, TCP supports more than 3,000 diverse and dedicated volunteer Presenters in 55 countries around the globe who together have delivered 70,000 presentations and have reached a combined global audience of more than 7.3 million people worldwide.
