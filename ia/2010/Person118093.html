Nathan Martin’s Summary

Currently self-employed, I offer independent Flash Development and consulting services, directly to design & development agencies here in Melbourne, Australia.

I have worked across many various Flash projects over the last few years, including multi-player games, full Flash websites, desktop applications and applications for touch screen kiosks.

I offer my services purely as a Flash Developer, however I am qualified with a Bachelor of Design (Multimedia Design) from Swinburne University.

I have a keen eye for detail and am intensely passionate about all facets of new media, from the graphical implementation of a designer's vision through to the architecture and development of complicated systems.

Nathan Martin’s Specialties:

Attention to detail, design background, natural technical knowledge, excellent time management, single & multi-player games including AI, website scaffolding/frameworks, PaperVision3D, Flash based desktop applications and widgets, Adobe AIR, MDM Zinc, Flash integration with JavaScript, Flash integration with Content Management Systems.
