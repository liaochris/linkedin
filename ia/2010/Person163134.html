Peter Stael’s Summary

I started Across-consult in december 2008 working mainly in the environment of food and foodpackaging.

The company has developed six concepts, trying to support good products-making and good production-processes.

Management-consulting to help your company come out of the crisis. Helping you to become the good company you want to be. We walk the extra mile with you.....because we care.

Years of experience in companies that had to be built or re-built, make me an experienced entrepreneur and manager who can support you, especially when you are a SME, who can not do it all by his own.

Coming out of the crisis by making sustainable choises for your own future.

Our consultants in Switzerland, Benelux, Germany, Italy, Greece, Bulgaria, East-Africa will do this in the same way. Our network in UK, Eastern-Europe, Scandinavia, India, Middle-East is also available for you.
