Dorothy Vernon-Brown’s Summary

As a Small Business Marketing Consultant I am passionate about small businesses succeeding. I'm even more passionate about them using a proven marketing system that will help gain new customers and get more from existing ones.

As a fifteen year marketer, I've had the tremendous benefit of working in Canada (Toronto) as well as the Caribbean. I've come to learn a thing or two i.e successful businesses must market and innovate.
It is therefore my goal to help them do both.

Armed with a Marketing Communications Masters Certification from the Schulich Business School, York University and a Bachelors of Arts Degree, UWI, along with fifteen years in the trenches as a Marketing professional has given me some insights.

These insights have provided a solid foundation to tackle small business marketing challenges. Bring them on!
