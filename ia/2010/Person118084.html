David Walker’s Summary

I've been a professional designer for over a decade. I started my career by working with an in-house design team at a local college, while still attending college myself. I learned so much more by working alongside experienced designers rather than just sitting in a classroom. I learned to work effectively as part of a graphic design team. While there, I designed flyers, brochures, newspaper ads, posters, direct mailers, business forms, and business cards. I also received the Gold Medallion of Excellence from the National Council for Marketing and Public Relations for a catalog cover design.

In 1997 I became an art director for a local advertising agency. My career there led me into a deeper understanding of the pre-press and print world. I've also learned to work with many personality types and continue to be productive in any situation.
