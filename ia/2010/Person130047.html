Tom Maguire’s Summary

Entrepreneur and owner of PuroClean Restoration Rescuers, the leader in property emergency services, helping families and businesses overcome the devastating setbacks caused by water, fire, mold and other conditions resulting in property damage.

Before starting this company, I served in the Air Force for over 8 years after graduating from The Citadel. Time in the Air Force was spent in Logistics...including a tour with Air Force Special Ops and majority of time in the Middle East.

After leaving the Air Force, I owned my own consulting company for 2 years while working as the Chief Operations Officer for a small business in Michigan with 60 employees and $55M in annual revenue.
