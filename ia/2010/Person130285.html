Makenna Held’s Summary

I am a creative management and marketing professional with a solid track record of project management and marketing campaign management for both public and private sector companies.

I am proficient in topics of sustainability and development via extensive coursework and research at the Masters level. My greatest interest is in energy and clean water access solutions for the developing world. I've lived and worked abroad in both Cambodia and India as a grant writer, elementary education instructor, and program development assistant.

I am a graduate of Smith College and an avid believer in "the girl effect" which focuses on empowerment of girls in developing nations in order to effect widespread social change. While I am employed full-time, I still mange to work on this goal with the Small Victories Project.
