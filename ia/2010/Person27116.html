Brandon Kolybaba’s Summary

Most recently as co-founder and managing partner at SheepDogInc.ca, I've been in a leading entrepreneurial role for majority of my career. My primary area of expertise has been managing a team of high achieving technical staff (10 to 30+ individuals). Most recently our main focus has been related to Google technology solutions that leverage the Google Cloud to solve complex business problems for our clients. As a result, we have become a highly engaged, trusted Google Partner in Canada and are founding members on the Google Partner Advisory Board as well as key resources in their trusted tester programs.

I have a bachelors degree in Business with a concentration in the area of Information Systems. I've had the good fortune to have worked for several great leading Canadian companies. This experiance has given me valuable insight into developing practical solutions for our customers always from a business perspective. My leadership skills and IT know how come from a strong academic foundation as well as practical experience.

I've been in Project Management and/or Leadership roles for most of the companies I've worked with over the past 15 years. I've also had a fair amount of hands on experience as a Business Analyst, Data Architect, and Team Lead for a wide variety of business applications. I'm particularly interested in data modeling and finding effective solutions to interesting data design challenges that have an impact on business process.

I have well developed conceptual & strategic thinking abilities. Good communication, negotiation & interpersonal skills. Up to date knowledge of emerging technologies & trends in the IT and business sectors.

I have the ability to develop project plans, architectural models & to articulate solutions to complex problems. I have experience in the application of cost models & service level agreements as well as applied knowledge of capacity planning tools & techniques.
