    We are on a mission to turn the staffing function into a proactive rather than reactive form of recruiting. Gone are the days in which we post an opening and wait for candidates to respond. My team and I will be working very closely with our research scientists and development engineers to identify and proactively recruit candidates that have the emerging skills needed for Intel's continued success. Our goal is to have qualified candidates in queue well before the need comes about. It's the KANBAN concept applied to staffing.
