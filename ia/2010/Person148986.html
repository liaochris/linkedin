  • Member of ISO/IEC JTC1/SC17/WG8

    ISO

    (Privately Held; Insurance industry)

    January 2005 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    WG8 is one of the Working Groups of ISO/IEC/JTC1/SC17 "Identification Cards" and was established in 1988 to develop standards for contactless chipcards.

    WG8 has delegated its projects to three subgroups to achieve efficient and dedicated developments of the standards according to the follow:

    - WG8/TF2 (Task Force 2), The group has developed the standard series ISO/IEC 14443 and the related test methods in ISO/IEC 10373, i.e. ISO/IEC 10373-6.
    - WG8/TF3 (Task Force 3) The group has developed the standard series ISO/IEC 15693 and the related test methods in ISO/IEC 10373, i.e. ISO/IEC 10373-7.
    - WG8/TF4 (Task Force 4) The group has developed a standard which addresses the approved New Work Item Proposal on an "ISO/IEC 14443 test environment".

    WG8 is responsible to the following international standards:
    - ISO/IEC 15693 series (Vicinity, VCD & VICC cummunication)
    - ISO/IEC 14443 series (Proximity, PCD & PICC cummunication)
    - ISO/IEC 10536 series (Close Coupling, CCD & CICC
    cummunication)
    - ISO/IEC 10373 series (Contactless test plans)

    WG8 is frequently communicating with WG3 and WG10 related to the standardization of electronic MRPs (Machine Readable Passports) ,Driving Licences with a contactless interface and other contactless international documents and application.
