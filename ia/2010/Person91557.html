Jay McBain’s Summary

Jay McBain is an accomplished speaker, author and innovator in the IT industry. Named one of the Top 25 Newsmakers in Canada for 2009 by CDN Magazine, Top 100 Most Respected Thought Leaders by Vertical Systems Reseller Magazine in the US, as well as Top 250 Managed Services Executives in the global MSPmentor list, he is often sought out for keynotes, industry events, as well as business development opportunities.

As a member of the World Future Society, Jay is an expert in Pervasive Computing which is the study of computing and the impact on society in the future, as well as Managed Services, Voice over IP, Security and Cloud Computing.

Jay has spent his career in sales leadership, marketing and business development positions within IBM and Lenovo. He is currently responsible for the SMB Sector for Lenovo. His responsibilities include strategy, marketing, product development, pricing, and Channel development in the United States and Canada.

Jay has previously led the Enterprise (Fortune 500), Public Sector, Small and Medium as well as the Consumer businesses for Lenovo and IBM Canada. The Canadian division enjoyed a #1 ranking of all countries world-wide in Lenovo sales during his tenure including the most successful product launch. Previous to leadership roles at IBM Canada, Jay had several sales and technical roles living in Calgary, Winnipeg, Toronto, and Raleigh, NC. Jay won the prestigious IBM Golden Circle Award as well as the National Top Sales Contributor Award three times and was named to IBM’s 100 Per Cent Club eleven consecutive times.

An avid blogger, community and social media expert, Jay has widened Lenovo’s aperture to the market delivering unique content and messaging.

Jay actively gives back to the community in which he works and lives and has throughout his career been involved in the United Way, the Lenovo Employee Charitable Fund, Models for Charity and a member of the Board of Directors for Junior Achievement.

Technology Futurist, Channel Management and Strategy, Sales Leadership and Coverage, Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and Managed Services.
