Leslie Smith’s Summary

Leslie Smith & Associates works with mission-driven organizations providing organization development to achieve remarkable results. Grounded in interview, survey, and large group process methodologies our services include organization assessment, change management, planning, stakeholder engagement, leadership, team and board development, and facilitation.
