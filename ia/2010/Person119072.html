Kristi Dinsmore’s Summary

Kristi's professional mission is "Ridding the World of Bad Bosses - One at a Time".

Her executive coaching, leadership and organizational development work is focused on helping professionals avoid fatal career deraillers that can stop a promising career in its tracks. Leaders can accelerate their development through early identification of predictive strengths and career potential. While one "bad boss" can destroy a team, constructive leaders can build organizations able to create and sustain profitable growth.

Organizational Development: Strategic Planning, Organizational Structure, Talent Assessment and Placement
