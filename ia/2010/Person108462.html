Julie Lane’s Summary

Julie Lane has purposefully and successfully built a broad and athletic skill set in her career. Beginning with 17 years at Target Corp and making significant contributions in the areas of Merchandising (product development, category management, Vendor relationships, differentiated assortment offerings) to Best Buy VP of Supply Chain/Inventory Management/Vendor Management (reduction of owned inventory of $3 billion and in stock improvement to 97% along with inventory turn improvement from 4 to 7 times).

Julie's achievements reflect her approach to each business challenge:tackling each opportunity with a fresh, innovative, inclusive approach. She ensures success through building effective cross functional partnerships, and rallying the team around both the vision and the end goal.

Julie's core areas of expertise are: Merchandising, Marketing (Customer/Market segmentation, Branding & Positioning), Supply Chain, Lifecycle Management, Strategic Planning. Most recently, Julie has done Management consulting for domestic and international clients across areas of Business Strategy, Marketing Strategy (product development & Branding, pricing, promotion, channel strategy, development of differentiated product solutions), .Supply Chain, Early Read and Lifecycle management.

Julie Lane’s Specialties:

Retail Concept Development and execution, Technology Solutions (both product and Technology as applied to the business model), CPG, Passion for Customer centered companies, strategies, and the development of Integrated Operating Models reflecting this core competency, IT business requirement leadership and development. Team development and leadership.
