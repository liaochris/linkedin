Jeff Pugel’s Summary

jpugel@hotmail.com

Professional, experienced and not afraid. I develop marketing plans that actually produce.

What you get:
- Marketing plans that break through the clutter of the marketplace to increase sales, profits, and market share.
- Holistic and out of the box thinking - I see the parts as they relate to the big picture and have the know-how to bring them together.
- A problem solver – how to do more with less, faster, or taking things to the next level.

What makes me qualified:
- Twelve years of developing marketing plans for some of the world’s top brands – Intel, AT&T Wireless, American Express, Advil brands
- Have managed budgets from $25,000 to $200 million
- Experience in both consumer and business-to-business marketing, not just a one-trick-pony.
- Viewed by colleagues as a marketing and new-media thought leader – I’m the go-to-guy when they have questions on branding, direct response, lead generation, advertising, media planning/buying, and social media to name a few
- I know how to work across departments to build a consensus buy-in to marketing, resulting in the full organization’s support behind campaigns.

Why I am different:
- I believe that marketing is an investment in a company’s financial well-being and I have the responsibility to properly invest those funds so that they produce a positive ROI
- Obstacles or challenges don’t scare me - in fact they energize me. Give me a challenge and prepare to be pleasantly surprised by the outcome.
- I’m an advocate that marketing and sales are part of the same team, not adversaries. Make it a relay race amongst the two groups rather than a competition and watch sales and profits increase.
- I have the ability to rapidly assimilate new concepts and ideas and apply them to problem solving and new opportunities.
- A natural embrace of technologies impact on marketing today – it’s not something to be feared but something to capitalize on
