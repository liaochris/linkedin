Scott Boedigheimer’s Summary

Entrepreneur, recovering CPA, sales and management executive.

I am passionate about helping companies realize their potential by improving their core capabilities. For years companies have focused on improving their operations and supply chain. There are good models and metrics available to help companies improve operations. Theory of constraints, lean manufacturing and other methodologies provide the foundational concepts that companies need to understand their operations and then improve their operational capabilities. Many companies have made significant improvements to their operational capabilities but still haven’t realized their full potential because they haven’t improved their market capabilities. In short, they know how to manufacture products or deliver services, but they have excess capacity because they aren’t able to sell as much as they can produce.

Similar methodologies for understanding and improving an organization’s market (or customer-facing) capabilities do not exist. Companies looking to improve revenue have few options to consider when attempting to understand their own organization’s capabilities. Our patent-pending model and framework provides the structure that companies need to understand their capabilities and prioritize their actions so they can realize their potential. The action plan that we provide allows companies to remove sales as the factor limiting their success.
