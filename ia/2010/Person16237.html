Arikka Greene’s Summary

"Arikka" is a name which few can pronounce correctly (and believe me, many have tried). A lover of music, she sings, plays piano, guitar, saxophone, viola, recorder and sometimes even the triangle. Arikka grew up in a tiny central Florida farm-town with hopes to one day abandon her country roots to become an international superstar. With a marketing degree from Palm Beach Atlantic University and a dream, she snagged a job in online marketing right out of college and never looked back. With more than 5 years working online, 3 years of international experience, and a massive network of global clients and partners to boot, Arikka has established herself as a serious player in the international space. She was a pioneer in performance-based online marketing strategies for the UK and Australia; instrumental in establishing Intela's branch office in London, where she briefly lived. Her passion is bringing the world of online marketing together, one country at a time. There are no borders, boundaries or languages that will keep her from achieving her goal.
