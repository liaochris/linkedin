Kunal Bhardwaj’s Summary

I am currently working with MarketsandMarkets leading its Business Research Practice for FTE Engagements. My primary role here includes managing client engagements and providing them unparalleled outsourcing research support through various engagement models.

MarketsandMarkets is a market research report publisher publishing approximately 150 strategic macro level market research reports containing market intelligence information on over 7,500 mutually exclusive micro level markets annually across different industries such as health care, energy, chemicals, IT, biotechnology, food & beverages, advanced materials, semiconductors & electronics, automotive, and telecommunication among the few. Off late with our market intelligence repository being growing and continuously updated, we have started providing knowledge outsourcing and strategic consulting services to reduce the cost and increase the operational efficiency of our clients, which primarily include:

- Primary and Secondary Data Support
- Partnering Search
- Executive Biography Search
- Company Profiling
- Industry assessment
- Competitive Intelligence
- Custom Research Reports
- Due Diligence
- Comparative Landscape
- News categorization
- Strategic analysis including PEST, SWOT,
benchmarking studies, cost benefit analysis, channel
strategy, market entry strategies etc.

The various engagement models where in we engage with the clients are:

1. On-demand research requests
2. Long term research requests
3. Outsourced research desk
