* Solution Architect for a number of large-scale enterprise systems for both commercial and public sector clients.

Ben is a Senior Architect with 10+ years of commercial experience in Analysis, Design and Development. He's led teams of various sizes and has shaped the architecture of a number of major SOA & JavaEE based IT systems for clients such as Kingfisher, William Hill, GDF, and the NHS.

More recently Ben has been helping Screwfix to create a Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), influencing SOA Design, Development and Governance from early conception through to final delivery. Screwfix's web-services are now the backbone to the worlds first ATG based 4 channel sales order capture & processing system.

His general approach to engagements is to seek to add value through the use of innovative designs, tools, techniques and working practices. He is an advocate of Agile development methodologies, and has designed many ground-breaking enterprise systems.
