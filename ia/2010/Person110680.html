Karen Lundquist’s Summary

I am a business and employment attorney at Avanti Law in Minneapolis. I help growing businesses in Minnesota and abroad avoid legal trouble by helping them take a proactive and preventative approach to law. We believe that this approach helps companies to grow and be more stable and secure.

Although nothing can guarantee that a lawsuit won't be filed against a business, a good defense can be crafted to ensure that if it happens, the company can defend itself and hopefully increase the odds that the suit will be dismissed at an early stage or a favorable judgment entered. One way we take this preventative approach is with our Legal Audit. For a fixed fee, we review your company's legal documents, make written recommendations ranked on a scale of 1 to 5 and provide you with guidelines of what steps your company should take to best protect itself.

One of our innovative and creative services is our Business Per Te, a package of legal services that you purchase for a fixed fee each month. You get a set number of hours of phone advice per month, a Legal Audit, a certain number of contracts reviewed or drafted each year and a discount on other legal services. These business packages help our clients budget for an attorney, like they do for their accountant or insurance.

We also have packages for start-up businesses for a fixed fee. We like predictability and certainty and know that hourly fees, the traditional way of charging for attorneys, don't offer that. That's why we have our packages.

I speak Italian and Spanish and work with companies on international issues. I have lived in Rome, Santiago and Guanajuato, Mexico and love traveling and learning about other cultures. We work with foreign companies on product liability issues (with a preventative approach) so that they are less exposed to the US legal system.

I am also an business law adjunct at William Mitchell College of Law. I love teaching and incorporating education into the practice of law.
