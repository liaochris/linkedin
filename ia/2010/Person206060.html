Tom Haupt’s Summary

Known internationally for his enthusiastic and inspirational presentations, Tom’s heartfelt connection with his audience make for a memorable and transformational event!

Tom speaks from his own life experiences and what he’s learned from his mentors from the age of ten. He’s been an entrepreneur for over 27 years succeeding in the area of sales and service and as a trainer.

As a personal growth and development facilitator, Tom addresses audiences all across the globe that come from varying backgrounds and businesses. As a result of being a professional speaker and facilitator,

Tom has written a book titled, TIME-OUT! Winning Strategies For Playing a Bigger Game in Life, a must read to achieve all your goals and dreams!
