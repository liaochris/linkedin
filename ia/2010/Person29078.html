Brian Torchin’s Summary

HealthCare Recruitment Counselors (HRC) is the leading full-service staffing and consulting firm for the health care industry.

At HRC, we pride ourselves on our ability to customize our healthcare solutions to the specific needs of our customers, the healthcare employer. For job seekers, we provide individualized career consultation and nationwide search services.

HRC’s mission is to build long-term relationships with our customers by providing flexible staffing solutions based on respect, trust, and consistent delivery of excellent results.
