Claudine Bianchi’s Summary

A highly regarded, results-oriented senior executive with 20+ years of management experience in technology marketing. Skilled in constructing and executing strategy, defining positioning and value propositions, increasing brand awareness, building partnerships, coaching high performance teams, developing and launching new products and services, and representing the company as a spokesperson.
