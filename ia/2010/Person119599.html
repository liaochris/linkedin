Krystal Higgins’s Summary

I create engaging product stories through collaborative interaction design.

A good story, like a good user experience, has character. It immerses the audience and encourages them to shape it in their own, personal way. My skills are in researching, designing and partnering with others to define these kinds of rich product experiences. I thrive in an iterative environment where I can take a story and span it across multiple platforms or touchpoints.
