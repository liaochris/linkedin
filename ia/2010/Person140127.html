Meghan Callaghan’s Summary

Meghan Callaghan is an energetic and enthusiastic PR Professional with a slight media obsession, hungrily devouring as much daily news as 1440 minutes per day allow. From DVRing cable news, watching news podcasts on the iPod, reading dozens of newsletters, RSS feeds, blogs, websites, social networks, magazines, newspapers and apps….the true study of the media allows for a full understanding of what journalists, bloggers and the guy sitting down at Starbucks on his laptop want and need.

She believes that the cliché that life is a journey, a game, that can be played and made fun – which might also explain the Foursquare addiction.

Meghan graduated from Rutgers University where she double-majored in Journalism/Media Studies and Psychology. She grew up in Haddon Township, New Jersey and currently lives in Hoboken, NJ.
