Vinta Nanda’s Summary

Produced, Directed and Written several Television Shows including the trailblazing "Tara" which ran for 5 years and went on to create Television History. Director Ideation for Zee Network, strategically placing content across 16 channels from 2000 to 2002. I was the founder President of Project SMITA (Social Mobilisation and Information for Timely Action), an NGO established in 1989 of which the main aim was to harness the power of popular media and to utilize its impact for Social Change. I have made several documentary films on various womens, health, rural and socio political issues. My first full length feature "White Noise", starring Rahul Bose and Koel Purie was released in 2005. At present I am working on the scripts of three feature films, "Jaanta hai mera baap kaun hai", "Soulmate at any rate" and "Magic", and researching on my next two documentary films, one which explores Sexual Freedom in Urban India and the second on the Children in South Asia. Besides being one of the Directors on the board of Entertainment Tracks Pvt. Ltd., a company creating content for all media, I am also the founding President of the recently established NGO, The Village Project.

Vinta Nanda’s Specialties:

Production, Content Strategy and Development, Research, Networking, PR, Team Building and Writing and Direction.
