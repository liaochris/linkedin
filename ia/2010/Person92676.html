Jeanne Fishman’s Experience

  • voiceover artist

    Fishvo

    (Entertainment industry)

    2003 — Present (7 years )

    I have been working in the voiceover commercial and animation scene for over 5 years and have landed such roles as Koko the train for a new animated series called Chuggington directed by Sarah Ball, Zeek, on The Dripps, created by James Cahill - www.vimeo.com/615199 , Francine for Arthur live, and "Allie" for USBC. Voiced characters for Doggity's Diner Demo and characters for a web series The Mint Condition. Aside from my animation work I have recorded commercials, toys, and an audiobook series Keeker and the Sneaky Pony for audible.com. Training is from Stella Adler and the School for Film and Television. Private voice coaching with Cynthia Adler, Marla Kirban, animation coach Jeannie Elias, Bob Bergen, Melody Jones,and Paul Liberti.
    My other interests include the gift of literacy program, in conjunction with the Good Dog Foundation in NYC Public schools. I hope to lend my voice to many childrens educational, and entertainment projects
