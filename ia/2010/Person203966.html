Tim Kuglin’s Summary

Tim Kuglin; CEO & Executive Producer, has over 30 years of senior level television management and production/transmission experience. Tim serves as the Chairman of the Board & Chief Executive Officer of TKTV, LLC and has produced, co-produced or transmitted over 6,000 live television events. He has produced and/or transmitted live sports broadcasts including more than 10 years of NFL football, NHL hockey, MLB baseball and NBA basketball games. Tim has worked on Special Event Broadcasts that include 9 Superbowls, 1988 & 2002 Winter Olympics, numerous Academy Awards, several Grammy Awards, professional boxing and live HBO entertainment events including Comic Relief and Wimbledon Tennis. He has Produced the private broadcasts of numerous corporate meetings, events and conferences for many of the country’s largest corporations including the world’s largest private videoconference that was broadcast to 1.2 million people in 54 countries. More than 70 Television Networks have entrusted Tim and his expertise. Tim is an active member of the Society of Satellite Professionals International (SSPI) where he served as Treasurer and a Board member for over 18 years. He is also a member of the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE), the Cable Television Association of Marketing (CTAM), and the National Association of Television Programming Executives (NATPE).
