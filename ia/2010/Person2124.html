Adam Weiss’s Summary

As a technical recruiter I work to constantly improve and streamline the full life cycle recruiting process. My goal is to develop meaningful relationships with quality candidates to help them reach their career goals. Part of my recruitment process is to completely understand our clients wants and needs while pairing the qualified candidates that suit both parties. My responsibilities range from, but are not limited to: acting as a liaison between educated candidates and their potential employers, negotiating salary and hourly wages with potential employees & candidates, identifying qualified individuals from the internal database as well as through many social mediums, educate candidates throughout the full life cycle recruiting process and its direction, complete & evaluate technical screens both in person & over the phone.

The immediate focus of my work is within North Eastern Ohio, but our spectrum ranges from the Atlantic to the Pacific. I find honest, dedicated candidates spanning all infrastructure specialists. As always, I am here to exceed both client & candidate expectations. Feel free to contact me at - aweiss@exciteitpartners.com
