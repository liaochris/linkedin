Jennifer Uhll’s Summary

Located in Malibu, Juhll.com is a full-service online marketing agency leveraging advanced internet technologies to create measurable and profitable online campaigns for internet marketers.

Juhll’s mission is to be the new metrically-responsible agency that operates solely according to the client’s bottom line. Our goal is to significantly increase ROI for both small and Fortune 500 clients who invest in online marketing.

Juhll offers online marketing consultation & strategic planning, creative development, display campaign optimization, campaign management, landing page optimization, media buying & placement optimization, ad serving, retention email, marketing analytics and online public relations.

Juhll’s team has over 50+ years combined experience in conceptualizing and developing online marketing strategies. Clients include top Fortune 500 corporations in a variety of industries, including: entertainment, financial, health care, insurance, wireless communications, retail, education, automotive and lead-generation.
