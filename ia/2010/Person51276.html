David Leibowitz’s Summary

Av (highest possible) rated Attorney specializing in representation of people and business in financial matters since 1974. We are the leading financial service firm in Lake County, Illinois and intend to become the leading financial service firm in the area north of Chicago and south of Milwaukee. To that end, we established Lakelaw/Kenosha in 2006 and now have offices in Chicago and Skokie as well..

Only lawyer in Northern Illinois or Southern Wisconsin board certified in both business and consumer bankruptcy by the American Board of Certification.

Editor-in-chief and author of American Bankruptcy Institute Fraud Manual

Recognized nationally for expertise in mortgage foreclosure defense.


Designated as a "Super Lawyer" in Illinois in the field of bankruptcy since 2007.

Avvo rated 10.0 out of 10.0 - "Superb"

Licensed in Illinois and Wisconsin

Led World Bank project instituting modern bankruptcy laws in Mongolia in 1999

David Leibowitz’s Specialties:

Bankruptcy and insolvency, predatory lending defense, business organizations, estate planning, probate administration, guardianships and elder law. Civil litigation involving financial matters including mortgage foreclosures (both for creditors and defense), consumer protection matters including Fair Debt Collection Practice Act, Fair Credit Reporting Act, Lemon Law.
