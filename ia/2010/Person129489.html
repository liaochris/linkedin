Loïc SATTLER’s Summary

Loïc Sattler is currently crafting creative campaigns as a Senior Art Director in Berlin. Since 1999, he has proven his design-skills in web-design, multimedia, print collateral, clothes styling, and corporate identity.
He features clients such as Coca-Cola, Mercedes, L’Oreal, Warner Brothers, IBM…

Loïc was awarded a Masters Degree in New Media Theory in Germany (Stuttgart - 2005), with a Thesis curated by Pr. Olia Lialina and Pr. Dr. Helmut Draxler.

He works with special emphasis on aesthetics, creative ideas and communication goals, with a very high attention to details.
Known as efficient, he’s able to manage a creative crew and likes to have a global view on the projects he is working-on.

Loïc's aim is to be understood as an individual who wishes to take things further by empowering his profession to the best of his ability.
