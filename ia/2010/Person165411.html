Patrick Petit’s Summary

Senior software architect with over 20 years of experience in Unix, C, Java EE, system management and monitoring, identity & access management (IAM), identity federation, virtualization, Internet protocols and standards, Sun Microsystems's middleware products including; Glassfish, OpenESB, OpenDS, and OpenSSO, as well as other open-source products and technologies.

Completed an MBA program at the Grenoble Graduate School Business (GGSB) with a master thesis that has for subject the business economics of Cloud Computing and Green IT.

My goal is to work in the cloud computing industry sector in a position that mixes and leverages technology, innovation and business skills.
