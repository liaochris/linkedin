Walt Ribeiro’s Summary

I am the founder of Graywave LLC, a Music Consulting and Music Production company.

I arrange pop songs 'For Orchestra' professionally over at http://www.ForOrchestra.com. I also help companies generate sales and grow their online presence.

How I Can Help You:

*Music Production and Orchestration: Generating over 1 million views for my videos, my orchestration and music production has assisted in many commercials, videos, and most recently collaborated with MC Lars in 2009 along with Weird Al Yankovic and Jonathan Coulton. To hear my work simply go to http://www.fororchestra.com/music

* Social Media Consulting: Specializing in music, I consult with companies on their online marketing strategies that are in need of a young, fresh, experienced point of view on building their brand online. In addition, my knowledge of music publishing and distribution online can help generate and deliver more sales, and improve your return on marketing efforts all while building communities both online and offline.

My work has been featured in The Wall Street Journal, Wired Magazine, Guardian Newspaper, Home Education Magazine, Digg frontpage, Youtube frontpage, LA Times, Mix Magazine, Perez Hilton, Comedy Central, and more. For a complete and up-to-date list go to http://www.fororchestra.com/press

Walt Ribeiro’s Specialties:

Music Production, MIDI, Orchestration, Arranging, audio and video, guitar, piano, music technology, social media, Web 2.0, digital brand marketing, HTML, viral marketing, online music distribution and publishing
