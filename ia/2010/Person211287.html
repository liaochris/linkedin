Vicki Boatright, L.P.C.C.’s Summary

I am a professional artist as well as a Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor with extensive experience in both professional domains. I have worked as a therapist since 1991, specializing in work with children and adolescents affected by child abuse and other forms of trauma. As an artist, I have done numerous community projects combining my own talents with those of others for the purposes of community enhancement and individual self improvement. As the Curator of Exhibitions for the Ohio Arts and Crafts Guild, I plan, organize, and present exhibitions for a gallery of fine arts and crafts.

Vicki Boatright, L.P.C.C.’s Specialties:

Behavioral Health--Therapy with individuals affected by trauma; consultation and supervision.
Artwork--Contemporary Style Pet Portraits; "Dialogues Murals" for special events, conferences and other programs that include event participants in the creation of the artwork.
