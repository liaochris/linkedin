Scott Julian’s Summary

Scott Julian is a seasoned Internet industry professional with years of board level business experience.

Scott's current project is the launch and management of the venture backed, global audience measurement company Effective Measure, servicing a client base of multinational brands, advertising agencies and online publishers.

With offices in 5 countries and a global audience of 107,000,000 people a month, Effective Measure is the de-facto standard for web measurement in over 19 countries.

Scott is a Fellow of the Australian Institute of Management, and a member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors.
