Jenn Pelly’s Summary

Jenn Pelly’s Specialties:

Jenn Pelly’s Experience

  • Online Editorial Intern

    Spin Magazine

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    September 2009 — Present (6 months)

    Assist online editorial staff by writing blog posts, continually searching for music news and contributing blurbs to daily round up, reviewing concerts, formatting text, producing albums reviews, transcribing interviews, and other tasks. Research and pen weekly "Web Weirdness" and "Best of the Week" posts. Maintain an ongoing database of tour dates, assist with in-office "Spin Sessions". Also assist with administrative tasks like manning reception desk, answering phones, sorting mail, organizing received CDs and press releases. Helped manage SPIN's Twitter account by developing a "News" Twitter list -- assessed artists' Twitter accounts for legitimacy and added to list for daily review by editorial staff.

  • Net Radio Director/DJ

    WNYU Radio

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    May 2009 — Present (10 months)

    Oversee and develop sound of WNYU’s net programming. Tasks include training new DJs, airchecking DJs, scheduling and leading meetings, maintaining .ORG grid, reviewing and critiquing demos and mock shows, contributing to WNYU staff blog, and keeping close contact with staff members.

    Hosted the "The Sound Between" on WNYU.org, February '09 to August '09. Began producing "The Sound Between" in September '09.

    Began hosting the "The New Afternoon Show" on WNYU 89.1 FM in September '09, bringing the "best in new music" to the tri-state area every Monday from 4 to 7:30. Reviewed new releases on a weekly basis, with a focus on breaking alternative, independent artists to local music community. Interviewed live guests including Kurt Vile (Matador), tUnE-YaRds (4AD) and Male Bonding (Sub Pop).
