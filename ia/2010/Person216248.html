Wm Scott Patterson’s Summary

My current vocational passion centers around creative problem solving within the contractual risk transfer arena. I engage this passion in advising my sole client - Zurich - as to its obligations to its customers - policyholders - as defined by its contractual risk transfer products - insurance policies, and working with claims professionals, underwriters, producers, insureds, and claimants towards creative resolution of disputes regarding Zurich's obligations to the satisfaction of both Zurich and its customers.

I have worked within the property & casualty insurance industry for my entire career, starting at a large Florida litigation firm defending insureds in a wide variety of third party litigation, including premises, auto, construction defect, professional, and product liability. After ten years of liability defense, I transitioned to coverage. In different in-house and private practice settings, I have represented insurers and policyholders in a wide variety of complex liability coverage and extra-contractual matters. My coverage form expertise includes CGL, D&O, EPLI, PL, WC/EL, HO, auto, excess & umbrella, ocean marine, and inland marine.

I am licensed to practice law before all state and federal courts in Florida, Maryland, and the District of Columbia, as well as the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fourth and Eleventh Circuits.

I am regularly invited to speak at industry events, most recently at the Lima Solutions Value Engineered ADR: New Approaches for Construction and CD Dispute Resolution, and MC Consultants East Region Construction Defect & Insurance Coverage Conference.

Wm Scott Patterson’s Specialties:

Contractual risk transfer; property & casualty insurance coverage; extra-contractual/bad faith claims; complex liability claim strategy and resolution; insurance product development.
