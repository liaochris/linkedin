Wes Wingfield’s Summary

Wes Wingfield is the President of Highest Ambition LLC, a Kansas City based company that specializes in Executive Coaching, Strategic Planning and Leadership/Organizational Development.

He is affiliated with Resource Associates Corporation of Reading Pennsylvania enabling his company to meet his client's needs locally and nationally.

Wes spent nearly nine years as an IT Director & College Administrator for the Metropolitan Community College District based at the Administration Center in Kansas City, Missouri.

He lead several business & information technology consulting departments with companies both locally and regionally.
He was a Product Manager in Marketing at Sprint World Headquarters and has experience working with Financial, Health Care, Education, and Government sectors.

His industry experience includes the insurance, manufacturing, telecommunications, information technology and broadcasting industries. He also holds numerous professional business and technical certifications in these and other fields.

He spent 12 years in commercial television engineering including WTVJ (CBS at that time) in Miami Florida, KTHV (CBS ) in Little Rock Arkansas, ESPN Headquarters in Bristol Connecticut, and WDAF-TV in Kansas City Missouri.

Wes is a native Texan that spent his high school and early college years in the New York City area. He spent two years of undergraduate work in Communications at Queens College of the City University of New York and finished a BA in Broadcasting/ Business at the University of Miami in Coral Gables Florida.

He has completed post graduate work in education and research at Western Governors University based in Salt Lake City and has a Masters Degree in Organizational Management from the University of Phoenix at the Kansas City Campus.

He is also a (CBC) Certified Business Coach, RAC Certified Facilitator, a Certified InnerMetrix Axiological Report Interpreter and a Graduate of the RAC Coaching Academy.
