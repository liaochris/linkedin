Paula Lemmo, PMP®’s Summary

Innovative and dedicated PMI certified Project Manager with a passion for inspiring organizations to achieve synergies between people, process and technology to realize organizational efficiencies.

Hands-on leader with a proven ability to initiate and drive both IT and process improvement projects that deliver savings in both dollars and effort. Proven track record as an innovative problem solver, motivational leader and collaborative business partner. Highly regarded for initiative, integrity, adaptability and the ability to deliver both tactical and strategic results.

Selected Accomplishments:

● Managed complex business process improvement and IT projects for a leading provider of Financial and TBO/HR Outsourcing services with an emphasis on rapidly driving down costs and improving transaction processing capabilities.
● Recognized as an innovation champion with an Innovation Challenge Award for ideas leading to new business development opportunities.
● Led and contributed to projects that reduced operating costs by several million dollars and improved customer experience by modifying business processes.
● Led project in call center that reduced average call handle time and improved first call resolution by 20%. Improved associate user experience and compliance of best practices leading to consistent and accurate information provided to customers and improved call scores.
● Eliminated need to take call center associates off the phones for training on process changes by 100% by developing a training program to deliver computer-based training programs.
● Provided leadership for end-to-end QA efforts for strategic cross-practice projects, monthly code releases, bi-weekly configuration releases and a mission-critical Annual Enrollment Project.
