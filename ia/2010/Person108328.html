Julie Collins’s Summary

We are seeking leaders who are interested in launching new C2C Chapters nationwide.

Quick overview of people who excel in each position:

Special Events Manager - hospitality experience, event management, fundraising, likes working with NPOs (typically already has a full or part time job)

Business Solutions Manager: consultant, business coach, consultative sales professional, strategic selling, experienced interpreting assessment reports and offering solutions (typically self-employed or employed in a sales role -this is a great way to highlight the company this person already works for and gain additional exposure!)

Executive Director: entrepreneurial, experience as a public speaker, great leadership skills (typically self employed or looking to make the ED position a full time career)

Commit2Connect is dedicated to revitalizing the spirit of entrepreneurialism and championing the causes of charitable organizations through local chapters in every American city.

These Commit2Connect chapters, empowered by local leadership, connect members who are dedicated to stimulating their own local economies. Members help members by adopting community projects and developing valuable new professional relationships.

• Opportunities to communicate and collaborate online and at live networking events

• Coordination of fundraising and volunteer programs to benefit local non-profit organizations with the support of local businesses

• Promoting growth and profitability using a complimentary business assessment and report interpretation facilitated by Local Leaders

The dedication of the Founders working alongside a diverse Advisory Council, and with a host of National Alliance Partners who are equally committed to the professional objectives, provides fresh perspectives and proven experiences needed to help our members build long term business stability and growth opportunities for Commit2Connect local leaders and members nationwide.
