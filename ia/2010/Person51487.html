David Mezzapelle’s Summary

David's Professional Experience:

Founded GoliathJobs Inc., the parent company of GoliathJobs.com (for students & alumni) and JobsOver50.com (for baby boomers & retirees).

In 2009 GoliathJobs merged with SilverCensus to form the new Goliath-SilverCensus, the premier resource for senior living & health services. See http://tinyurl.com/mgu5e8 .

Prior to GoliathJobs, David founded Goliath Technology in 1990 - a data center procurement & integration company and a global leader in IT distribution.

While at Goliath Technology David created the Goliath ABA Program (Academic Business Advancement). This program included staffing of college students & recent graduates for jobs/internships throughout North America. This program yielded significant buzz and intensified the company’s growth at a rapid pace. David sold the company in 2007 and has focused his efforts on GoliathJobs.

David Mezzapelle’s Experience

  • Founder, Director of Marketing & Development

    Goliath-SilverCensus, Inc

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    January 2006 — Present (4 years 5 months)

    UPDATE: GoliathJobs and SilverCensus have merged to form Goliath-SilverCensus, Inc., “The Premier Resource for Senior Living & Health Services."

    About SilverCensus:
    SilverCensus is an unbiased senior living and health services resource where consumers are educated on options, facilities & rights. Our services are completely free for seniors as well as hospital Case Managers & Social Workers. Clients, including nursing homes, assisted living residences, rehabilitation facilities, home healthcare agencies & independent living communities are offered tools to market their organization such as directory listings, SEO profiles, video/photo galleries and much more.

    Clients utilize our service under a no-fee placement agreement or a flat-rate monthly agreement depending on their needs. What makes us different is the fact that all referrals are fully qualified by our care managers before distribution to our clients. In addition, we penetrate powerful marketing channels including doctors, hospitals & senior services. Plus we provide free job posting capability as well as discounted headhunting services to reduce or eliminate HR costs. We also handle VA-related applications & submissions at no charge for veterans and their spouses.

    About GoliathJobs/Jobsover50:
    We connect job seekers to employers via schools & alma maters. GoliathJobs creates partnerships with schools throughout North America which serve as liaisons. This model delivers a powerful career service edge to schools at no charge (and lifelong career services), a competitive edge to job seekers and high-quality results to employers.

    JobsOver50.com is a free web-based employment service for Baby Boomers & Retirees built on the same platform.

    We believe that employment starts with education regardless of age, experience or educational level. We also believe that connecting job seekers to employers through schools & alma maters is the optimum way to secure a job or find an employee.
