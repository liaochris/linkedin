Laura van Straaten’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
Editorial/content leader with a good head for partnerships, marketing, PR, UI and digital strategy and a dozen years of experience in TV journalism, content development production and online media.

Since 1994 I have worked as a journalist, producer, director, editor, writer, consultant, show-runner and executive in development and production. I'm most interested in the intersection of editorial development and entrepreneurial ventures; in launching new content, no matter what the medium; and in helping traditional media companies and talent to use video and other media in an entrepreneurial fashion to create exciting and dynamic experiences.

COMMUNITY AND CAUSES
I am currently working on two charitable endeavors as well. I'm creating a public-private program to train thousands of people in CPR at health clubs and exercise studios in 2010. And I'm developing a multiplatform program to enable citizens to use their mobile phones to document and broadcast violations of local environmental laws and ecological best-practices so that business owners (even those in far-away corporate headquarters) will be crowd-shamed into compliance.
