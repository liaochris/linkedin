Hugo Aponte’s Summary

Leader of Batanga’s development efforts and IT department for around 6 years, being a key player of its growth not only as the largest Latin Internet Radio Network but also the largest Hispanic Ad Network in US, reaching around 18MM unique visitors per month.
Teacher of topics on Web and Internet Applications at Universidad Central de Venezuela for 5 years, defining new courses oriented to Web 2.0 applications management.
My career started at Petroleos de Venezuela (PDVSA) as researcher and developer resulting in several real time web applications developed applying artificial intelligence concepts, several papers published and patents.
I hold a Bachelor degree in Computer Science and specialization course in Artificial Intelligence.
