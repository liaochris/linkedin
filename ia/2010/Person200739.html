Ted Stearns’s Summary

HARD-HITTING with an impressive background for a 34-year old.

Ted Stearns is a serial entrepreneur, Board of Advisor for the Center for Wealth and Legacy, Sr. Estate Planner for the Guardian, Chief Operating Officer for the Wealth and Legacy Group; Co-Founder of the San Diego CEO Roundtable, Chairman of San Diego based iMerchantAdvance and Partner of Aegean Capital Partners.

“As an entrepreneur myself, my passion is to help create an environment where business owners have the best odds at success,” says Stearns.

Stearns is not a newcomer to the world of finance. His experience began with Merrill Lynch and AG Edwards, then with ADM and as an options and futures trader 13 years ago.

As a financial advisor hosting “Money in the Morning” a live radio show on AM 1000 KCEO based in San Diego for a number of years, he instructed callers and listeners of the capital markets. “I received my first real hands on, in the trenches experience of the capital markets when I was a futures trader with ADM,” says Stearns. “The experience at that young age gave me a genuine appreciation for the opportunities we have as Americans to follow our entrepreneurial instincts”

Ted has always had a passion to help America’s entrepreneur, “the American entrepreneur is the life blood of world economies, I have never met a more creative, ambitious and hardworking class in the world. Where I come in is to simple help my clients exploit their initiatives and capitalize on the larger markets.

Stearns balances his time between managing his career as an entrepreneur and visionary and working as an advocate for the America CEO, Chairman and Executives.

“My experiences have shaped my future, my aim is to help inspire innovation in others to pursue their vision and lead extraordinary lives as well.”

Ted Stearns’s Specialties:

I am a serial entrepreneur and visionary always seeking to leverage my extensive experience in starting, growing, managing and exiting firms. My niche is to identify strategic opportunities, target candidate partners and develop and execute an engagement process that results in getting and growth business.
