Kim Brooks’s Summary

Kim Brooks is the author of Black Expressions' Bestseller, "He's Fine...But is He Saved?" (Kimani Press/Harlequin) its highly acclaimed sequel, "He's Saved...But is He For Real?" (Kimani Press/Harlequin) and non-fiction mini-book "The Little Black Survival Book For Single Saints" (Driven Enterprises, LLC)

As a licensed minister and 31-year-old virgin with abstinence until marriage as her platform, Kim is a contributing singles columnist for Anointed Pages Magazine, Hope for Women Magazine, and she authors and distributes a monthly eNewsletter for Christian singles entitled, "The Single Heart" that currently ministers to over 2,500 people and is subscribable through her website http://www.kimontheweb.com

Goals:

*More non-fiction and fiction books for singles
*Stage play for "He's Fine...But is He Saved?" (looking for production team for possible collaboration)
*Song publishing deals for the many songs written by Kim which promote abstinence and speak to the hearts of single women, as well as traditional gospel songs
*Acting

Read Free excerpts of her books and view book trailer on www.KimontheWeb.com

Email: kimbrooks@kimontheweb.com or, for booking info for your next singles, women's, or youth conference, call 1-313-541-2856

Kim Brooks’s Specialties:

Powerful, Word-based ministry to singles as it pertains to fulfilling one's purpose, remaining content as a single Christian, and how to avoid temptation in today's sex-driven society.

Workshops on Christian Book Publishing and Marketing from the standpoint of self-publishing and working with a major publishing house.
