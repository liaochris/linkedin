Tony Bechara’s Summary

Crocodile Special Vehicles is Division of The T135SS Corp.
The mission established for the division was design and manufacture of composite body, assemble that body on custom car platform, with engine and suspension adapted for better performance and finished compatible with a high end world class sport car.
T135SS will make a joint venture with VFS Ltda to perform a new firm; the VFS do Brasil SA,which runs in Brazil manufacturing SKD kit cars.
When operating the Crocodile Carrera will sold as Puma GT and a new Puma GTB will be offered.
T135SS will invest about US$ 400,000 in this joint venture.
As the some time T135SS buy a body shop for the operations of the Crocodile Division. For this purpose T135SS will invest about US$ 600,000, performing 1M in investment.
The capital to finance this expansion will be obtained by shares subscription by private investors both from Brazil and USA.
The company will sold 1,86 million of preferred shares in blocks of US$ 1,000 each, that represents 18.6% of the total capital of the company.
Year five sales and EBIT are projected in excess of US$ 19.0 million and 60%, respectively.
The relevant fact is that the VFS SA is licensee Puma Veiculos e Motores the world recognized Brazilian brand sport cars, with rights exclusivity on all Puma’s model and new models development.
These rights will be sub licensee to T135SS for the USA.
The 1, 3 & 5 Super Stores Corporation is a Florida chapter S firm privately owned by Antonio Bechara dos Santos and Roseli Guedes de Oliveira.
The VFS Veículos Fora de Serie do Brasil Ltda is a Brazilian firm owned by Luis Henrique Silva and Wladimir Silva.
By this joint venture T135SS exchange 30% of your capital shares by 50% of the VFS SA capital shares.
