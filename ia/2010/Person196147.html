Steve Wallace’s Summary

Steve Wallace has over 30 years of experience in sales and marketing of high technology products and services. He has had extensive international experience - working in South America, Europe and the Pacific Rim.

He started his career with Ontel Corporation in the 70’s whose innovative OP1 “Ontel Programmable” intelligent computer set the standard for the PC that was to follow… and yes the term PC had not yet been coined.

Steve successfully introduced the OP1 to Olivetti ICO, Siemens, Prime Computer and Telefunken AEG; to name but a few. Additionally he developed a distribution network for Ontel in South America called Latin Data as well as with Prime Computer in Australia and CDC in Taiwan.

Most recently Steve was with DEX – Data Exchange Corp; as VP of Sales, where he identified and successfully engaged major new Reverse Logistic supply chain opportunities.
