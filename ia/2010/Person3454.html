  • Moderator and Co-Founder

    #ufprchat

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2010 — Present (10 months)

    #ufprchat was modeled after other well-known Twitter chats, such as #journchat, #custserv, etc. It is a way for University of Florida College of Journalism and Communications students and alumni to network and interact while discussing the use of social media as a public relations tool.

    I initiated and led the first #ufprchat with Caitlin Murphy, @TravelPRgirl. The first chat took place on Tuesday, March 23, 2010 and received outstanding feedback from Gator students and professionals across the country. Approximately 70 people contributed 577 tweets with the #ufprchat hashtag. You can track the #ufprchat hashtag at WThashtag.com.

    ufprchat.com was created in conjunction with #ufprchat so that followers may review transcripts, job posts for Gators by Gators and other useful, industry-related information.
