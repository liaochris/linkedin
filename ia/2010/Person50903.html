David Black, Of Counsel at Jackson Lewis, LLP’s Summary

With 16 years of focused workplace law experience, several jury trials, teaching, public speaking and publishing experience, I exclusively advise HR departments and C-level executives, represent companies in litigation and trials regarding workplace issues, and provide training to HR executives, professionals and other attorneys regarding labor and employment law topics.

Outside of the courtroom, I focus on training, delivering strategic advice, and implementing litigation risk avoidance policies and practices.

If litigation has already commenced, I focus on delivering efficient, business-oriented, practical advice. The goal is a speedy successful resolution, often employing creative strategies.


Key Practice Terms: employment litigation and advice, union negotiation, discrimination, retaliation, drug testing, disability management, FMLA, ADA, FLSA, workplace privacy, social media policy, veterans, USERRA, federal, national, Washington, Oregon, Seattle, Portland
