Emily Bardeen’s Summary

Emily Bardeen, of emilyoncareers.com, is passionate about helping her clients achieve career and job search success!

An experienced career advisor, Emily has over 15 years of experience working with thousands of clients on job and career searches, educational planning and more.

Whether providing online, telephone or in-person coaching, Emily is skilled at adjusting her approach to support each client's personality and professional needs. She is known for her practical approach and thought-provoking advice.

Emily also consults on job, career and related human resource management issues. She is a regular contributor to workherway.com and a top expert on allexperts.com. She has been quoted in The Washington Post and The Daily News and her writing has appeared online and in print.

Emily Bardeen’s Specialties:

Resumes, interview coaching, job search strategy; job offer negotiation; career assessments, career planning and strategy; education planning and strategy; dual career planning and strategy.

Organizational career deveopment; organizational development; human resources management.
