Shalini Verma’s Summary

The Skyscrapers Academy partners with individuals/organizations to develop leaders and employees to achieve more success and satisfaction in both their personal and professional lives.

With a primary focus on leadership development, strengthening business relationships, conflict management, team building we act as a catalyst for change when performance requires a tune-up, turn-around or new focus.

With an inspired zeal to make a difference in the lives of individuals who want to go beyond the obvious, Shalini Verma, Founder - The Sky Scrapers Academy, has for the past several years co-actively engaged herself in the profession of helping people skyrocket in their personal and professional quest.

Her intensive approach involves assessment and observation to increase self-awareness and self-understanding. This allows for behavior change, personal growth and effective performance both at the individual and team level.

Accredited as Associate Certified Coach by the International Coach Federation, she recieved her coach training at International Coach Academy, Australia.

Extremely passionate about helping women leaders in their endeavour to achieve personal and professional success, she has also been actively engaged in assisting mid-career professionals redefine success, identify their ‘unique strengths’, and create more focus, vitality, and passion in their work.
