Chris Chandler’s Summary

I have been designing websites since 1998 when I first got a Windows 95 computer from Compuzone in Huntsville, AL. I learned to code html by hand on notepad and created images in paint. I later began to use other tools such as Frontpage & Dreamweaver when I needed to get the job done quicker. I started a company called Outdoor Tuff that made $30K in 6 months, but after some growing pains, the business folded. From there, I emerged to start a business of website design in 2001 by going from business marketing my services. My first job was a restraunt in Albertville, AL called Country Farm Family Buffet. Since that time, I have gained most of my new clients via word of mouth.

I have experience in almost anything internet related. I know html, xml (rss), search engine optimization, website design, graphics, internet marketing, php programming, flash, java, hosting, Wordpress... you name just about anything internet related, I can do it. I work with software such as Photoshop, Photo Impact, Dreamweaver, Frontpage/Expressions, Wordpress, Flash, and many many others.

My professional goals are to provide the best customer service that I can possibly give and to provide the best ROI for my clients.
