Ken Newman’s Summary

MAGNET PRODUCTIONS:

Ken Newman and his company, Magnet Productions have been developing live trade show presentations since 1985. High Tech, Retail Banking, Consumer Electronics, Automotive, Health Care, and other industries have used Magnet to help build their trade show presence and increase sales.

Magnet's presentations utilize humor, theatre, improvisation, and magic as powerful tools to generate booth traffic and to communicate their clients' messages. These clients include many leading Fortune 100 Companies, including Cisco, Microsoft, HP, Apple, Visa International and Genentech.

THE FUSION GROUP:

Our goal is simple: To take a room full of individuals and turn them into a group who knows how to work together and, more importantly, wants to. To turn strangers into friends, co-workers into colleagues. Creating a team is more than just getting people to know one another. It's more than just having a common game plan. It's about giving people the tools and the freedom to express their ideas. The freedom to look foolish. The freedom to fail.

Using a variety of improvisational exercises, we will develop the mindset and create the environment that encourages creative expression. That shows how 'crazy ideas' can become brilliant solutions. That allows your people to laugh together. To communicate. To share their 'crazy ideas.'
