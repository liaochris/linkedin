Jacqui Buchanan’s Summary

Strategic communications, media, and marketing consultant specializing in leadership branding, innovative communication and marketing campaigns (ext/int), video/broadcast production, talent=for-hire (on-camera/in-person/voice over). Jacqui has vast experience and networks across hi-tech, Silicon Valley, media, diversity, Africa and more.

As US national co-chair for not-for-profit South African American Business Chamber (SAABC) Jacqui drives vision, communications and operations for a business network of South African expats and affiliates, facilitating connections of peers, mentors, and clients. Proceeds benefit development of next gen leaders and empowerment of disadvantaged communities.

As Chief of Staff for Deloitte's Diversity/Women’s Initiative Jacqui drove leader/initiative eminence, helped develop/execute strategy, and managed operations. She built an extensive national and global network of leaders focused on advancement and empowerment of women and minorities, e.g. Dr Sally Ride (first American Women in space), Jane Swift (former Governor, Massachusetts), Peggy Klaus (author, How to Toot Your Own Horn) and many leading diversity organizations.

Jacqui spent 5 years with Deloitte Sr Marketing Management leading strategic marketplace approach for Industry and firm initiatives, specifically global Technology, Media and Telecommunications clients such as Intel, Cisco, HP, Yahoo, eBay etc.

For 14+ yrs Jacqui produced/directed/presented for numerous national and international broadcast networks and corporations. As Silicon Valley Foreign Correspondent for Murdoch’s "Sky News" and Dot TV she covered hi-tech, business, financial markets for 80 million+ global audience. Interviews included political/corporate leaders such as Presidents Nelson Mandela and F.W. De Klerk, Intel's Gordon Moore, Sun’s Scott McNealy, and Amazon’s Jeff Bezos, and many more.

Interests.. safari, scuba, salsa, fine wine and food, warm beaches, documentary, inspiring people.
