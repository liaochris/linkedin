Wayne Pagani - MCRS, MCIS, MCCS’s Summary

"connecting people with people, resources, and opportunities"

Career Management and Professional Development services.

Wayne has been coaching people from all walks of life for over fifteen years. He is a talented certified résumé strategist who provides unparalleled service delivery in the career development field complemented by sound management experience in the corporate world.

A seasoned professional with extensive expertise in résumé, interview, and career strategy, Wayne has increased marketability for a broad range of clientele. A creative writer, he is skilled at crafting compelling résumés and career portfolios.

Wayne is an ambassador for the industry and several foremost career services organizations. As a result, Career Professionals of Canada awarded Wayne the position of judge for the annual Awards of Excellence, where he has assessed the capabilities of résumé writers nationally.

An active leader within Career Professionals of Canada, Wayne is an assessor for the Certified Résumé Strategist credential, where he evaluates the capabilities of career practitioners and résumé writers across Canada who are applying for the prestigious credential. Additionally, Wayne also fills the role of Conference and Events Advisor, where he is spearheading the annual conference for career practitioners across Canada.

Positioned at the forefront of the nation’s capital, Wayne has been featured in the Ottawa Business Journal and the Nepean Weekender. In addition, he supports the Ottawa - Employment Networking Group where he has participated in various sub-committees contributing to the enhancement of services in the region.

Excellent knowledge and a grasp of how to effectively motivate clients to market themselves in today’s competitive business world. Passionate about networking and the privilege to be part of the process in witnessing people attain their career aspirations.

Available for subcontract and individual client engagements.
