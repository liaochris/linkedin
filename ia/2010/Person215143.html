Whit Tice {LION}’s Summary

I am a LinkedIn Open Networker (LION), which means I welcome connecting with anyone on LinkedIn in order to foster enduring positive relationships and networking.

Professionally, I am an MBA (2008) and MPOD graduate (2009) both from Case Western Reserve University (top 50 MBA, OD dept top in the world majority of years in the past decade) with several years of work in technology and management consulting arenas. I have demonstrated leadership, strategic organizational consulting, team coordination, product/project management, standard and process adherence, and problem solving. The combination of my MBA, Computer Science degree and work experience allows me to bridge the gap between business and technology. The MPOD degree gives me the tools and experience to bring it all together through organizational development and synthesis. My background makes me able to effectively drive and implement projects through solid decision making, incorporation of stakeholders at the right time and place, and follow through to ensure organizational evolution brings it all together.

With my independent consulting and company engagement, I focus on the following areas:
• Organizational change management and project management activities to ensure direction, fit, and results
• Product and project management
• Strategic alignment of resources and capabilities to win in the market
• Sustainability initiatives to cultivate and develop improvement for people, planet, and profit (Triple Bottom Line Sustainability - 3P)
• Coaching individuals for career advancement, visioning, and leadership development

I am seeking challenging positions in the greater Seattle and Bellevue areas that will build on my experience and education. Additionally, I am always interested in short term consulting opportunities.
