Alex Converse’s Summary

I'm an audio and video codec specialist. I have five years of experience implementing codecs on the PC and in embedded platforms. I have detailed knowledge of many MPEG specifications. I have experience, implementing, optimizing, debugging, and hardening in both open source and proprietary settings.

I offer FFmpeg consulting services, contract work, and priority support. Contact me for more information.
