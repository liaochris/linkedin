Logan Anjaneyulu’s Summary

I have about 10 years of Engineering and Management Consulting experience, primararily in the Heavy Civil, Energy, Oil and Gas Industry.

I currently serve as the Principal of Prilogsys, an Engineering Management Consulting firm specializing in the areas of Project Controls and Project Management offering services to Energy, Oil and Gas Industry clients.

At the Client location, I work as a Project Controls Manager (consultant), managing project controls efforts on a $100 plus Million a year combined Capital, Venture, and Maintenance Budgets.

I was assigned as an Engineer on the $ 1.2 Billion Benicia-Martinez Segmental Bridge Project in Bay Area, CA.

My areas of interest include Startegy, Marketing, Finance, and General Management. I am currently pursuing my MBA at the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University.
