Robert P.D. Kerr’s Summary

I have spent the past ten years working in the fields of investment banking and equity research for prominent Wall Street institutions, including JPMorgan, Bear Stearns and Bank of America. At Kerr Financial, I am currently dedicated to serving our high net worth clients' planning and investment management needs. I perform rigorous portfolio analysis and detailed due diligence of our investment managers. I hope to broaden our stable of managers over time, while developing client assets in both the U.S. and Canada.

Robert P.D. Kerr’s Specialties:

I maintain both my NASD series 7 and 63 securities licenses. Also, I am pursuing an Executive MBA at the University of Chicago Booth of Business.
