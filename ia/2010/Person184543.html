Scott Madsen’s Summary

MCB-Madison is a boutique investment bank that is both a Direct Lending Source and a Capital Placement Intermediary skilled in arranging both Debt and Equity Placements for Commercial Real Estate Properties throughout the United States. Our Direct Lending Programs range from Equity / Joint Venture placements to Mezzanine Finance and “Hard Money” placements.

MCB-Madison Finance operates from offices on the East Coast and the Midwestern United States…

MCB-Madison Finance has an active Loan Re-structuring service since the 1990's.. Market conditions have impacted all properties and Borrowers and many are facing mismatched existing high loan balances versus the sometimes “too low” new loan amount offers. For other Borrowers, the recession has corroded income streams and pushed Tenants beyond their ability to survive.

In such cases, MCB-Madison is able to negotiate debt service reductions, loan term extensions and even loan balance discounts. MCB-Madison Staff members have decades of Work-Out experience involving nearly 2 Billion in CRE assets. With a staff that includes former Bank Owners, Senior Loan Officers and veterans of CRE property investment, we know how to see and negotiate these situations from all the angles.
