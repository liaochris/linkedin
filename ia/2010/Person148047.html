Mohamad Turk {LION}’s Summary

Mohamad started his professional career with Lebanese American University. He was assigned in the University Library as a Desktop Support. He later joined Libancall, a Telecommunication Service Provider. Mohamad was assigned as a Development Engineer – Project Management. In this capacity, he was mainly responsible for Project management and development, Business Development, Key Account Management, Relationship Management Customer Support Operations.

Mohamad joined Rasd Ltd. – member of the Amrop Hever Group in early 2008 as Senior Researcher. By January 2009, Mohamad was promoted to Associate Consultant.
