Scott Kinnaird’s Experience

  • CEO

    a la mode, inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    2001 — Present (9 years )

    Founded in 1985 by its Chairman, David Biggers, a la mode has become the leader in real estate technology by developing software, workflow management and e-commerce technology for a wide range of participants in the mortgage lending and home selling processes.

    a la mode's WinTOTAL appraisal management software is used by more than half the real estate appraisers in North America, a market share greater than its next four competitors combined. The company also provides XSites for mortgage brokers, loan officers, home inspectors and REALTORS®. Lead management and workflow, property listings, and dynamic online loan apps are but a few of the services XSites provide approximately 100,000 real estate professionals, nation wide.

    a la mode’s Mercury Network, a vendor management platform for lenders, provides the most experienced local appraisers in an HVCC-compliant, easy-to-use loan ordering and management application.

    Privately held, a la mode is headquartered in Oklahoma City with satellite offices in Salt Lake City, Orlando and Washington D.C.
