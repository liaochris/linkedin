Philip Markwick’s Summary

Philip Markwick is an experienced and talented Web: Project Leader, SEO, Developer & Content Specialist. He has completed many briefs using a range of skills with established applications. He most enjoys projects that are customer facing and require learning something new. He has undoubted talent for communication and has excellent customer facing skills. He has planned and managed with character and enthusiasm to carry it through to the next milestone. Educated to a Masters level, he is a ‘self starter’ with years of experience managing and liaising prospects, clients and partners. Experienced with a range of technologies, he understands that the web is fast moving and he is continually training himself to meet dynamic business needs. He is used to managing professional designers and developers within objective orientated projects, and understands the importance of defining ‘completion’ reaching it. Philip is committed to not only succeed within his chosen field, but to become an acknowledged expert within his industry.
He is experienced with, and enjoys giving presentations and training clients/staff to use new and effective technologies such as Content Management Systems (CMS) and tools such as Web Analytics. He believes that the best strategies are based on fundamentals: Intelligent research that focuses on the ‘Wants and Needs’ of real people. Useful and compelling content creation that addresses these ‘Wants & Needs’. Effective and appealing design based on the fundamentals of typography and graphics. And ethics based on respect for web users and their experience. He has actualised whole projects around these principles and has created ‘AIDA’ focused information architecture for funnelling for best metrics. Web Experience that are compelling, effortlessly simple and effective. Philip believes in the ‘Don’t make me think’ philosophy of design content creation, W3C standards, Web2.0 technologies that empower the non-expert to create good sites.
