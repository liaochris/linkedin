Bill Pace’s Summary

Writing a 10 part webseries based on prize-winning short film, ECHO CANYON. Using this to help create my syllabus for a new screenwriting class I'm teaching at the New School this fall, "Screenwriting for the Web".

My experience includes having written 4 independent feature films, all of which have received domestic and international distribution, and several episodes of various syndicated TV series.

I've also produced three independent feature films and directed one of those, CHARMING BILLY.

My short film, "A Relaxing Day" was written by Pulitzer Prize-nominated playwright Theresa Rebeck. It played festivals both here in the U.S. and internationally, and won several awards. A trailer for it can be seen on YouTube at:

<http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kA03fRcZwnE>

William directed a series of videos for the legendary singer Tony Bennett's arts foundation, Exploring The Arts, including a shoot with Mr. Bennett at the iconic Guggenheim Museum. The video premiered at a sold-out Tony Bennett show at Radio City Music Hall.

I also occasionally return to my theatrical roots and recently directed the original musical SOUTH, a work commissioned by noted innovative NYC theater Dixon Place.

Bill Pace’s Specialties:

Writing/directing/producing film & video and teaching at The New School. I also provide production and screenwriting consulting services for others utilizing my 19 years of teaching filmmaking and screenwriting in New York City.
