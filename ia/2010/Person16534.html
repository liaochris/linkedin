Arnim Van Lieshout’s Summary

I’m in the IT industry for 12 years, working mainly with Operating Systems, including AIX, HP-UX, Linux, Windows, Novell and Mac OSX.

I have a college degree in software development, which helps me out creating all those fine scripts, using Perl, VBScript and PowerShell. I’m certified on Novell (CNE), Microsoft NT, 2000, 2003 (MCSE), 2008 (MCTS), Citrix (CCA) and VMware (VCP).

I currently work for KEMBIT, a Dutch high-end IT solution provider, as a virtualization architect, where I’ve been focusing on virtualization for the last 5 years. Using VMware’s solutions, I help our enterprise customers getting the most out of virtualization, especially in server consolidation and business continuity.

Arnim Van Lieshout’s Specialties:

Designing virtualization solutions, Server consolidation through virtualization, Business continuity using virtualization
