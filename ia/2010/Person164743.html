Paul Evans’s Summary

I am an experienced hands-on executive who has the proven ability to contribute quickly and adapt to rapidly changing environments in both established & start-up companies. In my most recent roles of VP-Strategic Partnering & Business Development at Alita Pharmaceuticals and President and VP-Business Development at STADA Pharmaceuticals, I completed an extensive deal sheet covering agreements for licensing clinical stage assets, technical transfer, contract manufacturing, API supply, exclusive development, supply and distribution of oral solids and transdermals, and the divestment of under-utilized ANDAs and intellectual property, along with evaluating many acquisition candidates. At Alita, I strategically positioned and developed the company’s urology & obgyn development pipeline and executed the entire deal process from opportunity generation through due-diligence, negotiation and close. In addition, I have diverse functional capabilities across Human Resources, Operations, Legal and Regulatory and a working knowledge of Finance, Trade Relations and Commercial Operations. Because of my broad international experience, I have developed multi-cultural business relationships throughout the US, Europe, and India.
