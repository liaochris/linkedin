Pat Hooper’s Summary

With more than 15 years in the environmental architectural products industry, assist product manufacturers developing or launching sustainable products in the commercial sectors. Provide services to sort through certifications, testing protocols, green building rating systems, emerging regulations and sustainable trends to find the best path to sustainable product manufacturing, business practices, and gaining market share and product profitability. Guide clients in selecting third party certifications and entry into such design/purchasing systems as CHPS. Provide new product marketing focusing on early product placement. Have worked with national and international manufacturers in the flooring, wall covering, resin/bonding products, decorative sheet and site furnishing industries.

Pat Hooper’s Specialties:

Environmental certifications, new product launch, sustainable trends.
