Kelley Connors’s Summary

Women are key influencers and decision makers for marketers of OTC & RX products, health insurance products, assisted living communities, alternative medicine and cosmetic surgeries and more. The problem is..most women don’t believe healthcare marketers understand them.

The challenge is for companies to go beyond "targeting" to engagement in "ecosystems" where trust is held. As a business builder, marketer and PR strategist, I go beyond “messaging” to two-way and consumer-led dialogue, marketing with women through their shared support systems where trust and credibility is held.

Signature Strengths
Marketing WITH women, Insightful brand story development, patient advocacy and empowerment and conversation building.

Strategic planning projects focused on healthcare professionals, patients and real women/patients’ lives, desires, attitudes and beliefs.

I conduct workshops, design and execute innovative marketing strategies, and connect marketers directly with women by:

1) educating internal staff members on marketing healthcare to women
2) integrating the 4 C's - our approach to marketing healthcare to women
3) helping companies find the insights that can move their market with women as key influencers and decision makers.

Published work includes: DTC Perspectives (pharmaceutical industry), The Ireland Report (hospital industry), as well as webcasts with the Advertising Research Foundation, Marketing to the New Healthcare Consumer Conference, and Spirit of Women Annual Meeting.

Digital/public health case study was selected for presentation at the 2009 Public Relations Society of America Health Academy Meeting "A Web Intervention Strategy for Young Girls to Prevent Smoking".
