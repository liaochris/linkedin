Steven Yorra, MBA, CEC’s Summary

The Illumination Group is a business coaching consultancy, specializing in leadership development and career transition, using our unique process: "Personal Core Branding."

In this challenging economic climate, people are looking for a competitive advantage. Our mission is to help professionals, executives, business owners and people in transition connect with who they are, so they are more effective at what they do. The result is a dynamic boost in their level of performance and impact in the workplace.

"Personal Core Branding" offers a competitive advantage: from retaining and motivating valued employees; improving client service, to improving operational effeciencies, greater sales - and profit. We offer individual coaching and group workshops to help:

• improve leadership ability: manage change, strengthen company culture; recruit, retain and develop talent; and inspire others.

• develop employees' skills: their ability to communicate, collaborate and think more creatively

• navigate career transitions: from staff to senior level positions

For those in career transition, we can help you effectively package, reposition and showcase yourself to help you get established as a consultant, start a business, or transition to another professional career.


"Branding You for Success"
