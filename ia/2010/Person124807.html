Michele Mattia’s Summary

Michele, a nationally recognized speaker and creative strategist, designs, customized sessions to discover and highlight your unique gifts, talents and skills, utilizing imaginative techniques to create your individual, or your company's vision.

Life Design Strategy is a creative, professional partnership that motivates, inspires and supports you while you design your perfect life.

As a Strategist, I enable you to redesign your life’s dash from one of stagnation and negative energies to one filled with positivity and passion for living life.

It is because I believed in the beauty of my dreams that Life Design exists. I’ve experienced and survived the fears, risks, what-ifs and my perception of impossibilities. When we work together, you will find my strategy style high-energy, motivating, personal, nurturing, creative and fun. My goal is to co-create YOUR FUTURE based on the BEAUTY OF YOUR DREAMS.

I understand the importance of transparency and formulating your company’s, or future company’s, authentic voice and the roll Social Media now plays in customer/client relationships. Utilizing creative techniques, you will discover your vision and create a strategic plan encompassing marketability, research and development, as well as long- and short-term goals. Important skills will be developed and/or strengthened from writing business plans to industry networking.

This is about you. It is about manifesting your desires and dreams. It is about doing what you love and the rest of your life falling into place. It is about the question: What would you do now if you knew you could not fail?
