Debbie Allen’s Summary

"Mastering Excellence in Escrow" began with a commitment to excellence; a decisive point; a turning point; to take the path less traveled.

Debbie’s quest for excellence began with Lawyers Title in 1973; having just graduated with honors from Bishop Amat High School, while attending Mt. San Antonio College, Debbie traveled every day to Los Angeles to work in the “Special Projects Division” of Lawyers Title; the project which came to be known as ‘the Phillips Deal’, involving Phillips Petroleum Corp., Southland Corp., TOSCO Corp., and GATX Tank Storage Terminals Corp.

The gigantic deal amassed some 760 properties located in California, Oregon, Washington, and Nevada; 45 various counties in California; 85 title officers and over 2,000 documents. On April 1, 1976, Lawyers Title Insurance Corporation celebrated its 12th anniversary by closing the $225 million dollar deal; twice the number of transactions that LTIC offices in the state closed in their entire year of operation. After the closing, James Mullen, of Phillips Petroleum Company wrote a personal letter of acknowledgement to Debbie, declaring,

“…without your assistance the closing would have been most difficult,
if not impossible.”

The Road not Taken, is a poem by Robert Frost, published in 1916 in his collection
Mountain Interval, the narrator declares that taking the road “one less traveled by” “made all the difference,” can be seen as a declaration of the importance of independence and personal freedom.

Crossroads Escrow Services, Inc. is the evolution of career decisions where two roads meet; a decisive point; a turning point; to take the path less traveled.

Mastering Excellence in Escrow is the vision, path, and mission of Crossroads Escrow Services, Inc.

A commitment to excellence; taking the path “the one less traveled by”
…which “made all the difference.”

Debbie Allen’s Specialties:

Debbie is a an active member of the California Escrow Association and a board member of the Southeast Escrow Association, Debbie is an expert in all areas of the escrow transaction; sale/refinance, residential, multi-family, commercial, bulk-sale, 1031 tax exchange, short-sale & REO's. Debbie and her staff speak fluently in English, Spanish, Korean and Japanese.

Debbie Allen’s Experience

  • President / CEO

    Crossroads Escrow Services, Inc.

    (Real Estate industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    As a Certified Senior Escrow Officer, Debbie brings over 35-years of experience to the industry. She began her career with Lawyer’s Title in Los Angeles where she spent over 3 years in the "Special Projects Division". As Debbie’s knowledge grew she spent half-a-decade as assistant manager at 1st Whittier Escrow, a broker-owned escrow company located in Whittier, California. Debbie devoted over 25-years in Whittier developing her talent and capability for comprehending and measuring the “big-picture” and then reducing it to workable manageable terms. In the closing of tens-of-thousands of escrow transactions, Debbie has become an expert in all areas of the escrow transaction; residential, commercial, refinance, and complicated multi-leg 1031 tax exchange transactions. As one of the most successful escrow officers at North Orange County Escrow, in March of 2007, Debbie announced her desire to venture out and realize her dream; Crossroads Escrow Services, Inc.
