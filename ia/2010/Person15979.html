    ARDEPHWERK STORY...

    So what is the meaning of Ardephwerk?
    Ardephwerk is a abbreviation which stand for ART DESIGN PHOTOGRAPHY WERK.
    'Werk' is German for 'work' and in French 'Werk' mean œuvre.

    Ardephwerk Design Studios is a combination of artistic ability and technical savvy.
    In 2003 ,the Studio was created in Paris, France by an American expat Rachael Hampton a Multidisciplinary, Multilingual Designer.

    She helps people and companies worldwide to design their personal brand or business that 'Communicate' who they are and actually make a difference to theirs business and theirs customers.
    With over 13 years of experiences her knowledge, cross cultural experiences, training, and her technical savvy approach helps her clients build more distinctive on and offline identity.

    It is the combination of her passion in working closely with clients and companies and taking the time to listen to theirs needs and suggest the best possible solution, create compelling marketing materials and apply design strategies that accelerate their professional and business success.
    Ardephwerk practices one to one contact with people and companies without intermediaries. Committed to excellence, offering clients sophisticated design, innovative solutions and INDIVIDUAL ATTENTION.

    Based in France, Ardephwerk connects with individual clients and groups on-site and in virtual environments.
    Ardephwerk has expanded from PARIS to Nantes, Rennes, Lille, Lyon, London, Doubaï, China and the USA.
