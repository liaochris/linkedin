Shannan Boyer’s Summary

I am the Director of Social Media at Wordsworth Communications, Cincinnati’s largest independent public relations agency. My professional background includes more than nine years of experience in local, regional & national consumer media relations, social media strategy and execution, special event planning, spokesperson training, and employee communications.

Leading the agency's social media efforts, I am responsible for evaluating client marketing plans and guiding strategic social media tactics as well as presenting on various social media topics to clients and outside groups and organizations.

Outside of work I am co-founder of Cincinnati Women Bloggers, the Tri-State's only organization specifically dedicated to connecting women in the social media community.

I have spoken on social media and media relations topics to a number of groups, including Leadership Northern Kentucky, the Northern Kentucky Chamber of Commerce, and the Cincinnati Chapter of the Public Relations Society of America. I have been featured in the Cincinnati Enquirer and Soapbox Cincinnati as well as appeared on Fox19, WLWT News 5 and on Blog Talk Radio on various social media topics.

I received my Bachelor’s degree in Communication from Northern Kentucky University in 2000 and I am a member of the 2008 class of Leadership Northern Kentucky.
