Sarah Waxler’s Summary

As the founder and owner of a 24 year old travel agency in North Carolina, I divide my time between two worlds I love: travel and commerce. In addition to managing the business and marketing side of things I also continue to work directly with vacation clients as well. My focus in the past year has been the expansion of my knowledge and talent for creative, extensive, and affordable advertising into our online presence, especially the relatively young social media markets. I have also obtained HUB certification for the travel agency and maintain an A+ ratng with the Better Business Bureau.

I lead a network of in-office employees and home-based independent professional travel agents throughout the Southeast, including Specialists in France, Spain, Japan, Mexico, Australia, the Caribbean, Canada, Alaska, Las Vegas, Disney, Sandals, cruises, honeymoons, destination weddings, and corporate travel. Amongst us we have 300 years of travel experience.

While I'm proud of my marketing strategies and superb team, I'm even happier to say I still believe in--and base my business upon--the value of relationships, local business, community, civic engagement, and old school business ethics. I have served on the Board of Directors at Triangle Day School and Judea Reform Congregation. With two sons, I try to contribute to the success of whatever their current projects or organiztions are.

Our customers clearly approve of our excellent service and knowledge: For the last four years in a row Travel Leaders has received the Reader's Choice award for best travel agency in Durham!
