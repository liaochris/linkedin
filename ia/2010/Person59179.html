Doug Schade, Esq.’s Summary

I currently work as a Senior Consultant for the white-shoe executive search firm Winter, Wyman & Company.

Winter Wyman & Company is one of the oldest (35+ years) and Largest Executive Search Firms in the Northeast. For 09, we received the following accolades:
* Inc. 5000 "Fastest Growing Companies"
* Boston Business Journal - Largest Executive Search Firm in Massachusetts
* A Staffing Industry Analysts' "Fastest Growing Staffing Firm"
* Named #1 Small Employer on The Boston Globe's Top Places to Work List

Our NY practice specifically focuses on placing the top 10% mid-to-C level candidates with our clients within a 40-mile radius around NYC. We work both on a retained & contingency basis and our niche specialty falls within the 80-300k+ salary range.

We work with a Diversified Portfolio of Clients in several industries, including: Financial Services (From the large I-Banks who produced record profits in the downturn to private alternative investment funds using state of the art computer-driven high frequency trading methods); Retail/Fashion (From the leading name brand designers geared towards mass consumption to niche designers who continue to buck the odds & redefine high-fashion); Marketing/Advertising/PR (From the Firms that shaped modern messaging to guerilla marketing start-ups); Non-Profit (From the large budget international entities tackling the important issues of the day at a macro level to the community based entities); Legal (From the largest international firms handling Fortune 500 M&A to the small boutique firms tackling intellectual property cases with foreign offenders) & Consumer Products (From the companies we rely on for our daily needs to those companies that provide us with hours of music/tv/video entertainment).
