Steve Siebold’s Summary

Former professional athlete/national coach turned author/speaker on mental toughness training for sales teams. Wrote business best-seller, 177 Mental Toughness Secrets of The World Class, which sold over 100,000 copies worldwide. Television appearances include The Today Show, Good Morning America, ABC News, FOX, CBS, BBC, NBC Australia. Created Mental Toughness University in 1999, which has helped corporations increase sales by millions of dollars. Over 25,000 sales people and entrepreneurs in 10 countries are graduates of Mental Toughness University and Mental Toughness College. Partnered with Bill Gove, the father of professional speaking, in 1996 to form the Gove-Siebold Group. Company conducts the Bill Gove Speech Workshop, which has trained more million-dollar professional speakers than any program in history. Ranked among the top 1% of professional speakers worldwide.

Steve Siebold’s Specialties:

Mental Toughness University teaches people how to think like professional athletes in high pressure, high performance sales. Bill Gove Speech Workshop teaches people the system that has created more million-dollar professional speakers than any program in history.
