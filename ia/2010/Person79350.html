Haig White’s Summary

Performed and managed full-cycle recruiting responsibilities for companies traversing growth technologies from PCs to video communications, graphics, broadband networking, enterprise and business workflow, and Web 2.0 platforms. Reliable quick learner and highly adaptive to workplace ambiguity typical of dynamic work environments.

Recruited software/hardware developers and sales executives for remote and international sites; and non-tech professionals in HR, marketing, business development, technical support, finance and accounting, facilities, and operations. Note: I am essentially a technical recruiter who because of my strong "soft skills" and team playing attitude have successfully and willingly moved out of my comfort zone many times over.

Combined innovative sourcing strategies with routine cold-calling campaigns. Proactively sourced active and passive candidates from personal database, professional networking websites, job boards (e.g., data mining older resumes), list and custom research, professional associations/organizations, and user groups. Applied complex Boolean search strings employing various types of search engines and tactics, e.g, metasearch, vertical (academic, technical/industry, patents, etc.), custom search engines, and cross-referencing.

Prior to high-tech recruiting I had several years experience in sales and marketing management including six years with an international consulting company in Managing for Quality as their marketing manager (Juran Institute, Ridgefield, CT, www.juran.com). Juran Institute fostered my problem solving skills and introduced me to practices and processes such as market analysis, cost reduction, Customer Quality, Quality Improvement, and project planning – shaping my work ethic to this day. Managed up to four direct reports and numerous vendor/suppliers.

I am open to hire on any basis (contract, temp to perm, permanent).
