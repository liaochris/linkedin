Ron Robinson’s Summary

Seasoned product development executive with 20 years experience of creating innovative, big-selling beauty products for some of the world's leading companies has formed a unique beauty social network called BeautyStat.com.

Featured in Womens Wear Daily, Self Magazine, Allure Magazine, Womens Health Magazine, Los Angeles Times and CBS News. Resident beauty expert for Allure Magazine.

There are two primary values which we can offer brands and agencies:

1. Social Marketing Expertise - we are the leading experts in social marketing for the beauty industry. We have significant experience developing and measuring social marketing programs both for our own BeautyStat community, as well as for our clients. We can provide this expertise to brands looking to further leverage their own social marketing channels as well as the overall online beauty consumer market. We will be publishing a white paper shortly on best practices in social marketing for the beauty industry, and I'll share that with you as soon as it's available.

2. Access to BeautyStat Community - we have developed the leading interactive online community which brings together consumers, industry experts and brands. Our community has grown steadily to over 25,000 members, and is composed of the most engaged and influential beauty consumers who have three times the social connections of the average Facebook user.
All told, we have access to over 9 million social connections, and high profile guests on our site including Ken Paves, Dr. Oz and others. In addition, we provide custom segmentation and market research solutions, with our ability to leverage 14 attributes amongst our community members.


If you are interested in our social media marketing, PR or research solutions, please contact me at Ron@beautystat.com.
