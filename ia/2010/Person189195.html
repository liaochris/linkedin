Shoba Spencer’s Summary

Attorney with expertise in mass communications, including broadcast and print journalism, public relations, and marketing. Interested in on-air broadcasting, including radio and television. Additionally interested in management consulting focused on the nexus between law, communication and government regulation and policy-making.

  • Law Clerk Intern

    U.S. District Judge, Joseph L. Tauro

    (Government Agency; Judiciary industry)

    January 2008 — May 2008 (5 months)

    My role included advising Judge Tauro on legal issues presented before the Court, ranging from ERISA class action cases to criminal trials. I wrote bench memorandums and drafted opinions on behalf of the Judge. During my tenure, I worked closely with other law clerks, and reported directly to Judge Tauro. On many occasions, my recommendations were implemented in court by Judge Tauro. As a result of my work, I received a Distinguished Service Award.

  • Editor-in-Chief, Editor, and Drafter

    Boston University Legislation Clinic

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    September 2007 — May 2008 (9 months)

    As Legislative Drafter, I worked with the Conservation Law Foundation to write a report and bill which addressed the issue of childhood lead poisoning due to lead-contaminated drinking water in Massachusetts. The bill was submitted to the Conservation Law Foundation to be lobbied in MA.

    As Editor, I advised a legislative drafter on a writing and researching a bill that would improve health care delivery to women with breast cancer in Massachusetts. I worked closely with the drafter to edit and restructure the bill and report each step of the way. As a result, the bill was submitted to Massachusetts Senator Karen Spilka for consideration.

    As Editor-in-Chief, I oversaw editors as they worked one on one with drafters to work on numerous projects. I oversaw meetings and helped editors advise their drafters. My role required attention to detail, exceptional writing skills, and an ability to edit and clearly articulate advice.
