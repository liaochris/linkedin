Lisa Rothstein’s Summary

I've been a copywriter and associate creative director on Madison Avenue and in Paris for ad agencies Y&R, Ogilvy and Lowe, on Fortune 500 brands such as IBM, Hanes and Dr Pepper.

I recently launched a screenwriting career as I continue to offer freelance copywriting and marketing consulting to select clients, as well as coaching for creative professionals.

My first script, Brit or Miss, was Winner of the Comedy Category in Screenwriting Expo 2, a Top 12 Finalist in the Slamdance Screenwriting Competition, and a semifinalist in the Austin Film Festival and the Chesterfield Writers' Project competitions.

I am represented as a screenwriter by Caren Bohrman (The Bohrman Agency) in Beverly Hills, and manager Andrew Kersey. I've written five screenplays (comedy & romantic comedy) and am working on a sixth.

My extensive background in advertising on two continents has been invaluable both in pitching my scripts to Hollywood and as a copywriter/marketing consultant in the US, UK and France. Beyond writing copy, I help my clients refine their strategy and brand "story": who they are and what they want their brand to mean in the marketplace. I employ all media, including social media, to raise their profiles and sell their products.

I also employ my writing talents in the important field of public education.

See www.yourwriterforhire.com for samples of my copywriting and screenwriting. For services, visit http://blog.YourWriterForHire.com

Along the way, I've also been a painter, cartoonist, TV news subtitle editor, singer at Disneyland Paris, corporate English trainer in France and a street performer in New York City.

So it's no surprise that I'm co-authoring "The Da Vinci Dilemma TM : Solutions for Multi-Talented People", a book of tips, strategies, exercises, role models & support for making the most of multiple talents.

For coaching around this topic & a free 30m session: http://www.TheDaVinciCoach.com
