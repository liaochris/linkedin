Julie Robbins’s Summary

Julie Robbins
Chief Executive Officer (CEO) & Creative Director
Idea Works Creative Group, LLC

Before founding Idea Works Creative Group, Julie Robbins worked as a network-level producer for ABC News, Good Morning America and Fox News Channel, creating feature, hard news and documentary pieces seen and heard by millions around the world. Her professional television career has involved every facet of production including field work, shoot direction, program development, set and lighting design, graphics and animation creation, guest and talent booking, editing, script writing and promotion.

In addition, Julie’s professional experience includes years spent as a Publicist and Public Relations/Marketing Communications Specialist, helping experts, talent, businesses and organizations gain extra attention, opportunities and revenue, enabling them to experience the type of growth only Public Relations campaigns can bring.

As a student, Julie studied Graphic Design at the prestigious Parsons The New School For Design (then Parsons School of Design), in New York City, and also obtained a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communication from The University of Tampa, where she graduated with high honors.

Her natural enthusiasm for people, culture, art, writing and production paved her way to a creative media career and eventually into becoming the principal figure of Idea Works Creative Group.
