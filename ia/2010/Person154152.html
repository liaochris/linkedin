Nick Nickitas’s Summary

Nick Nickitas is the Director of Business Development at FIX Flyer, a company that is pioneering advanced technology and services for high-volume, multi-asset institutional securities trading using the FIX Protocol. With a strong background in politics and civil-service, Nick has quickly established himself as a leader in the development of cutting edge trading systems and electronic trading solutions.

After graduating from Lehigh University, Nickitas began his financial services career at FIX Flyer where he has helped to expand Flyer's presence in the United States, Europe, and Asia. His passion, energy, and determination has helped to grow Flyer into one of the leading innovators of Electronic Trading Technology. As the Director of Business Development for FIX Flyer, Nick is uniquely positioned at the vanguard of the electronic trading community both domestically and around the world.

In addition Nick currently serves as the co-chair of the FPL Global Fixed Income Education & Marketing Subcommittee where he is working to encourage the adoption and implementation of next-generation electronic trading technology in the Fixed Income community. Nick also co-chairs the trading subcommittee for the Money Managment Institute working to establish industry wide standards for orders, allocations, and EOD processes in the wealth management industry.
