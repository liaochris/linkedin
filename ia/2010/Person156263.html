Brent M Norris’s Summary

Brent has over 30 years in construction with extensive experience in healthcare, educational, institutional, heavy construction, historical renovation, specialty and retrofit construction projects with roles ranging from General Contractor, Owner's Representative, Lead OSHPD Inspector and Construction Manager. Brent has a comprehensive technical knowledge in all disciplines of architecture, engineering and construction, strengthened by actual hands-on experience. Brent has outstanding proficiency in construction management and OSHPD inspection. Through proactive and responsive coordination of all parties, Brent ensures success for the client and for all project stakeholders.

Brent currently holds the following certifications:

- LEED AP - LEED Accredited Professional (US Green Building Council)
- OSHPD Level-A Certified Inspector of Record
- DSA Level-I Certified Inspector of Record
- NITC Med Gas Certified Inspector
