Ross Herosian’s Summary

I currently work at SIRIUS XM Radio. I started at XM in 2004 after interning there the summer of 2003. When I started at XM, my main responsibilities were related to data entry and reporting accurate performance royalty information for XM's 170+ channels.

Shortly after starting at XM, I began DJ'ing on XM's Ethel channel (Alternative Rock) from Noon - 4pm Monday-Friday. For about a year and half, I worked both as DJ and data entry clerk.

I then was hired on to completely re-structure, maintain, expand, and facilitate XM's Internship Program and Community Outreach programs. I am proud to say that after a little over a year, XM's Internship Program was recognized by Vault.com as one of the "Top 10 Internships In America" and 2007 generated an all-time high in employee volunteerism and XM corporate philanthropy.

After the merger between SIRIUS and XM Radio in 2008, my role was expanded to evaluate program synergies for both companies and create a unified, national internship program. This process required auditing all recruiting, on-boarding, and paperwork tracking activities in terms of efficiency and uniformity. Shortly after the merger, I was also given managerial responsibilities of a New York-based Recruiter/Operations person. While directly reporting to me, I have trained her on recruitment process, recruitment system (iCIMS), program administration, and how to properly manage disciplinary problems.

Over time I have come to be a "utility man" for SIRIUS XM. I have working knowledge of website creation and planning, reporting, managing, recruiting, mentoring, and marketing.
