David Talburt’s Summary

ProKicks Martial Arts Academy is a member of the world's largest centrally managed martial arts organizations, The World Traditional Taekwondo Union (WTTU) and the American Taekwondo Association (ATA).

We offer what we believe is the best martial art in the world. Taekwondo is an exciting and powerful martial art known for its dynamic kicking and hand techniques. Although these martial art techniques are centuries old, our programs are always evolving to keep pace with the rapidly changing world.

We realize that each student possesses different physical and cognitive abilities and understand how size, strength and cognition vary with each individual. Once a student has acquired a strong foundation in the basics, our program will enhance the student's physical capabilities and maximize self-defense skills. Students will mature in the understanding of what is required mentally and physically to defend themselves and their loved ones should the need ever arise.

ATA Martial Arts Academies are not solely interested in developing physical skills and teaching self-defense, but are also committed to the complete personal development of each student. Individual attention is a key feature of each class, helping students progress quickly and confidently toward their personal goals. Our instructors are the best instructors in the world and take their responsibility very seriously as role models for younger students, teaching them psychological and behavioral skills such as character development, self-control, discipline, goal-setting, etc. that will promote success in life. We refer to these essential skills as "Life Skills".

Two concepts drive the ATA teaching curriculum: "positive mental attitude" and "high goal setting". We feel so strongly about this concept that we promise to give you and your family the most professional, well-supervised, highest quality training available in any sport or art.
