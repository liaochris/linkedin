Darlene Smith’s Summary

For over a decade, Jacksonville, Florida native Darlene Smith has been a professional stylist. Her passion for hair started as a young child and her vocational Cosmetology education began during high school. Her motto is “If you love what you do, you'll never work a day in your life!” Darlene believes that education in hair and business, passion, a strong work ethic, plus experience is vital in for client satisfaction and career advancement.

Darlene can be seen at many hair & business seminars, shows and classes. Her education includes such companies as Mizani, Pivot Point, Paul Mitchell, SoCap USA, Brazilian Keratin (Marcia Texiera) & Design Essentials, plus training through educators such as Master Colorist Beth Mindardi, Patric Bradley, Barry Fletcher & Miki Wright & just to name a few.

Darlene is currently involved in doing on location styling for weddings & special occasions and photo shoots. She can be seen in Salon Digest’s Fabulous Magazine, Beauty Max’s Max Style, DLA Hair Magazine and more to come…

Darlene’s passion for hair crosses all texture lines. "Beauty by Darlene's" slogan “Specializing in all hair types”, with the sole focus of healthy hair care and client satisfaction. She offers services such as several methods of hair extensions & weaves, color/highlighting, cutting, bridal/special occasion styles, relaxers, perms, Brazilian Keratin Treatment (BKT), locs/ dreads & “naturals”, straightening/pressing, & eyelash enhancements.


“Beauty by Darlene” is located inside of Mosaic Salon Studios, a private studio concept salon. All clients are serviced using high quality products, in a timely matter, without long wait times, during scheduled appointments. Being located inside a private salon studio allows clients to feel like royalty, as they enjoy their professional hair care services. Her main goal is to maintain healthy hair care in a professional salon environment.


Don’t delay; contact Darlene today to book your next appointment.
