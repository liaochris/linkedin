Bruno Bensaid’s Summary

Founder and Partner of Shanghaivest Ltd, a boutique investment banking firm that builds and executes cross-border investment, expansion, fundraising and exit strategies for its clients in China and overseas (China outbound and inbound).
Angel investor with AAMA Shanghai angels, investor in two startups in the internet and mobile spaces in China.
Founder of Shanghai Chapter of MobileMonday
Old-timer in China (almost 13 years), Bruno Bensaid is one of the few foreigners who successfully led a Chinese mobile value-added services provider (under a mobile gaming license from IN-FUSIO) from 2002 to 2005, growing it into a 35-people profitable operation and a VC round.
Graduate from French top aerospace engineering school (“Sup’Aero”) and is currently following an EMBA program at INSEAD. Bruno is fluent in French, English and Mandarin.

Bruno Bensaid’s Specialties:

M&A, Cross-border, early-stage startup operations, angel investment, venture capital, mobile telecom, internet, technology and media
