James Graca’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL HISTORY

James Graca is an award-winning designer/conceptualist with over 30 years of professional experience. He is a graduate of ART CENTER COLLEGE OF DESIGN. He is an honored member in the 1996-1998 NATIONAL DIRECTORY OF WHO'S WHO of EXECUTIVES & PROFESSIONALS and has been listed in WHO'S WHO IN CALIFORNIA since 1981.

For several years in the early 1990's, James Graca Design Group maintained a satellite office in Paris, to better serve their international design clients in France, Italy and Great Britain.

Mr. Graca's work was requested and displayed in the "VISION WEST COAST ARTISTS TODAY" show in Japan, and two of his posters were requested and are on permanent display in the FEDIER'S WERBEAGENTUR POSTER COLLECTION in Zurich, Switzerland.

James Graca is also an Adjunct Professor & Experiential Educator teaching courses in becoming creatively fearless & fully self expressed, high concept visual communication, advertising & design in California, Havana, Cuba and Europe, (study abroad).

James Graca and his work have been included in the "Focus on the Masters" archives which will ultimately be housed in the Smithsonian Institute.
