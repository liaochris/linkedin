Mike Staff’s Summary

How can we help make your wedding fantastic?

Wedding planning can be overwhelming. Our job is to help you have the wedding of your dreams by simplifying the process and giving you the highest-quality wedding services at a realistic price. We work with brides & grooms just like you … who really want a FUN & classy wedding by working with talented people who love what they do.

We particularly enjoy working with clients who have a particular vision for their wedding day and are seeking an easy-going team of experienced professionals to help make it a reality. Mike Staff Productions focuses in the areas of professional DJs, creative wedding photography and HD videography. While some of our clients use us for just one or two services, many choose to simplify their wedding planning by utilizing us for all 3 services.

Our DJs have earned high marks from the clients that we’ve served, who have consistently voted us “Best DJ Service in Michigan” in polls by Michigan Meetings & Events Magazine, WDIV – TV Channel 4, The Knot.com, WeddingWire.com and The Perfect Wedding Guide. Our professional photographers have been published in virtually every local and national bridal publication. Our HD Videography has won several national awards including the Hermes Creative Award and the Aegis Video & Film Award.
