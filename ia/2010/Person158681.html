Michael Palko’s Summary

I'm a self-starter with a background in project management and training design & delivery. I have a penchant for developing creative traditional classroom and e-Learning courses using a variety of tools. I know the value of applications and web sites that focus on usability and design. I'm currently managing two online learning communities, one of which uses SharePoint as a platform. Both communities employ all that the Web 2.0 world has to offer, including podcasts. I'm am enthralled by the concepts and power of social media, collaborative intelligence, networking and viral marketing.
