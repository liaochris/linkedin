Kanani Masterson’s Summary

As the Director of the Technology Division for TriStaff Group, I manage a team that does technical recruiting, sales & marketing, and executive search, focusing on careers within the technology sector. I also work as a fulltime Headhunter for positions ranging from Software Engineers, Technology Management, Sales / Business Developent, and Marketing professionals, and C-level Executives for companies in the San Diego County area. We also work with a more limited clientel across the U.S.

I also act as Vice President of Business Development for TriStaff Group's other business divisions. Those include: clerical, executive administrators, HR, manufacturing and call center staffing, finance, media, publishing, energy, clean technology, and hardware, mechanical, and electrical engineering.

Kanani Masterson’s Specialties:

Technology Industry focuses include: enterprise software, security software, analtyics, clean technology, medical device, embedded systems, web 2.0, interactive media, wireless & telecommunications, data warehousing, and storage solutions.

Clients include both successful Fortune 500 corporations & Pre-IPO startups, and positions include: hands-on technical engineering roles as well as VP, CTO, CIO, CEO, etc. I also place candidates working in Sales, Marketing, & Business Development roles.
