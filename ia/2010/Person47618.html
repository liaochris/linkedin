Daniel McCord’s Summary

Over the past two years Tuenti has become the most trafficked website of Spain, surpassing even Google according to the latest report from ComScore. With more than 9,500 million pages audited, Tuenti is one of the most important services of the Internet in Europe and counts almost 9 million users.  

Tuenti is a tool that facilitates the transmission of information between people who really know each other that everyday day generates a huge activity: our users upload over 3.5 million pictures a day and share over 3 million videos daily.

Tuenti's goal is to get people to be aware of everything that is happening in their circle of friends and so we managed to achieve much higher ratios of activity including the social networking sector: the average daily time Tuenti exceed 70 minutes per user.

The company also opted from the outset about privacy and is therefore not allowing free registration but has grown into a model only by invitation. Besides, unlike other similar services, we don´t index any content on search engines, so the 800 million pages per day are generated solely by users' browsing.

Tuenti was born in late 2006 in Spain. Right now in Madrid our office consists of a team of over 160 people consisting of people from more than 18 countries, mostly integrated into the engineering department.

If you would be interested in exploring a career with us you can review openings here: jobs.tuenti.com. Feel free to contact me directly as well.
