Toni Hunter’s Summary

Goals:
To continue to develop the accountancy practice, create new business opportunities and hone my skills in respect of Charity client assignments.
To provide a good standard of living for my family and to travel the world.
To continue helping people improve their health and wealth.

I enjoy helping people and being sociable, so have become a bit of a networking junkie, although my young son is taking priority at the moment so I haven't been getting my regular fix!

I love to work with new and developing businesses and am interested in property investment.

Those who know me agree that my personality does not match that of a traditional sterotypical accountant!

Toni Hunter’s Specialties:

Accounting: As well as dealing with small and start-up businesses, I am an auditor of companies in a variety of industry sectors including manufacturing and financial services. I particularly enjoy working with owner managed businesses and charities.

Health: I am managing an severe skin problem that I have had since a baby, using high quality aloe products.

Wealth: I use the skills gained from my business experiences to help motivated individuals achieve a better lifestyle.

  • Chair

    (Non-Profit; Events Services industry)

    November 2005 — November 2007 (2 years 1 month)

    I chaired the committee for two years after having served as secretary and treasurer for the previous four years.
