Deborah Daily’s Summary

Building a business can be a wild ride, but CEO and Majority Owner, Deborah Daily has proven for more than 10 years that she knows how to tame that bucking bronco. Buckaroo offers its clients extensive, hands-on experience within the B2B marketing arena. Deb’s responsibilities include outside sales and creative development while her partner, Ken focuses on web programming, database development and operations.

A marketing expert with more than 20 years experience, Deb excels at helping growing companies develop effective business strategies and communicate their strengths through powerful marketing materials and various mediums. As owner of Buckaroo, a full-service marketing agency based in the Indianapolis area, Deb has helped facilitate everything from new business start-ups and new product introductions to creative sales promotions and customer retention programs.

Want to jumpstart your own brainstorming efforts? Deb is also a sought after speaker, whose energetic style and interactive approach get audiences excited about solving business challenges. Combining targeted strategic planning, highly original marketing ideas and superbly integrated technology, Deb helps growth-focused companies set strategic direction and achieve ambitious goals.
