Bob Waters’s Summary

I have 32 years of experience in a very diversified metal-cutting industry. These have included tool and die, workholding, production, R&D, job shop and custom shop. Of that period of time I have devoted 28 years to the CNC aspect. My foundation started on the ground floor in the US Navy with my basic and advanced machinist training. Not only can I machine parts and assemblies, but design, process, time study, research and develop, quote as well as select and procure the necessary machine to get the job done. My understanding of the industry comes from this evolution through the industry, from being on the customer side of the business to the distributor side as well. I have made this career my life's work and have a passion that drives me to ever evolving technolgical advances.

CAD/CAM softwares, Process development and improvement, Fixture design, Programming, Team development and training.
