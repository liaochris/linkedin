Marguerite Efdé’s Summary

"Comparing a company with an engine; I am the lubricating oil that keeps all parts working together."

I can be hired as an interim purchaser, project manager, organizer, or to assist you with reorganisations and change processes.

I like to work in all kind of business branches. I like assignments within technical / production areas.
I do have a lot of experience within IT, broadcast media, CRM software and also within wind energy sector, housing, Olympic Games organisation, mainly co-ordination positions, both national and international.

I like to work with all persons within a company, the people who actually make the product as well as the management team. I create the opportunity for people to actually work together. The results are better co-ordination, co-operation, cost saving and happier people at work.

My aim is to create a better atmosphere, so people do enjoy their daily work and like to build a better company together.

I like open communication and I am not all afraid to deal with great challenges. The way I work very often involves new technologies and new working methods.

I like networking, feel very comfortable to connect with new people. I think it is important to exchange information & knowledge, share this and combine it to build a better environment/company.
