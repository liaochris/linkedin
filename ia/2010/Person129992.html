Maggie Wright’s Summary

Maggie Wright is one of the country’s best independent PR practitioners according industry body, the Institute of Public Relations (IPR). A double gold award winner at the IPR PRide awards, Maggie Wright picked up the best small team award for strategic communications campaigns executed for a range of UK clients. Previously the IPR named her Independent Practitioner of the year.

Maggie has many years experience in the UK communications industry, gaining extensive skills in strategy development, news and crisis management, internal communications and marketing communications planning. She has particular strengths in executing international communications programmes.

A former board director of Citigate Scotland, part of Incepta plc which has subsequently merged with the Huntsworth Group, she left to set up Maggie Wright Associates and has directed a number of high profile PR programmes across chosen sectors.

Maggie Wright’s Specialties:

Maggie Wright Associates offers the highest level of personal and professional integrity to organisations seeking to build relationships and positively influence people who are important to their future. Through a network of associates we offer a fully integrated and managed marketing communications service using only senior practitioners with a proven track record in their field.
