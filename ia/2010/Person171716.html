Richard Sanders’s Summary

I have writen and spoken regarding legal and policy issues involving provider network structures, physician-hospital relations and managed care organization regulation and contracting. I am also a chapter author of the Third edition – Law of Medical Practice in Illinois (Thomson/West-2007) and its annual supplements. I am an editor and chapter author of the American Health Lawyers Association publication, the Managed Care Contracting Handbook (1993) and its fifth edition entitled Health Plans Contracting Handbook (2008). I am also the co-author of, The More Things Change, the More They Stay the Same: An Analysis of the Impact of the HIPAA Privacy Rule on Illinois Mental Health Care Providers (7 DePaul J. Health Care L.1 (2003).

Richard Sanders’s Specialties:

Illinois does not recognize lawyer "specialites", but I have concentrated my practice in the areas of Health Industry Board/Management issues;Corporate compliance and Health industry regulation of providers.
