Kevin Gaugush’s Summary

Kevin Gaugush has been the Chief People Officer since joining kgb in July 2007. His responsibilities include developing and implementing human resources related processes, practices and programs for the Company’s global operations. His goal is to create an environment with employees who all have the skills needed to deliver value to the customer.

Prior to joining kgb, Kevin spent eight years at ARAMARK. During his time at ARAMARK, he served in various human resources capacities, including Vice President, Human Resources for the Sports & Entertainment division, Vice President, Human Resources for the Facility Services and Education division and Director and then Senior Director, Human Resources for the Uniform Services division. Prior to ARAMARK, Kevin spent five years at Honeywell (formerly AlliedSignal).

Kevin has presented at various national meetings for leaders in Higher Education including NACUBO, APPA, TMSF as well as at many regional conferences.

He has served on the Advisory Council of Brigham Young University’s Facility Management program and on Washington State University’s Hospitality Business Management Advisory Committee. He is also a regular guest speaker at Cornell University at both the Hospitality School and Business School. Kevin holds a BA in Economics from Yale University and an MBA from the University of Michigan.
