Steve Stoll’s Summary

My name is Steve Stoll and I want to thank you for visiting The Stoll Group! You have taken the first step to exploring how we can potentially assist your dealership in saving time and money while still attracting talented employees to fill any of your needs, regardless of position and location.

I will bring a nationally recognized and truly innovative recruiting approach that will be specific and customized to your dealership. As you may know, Keith McCluskey, CEO of McCluskey Chevrolet (the largest volume GM franchise in Ohio, the largest medium-duty truck dealer in the USA and the one of the largest sub-prime /special finance dealers in the USA) and I were featured in both AutoExec Magazine and USA Today as he was awarded the NADA Dealer Innovation Award that was based upon creative recruiting practices within the automotive industry. I have over 20 years experience in Executive Recruiting and Headhunting with such firms as Procter & Gamble, Credit Acceptance Corporation and Robert Half International. Additionally, I have solid relationships established with a wide array of Dealer Principal's across the country! . I am endorsed by and have ties to dealers representing virtually every automotive manufacturer. I also have had proven success with NAMAD (North American Minority Automotive Dealers), AFMMD, FMMDA, GMMDA, CAC and CMDA stores among others.
