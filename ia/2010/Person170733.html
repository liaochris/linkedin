Reggie Wideman’s Summary

Reggie has made a career of turning change into opportunity for himself as well as his clients and partners.

Reggie Wideman is a business strategist with 10 years of experience delivering solutions to a broad spectrum of customers in diverse industries. During his career he helped the National Academy of Sciences redefine how they handled scholarly peer-review in the information age. For three years he helped Ethicspoint develop compliance tracking solutions for organizations large and small including KPMG, Blockbuster, Starwood and Cardtronics. Following that, Reggie worked for Attensa, developing new processes and technologies for cross-functional teams at Cisco Systems and Sun Microsystems to collaborate in the enterprise.

Currently Reggie is an account director, strategist and writer for tenfour.

He loves helping companies transform the way they do business and converse with their customers. More importantly, Reggie loves solving problems.
