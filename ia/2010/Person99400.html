Jessica L. Owens’s Summary

Jessica Owens completed her Bachelor in Business Administration degree at Northwood University in Midland, Michigan in May, 2010 with a double major in Advertising and Marketing, Management completing her degree in three years. While at school, she participated in American Advertising Federation and a study abroad trip to London and Paris. Jessica financed over half of her education in merit scholarships and the rest by working part time as a co-op at The Dow Chemical Company. Learning to multi-task in high-stress situations allowed Jessica to be a member of the Dean's list for each term with as many as 25 credits a term. At Dow, she excelled in leadership and project excellence and was twice awarded the One Carrot award and the Two Carrot award once. Jessica's philosophy is to create products and services that best fit the consumer and success will come.

At Northwood, she specialized in online media and social media marketing techniques and how to blend online marketing strategies with traditional media strategies. For example, she worked with the Great Lakes Bay Region Visitors and Convention Bureau to develop a marketing strategy for general tourism. The team project was complete with online marketing strategies that functions seamlessly with traditional media to access each target market effectively. Jessica learned how important it is for marketers to understand how to develop strategies and objectives for online media to effectively measure return on investment, a common struggle for businesses venturing into online media.
