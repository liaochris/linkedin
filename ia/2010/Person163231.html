Peter Kim’s Summary

Peter Kim is a Principal at ClearLight Partners, a California based private equity firm with $600 million under management. He has nearly two decades of experience investing in, working at, and advising high growth companies.

Peter is actively involved in new investment origination for ClearLight, and seeks opportunities to invest in strong businesses in the U.S. and Canada with $5 to $25 million of operating income and a history of growth and sustained profitability.

At ClearLight, Peter has been active in the education and training, business services, and light manufacturing/industrial sectors. He has served on the Board of Directors of four privately held companies.

Prior to ClearLight, Peter worked at McKinsey & Company in San Francisco. At McKinsey, Peter advised management teams in the United States and Europe on growth strategy, operational improvements, and organizational issues. Peter previously worked in Brentwood Associates' private equity group and at Brentwood's Educational Publishing Corporation portfolio company where he led the company's strategic planning process and assisted in the sale of the company to Tribune Company. Peter began his career at Morgan Stanley in New York in the mergers and acquisitions department and as a member of Morgan Stanley's Princes Gate Investors team.

Peter holds an MBA with Distinction from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University and a BA with Honors from the University of Chicago.

Peter serves on the Board of Directors of Junior Achievement of Orange County, a non-profit organization focused on workforce readiness, financial literacy and entrepreneurship.

Peter Kim’s Specialties:

private equity, mergers and acquisitions, alliances, business valuation, investments, business unit strategy, operational improvements.
