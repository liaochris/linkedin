David McRaney’s Summary

As the head of convergent journalism and new media for a broadcast television station, I am responsible for all Web-based content.

As a writer, my work has been featured at boingboing.net, FOX News, MSNBC, The Hattiesburg American, The Sun Herald, The Student Printz, The Lamar Times, The Petal News, The Advertiser News, digg.com, infowars.com, asylumnation.com, bodyburden.org, sexualscandals.com, righttothink.com, ewg.org, icanhascheezeburger.com and hundreds of blogs.

I am devoted to the idea of communication through technology and believe technology will progress exponentially regardless of what we do. I help media companies to not fall behind their audiences in the use of this technology.
