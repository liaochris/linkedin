Bradly Sax’s Summary

SHG Consulting|Management is a New York and Ontario based lodging professional services firm and a leading authority on the North American lodging industry. Utilizing decades of lodging experience and comprehensive industry knowledge, insight and contacts, SHG provides a wide-ranging catalog of services centered on maximizing a hotel’s market position, operational excellence, business development opportunities and bottom-line profitability. SHG’s diverse client base includes individual and multi-unit hotel owners, hotel operating companies, industry analysts, lodging lenders and franchise companies.

Mr. Sax is a proven lodging leader with a demonstrated record of results in exceeding revenue goals, the turn-around of underperforming assets, driving market share and property repositioning. His highly experienced, macro-level leadership abilities stem from over two decades of professional experience in the direct supervision of the operations and business development teams for individual hotels, multi-unit regions and as the Chief Operating Officer of a $100,000,000 portfolio of privately-held lodging assets. His excellent analytical ability coupled with an effective communication style beget a strong ability to engage stakeholders at all levels of an organization to launch a lodging asset to its maximum potential.
