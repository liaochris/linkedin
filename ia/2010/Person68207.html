Robert Rogers,CSEP’s Summary

Creativity is the ability to generate innovative ideas and manifest them from thought into reality. Robert Rogers CSEP is an extremely creative individual.
In his work he continually seeks out new trends and inspires new ones.
Robert has lived and worked in Asia for over 20 years. During this time he has created experiences for an A-List of companies including: BNP, Cartier, Calyon, Christie's, Conrad, Coutt’s, Credit Suisse, Dow, General Electric, Goldman & Sachs, Grand Hyatt, Hewlett Packard, Hong Kong Country Club, Hong Kong Jockey Club, Hong Kong Land, HSBC, Hong Kong Society for the Protection of Children, Hong Kong Tourist Association, ING, Investor’s Group, J.W. Marriott, Jardine Group, Marlboro, MGM, Motorola, New York Life, Nomura, Ocean Park, Peninsula Hotels Group, Royal Geographical Society, Royal Hong Kong Yacht Club, Sino Land Group, Smirnoff, Star T.V., Swarovski, Swire Properties, UBS, U.S. Consulate, Wallem Shipping, Westin Resorts, Wheelock Properties.

Robert also creates special events and life celebrations for an AA-List of Asian society including weddings, anniversaries and birthday events….

“Corporate events are great, I love the challenge of merging goals and brands into a live interactive environment, but I love doing social events… They create an opportunity to make memories that last a lifetime. After a great life celebration photos go on people’s mantelpiece and they cherish those moments forever”.

"My strong point is to be able interpret the vision of all the stakeholders and bring the event to reality. On brief, on budget and on time."

Robert Rogers,CSEP’s Specialties:

Robert is a Certified Special Event Professional, one of only 250 worldwide and the only one in Asia. The CSEP designation is the hallmark of professional achievement in the special events industry. It is earned through education, performance, experience, and service to the industry, and reflects a commitment to professional conduct and ethics.
