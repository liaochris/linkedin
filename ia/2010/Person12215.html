andrew graham’s Summary

Andrew Graham is an independent writer and media strategist in New York.

His areas of expertise are the financial services/capital markets and energy/environmentalism verticals and he specializes in matters such as globalization, public policy, regulation, capital markets, and the places they awkwardly overlap.

Previously, he has worked as a press contact for multinational manufacturers, large law and lobbying firms, hedge funds, and financial technology vendors, and also supported investor relations programs for global shipping companies.

He originates copy -- contributed articles, corporate newsletters, speeches, news releases, and internal communications -- and promotes pieces of corporate news for some of the largest companies in their respective industries. He also advises companies on the use of social media and new-media platforms and writes columns for Green Technology Daily.

He holds a graduate certification in global affairs from New York University, studied intercultural communications at NEGOCIA, and earned his undergraduate degree from Illinois State University.

He blogs his eyes red at http://upalldamnnight.com and on Twitter at @andrew_graham.
