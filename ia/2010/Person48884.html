Darlene C Doucet (dc_doucet@sympatico.ca)’s Summary

I am a Life Member with XL Results Foundation; I am the World Wide Wealth Ambassador for XL Canada, and I am also the Team Leader for XL Toronto.

I currently support a team of partners for a low cost international ATM/Debit card that is independent of any specific bank, therefore Payrolls can be changed from a "Cost" center to a Profit center. Money transfers are done in real time, so in just seconds you can transfer funds, even internationally, for just $3.00 and you are not charged an exchange rate "fee" or "gap." This is a Social Enterprise that funnels money earned back into charities within the XL organization.

I also coached my team of over 275 people many to 6 and 7 figure incomes using a Home Based Business as the vehicle.

I work with a local Air Cadet Corps as a Supply Officer; empowering the youth of today to be our leaders tomorrow!

I am also the Public Relations Officer for the Royal Canadian Legion, both at the local Branch level and at the Zone level, which means I represent 11 Branches and have a readership of my Newsletter to 12,000 plus members. I have held many leadership positions such as 1st Vice President, acting President, Secreatery, Treasurer, Poppy Co-Chair, and others. I have also held the position of Vice President of Public Relations with Toastmasters International.

I have written many articles, and given speeches on a varied array of subjects. My education and work history is equally varied, ranging from Industrial Millwright to Photographer, to Systems Analyst, to the Banking industry and beyond.

I have also been involved in the Sales field for most of my life. I enjoy creating Win Win situations, and having everyone walk away a winner!
