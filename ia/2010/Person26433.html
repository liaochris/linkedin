Brad Davis’s Summary

I have 8 years of design engineering experience covering RF Design in the wireless industry (802.11, and GPS), test automation for wireless products, research and development on 17 GHz radio-over-fiber systems, and systems integration for data logging and control applications. I enjoy taking on challenging problems that improve peoples lives. Especially if this can be done through the application of technology but also by building great relationships with, and supporting, the people around me.
