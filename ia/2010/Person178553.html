Tom Walton’s Summary

phone: 414-248-0123
email: rtomwalton@sbcglobal.net
email: tomwalton@me.com

Led multiple domestic as well as cross-border acquisitions in Australia, Canada, China, France, Germany, Singapore, South Korea, Sweden and Thailand. Led development of world class tools and processes for successful identification, negotiation, due diligence and integration of acquisition targets in multiple industries. Prior to Joy Global, I was involved in mergers & acquisitions for over 11 years, at Milwaukee-based Brady Corporation (NYSE: BRC), a world leader in identification solutions, where he most recently served as Director, Corporate Development. At Brady, I led numerous acquisition projects in the Americas, Asia and Europe, managing all facets of the acquisition process, from strategic planning, deal sourcing, financial analysis, due diligence, negotiation, and on to closing and integration. Was the senior dealmaker at Brady Corporation when the company won the ACG 2006 Outstanding Corporate Growth Award.
