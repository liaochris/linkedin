Mac McRorie’s Summary

I have developed products for over 14 years for many notable brands, including Compaq, IBM, Cooper Tools, Electrolux, Caterpillar, Rubbermaid and Irwin. In that time, I have developed a reputation that would describe me as a creative, innovative problem solver. I am highly skilled at creating mechanical and aesthetic solutions to best fit user needs. In addition, I am unique in that I can also develop solutions for manufacture in Pro/E, which significantly reduces development time and produces improved communication and dialogue with engineering.
