Saundra Hadley’s Summary

Owner and event engineer (planner) for corporate, weddings and social parties. Saundra left the corporate sales world to open a chic event planning studio that has a unique all-about-you approach towards your event.

Using her experience in marketing, sales, and event planning, her team offers their clients full event planning and design/decor services for weddings, corporate and social parties.

    As the owner of planning...forever events, I am working at the most rewarding and hardest job of my career. I'm living my dream and loving every minute of it.

    Not a big corporate firm, my team and I believe in giving our clients undivided attention. Whether it is the CEO of a local bank or an excited bride planning her wedding; we have developed a unique, accessible, all-about-you approach towards planning your event. We really enjoy people and we love what we do, so it's a winning combination.

    Let's face it, we're not saving lives here. But we do make a positive impact on our client's lives.
