Jon Morse’s Summary

Over 14+ years of technology experience has provided me an understanding of the technical, business and people issues involved with the leadership, development and deployment of mid-sized to enterprise-wide business and information systems projects.

I have been successful playing key roles in a variety of projects utilizing the full development life-cycle (SDLC) for production systems.

I am most effective structuring projects and leading the efforts of others to reach a common goal.

I perform best at a "10,000-foot" process-design level, but have the ability to dive in and get my hands "dirty" when necessary. I have been most successful where I have the autonomy to get things done and have the experience to deliver on-time and under budget.
