Frank Connolly’s Summary

Versatile senior executive with track record of using wide-ranging strategic, operational and functional experience to increase shareholder value in public and private companies. Executed nineteen successful M&A, equity and debt transactions for total value of over $1.3 billion. Global experience includes both large corporations and small, entrepreneurial firms having operations in North America, Europe, Asia and South America. Industry background focused on market research, digital marketing, interactive advertising, direct marketing, marketing information and technology consulting. Areas of expertise include strategic value creation, mergers and acquisitions, revenue and cost rationalization, business restructuring, process improvement, public equity offerings, private equity and debt fundraising, technology investments, financial planning, investor relations, financial systems implementation, Sarbanes-Oxley compliance, SEC reporting and accounting controls.
