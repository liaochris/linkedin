Michael S. Robinson’s Summary

Michael S. Robinson is President and CEO of New York Staffing Services, Inc. and Microbin, Inc. Both companies are dedicated to the staffing industry and workforce capital management of contingent workers in the United States.

Based in the Wall Street area and a presence in several states, NY Staffing sources, manages and places talent in both temporary and permanent positions at Fortune 100 companies, and other large organizations across the United States. In view of economic upsets, glitches, bleeps and downturns this twenty year old company that began as a financial staffing organization by founder and CEO Michael S. Robinson, a former Wall Street financial analyst- has evolved by adapting to the changing market conditions. Revamping it's internal processes, expanding it's market share, staying on the cutting edge of technology and empowering it's workforce makes New York Staffing Services, Inc. along with Microbin, Inc. competitive and strong.

We believe that 20 years in business makes us an expert in jobs as it relates to the economy. Our knowledge of the market allows us to form partnerships with clients by offering support and advice on best practices within the industry. Through discussions, meetings and other correspondences Robinson says, his professional staff keeps clients informed about competitive prices, industry shifts, trends in the economy, wages, taxes and compensation, providing expert information and guidance. This puts an added value to the service we provide.

“We’re constantly looking at how we can be a more relevant partner to our clients and for new and better ways to navigate the changing economy. At the end of the day, that’s what our business is all about.”

Michael S. Robinson’s Experience

  • President/CEO

    Microbin

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    January 2006 — Present (5 years )

    In a field of world class leaders, the Microbin Vendor Management System (VMS) is one of the most innovative workforce capital management companies in the management of temporary staffing for large companies globally.

    Always evolving to meet the needs of a changing market, in 2005 Microbin, Inc. was created by Michael Robinson as a solution to streamline and manage the temporary staffing process. Major corporate entities that utilize large workforces need a tool such as Microbin to save them time, money and manage their headcount effectively without pinching at profit margins.

    It is a fact that Workforce Capital is the backbone of the most successful enterprises. However, for hiring companies, navigating the complexities of that workforce along with developing supplier relations can be overwhelming, costly and time consuming. Hence the need for Microbin. The online based service maximizes productivity throughout an entity, eliminates paper files, improves efficiency and reduces operating costs across the board.

    In addition, Microbin provides a unique and formidable onsite personal service for its clients called a Master Service Provider (MSP). Teams of qualified personnel are onsite to assist in the management of talent from the on-boarding to the off-boarding of contingent workers. The teams also work with clients to set benchmarks and measure levels of productivity of approved suppliers. In order to provide effective results to its clients, the MSP also directly interfaces with line managers, suppliers and temporary workers.

    “The system is flexible and user friendly but most importantly it works for us. It frees up our managers to focus on core operational duties. We’ve become so accustomed to this system, it’s hard to imagine HR life without it”, says Betty McKay, Vice President of Human Resources at a leading New York hospital and research center.

    For more information, please visit our website : www.microbin.net

  • President/CEO

    New York Staffing Services, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    January 1990 — Present (21 years )

    Throughout their 20 years of operational history, New York Staffing Services, Inc. (NYSSI) has been a haven for exceptional talent. The characteristics and demands of the Human Resources Departments in today’s era crave a greater scope of knowledge and proficiencies far different from those of the past. NYSSI understands that the Workforce Management of major companies is multi-dimensional and multifaceted and is tantamount to ensuring organizational survival and success.

    Headquartered in the heart of the financial district near Wall Street, NYSSI stands ready to provide solutions to their clients in today’s rapidly changing market conditions. NYSSI is highly respected in the staffing industry for the quality of service and reliant contingent workers they provide to their clients. A strong reputation was built on their ability to consistently exceed their clients’ expectations while delivering the flexibility and personally tailored service.

    A service driven company with a product driven approach is NYSSI’s mantra. Their location in the heart of the financial district of New York City allows them to be in the center of the campus landscape to many of their Fortune 100 clients and offer a full service staffing concern to the financial world providing both permanent and temporary placements in all areas of the workplace as well as throughout the United States.

    The business has withstood the test of time since its creation in 1990, and has been independently owned and operated since then by Michael S. Robinson, President and CEO. “We continue our quest to deliver personalized quality services at all levels to our clients” says Robinson “and paying attention to the details matter, delivering quality service versus quantity is what matters the most. This in turn gives us repeat business thereby earning the customer’s loyalty."
