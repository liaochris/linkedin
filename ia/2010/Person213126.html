Vonnell Martinez’s Summary

Dear All,

My name is Vonnell Martinez. I am the founder of VIM Resources LLC, a financial institution whose goal is international presence and growth.

We recently celebrated our third year in business and are extremely grateful to our clients and investors for providing us with a very special momentum.

I possess a deep passion for finance and investments. I am a lover of life and work (which almost never feels like it), and one of the things which has helped me reach my achievements thus far is that I always keep two things in mind:
1. Pristine and sincere client relationships is essential to success
2. Not only am I an investor but, first and foremost, I am a businessman

Our three years to November 1 (right in the middle of the "Great Recession") performance was 24%. We are using this amazing energy to launch a new Distressed Asset fund in 2011, which we have to cap at $25 million.

Our attorneys are currently working on the Offering Memorandum and our excitement grows with every new inquiry and message of satisfaction from our clients, whom we look forward to continuing to provide with healthy returns for even healthier risks, as well as a clear, concise and pleasant experience.

I do not believe in the "black box" mentality most fund managers posses (this writing in the first person is an example) in which they lock clients out of "their space". I am a great advocate of client-manager friendship.

Another concept which has always helped us has been our solid "skin in the game" mentality and constant pursuit of greater financial efficiency. Our clients range from high net-worth individuals looking for a new place to invest to institutions simply looking to diversify their investment capital.

I personally thank you for reading. Please feel free to contact me with any questions; our doors are always open.

Don't forget to visit us: www.VIMResourcesLLC.com

All the best,

Vonnell Martinez
