Madeleine Blanchard’s Summary

Entrepreneur in the area of Corporate and Executive Coaching. Expertise in large scale corporate implementations of coaching programs, consulting to create a coaching culture inside of organizations, leveraging technology to increase effectiveness of coaching experience, teaching coaching skills to managers and leaders, coaching highly creative, multiply gifted individuals.
Author of: Leverage Your Best; Ditch the Rest (William Morrow 2004) with Scott Blanchard
Coaching Essentials for Leaders with Scott Blanchard and Linda Miller, and
Coaching in Organizations (Wiley 2008) with Linda Miller and
multiple articles in professional journals.
