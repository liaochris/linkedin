Chris Gauron’s Summary

Simply stated, over the last year I have grown my business by adhering to a solutions oriented philosophy. Regardless of how a potential client approaches us I always suggest services that are the best fit, even if it means we refer them elsewhere. I believe in providing services and solutions to clients they need and can financially benefit from.

I specialize in all things SEO, however, my intentions are always directed to the overall success of the business. I first began in the SEO/SEM/PPC industry in 2003 working for a large dealer group in Minneapolis. Since then much has changed but my passion for search has remained the same. I find it endlessly interesting because of the constantly changing environment.

Currently I have multiple clients in various areas. I service everything from large interactive agencies to small start-ups. By custom tailoring search solutions my clients experience maximum ROI with a minimal investment.

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions about internet marketing, I am always glad to help.
