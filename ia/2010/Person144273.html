Michelle Grimm’s Summary

My goals are to help my clients and customers every step along the way to buying or selling real estate. I want each and every one to feel like they are my only concern and trust me to handle their financial investment...their home. I believe in a strong emphasis on customer centered service.

I want my clients to consider me to be an on-call resource to assist our them before, during and after any transaction.

With sellers, I assist in preparing their properties with the aid of stagers, promote their homes with utilizing a full range dynamic print and online media, negotiate strong contracts and provide on-going communication throughout.

When working with buyers, I also utilize a variety of online technology to keep pace with the new inventory and offer fast responses, excellent contract crafting and negotiation with consistent follow-through on every purchase.

If you would like to receive a copy of the 7 Mistakes Sellers Make or the 7 Mistakes Buyers Make, please email me and I will gladly send you a copy!

Living in the area for over 22+ years I believe it is one of the best places to live and raise a family. I have 3 children and have enjoyed volunteering with many local organizations. My children have attended Irving Pertzsch Elementary, Eagle Bluff Elementary, Onalaska Middle and Onalaska High School.

Currently I am on the Board of Directors of Clearwater Farm Foundation, a non-profit 501C3 located in Green Coulee, Onalaska, WI.

Please feel free to contact me on how I can help you with your real estate needs. Who do you know who is looking to buy or sell real estate? I appreciate your referrals!
