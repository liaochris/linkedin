Andrew Roberts’s Summary

Currently CEO of Ephox, makers of EditLive!. EditLive! is the leading browser-based web authoring solution that is OEM'ed by most of the leading content management vendors. I helped found and raise angel capital for Ephox in Australia before moving to Silicon Valley in 2002.

At Ephox I have driven product development, capital raising, strategy and business development. I developed key strategic partnerships with IBM, Vignette, Oracle, FileNet, Open Text and others. Since becoming CEO in 2004, Ephox has more than tripled sales, been profitable and secured many major accounts.

Prior to Ephox and straight out of college I was the founder of web development company Webz Pty Ltd. At high school I was the co-founder of Home Computing Connections, a company similar to GeekSquad.

I have also been a member of the board of directors of Software Engineering Australia, a non-profit organization for start-ups in Australia and I have a keen interest in start-ups and the entrepreneurial process.
