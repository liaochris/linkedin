Jamie Williams-Jenkins’s Summary

From 11/2001 to 5/2002 I was in the automobile sales industry. There I met an individual who owned a wealth management firm and wanted to open a mortgage division for his clients. I obtained my Loan Officer License and opened a mortgage company within his firm. This mortgage company later sold. Meanwhile their investors had purchased Whataburger Franchises in Florida. I assisted this division with the tasks to get each Whataburger open and operable. Near the completion of the project, I was laid off. I returned to originating mortgages with the new owner of the mortgage company that I had initially began. During my employment with them, I was recruited by The US Rare Coin & Buillion Reserve in Austin, TX where I sold investment grade rare coins. During my employment with USRCBR, I was contacted by an original partner of the wealth management firm. He asked if I would join him in opening the first 1-800-PACK-RAT facility in Texas. I joined his team on a consultant basis in October 2005. Late December 2005, we opened the first facility in Austin, TX. By May 2006, this facility broke national sales records. We later opened 5 additional facilities. I have recently become a consultant for Ambit Energy. Ambit is an electrical service provider in Texas, Illinios and New York. Ambit offers one of the lowest electricity rates Texas. As a consultant for Ambit, I also teach people how to operate their own home-based business. Feel free to visit my website at www.jjenkins.goambit.com. Whether I'm selling automobiles, mortgages, rare coins, storage units or electricity, my passion is in the art of helping others save money and make money. I am in my element when I am teaching, coaching or consulting. My daily desire is to witness a difference in someone elses daily routine or life. My goals are simple: to provide for my daughters the things they need to become successfull, to be the best wife that I possibly can and to retire without any worries.
