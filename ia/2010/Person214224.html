Ashley Cline’s Summary

Ashley Cline is the Web Diva at Innovative Fox. Innovative Fox is an online marketing and strategy firm, specializing in web design and development as well as social media marketing. Ashley realizes that social media marketing is not just a trend, it is here to stay - though the distribution channels can and will change over time, the idea of online social interaction is a growing part of our lives. A fox is one of few animals that adapt to their environment and the harsh weather changes as oppose to hibernating or migrating. This is our goal at Innovative Fox – to educate our clients about online marketing so that they can adapt to technology as oppose to hibernating or migrating away from social media marketing.

She began her career more than five years ago at Enertia Solutions Corporate headquarters in Ann Arbor, Michigan when her training in Visual Communications technology catapulted her from there to California. In 2005 she opened an office for Enertia Solutions establishing their west coast headquarters in Huntington Beach. Then the creative process comes into play - brainstorming ideas for her clients to have a web marketing strategy that not only increases business sales and revenue but also creates efficiency, not only for their prospects on the web but for the company internally as well; all while making sure the company’s overall brand is accurately portrayed on the internet.

Ashley believes her secret to success is educating her clients on current technology to keep them apprised of this changing landscape. Not only does Ashley find it important to keep her clients educated on the fast changing industry, she also keeps herself up to date with the latest online marketing trends by taking online marketing certification courses through Inbound Marketing University as well as attending various industry related seminars and webinars.
