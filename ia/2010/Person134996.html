EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

SENIOR SALES Global MANAGEMENT EXECUTIVE, B to B Complex Solutions&Projects / High Tech / Complex Wireless Network Solutions / Software / LBS value Added Services / Security and Intelligence Solutions

Marlin Nelson is a sales marketing executive with global / international business acumen and 23 years of experience in the telecommunications, software and IT industries. Marlin is a member of a small circle of global sales marketing executives with professional and industry contacts on 5 continents. He is a sales leader that brings senior management experience in Global Sales, Channel Management, Marketing and Business Development to companies who are seeking to expand their global footprints, intensify market penetration, build brand recognition, increase customer loyalty and improve bottom line profitability.

He has also held senior management positions as GM-LBS Consulting Business Unit (San Francisco-USA), VP International Sales (USA), Vice President Global Sales (Cambridge UK), Vice President of EMEA Sales (Paris) , Senior Vice President and General Manager (London), Director International Sales (Amsterdam) and National Sales Manager (Atlanta-USA).

His experience ranges from start-ups to IPOs to industry leaders in enterprise / consumer software applications and wire line / wireless solutions including security, location based services, value added services and other radio access network (RAN) solutions.

Objectives:

Member of executive team in a senior management sales and / or business development role which leverages his ability to thrive in the rapidly changing global telecommunications, wireless and / or hi-tech environments. The position will also capitalize on his extensive global contact base and strong skill sets in global sales, marketing, business development and general management.
