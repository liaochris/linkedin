Dominic Garabedian’s Summary

Passionate and entrepreneurial Managing Director focused on exceeding sales goals, growing our business, delighting our customers, entering new markets, enhancing our product capabilities, and attracting and retaining the very best employees. Joined Mimeo as one of the first few employees and investors and have helped scale the sales and sales management organizations from the ground up. Currently focused on replicating this effort in the UK with our 2010 launch and serving as a member of the leadership team.

Mimeo is the innovator of online, on-demand business document and photo solutions. Over 60% of the Fortune 100 rely on Mimeo’s award winning cloud based document management tools and print on demand services to lower costs while improving employee productivity. Mimeo supports a wide variety of companies with the company’s open platform, a solution that enables partners and over 8,000 companies to leverage all or part of the Mimeo web to print platform as it were their own.

As a leading global technology company, Mimeo has been providing solutions to the market for over 10 years and operates the largest and most advanced digital print-on-demand facilities in the world. Leveraging technology, six sigma methodology, and outstanding customer care, we ensure unmatched service levels and quality with every order.

Mimeo is backed by Hewlett Packard, Goldman Sachs, Draper Fisher Jurvetson, DFJ Gotham Ventures, and HarbourVest.
