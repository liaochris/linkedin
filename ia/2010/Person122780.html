Law Info’s Summary

Established in 1994, LawInfo is recognized nationwide as a leader within the legal community, providing public access to pre-qualified, pre-screened attorneys, and to free legal resources.
LawInfo is a nation wide resources leader helping people find the quality legal resources they need to solve their legal problems.

Our signature service, the Lead Counsel Program*, was designed to provide a simple and reliable way for anyone on the Web to find a pre-qualified, pre-screened attorney quickly and easily. Lead Counsel Members must earn the right to use the Lead Counsel designation by passing our challenging certification process, which mandates peer recommendations, bar checks, and minimum practice area experience.

To help solve your legal issue by providing access to quality legal information and Lead Counsel Rated Attorneys.

If you're serious about growing your practice, LawInfo's Preferred Listings will help maximize your online exposure for optimum results.

Our Mission is To Help you Solve your Legal Issue by Providing Access to Quality Legal Information and Lead Counsel Rated Attorney.
