Dan Walker’s Summary

"Nobody ever went broke supplying what the customer is willing to pay for."

A properly defined problem has an obvious solution. I ask the right questions at the right time of the right people to define the real problem in simple terms. From this my clients can develop robust and long term solutions based on the correct information.

As an author and educator I teach my clients how to have "constructive conversations" with key individuals.

Using "The Customer's Way" I show them how to conduct deep interviews with key individuals to discover unmet wants, needs and desires. Based on the data we gather and proper analysis the client can strategically and systematically revise products, services and systems to deliver much higher value with less effort, time and investment.
