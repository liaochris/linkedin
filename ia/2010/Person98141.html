Jim Alvino’s Summary

Success Coach – Monetize Your Niche®

I founded Monetize Your Niche, Inc. to help entrepreneurs and small business owners find and follow their passion… and to profit from it. This is MY passion. My goal is to coach entrepreneurs and professionals, young and old, to assist them on this journey. Have a question? Email jim@monetizeyourniche.com.

This Is Who I Help

I am excited about coaching individuals who know they have more to offer than the lives they have been or are currently living. You know you have enormous potential and can do something great, but you feel stuck. Maybe you have a great idea for a company or a product but you don’t know where to start. Maybe you are in a dead end job or even executive position, and you want a viable way out. Maybe you have been downsized and you recognize this is YOUR time to strike out on your own. Maybe you just want to change direction. Whatever your WHY... I can help.

This Is How I Can Help You – Success Coach AND Business Coach

I bring two distinct but related, complex skill sets to the process in helping entrepreneurs pursue their passion for profit: 1) Success Coach – Personal Breakthrough Coaching that helps you identify and clear away your baggage and issues so you can bring your full power, inner resources and talents to bear on changing the quality of your life. 2) Business Coach – with expertise in strategic planning, business development, creative problem solving (CPS), and a host of related sub-skills to help assure your success. My Unique Value Proposition (UVP) is that I am able to bring BOTH sides of the equation to the table. The details: http://www.monetizeyourniche.com/coaching.html.

Recently I created a 3-CD Series which speaks to these areas and how they are integrated: “Secrets to Monetizing Your Niche: Advanced Mindset, Business Development, and Sales & Marketing Strategies for Pursuing Your Passion for Profit.” Check this out at http://www.monetizeyourniche.com/secrets.html.
