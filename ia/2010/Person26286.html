Boyko Aladjov’s Summary

Research and development in electrochemical engineering, emphasizing on electrochemical energy storage and conversion. Thorough knowledge in batteries for EV, HEV and Plug-In HEV. Charge/discharge algorithm, safety and abuse testing of different batteries and fuel cell systems. Strong background in variety of battery systems, fuel cells, electrochemical testing and evaluation, R & D of battery chemistries, such as nickel/metal hydroxide and lithium-based batteries.
Authored/co-authored 36 issued U.S. patents and many scientific papers and international conference presentations.

Boyko Aladjov’s Specialties:

Different battery chemistries and fuel cell systems, production planning, R&D, modeling, designing, performance testing, charge/discharge algorithm, safety and abuse testing, project management and new product introduction. Batteries for EV, HEV, PHEV, Stationary, Military and Consumer Electronic Applications.
