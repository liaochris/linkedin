Ann Lancaster’s Summary

I am a Seattle-based web designer with a passion for CSS, specializing in web-based content management systems such as SharePoint, Plone, Movable Type, and Wordpress. With more than 10 years of experience creating and maintaining clean, elegant, cross-browser-compatible and efficient websites, I pay close attention to web standards and emerging design trends with an emphasis on usability. My creativity, motivation and excellent eye for detail allow me to learn quickly and teach myself new skills and technologies which I enthusiastically apply to all my work. I am continuously searching for the most efficient solution to any problem thrown at me. I am very easy to get along with and work well both independently and with groups. I am equally comfortable working from an office or telecommuting.
