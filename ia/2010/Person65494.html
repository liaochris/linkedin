Erica Garvey - CSAM’s Summary

As an Account Executive, Erica is part of a team of 3 Medical Device specialists at Management Recruiters of St. Lawrence County (MRSLC). MRSLC, which specializes in recruiting in Healthcare, Medical Device and EMS, is among the top 3% of 1100 MRINetwork offices worldwide and was a 2008 Recipient of MRINetwork’s President’s Club Award.

Erica holds a BA in Public Relations and Marketing from Oswego State University where she graduated Magna Cum Laude and was inducted into the Phi Kappa Phi Honor Society. Prior to becoming part of the recruiting industry, Erica worked in Outside Sales and Account Management in the Media, Printing/Bulk Mailing and Window Fashion industries.

Mrs. Garvey has been part of the MRSLC team for 4 years. Most of her experience has been concentrated in Diagnostics, Orthopedics and Cardiovascular within all levels of Sales and Marketing. Erica has ranked among the top AE’s in the Eastern Region and Worldwide and has established relationships with leading companies in the Medical Device arena. Erica also completed her certification program and became a Certified Senior Account Manager (CSAM) in May 2010.

Erica resides on the banks of the Oswegatchie River with her beloved Black Lab, "Ossy." She enjoys kayaking, golfing, writing and reading in her spare time.

Please visit www.mrslc.com for more information
