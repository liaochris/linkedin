Steve Parks’s Summary

I'm a presenter, author and conference speaker focused on business & entrepreneurship.

I present online TV programmes, webinars and telephone seminars for Natwest, BT, the Business Continuity Institute and more.

My books include 'Start Your Business Week by Week' and 'How to be an Entrepreneur', and are all published by Pearson under their Prentice Hall Business imprint.

I speak at a lot of conferences and events, and specialise in chairing/moderating conferences. With my radio background I get the best out of panellists while keeping everything running to time.

I am also the founder of a number of businesses, including:
+ Pilot Ventures - develops new business ideas until they are mature enough to sell or spin-out.
+ Pilot Internet - a web development agency focused on building business websites with Drupal
+ Wraply.com - the group gift giving service. An idea from Pilot Ventures which is now a separate company.
+ SituationNews.com - a specialist news service for business continuity professionals and senior executives in large organisations for use in a crisis. Operates under the Pilot Ventures umbrella.

Prior to my interest in entrepreneurship I was a BBC radio journalist, covering the north of England for BBC Radio 4 and Radio 5-Live, as well as occasional work for the World Service, and TV news programmes.

When not working I indulge my inner foodie
