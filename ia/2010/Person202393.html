Therese Beale’s Summary

Therese established an independent consultancy in 1999 after nine years at global public relations agencies (Burson Marsteller and Fleishman-Hillard) and eight years as a daily newspaper reporter and editor. She works with organizations seeking clarity and differentiation in their sales and marketing messages. Clients range from non-profits to leading consumer brands and global professional services firms.

Therese Beale’s Specialties:

Business communication strategy; brand messaging and sales message development; marketing communication development; internal communications; message development training & facilitation.
