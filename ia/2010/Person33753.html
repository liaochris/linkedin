Casimir Fornalski’s Summary

I design striking and memorable presentation, packaging, and production graphics for film, media, and promotional campaigns.

My technical proficiency includes the entire Creative Suite (CS4: Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Flash, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Premiere, Media Encoder), as well as Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio Pro, Apple Motion, Soundtrack Pro, iDVD, Compressor, HTML, OSX and Windows. Very comfortable working in 3D space and currently developing knowledge base for mobile app design.

Casimir Fornalski’s Specialties:

Visual Art, Web and Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, Film + Video Production, Color Correction, Photographic Retouching, Vector Drawing, Cinema, Film, Media and Event Marketing - Posters, Postcards, Print, Packaging.
