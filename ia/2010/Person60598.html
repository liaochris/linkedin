Scott Hacker’s Summary

Let us help you reach today's active youth between the ages of 7-14 years old. We will take your brand to youth sporting events around the United States and interact face-to-face with the players, family members, and fans. Our traveling FANFEST interactive mobile tours reach millions of kids each year during the baseball, soccer, and football seasons, creating long lasting impressions and direct sales. Our sponsors include some of the best in the business.

Some clients include: ABC Network / Disney Channel (4th year), Major League Baseball Players Association (5th year), Topps (5th year), Upper Deck (5th year), Sports Illustrated KIDS (3rd year), Airheads (2nd year), Backyard Sports (4th year), Louisville Slugger (4th year), Disney's Wide World of Sports (2nd year), Major League Soccer (2nd year), California Cuties (2nd year), Disney XD (2nd year), Shock Doctor (1st year) and Activision / Little League World Series (1st year).

To learn more about Dugout Media, go to http://www.dugoutmedia.com

Scott Hacker’s Specialties:
