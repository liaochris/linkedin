Karenne Sylvester’s Summary

I'm a certified TESOL trainer, working as a freelancer in Stuttgart, Germany, specializing in teaching adult learners within the financial/ banking, engineering and IT sectors.

My teaching philosophy is heavily student-centered and my classes are tailored specifically to my clients' needs.

As a former Director of two international non-profit organizations, I am able to bring along experience from the business perspective into the classroom and as webmaster, ningmaster and blogger I am able to share my knowledge of the web 2.0 with students.

I've lived, worked and taught all over the world from the Caribbean to the US, UK, Australia, Hong Kong and Ecuador and enjoy discussing intercultural viewpoints.

I am the author of SimplyConversationsTM, a pedagogically sound speaking skills system designed to activate language learners' fluency levels.

One of my favorite activities is sharing my knowledge with other EFL teachers.

My current workshops include:

~ SimplySpeaking - teaching teachers to teach speaking
~ Using New Media and Technology in the language classroom.
~ Getting the most out of a business English textbook.
~ Blogging and the Language Learner

My email address is kalinago(dot)english (at) gmail.com and
I am always happy to network with those in the same industry, with the same interests or with people who'd like to discuss learning or teaching English issues.

No random, off-topic, networkers please!

I am also available on twitter (@kalinagoenglish) and you can raid my 'delicious' links at any time: http://delicious.com/KalinagoEnglish
