Tonya Harris’s Summary

Tonya Harris is the President and CEO of Dynamic Visions, a training consulting services firm out of Atlanta, Georgia. She has over 20+ years in the training consulting and motivational speaking industry.

She’s an Executive Coach, author of various business articles, mentors young women voluntarily and coaches Business Owners, Entrepreneurs and Professionals on a daily basis in local, regional, national and global businesses.

She believes strongly in giving back to the community and supports innovative neighborhood and business developments in her county like “Engage Gwinnett”, as a Citizen Leader and as a leader in her Homeowners Association. She's also a member of the National Association of Professional Women and most importantly serves as a Servant Leader at New Birth Missionary Baptist church for the past 14 years in the Deacon’s, Christian Education and New Citizens ministries.

She has several training and management certificates throughout various industries from faith-based, consulting, entrepreneurship, healthcare, finance, retail and the banking industry.

Her focus and continued goal is helping professionals, faith-based organizations, corporations, small businesses and colleges to implement solutions that help leaders, staff & clients understand how to make impacts to the bottom line results of an organization.

Her processes focus on the development of individual’s interpersonal skills for personal and professional growth. They benefit the individual, their families and their profession from their newly found paradigm shift.

Tonya Harris’s Specialties:

Healthcare Professional Educational Awareness, Strategic Planning, Leadership Development, Motivational and Informative Speaking Engagements and One-on-One Coaching for various industries (Banking, Finance, Mortgage, Insurance, Healthcare and Faith-based Organizations).
