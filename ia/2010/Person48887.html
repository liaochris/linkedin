Darlene Ford, MBA, PMI’s Summary

Darlene Ford has extensive experience developing and executing effective Web strategies and e-marketing programs within the information technology industry. Darlene is a seasoned technology marketer who, in the last decade, has led the evolution of digital marketing practices at brands like IBM, Microsoft and now McKesson. At each of these brands, she helped build teams and platforms to take advantage of new marketing practices in social media, e-mail, relationship marketing, and demand generation.

In her current position, she is responsible for optimizing existing marketing partnerships and creating and executing online experiences and marketing programs to meet lead generation goals and increase awareness of the McKesson brand. Darlene has served on Web Standards Committees and Communication Council Boards.
.
Managing and consulting for various business units, Darlene was able to lead a project whereby she delivered a usable lead management program that helped the team devise a lead nurturing system that automated multi-step marketing programs, and built relationships with prospects. Through the success of this program McKesson was able to understand each marketing interaction with every lead and opportunity and demonstrate marketing’s impact on revenue.

Another initiative Darlene led was the Newsroom Redesign which ultimately helped McKesson shift perceptions and be seen as a thought leader in the industry. Working with a design agency, Darlene leveraged Web 2.0 capabilities and was able to create a go to resource for medical news.

Darlene holds a MBA degree with a Marketing concentration from Clark Atlanta University, a BS degree in Business Administration with a Marketing concentration from Winthrop University, and a Project Management Certificate from George Washington University. In addition, she is a winner of the 2009 Web Award for Outstanding Achievement in Web Development and is a member of Who’s Who Women in Business.
