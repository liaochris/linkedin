Randy Dean’s Summary

For the last 5+ years, I've been running my own consulting, training, and speaking company on the topics of time management, e-mail management, and the related use of technology (planners, PDAs, Smart Phones, software). I've been leading programs around the US at national-level conferences, Fortune 500 companies, and for highly regarded universities. I am also developing information products, with a goal to have several products on the Internet within the next 12-24 months. I have written two books - the latest is an Amazon.com #1 Best Seller in E-mail and Manager's Guides to Computing, called Taming the E-mail Beast, and is all about how to better manage your e-mail account (learn more at http://www.TamingEmailBook.com), and I expect to soon develop several CD and DVD programs on my topics.

The company has grown every year since inception, and we have led live on-site programs, teleseminars, and webinars for clients, as well as some individual consulting. We have had a very strong repeat business rate, showing that these programs have performed very well for our clients. I have met the requirements to become a full member of the National Speakers Association, and am also an active member of the ASTD, SBAM, and the Michigan Society of Association Executives. I graduated with honors from both Michigan State University (MBA '97) and Western Michigan University (BBA '91). You can get more info on me and my programs by visiting my web sites: http://www.randalldean.com and http://www.emailsanityexpert.com. You can also see a "demo video" of one of my recent e-mail courses by visiting my You Tube channel (search my username: JanaRandyDean).
