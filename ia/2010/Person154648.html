Nicole Kirksey, MPH, MSW, CCLC’s Summary

My professional and ministry focus is to empower others to succeed in meeting their personal, professional, and spiritual goals through life coaching. I serve by drawing on over twenty years of peace practice experience, nearly twenty years of health and human services leadership experience, five years of mediation experience, and twelve years of lay ministry.

On LinkedIn, I am looking to connect with others who are interested in coaching, ministry and solopreneur business development.
