Scott Southron’s Summary

Scott brings a unique blend of experience leading both public and private companies as a chief financial officer and working on the banking side as a partner in investment banking. He has more than 24 years of experience working for industry leaders including The Walt Disney Company, MCI/Worldcom, Price Waterhouse, Lyon Apartment Companies (majority owned by Major General William Lyon), and Roth Capital Partners.
His experience includes in-depth expertise across corporate finance, accounting and reporting, treasury and operational functions including significant international knowledge and experience working in Europe.
