Manassah Bradley’s Summary

The Loupe Group is a Management Consulting firm based in Boston, Massachusetts. We are catalysts for economic growth and expansion within our client’s organization. While most consulting firms focus on problem solving within a specific industry, we are experts in assessing business models, creating growth opportunities and developing new business models that make use of those opportunities. This is how we create value for our clients, who are large and small, public and private, for-profit and not-for-profit companies.

Manassah Bradley’s Specialties:

We bring fresh eyes and an entrepreneurial mindset to analyze our client’s current business model and their approach to money making. We examine the model to see if it passes the narrative and numbers test. We scrutinize and question every detail of the model to understand the underlying economic logic and realities of the system. We then provide a range of tactical recommendations to ensure that our client’s business model is producing intended results – positive cash flow and profitability.
