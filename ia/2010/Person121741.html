Laura Duran’s Summary

Laura Duran & Associates is a public relations consulting firm providing communications leadership in support of two of the most valuable assets an organization can build – strong business relationships and exceptional brand presence.

Our progressive PR services include strategic brand marketing guidance, issue management and advocacy, multi-channel communication and content, traditional media relations, and social media guidance and training.

Through proven expertise and focused attention, we excel at helping our clients engage their internal and external communities, present their unique strengths and narratives, and manage key issues through meaningful communication. We succeed on behalf of our clients by believing in their efforts and getting behind their goals and brand as if they were our own.

The organizations we support and partner with include the Chester County Chamber of Business and Industry, International Association of Business Communicators, National Trust for Historic Preservation, National Charrette Institute, Smart Growth Network, Sustainable Cities Collective, and Essence of Humanity Award Program.
