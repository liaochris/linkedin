Philologist: One who studies a language in the cultural context that is necessary for understanding its literary works and other significant texts. Philology includes the study of the grammar, linguistic history, and literature of a language as well as the culture of places where that language is spoken and the people who speak it.

Geographer: One who studies the spacial relationships and distribution of the cultural, ecological, and physical features of the Earth and their interaction. Geography includes the study of human culture, meteorology, ecology, geology, cartography, and the Earth's physical landscape.
