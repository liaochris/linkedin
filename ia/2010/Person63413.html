Elisa Di Fiore’s Summary

After eight years of experience in the gaming industry, starting with product management, then moving into the game enthusiast press, I'm ready to bring all of my years of hard work full circle. My focus is on videogame localization. After extensive time invested in multiple elements of the videogame industry, plus the number of years I've spent abroad, I feel that my grasp on videogame terminology, from technical terms to dialogue, is an asset for anyone seeking professional English-to-Italian translation.
