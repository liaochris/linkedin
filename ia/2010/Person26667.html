Brad Shaw’s Summary

I am a creative strategist engaged in all aspects of graphic design for the web, print and corporate identity since graduating from design school in 1996, and have been involved in designing for the on and offline gaming sector (casino, poker and sports betting) for most of that time.

I have had the pleasure of working with numerous like minded creatives and technically oriented individuals over the years, both learning from those with greater experience than myself, as well as passing on my knowledge to those I have directed.

Most recently as a Creative Manager, in an art direction role, I have worked to bring together an effective team of designers recruited from the local market and the UK.

Overseeing a team of 4, we produced all the visual communications materials necessary for a large and constantly growing international gaming company.

Moving forward I look to expand on my experience and am currently designing for numerous large corporate clients, up and coming entrepreneurs, as well as developing my own internet based marketing business.
