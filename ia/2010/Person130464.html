Dan Mallin’s Summary

For nearly 20 years, Dan has established a solid track record in creating, growing and transforming businesses through roles at both large and small businesses, ranging from technology services, sales, marketing, manufacturing, new product development and business development. He is known for his ability to quickly understand any business problem and offer a solution.

Two time finalist for Ernst & Young’s “Entrepreneur of the Year” award in 2001 and 2004, and has been recognized by the Twin Cities’ The Business Journal, where he was named to their “40 Under 40” list.
