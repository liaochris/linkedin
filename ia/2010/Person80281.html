Harve Platig’s Summary

After marketing and executive experience in both film, video, and non profit sectors, I am applying my background (which includes VP and Board level positions) to helping organizations with their accounts receivable. I utilize long term strategy experience and the NCS cash recovery system to turn delinquent accounts into cash in your business. Breakthrough results include a minimum 400% ROI guaranteed! That is downright refreshing in today's marketplace.

My goals are to blend the background I have with this vitally needed service and make a positive impact on our economy. Bringing receivables up to speed and keeping them there mends a major hole in the financial health of many organizations. For instance, the average hospital in the US carries between 50 and 55 mil in receivables. This blocks progress, hiring, and purchases. By systematic cash recovery, collections costs are vastly reduced (by 40-60%) and effectiveness of the process soars from the average 15% success rate to up to 60% and more. All this is done for around 10% instead of the standard 30-50% fees that are found in the collections industry.

I have openings for consultative account executives. This is not a good fit for a first sales job. It is a great fit if you are looking for a long term opportunity with outstanding rewards. Collections is one of the fastest growing industries today. Admittedly, that does not sound like good news. However, there is a way to do this that restores communication between creditors and their debtors, thus preserving the business relationship if both parties are honest and willing. Our approach is proven over 25 years but it is as if it just came on the scene yesterday because so few of our potential clients have discovered it.
