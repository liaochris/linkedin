Andrea Belz’s Summary

My work focuses on transforming innovation into profits with a sharp focus on market analysis and understanding value propositions. My new book, "The 36-Hour Course in Product Development" (McGraw-Hill) can be used as a classroom text or for independent study.

I speak often on IP strategies and management, both in spin-out and spin-up environments, and on IP valuation and integration for spin-in strategies.

Andrea Belz’s Specialties:

- Market analysis for technology innovation.
- The "R to D" transition and acceleration to market.
- Corporate finance via government grants and standard private equity instruments.
- Product roadmaps.
- Technology transfer and licensing strategies.
- Intellectual property management.
