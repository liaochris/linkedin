Michael Vorel’s Summary

Michael Vorel is CEO and founder of Vastplanet Corporation, specializing in e-Commerce Web Development, Internet Marketing Strategy and Management Consulting for a variety of top brands since 1998. He has expertise in affiliate marketing, social media strategy, video, ppc, seo, sem, email marketing and competitive analysis.

As an affiliate marketer and outsourced affiliate program manager, he is actively involved in creating affiliate web sites and blogs in addition to managing affiliate programs for clients. Based in Tampa, Florida, he is a member of the Affiliate Summit Advisory Board, Everest University Advisory Board, Authorized Yahoo Store Developer and recently helped with forming the Performance Marketing Association as a member of the Governance Committee.

He enjoys a great cup of coffee, wine, running and gadgets. His latest project is Projectgadget.com and you can find him on Twitter at @michaelvorel, @vastplanet @projectgadget.

Available for short and long term management consulting projects, partnerships, advisory boards and speaking opportunities in e-commerce, social media, affiliate marketing, advertising and business development.
