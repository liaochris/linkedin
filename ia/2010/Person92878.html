Jeetendra Joshi’s Summary

I take this opportunity to introduce myself as a seasoned e-commerce / Marketing professional with over a decade’s rich corporate experience within Indian Luxury industry.

The most important among all was my association with ebay.com (India) Pvt. Ltd. I got the opportunity to venture into the field of e-commerce when the concept was in its infant stage. I joined the organization in July 2003 and learnt the basics of Business–to-consumer model. I was managing Jewelry and Watches category in ebay.in formerly Baazee.com, world's leading marketplace.

I was Instrumental in launching www.trijewels.com India's first jewelry portal.

Opportunity working with India's largest Jewellery company "Gitanjali Gems Ltd." Changed my out look towards internet and alternate channels as whole.

Heading ecommerce department for established brands Like Nakshatra, Ddamas, Asmi, Gili and many luxury brands is stressful but very enjoyable experience.

I hold the distinction of expanding Gitanjali's Ecommerce channel where Ecommerce is grown by more than 10 times YOY sales growth.
Success for the growth is because understanding customer needs and offering best service and products.

Correct merchandise and right price with quality service which is made us successful.

The aforementioned experiences and attributes have not only helped me grow professionally but also have complemented the academe. I am a Graduate of Parle College, Mumbai University with Statistics as my special Paper. I have also completed a Diploma course in Import & Export Management from Indian Merchant Chamber in 1995. Additionally, I have done a Certificate Course in Web Designing along with attending a Professional Program in Retail Management from Wellingkar’s Institute of Management to sharpen my professional skills. Currently doing specialized course in search engine marketing from worlds leading Search engine education institute Marketing experiments Labs, USA.
