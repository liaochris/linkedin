Matthew Warchola’s Summary

I am a web developer with a background in object-oriented ActionScript 3, JavaScript (JQuery), HTML, XML, and table-less CSS. I also have experience in the use of object-oriented PHP, MySQL, and SQL for server-side functionality, data integration, and support of client-side code.

I see technology as a powerful resource for achieving business objectives and I am extremely interested in creating automated, web based businesses that run on their own. Over the years, I've had the fortune of working with many talented teams, agencies, and business owners on a array of Web 2.0 projects, particularly in the area of online multimedia. I thrive in a collaborative environment and I enjoy working independently while leading projects and presenting potential solutions for technical and business challenges.

My current goal is to work directly with enterprise business leaders to develop strategies for media implementation and to enhance the effectiveness of their multimedia programs (through enhanced media delivery, increased ROI, etc.)
