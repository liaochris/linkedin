Lynne Zagami Riquelme’s Summary

I spent three years as a corporate attorney in two large law firms before changing course to focus on the business of law firms and in-house legal departments. I am currently working to help firms and companies respond to the changing climate by automating their document production and management processes. My legal experience involves a wide variety of corporate and securities law matters, including venture capital transactions, SEC compliance and corporate finance.

Lynne Zagami Riquelme’s Specialties:

Counseled emerging growth companies in venture capital and corporate finance transactions, as well as corporate governance issues. Represented publicly traded companies in SEC compliance and transactional matters. Represented private equity firms in the fund formation and capital raising processes. Advised companies of all sizes on labor and employment issues.
