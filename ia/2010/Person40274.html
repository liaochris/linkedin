Charles Harvey’s Summary

As co-founder and CEO of The One 21 I am building an online music center that focuses on redefining the meaning of "Christian music" and creating a mecca for music buyers looking for a broad spectrum of music of all genres made by Christian music artists.

Additionally, through my Infusiac consulting practice, I work with business leaders to create thriving companies out of successful businesses and help growing companies work through the inevitable strategic inflection points (acquisitions, financings, strategic re-toollings, international expansions, key leader transitions, etc) they encounter during their business's life cycles.

I have succeeded in small companies and Fortune 500 companies, in troubled operations and rapidly growing enterprises, in Internet marketing, online publishers, alternative energy, professional services, software, Internet services, manufacturing and restaurant businesses.

I learned how to quickly assimilate business models at Cooper's Lybrand, forged my financial leadership at Pepsico, developed treasury skills at Schlotzsky's and JMJ Associates, soldified my financing, M&A and operations skills at Pixel Magic Imaging and honed my metrics and merger integration acumen at Hoover's.

Charles Harvey’s Specialties:

Experiences includes search word marketing, organizational development, business metrics, planning/forecasting, business financing (VC, IPO, debt, leasing, strategic vendor), strategy development, mergers/acquisitions, accounting, financial reporting, systems design and implementation, benefits, product management, software development, QA, manufacturing, customer service, facilities, HR, transfer pricing, risk management, Sarbanes Oxley, project management, turnaound management.
