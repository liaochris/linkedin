Jaime Kosofsky’s Summary

Brady & Kosofsky, PA., represents the new breed of real estate law firm. We are borne of the current economic conditions, and have evolved into an agile, technology based firm which delivers big firm product with the velvet touch of a small boutique firm.

Our firm provides closing, title search, short sale support and legal services in North Carolina and South Carolina.

Brady & Kosofsky, PA., has the technology to do smooth closings, settlements and/or escrows, in house, via video closing, or telephone closing. Our firm can provide full closing services in Spanish and English.

REO Seller Representation. Brady & Kosofsky, PA., represents numerous Asset Management Companies, Lending Institutions, Title Vendors and Investors in the National Default Industry. Our team members each have a minimum of four years of experience in the highly specialized marketpalce.

Real Estate Information Systems. Brady & Kosofsky, PA., provides title searches, and product throughout the states of North Carolina and South Carolina. Our network of real estate professionals and contractors, must undergo a a screening and training procedure to make sure that they are properly trained, and that our clients recieve a quality product.



Kosofsky has been a member of the professional motorsports industry since joining the motor sports media in 1993. In 1998 (after becoming licensed to practice law, Kosofsky has negotiated, and drafted contracts for every aspect of the industry, including Driver Contracts, Crew Member Contracts, Motor Program Contracts, licensing agreements, event sponsorships as well as many others.

Kosofsky's current and past clients include competitors, teams, industry partners, and members of the media in the NASCAR Sprint Series, Nationwide Series, Craftsman Truck Series, IndyCar Series, Firehawk Series, ARCA, World of Outlaws, USAC, and many others.
