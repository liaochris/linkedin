Dan Rodriguez’s Summary

I am an engineer with a passion for finding improvements where needed and then implementing robust solutions.
I have managed engineering groups in multiple projects including Failure Analysis, Design review, Lean Manufacturing, Throughput enhancement, and implementing Quality assurance methods and Cost reduction.
Through my experience I have had the opportunity to work with Foundries world-wide generating new devices, introducing process improvements and maintaining quality control over existing products.

I am a self starter, highly motivated individual that uses a hands on approach to every project including leadership roles.
