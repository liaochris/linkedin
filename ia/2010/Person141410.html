Mireille Etienne’s Summary

Mireille (Mimi) Etienne, NCC, LPC, CCFC
Therapist & Life Coach

Mimi received her under-graduate degree in psychology at Clark Atlanta University, obtained her Masters of Arts in Psychology from Georgia School of Professional Psychology at Argosy University, and is currently pursuing her Doctorate in Psychology with a focus in Health Psychology at Capella University. Mimi holds a current license in the State of Georgia as a Professional Counselor, is a Nationally Certified Counselor and is also a Clinically Certified Forensic Counselor. Her work in the forensic field qualifies her to testify in legal proceeding as an expert witness. She is also a member of the International Coach Federation. She has years of experience working with adolescents, adults and families providing brief and long term therapeutic services addressing bereavement , depression, adjustment disorders and life stressors. Additionally, Mimi provides consultation and training to other mental health professionals and certified law enforcement officers.

Mireille Etienne’s Specialties:

Working with clients who suffer from severe depression, anxiety and life stressors.
