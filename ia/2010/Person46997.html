Daniel Binns’s Summary

I have 20 over years of experience working with global brands like Coca-Cola, GM, L'Oreal, Nestle, Unilever and Kimberly-Clark. I have worked in 7 countries and can be truly called an advertising internationalist. I have a network of contacts that enable me to gain high level access to a vast array of companies from a wide variety of industries.

I now specialise in helping companies define and manage their corporate brands and manage their CSR and sustainabilty programs.

I also help nonprofit organisations better understand how to approach the corporate sector for sponsorship support and can open the doors to help make their plans a reality.

I have run multi-million dollar, 200 man advertising agencies and can advise start up companies on developing their business strategies and plans.
