Jean Allary’s Summary

Passionate about consumer psychology and motivations, marketing uses, ideas, innovations, advertising and pop culture, I'm about to graduate in a Masters degree in Branding and Advertising (CELSA, France. Top Tier Marketing & Communication School).

I've been a trainee in several businesses : a Private Equity house, an industrial leader, an advertising agency, a publishing house and a photo-engraving studio.

My interests scope from actuality, innovations & ideas, digital, design, arts to an open-minded conception of culture : literacy, painting, cinema, photography, architecture, (re)cycling, gardening and so on... I'm keen to turn my passion into an exciting job.
