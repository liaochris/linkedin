William Truax’s Summary

My specialty is Short Term Projects for companies.
I have been involved with motivational speaking and sales training with my own company since 1978. I speak about and specialize in four skill bases.
First, Goal setting and achievement motivational presentations.
Second, Prospecting and making cold calls in the commercial/industrial market place.
Third, heading up short term project teams in a variety of areas.
[Fourth, uniquely unrelated, I conduct Homeland Security programs on preparing for bomb incidents, explained below.]

I have been involved in speaking, sales, sales and Prospecting training and consulting since 1972. I have trained thousands of people in the skills of goal achievement, Prospecting, and selling.

I have written 4 books on Prospecting as well as skill based training programs on public speaking and manners, courtesy, and etiquette for the business professional.

I have written dozens of articles on Goal setting and achievement and Prospecting

Our company goal is to provide our Prospecting and Motivational presentations, programs and materials to individuals and companies that can benefit from them.

YouTube videos are available at www.blitzcall.com and www.itsjustaskill.com

As a totally unconnected side line. I am certified by The Department of Homeland Security to speak about and teach programs to corporate First Responders on dealing with preparing for and dealing with Bomb Incidents.
I ran the 6th Army's Demolition and Booby Trap School at Ft. Lewis, Washington while on active duty and have over 3500 hours of instruction time teaching Demolitions.

William Truax’s Specialties:
