Caroline Chu’s Summary

Caroline Chu, a former Silicon Valley Creative Director, is the founder of Caroline Chu Inc. She holds her Masters in Industrial Arts, and seeks to incorporate her artistic visions into her brand of skin care and cosmetics.

With her eye for innovative, practical design, Caroline focuses on the creative medium most fundamental of all—the art of individual beauty. Caroline Chu first introduced her elegant line of nourishing and natural products in Jan 2007.

Caroline Chu serves as Board of Director and Advisor for various technological companies and for the university where she earned her Masters degree. She is also a contributing writer for the World Journal. Additionally, she has earned numerous awards in her design career.

Her company and products have been interviewed and featured by Fox News, 7x7 San Francisco Magazine, Beauty News NYC, Pretty LA, She finds, World Journal, AArising, StyleMaven, TheLuxurySpot, DivineCaroline, Styleammo, TheScentedLife,Eliza, 50Fabulous, My Intimate Affair with Fashion, urbandarling, myonramp, guidespot, thisnext and B5media hotmamadaily, Autotropolis, Spa Buzz, My Shopping Connection, Feteafete, Advicesisters, RedCarpetSF, Pretty by Natural, My Style Online, Jolienadine, ViViD Magazine and darkshadowsmagazine.com

She is also proud to have sponsored various non-profit organizations, such as AICI, OnLok, DIL, Lions Clubs International, Pacific Asian American Women Bay Area Coalition (PAAWBAC). San Francisco Hepatitis B Free.org and the Ovarian Cancer Research Fund.

Caroline Chu is active in her community, and has spoken at
University of North Texas, Chung Yuan Christian University in Taiwan, San Francisco State University and Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising.

Fiona Ma, the Majority Whip of the California Assembly, honored Caroline Chu with the California Small Business Award.
