  • VP Sales & Marketing

    Construction Data Company

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    2006 — October 2009 (3 years )

    Construction Data sells subscription access to an extensive database of current construction project information. This data is used by contractors, suppliers and other construction professionals to identify potential new business opportunities. The company serves the majority of the construction markets east of the Mississippi river.

    I served as the VP of Sales and Marketing and was instrumental in developing the first online marketing programs for the company. This included search engine marketing, organic search optimization, email and the redesign of the website. The program ultimately generated $1.7 million in internet assisted sales.

    In addition I oversaw the off-line (human based) sales team of nearly 70 inside professional salespeople. We made numberous policy and process changes that enabled us to grow sales at a rapid rate prior to the economic downturn. During the recession the online marketing program and other initiatives enabled us to hold our own and out-perform the competition.

    Lastly, I founded the first customer retention effort by creating a customer retention team that maintains continuous contact with customers and resolves customer satisfaction issues quickly and efficiently. This resulted in a significant improvement in subscription renewal rates.

  • President & CEO

    Parish Publications, LLC

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    2000 — 2005 (5 years )

    Parish Publications was a niche business providing weekly Sunday church bulletins to 450 Catholic churches in Michigan and New York state from two production plants. Advertising sales in the bulletins provided the revenue for the business. 'The churches received the bulletins free of charge. The company employed 85 people and was fully integrated provided advertising sales, pre-press, printing and distribution of the bulletins to the individual churches.

    The company had a church marketing department that solicited contracts with new churches and served the needs of the existing church partners. In general, 5 year contracts were signed and small incentives were paid to the individual parishes.

    The business was a portfolio company of Waud Capital Partners, in Chicago, Ill. (www.waudcapital.com) from 1997 to 2005. In 2005 the business was sold to Liturgical Publications, in Milwaukee, WI (http://www.4lpi.com/new) at a considerable profit.


    I was recruited to accomplish a turnaround of the business after prior management had created a toxic working environment that infiltrated the workforce and the customer base. Revenue and profit were falling dramatically. The root of the issue was an abusive management style that undermined productivity and created excessive turnover. In addition, a significant upgrade in press equipment, data systems and graphic production technology was needed.

    The turnaround was accomplished by replacing key managers, changing production processes, upgrading equipment and improving customer relationships. The primary initiative was to repair the company's reputation by exceed customer expectations 100% of the time. This required intensive customer interaction during the rebuild process and a full commitment from all our stakeholder.

    Thanks to a great team we succeeded and met our objective of returning the company to historic profit levels and a first place standing in our markets.

  • V.P. Sales & Operations

    Heritage Newspapers

    (Publishing industry)

    1987 — 2000 (13 years )

    Heritage owns a chain of award winning weekly and twice weekly community newspapers. The business is now part of the Journal Register newspaper group. We added 5 acquisitions in to the group in 3 years.

    My function was to oversee day-to-day operations in all areas of the business including production, sales, circulation, classified, accounting and editorial for the group of 17 publications operating out of 11 different locations.

    In addition, I played an active role in the due diligence process and implemented the integration plan for the acquired property. All these integrations when smoothly and the businesses became highly profitable assets in the group.
