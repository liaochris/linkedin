John Huffman IV’s Summary

John has become known as an innovative digital media & entertainment entrepreneur. Real Content Group (RCG) is the third company he has had a hand in founding, and he has served as the Company’s CEO since its inception. RCG is a mobile entertainment company that licenses, produces, markets and distributes content.

Prior to Real Content Group, John co-founded Real Hip Hop as a direct-to-consumer mobile content company with mobile industry leader m-Qube. Prior to Real Hip Hop John structured a strategic alliance with Rondor/Universal Music Group called Silverback Music Publishing. Silverback is a lyric focused music publishing company that uses the Internet and Mobile technology to acquire, develop and sell songs to new and established artists, producers and A&R executives.

Before being sold, John’s first entrepreneurial venture, Maximum Networks, spun off several online innovations including a lifestyle-specific search engine, an online e-commerce site, and an A&R Operating System that included content management, micro-market testing and work flow management.

European based “Digital Distribution In The Music Industry” voted John “30 Under 30” two years in a row. Other career highlights include John’s co-development of the community event model for the Hip-Hop Summit Action Network lead by Russell Simmons and Dr. Ben Chavis.

In addition to leading Real Content Group, John has established the Real Content Capital fund to invest in niche market entertainment properties including mobile web, applications and content.
