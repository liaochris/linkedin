Jose Alarcon’s Summary

Mr. Alarcon is the founder and Managing Partner of Intelligent Bills, an organization focused on helping its client’s draft, develop and implement Telecom Cost Management Solutions. Coupled with over 15 years of technical and consultative experience in telecommunications, serving most recently as a Partner and Vice President at Control Point Solutions, a firm that has used TEM Strategies to help enterprises secure more than $ 1.5 Billion in refunds and savings, he has piloted the development of several new successful TEM consulting service offerings that positively impacted several Fortune 500 organizations including 6 of the Fortune 10.
