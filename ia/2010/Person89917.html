Jari King’s Summary

As a former owner/manager of a $10,000,000 staffing service that was listed on the Inc 500, I have spent most of my long career in the business of recruiting and the management of sales personnel.

I have been very active in state and national trade associations that represent the staffing industry and have served on local, state and national trade industry boards.

For the last 4 years I have been engaged in the design and development of software created to help small to mid-sized companies compete for top talent at about a 75% cost savings when compared to the use of traditional recruiting methods. This product will launch in September of 2009 and my goal is to see it as a effective and successful recruitment solution for customers all over the United States and Canada.
