John Smith’s Summary

As Senior Executive Coach, Protégé Coach, and Council Chair with 35+ years experience, I provide encouragement, mentoring, executive training, and resourcing through coaching, collaborating, and connecting like-minded faith-based senior executives with one another.

Today, CEO is an enterprising executive community of seasoned CEOs, Presidents, Principals, Senior Executives, and Entrepreneurs of profit/non-profit organizations with a minimum of 5+ years of senior leadership experience. They have a well-founded faith-based perspective. Our CEO Community is local, regional, national, and international in scope.

As we address complicated issues in our Council Retreat Sessions, we encourage one another to affirm our roles by demonstrating how faith, hope, and love can dwell at the center of our personal and professional lives even within our hectic lifestyles. This is our Compelling Call.

Our CEO Members have a sincere desire to grow personally, professionally, and spiritually based upon new opportunities, honest counsel, and practical application of Judeo-Christian priorities, attitudes, principles, values, qualities, and commitments by utilizing our proprietary Paradoxical Leader Model. We encourage you to explore our membership opportunities.

As a CEO Member, you can connect with faith-based senior executives locally, regionally, nationally, and internationally. We are adding new CEO Councils and new CEO Members quarterly. We encourage you to explore all our CEO Member opportunities. Please contact me at 407.923.2293 or www.facebook.com/johnsmithceo or johnsmith@ceo-360.com.
