Lorna Li’s Summary

I am a Social Networking Maven, Search Engine Marketing professional and Web 2.0 Evangelist. I am fascinated by the power of social technologies to build community and power online conversations, especially if the user generated content (UGC) is optimized for search engines a.k.a. Social SEO.

On the side, I am a Green networking maven and feel most at home with fellow activists, artists, and social entrepreneurs discussing ways we can collaborate to save the world. I am also frequently seen at Bay Area Web 2.0 technology events. I love meeting and connecting people who want to make an impact in the world through new technology and social innovation.

Above all, I believe in the power of intention and gratitude.
