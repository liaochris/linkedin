Paul Dunn’s Summary

Paul is a Certified Financial Planner (CFP) and Senior Associate of the Financial Services Institute of Australia. He has 15 years experience in financial services and has become a highly sought-after professional adviser providing high level strategic advice and guidance. His commitment to service and long-term relationships have differentiated him in an industry often focused on financial products rather than people.

Paul established Meridian Wealth Management in 2004 with the belief that with a combination of fee-for-service and the right advice anybody can take control of their financial destiny. He felt that it was time to educate and empower people to take control of their financial future and ensure they live a lifestyle that is commensurate with their years of work.

Paul Dunn’s Specialties:

Wealth Management & Accumulation, Executive Remuneration, Tax Optimisation, SMSF, Superannuation and Risk Management strategies for executives, high net worth, families and businesses
