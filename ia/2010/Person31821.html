Candice Jackson’s Summary

Currently located in New York City, I have over 15 years experience in hair and makeup. I am licensed in NY, CT & NC. You have seen my work in music videos and film, on magazine covers, the runway, in fashion shoots, on makeover shows, and a few celebrities. I travel quite often for models, magazines and personal clients. I offer hair, makeup and consultation services and I always welcome any questions you may have!

In the past I have been a guest writer or beauty expert for publications such as BeautyAddict.com, Grand Manner Magazine, American Baby, Demographics Magazine and Babbalu.com. Recently I began writing as a Beauty Examiner on Examiner.com here in NYC.

visit: www.examiner.com/x-34933-Chelsea-Beauty-Examiner

I have been a business manager for companies like MAC Cosmeticsand Cutting Crew, a Merchandise Coordinator for Neiman Marcus Group, and an educational instructor for The Academy for Hairstylists and Hair Technology Institute, and a Technical Trainer for Hair Club. I am currently an Academic Manager for the Empire Beauty School in NYC, I am represented by the Johnston Agency and on occasion I still freelance for MAC Cosmetics.

I have also coordinated several industry networking events as well as fundraising events for the MAC AIDS Fund, AIDS Project New Haven and the American Red Cross.

I majored in Business and Finance at the College of Westchester and plan to combine all my education and hands-on experience to continue my success in the beauty industry.
