Jamie Stern’s Summary

Providing an online resource for people suffering with Food Allergies, Food Intolerance issues and other special dietary needs. The website provides products that are Gluten free, Wheat free, Dairy free, Egg free, Soy free, Peanut free, Tree nut free, Kosher, Organic, Vegan, Vegetarian, Yeast free, Salt free and Low Cholesterol. The intuitive search feature also allows for finding multiple food allergies. Allergy free recipes and links to support groups are also provided.
