Mark Landay’s Summary

Through an executive search focus, I am a strategic executive with a proven track record of guiding organizations to success. I have been of counsel to Fortune 500 companies, technology start-ups, and to both medium and large-size organizations. I identify growth opportunities, develop strategies for capturing these opportunities, lead in the subsequent business plan implementation and provide executive search services.

Working both independently and collaboratively in the on-going management of projects, I actively interface with executives and board members in addition to building and maintaining positive relationships inside and outside of organizations.

I am a retained recruiter of CEO's and VP's for venture capital-backed startups and growth companies.

Mark Landay’s Specialties:

My expertise includes executive level search and recruitment. I have completed countless searches for venture-capitalized, pre-IPO start-ups to world-leading Fortune 50 companies. Approximately 33% of the companies I have consulted to have gone IPO or been acquired by public companies creating a liquidity opportunity for investors. I am considered a top headhunter in America.
