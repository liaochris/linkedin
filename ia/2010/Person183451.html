Sav Banerjee’s Summary

I am an advertising and digital media marketing professional in New York. I co-founded Vercai Inc with Adam Leining in 2005. I have experience in creating marketing strategies for digital platforms, focusing primarily on social networks. My passion is the world wide web & social media/web 2.0 channels, and I constantly work on improving user interaction with brands over the internet. I use valuable customer insight research and follow in-depth analysis of emerging social & cultural trends. I also enjoy my client facing role, effectively communicating the client's needs to the internal creative and production teams - and following up on projects from concept to execution phases.

My other interests include user interface design to enhance user experience on interactive modules and account planning & strategy development. I also do a lot of number crunching and reporting modules to measure effectiveness and impact of campaigns.

I also recently completed a graduate program in database and digital marketing.
