Brian Thompson’s Summary

I have a mix of communication skills and interests as I'm a former journalist, still write a weekly humor column in a daily paper, run a media and marketing office for a liberal arts college, edit an alumni magazine, advise a college newspaper and do some freelance writing on the side. I'm also finishing my master's in media management through the University of Missouri's journalism school, and am president of the Florida College Press Association, which I founded. I'm looking to do more freelance writing, particularly for parenting magazines, as well as syndicate my humor column.

Brian Thompson’s Specialties:

Writing, editing, producing magazines, humor columns and essays, short stories.
