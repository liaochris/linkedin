Diane Schmidt’s Summary

We are a professional talent management firm committed to delivering the highest quality and most personalized consultation to organizations and individuals facing transition and growth situations.

Career Partners International KC is one of the nation's most progressive talent management firms. Our innovative strategies and tactics promote and foster the ongoing success of organizations, and advance the careers of today's top talent. Recognized for its unprecedented success with individuals seeking professional, managerial, and technological positions in today's highly competitive job market, our dynamic approach to career management makes it faster, easier, and less confusing. We are a co-owner of Career Partners International, LLC(CPI). www.cpiworld.com

CPI was formed in 1987 as a partnership of seven of North America’s premier local talent management firms, and has since grown to include over 62 partner firms with 200 offices in 45 countries. CPI partners are locally headquartered firms with international resources. We are highly selective and screen each potential partner to ensure that they’re the best human resources consultants in their local market.

Privately owned, our unique business model allows us to customize flexible solutions for our clients. Unlike our publicly-traded competitors, we will never think that Wall Street’s expectations are more important than your needs. We take the time to understand and appreciate the difficult business issues you face, and our capable consultants help you resolve them in ways that enhance your reputation as an employer and protect the integrity of your brand.

Diane Schmidt’s Specialties:

If you're seeking to maximize talent and unleash the power of your team, we can help. We take a holistic approach to managing human capital throughout the career cycle: attracting, retaining, developing and transitioning your most important asset.
