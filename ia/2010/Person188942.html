Rebekkah Hilgraves’s Summary

A highly experienced technical trainer, technical support specialist and speaker, I am a subject matter expert in Content Management Systems, as well as content delivery and management, document versioning and workflow, taxonomy, and other related web and marketing strategy subspecialties. In the past three years, I have installed, customized and launched over 100 web sites, nearly all driven on CMS platforms (Interwoven, Joomla!, WordPress, Ektron and other solutions).

Currently, I am on extended contract in the Web Operations group for OppenheimerFunds in New York City, brought aboard to help with a major web site redesign project, and continue to assist with both daily production and larger strategic efforts.

I also run a web design and development agency, SheTech and Company, building custom CMS sites for business clients around the country. As such, I have a strong understanding of and familiarity with several CMS varieties, as well as audience/user group segmentation, SEO, web analytics, design and UX best practices (which, as we know, shift on an hourly basis) and other strategic web design and delivery processes. My team is distributed across the country, and our development efforts are always collaborative in nature.

As a Managing Editor for NetPerformance.com, an online news magazine published by NetQoS, I supervised and project-managed the migration of the site from a legacy open-source content management system to a .NET-based commercial system. Once the migration was complete, I maintained the site and the content, updating the magazine with new content from industry analysts and technical experts on a weekly basis. I have additional experience in SharePoint administration.
