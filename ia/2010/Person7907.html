Allan Woolwine’s Summary

allanwoolwine!@gmail.com

An Art Director, Graphic Designer with solid agency experience.

Provide conceptually strong sound solutions to a large range of clients.

Created programs for both large and small clients in the financial, high tech, food service, consumer goods, beverage, automotive, insurance, electronics, communications, manufacturing and health care industries.

My work has been published and has received numerous awards from leading organizations for it's creativity and recognized by clients for its effectiveness and ROI.

Skilled in working across all mediums including online, broadcast, print, direct mail, point of sale, collateral and corporate identity.

Experience as an artist, art director and graphic designer gives me an edge when combined with an advanced working knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Flash, InDesign, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Quark Express and Microsoft Office.

Ability to translate complex business processes into visually appealing designs.

Staffed and trained a group of artists to work in the Digital Imaging Department and successfully managed that department as a profit center where we increased profits 900% over an 8 year period.

Excellent communicator with demonstrated ability to deliver results.
