Lenya King’s Summary

I recently worked as a brand design intern at Design Continuum, an award-winning international consulting agency. My experience at Continuum has both strengthened and refined my ability to think critically and creatively in close collaboration with others to achieve a common goal.

While pursuing a career in urban education, I was introduced to the creative use of professional graphic design to bring attention to important issues concerning social justice. As a result, I successfully pursued a BFA in Graphic Design from the Massachusetts College of Art and Design.

My primary strengths are within print and interaction design. I have been told that I have a strong ability to create, develop, refine and execute visual concepts while working in partnership with my fellow colleagues. I am a hard working, highly focused problem solver with a strong attention to detail. I look forward to collaborating with others to achieve project goals in visual communication and design.

Lenya King’s Specialties:

Brand Design and Strategy, Copy Editing, Packaging, Bookbinding and Book Arts, Photography, Video Editing and Production.
