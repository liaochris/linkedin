Adam Murphy’s Summary

I'm currently an entertainment writer and freelance artist development rep.

My working experience maintains two threads: client-relationship building and creative writing. In these two areas I excel the most, and I continue to look for opportunities to utilize one or both of these qualities. I worked for three years as a marketing rep. for Universal Records, servicing retailers, building relationships with our clients, and understanding the 18-25 demographic to provide Universal Music Group with invaluable insight in the challenges that come with stimulating sales in the music industry. I compiled weekly reports of personal insight and strategy, focused on building a stronger connection between the label and consumer.

I've also had experience writing for the online news and lifestyle publication KcConfidential.com, where I currently contribute as the magazine's Book Editor and as a paid music contributor.

Recently, I worked as the manager, sales leader, and assistant buyer at a boutique clothing store called Hobbs, where I led a humble team of 10 employees to reach daily, weekly, and monthly sales goals.

I've also spent my free time volunteering at the Douglas County Jail as a Creative Writing Instructor.
