Sven van Buuren Interior Issues’s Summary

As an Interior Architect I shape sustainable rooms by placing walls, floors and ceilings, directing daylight and artificial light. Together with the right materials, decoration and furniture, these rooms become warm, intimate but modern interiors, making the user feel at ease. I have over 10 years of experience in designing top-quality interiors, mainly residential and hospitality.

I can help you with renovating your house or designing a completely new interior for a new house. I can also help you out by redesigning a single room, furniture design or other interior plans.

With a team of creatives we are are planning to start designing holiday homes in France, Italy and Spain for the well-off retired. The main idea is to buy abandoned medieval villages in beautiful, easy to reach areas nearby larger cities. The villages become a mix of modern and ancient architecture.
