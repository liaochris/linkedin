Bryan O'Rourke, MBA’s Summary

Helping organizations and leaders become more successful by devising unique strategies that adopt cutting edge tools based on new technology is what I love. My present focus is on leading wellness, fitness, franchise and organizations adopting enterprise 2.0 disciplines. However, my organization's practices extend to a wide variety of for profit and non-profit organizations in an array of industries including government agencies and NGO's.

In addition to contract executive and consultancy work, I deliver engaging customized keynotes and researched half to multiple-day strategic working sessions for associations or organizations.

As a principal and senior executive with fitmarc, a leading supplier of innovative fitness and wellness solutions, Integerus, a technology and strategic wellness consultancy firm and the Flywheel Group, which develops and delivers cloud technology solutions, my team has extensive bandwith to serve the unique needs of any size organization.

Bryan O'Rourke, MBA’s Specialties:

Deep affiliate relationships shared to deliver expertise for small, mid-sized and large for profit and not for profit firms.
