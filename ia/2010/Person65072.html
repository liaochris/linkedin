Anthony Austin’s Experience

  • Executive Producer

    ELE Productions, LLC

    (Media Production industry)

    March 2010 — Present (5 months)

    Anthony's specialty is in handheld shooting in particularly in the NYC school system teaching students and teachers from middle and high school in incorporating video into the classroom curriculum.

    In 2001, he formed a company called CANN, "Caribbean American News Network" as the Director which acted as an international news bureau for Caribbean stations, providing them with North American news coverage. In 2003, he took on the Senior DP position for two shows "Que Pasa NY" and "What's the 411", both shows were successful in their own right, one covering nation events and in entertainment and the other in African Americans i the entertainment world. Seeing the benefits of both Anthony created with the help of partner and producer friend Gill of A&E to created "On the Scene" which combined coverage of both worlds.

    In 2004, Anthony joined CBS to work in their satellite distribution outlet in Connecticut where he helped coordinate feeds for Speedvision, History Channel, NFL.com, NBA.com and A&E respectively. At this time he joined forces with the show "Entre NY y Quisqueya" as the international field producer which was seen throughout NY, Boston, Chicago, Miami and the Dominican Republic in Spanish, this was the introduction of "En La Escena" fully into the Hispanic world. In addition, Anthony is part of the NYCTV Emmy award winning production team.
