Chris & Susan Beesley (LION)’s Summary

Chris & I are an entrepreneurial partnership - we have a management consultancy where we provide business development to SME's.

We also have a luxury ski chalet in the French Alps. This is let out to a ski holiday company during the winter and the remainder of the year the chalet is available to like minded people or groups who want to spend time together either socially or for corporate personal development or team building.

Our passion is in working with people to help them to achieve their dreams and goals and ultimate financial freedom. We specialise in businesses using the latest techologies with growth through the internet.

Our goal is to be financially free outselves and be able to give our time freely to help others to achieve and also to spend time working in India where we currently sponsor a child through Action Aid (our 4th child) His name is Kundan Kumar and he is 4 years old.
