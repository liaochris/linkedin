Michael H. Lewis, Jr.’s Summary

Michael Lewis has worked in the financial industry for seven years during which time he has driven sales growth, increased market penetration, customer satisfaction, brand awareness, process improvement and fostered internal partnerships. He was a key contributor to double-digit revenue growth when he was a valued team member of Citigroup's hedge fund and investment management administration business. In previous roles, Michael brought valuable insight, knowledge and tangible results to organizations seeking to launch new products or penetrate existing markets. His firm understanding of finance, from both macro and micro vantage points, compliments his team oriented professional sales approach.

In his most recent role, Michael added $4.1 million of annualized revenue to his business unit and approximately $1 million in cross-sold revenue for the enterprise in 2008. He was tasked with penetrating the United States investment management fund administration services business for Citigroup Security and Fund Services. His success at Citigroup can also be partially attributed to technological savvy, an understanding of how systems relate to each other and the ability to employ technology effectively. While at Citigroup, he effectively led colleagues, delivered results, and managed processes throughout the enterprise while maintaining an entrepreneurial approach.

Michael received his Masters of Business administration from the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan. He also honed his technical skills by engaging in other activities that would further enrich his education. Michael consulted for a non-profit Israeli incubator foundation that helped Israeli entrepreneurs conduct business feasibility studies and market assessment testing.

Prior to attending University, Michael was Field Artillery Soldier of the United States Army First Armor Division where he served from 1992 until 1994 and was honorably discharged from service.
