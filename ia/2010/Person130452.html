Jeff Mallan, CPA, CFF, CFE’s Summary

Jeffrey A. Mallan has more than twenty years of experience in the fields of accounting and auditing which includes seven years within the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD). In his last DoD position, Jeff contributed to the development of a fraud detection system and assisted investigators in uncovering unauthorized transactions using computer data-mining techniques.

Prior to forming Mallan & Associates, Jeff served as a litigation consulting manager with a well known accounting firm that primarily focuses on litigation and forensic consulting services. He also served as a manager in the business assurance practice of a large regional CPA firm on the West Coast. Jeff has extensive experience in financial statement audits and SOX 404 consulting engagements. He has worked on a variety of litigation and dispute-related accounting projects, including financial fraud/forensic examinations, lost-profit analyses, partner/shareholder disagreements, and economic damages assessments. Jeff’s work has involved entities in the areas of manufacturing, distributing, real estate, high-technology, life science, nonprofit and government.

He is a Certified Public Accountant, a CPA Certified in Financial Forensics, a Certified Fraud Examiner, a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and the California Society of Certified Public Accountants. Jeff is coordinator and founder of the North Bay Alliance of Certified Fraud Examiners (NBACFE). Jeff is also a member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners and an associate member of the Sonoma County Bar Association. He received his B.S. degree in Accounting from East Central University, Ada, Oklahoma.
