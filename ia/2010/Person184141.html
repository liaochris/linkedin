Scott L. Danielson’s Summary

Scott Danielson is a strategist, designer and builder who has created highly successful products and services worldwide.

His professional experience includes Fortune 25 executive, NYC agency executive producer and creative partner, user-experience design pioneer, early-stage Internet-entrepreneur, and consumer products designer. More specifically, Scott has led C-suite initiatives for IBM, Prodigy, AOL, ESPN, NBC, HBO, QVC, Microsoft, AARP, and UnitedHealth Group.

Scott’s knack for transforming obstacles into solutions, concepts into reality, and investments into returns has earned him wide recognition including more than 100 professional awards.

Danielson received his MS in Telecommunications from Syracuse University and his BA from the University of Minnesota. He has also studied in the Executive MBA program at Wharton, the Film Studies program at NYU, the French Intensives program at the Sorbonne, and attended Atheneum 5 in the Netherlands.

His lifelong love is growing, preparing and sharing food--an odyssey that included summers spent in the bio-dynamic gardens of southern France and Holland, stints in the vineyards of Epernay and Langedoc, and countless hours at service in commercial kitchens and family gatherings. A recent pilgrimage to el Bulli and meeting with chef Adria tops his food adventures.

Scott L. Danielson’s Specialties:

Connected digital technology; go-to-market business strategy and planning; new market and consumer product development, information and interactive design; complex project planning and implementation; team development and executive leadership.
