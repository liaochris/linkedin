Jeff Swartz’s Summary

I have been a designer and web professional for over fifteen years, working many roles around front-end web design and development. I currently hold the position of Director of Creative Services for Level Five, an interactive consultancy and software development firm in Kansas City.

Starting my career with aims to pursue traditional illustration, I quickly discovered technology with all the challenges and rewards it had to offer. This led me to also pursue a graphic design track, during which time I taught myself HTML and Javascript when most schools had not yet started offering classes on the subject.

My experiences have allowed me the perspective of designer, developer and strategist. Succeeding in the interactive space requires one to constantly evolve or get left behind. A single hat never really seems to fit me and I think thats a good thing.
