Tom McGhee’s Summary

I have been shooting since 93' originally based out of Sydney Australia whereby I ran a successful studio overlooking the beautiful Sydney Harbor. In 97' I relocated to London where I worked with some amazing people until 2003 when I had the opportunity to move to NYC after 2 successful exhibitions in DC and LA. In 05 my partner and I opened the doors of Tomsuey Inc offering full production,design and photography to the world.

Tomsuey Inc works for a range of clients from international Ad agencies to smaller independent design studios and direct clients.

We continue to explore new avenues for design and imagery.

Recently I have also taken on the role of DP on several projects shooting HD along with other formats. Although Still images to me are still the key to capturing a Moment.

Please visit my website to find out a little more of my work.
cheers
Tom

Tom McGhee’s Specialties:

We offer full production services along with the latest in digital capture technologies.
My shoots focus on people and location and the relation between the two.

I have also been commissioned on several aerial shoots for clients. No fear of heights here ;)
