Christopher Fox’s Summary

I am an experienced inventor, technical author and embedded systems designer, architect and engineer with many years in a wide variety of disciplines, many successful products completed and more than two dozen patents awarded. This includes integrated circuit design, cryptography and cryptographic module design, asset and people tracking, factory floor automation, physical security and access control systems, fuel management and measuring systems, global networked systems, system integration, wireless radio communications, audio recording, acoustic design, and commercial television and radio production.

Christopher Fox’s Specialties:

Innovation, solutions, hardware and software architecture and design, cryptography and cryptographic systems, embedded firmware, software, acoustics and facility design, embedded wireless systems, mechanical design. Decades of real-world experience in embedded systems, laser systems and power supplies, television and radio production and post-production, access control and security and fuel & fleet management.
