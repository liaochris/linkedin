Dave Beson’s Summary

New focus for 2010: FOCUS and INTENTIONS. I'll be showing $100,000 earners (and aspiring $100,000 earners) how to keep the wheels on and get a raise in 2010 by FOCUSING THEIR EFFORT.

We'll be teaching COW and HORSE techniques. Cow relates to being a grinder. Getting the daily work-plan done and getting the necessary PROSPECTING AND LEAD GENERATION TO GET APPOINTMENTS TO LIST AND SELL. Horse relates to thoroughbred techniques of Internet, Social Networking, and e-Smart tools to sell better and profit faster!
We'll need both in 2010.

Speaking engagements have taken me over 5 million miles, presenting more than 3,000 seminars all over the world. By coaching, training, and consulting with hundreds of top real estate associates during the past three decades, I have learned from the successes and failures and condensed that learning into ONE simple strategy to succeed.

Since entering the real estate business in 1974, I have evolved from top salesperson to training director to manager, speaker, author and LIFE SUCCESS COACH. More recently I have been sought after as a leading industry expert by REALTORS®, broker/owners and corporations for webinars, videos, and live training. Students are drawn to insights, humor, and wisdom from a 34 year career in real estate -can you believe it! When they see it...they believe it!
