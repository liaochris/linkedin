Dan Williams’s Experience

  • Founder, The Networking Community, LLC

    The Networking Community

    (Partnership; Broadcast Media industry)

    June 2003 — Present (7 years 4 months)

    Dan Williams is the visionary and leader for advancing networking with the purpose of enabling companies and individuals to more effectively meet the marketing challenges of the 21st century.

    Through recognition on LinkedIn, he is the highest endorsed individual in the USA and is the acknowledged thought-leader for networking in the United States. In founding The Networking Community, Williams has made his networking know-how available to all; through his audio CD and published book; "Tales from The Networking Community", his radio shows interviews, networking educational seminars, and speaking engagements.

    His success as a technology and telecommunications sales executive began in the late - 1970s and grew dramatically. Williams developed business for such companies as; Tymnet, British Telecom, Primus Telecom and more. When the tech bubble burst, like many, he went from being worth millions on paper to being unemployed and without a viable network overnight. From this experience, a company was launched.

    That network now includes more than 13 million individuals, who have become part of The Networking Community on LinkedIn, and those who have read about Williams’s efforts in a growing number of publications.

    In a world where it’s both what and who one knows that provide the business edge, becoming a member of The Networking Community and getting acquainted with their founder Dan Williams should be a top priority for anyone serious about networking.
