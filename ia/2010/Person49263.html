Norbert Kremer’s Summary

Senior business systems analyst in health care, biomedical research, and pharmaceutical research sectors.

Business Systems Analyst with strong database skills works in all phases of development lifecycle. Elucidates and documents business requirements for data integration (batch ETL and real-time), reporting and analytics. Writes functional specifications and test plans using data flow diagrams and use cases. Prepares logical data models to interact with developers and business users or clients. Designs databases and refactors database schema with modeling tools during application development. Tracks data lineage with metadata; performs map and gap analysis and interface design in data warehouse environments. Develops and debugs data layer code and generates reports based on complex queries. Skilled communicator, presenter and facilitator.
