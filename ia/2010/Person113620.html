Karen Keller, Ph.D.’s Summary

My name is Karen Keller, Ph.D. And for over two decades, first as a psychologist in private practice and then as an executive coach, I’ve been helping men get ahead in the world using one secret weapon: Influence

Using my guidance, dozens of executives and business leaders have transformed their careers. I’ve also consulted on million dollar deals in the boardroom to rewriting state policies in the legislature…all partnered with men.

I've taken my core principals previously targeted for men – the art of leadership, persuasion, influence, intuition, success – and developed a program specifically geared towards women.

With my help you won’t just learn it … you’ll live it. And success will follow in most every aspect of your life: personally and professionally. I guarantee it!

Karen Keller, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

My specialty is mastery. The mastery of advanced leadership building, expertise in establishing influence, communication, relationship enhancement and how to take your company to the top! I am a partner to leaders who are serious about making a difference in the business world by way of integrity, compassion and prosperity. Why Mastery? Because I believe in being the best in everything you do!
