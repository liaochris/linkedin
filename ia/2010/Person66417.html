Eric Shippam’s Summary

Eric Shippam is a versatile, accomplished professional with more than 20 years in leadership in Telecommunications and IT projects both as an executive and as a consultant. Shippam has worked in Fortune 50 companies and start-ups, leading hundreds, or working alone, always effectively.
After 8 years with the Sprint Corporation Finance Division where he was in Revenue Management, Audit, Credit and Collection, and a Sprint partner liaison, he resigned his position as CFO of Sprint’s Asian Group, to act as the Founder and Managing Director of Communications / Advantage LLC and Web-Breeze Networks, LLC in Sutter Creek, California.
In those positions, he was the decision-maker for all technology efforts and issues including the construction a 44 tower, 350 square mile wireless broadband network serving rural Amador County, California serving 20 communities. His companies also provided telecommunications, web site hosting, email services, and dial up Internet services, nationwide. Mr. Shippam has participated in all aspects of the construction, built a retail dealership channel, and negotiated tower deals with companies and communities throughout the region. His was the first company to do so.
More recently, he led a consortium of ISP companies in five counties to provide a high speed wireless data network serving 33,000 households. He led the team preparing, all elements of the grant including business plan, capital budgets and forecasting, engineering, construction milestones and regulatory communications. Once submitted, the project won approval for a $6 million grant from the State of California.
A second business plan was developed to build a seven county network in the Central Valley Broadband, LLC., which also won approval.
Mr. Shippam is a graduate of Anderson College, and the Sprint Corporation’s and Harvard Business School, Financial Management Development Program.
He lives with his son, Pierce in Sitter Creek, California.
