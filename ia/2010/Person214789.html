Carol Werner’s Summary

Corporate attorney with 30+ years of practice experience, including postings in Washington D.C., Hong Kong, Tokyo, New York and Denver with major law firms and six years as general counsel of a major hotel management group headquartered in Denver, Colorado. Practice areas include general corporate services, financing and securities transactions, business contracts of all kinds and Colorado water law. My firm's objective is to provide clients high-quality legal services at an extremely favorable hourly rate.
