Margaret Lynch’s Summary

Margaret Lynch is a Success Coach, Emotional Freedom Technique Expert, and author of the “Secret of Intentional Wealth”. Her passion and gift is helping success minded people break through their limits to attain higher levels of wealth, health and passion in life.

Margaret is widely recognized as one of the top experts in her field and was recently featured expert in the "Tapping World Summit" where she presented to over 47,000 people. She is the business and money expert featured on the acclaimed "Tapping Insiders Club" subscription site.

Margaret’s specialty is getting to the core of what blocks our success – the limiting beliefs and old programming that hold us back in our careers, businesses and finances. She teaches her clients how to tap into their inner brilliance for higher levels of creativity and inspiration, and even for finding higher purpose in life.

Margaret offers individual coaching as well as workshops in:
• Secret of Intentional Wealth for Entrepreneurs
• Law of Attraction in business
• Sales Force Motivation
• Persuasion and Influence
• Sales and Marketing for the Self Employed

Margaret draws on her 18+ years in management and executive sales positions in top fortune 500 companies. She recently joined the advisory board for Aspire Magazine as Sales Director. She earned a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Massachusetts, multiple certifications in Hypnosis and the Emotional Freedom Technique. She is a member of the National Guild of Hypnotists (NGH), the American Assoc for Comprehensive Energy Psychology (AACEP) and Business Networking International (BNI). References and industry resume available upon request.

Margaret Lynch’s Specialties:

Entrenprenuerial and executive leadership coaching. Eliminating public speaking anxiety, Persuasion and Influence , Advanced Sales Training for sales professionals and business owners.
