John Sim’s Summary

John currently runs his own company Digaset. Working on improving and developing Enterprise Content Management and Digatal Asset Management systems.

With eight years of experience in graphical user interfaces, web, application and database development. His primarily focus is with Digaset, where he hopes to develop leading technology and solutions for content manipulation and digital storage.
