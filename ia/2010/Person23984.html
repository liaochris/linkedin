Bill Pieper’s Summary

Bill Pieper has over two decades of experience in providing Business Process Outsourcing Solutions to companies of all sizes. The last twelve years have been in the Customer Contact Industry, which allows Bill to bring significant experience in managing a client’s customer contact initiatives and processes.

Bill’s career began with the pioneer in Business Process Outsourcing, Automatic Data Processing (ADP). Bill’s responsibilities included managing relationships with ADP’s largest clients through the company’s network of inbound customer service contact centers.

In 1996, Bill transitioned to the contact center industry as EVP of Sales and Marketing for ITI Marketing Services, now APAC Customer Services, one of the industry’s leaders. He went on to hold executive level positions with three other industry leaders including SITEL, First Data and TeleSpectrum Worldwide, now TRG Customer Solutions, prior to founding ICCS.

His experience on the agency side of the industry, as well as on the client side of the industry, has given Bill a clear understanding of the dynamics of a successful customer contact program.
