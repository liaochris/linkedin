    We specialize in location production work, audio, video & still photography focusing on medical, pharma industry, health care, & public health communications.

We continue to provide a unique level of experience that allows us to provide the highest level of production quality while remaining light and fast on location. We work well around patients and world class health care provider professionals, researchers and scientists.

    Reasons why we work for you:

    COST: We commonly charge less then other production companies because we work with smaller cross-trained crews. We also utlize simple licensing models that make your ability to use our creative work hassle free.

    CONTROL: Too often the solution is using stock photography. Stock seems to be the way to go but in the end most clients realize that for the same time invested in image research and for the same licensing cost, we can shoot original work that will support the brand and be perfect for their needs.

    NATURAL and REAL photography always beats the "generic" look of stock - but considering our over 20 years of shooting Doctors, Nurses, Patients and Scientists, we know what allows them to relax and be natural and real in front of our cameras.

    STORYTELLING OVER TIME: We excel at documenting over time - telling the ongoing story of what you do and how you do it. Not only are we experienced with developing the visual icon and look that will let your brand stand out, we also believe that the story has to be maintained - updated continually which means we prefer relationships with our clients that last.

    SOCIAL - we are firm practitioners of new media social media and tailor our work when requested to fully exploit the benefits of utilizing rich media, video and audio to help tell the story.
