Sharon Callahan’s Summary

Sharon Callahan never sits still. For more than 20 years, she has delved into all aspects of strategic pharmaceutical marketing, and gained sharp skills in numerous disciplines including: sales and management of publishing, medical education, clinical programs, advertising, and interactive programs. Sharon helps clients leverage the value of their messages through the seamless integration of multiple communication channels, making sure that every message is on point at every contact point. From performing pro bono work to managing global programs for blue chip pharmaceutical clients, Sharon uses her boundless energy to promote good communications and maximize sales.
