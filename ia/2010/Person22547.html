Missions
Editions Profil designs and publishes softwares and drives them through a strong distribution network. In addition to its development and publishing abilities, Editions Profil builds close partnerships with French and foreign publishers in order to localize and market softwares.

Engineering
Editions Profil supplies high quality featured softwares with relevant content and user friendly interface. This quality standard is sustained by a dedicated developer team and up to date technological awareness. Furthermore, Editions Profil relies on the knowledge and the expertise of its partners as well as on their experience with content development and artistic creation.

Publishing
Editions Profil publishes its self built products, author materials, and republishes softwares developed worldwide. Before deciding to publish a new title Editions Profil ensures that it will match the market. Based on a long experience and know-how abilities, Editions Profil’s capacity of software localisation is not only a work of translation but also a real content adaptation.

Distribution
Editions Profil is also a software distributor and chooses its partner’s products as long as they fit to its range on a complementary basis. Editions Profil also carefully selects its partners with whom it builds a strong and specific partnership. Editions Profil takes advantage of a large distribution network composed of numerous outlets and runs business directly with the major actors of the software market. This distribution scheme is completed by specialised wholesalers.
