Matt Earp’s Summary

I am an artistically passionate individual with a wide range of experiences across the musical, film, teaching, technology and writing professions.

My overall interest is the changing paradigm of creativity that surrounds the consumption of digital media, especially music, and the social groups that coalesce around the production of that media. I have gained a broad range of experiences with the artistic, technical, organizational, social and legal issues that have affected digital media in the last decade.

My specific experiences include:
• Being a cutting edge DJ for 12 years, watching both music and music transmission methods change from a user's and creator's perspective.
• Being an event promoter and curator for almost as long, working to incorporate my background in theater, my ear for music, and my ability to understand visual space into exciting and unique experiences for attendees.
• Writing as a blogger and music journalist for 9 years, using my language skills to convey my enthusiasm and excitement for new music, media, and art, through a variety of outlets.
• Using my skill with people to create theater, teaching students about the issues artists and musicians face in a digital world, assisting in the creation of video and film, and giving well-received presentations on a wide range of topics connected to these endeavors.

I currently am the full time Information Director at the small upstart and kick-ass law firm of Durie Tangri, LLP. I handle the firm's internal information flow by managing their sharepoint site, their internal wiki, and whatever other information jobs might be needed in a pinch.
