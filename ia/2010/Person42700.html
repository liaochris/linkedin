Leslie Moore’s Summary

As an Interior Design Consultant I specialize in architectural color consulting, redesign, space planning and designs for custom cabinetry and storage solutions to increase organization and function. I offer my clients affordable ways to transform their interiors.

One to two hour makeovers to complete remodel projects are in my expertise for commercial and residential design projects.

Branding with color is another area I specialize in. I will help you create the feeling, mood and image you want for your home or business with color.

Leslie Moore’s Specialties:

Color consulting, space planning, floorplan layout, functional living and work spaces, commerical and residential design, branding with color.
