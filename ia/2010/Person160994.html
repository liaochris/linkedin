Paul Mitchell’s Summary

CMS specialist with ten years experience selecting, implenting and integrating enterprise content management systems in enterprise ecommerce infrastructures.

Selected and deployed content management systems for six Fortune 100 companies.

Proven history leading teams of tech leads, developers and project managers on successful integration projects and interfacing with client technology and management teams.

Paul Mitchell’s Specialties:

The design and implementation of multichannel, user-centric systems supporting localization, search, CRM and ERP technologies and multivariant testing and dynamic content personalization. Core competencies are information architecture, business and systems analysis, and enterprise systems vendor selection.

Expert in Agile and UML .
