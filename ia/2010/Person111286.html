Karuana Gatimu’s Summary

Karuana Gatimu a technologist & visual producer who specializes in enterprise application design & deployment, business analysis and process improvement. Since 1990 she has spent her time in the technology industry in technical, project and executive management roles. Karuana has been responsible for increasing top line revenue, decreasing operational expenditures and increasing the recognition of every brand she's been associated with.

A strong belief that technology is meant to aid people in making better business decisions has lead her to become an enterprise level solution architect. Throughout her career she has been able to bridge the worlds between technology and visual production leading creative teams in web, video, live event and print production.

Her motto: Enthusiasm is a Management Principle

When's she not spreading that enthusiasm at her current position she writes a blog on SharePoint, is involved in the non-profit that nationally promotes SharePoint User Groups and has a practice as a spiritual life strategist.
