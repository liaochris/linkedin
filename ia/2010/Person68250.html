Pam Larson’s Summary

Have you ever noticed how frustrated Moms get when they are at work and they get that call from the daycare? Something has gone wrong. Sometimes they have to leave work and go pick up their children because of illness. But, other times there are questionable circumstances. Do you have any idea what really goes on in your child's daycare environment? Do you really know if the babysitter is feeding your child what you told her to? Have you ever seen how the childcare provider interacts with your child? Most of the time parents barely make it to the daycare center before they close the doors for the day. It's not easy to have a conversation with the teacher or director to ask specific questions, there is just no time. Observing your child personally is impossible, because you are at work.
My job is to be your eyes, ears, and your voice. Make a list of those questions you've been needing to ask. I'll get the answers. I can go to that childcare setting and observe for you. I can even stop by your house and check on the babysitter for you.
Parents have a right and an obligation to know what goes in their child's life, every minute of every day. It's just tough to get that communication flowing. You need help. That's what I do. I do it in a friendly and professional manner, so that you only benefit from my involvement.
I can also provide you with information on other childcare facilities and links to information that you might need.
I am not affiliated with any organisations, government or otherwise. My information is strictly based on personal observation and research.
