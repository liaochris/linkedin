Dirk Karl’s Summary

A successful and perceptive procurement leader with an international career spanning 15 years and a proven track record in advisory services, outsourcing, vendor management and banking. Able to anticipate and qualify the need for change, set the appropriate directions and build the drive for implementation. Has strong business acumen, building the bridges between the lines of business and procurement with a focus on the global enterprise bargaining power to deliver the cost benefits in a timely manner. Develops, influences, and drives procurement plans at a strategic, global level and manages senior executive stakeholder relationships.

Dirk Karl’s Specialties:

Demonstrates strong people management and organisational skills through managing global sourcing activities, multi vendor outsourcing contracts, transition and transformation projects, and strategic supplier management programmes.
