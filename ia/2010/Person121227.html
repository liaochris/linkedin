Larry Hall’s Summary

I've been marketing online since 1999. After traveling down lots of different internet paths, many of which were dead ends and others barely productive, I came to learn the do's and the don'ts, the things that actually work versus the propaganda, to running a very successful internet marketing company and having the internet marketer dream of having the option to work from home or travel abroad and take care of my family and staff as well.

Some of my specialties involve setting up and running online marketing campaigns using Google Adwords, Yahoo Search Marketing and MSN AdCenter. There are proper ways to make these online marketing systems work and, when you have them working, they can be very profitable for you and your online business.

In addition, for over 10 years, I've trained new, intermediate and advanced online marketers in the subject of internet marketing.

I've also been involved in the fast expanding area of Virtual Real Estate where I've created, bought and sold many internet businesses using online techniques.

I've always been very hands on. This has enabled me to become proficient in everything from website creation and script installation, online research and search engine optimization through social media techniques and factors dealing with search engine marketing (which by the way is a much larger field then most think).

And being as hands on as I've been has lead to my company being one of the most successful online marketing consulting companies around.

Personally, I believe you should have a wide understanding of these subjects, whether you will be doing the work yourself or managing from a distance, knowing many facets will help you to succeed in many internet activities.
