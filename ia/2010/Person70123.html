Keith Schultz’s Summary

I’m a highly accomplished and dependable Senior Electrical Engineer with a BSEE degree with more than 25 years of product development experience at a tier 1 automotive and health care manufacture. My background includes 11 years electronic hardware design, 3 years program management and 15+ years of Vehicle Systems Integration Engineering experience using embedded microcomputers products. In addition I have performed system engineering at a major hospital and health care supplier.

As a system engineer I have extensive experience in client interfacing, gathering customer requirements and needs, conversion of customer requirements into internal specifications, defining system operation/performance, generation of validation and verification plans used by both internal groups to performed bench and field verification, vehicle system calibration, problem-solving, decision-making generation of schedules, and developed risk management plans. Provide guidance to development team in the face of ambiguity and influence product design to insure desired results are achieved.

As program/project engineering manager I directed a team of 12 mechanical, electrical and system engineers. I was responsible for generation/ developed/ presentation of business proposals, preparing responses to RFQs, program planning, cost reduction initiatives, quality improvement proposals, gate reviews, product launch and insuring all aspects of the mechanical, electrical, and software design met all timing/ cost/ profit/ performance requirements. Provided team leadership and mentoring.

As electronic hardware designer I have experience with both analog and digital electrical designing, circuit board layout, EMI/RFI requirements and putting products into production.

I’m well organized, professional, excellent interpersonal skills, detail oriented with excellent written and oral skills, able to function effectively in high pressure environments. I’m the holder of five patents
