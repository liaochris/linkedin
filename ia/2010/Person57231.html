DonKokko "DK" Burnaby, HCS, CSBA’s Summary

DK Burnaby is an HCI certified Human Capital Strategist. He is an accomplished professional and consultant in the field of corporate talent strategy. Leveraging a background in technology and business management, DK offers rare expertise in bringing together technology and talent strategy to develop and streamline talent management and acquisition programs that enhance efficiency and productivity.

Additionally, DK is an entrepreneur in sustainable real estate investment and development, and a Certified Sustainable Building Adviser.
