Carol Gignoux’s Summary

I am passionate about addressing the needs of people with adhd. Having Attention Deficit Disorder myself and having worked for well over 30 years with adhd children, adhd teens, and in the last decade add adults, I offer alternative treatments for adhd beyond adhd medications and a diagnosis.

I have worked in corporate settings, clinical settings, and school settings to dispel the myths surrounding ADHD and teach others how to offer support and structure. I do public speaking every chance I can in order to get the truth out about this condition. I believe ADHD is a gift that includes a style of learning and operating that doesn't always work well in the world but can always be improved. Some symtoms and challenges can be eliminated for good. I've seen people with ADHD learn to live happy and productive lives given the right coaching and understanding.

If you or someone you know has ADHD, or you work in any capacity with ADHD children, adolescents or adults, reach out to us. We're there 24 hours a day. We can help!

Below are some topics, sevices and resources we deliver with sensitivity, patience and respect. We are looking for referrals to those who may not have heard about us and what we offer. We're hoping to spread the word to greater numbers of people in the hope that they will come to us for help. Our vision is to make it possible for all people with ADHD to bring their gift to the world without the constant issue of struggling and suffering over behavior management issues.
