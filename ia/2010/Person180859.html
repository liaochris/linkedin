Sam Angus’s Summary

I am a partner in the Corporate and Venture Capital Group at Fenwick & West. My practice focuses on advising start-up companies and entrepreneurs, in seed/venture capital financings, M&A/joint venture transactions, and intellectual property matters. I have over 15 years experience practicing law, prior to which I co-founded, grew and sold a multi-million dollar publishing company.

I represented In-System Design, Inc. in connection with its acquisition by Cypress Semiconductor Corporation, and Naxon Corporation (Wineshopper.com) on its acquisition of Wine.com, Inc., Micro Focus Group on its $500 million merger with Intersolv, Inc., Ingenio, Inc. on its acquisition by AT&T Yellow Pages., Junglee Corp. on its $300 million acquisition by Amazon.com, Inc., and Blue Lava Wireless on its $140 million acquisition by JAMDAT Mobile.
