Katrina Joy Plam’s Summary

Katrina Joy Plam is a creative writer and web marketing consultant living and working in Portland, Oregon. She is the founder and creative developer of MyWritingGroup.us, an interactive online writing workshop series. She studied at Sarah Lawrence College and received her MFA in Creative Writing from Antioch University.

Katrina’s short stories and essays have appeared in literary journals and magazines including Perceptions Literary Magazine and Out Magazine. She is the author and creator of the short story collection Grocery Love, musings of a grocery store connoisseur thinking loud thoughts.

A contributor to several online publications, she writes regularly about innovative web marketing technology. Katrina has several years experience developing multi-media campaigns and marketing materials for a diverse and eclectic range of industries. Currently, she creates custom online marketing, linkbuilding and social networking campaigns for personal and business branding.

To learn more about Katrina Joy Plam visit KJPcreations.com.

Hard-core user of social networks with experience marketing on major social nets via widgets, interactive content, and new media applications. Deep interest in utilizing entertainment and media technology for brand building and to garner an online audience.
