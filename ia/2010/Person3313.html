Adrian Dayton Esq.’s Summary

After graduating from Brigham Young University I started my own import company, Trig Imports. We started by importing products from Thailand for the scrapbook industry, and somehow ended up bringing in high end construction materials from China. After a few years of the uncertainties of the import business, I decided to return to school to obtain a law degree. During law school I continued to operate my import business, but the primary focus was on school.

My first position out of law school was with Jaeckle Fleishmann & Mugel where I worked in the corporate department. Soon after I left to start my own company specializing in social media strategy and online marketing for attorneys and other professionals.

Adrian Dayton Esq.’s Specialties:

Imports, exports, Twitter, blogs, marketing, mergers and acquisitions, and international law.
