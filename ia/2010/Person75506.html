Gina Hayden’s Summary

I used to do any kind of facilitation that came my way! Now I am intrigued by how we view ourselves and how the contents of those views - i.e. our mental maps, beliefs and our identity - keep us playing small in our lives. In a quantum world of infinite potential, what could we become if we set ourselves free from the limits of our identity and that which we hold so dear and so 'true'?

I'm inspired by facilitation and coaching that fulfills the purpose of greatness in human beings - consciousness coaching, confidence workshops, building great relationships with clients, within teams and across organisations, how to see past our separateness so that, through great relationships, we can enjoy great business results and great results in life.
