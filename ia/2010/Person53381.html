Debbie Laskey, MBA’s Summary

With a BA Degree in Political Science and an MBA Degree with a concentration in Marketing and International Management, Debbie Laskey began her career in the accounting and legal fields. Her marketing experience has been even more diverse and ranges from the high-tech industry to the Consumer Marketing Department at Disneyland Paris in France to non-profit to insurance. Debbie has been a nominee for the "Women Making a Difference" award presented by the Los Angeles Business Journal, a "Member of the Month" presented by the Los Angeles Junior Chamber of Commerce, a graduate of the Riordan Volunteer Leadership Development Program, and a judge for the Web Marketing Association’s annual web award competition since 2002.

Debbie Laskey, MBA’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, brand awareness campaigns, marketing plans, positioning, website development, collateral and newsletters, corporate communications, public relations, email initiatives, advertising, direct mail, copywriting, tradeshows, seminars, special events, competitive/trend research and analysis, cross functional team leadership, budgets and forecasting, and social media marketing.
