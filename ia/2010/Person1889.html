Carrie Arsenault’s Summary

With nearly 15 years of recruiting experience specializing in the Accounting, Finance and Technology sectors Carrie is the President and Owner of Accountability Resources www.accountabilityresources.com whose recruiting specialists across Texas have worked and built networks to advance your career to the next level.
Accountability Resources specializes in both direct hire placement and temporary staffing/contract positions in the disciplines of accounting, finance, tax, audit and information technology.
