Steven Abramowitz’s Summary

Steve Abramowitz is a Search marketing expert and entrepreneur with more than 5 years of SEM and SEO experience and over 15 years of business and marketing experience. Steve develops successful B2B/C search strategies for start-ups, SMBs and global corporations. He is a proven leader who builds and manages high performing teams to get results, be accountable and educate clients and colleagues on the nuances of search. Steve is a strategic thinker whose quest for search, marketing and social media knowledge enables him to identify and implement new digital marketing techniques and search tactics. He takes a holistic approach to search as he understands the needs of various stakeholders and end users. Steve’s strong educational background coupled with experiences in several industries enables him to understand details and see the broader business goals. Steve currently manages global search efforts at Constant Contact, Inc. the industry leading Email Marketing, Online Survey and Event Marketing provider for Small businesses and organizations.
