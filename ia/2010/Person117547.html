Kishore A K’s Summary

I am currently the founder and CTO of Althea Systems, an early stage Internet TV startup. Althea is focused on building solutions that simplify online video discovery and consumption across screens. Three of us stepped out of SiRF Technology in late 2008 and started Althea. Along the way we met our fourth enthusiastic founder. We have come a long way from where we started.

Prior to Althea, I used to develop software at EmSyS, Samsung, TrueSpan and SiRF mostly as a part of multi-location and cross functional product teams. I also represented these companies on the working groups of a few wireless standards. I remain an open source enthusiast since my university days and enjoy contributing to community development projects.

I independently consult with companies as a technology architect/adviser. In addition, I advise early stage companies on SEO, online marketing and social media strategy.
