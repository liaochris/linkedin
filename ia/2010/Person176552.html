Roger Gregory’s Summary

Twenty five plus years designing, developing and implementing portfolio accounting and performance measurement systems. Emphasis on client involvement, thorough understanding of the investment process, translation into concise business requirements, development specifications and solution delivery. Detailed knowledge of multi-currency accounting, complex asset classes, corporate events, US tax and GAAP requirements, settlements, and performance measurement.

My focus has been on bridging the gap between Business leaders and Agile developers. Typically, the developers are very good at applying technology to create solutions; the problem is that the business has neither the time nor the skill set to efficiently communicate requirements, resulting in too many development iterations and extended time-to-deliver. My insight and vision allows me to close this gap by providing concise statements of requirements and alternative implementation approaches resulting in higher quality solutions delivered in shorter timeframes.
