Robin Christensen’s Summary

I am a writing, joke telling, acting, dog walking machine. I'm working on my own Good News Show focused on positive and funny stories happening with normal people trying to make a difference in this world.
I am also writing a couple of books. One is about my layoff experience with a positive and hilarious twist. I'm the oldest of 3 kids. I have 2 younger brothers and my parents are "happily divorced".
Since the layoff, I've been writing and performing stand-up comedy and taking electric guitar lessons (since I was getting too good for guitar hero:)
I'm married to Michael, a Scientist/ Entrepreneur and I have a 10 yr. old stepson Liam, who stays with us often. We have two 4 yr old dogs, Lila (Golden Doodle) and Piggy (Chocolate Lab).
We love to invent, write, travel and dream. We love our friends & family & although we are always striving to get to "the next level" we are very happy to be where we are.
