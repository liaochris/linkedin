John Finan’s Summary

Diversified career in the healthcare, pharmaceutical/vaccine and consulting industries. Highly experienced, analytical - pragmatic balanced enterprise-wide general manager, creative leader and mentor who loves supporting individuals to fully achieve potential and serving as organizations customer interaction emissary; diverse executive leadership experience with Fortune 20, Madison Avenue and entrepreneurial start-up organizations.

Repeatedly recognized with fast-track promotions and high-priority projects. Focus is on leadership, customer relation emissary, integration of creative, marketing, sales & customer account management, innovation, business and financial strategy, but includes general management, P&L – balance sheet – capital asset management, talent management, marketing and business development.

Strong experience with large companies (Searle, Hoechst, Euro RSCG, Cardinal Health, Pfizer, Merck), growth companies (Forest Labs, CommonHealth), and start-up/emerging companies (Founded BLUE Ox Group, EPS LLC.) Collaborative leadership style/character demonstrated with a consistent professional modus operandi centered on ethics and high standards of performance and includes accountability, responsibility, integrity, loyalty, flexibility and clearly defined expectations for all.

Long history of optimizing under-performing business environments both domestically and internationally; identify, secure and unify diverse A+ performers to create high-performing teams; lead team with collaboration, pragmatic metrics, best practices & delegation to deliver unparalleled performance & growth.

Visit www.blueoxgroup.com for more details.
