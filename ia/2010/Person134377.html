Mark Parrish’s Summary

Public, private, and private equity executive, military veteran, management consultant and public speaker committed to the empassioned hands-on application of transformative leadership principles learned through diverse industry, academic, and military experiences.

Ethical Leadership with Proven Impact
• Led the operational rejuvenation of a branded enterprise that had become inefficient and unresponsive to customer needs. Created and modeled a Code of Ethics while introducing a world class operations philosophy. Three year results included a one hundred percent increase in earnings (2000 EBITDA≈$61M; 2003 EBITDA≈$122M) and a three hundred percent stock appreciation (from $7.15 to $20.93).

Operations Excellence Core
• In two years directed company operations from a worst-in-industry OSHA incidence rate (>14.0) to a best-in-class, industry leading rate (4.1). Introduced a “Zero Waste” initiative focused on people development, quality, service, and cost control disciplines. Generated the best consumer quality rating in over a decade. Employees recognized management by selecting enterprise as one of Fortune Magazine’s “100 Best Companies to Work For.”

Sales and Marketing Focused
• As executive leadership team member and corporate director, represented the company on national and strategic sales accounts resulting in new and more profitable business. Critical member of team that designed sales development strategies. Worked in conjunction with executive leadership in developing and directing sales strategies, including focus group analysis and product development as well as acting as operational liaison to sales teams and helping to align sales initiatives with internal operations. Direct customer interface included sales calls and management of customer service and field service departments.

Mark Parrish’s Specialties:
