Blake Landau (Customer Mgmt IQ)’s Summary

Customer Management IQ is an online community for finding Customer Management and Call Center/Contact Center information. Executives within Call and Contact Centers and Customer Service turn to Customer Management IQ to discuss ideas, share best practices and interact with a vibrant network of Customer Management, Customer Service, Call Center and Contact Center Professionals.

As Editor I work with thought-leaders in the Customer Service and Call Center and Contact Center industries to develop articles, podcasts, video presentations, live online events on topics such as Call Center and Contact Center Management, Customer Experience, Customer Loyalty, Customer Service, First Call Resolution (FCR), Average Handle Time (AHT), Interactive Voice Response (IVR), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Social Media and more.

Please contact me if you are interested in contributing on CustomerManagementIQ.com.
