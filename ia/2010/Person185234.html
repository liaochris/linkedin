Sean Angus’s Summary

Sean Angus, President, brings over 15 years of hands-on experience in consumer product marketing, social media, product management, PR and brand development to PMC International. He has strong experience in the consumer products market and has worked with different companies on projects ranging from social media marketing campaigns, new product launches, digital marketing, website development, public relations, media outreach, eCommerce, sales, and brand marketing.

Prior to PMC International, he held the title of Marketing/Sales Manager at Fanatec, a video game peripherals manufacturer. During his tenure, he was responsible for all aspects of marketing and sales for the U.S. division. His marketing, sales and business development functions included new product roll outs, defining marketing strategies, managing key accounts, preparing sales presentations, online marketing and website development. Previous to Fanatec, he started his own company named LASR Accessories, which he became proficient in all areas of the business, such as marketing, sales, PR, logistics and business development. At USA Wireless Solutions, Sean managed the Marketing, Creative Services and e-Commerce departments. His responsibilities included overseeing the creation of all packaging, marketing materials, website development and promotional programs.

Sean also worked for Belkin International, where he was a Marketing Product Manager responsible for over $100 million in sales. His accomplishments include increasing sales for his product line by 80%, 15 successful new product launches, and identifying $6 million in cost savings. Previous to Belkin, Sean worked with Bijan Fragrances as a Project Manager. He was responsible for several products launches, including a $42 million fragrance launch. He also worked for 2 years in the purchasing and marketing department for Procter and Gamble. And he has direct hands-on experience in market research from his work at Lieberman Research Worldwide.
