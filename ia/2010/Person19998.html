Barb Heenan’s Summary

In my professional life, I do audits, reviews, compilations and tax returns. I do these for individuals, partnerships, S Corporations, and C Corporations. However, the best part of my job is helping people keep better track of their finances and have usable, informational financial statements so they can make better decisions for their personal lives or businesses.

I help many clients learn quickbooks and implement it in their daily functions. Quickbooks is a wonderful product and makes the day-to-day financial tasks easier to accomplish with the correct guidance.

In my personal life, I am an advocate for scleroderma education, awareness and funding to find a cure. I was diagnosed with scleroderma in March 1994. Scleroderma is a chronic, auto-immune disease that is the over production of collagen in the body. There are many variations and degrees of scleroderma. My scleroderma is clinically labeled "limited systemic sclerosis." Scleroderma is part of who I am, but it does not define who I am. If you would like more information about scleroderma please visit www.scleroderma.org.
