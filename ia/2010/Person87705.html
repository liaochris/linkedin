James Ketchell’s Summary

Serchen Interactive was founded out of a need to integrate multiple business directories focused on the hosting marketplace.

The creation of a network enabled us to greatly improve campaign management and communication across multiple advertising channels.

Our combined network has emerged as the recognized leader in matching buyers and sellers of Hosted Services.

Over a period of 12 years we have developed 10, category leading, web sites in the USA, UK, Canada and Australia, providing buyer information on over 11,000 individual service providers around the globe.

Our continued goal is to develop the worlds largest marketplace for Hosted Services, through a combination of innovative marketing products, and a dedication to the highest standards in data aggregation.
