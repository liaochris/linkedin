John Talarico’s Summary

Bio, John J. Talarico

John Talarico, VP of Quality Systems and Regulatory Affairs, joined Delcath, Corp, Inc in 2008 to spearhead the Company’s regulatory, clinical and quality commitment and efforts in procuring international regulatory approvals on their combination device (oncology drug and device). John's expertise ranges from the lifting of FDA warning letters, gaining PMAs, 510(k)s, INDs, 505(b)2, and IDE approvals as well as other international product approvals (FDA, CE and other non-EU countries).

John has over 25 years of extensive medical device experience in the areas of Quality, Clinical and Regulatory Affairs, working for companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Honeywell Medical and Bell Labs. John has a Bachelor’s of Engineering Degree from Stevens Institute of Technology, and received his Masters Degree in Business Administration from Florida Institute of Technology.

John Talarico’s Specialties:

Executive Management, International Consulting, PreMarket Approval, 510(k) and IDE approvals for Class I,II & III devices and other international product approvals (FDA, CE and other non-EU countries).
