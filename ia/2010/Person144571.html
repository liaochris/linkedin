Hallo,

Michel Tel helpt bedrijven bij het verhogen van de online conversie. Hoe? Door uit te gaan van de eindgebruiker? Zorg dat je de eindgebruiker leert kennen, kruip in de huid van de eindgebruiker, voel, handel en leef als de eindgebruiker.

Door uit te gaan van de eindgebruiker ben ik er van overtuigd dat de usability en het interaction design zo optimaal mogelijk kan worden gemaakt.

Het klinkt simpel. Wil je weten hoe jouw online conversie kan worden verhoogt? Geef een gil en zal je voorzien van advies. No problemo!

E-mailen mag altijd: info [at] userguru [.] nl

English? Read more information below the line.

-----------------------------------------------------------------

First of all, let me say that the discipline is important , not the title. As an user experience designer I am working on different disciplines as information architecture, communication design, interaction design, usability engineering and user interface engineering.

Most of the time I see myself as user experience designer. An experience designer need to understand his users and to balance the goals of the business with the needs, abilities, and goals of the users. Most important are the users, users know best.

They, who will be using a product or service know what their needs, goals and preferences are. It’s up to me to design fort hem and to find out the needs. There are so many great and exciting tools for user research. Use them. Let me say; these tools give you so much new visions. Find every solutions to solve user-problems.

Focus on your users. User centered design. Goals are really important in user centered design, focus on what the user ultimately wants to accomplish.

In spite of user centered design. I rely almost solely on my wisdom and experience as a designer to making decisions. If there isn't enough time and budget for user-research I'm left to my own devices, my organization and research from others.
