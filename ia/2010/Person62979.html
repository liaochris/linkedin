Elaine Pofeldt’s Summary

I am a freelance writer and editor who works with publications including CBS Moneywatch, Crain's New York Business, Working Mother, and Good Housekeeping. I also do online editorial consulting and help corporate, nonprofit and private clients write and edit proposals, RFPs, reports, white papers and academic articles.

Elaine Pofeldt’s Specialties:

I specialize in journalism on small business and entrepreneurship, green business, parenting, education and crime.

I help my consulting clients create inviting, high-traffic websites - drawing on my experience in building Fsb.com to 2-to 5-million page views a month and the #2 site in Google after the U.S. SBA.

I founded and ran a national business plan competition for FORTUNE Small Business and now advise clients on creating and improving contests of their own.
