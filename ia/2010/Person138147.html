Matt Simpson’s Summary

I am the Founder and Brand Strategist of The Infusion Group. I have spent 18 years immersed in the creative services and communications industry in various management and creative roles, including as a founder and partner in three design firms and printing operations.

I'm an entrepreneur at heart. I draw on my creative vision and real-world expertise to help guide my clients as they navigate the ever-changing terrain of the marketplace. It is important to me that my clients and partners understand that we are in this together!

I founded GraphicSource Creative Group in 2002. Providing creative, print design and Internet-based marketing, we quickly became known as a premium provider of brand development and creative solutions. Re-branded, Infusion in 2007, I continue to maintain an active, hands-on role in the day-to-day operations at Infusion. We are blessed to have an amazing staff of creative partners and incredibly talented associates that are dedicated to the company’s continued success.

I believe that great creative without a cohesive strategy is just a plan destined to go sour. You've got to captivate your target and keep them engaged. That's where Infusion stands out. We don't just crank out stellar design and state-of-the-art websites (those are givens), we soak up every detail to deliver refreshing, integrated, and brand relevant creative with a twist of juicy technology your customers can really sink their teeth in to.

Matt Simpson’s Specialties:

I am a designer, a brand strategist and a creative. My purpose is to bring cohesion and clarity to your organization's brand. This often encompasses all aspects of your print, website and Internet efforts, e-mail marketing, social media presence and search marketing. We help you look like the organization you are (or the one you want to be) then we help you take the steps necessary to get to the next level.
