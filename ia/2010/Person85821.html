Ishan Khosla’s Summary

If you're looking for design that is exciting, bold, has meaning and is appropriate for your business, then we are the firm you're looking for.

Ishan Khosla Design can handle print/collateral, web design, branding and editorial design projects. We are open to both freelance or full-time opportunities.

Our work has been published in Graphis, :Output, India Today magazine and shown on CNN.

Thank you for your interest.

Ishan Khosla’s Specialties:

We work on print & collateral design, web design, branding & corporate identity; and editorial design projects.

We have worked with a range of clients from independent designers and entrepreneurs, museums and galleries, book publishing companies, corporations and law-firms.
