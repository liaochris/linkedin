Jim Archer’s Summary

Following a bohemian youth, pilot training, Viet Nam, and Art school, I entered the business world marketing and leading professional courses in goal setting, leadership, and time management. From there, in January, 1983, I entered the insurance industry as a financial representative for New England Financial, Austin Agency. In addition to being a rep, I served in two agency management positions before leaving in 1997 to be an independent broker/advisor.
From 1984 to the present, I have been an advisor/broker for employee benefits for dozens of small companies, and to many more individuals and families for life and health insurance plans. It really comes down to helping people make the best important, and sometimes difficult decisions by helping educatre them on all available options and costs.
Today, information is not difficult to get. But "quality information" is still scarce. I strive to instill quality, relevance and usefulness, to the information I share with friends and clients.

Jim Archer’s Specialties:

My specialty: Educating my clients on their best healthplan options, and how they can get the best value for their money, so they can choose the plan they like best. Solid, objective information and advice on the best approaches to purchasing individual and family healthplans, and group insurance. Plus, the best kind of dental plans to buy. (This may surprise you.). Finally, the lowest cost life insurance, plus the absolute best identity theft plans anywhere.
