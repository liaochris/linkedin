Will Wiebe’s Summary

Will Wiebe – Life Transition Strategist - Guiding individuals and organizations to effect positive change.

Will’s mission is grounded in his intuitive gift for guiding others through a process of self-discovery. His coaching skills assist clients to un-lock their full potential. He provides a life-expanding environment for professional and personal human development.

A transformational speaker, professional facilitator, human development and team effectiveness trainer, Will brings wisdom and compassion to the task of coaching. Whether working with individuals or groups, he provides a partnership for renewal of self-worth, accountability and strategies for moving past creative blocks. His collaborative and compassionate approach to coaching develops a powerful alliance with individuals and groups. He empowers individuals to explore and discover their rich interior resources.

As a facilitator of individual learning, Will is deeply attentive and responsive to your needs, interests and concerns. Together, you generate a context for relevant, practical and powerful learning. You discover the strength in your own way of “Being.”

With Will as a guide, groups and organizations discover how to work together, by appreciating individual contributions for the success of the whole.

Will brings clarity and wisdom to his practice. Over 25 years of professional experience, he has served non-profit organizations, for profit businesses, executives and management. Community relations, civic engagement and human development are particular strengths. For 17 years, Will has been associated with PRH (Personalite et Relations Humanines or Personality and Human Relations) An International School for Adult Development.
