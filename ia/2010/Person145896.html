Mike Raybone’s Summary

Innovative Chartered Marketer, Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Marketing and Decision Support Specialist with in depth knowledge of Financial Services, IT and Automotive sectors.

Delivered worldwide predictive modelling projects for GM. Worked with London Business School introducing neural network models into credit scoring and risk
