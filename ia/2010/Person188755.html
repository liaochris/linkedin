Sheri Cowan’s Summary

My background includes free lance writing for numerous publications, creating promotional upper echelon events, modeling, personal image consulting, and marketing my clients to the general public. My goal as a publicity consultant is to make you "unforgettable". My "out of the box" creative ideas will promote your individual style as an author, artist or creator! Everyone has their own brand of uniqueness which needs to be explored, expressed and shared with the world.

Rather than get lost in the "corporate crowd" my client benefits from "personal attention". This results in a highly creative, customized and strategically crafted campaign. The public eye is always searching for a new vision and my job is to present you "front and center"! If someone doesn't stand apart from the crowd they get lost in the picture. My formula as your publicist is simply to have "Perseverance, Passion and Patience." Never give up your dream, believe in your magnificent creation and don't stop until you reach the finish line! Then it is time for the next race. You can be that "Triple Crown Winner"!
