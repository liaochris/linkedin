Kyle Creason’s Summary

Learning Technology Specialist / Multimedia Designer living in the Glen Park neighborhood of San Francisco, California with over 10 years of experience in instructional design, digital animation and multimedia production, including: digital video, editing, graphic design, 2D animation and compositing, 3D animation and web development.

I have worked as an Instructional Technologist for many years at institutions such as the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, the University of Southern California, Loyola Law School and San Francisco State University.

Kyle Creason’s Specialties:

Instructional design, needs assessment, evaluation, research, project management, writing, Learning Management System installation and administration, Wordpress, Joomla and Drupal installation and administration, Flash animation with ActionScript 2.0 and 3.0, digital video, video editing with Final Cut Pro, Maya 3D animation, graphic design, 2D animation/compositing and web development.
