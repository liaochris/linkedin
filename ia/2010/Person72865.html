Gary Alan Miller’s Summary

I am a higher education professional with 15 years of experience in a variety of career counseling, academic advising and managerial roles. My areas of expertise include dynamic presentation creation and delivery, career/academic exploration, technology utilization, and department marketing and strategy. I bring to the workplace:

- An applied focus on student success, with thousands of one-on-one session provided and survey ratings consistently in the highest percentiles.
- A proven track record of increasing student engagement and event attendance through creative marketing and public relations efforts.
- Nimble, big picture thinking that leads to idea generation and taking next-level steps with service.

I am a proponent of university career professionals using social media, and in addition to Linkedin, I am professionally active on Twitter, 12seconds, and two blogs -- my own (http://garyalanmiller.wordpress.com) and the Student Affairs Collaborative Blog (http://thesabloggers.com). Please feel free to contact and interact with me on any of those sites.
