Des Power’s Summary

I'm all about innovation and how to make it pay! As a senior executive with Harman International - a pioneer in premium audio and infotainment - and as a former senior executive within Philips Consumer Lifestyle - one of the world's leaders in innovation - I've been fortunate to work in environments where "thinking different" is a way of life.

From Turnaround Leadership, to Global Business Management, to Sales & Marketing Operations I've created profit-driving innovations by transforming traditional marketing, sales, finance, and supply chain silos into a single, fully-integrated, business driving force.

I observe, question, and "think different" by seeing new ways to solve problems or create opportunity. Here's what happens:

PROFIT CREATION / TURNAROUND
* Led Philips CE North America TV business to its first profitability.
* Delivered highest EBIT in Philips TV Europe history.
* Introduced Philips brand in TV North America, surging from 3% to 85% of our TV business in less than 3 years.
* Turnaround underperforming businesses for double-digit gains.

BUSINESS PROCESS INNOVATION
* Developed successful business model critical to Philips US / European transformation and double digit margin gains.
* Pioneered direct business model between business unit and 20 individual global markets to increase efficiency, transparancy and margins.

MARKETING INNOVATION
* Instrumental in developing / marketing premium Consumer Electronics innovations: Ambilight, Aurea, Net TV and Cinema 21:9.
* Created cutting edge 360 launch plans for key Consumer Electronics innovations.

LEADERSHIP
* Build and inspire diverse teams to achieve stretch goals through dedicated, inclusive, communicative leadership style.
* Entrusted with development of senior executive talent, as regular coach/observer at Top Talent Development Centres.
* Represented Philips as lead speaker at numerous press conferences and launch events with up to 700 reporters.
