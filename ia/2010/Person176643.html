Roger Smith’s Summary

Dr. Roger Smith is the Chief Technology Officer for Florida Hospital's Nicholson Center for Surgical Advancement. He was previously the CTO for U.S. Army Simulation, Training and Instrumentation and a Research Scientist at Texas A&M University. He remains a Graduate Faculty Scholar at University of Central Florida and President of Modelbenders LLC. He is focused on bringing innovative technologies into medical education, treatment, and services.
