Bruce Carlisle’s Summary

Bruce is an accomplished commercial real estate broker, developer and consultant. He has been working in the Atlanta commercial real estate market his entire career. Bruce has compiled a proven track record of closing various categories of land transactions ranging from single family and multifamily properties to industrial, office, mixed use, institutional and retail. Closed over $100,000,000 in transactions by strategically capitalizing on local real estate trends and shifts in sub-market land use and demand. He has represented builders, developers, retail end users and institutional purchasers to identify, negotiate and close transactions involving every type of undeveloped property. Bruce is also a partner in Mitigation Resource Group, LLC, a dominant player in the Georgia stream and wetland banking industry and he has obtained his CCIM designation.

Bruce Carlisle’s Specialties:

• Experience establishing the “highest and best use” of a property and targeting qualified purchasers.

• Ability to develop trusting relationships quickly, great people skills.
