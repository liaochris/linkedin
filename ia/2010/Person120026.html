Kurt Weisenberger’s Summary

Demand generation marketing professional with more than 14 years of experience in high-tech B2B and B2C markets. I develop, plan, manage and measure multi-touch integrated marketing campaigns to enterprise IT and Product Development decision makers in North America at Fortune 1000 companies. I am also responsible for managing the demand gen budget, and meeting and exceeding quarterly lead quotas. I work very closely with the Director of Inside Sales and the AEs in the field to maximize marketing results. I have initiated award-winning B2B 3D direct mail "door opener" campaigns. In the last two years I have been leveraging social media with B2B demand gen efforts using blogs, Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIN.

I'm also managing PPC online marketing, webinars, podcasts, videocasts, white paper programs, co-marketing/partner marketing, channel marketing, field marketing programs, surveys, print ads, media negotiation and placement, executive round tables, user conferences, seminars, direct mail, email, newsletter, radio, even display advertising.
