Jamie Williams’s Summary

Jamie is a risk management professional focused on business development and client service. He has over 15 years of international experience in the United Kingdom, the United States and the Caribbean. He is a chartered accountant and certified information systems auditor.

In addition to practice development his experience includes internal and external audit support as well as regulatory reporting on policies and procedures relating to business processes and information technology. He has reported and made recommendations on financial reporting and operational risks to audit committees, board level and senior management.

Jamie has worked with companies ranging from non-profit organizations and privately owned businesses to international conglomerates with revenues between US$1m to US$30 billion. His experience is across industry sectors covering investment management, banking, insurance, information technology, life sciences, retail, manufacturing, hospitality and entertainment and media.
