Dean Sadler’s Summary

A proven executive with over 10 years experience in leading business strategy for a top 5 UK ISP. Key executive in the IPO of the business in 2004, the trade sale of that business in 2007 to BT for £67m and merger and acquisitions totalling £30m+ for that business in 2008.

With proven expertise in leading both onshore and offshore / outsourced development teams to produce innovative and creative solutions which drive down costs and reach the business plan.

An advocate of emerging technologies for business process change and growth.

Proven expertise in utilising Open Source technologies for business benefit.

Proven expertise in Web 2.0 strategies for driving large scale online communities in the UK. Check out http://community.plus.net/ and http://community.mya4e.com

I am the founder of Tierlinear, a startup focusing on developing innovative business productivity solutions for small businesses. I am the technical director of Talent on View, a startup focusing on delivering online video resumes to the recruitment industry and I'm the technical director of TravelManager, a successful SaaS solution for the European and North American people transportation industry.

I am always open to speaking with interesting / creative people with innovative ideas / products or people / businesses who require assistance in moving from idea stage into production.
