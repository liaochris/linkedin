Paul Groth’s Summary

Paul is a well-educated and seasoned Marketing Strategist with 15 years of experience in multiple marketing disciplines. He has worked with companies from micro to large corporates in many industries.

Paul has a blog at www.marketingmixer.com.au/blog/ which has a central focus around marketing and business management stories. Most articles on Paul's blog have been published in national and international business magazines including Australian Anthill, Home Business (international) and the Australian Marketing Institute (AMI) publication to name a few.

Paul is the founder and creator of Workflow Central - www.workflowcentral.com.au - online workflow and CRM software for SME's. It's free for any business to join and it makes running an SME a much smoother and more organised process.

Currently, Paul is studying for his MBA at Murdoch University and working hard at Shelbyblue Marketing for his valued clients.
