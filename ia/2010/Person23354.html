Bhojaraju Gunjal’s Summary

Knowledge Management, Sharepoint 2003/MOSS 2007, Inquira, 10+ Yrs Exp

Bhojaraju Gunjal lives in Bangalore, India and serves as the Project Manager / Knowledge Management (KM) Consultant at TATA Consultancy Services (TCS) - one of the world’s leading information technology companies. He has more than ten years of professional experience in Knowledge management (KM) / Enterprise Content Management (ECM) and Library Management in IT Sector.

Prior to this, he served as KM-Lead in Cognizant Technology Solutions (Bangalore).

For detailed profile visit http://bhojaraju.info/cv.aspx

Presently he is pursuing Ph.D in Library & Information Science on the topic “Knowledge Organisation Systems in Digital Libraries: A Case of ETDs” from University of Mysore, Mysore. His academic background is MLIS (Master of Library & Information Science) from Karnatak University, Dharwar securing high First Class and Gold Medal. Recipient of National Merit Scholarship for twice. Completed PGDLAN (Post Graduate Diploma in Library Automation and Networking) from University of Hyderabad, Hyderabad.

Published & presented technical papers in the field of Library & Information, KM both in national and international conferences/journals and also attended various professional training programs. Authored few chapters in Books & Encyclopedia at national & international level.
