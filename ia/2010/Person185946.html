    About the Blog:
    When I announced to friends and family that I wanted to write a blog about the “workday experience,” they did not respond enthusiastically. It was as if I’d declared that I would be writing a blog about the hibernation habits of the Bongolava Mouse Lemur.

    This is a blog about the way we work. According to an “American time use survey” published by the Bureau of Labor Statistics, Americans spend an average of 7.6 hours per day working. And, since the same survey revealed that TV was the leisure activity occupying the most time after sleeping, you’ve got to figure that the workplace is quickly becoming the last bastion of the mental engagement.

    I learn a lot about others at work, but more importantly I learn something new everyday about myself. The purpose of this blog is to keep people, including myself, tuned in to the rewards, challenges, and often hilarity, of a “hard day’s work.” Enjoy!
