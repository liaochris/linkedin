Paul Stewart’s Summary

I own and manage Arck Interactive, a boutique web development agency, focused on the design and development of custom web applications, social networks and content management systems. We're interested in improving your organization's efficiency, communication and presence with finely crafted tools that allow teams to actively collaborate and share knowledge.

We've developed a number of tools that make this process **gasp** fun while allowing stakeholders to safely control how, where and when content is exposed -- both internally and externally.

We're open-source enthusiasts. Given the appropriate circumstances, we believe it makes the Web (and our lives) better. And our clients agree, because our solutions power some of the country's largest organizations and corporate institutions. Do you love LAMP? Brick does and so do we.
