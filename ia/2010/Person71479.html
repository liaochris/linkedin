Fred Cadena’s Summary

Motivated and ambitious describe me best. I am recognized as an innovative, strategic thinker with the ability to adapt to challenging and rapidly-changing environments.

My most rewarding professional accomplishments have always been while working for start-up companies. I find the ability to contribute meaningfully to the development of a promising business, while working with people at all levels in the organization, is worth accepting the uncertainty of working for a younger organization. I currently work for an superior 5 year old company that has attracted a lot of attention for its cutting edge platform and captured significant market share for a brokerage of its tenure.

Professionally I hold the NASD Series 7, 63, 3, 4, and 24 licenses.
