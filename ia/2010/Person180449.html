Sahil Gujral’s Summary

Since October 2009, I have been working on a start-up to change the way American health insurance is financed.

My business model offers lower costs and more coverage in areas traditional managed care will find expensive to serve after Federal Reform is implemented.

Part-time, I work with Alice Schroeder, an award-winning biographer of Warren Buffett, former insurance analyst, and former insurance regulator.

Prior work was for The White House, several hedge funds and family investment offices, Merrill Lynch, the U.S. Congress, and the Weill Medical College of Cornell University.

Sahil Gujral’s Specialties:

Professionally, I spend time on social enterprise, medical care, and value investing. But I am interested in many topics.
