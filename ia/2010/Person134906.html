Mark Weiss’s Summary

Mark Weiss has held roles in sales and business development at leading technology companies such as Yahoo!, HotJobs.com, Vibrant Media, and Eurekster. Prior to Trulia he was the head of business development for Federated Media Publishing where he secured partnerships with large online media companies such as BusinessWeek, BabyCenter, and Yahoo. He was with HotJobs.com from its pre-ipo days in 1999 to 2002 when it was bought by Yahoo. In 2002 he transitioned to Yahoo headquarters as a Sales Producer for Yahoo Local. Mark is originally from New York City, holds a bachelors degree from Boston University where he rowed on the Division 1 Crew Team and an MBA from the University of San Francisco.
