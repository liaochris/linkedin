Triqle Event Intelligence’s Experience

  • Developing "What's On?"

    Triqle Event Intelligence

    (Events Services industry)

    March 2010 — Present (9 months)

    "What's On?" provides real-time program information to event visitors through screens, mobile phones and the Internet. It shows what is going on now and what happens next, all in one glance. At any moment, the event organizer can make changes to the program or communicate additional messages. ”What's On?” makes events easier to control because visitors will be at the right place at the right time.

    With ”What’s On?” the event program is interactive before, during and after an event. Each program item has a detail page with more information and useful links. Visitors can add items to their personal program. By a like/dislike button visitors show their preferences, thereby providing valuable feedback. By sharing their program on Twitter or Facebook, a visitor increases the chance of having interesting encounters at the event.

    From January 2011, “What’s On?” will be available for the European market. A global release will follow in the first half of 2011.
