Jim Cassidy’s Summary

I've been actively involved in the cruise industry since 1991, and have experience in many different facets of this exciting business. After completing my Business/Marketing degree at California State University, Northridge, I began my travel career in the reservations department at Princess Cruises. This is where I quickly learned the business from the ground up, eventually becoming a District Sales Manager for Princess in Los Angeles.

In 1994 I relocated to South Florida, where I co-founded a company named 1-800-CRUISES with my Dad, who also owned a successful travel agency. This new company sold cruise vacations nationwide over the telephone via our memorable vanity number. We grew very quickly, and eventually sold the company to the largest cruise retailer in the country, TSI (aka CruisesOnly).

I joined the parent company in 1998, and enjoyed working in their Marketing department for six years. The company changed hands (and names) various times during my employment...Travel Services International (TSI), The Travel Company, MyTravel, NLG, and they are now known as World Travel Holdings (WTH). Their main brands are CruisesOnly, CruiseOne, Cruises Inc., Cruises.com, and Cruise411.com. They also fulfill cruises for such well known brands as Orbitz, Hotwire, Priceline, Sam's Club, Cheap Tickets, and many others.

When the parent company relocated to Boston in 2004, I started a new company in South Florida filming virtual tours of cruise ships. This new company gave me the flexibility to spend time with my young children, while also fulfilling my desire to be an entrepreneur. I have filmed well over 150 ships, and my virtual tours are featured on a number of large retail cruise sites.

While I continue to enjoy filming ships, I also sell cruise vacations at WMPH Vacations. They own various brands, including iCruise.com, CruiseCheap.com, AlaskaCruises.com, EuropeCruises.com, and HawaiiCruiseOutlet.com. Great company!

Jim Cassidy’s Specialties:

I specialize in sales and marketing, but also have a passion for buying domain names. I specifically like .com's that are generic in nature, ones that spell exactly what they sell. I concentrate on names in the cruise industry (since that is the business that I know best), and I now own hundreds of quality names. (CruiseTours.com, WeekendCruise.com, NewlywedCruises.com, GamblingCruise.com, and many others). I see these as long term investments that will continue to grow in value.
