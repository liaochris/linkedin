Chi Hsiao’s Summary

I am a user experience designer and a web producer with over 10 years of experience in web usability and interaction design. Holding a master's degree from New York University’s Interactive Telecommunication Program, a background in fine art and a project management certification (PMI), my experience ranges from interaction design and information architecture, visual and art direction, rich media digital publishing, RIAs to online gaming and social media technologies.

I am currently a Sr. User Experience Designer at BroadOn Communications Corp. focusing on next-generation gaming platform and TV-based interaction design.

Chi Hsiao’s Specialties:

Web-based product design and project management; Interaction design, information architecture and user-center design (UCD) process; Visual design, GUI prototyping and digital video & audio production; Online gaming, social media and social networking platforms; Adobe Creative Suite, Flash Action Script 3.0, CSS / XHTML, MS Visio, MS Project, Final Cut Pro; Trilingual fluency in Japanese, Mandarin Chinese and English
