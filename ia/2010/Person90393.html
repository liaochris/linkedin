Jason Kirk’s Summary

I've been a writer my whole life, and a professional since 2005. I'm strong at internet.

Currently, I write proposals, help develop Drupal and WordPress websites, blog, and seek new clients for ENGINE Industries, an Atlanta web design company I co-founded with a friend. Some of our most noteworthy clients include the American Museum of Natural History, the University of Washington, the Tanenbaum Center for Interreligious Understanding, professors from Syracuse University, international nonprofits, high-profile New York City real estate firms, local companies, and many more.

I also edit, write, and podcast for SB Nation as the regional editor for SB Nation Atlanta, contributor at SB Nation NFL, and staff writer at The Falcoholic.

I've written and edited textbooks for public schools, managed a grocery department, tutored college students, had a few articles published in unimpressive places, done professional SEO and content plan consulting, written short fiction and poetry that I obsess over for a couple days every few months, and assisted middle and high school English teachers. And waited tables.

My English degree hails from Kennesaw State University, hoot hoot.
