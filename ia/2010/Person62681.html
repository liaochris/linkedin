Randy Williams’s Experience

  • Director

    EHSCareers.com, Inc.

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    2003 — Present (7 years )

    Responsible for the strategic development, management & direction of this internet corporation based in Athens, Georgia. EHSCareers.com, Inc. was founded in 2003 to meet the online recruiting needs of major corporations seeking qualified environmental, occupational health and safety professionals. By serving only EH&S professionals and the companies that depend on them, we provide service and results that cannot be duplicated by the large, general online job sites.

    EHSCareers.com's mission is to use the internet to:
    (1) Enhance a company’s ability to hire highly-qualified EH&S professionals and
    (2) Provide EH&S professionals with greater access to career advancement opportunities.

    As an original source of job openings, our positions are listed by employers and career professionals themselves, not scoured from the Internet or traded with “partner” sites. Because of our consistent candidate quality and specialization, employers often place their most sought-after positions exclusively through our network.

    By exclusively targeting the EH&S niche, EHSCareers.com offers companies easier access to a global talent pool of EH&S professionals. We streamline and simplify the recruiting and hiring process. This results in a more efficient service at a lower cost to corporations and increased career development options for EH&S professionals.

  • Principal

    EH&S Search Associates, Inc.

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    1986 — 2008 (22 years )

    President and Founder of Environmental, Health & Safety (EH&S) Search Associates, Inc. - a national search and recruiting firm specializing in the environmental, occupational health and safety fields with offices in Palm Harbor, Florida & Athens, Georgia. For nearly 24 years the firm has assisted in the career advancement of many of the top individuals in these professions. During this time, I personally conducted searches for over 200 of the Fortune 500 Corporations while cultivating an extensive network of contacts in the environmental & safety field. As a result, the firm developed a database containing information on nearly sixty thousand EH&S professionals.
