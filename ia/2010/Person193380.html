Craig Stanziano’s Summary

DWG's founder, Craig J. Stanziano, has been engaged professionally in the field of RF Engineering since 1984. Mr. Stanziano received his B.S.E.E.T degree from the College of New Jersey in the 1985. The majority of Mr. Stanziano's career was applied to the design and analysis of Radar Electronic Counter Measure (ECM) Systems.

Mr. Stanziano was responsible for the design, deployment, and optimization of a (1000) Code Division Multiple Access (CDMA) Microcell Design for a wireless operator in the Southern California market. The design incorporated unique techniques since it utilized an existing Cable Television (CATV) system for its signal transport to Microcell locations.

To date, Mr. Stanziano has designed over 2100 CDMA Microcells in Southern California alone. Mr. Stanziano has embraced the Microcell architecture as the path forward for the delivery of wireless RF services. Unprecedented visual stealth properties and strategic RF delivery further promote the viability of this technology. Mr. Stanziano prides himself in being able to derive practical business applications for advanced wireless RF technologies.
