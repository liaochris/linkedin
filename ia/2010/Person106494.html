Joyce Schwarz’s Summary

Contact Joyce directly at joyceschwarz@gmail.com ,
310-822-3119 in Marina Del Rey, California.

Joyce is an internationally recognized media futurist, keynote speaker and best-selling author in the areas of Web 3.0, Future of media and Visual Literacy.

TV GUIDE did a 2 page interview with her in Feb 09 on future of TV. She spoke on th big stage in March '09 at SXSW and does corporate workshops for companies like Boeing & Nortel on how to cash in on the future of media and visioning your company's future! .

Right now she's delving into the intersection of personal growth & training & technology. She got $100K to write her latest book THE VISION BOARD www.visionboard.info from Harper Collins, Collins Design and it is the #1 mystical best-seller on Amazon

She loves emerging media and her consulting firm JCOM offers the ultimate TRIPLE PLAY 1) Biz Dev --, 2) Marketing & Launch strategy and 3) Corporate and CEO

Since 1990 Joyce has launched more than 75 funded start-ups and helped her clients raise more than $200 million!

She has launched scores of new products for such major firms as: Revlon, Sheraton Hotels, ITT, Kraft, Clorox, Pfizer, AT&T, PacBell, Bank of America, British Airways, Levi's, Disney & more.

What does Joyce really do? She does biz-dev-for-hire combined with brilliant corporate marketing partnerships and company & CEO positioning.

Joyce set up an astounding number --150 meetings at EVP level or higher in 2009 plus intros to VIP Press and analysts. These include: US Army, Electronic Arts, Nokia, AOL, Intel, Microsoft, Sony Playstation, Intel, Creative Labs, Linden Lab (Second Life), Google, Yahoo, Reuters, Tribune Media, OMD, Disney, EMI, Ericsson, Cisco & more.

Joyce works on a monthly retainer and will consider projects of 3 months or longer.

She is also the freelance conference producer for the new updated ShowBiz expo in NYC 3/28 and LA 4/24-25
