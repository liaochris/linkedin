Joris Eyck’s Summary

The entrepreneurial spirit caught me even before my graduation from the Hotel Management School Leeuwarden in the Netherlands. I joined HelmsBriscoe in February 2007 with the commitment to use my extensive network of hotel contacts for prompt and accurate solutions, while personalizing service to the client’s needs and speaking their language. With seven years of hotel sales experience, I have the knowledge and ability to negotiate the best terms and conditions. This ability, combined with the exclusive special offers and in-depth hotel and destination information available to HB associates, enable me to provide unparalleled service. HelmsBriscoe’s unique business model is in line with my main objective: leave no room for doubt!

From my start as a management trainee with Starwood in Chicago, I continued my international career in the convention department of the Sheraton Brussels Hotel & Towers. Here I was responsible for the entire booking process, from proposal development to the supervision of operations to final client feedback. After a position as Account Manager for the three Starwood Hotels in Brussels, I headed south to extend my expertise in meetings and incentives as well as in talent recruitment via the reputable company Courtesy Masters. Based in the province of Málaga in Spain, I look forward to assisting you in making every event a success!
