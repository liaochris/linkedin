Syed Mohib Hasan Rizvi’s Summary

After graduating (2:1) in Accounting and Finance from London Metropolitan University, I got an opportunity to work as a Supply Chain Planner for Cobra Beer. I worked there for almost a year where I was responsible for generating beer production plan, ordering bulk beer (Europe) and finalising production schedule with an independent bottling site. At present, I am working with Molson Coors UK in their Supply Chain Strategy department as a Strategy Analyst; where my foremost responsibility is to maintain and operate strategic industry model to identify any key changes in UK/EU brewing industry and presenting it to senior stakeholders to build a case for change and obtain project funding by demonstrating a clear link to the long term strategy. Furthermore, working with designated strategy stream leaders and external consultants to develop 0-3 years and 10 years long range plan. Most recently, I have successfully delivered company’s Logistics Scorecard (Balance-Scorecard) project.

I am a good team player, who knows how vital is to have strong relationship with people, I have contributed towards creating a positive “change” approach by putting people first and ensuring that they are involved in the process.
