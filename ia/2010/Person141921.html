Michael Freeman’s Summary

A proven creative veteran who skillfully crafts and orchestrates marketing, promotions, advertising, and public relations campaigns, emphasizing breakout thematic concepts and sizzling brand-messaging.

Co-founded and launched Biz FX Media, Inc. as a "marketing solutions" company, delivering over two decades of media-oriented trade journalism and Hollywood marketing experience.

Prior to Biz FX, employed over 15 years as a media trade journalist, covering Traditional Media-oriented broadcast and cable networks, TV stations, as well as emerging New Media-oriented Internet and Mobile platforms.

I am particularly fascinated in the ongoing deployment and convergence of digital technologies with all forms of terrestrial TV signals (HDTV, SDTV, satellite, etc.), wired copper- and fiber optic-based signal delivery, and ubiquitous, mobile/wireless telephony. Combined with ongoing advances in software and hardware platforms, Biz FX Media is focused on keeping up with emerging technologies, then translating those complexities into easy-to-understand, engaging layman's terms for a variety of consumer and business-to-business marketing/promotional collateral materials. In other words, allow us the opportunity to do the "heavy lifting" while we look to execute on your shared vision!

What tangibles (and intangibles) Biz FX Media offers its clients:

* Results-focused leadership through authentic, impactful and facile communication.
* Career-spanning experience in media/business communications — both in media marketing and journalism.
* Senior-level client relationships across multiple industry categories.
* Highly proficient multi-tasking while preparing creative branding campaigns for a wide variety of clients — specializing in meeting deliverable dates in intense, deadline-driven environments.
* Superior presentation and public speaking skills.
* Passionate commitment to individual and business integrity/confidentiality.
