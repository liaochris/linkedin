Jose A Gonzalez’s Summary

Mr. Gonzalez has 19 years of experience managing and starting businesses in various technology fields, namely outsourcing of software development and IT services nearshore, Internet connectivity, Internet applications, technology product support and VoIP telephony and virtual PBX services. His experience includes managing customer relationships and projects for companies based in the U.S., Canada, Europe, Central and South America.

Mr. Gonzalez has also been part of numerous civic and cultural projects on behalf of his community, including strategic planning for the city of Juarez, the Juarez Symphony Orquestra and most recently as founder of the Technology Services cluster. Other activities include singing for El Paso Chamber Choir and El Paso Opera productions.

Infolink's Mexican operations are strategically based in Juarez and Aguascalientes, but Mr. Gonzalez currently resides in San Jose, California as of June 2009.
