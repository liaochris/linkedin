Evan Howell’s Summary

Conducts business outreach, communications event coordination and marketing activities for SWAPSOL Corp, developer of the Stenger-Wasas Process (SWAP).

The SWAP is the only sulfur removal technology that reduces CO2. The SWAP reacts CO2 in the presence of H2S over a catalyst eliminating both. In the laboratory, H2S is reduced to below detectable levels. CO2 is broken down to form water, sulfur and carsuls.
Applications include:
Landfill gas purification
Flue gas cleanup
Sour gas processing
Claus tail gas cleanup


Swapsol Corp is engaging industry partners to commercially develop this breakthrough technology.
