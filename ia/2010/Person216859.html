Stuart Edmondson’s Summary

As a student I studied under photographic masters Ricker Windsor, Mark Klett and Brett Weston who each taught me different ways of dealing with people-seeing the world and getting the SHOT that tells the story. What I have learned and added to those teachings is that it doesn't take a big production crew to get the job done. A tight knit guerrilla talent team works better faster and has more fun which usually means the TV viewer does too!
