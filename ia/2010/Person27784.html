Bret Blount’s Summary

Bret leads marketing strategy and business development programs for Integrated Alchemy -- a digital agency specializing in disruptive video and motion graphics creative for consumer brands or their agencies. He brings the studio more than 15-years experience managing innovative PR and marketing campaigns.

Bret comes to IA from Sony Computer Entertainment America (SCEA) where he managed marketing alliance and product placement programs to increase the value of Sony PlayStation brands. Prior to SCEA, he directed marketing communications for Bowflex and led the talented PR team charged with repositioning SEGA as a leading third party game publisher.

Bret also created Mediam Communications, a Portland-based boutique that provided PR and integrated marketing support to regional organizations and early-stage tech companies. Bret first left agency work to establish the communications organization at Firetalk, where he directed initiatives to validate a new market category (voice chat) and influence the early consumer adoption of VoIP.

While on the agency side, Bret managed PR campaigns for clients such as SEGA, Pioneer Electronics, Skype, Toyota Motorsports, and AutoTrader.com.
