Scott Gohl’s Summary

Results-driven commercial real estate professional, offering a diverse perspective gained through nearly 20 years of experience in the industry. My real estate experience is multi-faceted, and includes mortgage lending (as part of the fixed rate CMBS industry), development team management, corporate and commercial consulting, and valuation.

My breadth of experience offers a unique perspective gained through exposure to a variety of real estate entities and investors, markets, and product types. I have provided financial analyses and underwritten real estate markets, assets, and associated risks throughout my career. My experience highlights my ability to identify issues and resolve challenges and/or create opportunities through critical thinking, detailed analyses, and independent decision making.
