Ignacio Lejarcegui Santos (Iñaki)’s Summary

I have worked over 20 years across a broad spectrum of architectural and design industries. Thus, my acquired experience has allowed me to work as an industrial designer, graphic designer, business assistant in strategic management and marketing and museum exhibitions advisor.
Today, I am the CEO of Lejarcegui Comporation, a design office located in Jacksonville, Florida, U.S.A., that offers a new opportunity for designers all over the world (GloDea™).
We are always accepting new designs and partnerships.
