Nick Oswald’s Experience

  • Owner

    Legends of Sports

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    June 2002 — Present (8 years 3 months)

    Based near Perth, we supply Businesses, Clubs and Schools with embroidered and printed clothing. We have a diverse range of customers from Building to IT Companies, Youth Theatres to Gymnastics Clubs and a number of Schools.

    Our website has an online clothing brochure with over 1900 items on it including:

    Corporate Clothing, High Visibility Safetywear (hard hats a speciality!), Polo Shirts, Fleeces, the humble T-shirt, the ubiquitous Hoody and much more. If you cannot see what you want we can probably source it. Just get in touch.

    DID YOU KNOW?

    If you buy clothing for your staff branded with your company name and/or logo - either embroidered or printed - you can claim 20% tax relief on the clothing.

    It's not often you can get the Government to pay one-fifth of your costs, but this is one instance where you can!

    Legends of Sports started in 2002 as a sports memorabilia business.

    We have worked with a number of leading sports clubs and governing bodies of sports either creating or supplying products. We still supply sports memorabilia and have several hundred products on the Legends of Sports website.

  • Chief Executive

    Edinburgh Rugby

    (Sports industry)

    October 1996 — June 2002 (5 years 9 months)

    Worked in the early days of pro rugby in Scotland for the Scottish Rugby Union from 1996 to 2002. Started as Adminstrator with Caledonia Reds, then appointed Chief Executive before moving to Edinburgh Reivers in 1998 which became Edinburgh Rugby in 2001. Left in 2002 to set up Legends of Sports.
