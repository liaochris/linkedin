Lisa Austin’s Summary

I am a Fine Art Advisor with more than 30 years experience in the industry. My expertise is providing acquisitions advice to corporate, municipal and private collectors. Since launching my firm in 1990, after more than a decade as in-house curator of a major corporate collection, I have conceptualized, designed and completed dozens of major projects in the visual arts. My clients range from Fortune 500 companies (General Dynamics, Gannett Co., Inc.,) to convention centers (Philadelphia, Knoxville) performing art centers, multinational law firms and a variety of corporate offices. I have extensive experience with site-specific fine art commissions, as well as all aspects of collections management.

Lisa Austin’s Specialties:

Contemporary art on a global scale, including paintings, prints, sculpture and photography. Appraisals for insurance, tax and donation purposes.
