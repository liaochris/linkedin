Daniel Bauer’s Summary

Praised by the media as “One of . . . the most . . . soul piercing performers,” and revered as “the successor to legendary escape artist Harry Houdini” by his worldly fans . . . Daniel Bauer is one of the world’s most respected and entertaining magicians, sorcerers, escape artists and revelationists of our time. He is on his way to becoming the next legend of our magical tales and story books.

He has appeared on the NBC, WB and FOX television networks. His international success includes sold out performances throughout the United State of America, Canada, Russia, Ukraine, Estonia, England, France, Spain, the Netherlands, Bermuda, Germany, Australia, Belize, and the Islands of the Caribbean.

After nearly six years of remaining invisible from the scene escaping the demons and horrors of an incurable illness, he is finally ready to return to the stage and continue his magical legacy. In the coming months he will capture the attention of the world as he unveils plans to challenge today’s renowned magicians to an escape duel where he will prove, once again, why he is one of the world’s greatest magicians, escapes artists and sorcerers of our time.

Daniel has reinvented the art of magic to rebirth the colorful side of the art which has been lost for many years. He returns to the stage with a innovative rendition of his award-winning show “Zero-parts Smoke & Mirror. . . One-part Pure” which is sure to rock your world. The pure innocence of magic, sorcery and escape come to life without the use of over-zealous props, smoke or mirrors. The experience alone is one that will captivate anyone’s curiosity who has ever had doubt that magic is real. Disbelievers will become believers. . .
