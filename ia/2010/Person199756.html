Tara Adams’s Summary

Currently, I am the Marketing Manager for PubMatic, a global Ad Revenue Optimization company that provides premier online publishers with a full service solution to manage and monetize non-guaranteed ad inventory. I plan, coordinate and execute networking events, trade show booths and sponsorships, as well as the annual 300+ person company conference. I also support the international sales team by helping to develop the pitch, printed sales material, case studies, email marketing and presentation decks, clearly communicating the company’s value proposition and contributing directly to closed business.

Prior to joining PubMatic, I was the Brand Marketing Specialist at Tiny Prints, a growing online retailer of stationery products. Just before moving to San Francisco, I was the Interactive Marketing Manager at Fairfield Properties. My duties included intense project management, including website development, HTML email blasts, banner ads, promotional websites and more. I was also the project lead on design and development of a new corporate website and a new content management system.

Previous to this, I was a Sr. Marketing Rep at Fairfield, in charge of the advertising, budgets, media plans, marketing plans, marketing and web analysis, print ads, promotional campaigns and more of a portfolio of clients. This position involved extensive client interaction, in-person presentations, and advanced analytical skills.

I have experience in both interactive marketing as well as traditional marketing. I also have a background in graphic design. With this collection of talents, I am extremely well rounded and able to cover a lot of ground!
