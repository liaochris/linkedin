Brian Walker’s Summary

Currently Principal Analyst at Forrester Research, focusing on eCommerce, mobile commerce, and multichannel technology, strategy, and operations.

Brian contributes to Forrester's syndicated research and consulting for the eBusiness, Channel & Product Management professional with a focus on eCommerce, mobile commerce, and multichannel technology, strategy, and operations. His research focuses on retail, direct-to-consumer eCommerce (B2C), and online business-to-business (B2B). Brian also provides strategic consulting for Fortune 500 companies on their eCommerce technology strategy, vendor selection, organizational development, and operations.

Brian’s roles have spanned both business and technology leadership within large, established online retailers, online travel businesses, eCommerce technology providers, and start-ups focused on eCommerce.

Experienced in senior roles within large, established online retailers, eCommerce technology providers, and has started his own firm serving B2B, SMB retailers, and non-profit web-marketing and eCommerce needs. Brian’s roles span both business and technology leadership.

Brian Walker’s Specialties:

eCommerce technology, mobile commerce technology, online retail operations, online customer experience development, interactive design, content management, online content strategy, online marketing, online merchandising, retail systems integration, online travel, organization & process development, product management, and software development.
