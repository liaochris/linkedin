Cathy von Birgelen’s Summary

Director of The eMarketing Learning Center @ eBizITPA (eMLC)
Established the eMLC in partnership with eBizITPA to develop and deliver researched-based unbiased curriculum and educational experiences to help marketers obtain the required knowledge and skills to effectively use new media marketing and technologies to compete in a digital world.
http://www.emarketinglearningcenter.com

The eMLC partners with business groups, universities and economic development agencies to provide best practice training, professional development, research, talent development, technical assistance, thought leadership and guidance to help business and nonprofits learn how to use the online channel and e-commerce for business growth and sustainable and competitive advantage.

Founder of The eMarketing Special Interest Group (SIG) a group of marketing professionals, practioners, academics and others interested in the topic who come together to network, engage in discussions and the exchange of ideas about different kinds of electronic marketing.
http://emarketinggroup.wordpress.com

Cathy von Birgelen’s Specialties:

Best Practices in e-commerce, digital media, marketing and technologies to generate leads, convert and retain customers.
