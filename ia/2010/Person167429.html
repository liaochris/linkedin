Dr. Rachel Talton’s Summary

Dr. Rachel Y. Talton is founder and CEO of Synergy Marketing Strategy & Research, Inc. (Synergy). She is also an expert and scholar in the area of brand trust and brand loyalty. Synergy is an award‐winning firm specializing in brand and organizational strategy, marketing research and communications. The firm’s clients include Fortune 500 corporations, several mid-sized firms, nonprofits, government entities, and universities. Synergy is a certified minority business enterprise and a certified women business enterprise.

In early 2009 Talton co-founded a communications, design, and branding firm called TRUST with four award-winning CEOs from Northeast Ohio with expertise in public relations, design, strategy, and leadership development in addition to her expertise in marketing research. A core philosophy of TRUST is the support of women in business. Talton designed a program called “Women in Business Invest Forward” whereby TRUST donates $30,000 in strategic marketing/ communications services to the contestant who writes the most compelling narrative about using their professional and personal resources to give back to women in business in the next twelve months.

In 2009, Talton also designed the Synergy Trust Index™, or STI, which measures trust and distrust in the largest corporations in the U.S. It also provides Trust Trends™, which ranks the most trusted brands in the U.S. Most importantly, it will provide executives critical trust drivers in three corporate sectors in 2010: Financial services, health care, and retail.
