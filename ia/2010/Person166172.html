Andy Y. Skalchuk, PE’s Summary

I am a results oriented and motivated professional with expertise in project management, project engineering, project business management, contracting, claims, cost and schedule control, front end planning and conceptual through definitive estimating. I will save your project big money. I have demonstrated my leadership, communication and interpersonal skills in a team environment. I am professionally trained as an engineer, manager, facilitator, negotiator and presenter. I possess strong technical and business skills developed in many years of practical project experience in every position imaginable, including conceptual design, project definition, detailed design, construction, mechanical & operational acceptance, and commissioning & startup.
