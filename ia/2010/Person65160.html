Mike Nolan’s Summary

Enterprise Search Inc, specializing in the placement of Sales/Sales Management, Pre-Sales, Professional Services and Engineering Management professionals. Prior to starting Enterprise Search, I consulted with leading organizations such as Siebel, Sonic Software, Progress Software, eDocs, Weblayers and FAST Search and Transfer, where I personally identified candidates for positions critical to achieving business goals, as well as building/managing national recruiting teams.

Enterprise Search prides itself on solving our clients recruiting needs and is able to work with clients on a Contingency, Retained, Contract, Contract to Perm or even by putting a team in place to work onsite at our clients locations.

If you are a candidate looking for that new opportunity or a hiring manager with a need you cannot seem to fill, please contact me. If I am not able to help you, I have a network of qualified people I would be happy to refer.
