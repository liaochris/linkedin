nxbizsuccess.com UNL NebraskaEDGE & CDR’s Summary

The goal of the nxbizsuccess.com is to enhance statewide entrepreneurship education and access to business resources, including business loans. By design, website blogs, discussion forums, videos, and podcasts will help business owners have access to mentoring and networking opportunities. Expanded services, such as a state wide events calendar and resource center, along with up-to-date educational tools will specifically deal with pertinent business issues, relevant to the needs of Nebraska’s small businesses.

This website will provide small business owners on-line education, technical assistance, networking opportunities and access to CDR financial products. This collaboration of business activities and resources is unique to Nebraska and will serve as a online "one-stop" information resources.

nxbizsuccess.com UNL NebraskaEDGE & CDR’s Honors:

Since 1993, the NebraskaEDGE has helped nearly 2,250 individuals, small business owners and their partners start and improve their businesses. Over 100 training courses have been held in urban and rural communities across Nebraska. Positive results of NebraskaEDGE programs can be observed all across the state.

In 2001, CDR became an independent 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization with a governing board of directors and began a plan to increase its community impact and presence.

Late in 2002, CDR became a U.S. Department of Treasury certified Community Development Financial Intuition (CDFI).
