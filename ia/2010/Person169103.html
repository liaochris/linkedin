Ranjit Ravindranathan’s Summary

Currently, I am leading the “Global Partner Program” which brings together worldwide C-level executives preferably senior sales and business development individuals , practice heads from research and consulting firms together on a “Preferred Equity” model. The group forms the corporate governance framework to control the operations of individual business units in line with the common strategic and operational goals. Partners will realize their long-term entrepreneurial aspirations to create ownership for themselves as a business entity.
The model enables the Partner to achieve preferential equity in this venture with a global identity backed by unlimited funding, infra-structural support, global branding and support.
Prior to this, I was responsible for Strategic Account Management at MarketsandMarkets - A Division of IBN Research.

In my previous experience as TL & Senior Research Analyst with the Frost & Sullivan European Healthcare Practice, I focussed on monitoring and analyzing emerging trends, technologies and market behavior in the Healthcare Industry in Europe.I completed several research studies and consulting projects on markets like PACS, RIS , Medical Imaging Contrast Media , Economic Outlook and Mobile Imaging Services markets.

I have an academic background in Biochemistry and MBA. My focus area was analyzing primary and secondary data on market conditions and business growth opportunities, developing and analyzing market forecasts and understanding complex market dynamics. I have also done projects in Brewing operations, Pharmaceutical Distribution and Marketing Strategies of Life Science Technology division of Biosuppliers.

I am certified in Visual arts & Technologies in Food Preservation. Have received acclaim for research and consults through client testimonials,articles and quotes published in Web newsletters, magazines, and other industry media.
