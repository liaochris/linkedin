Andras Bodrogi’s Experience

  • Program Coordinator

    Music Export Hungary

    (Music industry)

    December 2008 — December 2009 (1 year 1 month)

    Music Export Hungary (MXH) was created at the start of PANKKK in 2005, as one of the main pillars of the program. During the initial years, the Export Office was functioning as a part of Hungarofest Kht., the national organisation responsible for Hungarian cultural seasons in foreign countries.

    At the beginning of 2009, as a part of the structural changes within the whole program, the Export Office joined the newly founded PANKKK Program Bureau, thus enabling more efficient ways of sinergy between the domestic subsidies and the export projects. With the new system PANKKK is capable of assisting the whole life span of a band from talent contests till the possibility of an international career.

    New working methods

    From September 2009 the PANKKK-MXH export projects will be carried out with new instruments, fulfilling a streamlined mission. The different sections of this new working method are listed below:

    - PANKKK export subsidies

    - The setup and coordination of an Export Board, taking decisions of the MXH export roster

    - The creation and continuous update of an „exportable bands database”

    - The creation and continuous update of the „export partners database”

    - The development of mxh.hu as a public database and news source of Hungarian music

    - The development of the MXH online social media presence

    - Continuous spreading of Hungarian music-related news via online media

    - The coordination of Hungarian presence at music industry trade events
