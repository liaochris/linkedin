Brenda Ropoulos’s Summary

Brenda Ropoulos is a communications professional with more than 25 years of experience in the communications arts: public relations, media relations, investor relations, industry analyst relations, and marketing communications. She has directed global communications programs and worldwide teams for industry-leading high-tech manufacturers, developed special events and planned incentive and travel programs for Global 500 companies, and dabbled on the fringes of global construction and retail real estate. Writer, editor, strategist, speaker, coach, conductor, cheerleader. Direct industry experience, involvement and interest: Internet and messaging security, semiconductor, clean tech, networking/telecommunications/mobility, life sciences, destination management/special events. Goals: to deliver outstanding communications strategies for a few key opportunities in high tech and emerging technologies.

Brenda Ropoulos’s Specialties:

Strategy development, messaging strategies, campaign strategies/development/execution, relationship management, business development, contract negotiations, planning and execution, research and analysis, branding and identity, trade show management, public speaking, customer/investor targeting, social media strategies.
