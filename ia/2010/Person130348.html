Malay Verma’s Summary

Malay Verma is heading Wipro’s business for the Manufacturing and Pharmaceuticals Industry verticals in the USA. His responsibilities include revenue growth, operating margin growth, customer satisfaction, employee satisfaction and Industry vertical specific alliances. Recently Malay has been given the additional responsibility to head Wipro’s PLM business in the USA. A veteran of over 19 years, Malay brings in tremendous experience and value across various functions in IT, BPO, R&D Services, Channels and Global Sourcing.

Malay is currently managing a business of over USD 450M for Wipro with a large Sales, Program Management and pre-sales team. In his current portfolio, he is responsible for driving Enterprise, BPO and Engineering services across the Manufacturing Business Unit – Aerospace, Pharmaceutical, Life Sciences, Automotive, Hi-Tech, Chemical, General and Process Manufacturing. His client base includes Sony, Honeywell, Emerson Motors, Apple, Lexmark, Dell, Applied Materials, 3M, Novartis, J&J, Boeing to name a few. Apart from this, Malay is an Executive Sponsor for some key alliances with technology partners and analysts.

Prior to his current role, Malay was heading the US Northwest Region for Wipro’s Enterprise business and growing the US Northwest region from 18M to 125M in 6 years.

Under his leadership, Wipro has received various awards and recognition from customers for outstanding partnership. He is also the recipient of Wipro’s “Best Team Award” for Best Leader, “Turbo Charger” award for outstanding performance and “Star Team” award for team contribution. Malay has a proven track record in simplifying business issues, driving execution and forging a shared vision of success.

In his past career, Malay has worked in India and UK with IBM and ICL in various sales and marketing roles. He was a part of Product Management team and Channel Sales at IBM. Malay has an Engineering degree in Computer Science and an MBA with major in Finance.
