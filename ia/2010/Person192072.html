    Source Appraisal Management

    (Privately Held; Banking industry)

    May 2010 — Present (4 months)

    After firmly establishing myself in the banking industry, I have utilized my knowledge and experience of Residential Appraisal quality control to move into the world of appraisal management. New regulations and guidelines have made it difficult for lenders to negotiate a suitable path to compliance without suffering in customer service. Appraisers have fallen prey to the low fees appraisal management companies want to pay, as well as, when they get paid. By bringing together the financial resources of a fellow industry partner, I have been able to open my own Appraisal Management Company. At Source AM, we have put the priority on making compliance standards as customer friendly as possible. Appraisers love to work with us for a couple of very important reasons.

    1) We pay the appraiser immediately.

    2) Our technology advancements allow our profit margin to be lower, thus paying the appraiser more for their outstanding efforts.

    Our business model insures our clients will receive timely appraisal reports, as well as, priority fixes or changes that may be necessary for an acceptable final report. Our technology and emphasis on customer service is cutting edge and will soon be the industry standard. Just because the property valuation system has become more scrutinized should not mean a sacrifice in the service level our lenders were accustomed to receiving. My vast knowledge of the lending industry, coupled with a hands on staff of professional appraisers and account management teams will ensure the highest quality of service in our client’s customer experience.
