Richard Michaud’s Summary

I am a founding member and managing partner of the Michaud-Kinney Group, an international intellectual property law firm headquartered in Connecticut. We maintain a broad and busy intellectual property practice and have extensive experience in all aspects of intellectual property law including foreign and domestic patent preparation, prosecution and litigation. I am also experienced at trademark and copyright preparation, prosecution and litigation as well as in negotiating and drafting intellectual property licenses. Prior to entering this field, I was a senior design engineer at Dorr Oliver, Incorporated, where I designed and developed industrial centrifuges and process equipment. I am a popular lecturer on intellectual property topics. In addition, I am especially well-versed at counseling business executives and in-house counsel at both large and small corporations. Through my years of practice, I have also developed a diverse international clientele.
