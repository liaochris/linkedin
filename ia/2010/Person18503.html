Aunny De La Rosa’s Summary

Aunny De La Rosa is a Los Angeles-based marketing + public relations professional who has parlayed her passion for pop & the counter culture into a successful career in communications though strategic branding and tailormade pr + marketing campaigns with heavy social media marketing integration.

As a communications consultant, Aunny has a proven track record for securing media placements in a diverse collection of cutting edge broadcast, print and online outlets around the country and Internationally. A few include: The New York Times, The Hollywood Reporter, TOKION, USA Today, BusinessWeek, Daily Candy, SWINDLE, URB, LA Weekly, Mashable.com, House Beautiful, Juxtapoz, Nylon, Metropolis, Domino, Dwell, Lucky, The Los Angeles Times, TechCrunch and broadcast media outlets ranging from, CBS, FOX, and KTLA to LATV and HGTV.

For the last ten years, her diverse portfolio of clients have included a wide array of verticals including, social media companies such as Lunch.com, and Yelp.com, and luxury lifestyle brands like DayNa Decker Environments, REthink Development, Fitzsu Society, deasy/penner&partners, and Ligne Roset.

Aunny has also produced several events within the arts + entertainment industry having worked with companies, labels and private clients such as Universal Motown Music, Vimby.com, VirginMegastore.com, Swinghouse recording and rehearsal studios, R. Rock Enterprises, New West Records, Durkin Entertainment Group, Dokument (Sweden), and Gingko Press, to name a few.
