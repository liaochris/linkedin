Susan White’s Summary

Experienced newspaper editor and writing coach accustomed to working with smart, opinionated people. Projects I have edited have won the Pulitzer, been a Pulitzer finalist, and won many other awards. I have served twice as a Pulitzer juror. Am eager to work with reporters who want to expand their skills. Also interested in helping professionals in other fields write books, as well as opinion pieces and research projects. To see a sample of my writing, read my last project for The San Diego Union-Tribune, a blog of my six-week trip across the United States, at www.desperatelyseekingamerica.com

Susan White’s Specialties:

Am accustomed to coaching by phone or e-mail. I specialize in narrative, non-fiction journalism and in complex investigative pieces.
