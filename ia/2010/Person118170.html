Tom Kohn’s Summary

Tom Kohn is a digital media veteran and entrepreneur. Through 15 years as a high-tech executive, and 10 years of creative television production experience in New York City, he is a business/creative hybrid. Most recently, Tom launched two successful Internet-based companies, raising over $10M for each. He has worked in the Internet industry since it began, enjoying leadership positions with AOL, Viacom New Media, Freeloader, USATODAY.com/ Gannett and the Associated Press. Pre-Internet and business school, Tom conceived of, launched, and produced several television and radio programs in New York City, produced nationally-aired commercials for Ogilvy & Mather, and launched the east coast office of an LA-based television production company. Tom has a BA from Stanford and an MBA from Harvard.

Tom Kohn’s Specialties:

Helping to start, run and sell companies, building shareholder value.
