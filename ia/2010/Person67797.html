Eugene Armstead’s Summary

Eugene Armstead has an extensive and diverse background in the fields of mass communications, and information technology. He has worked with such organizations as NASA and FOX Network affiliate KXRM-TV (FOX 21.) He has served in the United States Military in the field of military intelligence as a Ground Surveillance Systems Operator. He has also worked as a system administrator with Colorado Technical University.

Mr. Armstead runs a successful videography business involved in various community projects and specializes in wedding videography. He has produced and directed a television show that aired for over 1 year on Comcast local programming and the FOX local affiliate.

His current professional goals are to continue to provide news directing services for his current company, KXRM-TV (FOX 21) / KXTU-TV (CW 57), and also to build his video production and IT consulting business based in Colorado.

Mr. Armstead is highly focused and determined, and possesses a keen attention to detail and an innate ability to think outside of the proverbial box. He has a high aptitude for problem solving, multitasking, decision making and adjustments to change. He is also very task and goal oriented and is highly loyal to employers, clients, and friends.
