Aric Hoek’s Summary

Photography has given me passion and purpose for the past 22 years of my life. I have traveled the world for my clients, and I am now located in Houston, Tx.

Solaris is the name of my studio, and Beth and I are here to serve your needs and provide you with a product that is so much more than a simple piece of paper.

Our studios is a place for people with a passion for photography. A place of creation. Our objective is to provide exceptional photographic services for our clients and photography classes for both professional and novice photographers alike.

  • Board Member

    Professional Photographers Guild of Houston

    (Photography industry)

    June 1994 — 2009 (15 years )

    Founded in 1950, the Professional Photographers Guild of Houston has grown to become one of the largest guilds in the United States. The Houston Chapter of the Professional Photographers of America is one of the most active guilds in the country. With over 180 members its purpose is to create an atmosphere in which professional photographers may associate and learn from one another and stay current with trends in the industry. Serious full time professional photographers realize the importance of being an active part of the association. The PPGH comprises professional portrait, wedding, videographers, corporate, industrial, advertising, and commercial photographers as well as electronic image makers and photographic artists, students, and individual firms servicing the photographic industry. Members are assessed annual dues according to the specific membership category in which they belong. All members are required to subscribe to our code of ethics and standards.
