Wendy Kayser Kirkpatrick’s Summary

Wendy Kirkpatrick is the owner of Kirkpatrick Partners, the One and Only Kirkpatrick company. Kirkpatrick Partners is the home of Donald L. Kirkpatrick and the Kirkpatrick Four Level Training Evaluation Model.

Wendy is a training consultant that draws on strong business background to create, implement, and measure effective training programs.

For her latest event calendar, go to: http://www.kirkpatrickpartners.com/TrainingEvents/tabid/57/Default.aspx

Wendy’s seventeen years of experience include retailing, marketing, and training. This mix makes her able to create business results for her clients.

Wendy and her husband, Jim, have written two books: Kirkpatrick Then and Now: A Strong Foundation For the Future (October 2009), and Training On Trial (February 2010).

Wendy is a national and local American Society of Training and Development (ASTD) member. She is serving on the ASTD 2010 International Conference and Expo Program Advisory Committee. Wendy is a faculty member for the American Management Association (AMA).

For further information and a writing sample, please download the newest white paper on the Kirkpatrick Model under the Resources tab at kirkpatrickpartners.com.
