  • President & CEO

    CrystaTech, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Oil & Energy industry)

    January 2001 — Present (9 years 9 months)

    CrystaTechS is a full service supplier of process technology to the energy industry. An eco-friendly technology company, CrystaTech develops clean energy and environmental technologies to the energy sector. CrystaTech is dedicated to providing the energy sector with emerging technologies that will reduce costs, improve reliability and exceed regulatory requirements. The CrystaSulf process is the first commercially available, eco friendly, product and provides low-cost hydrogen sulfide (H2S) removal from gas streams. CrystaTech is continually developing and commercializing new technologies and is a vehicle for developed technologies to enter into the commercial marketplace.
