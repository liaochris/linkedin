Bill Sabram’s Summary

Seeking design-for-hire projects with pioneers bringing gameplay to other industries. Creating games that improve our real-world behavior is my dream.

15 years experience in all manner of Game Design (from card, dice and board games, to hand-helds, plug–n-play TV and PC games). I have expertise in concept development, system design, electronic spec writing, usability and fun.

Bill Sabram’s Specialties:

• Game architect: Create, play test, balance and specify a game design vision.
• Sketcher: Expert drawing and storyboarding skills.
• Builder: Rapid game prototype development (High and Low Fidelity).
• Leader: Passionate learner and problem solver talented at integrating research, observations and fun into compelling solutions that inspire others.
• Thinker: Self-motivated, resourceful professional skilled with tools of human communication, writing, design process and technology.
