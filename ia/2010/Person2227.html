Adam Cohen’s Summary

I am leading Rosetta’s Social Media practice, as a strategist focused on integrating social media to impact and inform all of the component parts of the marketing mix for Rosetta's clients. I'm spending most of my time building programs that treat social media tactics as informed strategies leveraging deeper understanding of the brand’s most valuable customers and prospects through Rosetta's Personality®-based segmentation. I have over fifteen years of experience leveraging technology to drive and sustain business value for clients such as Coach, Borders, Citizens Bank, EMC, Kodak and other Fortune 1000 companies across a variety of industries. While at Rosetta I founded the Social Media practice and have spoken on behalf of the agency at industry events with partners such as BazaarVoice and IBM. I serve on Forrester Research’s Technology Marketing Executive Council and have been published in AdAge, PharmaVoice and other industry journals.
