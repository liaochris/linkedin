Paul Taylor’s Summary

Paul Taylor is the Founder and CEO of Global Citizen Consulting LLC, a leading edge Sustainability-driven Marketing & Innovation firm that helps business leaders to strategically position their business to capture significant sustainability-driven innovation opportunities.

Paul has unique combination of global Fortune 100 marketing, innovation, and sustainability experience gained while working for and consulting with The Coca-Cola Company, Colgate-Palmolive, P&G, H-P, Nike, Adidas, Kellogg, RIM, McDonalds, and Target.

Prior to starting Global Citizen, Paul worked as a global executive at The Coca-Cola Company for 7 years in Atlanta, Georgia, where he led the global innovation and marketing for two multi-billion dollar business units.

Global Innovation Director/VP, Coke Classic
Global Marketing Director/VP, Dasani & Powerade

Paul is a co-producer of THE SHIFT MOVIE www.theshiftmovie.com

Paul is a co-founder of the One Love Learning Foundation with his wife, Anne, and four other close friends, which is focused on creating open-source Education platforms with three schools in Jamaica, USA, and South Africa.

www.onelovelearningfoundation.org
