Jeff McLeod’s Summary

My position with my most recent employer was eliminated in January 2009. I am an experienced safety and health professional with a proven track record for designing and implementing a wide variety of health and safety programs. In my most recent position, I developed and managed the safety and health program for an engineering and environmental consulting firm of over 100 people. In this role, I was responsible for identifying safety requirements based on the project, designing site-specific health and safety programs, designing and presenting training programs to staff and conducting site inspections.

As a consultant, I have conducted hazard evaluations, measured and evaluated employee exposures, and made recommendations for turnkey solutions to ultimately reduce the client’s overall safety and health risks. I have designed and presented safety training programs to meet client-specific needs. I have the ability assess employee skill sets to build a collaborative environment where everyone’s skills are being utilized to the fullest extent.

I have over 20 years experience in the safety and health field and I am board certified in safety and industrial hygiene.

I would like to obtain a position with a manufacturing company.
