Sean Patrick MacDonald’s Summary

Executive with 15 years operational leadership and engineering experience in High Technology companies working within the Global 1000 including the Pharmaceutical, Telecommunications, Consumer Packaged Goods, Automotive, Engineering, Chemical, Food & Beverage, Gaming/Simulation and Healthcare industries. Has personally secured current and future business with customers under contract duress due to failed product delivery initiatives and has consistently achieved results with strategic and tactical planning responsibilities for products generating up to $50M. A degreed professional and engineer possessing operations and escalation management skills, has had several successes in sustaining viable, growing business by leading distributed teams in Asia, Europe and the US. Has lead several consolidation events, founded a company, built a new team for a start-up, directed product development initiatives, raised over $1.2 million in capital, is a patented inventor, and remains as the leading technology visionary behind SimCraft - a high fidelity simulation technology company that develops and manufactures military grade, full-motion, racing and flight simulation technology.
