Paul Bartholomew, ABIPP’s Summary

Paul S. Bartholomew is based in the Pennsylvania, New Jersey and New York region. He is a specialist in photography of architecture and interior design. His style uses selective composition while capturing environments as they are intended and knows the proper balance when using supplemental lighting. His sense of composition comes from his extensive background in the fine arts resulting in images that capture the essence of the subject.

Paul is proud to be an associate qualified member of The British Institute of Professional Photography (BIPP). He is also a member of the American Society of Media Photographers (ASMP) and is a contributor to the ASMP Strictly Business blog.

Paul Bartholomew, ABIPP’s Specialties:

Specialist in architectural, interior design, travel and hospitality photography. Proficient working with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, DXO Optics and Dreamweaver.
