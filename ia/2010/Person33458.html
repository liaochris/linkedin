Dan Carroll’s Summary

I have been in the print, mailing, graphics and ad specialty business since the early 1980's.

I owned and operated a print brokers business "Duck Soup" from 1991 to 1994 in San Diego California.

I am an honorary member of the Print Brokers Association since 1991.

My passion in the late 1970's was photography. I documented many subjects from Indy Style Race Car drivers to famous people like Ella Fitzgerald and the late Danny Kaye. I photographed hundreds of dancers and worked for a news paper as a photographer in Ontario California. My photographic heroes were W. Eugene Smith, Duane Michaels and Diane Arbus.

My love of photography and graphics lead me to my present career in the graphic communications and printing industry.
