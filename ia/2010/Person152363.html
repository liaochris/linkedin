Naveen Singha’s Summary

Naveen Singha is a successful entrepreneur and real estate professional with a track record of two decades of successful investment experience. His extensive real estate investment experiences include acquisitions in Europe, Asia and throughout the United States. He specializes in markets that protect assets from single property-type value fluctuations through diversified real estate holdings.

Mr. Singha is also a partner in several upscale restaurant ventures. His investment history includes the acquisitions of over 200 properties, increasing property value through the application of one or more investment strategies.

As a successful high-tech internet entrepreneur, he was, and still is, involved in numerous start-ups, including Hotmail.com sold to Microsoft and several innovative media and technology ventures.

Some of these companies include Pearl TX, a mobile money transfer company, Media Melon a video acceleration technology compnay and Acteva an online ticket and conference registration company.
