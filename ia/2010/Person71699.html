Fred Schraff’s Summary

Forty years of electrical engineering experience in diverse areas intersect to form vertical bands of competence in instrumentation, power and control. System integration design capabilities, from the highest block diagram level, through conception and specification of subsystem function blocks, to component level analog and digital circuit design.

Longstanding experience and up-to-date knowledge of the National Electrical Code (NFPA 70) and electrical safety in the workplace (NFPA 70E) especially as it pertains to arc flash analysis.
