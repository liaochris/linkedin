Aaron Ayer’s Summary

Aaron builds high performance organizations in growth companies. With core expertise in marketing and business development, he enhances capabilities to drive results - or creates them from scratch.

Aaron's current focus is CleanTech. With a concentration on green buildings, he has developed new products and markets for HVAC and building materials in both commercial and residential construction.

Aaron's general management background spans high-technology, manufacturing, and telecommunications. His company experience includes large, small, and venture capital-backed startup companies in both stable and turn-around environments.

Aaron Ayer’s Specialties:

General manager with successful experience in marketing, product management, business development, M&A, engineering, and manufacturing management.

Industry experience in CleanTech, HVAC, concrete, specialty chemicals, manufacturing, high-technology, telecommunications, construction, and the military.

MBA, MS Engineering, BS Engineering, Professional Engineering License, LEED AP (US Green Building Council) accreditation.
