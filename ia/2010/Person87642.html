Jaleesa Trapp’s Summary

I am a Human Centered Design and Engineering student at the University of Washington. My concentration is Human-Computer Interactions. My anticipated Graduation Date is December 2010. I am interested in everything usability and interaction design.

My experience in the user experience field dates back to the summer of 2005 when I completed my first high school internship as a technical writer at Microsoft, where I wrote error messages for Windows Mobile 6. Over the past three years I have held the Jr. Technical Writer position at the University of Washington’s Office of Research Information Services. I’ve been able to apply the knowledge acquired through my coursework to projects such as writing and editing technical how-to’s, writing style-guides, re-designing a support site, web design, assisting with user acceptance testing, usability testing, cognitive walkthroughs, pluralistic walkthroughs, contextual inquiries, and usability studies.

The University of Washington’s HCDE department has provided me with the best education and experience needed to succeed.
