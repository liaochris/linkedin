Kate Reyes’s Summary

As a freelance designer I have completed numerous projects in web site and layout design for such diverse clients as Bloomberg, Citibank, Us Weekly, Audible.com, MTV and the iphone application, I Heart Concerts. I work with each client to create a concept design that captures the clients’ style, audience, needs, and goals.

I maintain committed to creating end products that communicate the client’s concept in a clear and well-organized manner. As well an understanding the importance of incorporating the target audience which allows the client to be successful with their own clients.

As a Graphic Designer for the Salt Lake Magazine, I designed features and departments in InDesign and created advertisements for the magazine’s advertisers. In addition, I assisted at photo shoots, color corrected and edited photographs in Photoshop. As well as, created graphics and icons in Illustrator, and worked with editors on concepts
for magazine layouts. I maintained 100% deadline completion
of department and feature layouts.Comprehensive knowledge and experience in the prepress and print process.

Bachelor of Fine Arts degree emphasizing Graphic Design, with coursework in brand identity, web site design, print design, editorial design, and typography.

I completed my BFA project created in Flash. This web site demonstrated my knowledge of the software and of web design layout and typography.

Understanding of modern user experience.

A broad range of experience and knowledge of the software including Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Dreamweaver and Flash.
