Chocko Valliappa’s Summary

Chocko is a passionate entrepreneur born in a family of entrepreneurs dating to 1900s.
In 2000 he founded - Vee Technologies which has become a gold Std company working in: Insurance www.veeinsure.com; Healthcare www.veebill.com; logistics www.veemove.com, engineering www.veecreate.com

Vee features in Inforamtion Week List-100 Top Global Outsourcing companies & as one of the Top 5 Emerging Global Services companies.

Vee annually handles about $ 21 Billion of transactions, working with Fortune 500 companies, TPAs & also works with small & medium companies. Vee is a HIPAA compliant company (one of the few to be audited by the client).

Vee Technologies prides itself on offering 6 sigma levels of quality. Vee is the first Octave certified company (SPMM level 2- by the CMM institute) & BS7799 certified.

Started his carrier in textiles- joining the 4th generation family business of spinning. Grew the business from 180 employees to 1100 in 10 years.

Chocko co-founded an incubation company (VSTP Valliappa Software Tech Park) which brought IT to India in the 80s. VSTP provides total plug & play infrastructure solutions. The companies we incubated in India include- Oracle, CISCO, Verifone, &imo (CISCO), Torry Harris, ANZ IT, etc.

Chocko also founded Sonaversity www.sonaversity.org an offshoot of the joint venture that he set up between NBCC Miramichi & Thiagarajar Polytechnic. Sonaversity is an elearning company developing learning resources like CBT, WBT, specializing in content integration like instructional design, animation 2D & 3D etc.

Owner/trustee in the family Trust which owns & runs educational institutes like Thiagarajar Polytechnic, Sona College of Technology, Sona school of management. Were about 5,000 students study on campus.

Chocko is one of the founders of the Young Entrepreneurs Organization (YEO) Bangalore Chapter & is a Past-President. Chocko is also the current EOs Area Director for the India/Nepal region.
