Sophie Gray’s Summary

Sophie is an Account Manager in the Corporate Practice at Ogilvy Public Relations Worldwide. The clients she works on span corporate, consumer, digital and broadcast practices. She executes the media relations programmes and manages the accounts for clients such as FM Global, LexisNexis, financial institutions and major players in the telecoms market.

In the three and a half years prior to joining Ogilvy, Sophie was employed in public sector PR and Communications. Along with experience of managing integrated communications campaigns, crisis management and extensive knowledge of the local government sector, Sophie brings with her sound experience of account handling.

She gained a BA (Hons) degree in Advertising and Marketing Communications from Bournemouth University in which she specialised in PR, Account Management and Brand Management. Sophie has also worked in various sectors during her years in education; spending time in retail, leisure & hospitality and market research, to gain a wide knowledge base of business and communications.
