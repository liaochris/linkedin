Richard Shaw’s Summary

I am a producer, director, editor, and some consider me a pioneer in non-linear editing techniques going back to their development in the early 90's. I'm an expert editor on Avid and Final Cut Pro systems.

I was co-producer of Beat the Drum, a South African feature, (super 35mm anamorphic, Dolby SRD) winner of 30 awards. I'm the director of "The Wordz Project" film, where street kids and famous artists rap the words of Christ, recently picked up by Cinevision International for Blu-Ray in 2010. I was asked to master 70 minutes of additional features for DiCaprio's "The 11th Hour" DVD, a project which we completed and is available. Currently, I am working on one of the most exciting projects I've encountered, "Torah Code 2012." While Bible Code programs have been done years ago, this one will examine the interest in 2012 as seen through the Torah Code, along with the world experts in the Code. What we are discovering is currently mind-boggling, and is sure to generate interest world-wide.

My goal at Pinlight is to create provocative out-of-the box experiences in film and video productions, with a positive message.

links: www.pinlight.com/projects.htm
