Matthew Graczyk’s Summary

Entrepreneurial exec business leader with 20 yrs experience in large and early stage firms. P&L responsibility for six companies, directly managing more than 100 highly skilled professionals and C-level executive leadership for 500-person public company.

What I'm best at is launching and building companies, growing shareholder value and executing investment exits.

I've started more than my fair share of companies, not all of which have been great successes, but I have been fortunate enough to have had six public-company exits. I sold my first company to a pubco in 1994 and then was a principal contributor growing two early stage businesses into pubcos, in turning around another pubco and executing a reverse-merger along with a $25 million PIPE financing. In November, 2009 I sold my last company, Zubican - the online business community - to Inuvo, Inc. (NYSE Amex: INUV).

In Dec-2009 I started Coupeez - coupons made easy. Coupeez is an online coupons platform. We're unlike any other online coupon company: we have several key unique characteristics we're keeping under wraps for now, but when we launch...watch out!

What drives my life and my career is philanthropy. There are so many people in such dire need around the world. Those who have so much simply because we were born into better circumstances have a profound responsibility to help those less fortunate.
