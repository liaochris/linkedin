Jonathan Leehey’s Summary

Over 30 years of product design and development experience in the fields of motion control, robotics, power electronics, power generation, data acquisition, sensors and embedded systems. Expert in the hardware and software design of real-time control systems. Complex systems experience ranges from industrial automation and robotics to fuel cell power systems. Personnel and project management experience ranges from individual contributor to program manager to Director/VP in small start-up to medium sized companies. Experienced in all phases of bringing a product to market from initial concept through full production.

Jonathan Leehey’s Specialties:

Product development, control theory, system design, motion control, embedded firmware, RTOS, power electronics, alternative energy, energy storage, digital circuit design, analog circuit design, electromechanical design, Compliance engineering, LabVIEW, MatLab, SolidWorks, OrCAD
