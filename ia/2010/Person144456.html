Michelle Preli’s Summary

Media Executive with 20 years experience creating media brand extensions and strong editorial success for leading digital and television lifestyle brands such as Conde Nast, iVillage.com, MSNBC.com, NBC Universal, and the Today Show.

Michelle Preli’s Specialties:

Digital Editorial Strategy and Planning, Online Content Development, Editorial Management, Web Site Strategy, Brand Extensions, and Editorial Integration of Social Media
