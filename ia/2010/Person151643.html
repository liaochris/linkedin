Natalie Bisignano’s Summary

In December of 2009 I graduated from UAlbany with a Master of Arts degree in Organizational Communication. I am currently the publicist for an independent record label. I volunteer my spare time working as the publicist and events coordinator for 11:11A.M., an organization that raises money for cancer research. Additionally, I am a freelance writer and photographer.

I have lived and worked everywhere from London, UK to New York City to my hometown of Syracuse, NY. I pride myself on my loyalty, drive, and commitment to producing the best possible final product.
