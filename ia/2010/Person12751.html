Andy Reed’s Summary

I'm a Senior Account Executive for CBS Interactive (Sports Division) – the online community for CBS Network which includes; CBSSports.com, CBS Mobile, NCAA.com, CBSSports.com College Sports Network, and MaxPreps.com which is America's source for High School sports

As a media professional charged with strategic revenue growth, I've been either creating or furthering partnerships with Fortune 500 companies such as Outback Steakhouse, ATT Mobility, Nissan North America, The Coca-Cola Company, Darden Restaurants, The Home Depot, Porsche Cars North America, Arby's, CDW, UPS, Holiday Inn, The PGA TOUR, Buffalo Wild Wings, and many others including ad agencies here in the Southeast for 11 years. I'd say I have a big passion for strategic, consultative selling which I think has resulted in at least $50+ million worth of media, events and marketing programs over the last 16 years.

I grew up in the magazine business witnessing first hand from my dad how proper ad sales was performed at FLYING Magazine. Starting out, I was lucky enough to gain a background that included traditional print and event marketing. Then the digital era was born which lead me to learn all about interactive digital marketing. All in all, I learned alot from all these companies; Flight Training, Vance Publishing, Golfweek/Turnstile Publishing (Crain Communications), and most recently, CBS Interactive.

I'm a pretty confident guy and believe I excel at relationship building, strategic high-level sales and negotiations with C-level marketing executives. My responsibilities include the development and deployment of custom interactive marketing programs that not only engage the advertiser’s target audiences, but also deliver the marketer’s branding, efficiency and sales volume goals across multiple digital platforms. I have found that both clients and counterparts see that some of my biggest assets are honesty, integrity and belief in the company that employs my services.
