    Marketechts Media

    (Partnership; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2010 — Present (11 months)

    Marketechts Media, a web design and marketing firm, was established in 2010 by a group of mixed media marketing professionals. The company specializes in integrating traditional marketing into electronic media such as websites, social media and online advertising.

    Marketechts original focus was online/social media marketing and web development. As their clients voiced more specific needs for assistance in other areas, the company realized there was more opportunity in offering multiple marketing-related services in one place.

    Therefore, Marketechts sought out experts in various fields such as graphics, video, social media and usability in order to offer a wide range of services to smaller businesses at a reasonable price.

    Marketechts’ specialty is development through the WordPress platform, which provides a ready-made content management system.

    “Blogging software such as WordPress is familiar and requires very little training to use. By using WordPress’ back-end system, we can customize websites that our clients can then manage on their own,” says founder and CEM Natalie Straight.

    Marketechts is building their brand in Baton Rouge by providing website design, hosting and social media marketing for local business and organizations. The company has worked with The Big Buddy Program, Baton Rouge Social Media Club and Costal Social as well as local candidates for office and business consultants in need of new and enhanced sites.
