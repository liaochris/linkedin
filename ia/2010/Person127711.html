Lori Ruff’s Summary

You might think I work all the time! But Harvey Mackay quipped: “Do what you love and you’ll never have to work a day in your life.” I make connections, build relationships, and do business thru social media.

Social media is here. It’s not going away; not a passing fad. To be where your customers are: in social media.

There are thousands of blog posts, articles, and books about trends in Web 2.0 and powerful tools for sales, marketing, recruiting, educating, and running small business. But how do you sort through the information & decide what to do?

We can help. We’ve enabled companies & individuals to shorten their learning curves & increase their success by strategically utilizing LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook & many other popular platforms.

We’ve trained 7,000+ professionals in 400+ classes/workshops/webinars/seminars/conferences all over North America. In fact, we've trained 50 LinkedIn trainers! We have knowledgeable trainer/coaches coast to coast who have earned training certification from IA.

I love collaborative tools, making connections, and interacting with professionals – both online and in the real world. This has allowed me to fulfill some of my lifelong dreams, such as visiting Nova Scotia and working as an executive at the nation’s premier social media training company.

My job is a perfect fit for my interests and skills: I love training development and thrive on being a thought leader. As a recognized authority, I’m asked to speak, teach, and provide immersion programs for all types of companies & organizations – non-profit, for-profit, governmental, educational, and everything in between.

If you want to be Social-Media Savvy, you've come to the right place! Start by reading the blog and attending our webinars. Look at our training line-up and ask about consulting and professional services. IA is the largest and most experienced social media training company in the world, so you don't have to look anywhere else!
