Greg Hoffman’s Summary

Summit Advisory Group provides staffing and permanent placement services in the areas of Accounting, Finance and Human Resources.

We work with small, mid and large sized organizations to provide talented resorces to complete 1 - 12 month long projects, backfill for leaves, interim hire positions and permanent placement. Positions include: Financial Analysts, Controllers, CFO's, Staff and Sr. Accountants.

We also conduct HR assessments, outsource HR functions and provide stafffing resources for backfill or interim hire. Summit Advisory Group assists clients in creating or updating Employee Handbooks, Job Descriptions, Performance Reviews as well as numerous HR processes and procedures.
