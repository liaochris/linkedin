Alex Volocaru’s Summary

I’ve successfully completed courses of the University of Arts “George Enescu” Iasi, Department of Graphic Design. I’m passionate about the Internet and from January 2008 is responsible for the development of Email Marketing solutions, from concept, infrastructure and analysis, to design, strategy and communication.

First job? Email the designer WI, Bucharest. When? In the first year of college. I was design messages (newsletters) for brands like Skoda, Altex, Ford, Zapp, Media Galaxy, Cotidianul.ro, BRM, Accor Services, BMW, Seat, etc. In April 2007 I had the opportunity to work at the Broad Communications Group, a graphic design and interactive marketing company in the Netherlands. A unique and enjoyable experience. The Netherlands work has dealt with clients such as IMABO, Bobteam Kamphuis, Refema, TEB - The Economy Bank NV, Traffic Support, etc. ACCRES. In December 2007 I resigned also from this position, and since 2008, I invest time, energy and passion to develop solutions for email marketing, factors that ensure a fruitful development and different from other players in the segment communication via email.

Objectives imposed since the development of the first application were really clear: to increase the level of communication via email, identify performance, and revitalization newsletters and campaigns with a pleasant and aesthetic development of a market for email marketing to send emotional values, not just simple messages. I managed to turn the email marketing into an effective communication and appreciated at the international level.
