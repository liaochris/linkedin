Alejandro Miguel Quarto’s Summary

I’m a lead ActionScript developer.

Using Adobe Flash®, Adobe Flex® and open source tools, I build games and applications both for web and desktop platforms.

I’m living at Argentina and currently available for freelance projects.

In 2002 I've co-founded Platico Games Studio in Buenos Aires, Argentina where I have developed games for clients such as: Claxson Interactive Group, Budweiser, Mattel, Turbo Video Brazil, Canal 13 (TV) Argentina, and FM HIT in Chile.

I joined Persuasive Games. in 2005, first as lead engineer and later as a General Manager. In this role, i'm responsible for studio management, testing, technology and strategy.

I wrote articles for a print magazine "Flash & Flex Developers Magazine".

I'm currently teaching about Flash development, in an institute located in Buenos Aires. As an intructor, i'm teaching how to develop webs and games, for online content.

Please visit my site: http://www.alejandroquarto.com
