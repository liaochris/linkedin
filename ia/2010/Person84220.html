Iggy Pintado’s Summary

Iggy Pintado is an accomplished and experienced enterprise business leader with sales, marketing and online expertise.

His value to an organisation is in developing practical business strategies that deliver tangible results, implementing both traditional and online sales, marketing and channel initiatives that enable sustainable and engaging relationships with customers, prospects and partners and; establishing disciplined management systems for measurable opportunity identification and validation, revenue-generating and expense control outcomes for commercial benefit.

Over the last 22 years, he held professional, management and executive positions in enterprise sales, marketing, channels, operations and online management at IBM and Telstra.

His key achievements during his corporate career are:
• Business: Transformed Telstra Business online site from electronic catalogue to fully functional, 24x7 self-service and lead generating sales/support channel
• Sales Recognition through 8 x IBM 100% Club qualification: 1988, 1994-97 and 2003-05.
• Marketing Recognition through IBM receiving 20 Australian Marketing Institute Awards, 2002-2004 & IBM Australia ranked #1 IT Marketing Employer of the Year, 2004.
• Personal Recognition: Certified Practicing Marketer Award, Australian Marketing Institute, 2004.

In April 2009, he published his first book, CONNECTION GENERATION, a fascinating and remarkable study of how connection affects our place in society and business and the challenges and opportunities this connectedness presents.

He is a ‘super-connected’ networker. He is in the Top 25 most connected business networkers in Australia with over 10,800+ direct connections on Linkedin, 13,900+ Twitter followers and 1,900+ Facebook connections.

In 2008, he co-founded a business networking consulting, marketing and speaking business called ConnectGen, where he is CEO and CNO (Chief Networking Officer).

More information: www.iggypintado.com

Iggy Pintado’s Specialties:

All strategic and operational aspects of enterprise sales, marketing, channels, online sales and marketing, administration and business operations. Also, business networking, consulting, public speaking, connecting, coaching and mentoring.
