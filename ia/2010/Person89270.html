Janet Harllee’s Summary

To encourage and inspire the lives of others through entertainment, humor, story, and song.

It is my passion and core purpose to encourage and inspire others to hang on to their joy, hope, and faith in this ever-changing world. As a storyteller, character actress, speaker, and songwriter, I write and perform one woman shows usually as a character, 'Mitsy Lou Puppernickel'. She is a tacky southern hairdresser. I speak/entertain at churches, conferences, business/organizational events.

International storyteller, character actress, speaker, and song writer who has written and produced numerous Christian dramatic productions that combine, story, humor, and song to make people laugh and encourage them to hang on to their faith.

Vision - I want my life to be about encouraging and inspiring others to find joy, hope, and faith.
By: Offering a fun and inspirational workshop, "Sink or Swim - Survival Tips for the Christian Faith"; performing a musical concert of original music and song; delivering humorous shows with powerful messages; producing a newsletter "Fashion Tips to Live By".

Professional Experience:
Forty years in public relations corporate to non-profit to owning my own business, has given me a varied background of life experiences to share.

Organizations: N.C. Storytelling Guild (former president), S.C. Storytelling Network, Randolph Business Women, Inspired Writer's Group, MinistryWomen.net

Janet Harllee’s Specialties:

Speaking/Entertaining to encourage others to find and hang on to their joy, hope, and faith in an ever-changing world; writing a book; recording uplifting music and songs; writing skits and shows for church drama team; theatre and voice training.
