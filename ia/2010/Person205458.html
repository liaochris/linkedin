Todd Michael Krim’s Summary

I am the General Counsel and VP of Development for SoftScript, a national medical transcription & health information services company (www.softscript.com). As such, I am responsible for SoftScript's overall sales and marketing efforts as well as strategic alliances, joint ventures and partnerships.

Prior to joining SoftScript, I ran my own marketing, communications and government relations practice in which I represented health care organizations such as City of Hope National Medical Center (www.thekrimgroup.com). Earlier in my career, I was an Associate at Konowiecki & Rank, LLP where I represented health plans, hospitals and medical groups on a variety of legal and regulatory matters.

I have a personal interest in philanthropy and actively support numerous charities. In 2004, I formed the Reality Cares Foundation to serve as a vehicle for the reality television industry to give back to the community and help raise money for other non-profits (www.RealityCares.org). I personally produced events around the country that benefited charities such as AIDS Foundation of Chicago and Grassroot Soccer, including a highly successful event at Kenneth Cole's flagship store in NYC honoring Mark Burnett.
