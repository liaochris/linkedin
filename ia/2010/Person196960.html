Sue Cartwright’s Summary

SUE CARTWRIGHT
Social Media Marketing - Raising Profiles

THE UNISEY HUB
Social Media Networking - Connecting Business

I am an organisational development consultant with over 20 years experience in project management, staff survey facilitation. quality standards and research analysis.

Having facilitated over 350 surveys to help organisations engage with their people, a recurring issue was the quality of communication and how this impacted on productivity, commitment and the well-being of all stakeholders.

This led to my fascination for social media and how it could be used to improve business communications both internally and externally. I started using this amazing technology about five years ago to consolidate my local networking activities and it has proven to be the most rewarding and enjoyable journey - so much so, I haven't looked back!

Following the success of a series of Introduction to Social Media workshops in December 2009, I set up The Unisey Hub to support my clients, provide on-going updates and promote the activities of local networking groups in the Midlands.

Raising profiles as a means to connecting business is now my full time passion and it's wonderful to see so many of my clients and contacts proving that this is the way forward. All credit to them for making this model for success work so well for their business.
