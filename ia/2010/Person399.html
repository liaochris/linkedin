Anthony #41 McClanahan’s Summary

41Sports. Former Pro Football Player and TV Performer Anthony McClanahan Uses the fitness boot camp method to run his business side of 41 Sports, His Athletics, Nutrition, and Character-Building Program for Young People and Families CARLSBAD, Calif., /PRNewswire/ Anthony McClanahan doesn't have time for "try to." He just does. So, when he decided to take his experiences as a professional athlete -- football player, wrestler, and TV "gladiator" -- and use them to help young competitors and their families to reach their goals in sports and training, it was easy to create the kind of program he wanted. Running it efficiently was a different matter. Using his jersey number as a brand, McClanahan's 41 Sports would be more than just all sports all the time. It would combine training and discipline and extend them from the playing field to the dinner table, home life, and school work. "If kids don't know how to get along with others, " he says, "don't have the energy that comes with proper nutrition, and don't have the determination to succeed, whether it's on the athletic field or in the medical field, they won't be as good as they could be." Here at 41 sports we help young athletes to become better players on and off the field. 41 sports also helps the families to become students of the health and fitness game. 41 Sports educates families on how to eat better and take better steps in changing their life style and other poor fitness habits. We are 41 sports.
