Todd Litzman’s Summary

I have over 23 years’ experience in developing, installing and supporting computerized Point of Sale, Inventory Management, and Sales Process Automation systems for various industries. With our team we have grown Brandwise, Inc. (Showroom Solutions, Inc.) from a basement company with two employees in 1997 to the largest provider of B2B, Sales Force Automation, Visual Merchandising, CRM, Business Intelligence, Data aggregation and Data Consulting throughout various wholesale sales channels. Brandwise now employs over 45 team members supporting an excess of 450 corporate clients. Prior to starting Brandwise, I was an independent consultant for Paramount Parks, Inc., and was responsible for the recommendation and implementation of ticketing, concessions, merchandising and inventory control systems for five major theme parks in the United States and one in Canada during this two year project. Previous to this experience, I was Senior Vice President of Operations with Icon International Corporation, one of the largest suppliers of automated ticking systems for Movie Theaters, Stadiums/Arena’s and Amusement Parks in the US and Europe at the time, before being bought out by ETM Corporation in 1999.
