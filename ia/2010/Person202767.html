Tom Wagner’s Summary

Since 2004, I serve as the Director of Marketing for Signatec, Inc., a leading designer and manufacturer of high-speed, PC-based data acquisition, signal processing, waveform generation and mass storage systems. Signatec works with its customers to build affordable, real-time solutions for advanced radar, SIGINT, ultrasound, medical imaging and other high-speed communications applications.

What separates Signatec's solutions from other similar products in the industry is the ability to acquire, process and transfer data simultaneously with no breaks in the analog record for data rates up to 2.8 billion samples per second to the PC and disk storage.

My goal is to position Signatec as the only single source provider of complex, integrated signal technology solutions.
