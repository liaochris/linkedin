Julie Northrop’s Summary

In January of 2008, Julie launched Free Birthday Treats.com, a nationwide directory showcasing businesses offering birthday freebies and discounts to adults, kids and pets.

Julie created the website as a resource for the public to find and enjoy special birthday offers from local businesses. It offers search capabilities to view treats by demographic, location and business category.

Free Birthday Treats.com is experiencing phenomenal growth, was awarded an Honorable Mention for Most Innovative by StartupNation (Home-based 100 Competition) and has been featured on The Tyra Show website, local news stations across the nation, as well as in print (Woman's Day) and online media.
