Stewart Smith’s Summary

After starting my business in Portland, Maine back in 1995 I just recently moved down to Beverly,Massachusetts in September of 2010 where there are bigger and better opportunities waiting for me.

I shoot alot in Beverly as well as Boston where my studio is located. I travel throughout the country often due to the nature of my business.

I feel very confident in my craft. I do not feel that there are many jobs I would not take on due to lack of experience.
I find that my lighting is my strongest asset as well as my capability to connect with my subject.

I am not afraid to try new things. I am very easy going and open minded which I think helps in this type of business that I am in.
I always give 110% and will never make a client feel as though they didnt get their moneys worth.

My goals are to start doing more commercial work for various companies... I would love to shoot ad campaigns and also get better connected with the Modeling agencies down here in Boston.

I am also in the process of revamping my wedding division where I have hired somebody to take care of consultations and bookings.

Stewart Smith’s Specialties:

I specialize in Fashion, Model Portfolios, Portraits, Weddings, Sports, Events, High School Senior Portraits, Fitness, Swimsuit, Glamour, Head Shots and Pageants.

My specialties as far as technique goes is my lighting, My directing of a model/models, my creativity, knowledge of photography, adapting to situations and weather conditions, my personality and my passion to create images.
