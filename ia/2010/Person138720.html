Max Rayner’s Experience

  • CTO

Fly.com has been recognized by trade experts and the New York Times as beating Kayak, TripAdvisor, and Bing Travel in a number of key metrics and has given Travelzoo new channels, global search technology presence, and broader audience.

Introduced user behavior tracking and analytics which enabled full understanding of subscriber lifetime value.

Re-built internal systems so that internet facing products, advertising placement tools, IT systems, and SOX controls became much simpler and more flexible, leading to productivity increases and improved time to market.
    • Re-engineered IT infrastructure for Internet service delivery of Web, e-mail, syndicated, and search offerings, powering expansion of Travelzoo’s operations from 5 to 11 countries and enhancing subscriber lifetime value.
    • Deployed state-of-the-art mass e-mail delivery systems and engaged with ISPs globally to cut delivery times by a factor of 3 and reduce spam folder deliveries by 88%, reaching an additional 1.4M users (a $6M benefit).
    • Sustained product innovations and improved subscriber acquisition powered subscriber increases from 12 million in 2007 to 20 million in Q1 2010 and revenue growth from $20.9M in Q1 2008 to $28.5M in Q1 2010.
