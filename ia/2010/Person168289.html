Roy Alberts’s Summary

Splash for Health is a company that provides the consumer the best in water therapy for and not limited to, exercise, rehabilitation, physical therapy, relaxation, and fun. This is accomplished through the sales of swim spas and spas to businesses and consumers. With the philosophy of preventative health care as the foundation of wellness, the brand is “Splash for Health”. Splash for Health will educate, empower, and inspire all those interested to reach their own maximum potential to health.

Splash for Health will sell swim spas/spas, arrange production, delivery and installation, through various vendors. Our target market is and not limited to: rehab centers, physical therapist, sports centers, fitness centers, home owners and anyone interested and has a need for aquatic therapy.
