Anne Golden’s Summary

Intercultural trainer, coach, and consultant with 20 years of experience doing cross-cultural training and communication skills coaching. I have two main areas of focus:

1. Designing and deliver workshops for Americans and multicultural groups to increase their intercultural awareness and competence. I'm a graduate of the Coaches Training Institute (San Rafael, CA) and am a certified administrator for the Intercultural Development Inventory (IDI), and bring these experiences into the training classroom along with experience gained by 3 and 1/2 years of living abroad (Japan, and Scotland).

2. Coaching and training foreign-born executives, doctors, engineers and scientists to communicate more effectively and confidently while living and working in the US.

Anne Golden’s Specialties:

Intercultural competence training and coaching utilizing the IDI (Intercultural Development Inventory). Culture-specific business communication training for working in countries including Japan, China, Mexico, and India. (Collaborating with other facilitators and consultants who are host country nationals.)

Communication skills coaching and training including accent modification (certified Compton P-ESL trainer), vocal image, persuasive speaking and presentation skills.

  • Director, The Intercultural Communication Group, Xavier Leadership Center

    Xavier University

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    November 1997 — Present (12 years 8 months)

    I'm a coach and trainer specializing in workshops and coaching programs that help people communicate more effectively across cultures.

    There are two broad topics within my practice area: Communication skills and intercultural training. Regarding communication skills, I offer coaching and training for foreign-born executives, doctors, engineers, scientists, and educators. My focus areas include accent modification, vocal image and "style switching," concise and persuasive speaking, proactive and empathic listening, and business writing.

    Since 1996, I have coached and trained more than 300 professionals from Asian, Latin American, European and African countries. The coaching that I do helps people identify and correct problematic communication behaviors, and address "blind spots" that undermine their credibility. Most clients who I have coached report that it significantly increased their self-awareness, their self-confidence, and enhanced their ability to lead and influence others. Several clients have attributed job promotions to this coaching.

    In terms of intercultural training, I design and conduct workshops on intercultural competence based on the Intercultural Development Inventory (IDI). The IDI is an objective and reliable measure of an individual's or group's way of perceiving and responding to cultural differences. It can be used in a number of ways, including as an organizational "climate" assessment, as a foundation for diversity workshops, and as training for groups or individuals engaged in global business or educational activities.

Anne Golden’s Interests:

My professional interests center around the challenges that involve the intersection of business, communication, and culture. (For example, intercultural communication in professional environments, corporate diversity and inclusion.) I'm also very interested in helping professionals to benefit from the types of interpersonal and communication skills and talents typically found in the professional acting world. My primary clients during the past 12 years have been high-level professionals who speak English as a second language, and though I am also interested in working with other kinds of clients, I have a particular affinity for members of this community.
