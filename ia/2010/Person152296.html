Lisa Michaels’s Summary

I'm Lisa Michaels, author, dancer, facilitator and founder of the Natural Rhythms Institute. In 1995, I established the company, which began operating as Natural Rhythms in 2007. For the past twenty years, I have been sharing my expertise in the field of essence expression with thousands through my products, workshops and facilitator training/certification sessions where Natural Rhythms helps individuals reach their natural growth potential. Listed below are three informative products I have created to provide support and guidance for people in search of how to co-create and unlock their natural growth potential:

• Natural Rhythms, Connect the Creational Dance of Your Life to the Pulse of the Universe
• The Elemental Forces of Creation Oracle and Audio Book

These were created with the purpose of welcoming people into the world of conscious dance, sacred dance and alchemical transformation regardless of race, gender, creed, caste, sexual orientation, or religious practice. Natural Rhythms provides workshops and homestudy courses for 3 levels of Natural Rhythms training and 3 levels of Practical Priestess training along with many other products currently being developed.

Additionally, Natural Rhythms provides systematic Accelerated Learning Trainings to increase ones spiritual development, as well as, enhancing creational capacity. Specific exercises designed to develop ones ability to access the wisdom and guidance found from the elemental forces (of creation) and natural rhythms of Mother Earth through Life Coaching.

Located in the Atlanta area of Georgia, Natural Rhythms takes training sessions nationwide answering questions for people about what is, who needs, and how Natural Rhythms works. Natural Rhythms is a personal guide to astrological archetypes; through guidance, people will learn the 12 astrological gates of awareness, which will create a capacity of how to understand and communicate effectively with others.
