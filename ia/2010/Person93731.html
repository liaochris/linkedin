Jeff Iacono’s Experience

  • Founder

    CollegeJobConnect

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    March 2009 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    CollegeJobConnect is a connection platform exclusively for college students and for employers that realize the competitive advantages of having these talented individuals on board with them. Put simply, we make students discoverable, and we help employers find them.

    When you want to begin looking for career opportunities (beginning of your senior year is a good time to start!), you join and create your Resume Beacon, which highlights your educational focus, leadership achievements, and past work endeavors. For you, that is it! Plain and simple. It takes 10 minutes. Your personal resume beacon continuously broadcasts your abilities and attracts employers that "get it" throughout your search.

    We continually recruit a diverse set of employers and provide free, immediate access in a highly structured manner. When an employer is interested in connecting with you, we send you a request on their behalf. You decide if you would like to connect with the employer.

    For students, it is free. We never, ever would charge our student members. We know you are going to be making a transition from academics to the "professional world" and are actively looking for career opportunities. We are here to help you make this jump. We do charge our employer partners a nominal fee on a per hire basis. We believe this is the best model for a service such as ours. Charging up front fees (to either side) would throw up unnecessary boundaries and hinder the true value proposition of our service. It also aligns everyone's goals. Students want great, diverse career opportunities. Employers want to find and make the best hires. We want happy students and happy employers.
