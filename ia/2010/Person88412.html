  • Senior Claims Adjuster

    The Zurich

    (Insurance industry)

    October 1989 — July 1998 (8 years 10 months)

    Workers' Compensation claim investigation, compensability determination, medical management and litigation management for ME & VT. In the course of 9 years received "above standard" performance reviews every single year. One of our largest accounts... Prime Tanning Company was handled by an adjusting staff of four including myself. After a period of 2 years, the insured along with the agent put in a request with Zurich management that I be the dedicated adjuster. Having proven myself on this account and others...many of the larger new accounts brought onboard were assigned to me.Having gained the confidence of my supervisors and managers, I was assigned all new "large losses" those involving significant medical injuries an future financial exposures.One case in particular I was assigned was 10 plus years old and had been handled by 6 prior adjusters with no resolution. I was asked to dissect the case medically and legally and to develop a strategy on how to best resolve the claim. In short, I put together a panel of "experts" in Boston to exam the claimant and this IME as it turns out, provided a base of evidence that enabled me to settle the claim for significantly less than was demanded. Another case assigned involved a high profile homicide which was deemed "compensable" where I was working with the home office Special Investigative Unit" and federal authorities in the investigation of the claim and any subrogation potential. I was complimented not only by my claims supervisors and managers but by home office legal on the investigation and management of this claim. Another strength of mine was my ability to establish a trusting rapport with injured workers in particular the more serious injuries where the claimants were comfortable with my explanation of benefits and my overall management of their claims that they refrained from securing council. The significance came into light when settling the claim which was expedited because of no third party involvement.
