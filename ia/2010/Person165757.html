Praveen Shah’s Summary

Through Mobity LLC, a business & IT consulting boutique he founded in 2002, Praveen helps Fortune Global 500 & SMB clients improve their Business and IT Productivity, Innovation, and Efficiency.

Praveen also helps startup entrepreneurs and investors build profitable, scalable, and sustainable enterprises in the Electronics & Semiconductor, High-tech & Cleantech, Software & Services, and Health & Wellness verticals.

In his 20-year proven career, Praveen has
# 1. Successfully built and P&L managed 25+ staff big-4 & boutique professional services practices
# 2. Advised CxO’s and xVP’s of 75+ Fortune Global and SMB clients
# 3. Sold, managed, & delivered 100+ strategy, marketing, operations improvement, and systems development / technology integration client engagements
# 4. Invested-in, and nurtured six startups from idea to profit
# 5. Raised Series A venture capital, prepared for IPO, and conducted buy- and sell- side M&A due diligence

His delighted clients include Apple, Best Buy, Cisco, Commonwealth of PA, Deutsche Telekom, Exide, Frito Lay, Fujitsu, HP, Hexcel, i2, IBM, Johnson & Johnson (Ethicon, Inc.), Kimberley Clark, KPMG, Kraft Foods, LG, Mack Truck (Volvo), McDermott, Nortel, NXP Semi, On Semi, Oracle, Philips Electronics, Smurfit-Stone, ST Micro, Sun Micro, Toyota, Trimble, US Dept. of Defense, US Navy, and Zoran plus tens of SMB’s and Venture Startups.

Praveen is an active volunteer member of the Cleantech Advisory Board for The Indus Entrepreneurs Group (TiE). He is also an aspiring real estate investor. Praveen is passionate about educating children in positive psychology, entrepreneurship, and financial intelligence, and about innovating healthcare to realize wellness through self-care.

Praveen lives with his wife, Pallavi, and son, Mohnish in the Silicon Valley (CA, USA).
