Bhavishya Kanjhan’s Summary

Bhavishya Kanjhan is a marketing futurist and Intelligence Officer at Socialize, a Social Media training and consulting house based out of Dubai, UAE

Bhavishya Kanjhan grew up with and in Dubai. After finishing his schooling there, Bhavishya moved to England to study Computer Science & Business Management and later earned himself a rather fancy master's degree in International Business with Marketing. Passionate about Marketing and Technology, Social Media came naturally to Bhavishya. His obsession with Social Media (as a subject) began during his Masters degree and channelized it into a Dissertation on the topic.

Obsessed with everything computers and technology, Bhavishya tries to be an early adopter. When he’s not on the computer (like that ever happens), he tries to practice a bit of Digital Photography. His photoblog can be found at http://www.thefunkeye.com

Often known to dream in 140 characters, Bhavishya featured in the Twitter Tips Guidebook for SMBs by Small Business Trends. He also made it to the list of Dubai's "100 most compelling tweeters" and can be found at @Bhavishya

Bhavishya can be contacted at bhavishya.kanjhan [at] gmail [dot] com
