Rob James’s Summary

I am a skilled digital artist with a wide range of experience including: working for some of the top leaders in the video game industry; teaching modeling for the Academy of Art College; owning a company that made 3D animations for professional videographers; and having been an integral part of two successful video game start-ups. I have worked on games from console to mobile. I have used many technologies including, but not limited to: 3D modeling and animation (Modo, Maya, Max and Lightwave), 2D / pixel pushing (Photoshop), Flash, Programming (Java, C), Scripting (PHP, JSP), Web development (Dreamweaver), Databases (MySQL), and video production. I enjoy working with a talented group of people to bring a product from conception to fruition doing whatever it takes, from the glamorous showpiece to the more mundane tweaking to the technical problem solving.

My strengths include: Attention to detail, Solving technical issues, Organization, Creativity, and the ability to be a liaison between diverse groups.

My skill set includes: Modeling, Animation, Scripting, Web Development, Teaching, Training, System Administration, and Sales
