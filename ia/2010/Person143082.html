Michael Maurer’s Summary

Michael is a professional Film & TV writer/story-editor/developer specializing in family entertainment.

In FILM: Michael has been commissioned to write or has optioned 10 feature films (including one produced) in all genres. His most recent projects include a 3D animated feature for Starz Animation (based on his original idea) and a live-action family film scheduled to go into pre-production in September, both projects are anticipated to receive wide US theatrical releases.

In TV: Michael has written over 300 animation and live-action scripts, story edited series and developed series bibles for companies such as Disney, Universal, Warner Brothers, DIC and a dozen international companies. His most recent projects include redeveloping and writing all 26 episodes for the 2nd season of “Vipo: Surviving Time Island” which will begin broadcasting all over the world in 2011 and 7 scripts for Emmy award-winning animated series “Curious George”.
