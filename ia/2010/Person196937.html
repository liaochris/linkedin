Sue Atkins’s Summary

I am a Parenting Expert who offers practical guidance for bringing up happy, confident, well behaved children.

I am also the author of "Raising Happy Children for Dummies" one in the famous best selling black and yellow series published worldwide as well as the highly acclaimed Parenting Made Easy CDs.

I regularly appear on BBC Breakfast and The Jeremy Vine Show on BBC Radio 2 and have my own regular Parenting Q & A slot on BBC Surrey and Sussex every month. My parenting articles are published all over the world.

I'm a Mum of two great teenagers, a lover of people and fascinated by change in myself and others.

I like to encourage people to bring out and explore the best in themselves easily and effortlessly and to help them discover how to make themselves truly happy in their family relationships.

I'm vibrant, energetic and enthusiastic and want to make a BIG difference in the world through helping parents give their kids the ultimate gift of ....self esteem.

I have a B.Ed (Hons) Degree & am a qualified Life Coach having trained with the Coaching Academy. I am also an NLP Master Practitioner & Trainer taught by Dr Richard Bandler & Paul McKenna.

I love laughing, singing and dancing round the kitchen table (using the pepper mill as a mike) and having friends round for parties, meals and great conversations! I am an avid Chelsea FC season ticket holder.

My vision is to inspire millions, one parent at a time building confidence, positivity and harmony within families. Enhancing and fine tuning not judging, criticising or blaming as I want to pat parents on the back not point a finger at them !

I am an an Ambassador for the School Home Support Charity & I am also passionate about working with schools to enhance children's self esteem.

Life is full of colour and fun when you meet other people so please drop me an e-mail to say hello and download my ebooks bursting with ideas and practical tips from my website


www.theSueAtkins.com
