Rick Best, CSAM’s Summary

I have over 30 years of experience in the fields of professional recruiting, corporate Human Resources and operations management.
Since 2006, I have specialized in the Audio Visual Industry filling positions for clients in the systems integration, staging and rental, and equipment vendor verticals.

Clients:
My objective is to identify, qualify and present ONLY candidates who are Impact Players, people who will be filling critical need roles within an organization with an ability to positively impact a client's revenues and/or profitability. I have successfully filled positions from the executive level down to skilled/lead technicians.

Candidates:
I am "a good guy to know"for A/V Professionals who are interested in career advancing opportunities. I am in daily contact with decision makers, identifying opportunities with firms who are looking for you. My service is highly confidential and you always control who receives your information.

If you are looking to fill a critical need in your organization or believe you have the attributes of an Impact Player, drop me a InMail. I look forward to talking with you.
