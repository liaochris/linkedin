Ton Bil’s Summary

I'm director and co-owner of Workpatch, a start up in the labour market and social space. Workpatch is up to creating value for all stakeholders in these areas. The workpatchblog (Dutch) tells you more.

Value creation is essentially my business goal since I started working in 1983, and I have always loved to expand my experiences. I had the opportunity to do:

* business-to-business marketing + PR
* consumer services marketing + PR
* (semi-)government policy + marketing
* performing arts marketing + PR
* not-for-profits fundraising + PR
* volunteering + coordination
* teaching, training + writing
* presenting live + on radio
* moderating workshops

Oh, I got formal education in marketing and psychology and learned other stuff along the way.

Ton Bil’s Specialties:

Understanding key drivers of society, markets and businesses; Trendwatching and precise fortune telling; Qualitative market research; Sustainable development; Business ethics; E-business.
