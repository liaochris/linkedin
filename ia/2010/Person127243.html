LOGOS PR’s Summary

“Logos PR is your link to the U.S. Hispanic and Latin America Markets”Company Profile

Logos PR is a Miami-based result driven public relations and marketing communications firm specialized in the Hispanic and Latin America media markets. We develop integrated communications programs drawing from a broad range of disciplines including public relations consutling and strategy, branding and advertising, PR 2.0 - online and social media, media relations and events & promotional support. Logos PR helps clients in a variety of industries expand their brand awareness, reach new audiences and broaden their client base via targeted communications campaigns.

    Silvia Prado is a bilingual and multicultural account executive in Marketwire's Miami office, consulting clients in Florida, the Caribbean, Latin America and the US Hispanic market. Silvia is also a member of Marketwire's Hispanic and Latin America expert group as well as an integral part of promoting the branding of Marketwire within the Latin America and US Hispanic market. She enjoys working closely with her clients on their communication initiatives including using press releases to effectively compliment their overall social media and SEO strategies. Silvia joined Marketwire in June 2008 after working at several Latin American companies within the entertainment, advertising and publishing industries. Her experience spans across marketing, advertising, public relations and communications with a focus on the US Hispanic and Latin America markets. She is a board member of the Miami Chapter Public Relations Society of America and heads up the Hispanic Market Committee which has been involved with educational outreach programs and honored for their commitment to advancing diversity in the PR and communications profession. Silvia is also actively involved with various organizations to further expand her knowledge in new media and technologies including Social Media Club of South Florida, Miami Marketers, the Association of Hispanic Advertising Agencies and others.
