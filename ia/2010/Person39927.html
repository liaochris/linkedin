Christopher Raper’s Summary

Hello!
My name is Christopher Raper. I'm a Senior-level Moderator and Qualitative Specialist freelancing from Atlanta, Ga.

I've been capturing and converting consumer insights into actionable value across a diversity of brands and categories for over 13 years.

Acumen:
A unique blend of strategic problem-solving skill and creative ingenuity. As researcher, analyst, strategist and creative co-contributor, any point of contact between the brand and the target can be engaged and taken to its highest level of efficiency.

Experience:
And as a Moderator/Qualitative Specialist, I've significantly impacted the success and actionable-insight ignition of brands such as Anheuser-Busch, Miller-Coors, Purina, Marriott and Sony. Spanning both the traditional and the highly customized worlds of marketing/advertising communication and brand investigation, I've held Senior and VP-level Account Planning positions at some of the best ad agencies in the US (BBDO, The Martin Agency, Brighthouse).

Christopher Raper’s Specialties:

Focus Groups, Ethnography, IDIs and Triads, Experiential Observation and Engagement, Qualitative Project Management Services, Multi-media Editing and Reporting, Online Qual/Quant Surveys, Diaries and Focus Groups.
