Ville Tikka’s Summary

Beyond the obvious lies the potential to change everything.

I explore the forefront of social, cultural and technological change, working as a Futures Specialist and Creative Strategist at Nokia to identify new opportunities for breakthrough product innovation, brand change and creative growth.

I have over eight years of transdisciplinary experience from strategy development, innovation consulting, futures concepting, trends research, interaction design, youth marketing and cognitive psychology. All this is backed up with an MA in Psychology and Media Studies plus a good start for a PhD in design research.

The globe has been my playground. I have been researching trends and people all around with the world changers and tastemakers in Europe, Asia, Africa, North America and Latin America - and eventually translating the insights to support brand, design, and business strategy, as well as product, service, and marketing creation.

Besides changing the world with Nokia, I have co-founded the think-do tank Wevolve, worked in academia in several universities, brought Vice Magazine to Finland, received a top nomination for INDEX Awards 2007 - Design to Improve Life, the largest design award in the world, and exhibited design projects in North America, Europe and Asia.

I'm originally from Finland, but currently residing in Brooklyn, New York (where the future is being created).

Ville Tikka’s Specialties:
