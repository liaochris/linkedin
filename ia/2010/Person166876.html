Daniel Crowley, CCP’s Summary

Summary
A Quality champion with extensive quality assurance, requirement engineering and project management experience. Leadership and management roles with eCommerce, telecommunications, financial (banking, mortgage and insurance), manufacturing, aerospace, and healthcare organizations.

Goals-driven with proven success in quality processes and organizational development.
Dedicated Quality Assurance Professional providing customer focused quality with ROI.
Project team leader and mentor.
Professional speaker & trainer on quality and process improvement, both local and international.
