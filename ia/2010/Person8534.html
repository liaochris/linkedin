Al Timm, RFC, LUTCF, CITRMS’s Summary

We are a Professional Independent Agency with over 40 yrs. of experience offering Group Health, Life., LTC, LTD, Dental, Legal, Identity Theft Restoration, ROTH IRA's, IRA's, Financial Planning & Retirement Plans.
Born in a refugee camp behind the Iron Curtain, my mother, siblings and I escaped the claws of communism & united with our father via the Int'l Red Cross after WWII. Finally, we legally immigrated to the USA in 1956. I attended Public School, Tech. School, became a Naturalized Citizen, served in the Army during Viet Nam and then started my civilian life as a responsible citizen of my adopted country. I received my 1st insurance license in 1968 and the Series 7 Securities license in 1969 and added my Series 65 license in later yrs.. Became an active member of the US Jaycees, then Kiwanis International, elected 3 terms to City Council ect., ordained into the Ministry in 1991. It has been a joy serving & meeting the needs of people from all walks of life. I have been holding Public Seminars for Financial Planning/Retirement, Guest Speaker at Purdue University Life Ins. Mktg. Institute, Employers, & Civic Grps. Al is available to Business & Civic groups as a guest speaker at their meetings and discuss the latest FTC FACTA/RED Flag rules and regulations.

Al Timm, RFC, LUTCF, CITRMS’s Specialties:

Certified Identity Theft Risk Management Specialist
Financial Adviser to families & businesses for their employee benefits and assist employers in implimenting a Mitigation Plan & assist educating employees relating to FACTA/RED Flag Regulations.
As a Financial Advisor I assist clients in planning for their golden years of retirement with guarantees of principle in fixed, indexed or variable annuity products.
