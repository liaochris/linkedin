Tomaso Capuano’s Summary

Creative Director with over twenty years’ design and management experience in diverse environments; magazine, newspaper, online, music and branding.

Tomaso Capuano’s Experience

  • Creative Director, The Wall Street Journal

    Dow Jones

    (Public Company; DJ; Media Production industry)

    October 2008 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Responsible for overall visual identity, redesigns, launches and management of Design, Photo and Graphics departments across the US, Europe and Asia. Successfully restructured these departments and their workflows while evolving the look of the newspaper and website. Responsible for the relaunch of The Journal's Europe and Asia Editions, and the launch of the 'Greater New York' and 'Weekend Investor' sections.
    The Journal recently became the highest-circulation daily in the US (over 2 million) – and has so far achieved seven SND awards and three Photography awards for 2009-2010.

  • Creative Director, WSJ. Magazine

    The Wall Street Journal

    (Design industry)

    March 2008 — October 2008 (8 months)

    Seconded from The Times of London to work as Creative Director on the international launch of glossy quarterly magazine WSJ. – the first magazine in the newspaper’s history – and its accompanying website. Initial print run of around 1,000,000. Responsible for visual identity, commissioning and hiring/management of Design, Photo and Production staff.
    The magazine has already stepped up to full circulation (around 2 million) and has increased in frequency to six issues per year.

  • Art Director, New Projects

    Times Online/The Times

    (Design industry)

    August 2005 — October 2008 (3 years 3 months)

    Creative lead on full redesign/relaunch of the website in 2007. Also responsible for the launch of two electronic magazines, Select and LUXX – and for the evolution of various site elements.

  • Art Director, New Projects

    The Times

    (Privately Held; Newspapers industry)

Redesign of the following sections; Times2 (daily), entire Saturday Edition (including all sections, Magazine and supplements). Launch of the following sections; The Game (football weekly), Bricks And Mortar (property weekly), The Knowledge (arts weekly), Books (weekly). Management of Art staff.
