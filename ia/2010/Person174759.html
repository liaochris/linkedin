Dr Robert Brown’s Summary

Dr Robert Brown FIEE, FInstP, MAE

(1) University of California Irvine, Bren School of Information and Computer Science.

(2) CTO at Ostendo Technologies Inc., Carlsbad, CA.

(3) Professor & Director of Nanotechnology for Northern Ireland, based at Queens University Belfast.

(4) an Executive Director of the UK Institute of Physics.

(5) Head of Opto-electronics Research and Development for Sharp Laboratories of Europe in Oxford, UK.

As Principal Scientist in the UK Ministry of Defence’s ‘Royal Signals and Radar Establishment’ I was responsible for inventing new electronic correlator, photo-detector, laser-diode, liquid crystal and optical-fiber technologies that have since been developed into successful products for experiments involving jet engines, macromolecules, submarines, and aboard the Space Shuttle three times. I hold 22 Patents and have published ~ 110 peer-reviewed research papers.

Prizes: I won the UK Defence Prize for ‘Outstanding Technology Transfer’ and a Sharp Corporation Prize for novel laser-diodes. My Institute of Physics team collected a prestigious Queen’s Award for Enterprise in the UK.

I am:

- a Consultant to various companies and government research centers in the USA and UK, and continue to serve on NASA’s Microgravity Experimental Advisory Board and the (approx Homeland Security Dep't equivalent) UK Home Office - Science and Technology Reference Committee.

- highly experienced in business in China, Japan, Russia and South Korea as well as in the USA and EU

- Special Professor at the University of Nottingham and an elected member of Academia Europaea, the European Academy of the Sciences and Arts

- co-Editor-in-Chief of CRC's ‘Handbook of Optoelectronics’ and ‘Optics and Opto-electronics’ book series

- co-Chair of 4 International Photon Correlation Conferences

- a freelance professional flutist

- a traveller - 104 countries visited so far
