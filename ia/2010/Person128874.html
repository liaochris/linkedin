Luke Brown’s Summary

I am an experienced travel advisor, guide, naturalist and avid sports lover with an unwavering passion for entrepreneurship. I am proud of my beautiful homeland Zimbabwe and will strive always to promote it's unrivaled beauty. I have also traveled and lived in many places around the world. Each place has it's own special character and it is the uniqueness of each of these that I yearn to share with others.

My goal with my partner Suzanne is to become increasingly associated with experiential travel and hospitality excellence. Together we aim to bring the purist adventures to our guests.

I believe we should have a desire to achieve and surpass the highest goals we set for ourselves. It's not easy and there are so many things to doubt along the way, but unless we keep at it we'll never know. Every other possible route is fraught with dullness and unfulfilled ambition!
