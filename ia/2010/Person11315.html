Andre Gardner’s Summary

Broadcast professional since 1977, in major markets since 1978. Experienced in all aspects of Radio Programming and formats (Classic Rock, AOR, Top 40, AC, Alternative) including positions as Air Personality, Program Director, Music Director and Production Director.
