Kirsten Womack’s Summary

Wondering what a team of Virtual Assistants can achieve for you?

The tasks that are holding you back, the tasks that are essential, but you wish you had someone take care of them so that you could concentrate on your core abilities.

Companies hire people for that, they establish infrastructure for these activities and even if the top managers don’t carry out these tasks themselves, they know that their business depends on them. That’s why they have sales executives, support staff, PR department, web division, bookkeepers, writers and designers. If you want to run a business, you need to learn to assign the right tasks to the right people. When you want to do everything yourself, you’re just doing things, not running your business. Virtual assistants do things for you so that you can run your business. Isn’t this what you want?
