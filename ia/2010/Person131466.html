Marcie Davis’s Summary

I've been recruiting 12+ years recruiting in the DC metro area. Currently, I am networking in the cleared community for technology professionals who might be interested in joining CACI now or at any point down the road. Some of the types of candidates I regularly search for are CM Specialists, DBAs, Database Developers, Software Architects, QA Specialists, Software Developers, Web Developers, Software Engineers, Software Testers, Systems Engineers (software), SOA, ERP, etc.......DoD experience and/or security clearances are highly valued!

In addition to it being my job, I just plain enjoy connecting people! If I'm not recruiting on your specific skill, there's a chance I may know someone who is, so please feel free to drop me a line!
