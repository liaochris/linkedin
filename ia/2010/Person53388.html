Debbie Lopker’s Summary

Fourteen years in the marketing, sales and event planning. With a background in hotels and corporate management, she is well versed in all aspects of selling, planning and managing a wide variety of markets and events. Her career at Morgans Hotel Group, Hyatt Hotels, Starwood Hotels, Maritz and Woodberry Events features a vast background in hotel sales, operations, public relations and marketing, while her years at Gap Inc. showcased her fine eye for detail and provided an opportunity to manage approximately 200 events annually. Combined, her experience ranges from event promotion, marketing and meetings to community affairs and global incentive programs. Debbie’s innate collaborative and creative ability to develop relationships, create out-of-the-box solutions and focus on customer service allows her to maximize her client experience while also exceeding her sales quota.

Debbie’s career transitioned to coaching and speaking in the areas of sales, marketing and image consulting. Her experience in the world of sales and business development coupled with her high energy and enthusiasm has enhanced audience participation and development. Currently, Debbie is an ambassador in the field developing, marketing and nurturing relationships with key contacts and affiliates.

Debbie resides in Northern California.
