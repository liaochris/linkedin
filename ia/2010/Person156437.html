Nick Cooper’s Summary

I have gained thorough experience designing and developing financial systems, for the investment banking/ securities and retail banking industries. My technical skills have been complemented by formal financial training and certification, coupled with exposure to projects concentrating on fixed income, interest rate derivatives and credit risk. I can offer a keenness to gather business requirements, prototype/concept-prove designs and drive through solutions to implementation. I am keen to continue to develop my interpersonal skills, having benefited from a lot of experience dealing directly with traders, business analysts and project stakeholders.

I have experience of agile development methods, including pair programming (both new development and refactoring for around two hours per day) and iterative development/small frequent releases with a CruiseControl based continuous build process. I have developed systems using TDD; designing interfaces, creating JUnit tests and then building class implementations.
