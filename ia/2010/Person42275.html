Cody Simms’s Summary

I have focused on distribution, publishing and openness at Yahoo! since August 2005. I have worked in interactive media since 1999, including positions at The New York Times on the Web (nytimes.com), Sprint PCS, NBC Internet, and Snap.com.

I am always interested in learning about new companies or trends that are redefining media and content distribution.
