Kevin Briscoe’s Summary

Briscoe PR has been operating for the past three years in southern Hampshire and has continued to grow at a steady rate with new client wins on a regular basis.

Kevin is a widely respected independent communications and media relations professional, with more than 22 years of public and private sector experience.

Clients include:
• NATS (National Air Traffic Services)
• The Rose Bowl Executive Club
• SETA - Southampton Engineering Training Agency
• Lemon Sole Seafood Restaurant
• Southampton University
• Bournemouth University
• Lloyds TSB Bank
• Komori Ltd
• New Forest District Council
• Aldershot Town Football Club
• Ministry of Defence
• Indigo Press Ltd

and many more

Kevin is also the proprietor/editor of the Western Wards Gazette, a free newspaper distributed to 14,000 homes in Locks Heath, Warsash, Park Gate, Sarisbury, Titchfield Common, Swanwick and Burridge.

The newspaper was launched in September 2009
