Brendon DeSimone’s Summary

Among the top 2% of San Francisco realtors, having sold over $100 million in residential properties over the past 10 years. Currently a top producing realtor for Paragon Real Estate Group in San Francisco. Advises a wide variety of clients through all types of residential real estate markets.

Real estate expert frequently featured on TV and in print and online articles. Appeared in dozens of Home and Garden Television (HGTV) episodes as a real estate expert for the San Francisco Bay Area as well as for other U.S. markets. Helps journalists on deadline find quotable buyers and sellers and obtain the latest market data.

A national and international real estate investor, Brendon wrote the foreword to the book, The Complete Guide to Buying a Second Home Or Real Estate in Mexico (Atlantic Publishing) in late 2009.

HGTV appearances include:

* Bang for Your Buck -- as a real estate expert reviewing homeowner renovations;

* My House is Worth What? - as a real estate expert helping homeowners understand the value of their homes in both boom and bust markets; appeared with The Apprentice 3 winner/HGTV host Kendra Todd;

* National Open House - as a real estate expert comparing home values and prices across multiple U.S. markets.

Interviewed as a local and national real estate expert on CNBC, KPIX CBS, ABC15 (Phoenix, Arizona), The Motley Fool, Yahoo! Finance, the San Francisco Chronicle, Washington Examiner, BankRate.com, Continental Airlines In-Flight, and others.

“Brendon is really the go-to-person on San Francisco real estate,” says client and San Francisco City Supervisor Bevan Dufty. “Buying and selling multiple properties in a short amount of time can be complicated and full of risk. He treated both the sale of my condo and the purchase of our new home as if they were his own. He has that rare combination of business savvy and intuition to recognize the importance of a home as an investment and a key element of your lifestyle.”
