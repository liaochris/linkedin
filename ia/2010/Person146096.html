Mike Traeger’s Summary

My current role is Director of Client Service sor Resources Global Professionals which is a spin off from Deloitte. Resources Global Professionals is a public, project-based professional services firm that was created to help businesses manage change. We create value for our clients by helping them execute their strategies on a more cost effective basis by providing them with exceptionally talented accounting, finance, internal audit, information management, HR, Supply Chain Management and Legal professionals who have been there before.

My previous role was Director of Business Development for Hudson Financial Solutions. I was awarded a Hudson Award for Outstanding Sales Performance in 2008 and 2009. Our strength is assisting financial leaders with complex issues frequently encountered during events such as Sarbanes-Oxley compliance initiatives, mergers & acquisitions, financial systems conversions, department reorganizations, internal auditing, tax compliance and during other special projects in the accounting or finance department.

In addition my management consulting background, I was a Territory Manager for W.W. Grainger and acted as the e-business champion for the Midwest region and achieved President's Club status in 2005 for sales performance.

I also posses over 12 years of experience in the inventory finance industry with Transamerica Distribution Finance (now GE). Received the Pyramid Award for divisional performance several times in the mid 1990's. Transamerica specialized in inventory finance of recreational products (marine products, snowmobiles, atv's, watercraft, etc), and consumer products (electronics, appliances, lawn and garden and agricultural products). I held roles such as Business Development Manager and Regional Field Services Manager (oversaw a department of 3 Supervisors and 40 Field Service Representatives and led the team in operational audit, collections and sales for the Western third of the United States).
