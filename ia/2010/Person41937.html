Christopher Faust’s Summary

Christopher Faust | Founder & CEO

With over 17 years experience in marketing, public relations and creative services, Chris has developed a reputation for excellence in brand development, thought leadership platforms, messaging and positioning, media and analyst relations, social media community building, content marketing, demand generation, mergers & acquisitions and crisis communications.

Prior to forming Fastlane in early 2006, Chris was the Founder & CEO of BreakThrough Communications - a boutique PR consultancy he later sold to PFS. Previously, Chris was the Head of Communications for Lincoln Financial Distributors, the wholesaling arm of Lincoln Financial Group. Prior to Lincoln Financial, Chris was the Vice President of Marketing Communications for InterWorld Corporation where he helped raise the company's profile from relative obscurity to national prominence, becoming one of the top five enterprise commerce software providers at the time. Earlier in his career, Chris was a partner with the Merritt Group. He started his career with Burson-Marsteller in NYC, which was the largest reputation management firm in the world during his tenure.

Chris holds a B.A. in Mass Communications with a Concentration in Public Relations from the University of Delaware. He is a current Board Member of the Marketing Executives Networking Group (MENG), the premiere international community of nearly 2,000 executive-level marketers who share their passion and expertise to ensure each member’s success. He is also an Advisory Board Member for TalkAhead, a leading provider of Sponsored Comments and paid messages on blogs, news sites and other online forums as well as the past President of the International Association of Business Communicators, NJ Chapter.
