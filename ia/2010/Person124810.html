Jamar Cobb-Dennard - Business Coaching’s Summary

As a man of many talents, Jamar Cobb-Dennard is an award winning salesperson, nationally recognized speaker, author, and the newest addition to the Reachmore team as the Vice President of Business Development.

After graduating from Western Michigan University, Jamar moved to Indianapolis to cut his teeth in the training and development industry with Dale Carnegie Training.

After a short time with Dale Carnegie Training, Jamar proved his sales abilities with Vector Marketing Corporation, where he was an all-time national sales leader, and then was a multiple time performance-award winner with Ryland Homes.

In the latter portion of 2007, Jamar crossed the North American continent by bicycle, and emceed a national speaking tour with www.LifeClimb.org, impacting over 10,000 youth along the way! That same year, Jamar released his first book, The Winning Mind.

In 2007 and 2008, Jamar spent his time developing a deep client base for his private coaching and speaking services, with clients such as Veolia Water, The American Lung Association, TEI Corporate, Rising Stock, Inc., and National Seminars Group. Jamar most recently developed a training and recruiting program for AllAmericanDirect.com, that reduced their product returns by 29% and increased their sales per call ratio by 40%!

Jamar is not only known for his ability to deliver fantastic results in the business development field, but he is also a master at his ability to teach effective sales strategies by engaging audiences with his vivacious energy, to-the-point instruction, and a style of story-telling that hits home.
