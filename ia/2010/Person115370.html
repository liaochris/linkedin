Kevin Craine’s Summary

Kevin Craine has 15 years experience in professional business writing and over 20 years “in the trenches” as a corporate executive, technology director and business consultant with companies such as Hughes Aircraft, BlueCross BlueShield and IKON.

Prior to starting the Craine Communications Group, Kevin was the founding editor of "Document" magazine, an internationally published technology trade publication. Kevin has written countless magazine articles, white papers and feature columns. He holds an MBA in the Management of Science and Technology, as well as a BA in Communications and Marketing.

At the helm of Craine Communications Group,. Kevin’s writing services have been used by both large organizations and small businesses alike. Known for his white papers, solution briefs and case studies, he has been engaged also to write articles, web content, press releases, and newsletters for some of North America's leading companies. He is a certified process improvement coach and an experienced project manager.

Kevin is perhaps best known as the author of two popular books: "Designing a Document Strategy" and "The Document Strategy Workbook." Both books have been sold in over 30 countries and are required text at universities in the U.S., Canada, Europe and Australia. As a result, Kevin is highly regarded as a keynote speaker and trainer, and host of the "Document Strategy Podcast" heard around the world.

His three hour video training seminar has just been released on DVD. For more information visit http://www.document-strategy.com
