Itai Talmi’s Summary

ITAI TALMI&CO.
GLOBAL BRAND DEVELOPMENT

ITAI TALMI&CO. is an independent consulting firm working for private and public companies, and social entities, on brand strategy, experience and brand potential.

I specialize in highly engaged/highly involved brands with strong presence and influence in the public sphere.
Quite simply, my role is to be a change agent.
I work with organizations on creating changes in their brands that create changes in their markets, resulting with positive changes in their business results.
Changes by:
Applying a multi-disciplinary, multi-angle approach to customer experience strategy Vs brand
Matching relevant business models to brand-customer interaction & sphere.
Forming and executing comprehensive and detailed brand strategic experience plans.
Solving brand problems & miss-matches in all customer/space/employee interactions.
Improving the brand's social and cultural relationship with customers. Invention & re-invention of brands.
Developing brand agenda & ideology.

And the bottom line is: Applicability.
Without that all of the above is pure hot air.

I'm brand & customer experience expert, biz developer, lateral thinker, and brand sphere researcher, a with more than 20 years of vast experience in brand strategy & customer experience, via consulting and managerial positions for global brands, local brands, and social brands.

Categories: entertainment, retail, service, leisure, hospitality, fashion, lifestyle, well-being, telecommunications, retail banking, food & beverage, cruise lines, NGO, Non-profit.

So, if you are a global brand, planning on going global, or you have a desire to adopt global thinking, methods, or knowledge of brand strategy to enrich and empower your people, we have a lot to share.

Some of our projects are either confidential or in development.
If you want to know more about our work and my self please contact me directly for my presentation, or for setting a meeting.
