    Hewlett-Packard

    (Public Company; HPQ; Computer Hardware industry)

    • Designed, reviewed and approved questions used for market research projects. These questions uncovered valuable insights about customer needs and usage.
    • Initiated new market research tool that achieved a 50% cost reduction vs. focus groups.

    Hewlett-Packard

    (Public Company; HPQ; Computer Hardware industry)

    • Developed a user-controlled method for installing software to match user needs and PC capabilities, which created a positive impact on customer satisfaction.
