Boris Bogatin’s Summary

Founded NearVerse in 2008, a wireless service provider enriching mobile broadband through intelligent networking and offering a platform to support a new suite of proximity-based applications and services.

Professionally pursue start-up, development, and funding of technology companies that can produce disruptive advancements across wireless networking, enabling wireless technology and applications, and various Internet service and content segments. Experience spans corporate development and technology strategy for Mobile Satellite Ventures (SkyTerra), Terrestar, Cidera, Navipath, InfoLibria, BCE Emergis, with expertise in 4G wireless services, spectrum, technologies, and applications; content distribution networks and value-added network services; and next-generation satellite-based mobile communications.

Boris Bogatin’s Specialties:

Technology strategy and development across the wireless / broader telecommunications networking stack, corporate development, complex problem solving and deal structuring
