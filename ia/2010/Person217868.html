Ava Ross’s Summary

Ava Ross is the CEO of Praise Reports, Inc. the publisher of Your Positive News International ("YPNI") Weekly Newspaper, located in Glen Burnie, MD. YPNI is a weekly newspaper that focuses on the positive, striving to educate, inform and connect readers with the latest positive news, people, events and solutions in local communities and around the world. In addition, YPNI newspaper serves as a tool to showcase work of upcoming artist and to encourage the youths in their talents. Our motto is "Bridging the gap in the community through positive news and events".

Mrs. Ross believes the community must rise together and assist in the building of each other. No business can develop without the clients and a person cannot succeed alone. It takes a united front to do so. Her approach is based on the strength of positive influence to inspire and make aware that all hope is not lost.

As a Washington Metropolitan native, Mrs. Ross thrives on giving back to the community. She provides community events, works with the youth, holds educational conferences for the community and more. Mrs. Ross also is the Founder of Avis Clark Sewell Young Journalists (A.C.S.), which is a 501(c)(3) non-profit youth journalism program that provides hands on experience for a diverse group of youth learners through education, literacy & media production. Which offers participants resources and skill development for careers in print and broadcast media. A.C.S. holds summer camps, weekly workshops, mentoring programs, partnerships and community assistance.

In addition, she is the Founder of YEMPOWER? a youth entrepreneur program that encourages youth to turn their passion and dreams into a business reality. Mrs. Ross is always focusing on ways she can assist to make a positive impact on the lives of others.
