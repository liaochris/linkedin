Andrew Greenwood’s Summary

I'm a B2B marketer and problem-solver who helps organizations create and sustain customers by pulling the right marketing levers at the right times. It's all about integrated marketing these days as your message needs to be everywhere, communicated via many channels.

Understanding business problems, that technology can solve, drives me. I love motivating people to act, or at least to want to learn more, by creating and relaying effective business driven messages.  Marketing has to have an impact on the bottom line so measuring the effectiveness of various marketing tactics, and turning up the volume on activities that bring a higher return, are key components. Luckily, I've learned when to walk away from the activities that aren't working and make quick and effective corrections.
 
I think strategically and analytically, and also like an entrepreneur, which gives me the ability to overcome roadblocks and simply get things done.

I served as a U.S. Naval Ofﬁcer for 6 years. Sailing's been 'my thing' for most of my adult life.  My Naval service, as well as competitive sailboat racing, has taught me - and continues to teach - a lot in terms of navigation for a team as well as individual achievement, so perhaps that's something that further fuels my passion to win in business.
