PROFESSIONAL ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Net Realty Advisors specializes in single tenant and shopping center net-leased commercial investment sales throughout Texas and the Southwest.

Gavin has extensive experience representing some of the most respected retail commercial owners and developers. Since 2002, Gavin has been involved in more than 325 separate transactions with combined values exceeding $500,000,000. Gavin has a solid reputation in the industry for producing results and providing the highest quality service to his clients.

Prior to entering the real estate field, Gavin worked as a financial analyst for JP Morgan on Wall Street and Morgan Stanley in Dallas.

COMMUNITY & INDUSTRY SERVICE
Gavin is a member of the International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC), Texas Real Estate Commission, and the National Association of North Texas Commercial Realtors. He is the Co-Chairman of the Dallas Jewish Federation Commercial Real Estate Division.
