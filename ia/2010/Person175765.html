Rob Walker - Xcite Digital’s Summary

Robert Walker - Young Entrepreneur & Founder of Xcite Digital - A web agency that builds world class websites.

Rob carved a place out for Xcite Digital at the beginning of the recession in 2008, and in under two years has built a 10 strong team working with international brands.

Xcite Digital works with clients such as Adobe, NHS, EDF Energy, and RCI - the largest time share exchange company in the world.

By working with a passionate team clients see the benefit of improved customer service, efficiency and delivery, backed up by cutting edge technical excellence.

Having recently been asked to provide specialist digital advice for the Guardian, Rob is now a blog writer for the Guardian online.

When not working Rob can be found Thai boxing in his local gym, or relaxing with food through his passion for cooking.
