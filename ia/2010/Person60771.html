Dustin Brewer’s Summary

I am a web designer and CSS developer in Oklahoma City, OK. I have been within the web design industry through freelance and professional experiences for nearly a decade.

I currently run a web design news blog at http://dustinbrewer.com and work freelance as a web designer based out of Oklahoma City but have worked with clients all over the world including Ireland, Scotland, UK, India, Germany, China and most US States.

I am an active advocate of web standards and I write about that on my blog as well as other design theory related topics. I am passionate about everything design.

Dustin Brewer’s Specialties:

Web design, CSS development, identity/branding, web standards adherence and functional accessibility implementation through best practices in usability.

I am also very familiar with PHP, JavaScript and AJAX implementation through those technologies.
