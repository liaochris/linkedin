Christa Fletcher’s Summary

Christa Fletcher currently works as an Associate Editor for Channel One News, a division of Alloy Media + Marketing. She writes and edits online features for and about teens.

She also conducts interviews with bands and musicians for a weekly feature in ChannelOne.com's music section Hear It Now.

Christa Fletcher has freelanced for Marie Claire, Women's eNews and The New Black Magazine. Her freelance work includes articles about women's issues, music, fashion, entertainment, pop culture and travel.

You can view her work at www.ChristaWrites.com.
