Erin Beagle, Erin.Beagle@Cybercoders.com’s Summary

CyberCoders was founded in 1999 with a single purpose – to bring the best people together with the best organizations. We started out with the name "CyberCoders" because in our early days we recruited software engineers. We soon expanded and hired some of the best recruiters in the country and have branched into all different segments of professional recruiting nationwide.

Now, CyberCoders is the premier recruiting firm in the nation. We are your source for finding a new position that is a great fit for your background. We specialize in sales, executive, technical, financial, accounting, engineering, scientific, mortgage, construction and operational positions across all industries.
