A Cheung’s Summary

Annie has been in banking, financial services, and consulting industries for over 8 years. Her main banking and financial services focus are on trading, pricing, financial analysis, benchmarking, trend analysis, forecasting, and contract negotiations in Corporate Banking, Whole Loan Trade and Securitizations. She has worked with many of the top players in the banking industry from around the world.

In addition to her background in banking and financial services, Annie also has experience and background knowledge in the management consulting, logistics and telecommunications industries as well as experience in management, change management, HR transformation, change leadership, and training/development. Having worked in both Asia and North America, Annie brings with her a diversified background and knowledge in multiple industries.

She holds Psychology degree from Northwestern University in U.S.A.. Prior to entering into consulting she worked in the financial service and banking industry in the United States.
