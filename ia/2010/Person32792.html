Carmen Karam’s Summary

Carmen Karam started her professional career since the age of 19 and she has more than 12 years experience now in the field of administration, procurement, hotels and development.

She worked for the UNDP, Électricite De France EDF & other international and multinational organizations.

She currently works for the Internal Trade Development Authority an affiliate of the Ministry of Trade & Industry. Carmen is the person in charge of all funded projects and projects with any foreign entity

She worked as a business development manager for the Mobile Payment Project at an American Company in its office in Egypt that serves the Middle East and Europe

Carmen speaks French, English and Arabic fluently. She was graduated from "Sacre-Coeur Heliopolis" French school in 1995, afterwards she studied at American University in Cairo & obtained a certificated in administration & general secretarial studies in summer 1996. In 2005 she studied Project Management Diploma at Cambridge University & currently she prepares her Masters degree in Sustainable Development at Atlantic Intertional University

Carmen has many social activities, she was a member at the Rotract since 1999 where she was Head of Club Service Committee in 2001/2002. She was the Chef de Protocol at Rotaract Club of Le Caire Champollion (only Francophone Club in Egypt) at 2006/2007 and now is a Rotarian since 2008. In addition, she a member at Member in Le Club Francophone d’Egypte since 2004

She enjoys working in a team while she can work independently

Carmen can cooperate easily with multi-cultural environment as she always worked with foreigners management and her friends/colleagues from different nationalities

She likes to learn new stuff & read about other topics & subjects

Carmen loves to help others in need and works for social & community development activities

Carmen's goals in her professional career is to learn more & share her knowledge with others
