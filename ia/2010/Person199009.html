Terri Davis’s Summary

Terri Davis is a subject matter expert in recruitment. Her expertise includes 15 years of hands-on recruiting in key roles at some of the most respected and largest recruiting companies in the world. Terri’s strengths are creative sourcing and networking: she is a master at leveraging social media, databases, associations and search engines to uncover talent, and is highly skilled at building networks.

Today, Terri owns and operates her own executive search firm, TAD Executive Search. For the past eight years, Terri has worked with a broad spectrum of clients including T-Mobile, Alcon Laboratories, Seattle Genetics, Expedia, The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, Staples, Black and Decker, Teacher Created Materials, Skyworks, Kopin Corporation and Constellations Brands.

TAD Executive Search is a boutique recruitment firm defined by its incomparable sourcing capabilities, extensive talent network and the personal touch we bring to every client and candidate interaction.

Prior to TAD, Terri held positions including Regional Professional Placement Recruiter at Volt Services Group, Branch Manager at Manpower and Group Leader/Recruiting Consultant at Adecco Employment Services. A true generalist, Terri has expertise in recruiting talent in Accounting, Finance, Administrative, Distribution, Sales, Engineering, IT and Software Technology, Biotech/Pharmaceutical, Nuclear, Energy, Utility, Oil & Gas, Government/Defense and most recently within Online and Interactive Technology fields.

Terri is a Certified Internet Recruiter (CIR) and an active member of The Society of Human Resources Management (SHRM), the American Staffing Association (ASA), the National Association of Personnel Staffing (NAPS), the Dallas Recruiters Network and the Dallas Business Executives Network.

Terri Davis’s Specialties:

Clients seek our specialist proactive, research-based executive search services for strategic appointments including the functional heads of finance, marketing, sales, information, engineering, operations, research and development, and human resources.
