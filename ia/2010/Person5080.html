Al Andersen’s Summary

Compass Consulting Team takes you and your business team on an experience in the "art of being remarkable" through doing "Great Work". We are Coach-Trainers that believe that the way to grow and develop your business is through first asking "what's possible?" Only then can you know if you are stretching yourself to do Great Work! Our workshops range from the "Purple Cow Innovation Workshop", "Developing Tribal Leadership", and "Igniting Team Power", to "Leading Change" and " Speaking to be Understood and Remembered". We help businesses put innovation, team, and authentic leadership into everything they build and everything they do to become truly noticeable and worth "remarking" about. We also spotlight local businesses on our "live" internet radio show, "THE PURPLE COWch with Jane & Al" that airs Monday mornings at 8:00 a.m. We strive to provide our customers with the most effective and coolest tools, techniques and training in the world. If you're tired of the same old, boring training program, call us! We'll get your company moving in the right direction!
