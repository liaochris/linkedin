James Ward’s Summary

James Ward has over twenty years of varied Information Technology comprehension of which the past eight years have been concentrated in the Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery discipline. Has served as a Sr. Consultant and Sr. Disaster Recovery Coordinator with responsibility in designing, developing, implementing, and maintaining Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery programs to support a rapidly growing environment. Conducted Business Impact Analysis to access the priorities of these programs. Real world experience gained in the recovery of more than 60 datacenters and their vital business functions as the result of the 9/11/01 disaster. Experienced in risk assessment, business impact analysis, and the development and testing of both systems and their associated application recovery programs. A multi-task oriented individual recognized for implementation of projects on time and to full client satisfaction.
