Scott Kisling’s Summary

I can help your team see and eliminate the causes of delay in your manufacturing or repair processes by training and coaching them in the disciplines of Lean Manufacturing, TOC (Theory of Constraints), Six Sigma and GE-style WorkOut facilitation in a series of Kaizen sessions on the shop floor.

I specialize in working with businesses that refurbish highly engineered industrial products and their components.

Office: 801.467.1287
Mobile: 801.209.3936
Email: scott@kislingconsultants.com or scott.kisling@comcast.net

Scott Kisling’s Specialties:

- Design of self-sustaining programs that incorporate Lean, TOC and Six Sigma.
- Train and mentor all organizational levels in the use of these tools and methods.
- Lead Kaizen activities in which your employees – those most familiar with your processes – are shown where to find delays and how to improve your cycle and capacity.
- Broad knowledge in the manufacture and repair of industrial equipment.
- Financial management experience (GE trained).
- Union relations experience (GE trained).
