Joe Cowan’s Summary

Throughout my career I have enjoyed being thrown into the fire of small companies and startups -- requiring little supervision while handling complex tasks in front of demanding customers. I have a proven record managing, creating, and delivering training for complex software products and have been equally successful with systems and pre/post sales engineering and professional services. My goal is to work with an enthusiastic small team in creating a great company in a position that uses a combination of my skills. I could imagine a role that combines some or all of systems engineering, training, pre-sales engineering, professional services, and marketing.
