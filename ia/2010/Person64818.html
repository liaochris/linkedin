Emmanuel Vivier’s Summary

With a double background in marketing & web technologies, I have been conceiving 360° advertising, digital and word of mouth marketing strategies for leading brands for more than 10 years now.

Speaking French, German, Spanish and soon Luxembourgish (I am learning it and yes it is a real language!!!), I am now delighted to be the managing director of Vanksen Group (http://www.vanksengroup.com) and Vanksen|Culture-Buzz (http://www.culture-buzz.com), its 360° advertising, buzz and viral agency operating in Luxembourg, Paris, Brussels, Barcelona, Hambourg, NY, Hong Kong and Geneva.

Vanksen Group provides offline and online advertising services :
- Consulting and brand strategy
- Creative concept & 360° advertising
- Media planning & buying (including SEO, banners, emailing, viral video seeding, PR 2.0)
- Brand & buzz monitoring
- Marketing 2.0, buzz, viral & digital influence training

We have an in depth expertise of digital advertising, social media, viral, buzz & word of mouth marketing (http://www.culture-buzz.com), PR2.0 (http://www.buzzparadise.com) and search engine positioning (http://www.legitiname.com).

With more than 98 experts, Vanksen is proud to assist numerous leading brands & organizations such as the Luxembourg government, l'Oréal, Louis Vuitton, Givenchy, Guerlain, Thierry Mugler, LVMH, Richemont Group, Audemars Piguet, ArcelorMittal, Bic, Sony, Nokia, Warner Bros, 20th Century Fox, Microsoft, Ubisoft, Norton, Pfizer, Orange, Citroën, Ford, Louvre Hotels,...

We are always open to new partnerships with serious firms, collaboration with freelance talents (from webdesign, to brand architects to hardcore developers), or successful commissioned sales people or e-influents to help us win new markets and satisfy new customers.

Do not hesitate to contact me (+352 621.472.610) if you need hints for doing business in France, Luxembourg,... or want to know how to create a value added online presence or improve yours,
