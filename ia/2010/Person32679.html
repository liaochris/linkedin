Carlton Forbes’s Summary

I recently graduated from Harvard University, having received a B.A. degree in Social Studies. My coursework over the past four years has included social and political theory, the sociology of race and ethnicity, international relations, human rights, and constitutional law. I have spent more than 2 years studying Mandarin Chinese, including one semester spent abroad in Shanghai, China.
