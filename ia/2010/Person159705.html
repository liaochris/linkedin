Patricia Moore’s Summary

Consultant, certified coach, teacher, author, speaker.
Working with executive and leadership teams to successfuly accelerate business results, in the areas of strategy,alignment and execution.

Patricia Moore’s Specialties:

Design and facilitation of collaborative processes for strategic visioning and planning, transition management, high performance teams, large-group processes for change. I utilize participative methodologies that increase organizatiional alignment and commitment to change. The resulting clarity and focus throughout the organization leads to faster implementation of that change.
