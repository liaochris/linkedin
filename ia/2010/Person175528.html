Robin Acker Bush’s Summary

Robin is Principal and Founder of Voices of the Earth.

Voices of the Earth, produces custom architecture and interior design materials from our specialized close-up fine art underwater photography. We team with designers and architects in the creation of living, healing and working environments inspired by nature -- businesses, healthcare facilities, public spaces, premiere luxury resorts, yachts and residences.

We reproduce our fine art nature photography in ceiling and wall coverings, columns, acoustical panels, tile, furniture, LED lit single or dual sided ultra thin light boxes, front or backlit stretched fabric panels, carved or etched glass, room dividers, animations, projections, multi-screen monitor displays, mirrors that convert to HD monitor screens, lighting and beyond.

Our mission is to bring nature into business and personal environments to foster understanding of global connection, inspire innovation, and return the timeless beauty of nature to our lives.

Voices of the Earth is seeking to work with thought and design leaders in architecture and interior design who want to define a new future by harnessing the timeless model of nature’s resilience and create designs that connect to nature’s power to focus thinking, improve health, engage collaboration, foster creativity and inspire innovation.

Our blog currently has a piece on Harnessing the Power of Differentiation for Designers and discusses how nature provides a differentiator to grow today's design businesses.

Our book, "Voices of the Earth" features our inspirational imagery. It has been awarded "Editor's Top Pick" by our publisher, Blurb.com. For a preview, and review see http://www.blurb.com/bookstore/detail/1169828.

Robin is also an award winning media producer/director of environmental documentaries and has an expertise in strategic business development.

Robin can be reached by email, listed under Contact on our website.
