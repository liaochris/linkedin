Dan Janal’s Summary

As the author of six Marketing and Public Relations (PR) books which have been translated into six languages I can help you with coaching, consulting, or mentoring on your latest PR campaign.

Please let me know if you have questions about how to do PR yourself using our great tools: PRLEADS.com puts you in touch with reporters who are writing articles and need to find sources like you! Our BullsEyePublicity.com is a database of 500,000 media professionals who may be eager to receive your press releases and pitch letters. FreePublicityTools.com gives you access to many free PR tools and resources.

I've spoken everywhere from Beijing to Budapest and have taught at Berkeley and Stanford on Internet Marketing, Publicity and Branding courses.
