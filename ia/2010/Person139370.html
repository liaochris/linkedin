Mark Metzger’s Summary

I am a "recovering programmer" who advises businesses on Internet and technology-related matters by helping them navigate the layered complexities unique to high tech legal matters.

I advise software companies on all matters related to their business, including the development of agreements of all types necessary to the operation of a software business.

I advise businesses too small to employ a general counsel on corporate, contractual and strategic matters.

I assist larger entities and institutions with negotiations of information technology-related agreements.

I provide one-off project services to school districts and the law firms that represent school districts, such as conducting hearings and performing investigations.
