Raleigh R. Pinskey’s Summary

Founded The Raleigh Group, A Visibility Marketing, Online and offline Branding and PR Company in 1980. Wrote The Zen of Hype in 1991, You Can Hype Anything in 1995, and the international best selling 101 Ways to Promote Yourself in 1997. Began speaking on Branding and PR in 1990. Over 2,600 speaking and training engagements to her credit numerous TV, radio, and print impressions. Both online and offline. Current goals are to help spead the message of global engagement through social media.
