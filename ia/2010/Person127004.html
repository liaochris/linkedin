Lloyd Bryan Molander - Adams’s Summary

BIO OF LLOYD BRYAN MOLANDER ADAMS, J.D.
Executive Producer / Filmmaker
Represented by the Gersh Agency, Lloyd Bryan Adams has over 20 years of experience as a television and film executive and is currently CEO and Executive Producer of Tenacity Pictures, LLC and Tenacity Entertainment, LLC. He is a multiple Telly Award recipient and his films have garnished accolades at many prestigious film festivals, including The Hollywood Film Festival.

Lloyd’s executive positions include his recent tenure as former Executive Producer and Sr. Executive in charge of Programming and Production for the Outdoor Channel. Lloyd was formerly the Chief Operating Officer for Extreme Group, USA, and is credited as a Co-Founder of the Extreme Sports Channel, which is now in over 55 countries. Lloyd’s endeavors at Extreme led directly to the creation of Fox’s Fuel Channel, whereby he was on the launch team from day one, he was later hired as a Sr. Consultant / Executive Producer.
Lloyd has also served as a Sr. Executive at Havoc TV, Ripe TV, Bennett Productions and Planet X Studios as well as a Partner with Fusion Entertainment. Lloyd is an active producer and his shows have run on all major networks.

Lloyd is a member of the Producers Guild of America, the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences, Lloyd accolades also include being a finalist in The Hollywood Reporters / Producer’s Guild’s Digital 50 for most influential people leading digital entertainment in Hollywood.
Lloyd holds Juris Doctor from University of the Pacific, McGeorge School of Law, and has earned a Masters Degree in Business Management from N.U. and a B.S. from San Diego State University. His studies abroad included the Inns of Court School of Law London, England, as Summer Barrister Pupilage, and Salzburg University, Austro-American Institute of Education, Salzburg, Austria, and Pepperdine University School of Law, Straus Institute for Dispute Resolution.
