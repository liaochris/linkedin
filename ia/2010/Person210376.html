Van Badham’s Summary

My experience is in cross-platform writing (of fiction, theatre, film, short film, radio drama, TV, multimedia performance), editing, teaching and mentoring (university, secondary school, special needs and EFL, adult education), cabaret performance, public speaking and promotions and artistic direction (theatre festivals, touring and performance, film festivals and entertainment events ).

My professional training has included (all by invitation): BBC TV shadow writing scheme, Royal Court Theatre/BBC writing-for-radio development programme, Inscription Australia writing for television teach-in.

My previous commissioners include: BBC radio, Wilma Theater (USA), Nabokov (UK), IPAC (Australia), Paines Plough (UK), LAMDA (UK), State Theatre Company of Bern (Switzerland), Co. Theatre Physical (Australia), Pan Macmillan (Australia), University of Wollongong (Australia).

Van Badham’s Specialties:

Adaptations, cast restrictions, genre projects, multimedia/promenade performance, multi-arts event management, publicity and promotions
