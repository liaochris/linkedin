Ashley Harris’s Summary

When I was 16 years old, I decided that for the rest of my life, I wanted to tell the world why I love music. Since then I have dipped my hands into as many different aspects of the industry as possible, first and foremost working to communicate the experiences and body of work of artists. I have worked with several hundred artists throughout my career, and I have only just begun. I feel it's important to become involved with the artist in order to really communicate what it is they are trying to say with their music in order to be truly successful. I am very progressive in my views on digital distribution of music and reaching as many people as possible while being cost effective. I have has the unique opportunity to set my own schedule for many of my jobs, and hold myself accountable for doing a great job in whatever I'm doing. I have also worked the everyday hourly jobs such as hostessing at restaurants and customer service in retail, so I also take direction very well. I can't picture my life without music, and I feel the kind of passion I have is what makes a band, a label or any music related company successful. We're all fans, after all.
