David Diaz Commercial BPO's’s Summary

Meet David Diaz

Licensed in 1996, David has made a career of selling real estate in the South Florida Marketplace. With over a decade of industry experience within several of the market’s top Real Estate companies he has engineered a business model based on with the standard business practices of yesterday and today, while adding new elements to devise a stellar innovative platform for the business of tomorrow.

Accomplishments:

Mr.Diaz, has represented Builders and Developers in the Sale of Entitled Development Sites ranging in price from fourteen million dollars to sixty four million dollars throughout the State of Florida. .

He Co-Founded ICON Commercial Funding, a Nationwide Wholesale Commercial Lending company. His position as CEO and managing partner required such responsibilities that enabled him to underwrite nearly 200 Million Dollars in Loans for Small Balance Income Producing Investment Properties in 2007.

He is the former Leasing Manager for a 400 Million Dollar Multi-Family community in located in South Beach with over 1688 Units in 2004. He was charged to Pre-Lease Residential Apartment Residences still under Development. Under his management, the property benefited from high occupancy rates and a strong Retention program designed to save operating expenses and deliver a higher Stabilized Net Operating Income.

He presently is the Director for Commercial Real Estate Operations for GE Property Management an Independant Commercial Brokerage dedicated to distressed real estate sales, property management, & valuations.

David is also active in his community, with current and past Affiliations to the Latin Builders Association, Builders Association of South Florida (BASF), REALTORS® Land Institute, REALTORS® Commercial Alliance of Greater Miami & the Beaches.

In summary, David is a consummate Real Estate Professional whose experiences afford him to offer his clients incomparable service through effective transactional structures.
