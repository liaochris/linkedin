Robert Kirsch, Ph.D.’s Summary

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE IS TO SERVE AS HIGH-CALIBER PHARMA LAB DIRECTOR/MANAGER OR CONSULTANT IN R&D, QC, QA, STABILITY LAB and/or TECHNICAL SERVICE OPERATIONS. EMPHASIS ON BOTH TECHNICAL SOUNDNESS AND QUALITY SYSTEM COMPLIANCE.

Have managed various pharmaceutical analysis laboratories with emphasis on Analytical R&D (for preclinical, IND phases I-III, NDA, ANDA for API, In-process and Drug Products) Stability Admin., Tech. Support and QC operations for 13 years. Prepared CMC and eCTD sections for NDAs, ANDAs and BLAs. Have performed API and excipient characterization and structure elucidation on many occasions.

Acquired and demonstrated expertise in cGMP, GLP, ISO, USP/BP/EP and ICH laboratory requirements, procedures, controls, guidelines and have served as trainer and lab resource in many technical areas (HPLC, GC, UV, FTIR, HPLC-MS-MS, NMR, X-Ray Diff., various wet methods per USP and EP, etc.), as well as lab administration and regulatory compliance. Have performed lab Gap analyses/audits, Mock PAI, General cGMP inspections and subsequent remediation to ensure that labs consistently provide high-quality information and to facilitate successful Regulatory inspections. Have gained microbiological and biological testing experience as well.
Experience (characterization, structure elucidation and quantitation) has been based primarily on small molecules, but also familiar with large molecule characterization and quantitation (e.g., proteins, oligonucleotides, polysaccharides, etc.) Experience has encompassed solid oral, non-sterile liquid, parenteral, single dose inhaler and transdermal dosage forms.

As mentioned above, I will consider transitioning back to fulltime technical development or quality lab management in which I have 25 years experience (while still maintaining an active consulting practice, i.e., will still accept lab consulting assignments until such time as transition occurs). Education: Ph.D. in physical organic chemistry.
