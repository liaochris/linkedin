Phyllis Cheung’s Experience

  • Founder/CEO

    My Wedding Concierge

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    November 2008 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    My Wedding Concierge is a newly formed enterprise focused on the combination of a search engine for the wedding industry and the emerging market of mobile wedding marketing while leveraging the proven marketing impact of social media tools.

    The current products focus on features that are complementary to existing wedding planning websites. The My Wedding Concierge wedding search engine and iPhone app empower today’s busy bride with power to quickly find wedding answers, whether she be surfing from the comfort of her home or on her mobile smart-phone between client meetings.

    The goal is to provide brides with a wedding search experience that will allow them to quickly identify and manage important wedding answers for their wedding day. Whether these answers be a vendor’s expert opinion, a collection of blog articles, other bridal reviews, or personal photographs, the goal is to help brides find, organize, and share their findings in a fun social facebook-like environment.

    The social community is designed to encourage wedding professionals to build relationships within the bridal community. The more resources vendors provide, the greater benefit they will provide to brides and themselves. My Wedding Concierge’s system is designed to provide brides the most relevant content for their searches – the more content a vendor provides, the better chance they will be found by brides. Vendors build credibility, trust, and a relationship by helping brides solve their wedding problems. The hope is this connection between brides and vendor breaks down the walls that might otherwise prevent brides from initiating the first contact with the vendor.
