Mr. Lozano is a software Engineer with 15 years of experience in application development including 10 years implementing Oracle Applications. His experience includes significant history leading projects in the technical and functional areas as well as using Oracle Tools and Oracle Methodologies across multiple modules in Oracle Financials, Order Management, Oracle CRM and Oracle Procurement.

His functional experience includes using Oracle Financials (AR, AP, CM, GL), Order Management (OE, INV), Oracle CRM (Service Contracts, Installed Base) and Oracle Procurement (PO).
