Rukshana Hooda’s Summary

My forthcoming Exhibition Moods
At
OUT OF THE BLUE ART SPACE LE SUTRA
25th Aug-5th Sept 2010
Moods which is a collection of art-works, Figurative & Abstracts which are in a basic linear form- full of feelings emotions and moods.
The simplicity and the interesting complexities of Life intrigue me. Feeling is desire and desire is love -FAITH Is Knowing One Of The 2 Things Will Happen~There Will Be Something Solid To Stand On OR You Will b Taught To Fly.This is a feeling Universe. If you just intellectually believe something, but you have no corresponding feeling underneath that, you don't necessarily have enough power to manifest what you want in your life. You have to feel it.
I have tried to depict the simple emotions, feelings through my paintings with nature in abundance. My Paintings show simple symbols, which are around all of us like the rising sun, moon, trees, water, rain, seasons, people, flowers, birds, the universe ,music, hence its depicting new Beginnings, a chance to re-live in the beauty & bask in the glory of nature.
Beauty has no form still I try to capture & create it on my canvas, I love LIFE, It is beautiful, the toughness of it makes you Better, though maybe some feel it breaks you, actually, i feel LIFE shows us different roads, one more brighter than the other, new, mysterious, Adventurous, however narrow or steep, nonetheless, something to learn from, something that changes you, & change is compulsory, a necessity, inevitable.These are my thoughts, my vision & This again is something I try to capture on canvas
I feel very blessed to have this opportunity to express myself through paintings. I love it when people say my artwork enhances their lives. I have been wondering if programmed thinking and focus on control prevents one from reaching their soul, as my best paintings are created when I allowed my hands, my mind, my thoughts, to flow freely. I hope that my work touches your mind, heart and soul
