Cathy Green’s Experience

  • Owner/CEO

    UpperCrust Maids, LLC

    (Hospitality industry)

    December 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    UpperCrust Maids, LLC was established in December, 2007 and quickly positioned itself as the premier house cleaning company in the Maryland, DC area. It is owned by Greg and Cathy Green.

    At present, we service Montgomery County, Prince Georges County and Washington DC, specializing in finer homes.

    Although we send in two cleaners when homes are very large or complicated in some way, we specialize in using a one cleaning technician per home approach. We find that clients are satified with and appreciate the customized attention to detail that our fully trained employees offer.

    In addition to the business, Cathy also writes consumer articles for clients and consumers in general via her blog which offers tips and advice regarding the cleaning industry and the home.

    Cathy also is a member of ARCSI, the nonprofit organization for the house cleaning industry and she is a member of the Potomac Chamber of Commerce.
