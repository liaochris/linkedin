Kristin Bentz’s Summary

Kristin Bentz is President of Talented Blonde, LLC. She also writes the consumer investing blog by the same name, www.talentedblonde.com, providing an unconventional look at consumer retail stocks. A retail industry veteran, Bentz served as an analyst in the sector for Lehman Brothers, Northeast Securities’ Independent Research Group , and Ladenburg Thalmann. Most recently, Bentz was Senior Video Correspondent at Jim Cramer's TheStreet.com, joining the company in 2007 as managing editor of RealMoney. Kristin produced the weekly segment and podcast “Random Acts of Fashion: How Style on the Street Can Make You Money, ” as well as reported breaking financial news for TheStreet.com TV. She received a BA from Arizona State University, and MBA from Fordham University's Graduate School of Business Administration. Consistently recognized for her knowledge of consumer trends and how to invest in them, she is a regular contributor on Fox News Channel and Fox Business Network, and has appeared on CNBC and Fortune’s Wall Street Week. Bentz has been featured in The New York Times, Women's Wear Daily, Glamour, and the Wall Street Journal. She was recognized by PR Week magazine as one of 2008's "New Media Faces to Watch."
