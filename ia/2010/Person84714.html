Kevin McGuire’s Summary

Leading Imperative Resources in its pursuits is Principal and Founder, Kevin McGuire. Kevin specializes in executive level placement within the sales and marketing as well as a variety of other industries. Kevin is an 25 year veteran of the recruiting industry. His career started in New York City where his tenacity, strong work-ethic and vision quickly propelled him into a leadership role. Eventually, Kevin was given responsibility for all phases of the Permanent and Temporary Divisions of his company.

Kevin McGuire has placed creative, technical, strategic and key-management teams with some of the most prestigious companies in the world. During the span of his career, he has run specialty practices addressing the needs of leaders in the Advertising, Health and Wellness, Pharmaceutical and Information Technology to name a few...

At Imperative Resources, Kevin has assembled a network of recruiters who have a level of experience, expertise and professionalism that is unparalleled. If he can't find it he will refer you to someone who will. He knows how to attract talent and is a true "head-hunter. He offers reasonable, creative cost-effective solutions.

Kevin is actively involved in community theater and a variety of charitable organizations. Most importantly, he is also the proud and extremely active parent of twin 8 year old boys,
