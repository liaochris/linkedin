Chris Hinton’s Summary

Chris is currently responsible for the design and development of the majority of media asset management and file-based video production systems in use at CNN's US production centers and bureaus around the world. Chris has, for the last 6 years, led CNN’s development efforts to create an integrated, file-based, production environment based on a services-oriented model – including the creation of an integrated digital library, integration of CNN Digital Newsgathering, the launch of CNN i-report and the expansion of digital media management into CNN bureaus worldwide.

Chris has been putting together and leading teams to design, and build, large-scale mission-critical enterprise solutions for over 15 years.

Chris Hinton’s Specialties:

Enterprise architecture. Software design and implementation. Video and content management systems. Online and mission critical technology solutions.
