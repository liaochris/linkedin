Gayle Harris-Evans’s Summary

Harris Media Direct was created to ensure that business owners and decision makers get better availability to higher end marketing and advertising services for less. I am an expert negotiator and buyer of marketing and media services who is driven to create effective advertising plans and results-driven marketing consulting for any business no matter the size. From startups to Fortune 500, I can bring my high level expertise to work for you.

What is your 2011 marketing plan? What is the message you wish to convey and to what target market? How will you get this message out with maximum results? What are the statistics for utilizing phone books, mailings, emails, TV spots, radio spots, billboards, etc.? If you currently retain marketing agencies or are in the need for specific marketing services, how do you know what fair rate you should be paying? If you do not know the answer to some of these questions then contact me and let’s sit down for a complementary meeting and let’s explore your opportunities!

Gayle Harris-Evans’s Experience

  • President

    Harris Media Direct

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2001 — Present (9 years 4 months)

    After spending years on the client side and managing numerous advertising and media agencies, the founder of Harris Media Direct knew that the traditional agency model was broken and may even be extinct, and a new model geared for flexibility and client results was taking it’s place.
    Harris Media Direct was founded on the belief that a client’s return on investment should be at the forefront of its advertising efforts, that the agency should be rewarded on the success of it’s clients and that the days of the “big agency fee’s” are gone.

    As a full-service media planning and buying resource, the team at Harris Media Direct has delivered extremely effective results to our clients with innovative and measurable advertising strategies for over 10 years.

    When you trust Harris Media Direct with your ad dollars you are receiving unparalleled dedicated experience in strategic media planning and placement. With over 20 years of working with local, regional, national and international advertisers in TV, radio, magazines, newspaper and outdoor plus all the new media forms, Harris Media Direct team has you covered.

    We’ve worked in the areas of:

    Architectural/Construction/Development, Attractions, B2B, Commercial/Residential Cleaning Equipment and Service, Events, Food/Food Service, Flooring, Health and Wellness, Home Improvement, Information Technology, Media, Medical, Nursery/Greenhouse, Recreation, Residential Home Furnishings, Retail Chains, Senior Housing/Senior Services, Transportation, and Travel and Tourism

    With companies media choices becoming more diverse, more complicated and more important than ever, Harris Media Direct can make that complexity work for you to engage your target customers more effectively and seize market share with our strategic media planning and placement services.

    We clearly deliver your message across all media platforms to reach your target demographic and achieve your targeted ROI.
