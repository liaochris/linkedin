Gregory McDonald’s Summary

President, Cloudstar Consulting Corporation

Cloudstar Consulting Corporation provides Consulting, Integration and Strategic IT Services for smart companies looking to cut IT overhead and save money by computing in the cloud.

Our mission is to help you say goodbye to your IT Department by replacing expensive IT staff and hardware with lean, scalable Cloud Computing Solutions. In today’s world, the typical “purchase, upgrade and replace” model that’s been around for decades is simply absurd- and its days are numbered.

Our approach is to acquire a personal understanding of your business, its unique requirements, and biggest challenges. With this insight, Cloudstar delivers Value by implementing Cloud Strategies that significantly lower IT costs, increase performance, and maximize ROI.

We partner with, and offer integration services for, many of the largest datacenters and service providers in the world. All of our partner facilities offer unlimited on site power generation, multiple redundant Internet links, armed security guards, and 24/7/365 on site certified engineers so you know your data is safe. Services include ironclad 100% Uptime contracts and rock solid Service Level Agreements offering unparalleled protection and peace of mind.

Call today 1.888.731.6222 today to learn how Cloudstar can free you from yesterday’s technology.
