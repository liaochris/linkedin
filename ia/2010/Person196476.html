Kim Reynolds, CMM, CMP’s Summary

Kim Reynolds, MA, CMP, CMM
For almost 15 years Kim has been producing large meetings, events and incentive trips for such large corporations as Kawasaki Motors Corp., USA, Norsk Hydro, Alliance Data, AirMiles, Loyalty Marketing Services, and Colloquy. Ms Reynolds is the founding partner of Strategic Meetings Solutions and Shooting Star Meetings & Events and brings solid business acumen to the position with a focus on identifying, tracking and measuring meaningful performance metrics. Dedicated to the meetings industry Kim has served on many industry advisory boards in addition to Meeting Professionals Dallas/ Fort Worth Chapter (MPI DFW) as VP of Education & Events, Director of Education and Director of Leadership. Ms Reynolds holds the prestigious designation of Global Certification in Meetings Management (CMM), and Certified Meeting Professional (CMP).
Ms Reynolds holds a M.A. in Management, a B.S. in Business & Management. She was awarded the Cambridge Student of the Year award for all around student and leadership for her study abroad program and has twice been nominated as Meeting Planner of Year, MPI DFW 2005 & 2009.
Strategic Meetings Solutions has just received National Woman Owned Business Certification.
Ms. Reynolds currently lives in Dallas with her husband and a very exuberant dog.
