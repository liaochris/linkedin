Elizabeth Fairleigh’s Summary

Elizabeth Fairleigh is a results-oriented, PR professional with more than two decades of communications experience in the high-tech industry. Prior to founding thE Connection, she was PR Director for two Atlanta-based technology companies, one of which filed a successful IPO during her tenure.

In addition to in-house corporate PR, Fairleigh also has several years of agency experience working for an Atlanta-based PR firm. She began her career as a journalist in Rochester, New York and is a graduate of Agnes Scott College.

Fairleigh is a recipient of the Phoenix Award, presented by the Public Relations Society of America/Georgia Chapter, recognizing excellence among public relations practitioners. She currently serves on the Board of Directors for CRMA-Atlanta www.crma-atlanta.org.

Elizabeth Fairleigh’s Specialties:

Specializes in media and analyst relations for high technology and professional services organizations, especially those in the CRM space. She also enjoys working with companies and individuals outside of technology who have a passion for what they do.
