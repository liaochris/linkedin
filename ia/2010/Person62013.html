Edmund J. Eng’s Summary

I Am a Freelance Illustrator/ Image Consultant- "Specializing in Making My Clients Look Good!"

...with nearly 30 years experience in advertising and entertainment industries working in: multimedia presentation (comps, storyboards & animatic art), various roles in print-video & film production; digital image retouch & enhancement; corporate identity and graphic design. [clients include Warner Brothers; NBC; American Express; Mercedes Benz; AT&T; Squibb; Colgate Palmolive and Dassault Falcon DX-2000 private jets...etc.]

In the recent years, I have contributed to numerous individuals and organizations to strategically develop their projects and ventures for market [for profit and not-for-profit]. Projects like: TibetAid.org preserving and promoting Tibetan culture & education (Tibetan Sponsorship Project-"Sponsor a Nun or Monk Today- Clean Up Your Karma Tomorrow"); save the Victoria Theatre, Harlem NYC; develop the Harlem Culinary Institute in the landmark CORN Exchange building, Harlem NYC; Genuine HoBo products; Give Them A Hand Foundation; venture development of Major League Cricket (MLCUS) promote organization and expansion of the second largest sport in the world- "Cricket" in the USA among other eccentric projects too numerous to mention.

I enjoy being a catalyst to help create images and incubate ideas- and love working with fun progressive people . I look forward to an invitation out to play...tel: 973-748-5458 em: eje3adore@aol.com

...Choose to DANCE.
Every day lived as a Fresh New Canvas to be painted energetically, mindfully and passionately- "Art as Life and Life is Art." I invite everyone to create themselves stunning - from the time the eyes open till the head lays down to dream.
