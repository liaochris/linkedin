Jeevan Kalanithi’s Summary

I'm a co-founder of Sifteo - we are creating hi-tech, interactive tiles for play and learning.

Previously, I worked at Taco Lab, a company I confounded that does design and technology consulting, helping companies create products centered on physical+cloud computing.

Before Taco Lab, after 5 years of working in both research and the arts in New York City, I began graduate school at the Media Lab at MIT in 2005. I completed my degree in August 2007 with the support of a National Science Foundation Graduate Fellowship.

I earned my BS at Stanford University in Symbolic Systems.
