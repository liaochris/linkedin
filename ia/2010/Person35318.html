Chad Dressler’s Summary

I have over 12 years of experience in software development, 10 years of which has been in web application development and operations. For the last 7 years I've been specializing in developing web applications for the health and welllness industry.

My current primary development environment is Ruby on Rails and MySQL, running on Mac OS in development and Linux on production.

I have experience architecting, developing, and operating cloud-hosed web applications.

I have extensive professional experience in Microsoft ASP.NET and Microsoft SQL Server. I also have professional experience developing in PHP, using the Code Igniter framework.

During my career I have worked as a developer, development manager, and technical architect.

I am a Microsoft Certified Solution Developer and a patent holder (US Patent 7308487: System and method for providing fault-tolerant remote controlled computing devices.)

Chad Dressler’s Specialties:

Ruby on Rails development, Microsoft .NET web architecture and development, web architecture and operations, cloud architecture and operations, MySQL database development, Microsoft SQL Server database development

Chad Dressler’s Experience

  • Senior Web Architect

    MeYou Health

    (Public Company; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    September 2009 — Present (9 months)

    MeYou Health's mission is to use the power of the social graph to improve people's health through connected applications and services. We are still in the early stages of product development but we will be building and delivering a set of innovative and fun products that allow users to easily and effectively support those in their network in their quest for better health.

    We will be building applications on a variety of platforms, including Facebook and iPhone. These products will be designed, marketed, and distributed in ways that are intended to encourage viral growth. To support the exponential growth of the network, these products will be powered by a core services platform which will use cloud services to transparently scale to meet demand.

    An important auxillary feature of the MeYou Health system will be the capability to measure the effectiveness of our strategies. This goal will permeate our product design and technical choices. It is very important to us that we be able to show that our system is truly helping improve the health of those who use it.

    MeYou Health is especially exciting to me because it allows me to get back to doing what I really love to do, which is building applications that people like to use. In addition, MeYou Health provides the unique opportunity to build these applications in the exciting new world of highly-scalable connected systems serving multiple millions of users.

    At MeYou Health, I am responsible for all internal IT and production web operations. In addition, I am part of the core development team.

    We use Ruby on Rails, on a MySQL backend. We host our production services on Engine Yard, a cloud platform that uses Amazon Web Services under the covers. I develop on Mac OS 10.6.
