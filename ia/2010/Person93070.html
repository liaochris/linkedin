Selling the way I like to be sold to.
Most people hate to be "sold". Years of being manipulated by greedy "salesmen" have built a resistance to the sales profession. The stereotype of the pushy salesman that only cared about his next commission check is what kept me out of sales for over a decade. Eventually, I realized that I can sell, and do it in a way that everyone wins. By focusing on the needs of potential customers, I find ways to help them. In helping people, I am able to sell and make an honest living to support my family. It all comes down to treating people the way I want to be treated.

Marketing to drive awareness, engagement, sales opportunities:
Leveraging the "four Ps" to find the right mix that provides lasting value for customers. Developing marketing channels to effectively engage the target audience and create opportunities to match the right products with the consumers that would benefit. Employing education as well as active listening to influence perceptions and uncover demand.
