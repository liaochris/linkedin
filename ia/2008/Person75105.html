Harry Falber’s Summary

Harry Falber— Global consultant in strategy, logistics, sales and marketing to CEOs including Alcoa Consumer Products, CP Kelco, Becks Beer, Novartis. Previously president of Hallmark Flowers & Gifts - the on-line retailing unit of Hallmark Cards. He has extensive domestic and global retail marketing, strategy and sales experience. He was an executive at Nabisco, Kiamos & Tooker (global grower and importer of cut flowers), Trade Area Restaurant Group, Polaroid Corporation, and 1-800-Flowers. Currently leading start-up for BU of Alcoa/Reynolds and new product development for consumer products.
