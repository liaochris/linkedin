Hilmar Tomas’s Summary

I have worked with every media used today, printed materials, radio, TV, mobile and the Internet. I have more overall experience than most of other 39’s in this business today witch give me the edge over many others in producing the same quality results, every time. These are just some of my strengths as a potential leader of media oriented company. Designing: Using all major techniques available I'm able to deliver ideas to almost any media. Marketing: Experience of making high profile (sight and sounds) presentations to groups of people. Dealing face-to-face with customers, including dealing with complaints. Experience of managing a small business and supervising staff. I can work on initiative and enjoy the stimulation of working within a team. I have experience of prioritizing my work and keeping to tight deadlines.
