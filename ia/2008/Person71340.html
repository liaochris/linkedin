Adv. Eyal Gonen (LL.B MBA)’s Summary

EG Business Attorney Service Office is harnessing the broad-based technology knowledge to the traditional law practice. EG Business Attorney Service Office acts as business advisor's and advocates for a variety of clients—from small start-up entities to international businesses. EG Business Attorney Service Office, with its combined knowledge and experience of both, law, Intellectual property and technology, provides to its clients a thorough service and advice.
Israel is a very significant market for high-tech, bio-tech and other innovation-driven industries. It is important to maintain your intellectual property rights in Israel.
EG Business Attorney Service deals mainly with intellectual property and patents. Until recently, Adv. Eyal Gonen had been working as a salaried employee for the second patent attorney office in Israel and now EG Business Attorney Service is launching a campaign to expand business by offering submission of PCT patent to national phase in Israel and other services at special, very attractive rates.

Adv. Eyal Gonen (LL.B MBA)’s Specialties:

Preventive, time saving technological-law practice, with creative thinking and competitive pricing are the key advantages of EG Business Attorney Service Office.
