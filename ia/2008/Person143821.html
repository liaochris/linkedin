Luis Hernandez’s Summary

As both a senior technologist and business professional, Luis leverages his extensive experience in the architecture, development, deployment, and integration of enterprise information systems and software to meet a diverse set of challenges for a broad range of clients.

Over the last 15 years, Luis Hernandez has been instrumental, as entrepreneur and technologist, in the development of proven e-business initiatives and award-winning web sites for Fortune 500's, leading consumer brands, and ground-breaking startups.

Now at NearSource, Luis is focused on providing contract development services to businesses, software development firms, and interactive agencies, helping clients effectively scale their enterprise by providing experienced technical resources and contract development services on-demand to meet a broad range of business goals and objectives.

NearSource also provides technology training across the Americas to develop software engineering talent in non-traditional or under-developed markets.

Luis also provides venture coaching and strategic planning services for a variety of small to medium-sized businesses and technology start-ups.

Luis Hernandez’s Specialties:

Enterprise application architecture, corporate governance, business fundamentals, venture capital, e-commerce, outsourcing, business startup, software development processes and methodology, training, consulting, public speaking, podcasting, writing
