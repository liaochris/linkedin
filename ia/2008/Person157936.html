Ricardo Del Rio’s Summary

I am member of PajamaNation, the free and global community offering microjobs (small jobs outsourced to homeworkers) for Pajamaworkers (micropreneurs working from home).
Pajamanation mission is to enable people, from every country in the world, to work from their homes and empower them to express their potential without limitation of borders and without discrimination of Race, Color, Religion, National Origin, Disability and Social Status and thus bringing better global human social order. More? Please visit www.pajamanation.com (corporate website) and www.enpijamas.com.ve (local photoblog)

I am managing at same time my own IT+HR consulting firm Chudo T&C offering web-based solutions , from VIP vendors.

I was working many years like Venezuela Country Manager for Bumeran.com, the leading Ecruitment provider of HR solutions for +2.000 latinoamerican companies, delivering resumes data base outsourcing web-based.

I have been involved also with HR and Marketing Projects, focusing Web-tools for HR/Business Managers, Training and Development, Career Development, Business Development for Entrepreneurship and WiFi devices for Market Research and Customer Services.
