Annette Vaillancourt’s Summary

Annette Vaillancourt, Ph.D. is a Renaissance woman and a successful serial entrepreneur. A shrink, a geek, a coach, a seasoned motivational speaker and corporate trainer, Dr. Vaillancourt leverages her broad array of skills and expertise to provide a practical road to success for businesses and organizations through EnterTraining Seminars.

Over the past 15 years Dr. Vaillancourt has presented over 400 workshops and teleseminars to a wide variety of audiences and industries on such topics as stress management, interpersonal communication skills, conflict resolution, spirituality in the workplace, web design and internet marketing for small businesses. Now she’s taking the show on the road.

Dr. Vaillancourt’s seminars and workshops are first and foremost packed with useful information that can be applied immediately. Her warm, engaging and facilitative style, irreverent sense of humor and quick wit make learning fun and entertaining. That’s why she prefers to call them “play-shops,” not “work-shops.”

Besides earning a Ph.D. in Psychology in 1990, Dr. Vaillancourt is also a trained business coach, has a certificate in website design and is fast becoming an expert in search engine marketing. With over 125 websites in her portfolio, she now turns her interest to search engine optimization – helping small businesses, entrepreneurs and professionals get their websites to the top of search engines.

In 2005, she was awarded the National Business Women’s Week Certificate of Recognition.
