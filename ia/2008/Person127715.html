Meg Roberts’s Summary

Meg, a student at the University of South Florida (USF) in Tampa, will be graduating in May 2008 with a B.A. in public relations and a 3.9 GPA. She was also a recipient of the prestigious School of Mass Communication scholarship for excellence in her chosen major.

Throughout her undergraduate career, Meg took pride in her coursework and took advantage of numerous opportunities to enhance her knowledge of PR, marketing, advertising, and strategic thinking.

At USF, Meg served as the Director of Member Services for the Public Relations Student Society of America, Director of Marketing and Public Affairs for Student Government, Research Director for the USF Bateman Competition team, the Public Relations Coordinator for the Panhellenic Council.

In addition to these positions, Meg also held internships at the Environmental PR Group and Baymeadows Primary Care where she gained specialized experience in government, social responsibility, and healthcare.

To learn about the field outside of the classroom and the office, Meg started her own blog where she discusses current issues in public relations and communications. Her blog, PR Interactive, now averages over 50 hits a day. Based on her posts, she was one of 50 young professionals chosen to contribute to a group blog hosted by Brazen Careerist.

After graduation, Meg is determined to continue learning about PR while benefiting her future employer with her talents in PR and communications.
