Al Chan’s Summary

Multilingual leader who performs well in diverse environments.

Over 17 years of experience on Greenhouse Gas (GHG) related issues in natural and agricultural/anthropogenic ecosystems at business, non profit, academic and governmental organization.

Supports many critical business areas including market analysis, financial analysis, strategic business planning and business development.

A vital contributor to evaluations of global emission reduction (Carbon) opportunities with experience spanning over multiple countries, GHG emission reduction platforms and carbon markets.

Focused on ecologically sound high quality emission reductions.
