Ken Shiring’s Summary

I create leading edge VLSI design processes, which create high performance, low power circuits. My background spans logic design, design verification, design confidence testing & emulation, and EDA tool design.

I am a ex-professional day-trader of foreign currencies. I specialize in mathematically characterized automated trading algorithms. I have 2.5 years of independent currency day-trading, and have production tested of 4 different quantitative and game-theory based automated trading systems. I have some long-term IP under development, which I intend to move into production in the near future.

Ken Shiring’s Specialties:

High level logic synthesis

Asynchronous digital design
High performance microprocessor design
High performance day-trading algorithms based on Information Theory, Game Theory
