Dobi Lukauskas’s Summary

Dobi Lukauskas is an entrepreneur, online marketing strategist and Google Adwords Certified Professional specializing in pay-per-click advertising, search engine optimization, web analytics and creating an experience for a website visitor with the goal of converting the visitor into a customer.

As a founder of an online marketing consulting company Marketing-that-Delivers.com he has proven success in designing and running online marketing campaigns with the focus on getting the highest return on investment for owners of e-commerce and lead generation websites around the world.
