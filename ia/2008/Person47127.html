David Mahan’s Summary

I can help organizations looking for contract or permanent employees as well as IT professionals looking for employment.
My focus is technology placement within the Tampa bay marketplace where I have lived for over 20 years.

I would like to think I am unique in what I do. Not necessarily because I am the most technical, but because my work ethic is unmatched. I am available anytime for those I serve on both sides of my business. I will not mislead to further my cause because honesty is the most important asset I have. I shoot straight, and can be trusted to do my best to make sure you know everything possible to get the job. I love what I do and pride myself in being easy to do business with.

Many companies are big, connected, and successful. I think they are defined by the individuals that represent them. I am here to re-define what a recruiter is.

David Mahan’s Specialties:

I am an IT solutions Account Executive with Tampa-based Criterion Executive Search. My Recruiters and I do a very good job, and our objective is to quickly rise to favored status as we have with all of our clients.
