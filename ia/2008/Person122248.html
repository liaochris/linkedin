Mark Lopez’s Summary

I worked in the Market Research Industry for 11 years and felt that it was time for a change. As an Executive Search Consultant my focus is on Technical Professionals from Jr. to C-Level positions. Whether with a passive or active candidate, I aim to build solid professional relationships with our candidates and have their best interests in mind.

Below is a summary of my Market Research background

• 11 years of market research experience (Taste tests, product packaging, and advertising)
• Proven ability to lead effective data collection teams and supervisory teams.
• Familiar with all aspects of market research management.
• History of increasing production and quality of research.
• Bilingual English/Spanish, read, write, and translate both languages.
• Qualitative and Quantitative market research experience
• Adept at analyzing, planning, organizing, and forecasting based on analysis of all studies
• Outstanding training, leadership, and communication skills.
• Skilled with Microsoft Office 2007, CATI

Mark Lopez’s Specialties:
