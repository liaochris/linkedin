Tish Robinson’s Summary

Advertising, public relations and corporate communications. Experienced in developing, creating and managing a corporate blog for a niche market. Currently manage 2 blog sites and a Web site. Have created several corporate magazines for Master Lock Company, including a 4/c, 18-20 page business-focused employee magazine and a 2/c, 4-6 page tabloid newsletter. Newsletters have become a specialty. At Midwest Airlines, I also wrote the Midwest Miles monthly newsletter (both print and e-mail versions), weekly customer sales e-mails, and internal mid-management newsletters.
