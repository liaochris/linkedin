M. Jeffrey Hoffman’s Summary

Jeff Hoffman is the Founder and CEO of Basho Strategies, and the primary architect of analytical sales content for Basho Sell and Basho Professional Services. Jeff focuses on the delivery of products and services to Basho's worldwide clients and the introduction cutting-edge sales strategies to the general marketplace. He serves as the head of the Basho University Curriculum Programs and the Basho Community, Basho’s large and expanding online space where sales and marketing professionals worldwide interact to learn and develop professional skills based on Basho content.

Jeff also delivers keynotes and presentations worldwide bringing targeted sales strategy to audiences of all sizes. With a 20-year record of outstanding sales achievement, Jeff founded Basho Strategies in 2002, a firm dedicated to bringing sales productivity and increased revenue to its clients, the basis for the company’s reincorporation as Basho Technologies in 2008. Prior to founding Basho, Jeff held executive sales roles at Akamai Technologies and The MathWorks where his dynamic approach to the sales process drove sales productivity and achieved record-setting revenues. Jeff is a graduate of Cornell University.
