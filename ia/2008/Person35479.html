I will be responding to all LinkedIn correspondence on Thursdays. If you feel that the matter is EXTREMELY urgent you may send me an email. Please consider that if it is that urgent LinkedIn may not be the best way to communicate with that person.

My business goal is to design a business that allows me to take 7-8 two week vacations a year. A business that allows me to make money whether I am in town or out town and whether the economy is good or bad. I've found the design for that business in Primerica.

Our mission statement or mantra is "to help families become debt free and financially independent". That means completely debt free including their home.

If you would like your own business like this please feel free to contact me for more information.
