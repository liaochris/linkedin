Ken Cavalier’s Summary

I have primarily been an educator in my previous career and like to teach and consult. I do have experience in pursuing Creditor's Remedies through the Courts for clients and I have given counsel on share ownership structures. I have considerable experience with the Federal Court of Canada and with appellate advocacy before them. In addition I have experience litigating before the Tax Court of Canada. As a former scholar in academia I have a natural affinity for legal research and consulting. My most recent publication concerned the importance of using local legal consultants familiar with the nuances of copyright law to avoid potential liability from differing national regimes. It appeared in the Winter issue of the Journal of the Copyright Society of the USA and discussed the "Lion King" copyright infringement case before the courts in South Africa.

Ken Cavalier’s Specialties:

I specialize in Copyright, Trademark and entertainment law and in the protection of intellectual property rights on the Internet. I also have considerable expertise in tax planning for capital gains realized in Canada for art and antiques and non-depreciating personal property.
