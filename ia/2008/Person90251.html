Jessica Perilla’s Summary

Jessica Perilla Design is an established design studio that specializes in website design, print design, corporate identity, digital video and illustration, catering to personal projects and small businesses, at an affordable non-industry cost.

We have a very diverse range of clients which include but are not limited to: Artists, Musicians, Colleges, Schools, Doctors, Chefs, Writers, Producers, Directors and Nutritionists.

Our work was recently published in Web Design Index by Content 3 by Günter Beer published by the Pepin Press.

Jessica Perilla’s Specialties:

Website Design, Professional Blog Design, Corporate Identity and Print Design
