Jeremy Remy’s Summary

Jeremy Ethan Remy has worked as a writing consultant and volunteer coordinator for the SLCC Community Writing Center, and a freelance writer, editor and graphic design artist with 14 years experience. In addition to extensive knowledge in a variety of writing forms and publications, he has assisted other writers in developing their skills by presenting for Westminster College, the League of Utah Writers, the Rocky Mountain Peer Tutoring Conference and the Utah Arts Festival.
