Ryan Lucia’s Summary

I am currently at eLearning Systems International. Our product, WebLessons.com, brings the Best of the Web within a focused environment to students and teachers. We are heavily involved with the US Department of Education's, Teaching American History Grant. I serve the company as the Manager of Marketing and Special Projects. We are looking to expand out of our niche in 2008.

I was with DHL Express (and Airborne Express) since I graduated from college. I have steadily worked my way from Inside Sales to District Sales Manager. Along with strong growth and sales along the way, I had some unique opportunities to help in product development and marketing. Further, I was involved with a team that developed, implemented and rolled out a SFA/CRM. Working on these side projects helped me find that I am best at developing products and ideas and driving through implementation. I look forward to continuing my growth on this path with eLearning Systems as I believe that it is best to always continue develop not only your primary and known talents, but other areas of expertise.

As a manager I have learned that it is not only your job to guide and lead your team to success, but also to learn from their strengths, knowledge and experiences.
