Daisy Whitney’s Summary

I love being a part of this fast-paced new media industry and love to meet and help others. How can I help you?

I specialize in covering Internet video, social networking, YouTube, iTunes and other forms of online and new media distribution of content. As a multimedia reporter I am one of the first journalists to launch her own online newscast that covers the business of Internet video. That is the New Media Minute and it is carried on TVWeek.com and ABCNews.com.

I am currently TelevisionWeek’s new media reporter, extensively covering broadband video, iTunes, online television, consumer-generated media, interactive television, video-on-demand and mobile programming. I also contribute on-air reports to “Tech Now,” a program produced by NBC’s KNTV in San Francisco, and host the audio podcast “This Week in Media,".

I have written for a number of publications including TelevisionWeek, Advertising Age, Shape, Business 2.0 and The Denver Post.

As an expert on new media, I have appeared on CNN’s Showbiz Tonight, CNBC’s Closing Bell and G4’s Attack of the Show and been interviewed by NPR and the Wall Street Journal. I consult on new media and am a frequent moderator for industry panels at conferences such as the Consumer Electronics Show, the National Association of Broadcasters and the National Association of Television Programming Executives.

I am fascinated by how consumer interest is changing business models for the television and media industries. I also am interested in how TV viewers decide to make the switch from cable and satellite to Internet only.
