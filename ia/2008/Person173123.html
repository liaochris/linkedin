Jeff Clark’s Summary

Super Geek always looking for The Next Big Thing. Highly interested in startups and creative projects.

After my brief internship at Walt Disney World (true story, I wore a costume and everything), I worked as a graphic designer creating ADDY Award-winning advertising, signage and collateral. Since then, I have moved into a marketing position working on user acquisition, community management and evangelism.
