Todd Hanle’s Summary

Bringing more than 25 years of experience from both corporate and agency sides, my extensive knowledge leading blue-chip brands through creative and brand strategies, creative services operations, marketing, and communications in online and offline channels is immensely beneficial to my employer companies, partners, employees and customers.

To summarize just some of the advantages I bring at a variety of levels:

- 25+ years Brand, Creative and Marketing experience at increasing levels of responsibility;
- 15+ years developing and leading dynamic teams to operational excellence;
- 15+ years serving in senior and executive level positions directly interacting and supporting global SBU stakeholders;
- Demonstrated success building Fortune 500 brands in online and offline channels;
- Documented success in process and operational management delivering tens of millions of dollars in savings and accelerating speed-to-market timelines;
- Proven leadership skills resulting in less than 2% turnover among direct reports and personnel.

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me at your earliest convenience.
