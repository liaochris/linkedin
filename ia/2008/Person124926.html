Matt Harlow’s Summary

I'm one of the luckiest people I know. I love what I do. I love the feeling when research on a new market or a product line clicks into place and it all makes sense. I enjoy the planning and the strategy and helping my clients solve their problems and increase sales.

I got involved in the American Advertising Federation to help pay back the profession I love and now I'm serving as governor of the AAF 6th District!

Matt Harlow’s Specialties:

I'm a marketing strategist. Great advertising that isn't backed by great strategy is like hanging the Mona Lisa in the attic. I help clients and creatives find the single strategic thread that every message requires and then deliver it to the customer.
