Erik Metzdorf’s Summary

Twelve years of Buying experience for Regional Sporting Goods Chain, last 10 years as Senior Buyer for all Hard Goods categories plus winter wearing acessories. On the job training, as company grew from 5 to 15 stores. Primary focus on making and meeting sales projections, as well as developing and maintaining Departmental Sku/Merchandise plans that supported the overall company's philosophical direction. Team environment with as many as four support staff.

Practical experience of organically growing categories, requiring consistant and constant pressure toward improving product quality and assortments, integrating in-the-field team members in self-initiated store marketing and sales promotion initiatives, and identifying/capitalizing on consumer purchasing trends.

Strengths include -

* High level of entrepreneurial drive toward capitalizing on new/hot product ideas and trends.
* Dogged determination toward finding the best source(s) and the best products for the best price(s).
* Extensive experience in negotiating, developing and improving relationships with factories and vendors.
* Extensive and continued attendance to industry trade shows, staying on top of emerging trends and products.
* Extensive and continued on-site company and factory visits to inspect and approve sourcing locations, quality control procedures, etc.
* On-the-ground US citizen partner, living outside of Hong Kong in China, for immediate sampling capabilities - straight to the source product development visits, as well as Quality Control FOB China
