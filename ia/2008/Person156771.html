Randy Tangco, PMP, CSM’s Summary

Goal-oriented, action-focused and self-motivated with valuable combination of success in all areas of involvement (medium to large complex projects) for the last 15 years with emphasis on excellence and results; typically leads to long term financial savings for the organization. Managed and implemented card management and switch systems (EFT and ISO8583 message formats) and other I.T. systems (banking applications and other related systems) to include VISA and Mastercard compliance activities, testing and certifications, and ATM management, installation and configuration. Capable of handling multiple large systems in different dimensions including infrastructure management and configuration, technical and business analysis, and systems/software development. Managed and supported (with knowledge and understanding of each layer; hardware, software, communications, computer operations) systems in a cross-functional environment. Conducts process improvement analysis relative to the development of a new system. Able to lead a team and communicates with various levels of people in the organization up to the executive management level.

Has working experience on card center operations and supporting its business requirements. Has functional experience in managing and running unix and oracle database servers with PL/SQL and shell (unix script) programming skills. Recently worked as project manager of two payment systems-related projects (Debit Card Management and Switch Systems Replacement, and Chip Card Migration) managing changes needed to support the new system, the internal I.T. resources, the systems integrator and the vendors, using the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) from the Project Management Institute (PMI). Certified project management professional (PMP).
