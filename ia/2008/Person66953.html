Gabriella Sep’s Summary

Philips Recruitment Services is a shared service center for all recruitment activities (for Bachelors, Masters degree and higher) within all product divisions of Philips.
As a Sourcing Specialist, I am continuously looking for candidates for Philips Consumer Lifestyle, Philips Healthcare, Philips Lighting and other departments such as Philips Corporate Staff.

My specific focus areas are:

# Marketing & Sales (Marketing Managers, Product Managers, Sales Managers, (International) Key Account Managers, Marketing Communication Managers/ Officers, Category Managers, etc.)

# Finance (Finance Managers, Business Controllers, Assistant Controllers, Internal Auditors, Financial Analysts, Junior & Senior Business Analysts, Accounting & Reporting Specialists, Management Accountants, etc.)

# Legal / Fiscal (International Tax Advisors, Tax Managers, Patent Attorneys, etc. )

# HRM (Recruiters, Senior Job Grading Officers, HR Managers, etc.)

Interested to work at one of the top 3 favourite employers in The Netherlands? Please take a look at our careersite: http://www.philips.com/careers, or contact me for any inquiries that you have.
For other functional areas such as Supply Chain, Purchasing, IT, SAP and Technology positions, I will be happy to forward your details to my colleague Sourcing Specialists or Recruiters at Philips as well.
