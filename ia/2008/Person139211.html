My professional experience and education thus far have broadened my marketing expertise - primarily in B2B marketing. I have knowledge of email marketing, online marketing as well as traditional marketing (print ads, collateral, etc.), and have worked within the health insurance and staffing industries.

Short-term goal:
To upgrade my existing skill set and knowledge by pursuing an MBA at the McCombs School of Business (The University of Texas-Austin). I intend to major in Marketing and will also take courses such as technology strategy, technology marketing and ecommerce.

Medium- and Long-term goal:
On graduation I plan to work in a marketing capacity in a corporation within the high-tech industry. SOME of the companies that interest me include Autodesk, Yahoo!, Cisco, Intel, Samsung, REI, Cargill, Genentech, and Philips.

As a first step, I have developed an informal organization - www.lighthouseffect.org - that attempts to connect people with organizations that they may not be aware of (but whose work they can relate to), as well as gives these organizations a stable presence on the World Wide Web.
