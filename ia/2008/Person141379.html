Nienke Gönning’s Summary

We’re an independent organisation working innovative and resolute in an existing market. We aim exclusively for the market of financial administrative talents and genies. Involvement with our clients and employees is our main focus and the basis of lasting relationships. It enables us to guarantee continuity of our customers financial department in order for them to maximise the results of their organisation. Vision Statement

Talents find solutions genies discover problems. The right mix of talents and genies is The key activity of Finance Interim Management is to solve every possible problem in business and public sectors.
