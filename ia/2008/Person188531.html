Chris Thomas’s Summary

Over 27 years experience in product design and development, with a strong focus on biomedical products, as well as other safety critical and scientific applications.

Roles have included technical and project leadership, systems engineering, and software & electronics design for both Fortune 500 and startup companies on products ranging from class III implantable devices, through clinical chemistry and immunodiagnostic analyzer families, to Laboratory Information Systems.

Strong emphasis on systems engineering during product development in order to ensure timely and successful project completion.

Key technical skills include Windows, web, database, embedded and real-time applications.

Well versed in the practical application of cGMP/ISO Quality System Regulations to biomedical product development.
