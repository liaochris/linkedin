Philip Kettle’s Summary

I provide interim management, designed to provide an experienced and flexible extra resource, able to get up to speed quickly. My base is in South Manchester, close to good airport, railway and motorway links, which means I am flexible on location.

As well as proven experience as a chartered accountant in finance and general management with smaller businesses and large PLCs, I have a track record of success at implementing major change.
