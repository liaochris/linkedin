Justis Peters’s Summary

Experienced IT consultant, reinventing myself as a visionary leader and innovator.

Objective: Improve the world through the application of creativity, emerging technologies, and entrepreneurship.

Accepting clients who:
- Place value on my creative thinking and involve me in solving real and important problems.
- Care more about usefulness and quality than price and timeline.
- Like the phrase, "Work smarter, not harder."
- Want 1-10 weeks of my time.
- Usually need me less than 20 hours a week.
- Allow me to delegate highly technical responsibilities to their employees, my employees, or subcontractors.
- Accept that long term support will be provided by someone other than me.

Courting angels and VCs who:
- Share my values.
- Understand that our chemistry and compatibility are as important as our profit potential.
- Will sign a fair and flexible NDA to see my ideas.
- Want a driven starter, not a marathon runner.
- Will give me a chance if I want to run the marathon, too.
- Put their money where their mouth is.

Courting employers who:
- Have an intrapreneurship program with a proven track record.
- Want to see me transition quickly from their existing initiatives to my own.
- Will tailor the position to my potential.
- Possess the same qualities I look for in angel investors (see above).
