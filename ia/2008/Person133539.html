MiShawn Williams’s Summary

As Executive Producer at a leading bi-coastal music production company, MiShawn is responsible for bringing in new business from advertising agencies nationwide such as TBWA/Chiat Day, DDB, Weiden & Kennedy and others and discovering additional branding opportunities involving music. She has researched and placed songs in commercial and film, negotiated music licensing deals and structured budgets for original music production. She has worked on commercial projects ranging from LEVI's, NIKE and Sprite to Budweiser, Mercedes, Sprint and Toyota SCION to name a few. Williams has represented artists and/or record labels in music licensing deals or arranged for artists to compose original tracks to build brand identity.

MiShawn Williams’s Specialties:

Music placement for Advertising campaigns, Film&TV, Producing for Composers, Managing Music Producers
