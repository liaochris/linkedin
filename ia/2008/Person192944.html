Jim Trotter’s Summary

- Jim Trotter has over 15 years experience in the technology, telecoms, and new media sectors. He was one of the founding members of NTL Inc. (now Virgin Media), taking leading marketing positions within the company over a 7 year period. He was responsible for much of its early marketing success and worked closely with the executive management team to create the brand that was to become the largest triple-play provider in the UK.
- Jim had an early interest in the growth of the www and was involved in the foundation of the UK’s first branded ISP’s, VirginNet and Which?Online. Jim helped create the concept of Virtual ISPs which dominated the market during the late 1990’s.
- Jim took a senior commercial role with Ericsson in 1999 using his passion for innovation to build a mobile applications division that could service Vodafone UK. He sold Ericsson’s first fully managed mobile applications service to Vodafone by creating a consortium of companies from the fledgling mobile games industry.
- In 2003, Jim moved to work within the VC and technology start-up community as an expert in sales and marketing foundation planning for early growth companies. He worked closely with the investors and management teams from Shazam, Arieso and Tel Aviv based Ki-Bi Mobile Technologies bringing a mixture of pragmatism, vision and experience to refine the business plans of each.
- Since mid 2006 Jim has been directing commercial marketing for Alcatel-Lucent’s global Vodafone account as Commercial Marketing Officer. He is married with twin boys, speaks Italian, French and Spanish and is enjoying taking on Ericsson.

Jim Trotter’s Specialties:

Commercial tactics and strategy. Planning expertise. High level problem solving. Team management. Highly numerate. Expansive network at a senior level within the telecoms and technology sectors.
