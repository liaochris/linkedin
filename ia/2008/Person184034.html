Susan Towers’s Summary

Excellent communicator, imaginative, creative marketer and results-oriented business director with international experience (and language skills) and extensive network. Effective in bringing together synergistic talent to deliver outstanding results.

• A leader, decisive, passionate, energetic, multifaceted
• Builds relationships, instigates new partnerships
• Identifies trends, conceives and develops brand strategy and direction
• Provides broad perspective as well as hands-on attention to detail
• Implements strategy, delivering on-time tangible results

Over twenty years experience in publishing, advertising, brand marketing and design. A former VP Global Communications at L'Oréal-owned retail brand, Kiehl's Since 1851, recently headed up boutique creative advertising agency with offices in NYC, London and Hong Kong. In August 2008, established NICE Partners with Susy Korb - a strategic brand consulting practice.

Brand strategy, marketing communications, media relations strategy, business development, retail experience, graphic identity, advertising, PR, etc. Production management (fashion shows as well as events / parties - e.g. Dior at the Guggenheim, Jennifer Lopez' inaugural fashion show) and B2B and consumer publishing (Fortune magazine et al) Custom book and magazine publishing (Assouline, Blakebeck, Reed, VNU)
