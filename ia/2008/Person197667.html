Vlad Lodzinski’s Summary

A pioneering and creative Media Executive; an expert in TV, iTV and video rich cross-platform entertainment formats, content & delivery; strong knowledge of the strategic, business, technical and logistical processes involved in making success in the competitive multi-platform media & TV environment. A leader: dynamic; determined; lateral thinker; motivational; visionary.

Vlad is an experienced Executive, having launched channels (creatively, operationally, commercially) for both large conglomerates (MTV, UPC) in the UK and in Europe, as well as smaller, entrepreneurial businesses. Vlad has also had a number of high profile creative roles at companies as diverse at LWT (ITV), Universal Studios and The Betting Channel (Avago). In more recent years Vlad has been working entrepreneurially with a number of new media ventures as well as acting as a consultant.

He has now taken his creative, production, entrepreneurial and operational skills to start-up LP33.tv which will go in to public Beta in the Summer of 2008.

Vlad is currently based both in London and Los Angeles.
