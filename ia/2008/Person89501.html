Jeri Kirschner’s Summary

I am a healthcare marketing and communications professional with experience in a variety of settings including: hospital, ambulatory surgery center, physican practice, Medicare Part D prescription drug plan and nurse staffing (per diem and travel nurse placement).

With the increasing importance of healthcare technology, I have transitioned to Vangent's commercial health division where I am the marketing manager.

Vangent, Inc. is a leading global provider of information management and business process outsourcing services to government, corporate, education, and healthcare entities. Our Health Solutions practice has more than 20 years experience managing award-winning customer interaction management contact centers for government agencies and the commercial healthcare market. Vangent’s clients include the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services’ Beneficiary Contact Center (1-800-MEDICARE), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s 1-800-CDC-INFO, multiple health insurance providers offering Medicare Advantage and Prescription Drug Plans, and large Pharmacy Benefit Managers supporting multiple Health Plans.

Vangent recently introduced informisTM—the healthcare industry’s only complete member interaction management solution for the Medicare market. This turnkey solution features extensive knowledge of CMS’ national Medicare program, a unique member service model and performance-based pricing accountability. Vangent was named a “Major North American Outsourcing Provider” by Gartner, Inc. in its 2007 CRM Vendor Guide. Visit www.vangent.com/informis to learn more.
