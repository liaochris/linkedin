Alysson Fergison’s Summary

I'm a budding SEO whose current strengths include on-page SEO, marketing copywriting, website/graphic design and portrait & commercial photography.

My professional experience varies widely. My career began as a Senior Portrait Photographer with Lifetouch Prestige Portraits. I was quickly promoted to Studio Manager, in addition to being solely responsible for all in-studio portraiture.

While still with Prestige, I accepted a temporary sales position with TruGreen ChemLawn (part of the ServiceMaster Family of Brands). That position quickly evolved into a permanent position as the Customer Retention Manager for a $6 million branch in Fishers, Indiana.

I moved to Jacksonville in October of 2005 help market Welcome Home Pet Sitting. While I accepted a Customer Retention Manager position with TruGreen's Jacksonville branch, I ultimately left the company in July of 2006. My primary responsibilities with Welcome Home include graphic design, website development and SEO.

In October 2006 I accepted a Customer Retention Specialist position with Web.com (formerly Website Pros, Inc.). I have since worked in three customer-facing departments at Web.com: Customer Retention, Modifications and, most recently, SEO. I have managed to maintain my "front-line employee" status with Web.com for almost two years, despite several attempts to shove me a rung higher on the corporate ladder.

Working for large companies throughout my career has provided me with invaluable knowledge and experience I could never have gained otherwise. I am thankful for the advancement opportunities that have presented themselves throughout my career, but my aspirations do not include becoming a corporate magnate.

I would prefer to head a smaller operation - one whose commitment to the client takes precedence over shareholder demands and bottom-line revenue forecasts.
