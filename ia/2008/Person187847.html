Thierry Roget’s Summary

I have been working in the telecom field for 23 years and for more than 15 years in telecom for cellular networks. Prior to working for LOTIER,

I worked in different telcos in France as head of transmission and transport activities, I also worked as technical director for a wireless local loop operator.

I have also worked for various cellular operators in the world as a senior consultant in charge of business planning.

My initial experience in SAGEM (SAT) was in the field of transmission turnkey projects both microwave and fibre optics which allowed me to gain work experience in many countries in Africa, Middle East and South America.

I fluently speak English, French, Portuguese and Spanish and understand German and Italian.
