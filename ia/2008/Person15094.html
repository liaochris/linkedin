Scott Schumacher’s Summary

All of my LinkedIn Contacts can enjoy $10 off any massage and bodywork service!

I am a 1999 graduate of the Minneapolis School of Massage's Massage Practitioner program. In addition to Esalen Swedish Massage, I have trained in Acupressure, Reflexology, Aromatherapy, Bodywraps, and Zero Balancing. I am also a Reiki Master Teacher.

In my practice as a holistic practitioner, I strive to be as environmentally conscious as I can be. I use only certified organic oils and lotions, only biodegradable laundry practices, no plastic water bottles, and discounts to those who bike, walk, or bus to their appointments.

I am interested in the human body as an earth-based energy system. I believe that not only our structural body, but our energetic body, holds the keys to many of our aches, pains, and ailments.

I am also interested in expanding my client base to actors, singers, and musicians, whose bodies need relaxation for optimal performance in the arts.

Scott Schumacher’s Specialties:

I combine a knowledge of body structure with eastern philosophies, to bring an integrated approach to hands-on healing.
