Rajib Chatterjee’s Summary

His rich and diverse industry experience spans over 14.5 years, with the past 7 years in iGATE Global Solutions Limited, Chennai. During his tenure with iGATE, Rajib has worked with various MNC’s like TNT Express, Yazaki, AIG Edison, Everest Insurance New Jersey and General Electric Cincinnati in different capacities like Delivery Manager, Engagement Manager from Onsite, Senior Project Manager and Technical Manager across the Globe. His experience in project execution has been with different domains like Logistics and Express Delivery, Life Insurance and Retail.

Rajib is a Post Graduate in Business Management from Institute of Management & Technology, Ghaziabad with Major specialisation in Systems and Operations with an aggregate of 67%. [This is a 3 years course with 30 Papers and equivalent to MBA]

He graduated in Physics, from Burdwan University, West Bengal. Apart from this Rajib has also completed with Distinction , Diploma in Information and System Management from Aptech Limited.
