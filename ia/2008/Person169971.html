Satish Kumar’s Summary

At churchill (the first company I worked in), I worked on Oracle Developer Tools like Oracle Forms, Oracle Reports, Oracle Designer, I was also involved in day to day managing of the Oracle databases we used, which included installation, performance tuning etc. The work mostly involved re-engineering of the legacy application system developed using Oracle tools,which were of 2-tier architecture (client-server) and migrating them to new version of Oracle tools into the web enabled 3-tier architecture.
At Oracle (the second company), I have worked on all Oracle tools (Forms, Reports, Designer, JDeveloper), handled the upgrade of Application server. I started in the role of Member Technical Staff, got promoted to Senior MTS and then lead a team in the role of Project Lead (Was handling the Designer and SCM team) and was selected by my QA VP and SVP to handle the Upgrade of Application server as at that time it needed strict quality control, I got promoted to Principal MTS and then to Development Manager.
I joined Impelsys as Senior Manager, Product Development to handle various web portals, I succesfully delivered all the projects and took up the role of heading the development of our niche product iPlatform for publishing technology, which enables the publishers to put their books/journals on the portal, the iPlatform suite of Products consisted of Core iPlatform Infrastructure, Book Platform, Journal Platform, E-Commerce Platform and Order Download Service.
At Impelsys my role involves
Product Planning, Product Strategy
Teams setup, Hiring, Resource Planning
Appraisals, Compensation review, Performance planning and review.

Going forward I would like to play the role of Operational Head in a Product Engineering company which will provide me an oppurtunity to utilize my people management skills, my technical skills and also allow me to play the role of a techno manager.
