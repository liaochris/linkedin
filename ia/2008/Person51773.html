Dinesh Kulchandra’s Summary

On a personal note, i am very bullish on the Growth of the Digital Space – Internet and Mobile and am Confident that with every passing hour the Importance of the Digital Space would Increase. Been witness to various ups & downs (read: boom & bust) of the dot com space and the rise & rise of Mobile marketing; its been a passion to be a facilitator of the immense potential internet has to offer to Clients keen at targeting their audiences through the world wide web.

Globally, Search Engine Marketing is a part of the overall internet advertising mix; however, for us at Communciate 2, we believe that in the years to come by, Contextual advertising shall dominate all the essential touch points where a prospective resides. Thats where the rubber meets the road: performance marketing that is the norm with us!
