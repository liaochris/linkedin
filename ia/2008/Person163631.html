Roger Godfrey’s Summary

As video games professional with 14 years of industry experience I have worked with many of the biggest players within the video games community both as programmer and, more prominently, as a Project Manager.

I have twelve years experience of managing 25+ member teams and nearly all the titles I have worked on have gained 85%+ review scores. My background is technical which provides a key advantage when coping with the complexity of modern game development.

I am a Prince2 Practitioner and a Certified Scrum Master.
