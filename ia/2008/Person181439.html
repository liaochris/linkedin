Steve Hulford’s Summary

I have worked for the last twelve years in the Technology and Media space. My passion is to create a vision, and see it through to completion and profitability. I am product focused with an eye towards the top line. I am comfortable creating business cases around the products I build, and selling them to clients who can leverage the media value.

Recently I have been a founder of Filemobile which is a business to business media management platform for media companies, agencies and brands. I am also a partner in FSN.ca & PoolExpert.com. These two sites are the largest privately owned Fantasy Sports operation in North America with over 350,000 game players.

I started my career at IBM in Marketing and Sales, before moving into interactive content production with Sportsnet.ca. During the dot com boom I spent time in San Francisco with Quokka Sports as a Broadband Producer. After the bust, I came back to Toronto where I was Vice-President of Sports at Bell Globemedia. My role there was running TSN.ca and RDS.ca two of Canada's largest sports websites.

In 1995 I Produced and Directed a 60 minute documentary called: REAL TRAVELS: 60 Days in Indonesia which aired on The Travel Channel and Outdoor Life Network.
