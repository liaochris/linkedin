Laura B. Poindexter’s Summary

Laura B. Poindexter is the principal of laura b creative, a small business marketing and graphic design studio in Sterling, Virginia that opened in 2000. She has served as a Senior Marketing Analyst for international telecom company, Equant. Prior to that, she worked in the sporting goods industry for over 7 years directing a wide variety of marketing programs from logo design to catalog photo shoots to international billboard campaigns around the world.

She specializes in low cost marketing strategies for small businesses and publishes a monthly newsletter, Marketing Buzz, exploring one low cost marketing strategy in depth each issue, bringing you the latest ideas and innovations from the trenches.

Laura has recently honed her focus to help her clients set up and produce email newsletters through her partnership with Constant Contact. She offers help with planning, design and also writing.
