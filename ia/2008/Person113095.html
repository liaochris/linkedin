Les Ottolenghi’s Summary

Les Ottolenghi: Chief Executive Officer, Digital Commerce
Mr. Ottolenghi has more than 20 years of experience as a senior executive in Fortune 500 companies and as a founder of entrepreneurial technology ventures. Ottolenghi is widely recognized as an Internet commerce and technology visionary having testified before the U.S. Senate, the Federal Trade Commission, and having advised The Whitehouse. In 2006 Ottolenghi was awarded the Innovator of the Year by the Distributed Computing Industry Association.

Most recently, Ottolenghi founded INTENT MediaWorks the largest distributor of entertainment media on the Internet. Prior to INTENT, Ottolenghi co-founded and was CEO of AgentWare, Inc., the most widely used Travel Search Engine technology with clients that include: Orbitz, Travelocity, Expedia, AAA, and American Express.

Prior to AgentWare, Ottolenghi served as the Chief Information Officer for Carlson Wagonlit Travel where he led a team that built the first web based travel retail system for travel agencies. Ottolenghi also enjoyed a successful career at Holiday Inn Worldwide, where he led the launch of the first Internet-based reservations system in the travel industry. In 1998, Information Week nominated Ottolenghi CIO of the Year.

Mr. Ottolenghi earned his Bachelor’s degree from Duke University and his M.B.A. from Emory University's Goizueta School of Business, where he received a Woodruff Fellowship and graduated Beta Gamma Sigma. Ottolenghi presently teaches part-time at the Emory University Business School on the topics of information technology based business strategies.

Les Ottolenghi’s Specialties:

A Senior Executive with more than 20 years of experience in executive management, information technology and marketing with both start-ups and Fortune 500 companies. Co-Founder of two successful Internet companies who has demonstrated the ability to set overall strategic direction and manage all major contract relationships with customers. An excellent communicator whose leadership has led to the achievement of high company market valuations.
