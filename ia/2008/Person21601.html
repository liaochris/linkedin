Ben Le Brozec’s Summary

IT Engineer with a diversified experience in web development in San Francisco, California and involvement as a team leader in e-commerce applications for the travel and tourism industry in Sophia-Antipolis, France. Now owner and CEO at LE BROZEC.

LE BROZEC was set up to create dream holidays. In the fast-paced environment in which we now live we don’t have time to organise the perfect holiday, yet this is what we need to recharge our batteries.

Our aim is to organise your personalised holiday of a lifetime, using the know-how, contacts and cultural knowledge we have gathered over our years based on the French Riviera. We want your holiday to be the ultimate in prestige and as trouble-free as possible which is why we set up a 24 hour telephone hotline service to ensure your peace of mind.

Our offices are based on the French Riviera so we are on hand to organise everything and to deal with any eventual problems. Our staff is half British / half French and completely bilingual, thus guaranteeing complete ease of communication with you and the locals. Moreover everyone on the team has a great love for this corner of paradise which they are only too willing to share with you.

For more information: www.lebrozec.com

Ben Le Brozec’s Specialties:

IT, web application development, project and people management, travel booking technologies.
