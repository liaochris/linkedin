Larry Hoffman’s Summary

Many people like you are frustrated with low returns on their safe and conservative investments. But I have good news for you… my company has a great program that can replace some of your low yielding investments with much higher rates of return from well-secured real estate notes.

My company can offer a homeowner a quick, all cash sale when the negotiated price meets our buying criteria. Then we borrow the cash needed for the deal from private investors… always leaving with a safety cushion of equity. Our private lenders get a high interest rate on a note from us, secured by a recorded lien against our property plus hazard insurance protection.

If you’re interested, I’ll be glad to show you exactly how you can profit handsomely investing in well-secured, extremely safe, low loan-to-value real estate notes. I currently offer my investors 9% to 11%. It’s a hands-free, hassle free opportunity to enjoy very attractive returns. You can decide if you want a reliable monthly income or have your interest compound for a few years for exponential growth. And you can decide when the time is right to get involved.

Let me illustrate how you can enjoy higher profits using an example of $10,000 invested for 36 months with compounded interest. First, a bank CD paying 3% will grow to $10,941 in 36 months for a gain of $941. Next, a real estate note paying 11% will grow to $13,889 after 36 months for a gain of $3,889. Now that’s 413% more money on the same amount invested!

How would you like to have some of your idle cash or retirement funds working like that for you? With a real opportunity like this I suggest you get all the details.
