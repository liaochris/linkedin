Tony Walsh’s Summary

Tony Walsh is the founder and Chief Navigator of Phantom Compass, a Toronto-based studio offering expert consulting, project planning, and early-stage development services to international clients in the digital and cross-media entertainment industries. A multi-disciplined creative, he has helped plan, design, write and illustrate compelling, narrative-driven entertainment and educational projects since 1994.
