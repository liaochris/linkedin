Jennifer Winn’s Summary

I am a religious, non-profit, and corporate event planner. I have experience booking venues of all types from hotels and churches to arenas and stadiums. I also specialize in hotel booking, travel coordination, event logistics, and onsite event management. I am based out of Dallas, TX, but plan events across the United States. I cater my event packages to meet the needs of my client. Please check out my website www.winnevents.com for more information.
