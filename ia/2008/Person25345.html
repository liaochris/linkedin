Bonnie O'Brien, PHR, CTS, CPC, CSP’s Summary

Bonnie O’Brien is a human resource professional who since has devoted her career to staffing and placement, placing thousands of people in direct hire and contract assignments.

Until 2000 she owned two Norrell Services national franchise offices which she successfully sold. She then started Placement Professionals, a contingency search and contract staffing firm, certified by the State of New Jersey as WBE (Woman Business Enterprise) and SBE (Small Business Enterprise).

From 2000 thru 2002, Ms. O’Brien was President of the NJ Staffing Alliance, a statewide trade association of over 250 companies. She has been Chair for NJSA's Legislative Committee for over 10 years and as a registered lobbyist has testified before the State government on staffing issues and appeared as an expert witness in legal cases involving employment contract litigation.

As a leader in the Staffing industry and expert in her field, she has served as a facilitator to the North Jersey Chapter of SHRM for over ten years, providing guidance to those seeking the PHR and SPHR certification. She has also given dozens of workshops and training programs sponsored by the NJ Staffing Alliance on sales and full life cycle recruiting as well re-wrote the training manual for new recruiters.

Bonnie has numerous certifications including:
PHR (SHRM)
CTS - Certified Temporary Specialist (National Association of Personnel Services)
CPC - Certified Personnel Consultant (NAPS)
CSP - Certified Staffing Professional (American Staffing Association)

She graduated with Cum Laude honors and a BS degree in Business as a Management & Marketing major from Farleigh Dickinson University, Teaneck, NJ; and Paralegal Certification from FDU. She has sat on several non-profit organization's boards, received numerous awards from both private and public organizations and has been recognized by Business New Jersey (NJBIZ) magazine in the "Business Leaders Who’s Who" for the past seven years.

Bonnie O'Brien, PHR, CTS, CPC, CSP’s Specialties:

Full Life Cycle Recruiting, Sourcing Active + Passive Candidates, Interviewing, Selection, Executive + Direct Hire Placement, Interim Staffing, HR Training, Career Counseling.

Customer base: many diverse international and local companies including: manufacturing, consumer electronics, medical devices, packaging, financial services, government, insurance and IT.
