Vernon Adams’s Experience

  • Head of Technology Architecture & Strategy

    GeoPost UK (subsidiary of La Poste Group)

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    February 1999 — Present (9 years 5 months)

    Responsible for the GeoPost UK business technology strategy and solution vision to maintain the leadership position in the UK express carrier sector.

    Headed the IT teams to consolidate GeoPost's IT solutions by exploiting leading edge web technologies. Designed a single business process framework supporting 2 UK brands (DPD & Interlink Express) to deliver significant cost savings including a rapid growth in customer web shipping and tracking.

    The core solution foundation has enabled aggressive business growth and achieved seamless integration within the GeoPost Group across Europe (inc. Germany, France and Ireland)

  • Co-Founder

    Ridge Technology Ltd

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 1998 — February 1999 (1 year 2 months)

    Co-founded the IT software consultancy to specialise in bespoke design and development of software solutions.

    Delivered leading edge inventory optimisation solutions for a Logistics consultancy in Holland. The products required flexibility for world-wide distribution including multi-ligual capabilities, integration with ERP solutions and multi-database compatibility.

  • Head of IT Development

    Parceline (UK subsidiary of Mayne Nickless, Australia)

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    February 1992 — December 1997 (5 years 11 months)

    Headed the IT teams to ignite and transform Parceline's technology solutions, delivering process excellence and cost reduction. The initiatives resulted in Parceline leading the UK parcel carrier sector with the implementation of innovations to support the parcel delivery process.

    Solutions included bar-code routing, parcel tracking and document imaging. Parceline became the first UK parcel carrier to enable web parcel tracking services and exploit hand-held technologies to enable a paperless collection & delivery process. The leading edge solutions and concepts were consequently shared within the Mayne Nickless group in Australia and Canada.
