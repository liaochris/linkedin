Martin Schmidt’s Summary

Dr. Martin Schmidt is an experienced Entrepreneur and Software Development Manager.

Martin worked with clients around the world in Europe, USA, Japan and Australia for companies such as Unilever, ABP, Dentsu, Bank of America, General Motors, Ford Motor Company, US Naval Research Laboratory, Orlando Wines, Michell and DSTO.

While in Australia, Martin managed projects for clients in Japan, Europe and Australia, including:
- Dentsu (the world’s largest brand marketing company; based in Tokyo, Japan),
- ABP (one of the world’s largest fund management companies; based in Netherlands),
- Suncorp (6th largest Australian bank),
- Intercast & Forge Pty Ltd (one of Australia's largest iron casting and forging companies),
- Michell Pty Ltd (one of Australia's leading wool processing and exporting company),
- McGuigan Simeon Wines Pty Ltd (Australia's leading privately owned wine producer),
- Orlando Wines (one of Australia's leading wine producers, owned by Pernod Richard Pacific), and
- several R&D projects (including a project for DSTO).

In addition to writing 40+ scientific research publications and several newspaper articles, Martin is also author of the applied Artificial Intelligence book “Adaptive Business Intelligence” published by Springer Verlag, and possesses knowledge of agile development using Java, C++ and VB.

Further, Martin was Adjunct at the School of Computer Science at the University of Adelaide supervising Ph.D. students and teaching Artificial Intelligence, and lecturer at Ballarat University IIBIT teaching Project Management.

Martin holds the following degrees:
- Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degree in Computer Science,
- Master of Science (M.Sc.) degree in Computer Science,
- Graduate Diploma in Business Administration (GDBA) degree and is currently finishing his
- MBA degree at University of Queensland.
