Sue Cartwright’s Summary

My name is Sue Cartwright and I specialise in delivering innovative performance solutions to business owners through mentoring and project management consultancy, staff survey facilitation and on-going business research.

My key focus is to promote the well-being and self development of individuals within large organisations and to help business owners and those just starting out in business to achieve their vision.

I have over 20 years experience in the research and development of motivational best practice, people management, business leadership, organisational culture and quality standards.

Having facilitated over 350 surveys that measure the performance and attitudes of people within organisations across a wide range of industries, I help leaders engage themselves and their people to work towards clearly defined, mutually beneficial goals and outcomes.

Sue Cartwright’s Specialties:

Business Mentoring, Networking, Quality Standard Research & Implementation, Staff Survey Facilitation, Project Management, Benchmarking, Management Training, Business Research, Writing and Self Empowerment

Sue Cartwright’s Experience

  • Director

    Unisey Limited

    (Management Consulting industry)

    August 2008 — Present (3 months)

    Unisey is a business development tool used by owner-managers for effective problem solving, planning and performance improvement.

    I work with clients to identify options and manage a project plan to implement improvements within given time constraints, resources and budgets.

    The Unisey framework is structured according to proven quality strategies and best practices that transform operational effectiveness to achieve desired outcomes.

    The Unisey format is simple – 1) one to one meeting to complete checklist and action plan, 2) provision of project plan and 3) delivery of a managed agenda to complete agreed tasks and measure progress.

    There is no time like the present to get a fix on where you are, where you are heading and what you need to do to successfully fulfil your goals. Unisey helps busy business owners gain control, save time and make money by incorporating this important discipline into their regular working practice.

  • Project Manager (Tetra People Survey)

    Online Profiling Limited

    (Human Resources industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Online Profiling Limited (OPL) is a quality performance management company providing web-based development software to organisations focused on attracting, developing and retaining talented people.

    In my role as Project Manager, I deliver the Tetra People Survey to organisations mainly in the financial, construction, engineering and building sectors. The surveys I facilitate with up to 4,500 respondents, identify strengths and issues across locations, departments, teams, job roles, gender, length of service and other demographics.

    The Tetra survey provides leaders with the opportunity to understand how best to motivate their people whilst giving employees the opportunity to play an active role in continuous improvement and business success.

    I also assist with survey development and benchamarking research to consolidate a range of common questions and other data from surveys carried out in the previous year.

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    The Black Country Enterprise Loan Fund (BCELF) is an independent trust that provides finance to new and developing businesses in the boroughs of Sandwell, Dudley, Wolverhampton and Walsall in the West Midlands.

    Through a straightforward and supported appliation process, BCELF considers loan applications of up to £10,000 from sole traders, partnerships and Limited Company / CIC directors in any secor or industry.

As well as planning and managing the project, I provided mentoring support to 7 TWIB members.

    Project 2 - Torbay Tourism Workshops delivered seasonal programmes of industry related Health & Safety training workshops to small businesses in the Torbay area. During the period we provided certificated training workshops for over 300 business owners and staff members.

    Project 3 - The Disabled Entrepreneur Project delivered specialist business advice, training needs analysis, action and business planning support to clients starting in business across the South Devon area.
