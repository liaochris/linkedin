Ross Page’s Summary

Have worked extensively in the United Kingdom, Australia and New Zealand Europe, the Middle East and Far East. Client list includes: Ericsson, Vauxhall , KPMG, Rank Industries, Zurich Insurance, MacDonald’s, Philips Industries, UK Employment Service, Allianz Cornhill, Australia Post, Westpac Banking Corporation, East Sussex County Council, Axa Insurance and Arriva Passenger Services ... and more.

Humour - bordering on ridiculous. I also like to challenge ‘participants’ (no-one gets to just sit and watch!), increasing their personal awareness, breaking personal paradigms and releasing new talent and capabilities previously hidden or untapped.

Achievements and qualifications include:
• Author of 'Everyone agrees with TOM and 'The Enlightened Response' (books, presentations / workshops/ training programmes) focussing on creating personal and professional success and the pursuit of personal excellence using a unique blend of Accelerated Learning, Personal Development and Leadership skills.
• Creator of and the En-Lightning Learning/Training concepts
• Creator of the Chain of Friends – an international cyber-space peace rally ( www.chainofriends.org )
• Six years as resident life coach at two of the world’s leading health retreats: Camp Eden and Clear Mountain in Australia
• Composed, performed and recorded ‘Pathway to the Rose’, A CD and song book containing 20 songs about human values and life skills for children and adults. Now available in thousands of schools across the UK and in six other countries.
• Graduate of 'Super Teaching', which combines the study of neuro linguistic programming (NLP), accelerated learning techniques and advanced presentation skills
• Developed the range of powerful TOM models which are currently being employed with amazing success in many of the companies listed above. These include:
En-Lightning Learning ©, Behavioural Blends ©, VISTA Goalsetting ©, and Triple A Communication ©
• Qualified masseur, Reiki practitioner
