Patrick Perelsztejn’s Summary

In the 17 last years, I developed and manage the last 4 departments I worked for. I have a wide horizontal and vertical experience with deep technology knowledge.
I managed about 180 projects some of them multi-million dollars envelope.

I have obtained throughout the years of working in various capacities such as Department Manager to the actual architecture of projects themselves a huge portfolio of knowledge in the field of Hi-Tech.

To be specific, my accomplishments are in the fields of: IT Infrastructures and Communications, system engineering, software development, software, system and network architecture, network and software application security management as well as Manager of large teams mostly in the internet realm since 1994. Take into account that I have been given the privilege of being consultant for major organizations and companies with outstanding results and lecturing to audiences of professionals in my field. The audiences have allowed me to feel their feed back as being productive and as such I was interviewed in the professional magazine of CA.

The world of management that I deal with includes manpower, budgets and time structures that are under my guidance, as well as negotiations with clients and contractors. The duty of manpower includes the capacity of selection, recruiting and organization of co team workers. Although I am in the field of management, my judgment is to make my fellow workers feel as we are a working team towards a goal. I would like to stress due to my rich and developed knowledge in my field I can follow my co workers progress with intimate and minute detail of the project at hand.

With my past three employers as well as my present position, I have been given the privilege and opportunity to create and develop the departments I, myself manage.
I find that the passion for my field of work is a learning experience which I can share with others as well as develop in the atmosphere of such professionals.
