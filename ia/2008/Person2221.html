Adam Brockett’s Summary

I have focused mainly in three industries, foodservice, labor, and now sales. I worked in a number of different restaurants starting as a prep cook at 15 and quicky becoming a line cook by 17 and managing by 22. I received my culinary training at A.W. Beattie Vo/Tech while in highschool. When I decided to take a job at Watson Standard in 2001 I was no stranger to hard work, and so the transition from food ingrediants to chemical ingrediants went smoothly. The work was good and the job was very secure, but I knew that I wanted something more. Several years after joining Watson my father and I began talking about me starting a career in sales and it seemed very natural. It has been an adventure so far and I can't thank him enough for giving me the opportunity. I have really enjoyed the experience and am looking forward to making it my life's work. I love interacting with people and welcome the challenge of "voicemail purgatory". Everyday I strive to improve my presentation, professionalism and product knowledge. I am very serious about working with my clients on any project, large or small, and providing quality service and products. Coming from a labor intensive background I am not afraid to dig in and get my hands dirty, in fact I find myself having to roll up my sleeves as I am not quite used to the dress clothes yet. I am learning everyday and I don't plan to stop. I appreciate constructive critisism and will never turn down an oppurtunity to learn something new.
