Yohan Launay’s Summary

(Executive Summary from my partners)
<< MSc from EFREI French engineering school, specialized in Algorithms & Languages, Yohan Launay has always been passionate about technology. During his master, he developped several video-surveillance software for Scutum, French leading company in video-surveillance systems. Yohan and his team have been awarded with the 'Label Ingénieur 2004' (French Engineering Award) decreed by Microsoft for one of these projects. After a few months at Lafarge / Edifixio as a Lead GUI Designer in a critical project rebuilding Lafarge logistic system, he spent his master's final year at McGill University in Montreal (Canada). He did his master project at the McGill Simulation & Design Lab in Multi-Threaded Architecture Optimization where he got an A for the quality of his job.

Back from Canada, with his partner Nicolas Mas, former VP and President of EFREI's Junior-Enterprise, he created H2I-France, think-tank whose goal is to identify, study and develop projects based on new technologies. The consulting activity, ConceptSL, is born from their analysis. >>

I'm used to work with international customers and I've already dealt with highly critical projects (Bank, insurance, security, etc.), that was the purpose of my job at Scutum and Lafarge : designing large, critical industrial software. Software now used by big private companies like banks, French Telecoms or UPS, Lafarge; but also public ones : airports, ports, townhalls, etc.

I'm very mobile : I don't mind being in some (exotic?) place for a couple of months and the next months being in another's.

I like challenges, innovation and I'm passionate about technology.

My tagline : << You don't sleep till you're not done ! >>
