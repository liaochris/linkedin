Michiel Schuurman’s Summary

I've studied graphic and typographical design for two years on the Koninklijke Academie voor Beeldende Kunsten in Den Haag before transferring to the Rietveld Academy where I graduated in 2002.

I have then been working together with Floor Wesseling in a studio called IXOPUSADA. We designed all sort of stuff but we were kind of specialised in event-design (invitations posters website programmes etc.). In 2005 I decided to work entirely for myself.

Since 2007 I really developed my own style and focus. Right now I really like designing posters and invitations.

Michiel Schuurman’s Specialties:

I'm mainly focussed on using typography as 'image'. My designs can best be described as black and white, highly typographical eye-benders.
