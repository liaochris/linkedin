Mohd Khawaja’s Summary

I prefer to include my short bio here:

Mohammad Khawaja is currently working as the deputy director of the Queen Rania Center for Entrepreneurship, and a senior project manager for the Queen Rania National Entrepreneurship Competition – The first and Technology specialized business plan competition in Jordan.

Khawaja has received his B. Sc. in communication engineering before he co-founded his business "overscore Inc." that offers compelling content management services to hundreds of clients from all over the world during the past five years.

"On campus" Magazine featured the progress of his startup business and highlighted his success story with an interview entitled: "Khawaja... Setting the standard for young entrepreneurs in Jordan" in Dec 2004 issue.

Khawaja is married with one cute little girl !
