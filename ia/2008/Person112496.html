Leila Amirsadeghi’s Summary

"Leila is a wonderful force of nature. She's a whirlwind of ideas and communication and has the amazing ability to multitask a large number of jobs at once. Leila is a charming diplomat with a sharp business sense. I would highly recommend Leila to anyone seeking a talented communicator with a powerful ability to encourage the best from her team."

As leader of the business development group, Leila is responsible for managing and growing the BLITZ portfolio. With a solid understanding of technology, creative and a passion for communication, she develops long-term brand partnerships by aligning BLITZ’S services to meet client requirements and deliver strategic, successful digital advertising and marketing campaigns.

Leila joined BLITZ after a decade of helping numerous Fortune 500 and start-up brands develop campaigns across various digital platforms and new media channels to increase business, brand exposure and market share. Her technical and creative experience comes together with a strong imagination and whole-hearted enthusiasm, bringing a fresh perspective to strategies in interactive branded experiences.

Leila has a straightforward approach her clients appreciate, combining nuts-and-bolts knowledge with strategic thinking. As one former colleague put it, “Not only does she talk the talk, she also helps build the damn thing after she sells you on it.”

A long-time burner and Black Rock City Ranger known as "Mad Chatter", Leila holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Investment & Financial Risk Management from City University Business School in London. She’s been published in various magazines across the UK, US and Middle East and is fluent in English, French and Farsi.

Leila Amirsadeghi’s Specialties:

Digital brand strategy, interactive business development and account management, consultative sales, negotiations, viral marketing, branding and strategy, interactive marketing and advertising, social media and networking, mobility.
