Peer Lawther’s Summary

Seeing the rise of the web from the inside, working my way up from an internet start-up during the dotcom boom years to overseeing the website of a medium-sized global software company. I use and appreciate the rise of new web technologies to inspire, create, market and communicate to our customers whether new or old.

Whether it's using Flickr to highlight our latest endeavours, Youtube to promote and diseminate our promotional videos or Wordpress to power our blog, I have a skilled knowledge of, and confidence in, the web from its early years that I've put to good use in getting a company's message out into the wider world.

In future years I'd like to be seen as an online marketing consultant for businesses wanting to establish within the online arena, helping them get results using social web networks, and established as a thought-leader on how the internet is revolutionising the way we do business and present ourselves.

Peer Lawther’s Specialties:

Successful online marketing on a small budget, knowledge of CAN-SPAM and EU email legislation, online customer care, online promotion using customers to evangelise, confidence in talking to an online audience of millions, and affiliate and SEO marketing.
