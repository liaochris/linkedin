Harry Aldridge’s Summary

Harry is Founder & Managing Director of Bluenowhere, a municipal wireless network operator which has developed a business proposition that utilises a dual layer WiMAX and Wi-Fi architecture to enable high value services to be provided on a wholesale basis over the network. Our business model makes it possible to serve multiple markets over a single infrastructure and is fully commercially sustainable, not requiring council subsidy or financial commitment from an anchor tenant. Both WiMAX and Wi-Fi layers are architected for maximum quality and functionality and are mutually supportive in driving revenue over the network.

Bluenowhere has also developed a plan for deploying an alternative wholesale access infrastructure to the existing last mile copper outside of urban areas, using WiMAX technology, for the provision of broadband, VoIP telephony and IPTV/VoD “triple-play” services. This allows service providers to capture the whole market and eliminate the revenue deficit from customers with restricted product availability. It also facilitates service bundling to reduce churn and boost ARPU, in addition to increasing the economies of scale of content provision, in effect acting as an LLU extension to non-urban markets.

A member of the Mobile WiMAX Acceleration Group, leading a major showcase demonstrating the end-to-end supply chain for WiMAX and exploring the business case for mobile WiMAX in the UK.

Bluenowhere also provides a range of consultancy services for local authorities, service providers, and other businesses and organisations seeking to understand the wireless communications market. Some of the areas in which we can assist are:

- Spectrum Auctions/Releases (particularly the UK 2.5GHz – 2.69GHz auction in 2008)
- Business Planning & Modelling
- Network Planning & Design
- Equipment Vendor Selection
- Municipal Network Business Case Development for Local Authorities
