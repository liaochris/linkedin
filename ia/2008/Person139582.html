Neil Gordon’s Summary

I have been in the window covering business for over 20 years. My business Decorating with Fabric serves both the residential and commercial markets.

Among our many clients are Architects, Designers, Facility Managers, Building Managers, General Contractors, Schools, Hospitals, Nursing Homes, Hotels, Restaurants and many other commercial and residential clients.

My experience has been in all aspects of creating window coverings. With design, fabrication, installation and educational experience, there is very little that I have not done in the industry.

Our products support buildings that are working toward LEED Certification. We work with architects, designers and owners in achieving improved Daylighting for work environments.

Neil Gordon’s Specialties:

My company specializes in:
Leed Certified Projects that require Window Coverings, State-of-the-art motorization, Eco-Friendly Fabrics, including a large selection of PVC Free and 100% recyclable fabrics for solar shading.
