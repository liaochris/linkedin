Jennifer Messinger, RN, BSN, COHN-S, CCM’s Summary

Jennifer Messinger, RN, BSN, COHN-S, CCM is the Executive Vice President of The ALZASA Consulting Group, a safety, occupational health and workers’ compensation issues consulting firm.

Jennifer began her medical carrier as an EMT and has spent over a decade in acute and cardiac nursing settings, including emergency medicine prior to moving to occupational health, a specialty she continues to maintain an active practice in. A Certified Occupational Health Nurse (COHN-S) and Certified Case Manager (CCM), Jennifer also holds the OSHA 10-Hour General Industry card and has ICS 100 training.

In addition to her work with The ALZASA Group, Jennifer is also the Program Director for The Ryan Alert Program, named in honor of her son, The Ryan Alert Program is a non-profit which assists pre-hospital EMS providers with indentifying and planning for the emergency needs of special needs children in their communities.
