Naeem Zafar’s Summary

Expert in the Silicon Valley startup process. Defining market opportunity, fundraising, business plan development and building teams. 24-years of experience in mentoring entrepreneurs, starting companies and scaling them for success. Domain experience in the electronics systems, semiconductor, EDA software, biometric security, web 2.0 and enterprise software industries. Worked at six startups, CEO for three of them.

Currently teaching Entrepreneurship at Haas School of Business at University of California Berkeley.

Partner at Concordia Ventures (www.ConcordiaVentures.com) , an organization for mentoring entrepreneurs, advising companies and coaching CEOs.

Naeem Zafar’s Specialties:

Method of entrepreneurship {opportunity identification, business plan development, raising money, building teams and forming companies}. International expansion and channel development. Coaching CEOs in all aspects of running their business.
Teaching. Marketing. Product development.
