Bahar Gidwani’s Summary

What I offer:
Broad management skills: Finance, technology, strategy, business development, and sales--I've managed them all.
Leadership: I'm smart, patient, technically savvy, hard-working. A mediator. Able to find a third choice between two strongly-held positions. Frank, concise, clear.
Energy: Started my first business in the third grade (selling goldfish). Haven't stopped looking for new ways to make money and succeed, since.

What I want:
A good team: I don't need a lot of help. However, I'd like to share the load with one or two senior people who know what they are doing and have a few more smart people who can move things forward, fast.
Upside: My bias is towards equity and long-term upside. I'd like as much room to grow, as possible--even if it means some risk of failure.
A higher purpose: I want to build a company that makes a difference--one that improves the world or the lives of the people in it.
