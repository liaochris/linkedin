Tracy Tedesco’s Summary

Tracy is a nationally touring comedian working across the US for the past 5 years as the Urban Cougar Comedian. She offers savage, sexy social commentary for the urban singles ghetto crowd.

Although she didn't start in the comedy circuit until her late 30's, Tracy had no problem breaking into the business. Her punchy, conversational style and larger than life personality made for a natural transition to stage. Tracy quickly started working around the country, building a strong reputation for keeping the audience laughing with her hip, urban act. Ten minutes with Tracy is like sitting in on cocktail hour with the girls from Sex & the City.

In addition, she’s done two military tours, and survived to speak of it, entertaining the troops in Europe in 2004 in the “Leon and Tracy Comedy in the Med Tour,” with headliner Leon Rogers. In 2006, she traveled to Japan with the “BET Comedy All Stars”, and performed at Misawa and Yokota Air Bases. Since 2004, Tracy has been producing and performing across the country with Les Funny Femmes- Queens of Comedy. The Funny Femmes enjoyed a successful run at one of the Chicago's biggest nightclubs, Joe's Bar on Weed St in 2004, and in May 2006, the Femmes toured Washington and Oregon. In 2007, she unleashed the Urban Cougar Comedy Tour across America.

In February of 2006, Tracy was featured in the Red Eye www.redeyechicago.com, a
Tribune publication, in an article about Urban Cougars, women who date younger men. While she was thrilled with being called a Cougar, her friends persisted in referring to her as an Old Cheetah. The staff from Geraldo at Large, www.geraldoatlarge.com, saw the article and featured her in their 3/1/06 segment on Cougars, which was aired on Fox News and CBS affiliates nationwide. In 2007,
Tracy was featured on WE’s Secret Lives of Women, www.we.tv/uploads/SecretLives, a critically acclaimed documentary series, on
their episode on Cougars.

Stand up comedy, political satire, corporate humor.
