    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Design industry)

    March 2001 — Present (6 years 11 months)

    With Mutations I have created sites for various companies. I have a corporate background as a web and interaction designer but my aim is to use the lessons learned in the commercial world on websites for non profit organisations, particularly in the arts.

    Right now I am working with some other specialists on transforming the site for one of the oldest and most well-known theatres in Holland, Theater de Kleine Komedie. With this redesign we will bring the latest technologies and features to their website, transforming it from a basic performance listing to an interactive website with features like cross-selling, personal recommendations, visitor feedback, multimedia and a weblog where performing artists can talk about their experience at the theatre.

    We want to create websites with similar features for other arts organisations at a low cost. For more info, check out www.mutations.nl.
