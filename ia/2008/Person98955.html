Joseph Rogel’s Summary

I am a Solution Sales professional who enjoys and values the customer relationship part of my job and selling bleeding edge technology. Having worked for several Microsoft Gold Partners in the past 8 years, I thrive on the constant change that technology brings to organizations.

My prior professional History with Government funded Employment & Training Programs include skills in individual assessment, hiring, recruitment, job development, program planning and development, to include program design for a 100+ employee organization that created one of the first Employment & Training "One Stops" nationwide.
