Terri Starnes-Bryant’s Summary

I have been in the Vending industry for over 20 years. I see the need for Cashless technology in the industry to help operators protect their money and equipment. I teamed up with Microtronic Electronic Systems out of Switzerland after seeing their unbelievable technology options at a few trade shows. The company I previously worked for is a Vending company and we purchased some of the Microtronic equipment for his best accounts. I was in charge of programming and determining how every possible configuration could be accomplished. That is when I made the move to actively pursue a distributorship with Switzerland. I was able to form a partnership with them in October of last year and now selling and training this technology throughout the United States. My goal is now to get the word out to all Vending operators about how advanced our system is over our competition.

Terri Starnes-Bryant’s Specialties:

Selling and training of the Microtronic Electronic System software and hardware. It is an intense, yet easy to learn program that allows options of Access Control, Time/Attendance, Copier, Laundry, Parking Garages, ID Badges, Tanning Salons, all into a microchip base media.
