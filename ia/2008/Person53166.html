Don Carter’s Summary

Don is an accomplished leader with over 15 years of increasingly responsible Human Resource experience, including 10 years in HR management roles with United Airlines and Motorola. He possesses strong HR business partnering skills and has worked with a number of departments over his career including product development, engineering, manufacturing, field sales and marketing among others.

Don has been an HR practitioner, consultant and outsourced HR service provider to various industry sectors including high tech/electronics, travel & hospitality, internet services, FMCG, professional services, petrochemical and pharmaceutical among others. He is experienced and skilled in various HR disciplines, with particular emphasis in the areas of employee relations, performance management, talent acquisition and retention, compensation, employee communications organizational and leadership development.

Don also has an international Human Resources management background to include multinational HR management, HR consulting and agency executive search and recruitment experience. In addition, Don was founder of two Asia start-up HR firms - one of which was named “Best HR Outsourcing Firm” in China for 2004 and twice nominated for “Best HR Consulting Firm in China” for 2003 and 2004 by CCH Asia and China Staff Magazine.

Don is an honorably discharged U.S. Army Reserve Captain and he currently resides in the Washington D.C. area.
