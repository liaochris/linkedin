Rosie Kay’s Summary

Rosie Kay Dance Company is fast gaining a National and International reputation as one of the most exciting and cutting edge dance theatre company's in the UK. Talented choreographer Rosie Kay set up the company in 1994 after several year of experience as a dancer and freelance choreographer in Poland, Germany and France.

This year has been outstanding, with the unique sell out show 'The Wild Party' that became a hit at the Edinburgh Festival, winning 5 stars and critical acclaim. This amazing show combined dancers and actors on stage with a live jazz band who joined in the action. Rosie Kay has also been commissioned to create 'Trapped' for Ludus Dance Company and was choreographer for the 5 star Barbican alternative christmas show 'The Class Club' by Duckie. This show had three seperate restuarants, 'Working Class' 'Middle Class' and 'Upper Class' and combined actors, opera singers and contemporary dancers for a unique dinner-tainment experience.

Rosie Kay Dance Company aims to expand in the future and begin an International touring network and gain wider audiences. A new production is in early development, and the future is looking exciting!

Rosie Kay’s Specialties:

Dance training and teaching (all levels, speicalities in teaching disabled children, young people with mental health issues, undergratuates at University Degree level and professional)
Choreography for all types of work, especially dance/theatre and dance in opera.
Dance shows for corporate events- an alternative entertainment package for companies that want something memorable.
