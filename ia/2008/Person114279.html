Lisa Capezzuoli’s Summary

I was born in Tuscany, Italy on February 25th, 1982. In 2005 I have successfully achieved an honours diploma in Graphic Design at the European Institute of Design in Rome. In 2008 I studied at SVA in NY.
In July 2004 I studied at the University of Canberra, Australia, as an exchange student and I also had a work experience at the FCB in Sydney with the Executive Creative Director M.Simons.
In 2000 I won a grant for an English course in Oxford. In 2002 I lived and worked in Germany for six months. I can speak and write German and English.

I'm currently working for the award international company NICE ltd in New York. I have followed projects for clients such as: P&G, Max Factor, Covergirl, D&G, Kraft, Puma, Pantene, Dunhill, Venus and Satin Care.

Few prints on cinema magazines, graphic for an architecture book, and events catalogs. Since 2005 I have been working as a freelancer for Area Strategic Design, an international company in Rome, together with the Creative Director S.Aureli.
In particular I have projected brand identity analysis and systems for clients such as: Italian Post; Italidea (a project for the next Olympic games show in Beijing 2008); FIVB Men’s World Championship Italy 2010; Enpam
(a national public medical company); Sara assicurazioni (an international insurance agency); Aedes Immobiliare spa (an international property agency); Unaprol (a national oil association); SCS Francoforte (German company); Gruppo Leonardo Caltagirone (an international construction company).

Further I worked 2 years in Rome for “Modo Comunicazione”, an advertising company following the project for “Artwo” association,
which produces design objects created by contemporary artists.
In particular I have worked with clients such as: Metro C spa; the exhibition “Domenico Modugno 2008”; Boeing - Italy; Minister of Environment, Artwo’s exhibitions at the national design fairs and a project for winter Olympic games in Turin 2006.
