Jon Powell’s Summary

Over six years of experience in environmental consultancy, specializing in contaminated land and real estate transaction projects. Directed project/field management tasks and the delivery of projects from proposal to reporting stages. Oversaw design and implementation of on-site intrusive investigations and interpretation of environmental site investigations for a variety of industrial, commercial, and residential sites, with particular emphasis in the oil and gas, gasworks and rail industries. In addition, reviewed risk analysis based on third party environmental reports to underpin environmental liabilities for risk management plans.
