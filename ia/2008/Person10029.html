Ana ADI’s Summary

I am a Public Relations consultant with extensive experience in multicultural and multinational environments gained working in the US, UK, Belgium and Romania. I am also a polyglot, proficient in English, Spanish, French and Romanian which means I can make the same copy, press releases, brochures, presentations, websites, concepts and not only appeal to half of the world. I work well under pressure and work hard to deliver the results you want.
