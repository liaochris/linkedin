Douglass Dixon’s Summary

I help small green businesses get new clients. This could be through sales or social networking or through coaching. My specialty is to create new opportunities over the phone.

Let's face it calling can be intimidating even to people you know or where you have a mutual acquaintance. It's important that you find a way to call where you are comfortable. With a little help this can happen quickly.

Of course once you are at ease there is more to learn. You may be asking yourself who do I call, what do I say, how do I capture a prospect's attention, how do I follow up convincingly so that influencers help me or so that prospects buy? Telephoning should be about the phone being your friend. There is a right way to do it. Done right calling is a powerful marketing tool because it gives you direct contact with the people you need to reach.


The last 14 years my experience is with telesales and coaching. Previously I had a long history as an educator. A huge part of the life is also about learning how to go beyond limits with spiritual work. That dove tails into a commitment to support cooperative sustainable communities, especially ones sensitive to how we eat affects the earth and the quality of our lives.
