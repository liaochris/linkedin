K S Ahluwalia’s Summary

K S eighteen years of business experience has spanned sales, operations, logistics, consulting and management roles. He is an experienced organization development consultant with primary expertise in leadership assessment and performance management.

KS also has expertise in directing and providing consulting services in areas such as: executive assessment, performance management, career transition, change management, leadership development, succession management, and business restructuring. His ability to partner with senior leadership, adapt well to change, and collaborate with people throughout an organization has allowed him to build productive relationships and solutions that have had a positive impact on the bottom line for numerous corporations.

• Presently has facilitated over 5.6 million man-hours in consulting space with apex corporations, across 9 industries, 8 management levels and at 23 locations. In addition has coached and mentored students and academia for over 1.3 million man hours.

Coaching Experience

K S is an experienced coach, helping primarily new and under-performing leaders to gain a better understanding of their strengths and developmental needs based on formal assessment tools. He has helped leaders in various ways that include building key internal relationships, improving business acumen, incorporating an effective management style, establishing a healthy work-life balance and improving time management skills.

K S works extensively with academia and students in professional institutions, where he assists in transforming them in arenas of goal setting, relationship management and adolescence challenges.

K S Ahluwalia’s Specialties:

He has used his expertise to serve clients in varied industries, including insurance, pharmaceuticals, financial services, retail, banking, manufacturing, IT & ITES, healthcare, oil and power.
