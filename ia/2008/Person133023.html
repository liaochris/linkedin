Milissa Rick’s Summary

Currently, as Director of Research at Sway, I manage the Social Media Map (SMM) product and develop proprietary research reports and tools for Sway. The SMM is a research-based plan for how to effectively target customers in an online environment. We identify where customers are spending time online, how they behave online, what motivates them, what influences them, and how to effectively reach them. Recent brands/companies that I have done Maps for include: Fiskars Gardening Products, Stabil, Start Your Engines!, Garage DOGG, Anastasia Marie Laboratories, TravelWisconsin.com, and Wisconsin Dells Visitors and Convention Bureau.

As Director of the Wisconsin Innovation Service Center at the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater, I assisted over 2,500 innovative manufacturers, technology businesses, and entrepreneurs with making more profitable product and market development decisions. WISC serves clients from a wide range of industries providing detailed research reports on new product feasibility, competitive intelligence, licensing/strategic partnering, distributor assessments, and customer satisfaction. In addition as Director of WISC, I oversaw the business development strategies for the Center, and I was part of the business management team at the Center for Business and Innovation Development, which acts as a gateway to the resources of UW-Whitewater.

I have been a marketing consultant for over twelve years, most recently working as a research consultant for a full-service, market research firm Chamberlain Research, in Madison, WI. I have worked with major companies including SC Johnson, Johnson Diversey, Kraft, Kimberly Clark, Capital One, Wachovia, and Potawatomi Bingo and Casino.

During my career, I have taught a multitude of workshops on new product development and early stage market research, as well as several undergraduate courses at UW-Whitewater on Marketing Research, Marketing Principles, and Product Policy.
