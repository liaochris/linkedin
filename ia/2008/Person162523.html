Owen Roberts’s Summary

I am a Project Manager/Senior Analyst with over thirty years experience in a wide range of business functions and environments, dealing with all levels of staff from International Directors of Global companies through to shop floor/front-line staff within, an extensive span of industry sectors.
I have worked as both a permanent member of staff and as a contracted temporary team member, in both cases my attitude remains objective oriented striving to meet the goals set in the time and manner set, whilst maintaining the appropriate level of rigor.
I have worked in the finance sector (Insurance and Banking) for some 15 years, 10 in Insurance; 5 in Banking.
I have also worked in the Business Consultancy sector for 6 years, and latterly I have worked within the Pharmaceuticals Industry for 4 years.

*An experienced Project Manager, including recruitment; staff development; & user training.
*An ability to identify, analyse, and solve business problems, and then implement the solution in a timely manner.
*An objective orientated approach to business problems and their solutions.
*Strong interpersonal skills, established by developing good one to one relationships, with users at all levels & IT staff.
*A business aligned outlook on the I.T. function, providing a practical view of technology.
