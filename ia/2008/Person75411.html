Carolyn Hayes’s Summary

I am President of a small technical recruiting and search firm. While we are based in Maryland and Oregon, we search locally and nationally for high level and hard-to-find technical positions.

We are a contingency recruiter (clients pay only when we've done our job) and perform both direct hire and contract searches for our clients.

My goal is to build a base of clients who think of me first when they have a technical staffing need. If you are a small firm needing great additions to keep your project and business plan moving forward - I can help.
