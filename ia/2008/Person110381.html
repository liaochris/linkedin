Lance Hoffman’s Summary

Dynamic leader and successful professional with in-depth global experience in executive management, marketing and all aspects of e-commerce. Online marketing expertise in defining, executing and measuring the effectiveness of direct programs including email marketing, site optimization, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), viral marketing, web analytics, A/B multi-variate testing, and Comparison Shopping Engines (CSE). Skilled and accomplished in product marketing, product management, strategic planning, branding, positioning, advertising, PR, analyst relations and building productive teams in diverse and complex environments. Respected executive, team builder, mentor, leader, colleague and business partner.

Lance Hoffman’s Specialties:

Ecommerce, online marketing, International expansion, account management, product marketing, and product management.
