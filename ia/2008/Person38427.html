Carol Lynn Martens’s Summary

Over 18 years experience in DbM with expertise leading cross-functional teams in organizations from start-up to F500.

Drawn to multi-channel business models and the rigor of database-driven marketing.

Develop intelligence from primary + secondary sources to fuel predictive models and insights - from segmentation schema (consumer and enterprise), lifecycles, contact strategies, to behavioral data and personae.

In an era when data is increasingly dynamic and can be captured across an array of interactions, the challenge becomes identifying datapoints which are predictive, actionable, and scalable.

Expertise in full complement of marketing mix:
- Product to Brand
- Client + Agency + Production experience
- Co-marketing, Affiliate + Channel
- Online > Offline integration
- Lead generation > Conversion > Retention/Development
- Unica, Oracle, Siebel, Ephiphany, Crystal Reports, SAS, SQL, Omniture

Industry background: Technology - Hardware + Software, Internet, Marketing/Advertising Services, Travel, Financial Services (banking + brokerage), Publishing, Education, Telecomm

Carol Lynn Martens’s Specialties:

Design + build marketing databases (CRM/iCRM is one facet) from new systems to legacy upgrades; then develop organizational infrastructure, processes, and marketing programs which drive engagement, facilitate dialog and build relationships and communities.

Intelligence applied in new ways fuels breakthrough thinking and creative solutions. My objective is to spark and enable that process, by empowering the best of each team member's talents and abilities.

Thought leader. Change-agent.
