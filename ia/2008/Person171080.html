Scott Jost’s Summary

With 18 years studying, teaching and practicing product design and innovation and a dual MBA in Marketing Management and Corporate Strategy, Scott Jost is equally fluent in both left and right-brained dialects. His area of focus is consumer packaged goods branding and packaging consulting, where he has spent the last 10 years of his career applying design process methodologies to benefit his clients and their brands.

He currently leads the design and innovation division of the US’ largest stocking supplier of rigid packaging. In a few short years he has led the Studio from a 2-person internal service capability to nationally recognized 8 person industrial design / brand strategy firm in a purpose-built Chicago loop loft space. The procedures and operations processes he developed and implemented have since earned the division ISO 9001:2000 certification.

Scott’s brand strategy / industrial design client list spans categories from food and beverage to personal care to medical consumables, and includes such firms / brands as The Coca-Cola Company, Procter & Gamble. Gillette / Oral-B, Pfizer, Kraft Foods, Nestle, Miller Brewing, Reckitt Benckiser, Warner Lambert, Frito-Lay, Novartis, Medtronic, Johnson & Johnson, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Monsanto and Baxter Healthcare.

Scott has been quoted in numerous industry publications, including Sci-Tech Today, Brand Packaging, Packaging World, Packaging Digest, Package Printing and Cosmetics & Personal Care.

A frequent speaker on the subject of CPG design innovation, Scott holds numerous US design patents, and is a member of the Industrial Design Society of America, the Institute of Packaging Professionals, and the American Marketing Association.
