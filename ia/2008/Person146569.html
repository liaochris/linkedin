Deepak Malhotra’s Summary

Intellectual Property attorney, registered to practice before U.S. Patent and Trademark Office with Electrical Engineering undergrad and over 20 years experience in Intellectual Property Law. Drafted complex patent applications and prosecuted them to allowance at the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office. Prepare and prosecute trademark applications. Have a network of associates around the globe to assist U.S. clients in filing abroad, and assist foreign clients in filing in the U.S. I accept a limited number of project for clients who are willing to pay competitive rates for high quality work, and I give them my utmost attention.

I also assist companies whose patents or trademarks are being infringed in vigorously enforcing their rights. Through associations with contingency fee or conventional fee litigators, I help IP holders with enforcement.

I have the highest possible peer review rating, AV, by Martindale-Hubbell for quality and ethics.

I am also a real estate investor with over 40 units of residential real estate in the Spokane, Rapid City, Austin, Cabo San Lucas Mexico, Paris France, and Grand Cayman areas, and encourage others to get involved with real estate investing to benefit from long term low interest leverage.

Deepak Malhotra’s Specialties:

Software patents, business method patents, EE patents, mechanical patents, trademarks.
