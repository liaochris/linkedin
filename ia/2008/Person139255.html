Neal Fullman’s Summary

15 years' International marketing & comms (consultancy & client-side) providing C-level strategy & counsel to digital, mobile telecoms, broadcast & consumer brands.

Relocated with wife, children & tortoise to Israel Aug 07 & joined fring, an award winning mobile internet mash-up of communication, community & content.

Responsible for global consumer, trade & corp comms strategy and EMEA & US marketing partner business development.

I manage digital & traditional influencer & media relationships globally with a self-styled network of agencies in territories as far afield as London, NY, Johannesburg & Finland.

Recent achievements incl fring's iPhone app launch; over 100,000 new active users in 12 hrs & ranked as AppStore's No1 free download worldwide.

I have detailed working knowledge & appreciation of communication strategies, commercial structures & partnership models across a variety of digital platforms, mobile, broadcast, online & social networks.

Prior to fring, held senior roles at several global agencies, devising & implementing domestic & international campaigns, managing large multi-lingual, multi-national teams across multiple markets.

Brand experience includes Sony Ericsson, O2, BT, Associated Newspapers, CNN/Turner Broadcasting, Reuters & a variety of P&G, Unilever & other fmcg brands.

Having seen both sides of the agency/client fence, as a client I now have intimate appreciation & first hand insight into why agencies get it so wrong, so often! Client-side has been both challenging & rewarding and added an invaluable new perspective to my career.

Cool things about Israel: Palm trees in the back garden, sun 10 months of the year & the incredibly fast paced, entrepreneurial, can-do spirit that pervades the digital industry here; a week working in the Israeli hi tech sector is like a year anywhere else!

Things I miss about the UK: walking the dogs on a crisp, foggy October morning, great sausages from Borough Market. And Xmas.
