Ken Schmitt’s Summary

Ken has been placing mid and senior level financial professionals in San Diego for nearly 10 years. A native of San Diego, he offers a unique understanding of the local marketplace, its diverse industry base and business culture.

Ken’s vision of building a world class search firm materialized in mid-2007 when he was faced with the difficult decision of joining another search firm as a partner, or starting his own firm. It became evident that his talent for recruiting, networking, and strategy development was best suited for a new style of search firm. By combining his 6+ years of experience working for local and national contingent firms with his 3 ½ years at Heidrick & Struggles, a global retained executive search firm, Ken has created a truly unique organization.

TurningPoint Executive Search combines direct recruiting, customized and targeted searching, and the delivery of real-time market intelligence with the flexibility of contingent fee contracts.
