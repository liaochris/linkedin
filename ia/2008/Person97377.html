Jose Arocha’s Summary

I create new internet products that empower markets and communities. And I do it by teaming up with good people with vision, talent and passion to make things happen. This is what we do and who we are at LaborFair.com See my LaborFair description below.

Before joining Laborfair last June 2007, I teamed up with Thomas Goddard to help the Global Health Education Consortium build GlobalHealthEdu.org, a new online space with rich media and 2.0 capabilities for students, faculty and practitioners to learn, participate and collaborate in global health education. More than 100 experts are creating and delivering digital courses wrapped with wikis and forums to build learning communities around them.

During the academic years 2004-06, I worked as fellow and research scholar of the Reuters Digital Vision program at Stanford University. I researched and prototyped digital, social and portable media solutions and created organizational models for these to work in emerging markets in Latin America.

My experience spans the areas of online marketplace design, semantic indexing and search, product design and innovation, international R&D consulting, technological competitive intelligence, knowledge mining, business development and entrepreneurship in Latin America, web2.0 marketing, digital and social media.
