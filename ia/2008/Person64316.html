  • Owner, President

    Bonafrux Organics

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Food Production industry)

    March 2003 — Present (4 years 11 months)

    Bonafrux Organics is an organic tropical food producer and exporter.
    We work with R&D bodies, researching new techniques on sustainable and ecological farming and processing.
    Now Bonafrux is developing a joint work with one of the biggest cities in the state of CE, which main objectives are:
    - To process fruits from the
    mango, banana and cashew native areas.
    - To act on the proposition of R&D Projects to
    develop new organic farming knowledge.
    - To create up to 700 new direct workplaces in three years, increasing the local small-scale farmer families incomes.
    - To create 50 new small-scale family farm businesses in three years.
    - To qualify small-scale farmers to work with Organic Farming.
    - To preserve the public health, by avoiding the use of agrotoxics.
    - To set up a new freeze-drying industry able to supply
    up to 240 tons of product yearly.
    - To strengh the organics and nutraceuticals production and export chain of the city.
