Michael Weickert’s Summary

Primary Goal: Creating solutions to unmet medical needs that provide substantial benefits to patients and rewards for investors and stakeholders.

For 16 years, Dr. Weickert has held leadership positions in product development, marketing and strategic planning at a variety of biotech, pharma and drug delivery companies. As Chief Business Officer of StrataGent Life Sciences, Inc., Dr. Weickert managed their recent merger with Corium International, Inc., as well as all Marketing, Finance and Product planning. At Nektar Therapeutics, Dr. Weickert led proprietary and partnered product development efforts including leading engagements with the FDA and EMEA as Senior Program Executive. As Senior Director of Product Marketing, he initiated and led the anti-infectives business unit, New Product Planning, Product Marketing, and Portfolio Management, and collaborated with Business Development on product partnering efforts in the US, EU, and Japan. Dr. Weickert served as the global project manager for Targretin Phase III and US and EU registration at Ligand Pharmaceuticals, and Project leader for a Phase I/II product at Somatogen, Inc. Prior to joining industry, Dr. Weickert conducted gene regulation research at the National Cancer Institute of the NIH. Dr. Weickert received his Ph.D. in Genetics from the University of Wisconsin. He is the lead inventor on several pharmaceutical patents and has published dozens of peer-reviewed scientific papers in leading journals such as PNAS, J. Biol. Chem., J. Mol. Biol., Gene, and Current Opinion in Biotechnology. He has presented extensively both research findings and methods for Project and Portfolio Management, where he has also chaired international meetings and sessions.
