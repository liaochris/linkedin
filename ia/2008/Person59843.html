Eric Brown’s Summary

Eric Brown has a proven record as an entrepreneur and multi-talented professional. On any given job he brings to the table such diverse skills as project management, creative direction, writing, strategy, and more. Mr. Brown’s background includes 20 years of experience in print and design, 12 years in interactive media, and 20 years in leadership and training.

Mr. Brown has recently led WeirdGuy Creative Solutions Inc. from identity design to "creative" consulting in such areas as brand and resource development, leadership development, and training content development. His clients have included Rockwell-Collins, CDC Software, Hilton Hotels, Chick-fil-A, Sea Ray Boats, GeoLearning, and Washington State Dept. of Health and Social Services to name a few. He is ardent about "fun learning"—challenging those in the leadership and learning to move from “information to application.” Mr. Brown often says, "Change can only come about by applying what we learn. From the C-level to the Line-level, knowledge without application causes stagnation. Things either start to stink at the top or at the bottom."

Mr. Brown was a member of the In Touch team for nearly 17 years. He was a founding member of the Marketing department where he designed collateral for the entire organization. His drive allowed him to move to Art Director and later to Creative Director. After six years he started the online brand for In Touch.

As Director of Interactive Media, Mr. Brown coordinated multiple Webcasts, started the youth brands, and developed the organization's eLearning strategic initiative. His team-oriented leadership and ability to deliver earned him a promotion as the youngest VP, where he then led the International, training Institute, and Product Development departments while serving as a member of the Executive Leadership Team. His vision was to train people globally through relevant blended-learning experiences with an emphasis on accountability and personal application.
