Chrisanne (Dagit) Sternal’s Summary

Chrisanne Sternal is a marketing consultant with 10+ years experience, creating and executing marketing campaigns. She's worked to develop email campaigns, direct mail, websites, brochures, newsletters, online advertising, search engine marketing, search engine optimization and more. She's also done a lot of work to manage databases, segment different target audiences, test marketing and track results.

Chrisanne started out as a Graphic Designer but after a few years she realized that creating marketing strategies might be a better fit for her personality since she was always asking "why" are we doing this or that marketing tactic. So Chrisanne spent many years as an ad agency Account Manager and Marketing Manager. She worked with companies like Royal Caribbean and Norwegian Cruise Line to create successful direct mail, email and online campaigns, as well as strengthen their loyalty programs.

In April of 2008 Chrisanne went out on her own to create her marketing company, Sternal Consulting. Serving companies large and small with their marketing and public relations needs. Chrisanne works with a great group of designers, web programmers and copywriters to create marketing tactics that deliver returns.
