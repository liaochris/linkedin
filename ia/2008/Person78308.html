Humberto A. Fernandois’s Summary

Executive in a managerial and senior leadership position in a Chilean State Mining Sector, with an strategic endeavour joint to the senior executive team, particularly in an organization confronted with a challenge to make strong contributions to Chilean State, recovering and boosting their competitive position and organizational culture.

Chilean professional specialized in Human Resources with more than 20 years of practical experience in this field, in operations with and without labour unions in Chilean and foreign privately owned mining companies. Energetic leadership in carrying out leadership and professional development programs. Experience in organizational changes within the context of mergers and acquisitions. Sound knowledge of labour legislation, occupational health and safety requirements. Demonstrated capacity to work effectively throughout all levels of the organization, in highly complex scenarios of management and life cycle of mining industry.

Labour Relations Management and Organizational Communications, Negotiation of Collective Bargaining, Development of Corporate Training Programs, Evaluations of Risks and Operational Integrity and Conflict Management.
