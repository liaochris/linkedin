Don McCallen’s Summary

An experienced operations manager with a 20+ year record of accomplishment in managing electronic service centres with up to 200 employees and a $12M budget.

Recognized as a particularly effective leader, communicator, trainer and motivator, with a strong focus on achieving results and consistently exceeding performance targets.
