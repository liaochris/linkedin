Tory Thorkelson’s Summary

Educator with 15 years of teaching experience in Japan and Korea. I have taught students from middle school students to business people during my time in Asia. One of the real advantages to being here is that I have been able to teach an design classes from basic to advanced conversation, Tourism English, The Story of English, Presentation Skills, Introduction to Acting and Interview Skills to name but a few. I have always written textbooks, done interviewing for many companies and professional testing services, done online classes and voice work, and most recently designed a teacher training program with a team of fellow teachers that now has 75 elementary and secondary school teachers enrolled in full time studies.

Tory Thorkelson’s Specialties:

Content Based Instruction, Drama, Intercultural Communication, and Teacher Training. I am also a qualified Interviewer for 2 locally and internationally recognized tests with 10 years of experience administering interviews for various organizations in Korea.
