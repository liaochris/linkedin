Rick Bauer’s Summary

I love the spaces between the disciplines! From data to information, from technology to learning, from technology to the core business, my skills and talents have taken me to many interesting roles, but they have a common denominator in this--being able to distill the key insights of one world, and allow strategic thinkers to make good decisions in another world with the benefit of that insight.

I love watching an entire industry--digital networked storage--and marvel at its phenomenal growth, and how storage is transforming the world around us. It has been a rare privilege to watch storage standards get "baked", to see such developments as "green storage" take hold, and to work with a great board of directors and fellow staffers.

Long term goals include technology and business management wherever there is a challenge.

Rick Bauer’s Specialties:

Business alignment of technology, data center design, green IT, web project management, bilingual in Spanish/English, educational technology and rich media development.
