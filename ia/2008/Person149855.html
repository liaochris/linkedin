Peter Franks’s Summary

In June 2006 I joined Renovata Partners (www.renovatapartners.com). We are a boutique executive search firm that specialises in building out the VP and board level teams of early stage technology businesses. Our main focus is on working with the Venture Capital and Private Equity community to help scale the companies that they have invested in - and we also work with larger and more established technology businesses. The core team are the same team that helped to build the leading Renoir Partners search firm during 2000-2003.

I have worked on senior assignments for the likes of E-bay, Yahoo and SAS Institute, and have worked on C and VP level projects across all major functions.

Peter Franks’s Specialties:

Recruiting "C" and "VP" level candidates for high growth businesses in the consumer internet, telecommunications and enterprise software industries.
