jeff jack’s Summary

At the tender age of 13, more than 20 years ago - I began making video games. At that time, we adopted a "choose your own adventure" theme to create text based encounters.

I have since created games for Disney Interactive, been a founding team member of several substantial web properties including CarsDirect.com and PartsAmerica - and have continued to help make digital media more and more accessible to the widest possible audience.

Among my guiding principles are the understanding that Best Practices are benchmarks to be exceeded - that thinking 10 years down the line should be a STARTING POINT - that GREEN has always been in - that wasting money, whatever the source, is BAD for business - and that a small dedicated team that groks a project, will destroy much larger teams who don't.

I like to work with folks who are the best in the world... It is my great good fortune that I always seem to.

At 35 years old (in March 07) I am a seasoned senior executive with a stellar history. My current situation (SPROKKIT) allows me to enjoy a great deal of variety of client interaction, project development and creative freedom. As one of the premier franchise advertising, branding, marketing, and technology service companies in the industry we have enjoyed steady and significant growth, even in the short time that I have been here. We're in the middle of our own re-branding effort. Check out our website (http://www.sprokkit.com) and let me know what you think.

One thing that gives me the most confidence when interacting with prospective business partners, or clients is when there is laughter between us. I won't stand for stodgy or curmudgeoned thinking. I will admit that it may be the entertainer in me - but I love being with a group of jokers - who get serious business done.

jeff jack’s Specialties:

branding, identity, conceptualization, ideation, project and program management, negotiation and relationship development, new product creation, industry creation and development, optimism, reality

  • Director

    2004 — 2006 (2 years)

  • Content Guru (+)

    FirstLook.com

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    2000 — 2001 (1 year)

    FirstLook was the furthering of MusicNow/Woodstock.com.

Additionally, I offered my experience in large scale asset management to help map our content paths. We had an ambitious effort before us with the cataloging and content production of over 3 million titles.

    FirstLook was tumultuous from the start - however - several folks became and remain close and trusted friends and advisers.

  • Director / Brand Champion / Founding Member

    CarsDirect.com

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Internet industry)

    1998 — 2000 (2 years)

    As the 9th employee, my charge was to make the company happen. I assembled several teams, established several departments including our online marketing and brand development teams. I was in the unique position of being the glue for the company through the early struggles and growth spurts. I, along with the EVP of Operations, scripted every aspect and touch point of our client interaction. So "handled" was our system that it became the foundation and framework that Internet Brands operates with today. In addition, as I also lead our online marketing efforts, and established an internal agency, my founding principles have also demonstrated themselves to be solid. I spent no money foolishly - despite having a $100 million budget. Not a fan of "shotgun" spending - I preferred to target my spends. (This was unheard of at the time, but has now since become an industry standard.)

    I left CarsDirect shortly after our purchase of GreenLight.com - when our staff had grown to 750 people.
