Søren Leth-Nissen Executive Winning. Yes, that's Right!’s Summary

Soren Leth-Nissen's can be contacted at soren@nduna.dk. His core work has been change management through facilitation of paradigm shifts as well as executive coaching for key employees of the organisation. He is Associated Certified Coach (ACC) under International Coach Federation. Also, he is a trained The Leadsership Challenge Facilitator.

He is the founder of NDUNA which means "Executive advisor to the Chief." Major results have been achieved through facilitating change processes and coaching of executives. He has traveled extensively around the world and has worked in Scandinavia and Africa as well as South America.

Key clients include TDC A/S, Grundfos Management A/S; Kverneland Group, SLA A/S, CBD A/S and many many more. Check my website for detailed info at www.nduna.dk.

His passion is positive change for better lives and income for companies and people. Trained as an Ethnographer and Social Anthropology as well as Leadership Communication, he has a keen understanding of cultures and is able to remove any constraints in integrating into a future culture. He is skilled at business planning and has drafted and built numerous long-term strategic plans for organizations. He uses his dual skills of inter-personal and analytical abilities to manage corporate accounts build a client base as well as consult them. He builds international Superteams that works. He works with innovation and product development as well as personal development as effective tool to increasing the innovation power of a company.
