Darby C Doll’s Summary

From working as a newspaper editor in Taiwan to a tech reporter at the height of the dotcom bubble, to conducting marketing programs for multinationals such as eBay and MSN in Asia, to helping clients develop effective social media and online communications campaigns today -- I've always sought to stay on top of the transformation of media and communications.

*More than 12 years of experience in public relations, marketing and journalism, as well as expert knowledge in global business, emerging media and online communications.

*Currently lead JohnstonWells’ emerging media practice - developing and implementing strategic online communications programs that employ video, social networking, blogging and blogger outreach.

*Serve as a member of JohnstonWells’ executive management team, oversee the firm’s media training practice and bring valuable experience in issues management and crisis communications.

*Direct and support public relations programs for a number of clients in government, sustainability, technology, healthcare and consumer sectors.

*Current member of 2008-09 class of Leadership Denver under the Denver Metro Chamber Leadership Foundation.

*Previously worked in the Asia Pacific region for 11 years, helping to establish the Taiwan office of Hong Kong-based Upstream Asia Media & Marketing.

*Earlier in my career, worked as a journalist and as a freelance copywriter for a variety of companies in Asia.
