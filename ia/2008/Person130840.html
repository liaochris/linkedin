Micheal Huggins’s Summary

The pinnacle of my professional aspirations will combine intensive writing opportunity, a high premium on successful human to human interaction and a willingness to evolve through the experimentation of creative development. As a marketing and development manager, I excel in negotiations where the illustration of not only one-time benefits are displayed, but the possibility of sustained, evolving partnerships. My perspective is that of my generation's and I am increasingly successful illustrating to older generations the effectiveness of approaches to marketing they struggle to understand. I specialize in analysis of ideas, products and communication through details of the human senses, and I am keenly aware of human relationships.
