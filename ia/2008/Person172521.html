Valorie Sankey’s Summary

Selah by D'zyne started over 20 years ago as "Jewelry by Valorie". I started at home as a SAHM, making jewelry and selling to friends and family and from there to boutiques and beauty shops, until finally I made it to Goldman's' Department Store.

Selah Promotions, manager for Dorian Paul Williams, (formerly of the Gap Band), SP began management in 2007. We have been working hard on planning his upcoming tour and "Go On And Sang" concerts. Look for his next album "Acca Thera Praise Vol. 2"!

SP is partnering with GS Capital Services, Inc., and company that was created to encourage more people to invest by minimizing risk and maximizing returns.
