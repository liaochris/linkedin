Wendy Taylor’s Summary

Wendy Taylor is a senior executive with experience in technology and finance, a master networker, and a business consultant. She has been recognized as a pioneer in the fields of customer care, customer relations management, and social networking. She has served as the senior customer care thought leader and customer evangelist in entrepreneurial and fast-growth SMBs, as well as Fortune 200 companies.

Wendy is a founder and Chief Product Development Officer of Prosper Corporation. Prosper's products include training and consulting in customer care, executive development, and advanced approaches to networking.

Wendy also serves as Director of Sourcing and Performance Optimization at Driva Solutions in Seattle. Her work with Driva includes developing and implementing sourcing strategies for some of the premier global brands. Driva founder Bill Price's new book "The Best Service is No Service: How to Liberate Your Customers from Customer Service, Keep Them Happy and Control Costs" is now available on Amazon.

Wendy also has extensive experience in program management and administration for organizations involved in education and youth, family, and health services. Her program management experience includes work in the business sector, charitable sector, and public-private partnerships.

A classical choral soprano, Wendy has performed at the Grand Teton Music Festival with the San Francisco Festival Chorale. She has also performed with the Calvary Presbyterian Chancel Choir on a concert tour of France, including performances at the Rouen Cathedral, and most recently, in performances of Bach's St Matthew Passion with the Santa Rosa Girls' Chorus and Scarlatti's Stabat Mater.

Wendy is also a student of the game and business of baseball who has attended and kept score at hundreds of baseball games over three decades.

Wendy Taylor’s Specialties:

Integration of Customer Communications across Multiple Delivery Channels including: Contact Centers, Direct Marketing, and Web Presence. Building Customer Capital. Implementation management, training, and project management. Executive Development. Social networking.
