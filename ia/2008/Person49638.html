Denise Brosseau’s Summary

Denise Brosseau is the co-founder of Invent Your Future Enterprises (www.inventyourfuture.com), a professional development accelerator building the next generation of women leaders through programs, consulting, tools and publications for individuals and global companies.

From 2002-2006, Brosseau assisted Fortune 500 and start-up companies to identify and capture growth opportunities through innovative strategies, focused customer research, and business development alliances, through her company Brosseau and Associates. (www.denisebrosseau.com)

From 1998 until 2002, Brosseau was the CEO of the Forum for Women Entrepreneurs (FWE), an organization she co-founded in 1993 with Jennifer Gill Roberts. Denise made FWE (now FWE&E) the country’s leading organization for women-led startups, with more than $1 billion of venture capital flowing to its members’ companies in just 3 years. Along the way, she built FWE into a powerful network of female executives, earning her glowing profiles in Forbes, Business Week, Fast Company, Inc. Magazine, and Wired. (www.fweande.org)

In 1999, she co-founded and continues to be an advisor of the powerful venture conference series for women entrepreneurs, Springboard Venture Forums. The Forums have led to over $3B in funding for women entrepreneurs. (www.springboardenterprises.org)

Denise serves as the Vice Chair of the Business Leadership Council of Wellesley College and the Housing and Human Concerns Committee in Redwood City. She serves as a mentor to several start-up company CEOs, including Reach Communications (http://www.reachcommunications.com/), Invisitrack (http://www.reachcommunications.com/), Job Fit System (http://www.jobfitsystem.com/) and QT-Travel (http://www.qt-travel.com/).
