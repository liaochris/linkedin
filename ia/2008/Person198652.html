Warren Mason’s Summary

Warren Mason is the Vice President, Sales and Marketing at Clarus Marketing Group, an internet services marketing group, website retailer and e-commerce developer. Mr. Mason consults for Gerson Lehman Group on product development, marketing, branding, packaging, cushion packaging, and internet market dynamics. He has over 27 years of experience in the cushion protective packaging industry and internet marketing. Previously, he was the owner of Protean Packaging; Vice President, Sales and Marketing at Angel Guard Products; New England Sales Representative at FP International; and Vice President, Sales and Marketing at Astrofoam Inc. He founded the Polystyrene LooseFill Converters Network, a buying group that reshaped the polystyrene loosefill manufacturing landscape. He is knowledgeable in the protective packaging industry, internet e-commerce and new product development. He is a published writer whose op-ed's have appeared in the Boston Herald, Providence Journal, Hartford Courant and other national publications. Mr. Mason has been featured on 60 Minutes, the NBC Nightly News and on the cover of USA Today.
