Chad Richard’s Summary

As a consumer digital media entrepreneur Chad has developed products and services involving digital photography, video, music, gaming, mobile and CE devices for more than 15 years.

As co-founder and CEO of Simple Star, Chad has provided vision and leadership in creating a business that was recognized in 2007 by Inc. Magazine as one of the 500 fastest growing privately held companies in America. Under Chad’s guidance, Simple Star has maintained a leadership position in the consumer multimedia creation and sharing category and has deployed the award winning PhotoShow platform to industry leaders including: Comcast, Time Warner Cable, Bright House Networks, EarthLink, Walgreens, Ritz Cameras, Kodak, HP / Snapfish, Shutterfly and Nero.

Chad’s vision of converging PCs, online services and CE devices was realized in 2006 when for the first time in the history of television consumers could broadcast their own content to cable TV, through Simple Star’s PhotoShow TV service. In April of 2008 Simple Star was acquired by Sonic Solutions and is now the web products and services division of Roxio.

Prior to founding Simple Star, Chad served as Executive Producer of Macromedia's Shockwave.com for three years, helping to start and build a business that was sold to Viacom’s MTV Networks. Prior to joining Macromedia, Chad did pioneering work in the internet broadcasting and interactive television arena, successfully converging web-casting with interactive broadcasting. Chad has started and sold four digital media businesses which were focused on interactive telephony, local based web advertising, digital music and personal media sharing.

Chad Richard’s Specialties:

My career has been focussed on creating consumer digital media products and services in the areas of photos, music, video, gaming and animation. I enjoy providing vision for next generation products and services and then building teams that are able to create and deploy them to mass consumer audiences.
