Jamie Parks’s Summary

The nature of politics, technology, art, law, media and psychology all overlap inside of humanity. My goal is to continue gathering intelligence and launch a global web based application that will assist litigators in effectively utilizing technology (specifically the internet) to better manage their client's cases.

With an emphasis on creating a friction-free communication portal for all parties involved in the litigation process, my hope is to improve Client/Attorney relationships by enabling their interactions to be both more timely and meaningful.

The ability to orchestrate online interaction among all parties who are engaged in a lawsuit is a necessity in furthering the evolution of the Democratic Legal process. I plan to utilize Linkedin.com to help me in assembling a team that will bring the LinkedLaw.com and vaXzine.com vision to fruition.
