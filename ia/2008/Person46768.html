    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Just like DoubleClick created the first Internet Ad network, SocialMedia Networks is creating the first Social Ad Network.

    DoubleClick's innovation was targeted just-in-time advertising. SocialMedia's innovation is networked social engagement. The Internet is evolving from a platform based on pages and links to a platform based on people and connections.

    For the past 100 years marketers have engaged with content to reach consumers. Today SocialMedia enables marketers to engage with people to virally reach social networks of consumers.

    SocialMedia is social networked engagement for developers, users and marketers. Plug into the social graph!
