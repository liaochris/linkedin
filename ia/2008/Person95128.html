John Levy’s Summary

I work with executives in high-tech who are under a lot of pressure to get products out faster without using more resources. I help them get products out easier and faster by removing obstacles in their organization.

The key issue or challenge my ideal client faces is an urgent need for consistent, predictable and high-quality output from his technology groups.

The ultimate outcome of our business services is confidence (and a growing reputation) that his technology groups consistently and predictably deliver high-quality, innovative products on time.

John Levy’s Specialties:

High-tech product development and engineering; Agile development methodology; coaching of Director and VP-level technology executives; interim management of programs and projects; design of effective organizations and processes for innovative products; evaluation of intellectual property portfolios and creation of IP strategy.

  • Principal / Consulting Engineer

    Digital Equipment Corporation

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    July 1972 — October 1977 (5 years 4 months)

    Developed a 10-year I/O Architecture plan which led to the CI and BI interfaces.
