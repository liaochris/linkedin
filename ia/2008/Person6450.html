Alexandre Vilarinho’s Summary

I'm have a Master of Science degree on Internetworking Engineering, on MPLS, QoS and Traffic Engineering from Brasilia University. I graduated on Telecommunications Engineering of INATEL. I have CISCO SYSTEMS CCNA, CCDA, CCDP, Wireless LAN Design and field Specialist Cisco certifications. I have lived on England and the US for 7 years and I’m taking Spanish lessons.

Presently I’m searching for opportunities on telecommunications enterprises, to work as a sales engineer or in project management.

Currently I work at Getronics and I’m responsible for the LAN, WLAN and WAN of Novartis Bioscience data communication internetworking and for projects to enhance the utilization of the resources of link and the internetworking equipment. I also have do administer the contracts with the data communication carriers.

I worked 19 months for Brasil Telecom, Brazil, where I integrated the pre and pos sales team to elaborate and coordinate the implementation of last mile projects for the special clients. Responsible for the elaboration of the last mile, PDH, SDH and TDM projects, the business proposal, the management of the implantation process until it was ready to be used by the clients and pass the supervision the operation team. These project could user wireless (RF and satellite) and wire line (optical or cooper wire) access. Also act with contracts administrations from suppliers and builders, with the organization of courses for the ground team, the logistics of service and stock control.

I have 6 years of experience on data communications and internetworking environment, where I’ve done research on new internetworking architectures, work with the elaboration of business proposals, management of data network and its services implantations, with internetworking consultant, troubleshooting and diagnosis of internetworking environment, with the creation of a technical qualification laboratory to qualify the ground team and to develop courses.
