Mike Cross’s Summary

Mr. Cross has worked with companies across a wide range of industries, such as telecommunications, high tech software and hardware, semiconductor, and consulting services, throughout the U.S. and in the Asia-Pacific region. His notable clients have included some of the world's largest multi-national firms, including the Shell Group, Disney, Dell, Air New Zealand, Bell Mobility, Microsoft, JDSU, and Safeco Insurance. Mr. Cross has also partnered (business development, resellers, integrators, etc) with companies such as Microsoft, IBM, Software Spectrum, Getronics, Praxa and Avanade.

Prior to joining A&M, Mr. Cross was a Director at NetIQ Corporation. During his eight years at NetIQ, he held management positions in the direct sales, channel sales, marketing, consulting services and training divisions, with a focus on driving efficiencies, improving productivity, and increasing revenue. He established sales channels throughout the Asia-Pacific region by recruiting and enabling value-added resellers, served as a speaker at worldwide trade events, and generated sales revenue in new markets. Previously, he was a Manager with Platinum Technology and a Consultant with Andersen Consulting.
