Mike Uddin’s Summary

I co-founded and ran a small startup microsystems company for two years prior to joining Procter & Gamble. While at P&G, I delivered a global document imaging and archiving solution to enable strategic consolidation of business processes into 3 global business centers (Costa Rica, UK, and Philippines). The system quickly became classified as Category I (i.e. top 4-5 most critical IT systems within P&G), due to its strong integration with key SAP manufacturing and financial systems.

I moved to Hewlett-Packard in Aug2003 as an Account Manager on the blockbuster P&G account. I soon moved into an engagement role to drive a global process by which HP and P&G can nimbly contract 100's of projects per month with speed and efficiency.

Upon completion of my MBA in June2006, I moved to SunTrust Bank (STI). I desire to work across multiple business functions of companies to gain solid understanding of the challenges in each. Ultimately, I would like to contribute substantially to the culture within a large company, where I can leverage my strong vision and strategic mindset.
