Rebecca Coggan’s Summary

Rebecca Coggan is an accomplished new-media creative strategist with a strong and diverse new-media background. In addition to her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Cinema, Rebecca undertook minors in psychology and semiotics. She has a certificate in relational marketing and has also completed extensive training in business development, enterprise, and sales techniques.

At the age of 19 and still a student, Rebecca was able to attract the attention of well-known Hollywood directors, simply by presenting her CV in the form of a script. This experience not only landed her work with famed director Jonathan Demme, but also taught her that the product has to adapt to its medium. With this understanding, and a strong knowledge of communication arts, Rebecca changed the advertising format of Quebec’s music television station, MusiquePlus, by insisting that the creative match the on-air product. The change she instigated persists to this day.

Experienced and new-media savvy, Rebecca was asked by Ubisoft, one of the world’s top video-game producers, to act as Content Manager & Producer and launch a new music lifestyle website with branches all over the world. In its first few days, the site had over 15 000 unique visitors, just for the Montreal branch, without any marketing or promotion. DJ Nights, now know as New York Nights under the Gameloft mobile brand is one of their top selling games.

Nowadays and over the last several years, Rebecca uses the expertise she has garnered to provide strategy and counsel for major clients and advertisers.

Rebecca's experience has been recognized by various organizations and she has participated as a jury member on several interactive awards contests. To learn more about the work she does or to read some of her recent successes, please visit: www.exaltcreativity.com
