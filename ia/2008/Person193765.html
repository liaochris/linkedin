Ty Webb’s Summary

Current projects involve using LIS skills in nontraditional areas to help organizations meet their goals

· Early application of new technologies

· Tailoring information products to fit the needs of the organization

· Analyzing competitive information

· Improving communication techniques for products and services

· Effectively organizing and developing web sites

Before relocating to Denver, Ty was the Manager of Strategic Information and Principal Librarian for a biotech company in San Diego, twice receiving the company’s Quality Management Award. Her department was responsible for competitive and proprietary information in the areas of research and business development. Earlier in her career, Ty launched InfoWebb to provide training and research services and was employed by a division of Bayer as the Supervisor of Library Services.
