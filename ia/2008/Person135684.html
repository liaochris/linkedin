Moshe Becker’s Summary

Experienced technology entrepreneur with twenty five years (eight in the U.S.) hands-on technical and business experience in international engineering, marketing and general management in communications, hardware, software, solutions and services.

Demonstrated ability to take early stage ideas and build them into viable businesses.

Keen understanding of wireless communications markets, in particular, Cellular, WiMAX, and WiFi.

Moshe Becker’s Specialties:

General management of early stage technology companies, Inventions productization, Venture fund raising, Team building, Market validation, Corporate evangelist, International business development.
