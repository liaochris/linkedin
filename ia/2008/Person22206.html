Bertrand Nassogne |bertrand at nexway.com|’s Summary

E-COMMERCE PLATEFORM
There can be no success without a policy of appropriate means.
Since its creation, Nexway has taken pride implementing this strategy of means based upon infrastructures and services.

E-commerce platforms:
They have been designed and created with the guarantee of availability and security for our clients in mind. A complete download platform supported by a secure payment channel forms the foundation of Nexway's infrastructure, in addition to a call center to manage our customers' back office business.

Completely independent, the ESD platform is a totally interactive multifunction system. Hosted on the latest technology and totally secure, the systems are backed-up, redundant and totally protected against intrusions and loss of power.

The applications and the system work according to the rules of a three-tier structure concerning both front and back offices for sales administration and reporting, which allows global technical maintenance and a reduced set-up time.

The front and back offices are updated in real time, as in electronic sales there is no real possibility for human intervention in transactions. Our dedicated control tools allow our technical teams to act in real time or after the event to validate current transactions and to allow completely secure 24h operation. We offer anti-fraud detection, download server access codes, dispatch and redispatch management, license allocation, billing, activation code distribution engine and backup CD burning on demand.

Services:
Our team is the foundation of our business. From advice to execution, via webmastering and marketing, all areas of expertise are covered in order to cater specifically to our customers' needs. Our 60-strong team is split into two areas of expertise: e-commerce and an e-marketing agency. Nexway is still growing to meet the demands of new customers and ensure a high level of quality.
