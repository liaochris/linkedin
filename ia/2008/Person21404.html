Benjamin Chia’s Summary

Benjamin Chia is the Chief Creative Officer and Partner of elemental8, formerly designafairs USA. He is an experienced and multi-faceted industrial designer capable in all phases of the design process. As CCO and Partner, he mentors and manages the creative staff and develops new business potentials.

Benjamin was a design manager at designafairs USA, and then took over the ownership of designafairs USA with his partner Stephen Hooper. Prior to that he was at Pentagram Design as senior industrial designer / project lead since the inception of its San Francisco ID team in 1996. Before Pentagram, Benjamin worked with a number of other leading design firms in US and Asia.

Some of his client experience includes: Siemens, HP, Dell, Netgear, Microsoft, Intel, 3com, Gateway, SGI, Nike, Motorola, LG, Samsung, Toshiba, Acer, Compal, Hannstar, and the White House. He is fluent in English and Chinese, facilitating cooperation with international clients and efficiently manages the project to completion.

Benjamin's design work has been recognized with numerous design awards from BusinessWeek/Industrial Design Excellence Awards (IDEA), the Consumer Electronics Show Innovation Showcase (CES), International Housewares Awards (IHA), Good Design Awards, ID Magazine Design Annual, and others. His work can be seen as permanent collections at the New York Museum of Modern Art, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art, and the National Archives in Washington DC.

Since 2000, Benjamin has been teaching at Academy of Art College, San Francisco State University, and San Jose State University, focusing on design process, practice and application.

Benjamin Chia’s Specialties:

Industrial Design, User Interface Design, Color & Material Research, Branding, Design Research, Design Management, Program Management and Engineering.
