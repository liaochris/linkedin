Lloyd Shepherd’s Summary

Digital publishing and content expert with eight years of journalism experience followed by 10 years of building and managing online media properties, including lengthy stints at industry leaders Yahoo! and Guardian Unlimited. Seeking opportunities to build innovative and compelling digital media experiences on the Web which engage audiences, seed communities and create revenue opportunities.

Lloyd Shepherd’s Specialties:

Product development, technical and business management, Web technology (HTML, CSS, business analysis), digital media strategy, online and offline editorial management, journalism, writing, revenue and expenditure management, strategy presentation, board-level communication
