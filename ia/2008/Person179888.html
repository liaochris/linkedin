Steven Tedjamulia’s Summary

Steven Tedjamulia has 10 years of experience in the technology industry and has an extensive background in product management, outsourcing, community building, Web 2.0, and recruiting. Steven founded VR Recruiting and created VR Mashup, an innovative job board auction engine. Steven is also responsible for the research division of VR Recruiting and has published several research papers on virtual interactive recruiting technology and community building. Additional current responsibilities Steven holds include: advisor to Netkode, an outsourcing company, and Enterprise 2.0 and Collaboration industry blogger for CMP Media.

Prior to VR Recruiting, Steven managed several professional development teams in India, Bulgaria, Indonesia, Ukraine, Switzerland, and the United States. He was head of product management and strategy for Collanos, collaboration architect for Novell, collaboration analyst for TrendIQ, advisor to the Collaborative Technologies Conference, product manager, business development manager, and alliance manager for Novell, associate research director for the Kevin and Debra Rollins Center for eBusiness, and a member of Novartis Pharma global eBusiness best-practice team.

Steven has specific experience with Collaboration, Document Management, Content Management, Portal, VOIP, Virtual Worlds, Social Networks, Job Boards, Hosted, Community, and Peer-to-Peer technologies. Also, Steven has experience in building Web Based, Peer-to-Peer, Client, and Enterprise Applications.
