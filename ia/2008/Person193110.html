Tarek Hussein’s Experience

  • President and CEO

    QCAN International Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Medical Devices industry)

    April 2006 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    QCAN International Inc. is a Canadian based company, with a strong existence in the Middle East, aiming to target and provide specialized healthcare services for a wide range of people where available professional resources are limited.
