Joel Greenberg’s Summary

I've been involved in interactive _______ (everything) since 1986. Programmer, producer, executive producer, director, analyst, strategist, freelancer, etc. I'm currently creating an ad network for Virtual Worlds at The Electric Sheep Company, Second Life being the first we'll support. I love the challenge and I love working with my fellow adventurers who are pioneering a new medium.

In the recent past, I was an account planner at the ad agency GSD&M, where I specialized in consumer trends in technology. Understanding why and how people use technology to live their lives allowed me to see that people "get" technology before marketers do.

In my career, I've been fortunate to provide innovative marketing strategy to clients such as Chile's, Southwest Airlines, Motorola, The US Airforce, The American Legacy Foundation, Land Rover, BMW, and more.

I podcast (http://friendstalking.joelandkaren.com and http://freeradical.gsdm.com) and blog (http://blogs.electricsheepcompany.com/joel) about the changing trends in marketing that are caused by consumer adoption of technology.
