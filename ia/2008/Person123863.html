Marty Roop’s Summary

Marty Roop has over 25 years of executive level Marketing, Sales, Operations and Consulting experience collaborating with many Fortune 500 organizations worldwide, including specialty chemical & minerals, pulp & paper, textiles and sports entertainment companies. He is an analytical leader of innovation and growth with B2B, B2C and eCommerce experience. He is a solutions and value orientated leader who has co-developed two patented technologies that has helped to transform a major chemical company into becoming a market leader, effectively increasing annual revenues from $45MM to $300MM.

Marty has extensive pulp & paper manufacturing and process optimization experience and can quickly recognize profit opportunities while leading organizational change in company start-ups, mergers and expansions.

He is a successful communicator and strategist who is comfortable with empowering others, always future focused in developing “win-win” strategic alliances internally and externally.

He is successful at resolving conflict, experienced at quickly assessing customer relationships, re-allocating resources when necessary to meet share holder value.

Marty is a hands-on mentor and trainer who has implemented inventive organizational and metric systems; training over 100 engineering sales/service professionals.

He is a creative leader providing “Consultative Selling & Service” strategies leading market growth opportunities.

Also, Marty is a motivational change agent who is enthusiastic in creating cultural transformation through a cooperative strategic vision based on core values; incorporating project management techniques, open communication and team building; aggressively attacking under served markets.
