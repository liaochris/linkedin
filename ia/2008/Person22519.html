Beverly Smith’s Summary

Currently I'm a tv personality appearing on various cable networks (BET, E, Oxygen, TV One, Food Network and VH1). My areas of expertise are: fashion, music, travel and celebrities. I appear on a regular basis on both TV One and BET as a Fashion/Lifestyle expert discussing fashion trends and celebrities usually viewed through the lens of music.

I'm also a freelance writer, contributing to Paper Magazine, Interview and 21st Century Hustle. In the past year I've also done quite a few public speaking engagements, moderating the HBO Short Film Panel at the 2007 ABFF, Boost Mobile "Game Behind The Game" College Tour and hosting the Upliftment Jamaica charity kickoff event.

Based on my platinum rolodexes in fashion and music, I also host dinner parties for celebrities (Dinner With Bevy). My dinner parties give music artists an opportunity to really make a connection with the fickle and very insular world of fashion. In turn, my fashion people are able to dress celebrities that can potentially open up a new segment of press and marketing opportunities for their brands. Breaking through the clutter, going a step beyond sending a publicist or manager clothes, my dinners give fashion brand leaders an opportunity to witness up close and personal if there is a fit between their brand and a celebrity.

Beverly Smith’s Specialties:

Fashion & Lifestyle expert (print & broadcast), freelance writer, dinner party hostess, stylist, public speaker and all around fly girl!
