David Richards’s Summary

Globally successful psychologist, consultant, senior executive and entrepreneur with the special ability to identify and develop the full creative potential in relationships, which is the key to successful innovation! Well-rounded experience in international, multicultural, technical, for-profit, non-profit, private and public sectors. Strong track record creating value by tuning strategy, marketing, products and processes based on customer value and relationship psychology.
