James Whitley’s Summary

James is co-founder and CEO of GoLife Mobile Corporation, a technology start-up in the wireless sector.

Over half of the world’s population now carries a mobile phone and it is radically altering how people work, play, and socialize with one another. This revolution represents an enormous opportunity, and need, for mobile services which interact with the physical world and enrich life in new and compelling ways. To address this demand GoLife has a developed a next-generation technological solution that enables software developers to quickly and easily create, distribute and monetize interactive mobile applications that consumers can use to enhance ever day life.

Mr. Whitley is also a sought-after speaker, educator, and management executive. He is practitioner faculty member at a number of colleges and Universities around the United States. His areas of expertise include: management, corporate culture, leadership, ethics, organizational behavior, operations, transition management, and more.

In the 1990’s James spent his time in the technology sector building new teams as well as turning around dysfunctional groups at various organizations. James has a Bachelor’s degree from Oregon Institute of Technology in Software Engineering. He also has an MBA from City University and is a doctoral candidate in the area of Leadership and Management.
