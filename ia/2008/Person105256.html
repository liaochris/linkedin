Kenyon Weiss’s Summary

Kenyon brings over 15 years of experience in Interactive Marketing, Web development, and user-focused design to his leadership role as Vice President, Creative Director at Publicis Modem. His expertise spans the length of the interactive medium, having launched some of the very first websites and online advertising campaigns. Kenyon’s work, primarily for Fortune 500 Clients and Corporations, has been published and awarded internationally.

Kenyon’s wide range of expertise includes managing, mentoring, and leading multi-discipline teams, both in the New York/Tri-State area and remotely across other offices in the United States, Europe and South America.

At Publicis Modem, Kenyon currently manages and directs the Creative teams assigned to servicing the Interactive Marketing and Advertising needs of Heineken USA. Kenyon is also responsible for leading Modem’s Hispanic and Multicultural Marketing capability, providing creative and strategic guidance, support and leadership to clients such as Heineken, Delta Airlines, and Kraft, amongst many others.

Prior to joining Publicis Modem, Kenyon led the Creative design practice at Dimension Data / Proxicom, where he served for almost 4 years. Before then, he was one of the founding members of Dennis Interactive, where he participated in establishing and staffing the highly-recognized Interactive shop.

Kenyon has worked for a range of clients made up of almost exclusively Fortune 500 firms; some of which include American Express, America Online, ESPN, General Electric, General Motors, Heineken, JPMorgan Chase, Nike, Kraft Foods, Mercedes-Benz, and Toyota.

A self-proclaimed “citizen of the world”, speaking multiple languages, Kenyon was born and raised in Argentina, is a permanent resident of the United States, and a naturalized German citizen. He lives in Norwalk, Connecticut, with his wife Maida and his daughter Emma.
