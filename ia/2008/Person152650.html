Steven Podvoll (spodvoll@gmail.com)’s Summary

Passionate, yet pragmatic, autodidactic jack of many trades, master of one or two, my experience includes product and business development for military aerospace, hybrid electric vehicle, mobile and home audio applications. I invariably strive to mentor cross-functional teams, nurture customer relationships, develop profit-minded strategies, innovate, yet with a genuine respect for the fundamentals. I also strive to be an enthusiastic and sincere collaborator, as well as an articulate and persuasive communicator. My diverse background helps me appreciate customers’ needs from all perspectives, and provides me with a very broad understanding of multiple disciplines, encompassing the entire product life-cycle, from conceptual planning through warranty service, from sub-micron, semiconductor materials science to real-time control system integration.
