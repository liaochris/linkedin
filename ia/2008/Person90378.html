Johnnie Estill II’s Summary

I am a college instrutor in 3ds Max and Maya for the Video Game Art program at Art Institute of California-Orange County.Last year I had the great opprotunity to be a Lighting and FX/ Environment/ Concept artist at Blizzard Entertainment and was working on STARCRAFT GHOST. I was working on some new concept art for an as yet to be decided Blizzard Console title. At Blizzard Entertainment, I had a great and wonderful experience of working with some of the greatest creative minds in the video game industry and consider myself to be one of the luckiest people on the planet. I also had the pleasure for working with some outstanding people at Shiny Entertainment while working on THE MATRIX: PATH OF NEO. While at Shiny I was a technical artist and learned so much about how the video game industry works. I never had the intention of working in the video game industry. I was grooming myself while in school towards working in motion pictures. I have had some luck with some small independent films and enjoyed my time working on such animated shorts as THE LAST TRANSMISSION (2003) SCRIBBLE (2005), and the Star Wars short film CONTRACT OF EVIL (2003). I have just completed work on CROSSROADS OF DESTINY (2006) with Lou Klien as Digital Cinemetographer and FX Producer.The work that I have done on these and other projects has consisted of many different skills. Thanks!

Johnnie Estill II’s Specialties:

Animator, digital compositor, to 3d modeling, I have done it all. I even had the opportunity to work as a digital cinematographer in some instances. That’s about it I guess
