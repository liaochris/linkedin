  • Software Analyst

    (Public Company; TLEO; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2005 — December 2006 (1 year 2 months)

    Working as a software analyst in the R&D Integration team, I was responsible for designing, implementing and maintaining technologies and services aimed at integrating Taleo's ASP applications with other systems, like ERPs, databases, etc. as well as enabling different Taleo applications to communicate with each other through unified channels. I worked on implementing various Web Services in Taleo Professional and Taleo Onboarding. I also did some work on the Taleo Connect Client, a Java-based client software enabling customers to take control of their integration process and perform data import/export through an easy-to-use user interface.

  • Software Developer

In the latter, I worked on much of the first version of the indexing module used to power the desktop search application. I have also been responsible for maintaining our reusable libraries, enforcing coding and quality standards and performing frequent peer reviews of some of the code.
