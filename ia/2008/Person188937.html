Tim Eby’s Summary

I'm very enthusiastic about how social media/software and Public Radio/TV/Media can play a role in making our community, region, nation and world a better place.

As a member of the Senior Planning Team of WOSU Public Radio, I lead the Programming, Operations and Engineering areas for WOSU's radio services owned and operated by The Ohio State University.

This includes WOSU 89.7 FM, The NPR News and classical music service for Columbus, and WOSU 820 AM, a full-time NPR News and Information service, in Columbus, OH.

I'm also serving on the Board of Directors of National Public Radio having served as its Chair for three years..
