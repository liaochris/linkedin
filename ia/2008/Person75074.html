Harrison Rose’s Summary

Initiative, information and ingenuity allow for great opportunity. Great execution comes from follow through and detail work on a basis of historic case studies, communication and thoroughness. My role is to help startups achieve reality and assist established companies to grow through technology business partnerships. I find green fields, not red waters under blue skies*.

Prior to my MBA, I was an IT/software developer, analyst, consultant and project leader. After business school at USC, I moved in to marketing, business development and executive management roles for technology based Fortune 500 companies like Apple Computer, Sony Electronics and AT&T Labs, or various stages of Silicon Valley startup. The technology area I cover spans the spectrum of computing and the Internet from platform and component through application and services.

The industries of my experience includes, on the one hand: multimedia content creation; software development; IP middleware; ASP (Internet); and electronic cooling and carbon fiber components. On the other hand: consumer electronics; B2B applications; financial systems; communications; publishing; editorial; games (MMO, casual); and more. In addition, I have also been a Special Partner for a VC firm.

Since 2000, I have consulted or been an active member of several Silicon Valley startups including a Kleiner Perkins backed startup with an ASP model for the CLEC OSS/NOC management market. The business plan I co-wrote for Reclaim Electric, an energy co-generation solution amd finalist in the 2006 California Clean Tech Open. Other areas of business opportunity: online gaming, casual gaming, collaboration applications and other Internet and Web 2.0 (or 3.0).

I continue to explore for a good personal opportunity where until I join the right, fully funded firm in an appropriate position. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me.

*I’ll translate that if you request.
