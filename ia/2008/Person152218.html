Pj Lightfoot’s Summary

During my 9 years of software development, I have acquired expert technical knowledge and –skill with several software technologies, especially with C# and MS-SQL, through in-depth experience in several industries. In addition to development, I have a strong familiarity with a broad range of disciplines and methodologies used during the SDLC (software development life-cycle), such as software architecture, planning, design, management, documentation, testing and maintenance.

Most of my knowledge of software development and the SDLC has been learned through self-study, research and thorough experience. I have a good understanding of what can be accomplished with software development and I am able to pick up new technologies with ease and confidence – albeit without prior experience.

I have extensive experience working with clients on-site and a substantial background of working with and training other people (including the disabled). Client-facing responsibilities, including establishing requirements and relationship management, have since early in my career routinely been part of my general activities.

I have found that I am very capable of handling a high level of pressure with dedication and without a drop in performance. I recognize in myself an intuitive ability to identify potential and actual problems and prevent or fix as required.
