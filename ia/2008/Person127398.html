Mechele Pellebon’s Summary

With every computer keystroke and flick of the pen, my objective is to grab the reader by the heart with well-written words. Clients hire me to create unique website content, career and entertainment advertising that snatches readers by the mouse and magnetically drags them by their eyeballs to their websites in droves.

As the founder of Pellebon Copywriting, with an emphasis on film, television, music, entertainment and recruitment advertising, clients such as Piedmont Healthcare, Adidas, Seraphilm Studios, Emmis Communications, and Clear Channel hire me to write engaging copy that generates more sales, recruits top performers and raises their visibility in the press.

As the Executive Editor of CareerVanity.com BagVanity.com and ShoeVanity.com, I create timeless and effective copy that commits people to action and resonates with their psyches, over and over and over again. My creative forte is delivering fresh content for job seekers and fashionistas, all sprinkled with a few dashes of magical entertainment pixie dust.

Prior to creating my online media and marketing consultancy, I was an exceptional recruiter, non-profit program manager, and highly sought after workshop presenter serving educational institutions, students, teachers and faculty, and the most progressive and forward-thinking marketing and advertising entities in the country.

Clients included: The Los Angeles Unified School District, Emory University ESEP, Burrell Communications Group, the Coca Cola Company, CNN, KnowledgeStorm.com, Northside Realty, Carter and Associates, UPS, West Wayne, Russell Athletics, Merrill Lynch, Delta Airlines, Anderson Communications, The Medical Post Program, the Minority Health Professional Network and the American Medical Team for Africa.

As a seasoned content provider and marketer, my goal is to empower women with information that makes them feel more beautiful, magnificent and special in every way.

Mechele Pellebon’s Specialties:

career coaching, marketing strategy, brand development and management, online information packaging, search engine optimization, fashion editorial, blog writing and online content development.
