David Gifford’s Summary

20 years of generating effective interactive and technology strategies for Fortune 100 brands and major financials. Now applying that experience to assist Organic in continuously improving its partnership with Bank of America.

Recently satisfied my intrigue with the mobile experience by being on the ground as the Engagement Manager/Client Partner for frog design at Sprint's XOHM startup, at the leading edge of mobile broadband products.

From 2 person garage startup to acquisition by WPP, coolfire.interactive was an agile, in-the-black, early entrant agency that provided enormous value for our clients, Ford, Lincoln-Mercury, Ford UK and Ford Customer Service. Our ideas and managed portal services became the essential link for marketing communication between the mother ships, their dealer networks, and their regional ad networks.
