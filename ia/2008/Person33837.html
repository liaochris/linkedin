Charlie Loo’s Summary

With the conviction that customers should experience the brand and not the technology, Charlie is an individual who has been successful in bridging the digital gap between marketing and technology through the witty use of technology that integrates the arts and sciences of the marketing business.

As the architect behind the vast number of enterprise web solutions that power the underlying mechanics of various online branding and digital marketing strategies, Charlie has proven to work well with cross functional teams to see through the completion of online campaigns and product-based projects for MNC clients such as Visa, Hewlett-Packard, Nokia and Nike.
