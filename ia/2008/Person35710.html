Chris Moriarty | Top Linked | LION’s Summary

Chris Moriarty harnesses creativity, profitably.

Initially a journalist, Chris' quickly rose to become Editor In Chief at Australian Consolidated Press where he oversaw a line of business-to-business publications. An online pioneer, his understanding of writers' and artists' work styles saw him develop online/offline publishing models that achieved the still-rare feat of constructing production teams capable of (and happy to) work on simultaneous print and online publications.

Those successes saw him catch the eye of News Ltd, which in 1997 recruited Chris to join the leadership team of its new interactive division as a Director. In this role Chris developed processes that led to the creation Australia's first online real estate advertising websites.

A later role saw him serve as General Manger of a boutique marketing agency where he developed complex strategies that directed targeted marketing campaigns aimed at maximizing, rather than creating random activity that was hoped might build a brand. Managing diverse teams of writers, artists and marketers, he delivered profitable campaigns for clients including National Australian Bank, Ericsson, Compaq and CA.

Chris' next step was to acquire an MBA, formalizing his understanding of how to direct creative energies into predictable and repeatable business processes.

That experience led him to his current role as Co-founder of Strikeforce Sales and Managing Director of Prestige Posctcards. Both businesses are currently enjoying strong growth, with Prestige Postcards' revenue growing 500% since Chris took on the business.

Chris' entrepreneurial style combines hands-on understanding of how creative people work and a proven manager's pragmatism and respect for achieving financial outcomes.

"The best ideas in the world mean nothing until they can be made to work for a business," he says. "And I think my diverse background equips me to do that very well."
