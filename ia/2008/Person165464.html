Randy Stebbing’s Summary

Randy Stebbing's career as a technical artist has spanned 20 years. He is currently the Technical Art Director for High Moon Studios, responsible for all of the studio's art related tools. In this role he manages both designers, programmers and artists. Much of his development work includes scripting in Autodesk Maya and Autodesk 3ds Max. Before joining High Moon, Randy was at Acclaim Studios in Salt Lake City, Utah, where he served in technical art, project management and lead artist positions. Randy continues to be a speaker at trade industry events related to the technical aspects of video game production.

Specialties: Management, team building, consulting, video game development, Autodesk Software, technical artist, 3d software, Maya Specialist.
