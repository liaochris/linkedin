Jason Katz’s Summary

Headquartered in Los Angeles, Championship Gaming Series (CGS) is the only worldwide professional video gaming league. Launched in 2007, and a joint venture among BSkyB, DIRECTV and STAR, CGS features teams of the world's best gamers from North America, Latin America, the United Kingdom, Europe, the Middle East, Asia and Australia. The league distributes entertainment content to more than 425,000,000 potential viewers worldwide via DIRECTV and G4 in North America and Latin America, BSkyB in the UK, Eurostar in Europe, and ESPN STAR Sports in Asia. Sponsors of CGS include Mountain Dew, Dell XPS, Xbox 360, and Creative.

------------------

I am a marketing executive who is responsible for the league's worldwide marketing efforts, including branding, advertising, promotion, public relations, interactive and event marketing. Most recently, I served as Director of Gaming at the world's #1 interactive agency, Tribal DDB, on Madison Avenue in New York, where I was responsible for creating the gaming strategy for clients such as Mountain Dew.

I began my career as an entertainment lawyer and later made the transition from attorney to marketing strategist at DraftWorldwide in Chicago, representing such clients as M&M/Mars, Anheuser-Busch, and RJ Reynolds.

I hold a BA from Northwestern University and a JD from Northwestern Law School in Chicago.
