Suzy Wise’s Summary

I have been employed at The George Washington University since graduating with my bachelor's degree in Political Science & Women's Studies. In this capacity, I have worked with all the area K-12 school systems to make internship placements for our students. I have worked with certification, licensure & HR offices for students who have completed their programs and I have assisted them in obtaining initial teacher licenses, in accordance with NCLB requirements. I have assisted the faculty and staff in two successful cycles of NCATE accreditation renewal.

In February of 2006, I was accepted at the Institute for Integrative Nutrition in affiliation with Teachers College at Columbia University in New York City. There I have been trained to be a holistic health counselor in integrative nutrition. I now am founder & director of Living Large Wellness, a sole proprietorship in which I counsel clients in a structured 6-month program on all matters around health and wellbeing. I am certified through the American Association of Drugless Practitioners.

administrative/organizational expertise, exemplary diplomacy & communication skills, holistic health counseling, integrative nutrition
