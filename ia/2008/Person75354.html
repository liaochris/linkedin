Jeff Echols, Assoc. AIA, CGP’s Summary

For the past 20 years Jeff Echols has worked in the construction industry across the Midwestern United States. Having experience in the masonry, electrical and carpentry fields allowed him to enter into the field of architecture with a great deal of “real world,” field experience. His familiarity with the construction trades assists him in developing good working relationships with craftspeople and in designing and managing successful projects.

As a one time leader of Historic Preservation for one of the Nation’s leading firms, Jeff has been involved with a large number of renovations, both Historic and not, ranging from single family homes, to covered bridges, to fire houses, to train depots. Currently, Mr. Echols’ focus is on the residential marketplace, often in the design + build arena, spanning from single family custom homes, to townhomes and condominiums, to apartments.

What does all of this mean to you? Jeff Echols brings experience in building and design, as well as, relationships with craftspeople and designers to your home renovation. His expertise in all aspects of renovation, will provide comfort and confidence throughout your journey to a satisfying and successful home improvement project.

In addition to his role as Renovation Consultant, Jeff also focuses his design and architectural talents on a variety of project types for HAUS - The Architectural Studio. By taking this dual approach, Jeff can provide a full range of services for all clients for all project types, large and small.
