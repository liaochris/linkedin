Ana Maria Salazar’s Summary

Recognized international law and national security expert on Latin America with extensive experience in criminal law enforcement. Director of Mexico-based consulting firm that provides services to domestic and foreign companies and local government on political strategies, risk management, international negotiations, mediation, and training. Hosts a nation-wide daily news show for Radio Imagen in Mexico and a weekly TV show for TV Azteca. Author of two books on foreign policy and is a weekly columnist published in major Mexican and US Spanish-speaking newspapers. Former deputy assistant secretary for the Department of Defense, White House policy advisor for the Office of the President’s Special Envoy for the Americas, and special assistant to the Bureau of International Narcotics and Law Enforcement Affairs.

Ana Maria Salazar’s Specialties:

Negotiations conflict resolution expert and trainer. TV and radio host. columnist, political analyst in both English and Spanish. Expert on security matters in Mexico.
