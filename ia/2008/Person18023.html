B. Thomas Atterstam’s Summary

Passionately dedicated to the advancement of business and individual performance by improving leadership, organizations and ways of working. 13 years experience as visionary leader, trusted advisor and agent of change. Expertise in: visioning and strategy creation processes, implementation of large-scale transformation efforts and application of the science of positive psychology in business settings.

Representative experiences include:

- Championed the development of a Management Excellence Program – an in-house global management development effort aimed at 7,000 first-level managers in pharmaceutical company.

- Drove a large-scale global change initiative through the design, development, and implementation of a global road show across four continents. The initiative enabled senior management to engage regional and local business leaders in order to gain support for the new corporate direction and directives.

- Provided one-on-one coaching to senior business and technology executives on best practice leadership, organizational development and communication practices.

- Designed, delivered, and facilitated more than 35 interactive critical meetings with up to 1,700 decision makers.

- Lectured extensively in leadership and project management for adults in continuing education

Thomas holds an honors B.Sc. in Management of Information Systems and an honors MBA in Management from Columbia Business School with a special focus on new methods for leadership development and management training. Thomas furthermore holds a Masters degree in Applied Positive Psychology from the University of Pennsylvania. Through this program he has acquired unique competence in strengths-based leadership and organizational development methods enabled by cutting edge research within the Positive Psychology and the Positive Organizational Development movements.

B. Thomas Atterstam’s Specialties:

Thomas is a thought leader in using the science of positive psychology to enable both individual and organizational development processes. He is furthermore deeply experienced in building strategic communications, designing and leading organizational alignment and transformation processes, and conceptualizing and selling ideas to multiple stakeholders.
