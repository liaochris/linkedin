Laurent de Bar’s Summary

I have extensive consumer marketing experience both on the agency and client side with valuable brands in various industry segments in North America, Australia, Europe and Asia.

Promoted in September 2005 to the position of Global Interactive Marketing Manager for Nissan & Infiniti at Nissan HQ in Tokyo I have recently taken on new responsibilities within the revamped Global Marketing group.

My focus today is to improve marketing and sales efficiency through a number of tangible initiatives such as assets production & sharing, expenses optimization, productivity enhancements and best practice sharing with our alliance partner Renault.

Prior to this, as part of the Global Interactive Marketing team my role was to provide strategic direction and leadership, governance framework, subject matter expertise, asset deployment/repurposing and global economic investment for the Nissan and Infiniti brands specifically in the area of Online Marketing (B2C).

I joined Nissan HQ in Japan from Nissan Europe, where I was responsible for over four and half years for B2C Internet operations across more than 30 European countries, leading digital marketing, branding and acquisition strategies. Over this period, I built a central model, contributing to the brand reconstruction in Europe as well as participating in making substantial financial savings.

Prior to Nissan, I spent time in NY, Sydney and London on the agency side where I held client services positions at two major global agency networks: Wunderman Cato Johnson and Bates. I managed brand, retention and acquisition campaigns both in the direct marketing and interactive world.

I hold a Communication & Advertising degree, which I obtained in France after having completed my one-year National Service.

I enjoy the outdoors, sports in general and I am a passionate horse rider. I love traveling and insist on going somewhere new at least once a year. I've also developed a recent interest in photography.
