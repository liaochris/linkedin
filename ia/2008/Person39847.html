Valeria Maltoni’s Summary

Expert marketer, communicator, consultant and speaker Valeria works effectively with CEOs, and senior management teams to make a significant impact on the areas that are important to them: the organization’s measurable objectives of growth and profitability.

Versed in the business environment, challenges and languages of five different industries, Valeria negotiated win/win partnerships and alliances, launched new brands in crowded and highly competitive marketplaces, and rescued old brands to favor top line growth. She was decisive in penetrating those markets via extensive knowledge of sales cycles and future trends.

Managed corporate image and reputation to enhance credibility, building significant brand name equity. Her public affairs expertise includes extensive crisis response, which she employed successfully in two situations to avert potentially devastating publicity and financial consequences to the organizations she served. Valeria coached senior management through complex internal and external communications.

Inspirational dream: To be the go to person for world-changing creative ideas that transform businesses, brands and reputations.

Focus: Catalyst for creating and spreading ideas people are passionate about, with authenticity.
