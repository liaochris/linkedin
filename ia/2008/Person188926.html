Tim Tribe’s Summary

Certifications Include - Certified Public Accountant (CPA) - Certified in Financial Forensics (CFF); Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE); Certified Internal Controls Auditor (CICA)

More than eleven years of experience in accounting and auditing and have worked with a diverse set of clients in a variety of industries ranging in size from less than one million dollars in annual revenue to over one billion. Post-graduate education has provided an additional level of experience in statistical analysis and accounting research.

Combined auditing, academic, and forensic accounting background has yielded experience in the following areas – Accounting and Reporting, Accounting Research, Audit of Internal Control, Fraud Investigation, Litigation Support, Analysis of Loss of Business Income, SEC Reporting, and Statistical Analysis.

Have experience in providing litigation support in both criminal and civil matters; have assisted in the development of expert testimony and materials and have provided testimony in arbitration.

Supervised or directly performed more than 100 business income loss analyses on behalf of various insurance companies in connection with catastrophic events.

Supervised or directly performed the analysis of fidelity losses (e.g. fraud, embezzlement, employee theft) on behalf of various insurance companies for evaluation and settlement of claims.

Tim Tribe’s Specialties:

Have accumulated significant experience in the following industries – Oil & Gas Refining & Retail, Furniture Manuf., High Technology Product Manuf., Hotels & Resorts, Not-for-Profit Org., Public Universities, Retail Consumer Business, Restaurants, Semiconductor Manuf., and Sporting Goods Manuf.
