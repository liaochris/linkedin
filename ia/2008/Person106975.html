Sam Kim’s Summary

Mr. Kim is Vice President, eCommerce business channels responsible for $1.2B in eChannel revenues including www.grainger.com for Grainger Industrial Supplies headquartered in Lake Forest, IL Grainger is an industry leading broad line distributor of facilities maintenance products serving businesses and institutions in Canada, China, Mexico and the United States with 2007 sales of $6.4 billion).

Mr. Kim has served Grainger for two decades, including as VP of Enterprise Systems Services and VP of Business intelligence and Knowledge Management Services, and Regional Branch Operations Manager for the Midwestern region.

He is a proven leader with a solid track record of developing high performance teams, measurable improvements on revenue, profitability, competitive advantage and customer service.

In addition, Mr. Kim serves on various groups such as:
