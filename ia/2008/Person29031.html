Bianca Taulman’s Summary

Bianca Taulman is an Information Services Developer at the Lance Armstrong Foundation. She is a Microsoft Certified Professional with over ten years of technology experience and holds a Bachelor of Arts in Japanese Language and Culture from the University of Texas. Her websites have been Notable Achievements at the Texas Interactive Media Awards and a finalist in SXSW Web Awards. Bianca was a finalist for the 2003 Dewey Winburne Community Service Award and is a member of the Leadership Austin Emerge class and AWTAustin. Bianca is a member of St. David’s Episcopal Church and an active volunteer with organizations including Austin Free Net, Knowbility, and Animal Trustees of Austin.
