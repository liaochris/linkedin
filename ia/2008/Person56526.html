Edoardo Zubler’s Summary

Ten years web and multimedia development experience focused on creating web applications and rich media solutions for a wide range of devices and platforms. Produced both front end and back end systems for Tablet PCs, Set Top Boxes, and handheld PDAs. Been involved in pioneering projects such as the development and implementation of a content repurposing system based on Macromedia Generator for the first regular digital terrestrial (DVB-t) data broadcasting service in Europe.
Developed many Flash based rich client applications for fixed and mobile devices. Developed a number of extensions for many Macromedia products. Volunteered from 2003 to 2005 as Team Macromedia for Flash providing users support on Macromedia forums and authoring serveral articles, tutorials and books.
