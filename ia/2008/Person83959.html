Jan Guldentops’s Summary

As a consultant I am always looking for challenging complex projects that force me to think out of the box. My speciality is situated in the field of building secure, reliabel network- and server-infrastructure. I tend to use a lot of Linux and Open Source to built these solutions.

Another new interesting field we are working on today is virtualisation. We have a number of infrastructure projects running on VMWARE and are currently rolling out the first projects with XEN on AMD-V hardware. Especially the more difficult physical to virtual machine conversions are a challenge.

Next to this I research new technologies and potential security problems in our testlab. In the past we have created "proof-of-concepts" for security-problems in bankingsoftware, wireless networks and Domino-webservers. The result of our work is published in publications such as Smart Business, Network and Telecom and Datanews.

More info can be found @ http://www.ba.be

Jan Guldentops’s Specialties:

SECURITY : r&d, audits, forensics and practical solutions (Firewalls, IDS/IPS, Anti-virus, Authentication Systems,...)

NETWORKING & DATACOM : network architecture, Wireless, (international) Wide Area Network Setups, Mobile datacom (GPRS, UMTS)

LINUX & OPEN SOURCE : using Linux since 1992 with experience with Redhat, Suse and Ubuntu/Debian along with custom distributions. Linux setups as Internet infrastructure, database-server, fileservers, messaging infrastructure, failover clusters...)
