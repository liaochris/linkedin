Alexander Sirotin’s Summary

Alexander Sirotin (asirotin@hotmail.com) has over 15 years of management and consulting experience with start-ups and multinationals. With a background in management information systems, business management, and international relations, he works with C-Level executives in the banking and financial sector to facilitate industry best practice for technology utilization.
At present, he drives regional business for Algorithmics financial risk management solutions in the emerging economies of Europe and the Former Soviet Union (CEE/CIS). Previously, he directed business development for Temenos' core banking solutions; managed both direct and indirect sales networks for SCO operating systems; led the Scala EMEA indirect sales team and the CIS marketing department; lobbied for American Business in Russia with AmCham; and ran a USAID privatization project in Kyrgyzstan.

Fluent in Russian, Polish, and English.
