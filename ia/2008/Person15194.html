Apostolos Tsorakis’s Summary

Shortly, referring to my career, it is useful to state that I had the opportunity in mid 90’s to become a manager in new media business development. Since then I was lucky enough to participate in the business development of very pioneer projects for Antenna TV namely: Internet portal, SMS gateway, Big Brother and finally Digital integration of the value chain of the TV station. In parallel to my professional occupation to Antenna TV I served as consultant next to the minister of Foreign Affairs in Greece, 1998-2004, contributing to the establishment of a media center under the auspices of the News and Information department.

After fifteen years of professional employment in the media industry, I have a thorough understanding of modern businesses.
