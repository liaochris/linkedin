Harvey Yamanaka’s Summary

Harvey's real estate experience is vast and includes work in both residential real estate, commercial real estate, foreclosure & bank owned properties, as well as multi-unit income propertties. Harvey has been a licensed Realtor since 1987 and has worked in sales, asset management, and real estate valuation. Majoring in business with an emphasis on Real Estate, Harvey has always been passionate about real estate. He has an extensive knowledge of the Bay Area and is especially experienced within the Silicon Valley.

Harvey has been successful at every level in the industry and has earned performance awards with his previous brokers; Coldwell Banker, Cornish and Carey, Huntington Realty, Prudential California, and American Savings Bank. His awards include: Top Producer, Diamond Society, Presidents Club, International President's Elite and Award of Excellence. Currently, Harvey is in the Top 10% at Intero Real Estate, which places him in the Top 2% on a National level.
