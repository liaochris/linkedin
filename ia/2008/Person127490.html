Karin Dirkx’s Summary

My goal is designing attractive, intuitive and fun to use interfaces.

I've been a Sr. Web Designer / Interaction Designer at Partout (www.partout.nl) for 5 years. And a Game Designer at Fantazm for about 1 year. Currently I'm a Sr. Web Designer at Integrace, and I'm owner of Medialoog (www.medialoog.nl) which is a MultiMedia design company, on- and offline.
