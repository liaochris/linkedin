Simon Miles’s Summary

I started my professional life in engineering and electrical design, and programming, in conveyor belt systems and general MHE (Mechanical Handling Equipment). Since discovering a talent for sales, I have worked with complex technical and computing solutions providers, selling connectivity, mobile working and networking solutions. Whilst most of my experience has been in the warehousing and logictics industries, I have also worked with manufacturers, retail and service industries.

Being very much a technical sales person, I have expertise in Thin Client systems, Server and Virtualization technologies, Radio Data Terminals and mobile solutions, mid-range server systems and storage, LAN, WLAN and WAN communications systems and label printing solutions. I also have some experience with programming (BASIC and PLC) and design (mechanical, electronic and electrical).

My goal is to be regarded by customers, suppliers and competitors as an expert consultant in the technologies I sell, and the first choice for help and advice with any business computing issue or investment.

Simon Miles’s Specialties:

Thin Client Technologies, Virtulaization, RDTs, WLAN solutions and client devices.
