Ethan Giffin’s Summary

Groove Commerce is the brainchild of CEO Ethan Giffin, an industry expert in e-commerce, e-marketing and the dynamic technologies behind it. He designed, launched and managed Thingamajob.com, a portal for some 18,000+ continuously updated positions with a database of over four million members. For years, Ethan has lived and breathed conversion rates, search engine visibility and web analytics. His goal is for Groove Commerce to bring that expertise to clients who want to successfully conduct eCommerce – without becoming experts on eCommerce technology.

Ethan’s industry expertise has led to him speaking at many of the industry’s top conferences including:
• Search Engine Strategies
• Ad:Tech
• Frost & Sullivan

Among Ethan’s entrepreneurial and eclectic background have been stints as on air radio talent, a nightclub owner and a producer of a weekly music television magazine.

Ethan Giffin’s Specialties:

Search engine optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), PPC Management, Website conversion, eCommerce strategy, Website management, Web analytics, Product management, Internet recruiting & sourcing, Email marketing and vendor management
