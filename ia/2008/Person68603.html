Gene Moy’s Summary

I am a "big picture" creative with deep technical skills and 12 years of experience from more than 70 projects involving cross-functional responsibilities: information architecture/user experience, client-side development, graphic design/branding, and some server-side development. My work combines these responsibilities into user experiences of remarkable value for clients of any size or industry. I seek leadership opportunities in user experience, information architecture, interaction design, information design, and usability work where my deep knowledge of front-end web development and branding can provide the most impact where users and businesses intersect.

Gene Moy’s Specialties:

User interface design, interaction design, information architecture, branding, corporate identity design, persona design, wireframing, prototyping, server scripting, creative-strategy-technology integration, client-side programming, relational databases, usability testing, qualitative and quantitative analysis.
