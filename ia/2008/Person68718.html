Geoff Gaudreault’s Summary

Designer and Developer with more than ten years experience designing and developing web sites and museum interactives for progressive clients. Interested in online storytelling and creating interactive tools for unique online content creation.

Geoff is creator and owner of two online startup ventures. The first, Slideroll (launched in 2004), is a rich internet application and online service for creating video slideshows online. The second, Sploder (launched in 2007) is a social gaming site where users can create custom games using an interactive game maker.

Geoff is also creator of Zanpo.com (launched in 2000), a network of virtual cities created by users - winner of Macromedia Site of the Day and FlashForward nominee.

Geoff Gaudreault’s Specialties:

Design, Flash game design and development, social and casual gaming, multiplayer, avatars, web 3d. UI design and deep interactivity. Museum interactive and kiosk design.
