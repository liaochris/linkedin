Melinda Hileman’s Summary

I have a background in graphic design, web design, desktop publishing and public relations. I am used to juggling multiple projects, and thinking outside the box. My problem-solving skills and ability to look at things from another angle often result in streamlining or upgrading the method in ways things are done. Although I learned a lot at my past jobs with corporations, I decided it was time to try something different and go out on my own. I am excited to use my skills and build a client base as a freelancer!
