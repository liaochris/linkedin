David Dingley’s Summary

I joined the IT business because I could see it had the power to dramatically change how we live and work. Eventually the internet broke free from academia and the military and things really changed.

My enthusiasm for the ways technology can improve business and our lives is undiminished. Having lived through the hype of many generations of computing it is tempered with a healthy scepticism and an understanding that it is all really about people - their ability and willingness to learn, change and adapt. Working to facilitate that change is what really stimulates me.

I aim to combine a visionary attitude to what could be with a pragmatic attitude about how to get there, or as near as is possible.

Having worked internationally all my life I understand that attention to cultural differences is critical in a shrinking world.

Latterly my experience has focussed on the travel industry. As one of the leading industries of the internet revolution is has provided many insights that are applicable elsewhere.

David Dingley’s Specialties:

Strategic and practical thinking about the application of IT and its impact. My particular interest and passion is collaborative working tools that will, one day, take us "beyond e-mail". This is a cultural more than a technical challenge.
Sales and negotiation of IT contracts. Travel distribution, Passenger handling systems, reservations, inventory, departure control.
