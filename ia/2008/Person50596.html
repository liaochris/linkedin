Jason Devoys’s Summary

I have 15 years experience of designing and developing software applications within the IT industry, and have successfully taken many projects through the full software development life-cycle. My roles during this time have been many and varied and have included software developer, technical lead, project manager, program manager, people manager and enterprise architect. My areas of expertise include architecture design for large and complex business software applications, software reuse, software development life-cycles (especially Extreme Programming), software development, enterprise architecture process and governance, project management and team leadership. I have strong problem-solving skills and have been pulled in to many incidents to help resolve issues. I am currently an enterprise architect assisting Intel’s Strategy, Architecture and Innovation organization to implement Service Oriented Architecture for Intel. My primary goal is to be a technical leader. I want to develop high quality, leading edge solutions for my customers and work hard to ensure results are delivered on schedule, minimizing bureaucracy along the way.
