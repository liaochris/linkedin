  • CEO It's Alive Mobile Games AB

    It's Alive Mobile Games AB

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Games industry)

    January 2001 — January 2005 (4 years 1 month)

    CEO of It's Alive. The company was primarily known for its mobile location based Massively Multiplayer Online Game called "BotFighters", a game taking place out in the streets in the real world. Thousands of people all over the country would track each other on a radar screen on their phones and engage in electronic battle if within firing range.... The game was launched in a number of countries, with the biggest success being in Russia where over a million SMS / week was generated by the gamers. The technical solution behind the game was sophisticated, with both SMS, WAP, J2ME and web user interfaces, and using mobile location data and map data to create the user experience.
    It's Alive was acquired by Daydream, a publicly traded games publisher.
