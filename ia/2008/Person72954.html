David Gruder’s Summary

Dr. David S. Gruder, has provided training programs throughout the United States, as well as Canada, Mexico, England, Switzerland, Hong Kong and Singapore. While his topics have been many and varied, all have focused on building the skills required for personal, relationship and leadership integrity.

Dr. Gruder is the Executive Director of Integrity Revolution and Willingness Works in Del Mar, California, through which he lectures, trains and consults in worldwide Personal, Relationship, Corporate, and Leadership Integrity Development. For almost three decades, he has provided organizational analyses, staff training, team development and executive mentoring, for executives, administrators, leaders, businesses and academic institutions. All of this has been in addition to having maintained a waiting-list private psychotherapy practice from 1980 until 2000.

Dr. Gruder has taught graduate courses in Archetypes, Human Development and Energy Psychology at the California Institute for Human Science in Encinitas, California and is also the author of two major print books, four e-books, over 70 CD sets and more than a dozen training manuals. His first print book, "Sensible Self-Help," won two book awards, including Colliers Mental Health Book of the Year. "The New IQ: How Integrity Intelligence Serves You, Your Relationships and Our World," his second print book, is a road map for addressing the worldwide crisis of lack of personal, relationship, corporate, governmental, and leadership integrity. It has so far won three book awards in the areas of Social Change, Health & Wellness and Self-Help.

Dr. Gruder is also active in the ManKind Project, an international non-profit organization helping men deal with the blocks interfering with their integrity and leadership so that they can fully embody their personal, relationship, professional and service missions.

For more, visit www.TheNewIQ.com
