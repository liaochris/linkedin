Robert Mayes Penn’s Summary

SUMMARY: 11 years in Internet and Cable TV e-commerce, advertising and interactive media. Strengths and background include strategic business and product planning, cross-platform metric development, addressable ad models, and mobile CRM. Entertainment industry experience since 1991. Recent focus: Comcast eTV strategy & “Project Canoe” product planning and design.

Recent Focus: Comcast enhanced TV launch strategy & cross-MSO product planning and design for "Project Canoe."

AREAS OF INTEREST: Product development of converged, services for TV, mobile, and broadband; competitive landscape of video delivery; enhanced content and new business models leveraging metadata across platforms; customer experience & usability in time-shifted environments; permission-based models of personalization & targeting; whole home media networks.
