Dick Wingate’s Summary

With more than 30 yrs. experience in the music and interactive media industries, Dick Wingate oversees Nellymoser’s content, licensing and business development strategies. In his position, he is focused on biz dev. with major media companies and Nellymoser’s mobile services platform. Current projects include content programming for AT&T Mobility’s music discovery application (www.vipaccessmobile.com).

Wingate helped pioneer the digital music business during his years as SVP, Content Dev., at Liquid Audio, Inc. He played an integral role in bringing the company public in 1999 and was responsible for managing music business dev, label relations, affiliate mgmt and content mktg.

Wingate’s in-depth experience in the recording industry includes positions as SVP, Marketing, Arista Records (BMG); SVP, A&R PolyGram Records; Dir. Talent Acquisition, Epic Records; and Dir.,Product Mgmt, Columbia Records. He started his career at WBRU-FM in Providence RI, and has been integrally involved with the careers of artists such as Bruce Springsteen, Elvis Costello and Aimee Mann.

Wingate was one of the earliest music industry supporters of digital interactive media, and in the early 90’s held the position of VP, Market Dev., Intouch Group, developer of the i-Station, the first networked, interactive music- previewing kiosk for record stores.

Wingate has spoken at numerous industry events, including: Jupiter Plug-In, Webnoize, CMJ, Midem, Bear Stearns, Credit Suisse, Business Week, & SXSW conferences, has been featured in the Sunday NY Times Business section, a front page LA Times article as well as quoted in the Wall St. Journal, Time Magazine, USA Today, the NY Times Magazine and Billboard Magazine. In 2000 he was selected as a representative of NY’s Silicon Alley to meet President and Mrs. Clinton and five US Senators.

Wingate holds a B.A. in Communications from Brown University and is a member of the National Association of Recording Arts and Sciences.

Dick Wingate’s Specialties:

Music, digital media and mobile business development & licensing for online, wireless and kiosk services.
