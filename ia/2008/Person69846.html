Emerson Gibin’s Summary

Emerson is currently based in Dubai and is the Program Manager for Vertical Market studies in the region of Middle East, Africa and Turkey.
Before that, Emerson Gibin was Research Manager for IDC’s Asia/Pacific SMB Research. Based in Singapore, Emerson was responsible for the planning, management and execution of research engagements related to the Small and Medium Companies’ IT adoption and trends across the region.
Before moving to Singapore, Emerson was with IDC’s Latin America for more than seven years, based both in Brazil and Mexico. During this time, he conducted more than 1,000 interviews with vendors, channels, Internet companies, large end-users, and government agencies in Central America, Caribbean and Andean region to understand IT markets dynamics and competitive offerings in Puerto Rico, Dominican Republic, Guatemala, Panama, Costa Rica, El Salvador, Ecuador, Bolivia and Peru.
Emerson managed IDC’s Latin America regional IT Spending Patterns research, reflecting the region's past and projected spending in IT hardware, software and services. In addition, he also conducted most of the SMB and Vertical Markets studies in Latin America and managed the Consumer and Commercial Devices team in Brazil.
Prior to joining IDC Latin America, Emerson was a Senior Finance Analyst at General Electric where he completed the Financial Management Program. In this position, Emerson lived for extended periods of time in Argentina, Peru, Brazil, and the United States.

Emerson is a graduate in Economics from the University of Sao Paulo, Brazil, with extended studies in Marketing and Business at the Ecole Superieure de Commerce de Marseille (France) and in International Business at the University of California - Berkeley. He is a native Brazilian Portuguese speaker and he is fluent in Spanish, English, French and Italian. He is also conversant in German.
