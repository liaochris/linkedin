Adam Marczykowski, MBA, PMP’s Summary

My professional experience includes eight years successful management of international projects
supporting manufacturing from Investment Department and Project Manager's level as well as two years
working on Software Engineer position. I possess also working knowledge of two major ERP systems -
BaaN IV and SAP (mainly MM, FI and CO modules).
Presently I'm fulfilling duty of Investment Department Manager at Thomson Displays Poland - Color Ray
Tubes' assembly factory . I offer particular expertise in the manufacturing industry sector, supported with
in-depth knowledge of project management methodologies - PMI and KEPNER & TREGOE. Currently
I'm preparing for PRINCE 2 Foundation certification.
As a Head of Investment Department I'm responsible for development, business analysis and
implementation of yearly investment and long-range programs in accordance to CPT Plant strategy.
