Lorenzo Maffioli’s Summary

Twelve years of experience in production management with increasing responsibilities. Different production technologies managed: assembly, steel pressing, spot and line welding, machining. Experience also in Purchasing and Quality.

From 1995 to 2004 in IVECO (FIAT Group, trucks and engines production) in Italy; from 2001 to 2004, Plant Manager of a controlled company in charge of special vehicles production. From 2004 to 2007 Production Manager in new sectors (fork lift attachments, textile machines).

Back to IVECO in 2007 to join the team in charge of new China joint venture with Shanghai Automotive for heavy truck production.

Lorenzo Maffioli’s Specialties:

Project leader for new production organisation; outsourcing inbound of finished goods logistic activities; support to HR in new agreement definition with labour unions (at Promatech, 2005 and 2006). Project leader for new plant layout and material flow organisation (at Bolzoni, 2004). Project leader for achieving ISO 14001:96 company certification (at Astra, 2003).

Also assessor for high potential graduates selection (Iveco, 2000-2003).
