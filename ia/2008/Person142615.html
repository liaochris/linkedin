Bio: Nomalanga Nkosi, Executive: Marketing & Product Management

Hold a National Diploma Marketing, B.Tech Marketing through the University of Johannesburg and a PMD from University of Pretoria’s Gordon Institute of Business Science (GIBS). Having completed my studies i, I started a brand managemnt / marketing career with SAB Miller, where I worked on a portfolio of brands. Was later headhunted to join Pepsi, and appointed national channel manager: retail. Gained invaluable sales and marketing experience in the Leisure Industry with the Southern Sun Group and in financial services at African Bank Investments Ltd. Employed by Telkom SA as sales and marketing senior manager, where I was responsible for the management of multinationals, large corporates and key accounts prior to joining Verizon Business SA. My key drivers at Verizon include the conceptualization and development of a marketing and product management strategy for Africa Region in line with global and local business plan.
