Sergio Mendoza Corominas, PhD’s Summary

Since year 2000 as an executive leading some core business areas and process transformations at LAN Airlines in order to help the company become and stay one of the top competitors in the industry. Formed and lead high performace teams in revenue management, operations research, distribution systems, revenue integrity, commercial systems, commercial policies, commercial projects, etc. Had a relevant participation or leadership in the tranformation of our operations into a Low Cost Carrier type model in Chile and Perú, the introduction of value based segmentation, the development of ancillary revenue, etc.

My previous 5 years of experience were in the retail industry, holding executive positions leading areas of information systems, logistics, product and manufacturing.

My early work experience was devoted ro research in speech recognition, image processing and pattern recognition.

Other activities or engagements:
-Chairman of Amadeus Chile 2002-2006. Affiliate of LAN Airlines, supplier of reservation platforms and IT support for travel agents in Chile.

-Member of PODS Research Consortium at MIT since 2004. Devoted to applied research in revenue management for the airline industry.

-Member of Círculo de Innovación y Tecnología de ICARE since 2006. Devoted to promotion of innovation ideas and entrepreneurship in Chile.

-Co-founding partner of RMG Revenue Management Group (2006-), for the development of revenue management concepts, solutions and consulting for non-airline industries.

-Co-founding partner (1999-2005) of New Paradigm, POS software solutions for specialty stores.
