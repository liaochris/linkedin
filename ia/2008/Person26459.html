Brandon Young’s Summary

I am an RHCE Certified Linux Administrator with nine years of related experience. I have experience with many flavors of Linux, *BSD, Solaris, AIX, Tru64, and OpenVMS. In addition to my expertise with traditional Open Source UNIX technologies, I have extensive experience in High Performance Compute Clustering, High Availability Clustering, Storage Clustering, and Load Balancing Clusters. I have designed and built each type, and currently operate a large compute cluster and GFS storage cluster in support of scientific research. I have extensive experience with virtualization technologies including VMware, Xen, and KVM.

Previous experience includes two years of Software Engineering for a healthcare IT corporation, two years of Business IT Technical Consulting, three years of Systems Administration, and six years as a Nuclear Chemist and Radiation Controls expert in the US Navy. Taken in aggregate, my professional experience has given me highly developed problem-solving skills, a powerful ability to learn, strong interpersonal communication skills, and a strong work ethic.
