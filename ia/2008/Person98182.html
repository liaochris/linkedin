Joshua Maher’s Summary

I am always looking for something fun to do. I enjoy messaging and collaboration technologies and have a lot of experience with the infrastructure surrounding them (mobility, identity, storage, social networking, etc). I also enjoy the process of creation - hashing through a diverse set of ideas, moving those ideas into a real innovation, and shepherding that innovation down the long road (financing, budget, risks, requirements, timeline, UI, etc) into an actual creation - whether it be a new product, a feature, or technical solution to a business problem, I enjoy the process and have gotten good at it over the years.

I have worked for and lead teams in small companies, large companies, myself (no teams there), and everything in between - they can all suck and they can all be fun. It's the people, the project, and the goal that makes the difference.

I have co-authored books, technically edited books, created marketing material, presented to large audiences, and built curriculum that I taught and that other people have taught. All can be fun to do - so long as the people and the projects are interesting.
