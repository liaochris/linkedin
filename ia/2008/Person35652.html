Chris Mays’s Interests:

I've been in radio for over 30 years. I got into this career because of a combined love of music and drama. Radio is a 'theater of the mind' medium. I've had the opportunity to create 6 new radio stations and countless events and promotional projects over the years. I own a 1907 home in Seattle, which I've extensively remodeled and landscaped. Gardening is a favorite hobby. In 2002, I purchased a flower shop and rebranded it as "Flora Bohemia". I was the sole proprietor. It was a fabulous space, filled with great lighting and antiques, and gifts and FLOWERS. And even there, I got to choose: no carnations, no baby's breath! I did direct mail marketing campaigns and radio advertising. And weddings and Valentine's Day. I hired a manager and fulltime florist, and I did all of this while maintaining my fulltime job at KMTT.
