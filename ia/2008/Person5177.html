Alana Taylor’s Summary

I am a self-made new media evangelist, consultant and freelance correspondent. I have a strong interest in journalism, entertainment, and technology. I am 100% fluent in Spanish, and I have traveled extensively through the U.S., Europe, and Mexico.

Social Media and Viral Marketing Consulting:

I write for Mashable.com, Personal Brand Magazine, PBS.org and consult for Classic Media about how to brand companies, products, and services online. My goals is to help individuals and companies expose and expand their brand online so their businesses grow.

How I Can Help You:

I help individuals, companies, and publications who need a young, fresh, experienced point of view on bringing their brand online. If you are unfamiliar with Social Media, Web 2.0 applications, or Social Networking, I can help you easily and effortlessly overcome this fear to achieve your goals.

I also cover conference and events, do interviews, produce podcasting and web video content, and write about the transition from print to digital media.

My Contact Information:

Feel free to contact me at alana@mashable.com if you have any questions, or for a free consultation on how I can grow your business or brand online.
