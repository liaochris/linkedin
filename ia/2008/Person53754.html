Doug Jackson’s Summary

I have been intimately involved with the business of personal training for 8 years. My specialization is with helping start-up personal training businesses grow.

In addition, I reach the consumer market directly through my writing including website articles, online newsletter, and various fitness programs I've authoried and co-authoried. I maintain an research interest in peak performance, stress physiology, and optimal health.

I'm currently the Personal Training Manager at the highly regarded BB3 RESULTS-DRIVEN Training Center in South Florida.

In the future, I hope to further my positive impact on the fitness-wanting public and to become more valuable for any fitness businesses that needed 100% dedicated team members.
