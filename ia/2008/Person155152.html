Neil Gardner’s Summary

I have worked in professional radio for 18 years, with experience of the music management, music publishing, PR and I.T. industries along the way.

Roles have included everything from technical operator and presenter, through to programme controller, station producer and executive producer. I have worked for the CHiltern Radio group, the GWR Group, the BBC, London Radio Service/Disney, the FCO, and many others.

I have written, produced and engineered over 2000 radio programmes, winning two New York Gold awards for my Russian language comedy/dramas along the way.

Until October 2008 I was executive producer and creative director of the UK's oldest indepedent radio production company, producing features, documentaries and series for all 6 BBC radio netwoks, alongside creating bespoke audio solutions for commercial clients.

In October 2008 I took ownership of Ladbroke Productions (Radio) Ltd, and am now working with my three partners to grow the company's work in radio and audio production, as well as develop partneships in TV, film and animation.
