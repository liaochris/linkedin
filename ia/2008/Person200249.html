Willie Pritchett’s Summary

Willie Pritchett is an accomplished businessman, innovator and entrepreneur. He started his first business, Intellipages Designs, while attending Purdue University in West Lafayette Indiana.

Before college, Mr. Pritchett has always had a love for technology and graphic design. His parents often thought he would be an architect; due to his drawings of buildings and homes (without the aid of AutoCAD). In high school, he was an exceptional animator and cartoonist. His accomplishments included Snoogles, his animated cartoon bear, and several comic books. While in high school, Mr. Pritchett performed several illustrations for churches and schools.

His unique background caused him several problems early in his career. It was hard for him to establish a career because he was either over qualified or under qualified for most positions (was even over and under qualified for a position in the same interview). Willie’s father, Willie Pritchett Jr., is also an accomplished businessman with Primerica Financial Services. Through viewing his fathers struggles, his own personal struggles, and seeing how much easier his families life had become when his father started his own business, Willie opened Mega Input Data Services, Inc. to fullfill his dream of running an enterprise.

“With my unique background of schooling, innovative thinking, presentation skills, management experience, and technology knowledge, it was very hard for me to obtain employment. So because of this, I started my Mega Input Data Services, Inc. to showcase my skills and to work for companies in a different capacity”, says Willie Pritchett.

Owner of several patent-pending applications and methods.
