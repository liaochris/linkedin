Andy Waroma’s Summary

I have worked more than 11 years with SAP applications and SAP's customers out of which last 8 years at the regional SAP Asia-Pacific headquarters in Singapore. My current role is Director, S3 Market Development South-East Asia and Head of ESOA Office South-East Asia, SAP Asia Pacific Japan. In my previous role I was working for SAP's Global Platform and Ecosystem group focusing on strategic partner program creation and execution. Prior to that I was heading the SAP SRM Business Development team in Asia-Pacific. In my first role with SAP I worked as a consultant with SAP's procurement & sourcing applications and marketplaces. I started my SAP career with Cap Gemini in Finland as an ERP consultant. I've gained experience in working in almost all the Asia-Pacific countries and prior to that in Europe. As part of my tasks I've been working with multi-cultural teams, leading projects, business development and market development teams involving various Asia Pacific countries (India, China, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Philippines, Hong Kong, China, Taiwan, Korea, Australia, New Zealand and Japan) and industries (Telecom, Oil and Gas, Automotive, High Tech, Fast Moving Consumer Goods, Aerospace and Defense, Utilities, Retail and Public Sector). I see myself as a result oriented individual who thrives on a challenge and lives by the customer satisfaction. I welcome new contacts through LinkedIn or otherwise.

Andy Waroma’s Specialties:

Currently: focus on sustainable license sales growth for ESOA applications, expanding the capabilities of the partner ecosystem and bringing tangible value for SAP's customers through new technologies.

Prior: Creation of strategic partner development programs. KPI setting and execution of revenue programs. Demand generation programs, partner program validation, business development, partner relationships, event speaking, business process redesign and value assessment workshops.
