Damon Young’s Summary

After several years as both a software developer, technical architect, and project manager, I'm now applying my knowledge of the development cycle to help project teams become more effective through the use of ProjectLocker. With ProjectLocker's suite of hosted software development tools, including source control through Subversion & CVS, issue management through Scarab and Trac, document management, and wiki, businesses of all sizes can immediately deploy a secure and professionally managed development infrastructure at a fraction of the cost of installing and maintaining it internally, while freeing their developers from having to perform these administrative tasks themselves and focus on what they do best - Build Better Software.
