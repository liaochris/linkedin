Chad Massaker’s Summary

I began my technology career in 1996 working as a computer technician for Kennesaw State University (KSU). From there I took a position as an IT manager and eventually director for Sentry Hospitality, managing IT for several large conference centers and resorts. After 9-11, the hospitality market plummeted and I was laid off. I decided to form Carceron with my former manager from my college IT job at KSU, Jeffrey. We have not looked back ever since. Carceron is now a 6 year old company that has won numerous awards and has continued to grow about 40% on average per year.

I plan to make Carceron the premier managed IT services provider in metro-Atlanta and eventually the nation. I have some ideas for other businesses I'd like to start as well and hope to get those going in the next 3-5 years.
