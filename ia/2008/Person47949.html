Davin Ogden’s Summary

My knowledge in this area first began with Holly Mann and the teachings in her book Honest Riches. Later on, I became a member of Affilorama, which was a great experience for me at the time, as it explained by video some of the road blocks that I'd been running into and it helped me to cross over those barriers.

I've never really stopped learning about this magnificent field and have always worked on improving my knowledge. I've also always worked on helping other people entering the online arena with the knowledge I've always acquired.

Becoming friends with some of the people I'd started learning from has always meant a lot to me. I enjoy helping Holly with her forum, and it sort of went hand in hand for me because I'd already been helping many people in there to improve their knowledge or to help get them past some type of barrier they may have been having.

Holly's been a great example in showing that people can be involved in marketing in an honest, and respectful way and i strive to follow in the footsteps of these type of people in the way that business should have a great level of integrity and helpfulness to it.

A while after I got to know Tim Brocklehurst, and became part of his MVS Success Team. When it comes time for his Super League members I participate in consultations with his members and give them direction in which to go with their Viral Spiral systems and with their business.

As far as my goals go, I will continue to build on my business which has become known as Groovytastic Empire. I'll be incorporating more sites and products to this entity over the course of time. I'll also continue to help Tim and Holly out with different things when required. I just really enjoy being a part of everything I'm involved with. It inspires me to keep pushing forward.
