Alice Zhang’s Summary

Ms. Zhang is presently the Director of Strategic Projects at Forward Innovation Labs, a business incubator that helps companies innovate. While she works with companies of all sizes, stages, and across various industries, technology startups are Ms. Zhang’s specialty. She takes an active role in helping entrepreneurs implement their ideas and realize their vision, advising them on the creation of the business plan, the fundraising process, and the execution strategy. Prior to this role, Ms. Zhang was an investor relations consultant at Ashton Partners where she collaborated with management teams and institutional shareholders to manage and position the information that impacts the credibility, reputation and ultimately, the valuation of publicly traded tech companies.

At UC Berkeley where she majored in International Corporate Management and minored in Music, Ms. Zhang was a student-athlete who rowed on the nationally ranked NCAA Division I Women’s Crew team and sung leading roles in the university opera productions. In addition to her artistic, athletic, and academic endeavors, Ms. Zhang boasts an extensive and varied work résumé which includes financial research and analysis at Morgan Stanley, executive image and etiquette coaching, and genetic research at the United States Department of Agriculture. However, her wide range of experience is not the most notable of Ms. Zhang’s many assets and attributes. Rather, what sets her apart from the rest of her peers is the drive and inherent entrepreneurial spirit that compelled her to start her very first business at the tender age of fifteen. And this spirit continues to flourish today.

In her spare time Ms. Zhang enjoys attending operas and classical recitals, playing the piano, reading, traveling abroad, golfing, cooking, horseback riding, and snowboarding.
