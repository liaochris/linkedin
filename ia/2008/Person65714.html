LTC (Ret) Frank Zipperer [LION]’s Summary

Internationally known freelance photographer with over 50 years experience in both film and digital photography. Produces fine art photographic Limited Edition prints using museum quality archival materials.

Designs attractive but simple and easy to use websites for individuals and families, service organizations, groups and clubs and small businesses.

Professional goal is to increase the exposure of my work to the public through networking and gallery exhibits. I also seek networking with other photographers to share both technical and marketing skills.

LTC (Ret) Frank Zipperer [LION]’s Specialties:
