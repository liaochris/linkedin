mazyar hedayat’s Summary

I am a lawyer, blogger, and writer. In addition I own a title company and manage a growing network of blogs and websites. I am also active with the bar association.

Traditionally my law practice was active in restructuring, real estate, transactions, and business litigation. More recently we have been emphasizing succession and wealth management, as well as immigration and divorce, in response to the demands of our clients.

I am a an incessant blogger, writer, and speaker; especially when it comes to using Web 2.0 technologies and ideas to make law practice more efficient. In my opinion a technology or new business concept should shake things up or it's probably not worth adopting.
