Adam Page’s Summary

Adam Page Profile.

adam@azrek.com

Adam Page is BA Hons University Graduate who has grown from a marketing and sales background of brand management, marketing communications and customer service into an established and well experienced search and selection specialist.

With an unmarked background in recruitment in Information Technology and Development, Adam moved into Construction and Engineering within Blue Pelican Construction & Engineering during the boom period in the Middle East. Adam stayed focused on the Middle East and targeted the Telecommunications sector in the Middle East and African Regions for CommsResources UK.

After being based in the UK Adam moved to the Middle East to Bahrain in November 2007 to provide an Executive Search service to Middle East Firms at a level that is not possible being based in the UK.

Adam has a passion for what he does and a wealth of experience giving his clients a keen interest in repeat business.

With an honest and straight forward approach to recruiting if there are people out there and the right terms are arranged; Adam will successfully find them for you in the time agreed and if you are looking for a position you are reading a profile of some one that can most definitely help or at least be pleased to tell you who can.…..
