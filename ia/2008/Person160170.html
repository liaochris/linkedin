Richard D. Shepherd’s Summary

Richard opened his office for private law practice following 11 years as General Counsel of a nationwide residential mortgage company. He began as the company’s sole in-house attorney, and built a law department growing in tandem with the company’s life cycle of nationwide expansion, going public in 2001, and ultimate acquisition by a major Wall Street firm in December 2006. At Saxon, he handled securities and finance transactions, S.E.C. filings, mortgage lending regulatory compliance, litigation, and day-to-day legal matters while managing the company’s legal and compliance areas and serving as advisor to senior management and the board of directors. Prior to joining Saxon, practiced real estate, business law, bankruptcy, and tax law with Robert Musselman & Associates in Charlottesville, Virginia and Ragdale, Beals, Hooper & Seigler in Atlanta, Georgia. He is a 1980 graduate of the University of Virginia School of Law.

Richard has been married to Millie Travis since 1995. Millie is an event planner and photographer, volunteers at Covenant School in Charlottesville, Virginia, and was coordinator of all special events at Monticello from1981 until 2002. They reside near Zion Crossroads (Troy, VA 22974) on the farm that has been in Millie’s family since 1962; her son’s family and two grandsons reside on adjoining property. He enjoys clearing woodlands, building trails, making hay available to area livestock owners, hiking, and bicycling.

Richard D. Shepherd’s Specialties:

Debt restructuring and bankruptcy; tax controversies; bank regulation (completed ABA's National Compliance School, 2007); regulatory compliance for mortgage lenders and servicers.
