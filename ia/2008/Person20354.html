Beth Atkins’s Summary

I have over 15 years of experience as an electronic production artist, combined with management skills for digital assets and both full-time and freelance artists. I have worked at large and small ad agencies, and have most recently been working for a small self-run design shop. Thanks to my vast Macintosh knowledge, I have been able to be take care of a number of in-house IT issues and help train others in all Adobe software.
