Kim Gredsted’s Summary

I strive to create value on an international level by investing in and by creating, developing and growing businesses that have a value proposition that somehow makes a positive difference and have an impact. Here are a few examples:

Teleport East Africa Ltd. in Uganda is in the process of significantly improving the availability of broadband connectivity and the quality of IP-services in East Africa.

CareWorks Africa Ltd. is a pan-African business that brings to the market a concept of addressing HIV/AIDS via effective HIV/AIDS workplace programmes for businesses operating in Africa that significantly increases the chance of winning the battle against HIV/AIDS in Africa.

Optimal Stream ApS (MPEG-4-streaming of TV via www.streamTV.dk) was a first mover and is part of the current revolution and improvement of the TV and video distribution industry.

Trunk Images A/S and Trunk Images Ltd. literally make this world a more beautiful and interesting place by bringing art of the World's top photographers via the on-line platform www.trunkimages.com to the public domain via licensed usage of such photos in ads and magazines.
