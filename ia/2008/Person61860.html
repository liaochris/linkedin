Ev Nucci’s Summary

I was an Executive with two Fortune 100 companies, involved with three start-ups and ran 15 operating companies. Entrepreneurial to the core, I founded a company that revolutionized an industry. Five years later, we sold the company to the industry giant.

Since 1995, Nucci Consulting Group has provided specialized services to the institutional asset management industry. We do three things…

1. Strategic consulting. What does that mean? We help the CEO, Vice Chairman or Chairman figure out how to grow their business, achieve their business plan goals and strategically out-innovate the innovators.

2. Second, we grow their business. How do we do that? There’s three ways to do grow a business.
a. Organically (through retained executive search)
b. Mergers and acquisition
c. Lift outs
We specialize in retained executive search and lift-outs.

3. Finally, we provide leadership development through executive coaching.

Nucci Consulting Group provides clients with strategy, vision, objectivity, and integrity. We focus on long term solutions, calculated risk, bring the wisdom of mistakes and provide our services with the passion of excellence.

Since 1998, Nucci Consulting Group has provided high level consulting services to BlackRock in the areas of strategic consulting, talent acquisition, and executive coaching. Our success rate is 100%...our results speak for themselves.

When our clients need additional expertise, we find the experts for them!
