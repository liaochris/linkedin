Greg Lanz’s Summary

Accomplished sales executive with over 9 years of Internet and game industry experience. Solid track record of closing AAA deals, managing sales and publishing teams, and growing revenue. Currently oversee sales and advertising of mainstream casual games across many channels and platforms. Previously developed affiliate distribution networks, sold digital rights management and e-commerce technologies. Extensive Rolodex of casual game websites, portals, ISPs, game developers, publishers, and online advertisers. Additional experience includes third-party publisher relations for Sega of America Dreamcast, and channel management, licensing, and royalty consulting for consumer and enterprise software companies.

Greg Lanz’s Specialties:

Sales and revenue analysis. Sales management, training, and compensation plans. Intellectual property licensing (in-bound and out-bound). Extensive experience with Salesforce.com including set up, administration, customization, and user maintenance.
