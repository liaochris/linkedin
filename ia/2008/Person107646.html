Maria Kliatchko’s Summary

I work for Pfizer, where I lead Data Solutions, a team of internal consultants, who architect and develop solutions across USP and help to integrate internal systems and external 3rd party data from variety of sources. Prior to this I led Incentive Compensation team, supporting of a suite of applications, such as territory alignment tool, sales data processing system, quota development application, bonus processing, contests and awards processing as well as monthly production of over 50 reports and extracts.

Before Pfizer I worked for Acxiom Corp.’s Health division, leading accounts of Bracco Diagnostics, Otsuka America. and Sankyo Pharmaceuticals. For these companies I led the outsourced teams, supporting a wide variety of solutions, such as Customer Data Warehouse, Incentive Compensation, Sales Force Automation and Territory Alignment systems.
