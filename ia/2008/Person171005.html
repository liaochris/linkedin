Scott Hajer’s Summary

I am passionate about recruiting - helping good people find new, satisfying positions and establishing a good fit between the company and the individual. I've built a college recruiting program from the ground up and I've also hired entry- to director-level experienced professionals in a variety of roles (technical, accounting, administrative, marketing, human resources, sales).

Recruiting is not about 'slamming' a person with the right skills into a position, or convincing a candidate to take a job. It's more important to establish a good mutual fit, to find the right person for the right position. Career growth and retention are key elements on both sides of the equation. Truth breeds trust.

Scott Hajer’s Specialties:

Full life cycle recruiting, from sourcing and negotiating through onboarding and retention, strategy and tactics, establishing and refining processes and procedures, creating documentation, developing efficiencies, managing, coaching and mentoring, search, relationship building, sales, internet sourcing and internet research, retention, hiring quality people, refining the hiring process, thinking strategically, and writing solid, compelling, effective documents.
