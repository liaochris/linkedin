Simon Salt’s Summary

Currently CEO of International IncSlingers. He is a co-founder of the company. He holds Associate degrees in Business & Finance and Information Technology, a Bachelor of Science in Behavioral Science with a major in Anthropology, and a Masters in Information Systems with a focus on Logic, Text & Information Technology. He has previously worked in the UK for an e-business consultancy, Company Net, where he started as web-developer & Project manager and rose to become the Head of Business Development, where he pioneered a partnership with Microsoft and created a first of their kind series of new technology briefings for businesses in Scotland.

Following Company Net he joined eClerx an India based outsourcing organization as Associate Principal, Client Relations. Having moved to the US, he captured the company’s first Fortune 100 account and grew annual revenues exponentially.
He ahs consulted to Fortune 500 companies regarding their online marketing strategy and leveraging online shopping behavior metrics. He has been recognized for his ability to provide copy that captures business as well as giving strategic marketing guidance.

Outside of work he currently serves as the Director of Technology for the Austin Chapter of the American Marketing Association.
He has a strong community focus and is an instructor for Volunteer Search & Rescue teams and is a FEMA certified Community Emergency Response Team trainer.
