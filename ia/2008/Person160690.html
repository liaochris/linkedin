Rick Sheppard’s Summary

Dynamic, senior marketing executive with demonstrated global leadership skills and a proven track record of success across a diverse set of business environments within Procter & Gamble’s Beauty (female and male) businesses. Demonstrated capability to lead complex, fast paced businesses (i.e. 500+ skus and 8-10 new product launches/year) with limited resources based on extensive experience outside of corporate headquarters. Consistently delivers strong sales, profit and organizational growth by creating a compelling vision and then developing and energizing the organization to deliver specific goals. Extensive experience in multi-functional leadership, new initiative development, marketing strategies and communications, customer plan development, and P&L management. Exceptional recruiter, builder, and leader of strong people and teams. Cross functional transfer from Finance division of Procter & Gamble.
