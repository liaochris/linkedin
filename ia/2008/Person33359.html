Chandler Peterson’s Summary

I love Apple's old slogan, "Think Different". Many who know me would say that sums me up pretty quick. I'm not know for conformity or as someone who takes things as they are. I believe "That the practical man conforms to the world around him. The impractical man conforms the world around him to himself. Therefore all progress is dependent upon the impractical man". Most of the conversations I am a part of are about how to take an idea and build a business around it. How to take a business and reinvent it. How to help a client see opportunities in their business they haven't seen before. Introducing and educating people and businesses to technologies and processes that can decrease costs, increase efficiency and in many instances transform the way they work. These days, my favorite conversations with clients are about how to leverage blogging, videoblogging, podcasting and social media to reinforce their brand and open up new ways of interacting with their customers.

My sweet spot is in being face to face with people. This is where my value shines brightest. So opportunities of interest to me involve being in front of people and making an impact on their life and business. If you are looking for a cubicle dweller or phone jockey I'm not your guy.

My business philosophy is best summed up by this quote from Thomas Edison " Vision without execution is hallucination."

My personal philosophy on life I learned at 11 years old when Col. Hart said, "Son, leave the wood pile higher than it was when you got here."
