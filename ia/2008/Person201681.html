Yana McConaty’s Summary

Mrs. McConaty has over 13 years of professional and in-depth technical and managerial achievement in business intelligence, data warehousing/mining, business process redesign, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), supply chain, commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) integration, telecommunications, and organizational transformation. She held various management and consulting positions with the leading software and system integration providers, such as Baan, KPMG Consulting, Accenture (former Andersen Consulting), and Booz Allen Hamilton.
