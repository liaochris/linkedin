Ed Callahan’s Summary

I provide sales advisory services to the CEO, management team, advisory group or investors of growing businesses of any size, including well-funded start-ups, who want to grow their top line, profitably. My methodologies are industry and channel agnostic.

My typical engagements are either as a part-time sales coach for the CEO or as a part-time VP of Sales reporting to the CEO.

Download a complete description of my Sales Advisor practice by clicking on the "Sales Advisor Practice PDF" link in the Websites portion of this profile, immediately above.

I am typically retained for a few days a month for a fixed period of months. The retainer contract is very simple. Either party can cancel with 30 days notice for any reason or no reason. During the first 30 days no notice is required. So your only risk is the amount of my first month's retainer.

Here are just some of the ways I add value as your sales advisor:
- Help you craft a repeatable and scalable sales process
- Help you build and monitor a reliable revenue pipeline
- Work with you to create a sales management methodology which assures accountability, scalability and increased sales productivity,
- Facilitate your assessment of your current sales team and help you make what corrections are necessary in sales plans, territories or quota assignments,
- Help you align your sales compensation plans and your company goals,
- Manage your sales force while I help you find a permanent sales chief,
- Utilize my network to help you penetrate target accounts, find strategic partners, attract advisory board members, and engage appropriate sales channels,
- Teach your sales team how to utilize LinkedIn to develop prospects, develop alliances and penetrate large accounts.

Experience: Managed organizations ranging in size from two to two hundred and fifty people, across all functions . . . Managed every type of sales channel: direct, OEM, VAR, systems integrator, distributors, and independent agents.
