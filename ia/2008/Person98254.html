Joshua Swanson’s Summary

Young male actor & voice talent. Working in film and television as an actor for NBC, CBS, Lifetime, ABC, New Regency, Artisan, and more. Providing voice over work for major corporations such as McDonald's, Meijer, AT&T, Budweiser, Subway, Verizon and more.

Voice Over specialties include narration, radio and television commercials, radio voice imaging, audio books, cartoons, video games, television promos and branding, voice prompts or on hold, and multimedia.

Currently located in New York, but able to do voice over work out of Los Angeles and Chicago as well. ISDN home studio and can provide voice over via MP3/WAVE/AIFF or CD to anywhere in the world.
