Denise Tarka’s Summary

I am currently a fiction writer, specializing in crime novels and edgy short stories. I am enrolled in the Creative Writing Certificate at the Faculty of Continuing Studies, University of Toronto. My final project will be mentored by Ray Robertson. My work has appeared in print and online and my non-fiction work has been syndicated across the web. I used to practice criminal law as a defence lawyer, specializing in trials and appeals and have been a research clerk for the Ontario Court of Justice.
