Virendra Mane’s Summary

Virendra Mane is the spearhead of QualEx Systems, a company that has created a niche in data storage software services in a short time. With an experience of 14 years in the storage and systems software industry, Virendra has held executive management, engineering and consulting positions at leading companies.

Virendra founded QualEx Systems in Jan 2000 with a vision to provide services in high quality systems software development. Since then, he has single handedly built the company by providing meticulous attention to all aspects of business, including strategic planning, technology, finance, infrastructure, operations and business development. His efforts saw QualEx grow multifold and also achieve preferred software vendor status from a multi-billion dollar storage leader.

Virendra has also acted in various roles as Project Lead, Area Architect, Core Developer in areas like Unix File Systems, Quotas, ILM (Information Life cycle Management); he has developed several innovative features and solutions, some of them are in different stages of patent process.

He recently headed a business contract transfer activity with a global software services company and acted as the lead negotiator; he managed the due diligence, negotiated the term sheet, contract transfer agreement, planned and transitioned large project team successfully.
