Seymour Hersh’s Summary

Objective: To enable enduring relationships globally.

Title: Matchmaker

Corporate matchmaker. Searching for and finding great leadership talent that meets your defined skills and needs, as well as your cultural and emotional environment. The benefits of this kind of matchmaking for you are:
• greater employee retention,
• organization stability and
• corporate growth

The difference is that Dynamic Dialogues’ matchmaking process engages you, as a partner, from the beginning to the final fulfilment of a match.

Our role, as partner with you, is to define the job that meets your needs, from
• strategic,
• organizational and
• cultural perspectives

Applying our skills, across all industry segments, in the areas of Information Services, Human Resources, Project Management and Engineering, Marketing,& Sales, uses the depth and breadth of our experience.

Change requires a sensitivity and empathy that is implicit in our relationship and dialogue.

With a finely tuned search strategy (at both local and global levels) we identify and assess the best fit of great company and great talent for you.


A little history

Seymour Hersh and his team have led and coached global organizations and project teams in many countries including Canada, the United States, Denmark, and France. Seymour has also re-engineered business processes in Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology, and Transportation organizations, reducing time to market

Senior leadership experiences in the telecommunication, pharmaceutical, transportation, manufacturing, and technology sectors enable Seymour and his staff to cut through the chaff in the executive search process, and craft relationships that are effective and lasting for you.

Dynamic Dialogues has, and continues to develop, a local and global networks of cross-functional contacts and professional talent to meet your needs.
