Thomas van Maaren’s Summary

I started my proffesional internert career in 1995 when I created my first website. From that I learned the trade from first hand. I became involved in webstatistics, managing complex internet projects, building a company from 4 to 100 employees, interact with television and radio and digital channels. I am capable of working with people and am mainly focused on getting them to do what they need to do in an harmony model.

I like to create totals concepts not only website but websites as part of a compleet media, multi-platform environment.

Starting 2005 my projects became more crossmedium. More crossover to Tv became part of the projects I managed and initiated. The most recent is the realisation of a new digital TV station - sterren.nl. From we started this project. Both the website and the tv station are part of a larger crossmedia concept "nederlandse muziek" of which the TROS is famous.
