Darris Hoskins’s Summary

Digital Media Architect with more than 2 decades of diverse experience in:
• Content Production
• Content Protection (DRM)
• Digital Media Distribution
• Media/publishing
• Interactive entertainment
• Software development

Darris is Chief Architect of Mix 1 Digital Networks, LLC. a privately-held digital media company specializing in solutions research marketing and development of digital distribution technology, media content, and marketing collateral for the media and entertainment industries.

He previously served as a founding member and Sr. Principal Engineering Director of Intent MediaWorks, a leading digital music aggregator/distributor, Senior Quality Engineer of WebTV/Microsoft, and world renowned technology company.

Prior to founding his first company in the spring of 1990, mixed for radio and various nite clubs in St. Louis MO, New Orleans LA, and Atlanta GA. Independently produced & distributed mix shows and performed airshifts in the same markets. Integral to founding the world's first commercial Hip Hop radio format (Y94 WYLD). Produced first major label remix for Reprise (WB). Served as Production Director for Clear Channel Radio (WQUE) in New Orleans.

After relocation to LA in summer of 1991, served as Promotion Assistant for Motown Records and Pool Director for National Urban Dance Promotions, (founded by the late Captain G of KKBT/KMJM). During this 3 yr. Relationship, produced dynamic remixes for various major & inde record labels and ghost DJ mixes for LA radio stations (KKBT, KJLH).
