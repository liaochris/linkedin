Lara Bersano’s Summary

Grupo Empresarios is a software and communications company, based in Argentina and with operations in Latin America and the US Hispanic Market.

As President of Communication Strategies at Grupo Empresarios, I am the first person to talk with the client and identify its needs and I manage an expert team of communication, technology, editorial, design and media experts.

My business approach includes working together with the client's executive team, identifying every single piece of important (or irrelevant) information. And in particular:

1.- Identifying the company's needs, the stakeholders' needs and community's needs.
2.- Talking with our clients constantly about the goals to achieve in the Hispanic Market.
3.- Listening to the market, and what it is telling us about our client.
4.- Considering every single person in a company as a potential spokesperson.
5.- Creating a Public Relations' plan that includes every valuable asset in the company.
6.- Understanding that there are no boundaries to business and including every potential market and every language in our strategies.
7.- Planning for the short, medium, long and very short term.
8.- Evaluating the actions taken and their consequences and reconsidering positions.

Preferred business partners:

1.- Technology or internet-based companies doing business or interested in starting business targeting Latin American or Hispanic Markets.
2.- Individuals that want to position themselves as industry leaders in any field, and want to start an international campaign.
3.- Social and political leaders.
4.- US Companies or investors reaching Latin American consumers.

In every case, we work only with individuals and companies with a deep sense of involvement and commitment with the community and their social environment. That is our "must".

Lara Bersano’s Specialties:
