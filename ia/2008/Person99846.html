Jennifer Uhll’s Summary

Juhll.com is a full-service online marketing agency leveraging advanced internet technologies to create measurable and profitable online campaigns. Our mission is to significantly increase ROI for both small and Fortune 500 clients who invest in online marketing.

Juhll offers creative optimization, creative development, display banner testing, website optimization, landing page optimization, media buying, campaign management and online marketing consultation. Our highly successful team includes over 30+ years combined experience in conceptualizing and developing online marketing strategies. Juhll clients include top corporations in a variety of consumer and B2B industries, including entertainment, financial, education, automotive and internet lead-generation.

Juhll’s specialty is incorporating client marketing objectives into the design and development of fully integrated online marketing channels, including websites, landing pages, games, banner ads and email. Clients have received an average 20% lift in conversion with Juhll's website & landing page optimization services.

Juhll's campaign management team has increased client click through and conversion rates as high as 300%, with an average 20-40% lift. Our team provides advanced creative optimization, display testing and performance reporting. We optimize banner campaigns through a/b and multivariate tests. Advertisers significantly lower creative development costs with Juhll's ability to instantly change critical banner elements in real time, including offers, headlines, click actions, images and animation.
