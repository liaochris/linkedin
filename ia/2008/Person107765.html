Kristina Messdaghi’s Summary

Bio

Kristina is a thought leader at the vanguard of the alternative energy market. To achieve her MBA from University of Liverpool (after 15 years as a high-tech executive), she authored a dissertation entitled “How Biofuels Can Boost the GDP of Developing Countries While Challenging the Current Fossil Fuel based Economy”. The paper addresses the continuing demand for fossil fuels despite dwindling resources and rising costs, and presents biofuels as a possible solution to the increasing energy crisis, with a focus on the potential economic benefits for developing countries.

As Founder of BioFire, a unique venture addressing the growing need for alternative energy sources, Kristina proposed to reduce investment risks by presenting innovative strategies to serve the global market, while assisting communities within developing countries to acquire energy independence via biofuels. To achieve these ends, Kristina created a business plan with a supporting financial model to diversify investments across emerging growth markets, crops, biofuel types, and technologies. As part of BioFire, she established a non-profit arm to contribute a percentage of the Company’s revenues towards education and micro-financing within countries where BioFire was to be invested. BioFire was one of 35 companies selected from 800 applicants to present at the World's Investment Conference in La Baule, France this summer of 2007.

Prior to BioFire and her MBA, Kristina established a reputation as a senior marketing and business development leader with an outstanding track record of delivering high-level corporate growth / expansion strategies within the high tech market. Her expertise has consistently resulted in elevated visibility, improved brand equity, and optimized positioning for all organizations to which she has provided her talents to, from start-ups to pre and post IPO and already established companies.
