Michael Nelson’s Summary

Mr. Nelson is a leader in improving the business operations of world-class organizations in the commercial, federal, and defense sectors. Michael has extensive experience in strategy creation and analysis, process improvement including BPR, BPMS, and Six Sigma, Change Management, Communications, Training, Organizational Design, and Requirements Analysis and is a self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced environment. During his tenure in the Marine Corps, Michael led efforts in strategic and operational planning and execution, as well as maintenance, logistics, and human capital processes. He possesses more than 17 years of diverse international experience in more than 60 countries, with expertise in operations, project management, business transformation and organizational leadership.
