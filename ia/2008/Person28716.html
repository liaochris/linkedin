Bryan Ingram’s Summary

Bryan Ingram's multi-faceted career and experience has brought success in numerous industries. Having been called a "modern-day renaissance man" by the Rocky Mountain News in Denver, Ingram has comprehensive experience in strategic vision and expansion of small to large scale businesses.

Presently, Ingram serves as the Executive Vice President at Neoganda Digital Agency.

Previously, Ingram served as RED Interactive's Director of Corporate Strategy and Growth, and has successfully launched two start-up companies: EOS Enterprises, Inc. and Greenleaf Financial, Inc. EOS, an online strategy and web development consulting firm , whose client base has included Disney Core Services, multi-million dollar real estate holding companies, to celebrity personalities such as Val Kilmer; and Greenleaf Financial, Inc., a progressive green real estate financing firm.

Prior to starting his own companies, Bryan served as Director of Development at Southern Arizona Community Academy (SACA) While at the Academy in 2004, Ingram not only made SACA the first and only Microsoft IT Academy in Arizona, he also developed and executed a comprehensive growth plan which resulted in attendance doubling from 600 to 1200 in two years. Ingram now serves as a Governing School Board member in Arizona.

Concurrently, Ingram is an award-winning songwriter/composer with credits including CBS, ABC, E! Entertainment Television, Fuel TV, and global advertising campaigns including Adidas Originals. Not just musically inclined, Bryan played professional baseball with the Colorado Rockies Organization before sustaining a career-ending shoulder injury.

Ingram's eye to consistently identify upcoming talent and his ability to "see ahead of the curve" has continued to serve as a catalyst to bring together fantastic people and ideas in bringing about growth and innovation.
