Sam Ford’s Summary

I maintain three professional selves. As "Director of Customer Insights," I work with PR agency Peppercom to provide thought leadership on how audiences are using tools to communicate with one another and with brands. As a research affiliate with MIT's Convergence Culture Consortium, I have ongoing academic conference presentations, publications, and projects in media studies more broadly and specifically in fan studies and television studies. Finally, as a journalist in Kentucky, I currently write a weekly column entitled "From Beaver Dam to Brooklyn."

Sam Ford’s Specialties:

Fan studies, audience research, trends in digital communication, transmedia storytelling, television studies.
