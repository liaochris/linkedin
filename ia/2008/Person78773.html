Ian Gertler’s Summary

Ian Gertler is the President & CEO of Symplegades - a respected marketing, branding, public/investor relations, fundraising (corporate/VC/angel), communications and corporate strategy consultancy. His efforts have generated countless successes in the consumer and business sectors.

Gertler has served in full-time, consulting and volunteer capacities with established Fortune 500 leaders, early-stage entities, community venues and advertising firms. He has guided Internet, technology, financial services, telecommunications, media, education, healthcare, business services, CPG, sports and government organizations. These have included Computer Associates (CA), TRAVELSAVERS (international travel consortium), American Home Mortgage, 1-800-FLOWERS.com, FindWhat.com (now MIVA), CMP Media, Trade Show Fabrications, The Nexus Group and Special Olympics among others. He has also been active in the wireless/mobile arena (text messaging, mobile marketing & applications). As a result, he has been a sought-after speaker.

Among the accomplishments & honors:

* International Who's Who List of Professionals

* Web Marketing Association competition judge (numerous years)

* Leading creative marketer -- the first edition of Seth Godin's "Bull Market"

* Delta Mu Delta International Honor Society (Business Administration)

Gertler received a Bachelor of Science in Business from the State University of New York (Oswego), with a concentration in marketing and a minor in Economics. He currently serves in advisory & Board capacities for Intellareturn (RFID), www.iwearitwell.com (apparel/cancer research) and College Tonight (social networking). He has also been an active entrepreneur serving as a founding member or advisor to many start-up initiatives (i.e. a proposed international venture addressing China's small-to-medium size business arena with Inc., an online viral marketing business and efforts with NFL Hall of Fame legend Lawrence Taylor, NY Giants #56).
