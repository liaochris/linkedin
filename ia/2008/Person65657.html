Frank Rios, MBA, MCS, Six Sigma’s Summary

Frank Rios is currently a Program Manager on contract at the USDA where he is providing leadership and technical expertise for part of the Optimization and Stabilization initiative funded by Congress. He is primarily responsible for maintaining the joint-venture contract between BSIT/n-Link and the Government.

He holds an MBA in Project Management as well as a Masters of Computer Science degree. Finally, as a Six Sigma Certified Greenbelt, he is able to improve existing applications as well as business processes.
