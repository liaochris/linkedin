Imran Hameed’s Summary

Imran is a Principal in the Technology Services practice at Capgemini. He has over 13 years of Business & Technology consulting experience with Fortune 500 clients in North America & Europe. Imran specializes in Enterprise Architecture and large-scale technical program management delivery, focusing on mission critical systems. Other responsibilities have included business process engineering & transformation, and design and development of enterprise-scale n-tier applications.

Imran has also led various successful sales initiatives with roles encompassing, pursuit leadership, cost estimation, staffing, financial accounting, and contract analysis. His extensive professional experience has allowed him to author white papers for client focused summits & industry recognized publications & conferences.
