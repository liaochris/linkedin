Tommy Templin’s Summary

I help Call Centers improve their service quality, cost, and profit performance by improving the speed and quality of their forecasting, planning and analysis process. The goal is to spend less time generating more accurate performance forecasts, hiring plans, budgets, investment business cases, and “what-if” scenarios for your contact centers.

Are you addressing any of these common concerns?

• Time and difficulty managing the budget process
• Need to align the call center with corporate goals and objectives
• Inaccurate & unrealistic budget data
• Inability to accurately forecast staff plans
• Spend more time managing the spreadsheets that the data they are supposed to contain
• “What if” scenario planning difficulty

If you are interested in strategic planning for Call Centers I am interested in talking with you!
