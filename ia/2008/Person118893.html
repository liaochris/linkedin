Manish Talwar’s Summary

Highly talented and accomplished Engineering Manager with over 17 years of software architecture, design, development and management experience in real-time embedded systems. Significant contributor in VoIP, ATM, Broadband Data Communications and Class 5 switching products. More than a million lines in deployment in PacketCable environment.

Lead Call Processing Team for development of Call Processing features, CALEA solution, Media Gateway Controller and Media Gateway Architecture, Design and Development. Responsible for OEM of MG from Audiocodes. Managed system scaling effort in Carrier Class Cable VoIP switch to 55 calls/sec. Lead Redundancy and Fault Tolerant Architecture, Design and Development. MGCP/SIP Development. Technical Coordinator for outsourcing team. Stabilizing system and troubleshooting initial customer deployments.

Architecture and Design of design of Fault Management in a distributed high capacity ca-service ATM Switch (up to 2.5Tbps). Lead a cross functional team of 12 engineers for central-processor redundancy in another ATM switch (10Gbps).

Managed a team of engineers for design and development of Call Control features in air-to-ground cellular communication system software for airplanes.

Significant contributor in scaling the architecture of a PSTN switch from 10,0000 Busy Hour Call Attempts to 3,00,000 BHCA and then subsequently to 8,00,000 BHCA. These switches have a deployment of more than 24 million lines in India’s PSTN. Lead a team of 20 engineers on performance analysis, traffic generators, protocol analyzers and automatic testers for PSTN switch. Interface with government regulatory bodies for switch certification, testing and acceptance.
