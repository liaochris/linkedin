Paula Stoltz’s Summary

Interior Redesign: a service the updates the style and décor of your home by utilizing your current furnishings and accessories in a fresh way. Your space will capture your style, revitalizing your space and deliver a highly stylized room that is unique to your life style. The room is emptied, architecture examined, focal points indicated and furniture placement determined. Additional accessories may be recommended to enhance the room.

Other Redesign Services: Art and accessory placement, pulling eccentric décor together, setting up a new home after a move, and making spaces clutter free.

Home Staging: By staging your home your home will sell quickly and at a higher price. First impressions are everything. Curb appeal is examined and proper suggestions made. A full service staging is available (contractors will be provided, if needed) or the home staging can be done by you and I will provide a list of suggestions.

Wallpapering: Wallpapering with precision and removing wallpaper services are available. Wallpaper suggestions either through online services or books available.

Painting: Color consultation, stenciling and applying paint is available. I produce a good, clean finish and pay attention to detail. I use the right paint, select the proper product, and use professional-style techniques giving you great results.

Exterior Decorating: Suggestions of plants and plant placements. Accessorizing on and around your exterior will give it life and help show off your family's personality. I can help you give your outdoor living spaces a beautiful look with a cohesive, harmonious feel. I will work with you to create areas that are both visually pleasing and functional. By appointment and for a nominal fee, I will make a one hour consultation followed by written suggestions to make your garden retreat more charming, cohesive, useful, and harmonious. I can also plant pots or window boxes and shop for accessories.
