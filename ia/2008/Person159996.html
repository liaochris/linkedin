Executive manager offering significant experience in operations, engineering, quality, and international systems consulting within a variety of businesses across diverse industry segments such as glass, metals, computer hardware and software, specialty chemicals and gas and fluid conveyance. Results-focused and effectual leader with a proven ability to take an organization through a start-up or a turn-around. Talent for managing change as a competitive weapon by proactively identifying and resolving problems – reversing negative quality trends, building relationships with customers and suppliers, maximizing productivity, controlling costs, automating accounting and inventory systems, improving new product introductions, and delivering multi-million dollar improvements to the bottom line with an emphasis on team-building.
