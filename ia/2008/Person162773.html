Robin Chakrabarti’s Summary

Robin brings over 10 years of experience in mergers and acquisitions across diversified industries both in North America and Europe combined with hands-on operational experience in consumer packaged goods and distribution.

Prior to his current role, Robin was General Manager and Regional Vice President of the largest division of the largest bottled water company in Canada, Canadian Springs, a subsidiary of Groupe Danone. In his role, Robin had full profit and loss responsibility including functional responsibility for sales and marketing, production, distribution, call centre, human resources and finance.

Prior to his operational success, Robin was Vice President Finance and Treasurer for Sparkling Spring Water Company, which operated in England, Scotland, Holland, the United States and Canada in the direct delivery home and office bottled water segment. The company consolidated 28 acquisitions over 10 years before selling its worldwide operations to Groupe Danone.

Robin’s began his career with the TD Bank Financial Group advising and managing relationships with companies in diverse industries such as newspapers, consumer packaged goods, shipping, construction, and sports and entertainment.

Robin graduated with an MBA from Simon Fraser University, Burnaby, BC and an Honours Bachelor of Commerce from McMaster University, Hamilton, ON.

Robin serves as the Chair person of Vancouver’s largest early childhood education provider; the Vancouver Society of Children’s Centers and is also a director of the Simon Fraser University Alumni Board.

Robin strongly believes in building a “work hard, play hard” entrepreneurial culture and summarizes his “rules to live by” as follows:
• “No Man is an Island”
• “The greatest risk in life is not taking one”
• “The wisest mind has something yet to learn”
• “Execution is everything”
• “Have fun along the way”
