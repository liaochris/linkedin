Toby Nixon’s Summary

Toby uses his unique blend of technical and relationship management skills in all of his endeavours to bring together people with diverse viewpoints to reach consensus and work toward common goals: over 30 years in the computer industry designing and developing communications products; management of technical standards organizations and processes; service in the state legislature and political organizations; officer and board member in many non-profit industry, charitable, community, and educational organizations; and leadership in church, including church-government relations.

Toby Nixon’s Specialties:

Influencing technical standards, public policy, legislation, and regulations; systems analysis; organization and process management; communication protocol design
