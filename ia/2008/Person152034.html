Chris Piper’s Summary

I am currently contracted to provide expertise, facilitation and grant compliance services for the development of Continuity of Operations Planning (COOP) for city, county and State government agencies in California. I am also under contract to represent non-profit emergency response and recovery service providers, such as the American Red Cross and the Salvation Army, in the integration of their services into city and county disaster response plans.

In addition, I am an established State of California government relations executive with more than 12 years experience in health care issues.My advocacy experience includes developing legislative strategies, developing communications programs in support of legislative initiatives, directing the activities of contract lobbyists, organizing and implementing grassroots campaigns, building coalitions, and monitoring and analyzing issues. I bring strong writing and public speaking skills to the tasks and have both prepared and delivered testimony before legislative committees.

My professional objective is to continue to function as a practitioner of county and state-level government relations in the areas of health care and emergency response.
