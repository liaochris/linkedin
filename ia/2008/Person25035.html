Bob Senior’s Summary

A dynamic, inspirational international business, change manager, team builder and leader with a track record of successful, profitable start-ups, turnarounds and exits in Hi-Tech hardware and software B2B environments. Extensive experience in global direct and indirect channel development, licensing, distribution, fundraising, acquisitions and post acquisition integrations. A customer focused, sales and marketing expert with track record of leading successful multi million pound contract closures, new product development initiatives and establishing and driving multi site, ISO quality manufacturing facilities. Proven ability to establish and grow multi-cultural, high performance management teams.

Bob Senior’s Specialties:

International business development, recruiting, developing and motivating high performance management teams, strategy, planning and execution. Fundraising and exits, start ups, turnarounds.
