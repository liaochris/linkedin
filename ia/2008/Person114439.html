Lisa Maggart’s Summary

I am an integrated marketing and public relations professional with more than twelve years experience in both the corporate and agency sectors. My industry expertise includes healthcare technology, healthcare, hospitality, finance, education, consumer products and real estate. Special skills include integrated communications strategy development, media relations, custom newsletters, business and web strategy, and writing (including press releases, marketing collateral, web content, feature articles, custom newsletters, executive speeches and more).

I have been fortunate to work for some of the world's most recognized brands, including Kraft General Foods, First Union Banks, Century 21, Popeyes Chicken & Biscuits, Jello, Light N'Lively, and QVC.

I currently work as a consultant, providing the above services to clients who want to increase their visibility, improve sales, maximize web site usability, gain publicity, and increase company sales and success.

Lisa Maggart’s Specialties:

I have an innate ability to take complex information and communicate it at all levels of an organization, from administrative to the C-suite. I am a strong writer and editor, with experience in marketing copy, public relations, executive speeches and web copy. My experience with both small and large companies has given me the ability to provide expert strategic counsel to help a business achieve its goals and objectives. I am results-oriented, detailed, and deadline-driven.
