Ryan Pollock’s Summary

I am a proven leader in product management and marketing in the technology industry. I am an expert at applying consumer product and marketing techniques to enterprise technologies. I'm currently part of the marketing team for Google's enterprise products, including Google Apps, the Google Mini, and the Google Search Appliance. In October 2006 Google acquired JotSpot, my previous employer, where I was a product manager and the tenth employee. I was responsible for customer acquisition and defined and implemented product features and marketing programs that led to JotSpot having more than 2,500 paying customers and 100,000 free customers at the time of its acquisition just two years after initial launch. Before JotSpot, I was a software engineer at Oracle, where I contributed to the development of visual editors for XML and Java Server Faces. I hold a B.S. magna cum laude in Computer Science from Cornell University.
