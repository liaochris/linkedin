Brent Lampe’s Summary

Your Urban Home Specialist

As a specialist in high-end, urban properties, it’s no surprise to learn that Brent lives and works in downtown Seattle. His interest in real estate began as an owner of investment properties in both Utah and Washington. He chose to make a career of his passion in 2002 – applying a rich business background including a focus in customer service and support for the high-tech industry. Brent has an warm, approachable style and genuine interest in the needs of his buyers and sellers. As a member of Luxury Living Northwest, he offers professionalism, integrity and discretion to every relationship.

“I love living in the city and sharing what it has to offer with people who are new to the area. It’s a priveledge to help people find their perfect home.”
