Meri Aaron Walker’s Summary

I'm a communication consultant and educator with three decades of success at helping people with different skills and types of intelligence get things done together. After 25 fast-paced years in Austin, Texas, I'm now living in a small town in Southern Oregon where I work mostly online, helping independent business people and teachers use virtual meeting tools to create and sustain crucial relationships - without having to drive or fly all over the globe to do it.

I'm committed to helping people who make a living sharing their expertise recalibrate their communication strategies so it's easy for them to release peoples natural intelligence, creativity, and generosity in virtual meetings.

You can learn more about this exciting new avenue of my practice at http://www.virtualmeetingstartup.com and on my blog at http://www.thevirtualmeetingcoach.com.

Meri Aaron Walker’s Specialties:

Business communication and relationship development, business management, online learning, online meeting management, instructional design for adult learning, Web 2.0 in small business and education, application of natural strengths metrics to individual and small group performance improvement.
