Darren Wallis’s Summary

Mr. Wallis is a Partner and Portfolio Manager of Permit Capital, LLC. He leads the direct private equity efforts and assists in overseeing the fund of funds investment activities of the firm. Permit Capital is an independent investment firm that is dedicated to managing the financial assets of its members and other limited partners. Their investments span many asset classes domestically and internationally, including public and private equity, distressed debt, real estate, venture capital and hedge funds.

Prior to joining Permit Capital, Mr. Wallis was Managing Partner of Osage Ventures. At Osage, Mr. Wallis was responsible for fund activities including strategy, deal sourcing, negotiation and execution, portfolio management, and harvesting. He led and served on the Boards of Directors of numerous fund investments. Before Osage Ventures, Mr. Wallis was a Director of Corporate Development for SAP AG and later a Senior Director of SAP’s Corporate Finance group. Mr. Wallis focused on the strategy and execution of the company’s global mergers, acquisitions, investment lifecycle and minority investments. He led numerous acquisitions and minority investments and helped supervised the activities of SAP Ventures.

Prior to SAP, Mr. Wallis was a Venture Partner at Cross Atlantic Capital Partners, an international private equity firm with over $600 AUM. Prior to Cross Atlantic, Mr. Wallis was with Sullivan & Cromwell as a member of their M&A group. Earlier, Mr. Wallis was a Senior Consultant with Accenture Technology Labs in Palo Alto, CA.

Mr. Wallis was recognized in 2007 by the Philadelphia Business Journal with its prestigious “40 Under 40” Award. Mr. Wallis is a commissioned officer in the United States Navy and was accepted into the Naval Nuclear Power School. Mr. Wallis received his B.S. in Chemical Engineering from the University of Notre Dame and studied law and capital markets at Duke Law School, where he received a J.D. cum laude.
