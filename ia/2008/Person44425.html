Danny Weissberg’s Summary

Mr. Weissberg has over 10 years experience in the High-Tech industry, mostly in developing telecommunications and networking software solutions.

Prior to Net-VI, Danny worked on key-projects at leading High-Tech companies: leading the Technology Infrastructure team in iPoint-Media developing large-scaled networking software solutions, in Mercury (developing the QuickTest open SDK implemented by Flash-Macromedia), in Radvision (developing the H323 Gatekeeper) and Checkpoint (developing the SecurClient).

He holds B.Sc Honors (Cum Laude) in Civil Engineering from the Technion, B.A Honors (Cum Laude) in Computer Science from the Open University, and M.A in Philosophy and Religion at Tel Aviv University .
