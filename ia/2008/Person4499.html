Alessio Jacona’s Summary

I'm a journalist, a blogger and a communication consultant, as well as the editor of Blogs4biz.info, a blog part of the Italian nanopublishing network Communicagroup.it founded and owned by Luca Ajroldi. Since December 2003, I write daily about corporate e business blogging, social networking, web 2.0, internet industry and market, new media. Since January 2008 I write about the same topics on Nova24 – IlSole24Ore as a freelance editor. My job is to keep the Italian ruling class updated on the internet revolution going on "out there".
Since September 2002 I also work as a communication consultant for Mario Dal Co’s Conmedia. Srl, where I have been working as senior web communications account.
My Goal is to help companies joining the worldwide online “conversation” among their customers and prospects.
