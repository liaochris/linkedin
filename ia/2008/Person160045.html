Richard Fenwick’s Summary

Richard is an exciting and experienced professional who holds a Warwick MBA. He has succeded in a variety of roles ranging from claims, marketing and communications, sales and finance.
Richard possesses in depth business performance skills and excellent knowledge of business administration. He is a strong team player with a documented record of delivery.
He has an excellent engaging personality and has a very analytical mind. He is outgoing, confident and seen as someone with innovative thinking processes that always suit the context to which they are applied.
