Susan Logan’s Summary

My interest in real estate began at the mere age of 20 years old whereby I attended classes at Rollins College in Winter Park, FL and received my Florida real estate license. I personally have bought and sold over 25 properties during my career and have assisted many clients buy/sell their primary home or investment properties as well. I have worked with many corporate employees transfering to or moving from the area including Chrysler Corp, Lockheed Martin, American Automobile Association (AAA), Westinghouse, University of Central Florida, Florida Hospital, Pfizer, Hilton Hotel, Suntrust, and Federal Express to name a few. Our company is a leader when it comes to corporate relocations. The goal for Logan Land & Homes, Inc. is to provide the highest and best level of service to each and every customer. We thrive on repeat business and referral business because of this philosophy. We have the years of experience and the integrity of all our realtor associates within the company. We were recognized by our peers and featured in the May, 2007 Broker Agent Magazine (Greater Orlando Edition - front cover) as being one of the more successful and highly regarded agents in the area.

Susan Logan’s Specialties:

Logan Land & Homes, Inc. has been in business since 1989 and has withstood the test of time in both good and bad real estate markets. Negotiating effective contracts is our strength and having the knowledge of the business ensures that we get to the closing table with no detail overlooked for both our buyers and sellers alike.
