Shuki Idan’s Summary

More than 20 years of international experience in the high tech and telecommunications industries, as an industry professional, as well as entrepreneur and consultant. Managed large groups of professionals working globally and generated multi million dollars of quarterly revenue streams.
Worked closely with many of the world leading corporations and more than 30 communications service providers in Europe, America and APAC, as well as with major System Integrators and partners.
Widely recognized as a subject matter expert in the area of CRM and Business Intelligence - data warehousing and data mining, credit risk management, analytical CRM, segmentation, churn management, fraud analysis, customer profitability, sales analysis and marketing optimization. Other expertise domains: information systems, telecommunications and networking, VoIP, signal and image processing, embedded systems, neural networks, predictive modeling for financial applications.
Recipient of numerous clients, industry and scientific awards and contributor to major domain related publications, ITU standards, European Union professional committees, industry forums and events.
Holds B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering from Technion in Israel and Ph.D. in Computer Science from the ENST in France.
