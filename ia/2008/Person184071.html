Sushil Siddesh’s Summary

Sushil is an Engineer whose comfort zone ranges from designing circuits to writing software. He is always interested (excited about) in new technologies, meeting new people and exploring new avenues. These days he has the notion that he can (ahem ahem) change the world. Silly him!

Going back in time - working with HuaWei was an incredible experience for him, more because he got to work with a great bunch of people, than it being his first job. He even co-authored two patent drafts, the first for a testing team at HuaWei!

In his quest for that extra zing in life, he accepted an offer to work at a startup. It was fun working at that startup - Sensact Applications. The technology was exciting, he met a lot of interesting people, and the problems were endless (and not confined to just technology), what else could he ask for! But then, the "zing" remained elusive. So, after a lot of thought, he decided to go a step further in the "zing" search - do it himself. He promises to explain this cryptic paragraph soon!

Apart from work, he is also passionate about movie making - though he hasn't made one yet! He has recently been introduced to the world of stained glass, and that he claims is his current "hobby".

He is a chronic biker, who can't help but get on his bike and explore the country side once in a while. His travels have taken him from the quaint Western ghats to the mesmerizing Himalayas.
