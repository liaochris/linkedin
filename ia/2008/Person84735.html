Jason Cranford Teague’s Summary

Jason has been an innovator in the Web design world for over 12 years. His work has included the first Web based magazine in 1994, Computer Mediated Communications, to today where is the Creative Director for RED (www.beRED.com) which he recently successfully launched from being an AOL exclusive experience onto the Web.

Jason has written numerous books about the Web, print design, video production, and photography, including the best Selling CSS, DHTML, and Ajax (Peachpit Press) and Photoshop at Your Fingertips (Sybex). You can find Jason online at webbedENVIRONMENTS (www.webbedenvironments.com).
