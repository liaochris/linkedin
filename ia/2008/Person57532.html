Saeed El-Darahali’s Summary

Saeed El-Darahali has more than five years of management experience in the IT industry, with an interest in strategic partnering, corporate financing, strategic growth, operations, and sales and marketing management. Since 2004, Saeed has been working as a venture analyst and adviser with InNOVAcorp, an organization enabling high growth early stage Nova Scotia companies to accelerate commercialization of their technologies and increase competitiveness in export markets. In this role, Saeed is responsible for assessing investment opportunities, conducting extensive due diligence, and business planning.

Over the years, Saeed has helped companies identify alliance opportunities through industry/business research. He also mentored numerous companies, helping them translate goals into tactics. He has spearheaded IT improvements that supported growth, enhanced efficiencies, and increased customer satisfaction for a number of companies. He has experience deploying CMS, CRM, e-commerce, lead generation, and web systems.

A serial entrepreneur, Saeed started his first business at age 14 and has since started and sold a number of other businesses. He enjoys sharing his experiences with start-up companies, offering insights into growing a business and becoming successful.

Saeed has leveraged his experience in online marketing, web development, blogging, press release strategies, multi-media, branding, and e-mail marketing to help companies achieve a strong presence on the Internet and increase year-over-year sales.

Saeed has participated as speaker at many conferences, including NAWeb, CABI, 7th World Congress of e-Business, Enterprize Atlantic and ARCN. A part-time lecturer at Saint Mary’s University, he has taught a senior level computer information systems course, System Analysis and Design.

Saeed El-Darahali’s Specialties:

Saeed has extensive experience in venture capital financing, patent knowledge, project management, system architecture, search engine optimization, e-marketing, ecommerce, emerging internet technologies and enterprise growth strategies.
