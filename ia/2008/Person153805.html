Etienne Charlier’s Summary

Founder and Managing Director of procurAsia. Created procurAsia to provide industrial sourcing assistance to Western companies. Company builds on his extensive management and industrial experience in the Chinese environment as well as from his deep network in China. procurAsia is now a dynamic and growing company with clients in Benelux, France, Switzerland, UK, Skandinavia Middle East and US. Also developing business in Africa with partners.

10-year+ management experience in China, working in several executive positions for Alcatel (Telecom).

While working for Alcatel, was instrumental in the development of the broadband business in China , led the launch and ramp up of production of ADSL in China from nothing to 10 million lines per year and created a R&D center that has produced several new products for the worldwide market.

Member of the Board of Director of the Benelux Chamber of Commerce in China, a non-profit organization aiming at supporting the enterprises of the Benelux in China. (www.bencham.org)

Lives in Shanghai, China, with his family.
