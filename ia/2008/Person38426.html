Carolyn L Burke’s Summary

I work with leaders, officers, directors and executives to shape personal and in turn guide organizational integrity and ethics. The key to driving ethical business is to take personal responsibility for every decision made, to stay true to your personal values while engaging organiztional integrity: Manage your personal liability. Reduce corporate risk. Prosper ethically.

My keynotes on integrity and ethics, on how to integrate ethical practice with long term planning, emphasize individual responsibility and integrity within the larger ethical practices of your organization.
