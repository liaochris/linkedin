Scott Frost’s Summary

Scott is currently a Principal Consultant with Adea (Dallas-based Technology Services company) an founder of Frost Consultant Group, LLC

Scott holds a BA in Music and a Masters in Total Quality Management

Scott has 18 years of experience in Information Technology in the food service and retail industries. He brings a wealth of knowledge from inside corporate IT organizations on the design, use, and maintenance of dashboards and scorecards. Integration with both the strategic and delivery decision processes is a crucial success factor.

More recently, as Vice President, Scott lead end-to-end Supply Chain Solutions for Blockbuster, the world’s largest retailer in the entertainment rental industry. He supported 8,500 stores, tens of millions of units/week, and a million square foot warehouse with light manufacturing.

Scott has delivered over $70 million in capital projects in the past 5 years with 100% OTOB.

PREVIOUS EXPERIENCE
From his 10 years in restaurant systems at YUM! Brands and eCommerce work in B2B data synchronization the viaLink Company, Scott progressed through IT roles from network operations, programmer, BPM, QA, application portfolio management, and operations and finance consultant. His multi-disciplinary approach, passion for excellence, and ability to translate between Business and IT has made Scott a recognizable expert and powerful strategic partner for companies.

Scott participates in the Dallas chapters of SIM and TENG, is an upcoming speaker at Chain Store Age TOPSS Conference, and sits on the Advisory Board for the University of Texas at Dallas Center for Intelligent Supply Networks (C4ISN).
