Fernando Samaniego’s Summary

Fernando Samaniego is an international new media specialist and the President of International New Media Consulting. He has a fifteen-year track record of transforming traditional media companies into thriving centers of profitable innovation.

Fernando builds high performance teams and collaborative organizational cultures to transform M€ companies from market players to recognized market leaders. He leverages Greenfield, M&A and organic growth strategies and quick to market execution to create sustainable growth. He has transformed failing companies in the face of decreasing income, large debt and economic recessions.

As a multilingual, multicultural, forward-thinking intrapreneur, he has an incredible breadth and depth of experience, having worked with organizations across the globe in all areas of the media, including newspapers, magazines, television, radio, mobile, Internet, news, classifieds, and portals.

In addition to his experienced, global perspective, he has a keen understanding of social and technical trends that keep his clients on the cutting edge and on the front lines of changing how we live, work and communicate.

To learn about Fernando's thoughts on the world of new media, click on the link above or visit his website and blog at www.fernandosamaniego.com.
