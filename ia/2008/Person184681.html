Sylvain Hellegouarch’s Summary

Enthusiastic technologist specializing in software development as well as performance and load testing of medium to large infrastructures.

I have a solid experience of Python web development: gathering of user scenarios, design with separation of concerns, implementation, testing and documentation.

I also have a strong experience of putting up a test plan for load or performance tests of client/server applications: understanding the environment through various channels, definition of the test scope, evaluation of the risks and their mitigation, creation of realistic scenarios and their implementation, results analysis.

For the past few years I've been focusing on interactions of distributed and heterogeneous services for the web and the way they can help the gathering, processing and filtering of localized data to improve both user experience and business value.
