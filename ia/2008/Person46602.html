David Friedman’s Summary

I have achieved one of my goals as Chief Marketing Officer for a medium to large technology-based or service company.

> Broad background in strategic marketing, product development, multi-channel distribution systems and P&L
> An ability to blend technical expertise with business acumen to grow businesses by thinking strategically and executing tactically
> Equally comfortable in large corporations or small startups
> Background in the high tech fields of telecommunications, internet, wireless, broadband, and satellite services.
> International experience particularly in Europe and Asia

Currently I am EVP and Chief Marketing Officer for ATX Group which provides telematics and information services to drivers and automobile manufacturers in Europe and in the US.

Prior to this position, I was VP, Marketing for Connexion by Boeing, a startup within the Boeing Company and which provided internet and entertainment services to passengers and airlines thorough on board routers connected via satellite communications.

Prior to my position at Connexion, I was interim VP, Marketing for Internap Network Services. I was VP, Marketing (Chief Marketing Officer) for US Cellular, the 7th largest wireless company in the United States and was a senior officer of this publicly traded company.

My career spans a who's who of top telecom and communications services, having served in an executive capacity with companies such as Ameritech and RCA.

I hold a Master's Degree in Business Administration with a specialty in Economics from George Washington University; a MSEE from Columbia University; and a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from City College of New York where I graduated Summa Cum Laude.

I am a charter member of the Marketing Leadership Council of the Conference Board, a member of American Marketing Association, and a prior officer of the international Product Development and Management Association.
