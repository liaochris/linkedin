John Sisler’s Summary

Since starting st Brulant John has worked in three areas. In the Microsoft Practice John Sisler was engaged with Marriott who needed a portal based solution that would maintain corporate branding while not exceeding franchise budgets. The platform chosen was DNN which was used in a leveraged support model and is hosted by Brulant to accommodate the enterprise needs while not bursting the franchise budgets.

Next John Sisler worked on an IBM WebSphere Commerce and Web Content Management system at Borders book stores. Borders goal was to bring their commerce site in-house and end their hosting relationship with Amazon which was the largest migration away from Amazon recorded. As part of this strategy Borders decided to focus their effort towards the online channel and sell of parts of the company that were not as profitable such as their international brick and mortar locations.

Finally John Sisler moved into Brulant's Search Engine Optimization practice where as a technical architect he has worked towards designing SEO solutions for clients that are platform and strategy specific. Working with the team in Brulant's SEO practice makes working with several dozen clients not only possible but rewarding. This last year Brulant was named in the top 20 SEO firms in the world and this year already we have increased our visibility by accepting speaking engagements from search conferences located thoughout the country. John Sisler most recently was invited to speak at Microsoft Corporate offices on the topic of MOSS SEO.
