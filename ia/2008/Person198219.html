Travis Wade Ivy’s Summary

As a visual effects artist, Wade creates composite images for feature films, digitally modifying sets (and actors, and you-name-it), integrating live-action greenscreen and CG elements, executing complex wire & rig removals, and performing acts of digital color correction and image manipulation of all kinds.

With almost 30 feature film credits to date, as well as various TV shows, music videos, commercials and web projects, Wade brings a wealth of practical experience and creative problem-solving to every shot. His training in traditional optical printing, on-set experiences early in his career, and enthusiasm for film, its history and its future, give him a unique perspective on the art and craft of visual effects.

Wade lives in Culver City, California.
