Joe D'Andrea’s Summary

Principal member of Liquid Joe LLC, a New Jersey-based company focusing on the application of standards-based internet technologies. A Google Enterprise Professional with over twelve years of search technology experience (five with the Google Search Appliance), I offer consulting, development and training services to enterprise businesses, vendors and design agencies.

Previously technical producer for (pre-merger) AT&T's corporate site - www.att.com, lead architect and developer for AT&T's Web Standards Initiative, Google Search Appliance wrangler, and technical liaison. Experienced in cultivating productive business relationships with vendors. Troubleshooter and technical coach for customers and peers. Frequent advisor to Corporate Security, Law and Government Affairs, Branding, Architecture, Information Technology Solutions, Research and Public Relations regarding internet technologies and best practices.

Interested in high-profile web standards-based internet and client-side applications.
