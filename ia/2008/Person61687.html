Evans Wroten’s Summary

Currently I serve as CIO for SilkRoad technology, inc (www.silkroadtech.com) and InterAct Public Safety Systems, Inc. (www.interactsys.com).

I am responsible for driving SilkRoad and InterAct infrastructure initiatives. I bring more than 15 years of experience in product development and senior management roles within the technology industry. Prior to joining SilkRoad, I served as the vice president for Technology Infrastructure Services at divine, inc. I joined divine through the acquisition of NetUnlimited, an integrated full-service provider of Internet services and computer network solutions, where I was the founder, president and CEO for 8 years. I've been fortunate enough to be designated one of the Triad's Top Ten People to Watch, was a finalist for the Ernst & Young Carolina's Entrepreneur of the Year and have received numerous awards including the North Carolina Small Business Person of the Year and the Triads Top 40 Under 40 Leadership Award. I have been elected to many boards and committees including the Winston-Salem Chamber of Commerce Technology Council in which he served as chair, the board of advisors for the Winston-Salem Chamber of Commerce and the Charlotte Chamber of Commerce, and the board of directors for the Winston-Salem Science and Technology Roundtable, Techcentricity, Piedmont Club, and !diealliance.

I also am founder and CEO of EVCO Enterprises, LLC which is a holding company for several web properties, and founder and CEO of Elizabeth Evans, LLC, a real-estate development and management company.

I've been an entrepreneur and risk taker all my life. Nothing gives me more pleasure than starting something from nothing and building value and relevance. I loath failure even though I've had my share. Technology, marketing, custom choppers, travel and real estate are a few of my passions.
