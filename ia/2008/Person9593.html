Amol Pande’s Summary

e-Zest Solutions is an ISO 9001:2000 certified offshore software outsourcing company with expertise and focus on Outsourced Product Development and custom software development services through global software development model.

Founded in 1998 at Mumbai India, and pioneering the offshore software project and software product development process, e-Zest has consistently delivered quality software products and solutions to leading software companies and global corporations.

Amol is an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineer. He is also an industry expert in e-business architecture design and implementation. Amol is responsible for leading e-Zest's global e-Architecture group which helps customers turn new technology into powerful business solutions, and re-architect mission critical legacy systems for the new demands of e-Business.
