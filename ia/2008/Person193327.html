Luis Guzmán’s Summary

In August of 1996 Luis arrived in Colorado at the home of his aunt and uncle...no job, no plan fresh out of high school and just a dream. After years of life and love, Luis applied his experience and schooling to a company born out of necessity to help those who could not communicate properly in their ideas and concerns. Companies and individuals have now realized that a bridge must be built in this new global community we have created. Tulingo Services and Luis Guzman are just the team to get it done.

By offering services at a better rate and adding superior client treatment, Tulingo is aligning itself to be the model for excellence in the industry of communications between multiple languages. By having innovative ideas to age old dilemmas Tulingo can secure a place in the ever increasing market of communications. Tulingo Services is ready to offer clear communication at the speed of technology....
