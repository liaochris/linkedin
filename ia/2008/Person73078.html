George. Spafford’s Summary

George is a Principal Consultant with Pepperweed and an experienced practitioner in business and IT operations. He is a prolific author and speaker, and has consulted and conducted training on regulatory compliance, IT Governance, and process improvement in the U.S., Australia, New Zealand and China. Publications include co-authorship of “The Visible Ops Handbook" and “Visible Ops Security”. George holds an MBA from Notre Dame, a BA in Materials and Logistics Management from Michigan State University and an honorary degree from Konan Daigaku in Japan. He is a ITIL Service Manager, TOCICO Jonah and a Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA). George is a current member of the ISACA, the IIA, and the IT Process Institute.

George. Spafford’s Specialties:

Consulting, professional author, strategy, IT governance, IT process improvement, ITIL v2 and v3, CISA, Theory of Constraints (TOC), ISO 27000:2005, ISO 27001:2005, COBIT, regulatory compliance, compliance frameworks, project management, and training.
