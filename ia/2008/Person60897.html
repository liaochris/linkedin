Erling Paulsen’s Summary

Currently Sales and Commercial Director at Elkem Solar (Norwegian based company delivering Solar Grade Silicon). My department's main responsibilites are:
* Sales and marketing
* Raw material sourcing
* Purchasing
* Business development

I joined the company June 1st 2007, and held the position as Vice President for Business Development until January 1st 2008 (mainly focusing on M&A and strategic raw material sourcing).

Broad management consultancy experience after ~4 years with The Boston Consulting Group (BCG). Project experience both from consumer goods and industrial goods companies, and deep knowledge within the media (newspaper/online) industry. In addition, project management experience after ~1 year as Project Leader within BCG.
