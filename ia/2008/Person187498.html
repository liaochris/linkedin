Theo Bell’s Summary

U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) Public Buildings Service (PBS) Consultant and Corporate Real Estate Advisor. As a veteran in working with military installations, Theo is recognized by his peers as a results-oriented senior-level government relations and real estate consultant for UGL Equis’ Washington DC office. He has extensive experience in developing and managing government contracts and expanding commercial market presence. He has more than 20 years in business management, construction and real estate. When combined with his expertise in leading edge technologies, strategic marketing, tactical sales and key account management, Theo's experience indicates a successful combination for high-visibility public projects. Having worked with over 40 agencies worldwide, Theo's record for completing complex negotiations with governments and their suppliers is outstanding for its high degree of compliance with public procedure and standards.
Significant positions Theo has held include Executive Vice President of Wellington Knolls Real Estate, Executive Vice President at Capitol CREAG Real Estate, Senior Manager of Government Affairs for SMED International; Director of Government Affairs for Formica Corporation and National Account Manager to the U.S. Government with Wilsonart International. Previously, he served as a pilot in the U.S. Army during first Gulf War flying UH-60 “Blackhawk” helicopters and has a TSSCI clearance. Theo has obtained and managed government and military contracts throughout the United States for firms specializing in interior construction services and products. He promotes all services and product lines for both military and civil accounts. While being responsible for new client development, he also monitors Federal industry trends on behalf of UGL Equis clients.

Theo Bell’s Specialties:

Theo currently serves in an officially appointed position as both the Howard County, MD Council Representative and Co-Chairman for the Fort Meade Growth & Base Realignment and Closure (BRAC) Task Force. He is also Vice President and Board Member of the Federal Real Property Association.
