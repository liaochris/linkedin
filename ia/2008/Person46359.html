David Decraene’s Summary

As from 2004, David has been employed by Language and Computing NV (L&C) as a subject matter expert in ontology applications and as an active developer/designer of large scale (formal) ontologies.
In the past David Decraene has been active as a researcher in the field of signal transduction and oncology at the department of dermatology, University of Leuven and in the field of photoprotectant and anti-oxidant compounds in research cooperation with the Estee Lauder Research Laboratories. He achieved a Ph.D. in medical sciences in 2004.

David Decraene’s Specialties:

OWL/RDFS, RDFa, Formal Ontology, Ontology applications, Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Discovery, AJAX, jQuery, semantic web technology, microformats.
Over 4000 hours of active ontology development.
Terminologies: SNOMED, ICD, MESH, MEDDRA, MEDCIN, RXNORM, LOINC, ...
