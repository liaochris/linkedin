Bruce Potterton’s Summary

I have over twenty years of managing multi-functional teams that combine strategy, product positioning & corporate marketing.

My management experience includes successfully building and leading start-up, turn-around and high-growth high-technology organizations to achieve strategic objectives. I have built highly effective teams for organizations, which include recruiting employees, consultants, agencies and or freelancers, pressed to accomplish extraordinary things.

I founded and was managing partner of Potterton/West, a corporate marketing firm specializing in strategic product positioning/messaging, corporate marketing and channel development. My management role in the firm encompassed initiation and implementation of strategic business plans, account management and planning, budgeting and operations. I am experienced at generating and servicing corporate clientele, both as a executive and as a project manager. Our clients were in a wide range of industries including, retail, banking, insurance, hardware and software. The spectrum of these clients ranged from emerging to Fortune 500 companies.

I hold a B.S. Degree from the University of Minnesota.
