Steve Cherry’s Summary

I have been recruiting since 1995 in various technical business domains. I have been in defense and aerospace for the last 6 years and currently recruit for the Unmanned Systems group of Northrop Grumman in San Diego, California.

I do freelance photography in my free time and specialize in pro sports, event, digital art photo and slideshow creation. My portfolio and photoblog can be viewed here: http://www.stevencherry.com

photoblog: www.dailyframe.com

Steve Cherry’s Specialties:

Technical recruiting and specialized sourcing in software development and systems engineering, semiconductor, telecom/wireless, mobile handsets, bluetooth, healthcare, imagery intelligence, C4ISR, all aspects of aerospace including avionics, electronic warfare, flight control, GN&C, low observables(LO), rotorcraft, and UAV development.
