Steve O'Donnell’s Summary

A highly motivated and experienced senior executive with excellent business acumen coupled with deep and broad technical skills and a successful track record in Programme and Project Management

A progressive career with over 28 years experience working up to and including board level in large multinational organisations in both international and domestic markets.

* International experience in Europe, North America and ROW.
* Strong strategic thinker strengthened by completion of “Leadership in Professional Services Firms” programme at Harvard Business School.
* Experienced in Business Process Management and delivery of Six Sigma and Lean BPM Projects.
* 100% track record of success in delivering project and programmes including project rescues.
* Clear understanding of application and infrastructure architectures in the Investment Banking and wireline and wireless Telecommunications industries.
* A proven self-starter, having taken a consulting business from start-up to winning a major industry award for rapid growth within 3 years.
