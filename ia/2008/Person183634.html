Lars-Bo Johansen’s Summary

Established in 2001 as a webagency, today leading mobile agency in the nordic region. At Mobile Marketing we take our responsibility seriously. When we take on an assignment, we are committed to delivering the promised results. This is among other things reflected in our high operational reliability, our excellent safety performance and our commitment to serve the best mobile solutions. Mobile Marketing's performance demonstrates the ability to prevent negative return of investment and our well-educated staff solves problems on the spot and our in-house designers currently develops innovative solutions to make the operation even more efficient. Our focus is campaignsites, 30 days MAX and 4-600% return of investment! Thats a succescriteria and standard for our solutions.

Effective tools, easy-access, userfriendly reports and a 2-language support is what we aimed at, and we did it! We have many kinds of customers, and normally our API is more than enough, but our whitelabel-solution for advertising and mediaagencies are more than relevant to ensure that Mobile Marketing is 100% anonymous and only provides hosting, support and software. Our webprogramming goes in many directions. Our customers are used to quick support and affordable solutions. They are private and public institutions, small and large companies, and media- and advertising agencies.
