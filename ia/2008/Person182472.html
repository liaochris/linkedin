Dale Stokdyk’s Summary

Internet marketing consultant with experience helping companies small-to-large grow their online sales and profits.

I founded 'marketing2oh' in 2007 as a consultancy to small-to-medium size businesses, dedicated to identifying and implementing the right mix of internet marketing tactics to grow clients' sales and reduce their cost/sale.

As the number and complexity of internet sales channels have grown, I've helped clients to prioritize, focusing on their best, high-ROI alternatives. Typical projects include search engine marketing (SEM) -- including search-engine-optimization (SEO) and pay-per-click (PPC) advertising -- eCommerce / shopping cart solutions, email marketing, and blog development and marketing.

My personal involvement with internet marketing began in 1997 as co-founder and President of an online gift store, me2u gifts.

I've also worked for small-to-medium size companies (NEBS Inc.; Optelec US) as well as well-known brands (Hallmark Cards Inc., and Tandy / Radio Shack) in advertising, product management, strategic planning, and sales management roles.

Honors graduate, University of Texas, Austin (MBA), and Trinity University, San Antonio (B.A. in English and Business Administration).
