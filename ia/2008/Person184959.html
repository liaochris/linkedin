Tom Freeman’s Summary

McDermott & Bull is looking for a Senior Executive who has worked at the director level or above at fortune 1000 companies.

Ideally this person will have 15+ years of intimate involvement within the c-suite and have a thorough understanding of how companies function and how the various roles in an organization both interface and support the overall growth and development of an organization.

We are looking for an individual who has come to the realization that they are looking for a new challenge in the next phase of their career. This opportunity will allow you to hold personal ownership of your own success rather than being dependent on the success of others whose success factors are outside your control.

We are offering the opportunity to leverage your experience and knowledge in a business development role within our firm as a consultant who will work with clients helping them find there executive talent. This is a Principal Search Consultant role, NOT a recruiting role. We are looking for people who wish to create relationships with companies at a senior executive level and assist them in their internal development by making the right hires.

We have found that individuals who have had the most success in this role have not come from an executive search background, but instead have come from the corporate world. Samplings of these roles include:

· Managing Director, Bay Area, with a $8 billion international supply chain, shipping and logistics company (warehousing and transportation)
· Vice President, Commercial Services with a $20 billion aerospace company
· Vice President Sales & Marketing with a mobile power technology company

If you have the entrepreneurial spirit to step out of your comfort zone and explore a new career path please contact me at
freeman@mbsearch.net
