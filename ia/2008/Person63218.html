Federico Pomi’s Summary

I started developing computer programs when I was a teen and I soon discovered my interest in user interface design.

As soon as a modem came in to my house, I was also immediatly fascinated by the way people interacts behind a monitor and by the social dynamics I could study by operating a BBS and later managing popular comunity focused web sites.

In the last few years I started looking into marketing and economics strategies, with a focus on the Internet businesses.

I strongly believe that the marketing opportunities opened by the Internet are actually unused or misused, even by world class brands.

I'm mainly interested in Internet B2C projects, where the mix of my skills in interface designs, communities knowledge and project management gives the best.

Project Management, Web Development, Web Marketing, Database Design, User Interface Design, Web Communities.
