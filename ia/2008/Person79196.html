Michael Hill’s Summary

Michael K. Hill is a leading expert on Identity Theft. While others are scrambling to tell people to lock their mailboxes and shred their credit card statements, Mr. Hill is warning consumers of the potential health risks, Social Security and tax issues, financial disasters, and the reality that they could face false imprisonment as a result of someone stealing their identity. He has helped businesses to implement Identity Theft Protection programs which today protect hundred's of thousands of American's Identities and even more recently, he became Technology Director for the National Identity Theft Awareness Project providing companies and individuals access to the FTC, BBB, state and local governments and other organizations about identity theft. He has helped individuals to protect themselves from all five areas of identity theft. Mr. Hill is speaking to groups and providing training to many organizations. He has presented to various Rotary, Kiwanis Clubs of Metro Atlanta, American Business Women’s Association, Greater North Fulton Chamber, Gwinnett Chamber, Georgia Association of Business Brokers, Society for Human Resource Management, Georgia Funeral Directors Association and Independent Insurance Agents of Georgia as well as to real estate brokers, mortgage companies, banks, credit professionals, Human Resource Managers, Risk Management Professionals, corporate leaders, and average citizens.
