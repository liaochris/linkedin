Menachem Wecker’s Summary

Arts writer with experience teaching art and art history on an elementary, middle school and college level.

Painter, with work exhibited at the Makor Gallery at the 92nd Street Y (Manhattan) and at the JCC gallery in Baltimore. Illustrations and photographs have appeared in New Voices, B'nai B'rith Magazine, Forward, Jewish Press, Yeshiva University Commentator, and B'nai B'rith Today.

Freelance reviews have appeared in a wide range of publications, including Religion News Service, Arab American News, Muslim Times, Arlington Catholic Herald, Forward, Jewish Press, Jewish Week, NY Arts Magazine, NY Press and others.

Edited book, "Moses and Jesus: A Conversation," by Joel Cohen.

Menachem Wecker’s Specialties:

Researching and reviewing art, interviewing artists, studying and interpreting religious texts, tracking museum and gallery trends, editing, graphic design (print and digital), illustrating
