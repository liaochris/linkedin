Shawn Hank’s Summary

Sr. Security Engineer with 12 years of combined Security, Networking Communications, and Services experience. Providing common sense and practicality in a world of jargon and buzz words related to security and networking solutions for organizations, regardless of size.

Provide support for all technical aspects of Pre-Sales, Planning, Service Deployment, and Post-Sales cycles with Global Fortune 1000 and FTSE 200 companies desiring to incorporate various levels of security in their infrastructure. Proficient in supporting hardware solutions, managed services, and Software as a Servie (SaaS) delivery models.

Pre and Post Sales support for design, engineering, and implementation of a wide variety of vendor Information Security solutions including firewall, Network and Host based IDS, Network and Host based IPS, VPN, SIM/SEM, NBA, Managed Security Services, Professional Security Services, and Application Security.

I utilize a consultative approach to determine the best overall solution based upon customer requirements rather than force fitting requirements into a product’s capabilities. Being able to breakdown complex ideas into easy to understand concepts is one of my strengths.
