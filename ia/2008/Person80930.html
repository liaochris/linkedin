Ian McDonald’s Summary

Wide engineering experience at management & hands on across development process from bidding, requirements analysis, design (hardware, systems and software) to testing & commissioning. Experience rooted in manufacturing including large systems. Full hands-on project lifecycle experience, specialising in Test Analysis and Test Management. Frequently turned around projects that are behind schedule.

Within engineering, contributed significantly towards a number of innovative projects, that have been exhibited at international air shows and featured on BBC TV science programmes.

Tested new technologies including: Air Traffic Control, Telecommunications (Satellite, Airborne Telephone, WAP, WEB, Email plus NATO and MoD Battlefield systems), One of Europe’s first WAP sites, Fluid Dynamics, warehouse systems, desktop migration work and e-government projects.

Hands on Test experience include the full product life cycle, using extensively the V-model, Rapid Application Processes and Lean. Have used wide-ranging tools including Mercury Test Director, Mercury Quick Test Pro and the full Rational Toolset (Test Manager, Clear Quest, Clear Case, Functional Tester, Requisite Pro, Software Architect and Rational Rose).

Ian McDonald’s Specialties:

Testing: Across complete lifecycle.

Defence, Police, Aerospace,Telecommunications, Computerised Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Internet, Topographic (Mapping) Data, Digital TV, WAP, Mobile Devices, Gaming, Insurance, Local & National Government.

Lean, V-Model, Waterfall, Spiral, XP, OO, Capability Maturity Model (CMM).
