Olga Brunner, MS CMC’s Summary

Long standing career in hospital administration. Moved to California with brief involvement in the marine industry. Relocated to Florida to care for a parent who had Alzheimer's Disease which began my quest with caregiving issues both personally and helping neighbors in my community who had no children living nearby. Completed MS degree in gerontology pursuing nursing home administration and learned the value of cognitive stimulation for memory impaired seniors as an Activities Director at a nursing home. Founded A Good Daughter to continue my work in the community. Have worked with Dr. Robert Homer and Valerie Kolbert, ARNP who work with an elderly dementia population in Boca Raton. Looking forward to branching out and collaborating with Florida Chapter of Aging in Place towards researching other business pursuits.

Olga Brunner, MS CMC’s Specialties:

National designation as a care manager, certified. Member of the National and Florida Chapter of Professional Geriatric Care Managers. Maintain current through list-serve and annual conferences. As Dept of Elder Affairs approved training provider for AlF Core Training and Alzheimer's Related Disorders, produced curriculum to train professional caregivers on working with an Alzheimer's client. Produced hurricane disaster manual for Care Managers available online.
