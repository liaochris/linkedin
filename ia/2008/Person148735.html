Prabhakar Chaganti’s Summary

CTO of Ylastic, a start-up that is building the next generation web and mobile tools for the Amazon Cloud Computing environment. Author with Packt Press and IBM Developer Works. Won the community choice award for the most innovative virtual appliance in the 2006 VMWare Ultimate Global Virtual Appliance Challenge.

Prabhakar Chaganti’s Specialties:

Cloud computing, strategic thinking, agile development, technology incubation, open source evangelism and entrepreneurship.

Prabhakar Chaganti’s Experience

    Ylastic

    (Computer Software industry)

    May 2008 — Present (8 months)

    Ylastic is an Atlanta based start-up that is building the world's first unified user interface for managing all the component services of AWS - S3, EC2, SQS and SDB. You can manage your AWS cloud from a web browser, iPhone or an Android G1. Our goal is to make it easier and simpler than ever to architect and monitor solutions built on the AWS cloud computing environment for both individual and business users. We want you to spend your precious time focusing on bringing your ideas to life rather than dealing with the daily nitty gritty of the environment.
