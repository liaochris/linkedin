Georgios Chiotis’s Summary

Chiotis Georgios works as a Programmer and IT Instructor. He is taking part in the Informatics since 1989 and professionally since 1995 in parallel with his studies.

He has a B.Sc(Hons) in Computer Science from University of Portsmouth, UK (distinction) and a Greek National Diploma in Multimedia (scholarship).

He is a Professional Member of the British Computer Society (MBCS) and founding member of the BCS Hellenic Section. He participates in the BCS Wireless IT Specialist Group (WITSG), too.

He is a member of the International Game Developers Association (IGDA) and participates in the IGDA Mobile SIG and in the IGDA London Chapter.

He is a Member of the Professional Chamber of Piraeus.

He is a Mophun Certified Premium Developer and ECDL certified.

His business site address is http://www.gc-soft.gr. His business e-mail address is gchiotis@gc-soft.gr.

He is an active Game Developer, too. He is one of the founders of the Games2Gaze production company (http://www.games2gaze.com). In the past he was developing games for mobiles under the name: Ge.Mi-games on the road (http://games.gc-soft.gr), which is an integral part of the TCF games (http://www.tcfgames.com), now.

His development team named Poisonous06 targets computer graphics for games.

He supports the coder.gr community (http://www.coder.gr) as a Site Administrator and it was one of the main members of the MOPHUN development community (http://www.mophun.com/developer/forum.php).

Finally, he is blogging at: george the lonely game developer (http://gchiotis.blogspot.com), mhebjarion (http://mhebjarion.blogspot.com) and gchiotis About Informatics (http://blogs.pcw.gr/gchiotis) when it is possible.
