Bruce Ge’s Summary

Entrepreneur, CEO and founder of Jobirn.com.

Jobirn.com's mission is to get job seekers and employers/recruiters to match accurately, easy to contact through real time referral network.

Jobirn.com provides cost-efficient recruiting service and S-a-a-S ERP (software as a service employee referral program). Jobirn.com provides live video interview; instant messaging; manage internal referral programs.

Jobirn.com profits by charging service fee, not job listing fee, to recruiters and hiring companies, similar to salesforce.com.

Currently, Jobirn.com is seeking Series A funding to finish up the development and market the site to the public. Please Contact me (bruce.ge@jobirn.com) if you are a potential investor (VC).

Take advantage of JOBIRN (http://jobirn.com) to IMPROVE YOUR RECRUITING QUALITY AND EFFICIENCY!
