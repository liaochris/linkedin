Gary Pick’s Summary

After college, I took several sales positions at corporations selling communications and information technology solutions for companies, including ATI Communications of Beltsville, Maryland. At the age of 25, I went to work for Adelphia Communications Corporation (formerly known as Hyperion), a Fortune 500 company as a Sales Executive selling CLEC(Competitive Local Exchange Carrier) services including Data Communications, Carrier Level Network Solutions, Integrated Communications Solutions (Voice/Data) and other solutions. I was also acting marketing coordinator for the Baltimore/Washington Corridor.
After several years at Adelphia, and the company's decision to close my region following a company-wide reorganization, I went to work for USLEC of Charlotte, NC (now PAETEC). After a few months with USLEC, I reorganized and restarted American Digital Networks, a company I formed as a Communications Solutions Provider in 1996.
Some notable clients and contracts include Easton Utilities, University of Maryland, PCBANK, Maryland Institute College of Art, Baltimore County School System, Cienna, Ikon Office Solutions, Qwest Communications, Omni International Hotel, Radisson Hotels, Bulk Register, Manganaro Corporation, Helix Health (part of MedStar, Compass Group (Canteen) and Advanced Radiology(now part of MedStar Health).
Initially, I began working as a consultant for several clients . I established a network of sub-contractors and associates throughout North America. The company still maintains a limited staff of only on-call contractors, contract employees and temporaries project specific staff members.
In 2004, my team began work on several Internet projects including a Web Network initially, a kind-of enhanced online business network for searching for information and people, similar to LinkedIn.
The dba Company is Connectus and our newest solution called PRSafe, a Web 2.0 social media application for public relations, online visibility and media relations.
