Scott Dauner’s Summary

I've been working in usability, design, user experience, etc since 2000. I love helping make interfaces more meaningful to their intended audience. It's an amazing career that I feed lucky to have the opportunity to work in.

I consider myself a pragmatic idealist, meaning (to me) that I have a vision of user experience being perceived for what it is: an indespensible area of expertise that, if utilized properly, is the difference maker in the success or failure of any product, be it the web or a coffee mug. I'm very pragmatic in how I go about influencing that vision, just trying to keep my eyes and ears open for opportunities. Have I mentioned I really like what I do? :)
