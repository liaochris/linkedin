Mike Cobb’s Summary

Mike specializes in the sale and leasing of R&D, office, and industrial properties on the San Francisco Peninsula. Mike possesses a depth of market knowledge and experience, and his expertise has proven a great benefit to his clients. One client recently wrote, "I strongly believe that the transaction was successful due to (Mike's) diligent efforts. The level of attention I recieved was exemplary".

Mike’s clients have included prominent local technology firms, start-ups, and national manufacturing concerns. He has also successfully represented various non-profit organizations on highly challenging facility acquisition and disposition assignments.
