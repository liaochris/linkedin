Steven Fisher’s Summary

Most of my background is has been focused on successfully designing and launching products for Fortune 500 and emerging technology companies. I am currently CEO of the SlipStream Group, a global software company focused on providing reservation, scheduling and fleet operations solutions for the airline, air taxi and air charter markets,

Prior to SlipStream, I was founder of AppSolve, a product management and marketing consulting firm where I got an amazing opportunity to manage product lines with a value $600M in revenue. Most notably, I was responsible for external product marketing and field marketing for the Sybase ASE database product line. I also oversaw the product design and implementation of the CIO portal for the DOD which was the foundation for their current 125,000 user intranet. Prior to AppSolve, I was Director of Professional Services at OnSite Technologies where created one of the first professional services practice dedicated to Internet strategy and development which grew to a $5 million division in 24 months. I have also held key product engineering and manage¬ment positions at IKON, USConnect, Ryland and Wells Fargo.

I am a social media junkie and I blog at www.venturefiles.com and soon www.crazyirish.com. I also get to advise startups on funding and technology strategy. Thinking about writing a book one day but that will have to wait.

On a personal note, I am an avid pilot, musician and photographer. I currently reside in Columbia, MD, USA.
