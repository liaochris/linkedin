Hiroyuki Noguchi’s Summary

Before starting his management career as CEO/President at DAVE, Hiroyuki was a consultant specializing in international high technology business, assisting corporate executives and counsels on strategy matters for 18 years. He worked for major international consulting firms such as Coopers & Lybrand (PWC), KPMG, LECG (IPO in 1998), and Navigant Consulting Group.
Hiro has functioned as an advisor in corporate strategy, technology development, licensing, marketing, finance, and industry related matters with major consulting firms. Hiro also has worked as an interim CEO and a board member for Silicon Valley startup companies. His work focuses on formulation and implementation of business strategies involving research and development, commercialization of technologies, development of new products and product introduction to market, development of new market and various forms of technology transfer activities. These projects include licensing, joint ventures, mergers and acquisitions, business planning and valuations, intellectual properties and technology valuations as well as capital sourcing for Silicon Valley type startup companies and large well-established corporations. Hiro has also substantial experience in analyzing damages in intellectual property litigation, breach of contract and other commercial disputes both domestically in the US and internationally.
Prior to his consulting career, Hiro worked on engineering, and corporate management and strategy positions with major international corporations such as Intel, Fujitsu in US and Pioneer in Japan. Hiro has initiated, operated, and managed a custom integrated circuit design center in Chicago for a Japanese corporation to assist US corporations in the joint development of custom design semiconductors. His role required significant interaction with the corporate business planning, engineering, manufacturing, marketing and sales functions in both US subsidiaries and corporate headquarters in Japan.

Hiroyuki Noguchi’s Specialties:

His industry experience in consulting includes semiconductors, computer hardware and software, artificial intelligence robotics, e-commerce, and various IT products, video products, consumer electronics, telecommunication, data compression, transmission and storage technology, medical imaging equipment, factory automation and machining tools, and high precision parts.
