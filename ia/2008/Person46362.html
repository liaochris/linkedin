David de Kock’s Experience

  • Managing Director & founder

    TNmedia

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    December 2005 — Present (2 years 11 months)

    TNmedia creates Interactive Video for Triple Play channels. We combine Creativity and Technology to produce media that is great to interact with and, at the same time, is cost effective/profitable to produce.

    To accomplish this we have developed a Video Asset and Content Management system that enables producers to create highly interactive video items for Internet and mobile channels. In the next phase we will add support for digital TV and other Media Center products.

    The items we produce can contain a mix of Windows media, Flash or QuickTime formatted video. And there are no limitations to the where the video is hosted. As long as it’s available on the internet we can work with it.

    We have applied latest Web 2.0 features and the scalability of the TNmedia platform is assured by using innovative technologies like Amazon’s Elastic computing services. TNmedia is able to manage thousands of video/image assets, associated metadata and user-generated content.
