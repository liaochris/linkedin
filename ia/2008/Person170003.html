Satyajit Deb, PMP’s Summary

 An Electronics & Communication Engineer with 12+ years of experience in project execution, techno-commercial support for leading telecom multinational companies like Ericsson, Nortel, & Nokia.
 Demonstrated talent and expertise in Project Management of 3G (MSS, MGW, CDS, 2G SGSN, 3G SGSN, RNC & NodeB) network rollout & GSM (MSC, BSC, BTS, Transmission Systems (Microwave and DXX)) network rollout, Enterprise and Optical networks (SDH, DWDM).
 Project management of 3G & GSM sub-contractors and partners to facilitate the acquisition, design and construction of multiple GSM Base Stations & Core networks for the Mobile Networks within agreed timeframes, within budget, and in accordance with agreed quality standards.
 Adept in end-to-end project planning and execution of 3G & GSM cellular infrastructure projects, with skills in monitoring project progress, analyzing post-implementation maintenance issues to ensure optimum performance and productivity.
 Expertise in operations and maintenance of Wireless Communications-VHF Systems, Radio Paging Service, GSM900 Cellular Mobile Communication Systems, Ericsson Microwave transmission systems –Minilink.
 Qualified Diploma holder in Project Management (AQF-level 5) from Corporate Management Group (CPMG), Australian Institute of Management, Australia.
 Acquired the Registered PM (Reg. PM) title from AIPM, Australia.
 PMP certified in April 2007. In-depth knowledge/understanding of project management methodologies such as PMBOK.
