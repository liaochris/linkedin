Emem Ema’s Summary

Media and Entertainment Entrepreneur whose drive and desire to contribute to the growing global media industry beginning with the Nigerian Entertainment industry; creates a professional experience that cuts across all media and locations.

Providing wholesome and thought provoking entertainment channels, using unconventional platforms and promoting the Nigerian culture through same. Her experience includes work in music, print, TV and film.
