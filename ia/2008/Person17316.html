Ashok Majumdar’s Summary

Founder director of :
>Abhiyan Marketing Services (P) Ltd. (a marketing services co.) &
>Ebony & Ivory (an advertising agency accredited to Indian Newspaper Society).

After 6 years with The Times Of India Group, in various capacities—started with Editorial, moved to Corporate, then to Marketing Services and finally to Ad sales—I quit in 1994 to start Abhiyan, a Communications Design and Marketing Services firm with focussed initiatives in branding, direct marketing, direct mailing, interactive marketing, loyalty management programs, newsletters, design and development of POP displays and corporate awards.

Also partnered Master Ads in 1994, to set up the advertising agency's Delhi branch, which broke away amicably in 2002 to form Ebony & Ivory. The latter is today an agency with a capitalsed billing of Rs.100 million & accredited to INS.

My ventures have since then worked for some of the world's largest brands—IKEA, GE Money, Bausch & Lomb, Fluor Daniel, Valvoline Cummins, Korea Trade Centre & for some Indian brands as well—ICICI Bank, Wipro Spectramind, Maruti Countrywide, ITPO & HDFC Bank—and continue to be the brand custodians for some of the world's leading brands in India. Visit http://abhiyan.in/ to see more about us.

As An Employee of The Times of India Group (1988-94):
Highlights:
Interned at the Sports Desk, Editorial, TOI, Delhi-1987
