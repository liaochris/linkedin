Cameron Van Trump’s Summary

Recently graduated with a Bachelor of Business Administration Honors degree from the University of British Columbia in April 2008. I am now busy focusing on building up my investment firm, Trump Alpha Capital, which I formed during my last year of University. This venture will allow me to stay up to date with the financial markets, build capital, and provide me with a challenging job for the mean time.

It was a calculated decision, but I decided to start up my own firm rather than pursue a job right after graduation with an established investment firm. The decision rested on the fact that Trump Alpha would allow me to take maximum advantage of the most volatile and lucrative era in financial markets since the great depression.
As of November, Trump Alpha's returns in its first year of operation would even impress John Paulson.

I write my CFA Level I in June 2009, and then will be looking to gain employment with an investment firm come mid to late 2009 in order to further my investment education and potential. I hope to prove myself within the industry and continue to grow and build my financial prowess.

I hope to parlay my knowledge and sound financial experience into a challenging and rewarding career in the capital markets.
