Jerald Reichstein’s Summary

I like to joke that I go through a major career change every few years but never have to change jobs. The kind of work I find interesting puts me in a position to follow new technology and find places where it intersects with graphics, design, entrepreneurial opportunities, and creativity. This is what my company, Archimage, is built upon and we're constantly surrounding ourselves with smart people who share our interest.

My formal training is in architecture. I'm a product of a superior program at the University of Houston College of Architecture. The daily pressure to be creative, innovative, and responsible while studied there made me start thinking in a new way. The result is that my career evolution has carried me through a variety of fields, including architectural planning and design, computer animation, multimedia development, 3D stereography, web and intranet projects, and computer games. Making computer games for health is probably the last thing you might find an architect doing, but to me it feels like a natural progression. In addition to bringing my clients' projects to life, I enjoy spending my free time being an Internet entrepreneur.
