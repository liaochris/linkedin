Mukesh Singh’s Summary

Currently I (mukeshsingh30@gmail.com) am working with Facetime Communications as Technical Architect for their IM & P2P product line.

Previously I was Technical Team Lead at Sipera Systems which develops first in class VOIP Security Appliances which detect & prevent VOIP attacks in real time. Sipera Systems offers carrier class and most comprehensive security solution to protect VOIP network.

I started my career with Texas Instruments where I was involved in Developing world's first PacketCable1.0 compliant voice enabled cable modem (called Multimedia Terminal Adapter). There I worked mostly in PacketCable Security involving Kerberos, PKI, PKCS,TLS & IPSEC technologies and Packetcable provisioning involving SNMPv3, TFTP,DHCP,DNS,DNS-Srv etc.

After that I worked for Juniper Networks for a brief period developing Security features for their Intrusion Detection and Prevention product line.

I have got couple of patents for Texas Instruments in the areas of Network Security and Cryptography. The following patent work had been presented to IEEE conference and is available in IEEE library.
1. Public key cryptography with matrices (IEEE ISBN: 0-7803-8572-1) & (Patent Serial No. 10/772,667 & Publication number : 20040174995)
2. Matrix based asymmetric bulk encryption algorithm ( IEEE ISBN: 0-7803-8572-1) & (Patent Serial No. 10/836,935 Publication number : 20040258240)

I have got the following patent for Sipera Systems for innovative Source Limiting algorithm to detect and prevent high volume (D)DOS attacks.

3. System and Method for Providing Network Level and Nodal Level Vulnerability Protection in VoIP Networks. Patent Serial. No. 11/502,244
