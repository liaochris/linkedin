Mark Corby’s Summary

A multi-skilled, 'hands-on', senior global customs & trade compliance professional;

I have wide experience of working in UK Customs, in the freight industry (from small independent companies through to huge MNEs) and in the high-tech, manufacturing, defence & aerospace industries.

I have worked in senior management most of my career including as a consultant and director.

I have extensive practical & technical knowledge of customs legislation, US & EU export control legislation (ITAR, EAR, ECA etc.)

I have extensive experience in international trade, and supply chain, including customs compliance contracts with suppliers, customers & service providers.

I have excellent risk analysis & management, problem-solving and soft skills

I focus on balancing full compliance & security with trade facilitation.

I create & deliver simple, comprehensive compliance processes and add value value by delivering savings, recovering funds and mitigating (hidden) risk in supply & value chains, customs areas - classification, valuation, origin, trade agreements and export control.

I focus on gaining opportunity, facilitation & 'edge' for the business by approaching the compliance burden positively within the legislative bounds.

I drive new synergies by involving all business functions in the area.

My specialities include:

A holistic approach to global customs compliance, US, UK & EU export control, business process design, global supply chain security (AEO), education & training, presenting to all levels & types of business and government.

Mark Corby’s Specialties:

Freight service provision, customs processes and regimes, US, EU and UK export controls, contracts, customs issues in life cycle management
