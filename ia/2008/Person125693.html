Matt Paulin’s Summary

I am interested in helping companies develop and deploy new software products. Think of me as a general contractor for system and software design. The perfect client for me would be a company who wants a product built, and doesn't want to deal with the drama of developing software, integrating technologies, or managing IT personnel.

Our process goes a little like this. First, my team and I would work with you to define your product vision. Then we would walk you through the different processes and trade offs of the most recent technology. Once the vision is defined, and the technologies selected, we would handle the construction and deployment of the software. Finally we would train your permanent employees to support the system and then our work is done.

You bring the idea, we bring the labor, you own the product.
