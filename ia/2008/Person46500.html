Davide Potente’s Summary

I have an High School specialisation in Computer Science. In March of 2006 I took my degree in International Communication at University for Foreigners of Perugia, Italy, discussing a thesis about the information classification model for a web-software interface, focusing on the information scent and the berrypicking process.

During my studies I started to get interested in Information Architecture: how users operate with different types of interface and classification models, so I decided the topic of my thesis, from which an article was published on <http://trovabile.org/articoli/itunes-profumo-informazione> (Italian).

I attended a master in Web Communication and Design at European Institute of Design where I specialised myself in User Experience Design.
I am an Information Architect consultant and I would like to focus on Bridge Experiences and their relationships with wayfinding strategies. My article related to these topics is "Architettura dell’informazione integrata secondo Apple: Fra web e t-shirt colorate" <http://trovabile.org/articoli/architettura-informazione-integrata-apple> (Italian).
