Steven Woodward’s Summary

Biography
Steven Woodward is the founder of Executive Order DAS – Defense and Aerospace Systems and is a member of the technical elite Counsels of Advisors New York. He holds two advance degrees in Computer Engineering and Computer Science. Steven holds two US Patens for advance research in RF communications and software engineering. He has over thirty years of professional experience with the research and development of software applications in the Information Technology field. Steven is an acknowledged authority on software methodology, software development life cycle SDLC, software architecture and has performed the following critical functions:

* Research / Mentor / Advisor
* Programmer / Developer
* Director of Development
* Enterprise Architect / IT
* Application Development
* Methodology / Architecture
* Wireless / WAP / Mobile
* XP - Extreme Programming
* UML / GoF - Design Patterns
