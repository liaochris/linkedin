Gina Schreck- Professional Speaker’s Summary

I have been working in the training and speaking industry since 1995 with my corporate learning company, Pinnacle Achievement, and now I am taking my experience and helping to connect experts to organizations through live events and varied digital media, such as podcasts, videos, and Second Life (3D virtual world) learining events. We help meeting planners and event planners bring experts to their events and add value through pre and post learning bites. Look at our outstanding and innovative professional Synapse speakers and trainers at www.Synapse3Di.com. You can find the Second Life Synapse 3Di campus "In World" at MSIT. I welcome connections in real life: Gina@Synapse3Di.com and in Second Life: Jianna Zerbino.

I am a professional speaker and trainer (CSP is the certified speaking professional designation awarded through the National Speaker's Association) and I help audiences around the globe to "Make Business Sense of Today's New Technology." I am passionate about delivering information through E-learning or M-learning (mobile learning) and want to explore opportunites in virtual worlds to bring virtual teams together.

If you need Presentation Skills training, I have been working with executives and management teams for over 10 years to make even the most technical (or dry) presentation, SIZZLE! I am the author of 7 books, including: Quit Putting People to Sleep-Presentation Skills for Today’s Sleepy Work Environment (presentation skills); Perfectly Imbalanced (Stress); Tony-the-Toenail, Hot Air Balloons and Other Stories to Lift Your Spirits (Inspiration); 10 Steps to Become a Pessimist (humor); and Marriage Mechanics-A Tune Up for the Highway of LOVE! (relationships)
