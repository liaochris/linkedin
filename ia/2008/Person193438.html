Levent Tüter’s Summary

I am Master's student studying Nanotechnology in one of the most respected universities on the subject in Europe. I want to start a career in a company that has the passion of defining the state of the art, where I will see my contribution for achieving this challenge gets exponentially bigger day by day. I would love to see the same for my responsibilities as well for I believe I have the skills and the ambition for always greater challenges.

I have been gaining a considerable amount of clean room experience (Class 10 & 1000m2) as a part of my master's thesis at Chalmers University of Technology within the Quantum Device Physics Group.

In addition to academic work, I have also had the chance to gain an important professional work experience at BOSCH Sensortec GmbH. During my term of employment, which lasted 6 months, as an application researcher, I contributed the company with delivering new ideas and system models for their MEMS products.

More to my social skills, an important asset I possess is my communication skills. This, I believe, has a critical importance for delivering my ideas. What is more, I fully trust in my leadership skills, which I used to a full extend when I headed university's biggest student organization COMPEC. In my command, I and my team led COMPEC to be even a part of CEBIT Euroasia, one of the biggest IT exhibitions. I have the knowledge required as well as the creative and abstract thinking skills to bring forth the best solution for the challenges I will be taking without hesitation.

I would like to stress that I am a young, highly motivated, creative team player, with great communication skills and a long experience of living abroad. I always look for new things to learn and am always open to new challenges. No matter how hard the challenge is or how scarce the time is I know how much difference it makes to stay calm.
