Felipe Memoria’s Summary

I'm an Interaction Designer with over 10 years experience in internet projects. I was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and am currently living in Brooklyn, NY, working at HUGE. Prior to HUGE, I have worked for 4 years at Globo.com, the internet division of the largest media company in Latin America, Rede Globo.

Throughout these years, I tried to blend my academic vein with practical market applications. Together with the work as Creative Team Leader at Globo.com Sports Center, I got my masters degree in Design with emphasis in Human-Computer Interaction, and was professor at two post-graduate courses in PUC-Rio, a renowned university in my home city. I am also author of the book Design para a Internet: Projetando a Experiência Perfeita (Design for the Internet: Designing the Perfect Experience) by Campus/Elsevier, 2005.

I have presented articles in Brazil, USA, and England, and am often invited for lectures and events in different cities in Brazil. I am also a consultant for the Brazilian government in accessibility projects, and was invited to write the fourth-cover in Jakob Nielsen’s latest book published in Brazil - Prioritizing Web Usability.

Felipe Memoria’s Specialties:

Usability, user experience, accessibility, strategic thinking.
