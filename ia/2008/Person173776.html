Kashif Ali’s Summary

I'm a Graphic Designer working as an Art Director for a Pakistan based Consulting company AbacusConsulting, As well as designing beautiful things - I like to further my knowlege of the web - and am proficient in CSS, (d)HTML and Flash amongst other things.

At AbacusConsulting I am a Lead Designer and I am growing everyday, not just as a designer but also as a person. Other important things in my life are music and all kinds of art forms; for example my 8 years of playing with colors.

My creative background comes from several hours of work at home. I have mostly worked on my own personal things or in internal design agencies and I haven’t studied one single minute to get where I’m at today. I’m a lucky man.

My typical day at work is ...Coffee, some ironic chat in the designer room, buggin' my workmates, e-mail check, answering e-mails, getting iTunes going, start working with whatever is priority one, eating lunch, coffee again, start working again, getting in contact with the other designer, more work, writing some e-mails, sending comps of what has been done. That’s a typical day for me at the office.
