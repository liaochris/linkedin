Jin Kim’s Summary

Goal: To be part of an organization that values business excellence in the areas of simple, elegant and functional service & product design, leading-edge & easy-to-use technology.

1. Industry analyst from core LCD components, complete sets, product, geographic and global trends. From analyzing LCD components such as LEDs (Cree, Lumileds, Nichia, Toyoda Gosei, Seoul Semiconductor, OSRAM, etc.), LCD glass substrates (Corning, AGC, NEG, NHT, SCHOTT), LCD panels (Samsung, LG.Philips LCD, Sharp, AU Optronics, Chi Mei Optoelectronics, etc.) to completed LCD monitors (Dell, HP, NEC, Samsung), notebook PCs (Dell, HP, Apple, Lenovo, Samsung, LG), and LCD TV sets (Sony, Samsung, LG, Sharp, Panasonic, Philips) and their respective markets, I am deeply exposed to the specific and general dynamics of the LCD industry. Knowledgeable in LCD production, ASPs, LCD/PDP manufacturing costs, retail, end-user and market dynamics.

2. Product management experience from concept inception, engineering sample, volume production, product marketing, product support, and EOL management.
My roles at Jazz Multimeda were Assistant to the President, Webmaster, and Product Manager. Jazz Multimedia was a small and dynamic startup company that competed against industry heavyweights such as Diamond Multimedia and Creative Labs. Team-based product management of 3D graphics accelerators that made use of Rendition and 3Dfx chips from concept inception to end-of-life. Increased website pageviews more than 100x within three months. Assisted president in acquiring One-O, which led to the eventual creation of My Simon that sold for $700 million to c|net.

Jin Kim’s Specialties:

Product management, business development, logistics coordination, consulting, analyzing market dynamics, brand development, international marketing (esp. in the US and Korea).
