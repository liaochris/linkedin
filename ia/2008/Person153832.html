Prof Bhushan Lal Handoo’s Summary

Have been in Education for over 40 years. Taught English Language & Literature in a Degree College for 12 years. Headed Prestigious Public, Residential & International Schools. Have been Founder Principal of a Teacher Education College for 11 years and Consultant & Director to several Schools.
Have Conducted hundreds of Workshops for Teacher Training across the length & breadth of India.
Have been a Management Advisor & on Board of Directors of several Educational Institutions.
Had a very short stint as Vice-Chancellor of Aquatech University in Uttaranchal.
