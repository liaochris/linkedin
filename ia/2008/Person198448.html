Walter Kim’s Summary

Independent contractor available for Web and iPhone app development, handling everything from design to deployment. I'm a generalist that can do programming, visual design, interaction design, information architecture, usability analysis, testing, and strategy. On the Web development side, I develop primarily in Django and WordPress, but can work with a variety of frameworks and technologies.

I possess 5 cumulative years of programming experience, covering Lisp, Java, Python, Objective-C, PHP, JavaScript, and other languages. My core competency, however, is interface and interaction design: I'm exceptionally skilled at conceiving of the simplest, most elegant, most efficient, and easiest to use interfaces for products with complex feature sets.
