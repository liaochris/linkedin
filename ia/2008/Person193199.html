T.S. Shawn Lin’s Summary

Invitations to Shawn can be sent to "contact@shawnlin.com".

Shawn leverages his understanding and experience with Information Technology to meet the needs of operations that extend across the East and West.

Born in Taiwan, but raised in Taiwan, United States, and Canada, he is able to gain a clear understanding of Chinese and Western cultural issues.

After completing an Honors Bachelors of Science at the University of Toronto in Computer Science and Economics, Shawn returned to Taiwan and was first employed by an US based software company then a plastics manufacturing operation.

He then gained admission to the Masters program at National Tsing Hua University in Industrial Engineering and Engineering Management program via the competitive annual entrance examination, instead of placements reserved for Overseas Chinese and International Students.

At the same time, he received an offer from FSI International for the position of "Senior IT Support Coordinator" in Asia. Thus, Shawn work at FSI International and enrolled at National Tsing Hua University on a full time basis.

Currently, Shawn has graduated and is still working at FSI International.
