Caue Figueiredo’s Summary

I am the person in charge for project management at Link Translation (www.linktranslation.com). We are an offshore business process outsourcing specialized on Localization and Translation Services, fully experienced adapting and developing software products, technical texts and advertising for global markets, together with the localization of websites, software and help material. We are inexpensive and focus on high quality work delivery via frequent communication. Our highly qualified translators provide professional solutions with the most advanced tools in the industry.

Caue Figueiredo’s Specialties:

Our language department supports over 50 languages including English, German, French, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, Russian, Portuguese, Arabic and others.

We have handled several long-term translation and localization projects for Visa, Yahoo!, Nokia, Philips, Husky, Activision, Avery Dennison, Intel, Citibank, Ubisot, AMD, ATI, Symantec and many others.
