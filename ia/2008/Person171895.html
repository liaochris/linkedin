Sean Kauppinen’s Summary

Kauppinen has represented videogames and gaming hardware since 1995, working on more than 400 titles in his career. Kauppinen is responsible for consulting, business development, branding, and for providing strategic counsel to TriplePoint's clients, as well as day-to-day operations within the agency. Previously he served as Head of Product PR for Sony Online Entertainment, and held PR and marketing management positions at Ubisoft, bleem!, 3dfx, and Golin/Harris. His career began with Morgen-Walke Associates where he worked on IPOs and provided investor relations counsel to clients including Galoob Toys, and Activision. Kauppinen holds a BA in Cinema Writing and Directing from San Francisco State University.

A frequent speaker on public relations for interactive entertainment, branding, and international PR and communities, Kauppinen has spoken at events including Games Convention Developers Conference (2005, 2006), PR for Games Conference (2004, 2005, 2006), Games Marketing Conference (2006), Making Games Conference (2006), Austin Games Conference (2006), GSTAR (2006), Game Developers Conference (2007), International eSports Conference (2007), and GC Asia Conference (2007).
