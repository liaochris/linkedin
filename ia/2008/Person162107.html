Robert Gibb’s Summary

Corporate communications director with a consistent track record of enhancing company profiles and coverage, primarily for multinational and global companies. Devised and implemented highly effective strategies and methodologies, underpinned by efficient campaign management, hands on implementation and team building. Record of achievement across the three complementary disciplines of investor relations, external media relations and internal communications, with a spectrum of audiences, including the media, investors, analysts, customers and staff.

Key achievements:

+ Devised and drove global corporate communications strategies. Increased visibility in local markets through coaching and mentoring in-country marketing teams in EMEA, The Americas and Asia Pacific as well as hand-on implementation and reporting.

+ Quadrupled year-on-year press coverage following proposal and establishment of internal PR function and selection of key personnel.

Robert Gibb’s Specialties:

Good under pressure, wide experience from many industries, strong team player and people management experience, good lateral thinking and understanding of technology (Mensa memeber), excellent on analysis/ strategy, P&L/ budget responsibility, as well as Board-level/ CxO-level accountability.
