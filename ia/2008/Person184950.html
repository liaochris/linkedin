Tad Miller’s Summary

In Tad’s role as Account Director he works directly with Search Mojo's clients to manage and execute their search engine optimization, pay per click advertising and online marketing strategies. Tad has a broad spectrum of experience in working with Fortune 500 Insurance companies, Pharmaceutical companies, OEM automotive manufacturers and small start up online businesses to significantly improve their natural search engine rankings using Search Mojo’s proven Search Engine Optimization process.
