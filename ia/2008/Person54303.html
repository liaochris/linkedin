Dr. Dean McKay’s Summary

Think mountain streams and Alpine lakes, tall pines and clear blue skies. See a slowly circling hawk or a majestic eagle soar by. Inhale the crisp air of a cross-country ski trail. Watch two eager Labs trot up the trail in front of you. Sit on a wide deck in the middle of the forest and enjoy nature. You have just entered my world.

As rocket scientist with NASA’s Saturn/Apollo man-on-the-moon program in the 60’s to e-commerce entrepreneur today, my goal is to create 10X value for organizations. Recently I developed and our team is growing a portfolio of e-commerce business. The latest venture www.WineCentivz.com delivers your message “tastefully”. These online channels provide efficient direct-to-the-user marketing of niche products.

Adams Media recently release a new book I co-authored with P. T. Shank, “Business Words You Should Know: 1,000 Essential Words and Phrases for Any Job” a business addition to the Adams vocabulary and literacy series and now available on Amazon.com for advanced purchase.

Dr. Dean McKay’s Specialties:

I enjoy developing unique strategies I call "10X" because they have the opportunity to change the way things are done by at an order of magnitude. Today my executive activities and investment focus provide strategic advice and include corporate governance and strategy formulation.

Interactive guidance to acquire the necessary business knowledge, in bit-size pieces, to reach personal freedom.
