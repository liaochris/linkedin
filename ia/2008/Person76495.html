Henry DiCarlo’s Summary

A Security and Safety Management Professional with over 35 years of diverse experience in all facets of organizational security, safety, claims administration, investigations, loss prevention, training, and risk management. An accomplished teacher and trainer.

Provides Loss Prevention and Security consulting and training services as DiCarlo Consulting. www.dicarloconsulting.com .

Adjunct Criminal Justice and Management instructor with Newbury College.

Henry DiCarlo’s Specialties:

Significant background in security and safety programs within diverse Retail Operations, Consumer Products Manufacturing, International Sourcing, Distribution Centers, Supply Chain and Logistics . Additional experience with Day Care Centers, Public Libraries and Assisted Living Facilities.
