Joe Gan’s Summary

Joe has four years experience in a product engineering environment and successfully saw three cellular phone launched for the Motorola V1050, V365 and Rokr E8. He took on the opportunity to work onsite in Libertyville, Illinois for a 12-month period; which further strengthened and broadened his knowledge on the program cycle for consumer products. With the experiences in global product roll-out, he has worked with the R&TTE, FCC, PTCRB and GCF agencies in getting product samples tested and certified. His exposures in the in-house test labs prior to certification have equipped him with the ability to understand the required test cases for GSM and Bluetooth testing.

Joe Gan’s Specialties:

Product Safety & Compliance; Type Approvals and Regulatory Certification for mobile phone; GSM hardware design and 3GPP test requirement; Bluetooth hardware design and test requirement (v1.2 + EDR).
