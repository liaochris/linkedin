Kevin "mr.soul" Harp’s Summary

I was born with a box of Crayons so it has always been my destiny to provide my creative services to the world!
I am a senior level designer with over 10 years of professional freelance experience. I have primarily worked within the entertainment industry, but also have provided creative solutions to advertising, marketing and apparel companies.
I have the ability to facilitate a project from concept development to final printed piece.

Some of my clients have included the following:

-- Disturbing Tha Peace (Ludacris' label)
-- Ebony Son Entertainment (Ludacris' management company)
-- Patchwerk Recording Studios (the premier recording studio in the southest)
-- Sho'Nuff Records (Jazze Pha's label and home to R&B sensation Ciara)
