Kathleen Davis’s Summary

Kathleen brings with her a long and successful track record of designing and implementing Internet Applications/sites, client server systems and legacy systems, including business to business procurement exchanges for various companies in the financial services, telecommunications, logistics and service and repair industries. Kathleen communicates effectively with a wide spectrum of individuals from data entry clerks to corporate executives in both relaxed and extremely tense situations.
