Aashima Goel’s Summary

As a User Interface designer, I have seven-year experience with web design, UI, navigation, prototyping for web based applications. I have lead projects from design to the implementation phases and have directed teams to produce concepts for targeted audiences. I have mentored design teams to explore design options, create and apply design frameworks that consider the entire client site and user experience. I have excellent visual and conceptual problem-solving skills with solid understanding of web design principles, page hierarchy, visual and interaction design, color and page layout. Along with design talent, I possess excellent planning, organizational and project management skills. Additionally, I have hands on experience in developing corporate identity, packaging, print collateral, advertising, annual reports. I am both PC and Mac savvy having strong design and typographical skills. I have very effective interpersonal skills and able to work independently as well as in a group.

Aashima Goel’s Specialties:

My skills include technical aspects of User Interface & Interaction design for Web based applications, Corporate Web sites, Intranets using Adobe Photoshop, Macromedia Fireworks, Adobe Illustrator, Macromedia Dreamweaver 8, HomeSite, HTML, XHTML, CSS, Java Script, Macromedia Flash.

I am passionate about design—in and out of the "box"—and about creating high quality, professional web interface design. I bring enthusiasm, vision and skill to a collaborative development process.
