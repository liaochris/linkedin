Maurice Parisien’s Summary

A senior marketing executive with 30+ years of sales and marketing experience that has successfully served employers and clients across multiple industries including: packaged goods, health care, financial services, technology and telecommunications. Career success is attributed to focusing on the needs of both the customer and the enterprise delivering improvements in both top-line and bottom-line performance of companies served.
