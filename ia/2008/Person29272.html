Sergio Burani’s Summary

Born and raised in Northern Italy, Sergio first came to America at age 17 as an AFS Exchange Student. This experience was pivotal to his future personal and career choices. After undergraduate studies at the University of Turin and an MBA from C.W. Post College, he undertook a 32-year career in International Marketing Management with several major multinational corporations (Procter & Gamble, Carnation, Warner-Lambert, and lastly Pfizer). During this period, he lived in Italy and Germany, before settling in New Jersey. Sergio’s corporate career comprised extensive travel to almost 50 different countries across the world. His keen interests in the peoples of the world and his respect for cultural diversity profoundly impacted his management style and philosophy.

Now retired from corporate life, Sergio has decided to transform his interest in photography and travel into a second career. He scouts the world as a Travel Photographer, to capture and record the lives of the different people who populate our planet. He is specialized in photographing wine travel destinations.

Sergio has an Associate Degree in Photography Technology from the County College of Morris.

You can see examples of Sergio's extensive photography work at www.burius.com and at www.photosbysergio.com. His prints are available at www.printroom.com/pro/photosbysergio.
