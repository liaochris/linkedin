Brian Voge’s Summary

I have been providing training and quality assurance services to corporations and governmental agencies for over twenty years. Companies have repeatedly requested my services when they need help solving their training or performance problems. I've managed large and small programs and projects and would be a good choice to help you identify and solve the roadblocks to achieving your goals. I am adaptable, innovative, enthusiastic, educated, experienced and very computer literate.

Brian Voge’s Specialties:

Web and Computer Based Training (WBT/CBT) design & development (SCORM and ADA section 508 compliant); Instructor Led Training (ILT) and Blended learning solutions involving training analysis, design, development, implementation and evaluation (ADDIE Model); Computer literate, Project Management trained, trained and experienced Incident Investigator and Root Cause Analyst. Previously certified American Society for Quality (ASQ) Lead Auditor and ISO-9000 Lead Assessor.
