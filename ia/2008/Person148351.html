Paul Wlodarczyk’s Summary

Strategic marketing professional, expert at commercializing emerging technology and incubating IT services. Focused on new market entry and developing markets for technology that hasn't "crossed the chasm." Expert in R&D and business valuation. Consultant to Fortune 500 clients for business process innovation and technology adoption. Technology focus on all aspects of unstructured content.

Paul Wlodarczyk’s Specialties:

Start-up veteran. Strategic marketing. Market entry. Commercialization. Product management. R&D portfolio management. Services/pre-sales management. Lean. Six Sigma. IT services (selection, application development, implementation and deployment). International (Japan, UK, Canada, France, Switzerland). All things unstructured: XML, content management, authoring, publishing, localization, knowledge management, mining, analytics. Pharma, bio-tech, aerospace, hi-tech, discrete, insurance, retail.
