Monica Valentinelli’s Summary

My writing and editing professionally comes from a blend of my creative side and my business side. An early reader, I won my first writing contest when I was 9 and always showed a high aptitude for literary criticism and writing. Since those early days, I went on to design my own course curriculum through the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and I've continue to write and edit when time allows, to fulfill my passion for this art form. This year will mark the publication of two novellas and an online novel.

In addition to my creative efforts, I have experience in several aspect of writing including: ghostwriting, technical and business writing, writing copy, etc.

My game design experiences originate from working extensively with publishers in the gaming industry; I have published over a dozen games with more on the way.

In addition to blogging, I am often invited to speak at conventions to new and upcoming writers who also share a passion for this fascinating hobby.

I am currently looking for freelance writing opportunities.

Prior to working with Netconcepts, I worked in a range of sectors including financial, environmental, entertainment and government. These positions were structured around bookkeeping, statistical analysis and training on computer software. My new venture into web analytics reflects not only a shift in focus for my career, but my ability to translate skills and knowledge across different industries.

In my recently-held position, I was extremely rewarded by the amount of flexibility and growth that this opportunity had to offer me. One of the best things about working at my current employer, Netconcepts, was the supportive team environment and the talent that this firm had attracted.
