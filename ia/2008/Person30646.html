Carl Guse (cguse@astcorporation.com)’s Summary

Currently I am working for Applications Software Technology (AST) Corporation. We are a national certified Advantage Partner with Oracle for their eBusiness ERP applications based out of Naperville, IL. We are always looking for experienced Implementation Project Managers, Functional Consultants, Technical Consultants and DBAs who have Oracle Apps implementation experience. Areas we currently support for Oracle are Financials, CRM, HRMS and Mfg/SC. Presently we hire all of our consultants on a full-time salaried basis and we provide all of our staff with competitive salaries, bonuses and a comprehensive benefits package.

Positions prior to AST included Director of Staffing Services for DTI (Chicago) and also USI (Detroit) - both regional IT Services and Staffing firms. I have also held increasingly responsible positions in recruitment and branch/operations management roles at Inforizons, Sykes Enterprises, Johnson Service Group, Volt Technical Services and other national and international IT and Technical Staffing organizations.

My experience in the IT and Technical staffing industry span better than 20 years and my achievements and recognition have been numerous. I have supervised and guided the expansion of new and existing markets within the contract staffing industry with complete P&L responsibility.

My professional associations have included the National Association of Computer Consultants (NACCB), National Technical Services Association (NTSA), Data Management Association (DAMA), and the Society for Human Resource Management Association (SHRMA). As VP of Membership for the new Michigan Chapter of DAMA International I help grow the organization to over 100 members before moving to Chicago.
