Cinnamon Pollard’s Summary

I have been in the web development, strategy and online product management arena for over twelve years. I am experienced in information organisation and user centric design of Internet and Intranet sites for government departments and private companies including Fairfax Digital, ABC, Telstra and Department of Housing. I co-developed, designed and managed InfoBank Online – the world’s first web-based personal organiser. In 2001, I began freelancing and provided analysis, architecture and project management services to a variety of companies on a contract basis. I oversaw the complete redevelopment of the NSW Department of Housing’s website.

My career objective is to work on Internet projects with dynamic organisations that aim to fulfil their business objectives via websites built around a user centric design approach.

Cinnamon Pollard’s Specialties:

Strategy development, business planning, information organisation and user centric design of wesites, project management, online marketing.
