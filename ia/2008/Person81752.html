Jack Hawk’s Summary

John J. (Jack) Hawk, Sr., LUTCF has 20 years of experience in direct sales in the insurance and financial services industry, having held various field and home office positions including producer, agency owner, Director of Recruitment, Selection, Retention and Career Education for Mutual of Omaha, and Regional Sales Officer for Pre-Paid Legal Services, Inc. Jack is currently the Director of Individual Product Sales at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Nebraska.

Jack is a long-time member of GAMA, NAIFA and NAHU, a volunteer instructor for the American College and a past presenter at LIMRA’s Recruiting, Selection, and Retention Summit.

Jack has authored several articles on the topics of credit, banking and financial services. In 1993 he authored a how-to guide for business owners on establishing credit and using it wisely. Over the past decade this document has been, one of the most downloaded “how-to” guides on the internet.

Jack is involved in various community organizations and nonprofit groups in his home town of Omaha, Nebraska, including leadership positions with:

The Omaha Street School Board of Directors, elected to the board in 2002 – he is currently a member of the Champions Committee and served as board president from 2003 to 2007.

Omaha Association of Health Underwriters – elected to the board in 2004 and served two terms as a director.

Omaha Christian Academy’s Board of Directors from 2004 to 2008 and past Board Vice-President.

Living Faith Church board from 2003 to 2007.

Nelson’s Creek Neighborhood Association Board of Directors, elected to the board in 2002 and current board president.

Jack married his high school sweetheart; Camille (Brown) Hawk, a real estate, banking and lending attorney and partner at the Omaha Law Firm of Fitzgerald, Schorr, Barmettler and Brennan. Camille is also involved in the Omaha Community. Together they enjoy managing their rental property portfolio and spending time with their 4 children.
