Kevin Jefferson’s Summary

I am a graduate of the U.S. Military Academy at West Point. I served in the U.S. Army as a commissioned Field Artillery officer. My duty stations included Fort Sill, Oklahoma and Fort Benning, Georgia. I served in a rapid deployment unit which ultimately took me to overseas service in Kuwait.

Upon entering the civilian sector, I was selected for the fast-track management training program with Atlanta based Georgia-Pacific Corporation. I worked with the company in Memphis, TN and Huntsville, AL.

I accepted a position with Merrill Lynch in 2002 as a financial advisor, which began my financial services career.

I was recruited to Edward Jones in 2003 and built an office in a previously underserved market in Huntsville, AL. I left Edward Jones to begin Jefferson Capital Partners, LLC, which was the ultimate goal for my financial services career.

I am currently expanding my workforce, client base, and product offerings to accomplish my 12 year strategic plan, which includes diversification into multiple insurance and investment-related divisions and ultimately an international presence.
