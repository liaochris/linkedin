Scott Lee’s Summary

With strong product knowledge, experience and dedication, I am able to produce high impact music, top-notch audio effects for the aural and visual medium in filmmaking, television, video games, and elite media productions. Utilizing skill and technical knowledge to create professional auditory designs fast and efficiently with a fresh imaginative approach, an energetic participate of the team, becoming an added-value to the establishment.

Scott Lee’s Specialties:

Twenty eight years of experience composing, creating, engineering music and sound. My employment has included creating sound effects, scoring soundtracks, foley sessions, field recordings, voice overs / editing dialogue, dialogue coaching, flawless audio loops, digital editing, mastering, re-mastering, audio restorations, syncing audio to video, mixing post productions, 7.1 surround sound mixing, studio setups, microphone positioning, and interactive media.
