Gied Hermsen’s Summary

I focus on helping high-growth technology companies make key appointments within the areas of senior sales and management. Please don't pigeon-hole me as another recruiter, as I am passionate about the service I offer, and I can lay claim to a track record of consistent delivery and results, with projects in North-America, United Kingdom, France, Italy, Spain, Germany, The Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark, Poland, Russia, Belgium, Senegal, Dubai, Australia, Brazil, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Shanghai and Hong Kong.

The market is booming right now and if your business isn't growing then you're losing revenue! With that in mind, I welcome the opportunity to have an exploratory conversation with anyone looking to take their organisation to the next level. I have excellent knowledge of both the Pan-European and North-American market space and for my part I might be able to offer advice around salaries, industry trends, general market intelligence, and perhaps most importantly how to position your business to make it as attractive as possible to the best talent. I can also help with interview training, psychometric and personality profiling and management assessments.
