Richard de Nys’s Summary

Since 1998 I have produced websites and web applications for major international clients, in a team leader capacity. My experience stems from a creative background — trained in Industrial Design my career has progressed through various roles incorporating a fair application of technical, analytical and creative thinking. A self-starter with entrepreneurial skills, my passion is in making things happen, building great results.

A national of Australia and The Netherlands, over the past 14 years I have lived and worked professionally in Hong Kong, Düsseldorf, London, Amsterdam and Barcelona — I am now based in Sydney.

Richard de Nys’s Specialties:

Management of online projects — preparation of proposals, estimates, specifications and plans; budget and resource management; client liaison; team leadership; problem solving; entrepreneurial/business skills; product development

Architecture and design — information architecture; system architecture; user interface design

Hands-on web skills — user-centred design; client-side production (XHTML, CSS, Javascript, AJAX); server-side development (Coldfusion, PHP, ASP); database design
