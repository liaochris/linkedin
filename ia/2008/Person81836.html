Jack Macy’s Summary

Well-rounded graphic designer and creative professional with extensive experience in corporate marketing and communications for the high-tech, education, and non-profit sectors. Exceptional balance of creativity, leadership, and business focus. Strong conceptual development that is founded upon critical thinking and thorough synthesis of client needs. Strives for all work to be compelling, cogent and clever. Strong aversion to ordinary and trite.

Quickly takes ownership for and develops expert command of dynamic corporate brands. Brand experience includes Sun Microsystems, Cisco, AMD, Panasonic, Symantec, VMWare, Convergys, FrontRange Solutions, American Red Cross, Sesame Workshop/Sesame Street, and PBS/NPR.

Experience designing for print, online, and multimedia, and supervising creative teams in these areas. Creative direction style focuses on listening to diverse ideas, setting high standards, developing a solid concept, supporting the message, compelling the viewer, and mentoring other creatives.

Broad professional background has helped to develop effective professional skill set -- communication, organization, problem solving, critical thinking, leadership, staff management, program management, etc.

Jack Macy’s Specialties:

Graphic design for print, online, and multimedia. Creative direction for design and copy writing. Concept development for marketing communications, demand generation, and advertising.
