Sumeet Bhatia’s Summary

Currently the Chief SOA Architect for North America, Mr Bhatia has more than 11 years of experience in software and solution development, application integration, enterprise architecture, project and group management. Mr Bhatia established, and now manages the SOA Competency Center, which provides Fortune 500 companies proven methodologies, stepwise plans and reference artifacts to Integration and successful SOA Adoption and IT Governance using Industry Best Practices.

Mr Bhatia possess an in-depth expertise of the Integration space, Services Oriented Architectures (SOA), XML, Business Process Management Suites (BPMS), Security, Web 2.0, W3C and Oasis Standards, proven and emerging technologies.

Mr Bhatia is a successful leader, building excellent high performance teams by effective planning, mentoring, and commended execution techniques. Self motivated and have a proven success record with immense field experience in various verticals. Mr Bhatia has authored multiple white-papers and customer focussed recommendations on Integration and SOA.

Mr Bhatia has boosted sales revenue working with Account Team leaders, to help close key deals by establishing credibility with the customer and putting together actionable strategy plans. He is a trusted advisor for CTOs, CIOs and IT Directors in critical technology needs at strategy and tactical level. He is a skilled communicator with excellent white-boarding skills for closed groups as well as experienced presenter with large and small groups.

Mr Bhatia is a regarded Technical Consulting Manager having a successful track record of on-time delivery of all projects with a best practices based approach for effective planning, estimation, delivery and release management, building a repeatable team and state of the art solution architectures.

His technical expertise includes architecture frameworks like TOGAF, J2EE, .Net, Web Services - SOAP/RPC, MQ, Application Servers, multiple databases.
