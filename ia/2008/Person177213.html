Sarah Tyler’s Summary

Human Language Technologies research, particular focused on personalized search with additional interests in social networks, faceted search & novelty detection. My background includes machine learning, statistics and theory, with three years of experience in an applied research setting before I started graduate school. I also dabble in other fields of computer science such as networks and software engineering and am totally addicted to my own personal domain.

I am either a math-geek interested in words, or a word-nerd interested in math.
