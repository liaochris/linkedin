Yacco Vijn’s Summary

I'm a dutch creative working in interactive advertising since 1998. My work has been published in various books and publications worldwide and was featured in The New York Times, Apple.com, Guardian, Dazed & Confused, and others. I was member of the Art Directors Club jury in 2002 and do guest lectures and workshops (for Dove marketeers in Hamburg, Hyperisland students in Sweden). I founded ad agency Skipintro in 2001 which I left at the end of 2006 to work with Poke in London. In 2007 I co-founded digital hotshop KONG with N=5, who are the biggest dutch independent agency. KONG enjoyed a great start being the second most awarded digital agency of the year in the Netherlands.

Some of the clients I've worked with are AXE, ABN AMRO, Dove, KPN, MOMA Arnhem, Nuon, Orange, Oxfam, Postbank, BNP Paribas and Rijksmuseum.
