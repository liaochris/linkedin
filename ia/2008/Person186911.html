Tessa Cam’s Summary

I am a technical artist, currently based in the Philippines. I have been involved in the computer graphics industry since 2004, and have had adequate experience in gaming, feature film and post production. Apart from being a generalist in the field, I have also found a niche in education, sharing my growing knowledge of the field with young and eager students who have a real interest in art and animation and would like to pursue it as a career option.

Tessa Cam’s Specialties:

- High-end rigging for animation, facial expression and lip-syncing.
- Character, prop, set and environment modeling.
- Lead and background animation.
- Character and concept design, story-boarding, and look development.
