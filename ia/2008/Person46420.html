David DuPre’s Summary

A Performance Engineer with experience in application development, IT systems operations, IT support, disaster recovery planning, configuration management, and service desk operations.

Simulation modeling experience includes IT systems supporting: 3 wireless telephone companies, 6 commercial banks, 4 insurance companies, a foreign government, a retail company, a manufacturer, and 2 consulting companies.

Goal: To review application designs and recommend changes that will allow scaling to meet business needs while minimizing operational costs.

David DuPre’s Specialties:

Minimizing risk with performance engineering, cutting IT costs with simulation modeling, and improving application design with performance budgeting.
