George Koshy CISSP, CISA’s Summary

Professional Summary - George Koshy CISSP, CISA, CISM

Mr. Koshy is an IT Audit, Information Security & Risk Management Professional with 9 years of increasing experience in business, technology implementation, business continuity, security and risk assessment positions within the banking, financial, accounting and transportation industries. His background allows him a clear understanding of the business fundamentals as well as how to apply technology and security to tough business situations and problems to create value in the area of IT risk assessment and IT governance.

Currently, he is serving as an Assistant Vice President for IT Audit - Security / Infrastructure at the Bank of Tokyo - Mitsubishi UFJ group, one of the world's pre-eminent & largest financial groups.

Prior to joining Bank of Tokyo, Mr. Koshy worked with the IT Audit Group at the Metropolitan Transit Authority in New York, the largest transportation network in the US - moving 7.7 million people weekly with an operating budget of $8 billion and 67,000 employees spread across its seven operating agencies.

He holds a BS in Information Systems, as well as, a unique and specialized MS in Information Security and Assurance from a National Security Agency (NSA) School of Excellence. Currently, he is also finishing his MBA.

Within his current and previous capacities, his services have included performing complex IT Infrastructure Audits & Risk Management, IT Security Vulnerability and Risk Assessments, IT Operations & Strategy management, Business Continuity Assessments, Policy & Procedure Development, Penetration Testing, Security Testing and most recently PCI (Payment Card Industry) Data Security Standards (DSS) validations and general project management.

He is also a member of the ISACA, ISC2, Infraguard, HTCIA, IIA, American Writers & Artists International and the American Marketing Association professional associations.

George Koshy CISSP, CISA’s Specialties:

Mr. Koshy has received the CISSP (#74720), CISA (#0646077) and CISM (#0606412) professional certifications.

Mr Koshy holds a unique MS in Information Security and Assurance from a National Security Agency (NSA) School of Excellence in addition to his BS and MBA degrees.

In addition to his professional capacities, Mr Koshy also serves as the president of his private strategy and marketing firm specializing in Internet Direct Marketing and Copywriting services.
