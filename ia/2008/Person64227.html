Frank Klomp’s Summary

Principal consultant and owner of Alerta Marketing Intelligence, a research based consulting firm, located in Eindhoven, the Netherlands. Specializes in creating Marketing Intelligence, enabling Marketing Strategies and delivering Business Development for high tech companies. Also contributes to open innovation projects, building and capitalizing alliances between MNO's & SME's.

Previously held senior Marketing & Intelligence positions at Assembleon, formerly Philips Electronic Manufacturing Technology, and other divisions of Philips Electronics. Emphasizes the importance of qualitative information to support effective decision making processes, and is strongly focused on global venture studies, including turnkey & contract manufacturing, joint ventures and strategic alliances in Emerging Markets.

20 years of experience on the client side, engineering value propositions and designing proper business models, proactively informing and advising decision makers in banking, electronics and the public sector. Studied Business Administration in the UK and the USA, and earned an MA degree in International Economic Relations at the University of Amsterdam.
