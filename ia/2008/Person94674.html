John D. McCarthy’s Summary

John D. McCarthy has over 15 years experience in M&A, restructurings/turnarounds, capital raising and corporate development in the US and China for private (Distressed Debt/PE/VC backed) and public technology, manufacturing and service companies. Experienced in leading change and transformation in difficult situations. Broad experience in M&A deal sourcing, due diligence and post-merger integration. He was most recently EVP Operations & CFO for FitLinxx, Inc. the leading provider of fitness data technology solutions. Prior to FitLinxx he held senior positions, including acting CFO and SVP Sales & Corporate Development with Globix Corporation during its successful turn-around and restructuring through bankruptcy.

Prior to joining Globix, John held financial and general management positions with Young & Rubicam, United Technologies and the NYC internet incubator LC39 Venture Group. He received an MBA degree from The Wharton School of Business of the University of Pennsylvania and a Masters degree in International Studies from Wharton's Lauder Institute. He received his undergraduate degree in Asian Studies from Connecticut College and is proficient in Mandarin Chinese.

John D. McCarthy’s Specialties:

Turnarounds, Restructurings, M&A, China Investments, Capital Raising, Operations, Sales, Financial Management of publicly traded and private (distressed debt/venture/private equity backed) and public entities. Member Turnaround Management Association (TMA) and Association for Corporate Growth (ACG)
