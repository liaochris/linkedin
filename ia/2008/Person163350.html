Roderick "Rod" Brown, CSE’s Summary

A sales and marketing executive with extensive and diversified global experience with major automotive Tier 1 suppliers and OEMs. An innovative leader and change agent with exceptional ability to identify and pursue opportunities that grow sales profitability. Strong communication skills including formal presentation and writing. Team leader who can recruit, train, motivate, and retain quality professionals. Fluent in German.

During his career history, he has been privileged to work for high-profile automotive OEM and tier companies in North America, Europe and Asia in levels of increasing management and leadership responsibility. He has managed teams of up to 40 associates with sales over $2.5 billion annually. His goal is to become a member of a senior leadership team with a firm that has strong core values and is interested in growth, enhanced profitability and strong leadership.

In his most recent position, he was a member of the senior leadership team and was in charge of all product sales and component application engineering for Alps Automotive (a division of Alps Electric, Japan). Prior to Alps he held increasing levels of management and leadership positions at Delphi(Troy, MI), Siemens (Regensburg, Germany; Toulouse, France; Auburn Hills, MI,) Midwest Stamping (Maumee, OH; Dearborn, MI), Ford Motor Company (Dearborn, MI) and Caterpillar (Peoria, IL).
