Clive Turner, MIET’s Summary

2005-2007 Customer Care Training Manager, Nokia Head Office, Finland.
Leading a global team of 18 (24) through change management to be a high performance business-oriented team, providing ground-breaking initiatives to Nokia Customer Care through extended capabilities for Training Services and Competence Assessment.


2003-2005 APAC Technical & Product Security Manager, Singapore.
Hand picked, trained and built a 5-member high-performance team recognised across Nokia for consistently producing outstanding results in reporting, managing and resolving Product Quality problems. Key Member of APAC PSSL team.


2002-2003 APAC Technical Suppport Manager, Nokia Hong Kong. SARS!
Part of world wide team that wrote Nokia Health & Safety policy, another on Product Problem reporting process and system (CAP-GENIUS).
Lead a team of 7 including 2 Six Sigma master black belts to be a highly performing team. Key Member of APAC PSSL team

1998-2002 APAC & China Technical Support Manager, Nokia Singapore.
Developed standard product failure reporting Nokia wide, first extranet based Warranty Information System, and part of team developing and deploying first and subsequent successful extranet solutions for Customer Care.
Founded and chaired APAC Product Safety and Liability (PSSL) Team until 2003.
Member of team that permanently solved some of Nokia's worst recorded product quality problems.

1996-1998 AMS in RnD in Nokia Finland. Patent awarded. Developed first Nokia Customer Care Intranet, Technical Bulletin standard templates, and various other solutions.

1995-1996 AMS leader in R&D at Nokia UK, working in DECT and AMPS
