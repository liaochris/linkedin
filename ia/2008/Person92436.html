John Marchese’s Summary

Leadership, execution and results. I have a primary interest in bringing new products & companies to market and unleashing their potential and building shareholder value.

I have lead management teams and sales (P&L) organizations that controlled over $1 Billion in assets and $250 Million in revenues as well as pre-revenue startups in early formation.

Perhaps best known by people in the know for being one of a vocal handful who believed in the Citrix vision in the early 90's, and that lead me down a road of adventure, hard work, fun and accomplishment. I turned out to be right. I'll chose to believe I affected the outcome versus just taking the ride of an IPO rocket.

Then there was this time about 5.5 years later when Citrix needed to launch a new business division in the internet business space to promote the ASP (Application Service Provision) world... That was fun, profitable and filled with shareholder growth (about $5 Billion Dollars of incremental growth) over that time frame.

Google seems most focused on that time now, but I'm not sure was any more impressive than selling just $5,995.00 of Citrix in 1993. Neither was easy and that's why accomplishing both were so satisfying. All roads lead to Rome and my center, my core, was and has been expertise and leadership in the Citrix market as a small private domestic company and then as a large public multi-national company.

Much has been accomplished, experienced and further developed thereafter in terms of software, hardware, telecom & app services, mobile platforms, funding, app development, IP creation, M&A & invention.
