David Bruerd, CITRMS’s Summary

After becoming a victim himself in 2001, David Bruerd founded the Identity Theft Awareness Group™ (iTAG). iTAG has grown to become a national identity theft education and protection organization. iTAG conducts computerized Risk Assessments and provides onsite Privacy Trainings to help businesses create an ‘Affirmative Defense’ against the often unavoidable incident of data loss or breach. This helps businesses comply with current and proposed identity theft legislation.

Because of his early efforts as an identity theft advocate, David became the first person in the nation to receive the professional designation as a ‘Certified Identity Theft Risk Management Specialist’ and is considered Oregon’s foremost authority on the subject of identity theft. The CITRMS certification program is the nation’s only training program specifically developed for professionals who are dedicated to educating and assisting clients, customers, businesses, and the general public in combating the epidemic of Identity Theft and related fraud. David’s workshops address the ever-increasing threat of this emerging crime and the critical nature of providing business owners and the public with the knowledge and tools to address and manage risk.

Since 2003 David has provided over 620 seminars and trainings on the subject of identity theft and information security for businesses, associations and consumers groups. David’s workshops cover such key areas as: the nature and forms of identity theft and related fraud; consumer protection laws and related requirements; the public dossier and sources of information; identity theft risks and issues for businesses, including information security laws, related requirements and liabilities; risk management, identity theft risk management services, anti-fraud resources and more.
