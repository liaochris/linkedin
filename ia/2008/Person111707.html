Lavinia Weissman’s Summary

I am an expert in organizational behavior and methodologies for knowledge boutique companies under 2,000 employees that have to operate intelligently in a global market place.

The thought leaders and partners that I work with have successfully integrated their ideas into practice as it relates to pay for performance and corporate social responsiblity.

I have produced web based media that is available for syndication to any company, examining its culture and introducing its social network (market, multi-sector partners and employees) to the opportunity of sustainable change.

My subject matter expertise includes: Work Practices related to Leadership for Change, WorkEcology, Environment, Health and Sustainability.

I am a journalist with Strategy + Business. I have written for HR.com, Boston Women's Business Magazine, SuccessFactors Blog, and numerous other publications.

My recent articles are on the environment, health and sustainability.

Lavinia Weissman’s Specialties:

I have an exceptional intuitive skill that I draw from for my executive coaching practice that sparks leaders to draw from the practices of Core Group Theory and it's related practices to successfully introduce to market and society innovations aligned with SRI principles, the Earth Charter's Precuationary Principle and the UN Foundation agenda for the millenial workforce, women and creation of new jobs/economy.
