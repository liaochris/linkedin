Folkert Ringnalda’s Summary

When I saw the WWW for the first time (end of 1994), I realized that the Internet would have big impact on our professional and daily life. I decided to be part of that and started with two friends my first internet company (now called theFactor.e or TFE). Today I'm working as co-founder and director at Wowww! which I started with two partners in january 2007.

Before I went to the University, I worked for 8 years in the field of Healthcare (Crisis, Forensic, Biological Psychiatry). From 1991-1995 I studied Business Adminstration at the University of Groningen(NL). In 1995 I started a successful webdesign and development agency which was rewarded for best NL interactive agency in 2004 (ranked 5 in 2005). After 10 years I left TFE and sold my share. Worked for 9 months as MD Interactive for Ogilvy in Amsterdam. But I could not get settled in my role as employee. Worked in 2006 as independent internet consultant and developed e-business and web strategies for several clients, among them were a few internet start ups.
Started in 2007 a new web technology company called Wowww! We help organizations to make clever use of internet technology in the field of marketing, enterprise content management and strategic business applications.
