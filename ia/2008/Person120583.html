Margaret Cumming’s Summary

Margaret is a design and engineering expert at Insight Research.

Insight Research and Consulting serves the user research and product strategy needs of companies in the high tech industry. We distinguish ourselves from competitors with our notion of results-oriented consultancy.

We offer services for each phase of the product lifecycle so your products can meet their full market potential. As former business managers, we connect technology and users with an emphasis on business results. We serve as extended members of your team to gain user insights and transform them into tangible product recommendations.
