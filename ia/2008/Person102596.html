Karl Smith (LION)’s Summary

Business Activity

Paradigm Interactions consists of a number of highly creative thinkers and scientists who are able to create or take abstract concepts and interpret them for the design and development of new human computer interfaces and systems.

Our main strength is to enable our clients to meet the needs of their users in a way that increases transactions, builds brands and delivers users a quick, effective and reusable experience.

We are specifically involved in cross cultural internet, intranet and software developments. We operate a full lifecycle development process from working with stakeholders, capturing requirements, defining road maps, user and interaction definitions, personas and tasks, user centred design, application design, functional specifications, agile (xp) project management and testing procedures. We have been involved the creation of a number of frameworks for user generated content often described as web 2.0 applications, mash-ups and cross relational applications integrated into legacy or new data models.

Personal View

I am interested in People’s interpretation of technology, how they discover, rationalise and reuse it to suit their environments, goals and lifestyles, as this changes the rational between business and their marketplaces.
