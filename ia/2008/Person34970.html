Christopher Brenchley’s Summary

Chris Brenchley is a results-oriented leader with strong industry expertise in SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) based learning and performance solutions for working adults who leads with accountability, urgency and innovation in general and product management disciplines.

Currently a senior vice president with The Margrave Group (TMG) - a peer-to-peer knowledge exchange for affluent investors, Chris is a founding member of the management team and responsible for the firm's marketing and product management functions.

Prior to TMG, he served as Vice President, Services SBU for Thomson NETg – a market leader in corporate e-learning, where he was responsible for the firm’s $20MM+ professional services organization, its ongoing evolution/growth and customer success.

Throughout his career, Chris has held staff and management positions in the publishing, software and training industries – with responsibilities for product marketing/management, product development, marketing, technology and operations, all focused on delivering engaging, interactive and effective online learning experiences to working professionals.
