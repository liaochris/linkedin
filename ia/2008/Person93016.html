Joanne Shortell, PMP’s Summary

I prefer to work with people who want something more fulfilling than just the average American life. I help them identify and achieve their goals and help them ensure that those goals are what they want, not just what they've been told to want.

Professional and Life Experience:

I have three decades of varied experience in the public and private sector and an equally varied personal history.

I have worked for a small non-profit and also have worked with the Chairman, CEO, and CFO of one of the biggest investment banks in the world. I worked for the conservative CT Judicial Department and a Big Four accounting firm, but I also worked for a Manhattan publishing company with orange-haired employees who arrived to work on skateboards. I have been self employed and I have been disabled.

I have been an impoverished single mom who moved her small children into Hartford's inner city for lack of a car, and I have been a single Manhattanite with a six-figure technology job. I have been a musician's wife, a workaholic, and a recognized beer judge (really!).

I can relate to almost anyone!
