Jessica Jones’s Summary

In 2006 I finally returned to my element...Information Technology. Only this time, I am not the one fixing IT issues, I am the one finding jobs for those people. I have found my niche and enjoy what I do. I pride myself in being the recruiter that candidates and clients come back to over and over for career movements and needs.

I billed over $190k in my first 4 months as a Sr. Development Recruiter at BravoTECH.
