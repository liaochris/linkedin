Andy Edwards’s Summary

My music business career began at grass roots level while at university in the late 80s/early 90s – DJing, writing, promoting gigs; highlights including working with Primal Scream and Nirvana.

During that time, I was one of the first people to write extensively on digital music – it was my undergraduate thesis in 1992/93. Since then, my work in the areas of digital music and new business models has been a continual thread throughout my career, which includes spells for corporations such as BMG Records and Sony Music in marketing roles; and more recently in the start-up/ entrepreneurial sector.

I have worked across all aspects of the music business – records, publishing, touring, artist management, brand marketing and digital – and with some of the most experienced and successful executives and entrepreneurs in the global music business.

Andy Edwards’s Specialties:

Music business: marketing, business development, strategy, all with a heavy digital emphasis.

Increasing exposure to marketing services, mobile telcos and ISPs.

  • Marketing Manager

I also initiated one of the first mobile phone deals for music; supplying artist branded ringtone and logo offerings.
