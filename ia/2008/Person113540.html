Angela D.’s Summary

--Therapist/Counsel to clients with emotional, mental, and substance abuse disorders, adults, adolescents, and children
--State Certified in Grief and Loss, Substance Abuse and Addiction, Parent Educator, Child Development
--Experience in Child Protective Services, working with adult offenders of child sexual, physical, and emotional abuse, court testimony, case management, Court mandated reports, etc.


Currently, I am in the final days of development of the "Online Organization of Coaching Associates" (OOCA). OOCA is a grassroots associative organization dedicated to bringing about unity between individuals and online/ community personal coaches by way of information, education, and support services to coaches in all areas of expertise and to the clients that they serve.

OOCA will offer professional coaches and professionals in business, marketing, and other areas who utilize their experience to educate and mentor other to belong to an associate organization that offers extensive networking and the ability to share with individuals their expertise through the "Coaching Connection" discussion forum (with encouragement to post links to their websites, blogs, and to upload photos). Businesses have the opportunity to advertise at special rates. Each professional who becomes a member (qualifications and experience will be requested) will then become a qualified Coaching Associate, belonging to one of the first Online Coaching Associations nationwide. Additionally, an articles section with numerous pages and categories is available; thus, the OOCA will be looking for authors/writers familiar with Coaching, Business, and Marketing. Each author will receive a full-page bio with photo and the ability to list numerous links to their own sites.
