Martin Conner’s Summary

My 25 years of healthcare experience has been with generating business and establishing and managing the sales process for healthcare companies and/or new information technology solutions for the acute care arena.

I was among the first reps hired for B. Braun Medical (formerly Burron Medical) and worked for 14 years in the medical device industry. The last 11 years have been with new ventures for established companies and with start-ups launching new information technologies into healthcare. In 1997, I started my own sales agency providing products and services giving patients and families access to timely and better health information. Most recently I have been working in automating the clerical portion of discharge planning and enhancing revenue cycle management for hospitals to remain competitive and viable.
