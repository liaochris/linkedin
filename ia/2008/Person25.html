Heather Young’s Summary

I am currently an Independent Marketing Professional, contracting out to creative agencies and small businesses. Collectively, I have close to nine years of advertising and marketing experience, and have a BA in Mass Communication and Spanish Language.

My media capabilities include radio, television, print, out-of-home, and online planning and placement. I have also aided in the planning and execution of PR events for audiences of 20 to 700 people, and have written content for both BtoB and BtoC websites.

I am very organized, and systematic in problem solving, whether by conventional or unconventional means- whatever it takes to get the job done. I'm not afraid to "roll up my sleeves" and work until the project is finished. I like working in a team environment, and enjoy collaborating with colleagues to get the best ideas out on the table, and into execution. Even when I'm working independently, I am consistently bouncing ideas off of the other independent professionals around me.

My client history includes successfully managing advertising and PR campaigns from start to finish for such clients as Better Business Bureau, Blanco Lavendar Festival, City of Austin Health & Human Services, San Angelo Chamber, St. Louis School, Texas Music Festival, Texas Windstorm, and Village of Ruidoso. I have also played integral roles in teams managing regional campaigns for Samuel's Diamonds, Scott & White Hospitals, Time Warner Cable, and Austaco (Taco Bell).

My marketing experience has allowed me to hone my abilities for communicating clearly and concisely with my audience, whether through brochures and other leave-behinds, group presentations, via the web (site content, e-newsletters, online advertising), or even through client and interoffice communications.

Heather Young’s Specialties:

Media Rate and Added Value Negotiation, Marketing Material Content Writing, and Client Relations.

I also have the ability to bring team members together, and draw out the best of each of them.
