Sean Harmer’s Education

  • Keele University

    Ph.D., Astrophysics, 1999 — 2003

    My Ph.D. thesis is "X-ray emission from intermediate polars and open clusters". This was concerned with the theoretical modelling of X-ray emission from the accretion columns of intermediate polars including a study of parameter space and an analysis of an outburst of GK Per using the model developed. Also covered is a statistical analysis of X-ray emissions from various open clusters.
