Luis Cruz Diaz, MBA’s Summary

A business focused and results oriented executive with over 15 years of strategic supply chain management experience to his position with responsibilities covering Procurement & Warehouse Management, Supply Chain, Logistics, Operations, Marine Terminal Operations, Project Management, Administration and Telecommunications.

Strategically driven, with proven track record at working with and leading cross-geographical, cross-functional transformations and company-wide teams and program initiatives.

Recognized for delivering breakthrough results in global materials management and low cost country commodity sourcing based in Europe, Central and South America and in the Caribbean.

Excellent understanding of the supply chain with emphasis on new ideas, team building, innovation, measurement, process improvement and productivity, long range planning, supplier quality engineering, warehousing, logistics and performance which impact, lead to and represent the greatest savings and ensure continuity of supply.
