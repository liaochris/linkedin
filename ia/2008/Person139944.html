Neven Cvetkovic’s Summary

Neven Cvetkovic has co-founded Programming Mastery to help programmers achieve the Programming Mastery. As a former business co-founder, management consultant, event co-ordinator and IT Manager, Neven is experienced in creating and implementing effective business and IT strategies. Neven was the Co-Founder and Partner of a management-consulting firm, SynVier Inc., which specializes in assisting start-ups and small organizations in all aspects of business initiation including business plan development, software and systems analysis as well as marketing and communications. Through Headfusion Inc, his consulting firm, Neven directed the strategic IT initiatives for a leading IT training and consulting company, The Middleware Company, and its J2EE portal, TheServerSide.com. This included evaluation, recommendation and implementation of various technologies, corporate website management and administration of TheServerSide.com. Neven’s significant contributions to TheServerSide led to its immense industry success, which was a key contributor for the acquisition of The Middleware Company by Precise Software Solutions, and then further acquisition by Veritas Software in 2003. Neven has also worked at large corporations such as IBM and Canadian National Railway. Neven’s strong background in strategic business and process analysis coupled with his ability to see projects from inception to completion, enable him to complete challenging and complex projects on time and within the budget, delivering high impact results to his clients. Neven holds a Master in Business, Entrepreneurship and Technology as well as a Bachelor of Mathematics in the Honors Computer Science Co-operative Program from University of Waterloo. Neven currently resides in Waterloo, Canada.
