Michael Kirsch’s Summary

Michael Kirsch is Head of Mobile Marketing Solutions at U-Turn Media Group responsible for U-Turn’s mobile marketing solutions, strategy, partnerships and business development.
Owner of mlyk consulting - mobile technology consulting.

Prior to U-Turn Media Group Michael was CMO / Director Sales & Marketing at Ginger Alliance s.r.o. (acquired by U-Turn Media Group), responsible for strategy, marketing, product management, partnerships and business development for Ginger Alliance and its sister companies.

Before he was CTO for Whoosh Group Ltd. based in London.
Acquired by YooMedia PLC, www.yoomedia.com.
Whoosh provided TV / media interactivity via the mobile phone using SMS; for clients like the BBC, Celador and CarltonTV.

Before that Michael was Senior Sales Engineer for Wysdom based out of Toronto, Canada. There he oversaw the relationship with the leading mobile operator in Europe and provided technical sales support for EMEA.

Before he was Project Manager for Mobile Data Services at Mobilcom Multimedia GmbH responsible for UMTS mobile data services, partnerships with middleware vendors and strategy planning for new mobile technologies.
