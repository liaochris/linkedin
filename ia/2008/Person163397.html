Rodney Decarteret CISSP, CISA, GCFW, GCFA, CEH’s Summary

Mr. DeCarteret is a senior consultant with SGIS. Acts as a senior security consultant, and business development and staffing consultant in the areas of strategy and operations. More than five years of information security experience, and more than ten years of experience managing information technology systems and projects with responsibility for cost, schedule, and performance. Has experience with utilizing highly complex information management systems to successfully monitor security events and manage security incidents. Skilled in analyzing global information security trends and utilizing various INFOSEC data sources including classified sources. Has experience recruiting and developing information security staff. Client experience includes U.S. Department of Defense and its Service Components (USA, AF etc), U.S. Senate and various commercial clients.
