Ann Rowland’s Summary

Ann K. Rowland Inc. is the parent company to LiveWell Corporate Wellness Programs and SleepWell. In developing LIveWell, our goal is to put wellness into the workplace. In today's environment of skyrocketing healthcare costs, we firmly believe that the key to containing these costs and improving productivity is preventing chronic disease and diminished physical function in the first place. We work with small- to medium-sized organizations in both the private and public sectors to help them develop a cohesive wellness initiative for their organizations, improving the lives of their employees and helping these organizations with their bottom lines.

With SleepWell, utilizing my certification as a CBT Sleep Educator, I have eight years of experience helping people conquer their insomnia in individual, group and corporate settings.

Ann Rowland’s Specialties:

Corporate Wellness Consultant, Wellness Coach, Certified Sleep Educator, Certified Personal Trainer

    With dual masters degrees in psychology and exercise physiology and over 20 years of experience in health and wellness, I have developed a unique perspective to integrating both the mind and body into my wellness presentations.

  • Program Director

    Lake Forest Hospital

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    1997 — 2007 (10 years)

    In the area of Corporate Wellness, I supported the Occupational Health Department of Lake Forest Hosptial by designing and teaching a wide variety of wellness classes to their corporate clients; I provided the Occupational Health (OCH) Manager with marketing materials outlining wellness programs to distribute to their corporate clients. and assisted the OCH Manager by administering cholesterol, bone density, blood glucose, and fitness assessment screenings at corporate health fairs.

    Also independently taught symptom reduction programs for people with insomnia, arthritis,and smoking addiction.

    In Employee Wellness, I chaired employee wellness committee, designed, marketed and taught health promotion programs for 1,400 employees of the Lake Forest Hospital Foundation. and spearheaded the effort that resulted in the 1998 Winner of the Well Workplace Silver Award by Wellness Councils of America.
