Scott Robertson’s Summary

Scott Robertson is a passionate executive gifted in building teams, executing strategies, and maximizing the performance of executives at every level. His leadership skills have been refined through his experiences as a collegiate coach, professional athlete, and high impact performer in business. Scott has achieved outstanding success in executing highly profitable business models, mentoring peers to reach higher performance levels, and influencing management to adopt and execute new business strategies. Whether it is creating a foundation for successful athletic training programs or establishing business platforms in the continental and international business arenas, Scott's personality, communication and leadership skills have resulted in enduring impact. As President of Leadership ID, he brings his extensive experience to the marketplace. His unique talents enable companies to maximize the skills of their executives and produce leaders of significance.

    Developing leaders has proven to be the number one priority of organizations through multiple independent research firms. The question is no longer, “Do we need to develop leaders?”. The question now is, “How?” The Leadership ID process highlights that every leader is unique and starts the leadership journey from their own experiences. Obviously, we are all different. This means each individual will derive something unique from leadership training. This also means those differences must be recognized and utilized properly so each leader complements one another.

    Identifying a leader is more than just a few typical and usually obvious characteristics such as appearance or mannerisms. Leaders prefer to adjust their reactions to meet each situation. They blend various talents and abilities to create a special style that makes it look like they lead just like everyone else, yet each leader is significantly unique.
