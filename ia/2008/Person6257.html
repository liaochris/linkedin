Alexander Marquardt’s Summary

I have worked in R&D in Software, Semiconductors, and Electronics, for 10+ years.

In addition to my work experience, I have an MBA from IESE (2 years full-time) in Barcelona, have an M.A.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (2 years full-time) from the University of Toronto, and a Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering (5 years full time) from the University of Manitoba.

In my most recent job, I worked as a Program/Project Manager for a Spanish consumer electronics company where I led the successful development of various Bluetooth consumer products. This required managing and scheduling all company R&D resources including electrical engineers, computer scientists, IT personnel, industrial designers, and graphic designers. It also required coordinating and scheduling interaction with Asian subcontractors, as well as reviewing all documentation, packaging, and other steps required for bringing products to the consumer market.

In my previous positions at Altera and Right Track CAD, I worked as a semiconductor architect and software engineer.

Prior to Altera and Right Track CAD, I co-developed a very large and sophisticated public domain software package called VPR that has been downloaded by thousands of researchers and that has spawned hundreds of scientific publications.

I also have co-authored a textbook about Field Programmable Gate Arrays (a type of computer chip with annual sales of approximately $3 billion), have written numerous scientific papers about semiconductor and software design, and co-authored 2 patents with the Altera corporation.
