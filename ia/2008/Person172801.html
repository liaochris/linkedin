SEO Rahul Shrivastava’s Summary

Developed SEO skills working as AVP, Project Manager, and SEO Expert in leading SEO Companies having clients from North America and Europe. Managing team for website development and SEO Projects for Foreign and Indian Clients

Now working as Consultant to Eklavya - a non-profit, non-government organization that develops and field tests innovative educational programmes and trains resource people to implement these programmes. It functions through a network of education resource centers located in Madhya Pradesh.

For over two decades, Eklavya has sought to relate the content and pedagogy of education – both formal and non-formal - to social change and the all-round development of the learner.

It evolves learner-centered teaching methodologies that foster problem-solving skills in children and encourage them to ask questions about their natural and social environment. This approach helps children become life-long self-learners.
