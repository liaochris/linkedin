Heather Larson’s Summary

I have ten years of experience in radio broadcasting. I've worked in San Jose, San Francisco, and now Phoenix since 2000. In ten years, I've done rock (AOR), classic rock, 80s modern rock, CHR (top 40), urban, CHR/rhythmic, sports, and news. In these formats I've been an on-air personality, producer, web content writer, and even a news writer-editor. I hold three radio broadcast certificates from Ohlone College in Fremont, CA and one journalism certificate from Paradise Valley Community College in Phoenix, AZ. The college degree is in progress "at" Boston University's Metropolitan College. The program is a b.s. in liberal arts and I attend online.

I've written for the Paradise Valley College Puma Press Newspaper, Sweat Magazine, Contact Magazine, Veg News Magazine, Suite101.com, CNET's Webshots, Batanga.com, Northeast Mesa Lifestyle Magazine, Gilbert Lifestyle Magazine, Senior Lifestyle (monthly newspaper in the East Valley at the time I was there), Westwood One Metro Networks, 987ThePeak.com, Phoenix Art Space, MelodyTrip.com, Submitawebsite.com, Qwixo.com, and The Bit X Change.

I am currently interning for Organic Processing Magazine. In the past, I have interned for Sweat Magazine and Razor Magazine.

My goals are to be full-time on the air as a radio personality while also functioning as a freelance music journalist. Until I can do it all the way I want to, I do it online at my own website, just launched: TheMusicStalker.com.

Another goal I have is to take radio out of the dark ages and into modern times, starting with the web.
