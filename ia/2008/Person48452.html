David Cote’s Summary

I consider myself a new media entrepreneur, social media evangelist and a branding thinker. With a unique blend of exceptional technical skills – hardware, software and new media – accompanied by solid business management experience, I have the passion and vision to marry these innovative technologies with new market opportunities.

- Responsible for the overall design, management, development and operations of a portfolio of targeted customer websites and internet strategies.
- Develop, manage, and analyze a full range of online marketing initiatives, including paid search (SEM), website optimization (SEO), blogs, podcasts and other interactive forms of advertising and promotion.
- Accountable for managing on-going expansion of pay-per-click (PPC) campaigns and working with a cross-functional team to constantly improve organic search results.
- Devise new tactics leveraging the growing usage of the web and other alternative channels to increase brand awareness for all clients over and above traditional media coverage.
- Provide detailed metrics of web traffic statistics, reports and blog comment feedback.
- Perform continuous enhancements and modifications to online properties by tracking industry trends and news occurrences impacting client business strategy both internationally and domestically.
- Develop new relationships while handling existing client relations by maintaining a network of media contacts.
- Coach client representatives in effective communication with the public and with employees.
- Arrange public appearances, lectures and exhibits for clients to increase product and service awareness and to promote goodwill between an organization and its public.
- Experience with point-of-contact, face-to-face client management.
- Study the objectives, promotional policies and needs of organizations to develop public relations strategies that will influence public opinion or promote ideas, products and services.

David Cote’s Specialties:

- Proven ability as an excellent communicator with a persuasive and forthright style, yet a team player with an entrepreneurial flair.
- Savvy and aggressive online marketer capable and experienced in using a vast array of available resources.
- Exceedingly knowledgeable in creating, developing and sustaining online properties including blogs, podcasts, wikis, forums and other social networking sites.
