Tim Allan’s Summary

I am an experienced designer for both web and print, and have worked in the advertising, design and marketing sectors for over 12 years.

Having worked for some of the biggest names in advertising and publishing, including The Condé Nast Publications Ltd, Cordiant Communications group, Bates UK Advertising, and XM.London (formerly CCG.XM), I am now working as a freelance art director, designer and animator.

Recent projects have included Ceative Direction and Production of Rich Media campaign for Orange Business Services, Brochure design and production for Lloyd's, Xerox and William Asprey & Sons.

I am interested in freelance/contract positions and commissions.

I have a network of technical partners and can offer a full range of interactive and printe design/production services.

Tim Allan’s Specialties:

Web design and production, Flash animation, Print design, Branding and Print Production.
