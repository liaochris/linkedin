Mark Hardison’s Summary

An inspiring brand champion with more than 14 years of marketing expertise, Mark Hardison, Vice President of Marketing, joined El Pollo Loco in 2001. In his six-year tenure with the thriving quick service restaurant chain, Mr. Hardison has played an integral role in positioning El Pollo Loco for nationwide growth and leading the company to achieve record sales. His enthusiasm and passion for brand building inspired El Pollo Loco’s marketing team and agency partners to reinvent the brand as a unique concept that combines the value and convenience of fast food with the freshness and high quality associated with food from emerging fast casual concepts.

Through the years, Mr. Hardison’s instinctive marketing savvy has impacted a number of dynamic business environments. Prior to El Pollo Loco, he held marketing management positions with CKE Restaurants, McKesson Water Products Company and Mitsubishi Electronics America.

Mr. Hardison’s many contributions to brand building include the development and execution of bilingual promotional strategies, creation of television and radio campaigns, direction of restaurant merchandising and point of purchase marketing, and design and implementation of market-specific media strategies. He was awarded El Pollo Loco’s coveted Premio de Oro for outstanding sales contribution and was recognized with an EFFIE award for El Pollo Loco’s advertising campaign.

A member of the Orange County Chapter of the American Marketing Association, Mr. Hardison contributes to a number of community outreach programs. Mr. Hardison holds a Bachelor of Science degree in business administration with concentrations in marketing and negotiation from the University of Phoenix. He is an avid mountain bicyclist who enjoys an on-the-go lifestyle with his wife and three young children.
