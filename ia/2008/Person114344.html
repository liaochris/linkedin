Lisa Fomin’s Summary

Creator of the 'NINE DIMENSIONS' assessment model, Lisa Fomin specializes in:

- Personality mapping and career assessment
- Creative consulting (accommodating theatre training to corporate and individual coaching)
- Performance management program development
- Change management for staff and executives (mergers and acquisitions)
- Staff Development

Prior to her affiliation with Reflections HOPE, Lisa assumed a number of project management and entertainment industry roles.

Ms Fomin has a B. in Economics from St. Petersburg State University of Economics and Finance and a Diploma in Health Counselling. She also serves on Board of Directors for Reflections HOPE and is involved in activities supporting her local community.
