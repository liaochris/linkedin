Barry White’s Summary

A digital distribution specialist with 8 years experience working in mobile and internet gaming platforms. Founder of GameJacket and part of the team that launched casual games company SoGoPlay.

  • Head of Sales

    SoGoPlay (now Eidos)

    (Computer Games industry)

    July 2006 — August 2007 (1 year 2 months)

    Recruited to help establish SoGoPlay as the new casual gaming division for Rockpool Games and was responsible for setting up global digital distribution channels for casual games and online games from the SoGoPlay portfolio.

    The first year saw an exciting period of growth as the market matured and strong relationships were formed with major game portals including RealArcade, Oberon, TryMedia, Reflexive, Zylom, Yahoo, iWin, BigFish Games and many others.

    Following the recent acquisition of Rockpool by Eidos Interactive (SCi Entertainment Group Plc), SoGoPlay has now been adopted as their global brand for Casual Games.
