Jim McMichael’s Summary

I have a long and successful background in Management, Sales and Marketing. I started in the automotive industry in 1977 after my honorable discharge from the military as a Disabled American Veteran. I have been involved in the automotive industry in some capacity for over 25 years, and I am well educated and versed in it and it's operations. I have held the positions of lot guy, sales (new and used), consignment management, finance manager, lease, commercial/fleet manager and new and used director. The auto industry is like most others in the fact that, if you don’t work in it daily and keep yourself in touch with all of the changes and different programs, then you most likely can and will miss some aspect that would work to your advantage. Hiring a professional who specializes in their industry is something we all do because we know that we “honestly” don’t know. We work on our computers daily, but if it breaks down, we call an IT person. Why? Because we don’t know! When we need a home we call an agent or “broker”. If we need a car, all of a sudden we’re a pro…not really!!! My best grossing deals were those that thought they got the best deal by really working me over. As someone with over 25 year’s experience, I can and will make sure that we protect your best interest. We as your consultant will be able to assist you in the purchase of the make, model and features that you want no matter what it is from a sedan, coupe, SUV, Pick-up or commercial vehicle up to 56,000 lbs gvw. If you have a want, need or desire… we will help you get it! I'll work hard to earn your business and just as hard to keep it.
