Peter Crowley’s Summary

Having founded & exited Webzone Limited (which owns carzone.ie Ireland's number 1 car website) I am looking for new opportunites to spark my interest.

Peter Crowley’s Specialties:

Taking an idea and running with it. Most recently my technical knowledge was in Adobe Coldfusion, SQL, Server Management (2000,XP), DNS, IIS, Javascript though it does not take long for me to gain a solid grasp of the technical side of any project I am focused on.

Peter Crowley’s Experience

  • Chief Technical Officer & Founder

    Webzone Limited

    (Internet industry)

    December 1998 — February 2007 (8 years 3 months)

    I founded Webzone in December 1998 & helped grow it's carzone website into the number 1 motoring website in Ireland, consistently one the top 3 most trafficked Irish websites (carzone, daft, myhome). Webzone was also a successful web design & development company in that it develops or manages many of the car manufacturer websites in Ireland and most of the car dealer websites. In addition to managing the development & production side of the business I assisted in all aspects of the management of the company which was sold to Trader Media Limited.

  • Contractor for SCO Professional Services

    The Santa Cruz Operation

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; SCOC; Computer Software industry)

    August 1994 — September 1995 (1 year 2 months)

    I used my porting skills to port applications from MSDOS to the SCO UNIX platform. These included a prescription ordering system for Walgreens and a point of sale tool for Radioshack. The later project involved 4 months onsite in the Radioshack HQ in Barrie, Ontario Canada where I advised & assisted the local development team.

  • Internet Demonstrator

    Digital Equipment Corporation, Western Research Laboratory, Palo Alto

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; DEC; Computer Hardware industry)

    May 1994 — August 1994 (4 months)

    I worked on creating demonstration sites for many of Digital's potential Internet clients, these included the local Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce, KQED a local PBS TV station, BankAmerica, HFC part of HSBC.

  • Senior Software Engineer

    Quarterdeck Office Systems

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry)

    May 1989 — March 1994 (4 years 11 months)

    I started in Quartedeck on a research project porting X Windows to the MS-DOS DESQview environment, which eventually turned in the DESQview/X product. I managed the early DESQview/X product releases & developer tools. In addition I created the initial OPTIMIZE component of the QEMM product for which I recieved the PC Magazine Award for Technical Excellence.
