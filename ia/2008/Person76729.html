Charles Heunemann 許瀚民’s Summary

Senior Executive with 25 years experience in the Information Technology sector including 10 years in IT security.

Managing Director for Webroot Pty Ltd (formerly Email Systems Pty Limited) Asia Pacific Operations. Webroot provides industry leading security software for consumers, enterprises and small and medium businesses worldwide. In November 2007 Webroot merged with Email System a leading global provider of SaaS (Software as a Service) security solutions for email and web protection, management and compliance services for Enterprise, ISP and OEM customers.

Prior to establishing Email Systems Pty Limited Asia Pacific Operations was Co-Founder and board member for SurfControl operations for Asia Pacific, achieving 43% compound annual growth rate, firmly establishing Australia as SurfControl’s fastest growing operation ultimately increasing market share to 40% (IDC) and achieving rankings in the top 10 security software vendors in Asia Pacific – Gartner.

Pioneered the Australian Content Filtering Market as Director of Sales for Lanvision. Achieved 12 consecutive record-breaking revenue quarters and established Lanvision as the leading distributor of content filtering technologies in Australia, resulting in the successful acquisition of Lanvision by SurfControl in 2000.

Commissioned three first-of-a-kind studies into the attitudes of Australian workers to Internet and email usage and security, further reinforcing my company's leadership position relating to the Internet at work.

At the forefront of knowledge on the effects of the Internet on modern workplace practices, with expertise cited in Australia and overseas. An industry commentator for leading information technology and news media.
