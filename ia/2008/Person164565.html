Ron Wiener’s Summary

Currently CEO of my 5th startup, Earth Class Mail Corporation (fka Document Command, Inc. and Remote Control Mail Corp). Previously was founder and CEO of four other investor-backed high-technology companies; two in aviation, two in dot-com: Azure Technology (San Jose), Distribution Sciences Corp / JetStream (Hillsboro, OR), PrintBid.com (Portland, OR) and SnapNames.com (Portland, OR).

In between founding and running my own companies I operate Venture Mechanics, LLC, an advisory and investment firm which works with early-stage companies to help them prepare for and obtain their first round of institutional venture capital investment financing. We also incubate internally-spawned companies, including most recently Earth Class Mail Corp., SnapNames.com and PrintBid.com, in our Portland-based facility.

Venture Mechanics is also the entity for my personal angel investments, but is otherwise in hibernation at the moment while I am 100% absorbed with the global expansion of Earth Class Mail.

In 2007 Earth Class Mail was honored to be the sole subject of the first season of the first TV documentary series of its kind, "Start-up Junkies," about people like me who apparently can't do anything but start-ups and stay sane. See http://www.earthclassmail.com/blog/category/start-up-junkies/ for links to the show if you don't have MOJO HD on your cable subscription.

Ron Wiener’s Specialties:

Envisioning, launching, running and financing early stage, rapid-growth technology companies. I am currently running my fifth investor-backed startup in 20 years of being a serial entrepreneur. Industries have included aviation, ecommerce, direct marketing, domain name infrastructure, and postal mail logistics. I have served on numerous boards as an investor, industry expert or advisor, mostly with software and ecommerce companies.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Services industry)

The service is used by individuals, small businesses, and major corporate and government enterprises, with users currently accessing the www.earthclassmail.com website from over 130 countries. It is a popular alternative to P.O. Box rental, executive suites and mail-forwarding services. Founded in 2004, Earth Class Mail is headquartered in Seattle, Washington, and has over 20 points of presence in the USA from New York to San Francisco where customers may have their mail delivered.
