Boris von Bormann’s Summary

I have been involved in the international trade industry for over 11 years. I attended the University of Dortmund/ Germany where I concentrated my studies on Management. In Germany I founded and partnered in the marketing firm “Ratioplan”, focused on selling trade show services and an in- house trade show system to the trade show industry. Upon selling my interests in Ratioplan, I then moved to the United States furthering my education and experience in marketing at the California State University at Long Beach.


I am the founder and CEO of accessio, an independent consulting company that focuses on the cross- cultural business aspects. accessio specializes in working with foreign companies to facilitate the market entry to the North American markets and US companies that are looking to enter the European market place. In the United States, accessio has its offices in Orange County, California.

I recently founded with my business partner Nik Hafermaas the design firm Uebersee, focusing on designing exhibitions, showrooms, and interiors using a unique narrative approach. UeBERSEE - www.uebersee.us - is located in Los Angeles, which allows me to bring my business experience into contact with the cultural and artistic realm.

Furthermore I am a shareholder in the company X-PROTECT, which provides copy protection solutions for the entertainment industries.

I am currently the acting Chairman of the German American Business Association in Southern California, a non- profit organization in Southern California and is the president of the board of directors of the Friends of Goethe of the Goethe Institute in Los Angeles.
