Sara Vetteth’s Experience

  • CEO

    The Design Cell (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Graphic Design industry)

    May 2006 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    The Design Cell is based out of Chennai, India and is a small focused design firm that specializes in usability and interface design. Since we started a year ago whe have been working with our partners in the US to deliver high-end design for companies like Symantec, Gladding-Hearn, IM21, Smith O Brian, NewWork Solutions and others. Locally we've worked with Sundaram Finance and have done some interesting work for various non-profits.

    We also support a graphic design course in the vocational training Institute, Children of the World, India which is funded by the French Red Cross.

  • Design Director

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry)

    January 2004 — July 2005 (1 year 7 months)

    Based in NY - Managed Interface design for all clients of the XL group including Abbott Pharmaceuticals, Ross, BMW, Jaguar, IBM and many others. Designed and maintained the coporate site and identity as well as an internal product to track the knowledge work's productivity.
