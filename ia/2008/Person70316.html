Giovanni Formenti’s Summary

After 20 years passed in my family enterprise , Formenti-Seleco ( TV-sets production with Seleco and lately Brionvega brands ) , I started in 2006 my own consultancy company , ASSET srl , with the aim of providing my personal experience to different companies interested to develop or review their strategic , marketing and commercial assets .
I currently operate in the fields of consumer electronics , design , furniture, hotel equipments, automotive and with some Italian little-mid size companies .
My aim is to provide “tailor made” solutions to any company in the areas of strategic and operative marketing ,business development, internationalisation , organisation, formation, and recently start-ups/ turnarounds .
