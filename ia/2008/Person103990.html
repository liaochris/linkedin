Kathy Venneman Donelan’s Summary

I started Aunt Kathy's in 1998 in my house. I was certified for 6 children. Soon after I became licensed for 11 children. In 2002 I moved to a commercial building and became licensed for 33 children. At present I am licensed for 59 children.
I believe it is very important for children to be home with their parents. That is why I offer hourly and daily rates as well as weekly child care rates. I have a small center in order to provide quality care targeting lots of individual attention to the child.
