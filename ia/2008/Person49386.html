David Johnson’s Summary

I have 30 years experience in providing professional mental health services in group, individual, and family therapy, diagnostic assessment, treatment planning, crisis intervention, and short-term intensive psychotherapy. I also have 16 years of experience in clinical supervision and mental health business management, program development, contract negotiation and administration, networking, building referral networks, quality improvement projects that included development of outcome measurement process.

David Johnson’s Specialties:

Mental health treatment and programming with serious and persistent mental illness and culturally diverse populations.
