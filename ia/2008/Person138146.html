Nancy Orlikow’s Summary

Nancy Orlikow has over 18 years of professional experience in the field of art therapy, counselling, psychology and expressive arts therapy. She has worked in a variety of settings, ranging from psychiatric hospitals, clinics, schools, and community-based non-profit agencies. She has worked with individuals, families, couples, and groups. Nancy is also an accomplished visual artist, specializing primarily in painting and children's illustration.
