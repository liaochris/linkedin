Marco Marlia’s Summary

WORK

I partecipate (mainly through the holding Kerios Group S.r.l.) different companies in Internet and E-business, Real Estate and Photovoltaic and Renewable Energy.

I am one of the promoter of a large photovoltaic plant in southern Italy.

Nextre Engineering is a software/system integrator, we develop and integrate web software mainly on the Java Enterprise stack (J2EE). We have a vertical expertise in Rich Internet Applications and Rich User Interfaces.

Biquadra is one of the top Web Marketing and Search Engine Marketing company in Italy.

Biquadra and Nextre have offices in Milan and are expanding in Spain and Hungary.

I also partecipate a new asset management company in Hungary.

I have recently co-founded Treeweb a web design agency.

I have started my first company (Juxt S.n.c, a small web design and web software company) during college.

I have also worked at Università Commerciale 'Luigi Bocconi' (ELAUT and MOET courses) as IT Tutor, and as Administrator at OLE.

I've completed a stage at Merrill Lynch IPCG in Milan.


EDUCATION

I graduated with 109/110 in "Economia delle Istituzioni e Mercati Finanziari" (Economics - Institutions and Financial Markets) at Università Commerciale 'Luigi Bocconi'.

After two years of research and following as assistant two edition of the Master in Online Education and Trainging my thesis was: "ICT, Learning Communities and Communites of Practice: a proposal for a decision-making model".

I have studied management, leadership, communication, NLP (I have completed a Master in NLP with Andrea Favaretto and attende courses with Richard Bandler and John La Valle - still waiting for the certification anyway).


SPORT

I have been the youngest instructor in Jeet Kune Do, Kali & Escrima with Combat Dept (Vinicio Del Beccaro). I teached for 6 years. I have also trained in Brasilian Ju Jitsu, MMA and Free fight. Now I'm training in Acrobatics and Capoeira.
