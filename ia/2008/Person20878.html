Becky Kessel’s Summary

Coolhires is a Managed Recruiting Services firm that offers a variety of recruiting solutions including: outsourced recruiting services (RPO), recruiting consulting and training, and traditional search services. By aligning our client's hiring strategy with their business plan, Coolhires shifts from a traditional transaction-based vendor to a professional services based partnership.

Coolhires has the vision and goals to become a leader in the recruiting industry. We are actively growing our business to new metropolitan areas, and over the next 5 years, we are planning an aggressive growth strategy that will call for Coolhires to open many more branches outside of Texas. Through Coolhires methodologies, our customers are able to lower their Cost Per Hire as well as improve their ability to acquire the needed talent.

We have designed, implemented, and managed recruiting processes for Fortune 100 companies such as Microsoft and Dell. We also have partnered with Start-up companies by designing and implementing their very first Recruiting practice. We believe whether you are a Fortune 100 or a small start-up, having a mature recruiting process will help you win the "War for Talent".
