Adesh Singh’s Summary

I am an Interaction designer with more than 3 years of industry experience in designing applications for medium and large scale enterprises. I started my career with PeopleSoft as a Usability Engineer and transitioned to the role of an interaction designer with Oracle Applications User Experience group. I worked upon various domains such as CRM, SCM, Manufacturing, US Govt. Contracts, OnDemand solutions etc. where I worked as lead interaction designer for various mainstream products from PeopleSoft Enterprise, JD Edwards EnterpriseOne, Siebel CRM. I have recently worked as the lead designer for Oracle's first ever Fusion Applications for Sales, which were showcased in Oracle Openworld 2007.

I am also involved in bringing HCI community together through organizing conferences, connecting with academic institutions and student community from various Design Schools in India. I am an active member of ACM SIGCHI, UPA, IxDA and Computer Society of India and visited various HCI conferences including CHI 2006 at Montreal Canada.

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur with Master’s degree in design. My research was focused on interaction over touch screen interfaces and I developed a touch screen based navigation system for IIT Kanpur’s Academic Campus. I also hold a Bachelors degree in Computer Science & Engineering with major in Software Engineering and Communication Systems.

Adesh Singh’s Specialties:

User Centered Design, Interaction Design, Software Usability Analysis, Leadership in Design Implementation, Developing Design Standards Guidelines and Patterns, Consensus Building, Project Planning and Management, Project Negotiations, Design Communication
