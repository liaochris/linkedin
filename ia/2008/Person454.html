Philip Koch’s Summary

From 3D Supervision to Technical/Creative Direction in feature film cinematics. In the past I have held positions such as Director, VFX Sup, Lead 3D Modeler, Lighting TD (PRman & MR), sculptor, texture artist, Previs/layout, Storyboard artist, Concept artist, animator and visual effects consultant.

I also write screenplays from historical fiction to political controversy genres. The films I write and direct often contain or gear towards heavy character development, unpresidented action choreography and seemless CGI integration.
Currently co-directing a featurette: The Human Instrumentality Projecft [H.I.P.] (live action) beging shot on the new RED one camera.

My work can be found in over 400 vfx shots in feature-films alone.
Please view my profile and feel free to contact me direct on the Los Angeles world clock.

Philip Koch’s Specialties:

Making stunning and deeply impressionable cinematic material with particular emphasis on beautiful cinematography and lighting.
