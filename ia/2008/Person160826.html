Riges Younan (JobGenie) & Referral Programs Expert’s Summary

I have over 12 years of experience building and running executive search, staffing and online recruiting businesses. This experience was gained in Sydney, Melbourne, New York and London.

I have a passion for innovative talent sourcing strategies using both on and off-line methodologies. My area of expertise is Employee Referral Programs and I am currently CEO of a Social Recruiting Platform called 2Vouch www.2vouch.com

2Vouch offers employers and recruiters the ability to reach passive candidates who come with personal recommendations. We deliver wide distribution of their job listings through blogs and social networks and provide tools to review and manage referrals. Best of all, clients pay nothing until they hire or place someone.

For professionals, the offer is cash rewards (and enhanced social capital and network standing) for referring people they know and recommend to job opportunities. We allow members to automatically track their referrals and payments, manage their contacts, show interest in positions and apply directly if they wish.
