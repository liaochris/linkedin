Sylvain Copon’s Summary

Hello and thank you to take time to read my profile.

I’m a French-Californian professional and well known artist living in Los Angeles, California. I own a fine art gallery in the heart of Beverly Hills (across from the Peninsula Hotel). I consult on decorating homes, offices, stores and other corporate settings (past experience includes hospitals, hotels and casinos, government buildings and offices).

I am a color combination specialist with master skills in any style and art form. From standard colors to gold leaf and patina, faux finish and the “new” and rare leather motif patina wall (end of XVIII Style), direct from Paris. I can create the perfect sky on the ceiling and copy any master painter. Include me in the start of your new construction or for improvements to your existing structures.

I have direct contacts for antique paintings, rare objects, rugs, furniture from France, Italy and Europe. My professional team includes authentic specialists who can complete the job fast and to perfection, exactly as you want it, and on budget.

If your project requires travel, I am ready to go anytime and anywhere with short notice. You will find that I am full of energy and bring a refreshing perspective to every situation. With over 30 years of being a professional artist and businessman, I will bring you new ideas and innovations. I’m unique and my clients will tell you that I always follow through.

If you need the perfect, one-source artist, please contact me. No job is too big or too small. I chose to build my network on LinkedIn because I value the strong relationships I have with my customers and my friends, and because no successful community can live without art and beauty. So how will you be decorating your new home and office?

Email me to discuss your new plans. I can make you dream and vision come true.
