Wojciech Rossa’s Summary

First area that I take care of is an on-going project of virtualizing IT infrastructure mostly as a backup specialist. Knowing Veritas backup software I am responsible for whole backup environment deployment from scratch. This includes conferences with clients to discover the needs, then designing the solution, preparing media management routines for local IT and ultimately - deployment. When later the service is handed over to an external IT contractor I'm the first point of contact in case of disasters, breakdowns and any other cases needing troubleshooting. As a professional I'm heading to the direction where I could architect, desing and engineer IT solutions on a Enterprise scale.
