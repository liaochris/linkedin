James Bland’s Summary

James Bland is a senior business administration student from Titusville, Florida currently serving as the Student Body Vice-President at Florida A&M University. Since his arrival at FAMU in the fall of 2004, James has been actively involved on campus serving as Freshman and Sophomore Class Vice-President, President of the Campus Activities Board and a member of the Beta Nu Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. In addition, James has served as a volunteer for the 2006 BET Hip Hop Awards and a marketing rep for Universal Records.

Upon graduation, James plans to pursue a career in the film and entertainment industry with the goal of becoming an independent filmmaker. James has written and directed his very own short film entitled "Dreaming in Color," and is also a writer/producer/actor for the smart and sexy independent television drama series "My Brother's Keeper."

James ultimate goal in life is to fulfill the purpose God has for him. He believes that in life a man plans his course, but Godorders his step, and he truly feels that his step are ordered by God.
