Ali Goler ’s Summary

Accomplished Executive Staffing Specialist with more than 5 years of demonstrated expertise in strategic staffing. Possess a strong knowledge of the online performance based internet advertising and marketing industry.

As a privately held firm, the Kevin Mitchell Group is able to dedicate our resources to what really matters -- providing outstanding service for our clients and candidates. Our experienced management team and consultants have over 30 years of experience working with and for fortune 500 companies.

We offer our clients advice on their management needs, conducting executive search assignments across a range of industries and functions, always in the spirit of partnership. At the commencement of each assignment we assemble the best team, comprising experienced recruiters and in-house research associates, in order to bring both sector and functional expertise to the search.

Kevin Mitchell Group offers a collaborative approach to executive search that is based on a thorough understanding of the strategic, financial and operational issues our clients face. Our proven processes and comprehensive analyses help ensure that no stone is left unturned during each search, with the ultimate goal of placing the best candidates for each of our clients requirements. We care about our business and this resonates throughout our culture and business practices.
