Mark Jacobson’s Summary

I began my career with Accenture Technology Labs, turning technology innovation into business results for major corporations and startups in the US, Europe and South America.

Specializing in CRM and targeted marketing, I created the first Web-accessible customer service center, the first personalized marketing solution in the financial services industry and a patent-pending solution for personalized television advertising -- along with innovative products in imaging, document management/workflow and advanced analytics.

After nearly ten years, I joined Amazon.com's affiliate marketing group, where I crafted business and product strategies to help Amazon's affiliate program compete more effectively for Web publishers' ad inventory. The products I delivered and/or designed include:
- Product Previews (top layer advertising)
- Omakase Links (display ads that show products visitors are most likely to buy)
- Context Links (automatically link commercial phrases within Web page content)
- aStore (an online store that can be created without programming)

When the chance arose to deepen my expertise at Microsoft Advertiser & Publisher Solutions (APS), I jumped at the opportunity. During my first 18 months in APS, I managed APS' ad targeting platform, delivering behavioral targeting, re-targeting and new demographic and geographic targeting capabilities which nearly doubled Microsoft's revenue from targeted advertising on owned and operated inventory.

As part of delivering that platform, I evaluated building, acquiring and licensing technology. It was that process which drove my current focus - managing APS' mergers, acquisitions and commercial deals. In this role, I develop APS' M&A strategy, manage our acquisition pipeline, and architect both large and small deals.

Mark Jacobson’s Specialties:

Digital advertising, business and product strategy, product management, mergers & acquisitions, yield optimization, targeted advertising
