John Taylor’s Summary

I'm an award-winning corporate communications professional with both in-house counsel and agency experience working for and with large corporations and small businesses. Unlike most of my peers in communications, I am a businessman first and a communications professional second. Because of this business orientation, my communications programs are measureable, driven by the bottom line and customer-focused.

Before working in corporate communications, I served in a series of staff positions which supported both appointed and elected officials in all levels of government. This work -- for two U.S. Cabinet Secretaries, a U.S. Congressman, a state Governor, a state Speaker of the House, and a major state political party -- informs my work in public affairs. Because I understand how public policy is made and how the political process is influenced by news coverage, I bring a depth to my media relations skills that others lack.

John Taylor’s Specialties:

Expert in crisis communications, public affairs, internal communications, media relations and communications planning. Strong ability to lead cross-functional teams to deliver communications programs that align media relations and internal communications efforts to achieve business goals. On the record spokesperson for telecommunications, financial services, government, education and non-profit clients. Significant experience in providing communications counseling to Fortune 500 executives.
