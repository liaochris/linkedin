Vod Kenton Hatch CPT, FMS, M.Ed.’s Summary

I have been running my personal training business since 2004.
My clients tend to be actors in film and stage or highly-stressed executives. My goals are simple: To be busy, effective and prosperous. I want to train anyone who is 100 per cent committed to changing their body. Please note: My rates range from 50 to 150 dollars a session and I require a three month committment, paid in advance, on your part. Clearly, I am only interested in serious clients.
