Bob Dececco, CPA’s Experience

  • Co-Founder, President and COO

    Typed-In LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    July 2007 — Present (9 months)

    Typed-In, is a domain name aggregator focused on helping businesses increase sales through the acquisition and development of geo-targeted domains (URLs). These domains, once acquired by Typed-In, are subsequently leased to businesses of all types and sizes (e.g., Car Dealers, Attorneys, Plumbers, Lenders, Dentists, etc).

    A geo-targeted domain name answers the question of what (product/service) & where (location); for example, consumers searching on the internet for a particular product or service in their local market may type the following in their browser; plumberatlanta.com, newcartampa.com, insurancemiami.com, etc.

    Consumers searching geo-targeted domains are a high quality lead for a business, typically converting 250% more often than conventional leads. These domains provide businesses the opportunity to increase brand awareness while driving high quality traffic for a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising while lowering their overall cost per lead.
