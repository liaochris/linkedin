Lisa Murphy’s Summary

Whether you’re looking for strategic insights on reaching new audiences via print or web, or simply need lively, well researched content on deadline, Lisa Murphy delivers. A leading Canadian editor, writer and brand strategist, Lisa is known for her successes as a former Chatelaine and Chatelaine.com editor, Canadian Family editor-in-chief and public-relations account manager. In her consultant capacity, she helped with the launch and relaunch of successful products such as Best Health magazine and besthealthmag.ca, Kaboose.com and more. As such, she offers unique ideas and proven results for media, brands, magazines and online sites and networks.

Lisa’s written work has appeared in Chatelaine, The Globe and Mail, Reader’s Digest, Canadian House & Home, Best Health, Today’s Parent, More, THIS Magazine, Canadian Living, Kaboose.com and other popular web sites. Topics covered range from health and lifestyle, décor, beauty and parenting to environmental issues, science and marketing. Lisa has also appeared in countless TV and radio interviews on media outlets such as Canada AM, EZ Rock, 680 News, CityTV, CBC radio and CHTV Morning.
