James Blake’s Summary

I am the Chief Product Strategist for Mimecast. I have responsibility for providing subject matter expertise with regards corporate governance, risk mitigation and legislative compliance solutions based around security, continuity and storage technology. I am also responsible for shaping strategic product direction and evangelising the Mimecast service.

Prior to joining Mimecast, I was responsible for establishing Proofpoint's beachhead technical operations in EMEA, including the recruitment of a sales team and managing channel relationships.

Before Proofpoint I was a senior sales engineer covering Northern Europe for netForensics, the World's leading provider of Security Incident and Process Management solutions. Before that I ran technical operations within the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland for TippingPoint, who now form the security business unit of 3Com.

Further roles have include Pre-sales Manager for Vtesse Networks, where I worked on several large-scale projects, including securing a £500M 5 year contract for a secure wide-area network for Lloyds TSB; Services Product Manager for a pharmaceutical services company and as the Business Development Director of a successful security software distributorship.
