Kevin W. Massengill’s Summary

We assist clients and partners to pursue opportunities in the CIS, Indian Subcontinent, Middle East, North Africa, and the United States. We provide companies with value added partners, experienced board members, talented management and capital.

Our team members have ran energy funds across Asia, successfully engineered over 70 mergers and acquisitions for Fortune 500 and regional companies in India, successfully implemented market entry strategies from single country to multi-continental rollouts and we are currently mandated to raise almost $5 billion for energy, industrial and infrastructure projects from North America to Asia.

Despite the global credit crunch, we have access to an estimated $750 billion in capital looking for Sharia compliant investments. If you have an industrial expansion, oil & gas infrastructure, real estate or other fixed asset project, we would be pleased to evaluate whether we can structure a hybrid financial package that can reduce your costs and get your project funded within 90 days.
