Audra Eng’s Summary

Audra Eng is the Vice President of Product Management at Raytheon Oakley Systems and manages release planning, roadmap priorities and direction for Raytheon Oakley Systems Insider Threat Management product suite.

Audra has been in the security software industry for 10 years, with her prior position as the Director of Product Management for Scansafe, Inc. responsible for product release planning for ScanSafe's leading web filtering managed services.

Audra's past roles also include being Director of Product Management for ArcSight, Inc. responsible for managing all components and product lines for ArcSight, including product release management, product market research, customer beta programs, and roadmap strategy. ArcSight is the globally recognized market leader in Enterprise Security Management solutions. Prior to ArcSight, Audra was Vice President of Product Management for Sygate (acquired by Symantec, Inc.), a market leader in Enterprise Endpoint Security, and Director of Product Management for Internet Security Systems, Inc. (ISS), the first leader in the security software industry. In these roles, Audra set strategy and managed teams including the intrusion detection and vulnerability assessment products for protecting desktops, servers and networks. Prior to ISS, Audra held senior information systems consultant positions with Sprint Paranet and 3M.

Audra has a Bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems from the University of Texas, Austin.
