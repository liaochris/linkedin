Yann LEBRUN’s Summary

My main tasks as Business analyst - Project coordinator, are to manage different projects from Development to Production, in accordance with the technologies, the planning, the quality and the budget.

In one word, it is a Team Work in order to achieve given goals and to answer the Business Needs of the clients :

- Business & Functional Analysis to meet the client's needs
- working as a link between the client and IT
- having contacts with all hierarchical levels ( from users up to CEO's)
- fix the structure of the projects in collaboration with the project manager
- facilitate communication between the different actors of the projects
- Respect Project Charter and Requirements
- Reporting and life cycle
- Respect Privacy and social security law (linked to my sector)
- Priority planning, capacity planning, financial boards
- ...

My goal for the future is to continue acquiring experience in managing different projects with different technologies and people in order to be able to easily adapt to any new situations.
