Ed Juline’s Summary

My manufacturing leadership and expertise spans a diverse range of industries and is international in scope. I am well-versed in the latest manufacturing methodologies such as the Toyota Production System and Six Sigma. I’m seeking a position as General Manager or VP Manufacturing in virtually any industry, based on a background that encompasses automotive, plastics, paint, high-tech and railcars. I am entrepreneurial and effective in a multicultural environment and am fluent in Spanish. Selected achievements include:

• Turned around a $50MM Mexican manufacturing plant for Nypro, reversed a 5% loss to a 1% profit in 3 months and delivered an 8% profit within 16 months.

• Led the relocation of a $350+ million plant from the US to Mexico for IBM in just seven months, well under the one-year target.

• Coordinated between the engineering and marketing departments for the world’s largest railroad industry equipment and services provider to develop new products, which opened untapped markets and generated much new business.

• Managed multiple lean manufacturing vendor engagements as a consultant to Boeing; delivered major cost and cycle time reductions and enhanced quality and floor space utilization.
