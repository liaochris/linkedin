Robert Miller’s Summary

Attorney with substantial experience representing California companies in the professional services, distribution, logistics, defense, electronics, construction and manufacturing industries.

Recipient and continuous holder since 1990 of an “AV” peer review rating (the highest possible attorney rating) from the Martindale-Hubbell Law Directory.

Robert Miller’s Specialties:

Negotiating and closing a wide variety of business contracts.

Counseling privately owned California businesses from start up through sale.
