Luke Stedman’s Summary

I am currently working as a Knowledge Database Developer and System Analyst at Tideway Systems, Tideway are one of the leading players, and the only independant one, in the service configuration management arena.

My role involves analysing Enterprise Software products from client systems, identifying the key components of these packages, from this I derive the required rules to ensure that future identification of these products is possible and create logical structuring information to present it in an understandable manner.

In addition I work on various internal tools and databases to assist in the development of the core Knowledge Database and the functions and processes of the Knowledge development team.

Previously I worked as the UK Data Manager and Extract Analyst for BDS Ltd, owned by Red Bee Media, where I was responsible for the data extraction and delivery of schedule metadata for all UK clients.

As a manager within the delivery team I was primary contact for a number of major platforms and projects including Telewest, NTL, Sky, O2, Three, HomeChoice, Chorus, Arquiva, the BBC, ITV, CNBC, Top-Up TV, News International, Timeout and the Express group.
