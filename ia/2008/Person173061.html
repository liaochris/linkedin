Sergio Palumberi’s Summary

I am working in the new Italian Bioregenerative Life Support Program (CAB), developing a system that will allow the regeneration of resources and the production of food for life support in long duration missions.
I am also involved in a Thales Alenia Space internal research project (Recyclab), developing both biological and physico-chemical technologies for regeneration of Water, Air and Food.

In parallel I am a member of the Engineering Support Team for the ECLSS of the european module Columbus of the International Space Station.
