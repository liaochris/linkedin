Edward Vielmetti’s Summary

I've been doing Internet related work since the mid 1980s from Ann Arbor, MI.
I organize a lunch which fills a restaurant every week whether I'm there or not.
I'm a blogger, and have been since the late 1990s.
I own page one of Google search results for my zip code, 48104.
I was online when the Morris Worm hit.
Tim Berners-Lee found a page that I wrote when he first surfed the web.

I have some simple goals.
I'd like to walk 10000 steps a day.
I never want to have lunch alone.
I'd like to hear about your new software for organizing groups together to take collective action.
