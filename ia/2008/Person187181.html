Thayer Driver’s Summary

I've been working in online since 2001, starting as a programmer at UpMyStreet.com and then moving into commercial and community based roles.

I have a varied background in statistics, web development and programming, recruitment and company development, commercialising and producing ideas, and a vast understanding of the social web and gaming.

My main interests currently lie in social media, gaming, entrepreneurial web and web 2.0.

In 2004 I won an award for my recruitment services courtesy of LloydsTSB Cash Friday.
http://www.cashfriday.co.uk/new_media_recruitment.html

In 2005 I co-founded Chinwag Jobs alongside Sam Michel - the UK's largest and oldest new media community's jobs board.
http://jobs.chinwag.com

in 2007 I was published in the Independent's Guide to Gaming.
http://www.independentezines.co.uk/theguide/games/?directlink=8

in 2008 Chinwag Jobs has been nominated as a finalist for technical innovation at the Onrec Awards in March 2008
http://www.onrec.com/conferences/050308A/finalists.html

I now consult across my multiple interests with a sway towards social and community web, and gaming (as in video games, not gambling).
