John Beisser’s Summary

Since August, 2007, I have served in the capacity of Assistant Athletic Director of Media Relations at Wagner College, located in Staten Island, NY whose sports teams compete on the Division I level.

In this capacity, I manage and direct the sports information department in support of the entire 22-varsity sport varsity program, while serving as the chief media contact for the Seahawk football and men's basketball programs.

A former sports director of the Rutgers campus radio station during my undergraduate days, I also serve as the primary play-by-play announcer for the Seahawk Broadcasting Network.

Prior to coming on board at Wagner, served at Rutgers in the Office of Athletic Communications for 15 years (1991-2006) before embarking on a brief stint as an Associate Editor at GOLF Magazine in New York City.

I began my career as a sportswriter for The Princeton Packet Group of Publications in Princeton, NJ where won five New Jersey Press Association Awards for writing excellence.

During my long tenure at Rutgers, from where I earned a B.S. degree in Marketing/Journalism and Mass Media in 1986, I was the managing editor of eight publications that received citations by the College Sports Information Directors of America (CoSIDA). My 1992 men's soccer poster was judged "Second in the Nation," trailing only the University of Florida's football poster that year.
