john papp’s Summary

John grew up in Greenbelt, Maryland and attended Gonzaga College High School in Washington, DC. He has a passion for sports, especially baseball and his beloved Baltimore Orioles. Despite the fact that he no longer entertains dreams of playing professional ball, John would love to have a career in sports management, particuarly in professional baseball.

John first got a taste of working in sports when he began working for the Bowie Baysox, the AA affiliate of the Baltimore Orioles, at age 14. Working as a clubhouse assistant and batboy, John enjoyed the commraderie of the team and having the opportunity to learn more about the organizational aspects of professional baseball.

After his time with the Bowie Baysox, John was fortunate to be able to work for the Baltimore Orioles in their Communications Department as a Promotions Crewmember. This job entailed assisting with pregame onfield ceramonies as well as coordinating ingame promotions and ball park activities. John also had the chance to work special Oriole's events such Orioles Fanfest and their summer concert series.

During the summer of 2006, John shifted gears and took an internship with United Cerebral Palsy, a non-profit disabilities advocacy organization in Washington, DC. Working as a Marketing Intern for UCP, John helped publish articles and "Action Alerts" for the UCP website as well as assisting with on-site UCP sponsored events at hospitals etc.

From September 2006 to May 2007 John held an Sales & Marketing Services/ Business Development Internship with IMG, the world leader in Sports Marketing. The internship focused on creating powerpoint presentations (decks) for potential IMG clients and drafting proposals for potential new IMG clients.

In his free time John loves to work out, go to the beach, camp and hike, and do community service as well as support the Fordham University Basketball Rams as Sixth Man Club President.
