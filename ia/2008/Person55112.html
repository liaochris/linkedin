Duane Storey’s Summary

I have a very broad background in electrical and computer engineering, and have excelled along those lines with every professional job I have been hired for. I have had manufacturing roles in leading fiber optics companies, as well as experience developing a large portion of a voice over IP engine. I have a master's of electrical and computer engineering, with a specialty in advanced video codecs and compression. In addition to my day job, I always continually upgrade my own skills -- this has helped me to become a respected blogger in the Vancouver area, and a well-known Wordpress plugin developer.

I love challenges, and have routinely exceeded expecations at all formal performance reviews I have ever had. I'm looking for challenging positions that also provide ample opportunities to have fun.
