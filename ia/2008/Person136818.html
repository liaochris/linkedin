    March 2005 — April 2007 (2 years 2 months)

Under my management, the company increased in gross profits from $1.3M to $2.5M and grew from eight to eleven stores. My primary responsibilities included overseeing a staff of 40, managing daily operations, profit and loss, recruiting and hiring store managers, overseeing accounting and collections operations and personnel, cash inventory management, banking relationship management, FINCEN and IRS Title 31 compliance, Franchisor relations and all other necessary operational responsibilities. Significant accomplishments include ACE Cash Express Franchise of the Year 2005, implementing employee training program and positioning the company for sale back to the Franchisor in April 2007.
