Pete Simon’s Summary

Graduate work ( MS ) in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University in Chicago, work towards a doctorate in Cultural Anthropology with an emphasis on the factors affecting the development of online communities.

Former Director of Social Network Services at Sway, Inc. I was charged with developing opportunities and shaping policy relevant to the emerging phenomenon of social network websites. This included evaluation of the existing social space(s) of an organization or company, recommendations for future directions for growth and emphasis, as well as some amount of education and public relations regarding these areas.

At the moment I am developing a small social media marketing firm specializing in optimizing an organization's voice for social media, in all my copious spare time.

Stay tuned.

Pete Simon’s Specialties:

- Developing online social network spaces
- Analysis of existing spaces for productivity, liability, and growth potential
- Analysis and strategic planning for new social spaces based on organizational goals
- Usability of social network sites
- Partnering with organizations to help them develop a "human voice" through blogs and other social media.
- Utilization of "social marketing" and buzz marketing tactics to increase brand awareness and solidify brand allegiance
