Ryan Barrett’s Summary

I'm a copywriter at Digitas, one of the largest interactive advertising agency in Boston. Not only is writing what I do, but it's also my passion. I am always working to perfect my craft, whether it's online through blogging or on a pad and paper with ink and my imagination.

I recently worked on a very cool project called The Age of Conversation. 103 bloggers wrote one-page chapters on conversation in 2007. The result: a 103-page conglomeration of fresh ideas. A book blog.

I also love to write short, witty pieces on the side for fun.
