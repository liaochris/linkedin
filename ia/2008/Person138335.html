Naren Jauhal’s Summary

My personal goal is to be a facilitator of positive change and prosperity for the clients and companies that engages my services. I define prosperity as a life that has much financial success, fulfillment and happiness. Such a life is lived with integrity, truth and respect for fellow beings while using one’s creative passion and imagination. I believe that when people fully apply their creativity in cooperative team environment to serve market and customer needs, they can achieve unparalleled success and produce long term competitive advantage for their companies.

Naren has over 25 years of management experience covering consulting, marketing, project management, market research, strategic planning, business development and financial control areas. He has worked directly with CEOs, Division Managers and senior executive teams in a wide range of industries including Aerospace, Environmental Engineering, Semiconductors and Wireless Communications.

At Business Prosperity Solutions, Inc., Our mission is to inspire you to transform your company into a healthy body. Within this healthy body, all areas work together as a community and all employees are inspired to discover and harness their SOUL power.

We define SOUL as:
S Self-leadership and motivation
O Ownership of responsibility to self, job and community
U Unity of personal vision with the company vision
L Loving what you do for a living

I am ready to help companies make quantum leaps and operate at a different level. Please contact me at +1-949-751-8870
