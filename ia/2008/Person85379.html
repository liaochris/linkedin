Jasper D. Steffens’s Experience

  • Managing Director/ CEO

    A2SP

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Automotive industry)

    February 2008 — Present (1 year)

    A2SP is an entrepreneurial business to business supplier of automotive related data, statistics and services for the automotive- insurance and finance companies. Being a high-tech, ambitious and dynamic organisation we strive for innovative and high quality data and services for our customers.

    A2SP offers vehicle information database services for the full Dutch vehicle market. These services can be extend by offering commercial and technical information. The information can be fully adapted to specific customer requirements and is linked to the Dutch vehicle registration. A2SP successfully started an online consumer portal www.autoafspraak.nl to offer consumers the possibility to online book an appointment for vehicle inspection and/ or services.

    Our main competitors on a specific segment of our product B-to-B portfolio are RDC and VWE. We are VAR (value added reseller) of JATO Dynamics data in the Dutch market.

  • Founder

    Donkervoort.info

    (Automotive industry)

    January 1999 — Present (10 years 1 month)

    www.donkervoort.info is a passionate independent website and information publisher about the unique Dutch Donkervoort sports car. Donkervoort.info wishes to unite Donkervoort drivers throughout Europe, inform about new developments, share and preserve as much information as possible for the current owners and Donkervoort ‘dreamers’.

    Donkervoort.info is autodidactically developed in Adobe Dreamweaver plus NVU and helps me to understand and experiment with web based technology in a hands-on manner.

Customers are car importers, large dealer holdings and/ or automotive groups.

  • International Marketing & Sales Manager

    Olyslager Organisation B.V.

    (Partnership; 51-200 employees; Automotive industry)

    1988 — 1999 (11 years)

    Olyslager Organisation is an extremely famous Automotive B-to-B technical agency with a multimedia product portfolio of services for the industry. The company translates and produces technical manuals and press related material for many car manufactures and importers. It also developed and markets it’s own technical workshop information system on CD-Rom and web based services.

    Olyslager is the world’s largest supplier of technical OEM specifications to the oil industry (Lubricant Information System). As International Marketing and Sales Manager I was responsible for the expansion into Europe of this unique company, the PR and product development. The company has been purchased by Eurotax AG. The name Olyslager has been bought by a Dutch ex colleague who has bought the rights to use the name Olyslager in the future.
