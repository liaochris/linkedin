Tony Law’s Summary

A senior professional Information Technologist, a technology specialist with wide knowledge of both core and leading edge information technologies, and bringing business experience gained through over 20 years in major British-based global companies.

I came to IT first as a user and then as a professional in a major UK University computing service. A focus on real user needs has continued to inform my career in industry.

In British Petroleum I worked first in database development and data management, and then in collaborative IT research specialising in management architectures for distributed systems. In SmithKline Beecham and its successor, GlaxoSmithKline, I have become recognised as a champion and researcher of industry trends in IT, of emerging technologies, and of the application of this knowledge to the needs of the business.

I create, work with, and learn through networks: both within the enterprise and across industry and organisational boundaries. This has been the key to success throughout his career - and makes LinkedIn a natural professional tool.
