Troy Allen’s Summary

Senior IT Executive with over 15 years in the information technology field who is just as comfortable in the “War Room” with the Developers and Engineers as he is in the Board Room with the Executives. A true leader who is just as capable in architecting enterprise-grade infrastructure systems as he is in leading the development of new and custom applications throughout the SDLC.
