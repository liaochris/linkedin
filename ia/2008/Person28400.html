Bruce Henderson’s Summary

Over eighteen years of experience in technical management, software design and engineering on the J2EE, Unix and NeXTStep systems. Experience applying distributed enterprise architecture, object oriented design, engineering and programming to Military, Intelligence, Telecommunications and Finance domains. Developer of systems in Java, Enterprise Java Beans, Web Logic, Objective-C, Enterprise Portals, XML, HTML, C and C++ systems. Eight years experience in team leadership, technical management and resource development.

Bruce Henderson’s Specialties:
