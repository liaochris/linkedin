Becky Bertram’s Summary

I am a software developer who has been consulting for over 9 years. Many of my assignments have primarily focused on helping clients implement web-based content management solutions using Microsoft SharePoint and/or Content Management Server. I have worked with such well-known clients as T.G.I.Friday's, IBC Bank, Amway, Verizon, Washington Mutual, Conoco-Phillips, the U.S. Navy, Clear Channel Communications, and Countrywide Financial Corportation, and have contracted through high-profile companies such as Microsoft, HP and Dell Professional Services. My goal is to provide my clients with top-notch quality work, drawing upon my experiences at many different organizations and my many projects.

Becky Bertram’s Specialties:

Microsoft Office SharePoint Server 2007 (MOSS 2007), Windows SharePoint Services 3.0 (WSS 3.0), SharePoint Portal Server 2003, Windows SharePoint Services, Content Mangement Server 2002, MS CMS 2001, C#, VB.NET, ASP.NET, SQL Server, XML, XSL, COM+, HTML, Photoshop
