David Gonda’s Summary

Accomplished informaticist and scientist, a former Ivy League professor, with over ten years professional experience in:

* Data analysis and data mining
* Data modeling, design and programming
* Software design and architecture
* Software development (object-oriented and scripting languages)
* Informatics project management under both traditional and agile methodologies
* Scientific systems management

Partners effectively with peers and stakeholders at all levels; regarded by all as an outstanding colleague, mentor, and leader.

Driven to tackle complex and significant problems using a variety of approaches and uncommon creativity. Learns and applies new subject matter areas and technologies quickly. A creative problem solver who can consistently deliver innovative solutions to business and scientific problems.
