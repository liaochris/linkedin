Jos Schuurmans’s Summary

Profile: visionary, entrepreneurial, participatory media strategist and seasoned journalist.

I aspire to work within an international setting, be it corporate or not-for-profit, with energetic and open-minded people who subscribe to the highest standards of ethical professional conduct, and who passionately push the boundaries of participatory media concepts, while adding measurable value to solid strategic objectives, based on a clear and sustainable shared vision.

Jos Schuurmans’s Specialties:

Organizational vision and communications strategy alignment; participatory media concept, process and tool development; conversational content creation; competence transfer.
