Dan Limbach’s Summary

I have always loved using technology to tell stories and convey unique and complex concepts. In most cases, online and multimedia technologies are better, faster and cheaper than many traditional methods. Specifically, emarketing is the most flexible and dynamic promotional medium available. These tools are excellent complements to offline initiatives, such as direct marketing, trade shows, print, and other offline advertising.

My passion is to develop innovative marketing solutions using the most effective technologies of the day - sometimes in ways it has never been used before. I love to collaborate with coworkers and outside resources.
