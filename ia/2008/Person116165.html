Selso Luanava’s Experience

  • Founder

    Luanava Engineering LLC (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Medical Devices industry)

    December 2004 — Present (3 years 4 months)

    I started this company to fill an unmet need in the startup medical industry. Many small medical companies have a great idea but do not have the infrastructure or knowledge to get to a working product. I take their lab prototype and turn it into something they can take on the road to show to prospective investors or customers

    Some of the projects I've worked on are listed below:

    1. Prototyped DLP based Surface Plasmon Resonance Microscope. Optimized design to allow for multiple fluidics configurations and ease of specimen handling and removal. Interfaced with vendors/consultants.

    2. Designed DNA Chip Incubator, allows storing/shipping DNA samples during chemical binding. Used to show concept to investors.

  • Senior Mechanical Engineer

    Verathon Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Medical Devices industry)

    May 2006 — December 2007 (1 year 8 months)

    Company was formally called Diagnostic Ultrasound.

    Lead mechanical design for new Ultrasound technology platform. Developed novel probe and console configuration in 12 months. Responsible for detailed designs and provided significant contribution/direction through pilot processes, process development, and qualification/validation activities.

    Instituted significant redesign to existing transducer actuation method. Significantly improved aiming accuracy, decreased field failures, and addressed obsolescence of components. Novel design able to incorporate preceding and current transducer designs.

  • Senior Staff Engineer

    Microvision Inc

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; MVIS; Defense & Space industry)

    August 1998 — May 2006 (7 years 10 months)

    Position consisted mostly of research and development of new products. Initially worked on the MEMS scanner then transitioned into incorporating the scanner into systems.
    Have worked on the following products/programs:
    Heads up displays for military jets.
    Wearable displays (eyeglasses with scanned light sources onto the retina) for the consumer, military and industrial markets.
    Laser camera (instead of just sending photons out we also collected the light).
    Miniature Endoscope. (3-5 mm OD)
    1-D and 2-D Barcode readers.
