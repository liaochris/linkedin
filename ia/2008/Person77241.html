Martin Calle’s Summary

Martin Calle is an expert in business management, business transformation, performance improvement, knowledge management, knowledge creation, marketing disruption, creativity, innovation and idea management, problem-solving, marketing, advertising, branding, new product concept development and positioning.

Calle & Company enables clients to create Perceptual Monopolies (#1 Positioning) with thought-leadership processes employed to invent, and reinvent, the world's most powerful consumer products, brands and categories. Calle & Company is the consumer packaged good industry's leading innovation management, knowledge creation and business transformation company specializing in new product concept development, new segmentation and category creation, breakthrough new product positioning, revolutionary product, packaging and delivery system design. Contact Martin Calle to outsource services that:

1) reverse the effects of mature product lifecycles,
2) turn mature earning's brands back into rapid growth businesses,
3) find the world's most powerful new consumer wants, needs and ideas.

Innovation, clean sheets of paper and working from scratch are top priorities at this innovation think tank.

I think of myself as basically a new products brand manager. A new products brand manager is a brand manager without a brand. I have to create organic new top-line brands or categories to keep my job, then I turn them over to someone else to manage. I am good at it. Tylenol Gelcaps, Baked Lays Potato Chips, Cold-filtered Miller Genuine Draft and other brands you use daily products of mine. Resurrecting declining brands including Pampers, Wheaties, Folgers are just some of the brands I've turned around. For more information you can visit my blog, MADISON AVENUE at http://advertising-age.blogspot.com, or my website; www.CalleCompany.com
