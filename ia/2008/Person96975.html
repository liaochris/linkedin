Joost Van De Loo’s Summary

I help brands to grow through meaningful action.
Which is a fancy way of saying that my job is not about pushing messages.
Brands should DO things, have a conviction and invite people to join in.

People should dream, they say. Well my dream is to, some day, be invited to speak at TED - the truly wonderful conference sponsored by BMW, with inspirational 20 min. talks by the world's greatest minds and storytellers; http://www.ted.com.

But for now, let's do some things!
