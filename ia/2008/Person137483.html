Andreina Urdaneta’s Summary

I like to think of me as a Talent Acquisition Specialist. I provide full life-cycle recruiting support for the Talent Acquisition team of many fortune 500 companies.
My Job is to manage all aspects of recruiting by partnering with the Talent Acquisition team and client groups to develop and implement comprehensive recruitment and staffing strategies to hire the right people into the right jobs while ensuring legal, social and company standards are met and exceeded.

I love to create and take a proactive approach to meet staffing requirements to include: attracting, sourcing & screening applicants using a variety of innovative sourcing techniques while staying abreast of developing market conditions & trends.

This includes: Identification of competitive companies; key skills needed within the business, and design of proactive sourcing strategies to fulfill client & candidate requests. Development of structured and repeatable candidate pipeline strategies and organization and consolidation of data sourcing tools used throughout the organization. Partnership with other Human Resources business functions to ensure effective succession planning and development of internal talent pipelines.

In addition, I provide a high level of customer service and one on one coaching to potential candidates, hiring managers and talent acquisition team members, and will serve as an effective liaison for the Talent Acquisition Department with special emphasis placed on candidate and client group experience.
