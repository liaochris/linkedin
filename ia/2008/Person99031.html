Jean-Samuel Chenard’s Summary

Researcher in FPGA/ASIC implementation of flexible data processing structures for high-speed digital data processing using de-centralized processing or multi-core architectures.

Jean-Samuel Chenard’s Specialties:

VLSI, cell-based ASIC and FPGA Digital Design
SONET/SDH Telecommunications (VT/VC cross-connect, LCAS)
High-level system modeling and verification of SONET/SDH telecommunication networks.
Digital verification with Specman Elite, Modelsim and SystemC.
Low-power wireless embedded systems/sensor networks.
Linux-based embedded systems on FPGA platforms (PowerPC and ARM), kernel device drivers.
