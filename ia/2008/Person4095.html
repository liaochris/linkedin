Agustin Vinelli’s Summary

Agustin started his career in Procurement in a FMCG multinational company, after making his first experiences in sales of customized software with a middle-size German IT firm. After thsi, he worked as lead buyer with the #1 aluminum producer. His past responsibilities ranged from the management of large sourcing projects and category lead, to managing consulting projects and leading a procurement team.

Currently he’s working for the leading manufacturer of blades for wind turbines.

Agustin grew up in Argentina, studied in Buenos Aires, Cologne, and Barcelona, and lived in Rome and Zurich. He’s fluent in English, German, Spanish, and Italian; and is learning French.

He graduated in Business Administration at the Cologne University and holds an MBA from IESE Business School.
