Dean Nelson’s Summary

Senior Director of Global Lab & Datacenter Design Services (GDS)

Responsibilities:
- Manage 1.3 million SF technical infrastructure portfoilo.
- Manage world-wide team responsible for scope, standards based design, and implementation of efficient, future-proof lab and Data Center environments.
- Manage $250M+ in datacenter projects including two of the largest & most complex datacenter consolidations projects in Sun's history.
- Implementation of Sun's ECO strategy in the technical infrastructure portfolio.
- Engineering feedback loop on implications of product design decisions in datacenters.
- R&D arm of new Sun ECO Virtualization Services Practice that includes datacenter design based on GDS best practices and processes.

GDS bridges the gap between Facilities and IT to enable operational efficiencies that contribute to Sun's economical and ecological goals.

Dean Nelson’s Specialties:

ECO/energy efficient Data Center Design, Engineering Lab Physical and Technical Infrastructure, Network Architecture & Administration, Unix System Administration, Sun HW/SW, Quality Test Engineering
