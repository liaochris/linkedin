I'm a WEB DESIGNER AND DEVELOPER with 13 years of experience. I build STANDARDS-BASED WEBSITES for small and medium businesses, and my goal is to have my clients take as much advantage as possible of the powerful business tool any website can become with care and attention.

I started my web studio RED KITE CREATIVE in 2005 and receive about 90% of my business through REFERRALS FROM SATISFIED CLIENTS. I talk to clients using PLAIN ENGLISH, not geek-speak. I am an XHTML/CSS expert and expert PHOTOSHOP user, too.

I build beautiful sites to web standards – clean code, TABLELESS CSS WEBSITES, cross-browser tested, and accessible.

I ran an online retail business for 9 years, so I have the FIRST-HAND ECOMMERCE and WEB MARKETING KNOWLEDGE that many designers lack. I work with SMALL TO MEDIUM-SIZED BUSINESSES and create new websites and redesign older ones. A successful client is the best testimonial I can ask for.
