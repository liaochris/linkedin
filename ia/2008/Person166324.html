Robert Womack’s Summary

Experienced in managing many phases of a financial firm’s operations, including direct oversight of trading and investment operations for both the securities lending and asset management divisions for MetWest Securities from 2004 to 2006.

Oversaw the integration of strategic acquisitions for MetWest Securities including the securities lending divisions of Merrill Lynch totaling $7.0 billion in 1998 and Cantor Fitzgerald totaling $1.8 billion in 2001.

Member of strategic development team responsible for the creation of Investment Trusts for CalTRUST and the California State University totaling more than $2.3 billion as of Q3 2007.

Analyzed and negotiated transactions including public and private debt and equity financings, mergers, acquisitions and leveraged buyouts for Atlas Holdings Group from 1996-1997. (Industries including Public Transportation, Golf, Home/Auto Security, and Financial Services) Oversaw and completed financial due diligence, deal process management and offering memorandum. Organized and directed marketing efforts and client presentations.

As President of Astra Management Corp., an investment management firm, oversaw the management of a fixed income mutual fund family, including emerging market, European market, and U.S. Treasury and Agency market debt. In addition, I managed the operations of the mutual fund company and the Broker-Dealer.

While employed at Golden West Financial/World Savings as a portfolio manager, my focus was portfolio management expertise in fixed income securities, including a $2.5 billion mortage-backed securities portfolio, a $1.0 billion liquidity portfolio, and the start up and managment of various publicly traded mutual funds. I am familiar with identifying relative value in respective asset classes, drafting communications for public distribution, and recomending changes to fund prospectuses for purposes of improving fund competitiveness and performance.

Series 7, 24, 63, and 65 licenses
