Alicia Heraz’s Summary

I'm working on leaner model and tutorial strategies. I'm interested in the learner's brain wave activity and I use brain-computer interfaces (BCI) to test many hypothesis via different experimentations. In the frame of NORA's project, I suggest a new extension to improve the learner model: the learner brain profile. Besides, I am testing two new strategies: neurofeedback strategy and neuropedagogical strategy. The aim is to reach the optimal conditions for learning as attention, motivation as well as to manage negative emotions as stress, anxiety and hyperactivity.
