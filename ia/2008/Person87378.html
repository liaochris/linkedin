Jeff Ismail’s Summary

Jeff is a hi-tech professional with 20 years of “hands on” experience in hi-tech sales, business development, IT operations, professional services delivery, project management, and technical support.

Pulling from his experiences as a former programmer, MIS manager, and network consultant, he has successfully provided leadership, direction and market expertise to small, medium and large companies including such as Cabletron Systems, Solectron/Force Computers, and CompuCom Systems, where his skills in project/program management, growing business oppourtunities, and managing customer support and consulting teams is highly regarded.

His broad knowledge of technology, matched with his sales & business development sills, and demonstrated ability to understand complex client issues, allows him to gain credibility within the clients' eyes and penetrate tough accounts at all levels. Once there, he quickly earns the coveted role of "trusted advisor" ensuring an ongoing and profitable relationship within the account.

He is well versed in establishing relationships with key players in SMBs and major corporations and can effectively grow revenues in established accounts by recognizing and developing additional services oppourtunites.
