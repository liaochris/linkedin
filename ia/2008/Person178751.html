Cave Ellson’s Summary

Digital producer and consultant with over 11 years experience in the creative digital industry. A motivated and creative thinker, I’m a team player, strong presenter and diplomatic with clients.

My skills encompass a broad range of abilities allowing me to take responsibility for every stage of digital projects and marketing campaigns from scoping and concept generation, scheduling and estimating through design & development briefing to execution.

Commercial experience has been gained contracting for agencies ranging from Top 10 NMA interactives to PR specialists. I have working knowledge of account direction, a solid grounding in project management and user experience (UX) practices including proposal writing and wire-framing and experience of managing and motivating small teams.

SKILLS

• Proven track record in producing excellent digital creative
• Scoping, scheduling and budgeting of digital creative projects with the ability to set accurate production timelines leading too successful implementation
• Understanding of the implications of change requests and the ability to monitor projects for risk
• Technical knowledge (Adobe Creative Suite/HTML/CSS/AfterEffects/W3C best practice/MS Project)
• Clearly demonstrable experience of working for agencies and large corporations
• Experience of managing design & development of multiple projects ensuring quality and alignment with project specifications
• Understanding of the latest industry best practice for website and form-based interface
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills
• Excellent client facing skills

Cave Ellson’s Specialties:

Digital media design & production - full sites, microsites, banners, blogs, flash video, email marketing, HTML/CSS best practice knowledge, IA and UX understanding, wireframing, proposal writing and an understanding of SEO principles and processes. Working knowledge of digital and litho print design & production - brochures, flyers, exhibition stands, stationary, corporate identity and branding.
