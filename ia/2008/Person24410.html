Joachim Andersson’s Summary

I've been working as a web accessibility consultant for eight years now. In this work I've helped public service organizations and companies to build and uphold accessible web content, according to W3C standards and WAI recommendations (WCAG). I've also been involved in making Content Management Systems (CMS) accessible for people with disabilities, and to make these tools produce accessible web content.

I am currently developing a new company website (under construction: http://etu.bluebirdsolutions.se) for ETU AB (according to XHTML 1.1, CSS 2.1 and WCAG 1.0 AA). At the same time I am building my new personal website (blog and portfolio), at www.bluebirdsolutions.se, in the same platform as the company website.

I am also involved in building a Swedish dating website, accessible for everyone, according to XHTML 1.1, CSS 2.1 and WCAG 1.0 AA.

My goal is simple: To get people to understand that international web standards is the only reasonable way to go.
