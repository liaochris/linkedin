Carlo Lipizzi’s Summary

Carlo Lipizzi has over twenty years of international – USA, EU and Brazil - management and operational experience in Technology, Media & Telecommunication, focused on business innovation/strategic marketing as well as process analysis & redefinition and project management.

Currently Managing Partner at the US branch of a European headquartered consulting group working with European clients in Telecommunication (mobile, fixed line and virtual operators) and Government (finance/tax) and also supporting US capital management firms in their technology-based investments.
Prior this position, Mr. Lipizzi worked with industry leaders in the Telecommunication and Information Technology at management/executive level, and some of the major national and international consulting firms in the US, Europe and Brazil.

Mr. Lipizzi holds an Executive Management Degree from IMD, Lousanne, CH, and a University degree in Mathematics ("Laurea in Scienze Matematiche") from the University of Rome, Italy.

Carlo Lipizzi’s Specialties:

Strategic Marketing, Business innovation, Competitive analysis/Business evaluation, Process analysis and redefinition, International business operations
