Kellye Crane’s Summary

An accomplished, award-winning communicator with more than 17 years of experience in strategic public relations and marketing communications. Expertise includes strategic planning, writing services, media relations strategy and marketing communications.

Client experience includes Fortune 500 companies such as ADP, BellSouth, Coca-Cola, IBM, Intel and Microsoft.
