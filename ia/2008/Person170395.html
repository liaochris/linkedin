Dianne Gill’s Summary

I'm a digital artist.
I have a slide and photo scanning service - Pictureperfectscans. I scan and enhance old slides, photographs and negatives then I digitize them to a DVD Slide Show.
When my clients receive their scanned images on DVD they are thrilled to re-live and share their past and their familys' past. I'm told the slide shows bring tears of joy to their eyes.
My goal is to network and expand my scanning service. And to get the word out - dig out your old photo books and slides and get your pictures digitized before they fade away!

Dianne Gill’s Specialties:

Photography, Adobe PhotoShop, Restoring old photos, Nikon SuperCoolScan, Epson Perfection Flatbed Scan
