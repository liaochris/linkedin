Stephen Kennedy’s Summary

A Corporate recruiter with a background in executive search. Working in close partnership with our discovery organisation, I focus on 'current needs' and building pipelines of high potential candidates for senior and hard to fill roles.

My focus is to decrease cost and time of hire and drive up candidate and Line manager experience and globally influencing the adoption of new ways of recruiting. Since I have been in post I have helped save over £600,000.

My approach is one built on true partnership to achieve the very best results. Over the last 4 years I have played a key role in establishing the Talent Search Department, challenged traditional ways of working and have successfully influenced a significant number of stakeholders to adopt a new model. In addition I have contributed to up skilling recruitment colleagues in sourcing techniques and approaches in UK and Sweden, developed an 'invite only' database for Talent and designed a conference tool to help recruiting managers at conferences.

On all projects, effective third party supplier management and efficient sourcing and assimilation of critical information such as candidates and companies to target etc has been essential to my success, as has maintaining the highest standards of candidate care throughout.

I am happy to accept invitations from people that would consider AstraZeneca as an employer either now or in the future.

N.B. AstraZeneca operates a strict PSL that's evaluated every 2 years. If you're a Third Party Agency and you would like to forward details please send them to psl.alderley@astrazeneca.com .
