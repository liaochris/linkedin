Bill Isbell’s Summary

Business Development for Panache (www.PanacheTech.com). Panache creates a new inventory of advertising opportunities by transforming the actual products, scenes and subject matter within a video into interactive commerce and branding opportunities. Our patent-pending, turnkey technology differs from all other solutions by keeping the interactive advertising elements physically detached from the original media files. Panache's capabilities solve today's advertising challenges while allowing offerings to expand and evolve for years to come.
