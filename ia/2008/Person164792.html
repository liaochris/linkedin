James Ross’s Summary

My name is James Ross and I am the Democratic candidate running for State Representative against the incumbent, Kevin Lundberg, in House District 49.

I am married with three children and a graduate of the University of Colorado. I have broad experience in the construction, marketing, public policy and higher education sectors.

The real issues facing the people of House District 49 and the State of Colorado require connection and collaboration to the find common values that will move us forward, especially in uncertain times. My service on local boards and commissions has been driven by the broad aim of restoring the dialog that will move us beyond insular fear to mutual trust.

We need a state representative to make sure that real issues facing all of us get a fair hearing at the Capitol. Dialog that moves us forward shouldn't be optional. I want to see a better Colorado where everyone has a fair chance to live the promise of a high quality of life.

For that I will work for excellent public education for all of our children, affordable healthcare access for everyone, personal privacy protection and a new energy economy that limits our dependence on foreign oil, protects the environment against uranium mining in our backyards and creates good jobs in Northern Colorado. Quality representation demands responsive leadership on issues like these. That's why I'm standing up so YOU can be heard on the issues that really matter to 49'ers in Northern Colorado.

Join me to answer the calling to provide quality representation on the real issues facing HD-49.

"Standing up so YOU can be heard!"
