Matt Komyanek’s Summary

I have a wide variety of experience in everything and anything that can be found on the OSI model - from application to physical.
I have many hobbies, but I could never live without my PC(s)...

Throughout my career I have attracted myself to graphic and video design. I say video design because I believe there is more to video than just editing it. I include "graphic" because there is more to graphic design than web.
For the past year and a half I have been creating DVD memorial slideshows. A very unique process of bringing still images to life with music and animation. What intrigues me is the emotion that can be invoked with a still picture.

My experiences can be utilized everywhere and I feel with creativity on my side I can do anything.
