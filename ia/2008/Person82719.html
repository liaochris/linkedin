Jim Bolin’s Summary

Attorney with extensive experience representing businesses ranging from technology start-ups to major multinationals. Have been lead attorney on matters including complex transactions, regulatory, compliance, enforcement, litigation and government affairs. Have represented business clients as a lobbyist, lawyer and advisor in legislative, regulatory and public relations matters at the federal, state and local level. Strong background in appellate and trial litigation. Veteran legal counselor with broad understanding of operational and business issues.
