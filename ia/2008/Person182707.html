Stuart Johnstone’s Summary

Stuart is an experienced start-up and turn-around professional with more than 28 years managing businesses in technology and consumer industries. In 2001, as President/CEO of CompassCare Inc, he led a team that created the first fully integrated clinical EMR and PMS system as a web-service/ SAAS in healthcare IT. In 1999, as Co-Founder and Chief Strategy Officer of boats.com, Stuart built the leading marine marketplace in the boating industry (sold to Trader Publications). In 1996, Stuart was Co-Founder and CEO of Vosaic LLC (sold to VTEL/ Forgent), a pioneer in the Internet streaming industry and partner with the University of Illinois/NCSA. In 1993, Stuart led an investment team and managed business development in a turn-around of Eidos Interactive PLC, a UK based leading games publisher recently renowned for its “Tomb Raider” game series and movie (starring Angelina Jolie). Stuart was a founding member of J/Boats in 1977 at the age of nineteen, which became the leading high-performance sailboat brand worldwide with over 15,000 boats sold in 50+ countries. Stuart also co-founded J/World, the premier high-performance sailing school for adults in 1980.

Stuart Johnstone’s Specialties:

Highly accomplished senior marketing/ strategic business development executive and entrepreneur with 27+ years comprehensive experience in cross-functional areas including business operations, marketing, and sales in both consumer and business markets. Talent for evaluating existing markets and business opportunities, identifying issues, and developing innovative solutions through a strong analytical approach, consistently producing increased revenue and profitability.
