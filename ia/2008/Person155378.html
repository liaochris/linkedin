Rafi Dudekula’s Summary

Rafi's passion is creating and marketing sophisticated web 2.0 listings and eLearning websites.

Rafi Dudekula has over 15 years of experience in enterprise software development, architecture and management. Before starting his own company, AgileSense Technologies, he was an application architect at Microsoft Corporation for 5 years. Rafi brought his unique, innovative approaches to executing large, complex, multi-site, multi-million dollar projects successfully. As a part of Microsoft Consulting Services, Rafi worked as an application and/or integration architect for companies such as Wal-mart, CGEY, Verizon, PepsiCo, Radio Shack etc.

Rafi holds an MBA from Southern Methodist University (SMU), Dallas and an Engineering degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT).
