Eyal Ben Cohen’s Summary

Inspiring and innovative marketing / business development director, with a pioneering attitude to building businesses. Seven successful years driving internet business forward have been founded on a natural talent for selling and marketing, combined with the energy and enthusiasm to inspire others to achieve the seemingly impossible. A pragmatic management style, based on common sense, objectivity and a willingness to help others, overcoming barriers to completion of projects, whilst clear communication of aims, assertiveness and quick-thinking have ensured that results have been achieved on time. Key business transactions have benefited from excellent negotiating skills and analytical abilities, whilst strong interpersonal communication skills at all levels have cemented client relationships.
