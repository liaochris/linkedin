Karen Miller’s Summary

I am a former high school teacher who segued into the educational publishing industry. I have worked on print and online products, and for large corporations, small companies, and individual authors. Most of my editing experience has been developmental editing--working with an author or from an outline to create a detailed and/or more coherent product--although copyediting is a significant portion of my jobs, especially with smaller companies.

I currently freelance out of my home. I prefer shorter term projects (four months or less) and projects that do not require me to be available every day during the business day. I can also write, although I prefer instructional and analytical text (such as an introduction to a chapter or a module) to marketing and promotional text.
