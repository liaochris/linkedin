David Wanika’s Summary

I am currently engaged in new business development for my company and focusing on introducing the KOR-Tx Print Management System to senior executives and decision makers who are ultimately responsible for managing the corporate bottom-line or a budget for their company.

If this sounds like your company, I can help:

• Do you struggle getting printed materials into the hands of your sales team?

• Does your company have information requests stacking up because you don’t have the staff or time to pull together the information, find a box, and then ship the information to the customer or prospect?

• A company who always seems to run out of their pocket folders; their sales sheets; and order forms.

• A company who is in a major growth phase and needs assistance going to the next level.

• Sales Organizations, Corporate Marketing Departments; Corporate Executives who need to increase profits.

KOR-Tx addresses age-old inefficiencies within the collateral and sales material procurement and fulfillment process. My job is to introduce corporate decision makers to KOR-Tx and to explain how we assist companies increase productivity and ultimately save money.

If you are interested in knowing more please feel free to contact me for more information.

I am a Visual Communications Professional with over twenty-five years of experience in product delivery and building relationships to accomplish bottom-line results while exceeding the needs of clients.

My background of proven success in providing leadership in planning and executing complicated multi-component print production programs, operations, production, development, collaborating, organizing and implementing successful IT driven client solutions.
