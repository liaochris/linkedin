Paul Oliver’s Summary

Paul is a leader who brings a unique business perspective developed from his experience in different manufacturing, service, and media companies. Driving both effectiveness and efficiency, Paul has gained a reputation for improving processes that increase productivity, reduce costs and accelerate businesses ability to implement change.

- Extensive expertise directing all aspects of technology operations and projects
- Recognized for spearheading project and performance management in diverse settings
- Dynamic communicator and cultivator of key relationships
- Big picture thinker driven to impact the top and bottom line while ensuring effectiveness of technology investments.
- Demonstrated skill in developing and motivating successful teams that result in a retention of top performing professionals.
- Positive and effective change agent who has orchestrated highly successful people, process and technology changes.
