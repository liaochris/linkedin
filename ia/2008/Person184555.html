Michael Swiszcz’s Summary

Bio -

Michael Swiszcz joined Ledgent (formerly Accounting Alliance Staffing Services) in April of 2001 and has over seven years of accounting and financial staffing experience. In July of 2005, Ledgent opened its Mesa Office specializes in Financial & Accounting staffing and offers services on a Direct Hire, Temporary/Project and Temporary-To-Hire basis.

Currently, Michael is an active member of the Phoenix CEO-CFO Group, Construction Financial Management Association (CFMA) and also the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA). He has been a member of the Board of Directors of the AZ Valley of the Sun Chapter of the IMA for three years. Michael has served two terms as the President of the 500+ member IMA chapter.

Michael started his career with a large national firm where he was responsible for outside sales, recruiting and servicing customers. With a Bachelors degree in Finance from Bentley College and active affiliations with organizations dedicated to the accounting and finance professions, Michael brings with him a knowledge of the profession and a vast network of resources to locate the top talent in the marketplace.
