Tracy Juliao’s Summary

At Juliao & Associates, LLC we offer consulting services to companies in transition and/or who are entrepreneurial and seeking to grow. We bring our expertise in business and psychology to help our clients develop business and human capital.

Additionally, I currently work as a Health Psychologist and Behavioral Medicine Faculty in a Family Medicine Residency program within a community hospital. My primary professional interests include health psychology within primary care, medical education, multicultural issues and training, pain management, group medical visits, women’s issues, and family therapy. I have been involved in the development of a comprehensive chronic pain assessment and management program within the primary care setting and have presented at regional and national conferences regarding the same.

I also teach undergraduate psychology as an adjunct professor at several local colleges and universities. I have been teaching as an adjunct faculty member for the past 15 years in New York and Michigan. I have also worked in college and university settings as an academic counselor and administrator. Some of the schools I have been affiliated with include New York University Stern School of Business, Teachers College, Columbia University, John Jay College of Criminal Justice, Kingsborough Community College, University of Michigan-Flint, Michigan State University, and Washtenaw Community College.

In addition to my professional endeavors, I am a wife and proud mother of a very active 8-year-old.
