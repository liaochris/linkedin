Stefania Lintonbon’s Summary

Stefania’s background in acting, from children’s theatre through to modern drama and Shakespeare makes her a uniquely flexible voice talent. She has a very distinctive, bright, warm, educated, easy on the ears, versatile voice specialising – but not limited to – animation, audiobooks, computer games, commercials, corporate work, narration, talking toys, training materials, and radio drama.

A native of the USA who now lives in England, she has learned RP as a member of the London Actors Centre; and is now receiving tutorials in the Southern Irish dialect. Stefania can also demonstrate a range of fantasy voices.

I’m professional, focused, friendly, flexible, versatile, creative, positive and take direction well; and am a skilled, trained, experienced actress.

Voicovers are deliverable via formats such as MP3, WAVE, web upload, or CD (International Signed for delivery).

Please have a look at my website where you'll find a variety of demos in different genres. I'd be pleased to record a custom demo if needed.

Stefania Lintonbon’s Specialties:

I speak intermediate-level Japanese. Dialects include General American, American Southern, New York, California, British RP. Character acting is my speciality, but I can happily do straight corporate voices as well!
