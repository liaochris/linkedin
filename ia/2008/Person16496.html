David Boylan’s Summary

Who is Artichoke Creative?

Passionate
We prefer to work with businesses where we feel a real connection with their product or service.It just makes sense to do work that excites and motivates us.

Your Marketing Department
We become an extension of your company, collaborating with you on strategy, creative, integration, and implementation of your marketing programs. We immerse ourselves in your products, then we’ll live and breath your brand, help you build your business, and celebrate your success.

Sales & Marketing Gurus
You should be skeptical of any agency executive who has never held a sales position. Sales and marketing are not mutually exclusive. Previously, as a national sales manager, AC President David Boylan has been responsible for the bottom line, moving products, and motivating a sales team. This experience has proven invaluable in working with clients to define objectives and understanding the challenges they face to meet them. On the flip side, David is a very creative marketing guy. From concepts, copy and events to staying on top of traditional and emerging technologies…he can bridge the gap between client expectations and the creative staff. He demands an understanding of client objectives and their industry from his creative team.

Integrated
This can be an overused word in the marketing space and most agencies use it but don’t practice it. At Artichoke Creative integration is our mantra, new mediums excite us. We keep our team up to speed on new technologies and how they can be combined with traditional mediums to help you sell more stuff.

Innovators
Our combination of creativity and bottom line focused business experience give us a unique ability to create innovative, effective, targeted, integrated advertising campaigns and events. We also come up with non-traditional ways to promote brands like our own Artichoke Apparel.

David Boylan’s Specialties:

Integrated marketing services that combine digital technologies - banner ads, email marketing, search engine marketing with all the traditional mediums to create a mix that works best for our clients.
