Jim Sproul’s Summary

The Sproul Company (TSC) is a customer-centric professional services firm with a rich history of helping companies build revenue generating alliance stratagies.

Jim Sproul’s Specialties:

We are passionate in our belief that strategic partnerships are the lifeblood to positive and predictable growth in today’s changing economic landscape. Using a proven partnership methodology, we help our clients establish new sources of revenue, share operational and marketing expenses among partner relationships, and optimize available capital to achieve greater market share.
