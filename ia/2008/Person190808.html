Thomas Crawford’s Summary

I am a seasoned executive search professional focused on leadership development for high-growth, emerging technology companies through a highly personalized service delivery. My search practice is complemented by more than 20 years of operational experience in Silicon Valley startups - including one IPO, three successful acquisitions, and extensive fund-raising activities. OTG Partners is committed to cultivating and delivering highly-targeted, top tier executive talent for Silicon Valley's most respected technology companies.
