Gurminder Marwaha’s Summary

Currently a senior account manager at College Hill PR, helping to develop clients' brand, identity and global communications. I write copy for articles, features, press releases, brochures, flyers, direct mailers, e-bulletins, websites etc. I am also involved in media training, generating new business leads and identifying communication opportunities for my clients.

Previously, I have been a PR Account Director at Gyro International in Manchester (UK), where I helped to set up a new global PR division; copywriting for features, press releases and adverts; directed a number of accounts, shaping PR strategy and with responsibility for achieving clients’ objectives within agreed timescales and budgets; positively influencing clients in areas such as media strategies, editorial opportunities, conference and exhibition attendance, corporate communications and crisis management; and actively developing new business opportunities.

I was also an editor at Advanstar Communications, where I managed a portfolio of print and digital international B2B pharmaceutical publications; interviewed senior industry figures, including CEOs and executives; researched, wrote and commissioned news, articles and features; managed custom publishing/corporate communication/customer magazine projects for clients; launched/maintained group websites, e-newsletters and new magazines (print and digital); provided advice and assistance to clients’ media strategies (PR and advertising); managed two assistant editors and a technical editor; responsible for setting and maintaining budgets; developed and implemented portfolio strategy and editorial schedules; was an ambassador for the publications at international trade events; and chaired/spoke at industry conferences.
