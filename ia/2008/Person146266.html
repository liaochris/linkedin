Paritosh Ambekar’s Summary

Dynamic technology leader in the medical device industry with dedication, clear vision and expertise in technical and management aspects of the industry. Strong at problem solving, consumer interaction and communication. Performance driven, with a proven record in delivering results of high quality and driving sound technical design while establishing operational processes. Led and been actively involved in mission-critical projects that are aligned to corporate strategy and have contributed significantly towards product and revenue growth, and customer satisfaction. Extensive marketing experience in successful clinical developments, surgeon relationships, conferences, clinical labs and trade shows. Skilled at understanding, learning and capturing new concepts quickly, working well under pressure, and communicating ideas clearly and effectively.
