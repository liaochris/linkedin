Omar Hamada, MD’s Summary

Accomplished medical professional and physician executive. Strengths include strategic business planning, marketing, and implementing processes to drive quality, efficiency, and medical service delivery improvements. Decorated military leader and extensive experience in board-level committees for corporate and non-profit entities. Excellent qualifications in public speaking, research, and print/broadcast media relations.

Serves as a Consultant/Reserve Officer for the Smyrna SWAT Team, Delegate to the Lausanne Committee on World Evangelization, Local Council Representative for the Christian Medical and Dental Association, and is on the Advisory Board of the Juvenile Intervention and Faith-based Follow-up in Memphis. Also serves as a Community Group Leader at Brentwood's Fellowship Bible Church.

Hamada entertained and investigated a run for the United States House of Representatives in 2002 representing Tennessee's Seventh Congressional District, but instead deployed to Afghanistan with the US Army Special Forces.

He served in the United States Army for 15 years, the last 10 of which were with the US Special Forces as Flight Surgeon and Diving Medical Officer. He was awarded the Bronze Star for actions in combat, as well as numerous other awards. Hamada is a Masters of Business Administration candidate at Vanderbilt's Owen Graduate School of Management. He is also an ordained Southern Baptist minister, a classically trained operatic tenor, and jazz trumpeter.
