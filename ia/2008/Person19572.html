Barbara Atkins’s Summary

• President of the Penna Group, LLC. An RSP-Trained Retirement Coach and Certified Retirement Facilitator. Specializing in pre-retirement readiness and post retirement coaching. Offers individual and group coaching services to adults (ages 40+) who want to maximize their life experiences, wisdom and knowledge into a creative passion for a fulfilling future.” Interested in working with individuals and couples who are seeking solutions to ‘what’s next’ after work disengagement and will make bold decisions about their future. Interested in presenting workshops to company’s pre-retirees.

• Over 20 years as an entrepreneur/business owner. President/CEO of Pica Systems Inc., Michigan’s largest minority woman-owned and operated Data Processing firm with over 185 full-time employees; and, co-owner/Vice President of BPA Enterprises Executive Search.

• She has been published in Money Magazine, Black Enterprise, The Washington Post, Detroit News and Detroit Free Press. Her company ranked #15 in Michigan’s fasted growing companies. After closing both companies, Barbara served as Executive Director of One Stop Capital Shop a non-profit federally funded project. In this capacity she assisted entrepreneurs and start-up companies with the basics of strategic planning. She has received numerous awards for her leadership and commitment to the community and for corporate citizenship.

• Attended Michigan State University, Wayne State University and University of Phoenix where she majored in Business Administration and Business Management and is a graduate of Leadership Detroit. She has served on numerous Boards including Greater Detroit Chamber of Commerce, Michigan Colleges & Universities, Women’s Economic Club, Economic Growth, Marygrove College, United Way and United Way of Michigan. Appointed by the Governor to the Board of Small Business for the State of Michigan and to the Michigan Women’s Commission where she chaired the Education Committee

Small business development, proposal writing, public speaking, retirement coaching
