Jorge Gomez’s Summary

Jorge was born on May 7th 1976 in Spain, where he lived till the age of 18. Later, he moved to France and took his undergraduate education in France and Ireland graduating in 2000.

In July 2000, Jorge signed for Schlumberger Ltd. (SLB) in Paris and joined the new SLB's Group. Jorge worked for almost three years leading several multinational projects with several global Oil&Gas clients.

In 2003 Jorge was proposed to move to Madrid and launch the Consulting branch of SLB in Spain & Portugal. As Consulting Manager for the region, Jorge's main responsibilities were the business development of the activity in term of revenue, profit and headcount, the design and implementation of the local strategy and the international coordination of the activity.

In 2004 Jorge took a sabbatical period of two years. He spent part of that time backpacking in South America but also took an MBA program at INSEAD enjoying from an excellence award granted by the French Government & La Caixa Bank.

Following his MBA, Jorge joined General Electric (GE) where he held the position of Area Manager of the Homeland Protection Division for the South European & North African Region during the period 2005 & 2006. Jorge managed Goverment relationships in the areas of Homeland Protection and Security.

In 2006 Jorge accepted the position of Global Strategy & Business Development Director for a new Joint Venture of General Electric. He contributed to the reorganization of the Automation & Service Business Activity to double revenues from US$ 500 Millions to US$ 1Billion $. Jorge acted as part of the Business Steering Committe and represented the company in the arena of Homeland Protection & Security aswell.

Jorge is today responsible for the development of the Business of Thales Air Systems in the Latin America, Africa and Middle East Region.
