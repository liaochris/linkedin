Chris Li’s Summary

Senior IT professional with in-depth expertise in database/data warehousing, business intelligence, and Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP); strong history of project management and team leadership, complex problem solving and successful project completion; experienced delivering sophisticated business solutions using a wide variety of existing and emerging technologies and software.

• Expert in Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW), articulated sophisticated and satisfactory solutions that dealt with multi-tera byte data volume and met extremely complicated business logic and reporting requirements.

• Kernel level knowledge in Teradata, Oracle, DB2, and SQL Server; years of experience in how to utilize the power of all major data warehouse platforms;

• Master in complete functionalities of Oracle BI EE, aka, Siebel Analytics, platforms and applications, including backend (DW data model, ETL, and DAC), middle tier ( Metadata), and front end (Answers, Dashboards, and Requests); published various best practice white papers on those topics.

• Intensive knowledge in Siebel CRM applications as well as Oracle 11i e-business Suite applications, their data model, infrastructure, and foundation modules.

• “Hands on”, functional and technical IT manager with 12+ years of experience involving data warehouse systems development and program/project management experience; led complex, enterprise-level data warehouse development projects from concept to implementation; well versed in implementing complex, enterprise level, business intelligence solutions.

• In-depth industrial knowledge in retail, high tech, financial service, and pharmaceutical.

• Siebel Certified Master Analytics Consultant, Oracle Certified 10g DBA OCP, Teradata Certified V2R5 Professional, IBM DB2 UDB Certified Solution Expert, and Siebel Certification CRM Consultant.
