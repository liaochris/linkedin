Lauren Hertel’s Summary

Multimedia journalism trainer with substantial experience working with print, broadcast, and online audiences of all ages. Successful project manager, curriculum developer and grant writer. Interested in having maximum impact on news industry through large-scale training efforts.

Lauren Hertel’s Specialties:
