Gerard Burke’s Summary

The Business Growth and Development Programme (BGP) at Cranfield is the UK's leading programme for ambitious owner managers - and I have the privilige of being it's director. BGP is 20 years old in 2008 and we've just accepted our 1,000th participant on to the programme. We've had some very high profile past participants including Lord Karan Bilimoria of Cobra Beer, Angus Thirlwell of Hotel Chocolat, Mayank Patel of Currencies Direct, Charles Rigby of World Challenge Expeditions and Lara Morgan of Pacific Direct. Businesses which participate in BGP grow their sales and profits quicker than the average smaller business and quicker after the programme than they did before. If you're an ambitious owner manager and you'd like to find out more about BGP, visit http://www.som.cranfield.ac.uk/som/bgp
