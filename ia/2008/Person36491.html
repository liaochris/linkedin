Christine Clacey’s Summary

Christine Clacey specialises in leadership and organisation development, helping professionals make the most of their talent. She is an expert leadership and management coach and a highly skilled trainer in coaching and mentoring skills. Her career includes 19 years of success in a wide range of senior line management roles in international business. Her core areas of expertise are leadership, business strategy, business development, employee and career development, professional development, networking, strategic alliance development and business consultancy.

Christine is also a sought after professional consultant, mentor and popular professional speaker. She has for many years provided coaching and mentoring advice and guidance to employees, colleagues and external business contacts within the IT industry and associated industries.

Christine regularly delivers high performance coaching skills training. In addition to being a trainer for the Anthony Robbins organisation, she is a certified NLP Coach, a professional Mentor and Trainer and a member of the International Coach Federation. She is a certified NLP Master Practitioner and has a BSc joint honours degree in Mathematics and Computer Science.

Christine specialises in helping organisations to leverage their talent for business success.

Christine is a keen networker. She leads the Women in Business community for www.Business-Scene.com - the natural evolution of business directories, the new meeting place for business networks and the emerging powerhouse for local trading.

Christine also heads up Women in Business for Business In Berkshire. With its weekly newsletters covering events and news, and a database of over 3,500 readers, Business in Berkshire provides a PR vehicle for businesses looking to gain a profile in Berkshire and, from a search engine perspective, across the Globe.
