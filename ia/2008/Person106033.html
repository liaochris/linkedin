Kevin Spidel’s Summary

Kevin Spidel currently a partner at BlueDynamic, LLC based out of Phoenix, AZ with offices in Washington, DC, Michigan, and California. He specializes in political, marketing, public relations, and business development from a wide range of clients in entertainment, political and nonprofit.

He was previously the Campaign Director Denounce Torture for Amnesty International, USA. He also was the Statewide Field Coordinator for the American Civil Liberties Union of Arizona as well as a senior advisor to the Hip Hop Caucus. In the General Election in 2006 he served as Field Director for Williams for Arizona. and was the Campaign Manager for Cegelis for Congress. From 2004 - 2006, Kevin served as the National Deputy Director for Progressive Democrats of America and worked on several different campaigns at any given time as a political consultant.

Kevin was also the National Director and founder of Progressive Vote which merged into Progressive Democrats of America at Roxbury College in Boston, MA during the 2004 Democratic National Convention. Progressive Vote provided the field infrastructure and “inside the Democratic Party” state operations of localized chapters of Progressive Democrats of America. He also served as the National Political Field Director for the Kucinich for President in 2004.
