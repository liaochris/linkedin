Nicholas Corr’s Summary

Nicholas Corr started his real estate career at $6 an hour working for an investor that fix-n-flipped and wholesaled houses. For over the course of 4 years Nicholas Corr was the project manager for a local investor that specialized in purchasing properties below value in Phoenix. Before long he had all the necessary abilities, tools, and contacts to do just about anything with fixer uppers and was encouraged to do his own flip in late 2002. After receiving a check that was larger than what he use to make per year in just 90 days, Nicholas Corr set out to find more properties below value. With over 150 properties under his belt between his project management years and his own fix-n-flips, he decided to start giving other investors and friends he knew the opportunity to fix-n-flip and, by accident, he became a full blown wholesaler. Today Nicholas Corr locally owns and operates www.fastmoney4home.com, www.localrent2own.com, www.BelowValueRealEstate.com and www.videofuture.acnrep.com . Recently we have had so much success thanks to our customers that need to sell and our investors that need to buy we decided it was time to have a website that our Investors could see our properties and learn more about who we are and what we do. We have been fortunate to team up with Eric Ford with both acquiring and selling below value real estate. We look forward to serving you with great deals that you can add to your portfolio or fix-n-flip.

Call to ask us anything regarding real estate transactions. We don’t know everything, but we have sure been in this game long enough to point you in the right direction or save you a ton of money my by simply running a deal or scenario by us. We are here for you. Thank you for your support, and we look forward to growing our business with you.
