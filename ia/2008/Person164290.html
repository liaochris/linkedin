Ron Bower’s Summary

A Human Resouces leader with over 25 years of experience; currently leading a team of HR professionals with responsibility for recruiting, employee relations, retention, onboarding, and employee records. Significant focus and success during the last 2+ years to enhance the business case for recruiting (increasing the size of the recruiting staff, reducing overall recruiting expenses while increasing client and candidate satisfaction) and developing a highly effective employee referral program. Article on the AmTrust employee referral program was published in the May 2008 issue of the "Journal of Corporate Recruiting Leadership."

AmTrust Bank is always looking for top talent! Please visit our careers page at amtrust.jobs (link below) and explore the opportunities we have to offer.
