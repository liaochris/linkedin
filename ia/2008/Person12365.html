Andy Kipling’s Summary

I am currently living in Seattle, Washington after having lived in Southern California for the past 9 years. Since moving to Seattle I have taken a job with Zombie, Inc - a small game developer in downtown. Seattle is a wonderful place and I am extremely happy here.

I have been involved in the shipping of 8 video game titles. Some have done well, others not as well, but in all cases, I learned much. My experiences have taught me much and have allowed me to constantly improve my effectiveness and efficiency. My most recent success was serving as Producer on my first title fully managed and overseen by me. The title was developed out of house, on the PS2, and ran about a year from contract signing to release. While the mandate, and budget of the title were limiting, I was pleased with what we were able to accomplish and so far it has been a financial success.

I feel strongly that I bring many traits to the table that would suit many companies. I am a positive person who brings a good attitude to work everyday and is an able and effective communicator, which is important as many problems can be traced back to communication breakdowns. I am also experienced with managing and communicating remotely via phone and email. I pride myself on my well-rounded nature and my strong technical and creative backgrounds. At heart, I am a problem solver who enjoys nothing more than to jump in and help resolve issues or provide advice and feedback. I am an avid learner and am very capable at managing many tasks simultaneously. As a person, I am honest and straightforward and I value the respect and admiration of my co-workers while always working to make my working environment better and more enjoyable.

I would like to continue my success in the video games industry and always wake up each morning excited and happy to go to work.
