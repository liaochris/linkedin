Wojtek Kawczynski’s Summary

At SilverBirch I am responsible for setting the strategy, doing business development and overseeing the operations of the game studio division.

At Avokia I headed-up the Product Management group with responsibilities for product positioning, product roadmap, competitive analysis, pricing and analyst relations. In the last quarter of 2006 I also took on the responsibilities of initial customer acquisition and partnerships. I also acted as Secretary of the Board throughout my time at Avokia.

Prior to Avokia I spent five years at one of Canada's top venture capital firms, Ventures West. At Ventures West I focused on the enterprise software sector and was involved in deal generation, due diligence and represented the fund on numerous Boards of Directors.

Prior to venture capital I pursued a career in science during which I spent four years working at the University of Toronto.

I started my studies in Montreal where I did my B.Sc. in biology (1992) at Concordia University followed by a M.Sc. in molecular biology (1995) at McGill University. In 1995 I moved to Toronto where I completed my MBA (2001) at the Schulich School of Business, specializing in the areas of Strategy and Finance.

I'm also a co-founder and was a co-chair of CVCA´s Young Venture Capitalists´ Committee, a networking group for the next generation of venture capital professionals.
