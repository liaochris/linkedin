Chris Landau’s Summary

I am an artist and designer who takes a serious approach to anything I am doing. While I care a great deal about aesthetics, good information design is my true goal in both art and design.

My technical experience ranges the spectrum from digital media to traditional techniques. Many of my endeavors in the past few years have also equipped me to be a leader, manager, writer, and educator. I enjoy wearing all of these different hats.

Chris Landau’s Specialties:

Drawing, Painting, Printmaking, Graphic Design, Sculpting, 3D modeling, Animation, Interaction Design, Web Design, Information Design, Flash ActionScripting, Second Life design and building, Project Management, Creative Direction, Client Relations, Blogging
