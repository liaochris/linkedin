Paul Tittmann’s Summary

Global Sources Direct is a B2B internet retailer, sourcing from China and selling to a global marketplace. We use standard internet marketing tools - SEO, PPC, affiate, eDM - to reach a global community of internet retailers , who rely on us to be their stocking wholesale supplier of a department store range of 2,000 mobile phone, security, consumer electronics, gifts, gadgets, jewelry , etc. products sourced from China. We offer secure online payment through PayPal, Paily, Westen Union, credit card and wire transfer. Finally, int'l fulfilment direct to door via international couier comnpanies FedEx, DHL, UPS, and ocean/air forwarders.

We are quite deliberate about screening out counterfeits, or products with questionable quality certification .

We look for legitimate suppliers of quality products who want to develop alternative sales channels to global customers.

Global Sources Direct is a business unit of Global Sources, a NASDAQ listed public company (GSOL) committed to enabling global trade through a suite of integrated services including magazine, online, trade show .
