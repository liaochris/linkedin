Laurence Cumbie’s Summary

I've had many professional experiences, most of which are easily forgotten.

I despise business in general; but "busy-ness" suits me just fine.

Much of my life has been given over to feeding people - including myself. Therefore, you will find few others more learned or devoted to this subject.

I seek pleasure at most every turn - and love to provide it when I'm permitted. A former self-styled culinary artist and hospitality consultant for at least 25 of my years, I only know I'm ever-anxious to please myself by pleasing others.

A perfectly-suited specimen for life on this planet, I find I'm addicted to air, water, nourishment, and other perfectly-suited specimens.

I'm often contemplative about nature... sensitive about my entanglements... always spoiling for that next ecstatic experience.

When one comes into my sphere of influence I become their champion - and I champion their rousing success.

I hate for others to be hurt or get hurt... too often. I'll fight with the courage of my convictions if I'm forced into a corner - at any time or any place. But I also love collaborative arrangements... if goal-setting is aligned with our highest, most-noble purposes... in achieving the greatest good.

The food industry just happens to be one area I've been able to successfully exchange my time for money in the past... as I'm extraordinarily attentive to the little things which bring the greatest amount of pleasure.

I'm obsessed with style... and the heightening of all sensory perceptions... which contribute to my own amusement.

Of late... I tend to read and write a lot... and hope to make more time for it... as I sharpen and hone my skills.

Today, as for goals... I'm only interested in pampering my everyday addictions... and voraciously seek fulfillment... confident in my ability to positively affect any outcome.

Only truth will tell what the future holds for us. So put on your game face and let's start mixing it up!

Laurence Cumbie’s Specialties:

I enjoy helping people who seek my help; and I'm fairly adept at seeking it from others.

However politically attuned... to the facts... as they exist on the ground... I'm quite the contented fellow. In truth, I tend to laugh in the face... of trepidation or challenge... which quicken our hearts... yet soothe our souls. I'm competent to perform - with aplomb - in any arena I choose to participate. You could say I'm an expert about nothing - and I pull it off very well!
