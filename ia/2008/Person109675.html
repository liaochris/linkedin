Kurt Unkel’s Summary

Digital advertising expert, with over 8 years experience in developing digital media strategies, creating innovative measurement models, and customizing applications of existing and new internet technologies for clients such as Ford and General Motors.

Extensive understanding of the digital advertising industry's technological infrastructure and systems, including ad-serving, inventory/campaign management software, rich media vendors, syndicated research providers, and data management & analytics tools.

Very strong communication and analytical skills, with a proven track record of developing truly creative ideas and inspiring talent within others for solving a wide variety of advertising-centric challenges.
