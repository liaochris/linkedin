    What is Imagineering and what is thought in the master?

    Business organisations do not easily change from within. The chasm between today’s individuals and today’s commercial organisations leads to a ‘transaction crisis’. The fundamental changes in the nature of consumption today will be tomorrow’s changes in the nature and purpose of business. The ‘transaction crisis’ will pave the way for major transformations within the enterprise logic of many kinds of organisations and institutions.

    Imagineering, value creation and value innovation from the experience perspective, is a new approach towards the trinity of branding and business innovation. It is a way to discover the possibility of a new kind of convergence between consumers’ desires, technological capabilities and organisational innovations.
