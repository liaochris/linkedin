Miriam Cohen Salpeter’s Summary

I author a well-regarded career advice blog, advise and coach clients in the U.S. and abroad regarding career transitions and job searches and speak to groups regarding career search and social networking strategies. I’ve been offering clients confidence, clarity and job-seeking know-how for over 10 years. My goal is to encourage, enlighten and empower job seekers for success by offering a toolbox of practical tips and support.
