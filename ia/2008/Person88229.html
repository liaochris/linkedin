Eric Jenkins’s Experience

  • VP of Sales

    Ocean Tomo

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Investment Banking industry)

    July 2008 — Present (4 months)

    Ocean Tomo Patent/Bid-Ask (PBA) platform is the first and only public marketplace that allows buyers and sellers to place and receive offers for their intellectual property in a completely transparent fashion.

    PBA supports unsolicited offers or Bids for any patent. Using widely available analytics, including Ocean Tomo Patent Ratings® software, buyers wanting to expand their existing portfolio can anonymously contact owners of patents to initiate a sale. Bids delivered through the Patent/Bid-Ask market are well received as potential sellers know the offer has reserved funds supporting it.

    As an open global platform, it is the default resource for global price discovery data for patent transactions. Virtually any patent from over 81 patent authorities across the globe can now be easily transacted between a willing buyer and a seller.
