Robert Blaise’s Summary

For the past 6 years Robert Blaise has served as eCommerce Manager at Visuwire, an Interactive web development agency, that focuses on ecommerce and B2B solutions for small to midsized companies. In addition to ecommerce Visuwire developed several New Media properties for the following notable clients: MTV, Atlantic Records, Plus 1 Music Group & Clear Chanel Communications.

Robert’s key areas of responsibility at Visuwire are: Development of eCommerce enhancement plans to client from careful study of analytics, consumer data, and extensive research. Facilitate a complete enhancement of e-commerce engine thru click stream analysis, site performance and general stats.

Prior to this appointment Robert Blaise was as an Internet Marketing Specialist at the Madison Sky Agency where his responsibilities were: Research and development of internet marketing strategies, exploring and executing Internet marketing programs for various clients, as well as, niche web sites. Robert was directly responsible for the development and execution of landing pages, eNewsletters, and generating weekly campaign performance reports.

Robert’s professional career began in Operation Management for UPS As accomplished leader with an impeccable record of exceeding projections, Robert developed optimal solutions and strategies that maximized profits. In this role, Robert Blaise conducted transportation solutions analysis to identify opportunities to improve business processes into a cross-enterprise process driven solution.

Robert Blaise received his BS/Information Technology from Barry University in Miami Shores, FL, and he has recently obtained his Search Engine Marketing Certification.

With a career spanning more than 17 years, in a wide variety of industries, Robert’s extensive Management background has encompassed consulting, directing and implementing strategic solutions for the following industries: Internet, Transportation, Logistics, Telephony, and Mobile Phones.
