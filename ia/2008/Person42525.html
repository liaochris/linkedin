Dallas Tanner’s Summary

Filmmaker Dallas Tanner has worked in the entertainment and media industry for over ten years. He is the creator and producer of Destination X Hawaii, an internationally syndicated television series which went into syndication in 2006. Season #2, Destination X California was released in Summer, 2008. He has produced over 189 episodes of lifestyles, reality tv, and action sports tv programming which is distributed to over 50 countries worldwide. He has also launched several action sports magazines, served as a marketing director for a NASCAR motorsports team, headed up filming of professional motorsports rally races through several countries, and is a semi professional race car driver. He has overseen all aspects from corporate sponsorship, development of strategic marketing, worked with Fortune 1000 advertising clients and is a hands on multi- media director and television director including iPTV, print publishing, television and other media.
