Anand Chhatpar’s Summary

Anand is currently the CEO of BrainReactions LLC, a company that utilizes the most creative college students to brainstorm new ideas for Fortune 1000 companies. Anand is fond of meeting exciting new people and believes that opportunities can be created in any field. His connections in India and in the U.S. cover a wide spectrum of leaders in technology and business.

Anand Chhatpar started his first company, Pyxoft Infotech Pvt Ltd., even before coming to study in the United States. In just his second year in college, Anand was involved in his second start-up, OZ Innovations and went on to sell the product internationally in a retail chain of over 72 stores. Anand graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a stellar academic record supplemented by his research in various fields within Computer Engineering. He holds 6 issued U.S. utility patents and has 1 patent pending. Anand has interned twice at Pitney Bowes Advanced Concepts and Technology Division.

Anand has recently launched an online brainstorming network and idea management software called http://brainreactions.net recently that helps companies brainstorm online with their employees and their customers to build an idea pipeline and seed word-of-mouth marketing.

In October 2005, Anand was named as one of the "Top 5 Entrepreneurs Under 25" by Business Week Online:
http://images.businessweek.com/ss/05/12/25entrepreneurs/index_01.htm
...and also one of Madison's "Top 40 under 40" by In Business Magazine.

In January 2006, Anand was featured on CNBC TV's "Young Turks".

In February 2007, Anand's profile and business were featured as a sucess case study in the second edition of an MBA textbook titled "Entrepreneurship: Successfully Launching New Ventures" by Dr. Bruce Barringer which is used in over 120 US Universities.
