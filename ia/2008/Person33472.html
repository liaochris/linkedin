Charan Narang’s Experience

  • General Manager

    R & P Gelato Pvt. Ltd.

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Food & Beverages industry)

    April 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    Gelato (pronounced Jeh-LAH-toe) is an authentic Italian frozen dessert.

    R&P Gelato Pvt. Ltd. is a 100% subsidiary of an Italian Company called R&P Srl, which has a heritage of several decades in producing excellent quality artisanal Gelato.

    The company was established in Mumbai in February 2006, with the objective of bringing high quality frozen dessert to India, prepares fresh, natural and healthy Gelato in its production lab in Worli, Mumbai, and sells its Gelato directly to customers through 11 branded retail points in Mumbai and has already established its leadership in the market through its superior product quality.

    My current role in the organisation involves overseeing the overall operations and administration of the company's subsidiary in India including the Production, Retail, QC and QA, SCM, Marketing, Business Development and Franchising, Finance and Budgeting with a major contribution to strategy formulation and growth planning.

  • Manager- Food and Beverage

    Cafe Coffee Day

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Food & Beverages industry)

    July 2005 — May 2007 (1 year 11 months)

    Was responsible for all Food and Beverage related activities for Northern and Eastern India spanning 140 outlets in 23 cities.
    My core areas of responsibility in this role were Product Development, Vendor Management and Vendor development, Quality Control and Quality Assurance, Training and Development, Menu designing, Planning and execution of Food and Beverage promotions and new product launches, Food and Beverage Communication, Standardization of Kitchen Management Systems, Kitchen Design, layout, ergonomics, Standardization of recipes, systems, procedures
    I was also responsible for innovation of the Café Coffee Day Lounge Concept with complete responsibility for set-up of the lounge concept in Khan Market Lounge, New Delhi, Wood Street Lounge, Kolkata and The Mall, Mussourie.

  • Consultant Pastry Chef

    Hotel L'Archipel, Seychelles (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 51-200 employees; Hospitality industry)

    April 2005 — July 2005 (4 months)

    This was a consultancy assignment that involved imparting hands-on training and development of the patiserrie staff and introducing them to continental style Desserts, Breads and Viennoiserie, development of new menus for breakfast, a la carte, table d’hote and bi-weekly buffets, Standardization of all recipes and Operating procedures for Pastry using the Fiche technique and blending continental technique with local tropical ingredients to bring out the best qualityn products.

  • Chef de Partie

    Madinat Jumeirah

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Food & Beverages industry)

    May 2004 — May 2005 (1 year 1 month)

    Was part of the pre-opening team of Madinat Jumeirah, (a 900 room hotel with 40 F&B outlets) which was a rich experience that involved planning, trials and developing various types of breads and patiserrie from all over the world, Recipe standardization and costing, Quality Standardization and Control with Standard Specifications, Production planning and execution of the same.

  • Pastry Executive

    The Park Hotels

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Hospitality industry)

    June 2001 — May 2004 (3 years)

    Lead a 14 member team of Bakery & Pastry colleagues and was responsible for their training & performance management. Gained practical experience in Research, Development and introduction of new products through regular trials, interacting with Guests on a daily basis and obtaining feedback, Menu Planning, trials, standardization and execution of food promotions and festivals.
    Standardization of over 250 recipes for Desserts, Breads, Gateaux, Pastry and Cookies for the
    Pastry shop, A La Carte and Banquets at The Park, New Delhi.
    Had the opportunity to set up the Desserts section in the Contemporary Indian food specialty restaurant ‘Fire’ at The Park, New Delhi.
    Planning, groundwork, trials and execution of Easter Promotion 2003 at The Park, New Delhi that achieved record revenue and good press reviews in The Hindu, the Indian Express and the Financial Express.
