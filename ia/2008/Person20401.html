    About The Foundation

    The Baylor Bear Foundation, originally known as the Baylor Bear Club, was established more than 70 years ago with this purpose:

    To provide scholarship funds for Baylor’s student-athletes and to support and promote the interest and welfare of Baylor Athletics.

    The Bear Foundation supports all 18 Baylor sports. It is the primary fund raising organization for Baylor Athletics. A Baylor Bear Foundation membership runs from June 1 through May 31 of each year. The methods of giving can include cash donations (cash or checks), credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express), Bear-A-Matic (monthly bank draw), and Matching Gifts (gifts matched by your employer).
