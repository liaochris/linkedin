Raf Van Puyvelde’s Summary

Innovative, multi-lingual Marketing expert

I have +15 years of marketing and customer management experience in services. Industries I have worked in are: Energy, Financial services, Telecom, IT, Social management and Employee benefits.

My marketing experience is broad, as well on a strategic as on an operational level: Innovation management, Customer management, Segmentation, Lead generation, Market research, Buzz marketing, ... you name it. I have done it all.

I established a knowledge base of tools, methods, market insights and best practices based on my cross-industry experience. This makes me efficient and effective in new projects and challenges.

I am fluent in three languages: Dutch, French and English, which makes me the ideal candidate for multi-cultural and multi-lingual teams.

My shortest CV:

Born in '64, Male, Belgian nationality, Living in Mechelen (Belgium)

I started as a Marketing manager in the Financial industry: Centea (banking & insurance) and Swiss Life (insurance and employee benefits). I worked for customers like: Mobistar, Telenet, SPE, Nuon, ABN Amro, Carrefour, Microsoft, Securex, ... both leading companies and start-ups. I worked mainly in the domains of marketing strategy, new product development, product launches, customer management, loyalty programs, business planning and marketing planning.

My strengths: ambitious leader, innovative power, marketing power, multi-lingual, engaged, thinker and doer.
