Robin Matuk’s Summary

Robin Matuk, founded My Digital Coach to help women reach their true potential by utilizing technology to their full advantage. She is equally comfortable working with beginners and advanced alike, demystifying technology while stimulating creativity and building confidence. Robin began My Digital Coach out of her commitment to bridge the gap between our professional and personal lives and the real and the virtual. She especially loves to work with women to help them remove the obstacles to whatever is holding them back in life.

Robin brings over fifteen years experience as a computer trainer and coach and offers a range of highly accessible and easy-to-learn approaches to computer skills workshops and one-on-one coaching sessions. As a Corporate Trainer at Kaiser Permanente, Robin provided quality training and educational services for all levels of employees who used computer tools in their daily work. In this role, she researched, designed and produced written instructor and student materials; provided classroom instruction for a variety of software products and identified competencies, knowledge and skill gaps, while developing customized training approaches to close those gaps.

Her varied and diverse background combines business, technology and the creative arts to provide practical technological applications with a human touch. Robin has a BA in Fine Arts from Brooklyn College of the City University of New York and additional training in Counseling Psychology, Human Resources Training and Development and Web Design.
