Naill Momani’s Summary

Dr. Al-Momani pursued his B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from The University of Jordan (UJ), Jordan, 1997 and his Ph.D. degree in Engineering Management from George Washington University (GWU), USA in 2002 majoring in Crisis, Risk, and Emergency Management and minoring in Environmental Management. He is among few graduates in this field in the Arab world and he has appeared on several TV stations to comment about crisis, risk and environmental management issues among which Dubai-TV and Beati-TV. Dr. Al-Momani is Professional Member with American Society for Safety Engineers (ASSE) and he is recognized as HSE expert and has been invited to attend and provide expert judgment at national and international scales. During the last decade Dr. Al-Momani has participated in different activities from which he acquired immense experience in management and leadership training. Dr. Al-Momani has several publications in management issues and he has enriched the Arabic Libraries with the first reference in Crises and Disasters Management written in Arabic.
