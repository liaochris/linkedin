Terena Bell’s Summary

Terena Bell is the CEO of In Every Language, a language services provider based in Louisville, Ky USA. She started the company in August of 2005; a year later In Every Language was a globally functioning business with linguists working in 70 languages and in 60 countries.

In Every Language currently offers written translation of documents, websites, software, and other materials. Telephone and on-site interpreting are also available, as well as multilingual desktop publishing.

translating, interpreting, multicultural/multilingual affairs
