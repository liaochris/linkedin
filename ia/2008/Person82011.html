Jacob Shama’s Summary

Leader, entrepreneurial, with 20 years of experience in the fields of communications and information technology, with 10 years of experience in executive-level management.

Joined modu on June 2007 as Vice President for value added products, responsible for modu eco-system products design & development and for business development with strategic partners.

Today, Vice President for Operations at modu, heading company programs management, application engineering, engineering, manufacturing, Far-east partners and supply chain.

Prior to modu served with the Israel Defense Force, where attaining the rank of Colonel and gained over twenty years of experience in the fields of communications and information technology. Most recent position was as Head of a leading R&D Technology Center within the IDF.

Holds BSc and MSc in Electrical Engineering from Tel Aviv University, and
DSc from George Washington University, Washington DC.
