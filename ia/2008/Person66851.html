Gabren King’s Summary

Consultative, dynamic, high-performance executive with a proven ability to align and infuse core processes, technologies and human capital serving a ‘best in class’ customer service focus while increasing productivity, improving proficiencies and increasing profitability. A history of setting and achieving high sales objectives as well as developing successful training programs, marketing plans and strategies in order to affect immediate and long term success. A record of leadership, consistent sales growth and record profit contribution for more than 10 years. Highly successful at the art of recruiting, building and leading top-flight sales and management teams with an aggressive drive for results. Broad industry experience with special expertise in: process improvement, employee recruitment, sales force development, incentive programs, employee retention, management team development and revenue generation. A natural leader with consistent, proven ability to hit the ground running, inspiring others and producing exceptional results within a short period of time.

Throughout my career I have been involved in upgrading sales organizations through training, compensation structures, business planning, staffing, job description formulation, process re-engineering, restructuring of sales foundations and the overhaul of culture, process and disciplines. My focus has been the implementation of a winning attitude fostered through positive and proactive management in order to achieve a culture of discipline dedicated to the achievement of personal and corporate goals.
