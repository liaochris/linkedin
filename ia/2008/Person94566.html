John Cole Scott, CFS’s Summary

John is in charge of business development and operations for CEF Advisors. He serves on CEFA's Investment Committee. John holds the Series 7, 31, & 66 FINRA Licenses. In 2002 earned the Certified Fund Specialist designation (CFS).

For almost 8 years John has specialized in fund analysis: closed-end (CEF), exchange-traded (ETF) open-end (mutual) and managed futures funds.

He helps people with the management of most investment accounts including current qualified plans like 401(k)s. He is also a resource for investment strategy and available resources for investors and professionals on an hourly consulting basis.

He has been quoted in Investment News, the Money Section of USA Today and The Richmond Times Dispatch and published in SR Consultant. He has presented at conferences in Atlanta, GA, Charlotte, NC, Boca Raton, FL, Richmond, VA and Washington DC including several keynote addresses. In 2008 John founded CEFA's Closed-End Fund Universe, a comprehensive weekly data service covering the closed-end fund industry.

John is a member of The Richmond Association for Business Economics (RABE), Chairman of The Bull & Bear Club's House Committee, serves on the Investment Committee for The New York State Society of The Cincinnati and is on The Virginia Museum of Fine Arts’ Business Council. He is a past member of Rotary International and Business Networking International (BNI) where he served as an Assistant Director. He was awarded the "Character Counts" award from the Capital Area and Y.M.C.A. in 1999 and "Master of Networking" from Business Networking International in 2004 and 2005.

He has been a board member since 2001 and past co-president of the Richmond W&M Chapter which has received "Outstanding Chapter" for 2003-2005 and 2008. Premier Chapter for 2006-2007. In 2007 he organized The W&M Alumni Club, “96/06” which plans a hospitality tent at homecoming.

John Cole Scott, CFS’s Specialties:

Investment Sales: Global investing using funds both equity (stocks), debt/fixed income (bond) and both domestic & international real estate (REITs). Closed-end fund specialist. Managed Futures Funds, CEF & ETF Data. Mutual fund and exchange traded fund analysis. Alternative Investment Strategies.
