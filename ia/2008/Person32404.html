Scott Bishop’s Summary

I have over twenty years experience in the IT networking industry and have been focused in the Content Delivery Networking (CDN) industry for almost 10 years now.

I am currently responsible for Sales in Eastern North America (East of the Mississippi in the US and Eastern Canada) for CDNetworks.

We are ranked as the #1 CDN in Asia Pacific, the #2 CDN globally based on traffic, and the #3 CDN based on revenue. We are a relative unknown in North American, who's been around for 8 years, has a tried and true, proven technology. Tier 1 Research says we are one of the top 3 CDN's in the market today. Tier 1 expects the top 3 to combine for well over 79% market share by the end of 2008. Our December 2007 investment of $96.5M from Oak, Shinhan and Goldman Sachs should continue to make us a force to be reckoned with...

Prior to CDNetworks, I was a Senior Sales Executive at Limelight Networks selling CDN into Major Accounts. Before that I was a Senior Account Executive at INTERNAP, selling IP optimization products to Fortune 1000 companies.

As Director of Sales at Mirror Image, I led a team of sales professionals focused on the customer base. As Director of Product Marketing, I drove the strategy, marketing and product development for Mirror Image's Web Services, Streaming Video and E-Commerce initiatives.

Prior to Mirror Image, I held domestic and international positions at Nortel Networks, culminating in IP Product Line Manager for the Multi-Media Enterprise Division. Internationally, I served as Bay Networks' Manager of Europe, Middle East, and Africa.

I hold a BS in Computer Science from WPI. I'm a Wellfleet/Bay Networks/Nortel Networks Certified Internet Network Engineer and have spoken at numerous IT conferences in the past.
