Allison Crow’s Summary

I am a licensed REALTOR®, a specialist in new and resale homes, and a marketing expert...and I REALLY enjoy what I do. I am continually not only meeting, but exceeding legal continuing education requirements. Up-to-date and continuous education are a priority for me because it benefits the people I serve. I have a team of exceptional professional vendors and service providers available to all my clients. I hope to be a part of their home buying experience even after the transaction.

As your agent I am here to offer you the best service, look out for your very best interest, and make each and every real estate transaction as smooth as possible. I am a full-time agent. This is my only business and I am proud to offer the highest quality in service, knowledge, and experience as a professional.

My number one objective in making my business successful is making every client I work with extremely happy with my extraordinary service. My clients make my business successful. If it weren’t for them, I would not be able to do what I love.

I am a graduate of The University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor of Arts in Science and a Master of Education in Curriculum and Instruction.
