Ian Jones’s Summary

Crown Capital Global Pte Ltd (CCG) is an exempt Fund Manager with the Monetary Authority of Singapore.

CCG is the administrator of an investment fund that is based on the higher the risk, the higher the returns.

Typically our investors receive between 24% and 36% per annum.

CCG is registered in Singapore and consolidates funds provided by a small group of high net worth Qualified Investors and Companies.

CCG seeks funds from individuals and companies of between $50,000 and US$1 Million. The finds are forwarded to our partners in the United Kingdom for a term of 12 months.

Our UK partner provides Import Trade Finance, bridging the funding gap between purchasing goods and the final sale to the customer.

“We provide the funding against confirmed orders and once completed we share in the transactional profit”

Import Trade Finance is a popular method of funding for companies that have potential for growth but lack the security required for traditional banking facilities.

Import Trade Finance, also known as transactional finance provides funding for companies to pay suppliers for the purchase of finished goods,

Funding is provided against firm customer order or where there is a proven demand for the product.

An investment in Crown Capital Global will provide the investor with an exotic investment providing higher than average returns. Crown Capital Global does not provide an Capital Guarantee.
