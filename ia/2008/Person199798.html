Charlie Wiederhold’s Summary

10+ years of first person shooter development experience.

Associate Producer at Gearbox Software on "Aliens: Colonial Marines".

Associate Producer on Duke Nukem Forever with a focus on the programming and animation teams while working with the Lead Designer, Lead Level Designer, and Art Director. Programmed interactivity systems, tools updates, particle system updates, and gameplay systems. Designed single player levels.

Designed single and multiplayer levels for Sin. Heavy emphasis on scripting (SinScript) with special focus on increasing interactivity.

Worked with small group to do original paper design for FAKK2 and was responsible for all documentation.

Programmed the installer and addon manager for all Sunstorm Interactive build engine addons. Designed single and multiplayer levels for addons for Duke Nukem 3D, Shadow Warrior, and Blood.

Charlie Wiederhold’s Specialties:

Production and management, gameplay design, gameplay programming, tools programming, level design and construction.
