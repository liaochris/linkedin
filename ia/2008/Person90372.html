Jessyca Wallace Frederick’s Summary

Nearly 10 years of quality-oriented user-experience design and strategy with enthusiastic participation in all phases of the product lifecycle, for companies ranging from pre-IPO startups to multi-million dollar dotcoms in consumer and business spaces, including ecommerce. Skills include:

- Aligning User Experience work with business goals
- Driving creative vision and process
- Leading small production teams
- Cross-functional collaboration
- User profiles, scenarios, tasks, needs
- Interaction design and information architecture
- User research in the lab and in the field
- Defining and improving User Experience processes

Analytical decision maker with excellent problem-solving skills. Proven track record of managing many projects at a time and ability to quickly shift priorities. Recognized for ability to communicate technical and design concepts with all team members and ability to meet deadlines.
