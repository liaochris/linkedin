Mark Howells-Mead’s Summary

I have worked within my chosen areas of photography, media production, design and website development on a daily basis for the past eighteen years, within a wide range of environments; from individual remote freelance work to being a leading team co-ordinator within a large corporate environment.

I am passionate about photography and about the internet as a means of communication, and it is my goal to allow website visitors to gain the information for which they are searching, whilst providing them with a rich and interesting experience along the way.
