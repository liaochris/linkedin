Tim Dolan’s Summary

Columbus Associates is a boutique recruiting firm specializing in the placement of top professionals in Emerging Technologies of software, hardware and professional services.

We become trusted advisors and partners with our clients looking to grow their business by identifying key employee roles and their positive impact to the business. Collectively, our firm leverages years of successful start-up experience growing companies from 5 million up to 100 million dollars, an extensive network, and a proven methodology to systematically reach out to the top performers in the Emerging Technology industry.

We place people in the following roles in both permanent and contracted arrangements: executive management, senior management, sales, pre-sales engineering and sales management, marketing, product management, software development, professional services, project management, finance and general operational and administrative positions.
