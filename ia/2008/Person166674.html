Ryan Paul’s Summary

Ryan Paul is a multidisciplined designer offering fifteen years of hands-on design experience, with expertise in identity design, print and corporate communications.

His solutions have helped clients achieve their goals by shaping brand experience in all forms of communication.

Ryan is currently Design Director at CoreBrand, a firm specializing in branding, design and corporate communications.

Previously, Ryan held positions at DeSola Group, Luxon Carrà, March First (formerly Donovan & Green), Enterprise IG (formerly Anspach Grossman Portugal) and Lippincott Mercer. He has also served as design consultant to Landor Associates, Wolff Olins and Bessen Tully & Lee.

Ryan holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Rhode Island School of Design.

Ryan Paul’s Specialties:

- Graphic Design
- Logos
- Identity programs/visual systems
- Branding
- Brand architecture and nomenclature
- Print communications, brochures
- Identity guidelines
