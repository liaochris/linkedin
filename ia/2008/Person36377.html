Christina Goodwin’s Summary

I have been doing freelance graphic design for several years, most recently for artists and creative business people living in the Northeast, Boston, MA area. It has been a very rewarding experience helping these terrific clients realize their business potential with hard work and great design. My work last summer as a cover designer for Houghton Mifflin was also greatly rewarding because I was challenged to create excellent work in a timely manner with full consideration for the client and their budget concerns. The resulting covers were numerous and of the highest quality.

Recently I've taught myself Flash, and am working as an animator at Boston.com. The work is fast-paced, the people are terrific and the rewards have been wonderful!

To view my work, visit http://www.christinagoodwingraphicdesign.com. I know you will find my portfolio unique and exciting. I have experience with all aspects of design from packaging to web sites to logos to posters and everything in between. My resume speaks for itself. Currently, I am interested in relocating to the New York City area, and can visit for an interview if desired.

Christina Goodwin’s Specialties:

As someone with a degree in Painting who has been designing for several years, clients find I have a unique "fine art" flair to my work. My sense of design is rooted in simplicity, where I let a great brand or idea speak for itself. The classical fine art training I have in the fields of color, composition, and drawing skills, combined with a rigorous work ethic, gives me high technical ability, good taste and attention to details.
