Jeff D'Urso’s Summary

Extensive experience partnering with executives and business owners to convert “good ideas” into fast-growth multi-million dollar businesses. Successes include:

-- Co-founded IT consultancy Open Enterprise Corporation (OEC); built it to $3 million in revenue working with clients including Thomson Financial and State Street, and closed a $10 million acquisition of OEC by C-Bridge / Excelon.
-- Partnered with travel supplier to create business plan for DestinationWeddings.com. Joined founding team as Interim CEO, developing technology infrastructure to manage 150+ specialists, and marketing systems to attract 200+ new client couples each month. The venture ranked #149 on the 2008 INC 500 with revenue growth of 1,504% between 2004 and 2007.
-- Developed concept, business plan, and patent-pending technology for "BlitzTime" - the first social networking event platform that enables users to make new business connections through a series of live, 1-on-1 telephone conversations.

Leveraging deep expertise across technology, marketing, and strategy disciplines; I’ve been able to help startups and Fortune 500 clients alike:

1) translate ideas into major opportunities,
2) get those opportunities launched faster, and
3) make sure the resulting businesses are architected for long term growth.

A passionate strategist, innovator, and technologist, I am always open to hearing new ideas where I can help take a concept to tangible launch within weeks; and then apply my unique brand of "Rapid-Fire Iteration" to ensure the business is poised for long-term growth.

If you've got an interesting idea - don't hesitate to drop me a line...
