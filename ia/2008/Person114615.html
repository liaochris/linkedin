Lisette Howlett’s Summary

AREAS OF EXPERTISE
Lisette Howlett has a track record of achievement spanning 15 years with global Human Resources in blue chip companies and 5 years in local government and the public sector. This includes extensive global HR project and content leadership and internal HR consulting covering UK, U.S., Switzerland, Europe, and Asia.
PROFESSIONAL ACCOMPLISHMENTS
Lisette possesses a unique range and depth in HR - from compensation to organisation development and learning; from new business launch and business transformation to divestment and closure; from new policy development and implementation to individual coaching and mentoring; from recruitment and retention to strategic talent management. She has worked for many years in partnership with numerous Board-level executives, providing coaching, support, challenge, facilitation, ideas, and practical help in strategy development, organisation design, and consequent change programmes. Her experience is complimented by strong intellect, creativity, and commitment to deliver, and underpinned by academic qualifications, Chartered Fellowship of the Institute of Personnel and Development, and membership in the U.S. Society of Human Resource Management.

Lisette is the Managing Consultant/Director of mlh global hr consulting a global strategic business focused human resources consulting business. The unifying theme of her consulting offering is growth and development; of organisations and of the people within them. Implicit in both of these is change and the leadership of change.

Lisette Howlett’s Specialties:

• Business transformation, start-ups, mergers & acquisitions: delivering organisation & people elements;
• Capability development: global programmes, leadership team development, talent management, organisation development, recruitment effectiveness, executive coaching;
• Change leadership: stimulating & sustaining business & individual transformation & growth;
• HR transformation: reviewing, aligning & revitalising HR organisations
• Interim human resources management & project leadership
