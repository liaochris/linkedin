David Lemoine’s Summary

"Stay hungry. Stay foolish." - Steve Jobs' Commencement speech, Stanford University, 2005.

With 8 years of expertise in digital media and 6 years of experience in wireless/mobile entertainment, David Lemoine is a marketer/business developer specializing in fast-paced emerging industries. In 2003 his work was recognized during the Mobile Music Conference in Miami ("Most Popular Content Producer" award).

David Lemoine started his career as Promotions Manager when he joined Aucland SA (pan-European online auctions) shortly after inception. In 2001, he joined New York-based Zingy as Head of Marketing and employee #1. In 2004, in the early stages of the take-over by Japanese For-Side, David signed with Hachette-Filipacchi Media - the world's largest magazine publisher - as Director of Product Development in their mobile business unit (now known as Cellfish Mobile). Later on, he led Neocom Multimedia's operations in the US. He currently oversees the Client services dept. (b2c) of Proteus Inc.

David Lemoine has a Master's Degree in Business Management from Dijon University (France) where he graduated Cum Laude.
