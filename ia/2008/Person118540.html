Elli Malki’s Summary

I am an economist, with 20 years experience in various fields. I have a BA and an MA in ecnomics from the Hebrew University. I have served as a Deputy Director and CFO of JDC in the Former Soviet Union (FSU); I established and managed JDC's real estate company in the FSU; I was a business development manager at Hadasit Medical Services, and was an economist in several governmental positions in Israel.
I also worked as a freelancer consultant and specialized in quantitative models for both the private and public sector.

My newest venture - WWW.INBEST.CO.IL is an Israeli financial website that provides professional assistance for savers and investors to make better decisions.
I am also a lecturer in the Hebrew University in the MA program for Community Leadership & Philanthropy Studies.

Elli Malki’s Specialties:

Rational investment management, budgetary, cost control and measurement models for NGOs, valuation of start-up companies.
