  • Internet & New Media Director/Webmistress

    Bonneville Chicago Radio Group, Chicago IL

    (Privately Held; Broadcast Media industry)

    November 1995 — October 2006 (11 years)

    During my tenure I created and oversaw the Internet department at the Bonneville Chicago Radio Group, starting with a single station, but eventually growing to 4 at one time. Internet revenue grew from zero to over a million dollars annually at a single station while I was there. For a year, I also oversaw and consulted for the Bonneville Phoenix Internet Department, which consisted of three station websites.

    During this time, I also wrote a custom spamfiltering program in PHP that integrated with qmail on the server level and effectively filtered spam for over 200 users. Each user was able to customize the filtering properties via a backend tool, and the program also allowed each user to create a vacation/out of office bounceback message. The software has been subsequently marketed and sold to other companies for their use and has been effectively used up to the present time.
