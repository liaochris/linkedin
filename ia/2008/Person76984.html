Himadri Chatterjee’s Summary

Himadri Chatterjee has worked in the diverse industries across the domains including Consulting, Retail, Pharmaceuticals Operations, has set up large infrastructure and organization structures which are lean yet profitable & efficient in delivering sustainable and competitive advantage to the whole value chain of the organization.

Himadri is currently handling a challenging assignment in the capacity of National Manager- Infrastructure & Facility Management with RR. His current role involves deriving and implementing an efficient, quality oriented and service driven FM organization for Reliance Retail across the multiple retail formats of Reliance Retail which includes complete Supply Chain (from Farm to Store), front ending formats like Reliance Fresh, Reliance Hyper Market, Reliance Digital and other Lifestyle formats and Malls.

His past experience includes working with diverse companies and fields which are leaders in their respective industries. Prior to this assignment he was working as a senior analyst in Deloitte Consulting (US Firms).

He is a management graduate (MBA) from Newport Business School, University of Wales, UK and his professional degree is in pharmacy (B.Pharm). He also is a diploma holder in Advanced Management & Leadership Courses from HBS (Harvard Business School) and Green Belt in Six Sigma (DMAIC).

Himadri appreciates the academic leaders like Sumanta Ghoshal, CK Prahlad and Prof. Eve Kelly, London School of Economics for their effective thinking which can be practiced in the real business world. He believes in creativeness of ideas as success mantra not only for the organizations but for individuals in this dynamic and complex business world.

Himadri Chatterjee’s Specialties:

Himadri believes in forming teams which are high performing with collaborative leadership by empowering yet creating interdependency by alignment on purpose. He is deeply task oriented and future focused.

He encourages creativity and innovation. He has been very successful in not only strategizing but ensuring successful execution all through his career.
