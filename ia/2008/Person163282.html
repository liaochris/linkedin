Rock Darlington’s Summary

To sum up my professional goals in a number of words: I want to produce excitingly awesome contextually relevant digital creative for my brands. As a producer I have a mantra I follow everyday when I work. Make S**t Happen. It's my job to Make S**t Happen for my clients no matter what the budget, no matter what the client, no matter what team I'm working with. I want to take the project, engross myself in every crevasse. From scoping the project, to managing the project, to finding cheap solutions in tight budgets, to launching the site (ON TIME!). I want to see each project from concept to launch. Errors are hard to come by on my projects because there is no room for them. I want to make the Producer roll a pillar in the company. I believe there's no other person more important than that of the Producer. Not one single person knows more about a project than the producer and I want to see that it's an integral position.

To sum up my professional experiences in three words: Made S**t Happen. I've spent the last three years learning every aspect of the digital world from media planning to project management to producing. I understand everyone's roles and responsibilities and have gained more knowledge in doing so. I've Made S**t Happen for clients such as PetSmart, Wamu, Sara Lee, Jell-O, Cool Whip, Lunchables, Kraft Singles, Kraft Mac & Cheese, H&R Block, and Lego.

I've won one major award (Bronze Cannes for Wamu: Trappedbanker.com). I want to win hundreds more.
