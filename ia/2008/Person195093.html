  • Director for Physics

    Ministry of Science

    (Government Administration industry)

    1996 — Present (12 years)

    Responsible for defining, implementing and directing national programs for scientific research in Optronics, Lasers, Internet technologies and Microelectronics. The Optronics Program was instrumental in spawning nearly fifty companies during eight years that succeeded to raise over 1 Billion dollars! Managed a portfolio of 54 research projects involving over 150 scientists, which had over 40% success rate in terms of technology transfer to industry and has lead to the development of a very solid base of novel technologies in Optronics and Microelectronics in Israel. Was responsible for introducing both Internet and Internet-2 to Israel by brokering the agreement with the US NSF, and set up the first non academic ISP for government and industry in 1987. More recently, responsible for the Homeland Security Technologies as well as the Nanotechnology at the Ministry.
