Ralf was raised and educated in Germany and Hong Kong, with his secondary schooling at the German Swiss International School.

After earning a Graduate Diploma from the Steinberger Hotel School in Bad Reichenhall, Germany, Ralf consolidated his training with a three-year stint at the world renowned Hotel Vier Jahrezeiten, rising from Food and Beverage trainee to Executive Assistant Manager – Front of House, during his tenure. This was followed by a one-year post at Westin International Europe, also in Hamburg, where Ralf honed his financial and project skills as well as instigating his long-term interest in procurement structures and supplier negotiations.

Following his return to Asia, Ralf rose through the ranks to senior corporate executive and consulting positions at blue chip companies including Mandarin Oriental Hotel Group, Air New Zealand and The Peninsula Hotels. His specialty was primarily procurement, whilst also developing his skills as a team leader, a strategist and business analyst.

A highly motivated and positive individual, Ralf’s core passion for motivating and leading both individuals and teams is complemented by his commitment to achieving results with a passion for excellence. He is an exceptional communicator who is both knowledgeable and diligent, whilst operating with both integrity and purpose is a pre-requisite in every area of both is professional and personal life.

He spends his private time either on horseback or pursuing his hobby as Webmaster for a selection of industry related websites. He commutes to work on his motorcycle, the ownership of which is a long-standing passion.
