Earl Buchan’s Summary

Earl Buchan is an inspirational leader that believes in the combination of Character, Chemistry, and Competence for the foundations of any team. Prior to working with Greenwood Nursery Earl started a successful internet design company in 1998 that has been rated as one of the top 50 internet services companies in Canada. Earl contributes regularly to west coast publications as a writer for internet technologies.

Earl has assisted over a dozen major tourism associations establish themselves on the internet. The Resort Municipality of Whistler contracted Earl's company to develop an online interactive mapping application that not only tied into their community website, but included a state of the art kiosk application located throughout the village. Passionate about the internet, and bringing communities together, he has published over 80 websites and has been a consultant for small businesses and major international energy companies like British Petroleum.

With a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce and a strong background in non-profit and volunteerism Earl has extensive leadership training and experience in Canada, The United States, and Mexico.
