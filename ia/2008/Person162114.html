Robert Gougaloff’s Summary

Robert Gougaloff is an experienced dental professional now specializing in the field of Implant Dentistry and Laser Dentistry. He has extensive knowledge in all surgical aspects of Implant Dentistry, including associated grafting procedures, as well as the prosthetic re-construction of dental implants. In addition, he has considerable knowledge of Oral Laser Surgery, Laser Therapy, Neuromuscular Dentistry, as well as surgical and therapeutic applications of lasers in dentistry. He has extensive teaching experience at a university and private level. He is a published author and has assisted and approved many research projects required of residents at the university level. He has excellent communication and cross-cultural skills, speaking two languages fluently.

Robert Gougaloff is currently owner and president of Gemini Dental Enterprises, Inc. A corporation serving as a contracting platform for various healthcare related activities. Prior to establishing Gemini Dental Enterprises, Inc., he worked in a variety of management and individual contributor positions at companies including Loma Linda University, Turnbull, Cardona & Gougaloff and the University of Southern California. He is currently working as an independent contractor at Redondo Beach Dental Group and is a clinical and research advisor to USA Laser Biotech, Inc.

Robert Gougaloff is a graduate of UCLA, earning his Bachelors degree in Life Sciences. He also earned his Doctorate in Dentistry from Tufts University School of Dental Medicine. He then completed a three-year full-time Residency at Loma Linda University at the Center for Oral Implantology, earning a certificate specialty degree in Oral Implantology. Additionally, he has obtained specialty certifications in Microsurgery and Laser Surgery for Oral Applications. He has also General Anesthesia training and obtained a certificate in IV sedation at Loma Linda University.

Robert Gougaloff’s Specialties:

Implant Dentistry or Oral Implantology, Laser Dentistry, Microsurgery, General (IV) Sedation, Research
