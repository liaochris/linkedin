Andrew Steele’s Summary

Andrew Steele is a dynamic, growth-focused executive and leader who not only brings a critical blend of strategic vision, work ethic, business acumen and technical aptitude, but also valuable international experience and perspective necessary for success in the worldwide marketplace. He has established an exemplary track record leading business & corporate development, sales and marketing organizations in the digital media technology, consumer web media & advertising and consumer electronics sectors. A veteran of both start-ups and Fortune 500 companies, Steele has not only helped orchestrate significant start-up funding, but also several successful acquisitions and strategic turn-arounds.

Andrew Steele’s Specialties:

• Natural leader with demonstrated ability to build, motivate and mentor successful organizations

• Demonstrated track record in raising early-stage financing and navigating strategic acquisitions
• Deep DNA in digital media technology, consumer web media & advertising and consumer electronics
• Broad international experience and network in the Americas, EMEA and APAC regions
