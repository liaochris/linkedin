Jamie Riddell’s Summary

One of the most experienced and innovative digital media strategists in the UK.

Jamie set up Digital Media Divisions for London Agencies The Media Business (now Mediacom), EHS and Grey Interactive between 1996 and 1999 before leaving to found Cheeze Ltd. in December 1999.

From the initial £5000 investment, Jamie [as one part of the original management] grew the agency to deliver £12m billing in 2006, with 30 staff in 3 offices around the UK.

2007 saw the agency's acquisition by the Digital Marketing Group PLC.

Jamie now consults for clients on various aspects of web marketing from advanced SEO strategies to content lead social media strategies. A constant digital innovator, Jamie is always at the forefront of trying new technologies, sites or gadgets to ensure his clients are always at the cutting edge.

A regular speaker and commentator of web 2.0, Jamie writes sporadically on his own blog, (www.jamieriddell.com) as well as the Cheeze agency blog (www.cheeze.com/news) and over at www.chinwag.com.

Jamie lives with his family, ducks, chickens and guinea pigs in Suffolk.
