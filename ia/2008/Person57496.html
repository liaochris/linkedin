Natalie Jobity’s Summary

Are you one of the many women who say "I have nothing to wear!" Do you spend (perhaps too much) money on clothes, shoes or accessories but still dread getting dressed? Does shopping for clothes create anxiety? Do you ever wish you had a plan and a purpose for your wardrobe or an image that was effortlessly pulled together? Élan Image Management can help you define or refine your look and translate it into a new and improved image, branded with your unique signature style which will position you for the success you desire.

Every woman, no matter her age, life-stage, race, career aspirations, size or shape deserves to have her own signature look, her own personal style trademark. And at Élan Image Management, that is exactly what we are here to do for you. We are based in the Maryland/Washington DC metro area, but don’t worry, geography is not a barrier as we also provide virtual consultations to clients worldwide. We are truly a full-service image consulting firm.

Natalie Jobity, President and CEO, is passionate about helping women look fabulous, and loves to incorporate color and accessories in her own personal style. She has an active blog on her website: www.elanimagemanagement.com and writes a monthly newsletter on image, fashion and personal branding topics.

Élan Image Management specializes in:
• image consultations
• coaching on ‘personal branding’
• personal shopping
• color and personal style assessment
• styling clients for special events
• wardrobe audits
• styling models for photo shoots or fashion shows

Natalie is certified by the prestigious AICI. She has also received styling and image training from the Image Resource Center of NY. Natalie’s formal education includes a B.S. (magna cum laude) in Accounting and Economics from Rutgers University, a Masters in Finance from Cambridge University and an MBA in Marketing from Baruch College, CUNY.
