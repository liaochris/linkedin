Catherine J. MacIvor’s Summary

Substantial expertise in complex litigation and appellate law with an almost unparalleled winning track record.

Innovative defense of class actions, business disputes, Unfair Trade Practices and Americans with Disabilities Act claims in state and federal courts.

Emphasis on preemptive legal support to clients to minimize exposure to costly litigation, including serving as outside general counsel, development of in-house training programs and risk management strategies.
