Curtis Greve’s Summary

In 2008, Metreks was started with the goal of providing services to companies that could benefit from the collective experiences of our team. The Metreks team has over 100 years of experience dealing with employees, managers, customers, unions, operations, contracts, strategic planning, better practices, improve sales, and helping teams improve a little every day.

In short, Metreks is a team of gray haired leaders who are specialists in labor relations, outsourcing, operations and strategic planning.

From 1994 through 2008, I was a member of the senior staff of Genco and was responsible for multiple business units and departments. Genco is a leading 3rd party logistics provider of supply chain management solutions to retailers and manufacturers. Prior to Genco. I worked for Wal-Mart in various roles over 11 years, the last four of which I was responsible for all reverse logistics.

Throughout my career I have received extensive training and experience in the services Metreks provides. I've been lucky to have worked directly for some of the leading business executives in the world, including Lee Scott, Jim Kent and Rollin Ford at Wal-Mart; and Herb Shear, Jerry Davis, and Gus Pagonis at Genco.

My passion is helping others achieve their goals and improving their profitability.
