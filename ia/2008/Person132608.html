Mike Seals’s Summary

Specialized division that installs Airfield Lighting for Approaches, Runways, Taxiways, etc., and for the maintenance and service of lighting systems after installation. Provide full service sponsorship to foreign companies operating in Kuwait including Visa acquisition, Residency, Driver's licenses, and Government Health Insurance Cards.
