Why you need an expert resume writer - and Why you need a Brand!

The Executive jobs market is  highly competitive, in order for you to secure interviews your resume needs to be first-rate; it must be visually appealing, brand who you are, market you and speak to your future. It must also possess a tremendous level of professionalism. Your resume must address the assumption that, in all likelihood, the person who will be reading your resume is an executive recruiter or HR specialist. Branding you is vital to all resumes and job hunters these days, defining who you truly are.
