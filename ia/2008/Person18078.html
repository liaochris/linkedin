Atul Rai’s Summary

I have been working in the space of Capability Management, as an umbrella concept, for large part of my career including stints as an instructor, courseware and curriculum designer, knowledge facilitator and formal Knowledge Management. I have been working on developing strategies for enabling clients to leverage knowledge attained through trainings, to build the required competency base across the organization.

I have extensive expertise in Knowledge Management and interest in Social Computing, and the way business is being transformed by technology with deep insights into leveraging knowledge to drive excellence. In short, i focus sharply on the people aspect of Knowledge Management, the "KM 2.0", in contrast to the content-centric "KM 1.0".

I am very good at mentoring, training, and coaching people in organizations. My core strength is developing expertise, and presenting complex ideas in terms and ways which are simple for everyone to understand. I get to an understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of my colleagues, and leverage these to drive them towards excellence. In other words, a highly capable trainer, and an effective coach.

Atul Rai’s Specialties:

Knowledge Management, Social Computing, Web 2.0, Training Strategy, Training Needs Analysis, Supply Chain Management, Customer Relationship Management
