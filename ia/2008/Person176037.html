Silje Standal’s Summary

Creativity has always been a huge part of my life. Early age experience of drawing, music and dance are some of the things which I believe have shaped my personality and also my goals in life.

I am a 28 year old Norwegian, looking to work in magazine design or a closely related publishing role.

For the past 6 years, I have been studying and working in the media industry, obtaining my degree in Media and Communication Science from Växjö university, Sweden, and a diploma from Hyper Island, School of New Media Design. As part of my studies at Hyper Island, I was responsible for various projects with real clients.

Towards the end of my education at Hyper Island I worked at Hyperlaunch New Media Ltd in Bristol and Ground Zero Underground Music Magazine, Copenhagen, Denmark. After ending my internship at Ground Zero, I was offered a full time job as Editorial & Design Manager. I continued working there until September 2005.

My latest job was project manager for the redesign of the Lavasoft corporate website, makers of the Ad-Aware application.

My website www.girlrestricted.com contains more detailed information about my achievements and access to references.
