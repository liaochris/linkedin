Arvind Gupta’s Summary

Experienced Product and General Management executive with deep experience in Mobile, Internet – Advertising, Search and Commerce/Payments, Enterprise Middleware and Applications, and, Multimedia spaces. Have clearly demonstrated success across a range of complex product management and development, business incubation, go-to-market, operations and team building challenges. Strong track record of mobilizing and working with executive leadership, and, coordinating, collaborating and winning buy-in from core stakeholders, in planning, developing and bringing new products to market.

Led development and launch of two Mobile Advertising Platforms – Mobile Panama, 2008; Mobile Resource Center, 2006.

I have been working on emerging technologies, as a product manager, management consultant and software engineer since 1999. These have been strategic investment areas for these companies. I have a strong track record of delivery and working with senior executives. My experience has allowed me to develop a fundamental understanding for how markets work and how money is made across the entire ecosystem of product and support.

In launching two Mobile Advertising platforms at Y! and Mobile Application Server at Oracle, I have led every aspect of the business and product - from making the business and investment case, to putting the product plan together, getting resources, finding and structuring the marketing, content development, editorial, product and sales support effort and team. At each stage of my job I have led the strategic analysis and planning for a case (as a management consultant) and then for products (as a product manager) and have put together implementable plans and structure resources and the project to meet the deliverables.

I enjoy all aspect of the product, development and GTM lifecycle, and, the way I approach my responsibilities properly reflects strong ethics, judgment and leadership.
