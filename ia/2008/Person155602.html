Rahul Sandil’s Summary

Rahul Sandil, Vice President - Gameshastra Inc., brings with him a breadth of experience and leadership in sales and customer management. Most recently, Rahul was Senior Manager and Client Partner at HCL America, a 4 billion dollar technology outsourcing giant. In this position, Rahul was instrumental in achieving a phenomenal five fold growth in revenue and managed a portfolio of accounts worth usd 100 million. Before that Rahul was senior director at a technology start up, Net Matrix Solutions, which grew their solutions practice from nil to usd 15 million under his leadership in under a year.
Rahul started his career with EMI (HMV) India spearheading sales and licensing for Ethnic Indian Music in Asia Pacific, EMEA and North America. He went on to manage the North America operations for EMI's subsidiary Saregama Plc. During this time, he also carved out an entire new business in North America in licensing Indian Music to mainstream labels for creating new genres like the highly popular Bhangra Rap, Neo Indian Classical and Indo Fusion.
Rahul Sandil holds a bachelors degree in economics from St. Xavier’s College and an MBA from Management Development Institute, both premier academic institutions in India. He enjoys Gaming, Golf, Collecting Music, Movies and Travel. He is married and has recently moved to Southern California with his wife, Mita, who is a school teacher.
