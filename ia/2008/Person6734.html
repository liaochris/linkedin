Alex Goldie, Realtor®’s Summary

Real Estate Broker Associate/Realtor in Baird & Warner's St. Charles, IL Office. Designated as a Senior Real Estate Specialist (SRES), and an Accredited Buyers Representative (ABR) by the National Association of Realtors' Real Estate Buyer Agency Council (REBAC). In 2006, member of Vice President's Club for Sales Volume, and Top 5% for Total Transactions.

Alex Goldie, Realtor®’s Specialties:

Typical clients are seasoned home owners. Having typically owned homes for the past 10 to 30 years, they are ready to transition into homes that better suit their present lifestyles, and their future needs.

Also experienced in working with first time home buyers, luxury properties, new construction, condo's, and investment properties. Being based out of St. Charles allows me to service home buyers and sellers throughout the West and Northwest suburbs of Chicagoland.

    Quite simply, I help people buy, sell and lease Real Estate. Typical clients for me have owned homes for the past 10-30 years, and are looking for a new residence to better suit their present lifestyles, as well as their future needs. Three factors to consider when evaluating a Realtor® are service, value, and technology.

    Prompt, clear, and concise communications are the cornerstone of the relationships I build with clients. I work hard to add value to my clients' sales and purchases through tremendous efforts of marketing their home, vigilent knowledge of the constantly changing local marketplace, carefully crafted negotiations, and extensive due dilligence from the time of contract to closing. I utilize technology at a level above and beyond other real estate practitioners- I believe that every aspect of business can be improved as technology is effectively integrated, so long as measure is taken not to lose touch with the people represented.
