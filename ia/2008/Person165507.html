    Award-winning Feature Film and Commercial Director (Reel Available Upon Request)

    RT is the co-writer, producer and director of the bleak independent feature film THE GOOD THIEF (USA / 35mm / 114-mins / Dolby SR) that was shot in Philadelphia. RT toured it around the country at various film festivals and private screenings. Via Los Angeles distributor Rogue Arts, RT's film can now be found on NetFlix, Amazon, Blockbuster, TLA Video, etc.

    "RT Herwig's debut feature THE GOOD THIEF is a haunting and very atmospheric piece of cinema, wherein darkness rules not only the streets, but also the hearts of the people living within the shadows. This terrifically, well-crafted film, features excellent performances by its cast, a very effective score and beautiful cinematography. A stunning testament to independent filmmaking." - Philadelphia Film Festival

    RT is currently developing his epic WWII drama THE RED CHURCH with screenwriter collaborator Jerry Jennings.
