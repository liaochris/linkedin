Dominique R. Antonini’s Summary

Dominique R. Antonini is currently Director, Business Development, of a new consultancy company based at Geneva, Switzerland specialized in Aviation Security and Facilitation projects (R&D, audits/inspections, training/e-learning, exercises, ...)

Prior to his new assignment, he was Chief, Aviation Security (AVSEC) and Facilitation (FAL) Specifications and Guidance Material (SGM) Section at the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO).

Prior joining ICAO, Mr. Antonini was working in the AVSEC field in Research and Development programmes, national inspections, equipment and airport design issues in the French Civil Aviation Authority.

Mr. Antonini holds a Degree in Civil Aviation Engineering, Air Transport, from the Ecole Nationale de l’Aviation Civile (ENAC) in Toulouse, France (promotion-1987) after high level mathematical studies.
