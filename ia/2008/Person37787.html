Sanchita Saha’s Summary

Sanchita studied BSc. Maths with Business at UCL, and upon graduating (2:1) she chose to pursue her ambition to work in broadcasting, enjoying a 7 year career at the BBC, mainly in radio, entering Radio 1 in 2000 for 3 years, then working with 8 others to set up and launch the BBC's digital black music station 1Xtra.

Sanchita relished this challenge and 2 years after the station's launch in Aug '02, she left with a desire to "create something big", and build a business that would perform well financially and also make a positive social impact.

She began testing her idea for an events-based social networking service and community for young professionals relocating to UK cities, seeking to expand their local social networks and explore their local social scene.

Her unique vision was to support users' offline social experience with online social networking tools and, with a Prince's Trust loan, Sanchita built a test site, and funded the project through 16 months R&D, during which she was nominated as a regional finalist in the Shell Livewire Entrepreneur Awards '05.

Sanchita raised some private investment to build v1. site, CitySocialising.com. Launching in Jan '07, it quickly became London's leading social events community and network for professionals moving to, living and working in, the Capital.

It became profitable within 4 months and financed the expansion of CitySocialising to Leeds, Manchester, Nottingham, Bristol and Brighton.

With no marketing or advertising undertaken: Monthly revenues have increased by 950%, business has shown average 42% quarterly growth every quarter, currently operates with a 35% net profit margin, employs 7 part-time staff.

Sanchita is seeking major investment to build and market an extensively developed v2. website and significantly and quickly grow the business in the UK & Europe.

Sanchita was recently nominated as a national finalist in the prestigious Prowess Women in Business Awards 2008.
