  • Founder & Chief Technical Officer

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    April 2002 — December 2007 (5 years 9 months)

    I founded this company with Mark Hall to develop an off-the-shelf control system called Helm. The product was an instant success and now manages thousands of hosting companies all over the world. I sit between the backend and front line, managing the product team, dealing with customers daily and presenting at industry events to consumers and partners. Helm was the first ever off-the-shelf, multi-server control system, and the latest version (Version 4) is also the first class 3 system to also support a modular architecture, as well as being architected to become the first ever class 4 system in the world. The system was architected, designed, developed and released within a project span of just 2 years including embeded modular server management systems, provisioning systems, billing systems, domain registration systems and licensing/distribution systems with a development team of just 4 very talented guys.

    The company and product was aquired by SWSoft Inc in December 2007.
