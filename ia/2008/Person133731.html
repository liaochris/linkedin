Mitch Muroff’s Summary

Senior management consultant specializing in e-commerce payments strategy with extensive experience managing fraud risks and payment operations for multi-national, online e-commerce/internet businesses. Deep experience in development of teams, systems, and processes for monitoring, analysis, identification, mitigation, and control of complex fraud risks associated with online acceptance of credit card payments and non-financial fraud/abuse. Unique combination of strengths in both business (strategy, leadership, matrix management, project management, business analysis, communication, process development) and technology (web/internet technologies, software design, database design, data analysis). A team player who can be trusted to deliver on objectives by executing with diligence and enthusiasm. BA (Finance & Economics); MBA (Management & Marketing).

Mitch Muroff’s Specialties:

e-commerce fraud risk management, e-commerce payment processing, user interface design, business process analysis and design, global e-commerce (japan, korea, australia, europe)
