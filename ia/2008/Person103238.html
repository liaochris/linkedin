    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Medical Devices industry)

    2003 — 2005 (2 years)

    CCS Medical is one of the largest direct-to-consumer providers of diabetic, respiratory, ostomy supplies and prescription medications in the US. The company provides home-delivery of products from the healthcare industry's leading manufacturers such as Bayer, Roche and Johnson & Johnson.

    My role with CCS Medical required me to build a dynamic and results-oriented recruitment program from the ground-up. The company acquired 4 smaller medical-supply companies during my tenure and I was responsible for directing internal and external recruitment for CCS Medical and it's new subsidiaries. I supervised 3 recruiters and 1 HR Administrator and oversaw the hiring of close to 400 new employees during the time I was with CCS Medical. I was also responsible for training hiring managers interviewing techniques and legal considerations in interviewing and hiring.
