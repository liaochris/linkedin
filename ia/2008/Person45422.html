J. David Curlee’s Summary

I began my career in 1994 as a contract technical support specialist with Microsoft Corporation. My experience at Microsoft caught the eye of a manager at Dell Computer Corporation, who asked me to work for them and help start their software support division.

In the fall of 1995, I relocated to Austin, Texas and began my career at Dell. We launched the software support division and grew it successfully for about two years. For the remainder of my tenure at Dell, I grew my technical expertise with computer hardware and customer service.

In 1998 I was asked to film a short documentary in Romania. This documentary lead to other productions and in April of 2000 I decided it was time to launch my production company and grow it on a full-time basis.

Over the next two years, I produced numerous videos, print pieces and websites both corporate and non-profit. As my work with one client grew and became the vast majority of my production work, they decided to hire me as their Director of Technology and Art Director.

I have been working for Equal Health & Life since 2002 and to say that I’ve “done it all” seems to be an understatement. I developed their networking and telephone infrastructures. We took their sales / CC software from a glorified contact manager to a full web application capable of handling thousands of products and millions of clients.

I helped them create a corporate identity that we took through several iterations and campaigns until we arrived at the perfect brand which we have been marketing ever since. As the company grows many technical and marketing challenges have arisen that we have been able to attack and overcome with great success. As our brand increased and our products changed, our websites followed right along without missing a beat.

For the past thirteen years, I’ve grown a vast knowledge base in many technical, marketing and creative fields. I'm now looking for the next GREAT job and challenge.
