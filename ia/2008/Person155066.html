Rachel Toledo-Miller’s Summary

Integrated communications professional with more than 10 years experience in Public Relations, Community Outreach, Advertising and Marketing. Bilingual, Spanish and English.

I’ve worked in Communications in a number of industries including high-end real estate, finance and financial planning, higher education, telecommunications, and not-for-profit (museums and historic icons). Each position has been a new adventure, and I love applying my Communications expertise to each industry.

I look forward to taking on healthcare or gaming someday, as those are growing industries; but I’m open to anything. I want to work within an organization with a brand image and corporate culture I respect and admire, with a leadership philosophy I can believe in and follow, or even be a part of.

*** Fluent/Native in Spanish and English: Speak, Read and Write Conversational and Formal (Business) Spanish and English ***
