Sue Woodward’s Summary

Sue Woodward combines the rare ability of strategy conceptualisation, development and implementation. Sue has sound organisational skills, is able to take a brief and see it through to completion. Sue has the confidence to question the status quo and suggest alternative ways to achieve goals. By listening, researching and taking an interest in the business, Sue gains the confidence of senior management working with them to qualify and prioritise business objectives and implement them to achieve outcomes that build a company's credibility and market positioning. She is known as an energetic, fair, and respected manager.

In all her roles she has been charged by senior management to establish a marketing function for the firm. Along the way she has often repositioned a firm's standings in their industry segment for greater credibility and heightened positive profiling. Sue has moved each firm's thinking of marketing from purely hospitality/PR functions to discplined and strategic client and industry development functions. She has demonstrated the value a strategic marketing and communications function can play through revenue growth, client retention, staff attraction, profile within the industry and often outside the industry.

In all roles Sue has been an integral member of the leadership team, including: Andersens, Knight Frank and more recently Deacons.

Sue is currently a board member of Tennis NSW.
