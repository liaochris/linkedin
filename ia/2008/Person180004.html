Stefan Ilkovics’s Summary

Before being the creative director at Publicis Net (Dior, Yves Saint Laurent, Armani Beauty..), Stefan held the same position at Razorfish Paris (DUKE) where he most notably spearheaded the Playstation account and worked on the initial Nike ACG team.

In Parallel to his agency career, Stefan has also taken part in various other projects over the last 10 years, like co-founding Change is Good a holistic atelier, or more Recently STROKE.

With projects ( like Chanel, Charles Jourdan,..) taking him from Tokyo to New York, even the United Emirates, Stefan has specialised in fashion online, particularly in their ecommerce. His Collaboration since 2003 with jean touitou’s A.P.C. brand illustrates well this expertise. An expertise also compiled in a masters thesis on brand experience in ecommerce

Stefan had the privilege of studying Strategy with Michael Porter at Harvard Business School, as the finish touches to a business education started at ESCP-EAP ( Marketing Masters degree) and continued at Solvay’s Business school
entrepreneurship program.
