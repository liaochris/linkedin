Cara Hill’s Summary

Stylistically versatile, I have been a successful solo artist in both Seattle and Los Angeles.

For the past 26 years, I have run my own business which has been based almost entirely on referrals. I thrive on the details of providing excellent customer service, and have earned endorsements from the National Association of Catering Executives and Club Managers Association of America, The Knot.com, Citysearch and Yelp.com.

Sample clients include: Maestro Gerard Schwarz/Seattle Symphony, Boeing Corporation, British Consulate General, Bruce and Craig McCaw, Staubach Company, and Immunex. I maintain close communication with clients, agencies and media, reviewers and producers throughout the Pacific Northwest and California.

Visit my web site to hear audio examples of my work at http://www.carahill.com.
