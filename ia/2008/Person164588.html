Andrew Mitchell’s Summary

Andrew is currently part of the commercialisation team at the University of Edinburgh School of Informatics.

He was the Programme Manager for Enterprisers from 2003 to 2006 (http://www.enterprisers.org.uk); before leaving to spend a year in Australia to grow Enterprisers internationally. Prior to joining the University of Cambridge in November 2003 Andrew was a business development manager for Cambridge-MIT at Adastral Park, a collaboration between the University of Cambridge, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and BTexact Technologies; this role he held as a result of a secondment from BT to the MIT Media Labs in 2001. Andrew has a first class honours degree in Technology and Business from the Robert Gordon University in Aberdeen and has trained as an Architectural Technician. Previous employment includes Accenture, BT plc. and the Scottish Institute for Enterprise.

Andrew's goal is to continue the development of world-class entrepreneurship teaching programmes and work on the interface between university and industry.

Andrew Mitchell’s Specialties:

Programme management, fund raising, entrepreneurship education, technology commercialisation.
