Valentin Pertsiya’s Summary

The best in brands in the world
(please write to me brandaid@mail.ru if you have doubts!)

CEO of BrandAid Group (offices in Kyiv and Moscow).

12 years of experience in advertising, marketing and branding. Valentin has worked in various top managerial positions at advertising agencies Visage (an affiliated company of Euro RSCG Worldwide), Ark / J.W.Thompson, Bates Ukraine, Orlan company (drinks). Since 2000, he’s been a co-owner and CEO of BrandAid company.

Valentin wrote more than 100 articles on branding and marketing in the various mass media of Russia and Ukraine. He’s also the author and coach of the Brand Anatomy workshop and Orange Dragon differentiation workshop. Valentin teaches at the International Marketing Institute (Kyiv), kmbs and Kyiv Business School.
