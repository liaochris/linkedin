Richard Yadon, CPC’s Summary

Executive search professional, career agent, and leadership and employee motivation consultant. Specializing in the health care industry -- search services are focused around managed care executives and senior clinical managers. Our firm offers career agent and career coaching services for these professionals as well and leadership, management, and sales training, coaching, and consulting for their businesses. Also available for speaking engagements and keynote speeches.

Richard Yadon, CPC’s Experience

  • Owner

    Health Career Professionals, LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Hospital & Health Care industry)

    June 2006 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Our firm acts as agents for healthcare professionals and helps them get the best possible position for them personally. Much in the same way agents work with professional athletes, we discreetly and proactively refer you to potential employers after thoroughly getting to know you, your talents and your needs. Since we specialize in healthcare only, we are especially qualified to represent you in the marketplace as opposed to a one-time recruiter who may be a generalist.

    Our service is free to you and certainly beats the traditional way of getting a better position -- we do all the work upfront. We even help prepare you to interview so you will shine above any other candidates. We analyze what YOU want, where you want to work, the money you want, the schedule, the work situation you want and more. From there, we confidentially contact businesses and facilities on your behalf and look for matches to your qualifications and needs.
