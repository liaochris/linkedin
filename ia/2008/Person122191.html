Mark Knowlton’s Summary

  • Principal

    CoderProxy

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    December 2007 — Present (10 months)

    CoderProxy injects "technical" into the term Technical Recruiting. We specialize in recruiting software engineering professionals with an approach that is nearly non-existent in the industry: We conduct granular technical qualification interviews one would expect from a hiring manager, where most of the industry contends with buzzwords. Since we can establish credibility with candidates, we can more effectively recruit through networking and referrals.

    Our competitors will ask a candidate, "How long have been using Java?". We'd ask, "If I said it was possible to have virtual functions in Java, would you agree and, if not, why?". CoderProxy delivers value that competitors unable to ask those question can not; we deliver off-the-radar candidates referred by professionals who respect our approach. We deliver on a contingency and retained basis, and we connect our clients to our network of trusted peers and associates, most of whom would never put their resume on a job board.
