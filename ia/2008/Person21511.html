Benjamin Rouffet’s Summary

I am an Application Engineer at National Instruments France. Providing in-depth technical knowledge relating to NI products in pre-sales and post-sales roles.

During my training period in National Instruments Technical Department, I realized a bread board and a prototype of a Custom NI-CompactRIO Module, it gave me strong skills in LabVIEW programming (Real Time, FPGA, Embedded).

I am graduated in Physics and Material Engineering at INSA (National Institute of Applied Sciences) in Toulouse, one of the top French Engineering Schools specialized in instrumentation, with high level skills on LabVIEW and LabWindows/CVI, Real Time, VISA, Advanced Virtual Instrumentation programming and electronics. My different professional experience gave me a wide range of abilities such as my creativity, my open-mindedness and my communication skills. The sense of thorough, pro-active, tenacity and team motivator developed in my different activities like teaching salsa and maths. Moreover, I am really interested by the management and business engineering; in fact I have some courses about it like International Business Skills and Project Management.
