Lucy Windsor’s Summary

I am an accredited Business and Life Coach specialising in aligning personal goals with the goals of the business. One of my clients, Resolute Communications has been shortlisted for Best Coaching Programme for Coaching For All, that I designed and delivered for them through 2007.

I am a Partner in The Performance Business we offer:

Personal Impact, Presentations and Communication training/coaching
Business Performance Coaching
Roleplay Services

I'm also a professional roleplayer and facilitator. Our roleplayers focus people to communicate clearly.

We represent customers, professionals. members of the public, employees, managers, victims of and those responsible for discrimination – in fact, anyone and everyone.

In essence, we take education at work one step further, by making it real.

Interested in working with:

Organisations who's success is dependent on good, clear communication.
Recruitment, assessment and development companies who want to offer greater value to their clients.

Equally comfortable working with corporate organisations and medium sized companies.

Trust is my most treasured personal value.

Lucy Windsor’s Specialties:

Business Performance Coach, Life Coach, IT Sales experience, Commercial acumen, Professional Roleplayer (business actor), Communication, Creative, Performing Arts, Arts-based training.
