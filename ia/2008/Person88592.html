Jennifer Taylor’s Summary

My career blends strategic and tactical marketing with direct sales. This well-rounded background enables me to provide a strong foundation for my clients. Additionally, the copy I write is then in line with the strategic marketing plans.

I provide marketing consulting as well as copywriting services. From creating business plans for a start-up company to writing white papers, I love my work. Writing is my passion and I love being able to help my clients increase their sales and profits.

Additionally, I also write non-fiction books. The first book published was the history of the Eagle County Airport. I am currently editing a book for teens and compiling real-life stories from those who have suffered the loss of a loved one. A third book is in outline stage right now and is based on our experience of building our own home. I am also available to ghost write. A client from Australia hired me to write a book for him in 2007.

My clients span the globe. For a list of clients, please go to http://www.thewordtailor.com. The list gets updated a few times a year.

If you're looking to improve your marketing communications or need assistance with marketing strategies, let me know.
