Mauro Maich’s Summary

I am looking to help others here on LinkedIn by sharing my network and my expertise. If you see something here in my profile that might help to you, let me know. I would be happy to help.

I started Perfecto Translations in June of 2008 to provide English to Spanish translation services that help bridge the language barrier between Spanish and non Spanish speaking people in the US.
My company specializes in medium size projects, although we also handle small or personal projects. The idea behind Perfecto Translations is to make it easy for every organization to reach Hispanics in the US. We offer a hassle-free process and fast turn-around times, paying special attention to the nuances and subtleties of both Spanish and English languages. All the translators I partner with posses an extensive vocabulary and good sense of style, grammar, and flow. We convey the full message as if it was originally written in Spanish, not just a translation.
I also work for Echostar Corporation doing Quality Development and translation work on a daily basis. I have been with Echostar for 5 years and have helped with customer retention and quality development for the last 4 years.

Why I am here on LinkedIn:
• To network with other active, intelligent and generous B2B networkers.
• Meet insightful others that I may help to be more successful.
• To find business partners where we may work together on projects.
• To learn new things about business and technology from others.
• To have a network of resources at my disposal when I may need them in the future.

I see the power of networking and am looking to meet and develop relationships with others so we may all be more successful and prosper together.
