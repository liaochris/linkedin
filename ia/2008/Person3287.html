Adrian Diaconescu’s Summary

My first job for an important Romanian software development company was a great thing, especially since I was still in school back then. I got the chance to work with people that shared my interests, learn from them and maybe share some of my knowledge with them.

It was the first time I realized the importance of working as a team.

The next step in my career was actually an important one and involved some risks. I started out my own company, in partnership with a couple of friends. I used my past experience as an employee in order to create a pleasant working environment, but also an efficient one. We started with just a couple of passionate people and we built a company that is today a strong content development company. The company grew bigger and things changed. Eventually... I had to leave.

It was the first time that I realized the importance of working with the right team.

Now I try push my technical knowledge a step further and expand my creativity each day. I document myself about things I like: branding, entrepreneurship, online business, graphic design, start-ups, trading, hoping that someday I will change the world!
