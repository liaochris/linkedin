Susan Emmer’s Summary

Susan Emmer's 20+ years of creating and executing 360-degree marketing programs makes her an expert in building brands and developing strategic partnerships. The founder of Pinnacle Partnerships, Susan is recognized as a "connector" who has an extensive list of contacts built after years of developing successful alliances. Pinnacle Partnerships utilizes these relationships to create joint venture marketing programs between complimentary brands. Think "Match.com for business!"

Susan was ahead of the 'green" curve with a B.A. in Environmental Studies from Franklin & Marshall College, and she is thrilled with the renewed focus on sustainability and the environment. Susan is currently providing green marketing and green branding services to some of Pinnacle Partnerships' clients including H3Environmental and Global Green USA.

Susan has displayed an innovative approach to marketing and brand building since she joined the team at PEOPLE magazine in 1994. As PEOPLE's Director of Entertainment Marketing, Susan created integrated marketing programs for advertisers with movie studios, television networks, upscale retailers, non-profits, etc. In addition, Susan managed PEOPLE's high-profile charity arm, where she developed an expertise in special events, sponsorships and corporate philanthropy.

At Time Inc., Susan was a two-time President's Award winner, a prestigious honor of excellence in publishing. During her 11 years at the media giant, she worked for Time International, LIFE magazine, PEOPLE, and Time Inc.'s Corporate Marketing Group.

Before founding Pinnacle Partnerships, Susan worked for the Los Angeles-based Western Interiors and Design magazine as Director of Strategic Alliances. During her 4 years with the start-up, she increased revenue through targeted marketing and circulation partnerships. Susan also developed and produced profitable brand extensions including the Western Interiors Design + Home Show.

Susan Emmer’s Specialties:

Partnership marketing, joint venture marketing, strategic alliances, green marketing, green branding, entertainment marketing, brand building, cause-related marketing, high-profile special events, contract negotiation, sponsorship sales and development, advertising, promotions, public relations, media, audience development, creativity, idea generation.
