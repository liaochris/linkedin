Tony Rogers’s Summary

Innovative, pragmatic, proactive, focused and results oriented. Balancing controls with business needs. An open mind with a corporate perspective. Maximizing effective and efficient controls in Finance, IT and Operations. These are indicative qualities of a professional in Internal Audit and Enterprise Risk Management. Before joining Ernst & Young in SouthEast Europe I was a Partner with Deloitte Moscow; VP Internal Audit MasterCard; Senior Manager at PKF London; Chief Internal Auditor at a Ford subsidiary; and Head of Internal Audit for Mercedes/Perkins in Cape Town.

Tony Rogers’s Specialties:

Leading management in the development and implementation of cost effective internal control and corporate governance in manufacturing, retailing and financial services.
