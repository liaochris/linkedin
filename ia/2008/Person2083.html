Wojciech Swiatkowski’s Summary

We live in a time when businesses and competition grow rapidly. That process causes that today no existing market is big enough and/or safe enough and no business life indefinite. It refers not only to Blue Chips but to all companies, from family business to huge conglomerates , from clothing to IT. The truth is that competition is able to tear away not only niches but also whole chunks of well establishment business. Nothing is constant nowadays. Only one truth hasn’t change– if you want to be a winner you have to posses capabilities to develop your business faster then your competitor does. One of the proven way to do so is expanding your company operations to emerging markets.

I can help you develop your expansion to Central and Eastern Europe, both using greenfield and acquisitions, that will enable your company to achieve higher revenues, profits and thus build it’s shareholder value. I believe that properly designed and managed international expansion process can became a strategic weapon that will make your competitors irrelevant.
