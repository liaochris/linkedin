Anders Tryka’s Summary

Sales Coach is working as Sales Coaches & Business Consultants all over Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA). We help companies grow their business by working close together on their selected key priorities and targets.

In Sales Coach we seek first to understand, then to be understood. We cover 4 key business areas in our business 1. Analysis of people and organizations 2. Workshops & competence development 3. Professional Teambuilding 4. Development projects.

My tasks in Sales Coach include sales and fulfillment of analysis, sales trainings & workshops and 1-on-1 coaching of business professionals.

We work for customers like Hewlett Packard, Knauf Group, EMC, Visma, XP Consult, SAC, IBM, Fujistu Siemens, Bell Micro and many more across the world.

Sales Coach is also the company that has developed the famous sales tool The Resource Model. A tool that is distributed in more than 17 countries on 3 different languages. The tool is used by more than 225 people and has a proven track record of business growth by 400% in average in the companies that use the tool and the mindset behind the tool!

We are 8 people in Sales Coach and have a broad network of consultants connected to the company with specific competences. If you would like to hear more about Sales Coach or if your are interested in working as a Sales Coach or Business Consultant, please don't hesitate to contact us on +45 7026 0520.
