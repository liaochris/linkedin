Andrea Fiore’s Summary

As a freelancer and independent contractor I serve a large metropolitan market, telecommuting with clients all over the US and Canada. To communicate with clients I use the phone (I have a VoIP phone with unlimited US/Canada time), email (sending .jpegs or .pdfs of mockups and design proofs), FTP sites for uploading/downloading files, and through the Internet for putting up live demos of work to date.

Since 1994, as an Iowa State University student, I have worked in all aspects of document transcribing and conversion.

If you need accurate written documents transcribed from court proceedings, interviews, dictation, business meetings, conference calls, focus groups, press briefings, lectures, radio shows, panel discussions, seminars, or any medical-related sources, please contact me.

In 1998, I began coding my own websites, and was quickly recognized for creating carefully crafted designs that appeal to both aesthetic sensibilities and an edgy techno/nouveau flair. I was asked to start designing sites for others. With two degrees in education, I utilize my teaching experience in designing websites and communicating with clients.

If you are looking for affordable, aesthetically toned web sites—from ornate and complex to understated Zen-like professional simplicity in design and production, please write me and we can explore together your vision and needs. I have been designing web sites for 8 years, and have accrued a keen sense for imagery that matches my client's business or personal character.

Together with my partner, James, we also provide a wide range of print and writing/editing services (see complete listing below).

I am an independent contractor seeking a part-time or contract position. Email me at andrea@dolphindesigns.info to discuss your project needs.

Andrea Fiore’s Specialties:

I am versed in Dreamweaver, FrontPage, Photoshop, Illustrator, Quark, InDesign, Flash, Javascript, HTML, DHTML, XHTML, CSS, PHP and the creation of e-commerce websites.
