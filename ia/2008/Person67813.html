Gary Steinman’s Summary

Editor/publisher who cares as much about vision as bottom-line profitability. Extensive experience in all aspects of genre entertainment, including video games, anime, manga, TV, movies, more. Success in rebuilding and leading teams, redesigning magazines and other editorial products, and launching new ventures (including the launch of PiQ Magazine). Extensive experience running licensed products and balancing the needs of all stakeholders, inlcuding the licensors, advertisers, retail partners and, most important, readers.
