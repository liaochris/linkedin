Claude Martin Rainaud’s Summary

More than 40 years of international experience in Educational management, resources and systems design and development.
From documentary and educational movies in the 60s to educational and training Internet-based applications today, I came through educational television, video, audio, videodiscs, textbooks and CDrom multimedia e-Learning programes and Education management systems design, development and implementation.
Since ten years ago I emphasise promotion of Open Standards implementation in education and Open Educational Resources development and sharing based on Free Open Source Software development collaborative model.

Claude Martin Rainaud’s Specialties:

Collaborative Work, Facilitation, Learning methods and platforms design, development and projects management
Standards-based Learning Objects and Units of Learning systems design
Knowledge Management Systems design
Competence definition metodology
Competence-based training approach implementation
Open Educational Resources use and promotion
Free Open Source Software use and promotion
Usability design and testing, HTML, CSS
Zope, Plone web-based applications design and development.
