Joel Abraham jabraham1822@charter.net has over 16 years of experience in talent acquisitions & utilization in the High Tech Industry. His expertise lies in Business Development, Recruiting, Human Capital Analysis and Candidate Pipeline & Cultivation Development.

In May 2007 Joel moved to WiseChoice IT a niche IT contract resource company focused on identifying the top 20% niche talent in the below service areas:
• IT Security: Application, Identity & Access Management, Federation, Systems & Network. And Compliance.
• Enterprise Application Integration: Business Intelligence/Data Warehouse and Middleware (WebSphere)
• ERP: SAP, PeopleSoft, Oracle, Siebel (Technical & Function resources)
• Custom Talent Acquisition: sourcing and recruiting niche services by project or hourly

In March 2006 Joel went to GMR Marketing as Director of QuickStrike where he now works with companies to help staff live marketing events. www.gmrlive.com

Jan 2005 Joel was recruited to Adecco Technical & Scientific as a Regional Vice President where he is responsible for managing 8 states, 29 branches and staff of 85.

In 2003, Joel was recruited to Time Warner Cable Milwaukee division to turnaround the human capital process. With in his first six months Joel reduce turnover cost ½ and saved several million dollars. As a result in 2004 the Milwaukee Division was awarded the “Most Improved Recruitment Practices” out of 31 divisions in 27 states.

In 2000, Joel started a Midwest division of Duran HCP, a silicon valley based company, which was the first Technical Recruiting Consulting Company focused on Life Sciences, IT and Engineering. Duran HCP provides Recruiting & HR consulting services at a higher level for a fraction of the cost.
