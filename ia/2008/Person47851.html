David Donnelly’s Experience

  • Director of Software Services

    UNIS LUMIN

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 1999 — Present (8 years 5 months)

    UNIS LUMIN is a business solutions provider with a focus on software applications development, business process re-engineering, unified communications, and network design and support. Holding both a Microsoft Gold Excellence and Cisco Gold certification, this company offers a broad range of high technology oriented business solutions to assist our customers in achieving their business goals. We are also a manufacture of commercial software products marketed world wide.

    As Director of the Software Services department, I manage teams of developers responsible for both our consultative practices and our commercial software manufacturing lines of business. As the Director responsible for our Microsoft Gold Excellence status, I manage our relationship with Microsoft and am currently driving our involvement in the Microsoft Voice Partner Program. UNIS LUMIN is one of only six partners in Canada, 66 world-wide, selected for participation in this program.

  • IT Manager, Special Projects

    TSB International Inc.

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 1998 — November 1999 (1 year 1 month)

    TSB International was a developer of CTI and call center software applications and also operated a major telephony service bureau. With offices in the UK, Canada, and the United States, TSB boasted of major clients such as the Bank of Montreal and Bell Canada.

    In the role of IT Manager, Special Projects, I led an international team of developers working on a major new database driven Web based application designed for the management and processing of vast numbers of CDR records generated by enterprise level clients.

  • Systems Architect

    TSB International Inc.

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 1997 — November 1998 (1 year 1 month)

    TSB International was a developer of CTI and call center software applications and also operated a major telephony service bureau. With offices in the UK, Canada, and the United States, TSB boasted of major clients such as the Bank of Montreal and Bell Canada.

    In the role of Systems Architect, I worked with other archtiects and senior developers to design, construct, and deliver parts of the TSB CTI solution set. I led a team of developers ranging in experiece from juniors to senior level people and held shared responsibility for direct customer interactions.

  • Senior Research Engineer

    Ontario Hydro

    (Government Agency; 10,001 or more employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 1981 — November 1997 (16 years 7 months)

    The Advanced Systems Technology Department within the Research Division of Ontario Hydro focuses on the creation of computer automated systems for the inspection of nuclear reactor and fossil fuel generating station core components.

    As a Senior Research Scientist in the ASTD, I led the development of the software control and data analysis components of many of the principal inspection systems used in CANDU nuclear reactors around the world.
