Kevin Cable kcable@imageskc.com’s Summary

Media: advertising, marketing, branding, sales, and, training they all use some form of visual images to capture your attention. With outlets like the internet, television, print, and mass produced dvd’s, we are bombarded everyday by some type of media creative. Digital is now the main source for print and HD for television, although film is still used in commercials and features, it still remains the highest quality. In saying this Kevin has been shooting with the RED camera for 75% of his total work today.

Director/Dp: Kevin is known for his work as a Director and DP, directing for over 25 years and winning over 150 awards Including a Cine Gold Eagle for his first feature film as DP. His style as a director is heavily slanted from the camera side with outstanding visuals in Film or HD. He knows the tricks of the trade from time-lapse, ramping, aerials, process trailers, car mounts, film stocks, lighting, remote cameras, remote hot heads, specialty lenses, camera rigging and in High Definition that will enhance your next production. His directorial talents are just as impressive, working with talent of all ages, creating and blocking out storyboards, scripts, and working under pressure with deadlines. His specialties as a Dir/Dp are Image, Slice of Life, Auto, Promotional Branding, Entertainment, Theme Parks, Casinos and working with multiple cameras on a film style. He plans his jobs from pre- production through editorial, creating with images and telling the story that educates, brands and sells.
