Jeff Dailey’s Summary

Dailey Marketing Group (DMG) is a full-service marketing agency—and has been since 1997. We are an agency specializing in brilliant ideas—both strategic and creative—brought to you by the best talent out there.

DMG offers several important advantages that creates success to market to our clients target audience.

These include:

• Experience with Your Target Audiences – We have extensive experience marketing to Business-to-Consumer and Business-to-Business audiences in several industries.

• Market Research Driven Advertising – We utilize the latest market data to create the right messaging and creative to meet the strategic objectives. These data also drive the media strategy and implementation approach.

• Branding Know-How – From launching a new brand from scratch to refreshing established brands, DMG understands how to develop brands that speak to your marketplace and communicate differentiators.

• Marketing Strategy – We are committed to a process-based strategy development system that yields plans that deliver results.

• Strong Strategy and Creative Combined with Electronic Marketing Expertise – Our work is proven to deliver positive outcomes to clients in your market space; additionally, we have invested in the infrastructure required to execute on both the traditional and technical strategies that clients need today.

• Great Service – DMG takes great pride in our ability to support long-term relationships with our clients; these relationships are built on mutual trust and our commitment to our clients’ individual needs and desires.


As we do with all our clients, we would apply the DMG fresh approach to our work:

F - flexibility
R - responsiveness
E - economics
S - service
H - high standards
