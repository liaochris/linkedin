Rob van de Star’s Summary

Rob performed general Business management in the IT and Consulting industry, at both strategic and operational levels, with P&L responsibilities.

He is experienced in (re-)structuring / implementing change in companies and/or regional operations, with involvement in all process stages building high performance teams.

Rob has developed a generalist managerial profile, with in-depth knowledge of Commercial and Business areas, in addition to all aspects of Information Technology like:

- Engagement Management
- Change Management
- IT Strategy & Architecture
- IT Governance (CoBiT)
- Software Engineering and Package Implementation
- ITIL Service Management & CMMi
- Off-shoring and Outsourcing

and has a deep understanding of Marketing, Sales, Delivery, Contract & Product Management, Procurement, Finance and Human Resources acting on board room and CEO, CFO, CIO and other CxO level.

He prepared Strategic Planning of companies and products; prospected New Businesses, Alliances and Strategic Business Partnerships.

With more than 25 years of professional experience Rob acquired know how of cutting-edge Technology segments, worked in competitive and dynamic markets, at domestic and international levels.

He created, implemented and monitored Business and Marketing Plans and acquired international experience, through various business trips to countries in Europe, Asia and the United States.
