History:
------------------
* Have been knee-deep in interactive/online media since the early 90's, beginning with animation and multimedia development on Amiga systems, continuing through the Internet/Interactive Media's early days, on through the bubble, to today.

* Worked in the corporate interactive world for several years, before eventually breaking off with a few colleagues to form our own interactive firm in 2000. With no outside investment, no advertising, and no sales team-- just a commitment to quality work and a reputation-- we built the firm into a multi-million dollar enterprise.



MUSIC / MEDIA / ONLINE PRESENCE:
-----------------------------------------------------------
* 6 years blogging
* 3 years running a record label
* a lifetime of being in love with music
-----------------------------------------------------------

* Started one of the first mp3 blogs, Catbirdseat.Org, in 2002. Have maintained the site over the course of the next 5 years, always remaining focused on the original goal: to help spread the word about good music.

* Started the Catbird Records label in 2005. Over 15 releases to date, including discs from Someone Still Love You Boris Yeltsin, Tap Tap, Moviola, Jason Zumpano, and Manishevitz.

* Intense interest in the changing landscape of the music ( and media) industry, new technologies, methodologies, ways of thinking, policy, rights, fandom, and all that good stuff.
