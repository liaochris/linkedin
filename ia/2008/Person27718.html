Brian Polangin’s Summary

Mr. Polangin has more than 17 years of experience running successful marketing, sales and business development programs. Proficient with budgets of all sizes, he designs and executes programs that are efficient, cost-effective and, most of all, that really work. His programs have generated more than $100 million in sales and acquired over 1 million consumer customers.

Presently, he is CEO of InfoRockit, Inc., a revolutionary new mobile marketing tool, slated for launch in the Fall of 2008. Previously he was VP of Product Marketing at Agora Publishing, where he lead a talented team of direct and online marketing experts. Prior to that, he ran his own marketing and sales consulting company, called Home Run Solutions. During that time, he "took a year off" to be the VP of Sales & Marketing at Jaxara, an Offshore Software Development company. There he prepared the company for acquisition by re-branding, raising awareness, building a sales infrastructure.

In an earlier role as Vice President of Sales & Marketing for CreateHope, Inc., he brought the company from startup to nearly $2 million in annual sales in less than 2 years, acquiring a stable of blue chip clients such as Hewlett-Packard, Dell, Cisco, Blockbuster, American Express, and Cardinal Health Group. As Director of Marketing for Primus Telecommunications Group, he created and led both their Dial-Around and Consumer Online business units. With Startec Global Communications, he boosted annual sales from $6M to more than $70M, playing a key role in their 1997 IPO. Prior to that, he held increasingly responsible marketing management positions in various software product companies. Mr. Polangin holds a BA in Advertising from the University of Maryland.
