Philip Siddons’s Summary

I am an office automation and electronic publishing specialist and I absolutely love what I do. The same for the people with whom I work. I am committed to corporate excellence and I thrive on producing (and helping my colleagues produce) the most professional communication possible. I have a passion for helping others accomplish their goals and communicating in print, on the web or face-to-face the best of what our organization can offer to meet client needs. I bring the highest level of professionalism to the team and through supportive team building, adroit project management and the wise use of front-edge technology, I provide the highest level of internal and external communication possible.

As the Technology and Communications Coordinator for the Buffalo Urban League, I am leading the organization in not only its external marketing but also its internal staff communication and documentation. As the lead project manager for all of its major annual events, I maintain thorough staff communication with our client base and supporters through web and electronic publications as well as produce quarterly newsletters and multiple annual fund raising event print communications. I support all departments in the most efficient use of computer technology. Using cost-effective revenue-saving technology enables peek use of employee skills to meet mission goals. Management and staff are easily able to stay connected to our servers through secure encrypted access from PCs or smart phones anywhere in the world. As network administrator, I have overseen our migration from multiple and redundant NT server local area networks to a centrally-managed Windowsn2003 Server wide area network which includes a Citrix server farm, a voice-over-IP phone system, Wi-Fi and world-wide network access with Citrix and Windows mobile capable smart cell phones.
