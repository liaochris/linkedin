Ashish Mehra’s Summary

Past: Worked across mediums to launch interactive products on iTV (interactive television), web and mobile.
Future: Produce easy to use, intuitive interactive products that are platform agnostic.

Having worked with rediff.com between 1999 and 2002 in advertising sales, Ashish joined YooMedia Plc. in 2003 at London where he worked as a business & product analyst and then as a product manager for interactive TV, web and mobile consumer products.

Ashish got his bachelors degree in economics in 1999 from Loyola College, Chennai and his masters in the management of small media and creative enterprises in 2003 from the University of Warwick, UK .

Ashish's strength is his ability to identify trends, especially in the online interactive medium, early on. His skill lies in taking all the different elements involved in the development of a successful product and simplifying it into a clearly defined proposition and putting together a strategy and execution plan to cater to that proposition.
