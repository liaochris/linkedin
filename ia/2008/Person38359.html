Claus Sølvsteen’s Summary

I help media corporations and other businesses on use of New Media. I offer strategy and practical solutions to achieve the goals.

A special interest of mine is making new media work for press freedom. I have worked with media from all over the world – China, Africa, South America, Russia and Balkan.

I worked professionally with online media since 1991 (Just by chance I was the first Dane to have a private e-mail address.)

Claus Sølvsteen’s Specialties:

Online channels are to be treated as media: Either you make your content and services so compelling that your customers become interested readers and participants - or you will loose their attention in a second.
