Brett Cook’s Summary

Over 18 years of experience designing with embedded processors/controllers, FPGAs, CPLDs and analog interface circuitry in optical detection, vision and robotic systems.
Lead engineer on Lambda develpment team at Otis for past 7 years.
Developed and published measurement techniques for determining optical haze in IR transmissive plastics for Otis.
Experienced in trouble-shooting and root-cause analysis from system to component level and have worked with field, manufacturing, engineering and vendor personnel to analyze and solve a wide variety of issues.

Brett Cook’s Specialties:

Embedded processor and logic design, optical interface, detection.
