Isa Alsup’s Summary

A Maya/CG/compositing pro with substantial experience in film and television post production image manipulation and synthetic image/scene construction.

Among the 2nd wave of pioneers in computer graphics- first full time artist on the first 2D paint system sold and installed worldwide; lead 3D artist / 3D supervisor on the first 3D turn-key software system installed in Los Angeles. First user of D1 technology in computer graphics. Pioneered use field-rendering and gamma 1 in CGI as opposed to 2.2, worked closely with Wavefron in development of Composer software; trained/mentored many artists who have gone on to major roles in film and tv post production.

Isa Alsup’s Specialties:

Pipeline design, post-production planning and management and crew supervision; visual effects design and post-production; MAYA modeling, animation, character rigging, dynamics, lighting and shaders; After Effects compositing, motion graphic design.
