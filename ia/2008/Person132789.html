Mike Zammit’s Summary

I am an experienced IT and business consultant, with a high quality client track record and more than thirty years experience in the field of IT. I have worked for many organisations in both the public and private sector including International Banking, Finance, Manufacturing, Telecoms, Retail and Wholesale Merchanting, IT Hardware, Software, Services, Systems Integration, Local and International Government, Housing Associations and Charities.

I am a strategic thinker with significant experience in devising and delivering robust, flexible and long-lived ICT strategies that facilitate business change and increased efficiency. I have excellent communication, person management, negotiating and analytical skills and a good track record in delivering innovative ICT solutions which facilitate major transformational change.

I am very experienced in establishing, leading and motivating professional ICT teams that have a strong commitment to excellence and customer service and I am committed to encouraging appropriate, continuing personal and professional development.
