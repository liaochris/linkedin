Tabatha Manuel’s Summary

Manuel Public Relations is an upscale entertainment and events publicity agency. Since 2004, MPR has helped various clients get their name in the media and build public awareness.

Whether in the nonprofit sector or entertainment industry, MPR ensures each client red carpet treatment and heightened visibility. Tabatha Manuel, CEO of MPR, has a Bachelor's in Public Relations from Wayne State University and years of experience in journalism, marketing and public relations. With vision, creativity and a drive for client-satisfaction, MPR is the publicity agency that will aim all eyes on YOU!

MPR offers a variety of services to help propel you ahead of your competitors with increased exposure and unique identity conception. In addition to implementing strategic PR campaigns, MPR offers a cost-effective way to broadcast your news to the world with our innovative and convenient Writing Center. The Writing Center specializes in creative and professional writing services that allow you to order press releases for a small fee and also receive distribution through an online newswire.

Our mission is to provide innovative services with a dedication to commitment and client-satisfaction, while specializing in YOUR success!

Visit: www.ManuelPR.com
