Julio Romero Aguero’s Summary

Dr. Romero Aguero is an international expert in knowledge-based modeling and application of computational intelligence algorithms to power distribution systems. He has over 13 years of experience working with electric utilities and regulatory boards, his areas of expertise are planning, reliability, maintenance, operations, and regulation of power distribution systems, knowledge-based modeling and computational intelligence.

During his career in the USA, he has provided consulting services in the areas of planning, reliability, load forecasting, and distribution systems engineering to some of the most important electric utilities of the country and abroad, such as Commonwealth Edison, Pacific Gas and Electric, Philadelphia Electric Company, Dominion, Puget Sound Energy, Southern California Edison, XCEL, Consolidated Edison, Madison Gas and Electric, and Karachi Electric Service Corporation.

During his career in Honduras, he was appointed by the President of the Republic of Honduras as Commissioner of the National Energy Commission and held several key positions at the National Electric Power Utility of Honduras (ENEE). He was Professor at the Department of Electrical Engineering of the Autonomous National University of Honduras (UNAH) and Vice President of the College of Mechanical, Electrical and Chemical Engineers of Honduras (CIMEQH).

He is a Senior Member of the IEEE, author of over 20 technical papers and reports, and a reviewer of international journals such as IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, IEEE Transactions on Power Delivery and IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems. He is ex-grantee of the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) and has been listed in the 10th Edition of Marquis Who’s who in Science and Engineering 2008-2009.
