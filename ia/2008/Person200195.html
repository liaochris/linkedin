R. Shawn Williams’s Summary

Williams Consulting is located in downtown St. Louis, Missouri. Specializing
in branding development his entire career, R. Shawn Williams operates
Williams Creative as a firm specializing in brand design, advertising, and
publications.

Shawn has created brand solutions for a wide range of clients including Alpha
Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., Congressional Candidate Kevin Powell, Forest Park
Southeast Development Corporation, Jobs With Justice, Missouri Foundation
for Health, National Black MBA Association, SSM St. Mary’s Health Foundation,
St. Louis Language Immersion Schools, St. Louis University and more.
