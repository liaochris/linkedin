Sylvester Braithwaite M.D., F.A.C.O.G.’s Summary

I am a Canadian trained Obstetrician Gynecologist with over 25 years experience in adolescent health care in Canada, West Indies, Europe and the US. I am also a professional speaker and Edu~Tainment seminar leader (which Educate, Entertain and Empowers audiences) and author of "Teen Spirit"; 55 strategies to help parents talk to their teens about sexuality. Lastly, my recent project with 6 other experts in the field of sexuality included the production of an educational DVD entitled "Teens, Sex & Health" --a comprehensive approach to sexual education.

I am on the Miami Dade School Board's Speakers Bureau where I am involved in HIV/AIDS presentations. I have served on the board of several community organization including, Miami Dade Alliance for Career Education, Alert Health formerly called Hep-C Alert, Adolescent Pregnancy Prevention and Parenting Council, the Women Emergency Network and the National Heritage Foundation.

At present, I am the medical director of Women & Teens Healthcare and Alert Health in Miami Florida.

My mission is to provide accessible, available, and affordable medical care and to disseminate accurate research based information about both abstinence and contraception. My major objective is to prevent unintended pregnancies and sexually transmitted diseases especially HIV/AIDS. My basic message is "education now, babies later, STD's HIV/AIDS never."
