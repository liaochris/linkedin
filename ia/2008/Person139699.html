Nelson Melegrito’s Experience

  • Owner

    DIVA: Diverse & Inclusive Visionary Artists

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Entertainment industry)

    April 2003 — Present (5 years 9 months)

    As the Executive Director/Founder of DIVA: Diverse & Inclusive Visionary Artists, a non profit organization conceived in 2003 as a result of my involvement with other community organizations. I created DIVA solely to provide a forum for multi-ethnic LGBT artists an alternative niche to network with the underserved segment of the community, often sidelined in the media.

    Through its innovative marketing concepts, the membership has grown from 200 to 2,000 and hosted high-profile events, mixers, screenings, premieres, and film festivals, among them: award-winning movies such as "Tarnation", "Boy Culture" "Gay Republicans", "Paper Dolls" and "Blossoming of Maximo Oliveros"; Will & Grace Series Finale benefiting Lifeworks Mentoring; Equality U Soulforce Fundraiser, and the only organization to produce Madonna Confessions Promotion in L.A.

    In June 2007, I was profiled by Frontiers Magazine as one of the six (and only minority) LA area LGBT people for their Annual Pride Issue.
