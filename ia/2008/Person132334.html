Mike Leone’s Summary

Mike Leone is recognized as an accomplished pharmaceutical sales executive with broad expertise that drives performance. His 25+ years of experience & leadership includes positions of increasing responsibility at Bristol-Myers Squibb, First Horizon Pharmaceuticals, Eyetech Pharmaceuticals & Takeda Pharmaceuticals. Mike is currently president of Strategic Health Resources, and is consulting for a number of companies in strategic & leadership roles.

At Takeda, Mr. Leone was the Mid-Atlantic South RSD, where he led the Region to an impressive 106% of goal through the 2005 mid-year, attaining a #1 ranking. At Eyetech, Mr. Leone was National Sales Director. In this role, he led all facets of sales force operations & infrastructure development, including sales strategy, alignment, sales force automation & budgeting. Prior to Eyetech, Mr. Leone was VP - Sales at First Horizon Pharmaceutical Corp., where he led the sales force expansion to a 60% growth in revenues.

At Bristol-Myers Squibb, Mike held numerous positions of increasing responsibility. These included National Director, Federal & Institutional Sales, a position in which he rebuilt the Hospital Sales Team & strengthened BMS’ role and commitment to the VA and military customers. During this time, Mike developed the mission & revitalized the morale of the 275 BMS Hospital Managers and Representatives, and spearheaded the launch of Maxipime, the first fourth-generation cephalosporin antibiotic. Prior to that position, Mr. Leone was a National Accounts Director, where he developed & coordinated major account contracts & support programs, and Eastern Region Director for the Bristol-Myers Squibb Hospital & Institutional Sales Group, where he successfully led his team to exceed their goals in every year of the Group’s existence.

Mr. Leone has broad therapeutic experience in cardiovasculars, psychotherapeutics, community and hospital anti-infective’s, ophthalmology, and diabetes.
