Kelly Johnson’s Summary

Online business manager who offers creative, technical and administrative support to small business owners and entrepreneurs.

Professional background includes: teaching high school English and psychology; management position with education company that places teachers from foreign countries to work in the United States as teachers and cultural ambassadors; lead facilitator for education company that offered trainings and meetings to be conducted for participants via satellite broadcast, video conferencing and computer software.
