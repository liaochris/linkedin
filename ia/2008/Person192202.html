Tracey Kumer-Moore, JWIC’s Summary

"Certified June Wedding, Inc. Wedding Consultant"/Las Vegas Wedding planner specializing in assiting couples with planning a celebration of their love & their style..with Style that is memorable for all the right reasons.

Professional wedding planning, consultation, design & on-site wedding day coordination services for weddings & social celebrations in Greater Las Vegas area. Available for travel.

Since 2006, Course Instructor specializing in Cultural Weddings & Vendor Relations for "Wedding Coordination & Design" Certification Program at "The International School of Hospitality", Las Vegas specializing in training graduates for positions in the city's growing hospitality industry.

Panelist at 2005 "Wedding & Event Videographers' Association" & 2006 "Limousine & Chauffered Transportation Association" anual conferences.

"Las Vegas Conference Liason" for "21st Century Nuptials" assisting conference producer in referring and procurring venues, speakers (including Las Vegas Mayor Oscar Goodman), products & services for 3 day educational event for Professional Wedding Planners.

Successfully produced "The Knot's" 2006 Las Vegas Vendor Appreciation Party at Rio All Suite Hotel & Casino.

Featured or quoted as wedding expert & Las Vegas Wedding Expert on Wedding Podcast Network & in: Feb. 2008 "GO Magazine" for AirTran Airline, Destination Weddings & Honeymoon Magazine, Destination I Do Magazine, Casino Journal Magazine, Wedding Bells Magazine, Chicago Style Weddings Magazine, Las Vegas Review Journal & Jewish Life & Style Magazine (from Las Vegas), Jewish Reporter & the book, "Come As You Aren't: Feeling at Home with Multi-Cultural Celebrations" by Norine Dresser.

Since 2005, Las Vegas NACE Board of Directors member: Hospitality Chair, Newsletter Chair & Chapter Documentarian.

2007 Las Vegas "Spirit of NACE Award" honoree

"Hands on Hundred" volunteer:"The Special Event Show" 2004: Las Vegas & 2007: Los Angeles.
