ROB DAVIES’s Summary

I've been working professionally as an artist for over 27 years, the last 17 in animation. My calling as an entrepreneur began when I was 13, painting signs for clients in Mexico. At 19 I started my first company with business partner Trevor Bentley where we did a variety of illustration jobs from T-shirt design to poster art.

After that, I got a 'real' job in animation in my early 20's and was given the opportunity to work at many great studios in exotic locals such as Gastown (Vancouver), East Berlin and Sherman Oaks, California.

As the co-owner and President of Atomic Cartoons, I have been at it as an animation studio head for nearly a decade. My primary role in the corporation is that of VP of Business Development.

I strongly (but humbly) suggest that if you are looking for a very experienced and personable service studio to assist you with your next project, or are looking for a co-production partner, please drop me a line, anytime. Chances are that I and/or my experienced team can assist you.

Atomic Cartoons specializes in content creation, development and production as well as full animation services on a contractual basis.

Since opening our doors in early '99 we've provided animation pre-producion on over 400 half hours of television, and have also created and co-produced the hit series Atomic Betty which proudly airs on Teletoon in Canada and is seen in over 107 countries.

We also co-produce Heroic Films' Captain Flamingo which airs on YTV in Canada and Jetix world-wide.

Our goals are to expand our successful relationships and open our arms to other co-pro and development opportunities, as well as to high-end service contracts.

Work-for-hire clients have included Warner Bros., Nelvana, Sunwoo, Mainframe, Saban, BKN, Film Roman, Mike Young Productions, Mondo Media, Warner Bros. Online, Sony/Sunbow, Klasky Csupo and Disney to name a few.

ROB DAVIES’s Specialties:

Award-winning content creation, development, co-production, and animation production.

Production services including full animation pre-production, Flash animation production, specializing in high-end television series and direct to video animation.

Atomic's record for delivering on time and on budget while producing exceptionally good content is second to none.
