  • Owner

    www.TIMBA.com

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    November 2006 — Present (2 years)

    Due to the sheer amount of information it contains, TIMBA.com is unofficially the largest latin music website availble today. Boasting 10,000 visitors and 30,000 pageviews a week, TIMBA.com is the "Home of Cuban Music on the Web".

    TIMBA.com is brought to you by a handful of Cuban music lovers from around the world. Our objective is simply to provide the most complete and up-to-date information regarding Cuban music & culture. TIMBA.com has correspondents in Havana, Miami, Boston, New York, San Francisco, Santa Cruz, Puerto Rico, Switzerland and Germany. Our unique and dedicated team brings you updates, reviews, coverage of concerts, special events from around the world, plus news from the island.

    TIMBA.com is dedicated to music and culture, not politics. Consequently, we do not have an opinion on U.S. & Cuba relations. We do not have any political agendas nor do we support, represent or advocate any political parties and/or organizations. Lo que queremos es....TIMBA!
