Kevin Hunter’s Summary

Lead developer for four generations of client and server software as part of patented system to link printed documents to resources on Internet (“qode,” “PaperClick” and “NeoLink” products). Development of proprietary software for image-based 1D- and 2D- barcode decoding across multiple operating environments. Development and oversight of architecture for “intelligent document” and “print-to-web” product lines. Team leader in rearchitecture, production implementation and rollout of barcode-driven on-line shopping system. Design and development of a portable architecture for implementing high-performance custom web servers (Windows, UNIX). Development and support of portable PDF417 and MaxiCode barcode encoding software (Windows, UNIX, AS/400, OS/390). Rapid prototyping of several barcode-driven data systems (“MedThread,” “SecureDocs,” “PaperData”). Primary technical person involved in NeoMedia’s patent strategy, both in the development of NeoMedia’s own patent portfolio and the evaluation of competitive patents.

Kevin Hunter’s Specialties:

Software development: Java (J2ME, J2SE and J2EE environments), Symbian (Nokia Series 60 and UIQ), Windows Mobile, Palm
