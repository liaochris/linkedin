Tim Horan’s Summary

Results oriented executive with a passion for developing and managing high growth consumer and enterprise media and technology businesses.

• Proven skills in business development, sales, strategy, account management and product development.
• Track record defining and developing new market opportunities, executing go-to-market strategies and managing sustainable, cash-flowing businesses for private and public companies in businesses sized from $5mm to 500mm.
• Demonstrated ability to develop and manage teams, think strategically, and execute tactically across functions to bring new products to market, acquire and manage customers, exceed revenue targets, and manage P&L.
• Structured, negotiated and executed multi-million dollar, multi-year sales, business development and partnership licensing agreements with leading mobile operators, handset manufacturers, online portals, commerce, content and media companies.
• Deep domain experience in mobile, social and online media – revenue and distribution models, content platform technologies, device OS, search and related technologies.
• Member of senior management teams that successfully raised over $50 million in multiple rounds of financing from leading venture capital firms.
• Experienced in start-up, early and growth stage environments that are high risk and high reward.
• Entrepreneurial spirit coupled with practical business expertise.
