Shayla Roberts’s Summary

Professional Coach: Have inspired creative entrepreneurs in many industries for nearly 30 years by applying creative principles to business. Business development coach of choice to many Certified Executive Coaches. Motivate employees of large companies to excell while enhancing quality of life.

Co-Owner of Pond Productions: Create, publish and market innovative products and services for human transformation including the amazon.com best selling book, Bold Moves––Jump To Outstanding Self-Managed Action. Original work has been the foundation of curricula in public schools and high-end whole-health retreats.

High Creative and Visionary: Developing concepts for a personal growth theme park where customers will enjoy an outrageous and compelling journey to the final frontier––inner space.

Shayla Roberts’s Specialties:

Creativity in business, life/work integration, muse marketing, visionary systems design, creative and market writing, presentation design and much more.
