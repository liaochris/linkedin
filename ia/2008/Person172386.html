Peter Secor’s Summary

Over the last fifteen years I have been involved in many aspects of software development and delivery, including programming, product management, directing a business unit, and running a startup. Given my software experience, my education, and my general curiosity, my main strength is the ability to understand and appreciate new technology and determine how to leverage it into new or existing markets.

My short term goal is to help direct and grow my current startup into a successful and valuable company by delivering cheap, reliable data storage to the mass consumer and SOHO market. In the longer term, I would like to be in the position to help finance new ideas in software, mobile applications, and energy research as well as teach in and impart some of my experiences to the educational system at a junior high and high school level.
