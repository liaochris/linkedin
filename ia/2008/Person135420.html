Mir M. Moosavi’s Summary

As a professional I have always been focused on "Information Technology".
From the time in the 90's when the 'Bubble' took form to the time when it ended all illusions of IT and bursted in the face of reality... I kept track.

Amidst the great technical complexity of IT, I was always focused on "The Web".
The Web not only intrigued me but showed me a way for how I could play a pivotal role in defining it further by contributing meaningfully.

Today it's at a point of definition, and only now the technology has matured well enough to support it's true potential for growth.
I believe that the next age will be defined by 'collaborative technologies', based on the Web or the emerging "Cloud".

My depth of professional experience incorporates – marketing, strategic thinking, planning, SEO, web-technology development & implementation
along with innovative design for the web.
I have excellent analytical research & strategic thinking capabilities for the creation of plans, frameworks, SOPs for IT and business management implementations.

My professional drive comes from having been associated with areas of Business IT for some 7+ years - recently in a lead capacity for business development where I participated in top, strategic level development and management activities.
