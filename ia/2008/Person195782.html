Virgilio Fiorese’s Summary

I´m Telecom sales support professional, period. What I do well are support sales guys with necessary technical background they need for preparing economic arguments and sales strategy for closing deals. You can call this profile by different names, Sales Support, Sales Engineer, Pre-Sales, Business Development, Product Manager what ever, but at the end of the day, we close the GAP between Sales Team and Engineering Product Team.

I have more than 15 years of experience on customer facing supporting complex negotiations on Pre and Post-Sales stages, managing customer technical and budget expectations and sales targets never missing the focus on making the customer succeed.

Strengths includes verbal and written communication, customer facing presentation, complex economic and technical problems negotiation and solving and creative strategies for sales positioning.

Various leading positions, with extensive technical background.
- 5 years as Quality Assurance engineer for wireline telecom solutions,
- 2 years as Systems Integration in a start up Mobile Operator working with VAS over IN (SMS, VMS and PrePaid),
- 5 years supporting Projects and Business Development for Network Elements Suppliers, VAS, OSS and BSS suppliers such as CPM, LogicaCMG, Convergys and Openwave in Wireless (PLMN), Wireline (PSTN) and ISPs.
- 3 years as Business Intelligence Director for a Custom Development and Professional Services supplier.

Public Speaker Experience with active leadership/participation in events, seminars, congresses, etc.. supporting marketing initiativies.

Published several articles in the specialized media and authored a VAS e-Book and a written book "Wireless - Introdução as Telecomunicações Moveis Celulares"
