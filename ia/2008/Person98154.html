Josh Iverson’s Summary

I have been involved in online and interactive marketing and advertising since 1998 in both a management and individual sales roll. Currently, I sell advertising for United Online NASDAQ: UNTD (Classmates.com, NetZero/Juno, and others). I work with major ad agencies including Digitas, Arnold, Mullen, Carat, and national advertisers like Monster, Bose, PrivacyMatters.com, Singlesnet, Comcast, TDBankNorth, Mitt Romney for President and others. Before United Online I worked for Y2M, now owned by MTVu (a division of Viacom). At Y2M my main endeavor was to sell, coordinate, and promote large budget ROI based integrated marketing campaigns, as well as recruit, train, and build the sales team. While I helped build and manage the sales team, I was also the top producing sales executive and my accounts included Ford, Microsoft, Google, Sprint PCS, Avid, NetZero, Apple, Dell and other fortune 500 accounts in the youth space. Before Y2M, I founded ezADit.com, which later merged with College Publisher Inc/Y2M Networks. Beyond consistently becoming the top advertising sales executive, I have built and managed sales teams, created media buying/placement teams, and executed multi-media campaigns--online, events/guerilla promotions, and newspaper advertising. Call me Josh, Joshua, JI, J.R., Rapp, Big J, Little J, Papa J, J-ba-dank-a-dank, etc.

In my spare time I import Yerba Mate, a South American tea-like beverage, from Argentina and sell to Yerba Mate fanatics, specialty tea-shops, Yoga Studios, and online at www.miyerbamate.com
