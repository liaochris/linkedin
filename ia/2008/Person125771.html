Matthew Schreiber’s Summary

Results-oriented attorney with extensive experience in strategic alliances and global partnerships, technology licensing, distribution and development, sales and marketing, intellectual property, mergers and acquisitions, litigation management and general corporate and commercial matters for Fortune 500 enterprises, startup companies and leading law firms in the software, Internet, technology and media industries.

Worked with Yahoo!, Viacom/Simon & Schuster and other industry leaders. Strong analytical, negotiation and drafting skills; can-do, money-saving team player. Handled broad range of legal and business issues, including:
- sales, marketing, advertising and privacy matters;
- commercial, corporate and board of directors matters;
- counsel on a variety of intellectual property matters
- establishing and drafting company policies and procedures;
- employment law issues;
- regulatory compliance;
- litigation management and dispute resolution
- trademark and copyright review and registration;
- recruitment and management of small legal teams including attorneys, paralegals and administrative assistants; and
- preparing and presenting legal and business seminars and product demonstrations.

Efforts resulted in hundreds of thousands of dollars paid to or saved by employers, including by negotiating settlements in intellectual property and commercial disputes and handling various matters without involvement of outside counsel.

Graduate of Boalt Hall Law School, University of California at Berkeley; attended University of Barcelona School of Law. Phi Beta Kappa undergraduate, State University of New York at Binghamton. Associate at Ramos & Arroyo law firm in Barcelona, Pillsbury, Madison & Sutro, and Graham & James.

Matthew Schreiber’s Specialties:

Fluent in Spanish; considerable amount of oral and written comprehension in Portuguese and French. Extensive experience working in Europe, Latin America, and on international transactions. Proven ability to work effectively with business and legal teams.

Drafted and negotiated agreements with industry leaders in the telecommunications, electronics, media, hardware, financial services, and e-commerce industries.
