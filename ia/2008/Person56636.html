Eduardo Arroyo’s Summary

Graduated in Management Engineering, Minor Industrial Psychology, and an MBA from RPI, New York before turning 22. He became one of the youngest General Managers to run a high-technology organization for Microdata at age 25. He later held the position of VP for Emulex Caribe which he occupied for 10 years.

In 1992, he opened a management consulting firm, Arroyo & Associates, specializing in Leadership and Strategic Thinking. He also served as local partner for McKinsey & Company for a landmark educational project.

He succeeded in international markets through application of the latest organizational effectiveness methodologies. He worked under recognized consultants of the caliber of Dr. Harold Haller, one of an elite group of Dr. Deming's personal disciples. Dr. Deming was personally aware of this process– calling it a benchmark.

He holds leadership positions in boards for several organizations including the Santana Conglomarate. He is the Past-President and still an active board member ofPR2000 and of PRMA.

He is also a leader of Vision Puerto Rico, a major economic project that has gained support from the World Economic Forum.

In 1994 he published a successful guide “The Making of a Leader” (based on "organization as a system" authorized by Dr. Deming). His second book, an extremely successful teamwork guide “Trabajando en Equipo,” was published 1995. “The Making of a Leader” was republished in 2002 as e-book, which reached 105 countries.

In 2007 he published "RESULTS ORIENTED EXECUTION".

He has received many awards and recognitions, including Almirante Almézquita Award, maximum recognition given by PR2000 for supporingt competitiveness. He is listed in TOP TEN management consultants for the Caribbean.

He recently completed the BLUE OCEAN STRATEGY CERTIFICATION COURSE.

Strategy Planning, Blue Ocean Strategy, Leadership and Management Development and Coaching. English and Spanish.
