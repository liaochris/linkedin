Anna Pollock’s Summary

Often described as being irrepressibly curious and creative, "ahead of her time", and a "renaissance woman", I see myself as a change agent whose mission is to help clients and colleagues prepare for a very different future. My passion is helping create positive workplace cultures that foster curiosity, creativity and collaboration. I try to live the mantra "winning by sharing" as I believe there is more than enough work that needs to be done and none of us really "own" ideas - we are merely antennae. The core task is to be receptive and to know whom to engage in implementation.

I enjoy making complex subjects accessible and easier to comprehend, asking the right questions and approaching tired and worn subjects with fresh eyes by asking the right questions.

I am also happiest when I have my hands and feet in the soil or am exploring new landscapes and delving into stories of the past. Photography, being in nature and the company of good friends are other activities that bring pleasure.

Anna Pollock’s Specialties:

Strategy, business concepts and planning, analysis, communication, public speaking, preparing business cases, teaching, coaching.

    Heroes@Work Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Higher Education industry)

    May 2006 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Heroes@Work develops leaders for challenging times by focussing on self mastery and through working with the power of story. In our current environment of turbulent change, it is not enough to adapt - nothing less than personal and organisational transformation is required. Leadership of others starts with leadership of self. Heroes@Work uses the power of archetypal psychology and mythology to develop customized programs that create a safe yet stimulating environment in which leaders can discover, develop, ignite and nuture their individual and collective creativity. We offer two programs: Conscious leadership and re-Visioning retirement. Both programs are highly experiential and take place over two months in the form of three, two day retreats. See: www.heroesatwork.com.
