Hans Mestrum’s Summary

On August 1 2007 I started as an Advisor/Teacher on Orion College in Breda (http://www.orioncollege.nl). A school for Havo/VWO after several years as an (senior) manager in the ICT education business.

Hans is an experienced hands-on general manager in the ICT industry and training business.
He is capable of setting up businesses and build strong relations, company culture, operational excellence and customer intimicy.
Positive attitude and optimism are characteristic values.
Hans is going for the strong and long relationships.
He believes in the idea of a changing world: coming from an Information Society and entering a Dream Society (R. Jensen 1999) based on emotional values and stories around products, thus creating new markets.
He believes in bottom-up processes in which the user is in control, always on and superconnected.

His weblog Hans on Experience can be foud at: http://www.hansonexperience.com and his screencastingblog on: http://www.hansonexperience.com/screencasting
He was also author on the weblog MarketingFacts at http://www.marketingfacts.nl
