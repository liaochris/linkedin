Sona Shah’s Summary

A manager with broad technology experience in roles of increasing responsibility, across numerous industry sectors. After obtaining a Bachelor of Arts, I commenced my career with a large management consulting firm. Numerous appointments at blue-chip international firms gave me a sound basis in technology management skills, leading to my promotion to executive level with Accenture.

I resigned to take up a fast-paced, challenging position at M2006, a public-private partnership venture responsible for the Melbourne 2006 Commonwealth Games.

After the games, I moved to National Australia Bank in a technology management position. Having demonstrated a passion for developing people and teams, I was given broader management responsibility for staff development and engagement.

I completed my postgraduate studies in Psychology via part-time study in late 2006.
