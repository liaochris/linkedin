Nathalie Reis’s Summary

I am a qualified French translator and have been freelancing for agencies and private clients since 1995.

MY SPECIALIST SUBJECTS ARE

Business and finance, more specifically banking, economics, marketing, advertising, human resources and training.
Other fields include education, travel and tourism, art and general medicine.


LANGUAGE SERVICES AND EXPERIENCE

Translation and proof-reading of general documents, annual reports, questionnaires and market studies, brochures, web sites, press releases, business documentation, art books, magazines and catalogues, exhibition guides, tourist information and film synopsis.


A LITTLE BIT ABOUT MYSELF

I am French, born in the most beautiful island in the world "Corsica" and I am extremely happy to be living in the second most beautiful island in the world, "England". I am married to a brilliant lawyer and have 2 great children, Elena (11) and James (9).

Languages are my passion, especially the English language. I love words and going from one language to another. I love reading and writing. I belong to a French book club and am also very keen in English and American literature.

I enjoy working from home because it gives me the flexibility I need to accomodate my needs with my family's. Translating allows me to use my brain in the most pleasant way and to earn a living at the same time. Ideally I would like to be able to have more direct contacts with people needing my services and that's the main reason for joining LinkedIn.

Please don't hesitate to be in touch if you think you would like me to be part of your network.
