Matthew Rees’s Summary

Management Consultant for Logica working on a range of projects for external and internal clients. Currently a Programme Manager overseeing projects in over 70 countries.

Previously a Business Transformation Manager for a London Borough where I oversaw a number of transformation projects. My previous government experience includes 8 years in systems development for a county council.

I have worked in banking for a total of 8 years on projects for the Royal Bank of Scotland, Barclays Bank, National Westminster Bank, the Czech Saving Bank and APACS.

In the utilities and telecoms sector, I have 4 years experience in IBM’s world-wide utilities customer service solutions team and a further 4 years experience working on projects for Nokia Siemens Networks, Electricidade de Portugal, Cable and Wireless, and British Telecom.

I am a conference speaker and have had many articles published. For the last 14 years I have been a school governor and a member of various education committees.
