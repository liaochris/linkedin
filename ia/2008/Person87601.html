Jeff Kramer’s Summary

Experienced Wireless Technology Executive with a background in both large and small companies. Over the past 6 years, I led the development and technical direction of Motricity's Fuel platform. This includes building the Technical platform/Team and roll-out to a number of Wireless carriers including AT&T, Alltel, Bell Mobility, Leap, and Tracfone. The Fuel platform is extremely successful and has generated over $750 Million in mobile content revenue.
