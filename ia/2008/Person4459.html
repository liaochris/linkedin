Aislinn Dewey’s Summary

From product design to screen-based interaction, I am passionate about incorporating and translating user-expressed needs in the design process. Through observation, ethnography, persona and scenario development to user testing of rapid prototypes, I strive to implement innovative solutions that meaningfully impact peoples lives.
