Nito Waals’s Summary

After working for Andersen Consulting in Europe (3 years) and Asia (2 years), I came to the US in '93 with a smaller SAP consulting company. I traveled through all of the South American countries (by car from the US and back) looking to expand business in the Andean region and beyond). After I got my green card, I founded my own consulting company Jan. 1 2002 with 4 other partners. As the SAP R/3 consulting market has become saturated and development work is more and more being outsourced, we are looking at SAP's new "Business one" initiative for the SME market. At the same time we've added "profitbase" (A Business warehousing and reporting software) to the product line we will support in the near future. With these products we are now starting to look into penetrating the local SME markets (Small and Medium sized Enterprises). For me that is the greater Nashville market. We are therefore looking for people and partner companies that either have these contacts and/or products /services that may complement ours (or even compete in case merging makes sense).

Nito Waals’s Specialties:

Implemented SAP all over the world in a wide variety of industries in multilingual/-cultural situations. Conversions, system reviews, mergers, upgrades, add-ons, we've done them all. That's why, what we (Cognitus consulting) bring to the table, adds generally a lot more value then what a client asks for. FICO is our focus, CO is mine!
