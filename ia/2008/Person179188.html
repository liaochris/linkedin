Sridhar Gowda’s Summary

Co-founded the following internet, cultural and social initiatives over the last 10 years in the UK and India.

*Peak Literary Festival, UK - One of the largest literary festival in the UK hosting events for over 100 international and local writers annually in the UK.Previous speakers have included Sir John Major, Sir Mark Tully, Dame Ellen MacArthur and Lionel Shriver
Next Festival starting on 23rd October 2008
Speakers include Louis De Bernieres, Carol Drinkwater, Hardeep Singh, Ben Crystal, Rachel Elnaugh, Paul Rose and Gervase Phinn.
http://www.countrybookshop.co.uk/peakfestival

*Booksconnect.com -social networking for the book community, create and share bookshelves and events - connecting readers, authors, publishers, booksellers,librarians, event, festival and book group organisers. Promoting events, authors, publishers and book titles.
''BOOKS TOOLBOX: 50+ Sites for Book Lovers'' - Mashable.com Sept 2007.

*Countrybookshop.co.uk - Celebrating the 11th anniversary of the online bookshop established to complement the independent bookshop in the Peak District countryside. Shortlisted for the independent bookshop of the year, DTI / ISI e-commerce awards and Sunday Times e-league top 100 Pre- ipo European Internet Ventures.

*Calver Weir Restoration Project,UK - A charitable trust restoring the 18th century weir to preserve the heritage and biodiversity on the river Derwent in the UK. It was successful in its bid to raise £1.2 million from Heritage Lottery Fund in Mar 2008.Working to raise the match funding.
http://www.calverweir.org.uk

*Gnana Trust, India - promoting the importance of education, ecology and arts in rural rural schools in South India and has donated books, conducted workshops and events. Supporting setting up of nature clubs in and around Kokkare Bellur. http://www.inamaste.com

* Peak District Book of the Year Award.

*iNamaste, India- Indian venture based in Bangalore.
