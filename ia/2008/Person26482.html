Brandy Tanner’s Summary

After becoming a mother of three my goal was to continue working but from home rather than outside in the corporate world. I must admit this was a tough decision as I loved being in the office environment, daily interactions in person or over the phone with customers is pleasing to me. I enjoy knowing I have made someone smile. What better way to put my mothering and office skills together than to start my own blog and virtual assistant business?!

I have put my passion for being a mom, helping others and customer service to work from my home office. I started my Writings of a WAHM blog to write reviews, advice, and post giveaways so that other moms have a place to come when they seek a smile for the day or need encouragement to know that they can succeed from home.

Visit www.writingsofawahm.com to read more of what I am up to currently.
