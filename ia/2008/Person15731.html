Arie Shen’s Summary

Arie Shen has over 20 years of experience in the information technology industry with particular attention to development, marketing and sales, project management, and negotiations of business software applications. A UCI graduate in Management and Information and computer science, and an MBA, Arie has lead start ups and divisions of publicly trades companies in the strategy and execution of sales, management and implementation of large-scale projects with a significant professional service delivery programs for Fortune 100 companies. Arie has substantial relationship building with customers, vendors and partners for large-scale market penetration. He has provided services to Global 2000 companies involved in a broad spectrum of industries, including insurance, manufacturing, government, aerospace, financial, healthcare, sales and marketing, distribution and retail businesses. Arie served as an executive manager for publicly traded companies dealing with software, consumer product, bio and clean technology and founded several consulting and international trade companies. Arie has been a board member of California-Israel Chamber of Commerce, American Technion Society, President of the two local high tech network organizations, board member for the UCI entrepreneur committee, and served as a Marketing Advisor to a local corporations. He has been a dedicated volunteer in the Orange County community and associate with many local boards of community services.
