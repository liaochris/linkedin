Katrina Chesney’s Summary

Do you ever find yourself saying things like:

"There's just not enough hours in the day."
"I dreamed of running my business, but my business is running me."
"I need quality time for me and my family."
"It's too overwhelming. I can't get it all done!"
"Help! I need an extra set of hands!"
"I'm ready to take my business to the next level, but I don't have time."
"I can't afford an on-site employee but I need the help."

If you answered “Yes” to any of these statements, let Portable Hands lend a helping hand!

Portable Hands supports self-employed professionals and very small businesses who struggle with getting everything done. Our services help you stay focused on the BIG picture while we work behind the scenes.

Portable Hands' ideal client:

1. Values relationships
2. Has big goals
3. Is well organized
4. Is somewhat cyber-savvy
5. Can articulate needs & desires
6. Can let go of things
7. Is trusting & trustworthy
8. Is open-minded
9. Is on-line (PC & Internet access)

If this sounds like you, then ponder this:

What could you create in your business & life if you had even a few extra hours each week?
