Robert Casto’s Summary

I have been in the software industry since before college. I switched from C/C++ to Java at version 1.1. In addition, I have worked in a number of scripting and web technologies over the years.

My experience ranges from building workflow systems and interfaces to a number of languages, to insurance and lately eCommerce. I owned the technical side of the Gift Certificate Service at Amazon.com working with many Amazonians on the business side. I was also part of the on-call rotation for the eCommerce Service (ECS), now called the Amazon Associate Web Service (AAWS). researching bugs, handling operational issues, and anything else that came up during the rotation. I enjoy a challenging environment with a dynamic and experienced team who really know how to get stuff done. The people you work with make all the difference!

On the technical side I have a wide range of experience with various technologies. I enjoy learning new technologies and using them in my work. My first task at Amazon was to enhance the performance of a Perl CGI though I didn't know Perl at the time. A list of technologies can be found in my specialties list below.

On the business side I usually find myself working with managers and customers in a variety of ways. I enjoy this kind of interaction because it helps keep the code focused. Understanding those who use what is written is top priority in my opinion.
