Marc Thompson, Ph.D.’s Summary

President of Thompson Consulting Inc., a custom R/D company in the Boston/Worcester area. TCI specializes in custom analog, magnetic, power electronics and power supply design, fabrication, analysis, testing and debugging. I have the BSEE, MSEE, Electrical Engineer's and PhD degrees from MIT.

More info is at my company website: http://members.aol.com/marctt/index.htm

I am also Adjunct Associate Professor of Electrical Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Instutute, where I teach graduate and corporate EE courses, in the areas of analog design, power electronics, power distribution and electromechanics and electric motors.

Author of textbook "Intuitive Analog Circuit Design," Elsevier Science, 2006. Co-author of textbook "Power Quality in Electrical Systems," (with Dr. Alexander Kusko), McGraw-Hill, 2007. Contributor of 3 chapters to "Analog Circuits (World Class Designs)," edited by Bob Pease, available from Elsevier, 2008.

Full CV is available upon request. Principals only, please.

Marc Thompson, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Analog, power electronics, magnetic design, Maglev, electromagnetics; failure analysis and expert witness work for companies, attorneys and insurance companies. Industry seminars in electronics and magnetic design.
