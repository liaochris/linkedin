Peter Trzeciak’s Summary

Softhird Consulting LLC is a dynamic company, with a mission to design, implement and support software and hardware systems, based on Open Source solutions. Softhird Consulting LLC is located in Tampa Bay area, but operates worldwide, delivering its products using advanced Internet protocols. Softhird Consulting LLC is a limited liability company, organized under the laws of the State of Florida. Our focus are both small and large businesses, that can benefit from using Open Source and rapid development approach to improve their business model. In addition to the wide range of hardware-related services, we provide extensive software and Web development solutions.
