Jos Witteveen’s Summary

Executive Business and IT leader with proven track record in Logistics, Pharmaceuticals, Wholesale Companies and Information Technology. Over 15 years of experience in Senior Management positions aimed at putting his broad experience and skills to work for Business Growth and success. Track record in (re)organising service organisations for increased professional Service Levels. Fos example; assignment in a major Pharmaceutical Wholesale Company resulted in successfully reducing Costs (over 50%) and at the same time vastly increase Service Levels and Customer Satisfactionand while reducing complexity of IT systems and infrastructures.
