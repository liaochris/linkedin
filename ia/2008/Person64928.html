Francis Dimayuga’s Summary

Francis is senior manager of the search marketing practice at iMarketing LTD. A seasoned search marketing professional his team is responsible for the strategy, execution, management & overall performance of clients paid search campaigns. His experience includes working with clients in the pharmaceutical, business services, retail, finance, telecom, & automotive industries.

Francis came to iMarketing from his position as Search Supervisor at NeoSearch@Ogilvy, the search marketing division of Ogilvy & Mather. There, Francis was responsible for leading the search marketing strategy & management for the Novartis & Pitney Bowes accounts. Previously, Francis was a Senior Account Manager at Did-It Search Marketing where he has had the opportunity to work with top online brands such as Coach, Bluefly, PetCareRX, Deluxe Checks, Capital One, GotVMail, Paltalk, and Date.com.

Prior to joining Did-It in 2003, Francis spent 4 years working for computer software giant CA, working in various sales/marketing, & client relations roles and earning a sales support MVP award during his tenure.

Francis holds a BA in Psychology from Stony Brook University and an MBA in General Management from Dowling College.
