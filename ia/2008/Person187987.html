Thomas Anderson’s Summary

Leader of integrated supply chain organizations. Proven, effective and creatively flexible with experience developed across multiple industries. Special expertise in integrating supply chains and problem resolution leading to improved relations and hard cost reductions. Innovations lead directly to profit and balance sheet improvement.

Experience in all Supply Chain Management disciplines including: strategic sourcing and procurement; inventory control and materials management; customer service and technical support; demand planning and management; distribution and network planning; warehousing and transportation; manufacturing, assembly (skilled and unskilled), design and manufacturing engineering.
