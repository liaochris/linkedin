Colin Campbell’s Summary

Over 21 years experience in the animation and visual effects industry. I started shooting animated effects programming Tondreau motion control systems on Oxberrys and Mechanical Concepts downshooters, stage, and matte stand rigs. For the past 14 years I have been a digital compositor using a variety of software packages including Shake, Nuke, and proprietary packages.
