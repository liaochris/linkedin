Megan Fitzgerald’s Summary

I am an expat career and entrepreneur coach. I help expats use their personal brand to build a career or business that 'fits' who they are and their international lifestyle.

Having designed a portable career and business that supports my life abroad, I use an entrepreneurial approach to help expats find more freedom and success in their professional lives. Because I believe the key to a person's success lies in communicating their unique value in a differentiating way, personal branding is at the core of my approach.

My clients are forward-thinking, embrace technology and are willing to invest time and energy to reach their goals. They love learning and are open to trying new strategies because they want to thrive - not just survive - in the global market place. They see that building their brand on- and off-line are essential to their success.

With over 15 years of experience in career and business development, I've worked with professionals and entrepreneurs across the globe. My passion for travel, culture and service has taken me to over 25 countries on 5 continents to live, work or play. I've developed business training programs for entrepreneurs from Africa, Asia, Europe, North and Latin America and the Middle East, as well as career development programs for professionals and students worldwide.

Committed to excellence and applying innovative strategies to help my clients succeed, I am constantly working to hone my craft.
My credentials include:
• Certified Personal Branding Strategist - 1 of the first 100 worldwide
• Certified Career Coach
• Certified Executive Coach
• Certified Online Identity Strategist – 1 of the first 35 worldwide
• Certified 360 Personal Brand Assessment Analyst
• Trained in entrepreneurial and business coaching
• Masters Degree in Multimedia Communications

When not working I enjoy travel, film and a caffè macchiato to start my day.
