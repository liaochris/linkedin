    SuKadem Foundation

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    July 2007 — Present (10 months)

    www.sukadem.com
    "SuKadem" is a Native American name that embodies powerful cultural values. It is a name of honor, responsibility, honesty, integrity, cooperation and empowerment. The name and the qualities associated with it describe the entire SuKadem Project. The SuKadem Project has been divided into four components -- the SuKadem Construction Company, SuKadem Foundation, Chachoosen Center and ChaChoosen Village.

    SuKadem Foundation

    A 501(c)(3) organization which has been established to provide education/training for the Lummi Tribal members, Individuals from the surrounding Whatcom County area, employees and companies and businesses that choose to locate in ChaChoosen Center, a business park that is the third project component. The Foundation has a board of directors currently totaling 8 individuals, but with the potential to grow to 20 members.
