George Best’s Summary

A successful, seasoned, global general business executive who attracts top talent and builds loyal, high performing teams. With significant P&L, finance and operational management experience will build a worldwide organization and establish an exceptional record in revenue and profits thus providing an excellent return to the investors. Focus is on companies with revenues from Start-up to $200M.

As President, restructured the international division of a US multinational Telecommunications Equipment Manufacturer that saw revenues increase by 56% to $100 Million in 18 months. This was accomplished by amalgamating sales and support for the direct subsidiary and distributor operations. Managed manufacturing and R&D operations in China, Canada and Australia and the UK. Contracts with British Telecom, Mercury, Cable and Wireless and France Telecom, Shell Oil, HSBC Bank, Citi Bank, Nikki, and Hitachi among others.

As President and COO of the start-up of a Canadian and US long distance telecommunication switch reseller from its inception to its successful attainment of $5 million per month within 12 months. Developed all selling and marketing strategies, hire all staff and developed plan for continued expansion. Oversaw the installation of Northern DMS 250s, the lease of T-1 & T-3 circuits, the purchase of Newbridge 3600 Multiplexers and the implementation of a billing system. (The company eventually reached $500 million and was sold for $1.6 Billion)

As COO and Owner implemented the business plan for the startup of this broadband wireless company. Target market was industrial subdivisions around major cities across North America. Strategy was to invest in each subdivision, obtain a minimum of 30 clients and then move on to the next. Achieved first industrial subdivision target in 30 days, prepared and started implementation of expansion plans into Toronto, Ontario and negotiated for $3 million in VC financing from Toronto-based firm
