Jose Pino’s Summary

Principal Engineer in the EEsof division of Agilent Technologies. Responsible for the ESL products, mixed signal simulation and verification technologies.

Jose Pino’s Specialties:

* System-level simulation technologies
* Co-simulation of heterogeneous design languages including: SystemC, VHDL, Verilog, Verilog AMS, MATLAB
* DSP design methodologies
* RF/mixed-signal simulation
* C++, Perl, DSP fixed-point assembly
* Agilent ADS, Cadence ADE, SystemVue
