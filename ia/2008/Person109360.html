Kristian Themstrup’s Summary

After graduating from the University of Maryland, College Park in 1998 with a Bachelors Degree in English, I left Philadelphia and traveled to the Seattle area to begin work. I started at Apria Healthcare as a billing consultant and through various promotions attained the position of Regional Revenue Qualification Supervisor for the Northwest Region. In this position, I managed all the government compliance activities for the NW branches and coordinated with the corporate office.

After more than 5 years in this position, I moved on to a local search engine optimization company InternetAdvancement.com to begin learning sales and internet marketing. At this company, I learned the ins and outs of direct sales, customer support, and the intriguing field of search engine optimization. Over my 3+ years employed here, I implemented and managed many optimization and marketing campaigns with significant results. During this time I learned everything I could about internet marketing while also managing a sales team that grossed over $1,000,000 in sales per year.

Since then, I have moved on to iePlexus, where I currently work as a Training Manager & Website Architect. I am responsible for designing and managing our website, blog and corporate identity, as well as all sales training. We specialize in search engine, social media and blog marketing. I also run a small logo design business, Seattle Logo Design (www.seattlelogodesign.com) and a few personal websites that I designed and built myself.

I truly love what I do and am a sponge for new information. It's exciting and scary to be involved in a startup, but I really believe in my company and the people involved. My plans for the future are to grow rich with iePlexus, manage several websites and businesses of my own, and buy things on a whim just because I can.
