Michelle Hopkins’s Summary

Michelle is a senior ASIC Design Engineer and the owner of Sagetek Consulting, LLC. Sagetek provides ASIC and FPGA design and test services. We assist our clientele with the conception, design and test of integrated circuits. Sagetek provides consulting services in addition to employee referrals to semiconductor companies.

Simulation: VCS, NCSim, Modelsim, Verilog-XL, Silos, Summit Visual HDL
Static Timing: Primetime
DFT: TetraMax, Fastscan, TestKompress
