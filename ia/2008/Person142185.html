Nitin Todi’s Summary

I always had a goal of giving my best in whatever I do and I was confident that this will give me the career growth, monetary benefits, recognition and most of all job satisfaction which I need from my career.

I had keen interest in working for a Service sector organization as I saw lot of potential in it for me individually and also the sector as a whole.

Started off my career with an Logistics & Express(Blue Dart) company where in I learnt the basics of business & work. I was actively involved in Area Mapping, competition tracking & Key Account Management. I worked for a year there after which I planned a switch into the Online / Internet Industry which again I saw had tremendous scope of growth and was growing at the speed of knotts. I got an opportunity with naukri.com (Info Edge India Ltd) where in spend two and half years learning how internet can be effectively used as a medium to get better results than the conventional medium of Print. When I left I was responsible for a team of 6 management graduates with work experience and was handling business worth 2.5 Crores.

Currently I am employed as Head Business Development with Geotel Information Technologies handling business development for 6 internet properties of Geotel & also working for clients as an Online Ad agency where in we provide clients with customized solutions to generate traction & get results using tools on the internet.
We promote our services like Search Engine Marketing (SEM), E Mailer campaign, Site specific media buying & community building.

To sum it up I have dedicated interest in the Internet / Online Advertising field and have an experience of 3 yrs into the Internet space & a total of 4 yrs of experience.
