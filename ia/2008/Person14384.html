Seth Carus’s Summary

I'd like to start a revolution of the opposable thumb. Next gen? It's time to design an entirely different species of game. Something alien; something much closer to home.
I believe there's a movement beginning to form and we're all aching to start something that can't be stopped. We want to break all the rules without cheating. We want to evolve past the recycle and return to something original, the authentic. Somewhere in the middle of all this technique we've been developing for years and the completely new creative direction that social games are building for us, is the beginning of our future as game makers.
