Tom Hood’s Summary

Tom is recognized as one of the leaders of the CPA Profession in the U.S. due to his active involvement in the CPA Profession as a State CPA Society Executive, strategic planning consultant, speaker, and as a volunteer.

He led the national CPA Vision Team through the implementation phase from 1998 – 2003 and has experience working with CPA firms, national committees, and corporations around the country as a strategic planner and facilitator. He is co-author of the High Perfomance Model for CPA Firms and Organizations and the Competency Ladder for CPAs.

His passion is helping organizations develop and implement strategies to ccreate high performance organizations that can thrive in the rapidly changing environment.

Tom Hood’s Honors:

He is also the lead author in the webblog dedicated to CPA Success – www.cpasuccess.com and one dedicated to New/Young Professionals – www.newcpas.com.
