Peter Young’s Summary

Distinguished leadership career of building successful high technology groups encompassing all aspects of product development and support functions to deliver outstanding business results over the last twenty years. Strong leadership demonstrated through organizational development, team work and a passion for execution to meet Customer expectations.

• Practical visionary and technology evangelist with frequent speaking engagements at tradeshows and conferences. Enjoy interaction with Customers and industry community
• Customer centric in the understanding and definition of the business and technical requirements.
• Builder of strong multicultural and multilingual high performance teams. Created laboratories across US, England, Czech Republic, Russia, Israel, India and China.
• Broad knowledge of system software, application development tools, networking and computer systems.
• Practical understanding of global business practices though living and working overseas.
