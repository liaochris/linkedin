Linda Lynch’s Summary

KI Technology Group provides IT support to small business in mid Michigan. Our staff will keep your network up and running so that you can focus on your business. In my role as President, I focus on finding tools that we can use to improve the services we deliver to our clients. As a company, we strive to form partnerships with clients and serve not only as their service provider, but also as a trusted technology advisor. My goal is to develop KI Technology Group into a leading technology services provider in the Greater Lansing area by developing strong relationships with our clients.
