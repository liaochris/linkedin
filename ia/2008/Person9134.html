Alejandro Amezquita’s Summary

Professionally qualified Food and Bioprocess Engineer with a wide range of experience in the food industry. Accomplished in areas relating to the safe design of food products. Experienced in the practical aspects of thermal processing and thermobacteriology of foods in industrial environments. Highly-skilled in the area of mathematical modelling of processes typically found in the manufacturing of foods, as well as modelling of microbial responses to such processes. Able to work on own initiative and as part of a team. Capable of leading projects and holding people accountable under minimal supervision.
