Vincent Arlas’s Summary

Since August 2006 Vincent is in charge of setting up all the reporting needed to be in compliance with SOX/Bill 198 (52-109, etc...) involved in the Finance Dpt as well as in the IT dpt, for publicly traded OCEANEX, a Maritime Transport company. Partners are Raymond Chabot Grant Thorton (consultants) and Ersnt & Young (external auditors).

From early 2001 to mid 2004, Vincent Arlas set up, organized and managed the finance department of Memscap, Inc. in California, subsidiary of Memscap SA (France). Officer of the company, Vincent was in charge of all legal, financial, HR and IT issues pertaining to the entity. He managed up to 5 people directly.

Previously Vincent for Mentor Graphics in the Paris office as a Financial Controller. He was promoted to that position from Assistant Controller. His areas of responsabilities included Finance and Accounting (reporting, compliance, statutory accounts, budgeting, audits, etc...), HR, Legal and IT. He joined Mentor Graphics in June 1999 after having left Evian Waters (Danone Group) where he was a Financial Analyst for a year.

Vincent Arlas’s Specialties:
