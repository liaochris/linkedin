Javier Lopez’s Summary

Mr. Lopez has over 15+ years experience in the Software Development field. He has developed expert systems for General Motors and Hughes Space Communications. Mr. Lopez's responsibilities have included project specifications, design, documentation, development, and resource management. Mr. Lopez also functions as the primary development interface to clients for projects he oversees.

Mr. Lopez has extensive experience in developing sophisticated expert systems and is fluent with a wide range of programming languages and techniques including: C, C++, Java, J++, Pascal, Ada, Scheme, Lisp and SQL. Prior to Move.com, Mr. Lopez was the Director of Software Development at Internet Solutions Group. There he managed Internet Solutions Group’s development team and provided both design and guidance for their client projects. At Hughes Space and Communications he helped develop various Client Server applications and facilitated network integration of systems in launch facilities. Mr. Lopez was also responsible for the development of numerous heuristic systems at General Motors and guidance programs at Hughes Missile Company.

Mr. Lopez completed his Masters in Computer Science, Software Programming at UCLA.
