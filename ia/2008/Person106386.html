Keith Hanks’s Summary

Keith is an emerging MBA graduate with passion for social media and Web 2.0 technologies. Mr. Hanks is an Account Manager at the Atlanta-based search engine marketing firm TwentySix2 Marketing where he works with a team dedicated to providing leading edge SEO and SEM services.

Beginning with his undergraduate experiences Keith learned to work with a variety of diverse people from many cultures. This skill was further advanced through his work at Iron Mountain Digital where he fosters relationships internally and externally with clients.

Mr. Hanks earned his MBA from Providence College with a concentration in Marketing. Over the course of one year's research he completed an extensive Master Thesis titled "Non-Traditional Marketing Impacting Multi-Collaborative Networks."
- http://www.providence.edu/mba/theses/keith_hanks/
Keith graduated Cum Laude from Bryant College with a BSBA in Marketing and Computer Information Systems.

A strong proponent of work / life balancing, in his spare time Keith enjoys motoring (mini cooper driving), running, tennis and going to cafes with friends. Keith was born in Worcester, MA and currently resides in Atlanta, GA with his fiancé Nicole. They are planning an October, 2008 wedding.
