Karen Adams’s Summary

Karen Adams is a finance professional with more than 25 years experience helping companies achieve financial and operational discipline while allowing management teams to focus on achieving their companies’ strategic goals.

Karen provides interim finance and accounting services through K J A Associates, LLC, which was established in 1993, to service early stage businesses or businesses that are generally in a high growth or transitional mode and who have a need for a part-time professional.

In the past fourteen years Karen has worked with over 25 companies, primarily privately-held, venture backed businesses in the areas of technology or life
sciences, with a focus on financial forecasting and management reporting.

Engagements can run for years or months, generally dictated by the growth trajectory of the company.
