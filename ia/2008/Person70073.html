    CV at KBA:
    Ms. Youner joined KBA in 1987 and has since worked in conjunction with the two partners on many of their larger projects, becoming the firm’s first Associate Architect.
    Ms. Youner is adept at managing complex, long-term projects and is skilled in project management, working closely with clients in order to determine the perfect balance of interior design elegance and project budget. Projects include renovation of the NYU Law School, Siemens Corporate Offices, MLB Offices, SCAN NY headquarters, as well as a wide range of Lobby interiors and residences. She has extensive experience with on-site Construction Supervision, an ability to listen to the clients, develop a project according to their requirements and help realize their vision, as well as incorporating the latest materials and technologies.
