Andria Tay’s Summary

A Senior Consumer Marketing Executive with over 15 years experience in entertainment & technology marketing and promotions for major cable TV network, online & mobile entertainment services, record label & distribution corporations. An outstanding leader with expertise in management, analysis and strategic planning as well as tactical execution. Consistent track record building teams, motivating and mentoring staff for optimal performance. Skilled directing product management, artist development, new media marketing, marketing communications, corporate communications, branding, publicity and advertising. Proven ability to devise successful integrated marketing campaigns regardless of budget. Strong communication and diplomacy skills. Versatile, creative, resourceful and highly motivated.

Andria Tay’s Specialties:

integrated marketing strategy and execution, consumer & channel marketing, brand marketing, new media/ online marketing, product marketing, marketing communications, corporate communications, partnership marketing, teambuilding and organizational development, video production, DVD product development & marketing, project management, artist development, technology & new formats expertise, entertainment marketing, sports marketing
