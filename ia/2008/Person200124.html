Bill Rowe’s Summary

I have extensive experience creating and testing customer specific solutions from available industry products.
Such products include Avaya Communication Manager, Cisco Call Manager, many vendor's routers and switches, VPN equipment, Linux and Windows Servers.

I've tested with tools from Empirex (Hammer), Spirent (Abacus, Smartbits), Ixia, Mu Security, Agilent, Genoa and many "freeware" tools from sites such as www.insecure.org for security penetration test.
