Scott Sparrold’s Summary

Fifteen years experience in optical design for production friendly systems such as catalog vision solutions to cutting edge innovations such as free form conformal optics for aerodynamic missile domes.

In addition I have analyzed and reduced stray light in optical systems, conducted illumination design and optical metrology.

I have experience with laser systems, hyperspectral systems, and visible through infrared passive systems.

I like to instill enthusiasm and practical optics knowledge with the next generation of engineers, scientist and technicians!

Scott Sparrold’s Specialties:

* Lens design & analysis utilizing Code V and Zemax.
* Stray light analysis/reduction w/Fred, ASAP & LightTools
* Dabbled in thin film design & analysis with Essential Macleod
* Novice optomechanical design & drawings in Solidworks
* Knowledgable with optical & opto-mechanical drawings
* Author on six published papers in conformal optics
