Brian Begy’s Summary

Thanks to turmoil in the Middle East and poor job prospects in academia, Brian Begy never finished his Ph.D. thesis on the commercial history of the Persian Gulf. He speaks Arabic fluently, albeit with a hysterical Midwestern American accent.

He received his B.A. from Knox College in 1993 and his M.A. from the University of Chicago in 1995. He learned programming in graduate school, because it paid better than teaching assistantships.

He develops primarily for Microsoft’s .NET platform and spent seven years as an independent before expanding to a four person staff.

Chicago Data Solutions is a small web development, database programming, and general computer consulting/IT services company based in the Andersonville neighborhood of Chicago.

We are basically doing two things these days: building the greatest .NET bug tracking application in human history: www.bugsentry.com, and doing a ton of consulting/custom software development for Chicago based clients. Our recent experiences include building an application to transfer Homeless Management Information Strategies (HMIS) data from client's existing applications into the City of Chicago's systems.
