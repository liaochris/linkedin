Rick Kelo’s Summary

I recruit for MRINetwork; the largest recruiting firm in the world. Specifically, I work in the #4 office globally out of over 1,300.

I oversee the Accounting and Finance practice for our firm in Chicago, although I myself do more work in Tax than any other single area.

In the Chicago area we recruit for over half the Fortune 500 companies headquartered in town; approximately half of the Fortune 1000 companies; and a select group of privately-held companies that are either high growth or dominant in their industry.

The second link above is my YouTube site, where I post a 90 second video discussing each new search we begin working. The "RSS feed" link will send you a two sentence summary as I add new video job postings to YouTube. Only takes a couple minutes a month to stay connected to what is going on in the job market. Hopefully your career's worth a 30 minute investment per year!
