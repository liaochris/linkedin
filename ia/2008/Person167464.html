Bob Citelli rcitelli@sales-and-marketing.net’s Summary

Current clients include SEO tools provider River Gold (www.wordtracker.com); DRM experts Exent Technologies (www.exent.com); Javien Digital Payments Solutions (www.javien.com); Toucan Global which makes P2P communications products (www.toucanglobal.com); entertainment software publisher Viva Media (www.viva-media); Bid4Vid, an online marketplace matching videographers with brand marketers (www.bid4vid.com), web-based software analysis provider System Requirements Lab (www.canyourunitcom); the online video game rental service Gamerang (www.gamerang.com); software developer/publisher Cosmi(www.cosmi.com) and online tutorial services from HomeworkHelp.com (www.homeworkhelp.com).

Recent accomplishments include biz dev and affiliate marketing efforts for Cosmi delivering IP to republish from Eidos, Strategy First, Auran Games, Topware and others. We established electronic distribution for Viva Media titles with iWin, Big Fish Game, Metaboli,Trygames and Valve Steam.

Previously with former client Real Networks’ Trymedia/Trygames(www.trygames.com). we acquired IP for distribution from publishers Disney, UbiSoft, Take 2/2K Games, Cosmi, Avanquest, Capcom and others who used ActiveMARK Technology for DRM. We increased affiliate placements for Trygames with programs with GameStop, AOL, Buy.com, Ziff Davis and Bigfish Games.

At Gamerang, we established distribution and sales programs for with Target.com, The Minneapolis Star Tribune and secured a used games program with Overstock.com. Other business development initiatives here included cross promotions with leading consumer buff books and online positions within leading game player web sites.

At River Gold (www.wordtracker.com) we've secured data assets from Lycos and continue to work with other ISPs, telecoms and related businesses to increase the efficiency of the tools this organization resells.
