John Melanson’s Summary

I've been lucky enough to work in varied areas of energy management, computers, signal processing (DSP and analog) and IC design for over 35 years. I figure I've got another 30 years or so of serious contribution, but hope to take some time someday to finish writing a book (on the history of paper). In the interim, I humor myself with hiking and jewelry design and creation.
