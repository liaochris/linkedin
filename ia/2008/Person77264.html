Howard Mueller’s Summary

Founded mfg/Search in 1989 to offer executive search services that focus on positions, generally in smaller privately owned manufacturing firms, where the owner/CEO/COO/President is personally invested in the successful search outcome. After earning a BSE at the University of Michigan and an MBA at the Harvard Business School, from 1963 until 1989 held successively more responsible management positions in small business units of mid-sized manufacturing companies. Rely heavily now on that early hard-earned personal experience, not easily found in the search industry, to source and evaluate candidates requested by mfg/Search clients.

    (Management Consulting industry)

    1989 — Present (19 years)

    For the last 19 years have provided executive search assistance to clients in construction, distribution, engineering services, logistical, and manufacturing industries. Searches have ranged from skilled trades workers to company officers with fees designed to be well below the norm for retained assignments. We take particular care to ensure that our results are attractive to company owners and CEO/COO level executives who want to fill important positionws with a wider selection of candidates and with greater speed, confidentiality, and discretion than can normally be done by internal personnel.
