Don Anderson’s Summary

My current focus is on application design and development using Flash AS3 with additional projects including 3D animation and video production.

For over the past 16 years I've pursued multimedia design and animation taking advantage of a strong programming background to take complex engineering concepts and create clear, concise multimedia products. As a freelancer I've worked on non-timeline Flash AS 3.0 Object Orient programming to develop dynamic Flash apps.

Throughout it all I've focused on continuing to learn and thinking outside the box to deliver results.

Don Anderson’s Specialties:

Object Oriented Flash Action Script 3.0 programming and interface design.

Additional expertise with 3D Animation/Illustration, Digital Video Production using Lightwave, Maya, Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, InDesign and GoLive), Final Cut Pro and Motion.
