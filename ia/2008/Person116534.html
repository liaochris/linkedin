Matt Luckham’s Summary

An exceptional IT professional, with over 15 years commercial experience. Matt is a strong communicator, good team leader and motivator. Matt has a solid understanding of all areas of IT from working at the highest levels of IT management.

The services Matt currently offers are IT consultancy, IT project management and the design, development and implementation of Microsoft SQL Server databases, software and associated technologies. Matt has worked with Microsoft SQL Server for almost 10 years, developing numerous databases and complex analytical information solutions. He has developed numerous software systems including being a the joint architect for BizMap Pro - a SAAS software product being used by companies such as KFC, Restaurant Group etc.

He holds two company directorships and thrives to deliver the very best service for his clients.

Matt Luckham’s Specialties:

Technical specialities are anything to do with databases, data management and software development. He is currently developing database software in SQL 2005 and C#. He enjoys managing complex projects and getting the most out of the people working on them.
