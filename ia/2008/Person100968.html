Justin Gardner’s Summary

Currently, my position at AMC Theatres involves concepting and writing all marketing communications for the company, as well as researching social media trends and technologies.

In addition to my job, I maintain a popular bi-partisan, political blog called Donklephant.com, which boasts over 20,000 page views a week and a roster of over 20 authors.

From 2004 to 2006, I worked as a Communications Manager for kozoru, a startup that built a search technology that delivered answers to questions inside multiple IM clients.

Before that, I was a Copywriter for Muller + Company, where my work was recognized by Creativity, AdWeek, Graphis, AIGA and garnered numerous industry awards, including several Best of Show awards.
