Steven A. Culbreath’s Summary

As an immigration attorney, my cases revolve primarily around business immigration, assisting clients from the world over with working in the United States on various types of business- or employment-based visas.

I continually seek clients in need of legal services in the area of U.S. immigration law: B1/B2, E-1, E-2, E-3, F-1, H-1B, H-2B, H-3, J-1, L-1, O-1, TN, EB-1, EB-2, EB-3, EB-5, DV-Lottery, LPR, Green Cards, Visas, U.S. Citizenship (Naturalization), change of status, extension of status, adjustment of status, Labor Certifications (PERM).

Steven A. Culbreath’s Specialties:

My immigration practice focuses heavily on business owners, entrepreneurs, investors, managers, executives, scientists, researchers, academics, degreed professionals, specialized workers, inventors, artists, and athletes, to name a few.

I also represent casual would-be visitors and prospective tourists to the United States, as well as foreign/international students, interns, trainees, exchange visitors, and short-term business visitors.
