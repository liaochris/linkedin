Gabe Garms (gabe@swiconsultants.com) 2600+’s Summary

I have been in computer software/high technology for over 11 years (over four of which in a recruiting role). I have successfully placed many candidates in many different positions within high technology companies. I currently specialize in placing engineers, developers, qa analysts and technical sales people within IT departments of cutting edge technology companies. I believe that my work is an extension of who I am as a human being, thus integrity is and always will be my number one priority. I strive to create win/win situations for both the candidates and companies that I work with, for I am here to build lasting relationships. I don't look at other recruiters as competition, and I welcome to connect with them, for there are plenty of opportunities to go around. A wise person once told me to be the change that I want to see in the world and I strive everyday to treat everyone I cross paths with with the utmost of respect and integrity. I want to let people know that there are recruiters out there who actually strive to make a difference through their work and not just a buck. I am currently using Linkedin to find qualified candidates for the positions that I am currently staffing and to network in general.
