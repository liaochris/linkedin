Jerry Choate’s Summary

Jerry Choate is a Seasoned Business Development Professional with 16 years of professional experience that has developed into a focus in nano-, micro-, and small cap companies of growth sectors in life science, technology, and market niche sectors. Acknowledge throughout career for the capacity to initiate, maintain and develop positive business relationships.

Qualification Highlights

* Extensive experience in Sales, Information Technology Management, Telecommunication Design, B2B Business Processes and Internet Technology, Marketing, Training Development, Project Management, Financial Analysis, Staffing, Budgeting, Operations and Business Development.
* Deep sector-specific knowledge focused exclusively on growth opportunities of life sciences and technology.
* Excellent presenter with a proven ability of interfacing with senior management and institutional investors without the deal hungry bias, yet informed by prolific deal experience and research: PIPEs transaction insight.
* Actively manage a database of over 2000 companies in growth sectors with detailed information based on visits, discussions and in person briefings. Routinely update the database based on interviews with senior management.

Jerry Choate’s Specialties:

Reverse Mergers and PIPEs in the micro- and nano-cap arena. Recent deals include AMAR, CSUH, ADH, PP, MACM, DSKA, CLRT and DORB. Consultant to a hedge fund with a focus in the emerging markets; annualized return for the past 2 years over 22%. Actively involved in deal analysis, structuring and sourcing.
