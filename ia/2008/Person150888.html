Phil Guo’s Summary

Phil Guo (Guo Jingjin), born and grown in Shanghai, with his previous exploration in entrepreneurship world, has been trying to get into a new field merged with business essence and nonprofit mission.

He is currently working at Executive Learning Center (ELC), a learning consulting firm he's partnership with which providing integrated learning solutions for corporate talents. He knows it is the one of his steps in the pursuit of his dream.

His larger ideal and deeper passion is about social innovation and entrepreneurship field. Renewing the traditional education system, and protecting the earth environment are two of his favorite areas. His objective in coming years, to initiate a nonprofit on a social mission while in a sustainable business way, would drive him contribute more talent to this field.

In the other field of his favorite he has been into - human resource, he's been spending time studying adding the value on personal human capital in the professionals' market, the ultimate communication with a psychological angle, and the discovery and development of human potential.

As the corporate world provided opportunities for him to get experience and skills of being professional and effective in working, Phil knows it won't be the place he can make full use of his gifts and maximize his potentials.

Phil was born a peer leader while his leadership style is more like a humble and open one. He was leading a fair amount of groups of cross-college students on lots of social entrepreneurship and not-for-profit programs when he was still in college. His clear vision for the future of himself and every team he works with helps him stay visionary and decisive when envisioning the big picture for his teams.

His influence covers NGOs like AIESEC, Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE), and Junior Achievement (JA) that are the place he contributed and worked with.

Phil Guo’s Specialties:

Sophisticated skills in interpersonal communication, team motivating, group training. Strong leadership and entrepreneuship.
