Teresa Morrow’s Summary

I have over 15 years experience in the executive administrative profession. In late 2005, I decided to leave the corporate world and join with my husband to handle the daily operations of his floor cleaning business, Morr Services, Inc.

As I was getting more exposure for my husband's business to get online, I was checking for networking groups. One of the first was a women organization that I am very active in still is WECAI - Women's eCommerce Association International.

In 2007, I decided to start my own virtual assistant/online promotion business called Key Business Partners. I wanted to have a way to share my knowledge and my passion to help others. I also really like talking and getting to know others and what they do and finding ways to help them connect with others that will help their businesses grow.

I am a native Floridian...(yes, there aren't too many of us out there). I have lived in other cities in Florida, however, not lived in any other state. I really do enjoy living in Florida (for the most part). Sometimes the heat can get to be too much and I am not too keen about hurricane season. However, I have had a few opportunities to live other places and haven't left yet.

Teresa Morrow’s Specialties:

I have handled many online events such as teleseminar, webinars and web conferences for many clients. I also specialize in helping authors promote their books online by conducting virtual book blog tours.
I have over 15 years in the administrative industry working with start up to Fortune 500 companies in different industries such as financial planning, landscape architecture, floor cleaning and property management.
