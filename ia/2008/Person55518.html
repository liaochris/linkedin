Dwayne Alexander’s Summary

Goals Inc. specialise in change management.

Goals Inc.'s purpose is to assist people and companies to manage change proactively by using a goals focussed approach.

For companies, whether your goals are to get more out of your people ,your technology or strategic "change" projects, we create action out of ideas. Tired of projects becoming too complex and losing steam. Goals Inc. specialises in stimulating action and results.

For individual goalgetters, Goals Inc. has developed livemygoals.com as one of our first tools to assist you to use technology to boost your personal performance and manage change positively, in life and in business.

1. We need to proactively take advantage of the web and its "reputation", "profile" and "revenue" opportunities.

2. We need to proactively retain and attract talent into our organisations- and investing in our staffs wellbeing will come straight back to the bottom line.

Livemygoals.com, the social network for goalgetters was launched in beta in January 2008. It is a valuable platform to connect companies, coaches & mentors and goal setters together to achieve positive, meaningful goals.

Before launching his own company, Dwayne held the position of CEO at apnfinda ltd. He joined APN in 2005 with a brief to reposition the directories and data business to capitalize on the online explosion. The company is now poised to become the market leader in online local seach in New Zealand.

He came to APN from Fairfax, where he managed the sales & administration teams and helped relaunch the Sunday Star-Times.

Dwayne holds bachelors and postgraduate honours (cum laude) degrees in marketing, and has 16 years’ business experience in senior media roles, including advertising, marketing and sales.

Married with two daughters, Dwayne spends his downtime kayaking and building a library of inspirational business and life stories.
