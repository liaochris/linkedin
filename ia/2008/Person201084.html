Will Robertson’s Summary

Will Robertson is an experienced sales and strategic marketing executive having held board level positions for the past 15 years. He is also an accomplished corporate and convention speaker with over 400 keynote speaking presentations delivered to Fortune 500 firms since 1995.

While doing platform presentations Will has also headed up sales and marketing organizations including Market Scan Information Systems, Multicom Communications, Phoenicia Communications, Lite Wave Communications and Performance Strategies, Inc.

Will founded and operates Performance Strategies, Inc. - a strategic sales process consulting firm with current clients in product and service based sales organizations. His flagship program is "Selling Below the Price Line." (tm)

PSI operates a subsidiary firm called LCA Studio Productions that is responsible for instructional design and production of training course ware, audio and video productions and live seminar delivery as well as LMS E-Learning (Internet Training) and PLU (Private Label University - also Internet delivered customized training). Productions include:

Over 250 audio and video training programs created and delivered to corporate teams.

Radio and TV productions. (Founded Entrepreneur Magazine's Internet Radio Show)

Executive Coaching in areas of communication and task management.

Sales training seminars including Selling Below the Price Line (tm)
Management training seminars including Managing the Box (tm)
Connecting With Customers (Phone Skills 201)
Teamwork is an Attitude (tm)
Sales Performance Tune Up for Auto Industry Sales Teams
