Robert Mercer’s Summary

Dynamic management career, building top-performing businesses and leading new concepts to profitability and top performing financial units – combined operations, sales, and marketing expertise in the design and implementation of systems that drive revenue, maximize production, strengthen market position, and heighten brand awareness in highly competitive markets. Career history of creating and directing sustainable and profitable growth, domestically and internationally for major industry players, Ex Libris, SAS Institute, and Dell. Demonstrated expertise in energizing organizations and achieving greater performance.

Achievements include: reengineering key sales programs, developing strategic sales direction, introducing new targeted marketing strategies, establishing value-focused sales programs, division operations turnarounds, and the orchestration of training curriculum for sales and management teams. Efforts have realized high-end revenue increases (+35%) and efficiency increases (+10%).

- Key leader in establishing strategic direction and vision, aligning personnel, products/services, and processes to exceed corporate objectives.

- Dynamic builder of teams, leading them to top-tier success while fully leveraging organizational strengths to enhance performance.

- Industry leading sales producer and business builder, successfully leading domestic and international business growth initiatives.

- Clear, concise, and innovative communicator, who understands that effective communication, either written or oral, is key to operational fluidity and the development of a unified team and organizational mission.

- Ability to analyze complex issues and assimilate new information quickly; mature judgment and self initiative.

- Natural communicator and liaison with C-level teams and board staff regarding the development, administration, and implementation of key SOPs, sales programs, metrics systems, and sales training curriculum.
