Allen Price’s Summary

Delivering creative leadership and management expertise in high growth, knowledge-based environments. I have a great deal of experience in organizational development, talent acquisition and retention, and operations management in a variety of industries.

I have provided executive level leadership for a variety of successful, venture backed, technology start up companies since 1993. I started, built, and lead the Human Resources departments at Salon.com, a widely honored on-line news and media outlet, and Intrepid Systems, a leading developer of integrated merchandise management and decision support software for the retail industry. Earlier still, I held management positions at a variety of arts and service organizations.

SPHR (Senior Professional in Human Resources) certification, 1995

Allen Price’s Specialties:

Human capital audits, designing and staffing operational departments from the ground up. Employee and leadership development, culture and change management, organizational development, benefits analysis and planning, employee relations, and process outsourcing
