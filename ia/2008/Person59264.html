Edward Costello’s Summary

I have five years' experience designing, developing, and managing the premier technology teams responsible for IBM's corporate web site and worldwide web site operations. I have considerable experience deploying technology solutions across a global enterprise, managing personnel and projects at a cross-corporate level of a Fortune-100 organization. In parallel I managed technology operations at three Olympic Games web sites (Atlanta, Nagano, Sydney) and either supported or managed technology operations at other key "event" web sites.

Since leaving IBM in 2001, I have managed application development and systems operations at a startup and consulted to and developed solutions for a various non-profit and commercial organizations.

I am currently available to consult on internet technology development, operations, and strategy in the Boston-New York-Washington corridor.
