Christopher Zammarelli’s Summary

In addition to being the library assistant at The Brookings Institution, I am a graduate student at the University of Maryland’s College of Information Studies. I will be writing a Master’s Thesis on e-government and expect to graduate in May 2008.

I am the chair-elect of SLA’s Government Information Division. I write the library science blog Libraryola, and I am the co-webmaster with James Quinn of Library Underground.
