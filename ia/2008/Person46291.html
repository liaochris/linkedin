David Cooke’s Summary

I bring passion in recharging and re-energizing sales programs. As Sales Cooke, I provide sales effectiveness expertise to businesses looking for a program to increase revenues and improve profits. I have developed a customized, strategic approach that is exactly what businesses need to refocus, re-energize and reposition their sales team. This strategy includes understanding and addressing the shifts in the market, incorporating a social media strategy into the sales program, and complimenting the strengths of the team while adding those missing ingredients necessary to turn sales results around. I bring 25 years of sales, marketing, and executive level management into play as I focus on improving the sales effectiveness of organizations. The key to all high performing sales organizations are strong relationships, a strategic mindset, teamwork, and effective training, development, and management. I am committed to building effective sales programs through this model.

David Cooke’s Specialties:

Sales Effectiveness Expertise--results oriented training, sales management development programs, coaching activities and strategic planning.
Social Media for Sales Strategies to connect businesses to their clients and their market.
High Performance Selling Organizations--effective, disciplined and results oriented sales models.
Fully Empowered Cross Functional Teams--leveraging the entire organization to meet sales and customer service expectations.
