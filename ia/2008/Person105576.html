Kevin Cox’s Summary

Provides music for film, television, video, web, new media.

I am a composer and musician in the Seattle area. Originally a drummer/percussionist with a strong classical as well as rock background, I'm also a singer and keyboardist and pride myself on making music that is affecting, emotive, and unique, and that is an appropriate fit for the media. My diverse musical influences, ranging from classical to electronica, folk to rock, world music to avante-garde, to musical theater add breadth to my composition and songwriting skills. I am also, in the words of a well-known composer, a “gifted melody writer.”

My philosophy is to always please the client and to serve the project by creating music appropriate for the media it is intended to accompany. I want to make sure you are 100% satisfied with the product you receive.

Kevin Cox’s Specialties:

Music composition, music production, orchestration, drumming, singing, keyboards, electronic music.
