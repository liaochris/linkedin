Joseph Loll’s Summary

Joseph Loll is a serial entrepreneur who has over 20 years of high-tech experience spanning traditional hardware manufacturing and software development to contemporary Internet consumer services and technologies. Joe has held numerous senior management roles with multiple start-ups, all of which have had multi-million dollar growth, successful revenue models and exit strategies. Three of which included IPOs - Concentric Network (Now XO Comunications), Aimnet (Now iPass) and RasterOps.
