Vineet Thapar’s Summary

Having fun while doing meaningful, inspiring work that makes an impact benefiting the trifecta of clients, consumers and creative minds. Thirteen creative years leading the conception, definition, design, and realization of brand-focused and user-centered digital experience solutions at Digitas, Razorfish, Jaape and Oden. Six years before that in traditional media with JWT and Oden.

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    Helped acquire new and maintain existing client relationships. Served as the primary Experience Lead on successful pitches and many resultant projects for clients such as Western Union, CHEP, Logica, International Flavors and Fragrances, World Wildlife Fund, Fortis USA, and Concord Communications, among others.

    Ensured the quality of ideas and deliverables from the Experience team. Helped broaden the skill base of network members by consistently encouraging cross-discipline engagement of network members. Shared responsibility for the team’s utilization, productivity, capacity and staffing.

  • Director of Interactive Design

    Oden

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    Helped develop and position Interactive Media as a competency internally and externally: Defined and established creative and project management processes.     Helped deepen existing client relationships, and pitch and acquire new projects from clients including FedEx, International Paper, Thomas & Betts, Maybelline New York, and Memphis College of Art among others.

    Directed the core Interactive team in addressing user experience, creative, project management, and technical issues. Supervised the team's interaction with a pool of creative and client service employees. Led a team structure that accommodated a flexible number of project participants with varying degrees of understanding of interactive media.
