Victoria Sandbrook’s Summary

I have a passion for pushing the traditional codex and written word into electronic form. My experiences are varied, but valuable: I've copy edited, proofread and fact-checked; run product reviews; acted as creative and managing editor along the life of a publication; developed process for the formation of a large, scholarly database; worked in electronic document production; written copy--academic, journalistic, and otherwise; undertaken the management of a department project tracking list; project managed various media updates to existing products; and am currently acting as development editor on a substantial revision of a successful visual rhetoric product. I'm pursuing a MA in Publishing & Writing at Emerson College. Afterwards...who knows. I'm learning to envision the next 1, 3, 5, and 10 years but to let them live themselves.
