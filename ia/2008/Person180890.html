Stephen Kowal’s Summary

Stephen Kowal Joined CSC in August 2006 as the Chief information Officer for the Australian Operations. Stephen has an extensive background in the commercial and IT environment, including prior roles as CIO of BHP Billiton Base Metals based in South America, CIO WMC and BHP Billiton IT Integration Lead in which he led the technical integration of WMC into BHP Billiton as part of the $9.2B acquisition. Earlier in this career he held IT and commercial roles within Cable and Wireless, Boral and the Department of Defence. Stephen has significant global experience having worked in, and lived in, North and South America and having run complex projects and lead teams in Australia, Europe, South Africa, North America and South America.

Stephen is a Fellow of the Financial Services Institute of Australasia (FFIN), a Senior Member of the Australian Computer Society (MACS) and a Member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors (MAICD). Stephen also serves on the Board of Directors for the Australian Computer Society and is a member of the CIO Executive Council.

He has a personal passion for developing the role of ICT in society and business, increasing the profile of ICT in schools and universities and improving the professionalism of the IT.
