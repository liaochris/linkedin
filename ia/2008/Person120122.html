Marco Ranieri’s Summary

Researcher at the Department of Economics of the University of Turin. I carried out research on structural economy, foreign direct investment and foreign trade in transition countries.
I speak Italian, English, Romanian, Spanish and little Bulgarian and I have very good research and analytical skills, as well as organizational and technical skills.

I have good communication and presentation skills and university teaching experience.
