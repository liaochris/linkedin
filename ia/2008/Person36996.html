Christopher Jones’s Summary

I have over 16 years of real estate experience in the areas of appraisal,property management and inspection. I have worked with several national lenders including GreenPoint Mortgage/Bank, World Savings and Wachovia.

As a State Certified Real Estate Appraiser in both NY and NJ, I have performed appraisals for all types of residential and small commercial properties. I am also FHA Approved.

I currently live and work in New Jersey. My appraisal experience is extensive in the Northern and Central NJ areas as well as Staten Island NY. I currently manage the real estate appraisal process in support of the Company’s NJ and PA residential real estate lending businesses to facilitate profitable growth while ensuring compliance with appraisal industry requirements, regulatory mandates and sound appraisal practice.
