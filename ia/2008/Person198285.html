Waila Skinner’s Summary

Waila Who am I?
I am a designer who loves color. I believe the psychology of color theory should be in every designer's arsenal of tools. I am person who believes that inspiration comes from everywhere. Who believes in vibrational healing and energy medicine. I believe that President Obama will be the most interesting President of the United States since Kennedy, maybe even Lincoln. But most of all I believe in the power of design. It shapes the world around us in ways that most aren't even aware of, it's capacity to motivate, sway and capture and audience. Santa Claus in his iconic red and white suit is the result of a ad campaign done by Coke-A-Cola in the early 20th century. The CEO of Motorola was so motivated by the Star Trek an idea of a communicator that he pioneered the cell phone. Ever notice how those blue tooth devices look like Uhura's ear piece?

And that is the world I am eager to participate in, the world of design, and color, and light. I expect transformation because design changes the world. The world is ever changing, like fate's tangled skein I am weaving the ever changing currents to interface with mood of the times. I am a designer.

"I found I could say things with color and shapes that I couldn't say any other way - things I had no words for."
-Georgia O'Keeffe
