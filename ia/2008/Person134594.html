Marcel Heijmans’s Summary

Being a hard core IT professional, I have a broad base and a solid technical education, covering both Computer Science and Software Engineering. This combination in hardware and software is important, because it provides specific insights. Even in an object oriented world, very disjoint from hardware, thorough knowledge regarding the internal system functions is of great advantage.

Working on a limited contract basis, with many different customers is another valuable asset. Cooperation with all kind of experts, who share their knowledge and experience, is good soil for excellent solutions. This way I have gained numerous experiences in branches such as: financials, telecommunication and utilities companies, petrochemical industry, IT services, government and retail.

The ideal role consists of multiple dimensions, where technical skills are intertwined with team leading and coaching. It all starts with an innovative project with research, architecture and development aspects. The emphasis is on cooperation and sharing ideas. My strengths are communicative skills and independence to guide and coach autonomous teams.

One of the most successful products developed by me is the “ItemPublisher”. This Web Content Management (WCM) environment is implemented and used by various organizations. Another successful concept is "Java EE Development Coaching", which is a mix of developing and coaching junior and medior developers.

Apart from the numerous projects and products, I'm also author of several publications in leading journals, such as "WebSphere Developers Journal" and I have presented a few technical seminars on conferences.
