I have more than 15 years of experience in Management and IT Consulting with extensive experience in Enterprise Resource Planning system implementations (Oracle/PeopleSoft), Project Management, Business Process Improvement, and Internet marketing strategies & implementations. About 360 View, Inc.
360 View, Inc. is a privately owned California S corporation with headquarters in Long Beach, California. The company is a business technology consulting firm specializing in solutions for small and medium sized enterprises (SMEs).

With a vision and passion to help SMEs succeed, our founder acquired a franchise license from the largest global Internet technology franchise - WSI, as a value-added leverage in 2002.

360 View, Inc. recognizes that majority of SMEs are not leveraging from Information Technologies because of lack of understanding, intimidation and confusion. As an experienced consulting firm, 360 View, Inc. provides workshops, seminars, and webinars to educate SMEs about the latest technologies.
