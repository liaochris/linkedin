Hernán Braberman’s Summary

Born in Buenos Aires in 1972, I graduated with First Class Honours from the University of Buenos Aires with a degree in Industrial Design before co-founding Tridimage, the first Argentine integrated graphic and structural packaging and product design consultancy. Since 1995 I am Design Director of Tridimage, responsible for the creative direction and design management of its industrial and graphic design projects. My experience in 2D and 3D packaging and product design, spanning across industries including food and beverage, wine, health and beauty, technology and consumer goods, covers aesthetics, branding, form, ergonomic, technical and functional aspects of packaging and products. I taught Industrial Design at the University of Buenos Aires and at ORT Institute of My work has been displayed in numerous exhibitions and featured in international design books, TV programmes and articles. My ongoing quest to emphasize the marriage of structural and graphic packaging design has been instrumental in starting Tridimage´s international expansion and gaining many of the clients for my company. Tridimage's reputation attracts calls from both established and emerging international companies operating in Latin America, U.S.A., Puerto Rico, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Mexico, Canada, Spain, Hungary, Romania and Germany. I was chosen as one of the top ten finalists of the British Council’s first International Young Design Entrepreneur of the Year (IYDEY) Award 2005, a worldwide competition organised in cooperation with 100% Design from London.
TRIDIMAGE was recently voted as the first Latin American design firm to win the 2007 PACKAGE DESIGN MAKEOVER CHALLENGE organised by the renowned PACKAGE DESIGN MAGAZINE. This annual feature showcases the branding, design and innovation capabilities of five select packaging design firms around the world.
