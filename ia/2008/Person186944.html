Teun den Dekker’s Summary

Born in 1983 in The Netherlands and always looking for new adventures. After a Bachelor in Services Management and a Master in Innovative Hospitality Management I was ready for a challenge. Something different. Working on things that matter, things that are fun! And all of this in an innovative and creative setting... Pffff, does such a work environment exist? Yes, it does! In 2006 I started working for Co-creations in Spain and from 2007 back home to develop Co-creations in The Netherlands.

Teun den Dekker’s Specialties:

I am always looking for answers. Putting pieces together seems to be an going process in my mind, where logic and creativy are going hand in hand.

Teun den Dekker’s Experience

  • Partner

    Co-creations S.L. - Innovation through co-creation in the areas of events and tourism experiences.

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Research industry)

    September 2006 — Present (1 year 7 months)

    At Co-creations we support our client's innovation process by conceptualizing, realizing and optimizing the products, services and experiences they offer.

    Changes in society ask for new approaches. Dialogues between equal partners are taking over traditional top down methods. Not just 'being there' and 'watching it' is what counts; it is the very process of co-creating the experience that adds value to today's human beings.

    We match our clients with their target. The secret behind the match lies in the process of co-creation. Innovative methods and tools enhance and optimize dialogues between them and you, the creator of their experiences.
