Colleen Jones’s Summary

For nearly a decade, Colleen has created successful interactive experiences and communications in industries such as health, telecommunications, and retail as well as for brands such as The Home Depot, CDC, Cingular Wireless, AutoTrader, Boston Scientific and more. Colleen enjoys distilling complex considerations into a simple interactive experience. Her approach centers on the user / customer while achieving business results. Her specialties are customer self-service, interactive persuasion, and interactive content strategy.

As a consultant, Colleen draws on a wealth of academic knowledge and real-word experience to deliver results. With an M.A. in technical communication, she has contributed several presentations and articles to the Society for Technical Communication and ACM SIGCHI. Colleen currently writes a column for the online magazine UXMatters and regularly writes articles for industry publications such as iMedia Connection.

Colleen's experience has depth and breadth. She has led and supported projects in diverse channels such as information-rich Web sites, interactive voice response systems, mobile applications, call center applications, kiosks, dynamically generated collateral, and more. She is familiar with research methods to understand users and customers, to develop strategy, and to test interactive designs. Colleen also has the practical skills and tools to create prototypes / simulations, wireframes, process flows, content plans, research reports, and other project deliverables.
