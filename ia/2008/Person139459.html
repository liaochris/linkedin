Neil Blecherman’s Summary

Senior Marketing and Corporate Development Executive

Twenty years in high tech marketing and corporate development at Intel, plus product design at Raytheon; cultivated Intel technology and business long-term partnerships with both OEM customers and major corporations including HP, Oracle, Adobe, Google, Yahoo!, Citrix, Symantec and others.

Led major Intel corporate development efforts; built high-output teams; developed "green field" business opportunities; managed win-win technology and product alignment; drove sales; delivered strong revenue growth; developed corporate branding and communications.

Strong technical, business, and management background; diverse software and hardware leadership experience managing corporate development, brand strategy, product management, Web2.0 strategy, mashup strategy, Enterprise ISV partnerships, and strategic planning; BSEE, plus an MBA from Wharton.
