Croom Lawrence’s Summary

Croom Lawrence is Vice President / Strategy and Insights in the Life Sciences practice of RTC Relationship Marketing (RTCRM), a Wunderman affiliate.

In his role, Croom is responsible for diagnosing brand challenges and delivering innovative traditional and non-traditional marketing strategies that drive growth for Fortune 100 life sciences brands. Croom has been recognized as one of the industries most innovative thinkers, and speaks regularly at industry conferences on topics ranging from e-Pharma strategy, integrated relationship marketing, and best practices in delivering high touch patient support programs for targeted therapies.

Over the past 10 years, Croom has developed consumer and professional integrated campaigns for both blockbuster and challenger brands in Cardiovascular Disease, Diabetes, Mental Health, Pain, RA/OA, Fitness & Prevention, Menopause, Osteoporosis, and Men’s Health.

Croom also helps Life Sciences organizations establish centers of excellence in innovation – prior to RTCRM, Croom helped develop and grow the Wyeth Consumer Communications and e-Marketing group, and was an early member of the e-Business Strategies & Solutions group at Merck & Co. Croom began his pharmaceutical career in 1996 supporting Merck launch brands including Vioxx, Cozaar and Fosamax in the 24 markets of the Caribbean region.

Croom also served as CEO of a San Francisco online financial services startup, eTM, and led product development efforts for a Palo Alto e-Health startup MDVista developing remote monitoring programs for web-enabled medical devices. Croom is also founder and co-chair of the healthcare committee of the Word of Mouth Marketing Association.

Croom holds a MBA from the Am. Graduate School of International Management, and a BA in government and foreign affairs from the University of Virginia. Croom is an avid learner and has attended numerous executive training courses including Value Creation at the Wharton School of Business.
