Bennett Vivona’s Summary

Bennett began his recruiting career in 1989 with a local recruiting company as a direct hire recruiter for an agency in South Florida specializing in many areas of employment on a National basis working a full desk until 1993. Afterwards, Bennett was instrumental in the developement one of South Florida's most successful staffing agencies know as The Bentley Group. During the years and changing times and through many different obstacles The Bentley Group has remained to be one of South Florida's most reliable sources for employment.

Our team is responsible for the full cycled recruitment of candidates and solidification of clients nationwide on a daily basis. Our recruiters recruit candidates and develop and maintain clients on a national basis. By integrating The Bentley Group into your hiring platforms, we feel that any company can be reassured that it will acquire the human leadership necessary to catapult into the 21st century.
