Allen Shisler ’s Summary

The managing Host of Aviation Solutions Experts; Allen is a marketing/business development, entrepreneurial leader with over 15 years of experience in Gov't Industrial Aviation, Logistics and IT contract wins. School trained at Johnson Controls sales school; I am a professional hunter killer at winning opportunities. I have won over $215 millions in opportunities with over 75% win ratio and 45% net margins or better. I can win and program manage the win, and know how to manage the contract to a successful P&L.

Vertical markets include the US Gov't, foreign military sales, DOD, Air Logistics Centers, logistics, Special Operations, Industrial, IT, IETM, and managed services. I have been involved in acquisitions as well. I am an entreprenuer at heart.

I am an expert in management consulting, sales, contracts, pricing, proposals, HR issues, developing new markets, finding / winning opportunities, creating sales marketing plans, and collateral materials that win. I am a skilled negotiator with significant success in IT, Government, DOD, aviation and the industrial market.

My current efforts are in the aviation services company, but help the entire corporation in US Government and FMS efforts. I am currently involved in numerous US Government Omnibus contracts such as F2AST, R2, R23G, DESP II, CFT, DOCATS, and OEM marketing with significant contacts with Boeing, L3 Com, SAIC, SSAI, DRS, General Dynamics, MTC, BAE, and a host of others in both the Army and Air Force.

My service to the community has been varied from President BYU Management Society of Oklahoma which promotes integrity in business, to President AUSA in SE Germany for the Army.

I am a published author of the US Army Non-combatant Evacuation Operations program for Europe, numerous Gov't and commercial proposals, GSA contracts, marketing materials and presentations.

I am married (for 30 years) to the most wonderful woman and have 5 children. One is an eagle scout and we have 3 life scouts.
