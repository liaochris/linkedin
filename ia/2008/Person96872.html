Jon Thomas’s Summary

Voice actor, producer and copy writer. Recently voiced a national radio campaign for Mills Malls, and TV ads for Brake-fast, which ran on Animal Planet.
My voice has also been heard on radio and TV ads for Papa Gino's, Truth.com, Walgreens, Mattress Gallery, Museums of Lower Manhattan, and political campaign for CT State Senator Bob Duff.

Peformed industrial voice-overs for medical narration, sales presentations, web sites, corporate phone systems and podcasts. Clients include: Global Health Care Systems, The Canadian Bar Association, Islamic Bank of Sharjah, ELaw Marketing, Fujisankei International, Solid Profesor, Inc. and A La Carte Media.

Producer and voice talent for Sirius Satellite Radio. Voice and production work has been featured in numerous genres, including: Broadway, Swing, Jazz, Country, 70s, Contemporary Christian, Dance, and Rock.

Jon Thomas’s Specialties:

Commercial voice-overs, singing, copy writing, audio production and directing talent.
Proficient in Pro Tools, Microsoft Office.
