Jenny Vonderwerth, SPHR-CA’s Summary

My experience uniquely combines human resource and insurance expertise. I provide a wide-range of services and support to my clients creating a comprehensive full-service integrated service delivery model (“a one stop shop”).

I successfully negotiate with carriers on behalf of my clients to secure competitive rates for employee benefit plans. I am actively involved in supporting my clients behind the scenes and in front of executives or employees with matters ranging from evaluating creative plan design alternatives, cost sharing strategies, premium auditing, and claim adjudication with carriers if necessary.

Jenny Vonderwerth, SPHR-CA’s Specialties:

Group employee benefits plan design and financial analysis. Employee assimilation and new hire programs, merger and acquisition project management, HRIS/HRMS selection and implementation, HR project management, streamline HR Administration and Operations. Develop employee handbook, communication strategies, HR business planning and metrics.
