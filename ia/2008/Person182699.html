Stuart Gow’s Summary

We help small-medium size tourism businesses during their development and growth, and have a range of services to assist you in times of change. Our approach enables just the right level of support to suit your own particular circumstances, so that you can achieve a higher level of consistency in product, service and profitability!

We provide individually tailored, practical and effective solutions suitable for small-medium size businesses, and have developed a range of packages to suit your specific needs.

Stuart Gow’s Specialties:

Resort Support was established in 1998 to fill a need within the tourism industry for outsourced expertise in management consulting, marketing solutions and business development in the context of small medium tourism properties in the Fiji Islands.

The team at Resort Support have been operating many different tourism products throughout the the Fiji Islands and the South Pacific, from dive shops to cruise ships, from hotels to resorts.
