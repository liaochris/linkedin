Henk Greyvenstein’s Summary

Henk is leading a small section in the Organisation Development component of the South African Air Force, where he has been working since 2001. He is a strategic thinker and loves creating "order from chaos". He is driven yet has an easy way to him. Henk is skilled in a variety of OD interventions in large, bureaucratic organisations. He has consulted in the mining and education industries and is passionate about leadership development in multi-cultural environments.

Henk desires a permanent position and prefers a corporate environment leading or being part of a team. Henk is searching for a company culture that treats its employees with honesty, fairness, loyalty and respect in turn for exactly the same. The position should offer a variety of challenges and include opportunities and management support for continual professional development. Availability is negotiable.
