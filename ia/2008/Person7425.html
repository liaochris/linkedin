Alice Lankester’s Summary

I am a senior level marketing professional with more than 20 years experience working for companies in the heart of Silicon Valley and Europe. Over my career I have successfully launched dozens of software and Web products, as well as (probably) hundreds of product releases. Since 2005, I have been working at the center of the Web 2.0 world.

I thrive in fast-paced, challenging environments and I've been lucky enough to have worked with some of the smartest product visionaries in Silicon Valley.

My goal is to remain in startup (or startup-like!) environments, where I can remain close to a software/Web product's creators, the product itself, and the users.

Alice Lankester’s Specialties:

Product launches, all aspects of PR and communications, software product management and product marketing, program management, web site management, event planning. Steep learning curves and short deadlines are a way of life.
