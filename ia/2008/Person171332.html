Scott Rozic’s Summary

Social entrepreneur with a heavy foot on the metal. I've lived in 7 countries and traveled repeatedly to over 50 countries. I'm a startup person at heart, starting my first company right out of college. I've worked with people all over the world, on internet related products and projects. Social media, developing apps, China, building targeted search engines, context, all things web, and startups get me jumping out of bed everyday. I care deeply about the planet and I am heavily involved in changing the world through expanding the eco sector, greening the planet and performing sustainable business and personal practices.

If you are working on open source projects that involve apps, green products and services, social media, geek marketing, search or globalization then there is probably something we can do together. Let's connect if you are an entrepreneur, ruby on rails developer, SEO-type, programmer, geek marketer, a mind-bending designer or a fantastic client with a big plan. Let's see if there are ways we can help each other!

First connect with me on Linkedin (srozic@gmail.com) and then shoot me an email. I like to connect with people from all over the world, both places I've been and places I'm going. If I haven't been to your country or don't have experience with your language or culture, I'd probably like to. Only if I can find really fast internet. Have fun!
