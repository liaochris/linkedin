Jason Widmann’s Summary

My association with the online medium began in 1994 when I became the founder of the interactive group at a small Santa Clara advertising agency. During this time period I wore many hats--designer, writer, project manager, yada yada yada. Over the years, my interest has been steadily drawn towards user experience design, information architecture and the development of interactive business, brand, marketing and user strategies. In past lives, I have served tours of duty at many of the interactive industry's leading shops, including CKS, Studio Archetype and Sapient. I have a broad range of experiences working with clients like Nike, General Motors, SBC, Bank of America, E*TRADE, ConAgra and Nestle on projects ranging from websites, to Digital Asset Management systems, intranets, web-based applications, hand-held devices and WinX applications. I have been a founder or co-founder of 3 offices, including a stint in Munich, Germany where I was the Creative Practice Lead for Sapient's German operations. I've led many award-winning projects over the years, but the only ones that rate a mention here are a Clio, a One Show and recently a Macromedia Flash Site Of The Day. My work has been featured in the SJ Mercury News, NY Times, Business 2.0 and SHRM Journal.

Jason Widmann’s Specialties:

User experience design and strategy for interactive products/services; information architecture solutions that integrate business objectives with the needs of the target audience; development of interactive business, brand, marketing and user strategies; creative and art direction; touch-screen interface development; user experience design for children, tweens and teens; tons of CPG, automotive and interactive product development experience; copywriting and content strategy development.
