Lori Hepler’s Summary

My expertise lies in Legal Writing and Research, as well as the negotiation, drafting, and finalizing of all types of contracts.. Yes, I'm the nerd that sits in the back office in front of the computer all night to produce winning legal arguments in brief form for all the other members of the firm to argue or bulletproof contracts. I actually enjoy this process, finding it almost like a treasure hunt to uncover the best legal caselaw to support a client's position and win the day in court or protect the rights of my company/client. I've done more appellate briefs, trial briefs, position statements, memorandums of law, summary judgments, points of law, arguments, purchase and sale agreements, and contracts of any and all kinds than I care to remember.

Thus, I've worked on all types of litigation across the country as well as in corporate transactions. I have worked in Commerical Real Estate, negotiating and drafting purchase and sale contracts, amendments, and leases. I've worked with insurance defense (my first law firm job out of law school), and all aspects of a general practice. If it's been filed, I've probably had a hand in it. I have certifications in financial planning as well as in alternative dispute resolution ( a fancy way of saying that I'm a good negotiatior!).

Now, I am in Chicago llooking for new opportunities and challenges. If you have an idea or an open position, please write!
