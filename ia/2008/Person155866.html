Rajesh Jain’s Summary

Rajesh Jain has worked with leading stocks and commodity brokers in India and has over 20 years experience in the areas of investment strategy, technical and fundamental analysis, editor of magazine, managing a Company and Internet site giving consultancy on stocks and commodity markets. Currently he is Vice President & Head of Research in SMC. Rajesh is an expert at predicting the Trends of stocks, commodities, currencies or anything which has a graph.

Rajesh has lectured at institutes like BITS Pilani, ICFAI institute , MDI, IMT,etc. He has postgraduate qualifications in Management(MBA).

Rajesh is a regular speaker on the on various TV Channels like CNBC, NDTV, DD, etc. He has been interviewed live on these TV Channels more than 250 times. He is a sought after speaker on international & national seminars on stocks and commodities in India. Has spoken in seminars organized by Cnbc, Ndtv, Nse, Confedration of Indian Industry, Assocham, Commodity India, Mcxindia, Ncdex, Reuters, Indian Metal Junction, FIIE and others. His favourite subject is trading.

He is also Executive Editor of the Weekly Printed Magazine "Wise Money"

Rajesh Jain’s Specialties:

Investment research, investment management, consulting, hedging, hedge fund strategies, strategy design, development and testing, system development, backtesting.Predicting the markets. Content Management on Internet. Expert in Behaviour & Psychology of Traders.
