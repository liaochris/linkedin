Alan Moore’s Summary

Senior Computer Forensics Analyst for CASDAN Forensics and is the founder of the company. Trained and certified in Computer Forensics (CCE) and Certified as a UNIX systems Professional, Alan holds numerous qualifications and his experience is based on over many years working with a wide range of computer systems in the UK. He also has experience in the operation, architecture and development of computer systems. This includes many different operating platforms and equipment.
