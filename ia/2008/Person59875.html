Eric Holter’s Summary

Eric is the owner of both AgencyCritique, LLC his web strategy consultancy for advertising agencies and Newfangled Web Factory a website development company that helps advertising agencies work web smart. Eric has been writing Newfangled's monthly Web Smart newsletters since 2001 and contributes to the Web Smart blog. Now most of his content can be found at the agencycritique.com blog.

Eric Holter’s Specialties:

Web strategy consulting, content creation, business writing, search engine optimization and marketing, social media, website design, website development process, information architecture, usability.
