Becky Johnson Sabin’s Summary

Becky is an award-winning writer and communications strategist who has been in the corporate communications/public affairs role for over a dozen years in both the public and private sectors.

Her expertise is taking complex, technical, or bureaucratic information and translating it so it makes sense to the general public.

She has also served on numerous boards, including:

- Junior League of Sacramento (JLS). She has served in a leadership position throughout her active membership and is the current president of the JLS.
- Advisory Committee for Leadership Sacramento -- Sacramento Metro Chamber of Commerce
- Sacramento Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals
- Buffalo Chips Running Club (named "Best Running Club in the US" by Runner's World magazine)
- Friends of Light Rail
- Point West Area Transportation Management Association

Her continuing education includes:

- FBI Citizens' Academy, 2008
- Leadership Sacramento, Sacramento Metro Chamber of Commerce, 2006
- City Management Academy, City of Sacramento,1999
- Toastmasters "Competent Toastmaster" (CTM), 2006
- The Practice of Local Government Planning, International City Management Association, 1997
- Metropolitan Transportation Planning, National Transit Institute, Rutgers University, 1997
- Public Involvement in Transportation Planning, National Transit Institute, Rutgers University, 1997
- First Responder CPR/Automated External Defibrillator/Bloodborne Pathogens training, 2003, 2005, 2007
- Crisis communications spokesperson training, 2004-2007

Personally, Becky spends her time running on the trails, having expanded her running hobby to the ultra-marathon distance a few years ago. Since then, she has completed about 15 ultras, ranging from the 50K (31 miles) to 100K (62 miles) distance -- mostly on mountainous terrain.

To learn more about Becky, click here: http://www.jlsac.org/?nd=president
