James Derry’s Summary

Over the last eight years I have been involved with a number of start-up companies. I have joined these companies shortly after they have received their first round of Venture funding and are looking to grow rapidly and expand geographical coverage outside their national boundaries.
As Marketing Director for these organizations I have helped them to establish their essential brand position, built up the marketing infrastructure necessary for growth and assisted in creating a coherent communication strategy for the company and products.
These companies are now established players in their field with respected brands, a continuously growing customer base, and steadily increasing turnovers. They are all now in a position to move for their second round of venture funding or IPO.
I can offer my services to companies who have a complex product for distribution either through a partner channel or direct to consumers and wish to ‘cross the chasm’.

James Derry’s Specialties:

Marketing Infrastructure; Content Management Systems (CMS), Customer Relationship Systems (CRM). Communication, Branding and positioning. Marketing and PR strategy. Website Usability and Accessibility. Information Architecture.
