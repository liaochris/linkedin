Chris Turner’s Summary

At Cambridge Consultants I'm responsible for business development, marketing and sales of the XAP 16- and 32-bit processor cores and related digital and mixed-signal ASIC product design services. We offer a wide range of product development and consulting services to the semiconductor industry. I've worked in electronics, personal computers, communications and semiconductors for 30 years with considerable international experience. Previous roles include Design Engineer, Chief Engineer, Operations Director, Product Manager, Head of Operations, Vice President of Operations, VP of MIS and Business Development Manager.

Chris Turner’s Specialties:

Processor cores, Embedded Systems software Mixed-signal ASIC design, Semiconductor supply chain
