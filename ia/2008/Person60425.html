Erik Stein’s Summary

ERIK STEIN brings his specialized publicity knowledge and extensive experience, covering a wide variety of music genres, to Scoop Marketing. Stein worked for over 10 years for the Universal Music Group, starting in 1995 as Manager of Publicity for MCA Records and rising to Senior Director of Publicity at Geffen Records. At Universal, he represented a wealth of successful acts in the realm of alternative, indie, punk, rock and pop music, including: Aqua, blink-182, The Bloodhound Gang, DJ Shadow, New Found Glory, Papa Roach, Rise Against, Rob Zombie, She Wants Revenge, Sublime, Tommy Lee, as well as Drive-Thru Records, just to name a few.

While working for the large corporate labels, Stein realized that a creative, artist-driven, boutique agency would better serve the needs of many clients, and so he formed Arsenal PR. His company represented such chart-topping bands as Breaking Benjamin and Senses Fail, as well Suretone Records.

In December of 2006, Stein closed Arsenal PR to join Scoop Marketing; a firm he feels reflects the artist-driven ideals he strives to achieve.

Erik Stein’s Specialties:

Scoop Marketing is a full-service firm specializing in all areas of marketing and public relations including special events, album launch campaigns for new and established events and artists, tour press, “grassroots” campaigns, image building, product launches, executive/corporate profiles, and “hands-on” crisis management.
