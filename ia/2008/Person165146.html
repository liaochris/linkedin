Roy Zimmerhansl’s Summary

Over 25 years in the securities services industry with the past 16 years dedicated to the securities lending markets. Broad perspective in securities services gained over a career with investment banks, custodian banks, a central depository and currently an interdealer broker. Experienced in both leading an existing business as well as launching new business lines at banks.

Check out my new securities lending blog: http://securitieslending.typepad.com

Roy Zimmerhansl’s Specialties:

Securities lending - A global focus with a special concentration on ETFs and on emerging markets.
