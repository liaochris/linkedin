Stefan Silke’s Summary

My ethos: Great jobs. Unbeatable service.

Did you know that by changing jobs you have a better chance of getting a promotion or a pay increase? Perhaps you feel like a change of environment or a new challenge?

There are literally hundreds of IT vacancies in 2007 in Amsterdam for English speakers thanks to a strong economy and IT boom in Europe. Many organisations need to hire IT professionals from around the world for their 2007 vacancies, and they’ve asked me to find them talented people for their organisation.

I search for the best IT people from around the world for jobs in Amsterdam and The Netherlands and discuss with them the vacancies, their new employer and life in Amsterdam so that they can make an informed decision. I’m originally from Australia, but I’ve lived in Amsterdam for over 5 years so I understand the needs of internationals here.

I monitor and assist the interview process and keep everyone up-to-date throughout. Once a job offer has been accepted I also assist with relocation and organise work visas for the successful candidate and their family.

For you the candidate my service is 100% free, so there are no costs and no obligations.

My client list is ever expanding and currently includes these sectors and more:
- Web 2.0 agencies
- Enterprise software firms
- Games and entertainment
- Internet, Telecoms and Communications providers
- International banks
- IT consulting

To stimulate the economy the Dutch government have introduced a new visa which makes it fast and easy to start working in The Netherlands. Visas can also be organised for your partner and children – even if you aren’t married.

The strong economy and simplified visa process has created a great opportunity to move to Europe. To take the first step simply visit thewhitedoor.com

Stefan Silke’s Specialties:

I have an existing network of organisations in Amsterdam, The Hague and around The Netherlands who are actively recruiting IT professionals for international environments.
