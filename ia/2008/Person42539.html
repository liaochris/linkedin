Shirley Dalton’s Summary

Currently, as a business systems specialist, I Consult with CEO’s and company executives to identify strategic gains for companies by refining or implementing business systems.

Stage I includes an audit and analysis of the current systems and procedures and recommendations for improvements. Stage II includes engaging staff to develop and implement improvements.

Consultancy services also include:

* Executive coaching services for managers, in particular how to manage staff and develop Key Performance Indicators (KPI’s) and Key Behaviour Indicators (KBI’s) for staff and how to conduct performance reviews.

* Creation of company Procedures Manuals and staff training in the new systems.

My strengths are in identifying opportunities for improvement and communicating these in such a way that the recommended changes can be easily managed.

My achievements include:

* As the Chief Operating Officer for Kip McGrath Education Centres Limited I was responsible for creating and streamlining internal systems and structures which enabled the organisation to list on the Australian Stock Exchange and grow from 250 franchises to over 600 worldwide.

* Purchased carpet cleaning business with husband and sold for 36% profit within 12 months

* Elected Inaugural President for Australian Tutoring Association (ATA) Inc.

* Published Author – What My Favourite Teacher Taught Me – Volume 2

* Deans Award for Outstanding Academic Achievement (B.Sc Applied Psychology)

* Keynote Speaker NSW State & Regional Development Small Business Training Programs on Running Successful Businesses

* Guest Speaker for Davidson Accountants client conference – 2006 Vietnam

Shirley Dalton’s Specialties:

Business Systems specialist, identifying opportunities for improvement, refining and implementing business systems, Executive coaching and training
