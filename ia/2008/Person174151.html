Shann Vander Leek’s Summary

Most of my clients choose to work with me because I am a well trained coach, with a successful background in business, sales, marketing and leadership development. I have a track record of inspiring people to find what they are truly passionate about, and create lives, businesses and careers they love.

Coaching experience, education and training

I have a bachelors degree in communications and marketing. I am an internationally recognized coach, certified with my Coach Training Alliance Certification designation. I work with clients all over the world. I study Hatha yoga and am a certified and registered Yoga Instructor, and have completed training in professional speaking, book platform marketing, article marketing, sales management and leadership development.

Business experience

I have a lifetime of experience as a successful broadcast media consultant, sales coach, leader, entrepreneur, business owner, marketing specialist, volunteer, voice over announcer, life-skills trainer. I am a prolific reader, blogger and a published best selling author.


Creativity and compassion

My clients often describe me as warm, vivacious and insightful. I am kind but focused, with a deep well of practical experience and an ability to call forth your very best. I coach because I love to make a difference in people's lives.


Integrity

I keep my word, tell the truth and treat you as you would like to be treated. I know good coaching sells itself and I stand behind my work.

Coaching is my passion

I love to engage individuals in compelling conversations that inspire them to play much bigger, and live fuller, more meaningful, and happier lives. I know engaged, fully expressed people leading purposeful lives have a big impact on the world. Coaching is one of the most powerful agents of positive human change.

If you are seriously considering working with a coach, please contact me by email at Linkedin or at shann@truebalancelifecoaching.com

    (Management Consulting industry)
