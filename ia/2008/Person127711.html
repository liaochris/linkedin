Meg McAllister’s Summary

With over 50 years of combined public relations experience, the McAllister Rowan Communications Group team has represented some of the biggest names in the corporate, entertainment and publishing world. With offices in New York, Chicago, and Toronto and affiliations in many major cities, McAllister Rowan can satisfy all your global public relations needs, while still offering clients the one-on-one attention of a small boutique firm. With a reputation built on solid performance and strong client relationships, McAllister Rowan Communications Group is the strategic partner you need to achieve your goals in a clear, concise, creative and cost-effective way.
