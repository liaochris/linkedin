Stijn Verstraete’s Summary

After graduating Applied Economics at the University of Ghent, I started my career at Capgemini as a Business Consultant. With a few years of experience, I am now specialized in ERP HR Solutions, mainly Oracle-PeopleSoft. I was involved in a global HR Business case and two major global ERP implementations.

At different clients, I developed many different consulting skills and I was part of the complete life cycle of several projects: proposal, planning, scoping, design, requirement analysis, BPR, testing, training, conversion …

Qualitative and on-time teamwork, in close collaboration with colleagues and clients, is my preferred way of working to come to the best results.
