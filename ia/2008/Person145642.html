Tero Pajunen’s Summary

Less is Truly More
- as long as we know what is relevant, for whom, when and why.

My strongest design driver is that all online and offline tactics need to be based on a clear and realistic business and marketing communication strategy.

I hope to work with international brands, companies and clients who seek for better solutions than conventional mass marketing, i.e. who expects innovative concepts and cost effective methods that supports directly the set measurable KPI business goals.

One of the keys to success is to identify which customers (dynamic segments and single connections) play an essential role to the business, and secondly, to learn how to keep them satisfied and engaged with the brand through a continuous relevant dialogue, thus thirdly, improving the sought after Up and Cross sales.
