  • co founder

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    February 2008 — Present (11 months)

    love to the world is a collective of change makers who want to make a difference in South Africa. Through the arts (visual, photography, writing, drama) we empower individuals in local (underprivileged) communities to take leadership in taking on the issues that their community is dealing with. (HIV/Aids, crime, lack of education/self confidence/health care/..).

    Our main activity is the Leadership Academy that we have started in the Township Khayelitsha. Each week I teach 10th graders (15 years old) the importance of teamwork, of being a leader, how to serve their community.
