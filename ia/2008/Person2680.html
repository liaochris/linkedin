Adam Streets’s Summary

Adam Streets is a Web Project Manager at The Allegis Group, Inc., the world’s largest privately held staffing and recruitment firm. The Allegis Group, Inc. is the parent company of Aerotek, TEKsystems, Stephen James Associates, MarketSource and many others. The Allegis Group generates over $5 billion in revenues via 230 offices globally, which are located throughout the United States, Canada and Europe. Adam began his career at The Allegis Group, Inc. in July of 2007.

As a Web Project Manager, Adam works closely with marketing teams to create user and search friendly content, design upgrades or full website re-designs. He is also responsible for managing website entities such as:
• TEKsystems;
• TEKsystems Canada, Germany, Netherlands, England and Ireland;
• Stephen James Associates;
• Parkway Capital;
• Beltway Capital Management; and,
• Wexford Technologies.

Adam not only works with marketing teams to enhance their corporate website, he also assists the iMedia’s Creative team with collateral, web design and corporate giveaways. With over seven years of experience in web and graphic design, Adam has been given the opportunity to work with small and large regional and international clients.

In addition to working at The Allegis Group, Adam is also a President and Multimedia designer for a partnership that specializes in web/ apparel/ graphic design. His company works specifically with small businesses, international business and higher education institutions, helping them create a professional image through graphics, printed materials and web design.

Adam Streets’s Specialties:

Website design, project management, brochure design, brand identity, large format printing, apparel design, e-commerce websites, flash design and architectural photography
