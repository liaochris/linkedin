Tom Fraley’s Summary

What do you want out of life?

Looking for some help with those tuition bills?
Want to reshape your work week?
Want to build a better future for your family?

I'd bet that you wouldn't even consider your own business as a solution unless you knew it could reward you for your time and effort.

Whether you've never been in business for yourself or you're someone who's been in the businesses for 20 years, support from a corporate partner's infrastructure and resources can greatly reduce risk while helping you meet your goals.

When you join our community of Quixtar business owners, not only do you have access to and benefit from the infrastructure, resources and efforts of your corporate partner, you are also a part of a community of other Independent Business Owners (IBOs) who share your desire to achieve personal goals through a business of your own. This group of IBOs will provide business guidance based on their personal experiences because helping you helps them too.

Quixtar IBOs achieve when they help other IBOs in their community achieve. Your success as an IBO can help others in your community achieve their goals since they may earn bonuses based on your volume, just as you will be rewarded for helping others generate profit through product sales. So, while you might be in business for yourself, you're not in business by yourself because others in your Quixtar business community have an incentive to help you succeed, just as you have an incentive to help others succeed. We all work together to help one another reach our individual goals.

Learn more by clicking on the 'Learn about Quixtar' link above or copy and paste this address into a new browser window: https://www.quixtar.com/about/company.

Then, contact me right away to learn if this could be the right vehicle to help you meet your goals!

I look forward to hearing from you.
