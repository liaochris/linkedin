Andrew Paulsen’s Summary

Technology Licensing Professional with 10+ years of strategic selling expertise in creating and closing new business with enterprise and OEM clients. A proven track record of developing and penetrating new markets with cutting edge software technology. Participated in two IPO's, opened sales offices in Silicon Valley and one in London. International sales success in Asia and Europe. Solid technical knowledge base in implementing multi-lingual text-aware applications in the unstructured data management space. Excellent prospecting and negotiating skills focused on closing large ticket OEM deals (Technology Licenses) with ISV's, SI's and VAR's at the CTO and CEO levels. Ambitious, creative, motivated and revenue driven.

Andrew Paulsen’s Specialties:
