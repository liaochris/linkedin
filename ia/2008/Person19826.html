  • Managing Art Director

    Aviso, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 1994 — August 1996 (2 years 6 months)

    Primary role as change agent – directing dated advertising agencies’ design department to competitiveness and profitablity. 6 month evaluation process followed by, among other things, the development and implementation of a three-step program introducing Macintosh computers for first time in companies' 55+ year history. Staffing recommendations and reassignments. Created new Production Manager position to control cost and quality. Developed standardized procedures and rules of operation for creative staff and senior account management. Year two – further tweaking workflow systems, procedures, profitability and creative product. Completing all goals, discovered Aviso wasn’t able to act on that required for growth. Left on extremely good terms.
