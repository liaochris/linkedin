Bob Kieffer’s Summary

I've had a wealth of experience in the retail supply chain, starting with working in a well-established brick and mortar, as well as catalog company in New York city. Following that, I worked for one of the largest furniture component manufacturers in the United States. I then went on to oversee customer service functions for a $30M/year account for an office furniture manufacturer based in Marlton, NJ, with manufacturing plants located in Canada and a worldwide distribution base.

I now utilize this tremendous experience to help small and upstart retailers market themselves via print and the Web.

Bob Kieffer’s Specialties:

I have tremendous creative, sales, customer service, logistic, inventory, and technical knowledge, backed up with an excellent education and strong desire to keep learning and bettering myself.
