Meredith Barnhill’s Summary

I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Journalism with a concentration in Multimedia from the University of Texas at Austin. I have extensive experience with web editing, meta-tagging and many multimedia programs including Flash and Final Cut Pro. Throughout my coursework at UT, I developed several multimedia projects, including video narratives, soundslides and a class Flash package. I also worked as a web producer for The Daily Texan, held an editorial internship with the Austin Chronicle and most recently helped establish a web presence for a range of magazines as the first digital media intern for Southern Progress Corporation, a division of Time, Inc.

Currently, I'm working as a web producer at NOLA.com pitching package ideas and producing original videos, flash packages and photo galleries. NOLA.com is a regional news website that produces original content and works in conjunction with the Times-Picayune.

Multimedia project files can be found at www.meredithbarnhill.com.
