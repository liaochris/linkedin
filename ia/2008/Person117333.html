Lynn Hunsaker’s Summary

Lynn has directed go-to-market strategies, product management, alliances, market research, marcom, customer satisfaction improvement, predictive dashboards and metrics, quality tools, recognition and executive incentive programs, process improvement, and change management.

She is a Professional Certified Marketer, Certified Quality Manager, Qualified MBTI® Practitioner and past president of SVAMA. She has taught graduate and undergraduate college courses and is a frequent speaker and author on customer satisfaction and balanced scorecard topics.

Lynn has a knack for helping people clearly see what is needed for immediate action. Example resources:
- On-demand trusted advisor training podcasts (www.clearaction.biz/podcast.html)
- E-book "Metrics You Can Manage For Success" (www.clearaction.biz/metrics-incentives.html) -- upcoming ebook title is 'Customer Experience Innovation'
- Blog: Customer Experience Optimization (http://clearaction.biz/blog)
- Open-to-public training course 1/22/09: Customer Experience Optimization (www.clearaction.biz/hunsaker.html)
