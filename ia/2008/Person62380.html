Fabrice Boutain’s Summary

I started in the Internet industry in the 90s after working
in the raw materials trade for 3 years in China and elsewhere in Asia.

My first Internet venture was a French portal called Pagefrance, which reached one million users in just few weeks via viral marketing. The company was successfully sold in January 2000 to the Lycos Europe Group, where I remained one year as the marketing and business development manager.

In June 2001, I founded a new venture with my Pagefrance associates, registering the company as ANXA DIGITAL ENTERTAINMENT in London and Hong Kong.
We launched our services online in 2002 in the USA, France and China. Those services are available at http://www.anxa.com
We signed distribution and co-branding agreements with all major European ISP and portals and we were able to demonstrate a new business model on the market by selling more than 1 million online premium tests & tutorials.

In 2004 / 2005 ANXA successfully entered this convergence business on the WEB + MOBILE.
- partering with leading TV Channel (M6 Group) for two prime-time Knowledge Entertainment shows to manage interactive WEB Platform while audiences are watching the TV show.
- partnering with leading Press Group (EMAP) for several press publications (FHM, Closer, Max, Biba etc..) to manage interactive MOBILE Platforms.

2006 was a strong year with a clear positionning into the woman market with Health and Weight Loss services as well as the launching of the Brain Age game by Nindendo which boosted our Intelligence & Brain premium tests and tutorials.

2007 was our best year ever for the SUBSCRIBTION business model. We reached more than 25000 subscribers for both Brain&Career and Weight Loss catalogs and we are now looking at 50 000 for 2008 as our main income source.
We also successfully relaunched first in France our community portal www.aujourdhui.com which reached more than 500 000 unique visitors per month.

More info can be found on http://www.anxa.com
