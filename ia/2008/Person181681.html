Steven Cox’s Experience

  • CEO

    TakeLessons.com

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    November 2004 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    TakeLessons.com provides a quicker, safer, and more informed way for consumers to locate professional instructors who teach private and group lessons. We have served over 35,000 students in the past two years.

    Our customers are looking for leisure lessons and classes – activities like music lessons, vocal coaching, dance classes, personal training, learning a new language, cooking, and sports lessons. We bring greater efficiency to a large and fragmented market by aggregating thousands of independent instructors and class listings under one roof. This makes it easy for a consumer to find a trusted local instructor that offers the right services at convenient times and places.
