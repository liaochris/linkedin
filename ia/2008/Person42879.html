Dan Bellack’s Summary

• Active in the advertising and public relations field from 1966 to 2001

• 7 years with General Telephone and Raytheon Corporation supervising divisional marketing communications programs

• 20 years with agencies: Executive Vice President, Paul Pease Advertising; VP & Director of Account Services, Regis McKenna, Inc.; and Chairman, TFB/BBDO (formerly Tycer-Fultz-Bellack) and member of the BBDO North America Board of Directors

• Consultant to high technology companies and advertising/PR agencies from 1992 to 2001

My goal is to stay in touch with what's happening in the business world.
