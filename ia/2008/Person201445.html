Michael Healy’s Summary

Thinknicity is a woman-owned company since 1997
~We place Candidates in both fulltime and contract positions throughout the Bay Area in Biotech and across industries within IT
~We are a full disclosure company—we share all of the relevant information we have so that our Candidates can make an educated decision to move forward or not, based on facts
~We interview all Candidates/Contractors face-to-face before deciding to submit a resume to our Clients. That way, we ensure this Candidate/Contractor will meet your expectations.
~We obtain permission from the Candidate/Contractor before submitting their resume to any Client to ensure their interest in the Client’s opening.
~We evaluate and screen each Candidate/Contractor with respect to technical skill level, application experience, and scope of experience or environment, communication, and presentation ability.
~We inquire about previous experience, the success of prior projects and responsibilities, and the corporate cultures where the Candidates/contractor has been most successful.
~We verify the experience and nature of work completed by talking with at least two recent references for every Candidate/Contractor presented.
~We do not recruit from our Clients
