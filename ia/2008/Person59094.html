Enrico Foresti ’s Summary

Visit my website: www.enricoforesti.it

I am a freelance consultant with deep knowledge of the AIRLINE/AVIATION BUSINESS. My specialty is to support airlines and airline suppliers in managing their strategy, organisation, large projects, cost reduction programmes and procurement.

I have a special blend of expertise in information technology, planning, revenue management and distribution to the airlines/aviation sector.

Prior to consulting, I was Chief Information Officer (CIO) with Air One, the second largest Italian airline. Before that, I spent 14 years with Alitalia in Rome, where I held various executive positions. I led business development and sales at Alitalia Airport, Italy’s largest ground handling operator, I was Revenue Manager Europe, responsible for Alitalia’s international network and Yield manager worldwide for the Alitalia/KLM joint organisation. I was also heavily involved in supporting and managing M&A developments. I was part of the Alitalia/KLM project team, tightly involved in drafting the joint venture agreements.

Prior to Alitalia, I was an analyst and programmer with CISET SpA (aviation & space technology) and an Officer with the Department of Defence-Navy in Rome.

I hold an MBA from SDA Bocconi and a degree in Mathematics ‘summa cum laude’ from the University of Rome. I am fluent in Italian and English.

Enrico Foresti ’s Specialties:

Large airline IT projects (such as change of reservation system). Knowledge of the Italian airline sector. Creating financial plans in support to evaluations. NPV and other financial evaluations. Revenue management, business planning, sales of airport handling, code sharing, electronic ticketing, web booking engines, e-commerce. International negotiations.
