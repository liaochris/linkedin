Christopher Comeau’s Summary

Christopher Comeau has 25+ years of solid achievement in business and government. He has a particular flair for transforming complexity into simplicity and for aligning large numbers of people with a common purpose.

Christopher is a catalyst for the effective change that sustains business growth in an ever changing world. He applies high energy to the transformation of challenge into opportunity. He has worked in Senior Government offices, Defense, Health, Finance, Research, Transportation, Education, Aerospace, Economic Development and Tourism.

In 1990, the Governor General of Canada recognized him for outstanding leadership and service to his country. He has extensive academic training and certification in leadership and business. He completed post-graduated level studies in strategic planning and geo-political studies at L’Université de la Défence, Paris, France graduating at the top of his class.

Christopher Comeau’s Specialties:

A pioneer in transformational consulting. He leads both R&D and delivery teams. Clients say "his methods achieve more in days than we could have in months or years using any other approach we know of".

His teams deliver collaborative solutions for Strategic Planning, Stakeholder Engagement, Change Management, Partnerships/Mergers & Acquisitions, and Project Rescues. The greater the urgency, complexity and/or number of stakeholders the greater the need for the unique value his teams deliver.
