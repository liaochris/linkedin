John Keefe’s Summary

InkTree creates proprietary designs for educational/instructional consumer packaged goods, sustainable packaging, automation rate qualified mailers, and instructional inserts. InkTree also provides manufacturing services.

Our consumer packaged goods focus on alternatives to traditional learning and instructional formats, like books and board games. We like to make learning, presenting and receiving information fun and easy. Our formats are great for classrooms and formal education, and they also provide tremendous improvement to instruction for cooking, music, DIY, exercise, corporate/employee training, and on and on.
