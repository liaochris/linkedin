marco milesi’s Summary

Marco Milesi has over 17 years of management and advertising experience on the world biggest brands and over 3 continents.

He is a natural leader with a passion for innovation and a strong focus on business growth and people development.

Marco’s gift as “talent magnet” has proven valuable in identifying and developing local professionals into world class talents, “exporting” some of them to bigger markets.

He started his career in 1990 at Procter & Gamble Italy, where he gained a solid business and marketing ground on Vicks and Crest brand franchises.

In 1993 Marco joined Leo Burnett, where he has grown up to SVP, Business & Operations Development for Latinamerica. In this leadership role he has helped key clients Visa and Sony Ericsson expansion in key markets (Brasil and Mexico) and the agency perform double digit growth in ’04, ’05 and ’06 vs previous years, finally surpassing $50M in total regional revenues.

Prior to that, as CEO of LB Russia over ‘02/’03, Marco steered the merger of Burnett and D’Arcy, where he doubled the agency size (total revenues above $8M including media). Key success drivers were the acquisition of $2.0M net of new business in only 18 months and the launch of a CRM and Promotional business units.

Previously in Latinamerica (’95-’01), Marco has been MD and Founder of Leo Burnett Peru, today a role model within the global Leo Burnett network (it has been recognized as the “Best Leo Burnett Agency in the World” in 2005) and one of the most awarded agencies in Latin America. Marco developed the agency from scratch to become the undisputed market leader in only 3 years, with a total combined revenues of $6M+ and over $20M in billings.

Throughout his career, Marco has led the development of advertising campaigns for Procter & Gamble, Kellogg, McDonalds, Philip Morris, General Motors, Visa, Sony Ericsson, Ferrero, Fiat and a wide number of local brands in virtually any product category.

marco milesi’s Specialties:

Experience includes regional management, founding and merging organizations as well as developing highly successful communication campaigns for new and established brands. Worked over three continents (Europe, North and South America) and fluent in English, Spanish and Italian.
