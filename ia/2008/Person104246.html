Keith Keller - Career Coach’s Summary

Founder and Principal Career Coach at CAREER JOURNEYS AUSTRALIA.

Keith Keller is the Founder and Principal Career Coach of Career Journeys Australia. He is also a presenter of a weekly internet radio show about career related topics on the BLOG TALK RADIO network.

With more than twenty years in the workforce he understands all too well how it feels to be trapped in a stifling and meaningless job. This loss of control was the impetus that prompted him to ‘follow his bliss’ and change the course of his own career journey.

Keith holds a Graduate Certificate in Career Education and Development from RMIT University in Melbourne and is a professional member of the Career Development Association of Australia (CDAA).

Using his own life experience and focusing on Career Transition and Work/Life Balance Keith has developed powerful strategies to encourage his clients to explore their hidden potential and to find more satisfying work.

Email: keith@careerjourneysaustralia.com.au

Join Keith Keller and co-host Annemarie Cross live on Career Communiqué Radio or download the show on demand via I-tunes.
