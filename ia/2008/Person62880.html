Lesley Scott’s Summary

When I initially created my online fashion e-zines almost four years ago based on the notion of customizing style content for someone's fashion personality or "tribe", they were well received critically...but I quickly ran out of money. The Fashiontribes blog was a way to keep publishing my content.

However, I quickly became as enamored with this fascinating new medium where the traditional barriers to entry in publishing - serious investment capital and a byzantine network of gatekeepers - were no longer an obstacel. Instead, anyone with a big idea and something to say was welcome to jump in and compete. And thanks to fortuitous timing and an incredibly persistant (stubborn?) streak that kept me writing and publishing day in & day out, I've been able to grow my blog to the point where it's my full-time job.

    Fashion Expert Lesley Scott is the New York City-based Editor-in-Chief & Publisher of Fashiontribes.com, one of the Top 10 Fashion Blogs. She was one of the first to blog, podcast & vlog as an accredited member of the press from New York Fashion Week.

    She was previously Senior Editor for Coolhunt.net, a global fashion trendspotting firm, where her byline as a Lifestyle Expert appeared in countless international magazines about fashion, beauty, streetfashion, lifestyle, and pop culture - including Marie Claire in Asia, Jessica, 25 Ans, GQ Taiwan, and Elle Girl Korea.
