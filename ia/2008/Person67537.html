Garry Hartley’s Summary

Garry Hartley’s Specialties:

As Development & Commercial Director of one of the largest Outdoor Media Contractors in the UK, I led the Development team to increase the estate from 3,000 panels to 32,500 panels. Controlled the capital expenditure and £25M rent roll. Represented the Outdoor Advertising Association on the Outdoor Advertising Council - for two years as Chairman. Extensive experience in due diligence for corporate acquisition in the UK and Internationally. Developed large concessions with Blue Chip Landlords.
