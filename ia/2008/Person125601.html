Matt Lytle - VP Marketing’s Summary

VP Marketing of Shamir, a publicly traded company (NASDAQ: SHMR) and leading provider of innovative products and technology to the progressive optical lens market. Utilizing its proprietary technology, the company develops, designs, manufactures, and markets progressive lenses to the ophthalmic industry under a variety of trade names, including Shamir Creation®, Shamir Genesis™, Shamir Piccolo®, Shamir Office™, and Shamir Autograph®.

Former CEO & Founder of RaceRoute.com, an Internet-based, narrowcast network of advertising properties which offers advertisers the opportunity to reach a captive audience of 50 million active participants in the sports of marathon running and biking. To date, over 1.5 million minutes of video have been watched across the RaceRoute.com network. Former Sr. VP & COO of Component Concepts, the leading supplier of components to the worldwide portable battery pack industry. Held former Sales & Management positions with Unisys, Stratus Computer, AT&T & Aspen Consulting/IBM. Current resident of Carlsbad, CA.
