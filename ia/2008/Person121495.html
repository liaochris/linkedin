Mark Amtower’s Summary

Mark Amtower (aka, "the Godfather of Government Marketing"), author of Why Epiphanies Never Occur to Couch Potatoes (November, 2007) and Government Marketing Best Practices (fourth printing) is recognized as the leading authority on Federal marketing and consults with senior management in several companies on their government marketing programs. Known as the "godfather of marketing to the government", he has mentored a generation of B2G marketing professionals. He has been quoted in over 200 magazines and newspapers, including Forbes, the New York Times, Washington Post, Entrepreneur, Network World, BtoB and many others. He is a frequent contributor to national business publications, having written more than 150 articles for Catalog Success, DM News, Federal Computer Week, Government Executive, Government Technology Reseller, MC (Marketing Computers), Management Consulting News, Target Marketing and other business publications. His e-newsletter, The Amtower Report, is widely read in the Federal marketing community. He is frequently quoted in the Federal trade press and business marketing publications on matters relating to government marketing. He is a popular speaker at various industry events, including the Annual Catalog Conference, Axciom/Direct Media Business Coop, Business Mailer’s Group, Direct Marketing to Business, Federal Channels, Grant Thornton’s CEO Government Roundtable, HighTech Direct, MacWorld Summit, MeritDirect B-to-B Coop, TechExpo, and his own events. His web sites, www.FederalDirect, www.GovernmentMarketingBestPractices.com and www.GovernmentExpress.com, are frequently used resources in the government market. He is the co-chairperson of the Advisory Board for American Small Business Coalition, and he was on the Baord of Advisors of the anti-spyware software company Pest Patrol before it was sold to CA. His radio show, "Amtower Off Center", airs Monday at noon on Federal News Radio (AM 1500) in Washington, DC.
