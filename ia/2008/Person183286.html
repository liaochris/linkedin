Michael Friedman’s Summary

I am a Publishing Technologist: a business analyst with direct experience in the commercial and educational publishing industry.

I focus on:
PROCESS EFFICIENCY REENGINEERING: analyzing, documenting, and improving existing processes from the content authoring through production and publishing stages.

SINGLE-SOURCE PUBLISHING: designing answers to common publishing issues on how to create content once and deploy it to multiple media markets (print, online, etc).

AUTOMATION: utilizing automation (dynamic content creation, workflow tools, etc) processes and technologies – including trying to define some of those processes and technologies.

STANDARDS: using standards-based technologies which allows users to own their own content yet integrate well with existing applications.

Michael Friedman’s Specialties:

I have a knack for simplifying complex concepts, and for thinking abstractly. I can pick up new systems (conceptual or physical) very quickly.
