John Maihos’s Summary

Engaged in the Human Resources arena since 1985, I thoroughly enjoy the profession. As Director of HR at Essex County Newspapers, the local newspaper group of Ottaway Newspapers, Inc. & Dow Jones & Company, Inc., my education in the field of HR at the newspaper was provided by skilled corporate professionals. Later, as VP of HR at ITV - Direct Marketing Concepts, I learned the direct response field. One thing is constant. The people are great.

I am active in NEHRA and I am currently the co-chair of the North Shore Regional Breakfast Meetings. I am also a member of SHRM, and serve on the Cooperative Education Board at Gordon College.

Speaking in front of groups is a lot of fun, and I have earned the Competent Communicator designation from Toastmasters International.

Community service is important to me; I am active Kiwanis, and serving as Governor for the New England District of Kiwanis International through 9/30/2008. In am a Board Member for the North Shore United Way. I am a member Freemasonry, and serve as Senior Warden at Liberty Lodge A.F. & A.M.

As a Notary Public and Justice of the Peace in Massachusetts, I am able to help people in important life issues. I am also a member of the Soo Bahk Do Moo Duk Kwan.
