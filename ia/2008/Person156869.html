Ran Regev’s Summary

I am a child of the ‘net generation’, a self confessed internet junkie. But I do believe that is what makes me good at what I do. I understand the net, I live and breathe it and I am ALWAYS up to date on the very latest technologies and how they are marketed and sold within the space known as the World Wide Web.

What I do, and what I love to do is help companies get noticed online. increase online brand presence & grow sales with a highly targeted web traffic and a unique online marketing approach. Yes, it’s that simple. Sure there is a whole lot of babble I could write around it, but the simple fact is that there are very precise ways to achieve “being noticed online”.

My areas of expertise are:

• Strategizing & planning
• Setting up online marketing departments
• Increasing and directing online traffic & sales
• Coaching & mentoring staff

I believe that being around and on hand maximizes results and leads to faster success, but truth be told, I enjoy what I do and I enjoy seeing it all come together.
