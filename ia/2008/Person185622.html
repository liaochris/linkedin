Tara Solesbury’s Summary

I provide a range of strategic services and expertise to wide range of clients which include creative businesses, cultural support agencies and global brands, to ensure they achieve their ambitions.

For over ten years; I have been working with the people at the heart of creative and digital innovation in the UK, ensuring that ideas and projects make it successfully from concept to reality.
