Rakesh Bhatia’s Summary

Rakesh carries considerable Offshore Outsourcing, Projects Delivery and Source Management experience. He is intimate with the Global Engagement Model and has been associated with the Off-shoring industry since its pioneering days. He was a part of the initial team of executives that started a well known Tier-1 Offshore Services company from India, where he helped create the vision and implement the critical first steps in the journey towards being an Offshore Outsourcer of repute.

Rakesh has been involved with building a full service IT Outsourcing and Managed IT Services practice. He has also been CEO of a successful Australian IT Professional Services company. He has worked as Client Partner in major Outsourcing deals, looking after the interests of major Enterprise clients, and has run outsourced engagements with end-to-end responsibility, from client interfacing through service delivery operations out of centers in India and the US.

Rakesh himself is Global in thinking and character, equally at home in multiple cultures. He is a citizen of Australia and a resident of New Jersey. He grew up in Northern India, and completed his studies in England.

Rakesh is a hands-on specialist with deep experience in:
• Offshore requirements analysis – what to send offshore, risk considerations, savings potential
• Management of Offshore Programs
• Engagement Models, Contracts and SLA’s, ongoing monitoring, financial structuring
• Planning, implementation and ongoing delivery management of Outsourced Applications Management services.

Rakesh Bhatia’s Specialties:

IT Offshore Delivery Management, Offshorability Assessments & Planning, Engagement Management, Governance, Delivery Center Setup & Operations
