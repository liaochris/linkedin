Ernie Brogdon’s Summary

I worked at Intuit for fourteen years in various positions. I started as one of the first 100 employees at ChipSoft, maker of TurboTax, in 1988 and not only survived the Intuit merger, but thrived with a new, expanded position. After much success and growth at Intuit, I decided that it was in Intuit's best interests to divide my responsibilities among multiple departments within the company. I worked with a Six-Sigma team to plan the process, which we successfully completed nine months later. At that point, I was offered several positions within Intuit, but chose to pursue my entrepreneurial passion.

I love to interact with clients - I enjoy teaching them how to do new things. My PCC clients have given me high marks for my patience and systematic instruction. I do this mostly by phone with small groups of Postal Service and industry people who will be maintaining their sites.

I'm a fast learner and a self-starter. I am naturally curious, and enjoy digging deep into technologies and concepts. For example, I have been so fascinated by Salesforce.com that I joined the User Group in San Diego even though I wasn't a customer myself.

I'm ready for new challenges!

Ernie Brogdon’s Specialties:

Over ten years experience working with a major corporation and overseeing over $12 million/year in postage expenditures.

In project management I am best suited for overseeing web site development and redesign projects. I'm well-versed in the technologies and know from first-hand experience what programmers and developers can do and what it should cost. I'm a strong negotiator in this area particularly.
