Angelo Pettazzi’s Summary

The DTT Content Factory is a few people team in charge of creating and managing MHP interactive applications to be broadcast on the Mediaset digital terrestrial channels (i.e. "Canale 5", "Italia 1", "Rete 4", Mediaset Premium PPV channels "Joi", "Mya", "Steel" and the kids channel "Boing"). Specific competences – Human Computer Interaction Experts, Art Directors and Designers – are gathered to design and realize interactive concepts.
The DTT CF is focussed in developing: (1) TV content related interactive application where viewers are able to enrich their viewing experience with added content available through the remote control “red” button; (2) interactive advertising where ad investors are able to exploit new opportunities through DALs, ad banners and the return channel; (3) interactive services like t-commerce, t-government and t-banking.
Relevant examples of TV content related interactive applications implemented at the DTT CF are “Who Wants To Be a Millionaire”, where viewers are able to play with the TV game show through a synchronized MHP interactive application, and “Big Brother”, where viewers are able to make their own reality show direction through an MHP application.

I am also involved in activity related with Italian analog switch-off, planned for the so-called “All Digital Regions” (Sardegna, Valle d’Aosta, Piemonte and “Provincia autonoma di Trento”).

HDTV is another field where I am involved in; the digital switchover process in Sardegna allowed HD experimentation in the Cagliari area with very interesting results.
