Chip Nickolett’s Summary

I’m a Business and Information Technology (IT) professional with over 22 years experience. I have proven leadership, organizational, and technical skills honed in a variety of complex environments, and have successfully managed several decentralized teams and multi-million dollar projects in many large, mission-critical production environments.

A major strength is my ability to create a strategic vision, develop an actionable plan to support it, "sell" the vision and plan to create a "common view of success," and then successfully implement that plan. Aligning business goals with IT plans is essential, as is team building and communication. This adds incredible value to an organization by delivering tangible results that provide a true business benefit.

I have run consulting organizations and groups, led a R&D group at a software development company, and have experience and expertise in diverse, global groups. Efficiency / elimination of waste through standardization and automation are skills that have allowed me to increase performance and quality at every position I've held during my career.

Chip Nickolett’s Specialties:

Leadership, Ingres Consulting and Support, Project Management, Business Development, and Business Continuity / Disaster Recovery Planning, and Software Development.
