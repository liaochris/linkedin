Jim Lai’s Summary

Corporate attorney with significant in-house experience. Experience counseling senior corporate management and supervising internal and client facing transactions. Substantial experience in copyright and trademark protection and enforcement matters, licensing, and technology transfers. Expert at contract drafting, negotiation, and review. Experience in corporate, bankruptcy, and intellectual property litigation.

Jim Lai’s Specialties:

Contract negotiation, trademark and copyright prosecution, branding and identity, intellectual property enforcement, database protection, corporate asset purchases, fair use analysis
