Casey Williams’s Summary

Trained in neuroscience and molecular biology, Casey is certainly far from where she started. Since graduating from college, she has volunteered as a psychologist in government health clinics in Nicaragua, working as a customer service representative at a start-up before moving in a traditional marketing assistant position, and currently works at a leading Search Engine Marketing firm managing Fortune 1000 clients.

In her spare time, she (and her co-director) run a local non-profit called the On Your Feet Project that works to encourage volunteerism amongst people aged 18 to 34. This particular demographic is the least likely to volunteer, but OYFP is dedicated to starting a volunteer movement. "Local action, global change."
