Steve Phillips’s Summary

I am a Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Consultant and CEO/Owner of Purple Trout, LLC - a search engine optimization company dedicated to help businesses with websites obtain higher rankings in search engines.

Our services include complete website analysis; keyword analysis; extensive research to find better keywords; content analysis and rewrite recommendations, including site, page and description information. We also analysis your competition; submit your site to search engines and directories and track your rankings. Link Building, social media, article writing and submissions are also a part of our service.

Currently, our clients are averaging 4+ keywords in the Top 10 in Google, Yahoo and MSN.

Strong focus on our client's needs and customer service.

My background also includes 12 years of software, computer and Internet business consulting.

In addition, I have a strong writing and journalism background, which includes sports writing, copywriting, advertising and marketing writing for brochures, newsletters, display ads, sales letters, etc.

Steve Phillips’s Specialties:

Search Engine Optimization, SEO, organic SEO with a strong emphasis on keywords, keyword analysis, keyword research, website analysis, link building program, web marketing, social media, article writing and copywriting. Our goal is to obtain - and maintain - Top 10 search engine rankings for your website in Google, MSN, Yahoo! True search engine optimization never ends.
