Piet Noppers’s Summary

I was born in Daarlerveen (Hellendoorn, Overijssel) on 06-10-1962.

From my 18th birthday till today I like working in the ICT. During those years I changed from a technical 'nerd' into a system administrator/engineer who likes to work and communicate with users in a language they understand; I'm still working on this process ;-)
During last years a part of my job was coordination of the internal IT team. Till to today I like duing this, because it's a challenge to work on teambuilding.

I have experience with products like Microsoft Windows 9x/NT/2k/2k3/XP and Office 95/97/2k/2k3/2k7, network infrastructure, datacommunications and tools like pc-Anywhere, some Unix-, Linux- and Cisco knowledge, several Helpdesk tools and software. I'm also familiar with ITIL.
