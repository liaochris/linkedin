Paul Burke’s Summary

Senior Biopharmaceutical Scientist with 14 years experience in development of delivery-enhanced protein and peptide drug products. Strategic thinker balancing long-term vision with efficient execution. Recognized thought leader in application of novel technologies to drug delivery challenges from discovery through commercial product support. Strong track record of peer-reviewed publications and patents.

--Developing technology concepts into clinical candidates with supporting IP

--Communicating effectively and working collaboratively across broad range of functions
--Recruiting and leading high-performance technical organizations
