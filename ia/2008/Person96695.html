Jon Lebkowsky’s Summary

I'm an author, blogger, social media consultant, and web project coordinator. I have over fifteen years of professional Internet and web experience, focused on the strategic web consulting, the development and operation of content and community web sites, project coordination and management, and web presence consulting and management.

As a web strategist, I have experience with all forms of web-based social media (e.g. blogs, social networks, forums, live chats, tagging, participatory media, user generated content, etc.). I have xtensive experience with social media and search optimization practices, conversion support, and user experience/information design.

I'm an acknowledged thought leader on the subjects of technoculture, social technology, technology policy, and digital convergence. I am an experienced professional writer, editor, and public speaker.

Jon Lebkowsky’s Specialties:

Social media, and "architecture of participation," user experience, information and systems architecture, executive management, project management, facilitation, social networks, online community, emerging technologies, forward thinking, strategic thinking.
