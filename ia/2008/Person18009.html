Mark S. Berry’s Summary

We are a Toronto-based privately owned media and entertainment company. Attack Media Group: Attack Records, Kidz Atttack Entertainment, DefATTACK Records, AttackTrax Music Supervision, Attackin' Tunes Music Publishing, Attack Filmworks, Overdrive Xtreme Sports, Totally Useless T-Shirts and Deevel Promotion & Marketing.

Attack Media Group was formed in 1997 by record producer Mark S. Berry (www.markberry.com), and operates under his leadership as President/CEO. Berry has a music production/engineering/mixing career that spans 30 years with over 36 international Gold and Platinum record awards to his credit. Grammy and Juno nominated artists he has worked with include David Bowie, Duran Duran, Billy Idol, Boy George, YES, Joan Jett, Cameo, Kool & The Gang, I Mother Earth, The Headstones, Killjoys, Burton Cummings and many many more.

Originally from Brooklyn, NYC, Berry began his career at AIR Studios in London, under the tutelage of Sir George Martin, the producer of the Beatles. His first major engineering assignment; Carly Simon's No Secrets included, You're So Vain, and would become his very first engineering credit. YES, he does know who its about...

Already the owner of an extensive music publishing catalogue, Attack Media Group's mission is to target potential acquisitions in the music, film and television industries ranging from independent record labels, artists, DVD acquisitions, feature length films, to purchases in the music publishing sector, acquiring children’s CD’s/DVD’s and extreme sports content.

Attack Media Group is tasked with purchasing assets that would gain value from a consolidation of ownership and from being part of an overall global media strategy. Attack Media Group allows its acquisitions to operate individually within a strategic framework set by the parent company under the leadership of Berry. Additionally, Attack Media Group is looking to strengthen its holdings through the acquisition of international media catalogues.
