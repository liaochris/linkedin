    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2003 — January 2006 (2 years 9 months)

    The Intertribal Information Technology Company (IITC) is a consortium of Native American Information Technology firms that specialize in data conversion and data management. Our mission is to create jobs and foster economic development by providing quality products and services that are second to none. Our logo depicts a white buffalo, viewed by many Native Americans as an omen of positive change to come. To us, information technology (IT) has been our white buffalo bringing with it positive economic change to Reservation, Village and Homestead communities. We are joined in partnership with non-Native private sector vendors who have extensive experience in the IT business; they serve as mentors and join us in our mission of being not only economic, but also social entrepreneurs.

The mission of HHT is to create and sustain living wage technology jobs in economically challenged Native Hawaiian communities, and to transfer a plethora of technological and managerial skills to Native Hawaiians and Native Hawaiian Community non-profits. Please visit http://www.hhtech.net for more info.
