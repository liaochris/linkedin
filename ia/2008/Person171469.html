Scott Whitney’s Summary

After over 13 years of sales and marketing experience combined with 25 years of audio production expertise, I find myself the president & CEO of PodWorx, Inc. – a successful podcast production firm in the Las Vegas Valley.

To ensure a repeatable/trackable methodology toward the design, development and deployment of a corporate/business podcast, I developed The Four P's of Podcasting®, which sets out to educate sales, marketing, advertising and branding professionals about how to effectively Plan, Produce, Publish and Promote their business or corporate podcast. PodWorx broadcasts the "Podcasting for Business" podcast as an additional educational tool for these professionals.

The first podcast I produced increased my customer’s website sales by 23%, I've delivered the equivalent of 1,900 days of prime-time exposure for another client, have achieved #1 ranking on Google for each show requiring such a ranking, and have been featured on National Public Radio, The New York Times and the Las Vegas Review-Journal (three times). I've also spoken at the 2006 Podcast and Portable Media Expo and have other speaking engagements upcoming. (If you've read this far, you might enjoy the FourPsOfPodcasting.com website.)

Prior to the launch of PodWorx, I came up with a sales and marketing technique that helped me sell over 40,000 copies of my own music CDs. . .this lead to the creation of TimbreWorx Music, Inc., which sells branded music CDs performed by the best undiscovered artists in the country.

Prior to my entrepreneurial start in 1998, I was director of financial services & electronic commerce marketing for Compaq Computers and director of channel development for NetFRAME Systems.
