  • Sales and Marketing Manager - Business Products

    Epson (UK) Limited

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    May 2000 — December 2004 (4 years 8 months)

    My direct reports at Epson numbered 25 with a turnover responsibility of 40 million pounds per annum. The product areas that I was responsible for were laser printers (colour and mono), video projectors (business and home) and dot matrix printers. I took Epson to number one in the colour laser printer market with a share of 25% and number one in the UK market in terms of volume. Much emphasis was placed on market creation and awareness with the major focus being on SME, Education, MoD and selected Corporate Accounts. In 2004 I was presented with the ”Epson Outstanding Achievement Award”.

    Key responsibilities included:
    • Compilation and agreement of annual sales and marketing budgets
    • Ensuring all S.G & A expenses within budget.
    • Development of annual sales and marketing strategies.
    • Achievement of company sales targets.
    • Achievement of annual company KPI’s (stock, debtors etc.)
    • Input of monthly rolling sales forecasts.
