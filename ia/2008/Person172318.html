Sébastien MARTEAU’s Summary

Sébastien Marteau is Executive Director for France, the Benelux and Nordic regions for Jamba, the mobile industry’s largest vertically integrated mobile entertainment company. His responsibilities in these regions include driving sales and business development activities with mobile operators and media partners.

Prior to joining Jamba, Sébastien served as Sales Director at Hands-On Mobile out of Madrid, Spain. In this role, he managed all operators’ sales and D2C marketing activities in France, Italy and Greece. Sébastien successfully opened 15 new content distribution channels, increased revenues by eight fold in the region over three years and secured many major marketing partnerships with companies such as Marvel, Activision, Fox, Lego and Panini.

Sébastien has held various marketing positions, including working for Netsize on International Business Development activities and new market developments. Before joining Netsize, Sébastien spent five years with Gemalto, a world-wide leader for SIM cards, where he spent four years in both Hong Kong and Tokyo, as well as a year in Marseille. There he executed the first 3G launches of NTT DoCoMom, SoftBank Mobile, SK Telecom and KTF in Japan and Korea.

Sébastien originally started his career in the mobile industry in 1995 as Product Marketing Manager in Alcatel Mobile Phones Business unit.

Sébastien holds a Masters Degree in International Business and Marketing from the European Business School. He currently lives in Berlin with his wife and three children.
