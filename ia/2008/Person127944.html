Mel Bagnato’s Summary

Mel Bagnato is a unique consultant with the right mix of business, technology and finance. He has diverse experience in various industries both locally and overseas. Mel’s broad level of expertise around Enterprise & Solutions Architecture is backed up with a rare combination of Accounting, Marketing and Consulting skills.

Mel is highly qualified, including a Post Graduate in Strategic Management. He is recognised as an expert in solution development, from the interpretation of business requirements through to the deployment of fully re-engineered business processes.

In his past consulting roles, Mel has experienced Management, Team Leadership, People Development and Training (including lecturing at London University).Mel is thorough, conscientious and self-motivated, and an invaluable asset to any project.

Enterprise Architecture, Project Management, Strategy & Consulting, Technology Specialist in Portals and Knowledge Management, Development and Implementation, Change Management.
