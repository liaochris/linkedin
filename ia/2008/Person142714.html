Laurence W. Norjean’s Summary

Norjean works with media, advertising, entertainment, TV, radio, on-line & wireless companies. Developing & producing for the broadcast, online, & wireless industries; live events, sponsorships & integrated media partnerships. Clients include advertisers & agencies; WPP, Saatchi & Saatchi, OMD/Omnicom, Havas, Interep, Winstar, Virgin, Telemundo, Time Warner, etc.

-Prior to Norstar, Norjean was CEO/Co-founder of FMiTV Networks, a broadband, entertainment production & syndication company. technically driving & supplying content to major portals: NBC, Earthlink, MSN, Terra/LYCOS, Bell South, Clear Channel & Comcast, etc.

-Norjean transitioned to Inxsys & FMITV after serving as President/COO of StratiComm America, a creative/marketing group clients included: CBS & ABC Networks, Westwood One, Orion Pictures, Interep, Katz Media, Crain Publications & VNU (now Nielsen) & more..

-Norjean served as EVP (CMO), National Sales & Marketing, of the Radio Advertising Bureau, (Radio industry trade association) as senior marketing/sales strategist, spokesperson & business development head.

-At Metromedia, Norjean was director of national & local market advertising, PR & sales promotion for 60 Radio & TV stations. He previously held TV production positions at Metromedia News - WNYW-TV - NYC.

-Norjean started in theatrical film production, marketing, & promotion at Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, becoming NY TV Syndication Dir. for MPC-Wolper Productions, working on the National Geographic series, Untamed World, Hallmark Hall of Fame, Merv Griffin, etc...

•International Speaker on media - NAB (US & EUROPE), CES, Digital Hollywood, Nikkei Shinbun, Streaming Media, American Banking Association, RAB, + 150 Broadcasting & Entertainment, Media & Marketing Conferences in the US,
S. America, Europe & Asia
