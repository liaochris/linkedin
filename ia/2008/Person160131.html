Richard Rawson’s Summary

Dr. Rawson has been helping individuals and families for over 20 years to make desirable and sustainable changes in their mental and emotional well-being, and improve their effectiveness in their careers, relationships, and personal lives.

Dr. Rawson is passionate about learning and mastering skills that will make a positive difference in his own life and the lives of others. He is committed to sharing his know-how to help others overcome difficulties and live more robust and satisfying lives.

Dr. Rawson graduated with a doctoral degree in clinical psychology from the American Psychological Association (APA) accredited program of professional psychology at Florida Institute of Technology. He completed a one-year APA-approved internship at The Pennsylvania State University Center for Counseling and Psychological Services. He also holds master's degrees in counseling psychology and business administration. He has worked with diverse client populations in a wide range of settings, including college counseling centers, community mental health centers, private and public psychiatric hospitals, and private practice.
