Channing Walton’s Summary

I am a director of Casual Miracles Ltd which offers highly skilled teams and individuals for bespoke software development using agile methods.

I have two decades of software architecture, design and development experience in financial markets, investment banking, internet technologies and academic research. I am a Scrum Master, mentor and advisor in Agile Methods of software development and active in a number of open source projects.

Channing Walton’s Specialties:

Eclipse RCP and plugin development. J2EE architecture, design and development. Object Oriented Analysis and Design. I have worked with agile methods (XP) since the late 1990's and am a Scrum Master.

  • Research Associate

    University College London

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Research industry)

    March 1993 — November 1994 (1 year 9 months)

    Atmospheric Physics Lab: I did some crazy stuff with lasers and optics to measure the wind velocity and temperature of the mesosphere (the region of the Earth's atmosphere between 10km and 100 km). We did this by using a monster laser shooting up through the atmosphere and analysed the back-scattered photons. Temperature was obtained through measurements of linewidth broadening which occurs when photons are back scattered off hydroxyl molecules, and wind velocity was obtained by measuring the doppler shift of photons as they back scattered of said OH molecules.The instruments are running in the Alomar observatory at the Andoya rocket range.
