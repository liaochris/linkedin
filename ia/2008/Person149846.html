Peter Fontana’s Summary

Currently working in Data Strategy for Deutsch Inc, combining my experience in web analytics, quantitative, and qualitative methodologies in support of various clients.

While recently completing a master’s degree in Integrated Marketing Communication at Emerson College in Boston, I freelanced in a research capacity at the advertising agency, Arnold Worldwide. As a part of the Integrated Analytics team, I analyzed integrated campaigns with a concentration in web analytics.

Prior to pursuing a graduate degree, I was a Project Manager at Edison Media Research, a custom research company. For over four years, I managed multiple national and international media-based projects including client catered surveys, focus groups, exit polls, auditorium tests, and web research for various client types including television, radio, movies, advertising, and business.

Also at Edison, I played an integral role in the 2004 and 2006 Edison/Mitofsky election exit poll surveys, conducted for ABC, CBS, CNN, FOX, NBC and the AP.

In addition to my primary career, I maintain a position in broadcasting. Having previously worked in radio news, I currently serve as a DJ for an internet music station. My experience in the broadcast industry has made presentation one of my strongest talents. Staying in touch with the commercial media also strengthens my understanding of market needs as an analyst.

Having completed my master's degree, I continue to focus my skills and energy into a private sector research position, in web analytics and perceptual capacity including both quantitative and qualitative methodologies.
