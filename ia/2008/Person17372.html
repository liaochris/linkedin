Ashu Mathura’s Summary

MADS is a mobile marketing technology company offering integrated multi-channel mobile profiling & ad server solutions.

MADS is dedicated to bring advertisers, agencies, mobile content publishers, mobile operators and consumers together to realize the power of mobile advertising.

MADS offers a full range of products (for Operators, Publishers, Media Agencies and Advertisers) including a robust mobile profiling & ad server solution (MADS Server), an easy-to-use dashboard, inventory management, ad sales & trafficking (MADS Explorer) and reporting (MADS Analytics).

MADS enables advertising in multiple mobile channels:
* Mobile display advertising (WAP/WEB)
* Mobile SMS/MMS advertising
* Mobile TV/ streaming video advertising
* In-game mobile advertising
* Mobile search advertising
* Idle-screen advertising
* Ring-back tone advertising
