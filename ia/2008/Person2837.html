Adeeba Deterville’s Summary

I have been enabling the social sector for almost twenty-years. My primary focus has been with community-based organizations that provide economic resources to low-income communities of color. For the past six-years I have been focused on developing my knowledge and expertise in social entrepreneurship, sustainability and cross-sector partnerships.
