Darren Linck (recruiter@spyral.net)’s Summary

Our Services

Offering more than just outstanding candidate search capabilities, Linck Search Associates works in partnership with our clients so we may work as a team to insure that they adequately prepared for whatever the future may bring. Our services are carried out in accordance with strict guidelines and adhere to the highest standards of quality and ethical behavior to ensure a successful outcome. Our continuous investment in research and development ensures that our methodologies and research remain dynamic and consistently relevant. At Linck Search Associates, we do not simply surf the job boards and push paper. We pride ourselves on being ahead of the curve with our resources for finding the right candidate for our client.

Our Executive Search
services help clients identify top performers in a target market that not only have the required experience and expertise but are the correct cultural fit with the organization. In addition to finding top talent, we are involved in the interview stage, in negotiations and can even help ensure that the new appointment is appropriately communicated to the right parties.

Primary Focus is Senior Level Technical Sales, Sales Engineering and Management -

Within these organizations we build sales and presales teams across the US.

I have a talent when it comes to locating the "right" person. I have competed against MUCH larger firms in my career and made the placement more times than not.
