Maia Berens’s Summary

Maia is the online Relationship Coach, expert and author - successfully phone coaching for over 10 years. She knows what it takes to do relationship coaching on the phone.

Not only does she have her own long-term flourishing relationship of 23 years, but she gained skill by working and collaborating with the world famous "Mars Venus", author John Gray for 18 years. Working with John was a great jumping off point to help her realize how to help create the confidence in yourself and turn it into the thriving bond you want.

Recently she helped a man gain confidence to speak his truth to a woman he was dating. His first appeal was great but things started surfacing that didn't suit him.

In the past he would have gone on with the relationship until it fell apart in a negative, dramatic way. Instead they are taking it slower. He feels empowered and she doesn’t feel hurt and upset by his "going into his cave". Maybe it will work out in the long run but if it doesn't he now knows the value of self-responsible communication and having support while learning these tricky relationship skills.

"The last 6 months working with Maia has been the most effective personal growth work I’ve ever done. And I’ve been doing this kind of work for 10 years! The experience has taught me to gain new perspectives and replaced the critical voice in my head. Maia will go to any lengths with her desire to help. She's running a business but I never feel like she's running a business. Her sincerity and authenticity just shines through." Keith Frank, Las Vegas NV

She helped a woman who's been married four times unsuccessfully to find the relationship of her dreams. She no longer sees herself as having to settle. She is learning how to tell her partner what is truly going on in a loving, powerful way.

Another woman has begun the relationship of her dreams. She finally learned the secrets of attraction and self-confidence and is headed towards a lifetime of happiness with her man.
