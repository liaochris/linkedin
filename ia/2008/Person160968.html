Alex Fielding’s Summary

Alex Fielding, Ripcord (Silicon Valley) CEO of Ripcord. Ripcord's end-to-end security products enable secure-encrypted private conversations across a wide variety of handsets and networks. Prior to Ripcord, Alex co-founded wOz, a GPS products company, with Apple co-founder, Steve Wozniak. Alex has a past in R&D and Executive Management at Apple, Exodus, TVDC, Cisco, Cadence and DEC. Alex is an active contributor to IETF and a prior member of ARIN, SVASE and MENSA. He has contributed to several early patents on satellite routing.

Alex serves on the Board of Directors of Ripcord and CAWEW. He is on the advisory board of the Santa Clara County Sheriff's and TED MED. He is passionate about emerging technology, medical innovation, sailing, security and next generation satellite communications.
