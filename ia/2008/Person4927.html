Akindele T. M. Decker’s Summary

Akindele is currently studying Global Business and Public Policy. He is co-founder and CEO of Mikhell Group LLP. He is also the Editor-in-Chief and Web Developer for African Tourism and Development Organization. He also serves as the Secretary- General for the Sierra Leone Network (SLN) and the Sierra Leone Gullah Heritage Association (SLGHA). Akindele co-coordinated the 2006 Youth-Led National Development Conference in Freetown, Sierra Leone and the 2007 Sierra Leone Youth Empowerment Conference in Maryland, USA. In April 2006, Akindele attended the “Youth and the New Pan-African Renaissance: Re-building Africa for the 21st Century” at Harvard University. He has given presentations on the youth investment at the Freetown Solid Waste Management Company Online Consultation, and the 2007 Sierra Leone Network Voter Education Webinar. In November 2007, Akindele was selected and participated at the inaugural World Bank African Diaspora Open House in Washington D.C. He holds the position as Vice President of Young Leaders – Sierra Leone Global Network and the organization’s USA Branch Chairman.
