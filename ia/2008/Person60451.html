Erik Bondurant’s Summary

I am graduating in May 2008 with a MA in Public Policy. As part of this program I have written numerous research papers and policy analyses. I presented a paper at the 2007 Western Political Science Association conference. I am the co-author of two chapters in an edited volume on primary/secondary education law and policy, including primary authorship of a chapter on student self-determination in light of parental and public influence and school choice. In addition to non-partisan analysis, I have also been involved in local political organization and have a good understanding of state and national political operation and issues.
