Curt Moss’s Summary

Results-driven, communications professional with strengths in data management, businesss intelligence, brand strategy, corporate communications, advertising, electronic marketing and graphic design. Focus on quality communications, troubleshooting problems and business support with a strong creative side. Possess a demonstrated ability to conceive, execute and lead simultaneous projects across multiple organization functions.

I was the director of communications for the Las Vegas Chamber of Commerce from 1998-2006. I was responsible for managing the development, design and distribution of all Chamber publications and collateral materials. In addition, I led the Chamber through the design and implementation of a new logo in 2000 and numerous subsequent branding campaigns.

Many of the Chamber's printed and electronic communications projects received awards from the Western Association of Chamber Executives (WACE) and American Chamber of Commerce Executives (ACCE). In 2002, the Chamber’s website received the ACCE’s Grand Award for electronic communications.

In 2003, I led the Las Vegas Chamber of Commerce implementation of WebLink International’s WebLinkCONNECT membership management software. I was also instrumental in helping to develop several of the database’s features, especially those related to website and communications integration.

In 2006, I joined WebLink to develop regular communications with our clients to help deliver greater value and to solicit feedback for company products and services. I work closely with customer support and product development to improve our products and services. A primary goal for me is to ensure that WebLink clients fully understand and utilize WebLink’s products to their fullest potential.

I frequently present sessions at state and local chamber of commerce conference on the topics of technology and communications. I am a faculty member of the Western Association of Chamber Executive's Academy for 2006 and 2007.
