Bert Latamore’s Summary

I am a professional freelance editorial consultant with a worldwide clientel. I have 25 years of experience in the IT industry plus 10 in daily news journalism including four years with Gartner Group and five with META Group, where I created "Daily Wire", which was the most heavily accessed part of META's Web page. Today I freelance full time from my home for a list of clients, including ComputerWorld Online.

Bert Latamore’s Specialties:

Writer and both copy and style/content editor of articles, white papers, reports, and books on technical IT subjects for audiences ranging from technical IT and business to consumers.
