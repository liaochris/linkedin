Paul Tullis’s Summary

- Experienced media professional with senior editorial and production experience in magazines, books, online and radio.
- Contributor to such marquee publications as LA Times Magazine, New York, Wired, Brill’s Content, The New Yorker, and many others.
- Co-founding editor of Might, one of the most influential magazines of the 1990s.
- Produced highly rated regional, national and international radio current affairs programming; on-air experience with both public and commercial radio outlets.
- Humorist published in several books, all released by major publishers.
- Full spectrum portfolio available at www.paultullis.net.
