Amy Maestri’s Summary

I have broad-based experience in B2B and B2C integrated global marcoms, creative services, interactive, virtual management, cross-functional teams, business development, launch, vendor relationships and lifestyle brand strategy.

My skill set fits best where a fusion of creativity, results orientation and thought leadership is required -- always with originality, a passion for uncovering and consistently communicating unique brand values, a strong service orientation and collaborative team spirit.

After positions in new media, academia, and six years at Nokia, I consulted on creative to emerging markets; the synergetic relationship between brand experience and best practices marcom strategy and execution. This experience enables me to offer a comprehensive skill set: a solid blend of strategy, relationship building and hands-on creative.

Here is a brief profile:

- Unique, versatile cost-saving skill set: blending strategy, business development and creative with success on all sides of the marcom space -- corporate, agency, consultant and freelance.

- Technology-savvy relationship builder: drove 15% increase in online traffic at Nokia, by integrating online with global brand strategy, crafting Web 2.0 strategy, and budgeting, assembling and managing diverse, globally-matrixed content teams and agency relationships.

- Success in start-ups: from the ground up, built blue-chip interactive account base, launched innovative 11-Month MBA program, planned and executed VIP global event and drove Web 2.0 transition -- exceeded targets by 20-40%.

- Persuasive communicator: expressive, articulate and professional in person and in writing. Great presenter; astute wordsmith.

- Advanced content strategy skills: final editor of complex multimedia from multiple origins, transformed into integrated campaigns, targeted to diverse global audiences.
