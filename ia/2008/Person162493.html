Robert Sands’s Summary

Senior Development, Sales and Management Executive for a number of organizations, including:

- Kintera, Inc.
- Hyde Schools
- Sandmark Associates
- NetIQ Corporation
- Sandcastle Technologies, Inc.
- Cisco Systems, Inc.

Robert has more than 20 years sales, sales management, development, philanthropy and entrepreneurship experience working for many well-known and respected companies in the technology and education sectors. Robert holds a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from Villanova University and is planning to pursue his Masters in Industrial & Organizational Psychology.

Robert Sands’s Specialties:

Robert has become an expert in creating and motivating business development teams engaged in acquisition and retention of many of America’s top executives and wealthiest philanthropists.
