Jesse McMahon’s Summary

After college I started at an entry level ad copy position at Sell My Timeshare NOW. Within 4 months I was moved out of the Advertising Department and into the SEM Department as the company's first full-time SEO Copywriter. Today, I manage a four person SEO Copywriting Department. I contribute to the SEM Department as a whole with a well-rounded skill set and optimization and marketing knowledge outside of my immediate expertise. I attended SES San Jose in August 2007 and SMX New York in October 2008.

Outside of my full time position at Sell My Timeshare NOW I have completed large projects as a freelance SEO copywriter for Vermont.com and SR Productions. At SR Productions I was hired as Chief of Operations of Portal Writing. I was in charge of researching and writing articles for the portal which is billed as "The Owner's Manual to the United States". It was my job to research various aspects of life in the United States such as Social Security, Mortgages, Education, and many other topics. I also conducted research on 12 major cities and what was involved in living in them as well as various aspects of everyday life. This project targets not only visitors and intending immigrants, but also many US citizens who seek detailed information on the various subjects.

Editing Peace and Par: Enjoying Golf in the Now is my only professional endeavor in writing for print. I also headed up publishing and marketing initiatives for the book.

Thus far I have accomplished my goals of bringing my writing to a professional level and experiencing success in a new field (SEO). Over the last 20 months I have steadily progressed not only in my position at Sell My Timeshare Now, but also in my personal goals to begin my own production company that provides optimized content, marketing copy and design, as well as SEO consultation. My future goal for Ebsys Productions, LLC is to branch out into video and music production with a focus on brand marketing and exposure.
