Joe Stiles’s Summary

"If you don't have a selling system of your own when you're face-to-face with a prospect, you will unknowingly default to the prospect's system. The prospect's system never says: 'Sold.' It says: 'Salesperson loses.' " David H. Sandler

Is there a solution? Well, yes. But we're not a fit for everyone. In fact, our methods require commitment, hard work and a true paradigm shift in the way salespeople think and behave, and that sounds like (and is) a lot of work. Whether your company sells enterprise-wide solutions to the largest companies on the planet or you're a sole-proprietor who is sitting in a prospect's living room in the evening, and all points in between, you'd agree that sales is mostly about effective communication between people. Learning to communicate professionally is not just an intellectual process but a real life emotional challenge and it's not easy and can be very uncomfortable. But it’s a powerfully effective way to build a business relationship with your clients as a trusted advisor. We are experts in the science of human relations and effective communication, and we believe there is true power in reinforcement. Contact us to see if we're a fit for you.

Joe Stiles’s Specialties:

My specialty is organic growth through optimizing sales teams. I work with people who see value in investing in themselves. I ultimately help them get to the bank more often.
