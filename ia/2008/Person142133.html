Nitin Dhawan’s Summary

Nitin Dhawan is the founder and managing director of Malhaar iMarketing Advantage. iMarketing Advantage is a global organization serving small to medium businesses and professionals in leveraging on the internet to market their businesses effectively.

Trained as a business strategist and technologist, Nitin brings with him a unique blend of business, technology and people management skills. A strong executioner, Nitin has always enjoyed in addressing difficult challenges with ease. With a strong grounding in technology and business, Nitin has created several innovative business technology solutions with significant long term advantage .
In 2001, Nitin founded ITpreneurs, a successful product based company renowned for world class technology based products in IT Management and Governance. At ITpreneurs, Nitin has built strong organizational competencies in new product development, internal quality and knowledge management practices and architecting innovative business oriented IT products.

Prior to ITpreneurs, Nitin has played several business oriented roles including those at American Express as a global business consultant, driving audacious targets for cost saving as well as developing innovative revenue streams through process improvements, outsourcing and consolidations.

Nitin's friends know him for his infectious enthusiasm, sociable nature, spiritual vibrancy and mentoring nature. Nitin is known to view things from different standpoint that brings about new relatinships between events. He continues to build strong bonds and bring a positively influence wherever he goes.
