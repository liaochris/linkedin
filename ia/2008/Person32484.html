Cecilia Hughes’s Summary

Cecilia Hughes, PRSSA 2008-2009 vice president of internships and job services, is presently in her final year at Eastern Connecticut State University. She is pursuing a degree in communications.

Professionally, Hughes has held four internships since joining PRSSA. She is currently an intern with Powell Tate | Weber Shandwick where she is implementing a peer-to-peer money management and credit literacy campaign on college campuses in Connecticut. Previously, she was the copy, advertising and public relations intern with think creative group in New Haven, Connecticut. She also worked as a consumer marketing intern at Porter Novelli in Washington, D.C. For her last semester in school, Hughes will be working as a University Liaison with the Pulitzer Center on Crisis Reporting.

Hughes was recently promoted to Public Relations and Social Media Coordinator for Creative Concepts in Fairfield, Connecticut. On the weekends, she instructs Spinning and CPR classes at the YMCA of Southeastern Connecticut.

Additionally at Eastern Connecticut State University, Hughes serves as a member of the Connecticut State Universities Chancellor’s Committee. She is also involved in the theatre productions at Eastern Connecticut State and is a member of Omicron Delta Kappa National Leadership Honor Society, American Marketing Association and Lambda Pi Eta Communication Honor Society. Hughes was recently selected as her school's National Communication Association (NCA) Scholar.

Upon graduation, she would like to attend graduate school or conduct interpersonal and gender communication research abroad.

In her free time, Hughes enjoys coaching soccer, fencing, playing the piano, reading new books, traveling and spending time with her family and close friends. She loves autumn and collects quirky greeting cards.
