Kim Hagen’s Summary

Owner and founder of Hagen Illustration aps.
A full range creative and artistic source, located in Copenhagen, Denmark.
Hagen Illustration aps provides material for advertising, animation, computergames, the internet ,book and comic-book publishers..

Furthermore Hagen Illustration aps provides consulting, specially designed courses and lectures for a variety of advertising, corporate and institutional clientele.
Kim Hagen, has a background in high level classical animation and several years of experience as an illustrator and comic-book artist working with a lot of different styles.

Hagen Illusration images use a wide network of selected freelance artists with eksperience in animation 2d and 3d, web design, colouring, illustration and scriptwriting. Incorporating experience with creativity, craft and expertise Hagen Illustration aps creates high quality visuals.

Kim Hagen’s Specialties:

Animation: Storyboard, visuel developmant, directing.
Illustration: Imaginative, comicbookstyle, commercial.
Teaching: Visual storytelling, drawing, animation.
