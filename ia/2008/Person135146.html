Monica Gilardelli’s Summary

My professional career brought me to Dada S.pA. in October 2006.
Dada is a leading international provider of web and mobile community and entertainment services; as Events Manager I take care of the events of the company dealing with all the aspects of the organization. I’m also in charge of below the line activities and of the analysing of CSR proposals and sponsorship requests in order to promote brand awareness.

Looking back…
I took a degree in Foreign Languages and soon after the University I attended a Marketing Master course. I have a long and satisfying working experience.

I have worked for Riccardo Gay Models in Milan for many years, a company working in the fashion/communication area. I was responsible for the planning and organization of events and collaborations, supervising a team of 3 people and I took care of the collaborations of the agency with clients for co-marketing and licensing initiatives and below the line activities. Starting 1998 I was also responsible for the content of the company website and for the writing of texts and releases to promote company profile and services.

Looking for new opportunities from September 2005 to February 2006 I worked for a web provider, also located in Milan, and my tasks were to enhance and promote the company profile and services.
From March to the end of June 2006, I worked for an architecture studio, Architettura e Comunicazione, located near Milan. It was a collaboration for both the Communication and the Human Research area.
But Dada was looking for me…

I have very good skills in organizing on different levels, communicating and dealing with a wide range of situations, working alone and in team.

I feel I am naturally disposed to human relations and my ability in organizing is combined to high-level creative skills, which helps me adding new interest to every aspect of my job.

Anything I've organized have always been successful!
