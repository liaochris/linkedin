Phara Mclachlan’s Summary

Animus Solutions, Inc. is a vendor-neutral services organization offering business consulting and technical services for the management of IT. Services include best practices consulting and implementations to accomplish IT asset management, software license compliance management, ITIL-based IT security and IT service management. Animus Solutions offers packaged as well as customized services, including consultant engagements to supplement the client’s own project team.

Animus Solutions consultants have experience with the premier automation products for the management of IT, such as HP/Peregrine AssetCenter®, ServiceCenter®, CA Asset Management®, IBM MRO®, BMC Remedy®, LANDesk Asset Lifecycle Manager®, Marimba® and more.*

Phara Mclachlan’s Specialties:

Area of expertise lies in the ITAM (Asset Management) and ITSM (Service Management) fields focusing on strategic planning, assessment, training and implementation of industry best practice ITIL processes, ITAM processes and COBIT processes. CSAM and CITAM certified, as well as certified in premier automation products for the management of IT.
