Dawn Van Zant’s Summary

I am the President and founder of www.InvestorIdeas.com -a leading global investor and industry research resource portal specialized in sector investing covering over 30 sectors and global markets.

My site was one of the first online investor resources providing in-depth information on renewable energy, greentech and water sectors.

I have over twenty five years experience in the financial markets from trading at Wolverton Securities, Jones Gable, Osler then to IR, PR, for biotech, telecom, environment and water companies to entrepreneur and business owner.

Investorideas.com is a meeting place for investors, public companies, industry leaders and start-ups. Our content attracts everyone from a small retail investor to a more sophisticated audience.

We are currently focusing on the green, cleantech and organics sectors and have built a global marketplace based on inquiries from investors seeking investment opportunities and businesses seeking funding, partners or JVs.

We have a growing global network and would like to expand on it with industry professionals including investors, public companies, and private companies looking for funding or development and funding sources looking for innovative tech in the space. We are becoming a global hub and every day get new contacts to add to our network. We aim to help investing ideas thrive.

I also invested in and founded several online and business ventures from online music (MP3Musicgrams.com ) for my son who is following a path in music, to a toy, publishing and entertainment company that fills a creative void in my work ( Wildheartranch.com )- non -violent products that promote education and awareness of endangered species.
