Geoff Whiteley’s Summary

Geoff has extensive experience in significant generations of IT evolution, including e-Commerce, ERP systems and enterprise information integration and management, working with iconic industry leaders.

He implemented a major airline's first on line order receiving system and EDI system and, since those pioneering achievements, Geoff has continually applied his experience to the successful adoption of significant technology and organisational change initiatives.

Most recently, Geoff has worked with senior government executives to advise on information sharing concepts in support of improved government service delivery. This has enabled Geoff to develop a keen understanding of the core issues in this field and how to address them.

Geoff Whiteley’s Specialties:

Enthusiasm and energy for positive reults, combined with great depth of experience in creating solutions that derive maximum value from Information Technology and align investments with strategic business goals. Getting to the heart of customer needs and providing a mature, dependable approach to addressing them effectively.
