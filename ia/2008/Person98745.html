Jerome Poiraud’s Summary

Broadband has already fully changed the way we listen to music, watch TV, give a phone call, get in touch with our friends, be informed about the world or how to re-think the way elderly people can stay home longer and live better.

What would be tomorrow’s usages and services?

To anticipate these evolutions, FranceTelecom/Orange has put in place the LiveBox Lab. Acting as a truly innovative projects nursery dedicated to our partners, the Livebox Lab aimed to open the Orange world and especially its home network infrastructures to accelerate the creation of new products and services for the livebox ecosystem.

The Livebox Lab welcomes ideas from partners (startups, developers, etc.) and offers them privileged work place, tools and access to Orange experts (technical and marketing).

You are entrepreneurs, you have great ideas, so don’t hesitate, contact me or go directly to www.liveboxlab.com & www.broadbandthursday.com
