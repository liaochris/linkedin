Seth Greenberg’s Summary

Seth is a key driver of Web initiatives and strategy for TurboTax and works closely with all other internal marketing channels including Direct, TV/Radio/Print and Brand Advertising. He manages a team focused on new customer acquisition. His team incorporates traditional online advertising channels – including display advertising, SEO, PPC and Affiliate networks – with new and innovative channels and tactics such as Web 2.0 and guerilla marketing. Seth is also responsible for managing interactive agencies and is a member of Intuit’s Web Leadership and Marketing Leadership teams.

Over ten years experience in Internet Marketing and Sales positions. Seth has worked at startups and Fortune 500 companies and prides himself on his ability to execute strategies, regardless of budget and provide solid ROI and consistent YOY growth in traffic, revenue and profits.
