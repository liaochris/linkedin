Garett Wiley’s Summary

I possess a broad-based skill set in the start up company field with significant experience as both an entrepreneur and as a capital provider. This experience includes operating successfully on an international basis, in North America and Europe as well as in India and China. My areas of interest lie in information technology generally but extend to all types of start up businesses. I have proven skills as a general manager, in early-stage product development, in international line, strategic, and third-party sales, international marketing / PR, and in venture capital financing from seed to later stage. I am an expert at all aspects of the start up process, including China market entry and local, China-based general management.

I split my time between China, where I founded Red 5 Studios' presence in Shanghai, and San Francisco, California. My present focus is on identifying and participating in emerging opportunities in technology and consumer / institutional services businesses at the intersection of China / global development.

I am open to contact from anyone with a serious business objective involving new opportunities in China.
