Mark McPhee, Ph.D.’s Summary

Mark McPhee holds a Ph.D. in Medicinal Chemistry and has extensive experience in the Pharmaceutical Industry including work in the San Francisco Bay Area, Boston, and Research Triangle Park, North Carolina.

Mark places senior level scientists in leading Pharmaceutical and Biotechnology firms. These scientists span the range of those involved in early drug discovery through later stage clinical development. Mark has proven to be a competent partner to growing firms that are developing either small molecule drugs or larger molecule biologics.

Mark recruits for top talent in the following disciplines: analytical and bioanalytical chemistry, drug metabolism, pharmacokinetics (PK) and pharmacodynamics (PD), safety assessment, toxicology, medicinal chemistry, chemical development, process chemistry, formulations and product development, CMC team leaders, discovery biology, pharmacology (in vitro and in vivo), and quality assurance (QA).
