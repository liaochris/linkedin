Robyn Greenspan’s Summary

An earnest writer with a marketer's sensibilities.

Responsible for setting and driving the editorial content strategy across biweekly online and offline publications (Executive Insider and CareerSmart Advisor) which feature original career advancement and job search articles for the senior-level executive audience. Also writes and produces the company's widely cited and highly recognized industry publication, the annual Executive Job Market Intelligence Report.

Originated "Digital Dirt" and generated proprietary research, articles, reports and presentations focusing on online reputation management; how executive recruiters use the Internet to find facts beyond the resume and what executives can do to enhance their digital profiles.

Prior to joining ExecuNet, I was trend-spotting as a Managing Editor of Jupitermedia’s ClickZ network, one of the largest resources of interactive marketing news, information, commentary and research. A constant aggregator of market penetration and technology adoption data, I also managed CyberAtlas (now ClickZ Stats), which PC Magazine named as one of the most "Net-Obsessed Sites."

Worked as associate editor for Jupitermedia's Ecommerce-Guide.com, where I wrote a daily feature called ECTips, and then extended my knowledge beyond front-end e-business issues to ECRM-Guide.com, a site devoted to electronic customer relationship management issues. My articles were featured and syndicated across all of Jupitermedia's properties and internet.com's hundreds of websites e-mail newsletters.

Before becoming an expert in executive career issues, online business and technology market knowledge, I provided account service and creative advertising solutions for the clients of several well-known recruitment and real estate advertising agencies.

Robyn Greenspan’s Specialties:

Helping executives to positively manage their online reputations while suppressing their "digital dirt." The impact of technology on careers; the executive job market; Internet analyst with expertise in usage patterns, emerging technology.

Can provide objective writing on a variety of subjects, as well as commentary, opinion and analysis.
