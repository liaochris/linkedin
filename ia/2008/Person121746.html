Mark Desrosiers’s Summary

Professional Experience:

• While working my way up from the ground level, successfully doing and leading start-up and turn around operations, I helped a small business "reach new heights" and become the largest American owned company in the industry, ultimately as the EVP.

• The business went from being named one of America's 200 Best Small Companies by Forbes to the only company in the industry listed as one of Training Magazine's Top 100 and AARP's Featured Employers.

• More importantly, I helped owners exit to pursue their personal dreams.

• I've now come full circle and gone back to my passion, taking what I've learned over the past 25 years, to once again help small and medium sized businesses.

Differentiators:

• As President of Summit Business Solutions, I provide trusted advisory services, customized support, and vast resources "helping business owners reach new heights".

• Our focus is mostly on owners that would like access to a senior advisor or strong right hand but who are not ready to bring on someone at that level full time.

• Providing them with my services as a trusted advisor or part time COO gives them the best possible value; as well as access to the same level of talent, with network resources, that a CEO of a large company has.

• As an Accredited Associate of the Institute for Independent Business, I have access to over 4,500 experts world-wide in every discipline imaginable; and, in fact, also connect local specialists with owners who benefit from their services.

• As an Accredited Mentor of CMT International, I'm certified in leading edge technologies that drive and accelerate business success.

The Goal is Helping Others:

• Each month, I donate time to small & medium sized business owners in greater Philadelphia just to chat about issues important to them.

• If you, or anyone you know, would like to chat about making connections and "reaching new heights", please contact me, I'm here to help.
