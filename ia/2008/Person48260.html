Danny Boice’s Summary

Danny Boice is a proven thought leader experienced in building and managing global software development and systems integration departments responsible for the conception and implementation of high-visibility, mission-critical systems for varying organizations from Fortune 500 to Small and Medium Sized Businesses. Boice has a rare blend of technology expertise and business acumen.

Most recently Boice founded and headed Jaxara, which owned and operated global application development and systems integration centers. Jaxara specialized in E-Commerce projects which utilized data aggregation and quantitative analysis to help its clients make decisions driven by data. All projects contained both web and non-web components and ran the gamut of systems and 3rd party integration.

Other engagements have included EVP of Operations at Pantheon and Director of Technology at ASCII. Both positions included managing large, mission critical system deployments which relied on custom application development and/or 3rd party solution integration as well as building and managing large technology departments which included global development and implementation teams. Clients/partners during both positions included but were not limited to Knight Ridder Tribune, Microsoft, Ingram Micro, Dell, Comcast, eBay and more.

Early in his career, Boice was employed as a software engineer at Musicmaker.com where his team was the first to utilize SOAP/XML in E-Commerce.

Last, Boice has been a featured speaker and panelist at Comdex as well as other Technology Events and Tradeshows. He has also been featured in Baseline, CRN, Silicon Valley Journal, Business First, Yahoo Finance, Washington Business Journal, News Forge, the Massachusetts High Tech Journal and many more.
