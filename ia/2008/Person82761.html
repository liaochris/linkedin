  • CEO

    Bid Teams, Inc.

    (Government Relations industry)

    January 2005 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    Bid Teams is a subscription website that contains a database of over 695,000 vendors who are actively seeking government contracting at either the local, state or federal levels. The site also contains thousands of opportunities derrived from various databases from which these companies search for contracting. Unlike any other site, we utilize proprietary software polling companies to build a team which contains the best possible capabilities for a given set of opportunity requirements. We then employ experienced consultants who lead that team through the bid and proposal process to win an award. As far as we know we are the only turn-key solution to contracting on the web.
