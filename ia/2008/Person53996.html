Justin Downey’s Summary

Justin Downey is an experienced marketing professional with a talent for seeing to the heart of a situation, identifying what marketing needs to do to achieve business objectives and executing the right mix of marketing activities that will best serve to reach that goal.

Use contact@justindowneymarketing.com to add him as a connection.

Mr. Downey received his Bachelors degree from Southern Methodist University in Advertising where he specialized in Management and Creative Direction.

Entrepreneurial spirit in hand, Mr. Downey started "Big Ideas". Under the Big Ideas name, he was involved in campaigns for B2C companies including: Nokia, HDNet, Young Audiences and Rockfish. He was also producer in charge of marketing for the independent documentary, Listen, which won nation-wide acclaim and a student Academy Award.

Eager for a challenge, Mr. Downey joined Graphics Marketing and Communications as their Assistant Creative Director. He created direct mail campaigns and promotional collateral for some of GM&C's highest profile clients including: Six Flags Worldwide and The Houstonian.

In 2005, Mr. Downey broke into the B2B market when he joined Starr Tincup as their Director of Marketing Activities.

While at Starr Tincup, Mr. Downey worked to revolutionize the marketing and sales activities for a number of software and service companies including: HRmarketer, Fieldglass, QuantumClean, and The Trade Group.

Justin left Starr Tincup in 2007 a little older and a lot wiser to start Justin Downey Marketing. Justin Downey Marketing's business is providing the same high-impact marketing activities large companies enjoy at a low price his small business clients can afford.

Since then, Justin Downey Marketing has grown to service its client roster of over 30 clients in industries ranging from energy and commercial construction, to legal and surplus retail.

Learn more about Justin Downey Marketing at: http://www.justindowneymarketing.com.
