Vice Božić’s Summary

I am a Digital Media and Marketing Professional with 6 years agency experience and have been fortunate enough to work with some of the world's best brands at some great agencies with very talented people on award winning projects.

Currently employed as a Digital Strategist at Omnicom Media Group Croatia, I am responsible for planning and execution of digital and integrated communication strategies for clients like Unilever, Visa, Henkel, Adidas, McDonald’s, among others. I have a real passion for where digital is taking marketing and allowing brands to do. There's so much going on and the market is changing so fast, am keen to help clients navigate through this craziness developing some fabulous, high profile digital initiatives to help grow their businesses.
