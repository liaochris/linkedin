Christian Frisch’s Summary

Christian is available for a broad range of engagements, focussing on Lean Six Sigma (LSS) process improvement projects and Target Operating Model (TOM) developments within Financial Services Industry. Christian is an active member of the TOM Affinity Group that leads and develops the Deloitte & Touche TOM proposition and methodology in the UK, and holds a LSS Green Belt certification.

Christian’s engagements within Deloitte include LSS process improvements for the Credit Risk functions at XXX Investment Bank, LSS process improvements at the Pensions Administrations functions at XXX Bank, Product Rationalisation and Merger Integrations at XXX Building Society, Financial Data Warehouse and Fee Billing System Evaluation at XXX Asset Management and Target Operating Model and Cost Reduction Programme at XXX Insurance.

Prior to joining Deloitte Christian gained broad industry experience from Nordic Financial Systems in Sweden (Treasury Management Consultancy), Texas Instruments in France (World Leader in DSP and Semiconductor Industry), Brightvision (Marketing Consultancy) and Handels Consulting (Business Consultancy) in Sweden. Christian has also conducted a global study on Shared Service Centres and Corporate Treasuries’ Organisational Change, published in Treasury Management International, September 2003.

Christian holds one MSc in Financial Economics and one MSc in Informatics from the University of Gothenburg, Sweden.
