Dave Moya’s Summary

David Moya has worked on multi-million dollar service projects, closed multi-million dollars sales leads, and owned a company with over a million dollars in sales in its first year. Following the Jack Welch style of management and determination for success, David continues to grow and mature as a business professional. David is currently applying for a Masters of Business Administration at Georgetown University for 2008.

David Moya is currently a Lead Requirement Analyst at HAF/IME, a division of the USAF under the DOD. David started on this contract in June 2007, and has led the development of several versions of software development. David is also a major contributor to the Pilot program for Agile software development (known as CAP3) at HAF/IME. The Pilot program for agile development is now the model for team software development at HAF/IME.

David Moya is experienced in IT Business development and Sales at the Executive and corporate levels. His experience is with Product and Service sales and account management. His strength of presentation and articulation of a solution to a customer has grown business in the past. David's technical expertise helps quickly uncover the needs of a customer so we can better serve that need and fill the gap in productivity.

David Moya's goals as consultant is to grow his knowledge base of current and future technology, so he may always serve his customers as a trusted adviser. David's near term goals are to learn project management and business develoment skills on the job and through a part-time MBA program.

David's long term goals are to manage a team of business development and sales consultants that are billable while they uncover growth business potential throughout the Service Consulting communities (Gov't, Commercial, Public, etc.), and to grow business for ATS from within key accounts.
