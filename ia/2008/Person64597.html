Francisco Martin B.’s Summary

Currently I am leading two firms:

Umanify Lab (umanify.com)
----------------
Umanify develops and researchs new technologies geared towards people on a continuous basis in their R&D department. People's needs first, technology second.

Our main interest resides in human-centered technologies: speech technologies (TTS/ASR), artificial intelligence, multimodal interfaces for human-machine communication, artificial vision and 2D/3D humanlike digital entities generation.

Currently we are developing digital interactive assistants capable of being used in a natural and straightforward way, using voice, gestures and images, allowing people to interact with it without touching a single key, mouse button nor screen for it to provide information and process certain tasks on an autonomous way. These assistants have a real physical human look, with facial expressions and gestures that it would be practically impossible to distinguish them from a real person. You can visit our Lab: www.umanify.com

If you are interested in Umanify, don't doubt in contact us . We are looking for investors, partners and talented individuals that share our vision.

Moovil Solutions
----------------
I have been developing mobile games for 5 years with my own company, Kairoos Solutions S.L., an independent mobile game developer studio located in Barcelona, Spain

At present, we have developed more than 35 games for important publishers, Gameloft, Telefonica. MyAlert/Buongiorno, Movilisto, Ebrain, ElPais, etc.

Our porting, debugging and QA experience is valuable, with +100 porting projects from start to finish. (games like PandoraTomorrow, Rainbow 6 III, Asphalt Urban GT, Rayman 3, etc etc).

If you think that we could cooperate don't hesitate to contact me. ( info@umanify.com )
