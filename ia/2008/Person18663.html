Antonis Theofanopoulos’s Summary

My post-graduate degree, which was attained in Giessen, Germany, apart from knowledge on the economic science (Majors: Multinationals, Transportations, Environmental Economics, IT), gave me the chance to live in a multi-cultural environment, something I found interesting, if not enjoyable. Besides, it helped me improve my command of the German and English language, due to my parallel work experience in two American IT Research Companies.

As for my professional experience, I had the chance to work for companies which represent most parts of the IT Market (IT research (INPUT METAGroup), Knowledge and Content Management Systems (EXODUS), Data Warehouse Technologies (EXODUS), Workflow and Document Management Systems (K-NET, Velti)) as well as J2ME Mobile Applications (Velti). All Software Houses (EXODUS K-NET & Velti) are Microsoft Partner and develop their technologies on Microsoft Platforms. Also as project manager at VELTI my tasks included managing complex customer projects and presales activities and technical proposal writing. This has given me a fair experience on IT Systems.

Through the IT project life-circle and my former occupations I learned a lot in enterprise terms about the importance of fast and accurate information dissemination, transaction and process management. Another critical aspect was the way information must flow in large enterprises and organizations through collaboration tools (Workflow & Document, Content Management Systems, Transactional Systems, and Decision Support (Expert) Systems).

As Global Support Manager at Ulysses Systems my main task is the organization, supervision and management of the global support team of the company, delivering high quality after sales services to our customers (Shipping Companies).
