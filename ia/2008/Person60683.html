Erik Wecks’s Summary

My Mission:
I hold a real estate license in order to create opportunities to assist individuals in achieving their goals as buyers and sellers of real estate. I always seek to have my customer’s financial interests at heart. As an agent working to serve the total real estate needs of my clients I expect to earn a reasonable wage for my professional services.

Values:

Enjoyment of people:
No person should ever be seen as merely a business opportunity. I will strive to see each individual as a creation of great worth.

Long Term Relationships:
I will seek to build relationships around the whole person not just their current real estate needs. I will seek to maintain these relationships well beyond any immediate transaction (or lack of transaction).

A Servant’s Heart:
I will strive to follow through on my commitments in a timely fashion, every day.

Information Driven Service:
As a Realtor my most important commodity is information. I will constantly strive to have all the information myclients will need to make informed choices regarding their real estate transactions.
