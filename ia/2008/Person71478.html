Goran Ratkovic’s Summary

Goran have started as a freelander since 1998 and created few websites just for the sake of it and for fame. He connected with people at Chemical Faculty of Belgrade University. In 1999 he has been employed by local company Bozic & Sinovi. Since March 2000 he's been transferred to Gowi Group Ltd.

In 2000 he made many contacts in United Kingdom and United States, working as a web designer in Gowi Group but also working as a freelancer. Goran also visited London in early December 2000.

In 2002 Goran founded RatkovicDesign with his older brother Slobodan to expand their freelancing connections abroad. In 2003 they made several contacts with clients in United States, such as Nayobe, IP Dish, Click4Phone, Keating Hotel and clients in Switzerland.

Meanwhile Goran was still working hard to move up year after year on Faculty of Transport and Traffic on Belgrade University.

In late 2004 RatkovicDesign got connected with 9D Agency (now called MyOffice), a Belgrade software and web development company with which they still cooperate successfully.

In mid 2005 he was once again travelling to United Kingdom, this time visiting both Portsmouth and London to work with clients and finish up some projects for Gowi Group.

Mid 2006 and late 2007 Goran visited both Portsmouth and London again to work on several projects, but also to teach them about .NET design and .NET principles. In 2007 he traveled with his older brother Slobodan. December 2007, Goran graduated at the Faculty and got his master's degree.

In 2008 he will have time and opportunities to advance even more professionally. Hopefully he'll get the chance to meet new clients and professionals like himself and get an opportunity to improve knowledge and skills.
