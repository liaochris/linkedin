A dynamic, hands-on leader in marketing who has established and repositioned brands, developed niche markets and increased revenue streams for her Fortune 500 and high-profile clients.

Clients include Apple Computer, Brio Hyperion, Cisco Systems, Hyperion, IDG Books (e.g. Frommer’s, CliffsNotes, . . .For Dummies How-to Book Series, et al.), Macy’s, Mattel Toys (Barbie, Disney software), Nissan Motors, Sears, University of Maryland and Visa.


STRENGTHS:
1) Design integrated marketing plans to address buying motivations of specific target markets.

2) Demonstrates a keen ability to assess a situation quickly and provide creative solutions, with hands-on direction and teamwork.

3) Directs research to build or reposition brands including positioning, identity, design and voice guidelines for all media.

4) Set trends in online marketing for 13 years, especially in branding, web site & portal development, eDM, SEO, SEM and channel revenue.

5) Creates tracking techniques to ensure that Demand Generation and Online Programs deliver on benchmark metrics and goals.


GOAL:
To use my executive acumen and skills in developing a strong strategic marketing vision including branding and lead generation that will grow an organization to meet and exceed goals as well as become an industry leader.

Michelle Dunn’s Specialties:
