Steve Wilson ’s Summary

Steve (steve@citirecruitment.com) has worked within the Technical Development, Consultancy and Resourcing business for ten years, both in Australia and the UK. After six years as a Technical Consultant with LogicaCMG, Steve moved to Australia and joined a fast growing IT Consultancy (GLiNTECH) in Sydney.
He utilised both his extensive professional contacts and solid resourcing methods to bring on-board some of the best consultants in the industry as well as set up the Graduate and Human Resources Program. Later Steve moved into a Senior Account Management role and was instrumental in managing and growing key accounts with some of the largest organisations in Australia.

Steve founded CITI Recruitment in order to provide superior IT recruiting services that operate more closely to the IT consulting / outsourcing delivery model. Steve says:

"CITI Recruitment is highly effective at providing rapid and effective search & selection services as well as the supply of commoditised technical resources. Our consulting methodology means we are also able to provide strategic advice, service delivery frameworks, project risk mitigation and project governance.

The combination of these specialist in-house skills enables CITI Recruitment to command a unique and highly competitive position against similar suppliers in the market.”
