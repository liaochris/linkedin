sunil paul’s Summary

I'm an investor in early-stage clean energy companies.

I look for technology-enabled ideas that can change the world with teams that are smart, fun to work with and driven. If you have an idea or early-stage company that could transform the market and world, I want to talk to you.

My thesis: macro forces of environmental regulation, climate change, and energy security are creating new pressues on the energy market. New technology advances from other fields such as semiconductors, biotechnology, surface science, nanotechnology, and MEMS will create produce profound breakthroughs.

I have special interest in the following kinds of technologies:

* Solar and photovoltaic - break-throughs in PV, installation, finance, silicon wafer precursor and novel marketing and distribution ideas.

* Biofuels - Cellulosic ethanol, biodiesel, syn-gas, and novel catalyst and enzyme chemistries, and innovation on feedstocks, especially in ways that maintain strong IP positions.

* Fuel cells - The first wave failed. Second wave has promise.

* Transportation is ripe for a revolution. Advances in information technology, fuels, and materials are fodder for great ideas and companies.

* Energy storage. The world of electricity and fuels would be utterly transformed by a breakthrough in energy storage.

* Nuclear - Are there entreprenerial opportunities out there? If you've got a good idea for one, lets talk.

* Power electronics and system integration

* Other stuff - I'll look at almost anything if introduced by someone I trust or if its big enough to transform the existing regime.

* Internet: consumer products and services, messaging, and security.

In addition to my professional "for-profit" activities, I'm engaged in a number of non-profit activities: political integrity and accountability, advances in knowledge and learning, developmental economics, and global climate change.
