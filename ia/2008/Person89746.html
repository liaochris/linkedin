Jerome Sutter’s Summary

Co-founder and CEO of SonidoLocal.com, I wish to surf on the growth of the use of Internet in Colombia with strong e-business solutions focusing on cultural markets.

Now living in Colombia, I was in charge of the European Development of RideSpirit, Mailorder company (website and paper catalogue) specialized in extreme sports hardgoods, wear and shoes (snowboarding, surfing, skateboarding).

After having participated in its creation in 2000 and set-up its Spanish subsidiairy until 2004, I was currently in charge of the international business development on the English, German, Spanish, Swiss and Belgian markets through website and catalogues. RideSpirit is now leader on the French, Belgian, Swiss and Spanish markets and now considered as one of the main European retailers.

After having completed my mission and lived 6 great years of entrepreunarial experience from Scratch to Europe, I left the company to live new business experiences in Colombia.
I just launched SonidoLocal.com, an e-business company focused on cultural markets.
