    Baxterian Consultants were established in 2002 to help clients develop cutting edge bespoke technological solutions.

    Embracing 'Agile' methodologies, we involve the client from the very beginning. The iterative nature of Agile allows us to take very loose requirements from the client, prototype solutions, and continually layer functionality until the client has the system they need. Involving the client from the very beginning allows them to see their system evolve, from a limited functionality prototype to a fully functional system. Constant assessment and input from the client ensures we keep the development on track.

    We embrace change - We acknowledge user requirements are fluid, not set in stone at the beginning of a project. Our charging structure will not penalise you directly for changing your mind during a development iteration.
