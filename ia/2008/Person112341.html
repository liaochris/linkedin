Lee Roberts’s Summary

Currently serves as executive director of the Texas Independent Automotive Association. In this position, he is responsible for publishing the association's magazine and official website. He is also in charge of all internal and external communications, legislative affairs, media relations, and oversees all membership and administrative requirements on behalf of automotive garage and collision shop owners and associate members. Previously served nearly 24 years in the U.S. Air Force, including 16 years in Public Affairs career field, with experience in management, supervisory, leadership, command information, newspaper editing, writing, plans and programs, crisis response, media and community relations, and special events planning. Six years of joint service - posesses intricate understanding of Air Force and joint missions, people, themes, messages, principals, practices; and supervisory administration requirements. Has strong written and oral communication skills. Presents comprehensible information to world-wide audiences, evidenced by stories & photographs published by American Forces Press Service, Sea Power Magazine, Air Force Print News, and numerous military tabloid newspapers. Skillful at reviewing material for clarity and consistency with Air Force policies. Considerable experience with military protocol, customs and courtesies, history, terminology and policies. He is a graduate from the Basic Journalism Course (Defense Information School,1992), Editor's Course (DINFOS, 1993), Department of Defense Short Course in Communication (University of Oklahoma, 1996), and Joint Public Affairs Supervisor's Course (DINFOS, 1996).

Lee Roberts’s Specialties:
