George Bray’s Summary

Worked within the video games industry for over twenty years, and have a wealth of experience and an almost unhealthy knowledge of video games. I have held many senior posts throughout Europe & Asia from Project Manager, Global Licensing to Business Development and Acquistion Manager.

Currently focused on expanding MumboJumbo's business into Europe, working with key publishers and distributors within each region. We have several key launches within the next twelve months covering most major formats including Nintendo DS, Sony PSP, Nintendo Wii, XBOX 360, PSN and PS3.

This is an important area for us, as one of the leaders in casual games within the US, we're spreading our wings to cover Europe and throughout 2008 we intend to push our goals even further, and become the major casual games player within Asia, specifically Japan, as this my specialist area within the field of videogames.

Joined MumboJumbo in 2006, after spending more than a decade with a global developer/publisher, I was also responsible for overseeing the development of, and then bringing the two hit video games Platypus and Platypus II to market, and successfully launching it in over twenty countries, with as many localised versions including Japanese, Chinese and Russian.

Prior roles have included living and working within Hong Kong supplying video games and related accessories/merchandise to western publishers and distributors.

In the early 90's I set up and ran my own import/export company, bringing the latest Japanese video games to the western market where we supplied several European chain stores directly.
