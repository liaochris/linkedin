Nathalie Roman’s Summary

Head of the iAdvise Fusion Middleware Competence Team whose primary goal is to mentor and lead customers and consultants in Oracle Soa Suite Applications. Before the Fusion Middleware Team was founded her primary goal was to organize hands-on sessions and seminars regarding Web Services, EDA, ADF, BPEL, … to give customers and consultants the ability to have an in-depth view of the Oracle Product offering.

Currently Nathalie provides consultancy in Oracle ADF, EJB & Toplink, OWSM, BPEL, ESB and ODI. Using the company’s blog and actively participating on the different communities on OTN, Nathalie shares her experience and expertise with the community.

Nathalie Roman’s Specialties:
