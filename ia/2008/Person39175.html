Colin Campbell’s Summary

25 years experience of Software and Telecoms from a technical, commercial and marketing perspective with an emphasis on pro-active leadership.

Over the past 8 years my working life has been focussed on the emerging technologies to support mobile applications. This has given me the experience of working with many partners and customers in the areas of CRM, Service Management & Mobile Telecos. A dynamic technical environment and commercially challenging.

I am a great believer in competitive markets, free enterprise, change and innovation, thinking round problems, and business partnerships. I come from small town, fishing stock on the North East coast of Scotland, a background which has influenced and directed me in most things I have achieved.

I work hard to establish joint propositions. I think most failures in business come about through arrogance and isolation. What can be achieved in partnership is more likely to be significant change than that achieved alone.

My motivation mainly comes from those famous words of the A-team, 'I love it when a plan comes together'

Education wise I have an honours degree in Physics from Aberdeen University but never quite made it as a Physicist.
