Steve Horton’s Summary

Results driven Senior Executive with proven experience in taking early stage companies from concept to profitability. Strong background in sales, business development, operations management and finance. Unique, multi-faceted experience gained over 25 years, primarily in the Financial Services and Internet sectors. Known as a decision maker with excellent negotiation and client relations skills.
