Prudence Thompson’s Summary

Prudence Thompson, Partner/Director of Distribution


As a founding member of Egret Consulting Group, I have a unique insight into the issues and trends facing today's wholesale distributors. Recruiting exclusively in the electrical and industrial distribution space, I have established myself a trusted partner to both client and candidate. I am an award winning and nationally recognized recruiter throughout the recruiting industry. Clients include national chains, regional, specialty and local distribution with a strong understanding of the special needs facing the Allen Bradley distribution community. I also have a solid reputation within the membership of the industry's top marketing organizations such as Affiliated Distributors, IMARK, Equity, Vanguard and EDN. I act as a true partner to your management team and have established myself as resolutely reputable within my markets.

I am a Recruiter dedicated exclusively to the electrical and industrial wholesale distribution industry. I've been recognized as a leader in the industry being awarded Rookie of the Year in 1999, Rookie Office of the Year 1999, Pacesetter (top 5% of recruiters internationally) 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007 and fingers crossed....2008! My searches are fast and accurate, typical time from contracting to presentation of candidate dossiers falls between 10-15 days, with 90% of hires within 30 days. 92% of my placements are still with their new employer beyond year one against an industry average of 58%. I have filled 100% of my retained searches and 100% of my clients become repeat customers.

Prudence Thompson’s Specialties:

I have quiet relationships with over 90% of the executives in the country's top 200 electrical distributors and top 100 industrial distributors. Special areas of expertise; Allen Bradley, Rockwell, Square D, Cutler Hammer, specialty lighting, gear and wire & cable, automation, utility, industrial MRO, as well as C&I. Succession Planning and Top Grading as well as acting as a veritable talent agent for some of today's most respected leaders of industry.
