Mark Kemp’s Summary

Senior-level writer, editor and music culture expert with extensive experience at major media outlets, and a passion for music and journalism. Always pursuing new challenges at consumer music and entertainment magazines, Web sites, broadcast media, newspapers and music-related entertainment companies. I've led award-winning editorial teams, authored a book on music and culture, and been nominated for a Grammy for writing CD liner notes. I have an encyclopedic knowledge of popular music and its cultural context -- from pop and rock to country, folk, jazz, R&B, hip-hop and some of their more avant-garde hybrids -- as well as traditional and ethnic musical styles from other parts of the world. Adept at writing, editing, consulting on music and/or media projects, coaching younger writers and editors, and inspiring the best from grizzled veterans. I'm an upbeat manager, excellent at rallying the troops.

Mark Kemp’s Specialties:

Freelance: Reporting and writing news, features and profiles; trend stories, music and cultural criticism, album liner notes, TV documentary scripts.

Management: Leading editorial teams (line editing, editorial coaching, directing brainstorming sessions), negotiating with entertainment industry executives, working with publicists and marketing executives, conferring with online heads to maintain editorial symbiosis, brand and identity.
