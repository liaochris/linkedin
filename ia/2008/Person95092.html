John Roberts’s Summary

25 years of experience developing and launching optoelectronic, solid state lighting, machine vision and software products within the automotive, semiconductor, flat panel display and computer industries.

Adept at engineering in many disciplines and seasoned at building and leading teams that transform ideas into robust, profitable products and processes.

Knowledgeable in color and illumination sciences, photometry/colorimetry, optics, thin-film interference coatings, thermal engineering, optoelectronics, LED
technology, electronic display technology, glass processing, machine vision, image processing, sensors and controls, automated measurement, process control, data collection/archival/visualization systems and
applied software development.

Extensive history organizing new businesses, negotiating licenses and
supply agreements and developing unique specifications, methods and processes critical to new products.

Now working in applied massively parallel computing on GPU's at NVIDIA. Primary areas of focus are highly accelerated imaging, image processing, machine vision, computer vision and advanced user interface applications.

For recent sample work, see CUDA VisionWorkbench at http://openvidia.sourceforge.net/
