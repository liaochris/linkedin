Flora Wynn (formerly Nguyen)’s Summary

Flora Wynn (formerly Nguyen) is currently President of ClearWorth Financial, a leading provider of business valuation services for both public and private issuers. In her current capacity and as a former Wall Street equity research analyst and investment banker, Ms. Wynn has performed numerous business valuations for buy/sell agreements, M&A, estate/gift tax, and meeting the complex financial reporting requirements of public issuers. She specializes in SFAS 123R share-based compensation valuations, valuing and accounting for derivatives requiring bifurcation under SFAS 133, and determining the correct classification and fair value measurement for financial instruments requiring marking to market each reporting period under new FASB guidance. Ms. Wynn is a CFA charterholder and a member of NACVA.

Prior to ClearWorth, Ms. Wynn was Managing Partner of Xnergy LLC, a provider of business, tax, and financial advisory services. At Xnergy, Ms. Wynn was responsible for overseeing the firm’s entire financial advisory services unit. She also established and ran the equity research division within Xnergy, where she chose to focus her research coverage on the tech and life sciences sectors.

Ms. Wynn was also a principal with EquityNet Research, an independent market research and business valuation firm specializing in providing equity research coverage to orphan publicly traded companies. Ms. Wynn maintained a broad list of companies under her coverage at EquityNet.

Prior to EquityNet, Ms. Wynn was an equity research analyst at Wedbush Morgan Securities, where she focused on the specialty retail sector. At Wedbush Morgan, Ms. Wynn’s coverage universe, at 26 companies, was among the broadest on Wall Street. She also spent time in the investment banking division of Wedbush Morgan in the firm’s retail group. Additionally, Ms. Wynn has provided financial and business advisory services for a number of Wall Street leaders.
