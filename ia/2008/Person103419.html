Katie Juhl’s Summary

Katie Juhl boasts wide-ranging experience in video production, editing and packaging for broadcast and online. She broke into the business with ABC and PBS affiliate stations in her native Nebraska and moved on to Jim Lehrer’s NewsHour. She later worked as a coordinating producer for ABC News and as producer and talent for Reuters Television in Washington, DC. Katie is currently business development manager for Agence France-Presse’s video products in North America. Juhl has taught graduate courses at Syracuse University and is active in several professional groups, including the Radio Television News Directors Association.

Katie Juhl’s Specialties:

Television production, multimedia strategy, television consulting, instructor and coaching, piano teacher, digital media sales
