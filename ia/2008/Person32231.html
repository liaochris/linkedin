Cindy Clark’s Summary

As a Virtual Assistant, Cindy provides authors, professional speakers, life coaches, consultants, non-profits organizations and other small business owners with administrative and marketing solutions. When hiring an in-house assistant is not a feasible option for you, you may be surprised at how a Virtual Assistant can help you achieve the desired results you are looking for. When you are at the point of not having enough time in your day for everything you need to be done and you know you are in need of help, look to a VA for that assistance.

Cindy partners with her clients by asking the right questions and listening to them to learn what their goals are and what they are looking to achieve to provide the desired results. Cindy takes great pride in assisting her clients achieve their success. Their success is her success. She is their biggest cheerleader and greatest ally in everything they do. She is always willing to listen and provide direction when they feel they need something more or are looking to change what they are doing.

Cindy has received certification from AuthorSmart.com as a Professional Author's Assistant, helping authors from manuscript creation to book marketing. One of Cindy's greatest loves is reading and this seemed like a great path for Cindy to take.

Cindy has also received certification from Practice Pay Solutions as an eCommerce Strategist, which provides the tools to assist clients with email marketing, shopping carts, and autoresponders. Having a strong background in traditional off-line marketing, eMarketing is a natural for Cindy. Learning all of the tools involved provides Cindy with cutting edge technology and ability to provide the services that her clients need.

Cindy Clark’s Specialties:

Specialties include creating and implementing eMarketing action plans including email marketing, website development, sales pages, teleseminars, article marketing and distribution. Learn how to get your message in front of your target audience to gain the most exposure and bring in the greatest results.
