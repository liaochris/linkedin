Jeff Laurie, Copywriter’s Summary

Award-winning freelance copywriter and creative director with over 25 years experience. In-depth expertise in all creative elements of circulation marketing and, increasingly PPC marketing and SEO. My work has been featured in several industry publications, including a two-page spread on a 5+ year control, in Inside Direct Mail.

I am the author of "Writing for Online Media," a chapter in "Write On Target," by Donna Baier Stein. (It was written in 1997, so it's somewhat outdated now.)

I've worked in a wide range of subject areas and, in addition to my continuing assignments from publishers, I'm developing expertise in SEO and online lead generation for small to mid-sized businesses.
