Charisma A.’s Summary

Wesley International Consultants are authorised representatives of International Universities in over 27 countries across the globe including USA, UK, Canada, Ireland, France, Germany, Australia, New Zealand, to name a few. We recruit quality international students from the Indian subcontinent for the past decade.
Our main emphasis remains on recruiting deserving candidates with qualifying educational and 'financial' background for the respective educational institution and the consulate. In order to ensure that our company and our partners are working on genuine cases, we have indigenously devised 'The Wesley's Back Tracking System (WBTS) which filters deserving from the other ones.
The WBTS is a watch dog which identifies whether a student whose application we are about to work on and send to our partner institution is likely to get a visa in future or not as per the guidelines in the new PBS (POINT BASED SYSTEM) of the respective High Commissions. The entire idea is to ensure that only the deserving students application should be allowed to permeate thus we or our partners are not burdened with applications where is student is not likely to make it. We are also very concerned ethically that we are not wasting students precious career time.

I am currently looking for partner institutions who are willing to collaborate their English Language Proficiency (IELTS / TOEFL training) programs with our latest partner venture The Queens Intl in India. We have already got registration rights as an authorised centre from IDP Australia and the British Council. This will ensure that the candidates are well qualified in English before they take further education overseas. For institutions, they will have their target audience assembled at all our centres cutting down the massive marketing costs for them. It will be a also give institutions an opportunity to tap students at a rather early stage, before they appear for the English tests.
