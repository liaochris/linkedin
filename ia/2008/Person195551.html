Venu Gopala Reddy’s Summary

Venu has over 13 years of experience in Entrepreneurship, IT and General Management at Telecom, Banking, and Shipping sectors. His experience covers Organization change and Development, Performance Management and Employee Relations, IT portfolio Management, IT Cost, Vendor, Contract Management and IT strategy.

Venu has an MBA from XLRI School of Management, Jamshedpur.

He has been working for UASC a US$ 2 Billion company serves as a National shipping line for 6 Arabian Gulf countries. A brief stint at NBAD, a national bank for Abudhabi (capital of UAE) state. He has started his career with a small startup in telecom sector and the pursed his career at corporates both in India and internationally.
