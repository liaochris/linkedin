Jason Gaylor’s Summary

Prior to Departika, Jason did contract work as a web interface designer for both hotels.com and travelnow.com, and before that spent several years writing songs and performing as the front man for rock band Fern. He designed all the marketing and promotional pieces for the band, and as a result of his marketing on the web, landed the band a short tour with the Goo Goo Dolls in 1999. In his free time, he's currently a partner in songpull.com—a web community project for songwriters, encouraging artists to finish their songs. Jason draws from over 10 years experience in design, project management, and new business development in music and design projects.

Jason's original illustrations have been on the cover of Digital Scrapbooking Magazine, and featured in multiple National and International magazines, including Computer Arts, Digital Scrapbooking, and Simple Scrapbooks. They have appeared on many different mediums including, Sunkist and 7up cans, Chevy ads, PBS ads, Hewlett Packard design templates, FLOR tile samples, a network show package for the Versus Channel, and the Eastern Promises movie trailer.

Other web interface work includes Delta and Northwest Airlines.
