Roger Peden’s Summary

Strategy....Focused...Results...Solutions

A results oriented Human Resources and Social Services professional with over 15 years of experience in government, health care, corporate and nonprofit. I specialize in developing win/win situations for a variety of different populations in challenging situations. I've been particularly effective at being a bridge between two different populations.

My passion is to help low income, young adults take control of their lives. I have the huge benefit of seeing societal challenges from two distinct lenses. First, from the affected parties. I understand peoples' mindsets, individual passions as well as their challenges. I also understand the business-driven needs of corporations in a variety of different industries. My goal is to make great matches for both parties.
