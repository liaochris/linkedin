Peter S. Cook’s Summary

HELP FOR CORPORATIONS:
- Planning and development for new lines of business
- Business development and marketing advisory services
- Sales and marketing coaching for technical executives and teams

I work with successful companies who want to launch new lines of business, extend their capabilities, or move into new markets. My specialty is helping technical professionals develop and market their offerings with absolute power and authority. I lead and coach clients through designing and executing marketing and business development plans. I am passionate about value; during my career I have led many of my employees and clients to drop their "lowest price" approach and taught them how to sell value.

HELP FOR INDEPENDENT PROFESSIONALS:
- Marketing coaching
- Communications strategy & planning
- Coaching for solo professionals in transition

I serve as a marketing and business coach to independent professionals. I bring encouragement, insight, humor, and structure to both the day-to-day practicalities and the inner work of professional transition.

MY COACH TRAINING

I was trained by Julio Olalla, founder of Newfield Network and one of the pioneers of the field of coaching, and by the senior staff of Newfield. Newfield's model is a powerful, cutting-edge approach that enhances clients' self-understanding, generates more resourceful responses to business challenges, improves the quality of thinking and decision-making, and accelerates clients' ability to coordinate effective action.

My 27-year career has included senior management, marketing, and technical responsibilities across a wide range of businesses. I am an expert in the art of integrating sales, marketing, alliance management, business development, and service delivery to develop a coordinated, 360-degree approach to business.
