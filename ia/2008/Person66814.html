Gabe Mounce’s Summary

I'm wacky cool! I have a lot of experience in the Defense Acquisition Establishment, but I'm not a traditional beuracrat!

I have solid technical experience in the fields of Antennas, Electromagnetics, Lasers, Photonics, and Electro-Optics. Underpinning this experience, though, is my enthusiasm for neat, cool, innovative and creative "stuff".

I love the wacky and extreme and relish work that is on the cutting edge of insanity...stuff that people really consider "Way out there" or "Impossible". I also LOVE pushing the limits of what the establishment deems appropriate because I believe this is the only way to push into the unknown and spawn innovation. Innovation and creativity is where I feel most comfortable.
