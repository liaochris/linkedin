Joanne Guadalupe’s Summary

Aspire to look to the future with purpose.

A global specialty pharmaceutical and medication delivery company, Hospira is Advancing WellnessTM by developing, manufacturing, and marketing industry-leading hospital products and technology solutions. As important as what we do is how we do it. Hospira takes great care to hire, develop, and keep the right people – people of integrity who are motivated to take ownership of their careers, people who embody our values and entrepreneurial spirit.

We are excited to announce the Hospira Talent Network and invite you to the Talent Network.

At Hospira, we're always looking for the very best people to join our team. When you join our network, you can choose to be notified whenever new opportunities at Hospira become available that match your career interests. And if you see an opening that interests you, you'll have a direct connection to our hiring team. Even if you're not currently looking for a career change, we also would like to invite you to join our Talent Network, so you can keep track of new career opportunities that may appeal to you or someone you know.

Join our talent network & be the first to know about new opportunities by clicking the link or below, or copy it to your web browser:

We offer an agile, yet balanced work culture and the types of compensation and benefits you’d expect from an industry leader. We’ll provide the tools and resources you need to reach and exceed your career goals through professional development, education reimbursement, and much more.

As an equal opportunity/affirmative action employer, Hospira embraces diversity of thought and cultural perspective and fosters an environment of empowerment, fairness and respect.
