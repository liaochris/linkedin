James Shannon’s Summary

Most recently, my professional experience has focused on project management. I most enjoy planning for -- and solving -- the problems that inevitably comprise 20% of a project. My personal motivation lies in continual learning and professional development.

Ultimately, my goal is to move into progressively more high-level roles where I am able to plan the long-term strategy of projects -- or firms. I am an analytical person and very good at problem-solving by thinking outside of the box.

My background is in computer programming – something I’ve been doing for over 10 years. Programming is no longer my career but I enjoy leveraging the experience I have gained; I find the analytical and problem-solving mindset to be exciting and it provides a unique perspective in planning and management of all projects.
