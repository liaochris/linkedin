John Snyder’s Summary

John's career has been focused in Employee Benefits, HR outsourcing and Talent Acquisition working for industry leaders UNUM, Sun Life and Fidelity. With this experience as a foundation, he formed HCR Group to help small to mid size companies manage their Employee Benefits, Human Resources and Recruiting process through a business process outsourcing (BPO) model. HCR Group clients are based throughout the US (primarily in New England) and have employees across the globe. From traditional health and welfare benefits brokerage to recruiting, training, policy and leadership development, workplace safety and general HR advisory services...HCR Group is the trusted advisor to 125 clients and over 7,500 employees.

John Snyder’s Specialties:

Health and welfare benefits strategy, planning and administration, outsourced human resources, talent acquisition for small to mid sized companies
