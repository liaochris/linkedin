Byron Smith’s Summary

29 years in the Automotive Industry, 20 years as a Ford Dealer.

At the age of 21 Byron set a goal to own a Dealership before the age of 30. At 29 that goal was achieved. He is a Journeyman Partsman, who has been a Parts Manager, Service Manager and a General Sales Manager prior to becoming a Ford Dealer.

    The Sir Edmund Hillary Foundation

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    November 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    The Objectives of the Foundation are:

    To receive and maintain funds for the purpose of helping to preserve the heritage and to improve the quality of life of the Sherpa People of the Solu Khumbu region of Nepal by contributing to each of the following:

    1. the development and maintenance of health services and facilities and the implementation of community health education programs,

    2. the availability and quality of education by assisting in the development and enhancement of literacy and education programs and the construction of new schools,

    3. the development and implementation of programs to address the environmental concerns of the region such as reforestation, water purification and pollution control,

    4. the maintenance and restoration of historical structures and monuments; scholarships and bursaries for persons of the Solu Khumbu region of Nepal who engage in studies or training programs directly connected with and/or to further the objects of the Foundation
