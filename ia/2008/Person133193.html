Mindy Goldstein’s Summary

Mindy joined Ogilvy in 2004 working as an Account Executive on the DHL North America team. In February of 2005 she expanded her role to take on the DHL global business. Working in a 360 capacity, Mindy helped to manage advertising projects and sponsorships with the Deutsch Post World Net group, DHL’s parent company. In December of 2005, Mindy was promoted to Account Supervisor and spent the next 6 months working to launch the first ever DHL global advertising campaign.

In May of 2006 Mindy moved onto the BlackRock investment management business. Here she participated in the creation and launch of BlackRock’s first global advertising campaign.

Mindy began her career at Grey Worldwide working on Proctor & Gamble’s Clairol hair color business. Here she focused her energies on the domestic and global campaigns for product lines Nice ‘N Easy, Natural Instincts, Hydrience as well as the overall Clairol brand campaign.

Mindy received her B.A. in Economics with an additional degree in Media Communications from Tufts University.
