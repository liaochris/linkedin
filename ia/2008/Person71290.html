Randy Friedman [randy@golfmindpower.com]’s Summary

Speaker, author and sports hypnotist Randy Friedman, is an expert on the power of the mind. Competing in different competitive sports, first with racquetball and later on with golf lead to Randy’s interest in visualization and mind power for herself. The moment she realized she could help others learn these skills and improve their lives, she left the country club scene and was on the road doing corporate keynotes, workshops and seminars.

From small and large keynotes to one-on-one amateurs, and professional golfers on tour, I help people stretch their minds and release their inner limitations to become their very best. Understanding the Law of Attraction will help every aspect of golf and life.
