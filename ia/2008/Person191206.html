Tom Ng’s Summary

Over nine years biotech experience, including R&D, Program Management, Alliance/Channel Management and Business Development.

I am interested in making contacts for ExonHit's SpliceArray™ services. We offer a complete service for the analysis of your RNA samples on microarrays designed to detect and differentiate splice variants. SpliceArray™ can utilize the Agilent Technologies and Affymetrix GeneChip® microarray platforms. We can also sell worldwide directly to end-users SpliceArray™ on the Agilent Technologies' microarray slide platform. We offer custom and over 70 catalog SpliceArray™products, including a Human Genome-Wide microarray and microarrays for many Human gene families. We plan to offer SpliceArray™for Rat and Mouse soon.
