Boaz Weiss’s Summary

Boaz Weiss, is a seasoned international sales, marketing & business development executive in the high technology field with extensive experience in Europe, Asia, USA and South America.

Mr. Weiss serves today as VP Sales & Marketing at Telkoor Power Supplies, a global leader in the field of military and telecom power supplies.

Prior to joining Telkoor he served as Director of Sales Asia-Pacific at IOIMAGE, a world leading provider of Intelligent Video.

Before joining IOIIMAGE in 2006, Boaz served as VP Marketing, Sales & Business Development at Nextec Technologies and played a key role in its growth to become a global leading provider of non-contact measurement solutions.

Until 2001, Mr. Weiss held senior positions with Visoneix (part of the Visonic Group), Israel Military Industries - Reem Electronic Systems Division and the ADACOM Group.

Boaz holds B.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering and Management and MBA in Marketing from the Tel Aviv University and serves as a board member of "Shiluvim" the alumni of the Tel Aviv University Faculty of Engineering

Boaz has Twenty-six years of progressive front-line and management experience in International Sales, Marketing and Business Development of communications, military and production floor systems, hardware, software, solutions and services.

Mr. Weiss is experienced in establishment of distribution channels and OEM agreements in Western and Eastern Europe, USA, Asia, Africa and the Middle East for Data Communications, Telecommunications, Wireless and Security systems, power supplies, Laser, Optics, Measurement and 3D Non-Contact Digitizing Systems for various industrial applications.

Mr. Weiss has extensive international experience: in 40 countries. Lived and worked in USA.

His sales experience is of capital equipment and industrial systems to automotive and aerospace industries, factories, large accounts, armies and governmental organizations, including high-level negotiations
