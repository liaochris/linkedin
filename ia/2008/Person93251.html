Jodi A. Gusek’s Summary

Jodi Gusek is Senior Vice President, Corporate Research at Gemstar-TV Guide International. She supports the company’s business operations by initiating and overseeing all primary research activities as TV Guide continues to build upon its expertise and credibility as the authority on providing meaningful and engaging guidance solutions in a fast-shifting, multi-platform media marketplace.

Prior to joining TV Guide, Dr. Gusek was Vice President at Frank N. Magid Associates, a world leader in research-based consultation, where she worked for 12 years. At Magid, Dr. Gusek co-founded the new media research and consulting practice to provide strategic research and consulting services to interactive media companies for new product development and marketing. Her clients included Internet companies, cable and television networks, studios, syndicators, and MSOs.

She graduated with a Doctor of Philosophy in communication theory and research, and a Master of Arts in Communication Theory and Research, from the Annenberg School for Communication at the University of Southern California. Prior to graduate studies, she earned a Bachelor of Arts in Humanities from the University of California-Berkeley.
