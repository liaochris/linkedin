Arundat M Dasari’s Summary

I am currently part of the business optimization services division in Wipro BPO where my role is into product management including but not limited to development of concepts, product definition, development and execution of product marketing strategy, gathering of business requirements, business process re-engineering aiming at process excellence, providing technological solutions to business problems, patenting and protection of IP rights of the new products being developed. I am included in the core list of people on who's name the organization files patents on new products and technology.

After my MBA at IIML I was the marketing officer in the Corporate Business Division at Jumbo Electronics primarily looking after B2B sales and HP Category management. Past Experience prior to management studies include production planning and control in a manufacturing setup including responsibility for logistics and leading a large team comprising of individuals from varied backgrounds. After graduating in Mechanical Engineering from Delhi College of Engineering, I worked in the manufacturing sector with Coca Cola and Joyco, both the companies had world-class manufacturing facilities with well defined processes.

Integrity, Professionalism and Respect for Diversity best describe my values I exemplify at work.
