Chuck Everett, MPH’s Summary

My work aims at helping health care organizations assess current issues and finding solutions that increase efficiency, performance and profitability. I am known as health care product/service strategist and change agent. I consult with health plans, employers, union trust funds, physicians and other providers and health care technology companies. Frequent projects include relationship building between client companies and the health care marketplace, developing and launching strategic initiatives to retain and grow business, program management, provider contracting, payer and provider relations and research. Interests include working with payers and providers to find solutions that meet their unique needs.
