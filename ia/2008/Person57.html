The co. was recently bought over by Praxeva Group of Bulgaria, which was formed earlier last year under its Indian subsidiary Praxeva India Pvt. Ltd.

Web Cereal is the perfect resource for companies that are looking at having high-end websites, Online Business Solutions, Mobile Games/ Content/ Applications at an affordable price. Web Cereal has a genuine passion; proven methods and dedication to help you succeed by creating outstanding websites that portray a professional online identity.
