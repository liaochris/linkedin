Michael Bloom’s Summary

"If the value is there, the decision is clear." That's the motto in my mind in any business-to-business (B2B) marketing endeavor I undertake.

For the more empirical marketing folks out there, the "MARKETING SUCCESS EQUATION" (as I call it) is the guiding principle for success: MS=C2+Vme. MARKETING SUCCESS is the ability to CLEARLY COMMUNICATE the VALUE as it pertains to ME (your target audience). VALUE = (PERCEIVED BENEFIT - COST)RISK. It's that simple. No matter how complicated the sales process or how long the buy cycle, demonstrate the value, show your target the win-win scenario and your audience will convert.

Wow, if it’s that easy, you might ask why do we need marketers like me? Well, identifying the variables of the MARKETING SUCCESS EQUATION is relatively simple. My job, and the hard part, is to take lots of information, my high-level thinking and years of experience to deliver MARKETING SUCCESS. It all sounds very scientific, doesn’t it? Well, successful B2B marketing is.

A purchase decision in a B2B environment is very different from a consumer purchase decision. The implications of a wrong decision on a multimillion-dollar purchase are substantially higher than buying the wrong running shoes (unless you’re buying the shoes for everyone in your company—case in point). How you market to a B2B buyer requires a different approach. That’s where a marketer like me comes in.

I’ve been a B2B marketer for just over 10 years working in all aspects of integrated marketing communications. Prior to that, I was in foodservice as a manager at a fine dining restaurant. There I learned the art and value of delivering an “experience” through a balance of customer (centric) service and a superior product.

I’m a seasoned leader with a track record of success who leads by example. I’m highly respected and regarded by those I mentor as well as those who mentor me.

Michael Bloom’s Specialties:

I’m a high-level strategic thinker who believes there’s a solution to everything. My passion is determining the variables of the MARKETING SUCCESS EQUATION, implementing campaigns, measuring results and optimizing as appropriate.

I’m passionate about marketing and the trigger points that cause a buying decision. I’m also passionate about mentoring and developing those around me.
