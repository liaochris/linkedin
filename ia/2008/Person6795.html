Alexis Antonelli’s Summary

As an independent user experience consultant, I help create desirable, useful, easy to use, and innovative products that support strategic objectives.

With 10 years of experience in user interface design, I've created information architecture solutions and deliverables for clients including NBC, Fandango, Logitech, and Yahoo!. I am passionate about true user-centered design and have conducted usability testing and other research studies to guide conceptual modeling and inform critical design decisions for clients including Toyota and Verizon.

My academic and independent training in both business and human factors has helped me develop the right balance of advocating for user needs while staying focused on business goals. Lately, I’ve been working on rich internet applications, Flash, and Ajax interfaces for clients in the entertainment and automotive industries.

You may contact me at aa@uxconsultant.com

Alexis Antonelli’s Specialties:

Usability Testing, Information Architecture, Interaction Design, AJAX, Flash, and RIA, User Interviews, User Requirements, Competitive Analysis, Heuristic Evaluation/Expert Review, Card Sorting, Nomenclature and Web Site Content, Ethnographic Research and Contextual Inquiry, Survey Design and Analysis, Prototyping, User Profile and Scenario Development
