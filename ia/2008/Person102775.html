Kasey Crosby’s Summary

As a management intern at Wal-Mart, I learned the importance of customer service and developing strong relationships with my colleagues. In this position I developed the ability to multi-task and complete large amounts of work while being continually interrupted. Taking these lessons with me to WebEagle Design and ArdéoTech enhanced my ability to answer client phone calls while completing activities such as web programming, graphic design, budgeting, processing accounts payable and accounts receivable.

At ArdéoTech Business Solutions my duties included creating attractive web layouts, slicing them for use in HTML and then integrating them into the specified web application. I had the opportunity to develop and integrate my designs into various e-commerce applications, web portals, web driven content management systems and static websites. This position also allowed me to work with clients and establish business rules to ensure these sites worked in a manner similar to their business practices. As the company expanded into the print and promotional items market, I was exposed to an even greater level of responsibility. I began designing not only for the web, but also for promotional items, business cards, banners, postcards, etc. I also oversaw the bookkeeper while helping to maintain accounts receivable, accounts payable and order processing. Overall, my time at ArdéoTech gave me many opportunities to expand on my skills and experience.

I have a strong understanding of most web design and development technologies and supporting applications. I have experience helping companies from different business categories develop strong branding campaigns using print and promotional items. I work well in a team environment and in team leadership roles. I enjoy learning new technologies and passing my understanding on to others. I hope to one day find a job that is both challenging and rewarding.
