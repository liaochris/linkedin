Rashmi Ranjan’s Summary

My journey started with Cranberry Communications in 2004 with core area of business involving, web design, web development, online application development, multimedia development and mobile related content development and mobile solutions.

Our company was a typically out of garage company with a vision of being best service provider in online market. From day one our company's motto was clear to provide the best services and act as a online consultant for SMEs rather than a design or development company. Today we have a list of clientele who started very small on web and were really reluctant to think beyond small static website. We made it mandatory to optimise a website without charging extra money for search engines. today we are following the same fundamentals and when our client call us to tell how much business or enquiries they are getting. This is a real reward for us.

It might sound weired that for Cranberry " Team Members and Employees comes first and then customers". Because we believe in internal satisfaction and self believe. If a team member is happy and satisfied with their environment. Their output will be the best and clients will be delighted with out come.

In the coming years we might not become a big giants in terms of turnover or profitability. But we are sure that our clients satisfaction and their faith will be any day better from big money.

We would like to be a company who makes you happy and at an Individual level we will rest in peace with a smile on our face.
