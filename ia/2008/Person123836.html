Martyn Davies’s Summary

Top rank engineering professional with substantial experience addressing the needs of both internal and external customers. A wide ranging career encompasses: software development; customer facing pre & post sales support; product management; research, analysys and strategy development.

Interested in the convergence of VoIP and traditional neworking, including IMS, SIP and 3G. Also interested in the role of web services in bringing telecom and IT together, for example the uses of presence and pervasive communication.

See me also at Facebook (http://www.facebook.com) if LinkedIn is not your thing.

Martyn Davies’s Specialties:

VoIP/SIP; Speech enhanced IVR; CTI; application development; telecomm APIs; IM & presence. Podcasting and blogging.
