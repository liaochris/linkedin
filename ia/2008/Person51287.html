Didier Colombani’s Summary

Didier now works for Return Path International in charge of the business development activity for Europe.

In 2003, Didier co-founded ProActive-IT and headed the Sales and Account Management Team responsible for designing and deploying major international CRM programs.

Prior to joining ProActive-IT, Didier served as a Senior Account Director where he looked after Snarx Group’s most Strategic EMEA Accounts operating in the IT/Telco Industry.

Previously, he worked for Microsoft as a Market Intelligence Manager where he was responsible for delivering business-critical information and analyses to Microsoft’s Strategic Marketing Management Team.

Prior to this assignment, Didier worked for one of the leading European Competitive Intelligence Agencies as both a Key Account Manager and Research Analyst.
