David Barnes’s Summary

I'm passionate about communicating complex policy information to the media and the public in a format they can understand and use. My background includes stints as spokesperson for a Federal watchdog agency, a member of Congress and a national trade association. I've also worked as a journalist for national business-to-business publications and daily newspapers in Pennsylvania and New Jersey.

David Barnes’s Specialties:

Media relations, issues management, crisis communications, writing and editing.

  • Various positions, including vice president of public affairs.

    American Trucking Associations

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry)

    March 1998 — August 2000 (2 years 6 months)

    As part of a new management team at the national association representing the trucking industry, I restructured eight person public affairs office to align with the association’s new strategic priorities.

    I enhanced public outreach programs using professional truck drivers as spokespeople and developed and executed communications strategies to defend industry’s safety record in national and regional media.

    I subsequently helped revitalize the association's for-profit weekly magazine. I recruited and coached staff and led a team of journalists that won top national award from American Society of Business Publication Editors for coverage of proposed changes in Federal truck safety regulations.
