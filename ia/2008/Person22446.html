Betsy Zikakis’s Summary

Seasoned executive and marketing professional. Specific strengths in corporate branding, lead generation, product marketing and product strategy as well as direct marketing, e-commerce, internet and mobile marketing.

Experience Summary: Technology and technology-based Service Marketing; Marketing Leadership; Direct, Internet, and Mobile Marketing; Product Strategy

As head of marketing at Engage, Workscape, and Nellymoser, I have been responsible for all marketing functions, even IR at one point. During my tenure at Engage, I grew the marketing department from just a handful of people. Together, we quickly drove brand awareness from almost nothing to over 50% in an incredibly noisy market, generated leads that resulted in a run rate of $250 million in revenue annually across five product lines and three continents, and brought the company public and through seven acquisitions. At Workscape, I was hired to re-start marketing. That task included redesigning the marketing function, hiring top talent, creating a clearly positioned brand, and building a lead generation engine and a new product strategy. I just completed a long term consulting project for the CEO of Nellymoser addressing many of the same issues – branding, lead generation, and product strategy. I’ve run marketing organizations as small as 3 people to as large as 117 people and budgets from $300,000 to $22 million.
