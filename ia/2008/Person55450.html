Curt Fetter’s Summary

Dvee Media Productions is a full service script-to-screen video and film production company that provides all essential production expertise and crew/talent elements to create high quality and creative visual presentations for any business or broadcast program at the best possible budget.

Unlike other production companies, Dvee Media Productions also designs the Interactive Interface around the video presentation like you would find on a Web site or Interactive DVD.

Curt Fetter’s Specialties:

Production Consultation, Producer, Director, Video and Film Editorial (Avid, Final Cut Pro, Media 100, Premiere), Video/Audio Compression (Web,Streaming, DVD, CD-ROM, Podcast/RSS...ANY FORMAT), Interactive Design (DVD Authoring, Web Site Design) Audio Engineer/Live Sound Reinforcement and Camera Operator.
