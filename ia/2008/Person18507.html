Chris Fernandez’s Summary

I am a Philadelphia-area AVID editor with 10 years of professional experience. For the past four years, I was a freelancer in the NYC and Philadelphia markets, and I have numerous national and local broadcast credits. In September I began a new phase of my career, having accepted a staff position with Center City Film and Video of Philadelphia.

I am still open to the occasional short-term freelance opportunity, however, my staff position will be my top priority from this point forward.
