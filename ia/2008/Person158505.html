Reid Cooper’s Summary

I am a dynamic, goal-oriented professional with over twenty years of experience in major account sales and channel management. Extensive experience in project and market management in the enterprise software, telephony and data networking marketplace. Excellent reputation as a creative, energetic individual who enjoys being part of a team. Outstanding communication skills and a proven ability in delivering results.

I have worked in a senior management sales/channel role at several companies throughout my career including a director level at a fortune 500 company, and I have also been on the VAR/reseller side of the channel for over 12 years as the president of my own $15 million company. This experience has allowed me to understand a channel partner's motivation as well as assess a manufacturer's requirements to be able to bring a total business perspective to the table by being able to offer the channel a sense of walking in their shoes.

I have been involved in the strategic development of channel marketing plans and programs that have a proven track record of success throughout my career. I have consistently led my peers in achieving above quota revenues by assisting channel partners in closing enterprise software business to Fortune 1000 accounts.

Most recently, as Director of Channel Sales for a Security Software/Anti Spam Developer, I have successfully created a channel program from scratch and executed the plan to recruit VARs, System Integrators and resellers to sell an anti spam software solution.

The target customer has been the fortune 1000 enterprise and I have worked closely with the IT Management and CIO of these target companies. I bring a Rolodex of both leading channel VARs and System Integrators as well as relationships with enterprise accounts at the senior IT levels.

Reid Cooper’s Specialties:

Channel development, sales management, both direct sales force and channel sales force, large deal negotiations, enterprise IT connections, security software expertise and messaging. Channel Marketing, including marketing tools, outbound marketing programs, collateral development, seminar and presentation expertise.
