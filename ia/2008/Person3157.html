Adnan Shamsi’s Summary

Adnan Shamsi is currently with the Professional Economic Growth Group and a Registered Representative of Hornor, Townsend & Kent, Inc. (HTK).

He has more than a decade of advisory, financial structuring, investment and insurance experience. Major Fortune 500 corporations have sought Shamsi's opinions and advisory services. He has also worked with small and medium sized corporations and individuals advising on the mitigation of financial and operational risk and securing investment opportunities. Mr. Shamsi has worked closely with several agencies of the United States, including the Export-Import Bank, the Overseas Private Investment Corporation and USAID, as well as several multilateral entities including the World Bank's International Finance Corporation (IFC), the Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency (MIGA), the Asian Development Bank (ADB) and the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD). Mr. Shamsi was previously with Bank of America and Chase Manhattan Bank advising Fortune 500 corporations on the mitigation of cross-border and domestic risk.

Adnan received his MBA and Masters in International Management from Thunderbird, the Global School of Management, and his BA in Economics and Middle East Studies from Rutgers University.
