Cameron Newland’s Experience

  • Portfolio Manager/Analyst

    ScarletStream Capital Management, LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Investment Management industry)

    January 2006 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    I manage ScarletStorm, an alternative investment portfolio. I use a multi-strategy approach that involves investment in varied asset classes including deep-value and emerging-growth. As of September 2007, assets under management totals about $29 million. I buy independent research as well as conduct much of my own. My area of expertise as an analyst is wireless handset makers, often headquartered in Taiwan and South Korea, with a lot of production going on in mainland China. By using a proprietary approach to valuation coupled with superior knowledge of the current marketplace and product-pipelines of wireless device makers, I seek out valuation disparities and take out positions hoping to capture gains from their eventual movement. In addition to valuation-heavy analytical work, I occasionally utilize event-based investment, including buying ahead of known product announcements that will have favorable effects on future earnings.
