Amir Daliri’s Summary

Currently, Mr. Daliri Is the Chairman and CEO of de Latino Interactive- a cutting edge U.S. Latino Market online service provider and previously sat on the Board of Directors for Genesis Capital Ventures, an international Private Equity, Venture Management advisory firm.

Mr Daliri also served as the Founder and Chairman of Inovize, Inc.—a global business process outsourcing provider with offices in California and the Philippines.

Mr. Daliri brings knowledge in technology, capital markets and a strong finance background from his tenure in the private equity industry. He served as a Sr. Analyst for the PEM Group of Irvine, CA and a Business Development Associate for the public biotechnology company, Cobalis.

Amir studied molecular biology and biochemistry at the University of California, Irvine.
