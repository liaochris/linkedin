Jeff Amerongen’s Summary

I worked as a Retail Buyer for Surplus Direct.com, and SurplusAuction.com, both companies were purchased by Egghead.com, when Egghead.com was purchased by Onsale.com, I went to work for Amazon.com, from there I went to 800.com until they went out of business. from there I took a slight departure from Buying to Sales, working as a Manufacturers Representative for a National Rep firm (Lighthouse Marketing), selling primarily Consumer Electronics, Sporting Goods, and Housewares, to the Top 100 US Retailers. Initially based in the Pacific Northwest, (Oregon, and Washington), and later relocated to the Dallas/Arkansas area opening a new office for the company. I then went to work for RadioShack as a store buyer for Digital Imaging from there I was recruited by CircuitCity.com to rev up New Business and 3rd Pary Fulfilment, and after making significant positive changes there was tasked with the Audio/Video buisness.
