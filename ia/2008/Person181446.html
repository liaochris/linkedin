Steve Irvine’s Summary

Steve is an experienced entrepreneur with a history of building successful companies from start-ups to industry leaders. One of the early analysts in the Software-as-a-Service ("SaaS") market, Steve has a deep domain expertise in on-demand software, web 2.0, marketing automation, online marketing, social media and mobile marketing. Steve is recognized as one of the leading speakers and though leaders in the marketing automation marketplace and his current company, 80/20 Solutions, is widely considered to be one of the leaders in that emerging market.

Steve also sits on a number of boards as an adviser and director, helping to provide strategic and fundraising support for early stage technology companies.

Steve Irvine’s Experience

  • Chairman

    Opongo Inc.

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    January 2006 — Present (3 years)

    Opongo is an innovative start-up focused on creating an online community for Word of Mouth (WOM) marketing to the elusive 18-24 age demographic. This dynamic web property will focus on underground music, entertainment, and sports. Advertisers will be screened and select companies will be selected to be involved in innovative, multi-channel WOM campaigns.

    Steve is one of the visionaries behind Opongo and sits as the company's chairman.

  • Founder & CEO

    80/20 Solutions Inc.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    January 2004 — Present (5 years)

    80/20 Solutions is a leading provider of marketing automation solutions. They have developed a patent-pending Marketing Control Center (MCC) , on-demand platform, which helps marketers identify and engage customers in a more relevant and targeted fashion, resulting in more profitable, longer-term relationships. The MCC allows marketers to manage multi-channel campaign workflows within its powerful rules engine, which can be integrated with existing marketing vendors (e-mail, SMS, direct mail, call center, CRM, etc.) to leverage current investments in those applications.

    The MCC has been used by some of the largest global brands (Pepsi, General Mills, Mazda, Nokia) and advertising agencies (BBDO, DraftFCB) .

    Steve is the founder and CEO of 80/20 Solutions. He is responsible for the vision of the product, leading the strategic direction of the company, reporting to the board of directors, corporate development and global business development.

  • Managing Partner

    Irvine Capital Investments

    (Privately Held; Investment Management industry)

    November 2003 — Present (5 years 2 months)

    Irvine Capital Investments is a small, family-owned investment firm.

    Steve is one of the managing partners and oversees the investment portfolio.
