Stephanie Doyle’s Summary

I am taking the NASM personal training exam in early May - wish me luck, I've been studying my fanny off! I will be unveiling my personal training web site - www. TrainWithSteph.com very soon and am so excited!

After years of writing and editing I am now splitting my time between my two passions - writing and fitness. Well, actually my relatively new family - a beautiful daughter and son - are my two new passions, but working for myself allows for more time for them - sometimes!!

I ran my first marathon in 2001, eventually became organizer of that marathon training group, and now head a triathlon training group for beginners, teach swimming lessons for infants through adults (I was a fish in another life!), am a certified cycling and running coach, as well as a certified fitness nutrition coach and kid's nutrition specialist. I love love love helping "average" folks, whether it's helping them eat better in fun (yes, FUN!) ways, crossing the finish line at their first triathlon, or running a faster 5K.
