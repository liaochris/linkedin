Vinu Thomas’s Summary

Vinu Thomas joined Sirius Computer Solutions in January 2005. He completed his BE from Birla Institute of Technology, Ranchi in 1997 and has around 15 years experience across multiple Organizations. He started his career with Larsen & Toubro, one of the telecom construction majors in India where he worked for 1 year as a Graduate Trainee and was involved in setting up and operating diverse Network infrastructure for their fiber optic installations. Subsequently he spent over 3 yrs with Satyam Infoway Ltd (SIFY) as the head of their Data Center operations building world class data centers and network and security infrastructures in India. After a short stint with Network Translation (now Cisco) designing and developing the Cisco PIX code and the subsequent installation of Cisco PIX as an appliance, Vinu left to pursue a career with Williams Communications in USA. Vinu spent the next 8 months designing and buidling secure network infrastructure along the fiber optic lines in Oklahoma and Texas. In 2001, Hi-Link Computer Corp. approached Vinu to setup their network/security practice revolving around Cisco and other leading vendors like IBM, Microsoft, Symantec etc. In 2005, Vinu joined Strategic Computer Solutions (now Sirius Computer Solutions) as their Chief Security Officer & Architect to build and head a new Security & Networking Practice. SCS or Strategic Computer Solutions was IBM's 7th largest partner and Vinu grew the fledgling Cisco practice over 637% YTY adding many awards - the most prestigious being Cisco's Advanced Security Partner of the year for FY 2007. In the Fall of 2007, Strategic Computer Solutions was acquired by Sirius Computer Solutions. One of the key reasons for the acquisition was the business Unit headed by Vinu. Sirius is IBM's largest business partner. Vinu holds multiple industry certifications like CISSP and is recognized by the National Security Agency (NSA) and has consulted for the federal and state/local governments.

Vinu Thomas’s Specialties:

Vinu is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and holds over 20 high level certifications from industry leaders like Cisco Systems, Symantec, Microsoft, IBM and HP. He is recognized as an INFOSEC (Information Security) Professional, and by the National Security Agency (NSA). I have more than fifteen years of experience in the field of Enterprise Network/Systems Design and Implementation, Information Security, Threat & Vulnerability Assessment and Risk Management.
