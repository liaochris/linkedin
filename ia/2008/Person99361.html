Jon Tucker’s Summary

I am a business marketing student at San Diego State University as well as an ambitious entrepreneur. Through the launching of several small ventures since I turned 16 years old, I've been fortunate enough to learn many great lessons of entrepreneurship. In addition, being brought up in a family of business owners has allowed me to develop and grow my entrepreneurial passions from a very young age.

I currently own a web design company, creating websites for businesses around the country. Not to be confused with a one many website shop, this business consists of a worldwide team of designers and programmers, Project Managers to manage the various client projects we work on at any given time, as well as a few other key team members. Through the organization of this company, I've learned that building a quality business team is the key to success and scalability in any business venture.

Through LinkedIn, I look forward to meeting other business minded students, seasoned entrepreneurs, and other interesting people. Feel free to shoot me an email anytime, as I always enjoy a great conversation.
