Nigel Woodhouse’s Summary

A marketing leader with two decades of experience in a business-to-business enterprise software environment having held Director-level positions in $1 billion+ companies and Vice President positions in emerging companies in areas of corporate marketing, product marketing and alliance management. Extensive experience in creating awareness and demand for products and services through media relations, direct marketing, web marketing, telemarketing and customer relationship management. Significant experience building, managing and motivating a staff and working within a matrix structure with sales, development, partners and client services. Authored major marketing plans, helped grow revenues, performing against tight deadlines, exceeding objectives and taken a hands-on approach in environments of rapid change and growth.

I hold a BSc. honors degree in Mechanical Engineering Science from the University of Salford and an MSc. in Computer Integrated Manufacturing from U.M.I.S.T. in the UK.
