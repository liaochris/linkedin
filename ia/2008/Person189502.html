Tim Woda’s Summary

As a member of buySAFE’s founding team and managing director of business development, Tim is responsible for leading buySAFE’s business development team and establishing strategic partnerships with the Internet’s most successful companies. He is an expert in the areas of sales, customer retention, collaborative negotiation and the effects of trust and safety on etailers’ economics. Prior to buySAFE, Tim was the founder and managing member of The Telecom Recruiter, LLC, an executive search firm serving the telecommunications industry. He also severed as Southeast Area Director for MCI Communications where he managed several district sales teams and was inducted into the MCI Small Business Hall of Fame. Tim is a member of The National Association of Sales Professionals and a Certified Professional Sales Person (CPSP).
