Bart Ratliff’s Summary

I’ve been told that I’m a natural networker. I love to help others succeed in any way I can through building relationships and linking people together. My goal is to help others enhance their personal and business relationships, and help them achieve their goals.

Besides connecting people, my passion is working with charities. I provide effective tools to add a personal touch to their communication by showing appreciation to their members and supporters.

SendOutCards is a greeting card company that allows you to log onto your on-line account and create REAL custom cards with your charity or company’s logo and/or personal pictures, or choose from a database of over 12,000 cards. You simply choose the card, type your message and click "send." We print it, stuff it in a REAL envelope, add a REAL stamp, and mail it for you the very next day. The cards can even be in your own handwriting and signature and all for less than a dollar per card.

The name of the game is building relationships. In this electronic world of voice mail and emails, receiving a hand written card in the mail with images of your friends, family and clients will set you, your company, and organization apart from the others. If you would like to learn more about SendOutCards please call me on my cell at 412-608-5960, 314-783-4128 or toll free at 1-888-859-8969.
