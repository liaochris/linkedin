Kim Warner’s Summary

I have 7+ years of recruiting/staffing experience that includes contract recruiting, corporate recruiting and executive search. I prefer full life-cycle recruiting environments and have functioned in this capacity as a Sr. Technical Recruiter as well as a Recruiting Manager.

I'm a highly motivated, energetic and dedicated "hunter and gatherer" and have extensive experience utilizing Boolean logic and other creative, non-traditional search methods. Experience with various ATS and databases and in most recent position, assisted in the creation of company's specialized ATS and resume database.

I also have exceptional writing/editing skills and have made extensive contributions to various corporate websites and promotional/marketing material. My creativity and exceptional instincts, as well as my enthusiasm and passion for recruiting and building relationships, enable me to be successful in various work environments.

Kim Warner’s Specialties:

Ability to find quality candidates, negotiate compensations and close the deal. Experience managing full cycle recruiting/staffing processes (from open requisition to new hire orientations), formulating process and proceedures, managing Jr. recruiters, sourcers, and accounts and vendors.
