Pasquale Falconio’s Summary

My career in Space Industry began as System Engineer in the TLC Unit of Alenia Spazio, where I worked in the development of EODIS, an Earth Observation contents distribution system. As a member of the AIV Team, I designed and assembled Test Beds for the on-factory Integration of the Ground Segment and I took part to the deployment of the System Demonstrator at ESRIN premises.

As Technical Leader at ELIS I was in charge to carry out an assessment study for Alenia Spazio (and ESA) concerning the performance of OFDM satellite links in L-band for the provision of DAB services.

Later on, I worked at CTTC, designing “all-wireless” terrestrial architectures, assessing the ability of Multi-Radio Networks of supporting VOIP services.

As AIV Engineer at the MOS Dept. of Deimos Space I was responsible of the AIV Platform of the MSF of the GMS of Galileo. I took part to the PDR, supporting the AIV management in the production of the AIV Plan, defining the Verification strategies, tracing Technical Specifications with ERD requirements, defining and flowing down the technical requirements (ETSD) of the AIV Platform, for which I achieved the SW-SRR and PDR reviews with Thales Alenia Space.

I have recently joined Eutelsat, where I am working at the design, development and deployment of DVB architectures for both external television broadcasting customers and the Eutelsat internal video delivery platform sited at the Teleport of Rambouillet (Paris)

During these years I have got direct experience and an overall knowledge of System Engineering at different levels of a project Life Cycle from Implementation up to Component Design, in Navigation and Telecommunications domains. With the aim to both deepen my understanding of the interactions System Engineering and Project Leadership and to integrate my on-field experiences in Space Industry with a wider management theoretical background I have recently got a Master course in Project Management.
