Midy Aponte’s Summary

Bilingual public relations executive with ten years of experience across multiple areas of journalism and public relations, including in-house, agency and non-profit experience.

My background is primarily focused on corporate communications, public health, issues/crisis management and multicultural communications. Skilled at developing strategic public relations campaigns designed to secure media coverage, create issue awareness, raise brand recognition and empower audiences to become advocates for change.

Midy Aponte’s Specialties:

Strategic Communications Plan Development; Media Campaigns and Product Launches; Issues Management and Crisis Communications; In-House Communications and Agency Management; Advocacy and Corporate Social Responsibility; Media Relations and Spokesperson Training; Key Message Development; Coalition Building and Latino/Hispanic Outreach; Campaign Research and Evaluation; Public Health Education; Brand Management; Event Planning; Budget Monitoring
