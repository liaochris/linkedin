Farrukh Naeem (farrukh_copywriter@yahoo.com)’s Summary

Farrukh Naeem is an award winning advertising copywriter and journalist. He has worked as a copywriter for advertising agencies in Asia and the Middle East, a direct marketing writer for Wunderman under the Y&R banner, and a TV commercial scriptwriter. He is also a journalist and a popular online commentator on advertising, marketing and creativity in the UAE and Middle East and author of a popular ad blog at http://farrukh.wordpress.com

Multiple media expertise
==================

Farrukh's copywriting exploits include a wide range of media: from press and magazine ads to TV commercials and radio spots; from direct mailers to press releases; from corporate brochures to online catalogues.

Journalism credentials
================

As a journalist, Farrukh has covered a wide range of industries including telecom, cosmetics, health, transport, internet, education, finance, and more.

In the news
