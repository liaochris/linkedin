Leslie Munday’s Summary

Leslie has over seventeen years of IT industry experience and has worked as an analyst for the majority of his career. He has worked with the Rational Unified Process (RUP) and with Microsoft Solutions Framework (MSF) during the last seven years and has used the Unified Modeling Language (UML) for twelve years. His recent experience has been as a systems analyst and process analyst; writing requirements and defining a software development lifecycle (SDLC) based on the RUP. Leslie also teaches the RUP, UML, best practices for working with requirements and the Rational product suite and has been responsible for managing software development and defining development processes for several high-profile telecommunications and aerospace industry projects.
