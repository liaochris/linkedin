John M. O'Connor, MFA, CRW, CCM, ABI’s Summary

- 16+ years of comprehensive career services and outplacement organization specializing in Executive Outplacement, Corporate Outplacement, Federal/Military Career Transition, Select Permanent Placement Recruiting and Consulting in areas such as Online Identity Branding and Marketing for Businesses and Professionals.
- Appointed to the Board of Directors (2006) for Raleigh-Wake Human Resources Management Association (RWHRMA.org).
- Earned ABBA Business Broker Certification in 2008.
- Appointed Execunet Facilitator, Greensboro, NC monthly executive roundtable.
- Completed and Certified Career Coach (CECC); Certified Resume Writer (CRW); Credentialed Career Master (CCM).
- Recognized as the first private practice Certified Federal Job Search Trainer (CFJST) and the first Certified Federal Resume Writer (CFRW) in North Carolina.
- Featured in and is the author of many articles, including the Raleigh News & Observer, Resume Writers Digest, The Gladiator, Execunet, Career Masters Institute Monthly Newsletter, Monster Career News, Carolina Newswire, Tech Journal, Carolina NewsWire, and other national career publications such as JIST's Expert Resume guides.
- Diversified experience includes serving as Area Chairman for the Employer Support of the Guard and Reserve (ESGR.org) and volunteer for education and ombudsman work regarding USERRA law, rights and responsibilities of soldiers and employers.
- Multi-year service on the Business Advisory Council for Wake County Human Services including recent re-election to Programming Chair BOD role.
- Created unique web or e-based content for multiple websites, co-branded sites, LinkedIn Sites, executive biographies, web portfolios, web resumes, related corporate and personal branding tactical strategies.
- Developed and recommended innovative new links, pages and consulted on the visual and written content of existing small business websites, LinkedIn sites, Facebook and YouTube (visual and written) sites.
