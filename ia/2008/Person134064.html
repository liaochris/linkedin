Michael K. Connolly, MBA, BSCIS’s Summary

Michael is a dynamic, entrepreneurial, executive with considerable experience in Information Technology (IT) strategies, business development, global sales, channel management, strategic alliances, professional services, complex contract negotiation, market research, product training, and multiple forms of published writing.

Business and Technology Strategies’ (BTS) Primary Goal: To stress the critical importance of business planning and outsourced services that address potential threats to business operations. It is now coming to light, in many ways, that the commonly anticipated events such as hurricanes, floods, tornados, fires, and earthquakes are a mere subset of the overall guises in which outage events can occur; be it intentional or accidental.

To achieve this goal, BTS, structured as an objective consultancy / brokerage, provides a means for business owners to effectively evaluate their current state of risk and recoverability as well as discern among the applicable complex solutions, service providers, and cost-to-service breakdowns available to them for more informed decision making. BTS has access to a vast network of services from recognized and reliable providers as well as a wealth of direct contacts in multiple industries allowing clients more options, preeminent costs, and, ultimately, the right solutions.
