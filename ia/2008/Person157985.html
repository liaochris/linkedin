Kathy Brandon’s Summary

The Ready for Happiness Group is a self development resource house who offer transformational experiences through Life Coaching, Workshops, and Specialty Projects.

We work with individuals, families, small business, and non-profit organizations who are ready to create and sustain their happiness and growth.

Our revolutionary Happiness Mapping technology helps our clients discover and unleash their vision of happiness, giving us the ability to add the proper resources to bring about the realization of the map.
