Aron Baczoni’s Summary

I am a Management and IT Consultant, specializing in IT Service Management and ITIL (IT Information Library), which is a framework for describing best practices in IT service areas (ie Change Management, Configuration Management, Software Control & Distribution and Help Desk, Service Level Management, etc).

Currently I am looking for the next CIO who wants to steer his organization towards success (reduced and transparent IT costs, improved productivity and service quality, pro-active IT management, etc.) by the means of ITSM / ITIL.

If you have a troubled ITIL implementation project that you need to get back on track, if you do not see the benefits and results promised, or if you just want to know more about the benefits of implementing ITSM / ITIL feel free to contact me.

I can help you set up and launch your project so it produces significant and measurable results in the first six months, can help you overcome resistance towards ITSM in your organization, can help you choose a support system that suits your needs and help embed the solutions into your organization.

- Experience in IT service management, ITIL process implementations, business process optimization and re-engineering, service catalog and CMDB creating, implementing and embedding changes into organizations.

- Tendency to thrive in fast-paced business environments while remaining focused on attaining objectives. Ingenious and original problem solver.

- Passion for business with commitment and drive to succeed.

- Comfortable working with people from diverse backgrounds and nationalities.

- Self-motivated team leader with the ability to empower management and lead teams to achieve business and company objectives.
