  • Chairman & Founder

    Education for Free Research Center

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; E-Learning industry)

    June 2007 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    I started Education For Free (www.educationforfree.org) to solve some of the problems faced by public schools in India. Using our Virtual Classroom technology, educators can teach in schools from anywhere in the world, thus enabling children to learn from the best. we also provides other facilities like a platform for collaboration among teachers, learning and teaching resource repository, trainings and much more. It is a nonprofit charitable organization based in Hyderabad, India. Our beta version is being used by over 200 people now, and we plan to deploy it in around 5 schools in the next academic session, the first 5 years projection is 1000 schools.

    (Computer Networking industry)

    September 2001 — April 2007 (5 years 8 months)

For instance, advanced telephony and collaboration features are not within the reach of SMBs and PanTerra’s mission was to create a platform that seamlessly integrates all communication aspects for a small business conveniently and affordably. Today, PanTerra is an established on-demand, unified communications service provider, making a difference for a large number of SMBs.
