Rex Saunders’s Experience

  • Political Candidate & Humanitarian Organizer

    Citizens For Saunders

    (Political Organization industry)

    January 2000 — Present (9 years)

    Candidate and Democratic nominee for Hawaii State House 2004 & 2006 (see www.rexsaunders.com ) and Humanitarian Worker and Organizer (see www.alldonne.com ) around the world including procuring and delivering one of the first truckloads of aid to Baghdad in March 2003 with Jhelp (Japan Emergency Team) and the Assyrian Church of the East. Actives included teaching English in Serbia, India, Russia and China.

  • Sales Manager

    Brockhues AG

    (Chemicals industry)

    August 1994 — January 2000 (5 years 6 months)

    [acquired by Rockwood Specialties] Sales Manager first for NE USA & Canada and later for UK, Ireland and English-speaking International Market outside of the America's. Increased sales from $500K to $2.8M and the number of installed GRANUMAT®’s from 1 to 9. Brockhues AG is the leading supplier of granulated pigments and automated dosing equipment for the concrete products industry.

  • Sr. Sales Representative

    Unocal

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; UCL; Oil & Energy industry)

    July 1986 — February 1990 (3 years 8 months)

    [acquired by Ashland Oil] Responsible for sales & marketing of aliphatic and aromatic hydrocarbons (UNOCAL was the leading domestic producer) and a full distributor line of chemicals, while also providing technical service and support.Developed sales from $1.4MM to $3.1MM (performance ranked in the top 5% company-wide) while the remainder of Branch Office’s sales increased just 10%. Opened over 70 new accounts and consistently received the maximum incentive bonus (30%). Secured the largest single hydrocarbon account in Florida (IMC, uranium extraction) by working weekly with their R & D Department to determine the optimal extraction solvent for their process. Championed new products by providing internal technical training and market support. Worked on the Quality Improvement Committee to redefine pricing procedures and to optimize QC efficiency; received Quality Achievement Award in 1988. Prepared 5 year Marketing Plan, later used as a model within UNOCAL.
