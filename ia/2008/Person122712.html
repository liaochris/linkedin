Mark Taylor’s Summary

After earning CPA with international public accounting firm, worked way up the Finance ranks at Microsoft during the growth years '92 to '97, building and managing the Microsoft Network Financial Operations team then implementing company's first ever web based time tracking and expense reporting system. Moved to dot com startups where developed and presented business plans, developed internal financial and HR operations as CFO/COO, then just as the bust started, took experience to Pacific Northwest financial services firms where architected project financials for enterprise wide technology reengineering efforts. Earned six sigma based Operational Excellence certification, currently studying for the Black Belt exam.
