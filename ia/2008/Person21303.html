Ben Gottesman’s Summary

Well-known and respected authority in the technology industry with nearly 20 years’ perspective on the market and special emphasis on Internet services and software. Intelligent, creative, technically adept, and used to working under deadline pressure. Ability to understand products and their position, as well as users’ needs and interests creating research tools to gather further insight.
