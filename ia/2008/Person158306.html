Samantha Jones’s Summary

Samantha Jones is a professional actress and comedian. Born and bred in NYC, she holds New York close to her heart. Her 4th solo show, Butterfly Suicide, was selected as one of only 12 shows to be featured in the prestigeous Solo Nova Festival at PS122 in NYC. She was winner of the Toyota Comedy Festival, Houston Worldfest- Best Actress, and Syracue Film Festival- Best Actress. Her 3rd solo show I Wanna Be Like Her was produced in NYC on Theatre Row. Her other shows were produced by HERE Theatre and Manhattan Theatre Source. Her solo work was featured at benefits for Gilda's Club and the Special Olympics. Her credits include MTV Run's House, Comedy Central , Caroline's Comedy Club, Stand Up NY, and several colleges around the country. The feature film, The Last Christmas Party, produced by her company Dora Mae Productions and featuring her work as an actress will be submitted to Sundance Film Festival this summer.www.doramae.com

Samantha rounds things out nicely as a real estate broker for Prudential Douglas Elliman. Her specialties include residential sales and new development in NYC. She and her partner, Michelle Churchill, both have entertainment backgrounds which bring a wonderful angle to their sales approach.
