AmericaLinx Company (ALCO)’s Summary

I represent the AmericaLinx Company (ALCO) based in Las Vegas, Nevada. We have years of experience providing complete product strategy, architecture, launch and support services for some of the world’s leading companies. Our expertise can positively impact your company’s ability to design, develop, and market products in the global marketplace with a huge reduction in cost and time to market.

We have a very strong and robust quality framework for medical devices, electronics devices, automotive devices, product development, testing and support. ALCO has helped some of the biggest companies to bring out high quality, low cost products relatively early into the market.

AmericaLinx Company has the most diverse new R&D and product design / manufacturing services in the world. We provide segment specific solutions, acting as your extended engineering partners, contract manufacturers and distributors. We provide end to end product engineering services including electrical, electronics and mechanical. We offer complete hardware and software development services along with product testing, product lifecycle management, product data management and regulatory compliance support services.

Please write to us at sales@americalinx.com if you are interested in having a discussion about your project.

Also, you can visit us at http://www.americalinx.com/
