Jason Bernstein’s Summary

Jason Bernstein
WEB DEVELOPMENT MANAGER

Driven to exceed expectations, Jason Bernstein develops, manages, and improves services, sales, communications, and operations.

In 2004, he began marketing Lake Washington Technical College's training opportunities and recruiting students; achieving 100% growth in 2 years. Jason collaborates with international enterprises in Asia and Mexico to create and manage cross-functional professional development programs and services for students, managers, and executives.

Jason has enjoyed establishing productive working relationships with the U.S. Commercial Service, Washington State Department of Community, Trade and Economic Development (CTED), Trade Development Alliance of Greater Seattle and public and private offices abroad. Most recently, Jason worked directly with the Republic of Korea's Secretary of Labor to the President.

Prior to international and education management, Jason worked in the high-tech industry for both Amazon.com and Microsoft.
