Alan Johnson’s Summary

I am currently involved in identifying and pursuing new investment, business and product opportunities. Primarily, I try to adapt to the financial markets the concepts and principles I learned during my 30+ years in business development within the chemical industry.

A recent interest of mine is the concept of risk, reward, and regret as presented in the recent book "The Rules of Risk" by Ron S. Dembo and Andrew Freeman. I have found it to be an easier task to quantify these concepts for investments in the equity markets than it is to develop these same assessments for investments in early-stage business and product opportunities.

An ongoing interest of mine is to successfully apply these principles to investments in financial instruments. I welcome the exchange of ideas in this area.
