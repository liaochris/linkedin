Stacey Davis’s Summary

Graduate of Ball State University in 2000 - BS in Journalism Graphics.

My first few years in the “real world” I designed pages for several Indiana newspapers.

After a few years I joined the advertising world via hhgregg headquarters in Indianapolis.

For 3 years I interviewed with a small advertising agency in Broad Ripple called the White House Group. I finally got the job in 2005! I stuck around another 2.5 years learning A LOT about business.

Thanks to several of my friends I had several freelance projects which finally led to me quitting the position at WHG to explore my entrepreneurial side.

And so we have D2 - Davis Designs.
