Shawn Stone’s Summary

I have been in the game industry since '99, with a brief foray into other forms of software QA during the dotcom boom. I am an avid gamer and constantly have my metaphorical finger on the pulse of the gaming community. I love well-produced content that innovates and expressive games that have something to say. Having a QA background, I pay keen interest in the user's experience, ensuring that not only is their experience one that is polished but it is especially accessible and inviting.
