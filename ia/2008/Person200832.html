LaKesha Womack’s Summary

My name is LaKesha Womack and I am the Owner and Lead Consultant of Womack Consulting Group. I have a degree from Vanderbilt University and over 10 years experience in the service industry. I have managed, coached and trained team members in the retail, food service, real estate and financial services industries.

I believe that every customer is a client and deserves star treatment regardless of our natural tendacy to prequalify. I also believe that business owners have to decide to work on their business more than in the business. This is much easier said than done but it is not impossible. The combination of these two beliefs led to the creation of Womack Consulting Group.

I had several friends go to work for the major consulting firms and help big businesses trim the fat and operate more efficiently yet when I look at the small business, there is little if any practical assistance available. Of course, there are books you can read and seminars that get you all fired up; but no one comes back to the store/office with you and shows you how to apply those principles. There are lots of coaches that will stand on the sidelines and make recommendations but I decided that I wanted to get in the game.

What I love most about my company, is our committment to working in your business with you. We do this so that we can really get a feel for the day to day pressures and problems. That is the only way to provide realistic strategies toward improvement. We even take it a step further and stick around to help you implement our recommendations.

No other company will care about your business and its success as much as Womack Consulting Group. We understand that business ownership is the road to true wealth accumulation but you will never reach that point if you burn out, the business fails or it just doesn't move forward.
