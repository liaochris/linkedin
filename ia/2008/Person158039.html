    August 1991 — January 2000 (8 years 6 months)

Merged Real Time with Evans Hunt Scott in 1990 to become ehsrealtime, the first merger in the UK of a DM agency with a digital agency. I was vice chairman and operations director of the merged agency.

  • Managing Director

    APT Photoset

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Design industry)

    May 1979 — July 1990 (11 years 3 months)

    One of the UK's leading typography studios producing award winning artwork for the UK's most prominent design groups and advertising agencies before the advent of the Apple Mac. The company was known for its "panache" with its three dancing men logo, three yellow taxis (when all other taxis in London were black) and the famous APT 5-a-side football competitions for Design, Advertising and Art Colleges. Fun days indeed.
