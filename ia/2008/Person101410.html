Julia Zamorska’s Summary

Currently a Senior Account Executive at Ruder Finn in LA, I work with a variety of emerging business-to-business and consumer technology companies. I manage the day-to-day account work for Specific Media, #3 comScore-ranked advertising network, an account I led through a host of high profile thought leadership campaigns and significant funding news. As a result, the Specific Media team won PRSA-LA's 2007 PRism Award in the "One Time Media Campaign" category for a thought leadership campaign around recent acquisitions in the online advertising space.

My other clients include companies such as Interpolls, a next generation advertising company and New Age Electronics, a consumer electronics supply chain provider. My consumer experience includes a campaign for support.com, a remote tech support service, nominated for the 2008 Silver SABRE Awards in “E-Commerce” category, as well as a five-month-long campaign for Sony Vaio WA1, a wireless digital music streamer.

I previously worked at Antenna Group, a boutique public relations agency in San Francisco specializing in high technology and clean technology clients. I managed public relations campaigns for three clients: Jigsaw, MyNewPlace and MyBizHomepage. I worked with a variety of other technology start-ups, such as Dovetail, Eurekster and eqo, as well as with some more established companies, such as Fiberstars, Internet Archive and Pilot.

Born and raised in Poland, I have a BA in Social Science (summa cum laude) from University College Utrecht, Netherlands and a Masters degree in social anthropology from University of Oxford, England.
