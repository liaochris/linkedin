GHAZANFAR AZZAM’s Summary

Playing a leadership role as “Project Director / COO Designate” in establishing a greenfield microfinance bank in Pakistan. Built a team of professionals for core banking functions i.e. Asset & Liability Marketing & Mangement, Product Development, Information & Communication Technology (ICT), HR, Training & Development & Finance.

The team, with assistance from world class consultants, developed a 5 year business plan i.e. vision & mission of the new entity, business strategy, market analysis, menu of products and services, organizational structure, branch network, budgets for pre-establishment costs, branding (corporate logo, colour scheme, layout and interior and exterior design of the branches) and projected financials for the first 5 years.

In a career spread over 20 years, worked for four leading Pakistani banks i.e. Habib Bank, Union Bank (now Standard Chartered, Pakistan), Bank Alfalah and Prime Bank (Now part of ABN Amro Bank, Pakistan). Recently completed a consulting and project management job as “Financial Sector Specialist” for ShoreBank International on a USAID funded WHAM project aimed at building and strengthening Small Business lending capacity in Pakistani financial institutions.

GHAZANFAR AZZAM’s Specialties:
