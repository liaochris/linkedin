Bob Dudley’s Summary

Bob opened his Executive Search practice in 1993 after more than 20 years managing operations for Fortune 500 companies. The focus of his search activity has mainly been at senior levels within HR Management. He has been responsible for strategic talent acquisitions for Fortune 1000 clients including C-level searches, as well as multiple Vice President, Director and Manager engagements.
Bob and his office also have extensive experience in executive search within the Biotechnology, Manufacturing, Transportation-/Logistics and Information Technology areas.

Active in the local chapter of California Staffing Professionals, he was their Recruiting Consultant of the Year in 2000. He earned his Certified Professional Consultant designation in 1995 and his California
Accredited Consultant certification in 1998. Bob received his Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Administration from Roanoke College and his MBA from Lehigh University.

I am interested in developing new client partnerships as well as working with candidates to manage their careers.
