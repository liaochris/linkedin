Bart Matterne’s Summary

As I grew up I learned how to work with MacOS classic on an SE/30 and played games on a Commodore64, I wasn't so keen on Windows because 3.11 was just a MacOS clone in my eyes so I installed Slackware GNU/Linux onto my x86 machine at the age of 16. This had an impact on my whole life leading me to many UNIX like flavors like the BSD family and later on to Solaris. Until now this has never bored me and I’m eager to learn more about it every day!
