Rafael Muñoz’s Summary

A child of the Spectrum generation, I started fiddling with the Internet behind the screen of a DEC VT1000 terminal back in the late 90s, where I hand-coded my first webpage on vi.

I've been working on all fields related to the web: front/back end programming, flash, user-centered visual design, human-computer interaction, project management and marketing campaigns. This broad vision of the media allows me to build concepts and manage teams on solid, real foundations rather than expectations, as well as knowing when to arguably say "no" before it is too late.

I've also appeared in industry and mainstream publications and spoken at a couple of Web 2.0-related conferences, as well as co-founding the Spanish Rails Conference.

Rafael Muñoz’s Specialties:

cross-media projects, great eye for detail and quality assurance.
