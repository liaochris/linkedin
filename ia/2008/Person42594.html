Damian McNulty’s Summary

I have been very successful at placing high-level IT and Telco individuals with a number of organisations, ranging from start-up companies to big, international conglomerates. I have placed a range of people, from Sales through to Directors, and more technical specialists. I have recruited and placed on a Global level, including providing companies with no prior presence in certain countries with a recruitment strategy to establish that presence.

I am always interested in building relationships with individuals looking for work, as well as organisations looking to recruit the best possible candidates.

Through my experience in the ICT sector I have the largest global network of world-class candidates, many of whom work with me on a sole basis.

I provide headhunting and my company also offers executive retained search options.

My client list is extensive and includes start-up through to blue chip.
