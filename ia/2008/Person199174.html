Walter E. Gaines, MBA’s Summary

Walter is a Senior Operations Executive with 20+ years of experience in Operations and 12+ years managing privately held asset and non-asset Transportation and Logistics companies. He has have been involved in start-ups and have acted as a change agent in turn-around situations. This has involved migrating entrepreneurial companies to process driven entities, strengthening the infrastructure enabling sustained growth and positioning for sell.

His experience incorporates extensive knowledge in all modes of transportation such as parcel/small package, LTL, TL (team/single), Intermodal, Drayage (port/rail), Ocean (FCL/LCL), Air Freight (domestic/international). He is experienced in developing and managing transportation programs and negotiating tariffs in all modes of transport. He has also developed and managed distribution and warehousing programs, built and managed transportation operations departments, customer service departments, accounting groups, and sales teams.

His goal is to secure a position within an organization that values its employees and recognizes them for their involvement in the overall success of the company. Additionally, His desire is to be involved in strategy creation with a forward thinking executive team in conjunction with his desire is to manage the involvement and empowerment of associates which allows them to feel part of and take ownership in the successful strategy implementation.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    October 1994 — February 2006 (11 years 5 months)

    AMTREX Global Logistics is a global supply chain service provider focusing on the transportation links in the supply chain. AMTREX builds and manages transportation and logistics programs.

    At AMTREX, I approached business in three steps. The first step, I acted as consultant; interviewing the customer to determine the weakest points in their supply chain. Next, I developed transportation and logistics programs around the weak links and then I implemented the plan; utilizing AMTREX operational teams to the customer specific transportation and logistics supply chain programs.

    I negotiated and managed carrier contracts in all modes of transport (Ocean, LCL, TL, Intermodal, LTL, Parcel, Domestic and Intl. Air Freight, warehousing and value added distribution) In addition, I was responsible for internal operations management, general management, sales support and business development.
