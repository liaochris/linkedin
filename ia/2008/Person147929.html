Paul Louis Villani’s Summary

BIO i describe myself as a musician, composer, photographer and all round dreamer and i'm totally convinced that i was born in the wrong era! I should've been born in 1950 so that i would've been 18 years of age in 1968 and living in San Francisco or California! Peace, love, freedom, tie-dye and Kaftans!! Hendrix, Zeppelin, The Beatles, Warhol and Dali my creative minds nirvana! i'm extremely passionate about art and all its forms and dream constantly about one day owning my own gallery and studio. Current CV... Have been part of two group exhibitions at the Toyota Community Gallery in Hobsons Bay. Finalist in the Williamstown Festival - Tattersals Contemporary Art Prize 2006. Exhibition was held at The Substation Gallery. Finalist in the Kodak Salon Contemporary Photography Award 2006. Exhibition was held at the Centre of Contemporary Photography. Winner of the "Best Alternative Medium" Award at the Hung Art Exhibition 2006. Had work on display at the "He Who Belongs" Exhibition at the Walker St Gallery 2006. Winner of the "Peoples Choice" Award at the Digital Hardcopy Exhibition at The Gasworks Gallery 2006. Finalist in the Julie Millowick Acquisitive Photography Prize in March 2007 - Image entitled "The Way Out" was on exhibition at the Old Castlemaine Goal as part of the Castlemaine State Fair. Was commissioned to supply photographic artwork for the main public foyer of a new health care facility in Melton. Opening March 2007. Had work on display at the "Churchie" exhibition in QLD during June 2007. Solo Exhibitions - April 2007 at Cusp Gallery in Northcote and June 2007 at The Brunswick Arts Space. August 2007 have work permanently on display and for sale (signed and of limited edition) at The Brunswick Street Gallery in Fitzroy. Have been selected to exhibit four images at an exhibition featuring 4 world photographers at the GaleriaZero in Barcelona Spain, from Sept 14th to Oct 12th 2007.
