Lonnee Hamilton’s Experience

  • Congressional District Team Coordinator for Barack Obama Campaign

    Obama for America

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Political Organization industry)

    February 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    I created a grassroots group called "Pasadena and Altadena for Barack Obama" back in February, when Barack announced his candidacy. The group grew to become one of the strongest local groups in California.

    The campaign then asked those of us who were strong on the grassroots local level to become congressional team leaders. I then became leader of my congressional team.

    We secured the endorsement of our congressman, Adam Schiff, and received local and international media attention.

  • Communications Coordinator for California Congressional District 29

    Obama for America

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Political Organization industry)

    February 2007 — August 2007 (7 months)

    I created and maintained numerous local listservs as a volunteer for the Barack Obama campaign.

    Through my listservs, I was able to organize a locally based grassroots coalition of people interested in supporting Barack Obama. Through our organizing efforts, we won large sections of our congressional district and secured the endorsement of our congressman Adam Schiff for Barack Obama.
