Rob Kleeger’s Summary

I have lead several start-up entrepreneurial ventures in various technology-based industries over the past 14 years, three (3) which led the venture to be acquired. I pride myself as a well-seasoned strategic sales, marketing, and business development executive that has gained hands on experience in building and transforming businesses through strategic and tactical growth.

Currently, I am leveraging my experience in engaging and developing early stage complex technical digital forensic investigation services for law firms, all size corporations, corporate IT and/or security executives, human capital professionals, and other litigation support professionals. I have vast experience in matters involving data theft and crimes facilitated by computer such as (Employment related - Restricted Covenant, Non-compete, Theft of IP, Commercial Litigation, Embezzlement, Sexual Harassment, Discrimination and matrimonial related matters).

I work with clients daily to develop strategies and implement cutting-edge technologies to manage cost related issues related to ESI (Electronically Stored Information). I have assisted helping corporations and their outside counsel confronted with the challenges, increased costs of navigating the complex and seemingly ever-changing world of eDiscovery, Digital Investigations, and Legal issues pertaining to ESI. I regularly counsel, inform, and educate attorneys, business owners, general counsel, and HR professionals about this emerging and rapidly growing electronic discovery (eDiscovery) field. I have counseled clients through 100’s of cases in both the federal and state courts.

Prior to my career in Digital Forensics, I developed a passion and developed a "remarkable natural ability" to help other people. I was a high-end executive placement "headhunter" and use my experience, ability, and skills to build relationships with attorneys in all size firms and several with the nation's top law firms.

Rob Kleeger’s Specialties:

Matters involving data theft and crimes facilitated by computer such as Employment related - Restricted Covenant, Non-compete, Theft of IP, Commercial Litigation, Embezzlement, Sexual Harassment, Discrimination and matrimonial related matters.
He regularly advises both inhouse corporate counsel, general counsel, outside counsel in both Federal and State litigation matters.
