Frode Lundsten’s Summary

I am a self-motivated, enthusiastic, and experienced MBA professional with a flair for problem analysis and solving, strategy development and implementation. I feel comfortable working in an environment of constant change, matrix organization, and high pressure, where I have the possibility to influence decisions and execute these independently or through people. I have a positive and inquisitive outlook on life, and seldom get stressed over daily events or business turmoil.

My career is characterized by being international and cross-industry in scope and experience as I have studied and worked on three continents, where I have learned to deal with different cultures and cultural contexts amid working within business development of own and established companies, within management consultancy as strategy consultant, strategic marketing consultant, and due diligence team leader; and within sales as client manager and senior proposal manager.

My ambition is to move into senior management within a time frame of 3-5 years, possibly as a senior executive of a small company or as a director of a division of a large organization. The functional areas are likely to be within general management, sales and marketing, or corporate development.
