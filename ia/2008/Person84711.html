Jason Clement’s Summary

Jason is a Partner and Director for Neo@Ogilvy, a fully integrated division of WPP's Ogilvy Group. He's responsible for the company's search marketing operations in New York and oversees global search marketing strategy for several large Ogilvy clients. Jason is an eight year industry veteran, well versed in site optimization strategies as well as advanced auction buying methodologies.

After starting his career with a short stint at a pure-play search agency in 1999, Jason's found a new home helping to develop successful search marketing outfits for traditional media planning and buying agencies. He's successfully integrated company acquisitions and lead cross-agency teams to deliver on client demands for two of the largest agency holding companies.

Jason's currently managing a group of twenty search professionals in New York and overseeing strategy for campaigns across fifteen countries. In spite of spending most of his time on conference calls and airline flights, he still claims to live with his three cats, two dogs and tolerant girlfriend in Brooklyn, New York.
