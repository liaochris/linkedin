Jason Leonard’s Experience

  • Managing Director

    Talent Maze

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    July 2008 — Present (4 months)

    Talent Maze provides an Internet based exchange connecting Hiring Companies willing to use search firms with the extended marketplace of external Recruiters who represent top talent. The company is not a job board or sourcing tool, rather it represents an improved way to connect buyers (Employers) and sellers (Recruiters) within the Recruiting industry through a marketplace concept, at no additional cost to either party. (Comparable business models in other verticals include Ebay, Auto Trader and Lending Tree.) Talent Maze provides a secure, easy-to-use and relevant online product that solves for many of the inefficiencies and pain points that exist in the Staffing industry today.
