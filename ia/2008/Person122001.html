Mark Grindeland’s Summary

Seasoned executive in the areas of strategy, marketing, product management, and channel management. I have a broad range of industry experience and am comfortable working in both Fortune 500 and start up environments. I am highly strategic and customer focused, and able to devise powerful marketing and product insights that lead to innovative, exciting, action oriented and profitable marketing and product initiatives.

Co-founded m-Qube, one of the leading wireless technology companies in American, partnering with wireless operators (AWS, Cingular, Nextel, T-Mobile, Sprint, Verizon, Altell, Cincinnati Bell, Rogers AT&T, Bell Mobility, TELUS, Microcell, and others),leading media companies and advertisers to drive adoption and usage of data services by mobile consumers.

CEO and Corp. Generalist, having been the CEO of Wunderman (Europe, Middle East, and Africa). Have worked in both start up environments as well as fortune 500's.

Helped build one of the leading marketing service companies in North American, Digitas.

International business strategy, segmentation, experience based branding, CRM.

Consulted on growth strategies to some of the worlds leading companies, including American Express, Citibank, FedEx, IBM, Ford, General Motors, Ericsson, State Street Bank, AT&T, AWS, British Telecom, Sony, Chubb Insurance, Prudential, Sears, Harcourt, Houghton Mifflin, US Airways, Alliance Air, Mitsui, and others.
