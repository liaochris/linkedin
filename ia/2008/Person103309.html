Kathryn Hawkins’s Summary

I am the editor of Gimundo.com, a website dedicated to good news and positive stories from around the world. With my husband Jeff, I am co-founder of Hawkins Multimedia, which provides high-quality and reasonably priced web design and other media materials for small businesses and individual clients. I have extensive experience in writing for both websites and print journalism, and am open to taking on additional assignments on a freelance basis.
