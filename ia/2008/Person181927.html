Steven Robert’s Summary

My professional experience is probably best summarized as a passionate IT generalist. I'm of a rare breed whom attempts to balance both infrastructure (network, security and VOIP) and software development (SOA, GRID, XML and AGILE).

My best attribute has been my creativity and resourcefulness. I have a unique ability to visualize a problem (or opportunity) then analyze the business and technical requirements (and/or associated risks) and rapidly produce a proof-of-concept. I'm a big picture person who thrives on challenges.

My most notable [professional] goal probably surrounds promoting our latest incubator project, "Billhighway.NET". The service offers consumers (you and me) an unparallel social-money experience. Anybody, for any reason, can use the service to organize and track real money among groups (ok, individuals too). This includes collecting money via the web (and soon mobile) from your friends/family using a credit card or eCheck. A key differentiator here is the concept of “groups” – unlike PayPal’s emphasis on the “individual” we manage funds at the group level – enabling you to create virtual account balances on the fly! It literally has the ability to change the way you manage offline expenses with your friends via your online persona. Check it out - www.billhighway.net!
