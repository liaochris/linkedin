Javaid Ahmed’s Summary

Javaid Ahmed is now a Karachi based academic and consultant with over 30 years of a successfully progressive senior management career with the multinational health care industry including over a decade in Switzerland, Malaysia and the Philippines. He is Dy. Director Research, Professor of Management and Chairperson of the Department of Management and HRD at IoBM.

Apart from his background in business and academia, Javaid is a Fellow of the Chartered Management Institute (UK) and Member of the Chartered Institute of Marketing (UK). He is a member of Mensa. He is research active in strategy and marketing effectiveness in industry and in the supervision of CBM’s MPhil marketing programme. Javaid says he is a facilitator, not a teacher, nor an entertainer. A great believer in collaborative learning, his sessions engage participants in reflection and action leading to the development of practical knowledge and skills.
