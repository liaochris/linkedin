Alan Singer’s Summary

If you are a Senior Executive, Sales Professional/BDM, IT Professional considering a new career step or a move into contract or "executive interim" assignment then I can guarantee you FOUR things when you work with me;
1. Introduction to opportunities with the VERY BEST employers
2. An outstanding presentation of your professional details
3. Pursuit of your career and contact opportunities with "relentless pasion"
4. Presentation of opportunities that are a MATCH to your needs, skills and aspirations.

I specialise in the recruitment and placement of Executive, Sales & Marketing professionals with large corporates as well as Australian and Global Software & Hardware Vendors & Major ICT Providers.
