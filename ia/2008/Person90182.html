Jessica Monokian’s Summary

I currently work in Human Resources for AppLabs, Inc.; an industry leader in global outsourced testing solutions specializing in quality management, testing, and certification solutions.

My main role is in-house recruitment to build the AppLabs team. It is my mission to recruit and attain the very best. I also do a lot of event planning. I coordinate most of our local events and have assisted with global corporate events as well. I matured as a professional in this position and it allowed me to grow into the role I am currently in.
