Alan Handley’s Summary

EXECUTIVE-LEVEL MANAGEMENT: Finance, Accounting, Organizational Turnarounds
Seasoned financial expert, planner, and leader with nearly 15 years of experience impacting enterprise bottom line through skillful turnaround and planning of financial/operational structures for organizations valued at over $150M.
