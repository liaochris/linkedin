Samuel Li, M.Eng, MBA, P.Eng, AScT’s Summary

Structural & Façade Engineer of commercial, residential, industrial, institution and public facilities buildings with experience in preliminary and detailed structural building frame and building envelop design and analyses, performance testing, structural failure investigation, business and project planning and scheduling. Proven strengths in leadership, initiatives, communication and liaison, planning and scheduling. Excellent in technical presentation and analytical skills. Good knowledge of seismic design of buildings and structural dynamics. Proven 7 yr experience in structural and façade consultancy in Hong Kong and Mainland China and 4 yr Canadian experience in structural consulting, fabrication and manufacturing, and literature and experimental research.

Goals: To advance my career to a position of Executive Director with a growing, reputable consulting firm where new design approach, high team performance and cross-functional interaction are highly focused.
