Craig Tadlock’s Summary

A highly motivated and effective technology leader and visionary with 10 years of experience helping companies succeed by growing and building quality software products and teams. In depth experience in a range of disciplines including software architecture, team leadership and management, business development, project management and operations in a variety of company sizes and structures, ranging from start-up to Fortune 500. Industry experience includes traditional and online advertising, media, entertainment, ecommerce and fulfillment systems, mobile and online services. My focus is on delivering high-quality products quickly to market which provide significant revenue and competitive advantage to the business while building efficient and agile teams and processes while operating in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Accomplishments include:

-Led the architecture team responsible for transforming the advertising start-up Spot Runner from a beta class product to a feature-rich, cutting-edge product with enterprise class architecture, technology and engineering processes.

-Helped start and grow a technology consulting and incubating company with 75+ consultants covering the entire product lifecycle in a range of industries with clients across the country.

-Extensive experience in transforming technology start-ups to successful companies.

-Acted as a lead architect at several companies; transforming business vision into products with measurable results.

-Participated in a core team to analyze and subsequently acquire and on-board a related technology company.

-Author of several blogs, videos and white papers focusing on industry topics, technology and engineering processes.

-Authored and contributed to several innovative patents in a range of industries and products.

-Cultivated and maintain a network of top-performing candidates and partner relationships.
