Vicki Lederman’s Summary

In today's extremely challenging economic times, to gain and keep market share it is critical to be in front of the competition with innovative new strategies that can drive consumer demand. From fresh, exciting new products that will sell at affordable prices, to how we partner with retailers to build business, now more than ever we have to be proactive, creative, adaptable, and practical.

I'm a passionate Design/Product Development professional in the home furnishings, tabletop and housewares field seeking a new position in management/strategy. I believe great value and innovative design, not lowest price, offer the most profitable road to increased sales. To increase value and profit margins, I focus on spotting hidden product opportunities and unmet market needs, improving quality and design, and seeking ways to lower costs. I have experience strategizing and managing the design and product development process from trend analysis through costed collections, and have worked with buyers from the major retail stores, as well as mass merchants.

I am particularly ardent about integrating environmental and social responsibility issues into new product development where possible and appropriate. Consumer interest in these concerns is growing steadily and I believe they will become a major force in the coming years. Such proactive strategies provide opportunities to offer exciting, innovative products at the right price.
