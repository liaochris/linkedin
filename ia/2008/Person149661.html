Peter Bennett’s Summary

Peter is an IT management and security professional with 14 years experience in the industry and over 10 years as a manager. He has strong technical and project management skills. He is a qualified professional in physical and logical information security (CISSP). He has 7 years experience managing service delivery, security and infrastructure for an 8000+ user online financial system.

In 2001 he was a pioneer of the ASP (SaaS) model for delivering business to business financial services over the Internet, designing and managing all aspects of security and Internet delivery. The ASP business launched in 33 countries. Worked closely with the application development team to align software products with online requirements

Peter has negotiated security agreements with banks and corporations around the world and has in-depth understanding of how to build trusted channels between organizations.

He has introduced formal ISMS (Information Security Management Systems) into companies, following ISO 27001 and 27002 standards and guidelines.

He has written and podcasted on security issues for the UK Money Transmitters Association and others.

Strongly committed to raising safety standards in the community and an enthusiastic member of ISSA and ISC(2).

CISSP accredited.
