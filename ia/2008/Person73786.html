Guy Madgwick’s Summary

An experienced executive who has held senior positions across a range of energy fields. Has a proven track record in finding and delivering complex deals both through balance sheet financing and project finance. Delivered power deals in Germany, UK, Ireland and Eastern Europe.

Managed large scale organisations and has been responsible for departments of 50+ professionals. Presently leading an innovative development organisation that is entrapreneurial in outlook and has bought together a strong team of experienced developers,construction managers and financial experts. Guy is experienced in organisational restructure / change management and implementation and delivering demanding business plans.

Guy Madgwick’s Specialties:

Business development, project development, political representation, key note speaker on Energy matters
