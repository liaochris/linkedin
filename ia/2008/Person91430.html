James (Jim) Kamenelis’s Summary

Highly effective executive with an excellent record of transforming IT organizations and delivering results in alignment with Business goals. A competent strategist who establishes comprehensive plans, implements structure, and drives organizations to achieve objectives. Over 12 years of executive experience.

Co-founded PanGenesis in 2006, establishing a competitive Information Technology (IT) and Software services company in the IT Outsourcing industry. A U.S. company with operations in Costa Rica, PanGenesis offers U.S. Companies IT and Software services that deliver world class quality with the unmatched convenience of “NearSourcing”.

Previously held executive IT positions at Xerox or Harris Corp. for over 12 years. Most recently, the I.T. Director position at Xerox PARC, the internationally renowned research center in Palo Alto, California.

Fulfilled numerous IT management roles and worked in each of Harris’ major business units from Computer Systems, to Semiconductors, to Government and Communication Systems. Contributed to the business success by leveraging Information Technology for optimal benefit to business operations.
