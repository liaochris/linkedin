Patrick Feeleus’s Summary

After graduating as a Bachelor of Science (Mechanical Engineering) in 1994
I initially joined the IHC / SBM group. Within the group I held various positions including onshore / offshore site engineer, trouble-shooter, project engineer, site supervisor, and offshore / construction / R&D project manager. Within my last role with SBM, I was responsible for the (daily) management of the SBM’s Inspection, Maintenance and Overhaul / Upgrade Department.

After nearly 12years of service within the SBM group, I returned back to the Netherlands (May 2006) to join Noordhoek Offshore as a Commercial Manager. Within this role I’m commercially responsible for all Noordhoek’s Offshore activities as well for the marketing of Noordhoek’s Diving / Construction Support Vessels.

+ Offshore Contracting (Construction and Installation, Air, mixed gas, TUP and SAT Diving, IRM, Repair, Geotechnical and Geophysical Survey).
+ Business Development, Commercial Management, Tender / Proposal Management, Negotiations.
