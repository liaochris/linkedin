Ken Olling’s Summary

I am an accomplished graphic designer with 16 years experience. Born in the US I'm now living in Oslo Norway. I have been working in Europe for the past five years. Projects I have done include Digital branding for Nike football for the 2002 world cup in Aisa, rebranding Audi worldwide; this included websites, cd-roms and design guides for the Audi in each county they are represented. Rebranding Sony communications (So-Net) in Japan and building the modular housing brand Løvetann.

I have lived and worked in Los Angeles, Tokyo, Hamburg and Oslo. I'm a native speak English speaker and have business Japanese competence.

I am currently working as a freelance business developer between Asia and northern Europe as well as creating a retail product line.

Ken Olling’s Specialties:

Graphic design, international branding, product development, digital strategy and online development, creative direction, broadcast design and typography.
