Ravi Pandey’s Summary

As Sr Vice President and UK Head of NIIT Technologies Ltd, I am responsible for the Software Services and BPO business in UK. I report to the MD and work out of our offices in High Wycombe.

With over 16 years’ of experience in IT Consultancy and Technology Services businesses, I have been involved with nearly all the products and services that the industry has to offer. Have worked with some of the largest industrial houses of India including Tata and ITC. Have extensive prior experience of working with a Tier 1 global service provider, while at HCL Technologies, and have worked and lived around the world.

Have a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communications from National Institute of Technology, Warangal and a MBA from Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore. I have been the recipient of Principal's Gold Medal in Engineering and a Director's award in MBA.

For leisure, I enjoy a social round of golf, P G Wodehouse books and Asterix comics and cookery shows. I also have a passion for cricket. Am married with 2 young daughters and live in Berkshire
