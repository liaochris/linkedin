Nir Kahn’s Summary

Vehicle designer specialising in niche vehicles.
Past credits include the M1114GR and M1118GR armoured Humvees, the Navistar MXT-MVA and Navistar MaxxPro MRAP, Oshkosh Sand Cat and the Erez Raider rally prototype (the 2006 4x2 Israeli Rally Raid champion).
Vehicles currently in development include the Lockheed Martin AVA.

Nir Kahn’s Specialties:

Capable of arriving at intelligent creative solutions that combine technical integrity, ergonomics and aesthetic value.
Concept generation through to prototype construction and productionising.
Rendering, model-making, Photoshop.
