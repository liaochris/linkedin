Durga P. Mikkilineni’s Summary

Durga P. Mikkilineni is a successful entrepreneur, co-founder and partner of Global Nest LLC., a NJ based minority certified IT consulting and Managed Services firm. Durga co-founded Anthem Technologies and sold after 7 years of operations in 2005.Durga has over 15 years of Software Industry experience in managing, delivering offshore and on-shore projects. Durga has been a manager for sales, pre-sales, delivery and overall company strategy that accomplished more than $100 million in combined revenues.. Prior to Anthem Durga worked as an Assistant Vice President at Merrill Lynch, NY for 3 years managing and supporting Merrill's mission critical HR and Financial databases. He has also worked at AT&T for 4 years as a Senior Technical Staff Member in NJ. Durga received his MS in Computer Science from NJIT and BS in Electrical Engineering from India. Durga raised funds to support the families of Andhra Pradesh farmers who committed suicide, actively participates in various non-profit organizations and donates generously to various charity initiatives.
