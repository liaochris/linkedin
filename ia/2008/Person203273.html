W. Ross Wells’s Summary

Creative director and co-owner of Zenfilm, an innovative motion picture production and post firm that has been in business for many years.16+ years as a Film Director specializing in comedy, visual storytelling and music video. I have an extensive reel and and a tremendous amount of on-set experience both in the US and abroad.I want to expand my client base into more national/international clientele. Viral media, documentary film, pre and post roll work are all covered by our range of services.

Zenfilm has won numerous awards for our advertising, music video and documentary work. Occasionally we combine two or more disciplines to create moving human interest spots with an unusual edge.

Zenfilm has recently opened new facilities in Downtown Houston. Joining together two large spaces in the Dakota, a converted 1911 paint factory, the new Zenfilm offers more services, more uncompressed HD post capability and more staff to meet the expanded needs of our ever growing clientele.
