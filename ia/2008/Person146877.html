Patrick Fee’s Summary

I am an “out of the box” problem solver who cares about the growth and development of his employees as well as the success of his customers.

I have sixteen years of experience spanning all elements of software and web & database development, including design, testing, maintenance, upgrading, implementation, and documentation. I am proficient in all languages and environments related to web development. As the recipient of two Chairman's Awards for Innovation, I have become one of my company's leaders in the area of IT design. I have developed leading edge web sites and applications, allowing users to perform extensive real-time and online document and data management functions within the application(s) and individual web page development without Webmaster intervention.

My most recent experience involves managing up to 40 IT professionals in a matrixed group spread over three states and four locations. I hire and develop IT professionals with a plethora of different skills and backgrounds, and help them to reach their potential and meet all of their project's needs.

This requires many innovative and creative ways to communicate to a wide variety of employees, management, external customers and even community members. To that end, I have facilitated numerous initiatives for improving communication with local and remote team members including video telcons, new email tools, tying direct-billable travel with overhead visits, podcasts, and online tools to share data. Communication is the key to being an effective group manager.

The skills I have developed through my avocations have also made me a better manager. As an actor and technician in local community theatre, I have gained confidence and improved my communication and team-building abilities. I am also a committed, successful fundraiser for several charities, most notably the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation. Through these efforts, I have further developed my marketing skills and creativity.
