Pete Stiglich’s Summary

Mr. Stiglich is a senior level Data Architect with over 10 years of experience in enterprise level Data Modeling, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, ETL, and Meta Data Management, and over 20 years of IT experience in general. His Data Modeling and Data Warehousing experience spans applications such as Medical, CRM, Retail, Supply Chain Management, Judicial, and Telco. He has extensive experience in Data Warehouse architecture, project management, ETL and OLAP and has excellent communication, presentation, and training skills.
