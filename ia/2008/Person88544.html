Jennifer Kenny’s Summary

Technology and consulting exec. with 20+ years consulting experience in IT, Technology (HP, NEC, Novell, IBM), Telecommunications and Networking (British Telecom, UB Networks, Newbridge Networks, Net.com), Consumer Marketing (Clorox, Del Monte Foods) and Commercial Financial Services (Wells Fargo, Union Bank of California, Bank of America, Capital One, National Westminster Bank).

I have focused on Systems Strategy and Implementation (ERP, BPM, collaboration), New Product Introduction, Corporate Transformation and Operational Excellence/Process Redesign.

I have worked for 2 of the Big 5 (Booz.Allen & Hamilton and Accenture) and started my own profitable ($0-$3M in 6 months) companies where I have led Business Development, Sales and Delivery.

I have 15+ years of Vice President and C-level engagement with multi national global 1,000 companies. I am passionate about integrating people, process and technology to deliver measurable business results. I have a track record of producing satisfaction for clients.
