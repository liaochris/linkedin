Chuck Papandrea’s Summary

Chuck has a passion for teaching leadership principles to individuals who want to make a true difference in the world... identifying, equipping and empowering others to achieve uncommon results!

Together with a community of like-minded leaders, Chuck's purpose is clear. Identify and support leaders capable of developing other leaders - leaders who will inspire average people to accomplish extraordinary results. These results include driving business performance, reducing and eliminating debt, growing a culture of learning and personal growth, developing better relationships, and creating environments that will encourage individuals to "understand why they believe what they believe."
