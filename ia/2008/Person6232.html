Alex Godelman’s Summary

Executive level technology & engineering professional with 20 plus years of proven global leadership experience and solid track record of success and accomplishments in designing, developing and delivering Engineering and Information Technology solutions, possessing a unique combination of hands-on technical knowledge, leadership and business acumen.

A strong record of success creating modern and robust technology architectures, turning under-performing engineering and IT departments into high performance technology organizations. Proven ability bringing technology benefits to better high tech product lines and their revenue streams as well as to solving technology or business problems via technology while managing costs and mitigating risks.

• Diverse international experience with Fortune 50 - 200 companies, start-ups and turn-around situations
• Consistently was recognized as effective leader and Top 1 % performer enterprise wide, received several formal promotions and increases in responsibilities.
• Program manager for complex, large-scale, global, cross-functional software solutions across full life cycle including in-house custom and packaged implementations or engineering. “Go to” person for troubled organizational initiatives and projects and/or turnaround situations.
• Managed dispersed teams of 35 to staff of almost 150, and budgets up to $ 34 M
