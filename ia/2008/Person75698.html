Heather Abramson Krasna’s Summary

Nine years in college internship coordination and career counseling. Provisionally certified Dale Carnegie trainer. Expertise includes employer relations, large-scale event planning and management, career coaching, workshop design, outcomes measurement, resume writing and interview preparation. Creator of "Crafting an Excellent Internship Program" workshop for employers and co-planner of Baruch College's Wall Street Careers Program for students interested in investment banking careers. Additional experience in grantwriting, nonprofit management, organizational development, and as a musician.

Heather Abramson Krasna’s Specialties:

Career development, internships, music industry, early career development, careers for creative people, investment banking/financial services industry, nonprofit management, organizational development, resume writing, job search skills training, employer and client relations, college recruiting.
