Patrick Schwerdtfeger’s Summary

Patrick Schwerdtfeger is a fully licensed Mortgage Banker in Northern California (DRE #01424896) and originates purchase and refinance mortgage loans through Windsor Capital Mortgage Corporation (DRE #01050210), one of the largest brokerage firms in the country. Patrick provides the 'Beyond the Rate' information series as a service to prospective clients who wish to educate themselves on mortgages and the mortgage industry.

Patrick also holds regular seminars on pricing, underwriting and the untold truths of real estate finance. He has authored two industry handbooks: "Strategies for Seller Financing" and "Loans & Lenders; a Guide for Buyers & Borrowers". He has also been a repeat guest on "The Real Estate Connection" radio program on 960 AM "The Quake".

Patrick Schwerdtfeger’s Specialties:

The "Beyond the Rate" podcast series has been downloaded more than 17,000 times in 27 different countries, making it one of the most popular mortgage-related podcasts on iTunes. The series educates subscribers on the mortgage business. Listeners are given a detailed account of how the mortgage industry operates as well as tips on how to navigate through all the confusing loan programs and deceptive marketing techniques being used by some of today's Mortgage Brokers.
