Matt Nelson’s Summary

I have over 8 years of professional experience as a marketer and graphic designer. What makes my skill set particularly unique is that I have a BA in Graphic Design with a MS in Direct and Interactive Marketing. This has given me a sharp eye for what works best in a variety of communication mediums from a design prospective, and a keen marketing mind which allows me to analyze and measure those communications to make sound marketing recommendations based on the performance results of those direct and interactive communications.

One of my greatest strengths is my ability to collaborate, maximizing opportunities for the team and the organization. I have lots of experience working directly with clients and developing solutions that are customized to the unique needs and budget of the customer. I am well versed in both PC and Macintosh environments, my program specific expertise is in: Photoshop CS3, Fireworks CS3, Dreamweaver CS3, Illustrator CS3, InDesign CS3, Flash CS3, Word, Powerpoint, and Excel. I am also an expert HTML and CSS programmer as well as competent when it comes to reading ASP and PHP code as well as understanding how, where and why it can best be used in web site applications.

  • Graphic & Web Site Manager

    Mount Sunapee Resort

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Hospitality industry)

    June 2006 — June 2007 (1 year 1 month)

The database was also created to give me the ability to easily run exports of new email addresses to continue to grow our email universe. During my time with Mount Sunapee I increased their email list universe from 1,000 members to 15,000 members in a four-month period.
