Will Hunt’s Summary

As a financial professional in 2005, I became increasingly interested in the internet and Web2.0 companies. As I had always been involved in volunteering projects and fundraisers, I was able to (along with my business partner) come up with the idea for CharityFusion.com. We saw the start up process from scratch, with a few bumps and bruises along the way. Never had any experience during that time that I didnt learn something from. We launched our website in Nov. 07. We are in the process of gathering users right now. We are in talks with several large charities for partnerships and would love to meet more of them.

A year or so ago, I invested in a DVD rental kiosk from a company called VendVision. I kept in touch with the owner of the company and spoke with him about my work with CharityFusion.com. He asked me if I could run his development department, which he had just fused with his marketing department. It seemed like something that I could take on part time when i had time away from my true passion, CharityFusion.com
