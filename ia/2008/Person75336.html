Kirsty O'Hara’s Summary

I co-founded hateshopping.co.nz in December 2005, to develop and become the sole shareholder.

Hateshopping is a premium personal shopping service for men; that brings a variety of clothing and gifts to your home or work for you to try and select from. Check out our website if you like, www.hateshopping.co.nz. Currently we are based in Auckland - but are looking at growth to both Wellington and Australia. Obviously we are walking before we run.

I also own a patent for a towelling idea that I am looking to take to market on an international level.
This is something for both the hotel and retail markets.

As per both current adventures above - my advertising and communications background comes in extremely helpful – in positioning and promoting ideas; and most importantly in understanding what makes consumers tick.

My aim is to surround myself with great mentors and friends that I admire, trust and respect - to have good times and learn as much as I can to help make my goals a reality.

A favourite quotes at the moment is: 'If it is meant to be, it is up to me'
