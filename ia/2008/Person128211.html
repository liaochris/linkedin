Michael Elkins’s Summary

Michael Elkins is the President and Founder of Kestral Group. He has over 17 years of experience in planning and deploying information management solutions, working with companies both large and small around the world. Utilizing real-world experience, Kestral Group helps Energy, Public Sector and Financial Services clients to deploy successful enterprise information management solutions including ECM, Compliance, Collaboration and Search. We help companies realize the value of their information assets by accelerating the deployment of new and existing solutions and driving broader business value through more effective use of information throughout the organization.
Services include:
• Planning and Strategy
• Requirements Definition
• Solution Evaluation and Selection
• Content Inventories Development
• Information Mapping and Taxonomy Development
• Records Management Programs
• Business Process Mapping
• Enterprise Search Solutions
• Knowledge-Based Solutions
• Information Governance Programs

Michael Elkins’s Specialties:

Enterprise Content Management (ECM), Collaboration, Document Imaging, Business Process Management (BPM), Records Information Management (RIM), Taxonomy Development, and Enterprise Search
