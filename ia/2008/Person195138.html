Varun Jain’s Summary

Varun started off his career as an Entrepreneur in the BPO and IT space. Since selling off those companies, he has been involved in his Family Enterprise which is involved in the business of Real Estate Development.

Varun enjoys many things such as Real Estate, Finance, the feeling of being an entrepreneur (not just being a business owner), working on large deals and seeing them mature and doing loads of quantitative work.

Varun is working towards acquiring further knowledge of Finance and specifically Investments by pursuig his MBA (Applied Security Analysis Specialisation) and working towards his CFA charter. He has already passed CFA Level 1 exam.

Varun's short/long term objective is to be in the business of Investment Management.
