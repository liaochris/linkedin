Chris Obdam’s Summary

In 1998 I started my first company together with my brother Tim. Later on it developed into my current company Internetbureau Holder. I am a true Apple Fanatic, persuave lots of people to switch from the dark side of the force.

The same I do for the OpenID community, we have released the first Dutch OpenID server and have been promoting it since then.

All our applications/concepts our developed in Ruby in Rails. In 2007 I co-organized the Dutch RubyAndRails conference which was a huge success.

One of the latest projects I worked on i http://nakko.com
