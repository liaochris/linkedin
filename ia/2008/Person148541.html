Pawel Marzec’s Summary

My main area of interest is project management in form of understanding and leading people. For over 5 years I have been managing IT projects with use of Scrum and elements of other agile approaches. I know how to organise work of a small IT department, which technologies and tools could be used and how to motivate people to achieve predetermined goals.
