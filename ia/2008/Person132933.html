    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Wholesale industry)

    1999 — 2002 (3 years)

    The Aalsmeer Flower Auction (VBA) is the centre for international production and trade in floricultural products. Each day, on average 50 thousand transactions are concluded with exporters and wholesalers who export the flowers and plants worldwide.
