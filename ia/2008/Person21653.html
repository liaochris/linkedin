Robert S. Bennett’s Summary

Attorney Robert S. "Bob" Bennett is certified as a specialist in consumer law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization. There are fewer than 30 Texas attorneys presently certified in consumer law. Such a distinction marks an attorney as being exceptionally qualified in both expertise and experience in a particular field. Nationally, no other state in the United States has this type of certification.

It means, specifically, that Mr. Bennett, who has been practicing law for over 25 years, has counseled and represented clients in numerous consumer claims; he has regularly attended consumer law seminars to keep his legal training up to date. In addition, Mr. Bennett had to be evaluated by fellow lawyers and judges and pass a day-long written examination by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization.

Mr. Bennett is a partner with the Bennett Law Firm, L.L.P. The attorneys in this new law firm are especially proficient in cases involving consumer law, product liability, employment discrimination, personal injury, toxic torts, DTPA violations, ethics defense,... and white-collar criminal defense.

The Firm's website, www.bennettlawfirm.com has an extensive listing of professional articles that may help with your specific problem or concern.

Robert S. Bennett’s Interests:

Bob is involved in the community having served as a volunteer with Amigos de las Americas in Guatemala and on the Amigos Board of Directors. He also served on the Board of Directors of the University of Houston Law Center Alumni Association, and as the President of the Association 1992-1993. He has served as the president of his civic association. He coached little league baseball and youth hockey.
