Joe Whyte’s Summary

I have done work for fortune 1000 companies and have been working in the SEO and Search Engine Marketing industry for over 5 years. I gained a lot of my local search marketing and optimization experience from Advanced Access Real Estate Web Hosting company and further developed my link development skills while I was working for PRIMEDIA along with learning how to market and optimize sites for the most difficult keywords on the web. After starting my own company I opened up a slew of services via my own search optimization firm.

I have competed and been successful for ranking for terms such as "web hosting", New cars, used cars for sale, DVDs, sex toys, skin care, home theater systems etc.

I have been instrumental in helping high level companies improve their PPC campaigns, lower their ppc budgets and increase conversion and CTR. I have consulted to help build quality guidelines and infrastructure protocols to help search firms succeed.

I have recently started blogging at Joe-Whyte.com about some of my favorite interests in Search and have been lucky enough to meet many other leading SEO bloggers.
