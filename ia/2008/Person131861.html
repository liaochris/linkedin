Mike Bawden’s Summary

As a marketing and public relations professional with over 25 years' experience, I've created an innovative consultancy that helps clients clarify their marketing and communications objectives and plans; and then finds and manages the best outside service providers to do the job. We help clients with everything from strategic planning to service provider search and selection.

Brand Central Station's sister operation, B&LPR, is a full-service public relations agency that is often called upon to assist with message creation and management in support of the client's brand. My partner in that business, Liz Lareau, and I have worked together for over ten years.

Mike Bawden’s Specialties:

My personal area of expertise is strategic and creative thought. I oversee the research and development of impactful communications messages and then author the business, marketing and communications plans that drive those messages to a successful conclusion both inside and outside the enterprise.
