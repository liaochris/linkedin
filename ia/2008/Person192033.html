Tosha Lewis’s Summary

I attended Texas State University and received a Bachelor of Science in Psychology with a minor in mathematics in 2005. I was a member of the Psi Chi National Honors Society, Rho Lambda Recognition Society, and the Executive Leadership chair for Alpha Delta Pi. I also won an award for Academic Excellence in Liberal Arts, and the Psi Chi Regional Research award in 2005. I presented my paper at the Rocky Mountain Psychology Association convention . After undergrad, I decided to pursue my dreams of graduate school, and what better place to go than to the West coast!! I interviewed with Alliant International University (formerly California School of Professional Psychology) and fell in love with San Diego. I became a Ph.D. student in Industrial/Organizational Psycholoy in the Fall of 2005 and was also offered a small (but at least it was something!) GPA graduate scholarship. I quickly got invovled with many professional associations such as SIOP, SHRM, SDIOP, IOSA. I was the vice president of IOSA from 2006-2007. In addition, our paper was selected for presentation at the International Academy of Business conference titled, "Managerial Decision Making: Does it Depend on Sources of Guidance." I graduated with my masters in May of 2007. I took a summer internship in June 2007 at Amylin Pharmaceuticals in the Organizational Development deaprtment. I was then offered a part time position in Leadership development, in order to assist with the large leadership development initiatve. It has been an amazing experience.

Future Goals: Well, my short term goal is to gain experience as an external consultant. Preferably with a large firm. Way down the road, I hope to have my own practice.

Personal: I enjoy spending time with my family,friends, boyfriend, and my beautiful boxer Kyla. I love to be outside and to travel. I plan to hopefully get to be in the same city as my boyfriend sometime soon ;). Future personal goals would be to have a wonderful family of my own!
