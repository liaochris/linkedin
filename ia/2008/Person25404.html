Amy Applebaum’s Summary

THE COMPANY

Bootcamp for Your Mind® is a company committed to helping people, all over the world, achieve an amazing life.

It is a place to make your goals a reality. If you say you want more money, you’re going to create it. If you say you want to improve a relationship, you’ll start improving it immediately.

Bootcamp for Your Mind is not about talking, it’s about doing. No excuses, no blaming - just achieving!

Currently, Bootcamp for Your Mind offers Programs for individuals as well as Groups. Additional products, including books and Tele-Bootcamps will be released in 2008. I am currently in development of a reality show based on my process.

My immediate goal is to spread the word about Group Bootcamp! A 12 Week Coaching Program that will change your life! For more information visit www.bootcampforyourmind.com.
