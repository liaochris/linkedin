David Collier’s Summary

In his role within the Strategic Business Solutions group at Digitas, David leads a team of internal management consultants who focus on identifying and overcoming enterprise business challenges and through the strategic application of technology and business process.

Over the course of his career, Mr. Collier had served in numerous roles relating to management consulting, relationship management, business/systems analysis and project management in the professional services, real estate investments and healthcare industries. During this time, he has designed, managed & advised on a variety of enterprise scale projects including: portal and workgroup collaboration technologies, employee identity and lifecycle management programs, professional services automation tools, applicant tracking and recruiting systems and numerous business process improvement initiatives. David sits on the Digitas IT Senior Leadership Team and is a key resource in the IT Project and Portfolio Management process at Digitas.

David holds a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from Bentley College, an M.B.A. from The Sawyer School of Management at Suffolk University and a Professional Certificate in Business Analysis from the George Washington University. He is a member in good standing of the Project Management Institute of America (PMI) and is an active member of the Bentley College and Suffolk University Alumni Associations.
