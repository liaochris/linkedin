Gilles Bénichou’s Summary

Impassioned by programming since ever, I naturally chose that field of study at the University, having already realized many personal projects. Thus, I graduated to a Master in Artificial Intelligence and I'm specialized in object oriented programming and Web sites analysis and development.

My design process is very serious. After a strong study of the specifications, the team and I create the requested system, building its documentation at the same time. The systems I build are always robust, efficient and easy to evolve.

I proved reliable in many companies and have letters of introduction and references allowing me to back up my know-how and my professionalism. To me, nothing means more than team spirit.

My knowledge in Java / J2SE / J2EE techonology directly matches my field of studies and the very major part of the time invested in my professional experience.

Today, this background of more than 11 years allows me to involve actively as Chief Technology Officer and high level manager, to intercede in corporate strategies regarding the technologies. Indeed, my leadership and my experience back me up to insure this role with great professionalism and dynamism.
