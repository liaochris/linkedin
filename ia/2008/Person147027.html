Patrick Preston’s Summary

My goal is to assist companies to develop their Business Process Management Strategy. By facilitating or completing the strategy definition, documentation, and tactical implementation plans supporting these strategies for companies, we support their ability to leverage the most correct framework for Business Process Reengineering, Six Sigma, Lean, etc, programs to a successful completion.

It is my intent and it supports my goals to leverage what works over the academia and "book words" to find the heuristics behind BPM. Defining the correct "what", building a "how" that can be implemented, and then completing this process and delivering is my goal.
