Eric van Mullekom’s Summary

Senior project manager at Yourzine, responsible for project management of innovative and customer-driver projects, and management of the technical department.

Yourzine is a major E-mail Service Provider in The Netherlands, and is member of the LECTRIC Group, a Dutch full service internet agency (see http://www.lectricgroup.nl).

Eric is project manager since 1997, and is responsible for many on-line and off-line publications realised for European-based companies, international and governmental institutions, such as Dutch & German Yellow Pages, Wolters Kluwer Belgium, European Union, United Nations, Dutch Chambers of Commerce, Van Dale Lexicografie, and SDU.

Eric van Mullekom’s Specialties:

Process improvement & control, functional design, database design, performance & quality optimisation. Focus on quality, usability, and maintainability.
