Lee Tsao’s Summary

Speaker and Lecturer Biography.

Lee Tsao, Managing Partner at Business Technology Services (http://www.biztech.com)

King of Prussia, PA (January 3, 2008) – Lee Tsao joins BizTech as Partner of Sales and Delivery. As Partner of BizTech, Mr. Tsao will be working with the other members of the senior management team to lead and expand the company’s sales and delivery operations. Lee has over 18 years of experience in Oracle focused business and technology solutions, and “Big Four” Management Consulting Services.

Lee has held worldwide sales, consulting, and delivery positions at Boeing Company, General Electric, Coopers & Lybrand MCS (IBM), Oracle Corporation, Portal Software, and several Silicon Valley based Venture Capital funded companies to provide software and technology solutions for emerging, high growth business initiatives to Fortune 500 companies.

Lee is certified as Principal Oracle technologist, and certified as instructor from IBI. Lee received his B.S. Commerce & Engineering degree from Drexel University in Pennsylvania.

About BizTech
BizTech is a regional IT services firm focusing on applied technology solutions. BizTech's no-nonsense approach is derived from the cumulative experiences of the founders and senior staff, all of whom share a common belief in the simple principle that all technology initiatives must provide business value.

BizTech is committed to providing substantive business value on each and every client engagement. We do this through a combination of industry-specific business expertise, technical skills, proven project management methods and traditional values. We strive to work in partnership with our customers to build high-performance teams and create business solutions that will last.

BizTech has proven qualifications in the areas of IT Planning, Web-Centric Software Solutions, and IT Infrastructure - with emphasis on the leading products and technologies that work in an enterprise arena.
