Nico Dingemans’s Summary

Nico is a graduate from CHN (now Stenden) University, with a Bachelor's Degree in Hotel Management. Starting as management trainee with Sheraton in Indonesia, Nico gained over 10 years of international hospitality experience on 4 continents and has also worked & trained with Hilton and Moevenpick, as well as independent hotels and resorts in Belgium, the USA, Egypt and the Netherlands.

Based centrally in the Netherlands in Amersfoort, Nico represents 2 companies; HES Global and Hospitality Graduate Recruitment (HGR). Nico is responsible for running all aspects of the Benelux satellite office. In addition he travels periodically to the Middle East (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Qatar) for business development purposes and to maintain the personal relationship with GCC key accounts.
