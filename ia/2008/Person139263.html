    The Social Media Philosophy Project

    (Internet industry)

    July 2008 — Present (2 months)

    The Social Media Philosophy Project is a collective effort by members of the social media community dedicated to covering every aspect of social media and internet technologies from an insider's point of view. The ongoing goal is to consider the deep changes taking place within our world and attempt to address the philosophical questions raised by social media. The long term goal is to give birth to a new branch of philosophy studying social media and networking technologies and galvanizing the culture which has arisen from them.
