Moved into broking futures and traded options after my stint as a broker worked in information services selling technical analysis. Sold systems with data feeds for price and news along side research to buy and sell side institutions. In my time I have set up a couple of agency deals to resell other companies products and services such as Knight Ridder Financial and Action Economics. Set up my own research company to service both buy side and sell side financial institutions.
