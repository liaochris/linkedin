Vincent Carrella’s Summary

Award-winning creative director, writer, producer and interactive designer with more than fifteen years hands-on experience creating and producing influential and ground-breaking CD-ROM and casual games games, 3D and 2D web animation, digital entertainment and multimedia, with a unique background in interactive entertainment design and digital development. Long-term experience runs the gamut from creative direction and production management to writing and directing. Co-founded two digital studios; functioned as Creative Lead on several award-winning CD-ROMs and as a Creative Director, Project Leader and Lead Designer for 3D interactive games and episodic web mini-shows, Flash games and episodic web serials.


CAREER HIGHLIGHTS
Wrote and co-designed the in-game movies and cut-sequences for 3DO's action-adventure The Four Horsemen and the in-game dialog for 3DO's Portal Runner and Sarge's War. Developed and designed episodic properties for Warner Brothers' Entertaindom.com, Jay Leno and The Tonight Show, Saturday Night Live and NBC.com, Pop.com, Backslap.com, Stan Lee, DarkHorse Comics and Excite@Home. Designed, produced and wrote award-winning consumer games including the ground-breaking and top-selling CD-ROM Iron Helix, the critically acclaimed Bad Mojo and the award-winning Space Bunnies Must Die!. Games alone have sold over one-million units worldwide and have been translated into seven different languages. Appeared as a guest panelist and speaker nationwide, in Japan and England.

Vincent Carrella’s Specialties:

Business Development, Licensing, Publishing, Developer relations, game concept development, game scripts and game design ideas, stories, characters and plots. Creative writing for games, advertising, marketing, film, television and radio.
