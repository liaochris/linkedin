Brian Mills’s Summary

Having a passion for developing results-driven online strategy and web content, Brian Mills leverages a decade of electronic publishing experience in the finance, travel, publishing, retail, and nonprofit sectors. Specializing in the successful formulation and management of sustainable online communication initiatives for Fortune 100 companies – including Citigroup, The Home Depot, Merrill Lynch, and The Coca-Cola Company – Brian is chiefly interested in the synthesis of relevance, findability, signification, and taxonomy as methods for establishing meaningful, interactive dialogues. He holds a BA in American Studies & Journalism from Brandeis University and graphic design certification.
