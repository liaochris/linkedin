Baden Sharp’s Summary

10 years engineering in the IT sector through out the US, South East Asia and New Zealand.

3 years in the recruiting industry in Australia, and 1 year in Japan, specializing in the high tech market.

In addition to the challenges and rewards of recruitment, I really enjoy meeting such interesting, ambitious and impressive leaders in one of the world’s most technology driven cities. I also like being involved in the growth and development of more than just my own company. Talent Inc has provided an ideal platform for me to develop what sometimes feels like my own business, through a team-oriented culture, as well as autonomy and independence.

I believe that the key qualities for success in this business are resilience, tenacity, drive and not just motivation, but the ability to motivate one-self over and over again, despite inevitable set-backs. I’m very proud to be a part of Talent Inc team, development and success during my time here. In addition to our successes, I’m also very proud of our main assets, our people － an impressive, multicultural, driven and fun bunch of people!
