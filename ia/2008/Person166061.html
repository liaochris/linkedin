Russ Thornton’s Summary

Russ, the founder, President, and Chief Compliance Officer of Thornton Wealth Management, LLC, graduated from Furman University in 1993 with a degree in Business and Computer Science. Prior to starting TWM, Russ worked as a Financial Consultant with Merrill Lynch for over 12 years. In addition to 15 years of financial service experience, Russ holds the following professional industry designations:

* Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor
* Accredited Wealth Management Advisor
* Accredited Asset Management Specialist
* Accredited Investment Fiduciary (AIF®)
* NAPFA-Registered Financial Advisor

Outside of TWM, Russ serves as a trustee for the non-profit Georgia Eye Bank. In his spare time, Russ enjoys spending time with his wife Elizabeth and their two dogs.

Russ Thornton’s Specialties:

Independent Fiduciary, Dimensional Funds Advisor, Fee-Only Wealth Management, Institutional Asset-Class Portfolios
