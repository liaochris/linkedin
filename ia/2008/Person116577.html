Lucy Collins’s Summary

I have such a passion and respect for design; I am dedicated to the visual process, from conception to completion; and I'm fun, energetic and very hard-working.

Over the past year, I've been working as an in-house designer for Bumkins Finer Baby Products in Scottsdale, AZ. At this position, I've been lucky enough to truly explore my creative outlets by working there as not only the lead designer, but as an illustrator, web designer, and photographer. As a designer I was responsible for conception and design of all marketing collateral such as catalogs, sales sheets, magazine advertisements, press kits, banners, business cards and displays for trade shows. In addition, I was involved in product design and development from start to finish. I worked as a team to create innovative and original ideas for new products and patterns, created all visual mock-ups and conceptions, and finally, was responsible for ensuring the final product came out as beautifully as we envisioned. I also played a key role in the marketing and SEO of the company, in conjunction with my bi-monthly e-mail news blasts that were sent to wholesale and retail clients.

Throughout my time at Bumkins, multi-tasking and working under tight deadlines seems almost second nature. I thrive in overcoming challenges such as being responsible for a photo shoot, a website update and a print advertisement deadline-- all in the same day! I believe that through my exceptional organizational skills, I can manage anything thrown at me; and I wholeheartedly accept the challenge.

As an artist, I believe sometimes the best ideas can begin with a ball-point pen and the back of a magazine while waiting for the bus. I respect the sketch as much as the beautiful aqueous-coated brochure. I look forward to sharing my thoughts and ideas with a creative team almost as much as I look forward to working with, and being inspired by fellow creatives.

Check out my website www.lucycollinsdesigns.com!
