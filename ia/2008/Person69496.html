Greg Feigenson’s Summary

Heavy open source influence, educated by some of the finest minds in computer science. Software Engineer, Database Administrator and Web Developer with a history of supporting small companies and a desire to work within larger organizations. Particularly interested in supporting internal clients and producing in-house tools, but equally comfortable developing outward facing software components. Large amounts of experience with Linux and Windows systems, various databases and the software life cycle. Not so bad with documentation either.

I like to believe in my product and my co-workers. I like to be good at what I do. I like to take ownership of my own work, discover owners of unidentified work and generally get the job done. I prefer being long term in my vision, and solving things correctly the first time; software craftsmanship and elegance are to be admired.
