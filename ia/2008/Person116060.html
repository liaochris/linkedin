Luis R. Quintero’s Summary

Director Luis R. Quintero has an extensive background from working with Oscar winning Editor Anne V. Coates (Lawrence of Arabia), Oscar nominated Writer / Director Susannah Grant (Erin Brockovich), Oscar nominated Director Marco Martinez-Galarce (The Pissing Rain), & Oscar winning Director Robert Amram (Sentinels of Silence).

His film La Hora Del Te’ (Tea Time) has been selected for several film
festivals, including the Los Angeles International Shorts Film Festival ’06.

Tea Time has also earned him a ’08 Sundance International Filmmaker
Award Nomination.
