Kristin Goedert’s Summary

Senior level graphic designer who has worked in both agency and corporate environments. Over twelve years of experience creating impactful and dynamic collateral, presentations, web sites, and sales tools that drive revenue and reinforce the corporate brand.


-Highly creative, versatile designer who works in multiple mediums including print, web, video, paint, and illustration

-Strong project management skills ensure that all client objectives including schedule, budget and design goals are met, even under intense pressure and with multiple deadlines

-Effective communication skills allow for translation of client ideas, vision and goals into concrete project deliverables

-Ability to define and understand each project’s target audience so that every project speaks to the right audience with the right message

-Knowledgeable in the use of Adobe InDesign CS2, Adobe PhotoShop CS2, Adobe Illustrator CS2, Adobe Acrobat CS2, Quark, Macromedia Dreamweaver 8, Macromedia Flash, Microsoft Office
