Alan See’s Summary

Alan See is a senior marketing and sales executive with the rare ability to speak Web 2.0 and Sales 101 in the same sentence. He also serves as an associate faculty member at the University of Phoenix where he facilitates courses in Marketing, eBusiness and Management Theory. Alan has 25 years of industry experience helping technology companies develop marketing strategies and sales initiatives that power profitable growth. He has performed in senior marketing, senior analyst, management consulting, and sales management roles at Seapine Software, AberdeenGroup, Teradata, SAS Institute, Cap Gemini Ernst&Young, and NCR Corporation. He holds BBA and MBA degrees from Abilene Christian University and is available for hire for consulting or long term employment covering a wide range of marketing, business development and sales related assignments.

Alan See’s Specialties:

Go-to-Market Strategic Planning, Marketing Strategy, Demand Generation, Published Thought Leader and Conference Speaker, Change Agent, Sales Management, Strategic Alliances and Business Development, Channel Development, Product Marketing and Product Management. Budgeting, P&L Accountability, Customer Relationship Management, Business Intelligence, Data Warehousing and Application Lifecycle Management Expertise, Executive, Vice President
