Stephen Lee’s Summary

SAP Technical Architect and Senior NetWeaver Consultant.

I have more than 20 years of IT experience leading technical teams, providing technical project assessment, budget, and leadership for implementations.

Over the last 14 years I have been directly involved in dozens of SAP Implementation projects spanning Manufacturing, Public Sector, Government, Oil and Gas, Bio-Medical, Service Provider (ASAP), Telecom, Automotive, and High Tech (Electronics).

My skills and experience bring solid technical expertise and strong leadership abilities to any project with a focus on lowering implementation cost and long term total cost of ownership while building a world class support infrastructure and in-house technical team.
