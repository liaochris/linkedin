Christopher Rodriguez’s Summary

Christopher Rodriguez is currently the Global Head of Leadership Development for LyondellBasell Industries, a $47B privately-held petrochemical company. His primary accountabilities are to drive the creation and delivery of a global leadership development strategy to build a robust pipeline of world class leaders and to sustain a competitive advantage within the petrochemical industry. In addition, he is developing a leadership academy to prepare high potentials and other key talent for senior leadership roles.

Christopher has over 15 years of experience in Talent Management, Leadership, and Organizational Development. Prior to joining LyondellBasell, Christopher worked at Pfizer Global Research & Development where he held a series of progressively responsible leadership positions, calling upon the disciplines of project management, leadership development, change management, learning & development, organizational development, and talent management.

Christopher earned a Master of Science in Organizational Management (OD/OE) from Eastern Connecticut State University, where he graduated top of his class and received the Award for Academic Excellence & Professional Scholarship. He holds a number of assessment certifications and is an active member of the National Organization Development Network (ODN). Christopher currently resides in Houston, Texas.
