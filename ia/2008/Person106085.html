Kevin Walker’s Summary

I am working as Technical Recruter at ANR Consultants. which is Ranked #58 in the 2006 San Francisco Business Times Top 100 Largest Woman-Owned Businesses Ranked #46 in the 2006 San Francisco Business Times Fastest Growing Private Companies.

The founders of ANR have technical engineering and scientific backgrounds and have been in the recruiting industry for over 15 years and in the technology industry for over 25 years. Our approach is to bring more of a personal touch to our clients by establishing long term relationships with repeat business.

Our Sr. Account Managers take the time to meet with hiring managers or onsite recruiters so that they not only understand their business but also get a feel for the type of personality that would fit well into a particular group.

Our database methodology was developed to immediately take advantage of job boards, user groups, passive candidates and the top candidate databases. Scalability is built into the process so we can always meet and adjust to our client’s immediate workload requirements. Our candidates are informed of our client’s status through our interactive web site and can be onsite within a day’s notice.
Our goal is to provide a manager two pre-screened qualified candidates within 48 hours.We are truly one large global staffing office.
