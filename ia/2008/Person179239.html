Srinath Sridharan’s Summary

A statistician by qualification - have experience in in B2B & B2C sectors including Automobile, Power, eCommerce, Advertising & Consumer Business industries through my roles in operations, marketing & brand management, strategic planning.

Currently, I head Strategic Alliances at the Wadhawan Group. The group has opcos / brands including presence in Housing Finance (Dewan Housing Finance Ltd), Venture Capital fund (DHFL VC), Real Estate (Dheeraj & East Coast in UAE & Australia), Food & Grocery - Convenience store formats(Spinach, Sangam, Sabka Bazaar, Smart Retail), Food & Beverage (Aurus fine dine, Sanchos - Mexican format, Noodle House, Dish Central), Education (RK Wadhawan Institute), Lifestyle Retail (Ed Hardy, Christian Audigier), Hospitality (Luxury hotels, Business hotels, Spas & Resorts).

I hold the responsibility of ensuring strategic alliance initiatives for the group, cutting across all its business verticals. My team looks into all relationships in the current & prospective business areas. This includes leveraging knowledge within the system & competitive intelligence to identify and analyze opportunities, developing business plans with the various business units, carrying out financial structuring / organizational structuring, negotiating and closing business contracts. My team also holds the relationships in all M&As that the group is involved in.

Prior to this role, I incubated the Lifestyle Business unit at the group. My team invested in and/or incubated businesses in the lifestyle segment covering Food & Beverage including fine dine concepts, Luxury retailing, Lifestyle products including apparel & accessories.

My keenness for market dynamics with an eye for spotting consumer trends is my driving force. A strong logical mindset coupled with my people-management approach helps in my ability to lead multiple cross functional teams to drive multiple projects simultaneously to achieve the final objective.

In short - "Get things done"!
