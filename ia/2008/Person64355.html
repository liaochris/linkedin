Fletch Rickman’s Summary

At Sanford Rose Associates-Wichita, our objective is to efficiently, effectively and successfully assist our client partners in developing a high performance, results-oriented organization whereby establishing the means for increased growth and profitability.

Our vision is to be the preferred partner of our clients by offering superior consultation, highly efficient and effective placements and an everlasting professional relationship that supports the dynamics of the global environment.”

We are a specialized executive search firm that offers unmatched value in satisfying the position profiles and placement needs of our clients.
