Micheal Axelsen’s Summary

Micheal Axelsen is Director of Applied Insight Pty Ltd, and provides information systems consulting services to clients. Micheal has extensive expertise in providing direction to the information systems and information management function for businesses and government agencies in line with business strategies, goals, and objectives. Previous to this role, Micheal was Director of Information Systems Consulting for BDO Kendalls from 1999 to 2007.

Micheal’s area of expertise is in ICT management and governance, particularly regarding the legislative requirements of government in the maintenance and development of information retrieval systems and record keeping systems. In particular, Micheal's focus is on the structured storage of information and compliance with relevant information standards and leading methods in information systems management.

Micheal has had several publications in the area of information systems and business through membership of the CPA Australia Information Technology & Management Centre of Excellence (as Chairman), as well as being co-author of research papers in the area of information systems and databases in association with the University of Queensland and Georgia State University.

Micheal maintains a web site devoted to the management of information and information systems at his blog, http://www.michealaxelsen.com.
