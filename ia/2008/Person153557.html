Moji Doyle’s Summary

I have been a fitness enthusiast since I was 16 years old collecting articles about health, wellness, nutrition and fitness and a business owner for over 7 in the prenatal fitness arena. Through my no nonsense approach to fitness I empower new and expectant mothers, as well as women in the Southern Palm Beach county area, who have been struggling with weight management and weight loss and who are looking to incorporate all of the above elements into their daily lives.

The following are the services I provide:

Prenatal Fitness Training
Post-partum Fitness Training
Mommy & Me
Mom & Baby Stroller Workouts
Circuit Training
Nutritional Guidance
Fit & Fabulous Weight Loss Challenge
Referral Service for expectant and new parents and families relocating to South Florida.

I believe that optimal fitness and health can be achieved, and maintained throughout the trimesters and beyond with my personalized Fitness Training.
The benefits of exercise for both mother and baby to staying healthy and in shape during pregnancy and beyond are numerous.

I also believe that fun and invigorating workouts tailored to my clients' individualized needs, lifestyle and fitness level, conducted in the privacy and convenience of their own home, will help women to learn to prioritize and to put themselves first. Usually I am seeing the opposite, where the mother will take care of everybody else in her family leaving her exhausted and drained at the end of the day.
