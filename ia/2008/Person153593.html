Alex Parr’s Summary

Prestige Property Services is a "one stop property shop" operating throughout East Anglia. Our clients range from owner/occupiers to landlords with portfolios of one to 400 properties.

Relocations.
We manage relocations for both the corporate and the individual from start (sourcing the property, carrying out the legal work, arranging removals from one house to the other) to finish.

House sales.
We buy and sell properties on behalf of our clients.

Lettings
We arrange and manage commercial and residential lettings.

Conveyancing
We undertake inhouse all legal matters pertaining to the purchase and sales of properties.

Administration.
We will carry out the administration of property portfolios, i.e. tenancy agreements, sourcing up to date mortgages, debt collecting. etc., for those landlords who do not wish to be involved with the day to day running of their portfolios.
