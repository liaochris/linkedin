  • Owner/Founder/TEO

    Prostation Systems

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    June 2002 — May 2005 (3 years)

    On a beautiful day, a friend and college pal of mine and I decided we could do better on high-end personal computers for image professionals (designers, architects, engineers) and game enthusiasts. So we started "Prostation Systems". "Prostation" stands for "professional workstation" and we based it on a sturdy, well defined philosophy that put customization, customer service and quality above anything else.

    We "thought" there was a market for those products in our country - Portugal - but we were wrong. There's a small market for it in the USA and in the UK and perhaps in other countries, but no, not in Portugal, definitely.
    Here, game enthusiats build their own machines because it's cheaper and 99% of image professionals use a "white box" - OEM, no-brand, generic machines, because they're really cheap.
    The other 1% use only wide accepted and recognized brands like HP and Dell.

    So we went out of business, after less then 3 years, and went on pursuing different careers.
