Ben Brownback’s Summary

After working with McDonald’s Corporation as an intern helping to shoot and edit videos for internal communications, Ben enrolled in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign’s College of Liberal Arts & Sciences. Graduating in 1999 with a degree in Physiology, he moved to Las Vegas to continue his career with McDonald’s Corporation. After working with the West Division US Division of McDonald’s Corporation, Ben started a business with his former employer at McDonald’s producing corporate events, and corporate/industrial media.

DAV Productions has quickly emerged as one of the leaders of event production and is the largest of its kind in the Las Vegas Valley. Most recently DAV Productions was ranked 3,401 on Inc. magazine’s first-ever list of the 5,000 fastest-growing private companies in the nation.

Notable productions done for his Alma Mater include national half-time basketball and football commercials for the past 5 years, Executive Producer for the University of Illinois Foundation’s Campaign Kickoff event at Navy Pier and Executive Producer for the University of Illinois Alumni Association’s Rose Bowl events.

In addition to his work with the University of Illinois, Ben and his team have worked with such clients as Pepsi, Sallie Mae, McDonald’s, Wendy’s, and Stryker Corporation as many other Fortune 100 businesses nationwide.
