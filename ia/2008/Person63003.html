Frank Boosman’s Summary

I create product plans, marketing strategies, and business relationships to take ideas from inception through to customer delivery and market success.

Over the last 20 years, I've been fortunate enough to be part of some fairly groundbreaking projects (and if luck is "the inner man externalized," as Robertson Davies wrote, then hopefully this says something good about my inner man). I take new ideas and assemble teams of people to bring them to life, with a strong strategic marketing and business development perspective along the way.

Acrobat started when a VP at Adobe called a meeting of four engineers and me and said, "John Warnock has an idea to do platform-independent documents." Much hard work later, Acrobat was born, and is now on the order of a half-billion-dollar business. Rainbow Six started when a colleague and I visited the FBI's Hostage Rescue Team at Quantico and saw a demonstration of their abilities. When we were launching Red Storm Entertainment, I proposed building a realistic hostage rescue (later to become special operations) title. Much hard work later, Rainbow Six was born, inaugurating the category of realistic first-person shooters.

My goal is to keep pushing the envelope in whatever I do -- to do things that haven't been done before. I also want to continue to work with people I like, trust, and respect. Life's too short to do otherwise.
