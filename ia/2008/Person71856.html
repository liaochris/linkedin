Graham Ford’s Summary

I am a UK qualified chartered accountant with an an MBA from the Rotterdam School of Management, Netherlands (Exchange: Richard Ivey School of Management, Canada).

I am currently employed at Fuji Xerox as Asia Pac Financial Controller.

My last position was as a Finance Manager for Vodafone Australia, supporting the Marketing Director and his team on all aspects of finance: business cases, forecasts, financial performance and product performance
