Nigel Harper’s Summary

• Over 15 years experience in broadband entertainment and IT systems.
• Comprehensive knowledge of IP Interactive TV and related service and
solutions business
• Commercial experience in Due Diligence, business modelling/budgeting and
operations
• Asia Pacific region focus in Business Strategy and Development.
• Partnership and associate support activities for broadband and broadcast
services.

Before helping to establish Furious Entertainment my experience was largely in the area of Interactive Systems with the Asian based offices of Yes Television. I was responsoble for all core technical decisions and also the operational establishment of IPTV and television broadcast channels including Goal TV and the Fight1.tv concept for aggregated Martial Arts media where I introduced consultants and assessed content acquisition options.

Recent work with Yes TV included development of new channel concepts as well as ongoing management of operations for Goal TV and the co owned Yes Plus IPTV Web 2.0 services based in Hong Kong, Malaysia and Thailand.

I led the creative development and operation of the Cambridge iTV (IPTV) Service, which started in 1994 and consulting expertise for international operations (including the BBC, Singapore Telecom, Oracle and the EU funded project AMUSE) exploring technical and business cases for consumer services in the Interactive TV field.

My core expertise is in the technical and human interface architectures for delivery of interactive systems. This includes both the hardware systems for head end and consumer premises equipment as well as the software and standards that are beginning to influence the whole environment.

I worked on projects in the Set-top box arena for Online-Media as well as leading work on IPTV platforms and WWW on TV services including original work in Network Computing.

I hold an MSc in Interactive Computing and also a BA Honors in Design & Technology.

Content creation and sourcing in sport, lifestyle and entertainment genre.
