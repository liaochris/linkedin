Norm Williamson’s Summary

Senior-level ITSM Consultant with expertise in process architecture, process and services assessment, organizational change management, managing diverse I/T Services support teams, and overseeing large-scale projects with a pervasive focus on aligning I/T services to foundational business requirements. Recognized for providing services with measurable excellence and customer satisfaction. Hold ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library - a Best Practices framework) Practitioner Certifications in Service Desk/Incident Management, Problem Management, Configuration Management, Change and Release Management. Certified ITIL Instructor for ITIL Foundations and ITIL Practitioner Support and Restore. Skilled in hiring and developing high-performing teams. Experience includes consultative I/T process assessment, enterprise project management, applications support management, data center and network administration and support, call-center infrastructure and process management, and wireless data communications and telemetry.

Norm Williamson’s Specialties:

I/T Support Services Leadership; I/T Process development/alignment with core Business requirements; Organizational Change Mgmt.; ITIL Practitioner Certifications/experience in: Service Desk/Incident Mgmt, Problem Mgmt, Change/Release Mgmt, Configuration Mgmt/CMDB; Certified ITIL Trainer for Foundations and Practitioner: Support and Restore; tiered support team alignment; metrics and performance data analysis; budget mgmt; performance and development planning and mgmt. Member of itSMF.
