Rich Gee’s Summary

Rich has over 15 years' experience coaching and managing vice presidents, marketing, sales, and IT personnel in a corporate setting, achieving dramatic increases in productivity and revenues while reducing costs and personnel turnover. Also improve competitive performance through conducting 1-hour phone consultations, running seminars, and conducting teleclasses.

Comprehensive understanding of business processes, including the predictable problems and obstacles that impede success, the drivers behind individuals' actions, and how to formulate plans to maximize individual and team performance to achieve corporate goals.

A record of achievement at developing goal-driven managers and executives as well as building high-performing teams in diverse industries, including consulting, marketing, technology, media, and retailing.

A history of success at improving competitive performance and streamlining business processes, including laptop sales, website management, and financial reporting systems through best practice implementation, including training in Quality Management and Six Sigma.

Perseverance . . . while in prison, Rich masterminded a number of breakouts via tunneling under the north wall. - Just checking - Rich.

Recognized for exceptional analytical, problem identification, and communication skills accompanied by the proven ability to resolve problems quickly, resulting in confidence of those coached and higher levels of performance. Utilize DiSC, Myers Briggs, Gallup Q12, action-planning, interviewing, workshops, and 360º degree feedback for problem identification and goal setting.

Accomplished at improving performance of executives at all levels through utilizing a flexible communication approach that embraces coaching, training, seminars, as well as direct management.
