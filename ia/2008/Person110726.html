Larry Roadcap’s Summary

Larry’s knowledge and areas of expertise include:
* Banking, trading and trade processing systems
* Program governance and project management
* Integrated market, credit and operational risk management
* Operations improvement, business integration and strategic cost management
* Regulatory and treasury advisory
* Systems selection and development

Larry is a capital markets and technology management professional with 14 years experience working with clients to transform their business problems into technology solutions. Larry combines strategic and tactical expertise with strong qualifications in all phases of the business/technology life cycle, including: governance; project management; business requirements definition; technical design; system engineering; and testing.

Larry has directed large-scale business and technology re-engineering initiatives including the design and integration of trading and risk management solutions for fixed income, equity and derivative portfolios at such firms as Charles Schwab, the Commonwealth Bank of Australia, Barclays Global Investors, Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Medibank, National Australia Bank, Merrill Lynch, UBS and Wells Fargo.

Larry is known for his outstanding ability to foster high performance teams, establish credibility within organisations and build relationships across business units at all levels.
