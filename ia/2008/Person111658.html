Laurie Pringle’s Summary

Laurie engages life with drive, motivation and determination, as well as a healthy sense of humour! Laurie's career history includes sales and marketing managerial positions within the Educational Publishing and Educational Technology industries. Laurie’s perseverance, tenacity and strategic approach to alliance building and key relationships, have allowed her to enjoy outstanding success and tremendous friendships throughout her career.

Recently, Laurie decided to dedicate her skills and expertise to the charitable sector. Having completed the Fundraising and Volunteer Management, post graduate program at Humber College (with honours), Laurie is enjoying a newfound sense of passion and enthusiasm for her work.

Laurie is highly enthusiastic and passionate about education, healthcare, social justice, environmental issues and animal welfare. In her spare time, you're likely to find Laurie biking through High Park, the Humber River or along the Toronto Lakeshore. More often than not, she'll be behind a camera, attempting to capture the beauty of the environment and people around her.
