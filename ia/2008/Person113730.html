Linas Liubinskas’s Summary

Senior level global marketing, strategy and change management consultant, with an emphasis on sales and market development, target segmentation, all aspects of marketing planning and execution (traditional and new media), strategic planning, coordinated tactical execution, and multiple layers of corporate change management. Experienced in a number of industries, but primarily financial services and management consulting. Currently President and CEO of ConsultConsortium which provides senior-level consultation across Central and Eastern Europe. Company serves as a conduit between CEE countries and best-in-class consultants from around the world.
