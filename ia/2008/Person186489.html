Bulent Tekmen’s Summary

Founder and CEO Netron Technology:

Found Netron in 1997 with the objective to provide IT training services to national and international corporations in Turkey, Netron has rapidly become the market leader as the leading Microsoft, Cisco, IBM, Citrix, SAP, Apple, Adobe Technical Training Center in the local market, and one of the top 5 Technical Training Centers in the EMEA region. In addition to being a top ranking IT training services provider to both the corporate and retail segments, Netron is also a respected provider of IT consultancy services and a provider of custom software solutions.

Netron operates with 3 offices in Istanbul and New York.
