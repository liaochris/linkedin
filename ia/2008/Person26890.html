Brett Bachman’s Summary

We founded Growth Acceleration Partners to provide the full range of management-consulting and software/web-development outsourcing services that our clients need to grow revenues and profits faster.

We are team of experienced executives with strong operating backgrounds who work collaboratively to solve whatever problems constrain your growth. We have had long successful careers in leading technology and service companies. Four of us have run our own companies. Two of us have founded and successfully sold venture-backed software companies.

Brett Bachman’s Specialties:

Our firm focuses on three primary practices: Strategy, Search Engine Marketing (SEO/SEM), and Outsourcing. We are headquartered in Austin, TX, and have our offshore development center in San José Costa Rica.
