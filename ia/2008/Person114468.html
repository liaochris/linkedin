Lisa Moody’s Summary

The company I founded specializes in integrated marketing in multicultural platforms. Although we realize the importance of building brand awareness, our specialty is direct response/retail--selling a client's product or service as soon as possible, and over and over! In-language specialities include Hispanic, Filipino, Chinese, Persian, Armenian, and Asian Indian Campaigns. Lifestyle segments covered: GLBT, Christian, and Urban/20somethings.

I began my career as a media research assistant with a rather large media buying firm in Los Angeles. I detoured into production for a few years, working with several film and distribution companies until I formed my company in 1995. I have been instrumental in launching two 24-hour ethnic channels (DTS) and am working several new media projects (Asian)!

I still flex my creative muscles as often as I can...functioning as either Creative Director, Producer, writer, etc. to some of our projects in production. "All (paper)work and no (creative)play makes Lisa a dull gal!

Lisa Moody’s Specialties:

If a business is looking for 1,000, 5,000 or 10,000 new customers, I will create a strategy, a plan, a budget and a timeline for achieving this! My team and I are skilled at delivering specific sales objectives using multicultural and lifestyle segment marketing!
