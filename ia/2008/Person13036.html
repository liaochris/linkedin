Anil Garg’s Summary

I have successfully led both the sell side and the buy side of global outsourcing in my last 25 years of experience. I have used this global sourcing technique to accelerate and improve service levels as well as reduce operating costs. I have sold services, I have produced services, I have purchased services, and I have founded businesses that leverage lower labor cost advantages of other global economies. I have set up successful subsidiary operations and integrated them with corporate teams.

I have led sales, led engineering and product development, run data centers, and also led global sourcing organizations. I have had tremendous success in recruiting high caliber teams globally.

Anil Garg’s Specialties:

Global outsourcing - Both Sell side and Buy side.
Building products by marrying lower cost resources to innovative experts.
Rationalizing process execution costs by leveraging overseas operations.
Setting up and Managing subsidiary operations.
Raising Venture financing and executing high value contracts.
