Ronit Roy’s Summary

Ronit Roy is a recent graduate from the MBA program at the University of Toronto . He has over 5 years of experience in Strategy Consulting, IT Consulting and Software Development. Ronit is currently looking for Strategy / Business Development positions in Europe, North America, Australia/NZ and Asia. He is also allowed to work in the UK through the Highly Skilled Migrant Program.

Recently, Ronit returned from Sydney, Australia, where he finished his MBA on an exchange term. After Australia , Ronit traveled throughout Asia. His experiences abroad have made Ronit desire an international career. Last summer, he worked at the student run consulting firm, Rotman NeXus. Through Rotman NeXus, Ronit provided management consulting services to not for profit organizations in Toronto .

Before the MBA, he provided IT consulting services for the Canadian Federal Government, and he worked as a software developer for a variety of IT firms. Ronit has his undergrad in Computer Science from Carleton University, in Ottawa, Canada.
