Kavit Haria’s Summary

I'm interested in entrepreneurship and development.

I'm a web-author and music business consultant with Inner Rhythm. Recently, I've started to write a blog at www.innerrhythm.org. I also write a weekly newsletter to over 15,000 musicians worldwide. My most popular internet-based training program is the Musicians Mastermind - teaching you how to get more exposure for your music, sell more of it and get more gigs. I've been doing this since 2004.

In 2007, I started a web development and strategy formulation company to work with small businesses. Our goal is to help you turn your vision into a real concept that will be seen by thousands of people all around the world and bring you the results you desire.

I'm also a speaker on the topics of entrepreneurship, music business, marketing and web development. I've run workshops, seminars and given private talks too at small businesses and educational establishments.
