Mei Fong’s Summary

•award-winning Wall Street Journal correspondent.

Educated at Columbia University with a Masters in International Affairs, and the National University of Singapore, Singapore with a Bachelor of Arts (Honors) majoring in English Literature and a minor in psychology.

Mei Fong’s Specialties:

Currently based in Beijing, covering economic development in China, with focus on development of retail industry as well as Beijing 2008 Olympic Games.
