Gregory Wexler’s Summary

Gregory has twenty-five years of experience as an entrepreneur and business leader. He is the Chairman of SmartReply and Founder/member of The MortgageCoach (WowTools.com) -- both firms recognized by the OC Business Journal as top fastest growing companies in Orange County (2007).

Gregory is passionate about his entrepreneurial endeavors because he enjoys the process: casting the vision, creating, building, and growing. “There is something invigorating about working with a team of people to build something challenging that stands out and goes a step beyond.”

Gregory’s wife carries and equally motivating entrepreneurial spirit (BorrowedBling.com) and they spend time together with their daughter, currently attending USC in Los Angeles.
