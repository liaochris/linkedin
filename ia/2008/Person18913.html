Aysha Austin’s Summary

After receiving a degree in Graphic Design, I acquired a broad base of skills in the Salt Lake area in positions such as the Creative Director for FILA Golf and the VP of Web Design and Development for Singlepoint Resources. I chaired the Design Committee with FILA Golf and managed the creative and marketing departments for the primary company and subsidiaries. During my time with Singlepoint Resources, I developed and launched a successful and profitable internet marketing department in addition to teaching local workshops on search engines and internet based marketing techniques.

During the 9 years spent in the industry, I've assisted in the launch of numerous companies by providing services that assist clients in taking a business idea from concept to a fully launched and branded company. These services have ranged from design, branding, various types of marketing in addition to internet application scoping and development. In 2004 I incorporated Creative Fire, Inc., a successful web design and website hosting company which I currently own and manage. I have a nationwide client list that includes a wide variety of individuals as well as businesses in numerous industries ranging from TV personalities such as candidates of The Apprentice Season 5, professional NFL and NBA athletes, CPAs, sports agents, real estate brokerages and agents, doctors, artists, restaurants and commercial services.

In addition to owning and managing Creative Fire, Inc., I currently hold a position as the VP of Corporate Development & Production with FonRoots, Inc., a telecommuncation company DBA as BlackBook Mobile, a mobile phone directory and backup service.

Aysha Austin’s Specialties:

I am very diverse in my skill set and learn very quickly to accomodate the needs/requirements of clients and projects.
