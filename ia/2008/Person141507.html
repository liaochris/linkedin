Nikhil Kothari’s Summary

Nikhil Kothari has over 10 years of experience designing, developing and leading the development of Microsoft's Web platform. His experience is both broad and deep; specifically he has worked on ASP.NET, ASP.NET Ajax, Visual Studio and IIS.

As an architect, Nikhil is responsible for the overall product design and direction of Web frameworks and tools that enable development of Silverlight-based Rich Internet Applications and Ajax Web applications, smart-client applications and traditional Web applications. Prior to this, Nikhil led the development team and shipped multiple versions of ASP.NET and .NET. He has been involved in the development of these since their inception. Over the course of his career at Microsoft, Nikhil has filed over 40 patents in the Web development space.

He is one of the most recognized names in the Web developer community and blogs at http://www.nikhilk.net. He is a published author, having written the very well-reviewed book on developing ASP.NET server controls and components. He has spoken at numerous public conferences such as PDC, TechEd and MIX.
