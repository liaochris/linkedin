Lauren Jones’s Summary

Lauren H. Jones

Principal/Director
Business Development

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Effective marketing, branding and image strategy begins with knowing about the people.

Lauren Jones knows people.

Mrs. Jones’ legendary personal-communications skills are a key factor in The Vibe Refinery’s success and growing client base. Besides being an outstanding listener (a must in this field), Lauren is a gifted taste maker with a creative and entrepreneurial mind. Her unique approach to meeting and conquering clients' challenges is an invaluable asset.

Lauren’s marketing savvy is not an overnight phenomenon. It began at the age of 17. While still at John F. Kennedy High School in Riverdale, NY, she worked in commodities banking at Chase Manhattan N.A. She was an 18-year old marketing intern there and later began developing another important skill set as a clerk at the Johnson, Haysbert & Johnson Law Firm in New York City. Lauren holds a J.D.

More marketing expertise was developed in posts as Director of Educational Resources at Albert Merrill School of Business, a lengthy stint in the Catalog Specifications Unit at JCPenney Corporation and accounting and administrative positions at New York University.

Lauren’s hands-on creative talent is proven. She is an accomplished productions stylist for photo sessions, films and promotional events. Her photographic images have been published in Ebony Magazine, Essence Magazine, leading on-line magazines and other prominent mediums.

The civic-minded mother of four is a former member of the Allen, Texas Arts Alliance Board, The mayor of the bustling Dallas suburb appointed Lauren to two full terms. She is also an award winning past Program Director for the national civic organization Jack and Jill of America, Inc.
