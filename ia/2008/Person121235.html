Marion Jacobson’s Summary

The foundation of my professional experience is communication. From my first foray into Information Technology as a Technical Writer, through every position I have held since that time, I have been fortunate to have had excellent education, preparation and hands-on experience in communication.

My first technical writing experience was with Contact Software International, the creators of ACT! software. I wrote the first User's Manual for ACT! and have been involved in technical writing, editing, and communication ever since. For the past 9 years I have been able to combine creative and graphic skills with my communication skills and have been working in web design and search engine optimization.

My goal is to spend the next 10 years of my professional career exercising my communication skills, my training abilities, and my creative talents together in a way that contributes to my employer and also helps me grow.

Marion Jacobson’s Specialties:
