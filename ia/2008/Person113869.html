Linda Rantz’s Summary

I am a paralegal with over 20 year's experience in different areas of law, including bankruptcy, family law, entertainment law, estate planning, and probate. In 2006, I shifted my focus exclusively to bankruptcy law and began offering my services as a virtual assistant specializing in the preparation of Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 petitions. I work only for attorneys (never directly for the debtor) and handle all cases myself. For that reason, I limit the number of attorneys with whom I contract.

Linda Rantz’s Specialties:

I can work with any bankruptcy software program. I am familiar with (and blog about) various due diligence products that are marketed to attorneys.
