Vijay Pereira’s Summary

Vijay is a Human Resource (HR) professional with a wide range of experience in the field of HR, Personnel & Administration and Labour management. He has experience of working on his own initiative and as part of a team. Vijay has proven leadership skills involving managing, developing and motivating teams to achieve their objectives. He has exposure to most aspects of HR and managing employees from cross functional and cultural areas. Vijay also possess first-class analytical, design and problem solving skills and is dedicated to maintaining high standards.
