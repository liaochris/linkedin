Prudence Mathis’s Summary

I work as both a residential listing agent and as a buyer’s agent, specializing in assisting first time home buyers throughout the Dallas / Fort Worth metroplex area.

I believe that my background of work experience prior to becoming a licensed REALTOR has enhanced my ability to serve my clients. Over 3 years of full-time real estate investing experience has aided me in working with sellers as I can confidently recommend improvements that may aid in getting a quicker sell for the most profit. 8 years as a Human Resources Manager has given me the ability to negotiate successfully. Over 15 years of accounting experience allows me to keep an eye on the bottom line for all my clients.
