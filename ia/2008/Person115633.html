Lori Ruff’s Summary

12+ years in business process improvement, leadership and communications, team alignment, training, diversity, addressing issues of race & racism, and conflict resolution. Speaker, writer, coach, strategic partner.

International experience builds my expertise in impacting leadership skills and effecting positive change in organizations, working with managers to help them develop mentoring and coaching skills, and working with businesses to attract, select, engage, and retain the best people for the right jobs.

I do this by customizing solutions to fit client needs and choosing tools for hiring, coaching, retaining, and professional development with an emphasis on career development, and replacement and succession planning. I help leading organizations identify their best employees and teach them how to use that information to set the bar for selection and onboarding, as well as team performance improvement.

I am a facilitator of greatness; but it is the desire & work of the people involved in the process that make all the difference.

I can be reached quickly by email to lruff@whiteknightmc.com, or by phone to 704-224-0271. I look forward to discussing your concerns and how I might help you address them.
