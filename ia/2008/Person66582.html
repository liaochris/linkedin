Wayne Kay’s Summary

With services ranging from and including venture capital fund raising to recruitment, and sales training to outsourced sales, Fullbrook practically assist our clients to outperform their competition, time and time again.

Founded in 2001, Fullbrook’s unique service portfolio & business focus has provided the foundation for not only our own success but significantly that of our many clients in the IT & Telecommunications industry.


Today, Fullbrook has become one of the European IT and Telecommunications industries most respected niche partners and suppliers. We’ve helped our clients to raise over 30M Euro’s of venture capital funding, we sit on the recruitment Preferred Supplier Lists (PSL) of some of Europe’s largest blue chip organisations and through either strategic consulting & leadership, or by maximising our network and skill, we’ve enabled many of our clients to capture unimagined market share across the UK and mainland Europe.

In a nutshell, Fullbrook live and breathe the IT and Telecommunications industry; we don’t just stand in the wings and simply give advice or supply mediocre resource, instead we’re immersed in the industry, we understand it better than most and we know how to maximise opportunity, avoid pitfalls and help you to fast track your way to success.
