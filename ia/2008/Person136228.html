M.R.M. Parrott’s Experience

  • Writer, Publisher

    rimric

    (Privately Held; Writing and Editing industry)

    November 1997 — Present (10 years 3 months)

    I've always been inspired by writers whose creative integrity far outweighed the fleeting market concerns of the publishing business. Toward that end, I have written and released each of my books independently of agents and "big publishers". rimric press - one of the first independent publishers to be listed with Barnes & Noble - was launched in 1997 with this rather classical independence and literary inspiration in mind. I certainly hope you enjoy my work, from my experimental beginnings to fully-fledged series. Please join me in celebrating the new wealth of independent writers, as we gradually join our friends, who through their artwork, music, films, and publications, remind us of the value in creative independence :)
