Phil Williams’s Summary

I have nearly ten years marketing and management experience with large and small organizations. Throughout my career, I have balanced many difficult tasks while delivering outstanding results. Below are a few highlights that may be of interest:

• Developed, implemented and managed marketing campaigns using various media such as direct mailing, newspaper, billboard, magazine, radio, e-marketing, and television.

• Successfully led negotiation teams through three union contract negotiations resulting in $1.9 million in savings on health insurance for the City of Kokomo.

• Negotiated innovative contracts including a multi-company agreement resulting in the building of a new fire station at no cost to the City of Kokomo.

• Led team that turned around the City of Kokomo’s finances from negative $8.3 million and the brink of financial disaster to solid financial footing and a positive $713 thousand by the end of the year and $5 million after three years.

• Streamlined City Controller’s office by reorganizing an outdated structure, implementing a cross-training program and developing standard operating procedures and policies. The reorganization resulted in an increase of 25% in productivity, improved reporting, accountability, and annual clean audits.

• Rescued customer’s business, which was shut down due to system and process incompatibility problems, got system quickly back up and running thus relieving a $500,000 backlog in contracts and introduced a new system and process that reduced a complex three week process to a 48 hours smooth running profitable operation.

• Developed and organized a two-day young entrepreneur program designed to teach middle school children the basics of starting, running and growing a small business.

My experiences have required a team approach, and they also have required leadership during difficult times. I have demonstrated time and time again that I am such a leader.
