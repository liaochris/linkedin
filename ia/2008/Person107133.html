Kiran Hari’s Summary

Kiran began his career as a Management Consultant. He provided state of the art solutions for Business Process Innovation and Process Automation challenges for fortune 500 clients such as Coca-Cola, JPMorgan Chase, John-Hancock, GE Capital, Mercedes-Benz and New York City and Miami-Dade County Government Agencies.

Kiran now utilizes his strong consulting background in devising customized financial and risk management solutions for his corporate and individual clients. He holds an M.B.A. from the Edinburgh Business School. He resides in New York City. He has consulted with Ernst & Young, Morgan Stanley, Merrill Lynch, PNC Bank, AXA Equitable, ICICI Bank, Aetna, Oxford Health and Data Industries Ltd.

He has traveled extensively around the world. His interests include educating & helping others, social work, travel, reading and charity work. He is involved with several professional, cultural and social organizations to promote universal friendship.

He loves to educate professionals and families on how to SAVE and help them PROTECT their Wealth, Health, Income, Life and Estate.
