Lisa Westbrook’s Summary

I am a professional web and software interface designer with 9 years of design experience. My career and passion for user advocacy began at an internet bookstore and continued through various contract and full time positions. Proficient in user task analysis, rapid prototyping and iteration, user testing and functionality specification development. Familiar with both Internet Explorer and Mozilla/Firefox HTML requirements. Proven experience leading teams and organizing complex projects while meeting scheduling deadlines.
