Stefan Wurster’s Summary

Seasoned Semconductor Design Specialist with over 24 years of Mixed-Signal Analog CMOS technology integration experience

Stefan Wurster’s Specialties:

Mixed-Signal CMOS design since 1982, covering most aspects of chip and circuit design. Specialties include Phase Locked Loop Systems, High Speed DAC systems, Ultra-High-Speed dynamic Logic
