David Ko’s Summary

I am currently executive vice president at Waggener Edstrom Worldwide overseeing Asia Pacific operations as well as a network of affiliates that spans major cities from Seoul to Kuala Lumpur. I am also responsible for expanding the agency's presence in the region through organic growth or new affiliations.

Previously, I was the founder and managing director of Shout Communications, a PR/marketing consultancy based in Hong Kong with offices in Singapore, Beijing and Seoul. Shout was acquired by Waggener Edstrom in 2005.

David Ko’s Specialties:

Strategic public relations, marketing communications, crisis management, brand management, e-marketing, new media.
