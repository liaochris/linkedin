Miguel Ângelo Vieira, MBA’s Summary

Expertise in recruitment for emerging markets (Latina America, Africa, Eastern Europe, Asia) with major clients in the Oil & Gas, FMCG, IT & Telecom, Retail, Banking & Finance industries.

My expertise is in repatriation recruitment for these markets. We help multinationals and large local companies find candidates with Western Education and experience who are looking to return home through the following services provided by our company:

-International Recruitment Summits
-International Search & Selection Projects
-Recruitment Days

I aim to deliver the best candidates to my clients through effective research and networking through all international channels. Candidates delivered range from CFOs, MBAs, middle managent, analysts, recent grads, and a variety of technical profiles for different industries.
