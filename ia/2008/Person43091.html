Dan Gray’s Summary

Online communities have been a big part of the last 7 years of my life. Starting off initially in FPS communities as an indie developer, and eventually progressing into MMOs.

Throughout this time I have developed a broad range of experience. Co-hosting MMO radio shows, shoutcasting tournament matches, writing articles for websites, running forums, IRC channels and fansites... These are some of the many things that have helped develop my understanding of online communities.

My experience both as a writer and as a radio co-host have definitely improved my written and oral communication skills, something very valuable to this position.

I also have solid technical skills. Coming from a background of being a developer myself, I have a good basic understanding of all of the processes involved in development.

Dan Gray’s Specialties:

Providing support for the growth and retention of a gaming community.
