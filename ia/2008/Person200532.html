Winston Forrest’s Summary

I have transitioned from software engineer to internet entrepeneur to travel agent/manager. I consult in the fields of Federal regulation compliace and employee & individual benefits. I own an insurance agency which represents AFLAC, Pre-Paid Legal, Assurant Health, Illinois Mutual and others. I am also one of the few consultants that specialize in Federal Travel Regulation and FARs compliance.

I assist companies prepare travel policies and procedures that are compliant with the Federal Travel Regulations (FTRs), Federal Acquistion Regulations (FARs) and are cost effective. I also help companies of all levels prepare for Gov't/DCAA audits.

My prior experiences in the workforce led me to the conclusion that employees were not completely protected. They cannot afford to miss work due to their own or a loved ones illness, they do not have access to quality attorneys to handle their major legal issues, not to mention those everyday issues that need legal support. I started selecting companies to represent based on these issues. Evaluating the companies that I represent was guided by my personal requirement that the companies operate ethically, provide the services as they describe them and be sound financially.

I spent the previous 13 years in the travel industry specializing in independent, international, leisure travel. I have traveled extensively through Europe and the Americas.

Small/medium-sized business, pre-tax benefit plans, individual and group health plans, identity theft risk management, legal benefits plans, FACTA/Gramm-Leach-Bliley/HIPAA compliance, group travel, international travel, quality control, FTR, FAR and Fly America Act compliance, travel policy design/implementation.
