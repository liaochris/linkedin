Michael Scullin’s Summary

Mike is a seasoned technology strategist and business developer with over 25 years experience. He has consulted to ‘C’ level telecommunications executives, been VP of Business Development & Government Affairs for the Wi-Fi startup Vivato, VP of Business Development and R&D for the network equipment manufacturer Xircom and spent 7 years as an operations, business strategist and government affairs executive with Vodafone AirTouch. Mike has also been a patent practitioner, consulted for Booz-Allen & Hamilton and served as a Submarine Officer with the U.S. Navy. He has been a member of the Board at the Wireless Communications Association International (WCA), is a member of the Federal Communications Bar Association (FCBA) and is periodic speaker/panelist at various technology fora. Scullin received his JD from Catholic University’s Columbus School of Law, MEA from the George Washington University and BS from the United States Naval Academy.
