Todd Cloutier’s Summary

Naval Officer with 22 years of experience in
- Nuclear Power. Formally trained in the theory and operation of Pressurized Water and other types of nuclear reactors. Experienced in all aspects of repair, maintenance and operation of naval nuclear reactors.
- Operational Planning. Formally trained in the development of Operational Plans, with applications focused on multi-service military operations. These skills are vital to my current role as Submarine Operations Officer, as one of my duties is to assist with the development of all operational plans for the ships and aircraft attached to the Abraham Lincoln Carrier Strike Group. (One carrier with embarked air wing, five escort ships, and over 6000 attached personnel)
- Submarine Operation. Formally trained in the maintenance, operation, and tactical employment of both nuclear and diesel submarines. First US Navy officer certified in diesel submarine combat command through the Netherlands Submarine Command Course (Perisher), in 2003. Also qualified by the US Navy for command of nuclear submarines.

  • Submarine Command Course Student

    Royal Netherlands Navy

    (Government Agency; 10,001 or more employees; Military industry)

    January 2003 — July 2003 (7 months)

    Student at the Royal Netherlands Navy Submarine Command Course (SMCC), also known as "Perisher".
    - First U.S. Navy Officer to successfully complete Dutch Perisher course
    - Course conducted in various training sites in the Netherlands, UK, and at sea onboard Dutch diesel-powered submarines.
    - Historical graduation rate from course is approximately 60-70%, with officers participating from countries as varied as The Netherlands, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Israel, Denmark, and Brazil.
    - Course consisted of classroom study of submarine tactics, personnel supervision (bridge team management training), trainer practice in submarine operations, classroom training in the UK in Joint Operational Planning, and several weeks of operations at sea practicing and executing a series of intense operations in the face of a dedicated opposing force of ships and aircraft.
