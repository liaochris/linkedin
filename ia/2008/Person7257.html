Alfonso Rodriguez Lecuona’s Summary

Alfonso works in the Marketing department at NEC-Philips UK, Cambridge Business Park. He studied a Master in International and European Business Law (LLM) and holds degrees in Law and Business. During his three years at the Canary Island Institute of Technology (European R&D Centre), he gained vast experience in Project Management (Information Technology Entrepreneurship). Alfonso has been involved in the Judge Business School Entrepreneurship programme since 2004, being awarded in 2007 with a distinction certificate (CfEL - Cambridge University). He has also recently been appointed Vice-President of the Cambridge University Technology and Enterprise Club (CUTEC).
