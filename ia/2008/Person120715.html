Maria H. Andersen’s Summary

Primarily, I teach mathematics at Muskegon Community College (Algebra through Calculus). I also have a consulting business, Andersen Algebra Consulting, through which I conduct professional training for faculty, author textbook materials, and consult for software and Internet companies in the industry. I am on the tail-end of a Ph.D. in Higher Education Leadership, looking specifically at issues relating to faculty development and change.

Lately, I've been really interested in technology and math, and that has manifested itself in a math & technology blog (www.TCMTechnologyBlog.blogspot.com) and a website for college mathematics professionals (www.TeachingCollegeMath.com).

In my ideal job, I would be hired to be a Teaching & Technology Evangilist for a college or university campus, with the opportunity to teach classes in math or technology here and there and to have the freedom to explore what needs to be explored at the moment.

Maria H. Andersen’s Specialties:

Professional development for math and science faculty (particularly related to technology), expertise in technology for education, production of camera-ready copy and graphics for mathematics, and general knowledge of the organization higher education.
