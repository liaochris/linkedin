Lee Fox’s Summary

Lee is a passionate entrepreneur, parent, team builder and philanthropist. Her experience spans the entertainment, technology and new media sectors where she has taken title as CEO (internet/mobile); VP of Marketing (professional services); Content Creator (brand management); Writer / Associate Director (consumer TV); and has been honored with multiple board / advisory roles. Mother of three, coach, and active humanitarian, Lee has focused the last two years on the development of a revolutionary internet youth property, KooDooZ. Lee encourages social responsibility (both personal & corporate) via her blogs & public speaking engagements that emphasize the importance of CSR, cause-marketing and youth-empowerment.

Lee Fox’s Specialties:

From concept to completion, Lee's industry expertise includes: * operations strategy & management; * team building; * performance metrics; * content development & direction (printed collateral, video, TV, internet & new media); * message continuity; *brand management; *cause marketing; * social media integration; * speech writing; * competitive business analysis; * start-ups

Lee Fox’s Experience

  • Chair

    AeA - Los Angeles Council

    (Non-Profit; 51-200 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    October 2007 — Present (1 year)

    Known for her art of developing customer retention programs, mentoring, and securing net new business, the AeA Los Angeles Council expects to leverage Lee's expertise and grow its member base (specifically focusing on companies generating 75M-500M in revenues). In her role as Chair, Lee will lead the Council's strategy & messaging to effectively represent the interests of the high-tech industry in the way of advocacy, programs & related initiatives. In Los Angeles, the AeA also advocates for the industry at the State Legislature on such issues as higher education, taxation and business regulations.

  • Founder & Chief Innovation Officer

    KooDooZ

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    Lee founded KooDooZ on the premis that "KDZ make it happen!"

    Her mission? To catalyze an effective partnership between youth, communities and corporations by promoting online awareness of -- and participation in -- local, national and global causes.

    By blending e-Learning with social networking, KooDooZ offers both kids and companies of conscience a revolutionary online interactive technology designed to benefit social change.

    KooDooZ rewards a user's investment in a virtuous world versus a virtual one. Each online interaction promotes the development of philanthropic & humanitarian values with compelling opportunities to partner with forward-thinking foundations, non-profits, celebrities and corporations. Not only can users learn about a wide-range of issues that matter, they can use the site as a megaphone to voice their own opinions, and in so doing, earn kudos. "KDZ need KooDooZ to make it happen!"
