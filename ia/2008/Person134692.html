Maureen "Mo" Whelan’s Summary

Maureen "Mo" Whelan is a former automotive gear head turned animation geek. She started her career in animation 12 years ago at a small L.A. game design company. Mo's worked at various production and post-production studios in Los Angeles as a 3D/2D animation computer artist, visual effects compositor, video editor, technical coordinator, grip for live action and stop motion, supervised artists in a post-production environment and was a Discreet Training Center manager for a few years. Some of her onscreen feature credits include South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut and Purgatory House. Mo is currently is in charge of Film Licensing and Acquisitions for AWNtv.com as well as tackling her usual busy schedule on a few independent projects, and is a Corpsepondant for the comedy/horror magazine Girls and Corpses Magazine.
