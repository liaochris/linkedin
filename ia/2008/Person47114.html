David Lund’s Summary

I am a marketing executive with over 20 years working for global consumer products companies such as Chiquita, Heinz, and Lenox in both innovation and marketing roles. Chiquita recently reorganized the company, and eliminated 160 management positions, including mine. I am looking for my next company to help grow. I am seeking a senior leadership position with either a brand marketing & strategic growth consulting firm or an innovation-driven consumer products company.

As a brand innovation leader, I lead multifunctional teams to build businesses where they did not exist before. Whether it is a new product, new service, new program, or new company capability, I am skilled at leading change initiatives to achieve business results. My strengths include strategic business building, business reinvention, team building, transforming consumer insights into strategic growth opportunities, strategic alliances, and brand marketing leadership.

My teams add value to companies and their bottom line. A recent accomplishment is building and leading an international multifunction team to transform regular bananas from a commodity fruit to more profitable value added products. Our first new product, Chiquita Fresh & Ready, was recognized as one of Datamonitor’s Top 10 Global New Products for 2006--just based on our test market! Fresh& Ready is projected to be a $100MM+ business within five years.

I am also consumer and customer driven. At Chiquita, I used consumer insights to convert a low selling specialty banana into the company’s most profitable product. My team developed new positioning, new branding, new packaging, and a new pricing strategy that increased margins AND revenues by 300%. This brought excitement to our retail customers with the first banana new product in over a decade: Chiquita minis.

I earned MBA from the University of Virginia Darden School of Business, and I completed an 18-month leadership development program at Chiquita.
