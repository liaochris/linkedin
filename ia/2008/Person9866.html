Amy Wu’s Summary

Graphic designer with vast experience in designing from concept to production, various print and visual materials that include: logo and corporate identity design, product brochures, invitations, direct mail, tradeshow signage, website look and feel. Also experienced in working for a design firm with external clients, a freelance designer, as well as working in-house in the marketing department for a furniture design company.

Amy Wu’s Specialties:

Print design and production, photo re-touching and color correction, large-format printing. Adobe InDesign CS 2, Adobe Photoshop CS 2, Adobe Illustrator CS 2, Adobe Acrobat 7, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Powerpoint, Apple Keynote.
