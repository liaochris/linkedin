Kristen Minnich’s Summary

Goodrich Corporation; we are a Fortune 500 company and a leading worldwide supplier of aerospace components, systems and services with a record of profitable growth. With annual revenues of $4.2 billion, Goodrich ranks as one of Fortune magazine's "Most Admired" aerospace companies. Driving this performance is the company's vision to create value through excellence in people, quality and innovation.
