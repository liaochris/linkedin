Sam Medalie’s Summary

Since 1995, I have been the President and Founder of The Longfellow Search Group. I have created a telephone name generation research firm and a search firm that have worked in many areas, including sales and sales management, information technology/internet/software, finance, civil engineering, hospitality, management consulting and marketing.

I am a former researcher at Harvard Business School and perform telephone name generation research for retained search firms and corporate clients.My company specializes in finding truly passive candidates. If you work in a company, you should know that I save 80%--90% on recruiter's fees.
