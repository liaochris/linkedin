Wei-Yeh Lee’s Summary

I am the inventor for 11 US patents and 1 European patent. At Columbia University, I was one of a team of 4 who managed the central university systems for 65,000 users. At Unicast, I changed the course of the company's strategic direction which rallied around the software that I architected and implemented. At Credit Suisse, I defined the process for Global Delivery of the Equity Research Portal, ResearchView. At Citibank, I was the Portal Architect for CitibankOnline. While I was there we were consistently ranked #1 Online bank by Gomez and Forbes and led multiple large scale Portal integrations. At Goldman Sachs, I was the Architect for the Linux Provisioning System which provisions all GS Standard Linux servers. I also spearheaded the definition of the Enterprise Systems Management Strategy for which an organization sprung up. I also led process improvement initiatives for the SEC10-b10 confirms process and the provided guidance for the Server Delivery Process. Furthermore, I was the Program Manager for the GS Algorithmic Trading Environment Redesign Initiative. Most recently I have been at Citigroup was the Program Manager and Architect for Citigroup.net. At Citigroup, I have done a significant amount of vendor management throughout the project and led a 2 week intensive Load Test at Sun's Test Center to validate a 1/2 scale production lab environment.

I am now the Chief Architect for NAVTEQ and head of the Global Architecture Team.
