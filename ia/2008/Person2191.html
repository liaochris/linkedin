Adam Bedford’s Summary

I am an Apple Certified Motion Graphics Designer & Final Cut Pro Editor with a background in Sound Design and extensive international experience.

As Senior Editor for global agency ?What If! in London I worked on campaigns & projects for a number of clients including Citigroup, Astra Zeneca, Unilever, UK Home Office, AOL, Shell, Southwest Airlines, EA Games, Electrolux and Red Bull.

Working in Australia, England and the US, my international experience lends fresh perspective to corporate, consumer & entertainment projects.

I’ve also worked on Music Videos, Feature Films, Documentaries, Commercials and Syndicated Radio Programs.

Currently based in Los Angeles with my own edit /graphics studio in Hollywood, I’m available for freelance/contract/part-time opportunities in the greater LA area.
