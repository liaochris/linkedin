Martin Hofenk’s Summary

For Alfa Delta Compendium, Performance Improvement means:

´The best of our three core business (Finance, Operations and Procurement), united in one outstanding product´


Finance:

We have unique solutions for all your questions in the financial arena. Are you looking to fill an acute capacity shortage, or to supplement a structural knowledge gap? Maybe you’re thinking of outsourcing your entire financial administration, or, just the opposite, you want to implement an in-house improvement process. Our specialists are ready. With knowledge, expertise and an original vision. SOx, IFRS, Basel II don´t have any secrets to us.

Operations:

We know how to streamline a manufacturing process without any major capital expenditures; identify the fundamental engineering principles that affect process and product performance by completing statistically valid experiments and process capability studies; establish optimal process parameters and complete process validation; complete "time and motion" productivity, efficiency and benchmarking studies.

Procurement:

We create directions for setting up a proper procurement function within your organisation including procurement strategy, policies, processes and procedures, necessary system support and procurement management reporting;
We define procurement issues like contract management, supplier management, supplier monitoring, standardized tools (RFI, RFQ, GTC, standard contracts, SLA’s).
We always combine structural improvement with pragmatic, hands-on optimization (cost savings, supplier reduction, supplier improvement, contract management) and can support you with tender processes.
