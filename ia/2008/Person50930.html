Dheeraj Sanghi’s Summary

I am a researcher in the area of computer networks.
Have wide administrative experience: Running the Institute Computer Center, Managing Graduate admissions, Cultural advisor to students, Student counselling, Managing all issues related to UG education in IIT Kanpur (as Chairman of Senate Under Graduate Committee), Design of CS curriculum for lots of colleges, Chairman of Health Center Users Committee, and so on. Can advise Engineering colleges in improving their quality, ranking, etc.

I am looking forward to a short break in my career to do something different. I don't know exactly what, but I am open to suggestions/ideas. It could be from May 2008 to June 2010. Willing to relocate within India anywhere. I am not keen on doing just more research and more teaching. That I can do best by staying on at IIT Kanpur. I am passionate about technical education in India, and would like to remain in touch with colleges, and a job where one is responsible for building relationships between industry and academia may be one such possibility. But I am open to other options, including options which have a bit of research or a bit of teaching involved as long as it is substantially different from my current job profile.
