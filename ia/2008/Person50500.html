Jorge Acuna’s Summary

Jorge has been involved in training, development and consulting for over 15 years. His professional background combined with international and cross-cultural experience gives you a uniquely strong advantage.

Jorge can share with you experiences that can help you develop performance improvement strategies for Business-To-Consumer (B2C) and Business-To-Business (B2B) interactions, starting in your own back yard.

Through his observations and professional experiences developing business opportunities and relationships across the USA and with the Hispanic/Latino market, he has demonstrated his ability to establish relationships at all levels of management, and creates business strategies that are customized for different markets.

As a corporate trainer and training developer, he has delivered over 150 seminars in many cities across the USA, Canada, Puerto Rico and Spain. He has been a guest speaker at several regional and international conferences with expertise on training processes, staff development, quality of customer service, workplace safety, regulation and legislation issues, organizational culture, change management, publishing, and marketing.

Today, Jorge dedicates himself to helping organizations avoid common (but costly) mistakes. He will be happy to explore with you how to reduce cost and improve the ROI of your communication and learning initiatives.

Jorge Acuna’s Specialties:

Professional education, consulting, leadership/organizational development and customer service/loyalty implementation. Marketing and online product launches, with expertise in e-mail marketing, SEO, landing pages, etc. Development of e-learning and continuing education learning products, developing performance improvement strategies, leadership training and career path development, creation of value added services, Spanish Region business development and contracting (USA, Puerto Rico and Spain).
