Arun Chearie’s Summary

I have been in Marketing Management Solution Consulting since the last few years. In these years, During the last two employment engagements,I have had the opportunity to closely work a with some of the leading financial services organizations, retailers and telecom enterprises in India on Enterprise Marketing Management initiatives.

My professional association with these enterprises have ranged from designing and executing Plain vanilla campaign management solutions to defining approaches on marketing optimization and communication channels utilization through Operational Research and Linear Programming Principles.

In the last couple of years, I have played an important role in opening up the Enterprise Marketing Automation (EMA) areas in Indian market. In last 2003, I was part of a core team that sold first-ever EMA solution to one of the leading private Banks in Indian Geography. Likewise, a couple of months ago, played an instrumental role in selling Business Intelligence / EMA solution to one of the top business conglomerates in India. These deals have resulted in a few million in revenue to the my organizations.

In the last two years, having worked in a couple of fairly large assignments in Telco industry, I have had the opportunity to learn the nuances of managing customer churn in an effective fashion by using the power of Analytics and Technology. Going forward, I have decided to soak myself into Optimization techniques to be able to increase the marketing throughput for customers we have been working with.
