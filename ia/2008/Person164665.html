Rosario Attanasio’s Summary

Application developer, power electronics and electrical drives design in a wide variety of applications. Mainly working on renewable energy generation systems covering both stand alone and grid connected design of power systems in the 1-10kW range. Always interested in research projects and involved in national research programs for the development and testing of new converter topologies as well as new innovative control techniques. Currently working on the development of a new 3kW conversion system for electricity generation from photovoltaic source and auxiliary power supplies for HEV (Hybrid Electrical Vehicle) applications.
Main international publications on the above topics:


•A. Consoli, R. Attanasio, M. Cacciato, F. Gennaro " An Innovative Boost Converter for Fuel Cells Stationary Generation Systems", IEEE 30th Annual Conference of the Industrial Electronics Society, IECON’04, 2-6 November, 2004, Busan, Korea.

• R. Attanasio, A. Consoli, M. Cacciato, F. Gennaro, A. Testa, G. Scarcella “ A Novel Converter System for Fuel Cell Distributed Energy Generation” 36th IEEE Power Electronics Specialist Conference, PESC’05, 12-16 June, 2005, Recife, Brazil.

•R. Attanasio, M. Cacciato, A. Consoli, F. Gennaro “ A Multi Stage Converter System for Domestic Generation Based on Fuel Cells’ 41st IEEE Industry Application Society Conference; IAS 06; October 8 -12, 2006 ; Tampa, Florida (USA).

•M. Cacciato, A. Consoli , R. Attanasio, F. Gennaro, G. Macina “ A digitally controlled double stage soft switching converter for grid connected photovoltaic applications”. 23rd IEEE Applied Power Electronics Conference and Exposition, APEC 08, February 24-28, 2008, Austin, Texas (USA).
