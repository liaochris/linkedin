My strengths include leadership experience, a solid team player, strategy development, and strong business acumen. I am multi-culturally diverse with international travel. Being able to adapt in different circumstances and condition is a very important component to success.

Some of my duties entailed maintain business operations through relationship building, negotiating advantageous deals, securing strategic domestic and international alliances, developing effective marketing campaigns, branding, and closing deals with top decision makers. I have also worked with multi-million dollar plans and goals so I experience in understanding the magnitude of big projects.

I have demonstrated the ability to listen to prospects’ needs, develop solutions to meet their goals, and build trust and respect. Culturally diversity is important to me and I have traveled to over 25 countries. Understanding people and their needs and goals are very important in doing business. Public speaking engagements that I have conducted are workshops, training, guest presenter, some of which over 1,000 people were in attendance.
