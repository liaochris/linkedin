Klaas Coolen’s Summary

Coolen Expertise B.V. is ranked #2 in the Loss Consulting market in The Netherlands.

A loss consultant or assessor is an expert you can call in when you want your interests as an insured taken care of in the event of loss or damage. If you suffer a loss due to an incident like fire, flooding or burglary, the amount you can claim will often be adjusted by a loss adjuster from your insurance company.

You do not really know what you are entitled to and so you can hardly check or challenge the adjuster's opinion. As your loss consultant I will proactively support you and will exclusively look after your interests.

At the moment we are number two on the Dutch market. My goal is to hold this position and to grow in a healthy way to the highest position.

Besides I guard our high level of quality and service and even better it.
