Nancy Solomon’s Summary

I create, develop, write and produce a wide range of persuasive and effective communications in all media for all kinds of businesses and organizations. I try to work for companies and people I respect. I measure my success by how well what I create does what it's supposed to do. I'm thrilled when it does even more. You can see samples of my work at www.hypeinc.com (I wrote the site and many of the projects in the portfolio section) and www.doneastburn.com (I wrote the DVDs/Videos for Rose and Sky Ridge medical cetners and wrote and produced the DVD/video for AAEP).

Nancy Solomon’s Specialties:

I make complicated information easy for people to understand. I can start with a huge amount of information or no information at all. My job is to persuade people to buy your product, use your service, support your cause, change their behavior, follow instructions, attend your event, join your organization, whatever. You name it, I encourage them to do it. I assemble and manage extraordinarily talented teams to create whatever you need.
