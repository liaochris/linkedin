Bill Clem’s Summary

Chief Design Officer and Founder of Sokets. Sokets is simple energy management service that empowers consumers to control and manage their energy use by allowing them to see energy as they've never seen it before. Sokets unleashes the creative ability of millions of everyday people to work together to solve our worlds energy problems through increased knowledge and efficiency.

Founder and CEO of StrategixVision. Bill has over 25 years experience in bringing technology products from the idea stage to market winning solutions for Johnson & Johnson, Polaris, Schwinn, Precor, Murphy, Philips, Heartstream, Zebco, Alaris, New Wave, Ethicon Endo and many startups. He has been an inventor or co-inventor on more than 25 utility patents and has led design teams to win numerous design awards, including 2 Gold & 2 Silver awards from Business Week/IDEA and has three products on permanent display in the Chicago Museum of Modern Art. Bill is on the advisory board of Western Washington University, has lectured to several design and marketing associations throughout the world and previously was an assistant professor at Art Center in Pasadena, California. A judge for the Consumer Electronics Show, Home Automation, Home Networking, Distributed Audio and Vehicle Audio 2000-2007.

His extensive knowledge of user centered design and brand centric innovation strategy have been the cornerstones for both his, and his company’s success. He has invested personally as well as through StrategixVision in several startups, mostly within the medical and cleantech sectors. His fundamental principles use Biomimicry for human interaction and system designs.

Bill Clem’s Specialties:

Conceptual business systems, user experience design, conceptual innovation, Industrial Design, cleantech and alternate energy systems, medical productization and technical product commercialization
