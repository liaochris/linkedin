Erin Collopy’s Summary

I'm a great team player who thrives in a creative, fast-paced, collaberative work environment. I also have a real passion for technology and enjoy working with early stage companies bringing new technologies to the marketplace and working on emerging technology areas.

I currently manage worldwide public relations and analyst relations for 2 groups within HP's Technology Solutions Group: HP Scalable Computing & Infrastructure (SCI) and HP Secure Advantage. In my role, I drive the AR/PR strategy and execution working with teams around the world.

HP SCI is chartered with bringing to market the next generation of scalable infrastructrure soutions for customers in cloud computing, Web 2.0, enterprise scale-out and high-performance computing. HP Secure Advantage is HP's portfolio of security offerings that span from the desktop to the datacenter.

Just before coming to HP, I managed PR programs overseeing teams of various sizes for several technology companies including: wireless sensor network chip pioneer Dust Networks (www.dustnetworks.com), hi-definition wireless audio chip maker STS wireless (www.sts-wireless.com), Wireless HD video chip maker Amimon (www.amimon.com) and helped drive a thought leadership program for tech lawfirm Fenwick & West (www.fenwick.com). Since joining Hoffman in 2005, I also led PR programs for SonicWALL, Penthera, Dimatix, eGain, iPass, NetXen, Visionael, SenSage, SRI International (formerly Stanford Research Institute) and Amazon.

Prior to joining the Hoffman Agency, I worked on the corporate marketing and PR side for 7 years across nearly every aspect of PR and marketing. My broad background includes: PR program development and execution, media, analyst, partner and customer relations, product marketing, email marketing, promotions, corporate websites, partner marketing and tradeshows. From 2002 to 2003, I worked from my companies' overseas office in Bangalore, India for 7 months and managed a team of 5 marketing professionals.
