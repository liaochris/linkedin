Sébastien Letélié’s Summary

Always on the lookout for new technologies related to the Internet, my leitmotiv is to find solutions useful and effective for users. R & D is essential for me to do business and all part of my job.
In 1996, I discovered the Internet, HTML and OOP with Java. I quickly make this my favourite area. In 1998 I choose Improve (www.improve.fr) for my internship, an innovative company that had made the choice of OOP and Java. It makes me sign a CDI from the 4th month of my internship of 6 months and I entered in the professional world in full-time.
After spending 4 years in service department with different types of users and applications, I decided to complete my education with a DRT TISI (Technological Research in Information Technology applied to industrial systems) in order to allow me adding to my skills a R & D practice. I chose "enterprise mobile computing" for research subject, I wrote a thesis (http://xdi.improve-technologies.com/memoire/memoire.html), and developed an open source framework: XDI (http://xdi.improve-technologies.com) allowing the synchronization of data between applications sharing common data types.
After that I finally chosen to devote myself in medical computing. The Improve's health department is booming thanks to software ResUrgences (www.resurgences.com : Medical Record Management in emergency department), I bring my skills to improve the product and especially the HIS (Hospital Information System ) integration .
Following the success of this solution I developed two products:
- ResEDIm: EAI dedicated to health systems
- ResUrgences SMUR: a solution for mobile emergency department : SMUR (Service Mobile d'Urgences et de Reanimation)
