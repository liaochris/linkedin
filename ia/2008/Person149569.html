Pete Kistler’s Summary

I'm driven by creating tools that allow people to spend more time on what they love - tools that are useful, simple, and have a meaningful impact on the way we work and play.

I'm currently focused on Brand-Yourself.com, a career development and online reputation management platform geared toward students, recent grads and young professionals. It allows users to stand out form the competition, unify their portfolio in one place, build a personal brand online and be found when Googled.

Pete Kistler’s Specialties:

Personal branding, online reputation management, career development, Web 2.0 (social networks, crowdsourcing, wikis and blogs), Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
