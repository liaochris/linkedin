Allison K. Watts’s Summary

Allison Watts has been the executive producer at WHAM-TV in Rochester, New York since November of 2004. Since her promotion, Allison produced a three hour live special on a Presidential Visit to Rochester. She also coordinated a live telethon to raise money for Hurricane Katrina victims which raised more than $1 million dollars for the Red Cross. Prior, Allison produced an hour-long 5pm newscast for five years. Since she was hired, her station has won four prestigious national Edward R. Murrow Awards for Best Newscast and one for Overall Excellence.

Prior to her news management position, Allison was a news producer at 10NBC in Rochester, New York. She worked as an associate producer before graduating from college. She holds a bachelor’s degree in Journalism and Communication from the State University of New York, College at Brockport. She was also the president of her sorority, Theta Phi Alpha.
