John Green’s Summary

We connect people and wine.

www.wineosaurus.com is a service to the community of greater Reno/sparks. From Lake Tahoe to Yerington and beyond. In the future we will be expanding nationwide.

We have participated in several charity wine events these past months and part of our plan is to assist planning, consult and do wine events and tasting for these charities, organizations, corporations and even
in home parties as we grow.

With the web log up now for a few months we are now getting information sent to us to post from our friends
and readers for the listings. We support the venues with promotion and exposure as another source of information, albeit in one place on the web and accessible 24/7 like our town.

The web site is a growing and evolving process we are adding new features all the time. We have some links to books and wine accessories people can purchase,

As one of my mentors, Dr. Robert Schuller, taught me in the early 70's “Find A Need , And Fill It, and make sure there is enough parking” In this case the need is a central listing of the available wine and food events and tastings and I think that Web 2.0 has enough parking...

The wine industry is growing and many new people are moving up in scale and pricing Hopefully we can provide for and facilitate that growth thru information, education,
and even begin to demystify an sometimes confusing cult and increase enjoyment.

Wine is our passion and that makes this a very exciting journey... We have a growing team of a half a dozen wine loving, sharing people all adding their expertise and input, as well as energy and zeal.

Drink more wine and have FUN...
