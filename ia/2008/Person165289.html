R R Dasgupta’s Summary

RR has spent over 20 years in the I.T industry. He has extensive exposure to Consulting, Sales, Operations, Profit centre and People Management. His training and consulting experience spans across Automotive, High Tech Manufacturing, Defense, Aerospace, Petroleum & Chemicals, Telecom to name a few. RR has worked in India as well as in the Middle East, U. K., U.S.A, China and South Africa.
