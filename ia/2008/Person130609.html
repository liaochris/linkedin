Michael Spadea’s Summary

I am a US licensed attorney with a broad legal experience focusing on global privacy and data protection issues. I previously worked in the Corporate Privacy Office at one of the largest retail banks in the United States for an internationally recognized in financial services privacy. Currently, I am active in drafting articles related privacy and information security for the American Bar Association.

I also have substantial experience in technology related matters including how the developments of new technologies affect the development of the law. I have defended numerous persons accused of copyright infringement by the RIAA. I am the current chair of the Connecticut Bar Association's Technology Law Section.

Finally, I have subtantial state and federal trial experience as well as substantial state appellate experience.

Michael Spadea’s Specialties:

Identifying and solving privacy and data protection issues in a global organization.
