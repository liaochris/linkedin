Mark Vigoroso’s Summary

Seasoned business development professional with 10+ years experience in enterprise technology research, early-stage product management, strategic marketing, entrepreneurial leadership, and sales growth. Leading domain expert in the evolution and adoption of service-based business models in discrete manufacturing industries.

Mark Vigoroso’s Specialties:

Software product management, industry marketing, solution selling, strategic alliance development, thought leadership (publishing, speaking, etc.) on emerging service & supply chain technology trends
