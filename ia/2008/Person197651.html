Vladimir Seroff’s Summary

I have more than 20 years of professional experience, of which 18 years in various roles servicing financial industry; banking; information architecture; business processes optimization using business intelligence tools.

I have extensive experience in managing large teams of professionals tasked with design and development of complex real time financial data analysis systems, including risk management systems, as well as analytical reporting systems for major Wall Street firms (including those aimed at achieving compliance with Basel II standards).

I have extensive experience in risk assessment methodologies in credit decision making process at various banks and financial institutions throughout the US and Europe.

I have had substantial experience in deployment of credit, market and operational risk assessment systems across a wide spectrum of financial institutions.

I successfully closed a number of sales of US Business Intelligence solutions to a number of financial and other organizations in the Russian Federation.

I have over 3 years experience managing the US branch of a Russian IT services and business intelligence solutions provider company, ZAO "Otido".

In 1999 with three other business partners I co-founded a successful enterprise, Aleri Group Inc. (formerly known as Metasoft, Inc.), currently providing world leading financial organizations with real time data streams processing solutions.

I have 7 years experience managing off-shore development teams in India and Russia.
