Phil Tapsell’s Summary

IPM-Cornwell operates at the heart of entrepreneurial life in the UK offering business critical services to technology investors and entrepreneurs. The company provides strategic consulting services to new technology companies to help them make the difficult leap from early adopter to early majority products and beyond.

IPM-Cornwell’s success is measured by the value they add to the enterprise projects they work with in terms of their ability to survive and grow thereafter.

The primary client base for IPM-Cornwell are venture capitalists and other investors who have funded technology companies and their products. Such investors often need to deploy highly qualified business strategy consultants, such as those employed by IPM-Cornwell, into the businesses they are funding so as to maximise the chance of a high return on their investment. Such core clients are normally sophisticated, savvy, wealthy and technology literate.

A secondary client base for IPM-Cornwell are technology company CEOs and managing directors who realise the need for strategy and an ability to communicate effectively with the investor community.
