Stephen Skelly’s Summary

I have 5 years of web and graphic design experience including HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, Flash, etc. I have built and maintain a large number of websites please check out my portfolio site for more information on myself and my work.

In the last 2 years i have been promoting and repping shows for bands such as Reverend and the Makers, Reuben, Nine Black Alps, Eighties Matchbox B-line Disaster and Elliot minor to name a few; while working for Vman Events. I have also been an influential part of the Blackpool music scene over the 5 years and also perform in a band.

Last summer I worked at a lot of music festivals for 'Flair Events' where I worked worked myself up to the rank of Bar Manager and managed the Artists bar at V-festival in Staffordshire.

In the next few years I plan to pursue a career in Web Design/Development but with the eventual aim of starting up my own independent (primarily web based) record label or promotions company.
