Arnaud Collery’s Summary

Writer, actor, stand up comedian since 2004 in Los Angeles now producing/acting in my own mockumentary around the world during the whole year 2008.

You will see me on LAST COMIC STANDING season 6 during summer 2008 as the FIRST French guy doing comedy in USA since 1950.

Business Background.
His early education was in the field of international finance and banking and quickly worked in brokerage, offshore banking, financial advising, MandA all around the world. Paris, Gibraltar, Tokyo, HK, New York.
His last finance job was in 2001 when he decided to lead the investor relations team for a European Stock Exchange, EuroNext in Paris

Government and Politics
Graduated as a military officer (EIREL) and in international law (American U of Paris) he worked in politics in Brussels for the European Union researching corruption and financial crime under the leadership of Judge Thierry Jean Pierre

Luxury goods
Family history in the Champagne business, stints in the cosmetic sector and as a communication executive for Cartier in Japan

Entertainment Field
Moliere school of acting in Paris, some tv hosting in Paris, then..
In May 07, after three years in Hollywood he has directed 24 local tv shows, 2 shorts movies and has appeared over 200 times on stage doing stand up comedy. Now a regular at the comedy store and the improv in Los Angeles. In 2008 you can see him on MTV and E Entertainment Networks.
The two movies he produced in 2006 were selected as part of the competition at the MONACO FILM FESTIVAL in May 2007.

When times allows I lecture about motivation, passion and perseverance.
Those two passions of mine acting/writing and motivational speaking are in two different categories and I have not mixed them until now. Time will tell if I can merge them.

I am also keeping a close look on what s happening with the technology sector in terms of mobile and new social networking content as I helped several entrepreneurs over the years.
