Dan Boaz’s Summary

C-LEVEL EXECUTIVE: MARKETING, SALES & OPERATIONS

Dan Boaz heads up the leading Air Freight & Logistics Domain AirFreight.com. His background includes over 20 years of successful transportation experience, most recently as President. Having managed a Freight Forwarding Business, Boaz recognizes the need to balance the demands of sales and operations - and meet the needs of customers and employees, while monitoring the bottom line. Dan is so dedicated to providing customer value.

Boaz’s industry knowledge, strong work ethic, organizational skills and emphasis on integrity, confidentiality and strong personal relationships enables him to provide Executive Recruiting services exclusively for the Freight Forwarding and Logistics Industry. Boaz believes that the Expedited Freight Industry is always changing and that well-managed organizations require talented executives and employees, who can successfully manage and adapt to change - in order to gain new sales, improve operations and drive more profits.

• Accomplished Entrepreneur and Executive Leader with a strong portfolio of successes orchestrating the start-up, growth, and optimization of diverse businesses within online services, Transportation & Logistics, and Executive Recruiting, Competitive Branding, Franchising, and Growing a Company from an Idea to Reality.

• Orchestrated “Key” Brand Marketing Campaign by elevating an Industry website to “The Most Powerful Air Freight Domains on the Internet”, resulting in AirFreight.com to # 1 on Google and Yahoo.
