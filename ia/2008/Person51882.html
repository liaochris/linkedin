dipak sahoo’s Summary

Over a decade of experience in the insurance and financial services industry, having played multifarious roles on both the demand and supply side in the areas of strategic account management, relationship management with C level executives, regional solution delivery in the APAC region and IT functional leadership for top insurance companies globally. Mostly India-based engagements but with very good international exposure and experience, understands how major insurance companies apply technology to compete both tactically and strategically.

Joined Bharti AXA Life as a founder member and head of IT to help set up country operation for AXA to enable their business launch. At BALIC he was instrumental in migrating the suite of core business applications used by AXA in the SEA region and also establishing a national presence for BALIC through 77 proprietary offices in the first year of operation.
Prior to AXA, he was Vice-President IT with WNS Global services, the largest BPO in the country and was heading their IT for Insurance and Enterprise Services Vertical. He has also worked extensively on capital market solutions and has been instrumental in setting up some of the well known e-trading sites in India. He has also worked as a management consultant with PWC on some of their change management and BPR projects, consulting on behalf of clients like World Bank and DFID, UK. Dipak has got extensive application development and implementation experience in the areas of HRIS(Peoplesoft, Adrenalin), DMS/BPM(G360,Filenet), CRM(Onyx,Talisma), Datawarehousing, transactional portals and other revenue generating and customer service enhancement applications.
Dipak has been instrumental in driving the global best practices from AIG and AXA in their local business.
He is a certified Project Management professional (PMI) and a Fellow of Life Management Institute, US (FLMI).
