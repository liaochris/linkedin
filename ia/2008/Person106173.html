Noel Livick’s Summary

I have a very practical, delivery-focussed Project Management style, with a natural focus on a no-panic collaborative approach to ensuring project outcomes and team performance.

I have over twenty-five years IT experience working in a wide variety of hardware and application environments in NZ, Australia, UK, USA, China, Hong Kong and Chile.
Successful in Project, Regional and QA Management positions, and in senior consulting, design, build and implementation roles, I am valued for my customer relationship skills and Project Management record.

I am currently working as Project Manager at Housing NZ. Prior to this assignment, as Solutions Delivery Manager at Transpower NZ, I lead the successful delivery of the very complex multi-million dollar infrastructure transformation, plus major software upgrades to the core SCADA business systems.

Earlier, at IRD: as a senior Project Manager, I was responsible for the IT/Systems development on the Working for Families Phase II project, to meet the 2006 legislative requirements.

At Statistics NZ: as one of three Strategic Programme Managers: delivery of three new Programmes of work involving 12 projects with multi-million dollar budgets, as SNZ transitioned toward a Data Warehouse & Metadata driven organization, and undertook a three year Business Model Transformation.

The Simpl Group: Project Manager responsible for all aspects of developing the Schools Based Vaccination System for the Ministry of Health - a critical part of the Ministry’s strategy to undertake nationwide Meningoccocal immunisation campaigns during 2004/2005.

Westpac NZ: full IT Project Management responsibility for the $8m design, build & implementation of the new WebTeller application.

National Bank of NZ: implemented several large IT Projects.

IBM Australia: Results Systems Manager for the Sydney 2000 Olympic Games.
