Edward Borden’s Summary

My career in the PC industry has taken me from one end of the spectrum to the other. I've created and managed a start-up corporation in which I built a brand and a line of computers which sold into multiple niche markets. I designed and hand-built hundreds of PC's of different types, and assembled and trained a sales team to sell them. I've designed cutting edge solutions for the Content Creation market (for 3D, Digital Video, and storage), the super-dense, ultra-hyped gaming market, and the general consumer market. I've dealt intimately with every type of computer, from mass produced corporate machines, to boutique hotrod desktops, to advanced multi-processor servers and rackmounts. I've pounded the pavement, been down in the trenches direct selling both B2B and B2C solutions and commodities. I've been the customer, the salesman, the employee, the boss, big business, small business ... and I can cook a mean Penne Vodka e Pollo Vino Bianco.
