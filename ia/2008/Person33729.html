Charles Ryder’s Summary

I am Charles Ryder, a highly experienced international management consultant specialising in project and programme management. I have successfully delivered PRINCE and PRINCE2, MSP, PMI, ISEB, APM and generic project management course internationally for twenty years as well as project and programme management and consultancy. I also have solid experience in international HR management.

You can contact me at charles.ryder@kennedyryder.com

Kennedy Ryder Sdn Bhd is based in Kuala Lumpur to service SE Asia, where we concentrate on CMMI accreditations and process improvement; ITIL; project and programme management training including PMI and PRINCE2; interim IT management, establishing Project Offices; Management of Risk (MoR); and the selection and installation of electronic project management tools. My role is to select and supply contract and permanent staff to our SE Asia clients.
