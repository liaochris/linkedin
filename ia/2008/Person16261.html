Aron Smetana’s Summary

Aron has over fifteen years of technology experience ranging from hands-on systems administration, network management, and web site creation to senior IT management and strategic planning. He is a technology evangelist by nature, looking to improve processes and find new opportunities using technology.

His nature is to figure out the processes, even if unfamiliar, and look for opportunities to improve them, not just using technology, but with a varied background he is able to dissect the situation and find creative solutions.

Aron's background includes consumer electronics, large retail and manufacturing, financial services, and public higher education including support for a medical school and research professors. He is able to draw from the collection of experiences to find new opportunities and unique ways to improve things. Higher education and considerable international work gave him a unique insight into understanding people with widely different and often disparate interests and needs. The public university experience in particular required diplomacy and sensitivity to a widely varying audience in a collaborative environment that was still governed by state requirements. The retail and consumer electronics experience instilled a high level of customer service that he has carried with him to other fields.

In technology, Aron is well-versed in the current and emerging social networking services and is always looking for ways to effectively integrate these and other emerging technologies into the organization. To be the most effective with the new technologies, transparency will be key for continued growth.
