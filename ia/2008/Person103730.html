Matt Kropp’s Summary

I first started building and programming computers in 1976, and have been doing it ever since. As a result, if I'm not already an expert in some area of computers, I can be in a very short period of time.

A quick look at my job experience will show that I have a very broad range of experience in electronics, science, and computers. In addition to that experience I also have hobbyist experience with 3D modeling, ray tracing, radiosity rendering, graphic design, photo editing, video production, sound and music production, and building, playing, and recording on analog and digital music synthesizers.
