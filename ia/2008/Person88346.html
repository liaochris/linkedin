Jennifer A. Long’s Summary

For the past 5+ years, I have been working as a search marketing professional. I have worked with companies large and small, and as an independent consultant. I have experience with PPC, SEO, Banner design and implementation, basic HTML design, ad trafficking, marketing copy writing for print, web and SEO, press release copy writing, budget management, database reporting design, marketing and financial analytics and projections, social networking marketing, link building and lead generation.

I have worked in several marketing verticals including legal, online education, campus education, real estate, IT and web design services.

My education has focused primarily on writing and literature and other liberal arts, although I have also studied engineering and architectural design. Marketing has been my primary career focus, although writing is and always will be my first love.

I have published short fiction, essays and web content and I hope to publish my work as novels and nonfiction essay anthologies in the future.
