M. David Green’s Summary

I'm a web developer, publisher, and consultant in San Francisco. Online networking has been a strong interest of mine since I wrote my thesis on the subject in 1986, in a self-styled Social Sciences major program at U.C. Berkeley. I did my research on cutting edge technology at the time; private BBS systems set up by folks on their home telephone lines with 300-1200 BAUD modems. What I saw showed me that the human instinct to network is vital enough to thrive in any medium which allows one person to connect to another. I've worked in several industries since graduating, and applied this learning to everything I do.

M. David Green’s Specialties:

I've done writing, data modeling, graphic design, print layouyt, web traffic analysis, marketing, sales, video production, and any number of intricate, goal-specific projects. My real passion seems to be getting my fingers dirty mucking with code, new technology, visual design, and metrics analysis. I also really enjoy developing and learning techniques for increasing the signal-to-noise ratio of online social networking.
