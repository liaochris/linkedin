Karl Gilis’s Summary

- Active in the internet industry from 1996. Started as a web builder but soon moved to online publishing. Because income (from ads) was related to number of visitors, usability was a key issue.
- In 2001 I started AGConsult together with Els Aerts. AGConsult focuses completely on web usability and information architecture an we're without a doubt the specialists in that domain in Belgium.

Clients include: Vlerick, Telenet, Fortis, AXA, ABN-AMRO, Mobistar, AstraZeneca, Flemish government, Belgium government, Daikin, Wolters Kluwer, Nexans, Microsoft, Honeywell, Renault, Telenor, Bridgestone, Spector, ...

Karl Gilis’s Specialties:

- Information architecture and requirements analysis
- Usability and user testing
- Visitor behaviour analysis, logfile analysis, webmetrics.
- Newsletter usability
- Search engine optimisation
