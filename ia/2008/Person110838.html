Kim Larson’s Experience

  • Founder and CEO

    Larson-Marvine, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Design industry)

    January 1989 — Present (19 years 10 months)

    Larson-Marvine specializes in consulting engagements with a focus on high-level marketing and communication design in the following areas:
    • Corporate identity, branding, marketing, and advertising design
    • New product development and launches with an emphasis on interactive, rich media and streaming video
    • Website design including graphical user interface (GUI) design, information architecture, applications, and programming
    • Online content development, streaming video, web-based e-learning applications, sales training/incentive programs, business simulations, and interactive games
    • Broadcast television and online video program branding: package design, opening animation, sets, and promotion

    Larson-Marvine seeks to develop long-term relationships with clients and often becomes an integral part of the internal team. We have established relationships with international corporations, not-for-profit institutions, and new venture business launches.

  • Design Director

    WLS-TV (ABC 7 Chicago)

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    1984 — 1988 (4 years)

    Responsible for directing the visual identity and design of WLS-TV programming, promotion, and corporate communications. Implemented digital technologies for television graphic production. Coordinated the design and visual element production for the launch and promotion of:
    • The Oprah Winfrey Show
    • The 28th Annual Chicago Emmy Awards
    • Chicago’s 150th Birthday
    • Eyewitness News
    • Say No to Drugs
    Partner in establishing in-house agency to create all station print and on-air advertising and promotion. Part of the team that elevated the station market position from 5th place to 1st place in 18 months.
