Kress Franzen MCP CCNA’s Summary

Professionally, I started as a computer technician, and have worked various support, design, engineering, sales and project management roles over the past 10 years. I have designed complete small business networks from the ground up, and I have worked on teams responsible for networks spanning many states. I have a keen sense for logical organization which makes me a strong troubleshooter and project manager. My study of economics has been very enlightening, and I feel that I bring a broader perspective than most technologists with whom I have worked. I have solid communication skills, and I am very comfortable in sales and sales-engineering roles. I often am able to identify opportunities and bring potential customers to the table to create new business opportunities.

I have recently accepted a new job in the Seattle, Washington area, where my wife and I will be moving in September 2008. Thank you to the many who assisted me in my job search with advice or recommendations!

I am an avid learner, and I seek to be meticulous in all that I do, which characteristics I feel make me a great asset to all the companies for which I have worked, as well as in my own endeavors. I love technology, music and business, and I love to see a great deal come together. I enjoy connecting my associates with opportunities, and I am always open to new ventures.
