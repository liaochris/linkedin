Christian Marzy’s Summary

My interest and enthusiasm in digital images lead me to engage with appropriate software. The first visual projects inspired me to go deeper into programs like Photoshop, Bryce, Cinema 4D and 3ds Max. Several internships in this area supported me to get an insight on daily work with these programs.

With the registration of my own business in 2002 I extended my hobby into a profession and completed numerous 2D-projects. Along the way I expanded my 3D-skills at home. I chose 3ds Max because of its many tutorials that came with it.

In 2007 I passed the course "The World of 3D Professionals" succesfully at the German Film School which is congruent with the 2nd term. In this course I had the possibility to finish a 5 minutes 3D-soloproject in Maya and extensive exchange with the other students. During my time at the German Film School I learned how to use programs like ZBrush, After Effects, Combustion and XSI. In addition to that we created 2 Live-Action projects and several CG-animations in different teams.

Christian Marzy’s Specialties:

Digital Artist with a great portfolio of 3D-Short-Movies and professional skills in Autodesk Maya / 3DS Max, Softimage XSI, Adobe Photoshop / After Effects, Story Developing, Moddeling, Rigging, Shading, Lighting, Post Production
