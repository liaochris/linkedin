Rakesh Rathore’s Experience

  • Researcher

    University of Cincinnati

    (Educational Institution; 10,001 or more employees; Higher Education industry)

    January 2008 — Present (10 months)

    • Working in collaboration with MDS Analytical Technologies, Canada on development and integration of MALDI-MS based high throughput screening (HTS) technologies, and exploring the possibility to use Instrument (Flashouant-MALDI-QqQ) for different HTS applications.

    • Testing High Throughput Screening (HTS) technologies for wide ranges of enzymes classes (Kinases, Proteases, Phophatases etc.) as multiplexed reaction to opens the possibility to exploit a single primary screens through a compound repository to get “hits” for multiple therapeutic targets.
