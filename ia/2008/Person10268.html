Ananya Roy’s Summary

Ananya Roy, he’s got 10 years experience producing creative for the IT industry – a career spanning from visualization and computer graphics at 123grettings.com, to the role of Team Leader at GST India – and now Ananya Roy is choosing to put his talents to use in USA.

“I’ve completely fallen in love with USA. My first visit here was meant to last two weeks, and I ended up staying two months. I feel more at home here than I’ve felt anywhere before.”

Ananya is the CMO and Founder Member of Virtuoso Analytic Services Pvt. Ltd., a company specializing in Web Application Systems based in India with a market shares in USA. Ananya’s success in his role and contribution to the industry were recognized when he was invited to participate in the Pubcon 2008 at Las Vegas.
Ananya recognizes it’s the friendly, helpful and hardworking people in the USA market that make it what it is. “Most people now know about the incredible beauty and diversity of the American landscape. But it’s the people, the work ethic, and the fun-loving approach here that are especially great for business.”

But it’s not just the people who draw Ananya to USA. “The lifestyle is amazing. It feels like it was when I was growing up in childhood. There’s a sense of prosperity and promise - people feel they have the freedom to do what they want in life.”

Roy has a strong sense of entrepreneurship, networking and client communication. He specializes in search engine optimization. He is a proficient businessman with an eye for detail and thinks that God is great and he owes his success to Him and his family.
