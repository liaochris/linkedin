Douglas Hardin’s Summary

I enjoy the challenges I receive as the Facilities and Security Program Support Analyst contractor to the Air Force Research Laboratory, Optical Radiation Branch, Brooks City-Base Texas. I am responsible for the physical security and maintenance of our facilities as well as assisting the Government Security Managers in maintaining the Information Security programs.

I am self-motivated to find and fix minor problems before they become major issues. I take pride in my integrity and in providing top-notch service to my customers by instilling excellence in all that I do.
