William C. Head’s Summary

A national attorney rating site, www.AVVO.com, has currently placed the legal profile of William C. Head as the #1 attorney in any type practice among nearly 1.3 million listings, based on multiple "rating" criteria. Mr. Head was named the 2003-2004 “National DUI Lawyer of the Year”. He was one of the original 12 Regents who created the National College for DUI Defense, Inc. (www.NCDD.com), in 1995. He is Board Certified in DUI Defense by the NCDD. Mr. Head has authored or co-authored 12 books, several chapters of other books, and numerous articles on DUI-related issues and Criminal Trial Practice. His books for lawyers include the best-selling book on DUI law ever published, 101 Ways to Avoid a Drunk Driving Conviction (©1991 co-authored with the late Reese I. Joye, Jr.) and The Georgia DUI Trial Practice Manual (© Thomson-West Publishing Company 2001-2008). Mr. Head also authored a national book for accused citizens facing a DUI charge, The DUI Book (©2006 Headlines Marketing Corp.). He has also co-authored books based on The DUI Book with attorneys from other states.

Atlanta Magazine (March, 2001), Mr. Head was named Atlanta’s "best DUI lawyer" based upon a survey of Georgia attorneys. He has made every Georgia “SuperLawyers” list ever published. He is the only Georgia DUI criminal defense attorney named in a national directory of the top 3% of all attorneys, Best Lawyers in America. Mr. Head and his firm are "av" rated (highest) with Martindale-Hubbell. Mr. Head stepped down from this duty a year later, due to his trial commitments. Mr. Head also holds "Life Member" status in the NACDL, GACDL, and SCACDL. Mr. Head is a provisional member of the International Association for Chemical Testing (IACT) as well as being a jurisprudential member of the American Academy of Forensic Scientists (AAFS). Mr. Head also was a speaker www.icadts2007.org at the International Conference held in Seattle, WA in 2007. Member of Vistage CEO Group 177.
