Parviz Yegani, Ph.D.’s Summary

Dr. Parviz Yegani is currently a Technical Leader of Carrier Standards and Architecture Group at Cisco. He has more than 20 years of industrial experience in telecomunications and networking industries working at IBM, QUALCOMM, ERICSSON and CISCO.

Dr. Yegani is an expert in CDMA, IP, and ATM and is intimately familiar with 3G and NGN architectures and protocols. Currently, he leads various projects within Cisco focusing on mobile network design, system architecture evolution and network harmonization across different access technologies (3GPP, 3GPP2, WiFi, WiMAX).

He interfaces with major customers on a regular basis and leads Cisco in various standards bodies such as 3GPP2, TIA, WiMAX and IETF. He has chaired a number of subcommittees and working groups including TIA TR-45.6 Subcommittee, MWIF IP RAN and 3GPP2 TSG-A Architecture Working Groups.

Parviz Yegani, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

-> Mobile Networks: Extensive knowledge of architectures and protocols for mobile networks (3G and beyond) with paricular emphasis on CDMA HRPD, UMB, 3GPP SAE/LTE, WiMAX, IP RAN evolution, RAN-Core inter-operability and 3G-WiFi interworking.
-> Networking Protocols: IP Mobility (Mobile IP and Proxy MIP), QoS (RSVP, NSIS, DiffServ, IntServ), Security (IPsec, EAP), AAA (DIAMETER, RADIUS), DHCP and more.
