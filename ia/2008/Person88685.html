Jennifer Rushton’s Summary

What is it that initially attracts clients to Jennifer’s business? For new clients, it is her certifications within the industry, TORI (Toast of the Resume Writing Industry) Awards, international publishing credits in over 15 leading resume books by the industry’s leading resume writing professions such as Wendy Enelow, Louise Kursmark and David Noble, and her affiliation with international resume writing associations.

For existing clients and referrals, it’s her proven results in positioning them for job search success by creating personal branded resumes that build market visibility through her in-depth information gathering process. Client’s refer to Jennifer’s resume writing talents as ‘golden’ as they are key to securing the interviews that lead to job search success.

Jennifer’s success is attributed to her in‐depth understanding of what employers look for in a candidate and her talent for transforming a ‘mind-numbing’ resume into a unique and individually crafted marketing document modelled on client skills and experience where their ROI comes in the form of faster job search, a clearer understanding of their value in the market place, and the building of a personal brand. Jennifer has an innate ability for clearly communicating a candidate’s ‘true value’ to a prospective employer.

As a Certified Advanced Résumé Writer (CARW), Certified Employment Interview Consultant (CEIC), and Certified Web Portfolio Practitioner (CWPP), Jennifer has been writing résumés from entry level applicants to executives both in Australia and internationally. She specialises in highlighting clients skills and accomplishments into dynamic marketing documents that secure interviews and lead to job success through personally branded résumés, as branded résumés are a much more personalised and powerful approach to personal marketing than traditional résumés .
