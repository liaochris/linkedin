Craig Shaver, RAA’s Summary

Licensed IL Real Estate Broker with more than 10 years of sales experience.

Certified IL Residential Appraiser since 1997 with more than 5,000 field inspections completed in the Chicago metropolitan market area.

Owner of CMS Property Services, Inc. and nationwide collateral valuation specialist with more than 50,000 narrative valuations, appraisal review and market analysis reports completed in all 50 states for pre-fund risk mitigation, post-fund due diligence and secondary market portfolio transfers.

Designer of CMS’ custom collateral valuation and inspection reports utilized by banks, brokers, wholesale lenders and secondary market clients, nationwide.

Craig Shaver, RAA’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge and experience assisting the repurchase process for lenders and government entities nationwide by providing detailed retrospective forensic appraisal reviews.
