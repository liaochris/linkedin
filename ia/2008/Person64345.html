Marcellas Flenory Sr.’s Summary

Marcellas Flenory Sr. is a Principal Consultant at Flenory & Associates, a Texas-based Human Resources (HR) and Organization Development (OD) Consulting firm. He has over nine years of experience in the HR Management and OD field within start-up businesses, software development companies, big box retailers, non-profit agencies, governmental entities, and staff augmentation and recruiting industry. Mr. Flenory has vast exposure to HR and OD functions, processes, and procedures from its infancy. He has in-depth knowledge of U.S. and International VISA and immigration laws. Mr. Flenory is also a Master Trainer (Train the Trainer), Motivational Speaker, and Professional and Personal Coach.

Mr. Flenory holds a Bachelors of Science degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Human Resources Management from California State University, Sacramento. In addition, Mr. Flenory holds a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) degree in Human Resources Management from the University of Dallas, Graduate School of Management.
