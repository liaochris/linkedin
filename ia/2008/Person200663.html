Wayne Kulick, PMP, IPRC [LION][1500+]’s Summary

Wayne J. Kulick a Senior Program Manager for LGS. LGS is the wholly owned subsidiary of Alcatel-Lucent dedicated to serving the U.S. federal government market.

A retired Naval Officer, Mr. Kulick joined LGS in 2007 after his 25 year career in the Navy. Mr. Kulick came to LGS from the National Reconnaissance Office in Chantilly, VA where he served as a Deputy Division Chief in the Operations Group within the Communications Directorate. Prior to that, Mr. Kulick served as a Program Manager in the SIGINT Applications and Integration Office within the Signals Intelligence Directorate. Mr. Kulick is the recipient of the National Reconnaissance Office Bronze Medal for outstanding service.

Mr. Kulick brings a wealth of experience to LGS, having served in the past as a Signals Intelligence Pilot (EP-3), Catapult Launch & Arresting Gear Officer (“Shooter”), Information Systems Officer, Nuclear Propulsion Plant Supervisor and Mathematics Instructor. Notable accomplishments include selection as an Associate Fellow for the Chief of Naval Operations Strategic Studies Program and selection for the Naval Postgraduate School Center for Executive Education creative thinking course.

Mr. Kulick is a certified Project Manager Professional (PMP) with the Project Management Institute, completing Lean Six Sigma Master Black Belt Certification with Villanova University, a certified Program Manager and COTR with the National Reconnaissance Office, a DAWIA certified Program Manager (II) and a certified ITIL Practitioner in Release and Control (IPRC). Mr. Kulick holds multiple degrees to include a MS in Astronautical Engineering from the Naval Postgraduate School, a BS in Computer Science from the University of North Florida and a BS in Electronics Management from Southern Illinois University at Carbondale.

Mr. Kulick is a member of the Project Management Institute, American Society for Quality, American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics, and AFCEA.
