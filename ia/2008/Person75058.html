Harris Brandt’s Summary

Results-oriented advertising/marketing leader with standout accomplishments in developing breakthrough advertising strategies and campaigns across all media. My record of creative achievement includes:

- Creation and supervision of dozens of successful marketing campaigns and programs that beat long-standing controls and generated hundreds of millions of dollars in revenues in subsequent rollouts.

- Member of executive management team that formulated seasonal strategic plans as well as specific promotional themes for upcoming marketing campaigns.

- Development of successful launch programs for many high-profile products including AT&T Universal Card, Bloomingdale's by Mail, and Cablevision's iO Digital Cable Service.

My clients and co-workers will tell you that I am an inspirational director, manager and mentor ... strong strategic planner ... accomplished writer and designer ... and overall good guy to work with. I pride myself on being a lead-by-example team builder, with the ability to manage, train, and motivate others to develop innovative creative solutions to marketing challenges.
