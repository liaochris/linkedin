Cinda Baxter’s Summary

My personal mantra is “Pay it forward,” recognizing that the way we each become successful is by helping those around us do the same.

As the previous owner of a successful upscale retail boutique in Minneapolis for fourteen years, I look back and recognize that role was merely the launching pad for what has become a fruitful bounty of experience and knowledge. My insatiable curiosity, mixed with the ability to learn quickly and the belief that nothing is impossible, has allowed me to collaborate with some of the brightest minds in the field while continuing to produce 120% of what’s expected.

In my world, success is a journey, not a destination. Why be average when exceptional will do?

Cinda Baxter’s Specialties:

Professional speaking, writing, consulting, creating branding and identity focus for independent retailers, buying, customer service, freelance graphic design, self-taught webmaster (none of the template stuff, thankyouverymuch), founder of three successful online industry communities
