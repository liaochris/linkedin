Márcia Lunardi’s Summary

I am an Information Architect and Interaction Designer within 10 years experience in internet projects. I have great hands-on background through the entire product design process from concept, IA, graphic design to implementation.

I recently got my masters degree in Design and Technology at ESDI UERJ. According to BusinessWeek magazine, the best design school in Latin America and one of the best D-Schools in the World in its 2007 list. My BA is in Communication Studies with emphasis in Advertising.

I am a designer who believe that human goals and desires should be the driving force behind technological and design innovation, but who can at the same time work pragmatically within real-world constraints and solve detailed interface problems within a cross-functional, collaborative work environment.

Love to collaborate with software engineering to ensure the successful execution of user interface designs and the preservation of design intent in the final product.

I am willing to work within an agile/scrum release approach within a company of talented individuals.
