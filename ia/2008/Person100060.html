Julian Williams’s Summary

The majority of my experience has been around unix environments along with the various open source software / projects. I enjoy LAMP based development and have more recently been heavily exposed to Ajax and MVC orientated design/development as the supporting community of users and developers has grown and matured.

I'm keen to expand my technical management experience, However I'm also keen to stay involved with technology and build on my experience. I have a particular interest in music & video distribution and have been involved in building high volume services distributing content using various delivery mechanisms.

I have a keen interest in the usability and aesthetic of interface and application design and would love to more regularly combine my appreciation of these issues with the technology, some thing i feel is becoming more important and relevant in web and software development.
