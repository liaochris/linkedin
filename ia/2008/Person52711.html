Denise Moore’s Summary

Since its inception in 2005, the client list for DMSearch has included major companies such as Masco Corporation (NYSE; $13B) as well as boutique executive search firms like CES Group, DHI Partners and Flannery and Associates. Denise applies over fifteen years of experience and a collaborative, proactive style to managing all aspects of the candidate identification and development process. She is a widely-recognized, respected authority on current research and executive search methodologies.

Prior to launching her own firm in 2005, Denise served as Vice President Recruitment and Human Resources for Source One Network Solutions. Before joining Source One, Denise was responsible for building the executive search practice for HRValue Group, a human resources consulting firm serving the credit union industry.

The bulk of Denise’s experience was gained at DHR International, where she served as Regional Vice President. In that role, she executed senior-level search assignments and successfully managed recruiters in several regional offices. She also developed and implemented a recruiter training program that was adopted nationwide for DHR. Denise previously served as Vice President of Research, Director of Research-Milwaukee, and senior consultant for DHR. During her tenure with DHR International, Moore participated on the firm's research advisory board and was instrumental in the development of the firm’s proprietary candidate tracking software system. Moore began her career at a Wisconsin-based executive recruiting firm, Jonas, Walters and Assoc.

Denise holds a bachelor's degree from Wright State University in Dayton, Ohio, where she graduated Magna Cum Laude; and she has done additional coursework at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee. Before embarking on an executive search career, Denise worked as an entertainer, performing in musical theater productions in a variety of venues in the Midwest.
