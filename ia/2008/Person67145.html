Gail Sussman Miller’s Summary

I teach solopreneur women and women-owned small businesses how to love business obstacles, like networking, speaking and selling, and overcome them with ease. The outcome is people move from fear to action through compassion.

They learn to promote and operate their businesses as a natural extension of who they are and what they love to do.

I use a 4-step approach to accomplish this. I help women solopreneurs and teams:
~~ Get really clear about what they truly want
~~ Overcome "yes, but" thinking,
~~ Make inspired choices, and
~~ Take action through small steps.

Signature training programs, led in person or by telephone include:
~~ How to Love Networking (for those who are shy, introverted, or lack confidence)
~~ How to Love Speaking (for in-house presentations, workshops, marketing)
~~ How to Love Selling (for those who avoid follow-up calls and sales conversations)
~~ Women's tele-group to learn hard-earned marketing secrets customized to you

OFFERING:
* For women solopreneurs who are serious about creating positive change and overcoming business obstacles to grow their business, I am offering a 45-minute complimentary "Business Obstacle-Busting Session" to help you get more clear about where you are, where you'd like to be, and what steps you can take to get started to get there.
* Introductions to open doors within my network

SEEKING:
* Warm introductions to leaders in women's organizations and women's diversity and networking groups within companies to help inspire their members through one of my obstacle busting workshops
* Connections with women owners of small businesses to do some non-traditional deep impact training to improve communications, proactive work habits, and innovative action.
* Women professionals who are working primarily one-on-one with clients and wish to reach more people with less effort to achieve higher earnings and greater impact through public speaking.

Gail Sussman Miller’s Specialties:
