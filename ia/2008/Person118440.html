Malcolm Atherton’s Summary

I started with Business Wire in August 2004 and am based in our full-service Arizona newsroom (1 of 24 across the US).

My goals as a Business Wire account executive are to provide the best and most cost-effective options for news distribution, to provide sound counsel to clients & prospects, and to offer unmatched customer service.

Additionally, I am one of Business Wire's New Media Specialists. This internal team is responsible for assisting colleagues, clients, and prospects with questions on our suite of SEO services. We also work to optimize the integration of the Web/emerging media into corporate communications & the traditional press release.

I have represented Business Wire at the 2005 WorldCom International Conference in Scottsdale, 2006 PRSA International Conference in Salt Lake City, and the BIO2007 Conference in Boston.
