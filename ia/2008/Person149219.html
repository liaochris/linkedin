Gregory Masi’s Summary

Gregory Masi has a diverse background. Growing up in Rhode Island, he was always fascinated by the different types of weather the region would receive, and decided to pursue an education in meteorology. While at Penn State, Gregory participated in every aspect of the field, from forecasting and research to teaching and broadcasting. He decided that teaching and research seemed to be the right fit, and this decision led him to UCLA. While at UCLA, Gregory Masi pursued a Master's degree in Atmospheric Science. During this time, he taught Introductory Meteorolgy, Climate Change, and other course devoted to the sciences. His love for teaching continued as he also tutored high school and college students in Calculus and advanced math.

While working on his Master's at UCLA, Gregory began to visit weekend open houses to satisfy his interest in the local real estate market. What began as a hobby and side interest soon began to evolve into a career path. He continued to work on his Master's degree and simultaneously studied for his real estate license. Once he received his license, he began speaking with real estate offices across the Westside. Thereafter, he began working with one of the top Coldwell Banker offices, and started to assist clients in the buying and selling process. He noticed that being a REALTOR was very much synonomous with being an educator. As a real estate professional, he recognized how crucial it was to educate and prepare your clients for the buying and selling process. Gregory Masi embraced a full time career in real estate, and continues to give his clients the attention and service they deserve.

Aside from working in real estate, and having a passion for weather phenomena, Gregory Masi also enjoys following college sports, playing tennis, traveling, and spending time with families and friends.
