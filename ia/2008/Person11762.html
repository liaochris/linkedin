Andrew MacGill’s Summary

While only in his 20's Andrew has rapidly demonstrated his entrepreneurial passion and skill by successfully launching his second startup. Andrew's expertise is in the design and execution of successful marketing strategies and the development and integration of technology.

His first startup (ASM Group... developed during his junior year at the University of Illinois at Chicago) demonstrated his strong organizational skills, which resulted in a profit for ASM in its first 6 months of operation.

He has recently turned his attention to co-founding PeerSight, an internet based company serving the growth needs of small business. In his role Andrew has primary responsibility for the design and execution of all aspects of PeerSight's market acceptance and technology platform.

Additionally, Andrew scored a career high of 29 home runs and 87 RBI's during the '07 to lead his team to a 4-2 victory in the World Series.
