Ben Pearson’s Summary

For over a decade I've had my hands on web applications from fortune 500 companies to the smallest of small businesses. Each one has taught me something new about design, development, user interaction, marketing, etc. I've played numerous roles in the life cycles of various businesses and projects and have acquired a robust set of experiences to draw from. I'm a generalist with a strong emphasis on organized, user-driven design and am currently working hard to expand the limitations of application design by taking advantage of new technology including Adobe Flex and other RIA platforms.
