Shubhendu Parth’s Summary

Have set up Blue Slate Media along with my friend and colleague at Cyber Media and the former Executive Editor of Voice & Data magazine Pravin Prashant.

The "slate" refers to traditional knowledge. "Blue" highlights the 'e' or digital world as well as the "sky", while "Media" signifies information dissemination.

Hence, "Blue Slate" signifies knowledge sharing through varied media--traditional print media, more contemporary online media and digital publishing, with specific focus on the online and mobile publishing and new technology medium.

The first in the series from the stable is the info-portal www.igovernment.in, a dynamic, interactive web platform and info-portal on Good Governance.

While the prime objective is to create a platform for sharing knowledge on how technology can be used and is being used elsewhere or in parts across the country to improve governance, the portal also aims at equipping e-Gov champions with information for better strategies and policies for good governance.

i-Government aims at filling the existing gap of peer-to-peer learning and capacity building amongst policy makers, judiciary, bureaucrats, academicians, and the vendor community across India, as well as those in other public services.

The ‘I’ in iGovernment stands for IT-enabled, information-based, intelligent government and Internet-enabled citizen service delivery. It also stands for the critical component of integrated approach towards all government to government transactions, without which it would be difficult to meet the UN Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) within the year 2015 time frame. Not to forget that India has its own National e-Governance Plan (NeGP) to meet.

... and there are more in the pipeline.
