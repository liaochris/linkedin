Jeffrey Plumley’s Summary

With over 18 years in the international trade and transportation industry Jeff possesses unmatched experience. Early in his career he was specifically involved in Freight forwarding across all modes of transport, and secured his US Customs brokers License in 1993.

He completed his MBA in 2000 and transitioned into Software for the Transport Industry helping to start Neomodal.com, a web based ocean logistics software provider that is now part of the Descartes brand of software solutions.

Jeff spent several years developing and marketing IT for the global Transport Industry. He has held positions in Product Management, Product Development, Multi-National Sales Management,and General Management.

He has worked extensively across the globe conducting business on five continents. In 2007 Jeff accepted the challenge of leading ABX Logistics in the Southeast United Sates.
