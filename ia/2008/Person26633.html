Brendan Kavanagh’s Summary

Since the early-1990s, I have been involved in the development of cutting edge businesses focused on advanced technology for the digital media, consumer electronics and wireless industries. I have outstanding skills in the areas of strategic marketing, product management, marketing communications, web development and business development.

I was the VP of Marketing and Sales for SkyLink Design – a wireless software platform developer focused on collaboration and automation for the wireless industry. At SkyLink, I was responsible for revenue generation through focused strategic marketing, product management and sales efforts. I developed customer relationships with top carriers and OEMs.

Previous to SkyLink, I worked in the consumer electronics display business with two companies – Collinear and Clairvoyante. I was responsible for all marketing and business development activities including product management, technology partnerships, brand and web presence.

At iVAST, we developed a comprehensive content delivery platform for the digital media over IP, interactive web and wireless markets. I led the product specification and development of a hardware/software platform for the delivery and playback of interactive MPEG-4 video. I was instrumental in developing strategic partnerships with international content, consumer electronics and telecommunications companies. I directed the creation of interactive media content for web and IPTV applications with the creative team at iVAST Studio LA.

I started my career with the pioneering 3D animation software developer, Nichimen Graphics. At Nichimen, I led corporate marketing, product marketing and business development efforts around the company’s professional 3D animation software. We launched multiple products into the international digital media and gaming markets while developing customer relationships with the “Who’s Who” of film, gaming and web media companies.
