Paul Gatland’s Summary

An Electronic Engineer with over 24 years of Safety, Maintainability, Availability, Reliability and Testability Engineering experience.

Professional interests are in the design, development and assessment of safety critical and high reliable systems, particularly the electronic hardware and software aspects.

Successfully worked in the following application sectors; railway signalling, flight control and avionics, differential GPS, Spacecraft, industrial control, marine propulsion control and military systems.

Our mission statement is to support clients in identifying and achieving the most cost effective levels of dependability for their products, systems and assets.
