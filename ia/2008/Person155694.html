Dr. (Prof.) Rajan Bhonsle, M.D.’s Summary

Dr. Rajan Bhonsle, M.D., is the Founder Director of Heart To Heart Counselling Centre. He is a senior Consultant in Sexual Medicine & Counsellor from Mumbai, practicing for 21 years.

Dr.Bhonsle has been conferred with two of the most prestigious citations in the field of sexology (Sexual Medicine). He is the Diplomate of the American Board of Sexology as well as the American College of Sexologists. He is the FIRST one and the ONLY one from India to be honored with these two International titles of recognition together in the field of Sexology.

He is an Hon. Professor and Head of the Department of Sexual Medicine at K.E.M.Hospital and G.S.Medical College Mumbai. The Department of Sexual Medicine at the KEM Hospital, is the only one of its kind in the country.

Dr. Rajan Bhonsle passed his MBBS from Grant Medical College, Mumbai in 1981. He stood FIRST in the MD examination of Bombay University in the year 1985.
He has authored four books on 'Sex Education'. His book "Samagra Kaamjeevan" is in its eighth edition since its first release by the Health & Education Ministers of Maharashtra.

Dr.Bhonsle started India's FIRST full-fledged 'Pre-Marriage Counselling Centre' in Mumbai in 1998.

Presently he is writing a regular Question-Answer columns - "Let's Talk Sex" - for DNA & "Sex Talk" for Maharashtra Times. He has also been writing a regular column "Intimacy Issues" for the popular magazine "New Woman" for several years.

Dr.Bhonsle is a consultant for Raymonds India, NDTV and Shaadi.com for their websites www.ksontheweb.com, www.doctorndtv.com and www.shaadi.com.

Dr.Bhonsle has appeared as an expert panelist on popular TV shows on various TV channels such as NDTV, Star TV, Zee TV, Aaj Tak, Times Now, Alfa TV, ETV, Mumbai Doordarshan, Me Marathi etc.
