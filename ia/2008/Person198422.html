Walter Conn’s Summary

I own a variety of commercial real estate related finance and investment enterprises. Our predominant business lines are structured finance, equity syndication and investment sales. I have about 15 years of real estate operations, brokerage, investment and management experience.

We use an investment banking business structure, and our goal is to be one of the premier players in structuring significant commercial real estate investments in the Western US. We bring a vast network of investors and private equity sources, developers, lenders and financial institutions, brokerage and property management firms to the table to find, finance, acquire, improve and ultimately sell investment real estate.

We act as consultants as often as we structure debt or equity, helping investors analyze potential investment property and infusing whatever resources they need to assist them in accomplishing their goals. We are fee consultants for a variety of hedge funds and other institutions, and speak at a variety of commercial real estate events.

We work with passive investors and their advisors, placing capital in commercial real estate investments. Structured to preserve capital first and foremost, we use stringent analysis and due diligence. We invest our own capital side by side with our investors, and average about 5 investments annually with returns in the 14%-16% range.

We provide short term bridge and construction financing, short to medium term bank, SBA and Mezzanine products and long term bank, CMBS and Life debt. We work with all property types and loan sizes over $500,000.

We also love discussing our business as a career, so feel free to contact me anytime.
