Justin King’s Summary

B2B Ideation and strategy within Interactive Marketing and Technology are my passion.

For over 10 years, I have been a consultant in the interactive marketing and technology space. I equally love solution selling / business development and the delivery of creative solutions. I started as a developer, then as business owner, independent consultant, project manager, and now am an executive in one of the countries leading interactive marketing companies working with some of the countries greatest brands.

Leadership is a cornerstone of my career, as I place emphasis on mentoring and the building of high performing teams and have an excellent track record in leading mixed enterprise class teams.

In the end though, it's all about my passion for helping B2B companies use the online channel to create and grow relationships.
