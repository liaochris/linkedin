Takeya Takafuji’s Summary

After graduated Meijigakuin University, I worked for Advertising Agency in Japan for 9 years (5 years Tokyo, 4 years Osaka).
I was in charge of TV division,　so I know very well how to doing business in the mass-communications industry.

Soon after Hansin big earthquake, I established Internet Cafe in Kobe because doing support to recover Kobe city using Internet.　And I did presentation to Japan Government that it is top priority to support Kobe city using internet,　and Japanese Gov. support our project.　 t is called 'To built Kobe Gourmet town in Internet' It is in order to recover Kobe city using internet, especially support restaurant, cafe in Kobe .

I know very well how to planning and create new products and service sell them without using lots of cost for promotions, because I use word of mouth (its viral marketing).

So I know very well to grow not only mobile business but management the organization.
Now I am a President of Mobile Marketing Solution Association (http://mmsa.jp),
Senior partner of IMJ Mobile Inc. President of Uniquelab Inc.that I established in 2006.I am board member of MCN Inc. (http://www.mcn-inc.com) which has special search technology on the mobile world.
