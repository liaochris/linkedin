Christine Caine’s Summary

I fell for the business at 23 when I bought my first property. The three bedroom, two bath twin in West Philadelphia, came with a tenant, and instantly, I was thrust into homeownership, being a landlord, with some case management on the side...some of you landlords out there know what I'm talking about.

I first became licensed in 2000, in Pennsylvania. Since then, I've completed Real Estate Licensing courses in New Jersey and North Carolina, not including post-licensing classes, seminars, CECs and CAM (Certified Apartment Manager).

I'm excited about the idea of matching people with real property; it's somewhat romantic to me. The process can be very, and relentlessly, straining on even the seasoned buyer / seller. I work very hard to make sure my clients and customers are educated about it, and are honest with me and themselves the entire way through. For most of us, buying a home will be the single biggest purchase we make in our lifetime; it's best to have a professional on your side.
