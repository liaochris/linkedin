Serena Ehrlich’s Summary

With 13 years in the newswsire industry, I have been fortunate enough to work with some of the best companies in Boston, Dallas, Fort Worth and now Los Angeles. I am continuing my participation with NIRI (National Investor Relations Institute) and on their board of directors, which has proven to be very interesting as the SEC continues their crackdown on RegFD, SOX and now stock options backdating.

In addition, I am an active member of Business Wire's social media team, allowing me to play on such platforms as Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, Myspace, YPPR, Asmallworld.net and more.

I am also a senior member of YoungPRPros providing PR and interviewing/hiring advice to PR pros around the globe.
