Alexander Kwapis’s Summary

I am trained as an Industrial Designer and Graphic Designer.

It is my goal to help research + design become active in a companies strategic thinking, their development process and implementation of their products, systems, and services. I do so by focusing on customer experiences and using design to build bridges between creativity, innovation, and business.

Alexander Kwapis’s Specialties:

user research, branding, marketing, material sourcing, brainstorming, sketching, modeling, conceptualizing, blue sky development, light engineering, computer modeling, graphic design, package testing and research, creative management, interaction design, identity development, common sense.
