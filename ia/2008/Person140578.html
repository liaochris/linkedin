Nick Jonsson’s Summary

Nick Jonsson, Managing Director at Spark Communications Vietnam, leverages 10 years of international experience in nearly every aspect of communications, marketing and public relations for clients at spark Communications Vietnam.

Nick has been trusted to represent a number of the most recognized brands and companies in the world including Abbott, Ariel, D&G, Electrolux, Emirates, Etihad, Fuji Xerox, Herbalife, Lenovo, Le Meridien Resorts, Mattel, Mont Blanc, O2, Oriflame, Pedigree, Philips, Procter & Gamble, TOPS Supermarkets, TNT, and United Airlines.

He has worked in Sweden, Australia, UK, Thailand and currently Vietnam, across a wide variety of industries for both consumer and B2B clients. Some of the sectors Nick has worked in include aviation, IT, telecommunications, FMCG, transportation & logistics, travel & tourism, manufacturing and health care.

Nick is a public speaker, motivator and professional networker as well as an entrepreneur who co-founded European Young Professionals (EYP), a networking organization in London and Bangkok that promotes links between young business professionals. Nick is a Non-Executive board member of EYP as well as board member of Nordcham Vietnam.

Nick is actively involved in the society by supporting charities and local organizations. He was in 2004 recognized with a golden target award by the Public Relations Institute of Australia for his fundraising activities for the Make-A-Wish Foundation, which included organizing corporate golf days that raised money for the charity that grant children with life-threatening illnesses their cherished wish.

Nick Jonsson’s Specialties:

Nick specializes in strategic public relations counsel and planning, media relations, publicity, branding, crisis communications, copywriting, editing, article writing, industry and community relations, government relations, executive media training, media and industry research, media coverage research and analysis, corporate event planning & event management and communication tools.
