Dan Skwire dskwire@mindspring.com’s Summary

You can't get away with performing 'do-overs' in mission-critical computer systems!

You lose customers, business and money, if you need to perform 'do-overs in the data-center'!

An expert in designing, implementing, using, teaching 'first fault problem resolution technologies' - using the mission-critical Enterprise-class mindset to build superior datacenter system problem resolution environments in all computer platform environments. Study client attainment, and plan to fill their gaps in desired attainment. Then, help clients use all their platform first-fault tools, then help them obtain products selectively, and then help them custom-build, often with vendor tools, to maximize results. First use those unused 'free' features!

Expert practitioner and planner: product and process improvement regarding Reliability, Availability, and Serviceability, testing and Quality Assurance. Mission-critical Systems Management ("ITIL"), operations, availability management and improvement, methodology and best practices. A practitioner and specialist in remote service delivery, product planning, and infrastructure improvements.

Currently reading: "Windows Internals", "Self-Service Linux", "z/OS Principles of Operation".
Author of Enterprise Strategies Journal ("ESJ") article: "Best Practices for Solving Problems in Non-z/OS Environments". at http://esj.com/enterprise/article.aspx?EditorialsID=2916
