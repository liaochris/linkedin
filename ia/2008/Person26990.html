Brett Jackson’s Summary

I am a 22 year old entrepreneur, strategic consultant, and investor.

Crocs: I started my career when I was 16 years old at Crocs, Inc (NASDAQ: CROX), www.crocs.com as one of the first ten employees. I was given the position of co-director of The Sound-Board, of which I started within crocs. The Sound-Board was designed to brainstorm product diversification, strategy and new marketing channels for the crocs kids product line. I then worked on the collegiate crocs product line, developing strategies and selecting new products.

Getch Companies: While I was at crocs, I formed a small venture capital firm, Getch Companies, LLC where I helped start and operate several companies. Getch Companies eventually morphed into Generation Think Tank.

Generation Think Tank: With the proliferation of technology, information and globalization, among other factors, the informational gap between the aging workforce and the Generation Y demographic (13-32) has grown. While at crocs, and consulting/investing for various companies, large and small, I determined that other companies and agencies need outside consulting services to target/influence the Generation Y demographic. Thus, GTT was born.

AVX Aircraft Company: AVX is a manufacturer, marketer and distributer of safe, fast and technologically advanced helicopters. I was selected to serve as the Vice President of Marketing to help develop new marketing channels and strategies foreign to the industry. I serve on the Board of Directors as well.

TechStars: I work with smart tech start-ups in Boulder Colorado.
