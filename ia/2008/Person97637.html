JOSEPH KELLNER’s Summary

My life started in 1964 in Baltimore Md, and that is were I was born. After spending 17 years abused by my family I decided to join the U.S. NAVY. I attended boot camp in Orlando Fl and Moved on to Naval Training Command in San Diego, Ca. That is were I attended Telecommunications School. Morse Code Shool, Satellite Navigational School, And then Special Warfare School, normally named 'BUDS".


Hairdressing to me is a craft. There is only the mechanical or natural. My 18 years of experience has taken me to Miami, Los Angeles, Philadelphia and Orlando; from Apprentice to Master and from Student to Teacher. I am passionate about being a Colorist, Educator and Stylist. There are many Salons/Spas in the Orlando area but to stay on top of your game in this competitive industry, you must as a hairstylist/haircolorist be in a constant learning mode.
