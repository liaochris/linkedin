David Chappell’s Summary

Over 20 years of experience in the software industry covering a broad range of roles including R&D, code-slinger, sales, support and marketing.

A major market influencer on the creation and evolution of the Enterprise Service Bus (ESB) technology category. Posses a strong passion for shaping the future of technology, and enjoy sharing knowledge and experience with others.

Author of 4 books, including “Enterprise Service Bus” (Oreilly), “Java Message Service” (Oreilly), “Java Web Services” (Oreilly), and “Professional ebXML Foundations” (Wrox). These books have been translated and published in French, Korean, Chinese, Japanese, and Polish.

Have published countless articles in multiple technical journals such as Enterprise Architect, SOA/Web Services Journal, Java Developers Journal, XML Journal, JavaPro Magazine, XML Magazine, DDJ, SD Magazine, XML Coverpages, XML.com, SearchWebServices, and is frequently quoted in major IT trade journals which include InfoWorld, eWeek, ComputerWorld, InformationWeek, CIO Magazine, Business Integration Journal, Network World, ZDNet, and ADT.

As a public speaker, have delivered 100s of lectures and seminars at over 60 cities worldwide including London, Madrid, Barcelona, Paris, Brussels, Luxembourg, Amsterdam, Eindhoven, Frankfurt, Munich, Berlin, Zurich, Geneva, Milan, Oslo, Stockholm, Tallin, Helsinki, Singapore, Seoul, Hong Kong, Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Mexico City, Sao Paulo. Have also been interviewed, and quoted in major international IT journals around the world including IET – Financial-I (UK), Information Age (UK), Institution of Engineering and Technology (UK), Silicon.de (Germany), ZDNet Australia, IT Sitio (Mexico), JavaSpektrum (Germany), The Guardian (UK), IT Willkommen (Netherlands), Hong Kong Economic Times, IT Vikko (Finland), ComputerWorld Poland, ComputerWorld Brazil, InformationWeek Brazil.
