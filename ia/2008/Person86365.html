Jean-Christophe Lonchampt’s Summary

Jean-Christophe (JC or Chris) has 20 years of international experience in research, business development, channel and OEM sales, marketing, strategy, acquisitions and venture capital. Multilingual (English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, basic Finnish and Mandarin)

Managing marketing and strategic business development activities for TSMC Europe. TSMC is the world's largest maker of made-to-order chips for telecommunications gear, computers, cell phones, etc.. with headquarters in Taiwan.

Member of Wharton Fellows (Wharton School), a lifelong and global network of senior executives (CxOs) who explore enterprise transformations around the world.

Founder of ASKsherpa a next generation global consulting boutique originally founded in Australia in 2006-2007, and incubating highly innovative projects. Guest lecturer in Executive MBA programs in Europe.

Until late 2006 Regional Managing Director Europe and North America of AGERE Systems, Mobility (spun out of LUCENT BELL LABS). Merged with LSI and Mobility was carved out to INFINEON in 2007. Managed the mobile phones OEM business team out of London and the US.

Previously had a 10-year management career at INTEL Corporation and INTEL CAPITAL. Spearheaded Internet convergence industry firsts in late 1990s and Sales with ALCATEL Broadband, NOKIA and Service Providers.

Served on Boards of INTEL CAPITAL software invested start-ups and WWRF Wireless World Research Forum.

From 1989 worked successively for ALCAN R&D, DGA Research (French Defense), SKF in France and VISHAY in Italy and Eastern Europe on advanced materials and micro systems in Aeronautics, Automotive, Medical and Telecoms, prior to joining INTEL in Munich, Germany in 1995.

Lifelong support to this very innovative cross-cultural Education and Environment venture: visit www.clubedresort.com
