Evan Sims’s Summary

Specializes in developing unique and innovative user experiences using social technologies, such as massively multiplayer games and Internet-based social networks. Strong believer in using technology as a catalyst for social experiences. I hope to end up in an industry that can support these ideas, such as gaming or web applications, and that I can play a part in fostering the development of technologies that support deeper social interaction. I am also a strong believer in web standards, and designing with a "build once, render anywhere" attitude.

I have extensive experience with PHP and MySQL, XHTML and CSS, JavaScript and AJAX, as well as developing and building upon existing platforms and APIs.

My latest project, Squawk (a tool that bridges the 3D world Second Life with social networking services like Twitter and del.icious), has received some great reaction from the community. It was recently featured on Read/Write Web as one of the top 10 Twitter tools (it came in as #2!), and Mashable! as one of the top 20 "coolest Jaiku tools". Squawk was recently aquired by a German social networking firm, and I am currently working with them to move the service over to their network.

User experiences, user interfaces, PHP, Perl, Visual Basic, C++, MySQL, web design, AJAX, XHTML, JavaScript, CSS, web standards, web accessibility, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, APIs, crossplatform compatibility, portability, mobile applications and social technologies and platforms.
