Phillip A. Jones’s Summary

I'm a communication specialist, with a particular focus on communication strategies, branding and internal communications in the public sector. I can design and implement practical, elegant solutions to communication scenarios.

You can read more regarding my expertise at: www.phillipajones.com

Some of my other roles are:

Managing Director of Schmooze, a well regarded Canberra-based professional network for communication and client relations professionals. Schmooze holds several professional development and networking events every month as well as fostering several communities of interest, such as government communications, ICT, and young professionals.

The network has over 300 members, most at the manager/Director level. We hold several boutique professional development and networking events each month. Schmooze also has a young professional group with its own program of networking and professional developments open to any gen-y in the Canberra region.

I also conduct tailored short courses on networking skills and strategies for individuals and companies as well as provide master networking + mentoring services on a one on one basis.

For more information please see: www.schmooze.net.au

Phillip A. Jones’s Specialties:

Networking, communication strategies professional intermediary services, internal communications, event management, faciliation, event management, writing, mentoring.

Phillip A. Jones’s Experience

  • Communications Strategist

    Attorney-Generals Department

    (Government Agency; 501-1000 employees; Public Policy industry)

    April 2007 — Present (11 months)

    I was contracted to conduct a strategic review of Family Relationships Online portal including the design of the consultation process with a wide range of stakeholders nationally.

    The consultation process resulted in travelling around Australia over five weeks and the subsequent recommedations was accepted in full by the Department.

    In addition, I developed the design and publication process for webpages for the new Family Relationship Centres located around Australia.

    I have now been retained as an advisor to oversee the implementation of my recommedations including the establishment of a strategic advisory group for the website on a part-time basis.

  • Director and Founder

    Schmooze Events

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    2003 — Present (5 years)

    Schmooze is one of Canberra's leading professional networking groups that creates a wide range of events for fostering professional development, career and business opportunities and collaboration.

    The Schmooze network welcomes those working or studying in the communication, design, professional services, client relations, PR, marketing & sales, as well as the arts and non-profit sector.

    The network fosters links for Canberra's 'creative class' and its events are unique for their atmosphere, quality of venue and diversity of participants.

    For more information, please see: www.schmooze.net.au

    -Design of the visual identity, including logo and marketing assets (eg posters)
    -Development of the a marketing and communications campaign
    -Management of focus group testing of identify
    -Close direction and liaison with graphic designers
    -Development of communication strategy and key messages
    -Development of communication guide and resources for implementation of strategy
    -Re-development of the Australian Scholarships website
    -Providing strategic advice to AusAID and DEST managers
    -Close liaison with both agencies to ensure alignment and coordination of resources

  • Communication Consultancy

    Austrade- Australian Trade Commission

    June 2006 — November 2006 (6 months)

    Contracted to develop four major online training modules for Austrade’s staff worldwide covering key aspects of a corporate governance transition for the agency. Project requires working with subject experts, designing modules and liaising with senior management and designers.
