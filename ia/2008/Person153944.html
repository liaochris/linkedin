Jeff Borden’s Summary

Jeff Borden is a professional speaker, educator, consultant, and comedian who has presented to audiences around the globe. An expert on learning, eLearning, and communication, his informative presentations are always filled with humor, illustrations, and practical strategies! He has presented to academic, corporate, religious, and non-profit organizations. Some seminars include: Master Teaching, Online Education, Powerful Presentations, Narrative & Storytelling, Practical Preaching, Using Non-Verbals, and many more!

Education Consultant, Growing Successful Programs, Organizational Communication, Change Management, Content Management, Learning Outcome Strategies
