Julie Walraven’s Summary

Julie became of age writing resumes without knowing it would be her career and drive her into an exploration of computers, software, and the new world of social media. Networked with the best and brightest career minds in the world, Julie still bases her business on the desire to help others succeed in life. Coupled with development of professional career management materials, Julie has contracted with non-profits to improve operations, manage events, and build marketing strategies. Currently, Julie is also the Operations Coordinator for Wausau Whitewater.

In addition, Julie Walraven is a nationally published author of resumes featured in career-focused books, such as Expert Resumes for Engineering Professionals, America's Top Resumes, Expert Resumes for Teachers and Educators, College Grad Resumes to Land $75,000+ Jobs, and Best Resumes and CVs for International Jobs.

Julie Walraven’s Specialties:

Creative resume writing, strategic cover letters, proactive career coach. Well-versed in non-profit management and special event management.

Extensive software applications experience. Proficient in Microsoft Word & Outlook. Daily use of MS Excel, Publisher, & Access, QuickBooks. Experienced in Adobe Creative Suite 2, including Adobe InDesign, Bridge, Acrobat, and PhotoShop as well as Dreamweaver 8.
