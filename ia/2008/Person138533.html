  • Director

    Femin Ijtihad

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    2007 — Present (1 year)

    Femin Ijtihad was established in 2007 to increase the accesssibility of gender-neutral interpretations & stories in Islamic law, history and traditions amongst the Afghan people.

    FI aims to achieve that by producing:

    a) A manual compiling what scholars have written about gender-neutral interpretations in Islamic law. The manual will also contain gender-neutral and inspiring traditions of the Prophet and stories of key female figures in Muslim history.

    b) A series of story-books written in a non-confrontational fashion, using plots, characters and literary devices that will inspire the reader to embrace better attitudes towards the opposite gender. The story-books will deliberately possess some similarities with existing Afghan folktales, stories and poetry.
