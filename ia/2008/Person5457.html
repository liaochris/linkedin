Alastair Adair’s Summary

I am a hands-on new media creative developer and project manager

I co-founded Showstreams Productions Ltd - an innovative new media video production company - in Edinburgh, UK. I'm curently based in Barcelona and Edinburgh, working on a number of freelance projects and developing the companies' european business partnerships

My core hands-on skills are as a director, producer, cameraman, editor, music and graphic designer for video, web and print. I'm fully skilled in new media project management, business management and lecture at universities on entrepreneurship and new media

I'm trained and experienced in delivering the following enterprise services

Creative video production
Multi-camera live production
Live event webcasting
Interactive flash video development
Online and mobile video delivery
Multi-platform new media campaigns
Webtv strategy
Web 2.0 development

Selected track record

Channel4 - Production of a webtv training initiative, large-scale live event broadcast and video content commission

XFM - Crewing, consultation and equipment for a UK-wide video campaign

Scottish Government - Commissioned to travel to NASA, Houston to film an innovative series of daily web videos for the highly acclaimed Scottish Space School programme

NESTA - Production of an unique live webtv show broadcast in parallel to a large-scale conference event

Scottish Qualifications Authority - Multiple contracts to produce DVD course materials used by colleges across Scotland

Electoral Reform Society - Produced a large-scale live webtv show to support their campaign during the Scottish elections

The Nexuslive Show - a live interactive multi-camera music show. I was integral in its conception, development, funding pitching, production, crew management and live show direction. It has been a first in Scotland, in the UK in its format, and won various awards. Highlight: producing Sandi Thom's first webcast, the catalyst for her famous webcast series
