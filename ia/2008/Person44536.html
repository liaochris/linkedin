Dan Sandell’s Summary

Over 15 years of senior management level experience in the area of Operations, Supply Chain and Distribution. My present position has allowed me the opportunity to leverage my experience and the knowledge acquired from my MBA, while adding value to the existing organization.

I have managed internal operations and customer service in a manner that maximizes human resources and other assets, with an emphasis on workplace safety, reduced cost and strengthened production.

In addition to compliment my background in operations and general management, I have had the opportunity of leading the operational start-up of new divisions, as well as integration/rationalization of multiple sites, developing processes, new policy, procedures, performance standards, culture and vision.

This experience has increased my ability to see the bigger picture as it relates to building budgets, sourcing vendors and partners, articulating a business case, and of course, the impact on cost and profitability.
