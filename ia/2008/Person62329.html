Fabio Marangini’s Summary

Fabio Marangini can be defined an “innovator in continuity”, with a strong background and vision on the future. He took his degree in Tourism Science with top rates and his Master in Business Administration with a thesis in Tourism Marketing. During these years, he developed his knowledge in computer science and telecommunications.
His working experience has always involved both IT/TLC and Tourism. Marangini worked in the GDS company Amadeus Italia as Sales Manager and in leading Business Travel Agencies focusing on IT applied to travel management and business and web and mobile applications. In 2000 his wide experiences in the field allowed him to create the most relevant Italian “E-Travel” project: GialloViaggi.it (the first OBTA owned by Seat Pagine Gialle) and TravelOnLine (OTA owned by RCS and De Agostini Publishing).
As a result of his know-how, Marangini founded TCUBE, a leading content and service provider. Thanks to his personal relations, he become a top player in the market providing all Italian MNO and some of the leading European MNO and Directory Assistants with its services.
Today he leads Tmind as Commercial Director. Tmind is a software/content/service provider italian firm focused on Infomobility and Communication System.
High-energy, highly effective, results oriented director with proven international expertise in successfully executing during start up, growth and/or turnaround phase in both business-to-business and direct-to-consumer segments within the mobile entertainment and internet industry in Italy and Europe.

Highly skilled at developing business strategies, analyzing market opportunities, identifying customers needs, self-generating business opportunities and deals, successfully entering into new markets, solving problems, presenting strategies and alternatives to achieve results. Also, an expert in direct-to-consumer/direct response marketing of mobile entertainment services in Italy, Germany, Spain.
