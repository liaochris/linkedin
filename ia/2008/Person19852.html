Barry Cronin’s Summary

I graduated from the University of Limerick with a Degree in Business and Japanese and a Masters Degree in Human Resource Management. Deciding that continuing on to do a PhD was not for me; I took on a marketing advisor role in Tokyo with Enterprise Ireland, the Irish Trade and Technology board, where I specialised in helping Irish Digital companies in establishing themselves commercially in this exciting territory.

I was responsible for the conception and growth of one of the core business development functions within the EI Japan which gave me strong international exposure across the digital spectrum, in addition to leading a team that organised some of the most successful multi-sectoral trade missions and fairs in the region. My passion for the games and social networking sectors took me to London, where I was initially introduced to Fred from a placement perspective but joined them instead!
