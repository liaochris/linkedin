Emmanuel Verbeeck’s Summary

I am a trilingual IT manager with 18 years experience in SME, corporate, and international environments. I have a formal IT degree, and both ITIL Foundations and MCSE 2003 (Early Achiever) certifications.

My collaborators and colleagues see me as a real team player with an easy-going, no-nonsense attitude. I like to propose initiatives to improve efficiency and quality, and to concretely implement them. I also highly appreciate managing people and generating a positive, productivity-inducing workplace.
