Matt Platts’s Summary

Project Manager and Programmer in a number of web technologies with over 10 years experience. I have built large applications single handedly and worked on larger applications as part of a team in a number of areas from web development, multimedia transcoding for mobile media and design of full ERP systems.

My Speciality areas are programming in Perl, PHP, Javascript and (X)HTML, coupled with extensive experience of Microsoft Access, MySQL, Flash and moderate actionscript. I also excel at project planning and management and database design.

Always thorough and forward thinking I began building a framework (affectionately known as IsoTope) in Perl in 1999, that has recently been ported to PHP and has been in use on a number of web sites for many years. It allows me to create vastly complicated web sites in a matter of hours, and sometimes even minutes.

I began in design in the late '90s and am highly experienced with Photoshop, InDesign and also know CorelDraw inside out.
