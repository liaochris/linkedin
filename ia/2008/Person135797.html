Kathleen LoGiodice Fong’s Summary

Licensed in all three of the Nation's Capitol Area jurisdictions, I am experienced at helping my clients discover where they want to live based on lifestyle, commute or even proximity to an urban center or to nature! My knowledge of the DC metro area gives me a great advantage when assisting clients with sales or purchases- as I can more accurately know what the current real estate market is trending towards.

It's my goal to help you turn your dream home into a reality! Providing you with top notch professionals for financing, title work or even renovation projects post close!
