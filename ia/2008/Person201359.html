Xavier Nogue’s Summary

I am targeting a career in the medical device or hightech industry.

Currently, I am a product manager for Instrumentation Laboratory, a medical diagnostics developer, manufacturer and distributor of diagnostic analyzers for hospitals. I am responsible for the commercial strategy of IL's products in its critical care area of business.

I was a sales representative in the Canadian region for Instrumentation Laboratory during the years of 2005 and 2006. I was instrument in 2006 for IL's growth of 44% in business with respect 2005 exceeding quota expectations over 360%. I was awarded best sales representative in Canada, North American and the World at IL in 2006.

Before entering the MBA program at UCLA Anderson in 2003, I was working for Instrumentation Laboratory (Massachusetts and Michigan) for 5 years in research and development. During the summer of 2004, I worked for Lenovo in business development in Shanghai, China.

My future goal is to start-up a company in the diagnostics or hightech sectors.
