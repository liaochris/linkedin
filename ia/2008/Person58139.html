Ellerton Whitney’s Summary

Ellerton is completing his M.B.A. in Marketing at San Diego State University while he works in Irvine for Earthbound Media Group, a comprehensive cross-communications marketing agency. Ellerton has received formal education in Branding, Marketing Research, Consumer Behavior, and Product Innovation, among other subjects. He also has a B.A. from University of California San Diego in Political Science, with a minor in History.

Ellerton is Google Adwords Certified and is also a Yahoo! Ambassador. Ellerton has experience generating and implementing paid search campaigns in Google, Yahoo, MSN, Ask, and other online marketing networks. He has performed search engine marketing in multiple verticals, and has specialized knowledge of automotive, education, and B2C tech. Ellerton is an expert in Microsoft Excel (2003 and 2007), and he has a vast knowledge of specialized reporting skills to illustrate search engine marketing's value chain. He also understands the need for in depth data analysis for the proper implementation of complex search engine marketing campaigns with varied bidding strategies.

In addition to SEM, Ellerton has experience organically optimizing content for many of Primedia’s web properties, including Motor Trend, Mustang Monthly, and Automobile Magazine. As an avid car enthusiast, he orchestrated social media marketing campaigns utilizing Web 2.0 sites such as Reddit, Netscape, Digg, Yahoo! Answers, and bulletin boards to populate content links and build traffic. Finally, Ellerton has 5 years of operations management experience with AMC Theatres, and is a a big multi-tasker.
