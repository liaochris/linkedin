Carol Lazerick’s Summary

I have a rather crazy quilt professional background that works to my client's advantage as I have a multi-faceted approach to helping both non-profit and for-profit organizations. Every client's mission, needs and goals are different. I pride myself on doing what is needed and doggedly working until I accomplish what is needed. Someone described me as a portable spark plug.

Over the years, I have:
- Cultivated media relations in order to place new product releases, articles, announcements, etc.
- Pitched story ideas to local media about art galleries, health care practitioners, tai chi masters, financial planners, entrepreneurs
- Prospected for new accounts for manufacturers
- Developed concepts and written annual reports, fundraising appeals, letters of inquiries, etc.
- As a sub-sub-contractor, edited a series of training manuals for a national trade association
- Planned programs for non-profits, directed membership campaigns, served as Board Chairman for a domestic violence shelter, filled the role of interim director for an alternative fund of social justice groups

And more. I am up for new challenges. Ready to spread my wings.

I work with trade magazines to publish new product releases and encourage editors to use Tribco as a resource when writing articles on brakes and clutches and replacement parts.   • Marketing Consultant

    Kinetic Technologies Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    February 2002 — Present (6 years 5 months)

    At Kinetic Technologies, Inc. dba K-Tec , I do media relations, marketing, prospect research. K-Tec is a 6 year old company led by two dynamic guys, Larry Tyler and John Neumann who are dedicated to lean manufacturing. K-Tec manufactures material handling equipment, e.g., non-powered carts that are an alternative to forklifts. An exciting company, K-Tec prides itself on its elegant designs that are tailored to the needs of their customers. In a short period of time, K-Tec has built a reputation among top-notch companies including Ford, GM, Whirlpool, Value City and more.

  • Communications Director
