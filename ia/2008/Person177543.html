Jim Mitchem’s Summary

At smashcommunications, our main goal is to communicate messages clearly and effectually. Visit us online at www.smashcommunications.com and you'll immediately notice a difference. We sincerely care about the dialogue you have with your audience. To us, it's all about them.

We are a diverse group of virtual professionals who have outgrown the traditional ad agency mold and have latched on to the power of the Internet to grow our entrepreneurial endeavors. We know first-hand that our best work is always (ALWAYS) based on a sound strategy. By thinking strategically before creatively, we ensure that our solutions stand out from the sea of waist-deep crap that we all must muddle through each day. Ultimately, the purpose of everything we do is to steal away a few seconds with your audience to convince them to do something you want them to do. Yes, we want them to take notice, but more importantly, we want them to take action. Favorable action.

We specialize in high-profile, high-quality brand development and use this core appeal to create meaningful dialogue with any audience. We can laundry list our mediums of expertise here, and you'd definitely be impressed, but we're not. It would be limiting. Just take every traditional medium you can think of, add some of the more recent media you've experienced and throw in a handful of media we're working with now that you probably haven't even bumped into yet - and know we're capable of deploying communications via all these mediums. To us, knowing how to expertly deploy messaging is icing on the concept cake.

Remember - life's too short for people to pay attention to bad advertising.

Jim Mitchem’s Specialties:

High-concept, purpose-driven communications that strike a chord in the heart of your target audience, and fear in the heart of your competition. We do this through every possible communications medium imaginable for companies as small as single-person start-ups to huge Fortune 500s. Anywhere.
