Dale Capewell, Ph.D.’s Summary

Successful engineering consultant and team leader in both "start-ups" and corporate environments. Creative and technically diverse system designer and developer from concept to prototype to production. Skilled at integrating and optimizing a wide range of functional areas including mechanical, electrical, software, firmware, RF to optical, imaging, image analysis, and/or fluidics. Proven expertise in a wide variety of technologies and industries that include semiconductor, optics and coatings, flat panel displays, optical and wireless telecommunications, and medical diagnostics.

Dale Capewell, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Technical management of small to large engineering teams, optical design, mechanical design, fluidic design, system integration, automated control, automated data acquisition, automated data mining, image processing, Labview, Zemax, Solidworks, Visual Studio, OrCAD
