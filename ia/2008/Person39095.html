John Coonen’s Summary

With over twenty years of experience in marketing, technology and research, primarily focused on non-profits and associations, John now heads the start-up CMS Association, based in Chicago, Illinois. The CMSA produces CMS Expo Learning Conferences, designed to educate and empower Content Management Team members from its four areas of expertise:

- Business
- Administration
- Development
- Content

CMSA is also launching an awards program, called the CMSAwards Program, which will culminate with an Awards Banquet at the year-end CMS Expo, recognizing the best of the best in the CMS world.

See www.cmsExpo.net for more information on the CMS Expo and CMSAwards Program.

John Coonen’s Specialties:

If you are seeking ways to target the fast-growing Open Source CMS world, get involved in the CMS Association, and plan on promoting yourself at CMS Expo.
