Brent Marcus’s Summary

Brent Marcus co-founded Cyberspace Promotions, Inc (CPI) in 1994 with the launch of Virtual Radio, a Music and Advertising Forum on the World-Wide-Web. Since then, CPI has expanded into a Business Process Management Firm with partnerships with both IBM and Microsoft.

Brent's previous experience was in Accounting Software Systems Development from 1989-1994. He received a B.A. in Computer Science and Economics from the University of California, Berkeley in 1989.

Brent is also a Partner in CPI Ventures.
