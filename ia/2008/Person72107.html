Lisa Silverman’s Summary

RECLAIM YOUR TIME was founded out of a desire to assist people with obtaining and/or reclaiming balance in their lives. Our mission is to offer you the gift of time so that you can shift your focus to your priorities- family, business, recreational activities, all the things that nourish your soul. RYT can provide that opportunity by taking care of all the details in your life that prevent you from making the utmost use of your day. .
At Reclaim Your Time we care about you. We care about your family. We care about your loved ones. We realize that every single person’s well-being has a collective effect on the universe. We operate on the principle that personal satisfaction contributes, one person at a time, to a safer and more content planet for all of us to inhabit.
.
We are committed to living and working “as greenly” as possible.When possible, we don't use a car to get to a job. With permission, we provide bags for clients when we do their grocery shopping. We plan our errands to use the least number of miles when possible. Because of the nature of the business, it is sometimes necessary to use an automobile. We can all play a part to cut back our energy usage by having just one vehicle running errands & contributing to traffic, rather than many individual vehicles doing the same thing. We are very conscious about providing excellent customer service, while keeping in mind the most energy efficient way to do so.Our pledge is to be creative in providing excellent service while using the least amount of earth's resources to address each individuals' needs.
