Bill Macaitis’s Summary

Bill Macaitis is the VP of Online Marketing & SEO / SEM for Fox Interactive Media (FIM). Bill leads up the centralized online marketing department that services the entire FIM network which includes a variety of high profile sites such as MySpace, FoxSports, IGN, American Idol, Photobucket, AskMen, RottenTomatoes, Fox, GameSpy, Scout, Spring Widgets, Flektor and Direct2Drive.

Bill has successfully utilized SEO, SEM, display, e-mail, PR, viral marketing and social media to elevate Fox Interactive Media to one of the largest networks in the world with 161 million monthly unique visitors and 53 billion page views. Before FIM, Bill was the VP of Marketing for IGN Entertainment which he helped build to the largest gaming information network in the world. Over his time at IGN Entertainment he increased traffic ten-fold from 7 million to 74 million monthly unique visitors.

Bill has over 12 years of online marketing experience with a specialty in SEO & SEM PPC. He has a strong track record of building highly trafficked, profitable sites. Two companies he worked on, Case's Ladder and IGN Entertainment, successfully secured exit events in the form of acquisitions.

Bill is also a respected search industry leader. He has spoken at numerous conferences including Search Engine Strategies, Ad Tech, Pub Con, Searchnomics and iHollywood: Search & Media Summit. His work has been published in the Search Engine Journal. Bill is an active member of the Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization and serves on the In-house SEO / SEM committee.

Bill Macaitis’s Specialties:

Search Engine Optimization, Search Engine Marketing (PPC), Social Networking, E-mail Marketing, Web 2.0, Viral Marketing, Online Traffic Generation, B2C Marketing, Consumer Branding, Conversion Rate Marketing, Advertising Revenue Models, Subscription Revenue Models, E-commerce Revenue Models, Web Analytics, and Affiliate Programs.
