Eric D. Brown’s Summary

Aligning Technology, Strategy, People & Projects
Web: http://ericbrown.com

Core Skills:
Technology ~ Leadership ~ Strategy ~ Getting Things Done

I like to think I am a deep generalist in areas pertaining to the intersection of business and technology.

As a consultant (and person!) I have three over-arching goals:

1. Build long-term relationships
2. Deliver results
3. Continue to learn

What's different about me?

I focus on results not billable hours. This is a different approach than most consulting companies....but of course, I'm not like any consulting firm out there. So what do you get when you hire me? Easy...you get a partner who cares more about your and your results than billing you hours.

Here's what I bring to the table:

Real World Experience
I have over fifteen years of experience in corporate and consulting environments...with most of my experience coming from the corporate world. I've been where you are today and can bring that real world experience to your organization.

Technical and Business Acumen
Whether you need assistance with developing a strategic plan, help with selecting the proper technology, implementing a new technology, developing software or outsourcing your operations, I can help. I've been assisted organizations that range in size from 100 to 50,000 people and have experience in many types of technologies from many vendors.

Passion
You might think that this is a strange one to include here...but I feel that it's a vital piece of what I bring (call it a piece of my competitive advantage). I'm passionate about what I do...and I'm passionate about helping other people.

Budgetary Certainty
My standard fee structure is a fixed fee plus performance bonus or holdback. I feel that this approach frees me up to focus on results rather than billable hours. This approach also allows my clients to budget appropriately and have more control over project costs.
