Jennifer Moire’s Summary

A successful track record in strategic communications and media relations is the thread connecting a communications career that spans more than a dozen years of experience with top global companies, award-winning PR firms, high-profile national and statewide political campaigns and a national news network.

I'm a self-professed news hound and political junkie. Colleagues have described me as strategic, creative, tenacious and nimble, while achieving measurable results. I bring enthusiasm and energy to every project or initiative.

I'm leveraging a broad-based PR background to launch my own new "product"-- Lyon Park Public Relations. A major part of my practice is working on issues and initiatives related to climate change and the environment, two personal passions.
