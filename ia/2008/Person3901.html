Andrea Ghezzi’s Summary

I work in applied research for the development of WideBand Access devices.

The main activities consist of design, development, integration, process-engineering and testing of innovative antenna solutions.

Andrea Ghezzi’s Specialties:

Microwave devices and applications.
Innovative internal antenna solutions (printed antennas, dielectric antennas).
Multiple antenna solutions (Switched beam antenna).
Propagation of electromagnetic fields, wireless technology (802.11 a/b/g/n).

Realization of prototypes using photolithographic process.

Testing of telecommunication devices.
