  • Conference Coordinator

    UNESCO

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; International Affairs industry)

    November 2004 — March 2005 (5 months)

    "Youth for Human Unity: exploration for new values through Inter-cultural and Inter-religious Dialogue" was held from 21- 28 February 2005 in Auroville, Tamil Nadu, India. The conference was organized by Auroville and supported by UNESCO.
    The conference was open for student/youth of age group 18-25 from all SAARC counties. The main aims and objectives of seminar was to create an event that would foster interactive dialogue among culturally and religiously diverse participants and to explore the values and human qualities necessary to:
    Foster the emergence of a deeper sense of human unity and inter-relatedness within culturally and religiously diverse societies
    Create the seeds for a sustainable future
    Facilitate the conditions for visionary youth to feel confident and capable to make the transition into future community leaders.

    I was one of a core group of 8 people to define, create, organize and run this conference.
