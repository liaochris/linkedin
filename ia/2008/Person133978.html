Michael Jones’s Summary

Started business career in 1993 with the Black & Decker Corporation (DeWalt Division) as a Sales Representative managing a $1M US territory. Subsequently has held key management positions in virtually every core discipline of consumer durable marketing. Successfully executed Senior Management positions in product management, brand management, channel marketing and broadscale & direct response(catalog & web based) marketing. Joined the Danaher Corporation’s Fluke Division in 2002. By 2004 was promoted to the Director of Industrial Business Units where he managed & directed the global strategic & business unit plans for >$240M in annual revenue. Currently the Vice President & General Manager of the Test and Measurement business group for the Milwaukee Electric Tool Corporation.

Holds an MBA from the University of Washington (Seattle, WA)
