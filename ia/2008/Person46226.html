David Chambers’s Summary

Seasoned telecomms marketing professional with extensive technical and market knowledge of mobile operators worldwide. Originally started as a development engineer in telecoms, then moved to product management approx 10 years ago whilst at Motorola, then onto product/solution marketing in recent years. Very familiar with all aspects of mobile networks, especially packet data services (GPRS/UMTS) and real-time billing/prepaid.

My recent experience at both LogicaCMG and Cramer Systems is in product management and marketing, where I have shaped the propositions, trained and taken to market new products and/or specific solutions focussed on the mobile sector GSM/UMTS networks around the world.

David Chambers’s Specialties:

Mobile and Cellular Networks, Femtocells, OSS, Inventory, Prepaid Billing, GPRS, UMTS, SIP, IMS
