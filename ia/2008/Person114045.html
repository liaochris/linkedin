Kurt Arnlund’s Education

  • California State University-Sacramento

    Bachelors, Computer Science, 1991 — 1994

    I began as an Electrical Engineering (EEE) Major when I wanted to design audio effects for electric guitars and synths. Later I realized it was best if I stuck to what I killed at and that was programming.

    So I switched to Computer Science and pursued Systems Design, and Graphics as my specialties. I tore it up in the CS Logic Design classes because of my EEE class experience, and I went way overboard in my graphics classes. I rocked the A's in nearly all of my CS classes.

    Activities and Societies:
    My team and I were the winners of CSU Sacramento's student programming contest and we went on to place high at the regional programming contest held at CSU Fresno. I was a member of the ACM and Acoustic Society of America.
