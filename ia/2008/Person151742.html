Pieter de Zwart’s Summary

My goal is to present people, directly or indirectly, with facts and truths about different knowledge areas: 1) immigration, business and settlement procedures and 2) natural health and well being. It enables them to (re)claim their personal power, integrity and responsibility towards creating a more abundant life, be it personal, in business or spiritually.

Currently I also conduct workshops educating clients about alternative, non-invasive healing methods.
With a background and qualifications in Adult Education, complementary healing techniques as well as Interpersonal Communications and HR, I provide complementary modalities to clients to improve their quality of their personal and business relationships and interaction.

An experienced Educator in several industries such as: Aviation, Hospitality, IT, import/distribution, immigration consultancy and the many aspects of SME or SOHO Businesses.

I am an accredited Mentor with Business Mentors New Zealand, a 'Businesss in the Community' organisation. As well, being a YES (Youth Enterprise Scheme) mentor.

Professional experiences also include Business Management, Immigration Law and Practice, Operations Management, Sales and Sales Management, Marketing and General Administration.
