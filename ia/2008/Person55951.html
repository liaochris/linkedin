Trond Hjorteland-Rød’s Summary

Cand. Scient (equiv. to Master) in astrophysics, doing a thesis on numerical modelling in cosmology. During my studies, the Internet and all its available technologies intrigued me, which led to a position as a product consultant at Telia Norge AS (later Enitel AS), doing programming and logistics. Saw during this time the potential of the mobile part of the Internet, which resulted in relocation to djuice.com (Telenor Mobile). There worked with J2EE, WAP, XSL, XML, and such. After djuice.com was shut down, I have been working on J2EE in Middleware Mobile at Telenor Nordic.

My goal is to further my knowledge in both server and client side programming. I have detailed knowledge of Jav/J2EE, WML, XSL, XML, HTML, and CSS; good knowledge of Fortran 90, JavaScript, and PHP; and some knowledge of C, C++, SQL, Apache, Tomcat and BEA Weblogic. I also want to focus on good ways to do programming, i.e. utilizing code standards and patterns.

Trond Hjorteland-Rød’s Specialties:

Java programming, expecially in a J2EE system, utilized XP (Extreme Programming), refractoring, Scrum and domain modelling, Functional responsibility for CRM/Billing domain, and some MYSQL and PHP. Recently been part of an implementation of an EAI and SOA solution.
