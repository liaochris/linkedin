Robert van de Wiel’s Summary

(factor tachtig) is an agency for online marketing and more. Established by Jeroen van IJzendoorn and Robert van de Wiel. They both worked in online marketing for over 7 years before starting this company.

Online marketing -- We believe in ‘online’ as the starting point for all marketing communications. This 24/7 channel will always be the beginning and/or the end in your selling proces. With its rapid changes online marketing is a challenge on its own which should be managed professionally. Besides that, it’s not a static brochure. It needs ongoing attention.

And more -- But online can never become an island in the middle of all other channels. That's why (factor tachtig) will not stop advising when other channels are needed. We have extended experience in all channels and will use them when appropriate.

Philosophy -- We strongly believe in a combination of the words – experience, promise, and decision. Everything in marketing should be focused on those words. Experience will deliver the right branding of you company to the market. Promise is something that needs to be fulfilled from the biggest sales-deal to the smallest click online. Decision is where we always should drive to. Whether it’s buying or downloading a PDF file.

Get in touch -- As this is a summary, please feel free to contact us for an extended version. We’re more than happy to share our way of thinking and working with you and build an effective and efficient relationship.

Robert van de Wiel’s Specialties:

online marketing, usability, concepting, creativity, brand-building, online execution, intergrate online in other channels
