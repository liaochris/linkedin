  • Director of Dial-Up Product Management

    August 2002 — January 2004 (1 year 6 months)

Inherited a service with little to no product management controls, performed a turn-around by establishing operational metrics and P&L financial controls, which resulted in an improvement in customer satisfaction and increased gross margin to 75%.

    • Hired, trained and managed a staff of five product managers responsible for a product portfolio with over $900 million in revenue.
