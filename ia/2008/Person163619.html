Roger De Vos’s Summary

Roger started his career as consultant project director at Alexander Proudfoot Company and moved up very soon to chief of operations. He did this for many years.

Working with Comase in Belgium and F.R. Management in France, he acquired top level management disciplines together with computer software science.

Later, in the function of unit vice-president and vice-chairman of the European management team of Impac, the ‘united thoughts and common grounds’ team carried out reorganisation and management projects on international and top level resulting in an annual sales over 15 Mio £. A dynamic sales approach was applied in the realisation of the turnover, a professional method that makes the sales process so much more efficient than the classical model. A motivated and well-focussed operational team was the key formulae in ending successfully these missions.

Specialised in the domain of forecasting, make-to-order concept for manufacturing and servicing, goals, objectives, master scheduling and resources management, including work force and skills requirements, Roger directed projects on location with impressing outcome. In many cases the generated savings doubled the bottom line results while customer’s satisfaction was improved.

He has many years of international field experience in the management of the most various manufacturing methods and services, as interim plant and sales manager and as management and leadership trainer. With his large diversified expertise and his loyalty to the company he works for or with, he will contribute significantly to the profitability of that company.

The knowledge of computers and software strengthens Roger's operational and commercial effectiveness in getting perpetuating results through analyses and steering. He is an experienced VBA programmer at expert level for MS Access in combination with Visual Basic 2005. He is skilled in Ms Office, proficient in Excel and user of Sql and Oracle databases.
