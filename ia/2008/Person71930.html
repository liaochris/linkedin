David Poulos’s Summary

Acted as consulting marketer for Fortune 500 companies including Coca-Cola, Walt Disney, Madison Square Garden, Fannie Mae, and smaller trades and professional associations in the Washington Baltimore metro area. We offer marketing consulting services, including branding research and reinforcement, creative advertising and marketing campaigns, direct marketing and fully integrated marketing and public relations campaigns for companies large and small.

We strive to provide not only solutions for clients, but actual, measurable results that align with their overall corporate goals, including increased memberships, increased client base, increased sales figures and profitability.

David Poulos’s Specialties:

We specialize in research-based creative solutions - hard-hitting campaigns driven by and informed by primary and secondary research. We facilitate research interviews, long and short form surveys, focus groups and other techniques to reach in deep to the client or prospect base and root out the true purchase motivators and most active touch points among the buying audience to generate award-winning creative that packs a punch.
