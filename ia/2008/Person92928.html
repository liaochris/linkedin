Joanna Sherwell’s Summary

Joining forces with a coach is like having your own team of personal assistants dedicated to your success and with you every step of the way! A mentor to believe in you and help you set clear and exciting goals ... A manager to break down the goals into 'do-able bits'... A personal trainer that will keep you moving forward on your path ... And a sports coach to give you feedback to help improve your game. The coaching relationship is an alliance, a partnership where coach and client join forces to create ideas, solutions, and new learnings. We are constantly in awe at the changes and new found empowerment we see in our clients through their own focus and commitment! That is what drives us as coaches and as fellow human beings- to see others achieving so much around us.

At Untapped Potential we believe that every person on earth has the resources and power within them to create a life of 100% fulfilment; the life that as children we excitedly imagined ourselves living when we were grown ups. What changes as we grow up? What causes us to let these dreams fade into the background and become fantasies of some lost time? We believe that those hopes and dreams still exist within you, we just need to light the fire under them again! And we absolutely believe that you can be that exact person that is the main character of those dreams… YOU!
You can create that life for yourself!
We believe you deserve nothing less.

Contact Untapped now for your first introductory session on us!!
Your schedule is a priority and we can tailor a program to suit your lifestyle and budget.

Thank you for your interest in Untapped Potential and we look forward to hearing from you soon!
