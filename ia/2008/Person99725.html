judit price’s Summary

As a career counselor and job search coach with over 28 years experience, I work with each client to help them understand the uniqueness that represents their skills, experience, interests and values. I guide clients to analyze strengths and accomplishments to determine the right choices, create a clear career objectives and build a strategy to get there.

Once we determined direction, I work with clients to build practical tools, the resume, cover letter and other documents, to communicate effectively with prospective employers. Those tools are complimented with training in job search strategies, networking, and how to sell yourself with a powerful interview.

As a career coach and consultant I am dedicated to providing cost-effective services to individuals or firms on employment related issues.

I have an MS in counseling and am a certified guidance counselor in Massachusetts and New Hampshire, a writer and lecturer on employment, educational and career topics and have taught numerous educational/career counseling courses in local institutions. I am also an employment and career transition consultant and coach, and a consultant to outplacement and career marketing firms, and an accomplished trainer and presenter to local business and non-profit organization.

I write a bi-weekly column for a local newspaper, appear frequently in local media and have been interviewed by national media, most recently by the Wall Street Journal. I wrote and published a Career and Life Portfolio Guide through JIST publication and my resumes appear in many featured resume books, but most importantly clients gets job offers. My credentials include;

Certified Career Master, (CCM)
Certified Career Guidance Counselor, (MS)
International Job and Career Transition Coach (IJCTC)
Career Development Facilitator Instructor (CDFI)
Certified Professional Resume Writer (CPRW)
Certified Brand Specialist
