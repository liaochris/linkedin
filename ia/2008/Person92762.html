Jennifer Tracy’s Summary

Having lived in Washington my whole life, I have extensive knowledge of the area. My academic background includes finishing high school with a high school diploma and an Associates degree in the Arts and Sciences at Shoreline Community college through the Running Start program. At the age of twenty, I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor’s degree.

My personal goal is to make every real estate transaction go smoothly and look seemless from start to finish. If you are looking for a good agent who provides excellent customer service please give me a call or email.
