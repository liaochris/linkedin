Frank Mulligan’s Summary

Siemens Ltd China, Strategic Planning Manager in the mid-90's. Chief Representative of largest UK headhunter, Norman Broadbent International until 2000.

Now CEO(owner) of Talent Software and Managing Partner of Accetis International. We provide companies with a hiring solution that includes software (ATS). a Call Centre, a Toyota Production System (TPS) methodology and outsourced staffing and operations (RPO).

Accetis is focused heavily on the automotive industry and at all levels of staffing. We run the Toyota Production System (TPS) and work for automotive suppliers in a process orientated way that fits in with their method of operating.

More here on my blog and an online TV programme that I host:

http://www.talentinchina.com/ http://www.itv-asia.com

We operate on an RPO basis so we can break up the application so that we handle any or all parts of the hiring cycle.
