Brian Tucker’s Summary

Twenty years experience developing innovative teams and break-though personal computer and digital media products. Successfully managed multi-million dollar programs and large teams across a variety of disciplines - marketing, strategy, engineering and planning – in companies small to large. Extensive experience influencing customers and partners including major media and consumer electronics companies, and broadcast and broadband service providers. Awarded patents covering personal computer architecture, and media encode and playback. Technical expertise in content protection (DRM and conditional access), media architectures and codecs and computer and graphics design.
