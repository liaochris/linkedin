Ward Thomas’s Summary

Mr. Thomas is an internationally recognized expert in New Product Introductions (NPI), delivering first customers and predictable revenue/cash flow to management teams for 18 years. His proven New Product Introduction Process has delivered 100’s of millions in income statement value, which fostered trusted relationships within the Fortune 100, C-level management. His NPI process was developed with some of he biggest names in technology and has changed has changed the early adoption model “Crossing the Chasm”.

NPI Experience:
Ward delivered the “Go To Market” strategy for the first Semiconductor, ECAD, MCAD design systems on the Microsoft OS with Cadence Design Systems and Autodesk. He successfully sold this business to Cadence in 1998. During the DOTCOM he delivered the first eCommerce solutions to F50 C-Level executives along side IBM Global Service, Accenture and EDS. He was a member of the management team that sold this $150m business to i2 Technologies for $9.8B in 2001. During the Internet delivery wave, he planned, funded and delivered 4 Software as a Service and Managed Service Provider products to market to clients in the F50 and US Government.

Corporate P&L Experience:
He has lead a $50M division at Cadence and grew from zero a $100M division at i2 Technologies.(NASDAQ: ITWO) He has also run P&L for early stage business 0-$10M, several times.
Business Model Experience: Mr. Thomas has been on the forefront of new business models. Electronic components, Engineering Software, and PLM enterprise software through direct and franchised channels, ebusiness, Internet delivered software as a service (SAAS) and Internet delivered managed service. (MSP)
