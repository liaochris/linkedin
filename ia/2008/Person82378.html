Jaime Ursic’s Experience

  • Workshop Coordinator

    The HeArt Project

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Arts and Crafts industry)

    March 2008 — Present (3 months)

    The HeArt Project brings arts education to Los Angeles teenagers, providing them with ongoing workshops with professional artists and opportunities to present their work in major cultural institutions. Working with 30 alternative high schools, the program currently serves over 450 students annually.

    Key responsibilities include:
    • Ensure that each classroom boasts an effective learning atmosphere.
    • Manage classroom during workshops with teaching artists.
    • Act as liaison with artist, teacher, students.
    • Manage budget for purchase & distribution of art supplies.
    • Administer HP evaluation portfolios/journals.
    • Maintain class observations & student attendance.
    • Help students to participate in the HeArt Project’s progressively
    challenging ladder of opportunities.
    • Prep student speakers for workshop public presentations.
    • Manage, document, transport student art for public presentations.
    • Document art-making process by students
