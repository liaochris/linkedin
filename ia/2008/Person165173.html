Rahul Panchal’s Summary

Rahul has been recognized nationally and internationally winning almost every major advertising award. For two years in a row Rahul and Crispin Porter & Bogusky have won Best of Show at the One Show, the Grand Clio, the Grand Prix at Cannes, and Interactive Agency of the Year at Cannes. His work at CP+B has been showcased in publications including the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Newsweek, ID magazine, Creativity, Ad Critic, Ad Week, STEP Magazine, Communication Arts, and the Art Directors Club.

While at Wieden + Kennedy, he worked with the London office to win Nokia's 300mil global advertising contract. Then on to Y&R NYC helping to grow the agencies interactive capabilities.

Rahul's first word was Pepsi, he owns an acre of land on the moon, and his band Ayrwulf is available on iTunes with a cult following in Australia and Iceland. He also runs a beverage startup and a online creative marketplace for innovation and ideas.
