Bubonic Media’s Summary

Bubonic Media is a business development and graphic design company with an emphasis in branding and (viral) marketing.

Without looking professional in Today's market, an organization will sink before it floats.

Making a great and lasting first impression is where it's at, even if behind the scenes you're lounging around in your underwear.

Our goal is to provide the best our industry has to offer and take whatever measures are necessary to go above and beyond the client's expectations.

Bubonic Media’s Specialties:

Digital and Analog Graphic Design, Web Design, E-Commerce, Motion Graphics, 2D and 3D Animation, Audio / Visual Multimedia, Television Commercial, Radio Commercial, Print Media, Branding and Identity, Online and Offline Marketing, Non-linear Editing and Composition, and much, much more...
