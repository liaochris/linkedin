Igor Barenboim’s Summary

PhD in Economics, Harvard University. My main research interests are related to the biggest puzzles in Latin America: (i) under-education, (ii) crime, (iii) income redistribution. Read my papers on my Harvard website. http://www.people.fas.harvard.edu/~ibarenb/papers.html

Co-Founder and Director of Finance and Business Development of Power Ventures. We are a global company based in Brazil. We are building a new operating system for the internet. Check us out and become our user: www.power.com.

Founding Team Member and Global Economist of Brazilian Hedge Fund with Global Investments, Gavea Investimentos. I have a lot of talented friends working at Gavea and I truly admire the main partner of the fund Arminio Fraga. You should invest with them if you can get an allocation. www.gaveainvest.com.br.

Financial and Revenue Maximization Consultant for Farmacia Vita, a retail and wholesale of medicine company in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Buy medicines from us online. We ship everywhere. Medicines produced in Brazil are among the cheapest in the world. www.vitanet.com.br

Held positions at UN and Citigroup, Washington D.C. and New York.

Active participant in national economic policy debates in Brazil. Read some recent articles at http://www.people.fas.harvard.edu/~ibarenb/papers.html
