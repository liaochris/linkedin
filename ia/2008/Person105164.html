Kent Clothier’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur at heart and love the challenge of starting and growing businesses. I've focused primarily on sales-based companies and have served in almost every capacity: Founder, Board Member, CEO, COO, Business Development, and Sales. I've raised institutional financing, grown businesses, down-sized businesses, and sold businesses.

I have an extensive background in executive management of sales and marketing organizations for both private and public companies. I have a true passion for leading and motivating people to reach a common goal.

After many years of real estate investing and building a brand, I started a company to combine my two passions. 1-800-SELL-NOW has grown to become a nationally recognized brand in the real estate market. We have now taken our branding expertise into a new vertical and developed a new brand, 1-877-VIOLATION.
