Tiger Bitanga’s Summary

In August 1999, Tiger Bitanga co-founded The Design People, Inc., an Internet marketing and web development studio with over 7,000 customers as of 2008.

We combine high touch, personalized attention with high-tech, software-as-as-service (SaaS) tools. We make it easy for our clients to get online, professionally brand their businesses and start highly customized, geo-targeted marketing programs through search engines. We launch hundreds of new websites and search marketing campaigns every month.

The firm is headquartered in Los Angeles and employs an expert team of over 150 project managers, web designers, client success managers, web strategists and art directors.

The Design People, Inc. has worked with notable clients including Intermix Media (creators of MySpace.com), Finisar, UCLA Jules Stein Eye Institute, Hilton & Hyland, Prudential California Realty and Rusnak Auto Group.

Today, 98% of our customers are small to medium-sized businesses in the U.S. -- primarily agents, brokers and realty firms through Agent Image, our real estate division and pioneer in real estate technology and marketing.

We are currently expanding to international markets and other key verticals including legal, accounting, dental, mortgage, cosmetic surgery and restaurant industries.

Web strategy, Internet marketing, web usability, ecommerce, real estate technology, website design/development, search engine marketing/optimization, email marketing, contact center management, graphic design, desktop publishing, interactive media and branding.
