Aaron Fish, MBA’s Summary

As an individual with a passion for marketing & management, I am always looking for opportunities to use my extensive knowledge and experience to enhance the overall effectiveness of strategy, marketing and communications for an organization.

While the largest part my experience has been in the hospitality industry, the skills that I have developed translate well into many different areas. I have experience dealing with engineers, architects, sales staff, accounting, IT and almost every C-suite occupant (CEO, CFO, COO, and VPs). This has allowed me to gain invaluable insight into all facets of business and be a contributor. I have experience working on strategic planning, strategic development tools, marketing plans, and product development. I am known as the “go-to guy” when it comes to creating winning experiences and creating memorable events.

My current position as a Department Head has also given me experience leading and developing all facets of operations, marketing as well as responsibility for functional-level strategies. I have forecasted, managed and attained multi-million dollar operational budgets, while making the focus of all my efforts the customer. I believe that having a passion for building relationships--either mentoring and developing staff or listening to the customer to create an excellent product--is the key to creating an overall winning experience.
