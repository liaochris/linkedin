Gail Lynne Goodwin’s Summary

My name is Gail Lynne Goodwin and I’m the Ambassador of Inspiration and founder of Inspire Me Today™- the website that provides the Best of the Best Inspiration daily. Each day we bring you inspiration from a different Inspirational Luminary from household names like Jack Canfield (Chicken Soup for the Soul), Janet Attwood (The Passion Test) and Dr. John Dimartini (The Secret), to 7-year old author and inspirational speaker, Sami FitzGerald who teaches the art of gratitude.

I have always been a seeker of information, especially that of a spiritual or motivational nature. I discovered that on mornings when I went to the gym with something inspirational, my entire day went better, as my daily performance was directly tied to the level of my morning inspiration.

The challenge began to find a continual supply of dynamic, high-quality daily inspiration. When I realized this search was becoming a part-time job, I knew there must be others looking for the same thing. And on that morning, Inspire Me Today™ was born.

We welcome you to Inspire Me Today™ and invite you to begin your day with us, knowing you will experience a profound difference in your life. We'll help YOU connect to YOU and remember who you really are. We invite you to take that invincible “Yeeeeeehaaaaa!” feeling and make it a regular part of your day. From there, the magic begins.

Inspire Me Today™ is here to help you remember that you, and you alone, have the power to do, be or create anything in your life that you desire. Anything. Your power is unlimited. Your wildest dreams are attainable. There is magic in belief, promise in faith and delivery in action. We believe you were born to fly, and all you need is a little inspiration. Let us help you put on your wings each morning, for when you’re inspired, the magic of the entire Universe kicks into full gear to work on your behalf. Your dream life is waiting for you. So, what are you waiting for? Join us and let's make it happen together!
