Angelo Cavaliere’s Summary

Angelo Cavaliere joined Compaq (now HP) as the Manager of Storage Solutions Marketing, where he established and managed a strategically focused team tasked with applying storage technologies to enterprise business issues. As part of the transitional requirements pertaining to the merger with HP, he managed strategically focused product and marketing initiatives, which included the business negotiations for cross-licensing intellectual property with EMC, IBM, and Hitachi. This was followed by the management of three world-wide technical pre-sales support teams that were responsible for providing innovative solutions to complex customer issues.

Over the course of his career, Angelo has built expertise in Product Management, Solution Development, Product Marketing, Systems Engineering, Software Engineering, and Software Engineering Management. He has a successful track record of building and leading multi-disciplinary teams, fostering innovation, and generating sales revenues across a wide variety of jobs. His responsibilities have spanned global markets.

At EMC Corporation, he held positions in both Technical Sales Support and Product Management. Starting as the original product manager, he built and directed the Product Management team responsible for EMC’s Information Management software product family. This team was a key contributor to EMC’s highly successful entry into the storage management software marketplace.

Earlier, he held a variety of technical sales support and field marketing positions at other leading technology companies, including Amdahl and StorageTek.

Angelo holds a BS degree in Computer Science from the Columbia University School of Engineering and Applied Science, and has continued his education at the American Management Association, the Center for Creative Leadership, and the University of Connecticut.
