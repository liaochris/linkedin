Pierre Crevoisier’s Summary

A first diploma in psychopedagogy at the University of Fribourg (Switzerland). Many years working as journalist in various Swiss media (press, radio and television). Postgraduate student at TECFA in 97-98, Faculty of Psychology and Sciences of Education. Since 2001, working as information architect at EPFL, oriented in the development of "new IT projects".
