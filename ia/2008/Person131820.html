Mike Afford’s Summary

Former Senior Designer for the BBC in London, recently relocated to Ireland. Over 20 years experience in TV, mostly within BBC News at Television Centre. Many industry awards for Graphic Design.

Mike Afford’s Specialties:

Motion graphics design - animated information content.Title sequences. Design for real-time or data-driven graphics systems (weather, elections).

Web design - HTML & CSS (hand-coding and Dreamweaver), Flash

After Effects, Photoshop, Flash, Illustrator, Cinema 4D, various Quantel systems. Design for VizRT, tOG, Brainstorm, Weatherscape XT

Music - title sequences, e.g. Fast Track (BBC travel show), Reporters (BBC), India Business Report (BBC).
