Andrea Schwartz’s Summary

I am an IT Technical Recruiter with in-house, on-site, remote and agency experience. I love what I do, and love finding the right people for my teams. The tougher the technical need, the more excited I am about getting the right person on board.
Feel free to contact me, and to forward my contact information. It's my job!!

My last position was a contract at Credit Suisse, in beautiful Raleigh North Carolina. I was an in-house recruiter for their FID IT team, working with my product managers to hire and manage a workforce of software devs and testers, and some support personnel for the Raleigh Center of Excellence here in RTP.

Andrea Schwartz’s Specialties:

Building relationships with candidates and managers, and helping the right person into the right position at the right time. Specifically, I believe the core of my job is finding who the real person is, and bringing into my teams the best of the best.
