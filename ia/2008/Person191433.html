Ton Bil’s Summary

* typical sky-is-the-limit strategist with clear getting-down-to-earth flight plan
* ample research & consultancy experience with b-to-b marketing & sales
* inventor of the "compass approach" - on how to organize the innovation of value creation by businesses
* analytical & creative skills
* facilitator of change through meetings, trainings etc.

Since 1983 I am helping companies and other organisations to develop and implement strategic choices for their value creation. They flourish when they maximize their contribution to their stakeholders. I got formal education in marketing and psychology and am a member of Mensa.
