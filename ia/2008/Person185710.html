Diana Tarpanova’s Summary

Diana is a seasoned professional in interactive development with 9 years of web design and development experience, in the areas of creative direction, Web 2.0 and RIA application development, project management, and business consulting.

As a member of the Front End Development team at Macquarium Intelligent Communications, Diana has participated in the development Web 2.0 sites and applications for clients such as Yahoo!, UPS, CNN, Emory University, COX Communications, Choice Point, and more.

She builds result driven work, relying on usability centered designs, standards compliant web development, and synergistic application of technology. Diana works comfortably with high-level XHTML, XML, XSL, DOM, AJAX, CSS, JavaScript , ActionScript 1, 2 & 3, PHP and SQL, delivering work with web standards, accessibility, 508, usability, SEO, Object Oriented Programming, Flash, Content Management Systems (Joomla, Drupal, Alfresco, PHPNuke), Eclipse, AmfPHP, 3Ds Max, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and MySQL.

In addition to technical experience, she has a sound business background, with results in areas of Management, Negotiations, Business Development, Advertising, and Online Marketing. Diana has written several business plans and models for the introduction of innovative applications, expansion of service offerings, and development of new businesses. Following the latest in Harvard, Wharton, and MIT Business and Technology curriculum research, she strives to add value, innovation, and results to every project, team, and organization she has had the pleasure of working with.

For professional contacts or other inquiries, please, feel free to join Diana's network. ( diana@tarpanova.com )
