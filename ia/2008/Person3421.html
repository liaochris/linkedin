Adrian Wiggins’s Summary

Since 1998 I have produced and/or architected a wide range of sites: sports events, inflight entertainment, training, product promotion, brand marketing, customer relationship, online classifieds, news media and intranets.

Clients in my time as a web producer and experience architect include AMP, Qantas, BigPond, BankWest, Railcorp, DSRD, Country Road, the Cruising Yacht Club of Australia, NSW Cancer Council, Drive, Domain and RSVP amongst others.

Several sites I've worked on have gone on to win national and internation awards.

Adrian Wiggins’s Specialties:

I specialise in user centred design, experience architecture, interaction design, online marketing and web production.
