Daniel Villagomez’s Summary

Senior level Theatrical Screen Credits Specialist with 17+ years experience in the Motion Picture Industry with extensive knowledge of MPAA and Guild advertising requirements, policies and procedures. Credits Administrator on over 70 films inlcuding Paramount's "Lara Croft: Tomb Raider," "The Hours," and "Sum of All Fears"; New Line Cinema's "Hairspray," "Wedding Crashers" and the complete "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy; Picturehouse' "A Prairie Home Companion," "Fur" and "Pan's Labyrinth"; and animated projects including Nickelodeon Movies' "Rugrats in Paris," "Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius" and "Hey, Arnold!: The Movie."

Daniel Villagomez’s Specialties:

Theatrical Screen Credits, WGA credit arbitrations, WGA Policy Review Board, DGA credit/residual determinations, Guild signatory applications, MPAA Title Clearance, Copyright filings, Corporation formations
