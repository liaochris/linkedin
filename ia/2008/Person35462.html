Committed to delivering innovation, Accenture collaborates with its clients to help them become high-performance businesses and governments.

With approximately 175,000 people in 49 countries, deep industry and business process expertise, broad global resources and a proven track record, Accenture can mobilize the right people, skills and technologies to help clients improve their performance.
