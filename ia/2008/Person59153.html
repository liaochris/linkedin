Enrique Gaubeca’s Summary

After working in basic research, started activities in large CROs (1998). In his eight years at Quintiles Argentina, he both led regional/global studies as well as supervised clinical activities in Argentina, Colombia, Costa Rica, Peru and Venezuela. In his most recent position at PAREXEL Argentina, he was Sr. Clinical Operations Manager responsible for clinical operations and regulatory affairs. He is currently acting as Managing Director at KLIXAR, a Contract Research Organization with scope of activities in Latin America (enrique.gaubeca@klixar.com).
