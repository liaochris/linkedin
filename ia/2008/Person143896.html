Oliver Picher’s Summary

Oliver Picher is a Senior Marketing and Communications Professional with more than 20 years experience in the high tech, professional services, and human resource consulting fields. He is an expert in public relations, branding, analyst relations, and internal communications and understands how enhancing an organization's visibility, image, influence, and market position can drive the achievement of its strategic goals.

A creative and resourceful executive, Oliver has played an integral role in defining and executing the marketing and communications components of numerous business initiatives and has successfully counseled senior management in crisis situations.

public relations, branding, analyst relations, internal communications, and business-to-business marketing
