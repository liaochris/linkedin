I. Erdinc Mutlu’s Summary

Erdinc Mutlu is working as Senior Software Development Engineer in Amstrad Plc, located in Brentwood, Greater London, England. He is originally from Turkey.

Erdinc has been in the software and electronics industry since his graduation from university in 1998 and worked in several countries in different companies, on several positions, which enhanced his experience. His previous positions on previous companies were Senior Project Manager, Chief Software Engineer, Software Team Leader, Research and Development Engineer, Network Planning Engineer. He has worked in Belgium, Hungary and Turkey before moving to England.

He has solid knowledge of Digital Television-Digital Video Broadcasting (DVB-S, -T, -C) and interactive TV (OpenTV, MHP), Digital Communication Systems, especially GSM, and Transmission Technologies. Has extensive experience on embedded systems, device drivers, real-time operating system, ANSI-C, C++, project management, technical team leading, software project lead for smart cards.

After his study at Izmir Science High School (Izmir Fen Lisesi) Erdinc studied in Bilkent University (Ankara/Turkey) Electrical and Electronics department and got BSc degree in 1998, and after he got MSc degree in the same field in 2001 from Dokuz Eylul (9 Eylul) university (Izmir/Turkey).

He is interested in broaden his network.
