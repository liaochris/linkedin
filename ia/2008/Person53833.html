Douglas Marshall’s Summary

As director of Sales I work to grow business by supporting accounts with on-going training, incentives and planning special events. I work directly with buyers, salespeople and brand managers.
I oversee and manage the following accounts:Saks Fifth Avenue New York, Harvey Nichols UK and Dubai, Lane Crawford in Hong Kong and David Jones in Australia as well as two major web accounts: Bliss and BeautyExpert.co.uk.

In January 2008 we launched on ShopNBC and this has been my biggest accomplishment to date. I manage all aspects of the relationship including product registration (making sure all cosmetic claims are FTC approved), Before & After case studies, testimonials, fulfillment of large scale orders in kits. We exceeded our first round of show goals by 86% and are one of the most successful skin care launches that ShopNBC has had.

I drive press from IN HOUSE and also work with a PR company in the UK who reps our brand. I maintain relationships with beauty editors of top publications to create press opportunities for Dr. Steven Victor, including securing editorial coverage, coordinating public appearances, tv appearances, etc.

We are in the process of launching new products. Some of my duties on these projects include directing a biochemist to create new formulations, sourcing packaging, producing inserts and meeting launch deadlines. I have signifianct input in packaging creation for the stevenvictormd line, working alongside our in house creative director.

In my free time I enjoy writing articles on beauty, fashion, lifestyle and skin care for a couple websites. I take great pleasure in writing and hope to tap into that talent more and more. My most recent venture was to start a 'serial' type of column called "Confessions of a Product Junkie" and it allows me to do what I love: GAB about the BEST products out there which I love....The feedback on the column has been really great and has been syndicated on a few websites/blogs.

Douglas Marshall’s Specialties:

I started here before the products were even in boxes; now we are distributing globally, growing rapidly and extending into new and exciting lines. I like to think I have had a hand in the successful growth of my company. I have an instinctive knowledge of what consumers want because I have such a passion for skin care which started back when I was 16 and stealing my mom's Prescriptives AHA moisturizer and used it daily. This passion is translating into a fruitful career.
