Jagdish Chand’s Summary

At Yahoo, I am working on some of the toughest problem in large data processing and analysis. Here are some of examples:

Affinity Engine/Recommendation:

Invented mechanism to compute the affinity analysis in the large data set and built Affinity Engine based upon the invention. Received patent # : 6873996.

Evangelized the usage of the affinity engine across the different business unit across Yahoo. As a result many properties started using affinity data. examples are:
1. Yahoo! Personals: To recommend similar profile
2. Yahoo! Music : Playlist creation, similar artist, similar users, similar songs in Yahoo Music Engine.
3. Yahoo! Hotjobs : Recommendation of similar jobs when you applying for given job as well as personalized recommendation based upon past history.
4.Similar Keyword recommendation as well as Competitor discovery.
5. Plus many more

5-6 follow up patents are pending related to affinity analysis and personalized recommendation.

Path Analysis:

Invented a mechanism to compute the inflow/outflow and funnel analysis for large data set involving 100's of millions of users, traversing millions of pages and coming from millions of referral sources.

extended the system to compute most frequent paths between any two arbitrary nodes and discovery of starting/ending paths to/from any arbitrary node.

Conversion Analysis:

Implemented a system for conversion analysis and improved performance from 55 hrs processing time to 10 hr processing time on the same hardware.

Technical Leadership:

Managing team of highly energetic and motivated engineering professionals.
