Bo Lora’s Summary

I've been involved with the Internet since 1987. I've worked on the hardware as well as the software side of the Internet. I've designed web sites as well as written many programs. Then I realized that while I had fun doing all that, some few years ago I missed a boat. A couple actually. I didn't buy Yahoo or Google stock and here I was trading hours for dollars like the rest of us. That's when I got serious about changing the way I look at work.

I wanted to be leverage my knowledge and lifetime experiences as a consultant and work with corporations, small companies and individuals to help them maximize their production.
