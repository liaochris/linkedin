José Luis Iturbide’s Summary

Jose Luis is a software engineer with experience since 2000. He has worked in all phases of system development using several languages and platforms. He is interested now in integration of systems, security topics and web developing with new technologies.

He holds 5 Certifications of Sun MicroSystems, SCJP, SCJD, SCWCD, SCBCD, SCEA-I and he is developing a project to get the SCEA 5.0 certification.

At this moment he is working as JEE Architect for Vision Consulting Company and as instructor for Netec's Certification Center, both in Mexico City.

Jose Luis wants to continue working as expert consultant in analysis, design and implementation of Information Technology solutions.
