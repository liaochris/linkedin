Jeremy McMillan’s Summary

Business/media professional who embraces strategic thinking and entrepreneurial spirit. Extensive experience in management – business development, sales, marketing-advertising-promotions, new business platforms – for various start-up and SMSE’s in consumer-packaged goods and media where strategic planning and decisive execution of new business initiatives were utilized.

Entrepreneurial content producer and consultant active with the development/financing of multi-platform media projects and in the start-up stage for independent production companies. Heavy engagement in media has seen Jeremiah's work featured in various media and business magazines/periodicals, as well as receiving international exposure for media projects at acclaimed mobile/digital media film festivals like Palm Mobifest and LA TV festival 2006 (previously NextGen TV Fest), including a segment about mobile TV on ABC’s Good Morning America.

My goal is to continue advising TV/film production companies on re-purposing/managing their content for new and emerging platforms, as well as continuing to develop original content with working business/revenue models that provide measured and substantial growth potential.
