Faizan Siddiqi’s Summary

Faizan Siddiqi comes to the Manta Group with the operational expertise of founding a boutique Governance Risk & Compliance company in South East Asia. Faizan successfully led his governance startup through its formation, commercialization of specialist GRC tools, widening its geographic foot print and value-creating acquisition by a regional private equity fund. Subsequent to the acquisition he stayed on with the PE Fund and helped develop an incubation & acquisition model to diversify their portfolio investments.

Prior to that he was with Tyco International US Inc. where he served within Corporate Governance in the Corporate Audit function and played an instrumental role in setting up innovative audit techniques and processes to enhance Tyco’s Internal IT Audit practice.

Consulting with and advising Tyco’s Six business segments comprising upwards of one thousand entities in 50+ Countries to implement and foster a Risk Based approach, Faizan has frontline experience in Sarbanes Oxley Compliance. He has led select Acquisition Integration Studies and Project Audits for the 40Bn$ group. He has extensive experience around global compliance regulation and in leading global and virtual teams.

Prior to that he was with GE Advanced Materials (AM) in Holland and was intimately involved with designing and developing risk mitigation strategies for IT contractual agreements. Previous to GE Faizan served with the office of the CIO at Digex Inc. (MCI-WorldCom).

Faizan’s SoX & GRC experience includes Italy, Great Britain, The Netherlands, Spain, France, Germany, USA, Czech Republic, Canada, Belgium, Pakistan, India, Oman, Dubai & Abu-Dhabi.

Faizan servers on the Advisory Board of Amaana LLC, which is an alternate payment platform for emerging markets.

Faizan has a BS from Purdue University and a MSc. from the London School of Economics and Political Science. He is a Six Sigma Certified professional.
