Abhishek Kishore Gupta’s Summary

As Senior Associate with IT advisory of KPMG India, Abhishek has executed and managed projects primarily in the following areas:
- Business systems/IT controls design and review (with specific focus on SAP controls)
- IT Audit,
- IT Governance including SoX Advisory,
- ISO27001 advisory

Abhishek has executed projects in SAP Governance Risk & Compliance (GRC) including analysis of Access control processes, Segregation of Duties (SoD) review, identifying conflicting transaction, advising client on approach for remediation.

Prior to KPMG, Abhishek was with HCL Technologies as a Business Analyst, HCLT is one of the largest Indian IT firm.
An engineering graduate with MBA (Sys & Mkt), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), ITIL Foundation certified, and BS7799 LA ; Abhishek has over 4 years of experience in the area of Risk Advisory.
