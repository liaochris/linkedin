Linda Udy’s Summary

I have specialized in placing insurance restoration professionals of all levels on a national basis for the past 8.5 years. I have placed candidates from the VP level on down to the Superintendent level. I work with insurance restoration contractors who do 100% insurance restoration work or who have a division who specializes in this industry. I also work with related restoration industries such as garment restoration and also restoration vendors. I place Sales and Marketing candidates who are national to local sales people.
I established the Lothian Group in 2000 and grew it till in 2008 I moved my business and rolled it into a very large national recruiting firm called the Lucas Group. We have a team of over 400+ recruiters and 17 offices in the US and 2 in Germany. I made this move to be able to offer my clients and candidates additional services and bring their recruiting experience to the next level. Something I felt was an important feature for my restoration clients was Lucas Group has a large military recruiting division. I have struggled for years to come up with a solid solution to the lack of reliable candidates to assume the technician and crew chief roles. I feel that we can develop a strong source for reliable candidates for these roles from ex-military candidates and welcome feedback on this idea.
