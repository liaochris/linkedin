Jennifer Maggiore’s Summary

My business is growing yours.

Since 1998, I have worked for several large corporations including Nordstrom, M & I Bank, the Valley of the Sun YMCA, been an integral part of a start up company and owned my business since 2005. I have developed a fabulous network of business contacts and friends, and I work with the best consultants and independents in a variety of industries across the country. The result is powerful, effective marketing, branding and business development services, with a plan that is fully developed, managed and executed by an expert. Whether you are looking for a consultant or a “fire and forget” scenario, you just need one creative expert to bring it all together for you- call me today!

For a time, I was a consultant and acting COO for MetaMorph Strategies, an integrated marketing company, where I was integral in writing the business plan and launching business development aspects for this start up company. Some of the articles I’ve written about marketing and customer service are featured at eZineArticles.com.

I grew up in South Florida and moved to the Phoenix area in 2000. After attending the University of Florida and Arizona State University, I graduated from the University of Phoenix in 2005 and hold degrees in Journalism and Marketing. My background includes experience with small business entrepreneurship, business start ups, and both the corporate and non-profit sectors for national organizations, including banking, retail, real estate, the automotive aftermarket, restaurants and more.

Last but not least, I have a fabulous family who I love cooking for (that’s a big Italian family for you!) and spending time with. I also enjoy traveling, digital photography, and art, and have rarely met a bottle of wine I didn’t care for!

Jennifer Maggiore’s Specialties:

Successful business plan writing, start up scenarios, business development, sales consulting, public speaking, branding, marketing, consumer research, designing and executing surveys, TV production, bringing new products to market, creative concept development and public relations.
