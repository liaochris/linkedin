Anthony Love, ABR, CRB, CRS, GRI’s Summary

Born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Anthony Love has served in the U.S. Marine Corps 11 1/2 years prior to beginning an exciting career as a real estate professional. As an Onslow County (Jacksonville, NC)resident since 1988, Anthony joined the CENTURY 21 Champion Real Estate team in 1997. Anthony brings with him the same dedication, attention to detail, and untiring work ethics to serve his community as he did serving his country while in the military. Being a husband and father of three, Anthony knows how important family is and is enthusiastic about helping yours with your real estate needs.
