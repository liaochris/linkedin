João Magalhães’s Summary

I’m a passionate developer that loves to create powerful and scalable business applications, using the most recent "cutting-edge" technologies and methodologies.
I’ve started my business software applications passion when I worked at the Microsoft Development Center in Copenhagen. During that period I worked at a small development team that developed the product configuration solution for the Microsoft Dynamics AX ERP. The enthusiasm and the ideas that I acquired there inspired and encouraged me to create something new and powerful for the SME business application market.
That belief supported me in the creation of a new project that I'm currently working on. The idea of a business application that is completely service oriented and the idea of a software product that is a service (SaaS) are my most enthusiastic beliefs, and the basic objectives of my new project.
