Alder Crocker’s Summary

Partner at SFA, Inc., a brand calibration and communications company in Southport, Connecticut, and have 17+ years of experience marketing corporate, business-to-business and consumer products and services for a range of domestic and international client brands.

Strategic analysis / creative hybrid with expertise in:

- advertising, direct marketing, collateral, interactive, promotions, public relations, sales training, trade shows, new business development, and in....

- software and hardware technologies, entertainment, telecommunications, automotive, consumer and household products, specialty chemicals, utility, electronics, DIY, banking, supply chain management, healthcare industries.

Have worked at S-M-L agencies, and a client-side, Internet company stint where I handled all marketing and strategy, and raised VC funding. Have managed corporate, product and service brand programs for companies including IBM, Sony, BMW, Sherwin-Williams, O-Cedar, MacDermid, Integrated Logistics Solutions, Hunt Technologies, American Gas Association, United Technologies, ARCO, UST, Madison Square Garden, Crown Theatres, Centrix Dental, Connecticut Community Bank and others..

Currently handle strategic brand calibration programs for all SFA clients, and oversee creative development, account management and Internet-related communications initiatives for specific clients.

Also work with private equity companies on marketing forensics due diligence projects during their asset evaluation phase.

Alder Crocker’s Specialties:

Branding and identity is probably the "catch-all", but what I do is much more dimensionalized: I create and/or blend marketing and business growth strategies into thorough programs that help companies de-commoditize themselves to their target audiences, out-position their competitors and reinvigorate employees.
