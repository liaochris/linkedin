Marvin Powell’s Summary

As an author, lecturer, business coach and financial professional for more than thirteen years Powell has created value for the best and brightest of Northern Virginia. Marvin is dedicated to providing business owners with the professional attention necessary to help them achieve their financial goals, offering continued service as their needs change.

Marvin offers a variety of wealth accumulation products and services to clients which include annuities, insurance and investments, as well as business, estate and retirement planning.

Marvin cherishes the opportunity to work with entrepreneurs on executive consulting , business succession and key-man planning with some of his favorite business and professional associations:
*Retired Military Officers Association
*BNI Northern Virginia
*Fairfax County Small Business Commission
*INOVA Gift and Estate Planning Council

It seems as if Mr. Powell's personal motto "a good life is spent in the service of others" is actualized any his many charitable contributions and councils. Formally he has been affiliated with:
*Antioch Baptist Church Entrepreneurs Council
*Phi Beta Sigma Bigger and Better Business
*Compton Village Home Owners Association

Today's organizations include:
*Centreville Community Foundation
*Fairfax County Computer Learning Center Partnership
*Selection Advisory Committee-Consolidated Community Funding Pool

Mr. Powell, a devoted husband of Kimberly Teresa and father to daughter Kaihla Janais, has dedicated himself and his personal and professional life to helping others understand the wisdom that is revealed in his favorite scripture, Matthew 25:14-30. Powell has a deep and abiding belief that we are all accountable to use our talents wisely and hopes to help others live their lives and maintain their businesses with passionate purpose.

Marvin Powell’s Specialties:

I help busy growth minded business owners, consultants, and non-profit leaders avoid the all too often devastating financial hardships caused by our rapidly changing and complex business environment. By quickly deploying the best financial strategies and systems, together I help my clients create wealth, stability and intergenerational prosperity.
