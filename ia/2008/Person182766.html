Stuart Weinstein’s Summary

For the past 17+ years I have sold competitive and market intelligence. I began with 2 of the largest and most respected online publishers: Lexis-Nexis and Thomson. Later I was recruited to manage the financial services group for Datamonitor, which provided its own research and consulting to clients. In 2001 I was the Director of Sales for Round1 where I gained a very intense experience with developing and selling enterprise software to venture capitalists. Recently, I sold marketing data, software and analysis for Claritas | Nielsen Media Research, Division of VNU, which is a leader in marketing analysis of trade areas and customer data. Currently I am handling New Business Development of direct content sales, ad sales and partnership sales for FinancialContent, a publicly traded internet company that increases both traffic and revenue for our client web sites.

I received my BA from Tulane University and my MA from Sacred Heart University.
