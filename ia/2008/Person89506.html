Jeri Sessler’s Summary

I am an experienced senior consultant with over 25+years experience in the areas of business strategy, merger due diligence/ integration, supply chain, strategic sourcing/offshoring, and operations performance improvement.

I am recognized as a creative thinker who brings best practices and lessons learned from multiple industries to my clients creating highly effective and unique solutions to their challenges. My passion is producing results for my clients and those results have totalled over $1.7 billion in net bottom line improvements for clients in the last 7 years.

I enjoy intellectually challenging, collaborative environments and have the intensity and focus required to succeed. My consulting experience coupled with start up company leadership and interim senior management roles gives me a unique ability to create implementable strategies and tangible results for organizations of any size.

I produce results that can be seen, felt and touched for my clients and can do the same for your company. I am especially good at developing strategic alliances/ partnerships, providing thought leadership, program management, facilitating process improvement and value stream analysis, developing offshoring/outsourcing strategies, de-mystifying convoluted contracts/renegotiating contracts, assessing complex situations quickly and thoroughly, understanding multi-focal problems and bringing multiple resources together to resolve them.

Jeri Sessler’s Specialties:

Business and marketing strategy, procurement strategy/strategic sourcing, mergers/acquisitions due diligence and integration, competitive analysis, contract negotiation, operational cost reduction strategy/implementation, supply chain strategy/implementation, business turnarounds, and organization strategy.

My role is to work with companies to ensure their products/services meet the environmental standards established by The Green Registry as well as be the primary liaison to The Green Registry Advisory Board, a gathering of credentialed University Professors each with an eco-specialty or discipline.
