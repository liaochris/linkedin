Anthony Cauterucci (amc@talenthunter.net)’s Summary

I am an international marketing and business development professional with over 20 years of experience bringing people and deals together. I currently manage a global executive search firm with a sustained track record of sourcing and securing best in class talent for over 25 years.

My area of concentration revolves around senior level, high impact professionals in marketing, marketing research, consumer insight, advertising and media, product development and management, interactive technology and innovation. I work primarily with CEO, CFO, CMO, EVP, SVP, Board level to Director level executives.

Ours is a very simple yet effective methodology that places the greatest value on networking, talking with industry experts, and seeking the counsel of recognized and respected senior industry leadership.

I look forward to working closely with you in addressing your executive search needs.

I can be reached at amc@talenthunter.net or at 1 800 291 0642 ext 212
