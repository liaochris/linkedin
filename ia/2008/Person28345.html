Bruce Bley (envirosolutions[at]bellsouth.net)’s Summary

Bruce Bley is the founder of BCB Associates. He has a B.S. Mechanical Engineering from Cornell University and an MBA in marketing from University of Hartford. He has more than 27 years experience in the power generation and industrial environmental controls industry and 5 years experience with residential and commercial IAQ and energy issues. He has contributed numerous articles pertaining to family health and indoor environment to various media outlets and was a contributing editor for the Combustion textbook.

Mr. Bley has extensive hands-on experience in large project management and due diligence with over $1.3 BB IPP/NUG turnkey projects completed. In that capacity he has interfaced with partners, lenders, lawyers, vendors, engineers and government representatives.

His company, BCB Associates- Environmental Solutions LLC is an independent distributor of EcoQuest International residential environmental technologies, activTek Environmental commercial air purification and water treatment systems and EcoTrustEnergy electric energy management systems. It markets these products throughout North America with a team of independent business owners. We are expanding to accomodate the exploding GO GREEN Movement with our environmental products and are looking for Partners/Investors throughout the U.S. and Canada and individuals with international business connections to join us.
