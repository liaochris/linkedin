Bex White’s Summary

Graphic Designer with over 4 years experience currently working for digitial agency Pancentric in London. My experience is predominantly in the digital arena focussing on websites, microsites, email design, banners and other digital media but I also have some experience in branding, print design and illustration.

As well as my design skills I greatly enjoy speaking at seminars and conferences, my experience here has been mainly around email design and best practice; preparing, presenting and holding post talk Q&A sessions. I also have experience preparing and holding training on design and coding to groups of both colleagues and clients as well as writing best practice and procedural guideline documentation.

Bex White’s Specialties:

Design: Web design and front end coding. Email design and email complient html. Basic Flash animation. Layout design for print. Illustration.

Software: Office, Adobe Creative Suite 3 (Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Indesign, Fireworks, Flash (Without advanced AS).

Personal: Teamplayer, Pro-active, Confident speaker, Knowledge of email industry.
