Gene Dvoretsky’s Summary

Technical Recruiter providing full life cycle recruitment and career services for all levels of individuals and corporations. Primary focus on contract, contract to hire and permanent placements, focused on the Chicago Area and national searches.

I specialize in finding people the right job, which means finding out about your needs and wants and not just pushing paper around town and around the country. I spend time with both clients and candidates to understand the needs of both and to find the best fit for the candidate. I will commit time and energy to get you the right candidate/job. With this in-mind, I expect commitments and responsiveness from both clients and candidates on moving the process forward. If you are interested in speaking with me in regards to finding people or the right job for you, contact me at genedvo@gmail.com.

3) your contact information, including phone numbers that work best for you

I will make every effort in contacting you within 24 hours.
