Joel Schalit’s Summary

Joel Schalit is a writer and editor based in San Francisco. An Israeli-American pundit noted for his unique views on Middle Eastern politics and US culture, over the past fourteen years, Schalit has produced four books and contributed to numerous periodicals including Alternet, France 24, the San Francisco Bay Guardian and XLR8R.

The former managing editor of Berkeley's Tikkun magazine and associate editor of Chicago's Punk Planet, Schalit served two terms as the co-director of the world's longest running online publication, Bad Subjects. He is currently serving as the editor of Allvoices.com, working as an editorial consultant at VIZ Media and AK Press, and is the media editor of Zeek.

Schalit's fifth publication, Israel vs. Utopia, is forthcoming from Akashic Books.
