Peter Wright’s Summary

Acclaimed author of more than a dozen introductory books on computer programming, and the author of more than 300 articles in the print and online press.

Peter has been working in the software development arena since 1988, working exclusively with Microsoft technologies from 1992 to 2006. He recently made the switch to work exclusively with Macintosh and leading edge scripting languages such as Ruby and Python.

Peter is a regular speaker at user group and industry events.

Pete is currently Vice President of Software Development for PayPerPost, the Consumer Generated Advertising Network that's turning the Internet adveritsing model on it's head.

Peter Wright’s Specialties:

Software development, agile development, development team best practices, software architecture and design, failed project recovery.
