Bryan Tackett’s Summary

I was recently featured in an NPR interview concerning the 2008 Financial Crisis and the General Election. (http://marketplace.publicradio.org/display/web/2008/10/20/interested_parties_wolford/)

Laid off 9/25/08: I served as a Project Manager in Integro's Private Equity Practice, overseeing risk management & insurance due diligence initiatives for various alternative investment firms. Services provided included risk profile analysis and performing initial all-lines insurance and risk management due diligence studies, creating coverage specifications for newly formed or changed entities (leveraged buy-outs, mergers/acquisitions, seed/early stage investments, etc.). I also helped manage and formulate insurance marketing strategies, particularly for deal-threatening issues.

I have also been involved in asset and stock exit strategies and sell-offs and related insurance issues.

For a short period, I managed the insurance investment research practice at the largest investment research firm. In this role, I interacted with and recruited C-suite & executive-level insurance & risk management professionals to consult with buy-side investors.

While a broker at Aon and Willis, I served as an advisor on large clients' insurance and risk management programs, enhancing product knowledge & expertise across all lines of coverage.

Many of my former client contacts have included C-suite executives, Presidents, Senior VPs, Corporate Insurance Risk Managers, Treasurers, Heads of Research, and Risk Analysts.

Client firms I have served include American Capital Strategies; Clayton, Dubilier, and Rice; KKR, Crane Co., GW Premier America, Ingersoll-Rand, Capmark Financial Group, PRIMEDIA, USF Holding Corp., Elizabeth Arden/Red Door Spas, MetLife, BellSouth, The State of Georgia, SunTrust Bank, United Parcel Service (UPS), Coca-Cola, AutoNation, Kubota Tractor, Publix, Gulfstream, Gables Realty, General Dynamics, and CSX.
