Kris De Strijker’s Summary

Being entrepreneur, you definitely select your partners with care.
But do you take the same cautions when it concerns your personal savings?

Innovation is a way of living. Fuchs & Associés Finance, with headquarter in Luxemburg, the second world market for collective management, offers access to unlimited capacities and opportunities concerning asset management.

Our team of international experts is ready to apply our investment strategies to your personal capital, beyond the different management methods (stock picking, index management, ...).

Through a lithe management and an exceptionally flexible assignment of assets, our managers have the opportunity to withdraw (in part or completely) from certain financial markets, depending on the economic perspectives of those markets. Eventually, this fine art of asset allocation guarantees an optimal balance between performance and safety.

Fuchs & Associés Finance cooperates with a world player on the stock market, to assure you:
• that the money is kept on your personal account;
• a swift access and exit settlement without costs;
• the ability to request the state of affairs on a daily basis;
• low-cost transactions.

Our managers also invest their own means: what takes place on your account also happens to their capital. No commissions are charged on each transaction, Fuchs & Associés Finance only requests a compensation, equivalent to the performance of your portfolio.

Fuchs & Associés Finance is convinced that our managers represent additional profit to your personal capital. Through our company, you acquire access to a service, previously only accessible to large capitals.
