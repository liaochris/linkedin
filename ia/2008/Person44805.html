Darian Thomas’s Summary

I have a track record of using creative skills to influence customers and prospects. The past 5 years, I have been working as a Business Development Manager for two Canadian-based software start-ups, www.timespring.com (TimeSpring Software) and www.stonefly.com (StoneFly Networks).

While at TimeSpring I have been responsible for 60-80 outbound calls daily, prospecting and lead generation, including securing C-level appointments with software and technology decision makers, driving initial product presentations of company solutions via conference calls, on-line web presentations and face to face meetings.

In addition, I have a full range of marketing, business development, strategic alliances and sales over a 17 year period.
