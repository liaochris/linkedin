Sudha Jamthe’s Summary

High Energy, Passionate, Internet Marketing Executive with 14+ yrs

My real passion lies in consumer internet branded products focusing on marketing strategy, brand building and business operations.

My most recent success is at Dash in infusing contagious passion for a tech consumer brand with integrated marketing programs that delivered on brand positioning with local market regional campaigns.

Early on I started my career in product management and partner management roles where I led strategic planning across divisions of large companies and built their Internet products/groups. Then I found my strength in product marketing and outbound communications.I treat every division as a customer and have the same approach to evangelize and infuse enthusiasm for brands and team-buyin across the organization.
