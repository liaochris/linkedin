Brian Sanders’s Summary

Through all phases of my technical career I have successfully managed projects. Specifically, as a program manager, my responsibilities included resource management, schedule creation, risk assessment, scope of work creation, and vendor management. I had complete authority and responsibility over resources that spanned various groups, including development, quality assurance, technical writers, and operations. I successfully used project management skills including schedule tracking, oral and written reports on project health, risk assessment and resource coordination to consistently deliver and deploy high quality projects on time and within budget.
I have worked from the vendor perspective most of my career and recently I switched over to the client side to get a better perspective of understanding their needs. My desire and passion is with providing a solution as a vendor and the exposure to the client’s side I feel gives me a much greater advantage in producing a perfect solution.
