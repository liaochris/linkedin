Mark Tomizawa’s Summary

Here's where my career and life has brought me: To the creation of a new kind of organization that makes it possible for busy people to do powerful good, quickly. It's called One Good Egg. We help people find the missing pieces for their solutions––often without money. We also help assemble small, scalable pilots, using resources from various powerful silos. We called this Synchronized Outreach. It's mutual enlightened self-interest, with a twist.

Mark Tomizawa’s Specialties:

I work as a connector, networker and catalyst with leadership in media, technology, education, public health, government agencies, nonprofits, and universities. As a Chicago teen, I took classes at CBS and The Second City and led a citywide PR campaign that kept our innovative high school open. It changed the arc of my life. Now I'm creating opportunities and tools for today's young people, particularly in areas that involve media and technology.
