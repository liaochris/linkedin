Lisa Rothstein’s Summary

In my first life, I was a creative executive and copywriter at large multinational advertising agencies in New York and Paris. Now I've turned my hand to screenwriting.

My first script, Brit or Miss, was the winner of the Comedy Category at Screenwriting Expo 2, a Top 12 Finalist at the Slamdance Screenwriting Competition, and semifinalist in the Austin Film Festival and Chesterfield Writers' Project competitions.

I am represented by Caren Bohrman of The Bohrman Agency in Beverly Hills, and by manager Andrew Kersey. I've written five screenplays so far, all comedies or romantic comedies, and am at work on a sixth.

I still apply my advertising experience, both in pitching my screenplays and working as a freelance copywriter and creative consultant for clients and ad agencies in the US, the UK and France. Check out my website at: www.yourwriterforhire.com where you'll find samples of both my advertising and screenwriting work.
