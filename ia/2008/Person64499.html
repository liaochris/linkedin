Robert D. Ashby, CMPS’s Summary

My financial planning background has led me to focus on mortgage planning since 2003, opening Solid Rock Mortgage Corporation business in 2004. Using the same desire to learn and plan as I do as an airline pilot, I strive to constantly advance my knowledge and quickly saw the need for certification as a mortgage planner.

Once offered, I became the first Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist in the state of Florida and the 29th in the nation. With this designation I can clearly show my level of expertise in the proper integration of a mortgage into the overall financial and investment plans.

My goals are to grow Solid Rock Mortgage Corporation to the "name" to see for mortgages in South Florida, and I know I will accomplish this as the word gets out.

Robert D. Ashby, CMPS’s Specialties:

I specialize in the proper integration of your mortgage into your overall financial plan, using a mortgage as a financial tool to propel you to true financial freedom faster than you thought possible. Through my unique program, you will be ensured that your mortgage will be the best solution to meet your needs while increasing liquidity, safety and rate of return.
