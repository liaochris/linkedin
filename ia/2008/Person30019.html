Cameron Clement’s Summary

Cameron Clement

“As a Brand Communications Director I have the opportunity to work directly with clients and their staff in imaginative and creative ways. As we develop strategic solutions for their needs, we are always making brand touch-point decisions at micro and macro levels. These decisions must be based on principles that accurately lead the brand to its ultimate definition and perception to those who encounter it on any level.

This is an incredibly exciting process, and is always amazingly fun and remarkably effective. We have been rewarded with watching these brands lead long and healthy lives.”

With vast award-winning experience during his 25-years in the branding industry, Cameron Clement has learned how to provide effective and strategic brand-based solutions for winning in the market place. Clement has honed his skills as a brand developer by applying field-proven principles in holistic brand experience.

Cameron spent a majority of his years as a brand developer and creative director in large advertising agencies working on many national accounts. This extensive experience helped define his ability to provide brand-centric solutions. He now has his own brand design studio where he utilizes his experience of serving some of the world’s most popular brands.

Cameron Clement has created some of his own brand concepts such as Silver Joe’s Coffee®, Ricketty Skateboards®, Engine Joe’s® Motorcycle Wear and Conceptonauts® apparel and is also currently the owner of C3 Brandworks (Cameron Clement Creative Brandworks, Inc.) a successful brand communications studio in Arkansas.
