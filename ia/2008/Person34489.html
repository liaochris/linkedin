Chick Ludwig’s Experience

  • Sports Writer (NFL/Bengals beat)

    Dayton Daily News

    (Privately Held; 1001-5000 employees; Newspapers industry)

    June 1979 — Present (29 years 8 months)

    I currently cover all aspects of the Cincinnati Bengals (news, features, game stories and columns) and author the "Ludwig At Large" blog at DaytonDailyNews.com Web site. I previously was the beat reporter for high school sports (1979-87) and Wright State University athletics (1987-97) before taking over the NFL/Bengals beat in 1997. I have covered a wide range of sports, including 8 Super Bowls (2000-07), 16 Indianapolis 500 races (1980-95), the NCAA Division I men's basketball "Final Four" (1991 and 1992), the Centennial Olympic Games in Atlanta (1996), the LPGA Tour Star Bank Classic (1996-99) and the NCAA Division I Women's Basketball Regional (2007).
