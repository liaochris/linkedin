James Castronuovo’s Summary

James graduated from Boston University’s School of Communications with a BA in Film/TV. James has experience with some of the nations most notable brands, helping them to deliver information to the public and allowing them to be positioned in the forefront of their market. Clients that James has worked with include; Conde Nasté Publications, Baby Phat, Sean John, Cole Haan, Pepsi, The Rolling Stones, Sony BMG, Island Def Jam, Victoria’s Secret, J Records, Bally’s Fitness Center, Virgin Records, AOL and Keith Richards.
With 10 years of experience in the industry, James is positioned to take your project to its highest level of perfection.

James Castronuovo’s Specialties:

-Avid and Final Cut Pro Non Linear Editing software
-Adobe After Effects, Photoshop and light Illustrator.
-DVD Studio Pro DVD Authoring software.
-Knowledge of Broadcast Standards
-Color Correction in Final Cut Pro
-Knowledge of video compression and all types of video decks and media.
