Daniel Goepp’s Summary

Daniel has over 15 years of systems architecture, design, development and integration experience. He has been a technical executive for the last 10 years in the telecommunications sector.

Daniel is currently the VP, Network Operations at Vidtel, the world's first video telephone company. Previously, Daniel was the CTO of BroadVoice, a nationwide Internet Telephone Service Provider offering residential and small-to-medium business services. Daniel is one of the few in the world who has run a consumer VoIP and video VoIP network (2004-2007). For more than a decade, Daniel has been developing and overseeing IT and networking functions for a software development company (2003-2004), a competitive local exchange carrier (CLEC) (2001-2003), two nationwide telecommunications providers (1999-2001), and an Internet Service Provider (1998).

Daniel Goepp’s Specialties:

IT architecture, design, development and integration. System and network administration, development and design. Software development and database design, architecture and administrations. Internet technologies and protocols. VoIP and legacy telecommunications technology.
