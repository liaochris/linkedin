Michael Tanner’s Summary

As CEO at Bamboo Solutions Corporation, Michael is focused on driving revenue and profit growth in the collaborative software market using Microsoft-based techologies such as SharePoint, defined by Microsoft as being "the fastest growing product in Microsoft's history."

Prior to BSC, in 2004 Michael founded of Adexta, Inc.a Silicon Valley area consulting company that provides advisory services to growth-stage technology companies. Prior to Adexta Michael served as co-founder and Managing Director at The Chasm Group since 1995 where he provided advisory and consulting services throughout the technology industry.

Prior to his consulting career he spent 16 years in the software industry in executive marketing, sales and general management roles for companies such as Autodesk, Sclumberger/Applicon and several startup organiations.

A regular speaker, Michael has presented at both industry and corporate-sponsored events on two continents. He is a regular speaker at national and regional technology conferences, has guest-lectured at UC Berkeley and Indiana University in areas of strategy and marketing strategy at the MBA level.

Michael was a director at Savi Technology, Inc., Apexion, Inc. and Webproject Inc. He received his BSME from Ohio State University and MBA from Rivier College. He resides in Pleasanton, CA.
