    Virtual Button Technologies (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Consumer Electronics industry)

    November 2007 — Present (6 months)

    Virtual Button Technology (VBT) combines several well developed mechanical engineering and computer science principles with emerging consumer electronics technologies. Through the use of micro-sensors and vibrational mechanics, the patent-pending VBT provides value added functionality to electronic devices.

    This innovative fusion of existing engineering theory enables invisible buttons to be implemented anywhere on an electronic device. As a new form of user input, virtual buttons are well positioned to augment and/or replace mechanical buttons, touch screens, while opening new worlds of user interface opportunity. These invisible buttons are functionally equivalent to an electro-mechanical switch; however, they are entirely software generated. As a result, VBT offers:
    - Sleeker industrial design
    -Personalized ergonomics and flexible user interface design
    Hermetically sealed electronics enclosures
