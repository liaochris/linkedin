Doug Mandell’s Summary

Doug Mandell is the founder and managing partner of the Mandell Law Group, PC (MLG) (www.mlawgroup.com). The Mandell Law Group is a boutique law firm specializing in complex technology and telecommunications transactions, as well as all aspects of employment law counseling and litigation. Doug founded MLG in 2001. Clients of MLG include Fortune 100 companies such as Seagate Technology LLC; venture backed emerging growth companies such as Orb Networks and LinkedIn, as well as entrepreneurs and executives.

Doug specializes in negotiating complex international technology transactions and representing clients in employment law. In the last two years, Doug has been involved in negotiating international contracts totaling over $2 billion dollars. Doug also regularly serves as a virtual general counsel to venture-backed technology companies.

Doug also regularly advises companies and executives in all aspects of employment law counseling and litigation. Doug is recognized as one of the leading attorneys in San Francisco specializing in representing senior executives (including CEOs, CFOs, founders, entrepreneurs and managing directors) in complex negotiations and disputes.

Doug was recently honored as a 2007 Northern California Super Lawyer (www.superlawyers.com). Super Lawyers identifies the top 5 percent of attorneys in Northern California, as chosen by their peers and through the independent research of Law & Politics. Doug also received this honor in 2006 and is one of the youngest Super Lawyers in Northern California.

Doug is also an avid entrepreneur, having started his first business when he was 17. Doug is a member of the Entrepreneurs Organization (EO) (www.eonetwork.org) and is currently on the board of the San Francisco chapter. Doug has attended international conferences for EO in Tokyo, Marrakesh, Montreal and Chicago.

Doug Mandell’s Specialties:

Employment litigation, employment law counseling, technology transactions (including software license agreements and international supply agreements), providing General Counsel services to companies, sexual harassment, discrimination, executive compensation and counseling senior executives (chief executives, entrepreneurs and managing directors) in complex employment issues.
