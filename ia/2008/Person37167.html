Chris Unwin’s Summary

At The Much MTV Group, I manage a list of AAA advertising, music, and promotional partnerships across eight channels and three websites. The Brand Partnerships mandate is to create co-branded media programs with our advertisers that simultaneously drive revenue, maximize audience, while ensuring that the MTV and Much brands are protected.

My roster of clients revolve around the music, event, content development space. As such, some of my clients include Rogers Wireless, Virgin Mobile, Universal, Sony BMG, Warner, EMI, Live Nation, and West 49.

In my previous role at Dose, I was directly involved in the launch of the multi-platform youth media brand. Working in tandem with another manager, I was responsible for all online and offline marketing, promotions, record label partnerships, creative, and department strategy.

My appreciation and understanding of the music industry is grounded in my absolute love of all things music, as well as my experiences on the road as tour manager for Bedouin Soundclash.

Chris Unwin’s Specialties:

Strategy and implementation of AAA marketing initiatives, brand partnerships, and business development, particularly in the realm of music, events, and emerging media. My experience in working with a range of media properties lends itself to a holistic marketing approach across platforms - broadcast, digital, mobile, print, radio, out of home, in-store, grassroots, live events.
