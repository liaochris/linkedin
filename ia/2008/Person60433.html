Erika Stanczak’s Summary

As a former IBM Communications Manager, I have successfully created and executed powerful marketing communications, business development and public relations strategies, concerning small to large privately held and publicly traded companies. Having consistently ignited market share for a variety of types of businesses, my background includes expanding brand awareness, defining brand positions and developing performance measurements.

Under my tenure with Kidz-Med Inc., we achieved record sales for Q1 2008 and a 63% increase in online sales from the start of 2008. In addition, viral marketing and PR campaign's led to massive exposure on ABC7 San Francisco as well as other television, print and online outlets.

Marketing Communications expertise includes:
• Create and implement online marketing strategies to include driving traffic while maximizing conversions and boosting ROI through new media, social media, viral and WOM marketing and search engine and social media optimization
• Develop and execute marketing communication strategies to increase awareness of and effectively promote company
• Design website, or blog, collateral materials and television commercials
• Plan and manage all public relations activities
• Develop and maintain strategic partnerships
• Build the value of the brand proposition and expand brand awareness through marketing and public relations
• Design communication standards and processes
• Identify key performance indicators to develop measurement and analysis


My work has been published in various prestigious print publications, and I have produced educational documentaries for major nonprofit entities. I hold a Bachelors degree in Communications and English from Pace University, and continue to self educate on current practices.
