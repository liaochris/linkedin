Nancy Fulton’s Summary

I graduated from UCLA with a degree in Economics, started teaching and providing pre-sales support to customers in the 1980s, and have been writing professionally since 1991.

Writing became Producing in the late 90's and has recently become Film Production (credits include: Back from Iraq: The US Soldier Speaks, Jimmy Massey: At War, . . . I am currently in production on No Better Friend: A Military Ghost Story).

I know embarassing amounts of software, ranging from 3dsmax, Maya, AutoCAD, Autodesk VIZ, Inventor, Final Cut, some 3D motion capture software, EP Scheduling, EP Budgeting, Final Draft, Camtesia, Celtx, mSQL, etc. I also have some background in NLP, hypnosis and politics.

I enjoy working to communicate complex ideas and solving problems. I like to help well-intentioned people achieve their personal and professional objectives. I really enjoy supporting good causes. I do believe, the world is what we make it . . .
