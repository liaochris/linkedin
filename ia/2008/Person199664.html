Richard A. Whipple’s Summary

TRUTH IN ADVERTISING SINCE 1989
I use social science to systematically manipulate the way people behave leading to peak employee productivity; greater management sensitivity to the public voice; higher sustainable sales volume; and/or improved efficacy in the process of producing profit for clients.

Because the business of public relations is a social science that uses a systematic approach, it can be measured and managed against an R.O.I. calculation. I am neither an artiste nor a magician; I am a professional business counselor whose commerce in trade is rooted in matters of perception and image management. My expertise is applicable to a potential client in any industry, for-profit or non-profit, and available to any client whose product(s) by design pose no direct threat to the health or well being of society; to the ecological balance of the environment; or to the micro-macro economic equilibrium of fair trade practices.

I represent sustainable business and I practice in a professional, business-like fashion.

Most of my experience in Canada came before The Internet and eMail were connecting people. My chief job was and remains to be: to develop innovative connections between my client and the market; inter-relate them through issue responsive publics, and/or a secondary market; and manage these message channels to increase shareholder value.

In the early 1990's, I was privileged to be mentored by three retired executives from Executives Available™ and to become acquainted with Edward Bernays just before his death. These men worked alongside me as I learned the business models and applied the understanding given to me by Dr. Bernays.

I make extensive use of the symmetric or "4th model" of communications by applying it to FMCG and durables - increasing the usefulness of two-way communications to satisfy the vested interests of corporations while moving the model outside the traditional ghetto of associations and non-profit organisations.
