Carl Gosselin’s Summary

I completed a master’s degree in Organizational Psychology at Columbia University. My education and previous consulting experience enables me to conduct research analysis, identify current trends and provide practical resolutions to client-specific issues.

I have extensive work experience with change management during the adoption of enterprise-wide IT systems. As part of the Organization and Human Performance group at Accenture, I created and executed strategic initiatives for company-wide changes. My consulting experience has also enabled me to work in many different organizational cultures, such as American Express, Siemens, Cinergy, Telus and the Government of Canada.

My passion for organization development led to an internship with Mercury, a software vendor for whom I used to work. I provided them with organization change strategies that complement their existing customer-focused IT implementation approach. In addition, I developed, from scratch, a training program on organization change for their global consulting workforce.

I hope to join a company whose core purpose is to help leading corporations and governments be more successful.
