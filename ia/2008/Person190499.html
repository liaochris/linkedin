Toke Nygaard’s Summary

CUBAN COUNCIL is a small interactive agency which specializes in creating simply superb solutions for web & print & such.

http://www.cubancouncil.com/

Our areas of expertise range from UI, application and graphic design to iconography, multimedia authoring & complex back-end development. The main Cuban Council office is located in slightly sunny San Francisco, but we provide services for clients all over the world (we also have a rather minuscule office in the fair city of Copenhagen — just in case you were interested).

As a group we take great pride in developing solutions that are fun, usable, technically challenging, visually exciting and / or quirky, so if you or your company are looking for assistance with projects of that nature, please use the contact information below to get in touch with us.

The core CUBAN COUNCIL team is currently composed of four individuals — toke nygaard, michael schmidt, michael buzzard & per jørgensen.

We are 3 parts Danish, one part born & bred American, 50% bald and together we’ve worked on everything from huge CMS-controlled digital platforms to minimal independent websites.

We’ve all had “normal” jobs in the web industry, but have over the years gotten tired of being stuck in the inner machinery of large bureaucracies, and decided to start our own lil’ company in the summer of 2001.

Toke Nygaard, Michael Schmidt and Per Jørgen Jørgensen also masterminded the world's largest online web design community KALIBER10000 (http://www.k10k.net/).
