Lynn Failing’s Summary

Specialists in placing outstanding supply chain, distribution, logistics and transportation professionals with corporate clients worldwide. Our goal is partnership. Our process is based on building long term relationships with exceptional leaders and with companies who embrace change as well as valuing their most important asset: people.

Lynn Failing’s Specialties:

Successful executive search is a tripartite partnership: innovative companies, successful individuals and intelligent search professionals. Our expertise is identifying outstanding middle and senior management personnel for our clients with specific needs in supply chain, logistics, distribution and transportation. In order to provide a "full service" for our clients, we perform both retained and contingency searches. Our commitment is to excellence, integrity, thoroughness and confidentiality.
