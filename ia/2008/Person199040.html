Marina Krutchinsky’s Summary

I am a results-driven UI/UX strategist with extensive hands-on experience in all aspects of web site/application development. Trained engineer who found true passion in architecting, designing and coding user-centered interfaces for online applications.

17 years of combined engineering and multi-disciplinary design experience covering industrial, technical, product, software, interactive and online media fields.

200+ site launches since 1997.

Exceptionally skilled in leading fast-paced online projects through their full development cycle while meeting budget/time constraints and keeping team spirits up.

Marina Krutchinsky’s Specialties:

user interface design for web-based applications, information architecture, information design, user experience engineering, usability, web standards, web 2.0, web 3.0, wikis, user-centered design, XHTML, CSS, AJAX, Flash, front-end design, project management, cross-functional team management.
