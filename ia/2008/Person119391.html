Marc Dowd’s Summary

I am an experienced IT executive with an unusually deep appreciation of technology and corporate strategy. Able to understand and discuss cogently the salient aspects of complicated technology at different levels; from board level to deep geek. An all-rounder who has in past sales roles closed million pound deals, designed real time telephony systems and coded commercial software.

Currently working as a trusted personal advisor to CIO's of $1 billion+ enterprises I use my commercial and technical experience to validate or question the strategy and tactics of the people I work with as well as bring them innovative ideas to help them make the best decisions.

I have always been focused on people and have acted as a coach in English, French and Spanish over the last 25 years. I enjoy problem solving, creativity and success. I believe that being passionate about what you do is fundamental to an interesting and fulfilling life.

Marc Dowd’s Specialties:

Understanding and use of technology, negotiation, presentation at all levels, finding innovative solutions to business problems. Rapid analysis of issues. Tact and a facility with communication. Management and motivation. I cover the areas of business process re-engineering, change management, organisational development as well as strategy and hands on project management.
