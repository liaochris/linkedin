Mustapha Derras’s Summary

The software development environment changes and permanently adapts itself. We can see that constraints for the creation and the integration of “software solutions” are increasing with the emergence of elements like free software and/or the place that is given to the SAAS principles. The adaptation capacities of the customers and the software development companies if then defined by their ability to adapt themselves to this permanent movement. These capacities depend upon the organizational and technological agility that allows taking anticipative decisions concerning economical models and/or technological orientations.

A wide professional experience and a large range of knowledge, including education and personal investment, contribute to ensure the necessary movements inside this complex ecosystem. Among these initial considerations the complexity is naturally increasing and this involves the absolute necessity to be in a position allowing to master numerous domains like software, hardware, networks, operating systems, … This can be seen as an integral equation for which we must be capable to find solutions taking decisional contributions from human resource management, technological leadership, financial return on investment, strategical positioning, …

These are the critical inputs and knowledge that I can bring through my professional experience that leaded me to take numerous responsibilities for companies like General Electric, Cadence Design Systems or Dassault Systems, acting in the software and systems developments area. After 18 years of work in the “high tech” domains I’m currently the CTO of the Berger-Levrault group (member of the board).
