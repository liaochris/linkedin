Jamil Anwar’s Summary

Currently acting as a CEO of Kraysis. Responsibilities include directing the company in defining individual roles, defining objectives and a strategic vision, decision making, developing corporate connections and creating a focus of the company towards its goals. I have an undergraduate degree in computer science and a graduate degree in information systems management. has extensive research and development experience and had chance to work on leading roles like project coordinator and technical consultant for reputed organization named Responsible Business Initiative. I have worked as a pioneer researcher with Center for Research in Urdu Language Processing (www.crulp.org) for two years and also worked as a research associate at Lahore University of Management Sciences for one year. There are few international publications to my credit regarding his work in speech signal processing. I am also a certified information system auditor (CISA), a certification by Information Systems and Control Association (ISACA), USA
