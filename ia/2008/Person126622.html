Athena has departments throughout Denmark and in Northern Germany. Athena was established in 1995, and today it is the result of a merger of a number of enterprises. In May 2007, Athena was admitted to the growth exchange First North under the Copenhagen Stock Exchange OMX. This meant that the capital base of the company was strengthened by DKK 30 million.

Athena consists of the following divisions:

» Infrastructure & Outsourcing Division – supplying IT infrastructure, IT services, hosting, outsourcing and IT operation, as well as leasing complete IT systems under the name Athena LeaseIT.

» E-Solutions Division – which distributes, develops and implements conventional and web-based business systems, including also business systems from Microsoft and SAP and our own in-house systems Athena WTM Time/Sag and Athena CMS.

» Building Systems Division – supplying software, training course and consulting services to the construction industry, including programs from Autodesk, BlueCielo, as well as an extensive range of our own systems developed in-house.
