MILES NURSE’s Summary

As an interactive creative director, educator and advocate of the design process, I am always excited at the prospect of working with progressive institutions that appreciate the value of the creative process. With 12 yrs of team leadership, design, and instructional experience my passion lies with building and fostering teams, while refining a user centered design process to build compelling product experiences.

The design landscape is constantly evolving but the blueprint for success has been constant. Teams that yield the greatest results have first-class leaders, who motivate and bestow vision throughout the entire creative journey. With experience in all aspects of user-centered design my strengths lay in my ability to understand the “Big Picture.” As a “Director” I understand the importance of leadership and growing leaders in order to remain sustainable. I recognize how to attract and align talent, form strategic alliances, and exercise mature judgment in often turbulent waters.

As a “Creative” catalyst I love to collaborate, encourage wild ideas, and push the boundaries of creative expression. In order to achieve groundbreaking design you must combine the power of all creative mediums and explore multiple expressions of a great idea. The design process is a gestalt; it guides us on the user journey, helps us translate epiphanies into viable business solutions, and bridges the gab between “Art” & “Usability.” It allows us to create the “Xperience.” A delicate design balance that understands the goals of the user, meets the objectives of the client and realizes that too much Jakob Neilson without enough Joshua Davis is not necessarily a good thing. I thoroughly enjoy the challenge of leading projects from concept to fruition while having a steadfast commitment to loyalty, integrity and an ethical design process.

MILES NURSE’s Specialties:

creative direction, interactive design. innovation in product / experience design, motion design, underwater imaging, crafting product vision, brand & ID, team building, coaching, user experience consultation, interactive business development.
