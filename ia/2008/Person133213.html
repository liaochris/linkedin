Mine' Ogura’s Summary

I am a collaborative software leader with 15 years of experience in software test development, release / configuration management, managerial positions, process architecture, project management, and agile leadership in both startups and fortune 100 companies. In my roles, I have straddled the fine line between highly technical, development-centric quality assurance engineering and process management. I built teams with accelerated product release throughput, efficiency, and quality by creating automated pipelines for releases through configuration management, and infrastructures to continuously test applications. As an agile promoter at Shopzilla, I turned ineffective teams around and helped improve the efficacy of stronger teams.

• Changed the perception of test engineering in a company which was vehemently opposed to slowing development and releases, to one which demanded higher quality software
• Aggressively accelerated software development by implementing a configuration management team which built and implemented the Continuous Integration backbone for Shopzilla
• Replaced Mercury tools with open-source tools (Selenium & JMeter, integrated into ControlTier)
• Hired and coached highly technical Quality Assurance Engineering teams across multiple companies
• Subversively implemented Scrum through the test organizations
• Coach ScrumMasters and Scrum teams across the enterprise to improve their processes
• Lead a multi-team effort to release the next generation of Shopzilla.com as the ScrumMaster of the Scrum of Scrums
• Designed and implemented white-box and black-box testing architectures using Java, Unix shell scripts, Perl, SQL, QA Partner, SilkTest, SilkPerformer, OS/2 command shell scripts, and DOS batch files
• Managed budgets of $500K~$5 million
• Exceptional analytical and problem-solving aptitude
• Excellent written and spoken communication skills
• Skilled motivator and mentor
