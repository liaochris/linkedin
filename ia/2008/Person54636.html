Carl Colaizzi’s Summary

My name is Carl Colaizzi. I am a Senior Executive Recruiter with 9 years of solid, successful recruiting experience. This experience has allowed me to find the right match, at the right time. As a Senior Executive Recruiter, timing is everything. Candidates can have great skills, the opportunity could be perfect for you, but if the timing isn't right...it just doesn't work!

With me, I specialize in placing Solutions professionals across the U.S
Professionals such as Business Development and Account Executives in Technology Solutions (ERP, CRM, Offshore Development), Federal Business Development professionals (with Security Clearances), to Branch and Area Managers in Staff Augmentation (Staffing).
My clients make up the industry leaders, regional leaders, and up & coming starz of the Staffing/Solutions industry. They depend on me to identify, evaluate, and recruit outstanding performers in Sales, Recruiting, and Management. In other words…they depend on ME to find YOU! :-)

If the timing is right for you to explore a new opportunity that could be stronger than the one you're in currently...Call ME! The Staff aug/Solutions industry is BOOMING on many fronts…BE A PART OF IT!
