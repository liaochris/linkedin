Michael Resnick’s Summary

Former consulting Director at Capgemini, Oracle and Siebel running Siebel, Oracle EBS, Technology Architecture, and Integration practices. CTO for Oracle Applications for BearingPoint and VP of Professional Services for a Sequoia funded software start-up. Extensive technology management experience on large system integration projects. Over 20 years of IT consulting expertise.

Contributing Author of, "Tips From the Trenches - The Collective Wisdom of Over 100 Professional Services Leaders" and a Co-Inventor on a patent application entitled, "Methods and Systems to Effect Comprehensive Customer Management Solutions".

Past clients include VMware, DirecTV, T-Mobile, HCSC, Sentry Insurance, Whirlpool, Barclay’s Global Investors, and Intuit. I received my bachelor's degree from Simon Fraser University with minors in computer science and psychology. I am a member of Mensa and a volunteer for the American Red Cross Bay Area.
