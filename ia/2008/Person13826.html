Anne Wood’s Summary

Current position directs and supervises all operations the of Europe and Pacific programs at Mountain Travel Sobek, including but not limited to the following: Conceptualize, design and build out innovative, uniquely appealing world-class adventure itineraries; craft marketing strategy for the Europe and Pacific regions; develop competitive positioning and rationale for each trip; provide direction to marketing department on regional and on trip attributes; create custom trips for clients on an as needed basis; ensure flawless logistics and successful departures; build relationships with outfitters and guides; trouble shoot and solve problems in the field; assure quality; management of regional finance—forecasting, budgeting, reporting, yield management, negotiate contracts, invoicing; prepare and maintain accurate and current marketing and customer materials.

Anne Wood’s Specialties:

Extensive international travel; expertise in active travel field management, high-level budget tracking and analysis, and the management of fundraising and development strategies; professional special events planning experience; expertise in all human resource procedures, administrative management and supervision, and the use of PC & Mac computers; trained in the use of an ice axe and self-arrest; many races completed, including marathons; modeling experience for a women's running gear catalog.
