Carlos 'Charly' Maltagliatti’s Summary

Carlos, A.K.A. Charly, is originally from Argentina.
Graduated from the “Universidad Argentina de Empresa” as a graphic designer, prior to joining Microsoft as OSG Brand Solutions Specialist for OLA subsidiary (Other Latam) he worked at Globant leading the design team for Electronics Arts Redwood Shores(SFO), working onsite in UI design for XBOX 360 & PlayStation games.

Before that, he worked in E-Volution Digital Marketing as Creative Director of the media department supporting sales in solution for brands such as P&G, Coke, Walt Disney, etc.

Also Carlos is part of the Image Campus Staff in the flash master career, University Professor and Co-Author of the “Flash Master” book.
