Kerry Dugan’s Summary

For the past nine (9) years I have been an independent Consultant across multiple industries to include the Computer, Computer Telephony Integration (CTI) and Telecom/ISP industries. The first day I entered the Computer Industry at IBM, I was told that I am what IBM refers to as the “Cutting Edge” which is the Top 5% of the Computer Industry.

Over the years, as my experience grew, I excelled again at another IBM site and became a Computer Telephony Integration (CTI) Architect which resolved some major issues and reduced the Total Cost of Ownership, while raised the Return of the Investment (TCO/ROI) in the CTI architecture.

More recently, I have worked in the Telecom industry on Fortune 5 accounts and grew one which had been stagnant for five (5) years at USD $85 Million to USD $150 Million within the first year. From there I topped another account with an additional USD $135 Million before leaving to start my own firm.

Most recently, I have started my own Virginia based business “Internetworking & Consulting Studios, LLC™”.

Some of my core technologies revolve around Disaster Recovery consulting which includes ensuring Resiliency, Reliability, Redundancy, High Availability, Diversity, and Security of data across my client’s internetworking; reverse engineering and engineering, as well as, architecture and design of customer solutions of their internetworking, hardware and software.

Some of my recent projects include:

their business from traditional brick and mortar to opening the sales channels of e-
commerce and online transactions.
