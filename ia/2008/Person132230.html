Michael Horn’s Summary

I have 9 years of design and verification experience design multi-protocol, multi-million gate, first pass success ASICs. My verification experience includes module simulation and chip simulation using Specman E, Vera and SystemVerilog. I have experience prototyping in the lab with Xilinx Virtex FPGAs. In addition to design and verification, I have performed static timing analysis, logical equivalence checking, synthesis and I have taken a two week training course in the use of Magma (physical design tool).
