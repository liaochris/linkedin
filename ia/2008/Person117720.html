Maddie Joan Blaustein’s Summary

As a triple-threat force in the comics and animation industries, Maddie Joan Blaustein has used her eclectic body of skills in design, illustration, photography, copy-writing, and animation for over 20 years. Most recently she has been the Creative director for Weekly World News where she created a breathtaking volume of dozens of photoshop illustrations every week, each one as realistic as if she’d personally gone out and photographed Bigfoot or Bat Boy. Her images are not only perfectly rendered, technically, they tell the essence of the story at a glance. In a crowded, cluttered marketplace they stand out. She can handle drama, humor, science fiction, ancient history, romance – no subject will ever throw her.In short, she delivers brilliance on-demand.

Maddie Joan Blaustein’s Specialties:

graphic design, tabletop photography, photo-composites, photo-retouch,cartoon illustration, script writing, and voiceovers
