Martha Young’s Summary

Seasoned marketing communications and advertising professional with more than 20 years of experience working in a wide range of industries, including insurance, higher education, print journalism and non-profits.

For nearly one-third of her professional career, Martha has enjoyed a rewarding career with FM Global, a leading property insurer of the world's largest businesses. For the past 175 years, FM Global has helped its clients maintain continuity in their business operations by drawing upon state-of-the-art loss-prevention engineering and research; risk management skills and support services; tailored risk transfer capabilities; and superior financial strength. Today, FM Global insures more than one-third of America's FORTUNE 1000 companies and similarly ranked multinational businesses across the globe.
