Julian Tol’s Summary

Julian Tol is founder and CEO of Digital Republic. Digital Republic is the majority shareholder in three businesses:

- Digital Republic // Optenet - A main supplier of internet content filters to the Commonwealth Government under the NetAlert filter program
- BrandScreen - Digital Media Exchange that automates media sales transactions, decreasing market friction, facilitating transactions between the increasing volume of media market participants
- CheckUp - Online broking system that facilitates the selection and purchase of preventative healthcare (screening and monitoring) services

Julian Tol’s Specialties:

Operation of 3 start-ups from ideation to day-to-day ops:

- Campaign Centre BV (advertising agency - trade sale to Publicis in 1999)
- FreeVision Laser Eye Clinics (laser eye clinic group - trade sale to Optical Express in 2004)
- Digital Republic // Optenet - PRESENT - Main supplier of internet content filters to the Australian Government under the NetAlert filter program
