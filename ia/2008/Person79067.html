Jeff Lange’s Summary

Jeff Lange is a very motivated website designer. He started his first website, at the age of 17, for a friend and has never stopped learning. After learning basic HTML he quickly grasped domain registration and web hosting. Applying his knowledge of website design he started creating websites for clients. He has worked with his business partner Chris "Broadway" Romero at G-Unit Records with many projects for high profile musicians such as 50 Cent, G-Unit, Chamillionaire, Consequence and others.

Jeff likes to deliver custom functionality to websites with PHP and MySQL. He has delivered many custom coding and back end programming to many websites. He always enjoys being challenged to big projects.
