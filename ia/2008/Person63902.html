Filippo Ronco’s Summary

Graduated from the Faculty of Law at the University of Genoa in 2003, I was an apprentice in a lawyers’ studio for about 2 years, despite never trying to take the qualifying state exam. I am now a sommelier, for what it’s worth, although I haven’t been a member of the Association for a few years now, since I stopped feeling at ease in it.

I have been involved with TigullioVino.it since 2000, turning full time since the beginning of 2005. I also look after other online projects like Vinix.it – this social network – and VinoClic, an online advertising agency for advertisers of the wine & food world, active since October 2006. I have personally organized all the editions of TigullioVino.it Meeting, a national event in which all the best Italian and foreign wine makers are introduced to the press, traders and public, tasted by the TigullioVino.it tasting panels. I barely have the time to finish one edition that I usually have to start working on the following one.

I have taken part, as speaker, to some conferences on online communication, with a particular emphasis on the publishing legal aspects in the wine & food sector, on online advertising and c.d. web 2.0 instruments.

Filippo Ronco’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    VinoClic (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    October 2006 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    VinoClic was born from the online experience of TigullioVino.it, one of the main Italian portals on wine and on gourmet tourism, running since April 2000. The experience matured on the web and in the field of online advertising, together with the desire to offer Wine and Food companies a targeted advertising system, have set the basis for the development of the VinoClic project, the first online gourmet advertising network.

    There is, in the current market, a strong need for exploiting all the potentiality Internet has to offer, and in order to do it successfully we think it is necessary to:

    - be aware of the targets to be met via the Internet;
    - be aware of the available instruments;
    - to understand where, how and how much to invest online

    These are the reasons why VinoClic has created an eWorkplace focused on the real needs of the Wine & Food sector, by creating a network made up exclusively of specialized websites and blogs.
