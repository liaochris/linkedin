Jennie Marlow’s Summary

Jennie Marlow is a former Silicon Valley executive and turn-around consultant. Today, she devotes her life to bringing through the teachings of her spirit guides. Her body of work includes books, articles, numerous recordings, classes and workshops on a wide variety of subjects. Her behavioral and spiritual models for the Post-New-Age are used by a growing number of therapists, coaches, and consultants in personal growth, healing, and business leadership.

Marlow and her guides offer a unique perspective on human creative power. Her book, Personal Magic, offers an in-depth description of the gifts, challenges, and creative potentials of each of eight human creative powers, which her guides call personal magic. The book offers frank advice and revealing real-world examples of famous people, and how their lives clearly reflect these principles.

In an extensive series of recorded classes and workshops, available through her website, Marlow's guides present an extraordinary new psychological model based on existential fear and conditioned interactions which they call polarities. These elements are used to describe human fear- and fantasy based destructive behavior, and provide a pathway for resolving our most stubborn, persistent issues. This model is also used in her readings and mentoring, and in her leadership coaching practice.

Marlow offers private readings, consultations, mentoring, workshops, and intensive training for executives, coaches, professionals, and healing practitioners.

Sustainability, relationships, conflict resolution, energetic healing, psychological and transactional models, technologies for transformation, higher consciousness, and conscious business.
