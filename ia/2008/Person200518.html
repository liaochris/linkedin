Winnie Weintre (info@pri-management.eu)’s Summary

As a Project Manager and a Manager supply chain management my high 'ability to execute' characterises my way of doing things. I execute my assignments to solve / improve a situation and to offer added value. Never just to do the assignment.
The projects and programmes I manage always contain an important organisational and human aspect and mostly an important ICT aspect. The real unique aspect is the change in the organisation that is needed. In some cases it was the change in the project organisation in order to make the project a success.
The environments I like most are logistics and manufacturing.
It is better to work in an environment that has to earn money in stead of spend budgets. For me that creates a more positive focus.
