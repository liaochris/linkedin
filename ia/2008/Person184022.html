Susan Storms’s Summary

"SUSAN STORMS BELONGS IN THE SALESPERSON HALL OF FAME"
by Seth Godin - Fast Company issue #30 "Who's Hot 2000"

A promotion, marketing and sales career that has spanned the industries of radio, television and the internet.

Startup and rollout for companies including Time Warner, ABC Radio, 24/7 Internet advertising rep firm, The Promotional Broadcast Network, The Fox Network, MyPoints and Yoyodyne, where permission marketing was coined.

Susan Storms’s Specialties:

Susan Storms specializes in launching and building companies through relationship marketing and by creative and strategic use of new media.

Susan Storms’s Experience

  • consultant

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2002 — 2006 (4 years)

    Clients included The Promotional Broadcast Network, Synergy 6, The Doula Association, & The Delve Group

  • Chief Revenue Officer

    MyPoints.com

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; MYPT; Internet industry)

    1999 — 2001 (2 years)

    Part of launch team that took the company public, began as VP/East Coast Sales. Region consistently set company best practice standards and sales records, resulting in promotions to CRO post overseeing 40+ sales staff, client management team, affiliate program and partnerships.
    MyPoints is generally accepted to be the internet's largest and most successful loyalty program; a groundbreaker in merging the loyalty business with database marketing. The business model enabled us to lead ad market rates, deliver superior performance for both users and advertisers, and ultimately become a subsidiary of United New Ventures.

  • VP/Sales

    24/7 Real Media

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; TFSM; Online Media industry)

    1997 — 1999 (2 years)

    As a founding member, recruited and trained an East Coast Sales force to represent >100 high profile affiliates, in five industry verticals, including Earthlink, cars.com, Comedy Central, and Match.com. Our team led the company in consistently posting the highest average CPM rates, integrating affiliate relations management, advertiser reporting systems, and sales cycle reporting into a single database to provide full transparency between satellite offices and superior CRM. Other regions were patterned after the East Coast model.

  • VP/Advertising

    Yoyodyne

    (Internet industry)

    1996 — 1997 (1 year)

    Later sold to Yahoo, Yoyodyne was a pioneer of internet marketing techniques and introduced “permission marketing” to the online advertising community. Designing customized online business growth strategies for advertisers enabled us to tap national advertising, promotional and e-commerce dollars. Working closely with founder (Seth Godin) marketing (Jerry Shereshewsky) and development (Mark Hurst) to experiment with various product models, we ultimately introduced vertical products targeted to online shopping, small businesses, and automotive categories. Personally closed Fortune 100 client and managed sale through program development.

  • General Sales Manager

    Time Warner Cable / NY1

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1990 — 1995 (5 years)

    Broke all sales records at Time Warner CitiCable, increasing list sales generation by 485% in one year. Promoted to spearhead Agency Sales I delivered an increased team revenue yield of 145%.

    Next promoted to GSM post at NY1, (Time Warner's high profile entry into local news programming in NYC,) to address lagging sales.

    Using market research and a custom-built relational database, revised avail and inventory structures, and re-training the sales team already in place, was able to hit ad budgets for the first time in channel's history and move NY1 from 26th to 1st in revenue for Time Warner news channels nationwide.

  • Creative Services Director

    Time Warner Citicable Advertising

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1990 — 1991 (1 year)

    Founded and formed the Creative Services Department. Hired and trained a successful staff of marketing, promotion and sales strategists to support the sales division’s efforts. Developed sales product positioning and marketing communication plans, advertising campaigns, collateral materials and staged advertiser related events.
    • Executive produced award winning ad solicitation spots funded by partnering national cable networks.
    • Married cable network tune-in spots to client provided footage to create successful local ad campaigns.
    • Directed on-air promotions and tune-in spots for clients and cable networks.
