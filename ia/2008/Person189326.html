tim savage | syndications :: tim@savages.cc’s Summary

Tim is a 4th generation entrepreneur started in Business to Business Sales and executive search and eventually founding an executive search firm in 2001. By 2004 the executive search company had grown to over $3 million in sales and Tim had a business development idea. The idea focused on executive search, which sets up qualified candidates for clients, and classic business development. In June 2004 Savage Syndications was born.

In January 2005 he started up in Lexington Kentucky with his brother Geoff and a year later his brother James. To date, they have gained experience with Advertising Agencies, Interactive Media, eCommerce, Sports Marketing and a number of miscellaneous companies. Tim was a partner in New Dimension Marketing, which was the team that relaunched iHigh.com and spent more than a year completely focusing on interactive advertising and event sponsorship.
