Andy Gruber’s Summary

We provide real estate asset management services for private and institutional investors who wish to benefit from the attractive returns this industry can offer in Brazil but at the same time want to invest with total transparency and professionalism.

Through our services clients have access to significant local market experience accumulated during many years working for multinational companies that are leaders in this industry around the world and have been pioneers in introducing international standards to the Brazilian real estate market.

Our expertise allows us to better manage the risks that are specific to these asset class, we can anticipate potential problems and propose mitigation actions to reduce this risks throughout the real estate investment cycle. Our know how allow us to have a positive impact from the initial product conception for a real estate development to the liquidation of the investment by access to the local capital markets or international buyers.

Our strong network of contacts allows us to identify, select and then analyze and format a large number of high yielding real estate transactions. Many of these opportunities are not pubcily traded or have not reached the market in a pre-packaged format yet.

In regards to the deal pipeline we procure, we can act as developers/operators of the project or structure transactions where we partner with a local developer or general contractor and take over an active investment management position in the project, working exclusively as the owner/investor representative.
Regardless of the format, our focus is to maximize project returns for our investors
