Timothy Schwarz’s Summary

“Mastering the art of nuance is the greatest challenge in design…it’s all about speaking to character, culture and concept, without spelling it out. It’s defining the sense of discovery for the guest – the unspoken relationship between the designer and the experience.”

With this philosophy, I have enjoyed over a decade of shaping experiences through design, most recently as Design Director with Gettys, in their Chicago-based headquarters, a firm known for designing beautiful, hospitality concepts that succeed in driving revenues.

My experience includes residential, commercial and hospitality projects. Prior to joining Gettys, I worked as a senior designer and project manager for Jordan Mozer and Associates, Ltd. Some of my clientele included MGM-Mirage Properties, Lettuce Entertain You Enterprises and Holsten Brewery, AG. Prior to my commercial and hospitality experience, I started in luxury, single family and multi-family residential design and architecture with Morgante-Wilson Architects, Ltd. in Chicago. Coming into the firm as an intern/draftsperson, making my way up to Associate Principal.

Today, as Design Director, I am responsible for facilitating and maintain a steady, creative process, empower the design staff, enhance the quality of ideas produced by the design teams, and to strive to get those ideas implemented that generates value to the client, the user, and the firm. I provide boundaries and focus to the team; to support, convince and negotiate with all stakeholders; and to build and maintain trust with them, for without trust there can be no creativity.

Some of my recent project work has included the new-build Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Macau, the redevelopment of the famed PGA National Resort and Spa, and various restaurants and hotels in the United States, Asia and Europe.
