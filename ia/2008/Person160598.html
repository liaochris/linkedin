Rick Hubbard’s Summary

Texas Rooms And Windows (TRAW) is a Dallas-based, design/build firm specializing in ultra energy-efficient, Sun Space Additions (Sunrooms, Conservatories and Custom Windows).
Rick Hubbard created & manages the company with his wife, Laura, who is their Office and Events Coordinator.
They have recently expanded their DFW & Austin offices and are in the process of welcoming their first customers from their newest location in San Antonio, TX.
Rick has extensive experience in residential construction with a special interest in unique, well-engineered designs, cutting-edge technologies, energy-efficient materials and installation techniques as well as producing the highest possible return on investment for Texas homeowners.
TRAW is one of the fastest growing Sunroom and Window Companies in Texas and is projected to be one of the Top 500 Remodelers in the nation in 2008.
They are currently the only builder in Texas to utilize revolutionary building technologies created specifically to produce an extremely energy-efficient, and virtually maintenance-free sunroom wall system manufactured with a patented, Vinyl-Hardwood Composite produced by Crown Sunrooms.
TRAW is the Exclusive supplier in Texas for:
Crown Windows & Sunrooms as well as Lancaster, Solid Mahogany Conservatories.
These impressive product lines have put them far above the competition, of which more than 90% still rely on inefficient, aluminum framing for their sunrooms which are meant for non climate-controlled, “recreational use” only.
Texas Rooms And Windows has truly provided homeowners with more attractive and more affordable choices for adding beautiful, light filled rooms to their homes.
Rick has developed a Contractor Evaluation Sheet available as an insider’s guide to choosing a qualified contractor and he is available for design consultations or to give advice to homeowners or other contractors who are interested in learning more about improving their homes or their company product lines.
