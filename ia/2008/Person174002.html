Shane Williamson’s Summary

Shane Williamson’s 23 years experience in various Information & Communication Technology (ICT) industries has included involvement in arenas such as,
* enterprise IT infrastructure
* pc hardware development
* digital tv
* video games
* massively multiplayer online games
* software development
* Internet
* content management services
* telecommunications
* mobile applications
A core theme throughout Shane’s career has been an involvement in all aspects of ICT development including 8 years at Microsoft involved in various Windows & Internet developer programmes that introduced to potential development partners newly released products such as Windows NT, development environment tools, Internet technologies, streaming media & Application Service Provision (ASP) development.

Recently Shane was the Partner Marketing Manager at Hutchison 3G Australia (known as 3) running the Developers on 3 & Solutions on 3 marketing programmes that he founded in 2003. The Developers on 3 programme has attracted a large number of Australian & international mobile solution developers. Whilst at “3” Shane launched the multi award winning Real Estate application, LOC3 that was Australia’s first mobile sales force application for this industry.

Convergence and innovation of mobile technologies are Shane’s passions and he has established a Blog called Shane’s Place to publish his findings and articles written on these subjects and discuss them with the global community. Shane’s Place is syndicated from time to time, both here in Australia & internationally.

Residing in Sydney Australia, Shane is married with a daughter.

Shane Williamson’s Specialties:

Business & concept development, Speaking/presenting, contract negotiations, product/programme marketing, technical & marketing evangelism
