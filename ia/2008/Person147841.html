Paul Lange - Portable Empire Architect’s Summary

I'm an Australian born entrepreneur & have lived outside 'Oz' for 22+ years although I returned in 2006 with Pauline, my wife of 7+ years who was born in the Netherlands. We met, were engaged and married on Friday the 13th which should make it clear that I'm not superstitious. I speak/read/write English (please no jokes from the Brits) :-), Dutch & German; also currently learning Italian & Greek.

My approach to building business (opportunities) is that each party brings something to the table. I'm interested in people who wish to engage and help to build something whilst accepting their own portion of the commercial risk that's involved. This can be in terms of time, materials & knowledge and/or financial participation. People who simply offer products and/or services in return for their next pay check can be good once projects have been established & there is either cash flow or one of the participants is providing the seed capital however, generally these people don't grab my attention as quickly as those with a more risk oriented approach. By the way, you can approach me on the same basis. If you want to close a deal, be sure that you've opened the door first.

What can I do for you? Well to be completely candid, a BIG ZERO!! as in "0", which I will attach firmly to the end of your bottom line. I am a business innovator & consigliere to businesses of all sizes, & take SME's from 5-6 figures to 6-7 figures. Enterprise consultancy is on a more strategic or executive level.

My background is in the areas of Enterprise IT Management (heavily weighted to Financial Services), & hospitality; the definition of hospitality differs across the world so for the sake of clarity, I mean activities that range from development, to erection, and (operational) management of venues such as hotels, restaurants, bars, clubs, nightclubs, cruise ships, event centres et al. Please see my website www.paullange.com.au for more information on all of my activities & my latest news.
