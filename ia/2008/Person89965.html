Jesper Toft Olsen’s Summary

Jesper has a background in mechanical engineering and business administration. He has more than seven years' international working experience from the medical device and industrial rubber sectors.

He kickstarted his career in Malaysia, as the head of Asian operations for a small US company in financial difficulties. After sucessfully turning around the Asian branch office - through close collaboration with the American headquarters and regional partners - he returned to Denmark to further develop his technical capabilities and project management skills in a Danish manufacturer of medical devices.

In 2005, he decided to pursue his interest in strategic planning and customer relationship management by taking a position, which he still holds, as international product manager for the same medical device company.
