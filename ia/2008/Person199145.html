Bas Van Gaalen’s Summary

Bas van Gaalen is a senior web developer, currently working at TomTom. He graduated from the polytechnic of Eindhoven in 1997 with a degree in Applied Physics. He worked for ABN Amro in the late nineties and as freelancer until 2004. In 2005, Bas joined TomTom where he was responsible for the PLUS section of the main web site and is now busy with other projects.
One of his latest personal projects is EarthPOI which combines Google Maps, Ajax technology and Prototype.

Bas Van Gaalen’s Specialties:

Specialities range from back-end to front-end, server to client: PHP, MySQL, Javascript, Dynamic XHTML, CSS, etc.
