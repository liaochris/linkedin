Jeanne Fuller’s Summary

Our goal with the ForAccess.com site is to create a shift in the way consumers search for and evaluate licensed professionals.

We want consumers to return to 'the driver's seat' regarding their care and the way they search for services. Instead of selecting medical or mental health professionals based upon a list from an insurance company or a zip code, we provide tools and education so consumers understand the importance of verifying a professional's license before seeking care or services.

As the level of professional care provided to consumers is raised, increased job satisfaction for professionals is obtained, as they are receiving targeted referrals (they kind of clients they most want), while also saving time, money and energy.

Our goal is to provide needed information about professionals to consumers, empowering them to make more effective choices that positively impact their lives.

Enjoy networking that is constructive, positive and financially beneficial to all concerned.

We are ALWAYS interested in ethical, successful commissioned sales people to help us obtain new markets and to satisfy existing and future customers.
