Rick Stout’s Summary

Producer/Director/Owner of a Media Consulting firm: designing and producing projects that include corporate training films/videos, political campaigns, television ads, private & public awareness campaigns, event & convention technical coordination and fundraising for non-profit organizations. Freelance: T.D. / Producer for various feature and short film projects. With over thirty-five years in the industry, I have produced / directed & edited over 300 educational / documentary, training and web based instructional videos. Skills include: Non-linear editor, videographer, photographer, lighting, and sound. Special skill directing first time 'non-experienced talent' as well as experienced talent. I possesses a very strong sense of ‘teamwork’, good at building & organizing crews and teams by matching their skills and strengths for varied projects.

As usual, I am available to work on other people's projects. Skills include most all facets of production and post-production, instructional development, event coordination and management. Sales, marketing and fundraising are additional skills I have developed, as well as, interpersonal and group development directed toward 'team building' and success.

My Goal is to use my expertise in multifaceted projects such as film, television, political activism and worthy charitable causes, idealistically; bringing about hope, excitement, passion and compassion to enact positive change in the world around us.
