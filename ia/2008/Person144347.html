Tom Ransom’s Summary

I excel at corporate storytelling - I can translate features and benefits into a story that prospective customers can relate to - I can tell your story in a variety of ways - tradeshows, collateral, web, and direct mail.

More than 10 years of experience and success in high-tech product marketing (in-bound and out-bound), marketing communications, sales and sales management. Successfully launched hardware and software products, both consumer and business-to-business, frequently exceeding revenue and market share goals. Pioneered applications of technology to lower marketing costs, improve response rates, and reduce collateral production lead times.

Tom Ransom’s Specialties:

Creating brand identities and creating tactical marketing programs. Expert at Trade Show and Event marketing.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Accounting industry)

    January 2004 — October 2007 (3 years 10 months)

    Corefino provides FAO (finance and accounting outsourcing) services for early-stage companies. Unlike a bookkeeping service, Corefino provides the technology, procedures and staffing that a company needs to meet its reporting needs. Just as importantly, Corefino can grow with a company from its earliest stages through the later stages of growth - adding information systems and staffing as needed for each stage.

    Corefino is able to leverage economies of scale to implement technologies and processes that result in a cost effective solution for our clients. So at costs comparable to what a company would be paying to deal with accounting internally, Corefino’s clients have access to higher quality information systems and accounting services. Similarly, the technology platform enables Corefino to increase its efficiencies and reduce staff loading with each increase in client count.
