Mark G. Rockett’s Summary

Mark's 12 year advertising career began in Media and Account Management before moving into the more technical field of Interactive Marketing.

Mark's Account Management experience, including Clients such as Volvo Trucks, Continental General Tire, Bank One and Kellogg's, helped to hone his skills in strategic development, project management and supervision, creative production and evaluation.

Over the past three years, Mark managed Interactive Media for The Martin Agency in Richmond, VA. During that time, Mark planned and managed online advertising campaigns utilizing all forms of online advertising tactics. These include search engine marketing, email advertising, as well as display advertising and sponsorships on content-specific sites and portals. These campaigns were planned for Clients such as Champion Mortgage, Olympus America, ALLTEL, Careerbuilder.com, The Motley Fool, Gerber, and Reynold's Metals.

In addition to the planning and implementation of these media schedules, Mark also implemented and managed ad serving technology that allowed these advertisers to maximize their ROI through detailed tracking and thorough campaign optimization.

Mark is equally proficient communicating to a National online audience, as well as targeting online audiences on geographic and demographic levels.

Realizing a void for these services within the advertising industry, Mark left The Martin Agency in July 2003 in order to start his own Interactive Advertising Consultancy to help advertisers make the transition to the Interactive medium.
