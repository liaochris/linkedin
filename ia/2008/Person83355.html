James Towers’s Summary

My professional interests include the development and analysis of graphical models in languages such as UML, SysML and BPMN. The application of analysis, design and process patterns, plus identification and re-factoring of anti-patterns.

I strive to be both adaptable and pragmatic and always exhibit a “can do” attitude.

I am constantly trying to improve the quality and relevance of my work through both continuous professional development and peer review.

I am able to work as part of a team, meeting the objectives set for me, or alone setting my own objectives, and have frequently demonstrated the integrity and confidence required to act as an independent authority.

I appreciate the importance of good communication within an organisation and know the value of high-quality reporting and frequent information dissemination.
