Daryl H. Bryant’s Summary

Daryl H. Bryant, President and CEO of Hudson Horizons, has 10 years experience in the technology community and wields hands-on experience of many aspects of the Internet and technology industries.

Mr. Bryant has received a BS in Computer Science and Mathematics from Montclair State University in Upper Montclair, New Jersey.

Mr. Bryant has a wide range of experience in technical sales and management. His post-college career started at Knoa Corporation, a software company that developed a proprietary user-sensing technology that provided training and statistical analysis for large-scale corporate applications, including such companies as PeopleSoft, IBM, and Kraft. Mr. Bryant started as a Solution Developer and quickly moved up to take on more managerial and sales responsibilities. In one year, Mr. Bryant was promoted to Lead Sales Engineer. He was responsible for providing outside technical sales support to the Knoa Sales Team as well as giving presentations, technical demonstrations and product training to prospective and existing clients.

During the Internet Bubble Burst of 2002, Mr. Bryant left Knoa Corporation and started Hudson Horizons. During the initial startup phase Mr. Bryant worked at night as a waiter to subsidizing his income and build Hudson Horizons during the day. After pursuing through the tough startup times, Mr. Bryant, was able to establish a solid client base and build Hudson Horizons to a competitive website development company.

Daryl's goal is to continue and offer innovative solutions that help his clients achieve e-business success through the Internet. His vision is to continually structure Hudson Horizons in a way that always formulates them as "The New Light for e-Business".

Also, Co-Founder and CEO of No.1 Industries - providers of online social networking communities, specializing in niche markets. Their websites include www.number1talent.com, www.number1band.com, and www.number1artist.com.
