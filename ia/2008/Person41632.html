Christopher Tucker’s Summary

Eight years ago I left the comforts of NYC, my friends & family & moved west to start a career in technology while pursuing my desired lifestyle.

At SLI Systems we provide the premier (SaaS) site search (aka internal search, enterprise search) for websites looking to make it easy for visitors to find the products & information they're searching for. Just as Google serves up the most relevant results across the Internet & we take the same approach within a website but offer it as a service. We power the search box & serve up the most popular search results for the long-tail of search phrases used within the website. Our search results pages are highly ranked by Google & other search engines which results in new, highly converting SEO traffic. This is our automated, user-generated SEO service called Site Champion.

Having worked at VeriSign, PayPal & SLI Systems, I have gained extensive knowledge about ecommerce & find it fascinating. My expertise is specifically in the online security, payments & search space. In the last eight years I have primarily worked in sales, account management & business development- but also have made a concerted effort to learn all aspects of the businessworld- proactively working with colleagues, customers & partners to better understand their roles within the organization, collaborating to better understand the big picture. Taking on these various roles has given me a great perspective in working with & fulfilling internal, customer & partner needs. I'm always a top performer which I contribute to my curiosity, drive, work ethic, likability & competitiveness.

My short-term goals include:
- Remain top performing salesperson
- Proactively contribute to all aspects of SLI
- Build out my expertise in search & search engine marketing

My long-term goals include:
- Leverage my experience & apply it at a start up and/or run an ecommerce business
- Sit on the board of a technology company
