  • Systems Analyst

    State of MI UIA

    (Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Government Administration industry)

    January 1975 — January 2001 (26 years 1 month)

    I worked my way thru the ranks of the unemployment agency. In the late 70's & 80's I worked the front lines. I then became a TRA supervisor and maintained that program. I was a claims examiner for 7-8 yrs then I was promoted to the Payment Supervisor in the branches. I then was promoted to a systems analyst and worked on the MARVIN & RIC projects which allow claimants and employers to use the Internet to file claims & tax info, call in for payments, etc. I retired 2001.
