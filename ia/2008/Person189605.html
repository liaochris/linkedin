Tina Vaziri’s Summary

Award winning art director and illustrator with over 6 years of education and experience, predominantly in fast-paced global environments. Dynamic team player and project manager highly skilled in client relationships. Delivering results in deadline-driven environments.

- Strategic and creative art direction of concepts using successful communication design for lead generation and advertising campaigns

- Traditional and new media designs that make a lasting impression and create trusting B to B and B to C relationships
