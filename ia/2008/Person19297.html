Sandor Bakalis’s Summary

Professionally and full-time involved in E-Business projects since 1996, fully involved in Online Travel since 1998. Published author on the subject of E-Business.

98-03: Highly successful commercial and product development of KLM Royal Dutch Airlines online. Helped KLM to become the leading full service airline on the Internet in Europe and Asia. International websites in over 50 countries, over 20 languages. Booking engine development. Revenue development from zero to multiple millions booked online. User interfaces, user experiences, booking conversion mastering. Taken charge of entry of KLM online into Asia-Pacific markets, developed the region out of Singapore. Local and cultural targeting. Complex corporate environments, international mergers.

03-05: Commercial development, business development, marketing for Priceline.com in Asia (Priceline, Hutchison-Whampoa JV), based out of Singapore and Hong Kong. Responsible for strong growth of new online travel models in AP region. Strong intercultural skills. Only operating foreigner in local teams.

05-Current: Co-founder of Fare.net and Sprice.com, groundbreaking travel search platforms in Asia-Pacific and Europe. US$10.2 million first round funding. Commercial and technical development of Sprice.com multinational travel search engine. Responsible for industry defining deals with travel companies in Asia and Europe.
