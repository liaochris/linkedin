Lee Fulmer’s Summary

Over 20 years experience across a range of private, public and academic organisations ranging from the Halifax Bank of Scotland Group to the BBC, from the DIT to the US Department of Defense. A strong record of successfully managing large-scale business change, insourcing and consolidation programmes.

Extensive experience working government bodies to shape regulation and share best practice including the Department of Culture, Media and Sport, the Office of Fair Trading, HM Treasury and the Department of Constutional Affairs in the UK and the Department of Education in Ireland.

Strong background in developing industry policies and standards through participation in industry bodies such as APACS and BACS in the UK as well as ICANN and the IETF on an international front. Frequent conference speaker in the areas of Knowledge Management, Communications, Governance and Architecture for companies ranging from Marcus Evans to Gartner. Freelance writer on topics as diverse as Design, Technology, Travel and Interviews.
