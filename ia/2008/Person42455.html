Dale Fallon’s Summary

I’ve earned a living as a web professional in Toronto since the mid-90s. At The Score I am focused specifically on the emerging areas of mobile content and applications. Our initiatives span: downloadable java apps, SMS, WAP and mobile video. From 2001-2006 I worked at CHUM Television (MuchMusic, Citytv, Bravo! etc.), ultimately as head of the Interactive Department. In my capacity as Director, I was responsible for 45 staff, and a breadth of activities, including: online media sales, content production, technical operations, and web application development.
