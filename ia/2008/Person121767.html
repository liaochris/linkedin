Mark Dorff’s Summary

I am a partner at the international law firm of Brown Rudnick and have over 12 years experience as a corporate lawyer. I am engaged in a general corporate and commercial practise and focus on complex international and cross-border transactions. I work with both publicly traded and privately held US, UK and European businesses in a broad range of matters, including public and private capital raising transactions, mergers and acquisitions, licensing transactions, joint ventures and other commercial and financial transactions. I am qualified as a US lawyer and as an English solicitor. I have been based in Brown Rudnick’s London Office since 1999.

As part of Brown Rudnick’s Cleantech Team, I am also part of collaborative, cross-disciplinary initiatives to assist clients in this evolving sector.

Examples of my recent experience include:

• Represented both investors and investee companies in connection with numerous venture capital and private equity investments in the US, the UK and across Europe.

• Negotiated on behalf of both buyers and sellers on numerous cross-border M&A transactions involving the acquisition or sale of companies in the US, the UK and across Europe.

• Advised several publicly traded European companies with NASDAQ listings and/or ADR facilities in the US on a broad range of US securities law matters, including Sarbanes-Oxley compliance and related matters.

• Represented numerous European companies and investment banks on US aspects of global securities offerings including in connection with Rule 144A private placements in the United States.

• Advised several US companies in connection with public offerings and listings on the Alternative Investment Market (AIM) of the London Stock Exchange.

• Structured a broad range of complex licensing and strategic partnership transactions in the US, the UK and across Europe on behalf of numerous international technology businesses.
