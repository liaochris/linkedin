Claus A Friis’s Summary

Experienced management consultant with strong track record of successful formulation and execution of large, complex strategic programs for financial services companies. Experience from senior strategy positions with global financial services industry leader and as a strategy management consultant. Significant international experience from the Americas, Europe and Asia. Key areas of strengths are general strategy, growth and innovation strategy, sales & distribution strategy, sales force and channel optimization, strategic modelling, market and customer analytics, customer relationship strategies as well as corporate restructuring/re-engineering. Industry focus is financial services, including retail and wholesale banking, insurance, credit cards, mortgages and investment banking. Accomplished team builder, leader and coach.
