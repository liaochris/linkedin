Richard Zinno, CBCP’s Summary

Richard Zinno is a highly motivated professional with strong decision-making, problem solving, leadership and customer relationship management skills. He has experience in Business Continuity Planning (BCP) and Program Management in both the corporate and governmental spheres and is currently an active Certified Business Continuity Professional (CBCP) certified through the Disaster Recovery Institute International.

Richard has worked in the field of BCP, Emergency Management and Disaster Recovery since 2001 and has developed and implemented programs from the ground up with two separate organizations. His background includes: strategy development, risk analysis, business impact analysis, plan development & implementation, plan testing & maintenance, emergency management, exercise program management, incident command system implementation for the private sector and offsite data replication & recovery. Through this experience, Richard brings a deep and broad skill set with a unique perspective on Business Continuity strategy development and the ability to identify a step-by-step approach through risk analysis, plan development, implementation and testing. His methods, plans and incident command/crisis management skills have been proved through nine different incidents and have proven successful, ensuring organizational resilience and continuity.

In addition to his current responsibilities as a Business Continuity Manager with Sprint Nextel, Richard serves as the Program Director for the Contingency Planning Association of the Carolinas (CPAC).
