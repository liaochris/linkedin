Simon Hamer’s Summary

I was a shy individual. With age, I realised that I was actually quite intelligent and fortunately I found some excellent guidance through "The Magic of thinking Big" by David Schwartz and some books by Les Giblin. Through a liking for this type of self help culture, I read and listened to more and more of this uplifting mind food. This literally changed my life, by opening up my expectations and dreams for my future. On the strength of the knowledge of life in these gems I planned and hunted down my wife.. I changed my employer from Midland Bank (now HSBC) to TSB Bank plc (now Lloyds TSB Bank plc) and my career in banking took off.

With my new bank I won best branch in the region award with my first post and a four day trip to Venice. Several rapid promotions followed.

I left in 1991, when most banks were shedding staff.
I went into debt counselling, interest rates were at an all time high in the UK, and there was a need for proper cool headed advice. After learning all I could from the franchise I'd joined I set about doing my own marketing... it worked... as the most successful consultant ... I became the National Trainer for the Debt Counselling franchise.

These two dove tailed careers taught me one thing....... with a few hundred pounds a month extra you could drastically change most peoples lives or happiness. So the search began... what could produce this for most people... that they could all do.

I ended up getting involved in local business.
