Tammy Mathias’s Summary

I love to meet new people and help them - both off-line (via events and meeting face-to-face) and on-line, like here on LinkedIn.

I'm the Director, Colorado Event Operations Integrated Alliances plan and execute their networking events and LinkedIn Workshops. My responsibilities include; scouting and negotiating venues,food lighting,entertainment,set-up of exhibitor booths and exibitors, planning the events and in both Denver and our Northern Colorado networking event called B2B Boulder-to-Broomfield. These events attract 100-500 people and 10-20 exhibitors each month,depending on the type of evnet and time of season.

I assist IA’s Founder, Mike O’Neil, with the LinkedIn Hands-on Workshops that he delivers throughout Colorado. These Workshops help people learn how to use LinkedIn, almost from a dead stop. I run the check-in process and provide customer service to the attendees – before, during and after the Workshops.

Why I am here in LinkedIn:

• I am seeking partners, sponsors, exhibitors and attendees for our networking events in Colorado. I am also seeking individuals that wish to learn more about how to use LinkedIn for business purposes and career development.

• I am networking with people that are interested in networking for mutual benefit. I believe that the true benefits of networking happen “down the road” so I am looking to network (and connect) with a large number of people.
