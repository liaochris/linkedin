Rachel Samuel’s Summary

Highly motivated and versatile defence and technology consultant with a proven track record for delivery of projects above customer expectations and to tight deadlines. Experienced business process and system modeller with particular interest in the use of MODAF during the procurement and design of C4I systems in line with Network Enabled Capability strategy. Familiar with MoD, government and commercial business domains.

Strong technical background with over five years’ experience as a GIS Developer for Capgemini UK plc. Winner of the Outsourcing Outstanding Achiever Award for her work on the £2 million upgrade of the client system to a Service-Oriented Architecture. Strong background in problem analysis and solution, basic training in Java programming and experience of ESRI products including ArcSDE and ArcIMS.

Rachel Samuel’s Specialties:

Defence & Technology consultation, MODAF, UML Modelling, Geographic Information Systems, software development, system analysis.
