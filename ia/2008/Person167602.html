Daniele Salomoni’s Summary

Consultant and Business Developer, I measure and develop the intangibles of a company. The main tools are intangibles balance sheet, education, envisioning process, culture change, plan of initiatives.

My specialties:
> measure and develop the "intangibles" of a company (using intangible assets balance sheet)
> help the organizations who are searching for a personalized growth model with the envisioning process and the following focus on vision deployment (few vital action)
> help the organizations to realize their goals through culture change and change management
> ensure the vitality of a company by developing its intellectual capital, providing the opportunity to foresee which areas need adjustment and the right moment in which to activate change
> inspire people to mobilize their collective energy, maximise the competencies that generate value and find the unique and inimitable soul of their organization (cultural audit)
> innovation management, paradigm shift, creativity
> HR management, education program, training, human capital management, workshops, effective presentation
> improvement plan, strategy management, performance management (budgeting, planning and control solutions), decision support system, balanced scorecard
> project management
> business development, account management, sales, pre-sales
> relationship at executive level
