Brian Jerome’s Summary

I have been involved with sales and marketing for about 15 years. Both as an employee and an owner. Telecom and specifically VoIP have held my fascination for a number of years now. I have had a lot of fun working in the industry and I have learned a lot.

In 2000 I founded Market Networks Inc. MNI was primarily focused on the development of VoIP Billing Software, exploring VoIP hardware and creating a local online shopping portal concept called Calgary Market.com. It was an incubator for future endeavours.

In May 2002, after 2 years of development, MNI launched their proprietary online VoIP billing software primarily using Cisco gateways as well as early adoption of Asterisk VoIP platforms. Calgarytelecom.com is our retail operation and test platform. We have grown it to a few thousand customers and it has become fairly profitable.

All of MNI's assets have now been purchased by Friendly Telecom Inc. which was founded in May 2005. FTI is the next evolution of our marketing strategy. Our goal has always been to simplify the Voip billing process using next-gen software technologies.
