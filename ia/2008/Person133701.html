Mitchell Levy’s Summary

Mitchell Levy is a book publisher (Happy About has published over 50 books), strategist, educator & prognosticator helping companies succeed by putting tools in the hands of corporations/individuals to allow them to create their own success. He is a frequent media guest and a popular speaker, lecturing on business and management issues throughout the U.S. and around the world. He has started 13 companies/joint ventures since 1997 and has provided strategic consulting to over 100 companies.

Mitchell Levy’s Specialties:

Publisher, executive editor, author of business and consumer books, will help companies and individuals publish books in physical, eBook and podbook formats, Business Strategy and Execution Management, Change Management, Reengineering, Executive Coaching, Business and E-Commerce Management, Outsouring, Prognosticator, Evangelist, Futurist, Industry Trends, Pragmatic Learning, and Publisher
