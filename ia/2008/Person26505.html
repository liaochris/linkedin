Brannon Hollingsworth’s Summary

I am an anomaly. If you don't believe me, just check out all of the stuff that I've done over the years - I've worked in everything from a pants factory to the high-tech Defense Industry and all the while, I've been writing and coding on the internet. There's not much, generally speaking, that I cannot do or have not already done.

I have a rare ability to take my two distinct halves: my slavering creative side and my laser-focused analytical side and merge them into an efficient but highly adaptable presence that bewilders people at first, but when I deliver "the project that couldn't be done" in half the time, they really start to like it... Regardless, I think that if I had to summarize my professional experience, it would be as follows:

I don't believe in boundaries. I believe that if you have a job that you need done and if you point me in the right direction, I can do it.

Maybe that counts for something...
