Stan Yee’s Summary

CCIE training for: Routing & Switching / Voice / Security / Service Provider

Internetwork Expert, Inc. is the industry leader in CCIE (Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert) training to assist networking professionals in obtaining Cisco Systems' highest level of certification. Our highly regarded instructors, Brian Dennis – CCIE #2210 (Quad CCIE: R&S/ISP Dial/Security/Service Provider) and Brian McGahan – CCIE #8593 (Triple CCIE: R&S/Service Provider/Security), have developed, from the ground up, the most comprehensive and thorough CCIE training materials and programs on the market today. All of our CCIE programs are continually updated and are always based on the latest blueprints from Cisco. We continue to have the fastest growing list of candidates that have used our training and PASSED the CCIE Routing & Switching Lab Exam.

Training solutions include Self-Paced or Instructor-Led Programs, Lab Workbooks (with Solutions Guide), Mock Labs, Bootcamps (onsite/online/recorded), and Rack Rentals.
