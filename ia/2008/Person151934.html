James Hurster’s Summary

My company: Pinnacle Design Studio, LLC is a design company that specializes in building web sites for individuals as well as small to midsized companies. The services we offer include new website design and development, website redesign for those that already have a site but need a new look or more enhanced features for a better user experience, graphic design of all types and small video/audio productions.

Pinnacle Design Studio, LLC is located in O’Fallon, Missouri however modern day technology allows us to work with clients Worldwide. Though most of our clients come from the St. Charles, St. Louis, and surrounding areas, we do welcome anyone as a new client regardless of their location. We have the ability to conduct virtual meetings online via video conferencing and screen sharing so you really do not miss a thing.

We know that there are many designers you can choose from and we appreciate the opportunity to work with those that choose us. We have a strong desire to grow into a leading web site design, graphic design and small video/audio production company but will not sacrifice our core values as a company to get there. We would encourage you to give us a call with any project ideas or questions that you may have about our industry in general. If we can help you then we will, if we can’t we will make sure we point you in the direction of another professional that can take care of your needs.

Our main software applications are industry leaders:
- Apple's Final Cut Studio 2
- Apple's Logic Studio
- Adobe Creative Suite Master Collection CS3 – “The” collection of the top design programs that Adobe has to offer. Some of the most popular are Photoshop CS3 Extended, Dreamweaver CS3, Flash CS3, Illustrator CS3, and much more.

James Hurster’s Specialties:

Web Design, Web Redesign, Flash Web Design, Graphic Design, Business Stationary & Logos, 3D Graphic Animation, Analog to Digital Video Conversion, Video Picture Slide Shows w/ Music, Online Video Conversion, Audio & Video Editing, etc.
