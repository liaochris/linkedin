Arnold Roth’s Summary

Arnold Roth's background is on-line at www.roth2.com

As a lawyer, manager, entrepreneur and business consultant, his background is in commercialization of technology, business development, management and marketing in several technology fields: software, bio-informatics, technology services, simulation & modeling, Internet technologies and in the not-for-profit domain.

Roth's career began as a commercial lawyer in Melbourne, Australia in 1976. In 1978, he co-founded the firm Roth Warren Solicitors and remained there until 1988 when he moved to Israel.

As an entrepreneur and senior manager, he co-founded Software Corporation of Australia (IPO 1985) and served as its managing director (CEO) through two public-funding rounds.

Living in Israel since 1988, he served AMDOCS (NYSE: DOX) as in-house counsel; then practised as a solo lawyer for four years before becoming CEO or senior manager of a succession of Israel-based technology companies during the next decade. Their common factor: a focus on international markets and the challenge of turning leading-edge technologies into profitable business.

Roth and his wife also established a foundation which addresses the special needs of families home-caring a seriously disabled child.

Arnold Roth’s Specialties:

Thirty years of practical experience managing technology ventures on three continents, including twelve years as partner in a law firm, mean that Roth has an untraditional, hands-on approach to practical challenges.
