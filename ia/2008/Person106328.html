Khaled Amri’s Summary

- January 2007 - Present:
Project manager - PPP structuring unit within the RFF Financial division in charge of the financial aspects of the tender process for the GSM-R PPP project as well as HSL Nîmes-Montpellier bypass, HSL Tours-Bordeaux and HSL Le Mans-Rennes PPP projects.
Projects under supervision:
* € 800 Mios PPP for the inception of a global GSM system on the French railway network, availability base concession. Competitive dialogue on-going, binding and final offers expected in fall 2008
* € 1.2 Bio PPP for the Nîmes-Montpellier bypass. Availability-base concession, invitation to tender notice to be issued by year-end
Participation in other projects :
* € 7 Bio concession for the financing, construction, operation and maintenance of a 300 km high speed rail link between Tours and Bordeaux, also known as the South Europe Atlantic project, France’s largest concession scheme to date. Invitation to negotiate issued mi-February 2008
* € 2 Bio construction of a 200 km HSL in Brittany (Rennes-Le Mans). Preliminary evaluation on-going

- May 2003 - January 2007:
Credit Analyst in Real Estate Structured Finance firstly with "Société Générale" for 9 months (internship) and since January 2005 with Eurohypo Corporate and Investment Banking. I was in charge of the whole origination and structuring process, namely: approaching the target customers including new ones, responding to their financing requests by structuring competitive offers and then follow-up of the legal aspects. The deals size ranges from € 10 Mio to € 1,000 M for various projects including acquisitions, developments, share deals and corporate financings. The deals nature includes debt financing but also corporate mortgage backed securities (LBO/CMBS).

- 2001-2002: Junior auditor at French auditing company "Mazars et Guérard".

Khaled Amri’s Specialties:

Project finance. PPP contracts. Railways. HSL. Infrastructure. Hybrid finance: LBO/CMBS. Securitization. Real estate finance.
