Vincent ( Vinni ) Gaines’s Summary

I have 19 years of customer care and sales / service call center management experience and over 35 years in the airline business. I have been responsible for the successful creation and operation of the current Mindpearl (US) business, from its site selection to the expansion of its customer base.

I have worked my way up from the mailroom at Swissair (the airline of Switzerland) through increasingly responsible positions from reservations agent to Manager of Field Sales for the New York region before becoming Manager of Swissair's Eastern Region reservation center in 1989. Since then I have had responsibility for all Swissair USA reservations operations including: management of multiple sites prior to the consolidation of all USA reservations into a single call center; the integration of Sabena Belgium World Airlines and Swissair reservations centers into a single location and the improvement of overall productivity by one third; the sale of Qualiflyer Customer Care Center to Mindpearl; the development and implementation of business plans to meet Mindpearl's performance and profitability standard; the diversification of the business and in 2006 became the owner of Mindpearl (US), Inc.
