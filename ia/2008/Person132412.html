Mike Meiners’s Summary

I have been privileged to work at several excellent newspapers in a variety of roles. This has given me a broad range of experiences and skills, especially in the areas of management, research, technology, budgeting and administration.

I have been lucky enough to have several opportunities to do new things and in 20 years I have held 11 different positions at five different newspapers. This makes my resume a little bit difficult to read but the summary is simple: I worked at the Tampa Tribune for 7 happy and successful years before being asked to take a job at the Sun-Sentinel where I spent 10 years before being recruited by the St. Louis Post-Dispatch.

My 10 years in South Florida were interupted by a brief but exciting time in Washington, D.C. during which I worked at both USA Today and the Washington Post before returning to the Sun-Sentinel.
