  • Freelance Writer

    Sole Proprietorship

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Writing and Editing industry)

    July 2006 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    I am a creative, flexible, reliable writer and strategist with 20 years of professional experience. Having worked with some of the most well-known brands in the world, my publication, interactive, advertising, public relations and marketing collateral credits are extensive.

    My client work has included Wendy’s, Procter & Gamble, Hewlett-Packard, Nationwide, NetJets, HER Real Living, Florida Association of Realtors, The Scotts Company, The Longaberger Company and Ohio State University Medical Center. My writing has appeared in Ohio Magazine, Business First and other regional publications.

    I believe successful creative is a marriage of innovative design and masterful copy. Even the most eye-catching layout will fall flat if the messaging is off-track, uninspiring or overpowering.
