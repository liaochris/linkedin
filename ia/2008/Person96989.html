Jo Pabari’s Summary

I founded GoodStuff Consulting in early 2007 - a strategic research and insight consultancy that seamlessly fills the gap when the client doesn't have either the time or the expertise to manage the process themselves.

Both I, and the network of independent consultants with whom I work, have many years of being on the client-side, meaning that we understand what it’s like and the challenges faced. We have developed insight processes, trained hundreds of people across the world, and (importantly) used the thinking ourselves when developing new products, repositioning old ones and developing communication campaigns.

GoodStuff Consulting is based in the UK but works globally.
