Dave Venneri’s Summary

Extremely accountable and team-minded leader with excellent communication, motivational and strategic planning skills. Industry experience includes business consulting services, apparel, retail, sales process solutions, customer relationship management applications and pro sports. I have been successful in growing revenue wherever I have gone and have done it with great enthusiasm, work ethic, integrity, relationship-building and a sense of humor.
