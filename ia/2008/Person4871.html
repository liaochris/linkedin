Adekunle Dinah. MSc, MBCS, PMP’s Summary

My goal is to to maintain a professional position as a Project Manager responsible for managing, planning and coordinating project activities in order to meet and accomplish project set goals and objectives within the stipulated time frame, scope, budget and quality.
My area of speciality is in Business Systems Analysis and process improvement with superior implementation of Rational Unified Process (RUP) methodology and application of Project Management Life Cycle methodology. I have Extensive experience in gathering business requirement and defining functional specification for the requirement.
In addition to my skill sets, I posses great experience in creating workflow diagrams of development process in order to bridge the between the business client and development team. I also participate in all aspect of the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) spanning from business requirement gathering through to Quality Assurance QA and User Acceptance Testing UAT.
My professional experience in project management proves my ability to plan, organise and delegate duties with great awareness of timeline priority – task management.
