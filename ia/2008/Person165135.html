Roy Vella’s Summary

Recruited by RBS to lead mobile efforts globally as an "intrapraneur". Still active entrepreneur & advisor to startups and non-profits.

Previously, head of Mobile for PayPal Europe and helped jump-start multiple new businesses within PayPal during my time there. First Merchant Services by building the team and driving large channel partnership deals. Next, PayPal's launch into the travel space and finally with mobile in Europe. Prior to PayPal, served in various startup/VC roles from Partner/co-founder to VP of Sales & Marketing.

Highly active in the non-profit and arts arena, served as President, Board of Directors for Variety Children's Charity of Northern California and Treasurer, Bay Area Theater Sports Improv. Have a BA in Mathematics (with High Honors) from Holy Cross College, JD from Stanford Law School, and MBA w/certificates in Global and Public Management from Stanford Graduate School of Business.
