Maurice Pothof’s Summary

I started my career in Germany at a traditional media agency called Crossmedia GmbH.

After two years working as a media planner I joined a start-up called NetPlus in the roll of Managing Partner. NetPlus was a ad-network for leading online publishers in The Netherlands. AdLink Internet Media AG acquired NetPlus in 2001 and I stayed for three other years in the roll of Managing Director.

I left Adlink after the merge with Doubleclick and joined Google, which was at that time still a start-up in The Netherlands. I had different roles within Google, but I have mainly been responsible for managing the sales team and developing the Belgium market.

Together with two other Googlers we decided to leave Google to start up our own ‘search and new media’ agency (www.netsociety.nl). Netsociety B.V. is specialized in search engine marketing (SEA/ SEO) but also in other new media formats. Netsociety differentiates by using intelligent software to optimize campaign results and maximize ROI.
