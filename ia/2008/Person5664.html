Albert Poghosyan’s Summary

I was born in the small but happy city. Back in 1980 in early morning of 24 of June I was born in small Armenian city called Yerevan.
Soon after I have graduated my small Russian school, I have applied for education in the Most State University in Armenia and have been accepted on Faculty of the General Economics. From that time my life became a little more complicated but not interesting for sure. And very soon I have been invited to work in one of the biggest bank in Armenia. So shortly I just let everybody be on they own in that bank and started to read, as there wasn’t so much things to do. I graduate University with very good marks for Dissertation, which was by the way already at that time in the field of Internet Technologies and the title was “Information technology in system of global marketing”.
After army my career starts in real world. So I just take the first offer. Than in that short period of time, I have got promoted from the Merchandiser (read workman) to supervisor of procurement and tendering department and eventually to consultant of eGovernance projects in Africa and Latin America. This run was so hard that when I got the Chevening Scholarship from British Council - I said “yes” immediately, even though I eventually got the job of my dream. I had a little bit more important thing which I was missing while I was running for all this everyday tasks - thinking…
So than I have got into my second education at University of Salford on MSc eGovernance and were happy that I had plenty time to think and work. Then eventually I have graduated the master I was free and dear to start anything new. So, I have started from developing my own company and trying to get transparent and open environment in the shiny new office in Yerevan. I have started up Armenian Project Management Association which now I am developing up to creating National Standards for Project Management.
