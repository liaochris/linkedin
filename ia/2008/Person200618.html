Wolfgang Jaegel’s Summary

Strong background in Coding and Data Security. Dedicated since 1996 to pure and applied research in the area of search engine optimization, Web Analytics and Internet Marketing. A recognized expert in the area of SEO and Internet marketing, published author, consultant, coach and speaker on a variety of Internet topics.

WebTrends, Google Analytics, Omniture, WebSideStory, ClickTracks and more ... it's not about the tool, it's about what you do with it!

Web Analytics is a fundamental requirement of any online marketing campaign, however it's not easy for most organizations. Web Analytics is a series of identifiable business processes that not only require technology but most important talented professionals.

As a consultant and online marketing strategist I've done extensive work with a multitude of clients, creating significant branding and e-commerce performance improvements, generating conversions and ROI as a result.

I have worked on innovative marketing solutions for blue chip clients including: Symantec, CREATIVE, ACCOR Hotel Group, IndoSat, TRUE, AIS, Globe, HSBC, K-Bank, MasterCard, Samsung, Nike, Nestle, Ford, Bangkok Airways, Singapore Tourism Board and more.

Motivated by achievement, I thrive in entrepreneurial environments where fast-paced change and new technology is the norm.
