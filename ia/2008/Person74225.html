Hal Finkelstein’s Summary

I am Chairman of The South Florida SCORE, my office is located in Boca Raton Florida. Last year we helped and consulted with 3500 organizations in Florida and many other states.
I also head up our ecommerce team and as such I am presently working on 33 ecommerce websites, which includes web design, SEO and shopping cart interaction. Presently we use Expression Web 2, Dreamweaver, Ajax, Joomia, Drupal, Plone, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and most online site builders and server side software. I help our more advanced clients with Cloud, Grid and Cluster hosting.
Our Chapter has 42 active consultants and we also produce a business development book and provide seminars to help businesses prosper.

In my private life I am involved as owner/investor in several ecommerce websites and I wrote 12 engineering books. Many of my books have been translated into several different middle eastern languages, Spanish and German.
In addition, from 1990 to 2000 I gave 10 seminars a year throughout the U.S and Canada on
toxic environment control for hospitals and commercial facilities.

Presently, about 3800 organizations around the world utilize my books to train facility engineers. In the past I owned a construction and consulting company in New York and New Jersey. I was also Chief Engineer of the New York State Urban Development Corporation.
