Phil Lenton’s Summary

Compsoft Plc has provided cutting-edge data management solutions for over 25 years. Specialising in building Internet-based systems for high-growth, typically early-stage companies, we ensure the highest quality bespoke development due to our rigorous design and build processes.

My main goal is to see Compsoft Plc grow exponentially over the next few years.

I aim to acheive this by:
- Employing only the best and keenest staff
- Focusing tightly on our niche market of high-growth businesses
- Ensuring complete customer satisfaction by always delivering what the client wants, when they want it.

Phil Lenton’s Specialties:

Bespoke software development, Equinox, Microsoft .NET, working with high-growth companies
