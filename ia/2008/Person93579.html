Joel Binn’s Summary

Joel has over 20 years of technology and management consulting experience. Specialist in systems transformation, project management, technology design, and working within technology organizations, with experience in both domestic and international development. Built and managed a consulting firm whose clients were primarily Wall Street banks and brokerage companies including top firms such as Merrill Lynch, Citibank, J.P. Morgan / Chase, National Westminster Bank, Bank of America, Pfizer, AT&T and Government agencies including the Departments of the Army, Navy and the Merchant Marines.


Prior to his current technology and management consulting position, Joel held CIOs position at Blue Flame Data, a marketing services firm founded by executives of several Global 1000 companies, spent two years in the UK creating and heading up the Operations and Technology units for Orbian, a joint venture between Citibank and SAP, and School Construction Authority, a public agency overseeing over $5bil in construction projects.



Skill set includes:

1 - Strong background in management, business transformation, technology strategy, turnarounds, reorganization and startups.
2 - Created reporting procedures (Sarbanes-Oxley and MiFID) and methods, for financial institutions and technology groups .
3 - Developed Project Management process based on PMI's PMBOK for Pfizer.
4 - Technology delivery and consulting for over 25 years.
5 - Often called upon for M&A due diligence.
6 - Financial Services for over 20 years.
