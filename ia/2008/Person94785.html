John Fielder’s Summary

John is an accredited Master ECI & NLP Coach, (4000+ accredited coaching hours) Certified Trainer & Master Practitioner of NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), a knowledgeable Career Performance and Executive Coach and a Chartered HR and Management Professional, with over 35 years experience of working with individuals, teams and organisations, within a diverse range of companies spanning a wide range of market sectors.

His professional portfolio includes executive & management coaching, strategic career development coaching, NLP training, management development, psychometric assessment, top team building and stress management.

John is an enthusiastic innovator and change agent, who uses experiential approaches, practical and theoretical knowledge, plus NLP techniques to align organisational values, thereby increasing the performance, self-esteem and effectiveness of individuals, teams and organisations. He has coached and trained many thousands of individuals at all organisational levels in their career development, and successfully used psychometrics for the management of change.

John is an international presenter, trainer and speaker on the uses of NLP for career and performance coaching, and has published articles on stress management, performance coaching and the use of the MBTI and NLP for personal and organisational development.

John has worked extensively with HM Forces ~ Army ~ Navy ~ Royal Air Force, delivering NLP Communications Trainings and is on the academic faculty and final coach assessor with Noble Manhattan Coaching.

John Fielder’s Specialties:

Over 30 years experience working with individuals and groups on career management and professional development issues. Professionally qualified career strategist and human resource process consultant, certified Master ECI (European Coaching Institute) Coach, NLP Coach, NLP trainer, and Master TimeLine Therapist with proven success in delivering executive coaching, transition management, stress counselling, psychometric assessment and career development programmes.
