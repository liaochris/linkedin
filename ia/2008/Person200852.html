Gavin Wonnacott’s Summary

- Strategic and policy lead for SW science park and incubator network (total spend £500m over 10 years); Guiding three science parks and numerous incubator projects; shaping national policy and practice (member UKBI Enterprise Panel, member BERR Collaborative Products design team) (2006-)

- Successfully set-up and developed investment readiness programmes (2002-2004)
- Presented papers and courses and contributed to promotion of best practice in incubation and investment for regional, national, international bodies (1998-)
- Led cross- and inter-Departmental technology-finance co-ordination committee
- Chartered minerals engineer with mathematical modelling and process design expertise
