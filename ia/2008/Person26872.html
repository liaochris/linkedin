Bret Stout (bretstout.com)’s Summary

Bret Stout has recently been appointed as the creative lead for Aniden Interactive, leading strategy and creative for the Houston, Mountain View and Costa Rico teams - creating unparalleled brand experiences that redefine the online space.

Bret began his career in advertising in 1981 with an extensive portfolio in both traditional and online media. Other agencies include iccentric, imc2, Huge Advertising, tm: interactive (TM Advertising), Temerlin McClain, Chiat Day and Leo Burnett.

Bret Stout has amassed a very large collection of advertising and marketing awards for his work. He has been a guest speaker for many speaking engagements on advertising. In 1983, he wrote his first broadcast television commercial at the age of 21, Southwestern Bell’s “ET Phones Home - Reach Out and Touch Someone”. This one television spot won more advertising awards than any other to date.

In 1988, Bret took a temporary hiatus from advertising and opened the Stout-McCourt gallery through 1994 representing unknown, Nationally-known and Internationally- known painters and sculptors. He is the 1993 Recipient of the Multi-Ethnic Heritage Foundation Cultural Philanthropy Award.

This former Vice President of the Dallas Art Dealers Association has been featured in many publications including, Dallas Morning News (DMN) High Profile three (3) times (including “Stout Offerings”), DMN’s Today Section, and The Dallas Observer two (2) times. He has also been featured on the classical radio station WRR 101.1.

Mr. Stout has also created custom retail apparel for Harley-Davidson, Mercedes, XBox, and has designed uniform programs for DirecTV, Rio Casino, Frontier Airlines, among many others. He also concepted and developed Target’s “Bullseye Brand”, their new ‘private label’ retail apparel line.

He is a Combat-Decorated U.S. Marine Corps Veteran (eight ribbons - four medals) and was activated in 1990 from the reserves to serve in the middle east.

Bret Stout (bretstout.com)’s Specialties:

Brand Strategy, Creative Strategy, Creative Direction, Leader of Writing and Design Teams, Writing, Design, Illustration (both by hand and Photoshop and Illustrator)
