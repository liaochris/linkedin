Darrin Murriner’s Summary

I currently provide financial management for the Financial Institutions Division at The Midland Co, a specialty property & casualty insurance provider based in Cincinnati. In this position I provide financial reporting, analysis in support of our lender distribution channel.

Along with my wife, I am also the owner of BabysitEase, a babysitter placement service started in Cincinnati but now franchising to all cities across the country. The service not only finds quality sitters but we qualify the babysitters with interviews, background checks and reference checks and we schedule the sitters for families. We currently have franchises in Lexington KY & Baltimore MD and will be expanding to cities across the country in 2007.
