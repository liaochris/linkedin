Mic Mell’s Summary

Mic Mell is committed to excellence, and fulfilling artistic visions. He has worn many hats in his career, including producer, bandleader, performer, session musician, promoter, label owner, freelance writer, and career consultant. Mic is well versed in many styles of music, including funk, hip hop, electronic (of all styles), reggae, blues, jazz, dub, rock, and latin. He has toured across the U.S. with several projects, as a keyboardist, bass player, and electronic musician.

Mic is committed to bringing your project to the next level. Mic is a team player who brings enthusiasm to any project. If you are up to something in the music business, Mic can help.
