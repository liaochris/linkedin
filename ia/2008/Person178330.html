Max Chavanne’s Summary

Music Advisor & Sound Consultant, founder and driving force behind Sonic-Nurse.com. Solid knowledge of all genres of music, implementing key audio where appropriate. Keen eye for visuals.

Fluent in French and English, I am an experienced media person. Beginning with my tenure at Hachette Filipacchi Medias in Paris, I have experienced the communications industry from many angles, being on the staff of a variety of their publications.

In my freelance work, I understand the importance of out-of-the-box thinking, while still maintaining budgets and deadlines, and holding focus on the target demographic.

Max Chavanne’s Specialties:

Boosting advertising, special events and media projects — Channeling meaning and emotion through sound — Sound consulting — Sonic branding
