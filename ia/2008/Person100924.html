Justin Marks’s Summary

I have over 11 years of experience in Wireless Telecommunications, mainly working as a consultant on CDMA networks. In 1998 I founded Complete Wireless Networks, Inc. to provide consulting services to wireless equipment vendors and carriers.

I grew this company to $1.6 million in revenue by the 3rd year of operation, and generated close to $6 million total.

Company website: www.cpnets.com

In 2004 I co-founded Autonomous Network Technologies, Inc., a research and development company transitioning research from the University of Arizona to commercial applications. During 2005 and 2006 we carried out an unclassified R&D contract for the Air Intelligence agency.

Company websites: www.antllc.com / www.avirtec.com
