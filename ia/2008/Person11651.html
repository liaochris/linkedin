Andrew Wilson’s Summary

Andrew is now responsible for the management of the SAIC contracts function in India. This work includes the management and development of business proposals, managing internal authorisations to proceed, performing risk identification, mitigation and avoidance, implementing policies and providing broad and effective contracts oversight. Generally he takes the lead in the resolution of complex contracts-related issues and contributes to the business development and profitability management processes.

Andrew’s expertise is based on many years in practice with some of Scotland’s leading commercial law firms followed by work in-house. He has been admitted as a solicitor in Scotland since 1993. During that period, he has practised in commercial litigation and niche commercial practice before moving to work within the IT industry. He has considerable experience of the successful resolution of legal issues facing commercial organisations.

He has spent many years advising and representing clients ranging from owner-managed enterprises to local authorities and multi-national financial institutions. He has a detailed knowledge of the legal issues that face companies that are growing or changing.

He is now responsible for providing proactive and comprehensive commercial and contractual support to a broad range of business activities in the UK, US and India.
