My current position is working as Director of Advertising Operations at JumpTV International and JumpTV Sports as well as World Championship Sports Network (WCSN), my responsibilities are and not limited to project management and development, overseeing strategic digital partnerships, business operations and development, developing and maintaining strong advertising/business client relationships, developing strong relationships and working effectively with colleagues in a range of internal departments, e.g., Sales, Marketing, Creative/Design, Content/Editorial, Product Development, effectively managing clients’ campaign programs within specified timeframes, monitoring and tracking assigned projects for effectiveness and many other projects.
