Pat Snyder’s Summary

My passion is writing and speaking on life balance issues with a humorous twist. As a certified laughter leader with the World Laughter Tour, I can customize laughter exercises for any social or workplace group that would like to create a stress-free laughter zone in the workday or for a special event. You can see one of my laughter sessions online at:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=edXBD5LvDvU

As one who's managed professional staffs during periods of down-sizing and restructuring, I am booking corporate laughter programs for 2009 to help combat the extra layers of stress that come in times of economic downturn.

Pat Snyder’s Specialties:

Laughter sessions for lawyers and corporate managers, writing workshops on using humor-writing techniques to help boost creativity and overcome writer's block.
