Tony Palmer’s Summary

Tony is an experienced senior consultant, RPM (AIPM) certified project manager and IBM Certified Advanced Application Developer. Tony has a pragmatic and self disciplined approach to delivering professional services and solutions using IBM Technologies for Internet and Intranet applications, Web Content Management Systems, Document Management, Collaboration, Portals, Knowledge Management Systems and Ebusiness applications.

Tony has exceptional client facing and relationship skills and consistently received above average client satisfaction results. In addition he has been specifically requested by a number of clients for further projects.

Whilst at Dialog IT, Tony was a hands-on Project Manager for the delivery of both the Intranet Portals redesign and the External Portal proof-of-concept phases for a large NSW government department. Tony initially delivered a small proof-of-concept with limited resources. This initial project led to a number of additional projects and Dialog being invited to respond to a much larger project.

Tony has a proven track record of adapting to new technologies, such as IBM Lotus Quickplace, J2EE and IBM WebSphere Portal Server and successfully Implementing industry standard techniques, such as CSS, XHTML, ANT and UML.

Tony is a versatile and unique consultant who is comfortable engaging business stakeholders at the CIO level, engaging developers and programmers and taking part in the analysis, design and development of solutions for clients.
