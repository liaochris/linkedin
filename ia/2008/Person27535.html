Brian Kunzmann’s Summary

My personal mantra really has become "GET IT DONE." Once moved over from being strictly a salesman for someone else to a business owner, reliant entirely on myself, my entire perspective has changed. There is nothing more important than getting it done. And yet, people have thousands of reasons why they can't, won't or whatever.

The last decade has provided invaluable learning experiences for me as I have learned every possible aspect of running a business, what motivates people and most importantly, how exactly to get things done. I have spent a lot of time and money in learning very specific skills and tactics (primarily in the world of sales, leverage, direct response marketing and goal attainment) to be successful in almost any endeavor. And not to skip over uncovering EXACTLY what I want out of this life and how to ATTAIN it.

And now, I WANT TO APPLY EVERYTHING I HAVE LEARNED WITHIN THE RIGHT GROUP, AS PART OF A VERY DRIVEN, FOCUSED, FUN & ENERGETIC TEAM, no longer strictly out there on my own.
