Dag Grødal’s Summary

I’m a well-educated and action oriented generalist. My overall professional focus is change. Do we see a need for change? If yes: How does our preferred future look like? Who will be influenced by the new reality we are aiming at creating? What must be removed and what must be added in order to get there? Where does resistance to change come from? Can we learn anything from best practices and wisdom from the past? How do we implement change? How can we measure that real change has taken place?
