Eric Hines’s Summary

Eric Hines is the founder and managing general partner of The Kalla Group. Mr. Hines is also the founding partner of Riverwalk Capital and works for the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) Counter Terrorism division. In October of 2007, Mr. Hines executed his second successful M&A exit of his company Applied Watch Technologies, a security software company focused in the open source security space.

Prior to selling Applied Watch, Mr. Hines sold his previous company Netstream to AlphaTrade, a publicly traded company in British Columbia.

Mr. Hines has over thirteen years of experience in the information security space working several years as a defense contractor in information warfare to the United States Department of Defense. Mr. Hines presented directly to the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) Technology Insertion Panel, prior to that performing risk assessments on our nation's critical infrastructure.

Mr. Hines has published numerous writings, advisories, and has worked closely with both the NIPC and Federal Bureau of Investigation Counter Terrorism Division in investigating cyber crime.

Mr. Hines has been interviewed live on Fox News regarding the Al-Qaeda cyber threats, published numerous papers in network security at the Sans Institute, SecurityFocus.com, Linux Magazine, and numerous other industry respected sites and news publications.
