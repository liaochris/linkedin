Doug Pickering’s Summary

Analyst and developer experienced in finding ways to allow systems to interoperate to deliver a complete solution. Skilled in data extraction, cleansing and transformation on a wide range of operating systems, database and products.

Most of my positions are obtained by recommendation. Recommendations are often provided by suppliers to their customers when they see a need for advanced data or problem solving.

I enjoy solving challenging problems and pride myself in trying to reach the right balance in any work I take on.

  • Data Consultant

    Regenda

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Government Administration industry)

    November 2005 — November 2006 (1 year 1 month)

    This project involved extracting housing data to transform and cleanse prior to loading into Orchard ArcHouse.

    I was asked to manage the data extraction of housing data from 5 separate Housing Systems including HAMIS, SDM, Simdell and Comino Context. I developed a process to allow the data to be transformed according to rules that Regenda specified. These rules were then tested and the results loaded on a nightly basis into a test system to generate a model that could be user tested. Any corrections were fed back into the rules system to be re-tested the following day.

    The method developed allowed the successful implementation of Orchard ArcHouse taking data from 5 different organisations in a programme of work in a very short timescale.

    The Regenda Group includes Maritime Housing Association, West Pennine Housing Association (WPHA), Templar Housing Association, Limehouse Village Trust (LVT) and Wyre Housing Association.
