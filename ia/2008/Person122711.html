Mark Taylor’s Summary

With degrees in business and economics, Mark spent his early career in analytic roles with blue chip companies including Guinness and Hanson PLC, before switching focus to entrepreneurs and SMEs. In 2001 he was appointed Business Start Up Manager for Glasgow Business Services, responsible for the recruitment and development of business advisors across the city. In combination with MBA studies, Mark developed specialisms in Making Strategy for Entrepreneurs and SMEs and business modelling using spreadsheet simulations. He is now a full-time director of Eriskay Associates.
