Christopher Noe’s Summary

I currently serve as the Director of Development for Old School Square, a historic cultural arts center in downtown Delray Beach, Florida while I am also finishing my Ph.D. in Organizational Leadership. I have served as the director of development since October 1, 2007. Prior to this position, I served as the Executive Director for Suncoast Earth Force, an environmental awareness youth non profit organization in Tampa, FL. In addition, I consult other non profits across Florida with some of their strategic and development objectives. I have consulted up-starts in Texas and a non profit up-start organization in Tampa, FL.

I have a BA in Political Science from Maryville College in Maryville, TN, and an MBA in Non Profit Management and Public Administration from Argosy University in Tampa, FL. I am currently in a Ph.D. program focusing on non profit leadership and strategic management at Lynn University in Boca Raton, FL.

It is my goal to continue working and consulting within the non profit arena following my Ph.D. studies working with the up-start non profits as well as the giant non profits in areas of strategy mapping, development goal initiatives, and managerial issues such as rapid change management, innovation, adding value to existing programs, and leveraging existing metrics to find value-add within current paradigms that attracts new investors and funders and sustains those non profits.

Christopher Noe’s Specialties:

Legislative experience and expertise in non profit and social sector advocacy work. Managing political campaigns. Developing strategies to recruit and retain would-be funders to non profit causes. Elevating charities to new heights in their planning, board development, governance issues, and staff management.
