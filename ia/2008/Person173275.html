Seth Schachner’s Summary

Seth Schachner is Vice President, Digital Business - Latin America & US Latin for Sony BMG Music. Now in his ninth year with SonyBMG, his primary responsibilities involve driving new digital business and strategic marketing deals in Latin America and the US Latin market. Schachner joined SBMG in 1999 as Jive Record's VP of New Media.

Schachner is an executive with over seventeen years of senior-level experience spanning companies like Liberty Digital, Viacom, Universal Music, and 20th Century Fox. Schachner was one of AOL's first business development executives in the mid-1990s.

He earned BA and MBA degrees from Columbia University in New York City and has also completed courses in Madrid, Spain.
