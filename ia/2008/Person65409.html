Frank Connolly’s Summary

At AdMedia, I develop client relationships (digital marketing and market research focus) and provide M&A advice during the sale process. Seasoned executive with both public and private company experience in marketing and information industries. Areas of expertise include strategic and financial planning, operations analysis, mergers and acquisitions, public equity offerings, private equity and debt fundraising, leveraged buyouts, revenue and cost rationalization, investor relations, technology investments, financial systems, administrative management, SEC reporting and accounting controls (including Sarbanes-Oxley). Experience in private equity investing as part of boutique firm focused on media, marketing and information businesses. International experience includes Europe, Asia and South America. Track record of hands-on, measurable results.
