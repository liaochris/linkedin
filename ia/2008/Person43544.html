Daniel Haas’s Summary

"The Church's commission, which is the foundation of its freedom, consists in this: in Christ's stead, and so in the service of his own Word and work, to deliver all people, through preaching and sacrament, the message of the free grace of God." (Barmen Declaration, thesis 6)

As minister of the church it is my first and highest duty to deliver God's Word. As a communication professional I know that there are different ways of addressing different people. Because every congregation consists of people with different backgrounds pastoral calls have to meet people's needs. I have learned to act accordingly in all fields of pastoral work.
