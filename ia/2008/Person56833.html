Edward Thomas’s Summary

Graduate student in chemical engineering with extensive background in business journalism. Owner/proprietor of consulting business that provides numeracy training (and advice) for journalists.

(Skills listed below)

PROCESS ENGINEERING: Experienced with mass/energy balances, dynamic modeling, separations, thermodynamics, coupled transport phenomena, process control, statistics, P&ID diagrams and economic analysis.
ENGINEERING DESIGN: Experienced in use of QFD, morphological matrices, TRIZ, WDM, FMEA, DFM, DFA and other tools for design, prototyping and evaluation of engineered artifacts and processes.
TECHNICAL SOFTWARE: Experienced user of process simulation software (PRO/II), mathematical modeling (MATLAB/Simulink, Maple, JMP), spreadsheets (Excel), CAD (Solid Edge, AutoCAD), quantum calculations (Gaussian). Computer code-writing (FORTRAN, Java).
CHEMISTRY: Trained and experienced with analytical chemistry using data from stoichiometric methods, mass spectrometry, NMR, gas chromatography, HPLC, FTIR, Laue photography and potentiometry. Trained in synthetic organic chemistry. Basic training in quantum/theoretical chemistry. Familiar with basic principles of nuclear power engineering and radiochemistry.
MEDIA: Extensive background in commercial writing and photography with a concentration in business, science and technology. Background in public speaking and broadcasting. Experienced desktop publisher using Word, Adobe software suite and QuarkXPress.
LEADERSHIP: Three years of full-time managerial responsibilities for hiring, training and administration in a publishing environment. Formal training and experience in labour negotiations and conflict resolution. Four years of university design-team involvement in managerial and non-managerial roles.

Edward Thomas’s Specialties:

ENGINEERING: Process control, computational fluid dynamics and modelling, computer programming (FORTRAN and Java), statistics, design engineering and analytical chemistry.
MEDIA: Specializing in business & technology journalism, copy editing and design.
