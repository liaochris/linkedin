Jonathan Ziegler [LION] SF Bay Area’s Summary

I joined Trilliant Networks as their Vice President of Sales. The industry is evolving and there are opportunities all over North America and worldwide for our solutions. We are seeing "Green" come from everywhere!

Trilliant Networks, Inc. provides wireless network solutions and software for advanced metering infrastructure, demand response, and grid management applications. With more than 22 years experience solving meter communications needs, the company is focused on the adoption of open standards-based technologies for electric, gas, and water utilities.

The company's advanced metering infrastructure (AMI) solutions enable utilities to develop time-of-use (TOU) metering and demand response programs. These programs are transforming the traditional customer-utility relationship through interval based consumption data and 2-way messaging. Trilliant Networks solutions are also used by utilities to drive down operational costs and improve customer satisfaction through much improved awareness and data on distribution system performance.

Trilliant Networks has secured contracts for more than 3.0 million meters to be supported by its network solutions. Currently the company has over 150 utility customers including Baltimore Gas & Electric, Duke Energy, Hydro One, Hydro Quebec, Milton Hydro, Northeast Utilities, OneOK, Public Service Electric & Gas and San Diego Gas & Electric.

I strive to serve the client and the company's best interests. I am a skilled listener and explore the needs and goals of my client before I ever recommend or suggest any solution. Personal wins are important, and the client needs to have them before anything else.

Personal goals: To achieve success both in my relationships and financially and to know that if I need to call someone for help, they will be there for me, like I will be there for them. More than the Golden Rule, it's the Platinum Rule - Do something for someone else first, and they will help you in return.
