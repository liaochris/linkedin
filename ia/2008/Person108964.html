Kristi Zilliken’s Summary

With over 11 years in the recruiting field I've worked in both agency and corporate environments and have placed professionals at all levels: Executive to individual contributor. I bring a strong focus on direct sourcing of passive candidates.

I am a hands-on production recruiter. Even when managing a team I have always carried a production load. I love networking and being involved in the candidate management process. I believe that proactive talent pipeline building is the key to success as a recruiter. Targeted, focused searches are my specialty. Getting that passive candidate to consider new opportunities is what drives me.

My latest focus has been on senior and executive level searches. I've worked across the board recruiting professionals in all disciplines, including technology, finance, business development, marketing, client services, training and development, etc.
