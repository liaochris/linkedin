Adam Simms’s Summary

Design is my life, both professional and personal. I have a strong appreciation for minimalism and extreme use of monochrome. My philosophy for design is simplicity... "a designer knows that he has reached perfection not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to take away." –Antoniè de Saint-Exupéry.

I currently work for Xsilva Systems Inc. a Macintosh software development startup that focuses on the creation of LightSpeed, a retail Point of Sale solution. My role as designer involves branding, user interface (UI), print, and web design/development.
