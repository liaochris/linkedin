Mitchell Gilbert’s Summary

My professional career is diverse. I have over 24 years of experience in various business roles and 16 years in media. I am a professional with business acumen and success in emerging media, television production, post production, technology & start-up operations. I am currently the President of Optimize Inc. Optimize is a consulting company focused on digital technology, infrastructure and marketing in the field of converging media and Web 3.0. Our mission is to “Optimize" business's results utilizing new media platforms and methodology, creating possibilities that translate straight to the bottom line.

Prior to optimize I was the president of IDC digital. IDC's business is in video duplication, encoding, transcoding, digital delivery, asset management and editing (old and new media). While at IDC I focused on business development and reorganizing for efficiency. My experience in media and information technology enabled me to drive the company successfully to become one of the leading post production firms in New York.

Prior to IDC I created a media company with a unique approach that merged video production & marketing. This concept enabled businesses to market themselves beyond traditional advertising with an innovative “news magazine” TV show. This TV show approach enabled business leaders to showcase their organization in a “credible” format. The show aired on regional and national television. I started my career as a salesman for a garment importer cold calling accounts and was recognized as a top performer eventually selling major department stores and chains. I grew my relationships traveling extensively overseas as well as selling domestically.

I am motivated to expand my professional and business network by connecting with highly motivated professionals. I'm currently looking for new opportunities and to network with people in media/ new media, business development, management, and start up operations.
