Mark Hanna’s Summary

My experience in the logistics function of the chemical industry spans over twenty years. I have exposure to all five (5) modes of transportation. I have been responsible for international shipments and marine operations, terminal manager for our private fleet operation, supervised bulk and break-bulk packaging operations, arranged transportation procurement, and provided technical services in the customer service arena.

I do not want to limit myself to shippers. I have every confidence that I can bring a lot of talent to a carrier as well. My experience as a terminal manager for our private fleet operation and as the site truck coordinator monitoring the performance of our carriers gives me a vast working knowledge of carrier activities and organizations.

My people skills are evident in the ability to rise to the rank of Senior Master Sergeant (E-8) in the U.S. Air Force Reserves. I spent four (4) years on active duty in the Air Force and continued my military career as an active ready reservist until my retirement December 9, 2003. My entire Air Force career was in the air transportation career field.

Mark Hanna’s Specialties:

Freight bidding process, monitoring carrier's KPI's, coaching improvement in carrier's KPI's, streamlining traffic flow to average less than two (2) hours per load, safe handling and transportation of acrylic monomers and molten products.
