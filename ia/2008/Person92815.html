Jennifer Nichols’s Summary

I am currently a reporter/photographer working for a weekly newspaper, the Meadow Lake Progress, where I research, write and edit stories, do interviews and take photographs. I also do layout and update the website.

I also have experience writing for a twice-weekly community newspaper, a Canadian online music magazine, Soul Shine, and NovaNewsNet, an online magazine and news digest. Most recently, I have begun writing for GKFA.com, a women's online magazine.

For NovaNewsNet, I wrote, posted and updated feature stories, and wrote news summaries that were sent to over 400 subscribers a day.

I have also worked as an intern for CBC online Nova Scotia, and the weekly independent newspaper The Coast, writing and editing news articles.

I have some experience with photography, both digital and 35 mm SLR. I am interested in writing for any medium, but my main interets lie in magazine or online.
