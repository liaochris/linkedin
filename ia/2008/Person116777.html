Luis Henrique Martinez, Ph.D., CEM’s Summary

A Project Development and Sustainability Consultant.

Expertise in transforming complex development projects and project turnarounds into successful outcomes.

Over twenty years of business experience, combined with strong educational background in construction management and business administration. This results in a uniquely effective combination of international business development and project management skills.

I have worked in the energy, oil, and construction industries, and managed the acquisition, construction, completion, operation, and sale of several large developments in Latin America: multiple resorts and hotels in Mexico and Venezuela; power plants in Panamá, Perú, El Salvador, and México; and a number of high rise commercial and multifamily construction projects (http://www.luishenriquemartinez.com)

My educational background includes a Ph.D. in Construction Management from Purdue University as well as a Certified Energy Manager (CEM) from the Association of Energy Engineers.

Founded a firm (PERT-CPM, LLC) specializing in Project Development and Sustainability Consulting. Services include: (http://www.pert-cpm.com)

► Sustainability Consulting: Guidance through the “green” certification process and/or consulting on energy reduction costs. Leveraging our expertise in construction and project management to select and manage the optimal sustainability option for a project.

► Project Turnarounds: Managing at-risk projects to achieve the optimal outcome for the client. This include real estate developers and investors, energy utilities and developers, financial and insurance institutions (banks, insurance companies with real estate investments, etc.)

► Real Estate Investment Analysis and Consulting: Analyses, investment, and management of real estate properties for clients from both the US and Latin America.
