Michele Magazine’s Summary

In addition to specializing in senior level searches in the magazine and newspaper industries, I also offer expertise in finding talent in the conference area. Stephen-Bradford Search partners with established and emerging companies who are looking to hire the best senior talent possible. Previous to executive recruiting, I had a 25 year career in advertising sales and management in magazines, newspapers and public TV.
