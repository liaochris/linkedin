Pratheesh Nair’s Summary

I always wanted to be a pilot in my life. Though life took a different route but the urge to become a pilot remained and perhaps that’s what drives me in life. I was a part of the pilot batch in my school in 4th and since then it has been a plethora of pilot projects which has helped me mold myself by taking the lead on things closest to my heart.

My professional life started in college itself where I got an opportunity to give shape to a department in a banquet hall company as an Event Coordinator. I discovered very early in my career that I have a flair for marketing and words and decided to capitalize on the same. Since then it has been a endeavor to absorb the most from what life can teach me.

I was a part of many pilot projects thanks to my professional career stretching across different verticals and industries including BPO, KPO, eLearning, Online Marketing, Content Development, IT and Software consulting.
I am a student of life and willing to learn, relearn and learn yet again to inculcate the best practices that can catapult the vision I have for – 3volutions (pronounced Evolutions) as well as Bluesoft Solutions; my twin babies.

I love to read books, besides being a big movie buff. I also love to write and am in the process of writing a collection of short stories which I hope to publish some day.
