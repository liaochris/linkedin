Robert Bylett’s Summary

Robert is a Business Strategy Coach and Consultant. He works with entrepreneurs and business managers who currently have - or will soon have - responsibility for managing a hospitality business…and in particular those who want to build a sustainable business.

Robert founded Just Consultancy in 1998 and now leads a dynamic team of specialists who support both individuals and teams to explore possibilities, make choices and to create strategies that enable them to focus energy, optimise performance, and achieve their goals faster and easier maximising return on investment.

Clients in the main come from the hospitality industry, but they also coach other professions. Their common bond, though, is this: they want to build a sustainable business: one that contributes to community and environment as well as improves the bottom line and are all committed to their own success and are willing to put in the time and effort to achieve it.

Robert and the team offer a range of programmes and services - from troubleshooting to improvement programmes and strategic planning, they specialize in creating sustainable business solutions that create value in a highly competitive marketplace.

Clients who want to improve their business and contribution to their business, community and environment appreciate their refreshingly supportive, empowering approach.

To know where to begin, you need to gain distance and objectivity. Moreover, if you are facing difficulties, an outside perspective is crucial to overcoming your challenges and reaching your goals.

Robert’s step-by-step approach helps you analyse your business from all angles - quickly, efficiently, thoroughly, and cost-effectively.

His approach is straightforward, practical, and easy to understand. It allows you to act fast and make informed decisions that will improve your business.
