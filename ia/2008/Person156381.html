Ram Giri Braun, Ph.D.’s Summary

I offer the "Skills for Awakening," as an experienced coach, mentor and spiritual friend. If you are serious about change, the skills can be invaluable.

This revolutionary approach to Spiritual Awakening and Peace of Mind enables you to quickly overcome stress, worry, anxiety, depression, relationship conflicts, addictions and more. This approach is so ground-breaking and valuable because it permanently dissolves the root-causes of all stress and empowers the heart.

I developed the "Skills for Awakening" over 40 years of international study and practice in the spiritual traditions and psychology. They are most beneficial for those who are highly motivated to find a quality of life that is rare in today's world -- a clear and peaceful mind, an open, joyful heart, and lasting emotional harmony.

I also offer corporate training to conscious businesses in higher awareness skills: the next level of conflict resolution; master strategies to create a culture of cooperation and high level interpersonal skills. In other words: "Success beyond Stress."
