Tushar Bhatia’s Summary

Experience of 15 years in the software industry as Architect, Designer, Integration Specialist.

Running a successful software products and services company Saigun Technologies since 2001 out of Noida.

Tushar is driven in helping improve the profitability of client organizations through innovative use of technology and a focus on developing talent. With a significant part of his career in the area of developing HR automation software, Tushar is a founder of one of the most innovative HR platform in the world: EmpXtrack.

EmpXtrack (http://www.empxtrack.com) is a full featured web-based product that is available as a SaaS service. It contains 18 modules in four different categories viz Performance Management, Human Capital Management, Strategic HR and Recruitment. The uniqueness of the product is its capability to solve a niche problem by the use of one or two modules and then addressing the bigger business performance management issues being faced by most organizations.

Different aspects of HR are integrated to each other and tied through Job Descriptions, Competencies and Skills and EmpXtrack provides a platform through its 18 modules to link data from one module to another module to allow quick decision support.

EmpXtrack has clients across the world in multiple verticals and Tushar is currently focused on selling the product in multiple-geographies.
