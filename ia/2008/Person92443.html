Josh Markiewicz’s Summary

Continuing to enjoy my time in the computer games industry as a programmer, advancing graphics and gameplay using proven as well as new ideas.

I've been working in the field of computer graphics for almost 10 years now, starting in flight simulation at Evans & Sutherland. Since then, I have worked on several console titles for the PS2, Gamecube, and Xbox. I continue to develop my skills on various game projects.

I'm currently helping with gameplay programming on Unreal Tournament 3.
