Combining proven professional career development techniques withh success as an entrepreneur Carl E. Reid founded Savvy Intrapreneur, which teaches "career minded" professionals how to run themselves like a business. He has used his life experience and knowledge gained to assist others in achieving their own desired level of success.

Carl E. Reid knows what it takes to be successful. Using survival skills learned on the mean streets of the Edenwald projects in the Bronx, Carl evolved from being a temporary worker in a mailroom to becoming CO-Founder, CEO & President of a successful technology consulting firm NetTECH Systems Reid & Associates, Inc.. Carl combined his "must do" attitude and being a student of other successful mentors to climb the corporate ladder to success.

In addition to hosting WIIFM Success Radio and being a published author, C.E. Reid advises small businesses on Internet Business-2-Business opportunities. Combining 36 years of business experience with 22 years in the Information Technology field, Mr. Reid has a proven track record in identifying emerging technology business opportunities, before they become trends. He has also helped many people successfully advance their careers and start businesses with 15 years as a part time Business & Career Coach. Some of Carl's consulting clients include various non-profits, including New York City Health & Hospitals, IBM/Lotus, JPMChase and Citicorp.

The best time to hire a Career or Business Coach, is when things are going well. A Coach can help with tactical strategies for mantaining your momentum. S/He can help avoid or minimize those career or business land mines that surround us everyday. http://www.SavvyIntrapreneur.com
