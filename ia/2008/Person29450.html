Abbas Zaidi’s Summary

Technical Writer/Trainer/Developer, with diverse skills honed in a Database Consulting background in niche industries for a decade. Abbas has gained his Business Transformation edge through years of tactical experience in Information Management and Business Process Engineering. Information Management Consulting on Document Management Systems and Workflow Management Systems like SpyderWeb (by BTG at the GOMO, Pentagon,) and NovaManage (worldwide) through the mid 90's enabled Abbas to gain an advanced perspective of the field years before Documentum and Hummingbird defined an entire genre. Strategic vision is his specialty, as well as his passion. Abbas likes to examine an industry from a variety of perspectives, and using proven communications skills, builds bridges where none exist while gaining precious insight few can claim. This has proven to be the case time and again, and is the reason why Abbas finds great friendships with previous colleagues, even where he may not have maintained contact for years.

Abbas Zaidi’s Specialties:

Technical Documentation and Training Development. Strategic Business Transformation and Tactical Business Process Engineering, using available as well as newly developed applications of proven technologies.
