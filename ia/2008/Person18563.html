Avinash Singh .’s Summary

Avinash has almost two decades of rich experience in the areas of Software Consulting, Offshore Outsourcing and Professional Services.

He has over the years developed and honed skills in General Management, Sales Leadership, Strategy Development, Change Management and Leadership, Negotiation, Branding and Marketing, Human Relations and People Management as well as managing complex Global P&Ls.

Avinash is a founding member of Birlasoft and was responsible for the initial setup of the company. As Chief Operating Officer, Avinash is responsible for global operations across the USA, Europe, APAC, Japan and Middle East markets.

He is a dynamic and passionate leader who has led the organization through various transformations to what is today a respected Global Solutions company and is part of the Nasscom Top 20.

He is responsible for acquiring a number of significant customers including GE – a contract which has accrued over US$ 300M over the last 5 years and is expected to add another 200M in the next 2 years of the existing contract tenure.

As the Marketing Leader he has crafted and led various branding initiatives which resulted in positioning Birlasoft favorably in the Gartner Magic Quadrant.

He has an entrepreneurial management style and is a strategic thinker with the ability to take a vision from concept/strategy to reality through focused Execution.

An extremely goal oriented individual with versatile skills and interests, his strengths are his ability to build and lead high performance teams which work cohesively together to deliver exceptional results. He leads from the front and his teams down the line make a strong personal connection and commitment to him. He is consultative and believes in empowering with authority and accountability.

He has experience dealing with C Level and Board Executives of Fortune 500 type companies globally.
