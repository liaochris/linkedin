Anthony Milano’s Summary

Founder and executive officer who built, grew and sold two successful companies to venture backed, Pre-IPO companies. Nearly twenty years in analytics, market research, business intelligence, benchmarking, and software development. Experience includes executive leadership of both small and mid-sized companies, technical experience in analytics, and work in management consulting from McKinsey & Company.

Anthony Milano’s Specialties:

Appian Analytics, Inc. was acquired by Global Market Insite (GMI) in 2006. Now a business unit of GMI, Appian provides services in: analytics, survey research, data processing and automation services. We help support the market research industry with business intelligence, decision theory and management science.

Clients of Appian Analytics included: Dow Jones, Bain & Company, JD Powers & Associates, Washington Mutual Bank, PC World, The Gap, and many more.
