  • Principal Engineer, Platform Product Manager, Chief of Security

    E2open, Inc.

    (Computer Software industry)

    2002 — Present (6 years)

    As a Principal Engineer, I am responsible for selecting how the parts of the product will come together to deliver the E2open service. Consideration is given to both technology as well as the impact to maintaining the resulting product in a 24x7 service environment.

    As the Platform Product Manager, I am responsible prioritizing features and specifying the product roadmap for the E2open Integration Platform, including B2B protocols, service interfaces, customer self management, and operations.

    As the Chief of Security, I am responsible for specifying security policies and product security capabilities, defining and measuring operations/product security, communicating security posture to customers.

  • Director of Security Engineering and Operations

    Slam Dunk Networks

    (Computer Software industry)

    1999 — 2002 (3 years)

    Created and led a 5 member security engineering team, responsible for designing and implementing PKI based security infrastructure. Coupled mutually authenticated secure pipes with tightly coupled message security to enforce strict customer policies with end-to-end message authentication and end-to-end message privacy in an n-tier architecture. Supported identity life cycle management with transparent renewal and OCSP/CRL hybrid revocation.

    Led the 5 member information security team responsible for defining and implementing electronic and physical security, security policies, and overall security posture. Resulting security passed without exception American Express, Visa, and Sabre technical due diligence for a secure network service provider.

    Responsible for all security presentations to customers, technical due diligence reports, responses to requests for proposals, analysis of customer requirements, and international issues regarding cryptographic export.

  • Security Architect

    Borland

    (Computer Software industry)

    1997 — 1999 (2 years)

    Managed 7 member security team consisting of engineering, marketing, documentation, and QA. Led implementation effort, prioritized, coordinated, and assigned tasks to team members.

    Negotiated and reviewed technology licenses from partner companies. Managed customer audits and cryptographic export reviews by US Department of Commerce.

    Presented security architecture and vision to peers, executive staff, sales force, product management and customers through presentations, site visits, and conference speaking engagements.

  • Principal Member of Technical Staff

    Oracle

    (Computer Software industry)

    1994 — 1997 (3 years)

    Managed a 4 member team responsible for the design and implementation of network security services for Oracle 7 and Oracle 8. Successfully delivered the Oracle Security Server and network authentication adapters by coordinating with the test lab, network integration team, kernel integration team, and product lines.

    Reviewed, prioritized, and assigned bugs to team. Improved processes and inter-team coordination through evaluation and post-mortems.

    Designed and implemented Oracle Security Server, Oracle Digital Shrinkwrap, Oracle Cryptographic Toolkit, Kerberos adapter for SQL*Net, and SQL*Net Identity Impersonation.
