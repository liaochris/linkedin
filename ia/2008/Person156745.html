Randy Olsen’s Summary

Founded the independant publishing company, Celebrity International Press, Inc., with a team of top literary industry professionals. Books that inspire and empower.

Has produced and designed Academy Award after parties, live shows, sporting events, television show launches, book launches, movie studio events and many other happenings over the past 20 years (started when 19 years old). Featured on TV shows such as the Food Network, PBS, Home and Garden, Lifetime, and more.

Good at creating a "buzz," as they say. That is to generate public enthusiasm, or passion, for projects. Which flows into conceiving and supporting the publicity process.

Excellent at finding, often hidden, extraordinary talent, craftsmen, or artists. Gifted in empowering and inspireing people to see and attain their full potential and use those gifts to touch lives and hearts. Motivating with love, understanding, and zeal.
