lauren sheehan’s Summary

I have almost 3 years experience training people of all ages and of many goals. I have had the privilege of training people with goals such as weight loss, blood pressure reduction, 5 and 10K races, hiking a fourteener, increasing core function and stability, decreasing muscle and joint pain, decreasing stress, learning new exercises, increasing range of motion, and being able to get up out of a chair without pain. I consider myself a "hollistic" trainer meaning, I have a more wellness based philosophy of fitness and health and I am interested in educating my clients on how they can train their bodies for optimal function so that, for example, they can live into their 90s and still be able to live independently.

My Goals are:
To provide the most creative, innovative, and fun approach to fitness.

To educate you about how your body works and how you can keep it healthy and functioning throughout your life.

To educate you on all aspects of fitness and wellness and allow you to fully participate in your success.

lauren sheehan’s Specialties:

Corrective exercise (specifically lumbopelvic stabilization), circuit and bootcamp training, functional training, showing you how hard you need to work when you workout, core training, making exercise fun!

My style of training is based on how your body functions and how we can train you in all aspects of fitness so that you can be better in your daily life. We will work on improving balance, stability, strength, endurance, agility, and flexibility.
