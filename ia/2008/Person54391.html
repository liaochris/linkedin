Drew Campbell’s Summary

I have been working since 2005 as the manager of the User Experience Technical Group which builds the front end to all Group Health's web sites. We work using an Agile methodology and my staff work on small cross functional teams. Planning work, setting standards, code reviews, and keeping staff engaged and growing in their career are part of my job function. I have been involved in web architecture planning, creating standards for measuring work capacity, diagnosing web issues, and planning for projects.

I have worked in the internet industry since 1995 building simple html sites for a consulting company (Product Development Systems). For Group Health I have worked as a front end web developer, administering and managing the Content Management system and integrating application code into content pages and templates. In 2005 was promoted to the group's manager. Every year our sites win awards due to what we're giving our members through the internet.

I have worked as a design engineer for a manufacturing company, designing mixing equipment as well as their sales and marketing materials. I introduced this company to computers in 1990 and computer aided design to help remove human error from occurring in the engineering and design process. I also used the same computer drawings for their marketing and sales brochures which brought them to a new level of professionalism. I also reviewed contracts and helped negotiate them with vendors and purchasers.

My goal is always to help an organization become the best it can be using my unique skill sets that start with my Industrial Design degree where art and engineering are blended to meet the user's needs in an elegant solution for both the user and the manufacturer.

agile development methodologies, project management, xhtml, xml, perl, unix, and css code development, search engine administration and configuration, content management system administration and configuration, database development, design and usability, server architecture; mechanical engineering, drafting, and cad/cam; contract negotiation.
