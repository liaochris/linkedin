Philippe Vallee’s Experience

    CommValley (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    New technologies are seeing the light faster and faster. Most of the time they seem promising but some -even praised by some communities - finally end up no where.
    In this global environment where things change so fast, how should one approach the use of such new technologies?
    I believe the right question for a marketing manager is: "is there a benefit for my customers?"
    Technologies should be approached as part of a global plan of communicating with an audience, not as the solution to a given problem. Technologies should be selected because they respond to a need or bring value to a strategy and then implemented as tools, not as an objective.
