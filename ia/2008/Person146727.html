  • CEO

    Newport City Renaissance Corporation |

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry)

    August 2007 — Present (9 months)

    CEO of the nonprofit public benefit corporation that manages economic and community development projects benefiting the Newport City downtown area.

    The purpose of the Newport City Renaissance Corporation is to enhance the quality of life for people who live, work and visit Newport City helping the downtown develop as a vital and diverse community center for retailing, cultural and entertainment activities, education, recreation, business and professional services, dining, government and residential use.

    Ms Sears testified as a witness before the U.S. Senate Committee on the Judiciary hearing, "Economic and Community Implications of Northern Border Security"; she was featured in Vermont Public Television documentary, "Good Fences, Good Neighbors," an examination of the close relationship between Vermont and Canada, especially Quebec, focusing on life along the border and the trade of hard goods, energy and tourism.
