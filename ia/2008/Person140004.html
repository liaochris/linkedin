Juan Rodriguez’s Summary

I performed practices in Petroleos del Peru en RELAPA, Refineria de la Pampilla, Ventanilla in 1981.This same year I get into a job in Liofilizadora del Pacifico freeze-drying S. R. L, being a factory of dehydration from vegetables and fruits and seafoods- toward USA, Germany and Japan. Thanks to this job as Chief of Engineering until 1999, I accomplished enough knowledge in Biology, Chemistry and Engineering since I've worked in areas of Maintenance, Quality Control, Production and Research & Development.

In the year 1992 I belonged to the team for the preparation of cat's claw extract both spray dried and freeze dried.A valuable and acknowledge resource for modulation of immunity in patients radiation suffered in disaster at Chernobyl, Ukrania. Furthermore, latter experiences have pointed out the reliability from cat's claw extract as agent adjuvant immune in the treatment of tumours.

Already in 1990 I took a chance in Laboratories Medico Biologicos H. Colichon S.A.C. for compilation of summaries and translation since english, french, german for spanish about both oral, and gut,and vaginal probiotics and its wealth in the immunity from both human and animal health.

I claim myself as author of the “Program of Proactive Maintenance of the Health” –Programa de Mantenimiento Proactivo de la Salud” and Author of the " Disc of the Health"-“Disco de la Salud” , “The biological significance of breastfeeding"-"La importancia biológica de la Lactancia materna" using as pivots the functional foods as valuable outils for struggle against malnutrition, children morbility and mortality.

Finally, I've felt this matter is so paramount for the prevention of degenerative diseases and for improve both the life span and quality of life.

Since 2002 I’ ve managed global business both produces and natural supplements as minerals, metals and no-metals too. Since Lima to Destiny as U. K., U.S.A.,Singapore, China, India, UAE.
