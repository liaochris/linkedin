Susan Bernstein’s Summary

Susan Bernstein supports people to get unstuck and set their dreams in motion. Susan can help you to leverage your portfolio of skills, talents, and abilities so that you increase your return on life energy. Reap the rewards of greater fulfillment and higher financial returns.

Susan cares deeply about helping people navigate life & career transitions. Her specialties are:

(1) MAKING A MAJOR CAREER TRANSITION (www.WorkFromWithin.com): Tired of a feeling burned out at work? Move beyond just "having a job." Create a livelihood and a lifestyle that truly fits you, for the long-run. Build the confidence to pursue what really matters to you.

Susan has international clients in the US, Canada, Europe and Asia. Over 95% of her clients work with her exclusively by phone. She offers a limited number of in-person sessions.

INTERESTED in a potential coaching relationship? Susan works with people for a minimum of 3 months. You get full support to get clear about their next career moves and develop the confidence to pursue your dreams. Real, lasting change takes time, and they are willing. Contact Susan for a FREE "get acquainted" session. Check it out at http://workfromwithin.com/assessment.php

(2) SUCCEEDING AT JOB SEARCH (www.JobSearchGym.com): Stay accountable and find a job that really fits you. Power up your job search, from the inside out, with support.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    September 2004 — Present (3 years 9 months)

The first group are people who are tired of what they've been doing. They want their next work to be more meaningful, fulfilling and energizing -- but they don't know yet what that is. For them, Work from Within (www.WorkFromWithin.com) coaching is appropriate. The second group are people who know what job they want. They want a structured program to help them manage their job search so they get work that truly fits. For them, the Job Search Gym (www.JobSearchGym.com) is the right resource.
