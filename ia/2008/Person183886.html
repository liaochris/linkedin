Susan Ippoliti’s Summary

Function5 Forensics provides an art of discovery solutions for your digital forensics and electronically stored information ("ESI") collections.

Our trained, certified forensic examiners are properly trained to face the demands associated with handling and analyzing electronic evidence on a daily basis. Function5 invests continually in the technical expertise of our examiners, assuring you access to cutting-edge technologies, superior methods and on-going support.

Our team of project managers will handle each and every matter from inception to completion. Function5 also offers paralegal and litigation support for attorneys who need assistance navigating through the E-Discovery process.

Many law firms and corporations are in desperate need of assistance when it comes to data management, document retention, e-discovery and computer forensics. We offer a wide range of services to assist companies and law firms nationwide. We believe firmly in the delivery of quality legal services. Part of providing those services is having a strong foundation and an experienced legal team. We are there to assist attorneys in delivering quality legal services to their valued clients.

Susan Ippoliti’s Specialties:

I have over 12 years of experience as a paralegal in the areas of: E-Discovery and Litigation Management, architectural design and construction malpractice, asbestos litigation, insurance defense, labor and employment litigation, medical malpractice, personal injury, products liability and trade secrets misappropriation and intellectual property litigation.
