José Mauro de Almeida’s Summary

Currently I am part, as researcher of a project called D-GLUCOSE (Directed-Green Liquor Utilization Consortium Operating for Savings in Energy ).
This project is supported by a pulp and paper company's consortium and by US government and aims to promote improvements in kraft or sulphate process performance in pulp production and save energy. I am involved also in biorefinery and bioenergy.
In the past I worked as director for a technical consultant and representative company, business's manager and technical and sale's adviser for chemical companies. As Forest Engineer I was manager for activities involving plantation, handling and harvest a lot of trees in a forest company in Brazil. All diferents companies were involved in forest or pulp and paper or wood area. I took place in assemble a big pulp mill. I worked for Purdue University and for USAID (United States Agency for International Development) in alternatives energy's sources in Dominican Republic. I made training courses for companies like Aracruz Celulose, Suzano Papel e Celulose, Ripasa, Lwarcel and ABTCP (Brazilian Technical Association of Pulp and Paper)in pulping, bleaching and scale control.

José Mauro de Almeida’s Specialties:

I am a person who has a general and strategic overview of forest, pulp and paper area mainly in Brazil and South America. In spite of I am specialist in pulping yield, I know and have contact with different economics sectors including bioenergy, biorefinery, chemistry and agriculture.
