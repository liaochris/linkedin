Kirk A. Norris’s Summary

After graduating from Ohio State University's Fisher College of Business in June of 2006, with a Bachelor's in Marketing, I moved to metropolitan Chicago and began a career in residential real estate sales. Though the prospects of real estate were appealing, my goal is, and always has been, to find a place in the field of marketing or advertising. In September 2007, I started working at the Columbus Dispatch in the advertising department, and I am still currently employed there.

My mom once told me that, as a young child, she would put the TV on while I was playing. At most times I didn't pay attention, but she said I would always stop and look up at the commercials. So I guess it has been that long since I have wanted to work in advertising. The whole creative process thrills me. I always enjoy commercials, print, television, or otherwise, that are well thought out and capture me. Nike does some really great work....especially some of the commercials that were done during the last world cup. As a former rugby player, I also enjoy their "Keep the ball alive" commercial.
