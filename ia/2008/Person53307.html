Donna Iucolano’s Summary

At the forefront of innovation, e-business and business transformation since 1994. Currently applying a national, award-winning reputation and 20 years of experience as an e-commerce pioneer, a digital media executive, and a teacher through Spinach Candy, LLC. Spinach Candy is a strategic digital business / e-commerce consultancy and development company founded in 2006 to work with early stage, growing and well-established companies. Clients include: 1-800-FLOWERS.COM, Blue Tulip, Cranium, Fortunoff, Marc Ecko Enterprises, and Memorystone Publishing.

An entrepreneurial spirit, a passion for people and ideas, and an ability to engage others significantly contributed to the transformation of 3, New York City area companies: national floral and gift retailer, 1-800-FLOWERS.COM; the world’s largest children’s book publisher and global media company, Scholastic Corporation; and, a global specialty publisher and direct marketer, IMP/International Masters Publishers. Eleven (11) years with them generated combined cumulative revenues of $750 million through new initiatives, most of which continue to grow, and serve as a testament to strong foundation-building skills. Founded and led the Internet-based e-commerce efforts of 2 companies: 1-800-FLOWERS.COM and Scholastic – both consistently ranked in the “Top 50” by Internet Retailer.

Donna Iucolano’s Specialties:

Strategic advisor; digital strategy formation and planning; interim or acting senior executive; partnerships and alliances; market positioning / re-positioning; e-commerce platform reviews and selection; development activities (M&A support, interactive products, new customers, new channels, and new concepts); organizing for e-success (organizational structure, operating framework, recruiting, coaching and mentoring); writer and speaker.
