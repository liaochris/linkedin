Huub Koch | Zichtbare Zaken’s Summary

I live in Rotterdam, The Netherlands, Europe. I am a senior creative designer with more than 25 years of experience. Next to this I am a design entrepeneur since 2004 at my own company Zichtbare Zaken (Meaning: 'Visible Matters' and 'Visible Values').

My roots are in graphic and webdesign, multimedia, advertising, art, photography, stage design and writing. I love to blog and vlog and contributed several essays and articles to national and international books about Typography, Design Culture, The Music Industry and Videoblogging.

I love to work for all sorts of clients, but at my company Zichtbare Zaken I focus on Marketing and Communications for a variety of small business owners: Entrepeneurs, Consultants, Coaches, Surgeons, Therapists, Interior Designers, Artists, Booksellers, Actors, Lawyers, Filmmakers, Writers and other professionals.
