Annie Pane’s Summary

Annie's unique perspective helps individuals, businesses, builders, and developers recognize and understand the subtleties of environments at home, work, land sites or in floor plans. Whether office complexes, retail stores, subdivisions, or homes she applies intuitive design and Feng Shui principles to increase the overall success and profit for her client. Entrepreneurs especially benefit from her board base of knowledge and experise.

Annie Pane’s Specialties:

Intuitive design, home staging and design remix, organizing, de-cluttering, communications, sales, and entrepreneural coaching.

    (Privately Held; Myself Only; Writing and Editing industry)

    Annie has been featured on NPR, The Diane Rehm Show; FOX TV News; Voice of America TV; XM Satellite Radio, and in newspapers and magazines across the USA. After launching her company East Coast Feng Shui, LLC in 1998, she quickly rose to the top of her field for three reasons:

    First, she’s the only Feng Shui educator and advisor to help people learn this ancient science from a Christian worldview.

    Second, she showed enthusiasts how to reap the physical, mental AND spiritual benefits of Feng Shui. Another first!

    And, third, she intuitively meets people where they are. In other words, she modifies their physical environments to empower them in living a balanced life.

    Whatever they need is what Annie addresses…be it more money, love, happiness, health or getting rid of clutter. Receive two FREE chapters of her book by visiting www.anniepane.com
