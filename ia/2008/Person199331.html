Wendy Bronfin’s Summary

As Vice President of Product Development & Education at Smarterville Inc (Hooked on Phonics) I led a team of brilliant producers who shared my passion for developing educational programs that are fun for kids and get results. With more than 16 years experience creating programs for children, families and schools, domestically and abroad, I've worked across a spectrum of media including video games, web sites, interactive CD & DVDs, music, movies and television programming.

I began my career in video gaming making shoot 'em up and race car titles and quickly jumped to the education side of media where I developed some on the first web experiences for pre-teens including The Baby-sitter's Club. My passion for education led me to Children's Television Workshop where I produced dozens of software titles and interactive toys.
