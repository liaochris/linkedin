Mitch Matthews’s Summary

In regards to Coaching... I'm known as the "question guy.”

I love asking questions.

It’s a skill that I've used my entire life… but it really kicked in when I became a
certified business coach in 2001.

My “sweet spot” is working with biz leaders, entrepreneurs and sales people.

I believe in asking not telling, partnering not directing and sound principles not quick-fix advice.

I also love working with folks in a “quarter-life crisis.” They’re peeps wondering if they’re on the right path and not wanting to waste another minute of their lives! (If this you… you know what I mean!)

Yup. I love to ask questions that unlock purpose, passion and direction!

I have sat on the the Franklin Covey Coaching Innovation Advisory Board.

Other Experiences:

My love for asking questions combined with a passion for connecting people also led me to develop a question-based game series called “Q.”

The games have been sold throughout the world. To find out more…
you can check out doyouQ.com.

The passion for asking questions and for conneecting people also lead me to start BIGdreamgatherings. (www.BIGdreamgathering.com) These events have helped people to reawaken… reignite… and relaunch their BIG dreams.

I also spend a lot of time speaking to organizations

Lastly, my favorite and most important adventure is with my family. My wife Melissa and I have two boys that we are freakishly proud of! And although I am a dog person, our family currently has 2 cats, 4 fish and a snail named “Dangerous.” Go figure!
