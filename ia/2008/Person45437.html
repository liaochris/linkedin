Dave Delaney’s Summary

In the early 80's Dave Delaney operated a BBS on his Commodore 64. Late at night he would wake to the sound of his disk drive operating. Dave would jump out of bed, flick on his monitor, and switch into "chat mode" with whoever was online. His taste for online adventures began.

Years passed and Dave found himself studying Radio and Television Broadcasting and later, Business Marketing Management. He found a wonderful position as Marketing Manager for the Toronto production of (the Broadway musical) HAIRSPRAY. When the show closed, Dave moved on from working with the largest theater producers in Canada, to the largest newspaper in Canada, the Toronto Star.

Dave throughly enjoyed his time working as Promotions Executive with the Star, and once his contract ended he was grabbed by Global Television, and hired as Station Relations Manager in the Marketing department.

During his time with Global Television, Dave created a new process for communications between each of the affiliate stations across the country. He was brought on to the top secret rebranding project, that would turn to increase ratings and profit for the network.

In addition, Dave turned instantly to the online world where he is at his best. He helped redesign www.globaltv.com and manged the online marketing priority program information. He also proved very valuable as his colleagues turned to him for ideas on promotions both online and offline.

Today, Dave is more excited than ever about the future of social networking and online marketing. He hosts and produces the podcast, Two Boobs and a Baby+.

Dave now resides in Nashville, Tennessee with his family. He is thrilled to be working for Emma www.myemma.com, a stylish email marketing company. He recently was responsible for organizing Nashville's first BarCamp un-conference with Marcus Whitney www.barcampnashville.com and is currently organizing PodCamp Nashville www.podcampnashville.com.

Find Dave here: www.davemadethat.com
