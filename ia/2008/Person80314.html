Raymond Miller’s Summary

I am currently the President of a small California Corporation specializing in Private Investigations, Competitive / Business Intelligence and Training for various groups in the security / investigative / law enforcement industry.

I come to this corporation with over 7 years of law enforcement experience (4 years military and 3 years municipal), 9 years of military experience (5 years Aviation and 4 years Law Enforcement/Security), and 2 years of Executive Protection Experience (Working for various Security Providers including Andrews International and RJ Westmore).

I have worked various cases and through the variety of cases, I have found that there isn't one specific field that I want to specialize in with the exception of Family Law cases because you see the difference you make in a couple or child's life when you conduct that type of investigation.
