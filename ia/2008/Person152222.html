Patrick Murphy’s Summary

From Bank One to AOL and now my new venture, Brand Thunder, I've been consistently focused on developing channels for product sales and service to innovative product marketing and promotion. These channels include call centers, web sites, kiosks and direct point of sales contact. In addition, I have a true passion for technology and how it can enhance business operations and improve consumer's business interactions.

I'm an accomplished business professional with over 18 years of diversified marketing experience. Areas of leadership include strategic and management positions in e-Business, Marketing, Business Process Management, Information Technology, Insurance, Consumer Lending and Banking. A proven track record in meeting business objectives, translating business-to-technology, business development, project & product management, expanding business functionality and relationship management.

Patrick Murphy’s Specialties:

A common thread throughout my career has been alternative channel development and management. I'm as comfortable in the boardroom persuading executives on a technology solution as well the server room, motivating technical engineers to build that solution. I perform well as a business-to-technology translator.
