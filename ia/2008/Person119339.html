Marc Boschma’s Summary

Broad experience in business case development and managing the design and deployment of large scale IP convergent networks and related IT systems, content delivery infrastructure and technical strategy in support of business directions.

Successfully handed over the Strategic direction of Telstra's QoS enabled Broadband convergent network and systems to deployment vendors.

Previously experience includes large scale Cable and DSL ISP network and systems architecture; IN and Card Services delivery; mid-range, mainframe and network integration; UNIX and proprietary kernel development including TCP/IP stack; and graphics design.

Looking to continue to evolve my skills more in the Business and Product arenas leveraging my technical and commercial experience.
