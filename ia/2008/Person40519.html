Christopher Giordano’s Summary

I have worked from being an applications engineer, to a lead engineer and a program manager for 14 concurrent programs at my current company. The services department has since been spun into it's own sector at DiSTI and I now continue to manage the world wide software support and am the current Product Manager for all commercial off the shelf software products produced by DiSTI.
