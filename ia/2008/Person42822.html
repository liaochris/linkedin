Kate Wideman’s Summary

Hello, our names are Danny and Kate Wideman. Our main business is helping people get out of debt. We work for a company called U1st Financial, which is a very appropriate name. What we do is offer a software program that takes a snapshot of your finances and calculates, to the penny, the quickest way to get you to zero on all of your debts. It is basically like having a financial advisor at your fingertips that will tell you which way is the BEST way, with no emotion or opinion, to use your money.

At first, I'll admit, we were skeptical, since we have been in banking and finance for many many years. However, after taking a closer look, we could not believe what this program does, and how many people it can help. It basically turns the tables on the banks, eliminates debt in a fraction of the time, and you don't need anything but an income (must have more coming in than you have going out, even if it is just $1.00) a checking and savings account, and a credit card with a $300.00 limit.

This program was created by a mathematician, using calculus and complex algorithms. If one thinks they are smart enough to do these calculations on their own, we invite them to try, but even if you are smart enough, it will take you way too long to calculate what you should do, even for just one transaction.

This is not a bi-weekly program, or a refinance. Our goal is to help as many people as we possibly can, take advantage of this program, so that they can get out of debt, and start building their retirement, and achieve their financial goals in 1/3 to 1/2 the time.

We all work very hard for our money, why not make it work for you, in the best possible way?
