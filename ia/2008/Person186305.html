Ted Cohn’s Summary

Ted Cohn has over 20 years experience as a technologist, senior executive, engineer and entrepreneur. He has a passion for developing cutting edge products, services, and technologies, building collaborative teams, and delivering quality products on time, on budget. He has won numerous awards and has contributed to two patents with one pending and has filed several trademarks. He has taught graphics courses and written articles on algorithms and programming. His tech experience spans Internet, graphics, database, systems architecture and application development.

Ted developed the Apple II 6502 debugger (BugByter) in 1981. He has worked for a number of high profile Silicon Valley companies, including Apple, Radius, NeXT, Catapult Entertainment, WebCrawler/Excite, TenSquare, and Bridgespan. He developed one of the first image search engines called ImageCrawler and survey web apps called SurveySez.

Ted co-founded Ugobe Life Forms in 2004, which is slated to bring Pleo, an advanced animatronic dinosaur, to market in 2007. Ted developed Spautica in 2006, an innovative spa product to relieve stress and pain serving the trillion dollar health and wellness market. Ted presently serves on the advisory boards of Dreamslice and Embarka Digital Media and has most recently co-founded Talktiva, a Virtual Personality Network. He holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science from the University of California at Berkeley.

Ted Cohn’s Specialties:

Entrepreneur, technologist, software developer, algorithm development, Internet and graphics technologies, and system architect.
