Chuck Gose’s Summary

With MediaTile, I am responsible for establishing and developing relationships with digital signage content and technology partners.

Prior to joining MediaTile, I spent 10 years in corporate communications with the American Cancer Society, General Motors and, most recently, Rolls-Royce. With a focus on internal communications and employee enagement, I have developed strategies for delivering messages to internal audiences, with a special emphasis of using technology to communicate to “hard-to-reach” employees.

I am a long-time member of Public Relations Society of America (PRSA) and International Association of Business Communicators (IABC).

Chuck Gose’s Specialties:

Manufacturing communication, marketing communication, executive communication, crisis communication, employee engagement strategies, content management, digital signage (video bulletin board) solutions, internal branding efforts
