Tomas Krag’s Summary

Tomas Krag is a technology generalist with a focus on Internet technologies, low-cost wireless, Open standards, Free/Open Source Software and grassroots/community approaches to technology.

He is an experienced Enterprise Linux consultant, systems architect and trainer, with over 10 years of experience in various areas of the tech industry, including programming, systems and network architecture, database administration, management and mobile device specialist. He also has over 10 years of experience as a technology educator, and workshop facilitator, having been involved in technology training and workshop planning in 4 continents with participants from over 50 countries.

He is currently working as a freelance linux consultant with Casalogic and as a linux/open source trainer while continuing his effort to make open technologies more accessible in developing world projects.

An experienced speaker and facilitator, and a geek with a business school background, Tomas has the ability to talk about technology at many different levels; from discussing code with programmers, to laying out the business implications of technology choices to a non-technical audience.

He does what he does because it's loads of fun.
