Augustine Fou’s Summary

12+ years Internet experience, strategic consulting, and client service. Proven track record understanding client objectives, crafting strategies, and executing integrated, cross-channel marketing campaigns. In 2001, developed and executed the first-ever consumer sweepstakes to utilize virtual Visa numbers to instantly award cash prizes online to winners. In 2002, executed first-ever teen-targeted marketing campaign that fulfilled ringtones directly to winners cellphones as digital prizes.

Expert in data mining, analytics, and consumer insights research. Specific client experience includes serving Dr Pepper/Seven Up, KFC, Pepsi, Revlon, Atari, and Conde' Nast; specific knowledge in consumer payments, packaged goods, food/beverage, retail/apparel, and healthcare sectors. Served as expert witness for the Federal Reserve Bank of NY on Internet and alternative payment technologies.

Dr. Fou completed his PhD at MIT at the age of 23 and was hired into the New York Office of McKinsey & Company. He is an Adjunct Professor at NYU in the Integrated Marketing Department of the School for Continuing and Professional Studies. Dr. Fou, his wife Dr. Lisa Lin DDS, son Alex, and daughter Katerina call New York City home.

Augustine Fou’s Specialties:

consumer promotions utilizing debit/credit payment products, consumer insights research, datamining/analytics, strategy consulting, technology deployment project management, managing virtual and cross-functional teams
