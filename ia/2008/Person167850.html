Sameer Verma’s Summary

Dr. Sameer Verma is an associate professor of Information Systems at San Francisco State University. His research focuses on the diffusion and adoption of innovative technologies. He is currently working on several academic research projects which include the diffusion of open source software, mobility issues in wireless networks, identity management across disparate networks and communication modes in messaging systems. In addition to his academic work, Dr. Verma has worked with companies in the area of content analysis, management and delivery. Dr. Verma's latest professional involvement was with Airgram Networks, a network services company that focuses on providing access over localized wireless networks.

Sameer Verma’s Specialties:

Information Systems: Strategy, Policy and Governance.

Information Technology: Mobility, Identity management, Messaging, Dynamics of Open Source Software.
