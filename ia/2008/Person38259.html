Claudia Resta’s Summary

Journalist, TV author and anchorwoman and community manager with a broad experience in International Journalism, specialized in Travels, Entertainment, Women and Food. Several years of experience in web content and community management, two years working for a major Italian broadcasting group (SitCom S.p.A. - Sky Group). Recently hired as freelance by one the biggest publishers in Italy (Arnoldo Mondadori Editore S.p.A. - Panorama Travel).

Four years in professional TV documentary journalism corresponding from Italy and USA for both satellite channels Alice TV and Made in Italy Channel.

Eight years in professional radio journalism.

Expert in web creativity, online community generation and in the integration of the Internet with others media.

Trilingual: Italian, English and French. Highly motivated and focused, always interested in exploring new challenges in the means of communication.
