Timothy (Tim) Shannon’s Summary

I began working with computers as a hobbiest while still a student at Georgia Tech pursuing a degree in Industrial Design. Over the years I have become known as a problem solver and relish challenging problems.

My current customer profile is small to medium businesses. Too small to have in house IT and to large not to have it. Due to my diverse background, I offer a wide variety of services starting from physical network installation to management of users, systems, servers, routers and firewalls. Most of my clients appreciate the "One Stop Shop" aspect of my services.

Timothy (Tim) Shannon’s Specialties:

I specialize in the design and installations of new networks and systems, the management and maintenance of existing networks and systems and troubleshooting and repair of problematic networks and systems.
