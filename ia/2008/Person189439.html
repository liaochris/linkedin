As one of the few successful online video producers in the industry, measured by viewership and revenue generation, I have created online video properties that amassed over 30 million downloads which continue to grow at a rate of over a million views per month. I am at the forefront of the digital entertainment industry and frequently asked to speak at national conferences on digital media such as the National Association of Broadcasters and Digital Hollywood. Despite this success, I am limited by my available resources as an independent producer and would like work with different companies to move the digital entertainment industry forward.

That said, I am excited about exploring freelance digital entertainment jobs with companies as they pertain to online and mobile strategies.

As shown in my resume and on my demo reels, that can be seen at 1timstreet.com, I have a great deal of traditional broadcast production experience in interstitial short form programming as well as a writing, producing, directing and creative directing. In addition, I have experience budgeting, producing shoots, as well as running a multimillion dollar production and creative advertising firm and while overseeing employees both on and off the set.
