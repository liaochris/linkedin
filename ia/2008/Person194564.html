Vadym Utkin’s Summary

Mr. Utkin is an english-speaking Ukrainian, 32 years old, with over 11 years of relevant work experience, MBA graduated at Kyiv Mohyla Business School.

After obtaining his Master degree in engineering area, Mr. Utkin worked in a joint venture software company. In 1996, Mr. Utkin joined to consulting company as an ERP consultant. In Year 2000 he obtained regional director position. In October 2001 Mr. Utkin joined as a managing partner to ERP consulting company. In year 2008 he leads Ukrainian office of Sweden company - HansaWorld as a Country Manager.

His main areas of expertise include business development, work with corporate and key accounts, ERP project management.

He is a member of Ukrainian Association of Management Consultants (UAMC).
