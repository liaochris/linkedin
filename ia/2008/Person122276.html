Mark Marano’s Summary

One of my goals is to mentor as many other people as possible to create success in their lives. I believe everyone deserves prosperity and abundance. I help others realize their potential and achieve their dreams. I focus on guiding others through self-empowerment and personal development. I have many connections in the entrepreneurial and investment world that I can offer to anyone who's interested in learning the path to financial freedom and early retirement.

Another goal of mine is to create art centers, to serve as a supplement as well as an alternate to the academia. Through my business ventures, I am raising the necessary funds in order to open the art centers.
