Tamela Viglione’s Summary

Marketing Communications * Public Relations * Print & Web Content/Design * Identity/Branding * Writing/Editing * Strategy * Consultation * SEO/SEM

The Ilumina Group creates, launches, and delivers business and marketing communications solutions. We bring energy and acumen to every aspect of getting our clients' word out to the world whether via a single high-impact service or a powerfully scoped and fully integrated marketing communications strategy.

Tamela is an awarded business communicator, writer, and entrepreneur. She made her professional debut at age 18 with the publication of her first and national award-winning novel, Buck, and has been "wowing with words" ever since. An independent communications consultant since 1997, Tamela has forwarded her clients' growth and marketing goals for more than 17 years in roles such as vice president of communications, communications director, board member, communications and business manager, editor, speaker, author, and advisor.
