Daniel Hinojo’s Summary

I am a Software, Business Intelligence and Sharepoint Architect with a broad experience on different systems and platforms. Goal oriented and actively involved in all my projects and their complete development cycle, from the very early stages, with pre-sales and requirements meetings, going through different development and testing phases, until the last training and documentation has been provided, being an active part in all of them.

Projects I have been working on relate to Sharepoint deployments, customizations, integration, security, disaster recovery and training. Business Intelligence projects, Document Management, Dynamics CRM, Knowledge Management, Systems integration, KPI’s, Webparts development, .NET applications to integrate and answer business needs as well as other technologies like OLAP Cubes Dashboards, Silverlight Sharepoint webparts, RIA Silverlight applications and any other that might be a business requirement.
