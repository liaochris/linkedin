Alan Wolfe’s Summary

My goal is to become the best that I can be as a programmer and more specifically as a video game programmer. I love the wide range of challenges that comes with creating video games, and I love the creativity of creating games.

I have been programming most of my life and have been a hobbyist game programmer for just as long. I started just before junior high with qbasic, later going into C++ and Pascal, learning computer science topics in high school and putting those into practice over the next couple years as it brought my skills to a whole new level.

After high school I worked for 5 years in a small web software company called Current Solutions taking what I knew about programming and applying it to the real world. I gained many professional skills such as prioritizing, time management, making difficult decisions, and most importantly how to communicate with people in a professional setting. Since the company was small, the atmosphere was very much one of self employment so I learned what it is to have to get something done on a specific timeline no matter what (many late nights and early mornings) and also learned how to balance that with a personal life and still have time for friends and family.

Leaving there to explore other employment oportunities, I was hired by DC shoes as a senior systems analyst and after 6 months was offered a job doing game programming with inXile Entertainment and couldn't say no!

There I have resided ever since and things are going amazingly well.
