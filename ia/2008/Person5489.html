Alba Colitti’s Summary

Currently employed by a leading international consultancy, Alba has worked for various multinationals operating in EMEA on global initiatives. She is a regular speaker at seminars and symposia on Business Process Management with knowledge of the private and public sector. Alba has supported a number of transitions for process centralisation, harmonisation and outsourcing, some of which included an ERP migration or upgrade. Her expertise was put to use in successful realisation of Strategic Alliances and business readiness throughout EMEA for Acquisitions/Mergers and launch of new products/services. Engaged in the deployment of Lean Six Sigma in 3 major companies, she registered $ millions of financial savings on own projects, coaching over 20 green belts.
