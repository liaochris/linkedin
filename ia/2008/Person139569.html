Neil Morris’s Summary

Neil heads up F. Neil Morris, Inc., a boutique, retainer-based recruitment company. Our approach is predicated on the fact that the image an employer presents to a candidate, how well it distinguishes itself from other employers, and the quality of the candidate's recruiting experience are as important as job content and career prospects in competing successfully for superior talent.

We orchestrate the entire search process from beginning to end. We apply what we learn about the position our client wishes to fill, key stakeholders, our client's culture and their values to presenting a fully professional and thoroughly researched story to the market. We work closely with our client's people to ensure that their offering is competitive and that the necessary mechanisms are in place to build and maintain momentum from initial interview through reference checking and negotiation to extending the final offer.

In the 30 years we've been in business, we've replaced only one candidate for failure to perform within the first 12 months of employment. Many of our candidates have not only spent 10+ years with our clients but have developed and been promoted.

We pioneered Vested Self-Interest, a monthly focus group and think tank for IT managers. Topics included improving communication with business unit heads and senior management, women in business, stress management. We also introduced and implemented Functional Public Relations, an executive suite awareness programme for IT.

Our clients have included: Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce; Nabisco Brands; Nortel; Becton Dickinson; Campbell Soup; Reebok; North York Hydro; Ontario Ministry of Community and Social Services; Ontario Ministry of Correctional Services; Amdahl; Sperry Univac; Ainsworth Electric.

Neil holds a Bachelor of Science degree from McGill University in Montreal.
