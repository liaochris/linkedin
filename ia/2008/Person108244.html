Petro Koshukov’s Summary

A regional & local economic development, FDI promotion, and civil society development specialist with more than thirteen years of experience working on business, community, and democracy development projects in Ukraine, Moldova, and Belarus. Worked with Ukrainian national & local governments as well as international institutions to improve business environment, develop communities, and promote civic engagement in Ukraine and in the region. Experienced in public administration management issues with an emphasis on local economic development & investment promotion as well as government reform, anti-corruption programs, and public performance assessment and institutional quality measurement. Familiar with all aspects of project management. Speak fluent English and Russian and native Ukrainian.
