Todd Ramsey’s Summary

As a group we have over 50 years of combined technical expertise in every aspect of the automotive and consumer electronics sectors. Whether it be training, developing owner's manuals, or helping to engineer new products or techniques for installation of CE devices, our staff has extensive experience in presentation of concepts and ideas that improve the quality of product experience for the end user. We continually focus on delivering excellence to our clients while simultaneously maintaining an awareness that a profit oriented business model is a necessity for them. Thus, we try to focus less on the "hobby" aspects and more on the business aspects that maintain high standards and ensure a quality product experience.

We also work hands-on, actually installing consumer electronic products in homes and (primarily) in vehicles, which makes us somwehat unique in our field. Many consultants can talk or write about it, we can take it a step further and actually show you and your team HOW to do it, hands-on. We also write, engineer, illustrate, layout, and can publish all sorts of technical materials as our team has experience in graphic arts, AutoCAD, and all types of publishing software. We speak Quark, Adobe, PowerPoint, etc.

We believe that by creating and delivering outstanding technical communications along with related products & services, our clients will recommend us and continue to expand their relationship with RCGI. And this strategy has worked for us. We're very proud that we have quietly served the automotive and consumer electronics completely through word-of-mouth marketing since 1993 in more than 31 countries around the globe.

Visit us on the web at www.ramseyconsultinggroup.com.

Todd Ramsey’s Specialties:

Technical communication (both written and spoken), consumer electronics installation training, technical illustration, layout, and publishing, business development, engineering and product evaluation, supply chain development.
