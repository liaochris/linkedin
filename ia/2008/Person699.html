Dr. Ahmed Abisourour’s Summary

Having more than 30 years of international institutional experience (Boubyan Bank-Kuwait, BMI-Indonesia, IDB-Jeddah, BBI-Sarajevo, IMF-Washington, AMF-Abu Dhabi, BM-Rabat…) and field work experience in 34 countries. Acquired competency range spanning a variety of specialized fields including Islamic banking & finance, corporate strategies, investment & deal origination, investment portfolio management, Islamic treasury, exchange & trade relations, macroeconomic policies & country financial programming.

Holding senior managerial and professional positions as well as board memberships in selective banking institutions. Spearheaded the establishment of new Islamic financial institutions in Bosnia, Indonesia, Kuwait, Mauritania, Morocco, United Kingdom and other countries, the elaboration of Islamic business architecture & annual work plans and the creation of Islamic financial market infrastructure institutions (IIFM, IFSB...).

Undertaking the establishment of strategic investment vehicles namely: Marathon Group (for mega-deal origination and investment in Petroleum and Construction sectors), INMA Trading Networks (for Commodity trading & Project investment in OIC countries) and United Consultants International (for business networking and research & development)

Promoting cross-border investments and commodity trade in emerging markets. Promoting mega projects in real estate development, infrastructure projects and oil exploration & production.

Regular speaker at international Islamic conferences with numerous publications in the fields of Islamic banking strategies and emerging capital markets development.
