  • West Coast GM and Sr. VP, A&R

    Columbia Records

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Music industry)

    1995 — 2006 (11 years)

    Oversaw West Coast headquarters for #1 music label of Sony/BMG Music Entertainment. Signed and developed numerous multi-platinum and other artists for the label throughout 10 year career, including Train, Switchfoot, Aware Records (John Mayer, Five for Fighting), American Recordings (System of a Down, Johnny Cash), Brandi Carlile, Cake, Crazytown, the Ataris, Katy Perry, Sinead O'Connor, Tal Bachman, the Afghan Whigs/Twilight Singers and many others. Supervised recordings for Pete Yorn, the Offspring, Soul Asylum, Toad the Wet Sprocket, Sophie B. Hawkins, etc. Co-produced soundtracks for the movies 'Orange County' and 'I Know What You Did Last Summer.'

  • Vice President, A&R

    Capitol Records

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; EMI; Music industry)

    1987 — 1995 (8 years)

    Senior talent executive responsible for signing and developing numerous platinum selling, Grammy award winning artists including Bonnie Raitt, Blind Melon, Mazzy Star, Concrete Blonde, John Hiatt, Lloyd Cole and many more. A&R'ed multiple gold and platinum recordings for the Beastie Boys, Paul McCartney, the Butthole Surfers, Heart, the Beach Boys, Cocteau Twins, Eric Johnson and many others. Also produced numerous film and television soundtrack recordings for the label including for such award-winning films as Rainman, Clueless, Bull Durham, Moonstruck, the Addams Family, Tequila Sunrise, John Lennon: Imagine, the Indian Runner and dozens more.

  • Director of Artist Development

    MCA Records

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; V; Music industry)

    1984 — 1987 (3 years)

    Oversaw artist and career development for the label's roster of artists, which at the time included Tom Petty, Oingo Boingo, Charlie Sexton, the Pogues and others.

  • Product Manager

    Warner Bros. Records

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; WMG; Music industry)

    1978 — 1984 (6 years)

    Oversaw all aspects of marketing, advertising, promotion and publicity for large segment of label's artist roster. Created many first time Gold and Platinum successes. Client roster included: U2, Prince, Devo, Bob Marley, Public Image, Ltd., Gang of Four, Van Morrison, Steve Winwood, Thin Lizzy, Black Sabbath, Tom Verlaine, Robert Palmer, Grace Jones, Pat Metheny, Keith Jarrett, Jaco Pastorius, Little Feat and many others.

  • College Promotion Rep

    A&M Records

    (Entertainment industry)
