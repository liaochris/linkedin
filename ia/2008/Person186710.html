I am a senior recruiter of technology professionals within the greater Boston area, for Software Business Systems, an IT staffing and permanent placement firm, in business since 1977. I began as a recruiter in San Francisco, met my husband Ralf on a blind date (who owned Software Business Systems back here in Boston) and have been in the family business ever since. During my 20 plus years, I have also pursued personal fitness training, and life coaching, so I bring a whole life approach to my work.

We are truly a boutique, family business comprised of my husband, Ralf, my step-son Chris, and my daughter Alex (who is our virtual admin and researcher out of Brooklyn).
We live and work in Arlington and Edgartown (our home is on Martha's Vineyard) Massachusetts. There is no question that our success in both filling searches and assisting our candidates in finding great jobs, is all in the relationships.
