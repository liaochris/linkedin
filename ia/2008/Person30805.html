Carlos Ezquerro’s Summary

More than 12 years of experience in different management positions in Marketing and IT areas. In most cases in charge of national and international projects related with E-business, Internet Portals, CRM, Content Management Systems, Clasified Ads and Customer Care with a high technical component on each ones.

Senior expert in Call-Contact Centers and Mobile technologies (Wireless, SMS, MMS, GSM, UMTS, ...). during theses years, involved in the definition and developments of technical architectures and services using platforms based on Content Management, Web 2.0, WebTV, Mobile services and Web Portals.

Used to work in big international companies (VOCENTO, TELEFONICA, TERRA) being part of the Management team in the Corp. area and also used to work in business units with responsability on P&L statements always with a high capacity to lead teamworks.


Since 2005 Carlos has joined VOCENTOs Group in order to developed and lead On-Line Business within New Technologies Division. VOCENTO has 13 online local newspapers, 4 clasiffied sites (Autocasion.com, sacacasa.com, Infoempleo,com, tusanuncios.com), several social networks sites (dalealplay.com, ozu.es), 2 online national newspapers (www.abc.es, www.que.es), 7 vertical portals (hoycinema.com, finanzas.com, laguiaTV.com, hoytecnologia.com, hoysport.com, hoymujer.com, hoymotor.com) an e-commerce platfom and a lot of spanish web pages.
