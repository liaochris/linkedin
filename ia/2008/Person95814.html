John Wes Green’s Summary

Innovative leader who enhances shareholder value and customer satisfaction, with an emphasis on Relationship Marketing, eMarketing and Brand Management. Broad experience in the Pharmaceutical, Financial Services, Consumer Goods and Travel verticals. Concentration in Pharma product launch, growth and line extension efforts across the Metabolic Devices, Cardiovascular, Central Nervous System, and Pain Management therapeutic classes. Able to solve complex business issues to influence patients and win-over physicians by leveraging data driven insights while championing change by facilitating fluid and dynamic working relationships. Extensive experience in strategic planning, influencing investment, leading projects and managing vendors.

Pharma Brands invested aggressively in DTC advertising, (up to almost $4.8B in 2006) non-compliance remains an issue, costing the Pharma Industry an average of $150B per yr. Roughly 20% of all Rx's are never filled, 50% of all Rx’s are taken incorrectly & refill rates are disappointing across most of the therapeutic classes.

Organizationally, lines are drawn by product, not consumers. In addition, they typically develop Line Managers to be generalists. CRM efforts are usually outsourced to agencies, (which serves two masters thus efficiencies may or may not suffer). Relationship Marketing not well received because of long time horizon to see value, Sr. management typically demands immediate payback on programs, (some consider CRM a tactic or channel, not a business strategy).

Objective is to position myself logically in the Pharma arena to ensure that my next opportunity will be able to maximize & quickly benefit from my abilities. Proactively identifying the appropriate CRM/DTC career path by drawing upon colleagues & acquaintances experiences & insights inorder to confidently make an informed intelligent opportunity choice.
