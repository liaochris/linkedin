Adv. Saras Chettiar’s Summary

I started my legal career in 1995 serving articles at a company specialising in Labour law and business law. I have since been actively involved in litigation and advisory for business and labour law.I joined the Durban bar of Advocates where I wrote and passed my bar exams and subsequently joined the Pretoria Bar of Advocates. I have been actively involved in diversity management and gender mainstreaming /strategy since1988. I hold the following degrees :- BA(law),LLB,LLM (labour law and constitutional law)
During my career I have gained extensive practical experience in business and labour law , executive management,transformation initatives.
