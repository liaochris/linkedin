Karen Watts’s Summary

Born and raised in the Maryland/Virginia suburbs of Washington D.C., I studied dance and acting for most of my youth and teens.

In 1984, I moved to Los Angeles, where I embarked on my long career in advertising. 1991 marked the start of 15 ½ years’ work with the Kemps International Film Production Guide selling advertising, collecting data, and helping guide US producers in their worldwide search for production companies and services.

12 years ago, I opened up the Latin America territory for Kemps. Data was collected and marketing efforts were put into place to start educating the world about Latin America. Since that time, I have become acquainted, both personally and professionally, with the best companies and services in all the major countries and have traveled extensively throughout.

Since 1999, I have been the representative for Latin America for the Cannes Lions Dailies, the only official daily magazine at the prestigious Cannes Lions worldwide advertising festival, held each June. I have been responsible for editorial focus and marketing campaigns that highlight this area and I attend every year.

As a consultant for FilmBrazil in 2003, I organized a 3-day yacht event at Cannes Lions, educating festival attendees and promoting Brazil. That same year, I was invited by the Brazilian Minister of Culture's office to Brasilia, to assist in setting up a film commission and consult on their marketing efforts.

I have worked with the Assoc. of Film Commissions International, helping to further the formation of film commissions throughout South America. My expertise and reputation in the industry has made me a sought-after speaker, panelist, and writer. I was a speaker and panelist at a 2007 Location Expo seminar, been quoted in numerous industry articles about filming in Latin America and am a regular writer for Production Update. As a US citizen, I offer a unique perspective on the culture and diversity of the countries that comprise Latin America.

Karen Watts’s Specialties:

As a specialist in marketing and promotion, Karen has been at the forefront of the creative aspects of this region and has been instrumental in publicizing and promoting Latin America globally. Her knowledge and familiarity of the different country’s locations, is extensive.
