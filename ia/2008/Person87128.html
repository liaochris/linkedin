Jeff Beal’s Summary

Hardline360, a lifestyle marketing agency, specializes in live events and music entertainment. We merge traditional marketing tactics with non-traditional lifestyle and experiential events to develop revenue generating branding opportunities for our clients.

Hardline360 will also assist in the development and promotion of new and emerging indie artists through corporate branding tie-ins and cross-promotional engagements.

We use events to build powerful brands and tell compelling stories; to inform, to market, to educate, to entertain, and to astonish the audience. We believe in the capacity of live entertainment to move & motivate, to inspire & instruct, to please & persuade…. to leave a lasting impression.

Jeff Beal’s Specialties:

A thorough knowledge of principles of effective brand marketing, specifically live event marketing and corporate communications. Proven experience in media, publicity, consumer and client relations, and management, live event marketing, contract negotiations, business development and special event budgeting.
