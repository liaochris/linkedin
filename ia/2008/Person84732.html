Jason Coyle’s Summary

Throughout my career, I have been successful by combining the science of project management with the art of hands-on leadership. I use creativity, ingenuity and effective decision making to build teams, motivate them to achieve a shared vision, and guide initiatives to success. I have a track record of providing clarity in ambiguous situations and establishing broad-based Emergency Response programs for organization-wide initiatives. Further, I am adept at driving organizational efficiencies and making process management methodology improvements.

Some of my selected accomplishments include:

Established a national multi-jurisdictional emergency operations center designed to handle large natural disasters, national police operations, and counter-insurgency operations.

Played key role in the concept, design, and construction of a $5 million Operations Directorate facility which was completed 3 months ahead of schedule and $430,000 under its allocated budget.

Dramatically increased responsiveness to major incidents by successfully designing a security plan that coordinated key emergency operations organizations and national security agencies.

My goal is to contribute and drive value in a capacity that a company feels is the best utilization of my skills, background and experience – in whatever role that may be.
