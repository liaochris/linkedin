Damon Frugoli’s Summary

I have been in the graphic arts field for the past 15 years. My expertise has been in the production of highly personalized direct mail. I have a strong passion for personalized customer communications and the technology employed to create them.

My goals include providing vision and leadership in graphic arts industry while keeping an eye on tomorrow. There are many exciting new "printed" technologies emerging including organic electronics, I want to be on the forefront of these emerging techologies.

Damon Frugoli’s Experience

  • Vice President

    Segerdahl Corporation

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    March 2007 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    I joined Segerdahl in a full-time sales capacity, allowing me to leverage my 15 years of being a specialist in personalization and data technology to help companies create and produce successful, high-impact direct marketing initiatives.

    Although my role at Segerdahl will be as a full-time Sales Executive, I still have a strong passion for personalization and cutting edge technology. Along with my Sales role, I will play a key role at Segerdahl in researching and developing new direct marketing technologies.

  • VP of Digital Technology

    Lehigh Direct

    (Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2005 — March 2007 (1 year 8 months)

    The focus of my current position is research new marketing technologies and add incremental value to the organization through these technologies. I oversee all web development activities including e-mail marketing and various online tracking and reporting tools used to measure the overall performance of our customers direct mail programs. I am also developing a unique of customer communication tools that enable our customers to have a one to one conversation with their customers. My projects that have an elevated "cool" factor include rolling out magnetic stripe and radio frequency identification (RFID) technologies solutions to collect data at point of sale systems.

  • Manager of Imaging and Personalization

    Lehigh Direct

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Printing industry)

    August 2001 — August 2005 (4 years 1 month)

    I was recruited by Lehigh Direct to create a department to support the companies variable personalization. Responsibilites included the hiring and managing of a staff of Programmers and Data Technicians.

    During my tenure, the department grew in both size and revenue generating over $2 million dollars annually.

  • Production Supervisor: Data and Personalization

    RR Donnelley and Sons Company

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; rrd; Printing industry)

    December 1995 — August 2001 (5 years 9 months)

    I supervised the daily activities of a staff of Programmers, Data Technicians and Production Coordinators. My duties included:
    · Providing the materials necessary to ensure an accurate and high quality production run.
    · Gathering and writing programming specifications.
    · Scheduling production workloads
    · Administering department budgets
    · Setting and monitoring department goals
    · Conducting yearly performance reviews
    It was also my responsibility to suggest equipment purchases and coordinate support/upgrade for numerous department software packages and computer systems. Additional duties included developing and implementing various job tracking databases and delivering presentations to internal and external customers of RR Donnelley and Sons. This position required a strong knowledge of in-line printing, ink-jet imaging systems, data processing, programming logic, postal regulations and the direct mail industry.
