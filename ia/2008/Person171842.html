Sean Gabb’s Experience

  • Director

    The Libertarian Alliance

    (Political Organization industry)

    March 2006 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    The Libertarian Alliance is working to bring about a society based on voluntary association.

    We are against the State - the State and all the other structures of oppression that it creates or enables. This includes big business, which could not exist without incorporation laws and limited liability and patterns of tax and subsidy and regulation.

    For us, freedom of speech is the right to say anything about public affairs, whether political, scientific, historical or otherwise.

    For us, freedom of association is about the right to deal (or not) with any other consenting adult, for any non-aggressive purpose, and without need for explanation.

    We believe in the right to carry and use guns for self-defence, in the right to consume or sell recreational drugs, in the right to use or publish pornography.

    We believe that government spending should be cut. We want a Government so small, that it doesn't matter where it is, what it does, who's in it, or how they got there
