Mark Ericson’s Summary

Long time software product innovator on the leading edge of paradigm shifts in technology. Early adopter of object technology, client/server, distributed objects, Web, XML, and Service Oriented Architecture. Most recently focused almost entirely on SOA at both Mindreef and now BlueNote Networks.

Currently my interest in specifically on the convergence of SOA and interactive communications enabled by SIP.

Mark Ericson’s Specialties:

Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Web Services, XML, Object technology (OO), software architecture, product development, product management, business development, Java, C++, SOAP, http, client/server, n-tier, sql, database, object-relational mapping, performance tuning
