Greg Ferris’s Summary

In 2002, I co-founded Precision Development, a professional services firm dedicated to developing secure, user-friendly, web-based applications. We work with clients of all sizes, including 12 Fortune 100 and 28 Fortune 500 companies. While we have developed numerous public-facing, commerce-driven web applications, our primary focus is in providing our clients with more efficient and cost-effective processes through the use of technology. We have worked with technologies of all kinds, though our specific expertise is with Microsoft tools, particularly .NET and SQL Server database development. We have successfully integrated custom applications with existing inventory systems, ERP software and accounting applications, to name a few.

On a day-to-day basis, I am involved with developing client relationships, overseeing projects, managing the growth of our business, and (on occasion) writing a few lines of code.

We are currently a team of eleven, though we are looking to add new developers to our team. If you are an energetic and motivated individual interested in interacting directly with clients and participating in the entire software development life cycle, visit our careers page at http://www.precisiondev.net/careers
