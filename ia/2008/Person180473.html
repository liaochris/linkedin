Stephanie Lloyd’s Summary

Stephanie has more than 15 years of experience in financial services recruiting/sourcing and business development. Prior to starting Calibre Search Group in 2006, Stephanie was a VP Client Relations for TSYS Loyalty, a loyalty services company specializing in the financial services and retail industries.

Prior to TSYS Loyalty, she was a senior search consultant and team lead with Corporate Search Partners, performing executive search and business development specializing in financial/advisory services including commercial and investment banking, business valuation, audit, tax, treasury, litigation support, corporate finance, and M&A.

Early in her career Stephanie joined The MONY Group (formerly Mutual of New York) as a recruiter for the Tampa agency. She was promoted to Regional Director, Recruiting, and worked with 14 Managing Directors throughout the southeast to help them develop and implement strategies for attracting top talent. Additionally, she created and implemented a diversity initiative to increase the number of women in sales/management. She was then promoted to National Director, Business Development, where she developed a national strategy to attract and recruit top producers. From this position she was promoted to Vice President, Trusted Securities Advisors Corp., a subsidiary investment firm. She reported to the Chairman and was responsible for business development in the southeastern U.S. She worked with public accounting firms utilizing the brokerage services of Trusted Advisors, and trained and developed the CPAs and tax professionals to provide personal and business financial planning services to their clients.

Stephanie received a Bachelor of Science in Economics from Florida State University, and is currently involved in the following professional organizations:

Women's Finance Exchange, President, 2001-Present, Atlanta, GA
The Financial Network, Founding Member, 2001-Present, Atlanta, GA
