Jennifer Yarter’s Summary

I am a television research director in a Top 15 market. I have eight years of experience in the television business, and have worked in sales, promotions, and production before ending up in research.

As Research Director for WFLA NewsChannel 8 in Tampa, Florida, I manage all aspects of research, including Nielsen ratings and estimates, competitive information and qualitative research for the #1 station in Florida’s largest TV market. On a day to day basis, I analyze and interpret Nielsen Media Research and other qualitative and quantitative data for the purposes of enhancing and positioning our television station. I led the station through three significant Nielsen methodology changes, including Live Plus delayed viewing, Zero Cell Ascription and Local People Meters, developing training programs and working with managers, sales account executives and company wide research directors to bring them up to speed on industry changes and implications.

I also conduct nationwide research and tracking of syndicated programs produced by Media General’s in-house production studio Riverbank Studios. In the past year, I developed a multi-media cross training selling program taught to all television, newspaper and internet sales representatives at our location.

I have been personally recognized in Media Post, Broadcasting & Cable Magazine, TVNewsDay.com, Television Bureau of Advertising, The Television Business Report, and Spots N Dots for my contributions regarding the changing television industry. I was also a presenter at the 2006 Scarborough Sales, Research and Marketing Conference.

In the past year, I completed a Management/Leadership Certificate Program with training in hiring, diversity, teamwork, employee motivation, communication, Gung Ho! Management, and Focus Time Management.
