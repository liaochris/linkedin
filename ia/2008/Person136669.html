Michal Szalinski’s Summary

Michal is a Director and the Head of Architecture within the Technology Services group of Capgemini. He joined Capgemini in early 2006 after more than 12 years in professional services at PricewaterhouseCoopers and IBM.

In his client facing role, Michal’s primary expertise is in defining enterprise scale architectures to optimise and develop integrated solutions in a timely manner. They are large scale systems and range in different industries including government, retail, utilities, financial and telecommunications. Michal has significant experience managing and architecting software development and systems integration initiatives within the Telecommunications industry.

In Michal’s operational management role, he is a member of the Capgemini leadership team, responsible for managing the Architecture capability in Australia. Key responsibilities include definition and execution of function strategy inline with overall business strategy, line management of 20+ people in multiple locations in Australia, managing staff recruitment and retention, strategically building a sustainable architecture go to market practice, mentoring and staff development, business case development and influencing at the executive level. Under his leadership the local community has established a globally recognised training programme which enables global certification, established a global architecture publication “Momentum” and membership in the community has grown more than 300%.

In addition to his local responsibilities, Michal is the sole Asia Pacific representative on the Global Architecture Council which governs over the architecture community in Capgemini. He actively participates in global initiatives to guide and develop the community and contributes knowledge assets on a regular basis.

Michal holds a Bachelor of Arts in Computing Studies (Double Major Computing). Outside of professional commitments, Michal is an avid scuba diver with advanced certification.
