Bryan Watson’s Summary

My expert troubleshooting skills allow me to solve problems outside my core competencies.

Seasoned network engineer with security experience in intrusion detection, penetration testing, de-hacking and eliminating viruses and malware from small-business and ISP networks as well as designing and maintaining secure, available networks.

Experienced in practical security auditing, penetration/vulnerability testing and disaster resiliency planning and training as well as switching and routing protocols.

Skilled expert in root cause analysis of network failure and performance problems. Experienced with most modern routers and switches in implementing a variety of protocols including complex VPN and VOIP configurations.

Experience in IT since 1981 and in rf Electronics to 1976.
