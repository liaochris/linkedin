Ed Woo’s Summary

An experienced Staffing Professional with over 18 years of providing HR Solutions and strategies in the Human Capital Industry that includes over 10 years in the Staffing Industry in all facets of the business that included roles in recruiting, sales, management and training; over 2 years as co-owner and running a Recruiting search practice, and over 6 years in Corporate Recruiting and Recruiting Management, Staffing Consulting, Career Management Consulting and Training, and Diversity and College Recruiting programs.

My passion is connecting talented individuals and exciting companies together to create new and exciting business opportunities. My overall background of Consulting, Sales/Business Development, Recruitment in various industries and markets, Management, and starting and running successful Business Operations gives me great insights to identifying talent, building strategies and finding the best solutions.

I'm looking to build my networks to assist individuals with their career goals and aspirations and companies with bringing in the best matched talent to support their company goals and successes.
