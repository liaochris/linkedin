Jack Perkins’s Summary

I have had the good fortune to travel the country and share the best emerging software technology with manufacturers and process automation professionals. First with Wonderware, subsequently with Incuity, and now with Incuity incorporated into the Rockwell Automation group. Wonderware pioneered HMI and process historians in the Microsoft environment. Incuity added complementary functionality to any process automation software infrastructure by presenting this data in concert with other sources of operational information, thus adding value to the existing software investment. Recognizing this, Rockwell Automation purchased Incuity in May, 2008 to round out the information integration capabilities of the FactoryTalk platform

I get personal satisfaction knowing that these technologies are helping my customers make more product, with higher quality and greater efficiency than ever before.
