Heather Ann Dueease’s Summary

After becoming the number one corporate account sales representative in the U.S.A. for Cingular Wireless, Heather Ann Dueease knew she had found her niche in sales. She went on to become an expert in the seminar and information marketing / publishing world. Known as "The Closer," Heather Ann has exceeded expectations in closing sales for her own products and seminars as well as those of her clients. She has over five years’ experience developing highly successful direct response internet marketing campaigns. Heather Ann’s experience lies in three primary realms. In just eight years, she has attended, developed or was a part of over 198 seminars in internet marketing, real estate investing and personal development fields. Within direct response and information marketing, she has conducted, managed, promoted and produced events, both online and offline. She has helped clients exceed revenues of over $20,000 per month, and has consulted companies moving from offline event marketing to online.
She has effectively coached entrepreneurs and authors in personal development. Additionally, she is well-versed in real estate investing, and has conducted seminars and developed education materials in this field. Heather Ann has a strong ability to see from a bird's eye view a company’s entire business model and then see the possible systems and money avenues she can help strategically develop. She takes an energetic approach to coaching; every client is a unique business with specific needs that she knows clearly how to help acheive maximum results.
Heather Ann includes her extensive knowledge of marketing and sales in her consulting practice. Her clients, who range from start-ups to multi-million dollar corporations, are always impressed with the over-the-top results she produces. No matter where you are in your business’ growth, if you are ready to super charge your business and increase sales exponentially, contact a true information marketing and online media expert.
