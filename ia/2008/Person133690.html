Mitchell Caplan’s Summary

Mitchell Caplan is Chief Marketing Officer, North America, for Young & Rubicam Advertising and Chief Marketing Officer for Young & Rubicam Brands.

Early on in his career Mitch became fascinated with helping clients to think about how their marketing efforts could go beyond conventional thinking. And since moving to New York over ten years ago, he has become recognized as one of the leading authorities on the successful integration of traditional marketing techniques with digital marketing solutions.

He has also led the business development efforts at several major agencies, including helping The Kaplan Thaler Group become the fastest growing agency in the U.S. in 2004 and 2005, as named by Advertising Age magazine.

In his current position he is responsible for the business development and marketing efforts of both Young & Rubicam Advertising and Y&R Brands.

Mitchell Caplan’s Specialties:

Traditional and digital brand strategy development. New business development. Integration of multiple discipline organizations.

    Mitchell Caplan is the Chief Marketing Officer for Young & Rubicam, the world's fourth largest agency network.

    He is also Chief Marketing Officer for Young & Rubicam Brands, which is a global, collaborative network of preeminent companies in advertising, public relations, public affairs, brand identity and design, direct and database marketing, digital and interactive marketing, multicultural marketing and healthcare communications.

    After spending many years as an Account Director with some of the U.S.'s leading agencies, Mitch took a job with think new ideas, one of the U.S's first interactive agencies. After ascending to the role of Managing Director for think's NY and Boston offices, he left to become President of The Martin Agency's interactive arm.

    It was during this time that he became convinced that technology, and it's applications in the world of marketing and communications, was changing the agency business forever. And for the better.
