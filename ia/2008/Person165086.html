Roy Lewis’s Summary

Years of wide ranging technology experience with solid people skills make for a very effective sales and marketing professional. I've been working the tech world on the 'softer' people side for at least 20 years now, and most recently in the IT/Telecom/Datacom sector. In the past I have been heavily involved in advanced software tools for the EDA industry, Test and Measurement instruments, computer hardware and software, as well as channel development, both domestic and international.

I am very motivated by the opportunity to be a change agent, to bring new ideas to life and to bear in any sales/marketing/channel environment. I am also motivated by helping others grow in their careers, giving them the tools to also become 'change agents'.

I am now working with my UK partners for international commodities buying/selling/trading. Check http://www.icc-commodoties.com. You'll find me listed under 'Management' in the 'About' tab.

I am also involved in two consulting projects; one for Wireless services and the other for an eCommerce site for WiFi/WiMAX systems sales.
