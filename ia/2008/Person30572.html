Carla Ulbrich’s Summary

I have performed my original songs since 1995 all up and down the East Coast of the US, and also the UK and Canada. I have released 3 full-length recordings, which are heard on Dr. Demento, the BBC, XM and Sirius Satellite radio, PAX TV, USA TV, and various NPR stations.

I have performed for Club Med, the Bluebird Cafe, numerous Folk Festivals, private parties, comedy clubs, corporate events and medical conventions. I have a specialty in medical humor because of my experience as a patient (I sometimes call myself "the singing patient"). I have performed the medical humor for patient support groups, hospice workers, staff parties, a Medicaid convention, and hte Association for Applied and Therapeutic Humor.

My goal is to continue bringing humor to both the general population and the medical community, showing people how to find humor in everyday life, and how to use it to reduce stress and gain perspective, as well as letting them know that what they do matters. I hope to grow as a person through this work and to help others do the same. And I also hope to just laugh and help others do the same. Laughter heals!
