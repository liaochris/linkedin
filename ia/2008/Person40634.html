Craig Patterson’s Summary

Craig is a Manager in the Strategic IT Effectiveness (SITE) practice in San Francisco. Craig has over 15 years experience and has been involved in a wide range of management and technology consulting engagements such as program management office, enterprise IT planning, IT mergers and acquisitions, program management, project management, enterprise architecture, and IT support model assessments. Craig holds a bachelor’s degree from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo, completed the M.B.A. program at Golden Gate University, and finished Masters in Policy Management (M.P.M.) program at Georgetown University. Craig also has a master’s certificate in project management from George Washington University and a project management professional certification from the Project Management Institute.

Craig Patterson’s Specialties:

Business Architecture and Process Design, Business Process Management, Enterprise Architecture, Government, Government Health, IT Governance, IT Strategy, IT Transformation, Organization Governance and Support, Organization Strategy, Organizational Effectiveness, Portfolio Management, Program Management, Project Management, Public Service Value, Regulatory Compliance, Risk Management, Strategic Business Transformation, Strategic IT Effectiveness (SITE), Strategy, Strategy Development.
