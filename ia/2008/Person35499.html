    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    December 2002 — Present (5 years 5 months)

    In December of 2002, I founded Course-Notes.Org, a website that contains notes, outlines, practice quizzes and many other resources for classes such as US History, English, Psychology and Economics that augment a student’s studying ability. My daily tasks include moderating the community forum that consists of 40,000 members, marketing the website to potential private advertisers such as iChapters.com, working with online advertising networks to sell my advertising inventory as a source of revenue, responding to support request emails, and compiling a monthly newsletter to the members.
