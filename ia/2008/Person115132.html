Pascal Lobry’s Summary

Currently supporting a fast growing worldwide organization in the oil & gas business, I develop and sustain the HR network and services throughout the subsidiaries, with a special focus on attraction, talent management and career development.

Career backgroung: Senior manager in Human Resources, 15 + years managing international HR networks, mainly in pharmaceutical, manufacturing and high tech industries and services.

Talent and change management. Communication. Negotiation. Labor issues. Perimeters of 5,000 + employees worldwide.Various experiences of mergers, acquisitions and divestments. Involved with top management, researchers, engineers, sales teams and blue collar staff. Created twice the complete HR function, network and culture on a worldwide basis (Europe, the Americas, Asia, high and low cost countries).

Can speak and work in fluent English, French, German, Spanish and Portuguese.
