Joachim Crombez’s Summary

Responsabilities:
- Work in a pre-sales/technical capacity with the Director of Business Development to assist in the closing of sales with new customers and new projects with existing customers.
- Deliver engaging, informative, well-organized presentations before and during the project
- Review project deliverables before agreeing the deliverables with the customer or prospect
- In conjunction with the Director of Business Development create a costed project plan for agreement and sign off by the customer/prospect prior to the commencement of work
- Identify the resources needed in the project and draw to the attention of superiors if resources are fully utilised or are missing or if there is a mismatch between resource availability and a customers delivery requirements
- Execute project work plans and revise these plans as appropriate to meet the changing needs and requirements of the customer and company
- Manage the day-to-day operational aspects of a project and scope and effectively apply the company’s methodology and enforce the project standards.
- Manage the project budgets and minimize the Company’s exposure and risk on projects by making sure that the projects are achieved within the agreed budget.
- Ensure that the project documents are complete, current, and stored appropriately.
- Follow up with clients, resolve and/or escalate issues when a project is not proceeding according to the agreed plan.
- Maintain awareness of new and emerging technologies and the potential application on client engagements.
- Train and manage new technical staff (as they are hired) and other innovators and clients through both formal and informal training programs.
- Suggest areas for improvement in internal processes along with possible solutions.
- Complies with and helps to enforce standard policies and procedures.
