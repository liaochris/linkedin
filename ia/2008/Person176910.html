Shirish Jamthe’s Summary

I love solving complex business problems requiring massive data analysis. I like integrating disparate systems and most of all nurturing great teams of creative people.

I like to throw myself into totally new area of business or in challenging environments and solve problems by out-of-the box or lateral thinking.

I have 17+ years of experience in Product Development and Engineering and have an intimate working knowledge of the enterprise marketplace, especially for Financial Investments industry.

I founded and built a venture funded startup, hired executives in engineering, product development, sales and finance.

As a consulting manager I have presented to CXOs of large Financial Firms and won multi million dollar deals.

I have developed and nurtured teams (15-45 people, $3+ million budget) in large companies with creative and agile environment like a startup.
