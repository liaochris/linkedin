Stefanos Kofopoulos’s Summary

After spending years working into IT and being part of the amazing Broadcom family i decided it was time to pursue my dream and go full time with blogging. Time was right and here i am, the first Greek blogger who tries to make his living out of... oh well blogging! Lately i enjoy creating podcasts and video podcasts which seem to boost pestaola.g traffic a lot. Speaking of video podcasts, my Neonode N2 unboxing video made it to engadget and gizmodo, that is No1 and No3 blogs of the planet we humans call Earth, making me the happiest Greek blogger ever!

Recently, i've got free time to make a crazy idea come true via the first Greek Blogger Camp. Great people gathered for a 2 day event on the beautiful island of Ios in Greece. Have a look on the videos and photos to see all attendees and how fun it was.

My latest gigs include a new blog and live blogging. I love my city, Athens, Greece and Make Athens Green it's a new blog to prove that. As for live blogging, the idea it's been around for a long time but i made it happen recently via my Neonode N2 live blogging post.

It was only because of Greek Blogger Camp that i created a Flickr PRO account and got addicted to it. Some of my photos help kids learn history while others make adults drool. It's all fun and licensed under Creative Commons. Feel free to use but don't forget to give credit. Don’t steal my stuff, it’s bad karma.

I love sports, reading, traveling and drinking wine, preferably red wine but beer will do the job too. I hold a Bachelor in MIS [Management of Information Systems] and MCP certification [Microsoft Certified Professional].
