Ludovic Cailluet’s Summary

After two years as a research fellow and lecturer at the Center for International Business History at the University of Reading (UK) I joined in 1998 the University of Toulouse Graduate School of Management (IAE) where I am responsible for the Master in International Management programe (M2).

I have researched and published on the history of the international aluminum industry, commercial vehicles and consulting industries, the international transfer of management methods and the impact of foreign direct investment on French firms.

I am presently involved in two research projects:
The first one in association with University of Oxford Professor Richard Whittington is a comparative research on the practice of strategy and strategic planning in the France, Japan, USA and UK (funded by the French National Research Agency - ANR)
The second one is a clinical research on the relationship between corporate culture and strategic capabilities for a pharmaceutical and cosmetics company.

Ludovic Cailluet’s Specialties:

I am always interested in clinical research for companies, including strategy or business development analysis, intercultural management issues and corporate history as a performance improvement and change management tool.
