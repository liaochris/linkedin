Jessica Stram’s Summary

I am an emerging lobbyist and education policy specialist, with experience in both Connecticut, on the state level, and Washington, DC, on the national level. To this line of work I bring extensive research experience, proven relationships in the CT General Assembly, a high level of professionalism, and a dynamic personality that is suited for government relations. I am a self-directed person, who also succeeds as a part of a team. Most importantly, I love what I do, which transcends into my quality of work.

Jessica Stram’s Specialties:

- Research, including internet databases and opposition research.
- Coalition building within the state of CT and nationally.
- Tried and true relationships with members of the CT General Assembly, particularly involving members of the Education and Appropriations Committees.
- Holistic knowledge of education policy issues, including but not limited to budgeting and transparency, early childhood education, parent involvement, No Child Left Behind and Title 1 schools, and school bullying.
