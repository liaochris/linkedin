Shane Frizzell’s Summary

I have spent the past decade in various roles within the healthcare industry. Starting from the ground floor as a claims processor and working my way through the ranks as a configuration guru on the AMISYS platform as well as assuming the role of Reimbursement Manager and establishing fee schedules and payment methodologies for several Medicaid lines of business.

I have had the benefit of wearing many hats with lots of exposure to many manners of operationally addressing problems due to my extensive work history with several different companies.

As is evidenced by my various roles and my overall experience level, I would be a definite asset to any company looking for someone that could take charge of issues and develop quick solutions and streamline processes to make a claims shop run more effectively.
