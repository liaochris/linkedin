Sander Jonker (email@sanderjonker.com)’s Summary

USG Innotiv flexibly appoints professionals in ICT and Engineering. Companies make fast and simple calls on our specialists for short and longer-term assignments in specialist domains. At USG Innotiv, ICT specialists and engineers find variety, attractive job security and extensive training possibilities.

From application development through system and network management to sustainable and feasible ICT solutions: USG Innotiv (formerly Beaver ITServices – from 1988) is the specialist in ICT personnel and organisation for large and medium-sized organisations. Clients and employees feel that our flexible, pragmatic collaboration formulas are a real step forward. We guarantee career continuity to ICT specialists who work with us, as well as interesting projects, support, training and a homely feeling. For our clients and our employees, we are a partner and we offer quality, reliability and commitment to our partners.
