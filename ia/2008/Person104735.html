Kendall Card’s Summary

The title on my first business card was “the local’s voice”. Marketing by understanding and speaking with and not to customers has been my approach in business. With the increasing effectiveness of blogging, customer marketing and social media approaches these areas of business have become my focus and passion.

With nearly 10 years of experience working in marketing and sales capacities at The Canyons Ski Resor, Backcountry.com and now Base Camp Communications whether by choice or by circumstance I have often been forced to find ways to reach lofty goals with very limited budgets. For example, with a focus on blogging I helped Backcountry.com to find unique ways to incorporate blogging into the business model with fantastic ROI.

Current and past clients like Ski Utah, Bluehouse Skis and PROBAR are from the outdoor, health food, lodging and resort industries.
