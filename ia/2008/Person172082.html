Sean Tiner’s Summary

From skydiving to photographing a scenic landscape to launching business plans, I am not afraid to put my best foot forward and take risks. This sentiment translates into my professional and artistic endeavors. I have always set high goals and worked hard to achieve them. I went from riding the water polo bench my freshman year of high school to competing in a national tournament in Australia and earning All-Ivy Academic Honors at Brown University; all from hard work and believing in myself.

Today, I have a detailed eye for aesthetics and love to think beyond the dynamics of Web 2.0. I have my artwork hanging in the galleries of Fashion Island and have even replaced a Warhol in the home of a LA Philharmonic Conductor. Additionally, my personal blog has soared Alexa’s website traffic rankings, because I enjoy sharing creative brand insights from my master’s dissertation.

Embedded in my creativity is a willingness to give every good idea a chance, as long as its meets my standards of integrity and is something about which I am passionate.

Most importantly, I care about the people who shaped my life and support me today. I cherish the memories of my brother and work hard to keep his memory alive in his memorial foundation that I established. I listen to the advice of my mentors and try to give back whenever possible.

So, whether sipping tea in Istanbul, snorkeling the Great Barrier Reef or surfing the beaches of Rio de Janeiro, I enjoy expanding my horizons and learning from new settings.
