Troy Wilson’s Summary

We empower sales teams to shine by polishing Internet mortgage and debt settlement leads. We have developed systems allowing us to cleanse large amounts of Internet data so that only the best leads are made available for sale. We focus mainly on enhancing and reselling aged Internet mortgage leads and debt leads with our most popular products being week old, 25-45 day, and 46-90 day although we have data going back years for those clients looking for millions of leads to email or put into an autodialer.
