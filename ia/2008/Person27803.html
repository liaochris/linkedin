Brian Slawin’s Summary

Fall/Winter 2008 - We have launched BusyEvent Express (BusyEventXP.com) for small and frequent meetings. During the public beta, Express is free for fewer than 150 attendee events and will eventually cost only $100 per event, regardless of size up to 1,000 attendees. Express competes with the other online meeting services but includes many of the things that makes BusyEvent truly unique. All for flat monthly fees.

Winter 2009 - Launching BusyEvent Bookmarking which will connect event participants in real time with all the people, places and things they met at the event. We're modernizing the trade show and event experience so they are no longer where "Good Leads go to Die!" Running a green meeting and providing connectivity 365 days a year allows everyone involved to get the most out of their investment.

Panamedia Group, Inc. is an events software company that provides tools to facilitate effective communications among the members of corporations and associations such as:

Domino's Pizza, Marriott Vacation Clubs / Ritz Carlton, Coca-Cola, NBC/Universal, Swiss Army Brands, Daimler Chrysler/Jeep, Elsevier Science, McGraw-Hill, National Geographic Publishing, Saint Louis University, Fair Saint Louis / Celebrate St. Louis, Jewish Community Center, National American Indian Veterans Association, Holocaust Museum and Learning Center, US Environmental Protection Agency, Maritz, Darden Restaurants

Brian Slawin’s Experience

  • President | Principal

    Panamedia Group, Inc. & BusyEvent

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Events Services industry)

    July 2006 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    More than 30 years of event and interactive experience is core to Panamedia Group.

    Our main product line, BusyEvent, is an integrated software and hardware platform used to manage people, information and events.

    BusyEvent eliminates multi-vendor coordination by providing a complete system for registration through the on-site experience to lead tracking and social networking, turning a 3-4 day event into a year-round center of education, motivation and sales opportunities.

    With no impact on the corporate IT infrastructure, event managers can focus on productivity and outcomes and utilize the integrated reporting tools to track and oversee these expensive marketing activities.

    Corporations, associations and groups who meet regularly choose BusyEvent to leverage advanced data collection and business intelligence methodologies to connect the right people with the right information in real time.

    As part of an overall strategy, BusyEvent is often FREE or a profit center.
