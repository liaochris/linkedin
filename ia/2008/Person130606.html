Michael Trudel’s Summary

With a diverse background in creative leadership, communications strategy and concept development, I bring a unique perspective and expertise in creating powerful solutions to a client’s business challenges and opportunities.

Most recently I helped Budco, the Dialogue Company, win or retain over $35M in business with clients such as Boeing, Ford, Unisys, Chrysler, Toyota, Little Caesars, AAA of Michigan and Kraft Foods, to name a few.

I was the driving force in developing an unprecedented, and successful, public relations strategy that was instrumental in getting former Olympic Gold Medal winner Clarke Scholes enshrined in the Michigan Sports Hall of Fame.

My creative abilities helped the Grosse Pointe Library Foundation, Orchard Lake St. Mary’s and the Pontiac Area Vicariate reach and exceed capital campaign fundraising goals through the effective use of the video medium.

As a writer/producer I helped Fox Sports Net Detroit during their early years in the competitive Detroit sports market. My work was recognized nationally with 10 Emmy nominations, winning two of the coveted statuettes.

I’m a United States Navy veteran and a graduate in communications from Wayne State University, Detroit. I was also selected to complete a comprehensive business development seminar through Washington University in St. Louis.

Michael Trudel’s Specialties:

Corporate and marketing communications, proposal development, video production, training, public relations and event marketing.
