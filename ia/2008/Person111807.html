Lazaro Fuentes’s Summary

Hip Venture Co™ is a digital media company we created to develop socially relevant properties under the umbrella mission Help Improve the Planet™.

Our first property is an online virtual chat world that brings together pop elements from the fastest growing and youngest segment of the US market; Hispanics and combines it with the hottest and fastest growing media segment for the young; casual gaming and animation. We add a strong environmental and social responsibility focus for an offering that has a well defined market, is fun, entertaining and powerfully purposeful.

Playfully dubbed Hip Chicas™, the virtual chat world, games, music and activities are guided by five animated characters that make up a fictional band called The Hip Chicas™ - www.hipchicas.com. They each have unique passions, strengths and skills that have been developed to provide fun and positive role modeling opportunities, while appealing to parents seeking positive and engaging content for their kids.

I am a social entrepreneur and HIP™ is my next big idea. I am a firm believer that financial and social success are not mutually exclusive. We can do well by doing good. Further, if set up correctly, how well we do financially can be directly related to how much good we do socially. We have a strong team of evangelists on board that are helping to build this company who believe as I do that to Help Improve the Planet™ is a worthwhile and HIP™ objective.

Check us out and be on the lookout for the Hip Chicas™ at www.hipchicas.com!
