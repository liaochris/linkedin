Erik Wellen’s Summary

Experienced project management/product management professional in the IT/software development industry.

Responsible for the Professional Services Department at Bell ID. This department comprises of Project Deliveries, Training&Documentation and Consulting teams.

Part of Bell ID's management team.

Project management of projects comprising smart card management, cryptographic key management, PKI, etc. in various industries such as National ID, Corporate ID, Health Care and Campus ID.

Been involved in projects around the globe: Far East, Middle East, Africa, North-America, Europe:

--Project: National Health Card, Austria e-Card, Austria, 2004-2005
The e-card will replace Austria's current paper-based healthcare vouchers and approx. 11 million cards will be issued. The cost of the total e-card project is estimated to be EUR 116 million.

--Project: Multi-application Corporate ID Card , Boeing Corporation, USA ,2003
Boeing Corporation launched a multi-app smart ID card scheme (200,000 cards to staff globally)

--Project: Electronic National ID Card, Macau SAR, China, 2001, 2002
Bell ID implemented the ANDiS© Card and Application Management System (CMS/AMS) for the Government of the former Portuguese colony Macau, China.
2003: Larry Linden Award for Innovative Security Technology, awarded to Siemens Business Services and Giesecke & Devrient by CardTech/SecurTech

--Project: Campus Card, King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Saudi Arabia
Bell ID implemented a multi-application smart ID card scheme for KFUPM

--Project: Company Card, Schiphol Group, The Netherlands
ANDiS© enables Schiphol Amsterdam Airport to manage the life cycle of 80,000 multi-application smart cards.

--Project: Company Card, KPN Telecom, The Netherlands
KPN uses Bell ID’s ANDiS© CMS/AMS to manage the life cycle of multi-app company cards.

Erik Wellen’s Specialties:

ANDiS, Smart Card Management Systems, Key Management Systems, EMV, PKI, e-government, e-commerce, international project management, product management, planning

Completed OneMBA program in June 2005 (http://www.onemba.org) at Rotterdam School of Management

    May 2005 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Founder of Bell Group Mr Pat Curran leads current management team in acquisition of global market leader in smart card management and provider of ANDiS© Management Systems.
    Bell ID, subsidiary of London-based Bell Group, has been acquired from Securitas AB by the founder and current Chairman of Bell Group Mr Pat Curran. Bell ID’s software business was not considered to fit strategically within Securitas’ operations in the guarding, cash handling and security systems sectors. Financial details of the transaction have not been made public.

    Under the new structure Mr Curran will become Chairman of Bell ID, with the existing long-standing management team remaining in place. Martin Cox will take the role of Sales Director, with Erik Wellen becoming Head of Professional Services and Oliver Burke General Manager of Partner Management, Business Development and Marketing. Under the agreement, the entire team has committed to remain with the company for an undisclosed number of years.
