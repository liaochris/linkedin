Mike Morris’s Summary

I have spent my entire career partnering with leading organizations to help them achieve Process Excellence. My clients have included leaders in healthcare, financial services, manufacturing, and telecommunications.

My focus is to help my clients drive top and bottom-line financial value through developing processes that enhance the customer experience and eliminate waste. Improving the customer experience helps our clients to keep customers and grow revenue. Eliminating waste helps our clients reduce costs which can lead to reinvestment into an improved customer experience and increased shareholder value. I believe that executive-level commitment is critical towards these goals. Organizations need to develop process improvement and the use of appropriate process tools as core internal competencies, in support of their goals. This is where my expertise lies.

I find that many organizations are conflicted whether to make an initial investment in Business Process Analysis (BPA) or Business Process Management (BPM). In a perfect world, BPA feeds BPM. BPM can succeed without BPA, but only for the simplest processes. BPA allows organizations to model, analyze, and optimize their processes before executing them in a BPM environment. This is what allows organizations to leverage their processes into competitive advantage.

iGrafx is rated as an industry leader in the BPA space and supports all of the necessary industry standards to integrate with multiple BPM environments.
