Scott Radcliffe’s Summary

Scott leads the development of profitable analytical competencies for large organizations. A sociologist by training, Scott has over 17 years of experience developing customer insight solutions, and providing advanced analytics for marketing, sales, and service. His behavioral modeling experience spans all types of quantitative techniques, and a wide variety of marketing and customer management applications. In various internal and external consulting roles, Scott has built and managed Analytical CRM organizations in the financial services, entertainment, broadband, and wireless telecom industries.

Scott Radcliffe’s Specialties:

Advanced customer analytics, behavioral modeling, informatics, customer management optimization, organizational theory.
