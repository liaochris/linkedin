Ratna Kumar Chintamaneni, PMP’s Summary

Mr. Ratna Kumar Chintamaneni B.S., M.S., PMP; an Electronics & Communication engineer and a business process consultant with 25 years of industry experience in Business, Supply Chain, ERP and Information technology. A Professional SAP, Systems Integration and Management consultant for various multinational companies at senior management level. An accredited PMP (Project Management Professional) from PMI, USA and actively involved in developing systems, processes, integration solutions and methodologies to suit the ever changing global business collaboration.

Over 25 years of success in enhancing operational efficiencies, increasing revenues, and directing major IT initiatives to drive business performance. Extensive experience in Program and Project Management of global delivery resources for end-to-end Software Development Lifecycle, ERP applications Implementation, Systems integration, estimating effort & business proposal development, process reengineering and improvement and deploying shared services. Combines excellent technical and analytical qualifications with demonstrated achievement in team and project leadership.
