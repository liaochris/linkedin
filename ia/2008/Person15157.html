Alec Pettifer’s Summary

I am currently the proprietor of AP Market Research. I've built a network of top notch market researchers from various disciplines with a focus on healthcare research. APMR provides custom tailored research programs for product and brand managers who require something more than the average cookie cutter solution.

My personal background demonstrates the application of market research industry best practices in top firms in the US, Asia, Western Pacific, and Europe.
