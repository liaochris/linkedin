Narinder Singh’s Summary

Currently the Rehabilitaion & Pharmacy Director at Santa Clara Valley Health & Hospital System (SCVH&HS) which is owned and operated by the County of Santa Clara.

Responsible for setting direction of the Rehabilitation & Pharmacy Department at SCVH&HS which is comprised of Santa Clara Valley Hospital, a network of twelve clinic pharmacies (including primary care, outpatient psychiatry, public health and custody health.)

In leadership position for ten years. Experience includes working for a non-profit organization, a for-profit organization, a small community hospital, a large tertiary care research and teaching University Hospital, a stand alone hospital and a network of 42 hospitals. For last four years has been working for a safety net organization with the mission of “providing quality care to patients regardless of their ability to pay”.

Provide big picture leadership and strong belief in team approach.

Personal mission statement is to “do the best you can” and provide “better therapy”.

Narinder Singh’s Specialties:

Team Building, coaching and motivating staff to achieve exceptional results. Establishing simple systems in complex organizations to optimize outcomes.
