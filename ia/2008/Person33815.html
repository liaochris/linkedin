Charlie Gower’s Summary

I have been involved in creative production of some description on and offline for most of my working life. Directing and producing television, short films, music, art, live events and internet based projects for public and private consumption.

Every job I have had has involved me being a creative lead, generating ideas of what should be produced and how.

I have worked in England, Ireland, Wales, Scotland, Germany, France, Holland, Dubai, South Africa and America. I guess it’s like they say, variety is the spice of life.
