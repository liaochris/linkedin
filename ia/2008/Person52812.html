Joe Dougherty’s Summary

Joe Dougherty was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and raised in Brownfield, Texas. Joe served in the US Air Force for five years, traveling the world abroad from Korea, Japan and the Philippines to Portugal, Spain and visiting various countries in Europe.

Joe Dougherty has spent a great deal of his profession ushering in technology for companies that typically are reluctant to make the move. His method has been to develop an authentic and favorable relationship with the owners and upper management of his clients who have been apprehensive to make the necessary changes. Gradually and confidently he has helped "have old school shake hands with modern times".

Joe contributes "every moment of his life to every possible future".

Joe Dougherty’s Specialties:

Systems; technology; budget and finance; operations; administration

Joe Dougherty’s Experience

  • Vice President

    Hollywood Rentals

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Entertainment industry)

    May 2006 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Leaving 13 years at Paramount Pictures behind, Joe Dougherty consulted for the production support business to "bring the industry into the 21st century". For over a year, he operated under his own company, Jedifice Consulting, approaching the buyer, selling systems, consulting on behalf of the client to the software vendor and hiring out the networking resources. Joe says his favorite client provision was to lead formal training on their new systems.

    Currently, Joe Dougherty holds the Vice President position at Hollywood Rentals, which encompasses three production support companies - HR, ESS and Olesen - at four locations - Los Angeles, North Carolina, Florida and recently Baton Rouge. With a new system in place and a restructuring tied to the new system, Joe says he feels he is fortunate to be "contributing to the responsible growth of the largest and best production support company in the world - with the best team of people in the universe."

  • General Manager

    Paramount Pictures

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Entertainment industry)

    March 1991 — January 2004 (12 years 11 months)

    Joe Dougherty says he moved to Los Angeles "when my shoes were worth more than my name". The aim was to break into the entertainment industry, bringing his IT education and talents with him. Having been out of the Air Force for a couple of years then, Los Angeles seemed to be the place to meet his goals.

    Within three years, he was working as Department Head Assistant at Paramount Pictures. Joe says he remembers then how "the division's general ledger was in a little green book - and handwritten". Becoming part of the union and working many facets of the production support business, Joe gained a rounded perspective of the studio world. He managed various operations and administration departments during his time at Paramount.

    By the time Joe Dougherty left, he had heavily contributed to the IT development of the studio group as project leader in a number of significant conversion projects. He left the studio with automatic feeds from departments on through to end financial systems.
