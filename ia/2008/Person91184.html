Jil McIntosh’s Summary

I write on a variety of subjects. My main focus is automobiles, both new and antique, but my writing portfolio includes articles on travel, food and drink, pen collecting, pets and celebrity interviews.

Jil McIntosh’s Specialties:

I am an automotive reviewer with contacts at each auto manufacturer; I am also an antique car and hot rod hobbyist, with contacts in the world of custom cars and restored vehicles.
