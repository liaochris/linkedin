Carol Setters’s Summary

♦ CAROL SETTERS: Relationship Consultant to High Achievers and their Partners │ Author │Facilitator and Speaker
♦ INDUSTRIES: All industries
♦ LOCATION: Boulder, Colorado
♦ INVITE me to connect at CosmicBiker@gmail.com

I am looking to help others here on LinkedIn by sharing my network. If you see something here in my profile that might help you, let me know.

I am a resource for those successful individuals who are struggling to balance the dynamics of power, wealth, and fame in their lives; most specifically, in the ways in which they relate to others.

I also help those who are in relationship with high achievers. I provide an opportunity for partners to share challenges in an environment where people otherwise think they “have it made,” teaching them how to maintain their own identities and develop the skills to peacefully navigate what is often a stressful life that is dominated by others’ agendas.

You'll find additional information on my website, www.CarolSetters.com.

I give keynote addresses about best practices and fascinating case studies of high achievers who have accomplished more than just being merely successful. I facilitate experiential workshops and consult with couples and individuals privately. I have an extraordinary group of colleagues that often work with me.

Why I am here on LinkedIn:

• To network with other active, intelligent and generous B2B networkers.
• Meet or be referred to high achievers and their partners
• Find consulting and speaking opportunities.
• Drive attendance to my website (see www.CarolSetters.com)

Carol Setters’s Specialties:

Confidential relationship consulting for successful, high visibility individuals and couples, a deep resource for wealthy or high visibility families with family dynamics, wealth transfer, addiction, wellness, life skills mentoring, training, coaching, marriage workshops
