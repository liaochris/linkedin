John Delaney’s Summary

At the end of the day, I'm a recruiter at heart. I established myself early in my career as a top-performing recruiter in the mid 90's, and I'm currently an executive in a software and services recruiting company--as well as a business owner of a contingent recruiting firm.

I've been in the recruiting/staffing game for quite some time, and I don't see myself leaving this industry any time soon: I really enjoy what I do. And I love training new people in the art of our craft.

The project I have been working on with GravityPeople (for the last 3 years) has been a tremendous challenge. We have finally built an end-to-end enterprise recruiting solution (custom Flex-based ATS + professional services) that is truly unique, and--more importantly--it works great!

John Delaney’s Specialties:

Every day I try to help the following (in no particular order, and usually all at once): 1) our clients attract and hire talented and motivated people; 2) our candidates find really cool opportunities; 3) our employees become better recruiters; 4) fight fires when needed (i.e., life in the services industry isn't always sunshine and smiles)
