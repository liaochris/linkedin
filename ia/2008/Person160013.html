Rick Lawhorn CISA, CISSP’s Summary

I am a seasoned information security risk management executive with over 18 years of information technology and security experience . I have an exceptionally strong background in information security disciplines that includes e-commerce, compliance and technology architectures. I am also well-versed in risk analysis, risk management, incident response, compliance, and information systems assessments.

I have been published in numerous domestic and international security magazines and featured in multiple podcasts and interviews. I am currently serving on several advisory boards for innovative security products and also serving as an Security Expert for a New York based law firm focusing on corporate security breaches and consumer privacy rights.
