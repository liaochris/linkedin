Daniël Giesen’s Summary

Daniël Giesen has as a consultant experience in the areas of business administration, innovation, and collaboration. He is a teamrole talent and cares about people and business. He emphasizes details and guarantees quality. Daan is conscientious and feels urgency. Besides, he is an enterprising man and professional.

Daniël did several projects during his study Business Administration breaking new grounds in the US, Germany, and Portugal. First, he gained experience in marketing and e-commerce in the US. Then, he developed himself as a knowledge source for innovation and new product development. He cooperated in a consulting workshop in Germany and leaded his own research project in Portugal. Then, he did his graduation at Capgemini Consulting where he actively contributed to the Business Innovation team in the areas of co-creation and business model innovation. He did research in Automotive, Chemicals, High-Tech, Retail and Shoe industry. Daan takes active involvement in the development of new advices concerning the improvement of innovation at customers.
