Suw Charman-Anderson’s Summary

I work with businesses to help them understand and implement social software/Web 2.0 both internally for communication and collaboration, and externally for nurturing customer relations. I explain how most effectively to use blogs, wikis, IM, chat, RSS, aggregators, tagging, social bookmarking and other Web 2.0 services and tools. I provide a range of services, from writing high level strategy to management presentations to one-on-one coaching.

I have worked with a wide variety of businesses, from small PR companies like Jackie Cooper PR, to multinational investment banks like Dresdner Kleinwort Wasserstein, and pharmaceutical company Pfizer, both on-site or remotely.

I am happy to work on one-off engagements such as presentations to staff or board members, short-term projects such as getting staff trained and ready to blog, or longer term strategy/adoption projects.

Past clients include: Pfizer, NESTA, DrKW (now Dresdner Kleinwort), BBC, McKinsey, Oracle, Jackie Cooper PR, Holiday Extras, CHA, Band and Brown, Red Consultancy/MSN, Reuters, Fotango, Edelman/Cow & Gate, Bupa.
