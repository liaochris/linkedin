Brendan Ross’s Summary

Brendan Ross is the President of Ross Asset Advisors, Inc., a full service financial advisor specializing in index investing. RAA helps clients achieve competitive, long-term returns while controlling risk.

Previously, Brendan was President & CEO of Fanfare Media Works, a $65 million in-store advertising company with offices in the US and Canada.

Prior to Fanfare, Ross served as President of ReserveAmerica, a $28 million operating division of IAC (IACI).

Ross previously held positions at Mercer Management Consulting and in the strategy group at MediaOne Broadband (acquired by AT&T).

Ross graduated magna cum laude from Brown University with degrees in Sociology and Economics.
