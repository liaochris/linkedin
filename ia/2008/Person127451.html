Robert Preskill’s Summary

Robert Preskill works with professionals and companies in entertainment, media, publishing, video game and software licensing and development and leisure/restaurant franchises. He represents authors and markets media properties.

Preskill is licensed to practice in California and Illinois.

DETAILS
*Structures and negotiates deals for traditional (film, book, music) and non-traditional (gaming, animation, tech) entertainment/media companies.

*Counsels authors and artists seeking broader rights opportunities for publishing and production companies.

*Addresses protection of IP and drafting and filing requirements of licensing- or franchise- based businesses.

OVERVIEW

2000. Co-founded a West Coast based literary agency and sold several books (by award winning authors, best sellers and others) to major publishing houses.

2002. Formed a California law practice, described above.

2005. Opened a Chicago-based entertainment and media practice.

2007. Joined a corporate, securities boutique firm with technology and media industry leaders as clients.

ADDITIONAL

2003. Served on the board of Small Press Distribution, a 35-year non-profit serving small presses.

2003- present. Active panel member for California Lawyers for the Arts. Board member of Z Space Studio, a theater.

Robert Preskill’s Specialties:

technology and media representation, book publishing deals, movie deals, video game licensing, franchise law, multimedia agreements, corporate law, and trademark and copyright protection.
