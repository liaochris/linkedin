Kevin Ma’s Summary

I have more than eight years experience in the online arena, with two and a half years managing various online products. During the past two years, I have been in charge of overseeing online production, developing marketing strategies for various initiatives, and have been primarily responsible for seeing marketing strategies being implemented.

I work closely with producers and marketing managers to enhance online promotions for their marketing campaigns, media kits, dedicated e-mails and promotional microsites. I am responsible for assuring the integrity of our editorial products, while effectively marketing new media products such as Web 2.0 applications, web seminars, e-books, virtual trade shows, white papers and rich media advertisements. Working with these products allows me to interact with clients, internal personnel on both business and editorial sides, and vendors.

As a web developer with over six years of online development experience, including website design and marketing management, I oversaw the online operation of the New York Post websites from November 1998 to February 2004. Being one of two employees who established the company's interactive department, NYPOST.com became one of top news sites in the nation, with a 700% increase in traffic during my time with the company.

Additionally, as the former president and webmaster of the Ohio State Alumni Club of Greater New York, the local chapter evolved into the most active Ohio State alumni club in the nation. I graduated from The Ohio State University with a B.F.A. degree in Painting and Drawing in June 1997, which gave me knowledge of creativity and disciplines towards my works.

Overall, I have excellent communication skills, and am able to utilize resources and positively affect those I work with, and in turn, I thrive when working with others who are excited about their jobs. I am extremely well organized, detail-oriented, and have no trouble handling multiple tasks at once.
