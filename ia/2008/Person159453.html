Rhonda Lowry’s Summary

My focus in the media space is emerging technologies and markets surrounding virtual worlds, social computing and personal media. My diverse background and experience allows me to move between the technical, strategy and business worlds almost effortlessly. I'm a requested speaker about social media and making the transition to the new renaissance of digital and participatory media.

My personal mantra is: "vision, values, verve". I've had it posted over my office door/cubicle entrance as long as I can remember as it reinforces simply the things that are most important to me - the ability visualize the end game - the values to lead ethically and with purpose - and the verve to execute with shameless audacity. I've established my self as a collaborator, developer, systems thinker, integrator and executor of new technologies.

Rhonda Lowry’s Education
