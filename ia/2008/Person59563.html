Erica Campbell’s Summary

Erica Campbell has experience across multiple industries including automotive, real estate, heavy equipment, travel, sports and entertainment. Campbell joined Dominion Enterprises in August 2004 as the Marketing Coordinator for TruckTraderOnline.com and EquipmentTraderOnline.com. In this role, Campbell led the marketing efforts and assisted the sales force by creating media pieces, direct mail advertising campaigns and sales collateral.

Shortly thereafter, she was promoted to Marketing Manager to RentJillsHouse.com and RJHResorts.com, the vacation rentals portals in April 2005. In this role, Campbell was responsible for managing the SEM and SEO efforts, participation in large-scale corporate events and created budgets and business proposals.

After serving in this position, she was promoted to Marketing Manager of For Rent Media Solutions™ in May 2006. In this position, she develops, executes, optimizes and analyzes a public relations/social media strategic plan across multiple channels including blogs, social networks, video sharing sites, and other conversational media. She spends a great deal of time building relationships with social media influencers, bloggers and new consumer business to generate awareness of clients and products. Campbell is also responsible for managing public relations and internal marketing communications campaigns.

Prior to joining Dominion Enterprises, Campbell graduated Cum Laude from Old Dominion University in 2004 with a Sports Management degree with an emphasis on Sports Marketing and Promotions and a minor in Marketing. She also played on the women’s Division 1 soccer team and interned as a Marketing Assistant for Global Spectrum.

Campbell has attended the following conferences: Search Engine Strategies San Jose 06', Search Engine Strategies Chicago 06', Bruce Clay SEOToolSet Training 06', Search Engine Strategies New York 07', ad:tech New York 07', SMX Social Media Conference Long Beach 08'
