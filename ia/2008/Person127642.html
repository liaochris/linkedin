Meg Watt’s Summary

Between private and agency work, I have developed an extensive portfolio of experience and clients. I have a significant amount of expertise in the entertainment industry, and take a hands-on approach with all my accounts. I feel each person, business and industry is unique and should be treated as such, so I make sure that my clients know this.

In particular, I enjoy working with smaller businesses that are on the verge of significant growth. I think this point is one of the most instrumental ever in the development of a business and with the correct guidance, can prove fruitful and satisfying for the client. It's these clients that return; loyalty built on value, quality and mutual respect is not forgotten.

With years of writing experience, I am also a great source for copy. From press kits to releases, talking points to a full-fledged speech, I can help direct my clients to their best-possible presence.
