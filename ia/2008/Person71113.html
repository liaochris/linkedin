Eric Martin’s Summary

My primary daily activities involve building our warehousing business - DPL Corporation. We believe that more and more companies don't want to build, buy or lease their own warehouses. It's expensive, usually a poor use of business capital and often results in higher overhead expenses (thus, lower profit margins, which nobody wants). Rather, outsourcing to a professional warehouse 3PL company can help a business dedicate its resources to its core business - selling its products.

Send me an e-mail to see how we can help.
