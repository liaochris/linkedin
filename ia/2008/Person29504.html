Brian Ward’s Summary

Having six years Industrial Design experience in Silicon Valley California and the UK, a Masters degree in Industrial Design from Central Saint Martins College in London, I am adding a new area of design namely, Architectural Design to my repertoire.

I pursuing my independent projects involving Industrial, furniture, product and automotive design through my startup Industrial Design studio- Iterative Industrial Design.

Iterative Industrial Design is involved in Industrial/ Product Design projects Internationally and building a client base / body of work with clients and Design consultancies that harbor energetic/ creative people . Please do contact me if you are seeking an Industrial Design professional to work closely with and exceed your expectations.

Brian Ward’s Specialties:

Medical, Pharmaceutical, Furniture, consumer electronics, FMCG, white goods, design research, video ethnography, Interior, User interface, Aesthetic and functional product design: Material, sociological, cultural, lifestyle based design. Branding and Identity, Product Strategy.
