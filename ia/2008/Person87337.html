Jeff Hanna’s Summary

Jeff has been in the video game industry for over 12 years, working as a technical artist on titles including Warhammer Online: Age of Reckoning, Dark Age of Camelot, Planetside, and Air Warrior, all PC online titles. Along with his professional contributions to various companies including THQ, Electronic Arts, and Sony Online Entertainment Jeff has written magazine articles and worked with tools providers like Autodesk and ATI, along with numerous community sites such as http://www.scriptspot.com, to promote technical art and contribute tools and tutorials to help others in the industry.

In 2007 he was one of five Autodesk Max Master Award recipients. He is also the forum administrator for the Technical Art discussion on Autodesk's Area community site.

You can view his professional website at http://www.jeff-hanna.com/ and his blog at http://techartblog.jeff-hanna.com/
