Are you a high-energy, self-reflective person who wants to manage your career so that you're constantly doing more of the kind of work you love? I'd love to talk with you.

I help you make meaningful changes in your life and livelihood using out-of-the-box tools. What makes me a little different from other career coaches is that in addition to an MBA, I also have a PhD in somatic psychology. Simply put, that means I also deal with the emotional and psychological effects of career change and job loss.

If you are standing at a crossroads in your career, I can help you to gain clarity about what's next, and the courage to pursue a new, more fulfilling path. My specialties are:

(1) MAKING A MAJOR CAREER TRANSITION: (www.WorkFromWithin.com) Are you tired of feeling stuck, boxed in, or blocked in your life? Do you want to wake up each morning feeling excited and energized? Is it important to you to feel more authentic in all aspects of your life?

Interested in a potential coaching relationship? Contact me for a FREE "get acquainted" session: http://workfromwithin.com/assessment.php

(2) SUCCESSFUL JOB SEARCH (www.JobSearchGym.com):
Shouldn't your job search be easier and more enjoyable? Want to feel more confident and self-directed each day in your job search?

I offer webinars and group coaching programs for job seekers who not only want to write great resumes and interview effectively, but who also want to stay positive and motivated in their job search. Check out the Job Search Gym www.JobSearchGym.com
