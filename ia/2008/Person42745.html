Dana Ben David’s Summary

Founder and visionary of an innovative kids' virtual world startup with an urgent social agenda. We are developing a brand for kids, including cross platform applications as complimentary media, including a TV format. We have our focus is upon creativity and social responsibility.
I'm an Award winning creative director and branding expert, in the animation, motion graphics and SFX field. At JCS I was responsible for leading channel branding processes in Israel and in the international market, with emphasize on Chinese market, leading a team of 30 designers and animators through out all development and post production phases. I was also managing the commercials department, working with advertising agencies and interactive agencies, and mobile firms as complimentary media to the advertising campaign or TV channel. I was also responsible for exploring new fields and opportunities working hand in hand with the marketing director of the firm.
I'm also a consultant for start up companies, interactive companies, branding firms, post production and animation studios..
