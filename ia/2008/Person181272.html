Steve Donnelly’s Summary

Extensive HR/Recruiting experience with a strong background in recruiting for a wide variety of technical and non-technical skill sets. My industry experience includes e-commerce, travel & hospitality, consulting, staffing and software development. My previous experience and professional interests include recruiting trends, technologies and techniques, employee relations, and employment law.

Currently, I am the Sr. Manager, Human Resources at Match.com, a unique organization that is committed to bringing people together in relationships that bring meaning and depth to their lives. Prior to being with Match.com, I worked for over four years at Travelocity, both as a Technical Recruiter and a Sr. HR Generalist. In addition, I also spent time in the staffing and consulting industries, with Object Systems Group and ObjectSpace.

My team's role is to do for someone’s professional life what Match.com does for our customers’ personal lives. In doing so, our challenge is to find candidates with the skills and passion that make us successful. Ultimately, our goal is to find top notch talent that will allow Match.com to remain the market leader. If you are interested in working for a company with a great brand, fantastic people, generous benefits and a hip environment, let's talk.

Steve Donnelly’s Specialties:

Recruiting, compensation, benefits, strategic planning, employee relations, policy development, HR project management, applicant tracking systems, interviewing, sourcing, corporate career websites, job boards, hiring process improvement, employee referral programs, executive recruiting, international recruiting and recruitment advertising.
