Norman Katz, CFE’s Summary

Supply chain vendor compliance consulting focuses on helping client companies successfully implement and integrate the necessary technology (barcode labeling and EDI, and soon, RFID tagging) with the company's existing computer application software, as well as business process reengineering (modifications to the client's business operations procedures) and change management to meet the operational requirements of their trading partner customers.

Nationally published author & speaker on barcode, EDI, and supply chain vendor compliance < www.vendorcompliance.info >.

Business technology & operations assistance to turnaround management professionals in support of helping distressed companies.
See < www.turnaroundhelp.com > for more information.

Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE). Florida Private Investigator.

Convergence of business & technical skills + CFE + PI is:
< www.supplychainfraud.com > - the detection and minimization of fraud in the internal & external supply chain.

Suppy Chain Integrity / Goverance via the application of Sarbanes-Oxley compliance framework from COSO < www.supplychainsox.com >

Norman Katz, CFE’s Specialties:

Microsoft Access(r) and Microsoft Visual FoxPro(r). Detection & minimization of supply chain fraud through the use of technology & business operations changes.
