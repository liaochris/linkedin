Jerome Le Luel’s Summary

My experience mostly lies in consumer credit (credit cards and personal loans) across various geographies (UK, France, Spain, USA). I have held various positions with Capital One within the 'Marketing and Analysis' department, which is responsible for setting marketing, credit and pricing strategies. With Barclaycard, I now focus on optimising credit strategies for one of the largest credit card portfolios in Europe. I also have a good experience of business development, whether it is about starting activities in new countries, restructuring unprofitable businesses or selling non-strategic assets.

People say I have excellent analytical skills, strong strategic thinking and an effective leadership. I like to work with sharp and open-minded people who like innovation and risk taking.

Jerome Le Luel’s Specialties:

I have deep expertise in credit risk management: data-wharehouse design, datamining, customer behaviour modelling, scorecards development, risk forecasting, credit policy set up, risk operations optimisation.
I am also an experienced marketer, with deep knowledge in market research and segmentation, propositions development, targeting, direct marketing and telesales. I am very familiar with customer acquisition, cross-sells and retention.
