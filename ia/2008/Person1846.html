Andrei Burz-Pinzaru’s Summary

Andrei specializes in the Romanian finance and securities laws and regulations (over 10 years of experience in financial services industry). Has been a manager with Deloitte since 2002. Before joining Deloitte, worked for two years with Ernst&Young and its affiliated law firm and previously for three years as compliance officer and securities broker. Has a law degree (LLB) and a Bachelor of Science degree, and currently undertakes an LLM in International Legal Practice with The College of Law in London. Contributed to the drafting of financial legislation (mortgage lending law, mortgage bonds and securitisation, securities regulations).

Financial transactions: During 2004 - 2008 negotiated (either on lenders’ or on borrowers’ side) EUR1billion loan and security documentation in syndicated and bilateral loan agreements, Leveraged Management Buy-Out, project finance, real estate acquisition finance / refinancing, leasing finance.

M&A in Financial Services Industry: During 2006 - 2008 assisted in relation to the acquisition of several banks / non banking financial institutions. Among others, participated in the due diligence on three banks, the largest mortgage lending company in Romania, the largest leasing company in Romania and one of the largest consumer credit companies in Romania

Financial Services Industry regulatory/compliance: assistance to local and international banks / non-banking financial institutions, insurance companies, fund management companies and publicly traded companies in relation to financial and securities regulatory matters.
