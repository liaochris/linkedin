Paul Geurts’s Summary

I graduated from the Utrecht Graduate School of Art in 2007, a BA with honours in Interaction Design and a MA in Interactive multimedia. I studied abroad for 6 months at the University of New South Wales in Sydney Australia.

After my studies, I founded Postmachina together with Arjen Sondag and Renato Valdés Olmos. We are a multidisciplinary studio with firm roots in Interactive Design. We work for a broad variety of clients, ranging from research foundations to insurance companies. We established a sound reputation in a short period of time. We will do everything within our powers to keep our studio a reliable partner.

Within our design studio we are developing E, E originally started as our graduation project and is now becoming a company on its own. I love the diversity of my position as a manager and a designer within postmachina. E is a tool for social networking in the real world. For more information on the company check out www.hellomynameise.com.

Next to my commercial activities I am a member of the exam committee for BA Interaction Design students. A member of a discussion group of bright people and myself called Dfir. Dfir is a group where we all share our views on the emerging ambient intelligence economy and the impact of the implementation of RFID.
