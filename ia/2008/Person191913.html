Jan Sather’s Summary

Why I am here on LinkedIn:

I am seeking opportunities to provide Executive Search services to help two kinds of clients: the first is managed by visionary executives who have been very successful to date, but know that they can only continue that success by continuing to hire top talent; the second has not been as fortunate as the first, but through introspection have developed the vision that they have hired the wrong people and now know they must upgrade the level of talent in their company. Both of those clients have recognized the value of using search organizations with a track record for helping companies grow through the infusion of top talent.

With 34 years of experience I have learned how to research, qualify and validate "top performing" talent. I have experience in many industries-my best customer grow from $22 million in sales to $400 million by finding 66 "top performers" for them. That customer proved the value of cross-breeding top talent from other industries to fuel their growth to $400 million with new philosophies and technologies

I am a well-known search consultant, personality assessment specialist, and business leader in Colorado and elsewhere that has built a long-term track record with a network of many key executives in business and technology. My firm has accumulated resumes on more than 25,000 individuals and market intelligence on over 5,000 companies in our database of contacts. I am also networked with over 400 other search firms around the world employing more than 2,500 recruiters in disciplines such as: sales/marketing, engineering, information technology, accounting/finance, healthcare, banking. chemical process and petrochemical, and manufacturing specialties.

I am also very interested in meeting exceptional individuals with executive, management, and high level technical skills (including sales/marketing) who may be interested in working for our growth motivated, visionary clients.
