Dominic McGrath’s Summary

Educated in UK, after 10 year military technical career in various countries, settled in Germany and became technically involved in launch of world's first GSM mobile phone network D2. After some years became consultant trouble-shooter and formed my own consulting company. I consulted in Holland for PTT telecom and exclusively Ericsson in Italy for 11 years during startup of various GSM and UMTS networks. Most recently I have been consulting at Ericsson Spain and on the business side my company is a supplier of other consultants to different Ericsson markets.

During my trouble-shooting career, I have met many competent consultants and others completely incompetent. It is my immediate business aim to only supply top-class niche consultants to Ericsson at the same time as giving consultants the best financial deal as opposed to the many shark agencies out there. As I am still a technical consultant myself I have a much better chance of understanding Ericsson's needs than the usual agents.
