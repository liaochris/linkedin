Pete King’s Summary

Detailed knowledge of end-to-end radio systems, radio transceiver design, RF circuit design, antennas and radio propagation modelling & characterisation for aircraft, satellite, mobile and wireless communication systems.

Extensive experience of management of large multi-national EU and UK research projects.

Pete King’s Specialties:

Advanced studies, advanced radio development, academic research, research project management
