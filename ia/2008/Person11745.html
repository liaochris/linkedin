Andrew Liberale’s Summary

Andrew Liberale has an international pedigree in Project & Management Consultancy, Business Transformation and Service Delivery Improvement working with some of the world’s largest and most successful companies including EDS, IBM, Microsoft, Commonwealth Bank, QBE Insurance, Tower Insurance Group, Pfizer Global Pharmaceuticals, AAPT, TCNZ, Telstra, Optus, IAG, Caltex, Integral Energy, SOCOG, Barclays BZW, Sony Broadcast International, Greenpeace & ATO (Australian Taxation Office) on key core business initiatives.

His value is maximized as a change agent especially when engaged to work with business leaders to create effective core business improvements end-to-end involving both people and technology. These activities include business modeling and strategy, implementation of new, unique and business critical systems, customer service improvements including marketing strategy and contracts, service delivery improvements including workflows, delivery team restructures and rollouts of new technologies.

Passionate about personal & professional development he holds many accreditations including Master NLP Trainer, Business Improvement Specialist, Executive Coach, Performance Consultant, Professional Speaker & Presenter and facilitates personal development programs and workshops for individuals, consultants and teams.
