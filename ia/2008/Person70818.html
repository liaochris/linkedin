Glenn Murray’s Summary

Glenn has considerable experience in the establishment, funding, and development of commercial real estate outsourced services and technology companies. Over 18 years, he has invented entirely new business models and led them into substantial ventures on behalf of world class clients and investors. Glenn has worked on behalf of many of the most recognized names in real estate and has extensive experience in real estate technology solutions, property management operations, finance, outsourcing, financial processing, cash management, information systems management, software solution design, sales, and tier one customer management. He has written numerous whitepapers, is a regular speaker at industry events, and has been an active member of numerous industry organizations.

Glenn Murray’s Specialties:

C-level sales; ground up company development; deal structuring and negotiating; ROI analysis; product management and development; tier one customer relationship management.
