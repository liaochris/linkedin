Lars D. Pedersen, Ph.D.’s Summary

Creative and innovative achiever with broad-based experience in general management, sales management, and technology management.

High-integrity, energetic leader known for ability to envision and create successful outcomes in complex situations. Diverse functional and industry background with experience from Telecommunications, Oil & Gas, Investment Banking and Defense & Security industries.
