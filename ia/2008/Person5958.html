Álex Casanovas Ortego’s Summary

First, I'm an entrepreneur and man of action: "It's wonderful making things happen and bringing ideas to life. It's a kind of magic".

Second, I'm an exec with broad experience in business and IT alignment, team development, IT related business development and operations management.
After having worked in different companies - big and small ones - performing almost every kind of job, in 2004 I started up my first company, apser. At the end of 2007 we had almost multiplied the revenue by ten. However, the big momentum is still to come... It's near, I can feel it...

I've studied a lot and attended lots of courses and seminars, though I have developed the skills I find more useful through 12 years of basketball coaching and 9 years of amateur comedy acting.

apser delivers ICT Infrastructure Management services, Data Center Consolidation Services, SaaS enabled platforms and IT Service Management education, consulting and tool implementation services.

"IT is a fantastic tool, not an aim.
IT professionals must lead and enable: not just run and follow.
CEOs cannot abdicate of their responsibility over IT Governance."
