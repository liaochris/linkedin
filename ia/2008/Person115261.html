Lois Ryan (lois.ryan@saccomann.com)’s Summary

Working alongside Pete Fellows, in the Patent and Trademark Division at SaccoMann, on both a national and international level.
I work with clients and candidates alike who are seeking placement in either the UK or France/Francophone countries. I place qualified and part-qualified Patent and Trademark Attorneys within In-House and Private Practice firms.

Sacco Mann is a management and recruitment consultancy. We are not only able to help our candidates find a new role but can also improve your career prospects via our range of candidate support programmes. We can improve interview technique (training on competency/behavioural interviewing, general HR strategies, etc.); improve negotiation strategy (particularly useful for renegotiating salary) and help develop your networking skills.

We also work with firms to improve their selection strategy such as Partner training on interview techniques, how to market your firm to prospective candidates and how to retain your existing staff.

Many of our additional services are offered free of charge to our clients and candidates. For more information on this please contact me.
