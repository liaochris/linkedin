James Halley’s Summary

Senior account manager, specializing in systems integration and business process improvements; extensive successful sales leadership experience serving regional and national companies in public and private sectors; recognized for client advocacy approach to sales and marketing of cutting edge technologies; insightful analysis of business trends and development of customer loyalty.

Mr. Halley’s primary focus has been consulting in “convergence technologies” with a high concentration on business process improvements through root cause analysis and solution mapping. He believes in a “Client Advocacy” approach which enables him to understand Clients business, their current processes, where they are going and the challenges to reach those goals. His “business assessment” methodology has been beneficial to his Clients while they try to optimize their current technology investments and migrate towards secure enterprise virtualized networks.
