Richard John Jenkins’s Summary

Search Engine Marketing and Optimization specialist which includes setting up Pay Per Click (PPC) ad campaigns for clients using Yahoo Search Marketing (recently upgraded to "Panama"), MSN AdCenter, Google Adwords, AdSense, Analytics, and CheckOut.

Simply put, I help clients get their web sites and PPC ads ranking at the top of the list in an organic or paid placement search results and train them in all aspects of SEO and SEM.

My services are affordable, results driven with great attention to detail. Dozens of my clients have achieved page 1 ranking in the organic/natural listings across all the major search engines - no easy feat.

I specialize in the top three Pay Per Click systems: MSN AdCenter, Yahoo Search Marketing (Panama), and Google AdWords including, Google AdSense, Analytics, and CheckOut.

Your web site isn't showing up in search or has no traffic or sales? Stop by my site, www.WebSearchEngineer.com or give a call at (323) 428-1717 and learn why.

My background is in the Film, Multimedia, Web Publishing Tools, Technology Training, and Internet Marketing industries.

Technology training is another area I have extensive expertise in. I have been a trainer for over a decade focusing on web site creation tools, most notably Adobe/Macromedia Corporation’s award winning software tools: Captivate, Dreamweaver, Flash, Director, Fireworks, Acrobat Connect, and Contribute.

I have taught over 38,000 people how to use Macromedia's tools (now Adobe Systems) at over two hundred fifty schools, twenty five user groups, one hundred thirty seven companies, plus presenting at dozens and dozens of public seminars, workshops, webinars, webcast, and speaking at many top industry trade shows all over the U.S. and Canada.

Business Development comes quite naturally to me too and is also one of my major talents. I was one of Corel Corporation's top Business Development Managers where I resurrected their entire Education markets within one year.
