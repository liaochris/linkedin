Misa & Rose Chavez’s Summary

Misa and Rose Chavez are a husband/wife team with over 16 years experience in Real Estate, Contracting, and Business Management.

Misa is extremely motivated with a contagious energy that is sure to affect everyone in his path. He is a man of action and vision is full of innovative ideas.

Prior to relocating to his native country, Puerto Vallarta, Mexico, Misa was part owner of Novia Enterprises, LLC and CMV Properties, Inc in Washington State. He was in charge of Sales, Estimating, Customer Relations and Project Management. For the last 5 years he worked directly with Real Estate Investors, Realtors, General Contractors and Developers in the Real Estate Industry, assisting with locating, evaluating, and rehabbing both residential and commercial properties.

Rose has been in charge of Bookkeeping, Contract Administration, Office Management and Marketing for the above companies. She is organized, detailed, honest and professional. She enjoys assisting her husband and others in Business Management and Marketing. Besides building great Customer/Client relationships she is also great with research, data & finance analysis and technical & creative writing.

Misa and Rose's combined expertise, passion and team work create Puerto Vallarta's Real Estate Team that is bound to bring you success!
