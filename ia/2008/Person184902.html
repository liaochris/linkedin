Terry Bazemore’s Summary

As an information technology professional, I have operated successfully in a number of roles and capacities including IT Manager, Senior Enterprise Architect, Senior Software Engineer, Integration Architect, IT Consultant, IT Project Manager, CEO of a small software engineering and consulting firm, etc.

Senior Enterprise Architect
- Led proof of concept and software selection projects for Enterprise Integration, SOA and Service Infrastructure solutions. Recent proof-of-concept lead to decision for BEA's Aqualogic Service Bus to implement an Enterprise Service Bus Architecture Solution. Involved in Business to technology solution mapping and technical proposal management.

-System Administration - Led architecture design and implemention of fault tolerant, high availability web application server environments using Weblogic 8.1 and 9.1.

Manager and Project Manager
- Provided process oriented leadership to cross disciplined teams (that included developers, business analyst, DBAs, technical leads, testers and consultants etc.) in both the development and maintenance of enterprise software systems and packaged solutions.

- My management and leadership philosophy is grounded in the belief that "A rising tide lifts all boats". If you raise the skill of the individuals on the team, helping each to envision his or her success, the team, the project and the organization benefit.

Developer - I have performed analysis, design, development, testing and maintenance of complex high availability distributed solutions that were implemented on various technologies and platforms.

I am driven to continuous personal growth as I am constantly grooming my view of the cutting edge and future state of technology.
