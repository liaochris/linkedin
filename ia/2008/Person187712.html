Todd Gardner’s Summary

I have served many roles within the realm of Information Technology while gaining experience in many elements of the environment. This broad range of knowledge has positioned me for my current role as a Solutions Architect.

I am well respected for my strategic planning and logical troubleshooting, as well as presenting myself and my ideas in a persuasive manner. I have and demonstrate the following skills in my career as an IT architect:

LEADERSHIP - Quickly take charge of a project or task and drive it to completion. Promote new ideas and services throughout the enterprise.

COMMUNICATIONS - Able to quickly and accurately communicate ideas and concepts to others through training and presentations. Explain the most advanced IT concepts into business terms.

STRATEGY - Begin every project and architecture with a strategic target with considerations to changes in business needs and technology.

PROCESS AND TACTICS - Quickly understand and translate business requirements in IT architectures, deliverables, processes, and service level agreements. "Visio Wizard", Able to quickly and precisely document a project's architecture for fast development.

TECHNOLOGY DEPTH - I have an extensive knowledge in the enterprise server space, specializing in server hardware, Linux and Windows Servers, MS Exchange, MS SharePoint, MS Active Directory, and VMWare Infrastructure

TECHNOLOGY BREADTH - I have experience across all layers of the IT environment including data-center and environmental, storage systems, networks and routing, database administration, middleware platforms, application development, and service management.

My career goals are to broaden and enhance my experience and move into a Chief Architect or Technology Officer role in an organization. I also want to share my knowledge through a teaching role in a University setting.

Business Process Analysis and Improvement, IT Architecture, Infrastructure Architecture, Systems Administration, IT Research and Development, Presentation and Communications, Independent thinker and motivator.
