Howard R. Roitman’s Summary

Personal Injury and Estate Planning make up most of my practice today.
Most of my litigation involves negligence cases where compensation for injuries of a victim is called for. Personal injury is a harm caused to a victim, for instance a broken bone, a cut, or a bruise; bodily injury. Largely Personal injury is cased by car crashes, car accidents or slip, trip and falls. Personal injury also includes victims hurt because of mental suffering, false imprisonment, libel, Slander false arrest.
A Nevada personal injury attorney can file a claim for any kind of bodily injury, sickness, disease, or death of a victim where a person, government or government entity may be at fault in Nevada. The first step in a possible personal injury is figuring out who is to blame for the injury next how bad are the victims injuries, in other words after all the pain suffering lost wages etc. are added together what did the victim lose. I try to help people with questions and provide the guidance situations like this. I am also licensed in New York, California and the District of Columbia.
Estate Planning
As an estate planner I help people with the acquisition, preservation, and ultimate disposition of their wealth.
