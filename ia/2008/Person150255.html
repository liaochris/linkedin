Peter Schmid’s Summary

Netbiscuits operates the world’s leading B2B web software platform for development, publishing and advertising in the Mobile Internet. The platform serves the global Mobile Internet programs of premium brands such as eBay, Yahoo, AOL, BILD, Spiegel, RTL, Konami or Sixt. In Germany, Netbiscuits is market leader, serving 80 % of all off-deck Mobile Internet traffic generated by the major web brands. Globally, the Netbiscuits™ platform delivers more than 1 billion optimized page impressions and content items each month to more than 5,000 mobile devices in more than 40 countries.

As part of the Netbiscuits™ Partner Network, worldwide leading agencies such as Avenue A | Razorfish, AKQA, Ogilvy or companies of Omnicom Group use the Netbiscuits™ platform to provide multinational Mobile Internet campaigns to major consumer brand customer such as Coca Cola, McDonalds or BMW.

Netbiscuits™ is provided as a web service in form of an open and standardized software framework to allow any business to take advantage of the world’s most advanced Mobile Internet technologies. The framework is comprised of a mobile developer SDK, a mobile content & ad adaptation Web API, a mobile sitebuilder and a global service management and delivery platform for professional mobile websites & campaigns.

With Netbiscuits™, content & media publishers, marketing & advertising agencies, application developers, and service providers can achieve a faster path to profit from their Mobile Internet programs.
