Hein Hoek’s Summary

I am Hein Hoek of Philocon Philosophical Consultancy.

As an organisational philosopher I resolve tensions between people in organizations, I make people effectively cooperate and I lead organizational changes. And I coach the boss.

The instrument I use is the "Socratic Method".
This is a very effective method, taken from Socrates, for individuals in the shape of Socratic coaching or for groups as a Socratic dialogue.

The characteristical part is asking the right questions in a critical and profound way.
Because a good question makes you think!
And thinking leads to insight.
Thus arise solutions from within the organization, from yourself and your people.

Summed up in one single phrase:
As an organisational philosopher, by asking critical questions I make people in organizations reflect, whereby they, helped by the self-generated insight, can reach their own solutions.

Hein Hoek’s Specialties:

Socratic dialogue, philosophy of organization, philosophical counselling, logical reasoning, dilemmatraining, mediation, philosophical thought, reflection, coaching, teaching abilities and competencies, problemsolving.
