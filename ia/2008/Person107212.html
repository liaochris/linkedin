Kirk Henry’s Summary

I am a Creative Director from NYC with over 12 years print design experience and 10 years interactive design and development experience.

I take global brands from concept to completion in the interactive space by analyzing emerging technologies and trends to utilize them as a communications vehicle for creating brand awareness, product awareness and revenue.

My client list includes:
The Limited Brands, FedEx Corporation, Elektra Entertainment Group, Xerox, Jive Records, Nokia, Bertelsmann Media Worldwide, British Telecommunications, Vonage, Hilton Family of Hotels, IBM, Viking Range Corporation, HP, Dell, Motorola, Cellular South, Random House, Blockbuster Video, MSNBC, Microsoft, Gateway, Sun Microsystems, Hotwire.com, CNET and more.
