Gretchen Hidell’s Summary

I service the entire NW portion of Vermont (Chittenden, Franklin, Grand Isle, Addison, Lamoille & Washington Counties).

Real Estate is the largest financial asset that you can own. You need to work with a Realtor you can trust. I have made Real Estate a career for 7 years. Whether you are buying or selling I can calmly and competently guide you through the process.

As a Sellers Agent I will: market your home with print ads & web exposure, organize & arrange showings, provide you with feedback, provide updates on the market in your neighborhood, provide you with a complimentary Market Analysis including comparable properties for accurate pricing, and so much more!

As a Buyers Agent I will: provide you with real estate listings directly from the MLS, organize and arrange showings of properties, make very detailed notes on what you like and don't like to help match you with the right property, do the negotiating & discuss terms/conditions of the offer, provide you with a complimentary Buyer Consultation to review the paperwork & answer questions, and so much more!

Please call today to setup a free, BUYER CONSULTATION or MARKET ANALYSIS!!

Helping People Realize Their Dreams Through Home Ownership!!
