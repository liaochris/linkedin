Leon Fortin Jr.’s Summary

Recognizing that proper patenting is a complicated business, I apply my knowledge and skills to develop strategies that integrate with, and in many cases inform, my clients' overall business plans. Since I am a creative technologist, accomplished business leader, and a patent professional, my broad-based approach often results in unexpected insights that reveal critical patent opportunities in my clients’ IP. I often work across functions within my client's organization with senior management, engineering, product development, legal and sales to form a unified IP strategy of: IP protection, monetizing valuable IP assets, and building a robust IP portfolio of patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets.

My experience with IP spans a broad spectrum including technology area such as Computer Network Security, e-Commerce Payment Methods, On-line Educational Software, Publishing & Software, Wireless Telecommunications, 3D Image Acquisition & Positioning, Optics, Lighting, LED Controls, RSS, Secure Transaction Platforms, Optical Storage Technologies, Digital Video Sharing, Automatic Adjustable Beds, Semiconductor Manufacturing & Robots, On-line User Behavior Analysis, Anti-Piracy Solutions, Electronic Linking to Cited, Published Materials, Photovoltaics, Insurance & Retirement Products, Global Enterprise Software, Consumer Goods, Industrial Computer Hardware, Critical Care Medical Devices, User Interface Methods, and Consumer Plastics.

My wide variety of IP projects includes patent drafting, claimset development, claim drafting, IP strategy development and execution, patent valuation, due diligence, file history review and analysis, office action reply preparation, accelerated examination petition preparation, patentability analysis, prior art searching and analysis, infringement and obviousness analysis, court case research, non-patent searching and search management, application filing, assignments, and freedom to operate assessment and guidelines.
