Nathan Ulery’s Summary

Nathan A. Ulery is an Infrastructure Architect in West Monroe Partner’s Technical Infrastructure practice. He is responsible for and involved in a wide range of consulting assignments covering business and IT strategies while focusing on infrastructure solutions. Nate has been teaming with clients in integrating their business and information needs with innovative technology solutions.

Nate’s key skills include infrastructure architecture, design, implementation, and troubleshooting. With both networking and programming skills, Nate can play a critical role in working with both application development and infrastructure teams to facilitate successful application implementations.

Besides his client responsibilies, Nate is also responsible for overseeing the company’s internal infrastructure components including the converged voice and data network.

Prior to joining West Monroe Partners, Mr. Ulery was employed by two Big 5 professional services firms as a consultant and infrastructure architect. Nate holds a bachelor’s degree in Liberal Arts from DePauw University in Greencastle, Indiana, where he graduated magna cum laude with distinction. He studied Computer Science and Economics. As a Management Fellow, Nate developed skills in accounting, economics, and management. Nate has also earned multiple industry certifications.

Infrastructure Architect, Migration Planning, System Design, Application Performance Assessments.
