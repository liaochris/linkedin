Kendall Allen’s Summary

On an evolved path from print to digital media, from publisher to agency digital marketing services, Allen brings 15+ years from the front. Allen spent years covering emergent media + tech in the SF Bay Area. Moving from print to interactive during its most formative years, Allen worked on the founding team of Thrive, an AOL/Time Inc. JV in Health and Wellness, ultimately leading consumer marketing and distribution for the company, after helping establish the original content and community infrastructure.

Then moving to the agency side, management posts included Business Development and Client Services principal roles for USWeb/CKS and Euro RSCG Circle, where she served as VP Strategic Services and GM for the West and its practices in digital technology, marketing and design.

In the three years before her joining the team at Incognito Digital, Allen ran regional Client Services for two Search Engine Marketing firms, as founding executive team member at Fathom and then industry pioneer, iCrossing. It is during these years that she met the founders of IndustryNext and Incognito Digital, collaborating as partner firms during earliest iterations of these companies. She joined them for several years to build out the agency, a post she recently finished.

With a career spanning time in the Bay Area and New York, Allen has guided clients and teams for Ask Jeeves, SF Examiner, Chandon, Intel, Intuit, VOOM, American Express, Fairmont Hotels, McGraw-Hill and Colgate-Palmolive. Allen has spent the last few years bringing an agency from its nascent founding state to a respected entity in the marketplace with clients and partners everywhere. Her clients included Bloomberg LP, Haier America, and New York Sun.

She continues to work with media co's, clients and partners, consulting on digital marketing and convergence media. In October, she will assume a new executive post, spearheading the digital agency for a public company and diversified marketing group.

Kendall Allen’s Specialties:

Blended management profile, with significant business background on agency- and publisher-sides of the media business. Stellar reputation with reports, clients, partners and within industry. Long-term business operations and total client engagement orientation. Avid connector with a broad and deep business universe. Converged media background. Strong, balanced integrated marketing services orientation, due to progression from print to digital, in various capacities, during industry evolution.
