Simon Austin’s Summary

Big Cafe is the largest Chain of internet cafes in the world though only based in China. The cafes are unique in the fact that they are from 150 seat right up to 2200 seat. My job is to maximise the growth and profitability of the company in the following revenue streams Merchandising (everything from confectionary, drinks, electronics & FMCG), Media, Financial Services (our own credit card), Content Acquisition & exploitation, Equipment procurement (we buy 200,000 to 750,000 pc's annually) and research & data gathering from our 51 million registered users. The above makes Big Cafe the laregest 18-34 media channel in China.

We have just acquired some even bigger estates than Big Cafe which I will announce here in about six weeks

Before Big Cafe I ran a small boutique media company which acquired the exclusive rights to the above revenue streams from big cafe for 20 years by forming an exclusive joint venture with them.

Before that I managed 42 artists on mp3.com and became the first music artist manager for digital music. Indeed my artists accounted for tens of millions of downloads before i-tunes ever existed.

Before that I was sales director for the world's largest concert and event lighting and production company. I grew the company from a turnover of £200,000 to £25m in 10 years followed by a successful exit to Christian Salvesen



My experience is extensive in the following sectors: Internet, Media, Music, TV, IPR & Content, Live Concert production, Lighting / audio / AV.

My goal is to be excellent and the best at everything that I undertake.
