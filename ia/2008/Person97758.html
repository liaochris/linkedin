Joseph Wortmann’s Summary

I am an executive with a breadth of experience in operations, sales, marketing, technology, and product development. I specialize in innovation and change leadership. I have success as both an entrepreneur and intrapreneur. I relish the opportunity to make a real difference, both in the success of the corporation and in the lives of the people I interact with.

I am a self-starter that neither requires nor desires large amounts of oversight. I enjoy working at small companies where flexibility (chaos?) reigns, and have spent my time working at and founding start-ups since I left the Army. I find the dynamic environment offered by a small organization invigorating.

I have worked in the defense, insurance, telecommunications, information services and medical industries. Of these, I have enjoyed the medical industry the most; the products in the medical industry truly impact the lives of those that use them, often at the time of their greatest need.
