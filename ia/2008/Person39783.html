Jeff SKI Kinsey’s Summary

Producer of Results. Solver of Problems. Mentor.

Jeff 'SKI' Kinsey wrote a business leadership manuscript in late 2003, and after much effort chasing traditional publishers (and collecting rejection slips), decided to self-publish in April 2004. The book? "Purple Curve Effect" with Thayer Bennett.

The book has led to a number of adventures, as SKI helps the business owner align goals and missions statements, in order to produce the desired results.
