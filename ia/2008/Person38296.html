Claudio De Falco’s Summary

I have been in publishing since 1980.
At the beginning as a journalist skilled in information technology, later on as editor in chief and editorial director of several magazines.
In year 2000 I have been appointed publisher of Information Technology magazines of VNU Business Media Italy. In addition to this role in year 2002 I have been appointed publisher of Pubblicità Italia, a business unit of VNU Italia which published magazines specialized in advertising and marketing.
In these jobs I’ve been in charge of print and online activities.
Currently, I’m a contributor to Il Sole 24 Ore Business Media, a business unit of Il Sole 24 Ore financial daily paper. In this role I’m the editorial coordinator of .Ict&tech solutions (http://www.puntoict.ilsole24ore.com), a monthly magazine, and I manage a blog related to It security (http://blog.01net.it/?cat=57).
