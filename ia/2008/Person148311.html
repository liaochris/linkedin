Paul Turec’s Summary

I currently lead the Freightquote.com parcel division with responsibilites of continued growth of our parcel revenue stream, sales management, recruiting, hiring, working with IT to develop tools for our sales reps to be more productive and to provide our customers with technology allowing seamless integration of Freightquote services into their current supply chain. Building parcel brand awareness among our current customer base and new markets.

Partnered with DHL we will continue to offer innovative technology and supply chain solutions for a new group of customers currently not being serviced through our freight divisions.

Freightquote.com® is a leading provider of web-based freight transportation management services for small businesses. Our carrier comparison and shipment management solutions help customers simplify freight shipping and manage their transportation spend.

Based in Kansas City, Freightquote.com and our subsidiaries serve customers across North America and Europe. The company has over 500 employees and annualized 2006 revenues exceeding $200 million.
