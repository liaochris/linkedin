Lisa Hughes’s Summary

Spectrum Creative Services was initially set up with the intention of providing an in-house design and marketing service to Spectrum Financial Group, their sub-companies, affinity partners and introducers. However, soon after they established they decided to extend their services externally for everyone.

Set in amongst the greenery of Pulford (but close enough to the hustle and bustle of Chester), they're a small team with a passion and flair for design.

They live and breathe it.

They're proud of their strong customer relations and do their upmost to satisfy their clients.

Members of the team are fully qualified and have an extensive range of experience in graphic design, print and marketing. Also, all have previous experience in working within the financial services sector as well as an impressive list of client from various other industries.
