Jon Mell’s Summary

Have joined Trovus with primary responsibility for their consulting business around Web 2.0 but also an interest in growing the overall business. I have a passionate interest in the effect new Web 2.0 communication tools will have on the way companies will operate and interact with staff, customers and partners in the future. Trovus works with companies to provide them with a web strategy which focuses on conversation and dialogue rather than one-way communication.

I am also an experienced basketball coach with an interest in team building and elite team performance both in sports and in the workplace.

Jon Mell’s Specialties:

Portfolio of skills and experience across management, sales and technology. Strong presentation skills and an ability to explain technical concepts in terms of business impact. Can debate the technical merits of a solution as well as present a business case to the board. Experience of running teams (organisations of up to 25 people) at first and second line management level.
