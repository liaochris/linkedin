Arlette Melgar’s Summary

I am fortunate to have handled several management functions throughout my career. Even better, my exposure in management functions is across all sectors - private, public and nonprofit (foreign-funded). I have even tried being a full-time university instructor for one semester, and taught on part-time basis at different institutions.

As managing consultant of a very successful enterprise with 90 employees now, I am able to integrate all my management experiences. While at this, I also provide business consulting services to other entities. The goal at this point in my life is to learn to be a very good entrepreneur, which is the ultimate test of one's management skills.
