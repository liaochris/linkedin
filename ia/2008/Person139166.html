Nick Christensen’s Summary

Newly minted city planner endlessly taken with big cities and their demons. Particularly interested in transit policy and how it relates to land use and economic development.

I have experience analyzing policy and communicating recommendations as well as with the nuts and bolts of budgeting and financing projects. I know how to use ArcMap GIS to answer geospatial questions and solve problems. Being multilingual and having overseas experience, I'm also eager to explore how planning can be applied as a tool for international development.
