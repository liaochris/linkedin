Ben Winegarden’s Summary

Ben Winegarden is working with Accenture in financial services out of the Charlotte office. He has been working with SAP for three years with experience in several SAP Core Banking roles. Ben has worked internationally with Accenture for SAP BPP Lending, Deposits and Leasing in South Africa, Germany, Belgium, Singapore, and Indonesia.

Roles include, BPP configuration lead, writing Leasing MRD's, a DM POC at Bank Danamon, testing DM 2005, building SAP model bank assets and demos, developing an SAP DM 4.0 configuration guide, and supporting the SAP NA Core Banking sales team with RFP responses, presentations, and project estimation for BPP, DM, LM, CMS, BP, and MCM.


Selected Experience:

SAP BPP Charter Client Lending Implementation for South African Bank

Ben planned and lead configuration team through the design, build, and testing of SAP‘s BPP for the Lending portion of a core banking system replacement. He also managed the scope and coordination of 10 resources on the configuration team, maximizing resource utilization to deliver according to scope and QPI quality expectations. The initial design and configuration was done on DM and then transported to the first release of BPP where the final build was completed.


SAP POC for Indonesian Bank and Singaporean Holding Company

As a presenter, SME, and configuration expert, Ben led a Proof of Concept demonstration of the SAP DM functions to the business owners at the bank and the executives of the bank’s Singaporean holding company and at Bank Danamon's headquarters in Jakarta, Indonesia. After a month of preparation, the actual demonstration lasted for two weeks in which Ben made live demonstrations of the system’s functions with on-the-fly configuration per the client’s instructions. This included solo presentations for up to four hours at a time to an international audience.
