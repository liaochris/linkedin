Bob Grove’s Summary

Public relations and marketing specialist with expertise in tourism, recreation and hospitality services. Thirty years experience promoting and marketing destinations throughout the Intermountain West from resorts and hotels to outfitters and tour companies.

Tourism entrepreneur, started niche travel business in 1993 called DriveAway Vacations that assists travelers with information on places to go, things to do and sights to see within a days drive of Salt Lake City. We've been marketing staycations long before it became the hype of this summer's travel chatter.

Tour planner assisting national and regional tour companies plan FIT and group tours throughout the national parks of the west with emphasis on the Intermountain area, Grand Circle and Heart of the Rockies travel regions.

Tour director with 18 years experience taking groups on tours to the national parks and recreation areas of the west.

Freelance travel writer. Have written articles on road trips and soft adventure travel. Former travel editor at Sports Guide Magazine in Salt Lake City.

Frequent guest on KSL Utah Outdoors radio show and KSL TV's Studio 5 daytime talk show.

Bob Grove’s Specialties:

Small business public relations and marketing consultant. Press release writing, company spokesperson, media outreach, marketing plan development and execution. website design, social networking, blogging, web 2.0.
