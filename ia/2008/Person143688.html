Oleg Lagodiyenko’s Summary

I am a consultant in PwC Ukraine’s Performance Improvement Consulting, Financial Services Group. I have over two years of experience in consulting, audit, risk management, financial analysis and modeling working both with governmental institutions and private companies in Ukraine.

Currently, most of my projects directly relate to management consulting in Financial Services industry (real estate and development, investments and banking).
They cover:
- effectiveness diagnostics from different standpoints (organizational structure, operating model, management and operational efficiency, costs, strategy implementation);
- business process engineering;
- as is analysis;
- best practices adoption;
- ratio analysis;
- guidelines elaboration.

My industry experience includes automobile, banking, telecommunications, real estate, chemical, agriculture, oil and gas industry and metallurgy, where I participated in audit engagements, consolidation of financial reporting, spreadsheet modeling, calculation of provisions using discounted cash flow techniques as well as advising on industry developments and trends to major national and foreign corporations.

I am graduate of Kyiv National University of Economics and Taras Shevchenko Kyiv National University and ACCA student.
