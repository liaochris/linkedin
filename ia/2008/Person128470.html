    Primary responsibilities include commercial management of external suppliers, content budget ownership, and management of systems integration activities across content, identity management and related shared services within Sensis.

  • Content Systems & Integration Manager

    Sensis

    (Online Media industry)

    July 2006 — January 2008 (1 year 7 months)

    This role encompassed both strategic and operational management activities. The primary strategic task was facilitating the provision of Content Management Systems to achieve optimal outcomes from content integration and content management systems investment and related operations.

    The operational aspect of this role covered the assessment and management of all content integration planning for Sensis' online properties, and to provide leadership and direction to the business across all projects requiring significant use of content and/or content management systems.

  • Senior Producer

    Sensis Pty Ltd

    (Online Media industry)

    May 2004 — June 2006 (2 years 2 months)

    In this role my major responsibilities included managing the redevelopment of www.citysearch.com.au in partnership with the Citysearch Product Management team. Tasks included auditing all existing systems prior to setting about a rebranding activity, rebuilding all systems (including a new CMS), launching a new product set, and migrating a few thousand existing advertisers. The new site was relaunched mid-2006.

    From April to June 2006 I was responsible for a team of multi-skilled producers covering a number of Sensis' channels including voice (1234), online search (sensis.com.au), and corporate (Sensis Intranet).

  • Producer

    Sensis Pty Ltd

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Online Media industry)

    2000 — 2004 (4 years)

    Producer for Whereis.com and Location/Mobile Devices.
    Subject matter expert for site initiative planning and major projects, responsible for daily site monitoring/maintenance, user behaviour analysis/reporting, strategic planning to grow site usage, identifying and driving improvements to the user interface.

    In this role some of my key achievements included delivering three major refreshes of Whereis.com, moving Whereis.com to a new map database platform, designing and releasing Australia's first online driving directions functionality within Whereis.com, and delivering a number of small online interactive/tracking initiatives such as for the Sydney to Hobart Yacht race, Herald Sun cycling tour and the Targa Tasmania car rally.
