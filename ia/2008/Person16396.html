Art Edmonds’s Summary

Results-oriented SENIOR INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY & SECURITY EXECUTIVE who has invented industry standards in information security (Security Technical Working Group Co-Chair, http://www.snia.org/tech_activities/workgroups/), and networked storage security (Secretariat, INCITS/ANSI/T11.3/FC/SP http://t11.org/index.html). I have also been recognized as a thought leader in information security best practices: (http://goliath.ecnext.com/coms2/gi_0199-2996083/Hitachi-Data-Systems-Recognized-for.html).

My client and executive experience is with Sun Microsystems, Hitachi Data Systems, Standard Chartered Bank (SCB) of Hong Kong, Vision Service Plan (VSP), Oracle, LSI Logic, MIPS, and WebHarbor.com. My most challenging engagement was securing SCB’s Data Centre over the Trans-Pacific Cable from California prior to Hong Kong’s return to China on June 30, 1997. Under daunting deadlines, we proved, with two hours to spare, that sensitive SC Bank customer data accessed over Bank kiosks located outside of bank premises was indeed protected.

My approach to privacy, patient healthcare information, intellectual property (IP) protection, governance, and Business Continuity Planning & Disaster Recovery (BCP/DR) revolves around integrating Risk, Security and Compliance.
