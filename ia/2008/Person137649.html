M Brian Ladner’s Summary

Since leaving graduate school in 1995, I have more or less accidentally become, a master of being a jack-of-all-trades, but a true master of probably none ;-)

I have, however, founded, architected, and implemented divisions of IT departments (EarthLink's Web Hosting), both technologically and managerially; founded and run various IT and web campus training programs; acted as a data masseuse extraordinaire; performed dozens of site technology transformations and upgrades (operating systems, applications, languages, databases, development environments, geographic locations, etc.); and been a general "go-to guy" on small to mid-size internet site updates, upgrades, maintenance needs, and repair projects.

Previous to that, I was simply a square-peg in a world of round holes, thus my B.A. in History of Science and M.S. & C. Phil. in Cognitive Science, where my research focused on neural network models of brain function (theoretical neurobiology, computational neuroscience, psychophysics, etc.).

Overall, I guess you could say I was raised an ivory-tower academic, with latent internet system administrator tendencies that surfaced later in life.
