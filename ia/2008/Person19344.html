Balaji Lakshmanan’s Education

  • Indian Institute of Technology, Madras

    MS by Research, Computer Science – Machine Learning and Robotics., 2006 — 2009 (expected)

    Learning in robots deals with robots interacting with the environment and develop a policy (state to action mapping) to achieve a goal. Transfer learning aims to solve new problems faster, using information gained by solving related problems.

    My work is in transfer learning across heterogeneous robots, where learnt policy or path to reach a goal by one robot is used by another robot with different action model to reach the goal faster. The second robot also learns useful options, which are extended base actions and uses them for finding the policy and path for subsequent goals.

    Activities and Societies:
    Reinforcement Learning, Robotics, Artificial Neural Networks, Artificial Intelligence, Probability and Statistics, Data Mining.

  • University of Madras

    BE ( Bachelor of Engineering ), Computer Science, 2000 — 2004

    Human-Humanoid Interface [ Under Graduate Project ]

    Design and development of Humanoid (6 feet) – a human simulation built with geared DC motors for movement of head, shoulder, hands, hip, leg (7 motors in all).
    The Humanoid can be automated for performing specific movements and can be controlled using the keyboard.
    IR based sensor unit built to track the movement of human hand on sensor platform.
    Decode the direction and magnitude from the sensor and send the appropriate signals to the corresponding motor of the Robot. The movement performed by the human can also be stored for future operation.
    The Humanoid robot can be controlled using WAP enabled mobile phone over the internet through a web server.

    SECOND PRIZE for project presentation at Techkriti – National Level Technical Event IIT- Kanpur, February 2004.
