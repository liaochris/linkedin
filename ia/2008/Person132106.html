Mike Fish’s Summary

An inspiring and motivational leader; with a track record of driving accelerated business growth in both start-ups and fast growing companies in the high technology, VC, consulting, publishing and utilities. Trusted by investors to represent and communicate their strategic interests on Boards, having built numerous growth businesses - both directly resourced and partner enabled - across Europe, North America and Asia Pacific. Combines visionary thinking, strong team building, coaching and problem solving skills to deliver exceptional growth in shareholder value.
Actively looking for new projects - both board and operational.

Mike Fish’s Specialties:

Operational support and investment-readiness for accelerated growth, especially for B2B technology vendors developing international markets. Strong on refining value proposition for investors and/or early customers and improving sales effectiveness. Introductions to investors, partners and end-user enterprises, including European market entry for US vendors.
