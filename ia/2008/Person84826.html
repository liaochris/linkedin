Jason M. Friedman’s Summary

JASON M. FRIEDMAN, GENERAL MANAGER
QUALIA RESORT, GREAT BARRIER REEF - AUSTRALIA

Jason M. Friedman was appointed General Manager at Qualia effective July 7, 2008

A native New Yorker, Mr Friedman has lived and worked the past 14 years in the hospitality industry in countries around Asia.

His previous positions include Camp Manager at the Four Seasons Tented Camp, Golden Triangle Thailand, Director of Rooms at Four Seasons Resort Chiang Mai Thailand. Hotel Manager of the Mansfield Hotel in New York City, Director NYC Wine Company. Resident Manager at the Amandari Hotel in Bali, Indonesia, Assistant Expedition Leader on MV Cehili, Luxury Expedition Cruise Ship, Indonesia and Director of the Adventure Indonesia Tour and Travel full service eco-tourism company. Additional training at Raffles Hotel in Singapore and Hotel Le Royal (a Raffles Hotel) in Cambodia complete his background.

A graduate of Cornell University with a Masters of Management in Hospitality and a Bachelor of Science Degree in Biology (Forest & Marine Ecology) from Lewis and Clark College in Portland, Oregon, Jason is uniquely qualified to manage this luxury resort on the Great Barrier Reef, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Jason is a certified PADI SCUBA instructor with 23 years diving experience and 2,500+ logged dives. He enjoys adventure sports, sailing, boating, hiking, wines and cooking.
