  • Ticket Center Agent

    Ticketmaster

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; TMCS; Entertainment industry)

    February 2002 — May 2003 (1 year 4 months)

    "Ticket Center technical support. How may I help you?"

    "I have tickets to a cancelled show. Can you refund for them for me?"

    "I'm sorry, I can't issue refunds. This is a technical support line for Ticketmaster's walk-up ticketing outlets."

    "But I need to get a refund on these tickets."

    "I understand that. I'm just unable to do take care of it for you. Please return to the location where you purchased them."

    "Why can't I get a refund?"

    "You can get one. You just can't get one through me. I help ticket outlets with their computer problems."

    "So I can't get a refund?"

    "You can get a refund. Just return to the location where you bought them and..."

    "Thanks for nothing." *Click*
