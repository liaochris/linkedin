John Johnson’s Summary

Dr. John Johnson (CISSP) is a security professional, working for a Fortune 100 manufacturing company in the Midwest. He is also an adjunct professor at St. Ambrose University and Scott Community College. He lives in Bettendorf, Iowa with his son and two dogs and is active with professional organizations and he is an avid blogger on science, society and free thinking.

My ambition is to become more involved with professional organizations (I am currently chair of the IEEE Section for Iowa-Illinois, and a new board member for the FBI Infragard, as well as on some customer councils and other peer group boards.) I would like to write on security topics and present at conferences.

John Johnson’s Specialties:

Most technical security issues, policy setting, network and distributed security management. I have designed and taught computer security courses.
