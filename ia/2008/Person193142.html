T. Scott Rowe, P.E.’s Summary

Led product R&D from concept to installation of medical and aerospace/defense equipment for Alcon Laboratories and Ford. Played key role in developing cutting-edge products including medical/ophthalmic laser systems, consumable and disposable medical devices, non-invasive blood glucose measurement instruments, thermal imaging sets and laser scanners/photoplotters. Guided laser/optics R&D, commercializing many different surgical and diagnostic instruments.

* Established world record for output power and slope efficiency from crystal laser combination at Alcon.
* Managed development of critical consumable portion of Alcon product, enabling sales of four million units
* Directed all optical instrument engineering for non-invasive blood glucose measurement at Fovioptics.

Special Skills:

* Innovator - holds eleven issued US patents, with an additional four in process… superior problem-solving abilities on the product line…

* Leader - experienced managing in highly regulated multidisciplinary environments… can deliver products to schedule…excellent presentation and organizational skills…

* Communicator - Ability to derive essential customer needs and requirements through focused discussion and observation… enunciate product and/or corporate vision back to customers worldwide.

Professional Engineering License, California. BS, Mechanical Engineering, University of California, Irvine.

T. Scott Rowe, P.E.’s Specialties:

Laser based therapeutic and diagnostic instruments for ophthalmology and other specialities, optical consulting for attorneys and analysts, novel optical tools and processing equipment for industrial environments.
