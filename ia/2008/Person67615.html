Gary Cardone’s Summary

CEO of real estate portfolio in Florida and California with my twin brother, Grant Cardone, under Cardone Real Estate Holdings, LLC.

Additionally, I own Cardone Energy Activities where I am looking for investments in Energy assets/platforms in merchant function, clean tech, bio fuels and carbon related activities.

I run a farm in Idaho on 240 acres of land surrounded by the Snake River, 2 miles of River frontage. This land shall be developed for a large community in Idaho Falls around the Snake River with a 60 acre park, church or school, baseball field, horse riding facilities, jogging track of 2.5 miles around the river, comprising 350 lots/homes.

I am married to a wonderful and productive, creative woman, Monica and have a 18 month old daughter, and live between Florida and California.

Gary Cardone’s Specialties:

My expertise is in developing new companies and exploiting strategic gaps in that market in order to generate higher than normal returns.
