Eric Culton’s Summary

I graduated from Washington State University in the summer of 2007 with a B.A. in Communication with an emphasis on Organizational Behavior. The degree highlighted the importance of effective communication through all mediums from a business management perspective. The program focused on understanding current leadership themes as team-building, synergic communication, explorative learning, and thought leadership. My career goals are to build upon these philosophies and implement them into management/leadership roles in any industry.
