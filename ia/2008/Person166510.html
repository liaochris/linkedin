Laurie Ryan-Day’s Summary

Accomplished designer with over 7 years of experience in design, layout, illustration and production for print and web. Creative thinker with a talent for translating client ideas and goals into unique designs and logos. Perfectionist with exceptional work ethic who strives to deliver “over and above” the client’s expectations. Adept at finding ways to reduce costs without losing quality.

Design experience: Advertisements, books, brochures, packaging, catalogs, logos, stationary, corporate identity, direct mail, web sites, signage, special occasion cards, maps, puzzles, diagrams. Experienced in print production including pre-flighting.

Computer skills: Highly proficient in InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Acrobat, Dreamweaver; Mac and PC
