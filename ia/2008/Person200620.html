Will Jayroe’s Summary

It has been said that "Code is Art." As an artist, musician, designer and developer I am personally involved in the exponential progression of communications technology. The empowerment of the individual, the ability to connect to others and the multimedia potential of the Web drew me to the world of the Internet. What started as curious interest eventually grew into career that combined art, technology and the thrill of working in a rapidly advancing field.

For the past decade I have enjoyed designing and developing web sites and the dynamic structures that make them work. My fascination for the sights, sounds and significance of the web has taken me through broadcast television, global telecommunications, higher education, the emerging world of massively multiplayer online games and commercial Internet services.

What's next? I see the future as a network of possibility where everything is connected.
