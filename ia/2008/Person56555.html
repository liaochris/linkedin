Ed Parks’s Summary

Technology executive with over 25 years experience managing the development of software and computer hardware products. As VP of Worldwide Engineering at Neoware he managed seven development teams located in the USA, Europe, China and India. Software products included Thin Client embedded OS and applications, as well as multi-tier enterprise management tools. He was also responsible for specifcation and qualification of ODM sourced computer hardware.

Strong interests in technologies which reduce cost and improve security of corporate desktops, including virtualization, server-based computing, secure mobile access.

Ed Parks’s Specialties:

Seasoned executive with experience growing small private company thru IPO to over $100M. Manage global development teams, both outsourced and owned.

Advocate of Agile methodolgies (Scrum) for software project management. ScrumMaster Certification.
