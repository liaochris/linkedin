Thomas P. Della Torre’s Summary

President/Chief Operating Officer with expertise in turning business ideas into high-growth organizations producing strong profit margins. Experienced in entering new geographic markets, developing new customer markets, and establishing new distribution channels. Strengths include applying cutting-edge technology to drive up service levels and drive down costs. Hands-on, straight-forward, team-oriented, entrepreneurial leadership style in high-energy, customer-focused organizations. Background includes development of national sales forces and marketing information services to financial services institutions, insurance companies, and legal firms. Additional experience in transforming products and services from B2B to B2C markets.
