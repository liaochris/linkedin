Kate Pynn’s Summary

My expertise is in finding, acknowledging, and resolving company challenges. I help companies meet and overcome their challenges by setting up new departments and successfully maturing them, designing efficient processes to create competitive advantage and streamline, managing and turning around an existing troubled team to become motivated, effective, and efficient, and/or defining and managing a multi-faceted project to a successful outcome.

Teamwork is core to my success. I build and maintain superior departmental, client, and vendor relationships while solidifying the quality of value-added products / services within a variety of industries.

I enjoy implementing and managing Process and Quality concepts and initiatives, such as ITIL, Six Sigma, Capability Maturity Model (CMM), and ISO. To support these efforts, I have many years of experience in requirements gathering and documentation management, project management strategies, implementation execution, and change and workflow management.

Kate Pynn’s Specialties:

* Process Development – Process engineering and development; product development and client services lifecycles; organizational efficiency and optimization
* Strategic Management – Help generate momentum, think creatively, and imagine more by developing strategies to help organizations make their dreams a reality.
* Project Management – Building PMOs, managing a variety of organizational special project areas (such as quality and continuous improvement efforts)
