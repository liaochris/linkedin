Jose Cerdan’s Summary

My name is José Cerdán and I am 35 years old Spanish-Swedish citizen with a Bachelor (finished 2001) and Master (finished 2003) Degree in Physiotherapy Sciences by Sahlgrenska Akademi in Gothenburg University, Sweden.

Since September 2007 I work part time as physiotherapist for Falck Health Care and study the 2-years master in software development and technology (SDT) at ITU where I finished already the first semester of SDT passing both subjects (Object oriented programming with JAVA and Modeling and design) successfully. I also have finish the second semester where I conducted my specialization as a guest student in Ålborgs University Medialogy master where I am learning about technologies behind Virtual Reality.

Right know I have some different plans, but still waiting for the best opportunity. I know all ready that I can continue studies in ITU in the E-business master the last two semesters. But I am also applying to a industrial PhD employment at Ålborgs University with the help of different physiotherapy clinics interested in the development of an innovative tool to create multimedia home based exercise sets.I have already made a demo version of this software working on today’s technology that needs to develop further to work together with a feedback device.

I have been working as a physiotherapist in many different places so as ambulatories, rehabilitation centres, private clinics and elderly residences in a variety of countries so as Sweden, Spain, France, Norway and finally in Denmark.

I hope my academic studies, research, work experience and future projects I propose would match your expectations for future cooperation with me. I will be pleased of knowing your interest and proposals.

Yours faithfully



José Cerdán

Jose Cerdan’s Specialties:

Physiotherapy: I have been treating different injuries in the human body and I think I have achieved a good specialisation, through my studies and experiences, in knee rehabilitation after reconstruction of the anterior cruciate ligament and in the rehabilitation of people with Spondylitis Anchylopoetica.
Other: Acupuncture , Sports, Software Development, Business entrepreneur, Virtual Reality, Motion Capture, Assessment of evaluation methods.
