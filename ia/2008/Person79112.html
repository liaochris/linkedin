I-Chien Jan’s Summary

Hi, I am I-Chien Jan, Vice President of OTO (Optical Transformed-Output) Science, Inc. OTO Science is a start-up company in Taiwan focuses on miniature optical spectrometer based on semiconductor fabrication and MEMS technology.

Before joining OTO, I have worked for three kinds of VCs include independent VC, Banking VC and corporate VC in Taiwan. My investment industries and post management experiences include Medical Device, Pharmaceutics, Opto-Electronics, Mobile/Wireless, Internet Business and Alternative Energy.
