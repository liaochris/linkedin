Joseph Franklyn McElroy’s Summary

About Us

"Stars of the World Wide Web"

Corporate Performance Artists is a successful Marketing Agency and a respected Internet Research & Development firm. While we don't ignore traditional marketing tools to influence buying decisions, we focus on marketing campaigns that places advertising at the precise point a customer has made a decision to buy. We call this type of marketing campaign "Customer Initiated Marketing."

The perfect embodiment of this approach is Search Engine Marketing, where we identify the keyword phrases that indicate a customer is ready to buy and then optimize marketing content to be the most likely to be found by customers in their search of the Internet.

As an R&D firm, we have the capability to not only market Internet businesses, but to build them as well. Very often we identify profitable new revenue opportunities for our clients and help them realize the new business models.
