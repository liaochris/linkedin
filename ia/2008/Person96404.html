Jonathan Soares’s Summary

It all started back in 2005 when Jonathan Soares decided to market his mothers’ home made BBQ sauce while developing a business plan for a college project. Jonathan being a foodie at heart, with a love for gourmet cuisine and a passion for cooking perfected her original honey BBQ sauce recipe and developed the Hickory Teriyaki and Spicy Cajun flavors.

The sweet succulent and smooth texture of these sauces began to catch the attention of retailers across the Northeast. Before Jonathan knew it, his delicious Jonathan’s Q™ brand of gourmet BBQ sauces was featured in more than 1,000 supermarket locations. Jonathan being the aggressive entrepreneur that he is had a vision of national expansion. He wanted everyone across the country to be able to enjoy his amazing line of products.

Jonathan truly believed in his delicious sauces and in 2007 began traveling the country meeting with our nations largest supermarket chains. With faith and persistence he achieved the impossible and aggressively pioneered his Jonathan’s Q™ brand.

Now with 2008 in full swing, Jonathan’s Q™ BBQ can be found in more than 5,000 supermarket locations across the United States. But that’s not all! In addition to BBQ sauces, Jonathan spent hours in the kitchen developing his unique and innovative Jonathan’s Q™ Fusion line of marinades.

Jonathan’s Q™ is one of the fastest growing brand in the United States and this is only the beginning!
