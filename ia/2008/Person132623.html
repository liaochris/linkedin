Mike Sid’s Summary

I envision and apply process and technology solutions to business problems. For the last several years, my focus has been in the Media & Entertainment industry on such problems as entertainment distribution through the internet, managing massive amounts of entertainment content and providing the means to connect to audiences via the internet.

My goals lie in my interests which is to find interesting problems that can use a process or technology solution and in expanding the possibilities of all networks (internet, cable, telephone, wireless) in promoting a more efficient connection to our wants, desires and needs.
