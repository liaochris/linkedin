Bruno Ribeiro’s Summary

I was born in Brasilia, 1982, but it was in Rio, where I’ve been living for 20 years, that I had my first contact with internet programming.

Because of my father’s influence, I started dealing with computers when I was a little kid– our first computer was an Apple II+, manufactured by the Brazilian Unitron in the year of 1985.

In 1998, I started working as a freelancer. As time went by, I decided to specialize in ActionScript (Adobe Flash) and Coldfusion. Seven years later, I became a Macromedia certified Professional in Flash MX 2004 (developer) and in Dreamweaver 8. Since then, I’ve taken part in more than 70 programming projects.

As I’ve always been interested in learning and sharing knowledge, I set up in 2006 o Adobe User Group Rio de Janeiro. In this group, I had the opportunity of getting complementary knowledge, not only in programming, but also in Design theory and communication.

In this long way, I’ve met some excellent professionals as the journalist Roberto Cassano, with whom I’ve learned a lot about communication, and the creative designers Rodrigo Freire and Raul Queiroz, my partners in many projects. I’ve also been getting involved in many different projects, with different agencies from Rio de Janeiro and Sao Paulo, as AddComm, Xoxuá, Selluloid AG and Simples Studio. Among my clients are Nissan, Garoto, Universal Music, Duloren, Varig and Samsung.

At the beginning of this year, I had the opportunity of working for some English and Portuguese agencies and of getting closer to the Coldfusion, acting as a senior system analyst at Add Technologies, located in the Golden Cross Building. Now, at Simples Estúdio, I’m part of the creation team and work as a Flash developer.

Bruno Ribeiro’s Specialties:
