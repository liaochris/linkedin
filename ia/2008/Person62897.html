Scott Taylor’s Summary

As a proven Strategic Marketer, Speaker, FastPath Trainer, Coach and Marketing Mentor my friends and clients have been creating wealth systems using my FastPath Marketing Principles to the tune of 300 million+ in revenues and growing.

23 years of award winning sales and marketing strategies, tactics, and action plans delivered for companies ranging from Blue Chip Corporations to Mom & Pop Start Ups. I have produced unmatched results in the high-technology hardware and software sales and marketing circles in Hollywood CA.

I help my clients grow their business with proven, practical sales and marketing action plans based on solid strategies and affordable tactics that matter!
