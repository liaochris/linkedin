Bruce F. Pawlak (Major, USAFR, Retired)’s Summary

I am a retired reserve military commissioned officer with nearly 26 years of honorable service (includes 12 years of active duty). I am a late-vocations ordained deacon (studying for the priesthood) currently working as the midnight shift chaplain in an urban hospital, based out of the Emergency Room and Level One Trauma Center. I am called to offer a ministry to the dead, dying and seriously injured, along with their family and friends. I also provide a special ministry to the staff and health care providers who are attempting to provide emergency medical care - in the direct path of God's will.

Bruce F. Pawlak (Major, USAFR, Retired)’s Specialties:

Ordained clergy, grief counseling, pastoral care and counseling, conflict management (emergency interventions with troubled visitors/patients aimed at de-escalating potentially disruptive or violent situations), experienced staff trainer/project analyst/staff officer/leader/manager.
