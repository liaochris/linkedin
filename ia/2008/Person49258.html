Deep Patel’s Summary

Deep Patel is a promoter of "green" energy and has helped many people throughout the United States with their renewable energy projects.

Deep adds value by bringing his financial services personal sales experience, understanding the sales process, and heavy networking. Deep has a M.S. degree in Finance from Boston University.

He graduated from Cal State Fullerton with a degree in B.A. Business Entrepreneurship. Deep’s strength includes computer/internet technology and is a co-founder of rapidcite.com, an automatic works cited page web application.

Deep also has a understanding of Search Engine Marketing and other forms of internet promotion through his previous internet ventures.

He also is also familiar with Unix / Windows based web servers and security issues. Deep practices virtual project management.

    GoGreenSolar.com began in 2006 connecting renewable energy system buyers to local service providers and has grown into an easy to use online resource in the renewable energy industry.     About GoGreenSolar.com

    GoGreenSolar.com provides an innovative online network for buyers and renewable energy contractors to conduct trade. Buyers have the ability to post a Request for Proposal (RFP) in order to receive multiple quotes for their project, search through the contractor database, and review contractor feedback.
