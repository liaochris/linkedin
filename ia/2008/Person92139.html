Jeff Korhan’s Summary

I'm a professional speaker who works with associations, small businesses like mine, and selected corporations, especially in the green industry, to help business owners and sales professionals grow their business and always give their customers their best.

My expertise is cutting through unnecessary steps and “b.s.” that compromise productivity and take the fun out of the work of serving customers.

Jeff Korhan’s Specialties:

Sales and marketing, leadership and life-work balance.

Jeff Korhan’s Experience

  • Speaker

    True Nature® Inc

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 1997 — Present (11 years 5 months)

    Jeff Korhan combines over 30 years of marketing and sales experience with a passion for challenging how we can work to get the right results! From a corporate executive to entrepreneur - from cliff diving, triathlons, to meditating in the deserts of India, Jeff will bring new perspectives to your audience.

    Jeff’s company in suburban Chicago was named Business of the Year in one of the fastest growing communities in country. He has an MBA, is a former Sales and Marketing Manager for a Fortune 50 Corporation, and the founder of Treemendous Landscape Company®.

    Jeff is a green industry expert, with credentials as a Licensed Landscape Architect , Certified Arborist, and Feng Shui Practitioner. He is a board member of the Professional Landcare Network (PLANET), and a Chapter Leadership Consultant for the National Speakers Association.

    Jeff works with business owners and sales and marketing professionals want to grow their business and always give their customers their best.
