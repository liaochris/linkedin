Daniel Hower’s Summary

Daniel Hower has delivered successful results to numerous businesses in the internet industry. He has eleven years of professional experience in computer graphics with a special focus in web user-interface design.

Dan is exceptionally skilled at hand-coding validated xHTML markup language and implementing advanced CSS-based layouts. His expertise also includes information design, usability analysis and Section 508 compliance measures. Dan has top-notch interpersonal skills, an extensive background in the fine arts, and an interest in business development.

These characteristics allow Dan to handle diverse responsibilities and make him a significant asset to any company. His goal is to provide outstanding value as an experienced, highly talented employee on a creative or marketing team.

Daniel Hower’s Specialties:

Hand coded xHTML, CSS, 508 compliance and some JavaScript. Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, Director (basic Lingo), HomeSite, Dreamweaver, Visio, Power Point and some Flash (basic ActionScript). Strong interpersonal, presentation, communication and organizational skills. Competent on both PC and Mac platforms. Experience in both Java and PHP environments.
