Jon Hickman’s Summary

I have eight years in industry running a design agency.

I am currently working at Birmingham City University on a portfolio of media research projects, and I am a Visiting Lecturer at the Birmingham School of Media.

I am Chair of Sutton Festival of Arts, which is currently preparing for its second annual festival in July 2009.

I am involved in a number of community based media skills training initiatives across North Birmingham.

I undertake a limited number of freelance design and consultancy projects, particularly with community or charitable groups.
