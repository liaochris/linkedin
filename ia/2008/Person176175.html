Simon Graveline’s Summary

I have already been involved in several Nintendo DS and GBA projects and I’d like to keep specializing in handeld games (Sony PSP, Nintendo DS) or downloadable games (PSN, WiiWare, XBLA).

So far, I have worked on DS, GBA, PS2, WII, PC and N64, for companies such as Ubisoft, LucasArts, Disney (BVG), Konami, Activision and Game Factory.

I have been fortunate to work as a lead game designer on a few projects and I would definitely enjoy doing it again. I really had a blast working with junior designers, having to coach them and at the same time, having to deal with leads of other trades in the projects to find efficient working methods. This is a combination that I would, at any time, be glad to participate to again.

Overall, I'm just looking for a stimulating working environment filled with creativity driven people. Also, having the opportunity to work in this industry allows me to learn how to speak several different languages (coding, art, audio, marketing, editorial, press, etc) in order to get the best results in the idea communication process with so many different individuals and I'm always looking forward to learn and perfect these languages skills.

From the Atari 2600 to the PS3, I have own more than a dozen consoles and played probably a 1000 games in over 20 years of gaming passion. I like analyzing these games and try to understand what makes them good and fun (or not) and I like to believe that I'm getting good at it and want to get even better.

Finding the perfect balance between life, passion and work and be able to express my creativity is also one of my main life goal… so far, being a game designer answers to all these needs.

Simon Graveline’s Specialties:

- Conception of game world, mood, story, dialogue
- Definition of characters, rules, objects, controls, menus, rewards
- Overlook of design team (including level design and writer) and creative direction
- Working with other trades of the projects to insure the game direction (art, animation, audio, editorial, localization, marketing, modeller, code, level design, playtests and writer)
- Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Visio, Powerpoint), Adobe Photoshop
