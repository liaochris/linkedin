Lindsay Anvik la@rjlresources.com’s Summary

Unique from most agency recruiters, I was in corporate HR at a high profile media company, hiring candidates at all levels (including an assistant controller, executive assistants, sales executives, graphic designers and marketing executives).

Earlier in my career, as the executive assistant to several influential CEOs in the entertainment business, I worked side by side with celebrities, CEOs, politicians and other high profile clientele. The pressure and intensity of these roles provided me relevant insight about executive support that most agency recruiters do not possess.

In addition, I was the co-founder/Editor-in-Chief of an online media company that published weekly email blasts about fashion, beauty and culture to females ages 25-50 in major cities across the US.

After surviving being the assistant to several powerful CEOs in entertainment and owning my own business, I know there is nothing I can't accomplish. I've organized parties of 500+ at the Sundance Film Festival. I've helped orchestrate sponsorship at the Mix Show Power Summit in Puerto Rico. I've managed not to get arrested while accidentally turning into the lot where Air Force One was parked at JFK (where I was retrieving a crucial Fed Ex package that contained European yacht parts for my former boss). I've managed to de-crate a once-live but then dead cow that was imported from France on the West Side Highway.
