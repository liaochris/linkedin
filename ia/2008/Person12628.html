Angela Colston’s Summary

Angie Colston is a seasoned sales and marketing professional with over 20 years of experience leading organizations to success. Early on she had ambitions of attending medical school, but while pursuing her Master’s Degree in Physiology, she found a passion for sales. Since then she has never turned back, climbing the management ladder from salesperson to sales manager, marketing director, vice president of sales and regional vice president of sales and marketing for some of the top companies in the United States. Angie has also earned the prestigious MIRM designation (Member, Institute of Residential Marketing) awarded by the National Association of Home Builders.

Her zeal for sales training and love of sales professionals has made her a favorite for coaching, motivation and mentoring sales and management teams across the country. Angie offers a unique style, memorable seminars and undeniable results. Creating Customized Training Programs tailored to specific audiences and organizations is her specialty.
