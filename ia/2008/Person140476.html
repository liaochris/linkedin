Nick Elvery [LION] 1200+ Connections’s Summary

We at Intego recruit IT professionals through out the UK and mainland Europe. We are unique in respect that our consultants have had direct experience of working in the roles they recruit for. This gives us a fantastic advantage over other consultancies with the ability to understand our clients’ needs and tailor the solution to them.

We pride ourselves on having integrity through out the whole process and believe in providing a professional service to all our candidates and clients. We strongly believe in adding value by hand picking the candidates that completely fit the requirement. One of the most important parts of recruitment in our eyes is being able to listen. That means listening to the candidates and qualifying what their exact needs are and matching them to a properly qualified requirement. We believe that getting the basics right is of upmost importance and is often over looked by recruitment consultants.

Nick is a highly focused professional who is passionate about recruitment. My focus is on building solid business relationships with my clients and adding value to their business. I pride myself on having an extremely ethical approach to the recruitment process with the ability to talk on a technical level as well as having an excellent knowledge of recruitment processes.

Whether you are a client requiring IT professionals or a candidate seeking work then please don't hesitate to get in contact.
