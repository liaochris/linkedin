David Petras’s Summary

David Petras co-founded Sartep Consulting, a boutique finance and accounting consulting firm, to offer companies a cost-effective alternative to the “Big Four”. We provide experienced professionals and project teams in the following lines of service: Business Consulting, Accounting and Finance Services, CFO Advisory Services, Treasury Advisory Services, Outsourced Controller Services, SEC and Regulatory Reporting Compliance, Technical Accounting Research and Implementation Guidance (SEC and IASB), Financial System Implementation Services, Transaction Advisory Services, and Forensic Accounting Services. Our client portfolio extends nationwide and includes Fortune 100 to mid-size companies.

Mr. Petras has over 14 years of finance and accounting experience. He has held various management level positions including Global Finance Manager at DuPont, Manager at KPMG, Assistant Cash Manager at Vlasic Foods International, and Assistant Controller at KSM Associates. He holds an M.B.A. in Finance and is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA).
