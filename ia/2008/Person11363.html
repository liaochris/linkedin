Andrew Chang’s Summary

Andrew is an entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in the health, fitness, and wellness industries. Currently, he is launching a student health franchise to provide a one-stop solution for food products, fitness merchandises, and training services. He is also a sought-after personal trainer and life coach.

In his 4 year academic career, he studied business and marketing in HKU BBA program (Asia's top university); obtained a certification in professional financial analysis; and mastered peak performance psychology and sociology of innovation. He has also founded and lead over seven campus organizations.

Andrew is also publishing books and programs on strategies and tactics for learning how to learn, transforming the body, developing relationships, and finding meaning in life.

After overcoming three "impossible" health challenges, Andrew is working with revolutionary healers Dr. Molter and Master Zhou to document, write, and publish his miracle in healing what was supposed to be a crippling injury. He is on his way back to full health and looks forward to getting back into the bodybuilding and martial art world that he loves.
