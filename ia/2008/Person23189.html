Bill Burress (wburress3@gmail.com)’s Summary

Experience in New Construction and later owned and managed a remodeling contracting business. Began to originate mortgages in 1981 while running the remodeling business.

Migrated into mortgage originations full time by 1998. Originator, Manager at various mortgage companies. Founded and managed Intercontinental Mortgage, LLC for four years in Ohio. In December 2006, became a full time Florida resident and opened the first branch of Cincinnati Mortgage, LLC in Fort Myers, Florida. with a planned 35 branch offices to be opened throughout Florida in the future.

With over 25 years experience originating mortgages, Bill is one of the most knowledgeable people in the mortgage business. Specializing in getting loans done others cannot. Cincinnnati Mortgage, LLC originates commercial loans and residential loans. If the banks won't approve you, chances are Cincinnati Mortgage, LLC can.
