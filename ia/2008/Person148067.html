  • Partner / Marketing Director

    A ARCA Entretenimento

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Online Media industry)

    October 1999 — July 2007 (7 years 10 months)

    A ARCA (www.a-arca.com) is a personal project that turned into one of the most accessed entertainment websites in Brazil. The project started in late '99, and launched in September 2001. There you can find news and articles about Movies, TV Series, Animation, Role-Playing Games, Videogames, Comics and Collectibles.

    A ARCA stands out from most websites due to its editorial position, aimed at delivering not only information, but firmly based - and funny - opinions about all things entertainment. This has gained the respect of big internet players, like America Online Brazil (our content partner from September '93 to May '95). Nowadays, we are one of UOL's content partner, the biggest internet provider in Brazil (www.uol.com.br). We are reaching more than 200.000 unique visitors monthly, and counting more than 1.100.000 pageviews each month.

    I am the Marketing Director, also directly responsible for the Animation section (called ACETATO) and Collectible Toys (called TRECOS).
