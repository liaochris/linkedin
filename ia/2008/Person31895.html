Cathleen Tyson Baldauf’s Summary

"Although the media likes to tell us gloom and doom, it is up to us to keep the mind set that we can make a difference in this world; as well as in our own little world around us… so I am determined to Step Forward with Confidence into 2009. And looking forward to it! Won't you join me in being positive!"
Standing strong, Cathleen

Cathleen Tyson Baldauf started working in the field of computer graphic design in the 80's. She started in Phoenix in digital design, continuing to direct mail houses into the early 90's.

Cathleen moved back to her home town in Amarillo, Texas in'92 working again in print shops as the main designer as well as prepress coordinator. Currently, she works full time as an Art Director for APLX Advertising; direct mail, newspaper and magazine ads, specialty printing, outdoor boards, web design, and TV graphics. APLX is an in-house agency for Auto Inc.

Auto Inc. and CEO, Eddie Bradley, have over 15 business ventures, so the field of design is constantly changing.

Cathleen started web design 3 years ago, and host five sites on her own. Many of the web sites are pro bono, allowing many non-profit organizations the chance to show professionalism while reaching out through the internet.

Cathleen Tyson Baldauf’s Specialties:

Cathleen's design experience spans 23 years. Currently, she is working in Adobe Creative Suite CS2 & CS3. Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign and Acrobat, Dreamweaver and Flash. Previously, she has worked in Pagemaker and Quark Xpress for many years in the printing industry, but has preferred InDesign. Proficient in Mac and PC.
