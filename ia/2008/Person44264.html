Dan Miller’s Summary

I am a proven manager with the skills to get the job done. I am known for my ability to execute and take a project from cradle to launch and post-launch assessment and adjustments. I do not manage from afar, but rather get my hands dirty on a daily basis to have an intimate understanding of the tools, systems and challenges that stand in the way of executing on the strategies of the business. I have a firm understanding of technology and am passionate about staying up on the latest innovations. I am able to have an educated discussion with development to ensure we are not taking shortcuts and are always pushing the technology envelope, where appropriate. I am driven and determined to always produce the best products possible while at the same time recognizing that dynamic markets dictate the ability to shift priorities quickly and effortlessly. Extensive experience managing enterprise level ecommerce products and operations.
