Anthony Edwards’s Summary

I have enjoyed my role as CTO, being the technical visionary interacting with Sales, Marketing, Engineering, partners, and customers. Before that, I enjoyed heading a consulting team for systems integration for a world class network management NOC. My technical depth, analytical skills, and strong people skills help me excel in these roles. I am can-do, action-oriented, and energetic as I work toward business and customer success.

Today with HanoverSoft, I am focused on consulting activity. And even more specifically, I have constructed a proposal to assess enterprise monitoring technology for the CIO. In this day and age, many enterprises have many monitoring tools in place. Some monitoring is over the data network; some monitoring is over the telephone network; some monitoring is for security; etc. However, not all monitoring is consolidated into a single data center. As I meet with folks, I hear them tell me they want to unify their monitoring technology amongst these various disciplines. The first step is to assess their current monitoring technology so they know what they have and don't have. The second step is to provide a vision of how they can unify their monitoring technology. The third step is to realize that vision. I am providing consulting services for these three steps. Each step concludes with a report and presentation to the CIO, providing status, comments, and recommendations.

If your organization could use these services, please let me know. If you know of another organization that could use these services, please let me know (or forward this page). I am more than happy to meet and discuss these services.
