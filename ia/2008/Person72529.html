Gregory Foster’s Summary

I am a Computer Software Craftsman, an artisan who patiently accrues an understanding of his craft, taking pride in the manifestation of every detail of his creations, for they are a reflection of his soul. I am a Visionary, one who pictures things made manifest and near complete. This gift of vision is balanced with strategies and tactics for creation. Strategically, I learn the art of computer programming while observing the sociologies of development teams and weighing the feng shui of computing environments. Tactically, I absorb the latest development techniques while maintaining independence of thought to cultivate my most skillful means. I am interested in the cutting edge to better see where we are headed while enjoying the revelation of novelty. But I am more interested in human history, seeking to understand where we have been to apply what we have learned.

I am cultivating a deep network of relations for the long-term; family, friends, teachers and associates. Amongst my relations, I facilitate connections to strengthen common cause. To meet the people I will know and meet, to learn through myself and to work, I will travel. I join projects of value beyond mere compensation; I avoid purely economic relationships and volunteer for projects of deep merit. Success requires steady work as big dreams take time to manifest; it is only by steadily chipping away that we enable form and shape to emerge. This process instills faith and evidence that our world honors conscientious sacrifice, perseverance and hard work.
