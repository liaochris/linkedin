D. Patrick Lewis’s Summary

I began my "professional experience" in the hospitality industry, one of the best training grounds for a sales person, managing and opening restaurants until 1998. At which point I began my "professional sales experience" by becoming an account executive for the staffing industry. I then migrated to technical recruiting and Raleigh, NC until mid 2001 when the bubble burst. Having no success finding another position in the tech industry I returned to Phoenix/Scottsdale and began my real estate career.

I have been a Professional REALTOR with John Hall & Associates for 6+ years. I am also active in my community and give back to my industry by volunteering for the Scottsdale Area Association of REALTORS and currently serve as the Chairman of the Government Affairs Committee. Also recently completed SAAR's Leadership Academy that give REALTORS tools to become community leaders.

All of my past and present "professional experiences" have made this REALTOR an expert in real estate and customer satisfaction. Thoroughly trained in how to professionally serve your clients, friends, relatives and referrals with the utmost courtesy and respect.
