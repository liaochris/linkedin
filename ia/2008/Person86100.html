Jacques Bogh’s Summary

Jacques Bøgh has extensive experience in providing finance support to new or high-growth entities, identifying and realising major opportunities to protect or significantly improve the bottom line. He has worked as an independent consultant as well as in companies active in the fields of civil engineering, IT solutions, telecommunication services, life sciences and disease control textiles, in various senior financial positions.

He holds a MSc in Management (Grande Ecole) from the HEC Paris School of Management, which he has complemented with a DECF bachelor-level accounting diploma as well as the ACCA Certificates in International Financial Reporting (IFRS) and International Standards for Auditing (ISA).

He is currently a candidate to the Diploma of Advanced Studies in Risk Management at HEC and HES-SO in Geneva, and responsible for implementation of the Swiss risk management and internal control systems norms as per paragraphs 663 B and 728 A.

Jacques Bøgh is fluent in German, French and English.

Jacques Bogh’s Specialties:

Business Planning, execution support, financial management, decision support, strategic cost control, IFRS implementation, accounting, international accounting consolidation, transfer pricing, internal control, risk management, par. 663b and 728a of the Swiss code of obligations. Extensive CFO and Controller experience.
