Brant Bukowsky’s Summary

Since 1997, my brother and I have started 6 companies in a variety of industries. We have been fortunate to have achieved success with all of them and are always looking for something new.

Currently, I am looking to invest in companies (through Angel Investing) and provide support in building the business, mainly via assistance with online marketing.

We have been awarded the Inc 500 award for fastest growing public companies for two separate companies making us one of the few people to have ever accomplished this achievement.

Two of our companies have been acquired. ShowMe Tickets was acquired by industry leader TicketsNow in 2005 and LakeRentals.com was acquired by The Weather Channel in 2007.

Brant Bukowsky’s Specialties:

I consider myself a serial entrepreneur with expertise in Search Engine Optimization and Search Engine Marketing via organic search, PayPerClick (PPC) advertising, viral marketing, and conversion rate optimization.
