Peter Blom’s Summary

After 22 years in marketing -, product -, project - and general management in the Netherlands as well as in the U.S.A. and European countries; I founded my own company to deploy my experience, knowledge and skills for the benefit of the improvement of organisations. That is why I can be characterised as “rainmaker”; which means “One whose influence can initiate progress or ensure success”. I joined the OOA and am a Certified Management Consultant. My technical background as well as my experience in changing organisations to better align to existing and future challenges, has been an asset for me, but even more for my clients and customers.
In an implementation consultancy and interim - or change management are always somewhat combined depending on what needs to be done. From the start of the assignment, my objective is to keep the implementation as short as possible, of course in relation with a successful completion and realization of the results.

I am of the opinion that “change” includes a number of elements: Content, Process and Context. These changes should primarily focus on achieving the objectives of the organisation. “Change” of an organisation can never be the objective in itself.
When determining the assignment; I make the objective leading, which will become the basis for determining the change in Content, Process and Context.
The employee plays in most of the changes in organisations an important role. For that reason, the objective of the “change” will be determined by the company’s objective, its culture and the tangible results that should be achieved, determine my approach to the implementation.
I find this philosophy and approach in the term “rainmaker”; which means: “One whose influence can initiate progress or ensure success”.
