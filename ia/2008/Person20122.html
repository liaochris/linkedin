Bart Schrooten’s Summary

Hi, I am an ambitious entrepreneur and busines developer, born in The Netherlands. I am passionate about new technologies...so what better place to live than Finland! Currently devoting my time to Leiki's unique and patented technology that enables online content discovery and targeted advertising (www.leiki.com).

My experience comes from various technology sectors (e.g. information technology, telecommunications), negotiating on executive level (both as seller and buyer) and working with some of the brightest and innovative people in business. My goal is developing new ideas in to profitable businesses while having fun on the way!

I am also very interested in social and environmental development work, especially in East-Africa. Let me know if you have some thoughts or ideas to share.

In my free time I enjoy good food, playing the trumpet, rock climbing, traveling, spending time with my wife and dog.
