Alan MacKelworth’s Summary

AMaCLimited is a management advisory and consultancy business, operating in Europe, Middle East, Africa, Asia Pacific and North America with over 75% of our programmes outside of the UK. We have completed programmes with prestigious global industry leaders and niche emerging organisations.

Our core services are focused on helping executives develop alliance and boardroom relationships that improve performance and results throughout their organisation. AMaCLimited evolve with our customers to enhance operational efficiency by enabling rapid execution of business development plans, reducing timescales and achieving continuous improvement.
