James Tervit’s Summary

delivering content with the minimum of fuss and with no infrastructure overheads.

Channel samples are :

Sports Events

Golf - comprehensive coverage of The Ryder Cup, US PGA Tour, PGA European Tour, The Open Championship, U.S. Masters Tournament, U.S. Open, PGA Championship together with additional golf video content such as lessons with recognised experts, profiles of star players like Tiger Woods, reviews of previous tournaments - in fact everything required to provide a 24 hour golf channel on mobile.

Football - Highlights of Premier League soccer is available in many countries around the world. Live Premier League games are also available for a limited number of countries.

Horse Racing - Live horse racing from the UK and Ireland can be streamed to user's mobile devices. The feeds can come without betting information for countries which require this.

Formula 1 - News, views and gossip from most the teams engaged in Formula 1 racing, including interviews with drivers and team principals.

Red Bull Air Races - highlights of the Red Bull Air Race series is available around the world. Live coverage of these exciting events can also be supplied in streaming format on request. Joint venture company Swanbay Livestyle will be a Team Sponsor for future events.

Beijing Olympics - this event is now over, but we carried video content from the Beijing Olympics for a number of customers. We plan to do the same for the London Olympics in 2012.

Financial News

Live, 24 hour rolling financial news service, augmented if required with feeds of stock prices from a variety of Stock Exchanges.

Lifestyle Video

Coverage of films in production, including rushes, interviews and trailers, including Bollywood, reports from the major Fashion Week events in Europe and the USA, Music news and videos etc.
