Shawn P. Simon’s Experience

  • Enterprise Integration Architect

    Oshyn Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    With an emphasis on emerging technologies, Shawn has joined Oshyn to strengthen their Enterprise Integration Practice. Bringing experience in EAI, B2B and BPMS, he aims to standardize and evangelize the Service Oriented Architecture “way” of integration. He is currently involved in another green-field integration implementation with a focus on SOA and rapid ROI for an international for-profit educational services provider in Baltimore, MD

  • Integration Architect and Team Manager

    XM Satellite Radio, Inc.

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; XMSR; Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2003 — May 2007 (4 years 4 months)

    The company was a billion-dollar startup (subscription based satellite radio service) whose subscriber management system required front-end, back-end and infrastructure development and integration. Shawn designed and developed a holistic solution for the process automation within the middleware with significant attention to long-term flexibility, maintainability and scalability. The solution is a high-availability, high-performance Vitria BPMS interfacing their front-end Siebel CRM to their back-end proprietary provisioning and Infranet billing systems handling over 500 tps. Shawn was part of the implementation from design to launch, post-launch production support and helping create and manage an in-house integration team of engineers. As lead technical architect for integration, he also managed the B2B solutions comprising of mission critical Supply Chain Channel and the custom application SWAT team, leveraging latest technologies in Java to rapidly deliver utility functionality.

  • Senior Consultant

    Bearing Point Consulting

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2002 — February 2003 (1 year 1 month)

    As a cross-industry technology expert for the design and development of a Model-Driven Integration approach for the Utilities and Energy Market, Shawn leveraged a standards’ based data model called the Common Information Model (IEC CIM). He developed a custom tool set to facilitate CIM design and integration using current XML technologies. Features included transforming the UML representation of the CIM into highly organized XML Schemas used to form standard messages published onto any ESB. Contivo was integrated to allow for semantic design-time mapping translated into usable XSLT during run-time. Shawn aided in replacing the traditional EAI architecture with a Business Process Management System. The framework was to incorporate BPML as the processing language and Web Services as the integration platform with Sun’s JWSDP and the Intalio|n3 BPMS. Along with implementation, he held CIM-awareness training sessions as well as performing internal and client-facing MDI demonstrations.

  • Software Progammer

    Andersen Consulting

    (Privately Held; 10,001 or more employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    June 2000 — February 2002 (1 year 9 months)

    After maturing as a Java 2 Developer, Shawn became proficient in J2SE and J2EE along with UNIX scripting and Perl. As lead developer of middleware implementation, Shawn assisted in the construction of a fully functional EAI Solution Center of Excellence. This involved building an Enterprise Service Bus and Processing Management environment, aiding and overseeing the development of process automation models, establishing stable connectivity as well as managing structural modifications to ensure end-to-end data integrity. The center resulted in the conception and construction of a subscriber management system that is currently being marketed in the telecom, media/entertainment and communication services industries. This self-contained product is a fully integrated customer management solution that utilizes “best-of-breeds” packaged software combined with a sophisticated EAI middleware to deliver a robust, flexible and functional CRM, billing and provision symbiosis.
