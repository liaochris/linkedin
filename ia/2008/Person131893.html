Michael Bravo’s Summary

Worked as a network architect for Alcatel BSR, St.Petersburg, incurring major Cisco-related experience; helped found and maintain several on- and offline communities, including starting and coordinating St.Petersburg Fidonet network for some years, helping to organize St.Petersburg Linux user group, etc. Extensive system administration experience, primarily in Unix environment; good knowledge of web application programming in Unix-based application environments. Especially interested in connecting people, building topical communities, kick-starting innovative projects.

Contributed articles on various hi-tech, Internet and privacy issues to various publications; helped verify technical manual translations into Russian for Lucent/Avaya; worked as an independent technical consultant to oversee application of U.S.-based money to IT upgrades in St.Petersburg city government structures.

Cofounder and Editor-in-chief of premier Russian-language equestrian web publication, Equestrian Review.
