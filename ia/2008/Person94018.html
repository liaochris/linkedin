Joe Tooman’s Summary

My focus is to provide a clear picture of the overall customer experience and business activity on the Web and across related customer touchpoints, such as the call center. This insight is helping my current employer, a Fortune 500 financial services company, improve conversion rates, detect fraud as it is occurring, understand why people are leaving the Web and calling us, know which customers need assistance, and ensure high Web availability and performance through better problem detection and isolation.

To provide this insight, I have leveraged relationships with vendor partners to ensure constant improvement in analytical capabilities (on both data capture and reporting fronts) and partnered with the business to identify, adopt and effectively utilize enhanced analytical tools.

Joe Tooman’s Experience

  • Web Analytics and Customer Experience Monitoring Lead

    Sallie Mae

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; SLM; Financial Services industry)

    October 2003 — Present (4 years 8 months)

    My team's mission is to monitor, report on and improve the Web customer experience. 80% of our business is conducted through the Web, so it is critical for us to have an accurate picture of the customer experience through the use of intelligent monitoring and eventing solutions.

    We use Omniture's SiteCatalyst, Tealeaf's customer experience monitoring (CEM) products, and Coral8's complex event processing (CEP) engine to drive real time dashboards, business process alerts, and advanced analytics. We monitor customer behavior from both a marketing (e.g., conversion tracking, lead acquisition) and operations (e.g., fraud detection, cross channel correlation) perspective. Network World recently awarded us their 2007 All-Star Award for Outstanding Technology Project.

    We also support Tealeaf for our Call Center reps to replay Web sessions while assisting customers on the phone. We have the largest Tealeaf implementation in the world and I am president of Tealeaf's User Community.
