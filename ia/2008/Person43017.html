Daneeta Loretta Saft’s Specialties:

I believe in the true spirit of Independent Filmmaking: the story is king, save the drama for the screen, just get on with making the film & have fun because, if you're not having fun, then what the hell is the point? I also believe that one can earn an extraordinary living as an independent filmmaker (like, buy-your-mom-a-house & none-of-your-family-members-will-ever-have-to-worry-about-money & change-the- world kind of money), but I have not seen evidence of this first hand. I just have faith.
