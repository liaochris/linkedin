Brooke Hall’s Summary

Brooke Hall built her first website at the age of 18, her first business at 20, founded and managed numerous groups while attending The Wilkes Honors College of FAU and went on to study and live in Florence, Italy. Over a four year span, she was producer, director, actor and publicist for the young, angsty Theater in the Raw. Since then, Brooke has worked as a travel writer and managing editor for an online publishing house, contributing writer and film critic for the Baltimore City Paper, fashion photographer for Closer Magazine and proofreader for T. Rowe Price and Euro RSCG marketing firm. Public relations and communications has become her latest passion, along with Web design, journalism and building stellar professional relationships. Consistently securing coverage for clients in publications such as Washington Business Journal, Business Week, Information Week and Los Angeles Times, among others, Brooke has made her home in the communications field.
