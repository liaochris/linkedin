Steve Jennings’s Summary

With ten years of Internet advertising experience, I know how to reach consensus with the client and agencies on what the advertiser wants to accomplish, understand what is needed by the publishers to make that happen, prepare the ad creative, match it to the media, and see it through to success, from start to finish.

I thrive on the challenges of rolling out increasingly engaging rich media ads with sophisticated targeting and detailed tracking, including XML feeds with dynamic data, streaming video-based ads, targeted local content, site-event/transactive reporting and new things I have not done before.
