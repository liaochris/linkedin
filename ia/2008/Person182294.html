Stewart Chesters’s Summary

Bibby Financial Services is the leading indepedent global factoring business. Providing working capital finance to growing small to medium sized businesses at highly competitive rates.
In addition to realising cash tied up in your accounts receivable we also provide purchase order finance, letters of credit and export funding. We have specialist solutions for Trucking and Staffing companies.
By focusing on your business assets we do not look to your personal credit rating or tieing up your personal assets, we work with many family owned business especially being a 200 year old family owned business ourselves.
Contact me if you want us to provide a quotation or if you work with businesses we help (we pay for successful referrals).
