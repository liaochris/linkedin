Joe Cuviello’s Summary

My professional experience encompasses 4 main areas as follows:

Business Executive - I have run the operations for pacificoncepts for 6 years. This includes setup and the continued operation of the companies banking, accounting, scheduling, account management, project management, communications, and technology.

Designer & Developer - My primary job for pacificoncepts is design, production and consulting for our clients. My design skills encompass design of print, multimedia and web based projects. I pay particular attention to branding and brand development in my design. I have 13 years of experience designing and producing web sites, related web advertising, SEO, ecommerce and web applications. I have 18 years of experience designing and producing print. This includes print production skills to bring a project to press for successful printing.

Enterprise Management Software - I have successfully planned, setup, implemented, tested, operated, trained and currently manage an Enterprise Management system for one of our customers. The customer uses NetSuite for complete operations of a wholesale and retail sales business with an ecommerce web site. I also consult, setup, train and manage other client installations for CRM, Sales Force Automation, and Business Accounting solutions.

PC Consulting & Training - I have provided consulting and training for individuals and business on PC's and Macs for over 20 years. I make recommendations and provide information regarding all aspects of business technology. This includes everything from what systems and software to use, how to set them up, what online services to subscribe too, setup and operation, and what service providers to use for web site hosting, dedicated server hosting, and ecommerce.

My current goal is to bring pacificoncepts from it's existing position as a business-to-business consulting and service provider for small companies to a higher level.
