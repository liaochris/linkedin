Kelly Louch’s Summary

Established in 2003, The Connection specializes in providing professional models for sporting events, tours, tradeshows, parties and all aspects of live events nationwide.
The Connection brings experience totaling over 20 years in the model and talent industry both in the US and international marketplace.
Our experienced, reliable and enthusiastic models are the perfect choice to represent your company, from brand ambassadors, crowd gatherers, bilingual talent, demonstrators to glamorous models to welcome clients to your company event.
Wherever and whatever the job we always exceed our clients expectations with our hand picked promotional talent’s professionalism and enthusiasm!
With a wide range of clients ranging from Oakley, Monster Energy, Amp'd Mobile, KMC Wheels, Boost Mobile, Red Bull, Pirelli, Rockstar Energy and Suzuki to name a few. The Connection has provided talent for the following events: X Games, Boost Mobile Pro of Surfing, Amp’d Mobile Supercross, AMA Motocross, US Open of Surfing, ASR, Surf Expo, US Red Bull MotoGP, Games of Sk8, Major League of Gaming, UFC, Jeremy McGrath Invitational, DUB Custom Car and Concert Series, AND1 and many more!
If it has ANYTHING to do with extreme sports, we are there!
With such vast experience in the industry, we provide you with models with the appropriate look, experience and skills necessary to make your event a success!
Last year we provided talent in over 45 cities in over 30 states, from coast to coast.
Previous nationwide tours include AND1 Mixtape Tour 2005 (30 markets), DUB Custom Car and Concert Series 2005, 2006, 2007 (16 markets), Amp’d Mobile Supercross 2005, 2006, 2007 (16 markets), Professional Bull Riders 2006, 2007 (9 markets) and more
All successfully completed with repeat contracts! The Connection has models working every single weekend, in multiple states/cities, consecutively for the past 3 years
We provide more than just models, we provide unmatched service!
