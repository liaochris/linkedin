Mark Adkison’s Summary

I've spent the last 17 years in the music business and it has been a lot different than I originally envisioned it. From radio and publicity to marketing, promotions and the web, I've seen a lot of facets of the business in my time from radio stations, to sales at an independent label to ForeFront Records and EMI. At the end of the day...I love what I do and the people with whom I'm team members.

I currently find myself in the position of VP Digital Strategy & Development at EMI Christian Music Group looking after almost 60+ websites and all the strategy and devel;opment related to consumer marketing and infrastructure. I would've never dreamed in less than 3 years that we'd be where we are currently with our web initiatives. I continue to enjoy my time here at EMI, watching the industry struggle through the painful times their in right now and watching the light bulb go on with others that finally strating to "get it." Despite the challenges, I know we'll continue to strive to be the best in the business given our financial backing and commitment to growing the industry and continually look to the future but foresake the past as we lead and impact culture.

The best part of my job is pretty basic...find better ways of connecting people to our artists and our music using technology and the web. This basic premise continues to be our goal. The ability to touch fans and engage them in a meaningful interactive experience is a job we don't take lightly. Since accomplishing this is a continually moving target, my goal will always be to find innovative ways to reach fans and strive to do it with excellence. I find a great sense of enjoyment and excitement by watching the team push the envelope and seeing fans connect to the music, message and artists! I'm honored to work on such a great team and to have the fortunate circumstance to work for a great company with great leadership and vision for the future.
