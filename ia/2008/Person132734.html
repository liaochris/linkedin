Mike Walmsley mike @ MikeWalmsley.com’s Summary

I am an open networker, feel free to connect with me: Mike @ MikeWalmsley.com.

If you are a recruiter, please also feel free to request an unlimited and free, 7 day trial of my 28 online recruitment videos.

Entrepreneur with over 20 years high level recruitment experience. International recruitment trainer and presenter at recruitment trade associations and annual events. Formerly a big billing recruiter and MD of an international recruitment group, which grew organically from 3 to c100 staff in 7 years. Much of this growth was underpinned by the excellence of the recruitment training programmes I put in place.

In 2003, I filmed the world's largest collection of recruitment training DVDs, which I now sell around the world on DVD and online video.

In 2005, I founded Elite - Europe's largest recruitment-specific CEO development programme - I also speak internationally as an expert recruitment trainer in the USA, Canada, Australia, South Africa and Asia.

I hope this doesn't sound arrogant, but I am considered to be one of the world's leading recruitment trainers and I specialise in turning good recruiters into SuperBillers™
