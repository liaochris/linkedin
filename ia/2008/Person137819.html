Nadia Gruzd’s Summary

Nadia joined Emmanuel’s Staffing in South Africa in 1989 and worked as recruiter, manager, & trainer until immigrating to Canada in 1992. Nadia joined Medical Recruiters International and was asked to manage the company which grew from 4 - 22 people in 2 years. In 1995 she founded Worldwide Staffing in Canada and opened up the US division, Medistaff in San Diego in 1997. In 2001 she founded Unlimited Nurse Search.

In 2004 & 2006, UNS was nominated for Best Employer Work Place Excellence Award by San Diego Society for HR Management. The company has recently had a name change to Unlimited MedSearch to accommodate clients needs to place medical professionals across the board. The company began selling franchises in 2007.
