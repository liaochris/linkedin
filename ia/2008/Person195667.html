Vernon Wanner’s Summary

Vernon is the owner of First Webs, Inc., a web development and Internet consultancy firm. Vernon has more than 25 years of web design, business development, and management experience. He also operates and maintains several online businesses in the health and wellness fields.

Vernon has held Manager, Director and VP positions with leading manufacturing concerns. He is a CIW Certified Webmaster, an Electronic Commerce Certified Professional, and a Certified Internet Consultant.

Vernon's goal is to help each client use the Internet for increased profitability within their specific marketing and business plans. A few common mistakes Vernon can correct include just "plain bad web design", improper targeting of the site, and lack of knowledge on how to get visitors to one's site. Once a regular flow of visitors to the client's site is obtained, web 2.0 tools are used to promote the site, increase search engine ranking, and help close sales. Each client's needs are differents, and programs are totally customized to the client's business.

Vernon Wanner’s Specialties:

Vernon performs thorough research on a clients web presence and then outlines Internet changes needed. Sophisticated tools used include Keyword Discovery, nicheBot, IBP, and SEOInsite's analysis tools. He is active in search engine forums, uses article marketing, directory submissions, and designs/develops blogs.

Vernon is member of SEMPO (Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization), SEW (Search Engine World), and the Dot.Com.Secrets Protege Coaching program.
