Christine A. Matava’s Summary

Christine has also has worked in an Alternative Investments Accounting at a leading investment bank doing cost analysis, pricing public securities, updating NAV (net asset value) for private equity fund of funds investments, management reporting for management of all Alternative investments including profit and loss determinations, and reconciling cash flows for all fund of funds investments.

Christine worked with this group within the bank to value each investment monthly as well as organize and evaluate its position within the group. She reconciled the balance sheet, managed funding for new investments including obtaining legal documents and wiring to meet capital calls. She authored the first Accounting Procedures for a boutique international Investment Bank that recently became a publicly traded company, and also wrote the Sarbanes Oxley controls that accompanied it. In her career prior to joining Jefferson Wells, Christine traded most equity, fixed income and derivative markets.
