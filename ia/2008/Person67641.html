Gary Dahl’s Summary

I have over 17 years of experience in the retail/wholesale/manufacturing industry. Areas of experience include Store Management, Inventory Buying/Planning, Direct Marketing Analysis/ Planning & Product Line Management.

My experience includes exposure to Apparel Design/Development, Sourcing, Materials Planning & Financial Backlog Forecasting.

I have worked for/with both national and regional retailers in the categories of: Department Stores, Big Box Sporting Goods, Outdoor Specialty, Catalog & Internet in both the U.S. & Canada with some limited exposure to overseas retailers.

My long term goal is to become a senior executive in a company that sets the standard. I aspire not to be a leader by only merits of business success, but more importantly, one that creates a positive, fun environment where employees are encouraged to think for themselves, be creative, and most of all, respect me for my leadership and not merely my title.
