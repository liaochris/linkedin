Christopher "Chris" Miller’s Summary

Mr. Miller has over 20 years of combined industry experience including a successful military career. Over the last 10 years, Mr. Miller has individually performed and led recruiting campaigns responsible for over 310 placements representing some $22 million in payroll and $5 million dollars in sales revenues.

Mr. Miller's earlier experience includes recruiting directorships at two national information technology search firms and industry-specific experience as a marketing representative within IBM’s National Federal Marketing division.

Mr. Miller specializes in recruiting chief level executives, senior management and key individual contributors supporting companies in diverse industries both in early-stage technology startups and emerging growth companies within the U.S. Among these are companies within digital media, mobile, wireless, Internet, broadband, and software industry spaces.

Mr. Miller has further recruited and placed vast numbers of key individual contributors targeted more deeply within client organizations. He has placed candidates both within technology and non-technology skills domains of software engineering, applications development, technology operations, line-of-business operations, sales, marketing, finance and accounting, legal, R&D and general business functions.

Mr. Millers' other experience centers on supporting key expansion plans for organizations offering search engine services, E-commerce consulting, publishing, technology R&D, and distance-learning education.

Mr. Miller graduated from the United States Naval Academy with a Bachelor of Science in Finance and General Engineering. He is a retired military Commander with 17 years of active and reserve experience serving both as a Naval Flight Officer flying open-ocean missions in P-3B Orion anti-submarine aircraft and, in a separate role, as a Naval Intelligence Officer.
