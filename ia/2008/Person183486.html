Sunil Govind’s Summary

Combining my experience in the hospitality industry with knowledge of e-commerce, Presently president of three major travel Web sites based in Orlando, Florida: Orlandoescape.com, Floridaescape.com, and the newly launched Hotelsescape.com. These Web sites direct visitors to the top travel deals and valuable tourist information for some of the most popular destinations throughout Florida and around the world. In 2006, Sunil launched a business venture with a premier home builder to promote vacation home sales and rentals in the Orlando area.

Sunil is a seasoned professional with more than 20 years of experience in the hospitality industry and over 10 years in high level management. His job responsibilities as the vice president of operations for a major property management company included designing, developing, and implementing the electronic market segment for the company’s hotels and he was responsible for operations at 22 properties. Sunil’s numerous professional affiliations include the Orlando/Orange County Visitor and Convention Bureau (OOCVB), Hospitality Sales and Marketing Association International (HSMAI), and the Central Florida Hotel & Lodging Association (CFHLA).
