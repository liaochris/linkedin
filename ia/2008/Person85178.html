Jason Weiss’s Summary

First-rate communicator capable of earning respect via concise and strategic value propositions delivered to business stakeholders, while explaining the corresponding technical concepts and tactical architectures to technologists. In excess of 17 years of diverse business and technology experience, from entrepreneurial endeavors to working with Fortune 500 companies.

Described by my peers as a Java/J2EE and Flex “Code god” who excels at solving complex problems, capable of thinking on his feet and has an amazing ability to learn advanced concepts in short periods of time- a technological leader by every metric. Fluent in Java, Flex ActionScript and several flavors of SQL, each used on projects within the last 2 years. Microsoft Certified Professional, former Microsoft Certified Trainer.

Exceptional background in cryptology and security; formerly served as a cryptologist in the US Navy with a top-secret (SCI) clearance (expired), author of “Java Cryptography Extensions” published by Morgan-Kaufmann, conceived and developed patent-pending “Volume Mount Authentication” security technology, and recognized by the National Security Agency to be a “talented security designer” of critical infrastructure protection (27 Nov 2000, signed Paul A Pittelli, Chief, Information Assurance Research Office).

Jason Weiss’s Specialties:

Java (SE, EE and ME), server side architectures, Adobe Flex front ends.
