Stephen Wheatley’s Summary

It is very difficult to summarise exactly what I do but here goes.

If you have a business (or an idea for a business) and would like to accelerate its profit performance then it would be a good idea to talk to me.

I have nearly 30 years of experience of taking businesses and improving their performance.

I have raised or been involved in raising finance through private equity, IPOs, AIM and full Stock Market listings.

I would be interested in talking to people who want to improve their business’ performance and are looking for some external help and maybe a little bit of inspiration......

Stephen Wheatley’s Specialties:

I am an entrepreneur with a sales and marketing background, who loves the challenges involved in starting and growing businesses.

I have had multiple start up experiences, some sponsored by companies, some by external investors and more recently by my partners and I.

Increasingly I work with the boards of our client companies to guide and coach the directors, especially at times of rapid change.
