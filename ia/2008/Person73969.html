Gwyneth McNeil’s Summary

I happily serve as a Stress Management Consultant, Life Coach, Board Certified Hypnotist and Trainer; Marketer, Reiki Practitioner and Master Instructor, Author, Presenter and Poet. My background includes a BS in Psychology, with additional Certificates in Addiction Counseling, Breathwork, Breathwalking, & Chi Gong.

I am the CEO of the Academy of Life Management in Salt Lake City, Utah, where we provide products and services for Stress Management, Personal Development, and Self-Mastery from a Body-Mind-Spirit perspective.

Currently I serve as the President of the Salt Lake Chapter of the National Guild of Hypnotists, and the Western Regional Coordinator for World Hypnotism Day. I am also a frequent Presenter for the NGH National Convention and a delighted recipient of the NGH's prestigous 'Member of the Year' award.

I enjoy working with people and helping them find and achieve their personal passions in life. I love being there "when the light goes on".

What makes me unique? I am a woman with a vision, with a kind heart, and a passion for life and living. Everything I do revolves around assisting in the awakening of the planet.

But then, there are many women with visions, with kind hearts, and a passion for life and living. And there are happily more and more women and men who are about awakening. So, I suppose what makes me unique is the particular way I go about doing what I do, and being who I am - in my particular circumstances, moment by moment, day by day.

I love to connect with like-minded people that we may share the journey and be of assistance in the process.
