Amy Fulmer’s Summary

We serve clients throughout Ohio during case investigation, settlement negotiations, litigation, trial and appeal.

Our office is located in the growing city of Dublin, Ohio, in an office complex which provides free parking. Located inside Interstate 270, we are easily accessible from the major highways and roads such as Ohio Route 315, Interstate 71, Interstate 70, and State Routes 33 and 161. We are located approximately nine miles from downtown Columbus, 75 miles from Dayton, 115 from Cincinnati, 105 miles from Portsmouth, 60 from Chillicothe, 42 miles from Circleville, 73 miles from Zanesville, and 135 from the West Virginia/Pennsylvania border. Our central location means that we can reach even the furthest points in Ohio within a three hour drive time.

Our physical facility consists of a first floor office of approximately 3500 square foot office suite, with two conference rooms available for use. In addition, we have nine other office locations available to us throughout Columbus and its suburbs.
