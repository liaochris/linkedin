Randy Parker’s Summary

BACKGROUND: Experienced customer-centric software executive and architect of numerous software systems. Founded Constant Contact (Nasdaq:CTCT), www.ConstantContact.com, which is the leading self-service email marketing service (over 200,000 customers), and also a former Inc. 500 company.

STRENGTHS: Unique combination of expertise on the business strategy side (market understanding, sales, partnerships) with technical leadership. Relied upon by clients (startups to Fortune 500's) for 20 years to solve hard problems.

Randy Parker’s Specialties:

Focused primarily on SaaS companies targeting the sales and marketing function in small businesses. Small business, SMB, vertical market / industry-specific software, ISV strategy, software as a service (SaaS), online communities / social networks, CRM, email marketing, online marketing
