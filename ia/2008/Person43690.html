Danielle de Boer’s Summary

I have worked in educational consultancy (profit and in-house) for the past 7 years, nationally and abroad: EU-region countries, UK, Estonia, Finland and Indonesia. The past few years, I have mostly been researching, monitoring and coaching complex institution-broad (planned) innovation processes in higher education. I'm currently doing my PhD-thesis on that same topic. At this moment, I'm fulfilling a research position at Fontys/CHEPS and I'm project leader of the monitoring of 200 bachelors and 37 institutions on innovation progress and success and failure factors underlying this.

Danielle de Boer’s Specialties:

Research on innovation processes: compatibility of the innovation with the organization, reinvention, transformational and distributed leadership.

Coaching of teachers and managers on process and curriculum level

Design of learning and teaching in firms: education as a vehicle for companies to take up a more social responsible approach

Methodological issues of research in on organizations, in particular public services (bureaucratized market-forms and influence of New Managerialism)
