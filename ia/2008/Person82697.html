James Beane’s Summary

I place a high value on honesty, integrity, and ethical business practices. I have been both an employee and an employer, but enjoy and thrive on workplace positions that allow me to work largely as an independent.

In my position at Interlink Supply, offering chemicals and equipment to professional carpet cleaning and restoration companies, I was able to revive business and produce new sales in a temporarily neglected region of Arizona, the results were fantastic and the rapport and goodwill created generated additional business.

I started out in Horticulture and Landscape Architecture at Cal Poly Pomona, and developed a small landscape route in Laguna Beach, moved on to carpet installation both as a helper, and then a sub-contractor and employer.

Several years later, I continued on in the textiles industry, but found that carpet cleaning could be more profitable and less draining physically. I developed a one man operation that grossed in excess of 60,000 a year.

I have held several sales positions over the years, and with every postion that I have prospered in, it has been because of the opportunity to interact with clients and provide assets that truly met their needs.
