Don Coorough’s Summary

1976/77 - Murakami Wolf Films: Production Assistant, "The Extraordinary Adventures of the Mouse and His Child," "Puff the Magic Dragon" and commercials. Production Manager, commercials, by 1978.

1978/79 - Lisberger Studios Production Manager on "Animalympics".

1979/80 - Spungbuggy Works, Head of Animation, managed, produced and directed commercials, managed and directed mattes for 1980 titles for Magnum PI.

1981 - Kinney Vallas - Production Manager on commercials; Final Checker on "Stanley the Ugly Duckling".

1979/1981 and 1983/1987 - wrote songs for and performed in bands on "new wave" club circuit. Wrote song "They Died Young" for The Tooners, available on iTunes.

1989 - obtained paralegal certificate.

1990 - Flagstaff, AZ - paralegal in Criminal, Personal Injury, Family, Business, and Bankruptcy.

1996 - Baer Animation - Production Manager: "Rodeo" for The Fremont Street Experience, educational CD Rom "Connie and Bonnie's Birthday Blast Off", TV special "Annabelle's Wish", and commercials; Animation Director for 1998 Discovery Channel Eco-Challenge.

1999/2000 - Log Home Design Ideas - freelance writer. Ghost wrote a pilot teleplay and series proposal of a sitcom for Triscari Productions.

2006/2007 - Started Alliance for the Unification of Mankind (AUM) Publishing Company, wrote, edited, self-published and distributed three editions of chapbook, "A Critical Mass and Other Poems."

2007 - Poem "Parched Lips" published in October.

2008 - Poem "Flickering Candlelight" published in January.

2008 - Completing final editing of manuscipt "Love-ism: A Critical Mass, Other Poems and A Layman's Philosophical Ramblings" for spring publication. Seeking investors/corporate officers to establish AUM as a non-profit corporation and in publishing worthy manuscripts that advance peace, non-violence, cooperation, understanding and individual freedom.

Don Coorough’s Specialties:

Poet for 39 years, published by literary journal "edificeWRECKED" in their final issue and in issue 5 of The Aggregated Press; poetry to be featured in "Blood Moon Rising Magazine" (five poems in issue #34 and "A Critical Mass" in issue #35) and Eleventh Transmission (7 poems to be published in the March issue); freelance magazine articles published in magazine Log Home Design Ideas; 15 years in animation, 39 years in music, 10 years in law as a paralegal, 8 years as a freelance writer.
