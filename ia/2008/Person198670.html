Warren H. Wong’s Summary

Warren Wong is a senior human resources professional with several years of progressive human resources management experience. He has a successful leadership track record in working in high growth complex matrix organizations ranging from local start-up to Fortune 500 companies.

As a strategic business partner, change agent, and member of senior management teams, Warren established the Canadian human resources functions for national and global internet/technology companies.

Warren has the ability to effectively contribute, support and influence organizational operations and strategic business strategies working with company CEOs, presidents and business unit heads. He is able to contribute to and lead and/or build a full service human resources function that transforms, enables and inspires organizations.
