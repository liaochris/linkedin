Dominic Houston’s Summary

I am highly experienced in the internet and media industries and have a unique set of experience and skills:

- from 1992-1996 I ran my own music business;
- I qualified as a lawyer & practised law for 6 years during which time I worked for MTV Europe and negotiated all the deals for AOL Europe's music service including AOL's exclusive sponsorship & content distribution of Live8;
- I am completing an MBA at Henley Management College (ranked number 1 in the world for its full time course in 2006); and
- from 2006-2007 ran business development for AOL Europe's search advertising business. This involved negotiating a series of deals with Google Europe, including the implementation of AOL's strategic audience partnerships with Carphone Warehouse, Neuf Cegetel, & Hansenet.

Since August 2007 I have been leading LastFm's music & Licensing function and am responsible for licensing last.fm's content platform on a global basis. This involves structuring and executing a strategy for working with music rights owners in
14 countries.
