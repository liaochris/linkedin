Steven Ketchpel’s Summary

I help clients discover the patterns of usage and classes of users that signify a healthy online business. Who are the high value users of an online service? What traits do they share? Where do they come from? I mine hundreds of gigabytes of web server and application log data to find the key metrics that enable executives to focus on profitable growth. I work primarily with web startups to create the processes and platforms to track the metrics that CEO's and BoD's want on their dashboard.

Steven Ketchpel’s Specialties:

Data analytics; clickstream analysis; KPI definition; web log parsing; data visualization; customer acquisition
