Cathrene Rowell’s Summary

I have Just moved back to the UK after nearly 7 years in Germany. I moved to Germany 2000 upon completion of my Degree to work at MediaTransfer - an online Market research agency based in Hamburg. After 3.5 years experience in both qualitative and quantitative market research, I took a position of Project manager at rmm, managing qual and quant projects on behalf of a wide range of international market research institutes. I spent a year at at random international, which i joined in in February 2006 as a Senior Research Executive, before setting my sights back on the UK, Europe and the World.
