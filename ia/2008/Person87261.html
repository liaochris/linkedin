Jeffery Bray’s Summary

SAC Financial Services is in the business of helping companies achieve their goals and objectives through experienced and proven programs that positively impact both perception and performance.

The ever changing landscape of regulatory requirements and a general cynicism of investors have created a need for better accountability, transparency and credibility in corporate communications. Proactive and precise outreach, employed properly, has a decided influence on distinguishing a company from its peers.

Beyond clear and disciplined communications, a company must employ best-of-breed tools and solutions to ensure optimal fundamental performance. A third-party view, rooted in deep capital market knowledge, can provide a new vantage point on methods to best exceed corporate goals and objectives.

SAC Financial Services Corp. (“SAC”) provides comprehensive consulting and communications solutions for both private and public companies that go far beyond the basics of traditional “investor relations.” With strong capital markets experience and diverse sector knowledge, alongside broad media backgrounds, our team is positioned to provide full-service integrated solutions to clients.

Whether as a complete outsource solution or a value-added project based partner, SAC has the experience required to manage and enhance the investment community’s perception of our client companies while providing proven tools and solutions for operational success, leading to our clients reaching their true intrinsic value.
