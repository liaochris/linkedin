Connie Kadansky’s Summary

Why I am on Linked In:

I want to be a powerful resource to others within my network as well as target my market and build relationships and business more effectively and efficiently.

What I offer:
Solutions to Your Sales Call Reluctance through training, coaching, keynote and breakout speaking engagements.

I sell the SPQ*Gold -- only assessment in the world that measures Sales Call Reluctance for companies who hire salespeople who must prospect consistently. This assessment is also used for training and development of current salespeople and executive recruiters.

I am Certified consultant for Kolbe assessments, the world's leading provider of instinct-based performance forecasting software, that helps you take the guesswork out of hiring all positions within your company.

What I am looking for:

Decision makers of companies who have salespeople who must prospect consistently or they will fail, i.e. insurance, financial services, computer software/hardware, banking, real estate, print media, consulting, telecommunications, etc.

Individual financial advisors and salespeople who have the will to succeed, but they are not getting in front of enough prospective buyers on a consistent basis.
