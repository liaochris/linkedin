Peter Osicka’s Summary

Combining a unique creative insight along with marketing and retail trade knowledge, Peter is recognized as an ‘award winning’ marketing and creative expert. He has been called upon to judge numerous marketing awards such as 2008 Reggie Awards and 2004 CMA Awards. He has personally developed a ‘breakthrough’ promotional marketing campaigns such as BIC Inc./CFL “Kick for $1 Million” – Cadbury Beverages/Aeroplan 3 year-long Loyalty Program – High Liner Foods/NHL “100th Anniversary of Stanley Cup” Collector Cards among others. He possesses an extensive depth of experience working with international marketers such as Nestle, Cadbury, Unilever, Ferrero and BIC. His unique understanding of both the creative and strategic issues of brand marketing, advertising and consumer promotions help clients drive business and increase their market share.
