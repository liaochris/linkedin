Robert M. Lilienfeld’s Summary

Management consultant with 30 years of experience in strategic planning, marketing, branding, advertising, and marketing communications. Strong background in both consumer packaged goods and business-to-business branding, marketing planning, and creative development.

I am also a recognized expert in the areas of waste prevention, source reduction, and environmental trends. Co-author of Use Less Stuff: Environmental Solutions for Who We Really Are (Random House, 1998), and Editor of the influential ULS Report. Featured on over 500 tv and radio interviews, and have been a regular commentator for NPR on environmental issues.

My goal is to use my skills to help create a sustainable economy that also fosters a sustainable ecology.

Robert M. Lilienfeld’s Specialties:

Branding, strategic planning, creative development and evaluation, motivation research
