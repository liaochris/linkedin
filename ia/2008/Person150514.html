Gian Luca Petrillo’s Summary

In IBM: I'm working as a Client Executive improving relations in the central government area and selling innovation to improve customers' values. I'm working also as a security clearance administrator.
I worked in IBM as a business developer with PAs in 2006. I focused on open source and security.

Senior advisor of the Italian Ministers of Communications - Hon. Gasparri (2001-2005) and Hon. Landolfi (2005-2006). Responsible for keeping the relations with the telecommunication operators, ICT companies, the public administration organization and the main private enterprises operating with the broadband development, network security, new technologies and the law developments. Member of the Board of Directors of Infratel Italia SpA – the company that is developing the new strategic broadband infrastructure in underdeveloped areas. Also provides lectures and makes researches for the academic institutes. Has joined the Visitors Leadership Program of US State Department and keeps relations with the main American enterprises and administrations.

Lobbying ability, institutional relationship.

• An expert of the network security; the president of the Committee of Network and Information Security in the Ministry of Communications (monitoring and controlling the internal network, internal policy and web); the member of the National Observatory of Network Security (law enforcement, legal interception); wrote publications concerning the BS7799; also joins the international meetings concerning the critical infrastructure data protection.
• Software developer with PHP, Java, C (and other main programming languages). An expert of the digital terrestrial television (DVB-T), follows the MHP applications market. Possess deep knowledge about the television system and electronic communications’ frameworks.
• Possess valuable knowledge of the Italian public administration market (both - central and local) as well as analytical thinking and service planning skills.
