Jim Baker’s Summary

Contributor to Donald and James Kirkpatrick’s work on training evaluation, Jim Baker is a consultant in research-based performance and process improvement whose career consists of in-house assignments, consulting, and distance learning instruction in equal parts.

References in print by author -
• Chris Moore, Chief Learning Officer (CLO), In Practice, February 2008, 'The Regence Group: Blended Measurement'
• Donald L. Kirkpatrick and James D. Kirkpatrick refer in print to these assessments and techniques at Regence in their Implementing the Four Levels: A Practical Guide for Effective Evaluation of Training Programs
• Jim Baker, 'Evaluating an Information Technology Skills Training Program,' in Donald L. and James D. Kirkpatrick, Evaluating Training Programs: The Four Levels (3rd)

Presentations -
• 'Measuring Learning Success: More than an Afterthought' presented to ASTD Techknowledge Conference
• 'Integrating Kirkpatrick’s Levels 1, 2, and 3 in Initial and Follow-up Assessments' presented to ASTD Cascadia Regional Conference
• 'Kirkpatrick's Training Evaluation Levels 1, 2, and 3' at Regence' presented to Questionmark Perception User Conferences
• 'eLearning Measurement Tasks and Techniques at Regence' presented to Dev Con
