Juston Parker’s Summary

Juston Parker, President and CEO of Parker Hospitality Group, Inc. and Executive Vice President, Supplier Services and Strategy of Gusto LLC, is an experienced performer in the hospitality industry. He began his career in hotel reservations and has extensive experience in all areas of hotels from operations, sales & marketing, to food & beverage, banquet & conference services.

Mr. Parker's experience and ability to look at the big picture has helped him develop strategies for revenue maximization that have successfully help many industries in areas all around the globe. Prior to his career in hospitality, he lobbied at the state and federal level representing the economic interests of many and served on several congressional task forces including Emergency Medical education and standards and the ComCare Alliance (developers of "OnStar" technology). His experience in United States and foreign affairs helped to develop a base of global economic knowledge second to none.

Throughout his career Mr. Parker has developed and nurtured a passion for creating top line revenues and strategically growing businesses, through new and innovative revenue strategies. His analytical approach to revenue management has resulted in increased revenues, even in challenging times. His drive for cost of sale and pricing has impacted a wide variety of businesses which relied on his expertise to increase top line and bottom line revenue. As a published author on the subject of Revenue Management, Juston has been interviewed and/or quoted on CNN, Yahoo Finance, and Fox News, as well as featured in publications around the world ranging from HSMAI, to Thailand Hospitality, University of Florida, and Event Solutions to name a few.
