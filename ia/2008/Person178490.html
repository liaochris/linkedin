Alessandro Sordi’s Summary

Alessandro Sordi is one of founder of DADA, now he is a Senior VP . Dada S.p.A. is an international leader in both Community & Entertainment services for web and mobile, as well as Domain, Hosting & online Advertising services.
With more than 12 million users in over 40 countries worldwide, Dada gives people the chance to express themselves, have fun and make new friends through their “personal space” sharing thoughts, photos, music and videos. The Dada Group also boasts 840,000 registered domains with more than 22,000 new registrations per month.

Dada is listed on the Italian Stock Exchange (MTA STAR: DA.MI) and has among its shareholders the Dada management and founders, with 12.54%, and the RCS MediaGroup, with 46.54% of the share capital.

Today Dada is organized around 2 main areas of business: Dada.net (community & entertainment services focusing on the music) and DadaPro (professional services for the presence & advertising services on the Net).
