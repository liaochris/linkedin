Amit Haralalka’s Summary

Amit is managing an independent Business Unit at Quetzal Verify, dealing with Human Resource services.

Prior to this he has worked with Cognizant for over 2 years as an analyst with the CRM Practice. During this period he also partnered with a leading educational services provider to develop a training course to help over 700 engineering graduates get jobs. During his engineering days, he has interned with IBM and been technical consultant to a law-firm.

Amit holds a Computer Engineering degree from BIT Mesra. He was a part of the Placement Cell and has served as President of the UNESCO Club - where he helped launch the club's first Annual Magazine. Amit was part of a rock-band as a drummer and backing vocalist. He is involved with an NGO dealing with Child Rights and is also an avid blogger.
