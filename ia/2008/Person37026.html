Christopher St. John’s Summary

Call it marketing, propaganda, or meme war, my skillset is all about using art to make people do things.

In addition to my award-winning advertising work for companies like Google, Sprint, and L'Oreal, you may have seen:

• My articles in psychology publications and creative industry magazines
• My short films in film festivals around the world
• My comic strip in both Advertising Age and "alternative" publications

My latest television appearance was on the May 19, 2007, KRON morning show with film critic Jan Wahl. We discussed my short film "Canta Succubi," which appeared recently in the San Francisco International Festival of the Arts and the San Francisco Black Film Festival.

One of my recent magazine articles was the cover article for the July/August 2007 issue of Create Magazine. Called "Concept to Reality," it covered the ten coolest creative projects done in America in the last year.

Christopher St. John’s Specialties:

• Creative direction: Leading teams in creating and executing vivid, powerful advertising campaigns
• Writing: Scripts, advertising, positioning documents, essays
