C. Enrique Ortiz’s Summary

An industry recognized subject matter expert in end-to-end mobile computing, a known author and technologist evangelist, possessing skills and experience in strategy, management, architecture and development. My true passion is mobile and embedded technologies.

Prior to founding eZee inc. (see http://www.ezee.com) I worked in management and engineering positions at Artemis Wireless Werks, IBM, Aligo, and AGEA Corporation. With 17 years of experience in technology development, I also helped define the Java platform that powers millions of mobile handsets today.

I am the owner and principal at Artemis Wireless Werks (see http://www.artemiswerks.com), a consulting business in the mobile space that has helped dozens of technology companies with their mobile strategy and product development.

I’m a founding member of the Austin Wireless Alliance (see http://www.austinwirelessalliance.org), the Carnival of the Mobilists, and founder of the Mobile Monday Austin chapter (see http://www.mobilemondayaustin.org). I'm also a member of the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM).

My weblog is About Mobility (see http://weblog.cenriqueortiz.com), a recognized mobility blog.

C. Enrique Ortiz’s Specialties:
