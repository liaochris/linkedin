Julie Booth’s Summary

Experienced management professional with strengths in catalyzing teams to change and improve the way they are doing business. Specifically, I help companies develop and lead organization-wide user experience research programs that drive increased business success.

I am an expert in designing and communicating programs that employ a combination of techniques such as web analytics, usability testing, and competitive research to provide the most cost-effective, actionable data that helps business owners improve their efforts.

I have helped many major companies and brands (HP Shopping, Nike, Intel, Outrigger Hotels and Resorts, IBM, JP Morgan Chase and Company, American Airlines, McGraw-Hill) apply user-centered research and design to product development, marketing efforts, and customer support and training in ways that added business and strategic value.
