Adam Martin’s Summary

Mainly, I'm just good at getting things done, especially in complex environments where you need to see the wood, not the trees. I prefer finding techniques or solutions that cut out whole swathes of effort, or solve many problems at once. I can sell, I can draw, I'm good at solving problems.

I'm always interested in building successful businesses, whether my own or other peoples (or just standing on the sidelines offering whatever help or advice I can) especially where aided by strong project management and the intelligent use of innovative technology. I like analysing extremely difficult and vague problem-spaces and finding optimal paths - the greater the scope, the better. In that vein I've founded three startups, and used to co-run the �50,000 business-plan competition in Cambridge (in which I was also a finalist).

For the past 5 years I've specialized in MMOGs, and whilst working as a Chief Architect and gaining a patent in clustered server technologies, I've lectured and written books and articles on: Secure development processes, Designing + implementing servers for thousands of players, and numerous guides and novel techniques.

I've helped run several not-for-profit organizations - e.g. the IGDA Alternate Reality Games SIG, the Java Games Factory (currently in beta), Cambridge Futures, CU Entrepeneurs.
