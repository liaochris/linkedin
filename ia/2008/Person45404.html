Dave Clarke’s Summary

You can contact me anytime by email on dc @ nrg-networks.com (remove spaces) or text +447768330330.

My expertise is in helping Entrepreneurs and Professionals who are uncomfortable with traditional selling to generate rapid results with relationship based businesses development. I have extensive Business Development experience in both Corporate and Start Ups.

I am CEO of NRG Business Networks and have developed the NRG Networking System. A model of building a trusted network of advocates to advance your business. Rather than look for a continuous supply of one off transactions NRG helps its members build a close trusted network of advocates to accelerate their business development.

We are looking to expand the network throughout the UK during 2008 by franchising and internationally from 2009. If you know anyone with a passion for helping others and real business development expertise then I'd like to talk to them. My contact details are above.

I have been very happily married for nearly 20 years to Kate and we have 3 children.
