Mubin Panjwani, Equity analyst’s Summary

Extremely passionate about finance and particularly stock markets. Any talk of stocks and you can bet I'll join the conversation. Like my hero Warren Buffet, I too have the ability to sense multi baggers. Also like him I also believe in investing on basis of owning the whole company. My analysis is mainly on Fundamental analysis. Intuition forms a major part of my investing strategies.

Therefore I have been investing my personal money since the last 3 years. For a perfectionist like myself average returns of 18-25% are not motivating enough to invest into a stock. Most of my investments have yielded me anywhere from 50% to 180%.

After personal sucess I decided to share the strategies with the common man. I along with my friend, Mr. Yogesh Chabria (author of CNBC bestseller - "Invest the happionaire way"), started an entity GoldStone Investment and Financial Solutions (GSIFS). While with GSIFS I have successfully coached more than 50 people to start off in the stock markets and understand investing better. After the seminar these people were in contact through our online blogs.

I am currently pursuing MBA (Finance) and CFA from ICFAI. As my expertise lies in equities, I want to broaden my horizon and learn about other finance areas.

Ideal dream job after completing my education is to become a fund manager. As I love challenges, I would ask to handle their worst performing fund.

My dream career would be as a Fund Manager, Mergers and Acquisition Specialist (Investment Banking), or in Corporate Finance.

Also being the placement coordinator of my management institute, I also am looking to network with HR managers of many companies. Possibly fulfill their recruitment requirements.

Currently working on a simple book for the common man regarding investing and understanding stock markets.
