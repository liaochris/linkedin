Tony Newell’s Summary

Tony L. Newell, CPIM C.P.M., brings over 20 years of strategic supply chain management experience to his position. Prior to joining Walbro Engine Management, he was Director, Supply Chain Management for Engenio Information Technologies, Inc. He was responsible for the entire supply chain including procurement, supplier quality engineering, warehousing and logistics, strategic supplier management and supply chain Six Sigma initiatives. He previously worked at Dell Corporation as Operations Manager, responsible for the management of third party fulfillment strategies, and with Tandem Computers for more than 12 years in a range of increasingly responsible positions, including Materials Manager, Production Control Manager, Operations Manager - Distribution Services, and Sr. Manager, Demand Planning & Forecasting.

Tony has extensive experience managing multiple suppliers based in China, Asia, Eastern Europe and Mexico, and working with and leading cross-functional, cross-geographical teams involved in New Product Introduction projects and integrated product offerings with OEM and ODM providers. He has an excellent understanding of supply chain optimization and simulation tools and can effectively develop “total landed cost” models which represent the greatest savings and ensure continuity of supply.

Tony earned a BS degree in Business Administration from Regis University in 2002. He received his MS degree in International Logistics from the School of Industrial Systems and Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2005. He is currently pursuing a DBA in International Business at Northcentral University.

He is a Certified Procurement Manager (CPM) by the Institute for Supply Management (NAPM/ISM), and a Certified Production & Inventory Management (CPIM) professional by The Association for Operations Management (APICS), in addition to being a Six Sigma Process Champion and a Certified Six Sigma Green Belt.

Tony Newell’s Specialties:

Supply Chain Optimization, Low Cost Country Sourcing, Materials Management, Organizational Development, Quality Management, Six Sigma Processes, Contracts Negotiation, Target Costing, SCOR Modeling, Sales & Operations Planning, ERP Implementation, JIT and Kanban Initiatives, 3rd Party Logistics Management, P & L Management, Asset Utilization, Balanced Scorecard Metrics, Strategic Supplier Agreements, Total Landed Cost Analysis, New Product Introduction, Engineering Change Management
