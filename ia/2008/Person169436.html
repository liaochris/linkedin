Sarah Higginbotham’s Summary

Currently heading up the Editorial Department at a fast-growing, start-up media company that produces a national magazine for a half a million young adults. Interested in the flow of content through print and an increasing number of online vehicles that can enlighten, engage, and enable audiences in innovative ways with the hope of building more involved, inspired, and informed communities.
