Fabio Espindula’s Summary

My name is Fabio Espindula, I am 21 years old, from Brazilian descent, married and living in the U.S. less than a year. In Brazil I studied and did courses in XHTML/CSS, Web Standards, Accessibility, Usability and Web Standards and was attending the second year of the Information Systems at the University of South Santa Catarina (UNISUL), decided to lock my college credits to carry out an old dream, live abroad and learn another language/culture.

I studied Basic English at Harvard for 2 months and at this time I am currently doing online course of Ruby on Rails.

I Work full-time at a startup, called TownConnect (www.townconnect.com), which is located in Waltham, MA. Currently in TownConnect I am responsible for everything that relates to XHTML/CSS and planning.

When I was in Brazil I was co-owner of a small web development agency, (Zaib Technology, www.zaib.com.br) and coordinated a team of 8 people.

On my free time, I dedicate it to learn new things and am always reading about the new trends of the market. I track over one thousand blogs/websites related to IT through my "Google Reader" and currently participate in online communities: Rails Brazil, Boston Ruby and Web Standards Group.
