Lisa Preston’s Summary

Lisa Preston, founder of Canadian Impact Business Solutions and Canadian Impact Graphics & Web Design is a business development consultant, marketing strategist, entrepreneur and website design & development expert based in Ontario, Canada.

Considered by clients as an advertising and marketing expert, Lisa has created and implemented marketing strategies, concepts and techniques for many offline and online businesses, providing expertise to small, home-based businesses to large corporations alike.

Lisa has created, developed and deployed strategic business and marketing concepts and techniques, direct mail, space ad advertising, press releases and marketing materials for a wide variety of clients, businesses, products and services.

Her ad design & copywriting skills have been featured in the Ajax News Advertiser, Markham Economist & Sun, Metro Toronto, The Mississauga News, Oshawa This Week, Peterborough This Week, York Guardian and the Shopper's Market, to name just a few.

Lisa has spoken before numerous business groups, organizations and corporations including Metroland Publishing, General Motors of Canada and Chamber of Commerce executives, just to name a few. Lisa also designed and developed the 50+ Supplement, the quarterly Sunshine Guide and the annual Christmas Carol Booklet published by Metroland Publishing, and was instrumental in the marketing and design of Khard Magazine, a national monthly publication based in Toronto, Ontario.

Lisa owns and develops many websites, as well as special reports, information products and videos. Her latest ventures include exciting new membership sites which will provide unique, "cutting edge" ways to develop and market products and services on the internet.
