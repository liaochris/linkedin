Damon Fleury’s Summary

Throughout the last ten years, I have specialized in embedded systems and network protocol software design and implementation. My career has included the development of consumer products (car computers, PDAs), high-end servers (streaming platforms), IP Telephony and Network Security products.

I have been fortunate to have contributed in many individual roles of software development as well as multiple team lead and management roles. This breadth of experience allows me to immediately adapt to challenging situations and quickly begin contributing to the success of any technology team.

While I have experience in high level management, I am currently targeting positions which require my technical skills, either in an individual contributor or team leadership (hands-on) role. I am currently targeting contract positions.
