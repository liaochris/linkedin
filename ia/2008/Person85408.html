Jatin Maniar’s Summary

Entrepreneurial Product Mgmt and Business Development exec with deep Managed Services and Software domain expertise. Solid 10+ year track record of successfully building, leading and operating all aspects of Software and Managed Services business having worked at SenseIQ, VeriSign, CGI-AMS as well as consulting internationally and with Fortune 500 customers.

Strong team player who has successfully developed and managed broad range of products and technologies such as Managed Security Services platform, Enterprise RFID/Sensor Visibility platform, Consumer Service Portals, etc.

Customer focus and field experience which has led to a history of envisioning and building innovative products and establishing product leadership.

Successfully identified initial target markets and closed first reference-able, revenue generating customers.

Open management style that fosters loyalty and has proven effective at leading teams to reach measurable objectives. Passion for early stage companies and ability to get the right things done right.

My mantras:

2. For alliances - Create value and Think what's in it for You, Me and Customer
