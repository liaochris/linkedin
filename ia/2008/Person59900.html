Eric Jacobs’s Summary

Passionate about building world-class solutions to difficult problems. Extensive experience in software business development, product management and marketing for Enterprise, Desktop and Webtop applications, with strong technical and market knowledge. Feet firmly planted in both the software and hardware worlds, including nearly two decades in industrial automation (robotics, metrology, motion control, and machine vision) for telecom, auto, electronics, semiconductor, biotech, and pharma. Currently applying factory automation and operations expertise to mass digitization of media. Enjoy start-up/entrepreunerial environments with agressive goals.
