Austin Hurst’s Summary

I gained experience starting an eBay store at the age of 11, an affiliate networking site at the age of 13, and a web development company at age 14. Before graduating high school I owned and operated a web hosting company, ad agency, and full service design firm. My first internet startup was a user generated video & media conversion service which in a YouTube type fashion let users upload and share media across a social platform. Our differentiator was the on demand conversion of the uploaded media to download to proprietary main stream portable devices such as iPods, Zunes & Cell Phones. The business evolved into a white-label video sharing platform which harnesses the technology which powered the ugc site. My most active venture is a web 2.0 consultancy called Hurst Consulting, LLC. We acquire stakes in early stage startups and assist with connecting the missing pieces of the puzzle. Whether it involves business strategy, technology development, or a little of both, we will take these companies under our wing and accelerate them to the next level. This is a very selective process and each company is carefully evaluated to ensure that they are sound investments. Our company also brings our team with it. We have experience creating innovative social media technologies including social networks, video sharing services, Facebook applications, viral widgets, and customized Myspace pages; with an emphasis on data portability, semantic web, and standards integrations such as Open Social and OpenID. Specialties in visually stunning user-interface design, highend flash animation, interactive Flex applications, mobile applications, identity solutions, print work, ecommerce sites, CMS integration, optimization, scalability, and leveraging existing code.

Currently I have ownership in 5 growing companies and actively consult in the short and long term goals of a dozen more nation wide.
