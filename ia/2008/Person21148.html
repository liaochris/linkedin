Benjamin A. Boi-Doku, CISA, CISSP’s Summary

A co-founder and managing partner at Eosensa Inc. Responsible for the vision and direction of services offered by Eosensa and an Information Security Principal for the firm.

Demonstrating efficiency and effectiveness, he has assisted various organizations manage risk, improve operational performance, and achieve strategic business objectives through the development, implementation, and remediation of internal control systems. Ben has extensive experience in providing information security and auditing services for major financial and IT service providers in both private and publicly listed organizations.
