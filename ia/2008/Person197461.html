Vivek Varshney’s Summary

Vivek graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in December 2006 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering. While at WPI, he was actively involved with the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers student branch, including being President his senior year. In addition, he was a senior tutor for various courses in the ECE department. He is currently a Research Assistant in the Machine Vision Lab, working on the Precision Personnel Locator.
