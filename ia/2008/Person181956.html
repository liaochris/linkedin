Steven Slootsky’s Summary

As the firm's founder in 1991, Steven E. Slootsky focuses his practice on Personal Injury and Workers Compensation cases. Born in Bayonne, New Jersey, Steven attended George Washington University where in 1982 he earned a B.S. in Business Administration. A graduate of Nova Law School in 1985, Steven is a member of the Florida Bar as well as the Federal Bar for the Southern District for the U.S. District Court. During his legal tenure Steven he has served as the co-chair of the Workers Compensation section for Broward County, Florida. He is a Bronze member of the Florida Workers Advocates (and a former member of the board) along with serving as an "Eagle" member of the Florida Academy of Trial Lawyers. Steven is married with three children.
