Peter Parsons’s Summary

Experienced, proactive, business-minded attorney with significant experience successfully working with public and private technology and high-growth companies as a counsel, advisor, director and investor.

Actively creates opportunities for company growth. Founder and/or board member of organizations focused on nurturing young companies and contributing to a thriving entrepreneurial community. Well- versed in angel, venture and pubic financing and active participant in Seattle and Costa Rican angel groups.

Enjoys network of close professional relationships throughout the Americas and Asia.

Peter Parsons’s Specialties:

Representation of public and private technology companies in mergers and acquisitions, venture capital financings, initial public offerings, follow-on public equity and debt offerings, strategic partnerships and licensing agreements, as well as general corporate and securities matters.

Clients include leading aerospace, software, games, manufacturing, medical technology and software companies
