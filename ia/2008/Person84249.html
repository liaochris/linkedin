Jan van der Crabben’s Summary

Game Designer with a focus on PC games. Can design systems that are fun to the player, easy to understand, but difficult to master. Always work well in a team, give constructive criticism and receive it (and work it into my designs). Highly interested in games that feature either fantasy settings or periods of history. Always looking for games with an innovative design, or to create innovative designs. Strong interest in MMORPGs, which I am also writing about academically.
