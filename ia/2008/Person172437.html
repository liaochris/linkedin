Susan Anderson’s Summary

Susan Anderson is a retired public school teacher who
has been working in the holistic health field as a massage
therapist, energy worker and educator since leaving
teaching in 2000 after 22 years as a special educator.

Her intuitive abilities and natural teaching skills allow her
to assist clients at deep levels so they are empowered to
direct their own healing processes.

As an ordained non-denominational minister, Susan helps
couples as well as individuals plan special ceremonies to
commemorate major life events including weddings, child
namings and home/business blessings and transition
ceremonies

Along with her husband Scott, Seeds for Change
Wellness was established in 2004 as a grassroots
organization providing educational information,
programs and holistic health services. They are
committed and dedicated to helping others bring
balance and harmony into their daily living. As
a couple they specialize in Energy Clearings of
Homes and Properties due to geopathic stress.

A comprehensive monthly on-line newsletter is
available at their website with the latest information
on topics related to health, wellness, spirituality
and the environment.
