HUB is an exciting new social and humanitarian business model unconditionally committed to sustainable solutions for all and accelerating an end to the cycle of poverty. This dynamic new model for effective and exponential giving unites best of breed ‘non-profits’ in the field to transition communities from survival to self-empowerment to sustainable abundance. HUB creates a full solution for poor and neglected communities around the world; not content to deliver a fish, HUB teaches people to fish and equips them with essential tools (micro-loans, education, food, water and environmental training).
