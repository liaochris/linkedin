David Bixler’s Summary

Dave Bixler is the Chief Information Security Officer for Siemens IT Solutions and Services, Inc., the US subsidiary of Siemens IT Solutions and Services GmbH, a top ten global IT services firm with worldwide revenues of $6.5 billion (USD) and over 43,000 employees worldwide. In the U.S., Siemens IT Solutions and Services, Inc has 5,000 employees and caters to client companies who are particularly attracted to Siemens IT Solutions and Services’ flexibility, financial strength and full IT outsourcing services. Dave brings almost 20 years of IT experience to this role, and is responsible for for all facets of information security, including governance, auditing, compliance, vulnerability management, security awareness, and privacy.

In 2006, Dave was nominated for the Information Security Executive of the Year award in the National category. In 2005, Dave was nominated for the Information Security Executive of the Year award in the TriState and National categories.

Dave is a member of the Project Management Institute and holds a Project Management Professional certification from that organization.

Dave has written or contributed to 12 technology and security-related books, and has been published in 8 languages. He is frequently interviewed in industry and popular magazines, newspapers and technical websites, including security-related articles published in Information Security Magazine, The Boston Globe, TechNewsWorld, Mercury News, Database Trends and Applications magazine, The Seattle Times, and TechRepublic.
