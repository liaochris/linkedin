Brandon Werner’s Summary

I am Brandon Werner, a technology entrepreneur and architect in Seattle, WA with over nine years experience both locally and internationally specializing in the financial services field. I speak at various conferences, write articles when I get a chance and try to bring the most innovative of technology and business processes together. My most recent interest has been developing a high level language model for the parsing and management of semantic reasoning systems using Lisp and simplifying SOA architectures using SDO and SCA, with particular focus on the insurance industry. I also started the JavaCast podcast with Google guy Dick Wall, which later became the JavaPosse, and did some research with Lisp and the Semantic Web writing a collection of RDF/OWL extraction and relationship parsing macros in Common Lisp. I am a member of the ACM.
