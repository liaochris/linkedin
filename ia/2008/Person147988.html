Paul Jensen’s Summary

Paul M. Jensen

Paul Jensen is a seasoned CFO with broad experience in dynamic private equity backed mid-market growth and acquisition business models, a multi-billion dollar manufacturer, and an emerging food technology manufacturer. Mr. Jensen has created significant stakeholder value in startup to $40MM manufacturing businesses in the electronics, custom metal fabrication, and food technology industries, and held several key divisional management roles with a manufacturer of major appliances.

Mr. Jensen has been instrumental in more than a dozen acquisition and divestiture transactions, has direct workout experience in a troubled debt situation, and recently played a key role in the turnaround restructuring of a wholesale lumber supplier in the Twin Cities market. Mr. Jensen has deep experience in transaction analysis, integration, financial and process re-engineering, strategic planning and execution, performance measurements, and IT system migrations and IT management. He has also played a key role in launching subsidiary operations in Hong Kong, China, and the Dominican Republic.

Mr. Jensen earned a B.S. in Finance from Iowa State University, and Graduate Program credits from Drake University and The University of Iowa. He passed the Uniform CPA Exam, and is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the Minnesota Society of CPAs (MNCPA). Mr. Jensen is a Board Committee Member of Financial Executives International (FEI) and an active member of the Turnaround Management Association (TMA).
