Susan Jacobson’s Summary

Marketing Coach & Certified Personal Brand Strategist

Susan brings a unique blend of business coaching, marketing consulting and personal branding services to small and mid-sized company’s and solo-entrepreneurs, who want to take their business to the next level of growth, but who are challenged with not having enough time, resources or expertise to get there. Susan brings 25 years of strategic marketing and sales experience, having held senior management and executive roles in a variety of service industries, combined with 9 years experience as an independent marketing consultant and business coach. The depth and breath of her business experience allows her to hit the ground running and start tackling business issues quickly and with great skill and insight.

About Susan

Susan combines her passion for helping others with a real talent for generating creative business solutions that produce solid results. She draws upon her strong business instinct, creative marketing savvy and entrepreneurial mindset to help her clients achieve their goals. This process starts with her ability to really listen to what her clients are saying and as a result she uncovers the important issues quickly and with great insight right from the first meeting.


Professional Accomplishments

Rebranded companies, turned despondent branches and departments around, contributed to the success of start-ups, created new departments from the ground-up, developed high-profile media campaigns, highly successful consumer promotions, and helped solo-entrepreneurs build successful business ventures through professional coaching, mentoring, marketing consulting and personal branding. Started her own company in 1998, called Marketing in Motion, providing marketing services to a wide variety of companies and independent business owners. In 2006 launched Newfocus coaching, providing professional coaching and personal branding
