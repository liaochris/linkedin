Roy Bassave’s Summary

I cover small and large electronic products for review and preview for all types of media.

*More than 25 years of daily newspaper and online reporting-editing experience.
*Experience managing a team of writers and supervising special sections in print and online.
*Experience in Gift Guides & Special Sections and Hardware & Software Product Reviews for the English & Hispanic media.
*Created in 1989 the original weekly video game review format called "Videogame of the Week" and "CD-ROM of the Week" in the Miami Herald and the Knight Wire.
*Created in 1991 a Video/Consumer Electronics page in Spanish called "Videomundo" in El Nuevo Herald.
*Expertise editing and writing both product reviews, gift guides and features for Hispanic Magazine, Tribune Media Service, The Marin Independent Journal, Dealerscope Magazine, Twice Daily Magazine, Visions Magazine, PSX Extreme, E-Gear, GameWeek Magazine, Fusion Magazine, E-town, The Miami Herald, The South Florida Sun-Sentinel among others.

I provide complete Notary Signing Agent Services to banks, mortgage companies, Lenders, Finance Companies, Signing Companies, closing, escrow and title companies. I am a mobile notary agent and available for signing agent assignments.

I provide these services to my clients by going to their home and/or place of business to execute closing documents for first and second mortgages.

Roy Bassave’s Specialties:

*Expert in Consumer Electronics Writing, editing, product reviews, Hispanic consumer shopping trends.
*Skilled with Internet Web browsers, online content editing, Video Editing, Dreamweaver, Excel, Photoshop, Windows Vista, MSWord and Publisher.
*Attends the Consumer Electronic Show CES and has attended the Electronic Entertainment Expo E3 and Video Software Dealers Association Show (VSDA) in the past.
