Dirk Hoag’s Summary

Over 10 years of progressively responsible experience implementing and maintaining order management, warehouse management, and ERP systems for a variety of mid size businesses, mostly using packaged systems on the iSeries (AS400) platform written in RPG, Cobol and Java.

On the hockey writing front, have written for a variety of hockey websites in the 1990's, and more recently maintain a blog which covers the Nashville Predators and also digs into the statistical analysis of the NHL. A portion of that content is featured as part of FoxSports.com's NHL coverage.
