Toren Lehrmann’s Summary

Hello, my name is Toren Lehrmann, I am a 3D Artist working in the video games industry, and have over 3 years experience creating 3D assets for real-time game engines, as well as high-res renders for architectural visualization, television, and the web. I have contributed artwork on three professional game development projects, as well as two Award Winning Unreal Tournament 2004 Modifications.

Although originally from Austin, TX, I'm currently living and working in Hamburg, Germany as a 3D Artist for Bigpoint, the largest developer and publisher of online games in the country. My responsibilities include low polygon modeling and texturing for real-time games, as well as high polygon modeling and rendering for web graphics.

I'm interested in meeting new people and hearing about opportunities in the games industry, so feel free to contact me!

Toren Lehrmann’s Specialties:

3D Studio Max, VRay, Maya, Mental Ray, Photoshop, 3D Art, Graphics, 3D Modeling, Texturing, Rigging, Lighting, Rendering, Photo Manipulation
