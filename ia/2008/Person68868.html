George Eeken’s Summary

Do you have a challenging job? I hope so. Otherwise we should meet. It’s my job to bring employers and top talent together for the perfect match. As Consultant at the Challenge Group I am always looking for the right people for (interim) jobs in Finance & Banking, HRM, Legal and Sales & Marketing.

Our mission: "Be sure you are working with fun and passion".

Within the group we also have challenging job opportunities for passionate recruiters. Maybe you can help us with our mission to become the leading recruitment agency in the Netherlands with respect to quality and client satisfaction.

I posted my profile on LinkedIn to connect with interesting people. So don’t hesitate to contact me and find out all about your next career or how I can help to fulfill your vacancies.
