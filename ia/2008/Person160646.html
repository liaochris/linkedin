Rick Moran’s Summary

Management professional with experience in airline, advertising agency, travel management and credit card industries. Served as CEO, growing company from start up to over $45 million in sales. Have also managed marketing functions in larger organizations, developing innovative programs incorporating direct marketing, online marketing, loyalty programs and traditional marketing in both B2B and B2C environments. Strengths include strategic planning, competitive analysis, financial planning, ability to effectively manage change, integration of technology to improve efficiency, staff development and communication/presentation skills. Also have corporate training experience, and taught both marketing and accounting/finance as an adjunct instructor at Concordia University, St. Paul, MN.
