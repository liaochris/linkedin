Holger Bischoff’s Summary

stockcreator is a innovative web shop offering upscale graphic design templates for many fields of business at extremely affordable prices. Thus, it is an interesting tool for media designers, graphic design freelancers, and print shops: Graphic design templates are set to become more important and more widely-used in future, as graphic design templates are quicker to use and alter. Layout development costs can be saved; in particular, there no longer is a need to develop multiple alternative designs. The client can see the designs beforehand and can pick one at a very early stage. That leaves more time and scope for tailoring the design to the client’s specific requirements.

Lay users, i. e., those who are not graphic design professionals, may use stockcreator to purchase affordable high-quality graphic design that would cost much more if bought from other sources. Moreover, they enjoy the advantage of being able to see the finished product before purchasing it. No buying a pup here… A further advantage is that there is no need to use an advertising agency, instead being able to pull an ready-made product out of the drawer.

stockcreator only works with seasoned graphics design professionals with relevant agency experience. We source the best graphic design templates wherever we find them… Aachen, Berlin, Maastricht and Beijing, China are just some of the places in which we maintain a presence. Our graphics design professionals develop their graphic design templates completely independently. However, we do not meddle with the design, allowing our graphic artists to contribute their own individual styles. We particularly appreciate Dutch graphic design, widely recognized as being at the leading edge of contemporary graphic design. As our headquarters are physically close to the Netherlands, a mere five-minute car drive away from the Dutch border, the Dutch influence is almost physically tangible.
