Daniel Fahey’s Summary

Career began as a Mechanical Designer earning an ME Degree at University of MD. Worked at several engineering companies. 1976 was invited to try Technical Recruiting at TAD Technical Services. Fast growing career quickly becoming a key account breaker and one of the top Recruiters in TAD. Became Branch, later Area Manager, and company Start Up/Troubleshooter Specialist. Opening or reinforcing 10 offices in Florida and Georgia. Hooked with small company Academy Design for a few years increased their business volume 10 fold. TAD invited me back to redevelop Atlanta market.

1991 TAD bought out C&D. The new Leadership cut staff. Divorced and Cut the same day. Moved on to a IT consulting company in Maryland and was very sucessful. Cut because the company went out of business lossing its largest client. Time to do it !!!

Debt free and with excellent credit started DanSources. Incorporated March 1993. Built IT Staffing business working Commercial and government sector clients. Later won a small government contract merging into the direct government business.

Now have GSA Contract, Certified 8a/SDB/HUBZone. Maintaining a good mix of large,medium and small clients. Earning $3 to $4 million in annual sales. Planning new Branch Operations in Garrett County, MD.

DanSources will be expanding more into direct government business as we grow. Teaming partner with 10 other companies called the Alliant Information Technology, LLC to win a small business Alliant GWAC.
