David Fraser’s Summary

David has deep experience in the supply chain and logistics sectors and bespoke software development. Prior to his current role David has managed very successful inventory management and logistics operations in the Forestry Sector in New Zealand. In addition David was the founder and CEO of Logistic Information Systems Limited, a very successful software development company focused on the Forestry and Horticulture sectors in New Zealand.
In addition David was a founder and Director of Catalyst Management Services Limited, a management consulting group that focused on the scoping and implementation of Public Infrastructure and Operations Management projects.
David's current role involves developing opportunities for Ports and logistics based companies to significantly increase their operating efficiencies through the implementation of the Jade suite of logistics management solutions.

David Fraser’s Specialties:

Executive management of technology and logistics based organizations.
Leadership of fast growing, technology focused organizations.
Effective management and marketing of software development projects.
Analytical and project based focus on revenue and profitability.
