Gautam Ghosh’s Summary

Gautam Ghosh is Country Manager of ViewSonic Technologies India Pvt. Ltd., the Indian arm of US No.1 Display Expert. He is responsible for overseeing the entire ViewSonic operations in India including the Start up operations of the company, General Management, Sales, Marketing and Business Development.

He has rich experience from various industries like IT, Laptop Computers and Engineering Services, Office Automation etc. He is an industry expert and an IT veteran with a vast experience in setting up of green field operations of companies both nationally as well as internationally. He also has in his credit setting up operation of ZEBRA Technologies Corp, in India, an US based global leader in Bar Code solution and also was their first Country Manager for India.

As the Head of India operations, Gautam is responsible for spearheading the ViewSonic`s business interest in India by providing strategic business direction to ensure continuous and dynamic growth for the brand. He also works closely with the other BUs and sub-organizations within the Asia Pacific region to collaborate on Indianising implementation of global plans and structures within India. It’s his commitment to take ViewSonic, a rather late entrant in the monitor market in India, to the top most rank from its recently acquired position as the top 3 brands in India.

In his last assignment, Gautam headed the Marketing operations of ACi ( Asia ) Ltd., a wholly owned subsidiary of Allied Computer Plc UK. ( a leading IT Hardware & Lap Top Computer Manufacturing company in UK since 1992) where he established its marketing operations in India.

Gautam started his career as a Sales Manager with Godrej and Boyce Mfg. Co.Ltd.and later moved on to occupy top positions in companies like KODAK, TechNova-Agfa etc. He completed his Business Management from Indian Institute of Management & Technology, Chennai. He has done various training programs such as Leadership and Team building from IIM – Lucknow,

Gautam Ghosh’s Specialties:

Marketing, Channel Management, People Management, Negotiation and Institutional Business Management. Team Motivation and Effective communication is his key strength.
