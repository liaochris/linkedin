  • Manager, Financial Advisory Services

    price waterhouse

    (Partnership; 10,001 or more employees; Financial Services industry)

    1985 — 1995 (10 years)

    Responsible for a wide variety of consulting engagements including business valuations, economic impact studies, financial modeling assignments, and others. During this period, he was also a founding member of the Price Waterhouse High Technology Group (Vancouver) where he focused on emerging companies in the software, hardware, Internet, telecommunications and biotechnology industries.

    In September 1995, Paul left Price Waterhouse to for Business Valuation Corp (BVC), a consulting firm that initially provided independent valuation services to public companies engaged in merger and acquisition activity, and private companies in the process of going public. BVC subsequently expanded its services to include due diligence investigations, business-planning assistance, as well as various contract services for selected investment banking and management consulting firms, both locally and internationally (First Key Consulting, CWC Capital, Strategic Analysis Europe).
