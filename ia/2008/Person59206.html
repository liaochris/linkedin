Alexandra Profant’s Summary

My professional experience is in the realm of architecture and planning with a slant on natural resources management and investigative policy into the CEQA NEPA regulatory processes. My goal is to effect policy making to allow the effectual practice of a new kind of architecture I am describing in a book I am completing that will manifest in the Tahoe Basin as well as in other sensitive watersheds throughout the world.
