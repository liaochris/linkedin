I have more than 5 years experience developing in Java, ranging from customization of existing applications to developing new ones from scratch, while interacting with customers on different stages of the projects. Personal: I have a passion for technology and its relation to business. I read daily about the latest news on IT. I like to test new technologies and devote time thinking on new ways of solving some problem. My goal in the mid term is to make a PhD related to the interaction of IT and Business.
