Gaurav Rohela’s Summary

Gaurav is a Risk Oversight Manager with Lloyds TSB Bank. He has experience in Information systems audit, Network Security, Networking & Telecom solutions, IT General Control Testing, Sarbanes Oxley Act 2002 (Applicability to section 302, 404, 309),Advisory & Attestation on SAS 70 Type I and Type II, BS 7799/ ISO 27001 security standard Audit- Implementation and Compliance, Control Assurance and related fields of work. Gaurav is ISO 27001 Lead Auditor & Implementer, CCNA, MCSE, MCDBA certified and a Cisco Firewall Specialist (CSPFA). He is responsible for managing and executing Information Risk Management engagements.


Gaurav’s key strengths are in the areas of Information security, Network Management Consultancy, Information Security policy advisory, Telecom solutions, designing IT control processes, information systems security audit, due diligence reviews, business process analysis and IT audits as part of external financial audits.
