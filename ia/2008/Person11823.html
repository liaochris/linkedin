Andrew Nevelos’s Summary

In my position I have to translate clients’ business goals into successful search engine optimization strategies, perform technical site analysis, competitive/keyword research, and link analysis to optimize various site elements, including site structure, link popularity and copywriting. I also develop ongoing customer reports, which yield qualitative and quantitative trends for ongoing SEO efforts, establish and maintain project management processes to manage multiple SEO projects at one time and work with cross-functional departments to develop and track all details of SEO initiatives as well as collaborate with Client Services and Media departments to feed SEO-specific findings into broader online media strategy. I must also have the ability to quickly and clearly examine algorithmic best practices, opportunities and solutions that are applicable to a site property. Have an established quality of web site optimization recommendations, results & client deliverables and a strong aptitude in HTML languages with track record of developing content page templates for seamless implementation and positive SEO traction. I have a solid understanding of shopping & comparison search engines, data feed creation, spec requirements, implementation issues, and QA for various uploads and the ability to help Account Managers implement or push ongoing strategic search projects for algorithmic growth.
A very important but often overlooked part of my position is staying current with industry best practices and search engine technologies.
