Józefa Fawcett’s Summary

I am a Learning Specialist covering a wide range of areas within the arena of Learning, Training, Change and Evaluation, whether at a personal or organisational level in the UK or abroad I provide:

Training & Workshop Facilitation
Voice-Over & Speaking engagements
Instructional Design and Training Delivery
Evaluation
Cognitive and Attitutidinal Mapping
Coaching & Mentoring Supervision
Business Change & Project Management
Training Standards and Auditor Certification

My expertise has been gained over 30 years or working in the private retail and employment sector, public healthcare sector (UK) and the voluntary charity sector.

I retain a good practitioner.academic balance to my work and am a senior lecturer (p/t) and sessional lecturer at two Universities (University of Westminster and Birkbeck College, University of London). I teach on PG MA in Personnel & Development, Certificate in HE: Management, PG MSc HRD and Foundation Certificate for Pharmacy Technicians. My specialist modules are: Management Development, Organisational Development, Training & Development, Learning & Development, Pharmacy Management and Knowledge Management.

More information about my range of expertise can be gained from my company website portal http://thepolexperience.com/POLPortal.htm
