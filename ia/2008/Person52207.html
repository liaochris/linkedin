Dennis Knox’s Summary

Dennis J. Knox & Company, Inc. was founded in June1977. Since inception, we have represented many “Fortune 500” entities, as well as many outstanding “Public / Privately Held” entities. Since 1990, the primary focus has been serving client company’s “Real Estate Development” needs, within the “Retail, Restaurant, Hospitality, and Commercial Developer’s” arena nationwide.
The primary goal of Dennis J. Knox, President and Founder of the company is to provide expeditious results once a search has been assigned. Every search is guaranteed to be completed within a reasonable time, which is typically between thirty (30), and ninety (90) days from inception, to the candidate’s acceptance.
This is based on success principles which embody a well-researched plan, carefully developed recruiting skills, a high level of professionalism and an appropriate sense of urgency to each client assignment.
