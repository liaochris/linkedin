Sandip Manku’s Summary

National Sales Manager, Pharmacist with MBA and over 10 years Pharmaceutical experience including Hospital and Retail sectors.

*EDUCATION*

Bachelor of Pharmacy (Monash Uni, Melbourne, Australia).
M.B.A - (Nottingham Business School, Nottingham U.K).

*WORK EXPERIENCE*

Extensive experience within the Australian and UK Pharmaceutical industries including pharmaceutical sales roles for Bayer, Sanofi Aventis and Hospira.

National Sales Manager for Generic Health, responsible for achieving sales revenue and profits through managing our sales representatives. Responsible for managing key accounts and tender submission as well as advising on sales strategy to target the Australian pharmacy market.

Previously working as Key Account Manager for Hospira Pharma. responsible for negotiating the commercial angle to key customers selling the extensive generic portfolio including oncology lines, injectable antibiotics and other hospital lines. Responsible for maintaining gross margins and other KPI's for the entire Hospira range in a competitive dynamic environment as well as tender (open or closed bidding) submission presenting the economic and clinical aspects of our range.

As a retail pharmacist I have over 10 years management experience including owning a successful retail pharmacy for 2 years. This involved buying an under-preforming business and turning it around - developing new staff, implementing IT systems and drawing on various business/management skills. This business was eventually sold leading to a solid return on investment.

With clinical pharmacy skills taught at university, retail sales skills through business experience, MBA technical/management skills and my pharmaceutical industry experience I have a fairly unique set of skills to draw from and would be an asset to an organisation requiring such skills.
