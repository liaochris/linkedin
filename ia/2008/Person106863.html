Kim Hughes’s Summary

Hello, I am Kim Hughes, owner of KH&Company; I offer a unique opportunity to Real Estate Professionals who are ready to take the next step with their business. With over 17 years in the real estate and mortgage industry you can rely on me to know the business and you can depend on me to handle all aspects of your office needs.

As an industry leader, I am proud to say I am the first virtual assistant to specialize in real estate support and my goal is to provide personal, marketing/administrative support real estate professionals.


I am the first real estate virtual assistant to be invited to the Allen F. Hainge CyberStar® Summit – 2003.

I am the first real estate virtual assistant to be invited by Howard Britton to speak at the StarPower Stars Conference in Palm Beach, FL in December 2005.

I am also proud to be a founder of IREAA (International Real Estate Assistants Association), the leading organization for Real Estate Assistants and Real Estate Virtual Assistants. IREAA offers education, training and collaboration for both in-house and Virtual Assistants supporting real estate professionals.

With the real estate industry, more and more real estate professionals are turning to the Internet to conduct business and since KH&Company is a global company I can assist you no matter where you are located. KH&Company is equipped to work with you by using email, fax, telephone, Net-conferencing, etc. Working with KH&Company is like having your own personal assistant in your very own office, but off-site.

Since I only work with real estate professionals, I am up-to-date on the latest technology, marketing ideas, software programs, campaigns and on-line programs so that you can get the maximum exposure out of your business.
