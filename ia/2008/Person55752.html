Paul Drury - E3 Search’s Summary

Managing Director of E3 Search - an retained executive search firm specialising in Retail executive recruitment across CEE countries. Speak fluent Russian and German - passionate about Russia and Eastern Europe.

E3 recruits across all functional disciplines in Retail from senior management upwards. Key countries: Russia, Ukraine, Poland, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Baltics.

White Knight is a separate division of E3 Search that specifically provides Western expatriate or Russian repatriate candidates, across all industries (Consumer, Industrial, Finance, Technology) for businesses within Russia. This is run on a pure candidate marketing principle, where candidates pay a fee to be included in a fortnightly publication. This publication is then freely distributed to clients. If clients want to hire a candidate from this publication, there is NO FEE for them.
