Paul Silver’s Summary

I made my first website in 1996 for an accommodation guide of small hotels. I became a developer in 2000, making websites dynamic - e.g. making a shop able to take sales, vacancies show on a recruitment site, or search work across a site.

I also carry out Search Engine Optimisation on websites, helping them gain high up positions in the results of search engines like Google.

Recently I have been doing a lot of work with Google Enterprise products - making a search engine for Brighton & Hove for p2b.net and their partners using a Google Search Appliance; I have also been working on an educational search engine using a Google Mini which will launch soon.

Paul Silver’s Specialties:

SEO, programming in PHP and ColdFusion, technical documentation, specification writing, integration of Google Mini and Google Search Appliance servers, XHTML, CSS
