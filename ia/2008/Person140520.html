Nick Grande’s Summary

I am a TV industry professional, with over 12 years' experience in TV strategy and corporate finance, channel launches, developing revenue opportunities, broadcast contract negotiations and large scale change management.

In April 2008 I founded ChannelSculptor, a Dubai-based broadcast service provider offering specialist services in the design, creation, launch and management of television channels and multi-channel platforms (both Pay-TV and Free to Air).

Originally financially trained (ACA 1992), I have worked mainly in London and Dubai but have had extensive international experience.

Throughout 2007 and early 2008, as Launch Director I oversaw the creation of Arabian Television Network (for Dubai Holdings), which successfully launched MTV Arabia in November 2007.

Previous to this I spent seven years with ShowtimeArabia, a Pay TV joint venture between Viacom and Kipco. My work as Director of Strategy and Business Development included everything from preparation for an equity listing to channel joint ventures, new media deals and large scale change management.

During the 1990s whilst at MTV Europe as Business Manager for Network Production I worked on the launches of MTV2 and MTV Base

Nick Grande’s Specialties:

NEW BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT - channel launches, broadcast services, mobile TV, EPG & airline sales

CORPORATE STRATEGY - working with international investment banks, investors, analysts and financial PR

INDUSTRY FLUENCY - extensive negotiation experience with networks, international channel brands, broadcast service suppliers, investors and regulators

CHANGE MANAGEMENT - regularly working towards time-bound and budget-constrained objectives in every part of the TV environment
