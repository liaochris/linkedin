Timothy Morrell’s Summary

Tim has a remarkable talent for assisting people to recognise their natural talents and best qualities. With a real passion for achievement and growth, he channels his energy to enabling people to create a realistic vision for the future and pioneering the basis for enjoying life in the present now. Tim inspires and empowers individuals to operate at their best with a focus on strengthening relationships with self and people around them.

He combines his expertise in NLP therapy with a variation of outdoor challenges to help individuals learn new strategies to reclaim personal power, self trust and for accepting responsibility for change. Ultimately, Tims goal is for all his clients to realise their unique genious through the achievement of life-changing goals.
