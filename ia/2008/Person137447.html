Michael W Thomas’s Summary

I am Co-founder and Partner of CRM Essentials LLC, a management consulting/advisory firm focused on implementing strategies and technologies affecting profitability through customer relationship management.

In addition I co-host to Technology for Business Sake, a weekly radio show focused on SMB business segment.

I have has over 20 years of technology sales and management experience working for Automatic Data Processing (ADP), Oracle Corporation and PeopleSoft. Thomas received CRM Magazine's 2004 Most Influential Leader Award along with his business partner Brent Leary.
He is the National President and sits on the board of the Customer Relationship Management Association. Thomas is an Advisory Board member of CRMandDW Experts.com and on the Editorial Advisory Panel of Touch Briefings. He served as a 2005-2006 Peppers and Rogers Group 1to1 Impact Awards Judge and a contributor/panelist on 1to1 Magazine’s “Taking Issue” column.
