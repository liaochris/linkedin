Lori Talley’s Summary

Forget any boxed-up notions of what an interactive "producer," "creative" or "strategist" is. I am a hybrid.

-Fluent in contemporary interactive marketing practice
-Dedicated to research and engagement within social networks and supporting technologies
-Disciplined and inspired collaborator
-Excited by the many opportunities in the online space
-Always on the look out for fellow technically inclined creative thinkers

Currently, I am an interactive producer for independent ad agency, Cramer-Krasselt. I also teach sound design for installation and performance at The School of the Art Institute of Chicago and am co-curator of the international festival and symposium, OPENPORT.

catalytic thinking, natural collaborator, finds creative solutions to technically complex issues
