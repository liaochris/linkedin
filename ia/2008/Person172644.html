Justin Jung, SEM SEO Expert’s Summary

My job is to make sure that companies target the right audience to drive qualified traffic to their website as well as increasing their ROI with the following strategies.

1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) - I optimize websites search engine friendly so that search engines will rank them higher.

2. Search Engine Marketing (SEM) - I build and manage 100% measurable pay per click (PPC) campaigns that send qualified leads to the website.

3. Web Analytics - I measure and analyze the results of online campaigns through using Web Analytics in order to maximize the effectiveness of the online campaigns (i.e. PPC advertising, organic SEO, social media, emails, banners, etc.)

As a result, I provide every aspect of online and search engine marketing strategies - SEO, SEM (PPC Advertising), SMO (social media optimization), local search optimization, conversion optimization and web analytics.

I have a distinguished record in helping companies in a wide range of industries from e-commerce, communication, travel, franchise, financial, publishing, education, technology and not-for-profit.

The following is the list of the companies that I have created and managed the SEM / SEO campaigns for them.
Citi Cards, Visa, Marriott, Subway, H&R Block, BMO, CIBC, American Express, Henry's, Rogers, St. Joseph Comm., Transcontinental, Synovate, Canada's History Society, The Hockey News, Avery Canada, Heart & Stroke Foundation, WWF Canada, Walrus, Reader's Digest, The Folio Society, Unicef, Midas, I Love Rewards, etc.

In addition, I am also a Certified QuickBooks® and Quicken® Accounting Software Advisor.
