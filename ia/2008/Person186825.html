Terry Hanold’s Summary

Marketing and Business Development executive with experience in both enterprise and consumer software. My focus has been early-stage businesses, including both stand-alone start-ups, and new business initiatives inside larger companies (e.g. Microsoft and Amazon.com). My areas of expertise include defining market opportunity, developing the entry strategy (including funding), and building out the product definition, marketing infrastructure, and sales pipeline. As part of this process, I have done a lot of alliance work, including build-out of a partner ecosystems and developer/user communities.

Terry Hanold’s Experience

  • Director, Payments

    Amazon.com

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; AMZN; Internet industry)

    June 2005 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Currently, I run payment issuance for Amazon globally, including the credit, gift certificates, gift cards, and stored value businesses. Taken as a whole, these businesses are focused on developing new payment methods, functionality, and features with an eye to improving how we relate to our wallets and the ways that we give and receive money. My teams design the features and benefits of the Amazon payment methods; establish partnerships to promote their use and increase their value; and build the customer-facing and back-end systems required to support the business. We are early adopters of Web services and Web 2.0 technologies, and are part of Amazon's Web services-based e-commerce platform.

    Previously, I worked on Amazon's Web services initiative. AMZN is launching new businesses as an on-demand e-commerce and internet technology provider. My team was responsible for building out the technical infrastructure and developer/partner ecosystem around this initiative.
