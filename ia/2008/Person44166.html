Danial Kim’s Summary

EXPERIENCE and ACHIEVEMENTS

An innovative media expert with 15 years of top-level executive leadership success. A track record of using technology to develop award-winning interactive products and marketing, generating revenue and strengthening relationships. A passion for leading change, discovering and exceeding member, audience and client expectations. All of this, plus a J.D. from one of the nation’s top law schools.

DIGITAL MEDIA INNOVATOR
Created award-winning interactive media as Editor and Publisher of the ABA Journal and eReport, and as Vice President Media Integration at the Detroit Media Partnership. Created and redesigned web sites, electronic publications, mobile products, search, business directories and all new media.

SALES PRODUCER AND CLIENT DEVELOPER
Managed sales operations in a variety of settings – online, magazines, newspapers, consumer and B2B – growing revenue by helping clients connect with their customers. Increased revenues online by 50%.

EXECUTIVE LEADER
Top level executive at national, state and regional organizations, in charge of multiple departments including New Media, Publications, Sales, Media Relations, Public Relations, Marketing, New Product Development, Information Systems, Human Resources and Finance departments. Experience hiring, firing and building teams with diversity. Ability to successfully manage multi-million dollar budgets, with strategic budget cutting and expansion.

Strength in crisis management, long range and strategic planning and working with senior management and governing boards. Tapped to cover vacant positions in emergency situations (Interim Executive Director at one organization, interim head of Information Technology at another).
