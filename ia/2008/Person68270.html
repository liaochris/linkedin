Gennadiy Civil’s Summary

My role is to bridge the gap between business and technology.

I am currently involved as an technical manager and architect in a large development effort creating rich Web 2.0 AJAX application. Technologies include GWT ( Google Web Toolkit ) User Interface running against custom J2EE WebLogic application integrated with FAST Search Engine and various databases.

I am an expert in J2EE technologies and I also have good experience with PHP, LAMP as well as Microsoft platform. I have good understanding of SOA.

I am a hands-on person, I enjoy writing code and developing systems.

At the same time I am well versed and experienced in project management. I have experience with RUP and/or XP methodologies. I have Six Sigma green belt and Cmm/Cmmi experience.

I have been managing distributed outsourced development for a long time now. I have worked with Indian outsources (Tata, Cognizant, Mphasis) and Europeans ( ePam, Exadel, Contour)

I also have extensive experience with Performance Tuning/Load Testing. I have both hands-on and managerial experience with Mercury Tools ( Load Runner, Performance Center ), various open source tools as well as Rational Test products.
I have experience with PerformaSure, FogLight, AppSight and other similar products as well as expert level knowledge how to tune large enterprise applications.

I am also very knowledgeable in Internet Marketing, Affiliate Marketing, PPC ( Pay-Per-Click) advertisement campaigns and Search Engine Optimization.
