Susan Lervold’s Summary

With over ten years of experience as an administrative assistant, most recently a legal assistant, I consider myself a professional, and strive to conduct myself accordingly. I approach each assignment with resolve and determination, and I take great pride in the quality of my work, regardless of the task. I manage deadlines efficiently, and deal with deadline pressure calmly and confidently. I am flexible and creative, adept at devising innovative solutions to ordinary problems as well as uncommon ones, though mindful to avoid “re-inventing the wheel”. I believe that an administrative assistant, whether executive or general, has two key responsibilities: (1) to anticipate the professional needs of his/her superior(s); and (2) to ensure that those needs are met, fully, efficiently and agreeably. My goal is to personify, and perhaps even magnify, this definition.
