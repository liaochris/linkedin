Michael DeMirjian’s Summary

DeMirjian’s been an active editor for 19 years now, 14 years on Avid (with Final Cut chops coming soon). With a the keenest eye for movement & an impeccable ear for rhythm, Michael’s received a National Emmy for editing & 3 additional nominations. His range is extensive: agency spots & pitches; sports, series & reality television; corporate image pieces & motivational employee videos.
