Justin Hayward’s Summary

I joined Latitude in 2004 heading up a team in Warrington before relocating to London in 2005 and taking on the key role of responsibility for the development and execution of the London office. Starting with 3 people this has now grown to over 40 staff and has become the fastest growing part of Latitude.

I have overseen clients in my team including totaljobs, Simply Switch, Norwich Union, Alliance & Leicester, Betfair, HSBC, Swinton and British Red Cross. Prior to Latitude, I was responsible for the Nissan Europe account at TBWA\Digerati, enabling the first cohesive online dealer presence and a european-wide fully integrated used car system. Additionally at Tequila, I managed integrated campaigns for Going Places, MyTravel and Warburtons, helping to develop the MyTravel online presence and was responsible for Warburtons first ever direct response website.

I have considerable offline and online experience both on client and agency side including direct mail, sales promotion, web development, PPC, SEO and social media optimisation which I have developed into the team at Latitude.

My focus at Latitude is specifically directed towards delivering excellent customer service and results within search marketing. As testament to this, my team and I were awarded the NetImperitive Best Search Campaign 2007 award for our outstanding work on totaljobs.

As Strategic Development Director at Latitude, I now oversee our partnership and client opportunities consultancy with the Creston Group, and NPD.
