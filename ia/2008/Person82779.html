  • Founder & Technical Director

    Open Clustering

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry)

    January 2002 — October 2002 (10 months)

    Open Clustering was a new initiative born from Psi-Domain focussed on building large-scale clusters for clients. The majority of my work was concerned with the specification of High-Performance and High-Availability clusters utilising Beowulf and openMosix. The clusters deployed often comprised of Blade Server hardware - at the time we were the pioneering the technology in Europe - ahead of Dell, HP/Compaq and Sun.

    In order to manage the rollout of large clusters, I wrote a web-based management system that enabled System Administrators to remotely deploy and install OS's and services, hardware check-up (temperature, fan speeds and power), and environmental monitoring via SNMP probes.

    The most notable client of Open Clustering was the US FBI - who commissioned me to build a customised version of our software to control and monitor a large blade server deployment.

  • Software Engineer

    Reuters Group plc

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; RTR; Financial Services industry)

    June 1998 — August 1999 (1 year 3 months)

    I wrote software that enabled staff at European Stock Exchanges to administer tradable instrument data feeds. The instrument data is then fed to Reuters Terminal software that is used worldwide by investors, traders, banks, exchanges and corporates to view the status of stocks, shares and company information.

    My development concentrated on backend PL/SQL and desktop GUI for the end users. I built the GUI application using Centura Team Developer (alike to Visual Basic) that connected with and administered the data in an Oracle database sat upon a large VAX/VMS cluster. This part of my work involved accessing the Windows NT API and messaging system and other 'low-level' functions.

    I had to visit our Geneva offices several times during the project duration to demonstrate a latest beta release, elicit further requirements and to give training on the software. I learnt to communicate ideas, listen to feedback and co-ordinate my schedule and project to a successful conclusion.
