Erin Hoffman’s Summary

The fun truth: I am exceptionally passionate about what I do and that passion has now bought me the freedom to carefully select the projects I work on. I believe that creativity, philosophy, engineering, and cognitive psychology come together explosively in modern interactive media, and I'm interested to the point of persistent exhaustion in how that explosion can change the world for the better. I am a naturalist, a designer, a communicator, a storyteller, a hedge philosopher, an apprentice accelerator, and perpetually busy. But if you're working on something interesting I'd like to hear about it.

The more boring details are below.

Fiction author and contract writing and design consultant. Eight years' experience in the games industry. Focus on storytelling, world building, online games, and programmer-artist-licensor-publisher translation and peacekeeping; variety of skills including Photoshop, Illustrator, programming (light), scripting, databases, web design, music composition and arrangement, sound design. Always in search of tasty tasty learning opportunities.

For further information, please see my website, http://www.philomathgames.com .
