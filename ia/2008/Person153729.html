Priya Bellare’s Summary

– RNA & protein biochemist with experience in cell biological, biochemical, genetic and biophysical techniques.
– Developed an in vitro approach and implemented it to study the role of ubiquitination in biological processes.
– Successfully led many projects and addressed complex scientific questions independently and creatively.
– Demonstrated a high-level of interpersonal & mentoring skills through teaching, supervision & collaborations.
– Represented Northwestern University at many international conferences and gave oral presentations of work. Helped in the recruitment of graduate students at Northwestern University through oral presentations.

PUBLICATIONS:
