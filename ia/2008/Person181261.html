Steve De Vos’s Summary

13 years experience within the fast moving consumer goods market took me trough several experiences as supply-chain, product development, purchasing, category management, peoplemanagement and franchising.

"Le fil rouge" through my career is that each time I had to start or rethink an organization and make it performing better with new ideas and different approaches.

Young as still 35 I had the luck to work in different environments local and international, small and big companies and in different domains who all come together in my recent job completed with new experiences in helping to start up entrepreneurs and finding the right spot.
