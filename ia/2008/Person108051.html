Kolya Miller’s Summary

People focused, I am a confident, well-organised person who can analyse problems, influence outcomes and work effectively in a high-pressure environment. I have excellent presentation, writing, analytical and all round communication skills. My background includes delivering a broad range of work and a history working for strategic and management consulting firms and delivering solutions to both the private and public sector. Additionally, my experience in providing complex training solutions and strategic advice addressing major business issues meet your required criteria. I have delivered and assisted in large-scale training across organizations (St. George, McKinsey), and developed strategies for Enterprise Content Management (CBA) and Knowledge Management (McKinsey, CBA) systems.

My big picture approach coupled with a focus on the finer details and my ability to achieve results is just what you’re looking for.
