Scott Paden’s Summary

Whole system specialist focused hand-held and cellular devices. Drive the architecture and specification of embedded handheld systems from the context of the Symbian OS Operating System and it's associated Graphical User Interfaces. Use of strong software background to ensure a well thought out whole system view.

Background in system wide architecture of full feature operating systems including Symbian OS and PocketPC with a strong emphasis on audio, graphics, and multimedia. Strong knowledge of the board level impact on the whole system design. With a Career history including Intel, Digital Equipment Corporation, Norand (now Intermec), and Loma Linda University Scott brings a wealth of experience to a project.

Scott Paden’s Specialties:

Assembler, C, C++, OOP, Tektronix Logic Analyzes, Rhode and Schwartz CMU series GSM base station simulators, MS Powered Smart phone, Microsoft PocketPC, Win32, and COM. Microsoft DirectShow. Symbian 7OS, Symbian OS 8.1a, 8.1b, and 9.1—specifically MDF, MMF, and Devsound. ArmV5, StrongArm, ADI 652x baseband, and XScale™ Applications Processors PXA 272. Audio codecs like the UDA1341, UCB1400, Wolfson WM9705, WM9712, WM8753, and WM9713. I2S, I2C, SSP, AC97 and PCM, interfaces.
