Gregg Van Voorhis (gkvanvoorhis(at)yahoo.com)’s Summary

Currently I run the Call Center for BNA, a publisher of legal and business compliance information. I am an experienced manager with approximately 60 employees and a $5.6 million budget. I have extensive experience designing and creating departments as well as completely retooling workflows and business processes. We are a completely employee owned business so I've learned how to improve operations and technology with a constant eye towards both performance and costs.
I am typically more technologically adept than most of my peers and enjoy using that knowledge on the job. I program in MS Access as well as LotusNotes and have an deep understanding of relational database design. When software or databases fail to deliver adequate reporting for analysis, I use my own skills to make things work.
My title is "Operations Mgr" because, in addition to my department duties I have opportuniites to tackle challenging projects. In this capacity I act like an internal management consultant - analyzing issues, reporting on them and presenting strategic business cases. As a manager in the affected units I also have the pleasure of implementing my own recommendations. Some of the projects I've done include:
* Creating workflows to handle sales segmentation.
* Creating an Inside Sales Support unit within the call center.
* Consolidating multiple customer contact channels.
* Creating and implementing Performance and Quality Metrics in the call center.
* Designing telemarketing and web trial workflows.

I love my job and the company but I am always interested in new challenges, particularly those that would relocate me to New England to be closer to friends and family. The things I enjoy most in my job are the challenging projects, the technology solutions and any public speaking opportunities I can get.
