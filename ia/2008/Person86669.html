James Donald’s Summary

I am an agent with Windermere Silicon Valley Properties in Mountain View with the residential branch and I help people sell and or buy their homes. From my experience in working with others, I have found that I truly care about making sure my clients are happy with their decisions in real estate, whether it be selling or buying. I believe it is a trait that is often missing in this high demand market of the Bay Area.

Prior to Windermere Silicon Valley Properties in Mt. View, I co-founded and managed the award winning graphic design firm, Boomerang Design Group, LLC. In this role, my duties included daily business operations ranging from project schedules, cost estimates, and job tracking, to production scheduling and deadlines. Other responsibilities include art direction and design, financial management, and public relations.

James Donald’s Specialties:

Ares of specialization are Palo Alto, Mt. View, Los Altos, and the Rose Garden of San Jose, however I am able to help you buy or sell a home throughout the Bay Area.
