Richard Regalado’s Summary

More than 13 years professional experience, 10 of which in developing and implementing management systems. Working experience in risk management process and business continuity management. Competent in developing training programmes and delivering training either in-house or in a public setting. Tried and tested experience in projects that got certified in the first try. International working experience in Middle East, North Africa and the Far East.

Researcher, ISACA UAE Research Committee for IS Audit Methodologies
Co-author of "ISMS Auditing Guide" - for inclusion into ISO 27007 "Information technology - Security techniques - Guidelines for information security management systems auditing"
Co-author of "ISO 27001 Implementation Guide and Metrics"
Lead Consultant for 1st University in the Middle East to be certified to ISO 27001 (eTQM College - Dubai University)
Lead Consultant for 1st BS7799 (now ISO 27001) certified organization in the Philippines (Emerson Process Management)
Lead Consultant for the largest ISO27001 certified BPO in Asia (SPI Technologies)
Lead Consultant for the 1st and only ISO 27001 certified data center in the Philippines (ePLDT Vitro)
Lead Consultant for the 1st ISO 27001 certified software development house in the Philippines (E-konek Pilipinas)
Lead Consultant for the ISO 9000 initiatives of Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas
Guest Speaker for ISACA Manila 2007
Resource Speaker of ISACA for ISO 27001 training courses
Resource Speaker of the Philippine Society for Quality for ISO 27001
More than 60 completed projects for ISO 9000 and other management system standards
Qualified ISO 9000 QMS Lead Auditor
