Matthew Davidge’s Summary

Two and a half years experience as a Software Quality Tester, now focusing on software development management skills as the overall Team Lead for the Transportation and Energy division at my company.

Experienced in both Waterfall and Agile Life Cycles. Currently utilizing a large arsenal of self-developed QA tools for verifying and validating software, gauging the readiness to release products, and providing assessments to the development and management teams regarding the health and quality of the products.

Career path is to continue learning, advance both my technical and managerial skills, and progress in the field of Software Quality Management.

Agile & Waterfall SDLC experience. Knowledge of CMM, ISO, TQM, & 6-Sigma.
