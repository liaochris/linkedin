Devendra Deshmukh’s Summary

Devendra Deshmukh, founder and executive director of e-Zest Solutions Ltd., e-Zest Inc. & e-Zest (UK) Ltd. , also he is also co-founder of Webizus Technologies, the IT (Information Technology) consulting company. He has experience of working with Indian software companies in both the operational and business development functions.

He holds a master's degree in business administration with Information Systems as specialization from Mumbai University (MMS - Systems) and an engineering degree in electronics and telecommunication from Amravati University India (B.E. EXTC). He also holds certification in 'Gemba Kaizen' from JMAM (Japan).

Contactable at devendra-at-e-zest.net or devendra.linked@gmail.com .
