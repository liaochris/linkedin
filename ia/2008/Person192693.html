Michael Jones (michaelj@richgroupusa.com)’s Summary

Michael Jones started his career as an Account Executive with The Richmond Group USA as a specialist in the Food Manufacturing industry and quickly developed strong connections with clients across the US. His performance established him within the top 1% of recruiters in the country. Early on in his career he identified the demand for talent in the BioEnergy sector and began to work with grain and oilseed companies that were positioning themselves in the emerging BioEnergy and Renewable Energy markets. Just within the last two years, his team has had tremendous success in acquiring talent ranging from research and development, engineering and operations through executive management.

Strong industry knowledge and a thorough understanding of the position requirements are not the only things that set Michael apart from other recruiters. Simply put, he says, is the fact that "I care" and that he wants both clients and candidates to understand the level of commitment he puts into each and every assignment. The commitment to excellence has led to a deep level of trust between him and his recruiting partners.
