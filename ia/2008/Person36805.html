Christopher de la Torre’s Summary

I Deliver

I am an adept writer and editor with over ten years of experience producing and managing persuasive and engaging communication for a variety of clients in diverse professional settings including biotechnology, industrial manufacturing, entertainment, retail, academia, education, non-profit literary, non-profit legal, publishing, journalism, and alternative medicine.

Gosh, People Like Me!

Having a personable and disarming yet bold professional style gives me an edge with collaboration, diplomacy, and information retrieval. My empathetic sensibility allows me to work efficiently with people of diverse backgrounds and personality types.

Quality Control Is Key

My expert researching skills have been used for both laboratory work and performing complicated archival and internet searches using resources including but not limited to medical databases, academic journals, and the Library of Congress.

I Play Well With Others

I really love a good learning curve, and have no problem giving or taking ideas when necessary. My imaginative and innovative professional outlook has sparked useful discussion and inspired new ideas. I have the proven ability to work on multiple tasks simultaneously while exceeding expectations, even when under tight deadlines and limited budgets.

I Like To Clean Things Up

Creativity and independence give me my handle on projects from conception through to completion, and I have no problem delegating tasks when necessary. I make sure that all parties are involved during every developmental stage.

Christopher de la Torre’s Specialties:

I'm dedicated to bringing the truth to light, speaking for those who don't have a voice, and furthering the quality of life for all those within reach of my writing. That's the gist. Everything else falls into place.

For more information visit my portfolio, blog, and online art journal Urban Molecule, all listed on my profile. Cheers!
