Richard Holbach’s Summary

Career Bio

Rich Holbach is a lifelong Chicagoan with a 27 year career in business development. Rich has advanced his career through a series of significant accomplishments at Old World Industries, J.C. Whitney & Company, the Robert Bosch Tool Corporation, and most recently as Vice President, of E-Commerce for Bowe Bell + Howell.

From 1992-98, Rich led Old World Industries into the “Web Age” with the Internets 1st Spark Plug category web site in SplitFire.com . In 1997, Rich collaborated on an initiative to execute an AT&T Secure Buy e-commerce platform via e-commerce with 3PL fulfillment.

In 1998, Rich accepted the challenge to take automotive parts distributor J.C. Whitney & Company online. Rich proactively built the strategic plan, hired key staff, and executed daily e-commerce operations that would drive JCWhitney.com’s aggressive growth plan to reach $49 million in e-commerce sales for the year of 2001. JCWhitney.com is currently a top 100 Internert Retailer site.

As the senior e-business leader at the Robert Bosch Tool Corporation, from 2002-06 Rich built an innovative approach towards e-commerce that enabled this global BTB manufacturer of industrial power tools to grow its annual online business from $3 million to $20 million. Boschtools.com won the 2006 SOFIE Award for the “Most Effective Customer Acquisition and/or Online Direct Response Program”, and was also recognized by BtoB Magazine (Chicago Crain’s Publication) as one of the “Top 10 Great Web Sites” for 2005.

Over the course of his career, Rich has gained a wealth of insightful management experience. A believer in endless education, an avid book reader and competitive enthusiast of all sports, Rich makes his home in Chicago, Illinois, wth his wife and 3 children.
