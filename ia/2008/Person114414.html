Lisa Ladrido’s Summary

My journey in direct sales began after I had been using a product for years and found out that if I join, I could then purchase it at 50% off!! What girl wouldn't want to get their favorite makeup and skin care at cost?!!

Never in a million years would I ever think of selling it! I was shy, insecure; a single mom raising 3 small boys and working outside the home. When would I find the time and why on earth would I try to change my life for the better, I was comfortable living in stress and anxiety!? Well, one thing led to another and oops... I started selling!

Let me tell you these techniques do work for some, but not for me. Back in the 1960's women mainly stayed home with their children. Now the Today Woman is wearing many, many hats. She wakes up early in the morning and starts her day with her children, husband, daycare, work, after school activities, supermarket, dinner, homework, baths, bedtime stories, prayers, cleaning up, making lunches for the next day and then maybe she can lie down and fall asleep, exhausted.

I wanted to try to market my business but didn't know how. I was hearing "multiple streams of income", but multiple streams of my income were going to others not to me!

I was struggling, I was joining the next new Wowz fads, the make more money fad, the unbelievable opportunity that will change your business fad, the pay $32.95 month for nothing fad, pay per clicks and advertising, do I need to go on?!

Then one day my dear friend who had been in one of my "Secret" groups showed me the way, the right way, the only way. I joined My Private Classroom For Marketers.

Did I tell you the best part? Once you are a member of Leaders Club
http://www.aleaderisborn.com, the membership for My Private Classroom is FREE!!

Have fun, enjoy life, share, be grateful, visualize abundance and be thankful to God that He created you. See you on the beaches of life!
