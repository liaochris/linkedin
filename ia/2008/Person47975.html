Bob Yoder’s Summary

Bob, an Associate Broker with Keller Williams Boice Countryside Realtors, is passionate about the real estate industry. Before moving to Truckee in 1992, he gained valuable experience in investment real estate sales and land development. After earning his Business Degree in 1975, he created a land development company and completed projects in Hawaii; Whitefish, Montana; and Calgary, Alberta. He then spent six years focusing on land development in Sacramento. A short trip to Tahoe turned into a life changing event and he is still here. “How could you leave.”

Bob notes that, "The Davis Yoder Group is a dedicated team of professionals who have combined their talents to be one of the top real estate teams in the country. I love my job and profession. I cannot think of greater satisfaction than seeing one of our clients realize their real estate dream and the Sierra lifestyle, or seeing them complete a successful investment."

His associations include membership in a highly selective national Real Estate Agent mastermind group, and he has been featured in I Succeed and Rismedia, which offers real estate web training services for agents. Bob has enjoyed the Sierra lifestyle by parlaying his enthusiasm for golf into three club championships at the Tahoe Donner Golf Club. He would love to make some turns with you at one of the area’s premier ski resorts.

The Davis Yoder Realty Group mission is simply “to provide the best service in the industry and we will not settle for less. We want to make you so satisfied that you purchased or sold with us, that you gladly refer us to your friends.”
