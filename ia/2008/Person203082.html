Gary Zamchick’s Summary

Gary Zamchick works with clients and teams to arrive at solutions that bring brands to life in real and virtual places – developing strategies, establishing context, exploring experiential interactions, developing narratives, uncovering innovative and aesthetic options. Relentless attention to a client's business objectives.

It’s an evolution of varied work - from animating building display systems at the World Trade Center (ESI Design), to designing capabilities materials for intelligence agencies (PIIM), to helping to visualize corporate-wide learning models (IBM), to crafting innovation and collaboration spaces (Coca Cola) to envisioning hospitality brand transformations and large scale parades and festivals (w/Rockwell Group).

Gary Zamchick’s Specialties:

Cross-industry excellence accelerating the process of discovery to insights to design translation via the use of rapid ideation/visualization to achieve strategic outcomes. Novel and surprising solutions. High sensitivity to client needs, their customers, visitors and end-users.
