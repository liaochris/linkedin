Eric Davidson’s Summary

After almost two decades of working for the same company, I am seeking a new opportunity where I can leverage my skills as a Business Analyst and data expert. Quality is paramount to me-- as is meeting and exceeding expectations, helping others succeed, doing whatever it takes to get the job done, and being a good leader. I am an experienced analyst with a proven track record of simplifying processes and developing noteworthy cost saving solutions. My experience along with the ability to adapt to new roles has often provided opportunities as a point person. This includes database development, developing code and content management strategies, data security solutions, proposed Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) strategies, and EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) initiatives. I am a result oriented professional with hands-on experience working with developers taking projects to fruition. I am adept at solving complex problems and creating specifications for reliable processes, communicate effectively with technical and non-technical employees, and work well independently and in a team environment.
