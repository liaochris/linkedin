Joseph Skursky’s Summary

Recruiting is about creating a clear competitive advantage, not just simply filling an open position.

I solve top-line peformance problems for small businesses by recruiting, retaining, and developing talent in key positions. My focus is providing solutions exclusively for sales, marketing, and management positions through retained search and retingency recruitment.

I go beyond just saying that I provide the best talent - I prove it with my premium guarantee:
1. One full year to evaluate
2. 100% satisfaction money-back or replacement
3. Your new hire will meet or beat quota or other performance measurements

Management Track Record:

* Led a $35mm region for a business intelligence company from 5th place of 6 to top revenue producer within 6 months by a margin of 41% over the 2nd place region.
* Directed a 125% revenue increase for a small management consultancy in less than 18 months while executing a leadership change.

As a trusted advisor and consultant:

* Helped clients save from $50k up to $1.2mm from their middle line.
