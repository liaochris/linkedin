David Dworak MBA, M.S’s Summary

Senior Project / Program Risk Process Consultant / Manager with 15+ years Banking and Financial Institution experience in client-facing roles at tier one institutions such as Bank of America, Wachovia, Bank One, Chicago Mercantile Exchange among others. Expertise in Banking and Risk Processes, Compliance, Project/Program Management, back office merger integration and process design.

Background includes 15 years of experience supporting major financial institutions including Operational Risk Assessments/Audits, Process Design, Operational Support, project/program management and merger and acquisitions support. Roles have included primarily Project/program management working directly for the Lines of business or as a supporting group identifying new solutions, developing business cases, obtaining funding and ultimately implementing those solutions. Developed working relationships with many Senior Level executives at several major financial firms.

Led 10 and 15 full life cycle significant, strategic and/or merger related projects ranging from about $1.5million to $25million. Led teams and projects through both direct reporting (up to 40 people) and matrix environments (up to 65 people). Banking Projects range from Automated Banking Web Site Compliance Monitoring, Supplier Management Control Reviews to Technology Refresh Initiatives. As Compliance and Operational Risk Manager at Bank of America, currently travel often visiting Suppliers or Bank of America operational facilities.
