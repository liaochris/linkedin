Chester Ng’s Summary

In 2001, I joined a group of 20 hungry, resilient entrepreneurs admist a shattered tech economy with a crazy notion of creating a "better media future". We did (some of) that at DivX. 7 years and 1 IPO later, I'm now ready for an even bigger challenge.

I'm only interested in working with passionate A-players. "Disruption, impact, and meaning" are prerequisites. Beyond that, I am open to all possibilities (and yes, most geos). Give me a product I believe in, and I will create and execute on the vision, strategy and partnerships necessary to build a successful business around said product.

I am most interested in building from the ground up, at an early-stage start-up or a young company in hyper growth. Again, I'm open... so try me.
