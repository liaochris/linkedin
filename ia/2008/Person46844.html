David Ingram’s Summary

I’m a product management and product marketing expert that has developed a specialization in social media, web 2.0, and viral & buzz marketing. I have experienced fast growing startups to multinational corporations.

I’m presently VP Product in a 30-person web 2.0 social media startup in co-located in UK and LA (www.sharenow.com). I’m experienced in the full lifecycle of software product management; from the breakthrough innovations, through strategy development, product development and delivery, user acquisition, to tracking user behaviors and ongoing strategy development.

For a number of years I’ve provided independent consulting, chiefly around development of new revenue streams and business development, in a number of international blue chip corporations.

I live in a converted cowshed in Longstock in the heart of the Test Valley, UK, with my wife Roshi and our two dogs Hudson and Charlie. I'm into wakeboarding and snowboarding, and I represented the UK in the 2005 and 2006 cable wakeboard world championships.
