Anne Hunter’s Summary

Sales and marketing professional with broad experience in global markets. We are working to promote our line of RSID and SPERM HY-LITER products to various crime laboratories worldwide. We have over 6 posters/ presentations being presented by forensic laboratories (including the FBI, Wisconsin State Crime Lab, and the University of Ghent in Belgium) on SPERM HY-LITER at the upcoming 19th International Symposium on Human Identification.
SPERM HY-LITER, our newest product, is the first positive staining identification method for human sperm. This next generation fluorescent sperm detection from sexual assault evidence is used as an analytical tool and as a screening method for backlog cases.
RSID tests are lateral flow strip tests for the identification of body fluids: saliva, semen, and blood. These tests were designed to increase efficiency and decrease cost in the modern DNA-based forensic laboratory.
