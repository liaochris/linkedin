Chuck French’s Summary

An electric utility/independent power Executive with thirty years of domestic and international experience in the business and technical management of power generation facilities and district heating systems. Considerable experience and proven capabilities in the setup and management of power generation/cogeneration facilities utilizing numerous technologies (conventional steam, combined cycle, CFB/PC/Stoker boilers, etc.) and fuels (coal, waste coal, liquid fossil fuels, natural gas, MSW, RDF, biomass).
