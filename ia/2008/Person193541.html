Trevor Wilkin’s Summary

Engine, tools and game programmer having made complete solutions for games on many platforms including PC, Xbox, Xbox360, PS3, XNA, Nintendo DS, GP2X. I strive for clean code, with great efficiency and enjoy the technical challenges of new hardware and getting down to the metal to get the most out of anything.

I have extensive knowledge of highend graphics, GPUs and shaders, including ASM, Cg and HLSL. I have also implemented many advanced effects on hardware such as the RSX and R500 for PS3/360.

My goals are to continue mastering as many hardware platforms as I have access to, develop high quality games and have fun whilst doing it.

I'm currently in the States on a H1B visa and working towards my GC, but it expires in October and to transfer I'd have to find work outside of the US.
