Mike Coffey’s Summary

Decision Support Services has been helping its clients to manage their inventory and supply chain needs for over a decade. As consultants to companies ranging from Fortune 500 to middle market, we handle the processing, presentation, and communication of information throughout our client's organization. Our experienced and skilled professionals have over 40 years of syndicated POS and vendor experience, helping to provide our clients a faster path to higher sales and efficiency.

As DSS' technology lead, it is my responsibility to ensure we remain on the cutting edge of Business Intelligence to best serve our clients' needs. We use Microsoft SQL Server and its associated components, such as Integration, Analysis and Reporting Services, to help turn POS information into timely action for our customers. Through our use of technology, our clients see enhancements in sales performance, CPFR, VMI, and retail logistics, and save both time and money.
