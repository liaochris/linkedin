Lesley Pendleton Barker’s Summary

I serve small businesses and nonprofit (including faith-based) organizations with writing, grant-writing, strategic research, program design, promotional and fund-raising materials as well as creative solutions in general. Preferential pricing is available to clients who participate in my Development Office in a Box program.

I also provide a series of tele-web casted seminars for K-12 classroom teachers about the grant-seeking process and protocol. See www.grantmapper.com.

Because of my unique background in education and vision therapy, I have been honored to write a series of three books for home schooling families for Home School Inc. Each book contains academic activities that give PreK-8th grade students practice using various learning related visual skills. Called, Eye Can Too! Read, the first book is about ocular motility skills; the second is about laterality and directionality skills; and the third is about visual perceptual skills.

I am a regular contributer of short articles on a wide range of eclectic subjects for Demand Studios.
