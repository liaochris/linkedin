Lisa Downs’s Summary

Lisa Downs is the owner/president of DevelopmentWise Consulting, which specializes in customized soft skills (management, leadership, communication) training, executive and performance coaching, and organizational development solutions. She started her business to provide organizations with strategic initiatives for talent management, learning, and performance improvement.

Prior to starting her business, she served as Senior Learning and Organizational Development Specialist for The Growth Partnership, St. Louis, MO, and as Manager of Learning and Development for Clark Nuber PS, in Bellevue, WA. She also coordinated the Accounting Careers Program for the Washington Society of Certified Public Accountants and taught language arts courses at the high school level.

Lisa received her Master of Science in Education degree with a concentration in Adult Education from Western Illinois University in 2000 and completed her undergraduate degree in Speech Communications in 1991 at Augustana College in Rock Island, IL. She earned her teaching certificate in secondary education in 1996 and has also worked at both public and commercial radio stations in the Midwest. In addition, she is a Certified Program Planner (CPP), a credential that focuses on the development, implementation, and marketing of internal and external learning programs.

Ms. Downs is the current Vice-President of Membership for the American Society of Training and Development Puget Sound Chapter. She is also an active member of ASTD national and the International Society for Performance Improvement. Continuing her passion for education and youth development, Lisa serves as a volunteer for Washington DECA and Junior Achievement of Washington.

Lisa Downs’s Specialties:

Management, leadership, and communications skills training; executive, performance, and presentations skills coaching, mentoring programs, new hire orientation programs, strategic planning facilitation, curriculum and course design, performance standards and competency development, knowledge transfer programs.
