I specialise in enterprise software, Start-ups, Turnarounds, Merger & Acquisition and Interim Management.

I blend a solid technical background with extensive commercial expertise to produce tangible quick results.

Notable successes include Prism Solutions / Ardent Software (now IBM Websphere Data Integration), Tumbleweed Communications, Clearswift (instrumental in the increase in revenue from £2m to c. £30m in 3 years) and Mobix.

I have enabled many of the companies I have helped to build to increase deal sizes from thousands of pounds to multi-millions.

Personally my sales have included companies such as Clearswift, Gambro, Lego, Handelsbanken, Standard Life, Banco Fonseca & Burnay, CSFB, UCLES, Scandinavian Airlines, Swiss Post, Siemens, Siemens Electrocom, Polcomtel, Vodacom and France Telecom.

I am first class communicator and presenter, with proven management, opening, closing and board level negotiating skills. I have highly effective working relationships with Directors, VPs and CEOs in many companies.
