Jeffrey G. "Hodge" Hodgkinson’s Summary

Jeff Hodgkinson is a 29 year employee with Intel and has over 27 years of progressive Program/Project Management experience in Facilities, Construction, Product Development, & Information Technology.  Over his career, Jeff has managed numerous and diverse programs & projects in varying scope, budget, and schedule. Since Feb '08, Jeff has been in an IT Service Management (ITIL) organization as a PMO/Program Manager most recently driving the SM Portfolio Roadmap. For 2 years prior, his position was with the IT CBS (Core Business Services) PMO (Project Management Office) as a Process Coach where he supported several organizations in driving Intel IT's vision and goals of driving PLC Standardization and achieving a CMMI Level 3 equivalent across a global 6,000+ person organization. He has mentored numerous Project/Program Managers in getting PMP® and PgMP® credential.  Along with becoming the first PgMP® at Intel (and the 9th to receive it) he received his PMP® credential in 1991.  Jeff has an MBA along with certifications in Project Management, Quality, and Disaster Recovery. He has the distinction of facilitating over 130 MAPP (Make A Program/Project Plan) Days. He is active with the local Phoenix PMI chapter as the Director of Special Programs and a member of PMI's PgMP®PRC (Panel Review Committee). Jeff enjoys working out and competitively arm wrestles where he is placed 2nd for right arm in the World Amatuers heavyweight division and is also the Arizona State Heavyweight Champion (Right & Left arm).  Jeff is the 'Trainer & Coach' for the 'Phoenix Arms' armwrestling team. Jeff is writing a book on Program/Project Management.  Jeff has been married 24 years to Sharon and they have one son, Ryan age 23. Jeff and family reside in Mesa, Arizona, USA.
