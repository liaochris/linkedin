Kristine Schachinger’s Summary

I have eleven years experience in the creation, development, implementation and maintenance of websites in all areas including government, academia, entertainment and ecommerce. In my position at TravelWorm.com responsibilities included (but not limited to) site analysis, site design, UI design, white label code and design implementation, project management, SEO (for all sites), code documentation and new technologies research. In addition, I am a subject matter expert in site promotion, site architecture, UI development, SEO, W3C compliance, 508 compliance, site implementation, usability and accessibility.My responsibilities have included all areas of site development from design to implementation as well as site promotion and implementation of guerrilla marketing strategies.

In addition, my work on the United States Fire Administration (USFA) site also assisted my company in winning a Webby Award for setting standards on the internet.
