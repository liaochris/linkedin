Jason Premo’s Summary

Jason brings more than 15 years of experience in manufacturing having served in a variety of Engineering, Project Management, Team Leadership, Plant Management and Senior Executive roles for small, mid-market and Fortune 500 companies. No matter what the size, Jason has consistently applied practical Lean Manufacturing and Six-Sigma methods having successfully led more than 45 combined Kaizen and Six-Sigma initiatives in five countries.

Jason holds a Bachelor’s degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta, GA and is a certified Lean Manufacturing-Kaizen Facilitator and Six Sigma Black Belt. Jason is a frequent guest speaker at many industry events and trade shows presenting a wide variety of topics from practical Six Sigma best practices to the latest real-time factory floor to enterprise information technology trends and case studies.

Jason Premo’s Specialties:

Lean Manufacturing, Six Sigma (Master Black Belt), Company Turnarounds, Sales Management, Marketing Management, Manufacturing Operations Management, Mergers & Aquisitions, Employee Development & Coaching, ERP / MES / APS Software Technology.
