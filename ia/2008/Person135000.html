Molly Roberts’s Summary

Hello! I am Molly Roberts, MD, MS, also known as Dr. Molly. I am a holistic physician with a specialty in Mind/Body/Spirit Medicine, which is Alternative Medicine with soul! My credentials include that I am triply Board Certified in Family Practice, Holistic Medicine and Nutritional Medicine.

I am also a psychotherapist and an ordained Shamanic Minister and am on the Board of Trustees for the American Holistic Medical Association. I have written two books, one on Alternative Medicine and the other on Nutritional Medicine.

My husband, Dr. Bruce Roberts, and I do our work both individually and as a couple - he's my honey! Besides our work with LightHearted Medicine (come check us out at www.LightHeartedMedicine.com!), we also own and run the Synchronicity Center in Tucson which is available for others' workshops, seminars, weddings, meetings, etc. In addition, we are both holistic physicians at Canyon Ranch Health Resort.

Our focus is on helping people to find love, health, joy, vitality and "juiciness" in their lives, using holistic medical techniques and sensibilities. Besides bringing mind, body and spirit together for optimal health and well-being, we also help people get into right relationship with themselves, their partner, their family, and their coworkers. Our motto is "To Life!"

My husband and I are both available for speaking engagements, conferences, key notes, workshops, radio/TV/print interviews, etc. to discuss all manner of holistic health and relationship topics.

Molly Roberts’s Specialties:

Holistic medicine - bridging the divide between allopathic medicine and alternative techniques. Mind/Body/Spirit Medicine, Nutritional and Functional Medicine, Women's Health, Conflict Resolution work, Couple and Family Counseling, Sexuality Counseling, Counseling for Business Organizations, Spiritual Counseling, Shamanic Work, etc.
