Michael Manfredi’s Summary

The Commonwealth Group at Citi Family Office enables responsibly wealthy families to achieve the financial, social and legacy aspirations by an unwavering commitment to superior Private Wealth Management, an ethos of excellence and uncompromising integrity.

Citigroup Family Office’s mission is to provide clients with a truly differentiated experience, competitive with the family office alternative. Citigroup Family Office believes that our most important clients are entitled to a different kind of working relationship with the firm. It takes everything that the largest and strongest financial firm in the world is capable of, and enables our CFO Advisors to deliver it to clients through the uniquely personal experience of an elite, objective financial boutique.
