LetterRep.com
http://www.letterrep.com

LetterRep.com was founded in 2001 on two tenets: Sell an Information-based Product and be able to Run Any Business Role from Any Computer in the World.

LetterRep sells Custom and Pre-written Letters and has been operated from Puerto Rico, Dominican Republic, El Salvador, Honduras, Ecuador and Argentina. From January 2004 to January 2005, LetterRep was run from LSA Anaconda, Balad, Iraq, and has since been run in two different U.S. locations, Washington State and Virginia.

Since 2003 LetterRep.com has offered customers online letters and the ability to save changes to them, print them on any computer, leave reminders to write follow-up letters, access letters from any computer anywhere and submit requests for letters to be added to the site's inventory.

The Writer's Section was introduced in August 2007. From it, writers can view customer requests and write letters in response. Writers earn $10.00 each time a custom response sells and each time that letter sells afterwards. There are now over 400 writers whose roles are to edit existing letters purchased on the site and write custom letters in response to requests.

Consider us for your future business and personal letter writing needs.
