Rick Chen’s Summary

In 1992, Mr. Chen embarked on his journey by leaving his hometown of China for the United States of America. In the US, he graduated from University of California, Berkeley, #1 ranked Public University by U.S. News, where he attended college on a United States Army scholarship and received his Bachelor of Art in Philosophy.

Thereafter, Mr. Chen was with General Electric. During his tenure with GE's Commercial Distribution Finance Division, ranked #1 in Fortune 500's Diversified Financial Sector, he served as territory representative for Orange county and was personally responsible for client portfolio in excess of over $100 million.

Mr. Chen attained his real estate license in 1999 and entered into the field of real estate transactions. During his years with Coldwell Banker and Help-U-Sell, Mr. Chen won numerous awards for his outstanding achievements and is recognized as a top producer.

Mr. Chen enjoys reading, working out, running, hiking, boxing, travelling abroad, and spending time with friends and family.
