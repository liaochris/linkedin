Erez Naveh’s Summary

Come2Play offers a free platform for game developers to create, distribute, and profit from multi-player networked casual Flash games.

Using our flexible and open API, you only need to concentrate on the mechanics and graphics of the gameplay; we take care of the rest.

Our platform provides you with all the elements necessary for creating anything from a single game to an entire game channel: users, chatting, lobbies, tables, point systems, player rankings, game ratings, player rewards, redeem points for gifts, and so on.

Since it's an open platform, you can change every detail of the look, feel, and functionality of your games, as well as all of the associated game elements.

Come2us - was formed in January 2000 and is a leading provider of unique internet solutions in Israel.
We offer services in: internet oriented design and development, content management systems and administration interfaces, web portals and e-shop development, specialization in security and data encryption for e-commerce, expertise in flash and java development for multi-user platforms, search engine optimization, browser compatibility, web hosting, content localization, targeted advertising and promotions, development and utilization of databases (on multiple platforms : ASP, SQL, PHP, .NET), production, editing and internet optimization of video, animation and 3D graphics.
