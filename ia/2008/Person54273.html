Dr. James Chappell’s Summary

Board certified chiropractic physician, clinician, traditional naturopath, clinical nutritionist and medical herbalist.

Since 1971, avid health researcher, educator and nutraceutical - botanical formulator specializing in incorporating natural healing protocols for chronic, severe and terminal illnesses.

Credited with the discovery of The HONSTEC Syndrome; the cause of dis-ease. Best selling author, A Promise Made, A Promise Kept: One Son’s Quest for the Cause and Cure of Diabetes.

Consulted over 10,000 patients and spent over ten years as one of the "lot" doctors for Paramount, Universal, MGM and Burbank studios in Hollywood, California seeing actors, producers and directors.

Spent five years as the Youth Services Coordinator in Kennebunk and Kennebunkport, Maine helping families in crisis intervention, suicide prevention, family mediation, substance abuse counseling and runaway location.

Taped ten television infomercials as host/spokesperson to date. Had own prime time talk radio show for three years. Key note speaker, TV and radio personality, corporate medical consultant and president of Chappell Health Products, LLC and QCI Products, LLC.

Interested in world-wide marketing of products and services and developing a reality-based show incorporating weekly seminars teaching natural healing and The HONSTEC Syndrome.
