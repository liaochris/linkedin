Rob Lewis’s Summary

EquipmentJobs.com is now poised to embark on a major campaign across the country! The past month has been more of a planning period for us, and it has been necessary to take some time off to try to get my family situated in our new home. WE have undergone some structural planning of our company, set new marketshare goals, and recruited some top talent to ensure our goals are met. With that being said, we are ready to launch our marketing campaign to bring in more construction equipment companies and find that one special site sponsor. We are taking nominations and suggestions and will pay a generous bonus to the person who recommends that perfect fit. We currently have three that we are looking that I feel are in an amazing position to gain marketshare within the CE industry and one that is a relevant vendor.

On a side note, join me on Twitter at www.twitter.com/socialized and follow me around. I know, I am kind of a geek.....but hey wouldn't it be cool to know what I am doing? Check out my construction industry blog at http://www.equipmentmedia.com. I am always interested in new content. If you would like to submit anything to post on there like sales tip articles or industry news, just give me a shout. Thanks.

Rob Lewis’s Specialties:

With a severely damaged market, the main question now is "Can construction equipment companies afford not to hire from niche job boards that directly market to their industry?" The time is long gone where you can put an inexperienced, unknowing salesman in the field and assume your customers will instinctively call them or your office for every order. If you don't take care of their needs in a manner that instills trust, you should just go ahead now and introduce them to your competition.
