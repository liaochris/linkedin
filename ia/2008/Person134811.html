Mohan Dutt’s Summary

A warm welcome to you from the world's leading Oracle E-Business Suite Evangelist: Mohan Dutt.

For his contribution as an Oracle E-Business Suite Evangelist at large, Mohan has been awarded the OAUG Member of the Year Award for 2007 at COLLABORATE 07: Technology and Applications Forum for the Oracle Community Conference in Las Vegas, NV.

Mohan is an expert on Service Contract to Renewal, Procure to Pay, Order to Cash, Accounting to Financials Reports and Project to Profit business flows. Mohan has worked in varied roles of Project Manager, Functional Team Lead, Principal Consultant, Lead Analyst, Business Analyst and Production Support Specialist for numerous implementation, upgrade and roll-out projects involving Oracle E-Business Suite. Throughout his Oracle E-Business Suite career, Mohan been part of organizations that are Oracle Certified Advantage Partners namely Hyperion Solutions Corporation, Pricewaterhouse Coopers and Solix Technologies.

In the role of an Oracle E-Business Suite Evangelist, Mohan has preached to the choir, more than thirty times at Oracle and OAUG conferences worldwide at Beijing, Sydney, Melbourne, Toronto, London, Las Vegas, Dallas, Nashville, Orlando, Chicago, San Diego, San Francisco, San Ramon and Santa Clara.

As a Oracle Certified Expert (OCE) and Oracle Certified Professsional (OCP), Mohan encourages others to get certified and provides certification tips on his blog. Mohan is also spreading a good word about Oracle E-Business Suite Release 12: The Global Release by highlighting new features on his Oracle R12 blog.

Mohan looks forward to contributing in the coming years towards the world's best enterprise application solution yet, Oracle Fusion Applications.
Mohan is always looking for an engaging conversation with Oracle E-Business Suite thought leaders, practioners, power users and project managers.
