Jasmin Tragas’s Summary

Jasmin is a Managing Consultant at IBM in the area of learning, collaboration, knowledge, social media and virtual worlds. Her experience spans over a decade as a new media producer and training lead, working with creative teams to design quality websites, eBusiness solutions, new media packaging and print media, training simulations, eLearning, CD-ROMS, animations, and video production.

Jasmin is skilled in working with customers to create dynamic strategies, designing solutions then leading team members to design and implement the approach. Jasmin also has experience creating new, innovative programs and developing strategic market offerings. She is a volunteer on various web2.0 programs and initiatives.

You can connect with Jasmin on Twitter, Facebook , Flickr , LinkedIn and in Secondlife (Jazzydee Raymaker) and on her blog http://blog.wonderwebby.com. Jasmin is based in Melbourne, Australia and works part time. She is the mother of three young children.
