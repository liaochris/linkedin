Clark Phinney, STS, WSO-CST’s Summary

Clark Phinney, STS, WSO-CST - is the President and CEO of The ALZASA Consulting Group a safety issues consulting firm providing safety and occupational health/ and workers' compensation issues consulting services to clients across North America. Additionally, Clark is the Employee Health & Workers’ Compensation Coordinator for Central Maine Healthcare, a multi-location healthcare system involving over 3,200 individuals at three hospitals and two-long term care facilities, and their Self Insured Workers’ Compensation Insurance Trust.

A former HR Manager, Clark is a licensed property and casualty adjuster (Maine) specializing in workers' compensation. In December 2007, he was named as part of Facility Care Magazine’s 1st Annual “Who’s Who in Healthcare Facility Management” list. A frequent speaker and guest presenter at workshops and conferences nationwide, Clark is a dynamic speaker and trainer. He also has numerous articles and commentaries published in several HR and safety and other industry publications. IIn addition to his work in safety and workers' compensation, he is a Hospital Incident Command System (HICS) trainer, and also holds the OSHA 30-Hour General Industry card.
