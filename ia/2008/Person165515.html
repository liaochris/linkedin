Robert Hallock’s Summary

I have a strong background in writing compelling and technical enthusiast content for a large and diverse audience as a Technical Analyst at Icrontic.com. My submissions are deadline-driven and reviewed by our council of editors. One day I hope to leverage the experience I have gained in this fabulous opportunity to professionally tell the world about the beauty of consumer electronics through print or web media.

My current experience is with SoftTech Solutions, developer of the popular On-Line! Detective repair/administration/training suite for Sun Microsystems servers. As one of the two lead technical writers, it is my responsibility to correlate source material from Sun, Fujitsu and other vendors to provide approachable, informative and accurate content on mid-range Sun equipment.

Prior to SoftTech Solutions I was under the employ of the advertising firm Gas Station TV. The diverse needs of the firm in an ever-changing landscape required an individual to deliver approachable solutions to existing and unforeseen problems. As the Manager of Research & Development, I was in place to provide new platforms to improve efficiency or close gaps in information, and then train dependent employees on the use of these platforms. My contributions included network monitoring and management, issue tracking and resolution, lab development, digital signage platform analysis and serving as the liaison to Avocent Corporation's provision of hardware and firmware.

I have amateur experience in compliant HTML and CSS via Dreamweaver, graphic design via Adobe Photoshop, and a working knowledge of ISO 9000 documentation standards. I have a great desire to combine these skills in a role to educate millions on the beauty and wonder of the personal PC.
