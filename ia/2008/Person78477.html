Hwee-Ping Goh (B. Eng., MBA) 吴惠平’s Summary

An ex-Rafflesian with a B. Eng degree from the National University of Singapore and a MBA degree (10 distinctions) from the Nanyang Technological University coupled with multi-lingual abilities in English, Chinese, Japanese and French.

She has demonstrated significant track records in her ability to manage complex scenarios and streamline processes involving dynamic changes across various cultural and business environments. She thrives particularly in regional roles involving entrepreneurial challenges, uncertainties, multi-tasking and setting appropriate strategic directions under very challenging time and resource constraints inherent within today’s operating, business, industry and market environments.

In 2003, she began her career in the recruitment industry through her appointment as the Managing Director for Spherion with P&L, Sales and Business Development responsibilities in Singapore, Malaysia & Japan. She has been a consistent achiever of annual revenue numbers closed to SGD8 million and annual EBIT numbers closed to SGD2 million.

Prior to that, she was credited with extensive years of experience comprising cross-industry experience and proven delivery track records in the IT industry with established organizations including Hewlett-Packard and Systems & Computer Organisation and several years of senior executive and management experience with leading government agencies in Singapore including Singapore Economic Development Board (China Desk) and Singapore Tourism Board.

In particular, during the dot.com era, she was one of the founding members of SESAMi Inc. where she held two concurrent portfolios as Vice President, Electronics Sector (Asia Pacific) and Vice President, Japan market development as one of the exclusive solution sales partners of Commerce One and i2 Technologies.
