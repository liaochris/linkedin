Brian Carter’s Summary

I have a broad background because I have wide-ranging interests, which has worked well for me with the verbal/context aspects of SEO/SEM.

My experience as an acupuncturist led me to create a website for the public about it. That taught me SEO, which led me to become an SEO and AdWords consultant. That led me into an E-Commerce management position. Meanwhile, I've continued to develop writing and speaking skills, most recently learning stand-up comedy.

While I was practicing acupuncture full-time, I also taught medical terminology and medical report writing to Masters degree "Asian Medicine" students. Making it fun and funny, something I learned about informative speaking from Toastmasters, led me to think I might be able to do stand-up comedy.

Currently, I work for Fuel Interactive in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina.
