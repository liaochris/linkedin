Eric Pilkington’s Summary

For over fifteen years, I have been a leader in developing new and innovative approaches to marketing communications and technology. I am currently VP/Executive Director of Digital Media at Decision Counsel, a full-service marketing communications firm that specializes in technology and strategic development and implementation. Prior to joining DC in 2006, I served as Principal/VP of Client Services at Avenue A Media, a firm that I founded in 1999. There, I created relationships between smart companies and their customers through the use of electronic other media, leading a team of user-centric designers and technologists who focused on systematic and structured design/technology integration of landscapes and options.

In 2002, I co-founded Information Architecture Team, a firm that specialized in data quality, cleansing and migration, where I served as EVP of Marketing until 2004.

A former Marine, I have led independent projects with such government agencies as the Department of Commerce/NOAA and the Department of Health and Human Services/Centers for Disease Contol (CDC). Other clients/brands that I have worked with include AT&T, Blue Cross Blue Shield, BP/Castrol, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Burton Snowboards, IBM, IDG, Hewlett-Packard, Johnson & Johnson, Nike, Nokia, Oracle, Palm, Pfizer, Saab, Samsung, SAS, Time4 Media and Volvo.

Eric Pilkington’s Specialties:

Using proprietary technology, unmatched creativity, and a proven approach, I extend the reach and scope of traditional advertising and marketing campaigns and allow organizations to create stronger brands and increase market awareness through the use of both digital and intellectual tools.
