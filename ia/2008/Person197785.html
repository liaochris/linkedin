Laura Vogel’s Summary

I have over ten years of professional experience in both the financial services industry and in government relations pertaining to the financial services industry. Currently, I am a member of the largest and most respected grassroots advocacy team in the Washington, DC area as we represent over 1.3 million REALTOR members.

My education in political management has positioned me to be a vital player in the government relations field. I have a strong background in legislative writing, an extensive understanding of how the government works and what it takes to make allies and compromise with your antagonists. I also have proven and impressive results in motivating a grassroots network of advocates for the real estate industry.

In the coming years, I hope to expand that expertise into training and speaking opportunities, both with REALTOR members and with other organizations that could benefit from my knowledge.
