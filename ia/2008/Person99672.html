Jude Stringfellow’s Summary

As a motivational speaker I go places and share a compelling story of love, of responsibility, and of respect. I bring my dog Faith with me. Faith's message is so powerful and so strong that to see it is the only way to really appreciate it. I travel with her from coast to coast to show off her personal strength as an animal, but also her love and devotion as a companion. She is willing to be where I am, she is willing to share her love and empowerment with everyone.

I want to show off her positive spirit and courage. Through viral e-mails and videos online she has become a greater sensation that ever before. She needs to be remembered as the greatest dog to every love someone - and that someone is everyone. She truly is a miracle dog.

Writing about subjects other than my dog is also important to me. I have many diverse interests and goals. I hope to display my writing in many genres and in categories across the entire bookstore. I find it necessary for myself to explore other venues of interest, and it makes it that much easier to write about when I do. I write about dieting, exercise, love, friendship, family, future, animals, and of course Christ. I want to always include Jesus in my life and my work. I may not be the Bible-beating preacher on the corner, but I stubbornly defend my faith and my beliefs. I hope my life is a good example - I try.

Jude Stringfellow’s Specialties:

I am a motivational speaker and professor. I tend to teach from the stage as often as I am able to do so. I want to be the one to be called upon to help others. It is important to me to give back because I was given so very much.
