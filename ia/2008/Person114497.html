Lisa Parrett’s Summary

I am originally from Brownsburg, IN, but now reside in Fishers, IN with my son, Owen. I served 3 years in the United States Army, which included a one year tour to South Korea. After the Army, I earned my Bachelors of Art in Communication Studies with a Minor in Spanish from Indiana University Purdue University at Indianapolis. I began working for a local builder before earning my real estate license and joining Keller Williams Realty. I am able to help our Spanish speaking clients with their real estate needs.

I also conduct spa nights for ladies who are looking for a night of socializing, fun, and relaxation. The night includes drinks and snacks, a free facial for everyone, and 5-10 minutes of deep relaxation. The facial and other products are from the BeautiControl Skincare and Spa line. I briefly present the spa and skincare products along with an opportunity to purchase.

Lisa Parrett’s Specialties:

I developed great attention to detail while serving in the military. This is an added value to my client as I am very thorough with every transaction. I don't miss the details and always act with my client's best interest in mind.

Because I majored in Communication Studies, I have very effective communication skills, which help through the negotiations and with guiding my client through each step of the home buying and selling process.
