Michelle Bart’s Summary

Michelle was born and raised in Fort Lauderdale, Florida and has lived in Los Angeles County over fifteen years. Known to many in Southern California as the founder of the Foothill Arthritis Connection, Michelle has over 20 years experience in event production, fundraising, non-profit management, and media communications. Michelle holds a communication’s degree and has applied her education and expertise to helping businesses and organizations obtain visibility through the media, community relations, and the Internet. Michelle’s talents, creativity, and an eye for detail have captured the attention of community leaders, politicians, and the Hollywood elite throughout the United States.

Michelle currently serves as the District 1 Secretary of Soroptimist International Desert Coast Region and website coordinator, is on the business development committee with the Irwindale Chamber of Commerce, is on the Board of Directors for Visiting Nurses Association, and keeps busy contributing to Human Rights Campaign, Access to Seniors, and GLAAD. When given an opportunity to relax, Michelle likes to escape to the mountains, Temecula Wine Country, and any coffeehouse.
