Andrew Duck’s Summary

From a background in web and systems technologies I have over 12 years programming and application development experience. During the past 6 years I have owned and/or managed several companies internationally servicing such markets as: enterprise hosting, graphic design, website development, search engine optimisations, marketing and media consulting, networking engineering, security consulting and hardware wholesale.

I am currently the Executive Director of Quiqcorp, a web and marketing agency based in the Asia Pacific region with a global client base. We work with and heavily support eZ Publish within the Asia Pacific region.

I am currently an Invited Expert to W3C Working Groups and a returning judge for the Web Marketing Association's Web Awards.
