Michael Reyes’s Summary

For more than 26 years Michael Reyes has been a standout entrepreneur, innovator, marketing executive and corporate matchmaker. Michael‘s scope of experience in numerous industries has combined to give him not only a unique perspective on business, but it has given him a breadth of integrated knowledge that allows him to see and seize opportunities far ahead of the curve. His achievements include:

• Twenty-six years of marketing and advertising experience
• Ten years in Strategic Management Consulting
• Worked on some of the world’s most recognized brands
• Recipient of more than 150 marketing and advertising awards
• Global business and political relationships
• Global partnerships and alliances
• Created and directed an international innovation consortium
• Experience in Grant and RFP writing and execution
• Technology Innovation and Commercialization
• Experience Brand Development & Management
• Experience in Marketing, Advertising and Public Relations
• Experience in Public Finance/Investment
• Experience in Economic Development
• Experience in Product & Application Extension
• Pioneer in high-definition commercial production
• Pioneer in Electronics Funds Transfer
• Founder of a highly acclaimed international action sports event

Michael currently serves as the President and Chief Executive Officer of Reyes Global Holdings, a management, investment and strategic development company working the Pharma, Manufacturing, Media & Marketing, Healthcare Technology and Environmental sectors.
