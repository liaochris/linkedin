Tony Ellis’s Summary

Tony has been providing business, sales and channel development consulting to major corporations for more than 30 years. His focus has always been in technology for both commercial and governments organizations.

For the past several years Tony has had the opportunity to work closely at the corporate, government agency and DoD level to develop the commingling of open-source news and business information with internal government and enterprise information. He has been working with major commercial and federal systems integrators to integrate news and business content into enterprise search applications. His efforts teamed a major content provider and a large systems integrator to provide services for various government intelligence agencies.

Tony has developed and perfected the process of acquiring, analyzing and distributing accurate business information for competitive advantage. His competitive intelligence processes have been implemented by major commercial and government IT contractors for major bidding situations dealing with “price-to-win’ and competitive positioning. His tactics have helped win hundreds of millions of dollars in business in both business sectors.

Tony has delivered projects in information technology, telecommunications, finance and banking, insurance, manufacturing and federal, state and local government. Tony also owed and operated a full-service New York City promotion and marketing company. For more than ten years the ACEllis Communications was a pioneer in developing and delivering computer technology for advertising & sales promotion campaigns.
