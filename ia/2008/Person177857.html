Steve Nimmons’s Summary

Steve is a Professional Engineer, IT Practitioner and Principle Consultant (formerly with KPMG). He is a leading innovator and technical evangelist with pragmatic and sophisticated commercial acumen.

Steve has 17 years experience in the IT industry and currently leads a team of integration, security and content specialists in the UK in the design and implementation of multi-million pound IT systems across many industries.

He graduated in 1993 with an honours degree in Computing Science and Diploma in Industrial Studies (BSc Hons, DIS).

Steve's professional credentials include:

- Certified European Engineer (Eur Ing)
- Chartered Engineer (CEng)
- Chartered Member British Computer Society (MBCS CITP)
- Member, British Computer Society ELITE Group
- Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts (FRSA)
- Fellow of the Institute for the Management of Information Systems (FIMIS)
- Fellow of the Institution of Analysts and Programmers (FIAP)
- Member of the Institution of Engineering and Technology
- PRINCE2 Practitioner

Steve is an experienced writer and speaker on topics including RFID, Web2.0, Integration, Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), BPM, Security and Emerging Technology. He participates in an number of industry panels and is a member of the Conservative Technology Forum.

Steve Nimmons’s Specialties:

Pre-sales, Systems Architecture, Consulting, Technology Vision, Proposition Development, Innovation, Mentoring, Project Management, Engineering Best Practice, SOA, BPM, Workflow, EAI, Partner Management, Commercial Development, Technology Strategy, Entrepreneurship, Web2.0, VoIP, Conditional Access Systems, DVB.
