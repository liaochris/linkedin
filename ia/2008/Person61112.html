Bruno Depré’s Summary

B.A.S.S.is your partner to implement and manage electrostatic systems. We provide advice and approved products to implement your electrostatic safe working area. Our core business is electrostatic discharge (ESD). We are focusing on preventing electro static discharge and ensuring ESD protection. We also offer electrostatic discharge training and Quality Management by the company ElectroStat.

EFFECTS OF ELECTROSTATIC DISCHARGE (ESD)
All materials have different levels of sensitivity for building up electrostatic charges. When those materials are brought together or have contact, a discharge will occur. The magnitude of the discharge depends upon the tribo-electro properties.
Electronic components can be seriously damaged by electrostatic discharges, resulting in immediate or latent failures.

Immediate failures.
Unseen electronic damage can cause a higher manufacturing cost such as loss of production, rework and scrap. Defects which fail during production will probably not reach the customer.

Latent failures.
More problematic than an immediate failure, an electrostatic discharge can weaken the electronic component, with a higher rate of failure after reaching the customer. More than 90% of these defects are caused by latent failures. Resultant repair costs include personnel, travel and material costs. A still higher cost is the potential loss of the customer.
