Erick Mott’s Summary

I'm a San Francisco / Silicon Valley area professional with 20 years of results in product, service, and solution marketing, communications and innovation (B2B and B2C) at agency, startup, medium and Fortune 1000 organizations.

Hands-on, teaming and leadership experience includes: entrepreneurial achievements in new products, go-to-market and revenue creation; strategic planning; corporate marketing, messaging and collateral creation; integrated programs development and execution; Web operations and email marketing campaigns; Internet services and online community deployment; team building and cross-functional collaboration; product marketing, channels and sales support; PR and AR strategy and programs management.
