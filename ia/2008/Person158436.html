Reggie Wideman’s Summary

Experienced business consultant specializing in integrating software solutions with existing organizational protocols.

My career transitioned from editorial and writing projects at the journal, Proceedings of the National Academies, to more technology focused initiatives. Subsequent professional opportunities provided substantial experience as a project manager, client relationship expert, and a successful team builder.
