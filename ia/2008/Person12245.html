Andy Coleman’s Summary

Spent 8 years at eBay, as the company grew from under 200 employees to several thousand. As the Director of Business Development for the Strategic Partners group, was responsible for strategies to add partner related services to the eBay platform creating new revenue streams via value added programs for end users. In this capacity, lead a team responsible for business development, product development and marketing of shipping features on eBay, resulting in partnerships with the USPS and UPS and the creation of a new shipping platform for sellers. As a member of the Strategic Partners Group, also created revenue opportunities with several partners and helped form sales, pricing and inventory strategy for advertising placements on eBay. Prior to that, was the Director of Category Management for Collectibles and Sports, with P&L responsibility for these categories on eBay.

Andy Coleman’s Specialties:

Business development, marketing partnerships, branding, user acquisition, internet business models and alliances
