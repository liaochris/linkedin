Laura Micalizzi’s Summary

Creative thinker with a 360° architectural view of the project from conception to production with the current goal of supporting and facilitating brands and companies in the expression of their arts and delivery of their message through atmosphere and space creation.

Further aim of perfectioning relationships with clients to reach further independence in my professional role using travel as a tool to enrich views and perspectives.

Independence on development of a project from concept to brief, from mood and material board to final specification and to completion of project. Active and positive interaction with clients. Good team player.

Laura Micalizzi’s Experience

  • Co-Founder

    OR_PROJECT

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Architecture & Planning industry)

    September 2007 — Present (8 months)

    Or Project is not a company but a collaboration among architects and designers from the Architectural Association and Politecnico di Milano regarding the study and construction of a structure which environmentally reacts to the behaviour of the sun.

    The clear segments of the shading device turn coloured when hit by direct sunlight making Or a photoreactive chandelier.

    The installation may be viewed in via Borgospesso 21 during the Milan Furniture Fair in April 2008.
