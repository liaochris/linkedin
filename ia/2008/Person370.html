Chris Kay’s Summary

First a little about myself,

I am a self motivated level designer who comes from a strong background in multiplayer first person shooters, I started the never ending learning process of level design back in 1998 when Unreal was released. From there I expanded my knowledge working with Unreal Tournament and Counterstrike.

My understanding of level design and game development has grown to cover many types of FPS game from realistic to arcade styles. Although I spend most time working on first person shooters I often enjoy designing and building real time strategy environments.

On top of professional work I have been a proud developer of Insurgency for over 4 years, with a mixture of industry professionals and remarkably talented amateurs we have produced a game that is a major talking point of the multiplayer PC community that is sure to grow with time.
