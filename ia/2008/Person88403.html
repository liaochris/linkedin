Jennifer Laing Copeland’s Summary

Jennifer Copeland is a leading expert in interactive marketing for higher education.

As General Manager and Senior Consultant with DemandEngine, an interactive marketing firm for higher education, Jennifer works with colleges and universities across the country. Her expertise includes email and search marketing, lead generation, communications planning, and university outreach.

Jennifer is an award-winning speaker and has presented at the AMA Symposium for Higher Education, ACHE, UCEA, NCMPR, SACAC, GACRAO, CACRAO, NCUCCC, and ICCE.

Her previous experience includes enrollment marketing at a private university where she managed student tele-counseling efforts as well as yield events and recruiting territories. In her experience with non-profit marketing and fundraising, Jennifer managed public relations campaigns that resulted in national media coverage, spearheaded solicitations for corporate gifts and sponsorships, and lead the execution of fundraising campaigns that raised over $1.3 million.

A native of Savannah, Georgia, Jennifer lives there with her husband, Tim Copeland, and son, John.
