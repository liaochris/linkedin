Gennaro de Concilio deconcilio@yahoo.it’s Summary

Gennaro De Concilio

Gennaro de Concilio is a corporate consultant to small and medium sized businesses and to major industries. He specialises in helping companies to move into the international sphere and in providing advice on ethical matters as businesses develop. He is a member of the Italian Confederation of young entrepreneurs Apindustria e Confapi.

Recently he has facilitated partnerships between AISEC International and the Faculty of Economics of Roma 3, Naples Federico II and Naples Partenope Universities, and he also has a close professional partnership with Edwin Tinoco (Luciano Pavarotti's last Music Assistant) in a study aimed at creating and nurturing new projects (not SMO) in the fields of music, performance and arts administration.

Within the past year he has worked for Placido Domingo as an independent consultant on feasibility studies for future projects.

His current role is Area Manager, Purchasing, for Intercantieri Vittadello plc - annual turnover 120 million euro - which together with Valditerra – Gruppo Comsa, Sirti and Rotahem will take part in bidding for contract work with Algeria’s National Railways - a business plan that anticipates an investment of 5 billion euro over the next five years.



Gennaro de Concilio is available for external consultancy roles for both Italian and foreign companies. He is also happy to undertake arts projects that involve start up and implementation of commercial internationalization, using weblog technology as a means of two-way communication.
