Virginia Alimonda | va@mdtmdt.com’s Summary

Owner and Vice President of Valle Perdido Estate Winery + Wine Resort (member of www.slh.com), primarily focuses on P.R., communication and representing the company.
Director of Muñoz de Toro | Translation Bureau, a group professional translators, certified by the American Translators Association and/or the Argentina Sworn Translators Association, with expertise in the corporate, legal and financial areas, among others.
Also a photographer, working on the first photography book of Patagonia Wine Country, its people and its land.
