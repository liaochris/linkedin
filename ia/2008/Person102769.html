Karyn Zuidinga’s Experience

  • Director of User Experience & Principal

    Analytic Design Group Inc

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    September 2005 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    Analytic Design Group is a user experience consulting company. Our business is to craft an outstanding user experience for all users; one that meets both usability standards as well as the business goals.

    Our team of top-notch information architects, interaction designers, and usability specialists has been called on by some of the biggest brands in North America (AT&T, BC Hydro, Hitachi, Telus) to conceive of, create, and enrich the user experience of their products.

  • Interactive Information Design Instructor

    Capilano College

    (Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Higher Education industry)

    September 2005 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    I teach the Interactive Information Design course at Capilano College's Interactive Design Program. The course now runs through the first two terms. We cover all of the basics of user experience:
    - user modeling and personas
    - requirements gathering
    - site maps
    - wireframes
    - interaction design

    Students are expected to apply the theory of the course in both a critical thinking capacity as we look at websites throughout the course and as they work through the various projects.
