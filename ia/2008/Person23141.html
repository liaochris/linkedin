Bill Ashmore, CIR [Bill@numericjobs.com]’s Summary

Bill Ashmore is currently a Senior Account Manager for Numeric, LLC where he is leveraging his Social Networking and internet sourcing techniques to find quality passive candidates for clients. Numeric, LLC is a premier supplier of information technology professionals to America’s largest and most progressive businesses. We provide technical solutions nationwide through our professional consultants and permanent recruitment services.

Numeric offers specialized expertise in SAS and SQL programming, data analysis, database design and management, database marketng and campaign, credit/risk management and statistical modeling services. Our team of account managers and recruiters are experts in matching the right candidate skill set to your company’s specific requirements.

We offer a wide variety of solutions to meet your needs. Whether in need of a single programmer, analyst, or statistician or an entire team of consultants, we can meet those needs quickly. We can help boost your company’s effectiveness by supplying experienced consultants with skills in today's newest, most advanced technologies

Prior to joining Numeric Bill was the founder and President of MoreTech Consulting where he successfully placed several hundred information technology professionals in the last several years. Bill is highly skilled in sourcing techniques as well as developing and leveraging many of the Internet technologies to uncover the best in the technology marketplace. Typically, the “best” are not those with their resume’s plastered on the Internet, to uncover these people takes experience in searching technique’s as well as the required technical background and knowledge to “sell” the individual on the opportunity. He has helped develop rapid recruiting methodologies for start up as well as Fortune 500 companies. Prior to recruiting he was a Project Manager for the DOD/DOE nuclear weapons remediation.
