Linda P. Taylor’s Summary

I have spent over 30 years helping college-bound families achieve their dreams. Even when working in other industries, I volunteered through various nonprofits to continue my mission. After 5 years in the motorsports marketing field working in Yourth & Education, I created College Funding Network to make college financial planning my full-time job.

The goal of CFN is to help college-bound families rescue their college dreams from the confusing and stressful college "system". I have assembled a team of nationwide college, financial and tax professionals to provide consulting services that help students and their families, select, apply and pay for college without bankrupting their future. Simply put, we provide the right information at the right time so families can regain control of the process.

Linda P. Taylor’s Specialties:

College financial planning specializing in small and home-based businesses. Assist other advisors including legal, financial and tax professionals in offering services to their clients including marketing and technical support.
