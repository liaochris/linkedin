IT Career Summary

I am an IT professional with over eighteen years of outstanding accomplishments and successful business experience in delivering results, including the restructuring the technology of entire business operations; business impact analysis; budget management; personnel management; team building; requirements analysis; application selection, development and delivery.

I have a strong track record in delivering projects in key areas of the business to improve profitability, reduce duplication of effort, and increase the number of financial products traded. Most of my practical experience has been gained in the Investment Banking world in both the Fixed Income and Equity areas of business.
