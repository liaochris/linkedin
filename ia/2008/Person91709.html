Jim Stogdill’s Summary

Jim is CTO at Gestalt, a leading provider of collaborative technology for governments and Fortune 500 customers that has grown over tenfold in four years. As CTO he is responsible for technology direction, solution design, IP, and Gestalt's agile development methodology. He primary goal is to keep Gestalt innovating as it grows.

His current focus areas include SOA/ESB, energy use in computing, SIP and collaboration, Web 2.0 and NetCentricity, geo-spatial services, and open source software in the Defense space. He also draws attention to the importance of compelling user experience.

Jim was previously the Managing Director of Product Management with ICG Commerce, a leading Procurement Services Provider. In that role he delivered multiple generations of the technology platforms that made the company’s unique strategy a reality. Prior to joining ICG Commerce, Jim was the CTO of TreasuryPoint, a business of SEI Investments. Ahead of its time, TreasuryPoint brought an innovative heuristically-enhanced trade decision optimizer, multi-vendor fixed income trading, and a variety of decision support content to the middle market corporate treasury. Today TreasuryPoint had over $3B in assets under management within two years of its launch.

Jim's experience includes Product Development, Product Management, and Operations in the Financial Services, Sourcing and Supply Chain, Energy, and Defense sectors. He has made a career out of the creative use of technology and comfortably bridges the gap between the beneficiaries of technology and its developers.

In his previous life, Jim was a U.S. Navy submarine officer, a role which permitted him to travel the world; unfortunately it all looked like the inside of a submarine.

Jim has a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin. He also holds a MSE in Management of Technology from the University of Pennsylvania School of Engineering, co-sponsored by the Wharton School of Business.
