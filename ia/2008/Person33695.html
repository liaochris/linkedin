Charles Nurse’s Summary

I am a Web Developer specializing in ASP.NET, using C# or Visual Basic.NET. I completed a Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Object Technology Programming in 2003.

Since the fall of 2004 I have worked as Lead Developer in the DotNetNuke Web Application Project, and in May 2007 I was appointed Senior Architect of DotNetNuke Corporation the owner of the DotNetNuke project where I continue to work on the development of the leading Open-Source project on .NET.

In January 2007 I was awarded a Microsft MVP (Most Valuable Professional) award, which was renewed in January 2008, and in February 2008 I was nominated and accepted into the ASPInsiders - a group of ASP.NET Developers that work closely with the Microsoft ASP.NET development team, providing feedback on future features.
