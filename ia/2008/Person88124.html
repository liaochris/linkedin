Jennifer Sullivan’s Summary

Energetic business leader and visionary regarded for expertise in integrated marketing, partnership acquisitions, licensing strategies and brand building. Noted for ability to strategically align marketplace trends with business objectives to achieve breakthrough, measurable results
____________________________________________

Jennifer Sullivan is a multinational marketing and brand strategist with expertise in creating high-impact promotions and experiential marketing campaigns for the retail, entertainment, technology and consumer packaged goods industries.

An expert in branded entertainment and interactive technologies, Jennifer has held a series of executive level posts with leading marketing agencies, creating and implementing breakthrough, multi-million dollar programs for global brand leaders including McDonald’s, Burger King, Hallmark, Toys "R" Us, Kellogg’s, Masterfoods, Vivendi Universal and Kraft Foods.

From multi-category licensing programs to high-profile event sponsorships, Jennifer has been the catalyst behind mutually successful alliances between corporate brands and leading entertainment companies including The Walt Disney Company, 20th Century Fox, Warner Bros., Sony Entertainment, NFL, Viacom, LucasFilm and Universal Music Group.

Leveraging a global network of best-of-class technology companies and other creative resources, Jennifer's comprehensive skill set includes market research, strategic planning, branding and design services, partner negotiations, project management, business development and beyond.

A professional writer, graphic designer, opera singer, public speaker and educator, Jennifer currently lives in West Hollywood, CA and is an active member of Step Up, Women in Toys and Women in Technology International.

Jennifer Sullivan’s Specialties:

Entertainment, Marketing, Promotions, Licensing, Youth and Family Markets, Partnerships, Advertising, Incentives, QSR and Retail, Branding, Toys, Design and Manufacturing
