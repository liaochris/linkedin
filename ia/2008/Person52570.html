Dahna Chandler [LION]’s Summary

My LinkedIn objective is to "pay it forward" by being an information resource and facilitating relationships. I can do that by developing a large network and connecting you with resources, professionals and experts you need. I will also use professional networking in LinkedIn Groups to develop my network and resources as well as find subjects and interviewees for my articles and content and press copywriting work. Feel free to send me an invitation to connect at dahna@socialmediasavvyblog.com.

As importantly, I'm an award-winning, internationally published journalist* with over a decade of business writing experience for recognized and respected national publications. I'm also a recognized publicist and artist representative with a strong arts and entertainment reporting background.

I've written about small business development, careers, business and personal finance, banking and media business and their executives for publications including Black Enterprise and multiple trade and specialty publications. I've also reported on arts, events, theater, dance and music.

I'm putting that experience to use in my new business, eCopy & Press, writing effective print and online content that helps organizations deliver their intended message or inform and increase readership and creating press materials that generate media coverage, audience share and increased ticket sales and business profits.

I'm also putting that using my facility with aggregating scattered sources of data and taking complex concepts and turning it into reader-friendly prose into a blog, Getting Social Media Savvy (www.socialmediasavvyblog.com) to help others rapidly gain sophistication using social media as I do.

*********************

*With rare exception for editors already known to me, I no longer offer writing services to consumer-oriented publications. Please visit my website or contact me for more information.
