Fred Aiese, LCSW’s Summary

I am skilled at working with people of diverse backgrounds on challenging life paths. My background is in psychoanalytic and psychodynamic psychotherapy. I have a Master's Degree in Social Work from Fordham University with post-graduate training from Psychoanalytic Psychotherapy Study Center (PPSC). I have held clinical and administrative positions during my career: Jewish Child Care Association; Catholic Charities; Gilda's Club; and International Center for the Disabled. My aspirations are to complete my psychoanalytic training at PPSC, gaining certification as a psychoanalyst concurrently with continuing my private practice. I consider myself to have an eclectic approach, borrowing from Internpersonal, Object Relations, and Modern Psychoanalytic orientations.Harry Stack Sullivan stated it best: "People are more alike than otherwise."

Please refer to my website: www.fredcsw.com.
