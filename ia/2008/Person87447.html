Jeff Lipschultz’s Summary

Does your company need help finding and hiring talented technical professionals or executives? Or perhaps an onsite consultant or team of experts for a software project? A-List Solutions excels at sourcing and recruiting hard-to-find skill sets and takes pride in a targeted, relationship-based approach. Our team has experience in providing the talent for permanent positions, contract consulting and executive searches in many industries. We work with start-ups to Fortune 500 companies.

I am a strong leader with experience in staffing and managing teams in engineering, program management, and sales in diverse industries. I have a proven track record for developing, marketing, and selling a wide spectrum of products and services and managing large-scale products and initiatives. In staffing specifically, I have hired engineers, IT and finance professionals, sales reps, technicians, Six Sigma and Lean experts, and managers of many talents. Additionally, I have recruited at universities all over the country.
