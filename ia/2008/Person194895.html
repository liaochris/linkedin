    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Research industry)

    September 2005 — October 2007 (2 years 2 months)

    A 2-year post master/technological designer programme in Mathematics for Industry. Worked in mathematical physics stream to model mathematically industrial processes for problem solving and optimization.

    Modelling project for Wärtsilä Propulsion Netherlands B.V. Assessed a method to increase the efficiency of propellers by measuring signals generated by Doppler log.
