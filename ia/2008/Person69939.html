Gilbert Denizard’s Summary

Mission Statement
My mission is to fit each of my clients with the right mortgage program, one that takes into account individual financial needs and goals, not just the rate and terms. I will guide my clients throughout the entire process, answering all questions along the way, to ensure that each loan closes on time without any surprises. I will do this by providing a level of service that will leave you saying “wow” every step of the way.

Dedicated, passionate and professional is how I stay on top of the Mortgage Industry and the result is that most of my business is by referral from satisfied clients.

I have over 10 years of experience in the Mortgage Industry and I just completed my first year with Fairway Independent Mortgage. I have originated mortgages in 27 states. I am always prepared to guide you from start to finish in your mortgage transaction. I understand all aspects of the loan process, knowing that your home mortgage is one of the most important decisions of your lifetime. Remember, you only think about home financing a few times in your life ~ we think about it every single day. This is your home and your future. It is my profession and passion.

I originate conforming, FHA, VA and non conforming mortgages. It doesn’t matter if you are self employed, you have credit issues such as a recent bankruptcy or are a first time home buyer. My job is to take care of you better than anyone else.

Contact me with any and all or your questions. I look forward to meeting and helping you!!

Gilbert Denizard’s Specialties:

First time home buyers, credit rehabilitation programs, self employed borrowers, FHA, VA, conforming, sub prime, purchases and refinances.
