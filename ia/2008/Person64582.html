Flyn Penoyer’s Summary

Food for Thought…
Strategy without tactics is the slowest route to victory. Tactics without strategy is the noise before defeat. ...Sun Tzu

As an avid player/student of the strategic games, Chess and Go, this is a lesson I have learned all too many times.

Using my expertise I to help companies develop "selling systems" that enable sales and management to elevate their base level of performance and provide quality internal sales development for the team.

Most salespeople operate without support tools or the direction of a clear and well documented strategy. While most managers’ work without the structures necessary to support the development and improvement of their team’s effort.

"Franchising” a concept which made McDonalds restaurants so successful should be applied to the sales organizations and their selling processes. By having your team work from a tested process (As opposed to a high dependence on individual skills.) that incorporates the maximum number of best practices into the selling effort, you increase the performance and potential of every team member.

Additionally, this process/system provides the structure and resources needed to empower managers in effectively training, coaching, and improving the selling process.

These two things are especially important with regard to inside sales teams as most inside reps don’t have the skills or acumen to fly by the seat of their pants effectively. Most sales managers don’t have formal backgrounds in training and coaching not to mention the development of the support materials necessary to execute these functions effectively. By franchising (documenting) the sales process you enable these things.

I am an expert networker, web-marketer, and an ardent student of human potential and advancing my own skills and abilities. I currently run, within the LI Q&A, sales, management, and leadership questions to which I engage and challenge the participants sharing my expertise and ideas.
