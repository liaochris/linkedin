RJ Visser’s Summary

Entrepreneurial innovator. Broad and international experience in product planning, strategy and business development. Currently Chief Marketing and Sales at Beatbrew, part of Philips Lifestyle Incubator, offering a unique music delivery service to consumers leveraging unique audio formats and recommendation technology. Prior to this position served as VP Business Development at Philips Consumer Electronics North America, responsible for the business development of Set Top Boxes in the US, representing over $250 Million annual turnover. Prior to joining Philips worked as an independent consultant in new business creation to various organizations like Philips and start-ups involving hardware and software products as well as online services. Have held various senior positions in Product Strategy and Planning and Business Development at Philips Electronics and Compaq Computer Corporation.
