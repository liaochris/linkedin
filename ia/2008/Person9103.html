Christopher D. Coutu’s Summary

After 13 years of honorable service within the United States Air Force and Connecticut Air National Guard Chris founded AmericanWarrior to make a patriotic difference.

In September of 2007 hundreds of volunteers donated their time and funds to send 100 World War II Veterans to the WWII Memorial in Washington DC. This operation was our first step to send 1000 by 2010. The challenge is the average age of these heroes is over 85 and over 1200 move on to a greater place daily.

In order to preserve the legacy of these heroes we have established Operation Freedom Legacy. This operation utilizes informative seminars to motivate students to make a patriotic difference and volunteer. This seminar informs students about the roots of our national anthem, patriotism, sacrifice and freedom. To date we have provided seminars at senior centers, schools and patriotic events.

On November 6th of 2007, Chris was elected to serve on the Norwich City Council. This has been an amazing journey and a humbling experience to represent the people of Norwich. For more information about the campaign and Christopher Coutu please visit www.ChristopherCoutu.com or AmericanWarrior.org

"The leaders of tomorrow are busy tonight."
