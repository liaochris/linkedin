Frank (Francesco) S. Adamo’s Summary

Frank's mission is "Helping others to help themselves succeed." Because he had a terrible fear of speaking to groups, he developed into a great listener. He has mentored friends and business associates for most of his career because of his simple and common sense approach to problem solving.

Frank is known to many of his friends as Francesco, the Godfather of Basic Reasoning, Frank has taken his mentoring skills and expanded his efforts to offer individual/group coaching sessions. He is now a communications and a certified transition coach, an international trainer and instructor, and an author.

His first book, "31 Tips to becoming an Effective Presenter," has been well received and is available at http://www.fsadamo.com/shop/index.htm.

Frank also offers a series of “Success through Effective Communications” workshops designed to impact even the most successful people.Frank knows that the majority of individuals who speak in front of groups are not interested in becoming professional, public speakers, but they do want to be effective in their presentation skills. To that end, Frank brings a unique perspective to his workshops and publications by helping others improve their presentation skills.
