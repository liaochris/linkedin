Mikel McCall’s Summary

My background in Fitness Management and Outdoor Recreation is based on a practical combination of education and work experiences. Formal education and experience have equipped me with the necessary tools to effectively instruct and assist both adults and children toward physical, behavioral, and personal development. I have experience supervising, leading, planning, coordinating, and implementing a variety of adventure/recreational activities. I am a SOLO Wilderness First Responder, which equips me with the knowledge needed to deal with crises in remote settings.
Additionally, I have utilized the prevention and decision-making skills needed to design individualized exercise routines for anyone, in a variety of situations. I am an ACE and AFAA Certified Personal Trainer and an ACE Certified Lifestyle and Weight Management Consultant. These have enabled me to develop sound, balanced weight management programs that bring together the three critical components of nutrition, exercise and lifestyle change.
Moreover, having worked well with the public, clients, managers, and co-workers, I possess strong interpersonal, time-management, teamwork and service-oriented skills.
