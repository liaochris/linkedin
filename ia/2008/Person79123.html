BRIAN HAHN’s Summary

The first phase of my career experience consisted of mainly graphic design, cartooning, illustration and prepress production.

The second phase is best described as: “working way up ladder in corporate, computer game development” where I was a production artist, art director, producer and game designer for over 14 years.

The third phase took me to “independent game development.” Simultaneously I instructed middle schoolers the basics of game development.

The fourth phase came in the form of design of newspaper advertising.

Phase five was back to corporate game development, this time for PS2 and Xbox consoles.

Phase six became doing freelance graphics for independent game development, marketing, and many other types of art related activities.

Now in phase seven, I'm working as a marketing specialist for general advertising like web design, graphic design and printed marketing materials such as business cards, flyers, car wraps, signs, t-shirts, posters, and on and on...pretty much anything involving promotion or self or business.

In the future I want to expand marketing activities in include cartoon animation; film making, TV advertising; performing arts; teaching; games and research about communication behavior and how it works on the individual and society.

BRIAN HAHN’s Specialties:

Graphic design, web design, producing, art directing, storyboarding, conceptual art, character design, illustration, painting and 3-D graphics. I operated efficiently in Photoshop, MS Office, Illustrator; PowerPoint; HTML, Dreamweaver and 3D Max. I possess many years of experience in team management, including leadership, scheduling, budgeting and maximizing the effectiveness of inter-team and team to team cooperation.
