Henry Ward’s Summary

rEvolve is a resource consultancy for technology companies.
We are located in the bay area, a global hotbed for innovation.
rEvolve has over twenty years of combined experience facilitating our clients search and selection of technology professionals in the following areas: OOA/OOD, software architecture, design and development of web based applications, systems software and tools, quality assurance, front and back-end engineering, UI design, embedded firmware, wireless networks, audio/video, ERP, data storage, data driven, project management, business analysis, systems support to include database and systems administrators, security, CRM, and other infrastructure initiatives. Our goal is to continuously improve our services and to keep abreast of emerging technologies.

Clients include: AutoDesk, Dolby, Real Networks, Amazon, Qool.com, Salesforce.com, Cisco, Sun Microsystems, Microsoft, Symbian, Wells Fargo, Toyota, Nissan, Responsys, Cent'erd, Leap Frog, People soft/Oracle, A2Z and Alexa.

Future clients: We look forward to providing your direct hire and contractor needs. rEvolves proprietary database is filled with a wide range of passive candidates here in the bay area who are interested in new opportunities, but are not listed on any job boards. We maintain quality real time relationships with a variety of qualified technology professionals.

Candidates: We have a variety of Fulltime/Perm openings at all times. And always welcome the opportunity to work with quality, dedicated technology professionals.
So please get in touch with us if you are interested in pursuing new opportunities.
