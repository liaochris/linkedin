Eelco Kraefft’s Summary

Active in contract publishing and marketing since 1990, digital media since 1993. Most marketing media focus on what the sender wants. In the midst of information overload commercial media need more focus on the receiver, (clients, prospects, employees). Understanding the audience, defending their interests and creatively combining that with business goals of the sender is the added value of my business activitities. At Ehio Media we have developed a profound understanding of pull marketing methods, Google and other search engines. Another important added value is the knowledge of specific types of media. In that I specialise in: online media (websites, webshops), other Digital media (DVD and games), visual media (comics & animation, rich media), knowledge and educational media. Visit http://www.ehio.nl for more.

Eelco Kraefft’s Specialties:

internet marketing, contract publishing, corporate media & corporate learning, knowledge media, online communities, educational media, digital media (internet, dvd, 'serious' games), marketing of cities, regions
