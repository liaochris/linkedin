Per Rasmussen’s Summary

I have 15 years management and leadership experience from Danish and international assignments in the eBusiness and internet media space. Over the past 10 years I have especially focused on creating and growing a business based on the internet.

eBusiness, Internet media and Telecom are my main areas of experience. Where I have had positions as CEO of a internet media company, to Vice President in TDC - heading the internet sales, support and media channel of the largest Danish Telecom.

I have a passion for creating outstanding growth of a internet based business and the financial results, based on strategic use of the internet. And for the creation of web sites and ecomerce solutions with exceptional sales performance.

Per Rasmussen’s Specialties:

General Management, Online strategy, Channel strategy, eBusiness, eCommerce, Internet marketing and branding, Internet media, Sports media, internet security, consultancy
