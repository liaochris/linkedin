Rob Cipriano’s Summary

IllumAlliance International Humanitarian Group, LLC is an international humanitarian project development firm recognized for our creative approach to business, government and humanitarian issues facing our world today.

The work we do is often groundbreaking.

With approximately 100 associates throughout the past 20 years, and a worldwide network of partners, IllumAlliance creates partnerships with our clients on a wide range of matters.

Our approach to humanitarian project development is collaborative, with partner's and associate's functioning as integrated teams to bring the most appropriate response to client needs.

At IllumAlliance International Humanitarian Group, LLC we foster a culture where our partners take great pride in their fundamental skills, where all of our associate's are expected to have consummate expertise in their particular discipline. We strive to provide an environment where our associates can fully apply their wide-ranging experiences and skills and excel in the substantive areas of their particular practice.

We pride ourselves in delivering project development and finance services efficiently, assigning partners and staff that are appropriate to the task, not to the perceived bottom line, but in saving or improving lifes.

We adhere to the highest ethical standards. We know that our reputation for high quality services and integrity are our most precious assets. We maintain an approach to our practice that enhances our ability to identify ethical issues as they arise, and we pride ourselves in having the fortitude to do what's right.

This heritage creates an unusual sense of stewardship in our firm, and ensures that partners and staff alike look at generations past and consider future generations when making decisions about our practice.

Since 1985, the leadership team at IllumAlliance has been highly regarded for our integrity, our work ethic, the quality of our project advice, and our people.

  • Founder & Chairman

    Beyond Borders iTV LLC

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Beyond Borders iTV, LLC www.bbitv.tv is a wholly own subsidiary of IllumAlliance International Humanitarian Group, LLC., is an humanitarian IPTV service provider delivering humanitarian programming content via the Internet instead of traditional formats like cable or satellite received by the viewer through the Internet.

    It’s TV on the Internet but besides offering a true TV-like experience on multiple devices, it allows for video on demand, two-way interactivity, e-commerce capabilities and many other features – it takes TV and adds the best of the web, a truly convergent experience.

    It opens up new platforms for content – especially for longer form branded entertainment content. For brands and advertisers, it’s also exciting.

    We provide a unified platform for charities and non-for-profits organizations that allows them to take advantage of the new market dynamics in communications and specifically the new IPTV market.

  • Founder & Chairman

    IllumAlliance International Humanitarian Group, LLC

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    May 2000 — Present (7 years 10 months)

    IllumAlliance International Humanitarian Group, LLC is an international humanitarian organization comprised of hundreds of associates from every walk of life. Its primary mission is to develop companies, non-profits, foundations, projects, associates and teams for the effective delievry of humanitarian aid and assistance to global crisis areas.

    IllumAlliance was created in 2000 to maintain focus and humanitarian goals in numerous countries and in an assortment of charitable arenas.

    Currently, IllumAlliance is delivering impact and driving much needed attention to Darfur, Uganda and several centers in Asia, including China, Vietnam, Burma and Cambodia.

    In addition to the primary mission, IllumAlliance began in 2006 the Beyond Borders ITV group. Beyond Borders iTV LLC is a wholly owned and controlled subsidiary of IllumAlliance International Humanitarian Group LLC.
