Bill Spaulding’s Summary

Simply put, I'm a tech guy. I build rock-solid voice and data networks. My networks do not fail. I have my present job because my prior employer went belly up - not because of anything I did or didn't do, but because in the mortgage business it happens as markets shift. My network there went 3 years with no outages or infections and 26 months without an Exchange failure.

Here at ConquistAmerica, I designed, purchased, installed, and configured the entire data network in 6 weeks. The network was delivered ahead of schedule and under budget. When we turned it on, it worked flawlessly and has continued without interruption for more than a year. The network includes database servers, Exchange front and back end servers, patch management, anti-virus, file servers - the full compliment.

Keep it simple. Keep it strong. And always remember that the rest of the company are my customers.
