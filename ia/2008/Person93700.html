Joel Montgomery’s Summary

Joel Montgomery is currently pursuing his MBA at Thunderbird School of Global Management. He has a background that combines both private and non-profit experience and upon graduation, he hopes to promote economic development in less-developed countries through the application of business principles.

Joel is originally from Alabama, but he moved to New Haven, Connecticut to study Mechanical Engineering at Yale University. After graduation, Joel traveled to San José, Costa Rica, where he lived and worked in a poor suburb of the capital city doing community service. Afterward, Joel relocated to New Jersey, where he became the Director of Latin American Sales for a packaging machinery manufacturer. Joel took a territory that had been plagued with inconsistent sales and grew the business by over 300% to surpass $1M for the first time in company history.

In January, Joel was working with the Business Development Center in Jordan as an Operations Consultant for an office furniture manufacturer. This past summer, he worked as a Small Business Consultant with Endeavor Global.
