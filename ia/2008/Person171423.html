Previously I was the CMO/COO at Mindbridge Software

- Helped grow company from start-up to one of the fastest growing high technology companies in United States. Company named to the Inc 500 as one of the fastest growing companies in 2002 and 2003.
- Managed all aspect of Sales including hiring and managing direct and channel sales team.
- Developed and Managed all Marketing programs including media buying, Internet strategy and advertising strategy
- Advertising strategy include all aspects of Advertising including managing creative personnel, and planning media buys in print, online and direct mail.
- Developed and Managed Public Relation programs including writing and editing of press releases, public speaking events and building and maintaining relationships with local, national and international media.
- Managed the development of customer base by expanding the business from exclusively domestic to include a presence in 15 foreign markets.
- Successfully changed strategic direction from services-based organization to product revenue model tripling product margins.
- Built and maintained strong relationships with customers and strategic partners enabling the company to continue to grow throughout technology cycle.
- Experienced public speaker to audiences as large as 2,000 people in a variety of different venues on a regular basis.
