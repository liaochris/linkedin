    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Consumer Services industry)

    January 2008 — Present (2 months)

    Vlugger is a "Dutch auction" site, somewhat like an eBay-in-reverse. A Dutch auction is "where the auctioneer begins with a high asking price which is lowered until some participant is willing to accept the auctioneer's price, or a predetermined reserve price (the seller's minimum acceptable price) is reached. The winning participant pays the last announced price. Experiment indicates that a Dutch auction typically results in lower sale prices." — Wikipedia.

    "Vlugger", by the way, is the Dutch word for "faster": when you decide to buy an item at the announced price, it's yours immediately.

    We are currently in private beta only. If you want to be notified when we go live, sign at www.vlugger.com and we will put you on the list.
