Barbara Wertheim’s Summary

Wertheim Design provides creativity, speed and flexibility without the high costs associated with large advertising agencies. Wertheim Design was established in 1996 by myself, Barbara Wertheim, to service a variety of clients looking for enhanced image and increased consumer awareness through good design. We realize that we can't be all things to all companies, which is why we collaborate with specialists—from marketing consultants to programming specialists—to offer our clients a complete communications package. This flexible structure allows us to assemble the perfect team for your project. We will immerse ourselves in everything about your organization in order to deliver solutions that are strategically sound and innovative.
