The Telecom Efficiency Study will:

• Track telecommunications billing for customers
• Identify carrier billing errors
• Recover overpayment dollars from carriers
• Establish cost savings for customers through network configuration analysis
• Create reports to assist customers in tracking telecommunications expenses

    Cells for Cells | Recycle for a Cure.

    Cells for Cells | Recycle for a Cure program reconditions, resells, and recycles used cell phones that have been donated by individuals and companies. This fundraiser will help find cures for blood cancers and will keep electronic waste out of our landfills. A portion of the money raised goes to The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society, the world’s largest voluntary health organization dedicated to funding blood cancer research, education and patient services. If you know someone who has an immediate blood cancer need, please call the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society’s Information Resource Center (IRC) at 800-955-4572.
