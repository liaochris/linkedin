Rob Shore’s Summary

After 30 years in the corporate world focusing exclusively on Sales and Sales Marketing, "shorespeak" is born.

Through shorespeak our mission is to rescue sales forces and their managers from the 'Sea of Sameness' and move them to a place of Maximum Memorability. This is accomplished through speaking engagements, consulting and private management coaching.

Our keynote, breakout and seminar performances are now available for booking. Private coaching appointments are also being scheduled.
