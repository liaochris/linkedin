Jacob MAYNE’s Summary

Thank you for your interest in VERNISSAGE Ltd. We are based in London and Paris and are associated with a global network of experienced change management professionals. Our goal is to help leaders run successful change projects by engaging their people more effectively in the organisation. We do this by working alongside those in charge to highlight the realities of their organisation and their human resources.This adds value through richer internal and external cooperation, better alignment of effort with strategy and customer needs, a stronger focus on common interests and shared objectives.
Our clients are senior managers, board directors or trustees in the private and public sectors. We have worked for government ministries, national museum organisations, national airlines, multinational industrials, arts councils, business schools and local government in Europe, Asia and the US.
Before creating VERNISSAGE, I worked as CEO of a national arts organisation in Australia, and previously spent 20 years in management positions in HR, commercial, innovation and marketing with Unilever plc. Experience outside Europe includes 8 years in Japan and 5 in Australia.
I'm a programme director for HEC Executive Education in Paris and teach change management on HEC's MBA and tailored programmes. I have professional certification in group and individual coaching from the Centre International du Coach in Grenoble, and am affiliated to the International Coaching Federation.
I'm also a founding member of the Change Leaders, a global community of practice in the human side of change. Please visit our website at www.thechangeleaders.com.

Jacob MAYNE’s Specialties:

We support leaders engaged in strategy development and sense making in organisations, accompany organisational change and innovation projects, build and coach effective project teams, and help create conditions for successful partnerships and alliances, especially in international and intercultural contexts.
