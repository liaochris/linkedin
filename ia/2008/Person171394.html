Scott Spinola’s Summary

I am an avid writer, both professionally and personally. As a Technical Writer, I excel at simplifying complex procedures and technical information for a non-technical audience. Personally, I began taking an interest in creative writing about a year ago and have written several pieces of short fiction, which are available on my website at www.scottspinola.com.

A detail-oriented professional, I excel at finding creative solutions to difficult problems. I learn new skills quickly and my flexibility allows me to step outside my comfort zone. A solid work ethic and strong interpersonal and communications skills on the phone, in writing, and in person make me an ideal candidate for positions where teamwork is essential.

I will automatically see the big picture and can be a big help with strategic planning and identifying possibilities. I have a creative imagination and am gifted at using words to express new ideas and concepts. I can be called on when a fresh, new way to communicate important information is needed. I am most comfortable dealing with ideas and think conceptually, strategically and logically when making decisions.

I naturally use facts and data to solve problems and make decisions, and like to be assigned tasks that call for making judgments based on the careful gathering and examination of facts. I naturally generate lots of new ideas and ways of doing things and I like to be included in brainstorming sessions, or whenever new and different solutions must be found.
