Steve Dimmick’s Summary

I have 8 years experience dealing with the whole gamut of recruitment processes.

Personally, I tend to recruit for staff at senior manager to board level, although if there are needs for more Junior staff these are passed on to my team. We recruit for permanent, contract and part-time / consultative staff.

I am now aiming to further strengthen Dome New Media's client portfolio with a few carefully chosen new clients, that can get the best from us, whilst simultaneously ensuring that both my own and my teams jobs remain as rewarding as possible.

Steve Dimmick’s Experience

  • Director

    givefree.co.uk

    (Fund-Raising industry)

    November 2007 — Present (11 months)

    Why use givefree? Like other comparison sites we create revenue from introduction fees payable by the product providers when you buy a loan, credit card, current account, etc, through the site, when you take a product through givefree we’ll give at least a third of our fee to your chosen charity.

    How does that help a charity? A £10,000 car loan will generate about £50 to a charity of your choice. If just a hundred people do the same then we raise enough to dig a well that will provide water for a town or village somewhere in the Third World, or buy a bed for a terminally ill child in one of the UK’s many hospices.

    To find out more and donate to a charity of your choice visit www.givefree.co.uk
