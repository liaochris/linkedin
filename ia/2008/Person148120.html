Paul Pitsironis, CMT’s Summary

Began my career in 1996 with Morgan Stanley. Promoted to Retirement Planning Specialist in 1998. Promoted to Vice President in January of 2000 and subsequently promoted to Senior Vice President in January 2001. Joined Janney Montgomery Scott, LLC in April 2001 as a First Vice President / Investments. Completed the Chartered Market Technicicans Program and have received the CMT designation.
