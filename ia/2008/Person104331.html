Keith Stagner’s Summary

Implementation partner with 23 years systems integration and architecture design expertise, 15 in the telecoms field. Often support clients as interim CIO.

T-Impact recently acquired process automation technology based on Business Process Execution Language (BPEL) protocol. We offer fixed price service orchestration solutions with ROI in 12 months. Our solutions also help IT departments justify and acheive reusable services in a Service Oriented Architecture (SOA). Our apporach combines the best aspects of lean sigma, agile delivery and BPEL process automation to quick results and significant business impact.

Specialise in delivering telecommunications solutions for fixed, mobile, service provider, mobile internet and 3rd party application markets. I have established and built two integration practicies with no external investment, growing business of 25 Mio gross revenue per annum and a total of 170 concurrent employees.

My customers have included France Telecom/Orange Business Services (Equant), Car Phone Warehouse (UK), Telefonica/O2 (UK, NL, Germany), T-Mobil (Germany), AOL (UK), AT&T (UK), Comtel (USA), Deutsche Telekom (Germany) Energis (UK, NL, Poland), Belgacom, Tele2 (UK), Swisscom, Telia (Sweden), Cerillion (UK), MTN (S Africa), MTS (Russia), Theron Consulting, Norweb comms (UK) and MobiNil (Egypt), Cable & Wireless (UK), Hutchinson 3 (UK).

Managed service offerings include cost normalisation, systems integration and specialist staffing - bridging the gap between business and technology. Often retained by clients as Interim VP, Director or CIO.

Keith Stagner’s Specialties:

Process automation: BPEL, SOA, enAct, Activevos

Telco products: Local Loop Unbundling, Broadband, MPF, Wholesale Line rental, Carrier Preselect, Least Cost Routing, Interconnect, Prepay/Postpay convergence, MVNO, MVNe, Mobile telephony, SMS, Voip.

Architecture and integration of telco IT business support and portal systems. Specialising in: Amdocs, Intec Singleview, CSG/Kenan/Arbor, AMS, Geneva, Siebel, Chordiant, PeopleSoft, Vantive, Clarify, Convergys, Cerillion, Comptel, Openet.
