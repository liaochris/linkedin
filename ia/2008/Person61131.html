Elaine Sharp’s Summary

British Producer / Director / Camera operator / Writer with over 15 years TV production experience based in the UK, USA and Italy. Specialisation in international co-productions and overseas location filming on character-driven observational documentaries, history, travel, celebrities, biography, health and medical programmes.

I have also produced and directed programmes using dramatic-reconstructions, actuality, presenter-led, interview and archival-based programmes with graphics. Additionally, I have produced popular talk-shows and magazine style programmes with unusual contributors and celebrities.

Currently gaining a greater expertise with post-production techniques using graphics, archive, music and effects as a producer for sports promos.
