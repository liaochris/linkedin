Emily Sweeney’s Summary

I'm a lifelong Bostonian who's very passionate about journalism.

I have experience in TV and radio, and my work has appeared in the Boston Herald, Editor & Publisher, Yankee Magazine, Quill Magazine, and other publications. I've been blogging since 2001, and launched my first videoblog in 2005.

For the past seven years I've been a staff reporter for The Boston Globe. I cover local news and write stories for the paper, contribute to the Globe's nightlife blog, and produce multimedia content for the web.

I also serve as president of the New England chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists.

To learn more about what I do, check out my website: www.spikeyem.com

journalism, writing, shooting and editing video, blogging, podcasting, multimedia, social media, event organizing, public speaking, web design and development
