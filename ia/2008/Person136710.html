Michael Teitelbaum’s Summary

After 16 years as the owner of a successful direct mail marketing company, I sold the business because of my growing fascination with the Internet. In 1995 I co-founded e.magination, one of the pioneers in the web development industry. In 1998 I sold my interests in the company and co-founded Mom.com. As the VP of Sales and Marketing, I quickly learned about the Internet as a marketing vehicle, selling online advertising to a variety of companies including Disney, MasterCard, Mattel and WebMD.

Another business sale and it was off to Eisner Communications where I started the interactive division for what was once the largest ad agency in the Mid-Atlantic area. I served as President of Eisner Interactive for 5 years and had the good fortune of providing Internet marketing solutions to dozens of clients including AMF Bowling, Johns Hopkins Medicine, Maryland State Lottery, Medifast, US Airways and Washington DC Tourism.

In January of 2006 I was recruited to become President of TruePresence. In 2 1/2 years, I led the company in opening 25 offices around the country in the development of a national Internet marketing company.

During the summer of 2008, South Central Radio Group engaged my consulting services to help them expand the digital marketing side of their business. This led to a full-time engagement as their Chief Digital Officer. In this leadership role I am helping the company evolve to become a premier provider of multimedia content while delivering multi-platform marketing solutions that help businesses achieve their goals and objectives.

Michael Teitelbaum’s Specialties:

Digital marketing strategy, search engine marketing, web advertising, affiliate marketing, email marketing, web development and social media.
