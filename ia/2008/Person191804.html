Tony Suzda, CDR’s Summary

I started my career with a focus in information technology and I spent a lot of time engaged in new and evolving technology. Though I enjoyed this challenging path, I did not gain the personal satisfaction that I was seeking. On a whim, I made the career change to recruiting in late 2006 and found a great deal of success. I now have both a Talent Acquisitions and Information Technology background. These days I find very much personal satisfaction in my career choice.
