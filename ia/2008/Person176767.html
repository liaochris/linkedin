Jean-Yves Sireau’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur, founder and CEO of the Fortitude hedge funds, based in Hong Kong from 1993-1997. The Fortitude funds raised over US$ 20 million and specialised in futures and options trading using computerised algorithms.

In 1999 I founded BetOnMarkets (www.BetOnMarkets.com), now a leading fixed-odds financial betting website. In 2008, BetOnMarkets turns over US$ 130 million/annum, has 50 staff and offices in 3 countries.

In 2007 I founded Genseq Ltd., an online healthcare and corporate wellness solutions provider, offering the websites www.HealthierCompany.com and www.ReportMyHealth.com. Genseq Ltd. provides state of the art health risk assessments to corporations and individuals.

My companies operate service centers in Cyberjaya, Malaysia, where we also operate the www.Cyberjaya-Jobs.com website.

Jean-Yves Sireau’s Specialties:

My expertise lies in entrepreneurship, software development, financial derivatives, and health risk assessment.
