Rita Irani’s Summary

Intellectual Property attorney with broad, in depth knowledge of patent, trademark and copyright law with 25 years' experience in private practice with intellectual property attorneys, corporate attorneys and most recently trial lawyers. Experience includes representing small, medium and large corporate clients in the Patent Office, Trademark Office and Coypright Office and in federal courts. I am interested in short term or long term projects for attorneys or companies that can use my extensive experience and skills. Projects could include associate/young lawyer training, contract and license drafting and negotiation, patent, trademark and copyright applications, prosecution, registrations, litigation discovery projects, e-discovery management, motion practice, and briefing.

Rita Irani’s Specialties:

Internet issues in patent, trademark and copyright law, including domain name dispute resolutions and IP protection and enforcement. Legal research and writing and hearing and trial presentations.
