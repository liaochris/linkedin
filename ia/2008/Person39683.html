Connie Talbert’s Summary

I'm a Marketing/Sales-Service/Product Management professional. I've worn many hats throughout my long career in industry and sales/service. I have a strong electronics/service/training/sales/mechanical background, and with my teaching and sales experience am excellent at establishing good rapport/credibility with groups & individuals. I am a bit of a computer-geek and proficient in many software applications both in engineering and publishing genres. I have an entrepreneurial flare - I have persued multi-level marketing endeavors and also my own hobby-business in avian behavior that continues today. I love the outdoors, fixing things, and taking care of animals. For me, nothing beats the look of bewilderment and happiness on the face of the eldery lady down the street when suddenly her pretty old lamp works again, or the song (or squawk) of a companion bird who is now happy in its home.

CAREER ACCOMPLISHMENTS
Managed and marketed a global software solution for the HVAC industry produced in the UK. Worked with UK & US personnel to develop, promote, and position the product for entry into the US market.

Developed marketing strategies/tools with the help of McKinsey Corp. to analyze and increase profitability - later used as the benchmark for many business units of Invensys.

Maintained computer/phone/office (IT) systems for a large retail business, while simultaneously holding a primary sales position.

Developed and implemented a documentation management strategy for a business in the roll changing automation industry. Redesigned technical manuals, operational procedures & systems.

Managed an engineering project for the blind – modification of an electronic stand-up dart game for public or private use. Teamed with engineers from the National Foundation of the Blind to design modifications allowing blind persons to play with both sighted and sight-impaired people.

Developed and taught a complete curriculum for High School Voc-Ed electronics students.
