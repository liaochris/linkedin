William Thurmond’s Summary

William Thurmond is the President and founder of Emerging Markets Online, a global energy and biofuels intelligence boutique consulting firm started ten years ago in 1997. Mr. Thurmond is also the author of Biodiesel 2020: A Global Market Survey, and the forthcoming study Biodiesel 2020: The Next Generation.

Mr. Thurmond brings more than seventeen years experience in international, single-client management consulting services and multi-client market research services to this position. Mr. Thurmond has participated in the management and expansion of start-up firms focused on global biofuels, oil, gas, electric power utilities, telecommunications and trade business development.

Under Mr. Thurmond’s direction, Emerging Markets Online has received Forbes magazine's distinguished "Best of The Web" award seven years in a row 1999-2007, alongside peers FinancialTimes.com and Standard & Poors. William Thurmond speaks regularly at industry conferences, and has contributed original articles, data and interviews cited in the Wall Street Journal, The Financial Times, Business 2.0, Auto Focus Asia, The Futurist, Biofuels International, Swiss Derivates Review, Biodiesel Producer Magazine, Renewable Energy Access; has been featured in television interviews with Reuters TV, CNN and BBC News, including interviews with Al Gore; and was recently titled "Mr Biodiesel" by Fox News Radio's "Energy Week"

Mr. Thurmond is a member of The International Association for Energy Economics, the Association of Professional Futurists, the Houston World Affairs Council, and the Society for Professional Competitive Intelligence. Mr. Thurmond holds an M.S., Studies of The Future, from The University of Houston; a B.A, International Relations and Economics from Rollins College, and in 2006 started a PhD in Alternative Futures, at The University of Hawaii at Manoa.

market trends analysis, forecasting, strategic foresight, market intelligence, international trade negotation and facilitation, due diligence, feasilibility assessments, project finance support, competitive intelligence, regulatory impact analysis, scenario planning, joint venture facilitation, speaking and lectures on global energy markets, media publishing and production in print media, electronic media, video and audio streaming and podcasting, television, radio, and next-gen 2.0 media.
