Paul M. Heller, Esq.’s Summary

A SF Bay Area Institution for over 25 years, Heller Immigration Law Group, LLP, aka “HILG”, www.hilglaw.com, is one of the premiere employment-based immigration law firms in Silicon Valley (Palo Alto), CA. We obtain status for non-immigrant and immigrant workers incl: investor visas, H-1b, NIW and EB1 'extraordinary aliens', PERM 'fast-track' labor certification, adj. of status, and the myriad of other types of visas. Clients include: multinationals - to large and small corporations, to Silicon Valley startups, and outstanding and pre-eminent scientists, post-doc and other researchers, and academics, many of whom often wish to self-petition their employment-based cases. We also handle marriage, VAWA, and fiancé/e cases, and I-751 petitions (w/ or w/o waiver issues). HILG provides comprehensive and individualized attention to each and every matter. We are the "service oriented" choice.

Employment-based Immigration Law & PERM labor certification (1981-present). Green cards and working permits for non-immigrants.
