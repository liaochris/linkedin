Barbara Rogers’s Summary

My graphic pursuits are such that I look to continue work with other like minded individuals who have a passion for creative thinking, expressive design and an appreciation for artistic contribution by the integration and utilization of both
traditional and emerging technologies.

The ability for creative conception along with a design strategy through execution enables me to help companies & individuals alike reach their visual objective by putting words with the right images to ultimately communicate a clear engaging message utilizing a diversity of media. With my creative roots firmly in place and a background steeped in design, I look for opportunities for technological growth and evolution but always recognizing the need for a balance of form and functionality.

With an extensive background in print media and collateral materials, I consider myself far more of a right brain creative - although my left brain has its moments of greatness. It is one of my true desires for the opportunity to expand on these abilities and become more proficient in the execution of creative technology such as interactivity and online marketing to visually appeal to the senses and engage the end user by being better versed in the necessary applications.

Barbara Rogers’s Specialties:

Graphic design, print media including ad design, collateral and marketing materials, online marketing via SEO and banner ads, direct client contact, preflight, press checks
