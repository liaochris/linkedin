Gail Kent, ABC’s Summary

Gail Kent leverages her 30+ years of progressive experience in nearly every aspect of communications, marketing and public relations for her clients at The Buzz Factory.

She recently launched a FREE service to connect Hampton Roads reporters with businesses who want publicity at www.HRMediaConnectionz.com.

Gail's boutique public relations and marketing firm specializes in non-profits and small business enterprises, helping organizations manage their reputations, gain visibility and reach their marketing goals. Through its networking partners, The Buzz Factory provides a full range of services through its strategic alliances with networking partners. Services include communications planning, project management, publicity, branding, advertising, direct mail marketing, copywriting, editing, graphics, development of print materials, web site development, article writing for internet marketing, photography, media training, event planning, PR staffing, and more.

Gail has earned her accredited business communicator designation (ABC) from the International Association of Business Communicators and has won more than 250 local, state and national awards for her communications work.

Gail worked as an award-winning journalist with weekly and daily newspapers for five years and spent the past 25 years in marketing and PR, primarily in healthcare and higher education.

Recent or current clients include Sentara Healthcare; Sentara Health Foundation; Newport News Schools; Aerosolv, an environmental product solution for aerosol cans, owned by Katec, Inc.; Virginia Electronic Commerce Technology Center (VECTEC); Norfolk Collegiate School; Waste Reduction Consultants, Inc., in Atlantic Beach, Fla.; Bon Secours Health Center; MD News in Virginia Beach; Joseph W. Luter, III, School of Business at Christopher Newport University; and The Chesapeake Retirement Community in Newport News.
