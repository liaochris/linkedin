Steve Fawthrop’s Summary

20 years in the publishing and media sales field with newspaper and magazines. Sales at the local, regional, national and international level.

Started my career in Seattle at the Puget Sound Business Journal, a weekly business publication, and later returned as the Director of Sales and Marketing.

I have also sold and managed locally in Los Angeles and Orange County.

Sales includes eight years at USA Today selling at the national and international level out of the the Los Angeles office.

I have been Area Manager/West for Retailing Today and Drug Store News magazines since October 2005.

I am based out of the Los Angeles office and live in Orange County.
