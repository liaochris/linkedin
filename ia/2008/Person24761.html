Bob Christian’s Summary

Aerospace engineer, currently working as a Six Sigma Master Black Belt at Motorola. 24 years of experience performing reliability, safety and cost analyses/forecasts associated with Military Jet, Space and commercial telecom products. People oriented with over 17 years supervisory/management experience including training, hiring, layoffs, restructuring, performance appraisals and personnel issues (largest group over 50 individuals). Experienced with budget control; manpower planning; process development/improvement; project management; problem solving; proposals; and customer relationships. Background in hardware and software configuration management; airflow and heat transfer calculations; ISO 9000; cost benefit analysis; problem reporting and corrective action systems; reliability; safety, statistical analyses and six sigma tools. Outstanding analytical, mathematical & problem solving abilities. Some programming experience in several programming languages including: Visual Basic, C Shell, various older scripting languages and FORTRAN. Has in depth knowledge of MS Office Applications, as well as the Windows, Unix, TSO, VMS and IMS operating systems. Familiar with database system design including Oracle, VSAM, and Access.
