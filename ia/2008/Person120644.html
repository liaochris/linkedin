Jessica Margolin’s Summary

I think about and work on problems concerning the design (including measurement of outcomes) of organizations, institutions, and communities. Fundamental to these measurements include issues of corporate social responsibility, intangible asset valuation, and other areas of financial and economic reform.

My background is in financial analysis, business analysis, organizational design, and marketing strategy and communications. My educational background includes an MS in Materials Science in the area of nanotechnology and an MBA. I have worked primarily in the internet and financial industries, and also have done philanthropic work in the educational, music, and environmental domains.
