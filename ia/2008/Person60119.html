Eric Snowden’s Summary

Serving as the Creative Director and Director of Product Development for the Digital Media and eCommerce group at Atlantic Records I have two very distinct, yet complimentary roles. I created and currently manage a team of designers, production artists, and interns. I oversee design and Flash development internally and with our vendors to ensure a cutting edge yet cohesive visual brand identity for a leading music company. I am also responsible for creating new products and initiatives within the label, and for developing new prototypes and managing their progress to market. I manage relationships with design firms and emerging new media companies, and am responsible for forging new relationships as well. I also remain hands-on, personally designing and building websites, online advertising, widgets, gadgets, mobile and online applications for Atlantic Records and its artists. I am also responsible for the Creative Direction, photo editing, some photography, and Flash Programming on the newly designed AtlanticRecords.com.

Previously I have worked for Warner Music Group, CDMi Connect, Lynn Goldsmith LTD., Anderson Ranch Arts Center, and Thomas-Bradley Illustration and Design.

My clients have included Atlantic Records, Warner Music Group, Rhino Records, Pfizer, Sports Illustrated, Nikon, Olympus, Eureka, John Deere, Disney, Nascar, Kia, and Adobe.

Eric Snowden’s Specialties:

Expert knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, Actionscript 1.0 and 2.0, Flash Remoting, Flash Lite, Dreamweaver, QuarkXPress, XHTML and CSS.

Knowledgeable in After Effects, Indesign, XHTML, CSS, Coldfusion, SQL and JavaScript.

Additional experience in DVD Studio Pro, Final Cut Pro, Cleaner, Swift 3D and Soundedit.
