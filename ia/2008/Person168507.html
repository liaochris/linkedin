Sandie Law’s Summary

All parents want their children to grow up healthy and happy. Keeping up with nutritional findings, preparing healthy foods for your family, and getting your kids to eat them can be a full time job in and of itself. We teach parents to simplify the process of buying, preparing, and eating food with their family’s health and tastes in mind.

We know that kids can be picky eaters. We know that more and more kids are overweight these days. We know that you don’t have time to prepare a six course meal from scratch. We’re here to help you work through those obstacles.

I am a mom too. I work full time as does my husband. We don’t have time to prepare delicious home cooked masterpieces like our mothers did. But, like you, we want our kid to grow up healthy and happy.

I tested a ton of different things on the family. Some things were hits (macaroni and peas, for example) and others were not. I realized that I could make real food that satisfied my hungry husband and growing son and I could do it very quickly. I’m a big fan of the 20 minute dinner and the 5 minute breakfast and lunch.

In talking to other parents, I found that many of them struggled with what to feed their growing families. Many parents said they didn’t have time to cook or know how to cook. I want to share what I’ve learned with other parents. There is no reason feeding your family should complicated or time consuming.
