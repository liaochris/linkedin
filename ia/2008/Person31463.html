Lisa Wetzel’s Summary

Jim and I have worked as a team selling real estate in Carson Valley and the surrounding areas for over 25 years. Since 2002 we’ve associated with RE/MAX Realty Affiliates in Gardnerville. We find the RE/MAX attitude and atmosphere suite our service philosophy.

Our business has been built on a foundation of providing the best results and service possible for our clients and customers. We work hard to create and maintain long time, long term clients who appreciate and respect our dedication to their ownership and investment priorities. We are committed to private property rights and the realization of home ownership for everyone who shares that dream.
