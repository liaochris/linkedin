Chris O'Hara’s Summary

Mr. O'Hara is Senior Vice President of Reviewed.com, a growing network of consumer review websites that aims to revolutionize how people understand, shop for, and use the latest technology and consumer electronics.

Prior to Reviewed.com, he was the Vice President of Sales for Mediabistro.com, the leading employment services provider in the Media space, where he was responsible for selling online advertising and sponsorships, business development, and developing custom interactive solutions. He successfully positioned the company's online advertising sales to assist in its eventual acquisition by Jupiter Media.

Prior to his role at Mediabistro, Chris was Associate Publisher of Nielsen's photo group, where he managed print, online media sales, and face-to-face media sales and ran a stock photography business. During his tenure, he managed sales, custom media development, and business development for Photo District News magazine, Photoserve.com, IPNStock.com, and the Photosource series of photography sites and print directories. There, he successfully transitioned the company's custom media platform and created online revenue via partnerships with companies including Apple, Canon, Olympus, and internal Nielsen clientele.

He is an expert on transitioning traditional media companies into profitable new media enterprises, and frequently speaks at industry functions. He is a member of American Business Media's speakers bureau, and a contributor to Ad Club symposiums. He is also the author of 6 best-selling books. His most recent book, Great American Beer, was published in 2006 by Random House. He continues to contribute regularly to specialty magazines--and blog on topics he enjoys, such as interactive media, custom media, and online advertising sales.
