Todd Bernstein’s Summary

I have spent the last 13 years in pre-hospital care including the last 6 years as a paramedic in a 911 system. Over the last 3 years, I have been making a transition into sales, business management, and public speaking. I have used my time in the front of a class room and as a preceptor to develope leaders in paramedicine and now team building. I am currently working to educate small business owners in building trust and rapport. I use my training and years of emergency experience to teach people how to walk into any situation in which they may have less than thirty seconds to build trust and rapport.
