Miguel Paraz’s Summary

I am currently working on building solutions for telecommunications and fixed-mobile convergence. I work with foreign principals who offshore to the Philippines and travel for onshore work.

I've been using and programming for Linux since 1994.

I helped start one of the first Internet Service Provider's in the Philippines, IPhil Communications. This started operating in 1994. The partners and I sold this to PSINet, a publicly-listed US ISP, in 2000.

In the ISP industry, I've been working on IP routing, security, operating systems, and Internet-related business solutions software.

I've moved on to the software industry. I am interested in Java software design and implementation, computer science and algorithms, and open source tools.
