Alison Park Douglas’s Summary

Agent Alison is a Seattle-based company offering specialized consultations for professional photographers and other creative artists, as well as art buying / art production / photo editing services for advertising and editorial clients.

Alison Park-Douglas, Harvard graduate and founder of the firm, started her career as an advertising agency Art Buyer in Boston. Expanding her resume, as well as her network of contacts in the advertising and photography markets, she later worked as a Creative Consultant specializing in marketing and business consultations for photographers, a Sales Executive for a major stock photography agency (The ImageBank), a Studio Manager for a successful Boston photographer, and an Artist Representative with an established artist representation agency in San Francisco.

Alison has written articles for various publications, including PDN's magazine and website, and the ASMP National Bulletin. Her unique experience on both the buying and selling sides of the advertising business is an invaluable asset for her clients.

Alison is also currently available for Costume Design or Stylist opportunities.

Alison Park Douglas’s Specialties:

photo shoot production, art buying/production for ad agencies, photo editing, copyright for artists, marketing for artists, portfolio reviews, clothing/fashion styling, online marketing
