Joe Thorne’s Summary

Joseph Thorne is a Senior Consultant with Capgemini’s Financial Services Business Unit aligned with the Strategy & Transformation Consulting practice. Most recently he worked as the Lead Business Analyst on an Internet Business Strategy project within the Insurance industry where he was responsible for identifying and documenting business and technical requirements. He collaborated with the client to understand the desired functionality of the system and incorporate their feedback into the system’s design. The most significant role he had during this engagement was the opportunity to lead the Change Request initiative. One of his many responsibilities as lead was to utilize his understanding of both the Project's and Capgemini's organizational structure to identify the teams impacted by changes in the Statement of Work (i.e. architecture, design, construction, services, etc.). To accomplish this, he facilitated weekly meetings between the client and representatives of each impacted team. Following these meetings, he was responsible for coordinating with the team leads to obtain hours impact for each of the changes and preparing the changes for presentation to the steering committee. He was able to effectively manage this process which led to a significant amount of add-on work from the client.

Prior to this engagement he worked with the Organizational Change Management team coordinating Communications for an SAP implementation within the Life Sciences industry. While in this role he created messaging centered on the implementation occurring and the launch of a training campaign which were delivered through e-mails from executive leadership, websites, and newsletter articles distributed to over 6,000 employees.
