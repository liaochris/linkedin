Andrew Badera’s Summary

I have spent the last decade providing insight and guidance to a number of major corporations and smaller businesses alike. I've worked on data-driven enterprise projects for IBM, Xerox, Global Crossing and Davis Vision. I spent over three years as the webmaster and technical lead for one of Gannett's larger newspaper websites.

I am active in my local development community, as well as the global technology community. I participate in user's groups and well-known technology and news forums, as part of my efforts to keep abreast of the fast-moving Information Technology industry.

My expertise and experience are available on a consulting basis.
