Juan Pablo Milessi Sancoff’s Summary

My main field of expertise during these last 15 years has been English for communication. I have taught the language to different age groups by means of all sorts of methods and technologies. I have studied and taught English for specific purposes, basically for Economics and Medicine. I have also worked as translator and interpreter for several individuals and companies.

However, technology has changed the world dramatically and new career opportunities have spawned off the internet. My interests switched partially towards the internet and I began writing html code, designing and publishing online

These and many other activities took me where I am now, exploring and reinventing my communications skills.

I am currently working for Global Crew Logistics, a division of Corporate Travel Consultants based in Miami Beach FL. I am now learning about the airline business and training with the Sabre Reservations System.
