Jim Newton’s Summary

We have been building and enhancing HRA (Staff Essential) for over 16 years now and have installations across the US, Canada, the Caribbean and around the world including Hawaii, Africa and even the Middle East.

If you like, we can immediately arrange an over-the-web presentation.

Our HRA Professional and HRA Global are competitively priced, fully featured, and represent a complete HRIS solution for your HR management challenges. We also have other versions including a web-enabled version the MS-SQL based HRA SQL, a hosted version called HRA ASP and a Lite Version called HRA Personal Assistant for smaller companies.

HRA is an excellent HRIS and I have listed below some of the competitive advantages I feel VP Software and HRA will bring to you.

• We are an established HRIS vendor and we have been designing and supporting our HRIS systems since 1991. This gives our software a very mature feel as we have handled the requests and suggestions from clients and prospects for all those years.
• Service and Support to our clients is always our first priority. We have an excellent track record for providing first class service and always respond promptly to our clients needs.
• We can offer a totally integrated payroll solution or we will work with any Payroll vendor of your choice to integrate with HRA. If you want to change payroll vendors down the road, we can simply switch the interface to that payroll software.
• Our Report Writing capability is an area we continually receive accolades for... and have been told countless times how much more advanced and simple our Report Writer is to use compared to all major North American HR solutions.
• Finally, we offer very competitive pricing.
