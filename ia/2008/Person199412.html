Wendy te Velde’s Summary

There are times when a company desperately needs support in the area of communications. Additional practical and creative capacity, at the right moment and in the right place.

My name is Wendy te Velde and I have more than 13 years of communication experience in retail, advertising and business services, IT, government and non-profit. I offer temporary communications support on a project basis, which can be immediately applied as focused activities and/or resources aimed at meeting specific needs a company may have. I have a 'hands-on' attitude and can be employed in a flexible way. I am easily capable of handling day-to-day issues and also able to ensure a proper transfer afterwards.

Wendy te Velde’s Specialties:

Developing and implementing communication plans; drawing up and supervising communication budgets; purchasing of various media, resources and services; developing a broad range of marketing communication resources (from concept to final product); providing guidance to media agencies, publishers, advertising agencies, printers and other suppliers.
