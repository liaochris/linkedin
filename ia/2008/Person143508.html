Off Madison Ave’s Summary

Our goal is simple. We help our clients become more successful. How do we do it?

Off Madison Ave is an integrated strategic communications agency, located in Tempe, Arizona, that provides services in advertising, public relations, media, graphic design, strategy and Internet marketing. The driving force behind every piece of our work is measurability. We design, build and sustain programs that provide our clients with results they can see and measure.

We've put an immense amount of effort into building an atypical agency - we recognize that our most valuable resource is our people. We draw on the national and international experience of agency's talent and because of that we are able to offer our clients the highest caliber of creative and strategic expertise.

David Anderson and Roger Hurni started the agency 1998 in Roger's garage. Two guys, a business plan and some VC funding - you can read the VC as Visa Cards. But that quickly changed with Off Madison Ave's first client, Integrated Information Systems. Six months later our first employee was hired. Which, David and Roger admit was even scarier than starting the company.

From two guys in a garage, the Off Madison Ave team has grown to a staff of 60. In the past two years alone, the agency has grown by more than 60%. We service clients in cities all over the United States and the United Kingdom. What has allowed us to grow is our ability to adapt to the ever-changing marketing environment, adding new services and communication models that exceed the expectations of our clients. One example of this is the addition of Mighty Interactive in 2005. It's no secret that online advertising is a critical component of today's marketing strategies. We were able to identify this trend before many other agencies and bring the number one Interactive firm in the state into the agency, in turn expanding our list of services.
