Tim Kennedy’s Summary

My professional background rests mainly as a Senior Level Sales Executive. An essential element to my career has been my ability to quickly adapt to new settings and acquire the fundamental knowledge to perform at an elite level. This is demonstrated via my diverse expertise in a variety of industries/sectors (retail, financial services, airport security/system software and telecommunications).

I fully embrace that Senior Level Sales involves developing an understanding of all stakeholders involved throughout the organization and their level of decision making. Understanding the product/service is one element, although establishing a quality relationship and knowing how to properly leverage the relationship is of the upmost importance. I am able to eliminate sales obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches.

Tim Kennedy’s Specialties:

I have proven results managing and executing a full sales cycle including prospecting/lead generation, opportunity management, pro forma analysis, board room presentation, contract negotiation and closing process.
