Adam Firth’s Summary

I have over 7000 hours of flying, most of this is on multi engined jet aircraft. I started as an Assistant Flying Instructor in Cannes, progressing to become the Chief flying Instructor & UK CAA Examiner. I then worked at FRA, learning a variety of different military tasks on jet aircraft. now I fly modern high performance executive jets at Netjets. We have over 130 jets in Europe & form the largest exec jet fleet in the World with Netjets America.

At Netjets I regularly meet people who are at the pinnacle of human acheivement in their various fields. - this & the healing work that I learn at the Brennen School of healing are a great inspiration to me.

My main business goal is to use the healing skills that I am aquiring at school to combine them with my significant aviation knowledge & experience. I believe that Netjets could benifit from this marriage of apparently disparate qualities in the creation of a healing network for Aircrew. This will help Netjets to retain more of their most valued assets, (Aircrew), without significant expenditure & render Aircrew more efficient & significantly improve morale though a profound improvement in the way we relate to each other at work.
