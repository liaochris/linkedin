David Nickelson’s Summary

Internet executive with 12+ years of strategic web site development, information technology infrastructure integration and operations experience for energetic, entrepreneurial for-profit companies and non-profit organizations. Psychologist with deep knowledge of the health and mental health care marketplaces from the consumer, provider, and payer perspectives. Expert on healthy and pathological online behavior. Lawyer with contract drafting, negotiation, and local, state and federal policy development experience. Interested in full-time positions and contractual/consulting engagements.

David Nickelson’s Specialties:

Internet strategy; health care marketplace strategy; business strategy, technology implementation and eCommerce operations; electronic product development; non-profit strategy, organizational strategy, executive management and change management; CMS selection, development and deployment; legislative and legal advocacy/lobbying (federal and state); strategic marketing and promotion. Contract drafting, negotiation, and policy development.
