Mayank J Kumar’s Summary

I have done Masters in Business Administration from University Business School, Panjab University Chandigarh. I have done majors in Marketing and Minors in Operations and Information Technology.

I was selected from Campus by Web Neuron Services Limited, a unit of Monster worldwide, where I joined as the Corporate Relationship Manager in their prestigious retention team. At Monster I got acquainted with corporate sales (Monster.com and JobsAhead.com) and got the opportunity to work with clients like Microsoft, Adobe, ICICI, ING Vysya, Manpower consultant, Team Lease consultants, Xansa Systems, IBM Daksh etc.

After Monster, I joined Net Solutions, a Chandigarh based Software and Web Development Company as Business Development Manager. Here I worked on Resellers Channel Management, Project Management, Branding, Market Research, Internet Marketing, Internet Advertising and Business Analysis. I also played a key role in developing Web based projects focused at selling company web capabilities as distinct packages.

I worked with Net Solution for over 2.5 years before joining authorGEN Technologies, where I am working as Marketing Manager. authorGEN technologies specializes in developing e-learning tool. At authorGEN I drive the offline and online marketing of web 2.0 based education portal – WiZiQ.com. My work profile includes getting the portal popular in student and teacher community, all over the world. I specialize in celebrity teacher endorsements have been in touch with prominent authors and educationalists all around the world.
