Richard East’s Summary

Co-founder and CEO of a Business Services company with focus on serving the SME market, providing a relatively wide breadth of Marketing, IT, SaaS, and Business Consulting solutions for this SME business segment.

We also provide Lead Generation and BPO services.

Prior to forming our new company, I spent 15 years in the trenches of direct marketing, testing and tracking every form of direct response advertising, and
analyzing every field within a database. Spending years consulting with top US Auto Dealers and Mortgage Companies, this gave some key insights into pure data management, and the development and understanding of funky words like "trip wire systems," "Halo's and Hurdles."

Data Mining has become somewhat of a passion and the core of one of the major software projects that we are currently engaged in. We plan on releasing this new, cutting edge data mining software during the first quarter of 2009.

In addition, our team is internet savvy, with years of hands-on SEO/SEM experience to bring to every client.

In 2002 my family and I moved to Romania to open our first office abroad. We lived there for 4+ years before opening our offices in India.

In 2006, we opened an office in Hyderabad, India, then a second office in Ahmedabad, India in 2007.

I now reside in Switzerland with my wife and 3 daughters.
