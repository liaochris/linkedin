Steve Bramhall’s Summary

Global business is highly competitive and as your scarcity power erodes
and products and services become commoditized, the ability to win and retain
business becomes more challenging. When customers only see your price as your differentiator action is required. Around 80% of executives see increasing cost pressures. How are you addressing the dynamics of business today?
We help ensure that your company wins. We put you in the groove and enable you to avoid the ruts, ditches and graves that come from inertia. We help you retain clients, build client loyalty and offer better value propositions to existing and new clients. We help you grow, we help you develop different products with higher margin potential.

SB Purchasing provides companies with consistent and fair market pricing,
controlled and efficient supply chains by providing a trusted supplier management
process and by leveraging multiple client spends. We run a consortium buying
hub which maximizes synergies of all clients spend.

Simply we help you maximise profit, give you a controlled and stable profit plan and opens up new product development opportunities, new market and new revenue opportunities so you differentiate and raise the barriers to your own competition.The world is our market and we build competition and develop new sub markets and suppliers to get you the best deals. We leave no stone covered in understanding your value needs and our search for excellence at low cost.

We bring you better visibility on the world of truth and we tell you the truth. We work to ensure when a supplier says yes, we are confident he can deliver yes and we manage him to deliver yes.

I am always interested in discussing new global sourcing/project management/trading opportunities with any interested parties. I am happy to pay an agreed percentage of my fee for any successful referrals.
