Mark Ellison’s Summary

As a participant in Standards Development Organizations Mr. Ellison edited specifications and primers for open standards relative to the remote management of network devices, systems, and services.

As Principal Consultant with Ellison Software Consulting, Mark provides consulting services on the modeling of management information and the architecture, design and implementation of standards-based remote management technologies.

Consulting topics include Information Modeling; SNMP applications; MIB design and implementation; applicability of IETF RFCs; Use cases for remote montioring and control; and device and service management strategies.

Full resume available at : http://www.scguild.com/Resume/1321R.html

• Design SNMP Applications for Network Systems and Services

• Analyse Applicability of IETF RFCs for Network Systems and Services
