Luigi Cappel’s Summary

Founding member and past President of the NZ Wireless Data Forum.
Involved in mobility applications for many years.
Wrote and Published eBook 'Unleashing the Road Warrior'
A Futurist and member of the World Future Society
Published and active songwriter, have performed in several countries including New Zealand, United States and Jamaica in 2007. Also presenter of the popular podcast A Kiwi Songwriter.
I am an experieced public speaker and have presented at and chaired conferences in France, England, South Africa, Singapore, Japan, Australia, USA, New Caledonia and New Zealand.

Luigi Cappel’s Specialties:

One of the original mobile data evangelists, specialising in PDA's and Smartphones. I am focussed on GIS and LBS. Includes the maps used on Vodafone Live, In Car Navigation including Navman and Siemens VDO, adding intelligent maps with routing to company websites such as Wises, AA Smartmap, Bayleys Real Estate and much more. If you are interested in anything to do with mapping for PDA's, Smartphones, in car navigation, web sites, proximity based marketing etc, lets talk.
