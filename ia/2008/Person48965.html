Debra Adams’s Summary

I am researching the ways that news media use the internet to engage citizens with public affairs. In particular I am looking at news blogs and citizen journalism. My cases of study are: news.com.au, blogs.bbc.co.uk and Ohmynews International.

I also tutor and guest lecture in media and communication units at QUT.

I have a BA (Journ), Monash and BJ (Hons), QUT.

Other academic experience includes: creative writing, screen studies, philosophy, sociology and literary studies.
