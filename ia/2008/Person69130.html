Gerald Lopez’s Summary

Over 30 years studying the mind-body connection. Providing a health consultancy based on ayurvedic medicine and yoga; including diagnosis, diet and lifestyle recommendation, herbal prescriptions, massage and other therapies.

Conducting retreats providing deep relaxation therapies and tools, in New Zealand and Tonga. Conducting panchakarma (ayurvedic detox and rejuvenation) programmes in retreat settings. Conducting workshops in natural lifestyle principles based on ayurveda and yoga.

Gerald Lopez’s Specialties:

Retreat and spa development. Stress management with yoga. Health and human potential workshops.
