Jennifer Hough’s Summary

Jennifer Hough is the founder of The Vital You, she is an intuitive, author and speaker. Her mission is to empower teachers of all types, leaders, parents and children to remember who they are…magnificent, unlimited creators. TVY & Jennifer continue be the leading edge destination for those whose aim is Gobal peace, joy, and connection. TVY’s programs and affiliations:
• Get Out of Your Own Way™ - for those Leaders, Parents and Teens, ready to end self sabotage, and work in harmony with their gifts instead of paddling upstream.
• The “Being vs. Doing” Coach – Jennifer’s 1:1 coaching is life changing. She brings you into an experience of living in the “miracle zone”, where limits and ego are non-existent. It’s about becoming not working hard.
• The Mission Possible Project – Children are Teachers. This is a movie and web based project to wake the world up. The aim is to expand peace, through stories of children who are doing what it takes to change their world. It will connect adults & children from around the world, to “be the change”.
• Jennifer’s books: Best Seller: Wake Up and Live the Life You Love, Best Seller: A Search for Purpose, SOUL, the Ultimate Holistic Cookbook….
• Jennifer is in the following movie’s:
o The Time is Now – Leaders of thought coming together to create a vision of a world where clean air, peace and health becomes a reality….
o What is Possible – An assembly of teachers from all over the world filmed to open us all to our own potential and limitless power to change, grow, and experience joy.
• Member of the Board of Advisors to HM2U – A “for profit” company that created a cure for & personal transformation program for people with AIDS. HM2U developed a self-sustaining way to distribute the products to 3rd World Countries at no charge.
• Co-Creator of Wellness of World Re-languaging Project – This not for profit group that teaches us how to use language to inspire action and create momentum toward Heaven on Earth.

Jennifer Hough’s Specialties:

• Parent, teacher and children’s coaching toward excavating genius, joy, and connection with ease (your own & your children’s). Bringing the Laws of Meta Physics into your family and schools’ worlds with fun and play.
• Corporate coaching in the area of “becoming” your mission statement, rather than “doing” it – creating an effortless communication model and strategic plan. Bringing the Laws of Meta Physics into your workplace.
• Becoming a leader from the inside out
