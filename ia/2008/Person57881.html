Elise Fleischaker’s Summary

I'm a strategic marketing communications professional with senior-level experience in the high-tech and financial services sectors. I currently head up marketing for Trading Technologies, a privately-held company that makes software for professional derivatives (e.g., futures) traders. We have 10+ offices throughout the US, Europe and Asia/Pacific. I'm based at our headquarters in Chicago but I execute globally and have traveled through Europe and Asia to manage international media tours, trade shows, customer events and other activities. While I've spent the bulk of my career on the "corporate side", I also have experience working in agency and non-profit environments. My employers have run the gamut from start-ups to public companies, which has enabled me to work with organizations that are in varying degrees of growth. To this end, I'm experienced in managing pre-/post-IPO programs as well as M&A communications.
