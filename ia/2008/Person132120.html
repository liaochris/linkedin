Mike Forrest’s Summary

I have Account Management experience servicing primarily the Pulp & Paper industry Since 1993 through employment with two roganizations. For 7 years I was employed with a manufacturer of roll packaging products and equipment and covered North America for their equipment division and 6 Midwest states and central Canada for their roll packaging products.

For the next 6 years I was a Manufacturer's Rep. (my own company) for process control instrumentation carrying 5 exclusive contracted lines. This was also strongly focused on the Pulp & Paper industry, but also provided me with opportunities in the Petro-Chemical industry and Hi-Tech industry as well several other segments including municipal.

Currently representing material handling equipment and services in the NY/NJ area with only a small amount of contact to the Paper industry. Primarily working with distribution, transportation and various manufacturing customers.

Mike Forrest’s Specialties:

Main areas of concentration have been focused around roll packaging products and equipment and also process control instrumentation. I have visited a majority of the mills in North America and worked on projects of some sort at many of them.
