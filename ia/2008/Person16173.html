Arno Hermans’s Summary

CAREER:
Arno joined Atos Origin after graduating from his study Information Management at the University of Tilburg. Since joining Atos Origin, Arno has participated in many projects as a quality assurance specialist in various fields, including projects in social security/insurance, pension funds, financial institutions and telecom.

EXPERIENCE:
Arno has a broad interest in testing and has gained a lot of of working experience by participating in different testing projects. Activities have included test preparation, execution and analyses. Also included test automation (Test tools), coordination, planning, training of end users, maintaining customer contacts and quality assurance.

SKILLS:
Arno is a proactive team player and adapts quickly to new situations and environments. Besides being a teamplayer, he is also capable to perform tasks and activities individually. He is able to prepare, execute, analyse and automate a test (with Testtools). Arno is in great favour of a structured way of working within a testing project. Because of his broad IT knowledge in general, and his special interest in the testing profession, Arno has grown into an all-round and experienced QA specialist.

PERSONAL PREFERENCE:
Arno has a broad interest in ICT, especially in the quality assurance area. He wants to improve his skills within the quality assurance area with educational trainings and ‘training on the job’. Arno has a special interest in Testtools and Testautomation within the quality assurance area. He wants to further improve and professionalise his work on Testtooling and Testautomation, to extend continuously his knowledge and skills on this area.
