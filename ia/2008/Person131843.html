Mike Stapleton’s Summary

My career has encompassed marketing and advertising, finance for start ups, configuring a public offering using the 10SB process, consulting for business owners needing a sounding board, what "not to do" in Real Estate, mergers and acquisitions of small companies, computer hardware and software configuration from an administration perspective, start up junky.

Important note: InCredia's work is not the be-all/end-all of the start up process... for all you business people who think everything has a finite outcome. We help take ideas to the next level, not the final level, but the next level.

Mike Stapleton’s Specialties:

I specialize in providing a positive image of an investment opportunity to investors on behalf of entrepreneurs raising private capital. I assist in positioning the company so that investors can quickly screen their information and give it a thumbs up, or thumbs down. We take the entrepreneur from "no structure" to a "realistic structure." Our work saves our clients thousands of dollars and hundreds of hours.
