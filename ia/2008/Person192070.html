Sherrin Bernstein’s Summary

Sherrin Bernstein has integrated elements of dance, sports, Tai Chi, yoga, Pilates, injury rehabilitation, movement re-education, personal training and massage therapy to create Touch Fitness, Restorative Bodywork.

In 1991 Sherrin received a B.A. in Dance from New York University, with a minor in the Comparison of Traditional and Non-Traditional Injury Rehabilitation techniques.

Starting in 1987, she integrated dance, alignment and injury rehabilitation exercises into fitness classes and private training sessions.

In 1999 she graduated from the Swedish Institute of Massage Therapy, and became licensed in NY and later FL. In 2001 Sherrin honed her medical and sports
massage skills while running Touch Fitness from a NYC rehabilitation and wellness center and is a member of the NYS Medical Massage Therapist’s Association. She added certifications in Pre-Natal Massage, Essential Oils and Thai Massage in 2003, and Heliotherapy, Hydrotherapy, wraps and scrubs in 2005 while studying at Educating Hands School of Massage in Miami.

Currently Sherrin runs her private practice Touch Fitness, Restorative Bodywork, providing real solutions for real people, high quality fitness and massage services and is also on call at spas in NYC, New York and South Beach, Miami and is working on a line of Essential Oil Products.
