Steve Rangel’s Summary

At Human Arrow we know that every customer counts. We also know that in order for any business to be successful, it must stand out from the crowd. That is why we created a cost effective marketing solution that drives traffic to your location real time.

We direct traffic to your business by creating friendly and professional interactions with pedestrians and motorists. Our associates are stationed at major intersections holding an arrow shaped sign that displays your ad or sales event.

Human Arrows offers an alternative form of advertising that draws attention to your business. We provide a unique proactive process through which a client can promote their business by directing customers to where that product or service can be found. Human Arrow proactively delivers its clients' promotional messages in a manner that result in immediate customer action.

We offer a variety of packages for every industry. Human Arrow packages start at $399. This fee includes strategic placement, a customized sign and two days of marketing.
