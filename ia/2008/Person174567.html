Shawn Leonard’s Summary

Shawn Leonard is Webmaster for the Society of Corporate Compliance and Ethics and the Health Care Compliance Association and is responsible for developing and managing SCCE and HCCA Web site communication systems. Shawn is a seasoned Systems Analyst and manager with entrepreneurial, compliance, analytical, business processes, performance and quality expertise.

Prior to joining the SCCE, Shawn was Senior Systems Analyst, Service Level Management with Accenture, Inc. for Best Buy Inc. In this capacity, Shawn redesigned and implemented reporting processes in India, Canada, and the U.S. He invented a patentable multidimensional algorithm for Service Level compliance which is now the “gold” standard implemented internationally in Canada, Puerto Rico, Mexico, and the U.S.

While serving as Website Manager for Best Buy Inc., Shawn was responsible for service support of Incident Management and monitoring for BestBuy.com website 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Shawn Leonard was awarded a A.A., Psychology and Science, University of Minnesota and an A.A.S, from Dunwoody Institute. He is currently working on a B.S., Management of Information Technology from Cardinal Stritch University.
