Carlos Fuentes’s Summary

Carlos Fuentes
SVP & Chief Information Officer, Mitsui Sumitomo Marine Management (U.S.A.), Inc.
•Board Member at Mitsui Sumitomo Marine Management (U.S.A.), Inc.
•Board Member at Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance USA, Inc.
•Board Member at Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Company of America

Carlos Fuentes serves as the SVP & CIO of Mitsui Sumitomo Marine Management (U.S.A.), Inc. As part of the Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Group of companies, a global top 20 insurer, MSMM manages the member insurance companies located in the United States. MSMM provides P&C insurance in all 50 states. Mr. Fuentes holds several leadership roles and is a member of the management company and writing companies boards. Since joining the MSIG family of companies, Mr. Fuentes has championed tactical and strategic initiatives that continue to shape and improve the firms’ business, operations and technology. Mr. Fuentes led MSMM in obtaining its first SAS70 certification.

Mr. Fuentes has over 18 years of IT management experience across a variety of industries, including Insurance, Finance, and Consulting.

At AIG Mr. Fuentes was a Senior Technology Officer in the Office of the Global CIO, leading the Enterprise Architecture Group, a team with global responsibility for governance, architecture reviews, standards, Identity Management, operations, technology selection, and infrastructure. At Fujitsu Consulting, he was a Practice Director and Chief Architect with a focus on IT planning and development. At Reuters he was a project manager responsible for key global client and product initiatives.
