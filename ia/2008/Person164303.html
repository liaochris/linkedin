Ron Cathers’s Summary

What I do?

>Reduce Costs (TCO) to impact bottom line results.
>Increase Availabilty of Critical Applicatrions and Systems

How do I do it?

I work with companies to secure, maintain and ensure their data and systems to 100% availability– production and disaster recovery environments. NetTelcos/CBB investment in infrastructure help companies to secure and maintain their data and systems uptime and reducing total cost of ownership contributing directly to companies bottom line.

> Managed Hosting and Colocation
> Managed Servers and VMware
> Managed WAN (MPLS, Ethernet) - Device, Circuit, Security
> Internet & Mobile Data (IPASS)

Have you answered these three questions?

> Are you concerned with outages related to your systems and loss of productivity/revenue associated with availability of those systems?
> Are you running out of space and resources for your systems or do you have staff limitations to handle everyday IT maintenance and management?
> Is information & network security a concern of the organization?

With technology advancing so quickly, there are several reasons why companies rely on partners to provide Hosting & Managed Services:

> Companies with highly skilled IT professionals can more effectively deploy those individuals by focusing less on IT support and more on mission-critical projects.
> Building and maintaining a robust infrastructure requires significant capital investment. Why not leverage our investment and 8 data to achieve a more secure, redundant, scalable and highly available systems environment.
> The risks and costs associated with system and application downtime are rising. The business-critical nature of today’s systems requires unmatched security and availability while demanding 24x7 monitoring, maintenance and technical support.

Ron Cathers’s Experience

  • Sr Account Executive

    NetTelcos, a CBB Company

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2008 — Present (5 months)

    In 2000, Landmark Communications’ New Ventures group created a new entity, Continental Broadband, to test the technical and business viability of providing wireless internet connectivity to local businesses in Hampton Roads, Virginia. Well, it worked, and out of such humble beginnings Continental Broadband has grown to become one of the largest providers of data center and managed data network services in the United States.

    Today, Continental Broadband operates a group of businesses working together to serve customers through 8 data centers in 7 major markets. We continue to make investments to improve the businesses we own, while focusing heavily on growing each of them organically through the efforts of teams of carefully selected and trained professional groups. Our teams can bring significant bottom line operating expense benefits to your business, while at the same time addressing your needs for secure, fast, and reliable solutions.
