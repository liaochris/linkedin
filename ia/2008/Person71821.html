Anne-Marie Fagerlund Krogh’s Summary

I am a Graphic designer and Art Director educated at The Graphic Arts Institute of Denmark (DGH) Prior to that, I have a four-year vocational education as a Media Graphics Designer.

In my working process, I have an analytical, creative and conceptually approach with graphic design and communication – and the ability to apply this approach when planning my projects. In a project, I analyze and valuate the connection between design and communication, target-group, quality and product – and I am able to plan the production and follow-up by valuating function, aesthetics as well as economical and technical possibilities.

During my studies the last three years, I have had several relevant student jobs. This has given me the opportunity to use in practise the things I have learned, and also enabled me to work directly with clients, deadlines and tight budgets.

One of my main forces is my ability to take on different expressions into my designs, depending on the message and feeling that needs to come across. I have great respect for the old design conventions, but even greater respect for those who are not afraid of breaking them.
