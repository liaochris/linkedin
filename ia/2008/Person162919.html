Rob Johnson’s Summary

My career has been focused in the high-tech arena with a speciality in the technical selling of networking equipment. With over 15 years of experience in this field, I have become an adaptive learner of new technology. My sales experience includes a broad range of company profiles, from major accounts through startups and I have a particular expertise in creating collaborative partnerships with major accounts.

I have been very successful in recruiting and motivating sales people, having directly managed sales staffs at several companies.

My background enables me to manage teams of contributors and perform effectively as a bag-carrying revenue producer.

I have a reputation for excellent organizational, time management, leadership, interpersonal, and problem solving skills. I am a highly motivated self-starter, with team player abilities. Some of my strengths/areas of expertise include; presentation skills, strong leadership ability, and relationship building skills at all levels of the organization including C-Level.
