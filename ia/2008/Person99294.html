Jason Stradley, CISSP, CISM, SANS GSLC, CBCP’s Summary

Jason Stradley was most recently responsible for the information security program at TransUnion, LLC and in that capacity was accountable for enterprise wide information security for the TransUnion organization. That responsibility included the setting of a holistic, comprehensive global strategy for the organizations information security and data protection programs and policies. Mr. Stradley was accountable for leading the efforts in the assessment, design/development, implementation, management and compliance for the information security of the entire organizations assets, including data, network, business systems, applications, and infrastructure. Prior to his position at TransUnion, Jason was the Information Security Manger for Corn Products International, Inc., where he was responsible for the development and operation of the information security program.
Jason has more 25 years of experience in providing solutions for complex enterprise environments in the following areas; Multi-platform, Multi-protocol network architecture, design and implementation, information protection and network security, as well as, business continuity and disaster recovery planning. Jason has provided these types of solutions to companies such as; Continental Insurance, ITT Sheraton, GE Capital Corporation, GE Transportation Systems, Digital Equipment Corporation, Johnson & Higgins Inc., Chase Manhattan Bank NA, United States Postal Service and Brown Brothers Harriman & Company to name a few. Jason has held management and leadership positions for such firms as, Deloitte & Touché’ LLP, Digital Equipment Corporation, Bull Information Systems NA, Chase Manhattan Bank NA. Jason has also been involved in several of his own companies specializing in network security, disaster recovery and business continuity consulting. Mr. Stradley currently holds the CISSP, CBCP, CISM, GSLC certifications as well as several technical certifications.
