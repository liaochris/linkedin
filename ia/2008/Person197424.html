Vivek Kumar Mishra’s Summary

M.Tech in Computer Science (ISI-Calcutta; 1999-2001) and B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (IIT-Delhi;1992-1996) by qualification and a seasoned professional with rich industrial experience in different domains and industries such as commissioning, Mechanical Maintenance and ISO-9000 certification of large steel plant; software development, bug fixing and troubleshooting planning related customer issues in Oracle ERP; setting up training academy with certification benchmarks and training plans(including e-learning courses) for multiple ERP packages, leading and troubleshooting planning track in multiple Oracle ERP implementations and incubating a new service offering, TMS(Transportation Management Services) in SCM domain.

Also have several firsts to his credit.

Currently working with Infosys and leading a new TMS Service offering from SCM practice including OTM(G-LOG), I2-TMS, SAP-TPVS, Red Prairie and Manhattan suite of products in transportation domain, led planning tracks in multiple Oracle ERP implementations. Among the first few involved in setting up Enterprise Solution Academy (ESA) at Infosys.

Worked with Oracle India Pvt Ltd. and was the first one to start HLS (High Level Scheduler; key component of Oracle Advance Supply Chain Planning (ASCP)) team in Oracle India, also worked on MBP (Memory Based Planner) another component of ASCP, led the first PQE team (Product Quality Enhancement; special group of a development team which looks into customer issues) for planning issues.

Worked with SAIL (Steel Authority of India Limited) and was involved in commissioning of Continuous Casting Machines in Steel Melting Shop-2 (SMS-2) of Bokaro Steel Plant((1996-1997) thereafter took the charge as first Shift Incharge for Mechanical Maintenance and prepared Standard Maintenance Practices (SMPs) and Work Instructions (WIs) for Mechanical Maintenance for ISO 9002 certification.
