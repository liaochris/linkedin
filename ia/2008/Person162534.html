Robert Stanke ’s Summary

I am a Senior Reruiter for Talent Software Services - a Professional Services/Consulting company in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I am part of a great team that is responsible for finding top talent in the Twin Cities to help our clients achieve success on important business and information technology initiatives. We are a preferred consulting company with clients like UnitedHealth Group, Carlson Companies, Medtronic, Thrivent Financial, and Northwest Airlines - just to name a few of the over 30 companies we work with.

I also am a Professional Career Coach, Professional Resume Writer, and Employment Interview Professional. I am currently finalizing my certifications in all three of these areas through PARW/CC.

Previous to working at Talent, my career started as a Database Programmer for a marketing services firm in Roseville, Minnesota - Montage, Inc. - where I also held positions of a Database Administration & Reporting Manager, and Project Manager.

My recruiting career started at SafeNet Consulting in Minnetonka, Minnesota where I sourced and recruited IT professionals, Project Managers, and Business Analysts.

I am interested in networking with a variety of individuals: recruiting colleagues, consultants, and full-time professionals, so please feel free to contact me at: robert@robertstanke.com.
