Ankush Chatterjee’s Summary

A seasoned professional with extensive experience in product marketing/management/technology development of wireless products in diverse business and technical roles spanning over 10 years.
I started my career as a software engineer and worked extensively in the area of embedded software development on handsets in the wireless protocol space (Bluetooth,Wifi, GSM etc).
Having built handsets, I decided to sell some and hence, I moved to a more techno-business role in technical/product marketing after completing my Masters in Industrial Management at Northwestern University (Kellogg/McCormick schools).My role in the handsets business involved being the Voice of the customer & helping Motorola in developing/strategizing roadmaps and features for cellular carriers across regions.
I also worked in a Program Management capacity for a short period and hold a Masters Certification in Project/Program Managment from GWU's School of Business.
Currently, I am working as the Global Marketing Manager for a VoWLAN solution for the 2-way radio verticals/market segments in the SMB space and driving the development of a viable business plan for all global regions, defining target markets and the product portfolio for this space. I am responsible for the product management (subscriber and FNE) aspects as well.
I am also involved with the business case/financials/feature definition around Motorola's next-gen digital 2-way radio conventional and trunking platform (think TRBO !).
I am a recent graduate of the part-time MBA program (dual concentration in Finance & Marketing ) at the Kellstadt Graduate School of Business and joining Harvard Business School starting July '08 for post-MBA Executive General Mgmt studies.
As you can see, I am still trying to connect the dots :)
