Gus Garfield’s Summary

I am a successful professional in the areas of Organizational Strategy, Organizational Development, Process Improvement and Change Management. My tenure in internal OE has been marked by many successes in working with individuals, teams and organizations implementing their strategies and goals. My work is focused on realizing the business strategy through people and HR strategies – linking the objectives of the organization to the objectives and activities of the team and individual. I am recognized for providing Organizational Effectiveness services to leaders and teams, including coaching, facilitation, and thought leadership and practice as they embrace change.

In addition to my experience as an internal partner, I have over 10 years of external consulting experience applying the principles of organizational change and organizational effectiveness in diverse situations with clients as they planned and implemented changes and improvements in their workforce.

I have built a career in planning, managing and leading large scale Organizational Development and Improvement activities. I have been the Project Manager for many process change projects during this time. My skills range from the more traditional Change Management activities, to Process Analysis through to full scale Process-based Organization Design. My experience with Organizational Change is similarly wide ranging - from Technology-induced change (e.g., ERP installations) to the changes related to the implementation of a Shared Services organization, to the full scale redesign of organizations looking to be more customer-responsive.

Because my work is implementation focused, I am very experienced in process analysis and process redesign and created Process Change and Organization Development programs, methods and materials which integrate process change with organization change, leadership, performance management and job design.
