Jeroen Seegers’s Summary

Practice Area Leader with Right Management Netherlands, part of the Manpower Inc.-USA. Gained extensive experience (over 30 years) in a wide range of Human Resource disciplines during a twenty-six year career as both manager and as consultant. Experience within numerous assignments on design and implementation of Assessment Center Programs, Development Centers, Career Centers, Executive Coaching, Leadership Development, Competency Management, Performance Management, Performance Improvement and Strategic HRM.

Advised numerous organizations e.g. ABN AMRO Bank, ING, Bank, Rabobank, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Traffic, ABP - Dutch Pension Fund, Heineken, Mars, MSD, Hoogovens - Corus (Dutch Steel), NCR, AT&T, Nutreco, European Packaging Holding, Rockwool, Océ-Technologies, Siemens, KLM, Transavia, Essent, Academic Medical Centers of the Universities of Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Maastricht and Nijmegen, Teijin, and many other companies.

Also lecturer at the BAAK Management School and the Management Academy of the University of Groningen

Jeroen Seegers’s Specialties:

Particular expertise in management assessment and development, assessment and development centers design, assessor training, leadership development & coaching, performance management and strategic HR.
