Jean Ann Van Krevelen’s Summary

I am interested in helping companies move from good to great through business coaching and consulting. Building brands, business or personal, is one of my favorite things.

I am also a social media strategist and evangelist. I divide my social media time between a) organically building large networks and solid followings b) checking out the latest social media apps and sites and c) delving into established social media sites (like Linkd In) to discover what potential exists below the surface.

I am the Home and Garden editor for BlissfullyDomestic.com, an online magazine. I am also an author, freelance for local papers and have three blogs:
