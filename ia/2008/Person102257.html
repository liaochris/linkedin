Karen Michaeli’s Summary

Karen Michaeli is Director of Enrollment Services at Pharos Innovations where she develops and executes patient engagement and retention strategies for provider, payor and government clients. Karen is passionately committed to “behavioral disease management”, a term describing her conviction that while clinical management of chronic conditions is essential, a core factor in disease management program success is behavior change, necessitating the inclusion of behavioral program components. Most behavior-change models regard the individual as a closed system, and Karen draws upon her background in social work to address individuals with chronic disease as open systems with environmental supports and barriers. Health applications of social networking (patient empowerment and engagement via online community) is another professional passion.

Karen Michaeli’s Specialties:

Call center management; patient engagement and retention strategies for the disease management (DM) sector, technological solutions for patient retention, strategic planning, qualitative research and research management; extensive writing experience.
