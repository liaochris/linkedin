Nicole Sicuro - Leipski’s Summary

With a decade of dedicated experience in admission counseling and recruitment within higher education, Nicole's professional background is complemented by a B.A. with double majors in Communication and Psychology. She carries program management responsibilities in student personnel, recruitment programming, and school district/guidance counselor relations and outreach.

Nicole has been a featured writer several times in Chicagoland Pioneer Press education-related editions and most recently, the Illinois Association for College Admission Counseling resource website for students and their families, www.highereducationstation.org. Having served as an Educational Consultant to University of Wisconsin-System Multicultural Center for Educational Excellence (now Office of Academic Diversity & Development - OADD), she is often requested to present college-planning seminars and workshops to various student/parent groups.

Nicole Sicuro - Leipski’s Specialties:

College-preparatory counseling, higher education student-recruitment, event programming/promotion, workshops & seminars (various topics within the college-planning process, and student leadership topics for students in secondary/post-secondary settings) (Specialty: first generation).
