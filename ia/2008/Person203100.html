Zane Fikany’s Summary

I have been a sales associate at Coldwell Banker deWetter Hovious, Inc. since 2005. Over that time I have become a Multi-Million Dollar Producer. I take a lot of pride in having helped people with decisions as great as buying or selling a home. I have made a lot of life long friends in Las Cruces through real estate.

I moved to Las Cruces to attend New Mexico State University where I got a Bachelor degree in Business. I use my degree every day to help people successfully market their homes. Education has been very important to me. I have continued to educate myself by taking real estate classes so that I may provide my clients with the very best service possible. I have completed Floyd Wickman Sales Training where among other things I earned how to negotiate for my clients. I also look forward to continuing to educate myself in the future.

I pride myself in doing things the “right” way. I will always be truthful to all my clients as well as people I work with during all transactions. I promise to always do as I say, sometimes more but never less. I always make my clients # 1 goal, my #1 goal.

Coldwell Banker deWetter Hovious, Inc. and I have created a marketing plan that strives not only to sell your home but to make the experience of selling a home pleasurable. I have spent many hours talking to people to see what is most important to them when buying or selling a home. I have used what I have learned to formulate a plan that works for everyone. I am always striving to improve my services and gear them specifically to the individual needs of each client.

I have learned that nothing good comes with out hard work. My promise to you is to work hard from now until your property is sold and closed. I believe if I do my job correctly I will also gain a friend and that’s why I am in Real Estate.
