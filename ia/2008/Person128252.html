Melody Matheny’s Summary

Most of my experience has been with the Entertainment/Theme Park industry, designing signage, graphics, show sets, props, etc. My education was in Fine Arts with emphasis in Graphic Design, and I've always been interested in ALL kinds of Fine Arts. I love to draw, do illustrations, paint, sculpt, and make things with my hands, which heightens my love of professionally designing in 3 dimension. I love figuring out how things will work and cultivating it into reality. I am currently working in a Vision Planning firm who designs cruise ports and destinations in the Caribbean. I am assisting in designing Brands and assisting the Land Planners and Illustrators in compiling and communicated the Visions effectively to our Clients. I am also working on website design and implementation currently and am interested in continuing to learn and develop those skills. I aim to get back into 3 dimensional design for the theme park industry or a similar industry in the future, with heavy graphics involvement.
