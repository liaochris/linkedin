Sam Kimber’s Summary

Currently working at Everjoy in the capacity of Senior Web Applications Development Recruiter / SAP Resourcer. With over 2.5 years of experience based in the competative London market place servicing clients throughout Europe, the UK and Scandinavia, he brings exceptional knowledge of the recruitment process and the usual client resource needs and sourcing problems.

Through an extensive network of the most exceptional consultants built on trust and integrity, Sam is always able to identify those who are available now, highly sought after and most importantly relevant to requirements – both functional and technical.
