John Hegarty’s Summary

A General Manager specialising in the provision, operation and the development and enhancement of IT infrastructure services within global enterprises (on-line betting and gaming) and blue chips (Pharmaceuticals and Banking). Skilled in commercial contract management and organisational cost optimisation.

Provides and facilitates industry leadership as Vice Chairman (and Fellow) of the Communications Management Association - a charitable members organisation for ICT professionals.
