David Little’s Summary

A creative web developer with over seven years experience of server- and client-side development using a range of Web technologies. My main interests and experience are in Zope/Plone development (with Python, ZPT and DTML) and client-side design and development using XHTML, CSS and Javascript, with an emphasis on Web standards. Excellent communication skills with a proven track record of discussing requirements with clients and managing in the whole project life cycle. Always interested in using and developing further these skills.
