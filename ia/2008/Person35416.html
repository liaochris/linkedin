Chris Hsiung’s Summary

Currently, I run a practice guiding and coaching young professionals who are choosing to engage in significant life challenges. I combine the analysis of an engineer with the heart of a coach and the curiosity of a lifelong learner to bring out the best in my clients.

Coaching by itself is limited to the experiences encompassed by client and coach. Human Venture Coaching seeks to extend this experience to include that of humanity. Thus as a coach I also draw from the lessons we learn from other fields of human endeavour be it business, engineering, history, or the social sciences.

I am passionate about working with people who have a strong desire to extend the limits of who they are. My clients include people who are starting a new business, leading their communities or organizations towards a better vision, or seeking to transform themselves to break old habits and create new empowering ones. If you are someone looking to make a difference, to extend your capabilities, then I can be the coach that encourages and pushes, nurtures and questions, connects and reveals.

My background has included a diversity of roles. While I have spent eight years as an engineer working in a telecommunications company, I have also been a bridge between Canadian and French culture, a facilitator of workshops for troubled teens, a researcher of urban design of cities, an avid soccer player and coach, and a varied martial artist. All of these roles reflect my desire to make a difference through understanding myself and the world I live in.
