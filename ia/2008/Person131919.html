Mike Calma (michael.calma@gmail.com)’s Summary

Mike Calma joined GSI in 2007 and is the Business Development Manager. He is directly responsible for GSI’s revenue generation efforts as well as tactical operations of the company’s business development team. Mike also facilitates the company’s strategic planning and implementation. He has pioneered and is directing the company’s offshore outsourcing practice.

Mike has over 6 years of business experience and has been involved in several technology start-ups. Prior to joining GSI, he has worked as General Manager for LinkSphere IT Solutions (Self-financed/first failed IT start-up) from 2004 to 2006 and Business Development Manager for General Instrument Corporation (Family-financed Engineering Consulting start-up) from 2002 to 2004.

He has an MBA from the University of the Philippines, Diliman (2008) as well as a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of the Philippines, Los Baños (2003). He is a member of the Upsilon Sigma Phi (www.upsilon.com) and the UPLB Computer Science Society (www.uplbcoss.org).

It was just recently that he coined the term "Entrepreneurial Agitator" to describe his role in the company - Someone who starts trouble to create value! A catalyst for creative conflict!

He blogs sporadically (bstudent.blogspot.com) to share some of his lucid moments. He is starting work on his first book entitled "Entrepreneurial Agitation: Starting Trouble to Create Value".
