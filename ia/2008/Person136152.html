Henrik Ahlen’s Summary

My specialities:
- Business development, content production and project management in interactive media and TV/video.
- Management of development projects and international research.
- Business catalyst and evangelist, inspiring people to use services/products that I believe in myself.
- Creating documented, structured and efficient workgroup methods using smart online tools.

Seeking consultancy assignments, partnership or employment in projects involving interactive media and video. Also open for interim management and advisory boards.

International assignments welcome, I have an extensive international business network and extensive experience of international production and cooperation.

My roots are in film and television. I have worked as a cameraman and editor for national television news/documentaries and feature films, as well as a producer of corporate films. From that I became interested in interactive multimedia production. I founded Ahead MultiMedia AB i Stockholm, Sweden in 1988. It became Sweden's leading professional multimedia production house, growing to 40 employees by 1995 when it was sold to Bonnier Publishing Group.
I had a great time in Los Angeles, California in 1999-2000 when I was vice-president for Internet development at a start-up and responsible for an internet-based training portal.
After that I worked with e-learning research as Studio Manager at the Interactive Institute in Stockholm 2000-2001.

I am also an active partner in the Founders Alliance, a network for serial entrepreneurs in Stockholm, see www.foundersalliance.com

Henrik Ahlen’s Specialties:

Broad experience in interactive media production, telecom solutions, business development and international networking. I am always updated on current interactive media business trends and the latest technology.

My blog on interactive media development: http://alfabravo.com (in English)
