Sophia Mahmoud - Senior Recruiter’s Summary

If you are hiring and want to connect with qualified talent:

I'll consult with you and quickly present a short-list of pre-qualified candidates for your specific needs in terms of desired skills, experience, educational background, rate, and availability. I'll present you with pre-screened and evaluated candidates who have the skills you want and fit your cultural and budget requirements. I can also partner with you to build a pool of talent who fit your specific hiring criteria and organizational culture for your current as well as any of your future needs.

Next I will coordinate interviews between you and your top choices. Once you give the thumbs up on a candidate, I will handle the offer/negotiation phase in a manner making the process as smooth and efficient for you as I will for the candidate.

If you are exploring new career opportunities and want to discuss your options, or new positions germane to your career development, I enjoy guiding busy executives toward opportunities on a confidential basis. Let's talk about the type of work you want and the job market in your area of expertise.

Call me @ (415) 284-9445, or email "sophia@double-clickconsulting.com"

Thanks for visiting and happy hiring!
