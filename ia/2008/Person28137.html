Brian Roberts’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    HomeBase, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    February 2008 — Present (9 months)

    HomeBase, LLC is a professional services firm focused on highy-customized human-capital solutions for leaders in Construction, Real Estate and Renewable Resources. We work exclusively with owners and decision-makers to develop and support well-founded decisions around hiring, orginzational planning & development, training, employee relations and strategic sales and marketing. We have worked with clients from 1.5MM in annual sales, to Fortune 500 companies.

    We were founded on the premise that the single biggest difference between a good company and a great company is their ability to attract, train, manage and develop EXCEPTIONAL PEOPLE.

    Focus Areas: Recruiting, Training, Employee Relations, Outplacement/Career Planning (parent company for www.TurningPointBoston.com), Strategic Planning, Sales Management/Systems.

  • Vice President, Client Service

    Construction Recruiters, Inc

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Construction industry)

    August 2002 — February 2008 (5 years 7 months)

    Helped grow the company from 100K in revenue to just under 2MM in less than 5 years. Worked directly with Colm Allen, founder, in multiple capacities ultimately spending the last 2+ years as the VP of Client Service/Sales. Colm and I met and became friends when we started recruiting together (literally hired 1 week apart!) at Management Recruiters of Boston.

    Accomplishments Include:

    - Year over year sales growth of 35%+
    - Managed Staff of 8
    - Built scalable Sales and Recruiting Process and developed company Best Practices
    - Realigned business around Commercial and Regional focus PRIOR to fallout in the residential construction sector.
    - Individual sales contribution of over 1.5MM dollars over 3 years

  • Project Manager/Partner

    HomeBase, LLC

    (Management Consulting industry)

    January 2000 — January 2007 (7 years 1 month)

    In its first incarnation, HomeBase was a "multi-faceted" endeavor. Initially interested in the market viability of Fuel Cells and home-based solar systems, we hoped to help an up-an-coming player commercialize a cutting-edge product through a focused sales/marketing effort. As it turned out, the market and the technology just weren't ready. Just the same, it was great to meet people on the cutting edge of what is sure to be a vital part of our future.

    Running my own residential remodeling company seemed like a good alternative. The high-end custom construction market was really beginning to blossom and I have always loved "built spaces" of all kinds... but particularly homes (the older the better!). I quickly discovered, however, that despite my best intentions and love of the craft, I was NOT the person you wanted bringing sheet-stock up 3 flights of stairs! My ambitions as a craftsman met with a merciful end when I decided to help Colm grow Construction Recruiters.

  • Director, Priority Search Group

    Management Recruiters of Boston

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    August 1997 — December 1999 (2 years 5 months)

    Started with MRI in the early days of "New Media" and was really captured by the energy of -- what was then -- the beginnings of the internet era. Trinity College was one of the first "networked" Colleges in the country, so I was exposed fairly early on to the "on line" world. I met some great people both at MRI and in the industry, and had the privilege of hiring some of the most visible people in that sector into some very exciting roles.

    At MRI I learned the craft under the steady hand of the legendary Jack Nehiley. I left around the same time as my friend Colm in the late nineties... Colm to start CR in his attic... me to found HomeBase. Colm and I joined forces again in 2002 and the rest, as they say, is history.

  • Sales Associate/Buyer

    Harnett's

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    September 1994 — March 1997 (2 years 7 months)

    Worked with the founder of Bread & Circus (now Whole Foods), Anthony Harnett, as he launched two high-end concept stores in Newton Centre and Cambridge focused on natural products and holistic health. The stores were truly best-in-class for their type and time. This was my first job after college (what would YOU do with a Philosophy degree?? :). I quickly became the top producing sales associate in the company (even though we were a decidedly "non sales-y" environment), through a focus on deep product knowledge and high-level customer service. Promoted to buyer for the company's two most profitable product categories: Vitamins & Herbal Products. Great experience with great people that aligned with my longstanding interest in holistic and sustainable systems.
