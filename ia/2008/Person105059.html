Kenny Hopkins’s Summary

Kenny currently serves at Pegasus Solutions as the Manager of Application Development Services in beautiful Scottsdale. Arizona.

Kenny has worked on a number of large projects over the last 12 years of his tenure at Pegasus.

Kenny's primary call to fame at Pegasus is production triage. All software has bugs big and small some effect revenue directly and some are very annoying to any operational staff. He has spent a great deal of my career at Pegasus efficiently defeating those bugs with my team.

Kenny and his team have also had plenty of successful new products and applications launched during my time.

One of example of those applications was for several hotel chains (1500+ hotels) that required faster and more reliable interaction from the Central Reservation System (CRS) to the Property Management Systems (PMS) on site at each property than what they currently had.

Kenny and his team created the "Pegasus Hub" using the Open Travel Alliance (OTA) schemas with an SOA & Web services. OTA defines a set of XML messages which encompass common business transactions for hotels and the entire hospitality industry. The Pegasus Hub is a web service which exposes the Pegasus legacy enterprise applications by supporting a defined set of OTA messages. It allows a hotel's PMS to transact with Pegasus legacy enterprise applications using standard messages. The Pegasus Hub hides the underlying service so that migration to the next generation Pegasus products may be accomplished with no changes to the PMS interface on the customer side.

Kenny is currently doing research on using DTrace on Solaris to be able to utilize its powerful diagnostics more proficiently, writing a couple of web services for working with some Pegasus vendor applications and adding some enhancements to his company's legacy applications.

Kenny is also working on his SCJP and researching Cloud Computing.
