    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    2008 — Present (less than a year)

    Cashman & Associates represents and develops clients in the fashion & beauty, entertainment & gaming, restaurant & beverage, and real estate industries. With creative talent, strong relationships and proven experience, Cashman & Associates executes customized and articulate public relations programs for both retainer and project-based clients. Cashman & Associates integrates strategic marketing and customized special events to heighten consumer awareness and trade recognition.

    Founded in 2001 by Nicole Cashman, Cashman & Associates has quickly amassed a high profile and international client list including Starr Restaurants, Philadelphia Park Casino, Trump Tower Philadelphia, Hugo Boss and the Greater Philadelphia Marketing and Tourism Corporation. Cashman & Associates has offices in Philadelphia and New York with a network that extends through all major media markets across the country including Miami, Chicago, Los Angeles, DC, Boston and Las Vegas.
