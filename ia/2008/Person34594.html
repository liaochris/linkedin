Chintu Parikh’s Summary

A result-oriented marketing, product management and business development manager with successful track record of founding and rapidly growing Internet or software based business on a limited budget.

Most recently at Yahoo, as a business lead, he led successful launches of a niche ad-network and a mobile advertising platform globally; acquired customers & partners for the ad-network and developed/influenced market requirements & product strategy for the products. The ad-network revenues grew from zero to $6M within one year of launch among constrained business environment.

Prior to Yahoo, Chintu was a co-founder and CEO of a Wi-Fi startup where he raised two rounds of venture financing, build a strong team and led the dramatic transformation of a patented Carnegie Mellon University research technology into a market leading commercial product with marquee customers and significant media recognition within 20 months from first funding.

His ten plus years of professional experience is backed by an MBA, Enterprise Award with Distinction, from Carnegie Mellon University and graduate and undergraduate degrees with high honors in engineering.

Chintu Parikh’s Specialties:

New business development, general management, market entry planning, and new product development - especially within Internet and Wireless industries
