Todd Kempinger’s Summary

As founder and CEO od The Luggage Club (www.theluggageclub.com) Todd Kempinger has established himself as a genuine businessman, entrepreneur and executive with a proven history of accomplishments. TLC has blossomed into a significant player in the emerging luggage delivery service industry. Three years prior to the incorporation of the Company, extensive research and investigation was conducted to confirm the viability of the business concept and validate that the Company, would in fact, change the way people will travel in the future. Prior to the establishment of TLC, his occupation for the past 20 years thrived in the printing industry including owning two printing businesses, held upper management and sales positions at other printing companies and started his printing career by participating in an industry/university co-sponsored internship program. Concurrent with his printing career, other outside entrepreneurial activities included ownership and rental property management, owned a night club, consulting for developing businesses, developing business plans and constructing marketing plans. His numerous accomplishments include establishing a national screen printing business, attained the highest quality benchmark for any screen printer in the United States, successfully marketed several large and lucrative business ventures, designed several national award-winning catalogs and marketing plans, launched a distributor-based division for several printing firms, was listed to the Who’s Who in 1997, 1998, 2000, won numerous Awards of Excellence in the printing field and wrote several successful business plans for new companies while cultivated marketing projects.
