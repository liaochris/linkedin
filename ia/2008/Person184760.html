Ishtiaq Saddam’s Summary

Back in 2004 I joined ICCB, as the Corporate Protocol for His excellency Dr. Pascal Lamy (Director General WTO) for an entire conference.
After that, I got myself in GP in their CMD as a CM and worked there in various sub units. I was first assigned in their IR Unit's Call Center and later assigned as one of the first 11 people who started the Service Desk. I was transferred first to a remote location in Kushtia where I had to set up the office of the CMSD. After 2 months of around the clock service all was running smooth.Later for that success, I was transferred to Norshingdi to do a similar job. After setting up that I was again recalled back to Dhaka and was duly assigned under the BDR Unit of the same division.
After about 3 months I got selected for a permanent position in the Div of S&D (Now known as Commercial) under the newly launched SME dept I joined in there as a KAM & was the first guy to sell the fresh product for the first time ever for the whole department. Anyway, with time everything changes I guess & for a loophole in the system they made me quit the job. Even the day I lost my job, I exposed a simple way of how to remotely log in to your work/home pc from a simple internet enabled cell phone so that one can easily fetch the required files, print or edit them in a diff location.
These days I'm designing websites (I've designed my own site), graphics (available in deviantart),& most importantly NOKIA themes (www.skinned.co.nr).
I am also the only "Signatory of Symbian Signed" in entire BD who can validate a software developed by a firm/individual to be used in Nokia in BD. I'm the only Bangladeshi who designs themes for Nokia devices.
So if you are looking for an honest ambitious guy, who can make things happen & has the substantial proof to back himself up... look no further cause to me, my work ethics was, is & will be my first preference in work.
Shine on & try to keep the business clean!
