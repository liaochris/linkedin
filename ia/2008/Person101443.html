Juergen Kaack’s Summary

Experienced in general management issues, focus on change processes in telecom companies like founding, growth, internationalisation, restructuring, alliances, M&A-projects.

In a globalised business with high dynamics alliances and partnerships are a key to success. Defining strategies and concepts, identifying suitable partner companies and setting up a vivid partnership is a specific strength.

Identify and evaluate business risks and establish risk management processes in organisations.

Coaching and sounding board services in order to help the decision makers during change processes, internationalisation and M&A projects.

For recent publications and events see also my OpenBC page (in German language):
https://www.openbc.com/hp/Juergen_Kaack/

Juergen Kaack’s Specialties:

Design of innovative telecom services like voice controlled communication assistant and other value added services. Conduct business plans and set-up of successful companies in the telecom and high tech sector, organise management teams from scratch.

Review of business and product strategies and management of turnarounds. Identify suitable business partners in the value chain and make alliances alive and working.

Optimize marketing and sales organisations in B-to-B businesses.
