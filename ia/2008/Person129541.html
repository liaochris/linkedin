Michael Dal Cerro’s Summary

**Featured on TopLinked.com**

Nicknamed "Michael D", he has over 12 years of experience in the technology and management consulting industry, 10 of which primarily focused on the recruitment of human capital. His responsibilities have spanned across full lifecycle recruitment, employment branding, college relations, affiliate management and vendor relations.

HIGHLIGHTS
• Award winning corporate recruiting experience
• Award winning staffing agency experience including staff augmentation and contingency search
• Deep technical recruiting background, particularly in Microsoft
• Well connected across the Microsoft community and extended ecosystem.
• 11 years experience working at Microsoft Gold Partners
• Highly skilled in recruiting across all functional areas of the enterprise, from executive level to individual contributor
• Experience with legal devices such as NDA’s, non solicitation agreements, Master Agreements, Direct Hire Agreements and non-competes
• 3rd party/vendor management experience
• Implementation of Employee Referral Programs
• Knowledge of corporate and executive relocation
• Selected to champion the assessment, design and implementation of new tools, process, policies and procedures
• Passive and active candidate recruiting techniques
• Worked extensively in conjunction with executives in areas of retention programs, new hire orientations, rollout of new compensation programs, off boarding, and professional development

He lives in Roswell, Georgia with his wife and two children where they are active members of Fellowship Bible Church. He can be reached at michaeld@elumenotion.com.
