Killian Tobin’s Summary

I have a great deal of practical experience performing infrastructure master planning for municipal agencies. I received my M.S. in Computer Science to broaden my potential contribution in both fields. My multi-disciplinary background allows me to provide unique insight on specialized software applications for the Civil Engineering field.

I now work for MWH Soft, the leading provider of master planning software to major municipalities and their consultants. Since joining MWH Soft, I have been afforded numerous opportunities to provide input on the development of our software. I also enjoy the continual challenge of reaching out to new clients and assisting our existing users.

Some of my other interests include: designing web and kiosk applications; information design; being a weather geek; exploring sustainability issues; and sailing.
