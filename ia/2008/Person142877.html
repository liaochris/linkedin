Nathaniel Pool’s Summary

A visionary, results-oriented CEO/President with unparalleled background in starting, building, and driving multimillion-dollar, high-tech companies to significant success.

Intimately involved in the Internet space since 1993 with over 20 years experience in the satellite communications, cable TV and mortgage banking industries.

Interested in C-level management or investment opportunities within web-based / Internet services space, particularly in one of the following markets: social-networking; presence indicator, GPS or location-based; unified communications and mobility; or similar collaborative software solutions.

On the investing side, I look for individuals and ideas with *S*T*A*R* appeal:

*SOLUTION - A "must-have" unique or better Internet-related product or service that fills a strong niche with a market potential of $100 million.

*TEAM - A dynamic team of smart people that share the same vision and passion, preferrably with more than one founder.

*ADVANTAGE - Your idea must be unique or have a strong advantage or fill a niche. More often than not, it's an improvement on old technology or ways of doing things.

*RAPPORT - It must create an emotional bond with customers. Something they can't live without and something that all their friends will want.

I am interested in web-based services and technologies, GPS and locating services, social-networking and mobility technologies.
