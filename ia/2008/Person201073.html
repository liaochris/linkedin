Michelle Lentz’s Summary

I am an experienced technical writer and trainer. While running a successful small business, I rediscovered a passion for writing and hope to parlay my technical experience into a career in technical and/or wine journalism.

I am talented at designing and delivering corporate training on a number of subjects, as well as creating all types of technical documentation. My soft spot, as it were, is software documentation. I currently try to evangelize Web 2.0 learning methodologies that go beyond eLearning. (Go ahead - ask me more about this!) It's time to incorporate wikis, blogs, and informal learning into the mix. Learning is more than just clicking a next button - it should be a collaborative effort.

Michelle Lentz’s Specialties:

Specialties include blogs, wikis, informal learning, collaborative learning, online/computer-based help, and just-in-time learning & help delivery.
