Kellee K. Sikes’s Summary

Strategist, Entrepreneur, Facilitator, Keynote Speaker, Writer, Executive Coach, Authentic "tell it like it is" Advisor

Kellee K. Sikes founded and has lead Pioneer Technologies Consulting (PTC), headquarters in St. Louis, Missouri, since 1999. In the unique fair trade, free agent consultancy, Kellee guides PTC, entrepreneurs and companies to turn startups, BIG problems, and challenges into wild success by aligning and implementing harmonic strategy, communication and technology solutions through a talented and specialized staff of experts.

Prior to founding Pioneer, Kellee served as a senior leader with several organizations working across the United States and in over 50 international cities. As a social entrepreneur and social justice activist, Kellee focuses on expanding the Fair Trade Movement chief among several other social-good activities. She also serves on the boards of the Kirkwood Dog Park Association and the Justice Advocates.

Kellee attended University of Guelph, Guelph, Ontario, Canada to play ice hockey while managing to earn a degree and keep all of her teeth.

Pioneer Technologies Consulting (PTC), an abundance economics, fair trade, striving for green, company, serves social entrepreneurs, social enterprise and locally owned business by aligning people, process, and publicity for growth and success. We work with you to:
•Bring Business to Your Door,
•Develop and Sustain Your Identity in the Media,
•Put Your Name and Materials in the Hands of Customers,
•Design Your Strategy to Close the Sale, and
•Implement Processes and Services that Create Referrals and Repeat Business,
by creating business development, marketing and PR solutions that result in a sustainable, socially responsible, and successful business. Have a look at our LinkedIn recommendations to learn why.
