Paul Wood’s Summary

Paul Wood can be reached at: pwood@applesearch.co.uk

He has been working in executive search in Romania since 1998 and set up Apple Search Selection in January 2000. He read History and Law at Queens’ College, Cambridge and holds Diplomas in Law and Legal Practice from the College of Law, London. He worked as a civil servant in the House of Lords and as a political risks insurance broker in Lloyds’ of London before becoming a headhunter. In Bucharest he was Senior Consultant for Antal International before starting Apple.

Paul Wood’s Specialties:

We have been sourcing senior and middle managers for international and local companies on the Romanian market for six years. Like most executive search companies in Romania we are generalists although we are particularly known for recruiting lawyers and finance professionals.
