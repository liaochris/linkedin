Corey King’s Summary

My career has been focused in the Global Networking/Healthcare industries, and has included positions as a senior level technical trainer and a HR facilitator. Most recently, I worked at Coca-Cola Enterprises as a Learning Specialist. Prior to that I also worked for such companies as Troutman Sanders LLP, Orange/FT (formerly Equant), and Coca-Cola Inc.

I 'm upbeat, likable and very enthusiastic about training. Some of my areas of expertise include MSOffice Training (at a law firm,) facilitating conflict resolution and diversity classes, and internal applications training.

I've lived in Edwardsville, Illinois, St. Louis, Missouri and Atlanta and have enjoyed traveling the globe extensively. I graduated from Southern Illinois University with a BA in Mass Communication. Some of my hobbies outside of work are reading, studying European history, keeping fit, and socializing with friends.

Due to a recent reorganization at CCE, my position along with 1500 others was eliminated. I learned a lot about facilitation and continued to improve my training and communication skills.

Now I am looking forward to putting my experience and skills to work in a new environment.
