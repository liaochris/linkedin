Cheryl Snell’s Summary

Cheryl Snell is the author of four books of poetry--Flower Half Blown (Finishing Line Press, 02), Epithalamion (Little Poem Press, 04) ,Samsara (Pudding House Publications, 07)., and the winner of Lopside Press's chapbook competition, Prisoner's Dilemma. Her new electronic chapbook, Murmuration, is published by Gold Wake Press.
Snell's novel, Shiva's Arms (Writer's Lair Books), explores cultural identity, the power of reconciliation, and the meaning of home, against a backdrop of South Indian customs.

Cheryl Snell’s Specialties:

editing, reviewing, critiquing
