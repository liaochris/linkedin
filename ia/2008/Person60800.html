Erin Lyons’s Summary

Erin Lyons is a typical eldest child: she's comfortable being in charge, she always wants to get things done NOW, and she's never afraid to step up and do it herself.

A hard worker, a fast learner, and a staunch individualist; Jersey born, coming to New York City by way of Baltimore, this former farmgirl now makes her home in Brooklyn.

In 2005, Erin left the finance world with the intention of advancing her career in a creative field. She subsequently integrated herself into a new industry with a radically different culture, all while coordinating the most successful new business department in Saatchi & Saatchi history, achieving winning pitches amounting to over $1 Billion in new business in 2006 and 2007.

Erin was raised on a sustainable farm and was taught that each human's responsibility should be to minimize their impact on the earth. Furthermore, her personal values hold that each individual should always look out for their fellow humans. She is Saatchi & Saatchi New York's True Blue Champion and works closely with Saatchi S to bring sustainability to her colleagues at SSNY.
