Chris Potts’s Summary

Globe-trotting specialist in corporate strategies for exploiting Information Technology.

Dedicated to helping organisations create maximum value from investing in business changes involving IT by integrating 'expert IT customer' competencies into their mainstream business management, and guiding IT specialists to be highly valued by the company they work for.

Works on high value, short-timescale initiatives in collaboration with Chief Information Officers and IT Directors, their executive colleagues and staff. Author, columnist, and speaker at conferences and seminars. Accredited Expert on CIO.com. Member of Computer Weekly's panel of Strategy Clinic experts.

Author of 'fruITion: Creating the Ultimate Corporate Strategy for Information Technology'.
