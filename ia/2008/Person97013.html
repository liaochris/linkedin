Jordan Ayan’s Summary

Founder and CEO of SubscriberMail. LLC a leading permission-based email marketing service organization, and Create-It! Inc. A technology consultancy. Firms founded in 1993, are profitable, cash-flow positive and have no venture financing.

Previously Vice President - FastData Services a joint venture between Donnelley Marketing and First Data resources. Concieved and built the division into a $50 million business in 2 years.

Account Executive - Donnelley Marketing - Sales of Direct Marketing products and services to major organizations.
