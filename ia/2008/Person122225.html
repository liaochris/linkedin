Mark Lema, SPHR’s Summary

SENIOR-LEVEL MANAGEMENT: Human Resources Consultant

MARK A LEMA, SPHR, is a global human resources executive with 16 years of proven leadership results in top tier Fortune 500 manufacturing, consumer goods, multicultural, and multinational organizations. He is currently the Founder and CEO of Lema & Associates, a strategic HR Consulting and Search Firm specializing in the recruitment, hiring, training, motivation and retention of the Hispanic / Latino workforce.

Mr. Lema is a Dynamic Human Resources & Labor Relations professional with an extensive record of providing training and employee-resource management that propels large, multi-national companies forward. Specialize in human resources and Labor Relations issues relating to Hispanic workforces, especially coaching supervisors in American culture immersion, communicating with the Hispanic workforce, and recruiting bi-lingual talent. Mr. Lema is an expert in organizational development, change management and building internal relationships. Mr. Lema has demonstrated success in aligning human resources strategy with business goals to facilitate smooth operations and ensure corporate success.
Mr. Lema holds Bachelor's from the University of Southern Mississippi, a certificate in labor relations, and is a certified senior professional in Human Resources (SPHR). Mark is fluent in Spanish, a member of SHRM, the Tri-State HR, and HHRN (Hispanic Human Resources Network).
