Norméa Banner’s Summary

I have worked with youth in Detroit for over 5 years, specializing in creating foundations for future success. Having identified parents and families as the biggest contributor to the success of youth, I have worked to encourage parents and other adults to be positively involved in their child's lives - at home and in the community.

I have developed skills in volunteer coordination, special event planning, grant writing, project/program management, sales & recruiting, and resource management.
