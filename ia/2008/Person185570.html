Tara Gerner-Ziegmont’s Summary

I am a highly motivated professional with a lifelong passion for writing and extensive educational experience. I earned a B.S. in Secondary Education and Earth and Space Science in 2001 from Penn State. In 2006, I completed a Master's degree in Teaching and Curriculum and, simultaneously, a Writing Specialist certificate, also from Penn State.

Throughout my career as a teacher, I have been interested in writing. As a high school science teacher, I have the opportunity to teach the fundamentals of writing to students while focusing on the subject of earth science. It is always challenging, and helping students to become better writers helps me to do the same.

Since becoming a mother in 2007, I have chosen to focus my freelance writing and editing career on more domestic activities, including parenting, cooking, and crafting.

I write almost daily on my personal blog, and I am currently a regular contributor to several websites and online magazines in the areas of crafting, cooking from scratch, and only children. I am available for freelance assignments in these and other areas.

You can email me at tara at feelslikehomeblog dot com.
