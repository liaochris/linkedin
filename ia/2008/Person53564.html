Dorothéa Ionescu-Vanau’s Summary

I've been working in the media field since I was 20 and at 34 I can say that my area of expertise becomes wider with every project I take. Seven years ago, when I gave birth to my first child I decided to rather choose to lay down the employee status and have my own company so that I would better handle my duties as a mom.

And it's been a right decision, I think, because making my job remote and focusing on the on-line business, I had the chance to create a crowd of significant projects for some outstanding clients as Coca-Cola, British American Tobacco, Dacia-Renault Groupe, TVR, Beiersdorf or Ringier.

About a year ago I retired from the busy Capital city at a bottom of a hill which I own. Here I have an overwhelming sense of freedom which empowers me to think, write and create.

Now I'm in the research stage for a documentary I'd like to produce next year, in Jerusalem.

I've been traveling across the world and got in touch with many professionals in the communication field and felt happy watching how my ideas came into practice and turned into incredible results.
