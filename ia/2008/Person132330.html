Mike Leahy’s Summary

Business savvy hacker into bleeding edge web technologies, graphics/audio development for Java, and embedded development in C/C++ with uClinux on Blackfin & Sharc DSP processors. In the past I've held lead development and principal architect positions at various companies. Currently, I run EGR Software LLC.

I am contracting with various clients via EGR Software assisting them in their client/graphic development needs and embedded development with uClinux. Please do get in contact as I am an experienced software architect and would be glad to assist in projects small to large from conception/design to implementation.

- Principal architect @ GeoSpot, Inc, prefunded stage; core client architecture and real time rendering engine.

- Lead developer @ Mobile Complete, Inc. from 1st round funding to 2nd; continued as a contract software architect from September '06 to July '08 through seeing MC become a profitable company.

Mike Leahy’s Specialties:

"Web 3.0(tm)", Java based rich clients and desktop applications, OpenGL/3D graphics, Java2D/Volatile Image API, 3D engines, rendering engines for GIS/mapping, intimate knowledge of Java 1.4, 5.0, and 6.0 JDKs. C/C++ microcontroller/DSP w/ Analog Devices Blackfin (uClinux)/Sharc processors, real time audio synthesis and advanced audio spatialization.
