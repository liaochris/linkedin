Jennifer Morgan’s Summary

Experience in the stock photography industry includes picture editing and visual content management, product development, creative research, and recruiting. Thorough understanding of image licensing, rights management, and copyright issues.

Fine art/photographic teaching experience includes pedagogical studies and curriculum development. Established national art exhibition record.

Digital imaging competencies include extensive knowledge of DSLR systems, high-resolution scanning, digital color correction, and digital photographic output.
