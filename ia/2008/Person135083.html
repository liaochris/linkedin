David Mondelore’s Summary

I am a Senior Game & Animation Programmer and Technical Designer. I want to be part of an experienced and self organized team and the creative development of the greatest console games to come.

My professional career in the game development industry started in summer 2001. Since then I have been involved in the development of prototypes and several released titles, developed large and integrated systems, designed and implemented game mechanics, and closely assisted lead designers and animators.

More specifically I have hands on experience from designing and implementing character and vehicle game mechanics for both shooter and adventure gameplay. I have been working closely with top researchers in the field of edge animation technology and have the experience and expertise of how to realize a highly advanced animation system, pipeline and tools for next-generation games where responsive gameplay and realistic motions are both mutual goals.

After joining the Crysis team in mid project I'm responsible for bringing the character movement and animation in singleplayer and multiplayer to the very ambitious quality bar of the rest of the game. After Crysis I will work on animation technology and tools for CryEngine2 in paralell with prototyping new game designs.
