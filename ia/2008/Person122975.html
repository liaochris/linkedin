Marlene Cole’s Summary

A scientist, educator, writer, and leader with proven professional skills that bridge science and education, advocacy and outreach, as well as strategic and administrative organizational leadership, Dr. Marlene Cole has researched and written a wide variety of technical, regulatory, and marketing materials and has spoken before legislators, academic audiences and the public. Her focal interests include: land use, (re)development and natural resource management and restoration; quantitative and geospatial modeling; science education and communication; and development of science-based solutions to global environmental problems. (marlene.cole -at- yahoo.com)

Marlene Cole’s Specialties:

Habitat assessment and ecological impact assessment (deserts, forests, rivers, salt marshes, inland wetlands, suburbs, cities), ecological restoration, environmental advocacy; conservation planning, urban ecology and urban planning, ecological landscape design, watershed protection; statistics and computer modeling, GIS and remote sensing, habitat modeling, gap analysis; communication, outreach, education, and project management.
