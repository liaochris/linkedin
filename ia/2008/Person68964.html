George Papachristou’s Summary

Graphic Designer/Production Coordinator experienced in the
concept, art direction, design, production, and coordination
of a wide range of print collateral including catalogs,
direct mail campaigns, posters, brochures, and packaging.
Expertise in catalog design and concepts to promote products
and services in both business to business and business to
consumer markets. Able to use Quark, Indesign, Illustrator,
and Photoshop to create print pieces. Skilled in art directing
photo shoots to ensure proper product placement, lighting,
and aesthetic features.

I'm passionate about my profession. Always striving to
achieve a heightened awareness for quality design along
with a sensitivity and understanding for the project at hand.
Every project is handled with energy, desire and exacting
professionalism. There has always been a strong affinity for
aesthetics and style with the belief that every project is
important no matter how small it might appear. This self-imposed
attitude helps create an expectation of unremitting quality
and perfection. My attention to detail remains relentless...
no aspect of a project is overlooked.

It is my commitment to excellence that I would like you to
consider when making a final evaluation.

Thank you
