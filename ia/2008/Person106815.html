Kimee J. Balmilero’s Summary

Born and raised in Hawaii, Kimee started performing from day one. Although an only child, she competed for her Grandma’s attention with 10 other cousins!! While the others were getting good grades and setting carpets on fire (true story!) Kimee was running around cracking silly jokes and singing 80s pop songs. With a father that’s a huge fan of music and singing and a mother with a background in Filipino folk dancing, she just couldn’t help herself. Luckily, not much has changed since then. Kimee continues to perform for audiences worldwide with credits including: The 2nd National Touring Company of “Miss Saigon,” The Original Broadway Cast of “Mamma Mia,” and most recently “Hi-5” the three-time Emmy nominated TV Series on Discovery Kids and TLC (www.hi5us.com). With “Hi-5” she has had the opportunity to perform on “The Today Show”, “Live with Regis and Kelly” and “The Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade”.
Currently residing in sunny Los Angeles, in her spare time Kimee enjoys hanging with friends, shopping, cooking, dancing and designing jewelry. She launched her own jewelry line in December 2006 called Kimeejay (www.kimeejay.com)
