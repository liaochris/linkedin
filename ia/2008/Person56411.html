Rick Dietler’s Summary

Extensive management experience in all aspects of Supply Chain Management in pharmaceutical and consumer products industries, including purchasing, planning, subcontract manufacturing, packaging, manufacturing, logistics, and warehouse management. Decisive, results oriented problem solver with a focus on improving the entire supply chain to enhance productivity and profitability. Experienced in ISO-9000 and Lean Manufacturing. Demonstrated success in formulating, developing and implementing strategic planning, creative problem solving, and delivering Value Analysis savings. Supported worldwide manufacturing plants using global sourcing strategies. Results oriented team builderwith strong interpersonal and communication skills. Oracle and JD Edwards ERP systems and e-commerce experience.
