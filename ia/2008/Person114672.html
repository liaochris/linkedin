Liubov Grechen’s Summary

I currently serve as the Membership Coordinator at UNA-USA, working with Student Alliance, the Young Professionals for International Cooperation (YPIC), and Chapter Development. I work with UNA-USA members on their Adopt-A-Minefield and HERO activities, and our team in Washington to more effectively coordinate advocacy efforts across the organization, including the Council of Organizations and the National Council, and with partner organizations such as the UN Foundation.

I previously served as Chair, Vice-Chair and as the Director of the Economic Development Committee at UNA-NY YPIC. I am one of the founding members of the UNA-GYP (Global Young Professionals) Task Force, whose goal is to expand our young professionals network, by training and educating other young professionals from around the world on how to start YPIC groups internationally and how to build effective and sustainable partnerships that allow concrete and meaningful involvement in achieving the MDGs. I served as a USA alternative delegate at the 38th World Federation of United Nations Associations (WFUNA) Plenary Assembly in Buenos Aires, and I presented “Taking YPIC Global” as part of the UNA-GYP Task Force to the Youth Plenary Assembly. I have also worked as a consultant for the Friends of the Carpathian Foundation-US, a unique re-granting organization which channels funds to rural areas of the Carpathian Euroregion. During this time, I co-founded a groundbreaking initiative, the Carpathian Compact, an international movement designed to increase cooperation and promote greater synergies among the public, private, and NGO sectors to build a stronger more collaborative civil society and usher the Carpathian Euroregion into the greater global economy.
