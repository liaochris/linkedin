Kevin Sheehan’s Summary

Over 27 years of technical experience in the telecommunications and government sectors. Extensive technical and security architecture background including designing, hosting, monitoring, tuning and troubleshooting complex, scalable, highly secure and highly available business solutions. Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) experience includes Oracle Advanced Queuing, Oracle SOA Suite, Vitria and Tibco. Highly skilled at configuring F5 BigIP load balancers for continuous application availability. Monitoring experience includes Oracle Enterprise Manager Grid Control, Quest Foglight and Quest Spotlight. Security expertise includes applying technical controls to the entire technology stack (hardening) as well as participating in and developing documentation for the Certification and Accreditation (C&A) of business systems including System Security Plans.

I am a senior level technology manager experienced in large-scale enterprise application deployments in the government and telecommunications sectors with specialties in the entire Oracle technology stack including designing, hosting and securing such implementations.

I am an Open Networker (kpsheehan@gmail.com) and accept all invitations from the following networks. I pledge to assist my network in any way I can and expect to be treated in kind as this is the fundamental tenant of networking. Note, however, that I endorse only those members of my network with whom I have established a professional relationship since to do otherwise cheapens the concept of endorsement. I also do not accept blind requests for introductions to my network. If you can communicate a synergy that makes the introduction a win/win I will be happy to work with you.
