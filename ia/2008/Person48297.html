Dan Brewster’s Summary

Mr. Brewster has recently been named Director of Marketing for Solutions at Sea, a sister company of International Cruise and Excursions, Inc.. He is primarily tasked with establishing and growing the company's rewards program. He has also launched a new venture under the name, EventZazz Productions. EventZazz is a full-service entertainment and experience solution company specializing in bringing an extensive network of professionals together to make incredible experiences happen. He formerly served as the Director of Marketing for the Phoenix Coyotes. In this capacity, he supervised all advertising, promotions and in-arena entertainment. Prior to joining the Coyotes, Brewster served as Manager of Advertising & Special Events for the Los Angeles Dodgers and as the Assistant Athletic Director – Marketing for the University of Southern California. Brewster has won national recognition from the Promotion Marketing Association for Hispanic marketing programs at the Dodgers and is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and events. He has also taught Market Research at the University of San Francisco’s School of Sports Management. He is a graduate of the University of Southern California (BA ’93; MBA ’02).
