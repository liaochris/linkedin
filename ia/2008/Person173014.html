Sergio DaCosta’s Summary

Over 20 years experience implementing IS solutions, with complete and extensive exposure to all phases of systems life cycle, providing technical consulting expertise, training and sales support. Overall, accumulated a great deal of hands-on exposure both in UNIX and Windows based platforms, applications and integration of Microsoft Office and Back-Office. More than 3 years in project management in the steel manufacturing industry in Brazil; 8 years in project implementation in the US and abroad; 2+ years in Account Management at Oracle Corporation. A successful and results oriented professional who is self-motivated, industrious and possesses excellent people skills. At Oracle he specialized in Infrastructure and Technical Architecture and Siebel Upgrades leading from small teams of 5 to large teams of 20+ consultants. A very well prepared individual, consistently playing a leading role in every engagement he has participated in the last 10 years.
