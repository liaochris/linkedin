Steven Connolly’s Summary

With over 20 years in developing technology start-up companies, I have had the opportunity to lead many talented teams through the process of creating and commercializing new ideas and technologies. As a founder and president of WayCraft Biosoftware I am actively involved with applying information technology to meet the growing needs of the healthcare and life sciences industries. Prior to founding WayCraft, I worked as an independent consultant providing technology and management services to healthcare institutions and bioscience companies.
