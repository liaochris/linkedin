Molly Bennett’s Summary

Seattle-area freelancer, specializing in photography and graphic design.

Commercial photography work includes: wedding, portraits, lifestyle, children, pregnancies, events and assorted interesting projects that come my way. Photojournalistic style.

Graphic design work includes: logos, brand/identity packages, event promotions, annual reports, post cards, direct mail, information architecture, user interface design and web design.

Molly Bennett’s Specialties:

Helping people feel comfortable in front of the camera, and helping companies organize their information in ways that make sense to their users.

  • User Interface Designer

    drugstore.com

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; dscm; Internet industry)

    July 2000 — February 2003 (2 years 8 months)

    As the User Interface Designer at drugstore.com, it was my responsibility to create easy-to-understand, customer-centric designs for the features that we released to our online web-store and those of our partner sites. I worked with the Project Management Team and Creative Team to implement features that were visually appealing, technically feasible, and aimed at improving the user experience on our website. I helped implement changes to our login, checkout, and prescription ordering processes, made changes to our main navigation and color scheme, created process screens for "customer reviews" and "customer favorites," aided in determining the direction of the drugstore.com brand, and worked on re-designs of some of our partner websites.
