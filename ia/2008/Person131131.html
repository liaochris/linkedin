Michelle Kopper’s Summary

I hold the space to sustain a community of artists— and YOU are an artist— that thrives through shared risk taking, support and encouragement. I maintain an arena for established artists and those just daring to honor their own creative spark to nourish themselves and their creativity, fill their wells, rest and dream.

I offer a crucible to keep artistic exploration open, to keep performance craft fresh, pliable and immediate, to keep the muscle of "risk-taking" supple.

I want to empower authentic expression. I aspire to create and sustain an environment that supports risk-taking, artistic growth & vulnerable exploration of Mind, Body, Emotions and Spirit.

I combine 25 years as a professional actor/singer with 15+ years of yoga & dance meditation bringing a unique perspective to my coaching and teaching approach. I am committed to facilitating students with integrity, sensitivity and compassion.

After teaching, coaching and performing in Manhattan for 15+ years, I returned to Portland in 2004 with my passion for teaching and a strong commitment to my own artistic process. I have been reconnecting with the vital arts community here and creating a sustainable and balanced life with my family.

Michelle Kopper’s Specialties:

Actor/Singer/Songwriter, Performance Coach, SAG member, AEA member, Board Member: Portland Area Theatre Alliance. I'm also a certified teacher Rasa Yoga and a blue belt in The Nia Technique. These practices are an integral part of her work, using breath, movement & sound to enhance physical, emotional and creative well being.
