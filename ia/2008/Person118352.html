Tony Bezilla’s Summary

Meet one of the most outstanding IT Professionals in the industry, Raymond Anthony (“Tony”) Bezilla. Tony has provided strategic vision and leadership to a number of diverse organizations over the course of his 20 year career in information technology. He has implemented process and technology solutions at public and private companies, for-profit and non-profit organizations, government and educational institutions and US-based and global organizations. He provided leadership for a decade in the US Navy, spent a year at one of the premier global consulting organizations as a member of their highly selective Computer Risk Management Practice and has spent the last nine years providing leadership excellence for IT Services companies with temporary excursions as an independent consultant delivering highly successful cutting edge solutions and strategic consulting.

Tony’s broad range of valuable experience comes from dozens of projects and engagements which have involved strategic planning, systems integration, business process optimization, and organization (re)design. He is recognized for his ability to lead IT organizations, drive organizational turn-around and manage large complex projects. Tony is an expert in the area of IT Operations and is adept at process optimization and human capacity management. He is a published and respected author and technical writer. His leadership style and pragmatic approach have helped him develop a trusted rapport with superiors and subordinates alike.

Under Tony’s leadership, numerous organizations have reduced operational costs, improved processes, increased capacity, retained highly coveted talent, improved service levels, reduced systems downtime, passed IT audits with flying colors and projects have been delivered on-time and within budget. Tony has a long track record of far exceeding customer expectations and being the difference maker in driving change or closing the deal.
