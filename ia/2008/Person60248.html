Eric Sun’s Summary

With the thorough understandings of computing, application & network architecture and data lineage, I have proven approaches to improve the performance & ROI by 50%~200% based on the existing data warehouse and business intelligence infrastructure.

I believe that making the best use of the tools and platforms is the most important attitude - because what's the point to switching from one platform to another if you know little about how to tackle the problems in any platform.

In IT industry, we always enjoy the debates about which tool is better than the other. I like the practical comparison, but my goal is to provide the valuable consulting and eduction services to let more team members and clients be able to make the full use of the existing tools and investments, instead of being allured by the marketing brochures and then switching from one solution to another.

I am more than happy to help you and your organization to build enterprise data warehouse, double or triple the data integration performance for HUGE volume, improve data quality realistically, integrate/customizer metadata across multiple tools/platforms, optimize BI implementations, and fill the functional/feature gaps of the expensive commercial tools in your company.


My 10 years experience covers the following technologies:
Data Warehouse, Business Intelligence, MDM, UNIX, Oracle, Netezza, MS SQL Server, DB2, Informix, MySQL, Informatica, Trillium DQ, SAS, Cognos, IP Network (VPN/Firewall/Fast-Ethernet), LDAP, WebLogic/TomCat, VMWare, Aqualogic, SyncSort, C++, Perl, VBScript, Shell scripting, Wiki, Java
