Artie Romero’s Summary

ARG! Cartoon Animation Studio was founded by Artie Romero in 1994. Artie has been involved in comics and animation since the 1970s, and has screen credits for animation on 4 motion pictures, 2 TV series and one PBS special. Before entering the animation industry as an animator, producer and director, Artie was a comic book artist, publisher, and an illustrator of books and magazines.

After starting out in 1995 as just a few pages on Artie's AOL Web space, the artie.com website was launched in July, 1996, and quickly became one of the most popular animation sites on the Web. Don't take our word for it, see all the sites that link to artie.com. The ARG! studio has created over 40,000 original digital animations, and more than 15,000 of them are displayed on this web site. ARG! animation is available worldwide for download to your cell phone through AT&T, Verizon, Sprint, Tmobile and many other carriers, thanks to our representation by Wireless Developer Agency.

Since founding ARG! Cartoon Animation, Artie created the CityStar® brand and expanded his company's services into website development, web hosting, Internet access, SEO and online advertising. ARG! is now a division of CityStar® Group, Inc., a Colorado corporation, with Artie serving as CityStar's CEO and President.

Artie Romero’s Experience

  • Owner

    CityStar Group, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Animation industry)

    December 1994 — Present (13 years 4 months)

    ARG! Cartoon Animation Studio specializes in character animation. Founded in 1994 by Artie Romero, ARG! produces commercials for film, TV and new media. The ARG! client list includes Kaiser Permanente, Duracell, Safeco Insurance, Harper Collins and Snap-On Tools. The studio is staffed with professional 2D and 3D animators, and offers many styles and formats of animation.

    Since founding ARG! Cartoon Animation, Artie Romero created the CityStar® brand and expanded into website development, web hosting, Internet access, SEO and online advertising. ARG! is now a division of CityStar® Group, Inc. Starting in December of 1996, CityStar has developed a network of 200 city portals throughout the U.S., Canada and Great Britain. These city directory sites get up to 12 million hits a month.

    In January of 2008, CityStar® released a professionally produced, 5 DVD set of 10 one-hour workshops on Internet marketing called 'Net Profit$, featuring Artie and other Internet entrepreneurs.

  • Special Effects Animator

    Braid Media Arts LLC

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Media Production industry)

    October 1994 — December 1994 (3 months)

    I worked on the Digital Dream Sequence for 1995's Tri-Star Pictures release, Johnny Mnemonic starring Keanu Reeves, Takeshi, Ice-T, Dolph Lundgren, Dina Meyer, and Henry Rollins.
