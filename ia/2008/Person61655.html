Evan Miller’s Summary

Evan C Miller is an IT senior management veteran with over 25 years at Texas Instruments who built two global IT businesses at TI in the last ten years.

Mr. Miller is currently the IT Operations Strategy Director responsible for facilitating the strategy generation process for multiple parallel global cross-organizational teams to develop service strategy for the organization, and facilitating input to the annual plan. In addition, he is also leading an IT Service Management team to develop service management process strategy. He recently was also given responsibility for defining the Relationship Management role in IT Operations.

Prior to this role, Mr. Miller was the Mobile Solutions Director in IT Operations at Texas Instruments. He designed the TI internal global mobile employee strategy, designed the business to support it, built the organization, and managed the business to rapid growth in support of over 10,000 users. He managed a team of eighteen professionals that provided productivity impact to TI of over $60 million annually and increased sales force face time by 10%, generating a $250 million annual revenue improvement.

Prior to his current role, Mr. Miller led the creation of TI's email strategy and built the Internet email business inside IT Operations which grew to 30,000 users globally within 3 years, replacing a mainframe email system and increasing the speed of TI's business culture. Other experience includes three years of supercomputer sales with Convex, starting & selling a dial-up ISP, starting TI's Internet connection and starting the Unix and supercomputing services for TI's defense business.

Mr. Miller is an advisor and speaker to the wireless industry. He has advised handset OEMs, wireless operators, wireless middleware providers, and telecom startups.

Mr. Miller earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Graceland University, with further MBA coursework from University of Texas and Southern Methodist University.

Evan Miller’s Specialties:

Mr. Miller has proven skills in ITIL, IT Service Management processes, IT service strategy, leading transformation, developing and communicating vision, building organizations, managing talent, operations management, service management, program management and relationship management.
