Aleksandr Samoylov’s Summary

I am an emerging fine artist, writer, and creative media professional. Aside from painting, I have an especial interest in curatorial work, publications (especially independent publishing), film and theatre, and web design and development. I am available for a wide range of freelance commission assignments in painting, illustration, editing and proofreading, as well as comprehensive web design services for bloggers, artists of all varieties, small businesses, non profit organizations, and entrepreneurs.

I am also interested in opportunities to work on movie or theater projects in various capacities, and am always on the lookout for potential venues for showing my artwork or for the production of my plays/screenplays. Am very interested in opportunities to work on set design.

Aleksandr Samoylov’s Specialties:

Painting, as well as illustration/ fine art work in any traditional medium. Playwriting, production, direction. Adobe Creative Suite. Gimp, Inkscape, Scribus (design and layout). Python Scripting. Web design (X/HTML, CSS, with knowledge of PHP/MySQL, XML/RSS, FTP, SSH, SEO, Server side installation/configuration). Literary editing, independent magazine publication (editorial/organizational/administrative ends), journalistic blogging, creative writing.
