Stuart Oliver’s Summary

Feel free to connect with me info@stuart-oliver.com

Stuart is an independent executive with business interests in the USA and Europe. Stuart has successfully built startups in the media, publishing and real estate sectors and is now looking to identify the next business opportunity to build on and the team to deliver it with.

In addition, Stuart is an experienced technology operations executive and interim programme manager. He has a proven track record and extensive experience in shaping consultancy operations for the successful delivery of solutions on high-level strategic assignments. His clients have included major financial services businesses, retail organisations and City institutions. Stuart’s leadership roles have typically encompassed building an all-important network of clients, partners and influencers.

An effective leader and innovator, Stuart blends an approachable personal style with hands-on management expertise. He has consistently demonstrated his leadership skills by developing and mentoring strong professional teams. A skilled communicator, he is proficient at crafting and delivering persuasive messages and has experience of building and maintaining alliances and strategic partnerships. Stuart has a record of successful innovation in a variety of situations and has demonstrated a capability for managing change in both start-up and mature environments.

Stuart has a passion for technology, risk & innovation. He has considerable expertise in developing and championing business technology benefits which makes him a persuasive and visionary commentator. He has managed multi-million pound budgets and teams of up to sixty staff.

Stuart has a wide range of senior-level business contacts worldwide. His career history includes the Army, radar design & electronic engineering and financial services technology.
