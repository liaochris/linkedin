Lisa Moriwaki’s Summary

Currently a Senior Media Planner at Moxie Interactive, an online ad agency for Fox Movies and DVDs business.

I am also the co-founder and partner for AngeLiKa Promotions, LLC. We specialize in event marketing and promotions in Los Angeles and Las Vegas. We also represent talented and experienced DJs for any type of events including weddings, private parties, clubs, holiday parties, etc. Please contact me for more information.
