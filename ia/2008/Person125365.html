Matthew Wise’s Summary

Over 10 years experience in New Media, I was most recently the Co-Founder of the highly successful performance based affiliate network, Advaliant.com. I helped to start the business in 2004 and have recently left.

I thoroughly enjoy working with Clients who have either fully immersed themselves in the Online Performance Marketing Space (OPMS) or who are looking to break new ground. I have successfully managed 100's of accounts in my time and I specialize in the development of consumer, mass appeal, focused offerings which can fully utilize the OPMS. I also have a great deal of experience developing online distribution channels to obtain new customers, as well as creating new revenue streams via cross marketing and up-sells; during and after the sales process.

At my previous employ, and over the last four years, I consistently acted as the top revenue generator at the company. I also held roles on the Board of Directors, as well as an officer of the Company, Senior Team Member, Trainer, Network Director, and Performance Marketing Guru for the Sales Division. At the time of my departure, I was personally on track to hit over $20mm+ in sales for 2008.
