Martin J. Steinmann’s Summary

Innovative, strategic and results-oriented software industry executive with more than 15 years of experience in general management, marketing, product positioning, channel introductions, competitive differentiation, portfolio rationalization, and communication for both large and venture financed startup companies. In-depth understanding of the importance of sustainable differentiation and its implications on creating a successful and profitable business in the IT industry. Well aware of the critical need to be close to customers, markets and competitors.

Change agent, forward-thinking business leader with an aggressive approach to driving customer centric and market leading solutions. Capable of initiating and organizing strategic repositioning and company turn-arounds. Extremely competitive and able to motivate teams to win. Well respected and liked leader focused on winning people's imagination towards a common goal. Proven and frequent speaker at industry events and conferences.

Six year career in venture investing with focus on early stage opportunities in the IT industry and recaps. Extensive experience in industry sector analysis, new trends, deal flow and deal screening, due-diligence, investment recommendations, deal mechanics, financing and fund raising, board work, exit analysis, and management team building. Active investor known to provide tangible strategic and operational assistance to portfolio companies.

Martin J. Steinmann’s Specialties:

Visionary and technologist, startup marketing, company turn-arounds, team building, venture investing. Extensive experience in open source business models for venture financed startup companies. Founder and member of the board of directors of SIPfoundry (www.sipfoundry.org), an open source community focused on unified communication and collaboration and Session Initiation Protocol (SIP) based solutions.
