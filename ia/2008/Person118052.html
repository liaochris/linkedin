Francesco Magliano’s Summary

My professional career started with a shore based jobs which has given me the opportunity to develop my knowledge in rigging, electronic instruments and general maintenance of a sailing boat.
As my high school degree in information technologies can certify I’m skilful with computer and electronics.

I did a lot a lot of races with professionals on different kind of boats especially on First 31.7, First 40.7 and Baltic 50 as second bowman/mast.

I’m keen in diving and I got the PADI Advanced Open Water License

My long-term objective is to advance my career towards running a maxi sailing yacht.
