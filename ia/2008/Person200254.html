Willie Wimbush’s Summary

Wimbush-Inc.Com (Founder, Consultant, Producer, Coordinator)

Wimbush Inc. aka GDAWG Productions (www.wimbush-inc.com) is the central hub for a large array of networked services and resources focused on promoting and providing professional and community developmental empowerment on the local, state and global tiers across of groups.

Our Mission:

To provide enrichment tools and services to anyone who seeks empowerment and development within the business, entertainment and social community.


Our main focus is to successfully bring new resources to inner city and at-risk youth and communities which promotes alternatives to violence, crime, abuse and social ills.

Our Umbrella covers many talented artist, organizations and leaders including Fresh TV which records, edits and airs local talent showcasing, Gourdine Investment Group / Ladiez of Legacy and Empowerment Radio.

Suitlandfest Community Development Corporation 501c3

Executive Vice President / Program Development / Entertainment Director / Sponsorships , Community Relations / Event Coordination / Producer.

Suitlandfest Community Development Corporation / Suitland Recreation Council - A 501(c)(3) corporation which specializes in youth, teens, senior and family development and empowerment.

Our main focus is providing resources and platforms to attract and assist with providing positive change within the community.

We promote enhanced levels of education, recreation, health, artistic, social and economical development and revitalization for those in need.
