Todd Downing’s Summary

Founding partner of two media companies (publishing & film production), long experience in film & video production, music production, game design & entertainment content publishing. Over 30 published adventure game titles to credit, over a dozen original music albums, half a dozen videogames and several films. Shot first feature project at 17 (featured on a PBS young directors series). Several scripts (stage plays and screenplays, both produced and unproduced), 3 short stories sold, and 3 novels completed.

Todd Downing’s Specialties:

Game theory, game design, creative writing, technical writing, screenwriting, graphic arts, 2D animation, 3D animation, fine arts, desktop publishing (editing & layout), cinematography, video editing, audio editing, music composition (by ear), print purchasing, packaging design, promotions design.
