W.L. Pulsipher, CSA, ΔΣΠ’s Summary

W.L. Pulsipher has participated in the marketing and origination of Reverse Mortgages since 1993. He is President of American Reverse Mortgage® (ARM) which exclusively provides Reverse Mortgages and is one of the oldest and
largest Reverse Mortgage Lenders in the United States. W.L. is a nationally known authority and a frequent guest speaker on the Reverse Mortgage subject to various service clubs, senior organizations, mortgage associations and mortgage brokers interested in the Reverse Mortgage. He became a Certified Senior Advisor (CSA) in 2000 and has many other CSA’s on the American Reverse Mortgage® staff. The ARM website is www.AmericanReverse.com

Prior to entering the Reverse Mortgage field, W.L. was Customer Relations Manager for American Express Credit Card Division. He served as Northwest Region Trainer for Globe Life & Accident Insurance.

He is a member of the National Reverse Mortgage Lenders Association (NRMLA); Lifetime Member of the National Council On Aging (NCOA) and Associate Member of the American Bar Association (ABA).

W.L. is a Member of the Mortgage Bankers Association (MBA), a Member of and Past State Director of the Florida Association of Mortgage Brokers (FAMB) and Member of the National Association of Mortgage Brokers (NAMB).

Past State Vice President of the Florida Motion Picture & Television Association (FMPTA); Past Vice President of the Film Commission of Real Florida; FAA Licensed Commercial, Instrument & Multi-Engine Pilot; Lifetime Member Delta Sigma Pi; Charter Member Florida Atlantic University Parliament of Owls; Charter & Lifetime Member Florida Atlantic University Alumni Association.

W. L. holds degrees from Miami Dade College (A.A. Business Administration) and Florida Atlantic University (B.S. Business Administration). W.L. is a Florida Native, born in Miami, currently residing in Ocala, Florida, the home office of ARM.
