Kimberly Cheryl Elliott’s Summary

Manager with a wide variety of business experiences from Sales to Corporate Start up. Innovative and results-driven Senior Executive with twenty years’ of proven
successes. Strong business intuition with expertise in communications, leading people and organizations. A strong thought leader with outstanding leadership, collaboration and ability to drive results. Strengths include developing and executing a range of innovative marketing initiatives through advertising, promotions and public relations.

One of Kim's key principles is establishing and nurturing long-standing partnerships built upon mutual trust, respect, integrity and loyalty.

Currently focused on providing individuals, organizations and corporations Personal Safety seminars and self defense services. Specializing in women and teen issues as well as advising corporations on ways to keep their employees safe.

Interested in academic work as well, and educating on the ins and outs of the pharmaceutical industry and how we can change our current health care system for the betterment of all.

Have written two books: Escape from the Pharma Drug Cartel and Are Your Habits Killing You? Both are available at Barnes and Noble.com

Have been a guest on multiple radio shows and quoted in the British Medical Journal as well as on a variety of News Programs as an expert in my field of Safety/self defense.
