Michael Homula (mghomula@gmail.com)’s Summary

Michael Homula is the Owner and Chief Recruiting Strategist of Bearing Fruit Consulting. With over 13 years of corporate recruiting leadership, third party and consulting experience, he brings a proven and well documented track record of success and exceptional performance to business and ministry.

Michael Homula is an award winning and well documented leader of two corporate recruiting teams (back to back Recruiting Excellence Awards at ERE) and a successful and high billing veteran of third party recruiting. He delivers powerful and lasting results to companies and ministries of all sizes with proven solutions in the areas of recruiter training, recruiting strategy design and implementation, sourcing, recruitment process, retained and contingency search, performance / competency based interview and selection strategies / training and so much more.

Michael is a sought after key note speaker, seminar / conference presenter, recruiting trainer and inspirational speaker. He has been blessed to speak and share his story, testimony and recruiting knowledge and success with corporations, small businesses, ministries, churches and recruiting organizations all over the United States.

Finally, and most importantly, Michael is a Christian who realizes his gifts and talents are not his but, rather, a gift from God to be used for His purpose and glory. Michael knows that salvation and eternal life only come by faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour, that Christ's sacrificial death and resurrection is the only conduit by which all men and women can be forgiven and enjoy eternal life in the presence of a perfect and loving God. "The best and truest measure of my success in this life and on this earth is the amount of Jesus others see in me." - Michael Homula
