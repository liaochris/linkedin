Kamran Muzaffer’s Summary

A technology enthusiast known for creative thinking and analytical abilities.

Possess almost 7 years of experience in various areas of Technology innovation and maintenance, namely, Value Added Services (VAS), Systems, Networks and Systems Security. Also has good working experience in Project Management, VAS and enterprise IT solution designing, Information Security Management System (ISMS), IT infrastructure deployment and QMS documentation. Prior to current assignment, managed regional IT infrastructure, systems security and operations. Also worked as internal auditor for ISO review audits.

Apart from professional attachments, has been involved in different research and awareness initiatives in the field of IT, like Pakistan Honeynet Project and PakCon .

Co-founder of Pakistan Honeynet Project, a non-profit, all-volunteer organization dedicated to Honeynet research. Pakistan Honeynet Project’s goal is to learn and raise awareness about the motives and tactics of the Black Hat community targeting Pakistan's networks.

Also the Vice President of PAKCON, a non-profit organization which organizes yearly cyber security conventions in Pakistan. PAKCON is the brainchild of a group of capable security professionals who have employed their genius and aptitude to provide an overall extensive and comprehensive experience of information security in the form of a wide-ranging convention on information security.
