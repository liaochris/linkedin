Bob Corporaal’s Summary

As a freelance visual & interaction designer I work with clients to develop ideas and concepts into fullgrown websites. In this I take an integral approach balancing the different ingredients and stakeholders. My focus is on creating great user experiences keeping in mind that usability and brand experience are essentially one.

With 6+ years of design experience and a background in aerospace engineering I am comfortable on both the design and the technical level. But most of all I'm curious: I enjoy working in multidisciplinary teams, combining knowledge from different fields, learning new things and exploring new ideas.

Bob Corporaal’s Specialties:

Concept development, Information, Interaction & user interface design, Visual design, Marketing & Branding, User experience, Usability, Actionscript 2, Actionscript 3, Technology trends, Social media, Curiosity & attention to detail
