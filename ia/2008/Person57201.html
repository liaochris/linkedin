Eid Eid’s Summary

Eid is an accomplished technology leader with more than 27 years of hands-on experience in a wide range of computer technologies, including developing graphics and desktop publishing software, designing Linux-based devices targeted at the Broadband Internet market, developing scalable software-as-a-service, and deploying IT infrastructures. Eid has built world-class software development teams and developed many award-winning products.

~ In his last tenure at MarketTools (Jan-Oct/06), Eid held the position of Vice-President of Technology for the Zoomerang product line where he rebuilt Zoomerang's development team from scratch, and successfully released the first significant upgrade in 3 years.

~ Prior to MarketTools, Eid was President and CTO of Axentra (1999-2005), a startup he founded to develop and market Broadband Network Appliance solutions for OEM's built with open source technologies.

~ Before starting Axentra, Eid was the President and head of R&D at Corel Computer (1997-1998), creator of the NetWinder, a leading Network Server Appliance powered by Java and Linux. Prior to Corel Computer, Eid held many R&D positions at Corel Corporation (1989-1997), notably as Senior Vice President of Technology, leading a team of 1,100 professionals in charge of R&D, IT, and Technical Support for Corel DRAW, and WordPerfect lines of products.

~ Eid is fluent in English, French, and Arabic. He is a Project Management Professional (PMI), and holds a Engineering degree in Electronics and Computer Science from the "Ecole Superieure d'Informatique, Electronique, et Automatique", Paris France. Eid filed 6 patent applications for his work at OEone. He also serves on the Board of Protus IP Solutions.

Eid Eid’s Specialties:

Eid is an Information Technology entrepreneur with 27 years of experience in product development and hi-tech startups.
