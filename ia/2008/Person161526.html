Roi Hildesheimer SEO, SEM, SMM’s Summary

TENS (SEO & Web Marketing) is specializing exclusively in SEO (Search Engine Optimization), Web-based marketing and traffic-generating services. The SEO firm’s goal is to deliver the maximum possible amount of targeted Web traffic using SEO, SEM (Search Engine Marketing), SMO (Social Media Optimization), SMM (Social Media Marketing) and other web marketing strategies.

The SEO & Web Marketing company, active in the SEO field since 1998, employs a team of engineers and SEO analysts educated and trained in information theory and technology, complex systems theory, linguistics, industrial intelligence, business administration, technical writing and web marketing.

This team’s extensive experience, combined with its wide-ranging skills and expertise, positions TENS (SEO & Web Marketing) as one of the top Web marketing and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) firms in the world today.

Roi is leading TENS (SEO & Web Marketing) since 2005 after serving as CEO of Match Bio Systems, a start-up in the biometric identification industry, where he gained extensive experience in executive management with leading communication technology companies in numerous countries. Roi holds a B.A. in Technology Marketing and Management from the Jerusalem College of Technology.
