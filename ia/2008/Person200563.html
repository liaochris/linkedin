Charles Moore’s Summary

I work with VCs, BoDs, and CXOs to recruit the top 5% in talent who are movers and shakers in wireless infrastructure and mobile content. If you are a startup or small/mid sized company experiencing high growth, I can help. For 18+ years I have been recruiting Executive Decision Makers, Revenue Drivers, and IP Builders.

• CEO, CTO, COO, CFO, CMO, EIRs
• VP Product Management and VP Strategic Marketing
• VP Engineering - End-to-End Systems, Platforms, and SW Apps
• Partner and Sr. Principals Executive Technical Management Consulting
• Rainmakers - Sales and Business Development

I recruit in North America, Europe, LATAM, and APAC in:

• mPayment, mBanking, mGaming, Mobile Content Platforms/Apps
• Fixed Mobile Convergence, IP Multimedia Subsystems - IMS
• Mobile Social Networking and Mobile Search
• Radio Access Network systems, RFIC, NFC, Handsets

Potential Candidates:

If you are an exceptional talent with more than 7 years experience combined with a proven track record of accomplishments in the wireless industry, AND your peers consider you to be among the top 5% in the industry, perhaps we can talk.

Hiring Managers:

I work on only 3-4 search assignments at a time. Quality over quantity is the rule. You get the priority and commitment of my time AND the assurance the right candidate will be provided. That means you receive only the Top 15% in candidates (no flypaper, maybe's, or wanna-be's).

Confidentiality and Trust

On both sides of the fence. I don't reveal the candidate name or contact info to a client nor will I reveal the company name to a candidate until I am absolutely sure both parties are interested in each other’s capabilities, track record, and opportunity..

I've been fortunate to be interviewed for several publications, including Wall St. Journal, RCR, Xtreme Recruiting, Wireless IT World, Computer World, CNET, Wireless Review, and others.

Charles Moore’s Specialties:

My network of contacts in the wireless industry spans 18+ years. You receive an in-depth complete confidential profile using situational & hypothetical interviewing techniques to reveal tangible accomplishments in experience and skills; as well as the intangibles on problem-solving techniques, leadership, execution/project management style, cultural/product lifecycle fit.
