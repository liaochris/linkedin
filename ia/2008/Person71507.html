Gordon Coe’s Summary

Proven project manager with extensive experience of managing multiple projects to time and budget in both online and offline channels for uSwitch.com and British Airways. Key deliveries have included Online Check-in and the complete redesign of both the uSwitch and BA.Com websites and their underlying architecture.

Proven delivery skills throughout the product lifecycle. Hard working, eager to understand and learn new technologies and able to deliver whilst under pressure. Succinct and articulate both orally and in written form.
