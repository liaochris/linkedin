Bronagh Hanley’s Summary

After nearly 20 years in business, from nonprofits and multi-million dollar international companies to start ups and public relations agencies, I started Big Noise PR to provide strategic and sound public relations counsel to a wide variety of clients whose businesses I am passionate about. My expertise is in events, partnerships, media relations and brand marketing. Prior to branching out on my own, I was director of public relations for Netflix, the online movie rental company and oversaw all of their consumer outreach. My career has revolved around public relations and all that entails, from the launch of home improvement show Trading Spaces to deflecting a massive PETA protest against a previous employer.
