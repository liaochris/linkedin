Sergio Marrero’s Summary

Industrial Engineer with experience is in the manufacturing sector and business management. I have a direct familiarity with materials flow, layouts, automation and quality control. However, I believe my strength lies in business strategy and team management inside and outside of a corporate setting. I have dealt with management training, organizational development, portfolio management, streamlining business processes and managing a core team of individuals toward a common purpose in my summer internships at P&G and J&J as in my extracurricular activities leading the campus wide initiatives and events.
