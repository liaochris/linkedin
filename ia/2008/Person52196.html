Daniel Green’s Summary

Entrepreneurial spirited and creative sales executive and international consultant/sales trainer in the leisure real estate industry. Co-founder of Trooval.com which is due for alpha launch in September specializing in lead generation for the timeshare and fractional industry with a twist.

Trooval turns “Time” into a commodity that you can trade for goods and services. That “Time” is bought by marketing companies that want to target you and your specific demographic for face to face sales presentations, product reviews etc. As it is your “Time” that these companies are paying for, there are no obligations on your part other than the time that you have committed to the marketer for the presentation – once that time is over, you can collect your promised incentive.

Trooval.com brings permission based online marketing to the timeshare industry. Through our unique and patent pending application we enable marketing departments to target specific segments of the population and reward them according to their demographics. Therefore companies are able to reward more highly segments that have historically proven to purchase at a higher rate.

With over 14 years in the international timeshare market and working on all continents, Daniel is a respected sales trainer and consultant with Levitin Group - the largest timeshare and fractional consulting company in the world. Levitin Group's sales strategies and distinctions have shaped the industry and lead to higher sales and a number of best practices being adopted for the betterment of the industry as a whole.

Combining a love of sales with a passion for quality lead generation has lead to Trooval. Sales and Marketing can work cohesively together by taking the best from both departments and putting it online, creating a destination for consumers and marketers alike.
