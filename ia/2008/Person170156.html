David Savio’s Summary

Approaching 20 years extensive and continuous professional experience in Greater China - including business and market development in the areas of pharmaceuticals and medical devices (orthopedics, diagnostic radiology, minimally invasive surgical, pulmonary, rehabilitaton, patient care); project management and engineering consultancy for Fortune 500 multinationals; light industrial technology and process automation (food & beverage, textiles, construction materials), as well as FMCG.
A founding member of China's National Hospital Management Association for Radiology (2003).
Fluent in Mandarin Chinese and spoken Cantonese, have travelled extensively throughout the country and the region.

Pursuing strategic opportunities among China's growth industries, eager to continue to experience and contribute to China's economic and social development.

PERSONAL BIO: With my Cantonese wife, we are proud parents of three sons and a daughter, all living and being educated in Shanghai. A Seattle-native, my family is deeply multi-cultural, and have a profound appreciaton for the 21st century American-Chinese experience. Shanghai is the center of the universe, and China will remain the most important emerging market of our lifetime.

David Savio’s Specialties:
