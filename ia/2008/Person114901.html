Larry Tazuma’s Summary

I've been a newspaper boy, a busboy, a baker, a Domino's Pizza deliverer, and a Transportation Technician for the DOT. Even sold clothes for three years at the mall before starting my first writing job as a reporter at the Northwest Nikkei, a small community newspaper.

From there, I became a Broadcast Liaison at the 1990 Goodwill Games in Seattle, working the Track & Field and Cycling events. I then interned at KIRO-TV under anchor Aaron Brown, and soon after, became overnight Assistant Producer.

In July of 1992, just after the riots, I moved to Los Angeles, and even lived in South Central for 18 months in 1994-96.

It was in 1994 when I helped start (and coin) a magazine called Yolk and became its Editor in Chief. Soon after, did double duty as Associate Editor of KoreAm Journal. Some years later, I served as Senior Editor of Asia InPlay magazine, and Producer and Host at Radio Korea.

Among the ad agencies I've toiled at: Kang & Lee, K&L West, Terry Hines & Assoc., Eclipse, Team One, PanCom Int'l and now with the Omnicom Group, working with BBDO West, Organic and Javelin.

I've also tried my hand at producing, working on 10 TV commercials for K&L West, and the staged show "Exit the Dragon" for Eric Zee and Ming Na Wen.

Served as copywriter and proofreader at Artisan Entertainment/Lions Gate Films for three years. Among my DVD cover writing: Conan O'Brien's 10th Anniversary Special, Best of Triumph the Insult Comic Dog, Roger Dodger, Ginger Snaps 3, WWII: Road to Victory, Good Advice.

Current freelance clients include Princess Cruises and Motion Picture Editors Guild. I've also published hundreds of articles in Asian Week, Yolk, KoreAm Journal, Rafu Shimpo, Northwest Nikkei, North American Post, NY Times, NY Times Syndicated, LA Times, and others.

You can also see me in the following commercials: Safeway/Vons, Orange. Also appearing in print: TIAA-CREF, Riverside Financial, Wells Fargo, Microsoft.

Film, TV & Home Video; Automotive; Cruise Ship/Travel Industry; Asian American, Japanese American & Korean American; HMO; Frozen Yogurt; Telecommunications; also prolific as an actor/model/spokesman in TV commercials, industrial videos, and print ads.
