Amy Levy’s Summary

I have led a varied professional life.

I currently design and sew clothing, costumes, and corsets for my own company, Spider Threads. I have not always been a professional tailor, however.

I have managed my own software consulting firm. I worked for several years as a programmer/senior manager for iHigh.com. After earning a degree in Paralegal Studies, I provided pro bono legal assistance for Legal Aid of the Bluegrass. I have taught both adult education and college level courses.

I also write, and am currently seeking an editor for my first novel, tentatively titled "Vignette."

Previous last names include "Gerrish" and "Hall," provided for those who do not recognize my married name.
