Project Manager / Facilities Manager / Certified Plant Engineer with over 22 years of facilities management / engineering / project management experience. Demonstrated record of maintaining and operating large high-tech facilities over 750,000 square feet in the Telecommunications and Defense industries. Multi-State construction project management experience exceeding $10M
