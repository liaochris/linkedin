Eric Moncaleano’s Summary

I've been in advertising for roughly 18 years.

I am currently a SVP/Group Creative Director at Publicis-Dallas, where I lead a team of creative professionals with the responsibility of developing award winning communications ideas/executions in a variety of diverse media for clients such as BMW, Powerbar, Sauza Tequila (Jim Beam) and GlaxoSmithKline.

Past clients include Coors Light, Midway Entertainment (video games: Doom and Mortal Kombat), Ziosoft, John Deere, Boeing, Miller Light, Dewars, Progressive Insurance, Subway Restaurants, Bally Total Fitness, PocketCard, Nuveen Investments, Matrix Hair Products, Motorola, Johnson & Johnson and Career Builder.

Eric Moncaleano’s Specialties:

Creation of strong core ideas which lay the foundation for all forms of branding, communication and media executions.

Eric Moncaleano’s Interests:

I am interested in photography, surfing, video games, new technologies (I'm a gadget freak), playing guitar. I'm also a big fan of art (museums and gallery exhibits) and music (alternative, rock and rap) including my recent facination with opera.

Eric Moncaleano’s Honors:

I've won my fair share of awards including: Clios, One Club/One Show, The WIN (Women's Image Network) Awards, Creativity, NY Film Festival, Dallas Ad League, Local, Regional and National Addys. My work has also appeared in numerous publications and online sites such as: Adweek's Creative Best Ads (spot of the week and month), Creativity's AdCritic, Luerzer's Archive Magazine, ADME.ru

I was also a judge at the 1st Annual National Student Show and Conference for the DSVC (Dallas Society of Visual Communications).
