My Focus & USP:

• Human brain has mainly three quotients viz. IQ, EQ and SQ. However, majority of us just concentrate on the development of IQ and EQ. The commercial world is not fully aware of the power of SQ (Spiritual Quotient). There exists a myth among people that Spiritual Intelligence is mainly to be used by Monks / Saints.

• The mind functions in two ways viz. Conscious and Sub-Conscious.

• The mind has two ways of thinking viz. Right Brain (mainly SQ and EQ based) and Left Brain (mainly IQ based).

• I chose to focus on SQ development to explore the hidden potential behind Sub-Conscious mind by giving weightage to Right Brain thinking process.

• My training program also includes few exercises on ‘Pranayam’ (Breathing exercises) and ‘Meditation’.
