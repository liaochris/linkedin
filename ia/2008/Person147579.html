Paul Donovan’s Summary

Started age 7, taking apart tosters, tv's, telephone jacks, radios, and computers. From there I learned Basic on a commodore64 with a cassette tape drive. I fell in love with programming and decided to continue my career path with dismantling things, so I learned how to work on automobiles - removing the engines, replacing them with new parts, replacing transmissions, brakes, with the exception of the body. From there I read all the books that I could get my hands on about computers, programming, marketing, mailing, psychology, management, and other miscellaneous stuff to enhance my learning. I even wrote out most of the dictionary so I could understand the technical words and concepts that were vague at the time.

My goals are simple. Spend enough time with my family before my last breath. Put all of my children through College. Make friends along the way. Help out people. Pay it forward. Smile at least 40 times per day. Look up at the sky and not stop dreaming of bigger and better things. Help someone less fortunate than me Start a scholarship fund for kids that went through the same struggles that I did, Pray everyday that the world would be a better place.
