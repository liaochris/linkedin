Director of Corporate Chameleon.

Corporate Chameleon is an executive research and digital marketing consultancy servicing the recruitment industry.

The company was formed as a proactive solution to the ever-increasing talent shortage that all recruiters are experiencing. Now, more than ever, recruiters across all disciplines are seeking alternative methods to source and secure candidates for their clients.

We have a highly skilled research specialist leading our team of researchers. He has over eight years experience in researching and head hunting across most disciplines within commerce, industry and government. We embrace emerging technologies on top of time-proven methodologies, providing recruitment companies with direct access to the international passive candidate pool. This gives our clients a distinct competitive advantage over their competitors without investing the time, resources and ongoing costs of building an in-house research division.
