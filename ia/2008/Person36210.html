Christian Lutz’s Summary

Currently I am invested in three startup-companies in the consumer internet business. I am active chairman for Fatfoogoo.com an exiting new business that provides services to the gaming industry. Two other companies are currently still in stealth mode developing their prototypes. Before that I was CEO of Azzurro Investment in Switzerland, a small investment company founded in 2003, which basically invests in early stage internet technology businesses. After studying on the Technical University of Vienna I founded CMB Informationslogistic in 1994, which was a software business for digital image archives and interactive media. In 1996 I sold the business to the leading Austrian publisher group and helped to create Austria Online (Portal, ISP, Adnetwork) as CEO. Early 1999 I left Austria Online to create the VC-funded (42mio Euro in 3 rounds) ucp, which invented uboot.com and sms.at (6 million users, 180 employees). In 2001 we carved out ucp morgen to become a leading software business for the mobile entertainment and messaging industry. In 2003 the profitable consumer portals were sold, and I founded the investment company called Azzurro Investment in Switzerland. In 2003 I took the CEO position of ucp morgen and merged ucp with Qpass Inc. Seattle in Jan 2005, creating the leading player. In April 2006 Qpass was aquired by the public company AMDOCS for 275m$ and Qpass was integrated into Amdocs as standalone division, whcih I left after a couple of months. I continued my work as President in Azzurro Investment, which I did until I sold my shares in January 2008, allowing me to focus exclusively on my nurtured startups. Together with excellent teams I created and managed various successful businesses and could experience the exciting internet & wireless market from the very beginning and still we are in the middle of the storm. I am happy and proud to help a handful of promising technology start-ups, so we will see what will happen next.
