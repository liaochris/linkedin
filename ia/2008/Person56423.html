Eric Dirst’s Summary

I am currently the CIO of SIRVA Inc., but most people know us by our brands, which include SIRVA Relocation, Allied Van Lines, North American Van Lines, Pickfords, et al. At SIRVA I have the pleasure of experiencing both the aspects of a Financial Services firm (SIRVA Relocation) and a Transportation/Logistics business (Allied, etc.). My scope is Global, with IT operations in the U.S., Canada, U.K. and Australia. Our IT solutions support >5000 internal employees, and >4000 external Agents. We call this combination Relocation Redefined. My goals are always to improve the performance of the IT organization in the eyes of our customers, and employees. Performance in IT means many things, but the primary focuses for me are project execution, business process automation, business intelligence, infrastructure scalability and stability, customer focused applications, security, and budget control.

Eric Dirst’s Specialties:

Some of my specialties include such things as large scale document imaging (60k/docs per day), mobile devices with wireless connectivity to servers/mainframe and bar-coding technology, business intelligence portals using BusinessObjects, disaster recovery and business continuity, SOX and PCI compliance, client-facing web portals, and BPM solutions. I have extensive experience integrating IT platforms and applications post acquisitions, and extracting IT post divestitures.
