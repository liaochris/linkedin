Geoffrey Green | Open Networker | LION | Toplinked |’s Summary

Geoff Green is an accomplished Program & Project Manager with progressive experience in Product Design, Business Development, Aerospace Systems, and Information Technology. Geoff has managed numerous and diverse programs and projects in varying scope, budget, and schedule. He has rescued many programs from certain failure and has used sales and negotiation skills to successfully renegotiate program and project commitments with strategic clients. Geoff has also created additional business areas and products for companies that have totalled in the $Millions of new revenue for Employers.

He taught Effective Presentation Skills as part of GE's Corporate Training Program to over 500 students. Geoff uses those same skills to present and negotiate programmatic and technical issues with key strategic business clients.

Geoff is active in the local Phoenix PMI Chapter as Director of the Corporate Outreach program. Corporate Outreach is an initiative to improve the level of Program and Project Management usage within all sizes of companies and all types of product and services. In addition to the benefit of improved Program and Project Management, Geoff is instrumental in helping companies develop and benefit from a strong Program Management Office (PMO).

He has a history of improving process even before "process re-engineering" became a buzzword. Using skills in defining and improving processes enabled Geoff to improve the Software Engineering Institutes maturity assessment rating of a large corporation across 3 major business sites involving several thousand employees. Geoff is currently formalizing this expertise with a Six Sigma Green Belt certification.

Geoff and his family of 4 - wife Liz of 14 years, son of 6 years, and 3 year old daughter - live in Phoenix Arizona. They are open to new job and relocation opportunities.
