Felipe Mattos’s Summary

I was graduated from Metodista of Piracicaba University, Piracicaba with bachelor in Broadcasting. My specialization is 3D Lighting. During the last five years I have been working and learning lighting (color light, shadows, among others atributes of light), modeling, tracking, composing in Maya and feel confident in their environment. I have a very good knowledge of Maya, Mental Ray, Render Man for Maya, Turtle, Photoshop, After Effects, Digital Fusion, Boujou, Matchmover, Zbrush and Modbox. Currently I am working for Vetor Zero Studios (www.vetorzero.com), as lighting TD for TV comercials. My responsibilities include lighting, modeling, texturing and composing. I am confident in creating environments and lighting them and different situations of light, as you can see in my site.

Felipe Mattos’s Specialties:

Lighting (color, shadow map and ray tracer,etc), Modeling (polygons and surface modeling), Shading (mental ray shaders, Maya shaders, prman shaders), Rendering (final gathering, global illumination, image based light\ HDRI light setup, render passes, and so on), Tracking (Boujou, Match Mover), Composing (After Effects, Digital Fusion)
