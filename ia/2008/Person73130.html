Tim Hayden’s Summary

For the last 15 years, Tim has been involved in sports and interactive media, and his experience in marketing, sales, management, promotions, training, design and product development covers many diverse areas and facets. Throughout his career he has gained invaluable knowledge of the inner workings of professional sports organizations. In addition, he is able to pass on his experience to the students of St. Louis University where he is an Adjunct Professor teaching Introduction to Entrepreneurship and the capstone Advanced Business Planning class. For the last 10 years, SLU’s Entrepreneurship program has been ranked in the top 20 national programs.

Originally, Tim started his career at United Parcel Service as a Business Development Intern working his way up in five years to become head of Strategic Planning for the Missouri District. Tim was involved in the development of the organization’s business plans, market plans, and strategic plans. In addition, Tim was responsible for advertising, promotions, sports marketing, and creating the training program that taught new college Interns the ins and outs of selling UPS’ premium services.

Over the years, Tim has been on the cutting edge of emerging mobile, interactive technologies while creating interactive in-game experiences for sports teams from Anaheim, CA to New York, NY. The extremely talented teams he worked with pushed the envelope in all facets. Through the proper implementation of these projects, Tim was able to see first-hand how clients were able to reduce costs, increase return on investment, streamline processes, and enhance efficiencies.
