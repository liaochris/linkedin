Terry Otsubo’s Summary

I am extremely interested in coding. People say it is boring, just sitting in front of a computer typing away. I love it. I love to code. Ever since I was introduced to Basic and Apple Turtle, I was amazed at what I could do with a few lines of code. "Hello World," was amazing to me. So, you can imagine that ColdFusion and SQL Server simply blew me out of the water! I can't get enough of it.

I like to do line art. I should say, "I love to do line art." Nothing makes me happier than drawing in Adobe Illustrator. Making something that makes people say, "You did that from scratch?"

I have an iPhone and started to develop applications for it using XCode. I have to say it is different from what I do now, but the ideas and concepts are the same. I love ColdFusion and PHP. It allows people to think and develop... whatever they want. XCode gives me an excuse to run solely on my iMac. I was pleasantly surprised when Apple released their SDK. I downloaded it as soon as I got home and went to work on it.

I hope to eventually become an extremely good "XCoder".
