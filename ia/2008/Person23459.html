Bill Ringle’s Summary

At LearnWell, we unlock the growth potential of companies.

Because that takes a variety of forms, let me give you a few examples.

- Sometimes an executive will engage us because a sales team is not performing well and they need to assess the knowledge/skill/awareness gap, create a customized development program to bridge the gap, and experience the difference in measurable results;

- Sometimes a division head will come to us to straighten out a team that has been derailed or is consumed with a distraction and needs to get back on a productive track towards common goals and tangible results (happens more often than you imagine!);

- Sometimes a marketing head will ask us to design and facilitate a meeting/retreat to draw out and synthesize the best thinking on how to develop a go-to-market strategy or overcome a sales slump;

- Sometimes a business owner will ask us to conduct an on-site educational meeting and then follow-up with a series of coaching calls to reinforce the content and help shape the desired new behaviors.

We engage with the P/L manager responsible for a unit, the President/Managing Director/Vice President/C-level executive who can articulate both the current situation as well as the desired changes and outcomes.
