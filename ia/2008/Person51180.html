Diane Ring’s Summary

I have coached and consulted with senior level to C-level leaders across all disciplines and for a variety of industries to include service, utilities, manufacturing, consumer product, and not-for-profit.

My passion for creating strategies to thrive in this rapidly changing global economy anchors my coaching approach. I assist leaders to strategically expand their potential and skills to meet the inevitable new challenges organizations face today. Key to my client's success is supporting them to: have a deep understanding of their organization’s culture and industry, take responsibility for their own growth and expand how they apply their talents to impact change that makes a positive difference. I have been described as approachable, supportive and inspirational; challenging clients to explore complex issues to create maximum success, both professionally and personally.

Diane Ring’s Specialties:

Experienced designing coaching solutions for individuals and groups with the following areas:

• Strategic leadership coaching for high performance
• Coaching fast track future leaders
• Assimilation Coaching for leaders entering a new role/organization
• Coaching Senior women leaders
• Career Fit and Transition
• Customized coaching engagements integrating several learning strategies such as multiple coaches, mentoring, action learning, and learning partners
