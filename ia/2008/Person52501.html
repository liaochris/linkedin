David Sears’s Summary

Offering a track record of stellar results in the planning, design and execution of business strategies producing more than $1.6 billion in new revenue and $127 million in profit combined. Innovative leader with an MBA and 5 years of management consulting experience in retail and other industries. Six Sigma Blackbelt. Multilingual in Korean and Spanish. Solid financial, P&L, and operations expertise. Fluent in statistics. Core Competencies:

• Team & Project Management: Managed over $600 million in high visibility projects and processes across internal/external business functions in national/international corporate and entrepreneurial organizations. Projects range from flagships to multi-unit rollouts, retail to aerospace, construction, and high-end to mass-market projects.
• Finance, Profit & Performance Improvement: Created comprehensive financial analyses and reporting tools designed to illuminate profit opportunities, and enable additional revenue capture. Salvaged previously unprofitable operations by process improvement methods that eliminate waste, & stimulate dramatic market growth and profits.
• Strategic Planning & Operations: Spearheaded strategic alliance negotiations to increase long-term market share and global positioning. Simplifies complex situations, rapidly analyzes global to local markets.
• M&A Experience: Supported successful acquisitions and post acquisition transformation, includes AlliedSignal’s $300 million purchase of Lycoming; General Motors $350 million sale of Allison to CD & R.
• Value Proposition Development and Training: Created programs and executive training to establish industry leadership. Provided teams the tools for a competitive edge. Disseminated pro-active methods and best practices.
