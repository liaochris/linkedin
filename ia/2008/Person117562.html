mario chaves’s Summary

As a Silicon Valley veteran and software industry executive with a Latin American background, Mario speaks at conferences and events throughout Latin America on outsourcing and technology sector regional competitiveness.

For the past 10 years, Mario has led the growth of Avantica Technologies, a software development and QA services company focused on Outsourced Product Development (OPD) and serving clients all over the US from its development centers in San Jose, Costa Rica and Lima, Peru. Under Mario's leadership, Avantica has become one of the premier nearshore OPD providers serving the US market.
