John E. Haines’s Summary

Senior technology executive with strong sales and engineering background. Schooled in sales & marketing in large corporate environment in IBM and Sprint, followed by 15 years of international market success with technology products. CEO of four companies and took two companies to IPO on the NASDAQ exchange. Successfully brought technologies from identification of market need to R&D, quality certification, manufacturing and international distribution. Awarded two patents for telemedicine technologies. A rare combination of sales and engineering leadership, coupled with extensive P&L experience.

John E. Haines’s Specialties:

Business development, mergers and acquisition, P&L management, product development, FDA certification, IP networking, VOIP, manufacturing, quality control, medical devices, turn around. Fluent in English, German and Spanish.
