Ted Hodgins’s Summary

Marketing executive with 17 years experience in cable marketing and product management. 20 years working in the television industry - production, programming, promotion, marketing and results analysis. Strong background in ON DEMAND and PPV programming. Manage Video On Demand product including content, distributors, product performance and launches for 5 regions. Extensive experience in video including P&L management, product strategy, pricing, new product development and new product trials.

Ted Hodgins’s Specialties:

New Media & Video Product Development, Video On Demand, Pay-Per-View, Business Development, Marketing, Programming
