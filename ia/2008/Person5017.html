I have the desire to solve problems. I enjoy solving problems. I have the ability to hone in on what the buyer's problem is and offering suggestions that will solve the problem with respect to what products or services I sell.

I have the attitude that I can do just about anything that I put my mind to. I do not cower from meeting or talking to people or trying something new.
