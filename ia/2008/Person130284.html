Michael Dolan’s Summary

Michael helps David Allen Company clients around the world achieve breakthroughs in productivity, focus, and effectiveness through one-on-one workflow coaching. He has been a champion of the principles and practice of GTD since 2000, when he first participated in a two-day seminar led by David. Clients describe Michael’s style as authentic, supportive, present and focused.

Informed by more than a decade of management experience, Michael understands the intense organizational demands that successful executives must overcome on a daily basis. Prior to joining The David Allen Company, Michael held key positions in advertising, marketing management, and design strategy, at companies such as Ogilvy & Mather, General Mills and Clorox.

He holds a BA in Philosophy and Communications from Boston College and an MBA with concentration in Marketing and Organizational Behavior from the Stephen M. Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan.

Michael enjoys cycling, international travel, and playing guitar. He, his wife AnneLise, and their daughter Mari currently live in Marin County, California.
