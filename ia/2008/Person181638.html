Steven BerkHolz’s Summary

Steven BerkHolz is the Manager of Information Systems of Hirotec America, an engineering and tooling provider for the automotive Industry. BerkHolz has been responsible for all of HIROTEC AMERICA’s computer, network and telecom needs since 1995. BerkHolz started out in the tool and die trade as designer and builder of progressive dies. He later went into the special machine design arena. As machine design moved from paper to computer, he found himself having to support the software and computers in addition to designing machines. He later sought the opportunity to do Information Technology work full time when he joined HIROTEC AMERICA. BerkHolz is a certified MCSE. He was on the board of the ANX (Automotive Network Exchange) users group and is currently on the board of the ConnecTech Greater Detroit technology professionals group - www.connectech.org.

About Hirotec America - www.hirotecamerica.com
Hirotec America began in 1988 and since then has become a world leader in automotive closure manufacturing tooling and technology. Since we made the commitment to specialize in tooling for stamping, hemming, and assembly equipment for automotive closure panels. HIROTEC AMERICA has been recognized as a premier supplier to OEMs and the Tier One market. Headquartered in Auburn Hills, MI, HIROTEC AMERICA has several facilities worldwide and employs over 400 people.
