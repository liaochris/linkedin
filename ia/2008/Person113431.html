Lianne van der Kruk’s Summary

Marketing & Communications professional with extensive (international) internet & media experience. I have built my experience in marketing, communications and operations management in the interactive media sector. From 1997-1999 I gained experience in website management in Russia, rebuilding the website for the international newspaper 'The Moscow Times'. At the start of the internet-hype in Russia we tried to be as 'multifunctional' as possible, building an international site with very limited resources.

After my Russian experience, I started to work with the largest Dutch online publisher ilse media. Here I have worked in marketing & communications. Corporate Communications, Consumer Marketing & Sales Marketing & Communications have been my main focus in the last 4 years.

In 2007 I changed jobs within the company and at present I work as Business Unit Manager for newrulez - the youth interaction company. In this position I combine my marketing & communications experience with a natural drive to create a 'result-oriented' operation. I manage operations and hope to build the Dutch market leader in youth marketing & media together with my team.

Lianne van der Kruk’s Specialties:

Marketing for multimedia brands. Consumer, B2B & Corporate Marketing & communications. Multimedia Project Management.
