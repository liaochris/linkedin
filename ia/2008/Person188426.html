Thomas Fox’s Summary

Thom Fox is a power systems engineer with 30+ years experience providing technical and management consulting to government, utility and industrial clients regarding the application of SCADA, IT and Automation Systems to their businesses in Australia, USA, Korea, South America, the Middle East and Asia.

Thom has client-side experience overseeing the development of an Energy Management Unit and planning strategies for a 10-year $14 million per annum SCADA Program. He has functioned as a SCADA Asset Manager to oversee SCADA Infrastructure capability, performance and management of SCADA Support activities.

Thom was previously responsible for the global ICT consulting and ICT Business Development for SKM. Thom has been resident in Australia for the past 24 years.

Thomas Fox’s Specialties:

Application of broadband technologies to reduce carbon footprint, Power Systems operations, SCADA, DMS and EMS Systems, Operator Training Simulators, set-up and equipping of utility Control Centres, Water Industry SCADA & Automation, Industrial Automation, Communications Systems, Business Development for Consulting; Nuclear and conventional power station security and access control; containment vessel plume-release modelling for nuclear applications.
