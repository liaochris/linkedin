Tomas Eric Nordlander’s Summary

I am an experienced manager with strategic vision and ability to execute. I lived and worked in a couple of countries, speak several languages (Swedish, German, English, and Spanish) and have extensive background from the ICT industry, ranging from a project manager to a senior manager.

In addition, I have experience as an entrepreneur, starting up and successfully running a technology company in Germany. I built corporate structures and sales and support teams from the ground up and have a history of delivering results based on long-range planning with a high degree of accuracy. I am also a Member of the Board for two electronics companies, one in Switzerland and one in Germany. In addition, I am a founding member and member of two UK think tanks. Currently I am working as research scientist and the Public Relation Officer at 4C

My responsibilities throughout my career includes: Business Management, Business Consulting, Strategic Decision Making, Marketing, Sales, Product Management, Policy Making, Customer Project Management, Research, and Public Relation.
