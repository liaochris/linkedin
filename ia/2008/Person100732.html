Justin Barton’s Summary

Justin specializes in developing creative communications programs that meet clients’ real-world marketing objectives. For over 8 years, he’s directed the execution of websites, newsletters, blogs, flash animation, print advertising, printed collateral, direct mail and more. Beyond being expert in marketing strategy, he’s also highly creative – an accomplished writer and designer – and highly technically savvy – with extensive experience in design software, flash development, and CRM /CMS system integration. When Justin’s not in the office, he’s probably cooking, traveling, taking photographs, fishing, camping, or all of the above.

4) In-depth understanding of the Software Industry, its programming languages and philosophies
