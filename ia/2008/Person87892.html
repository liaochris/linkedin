Jeffery To’s Summary

Jeff is passionate about entrepreneurship. He is looking for business partners with the same spirit in wireless, application development, and consulting for the retail, real estate, and finance industries. Actually, any entrepreneurial idea, no matter how crazy, is fair game.

Currently, as a Business Development Executive at IBM’s Retail EBO (Emerging Business Opportunity), an IBM ‘start-up’, Jeff is directly responsible for securing venture funding, and coordinating the efforts of the solutions, marketing, and development teams to bring new solutions to highly targeted customer segments. An initiative which he began in 2004 has become a major driver of growth for 2006 and beyond.

Jeff also leads sales pursuits of global marketers embarking on "Next-Gen Retail" projects. Jeff heads a thinktank focused on the intersection of technology, real estate, and the retail consumer experience.
