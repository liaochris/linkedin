Deryck Jones’s Summary

Deryck Jones has demonstrated exemplary leadership credentials in numerous high-tech business arenas. His background includes product development and management in global markets for both established and startup firms. He has a proven track record of building strong organizations, leading initiatives to increase customer value, and creating strategic vision to drive growth.

Mr. Jones’s early career work included the development of software systems for construction equipment and machine control. He served as Executive Director of Ameritech Colleges before being joined by a partner to start a consulting firm in the construction engineering industry. Spectra-Physics Laserplane recruited him to continue his work designing PC-based software systems. He remained with the firm, renamed Spectra Precision, after a buyout by Thermo Electron.

He joined the British firm OneMondayGroup plc, an IT public relations consultancy, in 2000, where he served as Group VP and CTO. With that work completed, he and partners formed eInternational Group, specializing in credit card processing for high-risk merchants. When industry rules changes eliminated their niche market, he joined Marathon Staffing Group as VP of Business and Product Development.

Mr. Jones blends a variety of attributes that make him an effective leader in today’s competitive business world. He is a ‘hands-on’ leader with a confidence that allows him to succeed in turnaround and rapid growth environments. He not only creates a vision to ensure success but also communicates it effectively to the appropriate stakeholders. He understands the full spectrum of business operations – and can ‘fit’ the pieces together to help a company grow and prosper.
