Len Baker’s Summary

Data architect with over 20 years experience in the development and management of enterprise-wide logical data models, the physical design of databases for OLAP and OLTP systems, the application of data administration standards and techniques, the implementation of data quality improvement measures, and technical project management. Core competencies include: enterprise-level data architecture experience; solid data warehousing experience; full life-cycle experience on large/complex enterprise projects; strong consulting and leadership abilities; strong logical data modeling & relational and multi-dimensional physical database design skills; competency in database administration of database management systems (Oracle, DB2/UDB, Teradata, SQL Server, etc.) in open systems and mainframe environments; working knowledge of leading OLAP tools; solid knowledge of data administration; ability to build consensus within a complex organization; fluent technical and business strategic orientation; collaborative management skills; strong technical/business communication skills; ability to establish and implement best practices.

Len Baker’s Specialties:

Data Warehousing, Data Modeling, Enterprise Data Architecture, Metadata Management, Master Data Management, Data Stewardship
