George Twiss’s Summary

As a seasoned business leader with a breadth of experience, George brings sales, marketing, and business development expertise to achieve customer satisfaction and business goals. In a 30-year career he has demonstrated an ability to develop priorities, instill focus, and bring hands-on executive leadership. George excels at building relationships of trust and credibility with customers and co-workers. He is a Certified Sales Executive [CSE] with strengths in building and leading sales teams. He is skilled in sourcing, training, and motivating. Also, he develops leveraged compensation plans.

In addition, George has transferred these same skills to the non-profit sector to grow sustainable revenues and profits through a social entrepreneurial model.
