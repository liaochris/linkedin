Steven Johnson’s Summary

My new venture combines the best of all my prior experiences over the past several careers.

I will be working in a State (MN) wide Policy level position with the Department of Vocational Rehabilitation Services, developing relationships with vendors, lenders, investors, training organizations, and government services to assist in increasing the opportunities and success rates of clients with disabilities who are pursuing starting or growing their own businesses.

This exciting opportunity will operate as a kind of internal consultant to the Department and will develop tools, resources, guidance and relationships which can assist the 150 Voc. Rehab. counseling staff, 20 Site Managers and 14,000 rehab clients improve their chance to consider starting and operating their own businesses, and how to evaluate their ideas, plans, and the challenges they will face.

As a Sr. Employment Counselor with HIRED, I was involved in high impact Life and Career Coaching and counseling activities for a well respected Non-Profit agency which provides innovative and comprehensive solutions for those facing economic transition.

Sr. Counselor and Trainer in the Dislocated Worker program. Trainer and life coach for several hundred individuals and their families, each year.

Entrepreneur in residence.
