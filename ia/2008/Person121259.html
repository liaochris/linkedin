Mario Rosato’s Summary

I have a long and consolidated experience in Marketing and Commercial development either of big and complex companies and small realities. Along my whole career I have reached consistent successes in the sporting goods industries as well as in other market segments (sunglasses, furniture, educational toys). I have a particular and solid experience in relationships with american companies working in Italy or in partnership with Italian companies. Besides that I have a long and consolidated experience in organization and management of big events, trade shows and specific campaigns and in creating and managing specific retailing concepts and shopfitting.
