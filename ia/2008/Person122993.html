Marlin Buckley’s Summary

I have had over 30 years of experience in various positions within the construction industry, beginning with trade apprenticeships in Electric, Carpentry, and Plumbing. I hold active registrations as a Licensed Electrical Contractor / Registered Master Electrician, Registered Master Plumber, and Fire Sprinkler Worker, in Philadephia, New Jersey, Delaware, and Wilmington. Registrations can be viewed on the various governmental licensing web sites.

Additionally, I am a journeyman carpenter, having specialized in millwork and architectural woodwork.

The tool boxes and bib overalls were retired some years ago, and I now provide experience based consulting services to the industry.

I have held managerial positions at several prominent construction firms, before entering into private consulting practice in 1997.

Marlin Buckley’s Specialties:

Engineering, Construction Cost Estimating & Bidding, Project Management, Agency Construction Management, Forensic Analysis, Expert Witness, Project Management, CPM Scheduling.

My Firm provides technical services to Builders, Specialty Contractors, Owners, Attorneys, Bankers, Insurance Adjusters, Surety Claims Managers, and others who need construction related technical support.
