Chris Adamson’s Summary

Chris Adamson is an internationally recognized data warehousing expert who specializes in dimensional data warehousing, also known as “star schema.” Mr. Adamson works with customers in all industries to develop data warehousing strategies, identify and prioritize projects, design solutions, and manage implementations. His customers have included Fortune 500 companies, government entities and software tool vendors.

A thought leader on star schema data warehousing, Mr. Adamson has authored two books on the subject and serves as a faculty member at The Data Warehouse Institute (TDWI). He blogs at StarSchemaCentral.com.

His book Data Warehouse Design Solutions (John Wiley and Sons, 1998) provides a series of case studies linking dimensional model design to key business objectives. Written with long time colleague Mike Venerable, it remains a top seller in its category. His second book, Mastering Data Warehouse Aggregates: Solutions for Star Schema Performance, was published in 2006.

Chris Adamson’s Specialties:

Data warehouse strategy, data warehouse schema design, dimensional modeling / star schema design, project definition and planning, implementation leadership.
