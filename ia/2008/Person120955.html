Marieke van Dijk’s Summary

Strategic thinker that enjoys creating high-end, interactive design solutions for a wide range of clients. Committed to Design, Network Research and Innovation

Marieke van Dijk is also co-founder of Anderemedia, a Dutch web agency, active since 2001 in creating innovative web instruments, and co-founder of Floatleft.org, a socially conscious group of independent designers and web developers providing services to NGOs and non-profit organizations.

Marieke is a principal affiliate of the Govcom.org Foundation, based in Amsterdam.

She holds M.A. in Design Research and a B.A. in Industrial Design from the Design Academy in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Marieke van Dijk’s Specialties:

Behavioral & Experience design, Industrial design; Information visualization; Rapid Prototyping
