Paul Merrill’s Summary

Freelance writer for over ten years. Extensive experience writing stories and scripts for video games. Would like to continue creating great games while expanding into different types of media.

Recent projects include:


"That's So Raven" (Nintendo DS)
"Cake Mania 2" (Sandlot Online)
"Power Rangers: Super Legends" (Nintendo DS)
"Mystery Quest" (Real Networks Online)
"Backyard Football" (all major consoles)


I've also spent the past two year performing stand-up comedy, working with artists like Tig Notaro (The Sarah Silverman Show), Brent Weinbach, Emo Philips and many more. You can view some video clips of my act at www.myspace.com/paulmerrill.

Paul Merrill’s Specialties:

Creating unique characters and writing quirky, funny scripts for games/TV/Film.
