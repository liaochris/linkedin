Todd Outten’s Summary

Serial entrepreneur with interest in the intersection of technology, cognitive science and philosophy. Applying research and development to digital media and "pull" distribution models for content distribution.

Extensive experience architecting, implementing and operating digital technology infrastructures for Media and Entertainment organizations: Hollywood studios; visual effects and computer animation facilities; broadcasters; and wireless operators.

Primary inventor on patents for Digital Rights Management, Distributed Content Delivery and Caching, and Consumer Intelligence Marketing and Advertising.

Todd Outten’s Specialties:

High Performace Technology, Business Development, and Digital Media.
