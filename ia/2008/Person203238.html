Mike Zeinfeld’s Summary

Mike Zeinfeld has worked in marketing for ten years in various industries, focusing on technology and marketing. Mike is currently chief operating officer of ReachMD, a medical communications start-up. ReachMD launched the first ever 24/7 national radio platform medical professionals... airing on XM Satellite Radio.

Prior to ReachMD, Mike worked in product marketing Abbott's renal care franchise. Before Abbott, Mike managed direct to consumer research initiatives for TAP pharmaceuticals. Mike also was a management consultant at ZS Associates - one of the top consulting firms in health care - focusing on pharmaceutical strategic marketing and sales force strategy. Additionally, Mike founded several business networking organizations, which together have over 10,000 members nationally
