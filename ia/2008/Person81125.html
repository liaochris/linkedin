Mike Gong’s Summary

I started in the Internet business in 1996, the wild west days of the Internet. In my 10+ years of experience (at press time), I've touched many sides of the business. I've worked the editorial/publishing side and have lived the life of page views, minutes spent, user interface design, and content development. I've also been involved with the advertising business and have experienced the pressures of conversion metrics, results-driven advertising design, and high-end sales pitch design (my current gig).

I think having been on both sides of the Content Development and Sales fence has given me a unique and incredibly flexible perspective on the business, the technology, and the design.

I'm confident that anyone I've ever worked with will agree that I am an easy to work team player with a disciplined work ethic. You don't stick around AOL for over a decade by not harnessing inner talent, being a valuable and flexible team member, and (I admit it) having a little bit of luck. And a sense of humor.

I love this stuff. I really do.

Mike Gong’s Specialties:

Graphic Design, Interactive Advertising Design, Web Design & Usability, Sales Development/Pitch Communications & Design, Motion Graphics, some mild scripting skills (I'm not a hardcore technologist -- but I have built CMS powered websites from the ground up).
