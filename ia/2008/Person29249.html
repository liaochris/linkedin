Bunny Brown-Scott’s Summary

As Recruiter Coordinator I am responsible for keeping up with sporting events in the area and working with 7-18 year olds that are wanting to reach their potential in the sports field. I started March 2008 with D1 and having been involved with sports all my life this company has been very beneficial for the young boys and girls that are involved with the program..

I also work with the individual as a life coach in helping them with getting scholarships to attend college. This I have done for 10+ years. Having been involved with the National Scouting Association, it lead me to forming my own business of helping young people obtain their dreams of playing college ball.

Bunny Brown-Scott’s Specialties:

Working with coaches at all levels and understanding young people. Having served in the Human Resources field for several years gave me tools in being able to talk to teenagers and be supportive in helping them make decisions that will be the best for them.
