Carmine Tufano’s Summary

Carmine has a discrete experience in telecomunications space with focus on Business Processes Optimization and Enterprise Service Oriented Architecture aspects.
He started his carrier and interests based on "SOA world" during thesis activities for first and master degree in Computer Science from University of Salerno.
During the final period of this experience he stepped into the consulting career in Accenture where he has been working on System Integration and Business Consulting assignments in the OSS/BSS domain, spamming from IT Strategic Plan Definition, IT Service Rationalization and Application Portfolio Rationalization Program using SOA approach methodology on BSS/OSS processes.
Main interests are finalized on application of SOA paradigm in Enterprise environments.

Carmine Tufano’s Specialties:

OSS/BSS Processes & Systems, Enterprise SOA architectures (BPM, ESB, Policy Manager, WSM, Registry, Repository, Metadata management repository...), eTOM, IT Architecture & Governance, IT Scouting & BenchMarking, Object Oriented Design and development, UML 2.0, GUI Design, C/C#, J2EE 1.4, Java based web technologies (JSP, Servlets, Hibernate, Struts, Axis for web service ), XML technologies (XQuery, XPath, XML Schema, DTD) and more other skills.
