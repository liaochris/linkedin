Jenaro Sanchez’s Summary

Business Developer, Promoter & Project Manager.
Particularly interested in new ventures arising from CAFTA to manufacture industrial goods in Central America for export to the US. CAFTA is the US-Central America free trade agreement.

Results-oriented executive/entrepreneur with 30+ years experience in multiple industries with emphasis in chemical industry, cement production and consulting.
Skilled in setting strategic and tactical direction, leading high-performance teams that cross both organizational and geographical boundaries.
Collaborative, ethical leader who can orchestrate many situations simultaneously at multiple levels

My local coordination was with plant's General Manager in Managua, Nicaragua, while global decisions and reporting was to Latin American headquarters in Brazil.
