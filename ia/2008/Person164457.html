Ronna Mee Brand’s Summary

Ronna Brand, 2007 President of the Beverly Hills Greater Los Angeles Association of REALTORS (BHGLAAR), owns and operates Brand Realty, Inc., an independent Real Estate Boutique based in Beverly Hills. Established in 1984, Brand Realty has kept a strong presence through the highs and lows of the industry by combining residential brokerage with relocation and leasing services.

Ronna is a skilled negotiator and charismatic communicator which insures a successful outcome for those who work with her. Alongside her belief of supporting community through charitable contribution and involvement, her outgoing personality, warm smile, and infectious laugh are engaging to everyone she meets.

Committed to promoting and maintaining the highest quality of standards set forth by her profession, Ronna has served two terms as a director and active member for the Beverly Hills Board of REALTOR’s Grievance Committee, and served as BHGLAAR’s Treasurer/President Elect before she was elected 2007 President.

In 2007 Ronna had the privilege of being Keynote Speaker at a Real Estate Symposium "Up, down or Sideway?" at the famous Ebell Theater. She was recognized by Los Angeles Business Journal’s “Women Making a Difference” as a nominee in 2008.

An independent professional, Ronna brings objectivity and fairness to the table and hopes to give the individual agent a stronger voice.

Ronna Mee Brand’s Specialties:

Ronna's strength encompasses representing sellers with discretion and pairing buyers with their ideal home in a community suited to their individual lifestyles.

During her year as President of the BHGLAAR, she tapped into her marketing expertise and created and produced the first ever “Donald J. Trump Award.” The event was attended by Mr. Donald Trump himself and more than 500 of Los Angeles’ finest real estate professionals.
