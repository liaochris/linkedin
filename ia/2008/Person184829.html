Sabrina Zaremba’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

More than 8 years of wireless experience in managing Mobile Value Added Services, including products such as SMS, MMS, Java Games, Wallpapers, Ringtones, Truetones, Fulltracks, Voice Mail and Video based services.

Launched innovative products in the brazilian mobile market such as the first Mobile TV (TIM TV Access) and the first Music Store and fulltrack offer (TIM Music Store).

Managed innovative marketing campaigns that leveraged both penetration and revenues of value added services.

Strong team leader with ability to identify new business opportunities; dedicated coordinator with focus on quality and innovation. Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.

Educational concentration in Advertising as undergraduate. Proficient in Excel and other components of Microsoft Office. Fluent in English. Knowledge in Spanish and Italian.
