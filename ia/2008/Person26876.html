Brett Allen’s Summary

I have been working in the advertising/online industry for over 15 years. As a lateral thinker working in these industries has feed my love for the big idea, problem solving and strategy development.

I’ve enjoyed developing a broad range of skills in client management, creativity, technology and media. My career has hit many milestones and roles including desktop publishing, studio manager, art director, business development, project management, online strategist and account direction. The benefit to me is knowing how to work with people from all facets of an creative or technical agency.

I have a passion for everything digital including websites, gadgets, eDM, Mobile, IPTV, pod/vodcasting, installations to the fast growing of array of delivery platforms. I also can be as passionate about traditional media such as TV, Press and Print. What really sparks my interest is the combination of the new and the old.

Over my career I’ve had the pleasure of working with some of Australia’s major brands such as ANZ, Telstra, GMC, GNC Livewell, Sportsgirl, Mitsubishi, National Australia Bank, Metricon, Berri, Mitre10, National Stroke Foundation, Aconex, Honda, Honda MPE, Mazda, Shell Asia Pacific, Tennis Australia, Australian Open, Orange, Hutchinson Telecommunications, TEAC and iSelect, to name a few.

I’d like to add more FMCG clients to the list in the very near future.
