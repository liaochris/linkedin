Jenelle Van Eynde’s Summary

In my current position I take part in a number of departments at SW Global Limited. My primary responsibility is to the CEO of the company, that being so I play a role with Human Resources, Corporate Development and Governance, and Finance. I also act as an office manager; working with Ghana specific employment laws, employee housing, internal and external client hosting, and policy development, particularly our company health policy.
This current employment is a way for me to gain experience working with a relatively young company (5-years) that is moving from small to medium in size (approximately 450 employees internationally). I am also gaining experience interacting with a number of international workers. SW Global Limited has headquarters in Ghana but subsidiaries in Nigeria (where the company began), Liberia, Kenya, Sierre Leone, Ghana, South Africa (beginning stages), Uganda, India, and Sri Lanka.

I believe that my experience with SW Global Limited will lead to larger professional goals of working within the international community. Although I am currently working in a private sector field, I would like to try my hand with a nonprofit in the future. At this point I have enjoyed volunteering with nonprofits related to peace education and efforts and international, student travel.

Jenelle Van Eynde’s Specialties:

Jenelle Van Eynde’s Groups:
