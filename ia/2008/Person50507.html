Deven Shah’s Summary

In 1994, I started my career at 17 by setting up my own stock investing and advisory business. I used to attend morning college and then head straight to Bombay Stock Exchange in the afternoon. After graduation I worked for about a decade with several companies in the area of software development, internet marketing, marketing communication & information design.

Life seems to have come full circle and I am currently leading the efforts of a start-up called Money Mentor. I am really passionate about educating investors and simplifying financial concepts in a layman's language.
