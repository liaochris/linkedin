Lior YEKOUTIELI’s Summary

I have over 18 years of experience in the telecommunications field, mostly from working with Comverse.
At Comverse, I served in various senior customer-facing and operations management roles, including the COO of Comverse Fun-Dial division, and Associate VP, heading the handset-clients product unit. Since 2006, I am working as part of the Comtigo team, representing hi-tech companies in the internet and telecom arenas in France.
Throughout the years, I accumulated vast experience in management of people from various cultures and disciplines, and in management of pre-sales, sales and post-sales processes.
Holding Israeli and French nationalities, I've been working closely with the French telecoms market during the past 10 years, accumulating important experience with the various players in this market, while appreciating and respecting the different business cultures.
