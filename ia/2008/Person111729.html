Lawrence Coburn’s Summary

Internet entrepreneur and consultant with expertise in user generated content, online community, and social network theory. Pioneer in the user generated content space, and founder of one of the first commercial entities made up of 100% user generated content. Author of SexyWidget.com - a blog about web widgets and distributed web strategy.

Currently taking on limited consulting projects.

Lawrence Coburn’s Specialties:

Internet publishing, search engine optimization, local search, consumer reviews, user generated content, online community, social network theory, widget design and distribution

Lawrence Coburn’s Experience

  • Founder / President

    RateItAll, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    February 1999 — Present (9 years)

    RateItAll is the world's first long tail consumer ratings Web site. Consumers publish and manage interactive ratings lists on any topic (e.g. Best Coffee Shop in San Francisco, Best Teacher at Shipley High, etc.). Any RateItAll rating list may be published on any blog or social network page via a RateItAll widget, and list publishers earn a 50% share of any advertising revenue earned. To date, more than 25,000 lists have been published.

    RateItAll offers an interactive rating widget for every item in its database, and is also available on Facebook:
