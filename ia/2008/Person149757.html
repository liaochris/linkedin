  • Partner

    Livingroom Concert Network

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Music industry)

    January 2008 — Present (4 months)

    Livingroom Concert Network is looking for people who love music and want to open up their homes to great musicians with a story to tell.

    The concept is originally from The United States, where “Livingroom Concerts” or “House Concerts” have been a natural part the concert activities for a lot of musicians for years. It´s a whole different way of experiencing an artist, and the intimacy of a private home gives both audience and artist a very unique musical experience.

    Livingroom Concert Network is a direct connection between artist and audience. The idea is to cut off all the unnecessary “businessmen” of the music industry such as managers, bookers, promoters and venues, and give the music back to the audience!

    Aarhus Teater er Danmarks største landsdelsscene, indviet 15. september 1900. Den østjyske landsdelsscene er opført i slutningen af 1890’erne på initiativ af en kreds af teaterinteresserede borgere i Århus.
