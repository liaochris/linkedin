Frank Lynch’s Summary

Available for board directorships in both corporate and large nonprofits. Various degrees of expertise in a comprehensive range of industries including all forms of finance, media, real estate and REITS, hospitality, manufacturing, mining and energy.

Also available for public speaking appearances.

Expert in all areas of finance including commercial and investment banking; private equity; all categories of insurance; real estate, land development and REITS; broadcast and print media; mining; technology; retail; franchising; restaurants and hotels; theme parks.

Candidate for President of the United States 2007-2008.

Founder of Frank Lynch University http://www.fluni.com and the http://www.franklynchfoundation.org, both named after his father.

Many years licensed in securities, insurance and real estate.

As an entrepreneur, created a new business and built it up into a publicly traded corporation which was sold to a larger company.

Senior Corporate Finance officer with major investment banks of Wertheim & Co. and Loeb, Rhoades & Co. working with a comprehensive range of industries to design, negotiate, manage & market IPOs and seconday public offerings, leveraged buyouts, restructurings and private placements.

National security and intelligence consultant. Projected likely military conflicts 20 years and more into the future to guide the development of advanced weapons systems appropriate to future situations. Forty years ago correctly forecasted the current situation in the MidEast.

College dropout at age 18, and National Science Foundation biochemical researcher at age 19, exploring the effect of early experience on adult adrenal corticosteroid response to stress.

Frank Lynch’s Specialties:

CEO and corporate directorships. Setting direction and strategies. Corporate development and restructuring to enhance innovation and competitiveness. Friendly and hostile mergers and acquisitions, venture capital, LBOs/private equity
