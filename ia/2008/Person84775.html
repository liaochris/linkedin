Jason Drakeford’s Summary

Currently I freelance at BBDO as an Art Director full-time and also freelance in motion graphics. A couple of motion projects that have recently come into fruition is a music video for the Cassavetes (http://vimeo.com/1732485) and a tv intro spot for SOPO Bike Co-Op (http://vimeo.com/1583338).

Most recently I've been filming in HD, learning new techniques in After Effects and streamlining the process of creating a quality video piece in a short amount of time without skimping on the concept or quality.
