Alex Miroff’s Summary

Mr. Alex V. Miroff, M.S., is a versatile high energy technology manager, strategist, motivator, innovator with a proven track record of developing and delivering integrated offerings and solutions in Health and Financial markets. Mr. Miroff has over 15 years in IT leadership and senior management roles.

Mr. Miroff provided leadership to the following government and industry projects:

• RevolutionHealth.com portal (one of the top health care portals worldwide)
• On-line Personal Health Record integration for a major Prescription Benefit Management company
• Consumer-facing electronic Health Insurance e-Store
• Clinical Trial and Clinical Research Data Center for NIH
• Electronic data collection and management system (1500+ medical centers, labs, repositories)
• Adverse Event Reporting System for the Division of Microbiology and Infectious Diseases, NIH
• Medical Coding System (WHO, MedDRA) for a biotech
• Data Mining and Quality System for a major CRO
• Bio-Specimen Tracking system
• SOA ORACLE implementation for a major financial agency
• AREDS (Age-Related Eye Disease Study), NEI NIH
• International AIDS Vaccine Initiative, Bill Gates Foundation
• Vaccine research center NIH
• Cord Blood Transplantation studies, NHLBI NIH
• Longitudinal Investigation of Fertility and the Environment, NICHD and CDC
• Blood and Marrow Transplant (BMT) Clinical Trials Network (CTN), NHLBI, NCI
• Studies of Pediatric Liver Transplantation, NIDDK, NIH
• Expanded Participation Project , Cancer Therapy Evaluation Program (CTEP), NCI

Alex Miroff’s Specialties:

Deep expertise in developing and delivering advanced integrated IT offerings and solutions in Health care, Life science and Financial industries.
