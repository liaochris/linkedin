Paul Courter’s Summary

Currently manage the Golf Digest Planner (www.GolfDigestPlanner.com), the first online planning tool and tournament shop for the 1.5 million golf events held in the U.S. each year. We developed the site in cooperation with Golf Digest, the leading name in golf.

The new tool is designed to help non-profits and corporation set up golf events in minutes including a tournament website for online player and sponsor registration. Only 3% of the tournaments in 2007 had this capability so the potential is tremendous. We will be taking the planner international in the coming months.

We have also launched the Golf Event Guide (www.GolfEventGuide.com). which is the first site for event organizers to promote their event to potential players and sponsors. In addition, we have the Official Golf Digest Tournament Course program.
