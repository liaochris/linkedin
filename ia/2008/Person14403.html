Ben Taylor’s Summary

Management consultant with wide-ranging experience, public sector focus.

Currently seeking clients, collaborators, new colleagues, and contacts.

Always looking to develop myself, am excited by international, innovative, interesting opportunities.

PwC 'Lean Six Sigma Black belt', have studied and applied many 'lean' approaches, systems thinking, learning organisations (esp Peter Senge), and organisational development approaches. Critically, this involves both common sense (doing what the customer wants and nothing else) - and some counter-intuitive facts about how organisations and people work.

Am proud to offer the revolutionary Power+Systems Organisational Workshop (from Barry Oshry - www.powerandystems.com) as part of our cutting-edge offerings.

Look out for another soon - diversity and the power and effectiveness it can bring to your organisation!

Have been inspired by the 'rants' of John Seddon (www.lean-service.com), and the very practical, yet fundamental transformation approach of Stephen Parry and co at Transform (www.transform4.com). As well as Senge and Oshry, preferred gurus include Taiichi Ohno and Brian Joiner.
