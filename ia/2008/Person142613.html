Nur Ahmad Furlong’s Summary

I have been involved with Graphic/Web Design, XHTML Development, CSS coding, Brand Development, Conceptual & Strategic Development, Animation, Illustration & Information Architecture.

I have designed logos, corporate identities and other printed promotional materials, developed brands, designed websites, flash emailers, cd-roms, flash games, Animated flash movies, digital spaces and online viral campaigns.

I design for print or digital media, static or motion, vector or pixel based. Art direction for both printed and digital media, Illustration both digital & traditional media, online & through-the-line campaign strategies.

I aim to develop myself in all aspects of media & communications.I have ambitions of using media in a ways which would serve to educate communities and spread awareness of issues affecting society.

I wish to partner with organizations which makes it their goal to be beneficial to those they serve and are always pushing the boundaries to find new and improved ways of doing things and solving problems for both their clients and society on the whole.

Nur Ahmad Furlong’s Specialties:

I Specialize in web design and have been focusing on blog development recently. Web 2.0 technologies and approaches are of particular interest to me in that they provide ways of empowering business and organizational activities much more efficiently and cost effectively than previous online strategies.

I believe in creating media & communications which is engaging, informative, rewarding and which has a positive effect on society.
