Dan Engstrom’s Summary

EXPERIENCE:
I have been in the computer industry for 9 years. Starting out as a PC parts inventory manager, worked my way into a novice technician position, became A+ Certified, became a field technician, worked at Office Depot as the Customer Service of Technology (CST) to gain customer service skills, started doing personal house calls after hours, received an instructor position at Boeing teaching the A+ Certification program for 2 years, opened my own PC repair shop (UFO COMPUTERS) which I closed after overhead got too high, tested XBOX games for Live (VOLT), worked with the Boeing upgrade to XP project as a 2ndary Lead, got hired on for a 1 year contract at Microsoft as a Lab Test Engineer (VOLT), and now I am working with Real Networks with the Real Arcade division as an Associate Producer (VOLT).

GOALS:
I am working on marketing the EZ Kids Net browser, and to have it become my 'nest-egg'. I see it as truely valuable and a necessity for households with young children who like to have fun on the internet. Anyone who would like to help in this process is welcome, and will be financially rewarded. I am also working on growing the Moonlightechs.com project, and to have it become a nation-wide service.

Dan Engstrom’s Specialties:

PC hardware troubleshooting is my specialty. I understand the workings of a PC, and have the experience and long-lasting list of customers to back it up.
