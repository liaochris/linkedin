Steve Tennant’s Summary

I help software company CEOs attract customers and increase revenues through business planning, product management, marketing and alliance services. As managing director of Tennant Consulting, I help startup companies grow their business from concept to customer. I also help larger companies build the appropriate team and manage the product, marketing or alliance operations where the current team is maxed out, and in interim management and turnaround situations. For more information, visit www.tennantconsulting.com

My non-profit affiliations also add value to my clients:

1) I founded the PeopleSoft Alumni Network, a corporate alumni group of 2000+ PeopleSoft employees (www.psftalumni.net). Over 900 recruiters and employers, including many of my clients, have use the PeopleSoft Alumni Network's services to hire former PeopleSoft employees.

2) I am co-chair for the East Bay Innovation Group's Startups/VCs SIG, www.ebig.org. The VC/Startup SIG provides monthly educational and networking sessions for entrepreneurs the second Monday each month.

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area's East Bay, and I typically work onsite at my clients a few days a week, and remotely other days. My California clients are or have been in San Francisco, Livermore, Emeryville, San Ramon, Petaluma, Redwood Shores, Santa Rosa, Milpitas, Mountain View, Newark, and Santa Clara. Outside California, clients have been in Boston, New York, and Lakeland, Florida and Hyderabad, India.

Have been a frequent presenter and speaker in the Bay Area, including UC Berkeley's Lester Center for Entrepreneurship, Stanford's Continuing Studies program, P-Camp (the first unConference for Product Managers), Startup Camp, and Technology Venture's Entrepreneurship Training Program. I have been quoted or published in the Wall Street Journal, Entrepreneur Magazine, Business Week Online, Microsoft Watch, all the major Bay Area papers and several trade and industry publications.
