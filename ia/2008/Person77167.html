Huw Jones’s Summary

Have spent my working life at leading telecommunications and contact centre vendors such as Mitel, BT, Rockwell, Blue Pumpkin, Witness Systems and now Verint Systems, Witness Actionable Solutions. Professional Product Marketing/Manager and pre-sales Solutions Consultant for both partner, SME and direct enterprise sales engagements.

With my working experience and from relationships with partners, colleagues and experts in the industry I have a broad understanding of the processes and technology deployed in enterprise contact centres and specific specialist skills in workforce management (WFM) and performance management solutions.

My preferred approach is a consultative led engagement that allows me to understand in depth the issues, as-is process and roadblocks to business success. I adopt techniques such as group workshops, side-by-side operational discovery and executive briefings that allow me to deliver a detailed proposal that encompasses the software solution, the recommended process, key success criteria and tangible business benefits of our approach.

Confident and frequent presenter at internal and external events.
