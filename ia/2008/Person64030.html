Fiona Cherbak’s Summary

As VP of Marketing for GameRecruiter, premier search firm in the games industry, responsible for developing global marketing strategy and facilitating high-visibility marketing campaigns and initiatives, with focus on promotional partnerships, publicity and trade show participation. Also provide Sr. Account Manager services for select client accounts in games, mobile and Internet sectors.

Formerly Director of Client Relations and Sr. Account Manager for L.A.-based recruiting firm Enterprise Talent, supporting video games, mobile, Internet and high tech industries, with emphasis on recruiting high-level technical, marketing and creative talent for international clientele. Previously Director of Business Development and Sr. Account Manager for Mary-Margaret, and Senior Recruiting Specialist for Disney Feature Animation in Artist Development and CG Programming.

Partner/owner of ThemePark Studios, a marketing and media production company offering comprehensive marketing strategy and communications services, innovative public relations, event production, graphic design and web design. Clients have included Activision, Comedy Central, Fox Broadcasting Corporation, GameRecruiter, GSD&M Idea Entertainment, HBO's U.S. Comedy Arts Festival, Hypernia Hosting Corp., IEEE, Intelligent Transportation Systems, Jose Cuervo, KMFA Classical, Kinesoft Development, Nickelodeon, Origin Systems, RC Cola, Technical Business Network, Texas Book Festival and numerous others.

Fiona Cherbak’s Specialties:

Executive recruiting for interactive entertainment industry; marketing strategy and long-range planning; publicity and media relations; business development; account management; corporate partnerships, sales and events; non-profit fundraising; investor relations and investor presentations.
