Michael Moorman’s Summary

I'm a professional software developer with twenty years of experience. I am currently employed by Jewelry Television, where I find many interesting challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge.

I love the creative aspects of software development, and I tend to prefer the back end layers rather than flashy UI widgets - though I have designed user interfaces for a few fairly successful commerical products (hint: have you used a self-serve gas pump lately?) I've been team member and team lead for several employers. I enjoy being a technical contributor and also helping others. I've served as a scrum master and scrum team member, and can't imagine working any other way.

I enjoy learning the details of the domain I am working in. I have worked in many areas; here's a few: industrial process control, materials scheduling and managment, oil and gas accounting, gas pipeline flow balancing, general accounting software, embedded systems, file system drivers, security, internet banking, webmail and voicemail, time and attendance, aircraft pilot training, retail and e-commerce.

I'm competent in a variety of languages and platforms, including C, C++, C#, VB, ASP.NET and Javascript, common scripting languages and shells on Windows and Unix flavors, and most popular databases. Recently I'm using Java and Flex at JTV. I'm well-versed in techniques like asynchronous communications, multi-threaded programming, building for separation of concerns, IOC, O/R mappers, and design patterns.

On a personal note, my initial career interest was writing and journalism. I wrote for my college newspaper, and later I was an occasional contributor to a local free press paper. I'm fluent in Spanish, I speak beginner's French, and I speak a bit of Portuguese and Italian.

I enjoy spending my free time with my lovely wife and three young children. Together we enjoy hiking, swimming, cookouts, gardening, home improvement projects, and inventing new dance moves and silly songs.
