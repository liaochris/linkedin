Paul Carew’s Summary

25 Years of Product Development including a record of successful startups.

Close Reach Communications:
Founder, initial product architect and market building.

Celite Systems:
Vice President. Established SW Engineering, oversaw product architecture and drove development.

GenBand Inc. (General Bandwidth)
Co-Founder, VP Engineering, and CTO.
Established engineering, originated product architecture and drove direction as CTO. Involved in the formation and funding of the company, as well as it’s major growth. The company continues as a major VOIP supplier

Cisco:
Held the position of manager of the overall Cisco DSL Central Office product software development effort. This was through the successful acquisition of NetSpeed.

NetSpeed Inc.:
A technology startup specializing in DSL. An early employee and one of the three product architects for the central office DSLAM. Originated the S/W engineering group, putting together the S/W development environment, locating and hiring the development team and then driving the product development execution through to delivery.

Prior: DSC Communications, Siemens Communication Systems and Cray Communications.
