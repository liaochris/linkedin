Robert L Pritchett’s Summary

I am now managing web content and updates at the Pure Energy System wiki website - http://www.peswiki.com as a member of the New Energy Congress - and employee of PES Network, Inc. http://www.pureenergysystems.com/

I have established the "Alternative Energy User Group" as a non-profit organization, where folks can come and learn more about Alternative Energy Systems each month.

I am running the 3-Rivers Synergy Centre as a resource for alternative energy solutions. http://www.synergycentre.net We are actively looking for sponsors or angels who believe in what we do. "Green" Apple Corporation products and Alternative Energy Systems can be purchased through the website.

I've project-managed various telecom/computer network systems from inception to completion for businesses, school districts, cities and state institutions.

I currently manage a fantastic team of around 15 people internationally, who review books, games, hardware, software and training apps for the Macintosh environment. Together, we publish a magazine once a month and I participate in the Mac ReviewCast Podcast each month. We also look at and work with new computer-based technologies as they are released and experiment with them through our network and website at http://www.maccompanion.com
