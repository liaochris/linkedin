Stevie Ray Gilbert’s Summary

I am interested in writing stories that have never been written. Whether fiction or nonfiction, discovery best describes the writing process. Creativity and discovery are jealous and competitive sisters whose ambitions are ever expanding and never satisfied.

Stevie Ray Gilbert’s Specialties:

I am passionately persuasive when I believe in something. For some reason the comatose laugh or at least crack a smile when I'm in the room. Boardroom suits get a clue about wind direction. All ideas beget ideas. Obvious solutions are not always obvious or solutions. Poetry becomes prose becomes poetry becomes... Elephants forget. The good load opens a can of whiplash. I cannot fear what I love. I love everyone... almost.
