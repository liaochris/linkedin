Industry experts and analysts predict a huge shift in software delivery and management models towards SaaS. This shift will gather momentum over the next few years. Companies such as Google, Yahoo, and Facebook provide their core services, for example search, email, and social networking through SaaS. Oracle, the industry leader in enterprise software, intends to capitalize on the trend of IT organizations looking for efficiency by growing its enterprise SaaS offerings through the Oracle On Demand business.
