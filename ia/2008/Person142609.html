Nolte Burke’s Summary

I'm currently attending Western Washington University to get my masters degree in Computer Science with a focus on scalable infrastructure. I will also be working towards a minor in mathematics, focusing on statistical analysis in relation to the application of quality control processes to software engineering and manufacturing. I am a dual-citizen of the United States of America and Canada and hold a US passport.

I manage a load balanced "cluster" out of the Confluence Technology Center in Wenatchee, WA. The three-machine network has the capacity to host dozens of virtual machines via VMware and uses 100% renewable, hydro-electric energy.

Currently, I am working on a scalable PHP platform--developed with clustering, high-availability, fault-tolerance, and speed in mind. In addition to being fully Object Oriented, it adheres to a strict model-view-control design pattern, modeled loosely after Ruby on Rails. It supports database replication, Squid, & Memcache for acceleration.

In the past I've worked with financial services, internet companies, and leading manufacturers to develop software or offer consulting. I am also familiar with implementing RTOS solutions for mobile, thin-client, and embedded devices.
