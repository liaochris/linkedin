Karla Porter’s Summary

I have two distinct professional endeavors; that of Partner in a Mayan Riviera Real Estate Brokerage, First Investment Real Estate, and Talent Recruiter in the Contact Center industry with Telerx Marketing, Inc.

In my rather eclectic past I have managed a timeshare salesroom, owned a café and was the Pastry Chef, created a children's free lunch program and founded an SPCA all in Cozumel and Playa del Carmen, Mexico. I worked at a Spanish language TV affiliated of Telemundo, the now defunct Spanish language magazine El Espetacular and sold building supplies in the US. I spent a glorious year teaching English in Mallorca, Spain.

I would need many lifetimes to accomplish everything I would like to so I must pick and choose carefully in this one since I am thinking I am only getting one shot....

I believe in open networking and invite you to join my network. Hopefully, we will do better together than just be names on each other's page. ~Karla
