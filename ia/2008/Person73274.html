Guido Repaci’s Summary

More than 20 years of experience in ICT department within multinational companies playing roles with increasing responsibility, CIO (Chief Information Officer) in media industry since 1997. A considerable experience in complex technological environments based on SAP (ERP/CRM), business intelligence and multimedia technologies. Management of large ICT teams (100 persons, > 20 m€ Budget) achieving cost reduction, service level improvements and the development of team members competences by using using best-practises.

Management based on efficiency and innovation so to enable business development, with a special focus on processes, organization and people. A structured approach to the resolution of complex problems. A leader with experience in large, geographically-dispersed, multicultural and matrix-based business & ICT organizations and with an excellent knowledge of English language.
