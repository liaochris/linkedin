Dave Smith’s Summary

Currently working at D-Link as VP Business Solutions, responsible for their Business Solutions Division in Europe, covering the Europe-wide product, marketing, sales/channel & technology solutions for D-Link's business into SME/Enterprise segments.

Until June 2007, I was Senior VP Marketing for Swyx Solutions, where I led Swyx’s corporate, technology & channel Marketing functions worldwide. During this period, Swyx tripled its business within a two year period and attained market leader position for its SME segment in EMEA IP Telephony.

I joined Swyx in April 2005 from Cisco Systems, where I held various senior marketing and management roles, most recently as Director of EMEA Marketing responsible for Cisco’s Product & Technology Marketing and Market Intelligence, Strategy and Planning functions in EMEA. Through my Cisco career, I held marketing and technology leadership responsibility for Cisco’s Routing, Switching, OSS, Voice, Wireless, Security and Optical portfolios in the EMEA region.

Previous to Cisco, I worked for 11 years at Nortel Networks, where I held various management & engineering positions covering Worldwide Product Marketing / Management and EMEA Technical Support for Service Provider & Enterprise Voice, Call Centre and OSS/infrastructure products & solutions, as well as being responsible for the internal IT infrastructure.
