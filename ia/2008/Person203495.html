BJ (Zijun) Deng’s Summary

An MBA and MSc graduate who can work in both technical (MSc in Computing, BSc in engineering) and business environments (AMBA-credited MBA) and both Eastern and Western cultures. Four years experience in e-business trade with China in renewable energy and low carbon technologies including Wind Power, Hydro Power, Solar PV, and Solar Hot Water. I grew up and finished my 1st degree in China and have lived in the UK since 2001. I am fluent in English, Mandarin and Cantonese and travel regularly between the UK and China. Following several years working in renewable energy/low carbon technologies I have returned to university to study an MBA at Swansea University. i have won national and international prize including Student Enterprise '07 and 2008 China-UK Entrepreneurship Competition.

Seren Energy, specialises in wind energy and renewable energy advice. * Formerly EnergyTech Ltd
Seren Energy is specializing in renewable energy and low carbon technologies. The focus is on general energy consultancy and wind energy development. We are involved at all stages of renewable energy developments, acting as consultants and planning agents at the initial stages, and project managers and installers during construction. Seren Energy has worked on a number of wind turbine schemes through the UK. Most of these schemes have involved single turbines on rural, commercial and brown field sites. The client base is wide, ranging from landowners and farmers to factory owners and investors, and includes customers from the public, private and charity sectors. I am responsible for running and managing the Seren Energy Operations Department. I am also responsible for the new solar project sourcing high quality solar thermal panels from China.
