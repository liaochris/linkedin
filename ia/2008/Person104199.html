Keith Elder’s Summary

Senior Engineer, Team Leader, Speaker, Trainer, Consultant, and Educator who has an extensive background in open source technologies as well as technolgies on the Microsoft platform.

As an Educator, logged thousands of hours in front of the classroom teaching students of varying ages from the 6th grade to the college level. At the college level taught system certification courses as well as programming courses.

As a Consultant and Trainer, trained countless developers from various business sectors ranging from the top auto manufactures to Fortune 500 companies as well as Universities. Built large scale web-based applications.

As an Engineer, highly knowledgeable and experienced in building large scale enterprise applications surrounding both the LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP) platform as well as the .Net platform. Extensive experiece in building Windows Forms applications using a Services Oriented Architecture.

As a Speaker, highly regarded as a down to earth speaker that draws the audience in with his warm attitude and animated manorisms. Currently speaks at national events including conferences, area related events and local events about the .Net platform.

In October of 2007 awarded Microsoft MVP in Visual Client Development.

.NET, C#, Smart Clients, SQL Server, ASP.NET, Software Architecture, Design, Web Services, SOA (Service Oriented Architecure), Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP, Unix Administration
