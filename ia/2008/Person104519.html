Kelly Johnson’s Summary

Kelly Johnson founded Converta Design, Inc., a website design and internet marketing company located in Raleigh, NC. She combines information architecture, usability, search engine optimization, and internet marketing strategies with website design and development to increase online conversions or sales. Conversion Design is the process of implementing usability and search engine optimization techniques at the design level and integrating websites with business and marketing goals to increase online conversions.

Kelly has experience conducting usability analysis, information architecture, search engine optimization, competitive intelligence, and interface design projects for both Fortune 500 companies and startups.
