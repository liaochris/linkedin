Tom Peire’s Summary

Tom Peire, CEO of Four Pees, is a sales & marketing professional with extensive experience in the graphic arts market. Throughout his career he served in a variety of sales and marketing roles for different companies. As Director of Corporate Marketing at Artwork Systems he had worldwide responsibility for managing the entire range of marketing activities for the Enfocus and Artwork Systems product ranges. Tom joined Enfocus in July 2001 as Marketing Manager and has been a key player in the promotion and success of the Enfocus product suite of software tools. Before joining Enfocus Tom had an excellent track record with leading edge companies such as Scitex Europe where he held a variety of positions in marketing and sales.

Tom Peire’s Specialties:

Strategic Marketing, Marketing Commmunication, Product (Marketing) Management, Public Relations, Channel Management
