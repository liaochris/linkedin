Stephen Shakeshaft’s Summary

An enthusiastic Professional Mechanical Engineer, equally at home in an office or plant based environment.

With broad experience across a diverse range of industries from Petrochemicals, Utilities/Power Generation through Gas Production and Liquefaction to Pharmaceuticals/Biotechnology and Nuclear Energy, I have a very broad skill set.

Stephen Shakeshaft’s Specialties:

I offer interim project engineering and management expertise to clients looking to optimise current assets (both static and rotating equipment) or as a consulting engineer on new build projects. I have a proven track record of achievement as a competent engineer managing complex situations under time, quality and financial constraints.
