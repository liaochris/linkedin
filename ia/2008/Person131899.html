    1) At the US National Rowing Championships, Santa Clara was the first team ever to compete using a female coxswain. Tiny Santa Clara challenged the 100-year old rule and won the right to compete with the first woman coxswain (a coxswain is the strategic captain of the boat.)

    Today in international competitions women are coxswains for crews around the world.
