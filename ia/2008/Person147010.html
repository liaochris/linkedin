Patrick Ortman’s Summary

I consider myself a digital storyteller. I've created or helped create amazing and groundbreaking projects for huge Fortune 1000 companies, including Apple Computer, IBM, Motorola, BellSouth, Time Warner, etc. But I love working with companies of all sizes who have a forward-looking and creative attitude.

I create interactive and broadcast projects that deploy across a variety of digital media. These days, that pretty much means I deal with all kinds of media and platforms. I don't just do corporate work- my projects include a webisode series that's seen on SprintTV and now Verizon, films that have played in festivals throughout the world, and visual effects projects for major motion pictures. My projects have been talked up in People, Time, Newsweek, PDN, Japan's NHK Network, Discovery Channel, and lots of small local papers like the New York Times.

Basically, I want to do cool stuff with cool people. I'm capable of doing things very few other people can, and I'm always looking for a chance to shine.

Patrick Ortman’s Specialties:

I know an awful lot about digital media, and I can make your project or company look a lot better online.
