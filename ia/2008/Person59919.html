Eric Wilson’s Summary

Independent Management Consultant – Providing professional Financial & Administrative Management, Business Planning, and Interim Operational Management to small/mid-sized European firms launching operations in the United States.

I am associated with Marie Landel and Associates, a professional practice providing outsourced financial and administrative services to small/mid-sized European firms launching operations in the US market.

In addition to the outsourced F&A services provided by Marie Landel & Associates, I offer a range of additional services including business planning, business development, and interim executive management for start-up European firms developing their operations in the US.

I am also associated with Bridge-n, a corporate advisory firm headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, focused on start-ups and early-stage companies in growth industries, assisting them with their strategic and operational needs.

Marie Landel and Associates, located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, has been in the business of providing outsourced F&A support to European firms since 1990 and has an excellent reputation for the highest quality of service. The firm has roughly 100 clients, principally in the software and biotech fields, located throughout the US.

Eric Wilson’s Specialties:

I have over 25 years of international experience in corporate finance, consulting, and project management in software and biopharma with both large international companies and start-ups. I have worked in both the US and in Europe and participated in projects globally, including Asia and South America. I am fluent in English, French, conversant in Spanish and bring a solid understanding of cultural matters that impact working globally and in diverse teams.
