Aharon Rabinowitz’s Summary

A noted guru in the video and animation industry, with the ability to present technical information in a dynamic, entertaining, and understandable manner. Detail-oriented, hands-on media professional and teacher with a successful history in project management, broadcast, training, software design consultation, web design, video podcasting, and acting/voiceover work.

Began career in broadcast as a production intern at Sesame Street, and have since served as Animation Designer, Producer, and Technical Manager for several networks and production companies. Have worked with high-profile clients in branding and repackaging their on-air and on-line identities, and consult with them regularly on project management and technology specifications.

Currently serving as CEO and Creative Director at All Bets Are Off Productions, a NYC based company specializing in video, animation, and multimedia. In addition to these offerings, regularly provides clients with professional training in multimedia technology.

As Creative Director for the Creative Cow Master Series of Video training, is responsible for managing several Master Series training titles. Since 2005, has produced and hosted the weekly Creative Cow After Effects video training Podcast and Multimedia 101 podcast. In addition, as an Adjunct Faculty member at Pratt Manhattan, teaches advanced animation courses to graphics professionals in and around NYC.

Regularly presents and designs presentations for video and effects software shown at major trade shows such as NAB, IBC, ProMax and Siggraph. Has contributed content included in several graphics applications, and consults on the design and implementation of major software packages used by professional animators and video artists worldwide.

Acting and stage accomplishments include national recognition (Alpha Psi Omega) for acting, sound design and other stage production work. Voiceover work for corporate, advertising, and non-profit projects.
