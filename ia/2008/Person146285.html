Parker Tresemer’s Summary

Parker is an Associate of Program Management in Sapient’s North American Commercial business unit, and currently works out of the Oakland, California office. Prior to joining Sapient in August of 2006, his work experience was almost entirely in the fields of healthcare and biotechnology. He has worked in industries ranging from Medical Device Production, Biotech Research, Pharmaceuticals, and Retail and Service. Parker has worked individually and as a team, and is familiar with Microsoft Office applications, data analysis, and basic programming in C++.
