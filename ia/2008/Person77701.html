Ron Berg’s Summary

HTSE is an ASP (Application Service Provider) providing a complete call center solution for call center and telemarketing enterprises enabling companies to differentiate themselves with the level of service, functionality and dynamism.

As an ASP we deliver hosted (tele)communication services for the European contact center market, as a all-in-one turn-key solution; and that means that from day one we turn your enterprise into a high comprehensive contact center company. With the latest technology, HTSE offers multi-channel application unifying all aspects of business communications by allowing your business to manage multimedia interactions, enhance workforce mobility, improve security and increase competitive advantage. You get all you need through a simple pay-as-you-go structure that ensures you pay only for the services you really use.

HTSE has its headquarters in the Netherlands (Rotterdam), with international offices in Sweden (Stockholm), Southern-France (Sophia-Antipolis Cedex) and Ireland (Dublin).
