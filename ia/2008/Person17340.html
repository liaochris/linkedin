Ash Prabala’s Summary

DVC Company is a manufacturer of scientific grade CCD cameras that are used in scientific and industrial applications. Scientific imaging requires the generation of images from which quantitative data may be extracted, usually for the purposes of research, inspection or discovery.

While consumer digital cameras have reached 'commodity' status in today’s world, the scientific markets that DVC addresses require specialized, high-performance cameras with high-bandwidth interfaces. For example, as the drive for multi-analyte high-throughput screening accelerates in the life-sciences segment, imaging has become the enabling technology of choice, displacing laser-based and other conventional methods. DVC’s products create a platform that has been proven to be perfectly suited to meet such challenges across diverse market segments.

DVC cameras are used to solve difficult imaging applications, typically under light-starved conditions. For example, in Life Sciences Imaging applications such as Cancer Screening, it is necessary to generate quantitative images quickly and without exposing the live cells to excessive light. This requires the use of very sensitive, low-noise cameras.

DVC is a supplier of 'enabling technology' in the form of scientific cameras and software to researchers and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). While our active focus has been in the Life Sciences area, we also supply cameras to the Industrial and Defense sectors. In addition to a portfolio of 'standard' products, our architecture permits the cost-effective customization of our products for customer-specific applications.

Applications for our products include:

* Pharamceutical Drug Discovery
* High Content Screening
* Fluorescence Microscopy
* Electron Microscopy
* Materials Science
* Machine Vision and Inspection
