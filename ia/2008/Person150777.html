Chad Sicard’s Summary

[resume exerpt]
Chad is a well-rounded Print Designer with over 10 years of extensive experience in Corporate Branding & Consumer Packaging. His insight into the strategy and integration of Fortune 500-level branding coupled with a keen comprehension of production processes truly make him an exceptional asset to any project. He has professional high-end imaging experience and has taught both Design and Pre-Press at a respected & accredited college. Chad is highly adept at leveraging technology and excels at managing large and/or complex projects; instinctively streamlining the process and files for maximum efficiency. He is very personable and performs well in team settings, but shines in independent roles.
