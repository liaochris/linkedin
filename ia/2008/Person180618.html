Stephanie Vora’s Summary

I am a certified coach who loves to work with motivated professionals to achieve their ideal work/life balance. Let me start by clarifying that coaching is not therapy, rather its about pushing yourself forward to achieve whatever is important to you in life through awareness, action, accountability and results.

Who could benefit from working with a coach? I believe that coaching can be relevant to anyone who wants more balance in their life and is interested in making a change. Take a few minutes to answer the questions below to see how your work/life balance is....

1. Do you go to sleep at night feeling rewarded by what you did that day?
2. Are you in control of your stress and anxiety at work? At home?
3. Are you proud of what you do for a living?
4. Are you happy with the amount of time you set aside to do the things you love?
5. Are you spending enough “quality” time with loved ones?
6. Are you proud of how you take care of yourself both physically and mentally?

Ask yourself, what could you start doing more of? What could you start doing less of? What positive change would make the biggest impact in your life? As a certified professional coach, I can work with you to help put your life back “in-balance.”
