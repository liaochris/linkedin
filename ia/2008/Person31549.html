Cary Rosenzweig’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    IMVU

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    November 2007 — Present (4 months)

    IMVU Inc., located in Palo Alto, California, ultimately helps users make more friends online and have fun via the combination of the world’s most beautiful and expressive 3D avatars (a cartoon-like, online alter ego) instant messaging, elements of virtual worlds, and casual gaming. Users interact with one another via their online avatars, their text messages floating gently above their avatars’ heads in cartoon strip text bubbles. Users make “buddies” with one another and manage their relationships via their own home pages, groups, public rooms, forums and 1-to-1 chats. Taking a platform approach, IMVU enables thousands of third-party content creators to sell their virtual items (such as clothing for avatars) in IMVU’s online catalog, which currently features over 1.3 million unique items. IMVU is backed by Menlo Ventures, Allegis Capital and Bridgescale Partners.

  • VP and General Manager, QuickBooks Payroll

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; INTU; Computer Software industry)

    Promoted to VP in 9/04. Consistently earned top bonuses and option grants. In 9/05, won Intuit Excellence in Leadership Business Award, based on FY05 results. In 11/05, achieved one of the top employee morale score for business groups at Intuit (88% “employee engagement”). Launched first premium service in 11/04, Enhanced Payroll Plus, and it became of Intuit’s most successful launches ever.

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; CNET; Internet industry)

    Responsible for all marketing, PR, product management, creative and customer service departments for this early consumer comparision shopping service. Achieved rapid growth behind both traditional and Internet marketing.

More importantly, leads to merchants increased +300% versus prior year.

    Acquired by CNET in 2/00. After CNET layoffs in 2/01, asked to stay as business leader, but did not due to disagreement over strategy.
