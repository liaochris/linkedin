Dave Burkett’s Summary

With over 17 years of experience in product management, marketing, operations, and R&D for leading S&P 500 companies, Dave has proven himself capable of leading change, transforming business models, developing innovative products, growing teams, and instilling fiscal discipline. Dave currently leads strategy, product management, marketing and development for a $1.2B Creative Suite line of Design and Web products and services.

Dave was recognized by Adobe’s CEO as with a special award for “drive, persistence, and passion” in convincing the company to adopt the Creative Suite strategy. Dave initiated the concept to transform Adobe’s relatively mature creative business from individual products to a suite of products, simultaneously launched, fully integrated, and marketed as a solution. The Creative Suite reinvigorated the business. Revenue grew 70+% to $1Billion in 3 years, as customers adopted higher priced suites. Meanwhile, profitability grew 35% as the Adobe was able to streamline development costs, reduce cost-of-goods and hold marketing and sales expenses flat.

Dave Burkett’s Specialties:

Dave is a general manager, but specializes in:

* Technology innovation to establish market leadership
* P&L discipline, focused on the bottom line
* Recruiting, retaining and developing strong leaders
