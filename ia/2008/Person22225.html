Bert van Dijk’s Summary

Nine year experience in high quality business and on line financial reporting. Specialised in the economics of technology-businesses.

As of September 13th I became a China-correspondent based in Shanghai covering primarily - but not exclusively - business, technology and financial topics.

* Bi-weekly column in Het Financieele Dagblad about new technology
* Weekly column "Dansen met de draak" in Het Financieele Dagblad about life in China and Shanghai (since September 8th)

Bert van Dijk’s Specialties:

Covering the convergence of media, internet and telecommunications. Other topics of special interest: semiconductor-(equipment) industry, consumer electronics, ftth and broadband development, web 2.0, China - Dutch technology alliances, venture capital.

Read my weblog at http://blogs.fd.nl/shanghai

LANGUAGE SKILLS:

Dutch (native), English (Cambridge Certificate of Proficiency English), German (good), French (intermediate), Chinese (currently studying)
