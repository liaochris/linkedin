Srikanth Sampara’s Summary

Architect in Data Mining and Advanced Analytical solutions and held various senior management and executive responsibilities in this area.

My personal goal in life can be summarized as creating a collaborative decision-making culture in organizations. This can be achieved by 'democratization of information' within the boundaries of organization.

Personally I define 'advanced analytics' as analysis of data using data mining, text mining techniques, genetic algorithms, artificial intelligence, machine learning etc etc. Now this area has been a major mystery for organizations wanting to leverage their data. Questions like 'what is the value', 'which tool to use', outsource or insource, what tools to use, who will use them etc etc etc (lot more but this is not the space) often go unanswered or 'NOT satisfactorily' answered.

Crux of writing all the above info is simple, 'analytics' is a process heavily dependent upon in-depth understanding of techniques (& algorithms within techniques),domain knowledge & processes - we are working on creating these elements that will enable us to deliver the real-value of data to business users (on their email, PDA, Mobile wthatever works) while masking all the complexity from them.

Next time around business users and LOB's should'nt be scared to ask a question that involves analysis of data ;-)
