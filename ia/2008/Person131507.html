Dan Ushman’s Summary

For the past six years I have been involved with various ventures, including an advertising network (dkAds Network, Inc.) which sold its core asset, HostingCatalog.com to iNet Interactive in 2002.

Since then, Zak Boca and I co-founded a web-hosting company called midPhase Services, Inc., through a series of acquisitions and mostly organic growth we have grown to host over 120,000 web sites for over 90,000 customers, representing over 140 countries. Our staff has grown from just two (Zak and I) to over 75 total employees, 40 of which are in our Chicago office. We own and operate around 1,000 servers and currently have space for roughly 2,000 more. midPhase was incorporated in May 2003.
