Stephen Bell’s Summary

Steve Bell is the Executive Director for BNI Saskatchewan; and co-founder of “The Networking Masters TeleSummit” – a three day educational event that helps business professionals strengthen networking and sales skills .

Steve is a contributing author to Best Selling Book "Masters of Sales" Best Seller on Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Amazon.com, USA Today, and Business Weekly.

Steve is also primary author of the soon to be released book, “Average Joe to Sales Dynamo”.

Prior to BNI, Steve was a Sales Manager in the Telecommunications / Electronics Industry where he won many internal awards for his sales and management achievements.

Now, much of Steve’s time is focused on teaching business professionals effective networking strategies and how to develop mutually beneficial and profitable business relationships.

Steve’s diverse experience and achievements makes him sought after speaker for both local and International events.

During his free time Steve enjoys spending time with his family and coaching eager students in free style olympic wrestling.
