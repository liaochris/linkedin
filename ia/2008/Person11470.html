Andrew Dakin’s Summary

I have been designing websites since I was in Middle School, that was about 1995 - 1996. I've always been interested in working on the interactive part of websites and applications. I begrudgingly admit that I used FrontPage for a couple years when I first started then quickly realized how much of a mistake that was and moved over to Dreamweaver.

I designed several websites for the city of Clarinda, IA and then moved on to a local automotive tool manufacturing company. The website was basically database driven and had an administrative backend to update and maintain the tools they had as well as categories and certain content areas. We used ColdFusion for the website redesign. After finishing that website they acquired a new company that built patient lifts. They were also in need of a new website, so I was tasked with redesigning that website as well. They key thing they wanted to accomplish with the redesign was to add a way for their sales people to walk customers through a series of easy choices to create an order and send it to the offices in Clarinda via email.

After that I moved up to Omaha, NE and continued my education at UNO. I kept playing around with web design and started experimenting with Flash a lot at this time. I started ended up taking a summer course for actionscript and started my job at First National Bank as an Intern. Since I started there I have done everything from XML driven demos to internal video production to several redesigns for affiliate banks.

Recently I started working on a new application that is being built with php. I'm pretty familiar with application development, but this is the first time I will be executing the entire project from start to finish.

I hope to continue working in web design and would like to eventually work more with Flash Design since it has the best of both worlds, design with the satisfaction of programming that I enjoy.
