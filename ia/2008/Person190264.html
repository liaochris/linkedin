Todd Gailun’s Summary

Passionate about the dynamics underlying networks (neural, biological and social), the "creative destruction" being unleashed by competing wireless platforms, and the innovative business models taking aim at the Mobile Internet, Todd Gailun has an eclectic background committed to monetizing technological networks.

Having participated firsthand in Nextel's and Amazon's high yield financings, managed the product launch of Ericsson's VoIP and IP VPN programs while based in Stockholm, and pioneered the "speed dating" concept - foreshadowing "social networking" - with 8minuteDating, he seeks to leverage these unique perspectives to focus on the evolution of Social Networking and Virtual Worlds morphing onto the nascent Mobile Internet.

As a self-described systems thinker, he continues his education on the interplay between technology and humanity by collaborating with Life Science Ph.D.s from Yale, NYU, Columbia and Rockefeller, among others, to create a unique "salon" where entrepreneurial scientists create biotechnologies, and start-ups, in in collaboration with New York City's preeminent venture capitalists.

Todd is an maniacal spinner and cyclist, training to ride L'Étape du Tour, finishing this year at Hautacam. When not on a bike or engaging with people, he can be found reading, and looking at art, attempting to make sense of the human condition.
