Tyson Kirksey’s Summary

Since 1999, Tyson Kirksey has been creating, developing and marketing websites for clients and customers across the nation. With a bachelor's degree in Interactive Media and a programming and marketing background, Tyson is well-equipped to handle a project from start to finish (although he believes a website is never finished).

After receiving his degree, Tyson accepted a position with Zunch Communications, Inc. as an SEO Specialist and Web Developer. During his two and a half year tenure with Zunch, Tyson quickly emerged as a thought leader and respected project manager, and was rewarded with high profile accounts such as La Quinta, Compass Bank, FASTSIGNS, Experian and many others. After leaving Zunch in November 2006, Tyson accepted a position with New Media Gateway, Inc. where he is directly responsible for SEO and PPC campaigns for many national clients including some of the largest cities in the United States.

His approach is simple: Be honest and transparent with customers and work hard to demonstrate value for services performed.
