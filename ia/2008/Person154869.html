Barbara Kremers’s Summary

Summary of professional experience and goals:

I have been ambitiously searching for a cure for cancer. One of the projects I have contributed to has resulted in the vaccine for cervix carcinoma. Then, after a big change, I joined the commercial IT world as a business intelligence consultant, a completely different world but I adapted fast. After an additional study I became financial analyst at Oracle. My personal interest was then drawn to creating income online and I started a couple of websites. As I'm a continuous learner I also enrolled in a part-time study Professional Communication.
