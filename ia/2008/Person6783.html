Alexia Petersen’s Summary

I graduated from the University of San Francisco with my bachelor’s degree in Biology and Communication. Upon graduation, I briefly worked in retail while I looked for a job in Silicon Valley. Within a short time I found my current position at Cisco Systems/CLI as an Academy Support Representative (ASR) for the Cisco Networking Academy program, where I have remained for the past 19 months. I support three Area Academy Managers (AAM's), in the West Coast, Central/Mountain and Northeast/Ohio Valley Territories. This makes me directly responsible to market and support the Cisco Networking Academy program in 39 states.

The Cisco Networking Academy program provides skills students need to work in IT fields. The program offers web-based content, online assessment, hand-on labs, instructor training, and preparation for industry certifications. It is a comprehensive global e-learning program, which provides students with Internet technology skills. It is also Cisco’s biggest corporate give-back program with an investment of over $200 million.

Being part of the NetAcad Marcom team has been a great learning experience. It has given me the opportunity to be a part of an amazing hard working team. I am able to work with education and technology, which are both great interests of mine while learning from my management colleagues.

Supporting three AAM’s has proven my organizational skills, which I use to keep projects updated and on time. I am also a self motivator, an adaptive learner, working hard to advance within Cisco. I have excellent communication skills and am looking forward to more client interaction in the future.
