Kai Roer’s Summary

I offer unbiased information security advices to corporations around the world through my security consulting company.

Being vendor independent, our focus is to take the side of our clients and determine the best approach to security, technology and compliance. By analyzing the real needs of the clients, our services focuses on adding business value. IS is not about technology alone - it is about streamlining business process', and choosing policies, procedures and technology that is right for your organization.
And most times, vendors only helps themselves by advising you to buy their product.

Our value proposition is simply to help your corporation to choose the right tools for the job. We analyze and specify your needs. We take care of the evaluation and bid process. We help you select. We even do QA of the final solution. And most importantly - we train you and your staff to be more secure.

What we do not do is equally simple. We do not sell you anything but our knowledge. We do not implement any kind of systems. And we do not accept any kind of payment from vendors.

We care for you.

I am a strong believer that trust, ethics and open information is the only road to walk.
