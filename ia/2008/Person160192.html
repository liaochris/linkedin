Richard Sparrow’s Summary

Technically inclined director with over 12 years in the interactive television / convergent media space and a background that combines engineering management, business development, strategic planning, and product management.

Richard Sparrow’s Specialties:

- Developing product vision, strategy, and roadmap through coordinated effort with customers, support, development, marketing, and financial teams.

- Understanding the root causes of customer requests and issues

- Design of portal and social tools to support systems and integrate advertizing opportunities

- Media centric product design with emphasis on non-computer / non-tech savvy users

- Understanding of technology implementation plans from an enterprise perspective

Richard Sparrow’s Experience

  • Co-Founder, Director Product Management

    Fuze Media Systems

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Consumer Electronics industry)

    April 2005 — Present (3 years 6 months)

    Fuze Media Systems provides a media driven, whole-home entertainment solution that leverages the power of Microsoft’s Media Center platform. Built from the ground-up to manage media, FUZE delivers a complete system that stores, manages, organizes, and distributes all digital media to any part of the home.

    - Directed Development for Prototype Resulting in Company’s Funding
    - Reconcile Customers and Investors Needs with Available Technical Solutions
    - Cross Departmental Planning and Project Development
    - Manage End-To-End Product Development Cycle

    Fuze Media Systems started shipping product in September of 2007, meeting investor’s revenue expectations for the year.

  • Founder, Technology Director

    iTVDirector, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    February 2004 — March 2005 (1 year 2 months)

    iTVDirector, Inc. provided distributed location based menu and video management tools for hospitality, medical, residential, and video signage industries. The tools provided simple web based interfaces for input, maintenance, and distribution of content for new and existing video systems.

    - Product Planning for Web Based Systems
    - Technical Direction for Development
    - Development of Third-Party Partnerships
    - Customer and Investor Presentations

    In April of 2005 iTVDirector, Inc. was rolled into efforts from two other groups to become Integrated Space, Inc which was later renamed Fuze Media Systems.

    - Worked directly for three CTOs
    - Managed and Developed Partner Relationships
    - Simultaneous Management of Several High Priority Projects
    - Software Architecture Decisions and Strategies
