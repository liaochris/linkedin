I have made invited lectures at State University of Management (Moscow, RF), Stockholm School of Economics in St. Peterburg (RF), Moscow State University (RF), in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, Colorado State University, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, NASA Goddard Space Flight Center.
