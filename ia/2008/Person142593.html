Nolan Garrett’s Summary

Nolan is a graduate of Eastern Washington University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and minors in both Mathematics and Physics. He further has studied Network Security at the University of Idaho, a National Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance Education which is jointly sponsored by the NSA and the Department of Homeland Security. Experience with a variety of platforms including Windows and Linux provide Nolan with a strong foundation for security configuration and systems management. Nolan’s strong technical background and extensive knowledge of security issues enables him to facilitate secure systems management and the development of effective compliance solutions.

Nolan has served as the Vice President of The Sam Richard Group, Inc., a money management firm, and as the Chief Operating Officer of Alluvien, Inc., a company specializing in comprehensive IT Compliance solutions for financial institutions. His responsibilities in these organizations included management of infrastructure, systems and processes, as well as financial analysis and product development.

Nolan Garrett’s Specialties:

GLBA, HIPAA, CobiT, ISO 27001, ISO 17799, and SOX experience. IT Auditing, White-Hat (Ethical) Hacking, Penetration Testing and Vulnerability Assessments are services I have performed for a variety of organizations ranging from small banks and credit unions to Fortune 500 companies.
