Jon Tanner’s Summary

Co-founded MitchelLake in 2001. Over the past six years we have developed a multi-million dollar business in search, specialist recruitment and consulting for high tech start-up and professional services companies. We currently partner some of the Worlds highest profile technology, consulting and finance entities in addition to a myriad of start-up companies across mobile content, applications and services, web 2.0, social networking, gaming, integrated digital marketing, CRM & business intelligence.

Our goal is to become the foremost supplier of search, specialist recruitment and organisational development services for emerging markets created by innovation. We also offer service for equity or part fee / equity arrangements as an alternative to up-front fees.

Jon Tanner’s Specialties:

Executive search for start-up and specialist technology companies, divisions and commercialisation projects. Commercial introductions for alliance and partnership development. Expertise in recruiting for professional services consulting around Enterprise Architecture, Infrastructure and software.
