Ezequiel Mastrasso’s Summary

Digital Artist with a wide knowledge and experience in computer generated graphics, also deeply involved in the technical aspect.
Proactive, and self-taught.
Proffesional experience using numerous softwares and scripting languages as houdini, maya, motionbuilder, rhino, studio tools, xsi, max, renderman, mental ray, mantra, nuke, shake, fusion, mel scripting, python, tcl, bash, powershell, and hscript.
As a 3d generalist artist I have been involved in character design, animation, rigging, scripting, hair/fur tools, and mocap equipment operation and cleaning.
