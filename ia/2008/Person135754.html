Motti Vaknin’s Summary

CEO of BeInSync, a software as a service company that provide advanced data services to individuals and businesses. The company leading service allow users to Sync, Share, Access and (Online) Backup any type of digital data.
* Acquired by Phoenix Technologies (PTEC)

Chairman and Co-founder of WebLayers, Inc., a Cambridge, MA, based software company pioneering the market for Governance of XML, Web Services and Service Oriented Architecture.

CEO of Bridges for Islands, Inc. (Acquired on , an innovative BPM software company that developed in 1999 a software product for Business Process Integration, based on XML, J2EE and Service Oriented Architecture.
* Acquired by Attunity (ATTU)

Held VP positions with Satellite Communication company and Enterprise Information Infrastructure (EII) company, where he was responsible for the company product direction, positioning, and business development.

Mr. Vaknin is the founder and moderator of the SOA forum - an exclusive Fortune 500 and Government agencies roundtable of IT executives and enterprise architects. Mr. Vaknin has numerous speaking engagements and is enthusiastic about the role of information technology in achieving business leadership.

Motti Vaknin’s Specialties:

Building innovative technology companies to answer real world business requirements.

  • CEO

    BeInSync, Ltd.

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    January 2007 — April 2008 (1 year 4 months)

    On March 26, 2008, Phoenix Technologies (PTEC) signed a contract to acquire BeInSync. Deal Closed on April 30, 2008.

    With over 4 million downloads to date, BeInSync offers individuals and small businesses a complete freedom when dealing with their digital content, by allowing them to Sync, Share, Access and Back up their content seamlessly.
    Based on patent-pending secure peer-to-peer technology, BeInSync creates private data networks that makes it easy to stay connected and always in sync with colleagues, friends, remote files and computers.
    Privately held, BeInSync is venture capital backed and headquartered in Tel Aviv, Israel with Offices in San Jose, California.
