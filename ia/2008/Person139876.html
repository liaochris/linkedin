Bret Hollander’s Summary

Envision EMI is the world’s largest creator of leadership and success programs for high achieving young people. Founded in 1985, the company is dedicated to providing young people of high potential around the world with programs that “educate, motivate and inspire” their leadership and learning potential.

Privately owned and headquartered in Vienna, Virginia, Envision EMI has grown at a consistent double digit rate. Over 50,000 students a year participate in Envision EMI-run programs. Envision-run programs are highly selective. Students are typically nominated by their teachers. The company each year runs programs of various lengths on five continents, in nine different countries and dozens of different American cities. Envision-run programs are also highly effective, achieving consistent student and parent satisfaction rates in the high 90s.

Envision EMI competes in a rapidly expanding and highly fragmented global market for children’s educational experiences. With public institutions less and less able to provide meaningful growth experiences for students of exceptional potential, Envision EMI’s proven programs represent a global “best practice.” Moreover by continually and carefully refining those experiences, Envision EMI offers students and parents alike the opportunity to attend programs that are unique educational experiences.

Envision EMI has achieved its level of success by encouraging a culture of innovative thinking which is applied in an organization where all its employees have a deeply held passion for its mission. The company’s culture is one where people are encouraged to perform at a high level against clear, quantitative and well coordinated goals. The company is highly collaborative, and management is highly committed to attracting and developing great talent.
