Are you interested in environmental pictures or do you like to present your products, company or organisation by photography, don’t hesitate to contact me. I love helping you realizing your wishes. Please also see the (Dutch) summary of publications in which my photos were used (http://www.meindertvandijkfotografie.nl/nl/publicaties/).
