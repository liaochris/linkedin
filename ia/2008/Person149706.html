Peter Buytaert’s Summary

Founder of Shanghai China Global Leaders (CGL) Management Consulting, successfully driving positive change in organizations via change management training, interim leadership and international board advisory.
Chairman of Benelux Chamber of Commerce in Shanghai.
Past President Agfa Asia region, based in Shanghai and employing 500+ people with over 250 Million USD in turnover.
Previously based in Seoul, Kuala Lumpur, Manila, Tokyo and Hong Kong with progressive leadership roles in marketing, sales, general management. Covered both business-to-business and business-to-consumer industry segments with specific experience in healthcare, graphics and photo imaging.
Career achievements include both successfull start-up of businesses, as well as restructuring.
