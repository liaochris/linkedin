Greg Robinson’s Summary

I am an IT Manager with more than 20 years experience in UNIX systems engineering, architecture, and technical consulting projects. I have a strong background in personnel management, departmental budgeting and process improvement. I am a skilled manager and engineer for large scale, business critical UNIX solutions and projects. I have strong leadership and communication skills, and the ability to bridge the communication gaps between technical and non-technical groups.

My goal is to work for an organization which operates in close cooperation with the local community. I want to use my skills and experience to help improve and build a company that has very high ethical standards for its management and staff, and which works to sustain and strengthen the community in which the company and its offices are located. I believe I have a responsibility to ensure that the company in which I work provides concrete benefits for the local economy and community.
