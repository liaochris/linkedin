Adam Robinson [LION] 1300+’s Summary

Adam Robinson is co-founder and President of illuma. Launched in 2004, illuma delivers flexible, effective, and affordable recruiting services for small and midsized businesses.

Before launching illuma, Adam served as Senior Director, Professional Services at Click Commerce, Inc., a leading provider of collaborative commerce and RFID technologies to the manufacturing, high tech, financial services and retail industries.

Adam began his career as a Technical Recruiter and Account Executive at Maxim Group - now part of Allegis Group, the largest privately-held technical staffing organization in the United States.

He serves on the Board of Directors of the Chicago Chapter of the Entrepreneurs Organization, and serves on the Board of Advisors for DePaul University’s Coleman Entrepreneurship Center. He is a member the Executive’s Club of Chicago, Young Professionals of Chicago, and the Young Executives Club.

Adam graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and received his MBA from DePaul University in Chicago, IL.
