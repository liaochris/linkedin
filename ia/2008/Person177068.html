Steve Kemper’s Summary

I've spent more than a decade building global brands and helping clients succeed online in the U.S. and abroad. I am an experienced online business strategist and marketer, and project manager, working with clients in retail, entertainment, health care, publishing, consumer goods, travel & tourism and the public sector.

My broad experience within the Internet industry, with a strong focus on international business, over the past decade has given me a holistic view of client needs and opportunities. I have a track record of success in online market entry and am a published author of articles and white papers on this and other subjects. In addition to writing, I address audiences around the country, from the Japan Society of New York to the SearchFest search marketing conference in Portland, Oregon (see below for recent workshops and presentations).

I have significant experience living and working in Japan, and have worked and traveled extensively throughout Asia and North America. I live in the finest city in the world: Portland, Oregon.
