William Roland’s Summary

With over 18 years of media experience in film, video, and television, William is now focusing on developing creative media projects that address the needs of highly targeted niche markets. His varied skills and experience include; project development and project management from concept through completion, marketing and promotion, media buying, local advertising, local news media marketing, writing, producing, directing, photography, graphic design, editing, duplication and distribution. As a former director of photography his media production experience includes; feature films, commercials, live and live-to-tape television, music videos, military video, medical video, industrial and corporate video. He worked for Carsey/Werner Productions on multiple nationally televised primetime sit-coms under the guidance of Nick McLean Sr. (former DP of Friends.) As an editor William is and expert in many forms of media from analog to digital and strives to create new and unique visual presentations. Currently he is the News Marketing Manger for the ABC affiliate station KGTV in San Diego where he recently collected two Emmy Awards for his promotions. William is also developing ProjectBaby.com with his wife Amy. William graduated with honors from the Brooks Institute of Photography where he won the Kodak Award for Cinematography.

Founder/CEO of Media Image Partners. Launched in 2007 MIP specializes in on-camera training, media coaching, and customized promotional video packages for the web.

Founder/CEO of Media Speedway. Launched in 2005, the company’s primary focus is to develop fully integrated creative media projects that address the needs of highly targeted nitch markets.
