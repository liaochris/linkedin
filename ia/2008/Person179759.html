Stan Leszynski’s Summary

In my 25 years as an information technology entrepreneur, I have founded, grown, and led several progressive technology companies, including software services, retail software, online commerce, and training. I have been a software architect and product manager for hundreds of leading-edge custom applications for clients across America. Microsoft has acquired or licensed a half-dozen of my inventions, including the Windows Snipping Tool, the Microsoft Office Calendar Control, and the inShape shape recognition toolkit.
I am a published book author and magazine columnist, and a noted public speaker who has lectured to over 10,000 software developers globally at industry events like VSLive! and TechEd. I authored a pioneering work on structured development that appeared on MSDN and in over a dozen books and was an industry standard for a decade.
I am a co-founder or charter member of the Northwest Entrepreneur Network, the Washington Software Association, the R:BASE Users Group, and the Access Users Group.
I am a native of Seattle where I reside with my wife and three children. I am an avid global vagabond and have wandered around 52 foreign countries with a backpack on.
