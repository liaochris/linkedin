Abhishek Bali’s Summary

I am an idea-oriented designer who believes Innovation (not just Design) really drives an organisation's ultimate growth path and that Design, in particular, should not just be looked at as a skill-based vertical but as a key differentiator in Business. This will be my growth channel in the years to come.

I am starting to appreciate products as not just objects but products as a part of a system. Design intervention at Systems level and then Product level is what interests me. I am gonna do something about it.

Whatever it takes, I am getting there!
