Alessio Botta’s Summary

Alessio Botta was born in Livorno, Italy, on August 10th, 1980.

He joined McKinsey & Company in May 2008 as a Junior Associate.

He received a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from the IMT Lucca Institute for Advanced Studies in April 2008. Also, he received the Laurea degree (cum laude) in Information Technology Engineering and the Laurea Specialistica degree (cum laude) in Information Technology Engineering for Business Management from the University of Pisa in 2002 and 2004, respectively.

Previous working experiences include research and lecturing activities at Suffolk University in Boston and at the University of Pisa.

Alessio Botta’s Specialties:

Fuzzy systems, genetic algorithms, computational intelligence, fuzzy databases, Web programming, Java, J2EE, PHP, Matlab
