Leonard Boord’s Summary

4 Time CEO.

Current: Founder of www.theboaters.com, a social network portal for the 10 million boat owners in the US.

2000-2006: ProcuraDigital. My two partners and I established ProcuraDigital as one of the leading providers of e-auctions and strategic sourcing for businesses operating in Latin America. Funded the company with and Angel round, later with a $20m round from Banco Santander. Sold our minority stake in 3/2006.

2001-2006 Participated in a MBO of Tallard Technologies, a $40 million a year distributor that grew into $100m before Banco Itautec purchased it in 2006.

1999 - Current Co-Founded Commlogik the largest telephony distributor for Latin America with offices in 5 countries. I am not active in the day to day operations.

1983 - 1998 Co Founded a PC retail store that grew from into a South Florida integrator and IT distribution company for businesses in Latin America. We succeeded in establishing it as one of the top 20 Hispanic companies in the United States, with 5 subsidiaries, 200 employees and US$160 million in revenues. My partners and I found a willing buyer in a Fortune 500 Company for this business.

I studied Computer Science and Math at The University of Miami. I am fluent in English and Spanish and have a good working knowledge of Portuguese.
Married since 1982 I am the proud father of two boys soon to be college bound.
