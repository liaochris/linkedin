Matt Norman’s Summary

MATT NORMAN started in the film industry as an actor. His diverse range led him to gain experience on many of Australia's leading Television dramas. Matt then moved into Film with roles in such films as "The Bank", "Moby Dick" and more recently in the 2007 hit "Ghost Rider" opposite Nicholas Cage.

Matt's career in the film industry is certainly different now from that of his past. With a variety of exciting jobs including a soldier in 1 Commando Regiment, body guard to such people as Madonna, Charlton Heston and many others; Matt's ambition to make films came from his desire to make a mark.

The Actors Cafe film production company was then born. Matt's different roles in his life has given him the understanding and natural need to want to tell compelling stories about real people or real events. "To build a production around an event to show others the true nature of living is what makes me tick".

Matt Norman's company has grown over the years. The Actors Cafe has now become a global competitor in the Motion Picture film industry as well as a contender in Australia's leading production and post production facility sector with The Actors Cafe running independently in FULL HD through their editing studio (Studio 1).
