Damian Hamber’s Summary

I started my IT career in a manufacturing company in Huddersfield and joined Digica, 3 years later as a trainee. I stayed with Digica as a developer and progressed to Analyst working on a number of ring fenced and shared service customers before leaving to join West Yorkshire Police IT department managing their C&C and GIS systems.

The promise of opportunities brought me back to Digica as an Analyst Programmer in 1997. Since then I have been involved in transition of Applications service and progression of service to acceptable service levels and have worked my way up to the lofty heights of Technical Team Leader, reporting to the IT Services Director.

I currently manage a ring fenced team of 3 highly experienced analysts/DBA's working along side 3 Oracle Application DBA's and a number of third parties for a client who is migrating all its business applications to Oracle 11i applications over a 10G database on AIX.

In mid-2006 I also completed the transition of a 3 person ring fenced contact centre into the team, bringing noticeable benefit for both the client and supplier to shift left and develop a tier 1.5 helpdesk, releasing tier 2 & 3 to get more work done!

The addition of Oracle Applications and Discoverer developers in 2007 brings the current total size of my direct reports to 8, effectively and successfully delivering across numerous service lines to one client.

My time is spent managing people to ensure effective delivery of projects and service alike. I work very much along side the Service Delivery managers on both sides of the relationship and am the single point of contact for client programme management relating to all active project engagements.
