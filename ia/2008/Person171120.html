Scott Kuehl’s Summary

Scott Kuehl (pronounced KEEL)

Personal Brand Strategist for the Enterprising American

Scott is a sought-after personal brand strategist for enterprising professionals who strive to achieve the American dream. He guides his clients to capitalize on their authentic personal brand by uncovering and communicating their unique promise of value.

Leveraging his genuine demeanor and entrepreneurial passion, Scott provides valuable creative insight to those high-potential stars who have earned their stripes, yet resolve to take their reputation, compensation and self-actualization to a higher level.

An award-winning expert on brand strategy, Scott invested the first twenty years of his career in building unique, memorable and compelling brands for America's rising enterprises. Now he leverages this knowledge, process and expertise to do the same for America's rising entrepreneurs.

Throughout his career, Scott has influenced the success of over 100 top-tier brands. From Main Street to Wall Street, his consulting experience includes advising savvy executives at start-ups, Fortune 50, non-profit, consumer and business-to-business companies.

Scott's consulting services have helped grow some of America's best-kept secrets as well as its most-recognized brands including 3M, Best Buy, Ceridian, Fair Isaac, Honeywell, National Car Rental, Supervalu and Target.

Scott founded the Midwest's premier business-to-business branding and marcom agency. He was president and executive creative director for over ten years when he sold the Top 25 company which was ranked on The Business Journal's Book of Lists.
