Andrew Sweet’s Summary

Andrew Sweet serves as the Director of SOA Strategy and Portfolio Management responsible for the development and execution of IBM's comprehensive SOA strategy. He is the executive responsible for managing the portfolio of software, services, and hardware that comprises IBM's SOA capabilities. Prior to this current position, Andy served as the World-wide executive responsible for delivering solutions around SOA and Business Process Management (BPM) within IBM’s Software Lab Services organization. In that role he has worked with IBM’s largest customers assisting with them with their business and technology ransformations. These customers have included State Farm, Bloomberg, AT&T, and MCI. He is a frequent speaker around industry best practices and enterprise transformations. Andy has also served on the boards of several industry standards groups and technology councils, including OASIS WS-Security and UDDI, and Washington University’s Center for the Application of Information Technology program. He is the former Chief Technology Officer of Perficient Incorporated (NASDAQ: PRFT).
