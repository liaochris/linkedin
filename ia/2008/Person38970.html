Kevin Johnson’s Summary

I am a creative catalyst and coach, providing insight and strategies for organizational, professional and personal development. My experience flows from public relations and Internet marketing to product development and multimedia—touching large companies as well as the independent professional, small business, and non-profit organization.

My clients include the State of Michigan, Motion Marketing & Media Agency, Bolin Enterprises, Starr Commonwealth Schools, Leazon Image Consulting, StarrVista Family Services, Salter-McNeil & Associates, recording artists, professional speakers and even runway models. Since 1996, I've been a trusted advisor to hundreds of businesses and associations. I have a unique offering of high tech/high touch marketing savvy, strong gift for strategic planning and unconventional creativity.

My mission is to help you build more value into your menu of products and services, and in many cases, doubling or tripling your revenue streams.

If you work with me as an individual, you can expect an increase in your productivity and personal profit margin. By conducting a sweep of the key areas of your life, I help you identify the constraints and remove or minimize them so you can fully deploy your strengths and multiply your effectivness.

I often speak, and write on Internet Marketing, Personal Reinvention, Innovation & Creativity. If you're a part of my LinkedIn network, you can ask me anything...I'm happy to offer my advice and experience to advance your success.
