Marcel Cramer’s Summary

Born in May, 1972. Zodiac: Taurus. Traveled the world with my family till the age of 12. Got back into school in the Netherlands from the age of 13. Started working after that period.

First experienced the possibilities of Internet in 1996 and got hooked, instantaneously. Started my first Internet related job at Sony Netherlands in 1997. Now, many years later, I run InCircle an agency specialized in interactive marketing & communications and business development support, based in Utrecht.

My strengths are innovative thinking, being able to bridge disciplines, strong in consultancy, analytical and clear about processes and how to get where you want to go. Marketing, communications and inter(net)activity are my favorite subjects.

I look forward to get to know likeminded spirits who are inventive, hard working, honest and fun!
