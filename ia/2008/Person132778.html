Mike Bloom’s Summary

I started working when I was 11, in a family run service station. I worked throughout my teenage years after school. After High School, I attended college and got a degree. I worked while attending college and after graduating they promoted me to a managerial position. Soon thereafter, I was recruited away. That was a flop and so I started working at an office equipment company. We moved and I got into outside sales. We moved again and I got hired to sell office equipment, outside sales. We moved again and a big office equipment company hired me. I wasn't that successful there and got hired at a smaller more aggressive office equipment company. I did well. When that company got purchased a few of us joined another small company. Then our previous manager started his own company we jumped ship and started something special. We kicked butt! That company got bought out and I had had enough. Mistakenly I joined a small computer reseller business at the wrong time. Then a previous manager bought into an office equipment supplier I joined him. We grew the company and it got sold! I then reached out big and got hired by a manufacturer and was a major account rep. Unfortunately, that company had such a poor reputation my efforts were in vain and I was unsuccessful. For about a year and a half I was unemployed. Then I was rescued by a friend who needed help painting and I started painting. I excelled and grew to enjoy the work. In 8 years I became crew leader and business partner. Then it was time to start my own painting business. During the painting, my love of classic Cadillacs blossomed into a part time business in which I began buying and selling cars. Which has turned into an ongoing business and has branched out into multiple brands of cars. My long term goal is to grow that business and retire with the car of my dreams having built a business from passion and desire.

Mike Bloom’s Specialties:

Turning bad condition exteriors into great looking exteriors, managing a crew, hiring the right guys for the job, doing colorful interiors, restoration to original surfaces, and repainting homes, walls and woodwork.
