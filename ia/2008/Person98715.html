Jim Lammers’s Summary

President and founder of Trinity Animation, Inc. - a company that provides 3D animation and computer graphics services, in addition to sales, support and training of 3D software and hardware. Web store at www.trinity3d.com is a popular source among 3D artists for all software/hardware for production. Author of 4 books on 3D animation, each published by Pearson/NRP in at least 6 languages.

Jim Lammers’s Specialties:

3ds max, Maya, After Effects, Combustion, Photoshop, Network administration, training, file conversion, problem solving.
