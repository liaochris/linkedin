Paul A. Ridge’s Summary

I have been on the forefront of two business disciplines, project management and accounting, for the past 10 years. I have significant experience managing software projects from start to finish including needs assessment, contract negotiations, ERP implementations, user acceptance training and migrations. I am also well versed in the most up-to-date methodologies and statistical tools employed in most corporate improvement projects today. I also have more than 6 years' experience as a staff accountant for various corporations working on such issues as SOX compliance, arbitrage and cash-flow improvements and GL reconciliations. I think my combined experience as a PM and staff accountant gives me a valuable perspective on how front- and back-office business roles can complement and enhance each other's effectiveness. Here are some of my professional accomplishments just from the past year:

+ Championed the research, purchase and implementation of LexisNexis Legal in a completely cost-neutral way, significantly streamlining legal research process.
+ Negotiated with LexisNexis and Westlaw to get the lowest prices per user ever offered by those companies.
+ Streamlined accounting and payroll procedures to reduce time and cost of doing payroll by 33%.
+ Increased headcount and reduced support staff workload in a cost-neutral way by reassigning duties and eliminating redundant tasks based on process mapping.
+ Implemented GAAP and SOX-compliant internal accounting controls to achieve the company's first-ever perfect score on an external audit.
+ Key contributor in RFP-winning strategy development which made ACA the Puget Sound region's market share leader in public defense services.

While my most recent position was with a non-profit organization, I have also worked for Hilton (the hotel chain; in customer service for billing disputes) and such multi-billion dollar companies as CitiStreet (a Citibank/State Street venture) and Shire Pharmaceuticals.
