Jerry Land, CPC’s Summary

My articles published on Electronic Recruiting Exchange (www.ere.net): http://www.ere.net/articles/db/3F4D166406F6456F93AE993145340A5E.asp
http://www.ere.net/articles/db/DA5B2DF5CB414FA2A9C2602732A09653.asp

I joined Stephen James to build a team of Executive Search and Recruiting Professionals responsible for developing a strong pipeline of Sales & Marketing talent for our clients.

Because more often than not, the most talented professionals aren't actively seeking a new position, we take a proactive approach to locate and entice ideal candidates to entertain our client's opportunity. We attract candidates and get them to buy in on having an open mind to learn more about our client.

This is easier said than done. In order to accomplish this, we must have a complete understanding of the hiring need and what the client is looking to accomplish by filling the position. We do a lot of discovery with potential candidates to determine if and where the opportunity would peak their interest.

We continue to facilitate the hiring process because the candidates that we attract aren't making a job change because they have to. They are doing it because they feel it will better satisfy their career and personal goals. This is different from the traditional interviewing process that was designed to screen out candidates as opposed to lure individuals who are currently successfully employed. Therefore, the entire search and recruiting process must be handled delicately as things that could derail the placement often surface that must be addressed.
