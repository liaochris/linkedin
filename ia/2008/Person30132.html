Camilo Rodriguez’s Summary

Camilo Rodriguez has been in the Canadian financial industry for over six years.

Born and raised in Bogota, Colombia he finished his high school in Denison, Iowa, USA. He then graduated from Electrical Engineering finishing his studies in Toulouse, France in microelectronics.

After moving to British Columbia he became a financial advisor working with clients in insurance planning, retirement planning and investment portfolios.

He completed his financial experience and knowledge in Real Estate assets by joining TD Canada Trust as Mortgage Manager in Vancouver, BC. Currently he works as a Mortgage Broker for Origin Home Financial Partners.

He works with banks and mortgage lending institutions to bring Real Estate financing solutions for his clients.

Camilo specializes in Mortgage Refinancing and Mortgage Renewals
He runs a blog about Mortgages, Real Estate and Money: camiloblog.com
