Katherine Brungs’s Summary

Katherine lends 20+ years of advanced experience holding senior positions as Corporate Communications Manager at a Fortune 500 Company and Assistant Vice President of Marketing, along with other leadership roles at three major U.S. corporations. Throughout her extensive career, she developed strong competencies in strategic, integrated brand and marketing communications management. She is well versed in project management, internal communications, PR, media relations, Web site design and social media disciplines. Katherine has demonstrated sound business acumen and leadership in B2B and B2C communications and also is an accomplished artist, writer and graphic designer.

Katherine founded Lacewing Communications with a strong desire to partner with business owners and help them evolve their companies to be competitive, profitable and distinguished in their industries and target markets.

Lacewing Communications provides businesses with high caliber, innovative marketing consultation and communications solutions. Our seasoned consultants bring expertise from working with Fortune 500 and blue-chip companies, international design consultancies, high-end advertising and design agencies and more. Brilliant minds from a range of communications disciplines unite to closely collaborate with our clients to compass their specific objectives. Together, we deliver exceptional results to guide and meet our clients' financial and business goals using thoughtful innovation.

We truly believe that in order to succeed in any business today, marketers must embody a pioneering spirit, along with a strong desire to embrace new ideas, emerging technologies and evolving business trends. Our main objective is to deliver the fullest extent of today’s most vital marketing disciplines using a harmonized approach — brand identity, marketing, print/online communications, PR and media relations. An integrated, consistent approach to brand and marketing management is imperative to success.

Katherine Brungs’s Specialties:

Lacewing Communications customizes each project and provides premium level products & services. We specialize in developing personalized brand identity programs, and strategic, integrated marketing. Our Services~ brand (logo, business systems), naming of products & services, advertising, print (newsletters, brochures, annual reports), electronic and print direct mail campaigns, Web site design, social media, and PR/media relations. We effectively serve clients in the U.S. and internationally.
