Scott Siegrist’s Summary

Think about this… When was the last time you updated your Will? How would you handle a warranty claim if the company would not honor it? How would you handle an incorrect phone bill if the company was uncooperative? Have you ever signed a contract you felt a little uneasy about it or simply signed a contract without a legal review? Have you ever wished you could get legal advice without having to make a financial decision first? Have you ever had a speeding ticket?

This is where Pre-Paid Legal comes in…Pre-Paid Legal is to legal fees as health insurance is to medical bills. As a member, you are leveraging the power of 1.5 million other members and you don’t have to sift through the yellow pages to find an attorney.

Making "Equal Justice Under Law" is our business...our passion! For a low monthly fee, members have access to a full-service law firm for a wide range of valuable legal services. For less than they'd pay for a cup of coffee a day, you are provided benefits that cover most common legal needs you will encounter.
