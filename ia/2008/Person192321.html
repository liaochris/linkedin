Tracy Viselli’s Summary

New media strategist who successfully combines traditional public relations and social media strategies to increase brand awareness and online reach of online properties.

As owner and CEO of Reno Fabulous Media, I develop websites, including tcontent and marketing strategies that make the best use of social and traditional media, grow brands, create and manage online content, attract user-generated content, build and manage communities, and hopefully make a difference in the off-line world in progressive politics. I have won numerous writing and blogging accolades and have been an active participant in some of the biggest progressive blogger/media dust-ups including:

- Creative Commons campaign to license presidential debate video
- Fox News democratic presidential debate boycott
- Associated Press boycott over free use issues

Reno Fabulous Media properties include:

Reno Fabulous: www.renofabulous.com
