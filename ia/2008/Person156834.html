ranjeet gupta’s Summary

Presently, I am working with HCL Technologies as Technical Lead , setting up analog group for their analog facilities in Noida.

Before this I had worked with LIPP group at NXP Semiconductors. My team was involved in designing High Speed serial links like PCI Ex , HDMI , MIPI , USB etc. I worked on HDMI Transmitter PHY and PCI Express in CMOS065 node.

Before this, I worked for Front-End Manufacturing & Technology ( FTM ) group at STMicroelectronics , Noida. I was working with IO design team in NVM Technologies. My responsibilities included design of standard/complex IOs, supporting analog blocks and Oscillators. I was also responsible for mentoring other team members of the group.
