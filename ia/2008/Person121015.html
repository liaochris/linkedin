Mari Davis’s Summary

Mari has an impressive record of marketing and communications successes, including: dramatically increasing revenues, enhancing awareness of products and services, significantly growing web site traffic, and generating valuable media attention.

Mari’s background includes heading up marketing, communications, and publicity departments for a $105-million regional financial institution, a book publishing company, and an international non-profit organization.

In her own business (see: www.maridavisassociates.com), she and her team have worked with clients in many lines of business, including: retail, technology, property development/management, natural medicine, athletics/sports, the arts, travel/leisure services, animal-related services, real estate, and more.

Mari also has years of broadcast and print media experience, fostering ratings increases/successes at every media outlet.

She is an award-winning journalist who has been a TV news anchor, morning radio personality, national cable TV show host, and writer/producer. Mari has written more than 10,000 news/feature stories and documentaries, and has interviewed thousands of celebrities, politicians, business leaders, athletes, and people from all walks of life.
