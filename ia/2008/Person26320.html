Brandon Bethea’s Summary

Brandon Bethea is a balanced marketing professional with a proven record of strategic initiatives, creative solutions, and tactical executions.

Brandon is accomplished in developing and managing interactive marketing programs to develop brand position, enhance the user experience, and maximize the return on investment.

He has excelled within both the agency and corporate environments of Hancock Advertising, Cox Communications, Verizon Superpages.com, and Idearc Media. His efforts have produced the following profitable results:

* Email program generated a 500% ROI.
* Website redesign increased unit sales by 213%.
* Intranet streamlined workflow for 90 departments.

Brandon frequently contributes business-related advice and articles to various online communities, blogs, forums, and websites.
