Candace Brown’s Summary

I love helping companies find their unique path to success in the market. I have a unique combination of strong analytical and creative skills that provides the added insights to all the possibilities of creating a successful relationship and venture.

I provide a broad range of sales, marketing, and business development experience with major accounts, OEMs, and partners. President's Club and Major Account Awards. In top 10% of sales executives throughout most of my career (120-190% of quota 12 of 15 years selling).
