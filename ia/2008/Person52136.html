    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; XEL; Utilities industry)

    My role is a design engineer in the electrical group. The general role is to develop electrical projects or contribute the electrical portion of larger projects. The focus of my department (Design) is primarily small capital expenditures (<$200K) and operational modifiations.

  • Engineering Team Leader

    Liberty Paper

    (Paper & Forest Products industry)

    April 1996 — June 2008 (12 years 3 months)

    Liberty Paper is a division of Liberty Diversified Industries (LDI). Liberty Paper manufactures linerboard for making corrugated boxes. 200,000 tons of paper is created from 100% recycled material each year and this generates revenues of about $100M. In 2001, the operation expanded to include an off-machine coater and an entirely new profit center for the company.

    My primary role as Engineering Team Leader was to manage a small technical staff towards meeting the technical needs of the mill. This was a combination role of supervisor and individual contributor. It included the supervision of staff engineers, technician, interns, contract engineers, and consultants. The main responsibilities of the team included capital projects, operational modifications, environmental compliance, document management, lab instrument management, process trials and evaluations, management of technical service contracts, and general technical support to both production and maintenance.

    I had the privilege of being selected and assigned to a corporate team researching opportunities for growth into the healthcare Industry in general. The team was comprised of 8 leaders within the company; supervisors, managers, and one general manager. The team also had a Vice President assigned as a coach and advisor.

    The team elected me to be the team leader, and we worked together to research and identify worthy and fitting product & market segments within the healthcare industries. The results were delivered in February of 2006 and the company brought the recommendations to first fruition in June of 2008 with the acquisition of the Ergolet company of Denmark.
