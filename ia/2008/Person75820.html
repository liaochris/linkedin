Anthony Godoy’s Summary

Anthony Godoy started as a writer, columnist, photographer and managing editor working with newspapers and magazines. Following a term with The Orange County Register, Anthony joined a small development team incubating Pets.com.

His subsequent experience producing interactive projects for film and television was also a reintroduction to video. He specialized in producing interactive projects with integrated video, dynamic content and database-driven sites.

Between 2004 and 2006, Anthony juggled numerous responsibilities developing hi and standard definition broadcast television with Bennett Productions, a Los Angeles production company specializing in bikini television. While there he was a hands on designer, video editor, writer and producer. He split his time there between production, and developing markleting materials fo Bennett's branch distribution arm, Bennett Media Worldwide, where he wrote, produced or designed marketing materials.

Where chasing bikini models became Bennett's main focus, it never really appealed to Anthony, who in May of 2006 left Bennett forming Heavy Trick Media.

Anthony Godoy’s Specialties:

Script writing, story development and editing, content development and acquisitions, episodic format development, voice over development and direction, editorial review and content direction. Development of marketing materials, graphic design and interactive promotions.
