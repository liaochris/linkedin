Richard Trombly’s Summary

In the field of journalism, I have more than 10 years experience.
I have management experience as a news editor of both a weekly and daily newspaper and as an editor of a national trade magazine.

I am currently living in Shanghai, China where I have undertaken several projects including working in the film industry and co-founding EAST WEST THEATER, a semi-professional drama troupe.

I have developed experience and understanding of PR, media management and corporate communications both as a business journalist and through PR and marketing consulting work with various professional organizations and NGOs as well as corporations including PricewaterhouseCoopers, Hill and Knowlton and Haers. I have taught business writing and communications and academic writing at the college level as an associate lecturer at several institutions in China.
