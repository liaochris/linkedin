Craig Little’s Summary

I have been in the IT industry for 22 years. I started as a Junior Programmer, working on projects of varying sizes to become a Team Leader and Project Manager. I have worked in a range of industries, varying from technical companies such as IBM and Fujitsu to FMCG, Web-based Marketing, Communications, Manufacturing, Financial Services, Banking, and Government.

I would bring a style of leadership that would encourage people to grow and unlock their full potential. As a Manager and Team Leader I plan carefully and use consultation and innovation as the cornerstones of the teams’ effort. I am a patient, transparent, honest, ethical and approachable leader. I am able to communicate effectively at all levels of the organisation. I am a sharing teacher and mentor able to motivate staff in a trusting, results oriented team environment. I am easy to talk to and a good listener who generates a great deal of trust from team members by being approachable.

I am a member of the Australian Computer Society, the Australian Institute of Project Management, the Project Management Institute and the IT Service Management Forum (itSMF),

Craig Little’s Specialties:

I have experience with Policy Development, Change Management, Organisational Change, Budgeting and Forecasting, Project Reporting (Financial and Status), Problem Management, Issue Management, Contract Negotiation, Procurement, Quality Assurance, Training and Skill Transfer, Analysis of User Requirements and ITIL Service Management. My strongest asset is my ability to see through the technological clutter and deliver solutions that are elegant and fulfil the businesses needs.
