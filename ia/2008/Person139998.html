Valentine Makhouleen’s Summary

Valentine Makhouleen is an interactive art director with over six years of experience in driving engaging online creative. He has recently worked with several major advertising agencies; the Ontario government; major brands, such as McDonald's, General Mills, Bell Canada, Shoppers Drug Mart; and several graphic art magazines.

When Valentine is not working, he can be found in his rocking chair, strumming Johnny Cash on his mandolin. He is always on the lookout for avenues to produce great award-winning creative.
