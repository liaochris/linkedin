Rick Eberle’s Summary

I am a Public Relations and marketing expert having experience in this field at HJMT Communications and at Todd Shapiro Associates over the past several years working for both corporate and non profit clients helping them achieve positive results and generate buzz for their companies both on the street and on the web.

I've also enjoyed a long career in Artist and Venue Management, Sales/Business Development, Interactive Marketing & Online Content Management in the Entertainment industry working for Arista Records (Pink, Dido, Outkast, Usher), The Downtown (shows with Story of the Year, Fallout Boy, Spin Doctors) and at SoundSpectrum (visual content for touring and web for David Gilmour of Pink Floyd, Aerosmith, Avril Lavigne). Also, check out PopCore Entertainment www.popcore.net. Here I work with venues such as The Crazy Donkey www.thecrazydonkey.com through Loaded Rock Shows www.clubloaded.com, and for musical groups such as John Hampson (of Nine Days) www.myspace.com/johnhampson, Melissa Reyes (of the Search for the next Pussycat Doll) www.myspace.com/melissareyesmusic, Push Play www.pushplayrox.com, Paging Grace www.paginggrace.com, Tauk www.taukrocks.com and others. We also handle business clients such as Music School Inc www.musicschoolinc.com and the professional wrestler/actor Brimstone www.entrancetohell.com.

I am co host and co producer of the radio show Unlabeled on 94.3 WMJC every Sunday night at 10, also streaming at www.wmjcfm.com and archived at www.unlabeledmusic.net and am co founder of the EasyCore www.easycore.org music and media Happy Hour at Munchaba Lounge in Levittown www.myspace.com/munchabalounge every friday night from 6-9

I started in music at a young age and am a singer/songwriter/guitarist with 15 years of playing, writing and performance experience in the band Iridesense www.iridesense.com. Fun Fact: We have songs that have appeared on Zoey 101, Drake & Josh, iCarly and others.
