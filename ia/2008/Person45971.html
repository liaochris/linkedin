David Anthony (danthony@21ventures.net)’s Summary

21Ventures is a venture capital fund focusing on the technologies and ideas that will dominate the 21st century.
Our fields of expertise include clean technologies, mobile software and security. We invest primarily in early-seed companies possessing proprietary technologies poised to change the market landscape.
However, here at 21Ventures we are not traditional venture capitalists. Early stage companies might possess the technologies to change the world, but great ideas do not always lead to great returns. That is why 21Ventures believes in taking a hands-on approach to investing.
21Ventures leverages not only its financial capital, but its human capital as well, utilizing the diverse expertise of its partners and management to support our portfolio companies from their earliest stages through profitability, acquisition or IPO. Our partners are our strategic backbone, providing a vast network of business relationships alongside years of business and technological experience.
21Ventures leads its companies and its investors to success by providing the resources, marketing tools, scientific expertise and professionalism needed to thrive in an ever evolving, global marketplace.
With over $250 million under management, we are constantly seeking out new opportunities for investment and return; therefore, we work overtime to shift quickly and strategically into new opportunities.
