Oleg Kiselev’s Summary

Over 20 years of software industry experience.

Currently the Chief Architect at Parascale.

The previous 17 years have been spent at VERITAS Software (now part of Symantec Corp), where I helped it grow from a tiny start-up (fewer than 25 people when i joined) to the 5th largest software company in the world. My focus at VERITAS/Symantec has been primarily the storage management architecture and technologies in clustered and distributed systems. I hold a number of issued and pending patents in these areas.

Professionally, I am not very interested in doing the exact same things I've successfully done before. I don't work just for a salary.

IMPORTANT: In accordance with my moral, political and ethical beliefs, I WILL NOT WORK for companies that do involuntary blanket employee drug testing. I do not object to rational, individualized drug and alcohol abuse prevention and testing programs for jobs that involve public health and safety, or involve operation of heavy machinery. This has been my unwavering stand for over 20 years. I expect it to be respected by employers, much like they respect their employees' religious and moral views.
