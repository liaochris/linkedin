Paul To’s Summary

Paul To is in charge of Nortel's Incubator and it's Open Innovation programs. Paul joined Nortel in September 2006 with 18+ years of experience driving innovation and commercialization of new technologies. His mandates at Nortel are to reinvigorate the innovation culture within Nortel's 30,000 strong employee base, to modernize Nortel's open innovation models, and to manage the Incubator program which invests tens of millions a year in internal startups.

Before Nortel, Paul played leadership roles in very large companies like HP, Bank of Montreal, a Silicon Valley 2001 IPO called Riverstone Networks, and ran his own $1M+ consulting business. Paul has a proven track record in entrepreneurial innovation, and in his ability to recognize & incubate strategic opportunities from the very early stages. Paul's current passion is in studying and practicing open innovation models, and unleashing hidden power of innovation that is embedded within many large and complex organizations.
