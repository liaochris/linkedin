Anissa Varnell’s Summary

I have 14 years of recruiting experience ranging from agency to corporate. I love working with the executive management teams and pride myself in representing my clients and in building great relationships with candidates. I am very honest and ethical and enjoy working with a wide range of people.

My expertise is in start-up environments, acquisitions and rapid and long term company expansion. I work directly with HR leaders and executive management teams to devise and implement a hiring strategy that is highly efficient, cost effective and meets the hiring goals based upon corporate and investor commitments and product release cycles.

I am a full life cycle recruiter and my roles have allowed me to participate in and developing new hire training and orientation,implementing onboarding processes and procedures, provide interview training and other training as it relates to hiring as needed and mentoring hiring managers. I'm not afraid to roll up my sleeves and do anything that needs to be done. In addition, my roles have required that I act as an onsite HR business partner who laisons with direct HR business partners.

I thoroughly understand technical requirements and am very happy to have worked with Seattle and the Silicon Valley's top technical talent over the years. I have enjoyed college recruiting, implemented internship programs and have extensive experience in compensation analysis.

And I enjoy what I do very much! :)
