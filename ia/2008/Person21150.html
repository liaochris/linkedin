Ben Boswell’s Summary

A senior sales professional with ten successful years experience selling enterprise hardware, software and service solutions principally in the financial, manufacturing, media, publishing, telecoms and hi-tech vertical sectors.

I have just returned from a 6 month sabbatical where I traveled through South Africa, SE Asia, New Zealand and the US.

I am currently Data Solutions Sales Manager for f5 Acopia. f5 acquired Acopia in July 2007 - I am helping to build the new UK and Ireland sales organisation to accelerate revenue growth for Acopia's market leading File Virtualisation technology.

I am involved in both direct sales engagement with corporate and commercial enterprises whilst also developing a new channel orientated "go to market" business model

Prior to this I had a successful three year tenure at EMC. During this time I held a number of new business positions finishing as a Sales Team Leader for the London City Commercial territory. Each year I delivered on or above quota demonstrating considerable sales ability and continual drive for execution. This variety has allowed exposure to all commercial elements of both the corporate and mid-market sectors.
