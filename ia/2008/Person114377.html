Lisa Herrod’s Summary

Lisa Herrod is an Independent Web Consultant with 9 years of hands-on, professional experience working on the web, including web design and development, education and consulting at a senior level.

Lisa has a Diploma in Website Management and Production, is a qualified trainer and has taught website design at Sydney Institute, specialising in subjects such as (X)HTML, CSS, User Interface Design, Usability and Accessibility.

She is also an experienced presenter, having spoken at Web Essentials, Web Directions, Open Publish, the Web Standards Group and other corporate engagements. Lisa is currently completing a Master of Interactive Multimedia.

Lisa is known for her high level of professionalism, which has seen the successful delivery of projects across many industries, including: Banking, Travel, Online Publishing, Government, Commercial Enterprise and Mobile Devices.

She is well respected by industry peers, being invited to participate as a:
• Foundation Committee Member and Treasurer of WIPA (Web Industry Professionals Association, wipa.org.au)
• Core Member of the Web Standards Group
• Judge of the 13th Annual AIMIA Awards, and
• Judge (usability) of the inaugural McFarlane prize for excellence in web site design (2006).

Lisa is also a member of the Usability Professionals Association, the Human Factors and Ergonomics Society of Australia and the Australian Sign Language Interpreter Association.

Lisa Herrod’s Specialties:

Lisa’s strength lies in her knowledge and practical experience in website design and production, while applying a focus on user needs and best practice in site development.

Her expertise covers areas such as:
• Requirements Analysis
• User Centred Design and Usability Testing
• Accessibility
• Web Standards

Lisa's passion and expertise is in her ability to apply a holistic approach to site development with a true focus on current best practice and user centred design principles.
