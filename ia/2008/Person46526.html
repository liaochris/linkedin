  • Account Executive

    Dayak, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    March 2008 — Present (3 months)

    As an Account Executive for Dayak , my main focus is on new business development and account management. Dayak is an innovative recruiting marketplace that allows employers and recruiters to conduct business in a productive web based environment that foster the main themes of free market economics.

    There is no cost for employers to post jobs on the website. Employers simply create a job, and then set the fee they are willing to pay for a successful hire. Recruiters then compete to fill the jobs, resulting in faster fill rates and much lower fees. The site is also free for recruiters. Dayak allows recruiters to improve efficiency and make more money in more ways.
