Sami Niemelä’s Summary

I am a creative director and UX consultant, with a deep and throughout experience on visual communications and interaction design.

I have been involved within digital design for the last 10 years, mobile sphere since the first days of web/wap portals, lived to tell through the dotcom crash.

I believe an individual can and should change the world with his actions and I have a very strong will to also do so. We live in exciting times, and I am thrilled to be alive right here, right now.

Sami Niemelä’s Specialties:

Creative direction, visual communication design, interactive user experiences within physical and digital products, brands, services and spaces. Touch interaction and interface design.
