David Dickinson’s Summary

David is highly experienced in the related fields of learning, education and social inclusion, with more than 25 years involvement in the sector. His depth of understanding results from a decade of teaching and management, five years of curriculum development & research and seven more of project direction and consultancy. He spent a further eleven years as education lead for Fujitsu Services leaving in April '07. David has a client list which includes Cisco Systems, Nascent Form, Celltek Communications and Arc Software Consultancy. David is also a director of Socratic Dialogue Limited.

David Dickinson’s Specialties:

technology assisted learning, foresight mapping, personalisation, government liaison

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    February 2008 — Present (4 months)

    "A proven stimulus for innovation is the meeting of Unlike Minds."
    Unlike Minds is a capability network, based upon cooperative principles, whose associates are all recognised leaders in their respective fields. Each associate has in common a passion for working with organisations and communities to transform learning, encourage the development of human capital and community engagement. Client engagement focuses upon innovation in e-learning and information relevance, the application of cybernetic principles and continuing personal development. A number of associates are heavily involved in the Building Schools for the Future programme in the UK. Key clients include Cisco Systems, Giunti, consortia bidding in the Building Schools for the Future programme, a number of universities and colleges as well as communities and SMEs sharing our aspirations and values.
