Bob Johnson’s Summary

I've been involved with a number of start-ups over the past 15 years in Europe and the U.S. in areas as diverse as computer technology, international trade & distribution, and motorsports management.

Prior to founding Thothic Technology Partners, I spent over four years working with Sonork SRL and GTV Solutions, Inc. as Sr. Vice President responsible for the sales and support activities of the company related to the Sonork Enterprise IM product. I also served 10-years in the US Army during the 1980's as a cryptologic linguist, working in intelligence collection & analysis and related areas.

In founding Thothic Technology Partners, LLC, I siezed an opportunity to do some of the things I love most: meeting the challenges of building a new business from the ground up, being involved in helping to develop and introduce new technologies to the market, and working with some of the brightest and most creative people in the world. I thrive on new challenges and applying the things I've learned in meeting previous ones, especially opportunities to develop and apply new technologies.

Bob Johnson’s Specialties:
