Jim Graham’s Summary

Jim Graham, Directing Consultant with Maximum Business Advantage, has several years experience working with large organizations in technical, consulting and managerial roles. His most recent corporate experience was as a consultant for IBM working on large-scale government implementation projects as a quality and risk manager. In this role, Jim led a team of individuals who worked together and virtually. A strategic, blue-ocean thinker, Jim has consistently produced action plans for extraordinary efficiency-gains and cost-reductions for various organizations. Jim is passionate about service delivery, risk management, and ethical and sustainable business practices. Jim has an MBA with specialization in Services Marketing and Management and a Master of Health Services Administration degree.
