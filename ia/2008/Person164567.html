Ron Williams’s Summary

Proven ecommerce executive with over fifteen years of experience leading teams at fast-paced online game, Internet infrastructure, and financial services companies. Currently lead a team of over 50 in-house and outsourced marketing staff, software engineers, and customer service staff based in the USA, Korea, China, and Guyana.

Manage the P&L for the recent North American launch of Lunia a massive multi-player online role playing game (MMORPG) and the soon to be launched Digimon RPG, designed and lead the operation of online video game portal 12FootTall.

Extensive background in managing large-scale Internet ecommerce deployments includes capacity planning, quality assurance testing, and production management.

Responsibilities have included managing multi-million dollar P&L, large information technology capital expenditure budgets, JAVA software testing budgets, corporate infrastructure projects, and product deployments. Extensive experience managing, deploying, and supporting large Fortune 500 enterprise-level IT infrastructure and ecommerce projects servicing thousands to millions of users.

Certified to teach several Solaris System Administration courses for Sun Microsystems and have done so on multiple occasions.

Significant history of JAVA application and Oracle database tuning. Credentials as a software developer with training in SQL, COBOL, Assembly, and C programming languages.

Participated in several formal total quality management and process improvement projects including a large government development group’s migration through Level 3 of the Carnegie Mellon Capability Maturity for Software Model (CMM) and more recently, EarthLink’s and CompuCredit's move to formal service management and service delivery processes including the launch of the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) framework.
