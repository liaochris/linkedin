Sotiria Dimitrellou’s Summary

PhD candidate and Research Fellow at the Mechanical Design & Control Systems Department in School of Mechanical Engineering at National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) with research interests on:
Mechanical Design, Design for Assembly, Design for Manufacturing,
Tolerance Analysis and Synthesis, Cost-optimum Tolerancing,
Rapid Prototyping, Reverse Engineering,
Development of computer-based tools for CAD applications,
Modelling of operational processes and risk management.
Focused on the development of new techniques and software for analysis and synthesis of optimum dimensional and geometrical tolerances of mechanical products.

Assistant Educator of the undergraduate courses Mechanical Design Ι, Design for Manufacture ΙI and Mechanical Design ΙΙ for the years 2002-2007 in School of Mechanical Engineering at NTUA.

Mechanical Engineer in school buildings design (Electrical, Plumbing, Heating, Ventilations, Air-Conditioning).

Developer of the computer-based tool COPTOL for cost-optimum tolerance design.
