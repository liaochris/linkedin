    (Philanthropy industry)

    January 2000 — Present (8 years 8 months)

    Women's Economic Ventures (WEV, pronounced "weave') is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating an equitable and just society through the economic empowerment of women across the globe.

    Women’s Economic Ventures has been training and empowering women since 1991 to be economically self-sufficient. WEV provides a comprehensive 14 week, 56 hour self-employment training program, as well as provides individual business counseling, advanced training, coaching and networking opportunities. In addition, WEV has a Small Business Loan Fund that has loaned over $1,500,000 dollars to small business owners in our community.
