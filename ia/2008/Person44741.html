Daragh O Brien’s Summary

Experience

* Experienced Project Manager in complex information management environments ranging from Single View of Customer to Compliance Control functions.
* Recognised thought leader in field of Information Quality Management, with numerous articles published in a variety of forums and experience presenting at and chairing Industry conferences world wide.
*Extensive experience working with virtual teams and 'remote' team members, through both 'day job' and voluntary association activities.
