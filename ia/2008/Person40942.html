Michelle James’s Summary

CEO of The Center for Creative Emergence.
http://www.creativeemergence.com

Personal and Organizational creativity and innovation consultant, facilitator and Creative Emergence coach. Designs and delivers unique programs to unleash the self-organizing collective intelligence of groups, and the individuals within them, in functional ways. Works with thought leaders to develop creative and innovative organizational climates and cultures. Creates dynamic accelerated learning environments.

Work with leaders, executives, entrepreneurs, intrapreneurs and aspiring entrepreneurs to support them in developing work, approaches, products, services and systems that integrate their highest level of creative expression, purpose, service and business bottom line.

Passion includes awakening systems and the people within them to life - aware, engaged, and animated - to create inspired, evolved results. Focus includes the personal (values, meaning), emotional (heart), relational, cognitive (brain/mind/intellect), somatic (body),systemic, cultural, and environmental aspects of organizational and individual creativity. Bring into integration: reflection and action; listening and articulating; analysis and intuition; left and right brain; strategy and improvisation; and enjoyment with practical focus.

Established Quantum Leap Theater© - business improv theater for leaps in thinking, being, and innovating. Performer of full length improvised plays with Precipice Improvisational Theater Company (http://www.precipiceimprov.com) and CoreSomatics® Movement and Bodywork Master Practitioner.

Founder and coordinator of the Capitol Creativity Network in Washington, DC. http://www.capitolcreativitynetwork.com
