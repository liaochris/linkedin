Scott Brown’s Summary

Senior recruiter with nearly 14 years of experience, spanning a wide range of industries and disciplines (including Information Technology, Administrative, Financial, Accounting, Healthcare, Aerospace, Real Estate, Banking and Biosciences). I am an active and open networker who will gladly accept all network invites.

Goodrich Aerostructures is currently involved in a very large SAP implementation that will continue throughout 2012. We are actively seeking (updated as of 2/08) several senior level SAP professionals (all perm/full time positions here in San Diego) in the following disciplines (all functional): SCM (SRM and QM/Calibration), FI/CO (AP, Costing), BW (team lead or supervisor) and MRO. Please contact me if you or someone you know might be looking for a perm position within a growing Fortune 500 Aerospace company in the San Diego area. We are specifically seeking those consultants who are tired of project based work, and who want to settle down and grow within a stable company. Our positions require only 10% (or less) travel. Please submit your resume for immediate consideration; all of our current openings throughout the US can be viewed at www.aerostructures.goodrich.com/

We also have a wide range of accounting, finance, IT and engineering positions that are primarily based here in San Diego. All Goodrich openings may be viewed at www.aerostructures.goodrich.com/

PLEASE NOTE: I am unable to utilize any third party vendors of any kind in order to fill my SAP positions. Please do not send me your unsolicited "hot lists"; I simply can't use them.
