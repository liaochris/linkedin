Mehmet Yildiz’s Summary

Dr. Mehmet Yildiz has been working in IT industry for 24 years and currently working in IBM Australia as an Executive IT Architect. He is an IBM Senior Certified IT Architect in Enterprise Architecture discipline. He worked in large global organisations such as NATO and Siemens covering a large array of leading edge technologies. He is also Master Certified IT Architect by The Open Group. Mehmet worked as a principal consultant and technical specialist in the distributed computing and infrastructure areas with the credentials MCSE, MCT, Cisco Network Asscociate and ComTIA network professional before starting in IBM. He is member of the British Computer Society (BCS) and Institute for Engineering and Technology (IET) in UK.
