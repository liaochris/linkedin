Sara Beltrame’s Summary

My professional experience started more than ten years ago. I've published a book for one of the greatest publisher in Italy, Rizzoli, when I was 25. I moved to Rome for working and having an experience. The work in Rome moved from "pure writing" to screenwriting and teaching classes on creative writing.
In 2003 I've been asked by Fabrica to open and coordinate the Creative Writing Department.

My work for Fabrica started with the selection of young artists from all over the world. They had a one year's scholarship for finding the right way to express their world through words, pictures, short films, graphics. The result of these three years have been the Corporate Blog for Benetton, FAB, a magazine about creativity and many other editorial projects.

At the moment I'm writing a film for Amka & Film Export. My last work, "Principesse" has been performed in theater in Summer 2008.
