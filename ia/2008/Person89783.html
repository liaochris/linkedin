Jerrold Thompson’s Summary

Since earning his bachelor’s from the University of Illinois and breaking in as an intern at Motownin the mid-90’s, Jerrold Thompson has molded the careers of dozens of the music business’ most elite up-and-coming stars. As a manager of producers, songwriters, and artists, he's an expert at negotiating publishing and label deals, injecting success into projects for Destiny’s Child, Chris Brown, Busta Rhymes, Omarion, Jordin Sparks, and many more. In addition to being a stellar entrepreneur, he's also Head of Creative Affairs/A&R at Hidden Beach Recordings/Universal. After joining Hidden Beach in 2005, Jerrold has worked with many talents, including Jill Scott, Collipark,Dr. Cornel West, BeBe Winans, and others. Most recently, Jerrold became the first person to ever A&R an album by a US presidential candidate; the official Barack Obama Campaign CD 'Yes We Can: Voices Of A Grassroots Movement' features a galaxy of stars: Stevie Wonder, Sheryl Crow, John Mayer, Kanye West, John Legend, and more. During Jerrold’s tenure at Hidden Beach, the company has received at least one Grammy© win each year as well as multiple nominations.
His expertise in anticipating the shifting music business makes Jerrold one of the most sought-after mavens in urban music today. He has lent his perspective as a featured speaker at USC, Urban Network, BMI conferences, SXSW Festival, IMC, and the prestigious Anderson School of Management at UCLA. All of this and he’s still in his 30’s…

A skilled producer, mixer, and arranger, Jerrold is a trusted advisor to many young movers and shakers in music today. He offers insight on media-industry trends and how to take advantage of changes in music and the opportunities that are still emerging within (i.e. new media, touring, merchandising, 360-deals, artist-equity deals, gaming, mobile). With their careers entrusted to him, Jerrold’s clients can be sure his visionary insight and proven experience can deliver the best of many worlds.
