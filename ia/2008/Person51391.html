Diego Montenegro’s Summary

E-marketing consulting. Strategy management of digital media and interactive campaigns. Analysis and reporting of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). 360º strategic proposals in media, such as on-line, off-line, mobile, and new technology. Cost estimates and sales proposals.

E-volution Digital Marketing is one of the leading Digital Marketing agencies in Latinamerica. It has branches in Argentina (Buenos Aires), Mexico (DF), US (Miami) and Spain (Barcelona).

Some of our clients are: Microsoft Latam, Disney Latinoamérica, Procter & Gamble, Coca-Cola Company, Sony Latin America, Hewlett-Packard and MTV.
