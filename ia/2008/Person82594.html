Javier Albarracin’s Summary

Internet Entrepreneur, launched several succesful projects like the peruvian auctions portal Rematazo.com, the latin american blogs directory Blogalaxia.com, the videoblog Telurica.com and the newest launched polytical videoblog Utero.tv.

Management Consultant with substantial experience on CRM / CVM Related Projects in Latin America (Mainly in Brazil) Have developed and delivered several roadmaps for CRM related implementations.

Customer focused: Already worked for more than 3 TOP LatAm Banks analyzing their customer needs and designing offers for several business objectives: Adquisition, Development and Retention of Customers.

Application and Database Designer in a variety of business applications like: An automated marketing system (event-driven-marketing), Account Aggregation Portal, SMEs Portal, Balanced scorecard application, Credit card "save team" retention application, Telemarketing software, etc..

Also developed several segmentation schemes based on customer behaviour like Credit Card Customer Clusters, Propensity to Churn Model and a Collection Model.

Particularly interested on making Internet Technologies marriage with real Business Processes and Value creation objectives.
