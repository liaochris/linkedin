Tonny Madsen’s Summary

I am the founder and CEO of the RCP Company.

The RCP Company is an independent software development company that is specialized in development, training, consultancy and independent products based on the Eclipse™ platform, Eclipse Rich Client Platform (RCP), OSGi, and the corresponding technologies and tools.

We have a wide range of services that are all based on the Eclipse platform.

* development of specific plug-ins or integration of 3rd party tools
* the development and integration of complete products on RCP
* courses and training in the use of the Eclipse platform as well in how to extend the platform
* various consultancy

The RCP Company is located in Copenhagen, Denmark and we consider the Nordic countries (Denmark Sweden, Norway, Iceland and Finland) as well as the Baltic countries (Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania) our main market.

The daily management of The RCP Company is handled by me.

I am a log term user and developer of the Eclipse platform and has among other things reported more than 80 bugs for 5 different major releases (and still counting). I have 5 years of experience with the Eclipse platform (since version 2.0) and more than 20 years of experience with software development and system architecture. Of education I am a Computer Engineer (M.Sc) from the University of Copenhagen, Denmark.

Tonny Madsen’s Specialties:

Everything involving Eclipse, Eclipse RCP and OSGi. E.g. development, training, and consultancy.
