Charles Clifton’s Summary

Seeking a position that allows me to use my skills and knowledge at a smaller company or startup in the Boston/So. NH area. I'm also considering relocating to Seattle or Orlando and would be very interested in opportunities in those cities as well.

Currently employed as a Project Coordinator for Fresenius Medical Care where I am the main point of contact for the ordering and tracking of over 1700 printers for 1500 clinic locations. I assist clinics in ordering proper printers for their needs. I also review quarterly lease schedules supplied by the leasing vendor and make corrections before sending it on to management for approval and payment. Resolve customer and vendor issues, assist on ad hoc projects that come up such as the relocation of the corporate headquarters and occasionally assist with accounts payable for the monthly telephone bills.

Previously, I worked in Human Resources for Arkansas Blue Cross in their Providence, RI office for 2 years. I assisted with new hire orientations, processing time cards until the switch to Kronos, various company required trainings (some of which I videotaped and put onto DVD for new hires and absentees to view later). As a member of the Employees Association, I helped plan special events such as the annual holiday party, employee summer cookout and various employee appreciation days. Other duties included special projects for the Medicare Director and helping out the IT manager with resolving various hardware/software issues.

In my position as a Systems Security Specialist, I was responsible for helping to complete the annual Medicare Contractor survey, auditing responses to the survey, assisting other departments with special projects and supervised summer help.

I also have a very strong interest in music, video production, and online media and have completed an internship at TCI Cablevision (now Cox Cable) in Newport, RI where I managed the public access department during a company transition period.
