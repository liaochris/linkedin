Trevor Atkins’s Summary

Trevor Atkins is Principal Consultant with Silverpath Technologies. Most recently he was a Regional Director of Quality Services with UST Global and before that he was a founder and the Vice-President Operations of QA Labs.

After obtaining a degree in Applied Science for Electrical Engineering from UBC, for the last 12+ years Trevor has been involved in all aspects of hundreds of software projects, with a primary focus on quality engineering and testing activities. Additionally, Trevor has great experience with the varied responsibilities of operating a professional services firm in the software industry.

Trevor Atkins’s Specialties:

Trevor is dedicated to the design and improvement of quality processes for use across projects and organizations. He brings this dedication to all of the following areas of expertise:

* Customer Account Management
* Program/Project Management
* Software Quality Engineering & Test Management
* Quality Process Audits & Assessments
* Test Estimation
* Risk Identification & Management
* Training & Team Building
* Contract Negotiation
* Sales & Marketing
* Operations Management
