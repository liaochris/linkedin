About The Meehan Group:

The Meehan Group is nationally recognized as an executive recruiting and outplacement consulting firm located in Evansville, Indiana. Serving a wide variety of industries located across the USA, clients include pharmaceutical, plastics, automotive, power generation, distribution, steel, aluminum, refractories, consumer products, petroleum, mining, metal stamping, injection molding, extrusion, controls, labor relations, union, non-union, and other manufacturing.

Recruiting: The Meehan Group is a generalist firm that works with all levels of an organization including accounting, human resources, purchasing, engineering, supervision, sales, marketing, quality control, inventory control, materials management, and executive level positions.

Outplacement / Career Transition: The Meehan Group partners with client companies, getting to know their needs to reduce staff and provide services to help their displaced employees land new and better jobs. The Meehan Group thrives on building strong and trusting relationships with its clients allowing full access to all levels of the organization.

Public Speaking Engagements: Pat Meehan, Author of "Career of a Lifetime - Being Accountable for your Success," (Copyright 2008) will speak on career related topics with a focus on accountability to one's self in a career through self-awareness and self-improvement. Pat will speak at association meetings, college campuses, corporate meetings, as well as TV & Radio Talk Shows.
