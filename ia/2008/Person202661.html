Yury Buluy’s Summary

Internationally educated Software Engineering Processes Consultant with over 10 years experience of Software Engineering processes and ALM Professional Consulting, System Analysis and Business Operations Modeling and Software Development with leading Russian and international companies. Extensive expertise in:
- Software Engineering processes (Requirements Definition and Management, Configuration Management, Analysis and Design) ;
- Methodologies and Standards: RUP, Agile, Cockburn’s Effective Use Cases, OOAD and UML, IDEF;
- Tools: BORLAND (CaliberRM, DefineIT, Together, StarTeam), IBM Rational (Requisite Pro, Rational Rose/XDE);
