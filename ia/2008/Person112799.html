Leonardo Zangrando’s Summary

Maritime sector strategist with hands-on business development expertise, and a strong drive to release hidden business potential.

Currently developing a Resource Centre for Infrastructure Funds at www.infrafunds.net including IFDB - the Infrafunds Database - a repository of business resources about the infrafunds' target industries, and targeted daily news .

Since 2001 founder and Managing Partner at Longitude [enterprise evolution], a business strategy consultancy with specific expertise in strategic planning and business development in luxury goods, apparel, wines & spirits, motorcycles, yachts and shipyards, pharma & biotech, container transportation.

Since 2007 serving also as Industry Expert at De Matteo Monness, LLC, Coleman Research Group, and Gerson Lehrman Group, advising funds and institutional investors on container transportation industry.

Prior positions have included roles in business consulting, project management, business development, sales and marketing strategy, salesforce effectiveness, licensing, engineering design Locations in London (UK), Barcelona (Spain), Trieste, Ravenna and Genova (Italy) and projects developed in France, Portugal, Holland, Scandinavia, USA and NZ.

Member of IESE Alumni association, ADS the Italian blood donors association, LinkedIn, and e-cademy.

Attended the MBA program at IESE Business School in Barcelona and earned a degree in 1999. Also studied Naval Architecture and Mechanical Engineering at Trieste University and earned a Masters degree in 1991.

In my personal time, I enjoy blue-sea sailing, mountain biking, trekking, learning foreign languages and understanding world cultures.

You can visit my personal profile on leonardo.zangrando.com or leonardozangrando.blogspot.com
