Sean Campbell’s Summary

I began my career plugging together networks and writing code on Microsoft platforms as well as delivering training and authoring books and articles on best practices in the technology industry.

Today I use the hands-on knowledge of technical details I gained and a deep and broad understanding of the tech field, from hardware to software, to deliver deep-dive competitive analysis and customer evidence to Cascade Insights clients. At Cascade Insights we advise established industry leaders such as Intel and Microsoft, as well as start-up or niche players.

While other consulting groups define 'growth' in terms of adding employees and increasing billable hours, I've always measured growth by the complexity of the challenges that we take on for our clients. Since the inception of Cascade Insights we've focused on keeping the company small so that it is easy to personally service client needs.
