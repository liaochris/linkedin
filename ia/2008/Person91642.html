Jim Reece’s Summary

We create resources for the profession that engages people in business and personal development through our PEOPLE Model products and support materials which include workshops, diagnostics, on line tutorials, development bulletins, independent coaching support and facilitated group processes. We sell the Model resources to others and coach the coach, train the trainer in their use (and support our own clientele as well).

Personally my competencies favor organizational development both internal to people's power to produce and external to market opportunities; strong consultative and collaborative approaches to problem solving through facilitated group processes.

Among the more fun engagements I have:
Directed post construction operational start up for a family oriented sports and fitness facility growing to $2.3M and 70 employees with exceptional market reputation in 3 years.

Co created the first Keystone Activity Zone (KAZ) in eastern Pennsylvania whose mission was educating school aged students in the importance of activity through programs focused on outdoor family oriented activities available throughout the county.

Designed and implemented resolution strategies for international children’s sports and fitness facility owners in Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, England, Kuwait, Japan and the United States related to management and operational barriers.

Created customer service marketing function within newly organized call center.

Facilitated the integration of two distinct cultures which successfully merged old world customer service with new world sales and marketing.

Opened several new outlets including staffing, marketing, and merchandising for a small retail division (33 stores) of a large manufacturing firm.

Identified and implemented cost savings in telecommunications, insurance, pre employment processes, and organizational restructuring for a privately acquired small ($1M) transportation company.
