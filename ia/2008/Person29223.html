Ken Durham’s Summary

O V E R V I E W
Provides technical review, project management and on-site supervision during all phases of drilling and completion activities, including tubular and downhole tool manufacturing & inspection, drilling & completion operations, stimulation, testing & production.

Authored technical articles in SPE Journal, World Oil, and Petroleum Engineer for Lindsey Completion Systems from 1979 to 1987 including the seminal “Tubing Movement, Forces, and Stresses in Dual-Flow Assembly Installations (DOI 10.2118/9265-PA).” Authored two books about staff-to-management transitions.

Previously:
* Ten+ years in IT support with DEC, Compaq and Microsoft, both direct and staff roles: working directly with end-customers and service partners, leading a 250+ h/c service delivery organization as well as working as an individual contributor
* Founding partner of a consulting firm specializing in business-process redesign and quality control systems primarily in heavy manufacturing and Oil & Gas sectors
* Ten+ years with Masco, a Fortune 500 industrial conglomerate that built components, assemblies and products for the energy, automotive and construction industries. Joined the company in a technical capacity at one of their newly acquired energy divisions and advanced through the sales side of the house into operating & corporate oversight roles.
* Five years out of college with Exxon USA in drilling & production assignments
