Michael Ippoliti’s Summary

I am a Financial Advisor and Certified Financial Planner™ professional specializing in life-based financial planning, with over 20 years of experience in financial analysis and consultation. Valley National Financial Advisors, established in 1985, is an independent full range provider of financial management services with our own full service brokerage firm.

My Mission:
To provide clients with personalized financial strategies and innovative solutions for increasing and/or preserving wealth, and to develop long term relationships based on trust and confidence.

My Financial Planning Process is comprehensive. It starts by defining:
o WHERE YOU ARE NOW
o WHERE YOU WANT TO BE
o Then constructing a plan…HOW TO GET THERE

My clients --- individuals, small business owners and non-profits --- all look to me for personalized financial advice and support, and consider me their trusted advisor.

Services and Solutions:
Holistic or life-based approach to financial planning to help meet your goals, answer your financial questions, coordinate your financial planning -- assist in your tax, education, retirement, and estate planning, handle your investments and insurance, prepare your income tax returns, and provide comprehensive wealth management. Customized solutions employing asset allocation.
