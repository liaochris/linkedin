Manny Nunez’s Summary

The growth process requires a longer look at the future of our company. Combine that with the changes in medical aviation and civil aviation, we have a great opportunity to continue to develop our services.

The highlights for 2008 are:
1. Open a new trining program for At Sea and open water rescue program in association with Loyola Marymount University.
2. Offer a one to three month Aviation English course and exam to meet the ICAO requirements for pilots to reach english Level 4 to get or keep their pilot licnese
3. Assist Port Captains in the Mexican Riviera in the development and delivery of Aeromaritime Trainig for sea and air rescuers in open waters.
4. Develop a certification process for Port Physiscians like those offered to airport pyisicians.
5. Open a channel of investment for MD's and Neonatologists for the development and delivery of neonatal and ECMO aeromnedical modalities.
6. Offer pirvate, police and military Special forces/SWAT temas and SAR personnel the correct equipment for safety and afficacy in carrying their needes items.
7. Offer a Lear 35 or 35 A owner, the oportunity to enter a new market in medical aviation.

Our experience in the training, management and delivery of aeromedical and air ambulance service is unsurpassed. We are the first and so far, the only US based aeromedical training program for Latin america and the Caribbean.
