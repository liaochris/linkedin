Joseph Coates’s Summary

Academic -

I have taught all levels of graphic design and typography studio and seminar courses from beginning to advanced and graduate level. Served on graduate thesis committees. Taught large design history survey course (quarter system). Served on various university committees. Spoken at various national and international conferences on design.

Non Academic -

Freelance, corporate, in house print and digital designer and art director. Original digital media content and game development.

Joseph Coates’s Specialties:

Typography, identity design, photography, late 20th C. and contemporary international design research, Dutch design, Greek design, Polish design.
