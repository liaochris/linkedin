Stacy Geagan Wagner’s Summary

Stacy’s career started 15 years ago at Ketchum Public Relations in Atlanta. Since 2000, she has focused exclusively on consumer marketing communications for entrepreneurial ventures. As the Director of Corporate Communications, Stacy spearheaded the launch of two low-fare airlines, Virgin America and Delta Air Lines’ Song. More recently, Stacy was responsible for the successful public relations strategy that achieved Limbo two million members and recognition on The Today Show, CNBC, NBC and Fox as well as in the pages of the USA Today, Wall Street Journal, Forbes and Business 2.0. A graduate of Emory University’s Executive MBA and Oglethorpe University, Stacy was named one of the “Top 10 to Watch” from PR News magazine.
