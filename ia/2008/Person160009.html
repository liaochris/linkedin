Richard Lamb’s Summary

My experience comprises an unrivalled network of candidates across a broad spectrum of technology disciplines throughout the financial services sector in London and the Asia Pacific region.

I am extremely keen to engage with professionals with the technical ability to work with the most challenging product areas and business lines within financial services.

Excellent knowledge of the market, and the ability to provide a consultative service to end-users. I continue to create competitive advantage for clients & candidates, that leverages long standing relationships, provision of career advice, and review of recruitment processes.

Specialist in Development & Front Office recruitment requiring specific exposure and technical experience including: FIX, Murex, Fidessa, Calypso, Summit, Charles River, Longview, GL Trade, Ion, Algorithmics, Barra, Riskmetrics, SunGard, Kondor+, Datastream, Reuters Xtra 3000, Mint, OST Microgen / Business Rules, Globus, Gloss, Sophis, Anvil (ARTS), DMA, Program/Automated Systems, and Global One.

C++, C# and Java Front Office Development with product knowledge in Equities, Derivatives, Fixed Income, and Commodities. Solid understanding of Program / Automated trading and the candidates that excel in this area - whether you are looking at / for a role focused on low-latency execution infrastructure and GRID technology, or familiarity in complex financial products, client connectivity, and in-depth understanding of business functionality.

I have a great network of candidates in both the Asia Pacific region and the UK, and continue to build this on a daily basis. No-nonsense approach. Skilled commercial negotiator. Contract/Consultancy & Permanent experience. Knowledge and experience to manage/team lead.

Great network of the most senior candidates in Asia-Pacific - specifically those currently based in Hong Kong. Able to introduce firms to candidates with a high level of management expertise (Senior VP to "C" Level hires).

Richard Lamb’s Specialties:

Emphasis on candidate development using market knowledge to advise continual addition of skill to add value to prospective employers. Clients can expect me to understand requirements, decipher jargon, and access passive candidates.

Unafraid of the challenges the recruitment of candidates for demanding financial services firms presents. Delivery focus - I am happy to redirect business where I cannot help, but equally am always happy to look at related requirements and profiles.
