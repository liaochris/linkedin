Andy Renk’s Summary

The Internet has changed a lot in the past couple years. I have found that most people don’t know about, let alone understand, how to leverage these new tools and concepts for their businesses. Whether it is developing a cohesive online brand strategy, building a quality cost-effective website system, using social media and video or driving traffic and leads, building marketing systems that use these new technologies is what, in my opinion, is really lacking.

What we offer:
► On-Demand Internet Business Training: We provide an Internet-based, on-demand training platform to deliver courses covering a wide-range of subjects related to maximizing Internet business ROI and Web 2.0 systems. If you are new to doing business online, have a traditional brick and mortar business or an entrepreneur wanting to learn the latest online marketing tools this in-depth training is key success factor.

► Online Product Launch Consulting and Implementation: From design to customer-relationship management, we develop turn-key Internet business systems that will help you and your organization succeed online.

► Internet Business Coaching & Consulting: Customized one-on-one or small group training on how to use Web 2.0 strategies and actionable tactics to improve customer relationships, build your brand and convert more prospects to clients.

What I’m looking for on LinkedIn:
► I’m looking for content providers looking to expand their reach and build credibility. If you have a message that will help other business owners improve their business with online tools, we want to talk to you.

► I’m looking for channel partners to distribute my digital training products – I can add value to their product offering and expand their reach with their clients.

► I’m looking to consult with business executives, entrepreneurs and small business owners who are either looking to get started doing business online or have an existing Internet presence that needs some attention.
