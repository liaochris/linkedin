Simon Atkins’s Summary

*** FIRM SUMMARY ***

CEO, Advanced Forecasting Corporation (AFC).

B.Sc. Atmospheric Sciences, Cornell University
iMBA (International), Helsinki School of Economics
Ph.D. Candidate in biometeoelectromagnetics at CCNH.

Advanced Forecasting Corporation [AFC] specializes in Meteorological Intelligence, Planetary Risk Management, and VARM (Vulnerability Assessment and Risk Modeling) in connection with business continuity and contingency planning. AFC provides advanced quantitiative details in weather risk prognostications, threat-based impact prediction, and any long-range forecast of both nature-based and human-caused disasters from short-range time periods of an hour (such as electricity demand based on atmospheric parameterizations) to long-term periods of 10 years ahead (such as for investment and planning purposes). AFC serves any industry that is negatively affected or impacted by climate change, weather volatility and planetary disasters. AFC provides a wealth of strategy & risk management services and products, all customized to meet the exact needs of the client at the time. AFC clients receive an edge in managing risk and optimize their ROI and minimize asset loss from damage-related events in their investment portfolios. AFC conveys industry-specific strategic expertise in global risk scenarios.

*** PERSONAL EXPERTISE ***

Simon has extensive knowledge in product and service applications in planetary risk and climate change asset risk management and vulnerability analysis for many different industries all around the world. Simon is a professional speaker, book author, and visiting strategic consultant with exceptional interpersonal & tri-lingual communication skills. Simon's experience provides a strength in his ability to conceive, translate, market, debate & teach corporate & strategic management ideas in risk management, and then promote & adapt them into practical, business solutions.

Simon Atkins’s Specialties:

Simon is an analytical, energetic & entrepreneurial spirit as well as an ‘international citizen’ with vision, creativity, drive and innovation, with a strong belief in a digital & online teaching environment with a commitment to enhancing participatory presentation skills, ‘real-world’ interactive thinking and empirical research.
