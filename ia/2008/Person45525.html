Dave Herrington’s Summary

Dave Herrington is Co-Founder, Chairman and CEO of DARC Corporation, Oracle's longest-standing Certified Partner 100% dedicated to implementing the Oracle E-Business Suite of ERP, CRM and Business Intelligence applications. Since its formation in 1992, DARC has remained unequaled in the industry for successful Oracle application implementations and dedication to the Oracle user community.

One of the original 12 Oracle Applications consultants at Oracle Corporation, Mr. Herrington has over 20 years of experience with the Oracle Applications and has extensive knowledge and experience with Oracle Corporation's products, strategies, policies and operations. Mr. Herrington is an active and recognized leader in Oracle's partner community and is a charter member of Oracle's Industry Strategy Council that guides Oracle's development of Fusion Applications for the Professional Services industry.

Mr. Herrington is also Co-Founder, Board Member and Event Director of the North Central Oracle Applications Users Group (NCOAUG), the largest regional Oracle Applications users group, encompassing 3500 Oracle users across 7 states (IL, WI, IA, MN, IN, OH and MI) and a 5-time recipient of the OAUG GEO/SIG Certificate of Distinction Outstanding Achievements Award.

Mr. Herrington is also Co-Founder, Board Member and Secretary of the Oracle Business Intelligence Special Interest Group (OBI SIG), which focuses on the Oracle Business Intelligence Enterprise Edition (OBIEE) Plus platform and Oracle BI Applications. The OBI SIG also recently achieved the OAUG GEO/SIG Certificate of Distinction Award after only a year of operations.

Mr. Herrington has been an active member of Vistage International (formerly TEC), the world's leading chief executive organization since 2001.

Mr. Herrington graduated from the University of Illinois School of Electrical Engineering in 1988 and holds a Graduate Certificate in Business Administration from the University of Illinois at Chicago.
