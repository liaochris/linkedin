Misty Lackie’s Summary

As chief executive officer for Go Smart Solutions, LLC, Misty Lackie oversees programming, Web development, business operations and new developments for the company and its affiliated Web sites.

Lackie started her professional programming career working as a systems management supervisor for Alloy Inc./CCS, an action sports-focused apparel and gear company, charged with database management, as well as networking and programming. She’s also worked in networking, database and systems management with the Denmat Corporation, a dental products company located in Santa Maria, Calif.

In 2002, she combined her experience in database design and software development with her entrepreneurial spirit to form her own company, Go Smart Solutions, LLC, to help clients develop online applications. The company has since expanded to include online services, EmailFlyerAds.com and ClassifiedFlyerAds.com.

Misty Lackie’s Specialties:

Web development, project management, PHP, ASP, ASP.NET, CSS, XHTML, JavaScript, AJAX, XML, MySQL, MS SQL.
