Dave Hatter’s Summary

A seasoned, enthusiastic, results-oriented problem solver with over 16 years experience as a programmer, instructor, speaker and writer. I'm proud of a long track record of succcess helping clients wisely apply technology to solve business challenges, generate ROI and improve their bottom line. Career highlights include:
