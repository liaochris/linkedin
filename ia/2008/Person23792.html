Birgitta Hosea’s Summary

Artist and animator, Birgitta Hosea is a freelance senior presentation artist for Adobe and organises Adobe Inspired Media, a post-production user group at the Curzon Cinema Soho. She is the Course Director of Pg Dip Character Animation at Central Saint Martins and the author of "The Focal Easy Guide to Flash" series.

Originally working as an Art Director / Assistant Designer on films, TV series, pop promos, fringe and west-end theatre, she did an MA in Computer Imaging and Animation in the mid-90s and went on to work as a freelance web designer and motion graphic animator.
