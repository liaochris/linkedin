Dion Van De Coevering’s Experience

  • ANWB

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    May 2007 — Present (1 year 9 months)

    The focus has changed from outsourcing to "do it yourself". This is a challenging task for the IT department because of running the daily operation on aging infrastructure. This was due to lack of investment since we where about to outsource servers and storage.

    When that decision was revoked and even before;
    -I've expanded an c2d8 EVA 8000 to an c2d12 and an c2d3 EVA4000 to an c2d4 to cope with growth.
    -Upgraded the brocade switches from a mixture of firmware levels to v5.3.1a and v6.1.0a (but uniform within a fabric) depending on the models used.
    -Replaced old 2800 switches with new brocade 5000 models and later expanded the SAN with four 4900 models.
    -In a later stage transformed the, still, cascaded fabric to a custom configuration which can be described as a ring fabric with a core.
    In sync with the expanding SAN an DMX4-950 was purchased and connected, for the purpose of consolidating two DMX800 (on there on SAN islands) to the new DMX4, this is still ongoing.
