Jean Patrick Ascenci’s Summary

I started as an Analyst in the Enterprise Application Integration area (EAI).
I was in charge of the Data Flows and Architecture Modeling utilizing MEGA Software. In the same time, I managed the implementation of a Business Activity Monitoring (BAM) solution, which allowed monitoring the Data Flows/Messages through dashboards across
the whole business area.
Later I was promoted and assumed the position as a BPM Consultant. My main responsibilities were the Proof of Concept platforms management and BPM solution testings.
After this technical experience, I decided to move to scopes that were more business oriented.

Hence, I joined Casewise in March 2005 and started with the AIB in Dublin on their Basel II and SOX compliance Implementation project for 4 months. I was in charge of the Business Processes Modelling driven by interviews and responsible of the publication/ reporting management. During the 2005 Summer, I participated to the creation of Casewise Solutions focused on the Compliance (ITIL, ISO, Basel II, SOX, …)

Since September 2005, I have been mostly worked in France and Benelux, where I provide training, enabling consultancy and project consultancy whether on short term or long term assignments.
The Business Process Analysis and Business Process Management remain useful even mandatory in different typologies of projects; here is a sample of those, I have been involved in: ERP Adoptions Projects, IT Cartography Projects, SOA Implementation Projects, Business Process Improvement Projects; Risk and Control Projects
