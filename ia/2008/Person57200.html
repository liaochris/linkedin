Erik Ideler’s Summary

During his whole career Erik has occupied different management functions within the IT organisation of medium and large companies.

He not only gathered experience in the setup and the management of “Systems and Operations” within an IBM mainframe and DEC server environment. He also was responsible for the transformation of an IT department into an independent “shared services”-organisation, where as a General Manager he was accountable for the P&L.

Due to his experience with transforming organisations in order to increase their efficiency today Erik is the Program Manager responsible for the Service Delivery Optimisation Program at Belgacom SDE/Telindus Service Delivery.

During all Erik’s assignments Program and Project Management received special attention.

IT Governance being an area he is especially interested in he developed IT Governance models covering the strategic, tactical and operational level and assuring business guidance and leadership.

Erik also gained a large experience in “Activity Based Costing” by setting up and rolling out an “Activity Based Costing”-model at Toyota Motor Europe as well as at Telindus Remote Management Services.

Erik Ideler’s Specialties:

Development of vision, mission and objectives for an organisation
Development and implementation of governance within the overall organisation
Development and implementation of an organisation able to coop with the fixed vision, mission and objectives
