Mark Banini’s Summary

I have spent most of my career consulting across a variety of industries but mainly in financial services. I have operated at board level for many years, and have a proven track record with over fifteen years experience in successfully delivering change and enhancing performance.

I have delivered large-scale complex strategies and change programmes and have extensive experience of working at both Director and operational levels in identifying, planning and delivering both strategic and tactical solutions to client’s problems.

I have also been fortunate enough to travel the world and experience a myriad of different cultures and behaviours. The key thing that my experience has taught me is that it's all about people. I have led major change programmes across numerous industries, successfully managing stakeholders, their issues, behaviour change and culture change.

I am a skilled communicator who is motivated by the challenge of getting the best out of people so that they in turn can perform to the best of their abilities.
