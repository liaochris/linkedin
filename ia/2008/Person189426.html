Tim Stanley’s Summary

Highly successful, results-driven Information Security Executive with experience in successfully meeting business requirements in large scale and highly regulated organizations. Broad background in Management and Information Technology that includes 18 years of progressive experience in information security. Over 25 years of experience leading collaborative teams, overseeing successful technology programs, and administering complex budgets.

Responsible for development and execution of enterprise-wide IT security strategy and driving implementation of security related programs into each business unit. Also responsible for development and implementation of corporate IT security and control policies and standards, as well as ensuring that appropriate tools and metrics are in place to allow for effective monitoring, measurement, and control of risk as it relates to IT security.
