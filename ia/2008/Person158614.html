Steve Bernstein’s Summary

President of Zenbu Media and Publisher of Relix, Global Rhythm, Metal Edge and Metal Maniacs magazines.

We are a music multi-media company focusing on niche music genres and cater to the passionate music fan. We are a fully integrated media company in print, digital and events. We produce the live music events such as as the Green Apple Music Festival.

We also own www.jambands.com an online magazine focusing on the fast growing Jamband scene, have an Artist management division and record label.

Steve Bernstein’s Specialties:

We focus on the core passionate music fan and we reach the concert going public through print, digital media and events.
