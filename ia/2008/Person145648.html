Pak-Kei (Leo) Mak’s Summary

While versatile and proficient in designing in a variety of mediums, the best skill I can offer are on design projects that bridges the 2D (print, identity), 3D (product, interior, architecture) and interactive (web, UI) aspects together. My background in architecture enables me to translate between these different mediums at ease.

I like solving design-related problems and I am eager to learning new skills. I am looking forward to more collaborative work in order to enhance my management and interpersonal skills.

My ideal is to be able to work in a multi-disciplinary design firm, where I can integrate my experiences together into one rich, cohesive and meaningful design.
