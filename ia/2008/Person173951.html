Shane Kemp’s Summary

I have worked on many projects since living in California. I modeled environments for a feature length movie for Ocean Park Productions called Heidi 4 Paws. I have also worked on 3d Model cleanup for Mindcutter Studios. I have also worked on the special effects and prop animation for the game Smackdown vs. Raw 2007. I just completed Smackdown vs. Raw 2008 as a Lead Animator and Lead Technical Artist.

My main focus of work is animation. I also enjoy modeling as well. I have used both Maya and 3d Studio Max to animate and model. I also have used Shake to composite and rotoscope. I have knowledge in both texturing and 2d art, using
Photoshop.

To Sum it all up, I’d say that I’m a people person, always ready to meet new people and become part of the team. I have strong communication skills and I am always ready to work. I am looking forward to finding a company that I can grow with.
