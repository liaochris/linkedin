Frank Kenny’s Summary

My goals include making dramatic contributions to the success of business owners, executives, and my community. I am entrepreneurial and have 20 years of small business experience. I have started several companies and thrive on the challenges. I also understand the frustrations.

One of the frustrations is that most people don't realize that about 90% of new jobs are created at the small business level. I am concerned that many segments of our society do not fully comprehend how their decisions may adversely impact the small business component of our economy. And that adversely effects us all.

I see a large part of my job as defending the small/medium business owner/executive from burdensome over-regulation, job killing taxation, and short sighted planning. Another part of my job is to encourage entrepreneurs, promote their efforts so they will be successful, and provide training to businesses owners.

I am accomplishing my goals by leading and growing our Chamber of Commerce and working with various organizations associated with business. Through the concerted efforts of the Trustees and staff, our membership is up 48% so far this year.

In this position, I am supporting local businesses while building up our community. Long term, my goal is to lead our Chamber into being a much more powerful organization, benefiting the membership and the community.
