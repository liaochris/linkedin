Tom Bomar’s Summary

I am currently Managing Director of FIT Launch, Inc. As a company we provide a host of services for health and fitness professionals including business development, consulting, continuing education and employment services. Extending these services and assisting these professionals in bringing their skill sets to new markets has led us into the hospitality industry.

Hospitality services have become a major portion of our business over the past several years. Our work with group fitness instructors, personal trainers, tennis professionals, yoga instructors, etc. has provided us a pre screened group of experienced professionals, which have been utilized to enhance guest services in an extremely cost effective manor for the host facility. Interested in utilizing these services or would like to work with us, please feel free to contact me... fitpro@fitlaunch.com

Personally, I hold a Strength & Conditioning Specialist certification with education and experience in business development, human resource management, successful entrepreneur within the long term care industry as well as the fitness industry.

Tom Bomar’s Specialties:

As a company we are Regional Master Trainers for the Resist-A-Ball organization and Faculty providers for the American Council on Exercise. Our HG Educators provide wellness information to their local US consumers while our Fitness Educators continue to provide a host of continuing education programs for fitness professionals.

We specialize in continuing education, all-inclusive working vacations, business development & consulting, and retreat programming for the health & fitness industry.
