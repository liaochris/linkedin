Dakota Reese Brown’s Summary

Theorist, Strategist (User Experience Designer / Information Architect), Rapid Prototyper, and Appropriative Game Designer.

Industry-wise I am primarily a strategist who happens to be able to hold his own at design and development. I've been trained heavily in cultural theory and media engineering at two of the top educational institutions in the United States. This has allowed me to deliver, what can only be described as unique, media experiences to several top international brands.

Academia-wise, I am a published theorist in the fields of game studies, game design, and mobile design. My area of expertise is appropriative game design which seeks to create videogame-like experiences in the real world via pervasive or ubiquitous computational architectures.
