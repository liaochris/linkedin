Eric Peltier, a.k.a. the doormouse, is an entrepreneur and marketing innovator with a proven track record, and an extensive scholastic background, in the creation and execution of marketing initiatives in both new and traditional media in order to effectively reach target audiences. He is considered a respected and award winning music industry professional with more than ten years background in marketing; experience in promotion, publicity, project management, entertainment programming, analysis and communication with an extensive online and traditional portfolio.

My focus is to secure a position utilizing my experience in marketing, intellectual property law and communication, derived from the entertainment and telecommunication industries, in order to contribute to successful development and growth of the company.

I am looking for a position in Northeastern NJ or Manhattan. I prefer a permanent or temp-to-perm. role at the manager or associate director level, mid-career. Industries I am interested in working with include entertainment (music, film, tv, publishing, broadcasting, online media, sports, etc.), mass comm. (telecom, mobile assets, cable, satellite, etc.), or some consumer electronics (as would be related to entertainment, for example, telephony or personal playback) in roles that would involve interactive or new media as well as more traditional positions in mar-com, grass-roots marketing, strategy and analysis, etc. if they include digital or mobile responsibilities as well. I am fairly open to different opportunities and flexible on salary and start times.
