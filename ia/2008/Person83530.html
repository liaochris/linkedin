Jamie Gibson’s Summary

Jamie Gibson is an award-winning designer who is quickly becoming one of the most well respected creators of print materials, web designs and photography.

Whether your company needs assistance in the creation of a logo, print materials, website or brand photography, Jamie is dedicated towards launching your company to the highest level of brand marketing possible. She is exceptionally known for assisting a variety of unique organizations and companies worldwide all within the parameters of their artistic vision, company goals and, most important, budget.

Jamie comprehends today’s industry standards while incorporating her intelligence and sense of style to work with your unparalleled company on building its brand from the foundation up.

Jamie Gibson’s Specialties:

Expert level in standard industry design programs including Adobe Creative Suite, Flash, Actionscript 2.0, Dreamweaver, HTML, CSS, and Contribute.

Comfortable with advising clients on major marketing decisions, project management and business development.

Capable of devising effective advertising campaigns for maximum profitability.

Expert in the process of digital pre-press using PMS color separations, CMYK/4 color process, examining blue lines and attending press checks.
