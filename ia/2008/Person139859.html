Jim Clay’s Summary

I have been with New Era Tickets for 4+ years. Office is located in Chesterbrook, PA.

New Era Tickets provides customized ticketing, database marketing, and access management functionality to sports and entertainment organizations, enabling them to sell and manage their own ticket sales. In addition to helping organizations create their own ticketing brand in their individual market, New Era Tickets establishes retail outlets and also manages a large capacity call center. Owned by Comcast-Spectacor, the Philadelphia based entertainment management firm and powered by the Paciolan technology, an industry leader for over 25 years, New Era Tickets is bringing control of the box office back to the box office.
