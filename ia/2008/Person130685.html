Michael Wolfson’s Summary

Part-time in-house counsel to technology clients. I generally work with clients on an as-needed hourly basis on a wide range of licensing, intellectual property and general business and corporate matters. My clients are companies that either do not require a full-time counsel or that need additional in-house resources but don't want to bring in another full-time employee. Clients benefit from the advantages of high level in-house legal services (prompt, sophisticated and practical responses from someone who understands the legal and business needs of a growing business) without committing to an in-house position and at rates significantly lower than those charged by outside law firms.

My primary focus is on drafting and negotiating both in-bound and out-bound licensing, development, and sales, distribution and related channel partner agreements, including VAR and OEM agreements. I also have experience with a wide variety of the business and legal issues that clients confront, including working with revenue recognition issues; advising on intellectual property matters; negotiating real estate and equipment leases; advising on employment matters; assisting in developing sales and marketing strategies and coordinating corporate governance functions including director and stockholder meetings.
