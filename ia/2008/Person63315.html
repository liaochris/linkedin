Felipe García’s Summary

Born 1963. Felipe García. Create and heads Homonovus, his own business incubator, with two main research projects: KNOWDLE.COM (PhD), AUDITA.COM (ROSETTASEC), EVOLUTIO, INNOVR´US, KINCUBATOR (The Knowledge Internet Incubator) & FUNDESCOMMERCE.

December 2006 create and start-up ROSETTASEC to deploy AUDITA.COM/SENTINEL4i services and application to provide Professional Security Services.

KNOWDLE is a R&D project about new Cognitive Operating Systems with a Distributed Search Engine Embeded in collaboration with INNAXIS Research Institute.

Since 2000 create and heads intuitio group and has more than 22 years of professional experience in IT. Joined to Programa IRIS, in 1989, as RedIRIS got in touch with internet with a pioneer spanish technical group managed by Jose Barbera, the true spanish internet father´s. Internet changed his life forever.

At the beginning of the spanish commercial internet jumped to private business and lanched several spanish pioneer projects and their own companies.

New business development. Special focused in the goal to increase benefits of enterprises using internet technical and business architectures and process reingeniering.
