Tony Brunelle’s Summary

Professionally trained as a chiropractor at Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College in Toronto, 1975 - 1979. After 10 years in a traditional chiropractic practice I discovered the then new science of psychoneuroimmunoendocrinology and yes, this is a real word.

This is the science of how our conditioning and programming has affected us in our lives, how it affects our present and will affect our future. This completely turned my world upside down.
For the last 19 years I have specialized in the study of this new science. It is the science of how what happened to you in your past, be it as a child, infant, in the womb or earlier have resulted in how you think, feel, act. This has resulted in the life you now live.

During my studies of various techniques and methodologies I developed a strong intuitive ability to sense my clients deep core issues. As a result, I now practice as a consultant and trainer in helping clients find their major issues that have profoundly affected their lives and help them release these limiting conditionings. With the right guidance, it is possible to release the hold of these past negative events with the result that you see the world in a new light. Your creativity increases, self-confidence grows, your attitude changes and your prospects for a better tomorrow take over. These are permanent changes and not just a temporary adrenalin rush from the excitement of the moment!

You literally re-program your own internal computer.
I now as a coach and trainer help people to let go of their limiting conditioning and maximize their potential in workshops and in one-on-one consultations.
