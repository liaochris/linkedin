  • Founder & Site Manager

    Tech East

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 2003 — Present (5 years 7 months)

    Tech East was founded in 2003 with the specific mission of providing users the ability to quickly and easily find information technology, aerospace & defence, oil & gas and biotech news in eastern Canada.

    With many news sources continually releasing stories twenty-four hours a day, finding relevant news can often be a time consuming task. For complete coverage, users are forced to visit many different sites and sift through a variety of stories that may or may not be relevant to them. Tech East alleviates this problem by creating topically driven, specific news web pages and populating each of those pages with only news about that particular topic.

    So, whether you are interested in finding all the news about your community, or your company or industry, Tech East provides an intuitive, easy way to find the targeted technology news that is relevant to you.

    Visit Tech East at www.techeast.ca
