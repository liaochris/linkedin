Mark Kirkbride’s Summary

The fast moving pace of technological innovation means change is a guaranteed part of any progressive business; most critical is the way change is managed and its overall effects on the business. Some of the most significant issues that executives face include business transformation, business process change and changes in organisational and operational culture.

With experience of M&A, Integration, Consolidation, Turnarounds and other change led programmes; we understand challenges linked to decreased technology spending, new leadership strategies and industry convergence.

Interim Partners, Technology & Telecommunications practice supports business transformation and technical innovation through the provision of highly skilled and experienced Interim Managers and Executives.

With established relationships with many of the worlds leading Technology & Telecommunications providers, Wireless operators, Systems Integrators and Consultancies, we are exceptionally well positioned to provide expert advice on the resources required to execute your strategic initiatives. Our aim is to add value with a high return on investment.

The Technology & Telecommunications Practice at Interim Partners has significant experience in resourcing change led assignments, and provides senior and executive level interim managers across a number of disciplines; including board level appointments.

If you are an Interim Manager looking for a new challenge or an organisation seeking additional expertise please contact Mark Kirkbride on 01423 53102.
