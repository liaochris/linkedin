Colin Jackson’s Summary

I aim to help the Internet realise its full potential and to help people and businesses make the best use of it to realise theirs.

I am good at translating technical concepts into business language, and am a media commentator on technology matters.

Colin Jackson’s Specialties:

The use of the Internet and of domain names to achieve business results.

  • President

    InternetNZ

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)
