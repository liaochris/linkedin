Andrew Shaindlin’s Summary

An alumni relations professional since 1989, I have worked at Brown University, the University of Michigan, and now at Caltech. Past roles have included directing programs in alumni education, alumni career services, travel/study, and other lifelong-learning related programs. I have spoken and written extensively within the profession and have chaired or co-chaired a number of professional conferences on topics ranging from alumni education to technology in institutional advancement.

I was Vice Chair of the Commission on Alumni Relations (Council for Advancement and Support of Education), serving on the Commission 2004-2007. I also chaired the EDUCAUSE Task Force on Identity Management. I'm a member of the Advisory Board for Higher Ed Experts, and the CASE Web Advisory Board. I consult independently as well, on a very limited basis.

My blog, Alumni Futures (http://www.alumnifutures.com/), provides an outlet for new directions and ideas for higher ed professionals.

I am currently enrolled part-time in the Master's Degree program at Claremont Graduate University's School of Educational Studies (Focus: Higher Education administration).

Andrew Shaindlin’s Specialties:

Online services for alumni, business networking practices, alumni membership programs, social & business networking software for alumni, educational travel programs, alumni communications.
