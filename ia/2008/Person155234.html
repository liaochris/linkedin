Rafael Brown’s Summary

11 years as a game designer in the videogame industry.
Current: Need for Speed '09 (fall 2008) at EA Black Box
Still In Progress (now w/o me): an unannounced Bioware Action/RPG title
Shipped: Mass Effect, Tony Hawk's Project 8, True Crime: New York City, The Hobbit, Thief 2, Thief Gold, & Dark Vengeance
Consigned to Limbo: design on True Crime 3 & 4, Gun 2 & Nintendo's Thunder Rally

Unreal 2 & Unreal 3
3DS Max, Maya, AI Implant
proprietary company level/track/game/world editors

I love making games, and making worlds. It's not just about fun, it's about creating unique, memorable experiences.

I've been involved in design for a range of videogame industry companies. I've worked in independent 3rd party development, publisher-owned 2nd party development, and hardware manufacturer-owned 1st party development across large and small studios. I've amassed development and design experience in 1st person, 3rd person, and vehicle-based games across action, combat, adventure, stealth, platforming, RPG, racing, combat, open world, living city, and competitive multi-player gameplay types. New designs mean new challenges and I strive for a balance of product uniqueness & innovation with audience familiarity & accessibility in whatever I do.

Rafael Brown’s Specialties:
