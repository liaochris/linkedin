JC (João) Duarte’s Summary

My professional experience is vast, spanning across 3 continents over the past three decades. At age 14 in Philadelphia (United States of America) I initiated my professional career in a customer service segment of the dining and entertainment industry until I finalized my studies in 1984, at which time I received my degree in Computer Engineering and Robotics. I continued to pursue my career in customer service within the IT sector, specializing in Project and Transformation Management, whilst a senior executive in the North American computer retail industry. Relocating to Europe, Portugal, in 1991, I expanded upon my activities by incorporating product development and rolling out best practices to other divisions of companies such as Reuters & Sun Microsystems in my capacity as Director of Customer Services. During this time I had contact with Europe, Middle East and Africa. In 2003, I initiated my first entrepreneurial venture by creating an Interim Executive Management company based in Portugal and specializing in increased strategic effectiveness. My biggest projects were with Sun Microsystems & Hewlett Packard, where I revolutionized their Education departments.

My background in Strategic Management dates back to 1984 and I have exercised my niche practices serving the Entrepreneurs Organization (www.eonetwork.org), of which I am a member and Area Director for Southern Europe, as well as the Young Presidents Organization (www.ypo.org), just to name a few. As a certified Strategic Facilitator I empower individuals, groups & teams by helping them identify their core values whilst establishing short, medium & long term strategic vision. Through these practices I encompass specific niche skills in the areas of Values Based Strategic Management, Leadership Development Solutions, Dream Coaching® and Emotional Languages. I am also an experienced practitioner of Kepner-Tregoe's (http://www.kepner-tregoe.com) Rational Process Methodology’s.
