    Ph.D. Dissertation Abstract: Because the existing applications of Technology Opportunity Analysis (TOA) text data mining framework developed by Alan Porter et al used small datasets, previous research never pushed the limits of the methodology and failed to identify areas for future research associated with using larger datasets.
