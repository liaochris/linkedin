Jim Thorne’s Summary

Successful business career equps me to work with leaders of companies and help them take their businesses to a new level. Reported to CEO's for 17 years and bring both a depth of business moxie and a style to which leaders respond. Consulting business begun in 1998 and includes national and local clients. Led large company human resource functions for Monsanto Company and for Hillenbrand Industries. Transformed company from midwest focus to global effectiveness. Worked for excellent CEO's and function always contributed to the short term business goals while building leadership and employee strength for the future. Served as internal productivity consultant with dramatic gains in manufacturing, sales, new product development and marketing. Also served as head of all compensation for Monsanto Company.
