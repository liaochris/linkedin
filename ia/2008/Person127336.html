Matthew Dybwad’s Summary

As Director of Creative Strategy and wearer of many hats, Matthew produces and manages all creative aspects of Emotive’s online direct marketing practice. In addition to his creative duties, Matthew also plans and executes online outreach and fundraising campaigns as well as running Emotive's online advertising operation. Before joining Emotive, Matthew served as Creative Director for TSE Enterprises in partnership with DCI Group, where he specialized in web site creation, graphic design, multimedia production, and strategic online communication. Matthew has been working at the intersection of politics and the Internet for ten years with companies such as New Media Communications and Capitol Advantage. Matthew graduated from The George Washington University with a bachelor’s degree in Political Communications and Journalism.
