Garland Binns’s Summary

Garland William Binns III is the President and Chief Executive Officer of Media Rake, Inc. Under Garland’s leadership, Media Rake has grown from a small design and consulting boutique to a revolutionary digital landscape architecture firm specializing in technology infrastructure, helping businesses and professionals efficiently develop and execute strategic decisions with confidence.

Garland and his firm have grown Media Rake by building new solutions internally, making cardinal acquisitions, and establishing vital relationships with key technology partners. With operations all across the United States and in many foreign countries, Media Rake helps customers protect and cultivate their infrastructure, information, and interactions by delivering unique and customized services that address all of their technology needs.

Prior to joining Media Rake, Garland spent over 12 years designing, developing, and implementing technology solutions for both businesses and professionals. He has held several administrative positions overseeing and fostering technology growth in a number of market sectors including two long periods in both the financial and retail service industries. Garland is a pioneer dedicated to seeding Media Rake and its clients with new opportunities for growth and development in the 21st century.
