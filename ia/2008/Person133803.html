Matías Iturburu’s Summary

Self-taught in almost every aspect of my career. I've done all the way from being a kid who knew flash to developing python-based sites and web-applications using standards (and those other nice buzzwords out there).
As every other self-taught I've been developing quite unrelated interest, like music, social science and cooking.

As a long standing commitment, I'm activist on human rights defense, with participation in different kinds of social movements and organizations.

That background took me working mostly as self-employed, on startups or work cooperative, with part time jobs for people like Wamani (an APC member).

I joined Revoluciones on it's very beginning in a way of making a living out of being a techie activist with commitment on improving the way people that makes-the-difference uses technology to make-the-difference
