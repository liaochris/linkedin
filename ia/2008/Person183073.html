Sue Frame’s Summary

I have taught in an academic environment for 7 years, 2 of them as a Head of Department of Psychology, managing a team of 18 academics.
My main area of interest is around the interplay between learning and IT. I have an active and ongoing interest in the Psychological research of cognitive learning in IT.
I was Project Manager on a significant project within the Ministry if Education where I saw first hand the importance of good communication and quality leadership.
In the last yeat I have started my own consulting business specialising in Strategic Knowledge Management and Technical Delivery Advice for Websites and CMS systems.
