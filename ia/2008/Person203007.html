Zahid Torres-Rahman’s Summary

Director of Business Action for Africa (www.businessactionforafrica.org), an international network of businesses and business organisations set up at the 2005 G8 Business Summit.

Director of Inspiris (www.inspiris.co.uk) which specialises in the contribution that businesses can make to international development and poverty reduction. Our mission is to improve the lives of as many people as possible, as quickly as possible, by harnessing the best business brains and latest technology as innovatively as possible.

Steering Group for DFID’s Private Sector Development Strategy Review and Special Adviser to the UK Parliament’s International Development Committee on their Private Sector Development Inquiry.

Head of Business Outreach at the Commission for Africa Secretariat. Led the Commission's policy work on growth, investment, infrastructure, private sector development, employment and corporate social responsibility, and was the lead author of Chapter 7 of the Commission’s Report. Seconded to the Commission for Africa from HM Treasury, where led the Private Sector and Poverty Reduction branch of the International Poverty Reduction Team.

Management consultant with PricewaterhouseCoopers, where managed a number of projects for the UK’s Department for International Development. Have worked in Africa, the Caribbean, South America and South Asia.

Zahid Torres-Rahman’s Specialties:

Helping businesses understand, enhance and communicate the positive impact they can have on poverty reduction through their core business operations. Business engagement, business-to-business partnerships, business-to-government partnerships, business-to-NGO partnerships, business strategy, CSR, Corporate Social Responsibility, policy analysis and design, workshop facilitation, training, stakeholder engagement, network management.
