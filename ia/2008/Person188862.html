Tim Buchholz’s Summary

Tim Buchholz of Broadlands, Virginia has spent a career simplifying technology products and services into easy to understand brands with benefits that sell. He has worked with 3Com as a Director, Global Marketing, and Newbridge Networks as a Director, Strategic Planning. He studied Marketing at Old Dominion University and graduated in 1980.

Experience includes: IP switches, wireless gateways, Internet access systems, test equipment, communications software, international long distance and data services, Retail Marketing Systems and Digital Signage.
