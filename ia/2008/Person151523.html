John (J.J.) Williams’s Summary

I have worked with many teams in my career so far. All in which have been a great experience in their own way. I have roughly about 3 and a half years of game development experience ranging from Art, QA, and Design. I work at Midway Games with a tight group of designers and we just finished John Woo Presents: Stranglehold. I am assisting our Newcastle studio in England doing mission design for, The Wheelman. I've been also helping on a new IP doing design, new game play ideas, conceptualizing characters, and building playable 3D environments. My goals are to put the best quality work into a project and let it speak for itself. I love being a game designer and it is truly my calling.

It has been a long road to get where I am today, but its been a great journey. I wouldn't want to be in any other industry.
