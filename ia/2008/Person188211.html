Thomas Konkol’s Summary

I am currently looking for contract animation work.

I have experience in both 2D and 3D animation. I have been animating professionally for 13 years in the video game industry. I have hand created animations and worked with motion capture data. I have also lead animation teams, created animation lists and directed motion capture sessions. I know what it takes to get quality animations done from the start to finish for a project. I am also a published author and have a unique writing style. I have strong painting and illustration skills.

Thomas Konkol’s Specialties:

2D animation experience. I am able to create both realistic animations and cartoon style animations with an exaggerated style.
