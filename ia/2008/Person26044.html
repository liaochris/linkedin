Brad Thomas’s Summary

I'm a British entrepreneur and software developer who's now a permanent resident of the USA. I'm presently focused on artificial intelligence, specifically natural language recognition and machine understanding. I have experience of building businesses from start to successful finish. I have developed financial forecasting software incorporating artificial intelligence, created a profitable business from that (marketing to investors) and sold the business. I have developed a cellphone game company, raised cash investment and sold that company. My experience includes software development, marketing, business strategy, administration, finance, raising investment, negotiation and sales.
