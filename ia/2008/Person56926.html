Ed Zwart’s Summary

I'm a software developer with a passion for online water cooler communities. I have worked for both a small startup and a large government agency, in the process putting together a wide variety of Web 2.0 development skills.

I'm also formerly a teacher, an editor and a decorative concrete finisher.
