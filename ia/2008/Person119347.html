Marc Brinkley’s Summary

I have spent the last 10 years in video game development working on some of Electronic Arts’ highest profile video games including NBA Street and Fight Night. Over the last 5 years, I have successfully lead several teams from pre-production, into production, and to finished product. I have built and run small departments of artists and then ran the art production staff of a single project and currently, all artists and technical artists for the studio on all projects. I have learned how to manage teams and projects of varying complexity and sizes, time tables and budgets to a high degree of quality.

My primary role is to ensure that the art teams have the tools, technologies and techniques they need in order to build the highest quality art for the latest generation of video game consoles. In order to do that, I must constantly be ahead of the technology needs of the game teams and be looking for tool sets that will enable artists to produce high end content.

Once needs have been identified, problem solving, integration and implementation is the next area of responsibility. Getting new technology or development processes, or new team members implemented is always a challenge. Over the last 10 years, I have developed good processes and skills to make and drive changes as well to help solve personal and technology issues as change happens.
