Sue Perry’s Summary

I am an experienced development / channel / programme manager in the e-business / web / online space, who also has experience in complementary areas such as relationship management, stakeholder management, service management and supplier management.

I have worked both as a consultant and in-house, assisting a number of organisations with shaping their online strategies and delivering their programmes and service improvements.

I have also maintained my own website, www.londonforfree.net, for the past 10 years.

My goal is to keep doing what I love doing, and that's to work with business stakeholders and web specialists in challenging commercial environments, delivering real business value through the effective use of web technologies.
