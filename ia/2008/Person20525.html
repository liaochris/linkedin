Bryan Caplovitz’s Summary

Born in Houston, Texas, Bryan Caplovitz studied Psychology and Marketing at the University of Texas in Austin. Throughout college, Bryan worked as a Customer Service Trainer. For 2 years after college, Bryan was on the road; he traveled to nearly every state in the U.S. as a consultant for Alpha Epsilon Pi fraternity. During his tenure at AEPi, in his position of Director of Alumni Relations, he laid the foundation for a new outreach program to former chapter members. He also created and programmed the membership database management system for the fraternity's national headquarters.

Bryan returned to school to earn a graduate degree (Ed.M.) from Columbia University in Higher and Adult Education Administration. At Columbia, he worked in the Office of Student Activities while simultaneously managing the office for the Enrichment Programs at the Leta Hollingworth Center for the Gifted and Talented. During this time, he created database management systems for the Hollingworth Center and several professors at Columbia.

Because of his deep understanding of database design and systems architecture, Bryan went to work for Flash Creative Management after graduate school — where he focused on creating technology-based business solutions for consulting clients.

His unusual multi-industry relationships in education, entertainment, consulting, and technology with his background in customer service eventually led to the creation of SpeakerMatch, the world's largest source of professional and emerging professional speakers — which is where you'll find Bryan Caplovitz today.
