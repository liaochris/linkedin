Lucilla Madamba’s Summary

Product designer with experience in interaction design, product usability and user research. Expertise in the domain of web, mobile and desktop software design, and 10 foot workflow design. Particular interest in ethnographic research, market research, trends in personal consumer technological behaviour and personal media consumption.

Lucilla Madamba’s Specialties:

User process workflow and interaction design, usability testing and expert reviews, field studies and ethnography, questionnaire design and data analysis, user analysis, requirements and specification writing, presentation, use of domain tools
