Possess the industry and engineering expertise to identify next generation technologies and foster development and acquisition efforts in support of venture capital investments. Exceptionally skilled in the development of key partnerships and alliances, market and product development strategies, and highly targeted business solutions.

Strategic relationship and partner development, forging key alliances through widely acknowledged reputation for industry expertise and leadership.
