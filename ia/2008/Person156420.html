ABOUT me...

My career started by developing and maintaining of radio-electronic devices; then in the late seventies and earlier eighties I worked with the various mainframe IBM 360/370 applications. In 1985 I co-founded a R&D and IT training company, which grow from 3 to 350 people over the course of 5 years. Since the nineties I am providing the management consulting services mostly in North America.

Since 1987 I have founded and co-founded a few R&D companies: BSTC (1987, sold in 2007), ATMIC (1995), WorcsNet (2002), GlobalBusinessMart.com (2007) and ABIOT (2003).

I have earned my master’s degree in computer science, bachelor in engineering and law; and completed the Canadian Securities Course (Investment).

During my "free" time I am studying business related sciences, developing my business networking and enjoying recreational activities with my lovely family (including our handsome orange-belton English Setter) and friends.
