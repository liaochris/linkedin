What have you got going?

Short BIO: I have over fifteen years professional experience working with all kinds of technology, with nine years in some form of management role from project management, people management to executive management. My introduction to technology really started when my mom bought my first computer at age 8 (a VIC20) and with my constant tinkering with electronics prior to that. I’m a principle driven person, with a passion (overused but true) for business, people and technology.

I have been fortunate to have met so many amazing people over the years and gotten my hands dirty on so many different technologies. From running a BBS back in the day, helping get several ISPs started from the ground up (you get to do and learn everything there), setting up the commercial network used for government business, security (both physical and data), to managing a tier 1 mission critical application teams in the Fortune 50, owning R&D labs worldwide with massive SAN / Server tech, owned multi-million dollar budgets and the list goes on. I have always enjoyed pushing myself and taking on challenges – hate being bored, so I don’t.
I have a strong technical foundation and truly understand the business (wish more technical folks did).

I was born in Sweden and grew up in the US (visiting Sweden in the Summers), I’m also a Naturalized US Citizen. I enjoy traveling, biking, hiking, blogging, social media, water sports, snowboarding, photography, videography, reading, SCUBA, continuous improvement / learning and especially spending time with my family.
