Janet Goldstein’s Summary

Experienced and innovative book publishing executive and editor consults with high-potential writers, entrepreneurs, and business and nonprofit leaders to bring their ideas, platforms, and brands to the next level.
Key areas: 1)"thought partnership and coaching" to develop ideas, positioning, and messages; 2) "visibility consulting" to build platforms and constituencies through targeted writing, communications, publishing, media, lecture, and community-building activities; 3) "proposal development" for new business ventures and book projects; 4)"launch planning" for authors and new ventures; 5)"speaker and educator" at publishing and entrepreneurial conferences, and as director of the NYU Summer Publishing Institute for Books.

"Janet's contributions to my work have been critical. She will remain a key resource on my virtual team."

“Janet provides motivation and a wealth of insight to inspire great ideas, and she drills down to help make them bulletproof.”
