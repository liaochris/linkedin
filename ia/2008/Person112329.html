Lee Perrett’s Summary

Extensive experience recruiting senior level executives and middle management candidates within multiple disciplines and industries including: healthcare, pharmaceuticals, consumer products, banking and finance, high tech, consulting, manufacturing, communications, and professional services. Experienced retained search and corporate recruiting experience - have worked in the US & several International locations in Europe, Latin America and South America, with The Coca-Cola Company. We conduct retained search assignments for wide variety of clients in the healthcare, insurance & biotech/life sciences areas.Tyler & Company was recently cited in Modern Healthcare as the country’s 7th largest healthcare search firm. We focus on healthcare searches, but define healthcare broadly to include integrated delivery systems, hospitals, medical clinics, HMOs, managed care entities, insurance companies and start-up/health service companies. Our clients include public and private organizations, faith-based systems, non-profit medical centers and tax paying corporations. We conduct searches and recruit candidates nationwide and internationally through our affiliation with The Taplow Group.

Lee Perrett’s Specialties:

Provide retained search services for client companies who want to hire excellent people in a fast, cost efficent manner. Specialize in working with clients in healthcare, including hospitals, medical clinics, insurance companies, managed care entities, biotech, and start up health service organizations. We have offices in Atlanta, Philadelphia, Charlotte and Austin as well as affiliated offices world-wide in 31 countries through The Taplow Group.
