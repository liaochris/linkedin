Joe Valentino’s Summary

Over the last 26 years I have had the pleasure of working in 3 different industries in distribution and manufacturing environments. I began servicing local mass merchants, and progressed to district, regional and finally senior level management. I also managed various disciplines from sales, marketing and customer service to R&D and purchasing. I had great mentors who were willing to offer me the opportunity to grow and the direction to get there. This provided me the ability to transfer my skills to different industries. I have also spent considerable time managing businesses in Europe which has helped me in a number of ways.

I relocated to St Augustine Florida in 2007 and decided to start Perfect Pool & Patio, Inc after having a fiberglass pool installed at my new home. Our business is unique as it provides the consumer with quality products along with the flexibility to complete their individual project in whatever way suits their situation. This means they can choose to have one contractor complete their entire project, have separate contractors, or do some or all of the work themselves. Also, these pools lend themselves to renovations as they are easily dropped into existing pools or holes left by old pools.

We also have an on-line store which sells better quality hot tubs, grills, fire pits, pergolas and outdoor furniture directly to the consumer. The focus of the on-line store is to provide a simplified shopping experience with higher end products and lower than retail prices.

I'm a self motivated team member and proven leader with a consistent record of results. I will always be grateful to the mentors and associates I have had over the years but I am even more grateful to the many customers it has been my privilege to serve. To this day they provide me with my most important lessons. I take pride in the satisfaction of my clients; it is where business begins and ends. I'm excited about the opportunity to help people turn their visions into reality.
