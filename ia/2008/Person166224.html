Ruud van der Meulen (CISSP/CBCP/CCM/PrinceII)’s Summary

Ruud van der Meulen is an IT professional with over 20 years of experience. He has worked on several information security, disaster recovery, risk management and business continuity projects. He holds an international certification in security standards - CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) and Business Continuity Management CBCP (Certified Business Continuity Professional), CCM (Certified Continuity Manager) and is awaiting confirmation of his certification as a MBCI (Member of the Business Continuity Institute).

Ruud has worked across several industries including two secondments with ABN Amro and ING bank. He is experienced in liaising between the IT dept and senior management. He is certified in PRINCE 2; which is an internationally renowned project management process which has been adopted by the majority of multi-national companies. He is also experienced in auditing and holds the internationally recognized BS7799 Auditor certification.

Ruud is experienced in managing large IT projects and has managed teams of up to 16 people. He is currently working on a security project for a Dutch Telecom Company called KPN.
