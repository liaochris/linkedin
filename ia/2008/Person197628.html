Vladimir Herrera’s Summary

I am an award-winning interactive art director with over 10 years of experience conceiving, developing, and implementing design solutions; working together with clients, creative directors, editors, web programmers, and copywriters to carefully design products that work best for each particular situation, positioning and target audience.

I have produced graphic sketches, designs, digital executions, and copy layouts for a myriad of interactive materials ranging from full websites, virtual 3D worlds, blogs, interactive CDs, e-blasts to PowerPoint presentations, flash banners, and event videos. I have also produced magazines, direct mailers, magazine ads, brochures, newspaper ads, posters, point of purchase displays, and environmental graphics.

Experienced in strategic brainstorming, campaign development, creative brief development, creative execution, brand and identity, composition, color, light, motion, typography, and a breadth of creative styles. Managing all printing related issues in timely fashion and within allocated budget, including the use of pre-press software and submitting jobs for offset printing, digital printing or photocopy.

Vladimir Herrera’s Specialties:

I am an expert in a variety of graphic design software including: Photoshop, Illustrator, QuarkXPress, Flash, DreamWeaver, Director, MS Office, AfterEffects, Acrobat, and InDesign among others. I have an intimate knowledge on web standards, usability, 508 accessibility and universal design using the following technologies: HTML, XML, JAVA, FLASH, and CSS.
