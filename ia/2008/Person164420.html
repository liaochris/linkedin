Ron Kleiman’s Summary

I Founded PCMusic in 1999, PCM quickly established itself as the leader in the on-demand music industry by being the first company to offer a legal on-demand music service for businesses. Eight years later, PCM is still the leader in on-demand music services and has secured some of the biggest names in the QSR, Hospitality, Fitness and Retail Industries as proud clients.

As well, PCM was the first company to introduce a fully digital touch screen jukebox to the Canadian Market place. Three years later and PCM is the largest supplier of on-demand music services in the background and jukebox music market.
