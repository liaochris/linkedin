Keith Giarman’s Summary

At DHR, I work closely with VC & PE firms across the country on their internal hiring needs as well as recruiting for their portfolio companies. DHR is the smallest and most entrpreuneurial of the big firms with approximately $100 million in revenue and 46 offices worldwide. (www.dhrinternational.com) My practice tends to focus on Partners, Principals and key executive staff in tech-related VC and PE firms as well as Board, C and VP level executives in tech-related companies (consumer, enterprise, communications). I also work with all DHR practice heads (Consumer, Retail, Industrial, Manufacturing, Professional Services, Financial Services, etc.) in executing searches for non-tech buyout firms.

While many recruiters transition into the field from general industry, there are few who transition from general industry into the start-up world and ultimately a President / CEO position. I believed when I made the transition in early 2006, and believe even more so now, that companies can benefit from my real-world operating experience. I have literally held every position for which I recruit -- VP Sales, Marketing, BD, President, COO, CEO – or I have hired them as direct reports myself (VP Engineering, VP Ops, CTO, CIO, etc). I have been a senior executive in small companies like NightFire (USVP / Sequoia funded startup as 5th employee) and as large as Citizens Communications ($3 billion public company where I was one of 5 on the executive management team). I am, in other words, well-versed in the operating and hiring challenges of small, mid-cap and large public companies.
