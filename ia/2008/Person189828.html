Toshi Kameoka’s Summary

Toshi Kameoka is an associate in the San Francisco office of Latham & Watkins with experience advising on and executing a variety of corporate development deals. His law practice focuses on acquisitive transactions, including private equity investments, acquisition financings and merger control and antitrust counseling. Prior to joining Latham & Watkins, Toshi worked for over three years as a strategy consultant for Ernst & Young and Capgemini, where he helped both Fortune 500 and startup clients in the US, UK and Japan to develop market entry and M&A strategies. In addition, he led growth strategy projects for Johnson & Johnson Consumer Products Company as a freelance consultant and worked on licensing and intellectual property matters for NHL Enterprises, the marketing arm of the National Hockey League. Toshi is highly proficient in Japanese and has had experience as both a business consultant and law clerk in Tokyo.

Toshi Kameoka’s Specialties:

• Corporate Strategy: growth platforms, market entry, strategic M&A due diligence, technology enablement
• Legal: mergers and acquisitions, private equity investment, acquisition and bridge finance, merger control and antitrust review, capital asset finance
• Industry Experience: financial institutions, consumer packaged goods, manufacturing, Internet commerce
