Rachel Rosemire’s Summary

Straight out of college I worked for almost 12 years at a San Francisco-based software company doing everything from I.T. Purchasing to Project Management and acting as a Senior Planning Analyst & Budget Manager to the CIO/CTO. That company was bought out by another, which resulted in the layoffs of many people, including myself.

For several years, in addition to the roles listed above, I also did music marketing & promotions on the side. I then decided to transition into a corporate marketing position to tie my corporate and music worlds together. While the company didn't work out, the job did just what I wanted it to - it pushed me further into the music marketing realm. With another layoff after only 6 short months, it was just the push I needed to pursue the music thing full time.

As of 2008 I now operate my own music management company full time.
