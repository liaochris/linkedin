Eric Ervin’s Summary

Seeker of new technologies with a skill set and personality to successfully accomplish any project or mission

Experienced in the following areas:

Geographic Information Systems (GIS) with a specialty in GIS for business and marketing. Analysis includes site selection, demographics, expenditures, psychographics (lifestyles), customer location/profiling, precision marketing and district/trade area allocation.

Economic development and business strategy, including eight years as a member of an internationally known economic development department that founded the Economic Gardening form of economic development. Our program offers high-end research, data and advice to over 300 businesses a year. As the GIS analyst, I assist businesses with their advanced marketing and strategy needs, and provide Internet marketing and graphic design services. I also speak both locally and nationally to organizations about our program.

Internet Marketing, search engine optimization (SEO) and applied Internet technology. Constantly looking for new ways to apply Internet technology for business, I assist businesses with their Internet marketing and technology needs. I currently am involved building Internet "buzz" monitoring/alert systems using customized RSS feeds and various feed readers. With my strong database, Internet, and marketing background, I find pleasure in keyword research, pay-per-click advertising, competitor research and all things SEO (search engine optimization).

Photography and graphic design. As a former professional photographer and as-needed graphic designer, I have the ability to “see” good design and apply it to any business need. From marketing to the “perfect customer” to visualizing a web page layout and structure, I can bring together skills with professional, appealing results using the latest in technology (Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Microsoft PowerPoint).
