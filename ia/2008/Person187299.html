Domenico Tassone’s Summary

Mr. Tassone has been innovating in the online industry since the 1993 launch of the seminal, Planet StarChild, a multimedia BBS that allowed fans to "try before they buy." The service was ad-supported and featured pix, music clips and news from indie musicians/labels. In 1994, Dominic co-founded one of the earliest Web development agencies - Streams Online Media Development.

Overview: A high-energy business professional that has worked extensively with new Internet ad technologies and Web media products. Domenico's business background is both broad and deep, bringing line management sensibility together with technical staff skills. In addition, he has taught marketing at the college-level.

Domenico Tassone’s Specialties:

Start-up expert. Functional expertise: business dev (alliances), marketing, entrepreneurial finance, product dev, contracts/negotiations, sales mgmt.
Industry experience: Web-based software (B2C and B2B), Internet media (B2C and B2B), online real estate, interactive agency services and retailing (online/offline).

Technologies: ad serving, behavioral/CRM targeting, content personalization; video(ITV/IPTV/VOD), cable TV, streaming/Webcasting, podcasting/RSS, wireless/mobile.
