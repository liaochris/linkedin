Perttu Tolvanen’s Summary

Designing large Websites and managing complicated Web projects is what I do best.

My daily work is doing user research, leading concept design workshops, developing information architectures and user interfaces for large Websites or intranets and using different kinds of measurement tools to support decision making.

Perttu Tolvanen’s Specialties:

    (Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Public Safety industry)

    2004 — 2005 (1 year)

Special attention is paid to society's essential information systems. My role as a client-side project manager and information architect was to design NESA's new Websites and coordinate the production projects. Besides leading the in-house team I was responsible for choosing the design agencies for the project and NESA's new web content management system.
