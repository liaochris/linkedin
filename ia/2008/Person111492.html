Laurent Bouty’s Summary

I have 10+ years of Marketing experience in ICT industry for B2B and B2C services, nationaly and internationaly. I worked 7 years for Belgacom Mobile and Vodafone Group where I held several marketing functions. Since 2005, I have started my owned management consultancy firm where I help leading companies to develop their business strategies (Marketing, Sales) and to execute it. Since 2005, I am been providing specialist advices on strategy, marketing and sales to many corporations in the B2B and B2C, in particular, the telecommunications industries.

I am also giving lectures at Brussels University (strategy seminars) and at MCE (Management Centre Europe)..

Latest projects:
-Worldwide rebranding in the Financial Sector
-Fiber network: Strategy definition, business plan and call for tender preparation
-Device management: from process to supply: how to improve device (GSM, accessories, ...) activities within an organization
-E-Care strategy and program management: how to use internet for improving customer relationship and reducing cost?
-Off-Net and On-Net communication strategy including new media development with leading players like Endemol
-Strategy and Marketing courses amongst TOP 100 world companies

Laurent Bouty’s Specialties:

Marketing Strategy, Program Management, Customer Experience Design, On-line and Off-line go to market, Business Plan, Product Management, Business Intelligence
