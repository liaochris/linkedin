Hubert Lee’s Summary

Hubert Lee is an audience builder passionately applying his formula for building community: Content + User Experience + Findability = Massive Adoption & Loyalty.

Known in the business intelligence community as The Dashboard Spy, Hubert Lee has created a collection of web resources around digital dashboards that has spawned a new category of BI reference material. His collection of thousands of dashboard examples is the largest in the world.

When not designing dashboards, Hubert directs teams of UX, UI and IA experts. Hubert envisions, defines and manages the implemenation of amazing user experiences for top companies. Director of Marketing. Director of Interactive Solutions. With his extensive background in marketing, communications, and technology, Hubert brings a passion to the challenge of championing the user’s needs and objectives throughout the project life cycle.

During the last 8 years at Helios & Matheson, Hubert has helped major clients by leading teams charged with envisioning new web applications and sites, redesigning and realigning existing systems with new objectives, applying the latest technology solutions, and maintaining and supporting crucial enterprise systems.

Hubert is expert at user centered design, information architecture, usability, art direction and internet marketing. He is especially passionate about the application of Web 2.0 technologies and philosophies to corporate systems. He manages a diverse mix of graphic artists, coders, information architects, project managers and business analysts to ensure compelling user experiences.

Prior to joining Helios & Matheson, Hubert spent many years as a marketer using web technologies and internet tools to build audiences and provide value throughout the sales cycle.
