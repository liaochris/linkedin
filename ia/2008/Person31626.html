Casey Martinez’s Summary

I have been involved in the clean energy field in various capacities since graduating from Texas State University with a BS in physics. I have experience with thin-film semiconductor fabrication techniques including deposition and etching as well as experimental design with direct experience with optics on the nanoscale.

I have gained valuable technical and business experience since college working with solar thermal and solar electric equipment, energy efficiency products, non-profit initiatives, and venture capital organizations.

My goal is to accelerate the adoption of clean energy technologies by improving photovoltaic design and fabrication techniques by using my knowledge of science and the skills gained by working 'hands on' in the clean tech field.

Casey Martinez’s Specialties:

Semiconductor physics, thin-film vacuum deposition, thin-film etching, nano/micro surface characterization, nanophotonic experimental design, computational data analysis, green building techniques, solar thermal system design for domestic hot water, solar electric system design for residential and commercial buildings, and public policy initiatives.
