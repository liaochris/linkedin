Lizz Harmon’s Summary

Lizz Harmon is the president/CEO, and majority stockholder of HarmonTampa Public Relations, a boutique firm she founded more than 10 years ago in Tampa’s Westshore Business District.

When you become a HarmonTampa client, you too will be in good company. The other businesses in the exclusive HarmonTampa family of clients who understand PR drives business outcomes are:

- Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Bailey,
- Disney on Ice, Blue Bell Ice Cream,
- The Florida Orchestra,
- Authentic Team Merchandise "Bucs & Bulls Heaven",
- Transitions Championship on the PGA TOUR,
- Metropolitan Ministries, Fintech,
- Florida Strawberry Festival,
- Cambridge Christian School,
- Lions Eye Institute Foundation,
- Pediatric Cancer Foundation, and
- Florida's Largest Home Show.

If you're looking for a Madison Avenue PR firm, the office with a view, and shelves of trophies and awards, you're looking in the wrong place. We know our clients have to grow sales, increase traffic or attract new business and that creativity alone or winning awards won't cut it.

At HarmonTampa, we deliver stand-alone and integrated marketing services based on our clients' needs and the specialized audiences they serve.

If you're looking for the best return on your investment, the most effective, "get-it-done" agency, you've found it.

Lizz Harmon’s Specialties:

We specialize in West Central Florida media relations and publicity primarily for entertainment, special events and hospitality clients.

We're all about results, not all the fluff.
