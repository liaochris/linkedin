Doug Reed’s Summary

I take pride in being able to build anything web based with any technology. I can lead teams, architect scalable systems, and still get my hands dirty with technologies ranging from the browser to the database.

I am actively developing my own ideas as well as looking for others to partner with for new start up opportunities.

Although my experience is primarily in financial systems, I am interested in creating consumer Internet applications.

Doug Reed’s Specialties:

Web standards, modern CSS layouts, DOM scripting, Flash/Flex, Ruby/Rails, PHP, all things Java, most relational databases, web security, Macs, solving hard problems, and building teams of people that can execute against a plan.
