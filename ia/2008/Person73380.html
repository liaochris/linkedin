Guillaume Alexandre Zietek’s Summary

ALMA Consulting Group has a wide and recognized expertise in the field of funding for Innovation.

My role is to support our clients by finding the best solution for them to benefits for Public Fundings for their R&D projects.

More specifically involved in European collaborative projects, I support clients setting up their R&D projects to give them the best chances to get fundings.

My experience covers project engineering in the fields of Energy, Material Science and transport in the European Framework Program. I have set up small (5-7 partners) to large sized (25 partners) projects involving organizations from all over Europe.

I am also involved in the management of such project: Strategic & Financial management, project monitoring are a key added value for the reaching of technical objectives.
