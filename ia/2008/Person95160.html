John R. Maculley, Jr., MBA, PMP (john@maculley.com)’s Summary

Visit - www.BlueSkyPM.info - to Read About My Latest Book on Innovation and Project Management!

Mr. Maculley spent the last 17 years helping a diverse group of start-up technology businesses, Fortune 500 corporations, and U.S. government agencies execute on their strategies and improve their operations. Most of his consulting assignments have been in the aerospace and high tech industries, with brief stints in software. He also teaches business classes at the university level to stay connected with the most current management concepts before they are published. Mr. Maculley looks for challenging engagements where the client's prior efforts have typically failed - the kind of projects where change can produce exponential growth.
