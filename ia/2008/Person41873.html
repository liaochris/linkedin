Codru Vrabie’s Summary

- representing civil society organizations on the National Integrity Council, a representative body overseeing the activity of the soon-to-be-established Integrity Agency;
- until recently, worked on public consultations related to the EU agenda, at the Romanian Parliament;
- trainer & consultant on matters related to good administration, (new) public management, anti-corruption, legislation drafting and institution building;
- 9 years working experience in advocacy NGOs in Romania, as well as with advocacy NGOs in South-Eastern Europe;
- interested in comparative legislative systems and processes, public participation in decision-making and policy-making, free access to information, and conflict of interest prevention;
