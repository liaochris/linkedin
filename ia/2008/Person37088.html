    December 2006 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Although worried about the slowdown in the US many business leaders are still optimistic about the state of the Australian economy.

    Their main challenge is indeed still the same: how to effectively deal with the skill shortage - a huge constraint on achieving their company’s budgeted sales and profit growth

    Every CEO knows that each morning there could be a resignation letter on his desk, and they are looking for ways to change this

    So what we do at RPO Group is help them making this change happen

    Our clients engage with us because

    They have a really tough time finding people quickly and cost effectively and are therefore forced to rely on costly recruitment agencies which leads them to look for alternative ways to attract talent.
