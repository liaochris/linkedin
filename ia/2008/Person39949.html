Didier Coquoz’s Summary

- International Executive who developed three successful pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical companies on both sides of the Atlantic, senior executive management level.

- Entrepreneurial, action-driven, problem-solving pharmaceutical professional with extensive experience in North America, Western and Eastern Europe, Russia, South America. Proven ability to solve complex business/ scientific/ regulatory/ marketing/ licensing issues. Demonstrated strong multicultural and multi-disciplinary leadership with excellent managerial, conceptual, analytical, planning, and communication skills.

- As CEO (Xigen S.A. Switzerland, IV 2004 - ):
• raised in 2005 the largest series A financing round of the preceding 5 years in European Biotech
• transformed in 24 months an academic spin-off with a lead compound into a promising Biotech of 15 FTEs with the first compound in clinics and 3 upcoming new leads.

As vice-President R&D (H3 PHARMA Inc., Montreal, Canada, VII 2000- III 2004)
• capitalized the company with 50 mios Can $ and structured it from scrartch
• succesfully developed therapeutic products (up to and including FDA NDA, Mexican registration)
• selected, due diligented, and gave major inputs in in-licensing, out-licensing and acquisition deals (over 450 pharmaceutical projects per year in 17 therapeutic areas)
• lead the development in North America and Europe of all projects of the portfolio with development budgets from 25 to 75 mio Can $

As vice-President R&D and Founding Employee (Triskel Integrated Services, Switzerland, I 1996- VI 2000)
• co-created the company from scratch
• generated corporate yearly revenues of over 2 mio$
• acitvely participated in the development and regulatory strategy of 22 therapeutics (with drugs on the European and US markets)

- Multilingual: English, French, German; Multinational: Swiss, Belgian (European); Ph.D. in Clinical Pharmacology
