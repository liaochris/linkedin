Tiffany Marshall’s Summary

After graduating from the University of Arizona in 2000 I accepted a position to become a team member with Wells Fargo Bank. My first eight months with Wells Fargo I worked closely with the public, clients and small business owners to meet their needs and demands with Wells Fargo banking products. It was at this level that my interpersonal skills grew immensely from daily customer service and face to face interactions.

I quickly transitioned vertically from the branch level to the Wells Fargo corporate offices located in Long Beach, CA. I began my new role as a Client Service Representative for nine months which allowed me to improve on my client service skills as well as gain a solid foundation of the basics of trust and estate administration as well as the basic ideologies of investment management.

After nine months I was promoted within Wells Fargo to become a Trust Officer. Over the next five years I managed over 350 trust accounts and became an advisor to more than 1,000.00 beneficiaries. Each account was unique and I gained the ability and knowledge to tailor my services to meet the needs of each individual client and their respective situations. Working in the trust environment was a personally rewarding and invaluable experience.

As of August 1, 2006, I transitioned my banking experience with Wells Fargo Bank and moved to the next level in my career. Working as a Financial Advisor with Merrill Lynch has been an amazing experience in just the few months that I have been with the company. My investment management knowledge and experience is growing by the day. Merrill Lynch has a complete assortment of products to meet the needs of all types of clients and our focus is to help our clients reach their financial goals and provide peace of mind. I am hoping to parlay my success and experience in the banking industry to a wonderful and lasting career with Merrill Lynch.

Tiffany Marshall’s Specialties:
