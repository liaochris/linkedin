Rick Carlsen’s Summary

Beauty, hand crafted exotic materials, innovative play and personalized experiences all come together in a Sculpted Game.

My goal is to have a Sculpted Game become a lifelong cherished possession for each and every owner and to be able to call each one of them a friend.

I am fortunate enough to be the proud father to five, and to be married to my soul mate. With Sculpted Games, I am showing them that, doing what you love, is the greatest path to follow as you help others.

My forte is to blend sculpture, game design and diverse technologies into a single personalized experience.

Previously I created and lead the vehicle software/electronic research division of the company that built the Sky Train, a driverless, computer controlled light rail transit system found in Vancouver, Toronto and Detroit. My team, in a little over a year, developed several advance computer control systems that leap fogged the established completion by several years while staying within budget. Several spin-off products enabled the company to enter new markets.

I hold a patent on an advanced computer design for their prototype train control system.

I worked Ford Motor Company,General Motors, Dynapro , UTDC as well contracts in the Bio-tech and numerous other industries.

I have numerous game designs and have been a pinball lover from the early days

Experience with numerous fabrication techniques and materials.
Experience with 3D software and its application in game design and simulations.
Extensive embedded control and electronic experience.

I love to create art, solve problems, and provide an experience that will get that WOW effect from a client or customer.

Please feel free to contact me. Hopefully I wil be able to help you in some way.

Rick Carlsen’s Specialties:

electronics, pinball, sculpture, embedded computer software, 3D modeling ,fabrication techniques, manufacturing , game design and running a technological based venture.
