R. Anouska Biswas’s Summary

Jumeirah International Hotel Group strives to be a world class international hotel and hospitality management company, committed to being the industry leader in all of our activities through dedication to our colleagues, customers, business partners and owners. The impressive portfolio of brands includes: Burj Al Arab in Dubai, as well as the Jumeirah Essex House in NYC. For a comprehensive list of hotels, go to: http://www.jumeirah.com/portfolio/

NexCen Brands, Inc. engages in the acquisition and management of established consumer brands in intellectual property centric industries focusing in three operating verticals: Retail Franchising, Consumer Branded Products, and Quick-Service Restaurant (QSR) Franchising.

UCC Capital Corporation ("UCC") team has created what has become many of the industry standards utilized in intellectual property whole company securitization finance today. In recent years, in addition to its innovative debt financings, UCC has expanded to include principal equity investments in IP-Centric companies, as well as a growing M&A and strategic advisory practice. UCC brings together a team of professionals with exceptional expertise in financing, exploiting, and capitalizing Intellectual Property assets.

Marriott International, Inc., is a leading lodging company. Its heritage can be traced to a root beer stand opened in Washington, D.C., in 1927 by J. Willard and Alice S. Marriott. Today, Marriott International has more than 2,800 lodging properties located in the United States and 67 in other countries and territories.

Renaissance Hotels & Resorts are a quality-tier full-service brand providing guests with the ambiance of a boutique with brand signatures that include interior design, a "street" restaurant, and savvy service. Today, Renaissance Hotels have more than 68 lodging properties located in the United States and 69 internationally.
