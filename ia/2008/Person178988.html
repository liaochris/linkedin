  • EVP Sales & Marketing

    June 2001 — May 2007 (6 years)

This included acting as the Product Owner within GSI’s agile development process (SCRUM) to set priorities and keep the Product Road-Map in synch with defined business objectives and revenue goals. In this capacity, I either led or had direct impact on all project prioritization schedules, technology investments, vendor reviews/selections, application UI designs, platform/architecture decisions, and integrated CRM support tools. GSI was acquired by VillageEDOCS in 2006.
