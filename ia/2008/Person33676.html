Charles Marinello’s Summary

Results driven leader with proven industry crossover successes at corporate, regional and field levels, national and international. Diverse industry operational experience in eCommerce, Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Government and U.S. Military. Senior level responsibilities in Corporate Management, Marketing, New Business Development, Purchasing and Operations up to $2.5 billion. Strong ability to bring immediate value to an organization. Creative, high energy, dynamic, people oriented executive of high integrity whose strengths are creating new businesses, selling ideas, driving sales, maximizing profits, planning, executing and creating a customer focused environment.
