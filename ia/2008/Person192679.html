Trey Martindale’s Summary

Trey Martindale is a professor of instructional design and technology. He works with talented graduate students to investigate the effectiveness of online learning environments. His work has been funded by the U.S. Department of Defense, the U.S. Department of Education, Microsoft Corporation, and other agencies.

As Associate Director of the Workforce ADL Co-Lab (DoD funded), he promotes e-learning standards and interoperability in the enterprise.

Dr. Martindale assists organizations on issues related to workforce training and learning management systems. Organizations include St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, State Farm Insurance, International Paper, and others.
