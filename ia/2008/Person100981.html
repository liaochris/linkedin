Justin Ramedia’s Summary

At a job or while freelancing I always like to leave things better than I found them. I truely believe that everything you need to know in life was learned in kindergarten and I like to keep that idea in my head at work and at play.

So far in my work-life I've been fortunate enough to work with some talented and brilliant people. My first design experience was with Reed Advertising Design where the creative director, Allen Reed allowed me to use my talents and ideas in an encouraging atmosphere.

I'm now at a medium sized ink and toner company where I work with the marketing team to develop effective campaigns to increase sales and company presence. I also help our sister companies with web and print design tasks.
