  • Owner

    Foreign Currency Exchange Services, L.L.C.

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Banking industry)

    April 2005 — Present (3 years 3 months)

    Foreign Currency Exchange Services buys and sells foreign currency. We maintain over 60 foreign currencies in stock which includes Euros, Pound Sterling, Chinese Yuan, Japanese Yen, Mexican Pesos and other major currencies. We also carry S. African Rand, Russian Rubles and Egyptian Pounds and can obtain other exotic cash within 72 hours. Our firm also offers foreign drafts and domestic or international wires for payments and emergencies as well as Travel Guard Travel Insurance. We are committed to quality customer service by building client relationships. We offer a value-added service, providing the business and leisure traveler foreign cash, competitive exchange rates, currency conversion sheets and travel tips. Our clients can make informed decisions when relying on our 26 years of experience. Courier service and appointments are available upon request. Foreign Currency Exchange Services is located at 251 East Merrill Street (in the Merrillwood Building) in Birmingham, Michigan.
