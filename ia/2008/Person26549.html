Jane Pak’s Summary

Ms. Pak is a solutions strategist. Prior to opening her own practice, Pert Strategies, Ms. Pak served President George W. Bush as the first Asian-American Presidential Appointee detailed to the U.S. Small Business Administration where she created and implemented strategic alliances with national and regional trade associations. Serving as Director of External Affairs for the Agency, she worked with numerous federal agencies, White House Initiatives and national business trade associations. Ms. Pak served as a liaison to the White House Initiative on Asian Americans; a Presidential Advisory Board. Currently, she still holds a Presidential appointment as an advisory council member to the Race and Ethnic Advisory Committee (REAC)

Ms. Pak founded Pert Strategies, an alliance development practice. Her experiences span across the board from political campaigning, television and film production and publishing, Ms Pak provides consultation services in the realm of traditional and modern business development plans, product development, strategic alliances, corporate sponsorships and marketing campaigns.

With affiliates in Washington, DC and Miami, FL, Ms. Pak continues to create coalitions between start-up companies, Fortune 500 corporations, trade groups, government agencies and advocates. Ms. Pak is actively involved in solving the climate crisis, helping corporations increase their corporate citizenship profiles and educating small businesses on energy efficiency and best green practices.
