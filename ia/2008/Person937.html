Aaron L. Gilbert’s Summary

Over the last decade Gilbert has functioned as a finance executive, facilitator and consulting producer to more than 40 companies who have operated primarily in the media, finance, entertainment, Internet and telecommunications industries in North America and across Asia. As Bron Management Ltd., Gilbert has provided consultation and facilitation services to content providers, distribution outlets, investment groups, and technology companies that support digital distribution and marketing of programming worldwide.

In 2007, Gilbert joined Rainmaker Entertainment to lead the company's business development and finance initiatives, focused on building and growing the Digital 3D Stereoscopic business. Rainmaker Entertainment (formerly Mainframe) is one of the worlds most successful and prolific producers of CG animation, and boasts a superior new management, creative, and technical team.
