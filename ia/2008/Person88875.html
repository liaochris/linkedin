Jennifer Gniadecki’s Summary

People like to do business with people they like.

How can people know if they like you...if they don't even know you?!

We believe that Google is nice. Yahoo is fun. But you're not going to build a business if your only relationship with the internet is some overwhelming desire to be on the front page of Google with keywords and SEO.

Who cares?

I hate to be the one to tell you, but the numbers game is dead.

Touching one person at a time and getting more customers than you can shake a stick at is what you want to do. Being everywhere is more important than being on page one of one website (even if that one website IS Google).

If you want to be on page 1 of Google, have us do an AdWords campaign for your company. As a Google Advertising Professional, we know our way around PPC and can tell you if it is a viable option for your company. (Because if anyone tells you PPC works for every industry, they're either lying or don't know any better...neither one is good for your company's bottom line!)

What we know and consult on:
Interactive/Integrated marketing - we take your branding into consideration at all times!
Find ways to use OPT (Other People's Traffic) to get you more traffic.
Blogs (we love WordPress, but others are welcome)
AdWords
Yahoo! Search Marketing
Microsoft adCenter
Podcasting (your own or being a guest)
Affiliate Marketing
Social Networking (Your company being where its potential customers hang out)

And pretty much anything else you've heard someone refer to as "That Web 2.0 thing"

For execution of the above strategies I'm a sales exec with VSI, Inc. - a virtual staffing company. Let us get you a Virtual Assistant to handle the execution. You only need to pay us to figure out what to have your Virtual Assistant do.

So...how can we help your company today?
