Francis Clauson’s Summary

Successful enterprise solution Pre-Sales manager who has worked for some of the best names in the industry - Computer Associates, Interwoven, Verity and EMC along with some small start up operations. For each of these I have been instrumental in growing their market presence within EMEA by working directly with the end customers and by developing appropriate relationships with partners who can assist with market penetration.

I have repeatedly been successfully in managing the acquisition and merger of companies and their associated technologies so that the combined solution offering offers a much more compelling market offering.

I also have considerable experience working within a corporate environment on the client side as prior to moving into sales I worked within large UK blue chip organisations as a consultant and project manager implementing core IT infrastructure projects within the transport, utility and manufacturing sectors.

I am a personable, tenacious, highly motivated, creative thinker with a good sense of humour. I excel when working in a team environment.

If I had more time and money I would revert to my passion of flying light aircraft all day.
