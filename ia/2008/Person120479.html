Marc Wilhelm’s Summary

I am currently working as the Creative Director at SGN (Social Gaming Network) in Palo Alto creating new and exciting social online game experiences. I've been working in the game industry for over 9 years and have shipped 10 titles including:

"Lair" From (PS3) I worked as lead designer at Factor 5 in San Rafael

"The Godfather" (PS2/XBX/360) game for EA's Redwood Shores studio

"Tomb Raider: Legend" (360/PS2/XBX) The most recent Lara Croft action/adventure hailed by most as the best TR since the original.

"The Sims 2" (PC) I worked as a design feature producer, auditing the many of the games key design features and working closely with the senior staff.

"James Bond 007: Everything or Nothing" (XBX, PS2, GC) I worked as a designer on the last decent Bond game :)
