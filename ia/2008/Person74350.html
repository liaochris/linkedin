Hamlet Azarian’s Summary

My passion is products, finding the best and learning about them. I’m not an expert and will never pretend to be, but I do love spending and making the time and meeting with unique designers who are expert artisan in their own right. It’s unfortunate some of this will never reach the end-user, cause at the core of retail, it’s the consumer. I think it’s lost in today’s overly saturated media, constantly fed via numerous marketing channels. The reality is, there are so many terrific products out there and I will do my best to find them or help create them!

Hamlet Azarian’s Specialties:

Business Development, Product Assortment, Customer Service, Communication, Fashion Trends, at the heart, attempting to develop new relationships and products that are a win-win-win for all parties involved; consumer, vendor, and retailer.
