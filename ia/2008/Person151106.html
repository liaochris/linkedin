Philippe Sauvan’s Summary

Professional profile

Senior Recruiter with more than 12 years of experience in the Consumer and Retail Industries, Philippe has solid knowledge and practice of Middle Eastern, European and Latin American markets particularly in FMCG, Cosmetics, Luxury Goods, Technology and Franchise Networks.

Philippe started his career in 1995 as an Associate for the executive search group WARD HOWELL INTERNATIONAL (now SIGNIUM INTERNATIONAL) in Latin America. Afterwards, he gained significant managerial experience performing as Country General Manager, Marketing & Planning Director and Senior Financial Controller for multinational groups such as AGFA, FUJI and L’ORÉAL during the last decade.

Vice President for the Middle East of the French Executive Search group TRANSEARCH in 2006, Philippe successfully launched in 2007 GULF EXECUTIVE, a Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) company based in Kuwait and specialised in head hunting and recruiting a large number of top & middle managers in Europe, Asia and the Middle East.

Education

Philippe holds an MBA from HEC Montréal (2005) with major in General Management & Strategic HR, in addition to a Master's degree in International Business Administration with Major in Marketing from the ESIAE Paris.

Additional information

Philippe is fully trilingual in English, Spanish and French. In Kuwait in 2006 he was elected Director of the French Business Council (FBC-K) and nominated for 3 years French Foreign Trade Advisor (CCEF) by the French Authorities. He is International Representative of the HEC Montreal International Alumni Association in the Middle East.
