Dragan Stiglic’s Summary

Justified Ancient of CG: From 1984 - creates films, videos, and games using computers. Academy-grown film and animation director with experience in over 300 short commercial projects, 5 feature films and several multimedia and game projects. Co-inventor of virtual studio technology principles (realtime combination of live action and CG via synchronized cameras) and Depth Illusion Digital Imaging (googles-free/hardware-free three-dimensional motion pictures). Currently focused on convergence concepts - unique packagings of content with innovative technologies. Experiments with content of instant and universal deliverability - from film to internet. Exclusive expertize in visual perception and storytelling.

Dragan Stiglic’s Specialties:

Directing live action, animation and vfx. Creation of concepts, storyboards, and advanced 3D previs. On-hands knowledge of each and every filmaking production stage - from storyboard drawing to editing/compositing.
