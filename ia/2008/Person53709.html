Doug Edwards’s Experience

  • Senior Partner

    Fenix Group International

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    July 1992 — Present (16 years)

    Doug established Fenix Group International after more than thirty years of corporate and consulting experience. For twenty years he held executive management positions in the retail, third party logistics, and manufacturing industries; Director through Executive Vice President. Since 1992 Doug has provided executive recruiting and business consulting services to major companies nationally and internationally.

    Doug received his Bachelor’s degree from Guilford College, Greensboro, NC, and is a graduate of the Lazarus Management Institute - Ohio State University/Federated Department Stores. He has published numerous articles and is a frequent speaker at national and international conferences and trade association meetings. Doug specializes in behavioral and skill assessment recruiting as well as skill and behavioral modeling. He is also the author of Skill and Behavioral Modeling for Selecting and Hiring the Best Talent ©.
