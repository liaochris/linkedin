Jim Selby’s Summary

As an industry entrepreneur and pioneer WISP, Jim Selby specializes in advanced wireless broadband technologies such as Municipal Wi-Fi, 802.11 evolution and WiMAX technologies with a focus on business strategy, technology architecture, product development and service delivery.

Recent accomplishments include the successful sale, engineering and deployment of the Town of Vail Wi-Fi network, where Selby was selected for the RFP response over notable major players such as AT&T, Qwest, Nortel and others.

Selby laid the foundation for the wireless architecture and infrastructure involved in CenturyTel's Municipal Wi-Fi, National Hotspot Network and choosing their Point to Point technologies.

Prior accomplishments include Co-Founder and CTO of the first National BWIA broadband network. Establishing a successful wireless distribution startup, producing millions of dollars in equipment sales. Jim was also founder of a mesh startup using his expertise in product development and manufacturing methodologies.

Jim helps start-ups and experienced service providers alike to decide on business strategy, technology architecture and services. Always pushing the envelope with licensed exempt frequency, Jim was called in to protect Homeland Security at events like the Presidential Inauguration with this once considered rogue technology.

Jim Selby’s Specialties:

Highly specialized, business and technology-neutral consulting for Telcos on BWIA, Wi-Fi and WiMAX technologies. Technology innovation and development for product and system architecture. Expert in licensed exempt wireless.
