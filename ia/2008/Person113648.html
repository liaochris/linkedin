Lilly Irani’s Summary

MS in Computer Science, Human Computer Interaction.

I have professional experience in user experience design at Google, ranging from conceptualization to detailed design and implementation, doing user research, rapid prototyping, wireframing, feature specifications, and some visual design. I was the lead (sole) designer for full applications Google Page Creator and Web History. I also worked extensively on Google Notebook and AdWords.

Prior to my design work and MS, I received a BS Computer Science. I had several internships as a software engineer, doing work ranging from Java applications development to semi-conductor systems engineering.

While at Stanford, I founded the Women in Computer Science organization, a group that organizes mentoring, a lecture series, and community activities for both women and men in engineering. We specifically tried to increase awareness of issues affecting minority groups in CS. I did a thesis exploring the reasons why women drop out of the introductory courses of the CS major at Stanford.

Lilly Irani’s Specialties:

user interface design, design research, online communities, human computer interaction, computer science, teaching, sociology of gender
