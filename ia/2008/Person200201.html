Bill Frank founded CareerLab in 1978 and serves as President/CEO. He has consulted to the managers and executives of more than 325 brand-name corporations, including AT&T, Coca Cola, HBO, Janus, KPMG, Lockheed Martin, Pentax, Schlumberger, TRW, and Village Inn.

SERVICE OFFERINGS
=================
Bill's work is typically focused at the middle and upper levels of an enterprise, including the CEO and senior management team. His industry-leading services to business enterprises include:

1. Executive Coaching for the CEO, senior leadership team, sales executives and high potentials (HIPOs). Even the best can be better. A partial list of executive coaching clients is at http://www.careerlab.com/billfrank.pdf.

2. Performance Improvement or turnaround coaching using using The Birkman Method, 360° evaluations, and Gallup assessments.

3. Executive Conflict Resolution – A framework to guarantee level-headed discussion.

4. Human Resources Audit - A high-level, strategic review of your systems, processes, procedures, infrastructure.

5. Leadership Retreats – As facilitators, we’re masters at focusing and aligning your team.

6. Difficult Outplacement – Protection from litigation. "Firing without firing."

Bill writes a monthly column on leadership for The Denver Business Journal [see past columns at www.careerlab.com/free-hr.htm]. He's the author of several best selling career books. Bill has also written for business publications and major market newspapers nationwide ranging from The Wall Street Journal's "National Business Employment Weekly," and Forbes.com to The Denver Post and Rocky Mountain News, Milwaukee Journal Sentinel and Atlanta Journal-Constitution.
