Richard Mudrinich’s Summary

I graduated from the Carnegie Mellon Tepper School of Business (MBA) and Penn State (Bachelor of Science - Chemical Engineering). I worked in industrial sales and business development in the chemical and oil industries for about five years. After graduate business school, I worked on Wall Street in New York City for about ten years in various areas, including fixed income, private client services, and venture investments. Also, I have a natural passion for entrepreneurship and small business, with a special interest in venture capital. I really appreciate the role that venture capital and small business plays in economic development and innovation. In addition, I am a yard safety advocate.

I strive to win the Nobel Prize. I work tirelessly to communicate the yard safety awareness message. For instance, Rescue Rick the Grass Cut Man has made television appearances on ABC, CBS, FOX, and NBC; has been featured in the Associated Press, Akron Beacon Journal, Centre Daily Times, Cleveland Plain Dealer, Columbus Dispatch, Daily Collegian, Detroit Free Press, Erie Times-News, Greenville Record Argus, Herald, Los Angeles Times, New Castle News, Philadelphia Inquirer, New York Post, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, Scranton Times Tribune, United Press International, USA Today, Washington Post, Youngstown Vindicator, etc., and has made radio appearances on Radio America, Monster Radio, WYSP 94.1 FM in Philadelphia and WRKZ 93.7 FM in Pittsburgh "The Kidd Chris Show"; Ocean 98 "Bulldog & the Rude Awakening" (WOCM) in Ocean City, Maryland; 98.9 KQRC "Morning Show with Johnny Dare" in Kansas City, Missouri; Z93 WKQZ "Joe & The Poorboy" in Saginaw, Michigan; SPIN 103.8 "SPIN Talk with Jack & Ali" in Dublin, Ireland, and WPLR 99.1 ("Connecticut's #1 Rock Station") "Chaz and AJ" radio show. In addition, Rescue Rick the Grass Cut Man has been published extensively on various blogs and websites. In short, Rescue Rick the Grass Cut Man is here today so that yard accidents go away!
