Camille Sobrian’s Summary

NPR interview on These Days with Tom Fudge - Creating New Energy Solutions & Green Collar Jobs: http://www.kpbs.org/radio/these_days;id=11574#

Camille has 22 years of management experience primarily in marketing and new technology. Prior to CONNECT she was with the private equity firm Equastone. Previous to that she was with Procopio, Cory, Hargreaves & Savitch LLP for four years and was Managing Director of Procopio Business Consulting, a subsidiary of the law firm. She was also Managing Director of Sierra Asia, a China market access firm. Before joining Procopio she held the positions of Senior Vice President of Marketing and Public Relations for matthews/mark and Senior Vice President of Stoorza Ziegaus & Metzger, and General Manager of the Agency's advertising division, Third Eye. She joined Stoorza after leading the marketing communications division of the Canadian operations of Transamerica.
She co-chaired and built the Special Interest Group program for CommNexus, co-chaired and helped revitalize the CONNECT Springboard program which provides free mentoring for life sciences & high tech companies in all stages of development. She also co-founded the Wireless-Life Sciences Alliance to bring business, medical and scientific leaders together to accelerate the convergence of wireless technologies, life sciences and consumers.
