  • Founding Partner

    (Partnership; 11-50 employees; Design industry)

    My business cards says that I do "code, stills and trivia". I am the person that gets the questions like "Does ultrasound kill people? Can we use it in a project? How much does a video projector cost? How do I spell Linux?"

  • PhD Student

    Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna

    (Educational Institution; 1001-5000 employees; Computer Software industry)

    2005 — Present (3 years)

    In the PERCRO (www.percro.org) laboratory of Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna I am working on a VR thesis. The title is "Beyond rectangles: an investigation of the effects of wide field projection of machine-generated images in virtual reality"

  • Professor

    Interaction Design Institute Ivrea

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Higher Education industry)

    2001 — 2005 (4 years)

    I was responsible for part of the technology education component of Interaction Design Institute Ivrea. In this position, I have taught for iterations of course that was more or less "Programming for non-programming designers".

    I have been a thesis advisor for several students, and I have worked on commercial design projects.

    During my time at Ivrea I have also written a book, the "Interaction Design Primer", together with Britta Boland and Stefano Mirti.
