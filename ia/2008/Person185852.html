Taulbee Jackson’s Summary

Taulbee Jackson brings over a decade of media and marketing experience to his current company, boost media : entertainment. His unique blend of expertise in brand development, digital strategy, traditional, online, and nontraditional media planning, and integrated marketing brings a well-rounded, consumer focused perspective to bear on his client's business situations.

Taulbee is a recent alumnus of one of Indianapolis’ premier marketing strategy firms, Meyer and Wallis. Taulbee led the firm’s efforts on several groundbreaking marketing campaigns as Account Supervisor on some of the agency’s largest accounts, including Marsh Supermarkets, the Indianapolis Museum of Art, and Indiana Blood Center. He also served as Interactive Strategist for Community Health Network, Centex Homes, Palermo’s Pizza, and Wisconsin Conservatory of Music.

Taulbee Jackson’s Specialties:

Brand strategy development, consumer insight, integrated campaign strategy, planning and execution, experience in all facets of digital marketing.
