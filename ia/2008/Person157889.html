Richard A. Deryckere’s Summary

I currently work for Norwegian Air Shuttle which is Scandinavia's Largest Low Fare Carrier and one of Europe's leading low fare airlines. We are currently the fastest growing airline in the world and the 4th largest Low Fare Carrier in Europe. Our fleet consists of 22 Boeing 737-300's and 8 MD-80's which we acquired after the buying of the Swedish Low Fare Carrier Fly Nordic. Norwegian has also ordered 53 Boeing 737-800 with Winglets with first delivery expected January 2008. Norwegian has a total of 1250 employees with 6 bases. We have a total of 122 routes to 75 destinations. Norwegian is expecting 8-9 million customers for 2008.

I have added the Website as well as the Route Map to Norwegian Air Shuttle in the Links below if you would like to know more about the company or where Norwegian Operates.

My background in the Airline Industry started in the summer of 1988. I have experience and working knowledge in Flight Operations / Crew Planning / Fleet Scheduling / Ground Services / Slot Management / Public Relations / Traffic Rights and other areas related to the planning perspective.
