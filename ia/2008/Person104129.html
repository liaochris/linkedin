Keisha Longwell’s Summary

My passion is Data Entry and Research. After working on the research and entering of data on a genealogical project, I decided I wanted to work professionally as a Virtual Assistant.

My services as a Virtual Assistant:
Keying handwritten documents for print, fax, email or postal/parcel delivery
Delivery of documents (as above, via mail, email or parcel services)
Researching information by whatever means necessary
Filtering email, mail and faxes by priority
Schedule appointments, meetings and events
Make travel arrangements and reservations
Proofreading and copy editing
Act as your reminder service
Billing and bill paying

I would also be available to assist in coordinating web design and/or web hosting, and blogs. All of this is, of course, negotiable and based upon your needs.

All this I can do from my home and be available to visit the office as needed. I have a personal computer and a laptop for work portability, as well as a high speed internet connection, color printer/copier/fax machine and scanner. I also have cell phone service, email and instant messenger services for availability, questions or just to keep updated on the hours and work being done.

I can assure you a 40 hour work week for a single flat weekly rate. You would be hiring me as an independent contractor. If, for any reason, there is a need to work over 40 hours or on holidays, I am available for an additional fee.
