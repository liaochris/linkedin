Kim Phillips Sasso’s Summary

I currently co-own a successful restaurant, directing all aspects of marketing and branding. In November of 2007, I resigned from my position as Manager of Marketing and Communications with CTR Professionals, in order to focus on the restaruant and cultivate freelance writing and consulting opportunities.

Outside of these core professional activities, I have founded an organization focused on civic engagement, heritage preservation and community development, and developed a community website serving my small west Georgia town.
