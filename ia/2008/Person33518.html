Charlene Li’s Summary

Charlene is an industry analyst who interprets current media and marketing trends so that businesses can take advantage of opportunities and protect themselves from pending threats. Charlene brings to the analysis not only first-hand experience in media, but also strong strategy and business training and experience. She's not afraid to tell businesses what they should do, but will always do so in a respectful, cooperative manner.

Charlene Li’s Specialties:

Business strategy and market positioning, trend analysis, market sizing, Web site design, email design, media strategies, writing and synthesis of ideas. Coverage topics include: social computing (blogs, RSS, podcasting, and social networking), consumer portals and search, media site design, local media, and online classifieds.
