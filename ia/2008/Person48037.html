Dawn Bearden’s Summary

I grew up in South Jersey and went to the Pennsylvania College of Optometry (PCO) in Philadelphia. After Optometry school I went to Northeastern State University (NSU) in Oklahoma for my post-doctorate residency.

In Oklahoma an Optometrist can practice at the HIGHEST level possible as an Optometrist -that is why I chose to do my residency there.....I wanted the kind of solid educational background that my patients can depend on and trust.

After my residency, I was recruited to South Florida in 1996 to direct two Laser Vision Centers because of my training in PRK and LASIK (refractive surgery).

After my experience there, I bought my first office located next to Lenscrafters in 1997 in Pembroke Pines. It is here where I started the creation of Visionary Eyecare.

Around this time I also became a part-time adjunct faculty member at Nova University College of Optometry and also became one of the examiners for the National Board of Examiners in Optometry.

I have always enjoyed providing the highest quality, thorough eye examination possible. I invested heavily in state-of-the-art equipment and technology to provide the most comprehensive and acurate eye exam possible.

I often heard patients say "Wow, this is the MOST thorough eye exam I've ever had! I've never had an eye exam like this before!!".

I do believe that we all want PRECISE vision....and this begins with a state-of-the art, high-tech eye health and vision evaluation.

Several years later, I opened my second Visionary Eyecare next to Lenscrafters in the Sawgrass Mall and in 2007 I opened my third Visionary Eyecare in Davie next to Pearle Vision.

All 3 offices provide innovative high-tech eye exams for ages 4 and up, contact lens exams, glaucoma, cataract and retinal evaluations, Laser vision evaluations and Emergency Eye Care.
