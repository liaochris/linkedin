Gaia Morelli’s Summary

My latest post has been within RAI, the Italian national broadcasting company. I was assistant to the editor in chief, with responsibility for the production of content for the all-news channel RAInews24.
I was specifically appointed to produce and overview the content of the afternoon magazines. I have been supervising the editing of 25-minute long pieces aired in the 24hrs and assisted journalists with documentation, in particular internet research.
I previously worked with a small independent film production group in Italy (www.spaceoff.it). There I was involved, together with my design company, in the production of a 12-episode miniseries, aired on FOX Channels Italy-Sky. At the same time, I was freelancing as a web designer (HTML) and graphic design producer.
I am used to work cross-platforms and have a good sense of timing and timeline management. I am familiar with the editing and cutting of content and adaptation to satisfy a given concept or line of production guideline.
As an art and design graduate I am familiar with the creative process and are prepared to apply it to any multimedia situation. As a natural mediator I like to see many different identities come together and act for a common goal.
