I love writing and the power of words! My clients say my enthusiasm energizes them for the job search ahead and my bold, razor-sharp, brand-driven writing makes them come alive on the page.

I’m jazzed by helping forward-thinking executives and leaders capture their next great career opportunity. I know how to draw out and showcase their unique brand attributes and promise of value in a striking first impression of them that really turns heads.
