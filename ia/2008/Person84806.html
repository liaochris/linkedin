Jason Felix’s Summary

I have over 13 years of working experiece in the entertainment industry creating everything from concept art, illustrations, 3d animation, 3d models, and cinematic direction.

I've worked on high profile video games such as StarCraft:Ghost and Prince of Persia 3d. Currently working on Hellgate:London creating concept art for the character suits, some creature designs, 3d assets, and animation.

My goal is to visually create the most compelling and unique visuals.

Jason Felix’s Specialties:

Concept art of characters & creatures design, illustrations, 3d animation, and cinematic direction.
