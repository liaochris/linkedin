Gray Rothkopf’s Summary

Management, marketing, sales experience with practiced, hands-on IT skills.

Started a computer consulting business in 1992 specializing in Apple Computers. Company developed an ISP in 1995 specializing in serving businesses in the Chicago area. Sold the ISP in 2002.

Joined Apple Computer, Inc. to help open the flagship North Michigan Avenue store, managed the Service Business, its Genius Bar, and co-managed the sales floor for almost two years. Learned so much from the great team at Apple Retail, made good friends and gained insight into one of the best companies in the world.

Initiated radio show representing Apple on WGN radio, AM 720, 4 hours/week, answering tech call-ins. Left Apple at the end of 2004 to start a company specializing in consumer and business consulting about Apple technologies, named OnDeckTech. Still represent the Mac-side as a weekly guest on the radio show, Website Wednesday Nights with Steve and Johnnie, from 11PM until 3AM (!!) CST.

Have written for blogs, various websites, forums, and articles, including promotional pieces for the Apple website. Having started deploying Zimbra early in 2006, founded 01.com in August of 2007 to focus on the growth of the open-source-based Zimbra Collaboration Suite (ZCS), delivering both Zimbra hosting (SaaS) and professional services.

Apple Certified Systems Administrator (ACSA, 10.3 & 10.4, Network Security and Directory Services). Cisco certified network associate (CCNA), Comptia Network +.
