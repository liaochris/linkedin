Jason McDowall’s Summary

Hi. Thanks for taking the time to review my profile. In short, I'm a software development executive with startup experience and a strong history of innovating to where the market is going to be, building strong teams, and enhancing executive team thinking and execution.

Most recently, I was the VP of Product Development for an exciting startup in the Business Analytics field. We worked with leading business experts at Stanford, Wharton, UCLA, etc. to create purpose-specific analytics tools in the areas of finance and marketing. Our goal was to deliver easy-to-use, web-based tools to improve company productivity and performance.

Previously, I was the co-founder and CEO of Adhoc Mobile, where I led the strategy, product, and business development for a flexible, white-label mobile advertising platform. I also co-founded and served as the CEO of Tomo Software, a software company focused on developing a sophisticated mobile phone content delivery and virtual world platform.

Prior to these entrepreneurial endeavors, I was a Captain in the US Air Force serving as a project manager for $50M to $150M computer and communication system projects. I hold an MBA from UCLA as well as a BS and MS from Carnegie Mellon University.

Currently, I'm a Sr. Product Manager for Salesforce.com. On the side I am consulting and doing some market research on a new business idea in the mobile advertising space.
