Hessel Visser’s Summary

Hessel is very practical consultant with a long time experience in Operations Management and Logistics. At this moment he is very active in Lean projects. His work is his passion. He is very enthusiastic, optimistic and helpful which lead to direct profitable solutions with a strategic scope for the future.

Some of his customers call hem their sparring partner. The reason therefore is that
beside the practical attitude he is well informed in the theoretical background. See for that the books he wrote together with prof. dr. Ad R. van Goor.

A lot of lecturing work is done for Nyenrode Business University for the Part Time MSc -degree in the field of Operations Management, the MBA education of NCOI, the VU in Amsterdam and the NHTV in Breda and the Executive Masters in Logistics (EMLog) which is a cooperation of TIAS Tilburg and the vLm.
Beside his work is Hessel an active member in the board of the Dutch Association for Logistics Management (vLm= vereniging Logistiek management).

Hessel Visser’s Specialties:

Operations Management in industrial environments, Logistics, Purchasing and Supply Chain Management. Beside that he is author of the bestseller "Werken met logistiek" (over 100,000 copies sold). This book is written together with prof. dr. Ad van Goor. In 2006 an English translation has been introduced: "Logistics Principles and Practice". See also www.logisticsprinciplesandpractice.wolters.nl. In 2007 a Dutch book appeared: "Beginnen met logistiek" See for all books: www.logistiek.wolters.nl
