Norman Sigler’s Summary

Experienced Seattle-based executive search consultant for Director level and above executives in green jobs, the financial services, healthcare, and life sciences areas. If you are a hiring manager with a need fitting the description above, please do not hesitate to contact me at norman@normansigler.com.
