Natalie Friton’s Summary

I aim to combine my personal interests with a rewarding and challenging career path that offers the opportunity for growth and leadership. I found these opportunities for almost five years at PassageMaker Magazine where I was involved in all aspects of publishing from editorial to production to budget management to circulation and gained a subject matter expertise of the trawler niche within the marine industry.

Currently I am project managing the development of a new website for DeFever Yachts International as well as managing the collection of materials from consumers and industry members to tell the story, career, and design history of naval architect Arthur DeFever.

I am involved in a freelance capacity with marketing and public relations materials for other top end boat builders in addition to providing copy writing services for Chesapeake ProCon, a search engine optimization, design, and web marketing company.
