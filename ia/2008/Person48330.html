David Wolgemuth’s Summary

I graduated March 31 st 2006 from Full Sail Real World Education, With a Science Degree in Computer Animation. At Full Sail my concentration was on Compositing and Scene finishing, with a secondary concentration on organic, and hard surface modeling. After completed a three-month Internship for Full Sail under the Course Director Frank Fieser I began working for Full Sail as a Lead Lab Specialist for Compositing and Scene Finishing.

Using my knowledge of compositing and scene finishing I strive to push the envelope and create complex and creative composites in a timely manner. I would be a great asset for your company because I am hard working, dedicated to my trade, love working in team environments. I am familiar with the inner workings of a studio environment and feel confident that I would be a valuable addition to the team.
