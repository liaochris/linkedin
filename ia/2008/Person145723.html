Paloma Figueroa’s Summary

Paloma graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in May 2006 with a B.A in Psychology and substantial coursework in Creative Writing. During her final year at Carnegie Mellon, she worked as a consultant to the CEO of Entermedia Corporation, working on market research and promotions. She has also worked as an event planner for Carnegie Mellon events and programs and as an editorial assistant at the New York Daily News Features Section.

Shortly after her graduation she interned with the Membership and Development Department of WQED, volunteered, and worked on starting a Pittsburgh chapter of The Lunch Club, a social networking group with chapters around the world.

In November of 2006 she was offered a job as Marketing Coordinator at Vivisimo, Inc, an Enterprise Search company based in Pittsburgh, PA. For the next year she spent her time coordinating trade shows, planning Vivisimo events, supporting the sales team through lead generation campaigns, and delving deeper into the field of online and email marketing. After an incredible year, she decided to move on to explore the numerous opportunities available to her because of the experiences she has gained from being a part of a top notch and collaborative marketing team.

UPDATE: As of late March, Paloma will start a new job as a Production Coordinator at an international think tank in New York City.

Her main passions include cooking for loved ones, reading ferociously, traveling whenever she can, and saving the world.

In the future, Paloma would like move into the non-profit sector, return to graduate school at least part time, become more involved with the Pittsburgh community, and, one day, own her own business.
