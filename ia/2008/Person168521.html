Sandip Jadhav’s Experience

  • Co-Founder and CEO

    Centre for Computational Technologies (CCTech), Pune

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Software industry)

    April 2006 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    CEO & co-founder of Centre for Computational Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (CCTech) started with IIT Bombay colleagues in April 06 [www.cctech.co.in]. CCTech is operational in CAD,CFD and PLM domain in the area of software developments, consultancy and training.

    Current Product Developement projects:
    DiscreteWorld: A Feature base CAE Preprocessor
    DiscreteSimulator: 3D NC program simulator
    DiscreteViewer: CAD Viewer for mobile application
    DiscreteEngine: Web base 3D model search engine

    Current Add-in developement projects:
    FeatureTech - geometric feature recogniser
    FacetTech - 3D faceted boolean toolkit
    PDF3DTech - 3D PDF generator
    TransTech - CAD data translator
    RepairTech - CAD repair toolkit

  • Senior Software Developer

    SolidWorks, 3D PLM Software, Pune

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Computer Software industry)

    July 2005 — March 2006 (9 months)

    Part Modeling Group, SolidWorks (India)
    I have mainly worked on feature base solid modeler development using Parasolid B-Rep geometric kernel. Worked on some of the advanced and complex features such as shell, surface extend and surface knitting. Gained immense knowledge about CAD product architecture and software development at SolidWorks.

  • Group Leader (Geometry - Grid Generation)

    IITZeus, IIT Bombay - Zeus Numerix

    (Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Research industry)

    July 2000 — July 2005 (5 years 1 month)

    Co-architect of two commercial CFD products, CFDExpert and CFDTutor. During my tenure of more than five years with IITZeus contributed in the various areas of geometry, CAD, grid and CFD development. My major contribution was towards NURBS base Kernel development for geometric modeling and grid generation. Developed CAD interfaces and repairing tools which form bridge for automatic grid generation from imported CAD Models.

    I also become a co-founder of CFD product and consultancy company, Zeus Numerix Pvt. Ltd. [ www.zeusnumerix.com]. Zeus Numerix was started with IITZeus colleagues and professors, now a major CFD product consultancy company in India.

    Development projects in -
    Geometric modeling, grid generation, computational geometry and computer graphics

    Consultancy clients worked with -
    General Motors, Detroit, USA
    Mahindra and Mahindra, Mumbai
    HAL, Bangalore
    DRDO, Hyderabad
