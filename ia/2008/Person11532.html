Andrew Ewing’s Summary

With over 17 years experience in the enterprise software arena, Andrew leads Tower Software Emerging Market and Channel sales in EMEA. Prior to that he has held a number of senior roles in EMEA alliance and channel management, as well previous senior technical positions with Open Text, Hummingbird, Novell, Sybase and Oracle Corp. He has focused on developing multimillion dollar high growth solution and industry alliance practices with major technology and consulting organizations across infrastructure technologies, business packaged and applications and latterly enterprise content and document management solutions .

Andrew is a member of several professional and international consulting bodies in the private and not-for profit sector in sales, marketing and alliance strategy and has also founded his own alliance sales and strategy consultancy, The Alliance Forge in the UK. He holds an MBA (Cranfield), Professional Diploma, (Chartered Institute of Marketing), MSc Information Technology, Certified NLP Practitioner and BSc (Hons) Physics (Loughborough).

Andrew Ewing’s Specialties:

Demonstrable experience of defining and leading innovative market strategies to achieve rapid revenue growth, sales & marketing programs and business development in enterprise solutions, emerging technology and consulting services in the EMEA market.
Comprehensive knowledge of alliance strategies and best practices. Accustomed to operating in demanding and goal orientated environments.Background as a senior technical analyst supports broad solution and cross sector market knowledge.
