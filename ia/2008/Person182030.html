Steven Ericsson-Zenith’s Summary

In 2002, after many years in industry, I returned to research and development, to science. I continue to apply the results of that effort to problems I know well in high technology.

IASE now has its 501(c)3 determination (July 2007) and I am fundraising and recruiting for this project/obsession.

The IASE program of inquiry is focused on the explanation of experience in nature, the foundations of logic and apprehension. In addition, we explore the development of advanced technology that exploits successful theories. We are solving the recognition problems and identifying new solutions to sensory differentiation and motility.

The IASE goal is to establish a world-class international institute of scholarship, science and engineering focused on the explanation of experience in nature. Our scientific mission is to integrate a theory of sentience with other theories of nature and to develop technologies based on successful theories.

I am available to consult and advise on projects that can benefit from my expertise and creativity.
