Hein van der Wielen’s Summary

Hi, thanks for checking out my profile.

I am an all round online marketer (1974) located in The Netherlands. I have been around on the Internet since the mid 90's. The things I love most are (except for my girlfriend) the Internet, traveling and music. That's probably why in '98 I started my first (small) company focused on Internet and music: VanderWielen Productions.

I focus on optimizing e-commerce sites, improving your online advertising campaigns and optimizing online commercial processes.

My specialisms are:
1. Search, both Google Adwords (I am a Google Adwords professional tm) as well as search engine optimization (or SEO in short).
2. ROI effective online bannering based on post click behavior.
3. Behavioral targeting.
4. Web analytics (HBX - Hitbox by Omniture and Google Analytics).
5. Managing or being part of a successful online marketing team.
6. Online media buying.
7. Online marketing and web development in general.

Together with my 35+ buddies at Blue Mango Interactive (Eindhoven, Holland) we offer online marketing knowledge and work force for companies looking for online successes. We do this for a small group of big clients such as Vodafone.nl, Ditzo.nl (Fortis) and Typhone.nl. Next to the larger clients we also offer services for smaller companies who want to have a larger online presence.

Our specialists have extensive knowledge and real hands on experience. Blue Mango services include a.o. search marketing (SEO/SEA), website optimization, banner advertising, affiliate marketing, mail marketing, graphical design as well as flash, game and web development.

Blue Mango is a knowledge driven company with a focus on ROI driven sales campaigns and successful online branding campaigns. We try to bring the latest technology and top shelve online marketing knowledge to Europe. The best part is that we like what we do since we have a passion for online.
