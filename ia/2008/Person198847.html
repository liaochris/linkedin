Wayne Mallinson’s Summary

My goal is to expand internationally a vibrant software testing consulting, services and training company.

This will include using our UK, offshore Indian, Australasian, and South African software testing and outsourcing capabilities

Chairman of Special Interest Group in Software Testing (SIGiST) of the computer Society of South Africa (CSSA) 2004/2005

Editor in Chief of 'Test Focus' a software testing magazine (2000 to present).

My career began as a mining geologist 1979 through 1988 (Zimbabwe and South Africa).

I have 18 years software/system testing experience and now help companies with test training, test consulting, and services through my company. I have worked in industries spanning(financial services sector (banking/insurance), air traffic control systems (air picture, radar, radios), telecommunications and sundry diverse others.

I currently speak internationally (UK, SA, USA, Australia, New Zealand) at software testing conferences and have ways to help your organisation improve productivity, profit, schedule and quality goals through the application of business and user savvy software testing
