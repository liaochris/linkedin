Randall Villeneuve’s Summary

Biography:
Randall is a VP in Oracle’s Industry Business Unit, leading the Industry Strategy & Insight team in Europe, the Middle East and Africa (EMEA). Randall was given the mandate to recruit and train an EMEA-wide team of business & IT strategists responsibile for supporting Oracle's license business with strategic consulting and C-level business development services. Randall, a results driven strategist and manager, brings more than 15 years of experience in in consulting, sales, business development and marketing.

While at Oracle Randall has worked with business leaders from firms like BT, Michelin, ING, Dell, Intel, GE, Emerson, Motorola, and Tyco, to align business and IT strategies resulting in new initatives driving growth and efficiency gains. Randall has successfully launched and built a team across EMEA seeing growth in services provided expand by more than 100% per annum. Randall as held various positions in Oracle leading vertical sales and marketing teams.

Randall joined Oracle from IBM Business Consulting’s Value Insititute (formerly Mainspring). Randall drove value assessment engagements for leading players in the financial and service sectors. Prior to this Randall was a consultant for Pittiglio Rabin Todd & McGrath (PRTM) where he managed e-business strategy and operations consulting engagements. Before joining PRTM, as a Director at the Mercury Marine division of Brunswick, he helped launch the firms newest business and integrate several acquisitions of competitors. Prior to joining Mercury he held key roles in the manufacturing and the software industry.

Randall holds a MBA with concentrations in finance and economics from the University of Chicago's Graduate School of Business. Randall also holds masters in mechanical and ocean engineering from MIT, and a BS degree from the University of Massachusetts.

He is certified CPIM and licenses Professional Engineer in the USA.
