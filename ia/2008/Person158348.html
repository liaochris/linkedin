Roger Copeland’s Summary

I have been in the IT industry for over 25 years; primarily in senior management roles. However; I am a "hands on" kind of leader that not only believes it is necessary to understand the intricacies of computer technology, but to also be able and willing to develop systems alongside the people employed by one's company and to understand that hierarchy is counterproductive in a team environment, and all organizations are team environments.

My professional goal is to develop software products and services of exemplary quality for the purpose of definitively solving operational and strategic challenges for the customers we serve and for those whom we will serve.

I am of the informed opinion that, as it is with our brains, we are only tapping into about 10% of the full potential of the computer technology that currently exists. This is partially because of the speed at which computer technology is evolving, but it is more a result of a phenomenon known as "cultural latency", which is to say that computer technologies are evolving at a much faster rate than our ability to fully appreciate how we might use those technologies. As it has been for several millennia technology plays a critical role in human evolution. Computer technology is just another step along that path to enabling humans to achieve their full potential. I am grateful to be a contributor to that process of enabling us to be smarter tomorrow than we are today.
