Jay Rao’s Summary

On February 17, 2009, over-the-air digital TV signals will replace the current analog signals across the entire United States. If you are using rabbit ears or an external antenna, your analog TV will no longer work. Most US households are unprepared and lack the equipment for this change. The US Department of Commerce’s NTIA launched a Coupon Program on January 1, 2008 to help ease the digital transition. US households can request up to two discount coupons worth $40.00 each toward the purchase of up to two digital-to-analog converter boxes. The coupons have a 90 day expiration and cannot be reissued. There is a limited supply of these coupons which are distributed on a first come, first serve basis.

I am in the process of establishing ConvertMy.TV to be America’s premier web-based retailer of digital-to-analog converter boxes for the upcoming digital conversion. Through our www.ConvertMy.TV website and our 877-DTV-SITE toll free call center, we will offer consumers the opportunity to request coupons from the NTIA as well as redeem those coupons towards the purchase of our MaxMedia ATSC Converter Box. We are focused on providing the product via national community outreach efforts. These outreach programs are making an impact by providing America with a safe, easy, and quick process to learn about the transition, obtain two government issued $40 discount coupons, purchase Maxmedia converter boxes and have them delivered directly to your doorstep.
