Russell Bennett’s Summary

Russell has nine years of experience in Law enforcement in the Metropolitan Police, UK where he was a Detective specialising in various fields including Covert investigations, Drugs, Sexual offences and Fraud. He then went onto to work for a major risk consultancy company based in South Africa prior to becoming a recruitment consultant within the specialist risk management and security field.
He is currrently an independent security consultant operating across South Africa.


Education/Research:

Russell completed various specialist courses whilst working in Law enforcement in the UK and is a member of the American Society of Industrial Security (ASIS) and the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE). He is also a registered member of PSIRA in South Africa and is a fully certified Close Protection Officer.

Russell Bennett’s Specialties:

Consultancy,Investigations, Surveillance , Risk assessment and Close protection. Russell also has an extensive network within the recruitment industry in South Africa.
