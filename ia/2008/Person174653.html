Shawn Maynard, CPC’s Summary

It’s simple. People are the difference. Today, businesses search for the right people to help build their vision. Companies also need professionals that will help them develop a competitive edge. Delta Dallas has a world-class team who helps companies find those professionals. We are committed to working with only the best. Our standards are high and our testing and screening process is tough. We don’t work with just anyone – we work with only those individuals we would personally hire to work for us. Our goal is to bring employers and employees together for a more productive future. We believe our extra efforts pay off for you.

The Delta Dallas Staffing companies have served the Dallas metroplex for more than 25 years.

Our woman-owned company exceeds staffing percentages for recruiting by more than double the national average. We are proud that the Delta Dallas recruiters represent 18 percent of all the double certified recruiters in the entire state of Texas. Our recruiting team’s tenure averages more than eight years. We can assure you that once you start working with our recruiting team, you won’t find yourself in a revolving door of staffing specialists. You will enjoy the benefit of working with a specialist who knows you, your company and your business.

Delta Dallas focuses on more than staffing.

We want to be a business partner with you and help positively affect your bottom line. As your staffing resource, we want to make your staffing dollars work for you. We conduct workforce audits to ensure your staffing investment provides the return you deserve and want. We offer the solutions you need when you need them.

We have your business covered. We offer administrative, accounting, financial, contact center and customer service staffing solutions. From administrative assistants to executives, we know the people your business needs.
