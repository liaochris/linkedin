Tracey Tee’s Summary

I am the Co-Founder and Vice-President of Delight.com; a daily online shopping experience that merges ecommerce with web 2.0. Delight is a seriously great place to discover unique, insanely useful gifts and treats for yourself.
I am also the former owner of an online / e-commerce boutique and gallery: AuntBeep.com, which showcased Los Angeles designers and artists. I offered hands on consultation to emerging artists & designers and oversaw development of their products from idea to production.
Additionally, I am a freelance writer for several nationally recognized blogs. Areas covered: design/interior design, entertaining, art, media, product reviews.
I also consult & set-up blogs for individuals and businesses wishing to integrate into the growing world of new media.
