Jessica Delfino’s Summary

I have done a lot! But here are some of the basics:

I have performed on Good Morning America, VH1, XM's Opie and Anthony Show, in the Montreal Comedy Festival, I've written for MTV, Comedy Central and Cartoon Network, I've toured the country with the Trachtenburg Family Slideshow Players, I've been both nominated and have won several comedy, writing and music awards, I write an award-winning blog, I've created "Delfino's Diatribe", a weekly comedy newsletter which was published on NYPRESS.COM and went out to over 1000 media and performer contacts each week.

My performances and my first CD, "Dirty Folk Rock", have been highly acclaimed and reviewed by JANE Magazine, The NY Daily News, The NY Post, The Village Voice, High Times, Arthur Magazine, The Washington Post, The Onion, Time Out NY, Backstage.com, ComedyCentral.Com, Fleshbot.com, Gawker.com, and many more.

I have produced many comedy shows and short videos, I perform nightly in NYC, and have an extensive, in-depth knowledge and expansive network of contacts within the NYC performing arts, music and comedy scenes.

My goal is to tour the world and become more famous than Bon Jovi.

  • Campaign Manager

    Angry Taxpayers for CXB

    (Non-Profit; Political Organization industry)

    January 2004 — November 2004 (11 months)

    I managed the mayoral campaign of Christopher X. Brodeur, NYC fringe political candidate of note. His platform included some radically logical ideas such as "free subways" and "contractual responsibility for politicians". Christopher's campaign broke all dollar per vote records last fall when he acquired about 4% (17,000) votes using a technique that relied mostly on internet technology.
