Leah Jewel Alexander’s Summary

My career has spanned from Teacher to Reading Specialist, to Director of Prevention/ Intervention Programs (Youth and Family Counseling, Specializing in Substance Abuse), to Playwright/ Author/ Columnist, (ongoing descriptions), to TV Talk Show Host, to what I'm doing now.

My educational degrees are as follows: B.S./ Elem. Ed., MA/ Communications, MA/ Spec. Reading, MA/ Business, Human Resources Training and Development.

The last few years, I've been working as an Actor, Model, and Comedienne, doing a wealth of voice-overs, commercials, four movies, several radio and TV commercials, infomercials, industrial films, etc. To date, I have acted in "The Divas of Comedy" (original Stand-up Comedy), "Ghost Image," "Alice," and recently wrapped my first feature film in which I had a starring role, "Em," produced by Matriarch Motion Pictures. I continue to pursue this passion of acting, modeling, and performing as a Stand-up Comic and loving it!

Currently, when I'm not performing, I work as a professional Motivational Speaker and Personal and Professional Coach, in addition to writing articles for various Internet magazines and Blogs. This is the ongoing work that I have done throughout my career.

Please feel free to connect with me for your advertising and publishing needs, including digital publishing, Blog/ Website Building, Search Engine Optimization services, Motivational Speaking, Event Hosting, Personal and/ or Professional Coaching services or if you need a Stand-up Comedienne for your next event.
