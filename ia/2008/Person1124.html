Aaron Zide’s Summary

I am currently Jr. Art Director at The Skiver Group, an ad agency in Irvine, Ca. Where I have thrived for well over one and half years (check out our newly redesigned site @ www.theskivergroup.com.) I started out as a production artist, with 6 months experience in the industry, and 2 years of freelancing for myself and individual clients as well as for, True Parallel Marketing firm, based in Newport Beach. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Design, where I worked on projects such as the quarterly school newsletter, specialty group needs and non-profits marketing and advertising materials.

One of my many goals is to become an established Art Director at a prestigious studio or agency by the young age of 27. And by the end of my career to own and operate an agency or studio to the point of being purchased by a desirable competitor. As well I'd love to experience, travel and enjoy the little blue and green marble that is so large and yet so small.
