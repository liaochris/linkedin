Brian Batchelder -- (brian.batchelder@fleishman.com)’s Summary

Brian Batchelder
Senior Manager, Talent Development
Fleishman-Hillard (Washington D.C.)

I am an in-house recruiter for Fleishman-Hillard’s Washington D.C. office. Fleishman-Hillard is a global public relations firm that also as an award-winning, full-service digital team. I also support two other DC-based Fleishman-Hillard companies: Strat@comm and VOX Global Mandate.

I proactively source, network and cold call candidates for a range of openings: public relations, public affairs, web developers, web designers, print designers, project managers, bloggers, social networking experts, OEO, SEO, SEM folks... pretty much anything under the digital umbrella.

*** Please email me at brian.batchelder@fleishman.com if you are interested in networking or employment opportunities with Fleishman-Hillard, Strat@comm or VOX Global Mandate ***

I previously worked with Fleishman-Hillard’s Boston office for four years as a public relations professional. In 2001, I was presented with Fleishman-Hillard's "Team Player of the Year Award" at the firm's worldwide conference.

Between my two stints at Fleishman-Hillard, I worked at American University in Washington, D.C. as the assistant director, employer outreach. In this hybrid role, I provided career coaching to undergraduate students and also led aggressive outreach to high-profile, private sector companies. In two years, I placed more than 30 students in jobs and internships at top firms, including Adidas, GE, Genworth Financial, Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan Chase, and Smith Barney.

I began my professional career with the New England Patriots’ media relations department.

I graduated from Syracuse University’s S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications with a degree in broadcast journalism. I'm a diehard fan of Syracuse sports, the Boston Red Sox and New England Patriots. The Celtics are pretty good this year too!
