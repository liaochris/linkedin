Why I’m on LinkedIn:

1.) To provide outstanding executive search services to the leaders of Architecture, Engineering, and Construction companies doing business with federal, state, and local governemnt clients and industrial/commercial firms worldwide who desire to recruit, hire, develop, and retain world-class professionals. My focus is in working with professionals with Environmental Science and Engineering, Brownfield Re-Development, Design/Build/Construct, Green Buildings, Alternative Energy, Fuels Management, and Facilities Management sub-niches.

2.) To do the same for the leaders of Civil Engineering firms in the Rocky Mountain and Southwestern regions of the U.S.

3.) To build successful career-long relationships with executives, managers, business development, and technical professionals in the above industries that are interested in keeping abreast of wise career moves that will assist them in accomplishing their professional and personal goals.

4.) To collaborate with other search consultants both in closely aligned and diverse industries to develop long-term, mutually successful, partnerships in which we support each other in filling our key clients’ search assignments with world-class professionals.

5.) To develop strategic alliance partnerships with five to ten professionals in non-competitive, closely aligned industries to the AEC business, for example: commercial real estate companies, developers, specialty contractors, suppliers, equipment/machinery companies, etc.
