Richard Gee’s Summary

I am an attorney licensed in both Colorado and California with substantive law firm experience as well as in-house experience. My practice focuses on business, real property, internet advertising, new media, intellectual property, music, film and television transactions.

I am a native Spanish speaker, and can deliver legal services in both Spanish and English. For the last two decades, I have represented clients throughout the United States as well as Latin America, Asia and Europe with their legal needs.

Please feel free to contact me at info@gee4law.com if you should have any questions or need legal assistance.

Richard Gee’s Specialties:

Business: Corporate formations and dissolutions, including LLC’s, partnerships and limited partnerships; mergers, acquisitions and asset sales; fiduciary disputes.

Real Property: Purchases and sales; commercial leasing; redevelopment; hospitality and resort industry contracts; and land use matters.

IP and Entertainment: Internet advertising, branding, internet, domain name disputes, copyright, trademark, music, film, television, new media, interactive media and UDRP proceedings.
