Lisa Liputra’s Summary

Lisa is a highly dedicated and well respected se consultant, passionate about people, technology, and life-long learning. A proactive, resourceful and self-motivated person with great interpersonal and communication skills -- Lisa has consistently been commended for her work.

She graduated from Central Queensland University top of the class and was consequently presented with the Outstanding Academic Achievement award. Her philosophy of continuous and never-ending learning drives her to always keep up with the latest technology trends and to continuously expand her knowledge.

An MBA degree is her current pursuit. Lisa has a prestigous scholarship from the top-ranking Macquarie Graduate School of Management. Through MGSM Mentoring Program, Lisa has been fortunate to be presented with the opportunity to work with Oscar Trimboli, Director of Unified Communications, Microsoft Australia. Lisa recently established a mentor-mentee relationship Jude Munro, CEO of Brisbane City Council. In Lisa's words, "Jude is an inspiring woman and a visionary leader. I always feel empowered when I speak to Jude."

With the Austereo Group, she demonstrated her passion for people and technology by greatly assisting the business implement systems to solve business problems and to stay ahead of the game. In her capacity as a senior consultant for Avanade, she has consistently performed tremendously well (refer to the Recommendations section and Awards list).

Since joining Avanade in 2007, Lisa has had abundant opportunities to showcase her strengths in customer liaison, relationship building, and helping customers analyse their business requirements relating to the customer's technology investments. In March 2008 (after 10 months with the firm), Lisa was promoted to a senior position.

Lisa's passion for people also drives her to give back to the community through volunteering efforts and pro-bono work. Lisa is a keen networker and is open to requests to get in touch.
