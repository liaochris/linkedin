Marvin R.R. Cole marvin.cole@stratsolutionsgroup.com’s Summary

I am currently the Director of Global Operations at Strategic Solutions , an emerging markets focused consultancy and alternative finance firm. Strategic Solutions is a boutique consulting firm that assists Small and Medium sized businesses in the US, China, and Brazil raise capital from a network of more than 25 institutional investors in addition to providing strategic and operational advisory services.

Prior to joining Strategic Solutions, I was a management consultant with the Strategy and Operations Practice of Deloitte Consulting. A $7 Billion dollar division of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu. I have industry experience in international trade, OTC commodities trading, procurement and marketing in automotive and consumer business industries. Additional areas of experience include strategic and operational planning, data mining and trend analysis, China focused trade financing, negotiations, structuring deals, international sales and marketing, and commodities procurement. I have also been involved with start-ups in the High Tech and Real Estate Industries.
