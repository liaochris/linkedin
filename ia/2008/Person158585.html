Patricia Damon’s Summary

A graduate of The International Dermal Institute in California, I am trained in both medical and ayuredic techniques. My expertise has taken on greater dimension as a result of my work with a leading plastic surgery practice, and as a national educator for a top skin care line in Europe. As an instructor at one of the first accredited esthetic colleges in the United States, I have dedicated my life on educating people about skin care and esthetic techniques.

After realizing the need to provide passengers who fly on board private jets with quality skin care programs, I designed a one-of-a-kind in-flight spa service. My successful training led to the implementation of licensed estheticians to provide luxury facial treatments to elite clients on board a fleet of Gulfstream IVs and Vs. Her accomplishments have been recognized at the Sundance Film Festival and on the Travel Channel, where I designed a program for a leading air charter company. In Park City, Utah, Ms. Damon created and executed a VIP lounge that provided customized facials for celebrities. My enthusiasm and devotion to her skin care business is revealed through her expertise and high-quality performance.

My goal is to provide the best service possible to an elite and distinguished clientele.
