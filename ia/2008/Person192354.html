Pat Neal’s Summary

Helping people reach their full potential is my personal objective. Career highlights include an international posting managing a multi-cultural team, developing and delivering technical and soft-skill training to internal and external customers. A foundational principle behind my success is thinking outside the box. When people succeed, organizations succeed.

I currently serve on the Board of Directors for ECWR - a grass roots volunteer organization, is a nondenominational evangelical resource providing a community of fellowship that is a safe place for gay, lesbian, bisexual and transgendered Christians to reconcile and integrate their faith and sexuality, and to grow toward Christian maturity.
