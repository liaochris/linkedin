Ashley Collins’s Summary

A long-time eCommerce junkie… I’ve worked with website development, programming, web design, etc. since 2001. Under guidance from leaders in their fields (marketing, eCommerce, and other areas of expertise), I evolved from a lowly “catch all” and “air traffic controller” in a budding eCommerce department into a Web Designer, then eCommerce Director for Wayside Gardens and Jackson & Perkins (both of which are hybrid mail-order catalog and eCommerce companies that sell and ship live plants and gifts to customers across the country), and then sole Brand Manager for Wayside Gardens where I guided product selection, the brand’s styling, marketing (both web and print), and much more!. Learning something new every day and helping colleagues and friends grow is my goal.
