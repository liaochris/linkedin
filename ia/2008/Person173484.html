Sophie Godon’s Summary

Bilingual Administrative Professional with specialization in translation and quality assurance. Coordinated translation projects (English/French) from planning phase to completion. Supervised the overall quality of French material distributed to Canadian customers and partners from an external translation vendor's content to that of external advertising agencies, production companies, partners and other subsidiaries. Also involved in product terminology decisions and packaging revision. Undertook public relations work interfacing with the OQLF (the Quebec Government’s French language protection department.)

Excellent interpersonal and communication skills. Reliable and efficient, with over 12 years of successful experience in a fast-paced and ever-changing leading-edge environment.

Extensive volunteering and working background with children, hearing-impaired (Quebec Sign Language) and disabled individuals (across all age groups) and seriously-ill children (mostly Oncology.)
