Curt Fell’s Summary

I have 22+ years of experience, including 9 years in Germany, in the Quality Assurance/Testing field as both Manager and Project Manager of QA. I have extensive experience managing projects and personnel, writing product requirements, developing test procedures and testing complex systems and software. In 1998 I received my certificate as a Certified Software Test Engineer (Quality Assurance Institute in Orlando, Florida).

My broad knowledge and experience in the electronic check processing, payment card industry, telecommunication, computer research & design, publishing and pharmaceutical fields has given me the ability to complete all types of testing on schedule while maintaining the quality and reliability of the product. I have been extensively involved in QA Audits from both internal and external audits (Bellcore, FDA).

I have experience in the following operating systems: MS-DOS, MS-Windows (all versions from 3.1 to Vista), IBM OS/2 2.11, IBM OS/2 Warp 3.0, IBM Warp 3.0 Connect, IBM OS/2 Warp 4.0, MacOS 8.x, MacOS 9.x, SCO OpenDesktop, SCO OpenServer, SCO UnixWare, Novell UnixWare, Sun Interactive Unix, Sun Solaris, QNX, IBM AIX, LynxOS, Hewlett Packard HP-UX, Intel iRMX for PCs, NeXT OS, VMS and Novell NetWare.

Ich bin außerdem auch in der Lage meine Tätigkeiten in der deutschen Sprache zu verrichten da ich seit 8 Jahren fast ausschließlich in Deutsch arbeite.
