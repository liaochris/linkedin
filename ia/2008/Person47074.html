DAVID LISNEK, ACG’s Summary

As an Award-Winning Speaker, Author & Financial Expert, I focus my time and energy on solving problems on both the individual client level, as well as the general public scale.

Sometimes controversial, I routinely write about and discuss topics of personal finance that most commissioned based brokers and insurance agents would rather remain hidden.

Always one to lend a hand, I donate a significant amount of money from my speaking fees, book and DVD sales to local charities.

    (Financial Services industry)

    2004 — Present (4 years)

    After 8-years of working on Wall Street, I made the move to establish my own business as a Fee-Based Financial Planner and Registered Investment Advisor in private practice. My private client base primarily consists of business owners, professionals and retirees who are seeking objective and honest advice that is not motivated by commissions.

  • President

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    April 2004 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    As a professional speaker, I continue to develop and deliver programs on communication skills, personal finance, and motivational workshops.

  • Owner

    North Avenue Publishing

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    April 2004 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    After working on Wall Street for eight years, I retired at the age of 36 and launched my own publishing company eventually releasing my first book "How to Hire & Fire Your Financial Advisor" in 2005.

    Since then, I have come out of retirement and moved my financial planning practice to Troxell Financial Advisors. I continue to work as a free-lance writer with readership in over 90-countries, and will be releasing my next book "All Rolled Into One" in November 2008.
