James Krouse’s Summary

I have worked for over ten years in marketing and communications for industrial, technology, pharmaceutical, and other business-to-business companies. I also have experience working with non-profit organizations. For many of my clients, communication strategy has not been a common practice. Building a communications plan from the ground up and executing that plan is where I thrive and also have a lot of fun. Right now building reltaionships with large and small companies is my goal. Sometime small things lead to big things, but doing a job right, big or small, is the most satisfying thing. There are also new technologies that are very un-technological (if that's a word) that are helping people communicate in a much more effective way. Ask me details and I'll talk you're ear off about it so be prepared.

Writing -- I'm putting this right at the top. People, there is some terrible writing out there. Get a professional to do it.

Communications Strategy -- you need a plan for how to talk to the people who are or could be your custmers. Most people don't have a plan. I can help.

Internet Communications Strategy -- This reall belongs in the bullet above but it's big so I'll seperate it. There are new ways of getting out your message and I can help you do it.

Management -- I used to manage large account teams and an entire office in London. Right now I manage mostly projects or teams of people working on those projects.

There's more, but I'll summarize. There are things that you're good at and your day is filled with things that customers or your boss asks you to do. But there are things that no one is asking for. These are the things that you know would communicate the value of your company and build business, but you never do because no one is asking. I like to help people with most of those things.
