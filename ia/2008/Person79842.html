Imran Ashraf’s Summary

As a MSc graduate of the study Informatics & Economics at the Erasmus University Rotterdam Imran Ashraf completed his studies with a strong academic record and a high exposure to economics and IT.

Imran has shown that he can adapt well under pressure, he simultaneously juggled multiple tasks successfully as shown by managing extensive extra-curricular activities as a final-year student. During his time as chairman of the study-trip committee he has demonstrated his leadership skills by organizing a successful research trip to Ireland for a 20-person group.

His educational background and experiences have taught him how to analyse an environment and make appropriate recommendations to increase a company’s competitive advantage.

His creative way of thinking and compelling presentation skills resulted in a 3rd place in the dutch national finals of the "Imagine Cup", an international Microsoft contest.
