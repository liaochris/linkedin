Karen Wells’s Summary

SalesPartners was founded in 2004 by Blair Singer (Robert Kiyosaki Rich Dad Advisor™), best selling author, and internationally respected sales, leadership, and business development guru. SalesPartners was born from a vision…a vision of improving the quality of life for everyone on the planet through transformation of the marketplace.

I joined the SalesPartners Worldwide team in 2004. I was hand-selected by Blair to be the first SalesPartner, pioneering the testing and development of the franchise, and currently I am the Regional Developer for Northern California and Northern Nevada.

I have worked with local, national, and international businesses to significantly increase their revenue and profitability. Prior to becoming a SalesPartner, I spent my career in the corporate environment of the retail, insurance and legal industries. I was instrumental in developing the marketing division of a large medical-legal consulting firm, enabling them to increase their customer base from Northern California to an international clientele in less than 3 years, with a sales and marketing staff of less than 5 people and a minimal budget.

Using the philosophies and training strategies of world-renowned trainer, Blair Singer, author of the bestselling book, $alesDogs, I have been able to build championship business teams and increase companies’ revenues by up to 600% in less than 6 weeks.

My background in Business Management and Marketing, together with the proven training methods of $alesPartners, have allowed me to help people move from the “S” (self-employed) quadrant to the “B” (business owner) quadrant in order to experience the ultimate of financial growth.

Some clients include Baskin Robbins, Togo's, The UPS Store, General Nutrition Centers (GNC), Lexus of Roseville, PrePaid Legal, World Financial Group, Quest Capital Group, Mary Kay Cosmetics, Skintopia Day Spas, NovaVine, Taylor Renovation, McKinley Company Construction, Wallach Glass Studio and YTB.
