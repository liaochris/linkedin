Sandeep Manudhane’s Summary

I am a proud first-generation entrepreneur based in India.

Having done my Engineering course from the IIT Delhi, and securing final admission calls from all IIMs, I became an education industry enterpreneur. PT education, the company I started in a garage with zero capital on 10th July 1993, is today a pan-India training institution (www.pteducation.com). My experience covers areas like startup management, HR, talent pool creation and upgradation, actual teaching - counselling - mentoring, student management and brand management. We have also ventured in mainstream business education with the launch of our ambitious Proton Business School (www.Proton.in)

15 years of grassroot level work has finetuned my business instincts and polished my personal skills to a certain degree, and I find it exciting to help others achieve their personal and professional goals. My institution (PT education) has trained more than 2 lac students since 1993, & most of them are now with leading corporations of the world.

I love to teach, motivate, read, travel, watch movies and play cricket.

My personal life-goal is to give back to society much more that what I will have taken from it. My professional goal is to create outstanding world-class education experience for each of my students, always.
