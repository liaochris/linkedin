Michael Beavers’s Summary

My job as a digital marketing and experience strategist is to:

1) Help companies get their heads around business problems and how the Web and various marketing communications disciplines can help

2) Design customer insights research and create frameworks and findings for amazing Internet marketing experiences

3) Interpret research findings and customers/user insights with User Experience, Creative, Engineers, or Production, Project, or Product management professionals who are far better than me at expressing what the experience should be

4) Assure the original strategy is realized with all involved professionals and stakeholders

5) Assure everything is measurable and accountable to the vision

6) Help the company achieve profitability, innovation, and a market position of thought, product and service leadership.

Michael Beavers’s Specialties:

The meta: vision setting, interactive strategy, consumer insights, ethnography, interaction design, information architecture, qualitative research, user-centered design, UED, social media, communities, marketing strategy, brand planning, marketing consulting, creative management, marketing and management consulting, business consulting, operations leadership, interactive consulting, marketing and advertising leadership.
