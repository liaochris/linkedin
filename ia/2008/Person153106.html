Pradeep Chopra’s Summary

Pradeep Chopra is a co-founder of OMLogic Consulting (www.omlogic.com), one of India’s leading online marketing companies. Prior to OMLogic, Pradeep was a co-founder of Whizlabs Software, an IT education company, where he built a global brand using online business model for which Whizlabs won the 'Most Innovative Indian IT Company' award from NASSCOM in 2004.

Online Marketing is Pradeep’s passion and he is among the pioneers in India who started using online marketing in 2000. He is an international speaker and an author on online marketing & entrepreneurship. His previous speaking assignments include speaking at SES, Global Entrepolis @ Singapore, NASSCOM Leadership Forum, TiE Startup Essentials and Web Innovation conferences. He is also an avid blogger and regularly blogs on online marketing at OM Careers, NASSCOM and SEMPO.

Pradeep graduated from IIT Delhi and is also a member of the SEMPO's Global Committee. He is also on the advisory board of TiE’s Internet SIG (Special Interest Group), the voice of Internet in India. He is also the founder and president of OM Careers (www.omcareers.org), a non-profit and world’s first online marketing careers community. He is also the brainchild behind OMCAR 2008, world’s first online marketing careers conference & expo.
