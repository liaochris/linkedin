Kevin Rutherford’s Summary

I am a leading agile software developer with deep experience in software quality, leadership, project automation and analysis. I am a Chartered Engineer and a Charterd IT Professional, with an extensive track record of on-time delivery.

My principal focus these days is in helping teams to exceed their customers' expectations. I do this not by coaching, but simply by setting an example and mentoring anyone who wishes to improve their practices. In my own practice I rely on agile principles, with strong emphasis on risk management, incremental delivery of tangible results and the creation of habitable, defect-free software.

I contribute regularly to the agile software development community. I founded the UK's AgileNorth group and I'm a member of the programme committee for the British Computer Society’s SPA (Software Practice Advancement) annual conference. I am also a leading speaker on the agile conference circuit, and I have run workshops as an invited speaker at numerous events of the British Computer Society and the Agile Alliance.

My blog (http://silkandspinach.net) is consistently ranked among the world's top 25 agile blogs by Technorati.

To find out more about me and how I can help you, or to join my network, email me: kevin@rutherford-software.com

Kevin Rutherford’s Specialties:

Defect-free Software -- test-driven development, object-oriented design, refactoring, beautiful code

Mentoring -- setting the quality, style and pace of software development projects by example

Project Leadership -- leading the development of defect-free software on time and within budget

Project Rescue -- salvaging working software and workable plans from the wreckage

Project Automation -- integrating toolsets to increase developer throughput
