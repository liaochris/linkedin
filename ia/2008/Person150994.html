Philip Morrish’s Summary

I was a leading investment analyst with many global investment banks for 27 years following the chemical and waste management sectors. I was noted for my forthright views on corporate shareholder value creation and destruction.
Thought out my career I have been a strong advocate and very active with the implentation of technology applications to improve investment research production and distribution efficiencies as well as development of new client facing products.
In 2002, I established Cogency Chemical Consultants (www.cogency.biz) with 3 recognised chemical industry industry friends to assist Private Equity and other financial advisers execute chemical industry transactions - acquisitions, exits, buy & builds. Cogency also assists management teamss within larger chemical groups with the structuring and execution of acquisitions as well as their own MBOs.
During 2005, entered the UK pre-IPO and Development Capital fund raising markets through Mackenzie Morrish & Associates; business was incorporated in 2006 and name changed to Mackenzie Morrish limited. This business connects specialist investment funds and high net worth individuals with attractive investing opportunities across all market segments.
During 2006, launched a low cost, fundamental-based independent investment analysis business, Intelligent Analysis Limited (www.intellisys.uk.com) to assist professional investors, experienced traders and high net worth individuals with the timing of UK equity transactions and valuations.
In January 2008, joined the rapidly growing Rivington Street Corporate Finance as Sales & Marketing Director to further develop its placing power as well as increase it corporate client listings.

Philip Morrish’s Specialties:

Financial analysis, mergers and acquisitions, strategic industry and business analysis.
