Justin Leader’s Summary

Justin Leader received a Bachelor of Arts degree in English from Cornell University in 2006 and is currently a candidate for the Master of Arts Management and the Master of Entertainment Industry Management graduate degrees at Carnegie Mellon University’s John H. Heinz III School of Public Policy and Management. Mr. Leader founded the Melodramatics Theatre Company, Inc. in 2004 and subsequently produced Assassins, Into the Woods, Bat Boy, An Evening with Jeff Marx, A New Brain, and Sweeney Todd. He also directed the productions of Assassins and Sweeney Todd and acted in Assassins, Into the Woods and Bat Boy. In 2007, Justin was the Marketing Associate for the Center for Arts Management and Technology. In 2008, he served as the Intern to the Supervising Producer of Carnegie Mellon University’s Entertainment Technology Center, a Development and Technology Intern for Pensé Productions, Marketing Director for the Pittsburgh Human Rights Watch Film Festival and a Marketing Associate for the Silk Screen Film Festival.  Mr. Leader is currently an intern in Fox Broadcasting Company’s Comedy and Drama Development and Current Programming Departments, Fox Reality Channel’s Programming and Development Departments, and is working with Weller/Grossman on their new show “Reality Binge.” He also proud to provide community service as a Graphics and Development Associate for the Non-Profit Organization, Riverspace, founded to revitalize the arts community in his hometown of Nyack, NY.
