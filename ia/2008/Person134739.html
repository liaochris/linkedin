Mohamed Ali’s Summary

I have been working in the teaching, facilitation and training fields since 1997, either on a full-time or freelance basis. I have done the above in very diverse settings, teaching or speaking on a wide range of subjects, to students and audiences of all ages and various nationalities. Due to my wide range of experience in this field, I have been able to amply demonstrate my versatility in imparting skills and knowledge to my students and audiences. Needless to say, I have been able to utilize my talents, skills, abilities and knowledge to the fullest.

What has enabled me to do this for such a long period of time is the simple fact that I possess the passion and the dynamism to excellently deliver the goods time and again. Together with the delivery skills required to succeed in this profession (which have been steadily acquired over the years), I am also a competent presenter and communicator. I do all of my work in the English language, although I am able to converse in Malay.

The most uncanny ability that I possess is that I am able to deliver on any non-technical subject, be it business-related or educational in nature, as long as the material is available. At times, I have even gone on to deliver presentations after simply having heard another presenter deliver it beforehand. Of course, I would then go on to infuse it with my own personality and style.
