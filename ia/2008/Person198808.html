Wayne Horkan’s Summary

Talented, successful & experienced Technical & Business Consultant, with an extensive career in IT / IS implementations for 14+ years. It’s been said that I’m intelligent, enthusiastic, objective & passionate – and I feel that’s especially true about technology & the people who enable the benefits it can bring.

Currently I am Chief Technology Officer (CTO) for the UK and Ireland at Sun Microsystems Ltd. (Sun) – Sun are a globally renowned Technology provider & a significant contributor to the Internet age.

A member of the British Computer Society (BCS), I hold a Chartered Fellowship of the British Computer Society (FBCS CITP), the highest and most senior grade of professional membership of the society.

I have broad skill base, from the highly technical; i.e. enterprise architecture (EA), application & systems integration, design & development of large-scale / complex heterogeneous systems; to more social capabilities; i.e. technical governance definition & implementation, people management & team building (mentoring, coaching & staff development).

Other positions at Sun incl. CTO for UK Public Sector, Senior UK Public Sector Consultant & Enterprise Technical Architect: I have been at the centre of a large number of strategically important, high profile engagements for Sun over the last 6 years, and I can honestly say it’s been an honour as well as a great deal of fun.

Previously to Sun I accelerated Harrods Ltd into the Internet Technology arena: as Chief Technical Architect I successfully led the technological implementation of Harrods’ Internet aspirations.

Prior employment also includes positions as a Technical Consultant at a Systems Integrator (SI), Senior Software Engineer at an Independent Software Vendor (ISV) & as a Computer Programmer before that.
