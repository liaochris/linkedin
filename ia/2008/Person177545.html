Steve Horton’s Summary

Professional writer since 1999 with years of experience writing for high-circulation national magazines, newspapers and websites. I've had articles published in POKER PRO, WPT MAGAZINE, ALL IN, BLUFF, POKER PLAYER NEWSPAPER, POKERBIZ, CAESAR'S PLAYER, POKERNEWS.COM, PC TODAY, LAPTOP, MOBILE, TECHLIVING, PCWORLD.COM, SCRYE, INQUEST GAMER, KNUCKLEBONES and ANIME INSIDER.

I've done live blogging and photography for several major poker tournaments. My clients include TropicalPoker.com, Borgata Hotel Casino & Spa, and PokerNews.com. For the second time, I was part of the official Live Update Team for the World Series of Poker in the summer of 2008. For the third time, I will be covering a major Borgata poker event in September.

Co-creator and scripter of Image Comics limited series STRONGARM.

Writer of two books: WEBCOMICS 2.0 from Course PTR, and PROFESSIONAL MANGA from Focal Press.

Writer of iPhone comic GAMBLING SOULS, part of Rebellion/2000AD's Clickwheel anthology, iNKED.

Launched a e-publishing company, Smashout Comics, in April 2007, securing and delivering content through multiple Internet channels. Though no longer associated with Wowio, Smashout continues to seek other outlets for digital comics publishing. Among Smashout's clients are some of the world's most popular comics creators.

Steve Horton’s Specialties:

AP style; HTML; Microsoft Office; writing for tone, audience and demographic; writing for script format; print to web publishing
