Michael Salort’s Summary

Was an award-winning journalist for more than 15 years, working for outlets such as ABC News 20/20, Dateline NBC and CNBC. Went on to help start the NIC Company, a SF-based tech company, funded and co-founded by Oracle Corp. chairman Larry Ellison.
In 2003, joined Viewpoint Corporation as head of Business Development, Marketing and Corporate Communications. At Viewpoint, helped develop and launch company's new initiative in Internet search, as well as intiatives to distribute and promote its 3D technology and ad-serving platforms. Spearheaded all product launches, marketing and branding, and developed new strategies for corporate positioning and outreach. Was part of the turn-around team that:

• Achieved three of four consecutive EBITDA positive quarters in 24 months, after years of previous losses
• Raised over $12 million through four PIPE transactions without banking assistance
• Launched new Search and Ad Serving products generating positive EBITDA in 17 months
• Quadrupled stock price within first 18 months.

After Viewpoint became Managing Partner of PersonalScreen Media at the Tribeca Film Center in Manhattan. PersonalScreen, a new film production and finance company, produces content and technology platforms for digital cinema and digital distribution. Co-wrote and co-executive produced the company's first web series, IN MEN WE TRUST, which along with its technology platform was nominated for a national Emmy Award in 2008.

In August 2006, became Chief Operating Officer of BrainPop, Inc. producing animations and movies for distribution on new and traditional media. During interim assignment, reorganized company's infrastructure and redesigned and implemented business development, marketing and financial operations.

In November 2007, founded Symbiotic Pictures Entertainment to invest in and leverage film assets and opportunities in New Media to distribute original content for film, television, Internet, mobile and other platforms.

Michael Salort’s Specialties:

Corporate and business development; organization optimization and growth; marketing and branding; brand management; fund raising; contract and partner negotiations; production management from both the creative and business perspectives.
