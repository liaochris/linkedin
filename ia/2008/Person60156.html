Eric Putkonen’s Summary

I have been in the recruiting industry since 1999.
I began my career in the recruiting field as an Internet Recruiting Coordinator in an accounting and finance recruiting agency. After about three and a half years or so, I moved on and spent a year as an Account Manager for a temporary office support agency. Then I spent over three years in technical recruiting. The past two years I have been working on the corporate side of recruiting.

Eric Putkonen’s Specialties:

Technical/IT is my specialty, but I have recruited for many different types of candidates in many industries.
