Evan Jones’s Summary

Evan Jones is the owner of Stitch Media, an interactive media production company and consulting firm that collaborates with traditional media creators to produce cross-media promotions and distributed narratives. A two-time Emmy Award winner, Jones' work spans many industries including primetime television, radio, web, mobile and games. Previously, Jones was the Creative Director at Xenophile Media where he spearheaded the ReGenesis Extended Reality Game, a groundbreaking integration of interactive television which won many awards including a Gemini and Banff World Television Award. His work has been named one of the 'Top 10 New Media Groundbreakers" over the past 10 years.

Jones has been a professor of Rich Media Production at Humber College and advisory board member to George Brown's Game Design Programme. He speaks regularly about his work at international events such as NextMedia, SXSW, GDC and SPAA. Jones co-founded the IGDA Alternate Reality Games SIG and works with the FITC Design & Technology Festival. He is a graduate of the Canadian Film Centre's Habitat New Media Lab, Sheridan's Interactive Multimedia Programme and McMaster University's multidisciplinary Arts & Science Programme. Over the years, Jones has worked with a diverse group of partners including Disney, CBC, Alliance Atlantis, Bell Canada, The Movie Network and Discovery.
