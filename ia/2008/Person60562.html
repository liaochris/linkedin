Erik Kopp’s Summary

Erik L. Kopp is a Corporate Communication Executive with expertise in driving business results through the development and implementation of targeted communication strategies. With nearly 20 years of experience, Erik works as Director of Global Corporate Communications at Barr Pharmaceuticals, Montvale, NJ.

Prior to assuming his current position, Erik worked as Regional Director, Corporate Communication for Cadbury Schweppes Americas Confectionery—a regional operating unit of Cadbury Schweppes, the world’s largest confectionery company and a leading North American/Australian beverage company. Here, he developed and implemented internal, external, and change communication strategies for a $2.6 billion, 20-country, 14,000-employee division.

Earlier, Erik worked as an independent consultant for leading companies such as McKesson Corporation, Sankyo Pharma (now Daiichi Sankyo) and Office Depot, delivering all-encompassing professional communication services. Prior to that, he directed global marketing and corporate communication for Dendrite (now Cegedim Dendrite), an international provider of pharmaceutical IT sales and marketing solutions and Customer Relationship Management services. He also held senior communication positions at Skila, a provider of brand effectiveness solutions and platforms to the global pharmaceutical/biotechnology industry; Merck-Medco Managed Care, LLC (now Medco Health Solutions), the nation’s largest provider of prescription drug benefit programs and services; and The Barash Group, Central Pennsylvania’s premier advertising agency/publishing company.

Erik holds a B.A. in Communication from Goshen College and an M.A. in Journalism and Public Affairs from The American University.
