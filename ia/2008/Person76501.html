Henry Frith’s Summary

I have twenty years of technical experience as a sales engineer, I have successfully sold and supported hardware, software and outsourced services to Enterprise, Higher Education, and Service Provider accounts.

My sales engineering experience includes security, network hardware, network management, Internet solutions and performance monitoring software. I have work as a sales engineer for companies such as Cabletron Systems, Entuity, Sophos and Internet Integration which I founded and managed.

My experience in developing and managing technical sales strategy and identifying new markets has allowed me to be very successful in selling to large enterprise customers and service providers. Utilizing strategic selling skills, I am able to help the sales team and channel partners maximize sales and achieve greater revenue.

With excellent presentation skills to both C-Level executives and technical personnel I am able to find the customers business or technical issues and address them during the presentation. I have excellent PowerPoint and web presentation software skills.
