Management Summary

Paul De Vree has a Bachelor degree in Computer Science. He has hands-on experience on most System Life Cycle stages, through positions ranging from system engineer, technical consultant and project manager. Paul specializes in various types of Client/Server Infrastructure implementations. His added value is not through theoretical knowledge, but scientific approach in managing practical, proven technologies.

His strength lies in combination of solid technical background with a natural ability and eagerness to push a team forward. Paul can easily delegate and keep his distance. But if needed, he can dig through the technical details, giving him a broader basis for swift, accurate decision making.
