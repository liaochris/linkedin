Brian Green’s Summary

I started programming text MUDs in 1993, and worked professionally on Meridian 59 in 1998. With a team of 3, I helped re-launch Meridian 59 commercially in 2002. I'm familiar with all aspects of running an online game, from development, programming, administration, customer service, and business management.

I am working on projects for Near Death Studios, Inc. but would be happy to provide my expertise and consult on other online game (MMORPG) projects.

I maintain a professional issues blog at http://www.psychochild.org/

Brian Green’s Specialties:

Small business management, game design, game server programming, gameplay system programming, team management, localization, international agreements, legal expert on online games.
