Fiona Mackenzie’s Summary

Founder of Undercover Strategist, a strategic agency for small and medium-sized businesses looking to compete. The agency provides a range of services including marketing, business and brand strategy both online and offline. For more information, either visit the agency online at www.undercoverstrategist.com or check this link: http://www.news.com.au/business/story/0,23636,21289464-36437,00.html

In addition to her agency interests, Fiona has a number of internet businesses.

Currently on working with Commonwealth Bank of Australia devising and executing strategy for business products online product library.

Prior to this, Fiona worked for Telstra Corporation. She is a highly experienced senior manager of large business units/product portfolios (up to $2B p.a.) in the telecommunications sector. Her experience includes financial management (revenue, costs and capital), marketing and sales management (all facets of channel, product and marketing strategy), line management and the management of legal and regulatory issues. She is an experienced multiple brand manager and B2B Marketer.

She has a MBA, plus Diplomas in both Marketing and Public Relations.
