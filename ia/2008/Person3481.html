Michael "Adrir" Scott’s Summary

After a focused secondary education in Engineering, I decided that it wasn't the route I wanted to go. During my Computing studies at A Level, I was introduced to object-orientated programming and got really into it.

As I got into programming, I thought I would try my hand at some games. It started out as writing a few simple mods for Freelancer using tutorials at LancersReactor.com. As my Java skills developed, I wrote some tools that parsed and wrote directly into .ini files more quickly than I could edit things manually.

So, I decided to do a degree in Computer Science. Currently I am at Brunel University learning more advanced Java, although I am learning C# and the XNA Framework in my free time. It is going well so far.

My current goal is to get my foot in the gaming industry, although I have a long way to go to improve my game programming skills and create some demo material. Someday, though!
