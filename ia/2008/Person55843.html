Earon Davis’s Summary

I have a broad, varied background, from years as a writer and in environmental health law and consulting to nonprofit management, integrative bodywork and holistic healing. I am currently writing a book that integrates much of what I've learned in my life and career. It presents a unique view of human nature that gives new hope as we attempt to change our collective awareness in ways that foster sustainability.

My current professional goals are to develop a private massage practice, become an author and teacher, and eventually create a Center for healing, retreat and interspiritual education. I recently focused on the development of a wide assortment of hands-on and energy bodywork healing skills, practices and tools. Currently, I am also focusing on writing and teaching college level courses.

Hence, my networking value at this time is varied. Providing professional services to linked-in members (and their organizations) interested in exploring bodywork and energy work and/or interspirituality as a modality of spiritual and traditional healing and transformation - as a way to understand and access the inner voice and strength. I could also be involved with projects in which massage and spirituality are used for clients/employees as a stress-reduction, health education, teambuilding experience. (I've had marvelous results in 15 minute fully clothed massages at the workplace or classroom.)

In other terms, through bodywork, energywork and breathwork, I try to help people find their "reset button" so that they can go outside the box - and envision their own futures. My premise is that it isn't chiefly our logical minds that hold us back - but rather our unconscious minds - which can often be opened through touch, body awareness, relaxation, breath work and imagination.

Earon Davis’s Specialties:

I have a healing massage practice in Evanston, Illinois, as a nationally certified bodyworker licensed by the State of Illinois. I also have a Master's degree in Public Health and a law degree, as well as experience in nonprofit management, environmental advocacy, teaching, holistic healing and a wide range of publications.
