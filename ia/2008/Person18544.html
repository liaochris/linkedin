Avi Mally ’s Summary

Avi Mally's experience spans 7 years in multiple disciplines, including senior level executive recruiting, investment research, and business development. Most recently, he was Director, Business Development at AC Lion, an executive search firm with a focus on emerging digital media. Previously, as Research Manager for Nitron Circle of Experts (Now Evalueserve Circle of Experts), he oversaw the delivery of high level industry research to hedge funds, venture capital firms, and top tier investment banks. With the research department under his stewardship, his leadership led to the firm's acquisition by Evalueserve, an offshore research and analytics firm founded in 2000 by former McKinsey and IBM executives. Evalueserve has been named one of the fastest growing companies in Asia-Pacific by both Red Herring and Deloitte Technology. They have grown to 2,250+ employees in 8 years, and have over 1,100 clients, including 7 of the 12 largest investment banks.
