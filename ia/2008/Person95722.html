John T. Johnson’s Summary

Telecommunication Special Services: meaning, we provide all type of specialized service outside of regular dialtone. Our group handles implemention circuit design to provide service requested by customer. Service range from 2.4k LOTTO circuits and 128k/144k ISDN circuits to T1/DSL circuits which provide telephone and data-interent connection.
Our Goal: To provide outstanding customer service, understand customer needs, and providing answers that will suit their business and telecommunication. Also, to provide word of mouth advertising by our customers to increase sales and most important; provide excellent circuit installation to prevent circuit swing (intermittent operation) and installation by AT&T Guidelines. Finally, using our resources to provide quick service proper installation.

John T. Johnson’s Specialties:

Reading circuit design, determine if redesign is warranted, trouble shooting circuit failure, testing loop design for integrity, discussing customer needs, and knowing what types of circuits at needed to handle circuit business operations.
