Bill Fanning’s Summary

With over 20 years experience in the creative industry, Bill brings a variety of skills to the table, including operations, advertising, trafficking, sales support, systems administration, and project management. Before leaving Boston in 1997, he spent eight years at IQ&J where he did extensive broadcast, corporate communication, and public relations work for firms such as Raytheon, MCI/Worldcom, Steinway, TJ Maxx, Ocean Spray, and Braun. Since relocating to the Bay Area he has lent his project management skills to Maiden Lane, Brandfusion, TDC and HDA for clients Nextel, E-Trade, Cannon Wines, AARP, Delta Dental, The Examiner, Fathead, Del Monte, Fisher-Price, Michelin Guides, Williams-Sonoma, and Oracle Services. Bill is a member of the Electronic Frontier Foundation, GLAAD, SF Cutters, and DVPA - the Digital Video Professional’s Association.

Bill Fanning’s Specialties:

Project Management, Creative Services Management, Sales and Marketing Support, Organizational Prowess, DVD Authoring, Video Editing, Operations and Facilities Management
