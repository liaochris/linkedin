Billy Mallery’s Summary

Malleryscores has created original music and sound design for a multitude of mediums for over 15 years, including national commercials, corporate logos, independent and feature film projects, video games and unique websites.

We have a network of selected composers, music producers and sound designers helping us to create some of the most innovative original music and sound in the industry. Headed by Creative Director and CLIO/AICP Award winner Billy Mallery, this team of sonic creatives delivers an immense diversity of musical genres and strives to invent a few new ones as well.

Originality remains the driving force behind this boutique company.
