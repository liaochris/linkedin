Alain Jouan’s Summary

To this day I have dedicated my entire professional life to the development of synthesized 2D and 3D image.

My first experience in that field goes as far as the first ever available system on the market: the CUBICOMP.
I arrived in my office at Techex, there was a box in a corner that nobody knew what to do with, then my boss said to me in disbelief: "Here this new product: sell it!".

Through the years it seems my recurrent mission has been to either start developing new products on the market or to start the French/European subsidiaries of existing companies based in the UK or the US, with roles such as Salesman, Sales Director and finally now Managing Director - a position I've held for 10 years.
I've had the privilege of promoting software such as Thomson Digital Image, Softimage, or Alias - another product of which I started the sales in France, Europe and the Middle East.


My current role as VP of MySystems Ltd has led me to thrive in various fields: sales, management, marketing, strategies, negociator, leader, team member, but I am now ready for a new challenge. Once again I would like to bring my knowledge and expertise in the development of products or companies by bringing operational help from someone who knows the EMEA market very well.

Here are a few of the clients I have worked with:Dior, Cartier, LVMH
Renault, PSA, EADS, TF1/Eurosport, Telecom, TDF, France Television, Interia (Poland), Canal+, Euronews, BFM, M6, RTL, Dubai TV, El Arabiya, RAI, KTV1, BBC, Image TV (Turkey), France Galop/PMU, TBN, Walt Disney, Al Jazeera, Bouygues, Neuf, ORANGE, Alice, and references available on request.
