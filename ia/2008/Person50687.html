Dave Ficks’s Summary

"Life's a journey, not a destination" - that is the quote I have lived by throughout my professional life. I have had the phenomenal opportunity to work in such diverse industries as banking, university student activities, computer applications training, technology recruiting, education and non-profits. I am just finishing work with a new company start-up called LifeFoods, consulting for web and business services. This organization is striving to make a difference in the world by helping non-profits, school organizations, sports clubs and other fundraising organizations raise money. Working at a start-up is exciting, especially when it is an organization that has "Doing Well by Doing Good" as its guiding philosophy.

Additionally, I have had the opportunity to get to know and work with some extraordinary people in the past and I keep meeting new extraordinary people every day. I appreciate that LinkedIn provides the opportunity to reach out to those people continually. If you know me, please contact me to be in your network. If you'd like me to introduce you to someone I know, please ask. "Linking in" is one of the great opportunities we have to interconnect and I'm proud to be here.

Teaching (adult and at-risk youth), curriculum development, educational program management, volunteer recruiting, marketing and promotions, special event development, and technology skills including web design, technology training, and consulting.
