Anil Chhikara’s Summary

JUST OUT: We raised another $4m investment round from Network18, India's leading full play media conglomerates with interests in television, internet, filmed entertainment, mobile content and allied businesses. It controls India’s leading business news television channels - CNBC TV18 and CNBC Awaaz besides MTV, Nick & VH1. It also runs India’s leading Internet company Web18 which has interests JobStreet, Yatra, BigTee, moneycontrol.com, tech2.com etc.

Looking forward to a rocking time ahead!

I founded 24x7 Learning Solutions (www.24x7learning.com) in 2001 along with my dear friend Karthik KS. It today is India's largest eLearning implementation company. Some of 24x7 Learning’s marquee clients include: Aditya Birla Group, MindTree, Accenture, Samsung, Essar, Mahindra and Mahindra, Murugappa Group, L&T, Dell, Satyam, Pepsico etc.

A Mechanical Engineer, I have more than 19 years of experience in the IT industry with companies like HCL-Hewlett Packard, Wipro, Parametric Technology Corporation, USA, Patni and Cimatron, Israel. Before founding 24x7 Learning Solutions, I successfully set-up the operations for Cimatron Ltd., Israel in India. From an unknown brand, we were able to establish Cimatron as a major player in its target segment within a year of commencing operations.

I believe that eLearning has the power to democratise education and make it available to everyone at a very affordable cost - irrespective of the learner's geographical location. eLearning based distance education can help meet the challenge India's growth is facing today due to skilled talent shortage.

I believe each one of us should in some way give back to society from which we have received so much. I am involved with the “Freedom through Education” movement (http://www.freedomthrougheducation.org) of Round Table India to build schools in poor and rural areas for under-privileged children.
