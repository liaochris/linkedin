Chris Rawlinson’s Summary

My background started with a successful career in London with Intelligent homes sales, marketing, design and project management for very excusive clients. I then decided to take a risk and leave the UK to follow a dream of getting a Commercial pilots license and so moved to South Africa. After a year I accomplished my dream and moved to work in the mother city: - Cape Town.
After helping start up a local aviation company, I took another adventure and am now helping with sales and marketing for the Stormhoek wine brand. Through working with them and after some very successful web marketing campaigns I now find myself surrounded by the very best of the best South African tech and marketing visionaries and entrepreneurs. I still continue to fly, just more for pleasure than work at the moment!
