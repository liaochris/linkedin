Lucas Krech’s Summary

I am a freelance Lighting Designer based out of New York working in Theatre, Opera and Dance. I have worked across the United States and Territories as well as Europe.

Lucas Krech’s Specialties:

In addition to working in numerous traditional theatrical settings, my work has had a strong focus on site-specific and experimental theatrical and operatic works. I have developed unique skills for working in various and conflicting styles and situations from traditional union houses to installation pieces.
