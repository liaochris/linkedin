Lisa Mastroianni’s Summary

• professional networker
• charming
• adaptable
• risk-taker

Her career kicked off working as a PA and within 2 years, Lisa moved up to Production/Media at Celsius Films. She was an Associate Producer on the movie “Holly” in which she helped manage the Premiere at the United Nations, the After Party, the camera crew and interviewed people on the Red Carpet. Lisa also collaborated with Ogilvy on the branding and development of the Red Light Children Campaign, an international grassroots campaign with multi-faceted online interactive and viral advertising to help reduce child trafficking.

Lisa worked on "SURVIVOR" with CBS Productions and threw the After Party last minute...that's a good story. She says it was surreal. She also worked “Clash of the Choirs” with NBC and "Rip the Runway" with BET.

So on a whim, she just went to LA and 3 days before her visit; she gets booked on work with "Celebrity Circus" and stays for 2 weeks because she got extended on work with them and was booked on a commercial while she was there...ironically, she relocated to LA only after coming back to NY for a month and a half to work on "Brooklyn's Finest" starring Richard Gere, Ethan Hawke and Wesley Snipes (and of course throwing the After Party).

She is now currently in LA, specializing in Production and Events. Lisa’s vision, connections and energy are very unique - that combined with her natural instincts of coordinating makes her your mover and shaker...

"Let's make it happen people...and I never claimed I wasn't nuts."

--LM
