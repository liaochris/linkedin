Brian Riley’s Summary

Over 7 years experience architecting, leading, and developing rich internet application based solutions within the financial services and retail sectors in the Fortune 100. I work directly with technical architects and user experience directors to coalesce the technical requirements with the creative vision; I offer strategic consulting including technical assessments and informational sessions to clients diving in to the RIA space.

I have expertise in Flex, Flex Data Services (now LCDS), Ajax, (including Dojo and other popular toolkits and frameworks), Flash and J2EE development.

I've also led several RIA technical educational conferences, informational sessions, and workshops to IBM's global business services division and its clients.

Brian Riley’s Specialties:

RIA, Flex, Flash, AIR, Ajax, LiveCycle Data Services, ActionScript, Design Patterns, Object Oriented, SDLC, J2EE, Cairngorm, UI Architecture and Development, Financial Services, Insurance, Retail Applications.
