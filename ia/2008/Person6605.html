Alex Comninos’s Summary

Independent researcher into Sub-Saharan African political economy. I wish to do research work for Think Tanks, NGOs or international organisations focusing on political economy in Sub-Saharan Africa. I would like to do work with any companies/organisations interested in Information and Communications Technology (ICT) and development. I am interested in telecommunications policy and the developmental effects of ICTs, in particular the mobile phone. My Masters thesis is on the South African mobile telecommunications giant MTN and its expansion into Africa.

Alex Comninos’s Specialties:

Finding and archiving information and references, writing up research reports, critical thinking, firm understanding of Southern African political economy and Sub-Saharan African telecommunications policy and regulation.
