Ben Johnston’s Summary

I am currently the National Solutions Manager in the Microsoft Solutions - Application Integration [MS-AI] Practice of Dimension Data, a large global Systems Integrator, and Microsoft Global Alliance Partner.

I have 18+ years experience in the ICT industry. During this time I have worked with niche providers, large national and global System Integrators, and spent time working as a freelance contractor. I have a mixed Infrastructure Engineering and Application Development background.

For the last 10+ years I have been focused in roles that leverage the Microsoft platform to deliver outcomes that address collaboration, information management and application layer solution requirements.

I have worked in a range of roles prior to my current position - Infrastructure Engineer/Architect, Developer, Business Analyst, Application Architect, Project Manager, Senior Technical Consultant, Senior PreSales Consultant, Solution Architect and Practice Manager.

Technology areas where I am currently focused and have experience include - Microsoft Office System 2007, Microsoft Office SharePoint Server [MOSS] 2007, .NET, SQL Server, BizTalk Server and K2.net / [blackpearl].

I enjoy contributing to and managing teams to set and achieve bold targets, and the personal and professional growth that accompany these challenges.

I also enjoy learning, and the process of learning.

My professional goals include further growth and experience in managing and leading Professional Services teams in the ICT sector around the Collaboration and Information Management solutions category.

Ben Johnston’s Specialties:

Technologies - Portals, Collaboration, Information Management, Knowledge Management, Business Process Management, Application Integration, Microsoft, .NET, Application Architecture, Workflow, Enterprise Content Management.

Business skills - Built on my extensive technologist experiences, I have expanded my skills in areas of business management including - project and programme management, consulting, commercial business development and practice management.
