mohamed amami’s Summary

Since my childhood, I have shown great interest to computer sciences, mathematics and human sciences. Such an interest was translated in my academic choices and especially in my professional experiences. In fact, concerning experiences, I have been able to get in touch with new technologies like J2EE and Eclipse which have allowed me to discover more fields of interest in the computer sciences and networking. This was done during my engineering internship in the Net-Progress company specialized in developing rich client applications and J2EE frameworks for networks with n-tiers architecture. My first and richest experience with communication protocols was done during my graduation project in Integration Objects, an international company dealing with industrial software development and industrial installations. I worked on the design of an OPC Server and the implementation of the Distributed Network Protocol (DNP3) which is a TCP/IP like protocol aimed at enabling industrial communication between Intelligent Electronic Devices and Servers. I have been able to implement that protocol according to the DNP3 specification which is an IEEE specification and to get great real time performance for any DNP3 industrial communication by introducing the concept of real time database system in the design and implementation process.
As networking and computer sciences technologies are nowadays merged effectively in order to meet the new challenges of communication networks, it is obvious that any engineer, like me, who is managing to make a successful professional carrier in the entreprise software developement and networking fields , would be interested in integrating effectively the challenging engineering world of N-tiers software platforms and the RIA and REA. I look forward to be the confirmed engineer and the manager capable of resolving problems of the challenges of new emerging Software and web technologies
