Enrico Foschi’s Summary

My working life started when I won two first awards for two country promoted Web contest (Fausto Sartori Contests - Milan).

After some small application developement (web & stand-alone), I began to work for Sky & Mediaset television as a real-time video editor. After 1 year, I thought that with the right software, the work management could be easier. I developed an application by my own (the JOB Organizer), which is used for administrating employees timetable and calendars entirely through a web and a mobile platform with the use of an sms sending / receiving system.
Once the developing was completed, I showed it to my employers, they liked it and bought it, becoming my first big customers: with this first success, I opened a little company, the EIMTech (Engineered Interactive Media Technologies - an individual agency).

Then I worked for several Web Companies (Carta e Matita S.r.l., Kettydo S.r.l., BusyBeee S.r.l. and RMG Connect S.r.l. / J. W. Thompson S.p.a) until the last one (RMG connect) made me a great offer for working just for them with a very good leading position in a wonderful environment.

In all of these projects, I made most of the application design and all the C#+SQL / PHP+MySQL back-end and Flash/AJAX developing, including the developing of Flex 2 / 3 interfaces for services administration.

From September 2006 until June 2007, I taught Flash designing and developing (basic & advanced courses) in IED, an European Design Institute (IED – Istituto Europeo del Design): teaching is one of my hobbies: I had an excellent relations with my students and I loved to work with them.

From September 2007 until March 2008 I worked in the very great Ammado Internet Services Ltd. as a Senior front-end and back-end developer for the www.Ammado.com community.

At the moment I am an internal consultant in Microsoft.

(other info on http://www.enricofoschi.com )
