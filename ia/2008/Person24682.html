Bobbi Youngblood’s Summary

Professional management consulting, human resource audit, and executive placement firm consulting in both gaming and non-gaming markets; specializing in technical and executive recruiting, training and development, senior management initiatives, state & federal labor regulations, SOX, FLSA, EEOC, AAP, market-driven wage structures and metrics tied to performance and bonus incentives, resource and corporate planning.

As a former assistant in the Presidential Appointments for Reagan-Bush (Texas office), committed and experienced in finding and placing key executives and professionals in a variety of industries. Client driven, our firm initiates and executes to increase the Company's bottom line and discover growth opportunities for corporate clients and superior candidates.

Specializing in gaming technology, federal & state labor requirements, and human resources across many industries, our recruiting and consulting staff knows Las Vegas -- and its unique business needs and challenges to employ a vibrant, qualified workforce and leaders who achieve potential. Clients include national and international global brands in: commercial real estate, finance, gaming, law, architecture, education, engineering and technical industries. We honor the Company's mission and vision and contribute by consulting, researching, and providing unique short- and long-term solutions and top-tier, talented candidates for placement! We are not a recruiting agency - we are part of the inner circle for your company's vision to achieve results and we deliver!

Bobbi Youngblood’s Specialties:

Wage structures by specific industry or region; wages/bonus/benefit analyses; resource planning; corporate initiatives & methodologies to achieve objectives; consulting for commercial interests, gaming, manufacturing, supplier operations; internal investigations; confidential labor audits; employment law compliance; government affairs; employee relations, performance standards, mgmt training; organizational infrastructure, staffing and executive placement.
