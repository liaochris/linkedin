Stacy Nelson, CSA (TopLinked.com)’s Summary

I am an Entrepreneur at heart, always following what my brain finds intriguing. I have for the past five years concentrated my efforts on being a Reverse Mortgage Specialist. This has allowed me to personally help over 312 seniors leverage their equity to give them more financial wiggle room.

I also am the Owner and Founder of The Wino Club. The Wino Club kit has all of the materials and instructions required to start a monthly social wine tasting club of your own. Our friends have our own Wino club meetings and I cannot tell you how much fun we have together every month, gathering together for the sake of drinking wine.

From there, I also host a 10 minute wine tasting segment on the online radio show Only 2 Degrees. Here we simply get to drink and talk about the wine - how fabulous of a gig is that?

I am currently working on a book and speaking module and will keep you all posted as to my progress there.

Please be sure to invite me as one of your links! I love having new relationships and there's nothing better than networking online.
