Guy Genilloud’s Summary

Professional Objectives:
• Conceive information systems that satisfy the business objectives of an enterprise and the specific needs of users.
• Realize, in collaboration with domain experts, business models and information models for distributed and integrated information systems.
• Help software development teams improve their practice through the use of modeling and abstract specification techniques.

Acquired and Validated Skills
• Capability to formally specify a system or a service (using use case, activity, information and other modeling constructs).
• Ability to analyze and compare information systems and architectures for deducing integration strategies.
• Ability to adapt a software engineering method according to the specific needs of an organization.

Guy Genilloud’s Specialties:

• Object Modelling, UML, Specification of systems and components, and services.
• Modelling distributed systems according to ISO's RM-ODP viewpoints.
• Use cases modelling (relating business processes to use cases, reuse across projects, roles, actors and principals).
• Experience in the financial services domain.
