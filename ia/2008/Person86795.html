Jean Cummings’s Summary

Biography of Jean Cummings, M.A.T., CPBS, CPRW, CEIP

Jean Cummings has been building executive careers in technology and life sciences since 1992. She is a Certified Professional Resume Writer, Certified Personal Branding Strategist, and job search coach with an advanced degree from Harvard University.

She leverages insight and creativity to distill complex executive careers in technology into potent, valuable, and differentiated personal brands.

Jean recognizes that high-tech and other technology executives are managing their careers in a volatile sector made turbulent by fast market change, outsourcing /offshoring, and ageism. She enables them to proactively position themselves for strong career growth.

Her 14-year-old company, A Resume For Today, provides leading-edge strategies from initial personal brand strategy development through job search coaching to final salary negotiation training.

Jean is an award-winning resume writer, a personal branding expert, and a job search coach. She is a Certified Personal Branding Strategist, a Certified Professional Resume Writer, and a Certified Employment Interview Professional. Her resumes are widely published in resume and career books.

Jean is author of “Turbocharged Networking for $100K+ Jobs,” a compact, pragmatic e-Guide for both career coaches and job seekers. It reveals the “how-to” secrets to getting hired in this economy.

Jean holds a Master of Arts in Teaching Degree from Harvard University. She is also a Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Mount Holyoke College. Jean came to the career development field by way of teaching. During her 13-year teaching career, she specialized in teaching students with differences in thinking and learning styles.

This focus on the individual combined with a clear sense of what hiring authorities are looking for in candidates enables her to develop a unique, cystal-clear, highly marketable personal brand for each client.
