Marin MIGNOT’s Summary

As Business Development Director for Sogeti, I am responsible for the development and the management of the Sogeti operations for the aerospace market and more specifically for EADS (European Aeronautic Defence & Space, parent company of Airbus); today it represents €136 millions of revenues and approximately 1,700 consultants mainly in France and Germany.

The main objectives are to grow the revenues on EADS, as well as the profit; ensure a balanced sales mix between Infrastructure Services, Applications Services and High Tech Consulting; and develop our operations beyond current perimeter, by external acquisitions (in UK & Spain) and by pushing offshore activities (India).

As Airbus Sales Director for Singapore (Singapore Airlines, Silk Air, Tiger Airways), I was responsible for organizing, directing and achieving the sales processes through to the aircraft delivery by the coordinated use of all resources available.

International manager in various countries (Brazil, Malaysia, Germany, Spain), in high-tech services industry market, in an international and multicultural environment, I am quite at ease in business development functions, contracts, operations and programs management.

I do also have hands-on managerial experience, while during my tenure as Program Manager of the Fairchild-Dornier 728-Jet fuselage design and engineering, I have managed a three-year project, fixed-price contract, employing 200 engineers, for a total turnover of €60 Millions.
