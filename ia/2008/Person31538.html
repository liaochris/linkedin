Cary Johnson’s Summary

Cary Johnson brings over ten years of experience to developing and managing eVisibility's nationwide and worldwide Internet sales team. He has helped build eVisibility from a company with 3 employees to a company with over 40 employees and an annual revenue of over $5 million. He has helped create and build the culture of eVisibility, a work environment and atmosphere that has one of the highest employee retention rates of any company. A consummate team player, Mr. Johnson attended California State Polytechnic University on a soccer scholarship.

Mr. Johnson comes from a family of sales professionals and has over 20 years of experience in that industry. He pioneered the creation and implementation of real estate virtual tours and helped build the real estate division of iPIX into a major Internet company. He excels at conceptualizing and developing successful online companies.

As an associate with Advanced Access, Z57, and iStrategy, Mr. Johnson taught Internet marketing techniques to hundreds of realtors before being recruited by DoubleClick/Message Media to become their Sales Director with responsibility for the Western Region, including Asia/Pacific.

Mr. Johnson splits his time between eVisibility's Los Angeles and San Diego offices.
