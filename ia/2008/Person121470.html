Mark Cunningham’s Summary

I have been involved in numerous start-ups over the past 18 years.Each of them has provided different levels of experience in areas such as fund raising, investor relations, sales and marketing, product management, R&D, customer service and strategic partnerships. The great part about being a business founder and leader is that I quickly become aware of every area of a business...whether I like it or not!

My main area of expertise is business intelligence software. I was on the original team that created a product called Crystal Reports which was the worlds first windows based reporting tool. Since my Crystal days I have gone on to create multiple start-ups in the business intelligence market. I am also the founder of Symmetrics Pro Cycling...#1 road cycling team in North America.

I like sports....I am obsessed with kite surfing and since starting I have gained 8 lbs. One advantage to racing with the bike team I own is that it keeps you fit. Kiting, on its own, is not a calorie burner. I still ride but not near enough. I was a competitive cyclist and triathlete. I have done the Ironman in Hawaii twice and a number of cycling stage races with my team. Sadly work is sucking up more of my time and I can't seem to do ALL of the sports I love.

I fly airplanes....mostly bush flying in a small Cessna 185 taildragger or in a Turbine Beaver float plane. I fly to my kiting destinations as much as I can. Hawaii is a bit far for the airplanes I fly but I am game to try one of these days.

I have an artsy side to me....I am into black and white photography, I have my own darkroom but I have been shooting film and scanning more recently which is a good blend between the high and low tech worlds for photography. I play guitar, blues mostly, but from time to time I will crank up the Marshall amp and play a little Zepplin or Metallica. I dig Hendrix, Stevie Ray Vaughn, Buddy Guy, Muddy Waters and the rest of the blues greats.
