Andres Robles’s Summary

Andres Robles considers him self as a “Business Hunter” for Mexico, Latin America & US Hispanic for the TV & Radio Broadcast industry identifying new opportunities relating state of the art IT and consumer behavior. He has been directly related in the beginnings of DRTV (Direct Response TV) in Mexico and many Latin American countries. His extensive knowledge in the industry started in 1993 with QVC, launching the first join venture (with Televisa) out of the US. He has been related with the major TV broadcast groups in Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, Venezuela and US Hispanic. His lately activity is to broadcast TV via Internet (IPTV) generating a new media for Radio, Concerts, Sports Events, Education, within others. As promoter of this “New Media” he is generating revenues via interactive publicity with the major advertising agencies He has been a Member of Latin American council at ERA (Electronic Retail Association) active member of NAB (National Associations of Broadcasters) and board member of AMIPCI (Mexican Association of Internet).
