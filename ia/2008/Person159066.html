Rosa Vargas, Resume Writer’s Summary

Rosa E. Vargas is a (MRW) Master Resume Writer and (NCRW) Nationally Certified Resume Writer. She has written hundreds of effective resumes for careerist in an array of specialties with a concentration on creative resumes, high-tech, and the nursing field.

Her resumes are published in 'Designing the Perfect Resume' by Pat Criscito; 'The 30-Minute Resume Makeover' by Louise Kursmark' and 'Happy About My Resume: 50 Tips for Building a Better Document to Secure a Brighter Future' by Barbara Safani.

Prior to resume writing, she was an accomplished senior-level manager for over ten years, directing retail locations with a projection of over $3 million per year in New York City. She directed as many as fifty direct employees, including six sales representatives, and four managers. She exceeded at interviewing, identifying, hiring, developing, and retaining talented employees while consistently exceeding revenue projections.
