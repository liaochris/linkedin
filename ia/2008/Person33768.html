Charles van Ginhoven’s Summary

Business consulting, strategic planning, mergers and acquisitions, entrepreneur, private equity, project management, valuation, business launch, and development.

Jointly set up a boutique early-stage investment company which is doing great and boosting many companies internationally. Set-up an incubator as well in Rotterdam which is being re-launched. And participated in many entrepreneurial private equity investment transactions.

Highly enriching assignments exposed me to very interesting people and innovative adventures in the management treatment for hepatitis, sensors, a training facility for laparoscopic surgery, innovative umbrellas, a toy company led by elder ladies, a niche distributor of new mobile phones, narrowcasting technologies, a real estate purchase in an exotic island, a ringtones producer, and a company designing and manufacturing greeting cards with sounds (flew very high!).

My collaboration continues with Dutch (government and private) initiatives, in matters of developing countries and entrepreneurship in the Netherlands.
