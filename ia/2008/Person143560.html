Oddmund Granli’s Summary

The Candidate is a graduate with distinction and Dean’s prize from Lancaster University Management School (UK, 1999). Post graduate (PhD, studies and research) in Risk and Uncertainty Management in capital investment projects from UiS and Massacusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), USA, 2001-2005. He holds a MSc i Engineering Project Management and a BSc in Mechanical Engineering and a BA in Business administration. He has spent a number of years in the area of oil and gas technology and project management of capital investment projects and business development.
Positions held have been Project Manager, Project Control Manager, Planning Manager and Director of a consultancy in the area of process simulation, predictive control and risk analysis in the area of oil and gas. The hands on project work ranges from, -onshore plants (oil, gas), aluminium, ammonium nitrate, offsites and utilities, offshore platforms and pipelines. Capex from $100 Mill USDto $10 Bill USD

The candidate has performed senior and executive consultancy tasks for major international oil & energy companies, the Norwegian Government and the International Olympic Committee.
