Gilbert Verdian’s Summary

Introduced to the UNIX operating system at the age of 6, he has spent countless hours and nights picking apart programs and operating systems to discover their internals ever since.

Gilbert Verdian is currently a Senior Manager in IT Security at Ernst & Young in London.

Previously he headed up Professional Services in the Global Security Solutions division of CSC EMEA and the Global Practice Lead for the StrikeForce ethical hacking team. Before this role, he lead the StrikeForce team for Australia, specialising in ethical hacking, vulnerability assessments and remote exploitation of client’s networks During this time he has applied his technical skills & expertise in Consulting, Risk Assessments, Security Architecture, Privilege Management Infrastructure, Web Hosting, Directories and Security research.

Prior to this, Gilbert has had vast experience in the security field; assisting clients develop secure solutions, policies and networks, working for companies such as Telstra, Ernst & Young, Optus and BTLooksmart.

Gilbert is a member of the Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA) and is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA). He also holds certifications in Cisco, Checkpoint, Security, Unix and Linux.

Mr Verdian holds a Bachelor of Business degree majoring in E-business and a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). He is currently completing a PhD in risk management from Macquarie University.
