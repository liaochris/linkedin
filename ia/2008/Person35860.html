Christopher Riggio’s Summary

Chris Riggio is Director, Business Development (Americas), for Interactive Data Managed Solutions (IDMS). IDMS provides web based solutions utilizing their own market data, tools, and other related content to build and design the top financial, media, and corporate websites on the web. Previously, he worked at Dow Jones Client Solutions as well as their sister company SmartMoney providing similar solutions in the financial arena.

Chris Riggio has over 10 years experience in the financial services industry. His success has been attributed to his strong work ethic and his overall performance as a leader in business, which compliment his career goals and his desire to conceptually understand a growing work environment.
