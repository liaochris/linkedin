Molly Gray’s Summary

I'm passionate about being a resource and an advocate for people in need. I've loved working in women's centers, youth shelters, emergency rooms, senior service district centers, and now in a unique position in a ethnically and economically diverse, north portland community. Although I bring a lot to these organizations and jobs, I learn great things about building community, togetherness, resources and social services as a whole.

  • Outreach Coordinator

    Hands and Words Are Not For Hurting Project

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 2006 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    I implement projects into community centers, schools, shelters, companies, senior centers, and organizations. I promotes the mission and vision of the organization by giving the pledge "I will not use my hands or my words for hurting myself or others."

    I have been a keynote speaker for business presentations, schools and community centers with the intention of the employees training other staff and volunteers about the mission. The purpose is to educate people so they become activists and be a catalyst for change.

    My presentations inspired the Housing Authority of Portland's to implement the Hands Project into New Columbia where there are posters in the coffee shops, Boys and Girls clubs, and grocery stores in North Portland- to show the community that North Portland business owners are against all forms of violence.
