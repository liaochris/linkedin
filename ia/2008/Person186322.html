Ted Elliott’s Summary

Chief Executive officer with broad experience in software as a service and human capital management managed via the internet. Direct experience building applications for the Salesforce AppExchange and launching new products. Oversaw application development in a wide variety of business applications. Particularly interested in software as a service in the Salesforce, SugarCRM or Netsuite platforms. Corporate generalist attorney with substantial in-house legal experience. High premium on providing preemptive legal support in English. Focus on fund raising, contract drafting and dispute negotiation.Complete P&L responsibility and experience raising venture capital. Interested in early-stage startups as well as companies “on the bubble.”

Ted Elliott’s Experience

  • CEO

    Jobscience

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    December 1999 — Present (9 years 1 month)

    Organized the business in 1999 with financial backing from BrassRing, Kaplan Ventures (Division of Washington Post), Tribune Company, and Gannett, Inc. Determined original business plan for the company to create a niche healthcare career site was not viable and set company on a startegy of helping hospitals deploy applicant tracking software as a web based service. The business has over 175 hospitals utilizing our enterprise class service. In 2005 adopted salesforce.com as a business platform to manage the entire Jobscience Enterprise and began deploying product on the AppExchange in 2007.
