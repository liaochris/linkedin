Cynthia Mulder (www.cerritotropicalpanama.com)’s Summary

A dynamic individual with extensive experience in business related to travel, tourism and hospitality as well as writing, marketing and publishing. In addition, prior to the above, numerous successful years in retail management and purchasing. Experienced in building new business from the ground up in various countries.

Has written and designed a Dive Travel Guide published for Curacao with a grant by the European Union, her company has been part of a consortium that developed a fully integrated tourism plan for the island of Curacao, Netherlands Antilles which was also related to her book. This project included, planning, research, writing, publishing, marketing of: maps, book (in several languages), signage.

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Philanthropy industry)

    August 2002 — December 2004 (2 years 5 months)

    Founded a charity project which ran for two years in Japan. Collection and organization of used clothing, school supplies and toys destined for Ngobe Bugle indigenous people in the mountains of Chiriqui, Panama.

    Donations from families of the Fukuoka International School were collected, organized and packed for shipping by a group of volunteers from the school. Designed printed publicity, organized fund raising, and developed PR campaign. Created a system of inventory for customs use in Panama.

    Negotiated with airlines for shipping at no charge from Japan to Panama, in exchange for PR events organized with celebrities, covered by Japanese newspapers, and television.

    Arranged customs clearance and distribution to the families through a Panamanian group in Chiriqui.

    Project ended when Cynthia moved from Japan to Panama.
