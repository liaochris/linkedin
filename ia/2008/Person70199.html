Ginny Mata’s Summary

I am a writer, editor, and project manager who aims to build a fulfilling career as a creative advocate of youth and gender empowerment and education.

I write and edit features, personality profiles, investigative reportage, and biographies.

I do over-all project coordination of the production of books, special fundraising events, conferences, and media campaigns for CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) programs and non-profit organizations.

My portfolio includes:

*Magazines
Summit Media, Inc., MMPI, Businessworld, Hinge Inquirer

*Books
Big Dipper Publishing, Inc.,
Canadian International Development Agency

*Non-Government Organizations
Jesuit Communications, Inc., National Book Store Foundation,
Women's Crisis Center

*Universities
Ateneo de Manila University, University of the Philippines Press

Ginny Mata’s Specialties:

publishing, writing and editing of sociological, historical, biographical and literary manuscripts, magazine and newspaper features, marketing and public relations, fund-raising, development work, lead coordination of events and book projects.
