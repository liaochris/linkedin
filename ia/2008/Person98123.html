Joshua Davidson’s Summary

I seek to apply my literary talents to help individuals and institutions define themselves and develop successful strategies toward the achievement of their objectives.

My experience includes working closely with senior executives at Chrysler, IBM, General Motors and other large enterprises and institutions to develop strategic messages and develop speeches, correspondence and publications. Specific projects include the General Motors Annual Report, IBM EoN (Edge-of-Network) internal launch, Chrysler Fund Report to the Community (IABC International Gold Quill).

I have also published fiction in the "Atlantic Monthly," and nonfiction in a variety of national publications. I have won a number of significant awards for writing, editing and publications.
