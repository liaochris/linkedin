Raj Sardesh’s Summary

Raj Sardesh -- Professional Background
Raj Sardesh is a consultant with expertise in market assessment, marketing planning, partner strategy, product strategy, product positioning and strategic product marketing for high-tech and enterprise software companies. He has 20+ years of experience in senior positions in software development, consulting and product marketing from several leading companies, including, Sun Microsystems, Oracle, Sybase, BEA and Ford Motor Company.
For the last seven years, he has consulted with a number of emerging and established corporations on product strategy, marketing strategy, and product positioning leading to solid MRD/PRD and product marketing deliverables, including sales tools, collateral, Analysis Report and Summaries, and web materials and integrated marketing campaigns.
Raj has worked in product marketing for 14 years, including his tenure at leading companies—Oracle, Sybase and Trigo Technologies (now acquired by IBM). He was a Sr. Product Marketing Manager at Oracle for four years, a Sr. Program Manager at Sybase for two years and Director of Product Marketing at Trigo Technologies for a year.

For the last seven years, Raj has been successful in consulting with emerging and established companies in the U.S. and internationally. Some of his past clients include Microsoft, Sun Microsystems, BEA, PointBase, Trilogy Software, AlphaBlox/IBM, Saqqara, Versant, PointBase, Full Degree/Amphire Solutions, Questra, Helmi Technologies (from Finland), Opdex (from India), among others. Deliverables include marketing strategy, partner marketing strategy, marketing plans, product positioning, value proposition development, sales tools, technical competitive analysis, ROI & TCO tools, Analysis Report and Summaries, collateral, sales/analyst presentations and lead generation campaigns etc.

Raj Sardesh’s Specialties:

Market assessment, corporate strategy, partnership strategy, product strategy, product management, project management, offshore software development, product positioning, go-to-market strategy, product launch, and product marketing for hi-tech and enterprise software industries.
