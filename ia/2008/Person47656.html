Don Davidson’s Summary

The Davidson Group is a full service executive search firm specializing in all aspects of recruiting for the legal services market.

Located in the San Francisco Bay Area, The Davidson Group provides search capability for legal vendors, law firms and other information providers throughout the U.S.

The Davidson Group maintains access to the best companies in the industry and can open doors for talented individuals. Our experience and knowledge of the legal industry is assurance that companies are matched with the right candidate and individuals are placed in the right job for their skills and experience.
