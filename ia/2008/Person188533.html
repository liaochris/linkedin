Brett Hoffman’s Summary

To be a successful recruiter, it takes experience. Mr. Hoffman’s talent and evaluation skills were developed through 20 years of management experience in traditionally high turnover industries, resulting in an extensive amount of “hands on” experience in interviewing techniques and selection criteria.

After having processed thousands of applicants, conducted hundreds of interviews, and successfully built training programs and mentored supervisors on the successful integration of new hires into his employer’s cultures for two elite customer service programs, Mr. Hoffman switched gears and entered the recruiting field.

Mr. Hoffman joined with MRINetwork in 2001, the largest contingency search firm in North America. While there, Mr. Hoffman earned the prestigious CSAM (Certified Senior Accounts Manager) certification after completing a comprehensive training program.

After leaving MRI, Mr. Hoffman is now employed with THT Recruiting, a small boutique firm that allows Mr. Hoffman a greater flexibility to service clients.
