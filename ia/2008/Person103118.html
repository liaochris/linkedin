Katherine Pendill’s Summary

Katherine Pendill is a results-driven strategist with extensive experience spanning progressive strategic- and market-focused entrepreneurial and intrapreneurial roles in the USA and EU. She has worked across industries as well as in management consulting firms.

Katherine has developed and managed convergent businesses and their supporting technologies across media, technology, telecoms, and consumer goods, and has been a member of the start-up team for media firms and consultancies both in the USA and EU.

Her functional specialties are in strategy, communications, internationalization and marketing.

Her industry focal points are technology, telecoms, media, manufacturing, and consumer goods.

She has won numerous awards; published articles and white papers and been a conference speaker.
