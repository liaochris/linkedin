Prasanth "KV" Karumanchi’s Summary

Prasanth Karumanchi has been working in the investment management industry since 2004 and has advanced from a Summer Intern to the Director (Middle East Office) in two and a half years.

As the Director of the Middle East office, he is responsible for directing and coordinating the establishment and operations of the Middle East office including the business development, client services and marketing.

Prior to this, he was working as a quantitative research analyst and was responsible for the development and testing of CTA trading strategies (Managed Futures and FX). As a part of the research team, he also developed an equity trading strategy based on fundamentals.

He has a professional knowledge of and experience in managed futures and FX strategies and other hedge fund strategies like Dollar Neutral Equity, Statistical Arbitrage, Global Macro. Additionally, he also has experience in marketing, client services, fund analysis and due diligence.

Prasanth received an M.S. in Statistics and Computational Finance from Purdue University in 2004 and an Integrated M.S. in Mathematics and Computing from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), India in 2002.
