Gautam Ghosh’s Summary

Gautam has over 8 years of experience in HR and Learning & Development in the Consulting and IT, BPO sector with firms like Satyam, Hewlett-Packard, Dell and Deloitte & Touche. His experience covers Knowledge Management, e-learning, Organization Development, Management Consulting,Training Management, Performance Management and Employee Relations.

He is an MBA in HR from XLRI, Jamshedpur, and has two and half years experience before that in hotel operations in ITC Hotels and pharmaceutical sales with Eli Lilly.

He is also a contributing editor of internet community Recruiting.com and was a visiting faculty for HR at Kirloskar Institute of Advanced Management Studies in 2006.

He has won the NHRD Network young managers’ paper writing contest in 1999, and has written and done case analysis on HR areas for Businessworld, Human Capital, The Human Facor HR.com and Cybermediadice.com as well as youth magazine JAM. He has also been quoted extensively by publications like Human Capital, Economic Times and Daily News and Analysis.
