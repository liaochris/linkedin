Gianluca Trezza’s Summary

I dedicated more than 20 years in the military and law enforcement field as Commander, achieving solid skills in resource organization, decision making and problem solving.
My educational background is in Law (admitted at the Italian Bar since 2004) and in Economics. During my career in Guardia di Finanza (the Italian financial Police), I always had operational commitments as chief of investigation units, involved in fighting a wide range of crimes, from international drug trafficking to white collar crimes.
Recently, my strong ambition and the need to face new challenges took me out of the Corp, to a different Country, with a very different culture.
I aim to continue my growth as an Executive, to obtain a top level position in the Industry or in a governmental organization.
I'm Italian native speaker, fluent in English and quite good in French.
