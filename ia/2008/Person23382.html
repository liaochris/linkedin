Bill Mack’s Summary

My professional career includes 13 years of law practice and 20 years as a business executive and owner. This gives me a unique perspective about what it means to be of service to a client as a lawyer.

I work directly and closely with my clients to achieve their goals. No intermediaries, no assistants. I know that it is my client's needs and goals that must be promoted through each action I take as their lawyer. My client's goals are paramount, and no matter how clever or forceful I may be, I don't succeed if these goals are not achieved.
