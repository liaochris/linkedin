Luigi E. Luce’s Summary

1991 to present - Delta Software srl, Managing Director & Founder
As Managing Director of Delta Software, he defined the mission of the company. This mission is to provide and implement consultancy activities and leading-edge software tools in the areas of Extended Supply Chain,BI and BPM.

1988 - 1991 Delta Group, CEO
The Group was dedicated to the supply and support of software solutions, consulting and software development services for production management in a CIM environment.
Responsibilities included the establishment of the strategic direction of this "network enterprise", developing marketing and sales objectives, organizing product support activities and co-coordinating inter-company activity as well as overseeing of all finance and administration.

1984 - 1987 - RTS Italia srl, Managing Director & Co-founder
RTS was the Italian subsidiary of a European multinational group dealing with IBM's MAPICS and SPECTRA. This was a very innovative financial system working under the fiscal rules of USA as well as the European countries. The company was founded to localize, distribute and support these products. Also served as a member of the RTS Group's International Management Committee.

From 1980 to 1984 managed all IT activities of CSA Costruzione Sistemi e Automazione srl, a company providing consulting services and software implementation for manufacturing industries.

In 1979 and 1980 worked for General Electric Information Services Italia, responsible for commercial activities involving large clients and associated special projects.

Between 1971 and 1978 worked in Honeywell Information Systems Italia first as Marketing Representative and then as Sales Manager in the manufacturing sector. In 1976, during the "Worldwide Campaign", was given responsibility for the Italian group working with multinational companies present in Italy who were already clients of Honeywell Information Systems in other countries.
