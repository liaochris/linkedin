Lisa Friendly’s Summary

I have combined my past experiences to be able to become a director for a non-profit software technology company - a dream job.

I manage Bookshare.org, the world's largest, Internet based, digital library for the blind and print disabled. To learn more, go to www.bookshare.org or www.benetech.org.

In the past I managed large tech pubs teams for software organizations. I have managed very large budgets, very large and successful projects and done very successful partnering with engineering and Tech Pubs.

I have 13 years experience as a Tech Pubs manager, director and VP. I created and managed the tech pubs dept for the Java project at Sun where I ran a highly successful and industry acclaimed writing team.

I was the managing editor for the the Addison Wesley Java Series books. I created the Series for Sun Microsystems and Addison Wesley. It was highly acclaimed and commercially successful for both Sun and AW.

Hired as VP of Instructional Media Development at Macromedia. Left Macromedia after 13 months when it was acquired by Adobe Systems.

I love managing people and software products where the tech pubs team is highly integrated with the engineering team.
