Basia Szkutnicka’s Summary

On graduating from Central St Martin's School of Art in 1988, having been described as 'A breath of fresh air' by Susie Menkes at my first and last catwalk show, following in the footsteps of Galliano and Flett, I threw caution to the wind with the launch of a deeply self indulgent, uncommercial womenswear label (for the glamorous lady) in 1989. Collaboration with stylists and fashion editors (Isabella Blow, Judy Blame, Zanna) on a number of unique photographic, film and music projects followed this flight of fancy.
A desire to design 'real' clothes resulted in a wide and varied portfolio of freelance activities including:
• Design consultancy
• Trend reporting
I continue to provide consultancy on select projects.
Involved in fashion education since 1992, starting as a visiting tutor, eventually reducing freelance commitments to run an extensive Fashion programme in Westminster, I have been a visiting lecturer and academic course consultant at various Colleges in the UK and abroad.
Currently Study Abroad Director at London College of Fashion, an associate lecturer (womenswear & footwear) on a number of programmes at the College as well as visiting lecturer on the Fashion Masters programme at Kingston University.
Having amassed a vast collection of vintage wonderful things since the age of 11, Basia's House - an Ebay site, (selling vintage ephemera, clothes & accessories and 'fabulous stuff' ) was launched in January 2006.
Forever the child who always wanted to know WHY? How do trends evolve? How do they happen? How are they kicked off? It is an investigation into intuition... instinct.... what inspires us? What inspires them? What will they want? Why? What's next? Opinionated amateur psychologist, anthropologist and still wanting to know ‘why?’ – my aim is to simplify, demystify & clarify fashion and above all - take risks.
The aim is always to inspire, excite and above all - wake up…..

Basia Szkutnicka’s Specialties:

Programme development, project management & writing. Planning, forecasting & innovation, lecturing in design (womenswear, footwear).
