Chris Cooke’s Summary

I am an Owner and Director of UnLimited Media, the integrated media, marketing and content company I co-founded in 1998.

In that role, I have founded and continue to co-publish each of UnLimited's media, including:

CMU Beats Bar - the insiders music website.
CMU Music Network - the UK music business' favourite information service.
ThreeWeeks in Edinburgh - the complete guide to the Edinburgh Festival.
ThreeWeeks in Brighton - the complete guide to the Brighton Festival.
College Media Network - the creative network for college media people.

I am also currently developing two new media platforms for UnLimited - ThisWeek In London and StandUpDownload.tv.

I also lead UnLimited's media, marketing and content services divisions, providing these services to a wide range of media, music, consumer and corporate brands.

I am also an experienced writer and editor, writing for all of my own media, and for other people's media, and as a business and copywriter.

I also work as a freelance content and communication consultant, providing my media, marketing and content expertise to companies, or other agenices, generally at a strategic level.

Industries I have an expert knowledge of: media (print, online and broadcast), marketing, PR, music, theatre, comedy, education.
