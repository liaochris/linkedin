Michiel Trimpe’s Summary

I am a driven, creative all-round programmer who puts his skills to good use working for companies that want to unlock and share information and enable new ways of interacting.

In my free time I am busy trying to wrap my mind around various programming paradigms (currently mostly functional programming, DSL's, interpreters, monads, purity, lambda-calculus and the likes in Haskell, Lisp and Erlang, just like everyone else).

My main goal is to find a way of semantically expressing and unify all (programming) languages and information and to create a rich multi-dimensional environment for exploring and interacting with these representations.

HOW YOU WILL DESCRIBE ME WHEN YOU HIRE ME

"He's better than the Senior who turned out to be not so spectacular and worse than the Senior who 'just knows everything' even though he can deliver software of slightly lower quality almost as fast as him and if it happens to be necessary he can do the same in another language.

If you can't keep him busy enough though he will start slacking off and spend far too much time reading programming blogs, improving the 'elegance' of his code, generalizing the solution and thinking of beautiful new ways of approaching the problem."
