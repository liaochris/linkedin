Nitin Uchil’s Summary

Understanding the domain, optimizing processes and creating knowledge-based infrastructures have been the hallmark of my career. Utilizing best-of-breed applications and infrastructure, I have successfully managed the creation of numerous Engineering Portals that effected cost and time savings in the storage and intelligent retrieval of information. This enabled process optimization, collaboration and coordination of activities in the design and development of new products in upstream Product Lifecycle Management.

No information system is complete unless the end users are trained and empowered to use them. In a series of versions going from web-enabling training courses to providing on-demand process driven "learning-objects" on the fly to the Product Development communities, I have successfully managed and implemented Computer Based Education course-ware at the Ford Design Institute. These involved training engineers in Systems Engineering Fundamentals, Design of Experiments, Robustness & Reliability, Quality, Statistical Processes and Six Sigma Procedures, and accounting for the diversity of the community: engineering analysts, supervisors, technical specialists and managers.

Currently, am consulting with an IT firm that deals with optimization of business processes in operations, building collaborative frameworks and commercial transactions.
