Eric Druckenmiller’s Summary

Currently I work as VP of Media at Deep Focus - a rapidly growing full-service online marketing agency with high-profile clients in the film, music, lifestyle and gaming industries. Our differentiation as a media discipline is to go beyond simply securing inventory. We channel multidisciplinary strategic, innovative and creative thinking to arrive at platform agnostic media solutions that create a legitimate value exchange between brands and their consumers - and connect consumers to each other. Such solutions helped us win OMMA's Media Planning and Buying Agency of the year in 2007.

My professional career in communications began in general advertising at DDB on Frito-Lay. Inspired by the creativity, freshness and entrepreneurial spirit of the Web, I changed my stripes in the late 90’s and started at i-traffic. In San Francisco I gained additional online experience at Left Field, Exile on Seventh , and SF Interactive working on clients such as AutoTrader.com, WebMD and Red Envelope.

Back on the east coast I landed on the digital team at MediaVest servicing Coca-Cola – successfully planning, implementing and maximizing the online marketing efforts of such brands as C2, Hi-C Sour Blast, Diet Coke and Sprite. This subsequently led to a role on an innovation team at SMG United (a division of Starcom MediaVest) on a number of P&G brands with the goal of driving breakthrough media-neutral communication strategies.
