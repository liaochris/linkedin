Chris Airola’s Summary

I excel in senior level technology management (VP, Director of technology, or Executive management), and am most interested in recently funded startups and emerging growth companies.

I offer a track record of driving ambitious projects to completion within fast-paced, creative startup environments. My expertise also encompasses product development, business/strategic planning, contract negotiations, and team facilitation.

Presently, I serve as CTO of HomeSavvi (formerly Alphabet Lane), and am a Board Member of an arts non-profit, Stronghold Productions. My contributions have included implementing technology infrastructure and initializing a project for Red Llama, a company I co-founded and was CTO, that became the company’s main focus. I assisted in conceptualizing Stronghold’s primary mission, to foster expression and community involvement in the arts. Additionally, over the course of my career, I co-founded and served as CTO of Static Factory Media LLC, developed and managed a wide variety of projects for the University of Washington Human Interface Technology Lab, played a key technology role in the success of multiple collaborative installations, and have been a contributing author to several publications.
