Bryan Pereyo’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    ACE Consulting Group, LLC

    (Public Company; 11-50 employees; ACE; Insurance industry)

    January 2003 — Present (5 years 10 months)

    Bryan Pereyo is the Founder & General Manager of ACE Recruiting, llc. In his role Bryan is responsible for the strategic leadership of the company moving forward in addition to managing all searches. He pioneered the "The Field based Experience Recruiting" search, a method of search where clients get a recruiter who has worked in their field and has firsthand experience of what these positions perform on a day to day basis.

    Bryan began his recruiting career at the age of 36 while he was growing our construction arbitration division. What began as a part time job would eventually produce one of the most respected A & E search consultants in the nation.

    After a successful career as a consultant/Construction specialist, Bryan founded ACE Recruiting. LLC in 1998. His goal was to build a highly respected search firm whose values centered on providing the best people to the best companies. The company has continued to grow since inception.
