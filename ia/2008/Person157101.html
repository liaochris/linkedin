Jim Rathe’s Summary

As President of BDA, and alliance partner for ThomasNet. Jim is a key contributor to the strategic direction of many manufacturers, wholesale distributors and service companies. These local, national, and global business to business firms range from 1 MM - 6 B in gross sales.

+Doubled EBITDA earnings for 10+ Companies in 2007-2008
+Strategically repositioned them within their vertical market segments.
+Implemented an integrated online - offline strategy to reduce the cost of customer creation and using web 2.0 to better serve their customers.

We work with business executives, venture capital firms, board of directors, owners, and presidents to protect & grow their business investment by addressing key business challenges:

-Executives who are struggling to maintain margin in the changing global economy and striving to keep their company out of a downward death spiral.
-The largest customers continue to expect lowered prices which lowers profit margins by invoking the threat of taking business elsewhere.
-The sales teams within these companies are struggling to get in front of key decision makers, engineers, or technical buyers forcing the cost of creating new customers to SKYROCKET!
-Business owners are continually barraged with information on software, new technology, internet jargon; feeling overwhelmed, left out and unsure how to effectively use it to increase customers, reduce cost and time from existing processes.
-Leaders who are not seeing their team work together on solving problems, instead are playing the blame game with customers stuck in the middle.

Jim’s success originates from his ethical approach to business. Our PURPOSE is working with PEOPLE on key business challenges, resulting in increased PROFIT for customers. Jim’s diverse background has afforded him the ability to understand high-performance business culture from the inside and provide non-biased insight to the business leaders from the outside.
