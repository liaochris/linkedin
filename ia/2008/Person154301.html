Publio F De La Rosa’s Summary

I attended the SAN JOSE IGNACIO COLLEGE IN THE Dominican Republic
Where I GRDUATED WITH A BACHELOR OF SCIENCE, I ALSO ATTENDED THE UNIVERSIDAD MADRE Y MAESTRA SCHOOL OF Medicine.
ADDITIONALL TRAINING
General business courses at the University Of Miami, Miami, FL.
My career started with my family business in the food distribution in the Dominican Republic.
In 1978 I moved to New York City where I worked for News World Communications in New York as their circulation manager.
In 1983 from New York, we headed to South Florida where I founded Eden Floral Farms, one of the top 20 flower importers in the USA.
While at Eden, we created the brand 5 STAR FLOWERS sold in some of the top markets by wholesale distributors of fresh cut flowers in the USA and Canada.
In 2005, my wife and I bought the oldest flower shop in the city of Hialeah while also working in Real Estate and the financial market.
Both my wife and I are business consultants, advising businesses how best to manage their assets and prevent unnecessary losses from legal matters or lack of business focus or purpose.
We also have taken custody in a farm anchored project, Scarlet farms, a global marketers of fresh cut flowers. With headquarters Miami and other offices in Colombia, Ecuador, the Dominican Republic and Costa Rica.
The project of the most significant importance is the Casimiro foundation; providing education for the up and coming new entrepreneurs to get the culture they need to lead society, where I sit as a board member.
We also advise the Women Federation for world peace, an organization dedicated to help women around the world to be dependable and efficient in their homes and in business management.
