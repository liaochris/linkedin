Fabian Lange’s Summary

My work within such global companies like Hewlett Packard and Nokia especially supported me in gaining the required skills, hard and especially as well soft skills to effectively participate in and lead commercial software development.
During my work in Nokia I got involved in a Multisite development of a Middleware product. With about 200 people involved in the development in 3 sites, the main focus is clearly on effectively coordinate the work. Using Agile processes and daily builds with automated testing and doing model driven development helped in coping with this. Using state-of-the art techniques as model driven development and automatic testing enables the creation of higher quality complex software. I was responsible for defining the architecture of a Web Framework and involved in joint design and architecture of a new network management platform of Cisco Systems and Nokia Siemens Networks.
My role in 7P Mobile brought me new opportunities to apply my skills in more customer driven and shorter projects.

I drive for continuous learning (I am Sun certified Java programmer, Java developer, web component developer & business component developer. I am also Zend certified Engineer for PHP5, as I also deal with projects demanding a smaller footprint than Java. In my free time I am using the symfony framework to create a PHP 5 bases community site)
