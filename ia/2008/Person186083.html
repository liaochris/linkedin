Tzu-Chiang Liou’s Summary

My research interest is open and collaborative culture, ranging from open source to open content, my approach is to understand the people, practice and facilitate the strategies into policy.

In the past few years, my major responsibilities are cooperating with existing partners and developing potential partnership via resource and relationship management both internally and externally. To develop tight relationship between different stakeholders, I work closely with local communities, open source service providers and system integrators. Most partners are in charged of web and information technology related position in their field, ranging from government, education, academic, community and business.

Major in social science aspect of technology, began as a software engineer, experienced in project management; I have strong passion about the interdisciplinary nature of IT/web industry, I’m anticipating to take challenges of all sorts.
