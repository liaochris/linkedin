Tom Muller’s Summary

Thomas H. Muller,

Tom Muller is a highly effective, customer focused executive with a career spanning 25 years in both Fortune 500 and Fortune 5000 technical businesses. Tom brings a wealth of experience to his job from a broad variety of roles in Sales, Technical Engineering and Business Development. Tom has demonstrated highly effective leadership having held Executive positions at several leading Telecommunication and Technical companies. He is presently Sales Director, Application Services at AT&T. Previously, Tom has been Director of Enterprise Sales at MegaPath Networks, a National Facility based Service proivider of fully managed IP network solutions and also had been Director of Global Sales at Savvis, a managed IT Infrastructure outsourcing company. Tom has extensive experience in WAN technologies including VPNs, VoIP and IP Convergence. He was a founding partner and Sr Vice President of Linesider Communications where he launched a National VoIP Product offering. At ICG Communications, he was Vice President of Field Sales Engineering and Training where he built out a National Sales Engineering organization. In his five years at Qwest Communications, he ran their 500 employee National Sales Engineering organization as Vice President of Sales Engineering. Tom also has held technical and Sales positions at MCI, Siemens and Digital Equipment Corp covering both direct and indirect channel environments. He also brings a strong end user customer perspective having been Director of Networking at FTD. Tom has a BS degree in Electrical Engineering from Purdue University and an MBA in Finance/General Management from Lake Forest Graduate School of Management. Tom resides in the Chicago area with his wife and four children.

Tom Muller’s Specialties:

Specializing in Telecom optimization consulting / Managed IT Infrastructure outsourcing as well as VoIP, VPN, WAN, MPLS, and IP PBX technology solutions.
