Roxanne Ravenel’s Summary

I teach job seekers to get better, faster results in their job search by first improving their job search skills.

I help clients to improve their resumes; strengthen their interview skills; make a powerful first impression; and develop and implement a systematic self-marketing plan so that they can compete in this increasingly tough job market.

Experience

During my experience as a recruiter with a global outsourcing firm I reviewed thousands of resumes and screened, interviewed, and hired hundreds of job applicants.

That experience prompted me to develop job search strategy and career development workshops and group programs that teach job seekers to establish their "A" game and put it to the best possible use in their job search.

job search strategies, career coaching, interview coaching, interactive training, authentic vocation, recruiting, human resources, talent acquisition
