Wasim Ahmad’s Experience

  • Web Editor

    Naples Daily News

    (Newspapers industry)

    February 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    My duties as Web editor include planning and assigning multimedia coverage in coordination with our visual director. I am responsible for monitoring and updating two sites, naplesnews.com and its sister site, bonitanews.com. I also train photographers and reporters on shooting and editing video, and train all departments in various aspects of our online system, Ellington. I also shoot and edit video on a regular basis and occasionally shoot photographs as needed.

  • Newsroom Online Content Producer

    Press & Sun-Bulletin (Gannett)

    (Newspapers industry)

    August 2005 — February 2007 (1 year 7 months)

    I was promoted to the position of Newsroom Online Content Producer in August 2006, 1 year after returning to the Press & Sun-Bulletin as a copy/online editor. My duties include monitoring and updating the Web site and planning online coverage across departments. I developed training guides and worked to host sessions to train staff in the Saxotech Online system. I also developed and assigned videos to staff. I also shot photos and videos myself as needed.

  • Copy Editor/Page Designer

    St. Cloud Times

    (Newspapers industry)

    August 2004 — August 2005 (1 year 1 month)

    I was a copy editor/page designer with the St. Cloud Times. I rotated between designing and editing page 1, nation/world, local, life and business sections. I was responsible for selecting content, planning centerpieces and stories, and putting my section together. I also wrote a monthly column and I was sometimes called upon to do reporting work. I was in charge of a special section on Pope John Paul II after his death, and also worked with local high school students to produce the newspaper’s Prep Press section. I also served as the graphic designer for the paper during the weekends, designing maps, skyboxes, and other graphical elements for the paper as needed.

  • Reporter, Copy Editor

    Press & Sun-Bulletin (Gannett)

    (Newspapers industry)

    September 2002 — May 2004 (1 year 9 months)

    I worked as a Binghamton University intern for two semesters, one on the copy desk and one on the metro desk. I returned to the newsroom as a Chips Quinn Scholar for summer 2003, where I worked as a reporter on the metro desk. I was later hired as a part-time copy editor.

  • News Editor

    Pipe Dream (Binghamton University)

    (Newspapers industry)

    September 2000 — May 2002 (1 year 9 months)

    Worked for Binghamton University’s twice-weekly student newspaper. My duties included writing, editing, layout and photo work. In addition, I trained new reporters.
