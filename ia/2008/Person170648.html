drs. Sabina L. Renshof’s Summary

Science to Business is a registered trademark of Bureau Bd: a strategic market/clientapproach for scientific institutes, academia, consortia. We work for scientific and applied scientific institutes, for example in materials and space research. Our customers want to develop new markets, find new clients and improve their commercial capabilities.

Bureau BD is part of MATISS, Materials in Space: the commercial agent network for the International Space Station.
