Phil Ransom’s Summary

How rewarding!
- to help churches determine what they need for their media systems,
- tailor them to local ministry settings,
- install,
- and teach church volunteers how to use their new ministry tools!

I enjoy seeing that look of satisfaction when everything begins to make sense: "This is going to be awesome!".

Church Multimedia began in 1999, serving small to mid-sized churches, choosing reliable, reasonably priced system components for consistent, trouble-free service. Perhaps that's why an increasing number of larger churches are contacting us; we major in Quality and Excellence, coupled with Integrity and Ministry Experience.

After twenty-five years of professional church worship and music ministry:
- I know the process church leaders must go through to acquire something new like a presentation or camera system.
- I understand how long it takes for major projects to come through the church decision-making process.
- I know the official and unofficial check-points and approvals that must be obtained, and
- I know how challenging it is to strive for excellence with stringent budget constraints.

I help my clients patiently work through the above so the project becomes reality. It's always worth the wait. Always.

Phil Ransom’s Specialties:

Heart for ministry, pastoral experience, budgeting, planning and strategy, solution-finding, communication and long-range planning skills for clients and churches. Adaptability and flexibility in installations, always with excellence and reliability as a key objective.
