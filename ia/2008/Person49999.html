Dennis Young’s Summary

I am the founder and President of TELL/COM RECRUITERS. My business has been built one professional relationship at a time. I have one of the largest authentic LinkedIn networks on the planet consisting of Management, Sales, Marketing and Engineering professionals from the IP Telephony, Contact Center, CTI, CRM, IVR, Speech Rec, VoIP, SaaS, Telecom and Data Networking industries. (Some fluff - but not much).

I am also the exclusive reseller for www.cardbrowser.com to the Convergence, Contact Center, Wireless and Recruitment and Search Industries. CardBrowser is a hidden Gem to find Passive Candidates. This online widget has gotten great reviews from the recruitment blogs and is one of the main tools I use to find passive candidates.

I am also a member The Candidate Concierge (www.candidateconcierge.com). This site is for Independent Recruiters to find great talent from other Independent Recruiters… a candidate MLS! It’s a place where I invite my best contacts to join for their benefit.. Candidates are searched by hundreds of other Independent Recruiters confidentially … without revealing their name and when there is an interested 3rd party I’ll contact them.

Finally, I’ve started two new groups on LinkedIn; Hi Tech Sales, Marketing and Management Recruiters AND Link –IT “Information Technology” Independent Recruiters so feel free to join if you are an Independent Recruiter. If you own a recruiting firm, if you recruit or if you are in the Executive Search industry you should let us know what types of positions you fill so others in the group can contact you.

Independent recruiters – if you want to search my network and do splits ... allow me to introduce you to The Candidate Concierge an exciting concept in Candidate Multi-Listing.

I'm here to do business so invite me to your network and let's make something happen!
