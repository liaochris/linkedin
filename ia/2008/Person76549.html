Hens Zimmerman’s Summary

Protools mixer/editor and audio engineer for post production television, theme channels and DVD production.

Audio and video podcaster, see http://soundsgoodpodcast.com.

Blogger: http://37hz.net.

Often played member of Podsafe Music Network.

Lots of experience with pro audio and video apps such as Protools HD and LE, Logic Studio, Soundtrack Pro.

School of Audio Engineering degree with Letter of Recommendation.

Wide range of experience in teaching and creating (e)learning material. My classes for DTS, my workshops in the RuG University Library, my lectures in Cheltenham (Online Learning Europe) and Enschede, my various code examples and more recently my instructional podcasts are all very well received. Some of the code examples and my JPG converter are still used every day by Toolbook II developers:

http://www.plattecanyon.com/downloads.aspx

Well versed in digital audio theory and information and communication technology. Always updating my knowledge about music, technology, internet, gadgets and audio hard- and software.

Hens Zimmerman’s Specialties:

Digital mixing and editing (audio and video), producing podcasts, audio recording, producing music for podcasts, cleaning up audio, computer programming.
