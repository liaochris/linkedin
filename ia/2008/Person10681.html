Andrea Garibaldi’s Summary

Working for Banca Intesa, the leading banking institution in Italy, I am currently the Accounting Manager, charged with producing the Profit & Loss statement (P&L) for Banca Intesa, Italy’s leading bank. One of the most challenging projects undertaken has been the introduction of IAS (IFRS) accounting principles, which resulted in the successful release of fully IAS compliant 2005 Financial Statements for Banca Intesa.
Previously, as Finance Manager in Intesa Sistemi e Servizi , the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) service provider to the Banca Intesa group, I had the privilege to establish the Finance function by recruiting the staff, choosing and implementing the IT solution (SAP R/3) and designing the operating procedures.
I started my professional career as an Auditor in Arthur Andersen, moving later to business consulting, where I specialised in Business Process Reengineering assignments. This has enabled me to develop a comprehensive understanding of how companies operate at all levels.

Andrea Garibaldi’s Specialties:

Merger and acquisitions, business processes analysis and improvement, International Accounting Standards, ERP deep knowledge
