Charles Rodgers’s Summary

For the past 15 years I have been involved in software development in one form or another. I started out as a developer and for most of the past 11 years have been in management. The management experiences I have had are varied and range from project management, management of consulting practices, development team management and IT management.

What I have most enjoyed about my work is building teams that can leverage business and technical skills to build processes and systems that create positive change in organizations. My current team is an example. My team contains systems analysts, business analysts, developers, project managers and the Compass Group EDI Manager.

I am particularly proud of my latest project. This project result was an application and processes that allowed a labor reduction of ~6,000 hours of manual data entry per quarter. In addition, the data being collected as part of project is more accurate and has been used in audits to collect over a million dollars in rebates to date.
