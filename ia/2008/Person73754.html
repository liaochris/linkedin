Guy Eckstine’s Summary

GUY ECKSTINE is a distinguished Music Industry A&R & New Media Executive, Record Producer, Musician and Composer, who has Produced and Executive Produced over 40 records. Four albums that Guy has Produced/Executive Produced have won GRAMMY AWARDS, including, "The New Standard", by HERBIE HANCOCK.

Guy he has worked with a stellar array of artists including Quincy Jones, Herbie Hancock, Chris Botti, Vanessa Williams, Alexandra Scott, Wayne Shorter, Brian McKnight, Brian Culbertson, Jeff Lorber, Hubert Laws, Incognito, Art Porter, Pete Belasco, Terrence Trent D'Arby, Gino Vannelli, and Frank Zappa, among many others.

Guy has held executive A&R positions at Virgin Music, Columbia Records, and Verve Records, and was also a New Media pioneer, as SVP/Creative Development, at the original MP3.com, where his responsibilities included the A&R and second stage development for MP3.com's , "Music and Technology Tour", with Alanis Morrisette, Tori Amos, TLC and Christina Aguilera.

Guy is currently a partner at Iconique Music Group. Iconique Music Group looks for business opportunities in the New Media and Entertainment Industry, with a focus on innovative technologies, artist solicitation, and unique content of iconic and historical importance. Successful recording contract solicitation includes, Incognito, ( HeadsUp/Concord), Julia Fordham, (Atlantic), Carol Welsman, (Savoy), Def Jam Remixed, (Verve).

Guy is the Co- Founder and former CEO of MIAATV.COM. In addition to acquiring talent and interviewing over 250 artist and music industry legends for MIAATV.com, including artists QUINCY JONES, STEVIE WONDER, CHRIS BOTTI, KENNY "BABYFACE" EDMONDS, and JOSS STONE, he negotiated and implemented strategic partnerships and affiliations with major brands such as Atlantic Records, Gibson Musical Instruments, Hal Leonard Music Publishing, A.E.S, NAMM, SXSW, IAJE, and Digital Hollywood.

Guy Eckstine’s Specialties:

Guy has extensive expertise in the entertainment industry including, Music 2.0, New Media, A&R, Record Production, Music Publishing, Artist/Brand Development/Integration/Solicitation, Business Development and Web based TV Programming and Production.

Guy has an extensive Rolodex from A-Z, covering the Film/TV/Music/New Media and Advertising worlds. As a longtime music industry A&R executive, and former protege of Quincy Jones, Guy has Produced/Executive Produced 4 Grammy winning albums.
