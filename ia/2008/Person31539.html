Cary Kozlov’s Summary

I guess it started when I managed my own band back when I was 15 years old. Because we were too young to drive, our parents had to drive us to our gigs and rehearsals. First show business gig...my twin cousins' sweet 16 party at The Palmer House in Chicago. It was a terrifying experience as we played our entire repertoire...a grand total of seven...mostly Herb Alpert & The Tijuana Brass and Baja Marimba Band tunes. Let's just say we weren't about to open for the Stones. Things haven't really changed much since then except my parents aren't around to drive me to my meetings these days.

Today, thanks to Ric Beddingfield at The Light Company who back in the early '80's threw that first screenplay at me to read over the weekend, I'd like to think that I can help some really talented people achieve their goals as screenwriters. (Incidentally, just for the record I really hated that script Ric...LOL!)
