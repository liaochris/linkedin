Cheryl Carnal’s Summary

My professional experience ranges a span of almost 30 years, focusing in the areas of technology (web, network and software). I began in the back-end technical arena and have moved to the front-end of the business specifcially in areas related to complex technical sales support, and implementation of complex integrated solutions. Over the last several years I have focused more on the front-end in the role of a Snr. Business Systems Analyst, where I am enjoying immense success.

I have worn many hats since technology first ventured into the business world, and it has given me a unique insight into issues that the lines of business face.
