Cheely Ann Sy’s Summary

During her eight-year tenure in executive search consulting Cheely Ann has shaped the research strategy on hundreds of engagements, and has expanded the research function into a center of knowledge management. She has introduced numerous innovative research techniques to further enhance candidate generation as well as intellectual property development by streamlining both data management processes and market intelligence gathering. She has been instrumental in managing Diversity Programs and Candidate Mapping Projects for market expansion studies for clients.

Prior to joining RWD, Cheely Ann was the Head of Research Strategy at SeBA International, LLC, a retained executive search firm with offices in New York and London. Cheely Ann led the research effort for the recruitment of senior strategy, marketing, finance/risk management, and quantitative analytical professionals for Fortune 100 clients in the financial services and technology industries. Previously, Cheely Ann was the Head of Research at Mayer Search, Inc., where she orchestrated research and market outreach across the US, Europe & Asia in the pharmaceutical & financial services industries.

Cheely Ann is an active member of the Association of Executive Search Consultants and The Professional Risk Managers International Association. She resides in Bergenfield, NJ with her husband and two daughters.
