Joshua Thompson’s Summary

The best way to summarize my professional experience and goals is passion. I put passion into everything I do. I have been a web-head for more than a decade know. Completely fascinated in all areas of the internet. I especially have a strong interest in search and distributed computing.

I am also a huge fan of internet marketing. The life-cycle of a website is very interesting. From idea to concept to coding and publishing. Then promoting, monetizing, reaping rewards. Then reinvesting, growing, nurturing. So many people throw up a site, scrape content, slap Adsense on it and leave it to die. I would rather have one truly unique site, one with character and an active user base, than a 100 MFA's.
