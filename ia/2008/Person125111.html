Matthew Haver’s Summary

I’m a Senior Technical / Executive Recruiter. I specialize in hard to find and high level technical and supply chain/logistics positions. I truly enjoy learning about technology, helping companies grow by finding them outstanding people, and helping candidates by finding them good homes. I feel that my love of technology and helping people and companies is what makes me successful. I also have a diverse background and extensive training including personal experience in RF and Microwave technologies in the Marine Corps, Operations Management experience in the Marine Corps and at the iStore, and AIRS training and certifications. I employ a variety of methods to locate quality candidates including but not limited to direct sourcing, networking, online networking, niche and community sites, internet searches, blogs, internal database searches, and job board searches and postings.
