Katarina Ivanovic’s Summary

I have been working in Renaissance Capital in Moscow, for the last two years. I am a member of the RBS Renaissance securitisation team. My responsibilities include originating, structuring and executing securitisation of different asset classes for top Russian clients. The structures we are working on are quite complex and very innovative for the market.

I came to Russia straight after finishing MBA from Brandeis International Business School.

Before going to the US I worked as a Debt Analyst in the Serbian Treasury, which was the first sovereign Treasury ever established in Serbia. Most of the projects we did as a team was pure pioneer work and enabled me to learn a lot.
It gave me a unique insight into public sovereign borrowing and the system of government securities issuance. I also contributed to creating several models for restructuring defaulted debt and directly participated in an effort to settle Serbia’s bilateral debt with China, decreasing the public debt by $60 million.

During my MBA, I interned with Primary Energy in Chicago, where I was a member of a team, which developed a model for a project buyout, which increased the company's earnings by $8 million per year.
