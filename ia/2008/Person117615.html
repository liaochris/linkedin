Electronic Sports – emotions unleashed!

There is no doubt that digital entertainment and computer games rules among all kind of entertainment activities ever invented by human being in his history. Not only in scope of popularity but also as a fastest growing branch of entertainment industry. Computer games become most profitable part of it. The Electronic Sports League with its 830,000 active league participants and more than 5000 matches per day is the World's biggest computer gaming league. The league is subdivided into the ESL Pro Series (professional league), the ESL Amateur Series (amateur league) and the ladder system, which allows players to enjoy random games without further commitments.
