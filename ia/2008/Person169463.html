Sarah LaLiberte’s Summary

Sarah manages all the communications and public relations activities for Aware, Inc., a 120-person public (NASDAQ: AWRE) high-tech telecommunications and biometrics/imaging solution company in Bedford, MA. Her professional background includes more than 12 years experience plus education as a PR and marketing professional. She is a highly motivated energetic self-starter in marketing and business, with particular focus on brand and product marketing management, marketing/communications, and public relations and corporate communications. In addition to her work as a PR/marketing pro during the day, Sarah enjoys spending time with her family, camping on Lake Champlain, fishing (bass and ocean), personal and professional networking, new technology "stuff" (currently into Web 2.0 and social networking), and blogging (http://spunkymommy.blogspot.com and http://chickonaboat.blogspot.com).
