Richard Jordan’s Summary

UPDATE IF YOU KNOW ME: Back in the Bay Area. Left IncaGold in March 2007 to found Stiki Digital, to focus on our new content marketplace, with first product line Stiki Digital Promotions live now. Just got funded by prominent Silicon Valley investors and plan on formal launch in January, though we are live for customers now.

IF YOU DON'T: 12 years total experience, the last 8 years focused on high-tech early-stage start-up companies, in sr. mgmt. positions, to help drive them to the next stage. Experienced the dot.com bust, the subsequent downturn and VC crunch, but maintained a steady series of roles with escalating responsibility. This has enabled me to return to founding my own start-up which I am currently growing.

ACHIEVEMENTS:

• Founded Stiki Digital March 2007.
• Designed digital distribution system for IP content, in 2005. Built prototype and sold technology to UK-based public company Dec05.
• Launched the leading ASP product in the mobile workforce management space, taking product to the number one spot in the US market (now has over 50% market share), built channels from scratch, personally closed the first 200+ deals.
• Co-founded White Rose Entrepreneurship program to place UK science and technology undergraduates into internships with high-tech Silicon Valley start-ups, UK government and VC firm 3i.
• Founded InfoVault, Inc. in 1999. Named Entrepreneur of the Month in the July 1999 issue of European “Real Business” magazine, which profiled InfoVault
* In June 2000 I was featured in a BBC documentary on UK start-up entrepreneurs moving to Silicon Valley, for which I spent several weeks being trailed by a camera crew (which is an interesting experience in itself!).
• 1997-1999 First management role: ran Europe & Africa Service Desk for engineering giant; built team of 30; turned around first-call fix 17%-70%+, saving $8-10million per year!

Richard Jordan’s Specialties:

Confident, self-driven and well-rounded individual, with proven leadership abilities and interpersonal skills, allied to a bright independent and creative mind. An effective, motivational, people manager and excellent communicator.

Extensive start-up experience, building on strong enterprise track record. Flexible, goal-oriented and able to successfully take and manage risk. Proven relationship builder with success taking product to market.
