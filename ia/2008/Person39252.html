Colin Kinghorn’s Summary

Colin is a seasoned Senior Manager, of exemplary character, who has over 17 years experience of effectively leading, motivating and directing professional, highly skilled staff as well as administrative staff.

In the workplace, Colin is the type of "leader" that is not afraid to roll up his sleeves and get involved with the team at all levels. A key characteristic of Colin’s leadership style is that he does not ask people to do a task that he would not be prepared to do himself.

For the last 7 years Colin has lectured on business subjects to a wide range of students. He has spoken on Fraud, Corporate Governance, International Accounting Standards, Enterprise Risk Management, Data Protection, Strategic Planning and Management Reporting at events organized by various professional and academic groups at Conferences and Training events in the United Kingdom and Thailand.

Colin has previously held roles as an HR Manager and also a Project Manager.
He is a former Chairman of the Institute of Internal Auditors in Scotland, where his leadership, direction and vision led to the Institute’s profile and membership reaching an all time high.

Colin is a former Board Member of a UK Limited Company where he also sat on the Audit Committee.
