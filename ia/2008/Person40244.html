Cory Garrison’s Summary

From a brand management standpoint, the rubber hits the road with employees. They are taking your story to the streets and connecting directly with customers and prospects.

Organizations that have a great story to tell do a better job attracting the right employees. Employees who are well-equipped to tell a story, tell it best.

MergeToday brings these two critical factors together through cultural assessments, executive recruiting and brand management services.

"Cory Garrison has quickly comprehended the power that purposeful action has on a brand. As a former business owner, he understands the marketing challenges clients face, often with more opportunities than resources. And as a student of improvisation, he learned valuable business skills: listening, finding agreement and building relationships quickly and honestly.

He sharpened his people skills in a sales and management role in a large financial services business and as managing partner in a family-owned executive search firm. Along the way, Cory developed a sense of empathy that makes him an outstanding advocate for his client and enthusiastic about their success.

Cory is a keen observer of people, with the ability to identify strengths and weaknesses and the discernment to help people focus on the former."
