Jeff Ziegler’s Summary

Owner of Euro-Contrat specialist provider of contract IT personnel across France and the EU. Also Managing Partner of Certus IT Contractors, France.

Recently CEO of Specialist Search Limited. Former UK Director of Infinity Search, Inc. Previously experienced interim manager turned CEO/Managing Director of Interim Management Service provider in the UK. Previous roles include Director of Strategic Marketing (ICL-Tplc), CEO (Newlands Relocation), Marketing Manager (Industrial Purification Systems), Managing Director (AllWorld UK Ltd.) and numerous others.

Other professional experience includes Marketing Manager (ICL), General Manager/Marketing and Business Development Manager (FCB Associates), Executive Director - National Security Advisory Board (Council for Inter-American Security) and others.

Jeff Ziegler’s Specialties:

From our base in France, we run a specialized practice that supplies long and short-term IT contractors for businesses across industry sectors. My recent recruitment practice is Specialist Search, affiliate of Infinity Search and Exclusive UK provider of the Worldwide Executive Search Network.

We find our clients the right people: particularly specialist skills ... so when you or your clients need to find specialists, call on me to help find great people!
