Cherilyn T. Cepriano, JD, CAE’s Summary

Cherilyn Cepriano serves as Executive Director of the Emergency Department Practice Management Association (EDPMA). Cherilyn manages the daily activities of EDPMA, coordinates the organization’s legislative and regulatory advocacy efforts, plans and executes the association’s annual meeting, and facilitates the development and implementation of strategic planning to enhance the effectiveness of organizational governance.

Cherilyn’s experience in health policy reaches back into her undergraduate years at Smith College where she focused her education on the development of public health insurance programs and completed successful internships at both The White House and U.S. Department of Health and Human Services. Cherilyn parlayed these unique experiences into an advanced position as Legislative Associate for Health Policy with the National Governors Association (NGA) in Washington, DC where she managed advocacy strategies on state and federal health policy issues including Medicaid, the State Children’s Health Insurance Program (SCHIP), public health, and HIPAA health information privacy.

In addition to possessing a bachelor’s degree from Smith College as a double major in Government and Anthropology, Cherilyn received her juris doctor from George Mason University School of Law and is in hot pursuit of her certification as an association executive with an emphasis on association governance and business management. Cherilyn is a former member of the Board of Trustees of Smith College and a member of the American Health Lawyers Association and American Society of Association Executives.

Cherilyn resides in Northern Virginia with her husband, Paul Prados, an attorney in private practice, and their pugs, Pumpkin and Rascal.
