Kiat Lim’s Summary

Kiat started solving creative problems since the year 2000, providing his clients with unique solutions across both online and offline platforms. In 2003, Kiat revamped Nanyang Polytechnic's corporate website with a face lift and enhanced the template system, easing the overall management of the entire website. Following this success, he was subsequently commissioned to revamp Nippon Paint's International Website (www.nipponpaint.com), sporting a whole new look over its previous design.

His projects in the Commercial Interactive Applications category also won Bronze and Silver awards respectively in the 2003 and 2004 Crowbar awards organized by 4As Singapore. Recently, Kiat single-handedly designed the overall user-experience of Hardwarezone Community Networks, greatly improving and enhancing the overall portal aesthetics, functionality and usability with Web 2.0 features.

Apart from work, Kiat has been contributing back to NYP through regular short 1-hour sessions on the topic "Delivering Your Online Presence" which provides students with answers to the what, why and how of launching their own websites.

With an intimate knowledge and experience in the media, design, and business online presence, Kiat is able to provide sound solution for any business problem, especially in areas of strategic planning, internet marketing, information architecture, usability, web standards and user interface design.

Something else about him...
Kiat has always been misunderstood as the oldest person amongst his peers. Reason: He admits he's a full-time workaholic. Pure dedication and patience is his belief, also the sole reason to why he looked and acted a little older, too. No worries, the rest of the team is dissuading him to perform old acts.
