John Pollard’s Summary

John Pollard is US Account Executive for Infinity Diamonds bvba, a fine-make diamond manufacturer in Antwerp, Belgium. Infinity specializes in diamonds crafted with AGS Ideal light performance & superideal cut-precision and sells through select retail partners in Europe and the USA. Previously Director of Education for Whiteflash.com, John functioned as company spokesman, developed marketing strategies, conducted staff training and managed customer service. He was a guest speaker on the internet phenomenon for the American Gem Society at their JCK workshop series in Las Vegas. John holds degrees in science, education, music and a GIA Diamonds Graduate diploma and is currently pursuing his graduate gemologist's degree. Formerly a music educator in Texas, groups under his direction earned two Texas State Titles, a National Percussion Championship and the Sudler Shield of Honor. John assisted with MTV’s Super Bowl XXXVIII Halftime in 2004 and instructed BOA’s 2005 Tournament of Roses Honor Band. He is a two-time recipient of the HEB Education Foundation’s Award of Excellence and continues as a clinician-consultant with the Yamaha Corporation of America as well as a freelance music adjudicator & student leadership specialist in his spare time.

John Pollard’s Specialties:

Public Speaking, Writing, Educational Presentations, Curriculum Development, Diamond Grading & Analysis, Gemology, Music Instruction and Performance (degrees in percussion and voice), Specialization in percussion teaching, composition and performance.
