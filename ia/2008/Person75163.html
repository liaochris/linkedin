Harsha Anand’s Summary

Experienced Business Consultant who has offered advisory and implementation services to international Gas, Electricity & Water utilities. In depth knowledge and experience of UK Gas Industry, having been part of many de-regulation, unbundling and other industry changes.

Currently I lead a team of business consultants, managing the people, solutions, customer relations and sales. In addition, I also undertake consulting assignments based on the need.

* UK Gas Distribution - Solution Integrity Manager, SCADA, Business Apps and Control Room Ops setup

* UK Gas Distribution - advise on their strategy for the forthcoming Traffic Management Act

* National Utility - Business Process Modelling and supporting their Change Management activities

* UK Water company - evaluate & recommend products in CRM, WAM, Scheduling, Dispatch, Field Force & Doc Mgmt

* UK Gas Transporter - Lead Analyst of the Gas Transportation Programme. Architected solutions & processes for Meter Point Creation, Emergency Services, Metering Competition, AQ Review, Customer retention & RGMA
