Guy Sie’s Summary

I've been trained as a marketeer with a communication research background, but technology is my passion. Prior to communication studies I endeavored to become an electrical engineer and a computer scientist, but neither study included the social elements I craved in my education. Now I get the best of both worlds.

My niche is the overlap between these two worlds, the point where technology and marketing meets. How do you use technology to create new possibilities for marketing? And how do you use marketing to sell new technologies? A world of possibilities is opening up and I want to be at the frontier.

Starting in 2008 I'm looking for an internship at a company in the Netherlands where I can perform the necessary research to obtain my MSc degree. If you're interested, feel free to contact me!
