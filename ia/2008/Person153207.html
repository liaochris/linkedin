Philip Ramsey’s Summary

I have over 18 years of experience in the Information Technology arena. I am a project manager, but I specialize in streamlining processes and workflow to make future development cycles go smoother. This requires me to be both rigid and flexible in the handling of day-to-day situations. I am very strong in working with my teams and I'm very good at protecting the team from outside influences. I make it a point to get to know all members of my team, even those who don't report to me, I am an excellent mentor and accept mentoring when offered.

Over the past 10-12 years, I’ve managed development teams and products from concept to delivery, I’ve maintained systems, assisted in developing and enforcing quality assurance plans. I’ve assisted in the design of applications for data delivery, data security, end-user tools (such as saved searches and bookmarks), and alert software to assist in monitoring a 24/7/365 hosting service. I've also assisted in the design, implementation and management of a internet hosting service.

Until recently I was also working as a developer as well as a manager, so I understand first hand how development operates and what sort of protection and assistance they need.
