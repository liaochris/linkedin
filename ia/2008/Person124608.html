Matt Ahrens’s Summary

Matt Ahrens, founder of Solution Points, is an organizational development consultant specializing in experiential training, executive coaching, and conflict resolution with nearly fifteen years of corporate experience in organizations such as Levi Strauss & Co., Hewlett Packard, Intuit, Inc., and Pacific Bell. He has also worked with non-profit groups as well as state and local governments.

Matt holds a Master of Business Administration degree in Strategic Supply Chain Management from Arizona State University and a Bachelor of Science degree from The University of Wisconsin, Madison. He is also a graduate of the Helen Palmer – David Daniels Enneagram Professional Training Program, Ginger Lapid-Bogda Enneagram in Business programs, the Coaches Training Institute (CTI) CoActive Leadership program, CTI’s CoActive Coaching Certification program, and Starfish Training’s mediation program.

Matt currently serves as president for the Association of Enneagram Teachers in the Narrative Tradition and the Programs Co-chair for the Northern California International Enneagram Association as well as a board member and volunteer for the Peninsula Conflict Resolution Center.

Colleagues and clients alike find Matt personable, insightful, wise, compassionate, articulate, and, at times, humorous (but don’t tell him; we don’t want it to go to his head!)
