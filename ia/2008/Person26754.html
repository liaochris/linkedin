Brent Campbell’s Summary

I’m engrossed with building a real understanding and applicable business model for acquiring, retaining, and communicating intelligently and appropriately with customers in the Web 2.0 world.

Marketing services companies have done a lousy job keeping up with the times, as they appear to be insistent on stuffing the traditional advertising model into the interactive model.

We have an opportunity to become far more efficient and effective. More importantly, “consumers” won’t be as likely to tune marketing messages out if the messages they receive are relevant to their needs and wants. Yet alarmingly few companies in our industry seem concerned with tackling this issue.

While the changes brought on by better technology mean drastic change for the way marketers attract and communicate with new customers, in the long run it’s good for everybody. The sooner we face it and work through it, the better.

It’s an evolving topic, and one I struggle with constantly. I am eager to engage in a dialog with anyone interested in talking about it.
