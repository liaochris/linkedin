Chris Ebert [4000+](chris@alphasynergies.com)’s Summary

When I envisioned an organization to provide career services to professionals, managers, and executives, I had several very specific goals in mind. I was seeking qualities that I had been unable to identify in any of the service providers currently in the market. The principles on which AlphaSynergies was founded include:

* A "pay it forward" approach to servicing people in their careers, even when there is no direct or indirect link between that service and business revenues.

* A process of introducing people to that service which does not rely on pressure, manipulation, codependency or other slick sales techniques. Instead, we promote a gentle introduction to the concept of career development (whether it be job search, career transition or coaching), an introduction to the style and personality of the coach or coaches who would actually be providing the services, and an opportunity for the potential client to probe, test, and question the people and processes to be employed.

* A service that allows people to resolve the trauma of past employment experiences, identify and maximize their value statements and create meaningful opportunities to present them to managers, executives and other influential people.

* An introduction through free services that allow for the building of rapport and trust, including the ability for any prospective client to experience the service prior to making a commitment.

* A business model that has integrity, not only in the offerings, processes and relationships employed to deliver the service, but in the application of its philosophical model to its own business operations, both on micro and macro levels.

The creation of this culture is not a destination, but a continual journey. It is one that I require of myself and all my associates. It is not easily measured, but our primary yardstick is the success and satisfaction of our clients, our friends and our greater network.
