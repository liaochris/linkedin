Robert Dorfman’s Summary

Do you need to turn a strategic direction into a measurable outcome or get a complicated program or project started? I solve these kinds of problems. Most recently, I spearheaded complex programs for Earthlink, where I was Director of Strategic Initiatives. In the past three years I built, and deployed three Voice over IP products, and oversaw the integration of NewEdge Networks. My strong organizational, leadership, and problem-solving skills will help you meet your financial and market goals!

* Works with C-level executives to create roadmaps to translate strategy into executable roadmaps
