Thomas "itsTripple" Rose-Bolden’s Summary

Thomas Rose-Bolden (AKA ‘Tripple’), is a marketing professional that has always had a passion for music, design, marketing and technology.

He has over 16 years of successful career experience in the entertainment, food and beverage, and technology industries. Durring this time, he has worked with such companies as Time Warner, NBC/Universal, Yahoo, and Google to launch consumer and B2B products and services. He is also responsible for creating and launching Informer Hip-Hop magazine (Circa 1992-1999) and Supreme Hip-Hop Magazine (Circa 1999-2001).

During his career he has held the titles of Art Director, Senior Marketing Manager, and Creative Director
