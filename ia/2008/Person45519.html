Dave Hein’s Summary

Based on experience in a multitude of facets within the Automotive business including the management of retail dealerships, Internet service companies, technology and consulting firms as well as industry training and coaching, Mr. Hein provides insight, support and direction to early-stage technology companies that provide unique and innovative growth opportunities to manufacturing, distribution, retail and financing sectors of the industry.

Dave Hein’s Specialties:

A leader focused on execution, Mr. Hein has developed and guided organizations of all sizes in the automotive arena in the areas of Business Development, branding, identity creation, mergers and acquisition, contract negotiation, sales and business leadership coaching and funding.

  • President & CEO

    AuctionAuto.com

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Internet industry)

    January 1994 — February 1999 (5 years 2 months)

Having developed a network of over 7,000 dealership clients, Everything Automotive evolved into AuctionAuto.com, which enabled the first vehicle transaction online in 1998.

    In early 1999, Mr. Hein sold AuctionAuto.com in the attempt to continue to grow the company and it's services in the struggling Internet automotive marketplace.
