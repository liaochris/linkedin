Brian D. White’s Summary

Here's the deal...

I am an open networker and I want to be your recruiting connection to the world's leading creative memory company - Qimonda.

I work hard to build pipelines for any and all positions that support our 300mm DRAM computer chip design and manufacturing processes. This means I recruit for all types of engineering positions (electrical, chemical, industrial, material science, etc.), technicians (semiconductor, former military), and all the support groups (finance, accounting, purchasing, human resources, facilities) and many others.

I'm working on my eleventh year here at the Richmond, VA site so I can pretty much tell you where your skillset will fit in with this high performing team. But Qimonda is a global company, and if your skills are directed toward either design engineering, or sales and marketing I can also point you to any of our world class teams in either in the U.S. or abroad. With our technical advantage and lean manufacturing facilities, Qimonda has positioned itself for tremendous growth.

So, if you or anyone you know may be interested in working with our creative, passionate, and fast team, or would like to be a part of my personal and professional network please contact me directly at:

briand.white@qimonda.com

I'm looking forward to hearing from you.
