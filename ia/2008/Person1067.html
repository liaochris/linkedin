Aaron Salmon’s Summary

I'm a jovial lad who possesses passion for both technical growth and creative expression. I've been into Web Design & Development professionally since 1999. I'm an independent contractor based out of Chicago, Illinois and my focus is on architecting, designing and building hand-crafted websites and applications.

My goal is to use my experience applying creativity, design principles, aesthetics and development skills to create professional and innovative products.

When not sitting in front of my Mac; my life in Chicago is an abundance of friends, culture, the arts and creative inspiration…I can often be found on my bike, or out enjoying music, frisbee, politics, pool, coffee, cheap beer and gin martinis.
