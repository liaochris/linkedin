Rory Brown’s Summary

Currently Managing Director at Incisive Media - a 2,000 strong and fast growing international business media company backed by Apax.

I run the largely US-based interactive marketing business - websites (Search Engine Watch & ClickZ), conferences and exhibitions (Search Engine Strategies), which Incisive Media acquired from JupiterMedia in August 2005 for $43m.

In 2007 I also assumed responsibility for the Information portfolio of VNU (including IWR magazine and the Online Information show - a huge exhibition at Olympia for information professionals) following its acquisition by the company.

My background is in international marketing, specialising in the business and professional media industry. Throughout my career I have worked on some of the largest and most successful brands in this sector.
