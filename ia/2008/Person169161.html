Subject matter expert for topics related to IT infrastructure, applications and integrations for Telecom service providers.

Experience of working in depth with wireless and fixed line service providers for IMS,UMTS/WCDMA,GSM,IP,ATM,SDH/PDH ,Optical Technolgies and consulting on design and architecture of OSS solutions.
