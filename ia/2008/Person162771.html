Robin Cole-Hamilton’s Summary

I am currently leading a ground-breaking project in Syria to create a major discovery centre and national non-formal learning programme for young people. The aim is to encourage creativity, critical thinking, insight and social engagement amongst 5 to 15 year-olds.

I work as an independent consultant, drawing on the practical experience of understanding, managing and transforming public sector cultural institutions over many years. I have also worked in the private sector for telecoms and computer services companies.

My past positions have been both creative and managerial, most recently heading 400 staff as Head of NMSI Trading, the commercial and enterprise arm of London's Science Museum.

I am a strong ideas person, problem-solver, strategic thinker, shaper, project leader. Able to blend public service ethos and not-for-profit strategy with sound commercial sense.
