Mari Kono’s Summary

I am keenly interested in using the medium of fine art to help bring positive social change and the ideal of world peace through popular media. In the past 10 years, I have worked in the non-profit and education sector, directly working with at-risk youth and their culture as it stands affected by pop culture. Working directly with these young people and seeing first hand, their individual progress was rewarding but definitely not fast or effective enough. Being able to reach only a small number at a time was also frustrating. I see a need in mass media to provide positive, life affirming works in television, film, gaming, internet web media and educational materials that would appeal to these young people. My aim here is to find like minded people involved in mass media, the arts, and high volume production/business to joint venture/partner so as to produce high-impact, high quality materials/media that would combine sound business & high profit with a way to inspire young people to believe in themselves, become self aware, participate in one's community and find healthy ways to build self esteem.

Mari Kono’s Specialties:

Art making, specializing in collage art combining digital high-tech with low-tech, hand-made craft , High end photographic abilities through experience in the commercial arts field, Many years of experience in the non-profit sector working with at-risk youth and using arts education as a way to bring about social change.
