Hamid Alipour and Tess Strand Alipour’s Summary

We are a young, creative, married couple who met and developed both our personal relationship and our company while living in India, starting in late 2004. We fell in love with eachother and then fell in love with the idea of crafting a viable and completely fresh online business model out of literally nothing more than a truly third world internet connection and two very well-traveled laptops.

We provide personalized, high-end custom programming and design services, as well as virtual business assistance, to select clients who understand that business, particularly online business, has shed the old-fashioned model of success in favor of a more unique and unusual approach. Our cache of clients spans a wide range of industries and includes: veteran consultants in various industries, energetic life coaches, actors and media personalities, luxury retail and small business entrepreneurs, high level IT execs gone solo, jewelry designers, virtual assistants, photographers, authors, and more.

We have successfully managed our little company from it's early days until the present time from various apartments, houses, and hotel rooms in no less than six countries, including Hamid's native Iran.
