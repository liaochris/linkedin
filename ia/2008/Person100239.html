Julie Montgomery- jmontgomery@plexent.com’s Summary

Please feel free to contact me directly at jmontgomery@macrecruiting.com or jmontgomery@plexent.com

A respected industry leader in the IT recruitment industry, she has 12 years experience in both executive search and corporate human capital management, with leading firms such as Richard, Wayne and Roberts; Paranet; Sprint Paranet; and Sprint Enterprise Network Services.
During her tenure with Paranet, Ms. Montgomery built, trained and managed the 50 person recruiting organization that grew the company from approximately 250 technical consultants in 1994 to over 1,700 in 1997. In addition to her expertise in executive search, interview process development, candidate sourcing techniques and employee retention, Ms. Montgomery is a certified Behavioral Interview instructor. She authored the recruiter training class entitled, "Direct Sourcing in a Crazy IT Market,” as well as the People Capability ModelTM, an employee development and selection tool.
