Chin-Hock (C.H.) Low’s Summary

I have been involved in 6 startup/early stage companies since 1983. I have launched or was involved in the launching of over 60 major software products/projects/business lines. Currently I am focused on web-based technology oriented companies.

I founded Orbius in 2007 to provide a social media solution that better meets the needs of brands and marketers and to solve the issue of users outgrowing existing social communities as their life evolved to include additional sides and they developed multiple category and asymmetrical social relationships.

Chin-Hock (C.H.) Low’s Specialties:

Design, build and operate software-oriented businesses/products/companies especially small/early stage/startup or just a vague idea stage companies in launch/relaunch/growth situations.

Perform technology and business due diligence for VC investors and mergers/acquisitions.
