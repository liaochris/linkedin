Michael Bolanos’s Summary

Entertainment Drive® is the exclusive US representative for award winning Danish Candles from Blindkilde, website is at www.DanishCandles.com; the US Patent Pending and EU Patented Danish Candles use stainless steel sticks and glass oil lamps for a unique and innovative atmosphere. If you'd like to see the Danish Candles or hosting an event that would benefit from their presence please let me know.

eDrive joined forces with CBS Meteorologist Bryan Norcross to form Hurricane Enterprises LLC to market and promote Hurricane Almanac, Bryan's book from St. Martin's Press. www.HurricaneAlmanac.com contains vital information and preparedness products and is open to new ideas and partnerships.

eDrive created and produced Official Websites for celebrities including Cindy Crawford, Daisy Fuentes, Marilu Henner, Richard Simmons and Britney Spears.

Prior to launching eDrive, Michael was active internationally in entertainment as a manager, producer, director and performer. CompuServe tapped Bolanos as Entertainment Consultant for the H&R Block-owned service. Beginning in 1994, eDrive attracted over 1 million members and was 1st to offer digitized entertainment from Universal Pictures, Walt Disney Pictures, Warner Bros. and over 100 other media companies. The William Morris Agency acquired an equity minority interest in eDrive, as did CompuServe.

eDrive's CyberStudio was 1st to introduce celebrities including Tom Hanks, Mick Jagger, Jenny McCarthy, and Sylvester Stallone live online. eDrive was 1st to present live backstage online coverage backstage at the Oscars®, the Daytime, Sports and Primetime Emmys® and the Tony® Awards.

Michael was on the Board of the Friends of the American Theatre Wing, and helped to bring the Artists Rights Foundation, ASCAP, and the first Muscular Dystrophy Telethon online. More information is at www.MichaelBolanos.com.

Michael Bolanos’s Specialties:

Michael Bolanos is President & Chief Executive Officer of Entertainment Drive®, "Your Backstage Pass to Entertainment,®" an award-winning production company that creates and manages online communities around entertainment properties and personalities. An innovator and a showman, Michael Bolanos was one of the first to envision the potential of combining new media technology with entertainment, and with an amazing team created and creates some of the industry's most successful productions.
