Michael Dortch’s Summary

I have three decades of experience translating technologies and concepts into business benefits.

I conduct and write research intended to help IT executives craft and execute more effective strategies for engaging and collaborating with business counterparts. This helps to ensure that every significant IT initiative supports business goals and drives demonstrable, measurable business value.

I advise IT and business executives in ways intended to help them to develop and deploy effective strategies for successful initiatives focused on IT and business infrastructures.

I work with established and emerging IT vendors, and their channel partners and field forces, to help them more closely align their offerings and outreach with "real-life" customer requirements, and to achieve innovative, effective differentiation. I act as a "user ambassador" to vendor product development and marketing teams, helping translate user concerns into more successful solutions and strategies.

Michael Dortch’s Specialties:

IT and business architectures, IT-business alignment, branding and identity, marketing and sales strategies, alliances and partnerships, writing and editing marketing collateral, news, and opinion, public speaking, forging effective personal/professional relationships.
