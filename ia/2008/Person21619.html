Ben Loos’s Summary

I'm passionate about technology and to be a bit more specific the integration of traditional IT and AV technology as the next logical step in convergence. This is why I started my own company (www.mii.nu) focussing on AV and IT consultancy where I support clients in getting the right AV and IT infrastructure for their needs. In looking at the needs I like to think outside the box to come up with surprising concepts that fulfills the need.

Also love people management in an challenging international environment. To create and manage a great multicultural team delivering an outstanding job as value added partner for the business.

Ben Loos’s Specialties:

People management in national and international environement
Creating a value added partner for the business from a regular IT department
Creating smart solutions that are an integrated approach of all possible areas (mobility, IT, Telecom and AV)
IT Mergers and Acquisitions
Creating an integrated IT/AV Strategy
