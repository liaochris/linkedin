Sanne Roemen [sanneroemen.nl]’s Summary

How do technological development and the social web impact our life and work? Collaborating, doing business, interacting with others, working, communicating, learning.. How do we use technology to our advantage?
With my mix of social studies, business school and information engineering I know about people, technology and doing business. I think business is about standing out, creating value and being proactively transparent. I love to talk about how current innovations facilitate and stimulate authenticity.
