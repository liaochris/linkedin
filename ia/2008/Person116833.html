Luis Salazar’s Summary

Luis has over twenty years of experience in international sales and marketing and general management, and a successful track record launching new ventures in the software and services areas.

He is currently a General Manager at Microsoft, focused on the SaaS and advertising business areas. Luis also works as Entrepreneur in Residence for Voyager Capital, serving in advisory roles for portfolio companies in the areas of digital marketing and operations and evaluating potential investments on the West Coast. Passionate about leadership and execution, he mentors senior managers at Microsoft and professional athletes in Washington.

Luis has vast experience overseeing worldwide operation, business modeling and deal negotiation, marketing, product management and planning, customer acquisition and retention, social/viral media, UX, channel models, and advertising business strategy.

At Microsoft, Luis co-founded the Office Live division in 2005, leading a worldwide marketing and engineering team through three successful product launches. Office Live now counts 1+ million customers and is the first business-focused service from Microsoft that is monetized through advertising revenue.

Luis led Microsoft’s worldwide middle-market sales from 2000 to 2004, during which the billion $ business experienced steady double-digit growth. In 2004, he was appointed Marketing General Manager for MSN Services for Small Businesses. He started his career with Microsoft in 1997 at the Venezuelan subsidiary, and prior to joining Microsoft; he held leadership roles with Xerox and ABB in Latin America (1988–1997).

Luis enjoys spending time with his wife and their eleven-year-old son. He is an avid photographer, a writer of children’s books, and a jazz drums musician; all are passions shared with his son and wife. Luis is also involved in several nonprofit ventures in the Seattle area.
