Shivangi Yadav’s Summary

Shivangi has more than a decade of experience of working in media, which includes both in print and online medium.
For the last two years she has been heading The DQ Week, a weekly regional newspaper that is printed from Delhi, Chennai, Mumbai & Kolkata. CyberMedia India LTd, the foremost IT niche publications media house in the country, brings out The DQ Week. The paper covers various issues that will affect the channel partner community in the IT space, the people who form the mass of the IT business in India.
Before her stint with Cybermedia, for a year she headed, Trav Talk a publication that dealt with the happening and the issues plaguing the travel industry in India. As part of the job role, she routinely covered segments like airlines, hotels, foreign tourism boards and government tourism departments.
Her first stint in journalism was with The Times Group. Started off in Delhi Times, the city specific supplement, with The Times of India she later moved to the main paper heading a project for the upcountry market.
At the dawn of the dotcom boom in the country she moved to Indiatimes and spent a couple of year heading various verticals under the parent umbrella dealing with issues from fitness, health, spirituality, fashion, lifestyle, astrology to conducting celebrity live chats.
Shivangi did her graduation in Journalism from the Delhi University.
