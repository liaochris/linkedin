Frank Lempert’s Summary

Over 25 years of servant leadership experience, developing others, while creating a legendary service experience for both internal and external customers. 14 of those years serving with Starbucks Coffee Company. The last four years as the Regional Director of Operations for the state of Tennessee, encompassing 90 stores, 9 District Managers and over 150 store management partners.

Goals: 1) Ensure that My Second Half of life is built upon Significance. 2) Work and grow with a company that shares my values and passion for creating a positive and uplifting work environment that produces superior results, while delivering legendary service. 3) Use my strengths to leverage the most out of my team, my customers and my business unit. 4) Develop others to achieve their full potential, helping to create a pool of talent that can be exported to other business units and projects. 5) Have Fun!

Frank Lempert’s Specialties:

Training, developing leaders, recognizing potential, event planning, servant leadership, customer focus, creating a positive environment that is built on respect and dignity.
