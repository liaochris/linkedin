Elaine L. Barnes’s Summary

Awarded as the 2001 Energy Champion for K-12 Schools by the US Department of Energy, Elaine uses her skills as a scientist, educator and leader to develop enable school design teams to improve the way we build schools for Ohio's kids. Known for her passion and personal approach to projects, Elaine is an engaging leader who enables people to work together to their full potential.
