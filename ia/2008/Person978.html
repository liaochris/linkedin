Aaron Kirk Douglas’s Summary

Manager, Aaron Douglas Enterprises, LLC

Currently associate producer of the documentary VEER, about the bicycling scene in Portland, Oregon.

Co-producer of MONSTER CAMP, a documentary about live action roleplaying in the U.S. (2007) world premiere Cinequest (San Jose) March 2007, additional screenings: Independent Film Festival of Boston, Jacksonville (Fla.) Film Festival, Seattle Int'l Film Festival, Rome Intt'l Film Festival, Evil City Film Fest (NYC East Village), Calgary Int'l Film Festival, Atlantic Film Festival (Halifax, Nova Scotia), Swansea Bay Film Festival (Wales), Connecticut Film Festival, Culcalorus Film Festival (Wilmington, NC), Miami Underground Film Fest (2008), Wild Rose (Iowa), Santa Fe, Northwest Film & Video Festival, Icon Fantastic Film Festival (Tel Aviv), among others.

Theatrical presentation in Portland Oregon at the Hollywood Theatre June 29-July 5, 2007.

Awards: Audience Award, Best Documentary, Cinequest (2007). Jury Award: Best American Documentary, Rome International Film Festival (2007). Reviewed by Variety, April 9, 2007 "Pic is a guaranteed fest crowd-pleaser. . .as amusing as it is Endearing."

Producer of Oregon's first nonprofit fiscally-sponsored narrative film project, FREEDOM STATE, that played at seven film festivals around the U.S. in 2006 including Cinequest, Smogdance, Longbaugh, Santa Fe, Annapolis, FAIF, Moondance, Sunscreen, Rome International, and Eugene Film Festivals. (www.freedomstatethemovie.com)

Co-producer of THE MAN YOU HAD IN MIND with James Tuchschmidt, that had its world premiere in Oct. 2006 at the Portland Lesbian & Gay Film Festival. Signed in 2007 to an international broadcast agreement in the middle east. (www.manyouhadinmind.com). Theatrical release in Portland Ore. at the LivingRoom theatres June 22-July 5, 2007. Licensed for three year international broadcast on the YES Satellite network in Israel, the West Bank and Gaza Strip from 2007-2010.

Aaron Kirk Douglas’s Specialties:

Teaching classes in screenwriting; ability to teach in Pre-pre production and pre-production; all stages of production and post-production management of narrative film and documentaries; film marketing, especially through the festival circuit; public relations for films
