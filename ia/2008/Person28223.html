Brook Ellingwood’s Summary

I am a media communication professional with depth and breadth. My knowledge and experience, combined with an insatiable curiosity and quick learning ability, allow me to develop new skills, and/or develop them in others as needed. My view is holistic, and I synthesize disparate needs into unified solutions.

I am results-oriented and have had demonstrable success delivering excellence in numerous areas including:

* Information Architecture, including Data Structure, Usability, and Standards
* Personnel management and team development
* Media Product Management
* Editorial content development, writing on disparate topics for a variety of audiences

I am interested in strategic opportunities to define new features and improve end-user experience, through applied information architecture or product management. I approach even the most technical aspects of my work with creativity and user-focus.
