Peter Meijer’s Summary

Professional Experience

More than thirty years experience as civil and industrial management engineer in the field of supervision and management of dredging and marine projects and organisations involved in the execution of projects.

Re-organised various organisations, set-up marketing information systems and experienced in public relation affairs, setting up local organisations abroad and dealing with other cultures.

Lived abroad for over thirty years and has experienced working in Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Nigeria, Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, and India.

Have had dealings in respect of marketing and acquisition in:

EUROPE, The Netherlands, Belgium and Germany, MIDDLE EAST, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Oman, the United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, Iraq and Iran, AFRICA, Nigeria, Gabon, Ghana, Conakry, South Africa and Swaziland, FAR EAST, Hong Kong, China, Taiwan, Korea, Japan, Philippines, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Brunei and Papua New Guinea, AUSTRALIA, Victoria, New South Wales, South Australia and Western Australia, SUB CONTINENT India (Tamil Nadu and Gujarat), Sri Lanka and Maldives

Special assignments:

Has been the Company Representative of Volker Stevin, during the period as Head of Commercial Department, in industrial platforms.

As Head of the Commercial Department has led the reorganisation and implementation of a management information system operating on a main frame database.

As Head of Commercial Department gained experience in corporate internal and external communications.

Has been a Company’s representative on government trading missions to South Korea, Indonesia, the Islamic Republic of Iran, the Sultanate of Oman and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
