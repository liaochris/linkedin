Dirk-Jan Bode’s Summary

Business Professional with over 25 years Auditing, Consultancy, Sales and General Management experience in various industries. Focuses on delivering value to the customer, no technical solutions, no features or functions. What drives the business of the customer, where can added value be created, that’s what counts to me. A working experience of 10 year at one of the Big-4 auditing firms has been a useful foundation to really understand the business and the associating business processes of the customer.
