Mike Segelhorst’s Summary

Mike has 7 years of experience in the staffing industry. He joined MATRIX in early 2007 and was integral in the opening of the St. Louis office. All of Mike’s staffing experience has been spent working in St. Louis. Prior to MATRIX, he was at Application Engineering Group(AEG) as their VP of Operations. In this role, Mike was responsible for growing AEG’s IT staffing and project divisions. Prior to entering the staffing industry in 2001, Mike spent 8 years working as an independent SAP consultant and 7 years as an IT Manager in the automotive industry. Mike graduated with a B.S. degree in Math and Computer Science from Southern Illinois University.
