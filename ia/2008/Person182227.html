Steve Visser’s Summary

As a personal and comprehensive LPL financial planner, my goal is to provide my clients with financial peace of mind about their future. I help my clients grow and protect their money so that they might achieve financial security for their family. My recomendations revolve around the strategy and financial plan that we create together. By involving you in the process, I help you take ownership and control of your financial life. We first explore your financial goals and what is important to you about money. We then create a financial plan and strategy that, if implemented, will increase your odds of accomplishing your goals. We then monitor and track our joint progress towards that goal, keeping you in control and informed along the way. By partnering with my firm and I, you are relieved of the investment minutia, so you can focus on the big picture, your career, and your family. Every day, my firm and I strive to demonstrate our dedication to your financial peace of mind.
