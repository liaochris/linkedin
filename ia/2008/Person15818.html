Ariston Collander’s Summary

My professional experience has been a wild ride so far. I started working for my parents doing general computer related data entry and minor support. As a voracious reader I gained knowledge as quickly as I could. A chance meeting with a help desk employee for a local ISP privide me with the opportunity to get my foot in the door. At the ISP, I quickly moved from help desk to DSL Administration followed by a short stint as the Help Desk Supervisor.
At that point I decided to move on where I was hired on as a desktop support techician for Cox Computer Service, a small IT consulting company based in Rancho Cucamonga. Once again I learned as much as I could providing support a wide variety of clients in fields ranging from simple desktop support and printer maintenance, to such advanced topics as Active Directory FSMO role placement and Exchange 2003 Outlook Web Access configuration.
Here at PCV Murcor I have been managing both the virtual infrastructure running on VMware ESX 3.5 as well as the Citrix infrastructure which was recently upgraded to Presentation Server 4.5 with the aid of Agile360. My other roles involve testing new applications, managing our vendor contacts, and maintaining the overall stability of the back-end systems. I work closely with the CIO to determine best practices with regard to the infrastructure and recommend upgrades and improvements as needed.
I have implemented SharePoint Services, Microsoft Office Communications Server, Blackberry Enterprise Server, and the Spiceworks Helpdesk system for managing helpdesk trouble tickets. In addition with taking escalation issue I provide recommendations and assistance with any other area of the business I can get my hands on whether that be in training, project management, or marketing. I am always to challenge myself to get the answers for those in need.
