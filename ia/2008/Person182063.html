Steve Perlinski’s Summary

I am approachable to all and consider every request carefully. In doing so, I follow certain rules:

1. If I do not know you and you ask me to add you to my network, I would do so only after getting to know you. I usually request a phone conversation or a meeting if appropriate. I think you would like to know me as well, so this is to our mutual benefit.

2. If you ask me to forward a request to one of my connections, I will be much more selective as I do not want to cause undue burden to my friends and colleagues. When you forward a request to or through me, please be sure to provide me with information that lets me know why I should endorse/support this request, idea or introduction. I am not asking you to disclose any confidential information, just what would make sense for me to fairly evaluate your request. For example:
* You may include information about your relationship with the person you are trying to reach through me.
* If you are referring someone else or another’s idea; can you vouch for the person/idea?
* Any other information that will assist me in determining the suitability of your request.
