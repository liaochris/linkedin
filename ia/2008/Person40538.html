Cindy Piron’s Summary

At Beeline, we find the best recruiters in the country. With extensive industry and corporate experience, the Beeline team ensures that your company will have the most current and cutting edge perspective on recruiting and retaining employees.
We work hard to bring you the premium "passive job seekers" for hard-to-fill disciplines and requirements such as Information Technology and professionals with high-level security clearances.

Beeline offers competitive salaries, benefits, retirement, and a variety of opportunities for our employees. We have needs varying from recruiting coordinators to staffing managers and everything in between when it comes to recruiting.

Do not hesitate to call (703-673-1523) or e-mail (Cindy.Piron@BeelineRPO.com) if you or someone in your network is interested in exploring exciting new recruiting opportunities.
