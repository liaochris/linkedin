Joseph Matheny’s Summary

Some may say that my career spans varied fields and that is true enough, however I would add that I have a multi-discipline approach that is necessary in the modern age. As Robert Heinlein once said, "Specialization is for insects".

Some of the things I have achieved as an inventor, creator, marketer, product manager, CEO, CTO, writer and artist include: Playing a role in establishing and evangelizing standards and practices such as CD ROM, PDF, DVD, XML, RSS, Podcasting, ARG and digital video. I am the inventor of oneQube. I am a published author of screenplays, white papers, technology, sci-fi, marketing and gaming books.

"Joseph Matheny possesses a very rare combination of creative vision, authentic leadership, and pure guts. If you have a compelling new product opportunity to bring to market, I can't think of a better field general than Joe. " Lex Sisney, CEO ELC Technologies.

Joseph Matheny’s Specialties:

I have also been the executive producer and originator of the Santa Barbara International Film Festival Podcast the first film festival to podcast from the event, and the ABA All Star Game podcast. I am currently one of the two founding members of the Greylodge Podcasting Company, a Independent film review site covered on MSNBC , Wall Street Journal and Boing-Boing. I am also the executive producer of Alterati, an on-line culture zine.
