Jayadev .’s Summary

I’m a Photographer, Video Editor, and a Director/ Film Maker with an industry experience of about 8 years. I graduated from Vancouver Film School, Canada in April 07, and I have worked in one of the most successful TV production companies (SHAW CABLE VISION) in lower mainland Vancouver as Photographer, Videographer, Video Editor, and as Show Director.

Presently in India working as Director/ FilmMaker, Editor, Photographer, both in Indian motion picture industry, as well as in India television industry. I already worked in couple of Tamil (Indian regional language) features, Telugu (Indian regional language), short films, and Musiv Videos, in various departments both in India and in Canada.

Working and getting exposed to the world as a Multi-Media/ Photographer, Video Editor, Director/ Film Maker, and as an Entertainer is my goal.
