Scott Paterson’s Summary

I created The Saab Network on the Internet as a mailing list in the fall of 1988. In 1994, the collected discussions became the basis for a database driven web site for Saab problem solving. Saabnet.com has had over a million users, over 7 million page views per month, over 1.8 billion hits, over 400 million page views, and contains millions of pages of Saab info.
