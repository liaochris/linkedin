Jason Claxton, P.Eng.’s Summary

Established in 1994, The Comtech Group is an emerging leader in the Information Technology & Engineering Staffing industry. We specialize in providing contract technical staffing & direct-placement recruitment services to world-class organizations, to facilitate their growth, provide flexibility, and enhance their competitiveness. Our clients consist of organizations in the following industries: Energy & Utilities, Technology, Telecommunications, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Government, and Healthcare.

The Comtech Group is growing and evolving! If you have experience and a proven track record in the IT and/or Engineering Staffing industry, contact me to discuss how you may fit within our team. We are looking for talented, dynamic and passionate new team members in Southern Ontario (Markham, Windsor), BC (Vancouver), Alberta (Calgary), Quebec (Montreal), Texas and North Carolina. Whether you have 1 year of experience in the IT and/or Engineering Staffing industry looking for a chance to accelerate your professional growth, or 10+ years of experience looking for a senior leadership role, I welcome the opportunity to talk and explore your suitability for our team.
