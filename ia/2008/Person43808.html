Dan Kelly’s Summary

Executive with experience in consumer products and media, PC and video game software, and online commerce. I have turned around failing business units, held senior-level staff positions in large media companies, and built new operations from the ground up.

I am particularly interested in the transition of "old world" businesses into new business models (e.g., online gaming, digital item commerce, user generated content, etc) and in making it safer, easier and more efficient for members of online communities to transact with each other (e.g., RMT, real-money trade, c2c commerce).
