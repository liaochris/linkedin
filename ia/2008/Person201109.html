Bill Zahrte’s Summary

Most reciently devoting my interest to business development, "Touch Point Communication". Touch Point communication is a new dynamic process that can command results in a dealership from every department. Touch Point has the ability to deliver service opportunities that were in the past considered lost customers. In the sales department the Touch Point process has the ability to bring customers back that have been forgotten and thought they purchased elsewhere. Once the process is in place it is allmost on automatic pilot. Service attendance is higher, repair order gross sales increase and lost service revenue is totally under control. In sales . . . walk-outs turn into return buyers.

My automotive past experience includes dealership management and also includes over fourteen years of marketing a success winning acheviement with a major marketing firm, the Half-A-Car Group. Also most reciently receiving an Eddy award for the development and design of the Loyalty Umbrella project with First-Marketing. I have created, introduced and trained specialized programs for Automotive Manufacturers, R.V. and Boating companies such as: Ford Motor Co., Lincoln, Mercury, Red Carpet Financing, Import Manufacturers, G.M. Dealers, Chrysler and Lexus to name a few. References gladly furnished.
