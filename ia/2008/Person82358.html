Jaime Kosofsky’s Summary

BRADY & KOSOFSKY, PA

BOUTIQUE REAL ESTATE FIRM SERVICING THE CAROLINAS.

Brady & Kosofsky, is a boutique real estate firm which provides our clients with complete coverage of the Carolinas. Our practice is borne of the current economic situation as it relates to the housing and credit crisis.

REO Seller Representation. Brady & Kosofsky, PA., represents numerous Asset Management Companies, Lending Institutions, Title Vendors and Investors in the National Default Industry. Our team members each have a minimum of four years of experience in the highly specialized marketpalce.

Title Services. Brady & Kosofsky, PA., provides title searches, and product throughout the states of North Carolina and South Carolina. Our network of real estate professionals and contractors, must undergo a a screening and training procedure to make sure that they are properly trained, and that our clients recieve a quality product.

Closing and Escrow Services. Brady & Kosofsky, PA. , has the technology to do closings, settlements and/or escrows, in house, out of house, or remote. Our technology, and experience has allowed us to develop a highly efficient system for closing utilizing email, and telephone.
