Bharat Potdar’s Summary

My personal funda is "Build an Accessible Innovation". Looking at all the great innovations done by man, and all said and done I only know from what I've seen on the idiot box, I wonder how many of you have truly been able to use "great innovations". Which is why the Internet inspires me so much.

My first startup gave me the tag of an entrepreneur in the family circle. When I started as a young boy (rather a "kid", coz i loved milkshakes more than cocktails back then), I only knew of one thing: "the drive". Now I've managed to model it into something absolute (obsolete?) like "Find Problem > Innovate > Implement". But it all means just one thing. Maybe we all love fancy business models; to use at work everyday.

I am the CEO of eight companies at 24. The first one started at 16. I did my first trade when I was 7.
