Vagelis Theocharis [info@logicomm.gr; theocharis@bpm.gr]’s Summary

Dr. V.Theocharis has excellent academic qualifications, scientific background and deep knowledge of the Greek and foreign environment, related to both the industrial and service sector.

Dr. V.Theocharis has worked in the past for multinational firms in Greece and in Europe. He has been teaching Operations Management, Strategic Management, Leadership and Entrepreneurship, and Change Management for the Universities of Wales, Hertfordshire and Teesside. Dr. Theocharis holds the position of Public Relations Executive of ILME (Hellenic Institute of Logistics Management – member of the European Logistics Association). Dr. Theocharis was holder of the General Manager position of the the largest Medical Center in Crete, "Cretan Medicare S.A." He is the proprietor of the consulting firm LogiComm and Senior Consultant in Business & Project Management S.A.
