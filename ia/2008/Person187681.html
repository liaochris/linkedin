Waylon McCann’s Summary

"Since 1978, Waylon T. McCann has been an artist who creates visual, audible, and tactile art. Gifted with a wonderfully unique sense of humor, and a keen eye for subtlety Waylon has won people over wherever he has traveled with his unique approach to entertainment, conceptual design, and teamwork. If you find his wit is cutting, you'll find his passion is even sharper."
- M.F Ambiguous, author

A message from Waylon McCann:
'My interests all involve the arts, and I have been trained in, privately apprenticed, or taught myself many skills. I have done layout and design work creating promotional materials for various all-ages club nights in Surrey and New Westminister British Columbia, and for night clubs in Vancouver. I have done design work, photography and writing for various musical acts and, DJs. I have an extensive knowledge of the Canadian Rock, Industrial, Gothic and Alternative music and social networks and I have been actively building rapport and relationships in these networks since 1998. Through my efforts as a public personality, promoter, net worker and facilitator, I have met a great many of talented people. My focus, hard work and determination will help the people and groups involved, associated, employed or promoted by my company and its products, to succeed at our goals of being self sufficient and lucrative business persons in the entertainment industry. As an artist and entertainer myself, I can understand local demands for professional, legitimate, and image building exposure.'
