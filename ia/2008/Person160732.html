Rick West’s Summary

Technical Recruiter / Account Manager for Open Systems Technologies.

Open Systems Technologies (OST) has been involved in the recruitment of technologists for New York, Philadelphia, Virginia, Washington DC and other centers around the world for over 17 years.

OST is a $300 Million a year, privately held IT staffing company. Working out of our Philadelphia regional office, I am charged with both developing new business opportunities for our firm as well as locating domain specific technology candidates.

OST currently enjoys National Preferred Vendor Status with such firms as JP Morgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, AIG, Citigroup, Lehman Brothers, Merrill Lynch, Fannie Mae, Cigna, IBC, Comcast and Lincoln Financial.
