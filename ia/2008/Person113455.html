Libby Wilson’s Summary

As Lib Inc I'm packaging up my international and domestic marketing experience to help New Zealand technology companies get to market with the right products, fast and have a lot of fun along the way. I believe a marketing led organisation is focused and energised with employees, customers, suppliers and sales channels aligned to the same vision and working for the benefit of the organisation. It’s a great place to be.

My experience comes from 10 years working in a pre-sales and product management capacity in Europe for telecommunication carriers and ISPs CompuServe Network Services, WorldCom and PSINet, and 5 years working in marketing capacity in NZ for network infrastructure company Vector and hi-tech innovator Endace.

I have managed a pan-European product portfolio, run corporate marketing and internal/external communications programmes, developed product strategies, rolled out product development processes, written business cases, delivered training courses to partners, sales and pre-sales staff, prepared government funding submissions and award applications, coordinated external PR agencies and managed web sites.

Libby Wilson’s Specialties:

Marketing and product strategy; product portfolio management; brand, marketing programme and campaign management (internal and external); PR.
