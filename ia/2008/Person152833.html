Cris Popenoe’s Summary

Cris Popenoe is a creative executive and entrepreneur with a proven track record as a digital media strategist, content production and distribution specialist, and partner and deal maker. Her experience spans the distance from idea to consumer, and includes strategy, development, production, publishing, marketing and sales. She is a problem solver, able to execute rapidly and successfully with limited resources.

Cris has a vast network and long-term relationships with well-positioned global industry leaders and talent in all facets of the entertainment business in LA and New York as well as Europe and Asia. She has the talent, contacts and the credibility to develop and execute both large and small scale media ventures and the strategy and tactics for the deployment and development of content and businesses across multiple devices in a manner best suited to each device.

Cris excels at being part of the creation of a new initiative and growing it, and of leveraging and extending brands and products. Her particular area focus is the development, production, and distribution of interactive media with transactional and emotional hooks across multiple platforms, including online and mobile. Cris has extensive viral marketing and Web 2.0 experience, particularly with online video, widgets, new IP, and social networking, and with UI and action triggers.

Cris has 30 years experience in managing media businesses. An entrepreneur as well as an executive, Cris has had both a professional and personal focus on healthy living, eco-friendly goods and services, personal growth, and East Asia. Over the last decade, Cris has executive produced many award-winning digital media projects, including online videos, games, applications and widgets, and DVDs. She's also been responsible for their distribution and audience development.

Cris Popenoe’s Specialties:

Emerging and digital media including online video, mobile, iTV, VOD, HDTV; Web 2.0; viral marketing; online video advertising; leisure and casual games; widgets and mini applications; home entertainment; media acquisition and production; deal making and representation, partnering and connecting; business launches.
