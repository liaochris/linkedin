    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    HomeWell Senior Care provides in-home, non-medical personal care and companionship for seniors or home-bound individuals. HomeWell is an international home care agency providing hourly and live-in care. We provide assistance with the activities of daily living, which includes bathing, dressing, toileting, transferring, bowel control and feeding. A need for assistance exists when the client is unable to complete an activity of daily living due to cognitive impairment, functional disability, physical health problems or safety. HomeWell is licensed by the Virginia Health Department and can conduct business in the following counties and cities: Fairfax, Loudoun, Prince William, Richmond, Henrico, Hanover, Chesterfield.
