Debbie Zhou’s Summary

Debbie Zhou was an Integration Manager for Shell China focusing on M&A projects. She had integrated 6 plants and 1 R&D center and also worked on several partner related programs including share buybacks, exclusivity agreements and corporate communications. Prior to Shell, she was with AsiaEC (acquired by Office Depot), a leading Chinese retailer where she started two successful business units. Also, she worked in sales with Siemens & IBM.
