Dylan Parker’s Summary

Deloitte Bio

Experience:

Dylan is a consultant in Deloitte Tax LLP’s Global Trade Solutions practice. Based in San Francisco, he has several years of experience on helping U.S. clients enter the China market, product classification, business analysis, and export development. Prior to joining Deloitte, Dylan worked with the U.S. Department of Commerce in Shanghai, China. He provided a wide range of services, including foreign market research, entry strategy, advocacy and dispute resolution, and international marketing. Dylan directly led matchmaking projects for U.S. clients that identified and introduced potential agents, distributors, trade associations, and key officials in the Shanghai market. Before the U.S. Commercial Service, Dylan worked with The Export Consortium (via the Clemson University Center for International Trade), a boutique South Carolina-based trade consultancy. There, he conducted international market research, business process analysis, and product classification. Previously, Dylan worked as an intern with the South Carolina Department of Commerce’s Office of Foreign Relations and Export Development.

Education:

Dylan earned his Bachelor of Arts from Clemson University in Language and International Trade, specializing in Economics and Mandarin Chinese.
