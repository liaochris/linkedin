David Foster’s Summary

Strategy & Marketing Consultant with experience in three key areas.

First, has been at the forefront of the Brand Experience Industry for the last 13 years, being a Board Director of BFP Momentum and forming the Really Big Promotions Company in 1995, both of which companies were sold to McCann Erickson in 1997 to form the basis for Momentum in Europe.

Later exited to form Raisley, a strategic Brand Experience agency, in 1999, which was sold to Impetus Communications in spring 2007.

With each of these agencies, worked closely with client brands to design strategies to bring their marketing to life, allowing consumers or customers to experience each brand in such a manner as to encourage them to buy in to it, as opposed to having it sold to them. Whilst running Raisley, worked with over 80 brands and instigated over 25 million individual face-to-face communications.

Second, as an entrepreneur, understands first-hand the challenges that face SME owners and managers, which helps to develop approaches and types of communication that gain cut-through. The experience of running companies employing up to 20 office staff, 60 permanent field staff and 3,000 freelance staff per year gives a far greater range of skills than a standard consultant, making for richer and more implementable strategy.

Third, completed an Executive MBA from London Business School in 2003, with exposure to a wide range of challenges and industries and giving an invaluable network of fellow alumni upon which to draw. During the MBA, specialised in Consultancy and Change Management.

Since spring 2007, has been consulting to a diverse range of enterprises, developing cutting edge business and marketing strategy as well as helping to solve more straightforward business challenges.

David Foster’s Specialties:

Development of business & marketing strategy. Has wealth of experience in the development and implementation of highly targeted and synergistic 'customer-led' marketing activity.
