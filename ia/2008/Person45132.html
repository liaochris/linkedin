Darryl Dioso’s Summary

Resource Management Solutions Group is a Toronto-based full service company specializing in the Recruitment and Training of Sales Professionals.

Typically, companies we help are disappointed with their sales recruitment process. They diligently place ads, carefully screen resumes, schedule and conduct numerous interviews and yet still can not find TRUE sales performers.
We focus on sales recruitment and training and therefore really know what to look for. We get beyond the ability of the candidate to "talk a good talk" with our screening process that involves role plays, testing, reference checks and other tools.
