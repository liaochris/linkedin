Frank Grochocki’s Summary

* Past projects involved laser-based gas sensing, integrated optical device research, military applications, medical instrumentation, spectroscopy, free space laser communications, remote sensing, instruments for space exploration missions, illumination and non-imaging design.
* Optical design and analysis software proficiency includes ASAP, FRED, Zemax and Code V. Significant experience with stray light and thermal background analysis of optical systems and hardware design to minimize stray light effects. Novel non-imaging source illumination/collection design for instrumentation.
* Hardware experience includes design, testing, and alignment of Vis/NIR spectrometers, laboratory test equipment, and optical communications transceivers.

Frank Grochocki’s Specialties:

Stray light analysis, general optical system analyses (including diffraction, polarization, and thermal background), illumination design, non-imaging design
