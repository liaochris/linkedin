Sanjay Kalra’s Summary

• Successful track record of a decade and a half in business-to-business sales and marketing of software and information technology solutions including direct selling, sales management, business development, marketing and product management.

• Experience in selling software and services in the Business Process Management and Outsourcing areas – a hot growth sector of the IT industry.

• Over ten (10) years of experience in Electronic Content Management solutions (including its predecessors – Document Imaging, Document Management, Workflow, etc.) in India, USA, Japan and Asia.

• Successful sales, business development and account management experience with various Fortune Global companies, including Toshiba America Business Solutions, Konica Minolta, Canon Inc., Citigroup Private Bank, Citibank Cards Technology, Citigroup Credit Services, Citibank International LATAM, Deutsche Bank Investment Management / Scudder, Harcourt Publishing, Newspaper Industry Association of America, Sharp Labs, OKI Data Corporation and many other global corporations.

• Experience in running multiple ODC’s (dedicated Offshore Development Centers) for US and Japanese customers

• A good understanding of all aspects of the software industry including product, solution, professional services and outsourced (offshore) software development selling, corporate marketing and product management, software development life cycle management and quality assurance.

• Great degree of comfort in intercultural interactions - particularly in Japan, South-Asia, South-East Asia and the Middle-East.

• Substantial experience in all selling models – Direct selling to end-customers, OEM Sales, VAR Channels and Regional Sales offices.

• A deep interest in and understanding of information technology applications.

• An MBA (Master’s degree in Business Administration) from one of India's premier business schools.
