Eugene Hsu’s Summary

Eugene joined Dell in July 2005 as a Pricing and Profitability Manager for the Enterprise and the Relationship lines of business in Dell’s Asset Recovery Business. ARB is responsible for selling all product lines returned in the Americas. This role had P/L responsibility focused on pricing-to-market continuously-variable inventory. Secondary responsibilities included working with telesales, online and operations teams to drive the business.

In 2006 Eugene became a Brand Manager for ARB, initially managing the Enterprise products and then the Relationship systems. As a Brand Manager, Eugene performed not-only the work of a Pricing and Profitability manager, but also promotional and online marketing as-well as developing relationships within other internal segments at Dell. Eugene’s highest-impact work was to push the Outlet to a non-strikethrough discount Every Day Low Pricing.

Before joining Dell to develop his marketing skills, Eugene completed his MBA in Finance and Strategy at the University of Rochester’s Simon Graduate School of Business. It was there where he learned about fundamental pricing strategy, and realized that engineering never taught him about the time value of money! He served as a cohort representative in the Graduate Business Council, and learned that a half-mile walk from the student parking-lot to classes was not fun in the freezing Rochester winter.

Eugene has an undergraduate degree in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Southern California that he had leveraged to develop solid skills in information technology. Over eight years he grew from basic system management, to enterprise and datacenter support, to security and networks, and finally to databases and engineering at EDS and at a unique B2B startup called Zeborg.

At this time Eugene is interested in pursuing his next rotation in either another Marketing/Brand position or developing more skills in a Finance or Strategy role.
