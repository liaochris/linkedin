Larry Macfarlane’s Experience

  • VP of Marketing; Chief Marketing Officer

    MontaVista Software

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry)

    2007 — Present (1 year)

    MontaVista Software is the market leader for embedded Linux. MontaVista embedded Linux powers telecommunications carrier equipment from 7 of the top 8 network equipment manufacturers, 90% of Linux-powered mobile phones, and millions of other intelligent devices. Thousands of companies develop devices and applications that run on MontaVista embedded Linux. As VP of Marketing and CMO I am responsible for market strategy and all aspects of marketing execution.

  • President, Big Planet Division

    Nu Skin Enterprises

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; NUS; Computer Software industry)

    2003 — 2007 (4 years)

    Big Planet is the high-technology products division of Nu Skin Enterprises, a global, public direct sales and marketing company. As President I was responsible for product innovation, development, marketing and sales. My divisional focus was Photomax--helping preserve, organize and share memories. Successfully grew Photomax and its video companion Maxcast into a thriving global business unit.

    Wind River Systems

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; WIND; Computer Software industry)

    1998 — 2003 (5 years)

    Created global market segment strategies and products for Network Infrastructure and Consumer Electronics markets. Led the integration of leading edge technologies into Wind River product offerings including Java, graphics, and wireless protocols. Noted for turning around challenged business units.

    Zinc Software

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    1994 — 1998 (4 years)
