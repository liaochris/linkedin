Jared Libby’s Summary

I have over 10 years of experience in the marketing and interactive industries. I've been heavily involved in the interactive development of innovative business solutions for many globally prominent companies including: The Boeing Company, Charles Schwab, Applied Materials, Monster Worldwide, Agilent, Ryder, Hewlett-Packard, Midas, Air Force Reserve, and PricewaterhouseCoopers.

I entered the interactive industry in 1996 as employee 16 at Monster.com. As a senior interaction designer, I helped conceptualize, plan, design, and develop the website and a wide variety of synergetic tools and materials. In 1998, I began implementing a new interactive development process for TMP Worldwide by training several major centers in five countries. I played a key role in establishing and implementing these interactive development methods during a period when TMP Worldwide went from the #7 to the #2 Interactive Agency (AdWeek Magazine).

In 2005, Chris Pyle and I formed Alpine Interactive to provide interactive development and marketing services to businesses of all sizes in niche markets. The efficiency of our small team and process enables us to consistently complete projects on time, within budget, and on target with established business goals. We have built great relationships with over 100 clients and community leaders in a wide range of industries strictly through word-of-mouth marketing.

We've recently developed an exciting product, a web-based application that makes assembling customized, timely, and relevant travel plans easy. It takes advantage of unrivaled information and intelligent tools that guide users through the key aspects of travel planning. Travelers can confidently create plans, adjust itineraries, collect keepsakes, leverage the collective intelligence of local experts and previous travelers, coordinate group trips, and more! This new product--True Experience--launches early in 2008.
