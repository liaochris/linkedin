David Carralon’s Summary

Information architecture, brand development, design and web developement and web project management. I currently manage the development and promotion of medium size country website in the British Council network.
I provide consultancy on web content management, advice on the use of guidelines, brand parameters and implementation of web projects while adhering to corporate standards.

I am responsible for the writing, implementation and deployment of the country's e-marketing strategy ( SEM and SEO) and an emarketing budget typically ranging €20-30K annually.

I have been involved with coordinating web projects both at local and regional level and delivered tailored solutions that met customer needs. I also have experience of working with designers, suppliers, developers and editorial teams.

As an Online marketing specialist I have experience in driving targeted traffic to the British Council France website through managing an online marketing mix including PPC campaigns, search engine optimisation, email marketing and online partnerships, e-advertising and recent use of social networking tools.

I am the co-owner of E-scientia web communications, where I have developed further experience in web projects-management by delivering usable, standards-compliant websites and helping clients devise and implement online marketing strategies.

I have good experience in managing natural search engine optimisation campaigns, a proactive approach to campaign management and a genuine passion for the internet and the future growth of search. I have a good understanding of on page optimisation, keyword research, competitor analysis, link building, site architecture analysis, basic web development skills.

David Carralon’s Specialties:

I specialise in information architecture, writing for the web, usability and accessibility testing with a strong focus on delivering custom-built, standards-compliant web solutions.

I also have a professional and successful record of search marketing work and web analytics and reporting.
