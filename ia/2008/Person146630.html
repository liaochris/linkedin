Pat O'Donnell’s Summary

Pat began her recruiting career in 1994 with Hayden & Associates and joined the McKinley Group in July 2004. During her years as a recruiter she has specialized in all levels of marketing and product management, training, OD, and HR. 

She is a frequent guest speaker at industry associations and local universities on job search strategies and has been on the Board of Directors of three professional associations.

By popular request Pat has provided Career Counseling evenings and weekends since early 2002.

Prior to her role in the career industry, Pat spent 20 years as an executive in the advertising industry. She holds a BA from Wheaton College in Norton, Massachusetts.
