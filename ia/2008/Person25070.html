Bob Uda’s Summary

Certified manager (CM) with Institute of Certified Professional Managers (ICPM). Serves as an at-large member and vice chairman of the board (VCOB) of the ICPM Board of Regents (BOR). Over 20 years of proposal development experience in the aerospace and defense industries. Acquiring knowledge and research experience in PhD program in Business Administration (BA) with specialization in Homeland Security (HS). Possesses teaching, writing (published 14 books), researching, managing, consulting, and entrepreneuring experience. Desires future full-time work in counterterrorism teaching (brick & mortar and/or online), research, writing, and consulting.

Bob Uda’s Specialties:

Proposal development, program/project management, writing, research, entrepreneurship, teaching, career development, career coaching, and counterterrorism.
