Andrey Kovalevsky’s Summary

After 8 successful years in various Sales, Marketing and Management positions with the different companies in the field of commercial and residential real estate development and implementation of sales strategies and marketing plans I am now applying those skills to help people realize their Real Estate dreams, as well as real estate development and investments dreams. Whether it be selling an existing home, buying a home (pre-owned or new construction), locally, nationally or internationally my goal is to help make the Real Estate transaction process as painless and stress free as possible while completing the transaction in the shortest amount of time for the best possible price. I have built my entire career and reputation on my "Commitment to Customer Satisfaction" and "Attention to Details" which is exactly what is required to be successful as a Realtor in today's market place. I may be useful to potential real estate buyers, investors and developers in order to help succeed in very complicated Russian Real Estate market.

Andrey Kovalevsky’s Interests:

I am looking for partners and investors in Russian Real Estate market. Since the growth of the Russian economy fueled on the rising cost of oil, there has been talk of creating ‘international property funds' devised to take advantage of the rising value of property in Russia. Of course, for many foreigners Russia is an unknown quantity widely perceived as an unstable and corrupt market. But still the facts are there for those who want to see them, and now even Standard & Poors has upgraded Russia to a recommended investment level. The way in which the foreign investor can best enter the Russian real estate market is though investment in the development and subsequent ownership or disposal of the property. Each sector has its attractions and difficulties, but what is essential for every one is a sound and detailed knowledge of the market and legislation, coupled with the choice of a reliable construction partner accustomed to working in the Russian environment.
