Jack Beaton’s Summary

Project Manager and User Experience Designer specializing in Human-Computer Interaction, recently trained at the Carnegie Mellon HCI Institute. Mixed background in anthropology and computer science. Currently looking to begin a career with an interesting and ambitious employer.
