Julius Chan’s Summary

My background in film sound has been extensive and well credited. I have not only been involved with sound editing duties but also with the installation of studios/ edit suites, product development/beta testing, and lecturing about sound editing craft and tools within Australia and overseas.
In my role as Audio Product Manager for Amber Video and Audio Post I have been responsible for sales and customer support for ICON consoles installed within our major broadcasters and high-end post production facilities.
