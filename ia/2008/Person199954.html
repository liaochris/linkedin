Will Eagle’s Summary

Starting as a web developer, I moved quickly into project management of website development and co-ordination of marketing campaigns for Virgin Management. I then moved to Virgin Mobile in Canada, taking on online as well as managing all of the below the line marketing communications and the creative department.

I'm really a brand guy who loves to figure out what interactive can do, and how it can integrate with everything else - from other media in the marketing mix through to the consumers' day to day life.

Will Eagle’s Specialties:

Budget management, people management, strategy, e-commerce, usability and accessibiltiy, SEO, online media and even the backend infrastructure to support all of the fun online stuff.
