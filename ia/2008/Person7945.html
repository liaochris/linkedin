Allen Ellison’s Summary

My passion has always been about innovation -- about taking new ideas and making them real, whether it's creating fundamentally new technologies for detecting the position, spin, and velocity of an aerial projectile (U.S. Patent # 5,419,549) -- or whether it's helping Boeing create the next generation of eLearning and simulation technologies for the 747 Tanker Refueler program.

I find that the work I do now, of enabling and assisting other companies in creating fascinating applications and in discovering new ways of working with information and new ways of collaborating -- through text, audio, video -- is fascinating and fulfilling.

Allen Ellison’s Specialties:

rich internet application development, consulting, proof-of-concept and prototype development, product innovation, game & simulation development.
