Xavier Bonnet’s Summary

Journalist in sports and cultural press since 18 years now, my diverse experiences in it, mainly as editor in chief, brought me to manage many teams, making them share an energy, a daily implication and a real passion through the mission I had to fulfil : propose and realize the most accurate and distinctive magazine in its category, rather it was in culture, football or Formula 1.
Offering this expertise to a new media, a new structure, a new environment is precisely what I want to add now to my professionnal life.
Good writing, curiosity, sense of communication, rigour, team spirit, motivation to evolve in a dynamic surrounding : I consider fitting with all those criterions and I'd be delighted to develop them with you.
