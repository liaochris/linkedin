Craig Olson’s Summary

Optical engineer with multidisciplinary background in traditional optical engineering, electrical engineering, and semiconductor processing.

Professional interests involve optical imaging technology requiring application of both experimental and theoretical optics all the way through the imaging chain.

Core skills relate design, simulation, and testing of optical and electrooptical systems, from lens design to system metrology development.

Craig Olson’s Specialties:

Technical software skills include ZEMAX, Mathematica, and GLAD
Zoom lens design, infrared lens design, integrated optomechanical FEA
Transitioning between design and engineering to manufacturing
Managing and directing small technical proof-of-concept projects and experiments
