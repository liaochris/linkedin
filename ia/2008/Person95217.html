John Melendez’s Summary

EXPERIENCE

In the mid-1990’s, John Melendez worked several years in Hong Kong and Shanghai as Regional Manager for A/S Det Østasiatiske Kompagni (EAC), a Danish trade JV established with Beijing City Planning Commission.

After returning to the US, John changed careers and began writing among the ranks of the world's largest software providers and IT education companies. Mr. Melendez is a former Managing Editor for Computer Associates’ "Windows Tips & Secrets", a monthly technology newsletter with a circulation of 35,000 subscribers. John Melendez now works independently as a contract technical writer and courseware developer.

An alumnus of the University of Arizona with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Chinese Studies, John speaks, reads and writes Mandarin Chinese, German, and English.

John Melendez’s Specialties:

China business consulting, Mandarin language skills, online curriculum development, technical writing, editing, authoring, CSV computer system validation documentation for pharmaceutical and the bio-pharm industries
