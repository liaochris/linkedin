Peter Cobley’s Summary

I have been working in advertising since 1994, and moved into New Media in 1998. I have worked for a number of major media owners including Reed Business Information and Carlton Television.

My background is advertising sales and business development. I have specialised in display, search engine, and mobile advertising.

I am knowledgeable in all forms of new media advertising, whether driven by CPM, CPC, or CPA costings. I am also versed in ad serving technology.

I am legally qualified, holding a law degree and professional qualifications.

I have held a number of key roles in the past, and worked in London for eight years. For example, when working at Carlton Television Ltd I was employed as a Director responsible for third party partners, for whom we sold advertising. I was involved in the online roll out of Who Wants To Be Millionaire and Survivor.

I enjoy working in advertising, and specifically New Media. To date I have worked in search engine and online display advertising for nearly six years for Yahoo!, and find the areas fascinating.

Peter Cobley’s Specialties:

Sales of New Media advertising, legal knowledge, familiarity with individuals in the New Media business, understanding of the advertising business. Good negotiation skills, report writing, and understanding of accounts and financial metrics.
