Pamela Pope’s Summary

Pope Institute is a National elder care consulting firm specializing in helping families PLAN for elder care and disability health care to ensure quality care, prevent caregiver burnout, and reinforce a family's long-term care goals. I have ten (10) years of experience in elder care including long-term care administration.

Working with:
Families throughout the US
Businesses
Government Agencies
Legal Professionals

MISSION: To empower adult health care consumers with information and tools to navigate the health care system. To impact legislation, public policy, health policy, elder care rights, consumer education, and the legal system in a manner that reinforces "Quality of care is not optional, it is the standard."

My involvement in Health Policy and Administration activities include lobbying the Missouri state legislature, Illinois state legislature, contacting U.S. Congress regarding health policy for seniors, and grassroots activitites for professional standards and government regulations.

Pope Institute also offers a National Helpline for elder care and disability questions at 866#545.POPE. Questions we field range from basic how to's to complex what if options.
