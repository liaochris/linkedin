Diane Litchko, PHR’s Summary

Senior Human Resources Business Partner who specializes in providing strategic leadership to engineering and information technology functions. A take charge leader energized by dynamic changing environments utilizes broad HR expertise and communication skills to strategize and implement initiatives to achieve business results. Motivated to help others and use natural talent as a coach, mentor and trainer. Actively pursues responsibility and enjoys resolving complex problems.

Human Resources Expert, Technical Recruiter and Executive Search: Provides human resources leadership to wireless telecommunications, environmental engineering, and information technology functions.

Business Owner and Entrepreneur: Energized to achieve business results while making a difference in this world.
