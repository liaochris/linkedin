Pierre Jones’s Summary

As a professional Air Traffic Control Officer (ATCO) I spent 20 years in the Air Navigation Operations at Transport Canada, 5 of which as a manager in progressively responsible positions. I then returned to University and obtained an MBA to faciltate my transition to a Business Management role in the newly created Nav Canada Corporation set up to absorb the Canadian Air Navigation Services as a private organisation.

I became Director of the National Training program and of the National Techinal Training Institute at Nav Canada (NCTI).

I then assumed the role of special advisor to the VP operations with the responsibility of facilitating integration of new ATM/CNS technology into the Air Navigation Operations for Nav Canada.

After more than 30 years in the Air Navigation field I retired from Nav Canada and formed a consulting company in the field of Technical Training Systems Architechture (TSA), Air Navigation management, Entreprise Strategy and Business development.

I joined both IATA and ICAO (International Aviation organisations) as on demand external consultant in the field of Air Navigation Services Management, ANS Training and project development.

I also joined CESO, a Canadian NGO involved in international business support and development to emerging and developping countries, as a volunteer consultant in my various fields of expertise as listed below.

I have worked extensively on several continents over the past five years. I have been involved in various projects in Central America, Eastern Europe, Asia and Africa.

Pierre Jones’s Specialties:

ATM/CNS integration into the FANS.

I also have an excellent command of English and French languages as well as a working knowledge of Spanish.
