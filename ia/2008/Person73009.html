Greg Sarcona’s Summary

Experienced manager in high-tech companies for software, web development, and hardware design. Background includes PMI PMP (Project Management Institute Project Management Professional) certification, Six Sigma team leadership, and Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering. Consistently appreciated by co-workers, direct reports, and management for communication skills and immediate effectiveness in chaotic situations.

* Eight years experience in program and project management. I have been called in to re-energize teams that have lost focus, confidence, or direction, and have taken them to the leading edge of project team success.

* Managed teams for engineering, hardware physical design, software development, and research.

* Cross-functional team leadership. I led a Six Sigma project for cost and legal liability reduction in physical design of microprocessors. My recommendations to the Division Director saved over $3M/yr, and eliminated potential legal liability.

* Program management of 70+ at two sites.

* Direct management of team of 20.

* Program management of software qualification for internally-developed suite of tools. Reduced bugs by >90% in three months.
