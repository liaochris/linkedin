Salvador Becerra’s Summary

I have designed and directed hundreds of marketing and user research projects, both qualitative and quantitative, for corporate, political, non-profit, and governmental clients. Research projects include:

v Surveys (online, telephone, SMS, intercept, mail, and fax)

v Focus groups

v One-on-one interviews aka IDI's (in-depth interviews)

v Usability sessions (mobile, web, kiosk, paper prototypes)

v RITE studies (Rapid Iterative Test and Evaluation)

v Field studies (in-person and remotely using a variety of methods)

Leading corporate clients include: eBay, Honda, LinkedIn, Yahoo!, Toyota, The North Face and Intuit.


Experience in all aspects of both marketing research and user research studies includes methodology design (of both sample and instrument), usability and focus group moderation, collection and analysis of research results, as well as presentation and implementation of findings.

I've worked with qualitative and quantitative research studies in a variety of roles including: consultant, project manager, analyst, quality assurance specialist, and field director.

From the University of California at Berkeley, I've earned both a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Market Research and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics with an emphasis in Econometrics.

I've worked throughout the entire research process.

Where there are questions, I'll find answers.
