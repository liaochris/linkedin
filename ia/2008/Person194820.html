Valentino Berti’s Summary

I started in the IT-business industry in 1976 as an operator at Burroughs Computers (mainframes). My basic education is as systems analyst and Cobol programmer. During the 80´s I spezialised myself in Data Communications and wrote books in the matter. Further on I moved to the distribution business being Product Manager at Nordic Datadistribution for communication productc, primarly to the PC-market. I have been CEO for a company in the education area (Data Comm training) and furthermore Business Area Manager for a professional training company. I have experience in managing people, in marketing of complex IT-services and products and in developing business thru effective IT-tools.
