Donna Quesinberry’s Summary

DonnaInk delivers success-driven consulting, with over 16 years past performance, Ms. Quesinberry, DonnaInk President ensures practical, creative, seamless solutions to client needs - alleviating extensive overhead or burdensome bureaucracy. Donna remains mindful of this core value: "mission goals and objectives enhanced through proficient metrics and positive outcomes build client service support delivery."

Clear, compelling, creative commercial, government, non-profit, private sector and individual print or web-based copy requires masterful proficiency and subject matter expertise (SME) insight and sensibility. Ms. Quesinberry delivers strategic solutions that maximize opportunities, reduce expenditures and provide comprehension efficacy. Benchmarking performance measurements and evaluation criteria early-on in a project's development, results in positive outcomes that are both qualitative and quantitative as performance metrics. DonnaInk clients build on these positive metrics as they continue to grow and mature.

Whether your cup of tea is a poetry chapbook; proposal management; ghostwriting; grants; business plans; manuscripts, technical communications; or other fiction or technical non-fiction media - Donna guarantees formidable final products. Maintaining a solid understanding of ITIL, ISO 9001:2000, Sarbanes Oxley, Lean Six Sigma, SEI CMMI and other frameworks / methodologies in the current market as well as freelance writing trends, publisher calls and sales marketing - you can rest assured that DonnaInk will deliver quality that ensures business growth!

Our product customization recognizes industry-standards (national as well as international regulations adherence).
