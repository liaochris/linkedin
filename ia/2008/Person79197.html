LifeLock #1 In Identity Theft Protection’s Summary

LifeLock is a proactive identity theft protection agency, specializing in prevention of identity theft rather than the reporting of it. LifeLock was founded in 2005 and is already considered the industry leader in identity theft protection. Our management team and staff did solid research for more than three years, which we used to develop our system. We also built relationships with organizations, including the Better Business Bureau and TrustE. Our goal was to lock down every individual's private information so no one except that individual can approve its use.

We've been so successful at this that, without qualms, our CEO Todd Davis publishes his Social Security number (457-55-5462) in our advertising. So why publish his social security number? Because we are absolutely confident LifeLock is protecting Todd Davis's name and personal information, just like it will yours. And in the unfortunate event your identity is misused while you are a LifeLock member we guarantee our service up to $1 million.

We are based in Tempe, Arizona. Our company is led by experienced and successful entrepreneurs and industry experts. We are backed by Bessemer Venture Partners, Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, and Goldman Sachs, the leading venture capital firms in the world.

Some of what we do, you can do yourself for free. We even tell you on our Website which of these you can do for free. People use experts every day to change their oil, cut their kids' hair, mow their lawns, do their taxes, all kinds of things. Our customers have decided that it is worth $10 a month to have LifeLock protect them against identity theft. We'd love to do it for you and your family. LifeLock also provides its members with eRecon, TrueAddress, and WalletLock at no additional cost. Learn more about these services or for more information about becoming a LifeLock member or an Affiliate with LifeLock, please visit our Website(www.lifelock.com) or feel free to send us a message through this site.
