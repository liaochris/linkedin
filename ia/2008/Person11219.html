Andre Njotohusodo (anhusodo @ yahoo.com)’s Summary

Andre, a senior SAP Plant Maintenance (PM) Consultant has 9+ years business experience that includes more than 7 years in SAP environment; 4 full cycles SAP-PM implementation, rollouts and several support assignments.
His experience as user and as a consultant gives him a comprehensive view of the important and critical factors of the ERP implementation, especially in the PM area and its cross functional modules such as FI, CO, MM, PP, HR.
Andre had experience in many industries so that he had a complete knowledge of the PM business process in Pulp & Paper, Power Plant, Chemical, Food & Beverages, Oil & Gas and Mining Industries, also in Airline Industry and Taxi Fleet Industry.
Andre is a Certified SAP PM Consultant, and trained in SAP BI – BW (SAP Business Intelligence – Business Information Warehouse) and SAP SEM (Strategic Enterprise Management).
