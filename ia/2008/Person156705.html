Randy Hall’s Summary

Experience:
Almost 20 years of high performance Sales / Sales Management experience. Beginning with a privately help company where I was engaged in a successful IPO, to a Fortune 300 company, I have gained a wealth of experience that transfers very well into many senior positions.

Strength:
My passion to succeed and my ability to work thru others to foster a performance culture that rewards success and leads to personal and professional growth is my cornerstone. Being able to get along with the receptionist to the CEO is a gift that has enabled me to succeed in a diverse team environment and in a matrix managed organization. Giving people clear direction, setting aggressive but attainable expectations and rewarding for success is a formula that I believe will always work.

Accomplishments:
Consistently achieved financial plan, by driving sales through a matrix environment through inspiring others to perform by coaching, motivating and effectively managing.

Combining multiple cultures and sales units into a single cohesive sales organization was the most challenging, yet most rewarding initiative at IKON. I was able to work cross functionally and with other senior management to achieve exceptional sales growth during consolidations and restructuring of 96 sales office / call center locations over 3 years.

Full scale ERP/CRM Oracle implementation and project management experience compliments my Six Sigma background to form a uniquely successful sales and operations professional.
