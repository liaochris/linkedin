Kyle Racki’s Summary

Multi disciplinary designer with focus on interactive design. Traditional branding, advertising and print design experience - coupled with vast knowledge of how to implement design on the web so that it is usable, accessible and functional. Always looking for opportunities to further companies brands, and help them achieve business benefits by using print and the web.

Kyle Racki’s Specialties:

Web standards, Usability and accessibility, XHTML, CSS, Actionscript, Expression Engine Installation and configuration, broad knowledge of print design and production processes for large publications, eg: annual reports, catalog's and magazines.
