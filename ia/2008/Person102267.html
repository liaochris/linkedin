Karen Nielsen’s Interests:

MASTER THESIS: “Corporate branding - and the Role of the CEO - a Case Study of Lundbeck”. The aim was to explore the role of the CEO in relation to corporate branding illustrated by the case study. I examined and criticised the existing corporate branding theories with the purpose of developing new perspectives and metaphors in order to understand the role of the CEO in corporate branding both as product and as process. In other words, I perfected the analysis of corporate branding and the role of the CEO by giving a more detailed, closer and more true and fair description of the actual and possible roles of the CEO in corporate branding. Thus, based on theoretical tendencies as well as the empirical research, six new roles for the CEO in relation to corporate branding were put forward.
