Pierre Barbeau’s Summary

Dynamic and innovative team leader with the proven ability to motivate others to achieve uncommon results by taking direct responsibility for turning an idea into a profitable finished product through assertive risk-taking and innovation.

How I helped make a difference:

> Founded Moblico LLC, a mobile life company offering mobile software and consulting services.

> Conceptualized Sprint’s visual communication service and introduced the award winning mobile imaging service, Sprint Picture Mail, now supporting over 7M subscribers generating over $400M in gross annual revenues.

> Drove creation of Global Call Center (GCC) solution, a ground-breaking worldwide CRM infrastructure for emerging international wireless customer integrating 3 call centers on 3 continents to support over 18 languages, 24/7 valued in excess of $200M.

> Sold and helped design ground-breaking global call center solution; the most advanced and complex solution of its time with 3 integrated call centers supporting global calls in 18 languages and believed to be the largest outsourced call center deal of its time worth in excess of $200 million.

> Created innovative Direct Marketing Department at Bishop's University to support and drive growth of university ancillary profit centers.

Pierre Barbeau’s Specialties:
