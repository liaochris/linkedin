Saul Gascon Barba’s Summary

Experience

Working in small and medium companies such as "Novarama" and "Digital Legends" allowed me to be involved in all the aspects of the creation of a videogame. I think being "in the trenches" for 3 years has been an excellent way to start my learning process.

Now I'm starting a new adventure in Grin as Project Manager and Lead Level Designer.


Objectives

My main objective is to keep learning. I would like to never stop improving my game design techniques and team management strategies with fresh ideas and solutions for any kind of project I’m involved into.

My long-term goal is to create videogames that go beyond industry standards and shake people’s understanding of what videogames actually are. I would like to “touch” people’s minds and create game experiences that allow them to see new points of view and make they think a bit... and obviously while having a lot of fun!
