Sara Agee’s Summary

Let me be your personal search agent in your career plan! If you are an Oracle Professional, I'd love to hear what your career goals are, and become a passive source of information on job opportunities that fit the bill.


Benefits of networking with a recruiter, like me; whether you're actively looking for a change, or not!
1. Gain access to jobs that aren't advertised.
2. Receive resume editing and interview coaching.
3. Get real feedback after every interview.
4. Enter into a vast network of Professionals and Hiring Managers in your industry.
5. Describe your dream job once, and never be bothered to look for a job again. I'll contact you when such a position come available.
6. All of this is absolute FREE to you! I'm paid by my clients when they decide to hire a candidate whom I represent.


How to help me help you:
1. Be prepared. Have an updated version of your resume and invest some thought into what your career goals are and the timing you have in mind for making a move.
2. Be upfront. Clearly communicate your expectations on the types of positions, companies, salary ranges, etc. We take our direction from you and only inform you about opportunities that fit your description.
3. Be honest. Our job is not to screen you out! Our job is to find the perfect position for your experience, strengths and career goals.


If you'd like to explore the possibility of working together, please email me at sagee@cornerofficeconsultants.com. I look forward to making your acquaintance!!

2. Siebel Business Analysts and Developers
3. OBIEE Technical Consultants
