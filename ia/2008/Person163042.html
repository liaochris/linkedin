Rob Pedini’s Summary

Entertainment professional with 15 years experience as an actor; stage, film, television, radio and voiceovers. And has performed in every medium; big-budget/independent film, tv, off-broadway, equity showcase, children's theatre, digital viral-video and internet radio production. Also a theatre director and a partner, writer, creator and producer of his own production company, Mook Productions, LLC. I am always interested in producing original content and developing new projects in association with larger studios and networks.

Rob Pedini’s Specialties:

Business management and director of multiple entertainment companies, director of stage, screenplay/playwright and an accomplished character actor with expertise and masterful facility of accents, dialects, character voices and celebrity impersonations.
