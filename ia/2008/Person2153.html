Adail Muniz Retamal’s Summary

I have a passion for research, practical application of what I've learnt and, of course, teaching others. My main interest today is on Eliyahu Goldratt's Theory of Constraints (TOC), particularly with its applications to software development and our personal lives.

I'm the founder and moderator of TOC-Brasil group (http://br.groups.yahoo.com/group/toc-brasil). Among the several TOC applications, I'm very skilled at the Thinking Process and Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM), which I combine with Agile PM and Lean PM in my Project Management practice.

In the Agile arena I'm the main promoter of FDD (Feature-Driven Development) in Brazil, an excelent agile development and management methodology, deserving its own group (http://br.groups.yahoo.com/group/gufdd). I'm also a CSM (Certified Scrum Master) and use Lean/Kanban concepts as well.

One of my technical passions is Software Engineering as a whole and, particularly, Object technology (Object-Oriented Analysis, Design, Programming and Testing).

Adail Muniz Retamal’s Specialties:

Theory of Constraints (TOC), Critical Chain Project Management (CCPM), Agile PM, Lean PM, Agile methodologies (FDD, Scrum, XP), CMMI, Borland tools (CaliberRM, StarTeam, Together, Delphi), UML in Color, Software Engineering, Object-Oriented Requirements, Analysis, Design, Programming and Testing.
