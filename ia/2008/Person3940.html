Andrew Glover’s Summary

I am a huge fan of video games and I've been playing them for over 25 years now (back to the old Apple II). I consider myself very lucky to be a part of the industry and over the past 4 years I've contributed to the tremendous growth we are experiencing in Vancouver by recruiting quality candidates throughout North America, Europe, and Asia in a broad assortment of game development disciplines. I am eager to connect with experienced professionals within the game industry for current or future employment opportunities. If you are not from the industry but are considering a change, feel free to contact me and I'm happy to have a discussion with you on what it is like.

I am currently working for Action Pants Inc. and we are searching for the following positions to round out our PS3/360 and Wii project teams:

- Senior Software Engineer, Rendering (Runtime/Optimization)
- Senior Game Play Engineer
- Art Director
- Senior Software Engineer, Animation
- Front End Engineer (contract)

Although these are our current openings, we are always on the lookout for talented individuals and would love to hear from you if you are interested in the studio.

I am also happy to connect people through my network, please don't hesitate to ask if you need an introduction.

Add me to your network - andrewg@actionpantsinc.com
