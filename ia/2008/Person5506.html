Chris Wessell’s Summary

I am a recruiter for a division of Yoh (an SAP Services Partner) that focuses on SAP recruiting. Our value proposition is that we connect our clients to the high-impact pool of independent SAP consultants. Since we deal with direct clients, there are no additional layers, which means we can pay independents a good market rate. On the other side of the coin, our overhead is not the same as a consulting firm, so we are able to offer this talent to our clients at a rate superior to that of a Big 4.

Project leads for CRM, SRM, HR, FICO, BW are welcome and encouraged to connect. Also, I'd like to talk to anyone with a background in Outlooksoft, Business Objects, or Triversity, which are now SAP products.

Even if you're currently in a project, let's get acquainted! I can make a note of when you expect to be available, and keep you apprised of openings that coincide with that time frame.

Although our focus is placing consultants, from time to time my clients also enlist my assistance in finding permanent employees as well. If you are a full time SAP professional interested in making a change to another full time role, please feel free to reach out as well.

I can be reached at chris.wessell@yoh.com, and I will make every effort to get back to you the same day.
