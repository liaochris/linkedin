Michael Lefteris’s Summary

Autodata Solutions, Inc. is one of North America’s leading automotive software and data providers and has focused on the automotive industry for over 17 years. Founded in 1990, Autodata provides automotive content, research, and technology implementation services to most of the auto manufacturers who distribute vehicles in North America, helping them develop, market and sell their products more effectively and profitably. Autodata’s customizable ecommerce tools and content are utilized by over 90% of the largest fleet companies in North America and power millions of new vehicle orders annually. Additionally, more than 17,500 auto dealers and several of the most popular consumer research portals, with more than 23 million visitors per month, rely on Autodata’s data and tools to motivate consumers’ purchase decisions. Headquartered in London, Ontario, Canada, Autodata employs over 200 business and technology professionals with offices in Detroit and Los Angeles, and is a division of Internet Brands, Inc.

Autodata provides automotive content, research, software tools and technology implementation services that enable automobile companies to understand their markets and more effectively develop, market and sell their products. Through this diverse suite of products and services, Autodata serves most automobile manufacturers in North America.
