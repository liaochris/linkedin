Ben Johnson’s Summary

I have recently moved to Denmark and have been looking for opportunities in marketing and business development. I have an international profile as I have worked in the Far East and Europe for the last nine years.

Throughout my career I’ve been responsible for the coordination of marketing and company strategy. I have a project manager’s acumen, as well as important skills in business analysis, strategy execution and key account management. I successfully accomplished many interesting projects that include the implementation of a campaign management system and a new Web and IT strategy. From the latter I gained useful experience in e-marketing especially in web based affiliate marketing and SEO.

I am used to working within different cultures, I possess excellent communication and analytical skills to ensure that tasks and responsibilities are driven forward. I know that these skills combined with my natural personality will be valuable assets for communicating with management, partners and customers to ensure close cooperation.

In November 2006 I started a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) in Technology Management to hone my management skills. The MBA focuses on market and financial analysis, strategy formulation and performance measurement. My previous education was a Bsc Honours consisting of a mix of engineering and project management courses.

I am confident, committed and have a drive to succeed to ensure tasks are driven through to completion. I would love the challenge of a fast paced, dynamic, multi-national business environment.
