Jeff Knight’s Summary

For over 80 years, Electrical Equipment Company has specialized in electric motor repair, troubleshooting, and design. Three regional service centers are available to provide both onsite and 24-Hour emergency service. From full diagnostic electrical testing to IEEE and NEMA specifications, to onsite service of rotational equipment, to complete redesign of electric motors, Electrical Equipment has become a premier provider in the Southeast.

I proudly represent my company in many areas - one of those is to focus our resources on obtaining talent. So if you are interested in our company please contact me through Linked In.

Despite market conditions, we increased sales by capturing market share in 2008, with a 58% increase in the best performing center, setting an all time record. We are winning with value innovation, driven by direct customer feedback.

Recently I re-designed our sales process and implemented a sales force automation package to successfully manage it corporate wide. Reinforced with customized training, the process has decreased the typical sales cycle by 40%, increasing visibility and closure rate. We provide a very compelling package for our sales team, which is expanding. We are also hiring field service and mechanical technicians and engineers.

Currently we are developing of technology to automate management of customer specifications and documentation during service operations. We are also funding research to improve motor quality testing and integrate test and process equipment through a corporate data acquisition system.
