Brian Grossman’s Experience

  • Owner

    Wonderhouse Design (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    While at my former job, at an internationally known and highly-awarded advertising agency, I worked as a designer, art director, writer and photographer and was known as the "go to guy" – oftentimes the "in case of emergency" guy.

    So after being downsized In August, I decided to start my own firm, WonderHouse Design. To take the lessons I had learned at both that agency as well as other agencies and do the things they did well and be innovative as well. Because the traditional agency model is decaying, creating a new breed of agency which shifts the role of the creative and opens up opportunities like never before.

    I am committed to molding my firm into one of the region's best and brightest. One where collaboration replaces condemnation and ideas are as wide open as the imaginations behind them. To get that first look over a mountain range and realize "We can go anywhere". Because we can. Which goes back to WonderHouse's mantra, "let's wonder."

Brian Grossman’s Interests:

Finding the right way to connect with the true audience of a given product or service. Sometimes it is the traditional ad, but oftentimes it's not. Broadcast is dead, terrestrial radio is on life support and ads are, well, "ads". So the question is, how do we find and then truly serve those we're after? That's my interest. Finding, developing and then executing those ideas.
