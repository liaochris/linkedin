Nigel Reid’s Summary

I have had a very varied career working in a wide range of professions in Australia and abroad, including the period 1977 to 1990 in Japan pursuing studies in the Japanese Language, Martial Arts and Oriental Medicine while working in a variety of jobs: Actor, Model, Yoga Teacher, Personal Trainer, Shiatsu Practitioner and Teacher, Translator, Interpreter, English Teacher and Importer.

For the past 12 years my efforts have been directed towards sustainable development within the context of a carbon constrained world. The resulting Rivendell Village project is currently undergoing the development approval process, while its offspring, the award-winning ecoCity Farm is moving towards commercialisation, funded by a public float of shares in Urban Ecological Systems Ltd, holder of the ecoCity Farm IP.
