Ellen Petry Leanse’s Summary

Ellen Leanse has guided companies in building activation strategies for real and online communities for 25 years, with a current focus on social networks, viral initiatives and Facebook strategies for enterprises of all sizes.

As Apple's first user Evangelist, Ellen established outreach to online communities and the installed base. Her work shortened product development and marketing cycles, allowed Apple to quickly identify new opportunities, and engaged customers in all segments as ambassadors of the Apple brand.

Ellen extended this work into an consulting that guided Oracle, Apple, MCI, Samsung and a range of start-up clients to increased customer acquisition and retention. Recent work has emphasized market definition and member activation for social networks, with a current focus on Facebook applications and the "onboarding" of business opportunity to the Facebook platform.
