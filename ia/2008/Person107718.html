Kenneth Marinelli’s Summary

Ken is truly an individual with an entrepreneurial spirit. His background in financial services, experience as an entrepreneur, and his avid quest for knowledge give him a unique and powerful ability to address life and business issues from an integrated and comprehensive level. Ken Marinelli is a recognized business leader and entrepreneur who has developed several companies over the last 15 years. All of his endeavors have always been around the world of strategic marketing, branding and entertainment.

He began his early business career trading Financial Futures for Kidder Peabody. After 2 years of gathering a keen financial perspective his career path led him to his family run design and creative agency – Marinelli Communications (now Marinelli & Company) . Over the course of 15 years, Ken transformed Marinelli into a Strategic & Creative Marketing Resource offering its partners an integrated approach to marketing, design, promotion and advertising. Today, Ken is the CEO at Marinelli, helping such clients as the NFL, XO Communications, Loews Cineplex, Colgate-Palmolive and Altria - position and communicate their brands.

Ken’s entrepreneurial spirit shined through with several other business endeavors during this period, in which he was instrumental in developing and building: Canterbury Importers – an import company in the spirits industry; Solid Thinking – a creative manufacturing company specializing in product development.

In addition to Marinelli & Company, today Ken is a partner in Groupe Brandonology LLC – a luxury goods branding agency headquartered in NYC with a satellite office in Paris.

Born and raised in Bayside, NY, Ken's interests include golf, music and enjoying his family. He currently resides in New York, NY with his wife Gina and their children, Nicolas and Sienna Rose.
