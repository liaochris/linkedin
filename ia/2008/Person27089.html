Brian Bailey’s Summary

Since the early 1990s, Brian has played a lead role in the use of IT systems and knowledge at Optus, KPMG, AMP, Ernst & Young and other commercial organisations. Based on a strong user-centred approach, Brian has led the delivery of a number of sustainable business systems solutions.

Brian’s passion is to develop organisational capabilities to allow companies to be more 'self reliant' in finding solutions and capitalising and growing the intellectual capital of staff, systems and customers.

An experienced and accomplished people manager and mentor, Brian is able to deliver in line management and project environments. Brian excels in change programs that involve strategic leadership and people productivity issues; making sense of the integration of new technologies and weaving them into improvements to business processes in a way business people understand and engage with.
