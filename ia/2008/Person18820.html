Ayan Bhattacharya’s Summary

Currently working on corporate development and strategy for Investment Banking products in the Americas. My goal is to focus on international business development opportunities leveraging my background in media, finance and information management.

I completed the Management Associate Program, a leadership program where I completed rotations working alongside with senior management in various business segments: Most recently, I worked on the Growth Strategy of Thomson Reuters Markets and developing solutions for the buyside community. Prior to that, I have worked with the Chief Strategy Officer (analyzed new business opportunities and worked on the divestiture of Thomson Learning); Tax and Accounting (developed a new product offering); and Scientific (based in London analyzed M&A and partnership opportunity pipeline for the Pharma Chem business)

Prior to getting my MBA, I worked on the launch of ML Direct, Merrill Lynch's online brokerage product offering and Citigroup (developed benefits and financial portfolio solution and portal design)
