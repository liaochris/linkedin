Linda LeLievre’s Summary

As Senior Talent Acquisition Manager I coach and develop a sales and support team, manage P & L and develop recruitment and marketing strategies and training and recruitment materials. I have more than 5 years Recruiting experience specializing in matching the perfect candidate to the perfect company and position from mid-level to executive management .

With a focus on the entire recruiting cycle, developed recruitment and marketing strategies and training materials for well known organizations like Wells Fargo and AT&T Wireless and in the agency environment.

Direct experience in Technology, Sales, Management, Training, Human Resources and Mortgage ensures a well rounded knowledge of opportunities and competencies that transfer across many industries.

An avid networker, I belong to several groups like High Achievers, Riverside Chambers of Commerce and have been a past member of the Cypress Chamber of Commerce and Los Alamitos Chamber of Commerce.

I am also a commissioned notary for the State of California and a member of the National Notary Association and National Certified Loan Signers Association.
