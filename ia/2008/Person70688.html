Geir Leirvik’s Summary

Highly accomplished and capable networking professional, with excellent communication skills. Marketing, technical, product management and people management skills and experience. Worked closely with mergers and acquisitions, regulatory compliance as well as incident management.

Trained spokesperson in media and analyst relations. Excellent technical skills, having achieved top certifications in the networking areas of routing and switching.

Understand complex technologies and have experience in developing new features to extend the boundaries of what can be done. Skilled to educated in and evangelize complex solutions in a way that makes sense to all levels of audiences, including both technical and business decision makers.

Seeks to make a significant difference in the way the customers and users experience the company and the services the company provides. Making sure that the objectives and actions further the success of the customers and thus the company.

Longer term goal; executive leadership position to enable more profound impact and acceleration of my goals of customer and company success.
