Brian Temple’s Summary

There are lots of people in the computer industry that just sleepwalk through their careers - working just hard enough to get whatever project they're on done. Lethargic drones with no real interest in what they are doing outside of a paycheck.

I am not one of those people.

I am a person who is very passionate about learning new technologies and applying them to solve real world problems. I want to make people's lives better and/or easier with what I do. Whether it be design or development, libraries or applications, thick client or web - I strive to make a difference.
