Christopher O'Connell’s Summary

Christopher O’Connell is the president and Owner of Leede Capital Inc., a venture entrepreneuring firm that he founded in early 2000 with Denver oilman Edward H. Leede.

Leede Capital is engaged in capital introduction, planning implementation, and transition management, with capabilities to accelerate the design, funding, and launch of new business initiatives in financial services, real estate, and media. The firm provides in-depth due-diligence on seed, early-stage, and private-equity ideas, and formulates strategic business analysis, detailed project planning, and follows through with hands-on execution services.

During the 15 years prior to Leede Capital, Mr. O’Connell held senior roles in banking, consulting, and real estate development industries. Concurrently, he has maintained a passion for music and the performing arts. Having once been a struggling musician himself, he has recently formed mopion.net with Peter Denenberg of Acme Recording, perhaps best known as Producer of the multi-platinum Spin Doctors. mopion.net serves as an incubator for up-and-coming artists, providing them with the framework, guidance, and support to launch and navigate their careers in the music industry. In addition, mopion.net produces and sponsors fundraising concerts for various charities in the New York Metropolitan area, most recently featuring The Spin Doctors, Jon McLaughlin, and Paolo Nutini.

Mr. O’Connell is Vice Chair of the NYU Stern Alumni Council, an Executive Board Member of NYU Stern’s Alumni Finance Committee, a Board Member of Red Lotus Productions, Co-Chair of "Sail4Kids" benefit concert committee for the Children's Hospital at Westchester Medical Center, and a member of the Money Marketeers of New York University. Once a world record holding skydiver, he now enjoys offshore sailing with his wife and three children with whom he lives in Rye, New York.
