Jamie Rector’s Summary

- Regular photography assignment work that is published world-wide by numerous high profile newspapers, magazines, books, agencies and corporations. (Including The New York Times, Los Angeles Times, Bloomberg, Getty, Washington Post, USA Today, San Francisco Chronicle, Associated Press, Reuters, Time Inc., Pepsi, Washington Mutual, Siebel Systems, T-Mobile, Hard Rock Cafe International, Aga Khan, Operation Smile, Golden Boy Productions, Inner City Education Foundation, and many others.)
- Extensive experience with deadlines, captions, general post-production, cropping, labeling, digital transmission (including ftp), security proceedures for high profile assignments, travel, working in a range of environments (from very formal to rough and tumble), etc.
- Range of assignments include (but are not limited to) the Phil Spector Murder Trial, coverage of President Bush, immigration marches, wildfires, women's freedoms in Yemen, architecture, food, fashion, events, travel, corporate portraiture, environmental portraiture, the Oscars, lead paint documentary, and many more.
- She is often able to travel and take assignments with tight deadlines with just a moment's notice, schedule permitting.
- Stock photography is available! (Photoshelter should be up and running soon, otherwise, please inquire.)
