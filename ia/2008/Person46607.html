David F. Schmidt, AIA NCARB’s Summary

Goal:
My professional goal as an Architect and Planner is an innovative and sustainable legacy of design, planning and construction that benefits current and future generations.

Quote:
"Let's leave the earth in better shape than when each of us arrived. We owe this to ourselves and our children."

Experience:
I am an Architect, Senior Project Manager and Urban Planner with extensive leadership experience in all aspects of Architecture, Design, Planning and Construction. I've had a primary leadership role in the design and construction of over 500 projects, including a wide variety of project types; Commercial, Office, Industrial, Institutional, Government, Military, Health Care, Corrections, Public Safety, Transportation, Technology, Condominiums, Multifamily Residential, Custom Residential, Hospitality-Gaming-Hotel-Resorts, Golf & Ski Resorts, Historic Restoration, Mixed Use, High-Rise, Urban Design, Land Planning, Master Planning, Campus Planning, City Planning & Regional Planning. I also have had a primary role in the first new building to be LEED Certified by the USGBC in Nevada and in several other Sustainable Design building projects.

Synopsis:
Numerous projects have won design awards or recognition. Excellent interpersonal, communication, and managerial skills. Motivates other professionals to excellence and peak efficiency. Self-motivated; able to set priorities and meet impossible deadlines. Enthusiastic team player. Productive in fast-paced, high-pressure atmosphere. Very conscientious about budget and cost limitations. Adept at numerous managerial and design computer skills. 19 years of experience in New Business Development.
