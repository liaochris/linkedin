James McGillis’s Summary

With corporate offices located in Marina del Rey, California, MedIT Search is a premier executive search firm, specializing in the medical device industry. Employing a consultative approach to the needs of both our clients and our candidates, we offer customized solutions to each.

Our Areas of Specialization include:
Medical Devices & Related Consumables Sales, Marketing, Product Development and Engineering

With a strong background in medical device sales and sales management, we have the expertise to help medical device companies fill critical sales, marketing or engineering positions. At MedIT Search, we tailor the search process to your specific needs, utilizing Contingency, Priority or Retained search options.

At MedIT Search, we offer a range of free and fee-based candidate services. We will gladly accept a copy of your current MS Word résumé for inclusion in our confidential and searchable resume database. If requested, we will offer a free assessment on the quality and effectiveness of your current résumé.

If the candidate is qualified for one of our current assignments, we will offer free consultation on all aspects of documentary and personal presentation to our client. If the candidate is not qualified for one of our current assignments, we will offer fee-based Training on all aspects of a self-directed search for your next career position.

Please review each page of our website (www.meditsearch.com) for additional information and opportunities.
