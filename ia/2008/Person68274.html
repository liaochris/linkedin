Graeme Cloughley’s Summary

• Systems engineering professional with a thorough understanding of customer life cycle from business development, system specification & implementation, to client & project management.

• Specialized knowledge in Test & Measurement, Video, Telecommunications and Wireless markets, including Ultra-Wide Band (UWB), Wireless USB (WUSB) and Wimedia.

• Pragmatic Marketing's Practical Product Management with Requirements Certified (PMC).

Graeme Cloughley is a seasoned professional with more than 12 years of experience in the Test & Measurement, Telecommunications, and Wireless industries. He specializes in working closely with both internal and external customers to gather requirements, specify design, and implement solutions. Prior to Alereon, Graeme maintained his own consulting practice designing, building, and integrating computer-based test and measurement systems for clients in a variety of industries, ranging from ASIC validation to board testing to turn-key monitoring systems. A native of Scotland, he holds a BS in Instrumentation with Applied Physics from Glasgow Caledonian University, as well as a postgraduate diploma in Information Technology Systems from the University of Strathclyde.
