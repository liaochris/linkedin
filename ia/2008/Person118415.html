Alan Murphy’s Summary

Alan Murphy provides strategic counsel on F5’s technology to analyst, press, partners, customers, and others. Specializing in F5’s virtualization and security platforms, Alan is responsible for evaluating and analyzing current technical market trends and the overall virtualization and security landscapes. He also assists Product Management and Development with product lifecycle definitions and requirements, based on market analysis and corporate goals. In his F5 tenure, Alan has developed architecture for virtualized, secure, and robust large-scale network infrastructure components. He has over 14 years of experience in Information Technology.

Alan is an accomplished presenter, with a breadth of speaking experience both in the United States and abroad. Notable appearances include a keynote on Application Security at the Network Times 2007 Security Visions conference in Korea and a featured session on Application Security at 2007’s 3rd Annual Banking and Finance Technology Forum in Hong Kong, among many other internal and external events.

Alan is also the primary author of TheVirtualDC blog, where he and his team waxes poetic on security and management issues facing the virtual data center of today and tomorrow.
