Dan Perlea’s Summary

Since I have graduated from UC Davis with a degree in Performing Arts/Dance, I have taken on many aspects of the Arts. Beginning with performing on stage, I danced for the Sacramento Opera, acted in a one man show, and performed as part of an ensamble in the 2005 Fat Cow Theatre Festival. As a theatre technician and scenic/lighting/sound designer, I have constructed sets, hung and ran lights, set up sound for a veriety of performing arts pieces. My fascination with the performing arts, led me to video taping and photographing performances which has brought me to where I am now. I am a photographer, constantly expanding my portfolio trying to diversify and enhance my talents.

Dan Perlea’s Specialties:

Set design, drafting, construction, lighting design, supervising back stage crew/running of shows,
