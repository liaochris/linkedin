Richard Puyt’s Summary

Richard Puyt is a business economist and a Certified Management Consultant. Based on this knowlegde and expertise he is often involved in strategic and innovative projects by governement organisations and semi govenment agencies (like municipalities or provences). He often operates in political environments and knows the rules of the game. Topics like innovative ways of organizing and principality have his special intrest. He frequently publices about these topics on the weblog www.innovatieforganiseren.nl

Richard Puyt is bedrijfskundige en gecertificeerd management consultant (lid van de Ooa).
