Doug Devitre’s Summary

Doug Delivers low budget, high impact technology solutions for real estate professionals and associations to maximize return on investment for both money and time. Build an online brand using cutting edge web 2.0 methods and communicate your message specific to the type of consumer that you serve.

Doug’s philosophy on using technology in real estate:
•Know the why not the how.
•Outsource weaknesses.
•Age is not an excuse for not knowing.
•Work smarter not harder.
•Spend less than everyone else.
