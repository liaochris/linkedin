Cheryl Kanekar’s Summary

Film-maker, specializing in directing, writing and editing of documentary, fiction and informational videos/ films. Own editing suite, with Final Cut Pro, Motion, Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, DVD Studio Pro, etc. Also open to working at other editing studios, if needed.

Experience editing marketing and informative videos, TV promos, trailers, web video, documentaries, feature films and short narratives, and in DVD authoring and video compression. Skills: motion graphics, beautifying video to make it look film-like, rhythmic editing to music, and saving projects with problematic footage, both visually and in terms of the story.

Cheryl Kanekar’s Specialties:

Film and video editing, direction and writing.
