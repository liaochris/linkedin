Norm Daniels’s Summary

• Started with Home Design and Planning for RB Mohling Const of Poway.
• Continued with Design in Print Media and Illustration for Hume Lake Camps, Pine Valley Conference Ctr, Lots of Childrens Books and Periodicals.
• Took up "Fine Art" in 1995. My style would be called "Representational Impressionism. Paint in Studio and "En Plein Air" (On Location) primarily landscape and Surfing Images.
• Began illustrating for Surfer Magazine in 2006. VERY FUN!!
• Represented by Steve McKibben of McKibben Studios in Corona Del Mar
• Galleries in Corona Del Mar, Huntington Beach, Laguna Beach, San Diego and Leucadia, o-yea and one in Ocean City New Joisey.
• Meanwhile I am Direcor of Development for Pine Valley Bible Conf Center east of San Diego in the Cuyamaca Mountains....
