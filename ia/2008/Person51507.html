COMMUNICATOR: A team player skilled at bridging diverse people and organisations. I have been particularly successful in bringing technical, sales, finance, and creative teams together to deliver to a common goal.
CREATIVE: A successful history of delivering creative products and solutions. Frequently used as a catalyst in product development and overall strategy.

TECHNICAL:Skilled technologist and digital media evangelist, adept at keeping one foot firmly planted in business requirements, and the other in the technology state-of-the-art.

Founder member of the team which established the Australian arm of Two Way TV and responsible for deploying Interactive Services on all digital TV platforms. Having deployed on Austar and Optus Pay TV platforms, which also involved launching the first OpenTV EN2 games service and the first Liberate 1.2 service in the world and the first OpenTV deployment for the Two Way TV group.

As well as part of the senior management team involved in the day to day running of Two Way TV Australia, managing of the technical, development (OpenTV, Java, C/C++, J2ME, Symbian) and creative teams, the role requires operating at board level, formulating business and technical strategy as well as providing strategic and technical advice for new commercial deals, contracts and opportunities with Pay TV and Free to Air broadcasters across Asia.

Currently focused on expanding the business opportunities for Two Way TV Australia into Asia and across digital devices and delivery channels, from iTV to mobile devices, and maximizing our advantages in these areas by employing my creative knowledge and experience in Television and Broadband production, as well as a proven technological background in producing new entertainment formats with new technology.
