Tim Ferris’s Summary

* My service business is an independent sole proprietorship by intention and by design. It has its first duty to the clients, not to shareholders or to Wall Street. My purpose is to be able to serve my clients, and my community, without conflicts and without compromise.
* I work holistically on their existing business and ownership structures, their tactics and strategies, and their on-going financial plans.
* I strive to integrate their money and other assets with their spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being, to give them a meaningful connection to their money and their other possessions.
* I try to accelerate their successes by focusing on the elimination of unnecessary outflows to debt, taxation, and other less obvious expenses.
* I discourage debt that doesn't make sense and encourage clients to set up systems to provide for their own financing on their projects, leases, and purchases.
* I help manage and coordinate, as primary advisor, other expert, professional, and community relationships, strengthening their networks, making them more functional, building them out.
* I help determine best practices and executions.
* I continually strive for efficiency, economy, and balance.
* I keep our processes based in behavioral philosophies, open-space theories, and open-source implementations.
* I construct formal, written, comprehensive plans and back them up by orchestrating the planned implementations.
* I act as their guide and their companion as they make the transition from the second-wave industrial economy to the third-wave knowledge economy.
* I am my clients' advocate and protector; I am their voice, as needs arise, not only in my professional associations but also in the community at large.
