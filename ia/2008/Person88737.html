Jennifer Tidwell’s Summary

I studied abroad in France during my junior year of college. Although language study is not my forte, I developed a passion for study abroad. So after completing a BA from George Washington University, I completed a MA at School for International Training. All my professional experience has focused on study abroad marketing at organizations such as Youth For Understanding, CIEE, CUNY, Global College, and now CET Academic Programs. As the Director of Marketing at CET, I have responsibilities for marketing strategy and coordination, campus relations management, website and promotional material production, public relations and market analysis.
