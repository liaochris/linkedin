John Glenn’s Summary

My diverse background includes both the corporate and entepreneurial worlds, so I have a firm understanding of each. I perform several roles - as a business consultant, as director of business operations at a financial & tax planning firm, and as a residential Realtor focusing on the far western suburbs of Chicagoland.

The link through all these is capitalizing on what makes people and companies tick, helping people live more rewarding lives.
