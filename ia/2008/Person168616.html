Sandra Noble’s Summary

NOBLE & ASSOCIATES CONSULTING: Reducing the STRESS of Software Implementations Worldwide. Our business analysts help our clients select the right software, implement commercial off-the-shelf software (COTS) and cure Post Implementation Distress™.
