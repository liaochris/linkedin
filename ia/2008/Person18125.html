Aude Mathey’s Summary

Publicity Manager at Le Progres Group (within the EBRA Group, the most important group of regional press in France), I am currently in charge of the publicity and PR campaigns of the region public institutions and other several firms (EDF, Casino, Olympique Lyonnais).

Privately, I am also directing a blog on best practices for cultural and PR professionals. I will be soon add an English version.
If you wish to participate or get some more information : http://www.culture-communication.fr

Author of "The Virtual Museum : What future for Digital Culture ?", I have been invited to the international conference "Communicating the Museum", taking place in Madrid from July 4th to 7th, at the Museo Nacional del Prado, Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofia and Museo Thyssen-Bornemisza.
For more information : http://www.communicatingthemuseum.com

Aude Mathey’s Specialties:

branding and identity, global communication, Internet marketing, wireless museums, events production, patronage negotiation, cultural communication, museums, theatres, opera, dance, art foundations, science foundations, media campaigns, marketing and buzz
