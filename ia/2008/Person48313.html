Dave Savoy’s Summary

A bit about myself...

As Relationship Manager with KeyBank, I took a proactive role in achieving clients’ financial goals while over-achieving in my sales results and coaching and encouraging my sales staff to do the same. Through adaptability and effective project management, I have been able to positively influence the level of teamwork within my sales team and add value for clients through greater customer satisfaction. Service remained a major focus for over-achievement of my sales and individual branch development goals and I developed lasting relationships with clients and fellow financial advisors. These relationships and exposure to such diversity inspired me to seek opportunities to pursue an MBA education. During this MBA experience, I have also worked with Intel in the semiconductor industry in an IT business analysis endeavor.

During this time, I had the great opportunity of working with Amazon.com on a student consultant basis in 2005. This project was a joint effort of my university and Amazon.com to grant would-be MBA’s exposure through analyzing and presenting a strategic plan to address an issue immediately facing the company. Towards that end, I led my team of four graduate students to develop a method for strategic deployment of Amazon.com’s Search Inside the Book tool and proselytized our results to Amazon.com upper management. Presentational skills with a particular aptitude for report and documentation merged in delivering recommendations that were well received and ultimately applied by the company.

Now I am with Xerox as a Market Forecast Analyst and pursuing a Green Belt in LSS... and the story goes on!
