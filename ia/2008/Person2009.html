Achyut Menon’s Summary

Founder & Managing Director of a search firm- (www.optionsindia.com)

Options Executive Search Private Limited was started in the year 1992 with the mission of providing a personalized touch to the professional service of finding the right person for every job, by ensuring the right job for every person.

Member of the Executive Recruiters Association, a body that defines, establishes and promotes high ethical and professional standards for executive recruitment in India.(http://www.era.org.in/)

Presently, we have an active network of similar minded recruitment consultants, based across India, in the 10 major cities.This helps us provide a single window access to all candidates, and an offline presence in major markets in India for clients wanting to have an extended arm.

I'm looking to make more friends and be of help to people in anyway possible - professionally for career /business development and I will try best to draw through my experience to help you explore possibilities.

During the last 6 -7 years, we have helped over a few hundred Indian professionals, employed abroad and desirous of relocating to India, to find the right opportunities matching their competency and aspirations.

We have helped several startups ( Indian, large multinational companies, venture capital funded portfolio companies and others) to identify their core team to kick start and drive their businesses in India.
