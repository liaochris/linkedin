Diana Gilbert’s Summary

Diana is a “Big 4” CPA with over 20 years of corporate and operational finance experience in a broad variety of industries including medical devices, capital equipment, software, telecommunications, construction, banking, nonprofit and government. Diana is a Consultant at RoseRyan, the Finance Dream Team.

Diana is Board President for Grail Family Services (GFS), a family service agency located in the Mayfair neighborhood of San Jose. GFS provides family support programs with a focus on family literacy, adult education, and the empowerment of community residents. They are recognized as an incubator of high quality, community-focused, results-oriented programs that respond to residents' needs. To strengthen their impact, GFS partners with over 20 organizations to implement programs both on-site and off-site, to refer volunteers, and to conduct program evaluations.

Diana joined the Junior League of San Jose in 1994 and is currently serving as its Placement Chair and on its Board of Directors. She has served in a wide variety of roles ranging from community to fundraising and finance to membership, including: Community Projects Vice President, Fundraising and Finance Vice President, fundraiser co-chair for JLSJ’s 2005 Rummage Sale “The Greatest Garage Sale on Earth”, Community Issues Council Director, the Executive Committee for two years, the Board of Directors for three years, project chair for two first year community projects, Placement Committee, Membership Committee, several fundraiser steering committees, and several VRL committees. Diana is passionate about the Junior League’s impact in our community and is delighted to be mentoring its members this year.
