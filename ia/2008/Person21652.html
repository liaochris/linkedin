Elaine Bennett’s Summary

I write speeches, op-eds and by-lined articles for C-suite executives, and award-winning annual reports and marketing materials for their companies. Yeah, my work is terrific -- Warren Buffett has said I "have a great ear and turn straight thinking into straight writing" -- but what really makes my clients happy is that they know I’ll meet their deadlines and be responsive to their needs.

My favorite clients know great writing when they read it, but are just too busy to do it themselves. I've worked extensively in the financial services industry and also enjoy writing about public policy, diversity, ethics, talent management, education and the particular challenges facing different industries.

Elaine Bennett’s Specialties:

Sparkling prose; explaining complex concepts in sophisticated, yet easy-to-understand language; humor; heart.
