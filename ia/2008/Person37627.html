Cindy Hartman’s Summary

HARTMAN INVENTORY, LLC
We provide a thorough personal property inventory service to help small business and residential clients maximize insurance claims in the event of fire, tornado, burglary or other disaster. In addition to maximization of insurance, this documentation shortens the time you'll be 'down' since your claims will process and settle more quickly. The photographs and written report are also used to prepare for estate planning, financial planning, moving & storage, litigation, pre-nuptials, divorce settlements and more.

HARTMAN INVENTORY SYSTEMS, LLC
Complete startup kit and 3-year consulting/mentoring service includes equipment, a marketing package, business plan, extremely detailed manual. The manual includes a CD of downloadable worksheets, letters and marketing templates. Group members will also receive up to 36 hours of one-on-one telephone consultation during the first 3 years in business.

We take you through the process of learning how to start an inventory service business because it's easy to START a business, but it's tough STAYING in business. We will help you reduce mistakes, learn how/where to market the service and support you with mentoring and training.
