Jonathan Gilliam’s Summary

With a deep background in business dev, market analysis, and opportunity and relationship management, Jonathan is a partner at OneAccord, a national sales & marketing firm dedicated to accelerating revenue and providing predictable results to clients. The team are experts in market penetration, new product launches, improving flat sales, and increasing valuation, and any event requiring an improved top line.

Jonathan has appeared in international radio and TV broadcasts, worldwide press articles, and conferences as a speaker on high tech industry issues. He is a regular panelist on the NSF's SBIR Program in Washington D.C., where he helps evaluate various technologies for commercialization. He is an advisor and mentor to several area tech companies, and has also served the Selection Committees of Austin Technology and Clean Energy Incubators.

Jonathan currently serves as EVP Strategy & Markets for IDMetric, a software company based in Austin. He also recently served 21st Century Technologies, an analytics software company, as VP Business Development, where he directed business dev and commercialization activities, and led the company's go-to-market strategy.

In 2000, Jonathan joined Deloitte as Sr. Business Development Manager in one of the firm’s fastest-growing practices, where he helped lead BD efforts for DTECT, Deloitte's flagship data mining/fraud detection service. He also served the technology practice at Deloitte, building strategic relationships with growth companies.

Prior to Deloitte, Jonathan was Co-Founder and CEO of Hush Communications, a leading Internet security software company based in Austin, Texas and Dublin, Ireland. Prior to CEO he served as VP of Business Development.

Jonathan received his bachelor's degree from UT-Austin and later graduated from the Rice University publishing program.
