Laurent Pontier’s Summary

Laurent Pontier currently works in Business Objects's R&D Lab in Paris, France.
Prior to Business Objects (now SAP), Laurent was senior software engineer and project manager at 4D SA and worked during 6 years on a Rapid Application Development software suite. Before joining 4D SA in 2000, Laurent spent two years in Menlo Park and Redwood City in silicon valley working on ePicture at Beatware Inc, now Oracle.
