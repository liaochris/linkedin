Juan Miguel Taboada Godoy’s Summary

Centrologic was created in Málaga (Spain) in September of 2005 with the main idea to allow our clients to handle aspects of their operation conected with technology and computing. We work mainly with Free Software and we are producing many projects under GPL license.

Computational Logistic Center (Centrologic) is formed by a group of dynamic professionals with above 10 year experience and high qualifications. Our team is always learning and working with new technologies.

We mainly work with Linux and free software in general. Our main goal is to make your company save money and time. We can provide your company with the latest technologies, strong and stable software and services that will help your business expand.

What makes us better than most competition is quick response. We are specialist in Teleworking. We can work at distance, resolve problems from far. You contact with us and we resolve the problem. You will never need to wait for specialist to arrive, because your problem will be resolved before we leave. Only in exceptional situations we will visit your office.

Our experience in this area is huge and from the beginning Centrologic has been working with clients in Spain and Poland at the same time.

Usually we know before our clients that there is some problem, some service is not working. Forget about problems and half works, join to Centrologic, join to us. Welcome!

Nowaday it is not easy to entrust a young company withouth experience, nor technologicaly old company with experience. In Centrologic you will find experienced and commited people. We are constantly investing in knowledge of our specialists by trainings and implementing new technologies. In this way we can be sure that our clients have the most advanced and recent technology knowledge avaliable.

Your company can keep experimenting or instantly start work with our group of professionals.
