Jan de Mooij’s Summary

A software development expert with 17 years of experience in a variety of positions, Jan currently provides custom development services for different customers, besides maintaining/enhancing parts of the standard SAP/HR system for South Africa, in collaboration with colleagues at SAP Labs (Bangalore).

After working on a variety of SAP projects in South Africa in the roles of developer, technical consultant and independent contractor, Jan joined SAP A.G. (Walldorf, Germany) as a software developer in 1998, where he was part of the development team for the HR module.

Since returning to South Africa in 2002, Jan continued to be be responsible for the development of South African-specific standard functionality for HR and the support of HR customers countrywide via SAP Service Marketplace (OSS) and the periodic release of HR Support Packages (HRSPs) & notes.

His current focus is on SAP custom development for HR & Payroll systems, with a specific interest in enhanced employee interaction scenarios, using ESS/MSS, Workflow, Interactive Forms and the Internet Connection Framework (ICF).

Jan de Mooij’s Specialties:

HR & Payroll technical expertise, workflow, ESS/MSS, ABAP WebDynpro, Adobe / Interactive Forms, Smartforms, SMS/Email connectivity, interfaces, data migration, enhancement concepts including BADI, user exits, infotype development, ABAP OO
All releases up to and including ECC 6.00 / EP 7.00. Jan holds official SAP certifications for : TAW12 : ABAP Workbench (2002); TAWF10 : SAP Business Workflow (2002); TAW12_04 : Development Consultant SAP NetWeaver ‘04 – focus ABAP (2006)
