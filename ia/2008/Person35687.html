Chris Hubbard’s Summary

VeloDyne is a rapidly growing supplier of polymer activation and chemical feed systems primarily for water and waste water treatment systems.

Our VeloBlend and Hydramax polymer systems are becoming the yardstick by which others are measured in the Municipal Water / Wastewater treatment marketplace- almost every major dewatering equipment manufacturer now selects VeloDyne as its OEM supplier.

We are promoted by the best municipal rep firm in almost every territory in the US and are looking for representatives in only a very few remaining areas within the US, Canada, and Mexico. We will be expanding our coverage to Central and South America in the near future, and expanding into the industrial market place worldwide shortly thereafter
