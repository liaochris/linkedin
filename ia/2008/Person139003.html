Navin Poeran’s Summary

I got involved with the internet in 2000, fascinated with everything I saw online, I dreamed of working online and doing “something” online. In 2002 I got a CD with tutorials on making your own websites and in 2004 I launched my first free website – living in a country where internet at that time was more difficult as it is now – it was not a bad timing at all. In 2005 I registered my first .com and at that time I was more involved with PHP & MySQL apart from promoting my website via forums and social networking websites.

In 2006 I started my first job as a full time SEO and I have been working full time since then.

During my full time career I have been to Holland for training, have worked on countless projects and have come up with many-and-many out of the box solutions.
