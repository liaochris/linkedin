Steven Smith’s Summary

Real Taiji Quan--the most articulate, martial & healing movement system. It generates fast fitness, health, healing, and self-defense proficiencies. Based on the Taiji-Quan and Internal Arts training methods from the World Taiji Boxing Association, Real Taiji Quan teaches reality-based martial arts coupled with philosophical principles of living well.

The Martial Art of Tai Chi Chuan utilizes, defines, and builds every practitioner's tension-integrity, energy flow, and breathing power. A dynamic and powerful weapon, Tai Chi's articulate secrets hide in the longest forms, in repetitive drills, and in dynamic-fast and rooted-slow motions.

Qi-gong, Fitness, and Bagua Zhang concepts complete this powerful system. I'll teach you to fight perpetrators, fight disease, fight illness, fight stress, and along the way, I'll show you refined and superior health, conditioning, and longevity.
