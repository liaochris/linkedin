Fotini Liepouri’s Summary

Organic Chemist Ph.D. with focus on the chemistry of Multi-Component Reactions (MCRs) and more specifically in:
1. The development of a novel, fast and efficient one-pot, three-component methodology of synthesizing multi-functionalized allenyl, propargyl and homopropargyl amines both in a racemic as well as asymmetric ways. Artificial allenyl and propargyl α-amino acids, allenyl β-amino alcohols, allenyl β-aminopolyols and propargyl β-amino alcohols were synthesized in a single step by this methodology.
2. The development of a new synthetic methodology towards novel allenyl boron derivatives for a plethora of synthetic applications.
3. Pd-catalyzed annulations of the allenyl amine analogs to a variety of densely functionalized heterocycles.

Also experienced in:
The Synthesis of Fluorescent Ca2+ Indicators.
The Spectroscopic characterization and Ca2+-affinity studies.
The Intracellular visualization of the compartmentalization of the new indicators.

Fotini Liepouri’s Specialties:

1. Extended use of 1H, 13C, 19F, 11B NMR for structure determination. Also IR, UV, Fluorimetry.
2. Analytical techniques: HPLC, GC, GC-MS.
