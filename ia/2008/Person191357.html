Thomas Upchurch, MBCI, CBRM’s Summary

Tom has more than fifteen years of expertise in business continuity management (BCM), governance, risk management, reponse and recovey strategy development, and disaster recovery. He is certified by the Business Continuity Institute (MBCI). Fluent in all aspects of business continuity management, Tom has a proven track record of establishing sustainable BCM programs at numerous global companies and government agencies. He is currently establishing a BS25999 compliant BCM program for Dubai Holding.

Well-versed in project management, Tom has led projects consisting of cross-functional teams larger than 50. Two of his projects have been finalists for Microsoft Office of the Year. He has the ability to explain complex ideas in simple terms and to speak confidently with any audience.
