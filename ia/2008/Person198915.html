Wilton Alston’s Honors:

U.S. Patent 4,452,899 – Method for Metering Biological Fluids. The method is for repeatedly and accurately depositing predetermined amounts of fluid onto generally planar analysis slides. Fluid is aspirated into a disposable metering tip which is rapidly moved. During movement of the tip into the metering position, a partial vacuum is created on the fluid in the tip, causing the fluid to be drawn up into the tip and thereby preventing fluid from being prematurely expelled onto the slide.

U.S. Patent 5,246,327 – Apparatus and Method for Precisely Dispensing Solid Materials. A rigid container has an open upper end and at least one hole in the bottom of the container. The container has a flexible liner with a neck portion which is folded back around the upper end of the container to form a cuff that is sealed against the sides of the container. A gas is injected through the hole or holes in the bottom of the container into the space between the flexible liner and the container.
