Steven Shimek’s Summary

I serve as SVP Business Development & Marketing for Ruder Finn, one of the largest independent integrated communication firms in the world.

We work with a myriad of successful companies ranging from Mitsubishi Automotive North America to StubHub, The North Face, Novartis, Longs Drugs, Demand Media, Southwest Airlines and The NFL Players Association.

Prior to Ruder Finn I worked with communications and investor relations officers distributing their earnings and press releases, photos, and, on many occasions, complete broadcast productions. In addition, I assisted my clients with online monitoring and media relations tools. My clientele were primarily those within the entertainment industry/studios and new media corporations.

As one of PR Newswire's senior business development officers, I established working relationships and contractual alliances with some of the largest companies throughout the U.S. My client retention rate during this tenure was nearly 100%.
