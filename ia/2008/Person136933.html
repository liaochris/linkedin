Muki Haklay’s Summary

Active in Geographical Information Systems R&D since 1988, experienced in industrial and academic R&D.

Recent clients include: Ordnance Survey, ODPM, Camden Police, Sitescope, Argent King's Cross, London Sustainability Exchange, URBED and Geofutures ltd.

Supervised research projects in collaboration with Multimap, M&S, Shell, BP, 1Spatial, Dr. Foster Intelligence, UnLtd, and OpenStreetMap.

Taught on many aspects of GIS, including bespoke training.

Muki Haklay’s Specialties:

Lecturer, researcher, consultant for public and private sector bodies.

Experienced in designing and implementing GIS projects using ArcGIS, Mapinfo, Manifold GIS, Oracle Spatial, Radius Topology, Go Loader and Neogeography technologies.

Experienced in usability research and evaluation of geospatial technologies.
