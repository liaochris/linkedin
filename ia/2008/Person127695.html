Meghan (McInerny) Wilker’s Summary

My motto? I never give up. I either get what I want, or I change my mind. (I totally stole that from a movie that no one else saw: Dream for an Insomniac. Words to live by.)

I *heart* the Internets, and I've spent the past 8 years or so living, breathing, blogging and working online in some capacity -- from project management to creative direction and most everything in between.

I've been lucky to work for a variety of companies: from indie web boutiques to advertising agencies and am now lucky enough to work for -- and with -- a group of people I would lay down and die for.

Dirty little secret? I have an uncontrollable urge to sign up for every social networking site that is launched.
