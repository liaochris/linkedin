Roald Sieberath’s Summary

Working with teams, to turn innovative ideas into a reality that makes business sense.

I have an experience of more than 15 years in ICT (and more specifically Internet and telco applications) with roles including CTO and CEO (ranging from architectural design to general management). I bring an innovative, "fresh look" analysis, and an entrepreneurial, "hands-on" execution.

My goal is to get involved with dynamic teams in startups and SMEs, and bring my entrepreneurial and business experience to help them to take advantage of technological innovation, and deliver value to the (global) market.

I have brought to market several innovations (some patent-pending), setup several projects and ventures (streaming, search engines, mobile, wireless, telemedicine), pitched to major Venture Capitalists, raised 5 M$, hired teams, opened international markets.

I also have experience in networking teams, setting up international R&D projects, submitted to the European Commission's FP6/FP7 programs.

I also have a keen interest in the broader societal implications of business (environment, ethics, social entrepreneurship).

My technological interests are / have been / will be in : web 2.0, social networking and collective intelligence, bioinformatics, ICT in Healthcare, grid computing, storage, search engines, IT & software quality (ITIL, CMMI,...), wi-fi, digital identity management, open source software.

By education, I have both a deep technical background (engineering degree in computer science, UC Louvain), and solid background in Business (up to an MBA) from Brussels (Solvay), Louvain and Stanford Business Schools.

Connecting on LinkedIn : I'm open to connection requests *provided* that you play a role in the 'business ecosystem' mentioned above : companies using ICT innovation to leverage their business ; IT, eHealth or Internet startups ; VCs and Business Angels ; Business Developers ; Service Providers ; and of course, fellow Entrepreneurs,...

  • Founder, General Manager

    Nearyoo

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    October 2006 — Present (2 years)

    I am working on this startup, currently in stealth mode, gearing toward a public beta.
    The idea for Nearyoo stems from a discontent with current social networks and communities, mostly self-absorbed in their 'global' and 'virtual' aspects.
    Nearyoo can be described as a social community that brings people together at the *local* level, and in the *real world*. Simple, but catering to very real needs.
    I devised the general goals, strategy, architecture of the project, and currently oversee the technical development.
    I'm now devoting time to the business development and marketing aspects of it, looking for partners for mutually beneficial relationships.
