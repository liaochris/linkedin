Richard Obiso’s Summary

Dr. Rik Obiso is currently the President of The Microbe Company, a premier consulting company that specializes in project management, experimental design and analysis, and, Research and Development activities in the Biotechnology, in vitro diagnostics, and pharmaceutical Industries. Rik was most recently a project manager and special research faculty at the Virginia Bioinformatics Institute at Virginia Tech. Rik was formerly the Director of Life Sciences Luna Innovations Incorporated, researching and developing innovative technologies in for the detection of bacterial pathogens and the use of bacteriophage to detect, provide therapeutic treatment, and decontaminate bio-threats and clinically relevant pathogens. Dr. Obiso has more than 13 years of experience in the biotechnology field with an emphasis on basic research, product design and development, and project management. Dr. Obiso was a post-doc at Los Alamos National Laboratory in bio-defense diagnostics and has held senior level positions at Digene Corporation and IGEN International and has held faculty appointments at East Tennessee State University and Virginia Tech. He has been published in several highly-respected scientific journals, presented at numerous scientific meetings, and has successfully launched numerous products in the research and diagnostic markets, including the launch of multiple federally-regulated in vitro diagnostic products.
