Jim H. Moreno’s Summary

Began writing with the Wargamer website. Also penned book and computer game reviews for Epinions, while doing semi-professional photography work (weddings, sports, outdoor, and studio). Picked up by the Armchair General website shortly before the site and magazine went live, and have been contributing book and PC game reviews, and various military history related special projects, including WebOps and The Incorrect Art of War, since then. Now also working with The WarCry Network, writing a column dealing with the art of roleplaying in MMORPG's titled RoleCraft.
