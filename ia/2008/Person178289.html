Sonia Bessamra’s Experience

  • BUSINESS CONSULTING,

    EU EXPERT, FDI PROMOTION

    (Privately Held; International Affairs industry)

    1996 — Present (12 years)

    I have been working for the latest 12 years as an international expert on investment and trade promotion within EC funded programs mainly on the MEDA region (investment promotion tools, capacity building, trade promotion, development of marketplaces, territorial marketing, benchmarking strategies, etc.).
