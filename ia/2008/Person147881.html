Paul Kleijne’s Summary

Multilingual, focused and thorough business development and trade specialist with Dutch roots and more than 10 years of international experience under his belt. Direct access to relevant networks in Europe and North America within the public and private sector. Educated in several areas, ranging from technology to business and (business and real estate) law. Feels comfortable in marketing and sales positions. Aims to bridge the gap between companies/organizations and attractive overseas markets.
