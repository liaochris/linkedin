Chad Olson’s Summary

I have an entrepreneurial mind-set with a proven track record of increasing shareholder wealth through successfully building and turning around companies from idea and early stage to vibrant multi-state and international, profitable, going concern enterprises.

You'll see that I have a deep and diversified background in franchising. As CEO, I’ve started and lead four franchisors in retail and consumer services for over 23 years. The companies that I’ve founded and lead have sold over 400 franchises--the highest profile being our 210 store franchise chain called Pro Image, in 46 states and five countries. I lead this company to 1000 system-wide employees and doing $100 million in annual sales. I’ve innovated and grown several other concepts and positioned them to be franchised before selling them. For years, I’ve consulted for and evaluated numerous start-up franchise opportunities and developed my own model to evaluate the viability and potential of franchise concepts.

I've sat on many franchise and non-franchise Boards, including being the Boards financial expert and Treasurer. I've worked extensively with private equity firms and am experienced in raising investment capital, doing M&A transactions, and taking a company through the IPO process.
