Harlan Baum’s Summary

Harlan Baum is Vice President for new business for Larry Baum & Associates. LBA is a professional management consulting firm specializing in organizational planning, financial planning and fundraising counsel for not-for-profit institutions and organizations.

Before joining Larry Baum & Associates in 2006, Harlan was West Coast Advertising Sales Manager for the New York-based publishing company Penthouse Media Group International where he established and managed the interactive gaming division serving clients including Activision, 3DO, Acclaim, and Virgin Interactive.

From 1992 through 1996 Harlan was Director of Development for the Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County where he oversaw all fundraising programs including strategic planning and development of plans specifically designed to meet near-term as well as long-term fundraising goals.

Harlan worked for the Los Angeles County Medical Association as Director of Development where he provided fundraising leadership to the Foundation's annual giving, alumni relations, special events and major gifts fundraising efforts.

Harlan holds a B.S. degree from the
Graziadio School of Business and Management
Pepperdine University
