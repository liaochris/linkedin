Paul Gaffney’s Summary

Chief Operating Officer, Desktone, Inc. Delivering the promise of virtual desktop computing: slashing costs by cutting complexity.

Executive Vice President and head of Supply Chain at Staples: transformed a multi-billion-dollar supply chain into a lean, synchronized operation that delivers materially better sales and margin results with substantial improvements in use of working capital. Also EVP & CIO at Staples - lead the entire information systems global team at Staples to deliver technology innovation and high service levels at industry-leading low costs.

CEO and President of Website Pros: launched the company, raised over $60MM in venture capital and established long-term strategy leading to an eventual IPO in 2006.

Executive Vice President at Office Depot: built one of the world’s largest e-commerce business from scratch.

Senior Vice President of Technology at Schwab, transitioned technology from a narrow-market active trader boutique to a broader active trader marketplace, significantly accelerating revenue and profit growth.
