David Fradkin [LION]’s Summary

Experienced leader with P&L responsibilities plus expertise in Sales and Marketing Management, Strategy Development, Product Management, Project Management, Catalog Design, Process Optimization and effective leadership of people in the promotion of products and services.

David joined Manhattan Resources in 2008 as an Executive Recruiter after 18 successful years in corporate sales and marketing leadership. David transitioned to recruiting from the environmental services industry, where he led Business Development, Marketing, and Change Management activities at Allied Waste. Prior to that, David was Director of Product Management at United Rentals, where he was instrumental in launching and cultivating the Contractor Supplies Division. He led all Contractor Supplies sales and marketing activities company-wide and created platforms for demand generation, product marketing strategy, and vendor certification.

David also spent many years at Saint-Gobain Abrasives, where he held a variety of sales and marketing positions and honed his skills in industrial sales and industrial distribution while focusing primarily on the Oil and Gas E&P and Petrochemical sectors. David began his career at Dell Computer selling to the Federal Government and Institutions of Higher Education. In his spare time David coaches girls youth softball, and in recent years has served on the Board of Directors of his Community Improvement Association. David lives in Houston with his wife and two daughters.
