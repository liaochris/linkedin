Laura Akin’s Summary

Email: AkinRandT@gmail.com

As an accomplished HR Generalist, Benefits Consultant and leader with fifteen years of account management experience in leadership roles with Hewitt Associates, IBM Global Services, and Georgia Pacific, I bring a 'hands-on' knowledge to recruiting for these and similar roles.

My goal in recruiting is to establish a long-term relationship with my candidates. I have a strong interest and desire to learn about people ... what they do (or what they want to do!) and what motivates them.

These points have been used to describe my skills and experience:
• Expertise in benefits (pension and health and welfare), HR consulting, payroll.
• Impeccable client support, internal coordination and client relationship building through effective communication and technical expertise.
• Highly developed consultative selling skills with emphasis on “needs analysis”, presentation, networking, and delivery of superior services.
• Proven track record of driving positive results through effective new business development and expense control.
• Developed strong foundation in hiring and using outsourcing solutions.
• Outstanding presentation, training and communication skills with demonstrated sales aptitude.
• HRIS support, implementation and training: expertise in PeopleSoft and familiarity with SAP.

If you're interested in establishing a rapport with a recruiter who will work in your best interest for the right job match, email me at:
