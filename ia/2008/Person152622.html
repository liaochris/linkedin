Pascal Podvin’s Summary

Podvin – CEO of Compario - is a C level executive, and Business Angel, with expertise in B2B software.
Dual citizen (American / French), with 20 years of international experience in General Management and Sales, Podvin has started 4 businesses, conducted successful VC funding, and helped 3 established businesses to grow.
Most recently ran blueKiwi software as COO.
Previously responsible for Blue Martini's European business, and worldwide sales ops.
Managed Vignette Corp in Southern Europe, $30mil sales, 100 employees.
Served as VP of Sales at i2 Technologies.
PeopleSoft employee number 58. Worldwide strategic alliance executive role at PeopleSoft, based in the United States, then founded first subsidiary of PeopleSoft in Europe.
Sales executive at ADP/GSI North America.
MBA from ESSEC international business school.
Speaks French, German, Spanish.
Local Execution USA (8 years) / Europe (12 years)
