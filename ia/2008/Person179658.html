Stacy Laviolette’s Summary

Stacy Laviolette formerly served as commercial property manager for Oakridge Centre (Ivanhoe Cambridge). During her tenure in the real estate industry, Laviolette oversaw the physical aspects of property branding through five senses marketing. Prior to her career in commercial management she spent over 15 years in the fashion trade where she had oversight of public relations, creative direction, sales, marketing and brand development.

Laviolette is known as a creative powerhouse with strong business acumen. In 1998 she developed an international apparel company and split her time between New York, Los Angeles and London. Enthusiastic about sharing her expertise she is an avid volunteer with arts societies and pens a fashion and design blog, “Bijou Living”.

Laviolette holds an RSA/CELTA from the University of Cambridge, Business Development Certification from the British Columbia Institute of Technology and a B.A. in Social History from the University of British Columbia
