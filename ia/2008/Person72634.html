Greg Papanikolaou’s Summary

The combination of my experience as a management consultant (in a number of industries and functional areas) and my academic focus have enabled me to build a strong business acumen. These skills include but are not limited to the valuation of investments, projects, companies and mergers, as well as the ability to resolve business issues and develop innovative yet pragmatic ideas and initiatives that are aligned with the organization’s overarching strategic objectives. Throughout my work experience and the examination of numerous company cases at the business school, it has become clear to me that although strategy formulation is an important activity for any corporation, successful companies are those that are able to execute better than their competition. I strongly believe that my experience, skills and passion for continuous learning and development will enable me to assist a company to determine which investments and activities they need to focus on in order to attain a sustainable competitive advantage.

Greg Papanikolaou’s Specialties:

• Re-design processes to achieve targeted levels of performance

• Apply an issue based approach to frame, analyze and resolve business issues.
• Demonstrate strong analytical, communication, facilitation and research skills
