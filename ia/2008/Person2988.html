Adi Lev’s Summary

Adi Lev has wide background in advising, designing, and implementation complex tasks and has background in various fields, including J2EE web technology, Net protocols, mobility, Market study, security, CAD CAM, image and pattern recognition background through research and the development.

Adi hold a co-patent on bio technology ( U.S. patent 5,395,498 dated March 7, 1995) and pilot projects for many startup companies and technologies. He has raised mid stage startup companies in technology value like: Cimatron, Robcad, Memco, Eshed Robotec, Cardonet & Zlango and pending patent on encryption.

He had BSC degree 82 in EE and MSC 88 in Electrical Engineering on computers in the area of Digital processing, Pattern Recognition and Artificial intelligence with Prof. Miriam Furst, ”on adaptive pen recognition system using human learn and forget models”, an alternative to neuron-net learning, two years prior to the introduction of the first pen computers.

VP and R&D manager of Magam graphical systems , leading one of the first pioneer of OCR product on the PC, based on statistical font detection and learning system. This OCR technology bought later by Calera, Kodak now.

Co-managed Enterview/Penquest, with Gidi Cohen, a self sustain software house, which design and developed Graphical and Pen Survey and analyze systems, (it educational edition was teach on marketing in high school) and was a pioneer in Computerized Sport Evaluation as the main contributor to Wingate Institute software systems,and analyze system of
Neurological Lab in Belinson

Adi publish various web articles on Java technologies, including J2EE, J2ME and the founder of JSP section on www.webmaster.org.il , and its wikipedia Hebrew terms He created open source ASP2JSP & OOLP projects on www.java.net.

Adi Lev founder of forum HTPC in www.hometheater.co.il on video technologies, writer in Ynet and contributes to community by his personal web site in www.chelem.org.il.
