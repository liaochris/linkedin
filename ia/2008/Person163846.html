    (Management Consulting industry)

    June 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Roisin Markham is an artist, business consultant, social entrepreneur and mum to three small boys.

    She combines her creative savvy, business acumen and strategic vision with her passion for people. Enabling individuals and organisations to set their business or personal goals, following them through to achieve their own success is a theme for her business and creative ventures.

    Roisin primarily works with entrepreneurs, business owner/managers and not for profit organisations to enhance revenue and productivity, creating strong marketing and business strategies and develop career satisfaction. Roisin’s approach mixes personal development with business coaching.

  • Artist

    (Arts and Crafts industry)

    November 2004 — Present (3 years 11 months)

    Roisin Markham’s arts practice includes personal art works, workshops and unusual events for individuals and groups (not for profit organisations and businesses).

    Ballygarrett Art Studio offers paintings, photography, fine art prints and handmade textiles for sale. Viewing is by appointment. Some work can be viewed online, see links below. Certain collections are not published online and can be seen exclusively at the studio.
    Roisin Markham has an excellent range of work and prices. Corporate rates are available on purchases of five or more works. Irish companies can avail of tax breaks if purchasing art to enhance their office environment see www.revenue.ie .
    Roisin’s work will brighten any office and also makes an excellent corporate or special gift.

    She is an advocate of creative arts for all. Currently as an artist and social entrepreneur Roisin is exploring ways to bring opportunity and access to art and creative experiences for communities.
