Melissa Villanueva’s Summary

Born & raised in the heartland of America, Melissa Villanueva has always been fascinated by productions which speak to the human heart and touch the mind.

She grew up passionate about artistic expression, especially Theatre and Dance. In 1996, her first poem was published as part of a collection from students across the United States.

While working her way through college, Melissa got a job cleaning out the video vault at Flagler Productions in Kansas City. Soon, she was determined to use her passion for artistic expression, coupled with the power of technology, to reach larger audiences.

Since that time, Melissa has flourished under the pressure of live events, as well as the tight deadlines of creative production. She is capable and comfortable in the roles of Producer, Writer, Director, and Stage Manager. She has contributed to a broad spectrum of events, from widescreen productions for the Wal-Mart Corporation to satellite broadcasts for the American Century awards.

She has worked side by side with Liam Neeson, Gary Busey, Charles Durning, Dr. Maya Angelou, Antonio Banderas, Jeffery Katzenberg, Will Smith, Halle Berry, Susan Lucci, Col. Tommy Franks, Coach John Wooden, Cal Ripkin Jr., Derek Jeter, Phil Vasser, Brooks & Dunn, Mr. T, Carl Witherspoon, Michael Buffer, William Goldberg, Bryan Adams, Cindy Crawford, Mary Kate & Ashley Olsen, Jeff Gordan, Richard Petty and American Idol’s Taylor Hicks, Carrie Underwood, and Diana DeGarmo.

Melissa loves collaborating with others as much as she enjoys finding the story in everyone. Her incredible empathy provides her with the true gift of stirring emotion; not only in the subjects she interviews, but also in the audience she is intending to reach.

Melissa is currently the producer of Resting Places, a documentary narrated by Liam Neeson. Resting Places explores in depth roadside memorials around the world and the impact they have on society. For more information, please visit www.RestingPlacesDoc.com.

Melissa Villanueva’s Specialties:

Since 1999, I have worked on commercials, feature films, documentaries, corporate videos, and live event productions. As a Producer/Director, I've led crews as small as 2 to as large as 200, created productions for audiences the size of a small boardroom to a full arena, and been the client contact in almost every production. Serving a variety of clients, I have learned to exercise my creativity within tight deadlines, corporate messaging, and limited budgets.
