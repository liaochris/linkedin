Charles S. Assaf’s Summary

Mr. Assaf has more than 25 years experience in both IT and Financial Services. Since 2007 he been working with RPM Technologies Corp. of Toronto, building an advanced IDA compliant Brokerage Front and Back-Office system.

He also provides IT consulting through Harley-Bass Inc. specializing in Information Privacy and Security, and Market Data Services. In a joint venture with PC Quote Canada, Harley-Bass has recently launched the MyCompany™ market data system for BlackBerry devices.

In 2005 he founded Technology-Matters Inc. providing the protectAFeed Digital Courier System, and prior to that was Head of Technology for Standard Securities Capital Corporation in Toronto.

In 2004 he was Director of RFID with Atos Origin Global Consulting & Systems Integration based in Brussels, and from 2000 to 2003 he was in Montréal with Schlumberger, where he launched global programmes for Wireless LAN Technical Consulting and Hutchison 3 's wireless Product and Services Security network.

In the 1990's he architected the Equity and Fixed Income technology for TD Waterhouse Canada, and held responsibility for strategic technology planning for TDW's foreign brokerage acquisitions. Through Harley-Bass he also provided real-time information delivery systems to TD Bank, Midland Walwyn, and the Alberta Stock Exchange.

Mr. Assaf began his professional career in 1983 as a clerk in the stock cage at Davidson Partners Limited, a mid-sized brokerage firm in Toronto, was a stock broker with them from 1984 to 1988, and then moved into the market data business with Dataline Canquote (now part of Thomson Reuters).

Mr. Assaf has a Master of Science degree in Information Technology from the University of Liverpool, studied Marketing at Ryerson University in Toronto, and through the Canadian Securities Institute has completed the Canadian Securities Course (1984 and 2005), the RR Manual, Conduct and Practices Handbook, and Investment Management Techniques.
