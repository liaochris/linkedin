  • Paper Delivery Girl

    Journal News

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Newspapers industry)

    June 1973 — August 1973 (3 months)

    I was only 10 years old when I had my first job of delivering the Journal News newspaper. I would walk door to door not only at the three large trailer parks across the street from my house, but also all the way down Dixie Highway and onto the side avenues. It was quite a task for a 10 year old, carrying two large bags of newspapers, not to mention it was also my job to fold the newspapers and put rubber bands around them, and then I'd throw them onto the porch of the houses. I had to leave because all that weight of the newspaper bags hung on my shoulders was hurting my knees.
