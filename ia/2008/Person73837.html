Chris Dargiewicz’s Summary

I am a very open, creative person who ALWAYS thinks OUTSIDE THE BOX.
There is a saying by a very famous, intelligent guy called Einstein:
"The definition of insanity is doing the same thing over and over again and expecting different results.”
So I go with the flow whatever life brings me and I try to make something out of it.
In other words, whenever I face a "daunting" project I approach it analyse it
and think of a best solution.
And I stay cool and friendly all the time.
Even trying to tame my ego I think its good.

Marketing:
- customer is your boss and if he doesn't like it, you are FIRED!
- PPC, SEO??? GIVE ME A BREAK! I want to know a story. Whats yours then?
- Advertising? No, thanks I get sick of this stuff!!
Give me something that will actually make my life simpler.
- Go out to your customers asking what they REALLY want, not what you think they want.
- "We have to jump in this YouTube thing..." Aaa, well what about our marketing?
Is it new, fresh, innovative? If not you are just fooling yourself with the gimmicks.
