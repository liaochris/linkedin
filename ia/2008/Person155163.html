Steven Radlinger’s Summary

Since 1994, Radlinger has lead the startup and launch, from concept to product development, market/business development, fund raising and commercialization, of three highly innovative WI-based companies ranging from Internet pure plays to consumer electronics and transportation.

Today, he continues to seek out innovative and disruptive technologies to commercialize.

Radlinger networks extensively to secure strategic partnership relationship and in doing so leverages another parties expertise in a chosen field to facilitate the successful commercialization of the desired product or service.

Radlinger possesses a keen understanding of all aspects of marketing - grassroots, traditional, viral, cooperative & co-branding partnerships and Internet including online-offline integration - operations and business development strategy.

Steven Radlinger’s Specialties:

Market, product & business development, online marketing, advertising and branding, customer acquisition and retention, product/service design & development, operations and IP. Proven ability to leverage internal and external partners in finding new ideas and acting on them. Innovation driven. Effective presenter.
