Kellerey Lohman’s Summary

Kellerey founded Fake Grape in 1995 and has been fortunate enough to work with the best companies, people and brands in the country. With this expertise, she has helped many companies and has the opportunity to start up other companies like 180 Fuel a biodiesel production, transportation and marketing company.

From this experience she has learned to synthesize her knowledge of marketing communications strategy from a wide array of sources.
