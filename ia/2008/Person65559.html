Frank McNally’s Summary

My background is in Materials Science and Ceramic Engineering. I currently work in Yield and Device Engineering for MEMS devices and semiconductor integrated circuits. Past work has included reliability, optical switching, fiber optics and a variety of projects involving thin epitaxial films.
