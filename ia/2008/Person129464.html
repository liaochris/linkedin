Michael Carrington’s Summary

As Controller of CBeebies, Michael Carrington is charged with driving forward the BBC’s top performing young children’s brand in a multi-platform world, ensuring its creative ambitions across television, radio, online and interactive outlets. He commissions all content on BBC ONE and BBC TWO; the CBeebies digital channel, CBeebies Radio on BBC7; and the CBeebies website and interactive channel. Michael facilitates the relationships between Programming, Scheduling, On-Air Presentation, Marketing & Communications, and PR. He also has to maintain strong relationships with the independent production community. Michael is a member of the BBC Children’s Board, the BBC Vision Editorial Group, and the BBC Leadership Team.

In his previous role as Head of Animation & Acquisitions for BBC Children’s, Michael made some of BBC Children’s most successful acquisitions including LazyTown and the number one hit Charlie And Lola. He introduced a strategy to commission more UK animation, which resulted in Shaun the Sheep, Animalia, Tronji and The Secret Show.

With more than 18 years in international broadcasting, Michael is a highly experienced television executive with extensive knowledge of content development, channel management and rights exploitation. Throughout his career Michael has worked for some of the world's premier television companies including Network Ten Australia, The Discovery Channel (Europe), The Learning Channel, LEGO Media, and Create TV & Film Ltd.

Michael served on the founding advisory committee for Showcomotion for many years, he is a member of the British Academy of Film & Television, and regularly speaks at industry events.

Michael Carrington’s Specialties:

Content development, Channel management, Branding, Rights exploitation and acquisitions.
