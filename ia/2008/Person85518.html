Javier Lasa Castro’s Summary

Javier Lasa has been working in digital media industry since 1998, with 4 years professional TV experience, 8 years of multimedia and 8 years of Consumer Internet content management experience.

He received his MA in Television Production from Middlesex University and B.Sc degree in Marketing and Advertising form Complutense University, Madrid and B.Sc degree in Information Science form Basque Country University, Bilbao.

His professional interest include new media and electronic market business strategy and digital production of product and services for delivery through multiple devices, multimedia and large scale virtual community management.

Javier Lasa Castro’s Specialties:

Javier Lasa has achieved professional experience in digital multimedia management and production with strong technical and editorial skills gained in TV, multimedia, and Internet industry since 1988.
