Stacey Dycus’s Summary

Dycus has extensive experience coordinating communications for political, nonprofit and business clients.

She helped found Planned Parenthood’s Central Oregon clinic in 1997 and coordinated grassroots lobbying around women’s health issues. As executive director from 2000 to 2002 for Human Dignity Coalition, a social justice organization, she organized media, events and grassroots lobbying efforts around diversity, economic justice and human rights. She also founded KPOV, a progressive community radio station that began airing in June of 2005.

Since 2000, she has worked on local, statewide and national political campaigns including America Coming Together, the Oregon coordinated Democratic campaign and issue campaigns including election reform, health care and taxes. Her experience includes rural voters and strategies.

She has provided event organizing and public relations to businesses and organizations including St. Charles Medical Center, Dell Computers, Deschutes Historical Society and Strictly Organic Coffee.

In 2003, Dycus began working with state Senator Ben Westlund, a moderate Republican, and helped him also win the Democratic nomination for his seat.

In 2005, Dycus, with Michael Selvaggio, formed Oregon Political Staffer LLC, a full service communications firm.

In 2006, Westlund became an independent and Dycus managed his statewide campaign for Governor that gathered 50,000 signatures and raised over half a million dollars and in six months. The OSS-designed website, www.westlundforgovernor.com received Politics1 Site of the Day. So as to not be a spoiler, Westlund withdrew in August, endorsed the Democratic incumbent and, in December, they helped him switch to Democrat.

In the 2007 Oregon Legislative session, OPS developed message and strategy on the Healthy Oregon Act to lower costs, improve quality and provide every Oregonian with health care.

In October 2007, the firm's name was changed to One Source Strategies LLC.

Stacey Dycus’s Specialties:

Consulting, communications, graphic design and internet strategy. We specialize in political and public affairs campaigns and have legislative and policy experience. Issue areas include: health care, land use/ environment, labor, choice and taxes. We know rural and swing strategies.

Clients include: Senator Ben Westlund, Senator Rick Metsger, Dave Barrows & Assoc., Yes for Hillsboro Schools, Oregon Primary Care Assoc., Hope for a Healthy Oregon
