Kent Speakman’s Summary

To give you a better understanding of what I do,
I am the VP of an interactive web development agency that ensures companies maximize their presence on the internet. We understand the microconversions of a customer and create an interactive experience on the web that delivers ROI through collaboration with our clients. We are based out of downtown Calgary, and have a wide range of clients across North America and we've recently expanded into Europe. www.thinksuitcase.com

With a decade's experience in strategic marketing and communications combined with a hands on background in operations and management I am working within my skill sets and strengths at Suitcase Interactive. I am well-suited to managing our key accounts, overseeing business development and working with the strategy team at our agency. As a member of the International Association of Business Communicators, my professional approach ensures that standards and guidelines are met across accounts.

My goals are to continue to add value to the direction of our company, help our clients achieve their objectives, build our team and continue to cultivate our client roster. (and have some fun along the way!)

Feel free to take a look at www.thinksuitcase.com
