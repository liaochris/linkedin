Peter Tannenwald’s Summary

Serves in an executive role to grow large scale, strategic client relationships to generate increased revenue and profitability in the Hi-Tech and Manufacturing domains. Responsible for a $37M P&L. Leads sales and delivery ops to create Engineering, IT, BPO and Infrastructure Management solutions for clients.

Holds a leadership role that focuses on large deal strategy and pursuits. Serves as an executive sponsor for deals greater than $25M.

Peter Tannenwald worked with Infosys Technologies from 2001 to 2007. Responsible for strategic account planning and business development for the High Technology and Manufacturing industry business unit. Led the national team in all aspects of business development, entry into new markets, client relationship management, and successful project delivery of Consulting, IT and BPO services. Interacted extensively with client executives and served on executive level steering committees. In 2003 Mr. Tannenwald was promoted to lead strategic, national account management and business development for the Insurance, Healthcare and Life Sciences Business Unit. Prior to these national roles, Mr. Tannenwald was responsible for strategic account management and business development for the Midwest Region.

From 1995 to 2000, Mr. Tannenwald was member of the management team that grew and managed the Information Technology consulting and operations of a mid sized IT consulting firm based in Chicago.

From 1985 to 1995 Mr. Tannenwald worked with Accenture where he managed and contributed to over 30 information technology projects for clients in the High Technology, Health Care, Financial Services, Retail, Manufacturing, Government and Oil & Gas industries.

Mr. Tannenwald has a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics and Computer Science (May 1985) from the University of Illinois, Champaign/Urbana.

Member: Chicago Council Global Affairs, University of Illinois Computer Science Executive Advisory Board
