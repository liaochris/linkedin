Organizations require professionals capable of evaluating the stochastic variables to excel in the diverse and adverse conditions of the 21st century. I possess experience in a mixture of executive management, project management, and strategic engineering roles; demonstrating leadership, flexibility, and [most importantly] ethical business practices to yield high levels of success. In the world I operate, it is easy for one to misunderstand another’s customs, professional/personal behavior; these are the situations where I thrive in applying my unbiased ability to reason and make logical decisions to influence the engaged parties [suppliers, clients, and staff]. My relevant experience, as well as my confirmed diligence and reliability in facilitating any given task allow me to contribute to the successful implementation and support of any client. I currently possess one Bachelors of Science Degree in Mechanical Engineering from Tuskegee University CEAPS. I was also a chartering member of the Association of Fundraising Professionals chapter in Southfield, Michigan while pursuing an M.B.A. at Lawrence Technological University and I am currently pursuing a Master of Liberal Arts [concentration in Finance and Control] at Harvard University. My specialties are green business strategy, emerging domestic markets, the sustainability economy. My company’s involvement with the global sustainability crisis is a result of my ability to apply my informal education, formal education, and entrepreneurial fervor to create tangible value in the world of need. I intend for my informal/formal education to be life enduring.
