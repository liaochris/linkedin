Gustavo Andres Brey’s Summary

Mr. Gustavo Andrés Brey is an IT Architect (Application Architect) with experience in the design, development and implementation of information system. He has been specialized in object-oriented application technology for Internet solutions. Furthermore, he has experience using Workflow technologies on educational and quality systems. He has worked in the software industry, for more than 8 years, as architect, software designer and programmer under several platforms and environments and also leading technical teams.
