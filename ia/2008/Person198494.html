Walton Pantland’s Summary

I have worked as a freelance journalist, writing for quality broadsheets, as well as a freelance writer, researcher and consultant to trade unions, environmental organisations, community groups, small businesses and non-government organisatons.

I have worked on a contract basis for a number of trade unions and workers' education and media organisations.

I have a good understanding of the political and strategic issues facing the labour movement, and have developed and delivered educational material for trade unions.

I am skilled in most aspects of new and traditional media production, and can produce professional newsletters, blogs and more.

Walton Pantland’s Specialties:

Materials development, union negotiation, leading courses, organising events, all aspects of new and traditional media production, strategic and political analysis and intervention.
