Terri Nakamura’s Summary

"MY ANDY WARHOL '15 minutes of fame' WAS WHEN MY HUSBAND AND I WERE FEATURED ON THE COVER OF 'Money Magazine.' I WAS 29."

AND EVER SINCE, TERRI'S DESIGN REPUTATION HAS STEADILY GROWN, with prestigious projects such as the graphics redesign of the Seattle Center Coliseum.

DURING THE 1980s, she and three colleagues, Sue Brush, M.R. Bruns and Lynne Berry, were chosen over an impressive pool of advertising agencies and public relations firms by the Washington State Centennial Commission to plan and execute the state's celebration of an historic year.

TERRI'S FOOD EXPERIENCE ISN'T LIMITED TO EATING. From 1980 to 2000 Terri produced dozens of labels for Pacific Pearl Seafoods, Jonah Seafoods and Chicken of the Sea.

IN THE 1990s SHE FOCUSED ON PUBLICATIONS including annual reports and magazines for the Seattle Water Department, Harborview Medical Center, Boeing Commercial Airplane Group, Virginia Mason Medical Center, Safeco, Children's Hospital and Lakeside School. She's art-directed more than 200 photo shoots of food, editorial and commercial products and services.

CORPORATE IDENTITY CRISIS? Terri has handled rebranding efforts for large institutions such as Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, as well as many smaller companies. She has expertise in logo development, graphic standards and extension of the corporate mark to myriad applications.

NEW TECHNOLOGY IS A PASSION. Terri stays abreast of new software applications, color trends, and changes in technology. She has designed web sites for companies including The Good Mood Diet, the Washington Wine Commission, Look-Both-Ways, Iyabo Wellness and Nanostring Technologies.

HER WORK HAS RECEIVED MORE THAN 30 PROFESSIONAL AWARDS, and she's managed budgets from hundreds to hundreds of thousands of dollars. Terri can take a project from concept to design to production and manage all of the details. And she does it well.

Terri Nakamura’s Experience

  • Owner

    Nakamura Graphic Design (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Graphic Design industry)

    1976 — Present (32 years)

    Nakamura Graphic Design is a sole-proprietorship and has provided award-winning design and production services to public and private companies and institutions since 1976. (Former WMBE).

    My work includes creation of corporate identities, advertising campaigns, magazines, newsletters, annual reports, product packaging/labels, signage and building graphics, books and special communications collateral.

    When clients hire Nakamura Graphic Design, they work with the owner of the company. Their projects receive my personal attention as opposed to that of a junior or lower-level designer with less expertise in a larger firm. My business has the ability to expand or contract according to workflow so clients do not pay for expensive overhead. I can call on a stable of some of the finest talent available in both design and production when auxiliary assistance is required. In all instances I oversee projects personally.
