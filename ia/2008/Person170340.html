Salvatore Branca’s Summary

I've seven years experience as software engineer working at the IBM Software Group Development Lab, I've covered different leadership position in both software development and test teams.
I have a deep and broad knowledge of software development processes, best practices and techniques as well as of the technology used in today commercial applications.
As team and technical leader my goal has always been to drive effective changes in product design and development in order to improve product quality and to provide tangible benefits to the customer.
I've a good experience in working with international geographically distributed teams.

Recently my main focus has been on SOA and BPM (I've attained a SOA certification and a Business Process Modeling certification), particularly as a speaker at different SOA customer events.
I've also served as consultant for business solutions of the Government industry at the Rome SWG Briefing Center.

Before joining IBM I've acquired a deep research background working at the Quantum Optics Lab in the Rome "La Sapienza" University.

I'm a versatilist with both technical and business expertise, a fast learner with a continuing passion for technology and business.
I aim to pursue a consultant/managerial career in dynamic and international environments.
