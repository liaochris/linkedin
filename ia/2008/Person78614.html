Background
Iain has worked for a number of large organisations and start-up companies in a variety of commercial and development roles. Most recently Iain worked at Academee who design and deliver learning and organisational change initiatives. He was responsible for the engagement, delivery teams and relationship management for all these large-scale, global projects in partnership with key clients, stakeholders and client project teams. He also headed up the Academee team in the Asia Pacific region based out of Singapore working in Hong Kong, Beijing, Kuala Lumpur and Bangkok.
Throughout his career he has worked in many business sectors including: financial services, technology, outdoor education, oil and gas and the public sector. This has been as both an employee and as a particularly pragmatic consultant.
One of his life-long outcomes is to facilitate and run workshops in each one of the 7 new wonders of the world!
