Hilal Chouman’s Summary

I have graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics & Communications from Beirut Arab University in June 2005. Having the ambition to widen my knowledge in the field of Digital Communications and see how it applies within satellite Communications, I pursued my Masters studies in aerospace communication systems at Telecom Paris (www.enst.fr) in Toulouse, France. Through my studies, I had the chance to attend courses that were taught by people from the outstanding satellite manufacturing companies such as Intelsat, Eutelsat, SES Astra, and Astrium. I also had the chance to have two of my Masters projects supervised by Alcatel Alenia Space.

My extra-curricular activities have helped me to fine-tune my communication skills, time management habits, and team spirit. I had participated in organizing some cultural and cinematic shows at BAU during my undergraduate study. Furthermore, I was the monitor of my fellow international colleagues in the Masters program. This helped me later to adapt to the multinational environment of the MSCRC lab (www.mscrc.net), where I did my masters thesis and continued to work as a lab researcher after obtaining my degree.

I have practically applied my network knowledge during my training period at MSCRC. I can say now that I have some experience in the Linux platform. I succeeded in setting a multicast testbed that was observed as an essential part in the lab’s achievements as a part of the SatNEx European project (www.satnex.org).

I want to push my career further and work in the industry. I believe that my experience in research work will help me in that since I became more familiar with reading theoretical papers which in turn will support any related practical work. I am seeking a placement with a professional team in a reputable company, where I will be able to put my engineering background and inter-personal skills to proper use, and provide me with a good career start.
