Sanjeet Gujral’s Summary

I started my career as a PeopleSoft HRMS functional consultant with Satyam Computer Services India Ltd., working on projects ranging from life cycle implementation, customization to production support. After working with Satyam for close to 3 years, I moved to Hewitt.There I was required to setup and manage a team to handle the HR Operations for one of the biggest names on Wall Street. This profile gave me the exposure to process transition, client management, delivery and above all team management. This was also the first major client operation being done out of India under the Hewitt MPHRO flagship and its success laid the foundation for other similar processes being transitioned and worked out of Hewitt India. After successfully setting up and streamlining the HR operations for the client, I moved on to take another role within Hewitt in their Talent Programs Team. The role here was more strategic as it related to working across various HR domains on projects ranging from application implementation to the inception of new policies and procedures

In my current role with Lehman India, I am a part of the Corporate HR team and am primarily responsible for managing HR Info systems (HRIS) like PeopleSoft and other third party HR applications. Here I act as a Functional Consultant working closely with the business teams to understand systems requirements and overlook the development and implementation of these systems. As part of the Strategic HR role, I am actively involved in setting up and managing the HR Shared Service team besides working as a consultant on re-engineering some of the HR Processes for Lehman Japan and Non-Japan Asia regions (including India).

My goal is to continue to build expertise in the HR Domain where I can use my HRIS experience to support/upgrade/implement HR applications and at the same time continue to be actively involved in Strategic HR setup of the organization.
