Brian Broda’s Summary

Mr. Broda is a seasoned enterprise architect and program/project manager with over 19 years of business and consulting experience. Over this time Brian has contributed to value to his clients by providing consulting and advisory services ranging from enterprise architecture, ERP implementation project management, technology strategy and planning, application testing, and business process outsourcing transition activities. Some highlights of Mr. Broda’s competencies include:
- Proven Project management expertise – strategy, planning, budgeting, scope, delivery, and support
- Practical client relationship management and team leadership skills
- Extensive enterprise architecture and technology strategy experience
- Extensive application testing and integration testing experience
- Extensive business process redesign and operational process redesign experience
- Broad experience with business process outsourcing and transition execution
