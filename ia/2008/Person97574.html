Joseph D'Anna’s Summary

Joe D'Anna has 10 years experience as a financial derivative solutions expert in the global banking, insur-ance and asset management industries. A commercially focused quant with unassuming approach, he has a proven track record of technical leadership, problem solving insight, and quantitative salesmanship.

He has performed in senior, front-office roles at investment banks and a successful start-up, working best in teams focused on building businesses, not making “quick kills.”

Joseph D'Anna’s Specialties:

New Retail Product Development, Structured Products Design, Valuation & Hedging, Corporate Finance Solutions for Financial Institutions, Mutual Fund Derivatives, Exotic Asset Backed Securitization, Static Hedging Strategies, Hybrid Derivatives, Mortality and Insurance Risk Managment.
