Wojciech Zielinski’s Summary

Since 01.04.2007 I work for SAP Center of Expertise Central Europe on the position of Project Manager. During that time I was a part (as an consultant and project manager) of several internal projects CRM system customization and new services implementation (new release data migration, new services design, web applications).

Before SAP I worked as a Chairman and CEO of COBA Solutions Inc. Company deals with IT systems design and development using the PHP/PostgreSQL technologies. It’s flagship product – COBA system is a complete CRM/miniERP solution for small and medium sized companies. The newest product, yet not released on the market is PHP framework/environment for business applications design and development, based on Zend Framework. The complete architecture and high level planning for this framework has been designed by me, and currently developed by COBA Solutions.

While building and managing the company I used the most valuable experience and knowledge gained while working in international corporation Unisys (as an IT consultant working for the key company customers from the public and telecommunications sector) as well as while working in the Polish largest service company Computer Service Support S.A. (as a main network administrator in HQ).
In the past I was also engaged in OpenSource development, which result was the OpenSource released PHP class PEAR::Translation. Despite knowledge of several programming languages like Pascal, C++, Basic and Java currently I find PHP and DHTML as my first programming languages I know. Also knowledge of multiple system environments (Novell, Microsoft, *NIX) allows me to create and consult system architecture and integration designs, as well as previous technical area experience gained on different specialist positions (including systems and networks administration, consultancy and programming) helps me in creating strong professional respect among the technical team I manage.
