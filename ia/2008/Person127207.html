Michael d'Estries’s Summary

I've been involved with the Internet since the very beginning, but only started seriously blogging (along with most of my other colleagues) in early 2005.

From the beginning, I set out to make a difference in the one topic I was most passionate about: the environment. Having moved to Ithaca, NY fresh from a few years abroad in New Zealand, I was inspired by the amount of sustainable events and initiatives taking place in town; but frustrated by the lack of information available online. This led me to form the website, GroovyGreen (http://www.groovygreen.com) to keep track of all these cool, green events and tell the world. After only a few months, I broadened by scope to include world events.

From Groovy spawned Ecorazzi. (http://www.ecorazzi.com) I decided to enter the celebrity gossip scene -- but with my own unique spin: I would write about people in the spotlight doing good for the world -- whether through humanitarian efforts, charity, animal rights, or the environment. The spotlight, surprisingly ended up turning on us, and the site was quickly featured on everything from television to magazines. From this came meetings with Al Gore, opportunities to speak at conferences, and networking jams with some of the most influential people in the environmental scene.

Today, I write and manage for the above sites, as well as playing the role of Senior Editor for the site Ecotality LIFE (http://www.ecotality.com/life). I also perform speaking engagements for companies like General Electric and American Express to teach people involved in communications and public relations how to build a presence online, work with bloggers, and use social media to their advantage.
