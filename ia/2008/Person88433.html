Jennifer Dunleavy’s Summary

The Accuro Group is a professional services organization dedicated to excellence in strategic staffing and talent placement. Our firm proudly assists National and Global Industry Leaders in attracting and retaining highly-talented business administration, financial and technical professionals. Our award-winning services are delivered through a variety of custom-designed temporary, contract, executive consulting and direct-hire search services.

Founded in 2003 in the Raleigh-Durham area of North Carolina, The Accuro Group was voted "Favorite Staffing Company" by the readers of Business Leader Magazine. The company is perennially named as a Top Employment Services Company and Executive Recruiting Firm by the Triangle Business Journal. The Accuro Group has received numerous awards for its service and was recently named as one of the largest Women-Owned Businesses in North Carolina by Diversity Business. For more information, please visit www.theaccurogroup.com or call 919.851.9880.
