Mary B Relotto’s Experience

  • Marketing Director

    Mental Health America of Franklin County

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Mental Health Care industry)

    May 2008 — Present (9 months)

    Mental Health America of Franklin County (MHAFC) is here to help guide you on your road to better mental health. Finding your way through the mental health system can be difficult. We provide a personal, knowledgeable approach to referring those in need to the appropriate services. Mental health issues affect one in five adults and children each year. Unfortunately, only a mere 16% of those individuals will seek treatment. We are here to help you!

    MHAFC is dedicated to promoting mental health, preventing mental disorders and raising awareness and understanding of mental health issues through advocacy, education and support services in our community. We are a private, not-for-profit organization established in 1956.

    MHAFC envisions a just, humane and healthy society in which all adults and children receive the help they need, are well informed and are accorded respect, dignity and the opportunity to achieve their full potential, free from stigma and prejudice.

    January 2006 — Present (3 years 1 month)

    Dames Bond is a social based networking community in Central Ohio, whose goal is to foster relationships between all women by providing common ground where women consumers can connect and share with women in business.     All Bond Events are different. Bond Events are FREE and expose you to women doing business in central Ohio! You never know who you are going to learn about and who you will meet!

    Recent speakers include Secretary of State Jennifer Brunner, Councilmember Maryellen O'Shaughnessy, Dr. Leslie McDougal, Dr. Kim Kalfas, Bea Decourtivron, Karla Rothan, and so many more!
