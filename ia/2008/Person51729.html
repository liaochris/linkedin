Dinah McLean’s Summary

Dinah McLean partnered with her husband Mac with the goal of expanding beyond his publicity expertise and bringing the marriage of publicity and marketing to Click’s vast array of online services. In addition to providing marketing expertise to clients and staff, Dinah oversees all client reporting needs as well as the day-to-day operations of Click.

Dinah honed her marketing skills and online expertise in the live entertainment arena, most recently at Ticketmaster. During her seven years with the company, she rose through the ranks from Seattle Marketing Manager to West Region Sales and Marketing Director, leading a team of sales and marketing professionals in eleven cities to secure new clients, grow their business and drive ticket sales to meet—and often exceed—internal sales goals. She also held the role of National Account Manager and worked with national promoters such as AEG Live and VEE Corp to execute marketing strategies and drive ticket sales for national tours including High School Musical: The Concert and Sesame Street Live.

Prior to Ticketmaster, Dinah handled publicity for websites and high-tech clients in Seattle, as well as field publicity and promotions in Denver for Disney, New Line, Focus Features, and 20th Century Fox. Her well-rounded marketing career began with two-year stint in radio where she did everything from promotions to sales to hosting her own show. Dinah holds a BA in Journalism from the University of Northern Colorado.
