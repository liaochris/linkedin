Kylie Short’s Summary

I started my business with no clear vision in mind and no idea of what I was doing. It was a job and nothing more. I then started thinking about other business owners working from their home office and what they did in their business every day. How did they find new clients? Where did they find the information they needed to make informed decisions?

I started my research, stumbled onto the Virtual Assistance industry and my mind exploded. Here was an entire industry doing what I wanted to do. So I sat down with my coffee, a notebook and started making notes. I wrote my business plan, attended webinars and learnt as much as I could about becoming a professional Virtual Assistant.

I love technology and I love what I do. Each of my clients are different to work with and we work on different projects. Needless to say that after 18 months I made the decision to take on Associate VAs, each with their own speciality and I've not looked back. Sure I've reached my 5 year goals in 18 months but that's what planning is for. To be a guide and to be changed when needed.

My goal now is to learn how to become a better project manager and to manage my virtual team so each person has the opportunity to shine.

Hopefully my brain won't explode this time....

Kylie Short’s Specialties:

Tilda Virtual Services specialises in providing administrative, online marketing, e-newsletter design, Web site design and technical support to small business owners working from home. We know what it takes to balance home and family while building a successful and profitable business. Now you can concentrate on what you love – not what you have to do! It’s all about time management and what better way to manage your time than to have Tilda Virtual Services do some of your work!
