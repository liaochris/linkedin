Alok Vasudeva’s Summary

Media trained marketing professional with 10+ years focused on infrastructure technologies. Career has encompassed product management, product marketing, and marketing communications targeting small-to-medium businesses and large enterprises.

• Testing team lead who created best practices that led to a v1.0 product receiving 1st place in InfoWorld’s “SIP IP-PBX” review and 2006 “Technology of the Year ‘Best IP-PBX’.”

• Developed Marconi's first specialized channel partner program, the Marconi Telephony Partner certification program. Promotions included a “Discover Your Voice!” starter package and a “Build Your Voice!” rebate offer. Trained 15 resellers, resulting in 15,000 VoIP users.

• Created the company's first storage product sales guide. Promotional efforts led to becoming Hewlett-Packard’s largest storage reseller and attaining HP “Best-in-Class, Master Level Reseller” status. The program accounted for sales of $100M and 24% of HP’s high-end storage sales.

• Speaker on “SIP’s Changing Role in Enterprise Communications” panel, VoiceCon Tour 2006.
