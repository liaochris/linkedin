Entrepreneur/technologist spanning award winning Accounting systems, Mobile Commerce, Mobile Entertainment, Internet Micro Billing and now Web 2.0.

Recipient of several awards for the "Best Accounting solutions in their class".

I have a very keen insight into the application of new technologies, refining existing solutions and providing a "better wheel".

  • CEO

    SurEcom SA (Pty) Ltd.

    (Public Company; 1-10 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    June 1999 — September 2003 (4 years 4 months)

    SurEcom was formed as an ASP to mobile operators providing mobile entertainment services and mobile commerce services.

    I designed, prototyped and patented a mobile entertainment process where the mobile device was used as a user interaction device for data input and response for interactive games and shows delivered to media channels (via tv, satelite, radio, web) using IVR, SMS and USSD protocols.

    I also used the same protocols & processes to design, prototype and patent a mobile commerce platform for delivering secure OTA credit, debit card and banking transactions using vanilla mobile phones.

    I succeeded in raising generous seed funding to develop commercial platforms of these technologies and take them to market.
