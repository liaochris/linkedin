Mariana Florea - Ionita’s Summary

I believe that no matter where you are in life, if you want to do more, to become more, then yes YOU can! No one has to be limited by their past, every day brings a new beginning, new opportunities. Life Coaching is about achieving success in your life based on your own needs and desires, and your own definition of success. It is about setting and achieving goals in a way that makes you feel fulfilled. It is about making the right decisions. It is also about the whole of your life, about work-life balance. As a Professional Coach, I empower clients to discover their own solutions and strategies and help them rediscover their own unique talents and skills that will help them live the life they want.
