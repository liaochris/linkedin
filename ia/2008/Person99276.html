Jason Stengren’s Summary

I graduated with a BS in Information Technology from Humboldt State University, CA in 2001 and immediately began working as a desktop/network support technician with a local bank. In 2002, I moved to Portland, OR in search of brighter horizons and joined Northwest Software under the same basic job description. During the course of my employment with NSI, I gradually became more involved with development-type responsibilities such as redesigning the UI for their applicant-tracking website, writing requirement-definitions for new features, creating user guides and training materials, and also training new customers. In 2005, I was hired by ADP Dealer Services as a full-time training developer creating and updating training materials (print and online) for their software products. During this time, I have also taken several project management classes from Portland State University, and now persue more lead-type development roles which better utilize my technical, creative, and management skills. I am always looking for side-work, and have taken small jobs doing freelance web design, home PC troubleshooting and networking, and general consulting.
