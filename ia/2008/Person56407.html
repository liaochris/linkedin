Edie Abigail Dover’s Summary

Edie is responsible for account management at now-in.org GmbH and expanding its international customer base.

www.now-in.org is the leading Web 2.0 event portal in Germany, Austria and Switzerland and intends to duplicate this success in the United States. At present approximately 45,000 current events from all sectors are online. This event search engine allows users to register their own events.
Reporting directly to the owner, she is tasked with developing relationships with existing clients and new clients with a cultural or recreational orientation. Now-in.org currently contains more than 50,000 events and has more than 110,000 unique visitors monthly.

Edie has over 4 years experience in the Internet industry. She has a diverse background over a range of industries covering both business-to-business and business-to-consumer fields.

In her spare time, Edie keeps informed on international politics and contributes to a blog. She is a Communication Studies (BA) graduate from the University of Windsor and holds a Postgraduate Diploma (PgDip) in European Policy, Law & Management from the Robert Gordon University.
