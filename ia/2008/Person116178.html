Steven Lubetkin’s Summary

Leading producer of business podcasts in New Jersey and eastern Pennsylvania. Clients include NJ Bankers Association, Sun National Bank, Public Relations Society of America, Rutgers University (Camden).

Corporate photojournalist, skilled digital photographer and videographer with national photo credits. Public photo portfolio is at http://www.flickr.com/photos/lubetkin/.

Highly articulate spokesman with strong track record of persuading skeptical opinion leaders and public policy influentials. Experienced technologist, counselor to senior management regarding the business and commercial implications of leading edge technologies. MBA thesis, “Bond Ratings in Cyberspace,” studies attitudes of financial industry professionals toward computer-mediated meeting technologies. Fluent in Spanish.

I am the host of three blogs and podcasts, and I am invited to speak on these technologies at many industry conferences:

"Lubetkin's Other Blog" - Where I comment on public relations and journalism issues, and the podcast interviews people making news in the PR and journalism worlds. RSS feed is at http://feeds.feedburner.com/lubetkinsotherblog

"CompuSchmooze" - blog and podcast supplementing my monthly newspaper column about technology and Judaism in the Jewish Community voice of Southern New Jersey. RSS: http://feeds.feedburner.com/compuschmooze

"Middle Chamber Books Podcast" - which supports our Middle Chamber Book Store (www.middlechamberbooks.com) with podcasts in which we interview authors and other creative artists.

Steven Lubetkin’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in corporate public relations, financial communications, media relations, seminars, speechwriting, issues management, and employee communications, and community relations for Fortune 100 companies and their senior executives.

Nationally published writer of business articles on financial services, technology, genealogy, and other topics, interested in additional writing assignments for clients and major publications.
