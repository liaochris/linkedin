Michael Morell’s Summary

Mike has over 14 years of experience in executive search and technology sales. Prior to joining Riviera Partners, he founded and managed a technical search firm, Koa Networks, focused on VP Engineering, Director Engineering and Sr Software Engineer assignments for early stage venture backed technology companies in the Silicon Valley. Mike specializes in retained search work for executive talent including but not limited to CEO, VP Engineering.

Mike began his business career in sales representing Altera, Fujitsu, LSI Logic and Lucent.

Michael Morell’s Specialties:

Riviera Partners combines the best assets of large, boutique and solo search providers -- scalability, process, reach, partner-level service, personal relationships and guaranteed results – with unique IP and a sharp focus on venture-funded technology markets.
