Colin Lewellyn’s Summary

An experienced senior manager with 12 years leading creative teams in industries ranging from online journalism to e-commerce and mass market entertainment.

As a member of Blue Man Production's Senior Management team, Colin has helped expand BMP into an internationally recognized creative powerhouse with shows running in multiple countries around the world. Was specifically tasked with leading the casting, training and artistic direction departments to support fast, exponential growth while still maintaining high artistic standards. Also developed systems and processes which increased departmental efficiency and productivity while still maintaining a vibrant and creative environment.

Colin has long been interested in the challenges surrounding leading creative individuals in a business setting and looking for ways to achieve work that is both creatively satisfying and financially successful.

Colin Lewellyn’s Specialties:

Team leadership, growth planning, systems and process, restructuring, resource planning, creative problem solving.
