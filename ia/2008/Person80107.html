Inge Fernau’s Summary

After almost 6 years, Inge Fernau recently left Nektar Therapeutics. Nektar acquired Aerogen in October 2005. She performed many roles at Nektar/Aerogen including process engineering, management of the aperture plate process and device assembly, technical service for commercial spray drying and managing the maintenance systems. Inge is extremely motivated to embrace new technologies

Inge is an experienced professional with successful engineering and process management track record in a start-up environment as well as high volume manufacturing. History of improvement in high technology manufacturing. Highly skilled in working with multiple disciplines and diversities. Created an innovative product and improved the operational environment.

In the 20+ years of her professional career Inge has been exposed to start-up environments as well as manufacturing and therefore obtained extensive background in engineering and process management. She played a key role in process optimization and yield improvements.

Previous positions gave Inge an in-depth understanding of pharmaceutical manufacturing, device assembly and semiconductor manufacturing. Inge is experienced in a clean room environment, in both start-up and high volume manufacturing environments. Having demonstrated strong management, supervision, communications and goal alignment methodology skills made her a leader who believes everyone makes a difference. A positive can-do, "get the job done" attitude is her requirement!
