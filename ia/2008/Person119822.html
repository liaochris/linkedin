Marc Köhlbrugge’s Summary

Currently I'm a student at Communication & Multimedia Design (cmd-breda.nl), Avans Hogeschool. This study is best described as a mixture of graphic design, interaction design, concept development and technology. This year I'm focusing on Interaction Strategy.

Along my study I educate myself about subjects including psychology, marketing, philosophy and film.

When I'm getting tired of learning I put the theory into practice in my own personal projects and some client work now and then.

I'm currently working with crowdSPRING (www.crowdspring.com) implementing my winning design entry for their homepage contest.
