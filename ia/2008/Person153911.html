Erik Baker’s Summary

Erik has a long background in the entertainment industry as a Promoter, Manager and Project Developer. His Entetaiment career began with Nightlife Entertianment in Tampa,FL moving on to NEAR Entertainment Phoenix, AZ and eventually to EBM Radio and Media Promotions Nashville, TN as well as his latest ventue UNITE Productions which produces both Concerts and Multi-Day Music Festivals.

After leaving a career in Concert Promotion, Erik worked with The Wizard Connection developing major "themed" events for major corporations such as Crest and MOEN. While he also served as a MR/PR for several Entertainment Venues in Phoenix, AZ.

Erik is actively involved in his community. He has been a volunteer for the Edison Ford Winter Estates in Ft.Myers, Fl since 2006.

With these entertainment industry related experiences, Artist Management and Client Relations, Staff Management, Event Organization (Developing, Planning and Managing “On and Off Site”), Event Marketing, Media Campaigns, Strategic Planning, Talent Acquisition / Settlement and Pressured Problem Solving, Erik has been V.P. of Operations of EBM Radio and Media Promotions since Winter 2005 as well as working with Veteran Promoter Hal Royce at concert-promotions.com.

Erik's talented development skills, keen promotional capabilities, connections in the entertainment industry and strong work ethic can bring an artist, project or event to the next level through organizational planning.
