Andy Sims’s Summary

Andy Sims is currently CEO and President of Brothers By Choice, LLC (BBC), a full service new-media advertising agency he co-founded in 2006. At BBC help build the foundation for the agency, including all client, creative, financial management systems and practices. He also developed the infrastructure for offline and online media planning and buying capability, and implemented an international business model with web development, video production and technology partners in Sao Paulo, Brazil and in the US. Sims and his partners created notable campaigns for Heineken, ESPN, Seagate, Maxtor, 2KSports, Autodesk and Sagatiba USA.

Prior to that, Sims was SVP/Director of Interactive Services for ZenithOptimedia in San Francisco, where he drove client strategy and integration of digital capabilities across all of Zenith Media and Optimedia offices in the U.S. While at ZenithOptimedia, he managed the SF office and worked on clients including T-Mobile, L'Oreal, Sanofi-Aventis, Alcatel-Lucent, Zurich Financial, Toyota Scion, Puma and Denny's.

Before to joining ZenithOptimedia, Andy ran interactive marketing services at SF Interactive and was a partner at its parent agency, Butler, Shine Stern & Partners, in Sausalito, Calif. While there, he managed a 40-person department, overseeing website and online creative development and production; interactive media planning and buying; analytics and engineering. He worked with clients such as Coca-Cola's Sprite, Converse, HealthNet, MINI, Sun Microsystems and VeriSign.

Before moving to SFI, Andy worked at GMO/Hill Holliday in San Francisco where he developed consumer and B-to-B programs for Cisco Systems. Prior to that, Sims was at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners in San Francisco supporting the Hewlett-Packard and Sutter Home wine accounts. Sims started his career in advertising as an advertising assistant for the Charlotte Observer in his hometown of Charlotte, North Carolina.

Andy Sims’s Specialties:

Executive advertising agency management, interactive media strategy and buying, interactive and traditional advertising strategy development, global outsourcing and partnerships.
