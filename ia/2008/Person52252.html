DJ Rossstar’s Summary

My name is DJ Rossstar and for the past six years I have devoted my life to music and bringing music lovers as close as humanly possible to their favorite artists through various interactive means.

DJ Rossstar’s Specialties:

I am a very approachable and hands-on person who loves to get involved with as many things as possible that interest me. It's because of this that I have built my reputation as both a taste maker and honest individual in a sometimes untrustworthy industry.

DJ Rossstar’s Experience

  • Columnist

    Substream Music Press

    (Music industry)

    September 2008 — Present (2 months)

    Beginning in October, my own personal column will appear in Substream Music Press, a bi-monthy music magazine that is available at all 700 Hot Topic locations (and some F.Y.E. stores). I will be using this column to give advice to aspiring hosts / journalists and share stories from my experience in the music business thus far.

  • Interactive Moderator

    Havoc TV

    (Music industry)

    March 2007 — Present (1 year 8 months)

    Every Monday night (11 p.m. - 3 a.m. PT) I host Havoc on the 101 on DirecTV Channel 101. It's a live and interactive music video show and my job is to interactive with the viewers via text messages that appear on the left side of the television screen, while also making sure the messages received abide by the DirecTV guidelines (no swears, drug references, and so on).

  • Music Blogger, Interviewer

    Buzznet

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    My daily tasks include researching and organizing music news stories that appeal to the Buzznet audience. I am also an in-house host, having interviewed both musicians and celebrities on red carpets and elsewhere. I have built close ties with many artists, publicists, managers and agents through my long running online show. As a result of these relationships I have been able to obtain exclusive content for Buzznet, including track listings, tour plans and official announcements ... thus garnering a hefty amount of traffic to the site.

  • Host

    DJ Rossstar's Punk Rock Show

    (Online Media industry)

    September 2002 — Present (6 years 2 months)

    Since 2002 I have successfully run my own live online show. Currently broadcasting from my bedroom in Los Angeles, California, to an audience of over 40,000, I have interviewed countless artists including Fall Out Boy, Katy Perry, Shwayze, AFI, Jack's Mannequin, The Veronicas and Plain White T's. The show airs live on both Idobiradio.com and Stickam.com every Wednesday and Thursday night from 7-8 p.m. PT (10-11 p.m. ET).

  • New Media Assistant

    Thrive Records

    (Music industry)

    October 2006 — January 2007 (4 months)

    My daily tasks included updating MySpace pages for both Thrive Records artists and for Thrive Records itself, promoting contests online for our latest releases and mailing out packages.

  • New Media Assistant

    Nitrus Records

    (Music industry)

    May 2006 — September 2006 (5 months)

    My daily tasks included updating MySpace pages for the band Wired All Wrong, creating/promoting contests online and mailing out packages.

  • Host

    Takeover TV

    (Music industry)

    August 2005 — December 2005 (5 months)

    During its short lifespan, I hosted six episodes of Takeover TV (online-only) in which I attended concerts, went mini-golfing, road a ferris wheel, and engaged in countless other activities while interviewing Takeover Records artists (Hey Mike!, Strikefirefall) and other musicians/celebrities (The Matches, Kal Penn).

  • New Media Coordinator

    Warner Brothers Records

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Music industry)

    February 2005 — August 2005 (7 months)

    As an employee in the new media department, my job was centered around creating new and effective ways to promote Warner's smaller artists through the Internet, while working on various other clerical tasks from the department head.
