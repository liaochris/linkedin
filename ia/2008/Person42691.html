Damir Sudarevic’s Summary

Statistical data analysis and reporting, SQL databases, control systems design, programming. More than 20 years of experience in automotive and defence industries. Hands-on experience in all stages from design to final deployment.

Spent 8 years in an electronic lab building microprocessor and analog based controllers, writing assembler code.
