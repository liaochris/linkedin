Steve Wasser’s Summary

I have a wide ranging technical career spanning industries from accounting to wholesale petroleum. My core competency is hands-on technical management, and I enjoy troubleshooting and tracking down network bottlenecks, as much as I do managing projects and deliverables.

I would not be content sitting behind a desk, checking off milestones and timelines in Project 2000. I derive the greatest satisfaction working alongside my team to bring projects and deployments into production.

Steve Wasser’s Specialties:

Ability to communicate succinct business needs for technology driven projects. The most critical role a technical manager can fulfill is mastering the communication skills to convey the importance and budget for technical projects, understandable by non-technical executives.

Of equal importance is delivering that project on time, and within budget.
