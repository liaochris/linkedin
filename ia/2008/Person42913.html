Dan Brown’s Summary

I am a copywriter and editor with a meticulous eye for detail and the ability to identify the flaws in any copy or layout. I am an extremely professional and dedicated individual with excellent literacy and numeracy skills. Working on my own initiative or with other creatives, I develop and write vibrant content for a variety of advertising, websites and print media, showing the right understanding of both the client and their intended market’s needs. I have a good knowledge of PC and Mac applications (including InDesign, QuarkXpress, Photoshop, and Illustrator), HTML, and the content management system, Documentum Web Publisher. I have a flexible approach to problems, work well to budgets and deadlines and am highly organised. I take pride in being thorough and persistent in any job I am given.

While the majority of my experience has been in print media I am acquiring experience and skills in online media, specifically in editing and creating content for websites, virals and advertising.

Dan Brown’s Specialties:

I have experience in writing short and medium weight copy in SEO, personal finance, travel, crafts & hobbies, health and beauty, and online gambling for articles, reviews, virals, ads, web banners, and call to actions. I also have significant knowledge in sport, travel, lifestyle, science (including environmental issues) and TV & film. My skills allow me to research practically any subject though in order to create and develop accurate and exciting content.
