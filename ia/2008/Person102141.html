Career Acceleration Network - Karen P. Katz’s Summary

The Career Acceleration Network (CAN) is the channel through which I become an advocate for executives and professionals in career transition. Clients benefit from my collaborative approach and teaching style; they become successful navigators of innovative and effective career strategies. Engage CAN when you are ready to:

*accept and embrace CHANGE;
*explore career and entrepreneurial options;
*collaboratively write and revise CV's, biographies, profiles, and resumes;
*craft job search correspondence:
*expand and nurture your business network;
*conduct effective an job search campaigns, and;
*practice strategies to achieve long-term work/life balance and career health.

What sets me apart is my sincere commitment to the success of my clients, as I choose those I wish to work with. I share my penchant for research, writing, and networking, and most importantly, I help to manage the process so clients can work while engaged in their search.

Clients benefit from my understanding of the dynamics of the business world, based on my experience in Human Resources with two Fortune 200 companies; entrepreneurial experience as a Sales Manager/Educational Consultant, and experience as a teacher and corporate outplacement consultant.

Together We CAN is more than a tagline; it describes CAN's goal to promote engagement in career health and well-being as a life-long process.
