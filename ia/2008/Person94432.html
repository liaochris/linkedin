John Franklin’s Summary

Franklin Consulting, LLC provides political consultation and management to Republican candidates across America. Specializing in candidates for the U.S. House of Representatives, our firm offers access to complete campaign services including management, general consulting, fund-raising and web development.

With acccess to the industry's top players, principal John Franklin provides senior leadership and expertise to members of Congress and candidates for federal, state and municipal offices. Our wide base of associates and contacts can make your campaign a success.
