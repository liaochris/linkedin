Mike Diehn’s Summary

I troubleshooter and fixer with strong planning and organization skills.

n the tactical frame, I've exercised these skills doing clean-up operations after large virus outbreaks, quickly gutting and rebuilding collapsed networks cabling plants, shoring-up failing network infrastructure services, etc.

My strategic work has included designing networks that don't suffer catastrophic failures, designing and implementing monitoring systems that watch less-well designed networks for early warnings of trouble and helping administrators of larger systems prevent down-time. I use my skills to plan upgrade, move or conversion projects to avoid down-time.

Mike Diehn’s Specialties:

Rapid comprehension of complex situations; Crisis management; Risk analysis; Client management; Systems analysis; Structured change management; Near zero down-time system design; Network services protocols and the systems that support their use by higher level systems.
