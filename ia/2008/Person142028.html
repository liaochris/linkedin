Nish Amarnath’s Summary

Having begun my career at the age of 13, I have now been with the media for nearly 9 years.

Freelanced for publications like The Hindu, India Today, The Week, The New Indian Express and Economic Times, where I handled personality profiles, special commercial features and lead human interest stories . Also did soft stories for television and worked in celebrity branding and image management.

In 2004-05, I authored my first book The Voyage to Excellence, featuring inspirational success stories of India's top 21 global women business tycoons.
Won the standing of one of the youngest non-fiction authors and media professionals of my times at the age of 18. Since then, I have been a TV and public (off-screen) spokesperson for socio-educational causes.

At 20, I was the youngest Masters student in my chosen field at LSE, during which I got a merit award.

In London, I have been a freelance research analyst and marketing associate, working for prestigious organisations like LSE, World Energy Council and The People Group (Shaadi.com).

I am now 21 and presently based in London. I work for Commonwealth Finance Solutions / OCTRAD where I control the PR/ communications function for a start-up financial services company. I am also working on an independent media-literary project.

Looking at pursuing an MBA in Business Strategy / Marketing in a couple of years.

I see myself playing an integral role in developing sustainable business capabilities for a top-notch organisation whose potential I believe is worth investing in.

Obviously, my lifeblood revolves around ---- guess what ----- the media and public relations !!!!
