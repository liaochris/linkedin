Kandace Hudspeth’s Summary

Ms. Hudspeth develops interactive marketing strategies that combine the attitudes and interests of consumers, clients and creative’s to help produce effective advertising. Acting as the voice of the consumer within the agency, Ms. Hudspeth synthesizes research data to identify ideal audiences and optimum methods of communication.
