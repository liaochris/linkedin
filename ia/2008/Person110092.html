Lacy Kontur’s Summary

I am a Claims Analyst and Team Lead in the Managed Vision Care department of EyeMed Vision Care. I have worked in the Managed Care claims arena for six years. In the past few years I have been responsible for creating training manuals, conducting training, performing QA and testing responsibilities, leading teams and projects, and pursing a path towards management.

I have been extremely lucky to have had the opportunity to be involved in the One Sight Foundation. This is the Luxottica Foundation that gives the gift of vision screenings and glasses to those who cannot afford it both in the US and Internationally. For the past two years I have been a captain for my department during our Recycle Challenge and have worked to do vision screenings for children in Cincinnati Public Schools. If you would like more information about this wonderful organization and how you can also help, please check out the link attached to my profile.
