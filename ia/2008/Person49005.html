Debra Niewald ’s Summary

Debra Niewald is dedicated to inspiring individuals and organizations to harness their noblest intentions and bring them to life.

Prior to partnering with Vistage International as a Group Chair in Kansas City, Debra spent more than 20 years in New York City and Chicago designing and executing breakthrough programs, on both the client and agency side, for Fortune 1000 companies and start-up ventures in the technology, consumer goods and entertainment sectors.

For those looking to open up new market space, launch products and services, or drive innovation inside their organizations, Debra's strong suit is in identifying value innovation strategies that distinguish a company from its competition and elevate brand value.

As a Vistage Business Coach, Debra engages Chief Executives in a monthly board room setting, leading a discussion of the members' most challenging business issues and opportunities. This confidential Board of Advisors, from non-competing companies, has no other agenda than to support one another in achieving both personal and professional success.
