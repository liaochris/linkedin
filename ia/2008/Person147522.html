Paul Cowell’s Summary

I have an honours degree in Chemistry from Manchester University, and a Post Graduate Diploma in Textile Technology and Management. The Society of Dyer’s and Colorists (SDC) have awarded Chartered Colorist status in recognition of my 18 years experience in the coloration industry. In 1989 I joined ICI and provided technical service and support. Two years later, I moved into the research department and spent 10 years synthesizing novel reactive dye molecules for cellulose. After various mergers and acquisitions, including working for ICI, Zeneca and BASF, in 2001 I relocated to the DyStar HQ in Frankfurt, Germany, where I provided global technical support. A further 2 and half years based in Hong Kong, as the business development manager for DyStar retailer liaison and also Color Solutions International, I am now based in Singapore where I now take on the role of business manager for DyStar Textile Services, specializing in providing a total texile solution to the textile and apparel supply chain.

Paul Cowell’s Specialties:

Technical training, colour measurement, color management, business development, presentation skills, fastness profiling, color development, problem solving, communication skills, chemical environmental compliance, Restricted Substance List communication, cost effective textile processing
