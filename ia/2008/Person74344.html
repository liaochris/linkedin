Hamish Thompson’s Summary

My business, Twelve Thirty Eight, is a specialist PR, reputation and communications consultancy, more details on which are available at www.1238kmh.com.

Our team comprises some of the UK's most experienced in-house communicators and journalists.

We offer a service for businesses that is designed to support their reputational objectives.

We work with organisations of all sizes across industry to define their communications objectives and to deliver their story via quality national press, broadcast and online media coverage. Our focus is on the development of communications programmes that explain organisational purpose, raise customer awareness, spur customer action and build reputation.

Hamish Thompson’s Specialties:

Media relations, media strategy, crisis management, capital markets communications, corporate and financial media relations, consumer media relations, crisis management, speech writing, development of thought leadership materials, broad-based campaigning, public affairs.
