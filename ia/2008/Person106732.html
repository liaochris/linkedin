Kimberly Kwan’s Summary

Crafty from birth and with a professional background in motivational speaking and adult training, Kimberly Kwan has found her niche in paper and photographic arts.

Kim’s professional resume includes stints as a designer and freelance artist for product manufacturers Art Warehouse and Daisy D’s Paper Company and as a regular contributing writer and designer for many of the major industry magazines. Kim is a noted special events instructor at paper-arts trade shows and retreats across the country and internationally, most notably teaching three years in a row both at “Silver Bella” in Omaha and two years in a row at “Version Scrap,” a huge scrapbooking expo in Paris, France.

Kim also has a thriving, three year old portrait photography business in Scottsdale, AZ and has gained quite a notable following for her personality-filled natural light portraiture.

Kim’s over-the-top personality and slightly “off center” style bring a refreshing twist to paper and photographic arts.

Kimberly Kwan’s Specialties:

Photography: Headshots, corporate identity, families, children, maternity, newborns and high-school seniors.

Paper Arts: Vintage inspired mixed-media art and scrapbooking.
