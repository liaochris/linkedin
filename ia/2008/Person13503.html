anna hiltunen’s Summary

Anna Hiltunen has worked as a designer in London since 1998, gaining a rich and varied experience in concept design and research for new technologies, interaction design and interior architecture. She has been working in top design agencies in London and New York as well as running her own service innovation consultancy TEKOLondon Ltd.

TEKO has received UK wide recognition from NESTA (the National Endowment for Science Technology and the Arts) in form of the Creative Pioneer Award in 2004 and the D&AD (Design and Art Direction). Anna holds an MA from the Royal College of Arts, and BA(Hons) (first class) from Kingston University, London, where her work was recognised with awards.

Anna is an energetic, innovative and multi-disciplined designer passionate about great ideas followed through to distinctive design. She is an excellent team worker and project leader and strives to succeed whatever the scope of the project.

Her background is varied with design skills and experience: service design, interaction design, user research, interior architecture, web design, usability and also programming before design took over. From working in different disciplines she has learned many creative thinking techniques, attitude and energy to deliver innovative designs combined with real world issues in implementing the designs (people, politics, budgets, timescales, sourcing…). She has also developed sensitivity to being able to see the big picture, spot a great concept or a seed of a great idea, open mindedness and understanding of cultural differences.
