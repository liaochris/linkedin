Ryan Dowd’s Summary

I would like to be a part of the team that defines/re-defines the direction for a performance footwear brand and eventually own or be in an ownership group of such a brand.

I have planned product lines, developed product, and helped set the design language for multiple footwear brands. Most notably, my company has designed products that have won awards for our clients and helped our clients make an impact on their categories.

I designed the Wilson Open II and it is set to release in SS '08; this item is featured on the cover of Wilson's catalog, Teva Sampago was one of their top three sellers for the first six months following it release on teva.com, Backpacker Magazine gave the Danner GTX 453 the Best Buy Award in 2006, Running Network gave the Etonic Hybrid the 2005 Best New Shoe Award, And Malden Mills gave a Jibs product that I designed a Best Use of Materials Award.

Ryan Dowd’s Specialties:

I've been designing consumer goods, both athletic and in other markets since 1997. Athletic footwear design makes up about 80% of my business, currently. My clients are typically major footwear manufacturers based in the US with production facilities abroad.
