Andrei Shchegrov’s Summary

Engineering manager and technical contributor and with >10 years of R&D experience in general optics, lasers, metrology, and advanced modeling.

Several novel products developed from concept into manufacturing. Over 10 patents and patent applications, over 100 papers, conference presentations, and review articles in optics, lasers, and surface sciences; 2 book chapters.

Andrei Shchegrov’s Specialties:

- Technical project and team management
- Semiconductor, solid-state, and other laser design, UV to IR
- Linear and nonlinear optics expertise, hands-on (lab) & theory
- Development of technical and business plans
- Optical system development, optimization, and integration
- Semiconductor metrology methods and algorithms
