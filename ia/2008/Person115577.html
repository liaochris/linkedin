Lori White’s Summary

Fascinated by the interwebs and how they can be a force for good and help us to keep it real in the land of Marketing & Branding.

I work with clients to build their conversations, shape their story and create community through blogging, branding, some visual thinking and some no-nonsense traditional marketing, complete with bottom lines and all. The more hats for me to wear, the better I look - from project managing to research, recruiting to writing, technical to qualitative.

I hope to flesh what I have learned in an entry level position at a firm, start-up or company in need of passion for really communicating with and advocating for the customer while serving the needs of the market.
It can be done.

During my college years, I got the branding/marketing bug participating hands-on in the branding and re-branding of a couple of organizations at the U of P. In my un-free time there, I built up a background in deep socio-political theory, comparative literature, language and Conversation (wasn't too bad at the lower-case conversation, either) - skills I lean upon daily in one way or another for my as as new media marketing consultant.

Lori White’s Specialties:

Blogging and RSS, increasing community outreach and relevance within the on and offline community, a bit of pinko marketing thrown in there.
