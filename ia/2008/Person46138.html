David Bradford’s Summary

I am Founder/CEO or Advisor to a number of Web-based companies including currently Linking Universe, FundingUniverse.com, Turbulin.com and Greenwala.com. Linking Universe has been launched as the most powerful business application on Facebook. Funding Universe is a fast growing website that connects Entrepreneurs with Angel Investors. Greenwala is an important new social network for everything "green." Turbulin.com – is an important new web-based portal offering on-demand call center services

Most recently, I joined Fusion-io as a senior member of their Advisory Board. See - www.fusionio.com. Fusion-io has developed a truly disruptive technology called the ioDrive. The Fusion ioDrive is a high-density, extremely fast memory extension or drive replacement based on flash memory technology.

Fusion-io just inked a major deal with Hewlett-Packard. Fusion-io is causing companies to completely rethink their storage architecture. The carbon footprint of this technology is only 1% of current technology.

I am also heavily involved in private equity. My current passion is HG Equity a new buyout fund involving Jon Huntsman, Sr, Bob Gay, and Steve Young. I have 30 years in the computer industry in both a Business and Legal capacity and am known as Super Connector with a broad network of key contacts.

One of my latest endeavors is on the Advisory Board for Fusion-io which is redefining the computer storage market with Solid state Flash techology which puts the "power of the SAN in the palm of your hand."

Prior to my work on the Internet and in private equity, I served as General Legal Counsel to a number of entities. For 15 years I was General Counsel to Novell, Inc which between 1985 and 1992 was the fastest growing stock in America. I also served in a similar capacity to Dynix Corporation, Prime Computer and for Governor elect of Utah, Jon Huntsman, Jr.
