Daniele Busi’s Summary

From 1997 to 2003 he works for Quam (now nurun Italia), one of the very first Italian web agencies, leading project teams for the agency main clients: BancaDellaRete, Gruppo Miroglio, MTV Italia, Telecom Italia, Abbey National Bank, CHL, Finanza&Futuro, Ferrero, Galbani, L'Oréal, Altavista, Vitaminic, Alpitour, Autostrade, Prénatal, Baci Perugina, Enervit, La Repubblica.
In 2000, he is nurun Italia's Client Service Director and collaborates with Accedemia di Comunicazione and Politecnico of Milan as external teacher.
In 2003, he becomes Partner of dodicitrenta, specialized agency which works on the 12-20 years old target audience (guerrilla marketing, interactive design, cross media design).
In 2004 he joins Tribal DDB (A member of Tribal DDB Worldwide - A member of DDB Group).
In January 2007 he becames Business Unit Director at Tribal DDB.
In March 2008 he joins Publicis Modem (Publicis Groupe) as Business Unit Director.
