Alexandre Brabant’s Summary

Alexandre Brabant is a pioneer in Vancouver (BC) Canada in regards to the implementation of Search Engine Marketing technologies.

Since the year 2000, Alexandre Brabant has built, implemented and broke new ground in online marketing in multiple industries such as travel and tourism, social networking and telephone services. Over the years, he has been developing a thorough understanding of Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and highly effective online media buying. Being considered an search evangelist by his colleagues, his passion for search & new marketing technologies has surpassed his employers & clients’ highest expectations. As such, he delivers the bottom line and that is what matters to him most. His passion for helping everyone to understand and dominate the search space, while still upholding core branding principles, has helped a lot of companies over the years in implementing SEM best practices in Web site development and media buying.

Prior to joining Teligence (www.teligence.net) in 2005 as a Search Marketing Specialist, Alexandre Brabant has work with Intrawest Corporation (www.intrawest.com) to maximize their online presence & sales from websites such as whistlerblackcomb.com, tremblant.com, vancouver.com and 40 others destination-focused websites. Alexandre Brabant is also the founder & president of Vancouver eMarketing 101, www.eMarketing101.net, where he plays the role of Search Marketing expert for numerous clients in multiple industries. Alex has recently been appointed as Director of Search Marketing for Cossette Media in Vancouver where he delivers outstanding paid search programs for Tourism British Columbia (HelloBC.com)

Alexandre Brabant is also Co-Chair and Founder of SEMPO Canada Working Group (Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization in Canada) and as such is actively working in the Canadian Search Marketing community.

Alex is 100% fluent in both French & English.
