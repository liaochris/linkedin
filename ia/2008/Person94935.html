John Hauxwell’s Summary

Over the last 10 years John has worked in the content, fixed-line, iTV and mobile business arenas holding a variety of senior strategic, marketing and technical roles. He has managed and delivered many pioneering products in partnership with some of the worlds leading brands. These have included Bwin, Accenture, Vodafone, Real, Universal, O2, Nokia, Microsoft, BBC, RTL, EMI and Sony. He has completed and launched several large scale programs bringing together messaging, content and delivery solutions on time and with budgets ranging from 200,000 to 20 million euros. His knowledge of customer requirements, together with his end-to-end understanding of the underlying technologies, makes him a formidable player in today’s converging marketplace.

John has worked with many of the regulatory organizations including the SDMI, MPEGLA and the OMA and he is an advocate of Open Source (JAVA) and Content Protection (DRM). His strategic vision and, detailed knowledge of emerging technologies give him a valuable and unique perspective in all aspects of business development and direction. His excellent communication skills and business awareness guarantees that he gains buy in from stakeholders and decision makers at board level. They have also made him a charismatic evangelist and a welcome guest speaker at several events. His combination of creative, marketing, man management, and technical skills ensure he is an innovator and a provider of direction for both strategic and critical decisions.

As a champion of change, John has proposed and delivered several strategies, for “future-proofing” and extending the profitability of new and existing products. He has a wealth of experience in delivering key revenue generating initiatives. This wide scope of expertise and his market awareness, combined with an holistic, “open door” and hands on management style, makes him a dynamic, and creative leader, as well as an exciting and inspirational team member
