Yannis Nakos’s Summary

As ops. manager I was covering all aspects of the consulting team's management such as :
- Resource & purchase mgt.
- All legal & accounting aspects of the consulting/delivery department's life + according processes streamlining.
- Sales, pre-sales to post-sales of ECM solutions
- Marketing : partner engagement to execute common business plan

Today I'm focused in shaping an integrated BeLux Information Management practice at Fujitsu Services, to grow our business by helping our customers setting up meaningfull and innovative ECM strategies.

My time is balanced between:
-work on field for complex ECM/WCM architectures
-pre-sales/sales support on major deals or key accounts
-ECM process consultancy based on Fujitsu's methodologies such as Macroscope & Lean 6Sigma.

In order to "walk the talk", I'm involved as Change Champion in Fujitsu's BeLux transformation program based on techniques used at customers. I'm also board member at "Document at Work" the local professional body for ECM (ex-"BelAIIM", the Belgian chapter of the AIIM association).

Main achievements in the past :
- In 6 years as part of my team, I helped (it's never a 1 person's story though!) increase the revenue from about 600K€ to 4.4M€ with the same amount of heads (our revenue almost doubled every 2 years) in a ECM practice.
- Recovery of non-profitable projects for years, to a profitable (with about 30% margin) situation from the moment I moved as manager of the team.
- Definition of internal processes and according web-applications to streamline the making and approval of my department's invoices (A.P. & A.R.), + recovery of control on annuity-driven activities to consolidate more than 1M€ recurrent business
- Making the assessment for acquiring an activity which proved profitable as from 1st fiscal year, drawing full ROI in < 3 years.
- Preparing (as MD) the local branch of an IT services company being acquired as part of global acquisition plan : acquisition got through.
