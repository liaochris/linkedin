Ian Jagger’s Summary

I'm responsible for the marketing of HP's Data Center Services portfolio world-wide. I relocated to the US in January 2007 from Vienna, Austria where I was the marketing manager for HP Servcies for the CEEMA region. Previous to that I had joined HP in July 2003 as the HP Services Marketing Manager in the Middle East region, based in Dubai.

Prior to joining HP I had been a marketing consultant with a technology investment fund, and the Marketing Director of a telecom services company, both Dubai-based roles. Prior to relocating to Dubai I had previously been the Sales and Marketing Manager for Northern Europe for Steelcase inc.

My professional experience covers all marketing disciplines, team management and individual contribution, from strategic development and planning through to integrated marketing communications program building and execution. It also includes corporate marketing and AR/PR.

My goal is to apply sound marketing principles to drive growth of my portfolio at above market rate, gaining market share, in the process making HP the recognized industry leader within the data center

A Chartered Marketer, MCIM and DipM, educated at Loughborough University, England, graduating with joints Bsc honors in economics and social psychology. I'm based in Cary, North Carolina.
