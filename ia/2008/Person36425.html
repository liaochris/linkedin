Christina Ray’s Summary

Glowlab, the curatorial project of artist Christina Ray, develops interactive art/technology projects, events and exhibitions; publishes a web-based magazine; and produces Conflux, an annual NYC festival for creative investigation of urban public space.


We also offer custom website and software development services.

Christina Ray’s Experience

  • Founder/Director

    Conflux Festival

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Performing Arts industry)

    January 2003 — Present (5 years 1 month)

    Conflux is the annual New York City festival where visual and sound artists, writers, urban adventurers and the public gather for four days to explore their urban environment.

    At Conflux, people from a wide variety of backgrounds and cultures come together to re-imagine the city as a playground, a space for positive change and an opportunity for civic engagement. Over the course of the festival the sidewalks are literally transformed into a mobile laboratory for creative action. With tools ranging from traditional paper maps to high-tech mobile devices, artists present walking tours, public installations and performance, as well as bike and subway expeditions, workshops, a lecture series, a film program and live music performances at night.

    http://confluxfestival.org

    Conflux is produced by Glowlab.

  • Founder/Director

    Glowlab Productions LLC

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Fine Art industry)

    April 2002 — Present (5 years 10 months)

    Glowlab, the curatorial project of Brooklyn-based artist and curator Christina Ray, produces and presents experimental art and technology exploring the nature of cities. Glowlab develops interactive artwork and projects; curates events and exhibitions; publishes a web-based magazine; and produces Conflux, an annual festival in New York for the creative investigation of urban public space.
