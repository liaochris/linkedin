Vann Baker’s Summary

Vann Baker founded Design-First with the idea that marketing and web site projects should start with design . . . but include a healthy mix of understanding clients' marketing goals and audience, brainstorming and frequent reality checks in order to turn marketing ideas into reality.

As a seasoned professional with over 25 years of experience working with public relations firms, business owners, marketing directors and small business owners, Vann brings his common sense approach to design real-world experience to every project.

Design-First offers website design and creative marketing pieces for companies of all sizes with project budgets ranging from $5,000 to over $30,000.

While producing creative ideas is a large part of marketing, project management is just as important, and Vann's "hands-on" experience with project planning and working with creative teams allows him to meet--and exceed--client expectations.

Design-First has a reputation for being extremely detailed oriented, practical and creative, all at the same time.

Vann Baker’s Specialties:

Branding, Corporate Identity, Marketing Collateral, Website Design and Strategy, Application Integration, and Print Coordination.
