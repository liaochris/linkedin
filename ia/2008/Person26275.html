Michael Hemenway’s Summary

Mr. Hemenway has the innate ability to sense opportunity and work hard to achieve success. With a lifetime spent as a serial entrepreneur, and over 30 years of business, finance, advertising, marketing and franchise experience. Having developed, funded, operated and sold many businesses on his path to success; he brings with him a wealth of experience, creative vision and hands-on business knowledge. Mr. Hemenway played a major role in establishing and developing an internet network of franchise business opportunities and concept consulting services worldwide. Mr. Hemenway is the Co-Founder, Publisher and Chief Executive Officer of brandEXPANSION, Ltd., and oversees the overall direction of the company and future projects.
