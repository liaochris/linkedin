Jill Oana’s Summary

Jill Oana is a creative communicator, specializing in securing relationships with customers, partners and distributors. She launches successful go-to-market global marketing programs within budget. With an aptitude for defining strategy and implementing tactical details from concept through completion, while leveraging her broad experience in web production, video, advertising, trade shows, and integrated marketing campaigns, Jill has a proven track record in developing solutions that are memorable. She has held senior management positions with start-ups and large companies in retail, consumer and technology industries. Additionally, she has consulted for companies and founded Jillsicals, a custom gift company. Early in her career, Jill held positions with Walt Disney Pictures and Young & Rubicam Entertainment.

Jill Oana’s Specialties:
