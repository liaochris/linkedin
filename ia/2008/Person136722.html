Mark Thacker’s Summary

A proven start up executive in three emerging businesses, with a focus on product management and product distribution through indirect channels. I am currently running a venture backed data warehouse appliance company based in Southern California - www.datallegro.com - I have held a series of management positions in the marketing and sales divisions of companies such as Schlumberger and Cable & Wireless in UK, USA and Australia, launching a number of successful Internet products in the Security, Internet Banking, Directory Services and ISP infrastructure areas. Engaged and shaped a number of joint ventures working at senior levels with; Accenture, Microsoft, Compaq, Citrix and Netscape.

Mark Thacker’s Specialties:

Market Entry Strategy, International Business Development, Product Management, Solution Sales, Strategic Alliance Development, Partner Management, Start up experience, ASP,ISP, Telecom Managed Services.
