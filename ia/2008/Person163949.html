Scott Rains’s Summary

I publish daily news notes and commentary in the Rolling Rains Report on Universal Design, Travel & Disability at http://www.RollingRains.com

Presentations I have given since 2005 include at the Rhode Island School of Design and as conference keynoter in Australia, Brazil, Japan, Korea, and Taiwan.

General readership articles appear in the Travel & Tourism section of Suite101.com, Bootsnall.com, and at Travel & Transitions.com. A bi-weekly column appears online at the startup channel [with]TV.

Resources for tourism students appear at the Brazilian portal eTur.com.br in our Social Inclusion Forum.

Academic work includes guest editing the Travel & Disability issue of the journal Review of Disability Studies and contributions to the journal Disability Studies.

Scott Rains’s Specialties:

research, speak, and write on the adoption of Universal Design by the worldwide travel & hospitality industry.
