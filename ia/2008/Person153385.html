Krishna Prathab R V’s Summary

I hold a Masters in Software Engineering from a premier institute in India. I have a gold medal for academic excellence and a university rank for the same. I started my career with Siemens as a Software Engineer trainee and was working on Java, LDAP and Oracle.

Moved on to GECIS, the e-learning division of General Electric in Hyderabad, India where I was working as a Flash Programmer. I was developing e-learning applications for GE Modular Space and GE Energy Systems. Here I was awarded 5 times the Zero Defect Champion award for error free products that I delivered.

My third job was with Convergys where I was working as a Content Engineer leading technical requirements on e-learning projects. I was creating learning contents for Fortune 500 companies and was working on Flash, ActionScript, SCORM, AICC and industry standard LMS'es, Here I was awarded the Content Engineer of the month award for my contribution towards the success of the organization.

From there I moved on to join Macromedia R&D in Bangalore. I was working in the Flash Authoring Development team as an Engineer and was involved in the analysis of Data Components and the general Flash authoring tool. I was part of the Flash 8 shipping team. Here I was awarded the “Above & Beyond” award for my contribution to the Flash authoring team.

At present I am working as a Sr. Systems Architect (Macromedia Technologies) in the technology practice area of Hurix. My responsibilities here include, creating software architecture for enterprise applications, creating sales proposals for new business opportunities, customer interaction on the technical front and managing and mentoring a team of highly skilled technical resources. My technical competency lies in Flash, ActionScript and complex rich internet applications. I am a Macromedia certified Flash MX 2004 Developer and I am also a Brainbench Certified Flash MX Developer (Master Level) and Experts Exchange Certified Expert – Master Level.
