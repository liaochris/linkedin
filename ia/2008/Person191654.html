Tony Hay’s Summary

A graduate of Newcastle University with an honours degree in Politics & History (1985), Tony began his career in financial publishing in 1986 with Money Magazine in London. Unusually, he has worked both as a journalist and on the commercial side of business publishing.

Tony set up Responsible Investor with Hugh Wheelan in April 2007. Responsible Investor provides institutional investors with a comprehensive online magazine and information resource which recognises the growing requirement to achieve investment returns within an environmental, social and corporate governance framework.

Prior to Responsible Investor, he was sales director at IPE International Publishers for 10 years. Joining in 1996 he became a director in 1998 and developed a range of new initiatives such as IPE-Quest, the IPE Awards and the company’s widely acclaimed web site, IPE.com. Tony’s role at IPE was both creative and commercial with specific responsibility for business development.

Tony Hay’s Specialties:

Publishing, sales, product development, project management.
