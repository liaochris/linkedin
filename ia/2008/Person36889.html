Christopher Knight’s Summary

Chris Knight is a a Director of Acutus Trading Ltd, a privately-held small capital investment business. A former software developer, Chris is also an Associate focusing on commercial construction litigation at the Calgary office of Fraser Milner Casgrain LLP, a leading Canadian law firm.

Chris holds a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy with Political Science from Simon Fraser University and a Bachelor of Laws from the University of Western Ontario. He is an avid world traveller and has gained first-hand experience living and working outside of Canada, primarily in England and France, with professional and personal contacts extending around the globe from Canada, to the United States, the United Kingdom, Australia and Germany.
