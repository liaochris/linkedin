  • COO

Biggest customers being AGFA and Sony required major integration with SAP and Extranet solutions.

    Sold the company in 2003 to start Phoenix Imaging Technologies.

    Negotiated cooperation agreements with worldwide partners and customers including Acer (Benq), Kodak, Pioneer, Motorola and RealNetworks. Ensured realization of New Business strategy via the initiation and deployment of organizational transformation from product focused to customer focused business organization.

    Leader of the Business Unit’s change-project, responsible for the development and implementation of all New-Business processes.
