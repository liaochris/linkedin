D. Rob Haley’s Summary

D. ROB HALEY PhD, MBA, MHS, Director, Market Research, Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Florida, Inc. (BCBSF) and Assistant Professor, University of North Florida

I began my career as the Director in Strategic Planning with Martin Memorial Health Systems, a 336-bed integrated delivery health care system. I am now the Director of Research at Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Florida (BCBSF) where I research new products and services, brand performance, segmentation and targeting strategies and initiatives.

I have held several positions since joining BCBSF in 1999, including Director of Market Management and Senior Policy Analyst where I researched Florida’s health insurance market and identified public policy recommendations and initiatives. I earned a Ph.D. in Health Policy and Administration from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, School of Public Health, and an MBA and MHS from the University of Florida.

I am also an Assistant Professor at the University of North Florida's College of Health where I teach in the Department of Public Health's Health Administration Program. My research is focused on the US and China Health Care Systems. I am collaborating with China's Center for Health Policy and Management in the Shandong Province on identifying and implementing solutions to their delivery and financing challenges. Most recently, I presented at China Health Care Summit in Jinan, Macau, and Qingdao. I was also recognized as a Fulbright Scholar Senior Specialist in Global and Public Health and I am currently working with the Egyptian government to develop health management and leadership program.
