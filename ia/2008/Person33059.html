Chad Beasley’s Summary

Hi, I'm Chad Beasley. I'm originally from Tuscaloosa, Alabama where I graduated from the University of Alabama. I've lived & worked in the Birmingham, AL area since 1995. In 1997 I began my career in real estate. I've been with RE/MAX Southern Homes for most of my career. I specialize in residential real estate in the Greater Birmingham area including Jefferson & Shelby Counties. I have been listed among the top 20 individual RE/MAX agents in Alabama.

My wife Jennifer & I have been married since 1995. We have 2 children, Parker (age 9) & Halle (age 6). We live in the Shelby County city of Chelsea & our family attends nearby Oak Mountain Community Church.
