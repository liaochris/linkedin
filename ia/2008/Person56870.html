Edwin Kennedy’s Summary

Edwin Kennedy is an experienced executive with extensive senior sales and general management background. Over the years he developed a broad background in the global financial sector and has a strong track record of successfully developing new business opportunities. Edwin currently holds the position as Managing Partner at Aquila and at Scalepoint.

Prior to joining Aquila, Edwin did several interim assignments. He was responsible for selling Xansa Continental Europe and for integrating the operations. He also served at India based HCL Technologies where he played a crucial role in expanding the European footprint. Prior to that he served at EDS as Vice President, Global Financial Industry Group in Dallas, New York and Europe. He spent the first five years of his career at Inter Access, where he got the opportunity to grow to division manager and was the founder of Syfact, a leading global provider of investigative case management solutions. Edwin started his career as an analyst at the Dutch Military Intelligence Services.

Edwin is Associated Professor at Nyenrode Business University for the Managing Global Resources Seminar in the Executive Master of Business Administration programs of the Institute of High learning.

Edwin is the founder of a Foundation supporting the people in Chiang Mai, Thailand (www.mevrouwpon.nl) and a keen student at www.kenpokarate.nl.

Edwin Kennedy’s Specialties:

Business Process Outsourcing, Strategic Sourcing, Strategic Value Selling, Claims Management and Anti Money Laundering.
