Andy Cuff’s Summary

I am available as a consultant or contractor, either full or part time.
Having created and worked within a number of Security Operation Centres (SOC's) both with technical support and incident response, I understand the Information Security threats facing networks and the resultant risks that must be prioritised and mitigated through security architecture and design. I specialise in providing defence in depth solutions, covering all aspects of the Information Security battlespace, from the network boundaries, through content protection down to the hosts and endpoint security, including antivirus at all levels from the workstations and servers to the gateways and beyond. I also work with Network Access Control (NAC), anomaly detection through Netflow security and defending against information leakage using extrusion prevention systems. I am completely vendor neutral and work hard to match the technology to the environment.
Current SC & DV

My work as a Penetration Tester (Ethical Hacker) provides further insight into how best to defend a network.

Furthermore, I have been involved in some of the more advanced forms of corporate security, including communication, personnel and physical security.
Finally, as a CESG qualified TEMPEST tester and FCO trained TSCM bug sweeper I can also defend the electronic security spectrum.

CISSP Certified 119360

I have also partnered with a highly experienced Recruitment Consultant to provide candidates who are sifted more effectively. My role is to filter candidates from a technical Infosec perspective, my broad experience in the field reduces the chance of being mislead by desperate candidates. Together we form an outstanding recruitment team.
