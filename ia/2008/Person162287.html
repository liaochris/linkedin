Robert Graham’s Summary

I've been known to have strong relationships with the operating management team. Given that they are the ones that are making money for the organization, I feel that supporting them is key. Creative use of spreadsheets to provide timely, informative reports to the rest of the management team is how I express my creativity (yes, I know, once an accountant, always an accountant!).

Now, I'm looking for the right opportunity where I can truly be part of the management team and work with others to help everyone grow. I've worked to develop financial reporting for creditors, equity holders, management, and executives. Surviving the bankruptcy of Cornerstone Propane, I led the "fresh start" accounting and implemented improved accounting and reporting procedures at Titan Propane after the emergence from bankruptcy.

My experience with due diligence on acquisitions has given me a down to earth perspective on prospective acquisitions, and my hands on approach to reporting has led me to develop easy to update, simple to understand reports.
