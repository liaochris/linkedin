Harvey Langston-Jones’s Summary

M_AP is committed to providing architecture of a high calibre that responds to it's environment, and addresses the issue of context and sustainability. Attention to detail and a sensual appreciation of space, light and materials defines the work ethos.

To explore a sense of 'rightness' Langston-Jones investigate materials and methods of construction. Everyday materials are used in an unfamiliar way, allowing either the object or space to transform from the usual into the unusual. Laser-cut limestone, gold leafed resin or banana grass walls are only a few of the combinations so far realised.

Harvey Langston-Jones’s Specialties:

Each project is tailor made to specific requirements, fitting the Client like a Saville Row suit.
