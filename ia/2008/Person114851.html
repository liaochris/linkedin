Elizabeth Thorne’s Summary

Designers & Developers Ltd works within the creative world of print, web, marketing, IT and IPTV projects, helping clients and candidates to find their perfect person or job, this may be in the creative, techie, or suit department. This means that we don’t spread ourselves thinly by sourcing all roles, but instead, put all of our energy into understanding and developing the creative world.
We help companies that are looking for candidates who have flair, talent and passion for what they create and we offer candidates exciting companies to work for.
Designers & Developers has over 10 years experience recruiting in this space and holds a database full of talented candidates solely interested in the creative world. For these chaps and chapesses we also have a database full of creative agencies that they can choose from. How cool is that!
