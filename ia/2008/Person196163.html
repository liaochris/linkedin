Dominique Pryor-Anderson dominique@onevida.com’s Summary

I am a Publicist and I LOVE what I do. I have worked in PR and Marketing since 1997 and started my firm in 2003. We went totally virtual just recently, in 2008. My experiences have led me to work on campaigns for everything and everyone from Revlon to the Memphis Botanic Gardens to Dr. Mark Klein, who was working towards a nomination in the 2004 Presidential election!


What does VIDA Do?

We introduce your brand to the world. VIDA build visibility campaigns for our clients. The campaigns can include:

*Media placement (tv, print, radio, internet)
*Internet brand building (including building websites and social networking pages and creating social networking campaigns)
*Special Events (product launches, grand opening, galas, etc.)

And through Success Over Spanish (www.successoverspanish.com) we offer:
*Translation
*Interpretation
*Corporate Conversational Spanish classes
*Spanish on Wheels, a conversational Spanish program for 5-10 year olds.


In addition to PR & Marketing, I am also a Realtor. I specialize in luxury real estate. I work with buyers and sellers to complete the buying and selling process effortlessly and smoothly.

I am able to multi-task because VIDA has an outstanding team of experienced, seasoned consultants and wonderful and prompt freelancers, in addition to my having a super assistant.
