    Affinity Maker

    (Management Consulting industry)

    September 2005 — Present (3 years 2 months)

    Affinity Maker™ are business partnership and alliance specialists that help organisations with:

    - New Partnerships – to unlock the potential by spotting commercial opportunities between organisations across sector and geographical boundaries, identifying target partners, creating effective connections and brokering relationships

    - Existing Partnerships - to deal with the challenges faced in their partnerships by repairing underperforming, damaged or broken partnerships or objectively reviewing the status quo

    We lead or support each step of the partnering process:
    "Create; Enable; Maximise the Value"

    - As a Partnership Broker / Intermediary, we identify, build and strengthen partnerships, especially in the early stages of the partnership lifecycle.

    - As a Facilitator, we are appointed to manage a specific aspect of the partnering process

    - As an Educator, we help our clients develop their internal partnering and alliancing capability and become a Partner of Choice
