Bill Berg’s Summary

My experience includes twelve years in mortgage loan servicing employed with two industry leading firms (Ocwen and Wilshire) specializing in the servicing of sub-prime and non-performing mortgages for various large investment banks including: Merrill Lynch, CSFB, GE Capital, and Salomon Brothers. In this industry, I managed a diverse group of areas including: collections, customer service, investor reporting, and back-office operations. And for three years, I was Chief Information Officer managing mortgage analytics, customer contact technology, help desk, programming and IT operations. In addition, I was Director of US Revenue for Credigy Solutions, Inc. There I managed all revenue producing departments in this credit card receivable management company. For the past year, I have been doing consulting work and other private ventures.

My goal is either an interesting and challenging full-time position in north Atlanta or a contract/consulting position with travel. In either case, I want to use my experience to make a difference.
