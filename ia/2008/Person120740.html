Maria Lucia Uribe’s Summary

A highly motivated social development practitioner working on ethics education and interfaith learning, with eight years of experience working on leadership development in children and young people. Convinced about the importance of quality education based on competencies and ethical values that can foster a culture of peace and the ability in children to learn how to live together. Able to develop trainings and materials on cultural understanding and interfaith learning for children, young people and adults.

I see myself working on the development of new learning and educational methodologies for children and young people and implementing new models of quality education mainly in developing countries.
