Monique Terrell’s Summary

Sparkle Professional Office Services is a virtual assistant company launched by Monique Terrell on April 23, 2008. Monique Terrell is a veteran of the United States Navy and a certified virtual instructor. I have over 15 years of experience working in an office environment with specializations in office/HR management, software application support, marketing and professional development training.

I hold a Bachelors of Science in Business Management graduating Cum Laude and currently working toward my MBA in Mediation & Dispute Resolution. I am an excellent communicator, Internet researcher, trainer and project manager. Monique has served in multiples roles such as Technology Support Manager, Academic Technology Manager, eLearning Supervisor, Web Site Designer and Executive Assistant.

I am an active member within my community serving on several boards and committees. I am a graduate of Leadership Edmond Class XXI, a program hosted by the Edmond Chamber of Commerce.

Sparkle Professional Office Services is looking forward to serving your business needs!
