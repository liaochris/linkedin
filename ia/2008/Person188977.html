Tim Fox’s Summary

Tim is a software architect and engineer with over 13 years of experience in the industry.

Tim was also one of the original core developers of the mobicents project - the first open source implementation of the JAIN SLEE 1.0 specification, which was acquired by Red Hat to form the basis of the new Red Hat communications platform.

Tim currently works at JBoss where he is the Messaging Lead and his mission is to create the world's best messaging system.
