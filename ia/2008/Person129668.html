Michael Farish’s Summary

A founding member of Design Motive, Michael has twenty years experience advising, designing and implementing major brand identity programs for clients world-wide across a wide range of sectors.

Michael has worked long term for clients including Amadeus, Sibelius, Burgess, Amadeus, Wyndham Hotel Group, Hertz, Weir Group PLC and countless other B2C and B2B brands. This has given him a depth of experience critical to successfully lead the direction, development & implementation of global brands.

Before forming Design Motive, Michael worked for Landor Associates (US), Don Kline Co. (US) and Nucleus Design (UK). He is a graduate of St Martins School of Art, from which he holds a first class BA-Honours degree in graphic design.
