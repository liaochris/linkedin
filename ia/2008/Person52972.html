Dominic Lee’s Summary

At the age of 23, Dominic is already a seasoned ecommerce entreprenuer with two years of internet business experience under his belt and an internet marketing expert.

In February 2006, Dominic co-founded Guillen Global LLC, a B2C ecommerce company which focuses on serving niche markets such as concession equipment, pet supplies, and baby gifts.

Beginning with sites like MyPopcornMachine.com, LGTickets.com, and WholesaleCollegeTextbooks.com, He grew his companies from $500 initial investment to $400K in sales in the first year of operation (2006) and $700K in sales in 2007 by adding PetPetSupplies.com and BabyGiftsUnique.com.

In September 2007, Dominic founded Newpire Ltd., a consumer manufacturer based in Hong Kong which develops and markets cutting edge products in niches such as golf training aid and pet supplies.

Prior to Guillen Global LLC and Newpire Ltd., Dominic has campaigned for Hong Kong Legislative Council member James Tien and has interned at some of the most prestigious organizations in the world including Merrill Lynch’s private wealth management group, as well as for US Congressman Al Green.

Dominic graduated from Rice University at Houston, Texas in 2006 with a B.A. in Economics. He is also a certified Google advertising professional as well as a WebCEO certified Search Marketer.
