Maarten Van Laere’s Experience

  • Founder / Owner

    MicroShipping

    (Logistics and Supply Chain industry)

    November 2007 — Present (1 year)

    MicroShipping is a logistics company based in Zaventem, Belgium that is focused on helping non-European based small companies by outsourcing the sales, order handling & shipping of their products within the European Union.

    MicroShipping takes care of invoicing to the customer (incl VAT regulation), cash collection, picking & packing of orders and the shipping to the customer.

    Companies using MicroShipping are charged on a pay per use basis with a very limited monthly fee.

    More info:
    http://www.microshipping.com

  • Founder / CEO

    ServersCheck

    (Computer Software industry)

    January 2003 — Present (5 years 10 months)

    ServersCheck offers a complete solution to monitor server rooms:
    - software to monitor, report and alert on servers & IT systems availability
    - environmental sensors to monitor the conditions in which systems operate
    - security sensors to monitor physical activity within the server room

    By combining hardware sensors & monitoring software; ServersCheck is one of the only companies in the world offering a complete solution to monitor server rooms.

    ServersCheck distributes its products around the globe to over 3000 customers world-wide including blue chip companies like HP, Facebook, Fujitsu, Toshiba, Yahoo, BT, Easynet, Siemens, Ericsson, Philips, Citibank and HSBC.
