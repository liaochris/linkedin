Berna Erol’s Summary

Berna Erol received her M.Sc. and PhD. Degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of British Columbia, in 1998 and 2002, respectively. Since September 2001 she has been a senior research scientist at Ricoh California Research Center, USA. Her main contributions to research and development consist of mobile applications, content-based video and image retrieval, image and video coding and representation, E-learning and E-meeting systems, text retrieval, new era multimedia systems, and applications, and multimedia processing for mobile devices. She has authored or co-authored more than 40 journal and conference papers, three book chapters, and more than 40 patent applications in the area of mobile applications, social networking, web-based services, e-learning, and multimedia signal processing.

Dr. Erol is an associated editor of the IEEE Signal Processing Magazine. She has served in the program or the technical committees of IEEE and ACM Multimedia Conferences. She had been an active participant in the video coding standardization activities such as ITU-T H.263 and MPEG-7.

A complete list of Dr. Erol’s publications can be found at http://rii.ricoh.com/~berna/
