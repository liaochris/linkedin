Francisco Campos-Lopez’s Summary

I started my studies at the Escuela de Cine de Chile (Film School of Chile), back in 2002. Next year I decided to move to Buenos Aires, Argentina. And I begun my studies in Argentina in the Escuela Profesional de Cine ( Professional Film School). Besides I was attending to particular seminars and classes, of acting directing and advanced writing, with the Argentinian script master, Lito Espinoza.

Back in 2003 I shot my first shortfilms around the city, like "Coterraneos", "Coterraneos II", and "Oniria". In 2004 my filmography became more serious... I shot a polemic and shocking short film with the famous chilean actor Patricio Contreras, "BienAventurada". (aka She's Blessed), and the short film "Frenesi 05" (aka Frenzy 05), official selection of the IV Festival de Cine de Tematica Sexual.

In 2005 I shot a teaser and five spots tv of a feature film project, "La Película de Francisco" (aka, Kill for Film). The teaser was selected for the Work in Progress Section of the Valdivia Film Festival and Festival de Viña del Mar, both in Chile.
On that year I attended to a film production and international distribution with the producer Donald Ranvaud (City of God,The Faithful Gardener) in Viña del Mar, Chile.

2006 is the year of the break into the music video clips industry. I made the first single of the chilean indie rock/pop band, COMPOSE, now hit in Mexico and with rotation on the huge music network TeleHit, from Televisa. And i made a single for the famous argentinian rock/pop band, El Otro Yo.

In 2007, my short film, Acassis, featuring the new Argentinian TV hit, the young actress Sabrina Garciarena, was selected in the official selection of the New York International Independent FiIm Festival, and won the "Best Thriller" award.

Now 2008 I`m shooting music video clips for Chile and Argentina, and one of my goals of this year is to became a Commercials Director and shoot music video clips for foreign bands here in Southamerica.
