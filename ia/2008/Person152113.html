Interactive Creative Director- a collaborator, an architect, facilitator, and contributor to online creative communication strategies that enable companies connect and build dialogues with their audiences. For nearly 10 years, I have tapped into the fascinating power of the internet to help others stand out, reach out, interact with and inspire others to engage with the world and those around them.

The ability to bridge innovative, captivating concepts with clients’ aspirations, offers unique opportunity to continually create and maintain successful relationships. Experience Design first and for most is about stimulating emotional connections with people, cultivating trust and empowering communities to build a better tomorrow.
My career has enabled me to bring together passionate visionaries from around the world and directly create intimate relationships focused on measurable results. At Pixelfanatix, we gravitate towards clients who are independent thinkers, who expect personalized experience, high impact, innovative solutions, swift turn around and no-nonsense process.

We design and build interactive marketing solutions that increase visibility and brand loyalty. We frequently partner with agencies and corporate companies to help build unique marketing strategies, that harness collective intelligence, build relationships and give individuals control over data.

If you are a mentor, leader, teacher, entrepreneur, visionary or creative thinker or associated with non-profits, startups or environmentally conscious companies, reach out and introduce yourself, I'd be happy to collaborate in detail on how Pixelfanatix can help you reach your audience, strengthen your message and help you grow.

90% of Pixelfanatix's business comes from referrals. Thanks to all for your incredible support and continual belief in us!
