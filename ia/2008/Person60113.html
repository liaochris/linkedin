Eric Oyler’s Summary

Currently managing internet initiatives and project planning for web development and online marketing for the Americas region, as well as technical lead for all global web development projects. Coordinates with internal resources and third party vendors to build brand awareness and customer loyalty through improved web usability, feature/service building, and increased online communications. Works in a team to develop, design and implement a new Content Management System (CMS) to more efficiently manage product, company and support information as well as streamline web development initiatives across all Navico branded web sites. Works to develop not only marketing-oriented solutions, but customer-oriented solutions for both partners and consumers as well. Also works on the development of an e-commerce channel to supplement our well-established distributor channels.

Navico, Inc. - based in Lysaker, Norway - is a house of brands, including B&G, Eagle, Lowrance, Northstar and Simrad, and is recognized as the largest marine electronics company in the world.
