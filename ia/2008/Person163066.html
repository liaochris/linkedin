Rob Robason’s Summary

I am a software process engineer with special expertise in process troubleshooting and problem solving, measurement, and quanititative management, and with broad experience in all aspects of the software development life cycle, in both the commercial and government sectors. I've been particularly successful at introducing measurement to support decision making, creating innovative solutions to vexing technical and organizational challenges, and bringing the "voice of the customer" into the development process.

Rob Robason’s Specialties:

Product and Process Measurement, Requirements Elicitation and Management, Quantitative Project Management. CMMI.
