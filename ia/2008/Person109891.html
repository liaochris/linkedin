Kyle Hetchler’s Summary

Kyle Hetchler has more than 15 years of hands on sales and recruitment leadership experience in building corporate recruitment strategies. His vision and expertise in recruitment best practices have driven notable enterprise growth and cost savings for a variety of industries.

Offering a rare blend of creative solutions and operational strengths, Kyle has achieved exciting company turnarounds and is recognized for his success in growing sales and profits. His turnaround capability is highlighted by his accomplishments as Recruitment Advisor to Perot Systems. At Perot he lead a distressed company recruitment process to record ‘time to hire’ delivery via process change that included major shifts in design and product delivery, operations, packaging, communications, and relationship management.

Kyle Hetchler’s exceptional track record of business improvement is based on his philosophy of total enterprise engagement in change. He is known for his ability to quickly identify and diagnose recruitment and growth impediments by working with companies to refine their organizational structure, sourcing, recruitment channels, value propositions and ‘go to market’ strategy.
