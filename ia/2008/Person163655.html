Roger Kerk’s Summary

Roger Kerk now runs an integrated marketing promotions agency called Armstrong Integrated Marketing S/B. Since its inception in 2000, the one man show company has grown to an established team of about 20 staffs. During which he has provided marketing consultation services and executed numerous successful marketing campaigns for a variety of clients and industries ranging from FMCG to banking to industrial products to many others. Roger’s portfolio of clientele includes HSBC, Unilever, Clarins, Redbull, Anlene, Jobstreet, Bank Islam, Citibank, Resorts World (Genting), Guinness, Nestle, Cadbury, Bluescope Lysaght Steel (BHP Steel), etc.

At the age of 14, Roger wrote his first marketing proposal and conducted his first business at age 15. His record monthly income hit 4k a year later. During his tenure in his university, at about 20, he converted a sponsored university newsletter into a profit making magazine.

His achievements and many other community contributions were not only recognized by his university (5 Leadership Awards between 1991 to 1995), but also the Governor and Mayor’s offices of Oklahoma City (3 Governor’s Commendation Awards between 1993 to 1995 and Mayor’s Honorary Award in 1994).

Roger graduated with a BA major in Mass Communication and minor in Political Science from Oklahoma City University. Roger is a Certified Professional Trainer and Coach with International Professional Managers Association (IPMA) of UK (2008). He has also been a marketing lecturer and trainer since 1999.
