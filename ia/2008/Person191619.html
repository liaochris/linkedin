Tony Forcucci’s Summary

Tony Forcucci has spent the last 18 years in sales and business development working as an entrepreneur and for select companies in international sourcing, motorsports, world travel, luxury consumables, and online commerce.

Currently he is the Global Director of the Textiles Sourcing Marketplace at MFG.com, the world's largest online marketplace for manufacturing. He is overseeing the development of MFGtextiles.com to provide an efficient and global method of sourcing for pros in the Apparel industry, Home Textiles, and Commercial Textiles.

Tony was also the co-founder and President of Berik Design, the internationally known motorsports apparel brand seen on over 20 racers and teams in the MotoGP, World Superbike and Formula 1 championships. In this capacity he primarily focused on setting up the global sales and distribution channels. He built a multi-million dollar international sales network from the ground up, personally opening the market for the company in eight European countries as well as working directly with the company’s wholesale and retail partners in many countries.

Prior to that, he was Southeast Director of Sales for illy, the most exclusive coffee roaster in the world. Illy is world renowned for its product and image in the highest echelons of design and culinary arts. In that position, he had to specifically sell “on brand” with the main responsibility of partnering the illy product with the top retailers, restaurants, and luxury hotels. He grew the territory sales over 50% year after year consistently exceeding company goals.

In these roles Tony was a part of a larger team but worked independently dealing with the foremost players in those industries, traveling regularly between the USA, Europe, and Asia.

Tony received his BS in International Business from the University of New York at Buffalo completing his final year at the American University of Rome in Italy.
