Christopher Reese’s Summary

I have been involved in the game industry professionally for more than 14 years. I quickly moved up the ranks from a novice engineer to lead engineer while studying under and working closely with many talented and extremely bright engineers.

Every project I have worked on during my career has shipped. I have followed each one from concept to Gold Master. Out of 11 titles that I have been involved in 7 have received critical acclaim.

I specifically look for a technical challenge in every project I work on. Pushing the envelope is absolutely required when I get involved and only helps fuel the desire to achieve the ever evolving AAA quality in the games I work on.
