Brian Moriarty’s Summary

I run the M&A execution and acquisition integration functions for Sun Microsystems.

I've spent most of my career in M&A execution as an investment banker (ten years) and as a corporate development professional (seven years and counting); I've closed over 100 transactions ranging in size from very small to several billion dollars. I've closed virtually all types of business combinations including mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, joint ventures and minority stakes. As an M&A banker, I covered several industries including heavy industry, automotive, transportation, building products and consumer packaged goods. As a corporate development professional, I've worked in high tech and, to a lesser extent, healthcare services.

A few years ago I took responsibility for Sun's acquisition integration function, working with a high performing, cross functional team to integrate more than seven companies into Sun to date.

I've also closed several capital raises including a $60M venture round and KKR's $700M investment in Sun Microsystems.

Brian Moriarty’s Specialties:

Execution skills: business planning / financial analysis, structuring, valuation, negotiation, balancing risk / return issues through due diligence.

Integration skills: executive relationships, leading cross-functional teams, project management.
