Marcin Nowak’s Summary

EuroAsiaTime Group is an Group whose main goal is to help small and medium enterprises from Asia and Europe to reach much higher profits while reducing costs.
It is a form of building of a new and efficient distribution channel for products and services.

Euro Asian Business Group unites an electronic catalog of companies and trade leads.
Each company can publish both information about their activity, contact data, and also information about products or services offered.
Members of our Group have a full access to all information allowing them to establish a business contact and exchanging information with a potential partners from Asia and Europe.

Idea of Euro Asian Business Group is addressed for all Asian and European companies interested in Asian and European market, who seek there new sources of competitive advantages and chances for development. We would like to establish a promotion platform for Asian and European investments, companies and products, also to promote European market in Asia, and create a good image of Asian economy in Europe.

Euro Asian Business Group target is establishing a safe and effective conditions of running a business in Asia and Europe for Asian / European entrepreneurs and increasing of interest of Asian / European companies in Asian / European market and helping in taking advantage of Asian / European market opportunities.
