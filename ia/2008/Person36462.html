Christine Allard’s Summary

In a nutshell? I'm a Creative Gun for Hire.

Clients rely on my no-BS attitude to give 'em a dose of Marketing Tough Love. From strategy to offers, from concept to layout, from headline to CTA, I can quickly assess what's working...and what's not. From there, I help build a plan to improve results at every conversion point to optimize leads and sales.

Recent clients: Praxis HR, Payscale, Watermark Credit Union, T-Mobile, Microsoft, Big Fish, Irwin Union Bank, Northwest Flower and Garden Show
