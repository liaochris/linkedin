Raaya Design’s Summary

Raaya is creating a global exchange of design thoughts, concepts, ideas as a platform for multi-disciplinary teams in collaborative design and innovation. We work with entreprenuers, rural artists, award winning designers, academia and technology savvy minds to create innovative products and experiences.

We offer usability, interaction & experience design services, workshops and tours around the world to exchange with and leverage the thinking, ideation and execution of various innovative minds across the globe.

Digital Living, User Centric Design, Consumer Electronics, Media Convergence, Automotive Design and Emerging Technologies excite us.

Our greatest passion is Travel and we are exploring the application of design philosophy and principles in highly personalized travel experiences.
