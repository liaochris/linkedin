Mark McIntosh’s Summary

"The Comeback Coach" Mark McIntosh taps into a lifetime of athletics, more than two decades of television experience and a decade of organizational leadership, to deliver heartfelt, humorous and poignant keynotes ideal to kickoff and close your next convention, retreat or sales meeting.

The Missouri native’s expertise is challenging and encouraging companies, organizations, groups, communities and individuals to effectively deal – run to daylight - with the one constant in life: change.

In addition to amusing and inspirational keynotes, “The Comeback Coach" offers a weekly email newsletter "Pep Talk", motivational books, team-building workshops and personal coaching in providing simple and effective strategies to successfully handle life's challenges at home, work and community.

Currently the co-host of KUSA-TV’s “Colorado and Company” www.coloradoandco.com, McIntosh through personal experiences - injury, divorce, corporate downsizing and restructuring - has lived a life characterized by rallying from unexpected personal and professional challenges, earning the "Comeback Coach" reputation.

The father of two shares the “run to daylight” message with others in challenging and encouraging them with specific steps to transform obstacles into opportunities in their pursuit of victory in becoming superior to their former selves.

The Comeback Coach's goal is to honor the mentors of his life who have challenged and encouraged him, in the face of personal and professional adversity, to "run to daylight" in the constant pursuit of worthy goals.
