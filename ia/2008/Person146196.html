Stephen Istvan Agg’s Summary

Istvan Agg is a highly mobile and seasoned technology professional experienced across global markets including the United States, Europe, CIS, India, and China. Istvan spends considerable amount of time in Central-Easter Europe, and is available around the globe.

He is a highly mobile international project and business development manager, experienced in technology, outsourcing, channel partner, and general management with financial P&L responsibility. He has the capacity to work under pressure and the ability to manage global cross-functional teams.

As a Project and Business Development Manager he consulted with: On Line System Ltd a Hungarian high-tech start-up company, where he researched the CEE VOIP market and conducted direct sales activities for its partner - TELES Communications Systems a German equipment manufacturer. He was responsible for product launch of the Regional MPLS Network product for GTS Central European Holding B.V. based in the Netherlands. He supported market-entry and expansion into CEE/CIS territories for Axiom Systems a UK based telecommunications provisioning company. Istvan established a 24/7 Network Operations group at Asylum Telecom, an start-up venture funded by Motorola and other private investors.

He was also employed by Millward Brown a Global Leader in Brand Evaluation and Testing. As a Director of European Operations, he was responsible for P&L, as well as for organizational restructuring and outsourcing initiatives. He held various key positions, while at Motorola during his 13-year tenure in the USA.

Istvan earned his MsCS degree from Illinois Institute of Technology in the USA and a dual executive MBA degree from Purdue University at Lafayette, Indiana, and Tilburg University, The Netherlands; whose executive MBA program earned a global ranking of 12th best by the Financial Times.

Stephen Istvan Agg’s Specialties:

Passionate about serving client needs on international projects and business development assignments, with special interest in managing cross-functional teams with various cultural backgrounds, and languages; as well as managing client expectations and channel parters.
