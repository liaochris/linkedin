Peter Christopher Lau’s Summary

Peter leads talented multidisciplinary teams across a variety of clients while leveraging his background as a creative problem solver and strategic thinker. Peter has over 9 years experience in digital marketing and experience design. He is an active thought leader in the Brazilian digital marketing industry.

Design, technology, business, brand and human needs have never been so intertwined before that we now need people who can synthesize. We need to be able to go deep in certain areas, but also look at things holistically - "design thinking".

The combination of my current Masters program in Business + Design at ESDI in Rio de Janeiro (BusinessWeek Top 50 Business + Design Programs) that focuses on Design Thinking and my current profession, allows me to focus in on the essence of the problem. Having worked in several different industries, I enjoy the challenge of synthesizing my diverse background in taking the inputs, distilling them into something meaningful and articulating and designing them in compelling, meaningful ways.

Peter has spent time as a digital experience designer for clients such as Skol, Nokia Trends, Motorola, Philips, Itau, Terra, Toyota, PlayCenter, MTV, Samsung, Gradiente, Microsoft, Natura, Mitsubishi and Adidas.

Peter's past work experience includes management consulting, investment banking, auditing, software consulting, policy, digital marketing and start-ups for companies such as: Price Waterhouse, Weiss Peck & Greer, KPMG Peat Marwick, President Clinton International Labor Standards Taskforce, MKI Frustum, JP Morgan, DoubleClick, MundoMedia, Unicast, Unica and Garage Interactive.

When Peter isn't working, he enjoys traveling, music, snowboarding, rock climbing, DJ, reading, backpacking, jiu jitsu, kendo, mountain biking, poker, video games, street art, design, toy art, blogging and internet.
