Ryan Dunlavey’s Summary

My educational background is in traditional "analog" illustration and animation techniques, but I am as equally comfortable with a computer mouse as I am with a pencil. I have over a decade of expert hands-on experience with industry standard software including Flash, Photoshop and Illustrator.
You can see my portfolio at ryandartist.com .
