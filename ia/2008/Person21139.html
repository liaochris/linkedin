Ben Bergman’s Summary

Ben Bergman is an accomplished executive who connects clients with creative opportunities and valued assets. He brings both his entrepreneural spirit and quailty public sector expertise to the management of social media non-profit Takes All Types. Ben's extensive background in sales and marketing for early-stage Internet companies drives his recruiting practice at Bond Media Tech. He has worked with several enterprises from inception as well as more established and public companies, and is mindful of the human factor required to support rapid post-capitalization growth. Ben holds a Bachelor's from Georgetown University's School of Foreign Service and attended the University of Chicago toward a Master's in Public Policy. Ben is based in New York City.

Bond Street Group is a full-service staffing and recruiting firm. In the Media & Technology practice, I work in the Digital Media and Web 2.0 space. My clients use cutting-edge technologies to do exciting things with media, entertainment and content. Often these can be early-stage, high-growth opportunities, which require talent with the enthusiasm and persistence to enjoy a culture of rapid change operationally, and by virtue of the dynamic advancements in media technologies and content production.

There is a lot of exceptional talent out there. We at Bond Street Group are part of the New Media community and pride ourselves on our ability to identify those among it with skills applicable to the role, an appropriate industry focus, and a solid culture fit. We have proven efficacy in promoting an opportunity in keeping with a candidate's career trajectory. I strive to be supportive and transparent, and encourage a process that is mutually comprehensive, agile and personalized.

    (Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Online Media industry)

    December 2007 — Present (6 months)

    Takes All Types (TakesAllTypes.org) is a grassroots non-profit company that deploys social media applications in online communities like Facebook, LinkedIn and MySpace that enable users to register themselves as blood donor volunteers. Then when users' local communities needs their contributions to save lives, Takes All Types sends personalized alerts (SMS, FCBK, Phone, FAX and Email) to mobilize the local donors with the required blood type. This is the first centralized, real-time communications network for blood donation. It promotes regular giving and enables an immediate call-to-action in an emergency.
