Florent Stroppa’s Summary

I am currently working as Director of Product Management covering Voxmobili product line worldwide. The product line ranges from J2ME mobile applications to large scale Web applications used by tens of millions of end-users. Voxmobili is the leading provider of online personal data management and synchronisation for the largest mobile operators such as Orange, Vodafone, T-Mobile, at&t and Turkcell. I am currently designing new products around user generated content and integrated with the coming IMS infrastructure. I love leading teams and creating partnerships that make this happen.

I have a true passion for innovation, disrupting technologies and new business models. I really get excited when disruptive products come into the market and change the way people live.

My specialties include:
·Mobile applications and services
·Strong wireless, Internet and telecom technologies expertise on J2EE, IMS and SDP.
·Cross-cultural team management with the ability to independently manage multiple projects
·Product management in highly technical, fast-pace organizations with strong track record in delivering products to the market
·Good relationship manager towards customers, partners, and team members
·Management of strategic alliances and partnership
·Business development
