James O'Brien’s Summary

I started early in political communications, working my way through high school in New Jersey and college in Washington, DC as a political consultant. I launched IMPACT Strategies in 1996 and after 7 years leading the integrated marketing agency, I went client-side in publishing and interactive marketing.

I have worked for clients in 27 states on 183 political campaigns and 41 corporate engagements. I settled in St. Louis and launched a strategic communications consultancy J.O'Brien Communications, Inc.

I am currently the Director of Marketing for LashBack, LLC., the Email Compliance Authority- providers of compliance, reputation and business intelligence services to the email marketing industry.

James O'Brien’s Specialties:

Integrated Marketing: Corporate B2B Marketing, Strategic Communications, Public Relations, Public Affairs and Political Consulting. Interactive Marketing: Online Display Advertising- Rich Media, Search and Email Marketing,
