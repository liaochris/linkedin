Ken Steinberg’s Summary

A thought leader for many private and public corporations providing insight and direction for entry into new markets and/or increased traction in existing entrenchments. The originator of many patents in the areas of computer security, fire suppression, and post-operative wound care giving rise to several new businesses in the Northeast. Provided expertise and professional services for institutions such as the National Science Foundation, Hughes, Hitachi, Softbank, and Digital Equipment Corp.
