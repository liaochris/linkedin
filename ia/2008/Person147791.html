Pauline Crawford’s Summary

Sociology and Statistics BA graduate, Snr Marketing and Product Manager Thompson Holidays 1970-76; Housewife and Mother 1977- 88; Image Consultant in late 80's and President of TFIC (Federation of 200+ members 1992/94); since 1990 ran major programmes across Europe on Communication & Presence across many business sectors e.g. HP Consulting, BT, ICI and Coutts Bank. Founded Corporate Heart 1999 to present time.

Taking my heart into work is vital to me and setting up Corporate Heart in 1999 was for me a new way to resolve the growing dispassion at work - and help deal with major workplace issues such as negative stress, malicious bullying and damaging low morale and the effects these have on success and profits. I designed, developed and researched a new mapping tool for measuring, enhancing and embedding new ways to work; this is now delivered through Corporate Heart consultancy services.

My belief is that many organisations suffer from 'bad' behaviour in business because they lack understanding of the amazing human passion each person could bring to work but often chooses not to! Integrating the 'personal' with the 'professional' has worked in my own life. This integration brings huge benefits to both the individual and the organisation whatever the 'deliverable' is!

By tapping into natural human passion, I wake people up to be their extraordinary best - every moment. My own experience, transforming my life from a shy housewife at home to a dynamic entrepreneur with a national reputation, has shown me the key signposts, routes, choices and actions that are needed. By mapping out my own journey, I now wake people up the journey they can take and the key steps along the way.

My passion and mission is to enable everyone to find their own passion and seamlessly embed that in healthy attitudes and positive behaviour with everyone whom they come into contact with - at home, at work and throughout their life's experiences!

Pauline Crawford’s Specialties:

Professional trainer, motivation speaker and facilitator of personal and corporate transformation; designer of 'Behaviour Mapping' and 'Map-it' - a unique mapping approach to personal and organisational growth; I deliver 'Electrifying Passion' motivation sessions for senior and middle management; facilitate large scale interactive Best Engagement Seminars; and advise top leaders how to ignite their own passion so that others enjoy their lead!
