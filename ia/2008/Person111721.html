Lawrence Page’s Summary

Lawrence Page has been an attorney for 19 years, currently practicing Commercial Litigation, Family Law, and Appellate Law in Austin Texas. He is licensed in Texas and Illinois, and is a member of the Federal Trial Bar.

Prior to opening his own practice, Lawrence worked at several Boutique law firms concentrating in Securities Law, Health Care Law, and Family Law. Lawrence has represented individuals from all ranks of life, small businesses, and large corporate clients such as Dean Witter, Raymond James, Tenet Healthcare Corporation, Iasis Healthcare Corporation, HCA Healthcare, Hospital Partners of America, and Universal Health Services (UHS).

Lawrence was also a prosecutor in Chicago with the Cook County State's Attorney's Office and a Judicial Law Clerk for Justice Robert Steigmann of the Fourth District Illinois Appellate Court.

Lawrence Page’s Specialties:

Lawrence has also served as the lead associate defending a co-Defendant in the NASDAQ Market-Maker Antitrust litigation, one of the largest class action lawsuits ever. Lawrence was also the lead associate in several multi-million dollar broker-raiding arbitrations between securities firms. Lawrence was also the lead associate in a $200 million arbitration between a health insurer and 40 California hospitals owned by a common hospital corporation regarding contractual reimbursement for services.
