Amy Fowler’s Summary

I started my career recruiting for Administrative and Clerical positions with firstPRO, Inc's Marietta office. I really fell in love with this industry while learning the ropes at firstPRO and decided to specialize into IT Recruiting with The Proven Method. Since joining The Proven Method team, I have had the chance to really learn the ins-and-outs of the Atlanta IT Market while recruiting for some of Atlanta's biggest and best companies.

My goal is to become the leading source of Job Placement and Professional Networking information for IT Professionals in Atlanta. In addition to attending numerous networking events throughout the city, I will be launching a new website in 2008: www.AtlantaITnetwork.com! This site will be a combination of job search tips, networking information, career transition resources, and will also feature a e-zine designed to address the changing issues in the Atlanta Job Market.

If you or someone you know is looking for a new IT position, I would love to talk to you! I can be reached via e-mail almost anytime! amy@provenmethod.com

I look forward to helping you in the future!
