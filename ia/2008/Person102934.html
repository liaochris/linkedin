Kate Dean’s Summary

During the last eight years, I have been at the forefront of developing the UK’s leading integrated broadcast and video-on-demand television platforms at HomeChoice and then BT.

I have an accomplished track record in leading the design, development and market launch of PC and television services – including an International EMMY award winning children’s channel.

I was appointed to my current role of Head of Television at BT Vision in 2005 and since that time designed and executed a programming proposition across all TV genres including Drama, Comedy, Lifestyle, Entertainment, Paranormal, Documentaries, Replay and Children's programming. Responsibilities also include developing BT Vision's programming distribution strategy across multiple platforms including the TV, the PC and mobile devices.

I have a degree in Television and Radio from the University of Salford.
