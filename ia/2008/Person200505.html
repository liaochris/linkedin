Winnie Anderson’s Summary

I work with creative / administrative executives and high-level managers who want to stand out from the crowd of candidates. We work together to uncover and market their unique value proposition to their target companies. I design, execute and manage the job search for these busy professionals who don't have the time to run an effective search campaign themselves and who need to keep their search confidential. I love working with professional couples who are open to relocation, who both need to find the right job.

For my top level executive clients I:
-- work with them to uncover their unique value proposition
-- create the resume and other marketing documents
-- conduct real-time interview coaching over the web and / or phone
-- create the job search strategy, conduct and manage the job search

Imagine the reaction when recruiters discover you've hired your own career strategist to manage, support, and advise you during your search!

I have a human resources background with concentrations in recruitment, training, and organizational development. I've worked in a variety of industries including retail, manufacturing, temporary and permanent staffing, education, and casino gaming. I've also worked in the nonprofit sector.

In addition, I've spent 2+ years working as a brand strategist and copywriter with a brand design firm. I understand positioning and brand strategy and it's that knowledge combined with my recruitment experience that allows me to give my clients the edge.

Winnie Anderson’s Specialties:

Interviewing, brand strategy, personal branding, resume writing, cover letter writing, personal marketing strategy, job search strategy.
