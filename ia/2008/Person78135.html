Hugh Byrne’s Summary

Creative, resourceful, and innovative leader, with 15+ years general management and VP-level marketing and sales experience in the media and technology sectors. I have transformed and reinvigorated mature operations, developed new lines of business, and led growth while maintaining high standards of quality and operational efficiencies.
