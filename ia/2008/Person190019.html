Tom Mornini’s Summary

I've been involved with computers since 1978. From 6502 assembly language to Perl on UNIX systems, I've been there and done that.

Lately, I've been contracting for various companies in Ruby on Rails development. I've been actively pursing this for 6 months, am billing regularly, and consider myself at the advanced intermediate level, though I believe that most people would classify me as an expert. The difference is that I know it takes me about 1-2 years to become completely fluent in something like this, and my very complete experience and technical mastery hide what I don't know about Rails specifically. :-)

I'm *not* a Windows fan, though I know a fair amount and can always make it do what I and others want it to do. I *am* a *nix fan in general, and an Apple and FreeBSD fan in particular, though I've very comfortable on all Linux distributions I've come across as well.

My primary goal is to live a fun life that costs me 6% of my invested assets, per year. :-)
