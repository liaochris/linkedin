Dave Clare’s Summary

Personally, I have 19 years experience in both public and private sector industries and has held increasing levels of responsibility including Multiple-Branch Management, Sales Management, Project Manager, and President/CEO.

My studies and experience are in personal growth, goal setting, training and organizational development. My work is with many organizations and industries in developing effective processes that produce measurable results. Personally I have facilitated two “World Clients of the Year” and one “Canadian Client of the Year” for a large organizational development and motivational company.

I spent ten years launching pilot programs and projects in the Banking and Finance industry from Customer Service Centres to Call Centres throughout Australia and Papua New Guinea. Dave was instrumental in designing and developing the first Call Centre Program for PNGBC Inc. He has created training and development programs for various clients, some of which are F&P Manufacturing, Kumi Canada, CIBC Wood Gundy, Clarica, HockShop Canada, H&S Heat Treating and Inco Limited. I am a well respected and goals focused, results driven individual who has a reputation for delivering measurable results that exceed his clients expectations while delivering balance of life.

As an accredited facilitator of two international development organizations I am a well known as a powerful speaker and presenter that has delivered many motivational messages to large groups of business leaders both internationally and locally. Most recently I presented to:

- MTI (Metal Treating Institute) at their spring conference in Hawaii on two topics - The Work/Life Balance and Leadership in the New Economy

- TEM (Tertiary Education Management Association of Australia & New Zealand) at their annual conference in Perth, West Australia on two topics –The Work/Life Balance and Creating Value Innovation in the Education system.

Dave Clare’s Specialties:

Strategic Planning, Capturing the spirit of people, Goal planning, Leadership and Management development, Creative idea development, Public speaking.
