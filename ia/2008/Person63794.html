Mark Fidelman’s Summary

Over twenty years of industry experience founding and managing technology and real estate companies from birth through liquidity event. I have served positions ranging from VP of Business Development or VP World-Wide Sales to VP Sales and Marketing. The companies have ranged in scope from supply chain management and BPO, to media software and internet technology. I have hired professionals in Los Angeles, Silicon Valley, New York, Europe and Asia over the past 10 years, and managed teams from 5 to 100. My strength lies in creating strategy and executing global ventures, start ups, Fortune 500 or companies experiencing change.

Mr. Fidelman is responsible for Corporate marketing and tenant acquisiton for Commercial Townships and Global 3000 Corporate campuses in the Country of India. Fidelman is a Chairman's Circle member of the US India Business Council (USIBC), a founding member of ULI India and a member of TiE.

Mr. Fidelman has worked with world renowned consulting firm AT Kearney as a Regional Director. He managed Supply Chain/BPO related projects for the Global 3000 in India, Asia and Europe. His clients included HSBC, Unilever, Safeway, Cathay Pacific, National Australia Bank and Motorola, among others. He led and directed many business pursuits that led to revenue growth throughout the US, Asia, and India. Mr. Fidelman previously held sales director roles at Citadon and Autodesk providing value-added services to the architecture, engineering and construction marketplace
