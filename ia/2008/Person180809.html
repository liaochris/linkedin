Stephen Lynch’s Summary

I'm the General Manager of RESULTS.com - The global benchmark for business coaching firms - with offices in Canada, China and New Zealand.

(formally known as The Results Group)

My primary role is facilitate our global expansion, and maximise the success of our client firms by ensuring the business coaching service we deliver is at the leading edge of evidence-based, global best business practice.

I am competitive, future-focused, task-driven, and passionate about teaching and inspiring others about 'what really works' in the areas of: leadership and management, strategic planning, marketing, branding, positioning, consumer psychology, communications, sales, and personal development.

I’ve read hundreds of business-related books and regularly attend international business seminars to liaise with the world's best business thinkers.

I was 'Mr. New Zealand' bodybuilding champion in 1993, and continue to dedicate myself to living a fit and healthy life. I eat organic food wherever possible, and enjoy regular weight training, walking, yoga, and meditation.

I live with Bianca Te Rito – the love of my life for the last 12 years.
