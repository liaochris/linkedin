Mark Steele’s Summary

Sales and Business Development executive with 15 plus years of hands-on experience in regional management, customer development and software / high-tech sales management.

Private equity investor in several early stage companies with direct participation in initial revenue growth and business process development. Most recently with Ceryx Inc. a premier provider of on-demand messaging and collaboration solutions and eSSENTIAL Accessibility, an on-demand solution that empowers websites to be fully accessible to individuals with physical disabilities.

Currently working with enterprise companies to help improve online sales/marketing channels by leveraging the ATG application suite. Specific mandate has been to develop and grow ATG’s onDemand (SAAS) line of business in Canada.

Mark Steele’s Specialties:

Sales, eCommerce, Marketing, Sales Management, Business Process Engineering, Growth and Development of Early Stage Companies
