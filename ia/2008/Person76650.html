Hernan Romero’s Summary

Project leader for Integrated Circuit designs with 7 years of experience in the field and about 10 designed products in the market. Authored and co-authored on two US patents: US6995606, US7053674 + 3 more pending.

Designed IC's (Hall effect sensors) at Allegro Microsystems:
A1373/A1374 Programmable chopper stabilized Linear
A114x/118x Two wire Chopper-stabilized programmable switch family
A1425/1662 AC-coupled differential gear tooth sensor
A1171 Micro-power 1.8v omni-polar switch

Also involved in the design of other Allegro's products (GTS):
ATS616 Dynamic Self-Calibrating Peak-Detecting Differential Hall Effect GTS
1656 Chopper-stabilized speed/direction detection GTS

Hernan Romero’s Specialties:

Hall effect sensors. Broad experience on analog and mixed-signal processing, chopper-stabilization technique, etc.
