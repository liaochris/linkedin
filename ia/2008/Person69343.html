Gerry Gouy’s Summary

London-based global (ex-US) Director responsible for reviewing/approving MTV Networks digital deals across every digital platform - online, mobile, iptv etc.

Over twelve years' worldwide digital media commercial, marketing and operational experience (including eight years' mobile and over twelve years' online experience) and detailed understanding of commercial and partnership models across all major digital platforms for: broadcast media; music industry; mobile operators; online; publishers; device manufacturers; and games vendors.

Responsible for digital media deals which have grossed over $100 million in direct revenues, partnering with companies such as Universal Music, Apple iTunes, SonyBMG, Vodafone, EMI, Sony Pictures, Fox Sports, MTV Networks, Warner Music, Yahoo, Microsoft, T-Mobile, Fremantle, CNet, Nokia, Disney, Sky, EMAP, Google, Warner Brothers, BBC & Cricket Australia.

Lengthy experience in multiple European, North American, Asia Pacific & Emerging markets in:

• Structuring, negotiating and executing commercial relationships with music & television partners including record labels, broadcast media, rights holders and aggregators
• Conceiving and negotiating global deals
• Developing and maintaining ongoing relationships with counterparts within partner companies
• Driving the content partnering process, including initial contact with partners, creation
of presentations, proposals, commercial terms, modeling, contract negotiation, final approval by top-level management and ongoing account management
• Development and communication of strategic priorities to internal stakeholders and external decision makers
• Formulation and negotiation of all forms of partnership deals, ranging from content and revenue sharing deals through to equity-based deals
