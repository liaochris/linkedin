Mikael Breinholst’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur with a deep interest in marketing and finance. I love the challenge of starting new businesses, finding the right partners and investors, and making the company grow.

As a business angel I have invested in various technology and software development companies, as an entrepreneur my latest project is Switch-Mobile which is now part of the Via One family (www.viaone.com).

Switch-Mobile is the company behind the successful Globe Dialer solution (www.globedialer.com) that Red Herring calls: "Mobile VoIP, New York Style". Globe Dialer is a small application you download to your mobile phone - and voila, you can now make international calls at super low calling card rates.

I have broad experience in marketing and project management from the telecom and the financial industry. I have served in different capacities such as: Board Member, business development strategist, and managed construction of investment syndicates.
