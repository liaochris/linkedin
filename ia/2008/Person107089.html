    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    January 2008 — Present (10 months)

    As Product Manager I'm responsible for all the Nimbuzz products, the product roadmap and product strategy.

    Nimbuzz is an all in one social communication service which aggregates all major instant messenger networks and social networks in one client. It offers Presence, IM and VoIP to more than 1 million users in 176 countries in the world. Currently it supports more than 600 handsets and is available for S60, Windows Mobile phones and java enabled phones. Nimbuzz is also available as a webclient and a PC client.

    Besides being a client product for consumers, Nimbuzz also integrates all it's services in Social network sites and offers Community, Presence, IM and VoIP to operators as well (including IMS integration).

    Nimbuzz is funded by Mangrove Capital (Skype), MIH Group (QQ.com, Mail.ru, GaduGadu) and Holtzbrink Ventures (StudiVZ, Kyte).
