Scott Johnson’s Summary

Over the past twenty six years, Scott has been responsible for successfully establishing operational frameworks for a number of commercial endeavors in the technology, entertainment, manufacturing, marine, aerospace and not-for-profit sectors. He is well versed in the practices of moving an organization from early-stage concept to full corporate operations, particularly within a technology setting. Scott previously served with both the Canadian Navy and Canadian Airforce where he received his Engineering Technologist ratings in two disciplines.

Currently, Scott has re-entered the high technology arena as Partner, President and CEO of Woodscan Industries Inc., a Victoria, BC-based manufacturer of safety signaling devices and controls for use on a variety of light and heavy industries.

Most recently, Scott provided independent consulting services to a number of technology companies requiring expertise in operational logistics and product management. For example, Scott provided product launch and customer acquisition planning for a local software company who is currently the only licensed provider in the world to handle all transactional data generated by eBay worldwide.

Scott also co-founded and served as the Chief Operating Officer for Madesco Technologies, a developer of helmet-based mobile communications for the int'l first response community. While there, he was the principal architect behind moving the company from concept stage to being selected as a finalist for Best Emerging Technology Company of the Year for 2006. Prior to that role, Scott served as President of TimingPOINT Communications Ltd., a provider of sales, marketing and media management services.

Scott has volunteered as a business mentor within the technology sector and has served on several industry-related advisory boards. In particular, he was instrumental in founding the Vancouver Island Chapter of the AceTECH Academy for Technology CEO’s.

Scott Johnson’s Specialties:

Scott is a passionate technology executive with a strong understanding of evolving practices in corporate operations, product management, investor relations, business development, sales, marketing and communications for emerging, high-growth technology companies.
