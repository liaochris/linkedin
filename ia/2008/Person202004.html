Yelena Chervinsky (f/k/a Kiselgof )’s Summary

I have over 19 years of legal vertical market experience. From 11/2005 until 2007 I was a Compliance Specialist / Project Manager at LegalKEY Technologies where I was responsible for providing consulting and project management services to law firms using LegalKEY solutions. My area of expertise include customizing and automating New Business Intake processes, developing a conflicts database, external sources research, records management, document management, Business Intelligence (BI) Reporting, Interactive Conflicts Resolution (ICR) and knowledge of a multitude of products targeting legal sector. I have over 6 years of law firm experience - I worked in the New York office of Clifford Chance US LLP - and had undergone an extensive training on Conflicts (in the US, UK and Germany regions) & Anti-Money Laundering rules and regulations across various jurisdictions. I worked in the Conflicts Department as a Senior Conflicts Analyst and my responsibilities included performing conflicts checks, analyzing conflicts results data, reporting and providing assistance to attorneys in the conflicts clearance process.

Given my thorough knowledge of the legal vertical market and available solutions and products targeting the back end office, I can be a great resource to law firms of any size. I offer services in structuring and automating New Business Intake processes, in building and training conflicts department, speed training on the conflicts searching techniques and clearance/resolution process, assess and resolve law firm's potential risk management issues arising from Money Laundering, Market Abuse Directive, Lateral Hires, Mergers. Ethically Screened matters and work with Records and Document Management tools.
