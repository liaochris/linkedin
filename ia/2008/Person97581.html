Joseph M.P. De Wilde’s Summary

Over 15 years of experience in the Software & Services industry with Enterprise Software Solutions, Enterprise Software Engineering, Software Integration. Currently Managing Partner and Founder of Convergence Strategies Consulting Group, European Telecom Expense Management leader.
