Pierre-Alexandre Garneau’s Summary

During my 5 years of experience as a programmer and game designer, I shipped 16 games on PC, mobile, set-top boxes, GBA and DS. I started working in gaming as an indie developer, then moved to a small studio working mostly on mobile games where I climbed to the role of design director. I’m now working as a designer at the Montreal office of Gameloft, the #2 mobile game publisher worldwide. Outside work, I have written an article for Gamasutra (“Fourteen Forms of Fun”) which was used as training material at some major studios, and as reference in some academic theses on gaming.

Pierre-Alexandre Garneau’s Specialties:

While I have knowledge of project management and marketing, my main strengths are in game design, programming, analysis (I developed in recent years methods to evaluate a game’s potential based only on its design doc) and saving projects in trouble (I was called in to do so a number of times).
