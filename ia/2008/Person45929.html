David Dorn’s Summary

Mr. Dorn, for the past 20 years, has led efforts that have advanced both the engineering and science associated with space-based focal plane arrays (FPAs) at wavelengths from the UV to the IR. With a background originally in detectors, David has been able to optimize how focal plane packages and electronics can maximize the performance of imaging sensors. David has led teams that have built FPAs for the WFC3, ACS, and STIS instruments aboard the Hubble Space telescope, the NIRCam and NIRSpec instruments aboard the James Webb Space Telescope, in addition to FPAs for planetary instruments including HiRise and Ralph.
