Clark McBride’s Summary

6 years of working in scientific recruiting both in-house HR functions and staffing agency side. An additional 4 years experience in pharmaceutical, medical, environmental and quality assurance laboratory environments in the areas of physiology, genetics and quality control as they relate to scientific discovery, drug development and manufacturing.

Currently I am recruiting roles in G&A, Research, Engineering and Human Resources for LS9, Inc. An exciting biofuels research organization that had participation in the World Economics Forum in Darvos Switzerland earlier this year.

Metabolic Engineering, Microbiologists, Molecular Biologists, Enzymologists, Finance Corporate Analyst, Business Development Managers, HR Associate Director, Downstream Recovery Scientists and Fermentation Engineers are just of the few positions we will be adding on to our team in the first half of 2008 alone.
