Joey Mornin’s Summary

I'm interested in online organizing with new media tools. This takes shape in a range of organizational contexts. In my past life, I was an English major and I'm still primarily interested in the way meaning is conveyed via a variety of media. Now, I've broadened my scope to look at how individuals, organizations, and companies can leverage the tools of new media to add value and achieve their goals more effectively.

For more, please visit my website and blog at http://www.morninj.com. I'm in Seattle, and I love connecting with people doing interesting things in the online (and offline!) world.

I'm also an active environmentalist and non-profit supporter.

I think social media and the Connected Age have the potential to dramatically improve our democracy and our culture, and I'm working to figure out the best way to explore this new space.
