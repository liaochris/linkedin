Hank Marquis’s Summary

I am an IS/IT management strategist and analyst at Enterprise Management Associates. As a Director at a top-tier industry analyst firm, I provide strategic IT management research, industry analysis, and consulting to software vendors and their customers. For vendors I help define corporate positioning and advise on product development, sales and marketing strategies. For customers I perform strategic analyses and recommend solutions to complex organizational, operational, and technological problems. In both cases, I help senior leaders achieve measurable improvements in their organization’s profitability and competitiveness by showing them how best to manage their IS/IT investments.

Visit my blog and listen to my podcasts at http://www.hankmarquis.info/

Hank Marquis’s Specialties:

Strategic analysis and consulting to IS/IT organizations - reorganization, cost controls, competitive advantage from IS/IT. IS/IT strategies for software and Software as a Service (SaaS) vendors - market positioning, product and marketing strategies. In all cases working with senior leaders (CEO, CFO, CIO, VP, Director, etc.) to improve effectiveness of operations.
