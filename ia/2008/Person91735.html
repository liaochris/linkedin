Jim Turley’s Summary

Jim Turley is an acknowledged authority on microprocessor chips and semiconductor technology.

He is founder and principal analyst at Silicon Insider, a technology consulting firm specializing in microprocessors and embedded software, providing technical and financial / market analysis for microprocessors, semiconductor intellectual property (IP), embedded technology, computers, and silicon technology.

Turley is the author of seven books, was Editor-in-Chief of "Embedded Systems Design" (formerly "Embedded Systems Programming") magazine, editor and of the prestigious industry journal "Microprocessor Report," conference chairman of the Embedded Systems Conference (ESC), and is a popular speaker at industry events.

He is frequently quoted in The Wall Street Journal, New York Times, San Jose Mercury News, and appears regularly on television, radio, and Internet broadcasts.

Turley was previously President / CEO of Patriot Scientific Corporation (PTSC), a publicly held microprocessor IP licensing company. His experience also includes senior executive management at ARC International (LSE:ARK), a semiconductor IP corporation, three corporate acquisitions, and a successful IPO.

Jim serves on the Board of Directors and/or the Technical Advisory Board for several high-tech firms in the US and Europe. His consulting clients include investors, semiconductor firms, software companies, and computer OEMs from around the world.

Jim is a popular and sought-after speaker for conferences and seminars. He makes his home near Pebble Beach, California and races his Formula Ford as often as possible.

Jim Turley’s Specialties:

Microprocessors, embedded processors, IP, expert witness, investment analysis, design reviews, presentations, speeches, and keynotes.
