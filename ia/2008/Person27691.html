Brian Pagán’s Summary

My name is Brian, and I am a design-aholic. I believe strongly in a holistic approach to interaction and experience design, as the success of each product element depends on the success of the others. My primary interest is in mobile experience design, and my goal is to someday earn the opportunity to help design mobile technology solutions for the next billion consumers in areas like eastern Europe, South America, or Africa.
