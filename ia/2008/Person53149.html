Don Batsford, Jr.’s Summary

Over 9 years of solid online advertising experience.

Currently, Don is a principal with 31 Media, an online customer acquisition and consulting firm.

Previously, Don was in the business development department at Commission Junction, a ValueClick company. Commission Junction offers a suite of online marketing solutions that drive measurable results on a pay-for-performance basis for 45% of the top 400 e-tailers. Don was responsible for closing business with top industry players including AOL, IAC and CoolSavings.

Before Commission Junction, Don worked for LeadClick Media, launching their eAdvertising network. Don was also the co-founder of AskFor.com, a shopping technology and data management start-up and was at Engage Media, a CMGI company.

Don received his Masters of Business Administration from Clarkson University and Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Saint Michael's College.
