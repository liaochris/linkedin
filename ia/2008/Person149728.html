Peter C. Morris’s Summary

A professional, innovative, delivery focussed and results driven Payments & Settlements I.T. Department and Programme Manager within a variety of banking corporations and service providers/consultancies.

Over twenty five years experience in the area of delivering and integrating banking payments systems (package and bespoke). Extensive experience and success in the definition and delivery of customer focussed payments development programmes, utilising formal development methodologies and project management methods and tools supported by a strong technical and business background.

Peter C. Morris’s Specialties:

The definition, planning and implementation of retail and commercial payments and settlements strategies/systems on behalf of domestic and global financial institutions.

Payments speciality areas include Cards, ATM, ePOS, Payment Gateways, SWIFT, CHAPS, RTGS and STP.
