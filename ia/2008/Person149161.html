Michelle Kunz, CEC, ACC, AELC’s Summary

If you want to maximize your potential and that of your entire team -- and therefore maximize results -- in a highly confidential partnership where passionate engagement, challenging probing and customized action are the norm, PEL Coaching is for you.

We provide a framework in which you explore key aspects of your leadership and teamwork styles. We assess and define strengths, goals and vision/mission, practice new skills, and develop and implement a practical action plan using a highly personalized style designed to uniquely suit your needs and desires.

Our coaching philosophy and methodology are based on the core principles of Power, Energy and Leadership and how they interrelate in effective, empowering, creative, engaging teamwork and leadership. We are passionate about leadership, teamwork and coaching. Our passion shows in our deep commitment to our clients and to our work.

Michelle Kunz has more than fifteen years experience coaching, teaching, managing, facilitating and leading teams of varying sizes and disciplines. She has public speaking experience with groups of all sizes. Her training presentations are engaging and down-to-earth and her ideas are delivered in a high energy style. She is a certified trainer with NetSpeed Learning Solutions and is certified to administer and interpret the Myers-Briggs, FIRO-B and Energy Leadership Inventory assessments.
