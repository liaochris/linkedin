Orlena Yeung’s Summary

I work in the consumer entertainment tech space. Specifically, my experience centers around building global consumer brands through strategy and creative management.

In my last role as Business Director at Fahrenheit 212, I partnered with clients to develop new products to meet their innovation needs. Previously, I started my NYC experience at JDK Design to work on Zune, Merrell, and Pepsi.

Before moving to NYC, I spent 5 years at Xbox, Microsoft. My most notable achievements were launching Xbox 360, a $5 billion/yr business, Xbox Live, the digital entertainment and gaming service, and Halo 2.

With each brand/product launch (5 to-date), I have managed the process from positioning strategy to creative development and execution across marketing mediums.
