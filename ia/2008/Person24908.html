Bob King’s Summary

"20 years of experience in business and product management with companies including Motorola, General Instrument, ACTV and SAIC. At Vulcan, King worked with portfolio companies to identify technology partnerships in the consumer electronic space, and investigated new technologies relating to Broadband, Software and Content Distribution. King has also been active on numerous advisory boards of technology-based companies including co-founding two start-ups."
