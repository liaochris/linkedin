Mariette Johnson Wharton’s Summary

Mariette is responsible for marketing (including branding, advertising, company positioning, public relations, and search engine optimization (SEO), market research and competitive analysis) for Vidtel, the world's first video telephone company.

Mariette has over 10 years of experience in high-tech marketing, Internet commerce strategy, and management consulting. Prior to Vidtel, Mariette provided marketing consulting and strategy to high-tech firms, with a specialization in VoIP technologies.

She spearheaded multi-million dollar e-commerce projects. specializing in website strategy, intranet/extranet development and usability. As a management consultant for Fortune 500 companies, she conducted market research projects and created go-to-market strategies for leading financial service institutions. She is a published author on competitive business practices.

Mariette Johnson Wharton’s Specialties:

VoIP and video telephony, search engine optimization/search engine marketing (SEO/SEM), consumer marketing, competitive analysis, branding, competitive positioning, start-up marketing, alliance management, content development
