  • Cofounder

    IssueDictionary.com (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Political Organization industry)

    June 2007 — January 2008 (8 months)

    The IssueDictionary.com community is a comprehensive and unbiased source of information on United States politics. It exists as a sincere counter to the politics-as-entertainment landscape that we currently get from candidates and the media.

    We don't take money from any political group. Everything on the site is contributed, fact-checked, and approved by the growing IssueDictionary community. We also require citations for every claim, so you can double check everything yourself and learn more. (Or add new citations of your own.)

    Furthermore, we only use first hand sources, such as a video of a politician speaking, an online interview, or voting records. The end result is a source of sincere, unbiased political information. A source you can trust.
