I am a knowledgeable student development professional with multiple years’ experience in higher education, trained professionally in database management, interpersonal communication, supervision, and project management; trained academically in advanced research methods, statistical processes, and critical thinking. I have expertise in:

• Customer Relations

• Strategic Planning
• Relationship Building
• Consultation
• Analytical decision making

MY GOAL is to further the research of successful learning strategies for children and adults. Specifically, I am interested in the non-traditional learner (defined as any learner who struggles in a traditional learning environment). I am also interested in further developing and advocating methods of second language acquisition.

MY BUSINESS, Acquiring Excellence in Second Language Learning, is a scholar-practitioner organization designed to help develop best practices in the English as a Second Language, English Language Learner, Limited English Proficient, and/or transitional bilingual classrooms at either the k-12 or college level classroom; to assist current researchers gather or analyze data; to proofread, edit, or ghostwrite articles prior to submission to academic peer-reviewed or other journals; and to assist current students (of any age) learning English or Spanish with low cost tutoring.

MY DESIRE on LinkedIn is to connect with likeminded individuals, interested in language learning, learning strategies, intrinsic motivation, research, etc, who can help me hone my passion into a successful career.
