A Max Stubbs’s Summary

A successful and experienced Search Sales Manager / Search Consultant with a strong and consistent track record in B2B & B2C sales, marketing, business management & search consultancy.

In addition to my BDM Skills I am a fully fledged SEO and SEM consultant with over 8 years experience in the digital space. I am a consistent over achiever with an infectious enthusiasm for Search Engine Marketing. My outgoing and vibrant personality performs the sales while my knowledge and pedigree of SEO and PPC (SEM) validates my approach.

Our company ethos is different and yet strikingly simple. Whatever we do; it has to deliver profitability & competitive advantages. We're not in the business of paying lip service to search and neither are our clients. We appreciate that all clients have their sights set on the bigger picture, and larger slices of market share. Greenlight help's them achieve these through unique services and methodology.

In today's aggressive market places, it’s not enough to simply do SEO or SEM. Poor investment in average services, will deliver guaranteed mediocrity, and in a world where there is only 1 page that matters, your search strategy has to be ambitious and committed in order to succeed and become an engine of commerce for your organisation.
