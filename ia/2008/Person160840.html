Riikka Komonen’s Summary

MA researcher and R&D Project Manager in the field of smart and functional textiles. I have been working in textiles manufacturing company developing upholstery fabrics for transport area. I have done: Elastic and sound absorbant inner roof material for cabriolet cars; ESD-upholstery fabric for office chair; Optical fibre fabric; Electrically conductive fabrics (example electric circuit).

My MA Thesis subject: "Illuminating Fabric - using optical fibre and electroluminescent cable in a woven fabric".

Expertiese studies at Design Management.

Project Manager in innovation team leader; mutidisciplinary groups in technology, marketing and design. Collaborative approach for New Product Development process.

Teacher in Kymenlaakso University of Applied Sciences. Academic researcher in University of Art and Design Helsinki and Kymenlaakso University of Applied Sciences.

Also studied Design Management and company image branding. Looking forward Doctoral studies in that field of area.

Riikka Komonen’s Specialties:

Knowledge in textiles: Abracion resistance, Fireproofness, Electric conductivity, Acoustic properties, Elasticity in non conventional applications, Colour changing, Illuminating and light reflecting fabrics etc.

Good practice knowledge on how to create New Innovations - New Product Development.

Managing skills very good - knowledge on different methods.

Academic research writing.
