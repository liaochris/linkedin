Christine Lee’s Summary

Christine is a business and market development professional, executive coach, business strategist, executive recruiter, revenue generator for high-growth organizations, and a corporate transactional attorney.

She is experienced in business and market development, sales, and executive recruiting for venture-funded and public companies. Previously, she was a Principal at Vantage Partners, an executive search firm in San Francisco where she worked on sales and marketing roles for bay area public companies and venture-funded security and software startups. Prior to executive search, she was an Alliance Manager for iPass in its pre-IPO days, and provided services to the investor and entrepreneurial community. Prior to this, she was a Channel Marketing Manager for Siemens. She also held product marketing and program management roles at Centigram Communications (now owned by SS8 Networks). Earlier in her career, she was a software engineer at Amdahl. Christine has a B.S. in Computer Science from UC Irvine, and an MBA and J.D. from Santa Clara University.

As an attorney licensed to practice in California, she specializes in corporate transactional work and securities law. She is a member in good standing of the California Bar Association (Bar #249749) and American Bar Associations.
