V.K. Jaitly’s Summary

V K Jaitly did his M.Tech in Sat Comm and B.Tech (Honrs) in ECE both from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur and he worked extensively in the area of Web Marketing for his MBA project.

He got commissioned in the Indian Navy in 1979 and took premature retirement after serving for 22+ years. He was the commissioning crew of INS Viraat and became actively involved in Computerisation and Networking in the Navy with the installation of First LAN onboard Indian Aircraft carrier, INS Viraat way back in 1987.
He had a short stint of 14 months in the education field as the Founder Dean of Jaypee University of Information Technology, Distt Solan. He is on the expert panel of DOEACC. He was Govt and Defence Consultant - India with Cisco Systems and was responsible for business development in the sphere of e-Governance and for meeting the networking requirements of the three Defence Forces in India and SAARC region.
Presently he is heading the Indian Operations of STG as its President. STG is a renowned name in Software Training and providing total Software Solutions.

He has a number of articles to his credit and has delivered lectures/ presentations at National and International Seminars from the forums like CII, ASSOCHAM, AMCHAM, C-DAC, Common Wealth countries Forum for e-Gov, CSI, Cyber Media, Technical/ Management institutes and Defence Officers’ Conferences on diverse topics. Founder member of PanIIT Board India. He loves to trek, plays Golf and devotes his energies for promoting education in the tribal areas.

V.K. Jaitly’s Specialties:

Man magement, Change agent, Relationship Building, Human Networking, Leading Teams of people of any size, a motivator.
Equally comfortable with The President of a State and a rickshaw puller.
