Brandy Brow’s Summary

Author of adult and teen devotionals, articles, creative nonfiction, and essays; compilation book contributor; columnist for the Vermont Observer newspaper's faith column. Has business and nonprofit copy writing experience including scholarship programs and the 2005 Ohio Christian Writers' Conference. Executive Director of Christian Writers' Group International, Inc. (CWGI) and its prayer and critique sub-groups; co-moderator of The Christian Writer's View 2.

Editorial experience includes grammatical and content editing; developing content and writer guidelines; acquiring, editing, and formatting submissions. Experience with newspaper page layout, HTML/CSS and blog design/maintenance; also graphic design work on business cards, advertisements, logos, brochures, inserts, posters, bookmarks, and postcards.

Currently at work on a middle grade novel and several picture book manuscripts.

Brandy Brow’s Specialties:

- writing devotionals, children's fiction and nonfiction, Christian, how-to, informational, and copy writing
- content editing, copy editing, proofing, acquisitions
- administration, managing members and programs, developing programs
- HTML, some CSS, blogging, WYSIWYG layout tools, graphic design, Internet marketing
