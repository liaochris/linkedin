Derek Harp’s Summary

I am a committed serial entrepreneur with a long-term launch platform consisting of Sable Lion Ventures, LLC and The Sable Lion Group, LLC. Sable Lion Ventures invests in founding interests in early stage businesses and The Sable Lion Group - a wholly owned subsidiary of Sable Lion Ventures - enables these early-stage companies by aligning them with necessary experience and resources.

We are focused on changing the world through entrepreneurship. Our mission is to create important and profitable companies. We have a long-term view of harvesting significant value from founding interests in early stage companies, producing generous returns for all stakeholders (founders, team members, investors) and both the local and global communities we live in.
