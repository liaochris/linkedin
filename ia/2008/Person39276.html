Colin McShane’s Summary

As Owner of Orphididies Productions, I provide professionally recorded, mixed, and fully mastered original songs for use in Television, Film, and Commercials. My extensive catalog of original songs are available for licensing of any sort. All songs are mixed in a professional recording studio by engineer Dave Swope and mastered by Grammy Award winning mastering engineer, Joe Palmaccio. With the placement of four songs for recurring themes in the CBS daytime soap opera, Guiding Light, the buzz for Orphididies Productions has now begun.
