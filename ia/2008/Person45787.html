Dave Shaw’s Summary

Network Administrator with a wide range of experience in both Linux, Unix (AIX and Solaris) & Windows. Main focus is configuring and deploying nCipher cryptographic modules for various integrations, & also network administration, improving network infrastructure & security. Have worked with Windows XP, Vista, NT4, 2000 Server/Adv Server, 2003 Server, 2008 Server, OpenBSD, Solaris, AIX, RHEL, SLES, with specific expertise deploying/administering Debian based Linux distributions.

As Technical Support Engineer at nCipher, I have a strong knowledge of all of nCipher's product range. This includes planning, implementation, deployment and Tech Support for SCSI, PCI and Network based HSMs, SSL offload cards, as well as nCipher's Timesource Master Clock and Time Stamp Server. This includes integration with several 3rd party applications, such as Microsoft CA/IIS, IBM WebSphere, Entrust CA and numerous others. I am also well versed in PKI, IAM and have several secondary project based and systems admin duties.
