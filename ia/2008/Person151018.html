Philippe Back’s Summary

Philippe has been working with software projects for more than ten years now since he earned a degree in Civil Engineering in ‘92.
His core strength is the design and delivery of systems that gather information from various sources (databases, mainframes, and transaction processors) and must support pretty high quality standards.
He has a strong background in component architectures and end-to-end information systems modeling. Interoperability between systems is an area of sustained interest for him. In that respect, XML technologies are also on the radar screen.
Able to work with a fairly large set of Internet languages, protocols and tools, he has relevant experience using them on Unix and Windows platforms.
He has extensive background in leading technical teams and fostering collaboration between parties. He has applied his skills in various sectors including european commission projects, Government projects, interactive telephony, security, banking, food retail, telecom, media, insurance, education and automation.
Philippe is also active in the training field, providing UML, Java and J2EE courses in Belgium, Luxembourg and France.

Philippe Back’s Specialties:

RUP, Methodology, J2EE, Team management and leading.

Making things work in a large team producing Component Based Systems / Systems of Systems. This is quite challenging to say the least. I seem to be a pretty good catalyst to align people on the architecture of the system.
When required, I can program pretty well too :-) [Java for now]
