Frederic Cirera’s Summary

Ten years of experience developing high availability Internet services for consumers.

* Strong track record as an architect and developer of web services. Ability to lead a project from the concept phase to the final roll-out. Developed services around social networking, news syndication, and user generated content.

* Expert in email management, including anti-spam, security, and high volume processing.

* Deep knowledge of Unix system architecture, security, and high availability environments.

* Team-oriented, experience in building and managing engineering teams in entrepreneurial settings.
