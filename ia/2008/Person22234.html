Beryl Deskin’s Summary

Effective leadership communications has always been my passion. I founded Deskin Leadership Communications over a decade ago. We've worked with executives in a variety of industries and non-profits including: financial services,health care, women's business development organizations, small business development organizations, etc.

I've been invited to participate and present papers at leadership forums in the Netherlands, Turkey, and Russia. My 6 year old ezine, The Intuitive Leader, has subscribers in nine countries. My articles on ethics and leadership have been published in several countries. Currently our focus at Deskin Leadership Communications is helping women leaders create resilient businesses.

I've served in leadership capacities for several business organizations including: Minnesota Strategic Leadership Forum, Minnesota Facilitators Network, advisor to marketing committee of Women Venture. Membership in other groups includes Business Continuity Planners Association, NAWBO, Women in Communications, ITVA, and PRSA.

I have over 20 years of management experience in corporate communications for Fortune 500 companies. Between those corporate positions I have co-owned and operated a graphic services company and freelanced in corporate communications work. Entrepreneurship is much more rewarding and offers wider experiences to share with others than climbing a corporate ladder.

My career began with one of the first international training companies, where I had access to experienced organizational development research departments and career human resource trainers. Experience with this organization set my course for studying, writing and teaching on a variety of communications and leadership topics.
