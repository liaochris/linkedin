Jonathan David Sloate’s Summary

The New Picasso held a staged reading at New World Stages in 2006 followed by a Concept Album Cast Recording featuring Tony-nominated Robert Cuccioli (Drama Desk and Outer Critics Circle Awards: Jekyll & Hyde, Les Miserables), Felicia Finley (The Wedding Singer, Aida, Smokey Joe's Café), Max von Essen (Dance of the Vampires, Les Miserables, Jesus Christ Superstar), Brooke Sunny Moriber (The Threepenny Opera, The Wild Party, Les Miserables), Christiane Noll (It Ain't Nothin But The Blues, Jekyll & Hyde, Grease), Rodney Hicks (Jesus Christ Superstar, Rent), William Parry (Sunday in the Park..., Gypsy, Camelot), Alton White (The Color Purple, The Lion King, Ragtime, Smokey Joe's Café, The Who's Tommy, Miss Saigon), Joseph Dellger (Ragtime), Tony Freeman (The Lion King), Ashley Amber (Curtains, The Wedding Singer, Aida, The Boy From Oz), music director Jan Rosenberg (Assoc. Conductor: Dirty Rotten Scoundrels, Jekyll & Hyde, How to Succeed…, The Who's Tommy, Starlight Express, Cats), director Sam Scalamoni (Assoc. Dir.: Lestat, Beauty and the Beast), casting by Mungioli Theatricals (The Lion King, Showboat, Ragtime, Fosse, Candide), and several songs recorded by the 59-piece Prague Symphony Orchestra.

Jonathan composed the music for USA Networks' Mortal Kombat: Defenders Of The Realm animated television series in collaboration with Film Roman, and for Warner Bros.' Mortal Kombat Conquest live action television series. Jonathan composed the score for New Line's Mortal Kombat: The Journey Begins, #1 on Billboard magazine's Top Video Sales Charts®. His music has been featured in theatrical trailer campaigns such as Columbia Pictures/Sony Pictures Entertainment Group's Sense And Sensibility, live television events such as the ShoWest Awards, and television commercial spots for companies such as PolyGram Filmed Entertainment, Hasbro, BMG, Nickelodeon and the NBA. Jonathan wrote the main title theme to the Miramax Films/Dimension Films theatrical release of Beowulf.
