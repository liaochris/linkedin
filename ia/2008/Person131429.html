Mick Giles’s Summary

Electronic Arts Inc., incorporated in 1982, develops, markets, publishes and distributes interactive software games that are playable by consumers on devices, such as in-home video game players (such as the Sony PlayStation 1/2/3, Microsoft Xbox and Xbox 360 and Nintendo GameCube and Wii; personal computers; mobile platforms, including handheld video game players (such as the PlayStation Portable (PSP), Nintendo DS and Game Boy Advance) and cellular handsets, and online, over the Internet and other online networks. The Company publishes interactive software games for multiple platforms. EA also makes investments in facilities and equipment that allow the Company to create and edit video and audio recordings that are used in its games.
