Bruce Reichstein’s Summary

Instrumental in organizing and forming a new federally chartered bank which opened for business in January 2007 in Katy Texas (20 miles from downtown Houston). The bank raised 24.8 million in capital in a very short period of time. Today, the bank is growing rapidly and as of the Mid March 2008, has grown to over $100 million in total assets. Westbound has a state of the art online banking system that has been finely tuned since its online banking went live in March of 2007. The main office is located in Katy, there is a branch office in Bellaire and satellite offices in NW Houston and The Woodlands.

  • Partner

    WarehouseLine.com

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Banking industry)

    October 1999 — Present (8 years 8 months)

    WarehouseLine.com was formed in April 1999 to provide funding, brokerage and consultation of warehouse lending services to the mortgage banking industry. The company either directly or through its syndicate of participant banks provides temporary financing on certain one to four single-family residential dwellings, which have been originated. All of the loans are pre-sold in the secondary market to large institutional investors, many of whom are New York Stock Exchange companies. The warehouse line funding covers the approximately 15 to 30 day period between loan closing and the sale of the loan to an institutional investor. The company administers the use of the warehouse lines to its approved client base.

    WarehouseLine.com has vast experience with soliciting and maintaining warehouse lending correspondent relationships, with our primary objective to provide warehouse lending services to mortgage brokers, mortgage bankers, and financial services companies.

By 1997, the company was among Houston's 100 Fastest Growing Small Businesses, achieving the number 21 spot on the list and followed in 1998 by rising ten spots to number 11. In that time, sales growth increased by over 650 percent.

    Texcorp (www.valoans.com) has become one of the larger VA online mortgage originators in the country and has funded over 3.0 billion dollars in VA guaranteed loans. Known for its world-class customer service, Texcorp offers complete VA mortgage banking services with in-house Underwriting, processing, approval and closings. Texcorp is one of a select group of lenders that were granted VA Automatic status from the Department of Veterans Affairs. In addition to VA lending products, Texcorp offers state of the art origination services for conventional mortgage loan products.
