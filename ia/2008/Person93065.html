João Esteves’s Summary

Manager with broad experience in the marketing value chain and strong leadership and project management skills.

Currently at TIM w.e - a leading content, technology and service provider, offering value added services for the Mobile, Web and TV environments - as Regional Marketing Director, managing the company's growth and expansion in Europe.

In March 2006, became CEO of Grupo GCI (a top PR firm based in Lisbon, Portugal), playing a decisive role in the agency’s continued efforts for communication excellence and customer satisfaction, while impacting effectively and positively the business.

In 2001, accepted an international challenge in Denmark as a Marketing Manager at LEGO Company, developing several new product lines in the Innovation Centre (from concept idea phase to launch implementation phase), the lastest being LEGO Exo-Force, a market success.

Started my career in 1999 as an Assistant Brand Manager in Unilever, managing the visibility program of Lipton Ice Tea and assisting in the development and execution of the 2000 and 2001 marketing campaigns.
