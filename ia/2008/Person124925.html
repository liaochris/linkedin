Matthew Harding’s Summary

"A good solution should be simple but focus on the users, not the technology."

Enterprise architect mainly in the SAP space, very specialised in the development (and infrastructure) technologies but still have a good coverage of the functionality. Equally like to be involved in functional and technical design sessions.

Good proven architecture and hands-on skills in most Netweaver and associated enterprise components (XI/PI, Web Dynpro, ABAP, JAVA, Workflow, Portal, BSP, BC, ITS, Basis, Infrastructure, Siebel, Oracle, etc).

Pasionate about design of custom modules (where appropriate) to tie in with SAP modules, leveraging all of Netweaver, and utilising Rational/Agile methodologies to accelerate development timeframes and reduce risks.

In parallel, play a large part in managing and governing outsourced environment from the development, basis and infrastructure perspective.

SOA and other pattern reuse in the Enterprise, streamlined requirements focussed custom development, cross application architecture including visualisation, utility billing and maintenance, supply chain integration, bridging functional experts through common sense, identity management, process modelling.
