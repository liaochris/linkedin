Daniel Hjortholt’s Summary

I've worked in many levels of the computer industry, from computer assembly level to managing. But being a manager doesn't keep me from sweeping the floors to keep my feet on the ground.

I only have a few work rules. To keep my feet on the ground and do work I like and to do it with people I like to work with.

I guess being an entrepreneur has been the most rich experience in my professional life. I like to get my hands dirty to get the job done. It gives me a great view of every level of an organization and in depth view of what is going on, and has to be done to optimize all processes. Give me a banana and I'll turn it into a cake.

The support system became a seperate application called SUPPORTfighter which other major danish companies used as their support management system. The entire system was later sold to another company.
