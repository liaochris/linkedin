Anna Nowobilski-Vasilios’s Summary

Anna Nowobilski-Vasilios Pharm.D., MBA, FASHP, BCNSP is Principal at Anovation, Inc., an interdisciplinary health care consulting firm specializing in the clinical and operations support of the home infusion, home care, specialty pharmacy, and alternate site industries. The company’s goal is to inspire and support health care providers and product manufacturers by assisting in the development, provision, and evaluation of quality, cost effective and profitable health care programs, services, and products.

Prior to establishing Anovation, Nowobilski-Vasilios served OptionCare for 14 years in roles of increasing responsibility, culminating as Senior Vice President of Care Management Services. Previously, she held various management and clinical positions at home infusion companies, home health agencies, and hospitals. She received her B.S. (pharmacy) from the University of Illinois (1979), M.B.A. from the Keller Graduate School (1993), and Pharm.D. from Midwestern University (2000). She is board certified in nutrition support pharmacy, and a fellow of the American Society of Health System Pharmacists. Nowobilski-Vasilios is an active member of ASHP, NHIA, A.S.P.E.N., INS, ACCP, ICHP, the Illinois Chapter of INS, and the Polish American Pharmacists’ Association. She has spoken and published on topics of home infusion therapy, specialty pharmacy, nutrition support, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Anna Nowobilski-Vasilios’s Specialties:

Healthcare consulting, clinical marketing, clinical operations management, healthcare accreditation, professional development, continuing education, and team building.
