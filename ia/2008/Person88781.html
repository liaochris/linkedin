Jennifer Dupper, PHR’s Summary

If you want to work along side talented individuals that you will learn from on a daily basis, take ownership of your business, and drive your career, then contact me!

I started at Parker Staffing Services as a Direct Hire Recruiter, then transitioned into the role of Corporate Recruiter. We are always striving to acquire and retain the best and the brightest employees. I focus on identifying top talent within the staffing and recruiting industry. My other efforts include driving Parker's successful recruiting program and supporting the HR initiatives and corporate goals. I received my PHR certification in May 2007, and have put that education to use as an integral member of Parker’s Organizational Development Committee.

My past position as chairperson for the Recruiting Special Interest Group prepared me to take on my current board role as SIG & Careers Director with the Seattle SHRM. I plan to take my passion for networking and connecting with new people to expand and develop the Special Interest Group program and the Seattle SHRM Careers Center. I look forward to connecting with all members of the Seattle chapter and leveraging those relationships to raise community awareness of what SHRM offers and to increase involvement in the programs.
