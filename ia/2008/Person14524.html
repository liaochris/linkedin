Antonia Haythornthwaite’s Summary

I am a Human Resources consultant who helps business owners to achieve their goals through their employees.

Prior to establishing my consulting business (Blue Dot Limited) I spent ten years in a variety of HR advisory and management roles in the telecommunications and financial services industries, up to Director level in an international company.

I am looking for senior level HR contracting/consulting roles in my key areas of interest, namely the development of company-wide initiatives that are linked to the business strategy and increase employee engagement.

Antonia Haythornthwaite’s Specialties:

I am experienced across all areas of human resources including organisation design and job analysis, recruitment, performance management, change, employment relations, recognition, remuneration and development.
