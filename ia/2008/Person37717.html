Ralph Boethling’s Summary

- I've been helping companies achieve, and often exceed, their operational and financial performance goals through improvements in the way they manage their people, processes and technology for more than 25 years.

- Recognized organizational change agent with expertise in IT-based process change, cultural change, mergers & acquisitions, and restructuring.

- Former CIO with hands-on experience in all IT functional areas.

- Executive-level IT positions in large, complex companies such as Twentieth Century Fox Film Corporation, as well as start-up/early stage companies such as Financial Pacific Insurance Company and Total Pharmaceutical Care.

- Extensive project management office and project portfolio management experience with project budgets of up to $20 million and portfolios in excess of 250 global projects.

- “Big 8” management consulting and practice management (Price Waterhouse) for Fortune 100 companies.

- Broad industry experience including software development, technology, entertainment, healthcare, insurance, consumer products, higher education, government, foodservice, and retail.

- Creative and highly effective communicator. Equally comfortable talking with people in all areas of an organization – in their language.

- Lead editor, author and project manager on Prentice Hall’s best-selling book, CIO Wisdom: Best Practices from Silicon Valley’s Leading IT Experts (2004).

- Owner, Silicon Valley CIO Community of Practice

- Professionally trained voice actor for commercials, animation, video games, and narration.
