Gilbert Paquet’s Summary

Writer, designer and editorial strategist, Gilbert Paquet has been publishing on the web since 1997 (remember Netscape Communicator?). Recognized for his written communication skills, Gilbert specializes on content strategy, standards-compliant CSS and XHTML development, information architecture and content management. His goal? Providing clients with the best possible design & communication solution.

______________________________________

- 11 years of online production experience with (X)HTML, CSS, XML, PHP & MySQL®, including proficiency to hand-code (X)HTML and CSS for cross-browser, cross-platform display
- 5 years experience in Web hosting for small and mid-size organizations, including domain name registration, email hosting, database management, etc.
- Proven record of writing, designing & implementing sites that are scalable, extensible, and maintainable
- Familiarity with content management systems (ExpressionEngine) and database driven websites
- Excellent oral and written communication skills (French)
- Excellent analytical and conceptualization skills
- Good oral and written communication skills (English)
- Good visual design & web usability sensibility
- Proficiency in best practices pertaining to Web ergonomics and accessibility
______________________________________

- Ability to: handle multiple requests, competing priorities in a deadline-oriented environment, manage several projects at once
- End user + content + communication oriented
