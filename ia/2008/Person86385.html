Jim Nelson’s Summary

In my previous life I was a professional graphic designer and expert prepress production specialist with expertise in content development, typography, color use, desktop publishing, layout and publication design. Specialties included retail packaging design, corporate collateral development and large format graphic production. Far too many trade shows under my belt to even begin to describe ...

Together with my wife Rene, I now publish the Full-Time RV Lifestyle blog LiveWorkDream.com. I also publish tripawds.com, the online resource for three-legged dogs and their owners.

And, we just launched RVblogz.com where we provide free travel blogs for full-time RVers and other travelers who want to document their adventures and keep in touch with friends and family while on the road.

I have become quite proficient with Wordpress, have a good understanding of solar power engineering and am an avid MotoSat satellite internet user.

Jim Nelson’s Specialties:

Wordpress blog platform maintenance, RV maintenance, resting and relaxation.

When it comes down to it, I can always rely on my "real work" specialties: Information design; Retail packaging design and comp production; Trade show exhibit design, management, and production; Corporate identity development and collateral design; Bootstrap marketing tactics ...
