Andrew Wayman’s Summary

Andrew's passion for finding and understanding the needs of customers has led him to some diverse experiences, from developing an EMR platform for a network of doctors to architecting the user experience for some of the leading us retailers. This insight with customers -- plus a solid grounding in business analysis and a mastery of experience design methods -- allows Andrew to help organizations define and design more meaningful experiences for their customers.

Andrew has over a decade of experience developing new products, services, and user experiences for the web, desktop, and devices.
