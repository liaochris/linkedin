Alastair Cartwright’s Summary

Online recruitment specialist.

Goals: To drive the Enhance Media business forward and ensure that it remains the market leader in online recruitment communications. Also to establish NORAS as the definitive industry standard for assessing recruitment websites.

Through the engagement of our services, our mission is to enable recruitment professionals to recruit more efficiently and cost effectively through the use of digital technology and online media.

Alastair Cartwright’s Specialties:

Enhance Media has four core areas of expertise.

ONLINE ADVERTISING - Enhancing the employer brand online, filling vacancies and developing a talent pool.
ONLINE RESEARCH - Developing a greater understanding of candidates online, to identify the most effective online recruitment strategies.
STRATEGY - Working on bespoke projects that can span the entire lifecycle of the recruitment process.
TRAINING - Empowering resourcing teams to use the internet as an effective recruitment tool.
