Pritam Narotra’s Summary

ICT professional having 30 years of experience in planning, strategy development, policy formulation& project management etc. & government policy making, its implementation & International Trade issues.

In the Department of Information Technology, I spearheaded 2 important initiatives for 9 years from 1991 – 1999, as CEO, Software Technology Parks of India – mandated to implement the Software Technology Parks scheme of the Government & providing infrastructural facilities including international data connectivity & Satcomm Services India, mandated to supplement VSNL’s data communication services by integrating terrestrial Point-to-Multi-Point radio systems with its International Gateways at Mumbai, Delhi and Chennai. As founder Director of Software Technology Parks of India, I nurtured this institution from 3 centres in 1991 to 40 by 1999.

In addition, during my total stint of about 22 years in the Department of Information Technology, I was actively involved in policy making. I was closely associated with the opening of the Telecommunications equipment manufacturing.

Currently, I am responsible for International Affairs including coordinating & safeguarding India’s interests in International Multilateral Trade programmes like WTO, UN & its other bodies - UNESCO, UNCTAD, UNDP, ESCAP, World Bank etc. I am actively involved in the on-going GATS Services Negotiations.

Prior to joining Department of Information Technology, I served as Manager (Technical) for three years (1982 – 1985) in Punjab Financial Corporation Chandigarh with responsibility of Techno-Financial appraisal of projects for term lending by the Corporation.

I started my career with Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL) – one of the largest Public Sector Undertakings as Engineer (Instrumentation and Controls) for Thermal Power Stations (1976 – 1982). I was responsible for design and development of the Supervisory Instrumentation and Automatic Control systems.

Pritam Narotra’s Specialties:

Project Management, Government Affairs (including serving as company's lead on public policy, enabling new business developments and promoting company's visibility & brand image ), Techno-Financial evaluation for investment decisions, Business Development, Effective Profit Center Head, Branding and Identity & Human Resource Development.

Above all, a Self Starter, Pioneering, Adaptable, Honest, Hardworking and Meticulous worker with Killer Instinct (i.e. no rest till goals are achieved).
