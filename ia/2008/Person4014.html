Agostino Priarolo’s Summary

I’m a 3D artist with skills and experience in modeling subdivision/hard surfaces, next gen pipeline, level design and editing with the UE3 editor. I’m also interested in painting and concept design.
Curiosity, flexibility and creativity are my keywords. I’ve always been an autonomous person but also a passionate team worker.
I love to learn from the other artisans and I love to work with other creative people.

Please feel free to send me career advices and proposals.

Agostino Priarolo’s Specialties:

Advanced 3D modeling and sculpting in 3ds Max, XSI, Maya, Modo, Zbrush and Mudbox.
Good knowledge of anatomy. UV unwrapping. Normal map rendering.
Level design. Very good knowledge and proficiency with the Unreal Engine 3 Editor.
2D concept and digital painting.
