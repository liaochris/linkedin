Tim Carp’s Summary

Print Communications Technologist with 17 years professional production and management experience in the printing industry.

Journey-level knowledge of electronic prepress and assembly techniques for commercial, web, rotary, flexo, screen and digital printing.

Direct mail, list procurement, copywriting, and VDP (variable data printing) coding experience.

I am currently the owner of, Intelligent Data Printing, Inc., a solutions provider offering web-to-print production workflows, consulting and print brokerage services for businesses and retail clients.

Tim Carp’s Specialties:

Digital Prepress, Print Production Consulting, Direct Mail, Print Brokering, VDP (Variable Data Printing), Advertising and Marketing Campaign Analysis, Digital Imaging, and Digital Photography workflows.
