Kirsten (Nelson) Mohan’s Summary

Since 2002 I've been providing innovative marketing for homes, both single and multi-family, primarily in the Puget Sound area. I thrive in troubled markets specifically because my clients appreciate the innovative strategies I use in accomplishing their goals under short timelines.

I provide a full-service marketing package to all of my real estate clients, including (on the selling side) professional photography, staging as appropriate, savvy internet advertising, and professionally designed flyers. For buyers and sellers, I am an expert in contract negotiations - a must to ensure the best possible end result. Buyers appreciate my attentiveness to their tastes - ensuring that the home search is fun instead of frustrating while we find the best of what's available in a given price range. (Please visit my websites to learn more about my client relationship strategies and the full range of services I provide.)

I also direct all marketing campaigns and b2b sales for Bryce Mohan Photography, one of Seattle's premier architectural photography firms. This includes development and implementation of new strategies, both in print and online, as well as in person. Whether the campaign involves trade shows or direct mail, I specialize in opening doors and building relationships to the benefit of all involved.
