Christine Perkett’s Summary

- PR Executive of the Year, American Business Awards; current finalist, Agency Executive of the Year, PRNews.

- Founded PerkettPR in 1998 - day to day contact for a myriad of C-level executives

- Published author and subject: New York Times and WSJ current bestseller, "Will Work From Home," by Tory Johnson, Workplace Contributor on ABC’s Good Morning America, "Inside the Minds:Public Relations Best Practices" by Aspatore Books; "Entrepreneurial Small Business" by McGraw Hill (January 2008).

- Featured speaker on PR, social media & marketing (Join us at The New Marketing Summit in Boston, October 2008: http://tinyurl.com/5prdtb)

- Co-founder & Blogger at This Mommy Gig (http://thismommygig.org) - a group blog and community for working mothers and those who are interested in the issue of balancing work and parenting.

- Blogger for WomenForHire.com, PerkettPRsuasion.com, DamselsInSuccess; guest blogger: PRWeek.

- A no-nonsense, straight shooter who can quickly assess your company's needs and tell you how PR can play a part in meeting your overall business objectives

- Never a "yes woman" but a creative leader who always takes the path least followed - it's way more interesting

- Sits on the Board of the Boston Marketing Association as VP of Programming, as well as the Norwell Visiting Nurses Association.

- My goal is to consistently shatter all the expectations (or lack thereof) the industry has of PR agencies and execute creative, compelling and successful campaigns that continually surprise clients and help them to reach their business goals. I expect to do this while also nurturing a happy, dedicated staff from around the world.

- You can also find me on Facebook, Twitter (@missusP and @PerkettPR), Plurk, Plaxo, Utterz or Pownce!
