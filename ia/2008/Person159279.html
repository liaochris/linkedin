  • Founder/President

    T1pricer.com - Sold to TelWest

    (Privately Held; Myself Only; Telecommunications industry)

    2003 — June 2005 (2 years)

    Founder - T1pricer.com /.net. T1pricer was founded initially as a lead generation website for my group at MCI, it morphed into a lead referral business for different companies after MCI.

    The site was automated to distribute leads to different carriers sorted by NPA/NXX. So essentially, we signed agreements with carriers input their coverage into our database and assigned email addresses of sales reps to those carriers.

    The result - when a company input information about their needs we would take the npa-nxx requirements of the potential customer sort out the carriers that could fulfill the service, then automatically email the sales reps of carriers we represented. The lead would also copy into my CRM and was automatically followed up on with a list of auto-responses to the sales reps about the pending opportunity. If the lead closed T1pricer was paid a fee.

    This site was sold to Tel West upon starting as Director of Sales for an undisclosed sum.
