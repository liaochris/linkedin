Alison Bickford’s Summary

I am principle consultant for Connect Thinking, a proactive and creative e-learning consultancy whose aim is to help enable organisations to best leverage the business and learning benefits of technologies while minimising the obstacles associated with such change.
I have 7 years experience in e-learning strategy, project management and learning design.

Alison Bickford’s Specialties:

Feasibility Studies, Project Management, Learning Design, Web 2.0 Enablement, L&D Practitioner Support.
I have a Master of Arts in eLearning.
I am currently completing a Doctor of Education, thesis title "An examination of changes to work, learning and professional practice associated with implementing a virtual learning system".
