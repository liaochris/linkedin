Jerome McClendon’s Summary

Storytelling is my love. Creating, producing and publishing compelling content that informs, amuses and reaches a vast audience is what motivates me.

During my career I’ve explored many aspects of storytelling including original photography, writing, editing and assigning. I’ve worked with many types of media including print, video and web.

I have extensive experience in writing software requirements and often work with programmers, developers and designers to create quality visual experiences, asset management systems and easy to use publishing tools.

My knowledge of audience behavior and web metrics has enabled me to better serve and target customers resulting in increased page views and time spent on sites.

In the first six months of 2007 my team contributed over four billion page views to AOL after introducing interactive photo galleries. We then turned those page views into increased revenue through the unique use of interstitial and page-refreshed advertising.

My goal is to understand and serve a broad, continuously changing, audience with great content which can be monetized and thus allow continued publication of great stories and information.
