Henning Wibrand’s Summary

Co-founder of Resulture ApS - a software developement company specializing in social and collaborative content and knowledge management and datamining solutions for lifeScience and research.

Co-founder of FlightOps Systems - a SaaS (software as a service) company specializing in operational and quality management in the flight operations industri.

Founder of Wibrand Skilte - a sign company specializing indoor and outdoor visual communuication at malls and supermarkeds.

Founder of Wibrand Grafisk - a designcompany specialized in offline graphics, catalogs and magazines - later on merged with Burvad & Herskind- a fullservice subcontractor of businessgraphics.

+ 20 years of experience and leading possitions with the IT industri in danish and international companies. Covering salesmanagement, marketing, newbusiness developement, product development management consulting, topmanagement and administration.

+ 10 years of marketing consulting and graphic design.

Professional advisor and cofounder at several startups.

I am motivated by the excellent idea, good craftsmanship and a simple rock solid businessplan. I am allways open to new business with these ingredience present. Accompanied by first class design or even art - so much the better.

I am a networker by nature - businesswise as well as private. And the two tend to merge.
