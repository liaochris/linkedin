Evan LaPointe’s Summary

Founder of Marketing Alpha, a consultancy focused on making your online marketing work. After many years of observing the positives and negatives of various web marketing operations, Marketing Alpha was born to solve a host of common issues among some of the web's biggest companies.

Unfortunately, the Internet's importance has slightly outpaced many businesses' ability to adapt and identify the procedures, talent, and relationships necessary to get in front of the curve and create a competitive advantage in this meaningfully different arena. Marketing Alpha seeks to identify your web Nirvana and remove any obstacles that stand in your way.

Key areas of expertise and focus are web analytics, search engine marketing, and actionable reporting. The "yin" to this "yang" is another, equally important focus on acting as a reality check for our clients. In our industry, too many people have been hired and fired on the basis of their ability to generate impossible information, returns, reports, or improvements. This very costly process must end, and education on what is truly possible and probable in this space will ease this pain. Companies can no longer afford to place bets on agencies and employees who make promises they can't keep.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Capital Markets industry)

    Completed quantitative analysis on 500+ publicly traded companies, utilizing cutting-edge equity analysis tools and methods.
