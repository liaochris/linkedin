Kelvin Davis’s Summary

Kelvin has a broad depth of international and domestic business and IT industry experience including, creation of international companies, establishment of procurement and distribution channels, taxation and importation requirements. Business experience stems from a strong industry experiences leading to management of teams in many countries. Assisting many clients with the change management processes ensuring all stakeholders are in a win – win situation. Kelvin provided management of the client outsource engagement and transition of services from current providers to the new support structure.

Kelvin’s IT experience ranges from design, implementation and support of enterprise wide solutions with a focus on long term cost reductions through efficiencies in business processing and cost effective IT solutions ensuring good communications and efficient delivery of services.

Current responsibilities now include operational support for national and international businesses. His experience is of benefit to all clients by overseeing analysis and IT infrastructure designs. Kelvin has extensive experience and a high standard of customer service at all levels of support, managing small and large implementation teams, transitioning solutions into supportable environments ensuring operational staff to meet key performance indicators ensuring client confidence.
