Joy Uttech’s Summary

Trustmark, as a mutual insurance company, has long recognized its responsibility to manage the business in a manner that provides for the highest level of long-term security for the protection of our policyholders. Our pricing and investment practices have followed these policies since the inception of the company. The events of today’s business, social, and technological environment dictate that we apply the same high standards to prepare the company to properly react if an unforeseen calamity causes a significant and extended disruption to our business activities. As such, we needed to develop and implement a Business Continuity program to assure the survival of our businesses in the event of a disaster.

As the Business Continuity Program Coordinator, my main focus for program implementation (and ongoing maintenance) is for the purposes of:
· Protecting our image as a reliable, long-term carrier.
· Minimizing the negative impact a disaster may have on our cash, profit and revenue streams.
· Provide for continuation of reliable, compliant services.
· Perform advance planning on attending to the human welfare needs of our employees during this stressful period.
Prepare plans to perform essential business operations and IT processing at alternate locations.
