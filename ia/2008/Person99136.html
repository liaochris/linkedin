Joseph Shalkoski’s Summary

In my young career, I have taken advantage of many opportunities which will make me a well-rounded asset for this industry. Professionally, my background includes work with Sports Identity, a Boston based athlete marketing and sport event management agency, as well as two years of experience with the University of Massachusetts Athletics Marketing department.

Additionally, four years with the Sport Management Program at the University of Massachusetts at Amherst has provided me with the foundation to be a valuable asset in this industry. Through serving as President of the Association of Student Sport Managers, organizing volunteers for the NCAA Division II Basketball Fan Festival, or volunteering myself for on-campus athletic events, I have taken advantage of many opportunities to create a solid base to build on.

As far as what I personally seek for a career, I would like to someday work with athletes in some regards should it be either at the professional or collegiate level. Through Linkedin.com, I look forward to meeting other sports professionals and advancing my career.
