Scott Willeke’s Summary

Team builder with experience contributing to all aspects of software development. After working with senior management to form a corporate strategy, I help the teams carry that strategy out. Deep programming experience, a strong work ethic, and passion helps me organize and work closely with the implementation teams throughout the organization to implement our strategy. A listener and contributor with all software development roles and a deep, hands-on background in programming, software design, quality assurance, agile team building, and development process implementation. I regularly contribute to debugging and troubleshooting the most elusive bugs and most difficult feature request.

I am interested continuing to organize the teams needed to realize Senior Management's strategy. I love working with effective and transparent agile groups that have shared vision and commitment to constantly deliver business value.
