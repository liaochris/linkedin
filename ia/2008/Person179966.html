Stefan Gutman’s Summary

I oversee the recruitment, evaluation and placement process of Atlanta's most progressive Accounting, Finance and Tax professionals for EDT.
We are a full cycle Executive Search Firm that got its start in Architecture and Engineering about ten years ago. With the constant demand for help from our clients to help them fill financial positions as well; EDT started an Accounting and Finance search division in 2007.


I am also responsible for the continued development and training of experienced Recruiters, as well as new Recruiter training on a national level, supporting offices in Florida, California, Texas and Georgia.

I specialize in taking BIG 4 talent to national, regional and local firms that seek progressive individuals with BIG 4 experience. These firms show their appreciation through promotions, better compensation and developing their employees to be well rounded professionals; not just niche players. I also represent private industry clients in the greater Atlanta area.
