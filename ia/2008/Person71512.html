A senior international MARKETING PROFESSIONAL with a proven track record in maximising brand penetration and business growth across a wide range of media and audiences (with particular focus on the digital and mobile sectors), through strategic excellence, analytical grounding, pioneering approach and creative energy.

Gordon Ellis-Brown’s Specialties:

Gordon Ellis-Brown’s Experience

  • Director of Brand and Customer Journey

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    August 2006 — Present (1 year 11 months)

    Currently working as Director of Brand and Customer Journey for the market leaders in magazine subscriptions acquisition and retention, taking the company beyond traditional subscription offerings to cover a comprehensive range of lifestyle products and services. Responsible for brand development, marketing, creative presentation and the overall customer journey.

  • Marketing Director

    MonsterMob plc

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Broadcast Media industry)

    July 2003 — June 2006 (3 years)

    Meeting the challenges and complexities of an early market, spearheaded the development and beta launch of MonsterMob’s new music channel MobMusic, the first independent full track mobile download service to be launched on 2.5G.Responsibility for marketing, guiding the company through a crucial period of rapid UK growth, aggressive global acquisition and major regulatory changes.

    Pioneered brand creation and development, designed to strongly differentiate from existing music providers, build loyalty and trust, gain maximum leverage of services and firmly position MobMusic as the consumer’s champion in music to mobile.

    Defined marketing and brand strategy to align with the business strategy, to maximise response and revenue and reduce churn, focusing initially on an intensive test period followed by aggressive roll-out to include traditional media, viral, online, direct mail, guerrilla, CRM and partnership.

  • Founder and Creative Director

    Redcat - Redwood Group

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry)

    August 2000 — July 2003 (3 years)

    Founded and launched RedCat, a specialist direct marketing agency owned by Redwood Group, (Europe’s leading publisher of consumer communications, whose clients include Marks & Spencer, Boots, BT and Volvo), to extend business growth and carry Redwood’s editorial creative excellence on to new platforms.

    Recruited, lead and nurtured the maverick creative team, producing measurable, sales-driven campaigns that went above and beyond the traditional formula of picture, copy and price, in ways that challenged customers' perceptions and exceeded client expectations

  • International Marketing Consultant

    L'Oreal, Inmac, Red Herring, Lexington

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    April 1998 — August 2000 (2 years 5 months)

    A period of intense marketing consultancy work, covering senior brand and creative direction for high profile clients in the B2B and B2C sectors. Responsibilities ranged from leading the strategic direction for brand, marketing and creative presentation to managing specific product launches and niche marketing opportunities.

  • Marketing and Brand Consultant

    Carphone Warehouse plc

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    June 1992 — April 1998 (5 years 11 months)

    As part of the original Carphone Warehouse team, Gordon worked alongside MD and founder Charles Dunstone, laying the foundations to position CPW as the leading independent UK retailer of mobile phones and the true ‘consumer’s champion’ in the marketplace.

    Spearheaded the design, launch and development of the pan-European Buyer’s Guide (700K+ print run per month), which rapidly established itself as the mobile industry bible for driving handset sales and accessories, and as a vital support to the sales team

    Acted as brand guardian, developing and maintaining the brand, ensuring that key brand values were adhered to across all customer contact marketing material
