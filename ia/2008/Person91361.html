Okay…here’s a brief summary of who I am and some of my goals:

I am a Husband to my lovely wife Annette, a Dad to my children Julia, Erik, Stephen and Christina thus making me a CED (Chief Executive Dad) which is my most important function in life.

I believe that people are a leader’s greatest asset and I’m dedicated to helping every member of our team to reach for their dreams and change their lives for the better.

One of my personal goals is to help, train and mentor over 1,000 people to become financially free within the next ten years.

If you would like to learn more about me and/or what I do please visit one of my many websites or give me a call and introduce yourself so we can get to know each other a little better.
