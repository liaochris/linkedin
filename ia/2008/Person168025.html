Sam Marshall’s Summary

I've specialised in Intranets and Knowledge Management (KM) for over 12 years, on projects for companies such as Unilever, BT, Rolls-Royce, GSK, PwC and LLoyds Bank.

Until recently, I was responsible for the global implementation of Unilever's portal, replacing over 7000 intranets with a single approach - not just a technology change but a significant shift in how Unilever manages its communication and unstructured information.

I speak internationally on intranets, collaboration and KM including presentations at KM Europe, KM World, Knowledge Summit, Braintrust, KM Asia etc.

Consulting Services Offered:

INTRANET and PORTALS
* Designing an intranet strategy that directly support your organisation's goals and performance metrics

* Intranet Mentoring – I can be part of your Steering Group to offer an independent perspective

* Delivering value from intranets as part of an overall communication and content management strategy

* Global deployment issues – ensuring the right balance between global and local, and dealing with multiple languages

* Governance – ensuring the right support and controls are in place to fit organisational culture and objectives

* User engagement – how to use effective, targeted content to give rich user experience

* Intranet Resourcing – ensuring the right team is in place to sustain a vibrant and scalable intranet

COLLABORATION
* Virtual Teams - work with physically distributed teams to enhance how they work, looking at practices, technology, communication and leadership aspects

* Collaboration Environments - an organisation-wide review of how well team working is supported, including technology and how it fits together, awareness, training, practice and culture.

KNOWLEDGE MANGEMENT
* Knowledge transfer and retention during organisational change such as re-organisations or retirement

* Knowledge elicitation & mapping

* Building and sustaining Communities of Practice
