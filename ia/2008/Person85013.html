Jason Loveless’s Summary

* Group Benefits Services – With over 25 years of experience, this is one of The Reaves Agency's (TRA's) specialties. Group health insurance audit, design, implementation and service; qualified and non-qualified retirement plan design and maintenance. Superior service is our differentiator.

* Business Succession Planning – From funding buy-sell agreements to full scale business continuation consulting TRA can assist based on the needs of the client. Key Man Life & Disability Insurance; Buy-Sell Agreement Funding Flash valuation services, exit strategy coaching, client relationship and knowledge transfer are key tools we employ based on client needs.

* Key Executive Benefit Planning – We assist companies get creative in designing executive bonus/benefit plans. Non-qualified retirement plans; Deferred Compensation Plans, High Limit Disability, High Limit Accidental Death, Kidnap & Randsom, etc.

* Estate Planning – From solving for income replacement needs to high level estate planning needs. We can form a team of experts around the client to satisfy their planning wishes. Able to audit existing trust owned life insurance for goal and cost effectiveness (Life Insurance Audit). Asset Protection Services, Premium Financing, Life Insurance Settlements, etc.
