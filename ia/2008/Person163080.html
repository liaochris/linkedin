Rob Ryan’s Summary

My daily goals are to find meaningful opportunities to provide my senior executive leadership and business development skills to high growth companies. I want to do so by leveraging my relationship building and executive leadership and sales skills developed over the past 20 years in various industries in the US, as well as by leveraging my very well developed global network of executive, investor and government relationships in countries such as China, India, Taiwan, Japan, Singapore and Australia. My experience has proven my ability at being extremely adaptive to new markets and varied industries, with little learning curve or ramp up required.

I am a high energy, people oriented leader and individual achiever who succeeds as a result of an incredibly supportive family and equally motivating network of mentors and associates. The hallmarks of my career are personal passion, self-belief, leadership, personal achievement, ability to coach others, salesmanship and vision for new markets.
