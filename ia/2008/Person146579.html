Pat Fox’s Summary

I have personally developed dozens of courses on the eCollege platform, both for undergraduate students and for faculty. In my capacity as Online Faculty Mentor, I was responsible for overseeing the development of new courses and the revision of courses due to text edition or curriculum changes. Just in the last year that represented over 30 new courses, and the revision of over 30 more. I managed a group of faculty that averaged 50 in number, all from a distance.

I transitioned from teaching in the classroom to teaching online in 2001 while working on my dissertation. I have worked with adult and non-traditional students for about 20 years, and have a deep understanding of the challenges they face. I understand the needs of these students, and have the experience to meet those needs with:
• An enthusiastic approach to the learning process
• An in-depth understanding of online communication dynamics
• The willingness to go the extra mile to ensure satisfaction
• An incredible sense of humor
• The experience it takes to understand the administrative needs to run an online program with over 300 instructors, serving about 20,000 concurrent registrations.
