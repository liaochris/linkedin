Pat Brown’s Summary

Executive Director, Media Logic Systems Ltd. www.medialogic.co.uk providing interactive services, including audio, video and multimedia collaboration between remote locations.

Non Executive Director of Business Adrenalin Ltd., a Management Consultancy Practice.

Patrick, a resourceful and committed Chartered Marketer and Fellow of the Chartered Management Institute, brings to the table a strong background in project and delivery management from within the Business to Business world. From 1995 the Cable and Wireless group of companies afforded Patrick the opportunity to run Global IP programmes ranging from security, architecture, service definition to customer applications with a focus on delivery management.

Product and project roles within Cable and Wireless Communications, Mercury Communications, and Ferranti International, provided a solid track record in management of cross-functional multi-cultural and virtual teams driving both existing and future product solutions to market.

Prior to joining C & W Patrick spent four years with RAM Mobile Data (A Bell South Company) in Business Development and Alliance Management roles forming partnerships with key system integrators and product developers.

A strong engineering project delivery background spanning more than twenty years within IT and communications at Ferranti, Plessey and Muirhead has underpinned sales and marketing strengths both globally and in the UK.
