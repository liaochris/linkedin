Perry Anne Norton’s Summary

My goal is creation of compelling audio and narration with impact for use as a unique and cutting-edge marketing or training tool. Utilizing 20+ years of voiceover, digital editing, engineering and music experience, Panright Productions consults with businesses of all sizes and types worldwide on creative uses of audio to increase revenue as well as retention. Founder speaks at teleconferences and is a freelance writer on voiceover production. One-stop shop for talent, studio, engineer and editor; no location is an obstacle due to the nature of digital technology. Podcasts, ads, audio newsletters, audio books, audio training, voice prompts, educational / children's media, archival material, web promotion, English for speakers of other languages; character voices and dialects. Production done in state-of-the-art studio. Custom demos upon request. Same-day delivery available for smaller projects via email, FTP or overnight CD; file formats include MP3, AIFF and WAV.
