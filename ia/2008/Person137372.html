RELATIONSHIP CAPITAL is being formalized by the Relationship Networking Industry Association (www.RNIA.org). RNIA defines Relationship Capital as "a measurement assigned to people, products and business units based on outcomes of the relationship interactions among them, and the interactions they have within themselves."

RELATIONSHIP CAPITAL will greatly improve relationship networking, and create an entirely new form of wealth. Plainly put, it's a scorecard of positive (and negative) interactions among networkers, and their products and business units.

RELATIONSHIP CAPITAL is is made possible by a seamlessly-connected relationship-networking ecosystem. Networkers will use the ecosystem to automate most networking interactions. We'll collaborate automatically. We'll buy and sell our products and services to one another automatically. And we'll automatically create virtual enterprises and leverage a host of super-advanced features.

RELATIONSHIP CAPITAL will help us all create meaningful relationships free of the kludgy communications technologies we deal with now. It will also let the developers among us create exciting applications that are light years ahead of today's networking services.

To learn more, please visit RNIA's website shown above, and navigate to the CBOK Metrics Group.
