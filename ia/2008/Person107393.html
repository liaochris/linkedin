Kevin L. Jackson’s Summary

Kevin Jackson is a senior information technologist specializing in information technology solutions that meet critical Federal government operational requirements. Most recently, he has been named as the Director, Business Development at Dataline, LLC.

Prior to this position, Kevin Jackson was the VP, Federal Systems at Strategic Computer Solutions, Cryptek Global Solutions Vice President, the WebSphere Everyplace Worldwide Sales Executive at IBM, Vice President Global IT Project Office at JP Morgan Chase, Founder and Principal of ZOCOM, Inc. and CTO at SENTEL Corporation.

Mr. Jackson has been quoted in many periodicals, authored several articles and has presented at numerous venues on computers, speech technology and wireless networking. During his career with the US Navy, he held specialties in Command & Control and Space Systems Engineering while accumuklating over 2500 hours and 230 carrier landing flying the E-2C and C-2A aircraft. He holds a BS in Aerospace Engineering, a MA in National Strategic Studies and a MS in Electrical Engineering (Computer Engineering).
