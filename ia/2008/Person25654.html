Robert Bousquet’s Summary

Robert Bousquet is an award-winning web developer with an eye for design. His projects have been recognized internationally by the American Design Awards and Rails Day. Robert's seminars on web application programming have helped faculty and staff at the University of Southern California, and internal web teams at CBS Television adopt Rails into their organizations. As lead developer for CollectiveX, Robert led the development of a web-based software platform that enables individuals or organizations to easily setup private websites for collaborating and networking within a group. An experienced Ruby on Rails developer, Robert was the winner of the 2005 Rails Day international programming competition. Robert is a founding member and integral part of the New Leaders team.
