Jason Coates’s Summary

Jason Coates, senior communications manager for Yahoo! in Southeast Asia, is responsible for linking Yahoo!’s strategic PR and communications efforts to support the company's business and brand objectives across the region (Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam). His role also extends regional communications initiatives and campaigns into north and south Asia (Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong, India and Australia) markets as well. He works side-by-side with internal stakeholders in marketing, product, mobile, business development, public policy and legal and sales function teams to develop B2B and B2C communications campaigns targeted to press, analysts, government, business and internal employees.

In Singapore, Jason has played a senior role in successfully creating and executing corporate, consumer and B2B-focused campaigns that address key topics and trends including ASEAN strategies, biometrics and smart cards (Gemplus), cross-boarder networking and packet sequencing technology (Peribit), digital convergence (Samsung), the digital home (Samsung, Microsoft, Intel), the digital office (Symbol), gaming (Samsung, Intel, XBOX, Microsoft), PC benchmarking (Intel), RFID (Symbol), security, VPN (Cable & Wireless), WiMAX (Intel, WiMAX Forum), etc.

Previously, Jason was based in Silicon Valley with VCPR where he worked in PR and communications in the Venture Capital-related industry, working with some of the world’s biggest VC firms. At VCPR, he managed PR campaigns based on thought-leadership platforms for corporate VCs as well as the technology-related companies, which consisted mostly of early stage networking-based businesses.

He spent two years in Japan as a participant in the Japanese Exchange and Teaching (“JET”) Program and also worked in sales for the largest winery in the world – Gallo. Jason earned a bachelor’s degree in cultural anthropology from the University of California at Santa Barbara.
