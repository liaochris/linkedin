Bryan Fikes’s Summary

Since college, I have achieved a great deal in sales and marketing. My background in Radio Broadcasting started on-air in South Dakota. From there I moved into the sales arena in 1999 with the then "Amaturo Broadcasting". After a successful account executive career I took my first opportunity for management,taking a position with Aladdin Bail Bonds in 2002. After using a creative marketing approach, I succeeded in obtaining a 74.3% market share of the Northbay region. I moved back into media in 2004 as a consultant with New Generation Media. Traveling to over 35 markets a year, and consulting with clients of Clear Channel, Fisher Broadcasting, Cumulus, CBS, Infinity, Bonneville, my experience with small, medium, and large business excelled, rapidly. Life on the road grew tough, and taking a position as Senior Account Executive with KZST/ KJZY allowed me to get back into local radio. By combining my S.E.O and internet search marketing skills that I learned from my previous experience and by applying my continued knowledge of how the internet worked best for local search results helped me gain a competitive edge over my fellow radio account executives. Flash forwarding to 2008 with my local directory submission skills, SEO for small to medium sized business, and my overall Internet Marketing abilities, has made me a formidable account executive regardless of which market I work in.
