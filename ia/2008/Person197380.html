Vivek Sood’s Summary

Mr. Sood is an accomplished shipping, supply chain, logistics practitioner and consultant. As one of the founding directors of Global Supply Chain Group (www.globalscgroup.com), Mr. Sood has advised Senior Executives and Boards of large international corporations on their global supply chain strategies. Prior to that, Mr. Sood worked for the global top-tier strategy consulting firm Booz, Allen and Hamilton, specialising in Supply Chain Strategies. Mr. Sood was one of the youngest officers to be promoted to positions of very high responsibility in Merchant Marine and has recieved several accolades for his ground breaking supply chain strategy work, academic accomplishments, and pragmatic commercial outcomes.
