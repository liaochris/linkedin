Tatsuya Nakagawa’s Summary

Tatsuya Nakagawa is President and CEO of Atomica Creative Group, a specialized strategic product marketing firm based in Vancouver Canada. He studied at California’s University of San Francisco and is a graduate of Canada’s University of British Columbia with a degree in human kinetics. He has assisted Bridgestone, Hitachi and other global companies in their early stage deployments and has been involved in many successful product launches in North America, Europe and Asia in several industries. His multiple current directorships include the Japan Canada Chamber of Commerce of which he is a founding Director. He is a Director of the British Columbia Supply Chain Network and a member of the British Columbia Export Awards committee. He is also a Director of the Board of Trade for the City of Burnaby, British Columbia that supports its members by promoting, enhancing and facilitating development of trade, commerce and travel.

Atomica Creative Group is a specialized strategic product marketing firm. Through leading edge insight and research, sound strategic planning and effective project management, Atomica helps companies achieve greater success in bringing new products to market and in improving their existing businesses. Our clients engage our experts on an advisory, consultative or collaborative basis to support their product marketing initiatives.
