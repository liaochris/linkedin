Tejinder Singh’s Summary

Tejinder Singh, is a veteran political and business journalist who distils the mountains of EU paperwork into easily digested reports for readers.

Having lived in Switzerland, Germany, Greece, and Belgium, today Tejinder is editor-in-chief for the European Weekly, New Europe, based in Brussels, Belgium.

Doing a tightrope walk with time management, Tejinder also divides his time in lecturing at seminars, working as freelancer for other media outlets and as a media consultant.
