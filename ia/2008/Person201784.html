Gary Yanover’s Summary

A performance driven, innovative initiator with a helicopter view and an eye for details who thinks in concepts and processes and has a strong sense for quality and service. Enjoys complex challenges covering a wide subject area and can build bridges between business and ICT. More than 35 years banking and consulting experience in Business Consultant / Analyst and Process management rolls.

Born in London, England in 1956, Gary Yanover began his career working in the City of London in 1972. In 1978 he completed the required studies and was awarded the Banking Diploma of the Chartered Institute of Bankers. http://www.eubfn.com/cib-con.htm

Gary relocated to The Netherlands in 1979 where he was employed by several financial institutions and an ICT Consulting company. In various rolls he quite often assumed the managerial responsibilities for the department. His “hands on” methods were always appreciated. Gary also took an interest in the required changes in the business processes. These changes were a result of the large scale introduction of ICT applications in the financial world and or changes in legal or regulatory requirements.

With more than 35 years experience it is quite clear that the constant factor in his career is the management of business process changes and the building of bridges between the business and ICT.

For these reasons he has chosen “Working on The Future” as the motto of Nedbrit B.V.

Gary Yanover’s Specialties:

Operational Process Management in the financial world.
Buisiness Process Design / Redesign. Business Process Modelling (BPM)
Banking Operations, Backoffice Administration, Accounting and Control. Project Management.
