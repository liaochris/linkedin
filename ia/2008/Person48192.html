David Collier’s Summary

I am a computer industry veteran with over a decade of tech experience that became an attorney. I work at a litigation firm handling intellectual property and technology law matters. I enjoy the field immensely and look forward to a long career within it.


Publications:

Co-Editor-in-Chief of the American Bar Association, Section of Litigation, Alternative Dispute Resolution Committee’s newsletter “Conflict Management!” since winter of 2005.

Wrote articles in the New York County Lawyers’ Association newsletter “Best Business Practices in the Electronic Age.” (Part I) March 2007 issue, Vol. 3, No. 3, Pg 14 and (Part II) April 2007 issue, Vol. 3, No. 4, Pg 15.

Wrote the CUTPA section of the annual Construction Law Update for 2007.

Researched and wrote articles on Design Professional Liability and Appearing as an Expert Witness in Civil Matters for presentation at 2006 Construction Forum.

Panelist at the Connecticut Building Congress presentation on Talent Retention.
