Vidya Shaker’s Summary

Program Manager

Vidya Shaker, PMP drives the Security Compliance Review Enforcement and Audit Management (SCREAM) Program for Global Service Operations, aligning security and privacy requirements across online services at Microsoft, having previously played roles in Hotmail's monitoring and security programs. In her prior position as Operations PM for Cisco Systems in San Jose, CA, she focused on Compliance, Change and Risk Management as part of the IT Governance rollout in Cisco's Release Management Office. Shaker has years of experience in the IT security field, having worked with government and enterprise customers on areas including: risk & change management, BCP/DRP, application & operations security, and compliance, where she demonstrates expertise.

In addition to nearly 20 years of experience, her sidebar profession in the media "technicolorizes" her technology industry experience. Shaker has been an award-winning television producer and journalist for PBS, CBS, CNN and various magazines such as CRN, Lightwave, HR Executive Magazine, and siliconindia, covering technology and the workforce since the late '80's, and has also been a panelist, speaker and/or host for nationally recognized industry events, like the IT Consulting World Conference in New York/New Jersey, as well as the E-biz Conference in San Jose. Her strong relationship building & communication skills are leveraged in her Project and Program Management roles both professionally and in her voluntary efforts. Shaker currently serves on the Board of the Bay area Agile Project Leadership Network(www.apln.org), and participates actively in PMI (www.pmi.org), as a Director of the PMI Silicon Valley Chapter (www.pmisv.org) Newsletter and upcoming interactive news portal. She recently was a content provider/aurthor and chapter lead for PMI's Program Management Standards 2nd edition, which is scheduled for publication later in the year.
