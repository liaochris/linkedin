Wouter de Vries’s Summary

After my study Modern Dutch Literature at the Rijksuniversiteit Groningen and the Universiteit Antwerpen I moved to Amsterdam to work at publishing houses like Foreign Media Books and Prometheus/Bert Bakker. I have worked at the PR, Marketing and Sales departments and learned a lot about the business of publishing. With these new experiences and with my academic background I decided to start my own business as a Freelance Editor. At this moment I work for publishing houses like J.M. Meulenhoff, Ambo|Anthos, Artemis & Co and Nijgh & Van Ditmar. I am still expanding the list of publishing houses, other companies and individuals who need a a professional editor for their galleyproofs, their annual reports and their literary stories to be faultless and a pleasure to read.
