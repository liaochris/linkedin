Larry Maizlish’s Summary

Outstanding voiceover production and voice services used by TV, Radio, Film, and businesses of all sizes, and individuals. We connect clients with the perfect voices they need from our team of over 2,000 professional voice over artists.

From our fully digital recording studios, we professionally produce:

• Radio / TV Commercials
• Narration
• Corporate Training
• Documentaries
• Internet / Web based Flash and Video Productions
• Telephone greetings / IVR
• On-Hold Messaging
• Audio Books
• Political Spots
• Movie Trailers and Promos
• Podcasts
• And Much More!

Our voice Talents are some of the most experienced and creative in the industry. Whatever type of voice your are looking for, we have it or we can find it. Male or Female, English, Spanish, French, and a list of 20 additional languages.

We make the complicated process of voice Talent selection and audio production quick and easy!

Larry Maizlish’s Specialties:

Connecting clients who are looking for the perfect voice to trained, talented, professional voice-over Talents who can add life and energy to the client's script.
