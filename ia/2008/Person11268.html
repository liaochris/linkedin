Andres Barreto’s Summary

My professional experience has revolved around identifying problems, envisioning solutions and implementing strategies in the social and human side of information technology. From user experience and interaction design, marketing to new venture planning, product development, sales and public relations.

My goal has been and continues to be the empowering of people by not only the access to information but also by enabling the manipulation of that information through collaboration and community building.
