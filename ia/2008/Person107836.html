Kyle Neath’s Summary

I started working with the web in 1997 as a hobby, and haven't been able to stop building new things since. In 2004 I started working in the interactive field professionally doing front-end development. Since then, I've worked with several fortune 500 clients to help define their digital presence through the web and desktop. I've lead small teams of front-end developers to deliver bleeding-edge products under tight deadlines.

My primary passion is front-end development, however through my hobbies I have learned to appreciate the entire experience of digital products -- everything from the marketing, to the copy writing, to the design, to the underlying software architecture. I'm a serial inventor who can't stop learning and building new products.
