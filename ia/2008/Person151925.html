Andrea Avellan’s Summary

I am a Graphic Designer who recently relocated to the Seattle area. While my background for the past several years has been in print, I am also affluent with online mediums and have been responsible for the creation of various web project. I thoroughly enjoy going through the design process with others and relish team dynamics and atmosphere. When I am not making the world safe for good design, I am an actively blogging or gaming on Xbox LIVE.
