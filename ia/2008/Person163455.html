Rodrigo Angelo Ong’s Summary

Licensed Chemist with backgrounds on computational chemistry, geochemistry and food development.

Physician with experience in general and family medicine, medical acupunture with background on Traditional Chinese Medicine. Extensive experience as a team physician of several top amateur and profesional basketball teams. A veteran sports medical director of the annual Powerbar Tour of Matabunkay cycling races and Action and Fitness Duathlon.

Medical consultant on pain management and alternative medicine for Macropharma Corp.

Lecturer on Pain Management, Alternative Medicine and Sports Medicine in medical conventions, RTDs, seminars and conferences. An advocate of preventive sports medicine, nutrition and strength and conditioning programs.

Media Resource expert on pain management, sports medicine, alternative medicine for several radio and television programs including the socio-civic television show, Damayan shown in the national broadcasting network PTV 4.
