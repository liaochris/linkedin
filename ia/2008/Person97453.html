Jose Latour’s Summary

José E. Latour served as a U.S. Diplomatic and Consular Officer before beginning private law practice in the U.S. in 1990, focusing on corporate immigration and labor law compliance. Since then, Jose has assisted hundreds of regional, national, and multinational corporations in establishing multi-jurisdiction compliance programs. His areas of focus include I-9/employment authorization policy, Social Security enforcement, and global staff deployment planning.

José is A/V rated by Martindale-Hubble and has received awards and distinctions throughout his legal career from organizations including Inc. Magazine, Florida Trend Magazine, and Cisco Systems. He is a member of the Society of Human Resource Management (SHRM), the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA), and the Florida Bar.

In addition to his work with I-9 Advantage, Jose serves as General Counsel and co-founder of One World Development Group International, Inc., a non-profit enterprise delivering vector control, disease-prevention, and community education/information services in sub-Saharan Africa.
