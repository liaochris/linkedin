Tim McQuillin’s Summary

Tim brings 16 years of broad marketing and strategy experience in the United States and Central & Eastern Europe, primarily with early-stage companies in the high-growth telecom/IT sector. Experience includes management positions and consulting for: mobile operators, MVNO/MVNE, ILEC, converged telecom services provider.

Tim McQuillin’s Experience

  • Marketing Director-Ukraine

    Sonopia

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Wireless industry)

    July 2007 — April 2008 (10 months)

    Named one of FierceWireless' "Fierce 15" top wireless companies of 2007, Sonopia launched an innovative, integrated mobile/web software platform and affinity MVNO/MVNE approach on the U.S. market that tapped into Web 2.0 social networking, mobile services personalization, and deepening mobile customer segmentation trends.

    In his role Tim wore multiple hats. First, he provided lynchpin management of U.S.-based marketing and Kyiv-based product development and engineering projects. Later, he led the analysis of web marketing campaigns while also developing brand and marketing strategies for Sonopia's MVNO partners. Tim also delivered use-case input for Sonopia's online and mobile product development efforts.

  • Telecom Strategist

    Euro RSCG Worldwide

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 2005 — May 2007 (2 years)

    Euro RSCG Kiev
    Strategy Director-Kyivstar
    Tim developed Kyivstar's first brand positioning and guided strategic direction for its brand communication helping Kyivstar to achieve undisputed brand leadership in all relevant brand metrics. He provided strategic support for creative development of all major brand communication campaigns. He also kept Kyivstar on the leading edge by delivering best-practice case studies of global mobile brand leaders and analyses of consumer, competitive and industry trends both locally and in major international markets. Finally, Tim crafted the corporate vision, mission and values and developed communication strategies for Kyivstar's corporate communications to align its internal and PR communication activities with the approved brand positioning.

    Euro RSCG Prague
    Telecom Convergence Consultant
    Tim provided strategic advice on telecom convergence issues in support of the agency's client, Telefonica O2 Czech Republic.

  • Co-Founder & Consultant

    ROMDEL CIS (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    September 2003 — June 2005 (1 year 10 months)

    Tim co-founded ROMDEL CIS as a marketing and investment consulting company specializing in helping manufacturers take advantage of international trade opportunities between the United States and Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS). We provided turnkey export services from market research and strategic planning, to sales representative services, to export management outsourcing.

    Tim filled a broad role that included sales and marketing consultancy to clients as well as business development.

  • Sr. Manager-National Accounts

    Nielsen Mobile (was Telephia, Inc.)

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Wireless industry)

    July 2000 — May 2004 (3 years 11 months)

    Tim built and managed multi-million-dollar account relationships with 12 major U.S. mobile operators and other mobile-industry related organizations. He delivered actionable and insightful business analyses to clients in the areas of marketing, sales, CRM and network performance. He also led early-stage development and organization of the market intelligence function, whose primary task was to transform a huge amount of data from a variety of sources into useable intelligence for clients.

    Major clients included: T-Mobile USA, Nextel, AT&T Wireless & affiliates, Sprint PCS affiliates, ALLTEL Wireless, Virgin Mobile USA, Qwest Wireless, US Cellular, The Wireless Assoication (CTIA), Yankee Group.

  • Marketing Group Manager

    WellCOM GSM

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Wireless industry)

    1999 — 2000 (1 year)

    Tim led the strategic marketing team for this Korean-Ukrainian joint venture which included the development and execution of strategies in the areas of pricing, advertising and competitive intelligence.

  • Marketing Consultant

    Western NIS Enterprise Fund

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    June 1997 — October 1998 (1 year 5 months)

    Tim was a long-term marketing consultant to one of WestNIS's portfolio companies, a former Ukrainian state enterprise. He wrote the company's first sales and marketing plan, and helped build its first sales and marketing department. He led all aspects of the strategic marketing planning process and transferred that knowledge to the local marketing director.

  • Senior Marketing Analyst

    Windstream (was ALLTEL Publishing)

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Telecommunications industry)
