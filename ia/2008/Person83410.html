Jim Trotter’s Summary

Summary: Senior executive with a proven track record in managing complexity, building productive relationships internally and externally, and implementing strategic vision.

ALLIANCE MANAGEMENT
Built mutually profitable relationships by identifying and implementing “win – win” situations.
• Established and managed alliance relationships between Accenture and key alliance partners (Informatica, NCR Teradata, Dell Computer, and JD Edwards).
• Negotiated contracts for alliance relationship with NCR Teradata and for expanding contract relationship with Informatica in Europe.
• Developed NCR Teradata channel business through Accenture channel from zero to $12 million.
• Increased Informatica sales from less than $2 million to more than $5 million.
• Established actionable alliance business plans and implemented them.

PROJECT & PROGRAM LEADERSHIP
Created a project management culture that makes sense and produced tangible, measurable results.
• Authored Accenture’s project and program management methodologies.
• Implemented project management and program management disciplines in both internal and client situations.
• Reduced project start-up time from 7-9 weeks to 3-4 weeks.
• Established the “vital few” metrics to measure business value for projects.
• On time – on budget (need I say more?).

CHANGE MANAGEMENT
Successful change occurs with the right combination of emphasis on PEOPLE, processes and technology.
• Planned the initiation and growth of Accenture’s Change Management practice from 285 people to 1,600 people over a period of three years.
• Did the front-end analysis and subsequent project plan to reorganize an Eastern European bank from an agency of the government to the private sector.
• Coached executives to deal effectively with organization change issues.
• Designed and authored training programs that met business objectives.
