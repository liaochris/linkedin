Unisource NTC is a retained executive search and human resource consulting firm, founded in Chicago, Illinois in 1990. Operations were relocated to Malibu, California in 1999. Current headquarters are located in Thousand Oaks, California.

Clients continue to use Unisource NTC because we offer an unbundled search process that results in a fail-safe conclusion: a hire. We continuously save our clients thousands of dollars on each search assignment or research solution. Unisource NTC's methodologies have remained virtually unchanged since we started and have resulted in a tracked effectivity ratio of accepted searches to hires at over 95% for the last 18 years. Local, national and International client base.

• Please visit our website for history, methodologies, services and current pricing. We also offer custom solutions for clients.
