Jean-Paul LeDoux’s Summary

Experienced Visual FX 3D artist and Compositor, with a recent turn towards supervising and managing a compositing and FX department. Well suited to the communication, organizaiton and problem-solving demands of a production pipeline. Author of 3D training videos.

Jean-Paul LeDoux’s Specialties:

XSI, After Effects, Photoshop, Maya, Qube, Visual FX, Scripting, Pipeline Development

Jean-Paul LeDoux’s Experience

  • Compositing Supervisor

    Bardel Entertainment

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Animation industry)

    August 2006 — December 2007 (1 year 5 months)

    Managed a crew of 10 Compositors and 5 Support personnel on a 40-episode animated series called Chaotic. My responsibilities included defining the compositing pipeline, communicating and refining the pipeline with other departments, show breakdowns, mentoring junior compositors, developing and optimizing compositing FX, contributing to the development of the tracker software, and generally ensuring the shows were getting out on time.

  • VFX Artist/Senior Compositor

    Nerdcorps Entertainment

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Animation industry)

    April 2005 — August 2006 (1 year 5 months)

    Compositing and FX for Dragon Booster production. Built FX Rigs and mounting scripts, trained staff and prepared documentation for Stormhawks production.

  • VFX/CG Artist

    Northwest Imaging & FX

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    July 1999 — March 2005 (5 years 9 months)

    As a smaller VFX shop, all of us were required to be able to do everything for FX shots. So I modeled, textured, animated, lit, tracked and rendered any shot I worked on. Most of the work was for sci-fi dramas (Stargate, Outerlimits, Andromeda, Dead like Me, The 4400).

    We used Soft 3D, Maya and XSI mainly, with final compositing done by Inferno Artists. I also initiated or contributed to internal projects including the company web page.

  • Commercial Tutorial Author

    3DTutorial.com (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 11-50 employees; E-Learning industry)

    September 2003 — September 2004 (1 year 1 month)

    Produced an 8-hour Video Tutorial product called "Getting Started in XSI". Aimed at a 3D artist(Maya inparticular) looking to make the switch to XSI.
