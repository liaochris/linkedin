Tanja Miller-Nagy’s Summary

My goal is for people to have access to great car deals at fair market prices, purchased smoothly and free of hassle.

My perfect client is a business owner or busy professional who does not have the time nor inclination to spend half a day at a dealership and walk away unsatisfied.

I strive for honesty and integrity to be present throughout the entire car purchasing process. I purchase vehicles for my clients, as opposed to selling them whatever I might have in inventory.
My specialty is making sure the vehicle has structural integrity and is a great buy for my client. I am professionally trained in how to spot paint and previous damage done to vehicles that does not show up on a CARFAX or Autocheck Report.
