Craig Balfour’s Summary

I am currently the unix Systems Administrator for the Department of Computer Science at the University of Cape Town.

I am responsible for all of the unix infrastructural and teaching services in the Department.

The infrastructure includes various FreeBSD and linux servers running web, mail and file services.

The teaching facilities include a number of linux and FreeBSD shell servers and two large student computer laboratories running a mixture of Ubuntu fat and thin clients.
