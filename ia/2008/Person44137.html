Danit Nitzan’s Summary

Identity Management Expert. I use web tools, content tools, experience and expertise to create a distinguished Web presence for my firm's clients. That presence could be reflected in any kind of content that refers to the person, including news, participation in blogs and forums, personal web sites, social media presence, pictures, video, etc.
The process allows you to express your true character, value system, priorities, ambitions, knowledge, experience and motivation, in a consistent and a continuous manner; by using various avenues, platforms and means.
As your Identity Management expert I take you through the various steps necessary to define, construct and use your personal and business public identity.

Ptrviously: A staff member at Haaretz Daily Newspaper for 12 years, and a contributing editor and writer at The Marker Financial Newspaper. Dealt with all aspects of writing and editing, in the Weekend Magazine, the Arts & Leisure section, the Health Magazine and the Op-Ed pages.

My goals are to maximize contact with people, who are always my primary interest and joy, as well as making good use of my understanding of the exact needs of both traditional and modern media outlets; my experience in writing for print magazines and business and lifestyle sections, compiling business profiles and personal overviews: and my talent for writing personal and professional profiles in an authentic and relevant manner.
This will help people represent themselves better and more accurately on public platforms, and help me keep connecting and assisting as much as I can.
