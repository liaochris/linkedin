Arthur De Perthuis’s Summary

With the emergence of internet and low cost airline market, European travel market has moved from traditional tour operators & airlines to a dynamic online environment where price & marketing are key to generate new travel needs and increase loyalty.

Ancillary revenue strategy is one of the leverage to achieve this goal:

By increasing average basket value spent by travellers, this branded service will be a good way to differentiate airline product versus competitors as long as offering new traffic opportunity and frequent travellers loyalty

As one of the first online marketing innovator in the travel industry in Europe, I have helped major European travel actors from traditional airlines/train operators such as Air France, Eurostar to low cost airlines such as Vueling.

I am looking for an online marketing job in the U. S, Southern Europe or emerging markets.
