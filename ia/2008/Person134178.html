Chelsea Rustrum chelsea@rustrum.com’s Summary

I’ve been involved in online marketing for more than a decade, learning search engine optimization, lead generation, and email marketing techniques from the ground up.

I happened upon a lucrative business model when the web was first blossoming, and received mention in the Wall Street Journal, Netscape, and YM magazine, among others.

Throughout college, I worked for several clients that needed assistance in taking their business to the next level. I helped each of them grow their business' by offering my technical skills, business savvy, and online marketing expertise.

Today, I work for Lyris, Inc. in an online marketing role where I have taken on several capacities including: campaign management, optimization and analysis, media buying/planning, and creative and website project management, as well as partner marketing.

I am a self-starter, motivated, and driven by results. I like to make what's possible, happen and what seems impossible, possible. I have chutzpah!
