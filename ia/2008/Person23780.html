Ghose, Biren’s Summary

Formerly COO at one of Asia's largest animation studios UTV Toons – over 700 animators! Worked with broadcasters including Disney, BBC, Teletoon etc and Producers Cinegroupe, Funbag, Decode, Teleimages, Futurikon & Fox. He created an oroginal series for Walt Disney Television in 2002.
Biren was ealier a co founder with UTV of a broadband streaming company in 1999.
Prior to UTV he was at the Corporate Planning function at UB where for 13 years he transitioned across various assignments. UB is today the world's #2 Spirits company. He helped sow the early seeds to take Kingfisher to a global brand.
He also strategised UB's plans in biotechnology and IT & Engineering. He spent 3 years at the global HQ in Dubai, UAE.
He completed an AMP at MIT (Sloan School), USA in Corporate Planning and Management Control.
Biren has been a prolific speaker on convergence, entertainment, animation and new media at a variety of inter-national forums having spoken at Kid Power Asia 2007, Kidscreen Summit 2006 & 2007; NASSCOM Annual Conference [since 2002]; Animation Asia, Singapore [since 2003]; Streaming Media; IT Comdex, E-Biz, Convergence India, etc. He is a trade show “warrior” being a regular at Game Developers Conference; TIGA gaming content market since 2005; MIP/MIPCOM at Cannes [over 14 times!]; E3, American Film Market; NATPE; & MIFA Annecy, France. Judge at Superpitch Asia Animation 2006. Speaker at Shanghai Animation 2007. His programme with the Copenhagen Business School in 2005 on the business of computer games has made him an adviser since 2006. He is the co-founder of the iGITA [Game Industry Trade Association of India] & the Animation Producer’s Association of India [APAI] and was also the “Steering Chair” for “Animation & Gaming” 2006, 2007 and 2008 at NASSCOM – India’s #1 trade body for the IT / technology sector.He is conducting a seminar for leaders in Global Television in Australia in 2007 [November]
