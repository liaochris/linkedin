Travis Wolfe’s Summary

As a mortgage professional, Travis brings a unique and well rounded set of skills to your team of real estate professionals. Since 1997 he has invested in real estate here in the Pacific Northwest. Spending several years as an investor, and several years as a loan signing agent, he now brings his knowledge of the market, and the process, to mortgage originating.

Having a loan officer that is familiar with the needs of both the investor and the homebuyer is crucial here in the fast paced Pacific NW market. Travis brings that knowledge to the table with his personal experiences as a real estate investor.

"I can relate to my clients because I too am active in this wonderful market. I am constantly looking at investment opportunities and almost always have a deal personally in the works. This keeps me in tune with the market and I constantly come across investment opportunities that I can share with my clients" says Travis. "I enjoy passing opportunities on to my clients."
