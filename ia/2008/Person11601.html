Andrew Grill’s Summary

Linkedin member since February 2004 with 1900+ connections. Join my network at http://linkedin.andrewgrill.com

Based in London, Andrew is an experienced Senior Telecoms Executive, Business Developer and Marketer with particular expertise in sourcing and winning significant business across Europe in the mobile operator space. He has a specific focus on mobile advertising, mobile location and fixed mobile convergence. He brings to any role a unique mix of sales director, entrepreneur, marketer, rainmaker, mobile evangelist and company spokesperson.

Since joining Seeker Wireless in 2004, Andrew has secured business with Vodafone through an impressive network of contacts at all levels across multiple OpCos and Vodafone Group. He also has broad range of high level relationships across Telefonica, Orange, T-mobile, Tele2, Telenor, BT, O2, HP, LogicaCMG, Acision, Alcatel-Lucent, Motorola, Nokia, Mobily, Turkcell, MegaFon, MTS, Vimpelcom, STC, Hutchison 3, Google, Yahoo!, Optus, and Telstra. Andrew has developed a strong sales pipeline across these and other customers in challenging and competitive markets.

Andrew is experienced and successful in launching disruptive technologies and business models into existing markets as well as creating new opportunities around convergence and mobile marketing in the Telco space. His career spans 20 years working in senior positions across the entire value chain and has a deep understanding of the Telco market from vendor, operator, analyst, consultant and media viewpoints.

Previous to the current role, he held senior marketing and management positions with Australian operators Telstra, Optus and mobile messaging experts Legion Interactive. He is also experienced in the online space having developed and launched PropertyLook, a leading Australasian commercial property portal.
