Matthew Peters’s Summary

As Zonar Systems Corp's Database Engineer, I provide technical and strategic guidance for optimal use of the company's large telematics databases, re-design feature modules for improved performance and more reliable delivery of business objectives, and introduce best practices for large-scale growth.

As the Database Engineering Team Lead for Haydrian Corp. managed a team of talented engineers through all stages of design, development and production of core analytics and application business processes. Responsible for code review, database build and upgrade implementations, and last-tier technical support for field technicians.

I draw on prior experience as a software development manager producing n-tier JSP-Servlet Intranet and Extranet applications for the distribution industry.

I believe that a company's best investment is in its staff, and that my leadership and experience will help any organization realize the potential of that investment. My extended experience in the distribution and construction industries, supported by my formal design, administration, and management training, prove that I have what it takes to generate critical successes within growth-focused organizations.
