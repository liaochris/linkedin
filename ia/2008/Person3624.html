Eric Williams’s Summary

I have been very active working with information technology as a systems administrator at a Fortune 100 company, and secondly as a pre-sales and post-sales systems engineer for two very strong software companies. I am currently working in the Product Management at Symantec as part of the Server and Storage Management Group. I am currently a Technical Product Manager for the Server Foundation family of products, which focuses on datacenter management and automation under the VERITAS brand at Symantec.
