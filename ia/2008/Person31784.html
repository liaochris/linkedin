Chris Taylor’s Summary

My previous and now limited role was based in the financial market with a small mortgage company. Coming from a sales background I moved into the role of senior consultant which is a more comprehensive role dealing with consulting, financial planning and mortgage structures.

Over five years ago I set up the company Catdynamics.

Over that time we have gained a broad amount of knowledge of all things regarding internet business development. This includes and is not limited to online business startup, web design and development, blog design and development, affiliate marketing, internet marketing, web 2.0 techniques, SEO and online research.

Our current main focus and expertise for clients is in the area's of wordpress blog development, marketing and SEO as well as general SEO.

We also have a number of other related marketing sites of which we source our income through small services and affiliate products.

Our main goal is to become self sufficient through our own sites, affiliate and otherwise. This will allow us to then work with a core group of clients to which we can focus our maximum time, resources and skills available.
