Lenia Pilkonis, CKD, CBD, ASID, CAPS’s Summary

Professionally, I am the owner and principle designer of p3 Interiors, LLC (www.p3interiors.net) . I am a Certified Kitchen and Bath Designer (CKD, CBD), a Registered Interior Designer with the State of Georgia and Alabama (NCIDQ #018296), and a Certified Aging In Place Specialist. I have spent the past 18 years enjoying my craft as an Interior Designer, adult educator at the corporate and college levels, business manager, and Universal Design advocate. My passion is knowledge, as is evidenced by the books I used as mock tables in my house!

My goal right now is to obtain my M.F.A. degree in Interior Design. I have started on that path but have not gotten as far as I would have liked by now. However, better slowly than no movement at all!

Lenia Pilkonis, CKD, CBD, ASID, CAPS’s Specialties:

As a CKD, CBD, and Certified Aging-in-Place Specialist, I really enjoy working with clients to ensure their homes are accessible, adaptable, and provide visit-ability for everyone regardless of their abilities now or as they grow in their homes. I help clients understand how quality design affects the emotional, behavioral, and physical well being of everyone utilizing the space.
