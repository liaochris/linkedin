Gianluca Rodi’s Summary

As a professional with nearly nine years experience in marketing and sales duties strengthened with distribution activities, I am used to working in highly competitive and results oriented multinationals environments. I have gained extensive experience with growing responsibilities thanks to a consistent achievement of service and sales objectives, the ability to emphasize close customer relations and stay in tune with customers’ expectations about value and service in addition to strong team building skills. The ability to come up with creative solutions as well as the proficiency in all sales cycle phases, from lead generation and presentations to negotiation, closing and follow up.
