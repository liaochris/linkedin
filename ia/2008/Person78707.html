Ian Broughall’s Summary

Samian Environmental Solutions is a young, and dynamic environmental consultancy business based in Cambridge, in East Anglia.

Our small and motivated team have extensive experience in Telecommunications, Logistics and Electronic Waste Management.

We have more that 20 years experience in Light and Heavy Engineering, as well as Mobile Telecommunications and WEEE solutions.

For Electronic Waste Management we are able to provide a wide range of independent, practical and professional services to the Producer and Importer, offering valuable advice, guidance and help the 'Producer' understand their risks and obligations and manage compliant WEEE solutions.

Our small team of WEEE Consultants have many years of direct experience in the Electronics Industry and in particular, in areas of e-waste management, logistics and WEEE recycling, environmental sustainability, eco-design and WEEE compliance within Europe, Asia and China.

Our services are aimed at providing effective, compliant Producer solutions to:

* The WEEE Directive
* The RoHS Directive


We are able to provide solutions in the following areas:

* Definition and understanding of the WEEE & RoHS Directives.
* Transposition and implementation in each EU member state.
* Legal and commercial implications for the Producer.
* Household, B2C, B2B and Retail Waste streams.
* Legislative updates.
* Basel Convention and applicable international controls on WEEE/waste shipments.

With our flexibility, and vast experience, we are able to deliver a variety of business solutions to all sizes of organisations.

Whether you are looking for a short term solution or a longer term programme, we are capable of delivering a totally acceptable solution
