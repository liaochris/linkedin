Victor H. Nuila’s Summary

I am a highly effective, results driven top-level executive with extensive experience and a proven record of excellence and success at every step, beginning with my first assignment as an Industrial Engineer over 20 years ago. I earned my MBA with focus on International Business Management in 1987 and have held progressively more responsible positions in Engineering, Manufacturing, Logistics, Supply Chain and Executive Management. Furthermore, I'm a certified Six Sigma Black Belt with extensive knowledge of lean manufacturing business unit practices and principles, process control methodologies and their application across all operational disciplines. I am fluent in Spanish, English, Italian and have lived in Honduras, Spain, Italy, USA, Brasil, Mexico, China, Hong Kong and Belgium.
