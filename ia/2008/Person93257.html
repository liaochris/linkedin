Jodi Marchewitz’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    iGuiders Inc.

    (Internet industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Funded by a grant from the Civic Innovation Lab & The Cleveland Foundation, Jodi started a company dedicated to improving a user's search experience on the Internet. She developed a guided search method to augment Internet search. iGuiders has received follow-on funding from Flashline Partners of Cleveland and will be launching a prototype of the iGuiders site Summer 2008.

    iGuiders™ is an Internet search overlay tool that delivers relevant search results. An iGuider sits on top of any search engine, and acts as a pre-search mechanism to focus a search. It contains a set of topical questions and answers which are designed to guide a user through a search & decision process, and to pinpoint the precise keywords for optimal search results. It provides users the decision criteria to consider before determining what information, products or services they need.
