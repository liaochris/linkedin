Valentina Clerici’s Summary

I worked for the Tour Operators Alpitour SpA and International Suntur as Tourist Assistant in Gran Canaria, Ibiza, Palma de Majorca and Jamaica.
I helped Customers at the airport, in the chosen hotel and during trips, guaranteeing all the assistance and support they needed.
My experiences in the Hotel and Resort industry are different: I started with the position of front desk responsible, I was in charge of billing and the booking office. Over the years I have always worked into commercial department, taking care of statistics, contracting, coordinating groups and travel incentives, as well as inspections from travel agents and Fam trips. I took part in promotion activities such as international fairs of the tourism sector, as well as organizing programs for groups and incentives, treatment of VIP guests, arrange weddings and other events.
Since 2000, I went through a complete shift of expertise; Now I take care of preparation of documents and offers for tenders and contracts, besides order handling, shipping goods, logistics and customer care. I have good capability of issuing export documents such as Eur1, certificates of origin, opening letters of credit. I often replaced the chief of logistics during meetings with shippers and forwarders, negotiating rates of transport (land, sea and air) to the different destinations where we normally export.
