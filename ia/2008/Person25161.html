Bogdana Ivanova’s Summary

I feel great working on the field of software quality assurance. According to some of my colleagues, this is the hopeless profession in the world but I love it :) I love to learn new techniques, development and management methodologies. Some small and simple changes in our everyday work can vastly improve the quality of the applications which we develop. And if I could convince somebody to try those techniques it would be one more victory in the favour of the quality software development.

My main goal is not only to manage a team of QA egineers chasing bugs which are
already injected in the applications but improving the overall development process in order to preventing it from defects injection. I believe that the key for delivering of high quality software on time and on budget is hidden in proactive QA activities.
