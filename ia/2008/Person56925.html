Ed Zschau’s Summary

I provide retained and exclusive contingent search services for frugal entrepreneurs and resourceful investors. My clients create innovative consumer and enterprise products and services with sound business models delivered over the web.

Resourceful investors back frugal, creative entrepreneurs who leverage virtual talent, viral distribution, open source, standard hardware, broadband and user generated content to launch product, generate revenue and prove a low-burn business model quickly. However, frugal entrepreneurs are stuck working with recruiting firms that charge high fees to find executives even though the cost of talent acquisition is dropping.

The advent of resources such as LinkedIn, LinkSV, Facebook, Hoovers, hosted candidate tracking systems and mobility solutions allow me to provide retained search services at 30-50 percent lower cost than traditional retained firms, which the entrepreneur keeps to invest in other parts of the business.

I’ve completed over 60 searches in seven years in executive search.

VP/Director level search for marketing, product management, design, engineering, finance, sales and business development executives
