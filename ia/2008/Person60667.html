Erik Van Enk’s Summary

I'm a highly motivated and experienced director with a solid background in general management (P&L responsible), business development, project management, sales, ICT and human resources. I'm energetic, flexible and a quick learner. I have a proven track record of achieving results in complex projects / environments and organisational change.

During my last role at Getronics my team and I become Microsoft Partner of the Year for 2007 based on our succesfull solution Application Repplatforming. Below the formal statement of Microsoft.

“Getronics created a winning solution that manages the complexity of legacy modernization for large enterprises. Getronics leverages Visual Studio Team System to manage and maintain the applications during and after the migration by providing an integrated software change management platform. Through experience globally with clients, this Microsoft partner has seen how application replatforming can cut running costs by as much as 50% using the Microsoft platform,” said Pierre Liautaud, Vice President, Microsoft Western Europe.

Furthermore I'll organize, from a private perspective, an every year recurring event under the name Toys 4 Boys with six businesspeople to factories of exclusive car brands (Ferrari, Porsche, Maserati, Lamborghini, Spycker, Donkervoort, Jaguar, Lotus, Quvale Mongusta, De Tomaso, Pagani etc).
