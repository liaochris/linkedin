Alton Brooks Parker’s Summary

I have been involved in Film/Video/Television for 10 years and operate my own HD production studio under Lux Visuals, LLC. I provide live video/visual production, post-production, internet deployment, advertising and distribution for international touring bands and off-road racing teams. My clients include:

* The Allman Brothers
* The Doors (21C)
* Norman Motorsports Trophy Truck #8
* Red Bull
* BLUSIRKUT
* and hundreds of others

I am proficient in every major editing platform (FCS2, Sony Vegas, Adobe CS3, AutoDesk) and currently employ a MacBook Pro 2.4Ghz 2GB RAM Duo-Core HD laptop and a dual 2.3 PowerPC G5 desktop, along with 2 PC stations. I am intimately familiar with production pipelines for internet and hard media distribution. I shoot with a Sony Z1 HDV and a Panasonic HVX200 DVCAMPRO HD (P2 Solid-State Technology). I am an expert in shooting from moving vehicles (helicopters, trucks, etc) and harsh environments. . . . My extra skills include high-end internet marketing, PPC advertising, Content Management Systems, PHP & MySQL programming, Flash design, etc...

I am always looking for new opportunities and am flexible for long hours and weekends as well as telecommuting. My video links and project websites are below, please feel free to contact me for an official CV. Thanks for your time, I look forward to hearing from you!
