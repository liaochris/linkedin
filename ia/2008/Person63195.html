Francesco FEDERICO’s Summary

I show businesses how to use social media technologies for external community building and outreach, and for internal collaboration. I am Partner & Project Manager @ GingerCraft, a consulting firm creating crossmedia marketing strategies, leveraging the power of social media, virtual worlds as well as traditional media.

I blog, write articles, and collaborate with many online and offline magazines, among the most popular in Italy.

I also collaborate with PSFK as trend scout, focusing on emerging trends in the mobile industry.

My background is in internet law, project management and telecommunications. I mix my passion for emerging technology with my entrepreneurial abilities, to make things happen and produce innovation.
