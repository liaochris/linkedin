Christopher Adishian’s Summary

Founded by entrepreneur and former investment banker Christopher M. Adishian, Adishian Law Group, P.C., serves elite entrepreneurs and corporate clients operating in California who value our rare mix of high-level quantitative, strategic and creative capabilities.

Adishian Law Group, P.C. focuses on three core areas:

• Corporate;
• Real Estate and
• Employment Law [Plaintiff's side, Senior Executive-level wrongful terminations]

We differentiate ourselves by bringing broad understanding to any one set of facts, providing unique perspectives and delivering trusted legal counsel to our clients' decision making process. Our pitch is basically better, faster, cheaper professional services with more flexibility and more personalized client attention.

Born outside of Chicago and then raised in Massachusetts in his early childhood, Chris has been in California for over 29 years (nearly 15 years in the Bay Area). He regularly donates his time and expertise to charitable causes.

Christopher Adishian’s Specialties:

• Corporate law: Formation, General Counsel, Raising Capital
• Real estate law: Transactions (Finance, Acquisition, Leasing,etc.), Mediation, Arbitration, Litigation
• Labor law: Plaintiff's side, high-stakes, executive-level wrongful termination

Skills:
• Relationships and Introductions
• Strong Quantitative AND Strong Creative
• Complex transactions and deal making
• Strategic Execution
