Tiffany Taylor’s Summary

Tiffany earned her MBA in Business Economics at the George L. Argyros School of Business and Economics. The Argyros School is one of only 555 elite institutions worldwide to be accredited by the AACSB - The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business. Since forming Platinum Funding in 1998, Tiffany has provided leadership and strategic planning for the company. She has over 17 year experience in Residential and Commercial financing.


Tiffany is a top producer and has ranked in the Top 10 amongst residential originators in California for the past five consecutive years. She is considered one of the industry's experts, and is an author of several articles in such industry publications as Mortgage Originator and National Mortgage Broker. She is a sought after consultant and information source for American Banker, National Mortgage News, Origination News, Real Estate Finance and many others.


Her business affiliations include; Mortgage Bankers Association Member, California Association of Mortgage Brokers, National Mortgage Brokers Association, California Association of Realtors, National Association of Realtors, and a Licensed California Real Estate Broker since 1989.
