Bio

Lee shares his life and experiences so others can benefit and grow. Refusing to live as a victim, Lee has overcome a series of challenges and adverse circumstances and turned them into successes. By revealing the details of his own life and by sharing the triumphs of the lives of others, he warms the hearts, stimulates the minds and ignites the will power of people of all ages and backgrounds. His wit, charm, insights and wisdom are expressed in a way that allows audiences and groups to feel instantly connected. Lee is an engaging speaker and presenter who helps individuals link new ideas to real life scenarios. His keynotes and workshops motivate the participants to face life’s challenges with a renewed vigor. His energetic and outgoing style makes him a hit with students, employees and executives. Lee offers dozens of presentations under the following topics:


Overcoming Obstacles/Personal Growth
• “Don’t Trip Unless You Fall Forward” – Strategies for hurdling life’s obstacles while focusing, recognizing and removing distractions on the road to personal and professional success.

African-American History Month
• “Peeking Behind…Peaking Ahead” – Extracting wisdom from the past and using it as fuel for peak performance and tolerance for the present and future.

Student Life/Fraternity/Sorority Life
• “A Real ‘Wanna Be’ Will Be” – The importance of self-development, originality and diversity in social groups and Greek Letter organizations.

Diversity/Leadership Training
• “Diversity Stew” – Learn the secret recipe for satisfying and productive relationships in a multi-cultural environment. This high-energy presentation celebrates and embraces differences and reveals similarities.
