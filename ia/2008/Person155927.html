Raj Hussain’s Summary

Over 10 years proven experience in Executive recruitment (In-House and Services) in Sales, Marketing, CRM, Technology, Consulting and Finance across the UK, Europe, Middle East and Asia. Currently Talent Acquisition Manager for DAMAC (www.damacholding.com & www.damacproperties.com) , a leading privately owned conglomerate headquartered in Dubai and operating across property, manufacturing catering, insurance, investments and education sectors currently expanding property operations into new markets of Morocco, Egypt, Turkey, Qatar, Abu Dhabi, Algeria, Pakistan and Russia and establishing 12 sales offices in the UK.

Previously Head of Practice at Porter Freeman, a leading Executive Recruiter and part of one of the largest recruitment groups in the UK. Founded and ran TrustGo, a niche specialist in the risk management and security sector. Also held recruitment roles as Associate Director at Greythorn and Recruitment Consultant at The PSD Group.
