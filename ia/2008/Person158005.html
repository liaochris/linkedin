Mark Scholz’s Summary

Since 1994, I have had joint careers in clinical research and real estate. The discipline and analytical skills have freely transferred between these two disparate industries, enabling me to be successful in both!

As an epidemiologist and statistician, I have designed, conducted, analyzed, and interpreted clinical trials in a variety of fields. These include substance abuse, public health policy, leukemia, regulatory compliance, and surgical outcomes.

Alongside this, I have managed multi-family apartment properties and generated substantial wealth for the owners. My attention to detail, exceptional sales and leasing success, and long-term planning skills were translated into efficient budgeting and appreciating property values.

Now I have converted this combined experience with detailed investigation and habitual excellence into a winning combination as a Realtor with Keller Williams.

My diverse experience has allowed me to build a successful real estate team that focuses on exceeding the service expectations of my clients.

Many of my clients come from the autosports community who are looking for properties with multiple garages and/or additional shop space for their "toys" and projects.

I am also a professional communications specialist, working in the pharmaceutical industry as a physician speaker coach with Taylor Presentations based in Olympia, Washington.

Within Toastmasters International, I am a Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM), which is the highest possible level of accomplishment.
