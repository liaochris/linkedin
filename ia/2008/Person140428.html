Nick Carano’s Summary

I have grown my career with over 29 years in the inter-related fields of occupational safety, environmental, quality assurance and human resources management. I have over 20 years working as a private consultant, with an additional 3 years performed with the State of Ohio, Bureau Workers' Compensation Div. Safety & Hygiene as a construction/industrial safety consultant specialist. My career also encompasses more than 20 years in the United States Air Force with training and education in jet propulsion, flight engineering, bio-environmental engineering, and loss prevention/safety management. I have recently completed additional education through Villanova University in their Six Sigma, Green/Black/Lean/Masters Certificate program. I have held chair positions with my membership through the American Society of Safety Engineers Ohio-Pennsylvania Chapter, and have belonged to the National safety Council, American Conference of Governmental Hygienists and other organizations.
