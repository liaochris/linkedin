Angela Hill’s Summary

I have over 15 years of design, marketing, and branding experience in St. Louis, San Francisco, and San Diego. I am the president and creative director of my own firm: Incitrio design{brand}media.

We work with existing companies (product and service) looking to re-position themselves for acqusition or exit strategy. Or, companies experiencing exponential growth and looking for ways to maintain that growth level. We also work with new (incubator) companies to establish their brand and position them in the marketplace with a multi-phase strategy.

Our services range from:
branding - strategy and analysis
design for print - identity, mktg collateral, catalogs, annual reports, tradeshow displays, packaging, etc.
web - design & programming, e-commerce, SEO, PPC, html newsletters, etc.
advertising - print: magazines, billboards, etc.

Angela Hill’s Specialties:

branding, graphic design, advertising, marketing, networking, identity, catalog, annual reports, marketing collateral, tradeshow, promotion, tradeshow displays, packaging, point of purchase displays, photo art direction, ad copy, e-commerce, SEO, PPC, electronic marketing, html newsletters
