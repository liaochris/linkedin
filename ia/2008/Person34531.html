Chuck Hildebrandt’s Summary

Senior Marketing Executive with extensive departmental management experience and P&L responsibility. Expert in direct response and brand marketing in online and offline media. Deep knowledge of online marketing metrics and DB-based reporting design, especially relating to advertising, traffic, path analysis, activity, revenue, margin/profitability and customer lifetime value.

My career goal is to achieve a position of leadership responsibility for the online marketing operations of a forward-thinking, data-driven consumer-based organization, whether as a permanent position or in a long-term consultancy role.

Chuck Hildebrandt’s Specialties:

online marketing strategic planning; marketing/media systems conception and development; online advertising and media department management; strategic partnership and media buy negotiation; media and data analysis; advertising test design and implementation; project and plan development and implementation; personnel career development.
