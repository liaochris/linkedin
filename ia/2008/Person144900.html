Todd Mittleman’s Summary

Armed with a Bachelor’s degree in Advertising, I began working as a web designer in mid 1997. My duties included web site design and search engine submission for several local companies.

The following year, I became the Marketing Coordinator for a Fort Lauderdale web design company where I developed online marketing strategies for several local companies including Lens Express, Embassy Suites, Clarion, and many others.

From 1999-2006, I helped shape and build a successful million-dollar internet marketing firm. I led the team in productivity as well as inside sales. There, I worked with hundreds of companies including Miami Children's Hospital, Kennedy Space Center, Rooms To Go, Marriott, and Travelodge to name a few.

In April of 2006, I began employment in Wyndham’s timeshare division. I successfully increased tour volume while cutting spending, which turned the e-commerce department around and exceeded the original forecasted tour goals.
