Ian Gilyeat’s Summary

Ian Gilyeat is a management consultant advising firms on marketing automation, customer performance initiatives, go-to-market strategies and business process improvement. Formerly, Senior Vice President at Direct Alliance Corporation, a leading business process outsourcer, he was an executive member of the IT Steering, Product Management and Operations Committees. He held responsiblity for all marketing services and has developed and executed go-to-market strategies for clients and employers in magazine publishing, software, personal computers, consumer electronics, IT service contracts, printers and commercial foreign exchange services. He has managed and consulted business operations in the United States, Canada, The United Kingdom and Japan.
