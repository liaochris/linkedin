Steve Mann’s Summary

For over 19 years, I have led successful and profitable high-tech strategic organizations and businesses. I am motivated, passionate and dedicated to the success of any organization I am associated with. With entrepreneurial zeal and large enterprise savvy I aim to build high performing teams, set a vision, and develop the strategy and tactics to achieve organizational goals.

As a growth-oriented leader at SAP I develop new business units and enhance the performance of existing teams leveraging deep competence in innovative problem solving, sustainable leadership, social media, customer experience, entrepreneurialism, strategy development and execution and talent acquisition and retention.

These teams delivered significant incremental revenue and customer demand. As a creative business strategist, change agent and corporate turnaround specialist, my talents lie in the ability to develop solid executable strategies in both the B2B and B2C spaces as well as in driving execution of those strategies.

My focus is on profitable growth through leadership, customer-centricity and development of product, process and institutional innovations. As a result of my efforts organizations are better able to defend their core markets while making inroads into competitive positions and developing adjacent market opportunities.
