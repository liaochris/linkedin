Jonah Jones’s Summary

I do Automotive eCommerce Consulting to help Car Dealerships set up their Internet Dept and strategies, or to take their existing Internet Sales Department/Strategy to the next level.

Also Vice President of Bus Dev for Laser Stream Video, a video production company based in Metro Atlanta, GA, with a passion for providing clients powerful, yet inexpensive ways to market to current and potential customers, update their web sites with current technology, increase their interactivity and production, and ultimately increase revenues with our portfolio of web-based video services.

Previously I was a top E-Commerce Director in the Automotive Industry w/ years of hands-on experience and some of the best results in the country. I started selling cars when I left the Marine Corp in 1996... I became interested in the Internet Sales aspect of the business and went from that one Honda Store to a small group to the Western Region E-Commerce Director for Asbury Automotive before I left to do consulting and join LaserStreamVideo.

LaserStreamVideo's clientele currently includes boat dealers, credit unions, mortgage companies, lasik surgeons, motivational speakers, real estate agents/brokers and with the primary focus in the automobile dealership vertical. Any business with a web site that has a product or service to sell or market is a potential client. We offer website spokesmodels to promote products and services, direct visitors to specific areas of the site, or create a call-to-action.

We also provide a patent protected re-marketing tool that invites viewers back to the client’s website for a second look after having gone to a competing site, in addition to video emails, video lead confirmations, and a video credit application, we have more creative ideas awaiting development.
