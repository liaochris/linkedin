Kathy Sweeney, NCRW, CPRW, CEIC, CCM’s Summary

Kathy Sweeney, Nationally Certified Resume Writer, Certified Professional Resume Writer, Certified Employment Interview Consultant, and Credentialed Career Master started The Write Resume in 1987. She has helped thousands of clients secure interviews through effective preparation of targeted resumes and career search documents. Ms. Sweeney spent six years as a Hiring Manager with two Fortune 500 corporations prior to owning her business.

Ms. Sweeney holds four credentials which show her expertise in resume writing, career management, and interview coaching.

Nationally Certified Resume Writer - 1 of 26 resume writers world-wide to hold this designation.

Certified Professional Resume Writer - Awarded in 1995; one of the first to hold this designation.

Certified Employment Interview Consultant - 1 of 5 career services practitioners in the world to hold this important credential.

Credentialed Career Master - 1 of 45 Career Services Professionals world-wide to hold this prestigious credential.
