Peter Sieber’s Summary

My career in marketing and sales started at Procter & Gamble, Geneva, followed by an increasing responsibility in areas such as product and project management with L’Oréal, Unilever, Sanofi, followed by almost 10 years of world-wide sales and consulting with DHL International, and with Elizabeth Arden for prestige brands in travel retail as well as with local distributors.
Over the last two years, I acted as Sales and Marketing Manager to set up, and roll out the products of a small international luxury company in Europe and the Middle East. Accounts Director for Spirit Advertising for strategic accounts, such as Nespresso, Audmears Piguet, P&G
