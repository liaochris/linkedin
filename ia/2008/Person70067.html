Gilli Moon’s Summary

Born in Milan Italy, and raised in the wilderness "bush" just north of Sydney, Australia, Gilli Moon is a force to be reckoned with in the music industry. She is a singer, songwriter, recording artist, pianist, actor, producer, author, painter, motivator, entrepreneur, visionary and empowering community builder, and like her company name, she is truly a Warrior Girl. Her restless creativity and rebellious nature continually urge the artist to push the envelope in the studio, on stage, in business and with whatever she touches.

Gilli (pronounced as with a "J") is an illustrious, energetic poly-media artist, with multifaceted albums and dynamic live shows that have touched music lovers worldwide garnering high praise from the most jaded of critics. For the past 10 years she has been one of Australia's most influencial and iconic figures, forging an independent path before anyone knew what "indie" was. She has produced and released 4 studio music albums of her original music, to critical acclaim, winning songwriting and artist awards, performing across the world many times, and landing songs in film and television.

gilli moon is the quintessential renaissance woman, with a ceaseless contribution to the creative and artist community at large. She has received numerous press for her courage, power and dynamic energy as an artist and inspiration for other artistsand is constantly featured in the media, online, and radio with her music and outstanding achievements. Just google her!

She is the CEO of her own record company and production house Warrior Girl Music and co-founded Songsalive!, in 1997, and is now the largest internationally based non-profit songwriters organization.

Gilli has written a motivational book for artists of all genres called "I AM A Professional Artist".

Read the expanded bio at www.warriorgirlmusic.com/gillimoon
