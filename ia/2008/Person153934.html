Ann Marie Conway’s Summary

Served as a consultant for various organizations, in the roles of Proposal Manager, Project Manager, Technical/Medical Writer, Technical/Medical Editor, and Process Engineer. Worked with Federal Agencies such as the Centers for Medicare&Medicaid Systems (CMS), the Centers for Substance Abuse Treatment (CSAT), and the Centers for Substance Abuse Prevention (CSAP). Worked on projects as diverse as the development of IT system requirements and systems design; the creation of master project schedules for multiple divisions; the creation and analysis of contractual performance metrics; the creation of Service Level Agreements (SLAs), and the review of contract deliverables. Have created a number of documents, ranging from multi-million government proposals, to user manuals, policies and procedures, business process models, and contract deliverables. Am considered an excellent researcher, writer and editor, with the ability to describe complex processes in language easily understood by a layperson. Excellent grasp of medical terminology and concepts.
