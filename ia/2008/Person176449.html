Simon H. Leong’s Summary

Simon is a dual degree MBA/Master of Real Estate Development student focusing on international real estate investment and development. Prior to USC, he was a Research and Financial Analyst for Grubb & Ellis Company. Simon spearheaded the development of financial analysis/modeling support using Argus/Excel in the company’s California region. He was also responsible for managing market analysis and forecast on various sectors of commercial real estate. His work was used as sources by LA Business Journal, LAEDC, California Economic Forecast, and many others. Prior to joining Grubb, Simon managed family real estate assets in Asia and has led multiple investments in joint venture development throughout emerging cities of China during the stint. Simon earned his bachelors in business administration with a concentration on finance and real estate from Cal Poly Pomona in 2004, while working full-time as an assistant commercial real estate lending officer for Guaranty Bank of California.
