Michele Reynolds’s Summary

Why I am on linked in:
I am on linked in to meet other people with who I can connect with. My primary goal is to share SendOut Cards with business professionals or anyone who has a desire to stay in touch with others. There is always a reason to reach out and touch another person in a positive way. The card you send may bless someone else’s day or arrive at just the perfect time. I help others build strong business and personal relationships. I am also seeking people who want to create an additional revenue stream by having fun! Make a resolution to stay more connected today!

I also have a full service resume business where I help people write and edit their resumes. For this business, I am seeking people who need their resume updated, created or polished.

My customers can be physically located anywhere within the United States.
