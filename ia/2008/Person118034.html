William Anderson’s Summary

I've been a Lead Designer, Developer / Production Manager for well over two decades now, having worked in-house for many of the top developers and publishers in the world. I'm a US and Taiwan business consultant, writer and ghost writer for many years now, also contributing game development articles to industry books and gaming web sites. Also I'm currently writing a book on game production procedures for a well known US book publisher, out in 2008. (MagicByDzn@hotmail.com)
