Demet "Demi" Yezgi’s Summary

Intero Real Estate Services is proud to announce that Demet "Demi" Yezgi has joined our team of innovative real estate professionals - a team who is setting the standard for customer service in the real estate industry.

Demi has been a social entrepreneur and community leader since 1999 and brings her love of helping people to her nationwide and international clients in real estate. As a certified Ecobroker, she shows her care for Mother Earth as well. Her award -winning professionalism, evident from her outstanding achievements in the nonprofit sector, ensures her clients of a smooth and pleasant experience when buying or selling.

Her nickname, (D.E.M.I.) symbolizes her Dedication, Energy, Motivation, and Inspiration... She applies these attributes to fulfilling your real estate needs and continues supporting charitable causes through her success in real estate.

By joining Intero, Demi affirms her commitment to Intero's core values of trust, respect, and integrity. We are confident that with Demi's professionalism and expertise, supported by experienced management and state-of-the-art technology, we will consistently exceed your expectations.
