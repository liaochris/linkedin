Kees Froeling’s Summary

I have worked for over 14 years in strong social network based environments to connect people and companies to cooperate in new business. I am specialized in the developments in communications of the next generation (generation C or 'screenagers') and the combination of Human Resources and ICT, resulting in innovative eHRM.

I am co-founder of the HR Puzzle, a society to accelerate innovation in the HR-arena in the Netherlands. We focus on knowledge sharing and eHRM.

Kees Froeling’s Specialties:

New Business Development in HRM and Temporary Staffing, innovative thinking, alliance management, partnershipagreements, sense for complex organisations.
