Michael Reilly’s Summary

Michael Reilly is an independent consultant working with emerging biotechnology companies, venture capital, and private equity firms on commercialization and business development. His focus is on shaping and maximizing the future value of medicines for patients and investors through thoughtful choices in the product development process.

Michael has over 20 years of leadership experience in pharmaceuticals, devices, diganostics, and industrial biotechnology. His direct experience in developing and commercializing drugs spans pre-IND stage, clinical, launch, and patent expiration.

Michael Reilly’s Specialties:

- Analysis of investment opportunities for venture capital and private equity firms

- Work with management, boards, and investors to develop and improve strategic, operational, and product launch plans

- Understanding and maximizing the value of assets in preparation for raising capital, partnership discussions, and road shows

- Evaluation of emerging therapeutic markets

- Prioritization of potential indications for a clinical development program
