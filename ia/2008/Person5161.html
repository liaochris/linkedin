Alana Beth Troupe-Baker’s Summary

I have been with IKON for over 5 years. I started out in a data entry position and have worked very hard to become an Associate Analyst-HR, which is my current title. A large piece of my job is maintaining 3 of our HR systems including our ATS system, HR File Imaging system and HRMS (Oracle) system. I also recently worked on quite a few data cleanup projects verifying and updating employee work locations for the US and Canada in the HRMS system. Another task I perform regularly is creating and executing test plans regarding enhancements or fixes to my systems. I attend DeVry University and am working to obtain a BA in Human Resources Management. I also enjoy volunteering for additional HR projects like our United Way campaign, bring your child to work day and our Wellness Challenge champion team.
