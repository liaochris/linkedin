Daniel Rosenberg’s Summary

Daniel Rosenberg is the Founder of InVenture Entertainment a New York-based film and television production and branded entertainment marketing company. Recent film credits include RIGHTEOUS KILL starring Robert De Niro, Al Pacino, 50 Cent, Carla Gugino and John Leguizamo. In 2006, he Executive Produced INSIDE MAN Man starring Denzel Washington, Clive Owen and Jodie Foster and directed by Spike Lee. Through InVenture, Rosenberg continues to develop and produce a slate of feature films and television series including a sequel for INSIDE MAN (UNI), MOMZILLAS (NBC), THE FIXER (ABC), HITCH WITH THE RICH (E!) and CREATED BY: 2008 (LIONSGATE/3 BALL/DEVIL'S DUE/SCI FI). On the marketing side, Rosenberg works with agencies and companies looking to expand their brands through content creation. Currently, he is bridging the gap between Hollywood and Madison Avenue having assembled a collective of over 30 A-List Hollywood writers who work directly with agencies and brands to create original branded entertainment. He recently brokered a multi-million dollar deal between his client, Reebok, and Scarlett Johansson to create a collection of fashion forward apparel and footwear. He also consults for various Silicon Valley companies advising senior management on content production and acquisition.

Prior to InVenture, Rosenberg was a production and development executive at Revolution Studios (MAID IN MANHATTAN, MONA LISA SMILE) where he oversaw a diverse slate of motion picture and television projects. In 2001, Rosenberg produced NOVOCAINE starring Steve Martin, Helena Bonham Carter, Laura Dern and Kevin Bacon.

In 2005, Rosenberg co-authored, CLOSING THE DEAL (HarperCollins), with Richard Kirshenbaum, co-Chairman of advertising agency Kirshenbaum Bond and Partners.

Presently, Rosenberg serves on the Board of Directors of the British Academy of Film and Television EC. He credits his start in the entertainment industry with his four years at ICM in Motion Picture Talent.
