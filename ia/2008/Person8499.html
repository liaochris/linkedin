Alex Ivanov’s Summary

I'm wrapping up my doctoral study and a design instructor position at SFU's School of Interactive Arts & Technology.

My dissertation is about the application of information visualization in Group Support Systems. Related to this, I've developed a couple of online communities that make use of advanced information design.

In the days or Web 1.0, I was a user experience designer at a successful Silicon Valley dotcom, and associate creative director at a multinational ad agency.

I have an MA in Advertising from the University of Texas at Austin, and a BA in Journalism.

Alex Ivanov’s Specialties:

Distilling consumer insights for business planning and marketing (qualitative and quantitative research)

Web strategy and concept design

Pretty much the whole gamut of Adobe apps.
