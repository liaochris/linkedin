Chris Phillips’s Summary

My career spans 30 years in the financial industry. Formerly holding positions in global securities and derivative operations roles culminating in running global middle office and operations for Sakura Global Capital Inc. For the last 12 years I have consulted to the capital markets industry as well as private banking, asset management and insurance. My consulting engagements have covered trading and marketing analytics and support, market and credit risk management, ISDA documentation, settlements processing, financial control/regulatory compliance for buy and sell side treasury functions; portfolio management reporting/accounting, order generation, compliance and performance management for investment management; asset and liability management for actuarial risk management and reserving for insurance. Functionally, I have been engaged for program/project management, system search and selection, business analysis, business process reengineering, policies and procedures development, vendor management and QA testing programs. In these various capacities I have interacted with all disciplines within investment banking, asset management and insurance organizations.

Product Experience: Derivative Trading and Risk Management Systems (Misys Summit, Principia PAS, and historically SunGard Infinity, Opus and Devon); Order Management and Compliance Systems (Charles River, LatentZero, Linedata, Simcorp, and SunGard Decalog); Portfolio Management (PAM, Portia); Performance Measurement (Sylvan); Asset and Liability Management (SunGard Prophet).

As an affiliate to Gnosis Inc, I assist in the provision and support of their solutions and services to provide independent risk assessment of financial derivatives, securities, mortgages and other assets and liabilities. Such services provide for independent risk oversight or an outsourced risk management capability. Also, this may be white labeled for the purpose of providing risk services to institutional clients.
