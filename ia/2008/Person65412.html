Frank Conway’s Summary

8 years of experience building consumer web products for major media companies including MTV, ESPN, and Major League Baseball.

I have also scratched the entrepreneurial itch with my latest startup CrowdIQ Inc. CrowdIQ.com is an open prediction market community.

Frank Conway’s Specialties:

Real-time data applications using AJAX and/or Flash, innovative online video products, and compelling user experiences

  • Product Manager - Application Development

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; DIS; Internet industry)

    October 2002 — November 2004 (2 years 2 months)

  • Senior Front End Technologist

    MLB.com

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    June 2001 — October 2002 (1 year 5 months)

    Defined and maintained the JSP presentation layer architecture of MLB.com, WorldSeries.com, and the thirty team sites.

Gameday, a real-time live game simulator, represents all events in a baseball game including play-by-play, pitch tracking, and box-score details. Gameday was also integrated with and QuesTec pitch coordinate data to create an illustrated 3D representation of the path of each pitch in a game.
