Celine Horan’s Summary

Celine Horan is a sales catalyst and marketing strategist for the e-learning, recruitment and professional development industry.

Once a legal secretary, Celine has become an independent business woman, an amalgam of the e-learning and professional development industry. She recognizes the vital importance of continually developing and growing, to stay relevant in the market place. Celine understands how difficult it is for personnel marketers to make the “match” between what is available for manpower, and what is needed.

She's also keenly aware that a shortage of quality “human resource and capital” is just around the corner, with the retirement of the baby boomers. Celine’s ability to see “what’s coming” allows her to help companies in the e-learning and professional development niche to prepare for the considerable marketing challenges ahead.

Celine’s intimate knowledge of e-learning and professional development language, coupled with her deep appreciation for the niche’s unique marketing challenges, excellent closer abilities, and her strong copywriting skills, allows her to create powerful marketing campaigns and strong copy that meets or exceeds her clients' goals and objectives of ROI.
