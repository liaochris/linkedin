Asa Levin MBA’s Summary

TRANSFORMING LEADERSHIP DYNAMICS

Asa Levin, M.B.A., has a deep interest for what creates long-term success and sustainable greatness in people, particularly in the area of leadership. As a Luminosa director she specializes in the transformation of individual and group Core Dynamics™. With almost two decades of professional experience in the field of human potential, Asa has developed the skills required to quickly identify core issues and bring new inspiration and vitality to any process involving people. Working with indivuduals and teams, she assist in the initiation, building and completion of quantum leaps of performance. Her result-oriented approach has been proven to help clients build more successful environments and create more fulfilling lives.

Asa has substantial experience from taking business projects from scratch into viable, efficient and sustainable businesses. She has studied business administration (civ ek, M.B.A. from Stockholm School of Economics, International Program of Management at HEC in France), coaching (Coach University CoachU USA), psychology, holistic medicine and modern advanced techniques for personal transformation. This broad combination of business and holistic backgrounds makes Asa uniquely qualified to understand and navigate the complexities of a modern working environment.

Asa Levin MBA’s Specialties:

Advice and support to leaders, especially regarding the human element of highly complex and/or challenging leadership issues. Leadership training programs, conflict and crisis management.

  • Leadership Consultant, Facilitator and Executive Coach

    Luminosa Institute

    (Privately Held; Management Consulting industry)

    June 1998 — Present (10 years)

    As a leadership consultant, facilitator and executive coach in leadership related issues, I assist clients in clearing and repowering individual and team Core Dynamics™.   • Program Director of Adventure Tours

    Luminosa Institute/Luminosa AB

    (Privately Held; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    October 1995 — December 2007 (12 years 3 months)

    Drawing on a passion of nature, history and adventure in my own and my fellow explorer's hearts, I have created and led tours to extraordinary natural and historical locations in the world. The first tour lasted 21 days and included the Inca Trail, Machu Picchu and Lake Titicaca in Peru (1995). I have since then led tours in Egypt, the South American Andes and Scandinavian Lapland in collaboration with local experts. Exploring the Andes during the years 1995-2003 together with Andean expert guide Mallku Arévalo, rare and unforgettable experiences involving individual and human potential were shared among participants, as well as profound, challenging interaction with nature through an Inca perspective. Combining outer and inner experiences, participants claimed these journeys to be rejuvenating, sometimes even life-changing adventures.

  • Publisher

    Isis Publishers

    (Privately Held; Publishing industry)

    January 1992 — December 2000 (9 years)

    In 1992, I founded Isis Publishers (Isis Forlag AB), a publishing house specializing in personal growth. I built the business from a capital base of only 1 000 USD. I created the profile, content and distribution system of the company. In order to better reach the buyers in the small niche market, I founded the book club Vitalis. Providing customers with a selection of the best books in the market, Vitalis soon became popular. Growing too big to be contained in the mother company, it was incorporated on its own. After reaching over 6 000 active members in 1999 it was sold. Today, Isis is a quality publisher in the area of personal growth, presenting international best selling authors in the field such as Sanaya Roman, Denise Linn, Taisha Abelar and Gerald Jampolsky.

  • Managing Director

    Vitalis

    (Privately Held; Consumer Goods industry)

    January 1995 — December 2000 (6 years)

    In 1995 I founded the book club Vitalis, the first book club specialized in personal growth in Sweden. With a total of 500 members after the initial year, the number of active members more than doubled year by year, reaching 6 100 members in December 2000. The strong growth lead to Vitalis being incorporated on its own (Bokklubben Vitalis AB). As CEO of the company I concieved and developed the business, administrative and marketing structures, as well as the profile and content of the club. I was also chief editor of the monthly Vitalis newsletter, each month presenting the most significant new book releases in the field. In 2000, when turn over from book sales reached 570 000 EURO per employee, Vitalis was sold to Tidningen Hälsa/Energica, the current owner of the club.

  • Facilitator and Personal Advisor

    Self-employed

    (Self-Employed; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    July 1990 — June 1998 (8 years)

    I had assignments as a facilitator, lecturer and advisor in personal growth and management. In Stockholm, Uppsala, Linköping, Katrineholm and Gävle I worked with a couple of thousand clients in various constellations - large and small groups, as well as individually. The learning was mainly centered around empowerment and integration of the higher aspects of the self, and the significance of personal freedom in choosing life experiences. Clients gained assistance in changing fundamental beliefs and making key decisions for achieving more integration and balance in their personal and professional lives.
