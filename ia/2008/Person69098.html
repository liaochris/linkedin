Gerald Adler’s Summary

I have over a decade of digital media marketing and advertising management experience, Business development, consultative sales management, and increasing market share are strengths. I enjoy leading and working with teams to exceed the goals of our company and clients.

By taking a 30,000 foot view of an agency's/client's current needs and future goals, I develop a strategic plan to enhance campaign achievement.

Client ROI is maximized by understanding needs and goals, researching target/reach value, planning position and placement specifics, then implementing and managing their evolving digital media advertising campaigns.

My professional goal is to earn a client's long-term business by effectively using digital media to WORK for them!
