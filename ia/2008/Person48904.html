Deborah Chan’s Interests:

For fun, Deborah is passionate about skiing, tennis and cycling. She skies all around US, Canada and Europe, but especially at Squaw Valley where she has a second home (www.SquawVillage.com). In San Francisco, she loves to play the piano and attend performances of the Symphony, Opera and Ballet. Deborah also enjoys reading, watching movies, lifelong learning and traveling.

Deborah Chan’s Groups:

A native San Franciscan and U.C. Berkeley graduate, Deborah is always involved in the community. The Little Jim Club of CPMC (California Pacific Medical Center) www.littlejimclub.org, is the oldest non-profit organization in San Francisco History. In 2001, Deborah was crowned Mardi Gras Queen for this organization in recognition of raising the most funds that year http://www.littlejimclub.org/past.html. In 2003, she served on the Board.
Deborah is also a member of WIRE (women in commercial Real Estate, www.womeninrealestate.org. In 2005, she served on the Board. Other memberships include: SF BAMA (bay area mortgage association), SF REX (real estate exchange), Commonwealth Club and the SF Museum and Historical Society. She was also on the Board of Governors at the San Francisco Tennis Club (SFTC) from 2002-2005.
