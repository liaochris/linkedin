Matt Franz’s Summary

Over 10 years of experience in security engineering, research, consulting, operations, training, and development in commercial and government security organizations. Extensive experience developing and maintaining custom Linux and *BSD distributions for security applications. Open Source advocate, conflicted Python and Ruby coder. Broad range of experience breaking and fixing a variety network devices, applications, and protocols. Expert technical trainer and down-to-earth conference speaker. 5 years of project/technical lead experience in various R&D and consulting engagements.
