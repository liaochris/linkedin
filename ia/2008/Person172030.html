Sean Cunningham’s Summary

My passion is helping firms to become exponentially more efficient through the wise use of technology. Although technology offers great promise to make employees more productive, tech projects are too often unnecessarily complex and difficult to manage.

The value I offer is a clear vision of how to simplify technology into manageable component parts; professional and clear communication; discipline and focus on each client's issues, and over 14 years of experience at delivering successful projects.

I have brought multiple commercially-successful software products to market, as well as led troubled product line turnarounds.

I have developed an Agile software development methodology based on our team's accumulated experience. It isn't intrinsically magical, but the net effect of having a well-managed team following a clear methodology where every team member understands expectations and deliverables has produced magical results. It has been used by numerous local, off-shore and hybrid teams to produce great products on a predictable timeline.

Logic 20/20 is a Seattle-based management and technology consulting firm that focuses on the intersection between business and technology. Please see http://www.logic2020.com/ for more details, or feel free to contact me.
