Greg Boutin’s Summary

My role is to build killer value propositions, by turning innovative technologies into products that people love and pay for. I specialize in the semantic web, tagging and knowledge management spaces, and contribute as a regular member to the “Semantic Web gang”, the leading podcast in the semantic space (http://semanticgang.talis.com). I have also developed deep expertise in solar energy applications and a range of other industries, and I leverage a focused network of talented individuals, especially in the Toronto-Waterloo region and the Silicon Valley.

My services range from analytical support to market strategy development and deployment, including but not limited to brand evangelization and thought leadership, monetization strategies, consumer discovery, and competitive analysis. To deliver market success, I rely on a combination of proprietary frameworks and analysis, based on logic and common sense rather than fancy theories and marketing jargon. To that end, I also master a range of advanced analytical tools and techniques (e.g. SPSS, advanced Excel, data mining), and I am experienced with Pragmatic Marketing implementation and certified in Agile Programming planning.

Typical projects include:
• developing and deploying a lead generation and revenue strategy
• evangelizing a value proposition through a focused thought leadership and prospect acquisition strategy
• building marketing "into the product", honing in on core benefits and exploiting viral features to create sustainable buzz
• conducting initiatives to develop a practical understanding of a potential user base and ensure a consumer-centric product dev. process
• supporting new client accounts acquisition through targeted sales support initiatives
• developing and deploying online advertising and search engine optimization strategies
• any other action aimed at turning complex technologies into killer applications
