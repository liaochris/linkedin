Andrew Pedisich’s Summary

I am President and a principal technical consultant of Technotics, Inc. In January of 2006 I became an IBM Certified System Administrator - Lotus Notes and Domino 7. I am also a Microsoft Certified Professional.

Prior to getting involved with things technical, I spent a dozen years in broadcast as DJ and TV announcer in the Philadelphia market.

In the 80s, I entered the computer industry as an instructor and trainer. My career started as an instructor for Lotus 1-2-3 and DOS 2.1.

I am one of the first 10,000 Microsoft Certified Professionals in the country.

I became a Microsoft Certified Professional in 1994, a Microsoft Certified Trainer in 1995, and a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer in 1996. i have also held Microsoft Instructor certification for Windows NT Server and Workstation, Systems Management Server, and TCP/IP. He is also a Certified Lotus Professional.

For many years, my company, Technotics, has partnered with The View, a technical journal for Lotus Notes and Domino. I spoke at The View's Admin 2001, Admin2002, Admin2003, Admin 2004, Admin 2006 and Admin 2007 conferences.

I will also speak at The View's Admin Europe 2007 conference in Spain in December..

I have travelled worldwide with The View's Lotus Notes/Domino 7 Upgrade Tour, and am preparing for the Lotus Notes/Domino 8 Upgrade Tour.

I was a Senior Systems Consultant and a Certified Microsoft Instructor for 11 years prior to starting Twechnotics in 1998. I taught operating systems, servers, and networking for PTS Learning Systems in King of Prussia, PA.

My corporate focus is strategic consulting for infrastructure and messaging/collaboration systems.

When I am not behind the keyboard (which is extremely rare), I enjoy lazy days in the sun pretending to fish and takes road trips on my 1988 Yamaha Virago motorcycle.
