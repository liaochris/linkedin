Øyvind Lier’s Summary

I have been working in Sweden, Norway, Russia and Tanzania with hydrology and hydropower.
I have been involved in all stages of hydropower (from surveys, via feasibility studies, EIAs, planning, building to rebuilding).
I have done extensive hydraulic modeling in MIKE 11/21/Flood and HEC-RAS and combined the data with GIS to enable better area management (flood etc).

I also have some experience from other infrastructure projects such as bridge-building and tunneling.
