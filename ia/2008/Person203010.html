  • President

  • Director of Change

    Johnsey Insurance Agency (Now AJG)

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Insurance industry)

    Worked closely with agency ownership to transform a traditional insurance agency into a team based, consultative, risk management solution provider. Consulted to Dreyer’s/Edy’s Grand Ice Cream on the design, development, and implementation of a national risk management process with special emphasis on plant ownership and ammonia process safety. Successfully demonstrated the concept of utilizing Active Agenda as a value-adding product and service tied to insurance transactions.

  • Risk Manager

    Dole Dried Fruit and Nut Company

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Food Production industry)

    August 1995 — June 1997 (1 year 11 months)

    Responsibilities included the design and facilitation of safety and casualty insurance programs for three processing plants, one fruit grading facility and two dehydrators in California. Primary focus was on the development of non-existent administrative systems within a newly consolidated plant with an emphasis on floor ownership. Managed the division's workers' compensation, general, auto and product liabilities through third party administration and employee education.

  • Western Region Risk Manager

    The Häagen-Dazs Company

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Food Production industry)

    April 1993 — August 1995 (2 years 5 months)

    Development and facilitation of safety, environmental, Department or Transportation, and casualty insurance management programs for manufacturing facilities and distribution centers throughout CA, AZ, and TX. Delivered ongoing training to leadership, management, staff, and line employees on topics relating to risk management practices and team building techniques. Provided staff support to all facilities and management oversight of the company's third party administrator relative to casualty insurance administration.

  • Safety and Environmental Manager

    Dole Dried Fruit and Nut Company

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Food Production industry)

    July 1991 — April 1993 (1 year 10 months)

    Designed, developed, and implemented safety and environmental programs for six manufacturing plants and one distribution center throughout California. Formulated and expanded the divisional training resources. Managed the division's workers' compensation line of insurance through third party administrator coordination and plant employee development strategies. Achieved a sustainable annual reduction in workers' compensation costs from $385,000 to $30,000 in one of California's most difficult environments.

  • Site Safety Engineer

Facilitated semimonthly supervisor/lead person meetings and administered workers’ compensation claims.
