Pat Bockenstette’s Summary

I have managed thousands of great people over the past 28 years in a hundred different markets across the country. I have helped make hundreds of millions of dollars for the handful of companies I have worked with. Through it all, the real secret to success in business is very simple...surround yourself with great people that feel and understand your vision, have an obvious passion for what they do and can accept responsibility for their actions. Stay clear of the individuals that live in a world of blame avoidance and only see a downside to risk.

* Pursue your goals with passion and help others along the way to reach theirs.
* Find out exactly what you do not like doing or are not good at and stop doing it.
* Spend your time on what you are good at, you'll be much more productive

"The Art of Leadership accomplishes more than the Science of Management says is possible"- Colin Powell
