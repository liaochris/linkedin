    Athens Furniture Co. Ltd

    (Furniture industry)

    March 2008 — Present (10 months)

    We are the sole distributor in Hong Kong for the "AtWork" system office furniture collection.

    What makes us different?

    Currently it is the only Zero Emmission desking system on the market. Zero Emmission is achieved by powder coating MDF board which in turn completely seals in the harmful Volatile Organic Componds (VOC's), like formaldahyde, usually found in conventionally laminated desking.

    It can also be supplied in anti-microbial format for healthcare applications. Unlike any other system furniture, it can be colour matched to the Pantone colour system. Manufactured in China, we can offer expedient delivery to Hong Kong & Macau in around 4 weeks.

    We hope to be able to offer a prestigious executive furniture collection in the near future, to complement our locally made seating and desking, storage and compactus.

    As a small company, we are able to offer very competitive pricing.
