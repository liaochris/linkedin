Nathan Follmer’s Summary

Currently, I am a 3d Modeler, Programmer and Web Designer for Faramix Enterprises Inc. I am skilled in various toolsets including, but not limited to: 3DS Max, Maya, Blender, Zbrush, Mudbox, Photoshop and Dreamweaver. I have been 3d modeling for just over 3 years now.
