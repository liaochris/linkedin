  • Owner

    FFARE (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Real Estate industry)

    2002 — Present (6 years)

    Fred France & Associates Real Estate (FFARE) provides top quality custom service to buyers as an Exclusive Buyer's Agency (EBA) / Exclusive Buyer Office (EBO) in the North Carolina High Country in Avery and Watauga Counties. Our agency is affiliated with the National Association of Exclusive Buyer Agents (NAEBA) - www.naeba.org. FFARE never lists properties; therefore, our office never practices either as a dual or seller agency. As EBA's, we work with other agents, brokers, agencies and property owners (FSBO) to help you accomplish your goals. FFARE is an independent real estate agency and is not affiated with the REALTORS Association. Please contact us for your commercial, industrial or residential property needs.

    Let us help you find and buy the mountain home or land you desire. The Avery and Watauga County area boasts good schools, quality entertainment, an abundance of art, friendly people and excitement for all ages and all seasons.
