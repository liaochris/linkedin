Gautam Guliani’s Summary

Gautam Guliani is a software architect and developer with over 15 years of experience developing web-based solutions to business problems in publishing, finance and education areas. He currently works as Executive Director, Architecture at Kaplan Test Prep and Admissions, a Washington Post company.

At KTPA, Gautam is responsible for technical design of all enterprise systems and integration amongst them.

Before KTPA, Gautam served as software architect and head of development at Fortunecity.com, developing web applications, systems and data integration solutions in the managed web hosting space. Previously, Gautam has worked on web-based systems at Citibank, AOL/Time Warner, The New York Times, TheStreet.com, Audible, and at Grassroots Technologies Inc, a professional services company he co-founded.

Gautam specializes in promoting and managing effective use of mature open source technologies in the enterprise and is the author of a book on the subject with Oreilly. More info at http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/opensourceent/

Gautam's other technical interests include system design, software development process management, collaboration technology.

Gautam lives, works and plays in New York City. He blogs about life, universe and other things at http://gautam.guliani.net/
