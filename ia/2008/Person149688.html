Peter Boswell’s Summary

Experienced innovator of software applications and utilities. Responsible for the conception and design of a number of desktop and mobile software products. Specific experience in developing data compression and optimisation applications and solutions for both the desktop, mobile and WWW environments.

A founder of the ‘PowerPressed’ suite of optimisation products and the world leading digital imaging compression product 'Blubox'.

Focused on the marketing, positioning and business development of products in the global market place. Specialised in the concept and design management of product messaging and branding.
