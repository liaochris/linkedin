Lee Egstrom’s Summary

Strearing the ship at AVWebDesigns. I plan, design and manage implementation of interactive projects and coordinate the essential marketing services that support them. My specialty areas are brand development, user interface design, information architecture and technology integration, these disciplines transcend all of my work.

I have produced solutions for diverse clientele including several Fortune 500 companies such as Disney, ABC, AIG Financial and Motorola. My commitment to understanding each client's business objectives and translating them into successful interactive products permeates my creative and technical work. My solutions have the power to inform, engage and deepen audience relationships.

I am also a board member of Thunder on the Lot. A local charity event which has raised over 1.5 million and donated 100% to children's charities located in CA. Last year's 1 day event raised roughly $303,000.00
