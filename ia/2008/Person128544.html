Merry Rosenfield’s Summary

I’ve spent most of my adult life working in the arts. I think it’s one of the most joyous things a person can do with their life, and I encourage all of my friends, family and even bare acquaintances to find a creative outlet.

One of my goals is to produce an effect on this planet that will contribute to keeping us all here, able to survive and be happy. As an artist, I feel it’s my responsibility to create work that is uplifting.

I continue to learn, to perfect my craft on a daily basis. I admire other artist’s work, and feel a lot of kinship with creators across the planet.

Merry Rosenfield’s Specialties:

Besides designing animal jewelry in sterling silver, gold, pewter and enamel- I produce custom made jewelry of people’s pets in polymer clay. I’ve created specialty pieces for several large animal related organizations such as the ASPCA, the ACVIM and the Marshall Legacy Institute.
