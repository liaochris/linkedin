Dharmesh Jani’s Summary

Product Marketing Manager:- Ongoing development of executable strategy for next gen phy chipsets for 10/40/100GbE market including applications across NexGen Datacenter platforms, Enterprise class switching platforms, blade server and switch fabric line cards.

Strategic Marketing:- Currently working on cross pollination of ideas across lateral market segments which have results in successful product launch. Also working on ideas related to services for mobile space.

Technical marketing:- Directed technical response (to customer RFP/RFIs) that included technology analysis. RFPs resulted in field deployment of Corvis Optical systems for Broadwing and Williams Communication.

System Designer:- Project leader for OC-48 transmitter and receiver line cards. Worked on Forward Error Correction implementation on Corvis OC-48 line cards. Designed FPGA to implement SONET protocol processing on these line cards. Design and development of OC-48 line cards generated over $100 million dollars of revenues for the company. Worked at Infinera on their Digital Line Module which is transport portion of their Digital Optical Network.

Partnership Work:- Extensive experience of working across all segments of players in a value chain from Carriers to system vendors to sub-system providers to component players.
