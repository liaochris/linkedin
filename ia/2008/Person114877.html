LJ Gomez’s Summary

I am an experienced graphic designer and when the opportunity is given to me I always apply one of the most simple and important diciplines in design.

--Consulting: Consulting with the client or Senior Director to improve the look and feel of a marketing sale or client recruitment. Always being a good listener and bringing the right answers with professionalism and knowledge.

--Art Direction: Responsible for guiding the staff in a professional manner. Team work and the look and feel of a proactive plan. New ideas, new styles and always new plans for future work allowing updates to be produced in an easy and fast manner.

-- Design: Create the concept and development of an imaginary thought to the real world. Clients concepts, staff's ideas and work experience to bring the best quality of a amazing design work.
