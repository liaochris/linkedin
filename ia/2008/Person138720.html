Professional experience:

Senior Biomedical Informaticist, RemedyMD, Sandy, UT: Product manager for Investigate, a clinical suite of tools oriented at aggregating, augmenting, and querying clinical research data in a consistent way throughout a research enterprise. Responsible for building marketing collateral, web material, and lead generation processes.

Medical Knowledge Engineer, Intermountain Healthcare, Salt Lake City, UT: Directly involved in web application development, database transactions, encoding business logic and working with clinical end-users to in drafting application requirements.

Graduate Student/ NLM medical informatics fellow, Department of Medical Informatics, University of Utah: Developed the Knowledge Authoring Tool at Intermountain Healthcare, a web-based application designed to support the authoring of clinical knowledge in XML. Analyzed acceptance patterns of the software among end-users and the importance of automated feedback in the knowledge management cycle.
