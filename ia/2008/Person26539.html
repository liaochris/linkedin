Jorge Bravo’s Summary

Jorge Bravo was the former General Manager for Latin America for Expedia & Hotels.com. He was responsible for the Latin America Business Unit operation, as well as all aspects of the sales, marketing – online and offline -, research, PR, customer service, distribution, call centers, outsourced vendor relations, finances, P&L, strategic planning, product development and execution, He directed this division for the last two years, with outstanding grows in revenues and ROI’s.

Previously, Mr. Bravo, with more than 17 years of experience in the hotel & travel industry, was responsible for building and leading integrated sales and marketing operations for two of the leading Hotel chains in Mexico such as Presidente Inter Continental Hotels, where he handle positions from the Hotel level at the Cancun property all the way to Corporate Director of Sales & Marketing at the Houston Office.

Later he moved to Camino Real Hotels & Resorts, as Vice President of Sales & Marketing and it’s regarded as one of the Sales and Marketing experts for Travel Companies in Latin America.

He received his BA Degree in Hotel, Travel and Tourism Business Management at Universidad Anahuac in Mexico City and his MBA in Strategic Marketing at Cornell University.

After opening his consulting firm BRAVO Consulting, earlier this year, Mr. Bravo has started to consult for important companies such as Cunard, Presidente Inter Continental Hotels Group, helping them develop their interactive online marketing strategy, as well as redefining operations, sales and marketing processes.

His goal is to provide companies with integrated solutions to improve all aspects of their operations, sales, marketing, strategy and development.

Jorge Bravo’s Specialties:
