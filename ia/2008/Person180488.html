Stephanie L. H. Calahan’s Summary

Author, Speaker and Productivity Consultant/Coach focused on providing individual and group services. Our mission is to identify solutions, big or small, that minimize stress, enhance productivity, and maximize space so you can focus on priorities.

Ideal clients for me are people who want to maximize their time and increase productivity. So if you hear a relative, friend, colleague, or client say that they can't find something, or that they are buried under clutter (or piles of paper), send them my way! I especially want to connect with entrepreneurers that have grown to a point where they are experiencing growing pains.

We are always looking for self-motivated individuals that have great organization skills.
