Rich Nadworny’s Summary

Director of Digital Strategy developing digital marketing strategies and campaigns for businesses, government and non-profit organizations. Leading Contact Planning for integrated campaigns with digital at the core, rather than being the tail end of a traditional campaign. Leading agency groups into digital focus, with a focus on contact/media, PR and interactive.

I've developed award winning campaigns and strategies for clients such as Stowe, TimeWarner, and Unicel.
