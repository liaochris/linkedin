Darin Berntson’s Summary

I am a Certified Internet Marketing Consultant with WSI (stands for We Simplify the Internet). WSI is the largest Internet Marketing company in the world, 1500+ offices around the globe in 87+ countries.

My office is located in Salt Lake City and I service the entire United States.

What I do with WSI? First off, I am a business marketing consultant or adviser, a trusted partner to your business helping guide you to make the best possible online and in some cases offline marketing decisions. Most companies have such advisers in the way of attorneys and CPA's, but when it comes to advertising most small to medium size companies go it alone and get bombarded daily by so many advertising opportunities to make ones head spin. That is where I come in. I like to sit down with a business and help map out the most cost effective marketing solution possible centering around online marketing activities that can be tracked and assessed on a regular basis and, like in no other medium, actually track your advertising ROI.

We are not just web designers... most anyone can design a website, but not anyone can get you the results we can. No matter your industry, I understand the motivations of you the business owner, because at the end of the day, I am like you, an business owner.

With our global reach and production capabilities of all of our providers in our exclusive marketplace, you sill see that with WSI you are not just getting a designer or a coder, you are getting a team like no other, with the expertise to deliver results that best meet your needs.
