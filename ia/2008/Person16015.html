Aji Abraham’s Summary

Aji Abraham is a technology entrepreneur who has founded and led two companies (Armia Systems, Inc and SupportPRO) into profitability. His entrepreneurial leadership and innovative ideas has lead the companies into the fast track of success. Armia Systems, Inc and SupportPRO now has a great foundation in hosting and web technologies and has very impressive track record among the internet technology companies in Chicagoland. Under the leadership of Mr Abraham, the companies have grown to have very efficient and high-energy onshore and offshore teams.

Armia Systems, Inc offers web solutions to small and medium businesses in the Midwest. Founded in 2001, Armia Systems, Inc has an offshore development center in state sponsored Technopark in Kochi, south India. Majority of the technical development is done in India while architecture and requirements are done in the Chicago office, offering significant advantages in terms of value and visibility.

Under the direction of Mr Abraham, Armia Systems, Inc developed a number of open source web applications which are marketed under the brand iScripts.com

SupportPRO offers 24X7 technical support services and server administration services to web hosting, datacenter, ISP and ASP industries. The SupportPRO call center is located in Kochi, south India. SupportPRO is one of the leading technical support providers for the hosting industry for the last 4 years.

Prior to the entrepreneurship terms Mr Abraham was working Motorola Network Solutions as a Strategic Product Manager providing long term vision for the Networks business. He also worked as a software engineer with Motorola developing infrastructure products.
