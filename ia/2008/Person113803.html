Linda Henry’s Summary

Linda Henry has launched Internet strategy, directed website development and led content management initiatives for multiple companies. From 1999-2001, she created and produced the pioneering online series, AnyCo.com®, an interactive story and video series. With characters and plot ideas submitted by a global audience, the site became a popular destination during its two-year run. Since that time, she has consulted on a variety of digital media projects and served three years as an IT manager at a San Jose start-up, leading the development of an enterprise information portal. Recently she has taken on the new role of Digital Content Strategist for Network Appliance.

Linda Henry’s Specialties:

Linda is a writer and entrepreneur whose crossover career in regional theatre and high technology have led her to a specialty in developing stories for the web and the implementation of corporate content strategy and process. Her writing spans the gamut from multimedia scripts to business plans and market analysis.
