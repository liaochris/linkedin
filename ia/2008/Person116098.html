Lui Sieh JD PMP’s Summary

Lui Sieh is a seasoned professional with extensive experience in Fortune 100 and startup companies. He has a broad business background focused in program and project management, IT operations management, client relationship management and legal. Lui is particularly knowledgeable in the areas of project turnaround, business and leadership training, quality assurance and control, knowledge management, and customer relationship management.

Lui enjoys testing open source project management solutions and applications. Along with these projects he's active in promoting Project Management knowledge sharing through the SdB+PM Forum E-Group (also a LinkedIn Group).

Lui graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Bachelor of Science. He is a graduate of Brooklyn Law School, a licensed attorney in the State of New York, and has passed the California Bar. Lui is certified as a Project Management Professional (PMP) and Prince 2 (Foundation and Practitioner).
