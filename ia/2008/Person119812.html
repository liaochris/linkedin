Marc Jackson’s Summary

I am an expert in new financing methods in the interactive entertainment industries.

Since 2002, I have been an innovator and promoter of film-style production finance and risk management techniques in video games and interactive entertainment, and have worked as an advisor and facilitator to orchestrate ground-breaking production funding models for various industry participants.

My goal is to continue alternative financing trends, in support of the emerging Web2.0 video game delivery models — and in ways that advance game design innovation and creativity, and enable social gaming.

I have 20 years experience in the broader entertainment industry, 16 years in senior/executive roles of which 5 have been as CEO of video game companies. I hold an MBA cum laude from the prestigious HEC School of Management in Paris, a BA from University of Michigan, and am a dual citizen of the US & France/EU.

Marc Jackson’s Specialties:

Management consultant and expert advisor on new financing methods in the interactive entertainment industries. Specialty areas: project finance, structured finance, advisory services, management consulting in the interactive entertainment industry; production fund conceptualization and management.
