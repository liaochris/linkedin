    Palgrave Macmillan will be publishing this book in 2008. The Elephant Hunters:Chronicles of the Moneymen explores the enigmatic world of investment banking professionals. Through comprehensive interviews with and observation and direct experience of investment bankers, stock brokers, analysts and support staff, the book bares witness as a living history of the finance industry. This book incorporates quotes, case studies and current histories of ‘elephant hunters’ and the ‘hunt’. The book is both serious and humorous. The ‘secret’ lives of the ‘moneymen’ are brought into the open. The spread of observation covers the USA, the UK, Australia, Canada, Austria, Germany, France and Russia. What it means to make money through money is exposed whilst maintaining the confidentiality of those involved.
