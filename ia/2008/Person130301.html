Michael Perticari’s Summary

I have be a private mortgage banker/ home loan officer since October of 2003. I started my mortgage career at Arlington Capital which was one of the largest Private Mortgage Banking lenders on the East Coast.

As my practice grew, it was time to make a change to the largest mortgage company in the country and I was offered the sales manager position in Newtown, PA. I currently have 7 loan officers that I work with and help expand their practice, but I also have a team of 4 to help grow my personal practice.

My practice has grown to the Greater Philadelphia Area, New York, New Jersey, and several parts of the country. Some of my clients are from California, Florida, Colorado, and I am able to service the country as I would people right in my backyard.

Most of my practice is referral business is from WEALTH MANAGERS, FINANCIAL ADVISORS, ATTORNEYS, REALTORS, ACCOUNTANTS and personal relationships.

I am also Co-Owner/Managing Director of Tidal Properties, LLC which is a consulting/advisory firm that helps clients who are in need of commercial structured financing, either it be for large developments (international or domestic), the purchase of large companies, the structuring of equity placement, mezzanine financing, etc. Some of the commercial outlets that are used are private equity groups, hedge funds, and large cap equity funds that are ususally available through the larger banks of Wall Street.
