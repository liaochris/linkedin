Rachel Kokosenski, M.Ed.’s Summary

Over 8 years experience as a Career Counselor/Coach, both at the university level and in private business. I have written thousands of resumes, coached hundreds of job applicants, and provided thousands of hours of training to job applicants of all levels of education and skill sets.

I also have extensive experience working with people with disabilities - finding careers that match their abilities, helping them to answer tough interview questions, and how and when to discuss their disability in relation to their ability to do the job.

I would be happy to help you in any of your career planning needs - please don't hesitate to contact me! Email me: r_kokosenski at yahoo dot com.

In addition, you may enjoy reading my blog with career-related articles and advice at: http://rachelkoko.blogspot.com

Rachel Kokosenski, M.Ed.’s Specialties:

artist marketing, career counseling, salary negotiation, resume writing, interview preparation, and working with persons with disabilities.
