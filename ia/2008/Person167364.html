Sajjad Paracha’s Summary

I have done Masters in computer science with major in software engineering. After Graduation I have 6 years of experience in software development and my expertise are in J2EE, oracle, db2, SQL Server, Mysql, Hibernate , Struts. During this period I have been involved in development of large web based Inventory management, sales and distribution, Credit card banking host, call centre related systems.

Inventory Management system for Pakistan navy was the biggest project with 7 member team I worked on different roles in this project. I did analysis, database design/development, application development of my assigned modules.

Development of Credit Card Banking Host using ISO8583 protocol was another achievement in my life as it was totally a new domain we didn’t find anyone to guide us in this domain, I did R&D and suggested the tools and architecture of this application, then lead a team of 4 developers to develop this system.
