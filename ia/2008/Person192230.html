Traci Thompson’s Summary

I specialize in resume and cover letter writing for Administrative Professionals. Allow my years of experience as a Hiring Manager, Administrative Supervisor, and Office Manager to assist you with getting interviews in today’s competitive market.

While working as an Administrative Supervisor at the Orlando/Orange County Convention & Visitors Bureau, I collaborated with the Vice President of Human Resources to recruit and hire support staff members for their convention sales department. Additionally, I took a personal interest in assisting colleagues with resume and cover letter writing when they applied for positions within the organization, and outside in some cases. Coaching and mentoring those team members gave me an overwhelming sense of fulfillment.

Most resume writing services mass produce resumes using generic templates and provide service for job seekers of all professions. My custom-tailored administrative resumes and cover letters will adhere to YOUR experience and objectives. I will obtain pertinent information via e-mail and telephone, and through personalized service develop documents that get your foot in the door.

My services are affordable and you will deal with me directly at all times. I am committed to providing you with the necessary tools to secure employment in the administrative field.
