Kelly Schwalbert’s Summary

I work as a list broker and direct marketing consultant helping grow organizations with business development and customer relations management by expanding business with existing in house methods and developing new methods of direct marketing campaigns. At Direct Hit Marketing I help enhance companies marketing efforts by in-depth list ideas, list trends and targeted mail campaigns.I enjoy networking, meeting new people, and professional development.

My Previous experience in conference coordination, targeting specified audiences and developing successful program and topical conferences has given me the background knowledge to help develop successful direct marketing stategies for our various client programs. The transition to my position in Direct Hit was natural, as I have been around since it's beginning days in 1992. As a family owned company, we have sought to bring the best to our clients. By keeping our clients successful, giving them years of expertise and advice in the marketing world and providing first class customer service it has helped them to succeed, it has made us one of the top direct marketing companies.

Direct Hit Marketing, Inc accepted the 2007 Eagle Award from the Rocky Mountain Direct Marketing Assocation for "Supplier of the Year".
