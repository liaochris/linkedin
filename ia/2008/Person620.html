Nick Vaidya’s Summary

Author-Speaker-Advisor

**** On aligning Leadership Strategy Marketing & Sales into a singularly focused, integrated, and accountable revenue capture program ****

A business thought leader with sharp focus on growing revenue thru a premediated process of aligning Leadership, Strategy, Marketing, & Sales towards a single outbound revenue capture program

Nick Vaidya is a consummate marketing professional with leadership emanating from the cross pollination of backgrounds in sales, advertising, marketing operations, management consulting, off shore entrepreneurship, as well as the academia. His several years of consulting experience spans both Fortune 500 companies as well as small businesses and includes B2B as well as B2C corporations in retail, manufacturing, IT, & Finance. He is the founder & CEO of Teleologies, Inc., a 5 years old off shoring provider.

Nick has consulted with companies like Microsoft, Ebay, Harcourt, Trilogy, Dell, and IBM. At one time, he served the Strategy Team in the Office of the Chairman at Dell, Inc. as a Senior Consultant. Later he was responsible for leading the profitability & pricing team for almost half of Dell’s profits. He headed the consulting team at Ad Agency Avenue A in Seattle and worked for OC&C Strategy Consultants. He did graduate and doctoral work in Marketing and Psychometrics at Texas A & M University and has several publications and presentations to his credit. Currently, he is working on a book - “Contract With Growth: Managing The Short Circuited Leader Within”.
