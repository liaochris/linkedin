John Heinlein’s Summary

My background and experience offers a unique balance of both technology and business. My engineering degrees and experience during the early part of my career touched on nearly every aspect of computer architecture (including software, hardware, and system design). Since 2000, I've held key Marketing, Business Development, and executive management roles, including the management of profitable business units, contract negotiations, public speaking with journalists and conferences, as well as numerous other areas of experience.

John Heinlein’s Specialties:

computer architecture, microprocessor, business development, contract negotiations, engineering services management, technology licensing, software development, customer support, relationship management
