Catherine Fentress’s Summary

Since completing my MSc I have gained practical experience both working behind a desk for an net label and a UK Think Tank and in the field working as a photographer in Africa.

Undertaking a Masters in Development Administration and Planning provided me with the skills needed to plan and manage the day-to-day needs of a company.
My previous work as a logistics manager on the University of Pennsylvania Archaeological Tunisian survey and events coordinator for tours across Europe, gave me a broad set of organizational skills that I can easily apply even to an unfamiliar type of job. My knowledge of Italian, French and Spanish furthermore help me to communicate with a wide range of different people.

Working for a start-up record label, BeatPick.com, gave me the opportunity to test my abilities by applying myself to a wide range of activities both new and learned. I was expected to multi task, come up with original proposals and to learn entirely new skills on a daily basis. During my time at BeatPick, I was successful in broadening the customer base, recruiting new, award-winning talents and raising the company’s profile both on-line and on the ground.

My experience working as a photographer in Africa brought out instead my communication skills.Ideally I would be interested in working on some kind of a project that involved film, photography and other tools that help in the production of culture.
