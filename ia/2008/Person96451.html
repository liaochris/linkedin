Jonathan Smith’s Summary

Registered Representative from 1976 to 1987 for a regional stock brokerage firm. Gained broad experience in all markets, maintained brokerage and research relationships with founding principals of established mutual funds, and founded a private investment partnership in 1981.

Founder (1988) and Managing Partner of Jonathan Smith & Co. Investment Counsel, a SEC Registered Investment Advisor, provider of highly customized fee-only wealth management and investment advisory solutions to high net worth individuals, families, and foundations.

Jonathan Smith’s Specialties:

Wealth and investment management, financial consulting, behavioral finance, gerontology, philanthropy, transfers of wealth, life planning, multi-family office service.
