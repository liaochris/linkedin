Beth Caldwell’s Summary

Beth Caldwell, Owner of PA Family Publishing, former publisher of The Pittsburgh Kids’ Directory and the online resources www.PositiveMoms.com and www.PittsburghProfessionalWomen.com.

Beth is a publisher, public relations specialist and a mentor to other women trying to balance family and career. She is a single mom of two teenage sons and resides in Pittsburgh.

In 2006, Beth was presented with the Make The Connection Award for community volunteerism and was featured in the November, 2006 issue of Pittsburgh Magazine as one of Pittsburgh’s Top 40 Business people under 40 years old. She received The National Organization of Women Business Owners “Member of the Year” award in June, 2007

Beth is an active member of her community and her children both attend public school. Her mission is to have a positive impact on the community and to educate both families and professionals on diversity and acceptance of people from all backgrounds and abilities. She is an active member of NAWBO, (The National Association of Women Business Owners) a service provider and consultant for Single Step Strategies, and is the President of Pittsburgh Womens Networking Group. She consults with small business owners and non profit agencies, assisting them with public relations. She is available for speaking engagements and public relations consulting.
Beth can be reached at 1-866-635-9402.
