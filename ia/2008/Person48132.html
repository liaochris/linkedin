Dayna Steele’s Summary

Dayna Steele is the author of "Rock To The Top: What I Learned About Success From The World’s Greatest Rock Stars" (March 2008 Brown Books). Dayna is also a popular speaker, entrepreneur, writer and voiceover artist. She reigned for years as Houston's “First Lady of Radio”, building a large army of fans known as ‘Steeleworkers’. As one of the top female rock and roll air personalities in the country, Dayna was nominated as Local Radio Personality of the Year by Billboard Magazine and was also included in Talkers Magazine's 100 Most Important Radio Talk Show Hosts. Her radio broadcasts took her from the war zone in Bosnia to the opening of The Hard Rock Hotel in Las Vegas to concerts and interviews with countless bands and rock stars around the world. Her latest venture is Smart Girls Rock, an online community to encourage girls to participate in science, technology, engineering, math, medicine and business.
