Tzvia Shelef’s Summary

Tzvia is a producer with an outstanding track record in creating world-class film, television and commercial productions.

She served as Assistant Director to Steven Spielberg in Schindler's List and produced more than 600 commercials for Saatchi & Saatchi working with actors such as Claudia Schiffer and John Cleese.

Her work includes working for high-profile clients like Levi's, Centrum, Procter & Gamble, Ford and Mazda.

She also served as Production Manager and Line Producer for several popular Israeli TV series and much more.

Tzvia Shelef’s Specialties:

Accomplished, reliable and results-driven Producer with an outstanding track record of bringing together and leading many disciplines and personnel to create world-class film, television and commercial productions. Extremely well-organized and self-disciplined professional entrusted by top producers, directors and clients to consistently achieve astounding, award-winning results under strict time constraints while finding innovative ways to reduce costs.
