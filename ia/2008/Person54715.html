Dr Sandeep Singh’s Summary

Hi ! I'm Dr. Sandeep, practicing General Dentistry with my wife Dr. Tanuja, in one of the holiest city of India, Allahabad, which was earlier known as Prayag because of its unique geographical wonder i.e. union of three holy rivers of India at one point, called SANGAM.

Our clinic, which is situated in the heart of the city, is a State of Art Dental Clinic with all the latest and necessary modern equipments. This helps us to provide the best of treatment to our valued patients, all over world.

Our another Clinic with the same name "Sahaj Dental Clinic", is fully functional in : A - 105, Super Mart - I, DLF City, Phase - IV, Gurgaon - 122002, Haryana State, ( near Indra Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi ), INDIA.

We graduated from V.Y.W.S. Dental College & Hospital, Amravati, Maharashtra State, India with a dream to bring beautiful smile on every face. To fulfill this dream of ours, we worked along with India's few eminent Dental Personalities viz. Dr. D.G. Adwani, Dr. R.U. Thombre, Prof. B.P. Singh and Dr. Hari Prakash of A.I.I.M.S. New Delhi.

In this endless journey of ours :

I got First Merit in my final B.D.S.
I did my Residency from All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, India.
I presented a paper on "Prophylactic Removal of Third Molars".
We prepared a Thesis on "Management of Impacted Mandibular Third Molars" & "Sterilization In The Dental Clinic", respectively.
We did a course on Dental Implantology from A.I.I.M.S. New Delhi.
We attended various National and International conferences.

I served as a State Representative, CDE Convener & Vice- President [for two consecutive years] of IDA, U.P.State Branch; Hon. Branch Secretary of IDA, Allahabad Branch & at present I am President of IDA, Allahabad Branch, which has made me more responsible and more concerned towards educating and making common people aware of Oral Care & Dental Hygiene.
