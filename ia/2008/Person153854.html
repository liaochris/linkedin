Gary Martin’s Summary

* Unique ability to clearly describe technical topics and concepts in easy-to-understand terms by using real-life examples drawn from extensive knowledge of the digital commerce, wireline and wireless telephony, broadband cable, satellite, and petrochemical industries.

* Adapts easily to rapidly changing business conditions. Successfully adapted to multiple management and organizational changes, the assimilation and delivery of new technologies, being merged with and acquired by corporate entities, and the issues related to the transition from being an employee of a privately held company to a publicly traded company.
