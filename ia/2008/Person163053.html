Rob Pincus’s Summary

Rob Pincus is professional trainer, writer and consultant. He and his company, I.C.E. Training, develop, teach and consult on training for military, law enforcement, private security and others interested in developing efficient firearms, tactical operations and defensive skills.
Rob has published dozens of articles in print and online publications and is the author of the book "Combat Focus Shooting" (2006) and the developer of the Personal Defense Video series.
Rob's training programs have been the subject of media coverage in various magazines and newspapers including S.W.A.T., Guns & Ammo, American Handgunner and USA Today, Soldier of Fortune and The Washington Post.
Rob has been featured on various television programs including World News Tonight, Personal Defense TV, Weaponology and The Shooting Gallery.
During his training career, Rob has provided extensive training to personnel from US Army Special Forces, US Navy SEALs, special operations law enforcement personnel, patrol officers, executive protection agents and persons interested in self defense.
Rob's Combat Focus Shooting program has been adopted or integrated by a variety of military units, law enforcement agencies and private instructors.
Rob has presented training sessions at various conferences, including IALEFI's Annual Training Conference, The New York Law Enforcement Expo, The NATO Law Enforcement Training Symposium and SWAT Round-up International.
In addition to his experience in the training arena, Rob has worked in law enforcement, private security, marketing & media relations and corporate team building capacities.
