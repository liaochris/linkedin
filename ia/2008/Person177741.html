Simone Moreali’s Summary

I have recently relocated to Orlando, bringing with me an international background in Information Technology—mostly in managerial positions, coordinating staff and Project Development. I consider myself at an expert level in the design and delivery of cost-effective, high-performance information technology infrastructures and applications to address complex business problems. My strong international networking background is coupled with a proven ability to build and manage creative, highly energized, focused teams. While working in the U.S. and 5 other countries, I’ve achieved a unique level of experience in a multitude of IT fields, eventually becoming management level. Core competencies include, but are not limited to:

• Technology Architecture and Integration.
• Network Platform Design and Implementation.
• Project Design and Execution.
• Electronic Commerce Strategy/Development.

In addition to my focus in IT and Project Management, I have also had significant experience in Marketing, Sales, Training and Support for international clients in the U.S. and abroad. My professional background has made me comfortable in interacting with all levels of an organization and any type of customer---by e-mail, phone or face-to-face. I am fluent in English, French, Italian (native) and soon, Spanish.

Always passionate about new technologies, I stay abreast of the latest evolutions in the field, and am creative with the IT solutions I present to companies. Notably, I am a quick learner, tenacious, and a very hard worker. I’m accustomed to either working solo as a consultant, as a top performer of a group, or successfully managing teams from 1-20 members.
