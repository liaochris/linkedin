Sylvain Mansotte’s Summary

Sylvain joined Schneider-Electric Australia in June 2005 to manage the Non Production Purchasing Department in the Pacific Zone (based in Sydney, NSW). In August 2006, Sylvain has been appointed Business Development Manager for the Pacific Zone. At that time, Sylvain was in charge of the Competitive Intelligence and Business Intelligence for the Pacific Entities of the Schneider-Electric Group of Companies, also involved on M&A files, and in several Projects to speed up the organic growth of the 8 Entities in the area. Sylvain was a key driver to build the 3 year Strategy of Schneider Electric in the Pacific Zone.
In September 2007, Sylvain has been appointed Project Manager for a multi million dollars Building in SA, as well as Property Manager for Schneider Electric in the Pacific. A total of 80 buildings and a portfolio worth $180M.
Prior to Schneider-Electric, Sylvain has held Corporate Purchasing and Logistics roles for Faurecia in Paris, SAMSE Group in Grenoble (France) and ABX Logistics in Lyon (France). Sylvain holds a Post-Graduate Diploma in Purchasing and Industrial Logistics from the Graduate School of Business of Grenoble, France (http://www.grenoble-em.com/) and also a Master Degree in Logistics.
