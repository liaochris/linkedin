Phil Campbell’s Summary

Creative Director on major licensed franchises 'The Godfather', 'James Bond' and 'Tomb Raider'.

Highlights:

The Godfather - original concept, mission design, new characters, story and dialogue, cinematics, world map and building layouts. Maintained the 'game arc' and story-vision.

Took James Bond from 1st to 3rd person with the original concept design for 'James Bond: Everything or Nothing'

Worked with David Cage to create 'Indigo Prophecy' as an interactive episodic experience.

Phil Campbell’s Specialties:

Concept design, interactive storytelling, dialogue and cinematics, environment and mission design ranging from enormous 'Living World' projects to 5 minute online episodic content
