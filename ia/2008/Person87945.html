Jeff Whenman’s Summary

Intro...

Jeff Whenman is a successful Network Marketer working in Great Britain. He is currently a leader in Europe’s fastest growing large company. Jeff is building a large and fast growing team, using simple but rarely taught principles that most Network Marketing distributors never learn.


Background…

Jeff had spent many years in financial services dealing with the directors of Britain’s largest PLC companies, He built a large telecommunications sales team, for a large American international. Jeff also built several smaller businesses all of which were successful. Having learned that building a large business, was done at the sacrifice of the more important things in life, due to the huge amount of time involved, he knew there had to be a better way.

Mission…

Jeff’s mission is to pass on the experience of the last fifteen years, and help others to build large incomes of their own, without needing to sacrifice many of the far more important things in life. The only way you will ever have a large income, without massive effort over many years, is to have leverage. The ability to earn from the efforts of others is essential.

Vehicle…

Jeff has partnered with a British company that provides a royalty type income, to thousands of British families through the industry of utilities. Britain has twenty two million homes, spending thousands of pounds a year on landline telephone calls, mobile phone calls, text messages, electric, gas and broadband, add this to three and a half million businesses, and you have billions of pounds in revenue.

Currently most of the companies enjoying these revenues are not British. Foreign companies have taken over most of the market, because of this billions of pounds in profit, along with thousands of British jobs go overseas. There is a huge amount of money on the table for those who wish to help us bring this revenue back to Britain.
