Stefanie Ambrois de Pascarella’s Summary

Brands colour our world.
Strong brands grow from an idea. An idea worthy of people’s trust – and loyalty. Its value may seem intangible, but your customers feel it every day: through what you promise and what you deliver.

Why design matters. Your brand promise lives by how you communicate. From your logo and corporate colours, through to advertising, interactions with customers, and the culture you establish internally – everything has an effect.

To take root in people’s minds, your brand must be memorable and consistent. With great design you attract attention, promote your offers, explain your services, motivate staff – and, ultimately, increase profitability.

What we provide. Taniemedia brings your brand’s big idea to life, and communicates it effectively it across media.

Mixed media, the next step.
Our first love is design. But today’s market demands more. Our strategic approach has evolved into mixed media.

Images, music, text, and graphics create unified messages across all your touch points. The result is work that’s fresh, desirable – and unique to your brand.

Why us? We’re bursting with ideas. And we harness our creativity to a real understanding of branding and your business needs. Combining talent and experience, we hold ourselves to the highest professional standards.

Our international view. Brands transcend borders, and so do we. With associates in Barcelona, Moscow, Paris, Prague and Buenos Aires, our London agency can draw on a considerable pool of local talent.

Fluent languages: French, Spanish and English.

Contact: stef@taniemedia.com
