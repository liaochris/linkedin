Judith Germain (jude@dynamic-transitions.co.uk)’s Summary

Dynamic Transitions Ltd focuses on developing leadership talent and improving social intelligence amongst leaders, business owners and mavericks.

Judith Germain defines mavericks as 'wilfully independent people'.

We develop our clients with our:

# strategic management or leadership consultancy or training
# one-to-one or team facilitation
# reputation and credibility consultancy
# tailored interventions with individuals and teams
# speaking engagements

We also mentor mavericks and maverick managers.

The types of mavericks we deal with can be found here: http://www.developing-leadership.com/definition-of-a-maverick.html

Our work in developing leadership talent, concerns itself with how individuals (or potential leaders), gain and maintain influence. Our expertise focuses on mavericks. In this area we do three main things:

a) we help senior executives manage their mavericks within companies

b) we enable the maverick to behave their way to success and discover their expertise

c) we support business owners in building their reputation and growing their business via advocacy and maintaining focus

We focus on enabling all mavericks (aspiring or otherwise) to build their reputation, to improve their communication and interaction with others and become more effective leaders
