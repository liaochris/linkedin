William McCann’s Summary

In my five years as a financial advisor, I have worked with large and small companies as well as individual investors, and have a particular interest in clients in the healthcare and non-profit fields. There's far more to what I do than can be put into this brief summary, so please visit my website for more details.

Prior to my finance career, I spent more than 11 years as a healthcare and non-profit corporation executive in New York City. I held the title of Regional Director for St. Vincent Catholic Medical Centers of New York, working with physician leadership at their flagship hospital in Manhattan and their region-wide Behavioral Health division.

I served as the state coordinator for disaster media relations for the New York Red Cross, and was recognized for my fundraising and communications prowess by then-President Elizabeth Dole at a ceremony in Washington, DC.

Now I spend much of my free time volunteering as a board member for East Brunswick Public Schools, a role I was elected to by my community. I am the current chairman of Finance and am responsible for structuring a $130 million budget for a district that consistently ranks among the best in New Jersey and the country.

I am a member of the Stuyvesant High School Alumni Association, a non-attorney member of the American Health Lawyers Association, and a member of both the Healthcare Financial Management Association and the Medical Group Management Association.

A native of Brooklyn, New York, I have lived in East Brunswick, New Jersey, with my wife, Mara, and my daughter, Hannah, 10, for the past nine years.
