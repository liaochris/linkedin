Vito Scutero’s Summary

TekPartners is a leading company in the IT Staffing and Recruiting industry. With annualized revenue of over $32 million, TekPartners is one of the nation's fastest growing staffing companies. We are looking for Branch Leaders, Account Managers, and Recruiters to expand or open various markets across the United States. We offer an industry leading compensation plan, excellent benefits and an opportunity to be a future leader within our company.

MedPartners HIM provides hospitals, physician groups and specialty care providers with accomplished Health Information Management professionals with a proven track record of success. We focus on special projects, short and long term contracts as well as direct hire.

Based on revenue attainment, our branch leaders will have the opportunity to have Profit Sharing and an Equity stake in their respective markets. Our Account Manager and Recruiter compensation plans are considered to be among the best in the Staffing industry. We are currently looking to identify top performers to join our company in:

Fort Lauderdale, Miami, Orlando, Tampa, Washington DC, San Jose/Santa Clara, Los Angeles, New York, and Dallas.

Interested professionals should contact Vito Scutero, President/COO at 954-656-8600 or VIP@tekpartners.com.
