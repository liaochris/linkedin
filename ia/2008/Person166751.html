Ryan Spiegel’s Experience

  • City Councilmember

    Gaithersburg City Council

    (Government Agency; 201-500 employees; Government Administration industry)

    November 2007 — Present (5 months)

  • Maryland Coordinator

    Young Lawyers for Obama

    (Political Organization industry)

    April 2007 — Present (1 year)

    Ryan was the founder of the Maryland chapter of Young Lawyers for Obama -- a nationwide network of lawyers and law students working to support Senator Barack Obama's campaign for president -- through fundraising, legal work, and organizing. Ryan serves as the lead coordinator of Young Lawyers for Obama in Montgomery County and statewide in Maryland. Ryan is also a member of the steering committees of Montgomery County for Obama, Maryland for Obama, and Maryland Lawyers for Obama.

  • Associate Attorney

    Winston & Strawn LLP

    (Partnership; 1001-5000 employees; Law Practice industry)

    September 2003 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Ryan Spiegel is a litigation associate in Winston & Strawn's Washington, D.C. office who concentrates his practice in complex commercial and white-collar criminal defense matters. Mr. Spiegel has assisted with the firm's representation of clients in connection with complex telecommunications and trade secret litigation, construction and other contract disputes, disputes related to energy tolling agreements, enforcement of creditor rights, antitrust matters, banking disputes, international arbitration, internal corporate investigations, and various other litigation matters. He also has extensive experience with pro bono work on asylum matters, landlord/tenant cases, and death penalty appeals.

  • Chairperson

    Victim Services Advisory Board

    (Government Agency; 11-50 employees; Government Administration industry)

    January 2004 — December 2006 (3 years)

    Ryan was appointed by then-County Executive Doug Duncan to serve a three-year term on the Victim Services Advisory Board, a volunteer citizen advisory board that counsels the county government on improving services for victims of crime, particularly victims of sexual assault and domestic abuse. During his term, Ryan served as the Chair and Vice Chair of the Board and testified several times before the County Council in support of expanded legal, financial, and health services for crime victims.

  • Candidate

    Maryland House of Delegates

    (Political Organization industry)

    April 2006 — September 2006 (6 months)

    In 2006, Ryan ran a hard-fought and widely acclaimed campaign for the Maryland state legislature, earning him an endorsement from the Washington Post newspaper and several prominent organizations. Although he fell just a hair shy of winning, Ryan's extremely strong showing established an excellent foundation for a successful future political career. Ryan's District (number 17) includes Rockville, Gaithersburg, and Garrett Park in Montgomery County, Maryland. Visit www.RyanSpiegel.com for more information.
