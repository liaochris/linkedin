nirmal dhiman’s Summary

Born: UK 1975. Living and working in the UK.

I am a visual artist, illustrator and designer, who likes to make intimate, colourful and multi-faceted narrative drawings and paintings using a combination of watercolour, gouache, inks, collage, Letraset, found elements, verse or prose, as well as with various computer, photographic and video based means.

In 1996, I happened to find an unused diary from 1969. In which I found that I had started to make a drawing a day, a process which evolved into a drawing about that day’s experiences, a Visual Diary artist book project which I have been working on ever since.

Now that I have over eleven years worth of drawings (approximately 4,000 entries), using a variety of different styles and formats, I am looking to exhibit and explore what else I can do with this project, eg, make derivative art pieces, installations, wall designs, (miniature) theatre sets, artist films and animations, etc.

The project has recently been researched throughout the UK and used by a number of students as the basis for their response to an AS and A-Level Art exam question based on visual diaries.

The diaries have already been shown on BBC television, radio and various web sites on several occasions, as well as other on web sites and publications. They also receive positive comments from book showings/exhibitions.

nirmal dhiman’s Specialties:

As well as the painting and illustration work, I have over eight years agency and studio experience as a designer, I have worked on a number of successful and award winning campaigns for a variety of clients. I have worked on design, construction, and the updating of award winning web sites, email communications, banners/overlays, illustration, animation, typeface design, logotypes, interactive games, CD-ROMs, printed matter and presentation materials.
