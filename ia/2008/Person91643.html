James Reed’s Summary

For 21+ years I have dedicated my practice to helping people injured by the negligence of others. I help people put their lives back together. I am proud to have been selected as a member of the Multi-Million Dollar Advocates Forum. I am admitted to practice law in New York and Pennsylvania and I have successfully handled cases throughout both states.

James Reed’s Specialties:

Personal Injury and Malpractice on behalf of injured parties only. I have not, and will not, represent insurance companies.
