Cicely Rocha-Miller’s Summary

Life Design Event Planning is a company based on moments in time, moments in lives, the joyous occasions when family, friends, business, and charities create remarkable memories….these are the events that we love, enjoy, and cherish.

We invite you to get to know us, who we are and more about what we do. We invite you to decide if we are a perfect match and then take delight in every minute of the planning process. We thrive on your event being successful, and with that in mind, we promise to do whatever it is that makes your event exactly what you envisioned.
