Roger Fance’s Summary

Roger is a high calibre programme and project management professional with 25 years international experience in Banking and Wealth Management, ICT, Government and Not-For-Profit working on both technology and business oriented projects. He has exceptional strategic and tactical organisational ability in complex environments to manage a multitude of tasks and people achieving focussed, successful results that are on time, on budget, greatly enhance business relationships and deliver tangible benefits. He competently interacts throughout all levels in organisations to identify and respond to needs.
