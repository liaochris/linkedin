Quintin Gomez’s Summary

I have over 15 years of direct experience leading the development and deployment of nearly a dozen brand new products for the consumer, enterprise and financial institutions in start-up settings as well as medium and large-size companies. My expertise is in the management of high-reliability, complex project development across a wide range of systems and solutions: from real-time trading room systems to network infrastructure, enterprise applications integration, internet standards specifications, customer-facing web applications and shrink-wrapped products. Extensive background in developing new products from scratch as well as modifying existing ones into scalable solutions. Excellent problem solver with the experience to lead and manage large cross-functional teams.

Quintin Gomez’s Specialties:

Diverse experience managing requirements, projects and teams across a wide range of technology (NT, Unix, VMS, Windows 2000, Tomcat, Java, C++. C#, .NET) systems and solutions from complex real-time financial systems (FIST, Market Watch and Trade Innovation), enterprise applications (Siebel, Oracle, EDI, Order Management and Product Fulfillment), internet standards (OFX), ecommerce (eStore, QuickBooks Payroll Subscription) to shrink-wrapped products (QuickBooks Payroll Setup).
