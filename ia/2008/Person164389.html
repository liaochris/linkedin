Ron Hudson [LION] [TopLinked.com]’s Summary

Mr. Hudson is an author, radio talk show host, Certified NLP Master Practioner, Business Growth Strategist and Strategic Influence Trainer and Coach. He has had the privilege of training sales people at companies such as Coldwell Banker, Dean Witter, Goldmine Software Corporation, New York Life, Cellular One, Lexus, Pitney Bowes and others.

He has also taught and used his marketing, copywriting, selling, or negotiating skills on behalf of Jay Abraham, Tony Robbins, Jay Conrad Levison, Les Brown, Dr. John Gray and many small to medium companies.

Additionally, Mr. Hudson has attended Tony Robbins' one day business seminars Strategic Influence and The Competitive Edge. He also completed the video based UPW and Power to Influence courses offered by Anthony Robbins & Associates. After volunteering during the events, he went to work in the franchise and started facilitating and selling the programs. Currently, Ron is the group leader of the international Anthony Robbins' Graduate Community group Mastering Influencing.

Other training: Jay Abraham's Live Mastermind Weekend, Jay Abraham's Protege' Home Study, PEQ, How to Get From Where You Are to Where You Want to Go. Dr. Chester Karrass' Negotiation Training, Roger Dawson's Negotiation Training, Dr. Robert Cialdini's Instant Influence Training, and Dr. Wil Horton's NLP Master Practitioner and NLP Trainers Training.

In business and life, the people who have the ability to connect, understand, and influence others and themselves enjoy ever increasing levels of success. What could happen in your carreer, business, and life if you learned their secrets? Where can you learn the secrets of highly successful people? Tune into Ron Hudson's new BlogTalkRadio show entitled Influence.
