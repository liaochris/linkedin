Nancy Sutherland’s Summary

I started my business in 1986, but have been using the products all of my life (it seems that way) Mary Kay has given me the tools to keep going despite many obstacles in my personal life making me capable of leading others through adversity. Ten of my years in business I was a single mom raising 3 sons. During those times I was still able to buy the family home and maintain it. I have been driving a Mary Kay career car since 1987 with a new one every two years. The connections that I have made through my business are lifelong ones. I have second and third generation clients. My unit of beauty consultants that I am responsable for mentoring and educating are located all over the country with over half of them as part of my unit for over a decade. My goal is to continue to grow my customer base and my unit while looking for woman who want an open ended opportunity to become all that God intended them to be. I feel very blessed to have personally trained with Mary Kay Ash. She was a terrific mentor and her values live on in our Company.
