Debra Paynter’s Summary

I have recently accepted a position as a Strategic Business Analyst at WebTrends in the Portland, Oregon office. I am settling into my new role and very excited as I begin to take on clients for the company putting to use the excellent training. I’m pleased to have passed the certification testing for both the WebTrends Certified Professional and WebTrends Certified Engineer. The Client Services team under the direction of Gary Church has provided me with excellent support.

My role as a Strategic Business Analyst with WebTrends is to help our clients determine exactly what are their business needs and how best to configure the software to meet those needs. I love the personalization of the product to the customer and as an analyst with over a decade of experience in the field it’s crucial for me to be associated with a company and product that focuses on that important relationship.

My goal now is to move forward with my role and serve the needs of my clients. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions.
