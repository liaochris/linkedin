Kim Pickett’s Summary

Kim Pickett is founder and creative director of KIMBO Design. She started early as a creative professional when her classmates engaged her to complete their coloring book assignments. At this early age, Kim demonstrated an entrepreneurial spirit when she considered charging her classmates for her design work; though found that their lunch money barely covered her expenses.

After graduating with honours from York University/Sheridan College Design program in 2001, Kim could have chosen to decamp to any number of design firms. Instead, she chose to remain in Canada and founded KIMBO Design in Vancouver. In the years since, Kim has demonstrated an ability to solve difficult visual communication problems—creating branding, identity, and interactive assets for such clients as Benjamin Moore, Bruce Mau, Discovery Computers, the North Shore Multicultural Society, Abacus Capital, and the Christy Clark campaign.
