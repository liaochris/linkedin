Gary Jonghoon Koo 丘宗勳’s Summary

Owner of web 2.0 marketing agency and partner of a Northeast Asian (Korea, Japan and China) Ad network.

Gary Jonghoon Koo has 12+ years advertising, marketing, design / brand consulting and interactive experience. Gary is focused on digital / product design and internet marketing, web 2.0, social media marketing, blogging and contents business.

During his career, he helped 100+ web strategy / development / branding & on-line advertising / integrated marketing communication campaigns working with multinational companies and Korean conglomerates including AIG, Boeing, GM (Cadillac, SAAB, GM Daewoo), Eli Lilly, Johnson & Johnson, TEFAL, SAMSUNG SDS, SK Telecom, Hanaro Telecom, CJ group, Pantech, Amore Pacific, Hansol Group, Seoul Metropolitan City Government, Korean Government - Ministry of Planning & Budget (Korea) and many more.

Gary has wide view of design, media & technology and built a strong network in the advertising, IT technology and business communities by participating in groups/organizations such as AmCham (American Chamber of Commerce) Korea, EUCCK (EU Chamber of Commerce), Rotary International and KIDP (Korean Institute of Design Promotion).

Gary is currently establishing an Innovation Design Consulting business, working with internationally renowned product design gurus and marketing consulting / product design specialist companies.

He's also working on EU government supported R+D (Eureka) project on future museum interactive guide (on-hand contents viewing / navigating / searching solution) utilizing internet / RFID / UMPC / Digital Siagnage technologies as well as advanced museum administration system, working with European Partners and will launch the project early 2009. Currently part of the fund (1mil EUR from Korean government & Probe Design) has been already assigned to Probe Design and Gary's trying to find more European partners (project participants) and investors from world.
