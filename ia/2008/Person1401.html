Jennifer Abelson’s Summary

Jennifer Abelson is a Corporate and Executive Communications Strategist with extensive expertise building strong brands, shareholder value and strategic communications programs that drive visibility and results for companies dedicated to the telecommunications and technology markets.

As Chief Executive Officer of Abelson Group, Inc., Jennifer is responsible for defining vision and value, cultivating strategic relationships with stakeholders and influencers and developing market strategies that fuel growth and significant visibility for her clients. Over the years, she has provided advisory services for many companies, including mBlox, Sonim Technologies, Millennial Media, mPortal, Handango, Ubiquity Software, Sirius Satellite Radio, PriceWaterhouse Coopers Consulting, USinternetworking, Eureka Broadband and others.

Within the Telecom industry, she has developed particular expertise in the areas of VoIP and wireless services, mobile marketing and advertising, mobile content, service creation, application services (push-to-talk, gaming, campaigns using SMS and MMS, mobile video messaging, conferencing), cable and satellite radio services, service providers and more.

Within the technology marketplace, her firm contributes expertise in Customer Relationship Management (CRM), customer care and billing, outsourced IT services, online greetings and advertising, e-commerce and m-commerce, Business Process Management (BPM), handsets, open source, online travel and more.

Jennifer Abelson’s Specialties:

Jennifer has cultivated strong expertise in corporate and product branding, messaging, media relations, analyst relations, investor relations, marketing communications, direct marketing and executive communications.
