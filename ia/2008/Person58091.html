    HiveLive offers the first community platform that seamlessly integrates social networks and information networks, enabling you to build flexible, custom-fit online enterprise (corporate) communities.

    The fundamental advantage of the HiveLive LiveConnect Community Platform lies in its flexible "building block" approach to community design. The LiveConnect platform building blocks, or Hives™, support familiar community activities such as discussing, blogging, and collaborating. In addition, communities can be tailored to allow members to innovate and iterate in custom social media applications, supporting activities such as sharing images, brainstorming ideas, polling users, tallying votes, submitting questions, or building consensus.
