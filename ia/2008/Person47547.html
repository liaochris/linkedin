David Sowerby’s Summary

David has over 10 years experience in the technology and CRM industry. David is a Director (and investor) in Straker and founder of Sportsys Pty Ltd. David's background is in Technology, Consumer Marketing and the Internet. I have a strong entrepreneurial drive and a proven experience in building businesses. I have been directly responsible for growing a number of companies from both startup phase or as startup units within larger organisations. My exposure at a senior management level with some large organisations including Omnicom and Unisys has seen me deal with management issues across the spectrum of client management, staff retention, business development and I have led a number of large multi million dollar contract negotiations. I have an Undergraduate Degree in Science from the University of Queensland, a Graduate Diploma in Management from CQU and a Masters of Business Administration from Trinity College Dublin
