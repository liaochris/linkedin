Founder & CEO Zintilla

Founding President, Imperial Entrepreneurs

Imperial Entrepreneurs aims to act as the social catalyst for the next generation of world class technology entrepreneurs by providing inspiration, knowledge and opportunities to learn by doing at Imperial College and beyond.

The society will strive to generate innovative ways for promoting technology entrepreneurship to facilitate interdisciplinary thinking amongst the diverse and talented student body at Imperial as well as generating a dynamic and forward-thinking community of entrepreneurs.



The society will aim to be the most successful and dynamic student-led technology entrepreneurship society in the country and develop entrepreneurs with the ambition and ability to successfully create technologies or processes which have the potential to change the way we live.



To achieve these goals the society will provide:



Inspirational Speaker Events – inviting world leading entrepreneurs to share their insight and experience.
Practical workshops – Offering access to leading practitioners providing valuable advice and knowledge and enabling learning by doing.
Social events: to facilitate networking with the entrepreneurial community and to develop a community of student entrepreneurs.
Global Internship opportunities: enabling IE members to gain valuable experience in a start-up environment.
