Paul Allen’s Summary

Paul Allen is the CEO / Project Executive of the Project Executive Group, a Houston-based firm providing advisory and execution services. Paul has over thirty years in project development, project management & structured project finance.

Paul, an adjunct full professor of management at the Jones Graduate School of Management - Rice Univeristy, has taught in the Executive Education and MBA programs since 1997.

Paul Allen’s Specialties:

Specializes in strategic project development, structured project finance, risk and decision management, project management, and troubled project turnarounds in the oil & gas, power, infrastructure and engineering & construction industries.
