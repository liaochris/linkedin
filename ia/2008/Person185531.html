Paul McGuire’s Summary

I am a freelance writer specializing in poker, gambling, sports, and music.

I have written for... Fox Sports, MSN, Poker News, Borgata Casino, PokerStars Blog, UK Racing Post, Bluff Magazine, Poker Player Newspaper, Poker Pro Magazine, Poker Pro Europe, LasVegasVegas.com, Sportsbetting News, the OnGame Zone, and Expressen.

I am a contributing author to "Pressure Poker: Poker Strategy and Tools to Improve Your Game" by Scott Gallant. I wrote several sections as a guest author.

I spent the last four years covering the poker tournament circuit in Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Atlantic City, Foxwoods, Spain, Holland, Denmark, Sweden, France, Monaco, Australia, New Zealand, Hungary, and the UK.

I am a partner in several websites including LasVegasVegas.com and FantasySportsLive.com.

I am the founder and owner of several websites including Tao of Poker and Coventry Music Blog.
