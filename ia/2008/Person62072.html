  • A Senior J2EE Consultant , Lecturer, and a Member of the Java Group Management

    Tangram-Soft (Novelya Ltd.)

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Computer Software industry)

    April 2003 — July 2007 (4 years 4 months)

    My specialty is architecting and building framework for
    application development, must of the times based on the J2EE

    companies using state of the art technologies. Architect
    and developed J2EE based frameworks for several organizations.
    A technology leader for developer groups. Analyzing and solving

    bottlenecks hunting”). Planning and executing prof-of-concepts at

    leaders and architects at customer sites. Deep knowledge with
    all of the J2EE technologies (Servlet, JSP, JSF, EJB 2, EJB 3,
    JMS, etc.), and in commonly used tools and frameworks (Struts,
    Hibernate, Spring, etc.).

    As a senior lecturer (both in and out of Israel). I cover a large
    spectrum of subjects - from the basic Java courses to the most
    advances courses that deal with the JVM internals and the bytecode
    itself.

  • System Architect

    eXcedo Technologies

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    November 2001 — March 2003 (1 year 5 months)

    A system architect for a startup which developed a business
    activity monitor (BAM) product. I lead a team of 6 developers to choose
    the architecture and environment. Designed and implemented a framework
    based on the J2EE specifications. Designed and participated in the
    BAM product development using the framework - using EJB, JMX, JMS,
    Servlet, JSP and other Java technologies.

  • CTO

    ModusNovo

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry)

    October 1999 — October 2001 (2 years 1 month)

    CTO, System Architect, and the framework developer of billing,
    customer care and applications’ mediation for Application Service

    framework for enterprise information systems in the C++ language
    (on Windows systems). This framework was build without any

    (like entities definitions, ORM layer, transactions definitions, network

    The framework’s core was developed by me in a three months
    development process, and later on was maintained and enhanced
    by a team of five developers.

    Technology leadership of large developers group (about 25 developers)
    in a large system (more than 900,000 lines of code). The designed and
    implemented architecture gained excellent feedback from customers,
    technical analysts and academic figures.
