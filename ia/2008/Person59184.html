Mike Nolan’s Summary

I am a search consultant specializing in the placement of Sales/Sales Management, Pre-Sales, Professional Services and Engineering Management professionals. Prior to starting my own firm I consulted with leading organizations such as Siebel, Sonic Software, Progress Software, eDocs, Weblayers and FAST Search and Transfer where I personally identified candidates for positions critical to achieving business goals as well as building/managing national recruiting teams.

If you are a candidate looking for that new opportunity or a hiring manager with a need you cant seem to fill please contact me. If I am not able to help you I have a network of very qualified people I would be happy to refer.

Here is my email address: mnolan@enterprisesearchinc.com

LinkedIn Rules of Contact:
1) Cultivating a positive and mutually beneficial network is my goal.
2) I’ll always accept invitations from friends, acquaintances and former co-workers and clients.
3) If we haven’t met, I’m open to connect but please send an introductory note so we can get acquainted. Include how we would both benefit from connecting.
4) I seldom forward requests from people I don't know well unless they are compelling and demonstrate clear benefit to the recipient.
5) If we aren't close please craft thoughtful and detailed requests. I respect my connections and won’t bother them with frivolous ones. In return I promise to only send you relevant, well written requests.

I got these rules from Shally Steckerl who I met at this years Fordyce Forum 2008. I have also attended some of his training conferences and I think his rules align with my thoughts on networking thru LinkedIn.
