Art Scotten’s Summary

EXPERIENCED CEO

Startup entrepreneur who created synergistic alliances with Intel, Microsoft, and NEC resulting in numerous first-to-market, leading edge, high technology products. Strategic Business Development tactician with proficiency in negotiating key domestic/international, multi-channel, distribution contracts with c-level executives. Hands-on CEO who engendered profitable businesses from the ground up.

Performance Metrics

* Led continuous product cost reduction based on hardware redesign programs using new state-of-the-art chip technology to realize 14% annual COGS savings.
