Chris Jones’s Summary

I'm a design engineer with a need for artistic expression. I've created bicycle disc brake systems with a high level of aesthetic content and pleasing ergonomics.
They're not just bike parts. Some people make their living using these parts all day. People arrange vacations to go out and use these parts. It's more than designing something that works well, it's designing something that's enjoyable to use.

Chris Jones’s Specialties:

Blending aesthetic into function.
Innovative use of space for packaging.
Unique Product Identity through Innovative Design.
Grocking the box.
Playing with with concepts and realizing them in product form.
Pro/E surfacing and drawing. I like to use datums in the same way the manufacturer loads parts or the Quality Engineer inspects them. One way, right way.
I provide stability through mergers and acquisitions.
I create handle bar architecture and invest in manual real estate.
