Joanna Swanson’s Summary

a short bio: Joanna Swanson, received her bachelors degree at The School of Visual Arts in New York City. She started her professional career, while she was still in school, working for several design firms and publishing houses. In 2000, Joanna was recruited by one of her teachers and started working as an Art Director at Time Inc. Custom Publishing in New York City. At Time Inc. Joanna led a team of Designers that together created magazines and prototypes from scratch for global giants such as Citibank, Target and A.J. Prindle (Ford).

For the past few years Joanna has been running a little design studio in Stockholm, Sweden that develops and manages innovative design solutions for diverse clientele. She has had the opportunity to work on a wide range of projects that include: bedding collections, posters, cards, ads, invitations and catalogs. No matter how big or small, Joanna likes to work closely with her clients so that together they can develop a clear strategy and a well-defined path of action. This allows Joanna to create work that combines form, function and achieves its objective.
