Manu Lopus’s Summary

Post-doctoral fellow having extensive experience and in-depth understanding in the mechanism of action of anti-cancer drugs, anti-neurodegenerative agents, and screening as well as development of microtubule-targeted anti-cancer and anti-neurodegenerative drugs

I persevere to develop drugs that target and specifically eliminate cancer cells while leaving the normal cells intact. My research group is making good progress in this area with the use of humanized monoclonal antibodies and development of immunoconjugatable drug molecules. Similarly, we are working on methods for stabilizing microtubules in nerve cells that are affacted by neurodegenerative diseases like AD.

Manu Lopus’s Specialties:

Mechanism and regulation of microtubule dynamics in neurodegenartion and cancer, molecular mechanism of cell division and cell death, Mitotic checkpoints
