Mark Halpert, CPA’s Summary

I work with small businesses and non-profits to improve their financial systems. I help these companies and organizations to better understand their financial information so that it can inform their work. I establish efficient accounting processes and create user-friendly tools for cash flow management and budgeting.

I'm A CPA and Canadian CA with over 10 years of experience in accounting for businesses, non-profits and individuals. I worked to establish City CarShare, the Bay Area's non-profit car sharing program, as its Deputy Director.

As a consultant, I am fortunate to work with interesting and inspiring people. I am currently seeking new contracts with progressive companies and organizations.
