My experience with Linkedin:

Over the last few year, this professional networking site, LinkedIn have become extremely popular global media to build professional networks, share interests, exchange ideas and make new friends.

With this objective in mind, I went in and explored this networking sites and built my own networks, especially with professionals in the hi-tech industry.

Like everyone else on LinkedIn, I started building my network with people who were already known to me. From there, LinkedIn helped me expand and amplify the network, and make more contacts than I could have ever imagined! I realized (rather early in the game) that this could turn out to be power house of professional contacts and help me target some very high quality senior talent for Sapient.
