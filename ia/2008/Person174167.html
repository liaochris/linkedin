Shantanu Roy’s Summary

I am a Chemical Engineer with specialisation and experience in Pollution Prevention & Control. Professional consulting & training in Environmental Management including management system implementation, pollution prevention and process optimization & modification related issues has been my areas of work.

I have had the opportunity to service various national level government organisation including the MoEF, CPCB, NCPC and various state departments and SPCBs.

I have worked with various sectors of the economy and handled various project functions including Project envisaging, Project management, Business development and project execution.

The other features about my professional experience are:

* Experienced in planning and innovation.
* Able to manage multiple functions simultaneously
* Skilled communicator able to eliminate cultural barriers and adapt to business protocols
* Effectively interfaced with management, engineers, and staff across departmental levels within various organizations

I have added to my knowledge base built on my education, through various trainings over the years. A few significant among them are listed below:

- Design and development of Hazardous waste Management Systems through GTZ Germany in 1998
- Training of Trainers on Pollution Prevention from USEPA in 1998
- Integrating Cleaner Production and Energy Efficiency from UNEP in 2002
- Lead Auditor Training in OHSAS: 18001 from DNV in 2002
- Integrating Multilateral Environmental Agreements with Cleaner Production from UNEP in 2005
