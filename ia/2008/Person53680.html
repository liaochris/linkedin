Doug Cartin’s Experience

  • Senior Partner

    Fenix Group International

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    January 2007 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Doug is a joint owner of Fenix Group International after 30 years of corporate and consulting experience. For the last 25 years, he has held a number of senior level positions from Director to CEO in 3rd party logistics specializing in finished vehicle logistics; automotive parts, newspaper distribution, chemical and other industrial sectors.These experiences cover the US, Europe, South Africa and South American markets and Doug has been extensively involved in Mergers and Acquisitions during the last 20 years in the 3rd party logistics industry.

    With his partners, Doug now specializes in the recruiting of middle and senior level executives for the the 3rd party logistics industry; industrial manufacturing and also provides business consulting to businesses nationally and internationally.
