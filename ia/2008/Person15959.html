Arlette Bout’s Summary

Servant-Leadership is a practical philosophy which supports people who choose to serve first, and then lead as a way of expanding service to individuals and institutions. Servant-leaders may or may not hold formal leadership positions. Servant-leadership encourages collaboration, trust, foresight, listening, and the ethical use of power and empowerment.

Being a Center for Servant Leadership ourselves, Just Working Better helps companies and their leaders to search and define their (new) mission, values and behaviour.
To achieve the best possible result we use the Culture- and Leadership-cycle. This cycle contains eight phases in which step-by-step the whole company helps to build involvement with the companies values, and personal values. The leader is a servant first: he can help the employee to grow and thus enables the company to grow…
