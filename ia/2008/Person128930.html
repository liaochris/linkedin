M. Grace Sielaff’s Summary

M. Grace Designs, Inc. is a Chicago-based interior design firm dedicated to providing customized, functional design solutions for commercial, residential, and hospitality clients.

As a member of the U.S. Green Building Council, M. Grace Designs proudly practices “Green Design” in an effort to protect the natural environment. Green Design refers to design and construction practices that significantly reduce or eliminate the negative impact of buildings on their occupants and the environment. Our own goals in practicing Green Design are to increase workplace productivity, enhance energy efficiency through energy-efficient lighting systems, and conserve
natural resources by educating our clients about earth friendly products.

Our team is comprised of project management professionals, architectural designer, and design assistants. Accomplished career histories across a broad spectrum of projects from large-scale and small; hospitality to urban mixed-use; corporate to residential. We view sustainability from a broader perspective as an integral part of our design process.

Published in Who's Green 2007. M. Grace Designs has been featured in national and regional publications and television shows including HGTV- Home & Garden Television, MTV Production, Chicago Luxury Home and Condo, StyleChicago, Garden Design magazine, Spectacular HOMES,Chicago HOME, Chicago Tribune, Chicago Sun Times, and the Business Ledger.
