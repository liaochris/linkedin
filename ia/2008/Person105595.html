Kevin Davis’s Summary

Kevin Davis is a managing partner for Crush Media Group, LLC., a Detroit based media company specializing in visual media, communications,and strategic planning. Kevin is in charge of the day to day operations, strategic planning, and business development for Crush.

A photographer as well as managing partner, his photography skills are in high demand for many special events and performances, in and around the Midwest region for many local and national celebrities. Kevin has had work published in Ambassador Magazine, Essence Magazine, and Black Enterprise Magazine and is a freelance photographer for the Michigan Citizen Newspaper. Along with being published in print media, Kevin provides content for a number of entertainment based websites.

Kevin is 1st Vice President of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc- Alpha Alpha Beta Sigma Detroit Alumni Chapter, Vice President of the National Black Public Relations Society, Treasurer for the National Pan-Hellenic Council Foundation, Redford High School Alumni Assoc., Eastern Michigan University Black Alumni Chapter, Michigan Democratic Party, volunteers with the American Cancer Society, and also Prince Hall Mason. Kevin has been acknowledged in the 2007 edition of Who’s Who in Black Detroit.

Some of the clients Kevin has worked with for Crush include, Oakland University, Eastern Michigan University, Wayne State University, Detroit Public Schools, The Michigan Citizen Newspaper, Ambassador Magazine, Urban Organic Music, G-Unit Records, After5Detroit, International Detroit Black Expo, African Americans On Wheels Magazine, College for Creative Studies, The Freedom Institute, Eastern Michigan Black Alumni, Mark England De Mode, Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, The Metropolitan Detroit National Pan-Hellenic Council, The Java Exchange Cafe, Singlesdetroit.com, Musical Architect, and more.

Kevin Davis’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, promotions, event coordination, marketing, photography, graphic design, and communications.
