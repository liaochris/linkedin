Imran Bashir’s Summary

I graduated from Illinois Institute of Technology with a bachelors degree in computer engineering. After working for companies like Panasonic, Siemens, Auvo Technologies and Kirusa Inc. as a software engineer I quit my job to concentrate on a software business. However, during this time working for various clients I stumbled upon this opportunity to run an e-commerce business of marketing air and water purification products. I believe that the timing was right for the air and water purification business in 2003. I started the company and was able to find good people to work me. Together we also managed to raise money from private investors. After the initial success in Canada, we are replicating the same business model in other countries. As of today (June 06, 2007), Heaven Fresh has its own offices and distribution centers in the Chicago USA, Toronto Canada, Nuremberg Germany, London UK, Dubai UAE and Jeddah Saudi Arabia. Heaven Fresh also exports products to several other countries through these distribution centers. I visited China last month and we are planning to establish distribution in Shanghai China as well. Our goal is get bought out or take Heaven Fresh public by 2011.

My personal interest still remains in technology. While working for Heaven Fresh we have developed a business model that can be replicated with a very small capital in new markets. With the success of each new Heaven Fresh office we believe that we have a good grasp on creating and managing 21st century "micro multinational" companies. Essentially, it is a process that can be developed into a software system. It's a massive project but it can be done and the software system does not exist at this point in time that other companies can use. I am working with some colleagues/friends to finalize a business plan seeking $10,000,000 in seed funding to develop ZanthOS (Yes, that's what we have named it) platform for 21st century businesses.
