Michael Habib’s Summary

My specialty and passion is designing relevant and usable social tools around content such as books, articles, and related metadata (citations, bibliographic records, etc.).

I apply the concept of faceted tagging to collect semantically valuable user contributed metadata; then employ concepts of faceted friending to connect people to situationally relevant content and contacts.

I am particularly interested in opportunities related to social software, online communities, tagging, faceted tagging, faceted friending, Academic Library 2.0, and Library 2.0. Please feel free to contact me about speaking engagements or consulting opportunities related to my specialties.

Michael Habib’s Specialties:

faceted tagging, faceted friending, social software, folksonomy, folksonomies, tagging, user studies, usability, interaction design, user experience design, marketing, library services (reference and instruction), Library 2.0, Academic Library 2.0.
