Monique Roosen’s Summary

Myers-Briggs personality type is INTP
I am inquisitive by nature and I enjoy challenges.

In the business area I'm intrigued and inspired at the same time, by the book "The inmates are running the asylum" (Alan Cooper). This still influences the way I think about Information Science. The book is about the darker side of technology's impact on our lives. Are we making users happier? Are we improving the process by which they get work done? Are we making their work hours more effective? The author questions current assumptions and mindsets. Plainly, he asserts that the goal of computer usage should be "not to make anyone feel stupid"!! In my opinion technicians still have too much influence on IT software and systems we daily use and thus running the show.

I've written 2 articles for a magazine called Informatie:
(1) "Strategische Alignment bij de provincie Noord-Brabant" (november 2004) and (2) "ERP ontwikkeling bij provincie Noord-Brabant" (februari 2007).

Business experience:
7 years as Information Science Researcher (Requirements Elicitation, OO-languages, reverse architecting, concepts of WWW etc.)
5 years as a product concept developer (innovative new concepts in the area of scanning/printing/copying)
7 years (and still counting) as a (innovation) consultant/projectmanager in the IT-area for the Provincie Noord-Brabant (government) ==> Business/IT alignment
