Gini Swancy’s Summary

I have the distinct pleasure of leading a company that is dedicated to helping women to embrace their appearance instead of being imprisoned by it. Women often find their bodies and overall appearance lacking because they are comparing the way they look to the looks of very young, retouched and unrealistic images in magazines and ads. Our company provides encouragement, information and products that help women to look and feel great about themselves.

We speak to groups of women in corporate settings, spas, charitable organizations and women's conferences every month. Our model includes sharing our vision with as many women as possible to help them understand the impact of their thoughts about appearance and then guiding them to find clothes that help them feel their best. We have been fortunate to be invited to speak to thousands of women in different venues each year.

When women shop with us, they shop by individual shape and personal style. The clothing that they find on our website fits into real lifestyles and on real bodies. We work very hard to make women happy with what they can find in our online store. We also help women with how an item is going to fit.

We work with other business' to provide anything that we think will improve the mindset and allow a woman to embrace her appearance. There is beauty in every single woman that I have had the pleasure of meeting.

Gini Swancy’s Specialties:

My company is the only image consulting firm in the United States offering women a choice about how they work with their personal image. We have developed programs, services and patented products that allow women to take charge of their appearance and make it work for them. We are all about empowering women whether it is a client who decides to work with us or use our program, or a woman who wants to become an Relationship Manager and joins our company to open up another market.
