Dawn Block, dawn.block@comcast.net’s Experience

  • Principal

    dawn block consulting (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Computer Software industry)

    January 1995 — Present (13 years 4 months)

    With over 25 years of recruiting & HR experience in the Silicon Valley area of northern California, I established my independent search & HR consultancy in 1995. I specialize in the recruitment of executives and senior level talent for software start-up companies. Successful searches have included the hiring of dozens of executives, including Presidents, COOs, CFOs, CTOs, as well as Vice Presidents of Engineering, Marketing, Sales, Professional Services, Finance and HR. I’ve also hired hundreds of exceptionally talented candidates at the director, manager and individual contributor levels.

    In a related capacity, I also deliver general HR consulting to clients who require expertise in compensation, benefits, training, employee relations, hiring/firing, legal compliance, meeting facilitation, etc. On an as-needed basis, I often conduct HR "tune-ups" for companies who don't have regular access to an HR professional.
