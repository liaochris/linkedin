Nicholas Henry’s Summary

Nicholas’s goal for your web site is to deliver a clear business result. Whether the result is to register a website visitor for an event, convert them to a sales lead, or to sell them a product--Nicholas identifies how to persuade your visitor by defining web sites that speak to their needs and answers their most important questions.

With an education in both business computing and the arts, and over 10 years experience in the software-related fields, Nicholas has a unique skill set that qualifies him to use technology to convert a website visitor into your next customer. To ensure he keeps his skills relevant, and provides you with the most up-to-date advice, he continues his personal education in the following areas: consumer psychology, direct marketing, copywriting, usability and web analytics.

Nicholas has consulted for a range of commercial and non-profit organizations including: Clinivision (Tyco Healthcare), Sandman Sleep Systems (Tyco Healthcare), IS2 Medical Systems, Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP), House of Commons Security Services, Oscadca Group, Mounted Police Foundation, and Demarle at Home (US).
