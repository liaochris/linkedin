Matt Hopkins’s Summary

I'm a pro-active serial entrepreneur and senior executive with expertise and experience in both marketing and technology.

I founded Vertical Leap Ltd in 2001 (www.vertical-leap.co.uk) - a leading search marketing company based in the UK with other offices in Australia and the USA. Core expertise is in search engine optimisation (SEO), paid search marketing and local search. Much of our success is based on great customer service, our proven, repeatable business process, and the proprietary decision support technology we use to track and manage our campaigns. No one manages search marketing campaigns better.

Previously, I was Managing Director and significant shareholder of Dunstan Thomas Holdings Ltd (www.dthomas.co.uk) - a specialist solutions and consulting services provider to the UK pensions market with sales reaching £4M. Originally a bespoke/custom software developer, Dunstan Thomas is now one of the leading software and services suppliers to the UK pensions and financial services markets.

I also have interests and investments in a number of small online and traditional businesses.
