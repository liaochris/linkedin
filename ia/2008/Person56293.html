Edye Deloch-Hughes’s Summary

I've enjoyed a fun and fulfilling career as a writer, mentor, manager and leader in the advertising industry. Now I am focusing my creativity and leadership skills toward fulfilling my Life Mission:

“To ignite, inspire, motivate and educate others, so that those I touch may be a point of light to others".
