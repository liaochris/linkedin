Nick Zink’s Summary

I joined Cramer-Krasselt in early 2006 to creatively oversee the agency’s interactive development across its four offices (Chicago, New York, Milwaukee and Phoenix). With a fine art and heavy graphic design background, I bring a great deal of detail and attention to C-K’s online marketing and motion graphic work. I’ve worked for clients such as Bissell, TV Guide, Yellow Tail wine, Corona, MGM Grand Las Vegas, AirTran Airways, Monroe Shocks and Porsche USA.

Along with hands-on design, art direction and design management duties for the network, I spend my day-to-day managing the department’s Flash development & design team on a multitude of online projects (including websites, microsites, banner campaigns, graphical email campaigns, custom desktop applications and various other interactive marketing elements).

Prior to joining Cramer-Krasselt, he worked for a Phoenix, Arizona based Interactive agency as an Interactive Art Director on both large scale and boutique websites across the U.S. for clients such as Hilton, Marriott and their many subsidiaries.

I have over 15 years of experience in the on and off-line marketing and advertising industry, bringing a well-rounded perspective to my work.
