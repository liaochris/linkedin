Pete Rivera’s Summary

Peter Rivera has been working in IT for over 14 years and with Microsoft Corporation for the last 8 years in their DoD Practice.

Currently Peter is a Program Manager over Microsoft's multi-million dollar Navy and Marine Corps business. He is responsible for managing engagements, services sales, and people for Navy and Marine Corps customers. This includes people management of Microsoft Technical Account Managers (TAM), Senior Consultants, Premier Field Engineers (PFE) and partners. In addition Peter uses his technology knowledge to engage in high level discussions, by mapping a customer’s business problems to end-to-end technology solutions. This means having to interact with senior level technology decision makers on matters related to agility, business value, and return on investment (ROI).

Pete also manages sensitive and business critical issues impacting the Navy/Marine Corps Business or Microsoft's relationship with the customer. These discussions typically require the highest Security Clearance levels and deal with very sensitive information requiring need to know and confidential interaction.

To be credible Pete must have current and deep experience with the implementation of large-scaled secure enterprise computing environments including directories, messaging, collaboration and secure development environments.

In his own words "The security and preservation of our nation’s interest are critical to the safety and prosperity of the United States. Having worked with the Army, Air Force and now with the United States Navy and Marine Corps I have a strong passion for providing a vital role in supporting the successful and accelerated achievement of objectives for customers within the Federal Department of Defense (DoD) landscape."
