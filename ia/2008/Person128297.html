Melvin Simpson’s Summary

I have been in or around the entertainment market for most of my career. Recently I've been consulting on new media projects, mainly entertainment or sports.

I'm interested in talking to anyone about new assignments or permanent roles, or just because they're interesting...

Clients/assignments include:
* Acting Managing Director for a startup video archive and social networking site
* Virgin Digital - project management of new online store and subscription service
* Arsenal Football Club - creation of soccer skills website
* Premier League - development of "I Know The Score" game (over 60,000 users)
* 2 entertain - strategic research for UK's leading video company

I was involved in two startup technology companies. At KASL Enterprises, I developed business, marketing and product strategy for an online content and promotional platform based on USB flash drives. Prior to that, I was Chief Marketing Officer and UK Managing Director at Internet plc, where I developed a smart card internet content platform including Britney Spears, Star Trek and Disney content.

I spent five years in communication agencies, founding my own in 1997. During this time I won and delivered over £2 million of client business including substantial projects with BBC, Dixons Group (including The Link and Mastercare), The Marketing Society, Our Price, Universal Pictures, Video Collection and Virgin.

In my early career I held marketing and acquisition roles in music and video companies, where I launched/acquired a number of successful video (primarily children’s, sport and special interest) and audio (including Disney, PolyGram, Sony, and Virgin labels) properties, and worked closely with major entertainment organisations and retailers.

Melvin Simpson’s Specialties:

I'm primarily a marketer, and have spent most of my career in media and entertainment. I have agency and client experience - leading teams and projects, developing internal and external relationships, managing programmes and delivering solutions. I’m passionate about effective communication.
