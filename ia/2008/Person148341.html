Paul Vereijken’s Summary

Paul Vereijken is a freelance journalist and student journalism (Fontys Hogeschool Journalitiek in Tilburg, The Netherlands).
During his study he worked as an trainee at the editorial staff of Dutch newspapers Dagblad De Limburger and Limburgs Dagblad. After his internship at Dagblad De Limburger he worked there as a reporter for three months.
Most off all he is a 21-year old guy, with loads enthusiasm and creativity, that wants to learn and experience all he can about journalism and media.
