Thomas F. Comparato, Jr.’s Summary

A proven strategist with a dynamic blend of leadership, business knowledge and delivery expertise within the Product and Program Management domains, Tom brings Sunquest Information Systems more than 26 years of high-tech industry experience. While at First Magnus, Tom designed and implemented a very successful global, distributed, offshore development model with partners in India. Because of his superb execution and PMO delivery oversight, the DEV-IT program expanded beyond portfolio core competencies, and took on major strategic initiatives across the business that allowed the company to expand market share and revenue growth at an unprecedented pace.

After relocating to Tucson, AZ in 2003, Tom’s product marketing, management, and product positioning abilities served as the catalyst for rebranding Financenter, Inc into a new eMarketing solution provider (known today as Leadfusion, Inc) which provides over 250 of the world’s largest financial service providers with online eMarketing technology.

Prior to FinanCenter, Tom served as the Senior Director of Product Management and Marketing for Aprisma, Inc. During his tenure, he led a thirty one member team that transformed the company from an “engineering centric” to a “market driven” organization and his strategic efforts made a dramatic and positive impact on how the company designs and develops software products.

Outside of his recent track record of accomplishments, Tom brings with him a wealth of well rounded prior experience in OEM Management, Strategic Planning, Advisory Board, Consulting, AOP Development, Pricing, Operations, Project Management, Master Scheduling, Manufacturing Capacity Analysis, Technical Steering, and Program Management. He established dedicated tenure with companies such as Newmarket International, Keane, Inc., Heidelberg Web Systems, Bolt, Beranek & Newman, and Avco/Textron Systems. Tom earned his bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Westfield State College.
