Alexander Cameron’s Summary

Alexander Cameron is an acknowledged technological leader and visionary who has been described in the national press as “one of the greatest minds of his generation”. He founded the award-winning managing director of specialist IPTV/Video On-Demand consultancy Digital TX limited, based in Mayfair, London. In less than 5 years he has overcome a tragic youth and risen to become an authoritative and revered commercial master entrepreneur trusted by a network of hundreds of the most influential business leaders in the UK, earning him the title of “super-connector”.

His charismatic style and approach is incredibly unique, being laid back, acerbic, irreverent and rebellious. As a young upstart being renowned for his fearlessness, he is an entertaining and attention-grabbing public speaker and commentator whose punchy delivery and shocking bluntness has given him an unrivalled record approval rating scorecard. He is regularly interviewed in the trade and industry press, and is the author of a series of articles on digital media that have become seminal reference points for companies all over the world.

Alex is the author of 3 books and a large number of screenplays, where his interests spread to religion, personal development and social commentary. His PR staff describe him as “every PR’s worst nightmare because his don’t care/won’t care complex” and his fast-moving conversational style has been known to bring people to near exhaustion. He works regularly with a number of charities and has been a mentor to a large number of his peers. Despite suffering depression, thyroid dysfunction and a troubled past, his passion and determination are palpable and infectious.

Alex also spends considerable time in Westminster and Brussels where he is a highly-respected member of several Parliamentary committees focusing on media, technology and telecoms. He is a member of 3 out of 5 key London and university debating societies and is a regular panellist at industry conferences.

Alexander Cameron’s Specialties:

TV and music content management, sales, licensing, brokerage and distribution. IPTV, video on-demand (VoD), emerging interactive new media and TV technologies. Private equity, investment and fundraising.
