Jesse Hartgring’s Summary

I am a corporate generalist attorney specializing in providing legal support in joint ventures, acquisitions (shares or assets), buy outs and participations.   • Beroepsopleiding / Advocaatstage Orde van Advocaten bij de Hoge Raad der Nederlanden

    Beroepsopleiding / Advocaatstage 2003 — 2005

    A lawyer will begin his career with a three-year 'traineeship'. During the traineeship, he exercises the practice under the direction of an experienced advocate. The traineeship is an interaction between education and daily practice.

    In the first year, he will follow the Professional Education Programme for the Legal Profession. In the education programme, the emphasis will lie on knowledge of the procedural law and the Code of Conduct and the mastering of skills typical of the advocate, such as pleading, the hearing of witnesses and the preparation of litigation documents.

    In the second and third year of the traineeship, he will take the (partially compulsory) Continued Education Programme for Trainees (VSO).

    In addition, most districts also have their own education requirements, such as participation in plea competitions and attendance of lectures.

    (See: http://www.advocatenorde.nl/english/education/traineeship.asp)
