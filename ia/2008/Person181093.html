Andy Sternberg’s Summary

I am a skilled producer and editor of online content with a commitment to quality, journalistic integrity, and search engine optimization.

I'm a recent graduate of USC's Annenberg School for Communication (M.A. '07) with a Master's in Online Journalism. I am a freelance and contract digital media consultant, content producer, multimedia editor, and Web optimizer.

I am currently manager of online communication and digital assets for Warner/Chappell Music, the publishing arm of Warner Music Group. I also consult on the development and production of non-music-related Web sites and research projects, and volunteer as News Editor for LAist, one of the most popular city blogs in Los Angeles.
