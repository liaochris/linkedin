Lisa Morgan’s Summary

I provide executive research, planning, writing, and professional development services to leading and emerging vendors, service providers, agencies, and industry associations. Over the past 19 years I have worked with numerous pre-venture, pre-IPO, private and public clients on short-term and long-term strategic projects occasionally serving as VP or SVP. Since August 2006 I have been SVP and Principal Analyst at Online Market World.

In addition to working with clients, I have contributed, often on a regular basis as a feature writer or columnist, to Software Development Times, Practical eCommerce, E-Commerce Business, The Washington Post, InternetWeek, Planet IT, Wireless Developer, Network World, InformationWeek, Internet World, and Mobility. I am the author of How to Expedite Your Career Through Publishing, (IntelliPress Publishing, 1999) and co-author of Implementing Internet Security (New Riders, 1995) and The Internet Security Handbook (IDG/Mecklermedia, 1995). Most recently, I was a key contributor and an editor of The E-Marketplace: Strategies for Succeeding in B2B E-Commerce published by McGraw-Hill.

I also co-host a radio show on wsRadio entitled, "Online Market World Radio with Lisa Morgan and RSS Ray" which features e-commerce movers and shakers. To listen live, tune into wsRadio.com at 3:00 p.m. PT on the third Wednesday of every month or download the podcast free.

Since 1994 I have also served as an industry speaker and/or awards judge at executive, literary, and high technology events in the U.S. and internationally. Past and present engagements include Online Market World, the Professional eBay Sellers Alliance Spring Summit, Networld+Interop, Internet World, The Waterside Conference, Internet Alliance, Ishikawa Alliance, and the Software Information Industry Association Codie Awards.

Lisa Morgan’s Specialties:

Business development, strategic and competitive research, corporate analyses, M&A strategy, marketing planning, online and offline content development.
