    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 1998 — Present (9 years 7 months)

  • Director of Technology

    (Educational Institution; 501-1000 employees; Education Management industry)

    September 1997 — September 1999 (2 years 1 month)

    Having written a technology grant proposal that won the largest dollar-per-student award in the State of California, I was transferred from District Grant Writer/Evaluator to position of Technology Director. Over the next three years I managed the installation of a state-of-the art technology capacity in every classroom in every school in the district. I managed the process of developing a innovative web-based approach to standards-linked, project-based learning. I selected a team of 15 "early adopter" teachers representing a spectrum of schools and grade levels and worked with them to develop pedagogical methods for effective deployment of technology as an instructional support. I supervised the Netwok Administrator and Tech Support staff.

  • Grantwriter/Evaluator

    (Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Education Management industry)

    September 1996 — September 1997 (1 year 1 month)

    Developed systems to evaluate all grant-funded programs in the District. Also developed methods to closely anticipate STAR test results, and to produce API data in advance of the state's publication of these data. Successfully authored major grants; for example, a grant to transition two schools to dual-language immersion, and another to implement a comprehensive and innovative technology program. I brought in so much grant money so fast that the district declared a moratorium on seeking new grants until it could absorb the significant changes resulting from the new funds. I also worked out a system to combine a small percentage of all district revenue sources to create a full-time, dedicated evaluator position, for which an experienced, doctoral-level evaluator was hired. I then transitioned to Technology Director, where I managed that project.
