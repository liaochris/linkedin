Jof Walters’s Summary

I am an innovator and entrepreneur and an open networker. Over the past 15 years I have delivered market leading products and services in financial services and related markets.

I have recently completed the purchase of leading UK e-commerce provider Shop Creator.

I speak Mandarin, German and Japanese to GCSE level (that's enough to find a restaraunt and eat!).
