James Dennis’s Summary

At Sonik IT Solutions we offer a wide range of IT support and computer services to small businesses. We have grown steadily over the 10 years gaining a reputation for being helpful and responsive.

In the 21st Century technology is at the heart of your business success and the success of most SME businesses. Getting the right IT infrastructure requires expertise that for many years has been beyond the reach of small and medium sized businesses. At Sonik IT Solutions we offer a wide range of services to help you reach the level of expetise that you require. Whatever technology related advice you need, we combine corporate levels of knowledge with an intimate knowledge of small business reality. Our ability to consistently deliver the right solutions at the best prices, has made us one of the most respected IT Companies in the South East.

We have lauched our new SME Support packages, these are specially designed to attract organisations that are small through to the medium sized business that is looking for good quality cost effective support services.

There are four levels of support available for Companies in the Herts, Essex, and London areas, starting with the Bronze level for as little as £75 per month. The 4 levels, Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum have increasing amounts of support facilities built in that include, on site visits, telephone support, email support, remote support backup support, free software advice and procurement, free security audit and consultancy.

We have many "Bolt On" products and services where we partner with other Companies to help provide these to our customers. These include Remote Management of your PC or Entire network, broadband, VOIP, Hosting and Web Services, Data Recovery, Anti Virus and Backup Services. More information is available on our website, or you can contact us via Telephone or Email.
