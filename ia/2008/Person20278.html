Michele Bassett’s Summary

Michele Bassett has an extensive history in recruiting Chief Executives with search experience across the consumer, entertainment, new media, technology, and not-for-profit sectors. In addition, Ms. Bassett specializes in sales/marketing, corporate compliance/ethics, and general counsel functional roles. Most recently, she has conducted searches for clients such as Constellation Wines, Sony, Disney, Mattel, Pitney Bowes, PG&E, First American Corp. and Wolfgang Puck.

Previously as a Principal with Battalia Winston International and cFour Partners, Ms. Bassett placed top level executives in venture backed, pre-IPO, emerging growth companies in the high-tech, digital media, consumer, Internet and entertainment industries. Prior to that, Ms. Bassett was a consultant leading engagements for the technology and professional services practices of Ray & Berndtson, a top ten international executive search firm.

Ms. Bassett spent her early career in advertising account management with global advertising agencies based in Los Angeles. She managed client relationships with Jenny Craig, Hollywood Video, GTE, and Nestle, delivering advertising, branding, direct marketing and media strategies to major US corporations. She received her Bachelor of Arts degree in English from the University of California, Riverside and her MBA from the Marshall School of Business, University of Southern California. Ms. Bassett resides in the sea-side town of Manhattan Beach and enjoys her ocean view, wine tasting, golf, and beach volleyball.

Retained Executive Search Services in the following practices: technology, professional services/legal services, not-for-profit, compliance & ethics officers.
