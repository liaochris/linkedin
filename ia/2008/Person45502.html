Dave Guerra (dave@corpusoptima.com)’s Summary

Discovered and is the leading exponent on the theory and practice of Superperformance. Regularly addresses and consults with executives from some of the world’s leading companies, and conducts workshops and transformation campaigns based on this knowledge for a wide range of organizations.

Over several decades, has consulted extensively in the areas of large-scale change, operational excellence, and the transformation of corporate culture.

Past and current clients include Administaff, Baker Hughes, Brinks, ConocoPhillips, Emerson Electric, Exxon Mobil, HCA, HP, ITT, Motorola, Texas Children’s Hospital, Tenet Health, and others. Has partnered with CSC, Dale Carnegie, Deloitte Consulting, Motorola University, University of Houston, and others.

Works include the groundbreaking business book Superperformance: New Profound Knowledge for Corporate Leaders, as well as Moving the Needle: Conversations with a Superperforming CEO. Along with these, responsible for numerous related white papers and professional articles and currently at work on several additional books on the subject.

In high demand as a conference and keynote speaker, and over the past several years has introduced Superperformance to a wide range of business audiences on the local, national, and international stage, including ASQ, ASTD, CLO, International Center for Spirit at Work, ISPI, IMC, ODN, and SHRM.

Has contributed to or been quoted by a variety of well-known publications including Business Week, Forbes, Industrial Management Magazine, Journal of Performance Improvement, and Market Watch.

Dave Guerra (dave@corpusoptima.com)’s Experience

  • Corpus Optima Consulting

    Corpus Optima

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    April 1996 — Present (12 years 9 months)

    Corpus Optima Consulting provides business optimization projects, transformational business campaigns, and management and leadership consulting services designed to take performance to unprecedented levels. The Superperformance Proverb: Manage Process, Lead People is the essential heuristic for achieving outperforming results in operational performance, customer satisfaction, employee fulfillment, and shareholder delight.

    Since 1996, the firm has advanced large-scale initiatives for several major clients, including pioneering the “Superperformance Business Transformation Campaign”—a portfolio of products and services designed to apply the principles of Superperformance in organizations.

    In this capacity, Dave led the strategic planning for and provision of enterprise solutions for clients across a variety of industries including Chemicals, Oil & Gas, Technology, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Financial Services, Internet, and Nonprofit Industries.

  • Corpus Optima Learning Systems

    Corpus Optima
