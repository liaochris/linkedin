Billy Cain’s Summary

Billy Cain is the Studio Producer at Heatwave Interactive and remains one of the owners of Critical Mass Interactive, Inc in Austin, Texas. He has been in the industry since 1992, working on titles from Ultima to Wing Commander, EA Sports titles, SpongeBob SquarePants, and many more. Mr. Cain has worked on many gaming systems beginning at the SNES and Genesis, progressing through 3DO, PlayStation 1, 2, and 3, GameCube, Xbox 360, and of course PC, single player, multiplayer, 'serious games' and 'casual games.'

Billy Cain’s game industry career began in 1992 at the legendary development studio, Origin Systems, where he was ultimately responsible for the successful shipping of Electronic Arts’ multi-million dollar franchise title, Wing Commander: Prophecy. Since then he has designed, developed, produced or contributed to over 200 popular games, many of which have won numerous industry awards, including the Nickelodeon Kid’s Choice "Favorite Video Game" Award winner "SpongeBob SquarePants: Revenge of the Flying Dutchman" and Spike TV’s “Best Wireless Game” award for Vivendi Games’ Mobile action title SWAT Force. His experience working for companies such as Electronic Arts, Sony and THQ and on various game systems has given him great insight into what makes a great product; business cycles and trends; market dynamics; networking; technology; teambuilding; and process efficiency, with a focus on delivering high quality work on time and on budget.

Mr. Cain is a frequent speaker at international game-related conferences and universities and is a founding member and/or board member of numerous game related educational institutions and associations. Always an advocate for game developers and education, Mr. Cain was involved in the creation of the Austin Game Conference, the Texas Independent Game Conference as well as the Game Education Summit. He also volunteers at GameCamp.org, a summer camp that trains children, ages 10-17, to make video games.

Billy Cain’s Specialties:

Video game design, production, and creation (any platform or style), team building and management, video game education and speaking engagements.
