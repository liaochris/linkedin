BIG LLOYD LUNA’s Summary

He has been inspiring more than 50,000 people every year, writing more than 100 business and management columns around the world, and speaking to more than 10,000 audiences on career and personal development around the country.

He was described as “the man on effective career planning” by The Manila Times, “a life-saver for those who are confused in the uncertain, always-changing workplace” by The Manila Bulletin, “inspiring real-life story-teller” by ABS-CBN’s Karen Davila, “a very young, visionary entrepreneur who is becoming a strong brand in the industry” by RFM Corporation’s Joey Concepcion, “meaningful and relevant author” by Jollibee’s thinker Manny Lumba, “collegiate ambassador for peace” by the World Collegiate Association for the Research of Principles.

“Big” is a columnist of The Manila Times, Good News Pilipinas in Singapore, Kabayan Star Newspaper in Hong Kong, and The Filipino Times and Asian Review in the United States, a business consultant to start-up companies, Internet strategist to top 500 corporations, and the website architect of GONEGOSYO.net.

His other books: Do You Have a Life of Your Own?, Break Me Up, and Tachniques. He is the co-creator of The Roads to Success and the bestselling author of the book Is There A Job Waiting For You?

The president and CEO of LLOYDLUNA Communications and the founder of Webdeology.com
