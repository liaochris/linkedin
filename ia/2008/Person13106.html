Sarah Bird’s Summary

- Currently Company Director & Animator for AnimNation Ltd
- Organiser for the Brighton Animator's Networking Group (BANG) for Wired Sussex.
- Co-organiser for the Flash Brighton group.
- Founder and organiser of the Vine Brighton business networking group.
- Former Lead Animator at Blue-Zoo productions.
- Worked on a number of high profile animations for childrens television, adverts, corporate companies and animated short films.
- Creating BAFTA nominated animation & a gold Promax Award for "Best Childrens Promo" for her work with Blue-Zoo Productions & Red Bee Media on Level Up.
- Organiser of the Brighton Animators Networking Group through Wired Sussex & co-organiser of the Flash Brighton group & founder of the Vine Brighton Business Networking Group.
- In 2006, Sarah was asked to help set an industry brief for the final year BA Multimedia students at Sussex Downs College. Around the same time, she was asked to do a talk & be part of the industry panel at Brighton & Hove's City College media careers day - Navig8.
- In 2004, Sarah was awarded funding by UK Film Council/Screen South to create an animated short film. It was shown at cinemas in UK and went to many worldwide film festivals.
- In 2002, Sarah was featured on BBC South News promoting the National Centre of Computer Animation at Bournemouth University & her animation work was shown as part of the news piece. The same animation then got listed in the BBC Southern Counties Film Festival.

Maya, Adobe After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere, Flash, Dreamweaver, HTML, C & C++, Javascript.
