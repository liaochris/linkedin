Josh-Daniel Davis’s Summary

I take things apart and find problems. This started with phone jacks, power plugs, and my first computer (age 6, 1981, Commodore 64).

Since then, I've learned to put things back together and solve the problems I find.

Right now, I'm a travelling consultant and am pretty content with that. I'm IBM Certified in TSM Implementation, TSM Administration, AIX Advanced Technical Expert, pSeries Enterprise Support, Virtual I/O, p690, etc.

In the past, I primarily focussed on UNIX and Windows (Open Systems), as well as networking (Cisco, Ascend, Livingston, generic), though you can find Retail, Food Service, and Construction in the beginnings of my resume.

I generally qualify as "Geek" and work on infrastructure.

My primary goals are to learn, have fun, and earn for retirement.
