Arthur Bushkin’s Summary

My professional activities center around "giving back" my expertise and other resources to the betterment of society. I am interested in projects and opportunities that have a social good component. I tend to focus on activities related to information technology and services, although not exclusively. I am particularly interested in exploring partnerships or other collaborative endeavors with people or organizations with like interests and goals that could benefit from an affiliation with the Stargazer Foundation and the Stargazer.org Online Service for Social Good. We are always seeking help in promoting Stargazer's capabilities.

Contact me at: ArtBushkin@Stargazer.org

Visit: www.Stargazer.org - to explore Stargazer's Safety, Learning, and Collaboration capabilities. Learn how you can make a difference.
