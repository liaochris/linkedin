Matthew McLeod’s Summary

I first entered the IT industry 10 years ago from a technical management perspective. I was initially responsible for system deployment and maintenance, client support, external projects and the development of the internal intranet software. Through my last four years of independent development / consulting, I've greatly expanded my skills base, and become a highly proficient developer for the Windows platform, and an expert as regards web applications.

I have accumulated my skills almost entirely through work experience, with the notable exception of my development methodologies, architecture and software engineering knowledge which were first acquired in training, and then honed through habitual use. My independent consulting provided me with the experience and skills necessary for leading small integrated teams - meeting clients' needs for rapid system development and delivery.

I’ve had extensive industry exposure, both in terms of meeting the needs of practical business operation, and in fulfilling the almost all the roles required for a successful system's development. I am now ‘fluent’ in 10 application programming / scripting languages, and proficient in numerous technologies / platforms.

My preferred methodology closely adheres to several best-of-breed development and modelling practices - RAD, UML architecting, JAD, MDA et al. My use of these methods has been refined by my tenure at Inspired, an IT research and consulting firm that specialises in corporate architectures and development methodology for many South African corporates.

I have a model approach to application design and development, solid business acumen and generous team leadership experience.

Matthew McLeod’s Specialties:
