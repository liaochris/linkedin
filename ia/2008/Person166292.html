Randy Weiner’s Summary

As the Vice President of Production at VitesseLearning, a San Francisco-based eLearning company, I was responsible for the delivery of all production and development services and for designing and implementing the functional processes and systems to support our growth from $4mm in service revenues in 2003 to over $18mm in 2006 and beyond. I directly managed seven Director-level reports, representing the Instructional Design, Content Development, Resource Management, Flash Development, Quality Assurance, Research and Development and Technical Strategy Departments, and had over 100+ distributed full time and contract production resources within my purview.

I have deep experience in defining, engineering and implementing RIA and software development processes for globally-oriented programs. I pride myself on my ability to balance the demand for innovation with the requirement to deliver profitable services on time and on budget.
