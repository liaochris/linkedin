Vickee .R. (LION) TopLinked.com’s Summary

After spending a couple of years as a freelance graphic designer based back in Wellington New Zealand I decided to get back into the office environment, starting of as the Office Manager for Spherion Recruitment (now known as Verossity Recruitment), from there I was quickly promoted into a Candidate Manager position where I established a fantastic and solid grounding of being able to 'think outside the box' on how to find that 'spot on' candidate. I later moved agencies and into a fully fledged 360 degree Consultants position - here I was bought on by a NZ owned and operated company to develop, build and maintain their IT division.

After a year it was time to take the next step up in my career and I relocated myself and family over to Sydney and into a Team Leader position, where I managed a team of 4. Over the past year I successfully built up the team from 4 consultant to 7 consultants and 1 resourcer.

I am a very committed and professional individual who strives to produce a high quality service to both my candidates and clients.

If you are a candidate who is looking for their next challenge or just have a query about the state of the job market at the moment, or are a client looking to secure the services of a professional and ethical recruitment consultant/agency please don't hesitate to contact me.
