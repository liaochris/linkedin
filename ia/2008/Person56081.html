Alex Mordas - Tallahassee EcoBroker®’s Summary

I have over 12 years in the Environmental Field, having served on the Board of Directors of multiple NGO public health groups and non-profit organizations. During my Masters degree (in Ecology & Evolutionary Biology) i was a fellow with both the Smithsonian Institution and the US Environmental Protection Agency. I am an EcoBroker® and licensed REALTOR® with the Town & Country offices of Keller Williams in Tallahassee, FL. If you need to buy or sell a home in the Tallahassee area, I can help to find, develop, or highlight green aspects... including locating energy efficient financing. I'm also an experienced green home certifying agent with the Florida Green Building Coalition, and I am working on my LEED AP certification. With my extensive list of professional contacts in the area, I can help you with any home related issues that you may have..

If I can be of help in any way, please contact me at: (850) 559-4976 or alexmordas@kw.com
