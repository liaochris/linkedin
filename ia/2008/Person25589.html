Eric Bostrom’s Summary

Procom Services - "Bringing Technology & People Together".

We work internationally placing high performing resources throughout North America, South America, Europe and Asia. We have four offices in the United States, eleven in Canada and offices in Europe, Asia and Australia through joint ventures with offshore IT services firms like HCL Technologies. We do not specialize in any one particular skill set. With over 3,000 contractors currently on assignment and over 180,000 qualified North American resumes in our internal database, we cover a large number of skill sets. This includes mainframe and client server technologies along with positions ranging from business analysts, architects and project managers to desktop support, help desk and network engineers.

Since 1978, we have been finding high-quality jobs for our carefully selected candidates. Our policies of maintaining open communication with both clients and candidates, consistently make Procom Services the IT firm of choice. We have a track record of providing top quality candidates at very reasonable and competitive pricing to companies both large and small. All component rates are market competitive. We have highly automated back-office and front office systems that enable efficient submission and tracking of candidates at a low cost.

Procom Services is a Certified Gold Partner of Microsoft.

We are currently hiring people with the following skills: Java / J2EE developers, .NET, SQL Server, Microsoft SharePoint 2007 developers, and multiple SAP consulting positions. Send resumes to ericb@procomservices.com.

We are also HIRING top notch recruiters! Send me a resume if you excel as a recruiter.
