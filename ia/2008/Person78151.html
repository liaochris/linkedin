Hugh Finnan’s Summary

I moved to the US in 2001 after working my way up to senior management for Microsoft Ireland. I joined MS Corp as Worldwide Test Manager for .Net Enterprise Servers with teams in Europe and E.Asia. This role covered such products as SQL Server, Exchange Server, Biztalk Server and Commerce Server. Once my goals were met in this position I was approached by the Exchange Server team to take the role of IPM for the 2003 release. I took the role as it allowed me to get closer to the technology, and add value directly to the US product. Once this product shipped I got an opportunity to join my passsion for aviation with my love for software development by becoming the multiplayer core feature PM for Flight Simulator X.

After completing the functionality for FSX and 10 years at MS, I was approached by Amazon.com to come build a team and help them launch their M@ business in Europe and Japan. I believe I have built the strongest team of Program Managers at Amazon who will continue to do great things for this company. We have delivered on all launches assigned to us to date, on schedule and within budget.
