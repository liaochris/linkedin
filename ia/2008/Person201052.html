Ramona Wright’s Summary

Ramona Wright, a principal consultant at Mozaic Media + Communications (MMC), is the ultimate “connector” and teaches public relations at Loyola Marymount University (LMU). She is a key influencer and one degree of separation from anyone you want to know. Ramona has managed multi-million dollar budgets and has over eight years of international experience in public relations, influencer marketing, social and multi media. She has worked with such noted companies, organizations, and brands as Cialis ® , TribalGlu, Film Independent, and the Milken Institute.

Ramona is a communications expert who develops creative ventures that are unique to the MMC brand and on behalf of clients who are seeking exclusive business development and branded entertainment channels. Her academic background crosses continents and includes Oxford University and Loyola Marymount University's School of Film and Television.

Currently, Ramona is attached as the associate producer to THE KID STAYS IN HIP HOP. Along with Garth Trinidad — KCRW radio host — Ramona is the co-creator and co-host of MYHANGSUITE.COM — a Web 2.0 venture that includes on-line radio programming, video content and a live event series centered around urban alternative music and culture.

A committed civic leader and lecturer, Ramona has had numerous speaking engagements. Microsoft invited her to be a featured speaker for their "Youth Innovators" panel at their 2008 Imagine Cup. In 2007, she was a keynote speaker for Microsoft’s Tech Goddesses initiative and became the spokesmodel for Ziba Beauty and Sheer Cover. Ramona is affiliated with such national and international organizations as the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA) and the Rotary Club. Her commentaries, speeches, BLOGS, and creative works have been published in the Congressional Record , Black Enterprise Magazine, Vital Issues: The Journal of African American Speeches and the Journal of Popular Film and Television .
