Kenny West’s Summary

Mr. West founded PalmGear.com in 1997, credited with being the first mass market downloadable software portal, with the introduction of the first Pilot 1000 by Palm. Through Mr. West's leadership and passion, PalmGear grew to become the leading Palm OS® applications directory worldwide with more than 14,000 registered developers and 15,000 applications by 2002. Mr. West sold majority interest in PalmGear to Power By Hand Holdings in October 2002 which then merged with PinPoint Networkds in June 2004, now known a Motricity.com.
