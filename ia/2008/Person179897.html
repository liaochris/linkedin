Sarah Steele’s Experience

  • Structural Analyst

    Northrop Grumman

    (Government Agency; 1001-5000 employees; Aviation & Aerospace industry)

    June 2004 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    Responsibilities include structural analysis for both metallic and composite skins & panels. Tasks include detailed analysis, finite element analysis, layout sizing, structural integrity evaluations, durability and damage tolerance of aircraft structures, material allowables development, acoustic/dynamic evaluations, and weight control. Responsible for analyzing airframe parts and the assembly & installation for the F-35 JSF Center Fuselage, material review board (MRB) dispositioning of non-conforming structure, proof test & full scale test assignments. Involved in the development & testing of in-house NGC analysis tools such as PDB, IBOLT Runner, and FERIT. Serve as SSOR Point Of Contact (POC) with primary tasks including: attend and participate in all coordination meetings, communicate requirements & schedules, work with the Lead Engineers to ensure resources are assigned to active tasks, ensure consistent inputs are delivered, and provide accurate EVMS tracking.
