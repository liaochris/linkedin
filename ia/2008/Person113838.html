Linda Maichl’s Summary

A partner in the Cincinnati office of Ulmer & Berne LLP, my practice predominantly is in the area complex products liability litigation involving pharmaceuticals and medical devices, including state and federal appeals. My practice also includes litigation involving insurance coverage issues, antitrust, bankruptcy, securities law, admiralty and intellectual property. I have assisted companies with administrative and federal regulatory and compliance matters, including FDA approval for marketing products and compliance with manufacturing, marketing and advertising regulations.
