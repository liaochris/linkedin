Jim Holley’s Summary

Human Resources professional with particular expertise in executive search. Mostly I have focused on high tech with strong experience in telecommunications, software, and networking. I have both US and international experience and have filled positions in Europe and the Pacific Basin. My professional focus has been in sales, product marketing, engineering, business development and human resources. My avocation is teaching, and I have an adult credential in Teaching English as a Second Language. I am an adjunct faculty member of Canada College and have taught ESL in Pescadero since 2005. I also have a start-up language school that targets ESL training in company environments.

Jim Holley’s Specialties:

My executive search work has also led to my offering consulting in staffing and selection practices. I have also acted in a coaching capacity to human resources executives, particularly those in fast paced environments. I include new executive coaching as a part of my search practice.
