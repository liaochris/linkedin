Noah Starr’s Summary

Noah Starr

Noah Starr is an actor, writer, teacher, foodie and all-around swell guy based in New York City. He recently returned from a whirlwind tour of Europe where he hosted, wrote, and produced a series of comedy videos for LifeStyles condoms.

Noah appears regularly in commercials, on Late Night with Conan O'Brien, and has been featured on E!, AMC, MSNBC, MTV, and in the upcoming feature film Nail Polish. He can also be seen (and heard) in numerous commercial spots and PSAs and performs at comedy at venues all over New York.

Noah has written for Disney, Paramount Pictures, McSweeney's, ad campaigns, and World Wrestling Entertainment. He is currently working with Emmy Award-winning Catman and Mary Productions to create new children’s programming.

Noah hails from beautiful Camden, Maine.
