Joao Morais’s Summary

An experienced IT Architect from an engineering background. My 13 years of experience which was gained in Finance, Telecom, Publishing, Cinema/TV and recently Retail sectors, has given me a good understanding of project life cycle and the importance of using Architecture to deliver efficient IT systems and processes.

I have a proven track record of leading the design and development of scalable systems, the migration of systems, and systems fit for “business continuity”. My management experience includes managing small teams and recruiting.

I have passion for Software development, Enterprise Architecture and like challenging project team while also remaining flexible and adaptable.

Communication and coordination of tasks, people and priorities. Finding and delivering solutions.
