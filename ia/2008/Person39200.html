Colin Brown - LION - Colin[at]teksource.ca’s Summary

I ACCEPT ALL INVITATIONS TO CONNECT.

At TekSource, my technical recruitment responsibilities include but not are not limited to the following:

* Work directly with Human Resource departments and hiring managers to develop and implement strategies to overcome challenges associated with the identification, attraction, assessment and retention of candidates for junior to executive level roles in Research and Development, Telecommunications, Information Technology, Security, Finance, Accounting and Sales.
* Manage the requisition process including authoring performance based job descriptions, posting to major and niche job boards as well as professional and social networking sites, sourcing candidates through various resources; screening and assessing applicants, administer technical testing, prepare formal presentations, conduct formal and informal reference checks, security checks, present letters of offer, etc.;
* Salary and per diem negotiation for both candidate and client;
* Research and evaluate new avenues of sourcing and attracting candidates throughout Canada and the US;
* Assist clients with the improvement or development of their employment brand to increase candidate pipeline through their dedicated employment sites;
* Constantly endeavor to come up with new and innovative ways to improve the recruitment function and ensure industry best practices are being used.
