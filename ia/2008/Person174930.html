Shelley Stile’s Summary

Professional Life Coach: I work with individuals who are looking to maximize their potential and achieve specific goals in their lives, with a concentration in the area of divorce support for women. I work to help women survive the divorce process and then go on to create new and vibrant lives. I work with women both during the divorce process to support and guide them as well as divorced women to move them forward towards a new life after divorce, one that is fulfilling, purposeful and happy.

I am a member of the International coaches Federation and the International Speakers Forum. I am an instructor at the Adult Schools of Montclair, Ramsey, Summit, Milburn and The Chathams, New jersey as well as a staff instructor at the Center for Women in Livingston, New Jersey. I am also a staff coach for Harv Eker's ('The Millionaire Mind) Life Directions seminar.

Shelley Stile’s Specialties:

Divorce, Lives in Transition, Second Half of Life Direction.
