Rutger van der Linden’s Summary

van der Linden ICT specializes in Business Technology Optimization, the IT arena where applications, systems and business processes are tested and/or monitored and performance is analysed, all to the benefit of the customer's IT ROI, minimization of mean time to resolution of IT problems and making transaction/process performance transparent to customers.

For purposes of performance testing we employ the HP-Mercury product range, amongst which Loadrunner, J2EE Diagnostics, Sitescope, Business Availability Center and Mercury Application Mapping.

We advise and cooperate on test strategies leading to effective analysis of capability and stability of various environments, which may contain elements of Oracle, WebSphere, Weblogic, SAP, Siebel and many more.

Rutger van der Linden’s Specialties:

Systems/application testing (performance, load and stress), performance and availability management, systems integration consultancy.
