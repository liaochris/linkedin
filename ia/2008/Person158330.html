Mayur Jobanputra’s Summary

Since graduating from SFU with a Business degree in 1996, I have spent the last 10 years working in the IT field. I have gained extensive experience with Technical Services and Support, Technical Writing, QA, Release Management, Web Development, Web server management and much more. I have experience managing networks and software systems large and small: everything from a 10 PC small business networks to large Enterprise class banking servers running Microsoft Datacenter.

More recently I am actively using VMWare virtualization technology and manage a farm of 50+ virtual servers on a daily basis as part of my responsibility as a Release Engineer for Intrawest. I manage all builds and deployment for QA and Production and UAT environments using NANT, Visual Studio, and Installshield. I am also actively involved on-site for Production releases and play a key role in ensuring availability of our online services.

In my spare time, I enjoy creative work as a graphic and web designer providing everything from business cards, logos, to web sites for small businesses. I design web sites in ASP, PHP, SQL, mySQL, Photoshop, and occassionally Flash. You can visit my professional blog at www.redprimary.com for more information about me.
