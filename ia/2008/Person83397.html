James Windrow’s Summary

Comprehensive experience directing all aspects of the marketing mix from strategic brand planning to tactical implementation including forecasting, market research, new product development and launch, budgeting, campaign development and pricing.

Skilled in ecommerce best practices and innovation with an emphasis on persona development, persuasion and information architecture, user-centered design, multi-variant and usability testing, copywriting, landing page and search engine optimization (SEO), managing pay-per-click campaigns and interpretation of web analytics. Successfully directed the planning, design, development, testing and launch of multiple websites and intranets ($250 thousand to $1.5 million budgets).

Developed and directed the successful launch of several multi-million dollar marketing campaigns in various highly competitive industries, both B-2-B and B-2-C. These campaigns used multi-channel promotion, including social media, to target consumers, CXO’s, managers, and influencers (doctors, consultants, analysts, agents, press, and bloggers) as the key to establishing a competitive advantage in highly educated and affluent markets.

Proven ability to define and leverage customer hot buttons, product positioning and pricing strategies to reduce cost-per-acquisition and increase revenue.

Energetic leader and mentor with strong communication, presentation and team building skills.
