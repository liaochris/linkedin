Ian Webster’s Summary

Ian has over 20 years experience in business project/programme management, largely gained within the retail financial services and IT industries. Ian spent the first part of his career at Barclays Bank, with whom he held a number of managerial positions, before moving into increasingly strategic, consultancy and project/programme management roles. Immediately prior to becoming an independent contractor, Ian spent three years with London-based consultancy Charteris plc where he built upon an established reputation for project and programme delivery with a number of blue chip financial services clients and smaller organisations. A natural stakeholder manager, Ian is equally comfortable & credible at board, senior/managerial and operational levels. He is able to translate strategy into action in order to deliver demonstrable business results through successful project and programme delivery. Ian holds an MBA and is MSP accredited.

Ian Webster’s Specialties:

Project and programme management of complex business and IT changes; Payments and retail banking; Business benefits modelling/realisation; TCO and cost reduction; IT service management to ITIL standards; IT and business outsourcing and third party supplier management; Business planning; Mergers and Joint Ventures (M&A); Construction of complex business cases; The design and implementation of new organisational models/operating models;
