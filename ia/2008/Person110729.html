Larry Ross’s Summary

Larry Ross is a founding member and veteran of Broadway.com Television, Hollywood.com, Hollywood.com Television, Commerce One Global Services, and the Sci-Fi Channel. His passion is developing new digital media platforms, creating compelling content, and leveraging social media to market entertainment, lifestyle, B2B, and B2C enterprises. Larry is driven by ideas that engage people and can change industries, and is interested in advising or working with startups, innovators, and established companies.
