John Brander’s Summary

Some of you know me as John Eriksson which was my name up until 2003 when I married Elin and took her family name Brander as our own.

Most of my expertise consists of working with internet based solutions for information logistics, be that web publishing, intranet or structure of large bases of information.

I have worked with editorial web publishing since 1995, where I was part of building on of the first editorial intranets for Telia Mobitel. This company expanded heavily during the mid 90s because of the take of of mobile phones. It was really an exciting time where we could try a lot of new stuff. This I did as consultant, working for my own company.

I joined KÂPe Communication 1996 where I continued to work with internet publishing, but also with output in the print channel which added a lot of new things to consider while building the systems. I now worked as project manager and account manager, and later was chosen to be part of the management group when the company grew to about 30 employees.

I co-founded June AB with three other partners all contributing with our own expertise but sharing common values, interest in publishing and above all, sharing the dream of building the perfect company. This dream have evolved now during over seven years, and nothing really beats creating something yourself - that is the thing I love most.
