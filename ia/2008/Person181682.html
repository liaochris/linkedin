Steven Petersen’s Summary

PROFILE

Steve is currently focusing his supply chain and systems expertise on providing ERP ROI and Demand Planning & Forecasting consulting services to importers, manufactures, and distributors in Southern California. Steve also provides Business, Executive, & Life Coaching to a variety of clients.

Steve’s skill set is truly unique having spent the first 15 years of his career in various Information Technology capacities in the Aerospace industry leading to his most recent 11 years as an Executive, directing Supply Chain Operations in the Consumer Packaged Goods industry.

As a Supply Chain Executive he created, developed, and directed global supply chains and has extensive global sourcing, manufacturing and logistics experience having identified suppliers and sourced various consumer goods products in Mexico, South America, Africa, and Asia. The customer base Steve supports includes Wal-Mart, Target, Home Depot, and Lowe’s.

His primary focus within IT was the creation, implementation, and management of ERP systems giving him functional knowledge spanning the entire organization. Steve has extensive business process development, analysis, and reengineering expertise.

With over 15 year’s Aerospace industry experience he possesses in-depth functional knowledge and expertise in JIT, TQM, Kaizen, Lean, Continuous Improvement, and government contracting.

TARGETED OPPORTUNITIES

Importers, manufactures, and distributors seeking to improve their ERP system utilization to increase customer order fill rates, minimize inventories, and better manage and control their organization.

TARGETED NETWORKING CONTACTS

Business owners, CEOs/ Presidents, COOs, Supply Chain & Operations VPs and Directors
