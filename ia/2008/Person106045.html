Kevin Sturm’s Summary

As a Professional...
I am a successful and driven person with experience in finance, operations, R&D, and marketing. I'm passionate about technology and products that are functional first, but look so sexy you just want to use them. I'm a technology evangelist and love that technology will continue to change the world…and what an awesome ride to be part of it. I love new ideas and working with people that have an entrepreneurial spirit. I believe the customer is always right…except when they’re wrong, and that doing what is best for your customer is always what is best for your company.

As a Consultant...
As a consultant I know that nice people get ahead. You have to be smart, too…or better yet surround yourself with smart people. The core value of a company is the the core values of the people that work with and for it. I have a knack for turning troubled projects around and finding good people to help make it happen. I enjoy motivating and mentoring people, and have a proven track record of building fiercely loyal teams to achieve great project success. I believe the most important thing you can be to clients is open and honest...always.

As for Me…
One of my favorite things to do is talk about new ideas; even if they never happen. But seeing them happen is what makes it fun! I love spending time with my family and friends, being outdoors, and thinking I’m a pretty good athlete. I know a lot about a few things and little bit about a lot of things. Half the time I know what I’m talking about and the other half I just sound like I do. I pride myself on having integrity and accountability. I’m in no way flawless and screw up at least once per day…okay a bunch of times per day. The mistakes I do make have the upside of allowing me to learn…which gets me experience…which makes me a better person. Life is full of choices, and we have the opportunity to live life for enjoyment or regret. I choose enjoyment!
