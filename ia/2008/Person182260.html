Steven Wood’s Summary

Strong Microsoft based technical design and development skills with team leading, mentoring and client-facing skills developed throughout career. I have been involved in a large number of projects in a variety of roles, from solutions architect to developer. Below are various highlights from my career.

As part of a 5 strong developer team delivered a web based census application and was responsible for the showcasing of the beta and final versions to the Venezuelan government.

After successfully being awarded a Smart Award from the DTi, a grant given to fund innovative product development. I designed, implemented and maintained a fully working software application over the period of 9 months.

I have also authored technical documents, a sample of which can be found at http://www.15seconds.com/issue/001102.htm. This article has been widely read and resulted in me receiving web service technical consultancy work from a US based company.

Recently at HBOS I provided mentoring skills and was also responsible for the technical delivery and management of a 100,000 mailing shot designed to attract new Halifax customers to Halifax online. Further to this I also presented the HBOS E-Commerce department at fraud prevention workshops.

I have also applied W3C standards and usability guidelines to private and public sector projects.
