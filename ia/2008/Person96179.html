Jonathan Duarte - GOJobs.com’s Summary

Started GO Jobs, Inc. in 1996. In 1997, started posting our clients jobs to multiple job boards. As the years progressed, we added more and more job boards. Now GO Jobs posts jobs to over 2,000 job boards.

Our focus is on creating and implementing Internet Recruiting Strategies, including using mult-purpose national job boards as well as researching and posting jobs to over 10,000 Niche or Industry specific job boards.
