Michael Bergeron’s Summary

Software development manager with over 11 years of management experience creating software used in a variety of products ranging from embedded to large enterprise systems. Some examples of software products I have either developed or managed include: 3D real-time graphical simulations, embedded SW for tape cartridge validation, a parts inventory system, and a video watermarking system. I’ve also managed the development of software for consumer electronic devices including DVD players, cable set-top boxes and digital video recorders.

I am very interested in multi-media and video and have extensive experience in the IPTV, cable, TV and satellite industries.
