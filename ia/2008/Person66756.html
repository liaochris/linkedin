François-Xavier Charpentier de Beauvillé’s Summary

Francois-Xavier Charpentier is a Senior Information Technology Leader with 12 years of experience in several industries including financial services, publishing, and information services. Broad-based experience includes IT department management, IT project management, IT governance and best practices, IT strategy and leadership, enterprise software application development, complex systems design and implementation, and M&A IT due diligence and integrations.

He has delivered accomplishments in global IT organizational management, architecture development, technology roadmap development, Internet and Interactive product development, IT asset management, hardware/software planning and acquisition, end user training program management, and infrastructure development.

His management background includes success in turning around troubled IT departments and projects, establishing new departments and international offices, developing robust IT processes, building cross-disciplined relationships, and managing international professionals. He has conducted business in Hong Kong, France, Germany, Australia, Canada, Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Japan, the US, and the UK.

Francois has been successful in processing $1.3 billion in M&A transactions volume, capturing $25+ million in top-line revenue by leading acquisition integrations, designing a fund analysis product that enabled employer to tap into a niche market, improving a $3 million IT infrastructure, creating a new technology platform to support a strategic business alliance, contributing substantially to revenue growth, building web-based applications, and much more.

Francois’s career is underscored by a Bachelor of Business Administration and an Associates of Management Degree acquired from academic institutions in France. He also earned an ITIL Certification, a Public Speaking Certification from Toastmasters, and a Professional Certificate in Financial Analysis from New York University.
