Paul JJ Payack’s Summary

High Technology: Mastered surfing the technology waves for more than two decades.

Deep experience at launching new products and technology in new market spaces or deeper into current markets, including: product creation, launch and positioning, brand creation, communications, public relations and program management.

Global Language Monitor: Created a proprietary algorithm, the Predictive Quantities Indicator (PQI), that tracks word usuage in any database. The PQI is a weighted Index, factoring in: Long-term trends, Short-term changes, Momentum, and Velocity. As such it can create 'signals' that can be used in a variety of applications.

Paul JJ Payack’s Specialties:

Specialist in strategic thinking, planning and execution: building awareness, driving revenue and share price; building market share on a global basis.
