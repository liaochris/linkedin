Treve Wearne’s Summary

Provide strategic business consultancy services to companies in the Information and Document Management market that are repositioning their business to address market consolidation and competition. Major clients include Avnet and Kodak.

Co-founder of a £3m turnover document management business with presence in both the UK and US. Grew company from inception to sale. Company became self-financing after just 12 months, created an excellent annuity revenue model that allowed for growth and development within the business. Consistent ability to achieve in sales and contract wins.
