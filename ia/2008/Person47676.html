David Stein’s Summary

Act III is an independent division of Geiger, the largest privately owned promotional products distributor in the US. We can print your company name or message on thousands of different products, from caps & t-shirts to pens & magnets. I work with a wide range of customers, from global 1000 to startups, so I understand the unique marketing needs of different organizations. Tell me your marketing goals and I’ll give you ideas that get you there!

A native of Brooklyn, NY I live in Manhattan with my wife, Michelle Raum, and my son Daniel, a student at the Oberlin Conservatory, majoring in Jazz Performance, double bass. My daughter, Rebecca, lives in Brooklyn and is working on her Masters in Social Work at NYU.
