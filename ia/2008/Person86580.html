  • CEO

    AlbumCity.com

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    August 1999 — October 2003 (4 years 3 months)

    I was a co-founder and CEO of this B2C e-commerce retailer modeled on Amazon.com. Managed a staff of 24 in three offices in Central Europe (Prague, Bratislava, and Warsaw). I wrote most of the software for a cross-border, cross-language, e-commerce platform. Site was written in Perl and Java, with Oracle as the RDBMS. PL/SQL was used extensively to speed up database operations. U.S. patent was filed for inventions resulting from this development.

    I personally raised $2.2m from private investors to fund company’s operations. After the “bubble” burst in 2000, funding for e-commerce companies dried up, and AlbumCity.com had trouble raising funds. Company closed in June 2003.
