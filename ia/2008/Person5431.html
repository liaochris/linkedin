Alan Whitford’s Summary

Alan Whitford has over 20 years experience in the start-up and expansion of high technology businesses in Europe. Abtech provides strategic and practical insights to the automation and web-enablement of Human Resource and Recruitment Process activities. Alan is a frequent chairman and participant in European conference programmes. He has been published in a number of on-line and off-line media covering technical and strategic HR and Recruitment issues.

Alan and his partner Keith Robinson are launching Recruitment Community Europe to serve the European staffing and recruitment sector with a range of vertical community applications and services. Our aim is to partner and stimulate debate, best practices and knowledge sharing within the European Recruitment community. The signature event was the 4th annual Global Recruitment Conference in Amsterdam on 11-13 November 2008. www.globalrecruitmentconference.com

Alan is a founding Partner in Dutch technologist Classified Solutions which developed a new generation of recruiting technologies, using matching, extraction and a unique job and skills taxonomy. Kangarooster was the first of its initiatives to provide Web 2.0 solutions to candidate and recruiter alike that encourages true candidate relationship communications. The KangaTechnology product set for the staffing and corporate recruiting community will include a 'white label' out of the box solution which can power job boards and corporate career sites.

Whitford served a 2 year term as an Advisory Board member of Hire.com, was a founding board member of the European Chapter of the HR-XML Consortium and is a founding member of the UK ORMC (On-line Recruitment Marketing Council).
