Dustin Telford CBET, CRES, CLES’s Summary

Biomedical Technology Management Professional focused on patient safety, innovations in technology, best technology practices, and advocacy in the field.

Professional experience includes multi-hospital account management and supervision, multi-hospital project management, western states regional field service, clinical research and investigation, educational program development, and founding department operations.

Professional goals include shaping the biomedical technology management career field, advocating and intiating patient safety practices and initiatives, and fostering quality performance in individuals and the biomedical industry.

Dustin Telford CBET, CRES, CLES’s Specialties:

Specializing in customer service oriented operations, systems and organizational redesign, and enhancing the development of professionals in the field.

Technical specialities include high-end imaging systems, laboratory analyzers, lasers, anesthesia and intensive care ventilators, physiologic monitoring, and hemodialysis equipment.
