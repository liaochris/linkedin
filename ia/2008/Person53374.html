Donni-Jay De-Ville’s Summary

Able to record in a few takes, perfect pitch, perform ‘live’, with/without guitar, Classic Rock, Blues/Country songs, Big Band Swing, Soul, Jazz, Pop, Originals, 40's - to present day, Strong lead vocals, & harmonies, having five octaves, perfect pitch. 20+ yrs of stage/recording experience, professional solo dancer.

Survived nearly unbelievable, non-stop action, but extremely exciting, past life. Trauma & triumph. These experiences helped me to be successful as an 'agony aunt' column writer, in UK magazine, and as an article writer. Finishing autobiography this year.

Completed thriller, eBook novel, at present, to be paperback. Compilation of short stories, some of which will be script-written for plays, or TV. Soon to write self-help books on correct ‘attitude‘ to prevent looking ‘old’ whilst naturally ageing slowly, remaining young looking. My best exercises and weight training tips, shared. Another publication soon, on caring for tiny dogs, house training without scaring them. I am an expert!

Enjoyed being a talent show judge, invited on showcase panels. Also being a vocal and image coach, have, and can help those aspiring to go into show business. The way I still look is my reference! Although 'mature', look around late thirties and still shapely, with no implants or face lifts. I have been a professional dancer for many years and have enjoyed weight training most of my life. I still regularly lift for strength, in our home gym. (My hubby weight trains and is my personal instructor.)

* Prolific writer on many topics, serious and unusual with a recognised style.
