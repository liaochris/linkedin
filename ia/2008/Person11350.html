• Managed the introduction of Cisco’s wireless Internet access system for small and medium business and residential applications

• Managed the development and introduction of the first wireless PBX product line on the US market

• Structured, negotiated and closed OEM or similar business partnerships with LM Ericsson, Motorola, Safer Systems and numerous other companies
