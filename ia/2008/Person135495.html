Morgan Farris’s Summary

I am a hardworking, get-things-done Graphic Designer and Department Manager for Trimedia Atlantic. I've designed ads for popular brands like Car and Driver, Nissan, Neutrogena, and Corvette.

Professionally, my goal for joining LinkedIn and expanding my network is to connect with new opportunities and projects within the new media industry. I strongly believe in collaboration, and that it goes both ways.

Trimedia Atlantic specializes in content creation in audio, video, and web. We are Dolby 5.0 audio and High Definition video equipped.

What makes Trimedia a great company to work with?

* Creativity to carry the weight of your vision
* Experience to produce high quality content
* Ability to work within tight deadlines
* We're a fun group of people to be around

Have a project that needs experienced audio, video, or programming professionals? Just send a message, I'd love to hear from you!
