Overview
Dr. Baidya has an overall 10+ years of experience in the bioventure business development. He founded BioZak, Inc. in 1999 and served as one of the founding management for the past five years. At BioZak he provided the strategic vision for the business development and funding. He directly worked with primer legal firm for Private placements and maintained strong relationship with the investors as well as building a strong well-rounded IP-base.

He serves as advisor to several technology-based companies, e.g., International Technology University, Golden-Embryo, Pune/India; and CEMAC corp., Durgapur/India.
