Jeff Lyndon’s Summary

Nominated in the Hong Kong Government (OGCIO) sponsored & hosted by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council: “Person of the Year of Hong Kong Digital Entertainment Industry” at age 24. Jeff Lyndon is known as Hong Kong’s new generation digital entertainment pioneer.

With over 7 years of hands on experience with the digital entertainment industry both local and world wide, Jeff has helped materialize the launching of more than 17 titles locally and internationally.

My motto: Think outside of the box but in the same time, fill up that box as well!
