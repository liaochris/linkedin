Korry Richards – Designer

About

I see industrial design as having three major fields; one is the ability to design an innovative product in with a creative solution, two designing that product with the manufacturing process in mind to cut production cost and lastly marketing that product to make it more appealing to an everyday consumer. I enjoy designing because with these three aspects in mind of providing a product that solves a problem, designed to also save costs to manufacture and marketed properly so that it stands out against all competition develops rewards that are immeasurable. Being a part of that process of designing those products that encompass these three aspects is what I want to do. I feel as though my drive and design insight will be beneficial in your workplace.
