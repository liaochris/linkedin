Aryo Widiwardhono’s Summary

After few-years stint in a consultant company and Astra International - Strategic Management Group, Aryo joined Unilever Indonesia (topping The Asia BusinessWeek 50 list in 2007) in 1997. He enjoyed various positions from market research specialist, brand building & brand development both in Home and Personal Care and Food, to general management.

He started as a Research Executive for a number of brands in HPC then moved to Market Measurement and shoppers understanding.

He moved to marketing in 2000 as the brand manager of OMO cream detergent where he managed to double the size in just one year. He was then appointed Product Group Manager in Foods managing a new acquired Bango soy souce and other organic growth projects. He intensively involved ensuring a smooth brand operation during transitional period of the acquisition while creating the brand's first TV advertisement in 78 years.

In 2002 the Board of Directors appointed him Executive Assistant to the CEO. During his 2.5 years stint, he had an opportunity to witness a first hand bird’s eye view on how the business is led and worked with two outstanding CEOs. In his finals months he assisted the CEO and Board transition and worked with the new CEO.

Other than his 'official responsibilties' Aryo had also played key role in the Unilever Indonesia's transformation journey by leading the organisation of several company's big mobilisation events.

In 2004, he was assigned to Unilever Italia in Milan and assumed the role of Global Brand Manager, working in global brand development team. In 2005, he moved to Unilever Plc and continued doing his work from the Headquarter Office in London. He was then appointed Global Category Manager Oral Care in 2006. Aryo rejoined Unilever Indonesia in 2007 to lead Hair Care business there.

Over the years he has attended many Unilever training and courses in Indonesia, Philippines, India, Italy and United Kingdom.

Aryo Widiwardhono’s Specialties:

Brand development, brand building, category portfolio management, innovation project management, general management, consumer insight and market measurement.
