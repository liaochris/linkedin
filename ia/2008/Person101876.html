Kanani Moser’s Summary

As the Director of the Technology Division for TriStaff Group, my team focuses on careers within the technology sector. I’m a headhunter for positions ranging from software engineers, architects, sales and marketing professionals, and C-level executives for companies in the San Diego County area.

I was also recently promoted to Vice President of Business Development for TriStaff Group's other business divisions. Those include: clerical, executive administrators, HR, manufacturing and call center staffing, finance, media, publishing, energy, clean technology, and hardware, mechanical, and electrical engineering.

Kanani Moser’s Specialties:

Industry focuses include: web 2.0 technologies, social media advertising, enterprise software, wireless & telecommunications, security software, analtyics, medical device, embedded systems, data warehousing, and storage solutions.

Clients include both successful Fortune 500 corporations & Pre-IPO startups, and positions include: hands-on technical engineering roles as well as VP, CTO, CIO, CEO, etc. I also place candidates working in Sales, Marketing, & Business Development roles.
