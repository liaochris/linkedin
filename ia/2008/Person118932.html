Manlio I.A. Frizzi’s Summary

I begin working in 1999 as Junior System Engineer with Compaq: most of the customers were financial companies (Caboto Holding SIM, Banca Akros, Gestnord, Banca Intesa, Banca Popolare di Milano). In 2001 I continue working, directly as Brain Force Software, as Senior System Engineer in Banca Popolare di Milano. In October 2004 I leave BPM, and start working in HP Team in Telecom Italia (Rome, Italy) as VmWare Specialist: the project was about consolidating about 200 servers in their server farm on VmWare ESX architecture. Now, after a migration to Windows 2003 project in Vodafone (Milan, Italy) with Microsoft Team, I'm working in Credit Suisse for consolidating the Italian Microsoft Windows 2000 domain in the new "planet wide" Domain of Credit Suisse Asset Management.
From April 2006 to July 2006 I've worked with HP in Vodafone Italy in a consolidation project of about 200 servers with Vmware ESX.
From May 2007 to July 2007 I've worked with HP in Banca Popolare di Vicenza in a consolidation project of about 60 servers with Vmware Virtual Infrastructure 3.0: I've implemented HA/DRS cluster for the new environment and setted up the VCB feature.
PM in a DR project with VmWare 3.0.2 and EMC Storage (syncronous replication) from September 2007 to November 2007 in Schenker Logistic Italy.
Virtualization specialist (October- November 2007) for consolidation project of Uvet Infrastructure (implementation of all the ESX infrastructure + VCB with Backup Exec 11).

Presales Engineer for our new VAI product Suite ( http://www.vai.nl ) and Design Architect for Vmware Projects.
