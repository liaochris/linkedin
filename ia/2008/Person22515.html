Beverly (Bev) Kennedy, MBA, CISSP, CISM, CISA’s Summary

Beverly S. (Mr. Bev) Kennedy is a manager, a technologist, and a security specialist with a history of providing effective and secure technology solutions. His background in accounting, software development, databases, Unix/MS servers, and networking. provides a sound foundation for his IT Management and Security work. His undergraduate degree in Business Management (1997) and his MBA (2001) are from Southeastern Louisiana University. Recent projects have included developing a ground up Information Security Department including Single Sign On, Access Control, and Identity Management as Information Security Officer for a major Health System. Bev consulted at Tulane University in New Orleans for several years on their campus network and Information Security. He still finds time to teach his course at Tulane on Information Security. Bev holds the CISSP, CISM, CISA Certifications. He says “I enjoy meeting organizational needs with the secure use of technology.”
