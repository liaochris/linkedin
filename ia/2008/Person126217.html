Maxwell Finn’s Summary

I am still in college and am only 18 years old, but have had over a year of business management experience. Since September of 2007 my partner and myself have been building a company, MOHKAlife, from the ground up. We have met with dozens of the top business consultants in the country, all of which loved our business plan and model and one of which sits on our advisory board (Mike Deimler of The Boston Consulting Group). We have been accepted into Atlanta's best law firm, Alston and Bird. We have built a team of 5 talented employees, one of which is the lead website developer for CancerQuest. We have had at least two 4 hour meetings a week since September, which shows our dedication. We have created a business plan, media kit, 3 year financial outlook spreadsheet and other documents. In other words, I have had a massive amount of exposure to the business world over the last year and have learned a great deal. MOHKAlife is now being built and our BETA site will launch by December. In the mean time we are meeting with some of the biggest clothing lines in the world to sell our idea and future services. We are also taking the proper actions to increase our exposure to the public and increase our Search Engine Rankings, which I am talented at, as I have done over 4 months of research and real world testing on SEO. I plan to build MOHKAlife into a hundred million dollar company one day and have it be a household name. I am also the president of a startup holdings company called the ZMF Group, which will be a diversified company utilizing the talents of college students. More to come on this in the future, but I plan on building ZMF to a multi-billion dollar company one day.
