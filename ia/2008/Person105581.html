Kevin Crimmins’s Summary

Since joining Software Impressions in 2006 to lead the company’s sales and marketing efforts, Kevin’s role has expanded to include stewardship of the company’s business strategies as well as management of external relationships of all types. Kevin also handles the company’s legal matters as our in-house counsel.

In the mid-nineties, Kevin’s interest in the technology that makes practitioners more effective led him to transition from private law practice to a path that has combined his passion for and experience in sales with his legal expertise. As a first step on that path Kevin joined a leading legal and regulatory publisher, marketing and selling legal reference materials and law practice-related automation solutions to law firms, corporate legal departments and government agencies. After nearly a decade in a variety of roles with that organization, Kevin moved on to become a director of sales with a web-based solution provider focused on legal matter management and electronic billing solutions for corporate law departments and insurance claims organizations.

Kevin holds a Bachelor’s degree in Management from Bentley College in Waltham, Massachusetts, and a law degree from The John Marshall Law School in Chicago, Illinois.

Kevin Crimmins’s Specialties:

Technology, compliance, law department managment, organizational dynamics, management, privacy, employment law, human resources, intellectual property, contracts and commercial transactions.
