Rich Barnhart’s Summary

Entrepreneur with extensive experience in all aspects of Internet marketing, web site architecture and software development. Interested in consulting for small to medium sized growth oriented companies. Focus on Web 2.0 Technologies, Search Engine Optimization and eMarketing.

Rich Barnhart’s Specialties:

Internet Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, Search Engine Marketing, Integrated Marketing Communications, Customer Relationship Management, Demand Generation, Real Estate, Aviation, Education, Learning Management

Rich Barnhart’s Experience

  • Co-Founder, SVP, CTO

    Pharmacy Choice

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Pharmaceuticals industry)

    September 1999 — August 2008 (9 years)

    Rich Barnhart founded Pharmacy Choice in 1999 and quickly launched 3 of the most popular Internet destinations in the pharmacy industry - Pharmacy Choice (portal), RxCareerCenter (job board) and RxSchool (eLearning). In addition, the company started a recruiting and staffing division called Rx Pro Health. In 2007, Pharmacy Choice and Rx Pro Health were sold to a large public company.

  • Founder, President

    iNet Property

    (Real Estate industry)

    January 1994 — September 1999 (5 years 9 months)

    iNet Property was started by Rich Barnhart in Denver in 1997. Previously, Rich spent several years working for Colorado’s largest real estate firms (Van Schaak and Moore & Co.) and had successfully launched and managed one of the first real estate web sites in the country. Focused primarily on buyer representation, iNet Property relied entirely on Internet marketing to drive the business. In 1999, Rich decided to fine-tune his eMarketing skills in another industry. During that time iNet Property remained dormant and it is now being reorganized to service the professional real estate community. Rich is anxious to share his knowledge and skill sets with this community while reintroducing the iNet Property brand.

  • Founder, President

    Alpine Aviation

    (Aviation & Aerospace industry)

    January 1992 — July 1994 (2 years 7 months)

    After receiving his commercial pilot's license in 1991, Rich worked as a flight instructor for several small flight schools and operated an aircraft appraisal business under the name of Alpine Aviation.
