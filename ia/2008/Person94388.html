John Reynolds’s Summary

If you are passionate about your project, idea, or company and looking for creative ways to make an impact online, I'm your man! I specialize in developing alternative online presence, branding, marketing, and PR strategies. Using New & Social Media technologies, alongside proven mass communication methods, I will help you deliver a brand message that generates maximum awareness, absorption, and retention of an idea, service or product. I'm especially excited to work with/for non-profits or small businesses looking to give back to the community and contribute to positive social change.

I have over 10 years of experience using, remotely administering, diagnosing, and repairing Windows and Macintosh systems. I also have a few years of experience with various Linux systems and servers. I strive to bring a personal and friendly atmosphere to computer support while being respectful of a client's time and money. In addition, I am always happy to provide practical knowledge and training for those clients that would like to be further informed!

Don't hesitate to get in touch to discuss your individual needs, and find out how I can assist you and your business.
