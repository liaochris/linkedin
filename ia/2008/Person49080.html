Declan ONeill’s Summary

Prior to moving to the US in 2005, Declan was a business solutions manager with extensive experience with the premiere telecommunications company in Ireland, Eircom. Declan worked with Eircom for six years, where he managed formal bids and proposals, implemented business sales strategies that supported company growth, and identified optimal solutions for his corporate clients.

One of his notable achievements was selling the first corporate MPLS network in Ireland, for which Declan won the coveted Solutions Manager of the year award in 2004. He also gained experience while working with a telecommunications company in Australia prior to Eircom and gained much insight into web solutions as an intern with an Irish web design company.

Today, Declan is a Regional Account Manager with 352 Media Group. Declan helps new businesses make the transition to the web and more established companies define their web presence by offering tailored web solutions. While reciently moving to Michigan (Ann Arbor). One of his goals is trying to establish a Michigan foothold with some automobile industry business. Declan also serves clients in the northern regions of the country, Canada and Europe.

Declan continues to follow his interests in playing football (soccer), as a hobbyist web designer and extreme sports enthusiast. And and two marine fish tanks which keep him very busy.
