fran jurga’s Summary

My primary experience is as a niche-carver. Armed with tools I didn't know I had--curiosity and creativity--I have created two careers for myself that are unique and shared by almost no one else.

The first is as an editor, writer, and public relations person in the high-flying world of horse sports...but with the finely-focused goal of enlighteningi the world on the biomechanical abilities of equine athletes...and the tragedy and suffering of their injuries and diseases.

The second is an exhibit design consultant whose job is to be the storyteller and create a clear, easy-to-follow narration through museum, zoo, and aquarium exhibits. Using photography, multimedia, interactive computer kiosks, audience participation and good old text on the walls, I've told stories that translate complex scientific concepts into simple, strong images and clear, resonant sentences.

fran jurga’s Specialties:

Writing for print, web, blogs, and screen, editing, story development, photo and art research, artifact acquisition, meeting planning and development, event conception, book packaging and proposals, marketing and advertising, all forms of public relations.
