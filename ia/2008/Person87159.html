Jeff Briggs’s Summary

Jeff Briggs was Founder, President, and CEO of Firaxis Games from 1996 until 2006 when he negotiated the acquisition of Firaxis by Take Two Interactive.

Jeff played a central role in the birth and evolution of the computer game "Civilization" -- one of the industry’s most valuable intellectual properties. He wrote text and music for CIV I, was producer/co-designer/composer of CIV II, designer / executive producer of CIV III and composer for CIV IV.

A former university educator, with a doctorate in musical composition / theory, Jeff has composed and produced upwards of 30 game soundtracks; he was among the first to bring serious music to computer games.

Jeff served as FIRAXIS Chairman for one year after the Take Two acquisition, stepping down late in 2006 to pursue independent projects in music, technology, and history, and also to serve as a consultant.
