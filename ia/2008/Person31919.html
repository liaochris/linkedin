Cathy Colmenares’s Summary

Cathy Colmenares has over 15 years of experience in multicultural sales, marketing, and client services. She has worked with organizations including el diario/LA PRENSA, New York’s oldest Spanish-language daily newspaper, StarMedia, Latin America’s first Internet portal, and One Economy, an international nonprofit that expands the use of technology and the Internet. Her work has been featured in the Miami Herald, Miami Today, State Tech magazine, and on NBC and Univision.

At Todobebé, Cathy is responsible for developing and implementing the strategic direction of the online properties. In addition to new product management and development, Cathy leads the marketing initiatives for these properties including 360 degree marketing, SEO, PPC, social media, consumer engagement, and viral marketing.
