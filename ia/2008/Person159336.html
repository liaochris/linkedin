Rustam R. Urmeev’s Summary

The Boulders Tennis School approach to tennis is simple, because it is our belief that simple is powerful, it works! The School offers a unique approach to coaching and teaching with an emphasis on an organic approach to player development. We see everyone as possessing an athlete within and we are committed to developing that unique potential.

At the Boulders Tennis School, we approach everyone’s development holistically to facilitate their improvement and success. The emphasis is on stroke mechanics, sportsmanship, sound nutrition, personal development, self-respect, strategy and tactics, fitness and wellness.

We are inspired through our commitment to provide the highest level of tennis teaching available. This is accomplished in an environment where each player can grow and develop as a person, learning valuable lessons every step along the way. Our goal is to bring purpose, possibility and a plan that facilitates game development, personal growth and individual expression.
