David Korobkin’s Summary

Mentored by the best in class—Toyota, LEXUS and the Walt Disney Company—marketing executives, I develop STRATEGIC, innovative, unique and comprehensive branding campaigns and Marketing Communications Plans.

I combine an organization’s business/product essence with their Vision, Mission & Value Statements, to create a targeted, brand marketing message; communicating the company/product's true overarching brand and its Unique Value Proposition. This strategic process consistently surpasses expectations.

For instance, I collaborated with Toyota, conceiving and leading my design team to create the LEXUS LOGO, the world’s second most recognizable automotive logo. My Strategy Document facilitated building the foundation for the brand to become the number one luxury car in the U.S. market, according to Forbes.com.

My diverse experience in B2B, B2C and B2B2C marketing strategy spans the Automotive, Entertainment and Destination Market, Health GA/TPA and P&C Insurance, Toy Packaging, Software Packaging, Major League Sports Teams, Insurance Brokerages, Consumer Paint & Faux Finishes, and High Technology Global Manufacturing industries. Successfully communicating strategic messages to identified audiences, I improve visibility and ignite profitability.

I also led my high-performance creative team, creating the brand and logo for the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers TV Show and the packaging for the Action-figure Toy line. This effort generated ten times the first-year forecasted toy sales goal, producing $250Million in revenues.

My numerous, long-term collaborations with Fortune-rated clients, illustrates a dedication to continuous improvement and accomplishments.
