Mark Horwitz’s Summary

Thanks for stopping by & checking me out. I am always looking to build relationship with other people who network for career opportunities, business opportunities, referrals, ideas or just to help someone out.

I have been a Business Owner, Mortgage Banker & my favorite which I have been doing for the last 6 + years Recruiting & Corporate Recruiting. I have recruited sales reps (inside & outside) for my business in Portland, OR. I have recruited Top Sales Professionals to become Mortgage Bankers in Columbia, MD / Philadelphia, PA / Salt Lake, UT & Denver CO. Recruiting is a passion of mine & I really enjoy being able to find the right candidates to be able to take a company from "Good to Great".
AFN is a 3rd Party Logistics Company that is recognized as a Top 100 3PL by Inbound Logistics Magazine. We are located in the North Suburbs of Chicago. As the VP of Recruiting for AFN I am always looking for the next Top Sales Professional. If you are looking for an amazing opportunity in a Trillion Dollar Industry please feel free to send me your resume. If you would like to network with me please feel free to send me an invite I am always looking to build up my network.
