Dalton Parsons’s Summary

I have been working in web design, web development and internet marketing for eight years now; utilizing such technologies as HTML, CSS, DHTML, PHP/MySQL, ASP/ASP.NET and many others. During that time, I have become highly proficient in the standard designer/developer tools such as Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator as well as many platform specific tools. Moreover, I am very skilled at hand coding CSS-based (table-less or table-light) websites using only HTML/CSS, JavaScript and PHP using only Notepad or other text editors.

In addition to lead-generation and marketing websites, there have been a variety of web applications such as corporate intranet/extranet portals using Microsoft Sharepoint Server 2007 (MOSS2007) and PHP/MySQL, mortgage software based on legacy systems, corporate help-desk systems and other internal web-based products created over the years.

Furthermore, my skills as a web designer and developer are greatly enhanced by over 5 years of online marketing endeavors including SEO (search engine optimization), SEM (search engine marketing), PPC (pay per click), online ad placement/analytics as well as traditional marketing avenues such as the creation of regularly scheduled ads for national and regional magazines, ROI analysis, company collateral creation and PR/copywriting.

Finally - and what I believe is my foremost skill, my entrepreneurial and small business background has always required my to work as project manager. From the creation and management of successful tech teams to the administration and direction of third-party agencies, consultants and contractors; my leadership and organizational skills round out this resume. I enjoy a very 'hands-on' approach to project management, often choosing to utilize alternative project management and coding methodologies as Agile and eXtreme programming.
