Ben Gerber’s Summary

I am a Senior Managing Consultant and Architect and the
Privacy Services Competency Co-Lead (commercial sector, NGOs, state/local and non-US-federal governments)
with IBM Global Services' Security & Privacy Practice.

I specialize in international privacy strategy, solutions and compliance, SOA security, cryptography implementation, and data-related vendor management.

As organizations leverage new technologies and innovative business and governance models, I address the challenges of maintaining and enhancing individuals' privacy and everyone's security.

I lead clients across the globe in achieving and maintaining solid privacy practices and security for their most sensitive assets -- their customers' and employees' personal information. This results in:
* Managed risk, achieving and maintaining compliance with laws, regulations and industry practices
* Better experiences for customers and users of both services and products
* Respected employees valuing their employer
* Trust, confidence, and worldwide esteem for organizations and their brands


Specialties:
* Privacy enabling technology and enabling technologies to address privacy
* Management and technology solutions and strategies that address privacy, security, and data-management laws and regulations at the industry, local, national, and international levels for multijurisdictional firms
* Data related vendor management, outsourcing risk management; Complying with privacy and security requirements while leveraging outsourcing/global-sourcing/off-shoring
* Compliance (including PCI, SOX, HIPAA, GLBA, FCRA, FERPA, COPPA, PIPEDA, EU Data Protection Directive, Safe Harbor, US States' Privacy Regulations)
* Applied cryptography and encryption implementation
* SOA (Service Oriented Architecture) and web services security
* Security convergence, physical security
* Security frameworks and standards (including ISO 17799/27002, COBIT, NIST)
* Business process mapping and reengineering
