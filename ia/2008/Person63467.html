Fergal Kearns’s Summary

I have fourteen years of international experience helping global/Fortune 500 companies achieve business results through the successful execution of large-scale change events (e.g., M&A, strategy/operating model changes, process optimization, systems transformations, and overhead cost reduction).

For my early career, I worked in Europe and the Americas with the New York headquartered VISION Consulting in their Strategic Change Practice. Since 2006, I have been an independent consultant and I continue to specialize in post-merger business integrations and large scale business transformations. I formed my own Change Management company, Crossing Point LLC, in 2008.

My experience is centered on leadership coaching, re-designing and implementing new business processes, organizational structures, work practices and technologies in the financial services, manufacturing, and entertainment industries. I continue to grow intellectual capital and methodologies for designing and mobilizing change within organizations.

Some of my recent clients include Citicards; US Bank; NOVA Information Systems/Elavon; EuroConnex; Deutsche Bank; Asea Brown Boveri; Citibank; Warner Music Group; DaimlerChrysler; Cigna Insurance Group, Prudential Insurance.

I am also published in my area of expertise: “Application of Business Process Re-engineering and Information Technology to the Re-design of Personal Social Services”, with Gerard Lyons, Journal of the Irish Institute of Public Administration (1997), pp 17-44. This work was referenced in the construction of Irish child care legislation.
