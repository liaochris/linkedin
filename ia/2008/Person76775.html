Hillary Garrett’s Summary

Giving back to my community is very important to me and I find public libraries to be a perfect place for me to do just that! I have several years experience working as a reference librarian in public libraries. I particularly enjoy teaching library and computer instruction classes, working on library programming for adults, planning and leading book talks or discussion groups, and promoting library services through outreach to the community and advocacy. I am hard-working, creative, enthusiastic, and a team player. If you are a Portland, OR, librarian and would like to get in touch, please contact me!
