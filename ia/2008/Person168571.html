Sandra Fletcher’s Summary

Sandra has 14 years experience in Aviation & Leisure, employed by two of the world’s most prestigious Five Star Airlines. Sandra currently manages the eBusiness team for the IT Department at Qatar Airways, based in Doha, Qatar. She previously spent 9 years working with Emirates Airline in a number of areas including eCommerce Sales, Distribution, Operations, Marketing, and Destination & Leisure Management.Other areas in close proximity included Internet Communications & Advertising, Loyalty, CRM, Market Research, Events Management & Tour Operations.

For the last four years while in eBusiness at Qatar Airways, Sandra has built & led a strong team of professionals; together their mandate has been process reengineering, reuse of technology, and enterprise architecture. This has covered a multitude of projects across the airline & it's subsidiaries; key evolving eCommerce projects include internet booking & online payment. The eBusiness team analyse, model, architect, implement & support all customer facing applications/ touch points, along with related business processes using new technologies.

Sandra is currently expanding her team and looking to recruit Business Analysts/Project Leads/Solution Architects with airline/travel backgrounds and experience in RUP/UML and J2EE technologies.
