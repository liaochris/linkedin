Dan MacDonald’s Summary

I started my professional career in test at Microsoft where I learned the full product development life cycle from the ground up. After spending a few years there I sought other challenges as a consultant working on everything from mobile games to enterprise application development. From systems integration to custom application development I have worked on all kinds of projects some as small as $10-$20k and others as large as $3.4M.

As a developer I can learn and adapt to any technology or environment in a matter of days, a requirement for a successful consulting career. As a result I am familiar with many off the shelf technologies like MOSS, CRM, SMS and various other enterprise applications. I also have a passion for game development which is one of my hobbies.
