Dion Camp-Sanders’s Summary

After spending 5 exciting years at IAC/InterActiveCorp in various corporate development, strategy, and business development roles, I then spent nearly 3 years at a profitable, fast-growing start-up called NextAction Corporation where I led the company's New Media Division.

During the past few years, I learned about the magnitude of problems being caused by global climate change and our consumption of fossil fuels. The more I learned, the more I felt compelled to take action in a professional capacity. Like many entrepreneurial execs with significant high tech and start-up experience, I saw an opportunity to shift careers and leverage my skills and network of relationships to chart a new path in the world of clean energy. Our new company (Pacific Edison) is dedicated to finding business solutions to our energy and environmental challenges.
