Nima Badiey’s Summary

I’m a technology professional with a passion for product innovation and business growth strategies for fast moving and entrepreneurial companies involved in Social Media and the Web 2.0 space.

I am particularly interested in how to sustain and grow communities of innovation: 1) how to support developers to build and add onto Flickr's extensible API platform; 2) how to intelligently apply popularity algorithms and feature/function/usage protocols to rank/rate/position community developed apps; and 3) how to sustain innovation across cultures, languages and geographies using the tools at our disposal: the internet, the telephone, IM/Chat, wikis, blogs, Twitter and other user generated mediums.

I have over 10 years of product management and professional service experience, including product and business development at Flickr, corporate strategy and management consulting at Deloitte Consulting, and product development and operations at Sun Microsystems. I also consult for private clients, including NetSuite, 8020 Publishing, Data Robotics, Kinfo and Lootis.

I am comfortable in a wide variety of structures, from loose (startup) to rigid (consulting). I innovate, try new things, fail fast, and combine my analytical background with a pragmatic approach aimed at getting things done with a sense of urgency. I have a strong understanding of technological drivers and emerging disruptive innovations, and how they can affect penetration, growth, utility and productivity. I'm comfortable working and engaging in business and technical discussions with CxO, VP, Senior Management and Director levels in all organizational functions. I have excellent communication and presentation skills, enjoy working in high energy and urgent environments, and solve client problems with a pragmatic approach.
