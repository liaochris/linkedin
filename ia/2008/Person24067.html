Benny Ketelslegers’s Summary

During my entire career, I have been passionate about IT security and information security. In the beginning I gained a lot of knowledge on the many security aspects of TCP/IP networks and systems. In addition, I gained an extensive experience with network vulnerability scanners and IT Security Assessment systems. In the last years, I also gained experience in the more strategic and tactical aspects of information security, talking about policies, procedures, business continuity, disaster recovery, event/incident management, etc.
In January 2007, I started my 2 year postgraduate education at the Solvay Business School to achieve my Executive Master in ICT Audit & Security. In the beginning of this year I have also become a CISSP.

Starting with HostIT, I was responsible for the management and security of the network infrastructure. This infrastructure had to comply with strict SLA’s so a reliable and secure network was of the utmost importance. During my work at HostIT, I worked for several customers implementing their specific infrastructure within the HostIT environment.
After 4 years I wanted to expand my horizon and started at Proximus as a network design engineer. In this position, I was responsible for the security improvements in the network designs. Organizing and following up on the results of penetration testing, was also an important part of the constant improvement of the existing infrastructure.

After these successful projects, I continued my career as the network and security team leader at Vangenechten Packaging. Here I was the main responsible for the management and security of the international TCP/IP network in cooperation with several external partners. Outside the daily organization, escalation management and improvement of operational procedures belonged to my tasks.

I recently started at Ascure as a consultant, willing to further expand my knowledge of information security and information risk management.
