Ynema Mangum’s Summary

Ynema Mangum (ymangum@gmail.com) is the executive producer for TalkBMC, the BMC Software conversation channel which hosts blogs and podcast conversations on all IT and technology topics. Formerly, she worked in R&D at BMC as a senior product marketing manager. Prior to BMC, she held senior level, consulting, and principal positions in several start-ups.

Ynema is an experienced social media and product management professional who has played a key role in bringing Web 2.0 to BMC, a $1.7 billion publicly traded company with 6,000 employees. Her bloggers and podcast interviewees have been quoted on Digg, Slashdot, Wired Magazine, ZDNet, Computer Business Review, LinuxToday, and Forbes as well as others.

Ynema is a change agent. She considers herself a quiet influencer and a connector whose collaborative nature ensures success in introducing new concepts and services into the mainstream -- even in the most complex environments. She thrives on doing what seems to be impossible, and enjoys taking calculated risks in her personal life -- snowboarding, skiing, SCUBA diving or wake boarding when the season is right. She is certified in ITIL v3 best practices and is an active sponsor of the Austin Social Media Club.
