Marc Meyer’s Summary

I have held positions ranging from web developer, marketing specialist, sales representative, and sales manager, to positions of VP of sales, VP of marketing, Dir. of Internet marketing and CTO.

Those companies include The Internet Consulting Group, Computer Enterprises Inc., Marblejar, Mind Over Media, and Emerson Direct Inc. After almost 13 years in the industry, what I know now, has served me well, and what I need to on an ongoing basis, grows by the day. It's why I love the tech sector so much.

My current experience over the last year and half, has been focused solely on using direct response marketing and social media to drive traffic and create buzz. Prior to that my focus was on search engine optimization, search engine marketing email marketing and brand management.

My sales and marketing background consisted of being one of the top sales people at CEI for almost 3 years. I generated millions in revenue and was consistently a leader in everything from call volume, to opportunities uncovered, to training new hires.

As we enter into the challenging and exciting unchartered waters in regards to social media. I think I am uniquely qualified and poised to not only extend the conversation of what I know but to also help and educate others in their quest to understand one of the next great phenomenons in computing and technology.
