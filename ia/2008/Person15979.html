  • Board of Directors

    Altarena Playhouse a/k/a Alameda Little Theatre Inc.

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Performing Arts industry)

    November 2005 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Besides doing a lot of artistic work in community theatre, I'm now on the Board of Directors of Altarena, an intimate little theatre with lots of pluck. I'm getting an education in lateral management, helping to operate a nonprofit, and balancing artistic goals with realistic resources. We're always looking for volunteers to help out with technical/Web, marketing, publicity, fundraising, and outreach to younger audiences.

  • Freelance Music Director, pianist, arranger, etc.

    Self (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; Myself Only; Performing Arts industry)

    February 1998 — Present (10 years 4 months)

    I've been Music Director, pit musician, arranger/orchestrator, etc. for dozens of shows in the Bay Area (see www.armandofox.com for a full list). I grew up in NYC and have been doing this for community/amateur theatres since 7th grade. If I like the material and the people I'll usually work for very little; if not I won't work for any amount. I am good at squeezing down shows to fit limited musical resources, having recently reduced two full orchestrations (Oklahoma, Company) for 4-5 piece combo and written one complete orchestration for 6-piece combo (Oh My Godmother!). I've played piano since age 5 in just about every style (including synth & drums in various rock bands) and can sub for gigs on short notice, schedule permitting.
