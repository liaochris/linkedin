Stephen Toback’s Summary

Creative technologist with over 15 years experience applying high technology to the creation of entertainment and media. Areas of expertise include high definition and standard definition video, digital cinema, critical viewing environment design and implementation, data center design, color management, media asset management, animation production, non-linear video editing systems, digital audio systems, intranet application development, technology project management and software development utilizing open source tools.
