Jeffrey Grant’s Summary

I spent over 25 years as general counsel to some of the largest real estate and other companies in the New York metropolitan area (and as head of a law firm). I've dedicated myself in the last few years to service to others and to personal growth. I spend my days now volunteering in opportunities where I connect directly with people in crisis (with those who consistently show me that I need them as much as they need me). Recently, I've been working on larger projects to utilize my business and legal background to help in other and more varied ways.
