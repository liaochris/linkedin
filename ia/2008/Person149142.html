Pegotty Cooper’s Summary

If you are an Association Executive, New Manager, Project Manager, Team Leader, or Business Owner, and are committed to Transforming Relationships, Productivity and Reaching Your Goals, I want to be part of your team!

Do you ever wonder why you keep putting off doing the things you know you need to do, or keep having the same conversations, or keep doing the things that sabotage your efforts to reach your goals?

Successful people know they don't have to do it alone. Having a good sounding board and someone who can ask questions from a different perspective is vitally important. The gaps between what we see and what others see about us are known as blind spots. For leaders and managers, blind spots can be limiting, frustrating, and demoralizing. Seeing your blind spots allows you to step back and make different choices - and get different results!

With 25 years of leadership experience in state, national and international trade associations and in fortune 100 companies, I understand the obstacles and pitfalls you may be encountering - and I can be your champion in removing those obstacles and moving forward with great velocity toward your goals and dreams.
