William Peres’s Summary

After my graduation from ESTACA (Aeronotics and fluids), I directly joined DASSAULT SYSTEMES (WW leader of Product Lifecycle Management).

I have spent 10 years in Marketing activities. During the four last years at Dassault Systèmes, I was the Marketing & Communication Director for France, BeNeLux and Switzerland.

I have founded L'Art Pour Tous in 2001, co-founded VinPrixDomaine in 2004, co-founded SoBuzzy in 2007 and joined the board of AD-Invaders in 2008.

Today I'm CEO of SoBuzzy and L'Art Pour Tous and VP Sales and Marketing at AD-Invaders.
