Jawad Ansari (Jawad@GCubeVentures.com)’s Summary

Jawad Ansari brings more than 15 years of investment experience and extraordinary global relationships to the GCube. Ansari is the founder and former General Partner of Miven Venture Partners, a fund focused on Media and Consumer investments in China, India and US in 2005, targeting early stage consumer-focused technology opportunities (software, silicon, devices, web, and mobility) and media (content creation, distribution, management). Ansari is the founder and former Chairman & CEO of Corporate Metrix, a diversified investments firm in California founded in 2003, with assets under management of $3 billion. He has built strong relationships with leading venture capital funds, private equity funds, hedge funds, and leading investment banks globally and institutional limited partners; and brings GCube and its portfolio companies’ access to investors.

Jawad Ansari (Jawad@GCubeVentures.com)’s Specialties:

Jawad's venture investment focus areas are global interactive entertainment (including game ecosystem) opportunities in US, China and India.
