Ben Hulse’s Summary

Interdisciplinary designer with substantial experience creating identities and collateral for a wide range of clients from medical institutions to rock stars. Media work has appeared on regional billboards & newspapers, national television & radio, and on the shelves of record stores world-wide. Also heavily involved in music industry as a touring and recording musician, artist manager, designer and photographer.
