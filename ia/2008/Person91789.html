Jindrich Liska’s Summary

My mission is to free people from the job search burden so that they can spend more time on advancing their careers.

Try us at www.Vitruva.com.

Today’s average job search can take anywhere from weeks to months. For most people, this process is at minimum time consuming and, in the worst case, a very frustrating experience. With Vitruva, we have been able to reduce the job search to a fraction of a second.

We use an artificial intelligence engine to perform a nationwide job search in real-time on behalf of job seekers. The search is highly accurate and presents the job seekers with only those job opportunities which fit well with all aspects of their qualifications and preferences.

The users of our system come from all levels of an organization, from high level executives with multi-million dollar responsibilities to entry level staff members.

As well, we offer these same capabilities to both recruiters and hiring managers who can instantly locate the best talent without manually sifting through hundreds of often unqualified résumés.
