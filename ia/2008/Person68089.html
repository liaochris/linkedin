Gavin Pitchford’s Summary

Led the recruitment practice for Delta Management / Exigent, a leader in the telecommunications / networking recruiting industry since 1992. Working closely with many of the leading industry vendors and their partners to staff all their requirements in Canada, Delta started by exclusively staffing Wellfleet and Cabletron as they opened in Canada, moved on to exclusive relationships with Bay Networks, Nortel, IBM, and Stratacom as well as many other vendors through the 1990s, and now the company maintains close partnerships with several start ups as well as market leaders such as Juniper Networks and Alcatel-Lucent.

The company's differential expertise is in its ability to assess accurately which small number of candidates will meet its client's needs, and in so doing, substantially reduce wasted time for both sides. On average, one of every two interviews Delta arranges results in employment - and fewer than one candidate in 100 stays in the role for less that 12 months. In short - the company is highly adept at producing a lot of win-win situations, meeting needs of both clients and candidates.

Feel free to connect at gpitchford< at >deltamanagement.com to discuss your needs as a client - or the type of opportunity you are looking for as a candidate.

All enquiries treated in confidence.

Gavin Pitchford’s Specialties:

Delta Management is the trusted and exclusive staffing partner of a number of major net / com product vendors for their Canadian sales organizations. The company is well equipped to assist qualified professionals refine their position search, and in attaining their goals.

In addition, the firm can assist companies new to the Canadian market in developing proper employment agreements, refining their go-to-market strategy, and facilitating meetings with target customers or partners.
