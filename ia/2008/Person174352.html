Sharon Kay’s Summary

I balance my life by doing two things that I am very good at, and love to do:

As a decorative finisher, I use paints, glazes, and plasters to create rich effects on your walls.

As a technical writer, I can explain your product or process clearly and accessibly. I can create online material (working in raw HTML if necessary), and I can create the files for print documentation. Do you need advertising copy that discusses technical features clearly? I'm good at that too.
