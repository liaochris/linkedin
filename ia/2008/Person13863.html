Annette Wilde’s Summary

Annette is responsible for sales, implementation and services at Planimetron.

Creating Leasing and Space Management solutions for the real estate industry since 1983, Planimetron has offices in NY, Denver & Toronto. Our intuitive, sophisticated applications are used by some of the world's largest and most progressive real estate companies as well as corporate clients.

For the commercial real estate industry, we create dynamic visual information systems for decision makers by combining data from property management and financial systems with floorplans and other graphics.

Currently we are working with Argus on integration between Propidex and the Argus LeaseCRM product. Propidex currently integrates with CTI, JDE (world and one-world), MRI, SAP,IBS, Timberline, Dyna, RealmLease and other budgeting and leasing workflow tools. From Propidex you can create floorplans for your web pages, proposal documents, create scenarios, calculate areas and other.

For the corporate real estate world, Corpidex integrates floorplans with data from Peoplesoft, SAP and other HR and financial systems to provide a robust graphical space management tool to ensure that you have up-to-date location information, block planning tools, move planning, reservations, chargeback and extensive reporting.

Time-sensitive, real-time viewing and management of your commercial and corporate real estate holdings increase communications, data integrity and maximize value.

Annette holds a BA Psych and MBA and periodically speaks at industry conferences.

Annette Wilde’s Specialties:

Decision support software for real estate Leasing, Asset Management and Space Management professionals: cross-portfolio analysis, colored floorplans, stacks, budgeting, space inventory management system, charge back system.
