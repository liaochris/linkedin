Eudora Adolph’s Summary

As a results-oriented decision maker and an enthusiastic, visionary leader, I apply my engineering diligence, detailed problem-solving, financial acumen and motivational management style to help both businesses and individuals achieve greater success. Combining my business, teaching, entrepreneurial and investment experience allows me to be a true asset to my clients.

As a Financial Growth Engineer, I help my clients identify/develop their life and business strategic plan, create a detailed roadmap for realizing goals, help them stay on track, and provide knowledgable advice on how to continually use "the tool of money" to achieve that ideal life for oneself, one's employees, and one's loved ones.

Wealth Caretaking
401K Setup and Advice
Estate Planning
Strategic Business Development

I firmly believe you can achieve anything if you believe, work hard and smart, lead well, and never give up!
