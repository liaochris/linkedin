Lynn Marentette’s Summary

Consultant with a background in psychology, counseling, and education. Focused on developing and promoting usable applications and technologies that support interaction, collaboration, cognition, and communication. Particularly interested in multimedia applications and games suitable for touch screens of all sizes, including hand held devices, large displays, and interactive whiteboards.

School Psychology:
