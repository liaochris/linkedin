Gilles Bourdin’s Summary

Gilles Bourdin, joined NetBooster in 2000 and established NetBooster's presence in the UK and Germany. NetBooster has now become the leading independent agency for Internet business consultancy and Search Marketing services in Europe with 250 People working in offices in Paris, Nice, London, Frankfurt, Madrid, Rome, Milan, Helsinki and Manila.

Gilles has worked in the Information Technology sector for several years and with companies such as IBM, Escom AG, Scala GMBH and Nat Systems (now Cognicase). Afterwards, he joined Jobpilot, an European online recruitment company based in Germany, where he was responsible for building up subsidiaries in several European countries among which France and UK.

Gilles Bourdin’s Specialties:

NetBooster (Euronext: ALNBT) is an Interactive agency providing expert advice for online business development. Our fields of expertise are Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Search Engine Oprimisation (SEO), Affiliation, Web design, Website optimisation, Online Media, ad creation, viral Marketing & social Networks, online influence and reputation, Data Mining, CRM strategies.
