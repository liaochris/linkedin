Peter Desmyttere’s Summary

Desmyttere & Partners is a marketing advice firm, specialised in writing professional marketing plans for entreprises. We also offer strategic marketing advice and deliver interim marketing management services.

Our target is to set up and improve the marketing strategy of our clients. Most of our clients our launching a new product or service to the market, or are working on a repositioning for their brand.
