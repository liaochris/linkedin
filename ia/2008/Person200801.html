der reporter wolfgang frey’s Summary

Just a few lines about me & my philosophy.

I'm a curious person. I'm keen on news, want to know what's behind the surface, and I'm glad my job connects me to new, interesting and fascinating facts and people everyday.

I try to be as authentic as possible. That's how I work as a journalist, and that's what I recommend my customers in the field of bussiness communications: Just be who you are, be proud of your competence, services and products and communicate exactly this.
