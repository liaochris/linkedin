Angela Den Hollander’s Summary

My business - communic8 design
I run a graphic design firm and have a personal passion for marketing. I like to say communic8 design is all about giving our clients:

• More profits. We ensure our clients look as professional as the services they deliver.

• More clients. Using staying in touch tools like e-newsletters, printed newsletters and direct mail campaigns.

• And more time. We do it for our clients, from strategy through to implementation.

Personally - I have a somewhat nomadic past, having lived in Christchurch and Auckland New Zealand, followed by Adelaide and finally Melbourne.

I've worked in advertising and design since the tender age of 13, starting in the finished art studio of a major advertising agency in Adelaide as a pesky work experience kid who just wouldn’t leave.

Having learned the ropes at various large firms I decided that self employment was the way to go, the time I’d get to spend with my children, holidays, all that daytime t.v. I’d be able to watch...

Back in reality, I quickly realised that self employment wasn’t quite the life of leisure I’d been lead to believe. However, now that I run communic8 design with a great team of people to share the load, life is starting to resemble a dream if not quite the original dream. After all, now that I've discovered how much fun it is to help other businesses achieve their dreams, who’d want to waste a minute on daytime t.v.?

Angela Den Hollander’s Specialties:

• Brand development from logo through to implementation.
• FMCG and packaging in general.
• Copywriting and designing marketing materials that sell
• Helping other business owners position themselves so the they can make more profits, attract more clients and have more time in each day.
