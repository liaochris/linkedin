Bruno Delepierre’s Summary

- 1998 - 2005: University - During my university studies, I became fascinated by the internet for many reasons: its power to communicate with people, to drive business and more generally, to change the unwritten rules of the 'real' world.

As a consequence in these years I experimented with and dug into all aspects coming into play when using the internet: consumer behaviour, e-publishing and all forms of online marketing with a particular interest in performance based solutions.

- 2005 - 2006: Accenture - After graduating, I started with Accenture as I wanted to combine the expertise I built in the previous years with more hands on business and process knowledge to be able to fully manage projects within my own company.

- 2006 - Present: Adforce (http://www.adforce.be) - Currently I'm holding the position of founder/director at Adforce. My current activities within the company are mainly focussed around general management, business development and strategy.

Adforce is an online marketing and communication agency with a twist. We provide advertisers, agencies and governments the opportunity to use the power of the internet to its fullest to reach and interact with their target group.

Apart from our own capabilities and expertise, we strongly believe in a networked approach where we leverage capabilities of partners to generate the best possible results.

What it boils down to is that Adforce is able to provide all services (marketing, legal, technical...) needed to start an online marketing campaign focussing on reach and results.

Bruno Delepierre’s Specialties:

Thorough knowledge of performance based marketing, consumer behaviour, process & campaign management as well as PR.

Also good knowledge about e-publishing and different types of online marketing (SEO, SEM, Bannering, blog marketing, video advertising, e-mail marketing, content integration...).
