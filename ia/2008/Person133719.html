Mitch Joel’s Summary

Marketing Magazine called him, “Canada’s Rock Star of Digital Marketing” and in 2006 he was named one of the most influential authorities in the world on Blog Marketing. Marketing, Communications, Sales and Interactive expert, Mitch is an Entrepreneur, Blogger and Podcaster.

Mitch owned one of the first publications to go online in 1994 and his online marketing skills helped build one of the Internet’s largest web properties. Mitch was also the Director of Marketing for a leader in wireless entertainment. Now, as a President of Twist Image, Mitch develops digital marketing campaigns for global companies.

Mitch is an active community volunteer. In 2004, Mitch was awarded The MS Leadership Award, presented by the Multiple Sclerosis Society of Canada to people whose professional and social achievements have contributed to the growth of their businesses and the development of Quebec society.

Mitch is a public speaker for The Power Within where he has shared the stage with former United States President, Bill Clinton, Anthony Robbins, Dr. Phil, Mark Burnett, Po Bronson and many more. He is a guest lecturer at both McGill and Concordia University. Mitch's speaking is represented by both Speakers Spotlight and The Power Within (non-exclusive).

Mitch is the founder/moderator of the Montreal Business Book Review and also a certified self-defense coach. Mitch’s company, Twist Image, was included in Bull Market, marketing master Seth Godin’s directory/e-book identifying the top 500 creative/marketing firms in the world. Mitch is on the board of directors for IAB Canada (Interactive Advertising Bureau) and is the Co-Chair for the CMA - Canadian Marketing Association - National Convention and Trade Show as well as on the Quebec Board for NABS (National Advertising Benevolent Society). He has been featured in Marketing Magazine, Strategy Magazine, InfoPresse, CTV National News, The Montreal Gazette, La Presse, National Post, Globe & Mail and more.

Mitch Joel’s Specialties:

digital marketing, interactive advertising, Web 2.0, Blogs, Blogging, digital strategies, branding, marketing, business books, public speaker, journalism, publishing, search engine strategy, email marketing, online advertising, advertising, PodCamp, BarCamp, DemoCamp and all sorts of Unconferences.
