Jason Kwan’s Summary

Dynamic, hands-on Chief Procurement Officer with global general management and P&L experience.

Builder of organizational capabilities and driver of global transformation, including restructurings and integrations, to deliver profitable growth. Operationally savvy with a strong orientation for finance and supply chain disciplines.

Leader of high-impact procurement initiatives both at the corporate level and in low-cost, global sourcing centers.

This includes senior positions in General Management, Supply Chain, Procurement, Finance and M&A in UK, Netherlands, China, Taiwan, Indonesia, Australia, Brazil and USA.

Low cost sourcing and commodities management.
Collaborative sourcing specifically the development of suppliers.
Business development including building three businesses.
