Theresa Martin’s Summary

Held several senior management positions in different operational areas of First Union Bank and State Street Bank. In 1996 headed Internet Strategy and Design for the Retirement Services Division of State Street (which later became CitiStreet LLC). Chief Internet Strategist and Product Development. In 2005 began designing and developing websites for small businesses. Recently founded Novista Web offering full-service web strategy, design and development.
