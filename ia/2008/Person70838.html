Glenn Thaxton’s Summary

I am a highly motivated and creative hands-on leader with experience in all facets of computing, project management, team development, strategic planning, supply chain consulting and process / organizational redesign in a wide range of industries, in both academic and businesses environments.

My customer service experience includes management of a customer service organization, accounts receivable, and pricing for a food manufacturing and distribution company working on very narrow margins. In that role, working with the Vice President of Operations, formed and led cross-functional customer fo-cused teams.

As a communicator, I have presented to small groups and large audiences and provided training in technical subjects, manufacturing, customer service and management to industry groups, within companies and in classrooms. Further, with my focus on continuous learning and development, I provide a supportive, team-based, learning environment, which fosters growth and success.
