Xavier Mertens’s Summary

Graduated in 1995, I was always addicted to IT technologies. In 1996, I received a very good opportunity and started to work in the emerging Internet Service Providers business (Infoboard, Euronet, Wanadoo and finally Tiscali). This helped me to improve my networking skills and became Network Operation Center Manager.

Height years later, I joined Belga, the first Belgian press agency as Senior System Engineer. During those years, I was more and more involved in security projects and became addicted to computer security.

In 2006, it was time to really work in my privileged domain: security. I accepted an offer from C-CURE and started to work as Security Consultant.

C-CURE helped me to pass serveral certifications and to increase my knowledge.

My predilection domains are: security, design, high-availability, monitoring, reporting and audit.
