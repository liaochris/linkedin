Alistair Tutton’s Summary

I have been photographing commercial, residential and corporate interior and exterior architecture since 2000. I have photographed locations throughout North America and England. During this time I have shot for a variety of architects, interior designers, retail designers, contractors, advertising agencies and magazines throughout the country.

I have also photographed product and product lifestyle imagery for Fortune 100 companies that has appeared in national campaigns; my focus is in creating imagery that tells a story about the product as well as featuring exceptional lighting and framing.

My tertiary field of photographic expertise is in rustic lifestyle; where my naturally lit images of Middle America people and livestock have won a number of awards.

My primary goal at present is to cement my position as a national level architectural photographer by connecting to more companies beyond Kansas City. In time I wish to also procure a studio to practice my lifestyle and product photography. I feel that these three styles of photography allow me the greatest latitude to experiment with new lighting and framing techniques while giving a professional variety unrivaled in my competition.

Alistair Tutton’s Specialties:

My primary specialty is in design; my five years of formal architectural education and eight years as a creative director has taught me that any project, especially photography, is a team experience. That must be shared through the initial concept by the creative team, the needs of the client, the inclination of the audience and the vision of the artist.
