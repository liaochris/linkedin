Robert Krten’s Summary

I have extensive experience with small and large realtime embedded systems, ranging from telecom to medical. I grew up with process control and telecoms, and have since written three books on the QNX operating system, numerous articles, designed and presented training courses, architected several systems (medical, process control, telecoms), and written about a half million lines of C code.

My goal is to apply that experience to help companies succeed with their projects.

I've decided to transition out of consulting, and am now looking for full-time opportunities (in Ottawa/Ontario -- I might consider relocation to Prague).

I am interested in linking with people who share my interests (embedded systems, QNX, contracting, automated trading, antique computers) -- linkedin@parse.com.

Robert Krten’s Specialties:

Systems architecture, deep understanding of application programs through to assembly language and hardware, excellent communications skills.

I speak fluent Czech.
