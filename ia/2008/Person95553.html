John Say’s Summary

Founded in 1990, Say Design is an established video game innovation company.

Since our first game (A-Blast), we've been busy developing a large library of addicting, innovative casual / web games in Flash and other technologies. Our clients include: Yahoo! Games, ABC/Disney Online Portals, AOL, Warner Brothers, Electronic Arts, Pepsi, Toyota, Sony Pictures, 20th Century Fox, Nickelodeon, MTV, Shockwave.com, RealArcade, Oberon-Media, OneWorld, Miniclip.com, and many others. Say Design games has also developed games for use in Kiosks, Interactive Set-top boxes for AOL, and mobile phone games. Say Design is a proud recipient of such awards as Real Arcade Game Showdown 2003 for excellence in game design, accessibility, production value, and originality. Since then, many of our games have earned awards and critical acclaim. We've attracted attention from gamers and major players alike, including the likes of Microsoft, Nintendo, Sony, and EA for example to produce innovative games designs for a variety of platforms. We currently are working with top tier publishers in developing DS, Wii, and Xbox 360 titles which we know you're going to love. We hope to bring a refreshing line-up of new game concepts and genres to the industry in coming months and years.

The team consists of a hybrid structure which includes a global hand-picked team of experts as well as our studio located in Aliso Viejo, California.

Our game innovation includes platforms such as: Web / Browser-based free to play Multiplayer and MMO's, Virtual Worlds, PC, XBox Live Arcade, Nintendo DS, Nintendo Wii, Portable game devices, Kiosks, and more.

Our passion for gaming shows through every production we take on. Our determination to push the limits of technology help us to drive the end experience to higher limits. The combination of the two, the quality of our team members combined yield fun experiences we believe will command attention and respect from gamecentrics and non-gamers alike.
