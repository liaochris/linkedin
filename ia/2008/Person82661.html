James Adonis’s Summary

My experience is predmoninantly in management roles where I have achieved record-breaking milestones in my career. These include:

- Taking a team that had 70% employee turnover and reducing it to zero where it was maintained for 2 years
- Employee engagement results exceeding not only the industry average, but also world's best practice

I have achieved these by engaging and motivating people. My experience coupled with my extensive research have made me an "engagement expert", where I uniquely show managers how to engage their employees so that they remain loyal high performers, and show individuals how to engage themselves so that they focus on achieving what they want.

To download my complimentary e-book, "The 4 Key Elements of Employee Engagement", visit www.jamesadonis.com and click on the "With Compliments" icon.
