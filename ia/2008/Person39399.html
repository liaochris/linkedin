Collin Cornwell’s Summary

With more than 10 years of design and marketing experience, I'm a respected industry leader and a true partner to iCrossing’s clients.

I began working as a graphic designer in 1995 and began developing websites in 1997. My passion for internet marketing prompted me to start my own small business in 1998, which was purchased by a Southwestern Colorado newspaper two years later. As the Manager of Web Development, I played a pivitol role in the newspaper’s transition from a traditional media to a leading online resource for local news and information.

As the Director of the Natural Search Optimization group based at iCrossing’s headquarters in Scottsdale, Arizona, I provide training and leadership for the company’s NSO division and work with more than 25 talented and passionate search experts to develop best practices that are implemented throughout the company – and respected throughout the industry.

I have worked with and helped to develop successful online strategies for some of the largest brands in the world, including Citibank, General Motors, Coca-Cola and Federal Express.

I hold a degree from Fort Lewis College in Colorado, where I majored in Computer Science Information Systems.
