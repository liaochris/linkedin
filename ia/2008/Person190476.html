Eddie Tofpik’s Summary

A highly competent & personable financial services professional with twenty six years experience in marketing, sales, trading & management in a variety of products & services including FX, Futures, Options, Stocks, Bonds, Commodities & Banking services. A team player & a team builder! A skilled person with proven abilities in sourcing & developing business as well as running a 24 hour trading desk. A self motivated individual adept at initiating & completing tasks in a cost effective & timely manner. An ability to work in a global 24 hour marketplace with an understanding of client & potential client needs as well as a strong corporate citizen.
• A talent for identifying opportunities & formulating appropriate actions within a team structure. A conscientious attitude to seeking & presenting solutions to new & existing client streams and cross selling products within the same industry & also across industries.
• Demonstrated ability to gain client/counterparty trust & provide exceptional follow-up, leading to a consistent trading income flow & growth on a long term basis.
• Track record of assisting in the design and implementation of trading products & reporting procedures within both company & group levels.
• Expertise in resolving issues & overseeing implementation of appropriate action.
• Securing numerous client accounts & increasing the profile successfully of the London FX operations within the main group structure. Maintaining a consistent team with many years experience & low staff turnover.

Eddie Tofpik’s Specialties:

• Proficient with major trading systems (FXAll, Currenex, Reuters Dealer, Piranha, Atlas,…), technical analysis (Reuters Professional) & Microsoft Office System (Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint® and Outlook®).
