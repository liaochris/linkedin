Nima Taradji’s Summary

Mr. Taradji is a life member of the Million Dollar Advocate Forum exclusively reserved to accomplished trial lawyers with at least one verdict of one million dollars or more and has received his license to practice law in the state of Illinois and the 7th Circuit Federal Court in 1999. He has tried numerous personal injury cases before juries, and he has participated in countless arbitrations and mediations. He has settled millions of dollars in injury cases and takes great pride in getting results as fast as possible and obtaining the highest possible compensation for his clients.

Since 1999, Mr. Taradji has tried numerous jury trials and arbitrations. In 2001, Mr. Taradji went into private practice and is dedicated to representing serious and catastrophic injury victims.

Nima Taradji’s Specialties:

Mr. Taradji handles personal injury cases in the areas of motor vehicle accidents, premises liability, wrongful death, construction accidents, dog bites, slip and fall, nursing home negligence, elderly abuse and other serious and catastrophic personal injury victims.
