Antonio Buehler’s Summary

Former military officer with extensive leadership, turnaround and life experiences. I am the Founder and Managing Director of a private investment fund created to identify, acquire, and grow a small company ($5 - $50 million in revenue).

I am always available to meet like minded individuals, operators, and entrepreneurs looking for a liquidity/change of control event, or those who would like to personally invest in growth equity buyouts. Potential industries of interest include business services, end of life care, janitorial services, educational tutoring, and self-help services.

* Stanford Graduate School of Business (MBA)
* U.S. Military Academy at West Point
* Former US Army Officer; Engineer, Airborne/Ranger
* Former Restructuring/Investment Banking Associate at Houlihan Lokey
* Private equity, investment management consulting, and IT consulting experience

============================================================

It is my desire to make this world a better place for children; where they can hope, dream and grow to achieve their full potential. It is my belief that our best hope for a world with economic and social freedom comes through education and opportunity.

Personally I have been involved in efforts to improve the lives of disadvantaged youth in the US, Germany, Bulgaria and Kosovo. For the past six years, through Childreach, I have sponsored three children in Egypt, Malawi and Zimbabwe. I am currently on the board of directors for the New York based non-profit for child bereavement, A Caring Hand. I am interested in talking to people leading social or business ventures to improve the lives and create opportunity for children.
