Lucinda Connaker’s Experience

  • Freelance Makeup Artist / Fashion Stylist

    Independant

    (Apparel & Fashion industry)

    March 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    I am a freelance makeup artist available to work with models, photographers, actors, and filmmakers. I have experience applying makeup for both print and runway fashion work and count local designers and models as well as cosmetic companies among my repeat clients. I am also available to work with individuals who need makeup done for prom, weddings or special events. As I have been both behind the scenes and in front of the camera as a model, it gives me a unique perspective that allows me to understand the needs of everyone involved in a project.

  • Lead Dresser and Theater/Visual Arts Substitute

    Interact Center for the Visual & Performing Arts

    (Performing Arts industry)

    March 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Interact is a center for both the performing and visual arts that is inclusive of artists with disabilities. I work as a sub there, helping out wherever I am needed, whether backstage or assisting the artists throughout the day. This is extremely rewarding work that makes use of my strong and varied arts background. My duties include dressing, stage crew work, giving cues, occasional crisis control, and jack-of-all-trades flexibility.

  • Aesthetician/Esthetician

    Ulta

    (Cosmetics industry)

    I also retailed high-end cosmetics. This includes Smashbox, Studio Gear, Urban Decay, Garden Botanica, Rare earth, Bare Minerals, Elizabeth Arden, Bourjois, Lola, and Too Faced in addition to drugstore brands.
