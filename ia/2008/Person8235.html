Alon Hodir’s Summary

I am an experienced project and deployment strategy executive. I’m excellent in customer relationships, has great communication as well as top quality technical skills and perspective.

I am currently working as Intl. Convenience & Fuel PMO in Retalix. My company is one of the world leading companies in delivering a complete software solution for the retail market. On my role as a project manager I was responsible to successful design, quality and deployment of complexity software for T1 customers and major oil companies like BP, including scheduling, resource allocation and all communication with the customer regarding technical issues.
My passion is for establishing winning deployment strategies for customers and their business, and then leading by example to a successful execution of the strategy.

I firmly believe in the imperative need for a professional and close accompaniment for customer’s business deployment strategy, product strategy and corporate culture. I am actively involved in all these areas.
