Martti Kuldma’s Summary

I have been part of many international projects serving in different roles as a bid manager, project manager or domain expert. Main projects have been related to core systems implementation for the financial services industry: RTGS for the central banks, internet banking solutions for the commercial banks, trading systems for stock exchanges and clearing and settlement systems for securities depositories.

Currently I am involved in telecoms focusing on services development.
