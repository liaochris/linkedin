Brooks Van Norman’s Summary

bbkvn@shaw.ca toplinked.com

I am pleased to help you however I can. I am a "pay it forward" networker who is interested in furthering your cause with no strings attached, because the world is small and we each need all the help we can get. I do not keep a "favour bank".

You can expect me to share my expertise with you or your organization in any of the areas outlined below.

I am an energetic, empathetic leader who values execution and will support good ideas. I subscribe to the following pillars of business success:

* Speed of Innovation (This implies of course that you actually ARE innovating)
** Ability to Execute (I love ideas, but let's get them implemented!)
*** Operational Excellence (process, process, process!)

I'm enamoured with the life long study and practice of marketing, branding, selling and meeting consumer needs - especially where technology is involved.

My hair is brown and my eyes are blue (both recessive traits in my family). I use my right hand and 'right brain'. I'm of Dutch and Ukrainian descent. I am an avid reader and believe in the concepts of 'never eat alone' and 'a whole new mind'. I take my work seriously and myself lightly. I tend to over eat good food and am a lover of great red wine and fine automobiles. I'm grateful to have made it this far in life, but want to see how much further I can go...
