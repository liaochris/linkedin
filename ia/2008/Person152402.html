David Thomas’s Summary

After 30 years of various production positions on feature films and network TV movies, I began developing a TV series. My goal was to create a way to make blackjack as compelling to watch as hold'em poker.

The resulting game also works very well online and in live play. We are currently looking for investors for a private placement to fund the TV show and high level software.

The game is blackjack reinvented for the poker table.

David Thomas’s Specialties:

I am very experienced in all phases of production and post production of television and film. Skills include project managment, contract negotiation, union relations and finding creative solutions to problems which demand excessive funding.
