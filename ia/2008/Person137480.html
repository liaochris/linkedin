Pamela Lane’s Summary

I'm a writer, producer, director, showrunner for nonfiction (reality) TV and hybrids--game, sitcom, docusoaps, how-to, etc.

I started my career in entertainment on air at KRNW FM in Boulder Co, then moved to indie films and music videos as an AD then UPM/ line producer. I moved to writer/ producer for feature documentary and nonfiction TV in the '90's. I've worked on some groundbreaking reality shows including Temptation Island, Meet My Folks, ElimiDate, Who Wants to Marry My Dad and Outback Jack. Last year I worked with MWG Media developing a broadband network for women, ThisIsMine.com, designed to bring original 3 to 5 minute Mobisodes to a mobile device near you. I love working in the digital space, but after a year it became clear they were never going to launch, so I moved on--back to TV--although I am still developing my own digital projects.

In my second career, I'm a writer and editor. I've been writing for television for more than a decade and freelance editing scripts and books for 8 years. I studied the craft of fiction and nonfiction writing at Rice University, UCLA and Antioch University LA, where I was awarded the prestigious Eloise Klein Healy scholarship. My MFA is from Goucher College's graduate program in creative nonfiction.

I am an editor, coach and teacher of the writing process, which includes not only writing the book, but securing an agent and promoting your book's sales. Book proposals are a specialty. Check out my site at wwwMyBookDoctor.com

I also write essays, poetry and books. The compilation Silence Kills, which contains one of my essays, was just released as an audiobook (the hardbound version came out last year) and my new book, Growing up Naked in Public, is out to agents right now.

Pamela Lane’s Specialties:

My thousands of hours in the bay have made me an outstanding director in the field. I know how to shoot footage that will cut. I am also a published writer, as well as a TV writer, so I understand how to build and develop compelling story.

I also write fiction, nonfiction, poetry, articles, speeches, biography & essay. Line edit & teach novel, memoir, self-help, nonfiction & young adult. Making good books great and aspiring writers into published authors.
