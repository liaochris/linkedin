Deidre Hughey’s Summary

My primary interest is helping develop ethical sales cultures and educating ethical individuals in order to build their sales practice. This is done through formal classroom training, seminars and customized corporate training.

A current member of Toastmasters, the National Association of Women Business Owners (NAWBO), the Women's Business Owners Network (WBON) and several local Chambers. A columnist for Women's Edge Magazine, a founding member of and served on the board for the United Professional Sales Association (UPSA).
