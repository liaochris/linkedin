Mansour Belhadj’s Summary

Experienced in high-value engineering Solutions for Oil and Gas, Gas Processing, Refining, Petrochemical and Polymers industries in the Middle East region. Solutions include Operator Training Simulation, Advanced Process Control & Real Time Optimization, Asset Management and Advanced Production Planning and Scheduling.
