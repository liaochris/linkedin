Davin George’s Summary

I work with Linux and Unix Computer Systems to provide Internet Services and Facilities at an Enterprise level. Most of my career is based in either Defence / Internet / Telecommunications or Media companies.

I have designed and implemented high availability Web based systems using Apache, Tomcat, Weblogic, Jboss etc. I have a active interest in most things Open Source and constantly look for new projects/tools to assist my company.

My management skills have grown over the years having managed teams up to 20 staff however I enjoy keeping my hands in technically as well.

I have my RHCE (Red Hat Certified Engineer), RHCT (Red Hat Certified Technician) and LPIC(1) Linux Professional Institute Level 1 certifications. I am developing my skills and qualification constantly and an emphasis on both ITIL, Project Management and Service Delivery. I have added my ITIL Practioneer V3 qualification and will be working towards a Diploma in ITIL. In my private life I am working towards completing a Diploma in Photography.
