Allen Kruse’s Summary

Onling Gaming Media Experience

Allen "Kylian" Kruse got his start to the industry with Warcry.com, where he started as a news reporter, then Site Manager of several of Warcry.com’s MMORPG game sites, and ultimately became the Human Resources Director and part of the management team.

December 2003 he moved to Gamershell.com where he took on the role of Marketing Manager and Network Manager of MMHell.com, where he gained experience selling all mediums of online media to a global market. Doing deals with some of the gaming industries leaders, such as Sony Online Entertainment and Funcom.

Summer 2004, Allen decided to open his own Gaming News Network which launched July 2005, and continued to grow in circulation and popularity until the acquisition of Gamerslegend, LLC. by Society of Gamers Media Network, LLP, in February 2006.

September 2006 - Took on a role as Advisor with Neogence Studios - a brand new up and coming MMORPG Development Studio. Helping in all manners of business from funding acquisition to game design and concept creation.

Allen is now embracing his veteran background, and is serving the veterans within his region by holding the position of County Veterans Service Officer, in addition he was recently installed in July 08, as the Post Commander for American Legion Post #66.
