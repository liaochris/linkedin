Connor Murphy’s Summary

Connor Murphy has successfully managed and implemented technical solutions for public, private and development sector clients across America, Europe, the Middle East and the Caribbean. Connor has experience throughout the solution building lifecycle and is a six sigma greenbelt. Connor is passionate about delivering intelligent IT solutions that save time, reduce costs and improve effectiveness.

Connors client list includes Estee Lauder, Pfizer, British Airways, BP, National Health Service (NHS UK), Limitless (Dubai), Jamaican Fire Brigade, Jamaican Ministry of Education, National Highways Agency (UK), Partnership for Schools (UK), New York ISO (NYISO), New York City Housing Authority (NYISO), AEGATE (UK), AQUA America, The Home Office (UK), Job Centre Plus (UK) and The Department of Work and Pensions (UK).

Connors expertise includes:

•Solution Building - Connor’s solution building experience covers planning, requirements gathering, analysis, implementation and testing. Connor is an MCAD certified .Net developer and has delivered several successful solution builder assignments.

•Content Management - Connor led the development of the largest UK public sector e-Learning content management implementation project at Job Centre Plus. This project delivered over $12 million in savings and was a gold award winner at the MCA awards. Connor has also helped deliver the NHS Choices Sharepoint 2007 content management system for the re-vamped nhs.uk website

•IT Strategy - Connor has delivered, and presented, strategic analysis and performance improvement plans at board and ministerial levels. Connor helped define the national e-learning strategy for the Jamaican Ministry of Education. Internally, Connor is also involved with PA’s knowledge management service and is a leading thinker within PA on the Semantic Web, Google and Web 2.0
