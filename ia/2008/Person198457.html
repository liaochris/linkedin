Walter Myers’s Summary

With nearly 30 years experience in various media, photographic and graphics related industries, my focus is on astronomical and natural science illustration for print, web, film and television.

My work has been published and exhibited the United States as well as in Canada, Europe, Australia, and New Zealand. Clients include:

• Adler Planetarium
• Astronomy Magazine
• Capstone Press
• Cassini Imaging
• Discovery Channel
• Macmillan Publishing
• Wendell Minor (author/illustrator)
• Montréal Botanical Garden
• NASA
• National Geographic Channel
• Oxford University Press
• The Planetary Society
• Royal Astronomical Society of Canada
• SPIKE TV
• SynMusic
• Tucson Botanical Gardens
• WGBH/NOVA
