David Ma’s Summary

Corporate and commercial lawyer focused on technology matters, including outsourcing, licensing, distribution, development, procurement and e-commerce matters on the commercial side, and non-public financing, mergers, acquisitions and reorganizations.

David Ma’s Specialties:

Advising on, negotiating and drafting legal documents related to technology matters or companies involved in the provision or consumption of technological products or services.
