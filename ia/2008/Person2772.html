Adam Zak’s Summary

I help companies that are frustrated with their efforts to recruit Lean Leaders at the executive and managerial level.

They might be annoyed because they're just not interviewing the caliber of candidates they deserve. Or they're angry because a recruiter they've used never really took the time to understand their corporate culture AND each candidate's true strengths and deficiencies, particularly with respect to true Lean expertise.

Some of my clients felt betrayed when their (former) big-name search firm later turned on them and poached their existing executive talent. Maybe they learned the definition of "parallel processing" of candidates the hard way, when a "Big Four" firm presented their ideal executive simultaneously to another organization - and she joined THEM! Or they just felt like a commodity buyer when another recruiter turned out to be more of a paper-and-body-pusher than a value-adding business partner who puts their interests first.

But you've never had any of these problems at your company, right? And you've never lost any sleep at night wondering how your critical executive search engagement was going. Have you?

Maybe we should talk.

Can I solve your executive search problem, particularly when it comes to recruiting top Lean Leadership talent? I'm not sure. But there's just one way to find out. Connect with me today. Bring me up to speed on your situation, and we'll see what I can do.
