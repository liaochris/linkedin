Selina Ali’s Summary

As a graduate in Music Industry Management & Popular Music, I have spent the last six years dedicated to educating myself on the core principles of this dynamic industry. I have a thorough understanding of the principles and values of the major bodies in the industry, including statutory bodies and collection societies as well as covering subjects such as Marketing & Promotion, Copyright Law and Negotiation & Communication.

I am a confidant, hard-working and enthusiastic individual that constantly strives to achieve my greatest potential. I like to be actively challenged throughout my role, and work through any obstacles, creating new ideas and work methods along the way. I am punctual and efficient person and have developed time-management skills and have achieved a healthy balance between quantity and quality of the work I produce.

I have also acted as Publishing Assistant/Research & Development to a start-up company called Beat Makers Anonymous (BMA). This is an Artist Development and Production company specialising in nurturing and providing creative guidance to the urban music scene, as well as proving a production facility to create new material for these artists. At present, we are working with solo artist, Kyza, who has had previous success with the hip-hop group Terra Firma and works as BMA Artist & Repertoire director. His solo single ‘Fight Klub’ and album ‘The Experience’ was release September 2006 in all major retail stores. I have participated in writing up parts of the business plan for BMA as well as take an active part in Kyza’s project and working along side the director in research and development to improve business strategy, whilst also looking after the companies bookkeeping and publishing administration with MCPS/PRS.
