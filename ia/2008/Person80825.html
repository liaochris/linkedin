Isabelle Peyrichoux’s Summary

Seven years of experience in conducting user research and designing Web sites and applications, working on cross-functional teams.

- Generative research: Conducted 100 in-depth interviews, including field interviews and contextual inquiries for 4 different projects; conducted an international study in North America, Europe, and Africa; developed user mental models, following Indi Young’s approach.
- Concept and usability research: Conducted usability studies in labs (250 individual sessions over 20 projects), eyetracking studies (100 individual sessions over 8 projects), paper prototyping studies, concept testing and comic storyboard walkthroughs; led focus groups; performed heuristic evaluations; created personas; did benchmark studies.
- Product types: Corporate Web sites, e-commerce Web sites, Web applications (account management, ordering processes, financial, and transactional websites), search engines, business directories, extranets, and intranets.
- International background: Work experience in Canada, France, the UK, and Switzerland; fluent speaker of English and French; dual Canadian and French citizenship; well travelled.
- Author and speaker: Article for UXmatters; presentations at professional conferences; workshops
- Education: Masters Degree in Information Science; two-year certificate in Psychotherapy and Counselling; Masters Degree in Archaeology.

Isabelle Peyrichoux’s Specialties:

In-depth interviews, mental model approach (Indi Young), contextual inquiries, field studies, usability testing, eye-tracking, concept studies, comic storyboard walkthroughs, paper prototyping studies, focus groups, personas, heuristic evaluation, stakeholder interviews, brainstorm moderation
