Marc de Oliveira’s Summary

Marc de Oliveira from PYTHIA Information is the manager of the podcast portal podhead.net. He is also director on the ODTUG board and the chairman of the Designer SIG of the Dansih Oracle User Group (OUGDK). He possesses a M. Sc. in Computer Scinece from the University of Copenhagen from 1992 and has been working as a system analyst and system developer for the past 18 years primarely with the Oracle Designer tools.
He is a regular presenter at conferences and has written technical articles for both the ODTUG Technical Journal and OracleEkspert Magazine. He has also co-authered "Oracle8 Developer's Guide" published by IDG Books.

Marc de Oliveira’s Specialties:

System Analysis, Requirements Gathering, Application Design, Application Development, Methodology, Project Management, Testing, Oracle Designer, CASE, Education
