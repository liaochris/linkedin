Pat Scherer’s Summary

Working with executives, entrepreneurs and a hand-picked pool of freelance specialists, I create and lead teams to transform ideas into new businesses, products and services.

Offloading the details to our experienced teams quick-starts the initiative and frees executives to focus on critical ongoing business and expansion. In addition, we leverage new technologies, best practices and open source software to efficiently manage and deploy without incurring the overhead of large contract organizations and high-priced consultancies.

Products and services include: business plans, business analytics, roadmaps, design, requirements and deployment for websites, e-commerce sites and internet hosted applications.

Pat Scherer’s Specialties:

> Identifying a competitive edge and a strategy to grow it
> Productizing software and services (including business analysis, design and managing all phases of deployment)
> Bridging gaps by driving new initiatives
