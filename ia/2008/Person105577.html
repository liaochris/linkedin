Kevin Paulson’s Summary

I started with CliCKS Document Management in production in November, 2002. I worked the night shift for a year running the team before moving to Washington, DC with Brian Ranallo to assist in the opening of the DC operation. I remember the very first day we walked in with zero sales to our first customer. I was promoted to the position of Customer Service Manager and 6 months later took over the position of Managing Partner of our Columbus, OH operation. After a couple of years in Columbus and short stop over in our St. Louis operation I came back to Washington, DC to assist in the growth of our flagship operation. The last 9 years have been spent in Washington, DC where I have handled everything from business development, purchasing management, recruiting and training.

I am very exicted about my new role as National Director of Business Development and I look forward to working with our team to build our business and the skills of our sales and marketing team.
