Sébastien St-Hilaire’s Summary

More than 10 years ago, in 1995, I had the opportunity to work for the independent newspaper Le Devoir in 1995 where I discovered the Newspapers/Medias & Publishing industry. 5 years after, in 2000, I joined Transcontinental as a national account manager for the business publication group before joining Metro Montreal in 2003 as the Sales & Marketing director. Metro Montreal was a start up and a joint venture of Transcontinental, Metro International and Gesca. More recently I was the Business Developpement Director of the business publications group of Transcontiental

Having worked in the Media & Publishing industry over the past 12 years, in 2007, I had decide to jump into an amazing Internet business composed with a very talented group of people in a company called Generation Flash, a Gesca subsidiaries. I had the opportunity to work with experts on web developpement, programming, licensing of web2.0 & Social Media killer application & online marketers. After an amazing year with them plus a serie of personnal events that forge my decision to invest all of my time in my primary passions, numbers, finance & entrepreneurship.

Today, I am know a partner of Leblanc Martineau St-Hilaire, portfolio managers at VMD.

I have a bachelor degree in Mathematics with a minor in financial analysis.
