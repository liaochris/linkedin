Yvonne Levy’s Summary

Bilingual, AFAA Certified Personal Trainer, group fitness instructor and AFPA Sports Nutrition Counselor with 8 years experience teaching in the United States East and West coasts. Former Director of Personal Training and Group Exercise for Gold’s Gym and regular group instructor at Crunch Fitness in Miami, FL and San Francisco, CA. Regular presenter at the Miami Beach Sports and Fitness Festival. Creator of Hot Mommy Fitness, a program designed to meet the beauty and fitness needs of today's mommies. Current Group Fitness Manager at the Univeristy of Miami Miller School of Medicine's Medical Wellness Center.

Yvonne Levy’s Specialties:

Mamagement of group fitness programs. Extensive experience in group exercise training as well as one-on-one personal training. Also, very experienced presenting on stage in front of a live audience. Modeling, acting and radio broadcasting experience as well.
