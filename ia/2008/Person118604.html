Mamen Pradel’s Summary

Actually, I am working in Net2u, a internet company in Zaragoza (Spain), my roll is as Web Designer.
Previosly, I was working in London for Cobra Beer as a member of the creative team. My previous position was working for Inspired HR as a designer for 7 months. In Spain, I was working within the creative department at Vodafone Europe, whilst I was working at Vodafone I also worked on several freelance projects, including the acclaimed film "Descongelate" and also on the website for the famous Spanish painter Fernando Larraz.
I have a degree in Digital Design by Istituto Europeo di Design Madrid, and I also have produced multiple creative projects in Flash, Photoshop, Freehand and Dreamweaver.
I consider myself to be an excellent team player, as well as being able to work on my own initiative. I am self motivated, organized and always willing to learn. I pride myself on always giving 110%.
