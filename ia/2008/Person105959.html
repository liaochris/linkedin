Kevin Sheehan’s Summary

Over 27 years of technical experience in the telecommunications and government sectors. Extensive technical and security architecture background including designing, hosting, monitoring, tuning and troubleshooting complex, scalable, highly secure and highly available business solutions utilizing Oracle E-Business Suite 11i Applications, Oracle 10gAS GOTS, COTS and custom J2EE applications and Oracle Real Application Clusters (RAC) in both Massively Parallel Processing (MPP) and Symmetric Multi-Processing (SMP) environments. Enterprise Application Integration (EAI) experience includes Oracle Advanced Queuing, Oracle SOA Suite, Vitria and Tibco. Highly skilled at configuring F5 BigIP load balancers for continuous application availability. Monitoring experience includes Oracle Enterprise Manager Grid Control, Quest Foglight and Quest Spotlight. Security expertise includes applying technical controls to the entire technology stack (hardening) as well as participating in and developing documentation for the Certification and Accreditation (C&A) of business systems including System Security Plans. Experienced at securing environments to meet or exceed the Department of Homeland Security's Secure Baselines.
