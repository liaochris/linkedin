Dena Patton’s Summary

I am a trained coach specializing in life coaching for women and business coaching for successful entrepreneurs. Out of 8 years of coaching I created a company called Chat, Chew & Chocolate (CCC) which is an events and education committed to making a difference for millions of women worldwide. We have been in business for three years and have 8 chapters across the country, in addition to offering private/corporate events in Arizona. The super-woman era has caught up to most women leaving them with a drained sense of self, stressed out, burned out, heart disease, depression, confusion and lack of fun and fulfillment in their life. CCC encourages women to bring fun and friendship back to their busy lives and really create awareness around this subject. We have created a weekend retreats and workshops that will be traveling the country in 08-09.

CCC's vision: Together with our community we officially broke the barriers for women to once again incorporate play, boundaries, and time for themselves into their lives on a weekly basis. We provide products, messages, programs, events and services that profoundly enrich, enhance and empower women worldwide.

Dena Patton’s Specialties:

Corporate & private events that bring fun and relatedness back into your group. Our custom events are for groups of women from 10-1000. Groups like, professional organizations, associations, small-medium businesses, PTA groups, Mom's clubs, church groups, Red Hat Chapters, team meetings (.i.e. Arbonne) or simply a girlfriend group. We customize our events to tie in your mission, vision or message and we create a fun atmosphere that is unique, and of course we bring a fabulous chocolate buffet.
