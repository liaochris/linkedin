Matt McPeak’s Summary

Mr. McPeak specializes in Oracle Applications implementations and customizations. He is a widely-acknowledged expert in Oracle systems, technically and functionally, and has served in key positions on dozens of successful projects. His superior functional analysis skills and extremely broad technical experience allow him to consistently deliver the highest quality, most cost-effective solutions to his customers.

During his career, Mr. McPeak has worked on, advised on, or supervised projects for over 20 customers in industries such as manufacturing, financial services, food processing, pharmaceuticals, automotive, direct marketing, and distribution.

Mr. McPeak has written and presented several papers and articles on Oracle Applications and related subjects. In legal proceedings, he has appeared as an expert witness on the subjects of both Oracle and Manufacturing Scheduling systems.
