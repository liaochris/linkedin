Erica Motley’s Summary

After two years on Wall Street and a Harvard MBA, Erica began her film career as an international acquisition executive at HBO. She later joined Fox where she launched channels and negotiated deals with film studios, international sales agents and independent producers. As VP of Programming at BMG, Erica structured studio output deals, managed original productions and developed programming strategies for German Pay-TV. She later acquired content from emerging broadband companies at IFILM. As Head of Film for Shine, Erica was responsible for selecting the slate and managing the development, financing and production of UK-based films. She continued her career as head of international acquisitions for UIP, while she acquired local and American films for international distribution.

She is currently a consultant in content acquisitions, international co-productions, and film financing.

Erica Motley’s Specialties:

Negotiating content deals; managing relationships with rights owners; creative and financial evaluation of films; international co-productions, film financing, content acquisitions.
