Jo Guerra’s Summary

I am always happy to share my expertise. If you see something in my profile that might help you, a connection, affiliation, please let me know. I am happy to share information and help you in anyway I can.

Jo Jewelry Company is a wholesale jewelry company that sells to businesses as well as individuals. I started the business in 1992 as Mextique Jewelry importing sterling silver jewelry from Mexico. I rebranded the company to Jo Jewelry when I started importing jewelry & accessories from other countries including Thailand, France, Italy, the Philippines, China, India and Indonesia. Bilingual abilities in English Spanish.

With JoJewelry, I imported utilizing informal and formal entries into the United States and ensured legal compliance issues of NAFTA, US Customs and Harmonized Tariff Schedule. I also project managed, logistically planned, designed, and participated in retail and wholesale shows nationally.
