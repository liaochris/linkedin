Dev Amritesh’s Summary

Consumer Product Marketing has been the main area of my interest and experience. In my Stints at Domino's Pizza, Cadbury India and Whirlpool, I have essentially done the same thing - i.e. "start from consumer understanding and end at delivering solutions derived from this understanding". My experience in the Consumer Durables, FMCG and now in Retail Food Services Marketing/Restaurant Marketing has shown me that how much ever things change wrt Marketing and Consumer Understanding process ... it is that much more that they remain the same!
