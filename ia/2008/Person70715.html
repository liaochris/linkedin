Glenda Roberts’s Summary

C-level: Senior Marketing Executive/ Business Growth Strategist/M&A Executive:

For early stage technology companies, Glenda has served as outside director, interim marketing director, developed business plans and funding strategies, directed the marketing communication and sales development programs, created sales compensation plans and negotiated technology partnerships and channel arrangements. For enterprise firms, she has formulated leadership strategies, sales compensation plans, creative marketing initiatives, effective new product introduction programs and partner portfolio management systems.

Glenda has served in a number of technical and business positions at Microsoft and General Electric. Glenda’s thought leadership in eBusiness began in the mid-1980s when she helped drive GE Information Services’ innovation in business-to-business applications and network services. The breadth and depth of her executive operating experience helps Glenda bring a creative yet pragmatic perspective to consulting clients in North Ameria and Europe, including: Dictaphone, Vorsite Enterprise Search, Spinoza Technology, Spirus Applied Learning and Media Worktable AG.
