Sosa, Fernando’s Summary

I have experience in all sorts of digital mediums. I started web development as a my main source of income and developed over the years. However, my passion lays on 3d animation and game development and everything that comes with it.

i attended school as a traditional Art major and then moved to 3d animation and graduated with a 3d animation major with a minor in Art.

I'm very proud of my work experience since i been working in the industry almost right after i got into college. 1st as a freelance web developer and then as a 3d modeler for a small video game.

However, i finally got my foot in the door after landing a job as a lead 3d modeler for a educational video game for the orlando folkloric dept. Where my knowledge of work pipeline and my leadership landed me a production management job a little after i began working for the company.

Being a full time student and freelancer on the side proved to be a challenge but it was a great opportunity. Soon after that i landed a job as a production assistant for a leading architectural renderings company named renderings.com. i been with the company for a year and half.

renderings.com is partners with g&g advertising who later learn of my many skills and added me as an illustrator and a web developer. Juggling these positions sometimes is a major task but i like the challenge.

On the other hand my freelance has improved thanks to my new renovated website (www.amznfx.com) and i'm currently designing a video game with couple other artists. CHeck my website for updates!!.

Right now h'm still working with renderings.com and now have a BA in computer animation. With no school and lots of more free time i'm looking for better and bigger challenges, opportunities and maybe new jobs.
