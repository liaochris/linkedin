    Initiated company's foray into Smart Card Manufacturing in 2007. As on date have one of the largest Contactless Smart Card manufacturing facility in India.

    Trade Marks:e-Smart(r) and SmartScos(r)

  • Managing Director

    Magnum Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer Software industry)

    December 1995 — Present (12 years 7 months)

    Founded Magnum Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (Magnum) in 1995 after successfully running operations as a proprietorship company since 1992. The company focusses on providing Automation Solutions to the Textile & Apparel Industry in the areas of CAD/CAM and CIM.

    Magnum has had business alliances with some of the world's leading Solution providers amongst them Lectra - France (Since 1999 - Current), Nedgraphics - The Netherlands (1992-2007) & EDI Asia - Hongkong (2000 - Current) & Stork NV - The Netherlands (2006-2007).

    The company is a dominant player in India and has offices across 7 cities viz. New Delhi (HQ), Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Tirupur, Chennai & Jaipur which are major Textile and Apparel Manufacturing centers in India. We count some the largest manufacturers as our valued customers.

    The industries we focus on are Apparel; Automotive (Car Seats and Airbags); Technical Textiles (Aerospace and Aeronautics); Leather (Apparel & Footwear).

    Trade Marks : MAGNUM (r)

  • Director

    Magnum Resources Pvt. Ltd.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Textiles industry)

    2002 — August 2006 (4 years)

    I established Magnum Resources Pvt. Ltd. in 2002 to build a new product line in the Apparel Automation and Manufacturing Machinery segment and to retain focus in our other companies.

    The company has business alliances with leading international brands viz. Pegasus - Japan, Sunstar - Korea, Covemat - France and Cosmotex - Spain.

    The company is well established nationally and provides a unique blend of internationally acclaimed products Industrial Engineering and Consultancy support angle to the entire Apparel Manufacturing Process from Sewing to Packing.

    I successfully dis-invested from this company in August 2006 to focus on the other running businesses.
