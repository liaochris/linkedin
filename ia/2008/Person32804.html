Mack Frankfurter’s Summary

Michael “Mack” Frankfurter is Chief Investment Strategist for Managed Account Research, Inc., an independent Introducing Broker which specializes in managed futures investments. He is also a co-founder and Managing Director of Operations for Cervino Capital Management LLC, a commodity trading advisor and registered investment adviser based in Los Angeles, California. In addition, Mr. Frankfurter is founder and Managing Partner of NextStep Strategies, LLC which provides consulting services for firms in the financial industry.

Started his career in the financial services industry in 1989 with Bank of America. In 1991 he joined The Echelon Group, Inc. as Vice President in charge of operations of Echelon’s managed futures business. As an Associated Person, he oversaw the startup, accounting, client services, compliance, backoffice and marketing of multiple joint venture CTAs including Dreiss Research Corporation, Jackson Grain Management, Range Wise, and others. At same time was also involved in activities related to the establishment of other Echelon-related ventures, including Dignity Partners, Inc., a viatical settlement company that went public in 1996 and subsequently reorganized as Point West Capital Corporation. In January 1999 he left Echelon and Point West and worked as a consultant for FleetBoston Robertson Stephens. In 2000 joined Capco as Senior Consultant working on several long-term projects for various banks including Scudder Kemper/Thomas Weisel (startup private bank joint venture), Commerzbank (royalblue fidessa) and Bank of Montreal (T+1 business analysis). Founded NextStep Strategies, LLC in 2002, a strategic consultant focused on financial services. In 2005 co-founded Cervino Capital Management LLC, a money management company focused on derivatives trading, and in 2007 joined Managed Account Research, Inc. as their Chief Investment Strategist.
