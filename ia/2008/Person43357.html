Daniel Collett’s Summary

Operating a growing list management and email marketing campaign company. Combining computer and internet skill sets with two successful careers in marketing and publishing.
Building a nationally prominent marketing and monetization operation and positioning it as an industry leader in multi-channel direct marketing.
Newspaper and magazine editor for 14 years with award winning military publications, hotel and entertainment magazines, business newspaper and city magazine. Creative director and senior copy writer for overseas advertising agency with international clients in Europe, America and Asia. More than 20 years in printing and direct mail operations and former industry co-chair for Southern Nevada Postal Customer Council.
