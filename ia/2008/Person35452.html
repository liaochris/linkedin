Chris Beckett’s Summary

Experienced Strategic Business Development Manager

Co-founded Scholarly Information Strategies (SIS) in 2002 to help publishers, intermediaries and libraries develop and deliver electronic products and services. Specialising in:

Electronic Journals Consultancy
Electronic Content Services
The application of Web2.0 technologies to business
Business Development especially the Marketing and Sales of Electronic Content.

Prior to SIS held senior Sales, Marketing and Product Development positions at Ingenta, CatchWord and Blackwell Information Services; including Vice-President Library Services for Ingenta, International Publisher Sales Director for Ingenta, and Sales and Marketing Director of CatchWord. Prior to CatchWord, I held a number of senior management positions at Blackwell's Information Services including General Manager of Blackwell's Online Bookshop, which I initiated and oversaw and was a member of Blackwell Information Services Executive Board.

Chris Beckett’s Specialties:

Strategic Sales, Marketing & Electronic Product Development for the scholarly market.
My particular interest is in unjamming the jammed, bringing fresh thinking to persistent problems. I have extensive experience and connections in the UK and North American Library and Scholarly Publishing market place.
