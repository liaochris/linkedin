Allan McLennan’s Summary

Key operations, financial, development, marketing, sales and management executive with a career distinguished by breadth of experience, caliber of qualification and record of achievements in building teams, identifying consumer and market migration paths in digital media (cross-media technology, entertainment, media on-demand, advertising, digital distribution and advanced television - broadband, VOD, PVR/DVR, theatrical, cableTV, satellite, iTV, IPTV, mobileTV, wireless) hardware and software products worldwide. Experience in managing entreprenual to mid-size organizations with full operations and financial responsibilites, as well as developing and executing next generation product offerings that have expanded market acceptance, boosted revenues, driven growth and outpaced competition. Led multiple LOB's and teams optimizing operational performance and success with leading edge digital, entertainment technology, and advertising international companies. Active participant, board member and speaker on media technology industry acceleration, entertainment consumer and standards organizations in formulation of policy issues involving the media, entertainment and broadband industries worldwide.
