Allison Hughes’s Summary

I graduated college in 2001 with a degree in Image Design, giving me an understanding of traditional photography as well as applied digital imaging. Since then, I have worked with many photographers and advertising agencies throughout the US, and have developed a strong understanding of the commercial side of retouching, as well as a great artistic eye from working on many fine art projects. I not only take direction well when given an assignment, but i also bring a lot to the table and can offer great solutions and recommendations to make an image even better.

My goal is to further develop my skills and expand my freelance work into my own business, where I can assist photographers and artists in making their images as fabulous as they can be. I would also like to get back to my own fine art work and expand my own talents as an artist.

My job is to make images as beautiful as possible, while creating a specific look, unique to what each client has in mind. The retouching is meant to only enhance the image and not to look "retouched". Retouching is a process or a journey to make photography become a work of art, whether it be for galleries, magazines, or web.

Allison Hughes’s Specialties:

Traditional & Digital Photography, Extensive retouching/compositing skills, Typography, Design, Pre-press file management, Fine art printing
