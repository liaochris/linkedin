Ian Vernon’s Summary

Ian is responsible for leading the commercial operations of AmanziTel. Prior to joining AmanziTel he served as Business Development Director for companies such as LCC, WFI, EWI and Lorris Consulting.

Before focusing in business development and corporate management he contributed his technical expertise to Agilent, SAFCO, ComOpt, Smart Communications, Far East Bank, World Bank and served as an associate professor at the University of Santo Tomas. He also served in the board of Q Corporation and was CTO at FTT.

Ian also held consulting and advisory positions to various network operators, financial institutions, government agencies and expert witness to landmark telecom arbitration cases. Ian holds multiple degrees in engineering and master of business administration and business management
