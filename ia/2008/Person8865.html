Adam Martin’s Summary

I am a detail-oriented freelance graphic designer and creative consultant working Lexington, KY. I am able to work on a variety of projects in multiple environments, as well as capable of quick turnaround on specific projects; with the ability to work creatively in a team environment or as an individual. I was recently employed by the 3rd best sports marketing company in the nation (as ranked by Sports Business Journal). While there I worked with high profile clientele such as the SEC, NCAA, University of Kentucky, University of MIchigan, Boston College University, American Volleyball Coaches Association, Jim Beam Brands, Jefferson Pilot Sports, and Dave Campbell's Texas Football just to name a few.

I am skilled in all forms of print and publication design, as well as specializing in photo-retouching/manipulation and heavy Photoshop design. I am also proficient in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Quark, Acrobat, Distiller, and Microsoft Office, Dreamweaver, CSS and HTML.

I have a passion for design in all forms as well as a deep will to learn. I have been freelancing full-time under the Martin Design Studio, LLC for over a year now; however am willing to accept full-time employment and/or relocate to ad agencies or design firms in Lexington, KY; Louisville, KY; Nashville, TN; Knoxville, TN; Atlanta, GA; Cincinnati, OH; Charleston, SC; or any other southeastern city with a great design scene.

Adam Martin’s Specialties:

typography, branding, identity development, photo-retouching/manipulation, illustration, packaging, advertising design, direct mail, publication design, prepress/production, print collateral.
