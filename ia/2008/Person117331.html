Lynn Hoganson’s Summary

I am very excited to be a part of the team at KLEINFELDER. We are an Employee Owned Company with many exciting opportunities in the fields of environmental, geotechnical, energy, federal contracts and construction materials management. We have a dynamic staff of 2,000 employees nationwide. Since we are employee owned (and our employees are our future), we build strength in our staff by encouraging continuing education, developing leadership from within, and vesting each employee with the authority to provide responsive, value-added service. Our diverse staff includes a wide range of disciplines in the fields of engineering, earth sciences, construction management, and information systems management.

To learn more about us, please visit us at www.KLEINFELDER.COM.
