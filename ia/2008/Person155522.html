Rahul Choudhury’s Summary

I work at Q42 as a software engineer for project work (as everyone else here) with a specific focus on user friendly implementations and as an unofficial UI conventions advisor.

As personal growth takes me from my roots in graphic design, through web design and development, towards interaction and game design, I find myself applying these skills across many fields and coming out ahead as a result.

My insight and experience in a variety of backgrounds has started to give me an ability to foresee problems in projects before they happen, offer constructive options and solutions if they happen, and through my experience with being in the programming trenches, know how to implement them myself.

I'm currently trying to conceive of a way to effectively combine these skills into a single, optimized, "official" profession. I'd love to hear your experiences or thoughts regarding the user experience, interaction design, game design and copy writing.
