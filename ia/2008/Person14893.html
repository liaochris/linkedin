Anupam Saraph’s Summary

My personal mantra is "create enduring value". When all is said and done, I'm an ideas person- a lateral and systems thinker. I established my professional reputation implementing innovative projects and creating enduring value for businesses and governments from back in the 80s. Currently I lead an existence as an advisor, organizational learning consultant, systems specialist, scenario developer, venture designer, author, and speaker.

I am the co-founder of a think-tank and a management consultancy. I am also a life student of the roles of people and systems driving change and a ‘street-artist’ at capturing mental models of change for design, strategy and building enduring value.

Anupam Saraph’s Specialties:

Organizational learning, out-of-the-box systems thinking, innovation, strategy, reengineering, IS design, business and economic modeling and simulation, scenario based planning, evangelism, new-product design, keynote speeches.
