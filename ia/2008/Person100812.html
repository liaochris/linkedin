Justine Bellavita’s Summary

Web Content and Community Manager with a broad experience in International Journalism, specialized in News, Entertainment, Women, Food and Travels. Seven years experience in web content and community management, four years working for a major Italian media group (Telecom Italia Media S.p.A.). Recently hired by the biggest publisher in Italy (Arnoldo Mondadori Editore S.p.A.).

Three years in professional journalism corresponding from US and France for both magazines and the Press Agency ApCom.

Expert in web creativity, online community generation and in the integration of the Internet with others media. Assisted in the production of short format news features from pre-production stage through post.
Trilingual: English, French and Italian. Highly motivated and focused, always interested in exploring new challenges in the means of communication.
