Thomas Horsten’s Summary

Experienced and driven project manager, software engineer and entrepreneur. Has worked in the software industry for over 13 years in a variety of companies from start-ups to established corporations. Roles have included software engineering (embedded systems, Symbian OS, Linux, C, C++), project and group management, business start-up, technical consulting. Specializes in Internet protocols, Internet security, embedded software development, Linux and Symbian device driver and low-level software development.
