John Ridgway’s Summary

Multi-Emmy award winner John Ridgway's international media branding is viewed daily on six continents, roughly three-quarters of the world’s population.

Through his production company Via/Novocom John has provided brand design and strategy for some of the world’s most-watched channels and shows, including “NBC Nightly News”, “Meet the Press”, “CBS Evening News”, “Monday Night Football,” “Entertainment Tonight,” CNBC, MSNBC, and global events such as World Cup Soccer, and The Academy Awards. Ridgway’s world wide client base includes Paramount, Discovery, ESPN, NewsCorp, Fuji Japan, Hong Kong Cable, ORT Russia, STAR Asia, RTL Germany, MediaCorp Singapore, and SKY U.K..

Via/Novocom also specializes in Corporate and Institutional screen branding and media positioning. The client roster includes Infinity, ATT, J.C. Penny, Toshiba, and The Olympic Organizing Committee.

Prior to moving to Los Angeles Ridgway resided in Arizona and is a graduate of Arizona State University. John was formerly Art Director of the New Times and his photography has appeared in the Washington Post and Newsday.

In the 1990’s, he co-created the first television political campaign in Russia for the country’s then President Boris Yeltsin. In recent years he has consulted on America’s world broadcast news brand presence for the White house and the U.S. State Department.

Ridgway teaches media branding at
at UCLA, Otis College of Art and Design, the International Film School in Cologne and has established endowed scholarships at Arizona State University and Otis College of Art and Design.

Ridgway has circled the globe more than 25 times and visited over 75 countries and all seven continents. He now resides in Malibu, California.
