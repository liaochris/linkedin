Theresa Anthony’s Experience

  • PA CareerLink Site Administrator

    Bureau of Workforce Development Partnership - PA Department of Labor and Industry

    (Government Administration industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 4 months)

    Oversee daily operations of PA CareerLink Crawford County. CareerLink is the workforce delivery system, mandated from the Workforce Investment Act of 1998. Title I Providers include Partners for Performance whose staff deliver programs related Adult workers, Dislocated workers, CAA (Career Advancement Accounts), OJT (On the Job Training), and Senior programs. Venango Training and Development staff deliver programs to Youth (ages 14-21). BWDP is a partner from the State of PA whose staff provide programs related to welfare and unemployed workers and other programs. OVR (Office of Vocational Rehabilitation) provides services to those with various degrees of disability (either physical or mental). I provide functional management to all partner staff and direct management to BWDP (PA) state staff. I am responsible for general HR functions as well as budgeting, compliance to federal/state regulations, and strategic planning and implementation.
