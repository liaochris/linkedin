Jon Cooper’s Summary

Internet marketing strategist with a focus in the Music industry and the Search Engine industry. Producer of music recordings, composer and orchestrator. History of utilizing technology and innovative ideas to execute large scale sales and customer acquisition efforts.

* Coordinates product development and the emergence of underlying systems to improve the quality of Unreal's services.

** Responsibilities:
- Unreal's Marketing Technology Platform, integrating search engine marketing campaign management, quality control and analysis

- Campaign Content and Merchant Data Syndication, combining advanced feed building capabilities with a comprehensive product offering in the Paid Inclusion and Comparison Shopping space

- Development of next generation marketing products in areas such as Permission Marketing, Local and Mobile Marketing

* Served as Director of Distribution Programs at HighBeam, Inc., publisher of HighBeam, eLibrary and Encyclopedia.com.

** Responsible for acquiring new customers through partnerships with search engines and online properties. Cooper developed a marketing technology platform that regularly provided tens of millions of URLs directly into the major search indices.

* Launched Tucows' online music distribution initiative

** Was among the first to launch a pay-per-song download service along with co-promotions with established label artists and online music portals at MSN and AOL.

* Directed sales operations for Disc Makers, the largest independent CD replicator in the US.

** Was an instrumental part of the management team responsible for Disc Makers' dramatic growth, tripling sales over nine years of innovative service.

Completed a B.B.A. from the University of Wisconsin's School of Business and a degree in Music Composition from Boston's Berklee College of Music.

Jon Cooper’s Specialties:

Digital content distribution; Search engine business partnerships; Exploiting the intersection of marketing, digital distribution and technology.
