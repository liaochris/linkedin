Thomas Dinan’s Summary

Strong technical management skills; able to motivate and lead professional teams to complete projects both on time and within budget. Able to delegate effectively to fully engage the skills of subordinates. Key experience planning and implementing development projects as well as sustaining manufacturing processes. Outstanding presentation and communication skills. Two years of technical management developing and transferring processes into manufacturing in the disk drive industry. Twelve years of experience developing processes and transferring them into manufacturing. More than two decades of experience in electrochemical research, process development and process transfer. Skilled inventor and researcher with 26 U.S. Patents and 9 publications relating to electrochemical processes and equipment development. Ten years of experience conducting university research in electrolytic processes.
