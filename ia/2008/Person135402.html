Moondance Pictures’s Summary

Anny Slater is a freelance writer for the Australian sketch show COMEDY INC- The Late Shift. The show was recently nominated for a Rose D’or TV award and six 2006 AFI awards. She recently attended N.I.D.A. as part of the Institute’s prestigious Directors Intensive program and in 2008 is studying directing at A.F.T.R.S.

She has just helmed the much anticipated political comedies ATONEMENT TONIGHT and BARNEY THE TERRIER and is currently preparing to direct a music video for the DVD release of a major U.S. pop band’s latest album. A BARNEY series has recently been commissioned by Sony Pictures for international distribution on mobile and sell through to TV and Moondance Pictures recently delivered episodes 1 to 6.

She has directed and produced to rave reviews two Woody Allen one-act plays '”Death” & “God”, at Sydney's Tom Mann Theatre, resulting in a subsequent personal acknowledgement by Woody Allen himself. As a result of the success of the plays she was mentored through a W.I.F.T. mentor scheme with Mark Lazarus of FoxIcon, the then joint venture between Mel Gibson’s Icon Productions and 20th Century Fox.

Moondance Pictures’s Honors:

Her Australian political comedy THE BALL was short listed for nomination in the best live action short category of the 2004 Academy Awards, was nominated for a United Nations Media Peace Prize and won a Christopher Wetzel comedy award from Chicago’s Gene Siskel Center. The film has screened at more than 60 film festivals around the World and was sold to Australian TV and released on DVD in Europe.

Anny’s script The Adventures of Roman Pilgrim won in its category as best screenplay at the Moondance International Film Festival and at the Disney sponsored Santa Clarita International Family Film Festival in Los Angeles. The script was a semi finalist at the 2003 Slamdance International Film Festival. The corresponding film is expected to be released in 2007.
