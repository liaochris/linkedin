Brian Kerr’s Summary

Each person is different; no two groups are exactly alike. So why are we using the exact same tools to create web sites and applications? Let's map your resources and constraints onto the capabilities of the best open source software out there, and then figure out how to make it all happen.

You can do a lot with a little.

I know, because that's what I do.

    Grad school! Rolled my own curriculum, with emphasis on computing in the public interest and strategies for using design, analytics, and social media to nurture online conversation and communities.

I like the site since it wears its accessible and equitable motivation on its sleeve.
