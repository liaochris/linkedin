Erik Burckle’s Summary

Expert strategic thinker specializing in developing and executing business tactics thereby guiding executive management to improve current operations, invest in profitable new business segments, open new markets and launch new products.

High-performance leader with an extensive history of professional experience creating and executing value-based sales and marketing strategies and tactics that propel revenue/profit growth. A visionary with extreme savvy in establishing strong negotiating positions, communicating competitive value and exploiting new business ventures. Strong team leader who focuses on hiring the right people, promoting camaraderie and offering practical solutions that have big impact. An effective change agent with a high level of integrity.

I have established core marketing functions within Fortune 500 companies for the wireless telecom, consumer electronics and residential homebuilding industries. I have demonstrated success in areas such as key account management, new business development, new product creation and launch, competitive intelligence, advertising and sales support, primary and secondary research, and international marketing and research initiatives.

I have had the unique opportunity in my career to be directly involved with two category creating consumer electronic products – the FRS two-way radio with Motorola and Audrey (an Internet appliance) while with 3Com. In both cases I was part of the core management team responsible for developing and bringing these products to market. My efforts have included such endeavors as: conducting consumer research and segmentation, ethnographic research, analyzing distribution partners and channels, assessing various media effectiveness and monitoring the competition and the competitive landscape.
