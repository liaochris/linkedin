Turi McKinley’s Summary

I am an interaction designer, experience strategist, and multimedia enthusiast with an MFA in Design and Technology from the Parsons School of Design. My creative interests are in objects and spaces that build emotional and immersive experiences.

I have particular strengths in information architecture, research, design research and usability. As a design consultant my markets include education, healthcare, innovation design, and rich media services, and my platforms span the tactile to the conceptual.

I have lived, researched, and worked extensively in the United Kingdom, India, Nepal, Korea, and China. My academic background is in Anthropology with emphasis on symbolic anthropology and ethnography.

Outside of the office, I blow glass, travel the world, and experiment with bringing objects to life.
