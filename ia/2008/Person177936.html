Alexander Dort [The Social Business Club]’s Summary

The Social Business Club
Business with responsibility!

The Social Business club combines probably the first time the fields of social networking, online marketing and Corporate Social Responsibility in one single network.

The Club already offers a fantastic PR & News portal (where you can reach millions of people worldwide within minutes!), a job and event portal as well as a huge network for individual specialists groups, forums, blogs, media management, B-to-B marketplaces, an intelligent contact management where you can import and manage your online and offline contacts, Polls and much more to his members and has a good chance to get world's biggest and most complete business network.

But the Social Business Club is much more than this!

One of the main targets of the Social Business Club is to support international relief organizations with the income of the network.

100% of all donations to the club will be used for this support. In a quarterly poll and discussion, our members can choose the intended purpose of this fund.

The base of this concept should be clear:

Building and growing an additional social responsibility as an added value of social networking.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) in a new concept!

So the Social Business Club is a win-win situation for all participants!
