Verlaine Crawford’s Summary

Verlaine's professional experience includes marketing, advertising and public relations. As Vice President of Marketing and Business Development for NGG+Partners architecture and land planning firm, she promotes the talents and innovative design capability of the firm, which specializes in multifamily, mixed-use communities, resort, senior and student housing and urban infill projects.

Beginning in 2002 she has served as C.E.O./Executive Director of the San Juan Capistrano followed by the Laguna Beach Chambers of Commerce. In the 1980's she was Vice President of Marketing/Sales for two software startups in Silicon Valley and in Southern California from 1998-2001, she was VP Marketing for web based company and Archos, a computer peripherals and MP3 players, based in Paris, France. Verlaine has also done marketing for restaurants and resorts, interior designers, real estate and health care.

In the 1990's she wrote the book "Ending the Battle Within" and traveled around the world doing seminars, personal and business consulting and appeared on radio and TV shows nationwide.

Verlaine's goal is to use her experience and expertise to promote innovative, dynamic companies and to increase business development through the use of outstanding marketing, advertising, public relations, website design, optimization and online marketing.

Verlaine Crawford’s Specialties:

Strategic marketing, branding and identity, advertising and public relations. Utilize the full range of communications capabilities: writing, public speaking, photography, graphic and website design.
