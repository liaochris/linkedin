Mathew Needleman’s Summary

Mathew Needleman, teacher and independent filmmaker, has been integrating video production in the elementary curriculum for over twelve years. He remains on the cutting edge of twenty-first century technologies and web 2.0 tools in his own classroom and in his support of other teachers.

He is dedicated to closing the digital divide and providing technology access to low income students, English Language Learners, children receiving special education services, and those who are identified as gifted.
