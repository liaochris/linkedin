Les Wilcock’s Summary

Opening Doors is a strategic marketing communications company. Through our key strengths in idea generation, creative design, planning, and marketing consultancy we help our clients to have meaningful and engaging dialogue with their customers and prospects, producing innovative and winning campaigns that result in tangible improvements in sales revenue and profitability. For example in the last 2 years we have helped AIM listed Individual Restaurant Company Plc grow from £13 million revenue and a pre tax loss of £3million to £52 million turnover and a pre tax profit of £3.5 million.

We have recently assisted Sameday Plc to move out of low margin sectors into high margin defence (Guardian brand http://www.guardian-service.co.uk/) and commercial documentation and bio hazard (Diplomat brand http://www.diplomat-service.co.uk/) resulting in the client capturing market share and new sources of higher margin sales revenue.

We also help clients with new innovative products to establish market potential and need as well as assisting them to take the product to pre production prototypes including market research, packaging and raising market awareness - see www.tietowel.co.uk

The firm's list of clients continues to grow and now includes: Indiv
idual Restaurant Company Plc, Pierce CA, Booth Ainsworth, Lime Management, British Airways, BASF, Zurich Financial Services, CHD Enterprises Ltd, Cogent Sector Skills Council, Crest Medical Plc, Enterasys Inc, Sameday Plc, Open Mind Technologies and World Courier Inc.

We are retained by many of our clients to help them develop and execute marketing campaigns including business development focussed projects that produce measurable improvements in sales results.
