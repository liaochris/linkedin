Julie Thomas’s Summary

I have managed my own business, JTDA, for over 10 years, providing design, print and web/multi-media solutions to a wide customer base. Much of my design inspiration is found whilst walking on the Malvern Hills, with my head in the clouds but my feet firmly on the ground! Most of my work is from recommendation, and many of my clients are long standing.

Some years ago I realised the massive need for quality web/multi-media based design. Not wanting to run the danger of becoming a ‘Jack of all Trades’, rather preferring to remain a ‘Master of One’, I decided to go into partnership with my colleague, Terry – forming a new company, 'The Artitechs' – combining my creative designs with his technical skills to provide the optimum solution for all web/multi-media design requirements.

Over the years, Terry and I have both discovered a wealth of talented, like minded people; copywriters, illustrators, photographers, printers, website technicians – all of whom have helped us as individuals to successfully fulfil a wide range of projects. We now have a combined pool of talent, which we can call upon whenever required, forming teams of people, dedicated to the requirements of the client, although we are still most definitely ‘hands on’ and personally oversee all projects.
