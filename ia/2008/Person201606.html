Yves Ackaouy’s Summary

At 36 years old, I have accumulated 13 years of strong professional experience in sales, marketing, business development and business unit management within a variety of prosperous industries.

I started my career as a front line salesman for a renowned French liquor company in 1994, grew into middle-management positions in an American Multinational Company from 1995 to 2002 and since then am holding senior management positions in fast growing ventures.

A decade of successful business development has allowed me to build privileged relationships with a vast network of skilled professionals in over 30 countries. Throughout my life, I have been keen to travel and discover new horizons. My professional career has fulfilled my desire for world exposure.

I am self motivated and dedicated to my assignments. I aspire to head a Business Unit and actively partake in the strategic development of a promising company.

Yves Ackaouy’s Specialties:

My main specialty is new business development for companies highly focused on core activity. At IFF, I handled new business opportunities such as Private Label business which has significantly grown since then. More recently, I developed sales in a region remotely interested in personal safety equipment yet found a way to focus our efforts on highly concerned targeted industries (Construction, Telecom, oil & gas, utilities...) which eventually led to significant growth in sales.
