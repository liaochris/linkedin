Kevin K Lee’s Summary

Kevin is a senior consultant with Everest Group, a global operational strategy advisory firm headquartered in Dallas, Texas. He has advised on engagements for U.S. and international clients in various industries such as Media, Retail, Energy & Utilities, Drinks, Chemicals, and Financial.

Kevin received his MBA from UT-McCombs in Austin with an emphasis in marketing. Prior to the MBA program, Kevin worked as the Asia marketing manager for an engineering plastics trading firm and gained extensive international business experience with a specific focus on East Asia and Continental Europe.

Kevin’s business interest and strength lie in sourcing strategy formulation and execution, industry and market research, financial analysis and valuation, and customer insight generation.

In his spare time, Kevin volunteers as a business plan advisor for the Prison Entrepreneurship Program (PEP). He also enjoys reading, traveling, sketching, and singing Karaoke.

Kevin is fluent and literate in Chinese (Mandarin, Taiwanese, limited Shanghainese) and English.
