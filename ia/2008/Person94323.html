John Bruce’s Summary

With over 8 years of professional online experience, I continue to expand my extensive skill set pertaining to all aspects of Marketing especially Internet Marketing, and Web Development, Design, Promotion, and Administration.

My constant goal is: How can I make the customer/client feel like they are the most important aspect of our company? Do the best job I can for that client/customer because THEY ARE the most important aspect of a company and the reason a company is in business.

The websites I build/manage/promote always have the customer/end user as their number one priority. If the customer is not satisfied then I'm not satisfied. The goal of a company's web strategy is to attract their attention, give them information they want and need, then give them a good reason to return. Constant changes and improvements as well as continuous marketing will ensure a site's "stickiness" and growth.

In the online world, you must keep up to date with the latest trends and technologies and always be in a constant state of learning. I am ALWAYS ready and willing to do research and learn new programs, technologies and procedures and really look forward to it.

I consider myself an all-around Internet "junkie". When I manage a site(s), I dive right into all of the aspects of a site's successful web presence. I enjoy being able to handle the administration, designing, and marketing of a company's website(s), (basically a jack-of-all-trades web guy), and when the sites' numbers aren't increasing, I continue to work and find ways to increase visitors to the website(s). It is a constant goal.

John Bruce’s Specialties:

I take ownership of the website(s) I manage and make them a part of me. Ability to do all functions of online marketing including email marketing, SEO, SEM, and affiliate management. Also experienced with video editing and conversion formatting. Ad server maintenance and administration as well as graphic design, banner design, web design and image optimization.
