Amit Maheshwari’s Summary

Amit Maheshwari specializes in evaluating, creating, and growing information technology (IT) and business process outsourcing (BPO) business. He has created successful startups in the areas of automotive retail, healthcare operations, and outsourcing services. He has also worked for Fortune 100 companies such as General Electric and consulted for companies such as ADP and Baxter Healthcare.

Most recently, his startup AutoStyleMart, Inc. was acquired by DealerTrack Inc. (TRAK). He is currently responsible for growing the accessories solutions business at DealerTrack, Inc.

Amit Maheshwari’s Specialties:

Amit's key experience areas include: Software product strategy and management, Offshore / global based delivery, Strategic IT solutions development and selling, Venturing and angel investing, P&L and business operations management.

His focus verticals include: High tech, automotive, healthcare, IT/BPO Services.
