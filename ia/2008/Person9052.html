Ameet Mehta’s Summary

Interactive Designer specializing in Flash application development with over 10 years experience working in an array of web technologies. Currently employed as a Senior Engineer at Fluid Inc. in San Francisco, CA where I have served as Creative and Tech lead for brands such as Reebok, The North Face, Design Within Reach and Timberland. My interests lie in developing new user experiences which explore the mutual relationship between aesthetics, usability and simplicity.

Ameet Mehta’s Specialties:

Flash, user interfaces, information architecture, branding and identity, rapid prototyping, project scheduling, small team management, client interaction, AS2, AS3, Adobe AIR, XML, UML, MySQL, JavaScript, Photoshop, illustration, storyboarding
