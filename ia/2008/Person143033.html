Nicolette Toussaint’s Summary

Need to launch a brand or a company? Create a big, complicated website? Do you have multiple clients to please? An interdisciplinary team to build and manage? A need to coordinate the web with print, video or events?

I can make it happen - on time and on budget.

I'm a seasoned sales, marketing, and communication leader with more than 20 years of combined experience impacting corporate performance through skillful development, enhancement, and orchestration of multi-media marketing efforts. I'm able to expertly align communication efforts with marketing and branding campaigns, and I'm a successful team builder focused on the continuous delivery of top quality products and services.

I'm an accomplished project manager, adept at navigating the critical path through a complicated design project. Years in ad agencies and PR have honed my skills in account and creative management while client-side experience in industries ranging from travel/hospitality, packaged food, software, transportation, utilities and education has given me very transferable strategic planning and marketing skills.

I offer:

* Strong strategic planning and project management
* Successful creative direction, staff selection, mentoring and supervision
* Award- and grant-winning writing - a talent for communicating a vision and translating jargon into persuasive prose
* Disciplined and beautiful graphic design - articulation across video, audio, the web and print

The bonus is that I'm an energetic, results-oriented self-starter with the balance and focus that comes only with maturity. And I'm not too self impressed to laugh at myself.
