Geoff Katz’s Summary

10+ years experience defining, designing and managing the development of interactive entertainment and advertising products and services, including product managment for apps and services deployed on interactive TV set-top, DVR, mobile, DVD and IP platforms. I also have extensive experience producing broadcast television advertising. Throughout my career I have effectively managed teams of creative professionals and engineers and shipped a wide range of products. Currently I am co-chair of the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences Emmy Awards committee for Interactive Television.

Looking for opportunities to build new businesses, recruit creative teams and manage the development process for products and services that will change the way consumers enjoy entertainment, news and advertising in the 'Living Room of the Future'.

Geoff Katz’s Specialties:

Strategic consulting, product management, business development, user experience design, broadcast television production, creative direction.
