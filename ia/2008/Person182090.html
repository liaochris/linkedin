Steve Purkiss [steve@purkiss.com]’s Summary

1 Oct 2007 update:

Today I am excited to launch projectstars.com - a place to share expertise, network, and find work! It's an invititation-only network, please connect with me if you would like an invite.

What I do - Foster connections, both human and digital

My long term vision is to set up a network of local technology centers/coworking spaces based around Free/Open Source Software. The goal is to provide sustainable centres which help more people to find out more about Free/Open Source Software and provide a framework for individuals to flourish through education and networking.

I also run the virtual framework for the cafe concept - www.open4.org, a free social and business networking website which enables monetization for participating open content creators.

Who I am

I am a Web 2.0 technology expert, a serial networker, entrepreneur, and make things happen by fostering connections both technical and human. I am a visionary with a constant supply of ideas and creative solutions to problems. Throughout the last 20 years I have helped numerous businesses make the most of the very latest advancements in technology, including:

* Sun Microsystems
* ATI Technologies
* British Telecom
* Volkswagen
* B&Q (Kingfisher Group PLC)
* Foreign & Commonwealth Office
* Royal Commonwealth Society

How I can help you

As an independent consultant without products to sell, I advise on the best solutions for your business. Using my knowledge and worldwide network of trusted contacts, I bootstrap your concept or existing business into the Web2.0 world. With a mix of best-of-breed software and multi-disciplinary team selected specifically for your project, you can ensure the foremost competitive edge with the highest ROI.
