Frank Lord’s Summary

Strong senior executive; background in establishing corporate strategy, sales direction, and executing on business plans. Have led organizations that sell advanced technologies; eCommerce, networking, storage, data center, Microsoft applications, and ASP solutions.

Successful large enterprise, service provider, and SMB sales leadership experience. Service provider experience is very focused on selling advanced technologies to the SMB (1-5,000) space, through an on-demand ASP model.
