Mark Copeland’s Summary

Proactive and results-oriented IT executive with nearly 20 years of IT experience in a Big 4 accounting firm highlighted by 10 years of increasing levels of management and leadership responsibilities. Managed an IT organization consisting of a $40 million technology budget, 8 direct reports and 125 IT professionals across 36 offices supporting 11,000 internal customers. Demonstrated success in hiring, motivating and retaining quality personnel by creating a positive work environment.

Proven success in maximizing investment in and improving operational efficiencies of computer and printed output (print, copy, scan and fax) related equipment and services. Reduced operational costs by $3.8 million over two years. Strategic planning for lifecycle of over 30,000 computers. Consulted and collaborated with business unit leaders regarding new technologies and deployment strategies to ensure their ever-changing requirements and priorities were being met.

Built strong relationships with business unit and department leaders, customers, vendors and within all levels and departments of the IT organization. Excellent interpersonal, oral and written communication, presentation, customer service, negotiation, organization and analytical problem solving skills.
