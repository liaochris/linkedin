Tim Bock’s Summary

With more than 10 years traditional and interactive marketing experience, I have a solid background utilizing many marketing methods, including attention-getting techniques, cohesive tactics, and long term planning that delivers results. While technically the majority of my experience is in the interactive realm, I’m fortunate that my career to date has enabled and required me to be well versed in traditional marketing techniques.
