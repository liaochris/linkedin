Julie Trelstad’s Summary

I've founded the Plain White Press as laboratory to explore publishing ideas: products, formats, editorial process, and sales models.

Publishing Services
In addition to developing our own products, the Plain White Press offers publishing services for authors and organizations who want to publish illustrated non-fiction works that stand out in an increasingly crowded market.

We work with authors to develop salable proposals and acceptable manuscripts. We also provide additional publishing support services: pulling together a publishing team (ghost writer, editor, designer, illustrator, photographer) editorial project management, proposal writing, competitive analysis, marketing plans, publisher contract review, and even printing/binding, sales and fulfillment consultation.

Julie Trelstad’s Specialties:

Book publishing, book proposal writing, editing and development of illustrated books, publishing strategic planning and budgeting. Subject areas: architecture, design, fitness, reference, home building, illustrated how-to, productivity tools.
