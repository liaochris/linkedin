Abhijit Athavale’s Summary

We started Markonix to change the way technical/product and web marketing content is generated and delivered in the hi-tech industry. Due to very high costs of getting things done in North America, several pieces of collateral such as flash presentations, sell sheets, data sheets, brochures and websites, essential for demand creation are not generated and delivered. Ultimately, product revenues and margins suffer as the messages don't get to the audience effectively. We experienced this first-hand as marketing professionals responsible for several product and solutions launches. Cost is always the barrier to complete product launches.

Markonix fixes this by lowering the cost of generating technical documentation, marketing collateral, websites, multi-media flash presentations, interactive e-Learnings and printed material.

* Web Marketing: We can do:

o Web site design, development and maintenance
o Web site re-design
o 2D and 3D animations
o On line Transactions
o Flash presentations with or without voice narration.

* E-learning: We provide:

o Custom courseware development
o Re-conceptualization of existing content
o Instructional design and storyboarding
o Course authoring
o Web interface design and migration

* Technical Writing: We have expertise in developing

o Data sheets
o Sell sheets and brochures
o Web/print copy
o User manuals
o Product presentations

* Printing: We provide:

o Print design including graphics and copy
o Printing
o Shipping

Abhijit Athavale’s Specialties:

Technical writing (techdocs, white papers, articles, web/collateral copy), Website design/development and maintenance, Multi-media presentations, Instructional design, Flash, e-Learnings, Print management.
