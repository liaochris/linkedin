Ross Nunamaker’s Summary

Ross Nunamaker has a diverse experience working in the association management profession. He is currently the Associate Executive Director of a national association and serves on the Board of Directors of a local downtown development association. He has previously been responsible for programs, membership, and communications. He has been employed by an association with a dedicated staff, been involved in the integration of an existing association into a management company, in the merger of multiple associations into a new entity, and as a volunteer, has been part of a group of community members who created an all-new non-profit association.

Ross is an advocate of community building through information exchange and knowledge share. He launched a hyper-local community web site in April 2006, NewsOverCoffee, which serves the Nazareth area (pop. 24,000) in Northampton County, PA. Applauded by both daily print publications, the site continues to be a regular and consistent source of news and information for area residents.

“The best community blog I've seen hereabouts thus far has been NewsOverCoffee.”
Joseph P. Owens,Editor, The Express-Times, 3/27/07.


“I can't think of anyone who has done a better job of putting the Internet to work in the service of his or her community.”
Bill White, Columnist, The Morning Call, 1/6/07.

A member of ASAE (American Society of Association Executives) & The Center for Association Management, Ross has been focused on developing relationships and identifying mutually beneficial opportunities with members and prospects throughout his non-profit career. Today, he is most interested in developing and maintaining a high-performance staff team designed to deliver exceptional member service.

Previously, Ross has been employed as a Legislative Assistant for the Commonwealth of PA and as an Administrator in the Public School System. He has also engaged in political campaigns, coaching, and local sports reporting.
