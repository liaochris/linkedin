Vaidyanathan Nateshan’s Summary

After a brief stint in Business Process consulting, I moved on to become identified with the Pharmaceutical industry as a Business Turnaround professional.

Right from my first break in this industry with Shasun Chemicals, where I was part of the team that did strategic deals for the company and through my stint with Granules India, where I was responsible to raise USD 25 Mn for the company from various investors in the US, I have served as a very useful interface between investor expectations and customer expectations.

Having successfully done my part for these two listed companies, and getting a level of market recognition, I tried my hand at a start-up R&D company to bring valuation to it.

Now I have moved on to be part of a mid-size private limited company to help the management in achieving their goals for expansion and globalisation.
