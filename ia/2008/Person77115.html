Hitesh Agrawal’s Summary

Hitesh has over 9 years of experience in applied Human Factors, Usability Engineering and User Interface Design consultancy services. He started his professional career as a Usability Engineer at General Electric — Global Exchange Services. He has worked on numerous design projects through the entire process of Usability Engineering Life Cycle.

Hitesh is currently working as a Executive Director of HFI India. In this role, he is responsible for overall business development, portfolio management and delivering the entire suite of HFI’s consulting services in the field of User Experience Design. This includes services like Expert Review, Usability Testing, User Interface Structure Design, Detail Design, Standards, Strategic Usability, Training and Institutionalization of Usability in organizations. He manages multiple projects and resources as needed to grow independent book of business.

He has lead and directed over 125 user interface design projects for a range of local and international clients from industries such as financial services, telecom, healthcare, mobile, technology, media, e-commerce etc. Assumes responsibility for leading multidisciplinary and globally distributed project teams and mentoring them.

Hitesh has co-authored and conducted training and workshops on usability engineering in Europe and Asia. He is a lead course instructor for HFI's Certified Usability Analyst (CUA) Certification.

Hitesh Agrawal’s Specialties:

Hitesh’s expertise is in problem solving, user research, evaluation, Design of high-level user interface architecture for the Web, GUI and hand-held environments.

His skill sets also include work on corporate client server & Web Applications, Web sites, Intranets, Usability Testing, Contextual Inquiry, Information Structuring, and Design.
