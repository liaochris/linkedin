Boye Oomens’s Summary

Seasoned Front-end Web Developer with over seven years of hand-coding HTML/CSS experience and fully dedicated to web standards. Always aiming for semantic, cross-browser & cross-platform consistent code with a high maintainability level.

Strong understanding of how to handle back-end dynamics driven by PHP/MySQL, JavaScript (both native as well libraries like jQuery) and a healthy grasp of web development best practices.

Eye for details, well organized, and the ability to take direction as well as provide guidance to production and peers.
