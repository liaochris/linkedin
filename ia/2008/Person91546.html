Jim Durkin’s Summary

My experience is mainly in and around the retail auto industry for over 20 years. For over 30 years I have tried to satisfy a curiosity of improving my skills and ability so that I could help others achieve their dreams and goals. My passion to help others is as intense today as it was the first day I joined the retail car industry.

I live with five principals: live in harmony with God, exceptional health, positive interpersonal relationships with all I meet, a loving marriage and a profitable business. These five principals allow me the desire and energy to become more effective every day so I can help those seeking a better tomorrow.
