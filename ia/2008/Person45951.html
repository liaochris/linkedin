David Alexander’s Summary

I am a Fraud Prevention Expert with extensive personal experience and expertise in fraud and economic crime.

I was an economic criminal and have served a prison sentence for fraud. I now dedicate my time, expertise and experience to the prevention of fraud.

I have personal experience of fraud and fraudsters, of the methods and mechanisms used by fraudsters and economic criminals and of the techniques of influence, persuasion and manipulation common in fraud situations.

I have the knowledge, insight, experience and expertise to uncover existing fraud situations and to assist in the resolution of these situations.

Companies cannot rely on auditors or accountants to provide fraud prevention services, they cannot think like a fraudster and they cannot design a system that effectively prevents fraud.

Recent cases have proved that a different approach to fraud prevention is required.

My fraud prevention services consist of three steps; identification of the fraud risk, evaluation of that risk and the elimination of the risk.

I provide a complete range of fraud prevention tools and systems including staff training to ensure that your organisations fraud prevention systems are effective.

All of my services are rendered on a 100% confidential basis and action is only taken on the direction of the client.

My assistance will make sure that your organisation does not feature in the news for being involved in a fraud scheme.

My personal life goal is to add value and contribute to the society that I harmed by my actions in the past.

I am passionately committed to that goal.

  • FMP Consultant

    Mercury Advisors

    (Management Consulting industry)

    July 2008 — Present (6 months)

    A comprehensive Fraud Management Program (FMP) is critical to the proper control and eradication of fraud and other misconduct in your organisation.

    Your FMP must be specifically constructed around the industry and operational, financial, HR and other constraints of your company.

    The FMP must contain, policies, procedures and emphasise the organisations commitment to sound business practice and ethics.

    The FMP must detail exact lines of responsibility and procedures to be followed in the event of fraud or misconduct at any level in the organisation.

    The FMP should be contained in a single document available to all in the organisation.

    In certain jurisdiction a FMP is a legislative requirement and a proper FMP is in any event critical to proper corporate governance.

    I provide consultation, education and training on all aspects of a professional FMP to enable your company to compile its FMP.
