Steve Fanale’s Summary

After completing a Bachelor of Commerce degree at the University of Wollongong in 1991, majoring in Management and Marketing, Steve’s career beginnings were in finance, professional sport, voice-over and the restaurant industry. Then in 1997 he found his ultimate niche working in the Internet and digital communications industry.

First as a contractor and later as Production Director at Young & Rubicam’s Interactive Agency, Steve soon acquired specialist expertise in strategy, media and marketing through his immersion in mainstream advertising campaigns and brand development. His focus soon turned to using this expertise to maximise success online and, after moving to APL Digital as the Executive Producer, Steve further developed his management and production skills, focussing on process, functionality, utility, technical infrastructure and information design. This provided him with the solid grounding required to take the plunge in 2000 and start up his own interactive agency, MassMedia Studios, at a time when the industry was generally thought to be in decline.

Essential to the early success and growth of MassMedia Studios has been Steve’s own broad knowledge of business operations and issues, enabling him to lead a team that provides an exceptional level of consultancy in the digital environment. From its beginnings in Sydney with just two staff, MassMedia Studios has grown to become an Australian leader in the provision of digital and interactive services, opening offices in London and New York in 2006, expanding its team to more than forty employees and adding global brands like Unilever, Cadbury, Audi, Vue Entertainment, St George Bank, Westpac and the Commonwealth Bank of Australia to its key client list.

In 2003 Steve’s team began work on the development of Traction, now a leading multi-channel digital marketing platform that helps brands to interact with, learn about and effectively target customers using a variety of digital media channels.
