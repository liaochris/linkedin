    GreenLightKids, Inc.

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Renewables & Environment industry)

    January 2008 — Present (8 months)

    GreenLightKids, Inc is a CA 501(c)3 started by my daughter. The goal of GreenLightKids is to raise environmental awareness through an exchange program where "Kids" go door-to-door to exchange CFL bulbs for incandescent light bulbs. During the exchange process the people get one CFL for every new unused incandescent bulb and a fact sheet about the benefits of CFL bulbs.

    There is a two phase goal for the organization.

    Phase I is to exchange 10,000 light bulbs in the North Orange County CA area. Phase II is a website that provides all the details and information necessary for anyone else to start a GreenLightKids organization local to their community.
