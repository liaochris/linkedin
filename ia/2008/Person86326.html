Jean Holt’s Summary

Over 10 years experience in product marketing, communications and high level management. Hands-on skills include software development with a focus on Web development, graphic and web design and user interface design.

Personal strengths include ability to manage teams and large projects, work in a team or independently, perform multiple projects simultaneously and interact well with customers. Extensive experience in helping a company setup both an internal and external communication strategy and implement - from brand and identity improvements to Intranet facilities that help Marketing/Marcom communicate better with the sales organization.

Pride myself in my creative capacity and a desire to build amazing things. My passion lies in workflow-oriented Web sites.
