Mary Ann Geier’s Summary

Independent Product Manager & Product Developer, specializing in online information products for medical professionals. I integrate content with online technologies for health professionals. My experience includes "front end" customer expertise with "back end" technical development project experience. Rare for someone with a traditional publishing background, I'm also tuned into how social & mobile media are transforming our notion of what a "product" and a "customer" are.

I'm interested in issues related to authoritative medical content in the Web 2.0 world, and the movement for quality improvement in health care. Perhaps you've got information projects like these, or you have a vision for new ways of interacting with healthcare information. Maybe you (and your customers) can see the possibilities for virtual communities and collaboration around your medical information product. If so, I'd be interested in talking with you.

Organizing HealthCamp Philadelphia for Spring 2009. Follow @HealthCampPhila.
