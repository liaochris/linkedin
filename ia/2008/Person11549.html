Andrew Field’s Summary

A mature, dynamic and results-driven business development executive who succeeds in new environments; a person who always looks for the challenges and opportunities in life. An effective thinker who is self disciplined, with a proven ability to build good relationships for long-term high level results.

Andrew Field’s Specialties:

Having worked within the account & business development teams for the Call, Contact and BPO industry for well over 15 years; developing both onshore (UK), near shore (Eastern Europe) and offshore (India & Philippines) solutions. I have the knowledge, experience and more importantly the contacts to solve any challenge within this industry.
