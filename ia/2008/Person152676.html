Brett Pojunis’s Summary

Brett H. Pojunis, Chairman of the Board, Chief Executive Officer and President. Brett H. Pojunis is the founder of Club Scene Network, Inc. and has been Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and President since the Company’s inception in October of 2003. Mr. Pojunis is co-Founder and Director of XYZ Media Corp, a diversified entertainment holding corporation specializing in unique content creation and immense online content distribution through its portfolio of interactive channels. Mr. Pojunis is founder of DealGuys.net, an exclusive social network where “Deal Guys” can connect, recommend and present opportunities to each other. From 2001 until July 2005, Mr. Pojunis was the Chairman of the Board, Chief Executive Officer of The Liquid Consulting Group, Inc., a full service investor and public relations boutique firm that has been a financial consultant to a number of companies, some of which were involved in Internet related businesses. From 1999 until May 2007, Mr. Pojunis was the President and Chief Executive Officer of Liquid Design Group, a web development and graphic design outsourcing firm with offices in Indian as well as the United States. Mr. Pojunis have been a consultant to many start-up companies including high tech Internet to traditional Brick and Mortar companies. Mr. Pojunis resides on the Board of Directors of 4 public companies, 3 private companies and 2 organizations. Mr. Pojunis also has military training ranging from Civil Affairs Specialist (38A) to Combat Engineer (12B). While in the US ARMY he was awarded the Outstanding American award twice, the second time with honorable mention. Mr. Pojunis studied International Business and Finance at Green Mountain College and Entrepreneurship classes at the Simon School of Business at the University of Rochester. Mr. Pojunis also attended elective classes at Rochester Institute of Technology.
