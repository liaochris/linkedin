Dennis Miloseski’s Summary

Born in Detroit, Michigan - I studied computer science and hci at Carnegie Mellon University and interactive media and visual design at the Art Institute of Pittsburgh.

My accumulated design experience incorporates a wide range of projects. As a creative and design director I’ve lead graphic design and user experience teams bringing expertise in visual communication, interactive, web, print, and exhibition design.

I currently hold the position of Design and User Experience Mgr. at Google, Inc. where I lead and manage design teams responsible for application design at Google.

Prior to Google, I directed and lead design efforts in brand identity, interactive design, and user experience for DivX, Inc. and it's fortune 100 partner initiatives. My work and design campaigns have been exhibited online and in retail environments worldwide. Some recent campaigns include work for Philips, LG, Sony, and Casio Electronics.

Prior to DivX, I served as Director of User Experience at General Electric, Healthcare Solutions where I was the design and user experience principal for the business. During my tenure at GE, I planned, directed, and established all user interface, user experience, and usability initiatives for GE Medical Systems. During the ‘Imagination at Work’ re-brand, my contributions were instrumental in establishing the new brand identity in print, web, and interactive media for the medical systems division.

In previous positions and consultancies, I’ve worked in many environments. My extensive book of work includes designs for GE, Sony Electronics, America Online, Netscape, and a number of technology start-ups.

I currently reside in San Francisco, CA with my wife Joanne and dog Basil.
