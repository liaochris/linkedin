Joon Shin’s Summary

Mr. Shin possesses over ten years of well-rounded and seasoned leadership of complex, enterprise-wide projects, online / Internet / intranet and product development projects with proven experience to deliver timely solutions to business problems at different capacities. He is currently the President of an Internet TV company and has held positions as a senior manager at Niche Media, LLC and as an engineer, internal consultant and corporate auditor at The Boeing Company. He is a bottom-line process improvement specialist and problem solver; driven by measurable goals with strong interpersonal and communication skills with internal customers, key personnel and external representatives. Mr. Shin combines business and technical formal education and experiences, which enable success in positions that demand: business development, expense management, strategic positioning, and program management. His ability to quickly analyze business conditions allows for him to determine correct courses of action, motivate personnel, and implement programs and strategies that improve sales, processes and individual performance. Beyond his skills in adapting to changing environments, Mr. Shin has proven to be a strong team builder, which includes years of mentoring, training and coaching. Mr. Shin completed the Executive Master's in Technology Management program from Wharton School of Business and Penn Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania and received his Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Lehigh University.

Joon Shin’s Specialties:

Business & technology strategy development; product and interactive / online development; business analytics & intelligence; process engineering; organizational dynamics; risk analysis; project / program, general, relationships & talent management; critical research; investor relations; etc.
