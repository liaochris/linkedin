Nicole Andres’s Summary

CLC is a membership-based management research program of the Corporate Executive Board (CEB) in Washington, DC. In my current role as part of the content delivery team, I am responsible for presenting the Council’s research studies to members in a variety of forums.

Prior to joining the Council, I worked for Hudson as a Director of Recruiting where I advised both law firms and corporations regarding their legal talent acquisition strategies and placed individual attorneys in a variety of legal roles. Before joining Hudson, I worked as Associate Recruiting Director at The Partners Group where I focused on placing attorneys in law firms throughout the Southeastern United States.

Before entering the field of recruiting, I practiced law in both Indianapolis and Atlanta where I focused primarily on premises liability, disability law, and medical negligence cases.
