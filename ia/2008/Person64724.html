Robert Ford’s Summary

An accomplished entrepreneur and executive leader (CEO/CTO/CIO), my track record is characterized by successful start-ups, turnarounds and growth businesses with well-diversified information technology, business development, communications and general management experience. I have successfully led multiple integration, consolidation and transformational activities.

I’m currently considering new opportunities to put my skills and experience to work in an environment that inspires passion. My background is start-ups, turnarounds, growth and optimization of diverse businesses. A consistent theme throughout my career has been identifying, assimilating, and operationalizing technologies that positively change the way that business is done, and improving how people connect, communicate, and collaborate.

I am specifically looking at three different types of opportunities:

- Senior Executive position (CEO, COO, President) in a well-funded, emerging growth company / startup.

- Senior Executive position (EVP, SVP, VP) in an established mid-sized company that is looking to transform the way that it does business, and how it connects with its stakeholders (shareholders, employees, partners, customers, community)

- Senior Executive position (President, Executive Director, VP) in a non-profit that is making a difference in/for its community.

Robert Ford’s Specialties:

Operations, Information Technology, Strategic Planning, Business Development, Product Development, Marketing, Communications, eBusiness / e-commerce, Project Management , Financial Management, Integration, Mergers / Acquisitions, Negotiations
