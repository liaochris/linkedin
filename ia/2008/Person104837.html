Ken Hogan, PMP’s Summary

As a self-employed PMP certified Sr. Project Manager, I managed the implementation of several complex mission critical multi-phased projects, including but not limited to, Long Distance Network solution implementations, Competitive Local Exchange Carrier solutions, Customer Care infrastructure enhancements and Reservations and Ticketing systems replacements.

In the past I've sub-contracted members of my team’s services out to various organizations as sub-contractors, which allowed me to perform sales, estimating, project management and collections duties. I also gained considerable knowledge and experience in the following areas:
• Successfully managed multiple projects concurrently and completed all projects on time and within budget
• Supervised, trained and mentored developers, project managers and peers in areas of software development, solution architecture, project and program management
• Gained considerable knowledge of the PMI Project Management Methodology through first hand application of the best practices as a member of the technical team and as an instructor at a local community college while preparing students for PMP certification.

Additionally, my qualifications include:
• A Bachelor of Science in Math with a minor in Education
• Proficiency as a COBOL OS MVS Software Developer
• J2EE Software Development Manager
• PMI Certified Project Management Professional with the ability to use tools such as Microsoft Enterprise Server Project Management, Microsoft Business Suite (Excel, Word, Outlook, Access, VISIO), and more.

Throughout my career, I have fostered a reputation for being a very hard-working, intelligent, and industrious individual. My motivation is to completely utilize my accumulated experience and knowledge to the fullest extent possible.
