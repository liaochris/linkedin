Shane Pleasance MBA’s Summary

Shane is pursuing the career of a serial entrepreneur.
He graduated from Massey University in 2006 with a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree, having upshifted from the Otago BCom.

Currently he is working on a number of projects, having taken a counter-intuitive 'scattergun' approach to see what hits...

He is a Director of the Southland Chamber of Commerce, director and managing director of Talking Shop, a Consultancy service, and director of an Invercargill based property development company, Oreti Limited.

He is also vice chairman of the Otatara Community Group, and passionate about the area.

Projects he is currently working on include:
SeeThru, a company developing products which will increase numbers and amounts of eCommerce transactions by reducing the perceived risk by the customer. Two very exciting globally targeted products in the pipeline already, this companies products sit firmly in an exciting part of the eCommerce value chain. And its a bloody big cake...
Private health services with some fantastic colleagues looking to get out of DHB based health services here in Invercargill... wonder why...?
A great little product directed at media providers in NZ - this is innovation and a potentially a disruptive technology which he will also take global once successfully implemented here in NZ. helps to reduce 'murk' for customers and advertisers in an exciting contemporary business model. this is for sale or for lease to an organisation with media capability - radio and/or internet.

He lives in Southern New Zealand.
Shane is also a Registered Psychiatric Nurse and has a Post Graduate Certificate in Health Sciences, endorsed in Drug and Alcohol, from the Otago University National Addictions Centre.

But he has done his time as a charitable trust.

Shane Pleasance MBA’s Specialties:

Competence in governance, strategic planning and organisational behaviour.
Exploring Health service design & new delivery models.
