Adriana Oestreich’s Summary

Adriana worked for SAP for 10 years, having started as a Consultant/Consulting Coordinator, being responsible both for sales and delivery process. After leading the selling and delivering procees of successful ERP implementations and Quality Assurance in Brasil, took over a Customer Services Manager position. Responsible for large and strategic deals for SAP Brazil on manufacturing and also services customers, took over as Consulting Director, in charge of Services Sector (Utilities, Telco, Public, Services).
During 2006 as Consulting Director for Services and Public Sector at SAP was responsible for closing deals over USD 23MI, including huge utilities deals as well as the first brazilian public administration project for SAP Brazil. This required an special talent/experience to cross understand government rules and company needs/values in order to be able to execute contracts on a healthy way,

As a Director at Complex has as challenges, increase revenues and expand their market share on services area and also (according to the large previous consulting managing experience) takes part on the team that leads company re-positioning strategy - from body shop to a project company.

Networking to CIOs, IT Director and Manager as well the whole SAP ecosystem is part of day-by-day work.
