Julie Stanford’s Summary

20 years' experience in design for publishing; first in London for publishers such as Macmillan and William Heinemann, then in Brighton for a number of national clients.

Latterly, was one of three founder directors of a manufacturing company, selling into the US market.

Professional goals: to build on my reputation for extremely well-written and clearly designed business information materials, with excellent customer satisfaction.

Julie Stanford’s Specialties:

Design of books, magazines, newsletters and training manuals, with the emphasis on optimum readabiliy. Winner of a Plain English Award for the Essential Business Guide -- the only business book ever to win such an award. Over 25 years' experience in print management.

  • President

    (Non-Profit; 201-500 employees; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    March 2007 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Brighton & Hove Chamber of Commerce is a rapidly growing local provider of support and information for businesses in the city of Brighton & Hove. It is run by a group of passionate, committed volunteers and two member of staff. I'm proud to be part of it.

  • Managing Director

    Essential Business

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Publishing industry)

    May 2003 — Present (5 years 8 months)

    I started the company in 2003 and to build the company invited a highly skilled person to join me as marketing director: Jane Priddis. Our key product is The Essential Business Guide: a new type of business book. 230 full-colour pages packed with information to help small businesses start, build and grow a business. It is useful for small businesses at all stages of development and is the only business book ever to win a Plain English Award.

  • Owner

    (Sole Proprietorship; 1-10 employees; Graphic Design industry)

We produced work for BUPA, Joseph Rowntree Foundation, Pavilion Publishing Ltd, Faversham House Group, Sainsbury Centre for Mental Health, and many others. I still carry out some specialised design work and consultancy for a very small number of clients.
