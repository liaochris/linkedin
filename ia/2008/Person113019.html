Leslie Carothers’s Summary

My company is an internet strategy consultancy specializing in the following areas for furniture and other home industries related retailers, manufacturers and suppliers:

1. Setting up, managing and leveraging social networking communities to drive revenue for furniture and home industries related brands.

2. Data mining of website analytics for strategic insights that can be used to drive revenue for furniture and home industries related brands.

3. Online reputation management services to maximize the value of a positive online reputation and mitigate the impact of a negative online reputation for furniture and home industries related brands.

4. Keyword enriched writing for furniture and other home industries related brands' websites, newsletters, email marketing campaigns and blogs in order to increase sales conversions from online shoppers.

5. E-conversion sales training to help sales teams understand how to turn an e-based inquiry into a sale for furniture retailers and other home industries related retailers.

I am also an industry speaker and writer. For the past three years, I have written the "Retail Ideas" column for www.furnituretoday.com and write a series called "Technology and Retail" for Furniture World magazine.

As a speaker, I have been priveledged to present at industry conferences across the U.S. and internationally. In 2007, I spoke at the All-Industry Convention in the Bahamas and in 2009, will be speaking at two sessions at the Western Home Furnishings Association's Annual Conference in Maui, Hawaii.

My goals for 2009 are:

1. To increase my client base by 35%.
2. To help my client's leverage their investment in my services through increasing their sales by 10% or more with actionable, measurable e-initiatives-especially through social networking, analytics, online reputation enhancement and e-conversion sales training.

Leslie Carothers’s Specialties:

I have worked in the furniture industry for 26 years. My specialties are: strategic planning, thought leadership, understanding the furniture consumer's buying behavior, selling, writing, speaking, social networking, website analytics, online reputation management and e-training.

I do not design websites nor run email marketing campaigns, but my strategic partner, AYR1.com, the world's largest internet solutions provider to furniture retailers and manufacturers does.
