Stephen Longo’s Summary

For over 15 years, I has assisted 81 of the top 100 Fortune companies analyze and mitigate their risks, determine security solutions and evaluate proposed locations. I partner with loss prevention and risk management professionals at several companies/organizations globally, including retail, government agencies, QSR’s, financial, health-care, multi-family and commercial real estate businesses. Additionally, I oversee CAP's recently launched UK market and CAP’s vast reseller program including Claritas, G4S, and ISO. I am a member of the American Society of Industrial Security (ASIS), Risk Insurance Management Society (RIMS), and the International Society of Crime Analysts (ISCA).
