Shadi el-Hajj’s Summary

Shadi el-Hajj is a Rich Internet Applications developer leveraging the latest technologies in web development and usability design. Shadi aims at improving user experience in web applications through his familiarity with advanced frontend technologies and enterprise-grade backend architectures.
Another aspect of his work involves new media art and design, striving to explore various forms of interaction design in live and networked environments. His work has been shown at various exhibitions and his latest showcase selenotrope.net, a web design experiment is presented on its companion website blog.selenotrope.net

Shadi el-Hajj’s Specialties:

Operating systems: Windows, Mac OS X, Linux.
Programming: C++, Java, UML, PHP, SQL, Python.
Front-end Programming: Flash, Adobe Flex, HTML, CSS, Javascript.
Design: Flash, Dreamweaver, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Wordpress.
