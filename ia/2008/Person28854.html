Bryce Ives’s Summary

Bryce Ives is a media and arts practitioner based in Melbourne Australia.

Bryce is recognised as a dynamic and emerging leader in the Australian not-for-profit media sector. .

The Australian Newspaper in 2004 commented that “Ives represents a generational shift in how media is being produced and managed.”
