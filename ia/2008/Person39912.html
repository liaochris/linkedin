Stephen Copeland’s Summary

With a background and skill set that has involved most aspects of business operation, Stephen (34) has rapidly moved through a career path that to date has taken him to the position of Vice President of Operations for a PLC leading a team of over 40 people, responsible for a £12M budget and a client base of 45,000.

On route, after leaving Kingston School of Excellence with an Honours degree in Business Studies, he established a software-training company, which led him to develop a niche IT product that he progressed from concept to manufacture, marketing and sales.

Concurrent with this activity he went on to establish a rights-based media group raising over £1m of private equity and managing the production of a 52 part children’s television series that was broadcast on the UK’s ITV network.

Stephen currently provides hands-on interim management services to companies requiring problem solving, crisis management, product development, fund raising, process control, turnaround management and strategic and operational support.

He is also currently advisor to several senior executives on their management Buy in/Buy out strategies and options.
