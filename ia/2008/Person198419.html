Walter Biscardi’s Summary

Outstanding storyteller and long form editor. Documentary styled production and 2D animations a specialty.

Highly qualified and honored in all facets of film and video production. Honors and credits Include Emmy, Peabody, Telly and Aurora Awards.

Leader in High Definition Post Production for worldwide broadcast and film distribution including editorial, graphic design, color grading, dvd and Blu-ray disc authoring, disc replication.

Designed High Definition Post Production workflow for "Good Eats with Alton Brown" on the Food Network. Delivered the first three HD seasons to Food Network HD.

Recognized world-wide expert of Apple's Final Cut Pro video editing system and also the author of many tutorials on that application and Apple's Color.

Author: "Stop Staring and Start Grading with Apple's Color" on DVD.

Walter Biscardi’s Specialties:

Producer. Director. Writer. Editor. Excellent Chef.

    Revolutionized the Post Production workflow by moving it from linear to non-linear centric. EPN-TV was primarily editing out of two very dated linear edit suites and I moved most of the primary work to their Media 100 non-linear edit suite.

    (Privately Held; 10,001 or more employees; Gambling & Casinos industry)

    February 1995 — February 1997 (2 years 1 month)

Took a production company that completed less than 100 projects per year to over 300 per year after a major re-design of the entire production facility. On-line suite featured the Accom Axial edit controller, Philips Diamond Digital Series Switcher, Chyron Maxine!, multiple Beta SP decks and full client workstation. Media 100 workstation performed all non-linear editing and After Effects compositing. Full sound design and VO recording room. 3D Animation featuring Alias / WaveFront running on Silicon Graphics workstations along with a huge media library.
