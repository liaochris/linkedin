Simon Andrews’s Summary

I've just joined MindShare as their first Digital Chief Strategy Officer, with worldwide responsibilities. MindShare is probably the worlds leading media investment company, with a strong digital team in MindShare Interaction.

I've always been involved in strategy - firstly in media and then as an account planner. Having taken the digital pill early, I've been involved in building agencies since the mid 90s - first taking Poppe Tyson Europe from 6 people to 200 - with a merger with Modem Media along the way.

I then joined DLKW - one of the UKs fastest growing ad agencies - as a partner & was instrumental in its development as an integrated agency. The last 3 years were devoted to building DLKW Dialogue the digital and direct division of the agency - then in the top 5 of the fastest growing DM agencies and with a growing reputation.

As interruption marketing becomes less and less effective I launched Big Picture to specialise on next generation digital marketing focusing on permission / invitation - and grew this into a thought leading company working with brands like Halifax, STA Travel & Tetley. The influential blog we used to market the agency and share our thinking is still active - Big Picture at http://www.bigpictureblog.com

I'm leveraging all my experience in my new role - I've been a Board Director of the worlds biggest Advertising Agency (Y&R), the worlds largest Media Agency (Zenith) the worlds largest Direct Agency (Ogilvy) and one of the worlds biggest Interactive agencies, Modem.

Simon Andrews’s Specialties:

Building clients business through customer acquisition and retention by using effective creative and strategic thinking and implementation
