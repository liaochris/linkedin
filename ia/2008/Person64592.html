faarooq or Farouk mangera or Ebrahim’s Summary

My goals on the civil society/non-profit sector aspects now are to assist civil society organisations to take governance seriously, and not be "pressured" into performing like the private sector entities as many would have the organisatiions do. Civil society entities are the Third Sector - separate from Private and Public Sectors - and do have different agendas and foci, which require different models of practice.

With the wellness counselling my goals are to support clients in reaching their wellness goals, and in the case of terminal clients, to have the best possible and painfree period during the days they have.
