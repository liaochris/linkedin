Iris Barr’s Summary

I am an Engineering Product and Program manager professional with eighy years experience in the computer industry, working in both hardware and software capacities. I started out my career with an Electrical Engineering degree from the University of Waterloo in Canada. I have held positions that span large responsibilities within corporations such as EMC, Intel and ATI (now AMD).

At EMC, as a Product Manager, I focus on defining the future of the product, and on integrating a recently acquired start-up into the large corporation.

At Intel, as a Technical Marketing Engineer, I promoted our product to create partnerships globally; in the Far East, Europe and North America. Successful interactions were then followed up by the full project management cycle.

At ATI Technologies (now AMD), I owned the HP technical interface, followed by a Program Management position of managing the relationship with Microsoft. I was then promoted to the position of regulating process and creating order between the hardware and software departments within the company.
