Richard Sones’s Summary

Over 15 years experience creating business for technology companies. Strengths in strategy, innovation, developing new markets, partnership development, negotiating strategic contracts and financial goal achievement. Strong corporate management expertise with companies such as Harman International, MPR Teltech and Nortel, combined with significant international business experience. Exceeds in an entrepreneurial, team orientated, and business to business environment.

Richard Sones’s Specialties:

Business Development, Early Stage companies/business units, Sales, Marketing, Product Management
