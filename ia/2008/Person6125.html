Antoine Leven’s Summary

I have a 8 year experience working as a technical support professional for market leaders in their own field: Google and DoubleClick.

During my tenure at DoubleClick, I supported both publishers and Advertisers in their daily usage of ad serving systems on the French and UK markets. I was also the dedicated technical account manager for Altavista in EMEA.

I joined Google in autumn 2005 to support the launch of Google Analytics in EMEA. As part of team dedicated to provide technical expertise to the direct sales force (direct meaning selling to clients with deep pockets) my first mission has been to evangelise web analytics amongst the sales and operation teams. From their my role evolved and I got also involved on a daily basis with Google Adwords. The biggest part of the job involved analysing ads behavior and uncovering the secrets of adwords Ads Quality induced behavior.

Since February 2008 I have joined Omniture as a sales engineer. In this role I support Sales Executives in our approach of prospects, qualifying their needs and setting up ad'hoc product presentations and demos.

I have developed an rare set of skills in this market which combines business and technical knowledge, and both the advertiser and publisher needs. As e-commerce revenu and online ads spend share of advertising grow the understanding of how both interact is critical.

In my current role I am faced everyday with the challenge of convincing businesses to adopt sophisticated tools and adequate practices to harness technology for the better of their online business.

Having joined Omniture recently, I will take the time to broaden my knowledge. A mid-term goal would be to either joined a company to own their online marketing and internet endeavours or set-up my own consultancy.

In the meantime feel free to contact me for a chat about Omniture products and/or discuss online challenges you are facing.
