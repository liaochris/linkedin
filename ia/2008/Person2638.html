adam seever’s Summary

I am a diverse marketer with an emphasis on sponsorship and how to maximize ROI on marketing assets/properties. I have developed and managed large cross functional teams and coached interrelated disciplines on how to get the most out of their efforts and those around them.

I have general managed marketing offices both in the USA and Europe and am comfortable in many different work environments.

Properties and clients I have worked with in the past include (but are not limited to) the Super Bowl, the World Cup, the Olympics, the NBA, the NFL, NASCAR, NHRA, McDonald's, GE, Johnson & Johnson, Fiat, VISA, Coca-Cola, Levis, United Airlines, Animal Planet TV and the Sci-Fi Channel.

I have held positions at Coca-Cola, IMG Worldwide, Frankel & Company (ARC) and two small marketing firms. Average stint in any position is about 5 years.
