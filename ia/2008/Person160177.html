Richard Simonelli’s Summary

wwwDOTrichardsimonelliDOTcom

I develop and implement strategic marketing platforms that maximize shareholder value, attract capital and improve financial performance.

I believe in excellence. I am passionate about the companies and the products/services I represent. I enjoy empowering and inspiring the people around me. I like to win and be part of a team that seeks the same. My overall objective is to be part a company that shares these values.

I offer 20 years of experience in the global capital markets marketing international companies. I have worked with and advised CEOs & CFOs of global corporations to raise the profile of the senior executives/ministers and their company/institution, attract investment capital, increase global visibility and to drive sales and profit performance.

I am comfortable working in extremely complex situations with global companies with many branches and subsidiaries. Getting to the essence of the corporation and telling that story effectively is what I do best.

In this world of intense scrutiny by regulators, I have been fortunate to work 10 years with the New York Stock Exchange. On the public company side, I am well versed in Fair Disclosure and Sarbanes-Oxley in addition to disclosure requirements of the SEC and various Exchanges.

I work extremely long hours, I am not afraid to travel extensively or go onsite with clients for months on end if required and do what is necessary to succeed and make the company/clients I work for successful.


My specific areas of expertise are:
• Positioning and branding the corporation
• Financial, corporate and internal communications
• Capital Markets
• CEO preparation/ speech writing
• Capital Raising- IPO's, Secondary financing
• Mergers & acquisitions/crisis communications
• Change management
• Integration of new media techniques to traditional communications programs
