Mike Carter’s Summary

I've been working in the new media space since the early days of 1995. The path of my career started with the digital agency side of the business helping clients make sense of the web. It then progressed to encompass the display and search engine areas of online advertising. Today my focus is to understand the Web 2.0 world of social networks, video and user-generated content(ugc) and how that can compliment online strategies for products and services.

My current project is a B2C search engine for property called Zoomf.com, of which I am a founding director. We are now in the later stages of our development with over 2,000 estate agents listing over 350,000 properties. The near-future for Zoomf will see an acceleration of the product into the areas of Web 2.0 that are proving successful with consumers.

Mike Carter’s Specialties:

13 years in digital media managing clients, products, platforms, teams and developing revenue streams. Strong entrepreneurial spirit with particular expertise in forming and commercializing online platforms. Superior management, organizational planning and oral/written communication skills. Technology know-how including Web 2.0, UGC, social media, search engines, advertising platforms, ad-serving, ad networks, rich media, PPC, SEO.
