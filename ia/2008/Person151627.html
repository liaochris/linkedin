Pierre Col’s Experience

  • VP Marketing & Business Development

    UbicMedia

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Internet industry)

    February 2007 — Present (1 year)

    I'm involved in the very early steps of UbicMedia, a company providing a new system devoted to artists, musicians, movie directors, publishers, producers and globally speaking providers of multimedia content like films, movies, videos, music etc.

    UbicMedia designed, created and operates PUMit, an Internet media combining the advantages of downloading and VoD, P2P and streaming to widespread, promote, sell and exchange multimedia content.

    PUMit helps to widespread contents at a very low cost, guarantees a secure & fair use of the contents and, if requested, their easy online payment, with a detailed in-depth marketing analysis of their audience and uses.

    By providing the means to control their marketing campaigns and online revenue sources, UbicMedia helps the content providers in efficiently developing their business on the Internet.

    PUMit is protected by several patent families.
