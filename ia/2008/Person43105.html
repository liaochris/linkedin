Dan Goodman’s Summary

Close to thirty years of experience solving a wide variety of business and technical problems, primarily in consulting roles, for various blue-chip and startup companies.

Although I am willing to continue consulting, at this point in my career, I would prefer to find a permanent position due to my personal circumstances and the point that I am in in my life.

But either way, my goal is to deliver top quality service in an environment where both talent and effort are appreciated and rewarded.

Dan Goodman’s Specialties:

Solid knowledge of business and financial accounting procedures. Solid background in systems architecture and development methodologies. Over a decade and a half of in-depth Unix and Linux development and production management skills. The ability to lead others, and to address project and business level issues, and at the same time, to focus in on highly technical issues and see them through to resolution.
