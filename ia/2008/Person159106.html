Pierre-Yves Revaz’s Summary

Regarded a one of the most original and innovator spirit of Swiss French communication. Specialist in marketing coup, Pierre-Yves Revaz, 44 years, has a sound training : Master in International relations (HEI, Geneva), diploma in Marketing Communication (IAA, New York) and diploma of Director of Communication (SAWI/ISRP, Lausanne).
Begun his career with the Credit Suisse, as specialist in options strategies, then journalist with the Tribune de Genève, Pierre-Yves Revaz joined afterwards Lombard Odier Darier Hentsch & Co, in the function of Director of the Communication Department, which covered 12 countries, with a team of 22 people.
After having been cofounder of the agency Label Communication, in charge of the strategic council, he then develops activities as an independent consultant, specialized in marketing and communication via Internet, but also in the business intelligence for a whole series of banking customers.
In 2004, he joined Synthesis Bank, (became Saxo Bank since) where he applied all its mastering of branding and cross-media, contributing very strongly to profile this on line bank
Since 2007, at ERI Bancaire, he took the responsibility for the Administration Department Geneva, his role becomes more managerial : to supervise and coordinate the whole of the administrative services (IT, logistics, finance, information service, HR). But he will also take part in projects for the International Communication Group.
However he strongly remains in contact with the world of the communication and new technologies being administrator within the communication agency details.ch
