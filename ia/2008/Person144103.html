Olivier Verbeke’s Summary

Whatever, a Belgium-based “intrapreneurial” company, provides a full range of tightly integrated services to information-hungry clients: workshops on searching the Web and building collective knowledge, consultancy on the safe and effective use of IT in the information era, and tools such as Knowledge Plaza.

A well-balanced team of talents at Whatever are working hard on developing tools enabling Enterprise Social Search. In a nutshell, Enterprise Social Search is a way to search, manage, and share information within a company: combined search across many sources (with faceted search, contextual search, ...), intelligent ways to organize searched information, company-defined and user-defined tagging of all shared sources, and more.

Who can help you find relevant information and nothing but relevant information Your colleagues, of course. And thanks to Enterprise Social Search, they can do so even when they are not available for you.

Going beyond search engines, Whatever developed Knowledge Plaza™, a Web-based platform that assists knowledge professionals in searching, managing, and sharing information.
