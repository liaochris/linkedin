Jacqueline Wales’s Summary

Motivational speaker, author and coach Jacqueline Wales is the CEO and founder of Fearless Fifties, a community for women who are putting Life Into midLife.

As the owner of three small business enterprises she has successfully implemented and maintained all aspects of communication, marketing, publicity and sales for each company. As a writer, singer, life coach, martial artist and entrepreneur, she uses her highly creative and disciplined talents to explore and develop new ideas for each of her companies. Presently, we employ over 20 people in various capacities.

Future goals include development of Fearless Fifties into a major motivational women's community and coaching enterprise using innovative internet strategies, media exposure and publishing ventures. Our strategies inlcude publishing, retreats, and speaking engagements, as well as developing coaching programs that can be utilized within the corporate market.
