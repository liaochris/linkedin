Jen Reed’s Summary

Jen Valshan (Love) Reed has been an entrepreneur for most of her professional life founding three different companies and several e-commerce websites along with helping hundreds of small businesses develop their own web presence.

Jen graduated from Lake Forest College in 1994 with a degree in Computer Science and a degree in Mathematics and is self taught in the field of web and graphics design.

* Graphics Designer for over 11 years
* Web Designer for over 11 years
* Web Server Admin for over 5 years
* Digital scrapbook designer for over 3 years at ScrapDish.com, DigiScrapZ.com, Scrapbook.com, Scrapbook-bytes.com, ScrapperGuide.com and ScrapWOW.com

Jen Reed’s Specialties:

Graphics Design, Web Design, Web Hosting, Marketing, Product Concepting, E-business Consulting, E-Commerce, Digital Scrapbooking

Software: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Dreamweaver
