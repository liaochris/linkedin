Keith Sterling’s Summary

Practised in the dark arts of Agile, Extreme, RUP & Prince, using pragmatic approach to the choice of methodology. Strong development management experience gained managing teams up to 80 people within programmes in excess of £80+ million budgets. Been through the wringer with VC's ( and survived ) and succesfully managed post dot.com organisations. Significant hands on experience in Java, J2EE, C, C++ which enables effective communication both at a technical developer level and managerial level.
