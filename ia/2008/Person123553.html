Martin Wells’s Summary

Experienced software industry professional with a track record in product management and development, leadership and innovation, international sales and business development.

Extensive experience with the internet and telecommunications industries, including:

Founder and CEO of Tangler, a developer of web discussion tools.

Network Director of one of Australia's first ISPs (1994) – awarded Australia’s “Best Value ISP” in 1999.

CTO of Dot Communications, a provider of software to the telecommunications industry.

Expert level programmer and analyst with deep knowledge of large scale designs and development.

Line and senior management experience at three internet startups.

Author of “J2ME Game Programming” – an award-winning 800 page book on advanced mobile software developed (published by Thomson Course).
