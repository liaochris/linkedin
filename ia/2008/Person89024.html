Jens Dittrich’s Summary

I am a Senior Researcher (in German: `Oberassistent´) in the Databases and Information Systems Group (DBIS) of Prof. Donald Kossmann, ETH Zurich. My primary research interests include Personal DataSpace Management (iMeMex), efficient algorithms for large datasets (joins, indexing), as well as algorithms for the efficient analysis of large data repositories (OLAP, personal information spaces).

My research has a strong focus on system building: during my Ph.D. I coauthored the open source project XXL - a library of advanced query processing algorithms. After that, I worked for SAP's OLAP engine (SAP BW) and SAP's enterprise search engine (TREX) on a joint project on high performance analytics (BI Accelerator, a distributed main-memory column-store). Since late 2004, I have been working on the DataSpace Management System iMeMex (supported by the Swiss National Science Foundation (SNF) with a 3 year fund). Other than that I am working on indexing techniques for systems facing high update rates.
