Vivek Singh Jamwal, FRM’s Summary

I have been a Tech consultant for over 8 years. But with tech, over the years i was working on similar projects in different domains. The process was the same - take requirements, implement the project, and move on to another one. I wanted then to specialize in a particular domain. I always liked working with numbers, and with my analytical mindset, i though finance was the bext fit. I then did my masters in applied finance from Singapore management university, and thoroughly enjoyed the course. I am also a certified financial risk manager now, and a CFA level 3 candidate. I was very fortunate to get a job with a hedge fund - Tantallon capital, and i enjoy the job thoroughly. I am currently looking at asian equities and am involoved with financial modelling. I enjoy meeting/knowing people with diverse backgrounds, races and cultures, you can add me to your network - vjammy [at] gmail . com
