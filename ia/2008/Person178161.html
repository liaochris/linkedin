Steve Lawson’s Summary

Widely acknowledged in the media and music world as one of the leading pioneers in the field of solo bass performance, particularly in the use of real time looping. I've lectured widely, at music colleges, universities, conferences and music shops across the world. numerous accolades from peers and press (see my website for details).

I've also run my own record label and managed my own career almost entirely online for the last 10 years and now consult extensively with musicians and independent labels on the future of the industry in an internet world. I write for MusicThinkTank.com and have contributed to Creative-Choices.co.uk and spoken in Universities about the future of music.

Always open to new projects - my latest venture, The Recycle collective, features many of the most celebrated musicians in London in a unique improvised music environment.

Steve Lawson’s Specialties:

Performance, improvisation, composition, arranging, production, teaching, technology and the future of the music industry in a digital age from the perspective of a completely independent musician. Have given university lectures on the future of music as a career, and what happens in a post-record-label industry. I consult with artists and labels, emphasising the usage of social media in connecting artists and fans.

  • Music and Social Media Lecturer/Teacher

    Self Employed

    (Music industry)

    April 1992 — Present (16 years 6 months)

    My education work is in two broad camps - private music tuition and university/music school lectures.

    In Universities, I address 3 main areas - either 'bass masterclasses' for bass guitarists, looking at the potential for the instrument to move beyond it's perceived role, 'electro-acoustic masterclasses', looking at the possibilities of looping and processing sound for performance and recording and how it changes our relationship with sound both as performers and listeners, and finally 'musicians and social media' - how independent musicians can use the tools, community and ethos of Social Media to curate multi-leveled conversations with their audience.

    As I private music tutor, I teach bass guitar from beginners to post-grad students, and music professionals. Students have included multi-million selling artists, and winners of internationally recognised competitions. I also teaching improvisation and composition to other instrumentalists.
