Jose Maria Puzo’s Summary

Senior professional with over 11 years of experience and rising career in multinational companies. Chemical engineer, I started my professional life in the chemical sector in Spain, always dealing with innovative and complex projects.

During this period, I acquired experience in people management and key account management at all corporate levels, enhancing my important language skills and completing my education with a Full Time MBA at Esade (Barcelona).

During the last 4 years I have been focused in the chemical sector (Sanitation, Detergents, Powder Coating and Nonwoven) sector assuming management positions, which has brought me a deep knowledge and experience in dealing with a start-up up to its consolidation, in marketing campaigns, in preparing business plans, in key accounting, in new product and market development.

Proven ability to work under pressure and in fast-changing and growing organisations, in assuming responsibilities and in innovation. Strong entrepreneurial approach and enthusiasm.

Analytical and time management skills. Used to manage multiple projects and teams around Europe and LATAM. Fluent in English, Spanish and Catalan, with notions of Germa.

I am currently looking for management opportunities which could use my expertise and my multicultural background to achieve challenging initiatives at global companies.
My main interests are: B2B companies, Sales and/or Marketing departmente and General Management.,
