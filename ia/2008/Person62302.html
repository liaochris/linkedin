Fabio Favia’s Summary

Expert in variuos networking technologies and protocols, my approach to networking is not related only to its "use" but mostly to a deep knowledge and interest in protocols on which networks are built on.

Deep knowledge in wireless networks, since 1993 when I became a full licensed radioamateur I've experimented (and built on them) various protocol adopted in wireless networks (AX25, 802.11 etc.) and the behaviuor of typical application on different types of link and distances.

I'm been co-author of a paper on QoS in 802.11 Wireless LAN presented at a worldwide conference and published on its related transaction.

I know variuos programming languages (C/C++, Perl, PHP) and the concept server/client architecture in software design: I've developed different tools as WEB Application such as Content Management Systems, Tasks Tracking and Management, Data Retrieving, Data Collection. Databases backend have been used in mosts of mine programs.

I'm an active supporter of Open Source and Free Software in particular of GNU/Linux: actually i'm in the SysAdmins' team of LUGBari.org.

Main goals: acquiring an in-depth knowledge and experience on network systems both in design/architectural view and in implementation view thus facilitating the entire process of planning and building more and more complex networking scenarios (ISP, Large Networks, Convergent Networks, Security, IPTV)
