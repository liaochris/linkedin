Chad Moore’s Summary

I am an experienced Animator, Motion Editor, and Character/Pipeline TD with a firm understanding of all things related to 3D character animation. I have direct experience with creating character set ups and pipelines as well as creating animation with both Motion Capture and Hand Keyframing techniques.

My areas of expertise are animation for pre-rendered cut scenes and in-game animation. I have taught in classroom and lab situations and have years of mentoring experience in production. I have functioned as a Lead, Manager and Department Director.

My goals are to continue to contribute to high quality game projects, focusing on the Technical Art of production.

Chad Moore’s Specialties:

Motion Capture Animation, Keyframing, Rigging, Pipeline set up and management, Directing animation, Mentoring, Team management, consulting, Managing and Directing departments.
