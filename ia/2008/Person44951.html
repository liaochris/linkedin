Jeff S. Darmo’s Summary

Darmo Real Estate Holdings, LLC, a subsidiary of Darmo Enterprises LLC, is focused on the successful development, redevelopment and repositioning of real estate assets: including multi-tenant commercial properties, apartment complexes, retail facilities, special-use and mixed use properties; The Company's investment focus is to create value through the acquisition, redevelopment and repositioning of commercial properties and re-leasing and managing these properties for maximum cash flow. Initial “growth business” will be conducted in select sub-markets of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and will capitalize upon and seize real estate opportunities presented by the enormous growth potential presented by the predominantly facilities-oriented, retail, special-use and mixed-use markets expanding outward from the CBD Philadelphia. Outside of our primary market, in which all property sizes will be considered, Darmo R.E. Holdings seeks assets in major metropolitan areas and growing markets nationwide. Darmo R.E. Holdings will be conducting business through wholly owned property investments, co-ownership in properties with private partners, institutional partners, and structured finance investments.

Darmo R.E. Holdings anticipates that, in an inflationary environment, and with the current financial and property market dislocation; that the strategic acquisition of property, the consolidation of property ownership in the local market, combined with successful redevelopment and repositioning of assets will consequently raise overall property value, NOI and quicken community oriented growth.
