Linda Stanley’s Summary

Launched into my own horticultural manufacturing and distribution business at the age of 18. Grew the company to a national distributor winning awards for the best potting compost in Australia before selling the company and moving to the UK in 1984. On moving to the UK, Linda formed Fleur International to import and distribute Australian preserved flora in to the UK and Europe. Linda and John Stanley moved back to Australia in 1988. In that year Linda and John formed a partnership in Nursery Business Consultants, later changing the name to John Stanley Associates. Linda is in charge of the administration, research and marketing of the company. As a result the company now operates regularly in over 25 countries providing retail consultancy and conference speaking to retailers and libraries around the globe.
