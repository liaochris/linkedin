Tomasz Kurnatowski, PRM’s Summary

My current job is Sr Financial Analyst at Treasury Product Control at Citi Handlowy, Citigroup. We are middle office between Treasury and Ops, and we have control/audit function. I work with commodities, interest rate derivatives, fx products and others. My work need both excellent knowledge of financial markets and its instruments, and of treasury products accounting methods.

I have 12 years of experience at financial markets (equity, derivatives, FX, commodities). My risk expertise is confirmed by Professional Risk Manager's Certificate (www.prmia.org).

My previous job was Risk analyst post in Risk Management Departament in PKO BP, the biggest bank in Poland. My main scope of duties was fixed income risk management (balance sheet and derivative instruments), but I deal also with luquidity, complex derivatives, fx and credit risk. I worked with Treasury, Structured Products and Mortgage products.

I do my best to match professional and personal development. At the moment I am preparing to CFA Level 1 (June 08). My further plans are finishing my PhD studies at Szkoła Główna Handlowa (Warsaw School of Economics), where I am going to defend my doctoral thesis on risk and financial markets. I would like to pass investment advisor exam, too (local CTA equivalent).

My professional goal is to enlarge my knowledge about risk, financial markets, and finance every day, and to have a job which becomes a passion - to go one step farther every day.
