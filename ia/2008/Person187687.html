Simon Ford’s Experience

  • Consultant

    The Zoo Group Pty ltd, trustee for the swordfish trust.

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    February 2003 — Present (5 years 4 months)

    The Zoo Group Pty Ltd was formed in 1992 to develop quazi residential accommodation in terrace houses for semi transient, working travelers throughout Sydney, Australia.

    Pioneering a unique ‘social / community based approach’ to shared living, The Zoo Groups concept was ground-breaking in terms of economic and human resource management using the internet as a delivery platform.

    The companies focus was to instill pride, value and purpose in the way communities co inhabited shared occupancy dwellings. It was achieved by providing a social networking platform to introduce transparency of individual accomplishments in terms of contribution towards a shared living community.

    After selling out interests in it's property portfolio & property management in 2003, The Zoo Group Pty Ltd shifted it's focus towards consulting and developing social media and various online networking platforms to a variety of interest groups.

  • Founder / principle

    Sleeping With The Enemy Pty Ltd

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

    January 1992 — February 2003 (11 years 2 months)

    Sleeping with the enemy forged a new industry segment within the Australian Backpacker accommodation market.

    With 300+ residents under management the business had 14 different properties housing an average of 30 residence each for between 6 and 12 weeks stay.

    Residents were assigned responsibility for maintenance of designated areas within the properties. Inspections for cleanliness were held bi weekly. If areas were clean residents responsible earned reward points, if not they had points demerited.

    Points earned meant cash savings for local goods & services. Local business would purchase advertising using points acquired by providing discounts.

    The level of efficiency these self management systems introduced to the business meant each property was without on site managers, the entire operation was managed with no more than 3 full time staff on any given day.

    University campuses housing 100 less occupants near by were being managed by 14+ staff.

  • Founder / facilitator

    Just-Cruisin promotions (Self-employed)

    (Self-Employed; 1-10 employees; Events Services industry)

    1995 — 2002 (7 years)

    A controlling interest in Sleeping With The Enemy Pty Ltd, with a captive audience of 300+ 19 to 27 year olds looking to party, was good cause to establish Just-Crusin promotions in 1995.

    Perfecting the art of social media as a way of facilitating communications to a transient youth market, I was able to build a portfolio of well known recurring events to the backpacker market in Sydney, Australia.

    We harnessed social networking strategies to promote events to a revolving community of travelers who were interconnected by a powerful word of mouth phenomenon that carried world wide long before the emergence of facebook or myspace.

    Just-Crusin's events included;

    - Weekly, monthly & bi annual party boat cruises on Sydney Harbor.
    - Weekly night clubs nights
    - Weekly pub nights
    - 10,000+ capacity New Years Eve parties, under Sydney Harbor Bridge.
