Darren DeMartino’s Summary

Darren DeMartino has been in the telecommunications field for over 19 years and has been a principal at ATC, Inc. since co-founding the firm in 1999 after an extremely successful career at Cable & Wireless. ATC, Inc. surpasses other agencies by removing the complexities of selecting, managing and maximizing telecommunications services. We leverage industry expertise and strategic partnerships with 50+ service providers to pair our clients with solutions that meet their terms and enhance their businesses. In combination, our relationships, knowledge of the latest products and services, and proficiency sourcing contracts yield a savings range of 20-40%. And ATC's status as a neutral agent means we work exclusively in your best interest. Regardless of your needs, we can help you get the most out of your telecom investment.
