Cathy Larkin’s Summary

At http://WebSavvyPR.com, I bring new media tools to the traditional PR toolkit to help expand the reach of your brand and build your business.

I put my 18 plus years of Public Relations and communications experience to work helping companies and non-profits, and small-businesses owners, solopreneurs, and individuals to bring their message to their customers and clients through both the traditional art of PR, and with a Web-Savvy PR toolkit.

E+C=R or Experience+Creativity=Results

I am also acting as a Social Media Guide, helping organizations and individuals learn to navigate, and be effective in, the new social networking landscape. And while I can speak I “Techtalk,” also speak plain English as well, so if needed, I can explain geeky things in a non-tech, easy-to-understand way.

I am a writer with a knack for finding creative slants and crafting media pitches that match the needs of the company/organization with the needs of the editor, freelance writer or blogger.

In my PR work or in the social media niche I am developing, I get to know my client's needs and match them with the right on-line networks, the specific tools they need, and show them the appropriate ways to use the tools effectively in this new digital space.

If you are on twitter, you can find me at http://twitter.com/CathyWebSavvyPR.com or @CathyWebSavvyPR

As a PR consultant, I welcome projects large and small - I can create and execute full PR campaigns, or simply write a dynamic press release. I can edit website copy, or set up a full-blown blog. I can create a new media strategy, sifting through the many social networking sites and on-line tools, to target the ones that will help you spread the word about what you do. This avoids the "If I build, it they will come" trap. And I can support his effort with PR outreach to traditional media channels

Contact me and let me put my 18 plus years of communications experience to work for you.
