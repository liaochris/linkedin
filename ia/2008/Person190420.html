Todd Tomek’s Summary

As an Executive Recruiter, my specialty is working with cutting edge biotech and pharmaceutical companies, in order to help them establish world class regulatory affairs and quality departments.

Philosophy:
To find the best qualified candidates for companies in need of top talent for critical or urgent positions. I strive for both my clients and candidates to be successful. Through effective listening, understanding, communication and trust, I am an expert in facilitating a win-win situation for both parties.

Much of our valuable time is spent in our professional career. I strongly believe if you're not challenged, happy, or feel your not reaching your goals, then you should make a change. I enjoy the unique privaledge of helping candidates make that change, to discover or enhance their passions, and present them life changing opportunities. For my client companies, I'm committed to help them grow, by obtaining a thorough understanding of their business, company culture, and then introducing them top talented candidates who are passionate about the growth and success of the company.
