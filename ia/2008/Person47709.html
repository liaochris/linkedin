David Sword’s Summary

Are you serious about CHANGING YOUR LIFESTYLE? ...want a more REWARDING WORK LIFE? ...NEED BALANCE in both?

David Sword is an expert FRANCHISE BUYER's advisor, using knowledge and 15 years of practice in human relations and organization development to fully understand client's needs, experience and aspirations to help them evaluate, acquire and build businesses that will assure their lifestyle success. His company has developed personal relationships with the best brands in the franchise business and wants to help prospective franchise owners really understand the pros and cons.

David understands lifestyle changes personally, having successfully developed careers as an international business executive, SMBE turnaround expert and corporate coach. As a successful franchise owner himself, he knows buyer's confusion, frustration and need for learning all about the franchise marketplace and the best operating systems BEFORE THEY PURCHASE A BUSINESS!

David Sword’s Specialties:

Franchise Advisor, Franchise selection, Franchising, Start up specialist, Business coach, SMBE/WMBE development, Organization development, Business turnarounds, Executive coach, Financial analysis, Due diligence investigations, Franchise Disclosure Document analysis, Business funding.
