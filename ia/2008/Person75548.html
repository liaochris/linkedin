Hector Dominguez’s Summary

Hector has worked for several institutions in Mexico, Japan and the US in Research and development of technology and I find myself as a good interface between industry and academy. I have been trained on project management and software development management, as well as new product development.

He is currently interested in application of automation and engineering in daily life and its impact in people. I am looking forward to working on medical, biotech and innovative applications. I am very comfortable working in interdisciplinary teams and I adapt myself easily to new environments of work.

In addition to that, he enjoys traveling and knowing about different cultures and types of people. Enjoys all types of movies, art, food and cultural experiences and tries to keep up reading books.

Hector Dominguez’s Specialties:

Broad experience in different fields of automation: basic research and development, robotics, CNC machines and high precision mechanisms, real time control software, monitoring for oil industry, vision inspection and features identification, orientation systems, automatic data acquisition.
