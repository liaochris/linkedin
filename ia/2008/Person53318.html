Donna Maria Coles Johnson’s Summary

Donna Maria, affectionately known as the "Chief Executive Indie," is an award winning, speaker and author, and an expert in the field of business as a lifestyle. "Instead of working for someone else, use your gifts and talents to build your own corporate ladder. Enjoy your life, build your business and have your way --all at the same time!"

As a wife, mother, attorney and entrepreneur, Donna Maria knows what it's like to grow exasperated with the limited offerings of a traditional work environment. Ten years ago, she left a lucrative job as an attorney in a Fortune 500 company to create her own brand of happiness. She has been so successful that when her husband was laid off of his job, she simply hired him! "We use our God-given gifts, talents and passions to serve others as we simultaneously put our family on firm financial footing," she says. Her mission is to help other families launch businesses that allow them to enjoy the benefits of entrepreneurship, service, independence, flexibility and fun.

Donna Maria Coles Johnson’s Specialties:

I have a keen marketing sense, especially when it comes to helping moms and families use creativity and technology to start, own and manage successful businesses from home. Through the Indie Business brand, I inspire people to identify their chief assets and then turn them into an income stream that supports the family while supplying valuable products and services to the public. Along with my husband, I help people manage home and business, without compromising either.
