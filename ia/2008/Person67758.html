Gary Wells’s Summary

Twenty-three years application development experience as a development manager, project manager, product manger, systems architect, and senior developer. Nine years in the software development industry followed by seven years IT management at a leading investment banking and brokerage firm, seven years as a senior partner in two consulting firms, and three years product manager of a hosted ASP model CRM solution. Expert at management of offshore development with 8 years multi-vendor multi-project experience. Managed and developed projects on intranet/internet web, client server, and IBM Midrange platforms. Co-Author of the WebLogic Bible published by Wiley Press.
