Eddie Rehfeldt’s Summary

I am a creative professional experienced in design and production for film, television and electronic media. I have had the good fortune to provide both leadership and direction for a number of fine organizations. My career path began as a stage manager for my mother's ballet company which led to time working on film sets, televisions shows, documentaries, commercials, music videos and a eventual premiering my own film at the Sundance Film Festival.

Most recently I have meandered into the world of software. I have spent significant time developing a number of online properties and working on the executive corridor at Microsoft in Redmond, Wa. While there I whet my entrepreneurial appetite and eventually decided to signing on with Final Draft and run their global marketing, sales and business development organizations. After a year long commute to LA, I am back in Seattle producing a variety of content for the web and traditional media outlets and consulting clients in the Middle East, Asia and the US.

Eddie Rehfeldt’s Specialties:

Managing creative process from concept to delivery. Branding and identity, abstract thinking, logistics, negotiation, purchasing, solving problems. Leadership experience in film, television and electronic media. Developing technology/marketing/sales strategies, analyzing business opportunities, managing large teams, solid marketing skills. Solid sales experience in software. Understands system design. Proficient at relationship management and business solution implementation.
