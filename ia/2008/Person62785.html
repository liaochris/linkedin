Farid Ahmed’s Summary

Farid is a seasoned HR Consultant, with several years of sound achievements in strategic HR Management starting from Job evaluations & Analysis, Performance, Compensation, Benefits administration, Reward management, Learning, Development, Career and Succession Planning, Change Management and Organizational Development.
