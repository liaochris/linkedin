Chris Reinhard’s Summary

Chris Reinhard is currently serving as Lifestyle Marketing Guru at Motive, a hybrid agency at the forefront of the experiential and interactive marketing arena. His experience in online music journalism and grassroots marketing places him in a unique group of young marketers capable of satisfying creative needs for all manner of companies. Since joining Motive in 2005, Chris has helped bring to life a number of experiential programs for Pepsi, Qdoba Mexican Grill, Vehix, Good Times Burgers and Frozen Custard, and TCF Bank through extensive copywriting and “big idea generation.”

Before joining Motive, Chris acted as a sampling representative for PepsiCo, aiding in the orchestration of several programs surrounding both the pre-launch and launch efforts for Pepsi Blue and Pepsi Twist. Additionally, in 2001 Chris founded PunkDiscovery.com, an online music journal for which he acted as CEO, head articles editor, columnist and head webmaster.

In addition to his work at Motive, Chris is acting as Artist Relations Manager at Big Ear Music, an emerging media channel. Chris also sits on the advisory board at Zuzingo, a social networking firm located in Boulder, Colorado. Recently Chris completed a lead acting role in "Delivery," a film by Eli Berg.

Chris operates a personal blog, Reverie Machine, located at http://www.chrisreinhard.com.
