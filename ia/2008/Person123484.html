Martin Garland’s Summary

Experienced business leader in the software industry. Significant experience with:

1. Start up operations
2. Turn around strategies
3. Interim Management
4. Product build out

Have held many 'C' level positions including CEO, EVP Sales, EVP Marketing and VP Business Develpoment

Expertise in working with young and mid size companies throughout Europe, The Americas and Asia. Good aptitude for quickly assessing strengths and weaknesses, developing strong sales and marketing strategies and executing both direct and indirect channel models.
