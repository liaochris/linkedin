Lara (Allen) Fabans’s Summary

My extensive internet experience has covered the birth of the Internet revolution. I have gone from contributing to newsgroups to developing strategies for online marketing campaigns. I have led technical direction for numerous companies as a consultant.

With my strong analytical skills, I can identify and find potential solutions for problems quickly. My plan includes three options with my recommendation.

I'm a strong proponent of the new forms of old style marketing. I also believe that the internet allows us to grow marketing campaigns much bigger and much faster as the cost to send out two similar test emails is next to nothing. And the gains can be phenomenal.

Growing up in the Silicon Valley, I have an interesting perspective on how things have changed and yet remained the same. I have volunteered my time with the Campaign Ethics Foundation and motorcycle rights organizations.
