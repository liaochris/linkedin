Jeff Guillet’s Summary

I am a senior consultant for Convergent Computing (CCO), a Microsoft Gold Partner in Oakland, CA.

I hold MCITP:Enterprise Administrator, MCSE, MCSE:Messaging and CISSP certifications. I am the Technical Editor of the book "Windows Server 2008 Unleashed" and a contributing author for the books "Exchange Server 2003 Unleashed" and "Exchange Server 2007 Unleashed", all by Sams Publishing.

I work primarily with Microsoft products (Windows Server, Active Directory, Exchange, MOM, Systems Center Operations Manager, ISA, WSUS, etc.). I also work with various other platforms and products, usually in the form of migrations. Microsoft is not a "religion" for me. They simply provide the best products, in my opinion, and I like to work with the best.

I am a co-captain and judge in the Imagine Cup competition held this year in Paris.
