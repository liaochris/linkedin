Stefanie Zizzo’s Summary

I work with people who are seeking a personal and/or professional transition, helping them gain clarity and focus with regard to who and where they are today vs. who and where they would like to be in the future. My clients benefit by discovering what motivates and energizes them, learning what holds them back from achieving their goals, and choosing new paths/possibilities for their life.

I am known for my energy, skills in listening and understanding, intuition and perception, questioning and challenging, analyzing and synthesizing, and planning.

I am certified as a Life Coach through the International Coach Federation and Institute for Life Coach Training and hold a masters in Counseling and bachelors in Psychology.

I offer individual coaching sessions, by phone, two – three times per month and a variety of workshops and seminars. In addition, I just release by first book The Journey From Comfort to Possibility: A Workbook of Self-Discovery and Personal Transformation. Copies are available through www.stefaniezizzo.com/Workbook

I look forward to the opportunity to partner with you in designing your new life path! Call me for a complimentary trial coaching session to see if my style of coaching matches what you are seeking! I promise you’ll get excellent value from this complimentary session, regardless of whether you decide on me or another coach.

Also an affilate coach with VocationVacation.com and The Coach Connection.

Stefanie Zizzo’s Specialties:

During my 11 years as a career counselor, I have coached more than 1000 people through different phases of career development including the mid-career professional making a transition, women returning to a career after raising a family and clients focusing on a transition into retirement. I have also trained hundreds of clients to strengthen their communication and presentation skills.
