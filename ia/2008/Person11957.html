Andrew Hall’s Summary

I have worked in the Information Technology industry as a consultant and manager for over 12 years. I come from a Financial and Accounting background with over 10 years experience. This included being Financial Controller of two companies before my career change to Information Technology.

I have always had a quiet patient composure with an open mind to new ideas. I have made it a priority to always pass any credit to my Team, as there is no “I” in TEAM. I have also ensured that those that work with me are protected, from those that criticise and play political games, to ensure the Team is enabled to work at the best of team’s ability.

I have worked at both Technological and Representative Levels in multiple IT business landscapes including the following industries; Petroleum, Federal and State Government, Wholesale, Retail, Manufacturing, Energy Production and Distribution, Transport, Export and Aggregate. The infrastructure supported has included stand alone systems to Global Enterprises.

I have developed key ongoing vendor relationships with VMware, Novell and Red Hat to ensure I have a stake in the virtualisation and open source opportunities occurring at present. This has been very helpful in leading the Emerging Technologies Team at Fujitsu. Our Team’s objective is to develop, implement and support innovative systems and solutions for varying business needs with as much bang for buck as possible.
