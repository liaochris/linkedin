Emily Ross’s Summary

* Bestselling business author, freelance writer and consultant. Current clients include SmartCompany.com.au, Australian Gourmet Traveller, BRW Magazine, Media Manoeuvres, Australian Davos Connection, Diversity Management, the age (melbourne) magazine, Random House, Hardie Grant, TheCoolHunter.net, Floate Design, The Kalmor Partnership and Herbario. I work on a portfolio of writing, research, consulting and speaking projects as well as one-on-one media training, communications strategy and job interview coaching with senior executives in the public and private sector - from areas including biotech, resources, medicine, education, local government, infrastructure, finance and professional services.
* Co-author of 50 Great e-Businesses and the Minds Behind Them, Random House, September 2007. 100 Great Businesses and the Minds Behind Them, Random House, 2005.
Goals: Compelling writing, research and public speaking. Help others to improve the way they communicate.

Emily Ross’s Specialties:

Entrepreneurs, web start-ups, science, innovation, diversity, media strategy, work-life balance, lifestyle and business travel.
