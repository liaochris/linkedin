Heidi Panelli’s Summary

Experienced and versatile, I specialize in youth-oriented illustration & design, educational illustration & design and T-Shirt design using Photoshop and Illustrator.

I also run a few online stores selling T-shirts and gifts online with the help of Cafepress.com, Printfection.com, and ImageKind.com. I've been with Cafepress.com since 2003.

Heidi Panelli’s Specialties:

Whimsical illustration/design, kids illustration/design, educational illustration/design, realistic/scientific/medical illustration/design, web retail & marketing, web design, T-shirt design, giftware design, seasonal illustration/design, book design

Heidi Panelli’s Experience

  • Shopkeeper

    CafePress.com

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry)

    August 2003 — Present (5 years 3 months)

    I am not employed by Cafepress.com; though they play a major role in my online business where I design and maintain online stores selling my designs on T-shirts and giftware. Cafepress.com not only hosts my stores, but they print and ship my products. In addition, I volunteer to help their team develop shopkeeper tools and share my experience with other members of the Cafepress community.

  • Freelance Illustrator

    Spectrum Studio

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2000 — Present (8 years)

    Commercial illustrator from my home studio. Projects have included illustration, plush toy design, novelty signage and holiday decoration concepting and design, poster design, "bobble-arm doll" design, caricature and other projects for clients including General Mills, International Dairy Queen, Minnesota Orchestra, Lifeway Christian Resource, & Impact Plastics.

  • Owner & Designer

    BusyBodies

    (Internet industry)

    1998 — Present (10 years)

    I created the business, maintain the web site, and creatively market my products. I also create all the designs for the large selection T-Shirts and giftware featuring various interests, hobbies, sports and activities.

  • Sr. Designer/Illustrator

    TREND enterprises, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Primary/Secondary Education industry)

    February 2001 — August 2008 (7 years 7 months)

    I designed and illustrated print products for the education market including teaching aids, classroom decorations, activity books, posters, stickers, and a wide variety of supplemental learning matrials for Kindergarten through Ninth grade.

    While at TREND, my specialties included realistic illustrations, cartoon vector art, scientific illustrations, and whimsical styles all finished in Illustrator or Photoshop. Many projects were completed for press in Quark or InDesign. I also designed and assembled trade show booth displays, directed photo shoots and ensured print quality by attending press checks for all products I work on.

  • Graphic Designer

    Koechel Peterson & Assoc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Graphic Design industry)

    1999 — 2000 (1 year)

    Designed gift book covers, interiors, catalogs, and advertisements for major book publishers (Word, Zondervan, Harvest House, and others) and giftware companies. Applications used were Freehand, Quark, and Photoshop. Duties also included some hand drawn illustration, digital illustration, photo shoot direction and press checks.

  • Airbrush Artist

    Air 2B Difrent

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Arts and Crafts industry)

    1998 — 1999 (1 year)

    Airbrushed custom art on T-shirts and giftware, demonstrated for the public, and managed booth operations at ValleyFair! and the Mall of America. Worked under the direction of Kend Lind, a nationally know authority on airbrush art.

  • Caricature Artist

    Richmond Illustration

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Arts and Crafts industry)

    1992 — 1999 (7 years)

    Caricature artist for award-winning Mad Magazine artist, Tom Richmond. While contracted with Tom, I drew and airbrushed caricatures for live audiences, drawing thousands of faces over several years! I drew at ValleyFair! amusement park, Mall of America, as well as numerous corporate picnics, functions, and parties.

  • Caricature Artist-Independent Contractor

    Maund Entertainment

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Entertainment industry)

    1993 — 1999 (6 years)

    I drew live caricatures at school parties, holiday parties, corporate events, Mall of America's Camp Snoopy amusement park and other events on a freelance basis.
