Michael Puchol’s Summary

Having experience and knowledge in fields as varied as public safety, aeronautics, electronics, all kinds of wireless communications, hardware design, and security, with strong skills in management, I am particularly focused on entrepreneurship and engineering.

Michael Puchol’s Specialties:

Consultation on a wide range of technologies, particularly when several related ones interact in a project, as well as hands-on development on all my fields of expertise. Strong communication and management skills. Understanding of how business decisions affect engineering teams and vice-versa. Experience in dealing with multidisciplinary teams spread around the world working on the same projects.

Michael Puchol’s Experience

  • Founder and CTO

    Whisher

    (Wireless industry)

    March 2006 — Present (2 years 7 months)

    Managing a multidisciplinary team across six countries in three continents, my job is to make the magic behind the Whisher WiFi sharing and roaming platform happen. I deal intimately with various technologies, from software & driver development on multiple operating systems to server infrastructure and backend services.

    Key achievements:
    • Managed the development of a multi-platform WiFi connectivity manager with social features such as file sharing, instant messaging and WiFi quality ratings.
    • Helped secure a patent on a software-based secure WiFi sharing system.
    • Rolled out a billing and RADIUS infrastructure in record time, setting up roaming with partners such as Swisscom and WeRoam.

  • Electronics R&D Project Manager

    SouthWing

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    September 2005 — March 2006 (7 months)

    Responsible for electronics R&D efforts at the company, in the design of Bluetooth devices.

    Key achievements:
    • Oversaw the Bellesguard project, an innovative Bluetooth headset with touch-controls instead of mechanical buttons. Currently in production and sale.
    • Coordinated the design of the electronics with the rest of the departments, and liaised with Chinese OEMs and ODMs through the prototyping, pilot runs and production of the products.

  • Head of internal R&D

    Generalitat de Catalunya - Departament d'Interior

    (Public Safety industry)

    January 2002 — December 2003 (2 years)

    Responsible for the development and deployment of advanced technology on the existing TETRA digital radio network operated by the Catalan Fire Department, such as automated command & control systems to direct helicopters and aircraft to fire drop zones, vehicle and personnel location systems, and data communications for mobile C&C vehicles.

    Key achievements:
    • Rolled out one of the largest TETRA infrastructures at the time, with over 200 base stations and 15.000 mobile terminals.
    • Designed and built several advanced systems such as an analog-to-digital gateway with remote control over SDS messaging, and DMO-to-DMO failover repeaters.

  • CEO & Founder

    Sonar Security

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Defense & Space industry)

    April 1997 — September 2002 (5 years 6 months)

    Founded Sonar Security, a company dedicated to the development of highly customized solutions in the fields of electronics, mobile data networks, counterintelligence, SIGINT, security, and space communications. Also performed consultancy and development work for a number of public safety agencies on deployment of digital radio networks based on TETRA and TETRAPol technologies.
