Paul Zink’s Summary

A copy-trained Creative Director & writer with a wide range of B2C and B2B experience on both the agency and client sides, from magazine circulation to financial services. My work combines attention-getting advertising executions that are strategically grounded and deliver persuasive marketing messages that produce positive, measurable results.
