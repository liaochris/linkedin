Pat Ryan’s Summary

As a business owner, provide the intelligence, customer insight and analytics to measure and drive initiatives to increase revenue per user and decrease churn. Build innovative and user-centric products, which are profitable.

Experience managing a software product or consumer web properties from feature definition through product launch. Intellectually restless individual who can think big yet still hang on to the key details – in other words, a visionary architect with a contractor’s know how.

Strategic thinker who is extremely analytical and detail oriented and great at multitasking and prioritizing, as well. Able to take end-to-end ownership of the product line, identify market requirements, define the product vision, create preliminary design concepts and drive the implementation of the overall product roadmap. Build strong business cases for presentation to senior management and understand the technical requirements driving development.
