Jessica Aguilar’s Summary

Jessica attained a bachelor of journalism degree from Indiana University with concentrations in fine arts and computer sciences.

She joined WellPoint in 2007 as employment branding manager. In this role, she is responsible for overseeing and managing WellPoint’s employer brand across the enterprise and within all of WellPoint’s business units. Her role is focused on promoting and championing WellPoint as an “employer of choice” both internally to current associates and externally to support the organization’s talent sourcing strategies. Jessica manages the wellpoint.com/careers web site and also provides brand/marketing support as a member of the HIPP program team.

In 2007, her work focused on refreshing WellPoint’s external employer of choice image through initiatives which included: refreshing and re-launching the WellPoint careers web site, creating realistic job preview videos, and pursuing innovative methods of communicating WellPoint’s employer brand message.

Jessica Aguilar’s Specialties:

Experience in Branding, Marcomm, PR in the area of Human Resources/Corporate Talent "employer of choice" brand positioning and promotion.
