Colin Reid’s Summary

Colin Reid is a high energy yet polished leader who sells and delivers growth and revenue from both new and existing business. As an executive with over 10 years of management experience with a variety of multi-national, entrepreneurial and market-making businesses Colin has regularly lead companies through projects that have won awards around the world and are now used as best in class case studies.

Colin is known by his clients and colleagues as a highly creative individual who is not afraid to create a new paradigm or stretch the boundaries of existing solutions. An experienced and articulate professional, Colin is particularly strong in looking at existing processes and relationships and building more inventive, structured and profitable approaches.

With defined experience in managing and running all areas of a business Colin is an expert at channel relationships and partner strategies. He is proven in developing and selling long term plans that deliver sustainable competitive market advantages and collaborative revenue for his organization and partners. Throughout his career these skills have allowed him to deliver triple digit revenue growth and capture significant market share while improving overall efficiency and profitability.
