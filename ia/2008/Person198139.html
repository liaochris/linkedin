Viviana Venneri’s Summary

CURIOSITY for me is a key word: I would like to have 48 hours a day to do as many things as I can, to learn things I don’t know, to discover something new.
The other key word, that has to be matched with the first one, is PASSION.
CURIOSITY + PASSION: means that I don’t wanna learn just to be more than other people, to have always the right answer, to be considered smart, to get other people amazed.
It just means that I wanna squeeze life, work out life, be touched by life, be multicoloured, be multi-faced, be alive, be contradicted more than be supported, if it could be a different way to discover new stimulating points of view. And all of that, never getting weary.

I can’t stand: pessimists. moralists. rhetoricians. arrogant people. lazy people. sulky people. predictable conclusions. promises. sentences like “I’m sure I’ll never do something like that", Pablo Coelho books. Muccino movies. Romantic poetry. Da Vinci Code.

I love: dreamers. brilliant people. people keen on (....). twinkling eyes. sensitiveness. concreteness. grit. smiles. Godot. Swann. Dolls House. The Waste land. Cyrano de Bergerac. Woody Allen.
