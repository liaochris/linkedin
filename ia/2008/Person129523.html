  • Partner

    (Partnership; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

We found our audiences asking us to help them recognize our unique combination of IT professional skills and Presentation Skills. The Powerful Presentations Alliance was born to meet this unrelenting audience demand.

  • Toastmaster

    Toastmasters International

    (Non-Profit; 10,001 or more employees; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    March 2003 — Present (5 years)

    Toastmasters International is the leading movement devoted to making effective oral communication a worldwide reality.

    Through its member clubs, Toastmasters International helps men and women learn the arts of speaking, listening and thinking – vital skills that promote self-actualization, enhance leadership, foster human understanding and contribute to the betterment of mankind.

  • Technology Coordinator

    Fort LeBoeuf School District

    (Educational Institution; 201-500 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    July 1996 — Present (11 years 8 months)

    As the Technology Coordinator for a public school district, Michael Cortes is responsible for the management, integration, and maintenance of all technology in the district. This responsibility includes but is not limited to:

    Prepare and submit all government required reporting and oversight reports.
    Prepare, carry out, and evaluate all technology plans.
    Conduct all network administration.
    Lead and manage technology personnel.
    Oversee and approve all work conducted by outside contractors.
    Evaluate and monitor system security and employee/student usage on district's resources.

    Michael is also personal communications expert who can help by consulting and coaching in your personal communications whether addressing your team, your employees or a larger audience; and can also assist you by presenting to your employees or leadership team directly, with a keynote, seminar, or training session in his areas of expertise.
