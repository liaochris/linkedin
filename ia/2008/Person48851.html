Deb Gibb’s Summary

Deb Gibb is a key player you will want on your team to make sound business decisions and drive high-impact strategic initiatives.

With over 20 years of operations and technology experience in both rapidly growing and established companies, Deb is currently a Senior Technology Consultant for CSTV Networks, the collegiate sports division of CBS Sports. She has held executive positions at ProfitLine, QUALCOMM, CollegeClub, First Virtual Holdings, BioData, ComputerLand and CCH Computax. With a strong focus on effective execution, best practice processes and open, direct communication, Deb offers practical experience in successfully scaling technology as well as operational teams.

Deb's principal strength is providing leadership, inspiration and strategic direction that leverages information technologies to deliver competitive business advantages. Her fact-based, no-nonsense approach allows her to work effectively with cross-organizational business and technology stakeholders as well as customers and vendors. Deb's management experience includes advising business professionals, integrating information systems with business processes, recruiting and developing effective teams and assuring Product and Customer Service excellence.
