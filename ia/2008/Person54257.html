Prof.Dr.Bashir. A Siddiqi’s Summary

Dr. Siddiqi is the founder and president of the Elahi Welfare Foundation. He graduated from Punjab University, Govt. College, Lahore ( Pakistan) with a M.S. degree in Botany in 1959. He received his Ph.D. from London University, Queen Mary College ( England), in Molecular & Microbial Genetics in 1967. He was a post-doctoral fellow at University of Oslo, Department of General Genetics ( Norway), from 1970-72 and fellow of University of Upsala (Sweden) from 1972-73. He joined the faculty at Peshawar University, Department of Botany in 1959, served as chairman in 1962, and continued as associate professor till 1978. He was invited as visiting professor at Quaid-e-Azam University,Islamabad, Department of Biological Sciences, from 1978-80. He was appointed as professor at King Faisal University, College of Agriculture from 1980-83 and scientific advisor at King Abdulaziz City for Science & Technology ( Saudi Arabia) from 1983-93. He is well versed with American system of education where he served on teaching assignments for more than 10 years. He has dedicated almost 45 years of his life to teaching and research. He has 50 publications in the form of research papers, reports, and contributions in the field of Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering.
