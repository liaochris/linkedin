Michiel Janzen’s Summary

GBE is an advertising agency for offline and online communication. Getting attention and keeping hold of it. Moving people. Realising more revenues. That is what GBE is about. We interprete your message properly, but also add a little imagination. For an impression of our work take a look at our website.

Below you find a summary of what we do:

PRINT
The beginning of a successful ad campaign. Should we yell or whisper? Should we convince or inform? GBE is your agency for creating, planning and handling of local campaigns.

INSTORE
All the things like displays, P.O.S.-material and narrowcasting that form the solutions to affect the buying behavior of your customers.

ONLINE
Internet should be leading in your advertising strategy. When you want to explore online marketing activities do not hesitate to contact us.

DM
Direct mail is hotter than ever. The best medium for a one-on-one communication. No client database? Don’t worry, GBE selects (on a basis of criteria determination) a new database for you. We take care of it all.

GRAPHIC DESIGN
Our core business. Brand management or corporate identity, GBE handles overall marketing and branding strategies and sales promotions for its clients.
