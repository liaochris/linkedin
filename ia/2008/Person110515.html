Lanz Chan, Ph.D.’s Summary

Lanz Chan is partner and MD of the Finamatrix Group (BVI) LLC that has managed in excess of USD1B. Over the past 10 years, Lanz has worked for JP Morgan, Franklin Templeton, Jones Lang Lasalle and UBS in investment banking, asset management, real estate consulting and wealth management based in Singapore, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Zurich, Switzerland, Macau and Zhuhai, China.

He currently manages a real estate portfolio and the Finamatrix DSS research laboratories in China and teaches executive education part-time at universities in Macau, where he is based and in Singapore, where he is from.

He holds a Ph.D. (ABD) in Finance from Universidade de Ciência e Tecnologia de Macau, M.Sc. (distinction) and B.Sc. (magna cum laude) in Real Estate from the National University of Singapore as well as the Diploma in Wealth Management from UBS.

A real estate valuer or appraiser by training, Lanz has also published research papers in the Journal of Property Investment and Finance, Journal of Property Research and Market Technician Journal.

He was also President of the Graduate Students Society of the National University of Singapore and is also consulting director of a Singapore-based asset management firm which manages a real estate portfolio of USD200M. Lanz has also been invited to be on the board and member of various international finance and real estate companies and associations, and is currently in the process of publishing his first book on forex trading cybernetics and risk technologies.

Lanz also served the Singapore Armed Forces as a logistics specialist as part of National Service and has keen interest in hotel, casino, resort, spa management, hospitality, tourism and MICE industries.

He has recently been appointed as the fund manager for a Gaming related hedge fund based in China, and is also part of the media brand - MZM (Money Zero Maturity, Macau Zhuhai Model)
