Jyoti Banerjee’s Summary

I specialise in creating and driving new strategic initiatives in technology companies, with most of my experience being in business application software. Some of my noteworthy clients (since 1991) have included leading application software providers such as Microsoft, SAP, JD Edwards, and PeopleSoft. In this area, I have helped companies such as Navision and Great Plains build UK market entry strategies.

I am involved in a venture fund that invests in medium-sized businesses that are committed to ethical practice - all these investments are in third-world, emerging markets settings. And I am on the board of Interserve, an international faith-based charitable organisation where I have plenty of exposure to the Carver Policy Governance Model.

Since the summer of 2005, I have researched the performance of mid-sized organisations. As a result of the research, I co-founded M Institute, a resource for leaders of mid-sized organisations.

In 2008, I became a director of Agitavi Research, a global organisation focusing on research, business strategy and enterprise learning in the ICT sector.
