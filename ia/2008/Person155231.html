Rafael Atijas’s Summary

Rafael currently works for In-Style, a company specialized in providing technological and digital marketing services for the music industry.
He has developed strategies in digital marketing for Norah Jones, Lenny Kravitz, KT Tunstall, Joss Stone, Lily Allen, Coldplay, Belinda, Juan Luis Guerra and RBD among many other artists.
Rafael has a degree in Social Communications (emphasis on Advertising) from the Universidad Católica del Uruguay. He also has a Certificate of Proficiency in English from Cambridge University (UK), studies in graphic design at Ort University (Uruguay) and in spring 2008 Rafael completed courses in Online Promotions, Internet Marketing Strategies and Music Marketing at the New York University.
From 1999 to 2006, Rafael worked for a Uruguayan real estate development company at the head of the marketing department. Besides, he had a weekly music column on Uruguay’s most listened to FM radio station.
Rafael is also a professional musician and released two albums in Uruguay and one in the U.S. He studied songwriting at Berklee University (Boston), as well as guitar, bass guitar, audio engineering and acoustic theory.
