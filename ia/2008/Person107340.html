Jean Boyle’s Summary

After years in a corporate environment, on both coasts, I have now been living (12+ years) and working on the beautiful Kitsap Peninsula for the last 8 years.

I have the unique opportunity to work for the Visitors Bureau and provide them with photos, while also taking photos for my own business. We all win and I am having a great time with both the photography and my other responsibilities at the Visitor & Convention Bureau.

Kitsap includes Gig Harbor, Port Orchard, Bremerton, Belfair, Silverdale, Poulsbo, Suquamish, Port Gamble, Bainbridge Island, Kingston, Hansville, Little Boston and a collection of smaller communities.

My world is smaller and more creative than ever.

Jean Boyle’s Specialties:

KitsapImages.com maintains a website with over 4,000 stock photos of the beautiful Kitsap Peninsula . . . and beyond. Scenic, wildlife, lifestyle, communities. I can provide existing and assignment photos for websites and/or print.

Kitsap includes Gig Harbor, Port Orchard, Bremerton, Belfair, Silverdale, Poulsbo, Suquamish, Port Gamble, Bainbridge Island, Kingston, Hansville, Little Boston and a collection of smaller communities. Photos also include Seattle, Olympic Peninsula and beyond.
