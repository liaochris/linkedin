Giorgio Buccilli’s Summary

COO at EnginSoft SpA, Computer Aided Engineering company headquartered in Italy with over 20 subsidiaries worldwide. I manage Europe and Middle East channel partners and subsidiaries, aimed to the business development of our flagship software product modeFRONTIER.

From 2000 to 2007 I worked for the italian branch of Ansys Inc. (aka Fluent Italy, before Ansys acquisition) as Sales & Marketing Manager. Ansys develops and markets engineering simulation software, consultancy and trainings.

In the past I worked as research fellow for an industrial firm. I have been awarded of some patents in gas combustion technology.

I have a Master Degree in Aeronautic Engineering and a Master in Marketing. I took an extra qualification with an MBA (Master of Business Administration) as well.
