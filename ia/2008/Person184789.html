Synta E. Keeling’s Summary

My professional experience has entailed preparing and filing H-1B, L-1, E-2, and business visa petitions for professionals and executives. I also prepare and file PERM, I-140, and I-485 applications ("green card" cases) for employees of large and small corporate clients.

I am an active member of the D.C. chapter AILA. I am the chair of the New Members Division for our chapter.

In addition, I do pro bono work, filing appellate briefs, organizing naturalization workshops, and handling immigration matters pro bono.

In 1997, I received my AB in Government from the College of William and Mary in Williamsburg, Virginia. I received my J.D. from American University, Washington College of Law in 2007. I speak and write an advanced proficiency of Spanish that I gained while studying abroad for a year in Spain.

Synta E. Keeling’s Specialties:

• Obtaining business visas and legal permanent residence on behalf of foreign nationals working in highly skilled capacity or in executive capacity.
