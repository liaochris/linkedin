Stacy Buhbe’s Summary

Dr. Stacy Buhbe is a licensed clinical psychologist with a diverse range of expertise and experience. In her private practice, she provides assessment, consultation and treatment to individuals, couples and other relationship dyads. She has particular expertise with anxiety and depression, substance abuse, couples therapy, parenting issues, trauma, relationship violence, identity issues, LGBTQ issues, HIV/AIDS related issues, sexuality and sexual health, and personal growth goals. As the Director of Clinical Services for the Relationship Training Institute, Dr. Buhbe regularly presents at workshops and conferences, and is involved in networking, program development and expanding RTI's role in the community. Dr. Buhbe also presents independently at other conferences within her specialty field, and has been a regular guest on television programs, such as KUSI's Inside San Diego, and a consultant to NBC 7/39 news programming. Dr. Buhbe has pre- and post-doctoral intern supervision experience, and is currently a Probation Certified Domestic Violence Facilitator and Supervisor.
