Jeroen Hulscher’s Summary

In the past years I've worked for several companies in the role of designer, developer and SEO, before starting my own company (BohemianWorks) early 2006.
I graduated Cum Laude in July 2007 and started working as an Interaction Developer and a Social Networking Specialist at Tam Tam in Delft, while still continuing my own company. I'm constantly driven to get better and faster, and always working on my skills as a salesman and an entrepreneur.

I'm putting much effort in streamlining the processes at Tam Tam at the moment concerning communications and standards. On behalf of BohemianWorks I'm working out some ideas for the future which involves the digitalization of cultural heritage in the Netherlands. Feel free to ask more about this and other projects!
