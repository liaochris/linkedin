John Durand’s Summary

John Durand has a background in providing enterprise market information solutions and services to most of the large food, drug and mass merchandisers in the United States. John has worked with most major retailers in the United States including Wal-Mart, Kroger, Albertsons, and Safeway. He has also worked closely with many major packaged goods manufacturers and briefly worked for a company formed by the top 50 manufacturers including Proctor & Gamble, Unilever, Kraft Foods, Nabisco, Kimberly Clark, Kellogg's. John has fourteen years of experience working at a food brokerage, a syndicated data and market research provider, a consumer promotions company, and a procurement and data collaboration network. He has worked as a technology consultant creating custom applications using Visual Basic, > C++, ODBC, HTML, PHP, Cold Fusion, JavaScript and VBScript. John has a Masters of Business of Administration with a dual emphasis in finance and entrepreneurship from Pepperdine University and has taken coursework in various technologies and programming languages throughout his academic and professional career. In addition to his duties at SUMMA, John is a participating faculty member of Pepperdine University, teaching in the fully-employed Masters of Business Administration and Bachelor of Science Management programs.

John Durand’s Specialties:

Full service marketing services for small and growing businesses, consumer promotions, supply chain management, market research, sydicated data and space management.
