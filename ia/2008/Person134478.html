Mark Messina’s Summary

Mark Messina has more 15 year experience in management consulting specializing in traditional and broadband media clients. Over the last 10 plus years Mark has consulted in technology strategy and software development to such media giants as MTV Networks, Barnes and Noble (dot.com), CBS/Viacom and the NBA.

He has held positions at Booz Allen and Hamilton in the Information Technology Practice and Price Waterhouse in the Financial Services Industry Practice. While at Booz Allen, Mark was part of a team spearheading early efforts to realize digital movie distribution and projection. He also worked on a project to modernize the global royalties system for a major record label.

Mark later founded and ran M2Metrix Corporation which developed software and analytical techniques for media websites like MTV.com, VH1.com, Nickelodeon Online and iVillage.

Mark brings many years of experience in entrepreneurship and company building. Mark began his career consulting to start-ups and small business as a research analyst and consultant at the Wharton Small Business Development Center of the Snyder Entrepreneurial Center at the Wharton School of Business.

Mr. Messina holds a Bachelor and Masters of Engineering in Systems and Telecommunications from the University of Pennsylvania. While pursuing his Masters degree, Mark worked as a research assistant in Artificial Neural Networks which he later applied to modeling attendance behavior for a major theme park.
