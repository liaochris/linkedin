Stephen Grobman’s Summary

Seventeen years experience in the hedge fund industry, with broad strategy and financial product expertise. Diverse vantage points with experience ranging from entrepreneurial startups to institutional multi-billion dollar funds and also, single manager funds to multi-manager fund of funds.

Experience, while focused on risk management and quantitative research, has encompassed all facets of the hedge fund business (e.g., operations, business development, marketing, legal, compliance, recruiting).

Highly technical and quantitative skill set enables automation by leveraging technology to increase efficiency. Led multiple built vs. buy exercises. Hands-on experience with third party software installs (e.g., Imagine, LIM, Inforeach, Eze, Quickbooks, Pertrac, Backstop) and the building of proprietary technology. Demonstrated strengths include an acute strategic business perspective, the ability to successfully develop and implement complex investment procedures and processes, systems and analytics.
