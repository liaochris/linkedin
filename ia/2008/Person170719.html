Scot McKay’s Summary

Scot McKay is a dating strategist and life coach in San Antonio, TX. He is the founder of X & Y Communications, a one-stop-shop for dating resources. Sign up for the free newsletter at: http://www.deservewhatyouwant.com/.

He is the author of the books “Deserve What You Want” (http://www.dating-advise.us/), “Cook For Your Date” (http://www.romantic-dinner.com/), and "How To Manage Your Wildly Successful Dating Life" (http://www.wildsuccess.net/).

In addition, Scot has produced the world’s first audio program designed specifically to increase success with women for men who are shorter than average. (http://www.nottooshort.com/)

A related venture, DateToOrder (http://www.datetoorder.com), bridges the gap between profile-based dating sites and matchmaking services. DateToOrder helps inexpensively and dramatically cut down on unfavorable first dates.

Scot and his new wife Emily host the popular podcast series “X & Y On The Fly”, now listened to on six continents. You can subscribe for FREE at http://feeds.feedburner.com/xyonthefly. "Online Dating Profile Rating" (http://feeds.feedburner.com/onlinedating) "DatingCast" (http://feeds.feedburner.com/datingcast) and "The Chick Whisperer" are also part of the X & Y Communications Worldwide Mediacasting Network and each consistently featured in the worldwide Top 25 within their respective iTunes categories.

Scot and Emily also produce respective monthly programs entitled "Power Sessions For Men" and "Power Sessions For Women" that detail the very highest level of "game" for becoming the greatest man or woman possible. Scot's latest project is the VIRTUOSITY advanced series (http://www.deservewhatyouwant.com/virtuosity).

Scot makes it a point to be refreshingly approachable and available. He may be reached via email at scot@datetoorder.com or via voice-over-IP on Skype as “scotmckay”. Initial consultations via email or Skype are free of charge.
