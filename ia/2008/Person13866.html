Ann Evanston’s Summary

AME Institute specializes in a customized approach to training and coaching your leaders. Though dynamic, highly qualified speakers and trainers we specializ in not just training, but training with a return on investment. Getting measurable results is critically important to our success. Our clients can show a measurable difference in employee turnover, retention, productivity and customer satisfaction as a result of bringing the AME team to their organizations to develop their leaders.

Ann Evanston, CEO, has extensive experience with front line to executive level development. She has personally trained in the Leadership Pipline, and is abe to shift managers minds to create a highly productive enviroment.

Ann Evanston’s Specialties:

Leadership development, productivity and attitude training and coaching for individuals and teams. Specialize in coaching all passages in leadership to increase overall effectiveness. Staff of high qualified trainers that specialize in communications, supervision and productivity training.
