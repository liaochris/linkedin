A tul S algaonkar’s Summary

As technology delivery becomes more web-based and secure yet scale-able, it provides an appropriate solution to major problems facing the movers and shakers of the business world.

Wireless technologies current (802.11, IR, bar-codes,.) and emerging (RFID, sensor nets,..) provide some compelling scenarios for applications. And these data streams have to be managed and distilled and made meaningful (Data Warehouse, Analytics, Decision Support, Reports, Alerts) while still retaining the original flavor of the data capture.

SOA and Virtualization in particular offer game-changing approaches and despite some vendors' FUD methods, the key concepts can be easily grasped and applied by the business decision makers. The rewards include near-term cost savings as well as future-proofing of the enterprise.
