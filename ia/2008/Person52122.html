Dixie Feldman’s Summary

I've got over 15 years' experience working in TV, online and print media -- writing, editing, marketing, branding, training, and public speaking.

I'm passionate about building brands and guiding and supporting creative people to do their best work. I'm also very passionate about pumpkin pie. (Some might say *too* passionate about pumpkin pie. But they, of course, are wrong.)

I'm currently writing a book on "dames," training for a marathon, and learning the accordion.
