Partha Chakraborty, Ph.D., CFA’s Summary

I have over ten years of investments related experience. Currently I am Global Head of Investments for Flagstone Re (NYSE: FSR). Headquartered in Bermuda, FSR is a global reinsurance company with offices in Bermuda, Halifax, Zurich, London, Dubai and Hyderabad. I am responsible for leadership of all people, process, and ownership of returns for the investments portfolio of the firm. Previously, I was in charge of quantitative asset allocation for the Global Wealth and Investment Management division of Bank of America. Previously I was developing asset allocation framework for non-US retail investment clients of Citigroup. Before that I led US Financials equity research for Nomura Asset Management. I started my career in the investments field with Lehman Brothers as a Research Associate.

I have a Ph.D. in Economics from Cornell University and am a CFA Charterholder. I have Bachelors and Masters degrees in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute. While at graduate school, I co-founded Bookwormbooks.com - an internet start-up aimed at information exchange and trade of used text books at Cornell University.
