Bill Schweinitz’s Summary

My name is Bill Schweinitz and I am the Market Partner for Gecko Hospitality in New Jersey,
Delaware, Maryland, West Virginia. Gecko Hospitality is the largest management recruiting firm dedicated to the restaurant and hospitality industry.

I work with many franchise and corporate restaurants in my Territories and have some candidates who would make a good fit for the management positions you posted. Prior to recruiting, my background was operations and most recently I was a National Recruiting and Training Manager for Sportservice before 12 years of being a General Manager for Olive Garden, Macaroni Grill and Applebee’s.

While interviewing candidates for these companies I discovered that the best candidates possess four things. First they have High Energy which gives them the drive to succeed. Second they must have Intelligence to provide them the ability to learn the systems, methods and tools needed to be successful. Third, they must be Ethical, have the values and principles recognized as important by the organization, and most importantly, they must have the Passion for service and the desire to be guest driven.

We at Gecko could become an immediate asset to you by recognizing these traits before ever sending any candidates to your recruiting staff. We would not want to waste your time with weak candidates. I know first hand how precious time is to both recruiters and area managers. We are not headhunters. Managers seek us out and respond to our ads. We only specialize in the hospitality and restaurant industry and all of our placements come with a 90 day guarantee.

I would be very interested in speaking to you about presenting candidates for management positions. If we could be of service or you have any questions about our services please call me at 866-301-5702. I look forward to hearing from you
