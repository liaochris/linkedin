Douglas Sell’s Summary

At Forensic Resolutions, Inc. we provide expert services quantifying the financial aspects of matters in dispute. Whether it is calculating economic damages, analyzing and displaying underlying financial detail in a clear and concise fashion or issuing an expert report opining on our findings, we provide the utmost in quality and consistently add value to every engagement.

Douglas Sell’s Specialties:

Engagements involving Fraud Investigation, Personal Injury, Wrongful Death, Breach of Contract, 1st Party Insurance Claims, 3rd Party Insurance Claims, Subrogation, Product Recall, White Collar Defense, Workers' Compensation, among others. Services involving Expert Witness Testimony and Reports, assistance with critical financial issues during Deposition Testimony and Document Discovery, as well is identifying key financial and economic issues.
