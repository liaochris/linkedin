Hans M. Hirschi’s Summary

Well established professional with 20 years of multinational experience of training & development, communication & change management. Accustomed to work in large international projects. Multilingual and culturally well versed; behavioural scientist, adult educator & consultant. Gifted public speaker & performer. Socially talented leader & people manager.

Always looking for new challenges that allow me to grow and learn more while making a difference for you and your organization. Learn more about me at my website and welcome to read my blog!
