Marcelo Castro’s Summary

Marcelo Castro is Senior Consultant of BearingPoint. His professional experience has been developed mainly in the area of Contact Centers, more specifically in CTI technology (Computer Telephony Integration), PBX/ACD 's, IVR's, Dialers, FAX Servers, Workforce Management systems and integration of front-end systems. Currently working on the integration of mySAP CRM platform with telephony environments. He has certifications AVAYA in Multimedia Contact Centers Design & Implementation and Proactive Contact Solutions Design, in addition of having a large experience in systems of Workforce Management, particularly the ASPECT, WFM and the solution RIGHTFAX of Captaris. This experience is based in ten years serving as a consultant on technology and IT solutions for Contact Centers. During that time had the opportunity to work with a range of tools such as systems databases, servers and operating systems, programming tools, workflow systems, network architecture LAN/WAN, among others.
