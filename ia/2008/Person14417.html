Antoine Borg’s Summary

I have 14 years experience in IT in the UK and Malta, in architecting & developing information management and integration solutions in finance, telephony, marketing, manufacturing and education in the private and public sectors.

Since joining Ricston, I have worked within a dynamic team that authors, schedules and runs training for Mule (http://www.mulesource.com) around the globe. We also provide consultancy for Mule and integration projects and I have assisted our customers across Europe, Australia and USA.

I have spoken at public events about Mule and SOA:
- Enterprise Patterns in Mule: http://javasymposium.techtarget.com/index.html (Prague, June 2008)

Antoine Borg’s Specialties:

Software architecture, Pattern analysis, Software design, Lecturing, Public speaking.
