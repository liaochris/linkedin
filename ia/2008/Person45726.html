Dave Platter’s Summary

I am chief communicator for the REA Group to staff, investors and media.

I have been dedicated to effective communications for 10 years. As a principal and partner in successive New York City public relations agencies, he has placed his clients in some of the world's most influential media. His public relations successes include front cover stories in The New York Times, Time magazine, BRW and BusinessWeek; CNN, Fox News and the major US networks; broadcast and satellite radio; and highly targeted internet outlets.

I have spoken at seminars hosted at the United Nations and helped organize a group dedicated to analyzing the threat of the avian flu to the New York real estate industry that received the support of a chief United Nations public health official, who called its work 'vital'.

I am a member of the Public Relations Institute of Australia.

I have been an employee, an entrepreneur, an employer, a partner, a team member and a team leader.
