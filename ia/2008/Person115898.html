Louri Russel’s Summary

Louri Russel Boilard is the Owner of Your Distinct Career, by: Resume Brilliance LLC. Executive Resume Writer, Career Counselor and Coach.

Professional Memberships: Career Management Alliance - Career Directors International - American Couseling Association

About Resume Brilliance- We are a team of leading industry career specialists. We take our jobs very seriously, for it is the only way to ensure your success. All our services are about you and your successful career journey.

Our knowledge is extensive and unlike other companies we are Executive Career Experts. Our Career Solutions are on the cutting edge and we stay abreast of all the leading-edge career tools and assessments necessary to ensure your successes.

Setting the industry standard is our goal and through our customer centric approach and commitment to excellence we achieve this every day. Our flawless reputation for excellence and distinction quickly sets us apart from our competitors.

Your resume must clearly state your value from the start, if you are not communicating who you are, what sets you apart, and why you should be hired for the job your resume is not succeeding. Your resume is small pieces of a foundation and each section must articulate your worth.

Specializing in Executive Level Resumes, Coaching, Counseling, Career Assement & Planning as well as Interview Skills Training.
