alexandra mulcahy’s Summary

I am an upcoming Media Production graduate of the Emerson College Class of 2008. I am looking to start my career at an agency such as Arnold, Digitas, Modernista!, or a consulting company such as the Boston Consulting Group. I am focused and enthusiastic about creative advertising, and am looking to be part of a creative team or art department.

I am also a freelance photographer and digital artist, selling prints of my own work, providing travel/photojournalist, stock, commercial and editorial images for magazines, as well as real estate, event, and portrait photography. I am well versed in Adobe Photoshop image manipulation, and will provide photograph restoration services on a fee-per-image basis.

I am currently based in the greater Boston area, with flexibility to work in the San Francisco/LA area as well as countries abroad (Europe, Asia, Southeast Asia, and the South Pacific).
