Steve Garson’s Summary

I started my career in sales and marketing of CAD/CAM systems in the glory days of the VAX computers. After a career in tech related marketing, I moved over to the motion picture industry, building a stock footage library called Fabulous Footage to a point where I sold it to Getty Images. Since then, I have built and managed a number of companies in a range of industries. I am every adept at building company infrastructures that are cost effective and easily maintainable. My interest and experience in technology and telecom is very strong, which benefits my clients. My present company excels at increasing client profitability through utilizing our telecom and network expertise, along with our pricing benchmark database to assure that clients are paying what they should, and not more, for their goods and services. We also help clients improve productivity through the smart implementation of the appropriate technologies.

Our MPLS-Experts subsidiary provides WAN consulting, procurement and project management expertise to assist clients in migrating to MPLS based networks from legacy frame relay or VPN over Internet networks. We manage the entire procurement process and facilitate the implementation. Our services have helped many global companies reduce their MPLS WAN costs by 25%.

I am interested in making contact with any one with influence at companies that recognize the benefit of having an independent third party review of operations. Better Cost Control only charges a fee when costs demonstrably are reduced. The fee is then only a percentage of the savings. This is a win-win for the client, since there is no risk and fast action on projects that impact their bottom line.

MPLS-Experts provides its services at no fee, receiving compensation from whatever network carrier our clients select. With relationships with carriers all over the world, our approach is one that will never be proposed by an AT&T or Verizon.

Steve Garson’s Specialties:

Specification of requirements, management of RFQ process, selection of best suppliers, implementation locally or globally. PBX, VoIP, Videoconferencing, Frame Relay, MPLS, VPN, Local and LD, Wireless.
