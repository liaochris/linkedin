David Watson’s Summary

My story is simple yet multifaceted.

I am currently the MD of several privately owned small growth companies that provide consumer and security services. All the companies are automated so my direct input is minimal.

Solutions International Pty Ltd traded as Tangible Solutions until 1 March 2006 when the trading name and customer base was acquired by Australia Star Pty Ltd. During the three years leading up to the sale, Tangible Solutions was listed in Australia's top 20 fastest growing businesses as per the BRW magazine's Fast100 list.

I built a small callback company and grew it to become a full service telecommunications provider in Australia with a worldwide reach. Tangible Solutions provided fixed wire, mobile telephone, and Internet services (broadband and dial up) to small and medium businesses. We operated in more than 200 countries via our virtual office system.

I am still at the helm of Solutions International as we move into complimentary services that match or link with our extensive telecommunications background. We also provide outside sales of group and individual travel services through IFT.

In addition to my activities at Solutions International, I also work in executive positions with Circle Three Group Pty Ltd and Voice & Data Services Pty Ltd. Both companies are Australian with Circle 3 focusing on three essentials: Environment, Ethics and Education which we provide financial analysis/management expertise and marketing for services and products to third parties. Circle 3 also holds the license for the BioPod in Australia.
Voice & Data works with security firms in providing real time response for alarm panels, as well as providing international call & data termination at the retail level.
