Bruce Hubbert’s Summary

I am a Principal Systems Engineer in Silicon Valley. I am honored to have had the privilege of representing firms I have worked for to the media in such publications as the Washington Post, Wireless Week, Information Week, and on the History Channel’s Tactical to Practical and ABC News Business Now.

Now and then I get opportunities to address security events like SANS, ISSA, ISACA, HTCIA and MIS Training Institute where I discuss wireless security policy management and intrusion prevention.
