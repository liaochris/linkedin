Dave Cobb’s Summary

Take a background that combines writing, storytelling, and directing... add over a decade of working with high-profile entertainment brands & location-based attractions... mix in a good dollop of pop-culture & technology geekdom... and there you have it, a fifteen-year career that’s always been about dreaming up new and unique ways to entertain people.

From theme parks, rides & attractions to museums, retail & resorts, I’ve collaborated on numerous entertainment projects all over the world, constantly looking for new ways to integrate technology, interactivity, and theatricality to engage and excite an audience.

Dave Cobb’s Specialties:

Creative team leadership & artistic direction, story & screenwriting, ride & show programming, theatrical design & direction, interactive design & gameplay, user interface design, audio/video post-production, animation, graphic design, environmental & scenic development
