Peggy Etheridge, CFP®’s Summary

I am a partner in the Willow Bend Group at Smith Barney. By working as a team we are able to handle a variety of complex issues and strive to provide a higher level of service.

Our clients are each uniquely important to us. We work with those who take seriously the building, preserving, and distribution of their wealth.

Investing is a long-term process, not a series of short-term events. The Willow Bend Group works to build and maintain a long-term relationship with our clients that reaches beyond any single transaction. We recognize and address the complex issues raised by significant wealth:

*Preservation of assets

*Liquidity concerns and risk management, both for personal and for business needs

*Business services, including retirement plans, executive financial services and stock plan services

*Strategies for retirement and education funding

*Charitable and philanthropic pursuits

*Wealth transference and estate planning
