Martina Scheuring Rich’s Summary

I come from a diverse educational background. My undergrad degree is in Biology and Art (pre-veterinary). After realizing veterinary medicine wasn't for me (I'd do everything for free), I decided I wanted to get into Employee Development. I got my M Ed from Pitt in Administrative & Policy Studies focusing in Higher and Non-Tradidional Education. I will have my MS in Human Resources Management, focusing on Training and Development, in May 2008.

My clients range from small businesses to larger Fortune 500 companies. At any one time, I am working on between 5 and 20 jobs ranging in salary from $50,000 to $180,000+. Each position requires the right person-- the skill set as well as the cultural fit.

I love the challenge of recruiting for different companies on different positions. It's certainly never the same day twice!

I can be reached at mrich@basilone.net or 412-369-9501.
