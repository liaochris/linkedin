Hatef Yamini’s Summary

The stock market bubble of the 80's sparked my interest in business and led to my first job out of college as a broker for the world's first online brokerage. I spent two years in the financial markets as a licensed broker.

Later, my career gravitated toward sales and as I gained experience I worked my way up to sales management where I spent almost seven years at two top-tier retailers: The Container Store and The Apple Store. At each location I was responsible for recruiting, hiring, developing and managing up to 65 employees at a time. At the peak of my sales management career, I was on the team at the ninth highest volume Apple Store, with annual sales in excess of $65 million.

Today, I'm re-branding myself as a marketing professional with a background in management, sales and technical marketing with a focus on online marketing and corporate blogging. I have additional talent and experience in the areas of search engine optimization, social-web technology, strategic planning, and staff development.

Since my interest in marketing is at the strategic level, my commitment to management remains strong with an emphasis on changing the way businesses are managed--from environmental standards and practices to the way people are valued, treated and compensated.

I believe business can be a catalyst for positive social change and I intend to be at the forefront of the movement toward more conscious business practices--both in my own practice of business and through my support of business networks such as Net Impact and NetSquared.

Hatef Yamini’s Specialties:

Online and email marketing, search engine optimization, corporate blogging, management of teams, strategic planning, marketing management and staff development.
