Andrew Allen’s Summary

Andrew Allen is the Managing Director of Accredit Financial Services, a multi disciplined, award wining company based in Stockport. The company was set up by Andrew and his two brothers because of their perceived gap in the Financial Services market for a company that has close client relationships akin to the self employed adviser, backed up by the systems and technology of a large organisation. Being a family business there is a close bond between staff and clients guaranteed by the investment in enterprise level technology.

Accredit works with private individuals and businesses to raise finance, ensure financial protection and plan for the future.
