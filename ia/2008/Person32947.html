Cécile Galoselva’s Summary

With an MBA (Master of Business Administration) from the University of Oxford's Said Business School, I gained thirteen years of supervisory management and business experience in France, Germany, Hungary, the U.K. and the U.S.A., mainly for small to medium size enterprises.
I am currently an executive director of one of U.K.'s leading social enterprise, delivering financial success as well as environmental progress and social benefits. Alongside this, I am looking for a non executive director position in a non competing organization.
I'm keen to share with like-minded entrepreneurs.
