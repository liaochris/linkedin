Jeffrey Levitt CPA’s Summary

Jeffrey Levitt, CPA is the Chief Financial Officer for Statistics Collaborative, a DC based consulting firm that specializes in the design and analysis of biomedical research. Prior, he served as the Chief Financial Officer and Controller for various start up and early stage companies including Codeon Corporation, Edgemark Systems, Pathnet and Fore Systems Federal. In these roles, he established the finance and accounting functions and was the financial lead in raising private equity and public debt. Jeff began his career as an audit senior at Coopers and Lybrand. Jeff has an M.S. in Taxation from the University of Baltimore and a B.S. from the University of Maryland. Jeff currently serves as Chairman of the Howard County Department of Social Services Advisory Board. He is also a graduate of Leadership Howard County and currently resides in Columbia, Maryland.
