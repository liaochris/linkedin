Hello

My name is Matthew Solomon and I would like to take the opportunity to introduce myself and apply for your Capital Markets Program.

Currently I am a second year MBA student at Boston University concentrating on Finance with a focus in Capital Markets. Their CFA Institute recognized program, which contains a specialized curriculum in Capital Markets, allows me to leverage my prior work experiences and my undergraduate education where I had a dual concentration in Finance and Accounting.

Prior to returning for my MBA I had the opportunity to work on both Buy and Sell side trading desks gaining expertise in products including:
• Domestic Equities and Options
• Derivative and Structured Products (ABS, MBS, CMO)
• Corporate and High Yield Bonds
• Emerging Markets Bonds
• Government, Agency and Municipal Bonds

During this pervious summer, I had the opportunity to intern on State Street Global Advisor’s Fixed Income trading desk. The volatile market conditions allowed me to gain valuable insight into portfolio management, trading strategy and asset valuation. I was also featured on CNBC’s Fast Money where I gave long/short investment recommendations based on a possible market situation.

I thrive in fast paced, results driven environment where my keen communication, analytical and valuation skills allow me to excel. I have been constantly able to prioritize assignments in order to not only meet or exceed current deadlines but also to allow my responsibilities and net benefit to the firm to expand.

I look forward to speaking with you soon to discuss how my passion and skills can benefit your company.
