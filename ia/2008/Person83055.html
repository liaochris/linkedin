Jim LaBelle’s Summary

I have been involved in the recruiting industry for more than 20 years in roles spanning academic/student recruiting, contract engineering/high end staff augmentation, prestigeous global consulting firms to large Fortune 100 companies as manager, team lead and as a senior individual contributor. I've worked in both permanent or consultant roles throughout my corporate experience which has been technology-centric, but do have a good mix of non-techical corporate and executive experience as well.

Specific to technical recruiting, I have a very strong balance of large corporate IT (application and infrastructure services), software, hardware and systems engineering skill sets and typically peform in full recruiting life-cycle mode hiring both consultants and full time permanent employees within centralized or decentralized HR and recruiting organizations. Companies where I have worked include Lucent Technologies, Bell Labs, AFLAC, Blue Cross Blue Shield of South Carolina, Fidelity Investments and currently Netapp.

With Netapp, I'm managing the staffing needs for our global product engineering organization and multiple BU's in a full life cycle senior recruiter capacity based on-site at our fast growing location in the research, triangle park (RTP) area outside of Raleigh, NC.
