Rina Alluri’s Summary

Educated in political science and development studies with experience conducting research, writing project proposals and implementing campaigns and events related to governance, human rights, distance education and the use of ICTs for awareness-building. Particularly interested in issues related to the role of civil society and non-governmental actors in providing tools, increasing knowledge and empowerment through participatory methods. Would be interested in working in an academic or private organizational setting which focuses on work in developing countries where I would be involved in research as well as the implementation of projects.

Rina Alluri’s Specialties:

Strong communication and editing skills, trained in logical framework analysis, excellent research skills, intercultural relations and media relations training, proficient in MS Office 2003 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook and Explorer), web authoring, IT-based publishing systems, database updates, type 65 wpm.
