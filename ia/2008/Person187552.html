Theo van Westrienen’s Summary

Started out over 20 years ago as a system analyst. After working with various types of database management systems (hierarchal, network and relational) on various platforms focussing since 1994 on relational databases in a multi-tier environment. As of 2000 involved in data and application integration architectures, currently in a SOA environment.

President and founding member of the BeNeLux Modeling User Group (formerly: eBUG).

Theo van Westrienen’s Specialties:

Data modelling, database design and maintenance, data and application integration.
DB2, AllFusion ERwin Data Modeler, Prince2, MS SQL Server, MQ, VB, XML.
