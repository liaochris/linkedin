Dickinson Bransford’s Summary

Dick Bransford brings diversified, senior level manufacturing and services industry operational experience to his intermediary practice. He has worked in consulting, consumer packaged goods, financial services, information technology, and advertising agency account management.

He has over 20 years of experience in product marketing, strategy and operations, both inside organizations and as an outside advisor. He has been both a Vice President of Corporate Strategy and a Vice President of Marketing with a $2 billion company, and a product manager with P&L accountability for businesses generating $65 million in annual revenue.

He holds the Certified Business Intermediary (CBI) credential awarded by the International Business Brokers Association and is a Chartered Financial Analyst Candidate.

Dickinson Bransford’s Specialties:

Privately held companies whose needs are not adequately met by other business brokers or investment banks. Typically, though not exclusively, they will earn revenue in a range from $2 1/2 million up to $20 million, and will show favorable earnings history. In select circumstances, early stage companies may fit, as would buy-side assignments.
