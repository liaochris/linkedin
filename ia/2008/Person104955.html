Kenneth Price’s Summary

Seasoned corporate manager, small business principal, and consultant, with 20+ years of results. Demonstrated ability to fuse management, analysis, and technology in diverse industries - pharmaceuticals R&D, telecommunications, manufacturing and distribution, education, criminal justice, human services, health care, tourism, insurance, community development, and financial/asset management. Cross-Functional project responsibilities, participation on multifunctional teams, and leadership on projects involving various business functions. Innovation oriented, consistently seeking to exceed expectations. Accomplishments in a range of market situations, including:

• Launch of federal financial institution e.g., business plan, regulatory liaison, raising $1.2MM, site preparation.
• Creating an Efficient Consumer Response methodology and business intelligence software to reduce supply chain cost of 25 million annual product cases shipped to retailers with networks as large at 1000+ stores.
• Process reengineering and development of healthcare R&D technology to support 300+ scientists and increase efficiency of new drug introduction.
• Completion of diverse analysis projects, including: program analysis of Ohio’s Workforce Investment Act [WIA] implementation; industry analysis of $7 billion Greater Cincinnati regional tourism; business analysis of City of Cincinnati’s 32 departments; and feasibility analysis for new medical supplies distributor.
• Completion of planning efforts that received capital, including one that secured $510,000 from the US Department of Commerce as one of 25 winning plans in a national pool of 741 submissions.
• Securing capital and leading a 19-firm national team’s bid on an $800 million mixed-use waterfront project.
• Leading a $40 billion manufacturer’s first introduction of EDI with warehouse club retailer channel.
• Designing/executing demand forecasting models for Fortune 100 “ultra” detergents introduction.
