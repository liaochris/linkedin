    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Writing and Editing industry)

    March 2008 — Present (8 months)

    Barncat Publishing Inc. is devoted to helping writers finish their books and get published. Through online writing workshops, individual coaching and book doctoring, and specialized course books, Barncat brings writers to the next level, helps them get over writer's block, and offers detailed, personalized feedback that is unique in the field of writing instruction. As profiled in Crain's New York, Barncat is an entrepreneurial startup that translates skills from the field of journalism and arts criticism to meet the needs of the New Media of the Internet.
