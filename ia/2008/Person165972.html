Results oriented executive with an industrial engineer background and proven record of leadership in manufacturing, materials, project management, turnarounds and business systems re-engineering demonstrated through increasing roles of responsibility and successful project accomplishments by creating empowered teams, motivating change, improving business systems, and implementing cost effective solutions for all areas of the supply chain.

Russell Brown’s Specialties:

My speciality is supply chain optimization. World class supply chains require world class stragetic planning, master scheduling. production planning, inventory managment and procurement. I have a proven records of eliminating supply chain problems such as backorders, long leadtimes and high inventories while creating high service levels, high inventory turns, reduced manufacturing costs and higher profits. High customer satisfaction can be acheived without increased operational costs.
