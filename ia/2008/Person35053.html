    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    As a Utility Solutions Account Manager, managed the majority of water and wastewater accounts within the ESRI southeast region and also provided internal consulting support to other ESRI departments, divisions, regional offices, and international distributors. Routinely managed GIS implementation projects that include user needs assessments, physical database designs, applications development, database automation, and systems integration, as well as provides the primary marketing and sales support for the region. Particular strengths include helping water and wastewater clients to successfully integrate GIS with their engineering/drafting, customer service, billing, work management, hydraulic (hydrologic) modeling, water quality, SCADA, and other systems.

  • GIS Manager/Database Administrator

    Parsons Technica de Venezuela, C.A.

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    March 1998 — August 1999 (1 year 6 months)

    Managed a Geographic Information System (GIS) analysis and map production team for the Desarrollo Armonico de Oriente (DAO) project, under contract to Petroleos de Venezuela, S.A. (PDVSA), Venezuela’s national oil company. The planning project was responsible for analysis of impacts to the eastern region of Venezuela, with respect to PDVSA’s $40 billion ten-year investment strategy. Professional disciplines for which the team provided support include Land Use, Infrastructure, Socioeconomic, Environment, and Organizational/Institutional. All GIS products and analysis were completed using ArcInfo, ArcView, and ERDAS Imagine. Database Administration tasks include designing and delivering an Oracle Database while using CASE tools such as ERWin. All data and database designs were created to comply with PDVSA standards. The majority of business transactions were performed in the Spanish language.

Also responsible for Oracle database administration for all GIS projects within the Atlanta office.
