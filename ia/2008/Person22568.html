Brett Finkelstein’s Summary

I have over eight years of E-Business Development and Marketing experience including social networking, online lead generation, comparison shopping, dating/personals and apparel.

Currently, I manage global advertising sales for hi5, the third largest social networking site in the World. As a leading site in markets covering Latin America, Europe, and Asia, I work with a range of premier global media companies to represent the hi5 web property. In conjunction with hi5 partners, I have helped to tailor unique advertising offerings and packages to the needs of global marketers.

Prior to hi5, I have been involved in ventures spanning online advertising and e-commerce. At NexTag.com, I was responsible for helping to develop the mortgage services channel. In 2000, I founded 4-D Networks and hold US Patent 6,901,379 - Online Shopping with Virtual Modeling and Peer Review.

I earned my MBA degree from Temple University (Fox School of Business) and a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting from the University of Maryland Smith School of Business.
