Roland Regan’s Summary

Passionate advocate for children's health care issues. Co-founder of the National Childhood Obesity Foundation, Inc. Devised internationally recognized Advisory Board, comprehensive strategic plan, innovative programming, and created new brands and intellectual property trademarks for Foundation development. My professional work experience with both Harbridge House and ProMonde/NAGW, Inc. has included senior level management consulting and training, teaching graduate business related courses in consumer product marketing, business and intellectual property law, brand development and management, and entreprenuership. Clients included: GE Medical, GE Aircraft Engines, UTC-Automotive, UTC-Pratt & Whitney, Raytheon, Corning, Bard, Inc., Northwestern U. Medical School, Teradyne, Kraft Foods, and several European municipalities.

Roland Regan’s Specialties:

Competitive analysis, strategic planning, Non-profit development, intellectual property law, health care code compliance, contracts and purchasing systems analysis, brand development and protection, government contracting compliance, and international marketing.
