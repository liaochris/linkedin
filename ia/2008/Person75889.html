Dennis "Dutch" Heetbrink’s Summary

Dennis "Dutch" Heetbrink was born and grew up in The Netherlands (aka Holland). He moved to America in 1982. People from Holland are called "Dutch", and many people started calling him that.

Experience and Skills
In real estate since 2004, Dutch was awared the prestigious "Big Dog" award for customer satisfaction and sales excellence in 2005, 2006 and again in 2007. Over 25 years in the corporate world as a professional negotiator. Dutch is a State certified Real Estate Instructor.

Hard Work
Dutch credits his education and upbringing in The Netherland as well as his service in the Dutch military for instilling in him the values of perseverance, integrity and hard work. As a full-time real estate agent, Dutch has dedicated himself to the highest standards of client service .

Hobbies
In his spare time (ha!) Dutch is an actor. He and his family actively volunteer at "The Driftwood Players", an Edmonds-based community theater company.

Dutch is also the Executive Producer of "Play With Your Food", an outbound Dinner Theater group. Keep your eye open for local performances. If you'd like more information on bookings or performances, give Dutch a call at 206-234-5660.

About the dutch people
The Dutch hail from the country of The Netherlands (aka "Holland"). Dutch also refers to the language we speak. Holland is probably best known for its wooden shoes, canals, tulips, cheese (Gouda and Edam), and last but not least windmills.

The Dutch are known for being open-minded, non-judgemental and frugal. Their sense or fairness and correct behaviour is well known, particularly in business.
