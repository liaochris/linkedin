Adrian Kurup’s Summary

Surian provides design solutions in the all of the building services like Civil/Structural engineering, Architecture design and detailing HVAC, Electrical and Plumbing design and detailing

Surian is focused on adding high value to its clients by providing cost effective and time bound solutions by teaming its resources and leveraging the availability of highly skilled, trained architects and engineers with its partners and generate significant economies of scale, over a short period of time. A seamless integration is initiated with our clients where Surian is seen to be a part of the team than a consultant and at the same time ensuring that the customer has complete ownership of the solution from the very start.

Our innovative solutions for architects and engineers have helped us to establish our presence in this niche market. We have been a part of the team for many customers across the globe and have tested and proven systems and processes in place. Our customers enjoy tangible and intangible benefits of these collaborative efforts by way of reduction in engineering cost, significant decrease in cycle time, effortless scalability and ability to handle sudden surge or drop in design or detailing activities.

Adrian Kurup’s Specialties:

Surian Engineering is a full service civil engineering and architectural design house which has provided its services to the public, private and commercial sectors. Our highly trained engineers and architect are experienced in a wide variety of projects utilizing the latest CAD systems and design software. While Surian was established in 2002, our team represents many years of building experience.
