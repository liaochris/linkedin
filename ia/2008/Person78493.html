wen shiau’s Summary

Mr. Shiau, in 2007, founded Tai Tam Capital Limited, an Asian Alternative Asset Mgmgt Firm. We currently have 2 fund strategies: Asia-Pacific Macro Hedge Fund and the Asian Special Situation fund which invests across the full capital structure but predominantly in mezzanine debt of asia ex-japan mid-market companies, seeking expansion capital.

Mr. Shiau was recruited in 2006 to help rebuild WestLB's capital markets in Asia and hired as the regional co-head of capital markets and Executive and Investment Committee member for asia pacific, including japan. He has more than 20 years of experience in sales, asian corporate coverage, structuring, programming and trading in ny, hk and tokyo. Utilizing Mr. Shiau's derivative background and senior contacts in asia pacific, he has built successful businesses by integrating the origination efforts with principal investing and capital markets. Mr. Shiau began his career as a mortgage backed securities trader for First Boston Corporatioin in NY in 1986, moved to Hong Kong with Lehman Brothers in 1992 and later with CSFB for almost 10 years.

wen shiau’s Specialties:

Senior asian contacts and structured solutions skill set in both asset and liability management across asian capital mkts. As a senior executive in asia, I have lived in hong kong and tokyo and travelled extensively in the region since 1992. Wen began his career on Wall Street as a programmer and structurer on the MBS trading desk for First Boston and Lehman, leveraging proprietary research to create a mortgage credit default spread model.
