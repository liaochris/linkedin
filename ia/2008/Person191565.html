Tony Camero’s Summary

Tony Camero brings over a decade of vision and experience in internet marketing, online payment processing, and IP television technology start-ups. Since the very earliest stages of commercial development of the Internet, Camero has been involved in starting companies and pioneering services that leverage technology to bring commercial and social solutions to the marketplace.

Co-Founder and Board Member of Optical Entertainment Network, Inc
Mr. Camero incorporated Optical Entertainment Network (OEN) in August of 2003 to deliver entertainment programming and communications services in a new way and specifically over the new generation of communications infrastructure, fiber-to-the-home.

Founder and President of EarthChannels International, Inc.
Mr. Camero founded EarthChannels to deploy Enterprise Information Portal (EIP) tools and technologies toward the benefits of local real-world communities in what the company called Intelligent Community Networks (ICN).

Automated Transaction Services, Inc.
Camero contributed to the early evolution of Automated Transaction Services (ATS) pioneered electronic commerce as the first provider of online merchant check and credit card processing services in 1994.

Tony Camero’s Specialties:

Partnership exploration and negotiations, strategic planning, sales, business development.

Tony Camero’s Experience

    iAdVentures, LLC

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    January 2008 — Present (10 months)

    iAdVentures is offering interactive media and advertising solutions that enable consumers, advertisers and publishers to more effectively engage in rich, interactive exchanges that are relevant, engaging, and seamlessly tied across multiple platforms and with the additional advantage of local specificity and target-ability.

    iAdVentures solutions enable communities to develop stronger, more sustainable economic relationships among consumers, merchants, groups and organizations, through partnership with local media outlets. iAdVentures helps communities by enabling rapid deployment of interactive video portals, social networking tools, and targeted advertising systems that create powerful channels for social, civic and commercial interactions. Our solutions are blurring the lines between local culture, television entertainment, and electronic commerce.

  • Founding Member

    P3 Networks, LLC

    (Telecommunications industry)

    January 2008 — Present (10 months)

    P3 Networks delivers advanced telecommunications networks and services for rural markets by deploying people, technology, relationships, and skills in the planning and execution for financing, building, marketing and operations of an open access fiber network cooperatives. P3 specializes in the formation and management of Public-Private Partnerships (P3) that leverage community capital to enable advanced networks and services designed for the new era of open access networks.

    P3 Networks will develop and execute custom business models and project plans for rural communities covering transport options, network architecture, construction and project management, operations management, finance management, network services availability and options, sales and marketing, and cooperative structure governance options.
