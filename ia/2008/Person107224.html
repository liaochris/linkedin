Kirk Richardson’s Summary

Marketing Manager at specialty metals (titanium etc.) producer ATI Wah Chang, an Allegheny Technologies Company (ATI). Business Week magazine rated ATI No. 3 Best Performer in 2008. On the BW scoreboard, ATI was ranked ahead of such notables as Amazon, Apple, Coca Cola, Exxon Mobil, Google, Microsoft, Starbucks, and Verizon Communications. Key member of team promoting ATI products to aerospace, chemical processing/oil & gas, consumer, energy, medical, and other markets.

Outside of the corporation, has written for Business Viewpoint, Main Ingredient (resort, restaurant, wine, craft beer and other feature stories), and Stainless Steel World News (Industry Insights column at www.kci-world.com) as well as other magazines. Contributor of more than 1000 articles to a wide range of magazines, newspapers, web sites, and other media.

Marketing consultant to the Materials Technology Institute (MTI), an organization made up of the world's leading petrochemical and chemical companies (www.mti-global.org). Specializes in advertising, branding, brochure and newsletter publishing, e-promotions, market research and competitive intelligence, as well as PR.

Meeting/conference producer. From 1997 to present, planned and managed the international Corrosion Solutions Conference (corrosionconference.com), which draws participants from around the world (21 countries represented in 2007). Has also independentally developed and launched forums focused on Competitive Intelligence (World Trade Center, Portland, OR) and Lean Manufacturing (OSU, Corvallis, OR).

Long-time member of Northwest Art & Air Festival Executive Board. Named Oregon's Volunteer of the Year in 2003 by the Festivals and Events Association (and top 16 nominee worldwide). The NWAAF draws many hot air balloonists and thousands of visitors to the Willamette Valley every August (www.nwartandair.org).

Enjoys meeting people on LinkedIn and exploring new opportunities dovetailing with his strengths and experience.
