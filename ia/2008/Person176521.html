Simon Robinson’s Summary

Simon is a journalist and media innovator of 20 years experience in the fields of polymers and chemicals across a range of media. He started professionally blogging the business of chemicals in 2005 and podcasting on the subject later in the same year. Simon presents and produces much of ICIS web-TV out put. He is also responsible for the soon to be launched ICIS Connect chemicals community. Simon also currently blogs about biofuels.
Simon Graduated Manchester Metropolitan University with a BSc (Hons) Polymer Science and Technology in1987. During his degree he worked for a year at BIP, Oldbury, Birmingham. After graduation Simon, started working for Crain Communications' European Rubber Journal and Urethanes Technology magazines. In 1989 he moved to EMAP's Plastics and Rubber Weekly as Materials Editor, a post he held until moving to European Plastics News and Asian Plastics News. Simon joined Reed Business Information in 1998 as News Editor on European Chemical News and held a variety of positions until leaving in 2001 to become Editor of Chemistry & Industry, published by Society of Chemical Industry. Simon rejoined Reed Business Information as Deputy Editor on European Chemical News in 2004 and became ICIS' online editor in 2007.

Simon Robinson’s Specialties:

Innovating in new media and working practices in business journalism. Presentation skills, TV and podcast experience. Journalism, analytical skills, innovative thinking, project delivery.
