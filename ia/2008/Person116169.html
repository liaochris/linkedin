Luar Buso’s Summary

I, Luar Buso, currently hold the position as Vice President for Getupdated Eastpoint the largest and fastest growing Search Engine Marketing/ Internet Marketing company in Scandinavia in charge of well over 120 employees in Sweden,London, Paris, USA, and Norway making us one of the largest SEM in Europe. Getupdated.com is daughter company to Eastpoint.se (traded in OMX,First North Stock Exchange- Sweden).

Most people in Scandinavia recognize us as the leading providers of relevant traffic from Search Engines in Sweden. Our divisions main focus is end-to-end Internet marketing.

The vision for our company is working as a corporate matchmaking service. We have been interacting closely with a select group of vendors for the past few years who really view our company as an Internet outsourcing sales agency. What I mean by that is: when a company like yours wants to create a marketing brochure, you’ll go to your ad agency, if you want to issue a press release you’ll go to your PR Firm, when you want to have people interested in buying your products and services through the Internet and create sales, you come to us.

We are dedicated in establishing a long term relationship where you refer to us as your outsource sales agency by allowing us to help you supplement your existing sales efforts through the Internet and over the course of the next few years help you double the size of your company each and every year.

Internet Marketing is known as the most cost effective means to advertise. Companies ignoring this known fact are increasingly loosing market share at a rapid pace.

For detailed information on how we can increase your business as well as market your products with the most cost efficient strategies , please contact us at + 46 7344 05360.
