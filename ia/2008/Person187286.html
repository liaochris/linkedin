Scott Davis’s Summary

Servicing both the San Francisco/Bay Area and Los Angeles (catering only) areas since 2005, we at The Culinary Dude take the "Tell us what you crave and we can make it" approach to all of our culinary services. The main dude, Scott Davis, graduate of the California Culinary Academy Le Cordon Bleu Program, offers clients culinary skills and services that are limitless; focusing on the client's wants, needs and budget. His past employment includes head chef at Los Angeles's Baby Blues BBQ, San Francisco's Olive Bar and Restaurant and cooking instructor at Whole Foods Market in Los Altos, California for both adults and children.

If you want food for any occasion, we can make it! By putting the "personal" in personal chef, The Culinary Dude's focus is on the client's wants and needs including both a dietary and budgetary basis. Although we offer set menus and food items, we would really prefer to get input from you, the client, to truly "personalize" your eating experience. We offer both organic and regular supermarket food choices for all food preparation, it's your choice.

Utilizing degrees in Early Childhood Education and Culinary Arts, The Culinary Dude provides a hands-on, developmental approach to teaching and education in the kitchen. Our is on the process rather than the product.

Being environmentally conscious, all transportation aspects of The Culinary Dude services, including shopping and meal delivery, are done with a Hybrid Automobile. All shopping is done using recycled produce and shopping bags as well as utilizing all compostable byproducts upon the client's request. Our food packaging includes re-useable and sometimes biodegradable containers. To help save trees and paper, all menus, confirmations and billing are done via e-mail. We also offer Green Friendly, compostable utensils, cups and plates for any event.
