Stuart Turnbull’s Summary

Stuart Turnbull is a transformation specialist consulting to clients which are trying to change the way they work. In terms of scope he has successfully managed both large complex reorganisation projects over a period of years and smaller scale team development and coaching assignments.
He specialises in working with international organisations, in both the Private and International Development sectors, helping people make sense of the consequences of changes they wish to make.
This often means he facilitates dialogue, in circumstances where communication has become difficult or ineffective, in order to deepen mutual understanding and develop healthy, functional working relationships. At a personal level, his core skill is listening, calmly and carefully, in order to work with the full and whole person.

Stuart Turnbull’s Specialties:

Strategy Consulting focussing on Corporate Social Responsibility, Sustainability, Corporate Governance, Brand Management, Brand Reinvention, Marketing, Sales, Customer Service and Purchasing.
Consulting to working processes such as Business Process Re-engineering, and Virtual Working.
Consulting to Relationships through Dialogue, Facilitation, Large Group Process, Consulting Skills, Action Learning and Leadership Development
