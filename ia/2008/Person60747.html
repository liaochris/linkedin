Erin Cushing’s Summary

I am interested in conservation medicine, biodiversity and health, environmental management, ecosystem services, landscape epidemiology and infectious disease ecology. I am particularly fascinated with the relationships between emerging and re-emerging vector-borne and zoonotic diseases and the natural environment, as well as associated implications for sustainable development projects.

Erin Cushing’s Specialties:

Epidemiology, ecology, GIS, environmental management, sustainable development, field research, teaching and curriculum development, systems analysis
