Tom Churchill’s Experience

  • Secretary and Board Member

    Greater Charleston Call Center Alliance

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    January 2008 — Present (10 months)

    The Greater Charleston Call Center Alliance is a member organization whose mission is to advance the call center industry in the greater Charleston, SC area. This is accomplished by providing opportunities for members to network, share best practices and attend learning events, and creating opportunities to show the positive impact the call center industry has on the local community, culture, and economy

  • Blog Co-Owner

    Touch the Elbow - Blogging the Civil War

    (Internet industry)

    June 2006 — Present (2 years 5 months)

    After researching the Massachusetts Civil War unit, 18th Massachusetts Volunteer Infantry and running a website devoted to the history of the unit, along with co-authoring the book, “The Civil War Research Guide”, Stackpole Books, for 10 years, I started ” Touch the Elbow – Blogging the Civil War”.

    Along with two fellow researchers, we have spent the last year commenting on Civil War current events, Books and publications, researching the war, the 18th Massachusetts and anything else that comes interests us.

    It has been recognized by “The North and South Magazine” as one of the Best Civil War Blog sites and continues to be a leader in its field.
