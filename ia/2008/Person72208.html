Greg Cannon’s Summary

In the past 14 years I have called myself a Web Entrepreneur, Internet Strategist, Company Director, eBusiness Consultant, Project Manager, Event Planner, Researcher and a salaried employee. Maybe it's time to settle on one title.

The role that best defines me is that of Web Project Director or Project Manager. Working in this capacity I have overseen the successful deployment of 500+ websites and eBusiness systems that have changed the way companies do business, government agencies communicate and communities interact.

In March 2007 I left my last salaried job, as Head of the Web & Interactive Services team at Peter Gill & Associates (one of the largest media agencies in Wales), to start my 4th Internet company; this time to supply web based business and social networking services to cities and communities.

In October 2007 we launched WalesCymru.com, our prototype site. We plan to refine the templates and business model in Q1 2008, add a few more (social) networking applications and a marketplace, then replicate other city and/or community sites in rapid succession as either a full managed services or franchises.

If you're interested in tracking our progress, have a service you can offer or requirement I may be able to help with, please drop me an email. I'd love to hear from you.
