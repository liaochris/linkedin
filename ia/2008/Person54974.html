Daniel Rossi’s Summary

Principal engineer at SimCon Solutions, Inc. with 13 years of experience in industrial automation and controls. Extensive experience with Programmable Logic Controllers, Human Machine Interface and machine vision system programming. In addition, experience with Site Acceptance Testing (Commissioning) in the pharmacetucial industry. Primary industries are consumer goods, cosmetics, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals and power.
