Peter Moore’s Summary

20 years of experience in Information Systems and Technology, primarily in the Manufacturing and Consumer Goods Industries.

Applying technology to solve real world problems has always been my passion, followed closely behind by my talent for troubleshooting and solving technical issues.

I have a strong technical background, including computer programming, software development, systems management and day-to-day end user application support. This, combined with an MBA and 10 years of management level experience enables me to be an excellent liaison between I.T. and all other functional areas of the business.

For the majority of my professional career, I have generally been the "go-to" person for most technology-related matters.

My strengths and abilities really shine in a small to mid-sized company where I can apply both my management and hands-on technical skills on a day-to-day basis.

My goal is to continue having a "real" impact on the business, where my actions directly affect fellow associates along with improving the bottom line through efficiency improvements and effective application of technology.

Peter Moore’s Specialties:

Strengths include technical troubleshooting and problem solving, ERP (Integrated Manufacturing) Software implementation and Support, MANMAN/X, MK, EDI, Gentran, Microsoft Visual Basic programming, general Microsoft platform desktop and server support, telecommunications (voice/data) infrastructure, VOIP, Cisco Call Manager.
