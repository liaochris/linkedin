Mark Barnett’s Summary

Senior VP, Seasoned Business Operations Leader and Business Process Management (BPM) Professional with multi-industry experience including financial services, mortgage, health insurance, telecommunications, high-tech manufacturing, multi-level marketing and information technology. Certified Six-Sigma Green Belt, MBA, with engineering Ph.D. Outcome-focused, results-oriented, and an excellent facilitator capable of "herding cats."

Passionate and pragmatic, with exceptional people skills. Has developed and deployed a powerful yet simple interpretation of BPM principles and tools; used to drive a successful, lightning-quick re-engineering of the core business model of the sub-prime division of the nation's largest mortgage lender during historically challenging, rapidly-changing industry conditions. As Solution Owner, led the implementation in both front- and back-office operations; historically strong levels of performance are being achieved as a result.
