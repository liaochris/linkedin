Jonathan Stearns’s Summary

My clients are serious investors. They aren't interested in gambling with their money, and would rather interact with an actual person instead of a call center. My unwavering principles, investment philosophy, and client relationships have helped build my business.

For decades, my firm has believed in the same basic strategy of investing in quality investments that have proven themselves over time. We are focused on doing what is best for our clients. That's why we're dedicated to serving the needs of the individual investor and why we do so on a one-to-one basis from neighborhood offices.

1) If you're retired or highly focused on securing your retirement and achieving your dreams

2) If you'd rather travel, spend time with family and friends, and enjoy retirement instead of worrying about financial issues

3) If you dream of working with a financial advisor who explains complicated matters in an easy to understand fashion, and is upfront about how they get paid

4) If you are interested in working with someone who knows you

Then please call my office to schedule your free one-hour consultation. The only way you can determine if I am the right advisor for you and your family, is to meet me in person, and allow me to educate you as to how i run my practice and add value to my clients lives.

Thank you for considering my services.
