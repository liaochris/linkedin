Diane Abramson’s Summary

"The Ultimate Rainmaker" for Executive Search -
As a Partner with Rainmaker Consultants, Diane maintains a premier reputation for her expertise in sourcing and recruiting "world class" human capital and talent encompassing Vice President through "C" Level roles, leading and supporting Marketing, Operations, Risk Management, Strategy and Business Development functions. Diane's areas of focus include the Financial Services, Consumer Products and Media/Entertainment sectors and her expertise spans domestic and international territories. One of Diane's key principles is establishing and nurturing long-standing partnerships built upon mutual trust, respect, integrity and loyalty.
Rainmaker Consultants is a retained Executive Search firm whose expertise recruiting leadership talent spans well over two decades. Clients include a select group of Fortune 100 Companies and esteemed private equity firms.
