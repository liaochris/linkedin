David Castle’s Summary

My current position is the Aviation Mission Planning and Mission Support Manager for the Aircraft Carrier Alliance. Responsible for the provision of detailed expertise in all aspects associated with aircrew mission planning, briefing and post-flight debriefing for JCA, Harrier, Merlin, MASC and Sea King towards Main Gate success for the UK’s CVF. This expertise has also included being the lead for the physical design, security and processes associated with CVF mission planning, briefing and de-briefing compartments, and SME support to Mission Systems (BAE Insyte) for C4ISTAR applications, and NATO and UK C3I applications. Former RAF wing commander with operational experience in-theatre on 2 aircraft types. Former Deputy Director of the Joint Maritime Operational Training Staff at Northwood with experience in C4I systems and operational training. As an ex member of the Air Warfare Centre, responsible for operational training, fast-jet tactical air doctrine, and the planning and conduct of precision air-to-ground weapons and sensor trials. As a former OC Operations Wing at RAF St Mawgan, responsible for airfield management, RAF fast-jet operational Collective Training, the RAF School of Combat Survival and Rescue, and the Nimrod Forward Operating Base. Looking for career opportunities in the Defence Industry as an SME, consultant or a permanent position.

David Castle’s Specialties:

C4ISTAR, C2, warfighting, mission planning and mission support, JCA/JSF/CVF CONOPS experience, maritime-air experience, Test and Evaluation, weapons/sensor trials and evaluation, operational training, flying training.
