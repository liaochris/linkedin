Christopher Whittington’s Summary

Chris brings an international perspective and a successful track record of IT services definition and delivery. He has designed and delivered several strategic multi-year IT programs that have achieved impressive ROI and sustained competitive advantages and capabilities. He is adept at building strong and productive business relationships with colleagues, partners, suppliers and customers. Chris has worked with some of the most respected companies and included roles in Toronto, Montreal, New York City, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, France, Germany and Asia.

Chris has an MBA in Strategic Management as well as specialized post-graduate training in management accounting, and systems development. He works effectively with senior IT leadership teams to uncover core business drivers and highlight IT opportunities.

Chris has developed several IT product and service delivery frameworks to reliably identify, implement, monitor and grow sustainable IT advantages by leveraging industry best practices such as CMM, ITIL and others. He is the co-author of several IT management optimization methodologies, including Service Surround, Application Portfolio Analysis and Outsourcing methodologies. He also worked closely with Microsoft in defining its Infrastructure Optimization model.
