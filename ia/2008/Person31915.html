Cathy Bryant’s Summary

Project manager with the EDS US Application Delivery, .Net Capability, where she provides project management, integration and implementation activities for projects to help companies strengthen their competitive position, increase market share, improve customer satisfaction, and streamline processes. Ms. Bryant brings 27 years of collective operational and consulting experience in project management, SAP functional support, computer programming, business analysis and computer operations.

Ms. Bryant has managed numerous projects using CIE QMS/SLC3/GSMS/PM2 methodologies. She is currently managing several MYP initiatives for Service Delivery Automation. Among these are a project to provide Enterprise Monitoring and Instrumentation for EDS’ Virtual Control Center (VCC), creation of a Disaster Recovery site for VCC in Auburn Hills SMC, deployment of Passive Application Monitoring tools, NetQos SuperAgent and ReporterAnalyzer for EoE, deployment of mainframe monitoring tool, Sysview, Unicenter Workload Control Center for managing a batch scheduling environment and managing the engineering of Unicenter NSM R11.

Past automotive related projects include managing a customer project to protect company assets and employee productivity by eliminating email Spam in a customer’s network. Her prior project role was as an SAP functional support resource during the deployment of SAP to a major automobile manufacturer to improve their overall management of company resources in planning & scheduling, shipping, receiving and warehouse management. She provides support for project management for mobile/wireless initiatives as an ongoing member of the ISC Mobile Apps team. She has provided project management for contract implementations between EDS and customers. She was a project leader on the Y2K conversion project and various application development projects for a major automobile manufacturer.
