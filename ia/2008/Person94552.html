John Clark, CISSP, CIPP, CISA’s Summary

Executive with deep specialization in information risk management. 24 years of progressive experience in technology, information security, data privacy and protection, business continuity, and risk governance. 5 years as a Partner responsible for Deloitte & Touche's Midwest Security and Privacy Practice. 9 years as founder and global practice leader of Accenture's Security Practice. 9 years at Ameritech (now part of AT&T), serving in roles of application developer, IT auditor, and data security/business continuity manager.

Hold several industry certifications including Certified Information Security Provessional, Certified Privacy Professional, and Certified Information Systems Auditor.
