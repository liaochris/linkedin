Tyler Kelley’s Summary

I've been leveraging the power of the internet since 1999, and I'm just getting started. My specialties include online marketing, advertising, and social media pr.

Most of us realize that good organic rankings, effective pay-per-click campaigns, and relevant content attracts visitors to our sites, but the real challenge is moving visitors to action by enticing them to call you or complete a purchase on your site, ensuring they continue to revisit your site after the close, and making certain they refer you to their friends and family. So how do you retain customers and gain word-of-mouth referrals on the internet? Connect with your audience!

Today's internet provides several opportunities for us to connect with our audience (blogs, forums, MySpace, Facebook, Twitter, etc.), but if you think all you have to do is spend a few minutes signing up for a free account, think again. The secret to building relationships online is through effective communication. Without it, you run the risk of being tagged a business without a clue, or worse... a spammer. Let me help.

If you'd like to explore how my skills and experience can help you connect with your audience, don't hesitate to send me a personal message or Linkedin connection request. I look forward to hearing from you.
