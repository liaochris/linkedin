Elaine De Marzo-Robins, PMP’s Summary

Driven and results-oriented senior Project Management Professional with over 12 years of I.T. experience focused primarily on strategy, execution of rapid application development projects (SDLC) and stakeholder management. History of turning-around troubled projects into projects delivered with the highest possible positive ROI. Ensures project teams maintain the discipline required to execute within the agreed upon time, cost, scope and quality constraints.

A well-rounded professional who combines a pragmatic approach with highly developed communication skills, a keen business sense and strong technical aptitude. Has a reputation for leading multi-disciplinary teams toward project success.
