Quinn Taylor’s Summary

I'm currently working towards an MS in Computer Science, with an emphasis in Software Engineering. My research interests include code comprehension, mental representations, and software visualization and evolution.

I have gained broad experience in many CS topics, including operating systems, computer networks, 3D graphics, user interfaces, digital security, languages & compilers, signal & image processing, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

I began programming in elementary school as a hobby. I have over 20 years of experience with Apple computers, and am competent with Windows and Linux. I love working in environments dedicated to creating useful, usable, and intuitive software. I worked as an intern at Apple during Summer 2007, and am returning there full-time after completing my Master's degree in 2008.
