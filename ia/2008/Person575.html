David Taylor’s Summary

I am an associate in the Firm's IP / Media practice group where I advise clients on copyright, technology, DRM, and music licensing issues arising in negotiations, agency proceedings and before Congress.

I do some regulatory work usually assisting clients obtain approval for their investments in telecommunication and media companies, particularly where national security concerns may exist.

I spend most of my pro bono hours in service to our nation's immigration courts representing (or supervising) some of the most tragic and legally challenging cases - all of which have had positive outcomes.

I am admitted in DC and Virginia.

David Taylor’s Specialties:

copyright, music licensing, telecom, media and foreign ownership, immigration (asylum)
