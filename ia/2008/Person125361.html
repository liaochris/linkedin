Matt Gill’s Summary

As Senior Vice President, I oversee the recruiting function of Pile and Company - the executive search division of Pile & Company that focuses on marketing executives. Pile & Company is a leader in advertising agency review consulting as well as recruiting, executive retained search within the marketing and communications field. Prior to joining Pile and Company, I ran the Northeast division of a national recruiting firm including the marketing and operations. My experience includes successful start ups in staffing and recruiting as well as other industries.
