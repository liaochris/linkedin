David Vidal’s Summary

Now I work as International Programs Coordinator at CTY España - Johns Hopkins University, developing a Spanish as foreign language online course. I design and coordinate also the CTY Spanish Summer Courses, and our ESL courses.

I've worked as Spanish as Foreing/Second Language in the Netherlands at Instituto Cervantes (Utrecht - Netherlands) from 2006-2008.

I've collaborated with ITSTB (Laren - Netherlands) as teacher from 2007 until October'08.

Currently I've been teaching Spanish for five years. Before I studied Philosophy at Universidad de Granada and Linguistics at Universidad de Cádiz, where I specialized in Cognitive Sciences and Knowledge Theory.

I'm interested in applying my experience to the teachers training, working in several proyects to carry out for current scholar course. In this field I'm writing my Master Memory, called "Profesor 2.0, del Constructivismo al Conectivismo" (Teacher 2.0, from Constructivism to Connectivism)
