Mark Lynd’s Summary

Mark Lynd (CISSP - ISSAP, ISSMP, PMP, CE|H) is the President of FireScope, Inc.,a revolutionary Business Service Management company dedicated to simplifying IT operations for businesses of any size. Mark is originator of the FireScope technology and solution and began developing it nearly three years ago and continues to architect and drive innovation into the FireScope line with the excellent FireScope Ops team.

Mark was formerly the Global Chief Technology Officer for Hudson Advisors, He and his team were responsible for the technology infrastructure that helped propel Lone Star Funds/Hudson Advisors 'incredible growth that spanned seven data centers supporting numerous offices in Asia, North America and Europe.

Prior to Hudson Advisors, Mark served in various executive/senior management and technical roles for companies such as Metromedia, Perot Systems, Amerada Hess, CPS, Vectrix and GTE. He has 20+ years of experience in information technology and assurance.

An Ernst & Young’s "Entrepreneur of Year – Southwest Region" Finalist, Mark served on the board of directors for Southern Methodist University’s Cox School of Business and was named as one of D Magazine’s Internet Top 50 Professionals. He presented the Doak Walker Award on ESPN’s College Football Awards Show to a national television audience. Mark has spoken at numerous events and has been quoted by numerous publications including Wall Street Journal, Information Week, eWeek, The Dallas Morning News, Computer World, CRN, InfoWorld and others.

Mark holds a Bachelor's of Science degree in Business Administration from the University of Tulsa. He holds numerous industry certifications including the CISSP, ISSAP and ISSMP certifications from ISC2, The PMP certification from the Project Management Institute (PMI) and the C|EH certification from the EC-Council.

Mark served honorably in the United States Army as a paratrooper in the 3rd Ranger Battalion, 2/187th Airborne and the 82d Airborne.
