Alex Kaplinsky’s Summary

Alex Kaplinsky has been a leading figure in the web world since 1993, when he founded managed Networkers, an early internet consulting firm, and for the following decade has held key roles in innovative development companies, including USWeb, Intend Change, Liquid Thinking, and Form Studios, LLC. Alex has been the lead on a number of projects for clients such as John Kerry for President, McAfee, EFI, Yahoo, Hillary Clinton, and SFMOMA. He holds a Masters degree in International Economics and Finance and currently serves as founding president and managing partner of SolutionSet, a web development firm.

http://www.solutionset.com

Web development, back end development, UI design, print production. http://www.solutionset.com
