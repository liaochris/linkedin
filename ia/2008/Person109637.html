  • Co-Founder & Executive Vice President

    Lateral Technologies

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Oil & Energy industry)

    2001 — 2003 (2 years)

    Lateral Technologies is based in Midland, Texas and developed a patented ultra-short radius lateral jet-drilling system for the oil and gas industry. Technology system was designed to run inside 4" and 5" wellbores. First stage involved a mechanical milling assembly and second stage liquid jet-drilling assembly. Drilled using liquid CO2 with pressures up to 20k psi. System was designed to bypass the negative effects of near wellbore damage resulting from drilling and completion procedures. Proven test results showed average well production increases of 400%. Responsible for the launch of service company operating in TX, OK, LA, NM. Expanded into acquisition model whereby mature fields were purchased and technology was implemented to increase the underlying value of the lease assets. Effectively positioned company for successful acquisition.
