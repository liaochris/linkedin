Willem Velthoven’s Summary

Trained in visual communications and Art history, I have founded en developed Mediamatic as a cultural institution but also as a new media agency and technology developer. In the mean time we have also been a publisher, restaurant and an arabic department store. Currently we are looking into the possibility of becoming a dating service. I'm fascinated by the development of media culture and social software.

Currently i'm most interested in developing open social networks als part of public institutions. The currrent developments in this field yield enormous potential for the public sector.

Willem Velthoven’s Specialties:

Concept, Design, Management, Trouble Maker and Coach. As a consultant, I will ask hard questions rather than produce thick reports.
