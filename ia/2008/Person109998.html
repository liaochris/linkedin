KyNam Doan’s Summary

Hello, my name is KyNam or Ky (pronounced Kai), for simplicity. Thank you for being interested in who I am, and what I do.


::What I'm working on now::
Since the end of 2007 I have been working on building a startup, voterBLOC.com - a grassroots organizing application on a web 2.0 platform with the goal of increasing civic engagement and collaboration within a community. We are currently based in the heart of Cambridge, MA.

I am looking for a fellow entrepreneur adept at either web coding or raising capital to help get this off the ground! I invite you to contact me if you're interested in working with us, or even just to talk.


::About me::
In college and at work, I have tried a variety of vocations including IT, Marketing, Computer Science, and Biology, but only in the past two years have I found my calling - Social Entrepreneurship and Management.

As I move forward, whether it's overseas in a developing nation or here dealing with domestic issues, I want my every step to benefit society. My primary goal is to use for-profit management to assist non-profits in driving significant social change.

When I'm not working, I teach Bhangra classes at MIT, cook, and sip California Cabs.

My summary ends here, but I'd just like to add a few last words: I am really proud of my sister, who is now in law school and within arm's reach of her current goals.
