Paul Lloyd’s Summary

A Graphic Designer with over five years experience in website design and development, Paul delivers beautiful visual interface design, information architecture and HTML programming. He is a keen advocate of web standards and best practices, and actively follows latest discussions in this area.

Paul grew up in the UK and studied Graphic Design at Cumbria Institute of the Arts between 1999 - 2002. In 2004 Paul joined Orange Vision, a small web marketing agency, and assisted the companies adoption of web standards and accessible design practices. In 2006 he moved to the United States to take on the Lead Designer role at Ning, Inc., a Silicon Valley start-up based in Palo Alto California. His design direction and standards guidance for it's social web applications, helped the company become host to the largest number of social networks on the Internet.

Today Paul is a freelance designer working with emerging technology start-ups, as well as developing his own web-based solutions for small businesses.

Paul Lloyd’s Specialties:

Standards based (X)HTML and CSS, Fireworks, Illustrator, Flash. Print based packages such as InDesign and QuarkXpress. Visual and interaction design, page layout, writing.
