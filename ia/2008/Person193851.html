Uday Parmar’s Summary

Business development and International sales / pre-sales professional with over 6 years of experience in Software, ITES and Knowledge Services.

Education comprises of an M.B.A (Amity Business School), an M.A in Management (Durham Business School) and a B.Sc in Statistics (Bombay University).

Currently, I head offshore business development at RocSearch (www.rocsearch.com), a 100-analyst strong offshore research and analytics firm which is HQ'ed in London and has a global delivery center in Noida near New Delhi. We are one of the fastest growing players in the knowledge services space in India.

RocSearch focuses on working with professional services firms (consulting, investment advisory, research), marketing and communication agencies and corporates, helping them with their research and analytics needs.

Reporting directly to the Executive Chairman and CEO, I lead a team of executives and handle aspects pertaining to pre-sales, business development, strategy, PR, SEO and web marketing.
