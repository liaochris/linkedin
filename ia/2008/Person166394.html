Ryan Boyle’s Summary

Working as a freelance compositing and motion graphic artist has allowed me to explore and improve upon my creative and artistic skills. It has also given me the chance to improve my communications and self-management skills.

Additionally, I am employed as a Content Developer for Full Sail College. In this position I create content for the online Masters courses in the form of motion graphics, videos, and illustrations. I have worked diligently to further my knowledge of the FMV asset creation and the storytelling process.

As a former Instructor for the compositing class at Full Sail I have gained a wealth of knowledge of Shake and compositing techniques. I have exceeded my standard duties by hosting extra educational workshops and presentations to train our staff. While working as a Lab Specialist a brief time with the Demo Reel Creation course I learned a range of tools involving editing, composition, design, and motion graphics.
