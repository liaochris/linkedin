Francis Battaglia’s Summary

Currently, I'm focused on helping launch a global empowerment company, Humanity Unites Brilliance (HUB), and developing green business partnerships as a co-creative / producer. I also freelance on Flame as a visual effects artist. Originally from the East Coast, my father is an English professor in NYC, and most of my family lives at the 'Jersey shore' near Atlantic City.

Education, work and life experience includes traveling internationally for tv production (DP/On-line Editor), globe-trotting with Kelly Slater on the WCT pro surfing tour, touring nationally with Tony Hawk and pro skate tours; making big wave surf films, witnessing 60 ft waves in the middle of the ocean; creating live Award Show program events for the surf industry. Since 2004, I've worked on network promos, independent and feature films (Flame, Combustion and After Effects compositing for HD/35mm) and national commercial spots for Hewlett-Packard, Diet Pepsi, IHOP, Mattel and Nike (Flame). In recent years I've been experimenting as a co-creative on a few 'conscious media' network start-ups, including The G Living Network(2006), Green Health Live(2007), Luminara(2007) and now Humanity Unites Brilliance (HUB). I'm grateful to have worked recently with 2006 Nobel Peace Prize winner / Grameen Bank founder Dr. Muhammad Yunus, as well as Historian / Author Dr. Howard Zinn, world luminary Dr. A.T. Ariyaratne, Mark Victor Hansen, and many others, including athletes, artists, designers, inventors and humble effective leaders. I received a B.A. degree from the University of Delaware in Communications, Journalism and Political Science. While in college, I played soccer, worked as a newspaper reporter and radio broadcaster. I also studied abroad in Costa Rica and completed an independent study of bias in the news media using communications research methodology. I've won awards as a writer, producer, cameraman, editor and visual effects artist (latest project news at www.nomads-cafe.com).
