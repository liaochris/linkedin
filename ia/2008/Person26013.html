Bradley Forsyth’s Summary

As President & CEO and now Chairman, founded and managed the growth of Mxi Technologies, the market leading developer of Maintenix aviation maintenance management software. Since 1996, Mxi has grown from 14 employees to over 300 through profitable, self financed business growth of over 50% annually. The company has achieved an impressive customer list, including: Air France/KLM Airlines, Qantas Airways, Air Canada, China Airlines, Boeing, Pratt & Whitney, Dassault, Bombardier, US Navy, Swedish Defense and others.
