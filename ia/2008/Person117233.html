Lyla Naseem’s Summary

From customizing itineraries for two-week South Africa press trips, organizing launch parties for new vodka flavors and coordinating photo shoots with Tiger Woods, I have worn several hats in my 14-year public relations career.

I have represented an array of travel clients including hotels, resorts, destinations and airlines around the world where her work has included developing and managing public relations campaigns, promoting national programs, launching new hotels and air service and handling media relations.

I have also worked on several lifestyle product accounts, cause related marketing projects and not-for-profit organizations.

Lyla Naseem’s Specialties:

Media relations and strategy. Creative PR counsel. Press release, by-lined articles, and corporate newsletter writing
