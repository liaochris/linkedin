Jeff Crown’s Summary

Technology Executive, Rainmaker and Coach - For the last 20 years my track record and passion is starting and rapidly growing technology companies. I create strategy, ID and execute large partnerships, and implement operational leverage to grow revenues. Also spent 3 years coaching hundreds of diverse executives across industries on starting, buying, operating companies. I focus on internet, video, mobile, online media, software as a service, hardware/software systems companies and markets.

Created $5.4B in market value (from startup through profitable sale) as VP and Officer of Lycos, a top 3 global Internet and media company. Negotiated alliances and partnerships with Microsoft, IBM, AOL, Ericsson, Netscape, Standard & Poors, Time Warner, Viacom, Disney, MTV, McMillan Publishing and more.
