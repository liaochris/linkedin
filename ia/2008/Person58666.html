Emily King’s Summary

Nationally-recognized Learning & Development expert specializing in coaching for individuals and teams. 10 years in the professional services industry as internal OD consultant, People strategist, performance coach and trainer of professional trainers. Educated at some of the best schools in the country, winner of many ASTD Best in Practice awards, author and public speaker. Target audience for coaching is mid- to senior managers who want to achieve more or who need to overcome interpersonal or leadership issues. Advanced certification in Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) and other instruments; strong OD skills in team building and team interventions; expertise in the area of military transition.
