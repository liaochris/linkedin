Upkar Dhaliwal’s Summary

A Wireless Technology Business Developement Expert with extensively wide contacts and specialist knowledge on Future Wireless Direction – Standards, Engineering & Markets.

A System Architecture Engineering Manager with over 20 years of hands-on experience in Wireless, Space & Defence. As a Professional Chartered Engineer, an industry expert serving on numerous industrial/technical bodies, with specialized knowledge & technical leadership in design and development of state of the art wireless circuits and systems.

My focus for past 12 yrs has been on developing system requirements & specifications and developing market requirements in Wireless Communications. Worked for STMicro on a joint design team exclusively with NOKIA Mobile Phones, for QUALCOMM for 8 years thru the 3G –CDMA/UMTS/HSPA era working closely with up to 25+ licensees, and for SAMSUNG Telecom for early 2G/GSM.

Present focus is IEEE 802 standards like 4G: 802.16 WiMAX & 5G: 802.22 Cognitive WRAN, plus new study group on 802.15.6 BAN and .15.3 UWB, whilst keeping up with 3GPP LTE & 3GPP2 UMB

Recent focus includes Front End solutions for UMTS, CDMA NxOFDMA with MIMO & 802.11,16, 20 & 22 WLAN/WiMax multimode needs

I began my career with BS and MS Degrees in Engineering, leading to early career experiences & contributions in device modeling, circuit design & subsystem developments where I led World wide engineering teams in Four World Class Firsts – First Commercial Active Antenna for Space for ESA, First Active Antenna for Battlefield Radar using MMIC/RFIC, First cdma1x Voice call & First dual mode single stack UMTS-GSM voice call

Major accomplishments are world-class early call in 3G CDMA, UMTS & UMTS+GSM chipset development – which is the complete design cycle of HW & SW integration from product requirement definition to High Level definition thru to chipset verification & customer sampling/support into production of the First single chip transceiver, single SW stack & integrated modem
