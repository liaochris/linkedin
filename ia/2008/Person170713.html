Scot Cheu’s Summary

Scot is a self motivated Packaging Professional with over 20 years of practical hands on experience as a Manager and Technical contributor working in Pharmaceutical, Device, Industrial, and Food packaging. His broad knowledge and diverse skill set enables him to work effectively within all levels of an organization, at the development level through commercialization. Scot’s dedication to product development is most recently demonstrated by the distinction of being the only Inhale / Nektar employee to have worked continuously on the Inhaled Insulin Program from concept through product approval and commercialization (12 years). Scot enjoys development projects and has a proven track record of developing tomorrow’s products, today.

Scot Cheu’s Specialties:

Scot has an extensive background covering all aspects of flexible packaging (design, materials, processes, equipment, printing, and testing).
