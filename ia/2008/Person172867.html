Serdar Copur’s Summary

I am a Producer with 12+ years of experience in producing interactive entertainment and software titles. I spent many of those years at Electronic Arts Inc., working on top titles such as SimCity 3000 and The Sims. After EA, I decided to take the road less traveled and became involved with several start-up companies. I produced everything from top notch internet gaming portals (Gamers.com) to interactive entertainment titles based upon film licenses (Sinbad). Shortly thereafter, I was the Senior Producer in charge of Design for Apple Computer's Emmy Award winning Professional Applications Division (which included Final Cut Pro, DVD Studio Pro, Shake and Motion). Recently, I was the producer of City of Heroes and City of Villains.
