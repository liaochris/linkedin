Jeffrey L. Taylor’s Summary

Fleishman-Hillard Government Relations (FHGR) is a bi-partisan lobbying team of political experts, policy specialists, and skilled professionals who have served as strategists, appointed officials and elected leaders at all levels of government. Our unique insight into how leaders think and make critical decisions has helped us develop a network designed to provide tangible results for our clients. Our bipartisan team has extensive political experience, derived from decades of combined service within Congress, the executive branch, state capitols, and private-sector organizations. We possess broad and deep access to key players in Congress, the White House, and federal agencies and commissions.

Combined, our full toolbox of services include U.S. federal government relations & lobbying, public relations, polling, public opinion survey research, grasstops/grassroots business campaigns in all 50 States, State and local representation, issue advertising, data & research and political consulting.

We work in concert with two other well know firms: Mercury Public Affairs www.mercurypublicaffairs.com is a firm - based in NYC - and Crosslink Strategy Group www.crosslinkstrategy.com.

FHGR is backed by the tremendous global network of Fleishman-Hillard, the world's leading international public relations communications firm. The firm is widely recognized for excellent client service and a strong company culture founded on teamwork, integrity, and personal commitment. Based in the U.S., the firm operates throughout North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, Middle East, South Africa, and Latin America through its 80 owned offices.
