Brian Long’s Summary

Software trouble-shooting, particularly in .NET scenarios and with Borland's Delphi and C++Builder products. Often involves low-level debugging exercises or creative problem analysis.

Training and mentoring programmers in use of .NET, C#, Delphi and C++Builder.

Public speaking at many international conferences.

Malware detection and removal from live (Win32) servers and desktop machines.

Brian Long’s Specialties:

Low-level knowledge of .NET and Delphi systems that helps with trouble-shooting scenarios.

Years of experience in public speaking and interacting with programmers that makes training engagements productive and beneficial.
