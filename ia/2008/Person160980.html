Steve Harper’s Summary

I have been a successful business owner for the past 16 years. Having owned two very successful office technology companies, I learned first-hand what it takes to set myself apart from my competition and get myself noticed.

Having sold my company in January 2004, I have parlayed my extreme interest in helping others into a successful and ever growing consulting company. I enjoy helping my clients understand and recognize the importance of developing a customer concentric organization.

My main goal is to bring the concepts from my book, "The Ripple Effect" to the greater masses. Through a series of talks, workshops, and keynotes, I bring focus to the fact that creating positive experiences for those around you, can and will effect your life and career significantly. Success is virtually guaranteed when you go out of your way to help others achieve what they want in life. The ripples that are caused by your actions will undoubtedly be returned to you in ways that you can never imagine.

I am presently looking for organizations and individuals that want to put the power of the Ripple to work for them!

Steve Harper’s Specialties:

My specialty is connecting people. I have made it my mission in life to add value to those that I come in contact with. Because of that, I enjoy teaching my clients how to connect and keep their customers by developing a "resource" mentality.
