Willem Boom’s Summary

Professional:

Willem Boom is a consummate professional in the learning and development space. His experience in working with organizations on their learning needs is extensive. Willem has fifteen years of solid business experience in both the Information Technology and Learning & Development fields. His track record of success is highlighted by the results he has delivered for his clients.

Willem is Certified Trainer in Situational Leadership from the Center for Leadership Studies in Escondido, California, a certified Harrison Assessment Coach, and has a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Management with an emphasis in Marketing from Webster University. He is a native of the Netherlands and has had extensive international experience throughout his career and life.

Personal:

Willem lives in Bend, Oregon with his wife and children. Bend is an incredible place to live and raise a family. He lives in the foothills of the Cascades and enjoys a high desert, alpine climate with incredible access to the outdoors. Willem is an avid Nordic Skier in the winter and a Mountain Biking fanatic in the summer.
