Ivan Martynov’s Summary

In business I value two things: the faith, which my clients have in me and the challenges, which the business presents. The reason behind my choice of a carreer in law is precisely this: the possibility to closely interact with other people on complex, challenging matters.
In the course of my work with small-scale petroleum traders I learned what I deem is an invaluable skill of working on complex problems with very scarce resources. Quite often I would have to draft a claim or a contract or scrutinize a document intended for a jurisdiction the laws of which I did not know. Quick yet efficient research along with critical decision-making was needed.
I also came to appreciate the fact that healthy and sound business relationships rather than a stack of legal documents are truly conducive to business development. Still, in my view, law plays a crucial role in business, creating clarity and defending private interests.
