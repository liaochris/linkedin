Eric Suro’s Summary

One of my favorite quotes is by Cavett Robert where he states, "Any man who selects a goal in life which can be fully achieved has already defined his own limitations."

I didn't come across this quote until 2007, but it sums up many personal and professional experiences in my time and the way that I attack life. If you don't set a career goal that you are continually trying to reach then what is the purpose of getting out of bed in the morning. Achieving, focused, dedicated, disciplined, and determined are some of the key words that would summarize my work experience so far in my career. How can you measure the result of your achievement if your plan didn't have an end goal?

Jim Valvano once said, "No matter what business you're in, you can't run in place or someone will pass you by. It doesn't matter how many games you've won."
