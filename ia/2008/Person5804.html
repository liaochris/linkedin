Alec Stevens’s Summary

A FINANCE DIRECTOR with a hands-on approach, international, commercial and operational experience and achievements in establishing controls and reporting, including SOX and US GAAP, in high-growth high-tech companies.
In my position of supporting the General Manager and Sales VP, I had seen rapid growth and constant change. During my time I have been an integral member of the senior management team and instrumental in advancing the business in Europe, as well as a team leader and role model in the finance department. Until recently. I have also been responsible for the European Commercial team.

I had completed the Sarbanes-Oxley certification for the European division in 2004; implementing this across the organisation, including the following annual monitoring process. I have worked in a matrix position between finance and the general management, working internationally within Europe, Asia and USA.

On a personal level, I believe that I have the ability to liaise effectively with all levels of management and employees. I am a ‘hands-on’ manager and believe emphasis should be placed on communication and loyal team building. Having worked in the USA for four years and travelling world-wide has allowed me to develop these skills and appreciate many financial, environmental and cultural differences.
