Arlene M. Cooke, Ph.D.’s Summary

Licensed psychologist with over 10 years of wide-ranging clinical experience in both outpatient and inpatient settings. As a psychologist, I specialize in life transition challenges, relationship issues, depression, women and minority issues. I also am experienced in providing consultation and training to non-clinical professionals, involving the translation and interpretation of clinical assessment findings into lay language.

Open to career opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry. Interested in business consulting work as well, perhaps advising corporations on mental health issues and/or in leadership development.
