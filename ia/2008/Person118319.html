Maja Vujovic’s Summary

Having successfully practiced Communications in three distinct cultures of Europe, Asia & North America, I am now an educator and consultant in South East Europe, providing Communications training & knowledge management services. Add to this extensive experience in strategic communications, crisis management, public relations and business affairs and you get my - partial work profile.

There are no barriers I cannot breach with carefully chosen communication. Campaigns I created won coverage in news services and trade media worldwide. My pragmatic creativity, along with advanced Crisis Management skills, allows me to smoothly handle high-risk, high-profile situations.

Most colleagues rely on my writing, editing & research skills, in English, Serbian or French. I have spent more than 20 years, working on three continents, writing and editing effective articles, speeches, presentations, reports, scripts, advertising, PR & marketing copy. I handle rhyme very well and, long ago, enjoyed translating medieval and renaissance drama in verse.

For twenty years, I have worked in front & behind a microphone and camera, as a TV & radio writer, host, reporter and producer. A seasoned presenter, I’m at ease in front of any audience and can carry a dialogue with anyone.

It was my good fortune to also learn pre-press, database design and website development, so by now I know the production world inside out, in marketing, publishing, broadcasting and film.

In leading complex creative teams to budgets and deadlines, I rely on planning and motivating. I am a committed problem-solver with unusual organizational abilities. My secret strength is in research; I am natural Knowledge Manager - an "information bank". This ability to process and classify vast amounts of information has put me in the editor's chair on two encyclopedias to date (with a third one in sight).
