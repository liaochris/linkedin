Andrew L. Fox’s Summary

MindZap Media was founded in 2006 by Andrew Fox, a veteran of over twenty years of high technology sales and business development. He is a graduate of Duke University’s Engineering School (’85) with a double degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and also a graduate of Duke’s Fuqua School of Business (’91) with a degree in marketing.

Mr. Fox began his career with IBM as Marketing and Sales executive with IBM’s Network Division and was responsible for IBM’s wireless networking sales effort. .

In 1996, Mr. Fox joined the marketing team at RealNetworks and was involved in the launch of a number of industry breakthrough products including RealAudio 3.0, and RealVideo 4.0. These products revolutionized the Internet for the delivery of multimedia and paved the way for future products and services that now use the Internet as a broadcast medium. Mr. Fox also started and ran Real Networks Enterprise group and pioneered the use of streaming multimedia in numerous F500 companies such as Boeing, 3Com and IBM.

1999, Mr. Fox became the President & CEO of High Speed Net Solutions, and interactive marketing company which he later merged with Summus, Inc in the spring of 2001. As EVP of Sales and Business Development of Summus (now known as Oasys Mobile), Mr. Fox help transform a small government defense contractor to one of the industry’s leading wireless publishers. Mr. Fox was responsible for putting partnerships in place with a number of the industry’s best known brands including: Sports Illustrated/Time Inc., Disney/ABC News, ESPN, Mattel, Inc., Playboy, Fujifilm, AOL Time Warner, Sony Corporation, Wall Street Journal, Conde Nast/Golf Digest and many more. In addition, Mr. Fox helped productized and commercialize mobile services with these partners on over 80 wireless carriers worldwide, including Verizon Wireless, Cingular, Sprint, Telstra, Vodafone, O2, Telecommunications Movilnet, Rogers, Virgin Mobile Canada, China Mobile and others.

Andrew L. Fox’s Specialties:

In working with clients, Mr. Fox brings his insight, experience, expertise and valuable network of customers, partners and colleagues. He combines a unique talent of helping companies create industry leading strategies and then working with clients on executing these strategies through sales and business development efforts.
