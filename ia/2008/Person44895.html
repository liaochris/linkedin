Created a suite of software tools that allowed the verification of the correctness of the chip design and layout.

    Ubique Ltd.

Company was bought by IBM, which incorporated the technology we developed into their commercial Lotus Sametime and Lotus Notes products.

  • Senior Software Engineer

    Lotus Development Corp. / IBM

    (Public Company; Computer Software industry)

    1990 — 1994 (4 years)

    Developed a compiler and run-time library for a proprietary object oriented language which I helped design, supporting multiple inheritance. Served in a research group designing prototypes for a next generation relational database front-end and engine.
