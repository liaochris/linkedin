Rajesh Dubey’s Summary

A Chartered Accountant & Cost Accountant with over 17 years experience in Credit & Risk Management and institution building. In-depth understanding of credit and risk assessment (project finance, financing NBFCs, SMEs, Corporates) coupled with an in-depth understanding of the SME sector dynamics. Also involved in setting up and commercializing 2 institutions (for guaranteeing credit to SMEs and risk rating of SMEs) and achieving wide recognition, credibility & acceptability for the institutions amongst institutional lenders, SME trade bodies and associations and Government circles.

Rajesh Dubey’s Specialties:

Team building and management, government liaison, development of strategy and implementation.
