Stephen Axelrad’s Summary

With a doctorate in Industrial-Organizational Psychology, have more than seven years of consulting and full-time experience applying organizational development and program evaluation concepts and techniques to help organizations measure and improve upon their effectiveness. Worked with a diverse set of organizations on effectiveness-related issues in the financial services, healthcare, information technology, manufacturing, media research, and social services sectors. For more than three years, focused on developing performance management and quality assurance solutions on complex constituent outreach programs funded by several Government agencies in the US Department of Justice. Currently, leading an initiative to establish a performance measurement and evaluation system at an international non-profit organization.
