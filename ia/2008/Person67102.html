Alexander Gagin’s Summary

Manager and editor of traditional media's online projects. Involved with Internet development since its early start in Russia, being recognized as the first Russian blogger. Was a head of Internet department in several media houses (newspapers and television) and a consultant in a new media agency. Deep experience in Internet technologies and Unix systems, Internet advertising, media websites development and online innovation. Started Russian printed "Internet" magazine as chief editor and was a columnist of other publications. Native language is Russian.

Alexander Gagin’s Specialties:

editorial, internet publishing, internet projects management, usability, unix system administration, creative internet advertising, internet-related journalism, online media, traditional media websites
