Daniel Castro’s Summary

I have over 10 years experience as a UI Engineer and Interaction Designer for complex web applications. I have a real passion for user centered designs while elegantly using the latest technologies to achieve a rich user experience with the maximum user reach possible. Coming from an engineering background I have successfully assessed the technical impact and risks of UI solutions. I am a firm believer in applying web standards when possible and creating all designs with integrated extensibility. I have worked on a great variety of applications, including but not limited to Web 2.0, Ajax, Consumer, Business, Enterprise, Community, Instant Messaging, Web Mail, Mobile Devices, Multi-media, and more.

You can view my portfolio by visiting www.codedface.com (or simply clicking on the links bellow under "Additional Information").
