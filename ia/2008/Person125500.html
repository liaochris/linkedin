Matti Hemmi’s Summary

I have always enjoyed challenging the status quo of things, for the purpose of improving my company’s and my own situation.

During the first part of my career I did this working as an engineer in Spain, Finland, UK and the USA.

Later on I was hired as Managing Director of an engineering company, where we experienced substantial rapid growth (average yearly growth in sales of 18%). Thus, I gathered broad experience in change management. We were highly focused on getting results through developing human resource capabilities, and deploying innovative strategies. Within the tools we used most were: creative thinking and transactional analysis.

After this 17 years experience in the industrial market, I finally decided to jump into the consultancy arena, and give back what I had gained through years of development, and trial and errors.

At Instituto de Pensamiento Creativo e Innovación (Institute of Creative Thinking and Innovation), we offered our customers the development of the Creative Thinking competence within their team, as well as putting their new creative ideas into practice.

Currently I am fully dedicated to Inknowation, where we work with organizations in the development of their innovation capacity as well as in the development of their change management capacity. And as we also like to innovate, have developed some truly innovative training programs in the Corporate Social Responsibility area.

Nowadays I also enjoy speaking at international conferences on the development of innovation capacity within organizations.

Matti Hemmi’s Specialties:

Coaching, Change Management, Innovation Capacity, Creative Thinking, Creative Leadership, Emotional Leadership, Transactional Analysis

Matti Hemmi’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    Inknowation

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    February 2006 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Our primary goal is to support our customers in the development of the innovation capacity of their team. As innovation brings in change, our simultaneous goal is to develop our customers change management capacity.

    We believe organization’s main asset is their people’s talent. We believe one of the best ways to leverage it, is through the development of their innovative capacity.

    As it’s not always possible to drive your change, we encourage our customers to develop their change management capacity even in the case they are not interested in innovating in their organizations.

    The more aware teams and their leaders are of the change process, and of the needed competencies to handle it, the more possibilities for the organization to succeed when change comes.

    Finally, we work with our customers in developing their inner Corporate Social Responsibility. Within this area we offer training programs such as Training for Parents, Stress Management and Diversity Management.

  • Founder, Managing Partner

    Instituto de Pensamiento Creativo e Innovación

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    February 2006 — December 2007 (1 year 11 months)

    Although we know innovation can be developed through the creative ideas of one single person, we strongly believe there are many more success chances for those organizations whose teams have the attitude, techniques, and skills to use their knowledge and experience in an innovative way. Because of this, we consider it's critical that our customers' employees acquire the competence of thinking in a creative manner, and that their leaders have the ability to guide them through the implementation process at the same time they create a protected environment.

  • Managing Director

    GS-Hydro, S.A.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Mechanical or Industrial Engineering industry)

    January 1993 — February 2007 (14 years 2 months)

    In 1993 I was hired to launch the Spanish branch of the GS-Hydro Group. That year we started from scratch, and since then our growth was quite substantial. We diversified the company both in terms of the segments we attended (marine, defense, automotive, earth moving machinery, aerospace, heavy duty machinery, railroad industry), and in terms of the sales divisions through which we entered those segments (distribution, kits, turn key projects, after sales). In 2006, our sales grew by 44% versus 2005.

    During these years our attitude has always been defying the limits established by the market, and creating new ways of entering the market, creating a strong positioning through a clear differentiation versus our competition. Thanks to this approach, GS-Hydro Spain has always been perceived as a strong, capable and innovative supplier.
