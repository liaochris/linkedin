Francesco Milicia’s Summary

Started as Maintenance Manager I developed a system of periodic and preventive maintenance and implemented TPM program with the support of Porsche Consulting.
Lately I've been in charge of engine manufacturing according Toyota Production System and using the main tools of Lean Manufacturing and Value Stream Mapping.
Besides I took part to CEO's Support Team working on interfunctional projects involving HR, R&D and Finance.
In 2005 I moved in China where as GM and legal representative of Thermowatt China I'm developing the 3 business: Electric, Electronic and Solar in the Far East Area.
Since October '07 I also chair the Industrial Commitee of MTS China
