Scott Cunningham’s Summary

The Small Business Administration conducted a study to determine why customers leave. They found that the #1 reason (68%) was because customers perceive that the company doesn't care about them.

I help small business owners and salespeople show their clients that they care about them. For very little money and time, I demonstrate how to create clients for life.

I am interested in meeting entrepreneurs and sales professionals to exchange ideas, leads and referrals.

Scott Cunningham’s Specialties:

client appreciation, client retention, business networking, relationship marketing, referral marketing

Scott Cunningham’s Experience

  • Independent Distributor

    SendOutCards.com

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    February 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    I work with busy business owners and professionals to solve the problem of keeping in touch with customers and prospects. We provide an automated, yet highly personalized card system for customer follow up, thank yous, birthdays, product or event announcements, even for generating referrals.

    You can send your customers personalized cards through the mail using our unique on-line system. So, using our product does not add to your busy day, yet it gives you an easy way to have that personal customer contact.

    You can send 5 or 500 cards in 5 minutes, in your own handwriting and even upload a picture or logo into the card.

    Our motto is "People will forget what you said, they will forget what you did, but they will never forget how you made them feel".

    For about $5 per year per customer, you can make your customers feel special and appreciated, and they will never forget you.
