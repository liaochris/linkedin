Nathan Genez’s Summary

I am a former Platinum Consultant with SAP America and have been working with SAP since 1996. I've spent most of that time working extensively in the FI/CO and BI arenas and am currently serving as a Managing Parter at Serio Consulting LLC.

Within FI/CO I have a strong history with the capital accounting areas inclusive of Project Systems, Investment Management, and Asset Accounting. This experience goes all the way back to R/2, R/3 (release 2.2 through 4.7), and continues up to the current ERP 6.0 release.

I am also an experienced BI Team Lead and Data Architect who has supported numerous customers through their go-lives during initial implementations, rollouts, and subsequent upgrades. My focus has been data architecture, data modeling, and delivering robust solutions for Financials and Project related areas. I've worked with all releases of SAP BW including the new NetWeaver BI 7.0 release.

I'm very active in the SAP community and try and lend my support wherever it has value. I'm a frequent author and speaker on a wide range of SAP ERP and BI issues and currently serve as a technical editor for both the SAP Financials Expert and BW Expert publications. I regularly speak at industry conferences such as SAP's ASUG conference and those hosted by the "Expert" publications such as the SAP Financials Conference, BI/Portals Conference, and Reporting & Analytics Conference. I also have an ongoing blog series on SAP's SDN site that is worth reading.

https://www.sdn.sap.com/irj/sdn/weblogs?blog=/pub/u/251739612
