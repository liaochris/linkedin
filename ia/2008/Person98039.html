Josh Roberts’s Summary

Mr. Roberts designed and developed a complete web-based data collection, reporting and analysis system. Based on the powered by EyeOn Toolset developed by Eye On Solutions to provide advanced data collection, reporting, and analysis capabilities through a meta-data architecture in a hosted application. An entrepreneur of the Technology industry, after first automating aspects of a political consulting firm in Washington, DC, he realized a passion for working with computers, especially the Internet. While earning a Masters of Science degree in Information Systems from American University, Mr. Roberts worked as the Manager of Labor Management systems at Amtrak. He then went on to found Eye On Solutions, a Software-as-a-Service company developing and maintaining propietary hosted, branded, and configurable applications for Quality Assurance, Field Service Automation, Inspections, and Productivity Management.
