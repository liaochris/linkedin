Paul Kerr’s Summary

Coaching skills, coach-the-coach, leadership development, consultancy skills and group dynamics

Paul Kerr is a Senior Learning Consultant for the Executive Education department of the RSM Erasmus University. Paul has designed and delivered training programmes in areas such as coaching skills, coach-the-coach, leadership development, consultancy skills and group dynamics. His special interests include Leadership Development, Executive Coaching, Organisational Learning and HR Management.

Paul’s background lies in Management Training & Development, Human Resources Management / Consultancy and Coaching in The Netherlands, UK and Japan. Before joining RSM Paul ran his own training and coaching company in The Netherlands providing services to clients including T-Mobile, Robeco, and KPN. Prior to that he worked as a Human Resources Advisor and Senior Trainer in The Vision Web, an innovative Dutch consultancy organisation. Paul has experience with industries such as consulting, telecoms, banking, government, food, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, ICT and research.

Paul is driven by the desire to provide clients with an optimally tailored learning experience that fully meets their needs. He believes that quality executive education is a collaborative exploration between client and provider that helps an organisation to deal with real business problems through the application of well-targeted knowledge and skills.

Paul Kerr’s Specialties:

Executive education development and delivery.Training design & implementation. Professional coaching (individual/team). Leadership development and training. Myers-Briggs Typology Instrument (Steps I & II certified). Metaprofile Analysis. Success Factor Modelling. Facilitation (IAF Certified Facilitator). Coaching training and supervision. Certified NLP Trainer.
