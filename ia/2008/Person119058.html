Mansi Trivedi’s Summary

Its mid-night now and I am relishing flying in the swiftness of the night. I walk over to the window to check if the moon was delivered okay and if the stars look all right. I put my book aside beneath the frozen lamp, and start tic-clacking away on my computer.

So, here are 10 things I want to tell you about me:
I dream big.
I don’t settle for big ideas, I settle for remarkable strategies.
I write.
I love Grey’s Anatomy. Enough that I can quote Meredith Grey from Season 2, Episode 4.
I learn from people who sit besides me at work and the ones I fight ideas with during never ending group meetings.
I have had the opportunity to dip myself into a variety of fields where I have encountered greatest mentors. In the past 5 years, I have worked in a production house, my dad’s business, theatre, a non-profit organization, a friend’s start-up company and in advertising.
I observe things and digest every word said to me and before going to bed and then reanalyze and recapture the parts that really inspire me.
In the past six months, I have been to three concerts, two stand-up shows, about fifty movies and fourteen cities spread across the east coast. My mind's a little insatiable.
I listen even when no one’s talking.
I love planning.
