Karl Mattson’s Summary

12+ years of consumer web product and content innovation, management and operational experience. An expert in the business of content.

Accomplished, versatile, innovative media professional with wide range of experience (Senior Product Manager of AOL to startup to non-profit) in the product, business and editorial sides of print, broadcast, and online journalism and communications…

Strong web application and wireless product management experience…from building a product team to creating and executing on strategic roadmaps using prototypes, testing and market research as a guide. There is no need to shoot from the hip.
