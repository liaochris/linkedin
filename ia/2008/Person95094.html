John Krull, CPC (jkrull@pyramidsgroup.com)’s Summary

I joined The Pyramids Group in 2005. Since that time I have enjoyed the challenge of finding the best and brightest candidates for my clients. Building companies and changing lives is truly what we do.

As a Certified Personnel Consultant (CPC), I have had the opportunity through education and training to expand my professional knowledge base in employment law, changing regulations and best business practices. This has allowed me to achieve and maintain the highest standard of ethics and accountability as set out by the National Association of Personnel Services (NAPS).

In conjunction with our sister company, Career Transition Strategies, we are able to provide expert career coaching to individuals that are in need of job search assistance or are considering a career change. We utilize a scientific and proactive approach to career development and offer a variety of programs to accommodate people in every different stage of their career.

If you would like to learn more about our custom recruitment solutions or if you are in need of career coaching please contact me at 317-872-4960 or jkrull@pyramidsgroup.com .
