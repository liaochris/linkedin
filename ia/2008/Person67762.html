Gary Nip’s Summary

Secretly like to win a D&AD Yellow pencil before I’m 30, but my real ambition is to simply become a good designer and to know I’m producing good work for my clients.

Outside work I’m a founding member of a social enterprise helping volunteers based in Manchester and I’d also love to be able to contribute my skills to directly help others. I believe all disciplines of design come from the same desires, and that we can contribute for social improvement even in the most modest ways.
