Tomasz Jezierski’s Summary

During the MA and Ph.D. studies took part in numerous international conferences in Heidelberg, Mainz, Greifswald, Frankfurt am Main (Germany), Zagreb (Croatia) and Kraków, giving papers in the field of tax law, company law, energy law, telecommunication law and family law.

Graduate of the School of German Law of the cooperative program of Johannes-Guttenberg University (Mainz, Germany), Ruprecht-Karls University Heidelberg (Germany) and Jagiellonian University. Graduate of the Program of Austrian Law conducted by Vienna University (Austria).

Passed the final written and spoken exam (without repeat exam) at State Commission on Tax Consultancy at Ministry of Finance and sworn as Tax Consultant in June 2007.

Court referee’s apprentice in District Court in Kraków (Cracow).

Involved in many significant business projects, also worth over 150 million PLN (about 50 million USD), providing legal help for clients from USA, Germany, Italy, France, Czech Republic, Israel and others.

Languages: Polish (native), German, English.

Tomasz Jezierski’s Specialties:

Tax law, business and corporate law, contract law, public tender law. Especially M&A, restructuring (also cross-border), tax planning and legal representation in tax matters (tax authorities, administrative courts).
