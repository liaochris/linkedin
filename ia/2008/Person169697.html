Sara Upton’s Summary

I'm a graduate of one of the top Communications schools in the nation (Boston University), now practicing Web design in Columbus, Ohio.

My educational background is largely in Advertising as a whole, though I concentrated in creative. (I also have a Minor in Ancient Greek Civilizations, so if you'd like to know exactly how much of the movie 300 is actually true, feel free to ask.)

My previous work experience includes three years at WBUR, a highly-respected NPR station located in Boston, MA. I focused on Marketing and Membership work with WBUR, doing everything from dealing with member issues to writing on-air promotions. After WBUR, I spent almost two years with SBC Advertising in Columbus, where I got my proverbial feet wet in the world of Advertising and Interactive Design. And now I'm just beginning a new career with Blue Diesel, also in Columbus, which I hope will turn out to be an extremely rewarding experience.

I enjoy learning as much as possible, taking on as many projects as I can squeeze into a day, and simply trying to improve my skill set. I hope to move up the ranks in the Interactive world in coming years, and look forward to where Web advertising is heading and all the [freaking awesome] things we can do with Interactive design.
