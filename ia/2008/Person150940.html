Philip Farina, CPP, CLSD’s Summary

Philip Farina, CPP, CLSD is the Chief Executive Officer for Farina and Associates, Ltd., based in San Antonio, TX. Parent company of Enterprising Securities Training and Antiterrorismcareers.com. Mr. Farina is responsible for all business groups supporting client interests globally as well as strategic planning and corporate management.

Mr. Farina is an analytical thinker who possesses over eighteen years of in-depth industry specific knowledge and experience. He has been a pioneer in all facets of security and risk management for the hospitality/tourism industry. He began his career while serving in the United States Naval Intelligence Community, where he held top security clearances and gained expertise in antiterrorism, protective services and adult education. Mr. Farina participated in numerous military special operations, amphibious and law enforcement campaigns in the Middle East, Latin America and Africa. During his tenure as a federal law enforcement training officer with the U.S. Department of Defense, he was tasked with conducting penetration tests and exercises against military installations to analyze existing protective operations. As director of security for global corporations, he developed a wide array of innovative security and business programs with his core focuses being Hospitality Security Management, Corporate Security Infrastructure, Vulnerability Assessments, Business Continuity Analysis,and Crisis Management.

A leader for ASIS, Intl. he holds the position of Assistant Regional Vice President & Texas State Director of Education and Certification. Mr. Farina has been the Chapter Committee Chairman for Education & Certification responsible for conducting the Certified Protection Professional (CPP) Review Program/Chapter Committee Chairman for Membership & Recruitment. Serving as Subject Matter Expert for International Lodging Security Council. Board Certified in Security Management.

Philip Farina, CPP, CLSD’s Specialties:

Mr. Farina is an internationally recognized security expert, speaker, educator and noted author of Antiterrorism Careers - THE Ultimate Guide to Professional Employment Opportunities in Specialized Security. Actively involved in the Homeland Security effort, he manages & conducts programs for the safeguarding of critical infrastructure. Mr. Farina is Board Certified in Homeland Security Level 3 (CHS-III), Board Certified Lodging Security Director (CLSD) holds many clearances and certifications.
