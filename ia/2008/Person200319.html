Will Sheward’s Summary

I've spent nearly 20 years working in the IT industry in Sales and Marketing dealing with high-level multi-million pound contracts and low-cost consumer products. I've successfully launched products in both niche and consumer markets, designed and implemented customer relationship management strategies and advised on traditional and new media promotional programs.

Since mid-2003 I've been with Isode Limited, a UK software house specializing in Directory (LDAP/X.500) and Messaging (X.400, XMPP, IMAP/POP/SMTP) server software.

I was recently elected to the Board of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), an independent, nonprofit standards development organization whose primary mission is to define open protocols for presence, instant messaging, and real-time communication and collaboration on top of the IETF's Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol (XMPP).
