Tarun Hukku’s Summary

Started off with a business analyst profile and then moved to a sales role in my first two companies. Then it was time to hit the road with my own company formed with a couple of other directors. Successfully run for a decade, sold my stake and then took a move into the corporate world primarily to experience scale.

Managed the P&L for the conference business of Medi2India and its international partnership. Took the flagship event 'India Internet World' to a position of being the 4th largest in the world of its kind.

The past 8 years have seen me through a variety of roles and experiences, each of which has been invaluable in helping me develop a complete understanding of business and its most precious asset, people.

Over the past 3 years, apart from successfully fulfilling my professional obligations I have also been an active Community Network Hobbyist. As the Network Leader of India Outsourcing Network, the Bangalore Business Network, the HR Network and the Global Golf Network I have, built robust business commmunities that have enabled over 10,000 professionals to network and grow professionally as well as improve deal flow.

I believe that the community networks will play a key role in devlopment, marketing and sales of products in the future. Organizations that take the lead in developing and nurturing professional and community networks will substantially benefit in the future.

Tarun Hukku’s Specialties:

Have held several management positions that have given me an exceptionally holistic organizational experience of sales & marketing, P&L ownership, HR and Administration. All in all a very complete two decades plus of exposure to business in all its aspects. And of course the satisfaction of having created and rejuvenated functions and of having consistently met business goals.
