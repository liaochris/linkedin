Gilberto Loacker’s Summary

Highly driven, customer focused, result-oriented leader, manager with proven 14+ years experience in enterprise and startup settings in the areas of enterprise applications (Manufacturing, Healthcare, Services Request management, CRM, Business Process Management, Online Marketing, Online Portal Business).
Exceptional strategic and entrepreneurial thinker, problem solver, and communicator with diversified experience managing all aspects of product development, system architecture and product management. Proven track records of managing enterprise product development using various methodologies including Agile Models as well as traditional Hybrid Waterfall.

1. As a CEO and entrepreneur I build, grow and lead commercial organizations to meet customer and shareholder expectations.

2. As a software factory manager I create, re-organize or tune software factories which build software products for internal or external customers.

3. As an architect I invent, design and evangelize software product solutions while leading a team of software engineering experts ( up to 100) to professionally build long term software products.

Gilberto Loacker’s Specialties:

1. Executive level Management expertise leading, growing and running small to mid-sized organizations.

4. Solid hands-on technical experience architecting complex "Mission Critical Hosted Solutions".
