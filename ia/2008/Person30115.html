Camille K. Hedrick’s Summary

With over 15 years of experience in business marketing, communications, and public relations, Ms. Hedrick is a customer-focused leader and communicator with proven success in launching and implementing innovative strategies and processes to build positive corporate identities, promote businesses and professional organizations, and create customer loyalty. She possesses exceptional expertise in designing international public relations, corporate communications, and marketing programs to increase sales and generate repeat business. An adept diplomat at negotiating with C-level officers, engineering, and salespeople to achieve consensus and buy-in, Ms. Hedrick champions change in matrix-driven, competitive environments. With a participative management style, she consistently exceeds goals; she is willing to relocate.

Currently at EDS, an HP company, within Worldwide Marketing - Communications, she is responsible for developing language services and driving strategic technology direction in global information workflows for the company worldwide. At Alcatel, she spearheaded an award-winning customer-loyalty program spanning 100 countries, and also managed marketing communications for Alcatel’s largest division in the United States. Her earlier experience led her to develop and deliver large customer events in Cape Town, South Africa; Monte Carlo, Monaco; Cannes, France; and most recently, Dallas, Texas. Finally, from Paris, France, she launched a French-built magnetic-resonance imager in a very competitive, global marketplace.
