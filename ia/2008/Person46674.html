David Golding’s Summary

Hello, and welcome to my online professional profile.

During my undergraduate education, I have focused on marketing and business while completing a minor in management. This school year, I will graduate with a Bachelor's in European Studies and apply for graduate school in Christian history. My plans are to complete graduate studies and work toward a career in missiology (the study of missions and missionary work).

That being said, the past eleven years have been quite a journey through professional positions in technology, especially web development and design. I have a love for building online communications media and believe that the world will increasingly converge technologically around the internet; no matter the field, therefore, skills in web technology will prove extremely useful. My skills include proficiency in HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and CakePHP (I'm currently writing a book on CakePHP will be available soon!). I'm able to apply these skills to database management and online networking which create powerful solutions for myriad projects from historical research collection to full-blown e-commerce web sites.

I am excited for opportunities to consult with you on web projects and technology, even though my career will focus more academically on social sciences in the coming years.

David Golding’s Specialties:
