Graeme Cumming’s Summary

With more than 8 years working experience in the online space, I am both capable and passionate about delivering solutions that make a difference. From strategic conceptualisation, through to delivery, you will find me committed to excellence.

Having started my consulting career at The IQ Business Group, I have been delivering solutions in the online space ever since. I have been involved in the development of some of South Africa's biggest and most interactive websites - including Big Brother, Idols and the broader M-Net portal. I have also been involved in some of the most successful online retail initiatives, including Pick n Pay Homeshopping, Incredible Connection and Makro amongst others.

I have worked my way up the corporate ladder from Business Analyst at The IQ Group and MWEB Business, to Business Unit Manager managing the development team at South Africa's premier pay-television broadcaster, through to Operations Manager and eventually General Manager at MWEB Business's development division. I now find myself doing what I am passionate about - delivering technology based solutions, across converging platforms.
