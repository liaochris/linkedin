Edward Rovera’s Summary

LinkedIn is all about professional networking and the benefits that come with it. Originally, LinkedIn connections were to reflect those individuals with whom you have some experience and of whom you would be willing to recommend or introduce to another professional within your business circle. To that end, I created my initial profile at http://www.linkedin.com/in/edrovera. There, you will find all the details of my career, education, and aspirations.

To expand one’s business possibilities beyond the immediate "friends and family," it becomes necessary to include connections to people you don’t necessarily know directly. A broad network has its advantages but I believe some first-hand knowledge of a person and a way to contact them outside of LinkedIn regarding opportunities seem small requests when asked to introduce a person to a friend or colleague.

So, to expand my personal professional network and still have a genuine, albeit in some cases limited, knowledge of all the people for whom I might have to furnish a recommendation or introduction, I ask you to send me an invitation to connect at Edward.J.Rovera (at) gmail.com. Includes some details about yourself, about your business or expertise, and, most importantly, about what I can do for you. I, in turn, will respond in kind. If at all possible, a brief telephone conversation might also be arranged to further enhance our knowledge of each other. In this way, we can see if there is an immediate benefit to being in one another’s professional networks or if our communication will more likely bear fruit in the long term.

Thank you for your time and attention and I hope to be hearing from you soon at Edward.J.Rovera (at) gmail.com.
