Jim Ericson’s Summary

I have experience in all aspects of marketing and product management. I am currently working as VP of Marketing with Agilix responsible for marketing strategies and campaigns that increase awareness and drive customer acquisition for learning management solutions.

Prior to joining Agilix I was most recently VP of Marketing for Move Networks, where I managed and oversaw all marketing efforts to define, publicize, deliver and measure video streaming solutions that powered the online video experiences of leading broacasters, such as ABC.com, FOX.com, CWTV.com, ESPN 360, Discovery.com and others.

I have 14 years of experience in online marketing, including experience in product marketing, product management, direct response marketing, campaign management, Web analytics, market research, and marketing communications.

After earning my MBA from BYU in 1998, I was the first marketing employee at MyFamily.com (then Ancestry) and drove the Ancestry.com subscription service from 20,000 to more than 70,000 within my first year of employment. I was also product manager of Ancestry.com, MyFamily.com, Ancestry Family Tree, and Family Tree Services at various times. I designed print ads and Web ads and had some of the largest online successes ever in terms of response and conversion rates. I managed a small marketing team that achieved its goal of implementing marketing campaigns that in aggregate produced a positive cash flow within the same month of implementation.

As VP of Marketing for OneGreatFamily, that success continued. I also worked as VP of Marketing for 10X Marketing (an online marketing agency) and Infobase Media (direct response and online marketing).

Other related experience includes roles as publications director (creating printed catalogs) and Webmaster, where I first learned about online marketing back in 1996-1998.
