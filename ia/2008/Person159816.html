Richard Clarke’s Summary

My two passions for leadership and personal development have drawn me into the role of executive coach, where I thrive on helping executives on their journey to identifying and cultivating that authentic self, which I believe is a sure way for them to become the best leader that they can.

My own experience of business is broad: both across industry (through my consultancy clients) and in the types of challenges faced within the business environment. I have operated at executive level within a global firm, had P&L responsibility within a venture capital funded business and acquired, grown and sold businesses in my own right.

Richard Clarke’s Specialties:

Technically gifted individuals developing leadership and business skills.
Executives with "blind spots" that drive colleagues up the wall.
Young executives and key person retention.
Transition into more entrepreneurial roles.
