Selma Santos’s Summary

I live and work in beautiful Tampa Bay.

- I am currently working on my dream. I am the Chief Child Honoring Leader with the Dream Believe Inspire project. I am determined to touch the lives of as many Children as I can worlwide. Encouraging adults to understand the role they play in the life of a Child and how they impact one of the most precious and influencing gifts we give to our Children: their belief system, is part of our purpose. Working on my first book and course, I have the vision of encouraging Children to become individuals with minds of steel and hearts of gold. Striving to be the highest version of themselves at each given moment may sound too audacious and even impossible of a goal to some, however, it all starts with a wish, a dream, and the believe that it CAN be done! "Children are the living messages we send to a time we will not see. ~John W. Whitehead, The Stealing of America, 1983....Let us keep the message clean, strong, and joyful.

- For the past 8 years I have been a passionate International Real Estate Consultant. Having an International Portfolio of properties around the Globe and being myself a "citizen of the world", specializing in International Real Estate came easy and naturally.

- With my International background, I am currently involved in the introduction phase of a Brazilian company, Purific of America. Working with Mr. James Cheatham, involved in the first Wendys open in the United States, is an honor and an absolutely solid professional foundation for years to come of Purific's growth and marketshare. I currently hold the position of Director of Training with Purific of America. Have you stopped using plastic water bottles/jugs? It's time to stop the pollution and waiste! I am proud of my connection with this innovative and reputable company.

- Above all this? I choose to make a difference in the precious life of my child, my vivacious and beautiful Sade. I choose to live in an eternal state of Gratitude.
