Luciano Costa’s Summary

Obtained a B.S. Degree in Hospitality Mgmt. from the U. of Alabama in 1996 with Cum Laude Honors and a Minor in Spanish.

Luciano has enjoyed creating unique events since high school. He created and organized one of the most successful fundraisers in school history and helped raise thousands for the school’s new computer lab. Later in college he was chosen by the International Students Association to coordinate a banquet for 300+ attendees for their Annual International Festival, the highest attended banquet since its inception.

And last summer he was commissioned as an event coordinator by the Lambda Basketball Association, for whom he helped raise over $18,000 during his “Hoop Dreams” fundraiser and made possible to send 4 local GLBT basketball teams to compete in the Gay Games in Chicago. Luciano’s team won the gold medal!

In the Spring of 2007, Luciano formed SoCal Social Club, LLC-- a networking organization specializing in planning distinctive social events for gay professionals in Los Angeles.

Our mission is to provide the GLBT community with positive entertainment options, foster culture and the arts, and most importantly unite our community with lasting relationships.

Membership is currently FREE to our Original Club Members. Register online to receive weekly invites to upcoming private events.
