Iavor Tzolov’s Summary

My personal interests lie at the crossroads of the finance world and the Internet business. By education I am a corporate finance person, by affinity I am interested in applied technologies. My career path has allowed me to combine both: I started in a successful web strategy agency during the bubble times, worked in corporate banking and financial consulting after the dot.com burst. Some time ago, I decided to put my corporate knowledge and experience to contribution in order to drive the transformation of start-ups into successful ventures and lead them on the way to going global.

Iavor Tzolov’s Specialties:

Social web applications, web2.0, business development, project management, business process optimization, financial modeling and analysis, financing, investor relationship management.
