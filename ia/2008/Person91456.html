Jim Lane’s Summary

My field is energy - usually, the renewable kind - where I work as a journalist, analyst, consultant and investment banker.

As Director, Clean Energy at KEMA Consulting, I analyze business models, make forecasts, and clarify risk and opportunity in retail clean energy products and services. This is a subscription service focused on carbon services, energy efficiency, solar, wind, biomass, demand response and demand-side power generation, and includes the monthly publication Clean Energy Markets.

In addition, about 15,000 industry people each weekday read my blog, Biofuels Digest, which covers global news on producers, policy and distribution. Readers find it via email, RSS, the web, or syndicated on Reuters, the New York Times, the Chicago Sun-Times or Fox Business News. It's the most widely read biofuels daily! You can subscribe for free by visiting http://www.biofuelsdigest.com.

Weekends, I am writing a book on energy policy (fuel and power, both fossil and renewable) that will be out this year.

And if you are looking for insight into value creation in clean energy - ask me, as I would love to have you become a client of my KEMA service.

Jim Lane’s Specialties:

Forecasting, market sizing, market share analysis, news analysis, writing.
