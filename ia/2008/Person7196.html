Alex Yates’s Summary

Alex Yates has more than 12 years of professional experience including roles in marketing, training and sales. He has worked for a diverse group of employers including Webster University, Civic Theatres, KFMB-FM, Donjoy Inc., the San Diego Padres, Marriott Hotels, and Speak, Inc. Speakers & Trainers Bureau.

He has provided expertise on presentation skills, group communication and teambuilding, branding, creativity and brainstorming, leadership development, mentoring, public relations, and marketing communications. Experiences include working as both an internal and an external resource for engineering firm Smith & Nephew, Gayle Grant Publications, the California Wolf Center, the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society, the SDSU Alumni Association, and the Boy Scouts of America.

Alex served as the President for the San Diego Education Consortium and has been an active member in the Society for Human Resource Management and the American Society for Training and Development. He possesses a contagious sense of enthusiasm and passion for helping organizations and individuals achieve their maximum potential. Alex recently completed his Master of Arts in Management & Leadership and is seeking jobs in Organizational Development, Training, or Marketing in the Chicago area.
