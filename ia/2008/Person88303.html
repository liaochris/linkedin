Jennene Shanholtz’s Summary

My business is about service so I am not happy until my clients are happy. A home is one of the most significant investments a person will make in life. So, it is my job to make the real estate buying and selling process as easy and as smooth as possible for my clients. It is also essential that I provide results by working hard to get the job done, while giving the client more than is expected. In addition, as a licensed Realtor in Las Vegas, Nevada, I have the benefit of having lived in the Las Vegas area for over 11 years. So I have seen and lived through the city's huge growth and the many changes within the market. As a Prudential Americana Group sales professional, I can also offer my clients the power of the Prudential name, experience, expertise and distinctive “Rock®” logo, reinforced by an effective national advertising program and the prospect of nationwide property exposure – and search capability – on Yahoo!, one of the largest portals on the Internet.
