Doug Fisher’s Summary

A professional communicator skilled in dealing with new media, especially participatory journalism and related areas. A longtime Associated Press managing correspondent and news editor, and an award-winning broadcast and newspaper reporter. Primary editor on the Wireless Election Connection, which CNN called one of the best things about the 2004 political conventions, and principal behind the Hartsville Today citizen journalism project. Also author of the Common Sense Journalism weblog and the Common Sense Journaism monthly column used by press associations across the nation. Co-author of "Principles of Convergent Journalism" (Oxford, 2008).

Doug Fisher’s Specialties:

Coaching and teaching young writers, newsroom managmement that includes hiring, innovative thinking -- especially how to reshape the editorial product and find new ways of making money on the Web.
