    (Defense & Space industry)

    July 1986 — November 1996 (10 years 5 months)

Once the engine matured, I also took part in the engine - aircraft integration studies and related design / development. I was also involved in development of thermal systems for the Marinized version of this aero-engine.

    Coordination with other development agencies, research institutes, government agencies, vendors, etc, and related techno-commercial activities, project management, people management helped me to shape me up as an over-all techno-commercial manager large programs, teams and customers.
