Matthew Sosnowski’s Summary

Hi.

Fresh out of Ohio University, I began my career as a creative professional; oftentimes building something valuable out of nothing, as most artists--classical or otherwise--do. In my case, my art was limited to the digital world, which required me to work in solitude and perform monotonous edits for long hours 5 or more days a week. This began to take a toll on me both mentally and physically. I felt that I had pretty much accomplished all I could at my current job, so I sought out other opportunities that would enable me to broaden my skill sets.

My desire to get away from the introspective and sometimes redundant routine of video production led me to Jade Sterling Steel. Here, I had to repeatedly break out of my comfort zone and make 75-100 cold calls per day. Once things got rolling and I built an account base (and after my trainer left the company), I had to learn to run my own business through finding, contacting and managing over $750,000 worth of accounts.

I came on at Syntryx, a competitive intelligence firm for online advertisers, in early November '07. Through crawler-based technology, we track the advertising resources of websites throughout the world, enabling our clients to beef up their SEO and Affiliate Marketing programs.
