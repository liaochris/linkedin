Louis Marzano’s Summary

I am an experienced leader with a passion for product development and a complementary mix of technical experience and business acumen. I’m differentiated from other leaders in that I deliver “drama-free” results. While I respect those that can turn around a failing project in the eleventh hour (the “diving catch”), I believe it’s better to avoid problems in the first place. I am emotionally even-keeled and can think clearly during a crisis (should one still occur). I’m results-driven and commitment-oriented and I manage my teams by objective using metrics to measure progress and success. I stand for better results through better planning and clear thinking during the occasional storm.

I’ve led the development and delivery of successful products in multiple high technology industries, including wireless networking, computers, and medical device, improving delivery efficiency at each organization. I have worked in companies of varying size, from $ multi-billion with tens of thousands of employees, to less than $300 million and fewer than 1,500 employees. I’m drawn to growing organizations that recognize their need to establish more discipline in their product development and are committed to building consistency in methods. Such environments allow me to leverage in addition to my technical skills, my strengths in project management, organizational development, collaborative teamwork, and no nonsense delivery to commitments. Drama-free development.
