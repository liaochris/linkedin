LaVera Forbes {lavera.networking (AT) gmail.com’s Summary

Ms. Forbes has over 20 years experience working to positively influence individual and organizational development. She focuses on a company’s biggest asset—its workforce.

She has nine years experience directly managing education and training
programs that will attract, develop, retain and deploy talent to drive the company’s
strategic business goals.

She has provided thought leadership, consultative support and project management for key initiatives such as staffing, leadership assessment, succession planning, and development planning.

At the City of Sacramento, her mission was to attract, develop and retain outstanding talent for the organization, and provide them with the knowledge and work environment to perform their best.

From initial concept through full implementation, she created City University for a workforce of 5,000 employees. This comprehensive and highly respected program resulted in significant increases in employee recruitment, retention, job satisfaction, and reduction in absenteeism.

Ms. Forbes also created a successful Health and Wellness Program, a large scale Training division, a Diversity program, and various reward strategies that attracted the best and brightest people and created innovative learning and development opportunities for employees.

In addition to being an expert in education and training, Ms. Forbes is an exceptional leader. She worked as Executive Director for a non-profit employment and training organization in which she strategically turned the organization around from near bankruptcy.

California Governor Gray Davis recognized her solid leadership skills. He appointed her to revitalize one of the most difficult departments in the entire state of California, managing a staff of 1,200, in 20 offices, with a budget of $3.5 billion.

LaVera has a Master’s degree in Career Counseling and a Bachelor’s in Communications. She completed a Certificate program for senior executives at Harvard University.
