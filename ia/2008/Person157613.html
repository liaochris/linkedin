Robert Barr’s Summary

Robert joined the U.S. Navy as a Cryptologist in 1988 and served 6 years. After being awarded the Navy Achievement Medal in 1991 and serving in several critical positions, Robert left the Navy with a goal of taking his wealth of international experience and objective analytical skills to private sector business and finance.

With over 15 years of business and finance experience, Robert has held positions ranging from Owner, to CFO, to CEO. As a Financial Consultant Robert strives to help each one of his clients plan for a financial future in a way that best meets his or her goals, or in the case of a corporation, the goals of the board of directors.

Robert’s clients include corporate executives, small & large business – public and private, Individuals in their early planning for retirement, retirees relying on a fixed steady income, and non-profits organizations.

Whether you are planning your retirement, saving for your children’s or grandchildren’s education, running a business, or looking to preserve your wealth for future generations, Robert is here to help.

Robert and his wife, Candace, are devoted parents of two girls and are active members of the community. Robert is a Junior Achievement volunteer regularly teaching financial literacy and entrepreneurship to students. Candace and the girls regularly volunteer at assisted living and retirement homes in the local community.

Whether you are an experienced investor or just beginning to invest, you can depend on Robert for objective advice and well thought-out solutions that focus on your unique goals.
