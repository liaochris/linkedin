Sheri Oneal’s Summary

Commercial photographer Sheri O’Neal brings her storytelling spirit to every photo shoot. Each assignment is the chance to record a moment—an emotion—with timeless soul and style. For 17+ years, she’s captured memorable images for editorial, corporate and advertising use. A look through her voluminous, varied portfolio is proof that ‘commercial’ photography, no matter the subject or industry, can live and breathe with beauty and purpose. O’Neal’s creative intuition is revealed in every image.

Florida born and raised, O’Neal felt the call of the Music City muse and transplanted to Nashville in 2001. Approachable and professional, O’Neal has quickly gained the trust of art directors, editors and business associates in Nashville just as she had in her native Florida. Her career is built upon her unwavering commitment to the client and her love of people and the art of photography. Though a seasoned pro, O’Neal takes on new projects with fresh energy—always excited about the next story to be told. The result is a unique style of imagery that shows compassion, strength and beauty.

Clients include: American Spirit Magazine, Averitt Express, Baptist Hospital, Black Enterprise Magazine, Boy Scouts of America, Bruger Bagels, Business Finance Magazine, CBS, CFO Magazine, CIO Magazine, Cirque Du Soleil, Darden Restaurants, Florida Sports Network, Gateway Health System, Gaylord Entertainment, Georgia Trust Bank, Golf Magazine, Guidpost Magazine, Harcourt Inc., Hard Rock Cafe, INC Magazine, Kodak, Lifeway Christian Resources, Murcury Outboards, Nashville Predators, O'Charleys, Scholastic Book Fairs, Symantic, The Discovery Channel, The National Arthritis Foundation, Titans of Nashville, United Way, University of Central Florida, Vanderbilt Center for Better Health, Vogue Magazine, Warner Chappell Music

Sheri Oneal’s Specialties:

Offering a full service studio or the ability to work on location, digital and film formats, creative intuition, production of high quality imagery, attention to detail and impeccable service.
