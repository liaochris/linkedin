Dominic Arena’s Summary

Dominic Arena is a Director of KPMG’s Business Performance Services Advisory group. He is National Leader for Risk Advisory Services on the ICE Steering Committee and Head of ICE Advisory for the Business Performance Services practice. He has a proven track record in developing corporate, competitive, operational and business unit strategy and in implementing follow-through strategic and operational projects including business transformation, product-customer-channel profitability analysis, performance improvement and new business launch projects.

Over the past 13 years Dominic has worked across Australia, Europe and Asia. He has held senior local, regional and international expatriate roles in operations, strategy, commercial and technology development with global companies Vodafone and Orange SA, and professional advisory firms KPMG and BearingPoint, Inc. As a highly credible and internationally-experienced executive and advisor with a track record of delivering tangible results to companies, investors and Government, he regularly engages & advises clients at CxO and Board level.

Dominic Arena’s Specialties:

•Trusted business advisor and practical implementer in strategy & business planning, product & customer profitability analysis, performance improvement and business transformation projects - corporate & business unit operations, retail and wholesale channels
•Operations/P&L management and business performance improvement
•Market penetration and product innovation across Asia Pacific and Europe
•Commercial advisory for emerging consumer, demographic & technology trends
