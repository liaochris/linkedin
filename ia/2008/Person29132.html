Ben Weisman’s Summary

What I do:
Equal parts Digital Strategist, Creative Director, Creative Developer & Motion Theorist. Essentially, I plan & oversee projects & look for new, innovative ways to approach promotions & brand marketing. It’s been my responsibility to trailblaze, following consumers into new, often pre-commoditized landscapes & determine the opportunities & execute on it. Most recently, my efforts have been focused on social media & traditional media integration, allowing for immersive & hyper-targeted executions. Several tactics I conceptualized & co-developed have lead to client success stories & valuable agency case studies, as well as interaction reports that further influence, enhance & perfect user experience. Integrating with established social platforms (MySpace/Facebook/Google) & creating mash-ups with widget technology, I’ve had the chance to lead development teams on fun, creative executions that actually reach the user base.

Management Style
My management style takes different approaches depending on the team and project. In some instances, I’m an enabler, allowing my teams to fulfill their roles and tasks autonomously. Other projects require more directly guided, focused performance work. However, I always believe reassurance and encouragement as gateways to better work. Basically, I fluidly shift between four basic teaching styles depending on the team and work: Formal Authority, Demonstrator, Facilitator and Delegator.
