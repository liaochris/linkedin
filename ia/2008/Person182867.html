Fashion Stylist Michelle Washington’s Summary

Michelle is "the" Fashion Stylist with distinct skills and knowledge - a true fashion professional who has fun with clients while doing serious work. Michelle's forte is in creating a "TRUE STYLE" for clients – which means "being clever" and seeking out the "hottest" styles.

Michelle earned her Bachelors Degree in Fashion Design (soon after, earned a Masters Degree), she was a previous Adjunct Fashion Instructor for CCAD (Columbus College of Art & Design), and she was recognized in the 2006/2007 Manchester Who's Who for Executives and Professionals.

Michelle's fashion knowledge stems from years of fashion experience and a diverse history with various brand names and designer labels. She has worked for:
*Haggar

*Federated Merchandising Group
*Polo Jeans
