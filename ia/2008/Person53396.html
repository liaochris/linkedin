Mark Donovan’s Summary

Mark has over eleven years of executive level leadership experience building viable businesses and robust, network-related application products in varying verticals such as Healthcare, Education, Entertainment and Residential Real Estate. Prior to joining GoMed, Mark was CEO for Flyfone, Inc., an international provider of Voice-over-IP (VoIP) managed services. Under Mark’s leadership, Flyfone expanded its customer base to over 65 countries around the globe. As a founding executive and SVP of Operations for Rent.com, Mark was responsible for the entire scope of operations including: Product Development, Strategic Analysis, Sales, Customer Service and Human Resources. In March of 2005, eBay Inc. (Nasdaq:EBAY) acquired Rent.com for $415 Million. Mark is recognized for creating innovative, technology-based business opportunities through seemlessly integrating the executive suite, operations and technology groups. He thrives in effective strategic execution within aggressive implementation timelines. Mark’s professional experience covers Fortune 100 Corporations, the White House, leaders in the tech sector and entertainment industry.
