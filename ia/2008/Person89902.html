Jeff Schultz’s Summary

Senior marketing, product management, and business development executive with experience at startups and large corporations. Successfully launched and repositioned several companies, launched new product and service offerings, driven corporate and market strategy, and delivered substantial returns to shareholders.

Strong technical background including a degree in Electrical Engineering (with a focus on electron devices) and direct experience with product development, product support, and field services.

Passionate about adopting newer approaches to marketing, including the use of Blogs, Podcasts, Internet Advertising, Digg, del.icio.us, social networking sites (e.g., Facebook), to create a market conversation, generate awareness, build influencer relationships, and increase press and analyst exposure. Extensive experience implementing salesforce and marketing automation systems.

Ironman Triathlete (IM Wisconsin 2006) and amatuer photographer. Currently training for Ironman Arizona 2008.

Jeff Schultz’s Specialties:
