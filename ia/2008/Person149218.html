Rick Penn-Kraus’s Summary

Rick Penn-Kraus’s Specialties:

Art direction, production, design (logos, brochures, posters, key art, advertisements, books, packaging, web), illustration, photography, vendor relations, managing staffs, cartooning, fine art, writing, press checks, storyboarding concepts, editing, guitar playing/recording. Juror for national design/advertising/marketing competitions.

Teaching design/illustration to all levels of students, from 4th grade all the way up to graduate students at the Annenberg School for Communication at USC.

  • Owner

    RPK Design (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Graphic Design industry)

    1984 — Present (24 years)

    I have worked on numerous projects that include logo design, book design, catalog design, poster design, illustration and photography. Clients include WPP (international advertising/marketing/media holding company), UC Press (publishing), Reader's Digest, Tallfellow Press (publishing), Chalekian Management (finance), CareerFair (career events)

  • Group Manager of Visual Communications Dept.

    Weber Shandwick Worldwide

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    April 2007 — November 2007 (8 months)

    My role as group manager of the Visual Communications department includes coordinating projects with the graphics staff, as well as designing projects (brochures, websites, advertisements, illustrations, etc.) for numerous clients.

  • Design Director

    Hill & Knowlton

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    January 2001 — February 2007 (6 years 2 months)

    As design director in Hill & Knowlton’s Los Angeles office, I oversaw the creative department and coordinate with other H&K offices worldwide to provide comprehensive design services for overall art direction as well as quality control for all graphic design campaigns. In addition, I also produced original video projects, including filming, storyboarding, original music, editing, and do illustration and photography.

    Clients included Motorola, state of California, HP, Wal-Mart, BFGoodrich Tires, Adidas, University of California, Allied Domeq (Malibu Rum, Stoli), Tournament of Roses, PG&E, Avery Dennison, Feld Entertainment (Ringling Brothers), government of Thailand, Greeting Card Association, 7-11, Coldwell Banker, Mary Kay, Pfizer

  • Creative Director

    NewStar Media/Dove Audio

    (Graphic Design industry)

    1994 — 2000 (6 years)

    As creative director at book and audio publisher NewStar Media for more than five years I designed award-winning books for adults and children, as well as hundreds of audio book covers and movie posters; my designs have appeared on the covers of numerous local and national bestsellers.

    Regularly attended Book Expo, the industry trade show, in Chicago and Los Angeles to cultivate artist relationships and review latest publishing trends and publications.

  • Art Director

    Bruce Kennedy & Associates

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1993 — 1994 (1 year)

    I was responsible for creating ads, designing logos, and designing magazine layouts. In charge of model research. Wrote movie reviews and other columns on a regular basis.

  • Art Department Supervisor & Senior Designer

    Price Stern Sloan

    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; Publishing industry)

    1986 — 1993 (7 years)

    Price Stern Sloan is known around the world as a leader in humor publications, including the Murphy's Law series of books and calendars, Droodles, the Crazy Game puzzle series, Mad Libs, cookbooks, automotive and photography books, and many more. I created the designs and illustrations for many of these projects, as well as supervised the art department and coordinated with the sales department. I authored a humor book, "The Desk Book," which went to a second print run after selling out the first run.

  • Art Department Assistant

    California Magazine

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Publishing industry)

    1984 — 1986 (2 years)

    Assisted the creative director and art directors in magazine layout production. Responsible for photo research, archiving. Developed in-house newsletter.
