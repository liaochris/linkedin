Tony Barnes’s Summary

Unparalleled cross-cultural experience gained over many years in private equity, corporate finance and retained executive search with JP Morgan and Korn/Ferry International in the United States, Europe, Asia and Africa. Unique early career exposure to emerging markets. Recently founded Lydden Partners in Seattle after building successful professional firms in New York and London with offices in Europe and Asia.

The Partnership offers due diligence and strategy consulting expertise for private equity funds, financial services organizations and multinational companies. We work as one with our clients in designing innovative strategies for their most challenging management issues such as those arising in M&A, cross-border capital investment, restructuring and turnaround situations.

Lydden Partners has long experience in managing complex international assignments involving transaction participants from widely different geographies and cultures whose organizations invariably reflect a near unfathomable spaghetti of geographic and business sector reporting lines. Such assignments may require comprehensive 360° management due diligence done in complete secrecy and within extremely tight time frames on the track record and reputation of key executives in the context of their current and former employments.

This challenging task is performed by experienced professionals using proven high end executive search sourcing techniques beginning with confidential one on one interviews with current and former colleagues, competitors, clients, suppliers, joint venture partners, professional services suppliers, industry experts, securities analysts, investment bankers, financial advisors, business journalists, management consultants, and colleagues from industry associations, community groups and other non-profit organizations.

By definition this work is highly sensitive, exceptionally time intensive, fiercely demanding on the client organization, and not cheap.

Tony Barnes’s Specialties:

Thirty-five years in global private equity investing and strategic advice. Clients have included fund sponsors, general partners, limiteds, secondaries, placement agents and gatekeepers. Practical experience in fund design, international sourcing advisory, effective reward mechanisms and partner search.
