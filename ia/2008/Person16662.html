Arun Cavale’s Summary

Arun K Cavale is currently the Market Intelligence, Analysis and Strategy leader for IBM's SMB-focused businesses across the Asia Pacific region, and is based in Singapore. He has a broad and varied industry background in the areas of Business analysis & planning, Strategy development & execution, Market Intelligence, and Corporate performance management across Technology, Transportation & Logistics, and Manufacturing industries. He has led several internal consulting projects in designing and delivering change and thought leadership programs. He has engaged with several leading management & strategy consulting firms on various strategy initiatives. In addition, he has also independently consulted on go-to-market strategy and process reengineering engagements for FMCG and non-profit organizations. His areas of expertise range from defining strategies to improve organizational effectiveness to identifying / managing the change effort required to achieve desired business results.

Arun has an MBA from the S P Jain Institute of Management & Research, Mumbai (a top-10 b-school in India) and a Bachelors degree in Engineering from BMS College of Engineering, Bangalore.
