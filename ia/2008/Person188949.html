Tim El-Sheikh’s Summary

With experience in biomedical sciences (specialising in genetics, molecular biology and cytopathology/oncology), I have developed a passion for computing technologies and IT, with particular interest towards the Internet. This was due to my college and university studies, as well as my early career in biomedical science, which involved the use of numerous bioinformatics tools and clinical data management systems. The Internet was an important part in this work which encouraged me to undertake various training programs, which helped me to obtain my masters degree in Information Technology, specialising in Online Multi-tier Systems Design and Architecture.

My experience with the Internet (as a power user, a scientist and a developer) helped me to design and develop OBBeC online biotechnology magazine (www.obbec.com) as a pilot project in 2003. In 2004, I co-founded OBBeC Media Publications Ltd, where my role as a publisher involved transforming OBBeC from a basic concept to an extensive and a well respected information source serving the biotechnology and life science computing/Bio-IT sectors through brand and business development, marketing strategy and establishing new editorial and PR leads.

After one year of its release, OBBeC has attracted major PR support from such names as Agilent, IBM, Frost & Sullivan, EBI, and many other key players in the industry. Additionally, OBBeC established an average monthly circulation of over 20,000 C-level users and was invited to join the Periodical Publishers Association (PPA) in the UK at the end of 2005.

Through investment funding in 2006, OBBeC Media expanded into deSciner Business Network, and since then we developed OBBeC into a new advertising and marketing platform (code named OBBeC 2.0) attracting advertisers and sponsors of all sizes, from SME's to multi-billion dollar enterprises. Further information available at: www.obbec.com/b2b

Tim El-Sheikh’s Specialties:

My experience has expanded into online BtoB publishing, global business and brand development, Web 2.0/Semantic web, BtoB marketing strategy for vertical markets, online systems architecture, business investment and corporate partnerships. Main sector interests include biotechnology, bioinformatics, pharmaceuticals, computing and online media.
