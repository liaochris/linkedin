Jeffrey Kane’s Summary

For over 20 years, I've owned and managed small businesses. As with any small business, everyone must wear many hats.

Because I always wanted my companies to have the latest technical edge, one of the hats I often wore was the 'tech guy'. Over the years I have written programs, developed applications, installed networks, recovered data, replaced hardware, put out RFP's for infrastructure software, and even written a letter or two in WordPerfect on a mono-chrome monitor (I resisted Word for years).

Today, I am a Small Business consultant providing technology based solutions centered on Microsoft's Small Business model (Small Business Server 2008, Windows Vista, Microsoft Office 2007 for Small Business, Office Accounting Professional 2008, Business Contact Manager or Microsoft CRM). Specializing in these products allows us to provide greater value to our clients.
