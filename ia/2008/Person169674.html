Sara Stelwagen’s Summary

Sara Stelwagen's is currently the assistant production office coordinator for the Hallmark movie "Miles From Nowhere" starring Treat Williams and Andrew Lawrence. Additional industry experience includes various positions on "I Want To Work For Diddy" (2008), Game Show In My Head" (2008), "My Dad Is Better Than Your Dad" (2008), "American Gladiators" (2008), "Student Body" (2008), "Shear Genius 2" (2008), "Nashville Star" (2008), and the NBC television pilot "Welcome to the Family" (2008).

From April 2004 - September 2007, she was program coordinator for Leadership Grand Rapids (LGR), a program of the Grand Rapids Area Chamber of Commerce. LGR is a community leadership program committed to enhancing and connecting leaders from business, nonprofits and government. In this role, she was responsible for all of the details relating to Leadership Grand Rapids – The Series, Inside Grand Rapids, LGR’s Membership Program, and other programs and events. Prior to this position, she served as office manager for Community Counseling, a non-profit counseling center recently acquired by Project Rehab.

Sara completed prerequisite work for her Master of Business Administration at Grand Valley State University in Grand Rapids. She received her undergraduate degree in communications from the University of Michigan – Ann Arbor.

In August 2006, Sara took on a volunteer leadership role with the West Michigan Film & Video Alliance (WMFVA) to develop the West Michigan Film Commission and was elected to the WMFVA board of director in January 2007. Additionally, she served on the Maverick’s PAC board of directors and helped plan the 2006 Patriot Day Parade with the Grand Rapids Jaycees.

In her spare time, Sara enjoys travel, acting, golf, exercise and watching films with her husband, Matt, and Chihuahua, Tucker.
