*********************** Emerald Resource Group ************************

Emerald Resource Group is an Information Technology recruiting firm.

I recruit IT professionals for direct hire (permanent), contract, and contract-to-hire positions with companies that range in size from the Fortune 500 to start-ups. Working both sides of the desk, I work with hiring managers to fully understand the job descriptions and then personally recruit the candidates to fill the positions.

Hiring Managers: You are probably very busy with your work and personal schedules. Don't waste your valuable time sifting through hundreds of resumes and interviewing numerous candidates that are not qualified, serious, and in the compensation range for your budget. Let me save you the time, money, and frustration. My specialty is finding and recruiting your "perfect candidates" and screening them to make sure they are qualified, interested, and motivated about making a change. Call me at 440-922-9000 x103 for a free consultation.

IT Professionals: If you would like to confidentially find out more about career opportunities that could potentially make you happier, advance your career, and increase your compensation, call me at 440-922-9000 x103. If you email a MS Word resume to Sean@EmeraldResourceGroup.com, I will call you to follow-up. You will not pay anything for this service; it is entirely free to you.
