John Christiansen’s Summary

I have practiced law since 1985, with an emphasis on information technology issues since the early 1990s. After practicing in large, national law firm and consulting firm environments for many years, I formed Christiansen IT Law in 2005 in order to practice more flexibly and serve my clients more directly and personally.

John Christiansen’s Specialties:

Privacy and security compliance and risk management.
Information technology contracting and management.
Confidential incident response support.
IT-related litigation and dispute resolution expert support.
