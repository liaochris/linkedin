Dulama Radu’s Summary

During over 9 years IT experience in different companies, I’ve been in several positions as follow:

Project Manager / Team Leader – I led several teams for different applications. The teams were small, composed from 3-4 software developers and eventually 1 tester. I was in charge with analyzing and transforming customer’s requirements in software specifications, design, organize, distribute and monitor software development process according with RUP methodology; also maintain the permanent contact with the client in order to present the project progress and status.

Software Designer and Architect – my work consisted in translating different customer requirements into software modules, by analyzing first and then finding the best way to transform them easily into viable solutions mapped as good as was possible to the project costs and resources according with best practices.

Software Developer – I was in charge of developing both specific software modules and software applications from scratch.

Customer Support – my work involved a good communication with clients at their sites or remotely, in order to support and mitigate their problems as fast and efficiently was possible.

System Administration – I was in charge of installing different operating systems and software packages, administrating both LAN network and single computers. My work in this area consisted in installations, administrations, patching different operating systems and software modules or databases components.

Documentation Writer – This was mostly part of my Project Management job and I have written different types of documents such as: software requirements and functional specifications, high level designs, technical specifications, installation and operation guides, release notes, project presentations, demo scenario, etc.
