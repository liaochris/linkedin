Peter Sorenson’s Summary

I promote Communication and Design courses around the world, recruiting from China, India, Singapore, Malaysia, Taiwan, Japan, Korea, Thailand, Denmark, Sweden and Norway. I negotiate with many agents, organizations and individuals with high levels of success, consistently recruiting above targets.
