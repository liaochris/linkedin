Julian Holmes’s Summary

Julian has a proven track record in enhancing the software development capability of large and complex IT organisations. These skills stem from the experience of performing many different software development roles over his 15 years working within the industry, providing him recognition as a proven software development leader.

Julian adopted the Rational Unified Process as his approach of choice in 1999, becoming quickly regarded as a leader as to its successful use, and in its deployment for project teams and organisations alike. This has been demonstrated most recently by Julian's work with Capgemini, in the transformation of not only the Capgemini organisation into an IBM Rational reference site, but also Capgemini clients who have had to learn how to collaborate with Capgemini through the use of RUP.

Julian has also developed his standing within the software development community through initiatives including:
- His work with the invitation-only IBM Rational Methods Customer Advisory Group
- Presentations at many conference and seminar events
- His involvement in the Eclipse Process Framework Project
- Publications in industry journals

Julian Holmes’s Specialties:

Julian can bring many services to any organisation, complex, large or small, including his specialities of: Mentoring, training, and strategic planning for the use and adoption of software development practices including those from IBM RUP, Agile methods, Iterative Project Management, and Software Development Team Leadership.

  • University Qualified Facilitator

    Capgemini Group

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; FR:CAP; Information Technology and Services industry)

    February 2004 — Present (4 years 11 months)

    Julian joined the education team at Capgemini's global University when he wrote and started to deliver a training course called the RUP Academy.
    This one-week residential course, primarily delivered at Les Fontaines in France (http://www.les-fontaines.com), provides experienced software engineers the opportunity to learn new techniques from RUP, together with addional practices from more Agile approaches, and standards from Capgemini that enable successful project delivery.
    Since its pilot Julian has also introduced the course to other countries including Australia, USA, India, and the UK, where it continues to be a success.
    Julian now holds a senior position within the education team, advising on the development and delivery of the complete Software Engineering curriculum, whilst continuing to deliver courses for Capgemini around the world.

  • Software Development Process Consultant and Mentor

    Capgemini UK plc

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; FR:CAP; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2001 — April 2007 (5 years 8 months)

    Julian joined Capgemini to become a leadership member of the UK Accelerated Delivery Centre (ADC), with the primary task of transforming the delivery capability of the centre in support of many client projects from across many industry sectors.

    In February 2004, he was asked to join Aspire, Capgemini's IT delivery organisation for Her Majesty’s Revenue and Customs (HMRC), which employs more than 3,000 permanent staff. This required Julian to help EDS start the transformation of their software development capability prior to the transition to Capgemini in July 2004. Since that transition Julian has continued with the enhancement of Aspire's internal capability, specifically in the use of the IBM Rational solutions.

    Julian is a valued and top-tier member of Capgemini's Software Engineers community and a Capgemini "flying squad" member, used to address international "problem" projects and place them back on course for successful delivery.
