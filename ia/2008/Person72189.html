Greg Bolton’s Summary

After many years as a copywriter and creative strategist in the online space, I made the highly logical next step - opening Pantry, a fine food take-out and gourmet grocery with my wife, Liz.

We specialize in good honest food - organic and farm-raised whenever possible - that offers restaurant-quality cuisine that you can eat in the most relaxed venue in town: your own home.

Our aim is to help people eat better every day, and to do it at reasonable prices.
