Taylor Malbon’s Summary

Madison+Main, Advertising & New Media is a full-service advertising agency and new media firm that specializes in emerging companies between 15 and 1,500 employees. The company is based in Richmond, Virginia, with an additional office and San Diego. Clients include The Greater Richmond Chamber, Big Brothers Big Sisters, The American Civil War Center, Getloaded.com, Children, Inc., Shore Bank, Baby Jogger, Bag Boy and many others.
For more information on Madison+Main text NEWMEDIA to 68247 or visit www.madisonmain.com.
