Christina Hoggatt’s Summary

Public Relations professional and social media enthusiast. I am currently writing my thesis over the diffusion of social media tools in PR focusing on measuring interactivity and actual dialogue. The goal is see if there is a direct correlation with the amount/type of dialogue and the level of relationships with publics. I will be conducting qualitative and quantitative research through expert interviews and content analysis of successful social media initiatives.
