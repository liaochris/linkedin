Nigel Richards’s Summary

Founder director of executive-i.com, the executive search network for senior professionals throughout the UK.

Instrumental from design through to managing the operation of this leading senior executive portal which has the most extensive range of senior level opportunities throughout the UK.

About executive-i.com

executive-i.com is an executive job-site enabling experienced executives and leading corporates/ search firms to network, and providing a comprehensive source of executive jobs, market information and career advice throughout the UK.

Our innovative business model enables leading corporates and search firms to market their jobs and headhunt executives free of charge.

Our research team proactively scours the market for job leads giving executive jobseekers a comprehensive snapshot of their market, saving them time.

Our latest Corporate Plus option gives our corporate members even more exposure allowing them to highlight their jobs and brand on the public site, enhancing their response rates even further still.
