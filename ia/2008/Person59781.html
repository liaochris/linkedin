Eric Lawrence Frazier’s Summary

Mr. Frazier joined Wells Fargo Home Mortgage as a Vice President and Branch Manager on May 16, 2008 and brought his entire production team of Loan Consultants and support staff with him. Prior to Wells Fargo Home Mortgage, he enjoyed a very successful 11.5 year career in Mortgage Banking with Washington Mutual Home Loans as a Vice President and Senior Home Loan Center Manager. His career includes a total of 26 years in sales management and marketing. Currently he successfully manages 15 Home Mortgage Consultants, 7 Home Mortgage Assistants, and 3 Customer Service Representatives for the North Orange County Markets Wells Fargo Home Mortgage Centers. His base operation is the La Habra Home Loan Center. At Washington Mutual Mr. Frazier successfully revitalized his market area through recruiting and increased loan production. His mortgage lending production grew from 4 million a month in Loan volume in 1997, to an high of 95 million per month in 2006. In 2005, he achieved a new milestone in funding over 1.14 Billion in mortgage volume and has average 1 billion a year in production from 2003 to 2007. He was ranked #1 in Orange County and #3 in the nation for the last 5 years in production volume and units while at Washington Mutual.

Eric Lawrence Frazier’s Specialties:

Expertise in sales management, operations, human resources, customer relations, developing loan consultants, business and marketing plans. Extensive knowledge in all types of mortgages – fixed to variable, purchase and refinance transactions, FHA and VA, city and government DPA Programs. I am an expert in the development and implementation of winning sales strategies; establishing Realtor relationships, conducting investor and Realtor seminars and public speaking.
