Sunil Gupta’s Summary

An IT professonal having 25 years of industry experience and has successfully build and created software companies and leaders in the enterprise and business application domain. Has been involved in conceiving, analyzing, leading, designing, selling, managing, implementing and innovating software solutions and products.

Visionary in creating value foundations to alter the competitive landscapes and amplify sources of competitive advantage through technology, steering high performance technology organizations with an entrepreneurial energy, strategic mindset, fueled with an innovative and intellectual capability and strong business acumen.
Founded the first SAP offshore development and Application Management Company in India in the year 1995 and successfully lead the company to grow and create market leadership and credible brand in SAP consulting, services and add-on products business.

Exceptionally skilled in bridging the gap between business and technology, in providing elegant technical solutions to business needs. Skilled with superior people and project management, leadership, communication, presentation and inter-personal skills to set high standards of excellence both for himself and his team and has build, retained and motivated highly skilled IT professionals.

Zealous adherence to cost and unwavering commitment to quality and made significant positive impact to the organization and various professional associations. Built and continues to enjoy exceptionally good corporate relationship with the customers, partners and all the employees.
Hands on experience in mergers and acquisition activities and has successfully build and created values and wealth for the IT companies.
