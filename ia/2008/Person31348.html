Carolyn Reis, APR’s Summary

Carolyn Reis, APR, is a nationally accredited public relations consultant with more than 25 years of experience. Her consulting practice specializes in delivering results-focused strategic communications plans and effective corporate communications writing. Her career has focused on clients in the areas of business-to-business, infrastructure and health care including professional service firms ranging from architecture to law, banking and financial institutions, health systems and providers, public and private educational institutions, municipalities and quasi-governmental organizations.
