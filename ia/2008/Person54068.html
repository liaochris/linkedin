Dan Petrescu’s Summary

Creative, results-focused leader with an international record of profitable growth achieved by building and managing productive teams that get products to market ahead of the competition with significant volume ramp in the first year of introduction.
Dan's experience spans from pre-revenue high technology start-ups to mid-size public companies to Fortune 100 multi-nationals.
Dan has more than 25 years of experience in the manufacturing industry and has expertise in the fabrication and market introduction of fiber optics/photonics equipment and other telecommunications products. At Nortel, he was responsible for the manufacturing of multiple product platforms supporting integrated data network solutions for carriers and packet carriers He also has managed the development and introduction of the Nortel's highly successful OC-192 Sonet platform, which, along with Nortel's other Sonet solutions, enhances bandwidth and mapping capabilities in fiber optic networks.

Prior to joining Nortel, Petrescu served in a number of management positions with Sigma International in Quebec, ROM-Control Data in Bucharest, Romania, and IPRS Baneasa, also in Bucharest. He has a Bachelor of Science degree in electrical engineering from the Polytechnic Institute of Bucharest, Romania, and a Diploma of Management from McGill University in Montreal, Canada.
