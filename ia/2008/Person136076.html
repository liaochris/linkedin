Dan Banici, CESSP’s Summary

Dan helps business owners with exit strategies (valuating and selling their business properly) and also with sales, direct marketing and operational enhancements through automation solutions. If you are a "black belt" business or sales executive, or a professional with business clients, we're looking for a few exceptional people.

LOOKING FOR ADDITIONAL INCOME? Consider being a certified Option Quest agent. Be engaged in a profession in constant demand, which can greatly supplement your current income. We're talking about 5 digit commission checks 6-12 times a year from brokerage deals, plus other well-paid services you can offer.

DIRECT MARKETING HYBRID ENHANCEMENT
Option Quest has devised "hybrid" data marketing, an affordable method of offering unique personalized landing pages to mail recipients regardless of the size of your database. Coupled with a streamlined lead management database, the solution saves you and your fulfillment house time, raises customer perception of your firm, reduces anxiety and friction therefore greatly increasing your rate of response to otherwise good offers, and reducing your minimum sampling size requirements (cost of market testing). All inclusive for small per-campaign fee. If you do Direct Marketing without this, you're losing big money.
