Matt Barry’s Summary

Matthew Barry is from Philadelphia, PA. He started his career by working at a start-up marketing company that had a contract with the cable companies to sell the then-new broadband cable internet services. The company grew from just a few employees working out of the basement to a large 100+ organization. He moved on to study Information Systems at Drexel University on academic scholarship. During his full-time schooling, he has worked full-time at IT-related jobs at such companies as Comcast and SAP.

Towards the end of his degree, he worked nights and weekends at 1&1 Internet as it started its new US office in new the customer call center. After just a few months, Matt was sent to the UK to learn about a new project launch and spent several weeks assisting in the early stages of an outsourced call center in Cebu, Philippines.

After obtaining his degree, he applied to become the first Product Manager in the US and spent several months in Germany training for this role. After two years as Product Manager, he left his home in Philadelphia, PA to head for rival Network Solutions in the Washington, D.C. area where he worked on the Web Hosting Product Management team focussing on communication products.

Since March 2008, he has moved to London area to take a position as the Head of Product Management for the a market leader in hosting and domains in the UK. Less than three months into the job, he was also given the opportunity to head the New Media department. His unique blend of experience in customer service, sales, information technology, marketing, new media and product management, along with a strong in-depth knowledge and experience working within the top hosting companies makes him a perfect fit for this role.

Matt enjoys learning new things, travelling, and live music.
