Travel 2.0: Interactive Trend Report
http://travel2dot0.wordpress.com/

Originally created to provide insight into the interactive marketing and travel fields for the Arizona Office of Tourism, the Travel 2.0 blog has expanded to include hundreds of readers from various disciplines in the travel industry. In collaboration with my colleague, Mo Sherifdeen from Travel Oregon, the blog presents views and opinions on the ever-changing interactive marketing landscape, including subjects such as the latest travel trends, online (and offline) advertising campaigns, Google, user-generated content, airlines and social networking.
