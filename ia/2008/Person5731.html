Aldo Ferrari’s Summary

My name is Aldo Ferrari, I’m a 26 years old and I’m a Senior Web Designer /Developer. I live in Turin (Italy), working for a large communication company (TestawebEdv, ArmandoTesta group)

I love to work on web technology and interactivity: in my opinion web and graphic design is something more than just a job, it is a hobby and there is nothing more satisfactory that working on what you are interested in. I am extremely creative, imaginative, hardworking and devoted to the projects I am working on.

I worked in different companies for many years, so I am proficient in modern graphic techniques and current trends used both in web sites and interaction products, in particular I have more than 5 years experience in Multimedia & Web Technology. I’m fluent in HTML and CSS coding, expert of Flash design & development, as1 as2 as3 coding.
