Gregory Jones’s Summary

Gregory Jones has a Marketing degree (with emphasis in Corporate Communications) degree from Southern Methodist University. He has worked in numerous industries, from telecommunications to investments to global finance.

His greatest strength is his ability to leverage best practices from multiple industries and disciplines to provide quality analytics, insights and program/campaign development initiatives that produce increased sales, customer loyalty and brand recognition. He delivers results.

A great angle in Gregory’s skill-set is that he has the ability to create and delve into key sales performance and financial metrics; then identify gaps in marketing efforts; and finally, align the two into seamless marketing campaigns, pricing strategies and promotions.

Throughout his career, he has become accustomed to acting as a liaison for Marketing to Finance, Sales, IT and even HR.
