Sally Iwanski’s Summary

*SEMATECH in Austin, Texas since 1989. Director Assistant Front End Processes (FEP) 1990-1995. Assistant to the General Manager of I300I subsidiary and Start-Up team 1995-1998. Executive Assistant to COO 1998-2000. Returned to FEP Division 2001 as Director Assistant when COO left SEMATECH. United Way Department Coordinator, Project Manager for ITRS FEP Websites and webmaster for the FEP Internal website

* President, Minis and Friends, Inc - a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Co-Founder and Board Member in the creation and continuing development of this non-profit. Our mission is dedicated to bringing our "miniature horse (minis) ambassadors" to the community by creating an exceptional equine experience to eldercare, schools, medical facilities, special need camps, community and public events. www.minisandfriend.org. In 2008, our goals are to not charge anyone we visit a donation fee - and to find sponsors to fund our visits instead. 100% of donations go back to the daily care of the miniature horses.

* Volunteer Director/Board member, Hearts & Hooves, Inc. - a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Built all aspects of this non-profit into a full fledged business complete with advertising, website, marketing, press and media collections, fund-raising and collaborated in the creation of a curriculum to teach others to have their own 501(c)(3) miniature horse therapy businesses.

* Feis Chair, Lone Star Irish Dance, Inc. - a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that hosts an annual Feis (pronounced fesh) in Austin TX with up to 500 competitors and their families from 31 states, Canada and Mexico. As Chair organizing this event takes planning, delegation and creativity skills to run it effectively and efficiently. Update: In 2007, organized and co-chaired the first annual Shamrock Shuffle 5K Fun Run Walk and Irish Festival in Austin,TX - very successful with 500 participants and grossing $10K to help in the expenses of producing a successful Feis.
