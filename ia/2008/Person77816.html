Howard Olsen’s Summary

Howard Olsen is President and CEO of High Output Training Systems and is one of North America’s foremost sales performance experts. As a leading authority on the psychology of selling and the process behind peak sales performance, Howard’s gift lies in distilling the seemingly complex down to a simple process that can be easily learned and immediately applied. He’s a dynamic and engaging speaker equally comfortable in audiences of 5 and 5000. His specialy: arming his audiences with concrete ideas that get results quickly.

He has been a sales champion, coach and mentor his entire career spanning two decades. In his last corporate assignment prior to founding High Output Training Systems, he was stationed in Hong Kong as Global Account Manager with a $7 billion logistics company responsible for the Fortune 500 Hi-Tech sector world-wide. He's a consummate entrepreneur and is one of the most creative and high-impact business development consultants and trainers in the market today. In addition to teaching sales people he coaches other entrepreneurs to start and build their businesses.

With the experience of having worked in over 30 countries, many very different cultures, and a closing ratio in the 90% range, he still contends that 75 % of sales success is directly related to our ability to differentiate and to build trust and rapport.

Traveling with his wife Michaela and riding his Harley with dog Stanley on the back, are favorite pastimes, both of which provide relief from the pressures of a demanding sales training schedule.
