Menon V K’s Summary

Corporate Strategist turned Professor

As a Director- CAS( ISB), V K Menon is responsible for building relationships between the ISB and the corporate world. Menon has over twenty eight years of experience in the areas of Business Strategy, New Business Creation and Marketing with some of the best names in Corporate India both in the private and public sectors.

A postgraduate in management from the Indian Institute of Management (IIM-B), Bangalore, he headed the sales and marketing department of BPL. He also worked for Philips and Nelco.

After spending over a decade devising marketing strategies for corporates, V. K. Menon decided to don a different role.

During his tenure as professor of Marketing & Sales Management at SDM-IMD, a business school in Mysore, he put his practical experience to good use. Thereafter joined ISB as the Director of Admissions, Indian School of Business (ISB) . He was actively involved in creating awareness on the school's Post Graduate Programme (PGP) in management.

He also holds a Bachelors degree in Electronics Engineering from REC, Trichy
