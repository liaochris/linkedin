Rich Guard [LION] ►Rich@RichGuard.com◄’s Summary

Rich Guard is an internationally recognized IT professional who creates project execution solutions that significantly impact the quality of project completion.

Through the active monitoring of project risks while managing labor and capital resource allocation issues, Rich effectively implements proactive programs, processes and procedures that address issue trends before remedial action is required.

With 30+ years of management and leadership experience in Industrial and Systems Engineering, Operations Research / Management Sciences, and Information Systems Management, this accomplished executive is focused on implementing high-quality technology solutions to improve ROI measures, increase profitability and enhance organizational effectiveness.

Rich is also a professionally-trained Executive Coach whose coaching interventions apply innovative communication approaches that assist and support executives and business owners to accelerate the achievement of their next career step or business expanding accomplishment.

Rich Guard [LION] ►Rich@RichGuard.com◄’s Interests:

My work as a Management Consultant and Executive Coach can take me around the world, so when I am home in either San Francisco or Denver, I like to enjoy the local social, cultural and outdoor activities that both cities and their regions provide. When at home for a weekend (or a week) I like to go out with Jane, my wife, alone or together with a group of friends to one of the cities fine restaurants, followed by anything from a jazz or rock concert to a dance or comedy club to theater to ballet or opera. After I have been traveling, I enjoy staying home for the weekend to recharge by going to the gym for a workout, steam/sauna and massage, and then preparing and serving a quiet intimate dinner, and later viewing a DVD at home with Jane. On long weekends when I do have more energy, I enjoy city walks or bicycling to see the sights and watch people, and country hikes in one of the area’s many national, state, and county parks.
