Isaac Raichyk’s Summary

Mr. Raichyk is Chairman of Certainty Point Inc., a private holding company and undertakes selective strategic consulting assignments.

Mr. Raichyk founded Kolvox Communications Inc., an ASX listed company, and served as its Chairman and CEO from 1990 until it was sold in 1998. Kolvox pioneered automatic speech recognition applications and developed the first intuitive voice user interface for office applications. Kolvox reached market capitalization of $100 million and quickly became one of the world’s leading providers of voice enabled computer and telephony solutions. In 1997, the Company merged with Pure Data Inc. for combined sales of $28 million through a worldwide network of more than 500 resellers. LawTalk, OfficeTalk, VoiceCompanion and the Kolvox Telephony Suite were best of breed products in their respective markets. Kolvox customers included Fortune 1000 companies, technology leaders, healthcare providers, government agencies, the legal industry and other professions.

From 1998 and until it merged with a NASDAQ listed company in 2001, Mr. Raichyk was a major investor, Director and Chairman of Efax Communications Inc. of Oakland, California. Efax was a leading provider of computer based fax broadcasting services to businesses and consumers.

Mr. Raichyk was the founder, Chairmen and CEO of Simuplex Inc., an innovative e-learning company, until it was sold to The Learning Company Inc. in 2003. TLC was subsequently listed on the TSX.V.

Mr. Raichyk is a software developer by training. He was nominated for the Smithsonian Award for Excellence in Applied Technology and Entrepreneur of the Year.
