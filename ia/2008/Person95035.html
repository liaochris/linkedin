John James O'Brien, CRM, MA LT’s Summary

Consulting and organizational leadership experience in Asia and Canada.

Directorate and senior management experience including strategic planning, program development and evaluation in Hong Kong and Canada.

Board leadership in the professional and non-profit spheres.

Conference speaker, workshop facilitator skilled in open space, appreciative inquiry, mind mapping, and out of the box -discovering self- methodologies.

Teaching and curriculum development experience at the university and college levels (University of Victoria, Canada; Hong Kong University, Hong Kong Polytechnical University)

Partner, Garden Oaks Fine Accommodation, a residential property development and management (private) company.

At IRM Strategies, we are developing a new conceptual model for knowledge resource management, presented at KMAP 2006 and now grounding a book. Recent project activity includes readiness assessment at a top grossing Thai private hospital. My role was quality assurance on our assessment process, report and recommendations.

Currently creating distance education modules to ground & support records management professionals seeking to move into or upwards in management by preparing for the Certified Records Manager examinations administered by the ICRM.

2006 was start-up for IRM Strategies with dialogue with thought leaders from Sydney to Stockholm, development and testing of methods. We are proud to bring a rigorous, practically focused edge to our work. It is based on both consulting expertise and organizational leadership--where the rubber hits the road. You can pay many people to tell you what you want...we tell you what you need to know and build a roadmap for success--in partnership with you in that context.
