Sunita Badola’s Summary

Creative scientist and resourceful manager with 9 years of experience in biopharmaceutical industry. Recognized as a dynamic person with strong scientific and technical knowledge in transcriptional profiling, genetic analyses and target validation. Self-motivated, a recognized leader, an accomplished collaborator, and a flexible and dependable team player valued for diversity of knowledge and ability to achieve project goals. Earned a reputation as an excellent communicator, a proficient problem solver, and a well organized and competent manager.
