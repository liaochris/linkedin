* Advertising & marketing consultant, specializing in campaign management, point of sale design, letterheads, business cards, logos, emblems, mottos, creative scriptwriting & editing, print media presentations, multimedia presentations, website design & hosting etc ...

  • Chairman

    All India Collegium for Technical Education

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Civic & Social Organization industry)

    January 2006 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    The AICTE is a non-profit association of senior academics, philanthropists and eminent members of the community, dedicated to improving the standards of education in India. Our mission is to encourage the pursuit for excellence in education delivery throughout the entire Indian administration system (ie: from Govt downwards). We aim to ensure that world class standards of education and accountability are introduced throughout all education levels (primary, secondary, tertiary); that compliance to these standards is implemented & monitored vigorously; that more course options & flexible delivery strategies are introduced into the education system; and lastly, a national system for recognition of prior learning & cross-recognition of syllabi between secondary & tertiary institutions is established. The AICTE members have all pledged to work together to bring about significant change in India's education system. Join us if you feel you would like to contribute towards this endeavor!

  • Managing Director

    Indiaust Pty Ltd

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Higher Education industry)

    October 1996 — December 2002 (6 years 3 months)

    Indiaust Consultants was formed in Brisbane, Australia during my tenure serving as President of QTSAN and Chairman of the Southbank Institute Student Association.

    My exposure to academic circles all around Australia (including State Ministers of Education, Directors, Pro-Vice Chancellors etc ...) during this time provided the foundation of an education consulting company that went on to morph into INDOEURO Consultants.

    The name INDIAUST was created from a fusion of the first 4 letters of India & Australia ... since it was decided to target the fast growing Indian market. Indiaust (at the beginning) represented only Australian educational institutions in India.

    As the portfolio grew to more than 28 countries, some 700 educational institutions, and more than 7000 course options, it became clear that we should amend our name to reflect the concept of INDO-EUROPEAN education, immigration and employment services ... ergo INDOEURO was born.
