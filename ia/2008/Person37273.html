Chris Thomas’s Summary

With formal training as a PhD organic chemist, Chris Thomas has what it takes to pick apart any technical problem and get to the heart of the matter in short course. Coupled with his experience in customer service and teaching, Chris is well-equipped to relate abtruse information to specialist and lay audiences alike.

Chris is especially interested in social issues and the role that technology and management can combine to solve some of the most important problems affecting our world today. He is currently working in Sydney, Australia developing several projects in the environmental, publishing, and IT fields.

Chris Thomas’s Specialties:

Analysis and experiment design, process analysis. Data gathering and reporting, organic synthesis, purification and characterization, DNA synthesis and manipulation, literature research and review writing, editing/proofreading, computational chemistry, PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, purchasing and inventory, laboratory safety
