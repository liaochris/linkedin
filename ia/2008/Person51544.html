David Schmidt’s Summary

I am currently Vice President, Product for Ecast, the largest out-of-home interactive media network in America. Ecast serves digital content and advertising to more than 10,000 touch screen devices in out-of-home locations. Ecast is venture-backed by Mobius, Crosslink Capital, DCM, El Dorado Ventures, Escalate Capital Partners, and Focus Ventures.

At Ecast, I lead Product Management, Product Marketing, Creative Services, and Advertising Operations. I recently integrated DoubleClick’s DART Enterprise ad-serving technology with Ecast’s platform to create the largest interactive out-of-home advertising network.

Prior to joining Ecast, I was an Engagement Manager at Viant, were I led Internet strategy and implementation projects for clients in the financial services, high technology, retail, and business-to-business sectors. Before Viant, I was a Vice President at Bear, Stearns - a New York investment bank - where I rolled out global IT systems. I also worked at IBM helping to market PC software products, and at NASA doing quality assurance engineering for satellite systems. I have a BS in Electrical Engineering from Cornell University and a MBA in Marketing and International Business from New York University.

David Schmidt’s Specialties:

Strategy, product management, digital media, interactive advertising, product marketing, and user experience.
