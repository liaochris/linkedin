Eric Newman’s Summary

Eric rejoined Phoenix Technologies in mid-2006 to help return the company to sustainable profitability. Currently heading up operations in the Americas and EMEA, Eric’s responsibilities have included executing a significant restructuring of both regions, implementing new pricing strategies across both products and services, and re-prioritizing both the market and customer focus.

Prior to returning to Phoenix technologies, Eric was Vice President of Sales and Market Development for BDMetrics, where he had worldwide responsiblity for all sales and business development efforts. Before BDMetrics, Eric has held senior sales and business development positions for both private and public companies in such areas as security, wireless, disaster recovery, and core system software, including an earlier tenure running the business development team at Phoenix Technologies, where he was responsible for the global development and monetization of both hardware and software relationships. Eric has also guided the business and market development efforts of various other early stage companies, including his own company, LawConnect, Inc., which was acquired in 2000 by Sinar Mas Group.

Eric began his career as an attorney in the Bay Area, trying catastrophic injury cases, ultimately making the transition to counsel commercial clients on business issues including intellectual property, contract, ownership, and employment. Eric sits on the Advisory Board of Vigilant Privacy Corp. and BDMetrics and is a member of the State Bar of California. Eric holds a Juris Doctor degree from the University of San Francisco and a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Arizona.
