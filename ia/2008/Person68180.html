Grzegorz Bialek’s Summary

I've spent nearly seven years of my professional life creating some tools. I've been devoted to building tools not for programmers but to business people (analysts etc.) for the last five years. Today some of these tools are used to control digital exchanges in my country (Telekomunikacja Polska, Multimedia Polska,...) and the others will soon be used to create processes in financial domain.
