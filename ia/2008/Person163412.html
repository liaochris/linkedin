Rodney Orpheus’s Summary

Acted as evangelist and business development manager for several leading hi-tech music and entertainment companies, representing them at trade shows and seminars across the world. Produced about 40 records over the past 20 years, published a book, and been in anthologies several times. Written articles and edited magazines on music, gaming, and other subjects. Overseen development of several leading edge Internet sites and social communities. Currently very interested in Linux and open source software initiatives.
