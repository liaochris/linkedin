Fayyaz Alam’s Summary

Responsible for Management of IT for Operations division of Emirates Airline. Leading a team of over 140 Account Managers, Project Managers, Business Analysts and Software Engineers to deliver the strategy and agreed work program of over US$ 25 million per annum.
