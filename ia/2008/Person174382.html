Sharon Stiles’s Summary

I help people make positive changes in their lives by getting rid of what they don't want and achieving what they do want. I am based in central Bristol, UK but also do sessions online via webcam so can work all over the world. Details at www.mindblockssorted.com.

I use hypnosis, NLP and meridian energy. Typical clients include people who want to stop smoking, control their weight, increase their confidence or who want to get rid of anxiety, panic attacks, phobias or habits. I also teach women how to use hypnosis during childbirth and students how to use hypnosis to improve their studies.

Everyone is indivdual so I do not have a one method fits all approach. I combine my skills of hypnosis, NLP (neuro linguistic programming), Chinosis and EFT (emotional freedom technique) to find the most appropriate and effective way to help my clients achieve what they want. Find out more at www.sharonstiles.co.uk

My goals are to continue finding effective ways to help people make a positive change in their lives and also to help more people understand the power of the mind and what a difference it makes when it's working for you.

As well as working on an individual basis I do group meridian energy work as this is a low cost way for people to achieve significant change.

I also present hypnosis in a fun way - after all it's a great way of winding down and opening your imagination. Many people are uncertain about hypnosis because they think it's scary and that people will make fools of them. I always respect the people I hypnotise and never humiliate them. The group presentations and hypnosis evenings I do are a good way of introducing people to the power and fun of hypnosis, whether it's at a large corporate event or a small private party. Details are at www.hypnosisforfun.co.uk.

I also write articles about hypnosis and meridian energy. If you'd like one for your magazine or newsletter contact me at www.sharonstiles.co.uk.
