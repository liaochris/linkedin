Robert Corbett’s Summary

The Corbett Group partners with leaders to meet or exceed their organizational goals by inventorying and maximizing their strengths, improving the alignment between leader expectations and the performance of their teams, and improving the alignment between team members and team goals.

Bob Corbett is a seasoned consultant, a certified executive coach and a member of the International Coaching Federation. Bob is a seasoned practitioner and thought leader with 30+ years of experience in leadership, coaching and consulting roles in a variety of industries including manufacturing, technology, aerospace, healthcare, financial services, energy, education, consulting and governmental. He has effectively managed cross functional teams with as many as 90 members and in multiple countries.

A representative list of companies where he has served in a Human Resources leadership role, i.e., Vice President or Director, or a coaching/consultative role include, Price Waterhouse Coopers, The American Hospital Association, Exelon Corporation, Ernst & Young, Baxter Healthcare, TRW, Levi Strauss & Co., Computer Software Associates, Rexam Release, John Nuveen Company, Harris Bank, Hennepin County General Hospital, Provident Insurance Company, Towers Perrin, The Hay Group, the Chicago Public Schools, and the United States Armed Forces (i.e., Quadrennial Review).

He describes himself as a business generalist with Human Resources expertise. Consequently his focus on any assignment is to first thoroughly understand the nature and scope of the business, i.e., its vision, strategy, operations, challenges, the strengths and development needs of its management team, and its customers and competitive environment.

Robert Corbett’s Specialties:

Talent searches, global compensation & benefits, performance management, employee development, employee relations, succession planning, management training, team performance, meeting facilitation, acquisition planning and due diligence.
