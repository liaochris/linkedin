Alexander Goldman’s Summary

I taught a number of undergraduate courses at the University of Florida while pursuing my PhD in Sociology. I had extensive experience in the service industry during my teenage years. I volunteered for various nonprofits throughout my college years in Gainesville. I have been a group fitness instructor - yoga specifically. I am writing my dissertation and developing an innovative new online fitness site. I am working on other web projects as well, including an interactive website examining social problems and humanitarian tragedies and an open source social networking platform. My non-profit organization, The Evasion Foundation, facilitates the development of web applications that seek to improve the human condition. We provide financial support and virtual incubation services for social entrepreneurial projects.
