Erin Anthony’s Summary

*As the Creator and Founder of the renowned adaru Natural Hair Salon in Indianapolis, IN, Erin Shell Anthony was featured as a leading entrepreneur and business woman in the Indianapolis Woman and Power Magazines, as well as the Indianapolis Recorder newspaper. www.adaru.com

*Mrs. Anthony is the author of a new book entitled Nappturosity: How to Create Fabulous Natural Hair & Locs, to be released in January 2008. This book is a ground-breaking industry guide to achieving hair-peace, celebrating the beauty of textured hair, and understanding how to care for natural hair and locs. www.naturalhairbook.com

*Owner of Live Life Well, NOW!, LLC, a wellness consulting firm dedicated to helping women achieve financial and physical well-being. With a unique empowering style of speaking on business and wellness related topics, Erin Shell Anthony has provided more than 200 motivational and educational presentations to various business and community organizations in the last three years. www.livelifewellnow.com

*Corporate career in Brand Marketing encompassing positions at Pfizer, Inc. and Eli Lilly & Company, 1999-2004.
