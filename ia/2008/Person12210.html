Andy Blumberg’s Summary

Peer-credited vanguard of action sports media and community. With a solid commitment to content creation as a foundation, my works keenly assert active involvement and experience in marketing by way of various brand-, department-, and content- managing positions. My focus is brand development (ideation, product launch and placement, ongoing management), event conception, and public relations for consumer goods.

Today, I enlist marketing means and methods, new and traditional strategies that are intuitive and uniquely relative, making for a modern agenda in my approach. Insightful, conceptual, practical, passionate, communicative, consistent, and driven … I apply these traits and professionally gained knowledge to all projects I take on.

Andy Blumberg’s Interests:

My indoor environment consists of prints, books and paintings, current, post, graphic, storied. I am surrounded by an emergent collection of curiosities—items that are handmade, imaginative, pop smart, mid-century modern, fun, bright, dark, limited findings, premium works. An ever-changing soundtrack plays nonstop at low volume from my iPod or MacBook Pro. Kelly, my wife, is a luminary, designer, creative.
