Sean Smith’s Summary

I am a professional marketer with a specialisation in digital and new media looking to integrate these new marketing channels into the greater marketing universe with a leading-edge brand.

With a career spanning numerous fields including consumer banking, legal, sales, technology and entertainment, I have developed a strong overview of the commercial landscape.

By combining a consumer-centric focus with expertise in email marketing, website development, SEM, online advertising, mobile marketing, CRM, affiliate programs, online promotions and integration with traditional marketing activity, I look to find and influence the right audiences with relevant media.
