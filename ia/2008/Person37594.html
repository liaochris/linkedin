Cindy Andreatos ’s Summary

I have been in the Medical Industry now for nearly 25 years. I was a Radiation Therapist, Medical Biller and I sold a medical software package when medical offices first transformed themselves from manual to electronic submissions. It was then in the beginning when Medicare and Medicaid stated to received electronic submissions of claims. Then it fell into place. A variety of Private Insurance companies then saw the effectiveness of allowing for electronic submission of claims.

I so do enjoy helping the thousands of people in this country who have no medical coverage, those people who have high deductible Insurance policies. After being exposed to so many other Medical Discount Plans with components of insurance and evaluating all of their merits. I am proud to be an enrollment Specialist for The Design Savers Plan.

I believe in long-term relationships and that my job begins when you need service after the sale.

Please review the special features and benefits of Design Savers Plan. Feel free to contact me if you have more questions or, when you are ready, you may Enroll Online.

Thank you for the opportunity to meet you, network with you and bond with with you.
