Evelyn Wells, MD LION MyLink500 TopLinked’s Summary

I am the Owner and Medical Director of Physicians Fitness Solutions, LLC, a private wellness and weight management center located in beautiful Asbury Park, New Jersey. I promote healthy lifestyles and provide medical supervision and fitness counseling for those who want to attain or maintain a healthy weight. I provide a wide array of intensive testing for hidden food allergies, vitamin deficiencies, toxicity levels, hormone levels, BMR and BMI. My husband and I have both struggled with our weight in the past, so we have lived through the same struggles and temptations as most of our clients. We beleive in walking the walk as well as talking the talk. We are both available for 1 on 1, small and large group consultations. Our goals on LinkedIn is to share our experiences and gain from the experiences of others. Please feel free to invite me into your network or contact me in regards to anything that I may be of assistance with.

I am also a contributing author to www.JumpSnapNation.com and a Warm Spirit Distributor. Please visit those sites to gain a better understanding of what I do.
