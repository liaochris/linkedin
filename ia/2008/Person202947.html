Zack Gottlieb’s Summary

Zack Gottlieb is the Vice President of Product Development at the Professional Aptitude Council where he manages products development.

With over ten years working in the fields of interactive design and web strategy, Zack is an experienced professional who combines his passion for developing engaging and intuitive experiences for end users with a unique set of skills.

Professional Experience:
Prior to graduating UC Davis Zack worked as a director at MediaLink for two years during which time he coordinated the launch of ASUCD Online, the largest student run organization in the country. After graduation, Zack began his career as a project coordinator at Adjacency working with clients including Virgin Megastores, Specialized Bicycles, Powerbar, and Rollerblade.

At Sapient as a creative lead/project manager Zack began to manage larger and more complex web projects for a variety of clients that included main consumer sites for both Janus Financial and Nissan Motors.

In 2001, Zack opened Avatar Digital a small boutique web design firm specializing in developing web sites for entertainment and advertising companies. Avatar's clients included Lucas Arts, Electronic Arts, Hill | Holiday and the SF Giants.

Zack joined MetaDesign in 2004 as a Senior Project Manager / New Business. He led teams on a variety of different projects including mobile solutions for Nokia and Samsung and web projects including the redesign of PC World, Polo Ralph Lauren, and Shangri-La Hotels.

Zack Gottlieb’s Specialties:

Strategy and Planning, Project Management, Account Management, Business Development, Information Architecture, Experience Design, Brand Strategy
