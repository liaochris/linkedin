Dan Glegg’s Summary

Skilled application developer with substantial knowledge and experience gained from a wide variety of projects.

I derive great satisfaction from creating software beautiful both inside and out, and thrive in environments which encourage initiative and iteration. I'm happy to work and learn alone, and equally happy to have a team of interesting people to learn from.

I'm interested in web applications with novel social or technological aspects, especially those requiring the invention of new techniques or technology. I'm also particularly interested in online applications with heavy off-site integration, including widgets, desktop clients and other such tools. Chiefly, I adore solving large, horrible problems and crafting nice, smooth experiences for the people using the software we create as a result.
