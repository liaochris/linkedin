Clive Bishop.’s Summary

I operate a consultancy group called Blue Skies Consultancy Group that specialises in the travel and tourism industry both in the UK and Internationally. These services are generally provided to a wide range of companies in the travel and tourism fields from tour operators to tourist boards, hotels to cruise lines.

Our services are provided to assist clients in developing in an ever-changing industry. As no two businesses are a like, each approach and solution is tailored to meet the specified requirements.Key areas are:

Product Development - a service that is used to develop new services / packages for tour operators etc. This includes the contracting of services, development strategies and assistance with breaking into a new market with the formulated products.

Sales & Marketing - Consultants can provide project services in this field to help with company development on a one off project, as part of a long-term development plan or to test the market for new operations prior to a major recruitment drive.

Operational Management - This service specifically looks at making call centre/reservations offices/customer care operations more efficient by looking at ways of reducing cost of sale or increasing customer retention. Frequently this is where an existing project is failing or suffering from poor responses.

Management Training - complete range of "people skills" programmes each of which is designed to meet the specific needs of each client. In partnership with the client, objectives are taken and creative and positive ways are delivered to make these the learning focus.

Representation Services - This is very much a service provided for overseas companies looking to gain / investigate business form the UK and Ireland. Many projects are short-term investigations with the resources of Blue Skies being used to measure and attain business for the overseas clients, before they commit to growing their commitment to the UK and Irish market place.
