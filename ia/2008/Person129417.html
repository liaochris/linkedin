Michael Boylan’s Summary

Interior Designer with 25 years consulting experience within extensive multi-disciplinary project teams. In-depth knowledge of the construction industry with specific emphasis in the hospitality sector. Experienced in providing imaginative design solutions which are build-able. Familiar with working within the ever-reducing time frames available on today's projects. Knowledgeable in pushing technology to deliver better services.

Michael Boylan’s Specialties:

Business development, strategic vision, client liaison, design team liaison, construction team liaison, project management, team leading, studio productivity, package and deliverables management, fluent knowledge of the construction process and build-able solutions.
