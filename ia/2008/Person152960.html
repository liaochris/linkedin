Yves Pelletier’s Summary

Your Slide Production
Presentation specialists since 1992

At YSP we strive to achieve the perfect harmony between content and form to give your ideas maximum impact. This requires the capacity to analyze your data in order to present it as clearly, convincingly, and memorably as possible.

Years of experience and design excellence guarantee you that your concepts will be translated into a compelling, first-quality multimedia experience, weaving together text, charts, 2D & 3D graphics, sound and video to create an efficient and truly dynamic presentation.

We also take into consideration the particular conditions of your presentation. We make sure that your audience is deeply attentive, be they assembled in a large theater, watching you during a shared-application videoconference, or quietly seated in front of their personal computer.

We save you hours of playing around with presentation software, and we know how to handle the pressure of last minute production: we deliver, on time, the brilliant presentation you need to seduce and convince your audience.

In short: we help you make your [power]point.
