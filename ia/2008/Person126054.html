Maurizio Bianchi’s Summary

I'm involved in the development of hardware and software solutions for Hi-Tech industry. Due to my past activities in different company segments, I gained a quite wide expertise in hardware, firmware and software development; my key points are:
- System level expertise in Microsoft based platforms including Windows O.S. and Active Directory administration.
- Senior level developer in C/C++, VisualBasic, C# with particular know-how of both VisualStudio6, and VisualStudio.NET environments
- Firmware development and debugging on the most populars SDKs for the following microcontrollers:
- MCS51 / Keil uVision
- C8051F32X / SiLabs
- MSP430 / IAR Embedded Workbench for Texas Instruments
- PicMicro / Microchip
- RTS / SGS-Thomson ST6
- Software development of advanced database interfaces for Oracle, SQL-Server, MSAccess
- Web development for ODBC databases in ASP
- Know-how of HTML and Java language for web developing
- Interfacing of GSM/GPRS/UMTS Engines with particular expertise on Siemens' TC45/MC55 modules via AT-Protocol according to ETSI Standard 07.10
- Deep knowledge of GPS system with special capabilities in interfacing against the most popular protocols such as NMEA 2.0 or RTCM.
- Development of DEM (Digital Elevation Model) interfaces for GIS/GPS applications.
- Bluetooth interfacing knowledge with special regards to Mitsumi modules and CSR based BT Engines.
- Expertise in HeNe/NdYAG Lasers systems and Solid-State Lasers for Electronic manufacturing
- PCBA assembly and process tuning for both THT and SMT technology in traditional and RoHS versions.
- Functional Testing of electronic appliances for Military and Automotive industry
- Product Certification according to CE as EN60950/B for Safety and EN55022/B for EMI
- X-Ray diagnostics for electronic manufacturing in 2D and 3D on Bosello and Agilent 5DX
- Product documentation using OrCAD, EAGLE, AutoCAD
- Product simulation using MATLAB
- Process analisys using MINITAB
