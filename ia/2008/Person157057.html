Rasmus Hoejengaard’s Summary

I started out in the entertainment industry, by creating 3d content in a post production company. It was mostly TV ads, TV shows and Scandinavian movies. I did this for more than 2 years.

Together with an associate, I started a privately held company "Perspective", at which we produced various content for the internet, printed media and corporate identities.

I then relocated and started working for the danish game development company IO Interactive A/S, which at that point held about 25 people (now about 200). I started August 2000.

Three of the 6 years I was there, I worked as a level designer, after which I worked 3 years as as Game Director on the 2006 AAA release, Hitman Blood Money.

From the 1st of Nov. 2006 up untill August 2007, I have been working at C4-world as Director of Design, where my primary task was designing the platform www.myc4.com, which purpose is to further entrepreneurship in African countries via a digital community, networking and investment interface.

-

In October 2007 - together with 2 American partners - the corporation "Saboteur Inc" was realized. Saboteur is a full service motion picture production company - based in greater Los Angeles, CA.

The company is founded by Peter Gornstein (Director) and Ivo Horvat (Visual Effects Senior (SPI, ILM a.o.)) and myself.

Saboteur is a lean, high quality, cost efficient production house.

The company is rapidly moving forward, and we've completed our first commercial spot, which will be broadcast in 2008 on most major commercial TV channels in the US. We will furthermore submit the spot to all major Advertising festivals in 2008 (Cannes Lions, Clio etc.).

We are furthermore working on 2 other commercial spots as well as developing the script and investor pitch for a potential AAA feature film.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you would like any further information.
