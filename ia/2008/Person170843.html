Scott Carmichael’s Summary

Prodigy Sports, LLC, a metropolitan New York-based company with 30 years of unparalleled expertise in the professional sports industry, officially launched January 1, 2008. Prodigy Sports will focus its core business practice predominantly on executive search and placement exclusive to the sports industry, while also providing consulting services to leagues, teams and other properties within the sports community.

Headed by long-time professional sports executive Scott Carmichael, Prodigy Sports is poised to become the industry's preeminent executive search company based on the foundation of hundreds of key relationships with top level executives and organizations within the world of sports.

Prodigy Sports' initial placement came on behalf of the Dallas Cowboys, one of the world's top sports organizations. Hired to place a new senior-level ticket sales executive, Prodigy Sports assisted the Cowboys with the hiring two of the sports industry's premier talent.

Since then, Prodigy Sports was retained by the New Orleans Saints and New Orleans VooDoo to identify the organization's first Arena Football-specific Director of Corporate Sponsorship.

Headquartered in the New York City suburb of Freehold, New Jersey, Prodigy Sports also has client relationships with ANC Sports Enterprises, the Arena Football League, Dallas Stars, Florida Panthers, Phoenix Coyotes, Tampa Bay Lightning and Costco Wholesale Stores.

Prodigy's President, Scott Carmichael, has prior professional experience on the property side with the National Hockey League as head of the league's Club Marketing department as well as the Los Angeles Olympic Organizing Committee (1984), the team side with the Los Angeles Kings, Los Angeles Clippers and Los Angeles Lakers, and on the agency/marketing side of business with ANC Sports Enterprises of Purchase, New York.
