Jos van Haut’s Summary

During almost 30 years I have been able to work on a great variety of divisions in the Croon Elektrotechniek Company, from building / repairing / converting ships and offshore equipment to industrial constructions and maintenance. In this time I have taken the opportunities to develop myself further. First in electrical specialties, like fire-detection and design. Later I have taken courses to finally get business engineer in management.

Today, also working for this company, I am assigned to engineering department for the utilitarian building division. My daily work is a mix of designing and calculating electrical installations for large and complex office-buildings, as well as giving advice and drawing up business proposals to both management and our costumers. For this work I attend meetings from building-teams, engineers and management.

Working as Freelance designer.

I work as a freelance designer, desiging installations, make calculations for electro technical installations there, and give recommendation.


Working with people I strive to find a perfect balance between people, process and technology.
Having found this balance, I believe people can and will perform in a more effective way.

In my private life I do voluntary worker in nature preservation, and for my darts club I am chosen for the job of treasurer.
