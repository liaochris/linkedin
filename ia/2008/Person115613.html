Lori Logan-Mc Cormick’s Summary

Assisting college-bound students currently in high school with college search and selection, major and career exploration, and completing their application on time (jncluding essay and SAT tutoring referrals). Additionally, I work with community college students who are interested in transferring to a 4-year university. Together we explore various colleges, majors and post-college career exploration.

As a career consultant, I guide college students through job, internship or study abroad opportunities during and post-graduation. I also work with students in developing their resumes, setting goals, and defining thier skills/interests.
