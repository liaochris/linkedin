David W. Boles, MFA’s Summary

David W. Boles is an author and teacher based on the East Coast. He has written books, plays, movies and television shows. He teaches American Sign Language, Dramatic Writing and Technical Editing via the Boles University online network and he creates learning content modules for high schools, homeschools, colleges and universities across the world for their distance learning programs. Get in touch if you're looking for memeingful learning methods of knowing.

David W. Boles, MFA’s Specialties:

Writing, Teaching, Radio, Television, Film, Script Professor, Dramatic Medicine, Scientific Aesthetic, Urban Systems, Literary Criticism, American Sign Language, Publishing, RelationShaping, WordPunk and forever investigating the meaning and knowing for: Memeingful, Panopticonic, Urban Core and Carceral Nation.
