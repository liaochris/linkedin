Cary Evans’s Summary

Political and corporate public affairs, grassroots, and general campaign consultant and attorney. Cary brings 18 years of political, legislative, and finance experience to West Meridian.

Cary Evans’s Experience

  • Owner

    West Meridian, LLC

    (Public Relations and Communications industry)

    March 2007 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    West Meridian is a strategic political consulting and public affairs firm. It’s principal and founder Cary Evans brings a winning campaign record and strategic alliances across Western states that allows you to receive his undivided attention and benefit from his winning grassroots and complete campaign programs.

    We can provide everything from the most complete and complex turn-key political consulting service – allowing candidates to be candidates not managers – to the simplest creative printing or grassroots communications job.

  • Regional Political Director

    Republican National Committee

    (Political Organization industry)

    2005 — 2007 (2 years)

    “The RNC’s 72-hour program is not dependent upon a favorable political climate for success.” ABC’s The Note, June 7, 2006

    Before forming West Meridian in 2007, Cary served as regional political director for 10 Western states and territories at the Republican National Committee beginning in 2005. In that role he organized and implemented the grassroots turnout programs for many winning Congressional races in 2006, including two important swing seats – re-electing Congressmen Dave Reichert in WA-8 and Jon Porter in NV-3. Cary also ran the turnout program that helped to elect Congressman Brian Bilbray in the June 2006 special election in CA-50.
