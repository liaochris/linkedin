Scott McGregor’s Summary

-->Entrepreneur nearly 50 years. Founded and operated land development company with extensive involvement in original commercial analysis of land as to its highest and best use before the advent of calculators. Founder and developer of First American Mortgage Corp., sold. Co-founder McNeil-Mehew Group, Inc., and inventor of the Adjusted Balance Mortgage instrument for the US economy in 1980, which was credited with breaking the back of runaway interest rates. (Named "Father of The Instrument of The Decade" by the Federal Home Loan Bank Board").
Founder of a national airline, electronics importer, major cologne importer, and an international seminar presentation company.
-->Founder of technology cellular firmware company, became largest of its kind in the world...holder of 40+ patents in cellular & wireless telephony arena. Responsible for today's "Prepaid Cellular Technology" and "over-the-air" technology.
Presently involved in supporting the spirit of Entrepreneurship and the solving the challenges facing the financial industry as they relate to card and identity fraud.
-->Present goal is to assist the Federal Government by reducing the banking industry's credit card fraud and identity theft by $70 Billion by the end of fiscal year 2009.
