  • President, Supervisory Board

    APIS IT doo

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2005 — Present (3 years)

    The information systems and information technologies support agency, APIS IT, was founded at the end of 2005 by the contract between the Government of the Republic of Croatia and the City of Zagreb, building on the 40-year-experience and tradition of GZAOP, the Municipal Data City Processing Center of the City of Zagreb.

    The role of APIS IT is to develop and monitor implementation of the guidelines, standards and policies in eGovernment, provide support to the government administrative organisations in the development of their own strategy in eBusiness, develop and support common ICT infrastructure, promote the best practices in developing information systems, including the personal data protection, develop common eServices and provide controled access to the information resources of the central and local government.

  • member Management Board

    SRCE

    (Educational Institution; 51-200 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    2003 — Present (5 years)

    The University Computing Center (SRCE) was founded in 1972. While it is the oldest infrastructural institution of the academic and research community in the area of application of information and communication technologies (ICT), SRCE is still one of the key subjects in planning, designing, construction and maintainance of the newest computing, communication and information infrastructure (e-infrastructure). SRCE continuously maintains stable, reliable and high quality operations of the present e-infrastructure and central systems which support it, ensures connection among Croatian academic e-infrastructure with the related European and global e-infrastructure and gives practical, consulting and educational support to the members of the academic and research community in using ICT and acts as the computing centre of the largest university in Croatia – the University of Zagreb.
