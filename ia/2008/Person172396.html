Daniel Richter’s Summary

A creative, energetic, and driven professional with over ten years of event entertainment and promotional marketing experience with extensive knowledge in strategic product development, promotional activation, event production, and event management.

A visionary spirit with strong communication skills, a natural decisive leader that is able to identify issues, analyze the situation, find prudent solutions, and initiate action to completion.

Clients typically approach me during the concept development stages to help them find ways to creatively utilize their existing resources and identify partners to maximize their products potential.

I have the ability to see cultural and business connections that most people do not and the resources to bring together beneficial partners.

* Based in Whistler, BC the home of the 2010 Winter Olympics.

*Legal work status: US Citizen, Canadian Resident.
