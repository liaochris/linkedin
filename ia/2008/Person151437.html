  • Director

    Licuados

    (Retail industry)

    January 2008 — Present (11 months)

    Co-founder of Licuados which operates highly branded mobile (gazebo style) units selling snowshakes. These are drinks made from snow shaken with pure fruit concentrate. They are very much like a thick milk shake but made with water rather than milk as the main ingredient, they are very tasty, carry almost zero calories, no 'e's and are healthy (each cup represents ½ of 1 of the 6 units per day) – there is an option to then add various 'sweeties' and whipped cream toppings (making them less healthy!).

    The target market is 14 – 30 years old and we operate at events. festivals and town markets. During the summer of 2008 we are proving the concept with three units. The plan for summer 2009 is scale the operation to give national coverage.

  • Founder & CEO

    MicroFinace Himalaya

    (Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    October 2007 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    Founded, funded and manage MicroFinace Himalaya (MFH) - a not for profit operation that makes micro loans (£100) to the very poor in Nepal. The objective is to 'lift' people out of the subsistance trap in a sustainable way.

    MFH has three key objectives

    Make money available at affordable rates (to those that would otherwise not have access to lending)
    Give basic business education to its loan recipients
    Provide ongoing mentoring and support to those recipients

    MFH aims to achieve a default rate on less than 2%
