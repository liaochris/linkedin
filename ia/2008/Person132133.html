Mike Friesen’s Summary

Mike Friesen is a former Air National Guard F-4 and F-15 pilot and finance officer who served in the U.S. military for 21 years before retiring with the rank of Lieutenant Colonel. His diverse background also includes work in the banking industry, construction fields, and as clergy. Mike holds a M.B.A from Western Governors University and a B.A. in Management of Human Resources from George Fox University.

Mike founded Leading Strategies LLC to "build world class leaders and high performance teams." Leading Strategies provides executive coaching and leadership development consulting. Mike is an avid writer and student of organizational behavior.

Author: 'Expected End: What Culture Is, Why It Matters and How to Improve It'

Mike Friesen’s Specialties:

Developing up-and-coming leaders in an organization, assigning practical meaning to personalized 360 degree feedback and personality assessment
