Gus Pearcy’s Summary

Gus Pearcy has more than 20 years in communications.

I specialize in:

* Newsletters

* Promotional videos (check out my work at http://www.zachrosing.com/portfolio/project/hereforgood and http://shelteringwings.org/about/video.php)

* Media consultation

* Public Relations

* Speech writing

* Project leadership

Currently, I write for a monthly business publication and general consumption magazine. I also maintain a freelance writing business for different projects from media relations to Internet copy.

I have a very diverse background in the non-profit sector and have focused my service on the marketing and public relations aspect of organizations such as Hendricks College Network, The Indiana Medical History Museum, The Hendricks County Community Foundation, the West District Branch of the Indianapolis YMCA, and Wayne Township Education Foundation.
