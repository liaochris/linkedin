Nick Lucido’s Summary

I am currently in my third year at Michigan State University studying advertising, public relations and public administration; one day I hope to incorporate all of my studies in my career. MSU has provided me with countless opportunities to advance my career, my academics and myself. I am proud to be a Spartan.

My internship experience at two public relations agencies has given me a strong background in media relations, strategic planning, fundraising and research. Additionally, I have worked with clients in the health care, nonprofit, automotive, government and professional service industries. Back at school, I work at the State News in both sales and management positions. Also, as Chapter President of MSU PRSSA, I lead an organization with more than 150 members in developing a more professional, ethical and prepared group of students who will be entering the public relations industry.

When I graduate, I hope to find a career where I can grow and develop while leaving my mark on my company and the industry. I am active in PRSSA and hope to obtain leadership positions within PRSA in the future.
