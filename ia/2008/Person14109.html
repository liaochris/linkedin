Anshu Jain’s Summary

Anshu is a technical staff member at the IBM's India Research Lab based at Bangalore. He boasts of his object oriented skills and specializes in development and design of Enterprise Tools. Prior to joining IRL he worked at SAP for almost 3 years. He obtained a Post Graduate degree in Information Technology, before which he majored in Electronics and Communication.

He is closely associated with International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore (IIITB), assisting in the Programming, as well as Software Engineering and Design courses offered at the institute. He also guides students pursuing projects in Open Source Technologies, and application development.

Anshu Jain’s Specialties:

Modeling Tools, Enterprise application infrastructures, Eclipse Rich Client Platform, Object Oriented Technologies
