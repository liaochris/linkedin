John Currie’s Summary

ITERATE creates value for technology ventures, owners, and their investors by developing strategic partnerships and taking client's technologies into new markets. Principal has a proven track record of getting early stage technology companies through the "Capital Gap" by FINDING CUSTOMERS! Entrepreneurs need early customers and early investors - difficult deals to get done. Large co.'s also have under-utilized assets that could be monetized through entrepreneurs. ITERATE's value propostion is all about contracting for RESULTS.
