David Page’s Summary

David Page, CPA, M.B.A.

Associate Director of Finance and Controller of the Zoological Society since August 2004. Associate Director of Finance from March 2002 to August 2004. From September 1999 to March 2002, served as Corporate Controller for Daou Systems, Inc., a healthcare information systems consulting company. From March 1998 to September 1999, served as both Corporate Controller for Coyote Sports, Inc., a sporting goods company, and Divisional Controller for Unifiber, Inc., a subsidiary of Coyote Sports. From June 1997 to March 1998, served as Controller for Scantibodies Laboratories, Inc., a biotech supply company. From August 1993 to June 1997, served as an auditor with Ernst & Young, LLP, where received license as a Certified Public Accountant. I hold a B.S. in Accounting from San Diego State University and a Masters in Business Administration from California State University San Marcos.

David Page’s Specialties:

Significant experience in finanicial management, strategic planning, leadership and team building. Enjoy presenting to groups within the organization as well as to external groups.
