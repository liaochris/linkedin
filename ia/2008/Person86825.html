Jean Feminier’s Summary

I have been working for 18 years overseas in multicultural environment.
During my time overseas, as member of the management team, I have leveraged my grassroots experience gained in complex and competitive business environments. Most notably, I have been able to provide an inspiring and cohesive vision. Results driven, I am an entrepreneur used to take target oriented initiatives. I have actively contributed to the turnaround of my company through implementating their presence in a new market, and strengthening their long term sustainable growth through efficient sales operations, costing analysis, production performance optimization / rationalization, across different technologies.
I have acquired over my operational experience, an in-depth understanding of management in global environment. In particular I have led business unit teams, prioritized targets, created and presented strategic long term business plans, and I have dealt with national and international customers, NGOs, and government bodies.

I have completed the TRIUM Global Executive MBA program which is the alliance among three of the worldwide leading universities: London School of Economics [LSE]; New York University-Stern [NYU]; and Hautes Etudes Commerciales - Paris [HEC - Paris].
As part of TRIUM, I have also studied in Shanghai (instruction in part from Hong Kong University of Science & Technology [HKUST]), and in San Paulo (instruction in part from Fundaçao Dom Cabral [FDC]).
I have led "Value Creation" & "Strategic Management in competitive environment" sessions in Beijing.
