Ian Sagabaen’s Summary

Professional graphic designer & photographer with experience in digital printing techniques. Extensive knowledge & passion in typography & the Swiss design approach. Always trying to implement the fundamental idea of design: finding the most effective & ideal way to create the mark on the page & convey a strong message. Constantly finding ways to incorporate graphic design & photography to compliment each other in design work.

Firmly believes that although this new digital age has made desktop publishing much more efficient & easier, nothing beats a piece of paper & a pencil as the first step in the design process.

Ian Sagabaen’s Specialties:

Storyboarding, sketching, fluency in the Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, well-knowledgable in Macintosh & Windows operating systems, usability, CSS, WYSIWYG layout tools, proofreading, copywriting, typography, photography (studio, event, architecture), photo retouching
