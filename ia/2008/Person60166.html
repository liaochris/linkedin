Eric Reeder’s Summary

Bringing together business knowledge and the technology required to support it, Eric has delivered exciting business solutions through creativity and solid program management. A distinguished PMP and PgMP who began his career in retail operations, Eric has strategized, managed and actively participated in every phase of the software development life cycle (SDLC). His approach to building and mentoring teams has resulted in several successful multi-million dollar initiatives and numerous small and mid-sized development successes. An accomplished strategist and leader, Eric is equally comfortable implementing cutting edge technologies or maximizing investment in older (legacy) solutions.
