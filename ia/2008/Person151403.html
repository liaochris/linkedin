Philippe Lefebvre’s Summary

11 years experience in Rural, Sub Urban and Urban Telecommunication Markets, I had the chance to grow step by steps into the organization, acquiring a strong technical and then commercial background. I started to work as a "Supervisor of Installation" and moved quickly to the “Customer Technical Support Organisation (CTSO)” team of Lucent Technologies in France. Along the years within the CTSO team, I was in charge of several projects such as Egypt, Brazil and Australia where one of my responsibilities was to set up the Local Customer Team. After two years managing the Technical Support Department in close relationship with Telstra (Australia), I decided to move to the Services Department and was given with the “Services Contract Manager” responsibilities which included among other the promotion and sale of Remote Tech Support (RTS), Repair Services, Audit… to our customers. I acquired a good first commercial background which makes me join the Sales force group as Sales Director.
In Dec 2005, DUONS and SR Telecom have signed an agreement by which DUONS became the new SWING & IRT IP owner.
I decided to move to this new entity where I was appointed SALES DIRECTOR SERVICES for the global Telecom market and customers. In Dec 2006, Duons Mco re organized the Sales Department, and I was promoted Regional Sales Director Africa (English and French spoken area).
In 2007, as DUONS MCO acquired more products lines; June 2007 – Payphone (SP T Publicom - Ex Schlumberger) and Dec 2007 - SR 100 / 500 / 500 IP & Airstar from SR Telecom Inc. Effective on Jan 2008, the African territory was split in two main areas where the North, West and South parts were assigned to me.
Easy-going, team player and hard worker, customers’ services and satisfaction are key point to me, keeping in mind company's global interests and always looking for a win-win solution.

Philippe Lefebvre’s Specialties:

Radio link skills, IP / LAN / WAN networks, facilities to learn other language (indonesian, malay, portuguese...).

The years spent in work relation with customers and operators (Europe, Asia, Australia, Latin / Central America and Africa) helps me to acquire adaptability.
