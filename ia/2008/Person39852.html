Vince Rubino’s Summary

Bioengineer with experience in Quality Assurance, Validation and Facilities Engineering. Before biosciences, I worked in the media industry including video production, publishing and music.

I am now in Incheon, South Korea where I lead KEMTI's International Marketing for the Biosafety Evaluation Headquarters and manage our Quality Systems.

KEMTI Biosafety Evaluation Center is a GLP facility conforming to OECD principles. We provide medical device biocompatibility test services following GLP and ISO 10993 standards, GLP toxicity tests, chemical management services, environmental occupational risk assessments. We are 20 km from the world’s third largest cargo airport and provide rapid and reliable service to international customers.

One goal is to provide solutions to the global pharmaceutical supply chain issues faced by the US and China as well as support the bioindustry cluster developing in Incheon.
