Jon Justin’s Summary

Jon is a highly experienced and respected Test Manager/Tech PM/Implementation manager, gained through 14+ years in definition, testing and implementation. He has lead many testing engagements and has an excellent track record of managing technical teams within a variety of front to back office systems, in diverse areas including management of disaster recovery, design, migration, risk analysis and mitigation, test plans and test scripts, project planning, implementation and systematisation, to name a few responsibilities. During this time he also gained CMMi, PRINCE2 Practitioner, ISEB Testing Practitioner accredition, is ITIL Service Management certified is currently studying for his NLP Practitioner License and has just passed his MBA essentials.

Jon has been on site with his current client as a consultant since July 2004, where he has been continually renewed. In that time he has coordinated 9 OAT's and held various technical PM/TM/Implementation roles. These involved coordinating with global operational staff across London, US and Eastern Hemisphere. Prior to this role Jon had been heading up the Product Assurance, performance and automation testing within RBS Insurance (formally Churchill Insurance group and Directline).
