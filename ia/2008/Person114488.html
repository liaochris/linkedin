Lisa Nuske 847.593.2000’s Summary

I've worked in several industries which have now all merged into one career!

I am the Special Events Sales Coordinator at Tasty Catering, working with the special event sales department to make sure their clients' wishes are represented all the way through the production process to completion for an outstanding event.

My primary focus is creating the mood and tone of the event through decor, linens, room layouts and overall presentation. My experience of in the floral industry lends itself very well to this.

To stay current with trends, I am becoming more involved in Tasty Catering's memberships in National Association of Catering Executives (NACE), International Special Events Society (ISES), and Meeting Planners International (MPI), associations focusing on the business, networking, and trends for the food service industry.

Prior to Tasty Catering, I worked in the wholesale flower business in sales and loved developing relationships with my customers. It was the best learning experience I've ever had for interpersonal communications.

Other experiences that have led me here include music and working as a software specialist for several years. Everything together has given me an overall view of many things and I am lucky enough to use just about everything I've learned in my life here at Tasty Catering.
