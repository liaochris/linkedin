Jeff Salisbury’s Summary

Our internal motto is 'Clean, Lean, and Green'. Our goal is to become "America's greenest resource for labels, tags, and flexible packaging".

As of right now we have the widest array of eco-friendly material offerings available on the market. We are constantly striving to lower our own carbon footprint as well as to source and create materials with lower carbon footprints.

We learn every day from partners who have been on the green path much longer than we have, and every day I am humbled by what I do not know.

I am thankful that I didn't 'throw in the towel' a few years ago when, after a few medical setbacks I had the sudden revelation that I was simply 'creating landfill' for a living. I know that we can do better, and still grow as a profitable company, be a great place for employees to work and for clients/suppliers to work with.

Jeff Salisbury’s Specialties:

FSC (Forestry Stewardship Council) certification, Carbon Neutral certification.
We have always prided ourselves on being the best resource our clients and vendors have. If we cannot provide the solution we know someone who CAN, and we send you in the right direction. This is something I learned to do early in my working life and it has served me and my customers/clients well.
