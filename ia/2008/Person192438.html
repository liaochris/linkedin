Travis Alexander’s Summary

My goals as a kid were to be a firefighter, an attorney, a football coach, and an astronaut.
Currently in my position, I'm a professional Fire Fighter, putting out fires daily in a busy and sometimes volatile environment. I'm not an attorney, but I will represent the company in court on occasion. My main goal however, is to keep us out of court as much as possible. I'm also a coach, helping managers call the right plays without any infractions or fouls on the field of play. It's my job to make sure the team makes their goals while abiding by the rules of the game. I also make sure our team has the best players on it recruiting fresh young talent and training them to be MVP's some day. I guess you could say I'm living the dream. Oh, as for the astronaut, well... I just don't look good in a jump suit.

My HR philosophy is to act as a guide to managers. I think of myself as the pace car in a NASCAR race that sets the pace, gets all of the cars in line, and then gets out of the way to let the real race cars do what they do best. Or act as a Sherpa that guides individuals to the top of Mt. Everest by going before them, hanging the ropes, showing them the best route, supporting them, carrying some of the load, but then letting them have the credit that they well deserve for achieving their goal. I enjoy being the behind the scenes support and counsel that helps managers stay the course and achieve their main objectives.

Travis Alexander’s Specialties:

I enjoy the banter and strategy of a good negotiation. I also enjoy the thrill of achieving a mutually beneficial win-win agreement in a mediation or employee relations problem. I pride myself on my conflict resolution ability. I'm well versed in International business and managed the International Recruitment program for a world renowned resort destination. I'm also certified in interrogations and investigations when situations arise that calls for that skill set.
