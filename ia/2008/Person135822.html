Erwan Moysan’s Summary

Erwan MOYSAN is a senior IT consultant. Specialized in new technologies.
He focuses in Video On Demand (VOD), especially for live and sports events.

Recent customers are :
- CPFK, for their Moovyplay project (video hard drive rental)
- SportFive, to provide them a white label VOD platform for their customers
- France Télévisions, where he has built the VOD offer from billing to customer care (Roland Garros, Tour de france, documentaries and series)
- Editions Montparnasse, for all their B2C VOD catalog
- System TV, for their B2B and B2C mobile and VOD offer

He also works for Pfizer on a research project involing patents and chemistry, through a startup named DecrIPt inc, founded in Nevada.

Erwan is the former co-founder, manager for Europe and CTO of QEDSoft Inc. (San Jose, CA), where he created the 3D Streaming technology (http://www.qedsoft.com/gallery) recently licensed by a top Internet Player.

Erwan also worked at Duran-Duboi, where he managed the MendelStudio project, a tool dedicated to create real-time 3D cartoons. He worked for Cap Gemini as a consultant, Haïku Studio as a developper. He is the author of vectorization tools for Articque Informatique. He holds a Masters CS and was a (bored) PhD Candidate.

Erwan Moysan’s Specialties:

IT architecture, billing, video on-demand, Windows media DRM, online customer care, WEB, VOD, DRM, PHP, JAVA, SOAP, Akamai
