Joanne Jonah Domeniconi’s Summary

Consumer products professional specializing in all areas of product strategy and product development. Business savvy manager with competence leading cross-functional teams, large and small, to bring about innovative and commercial products to target consumer audiences. Strategic thinker skilled at ensuring that product development is in alignment with market opportunity, brand strategy and consumer need. Extensive overseas experience managing production resources through sampling, engineering, material sourcing and costing. Valued as a natural troubleshooter with the ability to consistently deliver results. Known to be a passionate 'keeper of the brand', a lover of new ideas and one who embraces change and challenge.

    (Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Retail industry)

The three year turnaround effort materialized in 11 straight quarters of improving sales and profitability.
