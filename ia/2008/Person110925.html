Lata Chawla’s Summary

Lata Chawla is the President and Senior Consultant at Performance Edge.

Performance Edge helps small to mid-sized organizations stay competitive in a marketplace which is experiencing rapid change. When change is the order of the day, having an edge is all about sustaining effort and moving towards actions that establish a stable and adaptive foundation for the future. Towards that end, Performance Edge works with leaders to; increase organizational effectiveness, increase organizational capacity and increase organizational health, through carefully planned interventions.

Chawla has over 15 years of experience in Human Resources in the areas of Organizational and Leadership Development, Training, Staffing, Diversity, Human Resources Information Systems, Employee Relations and Consulting.

Prior to her current role at Performance Edge, Chawla worked for ESPN, Inc. for 11 years. In her tenure at ESPN, she served in a variety of leadership roles within the Human Resources Department. Prior to ESPN, Lata held leadership roles in Human Resources Department at ITT Hartford Life Insurance Group.

Chawla holds a degree in Theology and a Masters in HR Administration. She has taken management, business and negotiations classes at Harvard, UCLA, and the University of Hartford. She is a graduate of the 2004 Leadership Education for Asian Professionals (L.E.A.P.) Program. Lata is a certified Human Systems Dynamics Professional (HSDP) from the HSD Institute.

Chawla serves as the 2008 President of Woman in Cable Telecommunications - New England. She also serves on the Board of Directors for The World Affairs Council of CT (WACC), Jazz’d 4 Life and The Association for Christian Character Development (ACCD).

Lata Chawla’s Specialties:

Organizational, Leadership and Team Development, Training, Diversity, Cultural Competency, Staffing, Executive Coaching, 360 Feedback, E-Learning, Change, Human Systems Dynamics.
