Jonathan Cortez’s Summary

As Director of Application Development at Statera, he is responsible for the SharePoint and .NET consulting practice in Seattle where he focuses on implementating collaborative solutions using Microsoft SharePoint and .NET technologies. He has over 15 years of IT experience in application architecture and software development with emphasis on Microsoft technologies. He has extensive experience in industries such as Financial Services, High Technology, Travel and Transportation, Aerospace and Defense, Publishing, Healthcare, Biotechnology, Retail, and Manufacturing.
