Robin Carr’s Summary

With extensive and deep communications experience, I have had an incredibly rewarding and versatile career in a wide-range of industries -- from brand communications, consumer industries, sports, entertainment, fashion, global, technology (gaming), corporate and cause-marketing public relations.

I’m passionate about PR -- from traditional media and media 2.0 -- and have extensive experience with branding, product launches, event creation, planning and promotion.

I have excellent media connections spanning top network contacts, national business media, regional press to columnists. I am a strategic and a driven thought-leader with well-honed communication, writing and management skills.

I thrive in a fast-paced environment working with people on teams who are smart and highly creative -- and fun!
