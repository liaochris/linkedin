Joost Mulders’s Summary

I am an ICT consultant and in my career I have fulfilled the following roles:

- Software engineer in several project teams from small teams with 3 team members to large teams with over 20 software engineers.

- Software architect with the responsibility for the technical design and architecture of an application as well as the planning for the development phase.

- Information analyst with the responsibility for the functional design.

- Test coordinator with the responsibilities of testing and issue management.

- Consultant with the activities consisting of software implementations at the customer, presentations in the definition phase of a project and technical support for the customer.

This large variaty of roles has allowed me to be involved in all phases of a project from the initial definition phase to the final support phase. During my career I have specialised in the specification phase of a software development project. Concentrating mainly on functional and technical design and assisting the development team and the project manager during the other phases of the project.
