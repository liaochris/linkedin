Philip Dodds’s Summary

An experienced executive and leader, with 15 years experience, who has managed large and small organizations, transitioning through change and also bridging new approaches to software development, delivery and technology. Successfully worked in a range of environments from London, Wall Street, West Coast Start-ups and Innovation Partners in Asia.
Provided both vision and execution to deliver products including Financial Services solutions, Service-Oriented Architecture middleware and tooling, Software Development and Build technologies as well as lead architecture and organizations through a range of technologies. Worked within both Enterprise environments, in open source projects (with both Apache and Eclipse) and in a global marketplace.
