Chuan Jer (CJ) Lim’s Summary

Strong interest and passion as well as hands-on experience in the digital marketing space, both at a technical level as well as a business development level.

Seen as one of the digital evangelists within Hardware Zone.

Handed the responsibility of managing the P&L of HWZ.com, the company's flagship online portal in 2003.
