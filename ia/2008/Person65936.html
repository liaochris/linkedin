Frederick Reber’s Summary

Industrial Designer with experience in developing consumer, electronic, and medical products from concept sketching to final production. Class-A Alias surfacing skills resulting in quick and proactive design solutions that address unmet consumer needs. Demonstrated leadership skills, individual contributor, and excellent team player working with consumer researchers, engineers, graphic and packaging designers, and marketing. Excellent communication and people skills.
