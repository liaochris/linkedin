Santhosh Babu’s Summary

An ITSM Consultant by profession, and an Educator by choice. Santhosh started his career in software development. Over a period of years, he changed his interest from Programming and Databases to IT Service Management. After a stint in the ME working on some large projects with some of top companies in the UAE, Kuwait and Saudi Arabia, in 2005, he left the helm of the tech division of a company he helped build, to pursue his career of choice - consulting & training, in the country he loves - India.

A Certified Trainer & Facilitator, he concentrates on making sure that the dynamics of the group is always maintained at a suitable level. He speaks from the heart with passion & humor. His command of the material is breathtaking; he is constantly updating his work, his clarifying examples are powerful and original. His unique unconventional style is what makes his programs popular and learning becomes an enjoyable experience for the participants. He uses his vast experience, war stories, metaphors, live examples and case studies along with a highly interactive session with games & exercises coupled with multimedia presentations. He has designed and conducted various successful customized in-house workshops and training programs for companies such as Mphasis/EDS, Tata Group Companies, and many more. His strengths lie in his personal involvement with the participants so as to bring out the best in them.

Nicked the GIN (thats genie in Arabic) by his clients in the ME - as a Consultant he has implemented over a 100 Service Desks with an unblemished zero failed project record for self & team, and as a Trainer, he regularly achieves an average rating of 4+ on a 5 point scale. In delivering the ITIL Foundations training program, he maintains a record 99% pass rate. His students often point out how much they enjoy their experience and a large majority of his programs are populated on the strength of referrals of past students.
