Bonnie Gillespie’s Summary

Eh... pretty much your standard average everyday sarcastic, cynical, anal-retentive, obsessive-compulsive insomniac perfectionist with a great rack. (Yeah, I know, that's not what LinkedIn is looking for in terms of a professional summary, but dangit, if you don't GET that I'm all about the casual, funny, raunchy-yet-professional, always a good time, meticulously-organized, open-communication business style, we won't really get along anyway.)

Bonnie Gillespie’s Specialties:

I will bring your vision to life through organization, open communication, cutting-edge casting technology, and a clear focus on serving the project. We will find the best, most passionately committed actors for your film within clearly-outlined goals, benchmarks, and budget constraints. Casting should be one of the most exciting, stress-free, and rewarding parts of the filmmaking process. I only sign on to cast scripts that I feel passionate about, and that passion shines through every day.
