Rags Srinivasan’s Summary

More than eleven years of technical and leadership experience in developing telecommunication solutions for the global market. Proven track record of leading global teams, effective project management, software engineering and strategic planning skills. Led teams that developed software for CDMA Radio Base Stations. Generated methodologies, designs and frameworks of lasting value. Thrived on changes in our business direction and led teams through the transition from developing products to developing services. As a line manager, coached several individuals reach their potential and realize their aspirations.

I am moving from operations to outer loop of business. I plan to work on setting strategy and marketing solutions. I am starting my full time MBA at Haas School of Business UC, Berkeley, this Fall.
