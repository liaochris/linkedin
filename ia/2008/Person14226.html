Anthony Escarcega’s Summary

I'll keep this short. I have a fairly extensive technology and engineering background, but a desire to start new and exciting businesses. Add in a business degree with a focus on entrepreneurship, and I'm in the world of starting companies. My first venture is Ratio, Inc., which is developing a new drug delivery technology. My job of putting together the pieces is done and the company is on solid ground, new management will take it to the next level. My new venture, Cega Nutraceuticals LLC, is developing dietary supplements that have therapeutic value in the areas of athletics and general health. Our first product is PR1pak, a performance endurance supplement designed to enhance training for endurance athletes.

    Ratio, Inc

    (Biotechnology industry)

    August 2005 — Present (3 years 1 month)

    Ratio, Inc. is developing a way to deliver drugs that you would normally have to take with a needle, painlessly. Our device is a small (size of a poker chip) drug pump that utilizes an array of microneedles to get the drug across the skin barrier. It can deliver a dose of drugs in a matter of minutes, or hours, depending upon drug requirements. It is disposable and non-electronic. It is an inexpensive drug pump that can ideally replace the needle and syringe.

    I helped start a biotechnology company, Semba, Inc. Semba developed a bench-top simulated moving bed (SMB) chromatography system that is used to separate difficult-to-separate compounds, such as proteins. I took the company from business plan and initial capitalization to product development. Semba is alive and doing well today.

In short, we fixed things and then broke them while at the same time measuring the forces. I was involved in the development of several orthopedic implants such as artificial knees, bone substitutes, artificial vertebral discs, and bone growth hormones.
