Russell Kern’s Summary

After I completed my BA in Graphic design and Minors in Illustration & drawing at the University of Akron, I worked at a couple of small design firms over a coule of years.

I then went to work for an Brubaker & Associates, and Advertising agency in Hudson Ohio, as a Graphic Artist and worked eon accounts such as Firestone, Alcoa Forged Aluminum Products and a number of other local accounts. I worked there for about a year and then Moved on to a small little group Called EIS (Enterprise Information Services)

While I was there I was exposed to a film imaging type of environment. while there, I was introduced to 3D modeling and imaging for video, and was a Line Art Illustrator, leading the Maintenance and repair manual production for Mitsubishi Catepillar Forklift.

After a bit, I decided that working there wasn't really for me.

For the Last 12 years of my career I have worked for ST&P Marketing Communications. I started here as A graphic artist and have worked my way up to where I am currently Creative Director for Consumer & Trade Promotions, as well as all Web and Interative activity. A lot of what I do is planning and designing programs and then co-ordinating with both internal & outside resources to produce the project or promotion. I am also involved in managing the Agency for an IT services and infrastructure standpoint. I work on and manage the creative efforts for Companies such as Mack Trucks USA, Bridgestone/Firestone Retail outlets, ConocoPhillips (Kendall Motor oil, 76, Phillips 66), Elizabeth Arden (Halston Z-14, White Diamonds, House of Taylor, Britney Spears brands), Meineke, Monro Muffler & Moen.

Russell Kern’s Specialties:

Purchasing, Project management, resources acquisition, Logo and Identity design, Promotions Planning, Illustration, 3D animation, Systems administration.
