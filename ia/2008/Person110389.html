Lionel Ancelet’s Summary

Lionel is a professional coach, trainer, and consultant.

He coaches professionals for job-related projects or issues (career move, conflict resolution, cross-cultural interaction, oral and written expression, time management, 'personal' marketing).

He also creates and delivers training for groups or individuals, in the areas of public speaking, visual aid creation, creativity tools, and cultural differences.

He is fully bilingual and offers these services in French and English.

Before Lionel became a coach, he built up more than 18 years of experience in multinational high-tech companies (computers, Internet and telecommunication networks) in France and in the USA. He held Engineering, Project Management, Marketing and Communication positions.

Co-founder of Presence Coach, a professional telecoaching network (www.presencecoach.com), he is also a Member of the Mediat-Coaching Network (www.mediat-coaching.com).

In 2005, he co-founded irenea (www.irenea.com), a network of intercultural management consultants. He joined the Net Expat network of intercultural coaches in 2007 (www.netexpat.com).

He also teaches Human Resources at the Institute of International Management of the CNAM in Paris (www.cnam.fr/iim/).
