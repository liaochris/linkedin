Cesar Gustavo Rodriguez’s Summary

As an artist with a passion for animation, my goal is to work in a studio where I can apply my talent for creating dynamic and believable performances that bring CG characters to life. I am looking for an opportunity to collaborate with and learn from other skilled professionals while contributing to the delivery of high quality, cutting edge visual effects and animation.

Cesar Gustavo Rodriguez’s Specialties:

I am primarily an animator but I have experience with every aspect of creating CGI characters including, modeling, texturing, rigging and lighting & rendering. I have extensive experience with all of the following: MAYA, Photoshop, Body Paint 3D, Motion Builder, Shake, Premiere, Microsoft Office Applications, and Perforce. I also posses a wide range of traditional art skills including painting and sculpture.

  • Associate Animator

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; ERTS; Computer Games industry)

    June 2005 — October 2007 (2 years 5 months)

    I began working as a character animator for EA right out of college and I had the opportunity to work with talented veteran film animators who helped me hone my craft.

    My primary duties were creating animation for MADDEN, NCAA Football, NFL Head Coach and NFL Tour using both key framing and motion capture techniques. I also did some pre-production work such as story boards and animatics for MADDEN and Head Coach. This work was originally done using MAYA but after about a year the team transitioned to working with Motion Builder.
