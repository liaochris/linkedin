Lalatendu Satpathy’s Summary

I recently graduated from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) with a Masters' degree in Human Computer Interaction. I also hold a Master of Science degree in Architecture from Mississippi State University (MSU) with a special concentration in design research and informatics. During my graduate work at MSU I received the Architectural Research Centers Consortium (ARCC) King Medal for my research on providing healthcare to elderly people with the help of assistive technology.

As a trained architect I have over two years of experience as a design specialist from various architectural firms in India. These days I am finding myself more into painting and have started my first oil-painting. I also love web site designing and am planning to redesign my existing social networking websites for Architects using the web 2.0 concept.
