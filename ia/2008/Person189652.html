TinTin Nguyen’s Summary

A good insurance agent doesn't just quote, he Risk Manage his/her client... I say "risk manage" because there's more to insurance than meets the eye. In a nut shell, I believe that insurance is like a good defense. Like in sports such as basketball, football even poker, the offense win championships, but the defense win the games.

We operate our lives on the offense. Everything we do, from working to provide for our family, investing, buying our home, having children, these are all offensive moves to gain a few yards, build a future or to make the extra cash. But without a proper defense, one single wrong move can cost the game or in the real world it can wipe out everything that you've worked for. One major accident can keep you from working and without someone paying the mortgage, utilities and credit cards, your offensive will crumble. That is why it is important to work with an agent to make sure that you are prepared, to make certain you are properly insured, and to know that what you've worked for is secure. From your auto to home, life insurance or renters insurance, a good agent can build an insurance program without gaps or overlaps in coverage and of course at the lowest possible cost.
