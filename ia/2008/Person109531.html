Kunal Mahajan’s Summary

Generated business worth 85 MT of Engineering Thermoplastics for various niche applications for various clients worth US$ 425,000.

As a part of a 4 member team, researched into causes for high haze values in Barium-Sulfate and Polymer composites and succeeded in reproducing and establishing the proof of concept for the "Barium Sulfate based Transparent, X-Ray opaque plastics composites" in a period of 3 months which created an NPI (New Product Inrtroduction) for application into medical equipments and high specific gravity transparent
composites.

• With a 3 member team, completed mechanical and electrical testing studies on GE LNP FARADEX(TM) which is an EMI shielding product in GE Plastics Portfolio. Although could not succeed in establishing the parameters for improving mechanicals and shielding efficiency for the composite simultaneously, made some significant key learnings from the processes used for preparation of the
composite.

• As a part of a 2 member team, conducted experiments on thermal degradation studies on two colors for coloration of GE LNP Verton product (GE Plastics range of long-glass fibre reinforced products) and have achieved success in establishing thermal stability of 250oC for 40min, for in-house synthesized colors (one of which is the color patented by me and the colors team in JFWTC) which shall eliminate the need for a special extrusion line for coloration of Verton class of GE Plastics.
