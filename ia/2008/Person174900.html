The reason that I am on LinkedIn is for visibility and to connect with clients and candidates. My vision is to make a profound difference in the success of people and companies.

My specialty is placing CPAs within Public Accounting
What I do for companies
• Help companies recruit top talented accounting professionals.
• Our focus is on a cultural match in addition to skills and talent
• Impact our client’s retention rate
• Increase profitability by reducing costs.
• Take away the hassles associated with recruiting and allowing our client to spend their time on managing the business. Which is what they are good at.

The benefits my clients get are:
• Retain good talent
• Moral in the company is increased and so is the profitability.
