Dale Dunphy’s Summary

In 2007, I joined with John Robison in SkillSearch Recruiting, based in Fredericton, New Brunswick Canada.

We assist skilled resources to find positions within Atlantic Canada under various Federal and Provincial immigration programs. We also assist investors who wish to invest actively or passively in new or existing businesses in Canada.

I am 1st Vice President of the Fredericton Chamber of Commerce. On May 22, 2008 I stand for election as Chamber President for the year 2008-09.

I was a founding partner with Halifax Global Inc., headquartered in Halifax Nova Scotia since 2003. Halifax Global is a professional management consultancy specializing in Business and Strategic Planning.

I worked for 16 years for DMR Consulting Group and left in 2000 having achieved Director of Technology Consulting.

Dale Dunphy’s Specialties:
