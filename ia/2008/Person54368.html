Andres "Dre" Armeda’s Summary

Andres Armeda is the Chief Technology Officer at Secure-i Inc., and has over 12 years of military and private sector enterprise-level network engineering expertise and project management experience.

Secure-i, Inc. is an online service provider of On Demand Surveillance Solutions. With his visionary technical knowledge, Andres conceptualized the technical vision of Secure-i Inc. and manages all of the companies technology divisions. He leads a team that has created a turn key on demand surveillance solution that is revolutionizing the surveillance world.

Secure-i Inc. was conceptualized from the team at Secure Integrations, Inc., Mr. Armeda was serving as the Director of Technology for Secure integrations, Inc. While leading the engineering and IT staff at Secure Integrations, Mr. Armeda managed the engineering team in creating, what was coined at the time, the largest indoor mesh network deployment using Firetide Mesh Technologies. The 30-node, 60+ camera application facilitates full IP video and wireless access.

Prior to joining Secure Integrations, Mr. Armeda was the technical decision maker as the Vice President of Engineering during the due diligence state of M&A at Applied Watch Technologies following his successful military career. During his time at Applied Watch he oversaw all operations of the software development cycle as well as all in-house IT responsibilities.

Mr. Armeda managed a team of 20 network engineers as network manager to over 300,000 users on the United States Navy Marine Corps Intranet (NMCI).

Mr. Armeda was the Operations Manager at Naval Satellite Communications Station, Lago Patria Italy, the US Navy’s premiere Standard Tactical Entry Point (STEP).
