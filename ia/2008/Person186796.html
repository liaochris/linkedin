Terry Carmichael’s Summary

Terry Carmichael has over 17 years experience in biotech marketing and sales. Starting out in a DNA lab, Terry accelerated his career into biotech sales, product management and business development. In 1997, Terry founded GeneTree, one of the first successful companies to provide direct-to-consumer DNA testing services. While with GeneTree, Terry placed the first human DNA testing product, a paternity test kit, on the shelf in a national retail drugstore (Rite-Aid) and a regional chain of superstores (Meijer). These products are currently being sold under the Identigene brand. Terry sold the GeneTree brand and assets to the billion dollar Sorenson Development, Inc. in 2001.

In 2002, Terry demonstrated his charter as a true serial entrepreneur when he founded the Biotech Learning Institute, a skilled e-Learning organization providing training solutions to professionals and corporations within the biotech, clinical research, pharmaceutical, and healthcare industries. In 2006, the Biotech Learning Institute merged its ecommerce and lead generating capabilities with GeneEd, the Institute’s online course content provider.

Terry’s career in the commercial biotech industry started back in 1995, when Terry received a Master’s Degree in Molecular Medicine and Genetics from Wayne State University School of Medicine. After working in an academic laboratory for nearly 3 years, Terry was hired by QIAGEN, the leader in providing DNA purification solutions to the life sciences industry. Then, in 1999, Terry completed a Professional Designation in Marketing and Sales offered through UCLA's Extension Program, and became Product Manager for Bio-Rad’s Gene Technology business unit, where Terry successfully developed and launched several high-throughput and manual DNA purification products.

Terry is currently Director of Internet Marketing & Sales for GeneEd, a provider of online training solutions for the pharmaceutical, clinical research and biomanufacturing industries.

Terry Carmichael’s Specialties:

Marketing consumer oriented DNA tests and skilled training services; creating new distribution channels; developing online and offline ad communication strategies targeting specific audiences; new product development; and selling to high-level positions in pharmaceutical, biotech, life sciences, and clinical research organizations. Terry has an exceptional understanding of internet marketing tactics and has demonstrated success in selling to all levels of organizations in several disciplines.
