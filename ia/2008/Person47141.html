David Marnell’s Summary

I started working within the recruitment sector in London in 1996 and mid 2000 I moved over to Ireland. Since then I have worked with national and international firms in the capacity of Snr Business Consultant and Manager. In total I have managed contract and permanent sales & recruitment teams supporting divisions within IT, Financial Services, Engineering, Sales & Marketing, Accountancy and Business Support.

David Marnell’s Specialties:

A proven background in providing high calibre candidates and project teams to the financial services industry, conventional blue-chip organisations, SME's and system integrators in all aspects of the IT & Finance arena. Equal experience in sourcing contract and permanent candidates for the life cycle of any given project and systems inclusive.
