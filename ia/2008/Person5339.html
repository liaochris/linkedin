Alan Mokbel’s Summary

I welcome all invitations, I'm a LION - send them to "alan [at] mokbel.ca". I'd appreciate a quick intro with your invitation, but it's not necessary.

My career has set me to the path of water and wastewater treatment with a specialty in membrane technology, which includes reverse osmosis, nanofiltration, ultrafiltration, microfiltration and membrane contactors. My expertise lies in technical solution development, membrane treatment design, project management and sales.
