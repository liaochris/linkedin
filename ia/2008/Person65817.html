Fred Bateman’s Summary

Based in San Francisco, The Bateman Group is a new full-service public relations firm focused mainly on infrastructure and emerging technology companies. Responding to market demand for more flexible, results-driven and cost-efficient PR services, the Bateman Group's distinct approach is characterized by a senior-level staffing model, smaller, more focused account teams, exceptional writing product and technical content expertise.

To maximize the value we deliver, The Bateman Group focuses on the following technology domains:

1) Software, for enterprise and industries, spanning business applications, infrastructure and tools, as well as for vertical industries, like manufacturing, healthcare and financial services;

2) Systems, including large telecommunications networks, enterprise networks and storage, security systems and consumer electronics devices;

3) Services, typically on the Internet, for businesses and consumers; and

4) Components, based on “building block” technologies like silicon, nanotechnology and optics.

The benefits are clear: You get experienced PR professionals who not only understand your business, but they have a longer-term perspective on your market as well. What worked? What didn't? Your account team will have an intuitive sense of market history that will be invaluable in guiding your PR strategy going forward.

Current clients include: Aspect Software, ATC-Onlane, Baynote, CITTIO, Guardian Analytics, InQuira, KickApps, MOJIX, Motorola, The Open Group, Panda Security and TIBCO Software (Nasdaq: TIBX)

Fred Bateman’s Specialties:

Strategic communications counsel, messaging/positioning workshops, writing services, industry analyst relations, business and trade media relations, vertical market programs, customer promotion, thought leadership programs, executive visibility, product review and awards programs, media training, competitive research and perception evaluation.
