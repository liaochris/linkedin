Kathy Dunn’s Summary

After working for over 20 years in the Non-Profit Sector in a wide variety of Marketing, Fundraising, P.R., Development, Volunteer Management and learning as I go all things needed in the computer world... Finally took a break, explored life, and started my own business.

I also had my hobby of dog training with my own dogs and being a member of Nor Cal Dog Training Club, doing Agility, Flyball, Tracking, Obedience. So became a dog trainer and now train dogs independently, with Nor Cal and with Petco.

I love to speak, do speaking engagements, sing, lead worship. My heart is to serve and to inspire especially in the face of crisis and tragedies.
