Ryan D Johnson’s Summary

Presently, Inner Fence is working on iPhone apps focused on small business and business travelers. Our flagship app is Credit Card Terminal for iPhone, which allows merchants to charge cards using their Authorize.Net account.

Prior to that, we launched Photosleeve, which provides simple, fast photo sharing for consumers. Photosleeve is still in active production.

For five years out of college, I worked at Microsoft on authentication protocols. My final position was as a Senior SDE on the team developing what is now called "Geneva". I also have fond memories of my two college internships at Microsoft, where I worked on Kerberos-related projects.
