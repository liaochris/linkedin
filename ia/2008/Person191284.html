Tom Rowan’s Summary

I love to get my hands dirty and actually install and manage hardware and software implementations. In particular, I am well versed in several firewall technologies, in content analysis tools for both email and web data.

I understand Windows and UNIX security - system hardening and intrusion detection. I understand both the need and the mechanisms for encrypting information.

Technical documentation is a particular forte of mine - I insist that all clients receive a decent document to support any project.

In addition, I am a practised technology trainer. I am well used to running training courses and workshops. I work with concepts rather than procedure. I focus on student driven learning; on letting people learn for themselves.

I am also a freelance writer, focusing on technical documentation, and feature writing. I am flexible enough to research and write on a very wide range of subject material.

I am confident, enthusiastic and self-motivated. As an effective independent learner, I am often able to undertake new tasks without a formal training requirement.

Firewalls, hacking, pentration testing, managed services, intrusion detection, IDS, IPS, Unix, Linux, NetBSD, IPSO, Mac OS X, Nokia, Checkpoint, NetScreen.
