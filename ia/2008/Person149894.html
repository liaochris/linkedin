Peter Gilliland’s Summary

Peter has extensive experience in the full range of Project Management responsibilities from project initiation, planning, execution, monitoring & controlling to closing, with specialisation in the mobile radio & telecoms infrastructure industry.

Peter has achieved certification as a Project Management Professional (PMP®), and is a member of the Project Management Institute (PMI®). He has a Masters Certificate in Project Management awarded by George Washington University, a Diploma in Action-Based Training from Atlantic International University, and is a certified Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) practitioner and coach with the American Board of NLP (ABNLP). He was awarded a Higher National Diploma in Electrical and Electronic Engineering during his education in England.

With his extensive working experience in the Project Management field, he is now delivering high-impact training courses in association with various training providers in the Asia Region.

Peter Gilliland’s Specialties:

Peter is a firm promoter of the concept of lifelong-learning, and applies NLP technology in all his training material to give participants the visual, auditory, and kinesthetic experiences that allow better, faster learning in a fun and inclusive environment.

His ability, sense of humour, and working knowledge enable him to deliver memorable training at all levels.
