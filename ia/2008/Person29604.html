Byron Lewis’s Summary

A Business Development professional with extensive marketing, sales and management experience in the B2B and B2C sectors. Accomplished in business ‘start ups’, creating and developing commercial concepts, retail management and asset management. A strategic thinker, with a proactive approach to investment, the ability to interpret trends, identify new business opportunities and advise on acquisitions. A team player and excellent communicator, financial reporting at Board level and strongly profit focused.
