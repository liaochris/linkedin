Stan Webb’s Summary

I have 18+ years of professional film and video editing experience. The majority of it has been on animated feature films, but I have also edited commercials, music videos, short films, documentaries and live action. I have experience with various styles of animation including traditional cel, stop motion and CG. I began my career cutting film on a flatbed before becoming one of the first editors to use the Avid Film Composer to cut an animated feature film. I'm currently cutting a independent live action feature using Apple’s Final Cut Pro. My love of storytelling is what initially drew me to film editing and I continue to spend many hours studying and attending seminars and lectures exploring the storytelling process. I have experience setting up new production studios as well as working at established ones and I am very capable of managing and handling all aspects of postproduction.

Stan Webb’s Specialties:

• editing the storyreel while establishing a cinematic pace and rhythm.
• helping to create a strong and interesting story structure.
• selecting and cutting production and scratch dialog.
• managing recording sessions to insure technical and creative quality.
• helping to select the blocking and camera angles during layout.
• spotting, selecting and cutting temp music and sound effects.
• meeting deadlines and efficiently scheduling all necessary resources.
