Brian Lucier bel@lid.com TopLinked.com’s Summary

Brian has been an integral part of creative design teams for over twenty-five years. In that time he has held a variety of roles, from Art Director of Granitar Corporation building e-commerce web sites and intranets for Fortune 500 Companies to Creative Director of LID creating branding and online strategies for many clients, including Bose Corporation, Fidelity Investments, Fallon Community Health Plan, Cingular, CCM, and both the American and National Hockey League.

Brian’s career before Creative Concepts includes years of valuable award-winning experience in the graphic arts field, working hands-on with clients to develop their online brand strategies from initial concept creation to completion. Brian is well versed in all aspects of illustration, animation, and graphic design techniques, not only with high-end internet graphics but also with traditional marketing solutions such as corporate logo design and quality print collateral.

In addition to his current role, Brian is on the Professional Advisory Panel for Mount Wachusett Community College for the advancement of their Computer Graphics Degree program. He also teaches computer design courses through Continuing Education at Massachusetts College of Art in Boston.

Brian earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts in Illustration from Massachusetts College of Art where he received Academic Honors and had a major interest in computer graphics, print, and color theory. He has done certified post-graduate studies at the American Graphics Institute, and professional training at Cyber Solutions, Inc. and the Adobe Professional Training Series.
