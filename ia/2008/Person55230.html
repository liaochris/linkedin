Duncan Bradley’s Summary

I began my career within IT recruitment after graduation from the University of Liverpool (England) in the year 2000 with an LLB Honours in Law.

As a recruitment consultant my role includes both contingency and retained permanent recruitment for clients both domestic and international, ranging from start up companies through to multinationals such as Hewlett Packard, IBM, Ciber, Experian, Dassault Systèmes, PTC, Sage, CSC, Fujitsu Siemens, Avid Technology and LexisNexis.

In summary my role includes:

• New business generation and account management of existing clients.
• Discussing business requirements and detailing job specifications.
• Agreeing financial terms.
• Marketing and advert writing.
• Candidate search and selection; including telephone and face to face interviewing, qualification of suitability, profiling and introduction to clients.
• Subsequent Interview preparation and debriefing for both clients and candidates.
• Offer negotiation and assistance throughout resignation and joining process.
• Billing preparation and administration.


As the consistently top performing consultant within the company I have also been promoted to help train, develop and motiavte the team of new consultants.
