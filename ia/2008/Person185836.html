Fabiano de Queiroz "Tatu"’s Summary

I started my career as a product designer, creating commercial furniture for an Italian company. But the technical projects remained in the background when I started to illustrate my creations on a 3D software. The graphic design became my priority when I was moved from the technical for a creative department and the internet became part of my life, because I learned to do some Flash presentations for international clients.

In 2002 I started to work on a small internet producer company. The challenge was very big, because I didn't knew how to made an internet site and the salary was smaller too. It was a great time, because I done big ideas with small budgets.

December 2002. I had an opportunity to join the Brazil's largest interactive advertising agency. I started at AgenciaClick on a design department and 8 months later, I was promoted to an Art Director, and I done some important works for clients such as Coca-Cola, Fiat, MSN, Sadia (the largest Brazil's food company), Bradesco (the largest Brazil's private bank), Johnny Walker, among others. I won some Advertising Internacional Awards, like OneShow, Clio, Art Directors, El Ojo de Iberoamerica, Fiap and 3 Cyber Lions on Cannes Festival (one gold and two bronzes).

Before 2 years and 9 months, on July 2006, I moved to McCann Erickson Brazil, to work as an Sr. Interactive Art Director. I create and coordinate important interactive advertising campaigns to clients such as MasterCard, General Motors, Johnson&Johnson, Nestle, Cadbury Adams, Santander, Unilever, among others.
