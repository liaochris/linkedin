Jim Kimo Storke’s Summary

Kimo has 20 years of business experience and 6 years of U.S. military experience. 16 of the twenty years in marketing/business development management and 4 years of Venture Capital Funding and Energy.

He owns KAS consulting. An advisement and consulting firm specializing in the areas of marketing and business development

Kimo has created strategic plans for companies in the areas of;

Software Web2.0 firm – Business Process Management
Media and Web Design- B2B, SEO, Flash and Video websites
Oil and Gas exploration – Upstream
Alternative Energy - BioDiesel

He is one of the founders of ForeRunner Financial Inc. in Austin, Texas which focuses on Energy (Oil and Gas, Alt Energy) projects and offerings.

He is also a partner at Donaldson Financial Incorporated, a loan consulting firm that assists in the funding of large scale "Green Energy" projects from $15M to $300M

Kimo was a Global Offering Manager at IBM for 3 years working as a Product Manager of managers in the areas of E-learning, On-demand services (now referred to as web services), and web services offerings with ISV's for IBM's Hosting group

Kimo was a Regional Manager of Business Development at RSM McGladrey for 4 years, The seventh largest accounting/consulting firm in the nation. He specialized in the areas of groupware, and knowledge management with Lotus Notes

He is the Co-leader of a WorkLife Ministry at Hill Country Bible Church NW which connects over 30 different vertical marketplace sectors for the purpose of building community and promoting philanthropic activity in Austin Texas.
