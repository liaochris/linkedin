Robert Prus’s Summary

I consider myself as person with strong technical skills but with sales oriented approach.

I served on different technical sales positions during past > 8 years.

I do have > 22 years expertise in computer systems (hardware, operating systems).

I do have a lot of experience with training other people in form of presentations, workshops, trainings, transfers-of-information.

I do like to learn myself new things and teach other people.

My goal is to solve complex problems related to computer systems and IT technology.

Details of my expertise in computer systems (hardware, operating systems):

- Processors and servers architecture & technology. As of today it is mainly universe of SPARC and X64 (AMD Opteron, Intel Xeon) and their competition.

- Operating systems. a) different flavours of unix (as of today it is mainly Solaris/OpenSolaris, but I had experience with a number of forgotten *ix systems from the past), b) Linux'es, c) some other OS'es.

- Application and OS tuning for performance. Benchmarking.

- System virtualization technologies.

- Computer, system and network security.

- Computer graphics, data & scientific visualization, computer algebra (Mathematica).

- Programming (C, C++, shell scripts, PERL, some other languages).

- HPC - High Performance Computing.
