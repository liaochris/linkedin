Vincenza De Petrillo’s Summary

Got my Graphology Diploma at “Scuola diretta a fini speciali di studi grafologici” - Urbino University (specialising in Couple and Family Graphological Counselling).
Family Graphology teacher at the “Istituto Toscano di Scienze Grafologiche” - Florence (1998-99).
From 1998 to 2004, Graphology teacher at L.U.M.S.A University – Rome.

Author of some publications.

Speaker at national and international seminars and congresses.

I shall mention just a few international meetings I had the privilege to be invited to: “The Moretti system” at the British Academy of Graphology, London, 1994 and 1995 - "The Compatibility in the Couple" at the International Congress of Graphology organized by N.V.G.S., The Hague, 1996 - “The Couple graphological Consultant” (workshop) at the International Graphological Colloquium, Barcelona, 2000 - “The Symbolic Dimension of Handwriting” at the 1st Symposium of Greek and Foreign Graphologists, organised by the Hellenic Institute of Graphology, Athens, 2004.

In 1999, under the auspices of LUMSA - Libera Università Maria S. Assunta, Rome, and Urbino University, and the Moretti Graphological Institute, I collaborated with the International Graphological Colloquium to set up a seminar on "The Moretti System" (Urbino, 7-12 Sep.) addressed at foreign graphologists from eleven countries.

Former member of B.A.o.G - British Academy of Graphology; Member of A.G.I. - Associazione Grafologica Italiana

Favourite fields of application: individual and compatibility analyses as well as vocational guidance graphology.

As a professional graphologist, my major goal - when outlining a personality profile: to help an individual, a couple, or a family see inner and outer reality more objectively, laying the stress on the resources and assets each one possesses in order to take life in his or her hands and give change a chance.
