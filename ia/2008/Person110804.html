My professional experience is large even though I am still a student. All through my university education I have held jobs, mostly relevant to my education. Previous to this, I have had sales positions in Denmark, Norway and England.
Besides the jobs mentioned in my profile, I have worked at a local computer shop assembling and selling computers, worked as a consultant/IT handyman for a local accounting firm (now merged with an international accounting company), worked as an all-round IT handyman for a small but international software company doing many different tasks and more.
Currently, I am writing my masters thesis titled "Dynamic network compression in DUT" (DUT is the digital pronunciation trainer project mentioned in my jobs section, which is one of the jobs I currently hold). I am very goal oriented and I work every day of the week - there's always some project to work on. I expect to move to the U.S. within the next five years to gain international experience and return back to Europe at a later time.
