John Nilon’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    J.N. Solutions Executive Search

    (Pharmaceuticals industry)

    April 2001 — Present (6 years 11 months)

    J.N. Solutions provides executive search services to the life sciences industry. The majority of our searches are conducted to identify senior executives and we often subsequently recruit team members for those that we have placed. We are driven by quality and our process is proactive and targeted. Our goal is to present a small number (3-6) of highly qualified candidates to our clients. We provide a level of service and feedback that enables our client’s to efficiently identify and attract the best people.

    • We conduct searches to identify Directors through C-level executives.

    • Our customers include major pharmaceutical companies, biotechnology companies and
    clinical research organizations.

    • We offer a unique fee agreement to align our interests with those of our customers. We eliminate the conflict of interest inherent in most search firms’ contracts.

    • We are creative and flexible; we work with our clients to find a solution to satisfy their needs.
