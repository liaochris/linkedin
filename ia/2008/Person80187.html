Dave Hughes’s Summary

I have over 24 years in broadcast radio, with over 20 state and national awards for writing and producing radio commercials. I have done numerous freelance vocal jobs while persuing my career, as well as writing a weekly humor column for various local newspapers, which was chosen as the "Best General Interest Column" by the Mississippi Press Association.

In addition, I was one of the four partners that originally formed Kudobang Inc., the parent company of MMORadio, an internet-only radio station devoted to MMORPGs. During my tenure at MMORadio, I helped lead the station to a good working relationship with many MMORPG publishers, which culminated in MMORadio becoming the first internet-only radio station to broadcast live from the floor of E3 in Los Angeles for all three days of the Expo.

I, along with most of the others associtated with MMORadio at that time, left the company in the winter of 2004 and formed World of Roleplayers Radio, an internet-only station devoted to gamers of all types.

My ultimate goal is to find my way into the world of the gaming industry, whether through my vocal talents, writing skills, or other avenues. At the present time, I'm currently seeing a writing career start to grow, with my own personal blog devoted to the MMORPG genre, http://lagorama.com, as well as paid commission work for other blogs.

Dave Hughes’s Specialties:

Voice work (including a range of deliveries and styles, including multiple character voices), writing (whether it be advertising copy, humor, or dramatic), and well-developed management skills, both "face to face" and remotely.
