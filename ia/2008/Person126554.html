Matthew Baran’s Summary

My current architectural experience ranges from the design of conventional multifamily and single family residential developments to the exploration of portable, adaptable, sustainable architectural systems. I aspire to continue to push the fusion of theory with an efficient and affordable practice. I am currently in the process of forming a development arm of 'architecture' to promote the construction of green, affordable residences.
