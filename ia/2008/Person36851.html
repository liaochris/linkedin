Christopher Higgins’s Summary

I was just another video gamer until I was invited to a summit at Sony Online Entertainment to talk about Star Wars Galaxies. When the summit was complete I met Garrett Fuller who suggested I write an article on the summit. The article never ran but I was asked to cover SWG news at SOE Gamer Day in June 2007 for Ten Ton Hammer.com. After seeing my article run for the first time I knew this was something I wanted to pursue further.

Since then I have covered events and wrote reports for Ten Ton Hammer.com covering changes affecting the Star Wars Galaxies MMORPG. I also pushed to get Star Wars Galaxies forums created on Ten Ton Hammers forums. Near the end of 2007 a few friends and I decided to try making a podcast. This podcast was going to be different and aimed at humor more than the serious podcasts about MMOs. Also this podcast was going to cover many games vs. a single game. Planning began in January 2007 with a targeted record date of April 2007. By the end of April 2007 we had recorded 2 podcasts, created a website, and created forums to develop a community. After 4 episodes real life began to present problems in recording the podcast and because this podcast was developed with the 3 of us in mind we all decided it was best to put the show on hold instead of changing the format.

At this point I am not sure how I am going to proceed. Right now I am developing a website with a blog but I am still deciding where I want to go with video games and learning more and more doing my own projects.
