Chris Prather’s Summary

Working with web related technologies for a decade now, I have focused on back end web work mostly. The gluey bits behind the whizz-bang GUI, and the quick and dirty web applications that get pushed out and never replaced. Recently I have done work with Swing applets, and spent time working on developing high performance whizz-bang GUIs myself for the financial arena.

Beyond that, I'm responsible for Bender, the first IRC chatter bot to be elected Perl Monger leader. I spent time unsuccessfully trying to figure out how to bring the fun world of RDF to the real time fixed income business, and now I am paid to wrangle Moose in the Perl community.
