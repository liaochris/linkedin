Cassie Hower’s Summary

I've worked as an administration assistant, secretary, personal assistant and paralegal in numerous office environments over the last 12 years, including a not for profit organisation, movie studio and many law firms throughout Queensland and in London.

I am now the proud owner of an online administration business as a Virtual Assistant. Your Extra Office provides administration support remotely from my home office.

My long term goals are to build up the business to become very profitable. Eventually I'd like to have staff so that I can take on more work and do less of it myself. Most importantly I want to help others achieve that balance that we're all looking for. My short term goals are to learn something new and exciting every day about the VA industry and the technology involved including how the internet can help my business grow.

Cassie Hower’s Specialties:

Administration for legal, small businesses, not for profit. I offer many services from general word processing to database maintenance, from arranging travel needs to virtual office management.

Please visit my website for a more detailed overview of Your Extra Office. www.yourextraoffice.com.au
