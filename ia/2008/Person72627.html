Greg Outlaw’s Summary

Greg Outlaw co-founded AllAboutGOD.com in 2002 and is the volunteer President and CEO. AllAboutGOD.com (AAG) is the largest evangelical outreach in terms of impact on the Internet using search engine optimization (SEO), social media optimization (SMO), and video search engine optimization (VSEO) techniques to drive surgically targeted traffic. In 2007, AllAboutGOD.com saw over 22.7 million visitors to its websites, nearly 18 million gospel presentations, and 104,875 people indicate a decision for Jesus from every country in the world! - That's a gospel presentation every 1.7 seconds and a decision for Christ every 5 minutes. The Lord has done all of this on an annual budget of only $250,000. Greg has spoken numerous times at the NRB, CMA, Internet Evangelism Coalition, Christian Internet Marketing Conference, and Liberty University. Greg sits on the Lausanne IT Committee for the 2010 Congress in Capetown, South Africa. Greg serves without pay as the President & CEO of AllAboutGOD Ministries, Inc. three to four days a week. Greg makes his living by selectively consulting on Internet marketing for a few Christian ministries and Christian-owned/controlled businesses in the areas of SEO, SMO, and VSEO.

Greg Outlaw’s Specialties:

Practitioner Consultant in search engine optimization (SEO), social media optimization (SMO), and video search engine optimization (VSEO) techniques to drive surgically targeted traffic.
