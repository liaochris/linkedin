Casey Clark Ney’s Summary

Howdy! Welcome to my profile. I am a fulltime freelance writer/graphic designer who wants to write for you. Check out the details below for information on my experience and what I bring to the table as a professional and freelancer.

Newspaper Writing
I have more than six years experience in newspaper writing for a daily publication. I began my journalism career writing education stories which evolved into feature writing. From there I moved up to Lifestyle Editor and then Associate Editor. Because of my experience in this field, I am fluent in the Associated Press Style Guide. I left the newspaper industry in pursuit of bigger career opportunities. Currently, I freelance for the paper I once called home. Mainly I write and layout special sections. I also fill in when needed for other projects. During my newspaper career I won numerous awards that can be viewed on my personal website at www.caseyclarkney.com.

Academic Writing
I am a strong academic writer with a BA in communications. My main focus in college was communication theories which translated into a lot of research papers. I never brought home anything less then an A on my college papers. I am fluent in the MLA and AP Style Guides as well.

SEO Writing
I have done a lot of SEO writing in the past and currently a considerable amount of my work comes in this form. You can view samples of my SEO writing in my iFreelance portfolio and on my Web site.

Summary
In a nutshell, I work hard and I want to work hard for you. I don’t charge outrageous prices, just enough so I can afford to live. I want to make you happy because that makes me happy. I want to write because my inner-being thrives on it. I’m a good person and a good freelancer. For more information on my skills and to view samples of my work, visit my Web site at www.caseyclarkney.com.
