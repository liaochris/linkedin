Boaz Babai’s Summary

Boaz Babai is the Founder & CEO of ARPU~UP, a Web 2.0 Marketing Strategy & Search Engine Optimization consulting firm. ARPU~UP is providing award-winning Internet Marketing and Digital Identity Creation Strategies to innovative business startup companies in Israel, Europe and US.

ARPU~UP is currently providing internet marketing consulting services to leading business caompanies such as:
> Mobixell Networks - Leading provider of advanced mobile multimedia solutions.
> DeveloPrint - Digital printing campaign management consulting company.
> innerActive Smart Media - Ad-funded mobile advertising solution provider firm.
> JagagMobile - A provider of Mobile WAP sites creator tool.

In the last two years ARPU~UP successfully completed online marketing projects for those innovative startups:
> Sportingo - A User-Generetad Content sports community website.
> MetaCafe - A Web 2.0 video sharing startup company.
> SupportSpace - Innovative online tech support stratup.
> BeInSync - File sharing & online backup startup that recently acquired by Phoenix Technologies.
> EPOS - A leading provider of advanced digital positioning technology.

As a leading social marketing strategist in Israe, Babai is a senior lecturer about Web 2.0 marketing in the MBA marketing courses at the Interdisciplinary Center (IDC), Bar-Ilan University and the Collage of Management.

Babai holds MA degree in International Relationship from the Hebrew University, Jerusalem, and MBA in Finance from Interdisciplinary Center, Herzeliya.

Boaz Babai’s Specialties:

Web Marketing Strategy | Search Engine Marketing (SEM) | Natural Search Engine Optimization (SEO) | Content Management | Link Strategy | Social Media Optimization (SMO) | PPC Campaigns Management | Corporate Blog Marketing | Interactive Word of Mouth Marketing (WoMM) | Press Release (PR) Optimization and Internet Delivery | Social Networking Marketing (Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn) | Smart Online Guerrilla Marketing services (Digg, Yahoo Answer, Propeller).
