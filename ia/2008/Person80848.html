Isaiah McGee’s Summary

The Des Moines Register has called Isaiah McGee "One of Des Moines' Movers and Shakers under 40” and those who know Isaiah know he is a passionate visionary concerned about people reaching their full potential.

McGee is owner of McGee Strategies LLC, a consulting, training, and coaching firm focused on seeking resources and solutions for individuals, organizations, and businesses that lead to growth, engagement, functionality and fulfillment. McGee Strategies specializes in the areas of generational competencies, professional relationship building, work engagement, and social networking

Born and raised in South Central Los Angeles. Isaiah attended Cornell College, where he was a two-sport athlete in football and wrestling, hosted a popular radio program, and served in many leadership roles. Isaiah also received a Master’s degree in Education from Drake University in Des Moines.

McGee has established bipartisan relationships with officials at every level of government and has successfully worked on numerous issues from local ballot initiatives to campaigns for local, state, and federal offices. At age 26, Isaiah was elected to the city council of Waukee, one of the fastest growing communities in the nation

Isaiah has also received recognition for his leadership and efforts in a number of organizations and non-profit boards including the Greater Des Moines Partnership, Jaycees, YSS, the African-American Chamber and other civic and leadership groups

Isaiah has been featured in numerous national, state, and local press and media, including Time Magazine, the New York Times, Associated Press, NPR, MSNBC, FOX News, Des Moines Register, Des Moines Business Record, local TV and radio.
