Robert Grant’s Summary

I am a creative, digitally focused professional specialising in technologically innovative marketing and communications solutions and developing converged products and services. I bring in-depth understanding from strategic, technical, creative and sales & marketing perspectives, I am a strong leader with good relationship building skills, the ability to manage and collaborate across functions and I can influence at CXO level.

My core strengths are along the lines of brand extension, innovation and growth strategy in the digital space which can mean anything from web development to improving SEO/SEM to developing a new widget to looking at strategic relationships or looking for new ways to reach the audience or ways to reach new audiences. I'm marketing-led, either tactical or strategic but I am also operationally used to managing outsource relationships with both clients and suppliers, managing live services, liaising with key stake-holders and monitoring SLAs and customer service. As project lead I generally start at the requirements gathering stage, develop use-cases, write functional specifications, develop the business case and liaise with designers and developers to shepherd a project through to completion, and this encompasses all aspects of IA, design, usability, accessibility and compliance.

I have an excellent track record of delivering complex, high quality solutions on time and on budget, an extensive and valuable skill set across business management, operations and development and as my background encompasses both technical and marketing disciplines, I pride myself on my ability to effectively communicate across all implementation groups and to lead, motivate and build strong teams.
