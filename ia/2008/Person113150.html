Lorna Hartman’s Summary

I'm an award-winning editor, writer, voiceover specialist, and desktop publisher (prepress services) with 14 years of experience in business communications.

My philosophy is to maintain an open hand with information--to be generous. You get back, in one form or another, what you put out there, and I enjoy putting people together with resources they need or putting them in touch with others. What can I do for you today?

By 2010 I'll have an associate's degree in software development. Long-term, a journalism degree and a computer programming or computer science degree could work well together.

I enjoy running, reading, picking up new skills, international travel, and quality chocolate. Life would not be the same without rejection, humor, and a good embrasure for French horn playing.
