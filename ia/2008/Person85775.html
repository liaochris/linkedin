Jay Kinder’s Summary

Jay Kinder has been a licesed real estate agent for 11 years and has cinched himself among the top agents in the world by closing an amazing 521 transactions in 2005 and continuing to grow his business to levels most agents never dream possible. Amazingly, Jay has been able to accomplish this before he turned 30 years old and all in Lawton Oklahoma where he maintains a 14% of the market for residential sales year after year. Jay has been a highly sought after public speaker and trainer for the past five years and has now partnered with a dynamic real estate agent and friend, Mike Reese, in Frisco Texas and formed Kinder-Reese Real Estate Partners LLC.

Jay Kinder’s Specialties:

Jay Kinder Inc specialize in residetial real estate sales and leasing, mortgage, title, development, new construction, remodeling, commercial sales and leasing.

Kinder-Reese Real Estate Partners is an organization that specializes in taking real estate agents to the highest possible level through customized coaching programs and by utilizing the industries latest and most innovative tools to become the dominant agent in your market.
