Timothy Lamb’s Summary

With experience in emerging market entry strategy both as a consultant and from first hand experience, Timothy brings with him knowledge of the Chinese market and culture coupled with his experience managing companies within China and the United States.

While working for the US Commercial Services, in the United States, Timothy assisted American companies with their market entry strategies throughout Asia Pacific and Eastern Europe. He has successfully managed several companies including a training company through its start up phase in China.

Timothy holds an MBA in International Management from Thunderbird: The Garvin School of International Management and a BA in Cultural Anthropology from the University of New Mexico. He is the Vice-Chair of the Trade and Distribution Work Group, European Union Chamber of Commerce.

Timothy Lamb’s Specialties:

Project Managment, Business Strategy, China Market Entry, Corporate Formation, Business Development, Due Diligence
