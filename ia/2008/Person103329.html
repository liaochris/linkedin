Kathy Mandelstein’s Summary

Kathy Mandelstein has been an integral member of the IBM Rational team since its acquisition, supporting the marketing program and web integration of Rational into IBM, as well as the Rational acquisitions that have occurred since that time.

Kathy is currently responsible for Worldwide Marketing Programs for the IBM Rational Brand which includes advertising, marketing campaigns, event planning and working hand-in-hand with the sales teams and channel teams to deliver results for the Rational brand. She also leads the worldwide IBM marketing program, Effective Software Delivery, focused on technical professionals and their up-line IT management.

Over the past six years, Kathy led ibm.com/developerWorks and ibm.com/alphaWorks, as well as marketing programs to developers for all of IBM. Her past history includes leading various Web development and marketing teams in IBM Software Group. She has focused on software development teams and Business Partners for the last eleven years and began at IBM by leading the Java and e-business campaigns for these audiences.

Before joining IBM in 1997, Kathy managed one of the earliest successful retail Web sites, www.jcpenney.com. Kathy has over 22 years of professional experience in the areas of management, marketing, sales, web development, advertising and corporate communications. Mrs. Mandelstein holds a B.S. from the University of Texas at Austin and a Global MBA from the University of Texas at Dallas.
