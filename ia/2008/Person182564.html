Rick Franz’s Summary

Over the past 40 years I have developed extensive experience developing strategic direction and establishng executable plans for companies.

Burroughs. I served in a variety of sales and management positions including Branch Sales Mgr., District Product Mkg. Mgr., and VP of Program Mgmt. As VP I was responsible for the development and release of one of the most profitable mainframe lines in the history of the Burroughs.

In 1988 I joined Zitel Corp., in Milpitas CA. I played an integral role in transitioning the company from a Solid State Disk provider to a storage subsystems company. My last position was Regional sales Mgr. for the mid-west out of Chicago.

In 1992 I returned to Unisys as Regional Mgr. of a new storage focused organization. The program was highly successful and I was promoted to Director of Mktg. Ops for the Storage Div. in Mission Viejo, CA. In addition to marketing I had the responsibility for developing the Division Strategic Plan and working with Bain and Co. on corporate strategy.

I left Unisys in 1996 and joined QLogic Corp. I was hired at the direction of the Board to generate a long-term strategic plan. Over 12 years QLogic became one of the most successful storage interface and networking companies in the world. During this period I also served as Director of Product Mktg., Director of Corp. Mktg., VP Investor Relations and VP Technology and Planning. As part of my duties I led Merger and Acquisition activities. We successfully identified and acquired of nine companies.

I Left QLogic to join ITU Ventures in LA. as a member of a new Private Equity fund focused on the investment in or acquisition of Media and Technology companies. As a result of the recent, subprime, financial crisis fund's IPO never came to fruition.

I am currently looking for a new executive position in a company that can use the vast experience and talent.
