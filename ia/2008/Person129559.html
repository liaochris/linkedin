Michael D. Brown’s Summary

First Under Secretary of Homeland Security for Emergency Preparedness and Response, confirmed by the U.S. Senate. Director, Federal Emergency Management Agency, Deputy Director and General Counsel for the Federal Emergency Management Agency, confirmed by the U.S. Senate. Original member, Presidential Transition Team creating the Department of Homeland Security, team leader for Emergency Preparedness & Response.

Serve as a government relations advisor, lobbyist, and strategy officer for public and private corporations. CEO of InferX Corporation, a public corporation exploring new frontiers in data mining and protection and security of data. Director/owner of Resilient Corporation, a privately held company focusing on resiliency issues. Director and COO of Charys Corporation, and member of Finance Committee and Governance Committee. Director and COO of Innova Pure Water, a public company specializing in providing new technologies for the delivery of clean water domestically and internationally. CEO of Michael Brown, LLC, providing public policy and strategic directions for companies navigating the public sector.

Public speaking to groups interested in leadership issues, public policy, matters of faith and survival during difficult times, crisis avoidance and management, personal and corporate resilience.

Public speaking worldwide.

Michael D. Brown’s Specialties:

Government relations, strategic planning, public speaking.
