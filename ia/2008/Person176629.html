Andrea Caesar’s Summary

Simplify is a newly started consulting company based in the Lakes Region of New Hampshire which strives to support all aspects of cultural adaptation and language acquisition. Clients of Simplify include New England boarding schools, international students and American students of language.

It is the first of its kind in its services, which range from analyzing, assessing and providing guidance to schools and universities regarding their international student and ESL programs. In addition to this, Simplify offers individulazed support to international students in the form of guardianship, temporary homestays and global citizenship training.

www.simplifyourworld.com

Andrea Caesar’s Specialties:

international student populations, cultural training, boarding school culture, ESL support and consulting, adolescent cultural adaptation, guardianship, language training, cultural training for professional travellers
