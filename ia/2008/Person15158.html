Adam Messner’s Summary

Skills and responsibilities related to my current position as Assistant Director of Admissions at The University of Akron School of Law:

- Develop admission and scholarship award models to facilitate student recruitment and ideal scholarship fund allocation.
- Design and optimization of office and business processes. Recent re-design of office processes resulted in elimination of 99% of application data entry.
- Design and implement databases related to Admissions, Financial Aid, Career Planning, and Academic Success.
- Produce extensive statistical reports for internal and external constituents, including the Assistant Dean of Admissions, the Director of Career Planning, the Director of Academic Success, the School of Law Dean, and the American Bar Association.
- Account for approximately $2.5 million per year in scholarship funds for entering and returning students.
- Maintain the Admissions section of the School of Law web site.
- Coordinate direct e-mail and mail campaigns targeted at prospective law students.
- Make presentations to groups of prospective law students on campus and off campus at open houses, information sessions, and meetings.
- Advise prospective and admitted students regarding the admission and financial aid process.
- Represent the School of Law at recruitment fairs around the country.
