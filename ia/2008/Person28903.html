Bartolome Salas-Manzanedo’s Summary

Senior business executive with more than 15 years experience in Wireless/Telecoms, Media, Entertainment, Internet and Tech markets gained from key management positions in some of the most challenging projects in Europe and successful business relationships with leading companies in Europe and the States.

Customer focused, results-oriented, with clear goal to deliver, a special ability to manage change and complexity -both in markets and organizations- and a personal taste for challenges.

Solid track record of outstanding results, normally achieved through good understading of the challenge faced, creation of winning vision and positioning, design of innovative and integral marketing strategies (to quickly penetrate markets, outperform competition and position the company as the preferred for customers) maximizing opportunities and achieving high growth and return rates in both new and more commodity busnisses.

Beyond mainstream career, have an entrepreneur spirit and a will to contribute to improve Society. I act as a business angel to support a selected number (time is limited) of entrepreneurs and start-up companies, particularly helping in marketing, strategy and management.

Also speak regularly at Industry events (as an industry expert ... so they say), contribute to lecturing in some Business Schools and Management Institutes in entrepreneurs programs, and also lecture in custom educational and training programs for first-tier industry managers.

Bartolome Salas-Manzanedo’s Experience

  • Director - Value Added Services, Mobile Broadband and Convergence Business

    Orange Spain

    (Telecommunications industry)

    2006 — Present (2 years)

    In 2005 Amena is acquired by France Telecom Group and rebranded to Orange in 2006. France Telecom is one of the major global players in Telecoms and a really great project to be part of.

    This new professional stage coincides with one of the most challenging moments in the telecoms/web 2.0/entertainment/media industries. True convergence is taking place, industry boundaries are blurring, business models are changing and competition increasing.

    In this new stage I also Direct the Mobile Broadband (3G+) and Convergence Businesses, in addition to the VAS Business I manage since 2003, with full responsibility for business operations.

    Define vision, strategy and direct annual plans and key operational functions across the year, including marketing, customer management and operations to ensure delivery of business results.

    KPIs I manage are revenues, margin, growth rates, ROI, customer acquisition, market share, churn, customer satisfaction and service usage rates.

  • Private Investor and Business Angel

    Spanish and European markets

    (Venture Capital & Private Equity industry)

    2006 — Present (2 years)

    After 10 years in contact with entrepreneurs and VCs in Spain, EU and the US, and with a will to contribute to Society I act as a Business Angel supporting others to succeed with their business ideas. As my time is scarce, I normally act syndicated in operations referenced by trusted BAs and partners that serve me as pre-screening.

    I only support 1) business I understand in industries of my expertise, 2) prefer end-user services vs. sophisticated middleware techs, 3) all products should be best-in-class, high-growth, with a disruptive business model, service or tech, or hold a unique exclusivity for a specific market.

    I focus on 1) spanish companies in start-up phase (others can also be considered), 2) companies needing turnaround and 3) international start-ups seeking expansion in Spain or other EU markets.

    Entrepreneurs should have a clear vision and ability to perform, give more value to experince than money and be willing to accept a hands-on approach.

  • Director - Value Added Services Business

    Amena - Grupo AUNA

    (Wireless industry)

    2003 — 2006 (3 years)

    End 2003 a new shift to the strategy was taken. The product portfolio was already developed, the alliances were well set and the challenge was moving more towards speeding up adoption of services and accelerating motenization.

    A new Unit for VAS Business Management with direct responsibility on revenues and commercial management of all business activities is created and I lead it .

    Directed development of new marketing vision and positioning for VAS offering, revamped and redefined the commercial value proposition and defined and implementated brand new commercial processes for marketing, sales, customer management and operations.

    I put much focus in introducing new marketing techniques such as true quantitative and ROI marketing, viral marketing, search engine/internet and mobile marketing. It was fun introducing innovation in commercial processes and seeing they can achieve better results and profitability than more taditional marketing approaches.

  • Director - Business Development, Alliances and New Products

    Amena - Grupo AUNA

    (Wireless industry)

    2001 — 2003 (2 years)

    When you are the small one in the market and you want to grow even faster and grab more market than your competitors it is a clever decision to adopt a strategy based on alliances.

    That was our situation in 2001 once the basic service and product offer was built. The focus was put in developing the business jointly with leading companies in entertainment, media, IT, banking industries.

    A new Unit is created and I am appointed to manage it. Identified target companies, defined business models, allocated budget, negotiated the deals and managed their performance through clear governance models. Key for success was to implement a true win-win collaborative model and strong operations management.

    Reached exclusive relationships with leading Groups such as Grupo PRISA, Vocento/ABC and El Mundo; Sogecable/Canal+; Real Madrid; Bankinter or Santander ; and major Record Labels, Videogames Companies and Internet Portals. The model was also extended to the business segment.

  • Director - New Products and Innovation

    Amena

    (Wireless industry)

    2000 — 2001 (1 year)

    As the Company grows, it is decided to create a new Division to manage the development of the Mobile Internet Business, and I am appointed to create it.

    The job included definition of the organization, creation of an optimized product definition and marketing process, SLAs with other Divisions and hiring of 30+ leading professionals, with a goal to outperform competition having fewer resources to compete.

    We constantly refined the product innovation / development process and it turned out to work very well. We achieved some very relevant results as in that period Amena marketed first and better than Telefonica or Vodafone key offers (i.e. LBS, Multimedia and GPRS Services) with our offer also perceived as most competitive and innovative by customers, and quickly equaled competition in data services revenue weight.

    Also, .... some of our products were awarded as one of the best ideas of the year in Spain !!!

  • Manager - New Products and Innovation

    Amena

    (Wireless industry)

    1998 — 2000 (2 years)

    In 1998 I joined Amena, 3rd mobile operator in Spain. The Company (currently with a turnover of 3.000+ MM EUR) was a start-up at that moment. With less than 200 people all was to be done for launch: defining the commercial offer, building all the infrastructure, develop sales channel, build customer processes and operations and make the Company grow itself. That was definitely one of the most challenging (and successful) entrepreneurial projects in Europe.

    My initial responsibilities were related to developing an innovative first Value Added Services offering as well as the Strategic Plan for Mobile Internet (incl. positioning in the value chain, products, offer, partnerships, business plan, resources quantification and roll-out plan).

    I also lead one of the three teams of the Amena’s UMTS License Bid Task Force, achieving award of license. In that period I also collaborated with Amena's own VC Fund (60 Million EUR), evaluating and assessing investment opportunities.
