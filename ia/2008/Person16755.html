Arvi Arunachalam’s Summary

An entrepreneur academician with multi-lingual skills and global consulting experience in capital markets, financial services and large sized publicly traded companies. Past entrepreneurial ventures include founding e-business consulting firm, call termination services for global VoIP firm and trading firm that specialized in sourcing automobiles from Asia to Central America.

Arvi has over nine years of multinational experience encompassing strategy consulting, technology strategy and management, software engineering and entrepreneurial experience in e-business consulting, information technology, telecommunications, and higher education. Fluent in English, Tamil, and has working knowledge of Spanish, French and basic Thai, Arvi has consulted for capital markets, Fortune 100 firms in financial services, retail industry and several large publicly listed firms in the North America and Asia.

Arvi has a bachelor's degree from St. Joseph's College, Trichy, and two Masters degrees: one in Computer Science and another in Finance and now completing his PhD in Finance.
