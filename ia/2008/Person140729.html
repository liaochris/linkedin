Nick Starr’s Summary

Nick lives in San Francisco, Ca, after recently moving from Tampa Bay, Florida and has gone on to develop many successful websites that have gone on to create media buzz, that has allowed some of his work to be featured in magazines such as Sports Illustrated and has been interviewed on hundreds of morning shows across America. He has been featured for his love of technology on the front page of the St. Petersburg Times as well.

Known for being an early adopter, Nick chronicles all of his tech knowledge and thoughts onto his blog, NickStarr.com. The blog caters to tens of thousands of subscribers daily, with even more visitors who are find the site thru linking of articles that have been featured on some of the industry's top websites, to include Engadget, Gizmoodo, The Unofficial Apple Weblog, BlackBerry Hub, BlackBerry Cool, Digg, and others.The site is written for anyone with even the slightest bit of tech interest to the hardest of hardcore tech junkies.

Nick has been an affiliate of ThoughtOut.biz and has helped to develop some constructive ideas for the company in terms of new products and design.

On his blog, Nickstarr.com, he chronicles developments in the world of techie gear and reviews every new piece he buys.The newest, the latest, the fastest - he has to have it. The site hilights many of the top technology companies around, and focus on what is hip and trendy in the way of personal gadgetry. He also manages a number of other blogs and podcasts that are all part of the PodStarr network.
Nick has also been a featured guest on some of the industry's top rated podcasts including The Dawn and Drew Show, Nobody Likes Onions, Urban Coffee, and others.

Nick continues to grow in the field of technology and sees no end in site for his love of everything tech.
