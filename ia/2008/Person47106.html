David L. Roberts, Ph.D.’s Summary

I am a Licensed Clinical Psychologist who has also written two books, and taught psychology classes for 4.5 years at the College of the Desert in Palm Desert, CA. I am also a proud father of one daughter, father-in-law to a wonderful son-in-law, and grandfather to four amazing grandchildren.

I grew up in the "Deep South" during all of the Civil Rights Movement and social upheaval of the 1950's, 60's, and 70's. Even though I am a native of Mobile, Alabama, I lived in California for 15.5 years (Los Angeles for 6.5 years, and in Riverside County for 9 years) where I received my education, training and experiences as a psychologist before returning ro Mobile in early 2005. I left California due to budget cuts in mental health services which served to eliminate my service provision to high risk kids. My heart goes out to all low income, disadvantaged kids around this country and the world. My time in California has given me a very different worldview than that of many fellow Mobilians. I take a great deal of pride in my abilities to effectively reach out to this population of kids often treated as society's underdogs - kids truly At the Mercy of Externals!

    ProKids, Inc. and Liberating Youth Inc. are joint ventures whereby PKI will expand into LYI once funding is secured to expand to full program implementation. LYI will be a comprehensive program focusing on three components:

    1. At the Mercy of Externals: Righting wrongs and Protecting Kids, 2nd Edition; and
    2. Psyche-Soul-ology: An Inspirational Approach to Appreciating and Understanding Troubled Kids.

    Once evidence-based treatment has been provided LYI will be franchised around the country for both rural and inner city programs.
