Nacho Hernandez’s Summary

Nacho Hernandez is an entrepreneur, consultant, and public speaker who has co-founded online businesses and helped companies develop and execute Internet strategies.

As CEO & Founder of iHispanic Marketing Group, Nacho has been involved with international business, e-commerce and marketing for the past 10 years and has been targeting the U.S. Online Hispanic Market for the last 6 years. He founded MexGrocer.com in 2000 and is now one of the very few profitable businesses in the online grocery segment. He has made strategic alliances with the major shopping portals, such as Amazon, Univision.com, Terra-Lycos, HispanicOnline.com, Yahoo! En Español and many others. He is a regular speaker at Search Engine Strategies Conference & Expo and a member of the Search Engine Marketing Professional Organization (www.sempo.org) and SMA-NA (www.sma-na.org) to help develop the Hispanic and Latin American divisions. He is also a Moderator for SearchEngineWatch Forums for the “Multilingual Search Markets & Non-US engines”, the "Vertical Markets & Specialty Search" and the "Yahoo! Paid Inclusion/Site Match" forums.

Search Engine Strategies Latino 2006 and 2007 were also chaired and programmed by Nacho Hernandez. This event is focused in helping businesses, marketers, webmasters and everyday Web users understand how search engines work and how to profit from them by taking an active approach with the worldwide Spanish speaking markets which primarily include the U.S. Hispanic and all Latin America.

Other publications where Nacho has been quoted include: Forrester Research, SearchEngineWatch, WebProNews, MediaPost, Entrepreneur Magazine, La Opinion, AARP Segunda Juventud, El Financiero (Mexico), Reforma (Mexico) and others.

Nacho Hernandez’s Specialties:

iHispanic Marketing Group works with professionals and business owners from the U.S. and Latin America to drive sales using advanced search engine marketing and Internet strategies. Consistency, enthusiasm and professionalism are what makes us one of the most respected search engine markeing firms in the industry today.
