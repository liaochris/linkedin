Desiree Koh’s Summary

Desiree Koh has more than eight years of PR experience with a wide spectrum of blue chip companies and non-profits, from consumer packaged goods and retail to professional and financial services. Her award-winning work covers all consumer branding and corporate communications disciplines, including media execution, grassroots marketing, strategy and event planning.

Most recently at Ogilvy Public Relations Worldwide, Desiree’s client portfolio featured Bausch & Lomb, Lipton, PLAYMOBIL, LG Electronics, Target, Deloitte Consulting and the Hudson Highland Group. She spearheaded projects such as product launches, analyst relations, media programs, trade show management and event planning. A firm believer in working closely with clients and representing the agency as a trusted and effective partner, Desiree excelled as a day-to-day client counselor while managing account teams and assignments. Successes include garnering over 120 million audience impressions for Bausch & Lomb’s PureVision and SofLens Multi-Focal contact lens product launches, a full-page TIME magazine placement for teen Web portal Habbo Hotel, and the introduction of Lipton’s pro women’s cycling team, TEAm Lipton. With hip hop star Wyclef Jean’s Yele Haiti foundation, Desiree executed a publicity blitz at a Hurricane Katrina benefit, handling media interviews with People, US Weekly and In Touch for celebrity guests Drew Barrymore, Lindsay Lohan and Jeremy Piven. A toy lover and collector, Desiree considered PLAYMOBIL her pet account – in 18 months, media impressions rocketed from a handful of media mentions to becoming a regular feature in top-tier consumer magazines such as FamilyFun, Nick Jr., and Parents, winning 15 major awards along the way and becoming a household name with industry analysts.

At Ketchum, Desiree traversed paths as diverse as Andersen and Kimberly-Clark to the Lions Clubs International and Mattel.

Desiree Koh’s Specialties:

Media relations, program planning, creative brainstorming, strategy mapping, messaging, research, client relationship management, and writing.
