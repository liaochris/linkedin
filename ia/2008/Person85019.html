Jason Ma’s Summary

My strength is in influencing and helping people and companies big and small in the U.S. and internationally to enhance their successes. My passion is in building a great company, selling, evangelizing, and counseling kids and parents in top-tier college prep. I bring 24 years of operating, management, and entrepreneurial experience in the education, e-learning, Internet, wireless and mobile, networking, and media sectors. This includes a wealth of decision-maker relationships across the U.S., Greater China, Indian Subcontinent, Japan, South Korea, Southeast Asia, Australia and New Zealand, and Western Europe.

Jason Ma’s Specialties:

High-level sales, evangelism, marketing, and speeches in the world theatre, with focus and passion in the education space, and counseling students and parents as to help students build strong fundamentals, max test scores, enhance GPA, engage in quality extracurriculars, and eventually get admitted to top-tier universities and colleges.
