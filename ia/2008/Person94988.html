John Huffman IV’s Summary

John has become known as an innovative new media entrepreneur. Real Content Group, LLC is the third company he has had a hand in founding, and he has served as the Company’s CEO since its inception. The company focuses exclusively on providing digital (online and mobile) distribution and business development services to an elite clientele of content owners.

Prior to Real Content Group, John co-Founded Real Hip Hop, LLC as a joint venture direct-to-consumer mobile content company with mobile industry leader m-Qube. Prior to Real Hip Hop John structured a multi-million dollar joint venture with Rondor/Universal Music Group called Silverback Music Publishing, LLC. Silverback is a lyric focused music publishing company that uses the Internet and Mobile technology to acquire, develop and sell songs to new and established artists, producers and A&R executives.

Before being sold to an investment group, John’s first entrepreneurial venture, Maximum Networks, Inc., spun off several online innovations including a hip-hop search engine, an online e-commerce site, and an A&R Operating System that included content management, micro-market testing and work flow management.

European based “Digital Distribution In The Music Industry” voted John “30 Under 30” two years in a row. He currently serves as a United Nations Goodwill Ambassador and Head of Global Technology with a focus on Eradicating Malnutrition by the year 2015. Other career highlights include John’s development of the community event model for the Hip-Hop Summit Action Network lead by Russell Simmons and Dr. Ben Chavis.

In addition to leading Real Content Group, John is the Founding co-Chairman of the Urban Special Interest Group for the Mobile Marketing Association.
