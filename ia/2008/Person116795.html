Marcelo Lopez’s Summary

I am a Software Engineer with more than 5 years of working experience in IT, mainly with Microsoft technologies.

I’ve worked for some of the major IT companies from Uruguay, including CMMI Level 5 and Six Sigma ones.

My job profile includes several .NET projects for international customers such as TCS (Montevideo), Motorola (USA), American Express (USA), Comicrom Bank (Chile) and Intheco (Puerto Rico), among others.

I’ve been working initially as developer and then as technical leader, always with latest Microsoft and .NET technologies. I have an MCP certification from Microsoft in web development field.

I currently hold a PMP certification as a plus to my technical skills.

Now I am leading our team at UruIT Global IT Services (www.uruit.com) to its global vision, applying cutting edge technologies and development practices.

All of these abilities and experience gained, allows me to successfully participate in any project, from customer interaction/negotiation, to technical or team lead.
