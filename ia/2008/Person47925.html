David Becker’s Summary

David is President and Co-Founder of PhilippeBecker, an award-winning branding and design firm whose clients include Clorox, Disney, GAP, Hewlett-Packard, Nestle, IDEO, Microsoft, T-Mobile and Walmart.

His background also includes branding and product marketing successes for firms like Intuit, Franklin Templeton and frog design.

In 2005 PhilippeBecker was the recipient of the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce's "Excellence in Business Award," and for the past three years running has made the list of the "100 Fastest Growing Private Companies" as ranked by the San Francisco Business Times. In 2007 and 2008 PhilippeBecker was named to Inc. Magazine's list of America's 5000 Fastest-Growing Companies.

David holds a BA from UC Berkeley, an MBA from HEC Paris. He is a Trustee of the San Francisco Chamber of Commerce, and serves on its Executive Committee and of the Fort Mason Foundation.
