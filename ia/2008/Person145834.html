Pam Kassner’s Summary

Pam Kassner is a five-time Ironman triathlon (2.4 mile swim, 112 mile bike, 26.2 mile run) finisher. That probably says more about Pam both professionally and personally than listing an assortment of titles. As an Ironman, Pam has demonstrated passion, discipline, focus, commitment, problem solving, time management, multi-tasking, strategic thinking, risk taking and humor.


Pam knew when she crossed the finish line of her first Ironman, if she could do that, she could do anything. So in February 2005, Pam and her partner formed Super Pear Strategies, a marketing communications firm.

Clients that have engaged Super Pear include USG Corporation, Rockwell Automation, Brady Corporation, Northwestern Mutual, Marcus Investments, Financial Service Group, Witt Actuarial Services, CG Schmidt Construction, Eppstein Uhen Architects, James H. Hoffman Builders, Work + Life Fit, Inc., FlexPaths, 100 Monkeys PR, Centurion Data Systems, ISCorp-Integrated Systems Corporation, OneProspect Technologies, Schlitz Audubon Nature Center, Wisconsin State Fair Park, Bloodcenter of Wisconsin, Dr. Bob LLC,Janal Communications, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, Potawatomi Business Development Corporation, John Michael Kohler Arts Center, Marcus Center for Performing Arts, United Performing Arts Fund and Cultural Alliance of Greater Milwaukee, Nissan Xterra Championship Series, YMCA of Metropolitan Milwaukee and Transition Training and Cycle. Pam is also actively involved with the Alliance for Sustainable Built Environments.

Before forming Super Pear, Pam’s 20-plus year career history included serving as Director of Corporate Communications at both Rockwell Automation and USG Corporation and as VP of Public Affairs at Bank of Montreal/Harris Bank. Pam began her career in broadcast journalism and continued freelance writing with work published numerous times in the Chicago Tribune and other publications.

Pam Kassner’s Specialties:

Marketing and Public Relations Programs; Thought Leadership Campaigns; Corporate Responsibility and Sustainability Planning; and Reputation Protection and Issues Management
