    Eran Ben-Dor's resume:
    Born in 1966 in Israel.
    Graduated from the “Bezalel” academy of art and design in 1993 and has a B-Des. in Industrial Design.
    During his studies he won the “Sharet scholarship” prize.
    He also studied in the architectural stream of "Technicom" specialized high school.
    Since graduating the academy, he has accumulated a great deal of experience as a self-employed Industrial Designer in designing, developing, planning and inventing products for his clients, which include several of Israel's leading companies in their fields.
    Parallel to his work as a designer, Eran taught a course called “Introduction to human engineering for product designers” in “Ascola” school of design in Tel Aviv.
    In the year 2000 Eran founded "SezA Ltd." along with Eran Conforty - Master of Science in Management from Boston University and a B.Des in Graphic Design from Bezalel Academy of Art and Design in Jerusalem.

    Activities and Societies:
    1.5 years in Graphic Design department.
    3.5 years in Product Design department.
    Additional courses in Photography and Video departments.
