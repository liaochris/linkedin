While at Consect, I co-created with the great people at Billboard the official US ringtone charts, launched in October 2004 with the support of the CTIA and Mobile Entertainment Forum and more than 80% of the distribution outlets for ringtones at the time. We developed the backend technology, signed agreements with data providers, collected the data - everything but publish the charts. It is the foundation of Nilesen's RingScan service (Nielsen took over running the chart system in 5/2005) and gave the mobile industry its first emprical entertainment sales data.

I hope that the future brings us opportunities to do business together.
