Jochem Leegstra’s Summary

...,staat is a creative agency based in Amsterdam.
We started in the magic year ’00 working in and for the underground
music / club scene.
Today we’re an international team of 25, working with diverse and international clients on pan–European and global projects.

...,staat consists of Jochem Leegstra (founder / creative director) and Julia
Kortekaas (partner / general director), together with an inspiring mix of art
directors, designers, strategists, project managers and copywriters. ...,staat
people are young, energetic, visually literate, multi-talented and share a
passion for what they do.

...,staat works in fashion, design, advertising and everything in between,
including brand building, retail / interior / environmental and graphic design.
We passionately believe communication has many forms.

Creativity is our platform.
