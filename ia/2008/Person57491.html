Evan Keith Langert’s Summary

I specialize in commercial real estate development, leasing, brokerage, syndication and financing. With twenty two years of high level experience, as well as hundreds of closed deals in over twenty states, I have done about everything that one can do in commercial real estate. I am listed in "Who's Who In Real Estate", a publication that honors only 1% of all real estate professionals. I have been a broker (on both coasts) since the early 90's, and also spent nine years as a senior transactional attorney in charge of half the country for a multi-billion dollar NYSE-traded real estate investment trust.

I have done real estate deals with such companies as Lord & Taylor (The May Companies), Circuit City, Outback Steakhouse, Merrill Lynch, Clear Channel Communications, Metlife, and many others. Prior to that, I negotiated deals for the owner of a major regional shopping mall anchored by Nordstrom, Macy's, Sears and Mervyn's. I also spent four years earlier in my career as a commercial real estate lender with one of the 25 largest banks in the U.S., also spending a year as an internal auditor for the bank. In short, I consider myself an expert in commercial real estate. I understand real estate from every side of the transaction, and have a good command of the numbers and financial analysis involved. I can easily handle any purchase, sale or lease transaction. I have also developed projects on behalf of clients.

Evan Keith Langert’s Specialties:
