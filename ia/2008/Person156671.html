Randy Cooper’s Summary

An accomplished Internet Developer specializing in object-oriented design and analysis, with extensive experience in the full life cycle of the application design process, including requirements definition, prototyping, proof of concept, design, interface implementation, e-marketing, search engine optimization, testing and maintenance. Possesses first-rate communication skills to collaborate on all business levels while effectively directing systems, processes, operating procedures, and documentation for on-site and remote locations.
