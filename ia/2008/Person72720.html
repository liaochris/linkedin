Greg Tarr’s Summary

Serves as Senior Advisor for Venture Investment/VC Fund Formation+ Active Board Member to CEO's across the full value chain of Mobile, Wireless, Internet, Digital Media Eco-System+Top Tier VC's- Silicon Valley, Japan, China.

Greg Tarr has 20 years experience with 12 years Living in Asia & Japan. He Founded CrossPacific Capital in 1998 and is Senior Advisor to Vodafone, Globe Telecom (Singtel) and Sumitomo/Presidio. He was Past Advisor to Vesbridge+ CDP Capital as architect of China VC Investment strategy in 2002. Board, Consulting, VP Operational roles include 20 Silicon Valley/Asian startups working with Partners of Kleiner Perkins, Redpoint Ventures, Sutter Hill, Blue Run. A 1999 Shanghai Seed Investment in Linktone- 1st Mobile China VAS firm led to a 2004 Nasdaq IPO (LTON)+Accel/IDG China Spinoff in Mobile Payments-SmartPay.

He was recruited by Kleiner Perkins to Pelago+ IP Unity’s Advisory Board and as Kodiak Networks 1st Employee in Asia securing the Asia reference customers as VP Asia/Japan. He Serves the Advisory Board of Networks in Motion and Board Observer/Catalyst in formation of Jibe Mobile. In addition, he advised Asia Online in a Landmark $100m PE Financing-Asia's Largest Regional ISP in 1999.

In 1997 Reuters ranked Greg a top Asian Equity Research Analyst at Deutsche Bank Securities Asia covering Telecom Equipment, Internet, Semis+ Software. In addition, he worked at Toyota’s U.S. HQ and in 1984/85 Time Warner selected Greg as MTV/Music Television’s New York summer intern during Startup mode. He also interned at Disney/ABC’s Flagship New York Radio Station. He has lived in Japan, Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Singapore, Sweden, Canada+ U.S.

He has served Stanford & MIT as guest lecturer at MBA/Engineering Schools and as a panelist at Mobile World Congress Asia/Europe. Mr. Tarr holds a B.A.- University of Colorado-Boulder and MBA- Pepperdine University-Los Angeles.
