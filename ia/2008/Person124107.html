Mary Song’s Summary

Nine years experience online marketing experience particularly in online travel and real estate.

Founded Rent101.com which was acquired in 2005 by Austin Ventures and was one of the founding websites of Homeaway.com. Remained with them for 2.5 years as their global search strategist with experience setting up large dynamic global paid accounts as well as natural search strategies.

Experience publishing print travel publication available by subscription and newstand sales.

Degree in Computer Science with over 20 years of project management experience for large scale legacy applications as well as web application development.

Goals are to develop web based applications that solve real world problems, organic traffic generation via SEO, PPC, social media and viral marketing, and developing web sites that people want to use.

Mary Song’s Specialties:

Mergers and acquisition, rebranding, migrating domains to new platform, new website launch, generating more traffic, improving conversion rates for existing traffic and optimizing paid online advertising campaigns.
