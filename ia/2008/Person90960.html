Joseph Hollak’s Summary

I am in the process of earning my Masters in Business Administration (MBA).

Prior to this, I spent nine wonderful years as a Financial Consultant (working with demanding, high-net worth individuals and 501(c)(3) orgs) specializing in fee-based wealth management using modern portfolio theory (MPT) at two top-notch firms.

I also have a Bachelor's degree in Mass Communication & Journalism, specializing in digital media and photo-journalism. It was an exercise in creativity and I was lucky enough to win multiple digital media and photo-journalism awards. You can see more of my work at www.hollakmedia.com.

I have an active interest in new projects, internships and employment opportunities, (especially in the areas of managerial finance and accounting, corporate leadership and sustainable business practices).

Feel free to contact me.

Joseph Hollak’s Specialties:

Extensive experience in department management, corporate sales, high-end consulting and an understanding of the global financial markets including modern portfolio theory (MPT) and structuring financial plans in order to achieve long-term growth / protection goals.

A strong understanding of mass communication and journalism, digital media trends / standards and the overall news cycle.

And finally, a passion for always learning something new.
