Oli Barrett’s Summary

London-based Connector specialising in bringing people together to make things happen.

My passion is for early stage ventures, which I help in return for funds, equity, love or a blend of the three.

Companies I have helped to launch (and remain a shareholder in), include FriendsAbroad.com (language learning), Wis.dm (social network) and SockRush (product subscription).

Non-profits I have helped to launch include Make Your Mark with a Tenner (micro-loans to school pupils) and The Web Mission (overseas trips for web entrepreneurs).

I sit on the Council on Social Action, chaired by the UK Prime Minister.

Speed Networking is a simple concept which I brought to the UK, and I host regular London Salons which attract an interesting blend of professional from a wide range of industries. In November 2008 I will helping to create Global Speed Networking, as part of Global Entrepreneurship Week.

Oli Barrett’s Specialties:

Making Valuable Introductions, Problem Solving, Compelling Idea Generation, Hosting and Facilitation, Communicating early stage ventures to potential investors/partners etc
