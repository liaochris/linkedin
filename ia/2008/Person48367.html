Don Broecker’s Summary

> Comprehensive experience in all aspects of employee benefit plan design, review, analysis and administration, and in creating cost management solutions that reduce employer costs and maximize employee satisfaction.
> Experienced in base pay and incentive compensation management and development.
> Develops leading-edge benefit cost containment tactics such as consumer-driven health plans (CDHP) for Fortune 500 companies, including Capital One, Charter Communications and Monsanto Company.
> Skilled in the review and recommendation of more cost efficient health care networks while limiting disruption of providers for participations, as well as disease management and healthy lifestyle programs and predictive medical episode services.
> Proficient in project management, vendor selection and implementation, including vendor contracting, performance guarantees and monitoring.
> Well-versed in change management, including employee preferences assessments, communications and decision support tools.
