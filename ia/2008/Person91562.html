Jimmy Hotz’s Summary

Record Producer, Engineer, Musician and Inventor with over 35 years of broad experience in a vast range of skills covering the entertainment and technical fields. Some of the major companies that have sought Jimmy's technical advice include Microsoft, Intel, Electronic Arts, Atari and JBL.·

Artists who Hotz has worked with include Fleetwood Mac, NSYNC, MOBIUS8, B.B. King, Dave Mason, Leon Russell, Yes, Kitaro, Steve Winwood, Chicago, Bonnie Rait, Gary Wright, Paul Haslinger, Phoebe Snow, Kim Basinger, Dan Aykroyd, Patty Loveless, Becky Hobbs, the Association, ArkAngel, Petra, the Memphis Horns, Mason Williams, the Smothers Brothers, Ray Kennedy, Shock Therapy, Giant Killer, the Natives, Joe Savage, Maya, Speedy West, Memphis, Palamino, Dove, Vision, Scott Gershin, Haven and dozens of other artists.

· Hotz has consistently remained at the forefront of technology by working with many major artists and companies, utilizing the latest equipment, computers and software available.

· Hotz has Improved many existing technologies and created a host of new ones
