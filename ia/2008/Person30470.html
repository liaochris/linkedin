Karen Johnson’s Summary

Karen started her education at Germain School of Photography in New York City. She received her BA Magna Cum Laude from the State University of New York at Purchase with a major in Liberal Arts and her Judicial Doctorate from UCLA School of Law. Her focus in law school was on Property Law - copyright to land use regulation. She was part of the UCLA Environmental Law Clinic where she participated in the litigation on land use, zoning and planning. She was also involved in the UCLA Indian Law Seminar where she focused on working with attorneys on Federal Land Trust and Indian Land Rights.

Karen was one of the law students picked to serve a summer legal clerkship with the U.S. Attorney's Office - Las Vegas, Nevada where she received permission from the U.S. Attorney's Office and the Federal Judges of the Las Vegas Federal Courts to try cases. She worked closely with agents in the FBI, DEA, ATF and Las Vegas Metro Police. She received from the U.S. Attorney for Las Vegas a commendation for Dedication and Hard Work.

Upon graduation from law school she acted as a Supervising Attorney Consultant for Fraudulent Sales Practices, Assistant Vice President for Business Development for Cybersettle, an online mediation company, and is currently Director of Corporate Sponsorship for the Practising Law Institute. Her specialty is in strategic marketing and partnership and alliances. Her interests lie in strategic marketing for start-up companies.

Prior to becoming an attorney Karen worked in corporate design and advertising at American Can Company, Christ Petrone Associates and her own Company, Johnson Associates. Some of the companies she worked on projects for are Kraft General Foods, Federal Express, Northern Tissue, Brawny, and Dixie.

She currently sits on the Board of Directors for the Manhattan New Music Project and the Town of North Castle Housing Board. Karen is a member of the Luxury Marketing Council and Legal Marketing Association.
