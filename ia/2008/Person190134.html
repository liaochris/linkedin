Toby Barazzuol’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur with an optimistic vision of the future. I believe that the power of technology combined with a deep respect for people and our environment will be the keys to business success in the future. I'm interested in exploring how businesses can integrate with society and learn to function in harmony with nature's rhythms.

As a company, our goal is to recognize excellence at any level. Some of our clients include NASA, the United Nations, Microsoft, Oracle and IBM, so we understand what it takes to supply and partner with some of the most successful organizations in the world.

Recognizing the leaders that move our society forward intelligently is a critical element in the movement towards sustainability - or any movement for that matter.
