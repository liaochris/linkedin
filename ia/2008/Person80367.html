Navdeep Trivedi’s Summary

I have over 7 years of experience with managing web development projects. I'm a project manager with expertise in the areas of web strategy, web customer support, search engine marketing and optimization, ecommerce, and web development.

I have worked directly with US based clients from varied business groups, provided leadership and direction to project team by understanding business processes, gathering requirements, identifying potential usability issues, managing scope, & ensuring overall quality and successfully deployed their projects.

As a project manager, I have spearheaded a team of web developers and graphic designers in design and development of various web development projects ranging from America's best chain of Pizza stores to premier hotel brands e.g. Choice Hotels International,Best Western, InterContinental Hotels Group etc.

I have proven record in resolving wide range of customer issues and elevate their satisfaction level. In all I managed the corporate web sites, web applications, vertical portals and intranet of 350+ US based clients. I have hands on experience in handling mid-cap Pay Per Clicks accounts (Google Adwords & Yahoo Search Marketing) of international clients from different industry domains.

I am also a Certified eCommerce & eBusiness Consultant, a fast learner with expertise in pay per click campaign management & search engine optimization.

While working with Yash Technologies, I am responsible for overall online promotion of the product web site on WWW including site analysis, recommending web usability suggestions to ensure they comply with major search engine guidelines and managing pay per click campaigns.
