Bruce E Flanagan’s Summary

Bruce is an Executive and Leadership Coach; he is a dynamic coach bringing a depth of human caring to his coaching and leadership. With over 17 years working in management in Asia and Australia including the role of Country Director for a humanitarian NGO In Viet Nam and Cambodia, Bruce has extensive leadership experience negotiating with governments, coaching and facilitating transformation all around the world.

He has been trained by Dr. L. Michael Hall, the founder of Neuro Semantics, as well as world renowned Master Coach Michelle Duval. Bruce is an Accredited Meta-Coach with the International Meta-Coach Foundation as well as being a Master practitioner in Neuro Semantics. Bruce has degrees in communication and social sciences.
Bruce also has a number of accreditations such as a Facilitator of Extended Disk, EL Coaching Matrix, As well as a licensed consultant of The COI Group Organisational, team and leadership effectiveness profiling tool.

Over the past decade Bruce has developed a reputation as a leading Executive and Leadership Coach as well as facilitator for organisational improvement, improving team performance, improving employee retention effectiveness and improving leadership effectiveness.
Bruce is enthusiastic about facilitating transformation both in individuals and organisations. His company the Leadership Coach is embarking on an strategy of rapid expansion throughout each ASEAN country with Leadership Coaching; Leadership Training; Training professional coaches; ASEAN Business leaders breakfast; ASEAN Business Leaders Magazine; Business Leaders Give Back; Islamic Business Leaders Forum.
