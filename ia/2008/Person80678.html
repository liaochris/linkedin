Irina Wardas’s Summary

I am a founder and director of NaturalCounselor.com and work as a Certified Holistic Health and Nutrition Counselor.

My holistic nutrition oriented approach to health counseling focuses on the whole picture of the healing, energetic properties of the food, and numerous factors shaping our bio-individualities.

I work with women who have stress or weight management issues and are experiencing increased appetite, mood swings, insomnia and lack of energy.

I help my clients get healthier by changing their lifestyle in step-by-step, enjoyable way for long lasting results. I work around food and lifestyle alternatives.

I conduct individualized Women's Health and Wellness Programs focused either on Health or Relationship, Women's Health Group Programs, workshops and seminars. I have just relocated from NYC to San Diego area, so I also plan Holistic Health Retreats for women and couples.

For more information and sign up for my free Newsletter visit my website.
