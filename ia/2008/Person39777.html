Stephen Windsor’s Summary

A very knowledgeable manufacturing business specialist, with 14 years experience as a Board Director of a £35m company, which was part of a £2bn international group, responsible for managing professional teams encompassing the entire supply chain, from Procurement through Production Planning, Manufacturing and Quality Control to Stores, Order Picking and Final Distribution to the Customer

Included in that 14 year span was also the management of the complementary disciplines of Technical Development of all manufacturing processes (Extrusion, Injection Moulding, Assembly and Packaging of plastics products), raw materials and capital equipment. On-site Planned Maintenance of site and facilities, Health and Safety and Environmental responsibilities.

Through my consultancy CONSULTEK I provide solutions and advice on a variety of manufacturing business challenges, including the introduction of Lean Methodologies, 5S, Process and Value Stream Mapping as well as project management and interim management support, the end goal being ‘World Class’ customer service coupled to real cost savings, business growth and enhanced profits.
