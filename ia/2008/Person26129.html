Brad St. Pierre’s Summary

Brad St Pierre has over 30 years of experience in the IT industry, in all aspects of IBM's data management, interactive and transaction systems (CICS, DB2, IMS, TSO, SAP, VM/CMS) with many of North America’s largest public and private corporations, introducing many of them to these products.

Brad has developed training courses in many aspects of IT and has written several books on IT subjects, including the IBM RedBooks:

- ‘OS/390 Parallel Sysplex Application Considerations Presentation Guide’, SG24-4743-00, 1996.
- 'SAP on DB2 for z/OS and OS/390: High Availability and Performance Monitoring with Data Sharing’, SG24-6950-00, 2003.

Brad is interested in challenging work at progressive IT departments in leading companies.

Brad St. Pierre’s Specialties:

- DB design, programming & support - DB2, IMS DB, VSAM.
- IBM Sysplex technology: author of two IBM Redbooks (technical reports).
- Systems programming - installation, customization, migration - OS/390, APL2, CICS, DB2, EPDM, IMS, SAP, TCP/IP, USS (Unix on z/OS), OS/390 & z/OS systems.
- System & application design; programming in IBM Assembler, COBOL, PL/I, CICS, IMS DB/DC.
- Customer & staff training
- Interactive programming in VM/CMS, TSO/ISPF, REXX.
- ETL support.
- Microcomputers.
