Kelly Baxter’s Summary

Processed real estate settlements for thirteen years during the height of the housing market. Specialized in high volume caseloads, customer service, precise and detailed data entry, independent work, sensitive issues and legal documents.

My entrepreneurial spirit led me to start working as a freelancer. This has involved professional blogging, website promotion and marketing for various online companies.

Strong involvement in social media, social bookmarking and networking. I have built a large networking group within the blogging and social community.

My passion for music inspired my blog "What Does This Song Mean To Me?". I write about the musical influences in my life and how I relate music to practically everything. In a short time, I have acquired a large audience and reader participation. Music really does tie people together.
