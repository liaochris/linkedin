Ken Richard’s Summary

Seasoned ERP Risk Management executive/attorney. Ken has unsurpassed experience (16 years) and success is managing and resolving risk in all size software ERP implementation engagements, including the creation of a framework for, and resolving engagement disputes. He has litigated over 250 software license and complex implementations for several public companies and have a unique yet broad understanding of why implementations fail. He is also qualified to draft, modify and resolve contract terms and vet issues with a goal of resolving any disputes and allowing the implementation to become referenceable through proper ERP project management. He has been involved with millions of dollars in engagement contracts over his career. Call him at 949-589-6460.

Ken Richard’s Specialties:

ERP contract and scope formation, risk abatement, and resolution. He can make ERP jobs much more profitable through proper risk management.
