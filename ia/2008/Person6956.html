Alex (1965), a doctor of Medicine, joined Across Health with 15 years of experience in Healthcare ICT applications, particularly for patient data capture in hospitals.
Alex also holds an MBA degree in marketing, which he obtained at the Catholic University of Louvain.

Alex boasts extensive experience as a sales & marketing director in different healthcare ICT companies (owned by Omega Pharma, a leading B2B and B2C healthcare company), developing & implementing electronic healthcare records (EHR) for hospitals, specialists, GPs and other healthcare professionals (dentists,...). He has also written numerous articles and participated in several eHealth-related projects.

In the process, Alex has helped a number of pharma companies obtain state-of-the-art patient data from hospital and physician EHR systems. He has acquired a unique insight into the various methods used to gather, analyze and use patient data.

Most recently, Alex has been involved in the re-organization of the patient data flow at the IT department of AZ-Groeninge, one of the biggest privately-run hospitals in Belgium.

Alex has a strong focus on further capitalizing on his unique insights into patient data, in combination with an innovative range of CRM tools, Business Intelligence and Performance indicators. He also has a strong interest in collaborative and community web applications (web 2.0) to enhance hospital services.

During his free time, you’ll find Alex jogging, on a mountain bike, in a gym or even on a golf course.
