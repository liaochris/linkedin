Deborah Johnson’s Summary

Tynan provides strategic business consulting and specializes in solving business problems facing companies today. We provide Transaction Advisory Services to help clients maximize value and minimize risk during all stages of the M&A process including, strategic introductions, negotiation assistance, deal-closing support, value proposition positioning, and assistance with management presentations.

We offer candid, realistic in-depth assessments, evaluate competitive positioning, uncover risks and opportunities, identify synergies within and outside organizations, and evaluate build-versus-buy as well as new market penetration strategy We help our clients to identify, understand, prioritize, and act on new verticles-we build just in time divisions and companies.


Tynan specializes in uncovering new business opportunities by doing a gap analysis, market research and identifying competitors, i.e. who is in the neighboring space and who has the lowest barriers to entry in the market. We talk to suppliers and analysts and utilize this information to determine future strategy and direction.
