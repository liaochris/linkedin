Martin Katz’s Summary

Principal of Middleton Katz Chartered Secretaries LLC in the Isle of Man.
Director of Middleton Katz Financial Services Limited in the Isle of Man.


Middleton Katz Chartered Secretaries LLC provides company secretarial, offshore corporate and offshore trustee services to listed companies, investment funds, private clients, other professional firms and regulated financial institutions. We are located offshore in Douglas, Isle of Man; we administer companies incorporated in many other jurisdictions as well as Isle of Man companies, trusts, LLC’s and other Manx structures.

Our clients range from large companies listed on the London Stock Exchange to which we are appointed Company Secretary, to private clients with diverse financial interests and requirements which include; residential & commercial property structures, offshore remuneration planning, employee benefit trusts, IP holding, personal services companies and asset protection structures.

We specialise in the provision of bespoke offshore fiduciary services, and we have experience in resolving complex or contentious matters in a practical cost effective manner.

Middleton Katz Financial Services Limited are Independent financial Intermediaries specialising in the selection and implementation of highly personalised whole of life insurance contracts.

Our expertise is focused on insurance arrangements that can be utilised to hold private assets, such as real property and shares of private companies of wealthy families as part of a bespoke wealth planning strategy.

Martin Katz’s Specialties:

Offshore Company & Offshore Trust Administration for private and institutional clients.

International tax planning.

We specialise in establishing offshore hedge funds and closed ended investment companies. We act for several major UK investment groups.

We have assisted start-up business which we have supported from inception through primary funding to ultimate listing on the UK Alternative Investment Market.
