Marco Roy’s Summary

I recently started my studies in Software Engineering at Montreal’s Ecole de Technologie Supérieure. Most of my knowledge lies in object oriented programming; I specialize in Web development (ASP, JSP/JSF and PHP) and in application development. (Java, VB.NET, C++/C#) Most of my projects involved databases at some point, so I can say I have a pretty good knowledge of database languages (SQL and PL/SQL) and systems. (Oracle, MS SQL Server and MySQL)

Even though I do not have much concrete experience in the domain yet, I look forward to becoming a game designer. I’ve been introduced to the gaming industry at a very young age and cannot deny that developing video games is my calling. I have a lot of imagination for designing stand-out games and finding new ideas and concepts. I would also like to try and apply software engineering concepts to game design.
