Mazi Hosseini’s Summary

The last 15 years of Engineering work specially in design and development thought me a lot, not just technical things but a lot of important facts in work environment like people, resources, project schedule and .....!
Learned how to do the research, since non of the daily engineering problems has a solution in university books! even simple design tasks needs to be verified and confirmed in many aspects, like durability, temperature stability, disturbance robustness, noise immunity, safety and so on...
Following standards is critical, otherwise your product will not be marketable, since finding the right standard to follow is a key thing! not always an easy task.
I have learned to start a design with a customer requirement document which will be written by the actual customer or a field application engineer! Then from this document we will drive other necessary documents like Design Requirements and so on.
