Brian Oravetz’s Experience

  • Founder, VP Business Development

    Risk Mitigation Systems

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Computer Software industry)

    November 2006 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Risk Mitigations Systems has developed a Software as a Service application that creates a command and control structure for organizational Emergency Action Plans. One of the most valuable capabilities of the system is its workflow-managed communications and response recording capabilities. The right people get involved at the right time with the right information, but the system also creates and auditable record of the actions taken by all parties to resolve or mitigate the incident. As a result all interested parties immediately know who knew what information, when, and whether each party completed the prescribed and / or ad-hoc actions in response. This provides an evidentiary chain and sound forensic evidence, showing that proper incident plans were in place and actually executed.

    This is a valuable tool for the incident handling and management capabilities of companies which need to do their utmost to protect the safety and security of their employees, assets, and guests.
