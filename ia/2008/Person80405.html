Scott Goldman’s Summary

"For the love of print" is more than just a tagline. Printing is our passion. The INTOgraphics presses have been rolling for 20 years, and our capabilities embrace everything from letterpress to digitalpress. We're dedicated, expert, innovative, and have a solid record of delivering outstanding results. This has earned us excellent long-term standing with Art Directors, Creative Agencies and Publishers.

Scott Goldman’s Specialties:

We produce media kits, annual reports, packaging, brochures, stationery, banners, books, and much much more.

Our state-of-the-art equipment is all in-house, and our full-time fulfillment team will turn your job around fast.

Scott Goldman’s Experience

  • President

    IntoGraphics Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Graphic Design industry)

    1985 — Present (23 years)

    I created Into Graphics in 1985. It started out as a commercial printing business, but even back then I realized that printing wasn't just a commodity -- clients come to me with their creative visions, not just their lists of specs. It takes technical excellence, creative approaches, tight coordination and collaboration to make their creative visions come through. In the past few years Into Graphics has evolved. We've broken the print barrier, and now offer a full range of production services, including design, web, and copywriting.
