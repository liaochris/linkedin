Juris Terauds’s Summary

Upon university graduation in 1997, started career in Latvian telecommunication company Lattelecom. During next 6 years was involved in business processes definitions using formal process description tools, designing order processing application on top of Oracle Workflow, then turned into overall process re-engineering, CRM and finally Siebel application designs and applications.

For the next 4 years worked as Business Developer and Siebel Capability lead Accenture's Riga Delivery Center, specializing in remote IT project delivery in various areas for Nordics countries, Netherlands and the UK. During these years gained significant experience in working with European clients, planning and delivering IT projects in remotes/offshore model, general project management (PMP). During last year was responsible for Business Developments in areas of Siebel, Oracle, SAP and Business Intelligence.

Last December with several ex-Accenture colleagues, established Joint Stock Company called Idea Port Riga, which I am managing. Our company specializing in business process management and IT consulting in CRM, BI and SOA areas.
