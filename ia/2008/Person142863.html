Meredith Halks-Miller, MD’s Summary

A physician-scientist, Board Certified in both Anatomic and Neuropathology, with over 12 years of experience in the global pharmaceutical industry. Developed and led a pathology services group that evaluated clinical and histopathologic data from animal models for relevance to human disease, that assessed drug efficacy and early safety by using a combination of classical and state-of-the-art histologic methods, and that validated drug targets and biomarkers in tissues. Strengths include team building, creating and implementing organizational structures, data analysis, project evaluation, influential communication, development of novel techniques, and clear scientific thinking. A creative, determined problem solver with high professional standards. Interested in applying these skills to help small and medium-sized biotechnology companies design, manage, and exectute pathology studies to obtain the most information possible from their tissue samples.

    NPATHY Consulting, LLC (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Myself Only; Biotechnology industry)

    April 2007 — Present (1 year 3 months)

    NPATHY Consulting's mission is to provide innovative, high quality histopathology support to biotechnology companies that may not have this resource fully developed internally. NPATHY will help clients select and analyze histologic endpoints for the evaluation of candidate drug efficacy; provide quantitative methods to evaluate biomarkers in tissues using unbiased stereology; and evaluate animal models for relevance to human disease. Clients will receive detailed histology reports that include quantitative analyses as well as descriptive histology and photographic documentation.
