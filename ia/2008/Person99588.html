Juan Martinez’s Summary

As SOA Practice Manager, after conform a senior consultants and architects basis, we are dealing SOA leads and opportunities with SOA architectures as core and evolving our portfolio to SOA Governance, BAM and CEP giving great visibility to Technology and Business teams.

We are mainly specialized in TIBCO, BEA Systems and Progress technologies and also service partners. Nevertheless we advise our customers to adopt the most scalable, flexible and reusable technologies according to present case and an goal objectives at business and IT levels.

With offices in Spain and UK, we are capable to attend European customers requests and give solutions to their requirements. We also have strategic alliances in Latin America which let us extend our services to latin countries.

Sales divisions are also supported by technical practices as well as HR, involved in coach functions, training and technical evaluations.
