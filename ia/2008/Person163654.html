Roger Jones’s Summary

Award winning Fortune 100 Chief Information Officer with extensive experience delivering increased capabilities and lower costs to all user functions within corporations of all sizes. Have aggressively delivered enhanced business value; business – technology alignment; and world class IT organizations to my employers.

I provide consulting services to a variety of industries with a focus on medium sized companies. My extensive experience in renovating IT organizations across a broad range of industries gives me a unique insight into both the common and the unique problems and opportunities for my clients. As an experienced CIO, a business executive, and a technology hobbyist I am positioned to blend the business, IT management, and underlying IT technologies into winning solutions.

I find in the small and medium business arena it is good to be a "jack of all trades." Some examples:

Assisting in defining technology strategy and the tactics to achieve the strategy;

Serving as an outside member of my client's Information Technology Steering Committee;

Assisting with identifying external vendors to provide solutions, services and/or support to my customers;

Helping to integrate IT solutions such as Web Site Analytics, PayPal, Facebook Applications, "Blogging" applications, and "message board" applications such as "Yuku" into my client's IT environment. I focus on finding high quality and low cost (or free) applications.
