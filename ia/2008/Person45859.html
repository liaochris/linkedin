Dave Walters FCCA’s Summary

By profession, I am a qualified Accountant with over 10 years of experience. I have a passion for interacting with people from different walks of life and more recently have been drawn towards helping more and more people. I also enjoy people, numbers, and business. In September 2007 the dream of starting my own recruitment business was finally realised with the creation of Inspired Careers. At Inspired Careers PR recruitment consultancy we tailor our services to meet your needs. We listen to our candidates, and advise, where possible, how best to manage their careers. And we listen to the requirement of our clients, and were possible, join the two together. Our clients are seen as partners rather than a fee paying opportunity. We aim to help you! Our service is personable, and extremely ethical, which is needed in the Recruitment industry today.
