Fabian Gonzalez’s Summary

Management consultant in the areas of information governance and privacy. Experienced consulting project manager and auditor with experience in designing, implementing and auditing information systems for Fortune 500 clients in financial services and Internet media industries.

Most recently, assisted clients in performing large scale security and privacy assessments, deploying privacy programs, performing SAS 70 audits, ISO 17799 audits, general IT reviews, and Sarbanes-Oxley compliance assistance services.

Representative clients include: Visa Inc., Ariba, Wind River Systems, Inc., Yahoo!, Google, Varian Medical Systems, and Gap Inc.

Fabian Gonzalez’s Specialties:

Information systems strategy, audit, security, and privacy consulting for Fortune 500 clients, Sarbanes-Oxley compliance, Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Information Privacy Professional / Information Technology (CIPP/IT)
