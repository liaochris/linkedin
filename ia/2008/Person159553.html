Ricardo Barbosa’s Summary

Ricardo is an experienced project manager in the area of safety critical systems for both space and aviation industry. Currently, he is team leader and technical manager on several development and testing projects. Has advanced technical knowledge on real time operating systems like VxWorks or RTEMS and embedded system development and testing.

As a senior engineer, Ricardo has in depth experience in the development of testing tools and assessment methods for busines and mission critical applications (spreading from financial transaction system, web services availability and robustness and safety related projects on railway, space and avionics).

As a software engineer, Ricardo has in depth knowledge on C/C++, Ada95, Java, Python and Perl among other programming and scripting languages.

Recently was assigned to position of product manager for the in-house developed product Xception, where he acquired advanced knowledge on fault injection techniques and methods, as well as product management concepts, marketing and delivery strategies.
