Amir Nikaein’s Summary

Experienced media professional possessing a wealth of sales and management background in B2B advertising with more than 10 years of 'C' Level sales experience. I have worked extensively with both agencies and enterprise clients in the European, North American and Asian markets, creating key partnerships with Gartner, Forrester and IDC as well as working closely with Citrix, SAP and IBM. I have spent the last decade in the global media and publishing industry developing, launching and selling some of the best-known titles and websites in the B2B market place and achieving long-term sustainable growth in every market served. My expertise is in revenue generation via business development, sales, professional services with a strong commitment to finding customer-focused solutions.

Prior to founding IMI, I was CEO and co-founder of Analysts In Media (AIM) which I set up in 2006 and successfully sold in 2008 with annual turnover over $5.5m. Prior to AIM, I was VP of Sales for a US based B2B advertising firm, taking the company from $2 million in annual sales to $10 million in less than 2 years by implementing an aggressive sales development and training strategy as well as an overhaul and change of the company’s products, I was also responsible for developing the company’s first ever B2B business forum. Additionally I have held senior sales management positions in media companies in the U.K. and in the U.S. winning multiple accolades. In 2004 I spear-headed the first ever one-to-one outsourcing forum in India, which was done in partnership with NASSCOM - India's leading IT authority, today the event is one of the most anticipated annual gatherings in the outsourcing calendar.
