Maureen McCann, Career Strategist’s Summary

Maureen McCann is Owner and Chief Career Strategist with ProMotion Career Solutions. She helps people improve and identify their skills and talents to increase their career development success. She is a resourceful and energetic career strategist who implements new media to shaping the future of her clients. She provides practical tools that empower people and lead others to meaningful and fulfilling careers. She enjoys an 85% success rate in moving unemployed individuals into their chosen field; as a result of her one-on-one coaching.

In 2007, Maureen received the Career Professionals of Canada (CPC) Award of Excellence for her continued contribution to her clients and her profession.

Maureen has been called the "ultimate professional in all her endeavors from voluntary contributions in the community to her role as a career professional" by some of Canada's leading resume writers and career professionals. She is an active member of the Advisory Board of Career Professionals of Canada.

Maureen McCann, Career Strategist’s Specialties:

I work with individuals who have been unsuccessful in finding their ideal employment situation and employees who struggle to get promoted within their existing field.

I help them to understand what employers want and I introduce them to concepts, exercises and skills that will increase their chances for meaningful and lasting employment.

"My ideal clients are people on the move - willing to make a change in their life and use technology to their advantage."
