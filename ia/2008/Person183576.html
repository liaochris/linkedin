Sunny Lowe’s Summary

Mr. Lowe has 15 years of experience analyzing the technology needs of medium to large sized companies, and providing solutions to those companies.

He consults regularly with organizations including companies from the Fortune 50, petroleum companies, hospitality companies, hospitals, law offices, school districts and other businesses to aid them in their technology infrastructure planning and implementation. He leads teams in business goal based research followed by implementation of technology, on time and within budget.

Business leaders appreciate the ability to describe a problem or need in business terms, and then have Mr. Lowe recommend technology and business solutions, with budgets and scenarios that the business professional can then choose to modify or directly implement to meet their business needs.

He holds many certifications, including Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE), Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator (MCSA) and Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) He additionally holds Messaging and Security certifications.

Recent projects (2007-8) have included:

* A Large Arkansas Bank - Chief Consultant to the migration team from Novell Directory to Active Directory on Windows Server 2008. Responsible for all documentation and design sign off.
* A Texas University - Repaired Active Directory to repair Exchange 2003 (125,000 users)
* 2 Major Hospitals - Upgraded networks from NT to Server 2003 and Active Directory,
* Nation Wide Hospital Chain - Repaired Active Directory,
* Regional Service Center in TX (a public school resource center).
Set up maintenance plans for SQL. (50 databases)
Upgrade all SQL 2000 Databases to SQL 2005,
* Consulting Company - Repaired an Exchange 2007 installation
* Plumbing Supply Chain - installing and implementing a Microsoft Network for a 50 locations
* Funeral Home Chain - Helping a local chain of setup affordable 15 meg links between their sites.
