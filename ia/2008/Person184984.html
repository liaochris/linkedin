Tahir Irtis’s Summary

I am a talent-seeker for well established firms. I am an "agent" for IT professionals.

Athletes, actors/actresses and successful professionals have agents who look out for them. Whether or not you are looking for a job change, obtain an agent that works free of charge to better your career. When the time comes to make a job change, you don't have to play the fishing game in the job boards like monster and dice. You will have an informative agent who has the right contacts to get you the right opportunity.

I use principle-centered approach to recruiting. Honesty, integrity and loyalty are the corner store of all my dealings. I work hard to further the career goals of job applicants by matching the "right" opportunity with their skills and experience. I take pride in my work.

I have over 10 years of experience in the IT recruitment industry. I have helped major companies with their IT staffing needs. I have partnered with the hiring managers of major companies to find them the right talent. I have great relationships with many of the top hiring managers in both the financial and non-financial industries.

I seek talented and skillful job-applicants. I believe every successful job seeker needs a good recruiter. If you want me to represent you - 100% confidential - contact me via email or call me.
tahir@softsolint.com or 212-480-2112 - direct line.
