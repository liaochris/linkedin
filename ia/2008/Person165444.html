Raffi Sosikian’s Summary

As a previous web designer, I fell in love with the ability to create sites and launch businesses on a shoestring budget, and still have the ability to literally reach the world through the Internet. After much prayer, I turned my web design business into a search engine optimization service and instead of just depending on referrals for new business, I launched registereverywhere.com. My office now handles the search engine optimization needs of thousands of website owners. In addition, in the past couple of years, I have launched businessnetworkingme.com and findattorneyorlawyer.com.

Businessnetworkingme.com focuses on getting professionals of all industries, such as mortgage brokers, insurance brokers, real estate agents, accountants, bookkeepers, CPA, lawyers, title companies, home inspectors, appraisers, stock brokers, certified financial planners, contractors, architects, engineers, interior decorators, even programmers, software developers, and computer trainers. I have even networked with motivational speakers who have added themselves to our mailing list to be notified when we formally launch businessnetworkingme.com.

Findattorneyorlawyer.com is focused on attorneys. Find Attorney or Lawyer does exactly as the name suggests; we find attorneys for people that need professional legal advice or representation. That site is up and running. I developed it as we have so many lawyer clients who have hired us to optimize their sites and get them listed on the first page of major search engines that I felt I can generate even more clients for them by actually delivering clients directly to them.

I also have another site called theChristiandepot.com. It is a personal site and not meant for business.

I also hold a bachelors degree in business and a MBA.
