Derek Chan’s Summary

As a User Experience Designer at Interactive Partners, Derek Chan has worked with clients in the entertainment and media industries such as BBC America, Madison Square Garden Entertainment, and Showtime Networks.

Derek is a graduate of Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, Canada, with a degree in Communication. He is particularly interested in the areas of interaction design, branding, user research, and the design process.
