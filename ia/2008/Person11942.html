Andrew Simpon’s Summary

I am a designer focused on the way people experience life. Yes, more than websites. I try to create work that is innovative, honest and sustainable.

My current outlet for this is as a visual designer, web developer and consultant. To help me along, I use tools like ethnography, testing and prototyping, web standards, simplicity, and what my mother tells me is a rapier wit.

In June, I finished my Masters in Interaction Design at Malmö University in Sweden, and I'm now living out of Mackenzie Duncan's studio in Toronto, Canada. Poor sap. Right now, business is freelance with an international list of clients working to create things that I love.

In 2006 I co-founded Weeza Collective as an international alliance of designers and friends. It's changing quite quickly, evolving into a kind of multi headed hydra.

Kiva.org is amazing, and I try to help.

Andrew Simpon’s Specialties:

interaction design, web development, visual design, branding.
