Paul Binder’s Summary

Originally in international banking for 5 years in my home country Austria, I changed into a Finance Director position in Hungary in 1991. Since 1994, I have been in managerial positions in the executive search industry in Central and Eastern Europe. Getting a joint understanding of the partner's business requirements and its impact on HR topics is the first step. Working on finding the solutions is our business. While we keep the client informed about the process, he/she can focus on the business. We propose some alternatives and support the partner with the implementation. Acting as a bridge between the cultures of the international investors and the local labour markets in the CEE region is my personal satisfaction.

Paul Binder’s Specialties:

executive searches for senior executives throughout Central Europe, interim management, general management experience, starting-up executive search firms in various CEE countries, building and maintaining a network of cooperation partners, motivating and inspiring people, understanding of emerging markets
