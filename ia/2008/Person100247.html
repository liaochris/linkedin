Julien André’s Summary

I am the co-founder of the jobboard CarriereOnline.com launched in 2003 on the e-recruitment french market. CarriereOnline has improved the way people manage their careers and the way companies hire talent. CarriereOnline's solutions, put job seekers in control of their careers and make it easier and more cost-effective for employers and agencies to find their talents. CarriereOnline was sold to Comareg (Groupe Hersant Media) in september 05.

I'm the co-founder of JobQos.com the first vertical job blogs network in the USA, that enable recruiters to simplify the hard process of hiring the right people, through innovative solutions.

I'm also the co-founder of JobMeeters.com that provides one of the best of breed solution for referral based recruiting, the most efficient way for talent acquisition.

Prior to developing these activities I founded Educangels in 2001, a new concept that enabled students to get its higher studies financed by companies (Ethical Investment in Education).
