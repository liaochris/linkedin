Michael Laffey’s Summary

Corporate Counsel offering over 20 years of legal and corporate experience. A relentless advocate with a keen understanding of the interplay between business and the law. Extensive leadership and legal skills are coupled with a seasoned, winning approach to business negotiations. Established reputation in business and staff development, project administration and the deployment of new technologies.

Qualifications include expertise in human resources, business operations, revenue generation and providing legal strategy. A proven record of working directly with leaders of multiple silos. Adept at teambuilding, consensus partnering, business plan development and winning at the table and in court. Led successful legal campaigns in corporate environments where growth and accountability were paramount.

I am seeking opportunities to use my legal and/or managerial skills for the benefit of start up, emerging growth and visionary companies. I am available for Corporate or General Counsel positions and have experience with companies of all sizes. I am also available for temporary, part time and consulting positions.
