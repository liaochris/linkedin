Adam Golden’s Summary

Adam Golden graduated from the University of Arizona and immediately started a career in the information technology area. He spent 6 years as a senior consultant for a company called Accenture.

In 2002, Adam made a bold decision that impacted his life forever. He left Systems Consulting for a career in Real Estate, and he has never looked back. Being a Realtor allows Adam to take better advantage of the things he loves about being a professional – working with people, solving problems, and making clients extremely happy.

Conducting business with people you respect and trust is essential to forge a working relationship that spans a lifetime. You relate to someone who understands your thoughts and dreams. Someone who genuinely listens and hears what you are saying, and who provides outstanding client care with total integrity. Adam does his very best everyday to exceed his real estate client’s expectations in all these areas.

Adam’s goal as a professional, full-time Realtor is to build lifelong client relationships. His superior dedication to his clients is evident not only during the initial transaction, but through the years. Long-term care continues after the initial transaction is complete and includes resource and information referrals, advocacy, on-demand counsel, and much more.

When you trust someone to guide you through one of the most complex and important processes of your life, you must know that you are working with an experienced market leader who has your best interest at heart. Adam is blessed enough to love what he does for a living. And when you love what you do, it flows through your work and into successful results and opinions of others. Adam’s love of the real estate business, combined with his total integrity, outstanding client care, and market knowledge, has garnered warm appreciation from his clients and the respect of his peers.

Adam is happily married to his high school sweetheart and they have two wonderful kids.
