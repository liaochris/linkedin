Carol Copeland’s Summary

Graduated in 1974 with BS Biology from Oklahoma Christian University. June 1974 entered St. Anthony Hospital School of Medical Technology and graduated as an ASCP Board Certified Medical Technologist in 1975. In 1999 through a laboratory integration project within Methodist Health System I was moved to Patient Financial Services from the clinical laboratory.. I have served on Methodist Health System vendor selection committee for the selection of the LIS (Lab Information System) as well as on the Cerner PathNet implementation team from 1995-1998. 1999 I joined the Patient Financial Services area and the alpha team for the Cerner ProFit patient accounting system development.

Having left the clinical laboratory setting and now involved with the revenue cycle and the demands of data requirements for successful revenue capture. This includes SQL report writing, knowledge of proprietary patient accounting system, Microsoft applications as well as project lead for the current Accuro/Woodmoor QMS/My Mentor/Denials implementation for A/R recovery.

Carol Copeland’s Specialties:

Data mining and analysis through Access/Excel from proprietary patient accounting system
