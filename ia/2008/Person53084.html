Donald E. Hester [DonaldH@MazeAssociates.com]’s Summary

Manage all aspects of an IS Department in a CPA firm including staff, budget, network migrations, servers, wireless, VoIP, security, software development, mergers, regulatory compliance, policy creation, performance based metrics, business continuity plans, access control systems, alarms, and paperless office conversion. Problem solver, typically given tasks outside of my job description. Solutions oriented. Built up staff with service based leadership. Conducted over 200 security reviews in support of financial audits, policy reviews and network vulnerability assessments. Conducted security reviews based on ISO 17799.

Guest lecturer and speaker on the following topics: information systems security, compliance issues, small business & local government network design & management, ISO 17799, COBIT 4.0, NIST and other standards.

Academic and corporate trainer with over 1,500 hours of instruction delivered on security awareness, CISSP, Security+, Safety, core MCSA/MCSE courses, MS Exchange and MS ISA server. Pioneer in online training and hybrid courses at Los Medanos College and San Diego City College. Designed courseware for CISSP prep course and other security classes. Consulted as a technical expert for security related books including Microsoft Official Curriculum, and Microsoft certification exams. MCT Ambassador for Microsoft Learning at the SQL 2005 Launch. Served on various advisory committees and as a Subject Matter Expert for various projects.
