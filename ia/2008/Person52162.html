"DJ CHEF" Marc Weiss’s Summary

W: www.DJCHEF.com
E: DJChefEvents@gmail.com
P: 888-DJ-CHEF-1

DJ CHEF Marc Weiss known for “spinnin' the beats while cookin’ the treats” is the only entertainer who simultaneously cooks gourmet food & deejays for special events around the world.

Marc started out a DJ in high school and then went on to culinary school & became a chef. After working in the food industry for a number of years honing his skills with top chefs like Bobby Flay & Matthew Kenney in fine restaurants of NYC, Marc combined his two passions food & music and started entertaining at parties & events where he deejayed while preparing a gourmet meal.

His unique style created a buzz in the industry and it wasn't long before he appeared on the Food Network’s “What’s Hot!, What’s Cool!” and co-hosted MTV’s Celebrity Spring Break Style special with Lindsay Lohan. Other notable TV performances include; MSNBC’s Entertainment Hot List, Discovery Channel’s Party Planner, FUSE TV’s One Year Anniversary live on IMX, & What’s Hot in the Hamptons with Gina Glickman.

DJ CHEF is presently in production of his own TV series and is performing nationwide at special events, corporate functions, launch parties, grand openings, food festivals, trade shows & private parties. Recent clients include; Viacom, Tommy Hilfiger, Gucci, Clairol, Target, Christie Brinkley, Madison Square Garden, MTV, James Blunt, Estee Lauder, Lifetime Television, W Hotels, Trump National, & Mercedes-Benz.

Marc was honored this year by his Alma Mater the New York Restaurant School and will be inducted into their Hall of Fame for his achievement in the culinary field.. He is also is active in supporting charities. He served as Honorary Chairman for an event to help in the fight against AIDS with LIACC and served as the honorary chef for the March of Dimes Star Chef’s Gala.
