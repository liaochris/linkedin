David Birnbaum’s Summary

Fifteen years experience working in venture capital, product development and global expansion efforts. Currently at the Bank of New York Mellon's Office of Innovation, originating and developing new banking and asset management products.

Prior to BNYM, founded and managed assignments for venture and hedge fund clients at Globeopen Advisors (www.globeopenadvisors.com; 2002-present).

Co-launched and served as Director, Operations for VC fund Softbank Emerging Markets (1999-2001), building out operations and reviewing/selecting investment portfolio candidates in multiple markets.

Management Consultant at PricewaterhouseCoopers in NYC and Japan (1996-99), serving bulge bracket financial services clients in front office strategy and back office process improvement programs.

Master of International Affairs with concnetrations in East Asia and International Finance, Columbia University.
