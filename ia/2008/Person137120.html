Murray Clarke’s Summary

After almost 5 years as a recruiter helping build BMES to become one of Japan's top recruiting firms in the gaishi technology sector, I have decided to jump in with both feet to join my partners full time in assisting US/Euro vendors into Japan.

Our business will be called WorkBrain Japan. Several deals already are making strong progress as we focus on OEM deals and major resellers agreements.

Seeing a need for many vendors seeking assistance in sales and channel development before any recruiting reqs, this is a logical extension of my work at BMES where I was regularly in touch with vendors looking at the Japan market for executive search, recruiting and hiring activity.

Lived and worked in Japan for 10 years, having completed University here. A family man (2 great kids) who likes to play and watch a variety of sports. Also a Japanese speaker who had a stint on scholarship at Tokyo University to round off my education. Top of mind "most influential books": From Good to Great, The Clean Tech Revolution.
