  • Vision Keeper

    History of Computing in Learning and Education Project (HCLE)

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Museums and Institutions industry)

    January 2004 — Present (4 years 1 month)

    The History of Computing in Learning and Education (HCLE) is a project dedicated to the conservation and interpretation of artifacts and stories illustrating how computers became essential tools for learning. Currently housed in offices at the Sobrato Center for Nonprofits, 580 Valley Way, Milpitas, CA, USA, the project will culminate in a Virtual Museum to be located at ComputingInEdHistory.org. To join our community of volunteers, visit and add to our wiki at http://www.seedwiki.com/wiki/learning_options_history_of_microcomputing_in_education/learning_options_history_of_microcomputing_in_education.
