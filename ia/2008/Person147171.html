Patryk Urban’s Summary

I am an Electrical Engineering graduate (Optical Communications, MSc. Eng., 2004) and Pedagogical Studies graduate (Post-MSc, 2004).

I have experience in broadband access networks and related topics, PON and FTTx technologies, optical hardware engineering, optical measurements and testing as well as in nonlinear optics. The projects I have participated in are: BBPhotonics, KM3Net, ePhotonOne, BONE. As part of my current duties I publish articles in IEEE proceedings and international conferences. I am a co-editor of the Proceedings of IEEE/LEOS Benelux Annual Workshop. I am also involved in event organization (national-scale workshops for industry and R&D people).

I have worked as a product manager taking care of www products which involved managing websites, contacting and negotiating with customers, webdesigning and webmastering.

Moreover, I have also taken jobs related to teaching (English teacher, physics teacher, computer studies teacher, educational assisstant at the university) on different educational levels.

Since 2005, I have been an IEEE member and since 2006 a member of IEEE/LEOS Benelux Student Chapter Board. In the same year I became an IEEE Region 8 News correspondent and since 2007 I have been acting as an IET Electronics Letters reviewer and a correspondent to Computerworld Poland where I publish on optical access.

Patryk Urban’s Specialties:

optical communication, hands-on experience in optical transmission measurements, VPI modelling, optical access network architectures, FTTX, WDM/TDM PON
