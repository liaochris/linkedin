Ruth Allen’s Summary

ruthmedia is a new force in freelance journalism, media and public relations in central Scotland.

ruthmedia builds on Ruth Allen's 30 years of experience in the world of media, journalism and public relations to offer clients a comprehensive, reliable and quality 'basket of services' through use of the established and the new media.

ruthmedia guarantees total commitment to any project, a prompt and speedy response to any request, and a quality end product, delivered on time.
