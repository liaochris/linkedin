Nikki Gordon’s Summary

I am a National Management Recruiter with 7-Eleven, Inc. I also have experience as a Human Resource Generalist with 7-Eleven. I have additional experience in recruitment, employee relations, talent management and development with previous employers.

My goals are to continue to drive the process and be an influential player in my company’s strategy to “Select the Best.” I continue to improve myself personally and professionally every day by seeking out new challenges and opportunities.

I also would like to have a larger impact on the evolution of our culture by hiring the right talent, training that talent effectively, developing individual’s strengths and treating people well in a way that will inspire our entire team’s personal and professional growth.

Persistent development of our team will directly affect our overall and continued success as the leader in our industry.
