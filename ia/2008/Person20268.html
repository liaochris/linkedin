Bas Raijmakers’s Summary

Bas Raijmakers is currently creative director and owner of STBY London and Amsterdam. He recently completed his PhD in Design Interactions at the Royal College of Art. During his MA studies in cultural studies at the University of Amsterdam, he focussed on how people use media, from magazines to tv. Early 1990s he co-founded internet company ACS-i that was later acquired by Lost Boys/IconMedialab. With his company he developed methods to research how people use interactive media. His recent PhD research focuses on how documentary film can be used for design research. The new research method Design Documentaries he developed helps multidisciplinary teams to explore the rich fabric of everyday life as a source of information and inspiration at the start of design processes. He has published about his work in Germany, the Netherlands, the UK and the US. He does his research in industry and academia, for instance with HP labs (UK), Philips Medical Systems (US and NL), the Equator network (UK), Goldsmiths College (UK), Intelligent Textiles (UK), DesignPlus (UK), France Telecom/Orange (F) and the Helen Hamlyn Research Centre (UK).
