Julie Chiesa’s Summary

Julie Chiesa is the President/Owner of InfoDirectors, Inc.

She has more than fifteen years of experience and leadership in emerging technologies. In 1994, she was an Internet pioneer working CMP's TechWeb, one of the first websites launched with internet advertising. Since 2001, she has been working in Healthcare IT, helping to foster the use of electronic medical records and other technologies among physicians and their practices.

InfoDirectors is a strategic technology and communications company dedicated to helping healthcare businessess succeed. We build lasting client relationships through dedication, integrity and professionalism. We ensure continued growth through our high level of client satisfaction and our commitment to new technology research and implementation.

We specialize in the development, implementation and support of the NextGen electronic medical records (EMR) and practice management (EPM) systems. Since 2003, we have assisted NextGen customers with pre-implementation planning and project management, onsite implementation support as well as post-implementation evaluation of systems.

Our expertise in medical office workflow coupled with superior NextGen template and document development, enables us to share our knowledge and experience to help clients succeed in the transition to paperless automation. Julie is a NextGen Ceritified Professional (NCP).
Note: InfoDirectors is NOT a reseller of NextGen or any other software.

InfoDirectors also provides full communications services for medical practices and all business sectors including logo and identity development, print marketing and distribution as well as marketing strategy in electronic medium like websites and e-newsletters.

Please read additional testimonials on our website at http://www.infodirectors.com/testimonials.html
