Russ Chesley’s Summary

Broad senior level experience as a global executive, producer and consultant in virtually all areas of the worldwide entertainment and media industries, in addition to real estate, retail and technology. A strong knowldge base and background in corporate management, funding and advisement/consulting.

Not afraid of new challenges and certainly not of change! Willing to explore uncharted waters, enthusiastic about the future and energized by unusual and tough jobs.

Effective and innovative leader and communicator with excellent international business and creative relationships, as well as outstanding interpersonal skills.

Skilled at managing cross-functional teams of strong individuals, particularly the highly creative and the technically oriented.

Particularly focused on both ends of the entrepreneurial spectrum -- developing and growing a new business or venture and also turning around a troubled organization.

Interested in long-term or interim positions in general management, marketing, sales management and business development, as well as consulting and contract assignments. Also interested in entrepreneurial opportunities.
