Alex Zivoder’s Summary

Contact: alex.zivoder@gmail.com
Member of MyLink500.com

Over the course of a diversified 20-year career, Alex Zivoder has held executive and senior management positions in online travel, telecommunications, credit card, co-operation and training industries, in various countries of Europe and in Russia, in public and private companies as well as in non-profit organisations and government agencies.

Zivoder is currently SVP of Expedia in Europe and leads its businesses in the UK, Germany, France and Italy. Before April 2008, Zivoder was MD of Expedia in France.

Prior to Expedia, Zivoder held 3 Managing Director positions in the Tele2 group:
- He was MD of Tango, Luxembourg's leading mobile operator, and Tele2 Luxembourg, Luxembourg's main challenger in the fixed telephony market. Under his leadership, Tango and Tele2 increased their market shares, Tango was among the first mobile operators in Europe to offer 3rd generation (UMTS) services to its customers, and he improved the top and bottom lines of the company better than the market.
- he was MD of 3C Communications, the worldwide leader in integrated credit card solutions for hotels, restaurants and parking operators
- he was MD of IntelliNet, Tele2's provider of low cost fixed-line telephony services.

Between 1991 and 2000, Zivoder worked for and at Eurostat, the statistical agency of the European Commission. He was in charge of the production of Europe's statistics on foreign trade, advised the Statistical Office of Russia in managing its transition to the production of statistics based on western standards, and managed EC's Tacis Statistics and Training of European Statisticians programmes.

Alex Zivoder founded OPAL, the Luxembourg Federation of Alternative Operators.

He holds a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Nice-Sophia Antipolis (France) and an Engineer's degree in Statistics from the Ecole Nationale de la Statistique et de l'Administration Economique (Paris, France).
