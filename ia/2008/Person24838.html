Bob Frye’s Summary

Over 15 years experience in delivering rich media to digital delivery channels, since 2000 focused on delivery of rich media via Wireless. World class expertise on production, encoding, and delivery issues, all focused on providing Best Possible User Experience. Have consulted with major manufacturers and providers (Qualcomm MediaFLO, AVID, AOL, Apple, Anystream, Popwire/Telestream, Pinnacle, Sorenson, Ripcode, Transpera, BlastRadius, NHRA). Have successfully supported VPs of BizDev, Sales, and Marketing in closing major deals with DoCoMo, Orange UK, Orange France while at PacketVideo. On-site management of commercial trial with Swisscom while at Ortiva.

Provide stragetic, technical, marketing and operational consulting and planning. Competitive product analysis. Research & Development, Beta Testing of codecs and encoding systems.

6 Years Experience as Product Manager while at Yamaha Corp of America/DMI Division. Managed full product life cycle of digital musical instruments for the professional music market.

Especially skilled at presenting complex technical issues to audiences large and small, from novice to world class professionals. Trained content production teams world wide on sucessfully creating rich media for Wireless delivery. Appeared as subject matter expert in solo presentations and on panels at major industry trade shows, NAB, DVExpo, NAMM, etc.

Bob Frye’s Specialties:

Video and Audio production and encoding focused on new digital delivery channels: Wireless, Internet, IPTV, HD. Product Management, Product Development, Competitive Product Analysis, Workflow Analysis and Strategy, Studio Systems and Integration.
