Terry Bean’s Summary

First off, what a great site this is. Kudos to all of you who make it so. .

A LITTLE ABOUT ME:

I am at once an attractor a facilitator and a connector. I attract and facilitate knowledge, partnerships, opportunities and resources. I uncover needs and connect the needy with the appropriate solution provider(s). I listen to people's thoughts, help them define their desires and connect them with the resources to see them fulfilled.

I am here
I am here to help
I am here to help you

Let me start by suggesting this:

Get clear on why you are here (true statement for every aspect in life, but here I am simply talking about linkedin)

Be ready to offer assistance every time you are in a position to do so

Understand that the good you give comes back to you

Be comfortable sharing how others can help you so they can have good come back to them too

Networking has to start with an expressed need. Guide us as to how to assist you so we can grow together.


Understand that my main purpose here is to help create opportunities for others.

Let me know who I can help so we can all benefit.

HOW YOU CAN HELP ME:

I have built a substantial network in the Detroit market and I have the goal of improving our local economy. I am looking to work with a couple of companies who really want to penetrate this market. If you are or know of such a company, contact me at tbean@networkedinc.com
