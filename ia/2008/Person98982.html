John Poeta’s Summary

Prior to Clear Vision Capital, John founded two regional investment banks and was a partner in two regional law firms. Over the past 19 years John has developed a reputation for offering solid, practical business advice. His expertise and insight in assisting middle-market companies with their transactional needs has proved invaluable in numerous situations.

In addition to his extensive professional career, John has held numerous positions of responsibility with a variety of professional and community organizations.

REPRESENTATIVE TRANSACTIONS

Served as financial consultant to a privately held, $60 million specialty chemical (personal care and performance lubricants) manufacturer facing financial difficulties.

Served as financial consultant to start-up digital television network seeking growth capital to expand its present offerings across new media platforms – broadband, IPTV and mobile distribution.

Served as financial and strategic consultant to start-up developer of web-based interactive sports strategy games.

Served as financial consultant to a $30 million manufacturer of photo frames and albums. This project included the refinancing of the company’s Far East subsidiaries.

Served as financial consultant on the transaction relative to Chartwell Investments II LLC’s portfolio investment in Richard Childress Racing Enterprises, Inc. See Buyouts, vol. 16, No. 9, April 28, 2003.

Served as east coast counsel to one of the nation’s largest commercial lenders, representing nine of 13 divisions including corporate finance, healthcare finance, inventory finance, mezzanine finance, resorts and real estate finance in a variety of situations over an eight-year period (1993-2001); served as lead counsel in nine states; negotiated multiple financing facilities,and managed the sale of numerous businesses.

John Poeta’s Specialties:

Raising Debt, Subordinated Debt and Equity Capital; M&A Advisory Services
