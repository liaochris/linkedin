Ty Smith’s Summary

With 28 years of executive search experience, I specialize in the nationwide recruitment of tax professionals up to VP/Partner level. This includes Federal & State Income Tax, State and Local Tax, International Tax, Transfer Pricing (Tax), and Sales & Use and Property Tax.

Successful searches include: International Tax Director, VP Taxation, Corporate Tax Manager, Director of Tax Compliance, Director of State and Local Tax, Transfer Pricing Manager, Sales and Use Tax Manager, Federal Tax Senior Manager, Tax Attorney.

I also place tax contractors.
