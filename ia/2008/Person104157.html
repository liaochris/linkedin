Keith Bilous’s Summary

I am a successful entrepreneur with an extensive history and proven competencies in business development, product development, sales, marketing and every other aspect of starting-up and running a business. I am Founder and President of content and community moderation company,ICUC Moderation Services.

With origins dating back 10 years in the wireless biz, I planted roots in wireless marketing, launching first-ever wireless solutions such as mobile communities and text driven events.

My expertise in managing wireless content and communities grew and evolved with the explosion of user generated content on the web. I am relied upon by some of the biggest brands to manage their online content and protect their online communities.

I am a strategic yet real world thinker that has a solid understanding of how brands can integrate user generated content into their marketing plans. Please contact me for the latest on User Generated Content strategy, and content and community moderation and management.
