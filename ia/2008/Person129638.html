Michael Edelson’s Summary

Mr. Edelson is a successful entrepreneur with more than 20 years of experience in e-commerce, software and credit card and risk management systems. Before joining Jettis as Chief Technology Officer in 2006, Mr. Edelson co-founded of Stealth Ideas Inc., a privacy software company which develops software and devices that allow consumers to surf the Web in anonymity in an effort to prevent identity theft. Before co-founding Stealth Ideas Inc. in 2004, Mr. Edelson served as chief technology officer and vice president of engineering at Network Telephone Services where he developed credit card, private billing, risk management and fraud control systems. He also has served as chief technology officer at R.J. Gordon Company and as president of Electronic Authorization Systems, where he developed credit card processing systems for audio text and Internet applications. As the holder of two U.S. patents (4,908,850 and 5,007,078), Mr. Edelson has pioneered a number of algorithms risk management practices central to e-commerce applications used today.
