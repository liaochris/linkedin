Stephen Furness’s Summary

Work force mobilisation software check out our web site www.activiser.com

Activiser is smart paper for field service teams. It puts their unique jobsheets on PDAs, so they send jobs out to their field workers as they occur and get the timesheets back.

It means they improve their customer service and maximise time use to get better profitability. What’s unique about Activiser is that our clients can use their unique data and automate this while minimising unnecessary changes to their business systems – that’s why we call it smart paper.

We are currently looking for dealers in New Zealand , Australia, United Kingdom, and North America.

We currently have a presence in all these countries but we are looking to expand this.

The margins are geared exceptionally well for the dealers if you have any questions contact me on my email address.
