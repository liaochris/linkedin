Paul Templin Ashford’s Summary

I am currently the VP of Development at Solstice Software and am responsible for both managing and leading the development team. Most recently, I was the lead software architect for Solstice Software before being promoted to the director position. Solstice Software develops an SOA Testing Tool. This has given me a deep understanding of the technologies involved with SOA (Messaging Systems, Web Services, etc).
I have over 10 years of experience working within the software development field. I previously worked as the Java Technology Lead for a Diamond Technologies, Inc. While there, I managed the development of multiple JavaEE based applications for multiple markets including pharmaceutical, insurance, newspaper and gaming industries.
I have a BS in Computer Science from the University of Pittsburgh.
