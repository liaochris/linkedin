Terry Goldman’s Summary

I'm a Senior Account Executive at ElectricArtists overseeing TV and new Product launches. I've overseen campaigns that range from video games such as Infected, GameTap, Advent Rising and Duel Masters to feature films such as WB’s The Phantom of the Opera, The Dukes of Hazzard, Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants, Must Love Dogs and Constantine. I've also executed online campaigns for brands such as WWE, BMW, Pirelli, Nikon, Cuervo, Microsoft, Clorox, Best Buy and Netflix. Television campaigns include Hustle (Emmy Nominee) and the critically acclaimed Broken Trail (AMC), Monk (Emmy Nominee), The 4400, Burn Notice, Nashville Star, The Starter Wife (Emmy Nominee), In Plain Sight and Psych (USA), Band of Brothers, 10 Days That Unexpectedly Changed America (Emmy Nominated), Desperate Crossing, Engineering an Empire (Emmy Nominated), The Dark Ages, Sherman's March, Star Wars: The Legacy Revealed, Life After People (highest rated History program ever) Human Weapon, Ice Road Truckers, Ax Men, (History Channel), Campus Ladies (Oxygen), King of Cars, Dog the Bounty Hunter and Driving Force (A&E), The Fantasia Barrino Story (Lifetime), Buy The Ticket, Take The Ride: Hunter S Thompson on Film, Head Case and Going to Pieces: The Rise & Fall of the Slasher Film (Starz). Prior to joining ElectricArtists, I have 13 years of experience in the entertainment industry including Universal Studios where I was part of marketing teams working on projects such as Shrek, Hulk, The Cat in the Hat, Jurassic Park, 2 Fast 2 Furious, The Grinch, Mummy Returns, Van Helsing and seasonal events such as Halloween Horror Nights and Rock the Universe. My feature film work earned screen credit on films such as Halloween H20, Lake Placid and Goodbye Lover. Other film projects assisting director, film composer and editor John Ottman (X2, Fantastic 4, Superman Returns, The Usual Suspects) include Apt Pupil for director Bryan Singer and Cruel Intentions for director Roger Kumble.
