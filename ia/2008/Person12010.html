Andrew Walmsley’s Summary

A founding member of WorkShares, (www.workshares.co.uk), specialising in Microsoft's SharePoint technologies to bring collaborative solutions to our clients.

An experienced Consultant\Project Manager, having worked for ‘Big 5’ consultancy in London and several Microsoft Gold Partners both here and abroad. I have gained 'grass roots' level experience in the design, build, delivery and support of Microsoft Enterprise Content Management based solutions to public/local government, education, not for profits, telecom and financial services sectors.
