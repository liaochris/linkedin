Frank Rousseau’s Summary

What is an ideavelopper ?
It's just neologism of mine to name an hybrid between a software developer (strong technical background acquired from my formation) and an idea "expresser" (coming from my experiences). I mean by idea expresser, someone who can clearly define what you think and communicate it efficiently.

What about my experiences ?
I have worked as consultant or developer on a wide range of subjects around data analyse, artificial intelligence and visual effects such as : peer-to-peer heterogeneous database, embodied conversational agent, eye motion capture, BI requirements analyse, ...
These projects needed good communication skills (oral and written) to understand needs, to realize what was asked, to statisfy stakeholders and to ensure a good useability.

And hobbies ?
My main hobby is webdesign. I built several websites and try to keep in touch with latest technologies and design tendances.

Am I looking for opportunities ?
Open minded, fully invested and very adaptive I am interested in every kind of your projects which can make me discover a new area of IT technologies and/or visual art, specially at international.

Business intelligence, data bases, requirements analyse, artificial intelligence, motion capture, webdesign
