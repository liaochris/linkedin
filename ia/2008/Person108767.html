Kristen Kouk’s Summary

As an eager public relations professional, I relocated to San Diego in January 2008 having completed a Bachelor of Science degree in Public Relations from the University of Texas at Austin within three and a half years. During my time at UT, I enjoyed studying abroad in France, acting as secretary of my sorority, and completing three unique PR internships. Upon arriving in San Diego, I was offered the exciting opportunity of joining a local, unprecedented political campaign. As a fresh face on the San Diego scene I benefited tremendously from the connections and relationships I cultivated during the fast-paced campaign. Following the results of the June 3rd primary election, I accepted a position with stellar, boutique PR agency Rachel Kay Public Relations. As the PR Coordinator for RKPR, I have placed our clients with numerous media outlets including, KFMB San Diego, Outside Magazine, Sports Illustrated for Kids and U.S. News & World Report.
