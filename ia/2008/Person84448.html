Michael Jarnum’s Summary

As a consultant I strive to develop programs what will create susatinable change and advantages for my clients. Working in close collaboration with customers, I develop programs to include strategic and tactical initiatives, buth woth constant focus on feasibility of implementation and change in the organisation.

High level of experience in sales, service and life science organisations, with focus on complete go-to-market initiatives as well as experience with category management, product development and training program development.

Academic background as lawyer and MBA, and with business experience of more than 10 years working with sales, key account management, business development and innovation and 4 years of providing Management Consulting services to medium and large companies.

As a Management Consultant I worked with growth initiatives : organisation and process development, ensuring a structured and strategic sound growth, creating internal and external focus on business critical issues and implementing change initiatives and operationalize strategic initiatives.
I have worked with both management and key field employees, incl. analysing and developing initiatives, as well as implementing strategy and optimised operations.
My experience is cross industry, midsize to large companies, primarily based in Scandinavia, but with iglobal activities.
