Gregory Ratcliff’s Summary

Gregory Ratcliff is an exprienced executive in critical systems. From telecom and data center monitoring and control through weapons launch delivery systems, Mr. Ratcliff has the in depth technical and business acumen to lead technical programs while expertly communicating to the public markets.

Most recently taking responsibility for product management and marketing efforts for Emerson Network Power's Monitoring Group in 2004, Mr. Ratcliff has focused on increasing the value monitoring and control systems bring to cooling, power and power distribution systems for ultra-reliable data centers. Mr. Ratcliff has written many articles and white papers, while conducting in-person presentations worldwide. Starting in 2006, Mr. Ratcliff has initiated several programs that advocate predictive critical systems management utilizing monitoring and control information to predict trends, reduce faults and increase operational efficiency in critical systems.

Greg has undergraduate degrees in Applied Mathematics, Information Systems and a Master’s degree in Business Administration, majoring in Technology Management.

Gregory Ratcliff’s Specialties:

Program Development, Product Development, Engineering Management, Marketing Management, Systems Architecture, Research and Development, Mergers and Acquisition. Professional experience in large corporate environments utilizing ISO 9000+, CMMI, Six Sigma. Commercial Pilot, Instrument Instructor.
