Martin Scicluna’s Summary

With formal qualifications in software engineering, Martin has over eight years experience in the design and management of complex enterprise-wide business systems. In the past, he has lectured and tutored at universities, presented at international conferences and has even won various scholarships.

Interests in interactive online technologies and Web2.0 social networks saw Martin also complete a Master’s research degree in the area of "knowledge networking within complex business systems." The research examined the behaviour and development of social networks to uncover ways of commercialising the "knowledge within."

Believing that interactive web technologies hold the key to improving our business networking and interaction capacity, Martin launched 2 businesses:

- SmarterSoft: To enable enterprises of all sizes to better engage, communicate and manage their customers online.

- Peak Online Business Advisers: To help businesses uncover the true potential of the Internet to make them more competitive, profitable and rewarding.

Martin’s primary focus now is to help businesses not only better understand their online opportunities, but to deliver the tools and training needed to maximise productivity and profits.
