Bryan L. Hearn, CIR’s Summary

I am an Executive Recruiter who focuses on recruiting and qualifying IT Professionals for some of the largest companies in Chicago. I also recruit professionals in other areas of expertise including Sales, Marketing, Human Resources, Management, and Finance.

I am very passionate about helping people make the next move in their careers. I do not view this profession as one where I am just fill jobs, but rather as one that I am helping someone get to a better situation in their life and building a long term relationship. I work very closely with my clients to insure that they have all the information and guidance necessary for the interview and offer process. I know I bring tremendous value to both my clients and candidates in the quality of people and guidance I provide. My objective if to find quality candidates for my clients and provide the best customer service I can to all people involved. I am looking to make contact with individuals that can turn into long term professional relationships and I can help them in the next phase of their career, would you like to be part of my network?
