Michael Singer’s Summary

Michael Singer is a triple-threat journalist (Television, Radio, Online) who has been writing about technology for 10 years. His most recent stint is serving as West Coast Editor for the nationally published InformationWeek. He has also written for ABC-7 News, KTVU-TV and CNET News.com where he covered PCs, handhelds, gadgets.

When he is not on deadline, Michael enjoys playing guitar and gardening at his home in the San Francisco Bay Area.
