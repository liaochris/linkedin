Alex Smith’s Summary

People come first. People always come first. Technologies are built by people to serve people, so people are always going to come first.

Perhaps I'm a bit idealistic, but my professional goals follow this line of reasoning, and any software project I am part of will be a people-first endeavor. Gone are the days of the lone hacker cranking out the killer app in his basement. Today, the key to a successful software project is teamwork based on solid communication and strategy. There will always be superstars, but the ones that shine brightest will be the ones who know the passing game just as well as how to run the field.
