Sue Crane’s Summary

Over the course of my career, I have worked in a number of industries and technologies, though the majority of my expertise is in the health care field. My experience encompasses the full life cycle of software development including coding, technical documentation, project management, business analysis, training, sales, QA, product delivery, implementation and maintenance.

My most important accomplishments include designing, coding, installing, training and maintaining software for a psychiatric clinic in Smyrna, GA. The software included A/R, A/P, G/L, Payroll, appointment scheduling, claims filing, and a highly complex collections follow-up routine.

As a Configuration Manager with McKesson's Business1 PFM Hospital Enterprise product, I gained experience with IBM Smalltalk, SQL and Oracle, often performing DBA functions.

While with Per-Se Technologies, I completed several acquisition data conversions, two of which were finished within a seemingly impossible time frame and saved the company millions of dollars in revenue by reducing the downtime of billing systems.

Currently working with McKesson's MDIV product as an Electronic Remittance Advice programmer/analyst and product specialist.
