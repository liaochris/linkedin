Barth Ballard’s Summary

Senior executive leader with experience as the CEO of a NASDAQ traded new-media company. Execution-focused strategist with deep expertise in leading web marketing, operations, and business development teams to exceed goals and drive revenue. Over 15 years of experience working with enterprises ranging from venture-funded startup operations to global corporations and world leading brands, including Levi’s, MTV, Sony, Google/YouTube, and Apple. Successes have been featured in the Wall Street Journal, USA Today, San Francisco Chronicle and Business Week.

CAREER ACCOMPLISHMENTS

• Achieved increase of 1.3 million audio tracks at retailers by improving operational processes.
• Grew audio revenue over 300% from $2.77 million in 2006 to $9.63 million in 2007.
• Established and managed over 15 new music, video and mobile partnerships with major brands such as YouTube/Google, Apple iTunes, Real Networks, eMusic, AOL’s IN2TV, Movielink, Helio and MobiTV.
• Doubled membership of social networking website from 600,000 to 1.2 million.
• Increased client’s e-commerce revenue from $300,000 (2003) to $2.1 million (2005).
• Gained an eight-fold increase in web traffic within three years by implementing SEO and SEM techniques.
• Launched one of the first concert cybercasts, which drove 1.2 million people to re-launch of apparel website.
