Charlie Liu’s Summary

I am an enthusiastic and driven individual with a MBA in marketing and five years of professional marketing experience in CPG who is seeking a position that will allow me to approach marketing from a holistic perspective.

As a media planner who represented clients such as Motorola, Kraft, and P&G, I have established a strong foundation in the classical methods of reaching consumers through various communication channels. However, I grew tired of the habitual media planning process; therefore, I took it upon myself to re-enter academia and pursue an advanced degree in marketing in hopes of building upon my professional foundation.

My goal is to become a marketing professional who creates innovations without sacrificing various classical approaches.
