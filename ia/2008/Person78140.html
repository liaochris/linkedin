Hugh Elliot’s Experience

  • Managing Partner

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Financial Services industry)

    August 2007 — Present (7 months)

    Mr. Elliot is a founding partner of 1066 Capital which provides business brokerage services, advises on mergers and acquisitions, and provides business financial consulting.

    Prior to forming 1066 Capital in 2007, Mr. Elliot had been an independent management, mergers and acquisitions, finance, and business consultant since 1979. He has specialized in assisting small and start up companies with strategic planning and financial management.

    Mr. Elliot has been active in mergers and acquisitions, since 1988, serving as a member of due diligence teams and in structuring proposed acquisitions and financings as well as undertaking financial management for small defense contractors.
