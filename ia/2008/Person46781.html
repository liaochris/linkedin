David Hewison’s Summary

With a background in establishing start-up businesses both in IT and Events Management, as well as a number of years in Advertising, I have a solid business background that I constantly draw upon.

Having a core focus on permanent appointments, I specialise within in the Project Services (Project Managers, Business Analysts and Project Controls) domain. Having proven myself in recruitment, I have access to pivotal accounts in the BRW Top 100 firms as well as start-ups and SME’s.

To ensure I offer my clients the highest calibre people, I work with and manage numerous candidates including, CTO’s, Programme Managers, Consultants, Project Managers, Business Analysts, Project Planners and Project controls to name a few. My focus is to constantly interact with both clients and candidates to keep up to date with the market and its movements. Most importantly I strive to constantly stay in touch with past and present candidates to ensure I have access to their network and to understand who is where and of those, who stand out.

**Please note: We can now offer 457 VISA Sponsorship to those IT candidates with highly specialised and/or rare technical skills who are interested in working in Australia *
