Eliot Wasmund’s Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur and philanthropreneur interested in developing a diverse set of businesses across a broad specrum of industries and sectors for the ultimate purpose of benefiting society as a whole.

I am currently working on laying the groundwork for a cross-industry business development, asset acquisition, and investment firm. For that reason, I am looking to network with the right firms and the right people. It's all about building mutual value, right?

I'm looking to network with the following sort of firms and people:

Investment Banks, Investment Firms, Buyout Firms, Recruitment Agencies, Consulting Agencies


Generally, I'm looking for shakers and movers, the leaders of tomorrow... Marketing Gurus, Innovators, Inventors, Entrepreneurs, Stand-Up Business Managers, Business Owners, Angel Investors, Businses Brokers, RE Gurus, Procurement Agents, and all the people that I can't bring to mind at this time of night.

I'm very interested in meeting the creative and analytical geniuses out there, those who can't help feeling and thinking about whatever it is you feel and think about all the time. Perhaps you don't define yourself this way... in that case, I want to meet individuals with Extreme Passion for just about anything. We need to talk.

I'm also looking for some top notch programmers, web designers, corporate attorneys, accountants, and more.

I want to meet individuals who want to change the world in a positive way.

I am also looking to collaborate in the areas of worldwide economic development, emerging technologies (such as nano/bio-technology and AI), advanced mathematics, advanced physics, and more.

I am not a fly by night individual. I am passionate about what I do and am extremely motivated to meet my goals and help others meet their own. In other words, I'm here to stay. Let's work together.

Use eliot@eliotwasmund.com to invite me to your network.
