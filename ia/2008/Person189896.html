Terry Christopherson’s Summary

We develop The Complete Professional

The Complete Professional adds polish to the technical part of their profession. They have something extra that makes people sit up and take notice. Call it charisma or call it pizazz, it sets them apart and insures their success in life and in their career. The Complete Professional is proficient in three key areas:

How to Plan for the Un-Planned Presentation

The Complete Professional presents their ideas with confidence and power, whether they have 5 minutes or 5 days to prepare. They respond to questions with confidence and persuasion. as a result they make a positive impression as one who is knowledgeable and competent.

Cerebration: the skill of memory and creativity

The Complete Professional is able to recall information and details. They remember names of the people they meet. They build credibility by being knowledgeable in their field and attentive to clients and customers. They have a trained memory and they use it.

Pioritasking in a Multitasking World

The Complete Professional gets the right things done when they need to be done. They know the difference between activity and productivity. They control their time and projects so they get results without sacrificing balance in their life.

At TLC Seminars we work with groups and individuals to develop The Complete Professional. In practical and relevant seminars, or private coaching our material is tailored to your needs to produce the results you desire.
