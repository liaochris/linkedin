Christina Baker’s Summary

I received my PhD in inorganic/materials chemistry with a focus on the development of conducting polymer nanofibers and metal nanocomposites for practical application. Some areas of focus have included sensors, actuators/artificial muscles, electrochromics, flash memory, transparent conductors and composites. Experienced with several smart polymers on the nano and macro scale for biological and other applications in addition to those mentioned.

Currently I am the director of operations for Fibron Technologies, a start-up company focused on the development of conducting polymer nanofibers and composites for avrious applications. We are interested in new collaborations with those that may want to utilize our materials in their applications. In the near future we may also be seeking a researcher at the postdoctoral level to develop biosensors.
