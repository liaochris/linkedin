NSK Nikolaos S. Karastathis BSc CS, nsk@karastathis.org’s Summary

My name is Nikolaos S. Karastathis BSc(Hons) MBCS MIET MIEEE MACM MACS(Prov), but you can call me NSK. For more info see http://www.karastathis.org/

I am a an open networker (LION = LinkedIn Open Networker) and keen to network with anyone. Just invite me to your network and I will accept all invitations! You can find me at themetanetwork.com as well.

I am an independent contractor offering state-of-the-art software engineering services worldwide. In the past I have been a programmer working on projects of the European Commission and a writer.

I participate in free open-source software communities, I am a Contributing Member of the FSF, the founder of the Wikinerds.org and AlgoLibre.org projects, and am also a science enthusiast with a particular interest in natural sciences and space exploration., I am a member of the Planetary Society and the British Interplanetary Society, among many other organisations.

I am completing an MSc in Management and my thesis research is on the business applications of swarm intelligence and its effect on business adaptability. Previously I have been awarded a Bachelor's with honours in Computer Science.

My LinkedIn network is growing at an exponential rate, and it gives me access to several important global economy centres (San Francisco 7%, New York 7%) and cutting-edge industries (IT 15%, Computer Software 12%, Telecoms 7%). You, too, can be part of my network!
