EXPERIENCE SUMMARY:
Over 18 years of helping clients develop technical documentation for their systems and products. My technical writing services have supported all phases of project design, development, implementation and deployment. I bring a high level of technical understanding and SDLC familiarity that facilitates quick and thorough development of the needed technical manuals and documents. Based in Mount Airy, MD, my clients are primarily located in suburban MD, Northern VA, Washington DC, and the Baltimore/Annapolis corridor, however I am available for short-term projects nationwide U.S.
