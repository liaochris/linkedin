Manish Devgan’s Summary

I am currently a Product Manager with Oracle WebCenter Product team with focus on Security and Web 2.0 Collaboration services. Prior to this, as a Security Architect, I was responsible for the technical direction and delivery of all security related features of Oracle's Portal/WebCenter product built on Oracle J2EE Application Server stack (Fusion Middleware).

In the recent past, I headed the BEA WebLogic Portal's Security team and designed some of the core features including WebLogic Portal's Entitlement Engine for fine-grained authorization. Prior to BEA, I worked at Persona, Inc. as a Senior Architect designing and implementing Privacy Frameworks around P3P standards.

My portfolio includes authoring a book on BEA WebLogic platform, speaking engagements in worldwide conferences like JavaOne and BEA eworld, and owning and filing several patents in the portal security, collaboration, and personalization.

Manish Devgan’s Specialties:

Enterprise portal security (Role-based Access Control and Identity Management), Collaboration software, Messaging, Role-based presence, Privacy standards, Enterprise portal services, and Enterprise architecture in general
