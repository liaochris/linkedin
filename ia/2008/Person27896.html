Brian Weis’s Summary

In early 1990's Brian began as a Recruiter and work for several staffing and IT consulting companies. Along the journey he was promoted into sales/recruiting manager roles. In the late 90's, he founded three companies, Recruiters Network, Recruiters Alliance and LocalCareers.com.

Recruiters Network, The Association for Internet Recruiting, is one of the leading recruiting portals. The Association offers a complete recruiting resource directory including an online forum, weekly newsletter, recruiting articles and tips, a directory of career sites. In addition Recruiters Network performs online studies and polls to track industry trends. http://www.recruitersnetwork.com

LocalCareers.com, Inc. comprises of several regional based career sites, including WiJobs.com, MissouriJobs.com, ArizonaJobs.com, MichiganJobs.com, TexasJobs.com, CaliforniaJobs.com, etc and industry specific sites like Jobs4HR.com, JobsinRecruiting.com and Jobs4Sales.com.

Recruiters Alliance is a network for third party Recruiters and recruiting agencies to perform splits by sharing candidates and job orders. Recruiters Alliance has over 3500 members. http://www.recruitersalliance.com

Brian's latest project is SalesResources.com, a portal site for Sales Professionals.

Brian has also been a speaker at many National Recruiting Conferences and has been a featured columnist in publications like (SHRM) Society for Human Resource Management, American Staffing Association (ASA), HR Today, HR News, and others.
