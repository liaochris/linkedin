Dario Passadore’s Summary

Passionate, international marketing executive with background in category management, strategic marketing, branding, direct marketing, PR, marketing communications, business development, and sales. Demonstrated excellent communication and leadership skills. Very successful in high-level strategic sales and in winning multi-million dollar accounts. Shown aptitude to coach, mentor and develop direct reports. Extremely comfortable in fast-pace changing environment. Proven creative skills and enthusiasm complete the profile.

Dario Passadore has more than ten years of international experience as a B2C and B2B marketing professional in the retail and both business and personal services industries. After serving as Category Manager for Carrefour Italy (supermarkets) for a few years, he decided to move to the United States to obtain an MBA from the University of Florida. As a result of a very productive internship with Viking Office Products (subsidiary of Office Depot), he then received a permanent job offer at Office Depot in the corporate marketing department, where he assumed different roles with incremental responsibilities over the years. After a short stint as Director of Strategic Marketing at Wal-Mart Stores in Bentonville, Dario eventually became the head of marketing at Mosaic Sales Solutions, the leading field sales and marketing company in North America. In February 2007, Dario founded a marketing consulting firm that has provided PR, marketing and sales solutions to clients like Strong Travel Services, a company specialized in creating luxury travel experiences for discerning clients around the globe, and HealeyGrisham, a renowned commercial photography studio that, among other things, contributes to the creation of retail catalogs. Dario presently serves on the Advisory Board of the Warrington College of Business (University of Florida) and on the Board of Directors of Craft College Inc.
