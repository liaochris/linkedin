Jeff Chang’s Summary

Provide cutting edge instrumentation for research in VLF remote sensing using DSP, FPGA, or microcontroller based data acquisition systems. We build custom systems with on board processing, on-board storage in CF or SD memories, and interfacing to low bandwidth telecommunication systems. Part of the job also requires extensive travel to deploy such systems in some of the harshest environments, including Alaska, Antarctica, and the Southern Oceans.

Jeff Chang’s Specialties:

Design, build, and testing of low cost, low power instrumentation with microcontrollers, FPGAs, and DSPs for deployments in a wide temperature and environment range. 5 years experience of field deployment of instrumentation in harsh environments, with lots of time spent digging holes in Antarctica. 8 years of production quality soldering experience. 8 years board design and layout experience.
