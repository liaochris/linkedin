Silvia Roth-Bruggers’s Summary

I have been granted Chartered Marketer by the Chartered Institute of Marketing and have extensive international working experience in Marketing and PR. Having lived and worked in Vienna, Hong Kong, London, Paris, Amsterdam and Madrid I speak five languages: German (mother tongue), fluent English, French and Spanish as well as working level of spoken Dutch.

I am looking for work in Marketing or PR, where I can use my knowledge and proven experience in strategic marketing communications to benefit an internationally operating organisation.
