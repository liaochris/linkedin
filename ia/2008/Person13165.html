M. Anis Motiwala’s Summary

As business advisor and human resources consultant, over 25 years of experience in providing HR/OD/Business strategy, change management consulting solutions at international and national level to multinationals, private sector, government and donor agencies using out of the box thinking to provide ultimate results for best practices and continuous improvement.

Have conducted numerous assignments for Prime Minsiter's Secretariat during 1994 in Pakistan. Have worked outside Pakistan, in Bangladesh on a SAARC asignment, in Ethiopia where worked on a private sector organziation and also interacted with government's advisory team on issues of Privatization and Disinvestment. In 2007 worked with CCFI in Saudi Arabia - Middle East GCC country - in the senior advisory position of management consultant. Extensively teamed with international consultants throughout the career.

I have co-teamed with various international consultant firms of repute like EuroConsult, BMB Consulting, IWACO Rotterdam, Swedforest of Sweden, Quest Pacific of Australia, MDF of the Netherlands, Growth Dynamics of the Netherlands, expatriate consultants of Andersen Worlwide SC (now Accenture).
