Combine technical knowledge and business acumen to successfully sell technology solutions to technical and non-technical people alike in large and complex customers. Experience working with partners and system integrators to compose and present solutions to customers at various levels. Ability to develop good rapport with customers and to manage clients’ expectations. Results-driven, detail oriented, and committed to teamwork and the highest levels of professionalism.
