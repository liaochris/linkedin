Hector Flores Cantu’s Summary

I started studying a bachelor in math and; at the same time, working as a teacher and in the Mathematical Olympiads in Mexico (organization, PR, test design, training, leading of teams, ... ) in Guanajuato and Nuevo Leon.
After that I moved to more concrete applications of the mathematics.
During the Masters Degree (Industrial Mathematics) and PhD, I had a scholarship and worked as a RA with mostly programming tasks related with the projects of the institute. I learned, modeling, simulation, computer graphics, GUI programming, most of the code was C++ but I have some knowledge on other languages.

I am looking for job(s) in Monterrey, Mexico. I had the long term goal of promoting the use of mathematical methods in real world problems. Any kind of applied mathematics is interesting for me and am avid to learn, understand and apply new techniques.

Currently I am "touching" the areas of Data Analysis, Data Mining and Applied Statistics. I was told those are important. They have tons of Tera-bytes of data in databases, but few people is able to extract information from it. But I am also open to explore new areas.
