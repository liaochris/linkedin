Mark Wang’s Summary

I'm a game programmer focusing on low-level rendering, tools and visual effects, currently working at Ubisoft in Shanghai, China and Annecy, France for a next-generation console title in one of Ubisoft's major franchises.

Fluent in English and Mandarin, I have a keen personal interest in the East Asia region, particularly China, Japan, and the DPRK (North Korea). To this end, I'd like to stay in East Asia for several years, getting more experience in the gaming world under my belt as well as improving my language, business, and cultural awareness.
