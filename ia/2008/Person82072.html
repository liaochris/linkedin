Jacqueline Burgoa’s Experience

  • President

    Group Benefit Agency

    (Insurance industry)

    September 1993 — Present (15 years)

    Group Benefit Agency analyzes, strategizes, sets up, services and administers group health care, employee benefits and wellness programs for organizations with 3 to 100 employees. We have successfully reduced group health insurance costs between 20% to 40%. We advocate that in order to control healthcare cost and improve corporate bottom line, organizations must control the healthcare of their employees first.

    Our business model and offerings make employees part of the decision making process, and by targeting at-risk employees we accomplish two things: 1) keep medical claims down and 2) make employees better healthcare consumers. Claims affect premium and premium affect employees when their share goes up. Therefore by making employees responsible for their healthcare utilization, businesses are giving them an incentive to become healthier.
