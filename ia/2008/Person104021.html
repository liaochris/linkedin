Thomas Kearns, CISSP [LION] TopLinked.com’s Summary

Thomas is a top performing, customer focused, information technology and security services professional with proven ability to drive solutions in technology-driven arenas across multiple industry and client segments, including Enterprise, OEM, Fortune 500, Mid Market, Government and Education. A track record of developing technical sales, marketing, business development, operations and finance groups in a multi-national team environment. Thomas serves as Principal Security Solutions Architect at Paranet Solutions in Dallas, TX. Prior to joining Paranet Thomas served as a Sr. Security Consultant at Verizon Business and Sr. Manager at MCI. He is an active member of the Dallas chapters of two professional security organizations: ISSA and ISACA. Thomas attended St. John’s University, New York where he studied Computer Science and earned a Bachelor of Science degree. Thomas is technical with excellent communications and interpersonal skills. He is a Certified VMware Sales Professional (VSP) and a Certified Information Services Security Professional (CISSP).
