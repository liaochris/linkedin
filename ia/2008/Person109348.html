  • creative collaborator / public relations

    Too Late to Run

    (Partnership; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    June 2003 — June 2005 (2 years 1 month)

    Webcomic that started in 2003 and ended abruptly in '05 due to a combination of hacker attack and loss of the domain. Has been completely offline for two years. Keep an eye out for it.

    The primary creator was Andrew Babb. I assisted with character design and influenced the story a bit, and provided the occasional filler piece. I did my best to talk up the comic around the 'net and made sure we had promo materials on hand when going to cons.

    Oddly enough, out of those of us who worked on the project, the one with the most successful webcomics career at present is our former web guy, "davean", who now works with Randall Munroe on xkcd. Who knew?
