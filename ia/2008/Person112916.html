Leonardo Xavier’s Summary

Mobile marketing is my greatest passion. It is absolutely exciting to be part of the Brazilian mobile marketing history. Over the last 5 years I´ve been involved in the creation and development of the major interactive campaigns based on mobile phones in Brazil. It is amazing what you can do with 160 characters, Bluetooth, mobile internet, voice and a little bit of creativity. On top of that, being an entrepreneur is such a marvelous challenge, especially in Brazil, and that´s what I´ve been doing since a left college 10 years ago. In 2007, I became the first (and until now, the only) Brazilian to be part of IADAS (International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences) mobile chapter, which is obviously a great honor. Since August of 2007 I´m running my new mobile marketing agency: pontomobi interactive. Since November of 2008 we became partners of RBS, the 3rd largest Brazilian media group (www.gruporbs.com.br). Take a look at what we´ve been creating at www.mobilizado.com.
