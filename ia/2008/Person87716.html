Jeffrey Murrah’s Summary

26 years of experience in the role of counselor/psychotherapist. Licensed by the state of Texas as a Licensed Professional Counselor, Marriage and Family Therapist and as a Chemical Dependency Counselor. Besides working as a counselor, I have set up utilization review and quality improvement systems in hospital and out patient settings.I also provide services compatible with an EAP model of care for dealing with short-term issues, assessment and referral.

My experience includes in-patient, residential and out-patient settings.

Jeffrey Murrah’s Specialties:

Working with couples dealing with affairs. I also have extensive experience in dealing with family and substance abuse issues from a Christian perspective. In addition I have experience as an expert witness dealing with psychological and behavioral issues.
