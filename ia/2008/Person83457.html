Jamie Bradley’s Summary

I work with creative marketers, designers and businesses to help them realize their ideas in print and produce more compelling and effective marketing materials (printing, direct mail, promo products, banners, apparel, packaging, etc).

Jamie Bradley’s Specialties:

PRINTING
- 30 years experience in graphics & printing
- Complex print projects (offset & digital)
- Print-on-demand web based ordering
- Variable Data Printing
- Direct mail & catalogs
- Large format banners & signs
- Consult on print costs of design decisions
- On-press expertise (300+ press checks)

PROMO PRODUCTS AND APPAREL
- Maintain brand identity
- Consult on products that achieve promotional goals
- Online company stores
- Integrate promo incentives with print campaigns
