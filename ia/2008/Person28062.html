Brit Furnes-Wilkens’s Summary

Project manager currently for the project: Type approval of the DATK system and for the project: Periodic control of speedometers. This includes leadership, planning, tests (velocity, timing, water break-through, climatic/temperature, on the road tests and Software and IT-security evaluation), LabVIEWprogramming, testreport and a type approval certificate.

I am insterested in the objectoriented programming languages (UML, Java, C#) and also LabVIEW.

Master thesis in Informatics, 1 year duration, at UiO (University of Oslo), :
The subject of my research was: "Automatic evaluation of measurement data", located at Justervesenet. I got experience in Measurement technique, Databases, C# programming, Statistics etc. I discussed if further development of my program can be used for authentication of electrical instruments, that are calibrated via the Internet.

First Master`s year I took in IKT, Information Security direction, at UiA (University in Agder). Here I got knowledge with principles and technology used for realization of security services based on encryption in modern ICT systems. I also chose courses in Information management and Leadership.

Software developer, experience in building telephone (voice) applications, functionality on websides, IIS. Database technology, familiar with the programming languages (VB, SQL, ASP, C++), client/server and relational database design using MS-SQL Server.

My Bachelor main project at department Semafor, at Ericsson AS, involved to develop a Protocol analyzer that analyzed the AT-commands that runs between a mobile phone and different devices. We used the programming languages Pyton, Pearl and Java.
