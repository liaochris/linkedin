William Shawn Hill’s Summary

“Pour Toujours Colline de Brooke d'Ashley”

Born and raised in Longview, Texas William (Shawn) Hill brings entrepreneurial instinct, immeasurable drive and considerable business experience to his role as founder and CEO of Hill Entertainment Group (HEG). Hill balances and directs HEG’s operating groups – which include Hill Venture, Hill Property Group, Hill Real Estate Group, Hill Consulting Firm and Hill Holdings – in all phases of acquisition, development and management.

Hill’s first entrepreneurial venture was in 1999, when he founded International Information Exchange (IIE), an ecommerce business specializing in the collection, organization and distribution of content-based Web services. Within 3 months of its founding, Hill guided IIE to 6-figure monthly income levels, and earned recognition as a leading provider and distributor of content over the Internet.

In 2001, Hill continued with his forward momentum by establishing SAAK (Shawn Ashley and Aleesa Kay) and successfully diversifying his online portfolio across a strategic grouping of hand-selected sectors. Under his watch, SAAK effortlessly secured partnerships with countless major players quickly becoming an internationally recognized brand. This hands-on experience, combined with a razor-sharp vision, provided the catalyst for his creation of Rx Holdings in 2004, parlaying his skills and growing assets into an unparalleled and diverse portfolio.

Hill most recently created Hill Entertainment Group (HEG). His singular vision and drive have attracted an experienced corporate team of executives and partners to HEG from the country’s most acclaimed brands and corporations, accepting the exciting challenge of redefining their industries for a new generation that’s already arrived.
