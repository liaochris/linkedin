Rick Darnaby’s Summary

Rick Darnaby is the Managing Partner of Darnaby & Associates, LLC. The firm provides consulting services in the areas of strategic planning, brand strategy, and strategic alliances and partnerships. The firm also owns a marketing services company with capability to provide a full line of marketing products ranging from marketing plans, creative development, and execution in all commercial media. Clients are primarily from the high tech, telecom, medical device, and health, fitness and nutrition industries.

Darnaby brings a wealth of global executive level experience from large corporations, as well as venture capital and private equity backed companies. He has built a number of large global businesses in a variety of high tech and consumer industries, while successfully advancing through the management ranks in three major corporations. Rick has a track record of developing world class strategies and executing to optimum profitability. He also has successfully recruited and developed many highly talented people many of whom have advanced to some of the top positions in corporations throughout the world.

He has served on a number of corporate boards of directors, as well as a number of government and academic boards and committees in the USA and abroad. Rick has been a guest lecturer at the top graduate business schools, including Harvard Business School and Northwestern’s Kellogg School of Business. He is a frequent speaker on a number of subjects, including transforming technology companies into market driving companies, global brand strategy, and high performance organizations.

Rick holds a B.S. and an M.B.A. from Oklahoma State University, and an Advanced Management Degree from I.N.S.E.A.D. in Fontebleau, France.

Rick Darnaby’s Specialties:

Organization Leadership, Strategic Planning, Brand Planning and Mangement, Investor Strategy, and all areas of marketing execution.
