Chris Belden’s Summary

At TravelWorm, I am responsible for the advancement of TravelWorm technology and online operating functions. In addition, I serve as the chief operating executive leading the customer sales, service and fulfillment functions for the consumer, groups and affiliates markets. My mission is to build adaptable products and teams to deliver superior innovation to the online travel and leisure reservations market.

Tapping more than a decade of experience consulting in business strategy and technology for start-up companies, as well as Fortune 500 and turnaround situations, I bring a disciplined and proven approach to delivering high revenue growth, profitability and utilization in the technology and operations functions. My passion is to drive competitive advantage and realize strategic capabilities through efficient use of organizational resources and execution of critical technology projects. Peers have recognized me for my leadership and ability to shape a strategic vision and to achieve the desired results in its implementation.

My leadership style is approachable and naturally attracts highly driven professionals who want to join and succeed on the team. I foster work environments which promote discipline, learning, continual improvement, and accountability while encouraging appropriate risk-taking, urgency and flexibility.
