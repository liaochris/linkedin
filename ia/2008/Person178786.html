Annmarie Edwards’s Summary

Workforce Trainer with extensive knowledge on staff development and performance assessment. An innovative thinker and performer who utilizes an experiential approach in training people.

Curriculum designer who creates materials not just to reinforce a seminar or a workshop but to create a deep sense of understanding and emotional ownership.

See current Guides:
http://www.work.com/energize-your-employee-workforce-14722/
http://www.work.com/finding-your-dream-job-14696/

Interactive exercises make up 80 percent of the presentation time- and this means the seminar content stays with attendees. Additionally, workshops topics vary from team building, energize your workforce, improve peak performance, journaling, how to create a workforce blueprint for success, what color are you?, and much more. Presenter customized topics for clients on a need basis.

Certified International Job and Career Development Coach focused on providing businesses and individual coaching services. Specializes in motivating staff, product development, case study, customer service, branding, and workforce training. Interested in consulting businesses on ways to energize their workforce and build their bottom line.

I have written the following tips booklets:
1. 50 Ways to Energize Your Workforce
2. 50 Ways to Maximize Your Potential
3. 50 Proven Strategies to Maximize Your Job Search.

These booklets are resources to help businesses in their promotion. For example, businesses can give these booklets to their clients, employees, or use in their mailing campaign or use it as a networking tool. The possibilities are endless.

I have written Intuitive Creative Journal and work guides.
