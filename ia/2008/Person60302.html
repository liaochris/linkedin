Eric van de Wetering’s Summary

Eric is Sr. Art Director and owner of Reclamebureau Circuit.

Reclamebureau Circuit is a compact full service ad-agency build on four pillars: ‘creative, when it comes to design’, ‘strategic, when it comes to communication’, ‘sincere, when it comes to collaborating’ en ‘coördinating, when it comes to implementing’. We are generalists in communication, building on brands and reputations in both a strategic and a creative manner. In close collaboration with our clients we direct the efforts of many communication-specialists and ensure a perfect result.
