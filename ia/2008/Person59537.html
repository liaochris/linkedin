Erica Pierantozzi’s Summary

Erica is a graduate from the University of Portland with a BBA in Marketing. During her studies, she was an E-Scholar at the Center for Entrepreneurship. As an E-Scholar, Erica traveled to several companies domestically and abroad including Wal-Mart and Bon Appetit to learn more about their business model. After graduation, Erica spent a year traveling in the South Pacific. She has a love for traveling, experiencing new things and learning new cultures. Her time in the S. Pacific was spent working at various jobs including picking apples and scuba diving to fund her travels. The experience was life changing.

Erica is very passionate about entrepreneurship and marketing. She has knowledge and experience in the areas of Business Development, Marketing, Human Resources and Distribution within the mobile, banking and retail industries. She has proven the ability of conceiving an idea and growing it into a success. Her drive enables her to be a strong team player that leads projects and creates value. Erica thrives on being in a competitively intellectual environment and welcomes challenges.

Erica has successfully recognized opportunities that have improved programs for employees and customers. She has gained knowledge in several areas and learned the skills to demonstrate within the business profession. Her self-motivation and direction would be an asset to any organization.

For fun, you will find Erica driving long distances for snow. She enjoys spending her time on the mountain, snowboarding.
