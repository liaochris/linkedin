Michael Baker’s Summary

Michael Baker is the Owner/President of Move To Action, a company dedicated to creating success for business owners. He believes that the first steo to success is a willingness to "Move To Action"! Michael’s training and coaching is designed for sales and business professionals who desire to concentrate their focus, energy and efforts on high-return actions that drive success and accomplishment.

Michael has over 25 years of sales and marketing experience. As the Director of Sales for COL with the largest computer training company in the world, he was responsible for providing the training, support and leadership for twenty-six locations that generated over $139 million in revenue.

Michael has conducted 100’s of training seminars and presentations on many sales and leadership topics. He has received national recognition on many occasions for record breaking sales and exemplary management performance. Michael is a very dedicated coaching professional and a highly motivated speaker with excellent communication skills who is passionate in helping people achieve success and happiness.
