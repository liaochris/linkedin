Avi Benamu’s Summary

Experience raising assets and managing client relations for hedge funds and private equity offerings. Hedge fund consulting on structure, marketing materials and operations.

Currently setting up and launching offshore asset backed hedge fund in Cayman Islands. We underwrite and fund hard money loans and syndicate commercial real estate in the US.

Have originated short to medium term bridge loans secured by a wide variety of real estate asset types including development property, commercial & multi-family properties.

Developed fund fact sheets & brochures and generated content for numerous client and manager communications, including websites, newsletters, e-mails, etc.

Completed and produced RFPs for Institutional investors and consultants, developing the responses, writing and logistical coordination of proposals, managing the proposal process and timeline to meet prospect-driven deadlines, as well as tracking the effectiveness of the proposals through the sales process.

I have led initiatives and campaigns to better inform and service investor base with respect to Private Investors products including the development of a proprietary software to effectively run all aspects of the Fund of Hedge fund business.

Have presented private equity offerings to potential clients and conducted client and prospect seminars, including board presentations, seminars, forums and conferences. Delivered clear presentations to the targeted audience and enhanced relationships with investors focusing on products, process, strategy, performance and outlook. Organized all aspects of conferences including content, catering, promotional products and follow-ups;

Have assisted Financial Advisors in developing appropriate asset allocation, determining risk tolerance, setting realistic return and growth expectations, negotiating fees, closing business and due diligence process;
