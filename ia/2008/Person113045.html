Leslie Goossens’s Summary

My name is Leslie Goossens and I have a background in IT.
I founded AOS in 2002 with a single purpose -
to provide high quality, practical and totally independent advice and guidance on the best client PSO project solutions. Where PSO stands for linking Persons, Systems and Organisation
Based on best practices projectsolutions for government and healthcare and business to these markets.
Our expertise in managing documents, information, communication and collaboration within your context is uncompared.
We drive innovative, accountable, measurable projects.
Our value proposition is : A, in your interests, managed and communicated project.
For a maximum 20% budget, a 40% lower investmentcost at minimum 80% of the requirements.
Our purpose is becoming Belgium's leading specialist Project advisory firm.
Today our team works in more than 10 client engagements in over more than 10 FPS and healthcare institutions in Belgium, amongst
them FPS FedICT, Chancellerie of the Prime Minister, UZ Sint-Jan...
We have unrivalled experience in achieving succesful results. and we want to work with you !

My ambition as project manager is also going into social projects where my technological and projectmanagement knowhow is an added value in support of the project ... anyway something with and for kids ...
However today it makes my day if our projects get done with a great team and to the satisfaction of our loyal customers, sometimes sad if there's no time to enjoy the results ... I'm also always struggling to find a balance between my broad interests, work to be done, my family, socializing, reading and many much more ;-)
One day I'll find that balance !

AOS, AllOpenSolutions, levert onafhankelijke advisering, voorbereiding en begeleiding van projecten voor kenniswerkers met informatie, documenten en samenwerking.
