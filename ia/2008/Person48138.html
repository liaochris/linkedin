Helping online trading losers (dissatisfied, not profitable traders) become consistently profitable winners - so they too can earn thousands a day – day trading stocks online.

There are 4 Types of Losers:
• Winners - hopeful, but not yet profitable
• Bored traders - break even results, at best
• Losers - "blowing out" trading account
• Repeat Losers - crash (emotionally) and burn (financially) - over and over again

Then, of course, there are want-to-be traders, with or without adequate risk capital to trades stocks, who must put up with whatever they earn on their current job, career, or business – unable to make the leap to trading stocks, for whatever reasons. Their loss? – major opportunity income as potential stock traders.
