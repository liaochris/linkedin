Scott Dunlop (scott@biviumgroup.com)’s Summary

Managing Partner (Recruiter/Headhunter) in Boston Software Engineering R&D looking to continue to establish and build strong, long-term relationships with exceptional clients and talent in New England.

When you demand experience, access and results for yourself or your organization, I'd love to chat - scott@biviumgroup.com

===

Specialize in Software Engineering R&D and SQA recruiting - emphasis (70%) on Private/VC backed startups as well as major (30%) Public company recruiting.

Both contingency and retained search models for recruiters.

The most important aspect of what we do is building long-term relationships that focus on RESULTS. We match our clients' workload with weekends, late night and a "do what it takes" attitude to reach our shared goals.

We specialize in recruiting for software engineers - from recent grads to VP/CTO levels. All flavors of technical backgrounds C, C++, Java, J2EE, VC++, C#, .NET - Associate Software Engineers to Principal/Lead Software Engineers.

===

The Bivium Group is comprised of some of the most talented and successful individuals in the recruiting industry. Our goal is to provide retained level service in a contingency-based model. We strive to offer the best recruiting experience available today. Our veteran team has founded and worked for both emerging technology companies as well as some of the leading national and regional recruiting firms in the United States.
