Gonny van der Zwaag’s Summary

Worked in the the consultancy and Finance industry for over eight years, which allowed me to gain a lot of experience in ICT and project management. After many happy years at CMG I decided to move to a position which would bring me even closer to cutting edge technology: be at the front row at product introductions, be the first to try new hardware and software - and be able to write about it, both for magazines and online media. For almost 4 years I worked for Personal Computer Magazine (PCM), but still kept working freelance as a co-owner of Textopus. Our company works for a steadily expanding number of clients and business partners, including PCM, Webwereld, Computer Idee, Infoworld, Pearson Education, Pocketinfo, Emerce, Home Entertaintment Today, PC Consument, Mac Fan, Van Duuren Media, Wireless Computing, Gizmodo, Sprout, Coolblue and others. Our current focus is managing an iPhone related website, called iPhoneclub.nl.

Gonny van der Zwaag’s Specialties:

Mobile technology, including iPhone, Windows Mobile, PalmOS, and Symbian. Desktop platforms like Mac OS X, Windows Vista, and Windows XP. Internet culture; GPS and navigation technology; knowledge management and innovation; coaching, project management.
