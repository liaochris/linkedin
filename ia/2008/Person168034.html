Sam Meyer’s Summary

18 years in video production, 9 years editing network news video. Familiarity with the blogosphere, with New York City, and truth be told, a host of less-useful information.

Sam Meyer’s Specialties:

Tape and non-linear (Avid NewsCutter, Pinnacle liquid.Blue, Final Cut Pro, Velocity NX) video editing, attention to detail while not losing sight of the big picture, visual storytelling, a healthy curiosity about the world.
