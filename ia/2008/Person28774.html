Bryan Rasch’s Summary

Accomplished senior marketing leader and digital marketing strategist, with a strong portfolio of successes contributing to my employers’ and client’s brand equity, revenues and profitability. Over 15 years of experience framing and leading 100’s of innovative branding, marketing and technology programs for major consumer brands including VW, Nike, Burton Snowboards, Trek Bicycles and Old Town Canoe. Proven track record leading strategic planning processes that drive the development of traditional, word-of-mouth, social media and promotional marketing programs engaging users with brands to increase revenue. Extensive experience successfully launching new and improving the sell-thru of existing products within major distribution channels including; independent dealer networks, sporting goods and big box retailers and direct-to-consumer catalogs and eCommerce.

Keen ability to take complex problems and distill them into clearly articulated strategies that teams can galvanize around – key expertise in visualizing, designing, planning and executing strategies that impact business processes and marketing programs alike

Extensive track-record planning, building, recruiting and managing, talented, cross-functional teams to deliver new services and disciplines within a fast growing environment.

Strong team leadership talents with the ability to successfully manage and drive complex programs requiring the orchestration of large teams (20+) on tight deadlines

Accomplished public speaker with a dynamic presentation and public speaking style that builds credibility and consensus with C-level executives, management teams, staff and conference attendees. Conference speaking engagements have consistently been ranked >4.5 out of five for content, knowledge gained and presentation style
