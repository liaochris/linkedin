Scott Klein’s Summary

I am a very detailed-oriented, analytical individual with a strong functional and technical Human Resources and Payroll background.

Have over 18 years of experience designing and implementing HRMS systems on a variety of platforms and operating systems and has been involved in full project lifecycle implementations of Human Resource, Payroll, Self-Service Human Resources (SSHR), Time and Labor, Benefits Administration and Recruitment applications for domestic and international companies. Have strong leadership abilities, excellent communication skills, and have led projects serving in both functional and technical roles.

I enjoy developing solutions to new and interesting global and domestic business challenges, and have a very strong understanding of the development of custom localizations. Widely considered a guru in the development and deployment of Oracle Payroll (multiple localizations) and FastFormulas. Also have considerable experience with the existing global versions (localizations) of the HRMS product family, enjoy international travel and always maintain excellent working relationships with co-workers and clients.

I've also led Oracle University classes for clients, partners and Oracle Consulting, in addition to teaching Oracle HRMS Bootcamp for new consultants.

Prior implementation experience included PeopleSoft HRMS, MSA (now GEAC), and NAI (now Pilat) Succession Planning applications.

Scott Klein’s Specialties:

International Configurations, Implementations and Deployments, Fast Formulas, Global HR, Global Payroll, HRMS/HCM Solution Architecture. Have worked with in the following countries: Australia, Canada, Cayman Islands, New Zealand, Norway, Puerto Rico, Trinidad & Tobago, and the United States.
