Lee Rodrigues, M.A.Ed.’s Summary

So what the heck is a "Technology Coach?"

If you are an entrepreneur, run a small business, or are an individual who uses technology for communications, operations or marketing, it can be frustrating when you don't know how to use your tools. You are probably frustrated by trying to get the various devices and programs in your life to work together, and you suspect that you could be doing things much, much better. But how?

You find a nerd-for-hire like me. I will sit with you to see what you're doing right now, and to find out what you wish you could be doing better. Sometimes I teach people how to use what they already have. Sometimes I turn them on to free and low-cost solutions they never heard of. And other times I help guide my clients through a comprehensive IT planning process.

While every situation is unique, I find that most of the solutions I recommend fall into three categories:

1. Use Better Tools

• Get support in evaluating new software purchases
• Set up an automatic, off-site backup system for critical data
• Learn how to successfully transition from Windows to the Mac OS
• Get help in identifying your goals and creating a technology plan

2. Streamline Communications

• Connect and manage multiple email accounts
• Set up remote access to email and documents
• Build auto-responders and organize web leads

3. Build Your Web Presence

• Learn how to build and maintain your blog
• Strategize the creation of a professional and flexible new web site
• Learn how to use social networking to increase awareness and generate leads

Get a free phone evaluation! Most of my new clients say they got a brilliant new idea after our very first conversation. If you like what I have to offer, I provide coaching and consulting service by the hour, with discounts for projects and monthly retainer. If you're not interested, then the good ideas are free.
