Kees Koek’s Summary

Fluent in Dutch, English, French and German and with a good working knowledge of Italian and Spanish, I can communicate with most people on this planet. With my engineering background, my commercial experience, my creativity and my communication skills I can produce and/or edit texts, have them illustrated with clear drawings and/or communicative photographs to convince prospects of the quality of technical products.

Kees Koek’s Specialties:
