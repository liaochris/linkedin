Brett Meyers’s Summary

Zloop is a web-based tool that facilitates, captures and organizes interaction between individuals and members of a community. Zloop combines the unlimited scalability and dynamic interaction capabilities of online community systems with the ease, convenience and privacy of email.

Left OptOR Systems in May of 2007 to work towards this end with the Zloop team. Formerly worked at OptOR Systems assisting in product development and overseeing both marketing/sales and product implementation. Began work at OptOR Systems in December of 2005. Prior to joining OptOR I worked for the ASC management and development firm Medbridge Development. From my time at Medbridge I was able to bring extensive ASC operations experience to my position at OptOR.
