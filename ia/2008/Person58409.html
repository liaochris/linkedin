Eric Martin’s Summary

I am the Manager of Sales & Marketing Recruiting with EPS Portland, a boutique professional search firm specializing in mid to senior level direct hire recruiting based in Portland, OR. Approximately 50% of my personal production is outside the Portland Metropolitan area so I have a sincere interest in networking with tier one sales, marketing, and operations personnel not only in Portland, OR but throughout the United States.

My search clients typically have openings ranging from Outside Sales/Account Management to VP/Director level roles. Over the past two and a half years I have been fortunate enough to help over 50 good people find good jobs with great companies and am looking forward to doubling that production over the next 24 months. So if you are the best person you know at what you do and interested in staying abreast of potentially stronger opportunities or simply just looking to find out if you have a competitive compensation package, etc. you owe it to yourself to have a confidential conversation with me about opportunities that may exist for you now or in your future.

I am also interested in bringing on new clients. If you have a need in the areas of Sales, Marketing, or Operations and are looking to bring on someone with a demonstrated, proven background it’s worth your time to have an exploratory conversation with me about how I may be able to act as a resource for you as well.

Please feel free to invite me to join your network.

My personal background includes:
11 years strategic sales experience, 8 years sales management, 6 years C-level selling/networking experience, 4 years executive level management, 3 years sales/marketing/operations professional search recruiting experience...
