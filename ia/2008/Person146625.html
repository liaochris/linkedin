Patrick Murphy’s Summary

Employed by Lawrence Associates, an IT Consulting firm and Preferred Microsoft Vendor, since 2005.I serve as Principal of the Seattle practice group. Our core clients are Microsoft, AT&T, GMI, Scholastic, Fidelity (FNIS), and other Enterprise companies in the Technology sector. We also consult with Executive teams of emerging firms that offer the potential for long term relationships. Our competencies center around developing marketing and sales strategy, and defining and communicating the business value of technology products and services. Related services include Technology Investment Justification studies, Sales Management Effectiveness Assessments, Economic impact White Papers and Case Studies, and Business Value Selling Training development.

I have co-founded two technology services companies and served as Partner/Executive of four. As Sr. Vice President Sales & Business Development, was key member of Executive Team that led the explosive sales and organizational growth of N2H2, Inc. through its IPO. As co-founder of Mosaix Communications, conceived, designed and executed on building new business concept from the proverbial “napkin plan” to fully operational post-revenue phase. As a CEO, and VP/Director for other early-stage ventures, leveraged ability to sell market-disruptive business models to successfully build companies and grew revenues, while leading teams in very competitive, resource-challenged environments. Have developed or re-engineered multiple direct and channel distribution programs to successfully introduce and expand sales of innovative products and services.

My goals for the future include building the Lawrence Team and client base in the Northwest, and growing our core business lines. I am always looking for talented people to either join our team or work as Contract Associate Consultants.
