Tawny Press’s Summary

25 years of broad industry experience, I have been privileged to experience Real World Solutions, in a variety of interesting and diverse organizations. I have worked with some of the most creative management and mentors in the medical device, manufacturing and technology industries. This diverse group of people and industry leaders has one common thread, education of associates, management, partners and customers.

In 2005, I became a partner of Innoventions, Inc., with a goal to create Rich Multimedia and Interactive simulations, to spark imagination and deepen understanding, essential to continuing education in organization development.

Our team design programs, to align with clients strategic business objectives, with solutions that not only educate, but engage and entertain.

Tawny Press’s Specialties:

Web 2.0, LMS, LCMS, Social Media, Streaming Video, Flash CS3 / Actionscript 2.0, Web Design and Development, CSS, Scorm 2.0, AICC, XML, HTML, PHP, storyboarding, procurement, administration and project management.
