Marcus Riley - Chartered Fellow CIPD’s Summary

With an early background in the British Army, Marcus served 6 years with the Elite Parachute Regiment before embarking on a successful career in IT recruitment in 1995.

Working for Industry leading staffing companies such as Best People, Adecco and Randstad. Partnering with leading Blue Chip companies such as Invesco, Merrill Lynch and Citibank. Working on prestigous RPO accounts such as IBM, McAfee, SAS, Xansa, SAP and most recently Rackspace.

Having joined Profiles in late 2004, Marcus delivered unparalleled service to the McAfee account across EMEA, which saw him end the year (2005) as Profiles Top Achiever, winning 9 months Top Performer out of 12 (6 months in a row) and breaking all previous hiring records set before him at McAfee and still retaining an outstanding level of service against the SLA. Subsequently winning the award again at the end of 2006.

Continuing his work within in-house/RPO recruitment, Marcus is currently freelancing as an EMEA recruiter. Having worked for SAP & currently with Rackspace.

Operating at both strategic and operational levels, Marcus is comfortable building long term, mutually beneficial relationships from Senior Management down to Operational Managers, providing expert advice in the areas of recruitment, assessment & selection.

Marcus also attained Chartered Fellow of the CIPD in 2006.
