Stuart Edeal’s Summary

An IT Professional of over 10 years with an emphasis on quality and clarity. By continually conduct research and practice in a wide range of topics, Stuart stays on top of current and upcoming technologies. Stuart is eager to work with new innovations in technology and ideas for helping people use them efficiently.

As a Technical Writer, Stuart develops and designs instructional and informational tools needed to assure safe, appropriate and effective use of science and technology, intellectual property, and manufactured products and services. He combines multimedia knowledge and strong communication skills with technical expertise to educate across the entire spectrum of users' abilities, technical experience, and visual and auditory capabilities.

As a Freelance Technology Consultant, Stuart brings his entrepreneurial spirit to play with his love for technology. He has helped clients with a wide variety of projects, helping users to better understand how to use tools and technology - such as computers, servers, software, the Internet, media, and electronics - to enhance and strengthen their business processes. Stuart enjoys new challenges, and teaching people to use technology better. He enjoys 'getting things done' and being there to help clear fear, uncertainty and doubt.
