Aaron Freed’s Summary

My professional goal is to eventually develop a quantitative model for a global macro fund that effectively uses options to minimize monthly fund return volatility while seeking out opportunistic developments both short- and long-term in the global macro landscape. I plan to market my model through a blog or newsletter platform to expose its ability to generate profitable ideas to both the institutional and non-institutional investment public as it pertains to their needs, which would translate into managed accounts and/or direct fund investment.

My professional experiences over the last 5 years have gained me exposure to numerous disciplines. As an option trader, I gained expertise to trade large-sized blocks of option spreads on listed index products. As a risk analyst, I was exposed to numerous ways to analyze risk exposure through attribution, simulations, and what-if scenarios using sophisticated quantitative techniques in conjunction with robust technological resources. Additionally as an analytics specialist, I acquired experience creating complex spreadsheet models that tap into real-time data from Bloomberg, Reuters, and other similar products in a way that reshapes the data for informed decision makers. Finally through a start-up venture, I learned the ins and outs of what is involved in managing a hedge fund from the ground floor from every aspect imaginable.
