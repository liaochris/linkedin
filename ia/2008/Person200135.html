William Sears’s Summary

I am a multidisciplinary visionary entrepreneur with strengths that include a great ability to dream up product strategy, manage and lead teams, a deep understanding of technology, business acumen, and a natural knack for marketing and business development. I have direct experience in many facets of the Internet space, having led teams at a systems integrator, an e-commerce web applications developer, a web design firm, an Internet service provider, a search engine, and now a cutting-edge search engine marketing and content monetization business. Twice in my career I have personally managed projects that have been spun out to form new companies. I pride myself on creating best-of-class technology solutions and leading teams of rising stars.
