George Bounacos’s Summary

OVERVIEW

Online content and information services leader who successfully creates and delivers profitable activities in B2B, B2C and B2G. I hold PPC certifications from Yahoo and Google and lead on-page SEO for more than 40 domains.

SKILL SETS

A technologist with online content, SEO/SEM , software and design/usability expertise, I can drive a page's rankings for keyword phrases, create high conversion rates and attract unique, link-worthy content. Proficient in web authoring tools, analytics software, I can write some of the coolest Excel macros this side of your favorite Excel guru, but don't confuse me with a coder. They're the real wizards!

A colleague summarized my skills this way, "You talk marketing to geeks and geek to marketers."

WORK HISTORY

The ultimate online generalist, I lead search marketing for an IYP platform, continue leading consumer advocacy efforts and manage a large content network I co-founded in 2003. I have also managed B2B help desks, cross-functionally teamed with Marketing at a top auto web destination, led a segment of an online community for an eBay affiliate and was first paid for generating online content in 1986!

Creating compelling online content accessed at 300 baud while customers paid $48/hour for access may be one of life's great online marketing challenges.

While leading service quality and operations teams at well known brands, I also participated in multiple ERP and CRM installations.

INDUSTRY EXPERIENCE

Information services, ecommerce, SEO/SEM (with a sub-specialty in PPC) alternative dispute resolution, online marketing, writing and editing for online audiences, automotive, publishing, travel agency, logistics and training.

THE FUTURE

Today's medium is the web. Constantly looking at the macro environment, I'll be there for tomorrow's medium too.

A "selective" open networker, between 5%-10% of my contacts at any given time have also provided a written reference.
