Brian D. Peterson, MA, MS, PMP’s Summary

Mr. Peterson is a business executive, visionary, and thought leader with strong conceptual and process management skills as well as the ability to balance strategic drivers with tactical activities while achieving results. He is an energetic leader with a passion for driving business value via strategic initiatives. He understands how to align business goals and objectives with Information technology enablers. Mr. Peterson has 25 years of experience, which includes building national consulting practices (e.g., at E&Y), functioning as a business manager (VP for a Midwest consulting firm), management consultant, technology advisor and project / PMO manager across complex heterogeneous environments and several industries (including Healthcare, Insurance, Finance, Manufacturing, Retail, Education, Non-Profit, Government, and Telecommunications). Mr. Peterson holds two Masters degrees is PMP certified.
