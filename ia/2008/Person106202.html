Kevin Feenan’s Summary

Mr. Feenan is a Project Management Certified (PMP) Project Manager and Business analyst with over 18 years in the IT sector, where he has worked in both the private and public sectors. His core areas of expertise include Project Management, Business transformations and re-engineering, Business Analysis, and Information technology software design and development.

Amongst his achievements he was active in the development of a national project control office handling 1500 projects per year. He increased billable workload by $10M through coordination of internal realignments and identified and corrected $8M in undiscovered invoicing and account payable errors. He is also talented at shortening project life cycles through process improvement initiatives. Mr. Feenan has also been involved in the development of ISO standards and procedures for a network infrastructure business unit.

Most recently Mr. Feenan has started a new private career college called the Rockcliffe School of Business. In 6 months he has provided the direction and leadership to grow the school to approximately 1350 students and 20 faculty by focusing efforts in virtual education methods specifically in an environment called Second Life.
