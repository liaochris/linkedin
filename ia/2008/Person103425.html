Katie Konrath’s Summary

Right now, I'm marketing manager at a very interesting eco-start-up called ecoEnvelopes. We design and license reusable 2-way envelopes that totally eliminate a reply envelope. So, companies save the environment... and they get to save money at the same time.

Hard to believe, but ecoEnvelopes actually make envelopes cool!

My blog, www.getfreshminds.com, is a tribute to my on-going love with creativity, innovation and fresh ideas.

When I was 11 years old, I fell completely head over heels for creativity. I was in a program called Odyssey of the Mind and my team had to create a short performance combining a scene from an ancient Greek myth with a modern day event. I performed as Aphrodite--who took advantage of the moment to wrap her fingers tightly around my heart. From that moment on, I was hooked!

For the next 8 years, I competed in various creative problem solving competitions, and was thrilled each time I traveled to the Global Finals.

Recently I completed instructor certification courses in Edward de Bono's Lateral Thinking method and I just finished a Master's course in Creativity and Innovation from the University of Malta's Institute for the Design and Development of Thinking.

In the fall of 2006, I worked as a traveling assistant for creativity guru Edward de Bono and enjoyed the opportunity to learn as much as possible from someone who has helped so many people become more creative.

I love the challenge of thinking of new ideas to help companies create a powerful connection with their customers, and guiding others through the creativity process.
