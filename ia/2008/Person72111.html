John Edmonds’s Summary

A creative marketing resource. Uniquely balanced in both creative and strategic skills, I help growing businesses firmly define their products/services as solid brands, and back it up with great ads that will move the needle.
I also offer a refined 'pitch' service, helping my clients find favour - and win business - with pitching their own prospective (and existing) clients.

I've been the creative lead on a vast array of national, global, and award winning advertising campaigns from packaged goods (Kellogg's, Procter & Gamble, Cadbury's, Pillsbury, etc) to services (Bell Canada, Manitoba Telecom, Business Objects, BC Tourism, etc). And almost everything in between.
I've led the evolution of existing major brands, as well as developing others from the ground up.

John Edmonds’s Specialties:

Creative leadership in branding and identity, including advertising concepting, pitching, design, and writing. And helping find the obvious when it seems elusive.
