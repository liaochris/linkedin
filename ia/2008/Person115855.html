Louis Riley’s Summary

Over 21 years of proven success advising clients, raising capital, and managing money.

Nine years as a stockbroker in Beverly Hills and Houston with top firms that included Merrill Lynch, Smith Barney, Oppenheimer, and Bear Stearns. Peak annual production of over $440,000, peak annual AUM growth of 114%, managed seven direct reports.

Founded and managed my own Registered Investment Advisor, as an affiliate of AIG, to deliver fee-based investment advice to qualified high-net-worth clients. Managed discretionary equity-only separate accounts. Exceeded Russell 2000 benchmark by 5100 bps in peak 12-month period. Attained peak 12-month return of 74% and a peak monthly return of 33%.

Founded and managed small cap aggressive growth hedge fund. Served as an investment adviser and co-manager to RBC Capital Markets' proprietary hedge fund.

In March 2008, as a consultant to a hedge fund, led a capital raising campaign that culminated with a presentation to Funds of Funds controlling over $200 billion at RBC Capital Markets' Capital Introduction Event at Christie's Auction House in NYC.

Currently employed as a National Sales Business Development Officer for DRDA, P.C. We are a CPA firm that specializes in advising businesses and entrepreneurs. We help individuals fund the start up or purchase of businesses with equity they have available in their qualified plans (IRAs, 401(k)s, etc.) by creating a legal structure that allows them to rollover said funds into a new 401(k) plan that can then invest in their new business.
