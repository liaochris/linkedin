Joseph Hunkins’s Summary

Professional Experience 1990-2007.

Coordinated Internet marketing and multiple website development for the Southern Oregon Visitors Association, a large regional tourism promotion group covering seven counties of Oregon and over 14,000 square miles. Developed organic and pay per click search strategies for several websites.

Developed the first commercial websites for Crater Lake National Park and Oregon Caves National Monument.

Managed grant and deployment of statewide touch kiosk Internet system for the state of Oregon, a partnership with the Federal Scenic Byways Program, Oregon Department of Transportation, State Tourism, and the Southern Oregon Visitors Association.

Presented findings of one of the first online Internet conversion studies at the Travel Industry Association of America’s (TIA) national education conference in Vail, Colorado. Conducted many Oregon presentations and workshops about internet marketing strategies for the travel sector.

Past board member of the California Oregon Intelligent Transportation Systems project. Helped review policies and deployments of travel technology (such as road web cams) for the Northern California and Southern Oregon regions.

Winner of 1997 Oregon’s Governor’s Award for Innovative Tourism Development for creating the partnership and grant that led to Oregon’s first state travel website “TravelOregon.com”.

Owner: USA3.com. Publish information at several regional and national websites featuring airport and travel information.

Partner: Online Highways LLC. This Florence, Oregon internet publishing company works in conjunction with two of the Pacific Northwest’s leading travel magazines “Northwest Travel” and “Oregon Coast Magazine” to produce one of the most comprehensive online travel sites in the world: “Online Highways”
www.OHWY.com.

Blogs as "Joe Duck" about travel and technology.

Member of Techdirt's 'Insight Community"

Professional goal: Happiness and prosperity for all.
