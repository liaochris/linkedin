Janice King’s Summary

A writer who can talk to your engineers and connect with your customers

As a marketing or PR professional, you know how hard it can be to find a copywriter who communicates effectively with your engineers . . . and your customers . . . especially for highly technical subject matter.

It takes a writer who produces clear, compelling, knowledgeable content in a variety of media and styles. Writing that sells your products. Supports your public relations. Educates your markets.

Janice King is a freelance copywriter with a rare focus and depth of experience in technology. A writer who knows the acronyms and buzzwords -- and what they mean to real customers with real problems.

A writer who can work effectively with your in-house staff, designers, and other creative resources. A writer who offers the industry savvy that improves the focus and impact of your materials -- for marketing and sales, customer retention, and public relations.

Janice King is also author of the groundbreaking book Copywriting That Sells High Tech (WriteSpark Press, 2006).

Janice King’s Specialties:

Advanced technologies for enterprise markets. Special expertise in data/voice networking, call centers/ACD, wireless, voice messaging systems, network and systems management, test and measurement. Additional experience in enterprise computer systems, consulting services, semiconductors, and databases.

Example Clients: Cisco Systems, Sprint, Hewlett-Packard/HP, Nokia, Texas Instruments, SBC/Ameritech, Philips Medical Systems, ADC/Kentrox, Nortel Networks, Spirent Communications, and more.
