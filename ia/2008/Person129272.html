Michael Albert’s Summary

Concept -- Implement -- Profit

My mission is to increase profitable revenue to a food, CPG or service driven firm by means of creative & cost-effective marketing planning & business development implementation. I am best suited for a position that calls for both strategic thinking at 1,000 feet and sleeves-rolled-up implementation at sea-level, conducted in the spirit of open and supportive communication.

My career highlights include:
- Market Expansion: Extended regional boutique food company to national retail & foodservice distribution and sales securing major accounts, e.g., UNFI, SunOpta, Costco, Wegmans, Central Markets, Melt Gelato, Juice It Up! & Robeks; launched new Belizza product line - #2 in its category. Privately labeled products for national brands.
- Marketing Management: Created $75M in revenues growing start-up casino.
New Business Development: Closed $40M+ in agency billings with key clients ADP, AT&T, Bank of America, TJ Maxx, Pitney Bowes, DEC and Taco Bell.
- Start-up Operations: Successfully launched world's first Web-based free communications service to realize planned exit strategy via acquisition two years ahead of schedule.
- Strategic Planning: Conceived core value proposition to create first mobile cosmetic medical clinic.
- Multicultural/Special Events: Produced and staged 200+ special events including concerts, sporting events, beauty pageants, trade shows, etc. for mainstream and multicultural audiences.
- Industry Leadership: Recent president, American Marketing Association, Southern California.
