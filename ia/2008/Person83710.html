Jeffrey Anbinder’s Summary

I have just started as an associate in the Labor Practice of a well-respected mid-size law firm based on Long Island; I am in their Manhattan office.

Formerly, I was a contract attorney, working for a variety of firms through agencies in support of major ongoing litigation (mostly corporate). Prior to that I was an associate with a mid-size law firm specializing in medical malpractice defense and other forms of insurance defense, where I learned a great deal about litigation from highly qualified and experienced colleagues. I am admitted to practice in New York State.

My experience during law school was primarily public interest, including a year with the Innocence Project and a summer internship with a federal district court judge. Prior to law school, I was a professional fundraiser for two Ivy League universities.
