Michael J Roach’s Summary

Currently working on a number of small business projects and with tertiary organisations contributing technical knowledge to IT and Web implementation projects.

My professional goals will ultimately include work in the IT industry and online content companies aligned with my personal values and professional interests. A self-directed and agile learner, I have a clear concept and vision of my professional pursuits, and I consciously modify my own behaviors to advance my own knowledge and expertise. I am able to set priorities and achievable goals, and manage external demands and requirements with professionalism, while thriving on well developed interpersonal skills and collaborative environments.

From 2005 to 2008, I have been an actively involved in humanitarian and trade organisations. I have mentored my peers, given inservice presentations, and worked collaboratively with my team to design and implement strategic plans for advocacy action and reaching an audience. I see myself as a change agent who is able to influence others in a positive way, and to support the development of other people's skills and understandings.

With several feature articles published on Web 2.0 technologies and emerging internet access for our global neighbours I have an interest in on the ground deployment of new technologies.

I am keenly interested in continuing to cross-fertilize my skills where they merge compatible aspects of the same field: design, implementing, adoption, and diffusion of technologies.
