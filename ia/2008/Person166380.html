Ryan Bennett’s Summary

I am a Director of Software Development, Systems Architect and Developer, Project Manager and Business Analyst supporting a wide range of business models and applications. My development expertise is focused on Microsoft software technologies, including .NET, ASP.NET, and SQL Server. I am often working on non-traditional projects that involve high degrees of creativity, learning new skills and techniques quickly, using agile software development techniques to stay on time and under budget.

In the short term, I am focused on pointing LocumTenens.com in the direction of a more efficient, organized, and tightly-integrated company. My immediate challenge is to find better, streamlined methods for managing large amounts of data and simplifying the day-to-day lives of the individuals on the front lines of our business. Our goal is to increase productivity along with growing the various channels through which we conduct business. Identifying bottlenecks and using agile methods for deploying useful functionality to resolve them in a timely manner is our team's gameplan for success.

Long term, I would like to do something entrepreneurial with my skill set and the business knowledge I have acquired through my work experience.
