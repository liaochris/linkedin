Courtney Robinson Feider’s Summary

The mantra of Adrian + Sabine Creative Services is "matching business and creativity". We provide contract marketing and design services for micro, small, and medium businesses creating an innovative plan designed specifically to meet their needs.

Functioning as a virtual marketing department, our services merge traditional advertising, branding, and awareness marketing with new media and interactive solutions while maintaining dedication to timeline and budget. We stay focused on business needs - specifically the importance of brand recognition and ROI. We are especially good at interactive solutions and working with new media.

Hiring A+S is a cost-effective solution for businesses who are concerned with the changing economy. Our pricing model and plans are flexible, catering to the needs of our clients while working with senior level talent and strategy. With A+S you get the best of both worlds - talent and value.

Some of our services:

* Three C's Marketing: (Custom, Creative, and on Contract)

* Branding: logo design, brand analysis, brand standards

* Creative Regional PR: innovative hand designed press kits, community outreach

* Interactive Marketing: social networking, mobile marketing, interactive planning

* Event Planning: wine/food events, private or corporate parties

* Functional Fine Art: watercolor, acrylic, sculpture, illustration

Goals:

* Facilitate the merge of business and creativity
* Creating cost efficient marketing solutions for small and medium businesses
* Structure effective creative strategies that are also fun, passionate, and innovative
* Nurture creative community in Boise, nationwide, and worldwide
* Vertical focus in film/tv, wine, fine art, and education industries
* Continue to specialize in high tech world and interactive space
