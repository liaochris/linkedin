Alex M Langenfeld’s Summary

I started my education at the University of Minnesota, focusing on English, Theater and Film Theory. After two years, I transfered to Columbia College Chicago, concentrating on Film Theory, Screen Writing and general production. I graduated with honors in the spring on 2008 with a BA in Film and Video.

During my time at Columbia, I found myself working in different capacities; mainly preproduction and idea development, but I also love gripping. In January of 2008, I began an internship with Pixel Brothers Inc. At Pixel, I was able to work on just about all aspects of production. It was a great experience and solidified my passion for creative media production.

I watch people. Or, I people watch. Either way, everything and everyone around me becomes the fodder for whatever project I'm working on. As I learn more about the world of creative professionals (Film, media, marketing, production, web), I use those experiences and interactions as the core of the ideas I'm kicking around. I'm in if you're in.
