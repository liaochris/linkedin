Susan Estes’s Summary

Prior to founding Zetian Capital, LLC, Susan Estes was most recently a Managing Director in the Capital Markets Division of Countrywide Financial Corporation. After joining in October 2003, Ms. Estes launched several non-mortgage rates trading businesses for the firm, inclusive of the Primary Dealership, launched in 2Q2004; the Futures Introducing Broker launched in 1Q2006 and the Derivative Products subsidiary launched in 1Q2007. In 2007, working with Countrywide Financial Corporation’s Mergers and Acquisitions department, she was instrumental in closing the first acquisition for the Capital Markets Division, CCM, a futures introducing broker.

Susan currently serves by invitation on the Treasury Borrowing Advisory Committee. The committee meets quarterly with the Treasury to provide advice on funding requirements and separately with the Federal Reserve Chairman and Board of Governors to address topical market issues. Discussions focus on the Committee’s various opinions regarding the potential credit market impact of said topics.

Prior to joining Countrywide in late 2003, Ms. Estes was Managing Director, Head of Fixed Income North America for Deutsche Bank. Prior to Deutsche Bank, Ms. Estes spent 15-years at Morgan Stanley where she was a Managing Director and Global Head of U.S. Treasury and Agency trading, with additional risk management responsibilities for Firm Finance in the Americas.

Ms. Estes served on the Capital Markets Steering Committee for the Securities Industry Financial Markets Association (SIFMA), formerly the Bond Market Association. Previously she also served on the Board of Directors for the Bond Market Association and as Chair for the US Government and Agency Division of the BMA.

She received a B.S. in Economics from the University of Redlands in 1981, was awarded an Honorary Doctorate in Business Administration in 2002 and currently serves on the Board of Trustees for the University.
