Mary Ann (Thoes) Bayer’s Summary

Mary Ann Bayer works as a residential buyer and seller's agent in Westchester County, NY for all price ranges.

She is certified in Assisting RELOCATION Buyers and is a certified ECOBROKER, designated to address and encourage the beneftis of energy efficiency and green features in homes. She is also a Graduate Realtor Institute (GRI) candidate.

Prior to real estate, Mary Ann managed accounts for a New York direct marketing firm and developed public relations plans for a national women's organization.
