J. Michael Peace’s Summary

J. Michael Peace is an Independent IT Consultant specializing in SAP Customer Care System (CCS) and Industry Solutions for Utilities (IS-U) and has been working with SAP IS-U and the Utilities Industry for over 7 years in many aspects, including taking on lead consultant roles in IS-U Billing, Invoicing & Rate Functional Areas during entire project lifecycles, with experience and extensive knowledge in the the Customer Services, Device Management and FICA areas as well. Michael is currently focusing on managing products & new requirements in post go-live SAP IS-U environments (including Deregulated markets), SAP IS-U post go-live Support, SAP Business Process Improvement, and SAP Quality Assurance.

Michael's SAP Utilities project experience includes Entergy Corporation, Reliant Energy, Pinellas County Utilities, Aquarion Water Company, and New York Power Authority, among other clients as well. His track record proves that he can provide creative solutions to complex business requirements and he ensures that solid, reliable processes & systems are in place for clients that have chosen SAP as their Customer Service & Billing solution.

While with Axon, Michael also participated in business pre-Sales Meetings with potential clients including Howard County Utilities and PSE&G, and in SAP Demo sessions for potential clients who have not yet chosen SAP as their solution.

Michael is also a former web designer and enjoys helping companies with their web site content, web site design & development, and web marketing initiatives. Past clients as a Web Development Consultant include Dover Dixon Horne Law Firm, CD Warehouse, and Little Rock Central High School. Michael also developed and maintained Entergy's internal web site for the Business Services group while he was with Entergy.
