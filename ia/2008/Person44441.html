Dan Orlando’s Summary

I am an expert Flex/ActionScript developer; particularly with data-driven rich web UI's that utilize a variety of web service protocols. I have been serving as Executive Director of Research and Development for Vision Media Group, a web-based software development company out of Phoenix and Las Vegas that focuses its efforts on video streaming applications. I have extensive experience in the research, design, and development of online software applications, and am currently part of the UI development of an Adobe AIR application that features a 3D user interface using the Papervision3D framework. Distribution of this application is expected to exceed 150 million in 2009 being that the distributors of the application include Microsoft, Apple, and Adobe, among others. Several of my recent roles as a consultant were working side by side with the Executives of client companies on a daily basis as a senior advisory and consultant for their entire IT-related decision making. Although I have been responsible for overseeing and project managing enterprise level product development projects, I like to focus my efforts more toward client-side development that leverages the power of FLEX and AIR.

Dan Orlando’s Specialties:

3D Actionscript 3.0 development, User Interface development for client-server RIA applications, Unix/Linux networking, PHP and Java programming.
