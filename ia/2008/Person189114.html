Tim Nicholson’s Summary

Over my 16 year career, I have had the opportunity to work in many different facets of lighting. I began lighting sets for television in the early 90's. Then found myself lighting medium sized (500 fixtures or less) concerts for broadcast. Along the way, I have lit short films, commercials, music videos, and documentaries as a Gaffer.

Having the versatility to light a variety of formats, I believe, is the key to my success and longevity in this very volatile industry. I search for project that make use of the skills that I have picked up along the way.

I describe my lighting as a balance of science and art. Good pre-production requires good science. Knowing how to manage a crew, budgets, equipment and timelines on a production is the science. Mixing light, shadow, colors and intensity to compliment the content of a production is art. Art can only be achieved with good science.

Working primarily in the Baltimore/Washington area, I do find the majority of my work is television, and political television to be specific. I would like to balance that with more dramatic projects. I am currently searching for short (15 days or less) independent films or commercials to work on throughout the year, in addition to my television lighting.

Tim Nicholson’s Specialties:

Extensive film, television and concert lighting experience. Specializing in achieving the perfect light for every project, from concept to reality.
