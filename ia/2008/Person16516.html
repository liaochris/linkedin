Joe Lombardo’s Summary

With a tenure of ten years in NYC as a talent representative - I found that Lady New York - would remind me daily that only the conscious survive.

Industry-educated and self-taught in the ways of photography, production, design, editorial, art, and exhibition - I now sit in the seat as a CEO (Crazy - Ethical - Optimist) of a company I now know will help me reach my goal of changing minds thru the visual arts. The people we represent have become my extended family and because of this - my associates and I are committed to service-without-boundary. We maintain the balance between ethical representation and boutique, art-inspired development.

We are members of SPAR, APA, ASMP. We pursue talent with clear vision and who specialize in niche markets of photography and design. We are innovators, thinkers, closers, and die-hard believers that our industry must redirect it's focus from cost-control to the art. Our goal is to facilitate pricing standards that inform the art in this way.

VISION STATEMENT

We are agents. We represent the most creative visionaries in the business who share our love for the art and "the deal" and who have a deep internal commitment to their personal viewpoint. We compete fairly. We bid with the end product and the person in mind. We foster a culture of development first and embrace that creative and financial fulfillment will be the result. We commit to the project being accomplished on-time, within budget, and with measured success based on creativity, accountability, high-level performance and trust. We contend that thru our initiative and resources - possibility for those we represent is only as far as the next person we reach out to.

We are happy and thankful to be amongst the best in our industry - turning NO's to YES's as quickly as our meetings will allow and having some fun along the way.
