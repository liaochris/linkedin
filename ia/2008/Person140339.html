Nicholas Paine’s Interests:

Nick has a passion for the organizational and strategic development of young companies, and helping manage their high growth. Nick's role is typically investor and consultant. In the summer of 2006, Nick both left and re-entered the Internet Dating industry. Nick has participated in Internet Dating as pioneer, to consultant, over 20 years. Nicholas was CEO of Lavalife between 1997 and 2001 during which time Lavalife raised US$31 M in VC funds , and ultimately reached revenues of US$90 M. Since 2000, Nicholas has managed a growing portfolio of North American, European, and now Asian consulting relationships and personal investments in, early-stage, mostly consumer-facing, technology-intensive service businesses. Nick’s residence is in London now with his wife and his 3 young boys. Nick's experiences have been primarily in start-up, private financings, rapid-growth organization development, and multi-national expansion and growth.
