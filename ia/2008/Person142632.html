Non Arkaraprasertkul’s Summary

My past professional experiences concern teaching at undergraduate and graduate levels in the fields of history theory and criticism, architecture, and urban design at several institutes of higher education both in Thailand and the United States. My specialties are design and research in cultural construction, architectural theory, urban design and environment. These are my current academic endeavors and pursuits. I have also received numerous awards such as the Fulbright Fellowship, the Rockefeller's Scholarships, the Starr Foundation Award of Excellence, and MIT's Graduate Research Award. Moreover, I have a rigorous architectural/urban design experience through internships and private practice. My future goals are to educate students through academic collaboration between Thailand and the world in order to strive for the mutual understanding of cultural grounds and urban environment we are living in. I believe that a better environment for happier people is in the making.
