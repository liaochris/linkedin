Jan Zeiger’s Summary

I have my BA and MA in elementary education and hope to
pursue another degree in the coming years. I'm interested
in alternative medicine and nutrition, so I plan to study
natural health. I'd also like to promote breastfeeding by
becoming a lactation consultant, volunteering my services
to low-income moms who need support and encouragement.

In addition to caring for two very active small children, I am
an independent consultant for Natural Family Boutique and
I serve as the editor for Natural Living at Bella Online. I've
just updated my website by adding a Natural Living store as
well as links for those interested in Juice Plus and Usborne
Books. My newest role is that of Children's TV writer at
Suite 101. As you can see, I wear many hats! I also hope
to write books on homeschooling and raising children naturally
when I have a spare moment. :)

Find out more about any of these ventures by going to my main website, www.janzeiger.com.
