John Paul Sherman’s Summary

My professional experience has always been in being able to develop creative solutions to find data and information. Whether it is analyzing physical evidence in an archaeological context, historical and cultural research in support of humanitarian operations or trending analytic data to create a picture of the online competitive marketspace, my professional experience has, for the past 10 years, been taking the raw data available and turning it into an actionable predictive model. Currently my methodologies are used to create more competitive online models. I have been able to define strategic and tactical models for pharmaceutical companies, financial companies, health care companies and entertainment companies.

I also have strong experience in art and design. As a Marie Walsh Sharpe Scholarship recipient, I have had extensive training in design and color theory. In my spare time, I create art in several different media.

My ideal job would be to work in a creative industry, where my data analytic skills would support my creative training.
