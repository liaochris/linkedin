Brian David Butler’s Summary

MBA, Thunderbird School of Global Management '08, Valedictorian

Mr. Butler has over 10 years experience in entrepreneurship, marketing, global business and technology, and is the co-founder of Brazil America Ventures.

In addition to authoring an online guide to business in Brazil (https://globotrends.pbwiki.com/Brazil), Mr. Butler has over 7 years experience working in Brazil. As founder of Marcusso Designs, Mr. Butler formed a Joint Venture with Suriana Trading in Sao Paulo, and successfully developed business relationships in Belo Horizonte, Sao Paulo and Recife, Brazil. Mr. Butler is currently splitting his time between Miami and Recife, Brazil and has been involved with market entry consulting for American companies entering the Brazilian marketplace.

Mr Butler previously worked as a startup consultant at NextLogics, a boutique investment and consulting firm focused on early stage endeavors with social impact. While working with NextLogics, Mr. Butler worked with “Global Social Capital”: a peer-to-peer micro lending / micro finance platform for Latin America. Mr Butler also developed business plans for a WiFi mesh network for Brazil which involved WiFi infrastructure investment project for the NE of Brazil.

Mr Butler is an entrepreneur and the founder of "KookyPlan.com", an online wiki-based resource for investors and entrepreneurs, with an emphasis on investing in emerging markets. Mr Butler also writes for GloboTrends, a blog dedicated to global macro economic trends and innovation, and is involved in teaching global economics, finance and trade with Thunderbird Global MBA in Miami.

Mr. Butler previously was a technology-industry analyst with the "Columbia Institute of Tele-Information" (of the Columbia Business School), focusing on new technologies and innovative business models.

At the beginning of his career, Mr. Butler worked as a global supply chain manager with Honda of America Manufacturing.
