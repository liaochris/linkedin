Arvind Raman’s Summary

Arvind Raman is currently the Director of Product Management for c360 Solutions ( a CDC Software Company) where he is responsible for the driving and managing the product the c360 product strategy and roadmap which includes over 25 add-on products for the Microsoft Dynamics CRM platform.

Prior to c360, Arvind worked with various companies leading and implementing successful CRM solutions with a focus on the Financial Services industry. Arvind worked with Easybroker in London where he was responsible for the deployment of a tightly integrated CRM and equity trading system. Arvind worked with Riggs Bank in Washington, DC where he was responsible for architecting CRM solutions as part of Rigg’s Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti Money Laundering (AML) programs.

Arvind has also led CRM projects in a number of other industries including High-Tech (Internet Security System and Factset Research Systems), Utilities (Keyspan Energy) and Retail Marketing (DCI Marketing). Arvind began his career in the CRM industry as a Senior Consultant at Onyx Software, a mid-market CRM software vendor where he worked with different clients helping implement the Onyx CRM solutions.

Prior to Onyx, Arvind was the Implementation Account Manager at Neovest Inc, a leader in the trading software market, where he worked with institutional buy-side and sell-side investment firms implementing Neovest’s proprietary trading technology. Arvind started his career at Morgan Stanley in New York, where he was a Business Analyst in their Institutional Equities group.

Arvind has also been involved with different professional and community organizations such as TIE (The Indus Entrepreneurs), MIT Enterprise Forum and GTBN (Georgia Tech Business Network). Arvind is an honors graduate from the Georgia Institute of Technology where he received a BS degree in Electrical Engineering and a Certificate in Economics. Arvind is also a Certified Pragmatic Marketing Product Manager

Arvind Raman’s Specialties:

Microsoft Dynamics CRM, Product Management, Project/Team Management, offshore product development, leadership, Agile development methodology, Consulting, CRM Implementation
