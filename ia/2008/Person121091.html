Marina Mendez. imagination > knowledge’s Summary

She was born June 11 th, 1974. During her childhood and teenage years she lived in Parque Patricios, a working-class neighborhood. She has been a hard-working person since she was only 14, and learnt to be “a doer”. She finally decided to study Communication at “Universidad de Buenos Aires” after studying English at “AACI". She has committed with social responsibility skills and participated in seminars both in Argentina and abroad; being her latest challenge the Executive Marketing Program (PEM) at IAE (U. Austral). In the beginning of '93 she started her first-class company career at Lloyds Bank TSB Ltd. She discovered her real call: connecting social skills with technology. In 1998 she joined Banco Sudameris, an Italian financial company, where she managed to climb the corporate ladder and reach the executive position: e-marketing & comm Manager. During 2007 Marina led multicultural marketing strategies for 500 Fortune companies as a Director: Marketing, PR & Services at Latin3 Inc. Her current challenge is CEO, Latam at Zed Digital /Publicis Group.Marina has become a “professional change maker”, her main areas of proficiency: marketing, communication, quality. She married Gustavo Begher, a 33 year-old accountant. They have 2 children, Nicolás & Lucca, and they have plans to grow as a family, performing their roles as good as possible, and always remembering to balance family, friends, job, spirit and professional projects. Marina’s leit motive: “happiness is about feeling well everyday”. She transforms the difficult into interesting with happiness, passion, intelligence and her characteristic drive. Once, a headhunter asked her how she managed to make things look beautiful. "It is only with the heart that one can see rightly; what is essential is invisible to the eye”. “It’s easy to make things beautiful”
