Walter Brandes’s Summary

Resounding successes leading Sales, Marketing, Operations and R&D teams from start-ups to multi-billion dollar public companies for over 3 decades. A proven track record of capitalizing on organizational opportunities improving the performance of existing sales and marketing teams, as well as building successful new sales, marketing, R&D, manufacturing and QA/QC teams from the ground up.
