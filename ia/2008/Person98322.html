Jos Kleijnen’s Summary

We at Kleijnen Systematic Reviews Ltd prepare systematic reviews (and meta-analyses), rapid reviews, and health technology assessments to support policy making, licensing and reimbursement decisions, local decision making about commissioning health services, fourth hurdle processes, economic evaluations and guideline development.

We provide training and consultancy in systematic reviews, health technology assessment, guidelines, research methods, basic statistics, meta-analysis, evidence based medicine and evidence based health care.
