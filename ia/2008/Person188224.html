Thomas Lee’s Summary

A video game executive with a deep understanding of game technologies, social networks, and free to play online games, Thomas is currently heading up the Marketing and Business Development for Gamepot in the US. Prior to this, he was at Nexon America managing Prepaid Cards distribution at national retailers, including Target, 7-Eleven, Duane Reade, Rite Aid, Best Buy, Wal-Mart, and CVS with the focus on promoting free to play micro-transaction item sales business model. A game developer by trade, he has over 15 years of experience at leading game publishers including Nexon [MapleStory] Square Enix [Final Fantasy 9], and EA [Wing Commander 3]. As a founding member of International Game Developers Association, Korea Chapter, he serves on several advisory boards. Thomas has been a speaker at industry events including Korean Game Developers Conference (2003, 2004), G-Star (2006), Asia Pacific Entertainment & Media Summit (2007), InComm Partner Alliance (2008), and The Digital Hollywood Spring 2008.
