Laurent Stadelmann’s Summary

Through my extensive experience of buying, renting and letting property, I have grown increasingly frustrated over the years by the lack of any level of quality service provided to property buyers and tenants. The whole purchase and letting process is geared towards sellers and landlords, while buyers and tenants are more often than not left tearing their hair out!

I also found that I was in constant demand by family, friends and acquaintances as a resource on the local property market, to offer advice on their property decisions or even to help in the negotiation process. And it was on this last point that I realised, if I could help my clients to save money on the purchase or rental of a property, savings greater than any fees I would charge as a business, then I would have a model that would be beneficial to all parties.

So from there it was a natural progression to establish Chez-Vous HomeSearch and to use my property experience, business skills and my fundamental belief in the importance of excellent customer service to aid buyers and tenants. By managing the time-consuming and frustrating process on my clients’ behalf, I offer not only convenience, enabling my clients to continue their everyday lives, but also the opportunity to save money.”

I am a French national who has lived and worked in the UK for over 15 years and I own a varied property portfolio.

I am a business and marketing professional who has worked for large corporations.

Additionally, I am well positioned to meet the needs of UK relocating employees, and expatriates moving to the UK.

Having lived both the experiences of UK relocation and being expatriate myself, I understand only too well the issues associated with long distance moving and settling into a foreign country. I know how difficult it can be to find the right location to live in, let alone the right house! So I will do my utmost to relieve the pressure and ease the process for others.
