Jeff Spoonhower’s Summary

I have over seven years of professional experience as a cinematic artist, animator and lighter. My work has been featured in numerous AAA blockbuster video games, including:

Resistance: Retribution (PSP)
Fracture (X360, PS3)
Saints Row 2 (X360, PS3)
SOCOM: US Navy Seals Tactical Strike (PSP)
Syphon Filter: Logan's Shadow (PSP)
Saints Row (X360)
NFL Street 2 (XBOX, PS2, NGC)
NFL Street (XBOX, PS2, NGC)

Jeff Spoonhower’s Specialties:

• Character, vehicle and prop animation for pre-rendered sequences and real-time game use.
• High poly and low poly character, set, environment and prop modeling/texturing.
• Full body and facial rigging for characters, rigging for vehicles, objects and props.
• Particle effects for pre-rendered games, film and broadcast, as well as real-time game use.
• Promotional videos and trailers
• HD/SD editing and compositing
• Concept art for characters, objects, sets and environments.
