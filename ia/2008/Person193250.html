Ted Theofrastous’s Summary

Ted has extensive experience in the field of high technology development and commercialization, both as a business architect/manager and as a legal professional. Prior to founding ThetaSquared, LLC, Ted served as managing director and fund counsel for Panzica Investments, where he created and managed a portfolio of new and existing technology startup investments. Before Panzica, Ted managed a diverse IP portfolio and launched startup companies for the Cleveland Clinic Foundation designed to commercialize products based on CCF intellectual property. Ted’s roles related to the tech startups he works with typically include business structuring and operational planning, developing an intellectual property strategy, negotiating financial investment and playing an active role as a manager and/or director.

Before joining the Cleveland Clinic Foundation, Ted practiced at the law firm of Squire, Sanders & Dempsey L.L.P., where his primary practice focused on the nexus of investment capital and technology. At Squire Sanders, he counseled clients in matters ranging from start-up formation to domestic and off-shore private equity fund formations. His activities in the private equity area included counseling clients on a range of regulatory and structural issues related to the Investment Company Act of 1940, private securities offerings, investment advisory issues and investor negotiations.

Ted’s information technology business background consists of over twenty years working as a member of various executive management teams and boards of directors in the re-engineering and automation of a range of business and workflow processes. As an industry certified professional, he designed and implemented a broad array of proprietary technology solutions, including distributed multi-platform data systems, interstate VoIP networks and a diverse set of hardware and information system solutions.
