Simon Barber’s Summary

Experienced software development professional with a broad knowledge base. Particular special interest in embedded, networking and wireless. I am especially good at innovation and thinking outside of the box - quickly identifying relevant customer and market needs and coming up with practical solutions to satisfy them. I have good experience working with industry standards bodies and patenting.

I was the technical lead on the founding team @ Instant802 Networks (now Devicescape). We successfully raised money in the worst market of 2001 with tier 1 VCs. Devicescape has repeatedly sold software to numerous companies from the top tier of OEMs/ PC OEMs.

In addition I have some knowledge of hardware design, FPGAs and microcontrollers. Interfacing with the 'real world' of switches, lights, pumps, etc.

Simon Barber’s Specialties:

IEEE 802.11, Wi-Fi, Embedded, Networking, VoIP, OO programming, Standards Development, System Architecture, OS internals, Linux kernel development, Device driver design for multiple OSs, C, C++, Java, Modula 3, Numerous 8 and 32 bit assembly languages - particularly strong in ARM.
