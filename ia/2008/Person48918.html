Deb Howard’s Summary

Director of marketing in storage with extensive outbound and inbound expertise in networking (LAN, MAN, WAN), storage (DAS, NAS, SAN) and security (encryption, authentication) markets including enterprise software, hardware and professional services.

Deb Howard’s Specialties:

My experience includes daily exposure to partner and customer needs for advanced solutions as well as solid awareness of technological developments in IP, security, virtualized storage, software development, and IP SANs. My keen sense of market requirements in financial, insurance, legal, health care, telco, manufacturing, and other vertical sectors provides me with the ability to articulate direction to diverse audiences: customers, channel, executives & staff, media & analysts.
