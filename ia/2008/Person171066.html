Scott Barlow’s Summary

Scott is a proven sales leader with extensive experience in bringing new products to market and building strong sales channels and strategic alliances. At Reflexion, Scott is responsible for worldwide sales of Reflexion Total Control (hosted email threat protection), RADAR (archiving, eDiscovery and business continuity) and Hosted Web Filtering.

Prior to joining Reflexion, Scott was Director of Business Development at start-up Virtual Access Networks, a leader in operating system migration and remote access that was acquired by Symantec. Previously he worked at Cambridge Technology Partners, where he developed a go-to-market strategy for a consulting practice focused on post-ERP implementation services.

Scott holds an MBA and a Masters of Science in Geophysics from Boston College.

Scott Barlow’s Specialties:

Scott has been a channel leader in the SMB space for the past 8 years, developing go-to-market strategies leading to rapid indirect channel growth. Scott frequently speaks at industry events on SaaS, Hosted and Managed Services, and ways for resellers to increase their revenue through additional SaaS offerings, e.g. BDR, eDiscovery, etc.

Experienced team leader, rapid growth of ISP and solution provider channels, worldwide expansion and developing operational go-to-market strategies.
