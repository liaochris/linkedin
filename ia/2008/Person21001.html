Brian Belefant’s Summary

What started out at a freelance copywriting business somehow blossomed into a full-on consultancy and a production company. I do brand strategy work, marketing development, and advertising. I also produce and direct commercials, films, and documentaries. And write. I like writing. Keeps me sane. More or less.

Brian Belefant’s Specialties:

Writing, producing, directing, marketing
