Brent Bouchez’s Summary

I have been fortunate enough to work for the best advertising agencies in the country on some of the best brands in the world. From BMW to Burger King, Bank of America to Chevrolet. I have learned from the best...people like Lee Clow, Jay Chiat, Ralph Ammirati, Martin Puris, Steve Hayden, my creative partner David Page and more.

Over the years, I have found that my real strength is strategic thinking for brands. Helping them to find their voice and their personality and then steering them towards the best ways to express those to their consumers. I love the process of exploring a brand and finding it's core truth, the thing that really makes it tick and then helping that brand get the entire organization focused on that truth so that they can own it in the marketplace. Every brand has something inside it that can help it move to the next level, my job is to find it and use it to create success.

Brent Bouchez’s Specialties:

I don't really like the idea of "specialties" as they tend to box people in...that said, I have deep experience in categories like automotive, financial, technology, restaurants and luxury. Personally, I am most excited when I get the opportunity to help a brand re-position itself for renewed success.
