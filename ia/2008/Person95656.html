John Steele’s Summary

After graduation from Imperial College of Science and Technology where he obtained a B.Sc. (Hons) in Physics he joined Services Technique Schlumberger working in the oilfield services group.

He worked approximately 12 years in Schlumberger, receiving his initial training in Australia before completing field assignments in the South China Sea, Burma, Abu Dhabi, Saudi Arabia and Egypt.

He was then assigned to the Eastern Hemisphere headquarteras based in Paris, initially in Recruiting and then as International Staff Personnel Policies Manager.

In 1980 he was promoted to Personnel Manager for the Far East Region with full HR responsibilities for over 1,500 staff of over 20 nationalities based throughout the Far East. The HR and recruiting department for which he was responsible comprised over 12 people based in Singapore, Australia, Malaysia and Indonesia.

In 1982 he returned to Europe and started his own executive search company, Manserv AG. In 1996 he joined the MRI organisation, the worlds largest direct search operation with over 1,000 offices worldwide, renaming his company to MRRI Maanserv AG. He and his office have won various MRI awards. In addition he has served on the Millenium Group, an internal company think tank as well as serving as an elected board member of the MRI Franchise Association.

His office is specialised in Automotive, ERP and consulting, medical technology, IT and telecommunications as well as the functional specialisations of Financial management, HR and sales & marketing.

John is also an Avatar Master and offers Avatar training (together with his wife Ursula) to interested people in various locations in Western Europe throughout the year.
