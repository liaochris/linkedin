Rick Valderrama’s Summary

Rick has over 17 years of experience in voice and data network engineering. He gained his engineering experience while serving 11 years in the United States Air Force. During this time he specialized in designing and implementing tactical local area and wide area networks with specific expertise in BGP routing and network security in converged environments. After separating from the Air Force he was hired by Quantum Technology Services, a Florida based military contractor, to launch their IT Engineering Division where he was first introduced to Telephony Partners. 5 years later he joined the Telephony Partners executive team and shares in management responsibilities for the overall strategic and tactical direction of the company.

Rick Valderrama’s Specialties:

Enterprise WAN, LAN, VoIP, MPLS design and implementation, carrier services and products, network security and business development.

    Telephony Partners is a communications Master Agency representing a diverse group of carriers offering local telephone, long distance, Internet, and enterprise data services. Our structure is unique in that we focus on the creation of a community of carrier and agent partners that share the common objective of leveraging individual capabilities to provide our collective clients with total solutions. Recognizing that we cannot be a jack of all trades, our approach has been to strategically develop our industry relationships to enable us to execute our core competencies of system design and engineering, support, and project management. Our capabilities represent a depth of expertise not often found in master agents, allowing our partners to offer a high level of service to customers of all sizes. These capabilities also allow us to provide unique solutions for enterprise customers looking to develop a comprehensive telecom strategy.
