Matt Evans’s Summary

I have a broad international web development background with a user driven approach to development, information design and UE.

Currently I work with the multinational company EF Education's Language group as technical project manager for their web development on ef.com and act as a guide to new product development for a forthcoming re-architecture that will be released world wide.

My aim with any project is always to provide both the user and client an exceptional experience by leveraging creative ideas and utilizing the most appropriate, effective and cutting edge technologies. I work hard with every project big or small to not just find the most innovative solution but also to make sure it is scalable, maintainable and will be built to deadline.

- Head in the clouds, feet on the ground! -

New product development - design conception to technical execution. Technical project management, front end & accessibility, web development & frameworks, CMS, usability & user experience, adaptive systems, interfacing and control systems.
