Troy Saxton-Getty’s Summary

An expert at managing a well balanced blend of people, process and technology.

I am highly specialized in fast growth companies with extremely critical transaction driven production environments as well as situations emphasizing a high degree of change management, efficient and streamlined process development, EBITDA control, cost and project containment.

Turn-around, high stress situations which later become "Zen" like in their operation with a cultural transformation. - a place people love to work and seldom leave.

I have over 22 years of technology experience, including software development, infrastructure, security & privacy, regulatory compliance, business continuity, knowledge management , collaborative systems, telephony, VOIP, major migration efforts, concept and product development, management of technical organizations, development of key accounts, contract negotiation, and budgeting. I have an impeccable track record of meeting profit, schedule and quality objectives, and have successfully built highly skilled teams to meet demanding technical requirements and development schedules.
