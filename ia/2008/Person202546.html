Michael Yudanin’s Summary

Experienced manager and software quality professional. Leads a consulting company that specializes in custom-made solutions for software process improvement, application development, quality assurance and testing, off-shoring assurance. Has direct experience in developing test automation and performance testing solutions. Works with global teams.

Certified by the American Society for Quality as Software Quality Engineer (CSQE). Awarded in 1999.

- Standards and Methodologies: CMMI, ISO, IEEE, RUP, Agile/XP(Extreme)

- Performance testing: WebLoad, LoadRunner, QALoad
- Independent Verification and Validation (IV&V) and Implementation Assurance
- Off-shoring Assurance: from setup to ROI
