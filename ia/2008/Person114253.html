Lisa Bastian, CBC’s Summary

Lisa Bastian is an award-winning business communicator with over 25 years of professional experience in the areas of editorial (writing, editing, managing a staff), business marketing, public relations and promotions.

As principal of Bastian Public Relations (established in 1983), she creates everything from features/news articles, Web site content, museum information panels and advertorials, to press releases, media kits, marketing brochures and corporate profiles.

Lisa is professional, ethical, dependable...and thinks she has one of the best jobs on the planet as a communications consultant!

Open to new partnerships and helping organizations with short- and long-term editorial/PR proejcts. See www.BastianPR.com
