Arthur D. Wright’s Summary

I was raised in the travel industry. Then “joined the industry” about 20 years ago, as a full time travel agent working in my family’s privately owned travel firm. Working with our clients directly, I was able to advise from personal travel knowledge and experience from all my previous years of traveling. I was trained to guide the travel client with personal attention, awareness, and experience with a product or industry, such as Holland America, Travcoa, Wilderness Travel, Cruise West, and a host of other travel firms, hotels, and airlines.

I was later fortunate to join one of the largest family-run travel agencies in the United States—Rich Worldwide Travel. As any traveler is aware, there are “just so many destinations you can personally explore.” Joining this firm “raised the bar” of expertise. The total amount of collected knowledge available to an agent just improves the agent/client relationship.

Generally we have current up-to-date knowledge of any changes going on within a destination, such as construction at a hotel. The knowledge is as broad as travel experience can be.

Rich Worldwide Travel is a member of IGLTA and Virtuoso Travel Associations. Rich Worldwide Travel was recently awarded by Virtuoso—to be recognized as one of the top-ten agencies in the “Luxury Travel field”. This is a great honor for both the travel agents and Rich Worldwide Travel as a whole.

It should be mentioned that there are “trip and planning fees” that are applied to confirmed reservations. These fees vary upon the type of trip requested; yet the client gets personal service and generally is alerted to value-added features when available.

You’re not only working with a Travel Agent. You also get the Concierge Travel Service, for your Personal, Business, Corporate Meeting and or Incentive Travel

Arthur D. Wright’s Specialties:

I listen to the clients, then help plan and advise. I question. I feel confident that if I do not know the correct answer I can find out. I focus on planning for the unexpected; personalized attention; expectation fulfillment; time management.
