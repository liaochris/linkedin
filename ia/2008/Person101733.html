Kalynn Amadio’s Summary

I am an Instructor of the world's most popular martial art, tae kwon do. I endeavor to live a healthy lifestyle through my martial arts dedication.
In my daily living, I'm a wife, mother of 3 sons and busy internet infopreneur.

Holding a degree in Architectural Engineering, I work as a Lighting Designer on high end residential work in the New York City area.

I am the creator and webmaster of www.Taekwondo-Network.com where taekwondo enthusiasts from around the world come to learn more about their favorite topic, Tae Kwon Do.

Also, I own and operate www.TKDspace.com, the myspace for taekwondo. A natural wellness site, www.Alternatively-Healthier.com and in deference to my sons who are lacrosse players, www.Lacrosse-Information.com.

I am a writer who mostly self publishes on the net. However, I have started more than one children's book and even a romance novel which is my favorite genre. Especially when it merges with Sci Fi.
