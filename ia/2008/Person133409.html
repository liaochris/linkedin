Miriam Axel-Lute’s Summary

I have been an editor and journalist for more than a decade. As a freelancer, I enjoy pursuing a mix of writing projects and editing gigs to satisfy both my inquisitive, creative side and my detail-oriented, perfectionist side.

As a writer, I have published everything from hard news to profiles, opinion columns to how-to pieces. I currently write a biweekly column for my local weekly paper, as well as articles and reports for a range of publications and organizations. My particular interests include cities, the environment, women’s health, parenting, food, and sexuality, but I also love diving into new topics.

In my editing positions, I have worked with professional and nonprofessional writers to sharpen their prose and get their message across to the right audience in the space available. When I was news editor at Metroland, the paper's news coverage won multiple awards every year.

I currently offer developmental and copy editing for anything from books to websites to annual reports.

I also:
-am a co-founder of the Sex-Positive Journalism Awards (www.sexies.org), which are currently taking submissions.

-visit classrooms, churches, living rooms and most other places I'm invited as a performance poet (see www.mjoy.org/poetry).

Miriam Axel-Lute’s Specialties:

My editing specialties include making academese accessible to the lay reader and trimming down overly wordy documents to concise and powerful language while retaining the voice of the author.

My writing specialties include explaining controversial topics in such a way that both sides of an issue consider it a fair portrayal.
