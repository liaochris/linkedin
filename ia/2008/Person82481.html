Jake Massey’s Summary

I specialize in high volume enterprise application development and planning. I have designed and developed systems to support over 500,000 online users, and built systems that process over 12,000 DML transactions per second with a total hardware cost not exceeding $18,000.00 US.

I have designed and developed systems that exceed 51 terabytes, and designed highly available and recoverable systems that include cross continent failover support.

I have managed and led teams comprising of many application developers, database developers, QA engineers, implementation specialists, and production support personnel across multiple continents with offshore facilities in Bangalore, Bangkok, and Tel Aviv.

I have managed customer service organizations to support both internal and external customers to ensure optimum issue resolution and turnaround time on all issues that came into various call centers and network operation centers.

Developed user friendly and intuitive dashboard systems for customers and agents to access so various customer service metrics could be viewed and analyzed in real time

I have run several IT organizations and developed several commercial products for use in corporate Intranets.

I now focus the majority of my time on helping lead organizations and aligning their overall IT objectives.

I currently live in metro Atlanta, GA with my wife Angel and daughter Lillian.
