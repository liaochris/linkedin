Gene Newman’s Summary

I currently serve as the Editorial Director for Specialist Websites in Hachette Filipacchi's Digital division, where I oversee content direction and implementation for Premiere.com, PopPhoto.com, Sound and Vision as well as other specialty/enthusiast properties.

Before this I served as Editor-In-Chief/Programming Director of Maxim Digital from 2005-2008, where I oversaw the editorial, photo, video and mobile content for Maxim.com, Stuffmagazine.com, Blender.com and other affiliated websites. During this period my staff and I created the most popular men's general interest destination on the Internet, garnering more than 5 million unique visitors per month, 60 million-plus pageviews and an average of 20 minutes time spent. I joined Dennis Publishing as a Senior Editor in November 1999 as part of the initial online division and was promoted to Executive Editor in early 2001.

At the beginning of the dot-com boom, I was the Senior Writer and one of the driving forces behind the independent Webzine Tag, an online community of artists and writers. I have written for a variety of consumer and trade publications/Websites and am a graduate of NYU journalism school. I began my career writing movie reviews at the age of 16 for suburban Philadelphia newspaper The County Press.
