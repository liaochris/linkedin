David de-Montaigne (Global Sustainability)’s Summary

David de Montaigne is a British national with over 30 years of experience within the Oil and Gas, Energy, Renewable Energy, Project Development and Investment Banking sectors.

David has over 18 years experience operating within China where he is now based as the Chief Executive Officer of Eco Energy Cities Plc (EEC); a Joint Venture between Davis Langdon International and China Developments Plc. EEC also maintains a close collaborative relationship with the University of East Anglia: www.eco-energy-cities.com

David also holds key investments and Directorships within ECO US Plc, China Developments Plc, CDM Development Ltd (Non-Profit), CDM Bank Ltd, CDM Securities Ltd , The de Montaigne Group, de Montaigne Inc, and several other energy and finance related corporations.

David started his career as a petroleum engineer with the largest global oil & gas service company in the world in 1975: the Shclumberger Group. During his nine year career with Schlumberger David was seconded internationally to; The Libyan National Oil Company, Petrobras, Britoil, British Petroleum, Shell, ARAMCO, ADNOC, Chevron, AMOCO, ARCO, Kuwait Petroleum, TEXACO, CONOCO, Marathon, Occidental, Amerada Hess, EXXON, Mobile, Total, Elf Aquitaine, STATOIL and many other energy companies.

David was appointed the youngest Regional Manager for Schlumberger being responsible for all operations within their most challenging environment (The North Sea – UK), and later as the youngest Director within the group. He has lectured at over seventy five international oil companies, government agencies and universities regarding oil & gas reservoir evaluation, exploration, oil reservoir remediation, logistics and production sectors as well as renewable and alternative energy development, project funding and management.
