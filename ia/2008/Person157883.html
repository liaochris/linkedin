Richard Denisevich’s Summary

Richard Denisevich is the founder and managing partner of RJD Solution Partners, an organization that focuses exclusively on IT Governance topics and specializes in IT Service Management strategy and implementation. He is industry recognized for optimizing organizational productivity and performance, architecting innovative process improvements and adopting effective technological, governance and service solutions for both Business and IT. For more than 18 years (13 years in Banking), Richard has held senior leadership positions or key consultative roles for top tier companies and start-ups within the financial, consumer goods and technology sectors in the United States, Switzerland, United Kingdom and across Europe.

Achievements and solid IT governance results are reflected in his work as a Senior Service Management Consultant for a multinational private bank (Switzerland)- Wealth Management Division, leading a multi-faceted team in the successful assessment, design and implementation of a comprehensive service management model. Richards governance capabilities are also highlighted by his accomplishments as IS Program Manager for Philip Morris International where he introduced and managed a best practice program for the provision of IS/IT services utilizing ITIL throughout Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) affiliates.
