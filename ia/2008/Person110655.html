Larry DeVries’s Summary

I have experience at every level of the restaurant industry from restaurant employee to senior executive, and I still enjoy the work at every level. While most of my career has been in line operations, I have significant experience in HR, Training, Field Marketing, and Financial Analysis. I have designed equipment, processes, and computer systems, and have been involved in the acquisition of capital.

I am looking for a role where I can contribute to either growth or turnaround, and I have extensive experience in both. I love the energy involved in high growth environments and the feeling of urgency in turnarounds. In either case, the challenge is channeling that energy in people to achieve the desired outcome. Getting people behind those kinds of efforts is the most rewarding thing I have done in my career and I look forward to doing it again.

Although my preference would be to find a challenging position on a fairly permanent basis, I will certainly welcome interim positions or I can also work on a project basis.
