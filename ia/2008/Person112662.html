Lenore Howe’s Summary

Internet publisher with a Master's Degree in Internet Strategy Management and a solid background in writing and editing. Have over ten years experience in managing web content, websites and custom Internet projects for pharmaceutical companies and international trade associations. Our company seeks and maintains long term relationships with clients; we are always happy to speak with potential new customers.

We publish three websites:
* SeekWellness.com for patient empowerment with health information
* ContinenceNurse.net to help nurses stay current with new treatments
* SEPHIAsChoice.com, a search engine to help patients and professionals find each other.

Lenore Howe’s Specialties:

Writing and editing of complex health and medical articles, publishing for consumers and health professionals, innovative design and construction of Internet projects.
