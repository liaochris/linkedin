Fernando Artigas’s Summary

3 years experience in telecommunications systems. I have worked with project, customer application engineering of fixed switching, transport and access equipment for NGN. I’ve also worked with installation, field support, configuration and provisioning of mobile technology equipments that use GSM, IMS and Wimax technology.

Experience in technical support, and introduction of new technologies coming from abroad into Brazilian market (Remote Line Unit, IMS and Wimax). I successfully elaborated the survey guides for PGMU (Programa Geral de Metas de Universalização) and Wimax NSN solutions. This work included elaboration of tests protocols for trial to customer.

Good experience in provisioning of NGN and WiMAX technology equipments, hiQ (Softswitch), hiG (Media Gateway) and WayMAX, using the NMC and NetViewer manufactured by the company NSN.

Trial of the NSN WayMAX equipment in Brazil. The work included connecting the Softswitch (hiQ8000) with one Media Gateways (hiG1100) and one Video Plataform (SURPASS Home Entertainment), at a wireless radio access provided by WiMAX technology, included both WayMAX Base Stations (BSs) in the coverage area and 4 Subscriber Terminals (STs) in the customer premises with four different types of modulations at different locations . The core network was a pure IP backbone operating on the basis of SIP and Siemens SURPASS Softswitch technology.

Good knowledge of field technical support, provisioning and upgrade of Remote Line Unit and BTS’s. I have worked with field support at bring-up of BTS and RLU, working directly with the operators Telemar and Oi (customers).

Performed other activities such as automation system verification for fault analysis of the interworking between hiQ4200 and hiE9200.

Strong knowledge of the many protocols used in telecommunications, such Internet protocols (IP, TCP, UDP, routing and switching) including hands on job with Cisco (CCNA Certified).

Fernando Artigas’s Specialties:

Solid knowledge of NGN: SIP Application Server Softswitch and Media Gateway; IMS: CSCF, HSS and Wimax.

Good knowledge in VOIP Signaling Protocols (SIP, MGCP, H.323 stack, MEGACO, VOIP Transport Protocols (RTP, RTCP) and Legacy Signaling Protocols SS7 and ISDN and its applications for fixed technology.

Good experience specify overall Test Automation concept for product integration test environment for GUI driven End-To-End test scenarios.
