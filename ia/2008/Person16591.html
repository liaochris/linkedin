Arturo J. Bencosme, PhD’s Summary

Designer & facilitator of board game-based workshops centered on the Hero's Journey and the Spiritual Warrior archetypical metaphors. These workshops are aimed both at individuals and work teams for rehearsing being the hero in their life's or business endeavors, and thus discover useful keys to an enhanced, successful path.

A enthused professional, combines technical skills and organizational development capabilities. Major strengths include: excellence driven, achiever, connectedness, learner, creativity and ideation. With extensive academic, consulting and service experience. Holds a PhD & 2 M.Sc. degrees from Stanford University. Is a graduate from the Insight Seminars series, levels I - IV.

Professional experience includes organizations within the public and private sectors, specially at La Electricidad de Caracas, having been in charge of designing and facilitating strategic planning and organizational alignment efforts for more than 12 years. Academic experience includes teaching at Universidad de Los Andes, Stanford University, IESA (MBA School, Caracas), and a number of management courses and conferences. Was Venezuela's Director for Insight Seminars (Personal Growth Workshops) from 1995 to 2001.

Mobilizes organizational energy by means of aligning the persons around worthwhile goals while attaining clarity of strategic issues.
• Construction and sharing of the organization’s Purpose, Mission, Vision, Guiding Principles, Visionary Slogan, Objectives.
• Comprehending business strategy dynamics to leverage performance.
• Facilitation of organizational transformation and alignment processes
• Facilitation of team integration
• Individual executive support on transforming adversity into advantages.

Utilizes a wide range of facilitation techniques and styles to insure that each of these events results into a high productivity occasion, providing an space for effective interaction and individual expansion.

Arturo J. Bencosme, PhD’s Specialties:

Coaching and assistance to individuals and work teams in attaining an epic life style. Experiential management & executive learning laboratories. Executive and managerial team building. Visionary strategic planning and organizational learning developmental processes.
