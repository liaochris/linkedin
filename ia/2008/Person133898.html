MJ DiBerardino’s Summary

MJ joined his business partner in the development of FreeTicketExchange in July of 2006. As a dedicated event attendee and Real Fan, conceiving the vision of FreeTicketExchange was easy. MJ recognizes bringing the Real Fans to the forefront of the ticketing industry is what the market needs in order to decrease the high markups and fees currently being paid. MJ’s role now is dedicated to deliver the highest quality Real Fan marketplace and ensure the integrity of FreeTicketExchange’s community.
