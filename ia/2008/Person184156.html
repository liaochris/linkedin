Suzanne Arant Wells’s Summary

My first passion is helping other stay-at-home moms find success using eBay and the internet to earn an income that allows them the flexibililty to be an at-home parent and raise their children. Having "been there done that," I understand the desire to be successful in business and pursue personal goals, while balancing a home, personal life, and children. My book, "The Stay-at-Home Mom's Guide to Successful eBay Selling," (Amazon), is a primer for helping moms learn how to earn an income from home, using eBay.

While networking on the internet, I've met countless intelligent, talented, and business savvy moms who have chosen to be a mom first, and a business person second. I support this choice 100% and hope to provide resources to make this possible for other women.

My (pre-internet) professional background is in the areas of banking and marketing. After spending 10 years with companies like Wachovia Bank and Equifax, I chose to stay at home and raise my children and pursue small business opportunities as our schedule allowed. The advent of the internet has facilitated home business opportunites beyond my wildest dreams.

I am an active eBay Power Seller, professional blogger, affiliate marketer, and heavily involved in many women's business networking groups. I enjoy using the internet to connect with people all over the world. I thrive on social marketing and contributing to social websites for the purpose of self-improvement and broadening my horizons, as well as for meeting interesting new people.
