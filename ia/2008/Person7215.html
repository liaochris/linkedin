Alan Fagen’s Summary

Circulation exec with hands-on experience in magazines, data bases, newsletters and web sites. Experienced in circulation and audience development of paid and controlled, weekly, bi-monthly and monthly magazines and newsletters. Comfortable using circulation models, planning, managing and reporting on product or promotion operations with ROI always the driving component.

Alan Fagen’s Specialties:

Believe in continually testing to continually improve direct marketing responses – in order to adapt to marketplace changes.
