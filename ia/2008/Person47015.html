David Lapin’s Summary

Helping companies grow their value by differentiating their offering and optimizing the contributions of Teams to the company's Purpose. We build high performance cultures; and develop leaders who can attract, retain and engage talent. 20 years of international experience in Financial Sevices, Manufacturing, Retail, Mining and Hospitality. Speacialized in Values and Ethics as drivers of performance, particularly in environments of cultural diversity.

David Lapin’s Specialties:

Building high performing diverse teams in hyper-competitive industries.

David Lapin’s Experience

  • Chairman

    YSA-LAPIN (Pty) Ltd. and SBE, Inc

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    July 1989 — Present (18 years 8 months)

    Our consulting and coaching firm operates in the USA as Strategic Business Ethics, Inc, and in South Africa as YSA-LAPIN (Pty.) Ltd. We consult to large and medium sized organizations in the Public and Private sectors in the US, Europe, Asia and South Africa. We design and articulate strategy, build culuture and develop leadership. Our measures are bottom-line focused.

  • Founder and Lecturer

    iAwaken.org

    (Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; E-Learning industry)

    1974 — Present (34 years)

    iAwaken.org is a living library of audio shiurim and written Torah Essays by Rabbi David Lapin. Rabbi Lapin's unique life at the intersection of modern international business and authentic Torah teaching, positions him to teach life changing and compelling principles from the Torah and provides tools to impact your business, professional and personal lives. Through iAwaken.org, Rabbi Lapin provides foundational principles by which to live and teaches you how to extract these principles from your own learning of Torah and Talmud.
