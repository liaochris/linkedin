Nick Jiwa’s Summary

For the past 23 years, Nick has focused on call center outsourcing, customer care, direct marketing, sales and business development. From the inception of the call center industry Nick worked as a customer service rep, operations and client manager and eventually promoted to Executive management. Nick has a grassroots understanding of contact centers and their role at every stage of the customer lifecycle. Prior to establishing CustomerServ, Nick held senior positions for call center and BPO firms where he was directly responsible for generating hundreds of millions in revenue. Throughout Nick’s career in the contact center industry, he’s earned a reputation of excellence, reliability and innovation. Many of Nick's clients discovered new outsourcing channels and improved the customer experience through his guidance, personal management and no-nonsense approach. Nick has always remained ahead of the curve on industry trends and specializes in emerging-first-to-market opportunities.

CustomerServ provides guidance, clarity, current and real-world solutions in terms of outsourcing suppliers and destinations. We have facilitated outsourcing projects spanning 15 countries throughout 5 continents. CustomerServ specializes in domestic, near-shore and off-shore contact centers that provide an excellent customer experience. We have a vast knowledge base including the tangible and intangible aspects for supplier evaluation, transcending the brick, mortar and technology to the human component.

Our clients include small to mid sized corporations to many of the Fortune 100. We provide value to the decision making process regardless of how new or robust your outsourcing engine is. CustomerServ has dramatically impacted the bottom line for companies that are redeploying work domestically, looking for new solutions near shore / offshore or just exploring ideas and recommendations.

Nick Jiwa’s Specialties:

CustomerServ and our suppliers have expertise in customer care, help desk, tech support, teleservices, collections, travel and hospitality, third party verification, bilingual, non-profit, e-commerce, chat, Email and more. New markets, vendors and innovations are constant. We have a real-world approach to outsourcing challenges and we look forward to helping you deliver a world class customer experience by selecting the right outsourcing partners and destinations!
