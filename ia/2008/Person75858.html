Heather Hedden’s Summary

Taxonomist, indexer, and content management specialist, providing contract services and also seeking full time employment.

Design and develop taxonomies and controlled vocabularies for information retrieval and information architecture. Additionally write indexes for books and web sites.

Also each online workshops in taxonomy creation and web site A-Z index creation through Simmons College Graduate School of Library and Information Science. http://www.simmons.edu/gslis/continuinged/workshops/online.shtml

Recently wrote a book, "Indexing Specialties: Web Sites," published by Information Today Inc. in May 2007.
