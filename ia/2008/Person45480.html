Dave Tomaselli’s Summary

SR1 is an all-in-one business management solution designed specifically for independent record labels. The system is currently in use at over 25 internationally recognized labels including Mute Records, Stomp Entertainment, Defend Music, ESL Music, Ghostly International, Tommy Boy Records, AnD Press, Rasa Music, Decon Media, Wave Music, Om Records, King Street Sounds, Vega Records, and many more.

SR1 is an internet-based record label management system that supports and streamlines every aspect of today's forward-looking music companies. SR1 incorporates over twenty years of industry-specific expertise in the areas of royalty accounting, sales management, licensing, production, marketing, and promotions.

SR1 was built for labels by labels with a focused mission to offer the independent music community an intuitive and affordable tool for managing their day to day operations while lowering costs and liabilities, increasing revenue, increasing brand recognition, and freeing up valuable time to develop more meaningful and productive relationships with their artists, staff, business partners, and fans.

SR1 was born from Dave’s 15+ years of trials, tribulations, and successes in the independent music business, including owning and operating the independent record label Statra Recordings (ADA) from 1999 - Present. In 2004 Joe provided business consulting support to Dave and Statra, leveraging his 23+ years of technology, consulting and legal experience to analyze the business and make strategic and operational recommendations. Together, Joe and Dave have broken down and analyzed the complex business processes and challenges that todays independent record labels face to create an innovative product that speaks the “language” of an Indie label, and supports all the critical business processes required to successfully run an independent music company.
