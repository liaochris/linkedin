Travis Vocino’s Summary

I am a South Florida based artist, designer, new media scientist, technologist and chocolate milk aficionado.

I’ve been publishing on the web since at least 1995 — which is a good portion of my life, to be sure.

My technology focus is mostly centered around designing and organizing data. This includes technology business direction, consulting and other immersive techbiz behavior. While my involvement is more executive-oriented (some friends call me “suit”), I do enjoy creating simple and attractive web interfaces — practicing web standards.

One of the best complete albums of all time is Check Your Head, although the Richard D. James Album will change your life.

In addition to internet studies, I am rather passionate about science — especially theoretical physics — and spend a good amount of time reading on the subject. I like art, too.

I’ve been known to fly to a city just to go peruse a museum for the day.
