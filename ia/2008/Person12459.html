Andy Randall’s Summary

Andy was a founder and the CEO of i-GRasp which he built from start-up to European market leader in just five years. In this role, he led the delivery of e-recruitment solutions for many major organizations, including Cadbury Schweppes, PricewaterhouseCoopers, and AstraZeneca.

i-GRasp were acquired by StepStone in July 2005, at which point Andy became CTO and took overall responsibility for StepStone’s technology strategy, products and operations.

Andy has more than 20 years experience in delivering innovative, technology solutions, with a focus on the recruitment industry. Andy spent four years at director level with Heidrick & Struggles, the leading global recruitment firm, where he was elected a Partner, holding the roles of Global Chief Development Officer and CIO Europe. Earlier in his career he held senior roles with BP, Citibank and Du Pont.

Andy has a BSc in Mathematics, an MBA sponsored by BP, and is fluent in French, having spent 4 years living in Paris.
