Keith McMillan, CISSP’s Summary

Keith is a software development professional with 20 years of expertise in the specification, design, development, deployment and security of object-oriented, distributed, real-time and message passing systems. He has extensive experience with delivery of multi-million dollar systems. He is a Sun Microsystems Certified Enterprise Architect for J2EE, and formerly an IBM/Rational certified instructor for both Rational Unified Process and Rational Object Oriented Analysis and Design.

He has experience in the ITIL services management, medical equipment, financial, insurance and telecommunications industries. He is experienced with working with off-shore and on-shore remote teams.

Keith has specific knowledge and expertise in the area of computer security and cryptography, including protecting and managing Internet connected and other high-value systems, such as financial and telecommunication provisioning systems. He holds a CISSP certification from (ISC)2.

Keith is a strategic and analytical thinker with a careful, deliberative nature, capable of anticipating risks and planning accordingly. He has broad experience with staffing and managing software development organizations, software development processes and standards, software requirements specification, and object-oriented software architecture.
