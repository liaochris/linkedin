Robert Fort’s Summary

An internationally recognized and awarded IT leader and innovator with over 25 years of progressive Information Technology development and Senior/C-level management experience. Champion and implementor of some of the earliest and most successful ERP (SAP R/2 and R/3), business intelligence, retail kiosk, voice-over-IP (VoIP), and system management implementations. A successful business and IT strategist; leader of large-scale package implementations and custom application developments; advocate of scalable, reliable, cost-effective network infrastructures. A skilled consensus builder; business and ROI focused; highly innovative and motivational, with excellent communication skills and professional presence.

Robert Fort’s Specialties:

Visionary, innovator, strategist, leader, communicator, applied technologist with experience in the Retail, Film/Record Entertainment, Hospitality, Consumer Package Goods, Electronics Manufacturing, Banking, Shipping, Computer Services industries.
