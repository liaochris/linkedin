Karin Buys’s Summary

As a Program and Project Manager Karin offers a broad advising and operational experience in the field of Human Resources.
As a Program Manager Karin is able to manage and balance the right project portfolio with the right project methodology.
Karin can thanks to her broad experience coach and support her client in a wide range of areas: program definition and management, project approach, business roadmap and process workshops, business solution design, development, test strategy and planning, data conversion strategy and approach, transition, roll-out and change management (communication, training, documentation and process improvement).
She was the Project Manager at several large PeopleSoft HRMS/HCM projects in both the private and public sector, local and international.
She was involved in feasibility studies, business case development, market searches, several package and provider selection projects in the domains of personnel administration and payroll, transformation and implementation projects. As an experienced Project Manager Karin participated in many knowledge sharing activities for the teams of consultants and clients she worked with.
She was involved in several extensive pré-sales activities, the development of service offerings, the making of proofs of concept, building and presenting proposals and extensive demonstrations.
Karin is very familiar with the bidding process, RFI’s and RFP’s, pré- and sales processes, negotiations for many different types of contracts, SLA’s and business deals.
Based upon her extensive experience working with people from all levels in the organization Karin was able to train and coach many individuals and groups on all communications and project management aspects needed to enhance efficiency and effectiveness. Her aim is to enthuse, coach and support her clients based upon her broad in depth and hands-on experiences making sure that the knowledge transfer is embedded in every assignment.
