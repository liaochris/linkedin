Richard Kimber(rkimber@rhklegal.cn)’s Summary

Qualified 1986 and became a partner at Stephenson Harwood in May 2002. Has worked over 14 years in Asia, the first 7 years in Hong Kong and 7 years in Shanghai to manage the Stephenson Harwood Shanghai office.Commenced practice as RHK Legal in early 2007. Wide range of experience of advising clients in the areas of commercial real estate investment, ship finance and transportation, logistics,trade and distribution, banking and trade finance and PRC foreign direct investment.He has over 18 years experience practising in the UK, Australia and the Asia Pacific Region and is an Australian, Hong Kong and UK admitted and registered lawyer.
