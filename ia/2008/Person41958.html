Christopher Yates’s Summary

Mr. Christopher Yates is the Director of Sales on the East Coast at 1020, Inc. 1020 is headquartered in SanFrancisco, CA. Mr. Yates is responsible for launching 1020's East Coast office located in New York City where he is also directly responsible for revenue generation for 1020's Placecast.

Prior to joining 1020/Placecast, Mr. Yates was the Sr. Vice President of Sales & Business Development at Precision Prospects, Inc. where he was directly responsible for Precision Prospects’ revenue generation efforts as well as the day-to-day operations of the company’s sales organization. He remains on the Board of Advisors today.

His extensive sales & leadership experience spans 18 years in print and online sales, with leading media companies such as Ziff Davis, Viacom, Primedia, Scholastic, Weider Publications & Wenner Media.

Mr. Yates joined Precision Prospects in July of 2005 to launch the business and oversee all aspects of Sales & Business Development within the IT/Business Technology, Finance & Health channels. Since joining, he had brought Precision Prospects from the launch stage to generating more than $3 million in revenue in his first year.

Prior to Precision Prospects, he was responsible for managing and training salespeople at Ziff Davis Media while selling network advertising across PC Magazine and 7 other computer publication titles. Mr. Yates was also responsible for sales at Yahoo Internet Life Magazine. He received high recognition while at Ziff Davis and was awarded for his sales and leadership skills.

Mr. Yates was also instrumental in launching Nick Jr. Magazine, Nick.com & NickJr.com for Viacom/MTV Networks and increased revenues from $400,000 to $3,000,000. He also managed a national sales team and developed and launched STMN.com at Scholastic, Inc. and drove print and online advertising revenues at Wenner Media, Primedia and Weider Publications where he was also directly involved in the launch of Jump Magazine.

Christopher Yates’s Experience

  • Director, Sales

    1020, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    March 2008 — Present (9 months)

    1020 is a media company dedicated to location-enabling online advertising across all devices and networks—Wi-Fi, fixed broadband and mobile.  
     
    1020 operates Placecast, the world's largest place-based advertising network. Placecast is powered by 1020’s proprietary engine and provides the first ad platform to use supply chain concepts to manage the perishable nature of user location and ad impressions. Placecast delivers location-enabled media across web, WAP, video, and downloadable mobile GPS applications.  
     
    Customers and partners such as FedEx Kinko’s, Amtrak, CBS, and the world's largest advertising agencies rely on Placecast to create uniquely relevant advertising campaigns through PlaceAds™. PlaceAds combine three elements:  where the ad is seen, when it is seen, and information about that location to determine audience. Privacy issues are circumvented because Placecast sees only the user’s position; it has no way to determine the person’s identity.
