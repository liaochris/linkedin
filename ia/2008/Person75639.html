Heather Christensen’s Summary

My past experience involved working in smaller companies with fairly open job descriptions. I have participated in the writing of my own job description, and worked at building several small companies from inception, to market entry strategy, fund-raising and wind-down. I have directly participated in the areas of finance and accounting, marketing, strategy, and the legal aspects of forming a company. My goal is to leverage my business education and experience to work toward sustainability in the textile and fashion industries.
