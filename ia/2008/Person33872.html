Charlie Wood’s Summary

With a CS degree, a decade of programming experience, and an entrepreneurial bent, I started my career as a software engineer. I gradually moved into more customer-facing roles, first as a Systems Engineer (Sales Engineer), and later as an Account Executive. My exposure to both technical and customer considerations later led me to product management, but I never lost interest in software development itself, and I eventually returned to my roots, developing online services to extend the functionality of Salesforce.com and Google applications.

I'm happiest doing what I do best, which is building bridges (hence the name of my company, Spanning). Whether it's explaining the benefits of technology to a nontechnical audience, introducing technologists to new platforms, or building integrations between applications, I enjoy and excel at making connections.

Charlie Wood’s Specialties:

Technology evangelism, enterprise applications of Web 2.0 technologies, RSS/Atom, OPML, GData, global nanocompanies
