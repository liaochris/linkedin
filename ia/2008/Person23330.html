Bill Hornaday’s Summary

Recruiting exec with broad experience in search and recruiting,
client and project management.

10 years of recruiting industry experience. Including development of custom recruiting solutions, diversity recruiting initiatives, technology upgrades and greenfield startups.

MBA from the Terry College of Business at the University of Georgia in 1992. Prior to recruiting career, pharmaceutical sales for Eli Lilly and US Naval Officer.

Cambridge Professional Group is a full service search and staffing company – recognized as one of Atlanta’s top 10 staffing companies by the Atlanta Business Chronicle – specializing in the recruitment and placement of legal, financial, technical and office support professionals. We work in “professional specialty teams", meaning our associates focus on the particular professions that we know best. Our recruiting specialists average over 9 years of staffing experience – more than any other agency in Atlanta – and our firm has serviced clients for almost two decades.
