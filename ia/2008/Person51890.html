Don DiPalma’s Summary

Extensive sales and sales management background with over twenty years experience in the staffing industry. Joined KeyTech Staffing, a division of Arrowpoint Technologies, to spearhead the companies growth in the Southern Connecticut, Westchester, and Tri-State area. KeyTech has broad capabilities to provide contract and full time staffing services across several professional disciplines: IT, Accounting & Finance, and Engineering. As a division of Arrowpoint Technologies, we also handle outsourced IT projects to be developed offshore and managed locally. Our Lynchval Systems division provides pension administration and processing software for mid-sized companies including municipalities, school systems, colleges, and universities.

Don DiPalma’s Specialties:

With my twenty years experience in the contract and full time staffing industry, I can draw on my broad experience to develop a services agreement or program to suit client requirements including full time hires, contract resources, and right-to-hire individuals.
