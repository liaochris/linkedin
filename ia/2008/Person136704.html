Matthew Cozzi, PMP’s Summary

I am an IT executive with global business and international IT experience that has been developed on a solid educational foundation of accounting and engineering. I have track record of delivering exceptional and innovative results for clients and employers. I have planning and hands-on technical experience in a variety of client engagements and industries that allows me to collaborate with business and technical leaders to drive results from strategies. I thrive on challenges and seek common-sense innovations that bring real business value for clients. My experience includes being:

 An innovative IT strategist with 6+ years of experience designing and adapting business models and strategies into IT strategies that leverage service-orientation for rapid deployment and close loop planning in the strategic planning cycle. These business/IT strategies anticipate emerging and changing market conditions that facilitate programming the IT technologies to meet business and economic windows of opportunities.

 An strategic and hands-on enterprise architect with 7+ years of experience collaborating with clients to design, build and document the business, application, data and infrastructure architectures that are built using service oriented principles for a adaptive computing models. The result is a service oriented enterprise that a business agility and focuses on the business value chain elements that drive the greatest ROI.

 A certified project manager with over 8+ years experience managing the projects through their full lifecycle in ERP, SCM, PLM, BI, applications. I have managed portal, EAI & EII technology projects and; projects that focus on specialties such as system consolidation, custom development, outsourcing initiatives.

I excel in enterprises that value an IT executive with a blend of business, IT and engineering backgrounds who is passionate about excellence and will deliver tangible business results which are value-oriented and innovative.
