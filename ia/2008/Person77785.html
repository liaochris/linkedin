Howard Beech’s Summary

Fourteen years launching new products for growing companies in categories as varied as photo, toys, video games, pc software, home security and in-store television. Five years at Mattel prior to that with a concentration on leading edge technology products.

Involved in several successful start up/early stage companies. Involved in a few that struggled. There's great learning in both that can be shared.

Success generating broad multi-channel distribution with a strong track record in big box retailers.

Howard Beech’s Specialties:

Development of sales, marketing and product launch plans for new retail products and categories. Customized, creative presentations, highly effective PowerPoint skills, the ability to get to decision makers quickly and a very good close rate.

Howard Beech’s Experience

  • Senior Vice President, Sales

    Wordlock, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Consumer Goods industry)

    August 2007 — Present (7 months)

    Responsible for sales of this newly formed company that provides a family of combination locks that are the first to allow consumers to set their combination using words NOT numbers. Words are easy to remember, numbers are easy to forget and Wordlock® products are unforgettable.

    These products (padlocks, bike locks and travel locks) are the next generation in personal security with distribution capabilities in all major channels. We expect Wordlock to redefine this category in ways never imagined, growing the business substantially for our online and retail partners.

  • Director of Licensing and Entertainment Marketing

    Qualex, Inc., a Kodak Company

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; Consumer Services industry)

    July 2007 — Present (8 months)

    Working as a consultant to this expanding division of the Kodak company to develop business and marketing relationships with sports, entertainment and corporate partners looking to license their brands and content for development of "print-on-demand" services and personalized photo products.

    Partners utilize the Qualex distribution network of over 33,000 retail partners and top online sites such as Kodak Easy Share Gallery to offer their brands for purchase on a variety of specialized products without the need to carry inventory.

    Qualex manages the development of white label and co-branded web sites and provides fulfillment of goods and services utilizing a wholesale model in which partners set their retail prices and keep the margins as their profit.

  • Co-Founder, VP Sales

    PhotoTLC

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Photography industry)

    February 2002 — February 2007 (5 years 1 month)

    Co-Founded and ran sales for the leading supplier of personalized photo gifts and custom photo restorations for the retail channel. Sales in 2006 were $17MM.

    Secured distribution in over 22,000 stores including Wal-Mart, Walgreens, CVS, Meijer, Rite Aid, Albertsons, and others. Became recognized leader this space.

    Established key technology relationships with kiosk providers; Fuji, Kodak/Qualex, Pixology, Lucidiom and Storefront.

    Created completely turnkey program including marketing, in-store POP and worldclass customer service.

    Developed and managed licensing relationships with NFL, Major League Baseball and the NBA for new lines of customized, personalized product.

    Identified critical new product introductions including personalized Disney character plates and laser etched photo crystals. These two lines accounted for 15% of revenue.

    Created original product line designed to increase distribution of photo gifts to reach over 40,000 stores.

  • Vice President, Sales

    Lasershield Systems, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Consumer Electronics industry)

    October 2001 — February 2004 (2 years 5 months)

Positioned as the first wireless, portable, monitored home security system, Lasershield has revolutionized the alarm business by eliminating the barriers that exist for over 40million US homes that are unprotected with a growing base in International markets.

    In addition to management responsibilities, worked alongside the CEO to raise millions of dollars in private equity from friends and family and have remained an advisor to the company through present day.
