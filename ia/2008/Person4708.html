A.J. McCann (aj@mccann.cc)’s Summary

Aaron “A.J.” McCann has gained over 14 years expertise in management and business development with both early stage and established companies alike. Responsible for guiding the business and technology developments for multiple companies as they’ve entered into new vertical markets, McCann has repeatedly demonstrated his corporate leadership skills in both the small and large business sectors.

McCann has cultivated and launched several IPO’s, including HIMC (OTC:HMCO), ClickPay (OTC:CKPY) and SimpleCash. He was responsible for raising over ten million dollars for HIMC, creating and introducing two multi-million dollar subsidiaries, fostering the introduction of four new product categories, and leading each business entity into its respective market.

In addition to experience with small and start-up corporations, McCann also held executive level positions with Fortune 500 companies. For Wells Fargo Bank (NYSE:WFC), he was head of the emerging payments division and he also served as the payment services director for Electronic Data Systems (NYSE:EDS).
McCann also has proven experience as a green energy sector executive, with an extensive background in renewable energy projects throughout the United States.

He is currently executive director of SwitchingGears, a growing environmental non-profit foundation and green fund. His present focus is on Wind, Solar, Biomass, Plasma Gasification, Hydrogen, BioDiesel, and Ethanol projects of all sizes and types, although he is well-positioned to further expand his portfolio in this promising US market.

McCann has had the rare privilege of serving in a variety of corporate settings and supporting a diverse set of emerging products. This experience has allowed him to repeatedly prove his proficiency at identifying sales channel opportunities, developing appropriate funding options and crafting business strategies that have consistently produced results-driven impact and bottom line progression.
