Dave Saunders’s Summary

Senior finance & operations executive with broad cross-functional & global experience in finance, strategy and operations for technology, consumer products service & manufacturing businesses.

Highlights of my experience that distinguish me from other executives include:
• Growth & Opportunity Focus - Identified & capitalized upon market opportunities to drive growth & expansion including working with key strategic alliance partners like Dell & IBM &leading reseller acquisitions in emerging markets.
• Business Model Approach – Developed profitable & sustainable business models for entry into business process outsourcing and document workflow services markets, new products such as software, entry into new channels and emerging markets.
• Operations Passion & Experience – Partnered with and delivered results across all critical management functions – strategic planning, finance, marketing, product development, supply chain, services & global sales teams.
• Business Transformation – Led business transformation initiatives supporting strategy & cost reduction initiatives. Developed systems, accounting process, controls & performance measurement for entry into services businesses to facilitate growth & expanding R&D & manufacturing platforms to low cost geographies & the deployment of shared service centers to reduce cost.

I am passionate about aligning the finance function, information & analytics with strategy & embedding my team deeply into all operations of the business to provide leadership & independent perspective & guide decisions. I have built business models, established supporting accounting and controls infrastructure & created key performance indicators and management tools to support business transformation & changes in strategy allowing the business to capitalize on opportunities in new markets & product segments.

I have just relocated to the Phoenix metropolitan area.
