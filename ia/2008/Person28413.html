Bruce Judson’s Experience

  • Senior Adviser

    Overall Nutritional Quality Index (ONQI

    (Health, Wellness and Fitness industry)

    November 2007 — Present (11 months)

    The Overall Nutritional Quality Index (ONQI) is a new health-related food ranking system that was developed by a team of the nation's leading nutrition scientists. (See www.ONQI.com) The ONQI was developed in stealth mode and unveiled at a conference in Washington, D.C. on Nov. 30, 2007. It was quickly endorsed in an editorial (both in print and online) by "USA Today." (See http://blogs.usatoday.com/oped/2007/12/grocery-cheat-s.html) A fully funded private company has been established to commercialize the ONQI. It will soon appear (with tools and content) on selected Web sites, in supermarkets nationwide, and on manufacturer packaging.

  • Founder

    Search Free Apps.com

    (Internet industry)

    September 2007 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    Search Free Apps.com (www.SearchFreeApps.com) is an innovative vertical search engine for small businesses, nonprofits, home offices, freelancers, and entrepreneurs. The engine searches over 500 hand-picked services that provide high quality free products and services across the range of Internet activities. The service provides an easy way to instantly find sophisticated free services, Users are, in general, surprised at the high quality of the services included in Search Free Apps, and the range of sophisticated services that can be found in the engine.
