Chris Graham’s Summary

Manager at an emerging accounting/audit/forensic firm, "LMD Africa", heading up the Eastern and Western Cape area to grow and develop the forensic practice. Prior to this, I was associated with the Forensic & Dispute Services division of Deloitte & Touche - South Africa. Before Deloitte, I worked at two different life assurance companies in the risk management environment, namely African Life and Sage Life. Prior to this, I was involved in law enforcement for a number of years.
