David Torres’s Summary

Mr. Torres is the Enterprise Application Practice Director for Avanade (US-West Region), a joint venture company formed by Accenture and Microsoft in March 2000. Mr. Torres’ focus is on Enterprise Architecture, Business Intelligence and Information Worker solutions implemented on the Microsoft platform using SQL Server 2005 (Database Engine, Analysis Services, Integration Services and Reporting Services), Office SharePoint Server and BizTalk Server.

Mr. Torres has been in the Information Technology industry since 1984 and has been accredited as a Microsoft Certified Professional since 1995 holding certifications as a Microsoft Certified System Engineer, Microsoft Certified Solution Developer and Microsoft Certified Database Administrator.

    (Public Company; 201-500 employees; Retail industry)

    April 1997 — April 1999 (2 years 1 month)

    As the Director of Development for SkyMall, Mr. Torres directed a team of 30 developers in the design, development and deployment of e-commerce solutions based on Windows NT, Microsoft Commerce Server, SQL Server, ASP, ISAPI and VB/COM. Mr. Torres and the SkyMall team has constructed e-commerce sites for SkyMall Inc, its subsidiary Durham Company and several branded partners sites for companies such as Lycos, The Globe, MyFamily.com, United States Postal Service, Delta Airlines, Continental Airlines, United Airlines, Marriott Hotels and Resorts, and the Carlson Companies.

With the migration to a co-location facility the sites were configured to run behind a Cisco Local Director to provide load balancing and fault tolerance for web traffic. The sites were also configured to use mirrored SQL servers providing data fault tolerance and ad hoc reporting.
