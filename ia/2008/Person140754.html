Nick Twyman’s Summary

I have enjoyed a fast track international career with over twenty years in technology industries.

I became Managing Director of Tektronix UK Ltd. at 32 and was responsible for a business of $150m and an organisation of 300 people in eight countries. I became a divisional Managing Director at SONY firstly in the UK then Europe and finally at a Global level.

I am driven to achieve results. I managed a turnaround at Tektronix returning the companies core business to growth and profitability well ahead of the market whilst investing in the development of two strategic new business streams. Through an innovatively managed restructuring and refocusing of the business and organisation the operation was turned around in eighteen months. Then as part of the European leadership team I led a number of change programmes and managed the French and German subsidiaries on an interim basis and supported the establishment of a European HQ in Munich.

I was then recruited to lead a significant change project to restructure the £120m Broadcast & Professional business for SONY as Managing Director in the UK and achieved record growth and profits in the third year of leadership. Concurrently I led a European business for SONY building a team that moved from 6th in terms of market share to market leader in 18 months. I then became the first person in SONY to carry responsibility for a business sector on a Global basis.

All through my career I have been fascinated and amazed by people, and I have noticed the difference when individuals feel truly engaged. I believe my success in business has been my ability to build relationships and motivate people as individuals and in teams. I truly believe that people make a difference! I have a personal passion for the development of people, and during the latter years of my corporate career took enormous delight in seeing individuals take back control of their lives and their careers and reach their full potential.
