Michelle Ogden, CRPS®, CFP®’s Summary

Our Firm handles Employer Sponsored Retirement Plans. As you may already know, the majority of Retirement Plans are “Sold”. Our firm does not sell but instead, shows the Plan Sponsor their options. We have found our niche in making the options easy to understand, the costs clear, and hopefully if we did a good job, the choice obvious. Our goal is to help Plan Sponsors make an informed decision, even if the overall decision is not to go with our firm. We are not a big fans of annuity based or commission based retirement plans because of the “revenue sharing” that goes on. There is nothing wrong with “revenue sharing” as long as it is disclosed, and mutually understood. It is our objective to provide exceptional service at a reasonable cost to the Plan Sponsor (aka Business Owner). Many Plan Sponsors do not understand their role as Fiduciary in a retirement plan and are putting their companies at risk. Retirement plans should NOT be taken lightly. The Plan Sponsor's responsibilities are ongoing and complex. A Retirement Plan should be reviewed annually evaluating fees, investment performance, & plan participation. Retirement Plans are our specialty and the majority of our business is in that area of expertise. We do work with individuals in the area of financial planning on a case by case basis. The clients we have taken onboard with us has been mostly because we really like them and would love to be involved with them for the Long Term.
