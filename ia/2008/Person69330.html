Gerry Booth’s Summary

Currently, CEO of TitleTrac Solutions (Pandimo, Inc.) an online workflow company providing payoff process management and title transfer solutions (www.titletrac.com) for the automotive industry.

Previous experiences are wide ranging and in reverse chronological order;
• VP of Business Technology Group, HSBC Auto Finance. Leading initial introduction efforts in online customer self-service solutions, and improvement efforts related to IT Governance, Project Portfolio Management, (PPM), Enterprise Investment Management, and, Loan Acquisition and Portfolio Management processes.
• COO, GEM Associates, an airline operations process improvement company driven by web-based software insertion.
• Senior Director of Business Integration and Consulting Services for Spirent Systems, a US based supply-chain, engineering and maintenance, and enterprise asset management software provider.
• Senior Application Architect for WPDS Inc, a US MRO and Supply Chain company providing corporate solutions to worldwide defense contractors and aviation companies.
• Director of Operations (Europe) and Global Enterprise Solutions Architect for WPDS Software Ltd, a UK based enterprise software development and Services Company.
• Manager, Change Programs with Andersen Consulting (now Accenture)
• Air Engineering Officer in the UK Royal Navy

Always interested in challenging executive management and IT consulting opportunities.

Gerry Booth’s Specialties:

eBusiness, (online process collaboration, supply chain and information portals)
Portfolio Management & Loan Acquisition Solutions (Auto Loans), IT Governance, Program / Project Management, Enterprise Investment Management, Quality Systems, Requirements Analysis, Application Design, Software Assessment & Selection, Business Process Analysis, Change Management, Purchase / Leasing, Supply Chain, Remanufacturing, Planning and Scheduling, Engineering Data Management, Maintenance Execution.
