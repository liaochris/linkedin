Neal Roscoe’s Summary

Neal Roscoe is a “free agent”, producing events, web experiences, presentations and other creative projects for networks, entertainment and communications companies.

Neal’s specialty is being able to work with, and bring multiple mediums (online, graphics, video, talent, lighting/sound, signage, music, physical locations, etc.) together.

Neal Roscoe’s Specialties:

• Working with senior executives and talent.
• Understanding branding and how to tie it into every aspect of a project.
• Ability to build scripts or rundowns for a show.
• Proposal, business and report-writing skills.
• Working with art directors, graphic designers & printers.
• Fundamental knowledge of video control room operations.
• Music selection and integration.
