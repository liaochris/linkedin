Sumeet Mehta’s Summary

A Chartered Accountant by qualification and an equity research analyst turned investment banker by profession, I bring with me a rich and rounded experience in equity research, investment banking, debt syndication, and tax and corporate laws, whcih is as under:

- Equity Research: Analysing metals and mining, power (utilities), engineering, construction, infrastructure, logistics, shipping, and real estate sectors. I have worked on analysing risks and opportunities in the sectors, identifying investment grade companies in these sectors, preparing financial models and research reports on companies, interacting with fund managers on investment ideas, understanding investment strategies, and doing all related activities like investor conferences, roadshows, earnings calls, conference calls, etc.

- Investment Banking and Corporate Finance: Advising companies in raising equity and debt. Currently, I work on capital raising for real estate developers, and also work closely with Real Estate Private Equity Funds in their fund raising program.

- Articleship: Worked on tax and corporate laws, tax planning, designing corporate structures, auditing - statutory, internal, systems, designing systems for companies, and debt syndication.

Sumeet Mehta’s Specialties:

Understanding valuations, relationship with fund managers and bankers, indepth industry expertise, and indepth understanding on structuring inbound investment.
