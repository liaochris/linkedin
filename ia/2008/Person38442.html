Eric Rosen’s Summary

Top-notch copywriter delivers results in tech, retail and financial services. He makes value propositions clear and uses time-proven principles of persuasion to reinforce them. Skilled at building relationships with senior managers, he helps everyone focus on benefits over features. His copy works online and in print.

Results achieved for the clients listed here are shown below under my current firm, Clear Crisp Communications.

Clients include...

* eBay – a world leader in online sales of electronics, fashion, collectibles and vehicles

* Smith Barney – Wealth Management Certified Financial Planners serving portfolios $2MM to $50MM

* Trend Micro – a fast-growing player in PC security

* HubSpot – a leading SaaS helping SMBs to master SEO and Internet marketing

* Jigsaw – a Web 2.0 provider of business contact data

* SHOP.COM – e-commerce site presenting thousands of retailers to users of a single shopping cart

Eric Rosen’s Specialties:

* Turn data and anecdotes into stories that sell

* Manage multiple projects under tight deadlines and elicit valuable insights from business leaders that enhance the message-to-market match

* Express the value propositions of complex products and services in terms anyone can understand and embrace

* Drive sales, leads and clicks with overt and covert persuasion in all forms of online and print media
