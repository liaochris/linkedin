Craig Keefner’s Summary

Working in self-service and kiosk technology for last 15 years. Started first industry website for kiosk industry and founded the self-service association in existence today. Still on advisory board.

Major skills are lead generation via internet, sales process automation, self-service automation software and hardware, internet search engines, developing new hardware and software technology for use in our industry, and working with clients and sales people for best practice. Quote systems developed utilize mysql backend with php frontend.

Manage and develop software application partners as well as spec new hardware and devices for use in self-service terminals.

Assets include one of oldest/largest personal and electronic networks in self-service, kiosks and now thin clients. Relationships with complementary and competing companies. Oldtimer who'll eventually move on :-)
