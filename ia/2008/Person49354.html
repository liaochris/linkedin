Deirdre Walsh’s Summary

Since 2004, I've worked at Austin-based National Instruments, a technology pioneer and one of FORTUNE magazine's top 100 best companies to work for in America.

As the Technical Community Marketing Manager, I'm tasked with improving the online and offline experience for our community of engineers and scientists. I ensure the success of our software and hardware customers and attract new users to our network by enhancing user connectivity and collaboration. Additionally, I provide marketing strategy for "ecosystem" projects, including physical and virtual user groups, software partnerships, Web communication tools and enterprise platforms, rewards programs, our annual user conference, discussion forums, and NI Labs.

Prior to this role, I was the NI media relations and public affairs specialist. As the primary contact for local, business, and several trade media outlets, I built and maintained relationships with editors, strategically pitched and placed articles, and monitored competitors. Additionally, I counseled the crisis communications team, assisted with pubic affairs activities, led media training, and planned press events at tradeshows. In 2006, I began leading all “2010” marketing communications initiatives, including social networking, blogging, and RSS feeds. I also managed the public relations intern and mentored five media coordinators.

At Hill & Knowlton, I performed a variety of media relations, public affairs and Web communications tasks for high-profile accounts, including The Allstate Corporation, British Gas, Hewlett-Packard, the Houston-Galveston Area Council, the Houston Marathon, SABIC Americas, and The Welch Foundation. I also served as the office awards coordinator, editor for the global e-newsletter, and an associate on the Netcoms team.

In 2002, I graduated with a bachelor of science in public relations and minors in business and English from The University of Texas at Austin.

Deirdre Walsh’s Specialties:

Throughout my career, I have developed strong skills in verbal and written communications as well as organizational and analytical abilities. My job responsibilities have included the following: online communications, media relations, crisis communications, public affairs, employee communications, trade show coordination, and technical community outreach.
