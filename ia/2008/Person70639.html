Glade Luco’s Summary

I have a strong passion for what I call implied functionality. Like a basketball should bounce, and a football should spiral, computers and software should work the way you expect it to. I make that happen.

With 11 years of hands on experience developing, deploying and supporting software I enjoy playing a key role in an organization's success.
