Tony Ellison’s Summary

Founded in May of 1994 and profitable since 1997, Shoplet.com has positioned itself as a leading e-marketplace. Our award-winning web site functions as one of the world’s largest online office supply and office furniture superstore, with over 2.5 million subscribed users, and over 200,000 products and equipments for the office. Our specialty stores include Office Supplies, Computer Hardware and Peripherals, Networking Products, Office Furniture, Ink and Toner Supplies, Janitorial Supplies, Cleaning Supplies and Paper Center.

Shoplet.com Unique Selling Proposition to our customers include:

-Free Fast Next Day Delivery on Orders over $45
-110 % Price Guarantee.
- Easy No Hassle Returns.
- Largest selection of Business Products.
- Free hosted e-procurement solution.
- Better pricing across the board.
- Best In class customer Service.

Our award winning free e-procurement solution offers business streamline their purchasing process by introducing spend management controls such as setting budgets online per department or user, raising compliance by setting purchasing rules, setting approvedList™ and much more.
