Richard Birt’s Summary

An innovative Senior IT Service Delivery Manager/Technical Project Manager with experience of in-house & client services management within a variety of market sectors.

Experience of service management, IT architecture, networking, installation and management of hardware and software and provision of technical and networking support (voice and data), for both server and desktop solutions.

Designed and implemented service-desk support for both onsite and remote technical support. Established a proactive management style with client-facing solution generation and service provision, gained a good reputation within the business.

CAREER ACHIEVEMENTS INCLUDE:

Technical PM for the Virgin V ROOM which is a new passenger lounge at Gatwick Airport delivering integrated solution including digital signage systems and wide area connectivity.

Planned the recent acquisition of Fast Track Cruises into Virgin. Designed WAN/MPLS and Windows/Messaging Integration including the migration of 150 Users/Systems. Other responsibilities were the provision of a new server/comms room and liaising with the IP provider for VOiP solution

Planned and delivered 7 remote retail site solutions over Citrix for Virgin into Debenhams and Tesco. Recently re-designed a solution for reduced TCO and support overhead, which has resulted in an approved plan to rollout to an additional 50 sites.

Technical authoring and gained good reputation within the business for producing Visio schematics, drawings and comprehensive documentation resulting from surveys, these have facilitated the technical project management planning of all projects.

Improved upon the windows deployment services image process internally using bare metal pc and laptop imaging technology, also delivering recovery to dissimilar hardware.

Recruitment of Senior Technical personnel, from CV acceptance, through technical testing to Interview stage. Other requirements included management/team and leader qualities.
