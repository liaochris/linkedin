Michael Aagaard Andersen’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL PROFILE

Dynamic senior level management career providing operating, P&L, strategic and business leadership for international operations, divisions and brands generating in excess of USD 200 million annually. Experienced in start-up, high-growth and established corporate environments.

Consistently successful in identifying and capitalizing upon market opportunities to drive revenue growth and expand market penetration. Applied groundbreaking marketing tactics to secure a strong consumer response. Build world-class distribution organizations and channels. Retrieved ailing entities by radically restructuring the business model, all within short time frames.

Proven team builder and catalyst, recognized for the ability to drive performance within a culturally diverse leadership team, and a geographically dispersed workforce. A plainspoken, straightforward innovator and problem-solver. Fluent in Danish and English. Good working knowledge of German.


PROFESSIONAL SKILLS

Geographic Expansion: Built and rebuilt international operations in the capacity of CEO, COO, SBU, and Country Manager. Excels at driving performance within a geographically dispersed workforce and a culturally diverse leadership team

Turnaround and Change Management: Streamlined a number of business models by orienting all functions, processes, and products around customer requirements. Fired, hired and replaced both key and operational personnel.

Sales and Key Account Management: Passionate and effective with training and empowering sales teams as well as assisting the field to acquire and retain key customers. Strong channel development expertise with acquiring, training, and growing resellers, distributors, and major accounts.
