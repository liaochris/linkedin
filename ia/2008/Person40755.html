Craig Minor ▀▄▀▄▀▄▀▄▀▄▀▄▀’s Summary

I began CAM Media in 2003 with the idea of financing unique independent films. My 20 years experience makes me an expert in the entertainment industry and provides me a unique perspective on how to work in the films and television. My music industry experience ranges from A&M Records to Valley Media Distributors, as the independent label buyer and Antone's Records of Austin, TX as the Label manager. As a talent agent for Diverse Talent Group for Five years I established a broad network of professionals, negotiated numerous talent contracts and managed multiple careers. To broaden my prospective and film experience I move into Post Production and was involved in untold productions, until I became Head of Production and Film Financing at Kosmic Films, then the move to Echelon Studios as the VP Int'l and Domestic Sales .CAM Media, brokers film finance deals for independent films and partnering with film makers to add business and production experience to their creative process.
