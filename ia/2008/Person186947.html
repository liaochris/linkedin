Jorn Teutloff’s Summary

Jorn Teutloff is Vice President of Business Development, responsible for strategic alliances, channel partnerships, and key accounts at Koders Inc. Previously, Jorn co-founded JV-Global, a Los Angeles-based strategy consulting firm, and was engagement manager with the High Growth consulting practice at Capgemini / Ernst & Young where he led IT Strategy & Transformation engagements for start-up ventures and Fortune 500 companies in a variety of industries. Select previous clients include American Express, AT&T, BEA-Systems, Carlson Companies, Countrywide Financial, Farmers Insurance, Hewlett-Packard, Motorola, Pfizer and others.

Mr. Teutloff also served on Capgemini / Ernst & Young's Knowledge, Learning and Methodology Advisory Board to enhance practices in consulting methodology, knowledge management, and engagement structuring. In addition, he led the development and deployment of the practice's mobile/wireless business consulting methodology. Mr. Teutloff is co-author of "The Enterprise Guide to Gaining Business Value from Mobile Technologies" (Wiley 2004), and "The Cap Gemini Ernst & Young Guide to Wireless Application Architecture" (Wiley 2002).

Mr. Teutloff holds an MBA from the University of Southern California and a Bachelor's of Science degree in Business Administration with a minor in Economics from the University of Florida where he graduated summa cum laude.

Past/current clients include: American Express, AT&T, BEA Systems, BioRad Laboratories, Cacheon, Carlson Companies, Cisco Systems, Countrywide Financial, Dole Foods, Earle M. Jorgensen, Everypath, Farmers Insurance, Grubb & Ellis, Guthy-Renker, Harbor Freight Tools, Hewlett-Packard, International Creative Management, Konover Property Trust, Motorola, Nautica Enterprises, Pfizer Pharmaceuticals, Powerlift, Standard Register, University of Southern California, and others.
