Maria Agrell’s Summary

Maria Agrell works as a consultant helping companies with PR, Marketing and Project management.

She has worked several years in the E-business and Telecom industry in Silicon Valley and Stockholm as Director of Communication and Head of Marketing.

Maria has been involved in both rolling out start ups and working with change processes in more established larger companies.
