joe cuccinelli’s Summary

Joe has a well established track record with over 10 years of Product Management and Consulting experience. He has been employed with m-Qube for the past 18 months, where he gained an in-depth knowledge of mobile marketing and the mobile ecosystem. He currently is the product lead for m-Qube's mobile Gateway billing and messaging platform. From his experience to-date at m-Qube, he developed a good strategic and operational understanding of carriers, aggregators and content providers. He also has extensive experience in carrier messaging and billing systems, as well as the subscriber acquistion cycle (online affiliate marketing). In addition to the above, Joe has gained basic knowledge in mobile content transcoding and mobile web 2.0 during his tenure at m-Qube.

Prior to joining m-Qube, Joe spent 5 years at Upromise, where he led the product team in building consumer loyalty platforms for partners, such as Coca-Cola, AT&T, Citibank and a variety of other consumer brands. He was resposnible for gaining an understanding of the consumer brand's objectives and defining and delivering the product that would suit their needs. Some highlights during his time at Upromise included the national launch of a 25,000 grocery store loyalty network and deploying a turn-key auto-registration solution for Citibank.

Joe worked for Viant and Deloitte Consulting for the previous 3 years providing consulting services for E-Commerce and ERP solutions. Prior to consulting, Joe worked as an accountant at Deloitte & Touche. He graduated Seton Hall University with a BS in Accounting.
