Donna DeClemente’s Summary

DDC Marketing Group has been creating and executing exciting marketing communications programs over 25 years. We service both large and small businesses that include brands and organizations as well as agencies and other marketing services providers.

Our mission is to enhance our clients' success by collaborating with them to develop exciting promotional marketing programs that deliver measurable results.

What sets DDC Marketing apart is our ability to focus a wealth of brand-building marketing communications experience on every unique client situation, leveraging the extensive experience (averaging over 25 years) of its core partner team to deliver cost-effective solutions that deliver measurable results.

No two clients are alike and neither are the strategic partners that are aligned with DDC Marketing. We bring together the expertise that is just right for our client's needs. This means you receive high quality service at the best value. We are dedicated to providing superior customer service, attention to detail, flexibility in our processes, and of course first-rate creativity.

DDC has a unique specialization in promotional marketing including the administration and development of interactive promotions such as sweepstakes, games, and contests. Interactive promotions are one of the most powerful ways to motive behavior by connecting with an unidentified person and converting them into a registered prospect. This enables the start of a dialog for a permission-based, data-driven relationship.

With over 25 years of experience we make implementing chance to win promotions simple and easy for our clients. It's our responsibility to our clients to stay abreast of changes in the area of promotion law and sweepstakes legislation. We accomplish this by working with the best promotional law attorneys in the business, attending industry-sponsored promotional law conferences, and are actively involved in trade organizations such as the PMA.
