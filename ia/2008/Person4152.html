Anders Hemre’s Summary

I have extensive international experience in technology, management and C-level advisor roles as well as multi-cultural experience from living in Europe, the Middle East, the Far East and North America.
I’m specializing in strategic management, the art of innovation, mission based enterprise architectures and the use of knowledge and expertise in organizations.
Channeling creativity, innovation and competence into worthy effort, sustainable development as well as the use of technology to amplify human experience and the ability to accomplish are all things I find both interesting and challenging to pursue. I'm curious about the power of ideas and passionate about the liberation of human potential. What else excites me? Working with interesting people and progressive firms on fundamental issues, crossing boundaries, bridging distance and closing gaps.

In addition to management consulting services, I offer a series of short, focused Master Classes on innovation management, collaboration & knowledge sharing and learning expertise. Find out more at the web, blog and newsletter links under Additional Information below.

You will also find me on Xing (openBC), FastPitch, facebook and Skype.

Anders Hemre’s Specialties:

Creative thinking and visualization. Management consulting in business strategy, R&D management, business performance, quality management, process management, management systems, innovation and productivity improvement, human capital development, change management, intellectual capital management, community building, knowledge sharing, expertise management and business intelligence in high tech and telecommunications organizations.
