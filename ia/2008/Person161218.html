RJ Frampton’s Summary

Since 2001, I have led and supported textbook production teams as a writer and development editor for the publishing subsidiary the largest English language school in Japan. I have worked in positions of steadily increasing responsibility and was promoted to Assistant Section Chief in 2008. In addition to language-teaching textbooks, I have written and developed several picture books for young learners. Since the end of 2007, I have worked as a volunteer proofreader for the Japan Association for Language Teaching (JALT).

I now wish to offer my proofreading and editing services freelance. If you need editorial assistance with materials you are developing, I can certainly help you achieve your goals.
