David Johnson’s Summary

My working life seems to have revolved around several recurring and overlapping themes: technology, systemization (both in terms of automation and process engineering), and marketing. I am driven by passion for creating and delivering value, and helping those around me succeed. What does it all add up to? Well, if you can define it, my wife would sure like to know what I do for a living!

In the meantime, I stay busy helping small businesses expand their reach, grow revenues, and enhance profitability. This happens through a mixture of coaching, consulting, writing, training, and speaking.

David Johnson’s Specialties:

Systems design, corporate training, strategic planning, online & offline marketing, business development, and paradigm shifting.
