Mark Ballantyne’s Summary

Eagle Imagery™ is a world-class graphic design service specialising in logo design.

Eagle Imagery delivers distinctive, memorable and effective graphic design solutions enabling you to market your company or product more effectively.

Eagle Imagery can help you:

• Establish brand loyalty
• Retain brand loyalty
• Gain more clients
• Increase sales.

To view my portfolio of work, please use the following URL: www.eagleimagery.co.uk

Although based in the UK, Eagle Imagery offers a truly global service for businesses and projects both large and small. Recent client design projects have been completed for those in many different countries across the globe.

I always like to hear from those members who are considering either a new business venture or even an overhaul of an existing one. In an age where your business image and identity is competing from the word go (and often on a global stage), a unique, credible and eye-catching identity can reap rewards.

As part of your business plan, it could even be one of the clinching factors if you're seeking investment.

All logos are 100% original...no clipart is ever used and designs are sold once and once only.

Eagle Imagery also specialises in vector conversion of existing low quality/resolution logos. Many online firms find that when business really takes-off and printed stationery is required, their website logo isn't in the correct format and resolution to achieve a professional result...and that's where the vector conversion services come in.

Work for resellers undertaken - subject to conditions.

If your business lacks a credible identity or simply needs bringing into the 21st century, do drop me a line - even if it's just to discuss your future plans or ask for advice!
