Alain Ghiai’s Summary

Alain is the founder, CEO and Chairman of GlobalPayNet Holdings Inc., Globus Payments Ltd. and GlobeXPayNet S.A.nd GlobeX Polska Sp. z.o.o. He won several articles in CRM Daily, USA Today, Business Wire, numerous other publications, the Entrepreneur Profile Award from the San Francisco Business Times and The Wall Street Journal’s Special Report: Technology Section as an expert on online commerce in the USA and Europe and numerous interviews with Bloomberg.

Alain founded and sits on the Board of several companies including involved in construction materials, skin care, media, telecommunications, e-commerce, internet technologies and payment technologies. In 2002 Alain’s online commerce experience prompted him to develop an IP Gateway processing platform, using existing knowledge and his solid technology team. After founding Spectre Technology Corporation in 2004, Ghiai started advanced technical work on his payment gateway platform and raised sufficient funds to take his company to the next level of commercialization. During this time, Ghiai filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”) in order to qualify his company as a fully compliant SEC entity and in 2007 Spectre Technology Corporation became GlobalPayNet Holdings Inc. Subsequently, a wholly owned subsidiary was formed in Canada in 2007 by the name of Globus Payments Ltd. Ghiai then decided to launch his European plan and with his team, incorporated Globex Payment Systems Ltd. in the U.K. and subsequently GlobeXPayNet S.A. in Switzerland. and GlobeX Polska Sp. z.o.o. Ghiai plans to use his experience, economy of scales and existing partnerships worldwide to turn his group of companies into a solid group of electronic payment and ecommerce services powerhouse.

Alain holds a B of ARCH degree in Architecture with 2 other specialties in structural engineering and urban planning. He is a citizen of Belgium and a resident of Switzerland.
