Pierre Collinet (collp @ sterling-conseil.com)’s Summary

Prior to joining Sterling International in October 2001 as Director of Research and Studies, Pierre Collinet was Research Associate with Russell Reynolds Associates in Brussels for 9 years creating the research function, and specializing in the sectors of Industry and Natural Resources. He began his career as Corporate Documentalist with Union Minière (Umicore) prior to joining Spencer Stuart where he remained 4 years as Research Assistant.

Pierre Collinet obtained a degree in Information Sciences from the IESSID. He speaks fluently French & English and has good command in Italian & Dutch.
------------------------------------------------
Sterling International (http://www.sterling-conseil.com) is one of the leading executive search and consulting groups, specializing in Fashion, Retail, and Luxury Goods.

Created in 2001, Sterling International is composed by a multicultural and multilingual team of about 50 experienced Partners, Principals, Consultants, Researchers and staff members from other large search firms (Russell Reynolds, Heidrick & Struggles, Korn Ferry, TMP-Highland, Boyden, Whitehead Mann, Ray & Berndtson, Eurosearch, Alexander Hughes) and/or large Fashion, Retail, and Luxury Goods companies (LVMH, Gucci, Polo Ralph Lauren, Armani, L’Oreal, Hermès, Yves Saint-Laurent Parfums, Promod, Emanuel Ungaro, Révillon, Pierre Balmain, Givenchy).

Till now, 4 offices (Paris, Milan, Brussels, New York) have been opened (Hong Kong will be the next office to be opened in 2008).
