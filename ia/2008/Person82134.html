Jacques Henno’s Summary

Senior journalist, writer, speaker focused on new technologies. My main fields of interest are "Technology and Privacy" (see http://www.tousfiches.com), "Children and New Technologies" (http://www.nosenfants.fr), "Green IT" (http://www.greentek.fr) and "Nuclear Proliferation" (http://www.atlasdunucleaire.com).
I write articles and books and give lectures about these 4 subjects.

Jacques Henno’s Specialties:

I have been interviewed by radio stations (Europe 1, RMC Info, France Inter…), TV (TF1, LCI, France 24…) and newspapers (Le Monde, Ouest-France, L'Express…)
