Oliver Grave’s Summary

BIOGRAPHY

Oliver has an extensive Marketing (food / non-food Consumer Goods) background that spans 25 years. His career started in 1982 at the “Basisresearch Institute” (part of the WPP group). Oliver spend 6 years working as project manager in various leading marketing consultancies and gained 10 years (hands on) operational experience by managing a regional retail and food service network. In this position Oliver negotiated advanced buying conditions and designed progressive trade and direct marketing (CRM) concepts. In 1997, Oliver joined “Harrods” in London, to further his experience within a prestigious retail organization.

In 1998, Oliver became Marketing (Product & Business Development) Manager of “Shamrock”. Oliver developed an innovative category of fresh, pre-packed and value-added seafood products for national retail organizations. He played a key role in sourcing / purchasing and was responsible for international business relations. In 2002/3, Oliver managed two projects for non-food FMCG (pre-paid voucher cards / telecom services). In 2004, Oliver joined “Phillips Foods” as European Business Development Manager and established (from scratch) a distribution network for chilled and frozen food specialties in Austria, Benelux, France, Germany, Italy, Scandinavia, Spain and Switzerland. In 2006/7, Oliver became International Business Development Manager at “Binca Group” and established new customer relations for organic and natural aquaculture products. Oliver is Member of the Advisory Board for “Friend of the Sea” and continues his career as Marketing and Business Development Expert for excellent retail products and advanced customer service concepts.

Oliver Grave’s Specialties:

“Specialised in International Marketing & Business Development – I assist enterprises in: Meeting market / customer-specific requirements, to expand global distribution – Management of ongoing communication / contact programmes, to target prospects – Development of new market and customer relations, to enhance revenues – Design and implementation of marketing strategies, to grow sales flow. My skills are focused on efficient business solutions, to save time and money”
