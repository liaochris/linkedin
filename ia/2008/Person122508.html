Mark Raspotnik [LION]’s Summary

Resource Accounting is a national placement and staffing firm that provides direct hire “permanent” placement, temporary staffing, and flexible project services in the accounting and finance industries. Our diversity of experience covers:

Accounting, Finance, Clerical and Administrative personnel.

In order to provide optimum service, Resource Accounting is staffed by accounting and finance specialists. Our staff is thoroughly trained to obtain the type of technical, company, and position information required to ensure the associate is the proper fit for a client's particular assignment in terms of knowledge, skill and abilities. We conduct in-depth analysis of our clients staffing needs, and then meet with their department leaders.

Resource Accounting is proud to be part of the EmployBridge family of staffing companies. EmployBridge's seven specialty brands have serviced a wide range of staffing needs for over 30 years providing specialized service in the following areas: logistics, transportation, medical, accounting, manufacturing, administrative and professional placement. There are 150+ branches in 20+ states and growing!

Prestige Staffing is a full service employment agency that takes great pride in building relationships with both the candidates and hiring managers. Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, and with a nationwide presence, Prestige Staffing can assist you with your job search anywhere you may be looking. We have four specialized groups to better assist you: Healthcare, Finance, Information Technology and Telecommunications.
