Jole Rotello’s Summary

Over the last 16 years, I have accumulated experience in the field of Communication and Knowledge Management, including:
- Planning of e-Learning courses, teaching in blended and in classroom mode
- Consultancy of content definitions using CMS
- Managing interaction during Knowledge Sharing processes
- Mass Collaboration on the Internet

During my professional history I have worked with a wide variety of public and private sector clients for whom I carried out jobs such as:

- Web Content and IA Architecture Analysis
- Planning and construction of e-Government portals for Public Administration
- Development of websites interface for Intranet, e-Recruiting and e-Commerce

Along with Web planning and teaching, I've also worked as a Tutor in the field of Business Games, to build up Team Cooperation through Role Interaction and Problem Solving simulation for the consolidation of their working team.

A part from work, I enjoy meeting people and learn about other cultures and ideas: feel free to invite me to connect - jole.rotello@gmail.com
