Paula Melhado’s Summary

I am an experienced HR generalist who has worked in HR for 15 years. My time in HR has allowed me to cover a wide range of HR activities gaining an in depth understanding and knowledge of processes, procedures and the development of company policy. With this knowledge also comes the understanding of employment law and best practice.

I have used a variety of IT systems and able to grasp new systems and databases quickly and easily. I am comfortable working on my own but also able to work within a team structure also. I have an open approach and view myself as a well organised person with good prioritisation and time management skills, not only using these skills for my own work but also to assist team members and have been actively sought out by more junior members of the team as a mentor. I am thorough in my work with a hands on approach and comfortable getting involved in the operational side.

Working at an operation level working but also closely with senior managers working with them and developing them towards the goals and success of the Company.
