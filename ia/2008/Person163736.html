Marc Rogivue’s Summary

In short: I possess simultaneously the sense of marketing, cultural knowledge, entrepreneurship and technological competencies in professional design and manufacturing of luxury products.

In detail: Swiss national with more than 13 years of experience in building, marketing and sales of luxury brands and its aftermarket services in Asia. This is supported and extended by a Masters degree in International Business & Marketing, strong entrepreneurial leadership and management competencies, profound IT and eCommerce-knowledge, and solid judgment, troubleshooting, and decision-making abilities.

I have built from scratch a regional sales and marketing office here in Hong Kong, including showroom, service center, relationship management, POS corners in Hong Kong and China as well as designed and produced complete watch collections.

Member of the Gehrson Lehrman's Network of Experts.
Consulted companies such as Goldman Sachs, Deutsche Bank, The Boston Consulting Group and others on all topics of Luxury Branding in Asia.
