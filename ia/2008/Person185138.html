Tal Rosenberger’s Summary

Tal Rosenberger (email / e-mail tal.rosenberger@gmail.com) is Director International Sales Operations in Check Point Software Technologies HQ. Check Point is the worldwide leader in network and computers security.

Tal is part of TopLinkedIn.com, MyLink500.com and LION member.

Prior to this Tal Rosenberger was in charge of the Professional Services in Sapiens International HQ and leading the implementation of Oracle eBusiness Suite globally and Israeli Homeland Security Project. Sapiens (http://www.sapiens.com ) is a leading global provider for IT business solutions for the insurance industry.
Rosenberger began his career in software development in Israeli Defense Forces Computer Unit - MAMRAM and Intelligence. With almost 15 years of technical experience, Rosenberger has held several senior management positions throughout his career. Prior to joining Sapiens, Rosenberger was the VP in Intercomp A Formula Group Company working with worldwide organizations such Deutsche Bank, BWS Bank, Bank Populaire with legacy understanding, migration, enablement and business rules extraction; General Manager of LiBi Software Technology managing software projects with ECI Telecom, Mercury Interactive, Intel, Orbotech, IAF, DSP Group, Bank Hapoalim, Teva & Harel Hamismar Insurance; and head of department in Malam Systems LTD, one of the largest IT groups in Israel.
Tal was also Product Manager of products such as DMS Document Management System, SMT-Sapiens Management Tool which is Testing Management, Bug Tracking, Action Items Management, Cost Estimation, Change Control...

On the last week of July I'm planning business trip to APAC - Tokyo (Japan) and Bangkok (Thailand). On August I'm planning to be in Europe.

I'll be glad meeting potential partners, distributors, resellers and customers.

Feel free sending me email to tal.rosenberger@gmail.com
