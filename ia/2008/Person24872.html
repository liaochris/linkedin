Bob Hardin’s Summary

Results oriented, self-motivated professional with over 30 years engineering experience, both as individual contributor and manager. Capable of leading groups through strategic planning, execution, development and growth to achieve corporate goals. Proven track record of effective management of engineering, production, and equipment maintenance groups in varied scenarios from start-up to mature organizations. Adept at responding to, and managing drastically changing corporate requirements to achieve desired goals.
