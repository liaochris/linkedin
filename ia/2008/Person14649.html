Antonio Saioni’s Summary

My name is Antoni Saioni, in the following pages you can find my work experience.
Over time, I acquired a solid sales experience in three commercial areas. The first area is that of employee time tracking, where the client base was very diverse, with a prevalence of mechanical and industrial B2B clients. During this period of my life I operated mainly in the Emilia region, but on clients with national and international resonance:
• Ducati motorcycle
• Cagiva Group
• Moto Morini
• Lamborghini Auto
• Cedir Ceramiche
• Ragno Ceramiche
• ENEA
• Magneti Marelli
• Philip Morris (Intertaba)
During my subsequent collaboration vie Euroview (Prologue France Group), I entered the world of IT consolidation and virtualization, selling systems such as Citrix Metaframe and deploying Microsoft Terminal Server and virtualization systems through VMWare and Softgrid solutions to a wide range of clients:
• Emilia Romagna Region, Work Directorate
• Ferrara Province
• Lombardini Motori
After this, I have worked in companies whose core business were data and workplace security management, monitoring and proactivity, and data encryption. I took on medium-to-large businesses and Public Administrations. At first we provided proprietary solutions, (Cryptogram was part of the Prologue Group), then we integrated third-party solutions into our family of centralized biometrics products. A sample of my clients in this period:
• Treasure Ministry
• Alenia Missiles
• Emilia Romagna Region
• Crèdit Agricole Indosuez Italy
• Radio 105 / RadioMonteCarlo Group
During the last four years I devoted my business and management experience to seed or startup companies, defining every detail of their commercial plan: setting up business development plans; defining price policies; building and instructing the commercial team; defining distribution plans and distribution agreements; building a network of distributors in Italy, Europe and North America.

Antonio Saioni’s Specialties:

Sales Manager, Country Manager, CEO on Seed and Start Up Company, Branding, Marketing and Organization and all it's necessary to hire, organize and let start a tech company.
