Liesl Semper’s Summary

Most recently I've been working as an Operations Analyst (temporary) at the World Bank. The position is responsible for donor reporting at mid- and end-year. I've also had responsibility for planning and executing (from DC) the Donor Council meeting (held in 2007 in Amsterdam) and preparing all the necessary documentation, including the FY08 business plan.

The business planning process involved editing project submissions from 25 countries (150 projects in all) and liaising with the task team leaders of those projects on clarifications of their intended outputs and deliverables for the fiscal year. Likewise, mid- and end-of-year reporting process involved similar liaising with task leaders on results and delays (if any).

My other work experience includes 3 years in HR (post-MBA) and 4 years in Information Systems (pre-MBA), as a consequence I have some well-developed analytical & systems thinking skills. I also have a particular skill at identifying possible impacts and unintended consequences of actions under consideration.
