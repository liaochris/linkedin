Chris Broden, CMP’s Summary

I have been working in the Meetings Industry since 1997 in a wide variety of roles. I have worked as a Program Manager for a large incentive house managing corporate incentives and meetings for Fortune 500 companies; I have worked as the Director of Meetings, Events and Incentives for one of the largest banking companies in the US and as the Regional Director of Sales for Prestige Resorts & Destinations offering site selection services to corporate and incentive clients in the MN/WI/IA region. I have also been providing on-site management of events since 1997 and continue to provide those services on a contract basis. Most recently I am working as a Procurement Specialist/Meeting Manager for American Express Corporate Meeting Solutions.
