Jonathan Ulrich’s Summary

I have 13 years of classroom experience, teaching primarily choral music and musical theatre. I also have significant experience in the competitive arts: Show Choir, Marching Band, Drum Corps, and Winter Color Guard.

My professional goal is to work towards the ideal that every student in the United States public education system will have equal access to high quality music, dance, media arts, theater and visual arts education.

Jonathan Ulrich’s Specialties:

Arts Education Management and Supervision. Curriculum Design and Supervision.
