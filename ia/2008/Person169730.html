Sareeka Malhotra’s Summary

A thought leader in the area of user research, playing a strategic role in driving product teams to the ideal user experience. Extensive experience in all aspects of user research from planning, designing, recruiting for, and conducting studies to data analysis, feedback compilation, and its presentation to cross-discipline teams. Excellent track record leading research to guide business strategy, executing high profile and often sensitive research, including large scale online and international studies in fast paced environments. Extensive exposure to all stages of product development life cycle including requirements gathering, high-level functional specification, detailed design, and usability testing. Leadership in building infrastructure for research organizations, mentoring/managing new hires including managing schedules, documenting processes, and fostering adoption of research best practices. Expertise in techniques such as contextual inquiry, focus group interviews, traditional lab-based as well as field usability studies, heuristic and cognitive walkthroughs. Familiar with eye tracking. Excellent communication with executive management, Rich analytical, presentation, and project management skills. A frequent speaker at usability forums such as UPA.

User Research, Usability, User Centered Design, Ethnography, Client Servicing, Product Management, Marketing, Market Research, Project Management.
