Simon Ackland’s Summary

After initially studying and working in real estate as a valuer, I discovered the financial services industry and joined property funds management company Growth Equities Mutual.

After 15 years, including 5 years with BT Funds Management (Bankers Trust) and a couple of years with Deutsche Bank (financial planning and private banking) I decided to pursue a new challenge in my own business and formed management consulting and marketing business which now trades as SMI Business Impact.

As you would expect, I retained links to the financial services industry (both funds management and financial planning) and consulted to and was a director of a number of startup companies. These include, among others, 2 wine businesses (one privately held premium grape grower in Pemberton and the other a 5 Star James Halliday rated winery...Chalice Bridge Estate in Margaret River), a salary packaging outsource company and a company involved in a unique forestry based biofuel project.

SMI Business Impact provides management consulting services which include the following key areas:
- marketing (both traditional 'bricks and mortar' and digital marketing) services
- business building strategies (increasing revenue, profits and business value)
- capital raising strategy
- acquisition analysis
- exit or succession planning;
to not only financial services businesses but to a broader range of organisations also. We also offer executive coaching and mentoring services as required.
