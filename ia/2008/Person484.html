Anthony Martucci’s Summary

I served in the information technology industry for more than 20 years. I have held a series of IT positions within major full-service financial firms, which provide brokerage, investment banking and asset management services to corporations, governments and individuals around the world. My expertise covers a wide area of managerial responsibilities and technical knowledge to provide leading-edge business communications solutions. I have an extensive background in project management, VoIP solutions, desktop deployment, client services, structured cabling, network infrastructure, financial systems, and budgetary planning/forecasting.

I began my IT career at Dean Witter Reynolds in 1985, where I gained exposure in Retail Sales, Trading operations, Headquarters and International technology divisions. In 1993 I joined Smith Barney serving as Vice President of Project Management. My responsibilities ranged from branch office openings, relocations, acquisitions and large-scale rollout projects (i.e. NextGen, Shearson and Schroders acquisition, 401K call centers for WorldCom and Cisco, WAN/branch network infrastructure deployment, etc.).

Prior to joining my current position, I served as Vice President of Telecommunications and Market Data Services at Ryan Beck & Company. I held the same position at Gruntal & Company prior to their acquisition by Ryan Beck. I received my Bachelor of Science in Business Management - Leadership from Kean University | PMP Certificate of Achievement - Villanova University.
