Simon Lewis’s Summary

Simon is an Executive Consultant employed in the Public Sector / Procurement Cross-Hatch of Atos Consulting. With seven years consulting experience and a strong track record in Industry. He is a results oriented, procurement and supply chain management professional with a proven record of delivering business benefit to tight deadlines in a diversity of industrial (ship maintenance, oil, aerospace, defence, food, fine chemical) environments.

Simon is currently working with the MOD assembling the contractual requirements for a maintenance contract which will embrace all the Military Fighting vehicles in the British Army. Other recent engagements have included developing a risk-based assessment process for evaluating MOT Test centres for VOSA, two pieces of work developing procurement strategies and organisational change initiatives with, respectively, the Royal Navy and the Royal Fleet Auxiliary. Prior to this Simon developed a procurement strategy for MOD batteries (1250 types!) and spent 18 months working with the Tornado Support, Integrated Project Team helping them to develop a partnered support programme with BAE SYSTEMS for a number of modules of the aircraft.

Prior to joining Atos Consulting Simon had a diverse career firmly rooted in project and programme management. In this role he implemented an ERP system concurrent with a number of business change initiatives (TPM, OEE, TOC) in a food manufacturing company; at British Airways he streamlined workflow - and reduced downtime - during the maintenance of 737s; and lead a considerable number of directional programmes in support of oil exploration around the world.

Simon's qualifications include a Cranfield Fellowship in Manufacturing and Warwick Masters in Business Management.
