Lisa A. Amaral’s Summary

Long & Foster Relocation Division Appoints Lisa A. Amaral to Director of Corporate Business Development

Fairfax, VA March 31, 2008 – Long & Foster Real Estate, Inc., the nation’s largest privately-owned real estate company, headquartered in Fairfax, VA, has named Lisa A. Amaral, CRP/GMS as Director of Corporate Business Development for the Relocation Services Division. In her new role, Ms. Amaral will be responsible for promoting and marketing relocation services to area corporations, relocation management companies, and corporate business entities.
Ms. Amaral has over 20+ of experience in the industry. Most recently as Director of Client Relations and E-Real Estate Services of the Long & Foster Relocation Division. Prior to joining Long & Foster over 6 years ago, Ms. Amaral held positions in real estate and relocation service roles with brokers in New York, NY, Southern Connecticut, and now the Washington, DC metropolitan area.
Ms. Amaral participates in the Employee Relocation Council (ERC) as a Meritorious Service Award member and is currently serving on the Certification Review Board for ERC. Lisa also holds a Certificate in Achievement for Applied CRM (Customer Relationship Management) Strategy. She often speaks on wide ranging topics such as Business Planning, new Business Development, Sales Associate training, Increasing Profits, and Lead Generation and Management for E-Business Development.

Long & Foster® Real Estate, Inc. is the largest privately-owned real estate firm in America. The firm has 237 residential real estate sales offices throughout Pennsylvania, Maryland, Virginia, the District of Columbia, Delaware, West Virginia, New Jersey, and North Carolina, with 16,000 Sales Associates and annual real estate sales for 2007 of $34.4 billion on 95,871 property transactions. Sales for all The Long & Foster Companies totaled $60+ billion for 2007.
