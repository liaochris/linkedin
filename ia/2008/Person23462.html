Bill Rose’s Summary

An expert marketer who, since 2001, has been focused on online marketing, advertising and lead generation. During this time, I have established a track record of driving positive results for Fortune 50 companies in positions ranging from customer-facing account manager to strategy-driving people manager. I develop strategies and media plans that drive interactive marketing initiatives as they relate to search engine marketing, affiliate marketing, interactive media and other online marketing channels. I also possess a strong understanding of direct marketing practices and principles, forecasting, analytics, and the evolving interactive marketing landscape.

Specifically, I bring the following:

-- Experience from the client-side (Dell), agency-side (Carlson Marketing, Performics), and publisher-side (MSN)
-- Deep knowledge of several online marketing tactics including search, display, email, affiliate, viral, mobile & digital word of mouth
-- Proven cross-group leadership and people management capabilities
-- Ability to function effectively while managing and prioritizing multiple projects with tight deadlines
-- Strong presentation, written and verbal communication skills
-- A track record of rapid career advancement

As Manager of Search Marketing at Microsoft, I was responsible for leading a team of twelve Search Media Analysts. My team, which drove $90M in fiscal year revenue, provided campaign strategy and optimization recommendations to enterprise-level search advertisers. I also managed day-to-day business operations for a 145 person department including revenue forecasting, resource allocation and hiring responsibilities.

I hold a BA in Marketing from St. Edward’s University and am a Google Certified Professional.

I can be reached at billrose @ live.com.
