Travis Giggy’s Summary

I am passionate about business on the Internet!

I have been doing business on the Internet for 10 years! I have been programming web sites and databases for 12 years - since the inception of Microsoft ASP technology.

In 1997, I started my first Internet business. It was called Carryout.com.

Carryout.com was an online food ordering service. You could order food from a local restaurant right to your door. In 1997, that was quite revolutionary! Although we had moderate success, it was not meant to be. Crash and burn in the year 2000.

So now, a decade later - I'm still learning what it takes to be successful in online business. The blog address below is how I record those lessons. Since I started the blog, I've learned the value of keeping a written record of my Internet business experiences. One lesson builds on the next and pretty soon some semblance of wisdom emerges. As long as I keep learning and growing, I'll keep writing about it.

I doubt I'll ever quit learning.
