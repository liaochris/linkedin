John Fitzwater’s Summary

John brings a background of many different worlds to Mobilize, with passion as the driving force behind the many verticals that he has been involved with. In the late ‘80’s John realized a childhood dream and opened a large Specialty Pet Store in Charlottesville, VA. After turning his hobby into a profitable enterprise within a few years, he sold his stake in the business and moved to the Big Apple. His next passion was music and so he applied to the Institute of Audio Research (IAR) in Manhattan and completed a 900-hour intensive right-of-passage in Studio operation, music engineering and production. Ticket in hand, John soon went on to work with some of the biggest names in the business, from The Allman Brothers and the Sun Rah Archastra to Perry Farrell of Jane's Addiction and Ziggy Marley.
Along the way, John discovered his passion for the world of motorcycle racing and engineering and soon found himself the President of the Northern California chapter of the Ducati Owners Club; John applied his social networking skills and knowledge of customer service and sales and quickly became one of the top performers in the business of selling the fastest and most exotic motorcycles in the world.
Growing with the world around him, John soon found another passion in the developing opportunities of the world of mobile communication. With an intuitive understanding of marketing and advertising, John is now putting rubber to the road in the industry of new media and mobile marketing.
