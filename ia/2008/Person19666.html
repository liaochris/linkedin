Barbara Whisenant’s Summary

Barbara Whisenant is a professional Realtor, specializing in serving the needs of buyers and sellers in Carlsbad, La Jolla, Vista, Oceanside, San Marcos and other North County San Diego communities.

Barbara Whisenant brings to her profession, both a legal background and a quality of caring for her clients.

While extremely professional, Barbara Whisenant is also quite personable. She likes to think of herself as your lifetime Realtor® as she enjoys working with her clients for not just one transaction, but every real estate transaction in a lifetime.

Respected by her real estate peers, Barbara Whisenant is known for her caring attitude, enthusiasm, integrity and persistence. Barbara believes that the sale or purchase of real estate should be a positive experience and will make it her job to mitigate or eliminate all problems. Through a vast store of knowledge and experience as well as continual updating of knowledge and the use of the latest technology, Barbara Whisenant creates transactions that are quick, smooth, successful and truly enjoyable.

Barbara Whisenant is a member of: The National Association of Realtors; California Association of Realtors; North San Diego Association of Realtors, SRES (Senior Real Estate Specialist), GRI (Graduate Realtor Institute). As a member, she is held to the highest caliber of service.

Member of Board of Directors and office manager of two non-profit corporations. Created policies and procedures with regard for all employees of the corporations. Corresponded with and prepared financial and status reports required for governmental agencies. Conducted radio and newspaper interviews.

As an office manager, I supervised the corporate office of Our Community In Action and American Council of Athletics, and was responsible for the interviewing, hiring and terminating of all office personnel.

I have been performing genealogical research for years and have published three family histories.
