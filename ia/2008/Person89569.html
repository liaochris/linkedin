Jeroen Gösgens’s Summary

Training Trips BV organizes among others football (soccer) training camps/tours for teams all around the globe. Training Trips website has already more then 60 destinations throughout Europe. All destinations have professional training facilities with pitches on site. Next to training camps we also sell travel arrangements to different youth tournaments in Europe. We have also started academy tours to top professional clubs in Europe. We are also involved in arranging academy teams for top academy tournaments in Spain, Holland and Belgium. Tournaments where the topteams in Europe attend. This year we also started an office in Melbourne, Australia. Football is booming in Australia and many club and school teams wants to tour through Europe.

Furthermore with our Playerspool we assist students who would like to play for a college/university in the US on a soccer scholarship. We started The Playerspool two years ago and lots of young student athletes are playing in the US now. This year The Playerspool will start in the UK. We also started a talentpool in 2008. Talents can get a personal profile for free on our website. In conjunction with player agents we analyse these profiles and look into opportunities for these players. Next to a player career we think education is just as important. Currently we are investigating the Chinese Football market and soon we will start The Playerspool in China. End 2008 / Early 2009 we will launch our new Playerspool website.

Furthermore I am working on a new social community in football. More soon.
