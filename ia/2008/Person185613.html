Tara Ramroop’s Summary

By day, Tara Ramroop writes and edits the online curriculum for an ever-expanding San Francisco art university. By evening/weekend/whenever she has time, she fiddles with her freelance article drafts and contributes to a handful of culture, travel and lifestyle Web sites. She wrote daily local and regional news in California on a wide range of subjects - including business, development, government, the University of California, religion and travel - from 2001 to 2007.

Tara Ramroop’s Specialties:

Tara has particular skill writing and editing copy meant for a wide, diverse audience ranging in size from a newspaper circulation of a few thousand to an online, English-speaking student community spanning the globe.
