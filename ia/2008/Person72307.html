Greg Goetchius’s Summary

Engineering management executive with 22 years of engineering and team management experience in the global automotive and consumer industries. Specific technical expertise in acoustics and noise & vibration control with an intense focus on the combined use of test and CAE methods. Deep knowledge of the product development process and the application of engineering tools during the early stages of a product's life cycle.

Ability to establish a vision and motivate team to achieve goals.

Experienced in TS16949, ISO 9000 and ISO 17025 standards.

Experienced in R&D facility design, construction and operations.
