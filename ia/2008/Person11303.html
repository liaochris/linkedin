Andres Vergara’s Summary

Andres is a Creative Director-Creative Group Head at Impact BBDO since 2007. Previously he worked as Senior Strategic planner at the award winning independent agency TONIC Dubai in the Middle East who brought him over from Sweden were he was running La Resistance, a brainchild of him and film director & producer Christer Engström, devoted to new idea development, film and brand building for Swedish and International brands.
