Jacqui Brett’s Summary

I partner with highly motivated, passionate key contributors who are excited by new ideas and fueled with a desire to make a meaningful difference in the world. They’re ready to realize the next Big Vision for their lives, whether it’s a new position, promotion, career change, striking out on their own in a for profit or not-for-profit venture, or taking time off to focus on family, go on sabbatical, or travel the world.

I’m committed to seeing clients have major breakthroughs as they transform their approach to work and career. My guiding principle is one of career self-management, in which people operate from the stance that the only business they’ll ever really be tied to throughout their career is the business of You, Inc.

My personal mission is to inspire people to use their innate talents and gifts in work they are passionate about, find meaningful, and can earn a great living at! I want folks everywhere to see the diamonds beneath the soles of their feet and to present them as an authentic contribution to the world.

The people I work with most often have a background in PR, corporate or marketing communications, at companies ranging from globally recognized consumer brands, multinational manufacturers, PR and integrated communications firms, broadcast media and technology companies, start-ups, and social enterprises.

By working as both a career coach and executive recruiter, I add value to the companies and individuals I work with, first by being on the front lines of the job market and second, by developing close ties with the talent most employers are eager to connect with in today’s “war for talent.”

Prior to doing this work -- which I'm deeply passionate about -- I spent 10 years in the corporate sector in roles that have included media relations, corporate communications, product publicity, and executive communications.
