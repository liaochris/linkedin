Jon Mummery’s Summary

- Experience making games for XBOX 360, Nintendo DS, Nintendo 64, PS1 and PC platforms.
- Lead/Senior Animator at Rare, Microsoft.
- Realistic and Cartoony Character Animation, Binding and Rigging.
- 6 years of Motion Capture experience - from directing sessions to setting up, cleaning data, blending data and re-targetting onto characters. (Motion builder and Vicon)
- Designing in-game Motion Trees & scheduling required assets.
- Quality control of Animation Assets & Bug Management.
- Scripting for level setups and animation blending using custom in-house editors.
- Reviewing and Interviewing new Animation applicants.

Jon Mummery’s Specialties:

Character Animation, Binding and Motion Capture. Leading Animation, Mentoring and solving technical problems / troubleshooting.
