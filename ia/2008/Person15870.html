Arjan J.W. van Druten’s Summary

Arjan is member of Orange Business Services BeNeLux Management Team. Heading up the Business Development of Orange in The Netherlands with existing Corporate Clients as well as new business logo's.

Before Orange Business Services (formerly Equant) Arjan has been active in Information Technology, Systems Integration and in Telecoms previously.

He has held several sales and management positions in this field, in different European countries. He has a broad and deep understanding of the industry and the challenges for the individual companies.

Arjan is an entrepreneurial, results oriented and strongly driven individual with a proven customer and business focus.

He has built and led multi-disciplined teams and has been responsible for budgets up to tens of million Euro's. Encompassing functional areas such as sales, business planning, finance, marketing and customer service.
