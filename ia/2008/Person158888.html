Rene Gomez’s Summary

Ditech Networks recruited Rene to become their new Regional Sales Director for the Iberoamerica market to take their newly developed digital voice processing technology for voice quality improvement and capacity enhancement enabler in order to help greatly serve the wireless operators' needs for rapid and affordable growth without loosing quality of service.

Rene introduced Telephonics Wireless' new proof of concept GSM wireless product to key US and international leading companies and Top prospect customers for feedback, trials and initial targeted sales turning an unknow company in the field into the new key potential competitor for traditional vendors in the wireless market.

Rene spent 9 years with Ericsson, where he led the sales organizations for three key divisions targeting different US and international markets executing multiple wireless sales and channel partnership initiatives worth over $100 M USD.

Previously Rene helped Philip Morris save over $3 M USD a month by conceptualizing and leading a new initiative consisting of IT development and changes in the purchasing processes improving the purchase of manufacturing materials for the International Tobacco operations.

Before Philip Morris, Gomez worked for Citibank in Mexico supervising several IT outsourcing projects saving the organization over $10 M USD in additional development costs.

Rene holds a master’s degree in business administration with a concentration in Corporate Finance from Virginia Tech, and he earned a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering combining courses from Virginia Tech and the University of the Americas located in Mexico where he was born.
