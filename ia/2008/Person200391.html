Glenn Wilson’s Summary

Recently graduated from the MIT Sloan School of Management with an MBA, I joined Google full-time as a Product Manager.

My experience is in technology management with an extensive software engineering background. Primarily specialized in internet technologies, I'm interested in any type of software. Interested in both large companies changing the world and startups with brilliant team members.

I was a research assistant at the Center for Information Systems Research (CISR) at MIT Sloan. I assisted with projects concerning IT innovation, and my work is currently in the process of being published.

I have experience designing, building and managing open source software projects, especially involving Monte Carlo simulations of AI.

Currently, I'm working on a personal pet project concerning automated web application regression testing.
