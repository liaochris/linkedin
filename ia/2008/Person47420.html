David Pollock’s Summary

The CEO of Asyst Consulting, and my mentor, is himself a former Software Engineer from Silicon Valley. His ethical guidance and commitment to our contractors, and my curiosity and thirst for technology has given me a solid platform for qualifying requirements, interviewing high-level talent and career counseling. I am an effective and understanding mediator / relayer of information, negotiating the expectations of both the client and candidate.

Counseling towards career goals with my candidates is the most important part of this business for me. Encouraging people to reach for what they want is essential, and we cannot press the jobs we happen to have upon them. I am not a salesman.

David Pollock’s Specialties:

Being a recruiter is about matching up the the right job for the right person. A lot of the candidates I have been unable to place in positions, have later become loyal clients and vice versa; because of my dedication to finding the right fit for all parties.
