Christy Wiseman’s Summary

I am a Technical Talent Executive for WorldLink Inc. and thier International clients servicing the United States. I take pride in "Connecting quality people to great opportunities." I proactively work with Hiring Managers and their teams within the WorldLink organization to help them identify their "pain points" within their talent acquisition needs. I do this by understanding my client’s goals through consultative methods. I help facilitate a successful hire that will ultimately align with each companies culture, growth, and vision. I manage full cycle candidate flow and sourcing efforts for 50 plus accounts (including drug screening, background checks, offer preparation and extension, including disposition of all reviewed candidates). I work both independently and with a team to produce a high quality level of qualified candidates enabling us to fill openings in record time.
