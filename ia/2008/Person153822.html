Michael Allegretto’s Summary

Accomplished, results-driven leader, with a proven track-record directing event production and site management activities within the entertainment industry. Handled site development for seven seasons, for Phish's multi-day, multi-arts festivals one of the top grossing and largest mass gatherings in North America. Recruited to support site management at the 1996 Summer Olympic Games.

Effective project manager with extensive experience leading cross-functional project teams. Combines meticulous attention-to-detail with exceptional organizational skills, and the ability to excel in time sensitive and high pressure environments. Possesses solid written and verbal communication skills. Willing to travel.

Michael Allegretto’s Specialties:
