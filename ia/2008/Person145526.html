Amir Perlmutter’s Summary

International experienced and goal oriented packaging Manager with solid technical knowledge. Motivated and ambitious with strong breakthrough thinking, influencing and team management skills. 15 years of experience of CPG / FMCG in Europe, Latin America and the US in the development of packaging in the home and personal care category, the medical device area as well as the foods business.

In-depth knowledge of packaging materials, converting processes, molds and packing equipment; Scope of responsibilities and achievements has covered areas of strategic support and implementation of innovative packaging systems as well as optimization of costs and processes at local, regional and global level. Fluent in english. german, spanish and basic portuguese.

Amir Perlmutter’s Specialties:

• Global project management, Category packaging strategy, Simplification, Packaging Innovation, Consumer understanding, Sustainability.

•Packaging design and concept development through prototyping and testing, specification management, benchmarking, manufacture and filling through to post launch support and trouble shooting.

• Broad and robust experience in rigid plastic with ISBM, IBM, EBM, IM, Thermoform Technology as well with flexible materials, paper and board.
