Stan Gibson’s Summary

Prize-winning writer and editor with talent for spotting trends, developing strategy and implementing plans; successful manager who has led highly motivated, entrepreneurial teams of 5 to 20, providing solid support and direction for junior and mid-level editors and writers; established strong rapprot with senior industry leaders through landmark series of CEO interviews; experienced keynote speaker and panel moderator at national and international conferences -- shared stage with President Bill Clinton; skilled in new media, including audio and video Webcasting nand podcasting.

Stan Gibson’s Specialties:

Information Technology industry thought leader through insightful columns and editorials; broad expertise in global outsourcing through on-the-scene reporting in India and Russia; well-known figure in the industry through public appearances; skilled writer and editor of news and feature stories.
