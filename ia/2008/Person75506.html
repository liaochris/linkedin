Hans-Christian Jehg’s Summary

Goals:
To work in an innovative, creative and dynamic environment where I can bring new groundbreaking ideas to life. To work where there is an environment characterised by teamwork, knowledge sharing, professional development and variety.

Profile:
Currently I am developing production pipelines (The IT backbone) for animation film productions.
Before this I was a freelance consultant in England (Salisbury) specialicing in advicing on building measureing instruments. Before this I worked in R&D for more than 8 years. My experience ranges from the early phases of research to the finishing touches required during the production transfer phase. This experience has been acquired whilst working on the development of measuring instruments for both liquid and solid food products: a multidisciplinary challenge.
My main expertise is in activities involving electronics and/or software (User Interface, Embedded and Control software), as well as within system structure, invention and implementation.
During my work in R&D I have also gained insight into: measurement methods, chemistry, spectroscopy, chemometrics, mechanics, optics and flow systems.
Although I feel that my strength lies in the innovative part of projects, I also take great pleasure in bringing a project to the finishing line.
I prefer working with challenging innovative projects and I usually work with several projects in parallel.
Besides my main work based activities I also maintain an experimental setup that I built, at a research lab for basic biological research at the Roskilde University Center (RUC, Roskilde, Denmark).
