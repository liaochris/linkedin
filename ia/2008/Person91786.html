Jindou Lee’s Summary

Jindou is an Award winning Art director with over 7 years experience. His experiences range from corporate branding, environmental graphics, online media and software applications; all the way to broadcast motion graphics and video games interface design.

Having the passion for all things digital, Jindou's equally balanced left/right brain has allowed him to create compelling user experiences driven by the power of technology.

An entrepreneurial spirit provides Jindou with the business edge to see the problems and concerns of his clients and project stakeholders. His creative problem solving ability in turn provides innovative and appropriate solutions to their needs.

Having lead a team of programmers and designers, Jindou's strong leadership and personal skills ensure that his team is constantly inspired. He is self-motivated with a positive attitude and strong communication skills which is needed to juggle multiple projects and work closely with clients. (Not to mention get through those last-minute deadlines!)

Jindou Lee’s Specialties:

Ideas, Rich media, User Interface, Web, Video Games, Concept development, Branding and Identity, anything that is digital and interactive.
