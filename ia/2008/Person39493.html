Pat McNamara’s Summary

Persuasive Pitches for Startups & Beyond

Think of me as a 'turnaround expert' for weak PPT pitches.

Why will some Entrepreneurs spend months, often years, developing their company idea - yet be casual about the most important 20 minutes of their career?

I help leaders in startup and "emerging" companies deliver compelling, successful presentations - investor pitches, product launches or crucial "make or break" sales presentations.

The work focuses on 2 crucial areas: their Story - and how they tell it.

In this "20 minute" niche I combine proven presentation-skills coaching with three decades of entrepreneurial marketing & sales know-how.

My work is especially valuable for clients who:
- have limited presentation experience, low-confidence
- get stage-fright when speaking to groups
- think they're good presenters, but aren't
- have data-dense, complicated slides
- present to testy, cynical audiences
- say, "this pitch means everything to our success!"
- don't get return calls from investors, alliance partners & prospects

Deliverables are:
- deep analysis of audience attendees
- elevator pitch groomed to multiple audiences/motives
- a credible, compelling story
- an story narrative that matches client leadership style
- simple, powerful slides
- pitch-skills practice with coaching & video
- prep for tough Q & A sessions
- tuned to live audience or teleconference

I have experience as a startup co-founder and in guiding early-stage companies to all-important sales results. I'm expert in all phases of strategic planning, product & program launches and go-to-market integrated marketing/sales campaigns. My best work is in innovative technologies, rapid change and fierce competition.
