Nic Tinworth’s Summary

I am currently seeking to further my career goals, and contribute the enthusiasm, knowledge, experience and leadership I have gained from work and volunteer settings. I have 10 years experience working closely with clients and their brands, in fast paced environments, to produce outstanding interactive and user interface design. This has led to developed interpersonal communication and leadership skills. Along with strong personal and work ethics, I am eager to bring to any team environment a solid sense of self-motivation, attention to detail, and experience. I have several years experience at an art direction/supervisory level.
