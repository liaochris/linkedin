Laura & Larry Morris’s Summary

Larry’s background of 25 years was as an executive in Corporate America, which required working 80 hours a week, and experiencing tremendous stress and no control of his life.He was introduced to Network Marketing in 1989. To say he became passionate is an understatement!

Larry quickly became a dynamic mentor, speaker and national trainer. His emphasis has always been on the vital importance of treating their enterprise like a “million dollar opportunity” . Larry is well known for his “tell it like it is” style.

Laura's began a home based business in 1998. It became apparent very quickly that this industry was where she belonged, and was to make a full-time career.

Laura has achieved top income earner recognition, has been a national conference call host, and national conference speaker and trainer.

She is especially known for her strong skills in long distance sponsoring.

Laura and Larry are unique in the fact that they found each other as life’s companions through working together in the same organization and were married in 2001.

Recognized as Number One Income Earners, Founding Executive Directors and Presidential Advisory Board members are achievements they are very proud of.

In 2007 Enagic and Kangen Water was shared with them.

Larry having had 2 back surgeries, 3 knee surgeries and Chronic Fatigue Syndrome in the late 1980’s, made Kangen water the perfect product. Larry tried Kangen water and his pain was gone in 3 days!

Laura suffered with Fibromyalgia and migraine headaches for about 17 years. Kangen water cleansed the acidic waste and…..the pain and the migraines are 90% gone and life is completely new for Laura, not to mention that she’s 30 pounds lighter!

"We have such a burning passion to help people who are suffering. We've been there..and it's no fun when your life is controlled by your health. When we found Enagic and Kangen water, we knew we were going to change thousands of lives!"
