Renée Barrett’s Summary

Renée Barrett, aka AAARenée, possesses over 10 years legal marketing experience including a not-for-profit alternative dispute resolution organization and two AM LAW 100 global law firms. Her familiarity with the intricacies of professional services firms empowers her to make a powerful contribution.

She appreciates that the practice of professional services is at its core a relationship driven business. As such, professionals constantly need to assess their key relationships, strategies, and profitability. Using awareness, action, and accountability she works with you to advance each component of this fundamental equation.

AAARenée is known as an advisor, coach, and professional trainer that instead of accepting “can’t” moves forward by asking “how?” When retained, she works tirelessly to boost her clients’ business development and marketing efforts.
