Kurt Knipe’s Summary

Kurt Knipe is a seasoned finance professional with proven success leading the development of new teams, tools and processes to support financial planning and reporting as well as operational analysis and decision support. He has over 5 years management experience in large companies - and over 10 years of financial analysis experience - ranging from FP&A responsibilities to complex business modeling, pricing and customer value analysis.

Kurt is an experienced project manager. His mission is to create processes, organizational tools and logically structured business models to understand and solve complex business issues and provide analysis and clarity for decision making at all levels of the business.

– Subscriber Valuation

Systems Knowledge & Expertise: MS Excel (and other MS Office Applications), Database Tools, Cognos, Hyperion, Essbase, Oracle, PeopleSoft
