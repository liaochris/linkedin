Harold C. Heard Jr’s Summary

Contact me via my personal email address at: HaroldCHeardJr@gmail.com

I am a seasoned, hands on executive with outstanding experience in exploiting technology, turning around operations, establishing offshore business process and system development operations. I focus on improving the effectiveness along with measurement models of an enterprise architecture (EA) teams by creating deep mentoring relationships. I am focused on creating a relationship-based leadership focus that will improve the effectiveness of organizations teams such as information technologist leaders and business executives by leveraging my deep experience, relationships and proven models in the discipline of enterprise architecture. Through my proven Enterprise Content Management (ECM) solution based functional framework business is better able to unify people throughout the organization into a model that speaks to both business and technical leaders. My view is; organizations next primary resource for future capital expansion is to define and better understand the content that is currently within the company and unmanaged.
