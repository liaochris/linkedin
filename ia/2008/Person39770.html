Dave Simpson’s Summary

Dave Simpson brings over 20 years of domestic and international experience with high tech and non-profit corporations as a leader and operations expert, a corporate university faculty/coach, and an internal consultant working with high-potential/top-talent leaders. He has helped leaders launch new services and organizations, transition into new leadership roles, and improve team performance.

His background includes thirteen years with Digital Equipment Corporation and Compaq Computers. His responsibility for P&L's, budgets, forecasts, and balance scorecard metrics has helped David develop strong skills in leadership, accountability, and business acumen. His three years in Hong Kong and Singapore, with operational responsibilities encompassing China, Japan, Southeast Asia and Australia, have provided him with invaluable lessons in international management and cross-cultural effectiveness. As a director with Cadence Design Systems corporate university, he was one of the original three members to launch the Executive College, serving 250 leaders.

He uses an “appreciative” approach, acknowledging that leaders are more effective when they leverage strengths and passions. His goal is to help leaders achieve extraordinary business results by aligning their character strengths, goals, and actions. Dave is described as possessing a steadiness of spirit and a capacity to inspire others. He is experienced in confronting and articulating complex problems. Dave’s clients include Spansion, Novellus, Oracle, Alza, and Rebecca’s Children’s Services.
