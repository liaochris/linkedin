Pradeep Tupe’s Summary

* Corporate Trainer and Facilitator. Conducted more than 300 training programs for Corporates on various topics and also advised them on their Management issues.

* Certified Lead Assessor for QMS (ISO 9001:2000).

* Visiting faculty to various Management Institutes and B Schools for variety of subjects like Entrepreneurship Development, Operations Management, International Marketing, Production Planning etc.

* Marketing Consultant by Profession, he has worked with many reputed Industries like KSB Pumps Ltd, Kirloskar Brothers Ltd, Rathi Industrial Equipment Co Ltd, Gujarat Steel Tubes Ltd, Atlas Steel Tube Industries in Marketing /Sales departments in different capacities.

* At present he is Consultant, Market Development to Atlas Steel Tube Industries-a unit of Atlas Cycle Industries Ltd and other establishments.

* He is also Consultant to Dnyaneshwar Vidyapeeth Pune, an educational Institute.

* Occupying prestigious position of Honorary Secretary of All India Management Association, Pune

* Vice President Pune City of Laghu Udyog Bharati, an all India organisation for small scale industries.

* Held unique position as the Chairman of The Institution of Engineers (India) Maharashtra State Centre, covering Maharashtra and Goa States having 71,000+ members and also of Pune Local centre. By adopting unique Management style he achieved many unique distinctions of growth, turn around to set these centres as a new role model (qualitatively, quantitatively and financially) for the Institution on all India basis.

* Active Rotarian, he is charter member of Rotary Club of Pune University and Chairman Environmental Awareness of Rotary Dist 3130.
