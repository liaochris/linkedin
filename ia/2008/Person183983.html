Susan Pope’s Summary

In May of 2000, Susan was invited to the White House to speak at a conference titled Raising Responsible and Resourceful Youth. Susan spoke on the impact of dance education in the lives of her students. She has published an article in the International Journal of AAHPERD titled Mourning Into Dancing – The Transformation of Lives: A personal Journey. Most recently she published a book titled I DANCE BECAUSE…, a collection of stories, essays and poems about dance. In 1998 she was selected to be in Who’s Who Among American Teachers. Susan served as the director of the St. James AME Church Dance Ministry for over 10 years. Under God’s anointing the group grew from 8 women to over 100 members. She and her husband minister in dance at conferences, conventions, weddings, funerals and seminars. As an independent consultant with Warm Spirit she leads workshops in health and wellness.
