Marco Barzaghi’s Summary

I was born in 1976. I am a consultant in information technology since 1998. I was at first a developer in CMS VM/ESA, but then also Windows, J2EE and now .NET. I got experience in the development of desktop application for backoffice, Client-Server, communication between windows and mainframe, and enterprise solutions. Since 2003 I specialized myself in the .NET Microsoft technology. I am charmed by OOP programming and I have special interest for the development of distributed applications with web interface or xml interoperation services.

I believe in the community experience. I am in founding members of UGIdotNET (User Group Italiano .NET - www.ugidotnet.org) and GUISA (Gruppo Utenti Italiani Solution Architect - www.guisa.org). Moreover Microsoft gave to me the MVP award for my support to the community.

Marco Barzaghi’s Specialties:

Software developer and architect (.NET / J2EE). My current focus is on .NET Framework, ASP.NET, SQL Server 2000 / 2005.
