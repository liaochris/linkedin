Zania Guy’s Summary

My name is Zania Guy. I have recently joined AKQA as Account Director on the Yell Group account. I have over eleven years experience in advertising and marketing with a specialization in online advertising and marketing. I have worked with worldwide brands including Metro International, McDonald's, Sky, Phaidon Press, Glaxo Wellcome, and niche brands. Previously, I was Head of Interactive Commercial Operations at Metro International, published in over 20 countries worldwide.
