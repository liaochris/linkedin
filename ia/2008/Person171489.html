Scott Yamano’s Summary

I own and operate Dedicated Media (A Dedicated Marketing Solutions Company) an online advertising and marketing company with offices in LA, NY and SF. Dedicated owns a display network (www.dedicatednetworks.com) a CPA division (www.cpadna.com) and our lead gen properties as well as (www.CarDevotion.com and www.pharmdaily.com)
