  • Technology Management Consultant

    Alberta IT Management Services Ltd.

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 2007 — Present (3 months)

    Operating as Alberta IT Management Services Ltd, I provide technical management services to help organizations, big and small, deal with the ever changing world of Information Technology (IT). While every business uses IT in some way, shape, or form, unfortunately that relationship is strained by the ever increase rate of change and, even more critically, the ever increasing costs. My goal is to help organizations meet these challenges head on, in a manner that meets their business needs and most importantly in a cost efficient manner.

    I am an educator, but I am also a technology professional. I spent the first years of my career working as a classroom teacher but I left the classroom to become an IT professional. After working for several years as a Systems Analyst. Over the past 9 years, these two paths merged together.

    As an IT professional my primary interest is security and network design. As an educator my interest is on using technologies effectively in the classroom.
