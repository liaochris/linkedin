Chris Bailey’s Summary

I've spent the last ten years in the non-profit world learning and implementing new ways to build better relationships both inside and outside organizations. I've worked to promote more effective and meaningful organizational processes, communications, and culture for employees. I've also worked to foster more honest dialogue and caring relationships with customers.

Currently, I'm an account operations manager for Convio, Inc. Through this work on the corporate side, I continue to help non-profit clients achieve their mission. I have fun everyday working with non-profits to build on their purpose through online fundraising and advocacy.

In my off-time, I blog at WorkPlay. Since 2004, this site is my playground for exploring innovative ideas for changing how we approach our work and jobs, as well as our meaning in life. Read more at http://www.baileyworkplay.com.
