Tiziano Fruet’s Summary

Adobe Certified Expert (ACE) and Adobe Certified Instructor (ACI) in Photoshop, Illustrator and Freehand (formerly Macromedia).

Adobe Guru in Italy for Creative Publishing in Adobe Italia since 2004.

Trainer, consultant and knowledge worker about graphic design, photoretouching,
photomanipulation and 2d visual effects (movie poster, advanced photo editing for fashion and design etc..).

Teacher in Istituto Design Palladio since 2001 in Desk Top Publishing,

- Speaker in many national events starting from webbit in 2001 about Photoshop Special Effects and creativity;
- articles writer for PSD and Italia Grafica, editor in chief for italian version of .PSD from 2005 to 2006
- book writer for FAG Editor (Photoshop I segreti del fotoritocco, Photoshop CS3 La tua prima guida, Photoshop CS3 Guida all'uso).

As a graphic designer partner and instructor freelance i worked on, or co-realize, many project for renowned brand such as Gardaland, Acquario di Genova, Zuegg, Cattolica Assicurazioni, UNESCO, Kawasaki, Yamaha, Suzuki, Hoover, Panasonic, Tre, Candy, Adobe, MTV, Gruner&Jahr Mondadori, Valentino Fashion Group, Tecniche Nuove Editore, Divani&Divani by Natuzzi, DSL55 by Diesel, Silver Care, FIAF (www.fiaf-net.it), Ducati Motor SPA.
