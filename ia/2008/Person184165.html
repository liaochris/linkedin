Suzanne Burge Taylor’s Summary

As CEO of Femaleadvertising.com, Suzanne executes online advertising campaigns, mix media design, original video content, radio/TV commerical production and experimental marketing initiatives which relate to women. By utilizing 15+ years of marketing experience to expand new product offerings and services for niche consumers and Advertisers alike, 2008 clients have included Ford, Disney and Comcast to name a few.

Former Creative Director at Sahara Media LLC., for Honeymag.com, online social-networking property for urban women. Created advertising partnerships including GLAM Media and Viacom's BET.com

For over 10 years, Suzanne worked under the Universal and Warner Music Group umbrella as Vice-President of Marketing/Product Manager at Island Def Jam Music Group and Elektra Entertainment for Grammy Award Winning Artists Missy Elliott, Jay-Z and Ludacris. Suzanne is credited in marketing more than 30 million albums word-wide and continues to develop new music talent today under parent company Taylormade Media Group LLC.
