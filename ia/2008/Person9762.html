Amy Byer’s Summary

In charge of the day-to-day creative and production for live action series TRUE JACKSON, VP on Nickelodeon. In the past I have been involved in the development of over 160 half hour comedy scripts from idea, pitch, sale, outline, various drafts, pilot order, selecting and building relationships with directors and line producers, approving sets, choosing wardrobe, representing the studio on set during pre-shoots and tape night, giving notes on all cuts, celebrating when the show gets ordered, all the way to staffing the show with writers at all levels and finally, launching the show for air. Series you may know include, Two and a Half Men (CBS), Old Christine (CBS), Notes from the Underbelly (ABC), The Class(CBS), Twenty Good Years (NBC), The War at Home (FOX), Twins (WB), Joey(NBC), Four Kings (NBC), Eve (UPN), All of Us (UPN), Center of the Universe (CBS), The Mullets (UPN), The Stones (CBS) among others.

Amy Byer’s Specialties:

Expert script problem-solving and analysis. Knowledge of and relationships with hundereds of writers at every level. Experience with every aspect of development, production and post-production process. Relationships with TV Directors, Line Producers, Casting Directors, etc. Relationships at all broadcast networks, studios and with every Literary agency.
