Ilya Venger’s Summary

My current PhD research project involves the investigation of genetic redundancy in the yeast Saccharomyces Cerevisiae utilizing metabolomics. For the metabolic profiling of yeast we are using GC-MS.
This project is a joint venture under the supervision of Dr. Yitzhak Pilpel head of the lab for Computational Genomics at the Molecular Genetics department and Dr. Asaph Aharoni head of the Plant Metabolomics Lab at the Plant Sciences department of the Weizmann Institute.

Ilya Venger’s Specialties:

Gas Chromatography - Mass Spectrometry (GCMS)
Multivariate Statistical Analysis
