Aki Spicer’s Summary

Strategy, Trends and Consumer Insight, particularly within sphere of burgeoning "social media".

Strategic Planning Director at Fallon Minneapolis, servicing Nestle Purina Pet Brands, TheLadders.com, ongoing new biz, and Children's Defense Fund.

Aki joined the Fallon team from Vigilante NYC, an urban and youth markets specialist of Leo Burnett/Publicis Groupe S.A. Aki also previously served as VP Marketing at Rocawear, an urban youth fashion brand.

Aki Spicer’s Specialties:

Aki presents workshops both within the agency and throughout the United States on topics such as trends, and social media.

He is also a founding officer in the philanthropic network, Planning For Good (more info @ http://www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=4736469785).

Read articles and updates daily from Aki on the Web at http://www.fallontrendpoint.blogspot.com/.
