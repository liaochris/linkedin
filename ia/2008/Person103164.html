Kathleen Harper’s Summary

Kathleen is a graphic designer with years of print experience. She's taken print projects from initial design consultations through press checks and distribution. Currently, Kathleen is seeking a position in a firm where she can be a very good fit, and where she can make a contribution that is a) inspirational, b) informative, and c) educational, in a firm that has a forward-thinking, ethical, and thoughtful culture, and one with lots of room for creative exploration. That's where she performs best, with her 15+ years of design experience and arts interests.

An added bonus for those who might be thinking about contacting Kathleen: She has a second love, writing, and has been able to apply her talents in various ways, editing, copywriting, and fiction writing, throughout her design career.

Annual report design and AR project management; Working with vendors, establishing rapport and good working relationships with vendors and clients, and managing projects both for in-house agencies and as freelance consultant/contractor. Meeting deadlines and budgets. Great energy when creatively challenged, and great results.
