Peter Le Lievre’s Summary

2008 Founder and CEO of Chromasun Inc. Commercialising next generation silicon CPV technology from The Australian National University.

2007 Founding CEO of Ausra Inc. Led the $43M Series A funding from www.kpcb.com and www.khoslaventures.com and $30M venture debt. Managed the development of the 175MW Carrizo Plain project for PG&E and the Ausra $25M robotic mirror line.

2003-2006 Founding Managing Director of Solar Heat and Power Pty Ltd. Developed and constructed the first solar array at the 2000MW Liddell Power Station

Past history in VC and technology commercialisation includes:

- VC Investment Analyst: Fairgill Investments

- Industrial Designer: Design and Industry Pty Ltd
