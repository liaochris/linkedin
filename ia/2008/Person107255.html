Kirsten Evans’s Summary

IT marketing executive with more than 15 years of extensive experience developing, managing and marketing products and services for companies wanting to gain entrance into new markets or who need to better define their existing services and products. Span of skills include strategic planning to team management to tactical marketing and promotion.

 Do your customers understand your services?
 Do you know how to market and sell services?
 Are your services repeatable?
 Are your services profitable?

Services Defined can help enterprises achieve financial success from their services by providing a methodology to create, modify or enhance their service offerings. Our Services Productization Framework provides business managers support on defining what exactly their services does for its intended customer, what value the customer will receive, as well as how it should be marketed, operated, and invoiced.

Enterprises can utilize any combination of the Service Productization Framework that is applicable to their specific needs. We can help to create new services, assess and improve existing services or develop a company-wide Services Development Lifecycle Planning program.
