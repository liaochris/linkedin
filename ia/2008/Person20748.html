Lillian Walker-DPME’s Summary

Started in Real Estate Lending as a Senior at USC in 1987, upon graduation worked for a mortgage bank in La Mirada. 1989 Started 1st Pacific Mortgage in Huntington Beach Calif.
Actively involved with the USC School of Policy and Planning mentor program (MPA) and have been an active participant in Clubnet and have used business coaching through Buffini & Company to grow the business. We support several local non profits i.e. Christ Presbyterian Church and Eagle Ridge Camps and Conference Center. We have a balanced approached to business and our clients come to us 100% by referral. We serve them before, during and after the transaction closes. We expect to have approximately 150 agents over the next 5 years and appropriate support staff. Everyone on our team participates in corporate growth. Our team members and our clients are our most valuable asset. Our mission is to facilitate educate, encourage and empower the clients we serve.

We are looking for busy Executives and Business Owners/Entrepreneurs who want to move and sell their home or buy a home and seek expert real estate service. We use state of the art technology to save time and streamline the process of buying or selling a home. Whether they live in Huntington Beach now or want to live in Huntington Beach we can help them. Just give me a call with their name and phone number and my team will be sure to take excellent care of them.Last but not least I have a transaction team and network of experts such as Tax Strategist, CPA, Lender, Contractor, Designer, Stager, Escrow, Title just to name a few to facilitate a full service experience from the moment we meet moving forward.
I appreciate your taking the time to read my profile and thank you for the opportunity to serve you and your referrals. My most sincerest thanks and have a fantastic rest of the day!
