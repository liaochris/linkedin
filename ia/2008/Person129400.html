Michael Bleyhl’s Summary

My goal is to help companies build a viable culture of learning while continuously fostering business growth and global competitiveness, which can be accomplished by:

• Identifying and nurturing key competencies worthy of strategic investment
• Creating a shared vision of learning’s added value in business
• Changing ingrained models of training and development
• Systemizing and integrating a standard learning process
• Establishing a global, technical and professional infrastructure for learning
• Aligning team learning with individual and organizational measurements
• Customizing for culture and
• Personalizing learning.
