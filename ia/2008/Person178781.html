Scott Paul Dunham’s Summary

Festival Manager of the Inaugural Detroit Windsor International Film Festival, coming in June 2008!

Founder of the Creative Energy Alliance and author of "Unconditional Networking"! The CEA is a growing network of talented and inspiring creative people who believe in collaboration and the power of community.

With David Huntoon, founded the Center for Creative Technologies, offering creative thinking classes to enhance our mental acuity and innovation.

Founded the SEMAFX Network, an amazing network of enthusiastic and talented digital artists! We've held a monthly event every month since April 1998. SEMAFX is the ACM SIGGRAPH Pro Chapter for SE Michigan.

Many experiences: Owner of Mosaic Marketing Services; Creative Director at TeleVideo Studios; Creative Supervisor and Rw Productions; 3D Manager at Armstrong-White; CGI Project Manager for BlueWater Technologies & eVox Productions.

A strong advocate for the Arts and evangelist for Michigan's amazing talent and resources for Entertainment Technology, including film, video, music, animation, web, writing, performance, design and more.

Produced, directed, animated & edited over 550 corporate videos, documentaries & TV shows. I've designed motion graphics for "Inside CART" on Fox and ESPN, & created animations for Ford, General Motors and Chrysler.

Visual Effects Supervisor for "InZer0", a popular SciFi television series. I also have contributed effects work for several other indie films.

Charter Board Member and Chair of the original Professional Chapter of ACM Siggraph.

Acted as spokesperson for Adobe, Autodesk; lectured at High Schools, Film schools and area clubs & taught animation to 5th graders for the ELCFF.

At SEMAFX, I'm very proud of the dozens of jobs and opportunities I generated. I've a knack for inspiring people to "Get Moving!" and I thoroughly enjoy what I do!

I'm the proud father of two amazing boys and husband in training to my lovely wife Meaghan! 143

Scott Paul Dunham’s Specialties:

I've been called a networking guru, tree shaker, advocate, evangelist, cheerleader, motivator and schmoozer! Promoting talent, resources and technology, and galvanizing the power of our combined Creative Energies is my gift, passion and mission, all rolled into one!
