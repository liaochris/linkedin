Jonathan Lundqvist’s Summary

My unique expertise is in-depth understanding of communicative processes in a global environment in combination with a broad technical know-how. First, during the years I was as a consultant, I acquired practical knowledge of web development. Second, my years at the university gave me the opportunity to focus more on how to use these technologies to help less developed communities. Specifically I concentrated on how technology can be utilized to strengthen democratic values and fight censorship through increased citizen participation and access to the public sphere.

Jonathan Lundqvist’s Specialties:

Internet concepts and development, censorship circumvention, web technologies, media and democratization.
