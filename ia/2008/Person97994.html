Josh Miller’s Summary

Resourceful leader with 8+ years experience in the water & wastewater industry, specifically with membrane separation technologies. Strong technical and management skills have resulted in rapid progression from Lab Technician to a series of management roles spanning new business development, marketing, product development, and customer support. Persuasive communicator and outstanding relationship builder, recognized for quickly gaining and maintaining the trust of key clients, as well as delivering powerful visual and verbal presentations that lead to increased sales.
Innovative thinker, able to bring proactive, dynamic solutions to difficult situations. Always calm under intense pressure, with superior decision-making abilities.
Possesses a thirst for knowledge; keen aptitude for both learning and applying new concepts quickly.
Successful mentor and coach, skilled at inspiring confidence, leading by example, and managing high-performance teams committed to success.
