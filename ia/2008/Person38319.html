Claudio Pousa’s Summary

Ten year's experience in sales force management consulting, including sales force training and coaching, in Argentina and other Latin American countries including Chili, Uruguay, Peru and Ecuador. Client's served include ALCON Labs, REUTERS, DUKE Energy, Ericsson, Electrolux, Sherwin Williams, KNAUF GmbH, and others.

Professor in graduate, posgraduate, and Executive Training Programs in Argentina (IDEA, ITBA, Universidad de Palermo, Universidad del Salvador) and Uruguay (Universidad ORT del Uruguay).

Former Product Manager in Philips Argentina, and Marketing Manager in CIDEF.

Master in Strategic Management (UADE, Universidad Argentina de la Empresa), Electrical Engineer (ITBA, Instituto Tecnológico de Buenos Aires), Mechanical Engineer (ITBA), Bac in Engineer (ITBA).

Presently, Doctoral student at Université de Sherbrooke, Québec, Canada.

Claudio Pousa’s Specialties:

Sales Force Management, Training and Coaching. Value generation strategies, Value creation through sales force and distribution channels. Relational and Consultative Selling.
