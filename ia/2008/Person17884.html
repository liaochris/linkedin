REG ATHWAL’s Summary

Reg Athwal founded the RAW GROUP in early 2002. He previously held the position of CEO of Specialist Capital Group (SCG) from late 1997 to early 2002, where he created and built a diverse range of European-based human capital consulting, executive search and interim staffing enterprises from start-up to $20 million in total revenues during this five-year period.

RAW GROUP (RAW LTD and RAW LLC) has offices in the United Kingdom and United Arab Emirates, with the headquarters based in Dubai. Reg Athwal is also Co-Founder & Managing Partner of ONE TVO, a new television broadcasting platform, launching officially in Dubai and Abu Dhabi in 2008.

Reg Athwal is a serial entrepreneur and world leading expert in 'Unleashing Your DNA' combined with a solid track record in human capital management. Reg Athwal has inspired millions of people with his inspirational business speeches and transformed the lives and businesses of over 10,500 CEOs and 150,000 managers in 36 countries since 2002.

Reg is a highly rated Livewire Conference Chairman and Moderator for large conferences and in-company sales incentive events in the Gulf Region and India. In addition he opens and closes conferences as a keynote speaker internationally.

Visit www.rawltd.com for indepth information, free articles, speech vidoes, free books, speech menus, photos, testimonials and much more.

Reg is the co-author of US bestseller Wake Up…Live the Life You Love series and the international published RAW Questions book-journal series. His new book comes out in 2009 titled: 'Unleash Your DNA - 18 Success Principles for Achieving Remarkable Results Fast!'

Reg is an elected member of the International Federation for Professional Speakers (IFFPS) and Professional Speakers Association of the United Kingdom (PSA-UK) and President for 2006-2007. Reg is also the Founding Chairman of the Professional Speakers Association of the Middle East (PSA-ME).
