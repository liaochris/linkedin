C. Erik Wilkinson’s Summary

InfoPro21 is led by C. Erik Wilkinson, a reference librarian with nearly a decade of professional service (public libraries - adult services). He has a social-sciences background and holds a Masters of Library Science from the University of North Texas, Class of 1998.

Erik excels at research for college students/academics & businesses/professionals; This includes document delivery.
