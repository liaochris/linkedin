Shaun Zelber’s Summary

I grew up in up-state New York near Woodstock where I started my schooling. I then lived in the Bahamas for a short stint before moving to France at 14. I finished high school in France before going for social work in India for 8 months.

My professional career started in France and has spanned India, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Italy, Spain, the UK and the USA.

I have worked extensively in software and I am particularly interested in both the mobile internet and offshore development. Which is why I have founded a mobile aggregation and solutions provider with an offshore development team.
