Michiel Bartels’s Summary

Michiel Bartels has a wide spectrum of experience in both shoot and postproduction of film, broadcast and crossmedia projects. While studying televisionjournalism he started his own company, producing and designing various projects for television and the corporate market. In 2001 he joined the Bijker group when he started as a postproduction manager at djbfacilitair. During these years he was involved in projects for various dutch public broadcasters and corporate clients. in these years he specialized in 'special projects' for the entertainment industry. In 2006 he joined another company in the Bijker group, Bijker productions. He is the executive producer for the EO Youthday event in the Gelredome stadium (33.000 visitors) wich is broadcasted live on national tv for 7 hours every year.
Currently Michiel is further developing his production skills and is eager on getting ahead of technical developments. He continues to search for the outer boundaries of the current state of technology.

Michiel Bartels’s Specialties:

Producing commercials, events and special projects in the enterainment industry
HD and HD Mpeg technical consultancy, technical shoot and postproduction management and consultancy.
