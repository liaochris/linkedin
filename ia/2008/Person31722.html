Cassie Rose Carnahan’s Summary

I have been a licensed Cosmetologist since 2001. I love the connection I have with my clients. I actually am most of my clients Hair Stylist and Personal Trainer. I care about my clients, and support and encourage them to strive to live a healthy active lifestyle.

I work for myself as a Personal Trainer anywhere (in home, outdoors, gyms, etc). My Personal Training targets 3 vital areas of health and fitness. Resistance Training, Cardiac/Respiratory Training, and a Healthy Balanced Diet. There are no secrets to good health. All you have to do is invest some time and effort in your health and the rest will follow. I'm here to help you reach any and all health and fitness goals!

Also, I have Competed and won in Figure Competitions. I'm a Cyclist, and I am learning the Martial Art of Muay Thai.
