Matti Heikkurinen’s Summary

Computer scientists turned into a management-type. Specialising in the areas of computing, R&D and technology transfer. Made a brief stop at the role of a software engineer during the journey...

Interested in the Grid broad sense. In other words, not just as a technology where the Grid might offer a bit more "bang for the buck" in providing the basic IT infrastructure services. I think the really interesting things start to happen when the paradigm - with its virtual organisations and inter-organisational aspects - starts to influence the ways organisations work and collaborate. My "dream projects" are ones that try to understand and speed up this interaction between technologies and organisations.

Matti Heikkurinen’s Specialties:

Lots of experience in communication, internal marketing and organizational issues in setting up and running research collaborations. Been a manager or a participant in several exploratory "skunk-works"-type of research projects - especially around Grid and cluster computing technologies.
