Andrew Soderberg’s Summary

30 years experience in marketing and product development in several markets; Internet, consumer electronics, PCs and peripherals, and broadcast TV. Successful product development experience managing Windows, Macintosh, and web based products and services.

At Atari, Inc, I was involved in the development of the first computer and laserdisc interactive information kiosks used in retail, Product manager for the Atari 600XL and 1200XL home computers, and was also the Production Manager for several of Atari's television commercials, one of which won a Clio Award.

At ViMart Corp., I further developed and marketed interactive kiosks to sell software at Sears and K-Marts stores nationwide. As Director of Marketing, at Aapps Corp., I was the first to market digital video recording products for the Macintosh, before Apple’s QuickTime was developed.

I joined Apple Computer in 1990, as Evangelist responsible for the adoption of QuickTime and Firewire by 3rd party developers. During my tenure at Apple, I created strategic partnerships with over 100 companies worldwide to develop products using QuickTime. As Apple’s Advanced Technology CPU Evangelist, I was one of the five-member team that managed the development of the 660 & 840 AV Macintosh. I am co-author of the book Desktop Video Studio published by Random House in June of 1995.

As a pioneer of multimedia – from my roots as a director in broadcast television, to my role on Apple's QuickTime team, founder of Merlin Media, and as CTO of Interact – I have built a career and several businesses based upon my experience in UI design, computer, video, and Internet technologies, providing new-media solutions for corporate and institutional clients.

3 years ago I relocated to the La Crosse, WI area to work closer with our strategic partner, Interact Communications, providing web services and solutions to the national community college market. As of December 2006, I joined Interact as CTO & VP. of Web services.

Andrew Soderberg’s Specialties:

Internet technology consuting, ADA 508 compliance, Enterprise content management systems, Corporate and Institutional web site development, Interactive media development (both CD-ROM and DVD)
