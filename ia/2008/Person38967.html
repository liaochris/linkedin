Coach Joan McMahon’s Summary

I am President / CEO of Career Solutions - Unleashing the Excellence Within! I provide a unique array of Corporate Coaching, Training and Human Resources (HR) Consulting Services to Corporate America and private individuals. I have a useful blend of HR, Coaching, Career Development and Training Design & Delivery skills providing my clients with insights from a variety of perspectives.

Corporate Services:

1. Corporate Coaching - for Executives, Managers and Sales Professionals in the areas of Performance Enhancement, Personal Development, Sales Skill Development, and Career Development.

2. Corporate Training - for teams and sales professionals including the Get Clients NOW! ™ Program, Myers Briggs Type Indicator ™ Assessment, Career Development & Transition Training and Custom Corporate Training Programs.

3. Corporate HR Consulting - provides advice to leaders on Performance Management, Staffing, Training & Development, and Outplacement Services.

The Private Career Coaching side of Career Solutions provides professionals with a one stop shop for all of their career development needs including:

* Career discovery and clarification coaching including career assessments
* Career transition planning & Career Management
* Job search coaching & resume writing

I use only proven methods and tools to help my clients to find a more satisfying career for themselves. I use a combination of online tools, individual coaching, and group coaching to meet my clients' needs. I have an excellent track record of success working with all 4 generations in the workforce and have a dynamic, energetic and caring style. The # 1 outcome my clients get is more career clarity which comes from self-knowledge and a rekindling of the use of their motivated skills. This work is incredibly satisfying to me and I am honored to be invited in to the growth and development of facinating people!

Coach Joan McMahon’s Specialties:
