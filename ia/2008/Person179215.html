Srikanth Sastry’s Summary

I am a graduate student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas A&M University . I work with Dr. Scott Pike on fault tolerant distributed computing. My research focuses on fault detection, containment, and masking in distributed systems. My focus is on solving coordination problems like distributed mutual exclusion and distributed Dining Philosophers under partial synchrony in the presence of crash faults, and additionally investigating the weakest models of partial synchrony in which the above problems (and their variants) are solvable.

Srikanth Sastry’s Specialties:

Fault tolerant systems, distributed algorithms, prototyping, system model analysis
