Abdul-Rahman Advany’s Summary

My name is Abdul-Rahman Advany and I am a social strategy consultant. I advise on the usage of new media and internet technologies.

SOCIAL STRATEGY is the involvement of employees and customers to innovate using new media by applying internet technologies. This innovation can be done in areas like marketing, advertising, customer service, research & development, design of products & services, production, sales, or distribution. This strategy is used to inform, communicate, entertain, interact, categorize, analyze, and facilitate groups of people by applying technologies like blogs, wikis, vodcasts, podcasts, widgets, tagging, analytical tools or collaborative filtering.

SOCIAL STRATEGY helps you to leverage the wisdom of a collective by allow big groups of people to participate in the co-creation of your products & services, gain the insights of into opinions of your customers to improve your products & services, and stimulate the sharing of knowledge of between employees by sharing of best practices.

SOCIAL STRATEGY is for organizations and individuals who want to compete in this digital age where innovation disrupts entire markets. Traditional research & development seems to be inflexible, costly, time consuming and doesn't produce effective results. And innovations like low-price strategies are difficult to sustain.

Need help with your SOCIAL STRATEGY? Contact me at abdulrahman@advany.com or +31 (0) 6 24290182.
