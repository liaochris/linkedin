Pancho Vilaseca’s Summary

Born in 1977 in Buenos Aires. In my first years I lived in Bariloche and San Martin de los Andes. Went to elementary school in Key Biscayne and started Jr High School in Philadelphia, USA. Came back to Buenos Aires to complete High School at Colegio Champagnat. That's part of the reasons why I like to travel, meet new people, have the ability to adapt to different cultures, being a diplomatic and flexible person.

My first work experience was related to International Business, were I begun interesting myself in International Marketing Strategies, Internet and Technology. Years later started working as Internet Project Leader in Establecimiento Las Marias (worlds largest Yerba Mate producer).

My diverse background, knowledge and skills make me a very creative, pro-active, result oriented Internet & Marketing professional. Able to manage simple ideas, converting them in to great projects.

Feel free to contact me - panchovilaseca@gmail.com.
