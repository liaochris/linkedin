Jared Shaw’s Summary

About Us:
31337 Recruiters is a third party recruiting agency based in Vancouver BC. All we do is video game industry recruiting and nothing else. Next to your buddy working at the studio you want in at we are the best people to come to for a job. With over 100+ games studios in Vancouver, we can help you sort through the opportunities and introduce you to the right people whether you are a programmer, artist, producer or otherwise.

Does it cost me?
No, game studios hire and pay us to find them good people.

Who do you work with?
If they are making games in Vancouver chances are we have a relationship with them.

Why not apply on my own?
Think of us kind of like agents. Pro athletes and creative talent like actors hire agents to find the best deal and fit for their careers so they can focus on what they do best so why not you? Do know of every studio and opportunity going on in Vancouver?, we do it's our job.

Rather than sending your resume into some anonymous hr@address, we speak directly with the hiring managers about your candidacy, so you find out immediately if you are of interest to them and most of all if the studio is right for your because there's nothing worse than wasting time interviewing for jobs that don't fit your interest.

Visit us at http://www.31337recruiters.com
