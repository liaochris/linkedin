Fernanda Wessels’s Summary

My professional experience covers a wide range, reflecting my interests and capabilities. I was trained and worked as a cultural anthropologist researcher, teacher social studies, (team)coach, trainer and internet recruiter.
I'm working on Ph.D. on Surinamese foodcultures in the Netherlands.
Because of my experience as a high educated, black, female entrepreneur I am very interested in women's issues and diversity management.

Fernanda Wessels’s Specialties:

The last years I founded several small companies/agencies. I like to advise, assist and coach other starting(female) entrepeneurs. I am a diversity consultant, especially for companies that want to recruit high educated (migrant) women. As a trainer I specialize in Belbin teamroles, internet recruitment, empowerment (migrant) women.
