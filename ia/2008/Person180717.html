Stephen Bridges’s Summary

I am in the midst of a career change, moving to corporate accounting from commercial banking. Most recently, I managed the business aspect of a general contracting firm, who was a former bank customer of mine, including the initiation of the accounting function. I am a third generation banker with experience, top to bottom, in the financial services industry. I have traveled around the State of Texas as a consultant to commercial banking institutions as well as management of a DFW commercial bank branch.

I am pursuing the opportunity to complete a Master of Science in Accounting with the thought of sitting for the CPA.

I am a Notary Public with my Commission expiring in 2010.
