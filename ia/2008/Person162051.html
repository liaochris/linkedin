Robert Joseph’s Summary

Wine International Magazine which I launched in 1983 won several awards and was internationally praised for the way it followed the development of New world wines in the 1980s and 1990s.

The International Wine Challenge, which I launched in 2003 wih Charles Metcalfe, became the biggest wine competition in the world, with over 9,000 entries.

The International Wine Challenges which I ran in China, Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong, Vietnam and Thailand were the first events of their kind in this region.

As a wine taster, I have been invited to judge wines in countries ranging from Australia and Argentina to Spain and South Africa.

As an industry commentator, I have addressed industry audiences in Australia (keynote), New Zealand, US, Chile, Argentina, Portugal, South Africa, Spain, France, Germany, Italy.

As a public speaker, I have spoken to groups including guests of the Royal Bank of Canada, IBM, the Royal Bank of Scotland, Towry Law, Futures & Options Exchange...

The Wine Travel Guide to the World broke new ground in wine tourism

The Robert Joseph Good Wine Guide was described as the best Wine Guide in the world by the Gourmand World Cookbook Awards

The Complete Encyclopedia of Wine won the Wine Guild of the UK Premier Award

French Wines is a best seller in English French, German, Italian, Dutch, Chinese and Japanese

My recently launched wine brands are now selling well in the N. America (le Grand Noir sold over 800,000 bottles last year).
I am now also editor at large of Meininger's Wine Business International magazine which I helped to launch late in 2006.

Robert Joseph’s Specialties:

wine evaluation, wine brand creation, publishing, event (especially wine competition) organisation, industry analysis, public speaking, book authorship/editing/publishing.
