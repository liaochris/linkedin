Victor Edinian’s Summary

Interested in Strategic Advancement, specializing in Online Marketing and Business Development opportunities.

Started my Internet Marketing Experiences in 1998 at W3PR, Inc. Where, I was very fortunate to work with Industry leaders in online ad placements, affiliate marketing and Search optimization. W3PR, Inc. laid the foundation to my marketing successes for which I am very grateful.

In 2000 went on to eGadgets to work as their Internet Marketing Director, growing my department from $25,000 per month to $250,000 per month in Online Sales. Here I had the opportunity to work with a world class CEO who was my mentor in Corporate business practices and practical Marketing applications, among many specialties.

In 2001, went on to form my own company with 2 of my college friends. Headed the Marketing and Finance department there. We grew to over 180,000 customers and over $3.2 Million in annual revenue, when we sold our business in 2004 to a manufacturing vendor in Southern California. Since then, the company eventually declined, due to management hardship (unfortunately). (Was a great business).

After wearing many hats at ezinkjets.com, went on to form an Internet Affiliate Marketing agency. Mainly focusing on High Keyword Volume SEM campaigns, Search Optimization, Affiliate / Merchant Relationships, Metrical analysis, Viral marketing, and many web 2.0 applications, including video optimization and blogging.

Currently applying my background at a leading horoscope and psychic network company, managing Search, Display and other direct marketing and strategic partnership/development opportunities.

Always interested in connecting and discussing opportunities, theories, applications, and recent industry trends, as they pertain to the Internet, mobile, and business landscape.

Also interested in living life to the fullest ;-)
