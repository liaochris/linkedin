Steve Hersberger’s Summary

We universally get three questions:

1. How do we properly identify customer evangelists?
2. How do we activate them as part of an effective marketing program?
3. How do we measure the incrimental performance of customer evangelists and word of mouth recommendations?

Our products, The Evangelist Effect™ and SpringBoard™ cultivate Customer Evangelists, turning them into a powerful brand asset.

Evangelists become a dynamic credible, persuasive and passionateword-of-mouth channel that exponentially leverages marketing dollars by influencing others to try, use, experience or promote product or services. For those organizations with the right dynamics, customer evangelism is an essential part of the marketing mix. It uses customer conversations and interactions to cut through the clutter and deliver a highly credible, believable market message.

SpringBoard utilizes proxies who are Category Influencers to assist in creating WOM and activating dormant evangelists.

ComBlu maintains an exclusive alliance with The Keller Fay Group. This alliance offers brands a turn-key solution to WOM generation, customer advocacy engagment and performance measurement.

I maintain equity and director positions at Symbios Medical Products and Hiring Smart. Symbios is the manufacturer of a 'pain pump',which serves as a viable alternative to narcotic pain-management for post-operative relief. Hiring Smart is a leader in Applicant Tracking Systems.

I also sit on the board of trustees for two Indiana University Graduate School of Business Entrepreneurship scholarships.
