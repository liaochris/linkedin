Rick Rumbarger’s Summary

Results oriented executive with over fifteen years of progressive hands on technology industry experience for a variety of consumer, business, and government oriented organizations. Has had P&L responsibility for budgets in excess of $60 million dollars. Has created and lead successful teams of professionals in product development, product management, strategic procurement, and IT operations. Extensive experience in Internet, telecommunications, energy, and professional consulting related verticals.
