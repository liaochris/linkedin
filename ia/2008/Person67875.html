Gaspare Marturano’s Summary

Gaspare brings more than 17 years of high-tech sales and marketing experience to ClickToBoat.com. He has had a distinguished career leading complex and challenging IT initiatives for many industry-leading firms (SIRS Publishing, Primedia, and ProQuest Information & Learning). Gaspare oversees the positioning, and promotion of ClickToBoat.com online navigation solutions, as well as revenue-generating online advertising sales. Prior to joining ClickToBoat.com, Gaspare served as Vice President of Sales & Marketing and Chief Technology Officer at TSC Consulting, Inc., where he led the company’s marketing initiatives, including product management, product marketing, and demand creation.
