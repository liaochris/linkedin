Alan Daly’s Summary

Worked for 8 years with Accenture in the Communications Practice based in San Francisco. Led organization change, supply chain, business program implementations in several Tier 1 Mobile and Fixed Operators including Pacific Bell, SBC, AT&T Wireless, U.S. West and Eircom Ireland. Led projects including organization strategy, acquisition and divestiture, IT programs, network build and operations improvement. End to end business process design and implementation.

Joined Portal Software as Vice President of Services based in California. Transferred to Japan as Vice President of Sales and Services. Completed successful turnaround.

Focused on clear and pragmatic change leading to substantial increase in business performance. Action oriented and bottom line results focused.

Presently focused on the development of new business territory for Arantech - the leading global provider of Customer Experience Management (CEM) solutions to the mobile industry. Responsible for the greater Asia Pacific region. Arantech is the global leader in CEM solutions for the worlds most successful mobile operators and our value proposition returns on average an increase in ARPU in the order of 1.5% and an ROI of 300%+ in the first year alone. We allow operators to see the actual experience of every customer on their network, across all services, in real time. At present, operators have no insight into circa 70% of the issues that a customer experiences (dropped calls, SMS failure, inability to connect to data services, roaming issues etc.). Our solution is unique and market changing - proven by a significant global customer base and substantial year over year growth.
