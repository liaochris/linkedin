Ashwanth Fernando’s Summary

Building robust, high performance enterprise applications based on Java/Java EE technologies. Specialities include development and design of enterprise applications, software framework development, payment gateways, fraud checking web services, Service Oriented Architecture based enterprise applications (SOA), functional knowledge on retailing and e-commerce and knowledge of many tools and processes. Extensive experience in talking to customer and framing a correct conceptual model of the problem domain.

I strive to be technology agnostic, believe that the right set of technologies should be used to solve a particular business requirement, reduce complexity and to increase development agility. There is no "one-size-fits-all" platform to solve business problems.

Furthermore, I follow pragmatic software development processes that do not focus too much on ceremony, rather increase agility without sacrificing on correctness or customer confidence.
