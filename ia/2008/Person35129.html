Chris DaSilva’s Summary

I am a freelance web designer based in NYC (but available for projects anywhere!). My expertise lies in designing and developing web sites using today's web standards. My sites are built using valid XHTML and CSS code, and all are custom designed. Custom, meaning that I do NOT use a WYSIWYG (what you see is what you get) editor, instead all designs are created from scratch using Photoshop, and all code is developed from the ground up, ensuring completely one-of-a-kind web sites that work in all browsers and platforms (Mac/PC).

I invite you to visit my web site located at gfunkdesign.com to see samples of my work and learn more about me, and what I do.

I am available for freelance projects, so please don't hesitate to contact me if you have a project that you need help with.

* Besides just web sites, I also design logos, flyers, business cards and any other marketing materials needed. Contact me with your project details, and I will be sure to let you know exactly what I can do for you.

Thanks for visiting my page!

- Chris DaSilva
