Martin Smith’s Summary

Martin is a trainer and executive coach who specialises in working one to one with managers and with small groups to develop the capabilities of them and their teams to deliver improved business performance.

Martin’s training specialities include change management, executive coaching, team building including Belbin team roles profiling, and leadership and management skills. His drive is to improve performance by helping people make sense of it all. By working with managers to develop their self awareness and build on their strengths, they acquire new self confidence and discover the right approaches for them to lead and enable others.
