Alex McCall’s Summary

After a successful career as a freelance and staff writer in the print medium I became fascinated with the potential of online journalism and became one of the first editor/publishers to offer an online version of my print magazine. I quickly became adept at online media and advanced my freelance writing accordingly. The growing popularity of video in online media prompted my desire to go "back to J-school" and learn broadcast skills, which I found to be natural and perfect match for me.

As for professional goals, I hope to continue to grow, change and learn in my media career.
