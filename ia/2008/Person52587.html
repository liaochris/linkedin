Roberto Dal Medico’s Summary

Reporting to the Country CEO and also the Sales Directors in the United States and Germany, was responsible for the Commercial area with a direct team of 18 professionals and annual revenues of R$ 450 million. Defined commercial strategies and policies, developing a sales plan (monitoring and analyzing actual versus budgeted) and maintaining relations with customers. Participated in meetings in Germany, conducting presentations of the local market and business.
- Developed the pricing policy for new products.
- Negotiated prices with clients with the objective of recovering the profitability of projects/products.
- Prospected and closed new business.
- Managed projects from the time the account was won until the actual launching of products.
- Prepared and presented monthly reports about the business for the Parent Company Commercial Board.
- Participated in global projects in South Africa and United States, carrying out part of the development in Brazil, reporting and monitoring their progress and profitability.
- Defined the global strategy during meetings every six months in China, Italy and Germany for clients such as GM / FIAT and Ford with other Regional Directors.
- Together with the Project Engineering area, defined the strategy for changing products and technology so as to maintain the company’s competitiveness
