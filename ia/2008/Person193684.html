Tyler Osby’s Summary

I am the Wealth Creation Specialist and Co-Founder at Four Legacies Mortgage. We are Iowa's first socially responsible mortgage company and we work hard to raise the expectations of the mortgage industry.

We specialize in working with our clients in selecting the mortgage financing that is best suited with both their long and short term goals. We work with clients both purchasing or refinancing a home. We also adopt abandoned mortgages (see below for details).

Once our client’s loan closes, our work has just begun. We are also Iowa’s first mortgage management company. In short, we manage your home financing and continue to make sure it fit's your families long term goals and we are here to make adjustments as they are needed.

Here are some quick questions to consider:

If you...

- Haven't heard from your lender since closing.
- Never have had a formal mortgage review.
- Don't remember the terms of your mortgage(s).
- Don't have someone actively managing your mortgage.
- Don't know what a 'Fed Cut' means to your mortgage.
- Don't know your credit score.
- Want to save more money for retirement.
- Want to keep more of the money you make.
- Want to be pro-active in your overall financial plan.

If you answered 'No' to any of these, we should definitely talk. Chances are, your mortgage has been neglected and abandoned. Let us adopt it for free. The service of mortgage management is completely free.

You only think about home financing a few times during your life - We think about it every single day. It's your home and your future. It's our profession and our passion. I'm ready to work for your best interest.

For more information about the mortgage market and what we do, please check out my daily blog Wealth With Mortgage (www.wealthwithmortgage.com).
