Shana Webb (Webb.Shana@gmail.com)’s Summary

Shana Webb, B.A., M.S. Ed., also known as "Windy" to her family and friends, was born in Indiana and lived in an additional eight states before calling Georgia her home. Having started her career in Buckhead, immediately after graduating from the University of Florida, Shana says this is her “second tour of duty” through Atlanta and it just keeps getting better!

Nicknamed “Windy” after she began talking at a remarkably early age, it comes as no surprise to find that Shana has built an accomplished career in language arts. She has written sales materials for everything from General Motors online to colon cleansers, adult diapers and vitamin-stuffed donuts.

In the business arena, Ms. Webb is well versed in technical writing, business communications, Web content editing, corporate intelligence reports, key employee biographies, company stories, and marketing newsletters.

In the education industry, she is known by many as "Ms. Z." and has taught over the course of a decade in Pennsylvania, Texas and Georgia. She graduated at the top of her class from Duquesne University (Pi Lambda Theta) and has received many accolades for her performance-based approach in reading and writing.

In the creative arts, Shana is a published journalist and short story writer. Recognized by Norm Rohrer (Christian Writer's Guild) as "mirific" in her field, she currently organizes and facilitates workshops for women interested in becoming published writers.
