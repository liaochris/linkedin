Barry Thornton’s Summary

Mr. Thornton has been active in electronic circuit, software, and product design as well as management, marketing and sales for over 50 years starting in retail electronics sales at the age of 15. Toward the end of his college life in the late 60s he started the Quintessence Group, evolving the company from touring sound (Jethro Tull, Joe Cocker, Jimi Hendrix) in to a high-end hi-fi manufacture in the 1970s. After selling the Q-Group in the late 70s, he consulted for a wide range of high-tech and entertainment companies including Monster Cable, Fostex, SAE, Hasbro Toys, the Playboy Mansion, San Francisco Ballet and Opera, Analogic, Adcom, and others. He maintained a 20 year consulting and intermittent management role with Monster Cable through the late 1980s directing the Professional Products Division. In 1989 he became VP of Marketing and Engineering for OptoDigital Design (A Monster Cable division) suppling fiber optic content networking to the entertainment industry (Disneyland, Bulls Stadium, ABC, Televisia, etc.).

Scattered through this time period he started and sold Sierra Sales (a consumer electronics rep company), Electronic Technology Applications (audio systems for aircraft), Helm Control Systems (electronics for the recording industry) and in 1996 he founded EB Technology which became ClearCube Technology of which we was the CTO and Chief Evangelist for enterprise-level selling to the military, health care, and financial markets. In 2006 he retired from ClearCube Technology to go have more fun.

In 2007 he formed Austin Medical Research developing non-invasive medical products. He currently has 27 granted patents and 23 more in application.

Barry Thornton’s Specialties:

Talking and thinking on my feet, studying, observing, and figuring out what is going on, causing people to confront "truth-aches" and get through them successfully, evangelizing business and technology. I am an active inventor and product developer engaged in an in depth study of sales, tribal and long-tail marketing with a special interest in how to sell around price in dealing with emerging counties.
