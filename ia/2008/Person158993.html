Anthony Renzette’s Summary

Frustrated golfer and land-locked sailor stuck in a marketing executive's body. I have worked in Internet marketing, product development (organic and M&A-based) since early 1994. During that time, I was fortunate enough to have worked along side of many very creative and talented high tech product developers and business managers. Some of their talent luckily rubbed off on me as well. My areas of responsibility have included product development ($300M/year business line), P&L management ($120M+/year business and product lines), product marketing and business development.

In addition to my high tech experience, I have worked at Anheuser-Busch (sales and marketing), numerous political organizations and a few non-technical small business ventures.
