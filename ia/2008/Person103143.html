Kathie Nelson’s Summary

Connectworks is an Oregon based firm serving growth oriented companies with executive consulting and strategic business development strategies since 2002. Kathie Nelson, President, is the founder and creator of Networking for Busy People™ series as well as an author, speaker and business strategist.

A note from Kathie: The market has been in a gradual state of evolvement for the last 7-10 years. To survive and thrive in this current economy it is critical to keep our business model relevant, package and price our services to reach savvy buyers and adapt sustainable sales and marketing systems to engage propsective buyers and partners in conversations that connect.

Bottom line: when we are ineffective in activities to produce sales, we don't serve our customers, our companies, or ourselves! The truth is, we only have some much time, energy, and resources. Now more than ever is the time to work smart.

How do I know this? I started my first business at 29 and which failed by 31. I picked myself up from this experience and went on to build a successful distributorship over the next 10 years. During this intensive business growth period I discovered the true meaning of building a strategic network. I set and achieved a goal of doubling my sales production in one year. I was one of 22 out of 14,000 sales representatives to be recognized for this achievement. What I discovered is that you can increase productivity without increasing the work...by using strategic partnerships! All this while coping with a physical handicap incurred from a near fatal auto accident and raising my young sons. I understand about working with stress, goals, and limitations.

I continue to use the same principles that doubled my sales to build my current company Connectworks. I am passionate about equipping others to work smarter, building from their strengths to better serve their customers and grow strong profitable companies.

Kathie Nelson’s Specialties:

Doubled sales production in one year, built successful sales organization, and founder of Networking for Busy People. My combined experience in business management, sales, and operations are real life tools you can use for practical business development & marketing. My objective is equipping you, the busy person, to work from your strengths and do more business easily while providing exceptional service.
