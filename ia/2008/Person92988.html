Joanne Davis’s Summary

~ 2007 is Done! Make 2008 Unforgettable! ~

With over 23 years in the real estate industry, Joanne brings a deep knowledge and expertise of land development, custom lots, new construction and commercial real estate, Entitlement Consulting as well as re-sale residential. Understanding client relationships, Joanne focuses on perfecting client communications. This allows marketing programs under her direction to reach their full potential.
Since 1983, Joanne has served on the El Dorado Hills/Salmon Falls Area Planning Advisory Committee, several HOA Design Review Committees and Land Development Consulting along with her thriving real estate career.

Turn to Joanne Davis when you want seasoned real estate professional who:
• Is committed to your success, whether selling or buying.
• Listens to—and hears—your specific needs and goals whether selling or buying.
• When selling, promote your property or project as if it were my own.
• Remain competitive, spirited, and flexible in an ever-changing real estate market.
• Anticipate and overcome your challenges and the challenges of the real estate market.
