David Ponce’s Summary

Over 12 combined years of professional award winning, user-centered visual design with the ability to create and translate complex designs into online interfaces such as web sites and web-based software products, as well as print related material. Most recently, I have been involved in Web 2.0 applications/plug-ins such as IPTV, mobile, toolbar, sidebar, deskbar and widgets.

• Contributed to one of the "Top Ten" most visited properties on the web (formerly GainPublishing.com) and two “Top Tier” e-commerce web sites (Genealogy.com and FamilyTreeMaker.com) with highly creative art direction and user-centered UI design.

• Also, contributed to 5 “Top Tier” web-based software applications (eWallet, DateManager, DashBar, PrecisionTime and Family Tree Maker) with iconography, UI visual design and advertising campaigns.

• Exceptionally artistic and creative in producing compelling online advertisements from prototype to deployment.

• Instrumental in blending the "look and feel" of client's logo identity and product branding within design creatives.

• Strong team player with the ability to work independently.

• Proven ability to manage, influence, motivate, and provide clear direction to designers

• Excellent communication skills interfacing with internal and external clients and vendors.

• Exceptional record meeting deadlines and delivering assignments consistently.
