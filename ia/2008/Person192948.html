Paul Johnson’s Summary

I'm always looking for trouble. Why? Because I work with organizations to convert trouble into double and triple digit performance breakthroughs. I am in the breakthrough business.

I study leaders who create business breakthroughs; folks like Steve Jobs, Michael Bloomberg, and Mary Kubitskey. I review and write about business breakthroughs. Then I apply what I've learned through my various businesses to help our clients achieve results.

Leaders hire me when they want to make a statement. "Trouble is not a problem. Complacency is." I often speak at association conferences, company meetings and sales rallies. Attendees find my delivery of this serious message from the perspective of "The World's Only Professional Trouble Maker"™ to be fun, informative and funny.

Organizations hire me when they want to see results. Together we create a legion of Trouble Breakers who become an unstoppable force for breakthrough performance improvement within their companies.

• Authorship:
Author of Let Your Customer Sell You, and co-author of Fantastic Customer Service Inside and Out. Breakthrough advice regularly appears in many national and regional publications such as Selling Power Magazine, The Atlanta Journal-Constitution, and Roller Skating Industry Magazine.

• Other mentionables:
- Past president of National Speakers Association (NSA) Georgia State Chapter (NSAGA)
- Founding board member of the Technology Association of Georgia (TAG)
- Co-Founder of the Technology Marketing Association in Atlanta (TMA)
- VP of Communications for Speakers Roundtable, the only Toastmasters International advanced club in Atlanta.
