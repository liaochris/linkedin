Anisha Gupta’s Summary

As a Business Development Manager, my role in SystemGuru is to engage with new clients and make sure we stay in touch with the existing clients that we have with us from past so many years now.

SystemGuru is an IT Services and Solutions Company with Head office in NJ and Branch office in Chicago. We are into IT business from more then 8 yrs now, we work with all Industry verticals like Financial Services (Banking, Trading, Insurance, Asset Management, etc) Pharma, Public Sector, Retail, Telecom, Manufacturing etc providing them support in project executions and end to end IT services.

We offer wide range of services and solution in different technology domains including; Web enabled application development (J2EE, Microsoft.Net), Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence, RDBMS Programming and Administration (Oracle, SQL*Server & DB2) Data Modeling and Enterprise Data architecture, Application design and Architecture Unix system administration (HP-UX, Solaris, AIX,) Core systems development (C++, Unix, Messaging, Embedded systems) ERP ( Oracle, PeopleSoft & SAP) Application server administration (Websphere, Weblogic, MS Sharepoint Portal) Automated Testing on Mercury tools, Manual Testing etc.

We have over 100 employees working with our top clients in different industry verticals all across US with complete domain understanding and excellent experience.

We also have very strong recruitment team, who is working closely with clients to determine the exact qualifications and skills needed in a prospect, and we interview candidates extensively to ensure their experience and needs are appropriate for the job and culture offered by our clients.

Our recruiters stay actively involved through the client interview and follow-up process. Our objective is to launch long-term, successful relationships between the individuals we place and our clients.
