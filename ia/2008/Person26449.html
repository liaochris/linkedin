Brandon Waldo’s Summary

Semiconductor design consultant with over 17 years of ASIC and microprocessor design experience. Worked solo and partnered with consulting teams to develop and deploy RTL to GDSII design flows to customers on multiple diverse projects. Specialties include low-power, physical design, RTL to GDSII design flows, and signal integrity.

=== RECENT PROJECTS ===
- Implemented RTL to GDSII 65nm multi-supply DSP core design for wireless application chip including MTCMOS power-down flow development
- Development on 65nm Multi-VDD physical synthesis flow for large wireless customer
- Developed power-aware RTL to GDSII flows, enhancements and instruction for several global customers
- Generated and evaluated extensive RTL to post-route low-power flows with ICC backend and PrimeTime-PX analysis
- Deployed, enhanced and configured Synopsys Pilot RTL to GDSII flows for multiple customers (including 65nm, Multi-VDD and technology library data-prep)
- Implemented digital core from RTL to GDSII for mixed-signal mobile communications chip
- Created custom integration flow enhancements for major IP core provider
- Implemented place and route, full-chip SI STA, and IR Drop analysis for PCI Express bridge chip
- Created and integrated full-chip PrimeTime-SI analysis flows, produced front-end design speed enhancements and consulted on design flow productivity improvements for multiple global engagements
