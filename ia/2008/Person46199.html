David Anderson’s Summary

Over six years of experience working in the biomedical/biotechnology field. Prior to Veritude, I worked at Biogen Idec, Genzyme, and Massachusetts General Hospital. My experience includes roles in diagnostics, quality management, and product development - with special expertise in flow cytometry. My work supported the drug discovery process for a robust R&D pipeline with oncologic, neurologic, and immunologic targets. My Quality experience includes the testing and product release of both FDA approved products and Phase II /III cellular therapeutic candidates indicated for orthopedic injuries, heart disease, kidney disease, and the treatment of severe burns. I currently support the recruiting efforts for Veritude’s biotechnology clients. I hold a Bachelor's degree from Clarkson University, and am pursuing a Biotechnology Master's degree at the Harvard Extension School.
