Robert Caldwell’s Summary

Robert J. Caldwell is a Attorney and Shareholder with the law firm of Kolesar & Leatham, Chtd. in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Mr. Caldwell received his Juris Doctorate from the University of Alabama School of Law in 1999. In law school, he was a member of the prestigious Bench & Bar Legal Honor Society and the Editor-in-Chief of The Alabama Column legal newspaper. Mr. Caldwell was Alabama's elected representative to the American Bar Association (ABA) Law Student Division.

Mr. Caldwell received his Bachelor of Arts degree from Miami University (Oxford, Ohio) in 1996. At Miami University, he was a member of the honors program, an Undergraduate Summer Scholar, a McMicken Scholarship recipient, and an ASG Student Senator. He was also a member of Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity, where he was elected as vice-president. In 1995, he worked as a legislative intern for U.S. Senator Mitch McConnell in Washington, D.C., advising the Senator on matters of military and foreign affairs.

Mr. Caldwell is a member of the State Bar of Nevada, the Kentucky Bar Association, and the State Bar of Indiana (inactive/good standing). He is also licensed to practice before the U.S. Supreme Court, the U.S. Courts of Appeal for the Sixth, Seventh, and Ninth Circuits, and the U.S. District Courts for the Western and Eastern Districts of Kentucky, the Southern and Northern Districts of Indiana, and the District of Nevada.

Mr. Caldwell is an active member of the leadership of the ABA's Tort Trial and Insurance Practice Section, where he presently serves as Chair-Elect of its Business Litigation Committee. Mr. Caldwell is also a Support Affiliate of the Souther Nevada CCIM Chapter (Commercial Real Estate) and a member of the Sports Lawyers' Association and the ABA's Young Lawyers Division.

Mr. Caldwell is a native of Akron, Ohio, by way of Louisville, Kentucky. He began his law practice with the Louisville firm of Morgan & Pottinger, P.S.C. in 1999.

Robert Caldwell’s Specialties:

Mr. Caldwell practices primarily in the areas of business/commercial litigation, sports law, consumer lending litigation, real estate and commercial landlord/tenant relations, and employment litigation. Mr. Caldwell regularly represents and advises businesses, property managers, commercial and consumer lenders, professional sports teams and professional athletes.
