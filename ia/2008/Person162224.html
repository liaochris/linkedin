Robert K. Logan’s Summary

Dr. Logan is
Prof. Emeritus of Physics at U. of Toronto,
Seniour fellow:
Inst. for Biocomplexity and Informatics - U. of Calgary
Beal Inst. for Strategic Creativity - Ont. College of Art & Design
Member of Science of Information Inst. Board

B.S. in '61 and a PhD in '65 from MIT

Active at McLuhan's Centre for Culture & Technology from 1974 to 1980 collaborating and publishing with Marshall McLuhan
Cross-appointed to Ont. Inst. for Studies in Education.
Member of the Pugwash Conferences on Science & World Affairs
Former policy advisor to Prime Minister Pierre Elliott Trudeau and numerous cabinet ministers.

Books

1. The Way Ahead for Canada; 2. Canada's Third Option; 3. The Alphabet Effect; 4. The Fifth Language; 5. The Sixth Language; 6. The Extended Mind: The Emergence of Language, Human Thought, and Culture;
7. Collaborate to Compete (co-authored with Louis Stokes); 8. Understanding New Media: Extending Marshall McLuhan (in preparation). 9. The Poetry of Physics and the Physics of Poetry (www.physics.utoronto.ca/~logan); 10 The Future of the Library (co-authored with Marshall McLuhan - in preparation under contract with Bowker Pub. and deposited in the Canadian Archives)
A selection of his writings can be accessed on:
http://www.physics.utoronto.ca/~logan

Business Experience

Co-founder and co-principal of Logan Design Systems Ltd operating as Desktop Training Centre (1982-2000), Gutenberg Internet Services Inc (1994-2000) and PM Rentals Ltd (1997-2000).

Knowledge management consultant to Siemens (World HQ in Munich), Intel Corp, SA Armstrong, Govt of Canada (Dept of Communications, Min of State for Science and Technology, Science Council), Ontario Min of Education, Life Insurance Marketing Research Association, numerous insurance companies and banks in the USA and Canada, Ontario Hospital Assoc. A full client list is available.

Robert K. Logan’s Specialties:

knowledge network construction, evaluation and consultation of collaboration, knowledge management and design ecology
