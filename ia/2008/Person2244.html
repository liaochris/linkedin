Adam Chavez’s Summary

I'm passionate about responsible entrepreneurship, especially in technology and life sciences. I think that if an entrepreneur creates real value for others, he will be much more successful than if he creates value solely for himself (Great products coupled with great sales and marketing vs. great sales and marketing alone).

I'm looking to land a job with a venture capital firm, as it would allow me to work in the area I'm the most passionate about --- new ventures.

My skills are quite diverse, with a solid understanding in both technology and business. I know Java, HTML, and a bit of CSS, PHP, and SQL; I also know VB, I'm an advanced Excel user, I know PPT, Word, Wordpress, Feedburner, RSS, I've toyed around a bit with CRM software.

Although I know these tools my real strengths are not in software development -- my real strengths are in the analysis of what the users/stakeholders need, business development, business planning, sales, marketing, my contacts in angel investing and venture capital, excellent listening skills, and a passion to create real value for others.
