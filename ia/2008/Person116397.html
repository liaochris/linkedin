Luc de Ruijter’s Summary

My experiences cover a broad range of IT-related activities, from webdesign to project management of corporate and internal websites, writing of manuals for information systems, to consulting on intranet and information management implementations. My goal is to innovate Communication by developing the communication managers capacity to deal with modern technologies.

Luc de Ruijter’s Specialties:

Intranet is a fastly developing environment in businesses and organisations. As a (non IT) independent project manager I establish and keep an optimal balance between IT and Communications without any strings with IT-suppliers.
