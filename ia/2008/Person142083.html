Nitan Pathak’s Summary

Mr. Pathak is a proven strategic thinker, hands-on leader and change agent, having created significant shareholder value as a Engineer, Senior Consultant, Program Manager, Business Development Manager and Corporate Finance Executive.

In addition to numerous leadership roles, he has advised the Boards/CEOs/CFOs of a number of public and private companies in a wide range of industries on strategic growth, risk management, strategic planning, financial restructuring, M&A due diligence and deal sourcing. His functional area and execution skills are broad: from product management, business development, risk management to corporate finance.

At Texas Instruments, Mr. Pathak was responsible for Business Development for the North America region based in Dallas Texas. His customers included Apple, RIM, Palm, HP, Garmin, Danger, Enfora... to whom he offers cellular and connectivity solutions. This business represents mulit million $ revenue with healthy gross profit margin.

Mr. Pathak was previously a Senior Consultant at KPMG Consulting in Munich, Germany. Mr. Pathak was part of the Strategy & Finance practice, specializing in the High-tech and Aerospace & Defense industry.

Mr. Pathak has over 4 years of experience in engineering with projects in Research and Development at Matra Marconi Space and Airbus Industries investigating mechanical systems subject to vibration induced by acoustic load. He was accountable from bid stage to post delivery support, for cost schedule and technical performance. Mr. Pathak is and Associate Member of the Institute of Mechanical Engineer (AIMechE)

Mr. Pathak holds a degree as MSc in Acoustic and Vibration and degree of BEng (Hons) in Mechanical engineering from the University of Southampton. Additionally, he holds an MBA from Oxford University and is fluent in French, English, German and Hindi.
