Homer Nievera hnievera@yahoo.com’s Summary

Homer is a go-getter who loves to make homeruns. A veteran salesman and advertising executive, he has transformed sales teams into powerhouse money-makers.

Homer loves to make and close a deal. After which, he develops a long-term relationship making sure a win-win situation is created all the time.

As advertising and publishing expert, Homer has re-positioned a number of magazines, newspapers, and web-based publications transforming them into profitable engines of media.

As a people-person, Homer has helped restructure organizations that have lost their zeal and direction towards sales and profit. He has recently managed Philippine sales for Manpower, the top global employment services organization.

Homer is Impaq Interactive's Sales Director handling Microsoft Digital Advertising Solutions.

Homer believes in giving back what he has learned and earned to the community. He is active in a global non-profit Catholic organizations such as Couples for Christ (since 1995) and its social arm Gawad Kalinga (caregiver) and once acted as editor of a Catholic foundation that publishes and distributes value-laden magazines to schools.
