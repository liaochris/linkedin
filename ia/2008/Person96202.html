Jonathan Fox’s Summary

I am a Senior Developer with a great deal of occupational experience in web application development, interactive television production and systems administration.

I have worked as a digital television consultant, implementing key projects on behalf of MMTV PLC for Sky, NTL, Telewest & various Government agencies including Job Centre plus, ODPM, UK on-line & NHS Direct.

In Q4 of 2005 I designed and developed the user interface of the very successful web based vehicle tracking system (www.crystalball.tv).
