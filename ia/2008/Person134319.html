Marci Nelson’s Summary

Through work experiences and soul searching, I realize that Human Resources is my intrinsic and natural profession; my "calling," however unromantic. Simply, I am most engaged when effectively serving the organization and people I work with and for. Therefore, I am actively seeking employment in this field and am ready to work hard for any good manager who appreciates the competence, loyalty and enthusiasm I have to offer.

After wrestling with the downside of the human condition for several years in social work/counseling, I’m determined to transition to a career that would allow me to unleash my idealism and ambition; specifically in the areas of employee relations, professional coaching, staffing and organizational change/development. I am especially intrigued with business strategy, legal/policy issues, systemic problem-solving, and facilitating worker potential.
