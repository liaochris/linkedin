Mike Havrilla’s Summary

I am a pharmacist, freelance writer, and index developer for ETF Innovators, LLP, which creates new global equity indexes for licensing and commercialization as exchange-traded funds. I have been investing since August 1997 and freelance writing for investors since April 2007 with a focus on the healthcare sector and small-caps. I am the Managing Partner and Founder of ETF Innovators who actively blogs at the company's website, which is then distributed to major finance portals such as Yahoo! Finance through Seeking Alpha and other syndicated channels.

I hold Doctor of Pharmacy (2003) and Bachelor of Science – Biology Major (1998) summa cum laude degrees from the University of Pittsburgh and am a registered pharmacist. Prior to attending pharmacy school in late 2000, I worked in the pharmaceutical industry for Wyeth Labs. I am also an avid runner since 1992 and have completed 18 marathons (26.2 miles) with a personal best time of 2 hours, 54 minutes.
