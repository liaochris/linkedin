Bob O' Neill’s Summary

An accomplished operations executive and visionary with an entrepreneurial spirit and 25 years of professional experience; background reflects achievements in Operations, Hospitality, Budgeting, and Capital Development. Proven ability to establish infrastructures, programs, and policies to maximize ROI. Adaptable, resourceful executive who achieves results through careful research/analysis, creative planning and timely implementation of cost-effective strategies that generate revenues and satisfy customers. Uses strong conceptual and organizational skills to produce and orchestrate major and minor special events involving state-of-the-art technical support. Adept at building highly motivated, customer-driven teams while consistently meeting tight deadlines and overall corporate objectives. Experienced in Multi-Union environments (trade councils) and Union grievance matters. Strong leadership qualities; able to recruit, develop, and manage highly motivated and productive teams. Computer literate in diverse software applications and technically skilled in Audio/Visual and Special effects design/installations.
