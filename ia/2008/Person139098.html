Nicole Byrkit’s Summary

I am located in the Buckhead/Atlanta area, and am currently an independent graphic designer, taking on a variety of projects, including logo design and branding, packaging, advertising, website design, editorial design, and anything else I can delve into!

Nicole Byrkit’s Specialties:

I am very skilled with the programs that are important for my industry, including the Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Bridge, and InDesign), as well as in QuarkXpress, all font libraries, Microsoft Office, and Aperture.
