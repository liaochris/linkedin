Steve King’s Summary

Steve King is a Senior Strategist at frog design inc. and a Co-Founder of Caboodle! Toys LLC.

At frog design, Steve is responsible for leading strategy engagements spanning both physical products and digital services. Most projects focus on identifying new opportunities for growth by analyzing competitive landscapes and defining unmet customer needs.

Steve brings an entrepreneurial view to his projects from his continued work at Caboodle! Toys LLC, a company he co-founded in 2005. Caboodle! Toys was born from a simple idea -- when you “collect them all” you deserve a reward for your efforts, namely with a Caboodle!™ (a box of special, exclusive prizes). Caboodle! rewards are not offered for sale and can only be earned by collecting a complete series.

Noah's Pals™ is the premier collection from Caboodle! Toys LLC and debuted at the American International Toy Fair in 2007. The award-winning line features lovingly sculpted and beautifully hand-painted animal figures. The forty pairs of meticulously detailed and realistic pals are built to 1:24 scale. In its first year on the market, Noah’s Pals has garnered eight toy awards and has been recognized as a "New & Notable" product by I.D. Magazine. During the Holiday 2007 season, Steve appeared as a guest on QVC’s “Santa’s Toy Shop” and introduced the collectibles to a nationwide audience.

Prior to co-founding Caboodle! Toys, Steve spent seven years as a business strategy consultant. He served as the Strategy & Change Consulting Leader in the IBM Institute for Business Value and developed the group's intellectual property. Steve co-authored two papers – "The Specialized Enterprise" and "Component Business Models" – that have driven the direction of IBM’s services businesses.
