John Westra’s Summary

Over twenty years of Information Technology (IT) and Business Services sales/ marketing experience. Hands-on experience designing, sourcing, implementing, integrating, delivering training and managing LAN, WAN, Client Server, Web-enabled and Web 2.0 systems.

Sales & Marketing experience includes millions of dollars worth of IT and business technology/services contracts with SMB and government clients. Management experience includes coordinating diverse teams of people in a variety of roles, including infrastructure implementation and integration, application development, web site development and support.

My goal is to leverage my business and IT background, combined with my 6+ years of community management experience, to help create real and virtual communities that are known for being great places to live work, play and be "connected."
