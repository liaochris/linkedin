paul golding’s Summary

Mobile technology/strategy expert in mobile industry since 1990 and holder of many patents. I have implemented numerous wireless projects and continue to invent new techniques for solving various mobilisation problems. I have trained, coached and mentored many wireless practitioners in major operators and leading companies. My book "Next Generation Wireless Applications" is widely use in the field with the 2nd edition now finalised for publishing with major new content around the Web 2.0 and Mobile 2.0 theme. See www.paulgolding.com for more details.
