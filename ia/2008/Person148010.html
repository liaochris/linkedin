Paul Walton’s Summary

An experienced Project Manager with over 15 year’s experience in management of all aspects of engineering design and construction projects for construction, electrical and electronic systems.
Experience with the end-to-end management of complex projects and solutions involving complex stakeholder groups and parties not directly contracted with. Delivering projects that bring together diverse internal departments, designers, consultants, sub-contractors and vendors, managing the relationships to maintain program and cost in matrix and hierarchical structures.
Technical Project Management and Bid Management experience including pricing, profit and loss accounts, risk analysis and management, proposal preparation, director level signoff and client negotiation. Also over twelve years experience of variation (change control) assessment, negotiation and approval as both client and contractor
Delivered process change and re-engineering internally and for clients, providing process analysis from strategic KPIs, to milestones and activities, ensuring the delivery of prescriptive not descriptive frameworks for processes and projects.
Experience with Health and Safety management and the culture that supports compliance, including management of specific Health and Safety and access regimes including those requirements specific to the transportation arena.
Experienced people manager with extensive experience of managing large project teams, with both direct and matrix reports
Good IT skills over ten years experience with Microsoft office suite, good PowerPoint and Word plus intermediate Excel, Project and Visio. Extensive use of bespoke databases for project reporting including Remedy for operational faults and quality reporting plus Siebel CRM, Viztopia (MYOB) and Oracle plus experience of data mining tools such as Business Objects.
