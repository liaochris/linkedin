Broderick Perkins’s Summary

DeadlineNews Group is seeking a partner, affiliate or news outlet to fill the growing residential real estate news void. You provide the outlet, we provide the news.

Given the volatile nature of the real estate market, the most up-to-date realty information is at the top of consumers' need-to-read heap.

Even when consumers aren't buying or selling homes, studies have shown, consumers don't lose their nose for housing news.

That's because buying a home is often the most expensive and the most trying transaction most people will ever endure. Information to help make the best decisions -- and hold onto their dreams -- is paramount.

But with newspapers shutting down real estate sections or turning them into advertorial cash cows, where are consumers going to get balanced news and analysis that really hits home?

From Broderick Perkins, a journalist for 30 years, who 10 years ago parlayed his career in old-school journalism into a contemporary digital news service that really hits home.

The award-winning consumer journalist, is founder, publisher and executive editor of the bootstrap DeadlineNews Group, which offers original editorial content and archived articles in a variety of formats to fit any budget.

In the past decade, Perkins has produced real estate, consumer and small business editorial content for more than three dozen real estate news and trade publications on and off the Net.

In addition to managing the DeadlineNews Group, Perkins currently provides content for Dallas, TX-based RealtyTimes.com (where he recently broke into television script writing), San Jose, CA-based Mercury News; Berkeley, CA-based Nolo.com; and Alameda County-based East Bay Publishing. Perkins also recently served as chief editorial consultant for Nolo's "Essential Guide To Buying Your First Home."

Broderick Perkins’s Specialties:

The DeadlineNews Group is a Silicon Valley-based editorial content and consulting service specializing in residential real estate, consumer news and related editorial consulting services.

The group includes the website, DeadlineNews.Com, offering real estate editorial content and consulting services, and its back shop, the Deadline Newsroom, an open house on news that really hits home.

Both are a living portfolios of Perkins' work.
