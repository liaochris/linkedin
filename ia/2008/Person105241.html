Ken Wood’s Summary

I am into my third career: originally trained as an engineer, turned salesperson, turned entrepreneur. Like many engineers, I enjoy taking things apart to see how they work - but the "things" in my case are markets and companies. Just as many an engineer has built a better "mousetrap", I am always keen to build a better business model.

So far I've started the 4 companies shown on this profile, and several others that I prefer to describe as "relatively inexpensive learning experiences". I'm not planning any more businesses at the moment, but when I think I've had or found the next good idea then you never know...

I particularly enjoy growing businesses by applying a well-planned strategy while building structure, systems and well-defined processes into the company as it expands. My next goal is to take a business past US$100m in revenue.
