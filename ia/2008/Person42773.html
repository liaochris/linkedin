Dana Gardner’s Summary

I am an analyst and forecaster of the business productivity effects of enterprise applications and software infrastructure. As a blogger (http://blogs.zdnet.com/Gardner & http://www.findtechblogs.com/soa) and a podcaster (http://www.briefingsdirect.com), my analysis and interviews become conversational, and powerfully distributed via social networking.

I provide highly effective Web 2.0/Enterprise 2.0 consulting services to user organizations and vendors. As president and founder of Interarbor Solutions, I have introduced and produce a new kind of podcast product, called BriefingsDirect. These sponsored podcasts combine the value of leading industry analysts' perspective and objective credibility with viral distribution, and a natural language ease of understanding complex subjects.

This Web 2.0 content equivalent of a deep-dive analyst briefing -- made available as a podcast/transcript/blog to any interested viewer/search engine user -- breaks the mold on exclusive knowledge and takes the best of what IT experts know and delivers it with balance and objectivity within a 35-minute discussion.

B2B informational podcasts jump-start viral conversational marketing and evangelism campaigns and escalate community-building strategies. Interarbor Solutions provides fresh and creative thinking on IT marketing, SOA, IT partnerships, and SEO strategies based on the power of thoughtful content, made freely and easily available to seekers of insights and information.

As a result, marketers and branding professionals can communicate inexpensively with self-qualifiying readers/listeners in discreet market segments. BriefingsDirect podcasts hosted by Dana Gardner: Full turnkey planning, moderatiing, producing, hosting, and distribution via blogs and IT media partners of essential IT knowledge and understanding.

Dana Gardner’s Specialties:

SOA trends analysis/forecasting, conversational evangelism advice, turnkey B2B and B2C podcast creation and distribution, IT competitive analysis, SOA consulting services, entrepreneurial business development, marketing strategies, sales strategies, evangelizing and educating on emerging IT trends, strong public speaking and presentations delivery, extensive written skills, and innovative Web 2.0 rich media publishing experience.
