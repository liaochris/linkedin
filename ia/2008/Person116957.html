Luke Carolan’s Summary

Luke initially started his career within the ITC industry working in Customer Service, Training and Retail Management positions for Optus, Telstra and Vodafone. Wanting to progress Luke obtained an Employment Advisory style of position with the renowned Australian Business Limited (NSW Chamber of Commerce). At ABL Luke gained an in-depth understanding of the constraints and difficulties faced by employers and job seekers along with Industrial Relations legislations, arrangements etc. After a brief inception Luke was quickly promoted to Account Executive. It was Luke’s role to provide employment council, mediation of disputes, business development and marketing for training in the workplace. This position also required management of government grants and the facilitation of workplace modifications for people with disabilities.

Whilst at ABL Luke was approached by BWAC New Apprenticeships Centre to undertake a position of Marketing Consultant performing similar tasks to his role at ABL.

Following on from the Education and Employment Industry Luke made the natural progression to recruitment with his first position at Pegasus Global. In this position Luke recruited in the market of SAP for six months and then was promoted to assist in the setup and development of another branch in Melbourne. The Melbourne office grew from 2 to 12 staff in the 12 months of his tenure. Following this Luke accepted an offer with RSP as a specialised Microsoft Development Recruitment consultant. Luke enjoyed working with the team at RSP and looked to increase public recognition of RSP expertise within the IT market.

Luke is now working with CXC Consultants exchange as Business Development Manager NSW and works closely with agencies and business direct to consult on payrolling, taxation, and legislative changes surrounding employment and immigration. luke@cxc.com.au

Luke Carolan’s Specialties:

Tax minimisation services for businesses ranging from SME through to Blue Chip corporate.
