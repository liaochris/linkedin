Mitchell Ashley’s Summary

My passions are creating businesses and innovative products, for customers and markets needing a unique approach to solving their business problems, all with highly talented people who are passionate about what they do. I've brought to market multiple new security, networking, multimedia and Internet products throughout my career for Fortune 500, SME, B2B and consumer markets.

My business, market and technology focus includes networking, security, convergence, virtualization, open source, mobility, VoIP, SaaS and user-centered product design.

I love to evangelize this work with analysts, media and customers, and I am fortunate to have appeared in industry publications including USAToday, NetworkWorld, ComputerWorld, InfoWorld, InformationWeek, CRN and others. I also authored a chapter of the book "Growth Strategies for Software Companies", and authored many byline articles.

Most recently I was CTO and General Manager of StillSecure where I led the successful creation and market launch of multiple industry award winning security products. I also co-founded and led BoldTech Systems, and designed and implemented a national data broadband network at Jato Comm.

Currently, I am CTO of Absolute Performance, a leading On Demand application performance management software and SaaS services company in Boulder, CO. I am also very active in my media company Converging Network, LLC, where I develop successful product, business, media and conversational marketing strategies. I also actively mentor and advise a number of entrepreneurs and startups.

I regularly blog about my activities and topics of interest at The Converging Network, http://theconvergingnetwork.com, the Network World Microsoft Subnet blog, http://www.networkworld.com/community/ashley, and I co-host the highly subscribed to podcast "StillSecure After All These Years", http://www.ssaatypodcast.com, and the Network World Converging On Microsoft Podcast, http://www.networkworld.com/podcasts/com.
