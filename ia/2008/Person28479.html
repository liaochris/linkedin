Bruce Snyder’s Summary

In April 2007, I helped take LogicBlaze through an acquisition by IONA Technologies. I am currently with IONA as a Principal Engineer and carrying on with my LogicBlaze colleagues to continue to deliver open source SOA. For information about the acquisition, see the press release: http://www.iona.com/pressroom/2007/20070410.htm?WT.mc_id=125789.

I'm formerly an architect for LogicBlaze where I spent my time handling client relations, designing and developing software and making critical business decisions with clients, partners and inside the company. LogicBlaze was a startup based in Los Angeles, CA with people distributed thoughtout the world and we're continuing to work this way at IONA.

I also spend a fair amount of time working on open source in one way or another. I am an Apache Software Foundation Member (http://www.apache.org/foundation/members.html) and I'm involved with Apache ActiveMQ, Apache Camel, Apache ServiceMix, Apache Geronimo, Apache XBean among others. I've been involved in open source for over 10 years and do everything from designing and developing software to building communities. I also write articles and books, I speak at conferences around the world and I reside near beautiful Boulder, Colorado with my family.
