Jason Crawford’s Summary

As an entrepreneur for over 12 years, Jason has earned the reputation as an individual with exceptional creativity and solid business acumen. Possessing the skills to innovate but also organize and plan has allowed Jason to conceive and successfully execute multiple business ventures that span a wide range of industries.

Jason Crawford’s Experience

  • Owner

    Brooklyn Games, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Computer Games industry)

    May 2003 — Present (4 years 11 months)

    Since starting Brooklyn Games in 2003, the company has provided development and consulting services to many clients including Atari, Hasbro, Sony, MGM Interactive, Sesame Workshop, Mad Catz, and JWT. Although mainly focused on creative direction and design, Jason has gained solid experience in various areas of the game industry including business development, licensing, publishing, and production. He has worked with many well known licensed properties such as Batman®, Q-bert®, Transformers®, Stargate©, Sesame Street®, Godzilla®, Jenja®, Bob The Builder®, Littlest Pet Shop®, and The Wiggles®. Brooklyn Games was instrumental in helping launch video game developer Sensory Sweep, currently one of the largest game developers in the US.

  • Owner

    Fuzzcode, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry)

    February 1997 — Present (11 years 2 months)

    Mainly a vehicle for Jason's own business endeavors, Fuzzcode has been developing software and internet companies since 1997. In 1999, Fuzzcode launched a proprietary turnkey e-commerce solution and business management system called BackFlip. In exchange for an equity stake in hip-hop entrepreneur Russell Simmons' PhatFarm.com, BackFlip became the backbone of the online venture with Fuzzcode also developing all aspects of the online venture including innovative warehouse management and CRM solutions. In 2003, Fuzzcode sold its interest in PhatFarm.com to fashion holdings company Kellwood (NYSE: KWD). Currently, Fuzzcode's main focus is a new venture called SwitchGames.com. SwitchGames is an innovative and rapidly growing online community destination where gamers can buy, sell, and trade used video games with each other.

  • Composer

    SoundPsychic

    (Music industry)

    1996 — Present (12 years)

    In addition to leading his band Grey Does Matter, Jason is a professional freelance music composer. Sound Psychic has provided lots of music for advertising and interactive. Jason's music has been utilized by many companies including: KPMG, Cadbury, Unilever, W Hotels, Fox TV, MTV, Sirius Satellite Radio, Casio, and 2K Games.
