Pat Cray’s Summary

Greetings! My name is PAT CRAY and I am the President of Gold Coast Lending. Our firm treats clients like family and we always make sure that we're doing what is in our client's best interest!

We specialize in FHA loans. These loans are perfect for First Time Homebuyers and for cash-out refinances up to 95% of your home's value. Some of our best markets are in Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, Florida, and many parts of Alabama including Jefferson and Montgomery counties.

We are also experts in Reverse Mortgages. Contact us today if you have any questions about this interesting product!

In this challenging real estate marketplace, we also help people that are in need of a mortgage modification - people that want to keep their homes, but can't afford the current payments.

We also specialize in commercial financing for apartment buildings, office / warehouse buildings, gas stations, and franchises. We have some very special financing for professionals (Doctors, Attorneys, CPA's) that can't be beat! Our commercial financing is available for anywhere in the USA.

.... We Don't Just Close Loans...... We Open Doors!
