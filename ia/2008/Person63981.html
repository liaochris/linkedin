Martin Finch’s Summary

So, a brief history of me. I've lived and worked in China for the past 2 years and set up a happy life here working within the games industry. Initially China was a big culture shock for me but getting used to the life style and way of thinking has finally started to grow on me, I've become localized :) Learning Chinese helps a lot in the studio as well, though many of the developers here speak good English so I spend my time learning with taxi drivers, who can't speak a word of English. It's a great feeling to be out here meeting people from all over the world, learning from each other and doing business together. My plan is to be out here in the East for a long time.


My experience in general:
Game industry and studio development, game production ensuring deliveries and quality are met, art work outsourcing, intermediate level programming, basic 3D modeling, game design within teams and business development in relation to generating strong relationships with clients and clear lines of communication.

My goals:
To become a key player within the games industry and a trusted partner for both my studio and its clients, to always learn new things, to continue meeting and connecting with people in the games industry globally, to always find new challenges, to become fluent in Chinese, to climb mount everest and to always enjoy life to fullest.
