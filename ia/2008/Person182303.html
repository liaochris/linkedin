Stewart Hirsch’s Summary

Business development/relationship coach and trainer for professional services providers; executive coach for leaders.

Former practicing attorney - in-house and in firms. Began coaching in 1994 and workshops and training shortly after, to help lawyers and others in professional services get out of their own way - past their own barriers and market themselves with integrity and naturally. Clients include lawyers, accountants, consultants and other professionals in firms - ranging from 800 professionals to 4 professionals - who want to increase their business development skills and their business.

I have helped practice groups in large firms create strategic plans, and provided leadership and other custom designed workshops.

I have also coached senior leaders and executives on strategic and tactical challenges, and worked with leaders on team building and change management.

I am a member of Duke Corporate Education's Global Resource Learning Network, and a certified Trusted Advisor Coach.

I have spoken at retreats, and at a number of national and international conferences including the AIPLA, IWIRK, LMA, ABA and others.

Stewart Hirsch’s Specialties:

Coaching and training includes networking, creating an action plan, leveraging relationships, increasing business from speaking, selling, overcoming personal obstacles and related skill sets. Coaching includes all of these plus, setting goals, implementing the action plan and scripting calls and e-mails for specific opportunities and strategic and tactical business development planning for individuals and small groups. Coaching also creates the accountability needed to implement plans.
