Michal Galdzicki’s Summary

My main interest is in knowledge representation of biological simulations of molecular pathways and synthetic biology. I am pursuing this interest through work in Dr. John Gennari's SemSim Group with Dr. Daniel Cook, Maxwell Neal and collaboration with Dr. Herbert Sauro. This effort leverages lightweight semantic representation to describe mathematical models of biological systems. Our project allows for computer assisted interpretation of the physical and computational components of models. The relationships which are described by our SemSim models can be used to help merge, translate,and analyze the described systems. The broad goal is to help develop infrastructure for the reuse of mathematical representations of biological systems.
