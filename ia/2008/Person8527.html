Alyson McNutt English’s Summary

I am an award-winning, internationally published freelance writer and photographer based in the southeast U.S.

Some of my main areas of interest include "green" topics of all kinds, home decorating and home improvement, parenting and family topics, women's health and fitness, safety and health, child development and psychology, and first-person essays. I also specialize in web writing. I am excellent with both the web-writing format and HTML used in coding the stories.

I've recently written pieces for Self, Kiwi, Organize, Conceive, Pregnancy, Wondertime, and Relish, as well as such high-profile online destinations like HGTV.com, BobVila.com, MSNBC.com, and BabyCenter.com. I am currently working on assignments for Real Simple, AARP The Magazine, REALTOR, Kiwi, ESPN The Magazine, Wondertime, Meredith Integrated Marketing's Solutions at Hand, DElight!, FrontDoor.com, BabyCenter.com and BobVila.com. I am also a blogger at BabyCenter.com's "Momformation" blog.
