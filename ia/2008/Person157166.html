Brian Rauschenbach’s Summary

I have been working in the industry since 1996 in online marketing & advertising, media planning and buying. I have also worked many business development partnership deals with online portals and ISPs. In 1999, I started an affiliate marketing business, Don't Blink Media, with my two partners Jason Llorin and Norman Clarke. We transformed this business into a full service ad agency in 2006 and recently branched off to start a new agency, Add Three Inc. that focuses on search engine marketing and optimization services for local and national brands.

We also develop have developed a few of our own properties which include a free social dating site Meeta.com, a hip-hop portal Hip-Hop.net, a Seattle real estate portfolio under Seattle-Condos.com and our online dating directory at Lovesites.com. We work closely in the dating vertical with select partners helping them grow their subscriber user base through performance based relationships.
