Joel Newman’s Summary

I am a young, energetic, and highly-professional actor with an extensive background in musical theater. This background includes training at the prestigious Collaborative ArtsProject 21 (CAP21), professional work with the Papermill Playhouse (Millburn, NJ) and ArtsPower National Touring Theater, and numerous community theater productions. As a community theater actor, I was twice recognized for outstanding performances by the New Jersey Association of Community Theaters, once as "Best Actor in Musical" (Nominee - Crazy for You) and once as "Best Supporting Actor in a Musical" (Nominee - Honk!).

Although I have a strong background in musical theater, I would be happy to make a career in any of the three major media - stage, television and film. In order to achieve this goal, I am constantly working to refine my craft, through dance lessons, voice lessons, and acting classes.
