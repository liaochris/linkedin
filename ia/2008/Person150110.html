Peter A. Mello’s Summary

Joined Northeast Maritime Institute to lead a new youth education and employment initiative and to develop and implement organizational Internet strategy.

Sea-Fever Consulting LLC was founded by Peter A. Mello following a 25 year career in the social and business sectors including experience as executive director and ceo of an international nonprofit and president and coo of a national insurance management company. Peter is committed to helping organizations that want to change the world.
