Carsten Cumbrowski’s Summary

I have over 5 years professional experience in the internet marketing business, including search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM) / PPC advertisement and affiliate marketing (from both sides, affiliate & merchant).

I have over 10 years of professional experience in programming and software development for the web and for the desktop.I learned my first programming language with the age of 12.

I am blogging about internet marketing, in particular affiliate and search marketing at ReveNews.com since July 2006 and SearchEngineJournal.com since October 2006. I also provide resources and tips via my website Cumbrowski.com. I am willing to take on internet marketing strategy consulting projects, if they interest me. I am only available part time and work from my office in Fresno/CA, with the option to travel to the client for meetings at the clients expense.
