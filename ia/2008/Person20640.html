Bryan Dahlfors’s Summary

I am a hard working, well established individual with twenty six plus year’s experience in Supply Chain. I have a proven track record of reducing inventories, increasing customer service levels, reducing work in process inventories, and implementing processes to achieve these results at a high ROI to the company. I have good team involvement skills having been a part of implementation teams of as many as twelve persons, and have lead teams with as many as seven persons.

I have an Associates degree in Business Management and a Bachelors’ degree in Information Technology. I am a member of The Association for Operations Management (APICS) and have achieved my certification in production and inventory management (CPIM). I have served on the St. Louis chapter board of directors for four yeas as Director of marketing and helped to increase membership by seven percent during a time when other chapters were seeing a reduction in membership.

I have a good working knowledge of computer systems and security and can write code in SQL, JAVA, JAVA Applets, HTML and UNIX shell scripting. I am above average in my knowledge of Widows Excel, Access, Power Point and Word. I also am proficient in Microsoft Visio and Project. I also have an expert level understanding of the Demand Solutions Supply Chain software package.

With my years of experience and education I believe I can enhance any company’s bottom line.
