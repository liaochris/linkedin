Samy Thuillier’s Summary

After a French bachelor in political sciences (Sciences Po Degree), I recently completed a Masters in International Risk Management in 2008 (HEC European Business School, Paris).

I worked for different companies and political organizations, to discover new disciplines and to complete my education. This gave me the opportunity to study financial disciplines, in which I became very interested. Financial services institutions operate in a world of constant change — challenged by new regulations, consolidation, market evolution, geopolitical uncertainty, and financial volatility. Working with professionals who understand these issues and respond quickly with solutions can give an institution a distinct competitive advantage. These are a few reasons why I became a consultant in financial services for Deloitte in september 2008.

I'm a sportsman too by the way and I'm looking forward to joining a sports association soon, after the great experiences I had during my student years in different teams.
