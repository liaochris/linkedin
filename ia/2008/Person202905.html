Zach Everson’s Summary

Fresh, compelling written material
My writing and editing expertise will ensure that your written material—be it a media publication, book, proposal, marketing piece, website, blog, or contract deliverable—will impress your audience and distinguish you from your competitors. However you choose to have me help, the end result will be the same: your readers will be impressed.

Helping clients reach their target audience
I also provide strategic communications consulting services to clients ranging from individuals and small businesses to U.S. and foreign government agencies and multinational corporations. I have helped my clients use wikis, new media, social networks, and microblogging (including blogs, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, Wikipedia, and Flickr).

And I have optimized websites for improved search engine ranking.

Excellent service
Quick deadline? All assignments are returned on time in a format of your choice. And I’m always available to answer questions.

Affordable rates
I offer reasonable rates that work with your budget.

To learn more about how my writing, editing, and communications experience can help you, e-mail zach@zacheverson.com or visit www.zacheverson.com.
