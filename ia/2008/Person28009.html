Kimberly Silk’s Summary

Kimberly Silk has over fifteen years of strategic online marketing and product management experience in the Interactive Media industry. Her company, BrightSail, has served a variety of clients including corporate, government, institutional and non-profit organizations.

Kim has a particular passion for digital collections and online products and services. As a librarian, she prides herself on understanding how to provide the right information to the right audience at the right time, with a creative flair that makes a lasting impact.

Prior to launching BrightSail in 2001, Kim worked at Rogers Media, most recently as Director, Strategic Internet Development. There, she collaborated with the New Media and Publishing divisions to develop Internet ventures from concept to market.

Prior to joining Rogers, she was Webmaster for Discovery Channel Canada, the first Canadian specialty channel to launch an interactive online component.

Kim earned her M.L.S. (Library Science) from the University of Toronto, and a B.A. in English Literature from the University of Waterloo.
