Edward Williams (ewilliams@coldclosers.com)’s Summary

Before going further... please read this with utmost caution as it may cause pain, apprehension, worry, disgust and/ or a host of other ill feelings. Additionally I will preface by stating that although I enjoy "cold calling" I may not "cold call" you. So If you hate "cold calling" (and the overuse of " ") then perhaps we will never speak.

That being written... I love cold calling! This is a statement you will not read/ hear from many sales professionals... you will not hear from many SANE people. Perhaps I would not classify myself as SANE but certainly not INSANE by definition; because I operate by a couple philosophies, "do what average people won't... and, observe the masses- do the opposite." So once again, "I love cold calling" because the average sales professional hates it.
