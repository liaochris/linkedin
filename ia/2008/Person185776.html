Pierluigi Tassi’s Summary

My main business company goal is to spread open source concept in each way, in the customer's office workstations, in the customer's websites, and much more in minds at all.

I'm an Internet writer since 1996 and still writing. I'm a web designer interested mostly to accessibility and semantic of informations.

I'm a PHP developer since 2002 and Community manager of Grusp, the italian association for PHPday in italy.
