Andre Galhardo’s Summary

I've been working for 15 years as an Art Director in leading brazilian agencies and recently, also as a Creative Planner and Interaction Designer.

My objective is create not only advertising campaigns, but new interaction platforms for brands. Simply put, Innovation though ideas.

In this Web 2.0 world, when yesterday's solutions aren't fit for today's problems, we must help companies innovate "design thinking" their businesses - and embedding marketing into products.

Right- and left-brainers, let´s unite to make things work better.
