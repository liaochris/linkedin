Chris Melville’s Summary

After 5 years as InterSAN co-founder and CEO, Chris sold the company to Finisar Corporation. Chris spent 2 years with Finisar's Network Tools division working on Enterprise SAN and LAN products; he has most recently taken on the role of managing a large and exciting set of projects for one of the worlds greatest software companies: Intuit!

At InterSAN, Chris was responsible for strategy, finance, marketing and business development. Over the life of the company, Chris raised $31M in venture capital, developed a world class storage management product, built a strong portfolio of customers, and expanded InterSAN's market presence through key strategic relationships. Chris co-authored a patent in 2001 covering Automated Provisioning in Storage Area Networks.

Prior to founding InterSAN, Chris held leadership roles at Auspex Systems and SCO, developing strategic plans and managing the development of technology, products, organizations, and processes. At Auspex, he was responsible for the planning, evolution, and release of software products for the company's family of high-performance file servers, generating $35 million in revenue. Chris was responsible for Auspex' high availability software and NAS/SAN gateway technology, and his SAN-based, coherent, data replication invention was submitted for a patent in 1999. At SCO, Chris managed worldwide development projects.
