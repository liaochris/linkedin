BIO:

Rolf has been active in the internet arena for over a decade while living and working in the US (SF Bay Area), Europe, Brunei and Australia. Rolf speaks, consults and innovates in the areas of emerging technology, social software, online communities, collaborative and open-source software and where possible, how these technologies can help lead us to a more sustainable planet.

Rolf holds a Bachelor of Business in Information Systems. He recently completed a one-year assignment as Director of Product Management at openBC/Xing in Hamburg and is currently working at Swisscom Innovations in Bern, Switzerland.
