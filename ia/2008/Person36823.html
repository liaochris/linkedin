Christopher Flynn’s Summary

An internationally experienced Enterprise Risk and Security Management consultant, with 25+ years engaged in a variety of roles and projects in Banking, Insurance, Air and Road Transport, Telecommunications, New Media, Online Marketing, Fashion, Food and Industrial Manufacturing, Online Gaming, Professional Services, Defence, Government and Out Sourcing Services sectors.

Being highly focussed on achieving business objectives, my core competencies are in Information and Asset Protection through integrated Governance, reusable Architecture, measurable Compliance, comprehensive Risk Assessment and effective Threat Management across diverse business and technology settings.

Possessing broad social, business and technical knowledge combined with outstanding leadership abilities and creative problem solving skills for both strategic and tactical outcomes.

Effective communicator able to explain complex concepts and ideas to non-technical people.

Able to translate legal, regulatory and government requirements into meaningful business compliance objectives as the primary focus of enterprise security, leading to greater brand protection and competitive leverage.

My goal is to save enterprises and organisations from misguiding themselves.
