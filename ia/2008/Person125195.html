Matthew Lloyd’s Summary

Matthew M. Lloyd
Proven public relations and marketing communications leader with over sixteen years of experience delivering results driven strategic campaigns for Fortune 500 and emerging companies in the US, UK and Europe. Multi-faceted PR practitioner with extensive experience in the areas of executive communications, thought-leadership marketing, product marketing and reputation management having counseled the leadership teams of some of the worlds most prestigious technology, wireless and digital lifestyle brands including: IBM, Fujifilm, Motorola, Microsoft, The Mathworks, Fujitsu Ltd, Compaq and Sprint/Nextel. Dynamic ability to grow teams, manage, mentor and motivate staff. Extensive experience in managing agency operations, budgets and driving business development.
