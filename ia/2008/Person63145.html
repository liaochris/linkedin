Bo Buckley’s Summary

I founded Fearmakers Studios in 1999 to fabricate props and effects for film, television, attractions and exhibits. In 2002, the company reshaped its objectives when we began the development of our first feature film, DEATH4TOLD. The features is a 4 part anthology in the vein of TWILIGHT ZONE: THE MOVIE or CREEPSHOW and stars Margot (SUPERMAN) Kidder, Tom (FROM DUSK TILL DAWN) Savini, Alicia (BOYS DON'T CRY) Goranson, Harley (SIMPLY IRRESISTIBLE) Kaplan and KC (THE HOWARD STERN SHOW) Armstrong and an amazing group of rising talent including Rich Sommer who has gone on to appear in THE DEVIL WEARS PRADA and the Golden Globe winning Mad Men on AMC.

Our goals are to produce quality horror, sci-fi and suspense thriller entertainment with mass market appeal. By using the collective efforts of highly creative and resourceful artists and filmmakers, Fearmakers is able to produce quality entertainment on a low budget.

Fearmakers is currently developing several projects including the thriller DISMAL we hope to produce later this year.

Outside of Fearmakers, I have worked on many independent features, commercials and TV shows as AD or UPM. This past year alone I worked on 3 feature and 2 TV shows as AD or UPM including GHOST TOWN: THE MOVIE which should be released later this year and THE LENA BAKER STORY shot in Georgia. You can also catch Whittaker Bay on WGN Saturdays at 11:30AM.
