Geoff Wainwright’s Summary

Business insurance is one of the places I can find extra profit. I guarantee to get better insurance covers and a reduced premium for my clients. If I don't achieve this, my clients owe me nothing and my service is FREE. I average a 22% saving on insurance premiums and I always get better covers for my client, without the need to change the insurance broker or agent.

How can I do this? I focus on providing independent, impartial and unbiased insurance reviews. I specialize in preparing and conducting tenders and conceptual tenders for my clients. I establish group insurance schemes for associations and provide benchmarking services for companies of any size in any industry.

I love, absolutely adore helping corporations of all sizes, including not-for-profits and charities. I partner with my clients and show them how to manage the relationship with their insurance broker and other suppliers. We work together to change the service agreement with the broker so it becomes a win-win for both my client and the insurance broker.

I have substantial experience working with a variety of businesses of all sizes in various industries over the last 6 years. I call on other cost area experts to find extra PROFIT, in other areas of the business.
