Vinay Gupta’s Summary

Vinay has worked across diverse functions with large corporations and start-ups, has lead teams, worked closely with the top management and worked across continents.

Some of his work areas: business development, hire people, run operations, profit center management, chase targets, network, work with customers across culturally diverse countries, live in US/ UK/ India, prepare collaterals and presentations and documents... pretty much a hands-on and supervisory experience with critical management functions in a typical web development/ IT outsourcing/ new media development/ e-learning/ BPO business.

As part of his current venture he has had a good experience of juggling between scarce resources, working capital management, financing, Indian and US accounting laws, hiring and supervising diverse individuals, setting incremental challenges for team members and the organization as a whole.

Vinay Gupta’s Experience

  • CEO

    Dimensions E Services

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; E-Learning industry)

    September 2004 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    Vinay started Dimensions in 2004 with a focus to target e-Learning outsourced development. He has evolved Dimensions with changing business needs and market dynamics. Dimensions is now a leading product company blended across technology and class room coaching. Vinay's job role stretches across revenue generation, managing operations, human capital management, business financing, networking, business travel within US and India. Vinay has been successful in raising outside capital for the company and is well networked with big corporations, software, content and new media fraternity in India.

  • Director Sales

    Mentorix/ Lionbridge

    (Computer Software industry)

    December 2000 — December 2003 (3 years 1 month)

    Vinay joined Turbograd when it was a 6 people start-up, Turbograd was re-named to Mentorix. Vinay worked out of Atlanta and was key in upscaling the US sales for outsourced custom e-learning development. Then in 2002 Mentorix decided to aggressively target Europe with Vinay in the lead. Vinay worked out of London for almost 2 years. Was focused on generating new business, managing key relationships, human capital management and interfacing with Operations teams in India. Lionbridge bought out Mentorix in Nov 2003 and Vinay cashed out his stock-ops to start his own venture.
    Some of the key job definitions during Vinay's 3 years with Mentorix/ Lionbridge were: Profit Center Management, Business Development, Key Relationship Management, Program Management.
