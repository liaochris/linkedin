Jordan Finger - jordan.finger@ gmail com’s Summary

Jordan Finger, founder & CEO of Ardis Health, has a proven track record of success as a savvy marketer.

Mr. Finger began his career in advertising sales at Turner Broadcasting Systems. There he developed strategic marketing programs that were sold as multimillion dollar promotional campaigns to Turner's Fortune 500 clients. Leveraging his experience in television marketing Mr. Finger turned his attention to the emerging internet marketing industry. Mr. Finger was an original member of the media and promotions team at the New York City based interactive advertising agency, K2 Digital. There he created and implemented multimillion dollar internet media and advertising campaigns for blue chip clients such as: Verizon, SmithKline Beecham, Cox Interactive Media, Standard & Poors and Comcast. Fresh off his success at K2, Mr. Finger was appointed the Vice President of Internet Marketing and Business Development for MediaBay, Inc (NASDAQ: MBAY). Mr. Finger was instrumental in building and launching the online business for MediaBay's Audio Book Club division (www.audiobookclub.com). During his tenure at the company, audiobookclub.com became the largest worldwide club marketer of audio books; was responsible for acquiring over 500,000 new members through the internet; was the third most visited "book" destination on the internet; and became a top 50 e-commerce destination with revenues exceeding $30 million.

Mr. Finger is also the founder of Curb Your Cravings, LLC which he has made a dominant player in the internet marketing business of nutraceuticals.

Mr. Finger is a member of the Young Entrepreneurs' Organization New York Chapter.

Mr. Finger holds a Business in Science and Management Degree in Marketing from the A.B. Freeman School of Business at Tulane University in New Orleans.
