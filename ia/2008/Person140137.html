Noemi Greyzdorf’s Summary

My professional experience ranges from Product Management to Sales to Business Development. As a Product Manager I worked closely with marketing to identify trends and needs in the marketplace and then communicate them as requirements to the development engineers. I was also responsible for defining positioning of the product/service, pricing, and assist sales to communicate the product/service value we were offering.

As a sales account executive, I worked with customers to identify issues and challenges they were experiencing with their Storage systems and then match them with the appropriate technology. I was responsible for outlining consulting services for evaluating backup system performance and storage utilization. My responsiblities extended to actually performing the analysis and delivering a report/presentation to the management team.

As a Business Development manager, I work with our CTO to identify new and innovative technologies that have great potential in the market today and in the future. I am then responsible for managing the relationship and bringing in accounts that are interested in these technologies to prove their viability.
