Laura Nathan’s Summary

Writer, editor, and blogger for magazines, websites, and companies addressing education, women's issues, health, fitness, nutrition, photography, travel, books, culture, and identity. Clients include Cooking Light, Photo District News, Prevention.com's Buzz Blogs, Mother Jones, Paste, Bitch, Barnes & Noble, The Travel Channel, and the Community Health Foundation of Western and Central New York.

Laura Nathan’s Specialties:

Content aggregation; planning meetings and events; web branding and marketing; social media; SEO; PC and Mac proficient; Dreamweaver; Photoshop; InDesign; CMSs such as Wordpress, TeamSite, Joomla, Content-Link, and Stylus; EmailLabs; highly proficient in HTML; familiar with the Associated Press Stylebook and the Chicago Manual of Style.
