Katy Tafoya (Terpack)’s Summary

I am both a coach and a consultant working with small businesses introducing social media and web 2.0 applications. I love nothing more than helping others learn how to use the current social networking tools that are out there to help establish themselves as an expert.

My main focus is teaching how to incorporate blogging and blogs into one current business model. Through blogging, you can both increase your search engine rankings and relevance, as well as get your name out there as a knowledgeable expert.

I've been working with online communities and blogging for the past five years. I know how to create and utilize online communities as an added service to your product, as well as how to interact and engage effectively in the various online social networking environments.
