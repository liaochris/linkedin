Nik Stanbridge’s Summary

A technical graduate with over 20 years industry experience in customer facing roles. Employs a flexible approach and is a good communicator with a wide skill set. An experienced Product Manager with extensive experience of global B2B and B2C market sectors. A strong software product background, with further practical experience of product design, marketing, sales and support, OEM product integration, service delivery and client / account management.

Key skills
-Product Management: Full product lifecycle, marketing specifications, product roadmap, product research and competitive analysis, product launch, presentations to customers and press.
B2B and B2C client and supplier liaison at all organisational levels, including board level and RFP process.
-Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written. Presented at industry conferences and trade shows.
-Membership and participation in industry bodies.
-Design, creation and presentation of technical specifications, support documentation, technical training courses, product brochures, marketing collateral and web site copy, and publicly available specialist white papers.
-Specialist knowledge in the field of electronic document generation, manipulation, analysis, use and publishing.
-Strong aptitude for self-learning in relation to personal development and the acquiring of new technical skills.
-Ability to lead, motivate and influence people outside my reporting structure.
-Fluent French spoken, written and read.

Nik Stanbridge’s Specialties:

Management of full product lifecycle; requirements analysis and prioritization; roadmap development; competitor analysis; writing product collateral, marketing materials and white papers; market segmentation; writing business plans.
