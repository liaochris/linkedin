Andrej Mrackovski’s Summary

Experienced, multi-disciplined designer
Able to deal with conceptual, visual and technical aspects of producing online media

Specialized in Flash (design, animation and programming)

Providing professional, flexible and cost effective service.

Powered by professional network of partners (designers, programmers, copywriters, graphic designers, illustrators, translators, consultants and managers).
