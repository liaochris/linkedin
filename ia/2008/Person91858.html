Jitendra Jain, PMP’s Summary

With Project Management Institute’ (PMI)’s ‘Project Management Professional’ (PMP) certification, B.E. in Electronics & Communication and 8 years of experience in designing, developing embedded / electronic control systems in automotive domain, I have gained fairly good technical and project management expertise.

Engine Management Systems and Chassis Systems have been the areas in automotive domain, where I have been largely applying my embedded systems software know-how, along the way picking up substantial automotive domain knowledge.
I have been leading teams of size 10+ for past 2 years, performing role of technical leader, bridging gap between mechanical engineers and software engineers as domain consultant. I have developed good project management & people management skills, by taking on challenges of large size projects and teams and successfully delivering them. ‘Accelerated PMP’, a training program helped me align my project management skills with best of PM approaches.

Started my journey with a large North American auto component company’s Off-shore development center, where I was involved in Failsafe (FMEA) testing & analysis, V&V, etc. I grew along with the teams and took upon the role of Team leader.
Moved to another large Auto Component company’s India Development Center, Where I contributed significantly in developing and testing a next generation Diesel Engine Management System. In next project, I handled good size teams, did on site project development & coordination etc.
I am currently associated with a small special Indian company where the focus has been into end-to-end embedded product development. I have been involved in interesting roles of Pre-sales support, technical and project management.
