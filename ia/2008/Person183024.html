Sudhir Jangir’s Summary

I am Sudhir Jangir, graduated from IIT Delhi in 1998, ex-Co-Founder of Algorism Softech Pvt Ltd, and presently Co-Founder and Director at S M Macario Software Pvt. Ltd. Before Algorism I had worked with Nagarro, Fiorano, Induslogic...

My Company, SM Macario is a young, vibrant, fast-growing company providing onsite and offshore programming services related to application development, custom software solutions, and product development R&D with prototype creation. The company is formed out of the collective effort of a group of IIT engineers.

It specializes in Java, .Net, and SharePoint/MOSS Development.

It has been developing high end applications for our clients using the most advanced technologies like Java / J2EE, Ajax, Open Source, PHP, Microsoft .Net 2.0/3.0, Adobe Flex with MySQL, Postgres, Oracle and Microsoft SQL Server.

SM Macario’s product development activities include leading-edge technologies like SharePoint Portal 2003 / MOSS 2007, Microsoft.NET 3.0 and Office 2007 XML API.

SM Macario is always ready for free trial of their work / services.

SM Macario would be happy to share our existing work, clients’ references, and case studies if needed.

We are based in Gurgaon, India and work for our clients in US, UK, & India.

We are looking for people who can work with us to grow our business further. If you feel that there is an opportunity of mutual benefit, I shall be happy to discuss the same.
