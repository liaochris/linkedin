Manuel Correia’s Summary

So far I have been through many different phases. While I was studying Arts I wanted to create flash Games and that's what I did for a while. I also wanted to create graphic novels and I did that too (mainly writing sctipts during the long train ride for school).

I tried to get into independent Game Developing and I have been in two teams these last two years, Varsoft and GADT (which stands for "Game Addicts Development Team"). At the same time I made a game prototype for the Games 2006 Independent Video Game Creation Contest.

Right now I am getting ready to go to Escape Studios for the Maya Comprehensive for Games course.

Manuel Correia’s Specialties:

concept art, character design, logo design

On the programs side of things, i use Photoshop, Flash, Freehand, Maya and I have basic knowledge of Premiere, XSI and 3D Studio Max.
