Dmitry Bestuzhev’s Summary

Kaspersky Lab

Dmitry Bestuzhev was born in Ukhta, Russia, and has lived in Quito, Ecuador, since 2003.

He has worked in IT security for more than 8 years in a variety of roles, including those of network administrator, IT security officer, IT security consultant and senior IT security engineer. In 2006 he taught post-graduate courses in network security at a local university and also supervised student dissertations. In 2008 he taught post-graduate courses of "malware analysis" at the Universidad Central of Chile - www.ucentral.cl

Dmitry has worked at Kaspersky Lab since 2007. Initially hired as a technical consultant, he now works as virus analyst in the Latin America region. This role involves collecting threats local to the region, performing preliminary analysis, and producing statistics and forecasts for the area. Dmitry still retains some of his responsibilities as a technical consultant; he provides the media with interviews and comment, and also participates in seminars for students at local universities.
