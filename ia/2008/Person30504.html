Carina Fiedeldey-Van Dijk’s Summary

Carina helps companies achieve top performance and leadership, grow their business and attract and retain valued employees and customers.

Using assessment results and consumer databases, she makes performance and the bottom line come full circle through:

* Competency development, leadership, team building.
* Effectiveness, enhanced productivity.
* Individual or group profiling, customer segmentation, competency matching.
* Validation of tests/surveys, prediction of success.

Carina Fiedeldey-Van Dijk’s Specialties:

* Emotional Intelligence, leadership, team building
* Research consulting, statistical analysis
* Public speaking, keynotes, competency modeling
* Assessment, training, coaching, personal development
