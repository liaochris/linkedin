Steve Roper’s Summary

My thirty years of experience creating advertising and print collateral materials began to take a turn towards the ocean in 1991, when I launched a small bareboat charter / scuba diving operation and I continued to develop / integrate this further in 2000 after my very successful tenure as Associate Creative Director for BBDO Windsor, where we launched the PT Cruiser for Chrysler Canada.

In 2000, my wife and I opened Ocean's Edge, a small eco-tourism resort in Belize and simultaneously ran a Five Star resort with a dive operation, which we designed and supervised the construction of. During this period, we led dive tours seven days a week and had the opportunity to lead the Board of Directors of the World Wildlife Fund on several occasions.

After selling Ocean's Edge, we have continued to pursue non-profit opportunities and have produced work for Doctors Without Borders, Habitat for Humanity and CUSO, raising several million dollars over an eighteen month period.

We have also successfuly developed www.oceansedge.com, which specializes in dive tours globally and is a small portal for conservation information.

I now sit on the Board of Directors for The Ogden Point Enhancement Society and continue to pursue opportunities to work with marine conservation societies globally.

I am currently building several web sites for a wide variety of clients including a Quebec based Vineyard, an inventive Traditional Toy company called TJ Whitneys, a Toronto Real Estate firms, PartyMart, an ingenius novelty company based in Montreal and a non-profit organization, The Ogden Point Enhancement Society as well and providing web strategy for several Caribbean based resorts.

My goal is to continue developing clients within the marine conservation, scuba diving, eco-tourism, travel industries by consulting on their web site strategy and development, search engine optimization, search engine marketing and social media initiatives.

Steve Roper’s Specialties:

Creative Direction, Brand Identity, Web Strategies, Internet Marketing, Conservation, Scuba Diving, Eco-Resort Development, Sustainable Tourism.
