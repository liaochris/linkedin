Thomas Atwood’s Summary

I have 15 years of investment industry experience with 10 years investing experience with long-short equity as well as institutional long-only products, with a strong, differentiated track record audited by Deloitte & Touche. As a portfolio manager, my portfolio's performance ranked among the top 10% of all diversified U.S. long/short equity funds on an absolute return basis and among the top 15% on a risk-adjusted return basis per Pertrac. The returns were also uncorrelated with U.S. indices as well as hedge fund indices.

My research experience is as a generalist, with stock selection responsibilities across all market capitalizations, industries, and markets. I have extreme passion for bottom-up stock selection, but also remain attentive to macro themes that may impact the fundamentals of specific companies or industries. This approach provides important perspectives and assists in avoiding value traps.

My investment style is fundamental and opportunistic, yet systematic, analytical, and disciplined. I thoroughly value all companies using both a discounted cash flow and multiples comparison methodology, and also evaluate companies to see if they possess a sustainable competitive advantage that will allow them to earn and sustain economic profits (or not) for longer periods.

I construct portfolios on a sector neutral basis (best ideas long and worst ideas short), and apply modest sector weighting tilts based on the attractiveness of names from my bottom-up research as well as perspectives gained from macro analysis.
