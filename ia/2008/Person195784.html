Vince Fitzpatrick’s Summary

In February 2001 I joined Radian Group as a Business Analyst/Project Manager whose responsibilities were to document requirements for Information Technology initiatives and then lead those projects to a successful conclusion. In the years following Radian begun to focus on the development of an internal security team. I began my involvement with security by managing the corporate building access card process. This evolved into gathering the requirements and project managing the implementation of a new corporate physical security system. After a successful implementation I began to manage several other security related activities such as a Quarterly Audit process, Security Awareness, Security Event Review, Incident Response and Identity Management.
