Kristen DaSilva’s Summary

I am a Technical Recruiter in the Permanent Placement division of FootBridge Staffing. My priority is to focus on Infrastructure including system/network administration/engineering, database administration/architecture, SAN/NAS, NOC, and operations. I am however, a small part of a larger entity here at FootBridge that handles all aspects of IT, including but not limited to areas such as: software development, tech support, product/project/program management, graphic/web design, pre/post-sales, and QA.

I have a broad sense of technical and functional knowledge useful for establishing relationships with internal/external hiring managers, and carefully matching candidates with selective permanent opportunities.

I specialize in permanent placement opportunities located in Southern/Coastal NH (Nashua, Manchester, Concord, Portsmouth), Northern MA (Lowell, Chelmsford, Wilmington, Tewksbury, Bedford, Burlington), Boston, Cambridge, Metro Boston, (Waltham, Woburn, Framingham, Worcester, Lexington, Hopkinton) and many others

I like to connect with talented people who enjoy working with a recruiter such as myself who will diligently seek out opportunities that satisfy the needs of both the candidate and the client. I’m proud to be a part of a company that prides itself in the desire to build relationships and provide individuals with opportunities for growth and career advancement. I’m a firm believer that there’s more to recruiting than matching keywords. So whether it's work-related, or just for the sake of networking, feel free to send me an e-mail anytime.
