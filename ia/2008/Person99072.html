Junsheng Chen’s Summary

Once a soft-spoken and reserved individual, Junsheng’s decision to shatter the shackles of fear and self-doubt became the best decision that he has ever made. Once resolved, he took every possible step towards a transformational beginning that saw astonishing returns in all aspects of his life.

The desire for self actualization further propelled him to the role of Publicity Officer, and then Vice President Education in the communication-centric NTU Toastmasters where he mentored and oversaw the education of over 50 members. His selfless efforts and uncanny drive won him many accolades and he was elected, by popular vote, as the Club Toastmaster of the Year. He was also the 2007 champion of the NTU Toastmasters' Evaluation Contest: a testament to his abilities to provide constructive and unbiased feedback to students in the realm of public speaking.

Recognized for his astute observation and keen judgment, Junsheng has also been invited to judge at numerous speech contests. His capacity to lead, excite and inspire saw efforts by the leadership of other Toastmasters clubs to bring him under their wing. He eventually challenged himself to take on the role of the Vice President Public Relations in an attempt to revitalize the Radin Mas Toastmasters Club.

A freelance emcee, Junsheng also saw action at the Singapore Street Festival 2008, an event endorsed by Dr. Vivian Balakrishnan, Minister for Community Development, Youth and Sports; and the Induction Ceremony for the prestigious Nanyang Fellows Program, where he spoke in front of his Excellency, Mr. Sajjad Ashraf, High Commissioner of Pakistan to Singapore. To date, Junsheng has worked with the Ehi-Passiko Training Company, Interconex Management, Performa International, and Public Speaking International.

Junsheng holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Bioengineering (Honors) from the Nanyang Technological University.
