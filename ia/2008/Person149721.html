Peter Chopra’s Summary

Senior finance professional, qualified as a Barrister-at-law (Gray's Inn) and Chartered Accountant (KPMG). 14 years experience in international, multi-stream businesses delivering exceptional shareholder returns.

Founding force behind the establishment of Synovate, now the 7th largest market research group in the world. During tenure, Synovate developed from a $137m revenue company operating in 2 countries with 900 staff to a truly international, fully integrated market research business generating $750m revenue in 52 countries with 6,000 staff. Led the successful closure of 37 acquisitions. This has been backed by a very successful integration and change management process, proof of which is that Synovate in 2006 has, for the third year running, achieved the highest organic revenue growth of all the major market research groups.

Previously, Deputy CFO of Aegis Group plc, having trained under the leadership of Sir Crispin Davies (now CEO Reed Elsevier) and Colin Day (now CFO Reckitt Benckiser) and latterly Douglas Flynn (now CEO Rentokil) and Jeremy Hicks (now non-exec First Choice Holidays). During the 1993-2000 period, Aegis went from a market capitalisation of £180m to £1.8 billion, turnover from £2.7 billion to £5.7 billion and staffing went from 1,700 in 18 countries to 5,800 in 40 countries.
