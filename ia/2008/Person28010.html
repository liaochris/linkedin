Cliff Flamer’s Summary

I've been immersed in the field of career development since 1998, first as a Recruiter and subsequently as a Certified Professional Resume Writer, Career Counselor, and Job-Search Coach.

As Executive Director of BrightSide Resumes, I bring forth the following qualifications to assist our clientele:

* Career record in developing 100’s of top-tier résumés
* Master's degree in Career & College Counseling
* Double-certifcation with PARW & NRWA resume associations
* Certification with NBCC national counseling body
* Multi-published author in print and online media
* Natural ability to find the ‘bright side’ of people’s work history

In addition to writing distinctive resumes and career documentation that will make your head spin, I'm equipped to assist folks who are ready to explore their career options, define (or redefine) their personal and professional selves, build up their qualifications and self-efficacy, and/or execute a successful job search.

My career experience also extends into the educational sector, as a graduate instructor of counseling, as well as an admissions counselor, advisor, and career counselor at 2- and 4-year colleges throughout the Bay Area.
