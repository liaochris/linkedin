Patrick Gunn’s Summary

Over 16 years experience as a business leader and online consumer marketer with Yahoo!, Hewlett Packard and Procter & Gamble.
• Led the delivery of product and marketing strategies inclusive of industry trends, competitive situation, target market, user needs, market opportunities, and recommended actions.
• Directed online and offline activities including an award-winning website, email, blog, advertising, promotions and product launches.
• Built multi-functional teams, managed people and led matrix teams of up to 25.

• Instilled culture of collaboration, data-driven decisions, continual optimization and passion for customers.
