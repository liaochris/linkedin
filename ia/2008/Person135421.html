Idris Mootee’s Summary

A business and innovation strategist who operates at the intersection of business strategy + experience design + emerging technologies.

With more than 25 years of strategic consulting experience including Blast Radius, Organic, McKinsey, CBIZ, Live Lowe. His job is to help companies to create and realized growth opportunities by bringing DESIGN THINKING into BUSINESS STRATEGY and embed "business strategy with experience prototyping".

Idris provides C-level executives and board-level strategic counsel, innovation and strategic guidance for multiple industries that often involve leading growth initiatives and innovation management. Frequent speakers at international prestige conferences and author of four business books in strategy and marketing. He can be contacted at imootee(at)ideacouture.com

Idris Mootee’s Specialties:

Innovation Strategy, Corporate Strategy and Business Unit Strategy, Multi-Channel Strategy, New Venture Development Strategy, Global Brand and Marketing Strategy.
