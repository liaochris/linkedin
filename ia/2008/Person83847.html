Jane Prugh’s Summary

Jane brings high energy, enthusiasm, and over 20 years experience with strong, customer-focused business development skills to Fortune 500 companies. The road to Jane’s success is paved with measurable results and demonstrations of her expertise in the areas of business development in IT solutions, project management training, leadership training, and process-reengineering.

While working with Cap Gemini America (CGA), Jane functioned in the role of both sales of IT consulting solutions and as a consultant for change management. She was identified as part of “Best Practices” to help implement an international Business Process Re-engineering (BPR) initiative to transform CGA from a transaction-based company to a business solution-oriented organization with increased revenue and profits.

At The Rummler-Brache Group (Deloitte & Touche), Jane was recognized as a top producer of multi-million dollar process re-engineering and performance measurement consulting services to management-levels of major companies.

Jane consistently exceeded customer and employer expectations with excellent work performance at The Ken Blanchard Companies (organizational effectiveness training), ESI International (project management and business analysis training programs), and AG Consulting (ERP solutions).
