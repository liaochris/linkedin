Perk's Profile
Marketing and advertising professional with thirteen years experience. Ten years advertising agency experience working across a large variety of industries in both consumer and B2B markets targeting many different audiences. Three years launching, owning and operating my own marketing and advertising agency.

I have worked in a marketing consultant role consistently since 1999. These projects have varied from smaller, campaign specific jobs to larger strategic initiatives. At the most complex, I have built entire marketing communication plans from the ground up. Managed creation through implementation and optimization.

I am a valuable resource for target audience and industry strategic insights. I also can analyze research findings and provide reports on implications against the business, the competition and/or the marketing plan. Make topline media recommendations based on your core objectives. Write/evaluate creative briefs and campaign objectives.
