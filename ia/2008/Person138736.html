Nathaniel McNamara’s Summary

I am a technology expert with a broad range of Internet/software/wireless experience.

I am currently making investments in growth stage companies with a strong interest in online/alternative advertising, online gaming, wireless applications/platforms, software as a service, business services, new energy alternatives, and rapidly growing traditional businesses. I am always open to companies with strong management teams in any of the areas mentioned above which generate between $8 to 20M+ in revenue, are profitable (or nearly so), and which are growing at a rapid rate.

Prior to HIG I was the President of Chintano, an online advertising company where I managed all of Chintano's day to day operations, including business development, negotiations, project management, and relationship management. I was extensively involved in fund raising and hiring of new staff. Datran Media acquired Chintano in 2007.

I had another successful entrepreneurial experience when I was one of the original team members of PrivacyBank.com, an online commerce company designed to streamline online transactions. The company was acquired for $30 million in 2000 by InfoSpace and the technology was used to help serve AT&T, Cingular, American Express, and others in their mobile and online commerce platforms.

I also worked as a Vice President at JP Morgan Chase where I led the release of four new wireless/mobile products to grow the credit card business. I was part of the original team that developed the PayPass/Blink card for contactless payments.

I began my career working for McKinsey and Company, and previously held internships at Andersen Consulting (now Accenture), Millennium Technology Ventures, and the White House.
