Pierre PÔLET’s Summary

I started a long time ago in IT working as a developer first and moving then towards project management responsibilities.
I experienced different structures and sectors before joining Emakina in 2004.

Working either as Project or Account Manager @Emakina, I'm involved in the follow-up of very different and challenging projects from the very first concept to its happy ending.

I love diversity which is my daily reality as I work for customers coming from various industries and sectors and for which we provide multiple and creative solutions.

As a member of the Emakinian adventure, I have been involved already in dozens of exciting and rewarding projects (e-business applications and solutions, e-marketing strategies and campaigns, interactive and information websites, consultancy in web architecture, usability, ...) and there are much more to come !

Since december 2005, I'm also responsible of the project management team which is a mix of 25 to 30 talented and passionate people focusing on success, team spirit and of course client's satisfaction.

Since january 2008, I'm leading our E-DM (Electronic Direct Marketing) department. The team, made up of project managers, web integrators, developers, e-dm strategist, is providing a wide expertise in direct marketing to all our customers.
