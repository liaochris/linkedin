Robert Huntsman, CFA’s Summary

Experienced executive with MBA, CFA charter and strong track record in sales, finance, marketing, and technology.

Member of management team of and global head of sales for leading venture-backed financial technology software company.

Career spans over a decade of building and leading sales and business development teams and helping to guide corporate strategy for new technology ventures in the financial services and telecommunications industries.

Results include generating 30% annual revenue growth for multi-million dollar companies, signing key early stage reference accounts, initiating and closing six-figure sales engagements, building and managing six-figure sales pipeleines from scratch, developing and executing corporate strategy that led to penetration of billion dollar markets, venture capital fundraising, business plan development, generating strong absolute returns for institutions and wealthy investors, and building and leading teams.

Robert Huntsman, CFA’s Specialties:

Corporate strategy, product strategy, sales, marketing, and finance. Time-series analysis, financial modeling, performance attribution, optimization, asset allocation. In-depth knowledge of equity, fixed income, and commodity futures trading and portfolio management.
