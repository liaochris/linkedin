Karl Simonsen’s Summary

A pioneer in Mobile Entertainment and Software with international experience. Co-founder of VectorMobile (Bidinski.com) and co-founder of Indiqu (now HandsOn Mobile) one of the worlds first mobile entertainment companies. Since 1998 he has been active in the areas of wireless and internet entertainment, and as a founder he has successfully sold three companies. Experienced in business development, strategy, and product marketing.

At Bling Software we are changing the way people will use their mobile phones by making the data capabilities EASY to use. Our software solution provides a fully extensible, AJAX-based cross-platform player that runs outside the browser on mobile handsets. The Bling player runs on top of existing user application platforms already deployed in today's handsets.

Now anyone who can build a web page can author a mobile widget or application in days, not months, and provide consumers the most visually stunning and data-rich mobile user experience while abstracting the complexity from the device handsets available today

Karl Simonsen’s Specialties:

mobile wireless ecosystem, international business development, sales, product definition, operations and management, business development, product marketing and development.

Familiar with all types of mobile applications and content delivery, presentation and standards. Experience with various mobile OS including Symbian. Hands-on experience with SMS, J2ME, WAP, xHTML, 3GPP, BREW.
