Angela Benton’s Summary

Angela Benton is a Creative Director and Designer with seven years experience in visual communications, including print and electronic media. As a Principal and Founder of Visual i Designs Studio, Angela has developed creative solutions for clients in a wide range of industries.

Her creative experience spans a variety of industries through her consultative relationships with companies such as UPS, Bizjournals.com, Lendingtree.com, and Realestate.com among others. She is presently leading design at Black Web Enterprises an IAC Company. Angela is also the publisher of BlackWeb20.com, a blog that analyzes the use of Web 2.0 technologies and trends on websites that target African-Americans and African-American culture.

Experienced in managing designers, freelancers, and agency relationships; Angela has also provided creative direction (web and print) to two publications as well as acquired celebrity content through her contacts. Angela's skill set ranges from providing creative concepts to moving those concepts through visual rough drafts to final implementation.

Angela graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Communications with a specialization in Digital Design. She has also completed postgraduate coursework in Graphic Design from Savannah College of Art and Design.

Angela is a member of the National Association of Black Female Executives in Music and Entertainment and currently serves on the Management Board of the Charlotte Network as Membership Director. In her spare time she is devoted to bringing awareness to the lack of African-Americans who hold management and executive positions in the Design and Web industries.
