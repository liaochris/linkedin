    (Public Company; 51-200 employees; VDSI; Computer & Network Security industry)

  • Product Owner

    Computer Associates

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; CAI; Computer Software industry)

    1988 — 1994 (6 years)

    Directed all development activities for the Security, Control, and Audit (SCA) division. SCA products were installed in approximately 65% of IBM mainframes worldwide. SCA products include CA-ACF2, CA-Top Secret, CA-VMAN, CA-Examine, CA-OnGuard and CA-PanAudit. Directed development of CA-ACF2 to meet and exceed the U.S. NCSC B1 level of trust.

  • Manager Systems Software Development

    SKK, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Computer & Network Security industry)

    1982 — 1987 (5 years)

    SKK was the first independent (non-IBM) security vendor in the industry. Began career with SKK as a systems software engineer developing ACF2. By 1987, ACF2 became the number one security product in the world, installed in over 50% of IBM mainframes worldwide.

    Specialized in cryptography, advanced authentication, IBM operating systems, and trusted systems as defined by the U.S. NCSC (C2 and B1).

    SKK was acquired by UCCEL December 1986 and UCCEL was subsequently acquired by Computer Associates International, Inc. in 1987.
