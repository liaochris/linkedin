Mac McCormick’s Summary

Mac is a hunter passionately dedicated to the art of business management, selling, and market development. A Fortune 500 consultative sales and project management veteran, with two decades-plus experience with entrepreneurial companies, as well as major business units with global and public corporations.

Mac is experienced in accelerating the growth of companies through sustainable, long-term business development and healthy organic growth. He has a progressively responsible professional work experience including the positions of VP of Business Development,COO, President, and founder. He is experienced in managing projects and staff of more than 35 including multi-disciplinary teams.

Mac has a BA from the University of Oklahoma, Norman, and keeps abreast of technology developments through great books. His training includes Huthwaite, SPIN® Selling, High Impact Selling, Miller Heiman LAMP, and Six Sigma deployment. He is a member of the Institute of Internal Auditors, International Accounts Payable Professionals, and a Certified Fraud Examiner Associate.

Mac is an experienced senior manager comfortable with responsibility for the development, implementation, and achievement of the annual work plan. He has nine years of consultative experience including company turn-around, integrations, financial auditing, and best practice assessments for public companies and privately held firms.
