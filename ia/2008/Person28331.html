Brian Rubin’s Summary

Brian Rubin is a cost out specialist who has experience in P & L management, process improvement, forecasting, financial modeling, business case analysis, and operations strategy, including work at The Home Depot, Lowe's Companies Inc., Nortel Networks, and the RadioShack Corporation. He currently employed by Bank of America as an Assistant Vice President in operations. In the past he has consulted on strategic, process and financial matters to companies such as Belk Inc., the largest privately owned retailer in the US, Bank of America and Commercial Trading Company Limited, a start-up phase importer. Prior to this Brian was the Controller for Adecco North America in their Southern Division Office. Here he was responsible for all financial aspects of 115 branch staffing division, including assignment of a $406,000,000 sales budget and pricing contracts. Before Adecco, Brian had a fifteen year business career encompassing corporate strategic planning, tactical execution, operations, and financial analysis as well as management of staffs of up to 120. He is the Chairman of the Brookhaven Homeowners Association Advisory Committee and holds a Bachelors Degree in Finance from Old Dominion University. Real world experience throughout the entire operations lifecycle give Brian the ability to analyze data, design, get buy-in, implement and execute strategic and tactical plans that result in additional revenue and cost savings for a wide variety of business situations.
