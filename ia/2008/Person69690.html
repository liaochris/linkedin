Sasha Giacoppo’s Summary

Innovative and experienced eLearning producer and Instructional designer with a PhD in Educational Communication and Technology. A strong and varied background in interface, interaction, and instructional design has led me to pursue opportunities for designing highly innovative and forward thinking multimedia-based learning experiences for a wide variety of corporate, academic, and not-for-profit organizations.

I have a great interest in designing and developing unique multimedia learning programs for any organization interested in pushing the envelope with digital learning experiences to achieve their goals. My continuing academic interests are the design of eLearning programs and experiences, particularly for application in developing nations and unique domains.
