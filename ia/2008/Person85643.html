Jayaram Vengayil’s Summary

* Mature, hands-on Chartered Accountant at the helm of affairs of multinational companies and emerging organizations in the Food and Beverage segment of the FMCG space in India and West Asia

* Presently, Group Financial Controller heading the Finance and IT functions of Al Yasra, a fast-growing USD 450 million Kuwaiti group with diverse interests ranging from consumer goods, fashion retailing and niche Information Technology solutions. Current Focus areas are forming and incubating Strategic Alliances, spearheading the financial and legal aspects of the Group's regional expansion plans through acquisitions and greenfield projects, building financial capabilities within the Group through setting and measuring Key Performance Indicators,.

Started with the group as Financial Controller of the flagship Al Yasra Food Company which is the largest distributor of leading international frozen & chilled food products in Kuwait and Iraq.

Previous experience includes:
o Over 5 years Finance & IT leadership experience with Coca-Cola in the Middle East and Britannia Industries, the bakery major in India.
o Over 10 years as Profit Centre Finance & Commercial Head with Britannia (Southern Region, Chennai) and Wipro (computer factory, Mysore)
o Stints in Corporate Controllership (Britannia) and Internal Audit (Wipro or Britannia) round off the profile.
