Sinnary Sam [LION]’s Summary

My network is open! Please connect professionally online as I am on most business networking sites. Invite me to connect at sinnary@forenetworking.com.

I believe that learning and playing the sport of golf does something special for a person. We mature, we grow, we improve and we become disciplined.

I love to play though you'll be surprised to read that I'm pretty new to the sport. My score is nothing to brag about. But, what I learned through golf reminded me that this same discipline can be implemented in goal setting for life and business.

I network quite a bit. I love meeting new people, learning about what they do and seeing if there is any way I can help.

My background is highly administrative. I have provided executive support to some of the founding owners of one of the largest subprime lenders in the nation. I have also managed an office for a technoligy firm. My background includes: Marketing, Finances, Administration, Management, H.R., Vendor Relationships, Vendor Contracts to name a few.

I took on golf primarily to spend some time with my husband and children. After meeting up with some business professionals at a local golf course's practic range so we could all practice our golf swing, I decided to expand my group and invite other participants. It turns out that this is a great venue for business professionals who are passionate about golf. Meeting at the driving range allows people to participate in a minimal amount of time, network with other business owners and still have time get some work done, whereas a golf tournament would take an entire day from someone's schedule.

I put together FORE Networking and the launch was a huge success. www.FOREnetworking.com. My goal is to create a venue for business professionals to truly use golf as a tool to build relationships, thus resulting in more business. Bringing a referral out for golf and making the introduction is a great way to connect people.
