Dana Lane’s Summary

Chief photographer Dana Kacedon Lane has been with the
Hunterdon County Democrat for over seven years. She
moved to Hunterdon County, NJ in second grade. Her
love for photography grew when her father gave her his
Nikon FM. From there she took classes in high school
and college. She attended Fairleigh Dickenson University
in Madison where she majored in Electronic Filmmaking,
Digital Video Design, and Computer Graphics in hope of
landing a job with a major motion picture company. After
working at Entertainment Tonight, independent films and
AT&T she realized what she had been searching for her
entire life was right in front of her- community, family
and a job worth waking up in the morning for. After
starting at the Democrat she felt she had found her nitch.
“I love meeting new people everyday; getting to
photograph their jobs, hobbies or them just having fun.
You can really put a smile on someone’s face when you
recognize them for just being themselves.”
In her spare time she does freelance photojournalistic and
portrait photography for bands, events, sports, animals,
landscapes and family photos. She is in the process of
doing a book on other peoples spirituality, whether it is a
religion or something that connects the person to
something larger then themselves.
