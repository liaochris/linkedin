Brian Kurth’s Summary

I absolutely love working with our hundreds of passionate VocationVacations mentors across 150 vocation types including culinary, fashion, sports, entertainment, working with animals, design, hospitality and the list goes on an on. Placing our clients ("vocationers" as we call them) into 2-3 day dream job experiences with our mentors goes beyond job fulfillment for me. I'm lucky. I have the epitome of the dream job as I get to live vicariously through our vocationers, mentors and our affiliated career/life coaches. Life is indeed good.

Now I plan to grow VocationVacations to include more ancillary products such as one-hour phone discussions with our mentors, dream job career coaching, telechats, CDs and speaking engagements. All very exciting. The business is really taking off.
