William Lohr’s Summary

Currently, I am working for Dynport Vaccine Company, LLC, in Frederick, MD.

Previously, I provided study management support to an international Phase IB systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) trial and a Phase IIA asthma trial at MedImmune, Inc.

I moved into "field monitoring" in 2005 in oncology and then overall study management in early 2007.

I became involved in clinical trial management in 2003. I started out working on a large Phase III heart failure trial performing in-house site management and troubleshooting for 150 sites.
