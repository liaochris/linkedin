Jamie Leo’s Summary

An imaginative multi-disciplined creative director who designs and writes, I have a solid track record of new business development, competitive analysis, and content development (including Web 2.0+ social network taxonomies and nomenclature) for holistic integration of vertical branding experience.

I have a unique ability to design and deploy cross-platform branding experiences (knowledgeable at a Heidelberg press check and with Ruby-on-Rails development), and I'm an effective mentor to designers due to a passion for craft, state-of-the-art hands-on Adobe CS3 competencies, industry awards adjudication, and participation on design panels.

Recent projects include development of creative strategy and deployment for Wolters Kluwer, Scholastic, McGraw-Hill, United Rentals, Office Depot, American Express, and other organizations, large and small.
