Phil Wilson’s Summary

"How we communicate with each other personally and how business communicates with it's customers changes rapidly. New channels that provide information sprout up daily, if not hourly. Sometimes we react with enthusiasm, or disbelief, or fear. And sometimes we react with full on panic at the overload. No matter what we think of the way we do it, we must "remain" communicating"

I have years of experience in communications and marketing, as well as an extensive history in successfully building and managing brands in unique and effective ways for broadcast companies. I'm adept at translating complex products and services to multiple market segments and decision makers. In addition, I can help you successfully deliver a message that resonates with consumers and keeps them coming back. I stand ready to bring skills and passion to a brand, project, or company that is looking to adopt or enhance "new media" channels to build lasting relationships with customers.
