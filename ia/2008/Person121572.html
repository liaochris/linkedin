Mark Bielecki’s Summary

In 1996, I started JL Blake, Inc. with the goal of being the best recruiter in the industrial field. Over the last eleven years I've learned how to do it right and how to do it wrong. We concentrate on doing it right. We concentrate on Marketing in the Chicago / Rockford / Milwaukee area (the ChiRocKee Triangle)

I believe the time has come for a revolution in the recruiting business. It’s time for us to become faster, more relevant & less expensive. We need to communicate more effectively in the Web world - and we’ve developed a program that does just that.

We call it joblish, and you can learn about it at www.joblish.com. joblish users will be able to communicate better & faster, reducing the cost of doing business for all of us.
