Yusef Svacina’s Summary

Yusef, after having worked in editorial on features such as Richard Linklater's, A Scanner Darkly, and working as an assistant editor on Linklater's barely known 12 year project, Boyhood, Yusef caught the eyes of 30 year film and editorial boutique veteran George O'Dwyer, and worked for 501 Post, one of the leading editorial boutiques in Austin, as a commercial editor. Some clients included AT&T, the U.S. Air Force, and a political spot campaign for Mindy Montford for District Attorney. Yusef now runs his own shop, Swatch Post, offering editing and post production services throughout Austin.
