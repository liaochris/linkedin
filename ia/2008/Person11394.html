Andrew Breen’s Summary

An experienced technology entrepreneur and executive ranging from startup software product and service companies to leading product development groups within larger organizations, Andrew has driven the formation and growth of several businesses to successful launch and expansion. Most recently, as Director of Palm’s Services Product Marketing Group, Andrew led the formation and strategic direction of Palm’s new wireless data services business. This included responsibility for product marketing and development; sales and market strategy; and infrastructure vendor evaluation and negotiation. The business grew from concept to one of the top three initiatives at Palm within one year.

Andrew leverages his experience and knowledge of business strategy and product development working to start and expand new businesses as well as advise and consult to financial and other businesses operating in the technology and consumer products industries.
