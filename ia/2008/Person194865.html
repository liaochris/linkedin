George Van Antwerp’s Summary

Technically savvy MBA who has developed and launched several offerings in the healthcare space. Passionate about the opportunities to drive change in the healthcare industry by applying new models and new technologies.

Responsible for leveraging Silverlink's technology for creating 1:1 personalized and automated phone calls for healthcare to offer clients (payors, providers, PBMs, pharmacies, fulfillment companies, pharma) turnkey solutions that leverage direct marketing fundamentals with data mining and multi-modal communications.

Known for helping clients (internal and external) design process-centric visions and solutions that cross functional and organizational boundaries.

To see more about my thoughts and background, visit my blog at www.patientadvocate.wordpress.com.
