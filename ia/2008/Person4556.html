Ajay Kurien’s Summary

I completed an MBA at Cass Business School in London (Sep 2006 to Sep 2007), and wrote my dissertation on the conditions necessary for a mobile NFC ecosystem to flourish in the UK (nearfield communications: touch-based interactions using mobile phones for payments, marketing, ticketing, etc.)

I'm currently working with a classmate on a new venture called KCGi. We have developed a framework to help law firms understand and tackle organisational change programmes for workplace diversity.

I am also interested in contactless and mobile payments, and would be interested in hearing about projects in these fields. My previous role involved research and analysis of the cellular/wireless market worldwide. The "Maximising Wireless Profit" Program is a key operational and planning resource for the strategy and marketing leaders at our mobile network operator clients worldwide (http://www.wirelessprofit.com).
