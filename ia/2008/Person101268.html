Joost van de Wijgerd’s Summary

I started my professional career in 95 as a programmer for a software games company. During this period I also studied artificial intelligence at the University of Amsterdam.

In 1999 I was one of 6 founders of SmartHaven, a company specializing in user profiling and community building through smart (A.I.) technology. The company had multi-million euro funding. I acted both as CTO and Architect within the company.

In may 2002 SmartHaven was sold. I left and started JTeam together with Alef Arendsen. Our goal was to build a company around Enterprise Open Source Software (EOSS)that would do in-house projects based on an agile methodology, consult other companies on how to apply EOSS and provide support on EOSS.

In 2004 we founded the JTeam Group that participates in JTeam and in SpringSource.

JTeam consists of 20+ people and analyses, designs and builds business critical systems for medium-size businesses (50-500 employees) using Open Source Enterprise Java.

SpringSource is a company that provides consulting and training services around the Spring Framework and other Enterprise Open Source technologies. SpringSource got funded $ 10 Million from Benchmark Capital in 2007.

In April 2006 I left JTeam Group and have taken a well deserved sabbatical. Currently I am working part-time as an Independent IT-Consultant as well as setting up new businesses. Currently I have active investments in the following companies:

IJO Technologies: Java Software Development company in Hong Kong and Chengdu,China
Bmbo Europe: "Painless" software development outsourcing with a revolutionary model
IRL: startup behind IRLConnect application, due to go into private Beta this summer

I am always looking for new opportunities. I can provide seed capital and time to set up your business. As long as it is IT related (web2.0 and software development)
