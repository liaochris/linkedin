Nat Darke’s Summary

I am 37 years old and have 8 years development experience, specialising in Client Side Technologies of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. My work experience includes 5 and a half years the BBC where, amongst other achievements, I wrote the BBC Browser Support Standard. My main skills are W3C compliant HTML, CSS and OO JavaScript (including jQuery and Ajax). I also have a strong interest in the design and user interface aspects that these technologies are used for. In all aspects of my work I write graceful, modular code and am very mindful of accessibility, multi-platform considerations and graceful degredation. I am also experienced with many other technologies including MS Visual Studio, XML and XSLT, JSP, ASP, XSSI, Photoshop and various CMS and versioning systems.

You will find me easy to get on with and hard working. I produce work of the highest quality and prefer to work with others who have some appreciation of this. I have good communication skills and am comfortable with sharing my understanding of the web with others at any level, when required.

Nat Darke’s Specialties:
