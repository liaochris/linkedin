Ken Baker’s Summary

Quantitative, results oriented human resources business partner with extensive accomplishments in supporting sales, service, and manufacturing operations in union and non-union environments at the corporate, division, regional, and local level.

Ken Baker’s Specialties:

Organizational culture change management, strategic organizational project planning and execution, staffing and recruitment, policy development, employee and labor relations, compensation, benefits, training and development, union negotiations, employee outplacement, legal compliance, EEO / AAP, grievance and arbitration administration, union free program development, mergers and aquisitions, performance management

Ken Baker’s Experience

  • Director of Human Resources

    Scientific Games

    (Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; SGMS; Information Technology and Services industry)

    2001 — 2006 (5 years)

    Reporting to the President of this 800 employee, multi-state, multi-national, high technology, lottery systems management company, with total responsibility for all HR management.   • Division Human Resource Manager

    Bridgestone Firestone

    (Automotive industry)

Managed a staff of 28 professionals in HR, Labor Relations, Medical, Security, Safety, and Employee Development departments.
