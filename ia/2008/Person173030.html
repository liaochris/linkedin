Sergio Guillen’s Summary

I work in environmental and community mediation and conflict management training. I also have extensive prior experience in renewable energy promotion assessment and finance. Currently, my work focuses on institutional innovations that apply the principles of deliberative democracy to environmental governance.

Sergio Guillen’s Specialties:

Transformative mediation, conflict management (particular communal and environmental) facilitation and training.
Renewable energy assessment, finance, promotion and environmental monitoring and evaluation.
Participatory research in peace psychology, environmental governance and deliberative democracy
