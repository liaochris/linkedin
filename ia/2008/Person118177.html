Mahesh Narayan’s Summary

Mahesh has 8.5 years of cross-industry experience. He started his career in Sales working for a web development company. He joined a reputed business school in their Consulting division working on projects from Multi-Lateral agencies. He also worked for a start-up consulting firm before joining for an MBA at IIM Calcutta. He joined the Aditya Birla Group in the Corporate Management Services division (Internal Consulting team). He worked on some high business impact projects.

He is currently leading the Strategic Projects & Consulting team at Hewlett-Packard, Decision Support & Analytics Services division. In this role he is leading some of the strategic projects in the area of Acquisitions, New Market Opportunity assessments etc. In his earlier role in the organisation he set-up the Industry Market Intelligence Practice.

He has exposure to varied industries like Information Technology, Commodity Industries like Aluminium, Copper, Cement & Textiles.

He has master’s degrees in Commerce (University of Calcutta) as well as in Economics (University of Madras) and is an MBA with specialization in Strategic Management from Indian Institute of Management Calcutta.

He keeps himself updated on business trends and contributes his thoughts to management journals and business dailies. He is an Member of the All India Management Association (http://www.aima-ind.org/) and the Consultancy Development Centre (http://www.cdc.org.in/).

In his past time he teaches at some of the leading Business Schools in the country. He has taken courses in Strategic Management and Marketing at Indian Institute of Social Welfare and Business Management, Calcutta, SCMHRD, Pune & JD Birla Institute of Management, Calcutta.

Mahesh Narayan’s Specialties:

He has business consulting experience in the areas of Acquisitions, Market Opportunity, International Trade, Business Process & Information System Improvements.
