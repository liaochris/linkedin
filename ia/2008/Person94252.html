Jeff Johannigman’s Summary

Versatile, innovative leader with particular strengths in training, staffing, coaching, and career development. An award-winning public speaker recognized for presenting complex topics with clarity, creativity, and enthusiasm. Degrees in Human Services and Computer Science demonstrate both interpersonal and analytical capabilities. Extensive track record motivating diverse teams and building successful client relationships.

Major accomplishments include:
• Managed development of over 50 eLearning courses for companies like Dell, Whole Foods, Centex Homes, and Motion Computing.
• Established the college recruiting program and managed worldwide staffing for the Tivoli Systems division of IBM
• Created one of the country's first alumni career services for the University of Texas
• Personally coached hundreds of people to find greater career success and fulfillment
• Founded two successful software companies, and produced over twenty published computer games
