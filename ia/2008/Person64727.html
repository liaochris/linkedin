Wade Fenner’s Summary

In just one year, there were 3,126 homes in foreclosure in the Edmonton and Calgary area alone. Foreclosures Canada Information Systems Inc. provides you with that information. Armed with the knowledge from our foreclosure data service & the two-day seminar, or home study program, you will be able to start purchasing, selling, and profiting from properties right away.

This two-day seminar titled “Foreclosures Made Easy” is a Two Day Intensive program for anyone who wants to know more about the real estate business. Even if you have never looked at an investment property before, we will help you find Alberta foreclosure homes, purchase them and flip them to make huge profits! It is designed for any investor and is well understood by beginner and intermediate level real estate investors and will pay for itself the first time you use the information and materials provided.

Our seminar and the Calgary & Edmonton foreclosure lists are full of information that a real estate agent or lawyer can’t ever tell you about simply because they probably don’t know about it. Foreclosures Canada Information Systems Inc. will reveal the truth behind several long-standing, but unfounded myths. Techniques will be discussed on how to purchase properties by the dozens, write contracts, negotiate a better purchase price, get financing, and of course the “ins and outs” of buying and selling your investment.

I created this seminar from all I have learned what and what not to do in the field of foreclosures.

After months of research on Alberta's foreclosure system, I did find out how to access the foreclosure information to produce our foreclosure lists.
