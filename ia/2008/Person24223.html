Blake Coleman (LION) (bcoleman@fgp.com)’s Summary

Modern Healthcare's 29th Ranked (Top 5%) Executive Search Firm

My focus lies in aiding health care organizations across the United States to "Find Great People" for their Director needs in Pharmacy, Laboratory, and Radiology departments. My approach is quite simple; I am a match-maker. I am here to ensure that the marriage between candidate and client is one where both parties are truly excited about one another.

Many professionals choose to work with me because they want to look confidentially. It is understandable that in the age of high speed information and in the health care environment where the "secret handshake" are all too common, candidates do everything in their power to secure their current situation while conducting their search.

I am a preferred vendor for some of the nation's largest health care systems and independent facilities alike (over 400 clients). In the competitive environment that we work in it is of the utmost importance to find the right hire and find them quickly. Because I am consistently recruiting the same skill sets day in and day out, I achieve this and do so while making sure that candidates are fully qualified based on skills, motivation for change, compensation, and relocation (if applicable).

I welcome the opportunity to speak with you or your organization. Please do not hesitate to give me a call if you have further questions, or need additional information regarding our team. I can be reached at the following; bcoleman@fgp.com or 800-638-1661.
