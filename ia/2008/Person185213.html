Anthony Tamburrino’s Summary

Specializing in visual creativity for print and commmercial advertising, Anthony has worked with countless respected brands. The coveted stylist also designed his own line of men's and women's sportswear apparel that was sold in high-end boutiques and select retailers. As the Couture and Evening Specialist at Neiman Marcus Bal Harbour, Anthony created an array of glamorous, sophisticated and trendy looks for socialites, celebrities and CEO's. He also met with influencial designers at their NYC showrooms to assist in buying for the Neiman Marcus luxury department stores nationwide. Also infusing real estate investing has made Anthony become an Interior Stylist with a keen eye and attention to detail. He has decorated and staged residential homes for sale. Home makeovers presents a unique opportunity to utilize these skills in creating a visual experience for buyers and sellers of homes, multi-units and commercial real estate. These skills combined with the self-motivation and entrepreneurial drive offers a competitive advantage in these markets. Visit http://www.tamburrinogroup.com .
