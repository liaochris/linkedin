Joe Parsons’s Summary

The Terminal Velocity Racing program was instituted by Terminal Velocity Processing, Inc. a payment services provider.

TVP is the title sponsor for the Pacific F2000 racing series and the Wide Open Baja Ultimate Challenge during the Baja 1000.

Terminal Velocity Processing contributes funding dollars to the Racing program through the net proceeds of transactions processing that are derived from automotive related merchants.

The Terminal Velocity Racing initiative is designed for worldwide expansion with opportunities for integration into virtually every racing series over the next several years.
